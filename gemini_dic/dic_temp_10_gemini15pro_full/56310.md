doghole:/ˈdɒɡˌhəʊl/ (British) /ˈdɑːɡˌhoʊl/ (American)| n.|狗窝；狭小的、不舒服的地方；隐蔽处；(航海)甲板或船侧的小孔|A small, cramped, and often unpleasant or shabby place.  Also, a small opening in a deck or ship's side. |例句：The apartment was a real doghole, barely big enough for a bed and a chair.  这公寓真是个狗窝，勉强够放一张床和一把椅子。

例句：Sailors used dogholes to secure ropes. 水手们用小孔来固定绳索。|近义词：cramped quarters, hovel, cubbyhole, den|反义词：spacious room, mansion, palace|词性: 名词
