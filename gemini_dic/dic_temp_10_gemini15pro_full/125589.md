multitudinous:/ˌmʌltɪˈtjuːdɪnəs/| adj.|大量的，众多的，无数的|Existing or occurring in great numbers; very numerous. |例句：The multitudinous stars in the night sky. 夜空中繁星点点。|例句：The actress received multitudinous fan letters.  这位女演员收到了无数的粉丝来信。|近义词：numerous, many, countless, innumerable, myriad|反义词：few, scarce, limited|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *multitudinōsus*, from Latin multitūdō (“multitude”) + -ōsus.
