gout:/ɡaʊt/| n. |痛风|A disease in which defective metabolism of uric acid causes arthritis, especially in the smaller bones of the feet, deposition of chalkstones, and episodes of acute pain.|My grandfather suffers from gout.|近义词:arthritis, hyperuricemia|反义词:(None - Gout is a specific disease without a direct opposite)|词性:noun
