misunderstand:/ˌmɪsʌndərˈstænd/
|v.|误解，曲解|To interpret something incorrectly.|I think you misunderstood my meaning. 我想你误解了我的意思。|近义词:misinterpret, misconstrue|反义词:understand, comprehend|词性:动词
