geer:/ɡɪər/ | n. | 齿轮，传动装置 |A toothed wheel that works with others to alter the relation between the speed of a driving mechanism (such as an engine) and the speed of the driven parts (such as the wheels of a vehicle). |The car's geer slipped, causing a loud grinding noise. 汽车的齿轮打滑，发出很大的摩擦声。| synonyms: cog, cogwheel, gearwheel | antonyms: N/A | noun
