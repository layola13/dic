half-fertileness:/ˌhæf fərˈtɪlnəs/
| n. |半不育性，半育性|The condition of a hybrid being only partially fertile, often due to chromosomal incompatibility between the parent species.  指的是杂交种只有部分可育的状态，通常是由于亲本物种之间染色体不相容造成的。|The half-fertileness of the mule is a well-known example of hybrid sterility. 骡子的半不育性是杂种不育性的一个著名例子。|Near Synonyms: semi-sterility, reduced fertility|Antonyms: full fertility, complete fertility|noun
