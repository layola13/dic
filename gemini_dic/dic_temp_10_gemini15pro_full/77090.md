garrisons:/ˈɡærɪs ənz/
garrison:/ˈɡærɪsən/| n. vt.|驻军，卫戍部队；守备；驻防|A body of troops stationed in a fortified place.  The act of stationing troops in a place for its defense.|The garrison defended the fort.  这支驻军保卫了要塞。|近义词：troops, soldiers, forces|反义词:civilians|词性:名词,动词

garrisons:/ˈɡærɪs ənz/| n.|驻军，卫戍部队|The soldiers stationed in a place for its defense.|Several garrisons were established along the border.  沿着边界建立了几个驻军点。|近义词：troops, soldiers, forces|反义词:civilians|词性:名词


词源: From Old French *garir (“to protect, defend”) (from Frankish *warjan “to defend, protect”), and ultimately from Proto-Germanic *warjaną (“to defend, protect”).
