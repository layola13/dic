on-the-fritz:/ˌɑːn ðə ˈfrɪts/ /ˌɒn ðə ˈfrɪts/
adjective
故障的；失灵的
malfunctioning; out of order; not working properly
My old refrigerator is on the fritz again. 我的旧冰箱又坏了。
近义词:broken, malfunctioning, out of order, not working
反义词:working, functioning, in order
