achinesses: /ˈeɪkinəsiz/
noun
疼痛；酸痛
A general feeling of pain or discomfort, typically in the muscles or joints.
I woke up with aches and achinesses all over my body. 我醒来时浑身酸痛。

My achinesses have increased since yesterday.  自从昨天以来，我的疼痛加剧了。


近义词: pains, soreness, discomfort
反义词: comfort, well-being
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From achy + -ness + -es (plural suffix)
Achy comes from ache (Old English acan) + -y (adjective suffix).
