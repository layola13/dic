overmuchness:/ˌoʊvərˈmʌtʃnəs/| n.| 过多，过量| An excessive amount; superfluity.  It refers to an overabundance or excess of something, often implying a negative connotation of being overwhelming or burdensome. |The overmuchness of the decorations made the room feel cluttered.  装饰品过多，使房间感觉杂乱。|近义词：excess, superfluity, surfeit, plethora|反义词: lack, scarcity, dearth, insufficiency|词性: noun

Etymology:
From 'overmuch' + '-ness'. 'Overmuch' is from Old English 'ofermycel', from 'ofer-' (over) + 'mycel' (much).  '-ness' is a suffix forming nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
