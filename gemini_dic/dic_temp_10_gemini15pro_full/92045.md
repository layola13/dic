hyperphoric:/ˌhaɪpərˈfɔːrɪk/ /ˌhaɪpərˈfɔːrɪk/ | adj. |  欣快症的；过度兴奋的；情绪高涨的 | Characterized by excessive elation or euphoria. Marked by or expressing extreme excitement. |  He was hyperphoric after winning the lottery. 他中了彩票后欣喜若狂。| Synonyms: elated, euphoric, exhilarated, jubilant, ecstatic | Antonyms: depressed, downcast, dejected, melancholic | adjective
