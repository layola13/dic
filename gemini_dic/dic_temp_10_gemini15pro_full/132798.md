nondyspeptically:/ˌnɒndɪsˈpɛptɪkli/
| adv. |无消化不良地|Without dyspepsia; without indigestion.|He ate his meal nondyspeptically, enjoying every bite. 他毫无消化不良地吃完了饭，享受每一口。|Synonym: easily, comfortably|Antonym: dyspeptically|Adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + dyspeptically.  Dyspeptically comes from dyspepsia + -ally. Dyspepsia comes from Greek *dyspepsia*, from dys- "bad" + peptein "to digest".
