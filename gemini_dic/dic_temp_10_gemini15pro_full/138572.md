/wɒt/ (UK) /hwɑːt/ (US) |pron., det., int., adv.|什么，多么|Used to ask for information about something; used to express surprise or disbelief.|
What is your name?|你的名字是什么？

What time is it?|现在几点了？

What a beautiful day!|多么美好的一天！

近义词: which, how|反义词: N/A|pronoun, determiner, interjection, adverb

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast?|你早餐吃什么？
What does he do for a living?|他做什么工作？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching?|你在看什么？
What is she cooking?|她在做什么菜？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned?|你学到了什么？
What has he done?|他做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on?|你一直在忙什么？
What has she been reading lately?|她最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say?|你说了什么？
What happened last night?|昨晚发生了什么事？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing yesterday at 5 pm?|你昨天下午五点在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party?|她参加派对穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you eaten before you came here?|你来这里之前吃了什么？
What had they discussed before the meeting?|他们在会议前讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I called?|在我打电话之前你一直在做什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do tomorrow?|你明天要做什么？
What will she say when she finds out?|她发现后会说什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):

What will she be studying next semester?|她下学期会学什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
