gluggable:/ˈɡlʌɡəbl/
adjective|易于理解的; 易于接受的; 容易下咽的; |Easily swallowed or consumed; palatable; easily understood or accepted.|The marketing campaign presented the new product as gluggable, emphasizing its ease of use and adoption.|近义词: palatable, digestible, easy to understand, accessible|反义词: unpalatable, indigestible, difficult to understand, inaccessible|词性: adjective
Etymology:  A relatively recent coinage (likely late 20th or early 21st century), formed by adding the suffix "-able" to "glug," which imitates the sound of liquid being swallowed quickly. This implies something that goes down easily.
