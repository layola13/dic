antithesis:/ænˈtɪθəsɪs/
n.
对照，对立，对偶
a person or thing that is the direct opposite of someone or something else.  A figure of speech in which an opposition or contrast of ideas is expressed by parallelism of words that are the opposites of, or strongly contrasted with, each other, such as “hatred stirs up strife, but love covers all sins”.
His flamboyant style is the antithesis of her quiet elegance. 他的华丽风格与她恬静的优雅截然相反。

Speech is silver, but silence is gold. 雄辩是银，沉默是金。

近义词:opposite, contrast, converse
反义词:similarity, resemblance, likeness
词性:noun
