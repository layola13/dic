acellular:/eɪˈsɛljʊlər/
adjective
无细胞的；非细胞的
Not made up of cells; not containing cells.
Viruses are acellular entities. 病毒是无细胞实体。
Acellular vaccines are safer than traditional vaccines.  无细胞疫苗比传统疫苗更安全。

近义词:noncellular
反义词:cellular
