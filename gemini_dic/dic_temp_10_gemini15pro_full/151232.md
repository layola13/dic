Example 2: Researchers are investigating the role of paremptosis in neurodegenerative diseases. 研究人员正在研究程序性细胞死亡在神经退行性疾病中的作用。|Synonym: programmed cell death, caspase-independent cell death|Antonym: cell survival, cell proliferation|noun
