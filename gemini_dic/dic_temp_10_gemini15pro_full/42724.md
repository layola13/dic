counterweight:/ˈkaʊntərˌweɪt/| n.|平衡重，平衡力，砝码|a weight that balances another weight, especially one that moves up and down with it, as in some types of machinery|The crane uses a large concrete block as a counterweight.|近义词:balance, counterpoise, offset|反义词:imbalance|noun

counterweight:/ˈkaʊntərˌweɪt/| vt.|使平衡，抵消|to act as a counterweight to something; to balance or offset something|His optimism counterweighted her pessimism.|近义词:balance, offset, compensate|反义词:destabilize|verb
