inter-coordinated:/ˌɪntərkoʊˈɔːrdɪneɪtɪd/
|adj.|相互协调的，互相配合的|Arranged or organized to work together smoothly and efficiently.|例句：The inter-coordinated movements of the dancers were mesmerizing. 舞蹈演员相互协调的动作令人着迷。|例句：The project requires inter-coordinated efforts from multiple departments. 这个项目需要多个部门的相互协调的努力。|近义词：coordinated, synchronized, harmonized, integrated|反义词：uncoordinated, disjointed, fragmented|词性:形容词
词源: "inter-" (prefix) from Latin, meaning "between, among, mutually, reciprocally" + "coordinated" from "co-" (prefix) meaning "together" + "ordinate" from Latin "ordinatus," past participle of "ordinare," meaning "to arrange, order."
