ferrule:/ˈferəl/ (US), /ˈferuːl/ (UK)| n. |金属箍，套圈；接头管；鞭梢|A ring or cap, typically a metal one, that strengthens the end of a handle, stick, or tube and prevents it from splitting or wearing. | The ferrule on my walking stick is loose. 我的拐杖上的金属箍松了。| The electrician crimped a ferrule onto the end of the wire. 电工在电线末端压接了一个套圈。 | synonyms: cap, ring, band, tip | antonyms: N/A | noun
