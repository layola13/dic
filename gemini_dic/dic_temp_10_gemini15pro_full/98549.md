instillment:/ɪnˈstɪlmənt/| n.|灌输；注入；(分期付款的)一期付款|the act of gradually establishing an idea, feeling, or attitude in a person's mind; a part of a sum of money owed that is paid at regular intervals until the whole amount is paid|例句：The instillment of good values in children is crucial.  在儿童时期灌输良好价值观至关重要。

例句：The final instillment of the loan is due next month.  贷款的最后一期付款将于下个月到期。|近义词：installation, inculcation, implantation, infusion, injection, payment|反义词：removal, extraction|词性:noun


Etymology:
From Late Middle English instilment, from instille + -ment. Equivalent to instill + -ment.
