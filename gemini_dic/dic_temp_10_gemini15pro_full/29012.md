cartogram:/ˈkɑːrtəɡræm/| n. |统计地图|A map on which statistical information is shown in diagrammatic form.  Areas are shown in sizes proportional to the value of the variable being mapped, such as population or production.|The cartogram clearly showed the distribution of wealth across the country.|Synonym: statistical map, thematic map|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "standard map")|noun
Etymology:  From French *cartogramme*, from *carte* (“map”) + *-gramme* (“something written or drawn”). Ultimately from Greek *χάρτης* (khártēs, “sheet of papyrus”) + *γράμμα* (grámma, “something written”).
