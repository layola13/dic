overboastfulnesses:/ˌoʊvərboʊstˈfʊlnəsɪz/
| n. |过度自夸，极度自负|The state of being excessively boastful; excessive bragging or bragging about unimportant things.|His constant overboastfulnesses alienated his friends. 他不断的过度自夸疏远了他的朋友们。|近义词：arrogance, conceit, vanity, egotism|反义词：humility, modesty|词性: noun (plural)

Etymology:
From over + boastful + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  "Boastful" comes from the Middle English "bostful," from "bost" (boast) + "-ful."  "Over-" is an Old English prefix meaning "above," "across," "beyond," or "excessive."
