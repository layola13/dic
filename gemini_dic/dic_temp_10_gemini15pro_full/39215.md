conglomerate:/kənˈɡlɒmərət/ (UK) /kənˈɡlɑːmərət/ (US)| n. adj.| 聚集物，企业集团，砾岩 |A thing consisting of a number of different and distinct parts or items that are grouped together.  A large corporation formed by merging companies in different industries.| The company is a media conglomerate. 这家公司是一个传媒集团。| synonyms: amalgamation, combination, corporation, aggregate | antonyms: division, separation | noun

conglomerate:/kənˈɡlɒmərət/ (UK) /kənˈɡlɑːmərət/ (US)| adj.|  聚集的，成团的 |Formed of separate units collected together.| The rock is a conglomerate of various minerals. 这块岩石是由各种矿物聚集而成的。| synonyms: combined, composite, mixed | antonyms: simple, homogeneous | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin conglomeratus, past participle of conglomerare (“to roll together, heap up”), from com- (“together”) + glomerare (“to wind into a ball”), from glomus (“ball”).
