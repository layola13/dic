cabbaging:/ˈkæbɪdʒɪŋ/
verb (gerund or present participle)
偷窃；窃取（尤指小东西）;  非法侵占
Acquiring something dishonestly, typically small items; pilfering.
He was caught cabbaging office supplies. 他被抓到偷办公室的用品。
synonyms: pilfering, pinching, filching, swiping, purloining, thieving
antonyms: returning, giving, donating
词性: v.

cabbaging:/ˈkæbɪdʒɪŋ/
noun
偷窃；窃取（尤指小东西）
The act of acquiring something dishonestly, typically small items.
The cabbaging of company property is a serious offense.  盗窃公司财物是严重的违法行为。
synonyms: pilferage, theft, larceny
antonyms: donation, restitution
词性: n.

词源:  The word "cabbage" in this sense likely comes from the 18th-century slang term "cabbige," referring to tailors' remnants of cloth, which they would often keep for themselves.  This practice evolved into the broader meaning of pilfering or stealing small items.
