cybernetics:/ˌsaɪbərˈnɛtɪks/ | n. | 控制论 | The science of communications and automatic control systems in both machines and living things.  | Example 1:  He's studying cybernetics at university. 他在大学学习控制论。

Example 2: The development of artificial intelligence is closely related to cybernetics. 人工智能的发展与控制论密切相关。 | 近义词: control theory, systems theory | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cybernétique*, from Ancient Greek κυβερνητικός (kubernētikós, “good at steering, governor”), from κυβερνάω (kubernáō, “I steer, I govern”).  Coined in 1948 by Norbert Wiener.
