mopsy:/ˈmɒpsi/ (UK) /ˈmɑːpsi/ (US)| n.|小兔子，毛茸茸的小动物，（尤指）孩子|A term of endearment for a child or a pet, especially a rabbit, suggesting sweetness and fluffiness.  It often implies that the person or animal is small and cuddly.|例句：She called her daughter "my little mopsy." 她叫她的女儿“我的小宝贝”。

例句：The little mopsy hopped across the lawn. 小兔子蹦蹦跳跳地穿过草坪。|近义词：bunny, rabbit, pet, darling, sweetheart|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "beast" in a contrasting sense)|词性:noun
