illogicality:/ˌɪˌlɒdʒɪˈkælɪti/ /ˌɪˌlɑːdʒɪˈkælɪti/| n. |不合逻辑； illogicality of an argument|The illogicality of his argument was apparent to everyone. 他的论证不合逻辑，每个人都看得出来。| The illogicality of her decision surprised us all. 她的决定不合逻辑，我们都感到惊讶。|近义词：irrationality, absurdity, unreasonableness|反义词：logic, rationality, reason|词性:noun

词源:  From illogical + -ity.  Illogical comes from in- "not" + logical.  Logical traces back to Greek *logikos "of or pertaining to reason," from logos "reason, word."
