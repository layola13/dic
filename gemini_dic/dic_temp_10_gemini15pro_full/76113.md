furtherment:/ˈfɜːrðərmənt/
n.|促进，增进，推进|The furtherance of knowledge is a noble pursuit. 增进知识是一项崇高的追求。|例句：His actions were detrimental to the furtherance of peace. 他的行为对和平的推进有害。|近义词：advancement, promotion, progress, development|反义词：hindrance, obstruction, impediment|词性:名词

Etymology:
From further + -ment.  "Further" itself comes from Old English "furthor", comparative of "fore" (before).  The "-ment" suffix is from Old French, ultimately from Latin "-mentum", indicating a result or product of an action.
