mensuration:/ˌmenʃəˈreɪʃn/ | n. |测量；测量法；月经| the process of measuring something; the branch of geometry concerned with finding the length of lines, areas of surfaces, and volumes of solids; menstruation | The mensuration of the room was precise.  房间的测量很精确。| synonyms: measurement,  gauging,  survey,  metering,  quantification | antonyms: estimation, approximation | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *mēnsūrātiō (“a measuring”), from Latin mēnsūrō (“I measure”), from mēnsūra (“measure”), from mētior (“I measure”).
