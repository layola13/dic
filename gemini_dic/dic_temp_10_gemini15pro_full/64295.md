equivalently:/ɪˈkwɪvələntli/| adv.|等效地，相同地|in a way that is equivalent; in other words|例句：The two statements are equivalently true. 这两个陈述是等价的，都是真的。|例句：Equivalently, you could multiply both sides of the equation by 2.  等效地，你可以将方程的两边都乘以 2。|近义词：similarly, likewise, correspondingly|反义词：differently, dissimilarly|词性:副词
Etymology->
From Latin *aequivalentem (nominative aequivalens), present participle of aequivalere “be equivalent,” from aequus “equal” + valere “be strong, be worth.”
