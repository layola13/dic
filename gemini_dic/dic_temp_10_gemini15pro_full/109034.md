legacies:/ˈlɛɡəsiːz/
noun
遗产，遗赠物；后遗症
Something (such as property or money) that is received from someone who has died; something that comes from the past or a past event or action.
His father left him a legacy of a million dollars. 他的父亲给他留下了一百万美元的遗产。

The war left a legacy of pain and suffering. 战争留下了痛苦和苦难的后遗症。

Synonyms: inheritance, bequest, heritage, aftermath
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider:  loss, forfeiture - for tangible legacies;  nothingness, oblivion - for intangible legacies)
