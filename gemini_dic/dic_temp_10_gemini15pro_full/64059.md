epitomization:/ɪˌpɪtəmaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 体现；典型；摘要 |  The act of epitomizing something; a perfect example or embodiment of something. |  Her work is the epitomization of elegance and skill. 她的作品是优雅和技巧的完美体现。 | synonyms: embodiment, personification, quintessence, exemplar | antonyms: antithesis, contradiction | noun

epitomize:/ɪˈpɪtəˌmaɪz/ | v. |  是…的典型；概括；摘要 | To be a perfect example of something; to summarize. | This painting epitomizes the artist's style. 这幅画是这位艺术家风格的典型代表。 | synonyms: embody, exemplify, represent, summarize | antonyms: contradict, misrepresent | verb
