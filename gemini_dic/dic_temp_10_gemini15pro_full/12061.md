artifactual:/ˌɑːr.tɪˈfæk.tʃu.əl/ | adj. | 人为的；因人为因素造成的；人工制成的 |  Caused by the process of observation or testing, and not a natural feature of the thing being observed or tested.  | The increase in heart rate was artifactual, caused by the patient's anxiety during the test.  心跳加快是人为的，是由病人在测试期间的焦虑引起的。 |  artificial, spurious, factitious | genuine, authentic, real | adjective

artifactual:/ˌɑːr.tɪˈfæk.tʃu.əl/ | adj. | 与人工制品有关的；人工制品的 | Relating to or characteristic of an artifact. |  The museum has a large collection of artifactual material from ancient Egypt.  博物馆收藏了大量来自古埃及的人工制品。 | artificial, manufactured | natural, organic | adjective
