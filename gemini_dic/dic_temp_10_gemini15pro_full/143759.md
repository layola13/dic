outmarch:/ˌaʊtˈmɑːrtʃ/
verb
超越，胜过
to surpass or defeat someone in a competition or race, especially by walking faster or marching further.
The smaller company outmarched its larger rivals in developing new products. 这家小公司在开发新产品方面胜过了规模更大的竞争对手。
outstrip, outpace, surpass, exceed
lag behind, fall behind
v.
