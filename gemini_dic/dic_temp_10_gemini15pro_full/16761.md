baryphony:/bæˈrɪfəni/
n.
沉重的声音，粗重的声音；不和谐的声音
A deep, heavy, or dissonant sound; harshness or roughness of sound.
The baryphony of the construction site made it difficult to concentrate.  建筑工地的嘈杂声让人难以集中注意力。
The old engine sputtered and coughed, emitting a baryphony that grated on the nerves.  老旧的发动机发出噼啪作响和咳嗽般的声音，刺耳的噪音让人神经紧张。
近义词:cacophony, dissonance, harshness
反义词:euphony, harmony, melody
词性:noun

Etymology:
From Greek barys (heavy) + -phony (sound).  来自希腊语 barys（重的） + -phony（声音）。
