melted:/ˈmɛltɪd/| adj.| 融化的，熔化的|Something that has changed from a solid to a liquid state, usually because of heat.  The result of melting.|The melted butter soaked into the bread. 融化的黄油渗进了面包里。|Synonyms: liquefied, dissolved, thawed|Antonyms: frozen, solidified|adjective

melted:/ˈmɛltɪd/| v.|融化，熔化 (melt的过去式和过去分词)|To change from a solid to a liquid state, usually because of heat.  Also, to become less intense or to disappear gradually.|The snow melted quickly in the warm sun. 雪在温暖的阳光下迅速融化了。|Synonyms: liquefied, dissolved, thawed|Antonyms: froze, solidified|verb
