cerulescent:/ˌsɛrʊˈlɛs(ə)nt/
adjective
蔚蓝色的；天蓝色的
Becoming or tinged with blue; bluish.
The cerulescent waters of the Caribbean beckoned. 加勒比海蔚蓝的海水在召唤。
Synonyms: azure, sky-blue, cerulean
Antonyms:  tawny, brown, beige
词性: adjective
