falsettist:/fɔːlˈsɛtɪst/ | n. | 假声歌手；用假声唱歌的人 | A male singer who sings in a high register using falsetto. | The falsettist hit a remarkably high note. 假声歌手飙出了一个非常高音。 | 近义词: countertenor, male soprano | 反义词: baritone, bass | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Italian *falsetto* "false voice," diminutive of *falso* "false," from Latin *falsus* "false, untrue, forged, counterfeit."
