conservative:/kənˈsɜːrvətɪv/ (UK) /kənˈsɜːrvədɪv/ (US)| adj. n.|保守的；谨慎的；保守主义者|Averse to change or innovation and holding traditional values.  Relating to or supporting the political party that promotes these values.  (Of an estimate) purposely low for the sake of caution.|
He has a conservative approach to investment. 他在投资方面比较保守。
She's a conservative dresser. 她的穿着很保守。
The doctor made a conservative estimate of the patient's recovery time. 医生对病人的恢复时间做了一个保守的估计。
|近义词：traditional, cautious, conventional, moderate|反义词：radical, liberal, progressive, innovative|词性:形容词, 名词
