matriarchalism: /ˌmeɪtriˈɑːrkəlɪzəm/ | n. | 母系制，母权制 | A social system in which descent and inheritance are traced through the female line, and women hold primary power and authority. |  Many anthropologists believe that some early societies practiced matriarchalism. 许多人类学家认为一些早期社会实行母系制。

The Mosuo people of China are often cited as a contemporary example of matriarchalism.  中国的摩梭人常被认为是当代母系制的例子。

| 近义词: matriarchy | 反义词: patriarchy, patriarchalism | 词性: noun
