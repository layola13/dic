magi:/ˈmeɪdʒaɪ/ |n.|贤士，法师，博学者|Wise men, especially astrologers (like the ones who visited Jesus after his birth).  These wise men are often associated with Zoroastrian priests. |The magi brought gifts of gold, frankincense, and myrrh. 贤士们带来了黄金、乳香和没药的礼物。|例句：The magi studied the stars for guidance. 贤士们通过观星来寻求指引。|近义词：wise men, sorcerers, astrologers|反义词：fool, idiot|词性:noun
