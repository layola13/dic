inspiring:/ɪnˈspaɪərɪŋ/ | adj. | 鼓舞人心的，启发灵感的 | giving you new ideas and making you feel you want to do something | Her inspiring speech motivated the entire team. 她鼓舞人心的演讲激励了整个团队。 | The inspiring story of his success moved many people. 他成功的励志故事感动了许多人。 | 近义词：motivating, uplifting, encouraging, stimulating | 反义词：discouraging, disheartening, demotivating | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *inspiringe, *inspirynge, present participle of inspiren, from Old French inspirer, from Latin īnspīrāre (“to breathe or blow into, inspire”), from in- (“in, into, on, upon”) + spīrāre (“to breathe”).
