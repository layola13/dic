majorities:/məˈdʒɔːrɪtiz/
noun
多数；大多数
the greater number of people or things in a group
例句：The majorities of students passed the exam. 大多数学生通过了考试。

例句：Majorities in both houses of Congress voted for the bill. 国会两院多数议员投票赞成该法案。

近义词：most, plurality, greater part, bulk
反义词：minority, few
词性：名词(plural noun)
