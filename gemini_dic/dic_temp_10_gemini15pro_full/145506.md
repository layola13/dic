overclutter:/ˌoʊvərˈklʌtər/| vt. n.|使杂乱，过杂乱；过多的杂物|To fill a space or area with too many objects, making it messy and difficult to use or move around in.  The room was overcluttered with furniture.|例句：She overcluttered her apartment with too many knick-knacks. 她把她的公寓塞满了太多小摆设，显得杂乱无章。

例句：The overclutter in the office made it hard to find anything. 办公室里堆满了东西，很难找到任何东西。|近义词：clutter, overcrowd, mess up|反义词：tidy, declutter, organize|词性：动词，名词
