must:/mʌst/ | modal verb | 必须，一定 |  Expresses obligation, necessity, or strong probability.  | I must go to the store today. 我今天必须去商店。 | have to, should, ought to |  don't have to, needn't | verb

must:/mʌst/ | noun | 必须做的事；不可缺少的东西 | Something that is essential or unavoidable. |  A good dictionary is a must for any language learner. 一本好词典对于任何语言学习者来说都是必不可少的。 | necessity, essential |  luxury, nonessential | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *moste (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *mustai (“must”), from Proto-Indo-European *mays- (“to be able, have power”). Cognate with Old Frisian mosta, most (“must”), Dutch moeten (“must”), German müssen (“must”), Gothic 𐌼𐍉𐍄𐌰𐌽 (mōtan, “be able”).  Related to may.
