concorporate:/kənˈkɔːrpəreɪt/
verb
合并，组成公司
to unite into one body or corporation; to incorporate
The two companies decided to concorporate to form a larger, more powerful entity. 这两家公司决定合并组成一个更大、更有实力的实体。
The smaller firms concorporated with the industry giant.  这些小公司与该行业的巨头合并了。
近义词: merge, combine, amalgamate, consolidate
反义词: separate, divide, split
词性:v.

concorporate:/kənˈkɔːrpəreɪt/
adjective
联合的，合并的
united in one body
The concorporate efforts of the team led to their success. 团队的共同努力导致了他们的成功。
The newly concorporate company held its first board meeting. 新合并的公司举行了第一次董事会会议。
近义词: united, joint, combined, merged
反义词: separate, divided, individual
词性:adj.


词源:
From Late Latin *concorporatus, past participle of concorporare (“to embody, incorporate”), from con- (“together”) + corporare (“to form into a body”), from corpus (“body”).
