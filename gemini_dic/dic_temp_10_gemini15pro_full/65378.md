euonymin:/juːˈɒnɪmɪn/| n. |卫矛苷|A bitter, crystalline glycoside obtained from the bark and seeds of the spindle tree (Euonymus europaeus) and formerly used as a heart stimulant and diuretic.|Euonymin was once used to treat dropsy. 卫矛苷曾被用来治疗水肿。|Synonyms:  euonymus extract|Antonyms: None|Noun
