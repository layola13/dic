leaflet:/ˈliːflɪt/| n.|传单；小册子；嫩叶|A printed sheet of paper, sometimes folded, containing information or advertising and usually distributed free.|They were handing out leaflets about the local election. 他们正在分发关于地方选举的传单。|synonyms: pamphlet, brochure, flyer, handbill, circular|antonyms: book, volume|noun

leaflet:/ˈliːflɪt/| v.|散发传单|To distribute leaflets.|They leafleted the neighborhood to advertise their new business. 他们在附近散发传单，为他们的新生意做广告。|synonyms: distribute, circulate, hand out|antonyms: collect, gather|verb
