annulation:/ˌænjuˈleɪʃn/ | n. | 取消，废除 | The annulment of the contract relieved both parties of their obligations.  合同的取消解除了双方的义务。 | Example: The annulment of their marriage was finalized last month.  他们婚姻的废除上个月完成了。| Synonyms: cancellation, revocation, invalidation, abrogation | Antonyms: ratification, confirmation, validation | noun

Etymology:  Late Middle English: from Old French annulation, from Latin annullatio(n-), from the verb annullare ‘reduce to nothing’, from ad- ‘to’ + nullus ‘none’.
