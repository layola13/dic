agrarians:/əˈɡrɛəriənz/
|n.|土地改革者，农业主义者，农民|People who advocate for land reform and agricultural interests.  Those who believe in the importance of agriculture and rural life.|The agrarians believed that farming was the backbone of the nation. 这些土地改革者相信农业是国家的支柱。|Synonyms: farmers, peasants, agriculturalists, ruralists|Antonyms: industrialists, urbanites|Noun

agrarian:/əˈɡrɛəriən/
|adj.|土地的，农业的，耕地的|Relating to land, agriculture, or rural matters.|The agrarian reform aimed to redistribute land ownership. 土地改革旨在重新分配土地所有权。|Synonyms: agricultural, rural, farming|Antonyms: industrial, urban|Adjective
