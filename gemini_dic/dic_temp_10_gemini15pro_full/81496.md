gravenstein: /ˈɡreɪvənˌstaɪn/ | n. |红玉苹果，葛拉文施泰因苹果|A variety of apple with yellow-green skin streaked with red, and crisp, tart flesh.  Aromatic and flavorful, it's often used for cooking and cider making. |例句：Gravenstein apples are perfect for apple pie. 红玉苹果非常适合做苹果派。

例句：The orchard was filled with rows of gravenstein apple trees. 果园里种满了成排的红玉苹果树。|近义词：apple, pippin|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From German _Gravensteiner_, named after the town of Gravenstein (now Gråsten) in Denmark (formerly part of Germany), where it likely originated.
