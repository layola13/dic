fave:/feɪv/| n. |最喜欢的东西（人）; 特别喜爱的东西（人）|Something or someone that is a particular favorite.| My fave song is "Bohemian Rhapsody".我最喜欢的歌是《波西米亚狂想曲》。|近义词：favorite, preferred, pet|反义词：least favorite, disliked|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
"fave" is a shortened, informal form of "favorite".  The clipping of "favorite" to "fave" likely arose in the late 20th century with the increasing prevalence of informal language, particularly in online and social media contexts.
