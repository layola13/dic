alkalious:/ˌælkəˈlaɪnəs/
adjective
碱性的
Having a pH greater than 7; alkaline.  Characterized by or containing an alkali.
例句：The soil in this area is alkalious and not suitable for most plants.  这个地区的土壤是碱性的，不适合大多数植物。

例句：The solution turned alkalious after the addition of sodium hydroxide.  加入氢氧化钠后，溶液变成碱性。
近义词：alkaline, basic
反义词：acidic
词性: adjective

词源:
From alkali + -ous.  Ultimately from Arabic *al-qalīy* "ashes of the saltwort plant".
