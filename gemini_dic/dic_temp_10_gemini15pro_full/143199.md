outcourting: /ˌaʊtˈkɔːrtɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的求爱；在求爱方面超过
Surpassing someone in courtship; outdoing in the pursuit of a romantic partner.
He was outcourting his rival for the hand of the fair maiden. 他在追求那位美丽的少女时胜过了他的情敌。
近义词: outmaneuvering, outdoing, surpassing
反义词: losing to, being surpassed in
词性: 动词
