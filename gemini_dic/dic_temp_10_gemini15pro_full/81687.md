greenfinch:/ˈɡriːnfɪntʃ/ | n. | 绿金翅雀 | A small European finch (Chloris chloris) with a stout bill and predominantly yellowish-green plumage. |例句：A greenfinch perched on the bird feeder. 一只绿金翅雀栖息在喂鸟器上。

例句：We saw a flock of greenfinches in the garden. 我们在花园里看到了一群绿金翅雀。 | 近义词：bird, finch | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology->
From green + finch.  "Finch" comes from Old English *finc, related to German Fink, of imitative origin.
