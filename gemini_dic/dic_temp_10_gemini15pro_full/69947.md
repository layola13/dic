fesswise:/ˈfɛswʌɪz/| adv.| 横向地，水平地|Placed horizontally, like a fess in heraldry.  The fess is a broad horizontal band across the center of a coat of arms.  So, "fesswise" describes something oriented in the same way.|The sword was placed fesswise above the fireplace.|horizontally, sideways|vertically|adverb
