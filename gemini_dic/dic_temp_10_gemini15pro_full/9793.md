anura:/əˈnʊərə/ /ænˈjʊərə/
n.
frogs and toads; tailless amphibians
Anura is the scientific name for the order of amphibians that includes frogs and toads.
The Anura are characterized by their lack of a tail in the adult stage.
近义词:Salientia (another scientific name for the same order), frogs, toads
反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider tailed amphibians like salamanders and newts - Urodela/Caudata)
词性:noun
