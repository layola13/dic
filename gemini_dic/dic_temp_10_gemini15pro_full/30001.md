cavorted:/kəˈvɔːrtɪd/
verb
雀跃；嬉戏
jumped or danced around excitedly
The children cavorted in the playground. 孩子们在操场上嬉戏。
The lambs cavorted in the field. 羊羔在田野里雀跃。

近义词:
frolicked, gambolled, pranced, romped

反义词:
sat still, remained motionless, stood still

词性
verb

词源:
From Italian *cavortare, frequentative of cavare "to prance, rear," from Late Latin *caballicare "to gallop," from Latin caballus "horse."
