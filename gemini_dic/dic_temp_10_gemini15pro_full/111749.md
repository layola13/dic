locutory: /ˈlɒkjʊtəri/ (British) /ˈlɑːkjətɔːri/ (American) | adj. | 谈话的，口头的；非最终判决的 | Relating to speech or conversation; (of a court order) not final or definitive.  |  The judge issued a locutory injunction to prevent the company from selling the property until the case was decided. 法官发布了一项非最终判决的禁令，以阻止该公司在案件判决之前出售该房产。 | The locutory proceedings established the key facts of the case.  初步的口头诉讼确定了案件的关键事实。 |  verbal, oral, interlocutory | final, definitive | adjective
