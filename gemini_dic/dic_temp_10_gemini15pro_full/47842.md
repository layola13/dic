decayless:/dɪˈkeɪləs/
adjective
不朽的，不腐烂的
Not subject to decay; imperishable.
The artist hoped to create a decayless monument to his talent.  这位艺术家希望创造一个不朽的纪念碑来纪念他的才华。
The pyramids were built with the intention of being decayless tombs.  金字塔的建造是为了成为不朽的陵墓。
近义词:imperishable, indestructible, undying, eternal
反义词:perishable, decaying, rotting, decomposing
词性:adjective

词源: "decay" (from Old French "decair," meaning "to fall, decline") + "-less" (a suffix meaning "without").
