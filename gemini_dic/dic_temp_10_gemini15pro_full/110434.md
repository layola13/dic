likeliness:/ˈlaɪklɪnɪs/| n.|可能性，相似性|The likeliness of rain today is high. 今天下雨的可能性很大。|例句：There is a strong likeliness of snow.  下雪的可能性很大。

例句：The likeliness of him winning the lottery is slim. 他中彩票的可能性很小。


|近义词：probability, chance, possibility|反义词:unlikelihood, improbability|词性:noun
