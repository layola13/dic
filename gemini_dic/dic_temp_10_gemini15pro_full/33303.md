chuck-it:/ˈtʃʌk ɪt/ | n. |  抛接球玩具; 扔掉它（命令）; | A toy, usually a ball attached to a handle by a rope, used for playing fetch with a dog.  It can also be used as an imperative phrase meaning "throw it away." | He bought a new chuck-it for his dog. 他给他的狗买了一个新的抛接球玩具。 | 近义词: fetch toy, throw it (as a command) | 反义词: keep it (as a command) | noun

chuck-it:/ˈtʃʌk ɪt/ | v. (informal) | 扔掉; 放弃; 停止; | To throw something away or to give up on something.  | I'm going to chuck it all and move to the beach. 我打算放弃一切，搬到海滩去。 | 近义词: throw away, discard, abandon, quit | 反义词: keep, continue | verb
