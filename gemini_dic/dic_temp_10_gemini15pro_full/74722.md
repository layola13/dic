freeness:/ˈfriːnɪs/
|n.|自由；免费；大方；坦率|The state of being free; generosity; frankness; the quality of being without cost.|The freeness of her laughter made everyone feel at ease. 她爽朗的笑声让每个人都感到轻松自在。|近义词: generosity, liberality, openness, candor|反义词: restraint, restriction, stinginess, reserve|词性: 名词|


词源:  From Middle English *frēnes, from Old English *frēness, from frēo (“free”) + -ness. Cognate with Dutch vrijheid (“freedom”), German Freiheit (“freedom”).
