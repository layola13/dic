albuminimeter:/ˌælbjʊˈmɪnɪmiːtər/
noun
蛋白定量器
An instrument used to measure the amount of albumin in a fluid, especially urine.
A doctor used an albuminimeter to check the patient's urine for protein. 医生用蛋白定量器检查病人的尿液中是否有蛋白质。
synonyms: albuminometer, Esbach's tube
antonyms: None.
n.
