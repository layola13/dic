ensear:/ɛnˈsɪər/ | v. |  使干燥;烤干;烧灼 | To dry, parch, or shrivel, especially by heat; to cauterize. | The hot sun enseared the crops, leaving them withered and brown.  炎热的太阳烤焦了庄稼，使它们枯萎变黄。|  The doctor enseared the wound to stop the bleeding. 医生烧灼伤口以止血。 | synonyms: parch, scorch, sear, wither, cauterize | antonyms: moisten, hydrate, soak | verb
