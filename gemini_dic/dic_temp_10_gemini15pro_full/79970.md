go-bail-for:/ɡoʊ beɪl fɔːr/| v.|为…做担保人；为…交保释金|to pay money to allow someone accused of a crime to leave jail until their trial|Example: He went bail for his brother who was arrested for drunk driving. 他为他因酒驾被捕的兄弟交了保释金。

Example:  She refused to go bail for her friend, believing he needed to learn a lesson. 她拒绝为她的朋友交保释金，认为他需要吸取教训。|近义词:post bail, stand surety for|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 prosecute 指控)|词性:动词短语
