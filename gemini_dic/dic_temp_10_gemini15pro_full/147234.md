overmedicating:/ˌoʊvərˈmɛdɪkeɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度用药
Giving someone too much medication.
The doctor warned against overmedicating children with antibiotics.  医生警告不要给儿童过度使用抗生素。
He was concerned that his elderly mother was being overmedicated. 他担心他年迈的母亲用药过量。
近义词: overdosing, overprescribing
反义词: undermedicating
词性: verb (present participle)
