gustier:/ˈɡʌstɪər/ | adj. | 更狂风的，阵风更大的 | Characterized by stronger or more frequent gusts of wind. | The weather became gustier as the storm approached.  暴风雨来临，风刮得更猛烈了。| The wind is gustier today than it was yesterday. 今天比昨天风更大。 | 近义词: windier, blusterier | 反义词: calmer, quieter | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From gust + -ier (comparative adjective suffix). Ultimately from Old Norse gustr "gust of wind."
