glycocoll: /ˈɡlaɪkəˌkɒl/ (US) /ˈɡlaɪkəʊˌkɒl/ (UK)| n. | 甘氨酸; 氨基乙酸 |A crystalline amino acid, the simplest naturally occurring amino acid. It is a nonessential amino acid, meaning that the body can synthesize it.  It is the smallest of the 20 amino acids commonly found in proteins. |Glycocoll is used in the treatment of schizophrenia and stroke. 甘氨酸用于治疗精神分裂症和中风。|Synonym: glycine|Antonym: None|Noun
