asiphonate:/eɪˈsaɪfəneɪt/
adj.|无虹吸管的；无水管的|Lacking a siphon; without a siphuncle.|The asiphonate cephalopod lacks a siphuncle, a tube-like structure used for buoyancy control.|近义词: non-siphonate|反义词: siphonate|词性:形容词
asiphonate:/eɪˈsaɪfəneɪt/
n.|无虹吸管的生物|An organism lacking a siphon.|Certain mollusks are classified as asiphonates due to their lack of a siphon.|近义词: non-siphonate organism|反义词: siphonate organism|词性:名词
