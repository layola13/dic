mighty:/ˈmaɪti/| adj.|强大的，强有力的；巨大的，浩瀚的；非常，极其|Having great power, strength, or importance; very large or impressive; extremely.|例句：A mighty warrior led the army into battle. 一位强大的战士率领军队投入战斗。|近义词：powerful, strong, huge, immense, enormous, extremely|反义词：weak, powerless, small, tiny|词性:形容词
mighty:/ˈmaɪti/| adv. (informal)|非常，极其|Extremely.|例句：That's mighty kind of you. 你真是太好了。|近义词：very, extremely|反义词：slightly, a little|词性:副词

词源: From Middle English *myghty*, from Old English *mihtig* (“mighty, powerful, strong, capable, possible”), from Proto-Germanic *mahtigą (“mighty”), from Proto-Indo-European *megʰ- (“great, mighty”). Cognate with Dutch *machtig* (“powerful”), German *mächtig* (“mighty”), Old Norse * máttigr* (“mighty”).
