nonregression:/ˌnɑːnrɪˈɡrɛʃən/ | n. | 无回归，不退步 | Absence of regression; maintenance of a desired state or level. |  The software update ensured nonregression of existing features. 该软件更新确保了现有功能不会退步。 | synonyms: stability, maintenance, consistency | antonyms: regression, relapse, deterioration | noun

nonregression:/ˌnɑːnrɪˈɡrɛʃən/ | n. | 无回退，不倒退 | The state of not returning to a previous, usually worse, condition or behavior. |  The doctor emphasized the importance of nonregression for patients recovering from addiction. 医生强调了对于戒瘾康复患者来说不倒退的重要性。 | synonyms:  advancement, progress, improvement | antonyms: regression, relapse, backsliding | noun
