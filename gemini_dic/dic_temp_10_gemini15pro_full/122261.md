mithridate:/ˈmɪθrɪdeɪt/| n. |解毒剂，万能解毒药|A confection believed to be an antidote to all poisons.|The king claimed to have a mithridate that could protect him from any assassination attempt. 国王声称拥有一种可以保护他免受任何暗杀企图的万能解毒药。|Synonyms: antidote, counterpoison|Antonyms: poison, toxin|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *mithridatium*, from Latin Mithridātēs (sixth king of Pontus, said to have immunized himself against poisons), from Ancient Greek Μιθριδάτης (Mithridátēs).
