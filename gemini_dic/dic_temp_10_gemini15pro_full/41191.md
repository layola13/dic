copyrights:/ˈkɒpiraɪts/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpiraɪts/ (US)| n.|版权；著作权|The legal right granted to the creator of original works of authorship, including literary, dramatic, musical, and certain other intellectual works. This right allows the copyright holder to exclusively control the reproduction, distribution, adaptation, performance, and display of their work for a specified period.|The publisher holds the copyrights to all of her books.|近义词: intellectual property rights, author's rights|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "public domain")|词性:noun


copyrighted:/ˈkɒpiraɪtɪd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpiraɪtɪd/ (US)| adj.|受版权保护的|Protected by copyright; having the exclusive legal right to reproduce, publish, and sell the matter and form of a literary, musical, or artistic work.|The copyrighted material cannot be used without permission.|近义词: protected, licensed|反义词: unlicensed, public domain|词性:adjective


copyright:/ˈkɒpiraɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpiraɪt/ (US)| vt.|为…取得版权|To secure a copyright on a work.|She copyrighted her novel as soon as it was finished.|近义词: register, protect|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:verb
