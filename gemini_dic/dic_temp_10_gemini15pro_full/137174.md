nonsuccessive:/ˌnɒnsəkˈsesɪv/ | adj. | 非连续的，不连续的 | Not following in a continuous sequence; interrupted or intermittent. | The meetings were nonsuccessive, taking place over several months. 这些会议并非连续举行，而是持续了几个月。| The patient experienced nonsuccessive bouts of pain. 病人经历了断断续续的阵痛。 |  近义词: intermittent, sporadic, discontinuous, nonconsecutive | 反义词: successive, consecutive, continuous | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From non- (not) + successive (following in order).  "Successive" ultimately comes from the Latin "succedere" (to follow after).
