loggias:/ˈlɒdʒɪəz/ /ˈlɑːdʒiəz/ | n. |凉廊，走廊|A gallery or corridor open to the air on at least one side, especially one that forms part of a house. |The apartment boasts two loggias overlooking the harbor. 这间公寓拥有两个可以俯瞰海港的凉廊。


The hotel has loggias on every floor, providing guests with stunning views.  这家酒店每层楼都有凉廊，为客人提供壮丽的景色。

|近义词：balcony, veranda, porch, gallery|反义词:room, interior|词性:noun
