multistakeholder:/ˌmʌltiˈsteɪkˌhəʊldər/ |adj. n.|多利益攸关方的，多利益相关者的|Involving or concerning several different groups who all have an interest in something (e.g., a business, an industry, a policy).|The government is promoting a multistakeholder approach to internet governance.|Synonyms: multilateral, participatory, collaborative|Antonyms: unilateral, exclusive|adjective, noun
