bise:/baɪs/| v. |二等分，平分|To divide into two equal parts.|例句：The line bisects the angle. 这条线平分这个角。|例句：The architect bisected the room with a screen. 建筑师用屏风把房间一分为二。|近义词：halve, divide|反义词: join, combine|词性:动词

词源: From Latin bīs "twice" + secāre "to cut".
