microphotography: /ˌmaɪkroʊfəˈtɑːɡrəfi/ | n. |显微摄影术；显微照相；缩微照相|The process of taking photographs through a microscope.  It involves taking pictures of very small objects or structures that are not visible to the naked eye.| He specialized in microphotography of biological specimens. 他专门从事生物标本的显微摄影。| 近义词: photomicrography | 反义词: macrophotography | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From micro- (small) + photography (the art or practice of taking and processing photographs).
