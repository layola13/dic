auto-destruct:/ˌɔːtəʊ dɪˈstrʌkt/ | v. | 自毁，自动摧毁 | To destroy itself automatically, often after a set period or upon fulfilling a certain condition. | The spacecraft is programmed to auto-destruct after completing its mission.  宇宙飞船设定在完成任务后自毁。

The device will auto-destruct if tampered with.  如果设备被篡改，它会自毁。 | 近义词: self-destruct, annihilate, destroy | 反义词: preserve, protect | 词性: 动词

auto-destruct:/ˌɔːtəʊ dɪˈstrʌkt/ | n. | 自毁，自动摧毁（装置或程序）| A device or program that causes something to destroy itself automatically. | The missile's auto-destruct failed, and it crashed into the ocean. 导弹的自毁装置失灵，坠入了大海。

The spy activated the auto-destruct sequence.  间谍启动了自毁程序。 | 近义词: self-destruct mechanism, annihilation device | 反义词: safety device, protection mechanism | 词性: 名词


词源： "auto-" (Greek origin, meaning "self") + "destruct" (from Latin "destructus," meaning "destroyed").
