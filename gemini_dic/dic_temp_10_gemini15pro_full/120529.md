miohippus:/ˌmaɪ.oʊˈhɪp.əs/ | n. |中新马|An extinct genus of prehistoric horse.  A three-toed horse from the Oligocene epoch, considered an ancestor of the modern horse.| Miohippus was smaller than Mesohippus and had more complex teeth. 中新马比间马小，牙齿更复杂。|近义词: Mesohippus, Parahippus |反义词: None (extinct animal, no direct opposite) |词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *meíōn* (lesser) + *híppos* (horse)
