noninstitution:/ˌnɒnɪnstɪˈtjuːʃən/ | n. |非机构; 非正规机构；非公共机构|A place or setting that is not a formal institution, such as a hospital, school, or prison.  It often refers to community-based care or services. | Many elderly people prefer noninstitutional care, such as living at home with assistance. 许多老年人更喜欢非机构式护理，例如在家接受帮助。| The noninstitution provided a more relaxed and home-like environment.  这个非机构提供了一个更轻松、更像家的环境。| synonyms: community-based facility, home care, outpatient service | antonyms: institution, hospital, asylum | noun
