hold-down:/ˈhoʊldaʊn/| n.|固定装置，压板；抑制，限制；(非正式)稳定的工作|A device or system that holds something in place, often under pressure; the act of suppressing or restraining something; (informal) a steady job.|The hold-down clamps kept the workpiece secure during machining.  固定夹具在加工过程中确保了工件的安全。|Synonyms: clamp, fastener, restraint, securement, job, position|Antonyms: release, freedom, liberation|noun


hold-down:/ˈhoʊldaʊn/| adj.|抑制的，限制的|(Usually used as a compound adjective)  Relating to or characterized by suppression or restraint.|The hold-down mechanism prevented the engine from vibrating excessively.  抑制机构防止发动机过度振动。|Synonyms: restraining, suppressing, limiting|Antonyms: releasing, freeing, liberating|adjective
