inconceptible:/ˌɪnkənˈsɛptəbl/ /ˌɪnkənˈsɛptɪbl/
adjective
难以理解的；不可思议的；无法想象的
Not capable of being imagined or conceived.
例句：The vastness of space is almost inconceivable to the human mind.  浩瀚的宇宙对人类的思想来说几乎是不可想象的。

例句：It is inconceivable that he would betray his own country. 他会背叛自己的国家，这是不可思议的。

近义词：unimaginable, unthinkable, incomprehensible
反义词：conceivable, imaginable, comprehensible
词性: adjective
