cledgy:/ˈklɛdʒi/
adjective
多岩石的，峭壁的
Characterized by or having many cliffs or ledges.
The cledgy coastline offered stunning views.  多岩石的海岸线提供了令人惊叹的景色。
The climbers carefully navigated the cledgy mountain face.  登山者小心翼翼地在悬崖峭壁的山面上行进。
近义词:rocky, craggy, cliffy
反义词:smooth, flat
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "cledge" (a variant of "cliff" or "ledge") + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Old Norse "kleggi" (cliff).
