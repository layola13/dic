gloaters:/ˈɡloʊtərz/| n.|幸灾乐祸者，沾沾自喜的人|People who derive pleasure from the misfortunes of others.  Those who revel in others' failures or suffering.|The gloaters were quick to point out his mistake. 那些幸灾乐祸的人很快就指出了他的错误。|synonyms: revelers, rejoicers, triumphalists|antonyms: sympathizers, empathizers|noun
gloat:/ɡloʊt/| v.|幸灾乐祸，沾沾自喜|To dwell on one's own success or another's misfortune with smugness or malignant pleasure.|He gloated over his rival's defeat. 他对竞争对手的失败幸灾乐祸。|synonyms: relish, revel, crow|antonyms: commiserate, sympathize|verb
