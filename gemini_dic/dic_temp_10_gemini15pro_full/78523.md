ghillies:/ˈɡɪlɪz/ /ˈɡɪliz/| n. |软底鞋，高地舞鞋，吉利服|A type of soft shoe, often worn for Highland dancing, or camouflage clothing designed to resemble foliage.  Ghillie suits are commonly used by snipers and hunters.|The ghillies allowed the dancer to move gracefully across the stage. 舞蹈演员穿着软底鞋在舞台上优雅地移动。

The sniper wore a ghillie suit to blend in with the surroundings.狙击手穿着吉利服与周围环境融为一体。|近义词：brogues (for shoes), camouflage (for suit)|反义词: boots (for shoes)|词性:noun
