define:/dɪˈfaɪn/| vt.|定义，界定；规定；明确|To state or describe exactly the nature, scope, or meaning of.|例句：The dictionary defines "love" as "an intense feeling of deep affection." 字典将“爱”定义为“一种深沉的强烈情感”。


例句：We need to clearly define our goals. 我们需要明确我们的目标。


近义词：explain, describe, specify, determine|反义词：confuse, obscure|词性：动词
