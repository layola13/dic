bailor:/ˈbeɪlər/| n.|委托人；寄托人|A bailor is a person who delivers goods to another person, known as the bailee, for a specific purpose, such as safekeeping or repair, under a contract of bailment.|The bailor entrusted the bailee with his valuable collection of stamps. 委托人将自己珍贵的邮票收藏托付给了受托人。|近义词：consignor, depositor|反义词:bailee|词性:noun

bailor:/ˈbeɪlər/| n.|出保人|Someone who posts bail for another person.|The bailor paid the bail to secure the release of the defendant. 出保人支付了保释金以确保被告获释。|近义词：surety|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
