bondmaid:/ˈbɒndmeɪd/ | n. | 女奴，婢女 | A female slave or servant, especially one bound to service for a specified period.  A woman bound to service without wages. |  In the historical novel, the protagonist disguised herself as a bondmaid to escape her captors. 在这本历史小说中，主人公把自己伪装成婢女以逃避抓捕。| Synonyms: slave girl, servant girl, maidservant | Antonyms: mistress, owner, employer | noun
