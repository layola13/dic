backwards-and-forwards:/ˈbækwərdz ənd ˈfɔːrwərdz/| adv.| 来回地，反复地|Moving from one place to another and back again repeatedly.|She paced backwards and forwards across the room. 她来回踱步穿过房间。|近义词：back and forth, to and fro|反义词：(没有明确的反义词，可以考虑  in one direction 单一方向)|词性:副词

backwards:/ˈbækwərdz/| adv. adj.| 向后地，相反地；落后的|Towards the back or direction from which something has come; behind. |He took a step backwards. 他向后退了一步。|近义词：reverse, behind|反义词：forwards, ahead|词性:副词，形容词

forwards:/ˈfɔːrwərdz/| adv. adj.| 向前地，提前地|Towards the front or direction in which something is moving. |She leaned forwards to hear better. 她向前倾斜身子以便听得更清楚。|近义词：ahead, onward|反义词：backwards, behind|词性:副词，形容词

词源:  "backwards" 和 "forwards" 分别由 "back" + "-wards" 和 "fore" + "-wards" 构成。"-wards" 是一个古英语后缀，表示“方向”。 "back" 指的是“后面”，"fore" 指的是“前面”。  因此，"backwards and forwards" 字面意思是“向后和向前”。
