nonsubscriber:/ˌnɒnsəbˈskraɪbər/ /ˌnɑːnsəbˈskraɪbər/
n.
非订阅者；非用户
a person who does not subscribe to something, especially a publication or service
The website offers limited access to nonsubscribers. 该网站对非订阅者提供有限的访问权限。
non-member, non-subscriber
subscriber, member
noun
