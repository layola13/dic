overreacher:/ˌoʊvərˈriːtʃər/
|n.|野心勃勃的人；好高骛远的人；自不量力的人|Someone who tries to do more than they are able to or should do, and is often unsuccessful as a result.|He was an overreacher, always aiming for things beyond his grasp. 他是个好高骛远的人，总是追求超出自己能力范围的东西。|The company's downfall was caused by the overreacher in the CEO position. 这家公司的倒闭是由首席执行官的好高骛远造成的。|Synonyms: ambitious person (often with negative connotations), aspirant (sometimes), striver (sometimes), high-flyer (sometimes) |Antonyms: underachiever, pragmatist, realist|noun
