oversweetened:/ˌōvərˈswiːtnd/| adj.|过甜的|Containing too much sugar; excessively sweet.|The cake was oversweetened and left a sugary aftertaste.  蛋糕太甜了，留下了甜腻的回味。|The lemonade was oversweetened, I could barely drink it.  柠檬水太甜了，我几乎喝不下去。|近义词：cloying, sugary, saccharine|反义词：tart, sour, bland|词性:形容词
Etymology: over- + sweetened (past participle of sweeten). "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," "too much." "Sweeten" means "to make or become sweet." Thus, "oversweetened" literally means "made too sweet."
