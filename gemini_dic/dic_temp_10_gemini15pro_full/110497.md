limar:/ˈliːmɑːr/ | v. | 磨平;锉平；修光 | To file or smooth something, especially metal or wood. |  He limared the rough edges of the metal. 他锉平了金属的粗糙边缘。

|  The carpenter limared the wood to make it smooth. 木匠把木头锉平，使它光滑。 | 近义词: file, smooth, polish, buff | 反义词: roughen | verb
