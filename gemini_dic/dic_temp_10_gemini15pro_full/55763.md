diversification:/dɪˌvɜːrsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ |n.| 多样化，多元化 | The process of making or becoming more diverse; increasing the variety of something. |  Many farmers are forced to diversify their activities. 许多农民被迫使他们的经营活动多样化。

Farmers should diversify to survive the slump in commodity prices. 农民们应该采取多样化经营来渡过商品价格暴跌的难关。| synonyms: variation, diversification, variety, assortment | antonyms: uniformity, standardization, homogeneity | noun
