chemisette:/ˌʃɛmɪˈzɛt/ | n. | 女式衬衫前胸装饰 | A decorative front piece for a woman's blouse or dress, often of lace or embroidered fabric. | She wore a beautiful chemisette with her low-cut gown. 她穿着一件低胸礼服，搭配一件漂亮的衬衫前胸装饰。| Example: The antique chemisette was adorned with delicate lace. 这件古董衬衫前胸装饰点缀着精致的蕾丝。|Synonyms: dickey, modesty piece, fill-in | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but could consider a plunging neckline) | noun
