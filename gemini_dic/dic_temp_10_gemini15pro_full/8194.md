anti-dotal:/ˌæntiˈdəʊtl/ | adj. | 解毒的；抵消的；对抗的 | acting against the effects of a poison, disease, or other harmful thing; counteracting or neutralizing something unpleasant or undesirable |  The medicine acted as an antidote to the poison. 这种药起到了对抗毒药的作用。 | Synonyms: counteracting, neutralizing, remedial, corrective | Antonyms: poisonous, toxic, harmful | adjective

anti-dotal:/ˌæntiˈdəʊtl/ | n. | 解毒剂 | a medicine taken or given to counteract a particular poison |  The doctor administered an antidote for the snake bite. 医生给蛇咬伤者服用了解毒剂。 | Synonyms: remedy, cure, corrective | Antonyms: poison, toxin, venom | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *antidotal*, from Late Latin *antidotālis*, from Latin *antidotum* (“antidote”), from Ancient Greek *ἀντίδοτον* (*antídoto*), from *ἀντί* (*antí*, “against”) + *δίδωμι* (*dídōmi*, “I give”).
