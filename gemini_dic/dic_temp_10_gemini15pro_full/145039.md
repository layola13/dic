overaggravate:/ˌoʊvərˈæɡrəveɪt/ | v. | 过分夸大;过分加重；使恶化 | To aggravate excessively or unduly; to exaggerate the seriousness of. | He tends to overaggravate his injuries to get sympathy. 他总是夸大自己的伤势以博取同情。|  He overaggravated the situation by shouting at the customer. 他对着顾客大喊大叫，使情况更加恶化。| 近义词: exaggerate, overstate, worsen, exacerbate | 反义词: understate, minimize, alleviate, improve | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + aggravate (from Latin aggravatus, past participle of aggravare, "to make heavier," from ad- "to" + gravare "to burden," from gravis "heavy").  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning of "aggravate," implying excessive or unnecessary aggravation.
