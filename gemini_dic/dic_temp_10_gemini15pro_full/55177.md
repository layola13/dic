dissension:/dɪˈsɛnʃən/
n.|不和，争吵，意见分歧|Disagreement that leads to discord.  A state of disagreement and discord within a group.|The dissension within the party threatened to split it apart.  党内的不和有可能导致分裂。|
近义词:discord, disagreement, strife, contention, conflict, dispute
反义词:agreement, harmony, concord, unity
词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dissēnsiō* ("disagreement"), from *dissēnsus*, past participle of *dissentire* ("to disagree"), from *dis-* ("apart, away") + *sentire* ("to feel, think").
