nonrespectableness:/ˌnɑːnriˈspɛktəbəlnəs/
n.|不体面，不值得尊敬|The state or quality of being not respectable; lack of respectability.|His nonrespectableness made him an outcast in society. 他的不体面让他成为了社会的弃儿。|近义词：disreputability, indignity, shamefulness|反义词：respectability, honorableness|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From non- (not) + respectableness (the quality of being respectable). Respectability comes from respectable + -ness. Respectable comes from respect + -able. Respect ultimately comes from the Latin word respectus, meaning "regard, consideration."
