deprived:/dɪˈpraɪvd/| adj.|被剥夺的，贫困的|lacking the necessities of life; suffering from extreme poverty.|例句：The deprived children in the slum had little access to education. 贫民窟里贫困的孩子几乎没有受教育的机会。

例句：Many deprived communities lack basic healthcare facilities. 许多贫困社区缺乏基本的医疗保健设施。|近义词：impoverished, disadvantaged, underprivileged, needy|反义词：privileged, affluent, wealthy|词性:形容词

词源: From the past participle of *deprive*, from Old French *depriver*, from Latin *dēprīvāre* "to bereave, rob, dispossess," from *dē* "from, away from" + *prīvāre* "to deprive," from *prīvus* "private, one's own, individual."
