desubsidized:/ˌdiːˈsʌbsɪdaɪzd/
adjective
去补贴的，取消补贴的
Having subsidies removed or reduced.
The desubsidized price of gasoline is much higher.  去补贴后的汽油价格要高得多。
Farmers are struggling to adapt to a desubsidized market.  农民们正努力适应取消补贴的市场。
近义词: unsubsidized, unsupported
反义词: subsidized
词性: adjective
