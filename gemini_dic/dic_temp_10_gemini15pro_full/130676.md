nonaesthetic:/ˌnɒn iːsˈθɛtɪk/ | adj. | 非美学的，不美的 |  Lacking aesthetic qualities; not pleasing to the senses or artistic sensibilities.  | The building's nonaesthetic design was criticized by many. 这栋建筑缺乏美感的设计受到了很多人的批评。 | The nonaesthetic plastic chairs were functional but ugly.  这些毫无美感的塑料椅子虽然实用，但很丑陋。 | 近义词: unaesthetic, inartistic, unattractive, ugly | 反义词: aesthetic, artistic, beautiful, attractive | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
non- + aesthetic

non-:  Latin prefix meaning "not," "opposite of," "lacking."
aesthetic: From Greek *aisthētikos*, "of sense perception," from aisthanesthai "to perceive."
