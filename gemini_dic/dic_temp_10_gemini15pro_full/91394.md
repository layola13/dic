hydrotropic:/ˌhaɪdrəˈtrɒpɪk/ | adj. | 亲水的，水溶增效的 |  Of or relating to a compound that increases the solubility in water of another compound. |  Hydrotropic agents are used in detergent formulations to enhance the solubility of hydrophobic ingredients. 水溶增效剂用于洗涤剂配方中，以增强疏水成分的溶解度。 |  Synonyms: solubilizing, cosolvent | Antonyms: hydrophobic, lipophilic | adjective

hydrotrope:/ˈhaɪdrəˌtrəʊp/ | n. | 水溶增效剂 | A compound that increases the solubility in water of another compound. | Sodium xylene sulfonate is a common hydrotrope.  二甲苯磺酸钠是一种常见的水溶增效剂。| Synonyms: solubilizer, cosolvent | Antonyms: - | noun
