condemned: /kənˈdemd/ | adj. | 被谴责的；被判刑的；被宣告不适用的 | Officially declared unfit for use.  Declared guilty of a criminal offense and sentenced to punishment, especially death. |  The building was condemned after the fire. 这栋楼在火灾后被宣布不适用了。 |  The condemned man awaited his execution.  这名死囚等待着他的处决。 | synonyms: doomed, convicted, damned, cursed | antonyms: blessed, approved, praised | adjective


condemn: /kənˈdem/ | verb | 谴责；判刑；宣告…不适用 | Express complete disapproval of; censure.  Sentence (someone) to a particular punishment, especially death.  Officially declare (something, especially a building) unfit for use. |  The government condemned the terrorist attack. 政府谴责了这次恐怖袭击。 | The court condemned him to life imprisonment. 法院判处他终身监禁。 | synonyms: denounce, criticize, sentence, doom | antonyms: praise, commend, approve, exonerate | verb
