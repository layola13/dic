as far as:/əz ˈfɑːr əz/ |conj. prep.| 直到；远到|Used to talk about distance or a point in space. | We walked as far as the edge of the forest. 我们一直走到森林边缘。| Synonyms: up to, until, to | Antonyms: from | conjunction, preposition
