optioning:/ˈɒpʃənɪŋ/
n.
选择权的授予;获得选择权
The act of securing or granting an option, especially in the entertainment industry.
The studio is optioning the rights to her novel.  这家制片厂正在购买她的小说的版权。
近义词: securing, acquiring, licensing
反义词: releasing, relinquishing
词性: 名词 (gerund/动名词)

Etymology:
From the verb "option" + "-ing" (present participle/gerund suffix).  "Option" comes from Old French "opcion" and ultimately from Latin "optio" meaning choice.
