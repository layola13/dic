cross-eyed:/ˈkrɔːsˌaɪd/ (US), /ˈkrɒsˌaɪd/ (UK)| adj.|看不清的；困惑的|(informal) Unable to see or understand something clearly.|I'm completely cross-eyed trying to read this small print. 我看着这些小字，眼睛都花了。|近义词:cockeyed, squint-eyed|反义词:straight-eyed|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From cross + eyed.  The "cross" refers to the crossing of the lines of sight.
