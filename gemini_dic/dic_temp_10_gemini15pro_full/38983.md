confirm:/kənˈfɜːrm/ (US) /kənˈfɜːm/ (UK)| v.|确认，证实|to establish the truth or validity of something; to make something definite or certain|例句：Please confirm your attendance by Friday. 请在周五前确认您的出席。|近义词：verify, corroborate, validate, substantiate|反义词：deny, refute, contradict|词性:动词

confirm:/kənˈfɜːrm/ (US) /kənˈfɜːm/ (UK)| v.|加强，巩固|to strengthen or make more certain|例句：The latest evidence confirms our suspicions. 最新证据证实了我们的怀疑。|近义词：reinforce, strengthen, solidify|反义词：weaken, undermine|词性:动词
