edituate: /ˈedɪtʃueɪt/
verb
教化，培养，陶冶
To educate or train; to develop morally or intellectually.
The program aims to edituate young people about the dangers of drug use.  这个项目旨在教育年轻人关于吸毒的危害。
He was edituated in the classics at a prestigious university. 他在一所著名的大学接受了古典文学教育。
近义词: educate, cultivate, nurture, train, instruct
反义词: neglect, ignore, corrupt
词性: verb

词源: From Latin *editus, past participle of *educare "to bring up, rear, educate," from ex- "out" + ducere "to lead."
