exactor:/ɪɡˈzæktər/| n.|勒索者，强求者，苛求者|A person who demands something, especially payment, without justification or mercy.  Someone who is very strict or demanding.|He acted as an exactor, demanding payment for services he hadn't rendered.|近义词：extortionist, oppressor, tyrant|反义词: benefactor, philanthropist|noun
