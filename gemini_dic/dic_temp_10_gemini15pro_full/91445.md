hygrology:/haɪˈɡrɒlədʒi/ | n. |湿度学；潮湿学| The study of humidity and its effects. |Hygrology is important in meteorology and agriculture. 湿度学在气象学和农业中很重要。| 近义词: hygrometry | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | noun


hygroscopic:/ˌhaɪɡrəˈskɒpɪk/ | adj. |吸湿的| Absorbing or attracting moisture from the air. | Hygroscopic materials are used in desiccators. 吸湿性材料用于干燥器中。| 近义词: absorbent, moisture-attracting | 反义词: waterproof, water-repellent | adjective
