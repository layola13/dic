inspissate:/ɪnˈspɪseɪt/ /ɪnˈspɪsˌeɪt/| v. |使浓稠，使变厚，使加浓|To make thicker or more dense, especially by evaporation or absorption of liquid. |The chef inspissated the sauce by simmering it for hours.  厨师将酱汁慢炖数小时以使其浓稠。

The long, slow simmer inspissated the juices into a rich gravy. 长时间慢炖使汁液浓缩成浓稠的肉汁。|近义词：thicken, condense, concentrate|反义词：thin, dilute, rarefy|词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *inspissatus, past participle of inspissare (“to thicken”), from in- (“in, into”) + spissus (“thick”).
