definitive:/dɪˈfɪnətɪv/| adj.|最终的，确定的，权威性的，决定性的|Conclusive, final, ultimate; serving as a perfect example.|例句：The definitive biography of the artist has just been published.  这位艺术家的权威传记刚刚出版。|例句：This is the definitive guide to Italian cooking.  这是意大利烹饪的权威指南。|近义词：conclusive, decisive, final, ultimate, authoritative|反义词：inconclusive, provisional, tentative|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *definitivus, from Latin dēfīnītus (past participle of dēfīnīre “to limit, define”), from dē (“from, down from, away from”) + fīnis (“boundary, limit, end”).
