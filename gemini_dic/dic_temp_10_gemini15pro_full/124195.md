mosel:/ˈmoːzəl/| n.|摩泽尔河；摩泽尔葡萄酒|A river that rises in France, flows through Luxembourg and Germany, and joins the Rhine at Koblenz.  Also refers to wine made in the Mosel valley. |The Mosel river is famous for its steep vineyards. 摩泽尔河以其陡峭的葡萄园而闻名。|The Mosel produces some of Germany's finest Riesling wines. 摩泽尔出产一些德国最好的雷司令葡萄酒。|Synonyms: Moselle (alternative spelling)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
