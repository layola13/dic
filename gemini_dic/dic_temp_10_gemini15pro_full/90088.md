hostilities:/hɑːˈstɪlətiz/
n.
敌对行动，战争行为
Acts of warfare; fighting.
Hostilities broke out between the two countries. 两国之间爆发了敌对行动。
Hostilities ceased after a peace treaty was signed.  和平条约签署后，敌对行动停止了。
synonyms: fighting, warfare, combat, conflict, aggression, violence
antonyms: peace, truce, armistice, ceasefire
noun
