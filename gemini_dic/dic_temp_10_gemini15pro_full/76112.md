furthering:/ˈfɜːrðərɪŋ/
verb
推进，促进
the action of promoting or advancing something.
例句：He dedicated his life to furthering the cause of education. 他毕生致力于推进教育事业。

例句：She is furthering her studies by taking online courses. 她通过参加在线课程来继续深造。

近义词：advancing, promoting, progressing, developing, enhancing
反义词：hindering, obstructing, impeding, delaying
词性: Verb (present participle of "further")

further:/ˈfɜːrðər/
adjective
更远的；进一步的
more distant or advanced.
例句：For further information, please contact our customer service team.  欲了解更多信息，请联系我们的客户服务团队。

例句：We need to take further steps to address this issue. 我们需要采取进一步措施来解决这个问题。

近义词：additional, more, supplementary, extra
反义词：less, lesser, nearer, closer
词性: Adjective

further:/ˈfɜːrðər/
adverb
进一步地；而且
to a greater distance or extent; moreover.
例句：We will discuss this matter further tomorrow. 我们明天将进一步讨论此事。

例句：Further, I would like to add that we are fully committed to this project. 此外，我想补充一点，我们完全致力于这个项目。

近义词：moreover, additionally, furthermore, besides
反义词：less, lesser
词性: Adverb

further:/ˈfɜːrðər/
verb
促进，推进
to help the progress or development of something.
例句：The company is looking for ways to further its growth in the Asian market.  该公司正在寻找方法来进一步促进其在亚洲市场的增长。

例句：His actions furthered the conflict between the two groups. 他的行为加剧了两组之间的冲突。

近义词：promote, advance, progress, develop, enhance
反义词：hinder, obstruct, impede, delay
词性: Verb
