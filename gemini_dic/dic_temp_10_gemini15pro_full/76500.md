galaxy:/ˈɡæləksi/| n.|星系，银河系|A system of millions or billions of stars, together with gas and dust, held together by gravitational attraction. |例句：Our solar system is located in the Milky Way galaxy. 我们的太阳系位于银河系。

例句：The Andromeda galaxy is the closest major galaxy to our own. 仙女座星系是我们最近的主要星系。

|近义词：cosmos, universe|反义词: (No direct antonym exists for galaxy)|noun
