nonpolice:/ˌnɑːn.pəˈliːs/
adjective
非警方的，非警察的
Relating to or involving people or organizations that are not part of the police force.
The nonpolice security guards apprehended the shoplifter.  非警察保安逮捕了商店扒手。
The city council hired a nonpolice agency to investigate the incident. 市议会聘请了一个非警察机构来调查这起事件。
Synonyms: civilian, non-law enforcement
Antonyms: police, law enforcement
词性: adjective
