miladies:/ˌmɪˈleɪdiz/ /ˌmaɪˈleɪdiz/ | n. | 女士们；小姐们 | A plural form of milady, used as a polite or humorous form of address to a group of women.  It's a somewhat archaic and affected term, less common in modern usage. | Example: "Miladies, please allow me to escort you to your seats." 女士们，请允许我护送你们到座位上。| Example: "Well, miladies, shall we proceed with the plan?" 好吧，女士们，我们可以继续执行计划了吗？ | 近义词：ladies, women, females | 反义词: gentlemen, men, males | noun
