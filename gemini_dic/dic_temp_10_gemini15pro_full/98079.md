inquiring:/ɪnˈkwaɪərɪŋ/| adj.|好奇的，探究的|Given to inquiry; questioning. Showing curiosity.  Characterized by investigation; inquiring into facts or principles.|例句：She gave him an inquiring look. 她好奇地看了他一眼。

例句：He has an inquiring mind and is always eager to learn new things. 他有颗求知若渴的心，总是渴望学习新事物。|近义词：curious, inquisitive, questioning, probing|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, uninterested|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *enquiring, present participle of enqueren, from Old French enquerer (“to inquire”), from Latin inquirere, present active infinitive of inquīrō (“I seek into, examine, investigate”), from in- (“in, into”) + quaerō (“I seek, ask”).
