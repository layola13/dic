offend: /əˈfɛnd/| v.| 冒犯，得罪，违反 | Cause to feel resentment or displeasure.| His words offended many people. 他的话冒犯了很多人。| synonyms: insult, affront, displease, annoy | antonyms: please, delight, flatter | verb
