commander:/kəˈmændər/ (US), /kəˈmɑːndər/ (UK)| n. |指挥官，司令官|A person who is in charge of a group of people, especially in the military.|The commander ordered his troops to advance. 指挥官命令他的部队前进。|近义词：leader, chief, officer, captain|反义词:subordinate, follower|noun

commander:/kəˈmændər/ (US), /kəˈmɑːndər/ (UK)| v. |指挥，统帅|To be in charge of a group of people, especially in the military.|He commanded the 7th Fleet. 他指挥第七舰队。|近义词：lead, direct, control, manage|反义词:obey, follow|verb
