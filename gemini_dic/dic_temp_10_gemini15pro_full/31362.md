charade:/ʃəˈreɪd/ | n. | 装模作样；做戏；字谜游戏 | An elaborate and obvious pretense or deception.  A game in which players act out words or phrases. |  例句：Their concern was a mere charade. 他们的关心只不过是装模作样。

例句：We played charades after dinner. 晚饭后我们玩了字谜游戏。| 近义词：pretense, act, farce, pantomime, travesty | 反义词：sincerity, genuineness | 词性：名词
