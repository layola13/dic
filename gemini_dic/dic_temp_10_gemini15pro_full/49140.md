deixis:/ˈdaɪksɪs/| n.|指示语|Words or expressions whose meaning depends on the context in which they are used, such as "here," "now," "I," "you," "that."|例句：The word "here" in the sentence "I am here" is an example of deixis, as its meaning depends on the speaker's location.|近义词：indexicality, reference, pointing|反义词:None|词性:noun

deixis:/ˈdaɪksɪs/| n.|指示|The function or use of deictic expressions.|例句：Deixis plays a crucial role in understanding how language relates to the context of its use.|近义词：indexical function, referential function|反义词:None|词性:noun

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I live here. = 我住在这里。
She works there. = 她在那里工作。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am standing here now. = 我现在正站在这里。
He is working there at the moment. = 他现在正在那里工作。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have been here before. = 我以前来过这里。
She has worked there for five years. = 她在那里工作了五年。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been living here for two years. = 我已经在这里住了两年了。
He has been working there since last year. = 他自从去年开始就在那里工作。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I was there yesterday. = 我昨天在那里。
She lived here last year. = 她去年住在这里。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was standing there when it happened. = 事情发生时我正站在那里。
She was working there at that time. = 她那时正在那里工作。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had already been there before you arrived. = 在你到达之前我已经去过那里了。
She had lived here for ten years before she moved. = 在她搬走之前，她在这里住了十年。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been living there for two years before I moved. = 在我搬家之前，我已经在那里住了两年了。
She had been working there for five years before she retired. = 在她退休之前，她已经在那里工作了五年了。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will be there tomorrow. = 我明天会在那里。
She will live here next year. = 她明年会住在这里。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):

PHRASE->
Pointing to a location:  This here is where it happened. = 事情就发生在这里。
Referring to the present moment: Now is the time to act. = 现在是行动的时候了。
Identifying oneself:  I am the one who did it. = 是我做的。
Addressing someone directly: You should go there now. = 你现在应该去那里。
Referring to a previously mentioned item: That is the problem we need to solve. = 那就是我们需要解决的问题。

注意事项:
词源：From Ancient Greek  δεῖξις (deîxis, “pointing, showing, indication”).
