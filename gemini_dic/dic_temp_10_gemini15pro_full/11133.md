archebiosis:/ˌɑːrkɪbaɪˈoʊsɪs/
n.
自然发生
The now discredited theory of spontaneous generation of living organisms from nonliving matter.
Archebiosis was a widely held belief before the experiments of Pasteur. 巴斯德的实验之前，自然发生说是被广泛接受的。
Life arising from inorganic matter by archebiosis was once a common belief.  生命由无机物通过自然发生而产生曾是一个普遍的信念。
