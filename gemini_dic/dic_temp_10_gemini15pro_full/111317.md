livable:/ˈlɪvəbl/| adj.|适宜居住的，宜居的|Suitable for living in; comfortable and pleasant to live in.|This city is very livable due to its low crime rate and excellent public transportation.  这个城市由于低犯罪率和优秀的公共交通而非常宜居。| The apartment is small but livable. 这间公寓很小，但可以住人。|近义词：habitable, comfortable, agreeable|反义词：uninhabitable, unpleasant, unbearable|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From live (v.) + -able.  Ultimately from Old English libban "to live."
