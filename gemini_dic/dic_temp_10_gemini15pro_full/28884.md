carpooling:/ˈkɑːrˌpuːlɪŋ/| n.|拼车，合伙用车|The practice of sharing a car with others, especially for commuting to work or school.|We organized a carpooling system to reduce traffic congestion. 我们组织了一个拼车系统以减少交通拥堵。|例句：Carpooling can save money and reduce pollution. 拼车可以省钱并减少污染。|近义词：ride-sharing, lift-sharing|反义词：driving alone|noun
