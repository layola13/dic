board:/bɔːrd/ (UK) /bɔːrd/ (US)| n. v.|板; 董事会; 甲板; 伙食; 上（船、飞机等）|A flat piece of wood or other stiff material used for various purposes, a group of people who manage a company or organization, the side of a ship, meals provided regularly, to get onto a ship, train, or aircraft.|例句：The notice was pinned to the notice board. 通知被钉在布告栏上。|近义词：plank, panel, committee, council, deck, food|反义词:disembark (v.)|词性:noun, verb

board:/bɔːrd/ (UK) /bɔːrd/ (US)| v.|上（船、飞机等）; 寄宿; 提供膳宿|To get onto a ship, train, or aircraft, to provide meals and lodging.|例句：We boarded the plane at 9 am. 我们上午九点登机。|近义词：embark, lodge, accommodate|反义词:disembark|词性:verb
