carnivoral:/kɑːrˈnɪvərəl/ | adj. | 肉食性的；食肉的 | Relating to or characteristic of a carnivore; meat-eating. | Lions are carnivoral animals. 狮子是肉食性动物。|  Tigers are carnivoral hunters, preying on large mammals. 老虎是食肉的猎手，捕食大型哺乳动物。 | 近义词：carnivorous, predacious | 反义词：herbivorous | 词性：形容词

词源: From Latin *carnivoralis*, from *carnivŏrus*  ("flesh-eating") (from *caro*, *carn-* ("flesh") + *vorāre* ("to devour")) + *-ālis*.
