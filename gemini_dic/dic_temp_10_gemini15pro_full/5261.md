allogeneic:/ˌælədʒɪˈniːɪk/ | adj. |异基因的，同种异体的|  Genetically dissimilar although belonging to the same species.  Relating to or involving tissues or cells that are genetically dissimilar although from individuals of the same species. |  An allogeneic bone marrow transplant. 同种异基因骨髓移植。 | Synonyms: allogenic, homoplastic | Antonyms: autologous, syngeneic | adjective

allogeneous:/ˌæləˈdʒiːniəs/ | adj. |异基因的，同种异体的| Originating within another individual of the same species.  Genetically different, but obtained from the same species.  Used especially of grafted or transfused tissue or organs. | The patient received an allogeneous blood transfusion. 病人接受了同种异基因输血。| Synonyms: allogeneic, homoplastic | Antonyms: autologous, syngeneic | adjective
