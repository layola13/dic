aligns:/əˈlaɪnz/ | v. | 使成一线，对齐，排列；与…结盟 |  arranges something in a straight line or brings something into a correct position relative to something else; joins with someone or something to achieve a particular aim |  She carefully aligned the edges of the paper before stapling them. 她仔细地对齐了纸的边缘，然后才装订。 The company has aligned itself with other organizations working for peace.  该公司已与其他致力于和平的组织结盟。 | 近义词: adjust, arrange, straighten, line up, ally | 反义词: misalign, disarrange | 词性: 动词

aligns:/əˈlaɪnz/ | n. | (罕用) 对准，队列 | alignment, arrangement | The aligns of the troops was impeccable. 部队的队列完美无瑕。 | 近义词: alignment, line, row | 反义词: disarray | 词性: 名词 (罕用)
