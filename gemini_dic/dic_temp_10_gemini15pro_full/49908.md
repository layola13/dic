demystify:/ˌdiːˈmɪstɪfaɪ/ /ˌdiːˈmɪstəˌfaɪ/| vt.|揭秘，阐明，使不再神秘|to make something easier to understand by explaining it in a clear and simple way|The book attempts to demystify the world of finance.  这本书试图揭开金融世界的神秘面纱。

The teacher demystified the complex scientific concept for her students. 老师为学生们阐明了这个复杂的科学概念。|近义词：clarify, explain, simplify, elucidate|反义词：obfuscate, confuse, complicate|词性: 动词
