outachieved:/ˌaʊtəˈtʃiːvd/
verb
超越；胜过
to achieve more or perform better than someone
例句：She consistently outachieved her colleagues in sales. 她一直在销售方面胜过她的同事。

例句：The small company outachieved its larger competitors. 这家小公司胜过了它更大的竞争对手。
近义词：outperform, surpass, excel, exceed
反义词：underperform, lag behind
词性：v.
