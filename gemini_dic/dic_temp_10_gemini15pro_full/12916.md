assuring:/əˈʃʊərɪŋ/
adjective
令人安心的，肯定的
making someone feel certain about something
Her assuring words calmed my fears. 她令人安心的话平息了我的恐惧。
近义词:reassuring, comforting, encouraging
反义词:alarming, worrying, disconcerting
词性:adjective

词源: From the verb "assure," which comes from Old French "assurer" (to make sure, guarantee), ultimately from Latin "ad" (to) + "securus" (secure).
