impishnesses:/ˈɪmpɪʃnɪsɪz/| n. |顽皮，淘气，恶作剧|The quality of being impish; mischievous playfulness.  It refers to a series of playful, mischievous acts or behaviors, often harmless but sometimes annoying. |His impishnesses were tolerated because he was so young. 他的顽皮行为因为他还小而被容忍了。|近义词：mischief, pranks, naughtiness, playfulness|反义词：seriousness, obedience, good behavior|词性:noun

Etymology: From impish + -ness.  "Impish" itself comes from "imp," originally meaning a small demon or mischievous sprite.
