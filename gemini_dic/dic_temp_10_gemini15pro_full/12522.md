asphaltum:/æsˈfɔːltəm/ /æsˈfɔːltəm/
n.
沥青，地沥青
A dark, bituminous substance, found in natural deposits and also obtained as a residue in petroleum refining; mineral pitch.
Asphaltum was used to waterproof the roof. 沥青被用来给屋顶做防水。

近义词:bitumen, asphalt, pitch
反义词:(无常用反义词)
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *asphaltum, from Ancient Greek ἄσφαλτος (ásphaltos, “asphalt, bitumen”).
