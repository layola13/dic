parlous: /ˈpɑːrləs/ | adj. | 危险的，不确定的 |  Full of danger or uncertainty; precarious.  Derived from the Anglo-Norman *perillus, from Old French perilleus "dangerous," from peril "danger." | The company is in a parlous financial state. 公司财务状况岌岌可危。| synonyms: dangerous, precarious, perilous, risky, hazardous | antonyms: safe, secure | adjective

parlous: /ˈpɑːrləs/ | adj. | 危急的，严重的 |  Grave, serious.  | The parlous state of the economy is a major concern. 经济的危急状况是一个主要问题。| synonyms: critical, dire, grave, serious | antonyms: stable, secure | adjective
