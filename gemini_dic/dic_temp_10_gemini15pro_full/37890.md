complaint: /kəmˈpleɪnt/ (US), /kəmˈpleɪnt/ (UK) | n. | 抱怨，投诉，控告；疾病 | An expression of dissatisfaction or annoyance about something.  A formal accusation against somebody. An illness or medical condition. |  She lodged a formal complaint against her boss. 她向老板提出了正式投诉。 | synonyms: grievance, criticism, objection | antonyms: praise, compliment | noun

complaint: /kəmˈpleɪnt/ (US), /kəmˈpleɪnt/ (UK) | adj. | 抱怨的，爱抱怨的 (less common usage) |  Used to describe someone who complains often. |  He's a complaint old man. 他是一个爱抱怨的老人。 | synonyms: querulous,  grumbling | antonyms: content, satisfied | adjective
