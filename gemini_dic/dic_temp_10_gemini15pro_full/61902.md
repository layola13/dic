empty-of:/ˈempti əv/| adj.|空的，缺乏的|Lacking or devoid of something; containing nothing.|The room was empty of furniture. 房间里没有家具。|The cupboard was empty of food. 碗柜里没有食物。|近义词：devoid of, lacking, bereft of|反义词:full of, replete with|词性:形容词

词源:
"empty" comes from Old English "æmtig," meaning "at leisure, unoccupied," related to "æmetta," meaning "leisure."  The "-of" element indicates the thing that is lacking.
