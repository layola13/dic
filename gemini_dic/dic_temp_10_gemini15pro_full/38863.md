confederation:/kənˌfedəˈreɪʃn/
n.
联盟，邦联
a group of states, organizations, or people united for a common purpose
The United States was once a confederation of thirteen colonies. 美国曾经是由十三个殖民地组成的邦联。

confederation:/kənˌfedəˈreɪʃn/
n.
联合，同盟
the act of uniting in a league or confederacy
The confederation of the tribes made them stronger. 部落联盟使他们更强大。


Etymology->
From Late Latin  *confœderātiō, from confœderātus (“united by treaty”), past participle of confœderāre, from con- (“together”) + fœderāre (“to make a treaty”), from fœdus (“treaty, league”). Doublet of federation.

Synonyms->
alliance, league, union, federation, coalition

Antonyms->
division, separation, disunity
