draughty: /ˈdrɑːfti/ (UK) /ˈdræfti/ (US)| adj.|有穿堂风的；通风的；容易进冷风的|Exposed to currents of air; letting in cold air.|The old house was draughty and cold in the winter. 这座老房子冬天有穿堂风，很冷。|The windows don't fit very well, so the room is quite draughty.  窗户不太合缝，所以房间很容易进风。|近义词: airy, breezy, ventilated|反义词: airtight, sealed, stuffy|词性:形容词

词源: From draft + -y.  "Draft" in this context refers to a current of air.
