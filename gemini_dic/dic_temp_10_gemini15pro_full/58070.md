drongo:/ˈdrɒŋɡəʊ/ (UK), /ˈdrɑːŋɡoʊ/ (US)| n. |伯劳，蠢货|A shrike-like bird found in Africa, Asia, and Australia. Also used informally to mean a stupid or foolish person.|The drongo swooped down and caught the insect. 伯劳俯冲下来抓住了昆虫。| He's such a drongo, he locked his keys in the car. 他真是个蠢货，把钥匙锁在车里了。|近义词：fool, idiot, nincompoop |反义词: genius, clever person |词性:noun

drongos:/ˈdrɒŋɡəʊz/ (UK), /ˈdrɑːŋɡoʊz/ (US)| n. |伯劳（复数），蠢货（复数）|Plural of drongo.|The drongos were chattering noisily in the trees. 伯劳在树上吵闹地叫着。|Those drongos lost the game because they didn't follow the coach's instructions. 那些蠢货输掉了比赛，因为他们没有听从教练的指示。|近义词：fools, idiots, nincompoops |反义词: geniuses, clever people |词性:noun
