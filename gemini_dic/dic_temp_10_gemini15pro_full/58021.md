drm:/ˌdiːɑːrˈem/ /ˈdiːɑːrˈem/ | n. |数字版权管理 (Digital Rights Management)| Digital Rights Management (DRM) schemes are various access control technologies that are used to restrict the usage of copyrighted works.  These technologies try to control the use, modification, and distribution of copyrighted works (such as software, music, movies and ebooks).|  The ebook is protected by DRM, so I can't copy it to another device. 这本电子书受DRM保护，所以我无法将其复制到其他设备。|  The music streaming service uses DRM to prevent users from downloading the songs.  这个音乐流媒体服务使用DRM来防止用户下载歌曲。|synonyms:  copy protection, access control | antonyms:  open access, free distribution | noun
