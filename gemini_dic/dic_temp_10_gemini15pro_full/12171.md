aryans:/ˈɛəriənz/ | n. | 雅利安人 |  A term originally used to denote the speakers of Indo-Iranian languages, but later appropriated and distorted by Nazi ideology to refer to a supposed "master race" of non-Jewish Caucasians. |  The Nazis falsely claimed that Aryans were a superior race. 纳粹错误地声称雅利安人是优等民族。|  synonyms: Indo-Iranians (original meaning), Indo-Europeans (linguistic connection)  | antonyms:  None (in its original linguistic sense);  Non-Aryans (in its distorted Nazi usage - a term promoting racism and should be avoided) | noun
