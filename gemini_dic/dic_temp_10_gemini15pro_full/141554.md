opposer:/əˈpəʊzər/ (UK), /əˈpoʊzər/ (US)| n.|反对者，敌对者|Someone who opposes something; an antagonist.|He was a staunch opposer of the new policy. 他是这项新政策的坚决反对者。|Example: The opposer of the plan voiced his concerns at the meeting.  该计划的反对者在会议上表达了他的担忧。

Example: She became a vocal opposer of the development project. 她成了这个开发项目的强烈反对者。|近义词：opponent, antagonist, adversary, foe, rival, competitor|反义词：supporter, proponent, advocate, ally|词性: 名词
