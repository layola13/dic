palmed:/pɑːmd/ | adj. | 手掌状的；藏在手掌里的 | Having the size or shape of the palm; concealed in the hand. | He palmed the coin and made it disappear. 他把硬币藏在手掌里，让它消失了。 | 近义词：concealed, hidden | 反义词：revealed, exposed | 词性:形容词

palmed:/pɑːmd/ | v. |  把…藏于掌中；用掌击 | To conceal something in the hand; to strike with the palm. | The magician palmed the card during the trick. 魔术师在表演魔术时把牌藏在了手掌里。 | 近义词：conceal, hide | 反义词：reveal, expose | 词性:动词

词源: From palm + -ed.  Ultimately from Latin *palma* "palm of the hand".
