groovy: /ˈɡruːvi/ | adj. | 时髦的，很棒的，绝妙的 | Fashionable, excellent, wonderful; conveying a pleasant sense of excitement or well-being. |  例句：That's a groovy song! 那首歌真棒！| 近义词：cool, hip, awesome, excellent, fantastic | 反义词：square, uncool, boring, terrible | 词性: 形容词


词源: 1950s slang, possibly from jazz slang, perhaps from "in the groove" (playing well, stylishly).
