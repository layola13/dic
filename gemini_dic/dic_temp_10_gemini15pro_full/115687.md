marginal:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪnl/ /ˈmɑːrɡɪnl/| adj.|边缘的，边际的，微小的|Relating to or situated at the edge or margin of something;  also, barely sufficient or within a lower limit. |The farmer grows crops on the marginal land.  这位农民在边缘土地上种植庄稼。

The increase in profit was marginal. 利润的增长微乎其微。|近义词：peripheral, borderline, insignificant, minimal|反义词：central, core, significant, substantial|词性:形容词

marginal:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪnl/ /ˈmɑːrɡɪnl/| n.|边缘，边际，页边空白|An amount by which something is won or falls short.|The company operates on very narrow margins. 这家公司的运营利润非常微薄。

They won by a narrow margin. 他们以微弱的优势获胜。|近义词：edge, border, rim|反义词：center, core|词性:名词


词源: From Late Latin marginalis "of a margin," from Latin margin-, margo "border, edge, margin."
