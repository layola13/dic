impressed:/ɪmˈprɛst/| adj.|印象深刻的；钦佩的；感到佩服的|Affected deeply or strongly in mind or feelings; filled with wonder and admiration.|例句：I was very impressed with his presentation. 我对他的演讲印象非常深刻。|例句：She was impressed by his knowledge of art. 她对他的艺术知识印象深刻。|近义词：awed, moved, touched, affected, influenced|反义词：unimpressed, indifferent, apathetic|词性:adjective
词源:From Late Latin impressus, past participle of imprimere (“to press into, imprint”), from in- + premere (“to press”).
