mandilion:/ˈmændɪljən/ | n. |披风，斗篷；（尤指）无袖的宽松外衣|A loose, sleeveless garment, especially a cloak or overcoat, worn over other clothing.  It was particularly popular in the 15th and 16th centuries.|The king wore a magnificent mandilion of purple velvet. 国王穿着一件华丽的紫色天鹅绒披风。|Synonyms: cloak, cape, mantle|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps undershirt as it is worn *under* clothing)|Noun
