hable:/ˈableɪ/ (Spanish)| adj.| able, capable|  Able to speak or write a language; capable of.  This word comes from the Spanish verb "hablar," meaning "to speak."| Example: Ella es **hable** en español. (She is able to speak Spanish.)| Example:  Es un niño muy **hable**. (He's a very talkative child.) |Synonym:  capaz (Spanish), competent, proficient |Antonym: incapable, incompetent|adjective
