disadvantagednesses:/ˌdɪsədˈvæntɪdʒdnɪsɪz/
n.
劣势，不利条件，弱势
The state of being disadvantaged; multiple instances of disadvantage. This word emphasizes the accumulation or concentration of disadvantages.
The disadvantagednesses faced by children in poverty can have long-lasting effects. 贫困儿童面临的多重劣势会产生长期影响。
The report highlighted the disadvantagednesses of rural communities in accessing healthcare. 报告强调了农村社区在获得医疗保健方面的劣势。
synonyms: disadvantages, handicaps, drawbacks, weaknesses, shortcomings
antonyms: advantages, benefits, strengths, assets
noun
