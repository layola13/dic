engraving: /ɪnˈɡreɪvɪŋ/ (US) /ɪnˈɡreɪvɪŋ/ (UK)
n.|雕刻,雕刻品,雕版|The act or art of cutting designs, letters, etc., onto a hard surface, or a design cut in this way.  A print made from an engraved plate.|The museum has a fascinating collection of medieval engravings.|Synonym: carving, etching, inscription|Antonym: erasure, obliteration|noun
engraving: /ɪnˈɡreɪvɪŋ/ (US) /ɪnˈɡreɪvɪŋ/ (UK)
v.|雕刻|Present participle of engrave.|He is engraving a message on the ring.|Synonym: carve, etch, inscribe|Antonym: erase, obliterate|verb
