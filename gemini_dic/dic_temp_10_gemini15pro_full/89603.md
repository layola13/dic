hoodooing:/ˈhuːduːɪŋ/
n.
施魔法; 厄运; 带来厄运; 巫毒魔法
The act of bringing bad luck to someone through magic or superstition.
He blamed his losing streak on hoodooing by a rival. 他把自己的连败归咎于对手的巫术。
jinxing, cursing, bewitching
blessing, good luck
noun

hoodoo:/ˈhuːduː/
n.
厄运; 坏运气; 不祥之兆; 巫毒教
Bad luck, a jinx, or a person or thing that brings bad luck.
That black cat crossing my path is a hoodoo. 那只黑猫穿过我的路是不祥之兆。
jinx, curse, misfortune
blessing, good fortune
noun
