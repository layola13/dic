copperplate:/ˈkɒpəpleɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpərpleɪt/ (US)| n. adj.|铜版；铜版印刷；铜版字体|A polished copper plate used for engraving or printing. Also refers to the print or style of handwriting produced by this method. |The artist meticulously etched the design onto the copperplate. 艺术家一丝不苟地在铜版上蚀刻图案。|  The invitation was printed in an elegant copperplate script. 请柬是用优雅的铜版字体印刷的。|synonyms: engraving, etching, calligraphy| antonyms: typescript, print (in some contexts)| noun, adjective
