arraign:/əˈreɪn/ | vt. | 传讯；控告；责难 | to call or bring (someone) before a court to answer a criminal charge | The suspect was arraigned on charges of murder.  嫌疑犯被传讯，罪名是谋杀。| synonyms: indict, prosecute, accuse | antonyms: exonerate, acquit | verb


arraign:/əˈreɪn/ | vt. | 责难；指责 | to criticize severely; find fault with |  He arraigned the government for its inaction. 他谴责政府无所作为。 | synonyms: criticize, censure, denounce | antonyms: praise, commend | verb
