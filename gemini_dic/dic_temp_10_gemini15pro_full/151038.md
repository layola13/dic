parapeted:/ˈpærəpɪtɪd/
adj.
有护墙的，有栏杆的
Having a parapet; protected by a parapet.
The parapeted roof offered a stunning view of the city.  有护墙的屋顶提供了令人惊叹的城市景观。
The castle walls were high and parapeted. 城堡的城墙高耸且有护墙。
近义词:walled, protected, fortified
反义词:exposed, unprotected, open
词性:adjective

词源: From the noun "parapet" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives). "Parapet" comes from the Italian "parapetto," from "parare" (to protect) + "petto" (chest).
