nonsensuously:/ˌnɒnˈsɛnsjuəsli/ /ˌnɑːnˈsɛnʃuəsli/
| adv. | 无意义地，荒谬地 |  In a way that is not nonsensical; sensibly.  | He spoke nonsensuously about the importance of brushing your teeth with mayonnaise. 他荒谬地谈论用蛋黄酱刷牙的重要性。| synonym: sensibly, rationally, logically | antonym: nonsensically, absurdly, foolishly | 词性: 副词

nonsensical:/nɒnˈsɛnsɪkl/ /ˌnɑːnˈsɛnsɪkl/
| adj. | 荒谬的，无意义的 | Having no meaning; making no sense. |  The plot of the movie was nonsensical. 这部电影的情节荒谬至极。| synonym: absurd, meaningless, illogical | antonym: sensible, meaningful, logical | 词性: 形容词

sense:/sɛns/
| n. | 感觉，意义 | A faculty by which the body perceives an external stimulus; one of the faculties of sight, smell, hearing, taste, and touch. | The dog has a keen sense of smell. 这只狗嗅觉灵敏。| synonym: feeling, perception, awareness | antonym: insensibility, numbness | 词性: 名词
