geomancers:/ˈdʒiːoʊˌmænsərz/
n.
风水师；堪舆家
A person who practices geomancy, a method of divination by interpreting markings on the ground or by casting figures.
The geomancers were consulted before the building was constructed.  风水师在建筑物建造之前被咨询过。
Geomancers traditionally used a handful of earth thrown onto a flat surface to create the patterns for their readings.  风水师传统上将一把泥土扔到平坦的表面上，以创建用于解读的图案。
Synonym: diviner, soothsayer, augur
Antonym: scientist, skeptic
