half-anglicized:/ˌhɑːf ˈæŋɡləˌsaɪzd/ | adj. | 半英化的 | Partly adapted to English forms or pronunciation, while retaining some original characteristics. | The restaurant's name was half-anglicized, making it easier for tourists to remember.  这家餐馆的名字是半英化的，让游客更容易记住。 | The menu featured half-anglicized versions of traditional dishes. 菜单上有传统菜肴的半英化版本。| Near synonyms: semi-anglicized, partially anglicized | Antonyms: fully anglicized, completely anglicized | adjective
