nemathecium:/ˌnɛməˈθiːsiəm/
n.
子囊盘被; 子囊壳被
A wart-like or other erumpent structure bearing acervuli, especially in certain ascomycetous fungi.
The nemathecium of the fungus erupts through the bark of the infected tree.  这种真菌的子囊盘被从受感染树的树皮中爆发出来。

近义词:acervulus, sporodochium
反义词:None

词性:noun

词源: From Ancient Greek *νῆμα (nêma, “thread, yarn, filament”) + *θήκη (thḗkē, “case, box, receptacle”).
