co-inheritor:/ˌkoʊɪnˈherɪtər/| n.|共同继承人|A person who inherits something jointly with another person or other people.|例句：She and her brother are co-inheritors of their father's estate. 她和她的兄弟是她父亲遗产的共同继承人。

例句：The co-inheritors decided to sell the property and split the proceeds. 共同继承人决定出售房产并平分收益。|近义词：joint heir, fellow heir|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "disinherited" - someone specifically excluded from inheritance)|词性:noun
