cosatu:/kɒˈsɑːtuː/
n.
南非工会联盟（Congress of South African Trade Unions）
The Congress of South African Trade Unions (COSATU) is a trade union federation in South Africa.  It is the largest of the three main trade union federations in South Africa.
COSATU played a significant role in the struggle against apartheid. 南非工会联盟在反对种族隔离的斗争中发挥了重要作用。
synonyms:  federation, union, labor union
antonyms:  employer organization, management
noun
