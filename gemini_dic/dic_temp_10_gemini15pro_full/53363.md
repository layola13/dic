disaffiliate:/dɪsəˈfɪlieɪt/
verb
脱离关系，断绝联系
To officially end a connection or association with a larger group or organization.
The local chapter was disaffiliated from the national organization due to disagreements over policy.  地方分会由于政策分歧而与国家组织脱离关系。
The school disaffiliated itself from the athletic conference. 学校脱离了运动联盟。
disassociate, detach, sever ties
affiliate, associate, join
v.
