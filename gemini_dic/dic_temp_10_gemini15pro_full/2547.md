admiringly:/ədˈmaɪərɪŋli/ | adv. |钦佩地，赞赏地 | In a way that shows admiration; with admiration.  | She looked at him admiringly. 她钦佩地看着他。

She smiled admiringly at her son's drawing. 她赞赏地对她儿子的画微笑着。 | 近义词: approvingly, respectfully, appreciatively | 反义词: disapprovingly, critically, scornfully | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From admire + -ing + -ly.  Admire comes from the Old French admirer, from Latin admirari "to wonder at," from ad- "to" + mirari "to wonder."

USAGE->
She listened admiringly to his speech. = 她钦佩地听着他的演讲。

He watched admiringly as the gymnast performed her routine. = 他钦佩地看着体操运动员完成她的动作。


PHRASE->
