intercept:/ˈɪntərsept/ | v. n. |拦截，截取；截距|To stop or catch something or someone before they are able to reach a particular place.|The police intercepted a shipment of drugs. 警方截获了一批毒品。|近义词：catch, seize, block, stop|反义词:release, allow, permit|词性:动词/名词

intercept:/ˈɪntərsept/ | n. |截距|The point where a line or curve crosses a coordinate axis.|The y-intercept of the line is 3.  这条线的y轴截距是3。|近义词：intersection|反义词: N/A|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *interceptus, past participle of intercipere "to intercept," from inter "between" + capere "to take."
