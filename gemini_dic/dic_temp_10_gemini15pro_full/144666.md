outwringing:/ˌaʊtˈrɪŋɪŋ/
n.
拧干；尽情表达；极度悲伤
the act of wringing something out, especially to remove excess water; an intense expression of grief or other strong emotion.
Her outwringing of grief was heartbreaking to witness. 她极度悲伤的表达令人心碎。
近义词：expression, outburst, effusion
反义词：suppression, concealment
词性：名词

词源:
From 'out-' (completely) + 'wringing' (present participle of 'wring', meaning to squeeze or twist).  Essentially, the word paints a picture of fully squeezing something out, whether it be water from a cloth or emotion from a person.
