communalists:/kəˈmjuːnəlɪsts/
n.
宗派主义者，地方主义者，公社主义者
People who advocate or participate in communalism, emphasizing loyalty to one's own ethnic or religious community rather than to the society as a whole.
Communalists often incite violence against other groups. 宗派主义者经常煽动对其他群体的暴力。
The communalists demanded a separate state. 地方主义者要求建立一个独立的邦。
synonyms:sectarians, separatists, partisans
antonyms:unitarians, integrationists
noun
