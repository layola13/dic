outgazing:/ˌaʊtˈɡeɪzɪŋ/
n.
凝视；远眺
The act of gazing outward, often with a sense of wonder or contemplation.
She stood on the cliff, outgazing at the vast ocean. 她站在悬崖上，凝视着浩瀚的海洋。
Synonyms: contemplating, observing, viewing, staring
Antonyms: ignoring, overlooking
