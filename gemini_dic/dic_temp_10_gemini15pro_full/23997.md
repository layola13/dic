breathinesses:/ˈbreθɪnɪsɪz/
n.
气喘吁吁；呼吸声的沙哑
the audible escape of breath during phonation
Her breathinesses were audible even from the back of the room. 她的呼吸声即使在房间后面也能听到。

Etymology: From breathy + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  "Breathy" comes from "breath" (Old English *bræþ) + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives).

Synonyms:  wheezings, raspings, gasps
Antonyms: clear articulation, smooth voice
