lawned:/lɔːnd/
adjective
有草坪的；草坪覆盖的
covered with a lawn; having a lawn.
The hotel has a large lawned area for guests to relax in.  酒店有一个很大的草坪供客人放松。
My dream house has a beautifully lawned garden. 我梦想中的房子有一个漂亮的草坪花园。
近义词: grassy
反义词: barren, treeless

词源:
From lawn + -ed.  来自名词 lawn（草坪）加上形容词后缀 -ed，表示“具有...的”。
