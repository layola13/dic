dynamists:/ˈdaɪnəmɪsts/
n.|精力充沛的人；富有创新精神的人；推动者|A person who is energetic and forceful; a person who initiates change and progress.|The young dynamos are the dynasts of the future. 这些年轻的精力充沛者是未来的推动者。|近义词:activists, go-getters, movers and shakers, innovators|反义词:slackers, conservatives|词性:名词

词源:
From Ancient Greek δύναμις (dúnamis, “power”) +‎ -ist.
