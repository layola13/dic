coverture:/ˈkʌvərtʃər/| n.|掩蔽, 覆盖; (尤指法律方面)已婚妇女的身份|The legal doctrine of coverture deprived married women of their independent legal existence.  已婚妇女身份的法律原则剥夺了已婚妇女的独立法律存在。|例句：Under the old laws of coverture, a married woman's legal identity was subsumed by her husband's.  根据旧的掩护法，已婚妇女的法律身份被其丈夫所取代。

例句：The historical concept of coverture meant a wife's legal rights were absorbed by her husband's.  历史上，掩护的概念意味着妻子的合法权利被丈夫吸收。|近义词：protection, shelter, guardianship|反义词:exposure, vulnerability|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *coverteure, from coverir "to cover," from Latin cooperire "to cover completely," from co- "completely" + operire "to cover."
