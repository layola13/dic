beseeming:/bɪˈsiːmɪŋ/
adjective
合适的，相称的
fitting, suitable, or proper
Her beseeming modesty impressed everyone. 她相称的谦虚给每个人都留下了深刻的印象。
近义词:appropriate, fitting, proper, suitable, decorous
反义词:inappropriate, unfitting, improper, unsuitable, indecorous
词性:adjective
词源:From Middle English *beseeming, present participle of beseem (“to befit”), from Old English besēman (“to befit, suit, be suitable, be proper”), from be- + sēman (“to befit, suit”).
