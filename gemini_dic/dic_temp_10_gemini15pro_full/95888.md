increated: /ɪnˈkriːsɪd/ /ɪnˈkriːstɪd/
Verb | 增加的，增强的 | Increased refers to something that has become larger or greater in size, amount, or degree. | The company's profits have increased significantly this year.  该公司今年的利润显著增长。| Increased production led to lower prices.  产量增加导致价格下降。 | synonyms: augmented, boosted, enlarged, expanded, grown, heightened, intensified, magnified, multiplied, raised, strengthened, supplemented | antonyms: decreased, diminished, lessened, reduced | v.
