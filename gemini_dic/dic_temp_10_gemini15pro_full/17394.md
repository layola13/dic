beaker:/ˈbiːkər/| n.|烧杯; 大口杯|A beaker is a lipped cylindrical glass container for laboratory use.|The chemist carefully poured the solution into a beaker.|近义词：flask, cup, vessel|反义词: N/A|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *beker, from Anglo-Norman beker, variant of Old Northern French bicchier (“drinking vessel”), from Medieval Latin bicarium, of uncertain origin.
