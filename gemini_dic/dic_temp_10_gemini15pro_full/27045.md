calendarial:/ˌkælənˈdɛəriəl/
adjective
日历的；历法的
relating to or based on the calendar
The calendarial year begins on January 1st.  历法年从1月1日开始。
The priest made a calendarial calculation for the festival.  神父为节日做了历法计算。
近义词: calendrical
反义词: (No common antonym)
词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *calendāriālis, from Latin calendārium "account book, calendar."
