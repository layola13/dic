mho:/moʊ/| n.|姆欧，电导单位|A unit of electrical conductance, the reciprocal of the ohm.  It represents the ease with which electric current flows through a material. Now largely replaced by siemens.|The conductance of this wire is 2 mhos. 这根导线的电导是2姆欧。|近义词：siemens|反义词：ohm|noun
