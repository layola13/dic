fad:/fæd/| n.|一时流行的狂热；风尚；时尚|An intense and widely shared enthusiasm for something, especially one that is short-lived and without basis in the object's qualities; a craze.|例句：The latest fad is wearing ripped jeans. 最新的潮流是穿破洞牛仔裤。|近义词：craze, trend, vogue, fashion|反义词：standard, classic|名词
