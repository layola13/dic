hexameter:/ˈhɛksæmɪtər/| n.|六步格，六音步诗|A line of verse consisting of six metrical feet, especially of dactyls or spondees.  It is the standard verse form of classical epic poetry.|Homer's Odyssey is written in hexameters.|近义词:dactylic hexameter|反义词:|名词
