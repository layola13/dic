chapter:/ˈtʃæptər/ (US) /ˈtʃæptə(r)/ (UK)| n.|章节，篇章，回|A main division of a book, typically with a number or title.|例句：I'm reading chapter three of this novel. 我正在读这本小说的第三章。|例句：This chapter of my life is coming to a close. 我人生的这一章即将结束。|近义词：section, part, division|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
