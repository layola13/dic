handcart:/ˈhændkɑːrt/ (US) /ˈhændkɑːt/ (UK)| n. |手推车，小推车|A small cart with two wheels, typically pulled or pushed by hand, used for transporting goods.|The farmer used a handcart to carry vegetables from his field to the market. 农民用手推车把蔬菜从地里运到市场。|近义词：pushcart, trolley, cart|反义词: truck, lorry|名词
