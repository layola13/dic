equilibrity:/ˌiːkwɪˈlɪbrɪti/ | n. | 平衡，均衡 |A state of balance or equilibrium. |The tightrope walker maintained his equilibrity with impressive skill.  走钢丝者以惊人的技巧保持着平衡。| 近义词: balance, stability, poise, equilibrium | 反义词: imbalance, instability, disequilibrium | 词性: 名词

词源: From Late Latin *aequilibritas, from Latin aequilibris "equally balanced," from aequi- "equally" + libra "balance, scales."
