apply:/əˈplaɪ/ (US), /əˈplaɪ/ (UK)| v. |申请；应用；涂抹；适用|To put something to a particular purpose or task; to request something officially, especially in writing or by sending in a form.|I applied for a job at the local library. 我在当地图书馆申请了一份工作。|synonyms: utilize, employ, exert, petition, request|antonyms: ignore, neglect, reject|verb

apply:/əˈplaɪ/ (US), /əˈplaɪ/ (UK)| n. |申请；应用；涂抹|An act of applying; a formal request.|My application for a visa was rejected. 我的签证申请被拒绝了。|synonyms: application, request, petition|antonyms: rejection, denial|noun
