memorialization:/məˌmɔːriəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
纪念；纪念仪式；纪念物
The act of creating a memorial or the state of being memorialized. The process of preserving memories of a person or event, often through a ceremony, monument, or written record.
The memorialization of the war dead took place at the national cemetery.  在国家公墓举行了阵亡将士的纪念仪式。
The museum is a memorialization of the city's history.  这座博物馆是对这座城市历史的纪念。
commemoration, remembrance, celebration
oblivion, forgetting
noun
