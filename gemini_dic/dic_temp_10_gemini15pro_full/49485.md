delusion:/dɪˈluːʒən/ /dəˈluːʒn/| n. | 妄想，错觉 | A fixed false belief that is not based in reality.  A belief that is held despite evidence to the contrary.| He was suffering from delusions of grandeur, believing himself to be a king. 他患有夸大妄想症，认为自己是个国王。| synonyms:  hallucination, illusion, misconception, fallacy | antonyms:  reality, truth, fact | noun

delusional:/dɪˈluːʒənl/ /dəˈluːʒənl/| adj. | 妄想的 | Characterized by or holding delusions.  Suffering from or relating to a delusion.|  His delusional thinking led him to believe he could fly. 他妄想的思维让他相信自己能飞。| synonyms:  deranged, demented, insane | antonyms:  rational, sane, lucid | adjective
