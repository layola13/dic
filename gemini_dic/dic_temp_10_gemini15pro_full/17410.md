beamers:/ˈbiːmərz/
n.
投影仪；（尤指汽车的）远光灯
A beamer is a projector that is used to project images onto a screen.  It can also refer to the high-beam headlights of a car.
The teacher used a beamer to show a presentation. 老师用投影仪展示了演示文稿。
He flashed his beamers at the oncoming car. 他向迎面而来的汽车闪了一下远光灯。
projector, overhead projector, data projector
low beams, dipped headlights
noun
