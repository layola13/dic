colloquialize:/kəˈloʊkwiəˌlaɪz/
|v.|使口语化；使通俗化|To make (something, such as a literary work) colloquial; to adapt (something) to the usages of common speech.|He tried to colloquialize the formal language of the academic paper.|近义词：informalize, popularize|反义词:formalize|verb
