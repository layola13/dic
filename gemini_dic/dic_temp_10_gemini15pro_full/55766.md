diversifier:/daɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪər/
noun
多样化者，多元化者
Something that diversifies, especially a business or investment that reduces risk by spreading capital among several ventures.
Many farmers are looking for ways to become diversifiers to increase their income streams. 许多农民正在寻找成为多元化经营者的方法，以增加他们的收入来源。
Synonyms: investor, financier, stakeholder, entrepreneur, backer
Antonyms: specialist, concentrator
