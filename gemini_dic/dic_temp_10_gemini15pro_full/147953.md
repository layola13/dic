overreserved:/ˌoʊvərɪˈzɜːrvd/
adjective
过度拘谨的，过于保守的
Having made too many reservations or commitments; booked beyond capacity.  Also, excessively restrained or reserved in manner.
The restaurant was overreserved for Valentine's Day and had to turn away many couples.  这家餐馆情人节预订过多，不得不拒绝许多情侣。
She's a bit overreserved, so it takes time for her to warm up to new people. 她有点过于拘谨，所以需要一段时间才能和新朋友熟络起来。
近义词: overbooked, oversubscribed, shy, reticent, withdrawn
反义词: outgoing, extroverted, gregarious, underbooked
词性: adjective
