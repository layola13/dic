horror-struck: /ˈhɒrə(r)ˌstrʌk/ (UK) /ˈhɔːrərˌstrʌk/ (US)
horror-struck: adj.
horror-struck: 惊恐万分的，吓呆的
horror-struck: Filled with horror; terrified.  The adjective combines "horror" (extreme fear) with "struck," suggesting being suddenly and powerfully affected by this fear.
horror-struck: She was horror-struck by the sight of the accident. 她被事故的景象吓呆了。
horror-struck: The horror-struck witnesses could only watch in disbelief.  惊恐万分的目击者只能难以置信地看着。

horror-struck: 近义词: terrified, aghast, horrified, appalled
horror-struck: 反义词: calm, unfazed, composed
horror-struck: 词性: adjective
