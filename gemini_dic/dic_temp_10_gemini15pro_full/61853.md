employing:/ɪmˈplɔɪɪŋ/
|v.|雇用；使用；运用；|present participle of employ|
|The company is employing more staff.  这家公司正在雇用更多员工。|
|He was employing a new technique. 他正在使用一种新技术。|
|Synonyms: using, utilizing, applying, hiring|
|Antonyms: dismissing, firing, laying off|
|verb|


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English emploien, from Old French emploier, from Latin implicare (“to enfold, involve, entangle”), from in- (“in, into”) + plicare (“to fold”).
