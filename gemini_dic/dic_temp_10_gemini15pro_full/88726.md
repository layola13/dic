hobnobbed:/ˈhɑːbnɑːbd/ /ˈhɒbnɒbd/| v.|交谈，亲切交谈；(与要人)过从甚密|To socialize with someone, especially someone important or famous.|He hobnobbed with royalty at the garden party. 他在花园派对上与皇室成员亲切交谈。|Synonyms: socialize, mingle, fraternize, consort|Antonyms: isolate, shun, avoid|verb
