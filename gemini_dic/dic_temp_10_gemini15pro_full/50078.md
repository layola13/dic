dentary:/ˈdɛntəri/| n. adj.|齿骨；下颌骨|The dentary is the main bone of the lower jaw in most vertebrates.|The paleontologist carefully examined the dinosaur's dentary.|Synonyms: mandible, jawbone|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a bone)|n. adj.

dentary:/ˈdɛntəri/| adj.|齿的；有齿的；齿状的|Some snakes have dentary glands that produce venom.|The dentary margins of the leaf were serrated.|Synonyms: toothed, denticulate|Antonyms: smooth, edentulous|adj.
