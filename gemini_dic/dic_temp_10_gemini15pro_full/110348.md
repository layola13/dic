lightman:/ˈlaɪtmən/| n.|光之使者；莱特曼（姓氏）|A person who brings or represents light, often used figuratively; a surname.|例句：He was a true lightman, illuminating the path for others. 他是一位真正的光之使者，照亮了别人的道路。

例句：Dr. Lightman is a renowned expert in deception detection. 莱特曼博士是一位著名的欺骗检测专家。|近义词：illuminator, beacon, guide|反义词：obscurer|词性:noun
