overstudiously: /ˌoʊvərˈstuːdiəsli/ | adv. | 过度用功地，过分勤奋地 |  In a way that involves excessive studying; too studiously. | He prepared for the exam overstudiously, neglecting his health. 他为了考试过度用功，忽略了健康。|  She pored over her notes overstudiously, barely sleeping the night before the test. 她过度用功地钻研笔记，考试前一天晚上几乎没睡。| 近义词: excessively, diligently, painstakingly | 反义词: lazily, carelessly, half-heartedly | 词性: 副词

overstudiously 词源:

* over- (prefix):  meaning "excessive," "too much"
* studious (adjective): from Latin "studiosus," meaning "eager," "devoted to study"
* -ly (suffix): forming adverbs from adjectives
