hyperintensely:/ˌhaɪpərɪnˈtɛnsli/| adv.|高强度地，高信号地|With an unusually high signal intensity.  It's primarily used in medical imaging to describe how bright a particular area appears on a scan.|The lesion appeared hyperintensely on the T2-weighted MRI image.  病灶在T2加权磁共振图像上呈高信号。|The tumor was hyperintensely bright on the FLAIR sequence. 肿瘤在FLAIR序列上非常亮。|近义词：brightly, intensely|反义词：hypointensely, dimly|词性:副词
词源: hyper- (Greek: over, above) + intense + -ly (adverbial suffix)
