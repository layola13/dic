nicotiana:/ˌnɪkəʊʃiˈɑːnə/ /ˌnɪkoʊʃiˈænə/| n. |烟草属；烟草|A genus of herbaceous plants in the nightshade family (Solanaceae), native to the Americas, Australia, and some Pacific Islands.  Many species are cultivated for their leaves, which are the source of tobacco.|Nicotiana tabacum is the most commonly cultivated species of nicotiana.  普通烟草是最常种植的烟草品种。|Nicotiana plants are susceptible to various diseases. 烟草植物容易感染各种疾病。|Synonym: tobacco plant|Antonym: None (no single antonym for a plant genus)|noun
