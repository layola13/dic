archangels:/ˈɑːrkeɪndʒəlz/| n. |大天使|An angel of high rank.  Archangels are often considered to be the most powerful and important angels. |The archangels Michael and Gabriel are frequently mentioned in religious texts.  米迦勒和加百列两位大天使在宗教经文中经常被提及。|Synonyms: seraphim, cherubim|Antonyms: demon, devil|Noun
