inlay:/ˈɪnleɪ/ (US), /ˈɪnleɪ/ (UK)| n. vt.|镶嵌物，嵌入；镶嵌，嵌入|An inlay is a decorative material set into a surface of a different material. As a verb, it means to set (something) into a surface as an inlay.|The antique table had a beautiful mother-of-pearl inlay. 这张古董桌子镶嵌着美丽的珍珠母。 He inlaid the wooden box with silver wire. 他用银丝镶嵌了木盒。|近义词：insert, embed, set|反义词: extract, remove|词性: noun, verb

inlay:/ˈɪnleɪ/ (US), /ˈɪnleɪ/ (UK)| v.|镶嵌，嵌入|To set (something) into a surface as an inlay.|She inlaid the jewelry box with precious stones. 她把珠宝盒镶嵌上宝石。|近义词：insert, embed, set|反义词: extract, remove|词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English inlaien, from in- + lay. Equivalent to in + lay.
