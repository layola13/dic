civic:/ˈsɪvɪk/ |adj.|公民的，市民的，城市的|Relating to a city or town, or the people who live there.  Pertaining to the duties or activities of people in relation to their town, city, or local area.|例句：The civic center hosts many community events. 市政中心举办许多社区活动。

例句：Voting is a civic duty. 投票是公民的义务。|近义词：municipal, community, urban|反义词：rural, private|词性: 形容词


The word "civical" is not a standard English word.  The correct word is "civic".  The following information pertains to "civic".

词源: From the Latin word "civicus," meaning "relating to a citizen."  Ultimately derived from "civis," meaning "citizen."
