archdioceses: /ˌɑːrtʃˈdaɪəˌsiːz/ | n. | 总教区（复数） |  The plural form of archdiocese, referring to the principal episcopal see of a province, under the direction of an archbishop. | The archdioceses of New York and Los Angeles are among the largest in the United States. 纽约和洛杉矶的总教区是美国最大的总教区之一。 | Synonyms: archbishoprics (less common) | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym; perhaps dioceses, which are smaller units within a province) | noun
