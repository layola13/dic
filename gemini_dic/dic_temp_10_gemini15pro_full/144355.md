outstayed:/ˌaʊtˈsteɪd/| v.|逾期逗留；超过…停留|to stay longer than the permitted or desired time|例句：He outstayed his visa by three months. 他签证逾期逗留了三个月。

例句：The guests outstayed their welcome. 客人们待得太久了，不受欢迎了。|近义词：overstay, remain too long|反义词:leave early, depart promptly|词性:动词
