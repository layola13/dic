broader:/ˈbrɔːdər/| adj.|更广泛的，更宽阔的|Comparative form of broad: wider in extent; of greater scope.|The company is looking for broader markets for its products.  这家公司正在为其产品寻找更广阔的市场。|近义词：wider, larger, more extensive, more comprehensive|反义词：narrower, smaller, more limited, more restricted|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *brodere, comparative of brod, from Old English brād (“broad, wide, large, spacious, extensive, vast, flat, level, plain; open, clear; liberal, generous; numerous”), from Proto-Germanic *braidaz (“broad”), from Proto-Indo-European *mr̥h₁dʰús (“broad, wide”). Cognate with Scots braid (“broad”), Saterland Frisian brood (“broad”), West Frisian breed (“broad”), Dutch breed (“broad”), Low German bred (“broad”), German breit (“broad”), Danish bred (“broad”), Swedish bred (“broad”), Icelandic breiður (“broad”).
