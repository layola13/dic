antiaggressively:/ˌæntiəˈɡrɛsɪvli/
adverb|非侵略性地；反侵略地|In a manner that opposes or counteracts aggression; without aggression. |He responded antiaggressively to the provocation, choosing to walk away rather than escalate the situation. 他对挑衅采取了非侵略性的回应，选择走开而不是让局势升级。|Synonyms: peacefully, passively, nonviolently, defensively|Antonyms: aggressively, offensively, proactively, hostilely|词性: 副词

antiaggressively:/ˌæntiəˈɡrɛsɪvli/
Etymology:  "anti-" (prefix meaning against, opposite) + "aggressive" (adjective meaning ready or likely to attack or confront) + "-ly" (adverb suffix)  词源：“anti-”（表示反对、相反的前缀）+“aggressive”（形容词，表示准备或可能攻击或对抗）+“-ly”（副词后缀）
