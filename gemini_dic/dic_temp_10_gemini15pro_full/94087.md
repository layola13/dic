impatience:/ɪmˈpeɪʃəns/| n.|不耐烦，急躁|The state of being unable to wait patiently or tolerate delay; restlessness or irritability.|His impatience was evident in his constant fidgeting. 他的不耐烦表现在他不停地坐立不安。|近义词：restlessness, irritability, eagerness, anxiety|反义词：patience, tolerance, forbearance|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: from Old French impatience, based on Latin impatientia, from impatiens ‘unable to endure,’ from in- ‘not’ + patiens (present participle of pati ‘suffer, endure’).
