humanizers:/ˈhjuːmənaɪzərz/
n.
人情味的东西; 使人性化的事物;  人性化者
People or things that make something more humane or relatable.  These can be qualities, actions, policies or even objects.
The company introduced several humanizers, such as flexible working hours and pet-friendly offices, to improve employee morale.  公司引入了几个使人性化的措施，例如灵活的工作时间和允许携带宠物的办公室，以提高员工士气。
The museum exhibit included humanizers like personal letters and photographs to connect visitors with the historical figures. 博物馆展览中包含了一些人性化的展品，例如私人信件和照片，以帮助参观者与历史人物产生联系。
近义词: humanizing elements, personal touches, relatable aspects
反义词: dehumanizers, impersonal elements
词性: noun
