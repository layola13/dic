eelery: /ˈiːləri/ | n. |芹菜；芹菜茎 | Celery is a marshland plant in the family Apiaceae that has been cultivated as a vegetable since antiquity. | I love adding celery to my salads. 我喜欢在沙拉里加芹菜。|  synonyms: celery stalks, celery sticks | antonyms: N/A | noun


Etymology->

From Old French *celerie, from Italian *celleri, plural of *cellero, from Late Latin *selinon, from Ancient Greek *σέλινον (sélinon).  Ultimately from a Pre-Greek substrate word.
