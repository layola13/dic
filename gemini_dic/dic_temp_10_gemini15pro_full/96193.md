indeterminates:/ˌɪndɪˈtɜːrmɪnəts/ | n. | 未定元，不定元，未知数 |A symbol or variable in a mathematical expression or equation whose value is not known or specified.  Representing quantities that are not yet determined.  Often used in algebra, polynomials, and matrices. | The polynomial has three indeterminates: x, y, and z.  这个多项式有三个未定元：x、y 和 z。

The values of the indeterminates can be any real number.  这些未定元的值可以是任何实数。 | synonyms: variables, unknowns | antonyms: constants, knowns | noun
