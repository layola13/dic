aliquot:/ˈælɪkwɒt/ (US) /ˈælɪkwɒt/ (UK)| adj. n.|等分的，整除的；等分部分，整除数|An aliquot is a portion of a larger whole, especially a sample taken for chemical analysis or other treatment, and contains the same proportions of constituents as the whole.|The chemist took an aliquot of the solution for testing. 化学家取了一份溶液的等分试样进行测试。|近义词：portion, sample, share|反义词: whole, total|词性:adjective, noun

aliquot:/ˈælɪkwɒt/ (US) /ˈælɪkwɒt/ (UK)| n.|等分，整除数|An aliquot is a factor of a whole number that divides the number evenly without leaving a remainder.|3 is an aliquot of 12. 3是12的整除数。|近义词：factor, divisor|反义词: multiple|词性: noun
