lets on:/lets ˈɒn (British) / /lets ˈɑːn (American)/| phrasal verb | 泄露；假装；装作 | To reveal a secret or to pretend to know something or be doing something. |

例句：
He let on that he knew about the surprise party. 他泄露了他知道惊喜派对的事。

He wasn't really angry, he was just letting on. 他其实并没有生气，只是装出来的。


近义词：reveal, disclose, pretend, feign, make out | 反义词：conceal, hide, be honest | 词性：短语动词
