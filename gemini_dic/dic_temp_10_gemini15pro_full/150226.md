pandemic: /pænˈdɛmɪk/ | n. adj. | 大流行，广泛传播的 |  (of a disease) prevalent over a whole country or the world. | The COVID-19 pandemic affected the entire world.  新冠疫情影响了全世界。|  adj. The pandemic flu caused widespread panic. 大流行性流感引起了广泛的恐慌。 | synonyms: widespread, global, universal, rampant | antonyms: localized, contained, isolated | noun, adjective
