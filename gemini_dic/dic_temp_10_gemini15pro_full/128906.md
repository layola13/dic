nimby:/ˈnɪmbi/| n.|邻避症患者；反对在自己家附近建设公共设施的人|A person who objects to the siting of something perceived as unpleasant or potentially dangerous in their own neighborhood, such as a landfill or hazardous waste facility, especially while raising no such objections to similar developments elsewhere.|例句：The NIMBYs protested the construction of the new homeless shelter. 邻避症患者抗议建造新的流浪汉收容所。

例句：He was labeled a NIMBY for opposing the wind farm project near his property.  由于反对在他家附近的风力发电项目，他被贴上了邻避症患者的标签。

|近义词：localist, parochialist|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
