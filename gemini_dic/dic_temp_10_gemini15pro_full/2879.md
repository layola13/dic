adverted:/ədˈvɜːrtɪd/| v.|提及，注意，谈到|To refer to something briefly or indirectly; to turn one's attention to something.  |The speaker adverted to the recent economic downturn. 演讲者提到了最近的经济衰退。

The chairman adverted to the company's financial difficulties. 主席提到了公司的财务困难。|近义词：mentioned, referred to, alluded to, touched upon|反义词：ignored, neglected, overlooked|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin 'advertere'  meaning "to turn toward," from 'ad-' (to, toward) + 'vertere' (to turn).


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He often adverts to his childhood in his speeches. = 他在演讲中经常提到他的童年。
She adverts to the problem in her report. = 她在报告中提到了这个问题。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:

They have adverted to the risks involved in the project. = 他们已经提到了该项目涉及的风险。

过去完成时:

He had adverted to the company's poor performance in previous meetings. = 在之前的会议中，他曾提到过公司糟糕的业绩。
