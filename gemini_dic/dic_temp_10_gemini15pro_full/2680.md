verb
梦见; 梦想
Dreamed of or imagined, especially as pleasant or desirable.
I adreamed in that holy hour. = 在那神圣的时刻，我做了一个梦。
She adreamed of a world without war. = 她梦想着一个没有战争的世界。

近义词:dreamed, dreamt, envisioned, fantasized, imagined
反义词:awake
词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From a- (on, in, at) + dreamed (past tense of dream). The prefix "a-" is an archaic intensifier.
