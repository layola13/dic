nonsyndicated:/ˌnɒnˈsɪndɪkeɪtɪd/
adjective
未辛迪加的，未联播的
Not syndicated; not sold or distributed for publication or broadcasting by a syndicate.
The local radio station played a lot of nonsyndicated music.  这家本地广播电台播放了很多未联播的音乐。
The author self-published her nonsyndicated novel. 作者自费出版了她未辛迪加的小说。
Near Synonyms: independent, local, exclusive, original
Antonyms: syndicated, distributed, networked
词性: adjective
