par for the course:/pɑːr fɔːr ðə kɔːrs/ |adj.|意料之中的；正常的；典型的|Something that is par for the course is what you would expect to happen or expect someone to do. It is considered normal or typical, often in a slightly negative or unexciting way.|The food was mediocre, but that's par for the course at this restaurant.|近义词：typical, usual, normal, expected, standard, routine, commonplace|反义词：unusual, atypical, unexpected, surprising, extraordinary|adjective
