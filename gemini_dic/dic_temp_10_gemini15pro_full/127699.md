negatory:/ˈnɛɡətɔːri/ /ˈneɡətɔːri/
adjective
否定的；禁止的；拒绝的
Expressing or containing a denial or refusal.
"A negatory response"  一个否定的回答
"The test results were negatory."  测试结果是否定的。
近义词: negative, no, nix, refusing, rejecting
反义词: affirmative, positive, yes
词性: adjective

negatory:/ˈnɛɡətɔːri/ /ˈneɡətɔːri/
noun
否定词；拒绝；否认
A word or statement expressing denial or refusal.
"His response was a firm negatory." 他的回答是坚决的否定。
"The boss gave a negatory to my request for a raise." 老板拒绝了我加薪的请求。
近义词: denial, refusal, rejection
反义词: affirmation, acceptance, approval
词性: noun

negatory:/ˈnɛɡətɔːri/ /ˈneɡətɔːri/
adverb (informal)
不；没有；并非如此
No; not so.  (chiefly used in informal speech, especially in aviation)
"Negatory, we haven't received clearance yet."  没有，我们还没有收到许可。
"Is that your final answer? Negatory." 那是你的最终答案吗？不是。
近义词: no, not at all, nope
反义词: yes, affirmative, yep
词性: adverb
