bef: /bɛf/
pronoun.|before的缩写|在...之前|例句：I'll see you bef dinner. 晚饭前见。|近义词：prior to, ahead of|反义词：after|缩写

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I always check my email bef I leave for work. = 我总是在上班前查看我的电子邮件。
She usually finishes her homework bef dinner. = 她通常晚饭前做完作业。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am finishing up some work bef I go home. = 我正在完成一些工作，然后回家。
She is studying for the exam bef she goes to bed. = 她正在为考试学习，然后睡觉。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have never seen this movie bef. = 我以前从未看过这部电影。
She has completed all the tasks bef the deadline. = 她在截止日期前完成了所有任务。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been working on this project bef you arrived. = 在你到达之前，我一直在这个项目上工作。
She has been waiting for the bus bef it finally came. = 她一直在等公共汽车，直到它终于来了。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I saw him bef he left. = 我在他离开之前见到了他。
She finished her work bef I arrived. = 她在我到达之前完成了她的工作。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was reading a book bef you called. = 你打电话之前我正在看书。
She was cooking dinner bef the guests arrived. = 客人来之前她正在做饭。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had finished my work bef he arrived. = 在他到达之前我已经完成了我的工作。
She had left bef I could say goodbye. = 在我能说再见之前她已经离开了。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been waiting for an hour bef she showed up. = 我已经等了一个小时，她才出现。
She had been working on the project for weeks bef it was completed. = 她在这个项目上工作了几个星期，才完成。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will finish my work bef I go home. = 我将在回家之前完成我的工作。
She will arrive bef the meeting starts. = 她将在会议开始之前到达。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be working on this project bef you arrive. = 你到达之前我将在这个项目上工作。
She will be studying for the exam bef she goes to bed. = 她将在睡觉前为考试学习。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I will have finished my work bef you arrive. = 在你到达之前我将已经完成我的工作。
She will have left bef the meeting starts. = 在会议开始之前她将已经离开。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
I will have been working on this project for a week bef you arrive. = 在你到达之前，我将已经在这个项目上工作一周了。
She will have been studying for the exam for days bef she takes it. = 在她参加考试之前，她将已经学习好几天了。


PHRASE->
bef long = 不久以后
long bef = 早在…之前
shortly bef =  就在...之前
just bef = 就在...之前
immediately bef = 就在...之前


注意事项:

bef主要用于非正式场合，在正式写作中应避免使用。  它更常见于网络聊天、短信等非正式交流中。
