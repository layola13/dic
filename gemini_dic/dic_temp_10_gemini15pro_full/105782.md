kirsch:/kɪrʃ/
noun
樱桃酒，樱桃白兰地
A clear, colorless brandy made from cherries.
He offered me a glass of kirsch after dinner. 他晚饭后给我一杯樱桃酒。
Synonyms: cherry brandy, kirschwasser
Antonyms: N/A
