magnum opus:/ˈmæɡnəm ˈoʊpəs/ /ˈmæɡnəm ˈɒpəs/| n. |代表作；杰作|A great work of art, literature, or music, especially one regarded as the most important work of an artist's career.  |例句：The Ninth Symphony is considered by many to be Beethoven's magnum opus.  许多人认为《第九交响曲》是贝多芬的代表作。

|例句：Her latest novel is her magnum opus, a work of incredible depth and complexity. 她最新的小说是她的代表作，一部具有惊人深度和复杂性的作品。|近义词：masterpiece, chef d'oeuvre, masterwork |反义词:potboiler, failure|词性:名词
