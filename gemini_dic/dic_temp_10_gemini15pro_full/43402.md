crankless:/ˈkræŋklɪs/
adjective
无曲柄的
Lacking a crank.  Specifically refers to engines or mechanisms that operate without a traditional crank and connecting rod assembly.
The crankless engine design offers several advantages, including reduced vibration.  无曲柄发动机设计具有多种优势，包括减少振动。
The new bike features a crankless pedal system. 这款新自行车采用无曲柄踏板系统。
Near Synonyms: direct-drive, gearless, camless
Antonyms: cranked
