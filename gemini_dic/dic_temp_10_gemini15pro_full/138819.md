nuncio:/ˈnʌnʃioʊ/ (US), /ˈnʌnsɪəʊ/ (UK)| n. |使节；教廷大使|A diplomatic representative of the pope sent to a foreign government.|The Pope's nuncio to France delivered a message of peace.|Synonym: envoy, ambassador, legate, emissary|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps *rebel*, *dissident* in a specific context)|noun

nuncio:/ˈnʌnʃioʊ/ (US), /ˈnʌnsɪəʊ/ (UK)| n. |信使；报信者|A messenger, especially one bearing important news.|He acted as the king's nuncio, carrying the declaration of war.|Synonym: messenger, courier, herald|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps *interceptor*, *obstructor* in a specific context)|noun
