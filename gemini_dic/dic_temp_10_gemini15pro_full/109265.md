lemming-like:/ˈlɛmɪŋˌlaɪk/
adjective
像旅鼠一样的；盲目跟风的
Resembling the behavior of lemmings, especially in blindly following others, often to disastrous consequences.  This refers to the misconception that lemmings commit mass suicide by jumping off cliffs.
The investors exhibited lemming-like behavior, pouring their money into the failing company. 投资者们表现出像旅鼠一样的行为，把钱投入到这家濒临倒闭的公司。
synonyms:blind, unthinking, herd-like, sheep-like, conformist
antonyms:independent, individualistic, nonconformist, discerning
