brawled:/brɔːld/
verb
争吵，打架
fought or quarreled noisily and violently.
The two teams brawled on the field after the game.  两支队伍在比赛结束后在场上打了起来。
The drunken men brawled in the street.  醉汉们在街上斗殴。
synonym: fought, scuffled, clashed, tussled
antonym: reconciled, mediated, made peace
词性: 动词
