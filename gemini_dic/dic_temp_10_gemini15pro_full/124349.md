MotionBuilder: /ˈmoʊʃənˌbɪldər/ | n. | 运动构建器；动作捕捉软件 | A 3D character animation software produced by Autodesk. It is used primarily by game developers, filmmakers, and other media professionals for creating, editing, and viewing 3D character animation. | MotionBuilder is often used in the previsualization stage of filmmaking.  运动构建器常用于电影制作的预览阶段。

MotionBuilder allows animators to create realistic character movements.  运动构建器允许动画师创建逼真的角色动作。 | 近义词: character animation software, 3D animation software | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
