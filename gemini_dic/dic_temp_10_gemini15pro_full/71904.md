flexible:/ˈfleksəbl/ /ˈfleksɪbl/| adj.|灵活的，柔韧的，可变通的|Able to bend easily without breaking; readily adapted or compliant.|例句：This wire is very flexible. 这根电线很灵活。|例句：She has a flexible work schedule. 她有一个灵活的工作时间表。|近义词：adaptable, pliable, elastic, supple|反义词：inflexible, rigid, stiff|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *flexibilis, from Latin flexus (past participle of flectere "to bend") + -ibilis (suffix forming adjectives meaning "able to be").
