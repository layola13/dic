ordon:/ˈɔːrdən/ | v. | 命令，规定，注定 | To command or decree; to preordain; to destine. | The king ordons that all subjects shall pay taxes. 国王下令所有臣民都要纳税。 |  The court ordoned him to pay a fine. 法院命令他支付罚款。 | 近义词: command, decree, dictate, order | 反义词: allow, permit | 词性: 动词

词源:
From Old French *ordener, from Latin ordinare "to set in order, arrange, regulate," from ordo "row, rank, order."
