dunes:/duːnz/
n.
沙丘
A hill of sand built by the wind, especially in a desert or near the sea.
The wind sculpted the dunes into fantastic shapes. 风将沙丘雕刻成奇异的形状。

近义词:hills, mounds, ridges
反义词:valleys, depressions

词性:noun

词源:From Middle Dutch *dūne, from Old French dune, from West Germanic *dūnō.
