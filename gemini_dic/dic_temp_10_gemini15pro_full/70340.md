fieldsmen:/ˈfiːldzmən/| n. |外野手，守备员|A cricketer who fields the ball in the outfield.  A player in sports like cricket or baseball who is positioned in the outfield to catch or stop the ball.| The fieldsmen were quick to react to the hard-hit ball. 外野手对猛击的球反应迅速。| The fieldsmen chased the ball to the boundary. 外野手追着球跑到边界。|近义词：fielder, outfielder|反义词:batsman, bowler|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
