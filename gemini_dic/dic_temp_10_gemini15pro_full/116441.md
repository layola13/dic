matching:/ˈmætʃɪŋ/| adj. n.|相配的，匹配的；比赛，竞赛|Something that is matching is similar to something else in color, pattern, or style.  A match is a contest or game. |The curtains and the carpet are matching. 窗帘和地毯很相配。|Synonyms: corresponding, similar, identical, twin, parallel |Antonyms: contrasting, different, dissimilar, clashing|adjective

matching:/ˈmætʃɪŋ/| n.|相配，匹配；比赛，竞赛|The act or process of finding something similar or compatible. A contest, competition, or game. |The matching of fingerprints helped identify the suspect. 指纹匹配帮助确定了嫌疑人。|Synonyms: pairing, coupling, comparing, contest, competition |Antonyms: mismatch, incompatibility, discord|noun
