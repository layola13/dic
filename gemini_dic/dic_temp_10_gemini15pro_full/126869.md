词源: From Middle English *noppe, from Old English *noppa (“burdock”), akin to Middle Low German noppe, nop (“burr, fluff, down”), Dutch nop (“fluff, nap”).  The original sense referred to the fluffy surface of cloth. The meaning "diaper" arose from the use of soft, nappy cloth for this purpose.
