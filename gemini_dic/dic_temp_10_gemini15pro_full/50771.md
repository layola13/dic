desaturation:/diːˌsætʃəˈreɪʃən/
n.|去饱和，脱饱和|The desaturation of color in the photo made it look vintage. 照片中色彩的去饱和使其看起来很复古。|例句：The desaturation of the market led to lower prices.  市场饱和度下降导致价格下跌。

例句：The artist achieved a haunting effect through the desaturation of the painting's colors.  艺术家通过降低画作的色彩饱和度，营造出一种令人难忘的效果。|近义词：bleaching, fading, dulling|反义词：saturation, intensification|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "removal or reduction") + saturation (from Latin saturatio, meaning "a filling").
