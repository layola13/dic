erupt:/ɪˈrʌpt/ (US), /ɪˈrʌpt/ (UK)| vi. vt.|爆发，喷发|To become active and eject lava, ash, and gases, as a volcano; to break out suddenly and violently.|The volcano erupted with tremendous force. 火山以巨大的力量爆发了。|The crowd erupted in cheers when the team scored. 当球队得分时，人群爆发出欢呼声。|近义词:explode, burst, erupt, blow up|反义词:calm, settle, die down|verb

eruptive:/ɪˈrʌptɪv/ (US), /ɪˈrʌptɪv/ (UK)| adj.|爆发的，喷发的|Characterized by or tending to erupt.|The eruptive phase of the volcano lasted for several weeks. 火山的喷发阶段持续了几个星期。|He has an eruptive personality, prone to sudden outbursts of anger. 他性格暴躁，容易突然发怒。|近义词:explosive, volcanic|反义词:calm, peaceful|adjective
