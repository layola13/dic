non-feasible:/ˌnɒnˈfiːzəbl/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈfiːzɪbl/ (UK)| adj.|不可行的，办不到的|Not capable of being done or carried out; impracticable.|The project was deemed non-feasible due to budget constraints.  由于预算限制，该项目被认为不可行。|The proposed solution was non-feasible given the current technology.  鉴于目前的技术，提议的解决方案是不可行的。|近义词：impossible, impracticable, unworkable, unattainable|反义词: feasible, possible, achievable, viable|词性:形容词
词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + feasible (from Old French faisable, from faire "to do," from Latin facere "to make, do")
