ghostlier:/ˈɡoʊstliər/ | adj. | 更鬼魅的，更幽灵般的 | More ghostly; resembling or suggestive of a ghost to a greater degree. | The old house felt ghostlier at night.  这老房子在晚上感觉更鬼魅了。| The abandoned asylum was a ghostlier place than I had imagined.  这废弃的收容所比我想象的更像鬼屋。| 近义词: spookier, eerier, more spectral | 反义词: less ghostly, more earthly, more tangible | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *gostlich, from Old English gāstlīc (“spiritual, ghostly”), from gāst (“ghost, spirit”) +‎ -līc (“-ly”).  来自中古英语 *gostlich，源自古英语 gāstlīc（“精神的，幽灵般的”），由 gāst（“幽灵，精神”）+ -līc（“-ly”）组成。
