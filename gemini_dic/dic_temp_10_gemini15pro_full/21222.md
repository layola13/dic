bloedite:/ˈbluːdaɪt/| n. |布莱迪特，钠镁矾|A hydrous sodium magnesium sulfate mineral, Na₂Mg(SO₄)₂·4H₂O, typically occurring as white to gray or reddish monoclinic crystals.  It is found in evaporite deposits.|Bloedite was discovered in the Chilean saltpeter deposits. 布莱迪特是在智利硝石矿床中发现的。|Synonyms: astrakanite, simonyite|Antonyms: N/A|noun
