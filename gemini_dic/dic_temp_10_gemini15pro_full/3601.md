age-mate:/ˈeɪdʒˌmeɪt/| n.|同龄人；同年出生的人|A person of similar age to another.|例句：She's been my age-mate since kindergarten. 她从幼儿园起就是我的同龄人。

例句：He found comfort in talking to his age-mates about their shared experiences.  他从与同龄人谈论他们的共同经历中找到了慰藉。


近义词：peer, contemporary, cohort|反义词:|词性:noun
