opinionated: /əˈpɪnjəneɪtɪd/
adjective
固执己见的；武断的
Having very strong opinions and beliefs, and refusing to consider other people's opinions or beliefs.
She's very opinionated and dogmatic. 她非常固执己见，而且武断。

He is so opinionated that no one can reason with him. 他太固执己见了，谁也劝不动他。

stubborn, dogmatic, inflexible, biased
open-minded, receptive, flexible

adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From opinion + -ated.  The suffix "-ated" forms adjectives meaning "having the quality of" or "characterized by."
