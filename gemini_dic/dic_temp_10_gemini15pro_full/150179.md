panar:/ˈpænɑːr/ (US), /ˈpænə(r)/ (UK)| n.|帕纳尔，一种皮肤感染|A panar is a skin infection of the finger or toe, typically around the nail.  It involves the soft tissues of the finger or toe, often starting near the nail and potentially spreading to deeper structures.|He developed a panar after getting a splinter in his finger. 他手指里扎了刺后得了帕纳尔。|Near synonyms:  paronychia, felon|Antonyms:  N/A (No direct antonym for a medical condition)|Noun
