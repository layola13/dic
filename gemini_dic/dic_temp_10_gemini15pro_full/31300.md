chaplains:/ˈtʃæplɪnz/
n.
牧师，随军牧师，(机构中的)专职牧师
A chaplain is a member of the clergy attached to a private chapel, institution, ship, branch of the armed forces, etc.
The hospital chaplain offered comfort to the patients.  医院的牧师安慰病人。
近义词:clergyman, minister, pastor, priest
反义词:None.
词性:noun
