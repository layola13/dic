outlung: /ˈaʊtˌlʌŋ/
n.
外肺; 辅助呼吸装置
An external respiratory organ or device that assists breathing.
Example: The scientist developed an artificial outlung for patients with severe lung damage.  科学家为严重肺损伤的病人开发了一种人工外肺。

Example:  The outlung allowed the diver to stay underwater for extended periods. 这种外肺使潜水员能够在水下停留更长时间。

近义词: auxiliary lung, external respirator
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider internal lung/natural lung)
词性: noun
