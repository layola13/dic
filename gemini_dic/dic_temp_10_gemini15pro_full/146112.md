overembroidered:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈbrɔɪdərd/
adjective
过度绣花的；绣花过多的
Decorated with too much embroidery.
The dress was overembroidered, making it look gaudy rather than elegant. 这件衣服绣花过多，看起来很俗气而不是优雅。
overdecorated, over embellished, fussy
plain, simple, understated
词性: 形容词
