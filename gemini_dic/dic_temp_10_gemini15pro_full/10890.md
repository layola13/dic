arachnophobic: /əˌræknəˈfəʊbɪk/ (UK) /əˌræknəˈfoʊbɪk/ (US)| adj.|蜘蛛恐惧症的，患蜘蛛恐惧症的|Having or showing an extreme or irrational fear of spiders.|She's arachnophobic and can't even look at a picture of a spider. 她有蜘蛛恐惧症，甚至连蜘蛛的照片都不能看。|Example:  He is so arachnophobic that he screams if he sees even the smallest spider. 他有严重的蜘蛛恐惧症，即使看到最小的蜘蛛也会尖叫。

Example:  My arachnophobic friend refused to go camping because she was afraid of spiders. 我有蜘蛛恐惧症的朋友拒绝去露营，因为她害怕蜘蛛。|Synonym: spider-fearing|Antonym: None|Word type: Adjective
