ice-skate: /ˈaɪs skeɪt/ (US), /ˈaɪs skeɪt/ (UK)|vi. vt.|滑冰|To move on ice using ice skates.|例句：She loves to ice-skate in the winter. 她喜欢在冬天滑冰。|近义词：skate|反义词: N/A|词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "ice" + "skate" (originally referring to a skate, a fish, then to a metal runner for a sled, and later to footwear with blades for gliding on ice).
