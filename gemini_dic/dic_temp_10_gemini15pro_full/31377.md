charcoaling: /ˈtʃɑːrkoʊlɪŋ/ | n. | 炭化，烧成炭 | The process of converting wood or other organic matter into charcoal by heating in the absence of air. |  The artist used charcoaling to create a dramatic effect in his landscape drawing.  艺术家在他的风景画中使用炭化技术创造了一种戏剧性的效果。 | synonyms: carbonization, charring | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for the process itself, potentially "combustion" if considering full burning vs. charring) | noun

charcoaling: /ˈtʃɑːrkoʊlɪŋ/ | v. | 用木炭画，烧成炭 |  To draw or sketch with charcoal; to convert into charcoal.  | She was charcoaling a portrait of her grandmother. 她正在用木炭画她祖母的肖像。 | synonyms: sketching, drawing, carbonizing | antonyms: (No direct antonym, possibly "erasing" in the context of drawing) | verb
