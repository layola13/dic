decivilizes:/diːˈsɪvɪlaɪzɪz/
verb
使丧失文明，使野蛮化
To cause someone or something to lose the qualities and behaviors considered civilized; to become less civilized.
The prolonged war has decivilized the entire region, leading to widespread violence and a breakdown of social order.  长期战争使整个地区丧失了文明，导致暴力泛滥和社会秩序崩溃。
Exposure to extreme violence can decivilize even the most gentle soul. 接触极端暴力即使是最温柔的灵魂也会变得野蛮。
近义词: barbarize, brutalize
反义词: civilize, refine
词性: verb
