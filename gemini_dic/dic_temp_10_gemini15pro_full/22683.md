borders:/ˈbɔːrdərz/ (UK) /ˈbɔːrdərz/ (US)| n. |边界；边境；国界；界限|A border is a line separating two countries, administrative divisions, or other areas.|The two countries have had several border disputes. 这两个国家曾多次发生边界争端。|synonyms: boundary, frontier, limit, edge, rim, margin|antonyms: center, interior, middle|noun

borders:/ˈbɔːrdərz/ (UK) /ˈbɔːrdərz/ (US)| v. |接壤；毗邻；接近；类似|If one area or thing borders another, it is next to it or touches it along one side.|France borders Spain to the south. 法国南部与西班牙接壤。|synonyms: adjoin, abut, neighbor, verge on, approximate|antonyms: separate, divide, detach|verb
