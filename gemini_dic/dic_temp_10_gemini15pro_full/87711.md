hexastich:/ˈhɛksəstɪk/
n.
六行诗; 六句诗
A poem or stanza of six lines.
Edgar Allan Poe's poem "Lenore" is a hexastich. 埃德加·爱伦·坡的诗《勒诺尔》是一首六行诗。
synonym: sestet, sixain
antonym: (No direct antonym, but could contrast with couplet, quatrain, octave, etc.)
noun
