expeditions:/ˌɛkspəˈdɪʃənz/| n. |远征；探险；考察|A journey undertaken by a group of people with a particular purpose, especially that of exploration, research, or war.|例句：The team mounted several expeditions to the Himalayas. 该团队组织了几次喜马拉雅山探险。|例句：Several expeditions were sent to explore the Antarctic.  几支探险队被派去探索南极洲。|近义词：adventures, journeys, quests, voyages|反义词：stays, inactivity|词性：名词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *expedicioun, from Old French expedition and directly from Latin expedītiō (“a setting free, disentangling; dispatch, expedition”), from expedītus, past participle of expedīre (“to disentangle, free, extricate, liberate; to procure, make ready, prepare, put in order; to dispatch, expedite”).
