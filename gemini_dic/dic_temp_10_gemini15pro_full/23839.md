breadboard:/ˈbredˌbɔːrd/ | n. | 面包板 |A board used for making temporary electrical connections, typically used for prototyping electronic circuits.  It has many small holes that components can be plugged into. |I used a breadboard to test my circuit before soldering it. 我在焊接电路之前用面包板测试了我的电路。| synonyms: prototype board, circuit board | antonyms: printed circuit board (PCB) | noun

breadboards:/ˈbredˌbɔːrds/ | n. | 面包板（复数）| Multiple boards used for making temporary electrical connections, typically used for prototyping electronic circuits. | I bought several breadboards to work on different projects simultaneously. 我买了几个面包板来同时进行不同的项目。| synonyms: prototype boards, circuit boards | antonyms: printed circuit boards (PCBs) | noun
