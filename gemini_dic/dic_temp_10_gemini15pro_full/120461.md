minorities:/maɪˈnɔːrətiz/ /maɪˈnɑːrətiz/
n.
少数民族；少数群体；少数派
A group of people who differ racially or politically from a larger group of which it is a part.
例句：Ethnic minorities make up 15% of the population. 少数民族占人口的15%。

例句：The government is working to protect the rights of minorities. 政府正在努力保护少数群体的权利。

近义词：minority groups, marginalized groups
反义词：majority, mainstream

词性: 名词(plural noun)
