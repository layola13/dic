incrassation:/ɪnˌkræˈseɪʃən/
n.
增厚；加厚；浓缩
the act or process of thickening or becoming thick.
The incrassation of the arterial walls can lead to heart disease. 动脉壁的增厚会导致心脏病。
thickening, condensation, inspissation
thinning, attenuation, rarefaction
noun


incrassate:/ɪnˈkræsˌeɪt/
v.
使加厚；使浓缩
to make thicker or more dense.
The sauce incrassated as it simmered on the stove. 酱汁在炉子上炖的时候变浓了。
thicken, condense, inspissate
thin, dilute, rarefy
verb
