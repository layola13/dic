impishly:/ˈɪmpɪʃli/| adv.|顽皮地，淘气地|In a mischievous or playful manner, often suggesting a harmless or amusing naughtiness.|He grinned impishly and hid her book. 他顽皮地咧嘴一笑，藏起了她的书。|近义词：mischievously, playfully, naughtily|反义词：seriously, solemnly|词性:副词
词源: From imp + -ish + -ly.  "Imp" comes from Old English *ympe, from Proto-Germanic *umpiz.
