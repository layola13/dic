holeless:/ˈhoʊllɪs/| adj.|无孔的，没有洞的|Without holes; having no holes. |This fabric is holeless, guaranteeing no leaks.  这种织物没有孔，保证不会漏。|The balloon remained surprisingly holeless despite the sharp branches.  尽管树枝很锋利，但气球竟然没有破损。|近义词：imperforate, seamless, unbroken|反义词：perforated, porous, leaky|词性:形容词
词源: "hole" + "-less"  "hole" 源于古英语 "hol," 指的是一个空腔或开口。"-less" 是一个古英语后缀，表示“没有”或“缺乏”。
