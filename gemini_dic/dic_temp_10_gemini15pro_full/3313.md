affluently: /ˈæfluəntli/ | adv. | 富裕地，富足地 | In a manner characteristic of wealthy people; richly.  |  They lived affluently, with a large house and several luxury cars.  他们过着富裕的生活，拥有一栋大房子和几辆豪车。 |  例句：She dressed affluently in designer clothes. 她穿着名牌服装，显得十分富裕。 | 近义词: richly, opulently, luxuriously | 反义词: poorly, meagerly, indigently | 词性: 副词

affluent: /ˈæfluənt/ | adj. | 富裕的，丰富的 | Having a great deal of money; wealthy. | The affluent neighborhood was known for its large houses and manicured lawns.  这个富裕的街区以其宽敞的房屋和修剪整齐的草坪而闻名。 | 例句：He comes from an affluent family. 他来自一个富裕的家庭。 | 近义词: wealthy, rich, prosperous | 反义词: poor, impoverished, destitute | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *affluentem (nominative affluens) "abundant, copious, flowing to," present participle of affluere "to flow to," from ad- "to" (see ad-) + fluere "to flow" (see fluent). The economic sense emerged in English late 18c.
