dolcemente:/doltʃeˈmɛnte/ | adv. | 甜美地；温柔地；轻柔地 |  Italian word meaning "sweetly," "softly," or "gently," used as a musical direction to indicate a tender and expressive style of playing. | Play the piece dolcemente.  演奏这首曲子要温柔地。| Synonyms: sweetly, softly, gently, tenderly | Antonyms: harshly, loudly, roughly | adverb
