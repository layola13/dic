otiosely:/ˈoʊʃiəsli/ | adv. | 无用地，多余地，懒散地 | In an idle or useless manner; unnecessarily.  Serving no practical purpose. | He otiosely flipped through the channels, unable to find anything interesting. 他百无聊赖地翻着电视频道，找不到任何有趣的节目。| 近义词: unnecessarily, pointlessly, needlessly, idly, uselessly | 反义词: usefully, productively, necessarily, purposefully, diligently | 词性: 副词

词源:
From Latin *otiosus* "at leisure, idle," from *otium* "leisure, ease."
