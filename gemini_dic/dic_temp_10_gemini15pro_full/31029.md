cham:/tʃæm/ | n. | 坎儿，裂缝，斜面 | A cham is a bevel or slope, especially one on the edge of a piece of metal or wood. | The carpenter planed a cham on the edge of the board. 木匠在木板边缘刨了一个斜面。| 近义词: bevel, slope, edge | 反义词: flat | 词性: 名词

cham:/tʃæm/ | v. | 给…加斜边，给…加坎儿 | To cham means to bevel or slope the edge of something. | He chammed the edges of the table to make it safer. 他把桌子的边缘削成斜面以使其更安全。| 近义词: bevel, slope | 反义词: flatten | 词性: 动词
