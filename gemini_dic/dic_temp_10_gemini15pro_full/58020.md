drizzly:/ˈdrɪzli/ | adj. | 毛毛雨的，细雨蒙蒙的 | Characterized by light rain; raining lightly. |  It was a cold and drizzly afternoon. 那是一个寒冷又下着毛毛雨的下午。| 近义词:rainy, damp, wet | 反义词:sunny, dry | 词性:形容词

drizzle:/ˈdrɪzl/ | n. | 毛毛雨，细雨 | Light rain falling in very fine drops. |  A light drizzle began to fall. 开始下起了毛毛雨。| 近义词:sprinkle, mist | 反义词:downpour, deluge | 词性:名词

drizzle:/ˈdrɪzl/ | v. | 下毛毛雨 | To rain lightly. | It's starting to drizzle. 开始下毛毛雨了。| 近义词:sprinkle, spit | 反义词:pour, downpour | 词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *drisle, frequentative of dreosan "to fall," from Proto-Germanic *dreusanan, from Proto-Indo-European *dhreus- "to fall, crumble, drip."
