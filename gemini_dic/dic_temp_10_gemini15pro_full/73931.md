forte:/ˈfɔːrteɪ/ (US) /ˈfɔːteɪ/ (UK)| n. |特长，强项|Something at which someone excels; a strong point; a special talent or skill.|Playing the piano is her forte. 弹钢琴是她的强项。|近义词：strength, talent, aptitude, gift, skill, specialty|反义词：weakness, shortcoming|noun


forte:/ˈfɔːteɪ/ (US) /ˈfɔːteɪ/ (UK)| adv. |(音乐)强音地，响亮地|Loudly; with force (used chiefly as a musical direction).|The orchestra played the final movement forte. 管弦乐队强音演奏了最后乐章。|近义词：loudly, powerfully|反义词：piano (softly)|adverb
