gibingly:/ˈdʒaɪbɪŋli/
adverb
嘲弄地，讥讽地
in a way that is designed to make someone seem silly
He gibingly referred to her as "the expert." 他讥讽地称她为“专家”。
近义词: mockingly, sarcastically, derisively, tauntingly
反义词: respectfully, seriously, sincerely
词性: adverb

词源:
From gibe (verb) + -ing + -ly.  The verb "gibe" comes from the Middle English "geben,"  possibly of Scandinavian origin, related to Old Norse "geipa" (to gape, open wide).
