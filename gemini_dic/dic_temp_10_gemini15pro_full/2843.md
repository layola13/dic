adventurists:/ədˈvɛntʃərɪsts/
noun
冒险家；冒险主义者
People who seek out and enjoy adventures, often involving some degree of risk or danger.
The group of adventurers set off to explore the uncharted jungle.  这群冒险家出发去探索未知的丛林。

The adventurists planned to climb Mount Everest without oxygen. 这些冒险家计划不带氧气攀登珠穆朗玛峰。

近义词:daredevils, thrill-seekers, explorers, pioneers
反义词:cautious people, conservatives, timid people
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From adventure + -ist.  "Adventure" comes from Old French "aventure"  meaning "that which happens by chance," ultimately from Latin "adventura"  "a thing about to happen."  The suffix "-ist" denotes a person who practices or is devoted to something.
