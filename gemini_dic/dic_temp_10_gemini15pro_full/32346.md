childish:/ˈtʃaɪldɪʃ/ (US), /ˈtʃaɪldɪʃ/ (UK)| adj.|幼稚的，孩子气的|Behaving in a way that is typical of a young child, and not as an adult should.|Stop being so childish! 别这么幼稚！|近义词：silly, foolish, inane|反义词：sensible, responsible, mature|词性:形容词
