glycogenesis:/ˌɡlaɪkəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
n.
糖原生成，糖原合成
The process of glycogen formation, in which glucose molecules are added to chains of glycogen for storage.
Glycogenesis is essential for maintaining blood glucose levels between meals. 糖原生成对于维持两餐之间的血糖水平至关重要。
The liver plays a key role in glycogenesis. 肝脏在糖原生成中起关键作用。
近义词:glycogen formation
反义词:glycogenolysis
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From glyco- + genesis.  Glyco- comes from Greek glukus "sweet". Genesis comes from Greek genesis "origin, creation, generation".
