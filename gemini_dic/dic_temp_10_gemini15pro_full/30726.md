cervantite:/sərˈvæntaɪt/
n.
锑华，锑氧化物
A yellowish mineral consisting of antimony tetroxide (Sb₂O₄).  It is formed by the oxidation of stibnite.
Cervantite is often found in association with stibnite deposits. 锑华经常与辉锑矿矿床伴生。
Synonym: antimony tetroxide, stibiconite
