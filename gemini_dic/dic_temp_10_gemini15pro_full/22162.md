bombardments:/bɒmˈbɑːdmənts/ (UK) /bɑːmˈbɑːrdmənts/ (US)| n.|轰炸，炮击|A bombardment is a continuous attack with bombs, shells, or other missiles.|The city was under heavy bombardment for days.  这座城市连续数日遭到猛烈轰炸。|The relentless bombardments destroyed the city's infrastructure.  无情的轰炸摧毁了这座城市的基础设施。|Synonyms: shelling, barrage, cannonade|Antonyms: peace, truce|Noun
