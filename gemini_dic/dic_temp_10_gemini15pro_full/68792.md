farthingsworth:/ˈfɑːrðɪŋzwɜːrθ/| n.|少量，一点点，极少量的|A very small amount of something, especially something of little value. | I bought a farthingsworth of candy. 我买了一点点糖果。|Synonyms: trifle, pittance, modicum, smidgen|Antonyms: abundance, plenty, lot|noun
Etymology: From "farthing" (a former British coin of very low value) + "worth" (the value of something).
