exequatur:/ˌeksɪˈkwɑːtər/ | n. | 执行令；许可执行外国判决的命令 | An official order by which a judge allows a decision made in a foreign court to be enforced in their own country. | The foreign court issued the judgment, but it required an exequatur from the local court before it could be enforced. 外国法院作出了判决，但在执行之前需要当地法院的执行令。 | Example 2:  The lawyer filed for an exequatur so his client could collect damages awarded in a foreign court. 律师申请了执行令，以便他的客户可以收取外国法院判给的损害赔偿金。 | synonym: authorization, permission, warrant | antonym: prohibition, injunction | noun
