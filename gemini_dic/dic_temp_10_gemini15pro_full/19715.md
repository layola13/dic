biliousness:/ˈbɪliəsnəs/| n.|恶心，反胃，脾气坏|A state of nausea and vomiting, often accompanied by a bad temper, traditionally attributed to an excess of bile.|She complained of biliousness after eating the rich dessert.|近义词：nausea, queasiness, indigestion, irritability, grumpiness|反义词:good temper, calmness, wellness|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "bilious" + "-ness".  "Bilious" comes from Late Latin "biliosus", from Latin "bilis" (bile). The connection to bad temper stems from the ancient belief that an excess of bile caused irritability.
