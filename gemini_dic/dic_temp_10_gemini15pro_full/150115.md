pampered:/ˈpæmpərd/ | adj. | 娇惯的，纵容的 | Treated with excessive indulgence; spoiled. |  The pampered child refused to eat anything but ice cream.  这个被娇惯的孩子除了冰淇淋什么都不吃。 | Synonyms: spoiled, indulged, coddled, cosseted | Antonyms: neglected, deprived, hardened | adjective

pamper:/ˈpæmpər/ | v. | 娇惯，纵容 | Indulge with every attention, comfort, and kindness; spoil. | She pampered her dog with expensive treats and toys. 她用昂贵的零食和玩具娇惯她的狗。| Synonyms: indulge, spoil, coddle, cosset | Antonyms: neglect, deprive | verb
