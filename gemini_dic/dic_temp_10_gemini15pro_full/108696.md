leaky:/ˈliːki/| adj.|漏的；有漏洞的|Allowing liquid or gas to leak out.|The roof is leaky and needs to be repaired. 屋顶漏水，需要修理。|近义词：porous, permeable, dripping|反义词:watertight, sealed|adjective

leaky:/ˈliːki/| adj.|漏的；有漏洞的|Tending to reveal confidential information unintentionally. |He's got a leaky mouth – don't tell him any secrets.  他嘴巴不严，别告诉他任何秘密。|近义词：indiscreet, gossipy|反义词:tight-lipped, discreet|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From leak + -y.  "Leak" comes from Middle English leken, from Old Norse leka "to leak, drip," from Proto-Germanic *lekjanan.
