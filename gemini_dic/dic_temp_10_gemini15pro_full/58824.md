dupatta:/dʊˈpʌtə/ | n. |  围巾，披巾，长巾 (尤指南亚女性服饰) | A long scarf or shawl worn by women, especially in South Asia, typically over the shoulders and head. |  She wore a beautiful silk dupatta with her salwar kameez. 她穿了一件漂亮的丝绸披巾搭配她的莎丽卡米兹。


She draped the dupatta over her head as a sign of respect. 她把披巾披在头上以示尊重。

| 近义词: scarf, shawl, stole | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: 名词
