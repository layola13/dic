echinozoa:/ˌekɪnoʊˈzoʊə/ | n. | 棘皮动物门 |  A major group of invertebrate deuterostome animals, characterized by radial symmetry, typically pentamerous, and a water vascular system.  Includes sea urchins, starfish, brittle stars, sea cucumbers, and feather stars. |  Echinozoa are an important part of marine ecosystems. 棘皮动物是海洋生态系统的重要组成部分。 |  The echinozoa exhibit a remarkable diversity of forms. 棘皮动物表现出惊人的形态多样性。 |近义词: deuterostome (as a broad classification) | 反义词: None (as it's a specific taxonomic group) | 词性: noun


echinozoan:/ˌekɪnoʊˈzoʊən/ | adj. n. | 棘皮动物的；棘皮动物 | Relating to or belonging to the Echinozoa; a member of the Echinozoa. |  Echinozoan larvae are bilaterally symmetrical. 棘皮动物的幼虫是左右对称的。 | The museum has a fascinating exhibit of echinozoan fossils.  博物馆里有一个引人入胜的棘皮动物化石展览。|近义词: deuterostome (as a broad classification for the adjective), echinoderm (partially overlapping) | 反义词: None (as it's a specific taxonomic group) | 词性: adjective, noun
