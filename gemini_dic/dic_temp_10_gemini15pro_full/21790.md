bobble:/ˈbɒbəl/ (UK) /ˈbɑːbəl/ (US)| n. v.|小绒球，(尤指帽子上的)；(棒球)漏接；失误；笨拙的错误|A small ball of wool or other soft material, often used as a decoration, especially on a hat.  (n.); To make a clumsy mistake, especially by dropping a ball in a sport. (v.)|例句：The baby's hat had a cute little bobble on top.  婴儿的帽子顶部有一个可爱的小绒球。

She bobbled the catch and the other team scored. 她漏接了球，对方得分了。|近义词：(n.) pom-pom, tassel; (v.) fumble, blunder|反义词: (v.) catch, grab|词性:noun, verb
