interclashed:/ˌɪntərˈklæʃt/
verb
互相冲突，互相撞击
Two rival gangs interclashed in the streets. 两个敌对帮派在街上发生了冲突。
The armies interclashed on the battlefield. 两军在战场上交锋。
近义词: clash, collide, conflict
反义词: harmonize, agree, cooperate
词性: v.

Etymology:
From inter- + clash.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between," "among," "mutually," or "reciprocally." "Clash" comes from the Middle French word "clascher," meaning "to make a sharp noise."
