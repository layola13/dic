metaplast:/ˈmɛtəˌplæst/
noun
后生质体
A cytoplasmic inclusion, such as a pigment granule, not forming a part of the living protoplasm.
Red blood cells contain metaplasts like hemoglobin. 红细胞包含像血红蛋白这样的后生质体。
Synonyms: inclusion, granule
Antonyms: protoplasm, cytoplasm
