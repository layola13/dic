delightingly:/dɪˈlaɪtfʊli/| adv.|令人愉快地；欢快地|In a way that gives great pleasure.|She sang delightfully. 她唱得令人愉快。|近义词：pleasantly, charmingly, wonderfully|反义词：unpleasantly, horribly, dreadfully|词性:副词

词源: From delight (n.) + -ing + -ly.  "Delight" comes from Old French "deleiter" and ultimately from Latin "delectare," meaning "to allure, charm."
