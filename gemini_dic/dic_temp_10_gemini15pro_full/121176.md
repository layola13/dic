misdirected:/ˌmɪsdɪˈrɛktɪd/
adjective
误导的；指向错误的
directed wrongly or to a wrong address.
The misdirected package finally arrived a month later.  这个寄错地址的包裹最终在一个月后到达了。
The misdirected marketing campaign alienated their target audience.  这场方向错误的营销活动疏远了他们的目标受众。
近义词:mistaken, misguided, erroneous, inaccurate
反义词:accurate, correct, proper, appropriate
词性:adjective

词源:
From mis- + directed, past participle of direct.  由mis-（错误地）+ directed（direct的过去分词，意为指导）组成。
