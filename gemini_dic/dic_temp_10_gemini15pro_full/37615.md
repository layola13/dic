communion: /kəˈmjuːnjən/ | n. | (尤指基督教的)圣餐；领圣餐 | A Christian sacrament in which consecrated bread and wine are consumed as memorials of Christ's death or as symbols for the spiritual union between Christ and communicants or as the body and blood of Christ.  | They took communion together at Easter. 他们在复活节一起领圣餐。 | synonyms: Eucharist, Lord's Supper | antonyms: N/A | noun
