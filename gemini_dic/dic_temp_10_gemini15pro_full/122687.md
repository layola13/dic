moelline:/ˈmoʊɛlin/ | n. | 莫林，软脂酸甘油酯 | A white, crystalline fat, a glyceride of palmitic acid, extracted from tallow and other fats. | Moelline is used in some cosmetics. 莫林用于一些化妆品中。 | 近义词: palmitin, tripalmitin | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific chemical compound) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From palmitic acid, named after the palm tree from which it was originally derived.
