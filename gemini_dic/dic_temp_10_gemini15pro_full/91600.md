hyperaggressiveness:/ˌhaɪpərəˈɡrɛsɪvnəs/| n. |过度侵略性，极度攻击性|The state of being excessively aggressive, characterized by frequent or extreme displays of hostility, anger, or violence.|His hyperaggressiveness led to several fights at school. 他的过度侵略性导致在学校打了好几次架。|Synonyms: belligerence, combativeness, pugnacity|Antonyms: passiveness, docility, meekness|noun
