nonburdensomeness:/ˌnɒnbɜːrdənsəmˈnɛs/ | n. | 无负担性；不构成负担的状态 | The state or quality of not being a burden; ease or lack of difficulty. |  The ethical principle of nonburdensomeness emphasizes the importance of minimizing the burden on others, especially in medical contexts.  无负担性伦理原则强调尽量减少给他人造成的负担，尤其是在医疗环境中。

Her nonburdensomeness made her a welcome guest in any home. 她的不构成负担让她在任何家庭都受到欢迎。| 近义词：easiness, lightness, effortlessness | 反义词：burdensomeness, onerousness, difficulty | 词性：noun
