nondeformities:/ˌnɒndiˈfɔːmətiz/ |n.| 无畸形，无变形 |The absence of deformities; the state of being normally shaped or formed.  This term emphasizes the presence of normal anatomical structure. |The surgeon noted the nondeformities of the patient's hands after the accident.  手术注意到病人手在事故后没有畸形。 | Synonyms: normality, soundness, integrity | Antonyms: deformities, malformations, abnormalities | noun
