cohabiters:/kəʊˈhæbɪtəz/ (UK) /koʊˈhæbɪtərz/ (US)| n. |同居者|People who live together, especially as an unmarried couple.|Example: The number of cohabiters has increased significantly in recent years. 近年来，同居者的数量显著增加。|Example:  Many landlords prefer not to rent to cohabiters. 许多房东不愿意把房子租给同居者。|近义词：partners, roommates, housemates|反义词: spouse, married couple|Noun

cohabiter:/kəʊˈhæbɪtə(r)/ (UK) /koʊˈhæbɪtər/ (US)| n. |同居者|A person who lives with another, especially as an unmarried couple.|Example: My sister is a cohabiter with her boyfriend. 我姐姐和她的男朋友同居。|Example: The landlord asked the cohabiter to sign the lease. 房东要求同居者签署租约。|近义词：partner, roommate, housemate|反义词: spouse, married couple|Noun
