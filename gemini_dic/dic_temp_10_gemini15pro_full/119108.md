metromania: /ˌmɛtroʊˈmeɪniə/ | n. | 地铁狂热，迷恋地铁 | An obsession with subways or underground railway systems.  A fascination, sometimes excessive, with all aspects of metro systems, including their history, technology, and routes. | He has a serious case of metromania; he spends hours studying subway maps of cities he's never even visited. 他患有严重的地铁狂热症；他花了几个小时研究他从未去过的城市的地铁线路图。|  Example:  She indulged her metromania by taking photographs of every station in the London Underground. 她沉迷于自己的地铁狂热，拍摄了伦敦地铁每个车站的照片。| 近义词: trainspotting (in a broader sense) | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) indifference to public transport | 词性: noun
