forgo:/fɔːrˈɡoʊ/ (US), /fɔːˈɡəʊ/ (UK)| v. |放弃，摒弃|To abstain from; to give up (something pleasant or valuable).|She decided to forgo dessert to save calories. 她决定不吃甜点以减少卡路里摄入。

He had to forgo his vacation to finish the project.  他不得不放弃假期来完成这个项目。

|近义词：relinquish, sacrifice, abstain from, waive, eschew|反义词：indulge in, partake of, embrace|词性：动词

词源：
