crenated:/ˈkrɛɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
圆齿状的，锯齿状的; (指细胞)缩水的
Having a finely notched or scalloped edge, like a leaf; (of a cell) shrunk or notched, as by the loss of water.
The crenated edges of the leaf were a distinctive characteristic.  这片叶子的圆齿状边缘是一个显著的特征。
Red blood cells placed in a hypertonic solution become crenated.  红细胞放在高渗溶液中会缩水。
近义词:scalloped, notched, serrated, wrinkled (for cells)
反义词:smooth, turgid (for cells)
词性:adjective

crenation:/krɪˈneɪʃən/
noun
圆齿状，锯齿状; (指细胞)缩水
The process of becoming crenated; the state of being crenated.
Crenation of the red blood cells was observed under the microscope. 在显微镜下观察到红细胞的缩水。
The crenation of the leaf margin is a key identification feature. 叶缘的锯齿状是关键的识别特征。
近义词:scalloping, notching, serration, plasmolysis (for cells)
反义词:smoothing, turgidity (for cells)
词性:noun


词源: From Latin *crenatus, past participle of crenare "to notch, indent," related to crena "notch."
