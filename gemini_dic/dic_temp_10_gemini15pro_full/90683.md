humbuggable:/hʌmˈbʌɡəbəl/
adjective
可欺骗的, 易受骗的
Susceptible to being humbugged; easily deceived or tricked.
That gullible tourist was completely humbuggable and fell for every scam. 那个容易上当的游客完全容易受骗，落入了每一个骗局。
Synonyms: gullible, naive, credulous, unsuspecting
Antonyms: discerning, skeptical, astute, shrewd
词性: 形容词
