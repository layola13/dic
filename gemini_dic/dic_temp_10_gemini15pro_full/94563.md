impotents:/ˈɪmpətənts/| n.|阳痿患者，无能的人|A person, especially a man, who is impotent. impotent:/ˈɪmpətənt/| adj.|无能的，阳痿的|Unable to take effective action; powerless.  (of a man) unable to achieve or maintain an erection.
