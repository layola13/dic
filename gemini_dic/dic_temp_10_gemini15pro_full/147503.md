overpackaging:/ˌoʊvərˈpækɪdʒɪŋ/
noun
过度包装
Excessive use of packaging materials, often beyond what is necessary to protect the product.
The company has been criticized for its overpackaging of products.  这家公司因产品过度包装而受到批评。
Excessive packaging contributes to environmental problems. 过度包装会导致环境问题。
近义词: excess packaging, wasteful packaging
反义词: minimal packaging, eco-friendly packaging
词性: noun

overpackage:/ˌoʊvərˈpækɪdʒ/
verb
过度包装
To package something with an excessive amount of materials.
Many companies overpackage their goods to make them appear more luxurious. 许多公司过度包装他们的商品以使其看起来更奢华。
The toy was overpackaged in multiple layers of plastic and cardboard.  这个玩具被多层塑料和纸板过度包装。
近义词: overwrap
反义词: underpackage
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive, too much") + "packaging" (noun derived from "package")
"over-" 源于古英语 "ofer"，意为 "超过，在...之上"。 "packaging" 源于 "package"，来自法语 "pacquet"。
