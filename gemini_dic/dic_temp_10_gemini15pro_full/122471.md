moblog:/ˈmoʊblɒɡ/ (US), /ˈməʊblɒɡ/ (UK)| n.|手机博客，移动博客|A blog that is updated via a mobile device, typically a cell phone. It allows users to post text, photos, and videos directly to the web from their phones.|She started a moblog to document her travels across Europe. 她创建了一个手机博客来记录她穿越欧洲的旅行。|近义词：mobile blog, photoblog, microblog|反义词:blog (in a broader sense, as it's not specifically mobile)|noun
