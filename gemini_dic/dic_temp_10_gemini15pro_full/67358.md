extension:/ɪkˈstenʃən/ (US) /ɪkˈstɛnʃən/ (UK)| n.| 延伸；扩展；延期；电话分机；（房屋的）扩建部分|An extension is a part that is added to something to make it bigger or longer.  It can also refer to an act of making something last longer, such as a deadline.|

例句：
The kitchen extension has added much needed space.  厨房的扩建部分增加了急需的空间。

They applied for an extension on their loan.  他们申请了贷款延期。


近义词：expansion, enlargement, prolongation, add-on, annex
反义词:reduction, shortening, abbreviation
词性:noun

extension:/ɪkˈstenʃən/ (US) /ɪkˈstɛnʃən/ (UK)| adj.| 延伸的；扩展的|Relating to extending or expanding something.|

例句：
He's taking extension courses at the local college. 他在当地学院修读进修课程。

This software has extension capabilities.  这款软件具有扩展功能。


近义词：expanded, enlarged, prolonged
反义词:reduced, shortened, abbreviated
词性:adjective
