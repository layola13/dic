heliogravure:/ˌhiːliəˈɡrævjʊər/ | n. | 凹版照相术；影印版画 | A printing process that involves etching an image onto a copper plate, which is then used to print high-quality reproductions. | The artist specialized in heliogravures, creating stunningly detailed prints. 这位艺术家专攻凹版照相术，创作出精美绝伦的版画。 | synonyms: photogravure, rotogravure | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps simpler printing methods like lithography or screen printing could be considered) | noun
