electromedical:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈmɛdɪkəl/ | adj. | 电医学的；医疗电子的 | Relating to the use of electricity in medicine. | Electromedical equipment is essential in modern hospitals. 现代医院里医疗电子设备至关重要。|  The development of electromedical devices has revolutionized healthcare. 医疗电子设备的发展彻底改变了医疗保健。 | 近义词:biomedical | 反义词: N/A | 词性:形容词


Etymology:
electro- (prefix): from Greek *ēlektron, meaning "amber," referring to static electricity.
medical (adjective): from Latin medicus, meaning "physician," relating to the practice of medicine.
