disciplinarian:/ˌdɪsəplɪˈnɛəriən/

discipline:/ˈdɪsəplɪn/
|n.|纪律，训练；学科；惩罚；纪律处分|A controlled form of behavior or way of working. |  Military discipline is very strict. 军队纪律非常严格。| synonym: order, control, training, punishment | antonym: chaos, disorder, indulgence | noun
|vt.|训练；惩罚| To train someone to follow rules or a code of behavior, using punishment to correct disobedience. | She disciplined her child for misbehaving. 她因为孩子行为不端而惩罚他。 | synonym: punish, chastise, correct, train | antonym: reward, praise, indulge | verb
