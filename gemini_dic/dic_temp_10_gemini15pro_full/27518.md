camphretic:/kæmˈfrɛtɪk/
adjective
樟脑的；似樟脑的
Relating to, or containing camphor; having the smell or taste of camphor.
Camphor has a strong, camphretic odor. 樟脑有一种强烈的樟脑气味。
The liniment had a distinct camphretic smell.  这种药膏有明显的樟脑味。
synonyms: camphoraceous
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, could use words describing opposing smells/tastes, e.g., sweet, floral)
词性: adjective
