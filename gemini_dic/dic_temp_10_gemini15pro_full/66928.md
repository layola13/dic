expending:/ɪkˈspɛndɪŋ/
|v.|花费，支出，消耗|The company is expending large amounts of money on research and development.  这家公司在研发上投入了大量资金。|
|例句：They are expending all their efforts to finish the project on time.  他们正竭尽全力按时完成项目。|
|近义词：spending, using, consuming|
|反义词：saving, conserving, hoarding|
|词性：动词|


expending:/ɪkˈspɛndɪŋ/
|adj.|花费的，消耗的 (通常作为动词的现在分词使用)|  The government is concerned about the expending resources. 政府对日益消耗的资源感到担忧。|
|例句：The expending budget is a cause for concern.  不断增加的预算是令人担忧的原因。|
|近义词：spending, consuming|
|反义词：saving, conserving|
