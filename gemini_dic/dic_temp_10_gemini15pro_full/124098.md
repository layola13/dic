morselize:/ˈmɔːrsəlaɪz/| vt.|摩尔斯电码化，用摩尔斯电码表示|To convert (a message) into Morse code.|He morselized the message and sent it via radio. 他将信息转换成摩尔斯电码，并通过无线电发送。|Example: She morselized the SOS signal. 她用摩尔斯电码发送了SOS信号。|Synonyms: encode, transmit, signal|Antonyms: decode, decipher|Verb
