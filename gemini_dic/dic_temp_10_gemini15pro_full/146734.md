overhostilities:/ˌoʊvərhɑːˈstɪlətiz/ | n. | 过度的敌意；过分的敌对行动 |  Excessive or exaggerated displays of hostility.  It implies actions or behaviors that go beyond what is necessary or appropriate in a conflict or disagreement. | The overhostilities between the two families eventually led to a violent confrontation.  两个家庭之间过度的敌意最终导致了暴力冲突。| The political campaign was marred by overhostilities and personal attacks. 这场政治运动被过度的敌意和人身攻击所破坏。| 近义词：aggression, belligerence, animosity, antagonism | 反义词：peace, amity, friendship, goodwill | 词性：名词


词源： "Over-" (prefix indicating excess) + "hostilities" (noun form of "hostile," meaning unfriendly or antagonistic).  "Hostile" ultimately derives from Latin "hostilis," meaning "of an enemy."
