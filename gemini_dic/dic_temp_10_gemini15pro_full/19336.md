bibliography:/ˌbɪbliˈɒɡrəfi/ /ˌbɪbliˈɑːɡrəfi/| n. |参考书目，文献目录| A list of the books referred to in a scholarly work, or a list of books on a particular subject.| Example: The bibliography at the end of the book lists all the sources the author used. 这本书末尾的参考书目列出了作者使用的所有资料来源。| Example:  She compiled a comprehensive bibliography on the history of the Roman Empire. 她编纂了一份关于罗马帝国历史的综合参考书目。 |Synonyms: list of works cited, references, works consulted|Antonyms: N/A| Noun
