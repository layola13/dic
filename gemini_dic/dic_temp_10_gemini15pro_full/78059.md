geocell:/ˈdʒiːoʊsɛl/| n.|地理单元，地理栅格|A geocell is a geographical area, often rectangular or hexagonal, used in mapping and spatial data analysis. It is a key component in systems like Google's S2 library, allowing for efficient indexing and querying of location-based data.|Geocells are used to divide the Earth's surface into manageable units for analysis. 地理单元用于将地球表面划分为便于分析的可管理单元。|The application uses geocells to quickly locate nearby points of interest.  这个应用程序使用地理单元快速定位附近的兴趣点。|Synonyms: grid cell, spatial cell|Antonyms: N/A|noun
