electrometallurgical:/ɪˌlektroʊˌmɛtəlˈɜːrdʒɪkəl/
adjective
电冶金的
Relating to or involving the use of electricity in the extraction or refining of metals.
Electrometallurgical processes are crucial for producing high-purity metals. 电冶金工艺对于生产高纯度金属至关重要。
New electrometallurgical techniques are being developed to reduce the environmental impact of metal production.  新的电冶金技术正在开发中，以减少金属生产对环境的影响。
近义词:electrolytic
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider traditional smelting methods)
词性:adjective
