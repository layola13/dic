overindexings:/ˌoʊvərˈɪndɛksɪŋz/| n. |过度索引|The act of overindexing, especially in investment, refers to allocating a disproportionately large amount of capital to a particular asset, sector, or market segment compared to its benchmark or overall market representation. This can lead to increased risk if that area underperforms.|The fund manager's overindexings in tech stocks led to significant losses when the bubble burst.  基金经理对科技股的过度索引导致了泡沫破裂时的巨大损失。|Overweighting, Overexposure|Underindexing, Underexposure|noun
