excommunicates:/ˌɛks kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪts/
verb
逐出教会
officially exclude (someone) from participation in the sacraments and services of the Christian Church.
The Pope excommunicated the king. 教皇把国王逐出教会。
Synonyms:exclude, banish, expel, oust, cast out, remove, eject
Antonyms:include, admit
词性:v.
