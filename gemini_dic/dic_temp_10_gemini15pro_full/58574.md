dulcet:/ˈdʌlsɪt/| adj.|甜美的，悦耳的，动听的|Something that is dulcet is pleasing to the ear, sweet and soothing.|Her dulcet tones calmed the crying baby. 她甜美的声音使哭闹的婴儿平静下来。|近义词：melodious, harmonious, sweet, soothing|反义词：harsh, grating, discordant|词性:形容词
词源: From Old French *doucet, from Vulgar Latin *dulcicem, from Latin dulcis (“sweet”).
