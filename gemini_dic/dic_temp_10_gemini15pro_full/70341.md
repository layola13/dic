fieldwork:/ˈfiːldwɜːrk/| n.|实地考察，野外工作|Practical work conducted by a researcher in the natural environment, rather than in a laboratory or office.|例句：The students are doing fieldwork in the Amazon rainforest. 学生们正在亚马逊雨林进行实地考察。|例句：Her fieldwork in Africa lasted for six months. 她在非洲的实地考察持续了六个月。|近义词：research, investigation, study|反义词:lab work, office work|词性:noun

Etymology->
From "field" + "work". "Field" comes from Old English "feld" meaning "open country". "Work" comes from Old English "worc" meaning "work, action, deed".
