flaggers:/ˈflæɡərz/
n.
旗手；信号员；铺路工
People who hold and wave flags, often to signal or direct traffic.  A person who uses flags to signal, direct traffic, or mark a location.
The flaggers directed traffic around the construction zone.  信号员引导车辆绕过施工区域。
The flaggers signaled the runners to stop. 旗手示意跑步者停下来。
signalers, traffic controllers, flagmen
