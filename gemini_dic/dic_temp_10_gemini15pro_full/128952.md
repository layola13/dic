ninjutsus:/nɪnˈdʒʊtsuːz/ | n. | 忍者术；忍术 |  The plural form of "ninjutsu," referring to the skills and techniques practiced by ninjas.  These skills encompass espionage, sabotage, infiltration, assassination, and guerrilla warfare. |  He studied various ninjutsus, including camouflage and silent movement. 他学习了各种忍术，包括伪装和无声移动。 |  Synonyms: ninja skills, ninja techniques, shinobi arts | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, possibly conventional warfare) | noun
