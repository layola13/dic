chiropractors: /ˈkaɪrəʊpræktərz/ (UK) /ˈkaɪrəpræktərz/ (US)| n. |脊椎按摩师|A healthcare professional who diagnoses and treats neuromuscular disorders, especially those of the spine, using manual adjustment and manipulation of the vertebrae.|例句：Many chiropractors use other therapies in addition to spinal manipulation. 许多脊椎按摩师除了脊柱推拿外，还使用其他疗法。

例句：I've been seeing a chiropractor for my back pain. 我一直在看脊椎按摩师治疗我的背痛。|近义词：osteopath, physical therapist|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps conventional doctor/physician)|词性:noun
