cassiar:/ˈkæsiər/ | n. |卡西亚尔|A region in northern British Columbia, Canada, known for its rugged mountains and wilderness.  It is also sometimes used to refer to the Cassiar Highway (Highway 37).|The Cassiar is a beautiful and remote area.  卡西亚尔是一个美丽而偏远的地方。|The Cassiar Highway offers stunning views. 卡西亚尔公路的景色令人叹为观止。|synonyms:  Northern BC,  Northwestern BC |antonyms: Southern BC|noun
