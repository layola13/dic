miraculous: /mɪˈrækjʊləs/ | adj. | 奇迹般的；不可思议的 | Occurring by miracle; supernatural.  |  例句：Her miraculous recovery from the illness astonished everyone. 她奇迹般地从疾病中康复，令所有人惊讶。 | 近义词：amazing, astonishing, extraordinary, phenomenal, remarkable, unbelievable, wonderful | 反义词：ordinary, common, usual, natural | 词性:形容词
miraculous: /mɪˈrækjʊləs/ | adj. | 神奇的；超自然的 |  Suggesting a miracle.  | 例句：The escape of the prisoners was truly miraculous. 囚犯的逃脱真是一个奇迹。 | 近义词：divine, supernatural, preternatural | 反义词：mundane, earthly | 词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin miraculosus "wonderful," from Latin miraculum "wonder, marvel, miracle," from mirari "to wonder, marvel, admire," related to mirus "wonderful, astonishing."
