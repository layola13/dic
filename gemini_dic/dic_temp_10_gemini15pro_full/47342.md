de-sludged:/diːˈslʌdʒd/
verb
除淤；清除污泥
remove sludge from something.
The septic tank was de-sludged last week.  上周化粪池被清除了污泥。
The workers de-sludged the water treatment plant. 工人们清除了水处理厂的污泥。
Synonyms: clean, clear, purge
Antonyms: contaminate, pollute
