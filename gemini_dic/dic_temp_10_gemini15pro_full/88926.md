holdback:/ˈhoʊldbæk/| n.|阻碍，障碍；扣留款；预留部分|Something that hinders or restrains progress or release.|The main holdback on the project was lack of funding.  这个项目的最大障碍是缺乏资金。|近义词：obstacle, impediment, hindrance, restraint, setback|反义词:advantage, benefit|词性:名词

hold back:/hoʊld bæk/| v.|阻挡；抑制；隐瞒；扣留|To restrain, prevent, or delay the progress or release of something; to refrain from expressing or revealing something.|She held back her tears. 她强忍住了眼泪。  The dam held back the floodwaters. 大坝挡住了洪水。|近义词：restrain, suppress, withhold, conceal|反义词:release, express, reveal|词性:动词
