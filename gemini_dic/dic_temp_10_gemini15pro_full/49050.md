dehorn:/diːˈhɔːrn/
verb
去角
To remove the horns of an animal.
The farmer dehorned the cattle to prevent them from injuring each other.  农民为防止牛互相伤害而去除了它们的角。
Synonyms:  remove horns, disbud
Antonyms:  grow horns
词性: 动词


dehorn:/diːˈhɔːrn/
noun
去角
The act of removing the horns of an animal.
The dehorning of the calves was done quickly and efficiently.  小牛的去角过程快速高效。
Synonyms: horn removal, disbudding
Antonyms: horn growth
词性: 名词
