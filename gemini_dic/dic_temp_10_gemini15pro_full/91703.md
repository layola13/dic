hyperconsciousness:/ˌhaɪpərˈkɒnʃəsnəs/| n.|超意识，过度意识|A state of heightened awareness of oneself and one's surroundings, often accompanied by feelings of self-consciousness or anxiety.|She experienced a state of hyperconsciousness after the accident, acutely aware of every sensation.|近义词：self-awareness, heightened awareness, mindfulness|反义词：unconsciousness, unawareness|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- + consciousness.  "Hyper-" is a prefix derived from Greek meaning "over, above, beyond". "Consciousness" comes from Late Middle English, from Old French conscience.
