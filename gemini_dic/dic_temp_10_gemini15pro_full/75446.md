fruitinesses:/ˈfruːtɪnɪsɪz/
n.
果味；果香；水果味
The quality of being fruity, typically in taste or smell.
The wine had a delightful fruitiness. 这款酒有一种令人愉悦的果香味。
The fruitinesses of the various berries mingled in the jam. 各种浆果的果味融合在这果酱中。
近义词: aroma, fragrance, scent, flavor
反义词: blandness, tartness
词性: 名词
