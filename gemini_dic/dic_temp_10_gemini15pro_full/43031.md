cowering:/ˈkaʊərɪŋ/| adj. | 畏缩的，蜷缩的 |  Cringing in fear; shrinking away.  | The dog was cowering in the corner after being scolded.  这只狗被责骂后蜷缩在角落里。 | synonyms: shrinking, recoiling, flinching, trembling | antonyms:  bold, brave, defiant, confident | adjective


cower:/ˈkaʊər/| v. | 畏缩，蜷缩 | To crouch down in fear. |  The children cowered under the table during the thunderstorm.  孩子们在雷雨中蜷缩在桌子底下。 | synonyms: cringe, shrink, recoil, flinch | antonyms:  stand tall, face, confront | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *kura, "to lie bent," related to Old English cūrian, "to cower, bend."
