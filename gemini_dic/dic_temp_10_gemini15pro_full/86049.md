healthy:/ˈhelθi/ (US), /ˈhelθi/ (UK)| adj.|健康的；有益健康的|In good physical and mental condition; conducive to good health.|例句：She has a healthy lifestyle. 她有一个健康的生活方式。|例句：A healthy diet is important for everyone. 健康的饮食对每个人都很重要。|近义词：well, fit, robust, sound, wholesome|反义词：unhealthy, sick, ill, diseased|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *helthy, from Old English hǣlþig (“healthy”), equivalent to health +‎ -y. Cognate with Scots healthy (“healthy”), West Frisian sûn (“healthy”), Dutch heilzaam (“wholesome”), German heilsam (“wholesome”).
