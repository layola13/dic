doricism:/ˈdɔːrɪsɪzəm/
n.
多利安式，多利安风格，质朴的风格
A style of classical Greek architecture characterized by simplicity and strength, exemplified by the Parthenon.  Doricism also refers to a dialect of ancient Greek spoken in Doris and its colonies, and can refer more broadly to the plain, austere, or unadorned style associated with the Dorians.
The temple's Doricism conveyed a sense of strength and stability. 这座寺庙的多利安风格传达出一种力量感和稳定感。
The writer's Doricism reflected his preference for clear, concise prose.  这位作家的多利安风格反映了他对清晰简洁散文的偏好。
synonyms:simplicity, austerity, plainness, starkness
antonyms:ornateness, elaborateness, embellishment
noun
