nonembellished:/ˌnɒnɪmˈbɛlɪʃt/ | adj. | 未加修饰的，朴素的，不加渲染的 |  Not decorated or enhanced; plain and simple.  It comes from "non-" (not) + "embellish" (to decorate or improve). The word "embellish" itself comes from Old French "embellir," from "en-" (in, on) + "bel" (beautiful). | The author presented a nonembellished account of the events. 作者对事件进行了未加渲染的描述。| synonyms: plain, simple, unadorned, unembellished, undecorated, austere, stark | antonyms: embellished, decorated, ornate, elaborate, flamboyant | adjective
