amalgamate:/əˈmælɡəmeɪt/ | v. | 合并，混合 | To combine or unite to form one organization or structure. | The two companies amalgamated to form a larger corporation.  两家公司合并成了一家更大的公司。

The school amalgamated with another nearby. 这所学校与附近的另一所学校合并了。 | 近义词：merge, combine, unite, consolidate | 反义词：separate, divide, split | 词性: 动词

amalgamation:/əˌmælɡəˈmeɪʃn/ | n. | 合并，混合物 | The action, process, or result of combining or uniting. | The amalgamation of the two companies was a success. 两家公司的合并很成功。

The new paint is an amalgamation of several different shades.  这种新油漆是几种不同颜色的混合物。 | 近义词：merger, combination, union, consolidation | 反义词：separation, division, split | 词性: 名词
