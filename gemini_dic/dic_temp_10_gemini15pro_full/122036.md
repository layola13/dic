missus:/ˈmɪsɪz/ (US), /ˈmɪsɪz/ (UK)| n.|太太，夫人，老婆|A term of address for a married woman, used without her given name.|例句：He asked if the missus was home. 他问太太在家吗？

例句：The missus will be pleased to hear that. 夫人听到这个会很高兴的。

近义词：wife, spouse, Mrs.|反义词: Mr. (indirectly)|词性:名词
