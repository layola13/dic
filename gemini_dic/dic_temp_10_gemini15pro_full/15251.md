backgrounds:/ˈbækˌɡraʊndz/ | n. |背景；幕后；经历|The circumstances or events preceding or surrounding something.|

例句：
The candidate's backgrounds in finance made him a strong contender for the job.
这位候选人在金融方面的背景使他成为这份工作的有力竞争者。

例句：
The photograph was taken against a variety of backgrounds.
这张照片是在各种背景下拍摄的。


近义词：histories, experiences, settings, backdrops|
反义词：foregrounds|
词性：名词|


ETYMOLOGY->
From back + ground.  Originally referring to the part of a scene or picture furthest from the viewer.  The metaphorical sense of "past history or experience" developed later.
