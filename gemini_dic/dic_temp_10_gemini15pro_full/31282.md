chapels:/ˈtʃæpəlz/
n.
小教堂，礼拜堂
a small church or a room in a hospital, prison, large private house etc used for Christian worship
The college has two chapels. 这所学院有两个小教堂。
chapels:/ˈtʃæpəlz/
n.
（印刷厂）工会会员
(printing) a meeting of the members of a chapel
The chapel voted to strike. 工会会员投票决定罢工。


近义词:sanctuaries, oratories
反义词:None.
词性:Noun
