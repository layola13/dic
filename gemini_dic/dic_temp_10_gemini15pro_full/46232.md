cystoidean: /ˌsɪstɔɪˈdiːən/ | adj. n. | 囊状的；海百合纲的；海百合纲动物 | Relating to or resembling a cyst or bladder; belonging to the class Cystoidea, an extinct group of echinoderms. |  例句：The cystoidean fossils were found in the limestone. 在石灰岩中发现了海百合纲动物化石。

例句：The researcher specialized in cystoidean morphology.  这位研究员专攻海百合纲动物形态学。 | 近义词：cystiform, bladderlike | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "solid" in some contexts) | 词性: adjective, noun


Etymology: From Greek *kystis* "bladder, pouch" + *-oid* "like, resembling" + *-ean* (adjectival suffix).
