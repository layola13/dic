moro:/ˈmɔːroʊ/ (US), /ˈmɔːrəʊ/ (UK)| n. |摩尔人；一种菲律宾的穆斯林；摩洛菜|A Muslim inhabitant of the Philippines, especially of Mindanao and the Sulu archipelago, or of Borneo. Moro cuisine is the cuisine of the Moro people of the Philippines.|The Moro people have a rich cultural heritage. 摩尔人拥有丰富的文化遗产。|The Moro Islamic Liberation Front signed a peace agreement with the government. 摩洛伊斯兰解放阵线与政府签署了和平协议。|synonyms: Muslim Filipino, Bangsamoro|antonyms: Christian Filipino|noun


moro:/ˈmɔːroʊ/ (US), /ˈmɔːrəʊ/ (UK)| adj. |摩尔人的；摩洛菜的|Relating to the Moro people or their culture, especially their cuisine.|Moro dishes are often rich and flavorful. 摩洛菜肴通常浓郁而美味。|They attended a Moro cultural festival. 他们参加了一个摩洛文化节。|synonyms: Bangsamoro, Muslim Filipino|antonyms: Christian Filipino|adjective
