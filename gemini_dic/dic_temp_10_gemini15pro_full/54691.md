disordinate:/dɪsˈɔːrdɪnət/ | adj. | 无序的，混乱的，紊乱的 | Lacking order or methodical arrangement; irregular, chaotic. |  The disordinate pile of papers on his desk suggested a chaotic mind. 他桌上杂乱无章的文件堆表明他思维混乱。 |  The troops retreated in disordinate haste. 部队慌乱地撤退。 | 近义词: disordered, chaotic, messy, untidy, disorganized | 反义词: orderly, organized, neat, tidy, arranged | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From Late Latin *disordinatus, from Latin dis- + ordinatus (past participle of ordinare ‘to set in order’).
