ostracoda:/ˈɒstrəkɒdə/ (UK), /ˈɑːstrəkɑːdə/ (US)| n. |介形虫，介形纲动物|A small crustacean of the class Ostracoda, having a body enclosed in a hinged bivalve shell.  They are found in both freshwater and marine environments.|例句：Ostracoda are useful indicators of past environmental conditions. 介形虫是过去环境条件的有用指标。

例句：Fossil ostracoda can be used to date sedimentary rocks. 介形虫化石可以用来测定沉积岩的年代。|近义词：seed shrimp (informal)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
