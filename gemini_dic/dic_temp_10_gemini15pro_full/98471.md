instability:/ˌɪnstəˈbɪləti/ | n. |不稳定，不稳定性|The instability of the government caused widespread unrest. 政府的不稳定性造成了广泛的社会动荡。|例句：The political instability makes investment risky. 政治上的不稳定使得投资充满风险。

例句：The instability of the market caused stock prices to fluctuate wildly.  市场的不稳定导致股价剧烈波动。


近义词：volatility, precariousness, insecurity, fluctuation |反义词: stability, firmness, security |词性: 名词
