inklike:/ˈɪŋklaɪk/
adjective
像墨水的；墨色的
Resembling ink in color or consistency.
The dark, inklike stain spread across the tablecloth.  深色的、像墨水一样的污渍蔓延到桌布上。
The squid released a cloud of inklike substance.  鱿鱼释放出一团墨汁状的物质。
Near synonyms: inky, ink-colored, inky-black
Antonyms: colorless, transparent
