crossword:/ˈkrɒswɜːd/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːswɜːrd/ (US)| n. |纵横字谜，填字游戏|A puzzle in which words are fitted into a pattern of numbered squares, by following clues which lead to the answers.|I enjoy doing the crossword in the newspaper every Sunday. 我喜欢每个星期天做报纸上的填字游戏。|Synonyms: puzzle, word game, brainteaser|Antonyms: None|Noun
