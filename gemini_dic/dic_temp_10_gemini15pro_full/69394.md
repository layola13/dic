feeding:/ˈfiːdɪŋ/| n. |喂养; 饲养; 进食; 食物|The act of giving food, especially to animals or babies.  The food itself can also be referred to as feeding.|例句：The feeding of the animals takes place twice a day. 动物们每天喂两次。

例句：The baby's feeding time is every three hours.  这个婴儿每三个小时喂奶一次。|近义词：nourishment, sustenance, provisioning|反义词:starvation, deprivation|noun

feeding:/ˈfiːdɪŋ/| adj. |喂食的; 饲养的; 供给…的|Relating to the act of feeding.|例句：The feeding trough was empty. 食槽是空的。

例句：She works at a feeding station for rescued birds. 她在一个救援鸟类的喂食站工作。|近义词：nutritive, nourishing|反义词:N/A|adjective
