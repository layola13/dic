colorfast:/ˈkʌlərˌfæst/| adj.|不褪色的，耐洗的|Resistant to fading or running of color, especially when washed.|This shirt is colorfast and won't bleed in the wash. 这件衬衫不褪色，洗的时候不会掉色。|Synonyms: non-fading, unfading|Antonyms: fading, non-colorfast|adjective


Etymology->
color + fast.  "fast" in this context means "firmly fixed or attached."  It's related to the Old English "fæst," meaning "firmly fixed, steadfast."
