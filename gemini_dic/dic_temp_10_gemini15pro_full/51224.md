destressing:/diːˈstrɛsɪŋ/
adjective
减压的，缓解压力的
Reducing or relieving stress; relaxing.
Taking a hot bath is destressing after a long day.  泡个热水澡在漫长的一天后可以减压。
近义词: relaxing, calming, soothing, unwinding
反义词: stressing, straining, tiring, aggravating
词性: adjective

destress:/diːˈstrɛs/
verb
减轻压力，放松
To reduce or relieve stress; to relax.
I need to find a way to destress after work. 我需要找到一种在下班后减压的方法。
近义词: relax, unwind, de-stress, chill out
反义词: stress, strain, tense up, worry
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From de- + stress.  De- is a prefix meaning "remove" or "reduce." Stress refers to a state of mental or emotional strain or tension.
