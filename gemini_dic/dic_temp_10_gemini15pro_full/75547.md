fuchsia:/ˈfjuːʃə/ | n. | 倒挂金钟; 紫红色,  灯笼海棠|A shrub with pendulous flowers, typically of a vibrant purplish-red color. Also, the color of these flowers. | The fuchsias in her garden were a riot of color. 她花园里的倒挂金钟色彩缤纷。|Synonyms: magenta, cerise | Antonyms:  (For the color) pale, muted | noun

fuchsias:/ˈfjuːʃəz/ | n. | 倒挂金钟(复数); 紫红色(复数)|Plural form of "fuchsia," referring to multiple fuchsia plants or instances of the color fuchsia. | The fuchsias were hanging in baskets from the porch. 倒挂金钟挂在门廊的篮子里。|Synonyms: magentas, cerises | Antonyms: (For the color) pales, muteds | noun
