distincter:/dɪˈstɪŋktər/
adjective|更清晰的，更明显的|Comparative form of distinct: More clearly separate or different.|The two flavors became distincter as the wine breathed.  两种口味随着葡萄酒的呼吸变得更加分明。|近义词：clearer, more obvious, more definite|反义词：vaguer, less clear, less obvious|词性:形容词

distinct:/dɪˈstɪŋkt/
adjective|清晰的，明显的，不同的|Clearly separate and different; readily distinguishable.|There's a distinct smell of gas in the kitchen. 厨房里有一股明显的煤气味。|近义词：clear, obvious, definite|反义词：vague, unclear, indefinite|词性:形容词

distinctly:/dɪˈstɪŋktli/
adverb|清晰地，明显地，无疑地|In a way that is readily noticeable or different from something else.|I distinctly remember him saying he would come. 我清楚地记得他说过他会来。|近义词：clearly, obviously, definitely|反义词：vaguely, unclearly, indefinitely|词性:副词

distinction:/dɪˈstɪŋkʃn/
noun|区别，差别，荣誉|A difference or contrast between similar things or people.|He makes no distinction between right and wrong. 他不区分是非对错。|近义词：difference, contrast, disparity|反义词：similarity, resemblance|词性:名词
