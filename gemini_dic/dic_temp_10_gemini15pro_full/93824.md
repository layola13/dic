immigrations:/ˌɪmɪˈɡreɪʃənz/
n.
移民（迁入）; 移居入境;
The act of coming to live permanently in a foreign country.
Large-scale immigrations from Europe to America took place in the 19th century.
19世纪发生了大规模的欧洲移民到美洲的情况。

The recent immigrations have significantly increased the city's population.
最近的移民潮显著增加了城市的人口。

近义词: influx, arrival, migration
反义词: emigration, departure

词性: 名词 (Noun)
