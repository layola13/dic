daddock:/ˈdædək/
n.
围场，圈地; 畜栏
A fenced or enclosed area, typically one used for keeping horses or other livestock.
The farmer kept his prize-winning bull in a secure daddock.  农场主把他那头获奖的公牛放在一个安全的围场里。
The horses were grazing peacefully in the daddock. 马匹正在围场里安静地吃草。
近义词: paddock, enclosure, field, pasture
反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly open range or wilderness)
词性: noun

daddock:/ˈdædək/
n.
(澳大利亚) 小牧场; 小农场
(Australia) A small farm or ranch.
He grew up on a small daddock in rural New South Wales. 他在新南威尔士州农村的一个小农场长大。
She inherited a daddock from her grandfather. 她从祖父那里继承了一个小牧场。
近义词:  farm, ranch, homestead, holding
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun
