over-editing:/ˌoʊvərˈɛdɪtɪŋ/
noun
过度编辑
The act of editing something too much, often resulting in a loss of naturalness or clarity.
The author's over-editing made the prose stilted and unnatural. 作者过度编辑使散文显得生硬和不自然。
overediting, excessive editing
under-editing, insufficient editing
n.
