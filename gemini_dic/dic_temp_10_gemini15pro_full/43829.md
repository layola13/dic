creosol:/ˈkriːəsɒl/ (US) /ˈkriːəsɔːl/ (UK)| n.|甲酚；木馏油酸|A colorless oily liquid with a smoky odor, obtained from wood tar or coal tar and used as a disinfectant and antiseptic.|Creosol is a constituent of creosote. 甲酚是杂酚油的一种成分。| Creosol is used in some disinfectants. 甲酚用于一些消毒剂中。|Synonyms: methylguaiacol|Antonyms: None|Noun


creosol:/ˈkriːəsɒl/ (US) /ˈkriːəsɔːl/ (UK)| adj.|甲酚的；木馏油酸的|Relating to or containing creosol.|  This is a creosol-based disinfectant. 这是一种基于甲酚的消毒剂。 |  The creosol smell was strong. 甲酚的气味很浓。 |Synonyms: None|Antonyms: None|Adjective
