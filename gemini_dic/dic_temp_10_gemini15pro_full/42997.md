cowage: /ˈkaʊɪdʒ/ (UK), /ˈkaʊɪdʒ/ (US)| n. | 刺痒豆，痒豆|A tropical American climbing plant (Mucuna pruriens) having hairy pods that cause itching. The hairs are sometimes used medicinally.|  The cowage plant is notorious for its irritating hairs. 刺痒豆植物因其刺激性的毛发而臭名昭著。

Cowage is also used in some traditional medicines. 刺痒豆也用于一些传统药物中。|近义词: velvet bean, cowitch |反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider plants with soothing properties like aloe vera)|Noun
