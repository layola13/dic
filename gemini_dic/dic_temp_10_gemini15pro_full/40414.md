contraption:/kənˈtræpʃən/ | n. | 奇妙的装置；新奇的玩意儿 |  A mechanical device, gadget, or other piece of equipment, especially one that is complicated, strange, or improvised. |  He built a strange contraption out of wood and wire. 他用木头和金属丝做了一个奇怪的装置。 | gadget, device, apparatus, contrivance |  - | noun
Etymology: Late 16th century (in the sense ‘contrivance, artifice’): perhaps related to contrive.
