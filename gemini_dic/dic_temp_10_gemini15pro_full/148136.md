overselling:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛlɪŋ/| n.|过度销售；过分吹嘘|The act of selling more of something than can be delivered or than is actually available, or of promoting something excessively.|The company was accused of overselling its products, promising features that didn't exist.|近义词：hype, exaggeration, overpromotion|反义词:underselling, understating|noun

oversell:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛl/| v.|过度销售；过分吹嘘|To sell more of something than can be delivered or than is actually available, or to promote something excessively.|They oversold the benefits of the new software.|近义词：hype, exaggerate, overpromote|反义词:undersell, understate|verb
