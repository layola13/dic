anthologizes:/ænˈθɒlədʒaɪzɪz/
verb
选入文集，将…汇编成集
to include something in an anthology
The editor anthologizes the best poems of the year.  编辑将一年中最好的诗歌汇编成集。
近义词: compile, collect, gather
反义词: exclude, omit
词性: verb
