metallographist: /ˌmɛtəˈlɒɡrəfɪst/
n.
金相学家；金属lographic专家
A person who studies the microstructure of metals and alloys.
The metallographist prepared the sample for microscopic examination. 金相学家准备了用于显微镜检查的样品。
Synonyms: material scientist, metallurgist
Antonyms: N/A
noun

metallography: /ˌmɛtəˈlɒɡrəfi/
n.
金相学
The study of the microstructure of metals and alloys.
Metallography helps us understand the properties of materials. 金相学帮助我们理解材料的特性。
