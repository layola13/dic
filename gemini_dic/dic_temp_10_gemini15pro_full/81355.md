graphophone:/ˈɡræfəfoʊn/| n. |留声机|An early sound-recording device using a wax cylinder.  It was a precursor to the gramophone.|Edison invented the graphophone. 爱迪生发明了留声机.|近义词: phonograph, gramophone|反义词: None|词性:noun

Etymology:  From Greek *graphē* (writing) + *phōnē* (sound).
