cliquey: /ˈkliːki/ | adj. | 小集团的，派系的 | Tending to form or associate in small exclusive groups. |  Example 1: The office was very cliquey and it was hard to break into any of the established groups. 办公室里小圈子很多，很难融入任何一个已有的群体。

Example 2:  The high school was known for its cliquey atmosphere, with the "jocks," the "nerds," and the "popular kids" rarely interacting. 这所高中以其小团体氛围而闻名，“运动员”、“书呆子”和“受欢迎的孩子”很少互动。 | 近义词: exclusive, clannish, snobbish | 反义词: inclusive, welcoming, open | 词性: adjective
