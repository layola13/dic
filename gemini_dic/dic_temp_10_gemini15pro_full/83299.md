gynarchy: /ˈɡɪnɑːrki/ | n. | 女性统治；女权政治 | Government by women; a society ruled by women. |  The mythical Amazons were often cited as an example of a gynarchy.  亚马逊女战士经常被引用为女权政治的例子。| Synonyms: matriarchy, female rule | Antonyms: patriarchy, male rule | noun

gynarchy: /ˈɡɪnɑːrki/ | n. | 女权主义；女权运动 (Less common but sometimes used) | The advocacy of women's rights on the grounds of the equality of the sexes. | Some radical feminists advocate for a gynarchy as a way to redress historical imbalances of power. 一些激进的女权主义者提倡女权政治，以此来纠正历史上的权力失衡。 | Synonyms: feminism, women's liberation | Antonyms:  misogyny, sexism | noun
