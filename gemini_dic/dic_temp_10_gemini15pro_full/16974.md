The translation bastardized the poem's subtle nuances.  这个译文歪曲了诗歌的微妙之处。|近义词：corrupt, debase, degrade, adulterate, contaminate, spoil, pervert, warp|反义词：purify, refine, improve, enhance, elevate|词性: 动词
