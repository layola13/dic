observant:/əbˈzɜːrvənt/ /əbˈzɜːrvənt/| adj.|善于观察的；机警的；敏锐的|Paying close attention to details and surroundings; quick to notice things.|例句：She is an observant child and noticed the small change in the room. 她是一个善于观察的孩子，注意到了房间里的细微变化。

例句：The observant detective quickly picked up on the suspect's nervous tic.  观察敏锐的侦探很快就注意到了嫌疑人紧张的抽搐。|近义词：perceptive, astute, watchful, vigilant, attentive|反义词：inattentive, oblivious, unobservant, unmindful|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *observaunt*, from Latin *observāns*, present participle of *observāre* ("to observe, watch, pay attention to, heed, regard, comply with, celebrate"), from *ob-* ("toward, against, in the way of") + *servāre* ("to keep, watch, guard, preserve").
