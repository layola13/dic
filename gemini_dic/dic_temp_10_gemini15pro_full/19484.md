bidding:/ˈbɪdɪŋ/ | n. | 出价；投标；命令；邀请 | the act of offering a particular price for something, especially at an auction |  The bidding for the antique vase started at $500. 古董花瓶的竞价起价为500美元。 | synonyms: offer, tender, proposal | antonyms: acceptance, refusal | noun

bidding:/ˈbɪdɪŋ/ | n. | 吩咐; 命令; 嘱咐 | an order or instruction |  He did the president's bidding without question. 他毫无疑问地执行了总统的命令。 | synonyms: command, order, instruction | antonyms: disobedience, refusal | noun
