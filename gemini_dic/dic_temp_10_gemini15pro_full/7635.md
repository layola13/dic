annulately:/ˈæn.jə.lət.li/| adv.|环状地，轮状地|In a ring-like or annually recurring manner; marked by or occurring in annual rings or cycles. annulate:/ˈæn.jə.lət/ | adj.|环状的|Having rings; ring-shaped.  Formed in rings or marked with rings.|The annulate markings on the butterfly's wings were intricate and beautiful. 蝴蝶翅膀上的环状斑纹复杂而美丽。|近义词：ringed, circular, banded|反义词: unbanded, ringless|词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *annulatus*, past participle of *annulare* "to furnish with a ring," from *annulus* "ring."
