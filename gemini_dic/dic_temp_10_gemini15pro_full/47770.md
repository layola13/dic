decandria:/dɪˈkændriə/
n.
十雄蕊植物；十蕊植物
A plant having ten stamens.
Decandria is a class in the Linnaean system of plant classification, characterized by flowers with ten stamens. 十雄蕊纲是林奈植物分类系统中的一个纲，其特征是花有十个雄蕊。
Plants in the decandria class have flowers with ten stamens. 十雄蕊纲的植物的花有十个雄蕊。
Synonym:  None (specific to Linnaean classification)
Antonym: None (specific to Linnaean classification)
