deanships:/ˈdiːnʃɪps/
n.
院长职位；院长任期
the position or term of office of a dean
The university announced three new deanships. 这所大学宣布了三个新的院长职位。
She held the deanship for ten years. 她担任了十年的院长。
synonyms: administration, leadership, directorship
antonyms: subordination,  followership
noun
