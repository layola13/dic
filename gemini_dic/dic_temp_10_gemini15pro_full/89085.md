wholesome:/ˈhoʊlsəm/ | adj. | 有益健康的；有益的；促进健康的 | Suggesting health and physical well-being; promoting health or well-being. | Example:  The children were raised in a wholesome environment. 孩子们在一个有益健康的环境中长大。

| Example:  She prepared a wholesome meal for her family. 她为家人准备了一顿有益健康的饭菜。

| Synonym:  healthy, nutritious, beneficial, salutary | Antonym:  unhealthy, unwholesome, harmful, detrimental | word type: adjective

Etymology: From Middle English *holsam, from Old English hālsum (“healthy, wholesome, beneficial, safe”), from hāl (“whole, sound, healthy”) + -sum. Cognate with West Frisian hâlsum (“wholesome”), Dutch heilzaam (“wholesome, salutary”), German heilsam (“wholesome, salutary”).
