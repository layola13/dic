historically:/hɪˈstɔːrɪkli/
adv.
在历史上，从历史上看
in a way that relates to history; from a historical point of view
Historically, relations between the two countries have been strained. 从历史上看，两国关系一直很紧张。
historically, this area was covered in forest. 从历史上看，这个地区曾被森林覆盖。
近义词: traditionally, formerly, previously
反义词: currently, presently
词性: 副词


Etymology:
From history + -ical + -ly.  Ultimately from Greek historia "inquiry, knowledge acquired by investigation"
