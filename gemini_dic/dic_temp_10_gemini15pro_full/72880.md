follow-ons:/ˈfɒləʊɒnz/ (British) /ˈfɑːloʊɑːnz/ (American)| n.|后续行动，后续事件；（板球）后续局|Events or actions that come after something else, especially ones that are a consequence of it; (in cricket) a second innings played immediately after the first by a team that did not score as many runs as its opponents in their first innings.|The company secured several lucrative follow-ons from the initial contract.  该公司从最初的合同中获得了几个有利可图的后续项目。

The team enforced the follow-on. 该队强制执行了后续局。|近义词：sequels, consequences, repercussions, after-effects (general); second innings (cricket)|反义词:precursors, antecedents|词性:名词
