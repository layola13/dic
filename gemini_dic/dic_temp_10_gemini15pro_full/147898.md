overreducing:/ˌoʊvərɪˈduːsɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度还原的 | Characterized by excessive reduction.  | The overreducing conditions in the reactor led to the formation of unwanted byproducts. 反应器中过度还原的条件导致形成了不需要的副产物。| excessively reducing | oxidizing, under-reducing | adjective
