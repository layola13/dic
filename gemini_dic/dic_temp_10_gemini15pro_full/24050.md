breezy:/ˈbriːzi/| adj.|有微风的；轻快的；活泼的|Characterized by a gentle wind; lively and cheerful; casual and carefree.|It was a breezy afternoon, perfect for sailing. 那是一个有微风的下午，非常适合航海。|Synonyms: airy, windy, fresh, lively, jaunty, carefree|Antonyms: still, calm, stuffy, serious, solemn|adjective
