cyathiform:/saɪˈæθɪfɔːrm/
| adj. | 杯状的，酒杯形的 | Shaped like a cyathus (a small, deep, bowl-shaped drinking vessel of ancient Greece and Rome). | The cyathiform flower of the plant attracted many insects.  这种植物的杯状花朵吸引了许多昆虫。 | Similar: cup-shaped, bowl-shaped, goblet-shaped | Opposite: flat, planar | Adjective

Etymology->
From Latin *cyathiformis*, from cyathus (“cup, drinking vessel”) + -formis.
