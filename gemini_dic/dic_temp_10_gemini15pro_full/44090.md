crippler:/ˈkrɪplər/| n.|致残者，造成严重损害的事物|Something that disables or severely damages something or someone. |The disease was a crippler, leaving many unable to walk. 这种疾病是一种致残因素，使许多人无法行走。|
The earthquake was a crippler for the local economy.  这场地震对当地经济造成了严重的破坏。|近义词：disabler, debilitator, wrecker, destroyer|反义词: healer, restorer|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From cripple (v.) + -er (agent suffix).  The verb "cripple" comes from Old English "crypel" and Proto-Germanic "*krupilaz," meaning "to creep."
