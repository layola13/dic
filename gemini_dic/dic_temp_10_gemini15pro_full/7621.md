annuitant:/əˈnjuːɪtənt/
n.
年金受领人；领受年金者
A person who receives an annuity.
例句：The annuitant will receive a fixed sum of money each year.  这位年金受领人每年将收到一笔固定的钱。

例句：The insurance company sent the annuitant a check every month.  保险公司每个月都给这位年金受益人寄一张支票。

近义词：pensioner, beneficiary, recipient
反义词: payer, grantor
词性：noun
