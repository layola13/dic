convertors:/kənˈvɜːrtərz/ | n. | 转换器，变换器 |A device or machine that converts something from one form to another, such as an analog-to-digital converter or a catalytic converter. |  The catalytic convertor in a car reduces harmful emissions. 汽车中的催化转换器减少了有害排放物。

|  Solar convertors transform sunlight into electricity. 太阳能转换器将阳光转化为电能。
| synonyms: transformers, adapters, transducers | antonyms:  None (lack of conversion is implied by absence of a convertor) | noun
