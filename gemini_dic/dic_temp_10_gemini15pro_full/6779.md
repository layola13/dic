anapest:/ˈænəpɛst/ | n. |抑抑扬格，短短长格|A metrical foot consisting of two unstressed syllables followed by a stressed syllable. |例句：The word "understand" is an anapest.  “understand”这个单词是抑抑扬格。

例句：The poem was written in anapestic tetrameter. 这首诗是用抑抑扬格四音步写成的。|近义词:dactyl (opposite stress pattern)|反义词:iamb (different stress pattern)|词性:noun
