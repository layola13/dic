nonconsumer:/ˌnɒnkənˈsjuːmə(r)/ | /ˌnɑːnkənˈsuːmər/ | n. | 非消费者，非用户 |  A person or organization that does not use a particular product or service. |  The survey targeted nonconsumers of organic food to understand their purchasing habits.  这项调查针对的是有机食品的非消费者，以了解他们的购买习惯。| non-user, abstainer | consumer, user | noun

nonconsumer:/ˌnɒnkənˈsjuːmə(r)/ | /ˌnɑːnkənˈsuːmər/ | adj. | 非消费的 | Not related to or involved in the consumption of goods or services. | The study focused on the nonconsumer aspects of the new technology. 这项研究的重点是新技术的非消费方面。 | non-consumptive | consumer, consumptive | adjective
