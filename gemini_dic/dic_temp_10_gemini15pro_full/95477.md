incivilities:/ˌɪnsɪˈvɪlətiz/| n.|无礼；粗鲁行为|Rude or impolite actions or remarks.|The constant incivilities from the customer made the cashier's job unbearable.  顾客不断的无礼行为让收银员的工作难以忍受。|例句：The politician was criticized for his incivilities towards the press.  这位政客因对媒体的无礼行为而受到批评。|近义词：rudeness, impoliteness, discourtesy, disrespect|反义词:politeness, courtesy, respect|词性:名词

词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French incivilté, from in- ‘not’ + civilté ‘civility’.
