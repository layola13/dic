hypervigilant:/ˌhaɪpərˈvɪdʒɪlənt/| adj.|高度警觉的，过度警觉的|Excessively watchful or alert, often due to anxiety or trauma.  Characterized by an increased state of alertness and sensitivity to potential threats, often to an unhealthy or excessive degree.|例句：She remained hypervigilant after the robbery, constantly checking the locks and windows. 她被抢劫后一直高度警觉，不断地检查门锁和窗户。|近义词：watchful, alert, wary, cautious, on guard, vigilant|反义词：relaxed, calm, oblivious, unmindful, unaware|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From hyper- (meaning "over, excessive") + vigilant (from Latin vigilans, present participle of vigilare "to keep watch").
