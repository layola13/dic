onomatopoeic: /ˌɒnəˌmætəˈpiːɪk/ /ˌɑːnəˌmætəˈpiːɪk/ | adj. | 拟声的，象声的 |  Formed from or resembling the sound associated with the thing or action denoted. | The "buzz" of a bee is an onomatopoeic word. 蜜蜂的“嗡嗡”声是一个拟声词。 |  mimetic, imitative | - | adjective

onomatopoeia: /ˌɒnəˌmætəˈpiːə/ /ˌɑːnəˌmætəˈpiːə/ | n. | 拟声词，象声词 | The formation of a word from a sound associated with what is named (e.g. cuckoo, sizzle ). |  "Meow" is an example of onomatopoeia. “喵”是一个拟声词的例子。 |  mimetic, imitative | - | noun

词源:  From late Latin *onomatopoeus, from Greek onomatopoios 'name-making,' from onoma, onomat- 'name' + poiein 'to make.'
