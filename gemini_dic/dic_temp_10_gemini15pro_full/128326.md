neurula:/ˈnʊərələ/| n. |神经胚|An embryo at the stage of development at which the neural tube is forming.|The neurula stage follows the gastrula stage in embryonic development.  神经胚阶段在胚胎发育中紧随原肠胚阶段。|The scientists observed the development of the neurula under a microscope. 科学家们在显微镜下观察了神经胚的发育。|近义词: embryo (at a specific stage) |反义词:  (No direct antonym.  Could consider earlier or later developmental stages like gastrula or early fetus) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *neûron* ("nerve, sinew") + *-ula* (diminutive suffix).
