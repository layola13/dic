clacker:/ˈklækər/| n.|咔嗒声; 响板; 饶舌妇; (旧时西部牛仔用以吓唬牲畜的)长柄响板|A clacker is a toy consisting of two wooden balls attached by a string. When swung, the balls clack together making a noise.|The child played with a clacker, annoying everyone around. 孩子玩着响板，惹恼了周围的每个人。|Synonyms: rattle, clapper, clicker|Antonyms: silence, quiet|noun

clacker:/ˈklækər/| v.|发出咔嗒声|To clacker is to make a short sharp sound.|The keys clackered as she typed. 她打字时，按键发出咔嗒咔嗒的声音。|Synonyms: clatter, rattle, click|Antonyms: mute, silence|verb
