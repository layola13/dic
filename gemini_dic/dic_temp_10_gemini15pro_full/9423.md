antirationalistic:/ˌæntiˌræʃəˈnəˈlɪstɪk/
|adj.|反理性主义的|Opposed to or distrustful of reason and rationalism, emphasizing intuition, feeling, or faith.|His antirationalistic beliefs led him to reject scientific evidence.|近义词:irrational, emotional, mystical|反义词:rational, logical, reasoned|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek)
rationalistic (adjective): based on or in accordance with reason or logic
-istic (suffix): forming adjectives meaning 'relating to' or 'characteristic of'
