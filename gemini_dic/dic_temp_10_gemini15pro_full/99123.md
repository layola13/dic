intercelebrations: /ˌɪntərˌsɛləˈbreɪʃənz/
noun
互相庆祝; 跨文化庆祝; 共同庆祝
Celebrations between different groups or cultures, emphasizing shared joy and understanding.
The annual intercelebrations fostered a sense of unity among the diverse communities. 每年一度的跨文化庆祝活动促进了不同社群之间的团结感。
The festival was a wonderful display of intercelebrations, showcasing traditions from around the world.  这个节日是对跨文化庆祝的精彩展示，展示了来自世界各地的传统。
synonyms: joint celebrations, shared festivities, multicultural celebrations
antonyms: segregation, division, isolation
