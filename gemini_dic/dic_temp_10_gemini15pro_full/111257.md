litigator:/ˈlɪtɪɡeɪtər/| n. |诉讼人，进行诉讼者|A person who is involved in a lawsuit.  A party to a lawsuit; someone involved in litigation.|例句：The litigator presented a compelling case to the jury.  诉讼律师向陪审团提出了令人信服的案例。

例句：The experienced litigator was known for his aggressive tactics. 这位经验丰富的诉讼律师以其咄咄逼人的策略而闻名。|近义词：litigant, lawyer, attorney, barrister, solicitor|反义词:defendant, plaintiff (Note: These are not strict antonyms, but represent opposing sides in litigation) |词性:noun
