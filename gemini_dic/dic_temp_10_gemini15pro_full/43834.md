creosoting: /ˈkriːəˌsoʊtɪŋ/ | n. | 浸渍杂酚油；杂酚油防腐处理 | the process of treating wood with creosote to preserve it | The railway sleepers were treated with creosoting.  铁路枕木经过了杂酚油防腐处理。 | 近义词: preserving, treating | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 decaying, rotting) | 词性: noun


Etymology: From creosote + -ing. Creosote itself comes from German *Kreosot*, from Greek *kreas* (flesh) + *sōtēr* (preserver).
