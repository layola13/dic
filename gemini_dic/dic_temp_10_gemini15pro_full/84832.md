hang-gliding:/ˈhæŋ ˌɡlaɪdɪŋ/
n.
滑翔运动
the sport of flying using a hang-glider
He went hang-gliding in the Swiss Alps. 他在瑞士阿尔卑斯山玩滑翔运动。

近义词:gliding, soaring
反义词:(None -  Difficult to have a direct antonym for a specific sport)
词性:noun


hang-glide:/ˈhæŋ ɡlaɪd/
v.
进行滑翔运动
to fly using a hang-glider
I've always wanted to hang-glide over the Grand Canyon. 我一直想在大峡谷上空滑翔。

近义词:glide, soar
反义词:(None -  Difficult to have a direct antonym for the action of a specific sport)
词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
"hang-gliding" (n.) and "hang-glide" (v.) are formed from "hang" + "glide".
"hang" (v.) from Old English *hangian, from Proto-Germanic *hanganą.
"glide" (v.) from Old English glidan, of uncertain origin.
