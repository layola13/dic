overstocking:/ˌoʊvərˈstɑːkɪŋ/ /ˌəʊvəˈstɒkɪŋ/| n.|过度库存，库存过剩|The overstocking of winter clothes led to significant losses for the company.  这家公司冬季服装库存过剩导致了重大损失。|The warehouse was filled to the brim due to overstocking. 仓库由于库存过剩而堆满了货物。|近义词：excess inventory, surplus, glut|反义词:understocking, shortage|词性:名词

overstock:/ˌoʊvərˈstɑːk/ /ˌəʊvəˈstɒk/| vt.|过度存储，进货过多|The store overstocked on Halloween candy and had to sell it at a discount after the holiday.  这家商店万圣节糖果进货过多，节日过后不得不打折出售。| Retailers often overstock popular items during the holiday season. 零售商在节日期间经常会过度囤积热门商品。|近义词：overbuy, oversupply|反义词:understock, run short|词性:动词
