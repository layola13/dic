leo:/ˈliːoʊ/ (US) /ˈliːəʊ/ (UK)| n. |狮子座; 狮子|A constellation and astrological sign representing a lion.  It is also the fifth sign of the zodiac, considered to cover July 23-August 22. Also, a male given name.|例句：He was born under the sign of Leo. 他出生在狮子座。

例句：Leo is known for being charismatic.  Leo以富有魅力而闻名。|近义词：lion (as a symbol)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
