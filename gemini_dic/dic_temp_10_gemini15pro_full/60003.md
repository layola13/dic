editorializers:/ˌɛdɪˈtɔːriəˌlaɪzərz/
noun
评论员；社论作者
People who write or speak editorials, expressing opinions or interpreting events.
The editorializers condemned the government's new policy. 评论员谴责了政府的新政策。
Synonyms: commentators, pundits, analysts
Antonyms: reporters (who strive for objectivity)
词性: 名词 (plural)

editorialize: /ˌɛdɪˈtɔːriəˌlaɪz/
verb
发表社论；进行评论
To express opinions in an editorial or editorial-like manner.
The news anchor was criticized for editorializing during the broadcast.  这位新闻主播因在广播中发表评论而受到批评。
Synonyms: comment, opine, criticize
Antonyms: report (objectively)
词性: 动词
