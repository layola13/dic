metropoles:/məˈtrɒpəliːz/
noun
大都市；首府（复数）
A metropolis is a very large city, often the most important city in a large area or country.  Metropoles is the plural form.
London and New York are leading global metropoles. 伦敦和纽约是世界领先的大都市。
synonyms: cities, capitals, urban centers
antonyms: rural areas, countryside, villages
