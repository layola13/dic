bayonetting:/ˌbeɪəˈnɛtɪŋ/
n.
用刺刀刺杀; 刺刀战
The act of attacking or killing someone with a bayonet.
The soldiers resorted to bayonetting in the hand-to-hand combat. 士兵们在肉搏战中 resorted to bayonetting in the hand-to-hand combat.
Soldiers were engaged in fierce bayonetting during the battle. 士兵们在战斗中进行了激烈的白刃战。
近义词：stabbing, spearing
反义词：None (The action itself doesn't have a direct opposite)
词性：noun


bayonetting:/ˌbeɪəˈnɛtɪŋ/
v.
用刺刀刺杀
Attacking or killing with a bayonet.
The enemy troops were bayonetting the wounded soldiers.  敌军用刺刀杀害了受伤的士兵。
They were bayonetting prisoners of war. 他们用刺刀杀害战俘。
近义词：stabbing, spearing
反义词：None
词性: verb (gerund or present participle)


词源:  From bayonet + -ing.  Bayonet itself comes from French baïonnette,  named after the city of Bayonne, France, where it is believed to have originated.
