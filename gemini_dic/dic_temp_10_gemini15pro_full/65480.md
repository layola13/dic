europarliamentary:/ˌjʊərəʊˌpɑːrləˈmentəri/
adjective
欧洲议会的
Relating to the European Parliament.
The europarliamentary elections are held every five years. 欧洲议会选举每五年举行一次。
She is a well-known europarliamentary figure. 她是一位著名的欧洲议会人物。
Near Synonyms: European parliamentary, EP
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could be considered in contrast to national parliamentary)
