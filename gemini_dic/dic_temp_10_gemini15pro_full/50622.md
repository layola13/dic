derelict: /ˈdɛrəlɪkt/
adjective

abandoned or deserted; (of a person) neglectful of duty; dilapidated.

derelict: /ˈdɛrəlɪkt/
noun
被遗弃的人，流浪汉
a person without a home, job, or property.
The city shelters provided a haven for derelicts. 城市收容所为流浪汉提供了一个避难所。
The streets were filled with derelicts begging for money. 街上到处都是流浪汉在乞讨。
近义词: vagrant, bum, tramp
反义词: citizen, resident, employed
词性: noun

词源: From Latin *derelictus*, past participle of *derelinquere* "to abandon, leave behind," from *de-* "down, completely" + *relinquere* "to leave, forsake."
