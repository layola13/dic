awlwort:/ˈɔːlwɜːrt/| n. |锥子草，山芥|A European plant (Subularia aquatica) of the mustard family, growing in shallow water and having awl-shaped leaves.|Awlwort is a small aquatic plant found in the shallows of lakes and ponds.|近义词:water awlwort, subularia|反义词:None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From *awl* (referring to its awl-shaped leaves) + *wort* (an Old English suffix denoting a plant or herb).
