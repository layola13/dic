misreport:/ˌmɪsrɪˈpɔːrt/ /ˌmɪsrɪˈpɔːrt/| v. n.|错误地报道；误报|To give a false or inaccurate account of something.|The media misreported the details of the accident. 媒体错误地报道了事故的细节。|The newspaper issued a correction for the misreport. 报纸为错误的报道发布了更正。|近义词：misrepresent, falsify, distort|反义词: report accurately, represent truthfully|verb, noun
