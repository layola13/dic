ethereal: /ɪˈθɪəriəl/ | adj. | 天上的，飘渺的，超凡脱俗的 | Extremely delicate and light in a way that seems too perfect for this world. | Her ethereal beauty captivated everyone in the room. 她飘渺脱俗的美丽吸引了房间里所有的人。| The music had an ethereal quality that seemed to transport me to another world. 这音乐有一种空灵的特质，似乎把我带到了另一个世界。| 近义词：heavenly, celestial, airy, delicate, otherworldly | 反义词：earthly, mundane, substantial | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Late Latin aethereus, from Latin aether + -eus. Ultimately from Ancient Greek αἰθήρ (aithḗr, “upper air; bright, purer air”).
