mirthlessly:/ˈmɜːθləsli/ | adv. | 毫无乐趣地，冷冰冰地 | Without mirth; in a way that shows no happiness or enjoyment. | He laughed mirthlessly. 他冷冰冰地笑了。| She mirthlessly recounted the grim details of the accident. 她毫无感情地讲述了事故的可怕细节。 | 近义词: joylessly, gloomily, sullenly | 反义词: merrily, joyfully, cheerfully | 词性: 副词

词源:  From mirth + -less + -ly.  Mirth comes from Old English *myrgþu, from Proto-Germanic *murgiþō, related to merry.
