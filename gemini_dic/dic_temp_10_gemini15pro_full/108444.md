laxator:/lækˈseɪtər/ | n. | 松弛肌 | A muscle that loosens or relaxes a part of the body. |  The superior oblique muscle acts as a laxator of the vocal cords. | 近义词: relaxer | 反义词: tensor, constrictor | noun

Etymology:
From Latin *laxator*, from laxare (“to loosen, open, slacken”), from laxus (“loose, wide, open”).
