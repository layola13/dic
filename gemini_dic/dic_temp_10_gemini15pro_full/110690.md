lineality:/ˌlɪniˈælɪti/ | n. |线性；线性度；线状|The property of being linear; resemblance to a line.  The state of being arranged in or extending along a straight or nearly straight line. |The lineality of the design made it visually appealing.  这个设计的线性使它在视觉上很吸引人。| linearity, rectilinearity | nonlinearity, curvilinearity | noun
