inauspiciousnesses:/ɪnɔːˈspɪʃəsnɪsɪz/| n. |不祥之兆，不幸，不吉利|The state or quality of being inauspicious; unfavorableness of circumstances; a lack of auspiciousness.  It refers to a series of unfortunate or unlucky events or signs. |例句：The inauspiciousnesses surrounding their wedding day made them nervous.  他们婚礼当天 surrounding 的种种不祥之兆让他们感到紧张。

例句：The series of inauspiciousnesses led them to postpone the trip. 一连串的不祥之兆 khiến họ phải trì hoãn chuyến đi.|近义词：unfavorableness, adversity, misfortune, ominousness|反义词: auspiciousness, favorableness, fortune, prosperity |词性: noun
