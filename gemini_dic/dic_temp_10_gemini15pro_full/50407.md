depositors:/dɪˈpɑːzɪtərz/
n.
存款人；储户
People or organizations who place money into a bank account or other financial institution.
The bank assured its depositors that their money was safe. 银行向其储户保证他们的钱是安全的。
depositors rushed to withdraw their money.  储户们蜂拥而至提取他们的存款。
synonyms: investors, savers, lenders, stakeholders
antonyms: borrowers, debtors


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "deposit" + "-or" (agent suffix).  "Deposit" comes from the Latin "depositum,"  past participle of "deponere" (to lay down, put away), from "de-" (down, away) + "ponere" (to put, place).
