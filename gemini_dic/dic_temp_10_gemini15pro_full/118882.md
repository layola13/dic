metastatic:/ˌmɛtəˈstætɪk/
adjective
转移性的；扩散的
(of a cancer) spreading to other sites in the body.
例句：The patient was diagnosed with metastatic breast cancer.  病人被诊断出患有转移性乳腺癌。

例句：Metastatic melanoma is a serious form of skin cancer. 转移性黑色素瘤是一种严重的皮肤癌。

近义词：cancerous, malignant, spreading
反义词：benign, localized
词性：adjective


词源:
From Greek *metastatikos*, from *methistanai* "to change one's place, transfer," from *meta* "change" + *histanai* "to cause to stand."
