goitre:/ˈɡɔɪtər/ (US), /ˈɡɔɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 甲状腺肿 | An abnormal enlargement of the thyroid gland, visible as a swelling at the base of the neck. |  She developed a goitre due to iodine deficiency. 她因为缺碘得了甲状腺肿。 | synonyms: thyroid enlargement,  struma | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a medical condition.  One could consider "normal thyroid" or "healthy thyroid" as conceptually opposite) | noun
