consolatory:/kənˈsɒlətəri/ /kənˈsoʊlətɔːri/
adjective
安慰的；慰藉的
Intended to comfort or alleviate grief or disappointment.
She offered a few consolatory words after I failed the exam. 她在我考试不及格后说了几句安慰的话。

consolatory:/kənˈsɒlətəri/ /kənˈsoʊlətɔːri/
adjective
抚慰的；起安慰作用的
Having the power to console.
The warm hug was strangely consolatory.  这个温暖的拥抱出奇地令人感到安慰。


近义词: comforting, soothing, reassuring, cheering
反义词: distressing, upsetting, disheartening, depressing
词性: adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin consolatorius, from Latin consolari ‘comfort’, from con- ‘together’ + solari ‘to console’.
