outcompeted:/ˌaʊtkəmˈpiːtɪd/
|v.| 胜过，在竞争中击败| To defeat (someone or something) in competition. | The smaller company was quickly outcompeted by larger corporations with more resources. 这家小公司很快就被拥有更多资源的大公司击败了。| Our team was outcompeted in the final round. 我们的团队在最后一轮比赛中被击败了。| surpass, beat, defeat, outdo, outstrip | lose to, be defeated by | Verb
