paradox-ologies: /ˌpærəˈdɒksˈɒlədʒiz/ | n. | 似是而非的理论，悖论学 |  The study or collection of paradoxes, especially as a means of demonstrating or exploring philosophical or theological concepts.  Often used humorously or ironically to refer to convoluted or contradictory explanations. | He presented a series of paradox-ologies to explain the unexplainable. 他提出一系列似是而非的理论来解释无法解释的事情。 | Near synonyms: paradoxes, contradictions, inconsistencies, absurdities |  Near antonyms:  truisms, axioms, certainties | noun
