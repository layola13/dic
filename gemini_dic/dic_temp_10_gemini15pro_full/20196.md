birdbolt:/ˈbɜːrdˌboʊlt/| n. |箭；弩箭；（尤指）射鸟的箭|A short, blunt arrow shot from a crossbow, especially one used for hunting birds.  It was also sometimes used as a general term for any arrow or crossbow bolt. |The hunter loaded his crossbow with a birdbolt and aimed at the pheasant. 猎人用弩箭瞄准了野鸡。|Synonym: arrow, bolt, quarrel|Antonym: N/A|noun
