caramelized:/ˈkærəməˌlaɪzd/ | adj. | 焦糖化的，焦糖色的 | Heated with sugar until brown and sweet. | Example: The caramelized onions added a rich sweetness to the burger. 焦糖化的洋葱给汉堡增添了浓郁的甜味。 | Example:  She enjoys the caramelized crust on crème brûlée. 她喜欢焦糖布丁上焦糖化的脆皮。 | 近义词: candied, glazed, sugared | 反义词: bland, uncooked | 词性: 形容词

词源: From French *caraméliser*, from caramel (caramel itself from Spanish *caramelo*, probably of Arabic or possibly Latin American origin).
