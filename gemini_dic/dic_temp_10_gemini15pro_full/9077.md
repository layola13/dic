antiministerially:/ˌæntiˌmɪnɪˈstɪəriəli/
| adv. | 反部长地；反对部长式地 |  In a manner opposed to the actions or policies of a minister, especially a government minister. | He acted antiministerially, defying the official line. 他采取了反部长的行动，公然违抗官方路线。 |  The opposition leader spoke antiministerially, criticizing the government's handling of the economy. 反对党领袖以反对部长的姿态发表讲话，批评政府处理经济的方式。 |  oppositionally, dissidently | ministerially, supportively | adverb
