indisciplinable:/ˌɪnˈdɪsəplɪnəbl/| adj.|难以管教的，不服管束的|Not able to be disciplined or controlled.|Example: He was an indisciplinable child, constantly getting into trouble. 他是一个难以管教的孩子，总是惹麻烦。|Example: The indisciplinable troops were routed by the enemy.  纪律涣散的军队被敌人击溃了。|Synonyms: unruly, unmanageable, wayward, rebellious, intractable|Antonyms: disciplined, obedient, compliant, docile|Word type: Adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + disciplinable (capable of being disciplined).  The root word "discipline" comes from the Latin "disciplina" meaning "instruction, training, knowledge".
