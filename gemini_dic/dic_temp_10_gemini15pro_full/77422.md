gavial:/ˈɡeɪviəl/ | n. | 鳄鱼，长吻鳄，恒河鳄 | A large crocodilian with a very long, slender snout. |  例句：The gavial is a critically endangered species found in the rivers of India and Nepal. 恒河鳄是一种极度濒危的物种，发现于印度和尼泊尔的河流中。

例句：Gavials are specialized fish-eaters, using their long snouts to catch prey. 长吻鳄是专门吃鱼的，用它们的长鼻子捕捉猎物。 | 近义词：crocodile, alligator | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
