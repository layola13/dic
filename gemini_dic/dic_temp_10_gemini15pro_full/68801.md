fasciae:/ˈfæʃiiː/
n.
筋膜（复数）
A thin sheath of fibrous tissue enclosing a muscle or other organ.
The fasciae of the leg muscles were tight after the marathon.  腿部肌肉的筋膜在马拉松比赛后很紧。

近义词:membranes, tissues
反义词:(没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用形容词描述其状态，例如: loose/relaxed)
词性:noun (plural)


fascia:/ˈfæʃə/
n.
筋膜（单数）
A thin sheath of fibrous tissue enclosing a muscle or other organ.
The fascia surrounding the heart helps protect it. 心脏周围的筋膜有助于保护它。

近义词:membrane, tissue
反义词:(没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用形容词描述其状态，例如: loose/relaxed)
词性:noun (singular)


词源:
From Latin fascia "band, bandage," from PIE root *dhe- "to set, put."
