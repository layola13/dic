empathize with: /ˈɛmpəθaɪz wɪð/ | v. | 感同身受，理解，同情 | To understand and share the feelings of another. | I can empathize with her frustration because I've been in a similar situation. 我能理解她的沮丧，因为我曾处于类似的境地。|  I empathize with his desire to travel the world. 我很理解他想要环游世界的愿望。 | 近义词: sympathize with, understand, relate to, share the feelings of | 反义词:  be indifferent to, disregard, ignore | 词性: 动词
