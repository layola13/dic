commendably:/kəˈmɛndəbli/| adv.|值得称赞地，令人钦佩地|in a way that deserves praise and approval|She handled the difficult situation commendably. 她令人钦佩地处理了这个困难的局面。|Synonyms: admirably, creditably, laudably, praiseworthily|Antonyms: poorly, badly, disgracefully|adverb

commend:/kəˈmɛnd/| vt. |推荐；称赞；委托；吸引|1. praise formally or officially. 2. present as suitable for approval or acceptance; recommend. 3. entrust for care or keeping. 4. make attractive or appealing.|I commend her for her bravery. 我钦佩她的勇气。  He commended the product to us. 他向我们推荐了这个产品。|Synonyms: praise, applaud, laud, endorse|Antonyms: criticize, condemn, censure|verb
