dusking:/ˈdʌskɪŋ/ | adj. |  黄昏的，昏暗的 | Becoming dusk; darkening.  Relating to or occurring at dusk. | The dusking sky hinted at the approaching night.  昏暗的天空预示着夜晚的来临。 |  The photographer captured the dusking landscape beautifully. 摄影师完美地捕捉到了黄昏的景色。| 近义词: twilight, crepuscular | 反义词: dawning, brightening | 词性: adjective

词源: From dusk + -ing.  Dusk itself comes from Old English *dux, related to Dutch duister "dark," German duster "gloomy."
