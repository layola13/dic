good-old-boys:/ˌɡʊd oʊld ˈbɔɪz/
|n.| 老好人，老男孩（常指拥有特权的白人男性群体）
|A group of men, often white and holding positions of power, who use their influence to help each other and maintain their status.  Often implies cronyism and exclusion of others.|
|The good-old-boys network at the country club made it difficult for anyone outside their circle to get a membership.|
|近义词：clique, inner circle, old boy network|
|反义词：outsiders|
|词性：名词|
