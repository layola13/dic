overcloyed:/ˌoʊvərˈklɔɪd/ | adj. | 过分饱足的，过分腻烦的 | excessively satiated or filled, especially with rich or sweet things; weary from overindulgence | The overcloyed guests could barely manage a slice of the wedding cake.  那些吃得太饱的客人们几乎吃不下婚礼蛋糕了。|  surfeited, glutted, jaded |  unsatisfied, wanting | adjective

overcloyed:/ˌoʊvərˈklɔɪd/ | adj. | (比喻)厌倦的，腻烦的 | (figurative) weary or bored from overexposure or excessive indulgence | He was overcloyed with praise and began to find it insincere. 他对赞美感到厌倦，并开始觉得这些赞美不真诚。| jaded, bored, weary | enthusiastic, interested | adjective
