nasogastric: /ˌneɪzoʊˈɡæstrɪk/ | adj. | 鼻胃的 | Relating to or involving both the nose and the stomach. | A nasogastric tube was inserted to feed the patient.  插入鼻胃管给病人喂食。 | 近义词: None (highly specific medical term) | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From naso- (combining form meaning "nose," from Latin nasus "nose") + gastric (from Greek gaster "belly, stomach").
