avowing:/əˈvaʊɪŋ/
verb
公开宣称；承认；坦白
openly declaring or acknowledging something
She was avowing her innocence throughout the trial.  她在整个审判过程中都声称自己无罪。
Synonyms: declaring, admitting, confessing, proclaiming, asserting
Antonyms: denying, concealing, hiding, refuting
