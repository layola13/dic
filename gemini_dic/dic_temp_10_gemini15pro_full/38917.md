confessionalists:/kənˈfɛʃənəlɪsts/
n.
信奉者，忏悔者，告解者
Those who adhere to a particular confession of faith, especially within a larger religious group.  A confessionalist emphasizes the distinct doctrines and practices of their particular denomination or sect.
The confessionalists within the Protestant church held firmly to their specific interpretation of scripture.  新教教会内的信奉者坚持他们对经文的特定解释。
adherents, believers, sectarians
nonconformists, secularists
noun

confessionalist:/kənˈfɛʃənəlɪsts/
n.
信奉的，忏悔的，告解的
Relating to or characteristic of a confession of faith.  Emphasizing the importance of adhering to a specific set of religious doctrines.
The confessionalist approach to theology focuses on the unique tenets of a particular faith tradition.  忏悔神学方法侧重于特定信仰传统的独特原则。
doctrinal, denominational, sectarian
nondenominational, ecumenical
adjective
