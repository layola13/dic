hyperborean:/ˌhaɪpərˈbɔːriən/ /ˌhaɪpərˈbɔːriːən/ | adj. n. |极北的；极寒的；(Hyperborean)极北之地的居民|  Relating to the extreme north or the far north; frigid.  (Noun) A member of a mythical people inhabiting a land of perpetual sunshine beyond the north wind. |  The explorers ventured into the hyperborean regions of the Arctic. 探险家们冒险进入了北极的极寒地区。| 近义词:arctic, frigid, northern, polar | 反义词:southern, tropical, torrid | 词性:形容词, 名词
