foremother:/ˈfɔːrˌmʌðər/| n. |女祖先，女性先驱|A woman from whom a person is descended, especially a woman who lived a long time ago; a female ancestor. A woman who was an important figure in an earlier period and who helped to develop something. |例句：She is considered a foremother of modern dance. 她被认为是现代舞蹈的先驱。

例句：Our foremothers faced many hardships. 我们的女祖先们面临着许多苦难。|近义词：ancestor, progenitor,  predecessor|反义词:descendant|词性:名词
