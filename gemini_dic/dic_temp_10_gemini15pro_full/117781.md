melene: /məˈliːn/ | n. | 黑色素瘤; 黑素 | A malignant tumor associated with skin pigmentation, often caused by excessive exposure to sunlight.  A dark brown to black pigment occurring in the hair, skin, and iris of the eye in people and animals. |  Melene can develop from existing moles. 黑色素瘤可以由现有的痣发展而来。  Excessive sun exposure can increase your risk of melene. 过度日晒会增加你患黑色素瘤的风险。| 近义词: melanoma,  pigment | 反义词:  None (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑depigmentation色素脱失，但并非完全相反) | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Greek *μέλας (mélas, “black, dark”).
