brusque:/brʌsk/ /brʊsk/| adj.|唐突的，鲁莽的，简慢的|Abrupt or offhand in speech or manner.|例句：His brusque manner offended many people. 他的粗鲁态度冒犯了很多人。|例句：She gave a brusque reply and turned away. 她简慢地回答后转身离开了。|近义词：abrupt, curt, blunt, short, terse, sharp, gruff|反义词：polite, courteous, gracious, tactful|词性:形容词
词源: From French *brusque*, from Italian *brusco* "sour, tart," perhaps from Late Latin *bruscus* "butcher's broom" (a shrub with sharp-pointed leaves).
