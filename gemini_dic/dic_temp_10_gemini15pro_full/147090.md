overlie:/ˌoʊvərˈlaɪ/| v. |覆盖；躺在…上面；压在…上面|To lie on top of or cover something.|The sedimentary rocks overlie the granite. 沉积岩覆盖在花岗岩上.|近义词：cover, overlap, superimpose|反义词: underlie|词性:动词

overlie:/ˌoʊvərˈlaɪ/| adj. |上覆的|Lying on top of something else.|The overlying rock formation was primarily shale. 上覆的岩层主要是页岩。|近义词：superjacent, overlying|反义词: underlying|词性:形容词


词源: From Old English *oferliġan, from ofer (over) + liġan (to lie).
