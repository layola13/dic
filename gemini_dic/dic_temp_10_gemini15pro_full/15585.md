bahaudur:/bəˈhɑːdʊr/| adj. n.|勇敢的，勇士|Brave, courageous, valiant; a brave warrior, a champion.  Derived from Persian بهادر (bahādur) meaning "brave, heroic".  Often used as a title or epithet.|He was known as a bahaudur, fearless in battle. 他被称为勇士，在战斗中无所畏惧。|近义词:valiant, courageous, heroic, gallant, intrepid|反义词:cowardly, timid, fearful|adjective, noun
