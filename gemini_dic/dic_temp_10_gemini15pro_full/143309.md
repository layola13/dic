outduelled:/ˌaʊtˈdjuːəld/ | v. |  战胜，击败（尤指在决斗或竞争中） | He outduelled his opponent in the final match. 他在决赛中击败了对手。 |  defeated, vanquished, outfought, bested | lost to | verb
词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning exceeding or surpassing) + "duel" (from Old French duel, from Latin duellum, meaning war).  The "duel" part ultimately comes from duo, meaning two, reflecting the one-on-one nature of a duel.
例句：

He outduelled his rival for the championship.
他战胜了他的对手赢得了冠军。

She outduelled the other candidates for the job.
她在求职竞争中胜过了其他候选人。

近义词: defeated, vanquished, outfought, bested, overcame

反义词: lost to, succumbed to, was defeated by
