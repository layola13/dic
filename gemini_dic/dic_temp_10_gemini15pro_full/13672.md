attractive:/əˈtræktɪv/| adj.|有吸引力的，引人入胜的|Pleasing or appealing to the senses.|She has an attractive personality. 她性格很迷人。|近义词：appealing, charming, alluring, captivating|反义词：unattractive, repulsive, repellent|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *attractif*, from Old French *attractif*, from Late Latin *attractivus*, from Latin *attrahere* "to draw to," from *ad-* "to" + *trahere* "to draw."
