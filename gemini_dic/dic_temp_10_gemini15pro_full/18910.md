besom:/ˈbiːzəm/| n.|扫帚，长柄扫帚|A broom, typically one made of twigs tied to a handle.|She swept the floor with a besom.|近义词：broom, brush|反义词:|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *bēsem (attested only in compounds), from Proto-Germanic *baismaz, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰais- (“brushwood, twigs”). Cognate with Middle Low German bēsem, besem (“broom”).
