miasmically:/maɪˈæzmɪkli/ | adv. | 瘴气地；秽浊地；恶臭地 | In a miasmic manner; like a miasma.  Characterized by or resembling a miasma. | The swamp miasmically spread its foul odor throughout the valley. 沼泽瘴气般地将恶臭弥漫在整个山谷。 | Synonyms: pestilentially, poisonously, noxiously | Antonyms: healthily, purely, cleanly | word type: adverb

miasma:/maɪˈæzmə/ | n. | 瘴气; 秽浊的气氛; 不良的环境 |  1. An unpleasant or unhealthy smell or vapor. 2. A general feeling of gloom or depression. |  1. A miasma of stale cigarette smoke hung in the air. 空气中弥漫着一股难闻的香烟味。 2. A miasma of despair settled over the town after the factory closed. 工厂关闭后，绝望的气氛笼罩着整个城镇。 | Synonyms: stench, stink, reek, fetor (for smell); gloom, depression, malaise (for feeling) | Antonyms: fragrance, perfume (for smell); cheerfulness, joy, happiness (for feeling) | word type: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek μίασμα (míasma), meaning "pollution, defilement".  Ultimately from μιαίνω (miaínō), "to stain, pollute".
