navigably:/ˈnævɪɡəbli/| adv.|可航行地，可驾驶地|in a way that allows easy navigation; in a way that is easy to navigate.  Can be used literally (of waterways) or figuratively (of documents, websites, etc.)|The river is navigably only during high tide. 这条河只有在涨潮时才能通航。|近义词：easily navigated, steerable, passable|反义词：impassable, unnavigable|词性:副词

Etymology:
From "navigable" + "-ly"
"Navigable" comes from the Late Latin "navigabilis," from "navigare" (to sail, navigate), from "navis" (ship) + "-abilis" (able to be).
