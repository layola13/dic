extradite:/ˈɛkstrədaɪt/ | vt. | 引渡 | to hand over (a person accused or convicted of a crime) to the jurisdiction of another country, state, etc. | The government attempted to extradite the fugitive from Canada. 政府试图从加拿大引渡逃犯。|  The US is seeking to extradite him from the UK to face trial. 美国正在寻求将他从英国引渡回国受审。 | synonyms: deport, hand over, deliver | antonyms: harbor, protect, shelter | verb

extradition:/ˌɛkstrəˈdɪʃən/ | n. | 引渡 | the act of extraditing a person accused or convicted of a crime | The extradition treaty between the two countries has been in effect for years.  这两个国家之间的引渡条约已经生效多年了。 |  His extradition to the United States was approved. 他被引渡到美国的请求得到了批准。 | synonyms: deportation, surrender | antonyms: asylum, refuge, sanctuary | noun
