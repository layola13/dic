ecosystem: /ˈiːkəʊˌsɪstəm/ (UK) /ˈiːkoʊˌsɪstəm/ (US)| n. |生态系统|A biological community of interacting organisms and their physical environment.|例句：The rainforest ecosystem is incredibly diverse.  雨林生态系统异常多样。

例句：Pollution can have a devastating impact on the ocean ecosystem. 污染会对海洋生态系统造成毁灭性的影响。|近义词：biocoenosis, biome, community|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

Etymology:  From *eco- + system. *Eco- comes from Greek *oikos*, meaning "house" or "dwelling".  System comes from Latin *systēma* and Greek *sústēma*, meaning “organized whole, a whole compounded of parts”.
