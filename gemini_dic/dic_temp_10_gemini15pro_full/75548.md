fuchsine:/ˈfjuːksɪn/ | n. adj. | 品红，苯胺红;  品红的 |A synthetic dye that produces a magenta color. Also used as an adjective to describe this color. | The fuchsine stain highlighted the cell structures under the microscope.  显微镜下的品红染色突出了细胞结构。| synonym: magenta, cerise, rose, ruby | antonym: cyan, turquoise | noun
fuchsine:/ˈfjuːksɪn/ | n. adj. | 品红，苯胺红; 品红的 | A vivid purplish red color.  |  She wore a fuchsine dress to the party. 她穿了一件品红色的连衣裙去参加派对。 | synonym: magenta, cerise, rose, ruby | antonym: cyan, turquoise | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *fuchsine*, named after the fuchsia flower, due to the similarity in color. The fuchsia flower itself is named after the German botanist Leonhart Fuchs (1501-1566).
