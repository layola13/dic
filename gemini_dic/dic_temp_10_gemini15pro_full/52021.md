diaheliotropic:/ˌdaɪəˌhiːliəˈtrɒpɪk/ | adj. | 向日性的，背日性的 |  Turning or orienting away from the sun.  It describes the movement of a plant or plant part in relation to the sun, specifically moving *away* from the sun, unlike heliotropic which means moving *towards* the sun. | The leaves of some desert plants are diaheliotropic to minimize water loss due to excessive sunlight.  一些沙漠植物的叶子具有背日性，以最大限度地减少因阳光过多而造成的水分流失。 | The compass plant exhibits diaheliotropic behavior during the hottest part of the day.  罗盘草在一天中最热的时候表现出背日性。 | 近义词: apheliotropic | 反义词: heliotropic | 词性: adjective
