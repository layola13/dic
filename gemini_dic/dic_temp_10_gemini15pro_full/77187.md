gasp-forth:/ˈɡæsp fɔːrθ/ |v. |喘着气说出；气喘吁吁地说出 |To utter with gasps; to say something breathlessly, as if struggling for air. |She gasped forth a few words, barely audible above the wind. 她喘着气说了几句话，几乎被风声盖过。

He gasped forth a plea for help. 他气喘吁吁地呼救。|近义词:choke out, stammer, stutter |反义词: articulate clearly, enunciate |词性：动词
