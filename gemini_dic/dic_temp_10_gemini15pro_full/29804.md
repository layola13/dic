caudex:/ˈkɔːdɛks/| n. |茎干，根茎；（尤指植物的）主干|The caudex of this plant stores water.  这种植物的茎干储存水分。|The thick caudex of the baobab tree enables it to survive long droughts.  猴面包树粗壮的茎干使其能够在长期干旱中生存。|近义词：trunk, stem, base|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps branches, leaves referring to other parts of the plant)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *caudex*, meaning "tree trunk," ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *keudh- meaning "wood."
