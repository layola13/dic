orthospermous:/ɔːrθoʊˈspɜːrməs/| adj.| 直立胚珠的，直生胚珠的 |Having ovules that are erect or straight, as opposed to curved or inverted. | The orthospermous arrangement of the ovules is a characteristic of certain plant families.  这种直立胚珠的排列方式是某些植物科的特征。| The *Magnolia* genus is known for its orthospermous ovules.  木兰属以其直立胚珠而闻名。 | synonyms: atropous, straight-ovuled | antonyms: anatropous, campylotropous | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *orthos* (straight, correct) + *sperma* (seed)
