googly:/ˈɡuːɡli/ | n. | 曲球（板球运动中）| A type of deceptive delivery in cricket where a leg-spin bowler delivers a ball that turns the opposite way to a normal leg-break, appearing to turn from the off side to the leg side. | The batsman was completely bamboozled by the googly.  击球手完全被这个曲球迷惑了。

| Example: The googly completely deceived the batsman. 这个曲球完全骗过了击球手。

| Synonyms: wrong 'un, bosie | Antonyms: leg-break | Noun
