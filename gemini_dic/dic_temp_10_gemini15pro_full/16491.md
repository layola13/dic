bargees:/ˈbɑːrdʒiːz/
noun
驳船夫；驳船工人
A person employed on a barge, especially as the owner or captain.
The bargees tied up alongside the wharf for the night. 驳船夫们把船停靠在码头边过夜。

近义词:bargeman, boatman, waterman
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps landlubber as someone unfamiliar with boats)
词性:noun
