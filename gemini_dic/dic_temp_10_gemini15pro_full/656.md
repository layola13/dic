abrogate:/ˈæbrəɡeɪt/| v. |废除，取消|To officially end a law, agreement, or custom.|The treaty was abrogated in 1998.  该条约于1998年被废除。

The government abrogated the law. 政府废除了这项法律。|近义词：repeal, revoke, annul, nullify, abolish|反义词：enact, establish, institute, ratify|词性:动词
