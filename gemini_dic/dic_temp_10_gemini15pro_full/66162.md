excerptions: /ɪkˈsɜːrpʃənz/ | n. | 摘录；选段；节选 |  Sections of a text or music selected and copied out. |  She included excerpts from his letters in her biography.  她在传记中加入了他信件的摘录。 | synonyms: extracts, selections, passages, quotations | antonyms: whole, complete, entire | noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *excerptus*, past participle of excerpere "to pick out, pluck out," from ex "out" + carpere "to pluck, grasp."
