hurry:/ˈhɜːri/ (US) /ˈhʌri/ (UK)| n. v.|匆忙，急忙；催促|A state of urgency or rushed activity.  The act of moving or doing something quickly.|例句：I'm in a hurry to catch the train. 我要赶火车，很匆忙。|近义词：rush, hasten, speed, expedite|反义词：delay, dawdle, linger|词性：名词，动词
hurry:/ˈhɜːri/ (US) /ˈhʌri/ (UK)| v.|催促；赶紧|To urge or cause someone to move or act quickly.|例句：Don't hurry me; I need time to think. 别催我，我需要时间思考。|近义词：rush, hasten, urge, press|反义词：delay, slow, hinder|词性：动词

Etymology: From Middle English *hurrien*, of uncertain origin, perhaps related to Old French *hurter* "to knock against, to clash".
