half-english:/ˌhɑːf ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ/ | adj. | 半英语的；英语夹杂其他语言的 |  Describing someone who speaks a mixture of English and another language, often their native language.  This term can sometimes be considered derogatory. |  He speaks half-English, half-Spanish at home. 他在家说半英语半西班牙语。| Synonym: broken English (sometimes derogatory)| Antonym: fluent English | adjective
