casserole:/ˈkæsəˌroʊl/ (US), /ˈkæsərɒl/ (UK)| n.|砂锅；砂锅菜；烤菜|A dish of meat and vegetables cooked slowly in liquid in a covered dish, typically in an oven.|例句：She made a chicken casserole for dinner. 她晚餐做了鸡肉砂锅菜。

例句：The casserole was bubbling hot from the oven. 砂锅菜刚从烤箱里出来，热气腾腾。|近义词：hotdish, stew, bake|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用salad 沙拉等表示不同烹饪方式的食物)|词性:noun
