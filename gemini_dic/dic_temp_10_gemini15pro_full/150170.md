panached:/pəˈnætʃt/ /ˈpænætʃt/ | adj. | 杂色的，斑驳的 | Having variegated colors or markings, especially of leaves.  Derived from the French word "panaché," meaning "variegated" or "streaked."  Ultimately from "panache," meaning a plume of feathers, suggesting the varied colors often seen in plumage. | The panached leaves of the coleus plant add a vibrant touch to the garden.  彩叶草的杂色叶子为花园增添了活力。| variegated, mottled, dappled, piebald | uniform, monochrome | adjective
