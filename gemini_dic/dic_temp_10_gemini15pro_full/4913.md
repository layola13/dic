aline:/əˈlaɪn/ | v. | 使成一直线，对准；与...保持一致 |  To bring into line or alignment; to align with. |  She carefully alined the edges of the paper before cutting. 她仔细地把纸的边缘对齐才开始剪。| synonyms: align, adjust, straighten, line up | antonyms: misalign, skew, distort | verb


/əˈlaɪn/ | adj. |  成一直线的，对准的 ( less common usage) | Arranged in a straight line; aligned. | The planets were alined, creating a rare celestial event. 行星排成一条直线，形成了罕见的天文现象。| synonyms: aligned, straight, linear | antonyms: misaligned, crooked, askew | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From the French "à ligne" meaning "to the line".  Ultimately from Latin "linea" meaning "line".
