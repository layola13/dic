paramilitarists:/ˌpærəˈmɪlətərɪsts/ | n. |准军事人员；辅助战斗人员|Members of a paramilitary organization, a group organized like a military force but not part of the regular army.|The paramilitarists seized control of the border crossing. 准军事人员控制了边境通道。|近义词：militia, irregulars, guerrillas |反义词: soldiers, troops |词性:noun

paramilitary:/ˌpærəˈmɪlətri/ | adj. |准军事的；辅助战斗的|Of or relating to a paramilitary organization.|The government deployed paramilitary forces to quell the unrest. 政府部署了准军事部队来平息骚乱。|近义词：militia, irregular |反义词: military, regular |词性:adjective

词源: para- (beside, alongside) + military.  "Para-" comes from Greek and means "alongside" or "beside."  Thus, a paramilitary group operates alongside or in a similar manner to a military force, but is not formally part of it.
