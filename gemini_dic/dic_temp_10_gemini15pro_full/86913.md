hemmer:/ˈhɛmər/| n.|卷边器；缝边的人|A device or person that hems fabric.|She used a hemmer to finish the edge of the skirt. 她用卷边器缝好了裙子的边。|近义词:sewing machine, tailor|反义词:None|noun

hemmer:/ˈhɛmər/| v.|缝边；包围；限制|To fold and sew the edge of a piece of cloth; to surround and confine.|He hemmed the tablecloth. 他给桌布缝了边。  The soldiers were hemmed in by the enemy. 士兵们被敌人包围了。|近义词:sew, enclose, confine, restrict|反义词:liberate, free|verb
