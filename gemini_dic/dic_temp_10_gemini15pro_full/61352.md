embellishment:/ɪmˈbɛlɪʃmənt/| n.|装饰，修饰，润色|A decorative detail or feature added to something to make it more attractive.|The dress was simple, but the embellishments on the sleeves made it special. 这件衣服很简单，但袖子上的装饰使它很特别。|synonyms: decoration, ornamentation, adornment, enhancement|antonyms: plainness, simplicity|noun
