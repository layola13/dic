boatmen:/ˈboʊtmən/ (US), /ˈbəʊtmən/ (UK)| n. |船夫；船员|A man who works on or operates a boat, especially one who rows or punts it.|The boatmen ferried tourists across the river. 船夫们用渡船把游客送到河对岸。|近义词：sailor, oarsman, ferryman|反义词:passenger|noun
