alkanes:/ˈælkeɪnz/| n.|烷烃|Saturated hydrocarbons with the general formula CnH2n+2, where n is the number of carbon atoms.  They are a homologous series of organic compounds.|Methane and ethane are simple alkanes. 甲烷和乙烷是简单的烷烃。|Propane is an alkane commonly used as fuel. 丙烷是一种常用的烷烃燃料。|synonyms: paraffins|antonyms: alkenes, alkynes|noun
