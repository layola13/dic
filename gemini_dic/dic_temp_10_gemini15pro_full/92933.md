identifier:/aɪˈdɛntɪfaɪər/ | n. | 标识符，识别符 |A name that identifies (that is, labels the identity of) a variable, type, template, class, or other entity in a program.  A sequence of characters used to uniquely name a program element. |  Example:  The "x" in the equation "x + 2 = 4" is an identifier.  x + 2 = 4 中的 "x" 是一个标识符。

Example: In Java, "myVariable" is a valid identifier. 在Java中，“myVariable”是一个有效的标识符。 | synonym: name, label, tag, handle | antonym: N/A | noun
