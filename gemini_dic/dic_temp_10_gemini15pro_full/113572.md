macaw:/məˈkɔː/ | n. |金刚鹦鹉|A large, long-tailed parrot with brightly coloured plumage, native to Central and South America.|例句：The macaw perched on the branch, displaying its vibrant feathers. 金刚鹦鹉栖息在树枝上，展示着它鲜艳的羽毛。

例句：We saw a flock of macaws flying over the rainforest. 我们看到一群金刚鹦鹉飞过雨林。|近义词：parrot|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
