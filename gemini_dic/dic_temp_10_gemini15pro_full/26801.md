caffs:/kæfs/ | n. | 咖啡馆（非正式，常用复数） | Informal, chiefly British, plural of caff. A small, inexpensive restaurant. |  We went to a couple of caffs for breakfast. 我们去了几家咖啡馆吃早餐。|  They met up at one of their favorite caffs. 他们在他们最喜欢的咖啡馆之一碰面。| synonyms: cafes, coffee shops, diners | antonyms: restaurants (more formal/expensive) | noun
