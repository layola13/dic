attractability:/əˌtræktəˈbɪləti/
n.
可吸引性，吸引力
The quality of being easily attracted, particularly to a magnet or magnetic field.  It can also refer to the ability of something to draw attention or interest.
The attractability of the new toy was evident in the children's faces.  这款新玩具的吸引力从孩子们脸上显而易见。
magnetism, appeal, allure, charm
repulsiveness, unattractiveness
noun
