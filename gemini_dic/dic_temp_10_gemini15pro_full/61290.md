embank:/ɪmˈbæŋk/ | v. |筑堤；围堵；保护|To enclose or protect with a bank, especially an embankment.  |The river was embanked to prevent flooding.  这条河被筑堤以防止洪水泛滥。|近义词：dike, levee, dam, enclose, protect|反义词: expose|词性:动词

embankment:/ɪmˈbæŋkmənt/ | n. |堤岸；路堤；筑堤|A wall or bank of earth or stone built to prevent a river flooding, or to support a road or railway. |The embankment protects the town from flooding.  堤岸保护城镇免受洪水侵袭。|近义词：dike, levee, bank, barrier|反义词: ditch, channel|词性:名词
