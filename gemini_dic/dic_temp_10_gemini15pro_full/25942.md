burntness:/ˈbɜːrntnɪs/
n.
烧焦程度;烧焦
the state or quality of being burnt
The burntness of the toast was alarming.  烤面包的烧焦程度令人担忧。

近义词:charring, scorch
反义词:freshness, rawness
词性:名词
