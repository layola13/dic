cloistral:/ˈklɔɪstrəl/| adj.|修道院的；隐居的；与世隔绝的|Relating to or characteristic of a cloister; secluded from the world.|例句：The library had a cloistral atmosphere, quiet and removed from the bustle of the city. 图书馆有一种修道院般的气氛，安静，远离城市的喧嚣。

例句：He led a cloistral life, dedicating himself to study and prayer. 他过着隐居的生活，致力于学习和祈祷。|近义词：secluded, monastic, solitary, withdrawn|反义词：worldly, gregarious, social|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *cloistral*, from Old French *cloistral*, from Late Latin *claustrālis*, from Latin *claustrum* ("cloister, enclosed place, barrier").
