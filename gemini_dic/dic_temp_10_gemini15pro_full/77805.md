generally:/ˈdʒenrəli/ | adv. | 通常地，一般地，普遍地 | In a general manner; usually; in most cases. |  People generally prefer warmer weather. 人们通常更喜欢温暖的天气。

Generally speaking, cats are independent animals.  一般来说，猫是独立的动物。

近义词: usually, typically, normally, commonly, ordinarily

反义词: rarely, seldom, infrequently, exceptionally, unusually

词性: adverb

词源: From Late Latin *generaliter, from Latin generalis (“general”).
