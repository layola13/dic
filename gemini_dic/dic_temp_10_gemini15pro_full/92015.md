hyperoxygenated:/ˌhaɪpərˈɒksɪdʒəneɪtɪd/ /ˌhaɪpərˈɑːksɪdʒəneɪtɪd/
adjective
过度氧化的；富氧的
Having abnormally high levels of oxygen.  Supplied with excess oxygen.
The patient was hyperoxygenated before the procedure.  病人手术前被过度充氧。
The diver breathed hyperoxygenated air.  潜水员呼吸了富氧空气。
近义词: oxygen-rich, superoxygenated
反义词: hypoxic, anoxic
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From hyper- (meaning "over, excessive") + oxygenated (meaning "treated or saturated with oxygen").  The word "oxygen" itself comes from Greek roots meaning "acid-forming".
