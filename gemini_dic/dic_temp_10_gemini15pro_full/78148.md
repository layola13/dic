geophysicist:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈfɪzɪsɪst/ | n. | 地球物理学家 | A scientist who studies the physics of the Earth, including its structure, composition, and processes. |  A geophysicist might study earthquakes to understand plate tectonics.  地球物理学家可能会研究地震以了解板块构造。

The geophysicist used seismic data to locate oil deposits.  这位地球物理学家利用地震数据来定位石油矿藏。 | Near synonyms: earth scientist, geoscientist | Antonym: N/A | Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From geo- (earth) + physicist.  "Geo" comes from the Greek "gē" meaning "earth". "Physicist" comes from "physics" ultimately deriving from the Greek "physikos" meaning "natural".
