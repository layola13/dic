handsprung:/ˈhændsprʌŋ/| n.|手翻，手翻跳|An acrobatic movement in which a person launches themselves forward and over onto their hands, then pushes off with their hands to land on their feet.  A forward handspring is a basic gymnastic skill.|She performed a perfect handspring on the balance beam.|近义词：cartwheel, flip, handspring vault|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps fall, stumble)| noun
