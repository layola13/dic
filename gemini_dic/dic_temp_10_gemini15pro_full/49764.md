demolitionist:/ˌdɛməˈlɪʃənɪst/ | n. |爆破专家，拆除专家|A person skilled in demolishing buildings or other structures, often using explosives. | The demolitionist carefully placed the charges on the condemned building. 爆破专家小心地将炸药放置在 condemned building 上。| The demolitionist wired the bridge for demolition. 爆破专家在桥上布线进行拆除。|  Synonyms: dynamiter, wrecker, blaster | Antonyms: builder, constructor | Noun
