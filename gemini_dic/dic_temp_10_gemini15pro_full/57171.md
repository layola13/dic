dowelled:/ˈdaʊəld/| adj.|销钉连接的，用木钉固定的|Joined or reinforced with dowels.  A dowel is a small wooden or metal rod used to strengthen joints in furniture or other wooden structures.|The bookcase was dowelled together for extra strength.  书架用木钉加固以增强强度。|近义词：pinned, pegged, fastened|反义词:loose, unconnected|词性:形容词
dowel:/ˈdaʊəl/| n. v.|销钉，木钉；用销钉固定|A small wooden or metal rod used to strengthen joints in furniture or other wooden structures. To fasten or reinforce with dowels.|The two pieces of wood were dowelled together.  这两块木头用销钉连接在一起。|近义词：pin, peg, fastener|反义词: N/A|词性:名词, 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Low German *dōvel, diminutive of *dōve "peg," of uncertain origin.
