darning:/ˈdɑːrnɪŋ/
n.
修补；缝补
The act of mending holes in fabric, usually with needle and thread.
She spent the evening darning socks. 她晚上都在缝补袜子。
mending, repairing, patching
creation, construction
noun

darn:/dɑːrn/
v.
缝补；织补
To mend a hole in fabric by weaving thread across it.
I need to darn this hole in my sweater. 我需要缝补一下毛衣上的这个洞。
mend, repair, patch
destroy, tear
verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *dernen*, from Old English *dearnian* (to hide, conceal), related to Old Norse *dyrma* (to darken). The sense shifted from "concealing a hole" to "mending a hole."
