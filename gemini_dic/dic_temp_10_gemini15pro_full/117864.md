melodically:/məˈlɒdɪkli/ (British) /məˈlɑːdɪkli/ (American)| adv.|旋律优美地，悦耳地|In a melodic way; with a pleasant tune.|She sang melodically, her voice filling the room. 她唱歌旋律优美，歌声充满了整个房间。|The pianist played the piece melodically, bringing out the beauty of the music.  钢琴家演奏这首曲子时旋律优美，展现了音乐的魅力。|近义词：musically, tunefully, harmoniously|反义词：discordantly, unmusically|词性: 副词

Etymology:
From melody + -ally.  "Melody" comes from the Late Latin *melodia, from Greek meloidia "singing, chanting a lyric poem," from melos "song, strain" + oide "song."
