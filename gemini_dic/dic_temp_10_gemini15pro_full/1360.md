accumulative:/əˈkjuːmjələtɪv/| adj.|累积的，渐增的|Increasing or growing by accumulation or successive additions.|The accumulative effect of pollution is devastating the environment. 污染的累积效应正在破坏环境。

The lawyer presented accumulative evidence to prove the defendant's guilt. 律师提供了累积的证据来证明被告有罪。|近义词：cumulative, increasing, gathering, accruing|反义词：decreasing, diminishing, subtractive|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *accumulativus, from Latin accumulatus (past participle of accumulare "to heap up"), from ad- "to" + cumulare "to heap," from cumulus "heap."

USAGE->
The accumulative effects of stress can lead to serious health problems.
长期累积的压力会导致严重的健康问题。

His accumulative wealth came from years of shrewd investments.
他积累的财富来自于多年精明的投资。


PHRASE->
accumulative effect = 累积效应
accumulative process = 累积过程
accumulative power = 累积力量
accumulative total = 累计总数
accumulative evidence = 累积证据
