gradualness:/ˈɡrædʒuəlnəs/| n.|逐渐性，逐步性|The gradualness of the change made it almost imperceptible.  This refers to the slow and steady pace of a change or process.|例句：The gradualness of the economic recovery was encouraging. 经济复苏的逐步性令人鼓舞。

The gradualness of her learning process allowed her to truly master the skills. 她学习过程的循序渐进让她真正掌握了这些技能。|近义词：slowness, steadiness, progressiveness|反义词:suddenness, abruptness, rapidity|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From gradual + -ness.  "Gradual" comes from the Late Latin "gradualis,"  meaning "by degrees,"  from "gradus" meaning "step" or "degree."  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns expressing state, quality, or condition.
