diehard:/ˈdaɪhɑːrd/| n. adj.|顽固分子，死硬派；顽固的，死硬的|A diehard is someone who is unwilling to change their beliefs or actions, even when there is good reason to.  As an adjective, it describes someone with such qualities.|He's a diehard fan of the team. 他是球队的铁杆球迷。|近义词：stubborn, obstinate, resolute, inflexible|反义词：flexible, adaptable, open-minded|词性: noun, adjective

词源: Originally referred to soldiers who fought to the death, even when defeat was inevitable.  Later broadened to describe anyone with unyielding beliefs or habits.
