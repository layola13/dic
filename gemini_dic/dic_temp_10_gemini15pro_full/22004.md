boilerplate:/ˈbɔɪlərpleɪt/ (US), /ˈbɔɪləpleɪt/ (UK)| n. adj.|样板文件，公式化文件；样板式，陈腐的| Standardized or formulaic language, often used in legal documents or other official texts. It can also refer to pre-fabricated pieces of metal, but this usage is less common now. |The lawyer quickly skimmed the boilerplate language in the contract. 律师快速浏览了合同中的样板条款。|Synonyms: formulaic, standard, stock, clichéd, routine, conventional|Antonyms: original, innovative, unique, creative|noun

boilerplate:/ˈbɔɪlərpleɪt/ (US), /ˈbɔɪləpleɪt/ (UK)| adj.|样板式的，陈腐的|Standardized and unoriginal; formulaic.  |The politician delivered a boilerplate speech that lacked any real substance. 这位政客发表了一篇毫无实质内容的陈腐演讲。|Synonyms: formulaic, standard, stock, clichéd, routine, conventional|Antonyms: original, innovative, unique, creative|adjective
