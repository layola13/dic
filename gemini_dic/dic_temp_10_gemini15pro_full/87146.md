herbicides: /ˈhɜːrbɪsaɪdz/ | n. | 除草剂 | A substance that is toxic to plants and is used to destroy unwanted vegetation. | Farmers use herbicides to control weeds in their crops. 农民使用除草剂来控制农作物中的杂草。| The gardener sprayed herbicide on the weeds. 园丁向杂草喷洒了除草剂。 | synonyms: weedkiller, weedicide | antonyms: fertilizer | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *herba* (herb) +‎ *-cida* (killer), from *caedere* (to cut, kill).
