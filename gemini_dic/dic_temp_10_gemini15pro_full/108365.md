lavishness:/ˈlævɪʃnəs/| n.| 奢侈，豪华，慷慨 |The lavishness of the wedding impressed all the guests. 婚礼的奢华给所有宾客留下了深刻的印象。| extravagance, luxuriance, profusion |frugality, economy, sparseness |noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From lavish + -ness. Lavish itself comes from Middle English *lavisshe, from Old French lavasse (“deluge, downpour”), from laver (“to wash”).  Originally meant "profusely given," later evolving to "extravagant, luxurious."
