oillike:/ˈɔɪlˌlaɪk/| adj.|像油的，油状的|Resembling oil in appearance, texture, or properties; oily.|The substance had an oillike sheen. 这种物质有一种像油一样的光泽。|近义词：oily, greasy, unctuous|反义词：dry, watery|词性:形容词
词源：oil + like.  "Oil" comes from Old French "oile," ultimately from Latin "oleum." "Like" comes from Old English "gelic."
