oralisms:/ˈɔːrəlɪzəmz/| n.|口语体，口语用法|Words or phrases characteristic of spoken language, as opposed to formal writing.  These can include slang, contractions, and colloquialisms.|Her essay was full of oralisms, making it sound more like a casual conversation than an academic paper. 她这篇文章充满了口语体，听起来更像是一场随意的对话，而不是学术论文。|synonyms: colloquialisms, informal language, slang, vernacular|antonyms: formal language, written language|noun

oralism:/ˈɔːrəlɪzəm/| n.|口语主义|A method of teaching deaf people to communicate by lip reading and speaking, rather than using sign language.|The school adopted an oralism approach to deaf education. 这所学校采用了口语主义的聋哑教育方法。|synonyms: speech training, lip reading method|antonyms: sign language, manualism|noun
