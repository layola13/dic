overcredit:/ˌoʊvərˈkrɛdɪt/| vt. n.|透支信用；过度授信；超额信贷|To extend credit beyond a reasonable limit; the condition of having been granted excessive credit.|The bank overcredited his account by $500. 银行透支了他的账户500美元。|近义词：overextend, overlend|反义词: undercredit|verb

overcredit:/ˌoʊvərˈkrɛdɪt/| n.|透支信用；过度授信；超额信贷|The state of having more credit than is justified or prudent.|He was in a state of overcredit and struggled to make his repayments. 他处于过度信贷状态，难以偿还债务。|近义词：overextension, overlending|反义词: undercredit|noun
