carpospore:/ˈkɑːrpəˌspɔːr/| n. |果孢子|A non-motile spore formed within the cystocarp or pericarp, characteristic of red algae.|The red algae release carpospores to reproduce. 红藻释放果孢子进行繁殖。|Synonym:  (loosely) tetraspore |Antonym:  N/A|noun
