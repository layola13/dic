cestode:/ˈsɛstoʊd/| n. |绦虫；链状带绦虫|A parasitic flatworm, the adult of which lives in the intestines of vertebrates. |例句：The cestode, a type of parasitic worm, can infect humans through contaminated food. 这种绦虫是一种寄生虫，可以通过受污染的食物感染人类。

例句：The veterinarian identified the parasite as a cestode based on its segmented body.  兽医根据其分段的身体将寄生虫鉴定为绦虫。|近义词：tapeworm|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French cestoïde, from Greek kestos 'girdle' + -oid.
