countervailing:/ˌkaʊntərˈveɪlɪŋ/
adjective
抵消的，抗衡的
acting against something so as to cancel its effect; offsetting.
The government introduced countervailing measures to offset the effects of the recession. 政府采取了抵消措施来应对经济衰退的影响。

The company increased prices to countervail the rising cost of raw materials.  公司提高了价格以抵消原材料成本的上涨。

Synonyms: offsetting, compensating, neutralizing, balancing
Antonyms: reinforcing, strengthening, supplementing
词性: 形容词
