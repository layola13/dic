inspectorial:/ɪnˌspɛkˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
检查的；视察的
of or relating to an inspector or inspection.
The inspectorial board reviewed the safety procedures of the factory.  检查委员会审查了工厂的安全程序。
The report highlighted the inspectorial powers of the agency. 报告强调了该机构的检查权力。

近义词: supervisory, examining, investigative, scrutinizing
反义词: uncritical, inattentive, negligent

词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *inspectorialis, from inspector + -alis.
