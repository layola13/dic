hawebake: /ˈhɔːbeɪk/ | n. | 烤面包；烘烤食品 | A baked good, especially bread. |  My grandmother used to make delicious hawebake every Sunday.  我祖母以前每个星期天都会做美味的烤面包。 | 近义词: bread, baked goods, loaf | 反义词: dough | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From Middle English *hawebake, from Old English *hāf-bæc, from hāf (“yeast, leaven”) + bæc (“bake”).  The word is now largely obsolete, except in some dialects.  The word "hawebake" is related to the modern German word "Hafen," meaning "yeast."
