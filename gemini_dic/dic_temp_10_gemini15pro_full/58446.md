ducks:/dʌks/| n.|鸭子|Plural form of duck. A waterbird with webbed feet, a broad flat bill, and relatively short legs. They are typically found in or near water and are known for their quacking sound.|例句：The ducks paddled serenely across the pond. 鸭子平静地在池塘里划水。

例句：Several ducks waddled up to the farmer, hoping for some food. 几只鸭子蹒跚地走向农民，希望能得到一些食物。|近义词：waterfowl, poultry|反义词：(No direct antonym for an animal.  Could consider "landfowl" as a loose contrast)|n.
