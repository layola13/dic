diamantine:/ˌdaɪəˈmæntiːn/ /ˌdaɪəˈmæntaɪn/
adjective
金刚石的；坚硬无比的；闪耀的
Resembling a diamond in hardness or brilliance.  Made of, or containing, diamond.
例句：Her diamantine eyes flashed with anger. 她的眼神像钻石般闪耀着愤怒。

例句：The diamantine blade easily sliced through the thick rope.  金刚石刀片轻易地切断了粗绳。

近义词：diamond-like, adamantine, hard, brilliant, sparkling, glittering
反义词：soft, dull, opaque

词性：adjective
