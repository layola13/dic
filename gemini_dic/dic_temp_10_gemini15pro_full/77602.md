geeks:/ɡiːks/| n.|极客，怪才，对计算机和技术特别着迷的人|A person who is especially enthusiastic about computers and technology. |例句：He's a real geek and spends all his time coding. 他是个真正的极客，所有时间都花在编程上。

例句：The geeks in the IT department are working on a new software program.  IT部门的极客们正在开发一个新的软件程序。


|近义词：nerd, techie, enthusiast|反义词: normal person, layman|词性:noun
