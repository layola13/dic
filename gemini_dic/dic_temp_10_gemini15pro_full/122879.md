mollusca:/məˈlʌskə/ | n. |软体动物|A large phylum of invertebrate animals (such as snails, clams, or squids) with a soft unsegmented body usually enclosed in a shell.  | Mollusca are a diverse group, found in both marine and freshwater environments. 软体动物种类繁多，在海洋和淡水环境中都能找到。| Snails and slugs are terrestrial mollusca. 蜗牛和蛞蝓是陆生软体动物。 | synonyms: shellfish, invertebrates | antonyms: vertebrates | noun
