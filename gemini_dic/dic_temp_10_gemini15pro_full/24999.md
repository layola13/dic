brutalize: /ˈbruːtəlaɪz/ | vt. | 残暴对待；使麻木不仁；使失去感受力 | To treat someone or something in a savagely violent or cruel way; to make someone insensitive to violence or suffering. | The soldiers brutalized the prisoners of war. 士兵们残酷地对待战俘。

The constant violence in the city had brutalized the population. 城市里持续不断的暴力已经使居民麻木不仁。 | 近义词: batter, abuse, mistreat, maltreat | 反义词: cherish, protect, nurture | 词性: 动词

brutalization: /ˌbruːtəlaɪˈzeɪʃn/ | n. |  野蛮行径; 残暴行为 | The act of brutalizing. | The brutalization of civilians during the war was horrific. 战争期间对平民的暴行骇人听闻。 | 近义词: cruelty, savagery, barbarity | 反义词: kindness, compassion, humanity | 词性: 名词

词源: From *brutal* (meaning "of or like a brute") + *-ize* (a suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or become").  *Brutal* comes from the Latin *brutalis*, meaning "of beasts," from *brutum*, "beast."
