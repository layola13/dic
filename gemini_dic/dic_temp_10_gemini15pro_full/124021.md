morosaurus:/ˌmɔːrəˈsɔːrəs/| n. |  迷惑龙（一种大型草食性恐龙） | A large herbivorous dinosaur of the late Jurassic period, with a very long neck and tail. | Example 1: The Morosaurus was one of the largest land animals ever to have lived. 迷惑龙是有史以来最大的陆地动物之一。

Example 2:  Scientists have discovered a new species of Morosaurus. 科学家们发现了一种新的迷惑龙物种。 | 近义词：apatosaurus, diplodocus, brachiosaurus | 反义词: N/A | 词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *mōros*  (stupid, dull) + *sauros* (lizard).  The name was given due to the small size of the braincase relative to its large body size, mistakenly thought to indicate low intelligence.
