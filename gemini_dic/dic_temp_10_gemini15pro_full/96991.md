infantilized: /ɪnˈfæn.tə.laɪzd/ (US) /ɪnˈfæn.taɪ.laɪzd/ (UK)| adj.|幼稚化的，被当作小孩对待的|Treated as if one were a child; made to appear or feel like a child; deprived of independence and responsibility.|She felt infantilized by her overprotective parents. 她觉得被她过度保护的父母当成了小孩。|The advertising campaign infantilized the consumer by using cartoon characters and childish language.  这个广告活动通过使用卡通人物和幼稚的语言将消费者幼稚化。|近义词: patronized, condescended to, coddled|反义词: empowered, respected, treated as an adult|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From infant (婴儿) + -ize (动词后缀，表示“使成为…”) + -ed (形容词后缀，表示“被…的”)
