civilisable:/ˈsɪvɪlaɪzəbl/| adj.|可开化的，可教化的|Capable of being civilized; susceptible to civilizing influences.|例句：The tribe was considered civilisable and receptive to new ideas. 这个部落被认为是可开化的，容易接受新思想。

例句：Some historians argue that not all societies are civilisable in the same way. 一些历史学家认为，并非所有社会都能以同样的方式被开化。|近义词：educable, teachable, refinable, culturable|反义词：uncivilized, barbaric, savage|词性:形容词
词源: From civilize + -able.  Ultimately from Latin *civis* "citizen".
