emphatic:/ɪmˈfætɪk/| adj.|强调的，着重的|Done or said in a strong way to show importance. |She was emphatic that she did not want to go. 她坚决表示不想去。|近义词：forceful, assertive, insistent|反义词：hesitant, tentative, weak|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *emphaticus, from Greek emphatikos "expressive," from emphainein "to exhibit, display," from en- "in" + phainein "to show."
