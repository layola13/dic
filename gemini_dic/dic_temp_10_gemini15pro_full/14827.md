axinite:/ˈæksɪnaɪt/| n. |斧石|A brown, violet, or yellow-green mineral, a complex borosilicate of aluminium, calcium, iron, and manganese, occurring as triclinic crystals.|The geologist discovered a rare specimen of axinite in the cave. 地质学家在山洞里发现了一块稀有的斧石标本。|近义词: mineral, borosilicate |反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific mineral) |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *axinite*, from Latin *axinis* (“axe”), from Greek *ἀξίνη* (*axínē*).  The name refers to the characteristic wedge or axe-like shape of the crystals.
