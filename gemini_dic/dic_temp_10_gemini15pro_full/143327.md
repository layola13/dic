outechoed:/ˌaʊtˈɛkoʊd/
|v.|胜过回声，盖过回声|To surpass or drown out in sound, especially by echoing more loudly or persistently.|Her voice outechoed the thunder. 她的声音盖过了雷声。| surpass, drown out, outsound| - |verb
outechoed:/ˌaʊtˈɛkoʊd/
|adj.|被胜过回声的|Surpassed or drowned out in sound, especially by a louder or more persistent echo.|The outechoed cries of the birds were barely audible. 鸟儿被盖过回声的叫声几乎听不见。| muffled, subdued | amplified, loud |adjective
