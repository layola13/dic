mistuned:/ˌmɪsˈtjuːnd/ /ˌmɪsˈtuːnd/
adjective
失谐的，音调不准的
incorrectly tuned; out of tune.
The piano was badly mistuned, making the melody sound jarring.  这架钢琴音调很不准，使得旋律听起来很不和谐。
The mistuned guitar made a horrible noise.  失谐的吉他发出可怕的声音。
near: out of tune, off-key, discordant
antonym: in tune, tuned
adjective
