charlatanically: /ˌʃɑːrlətˈænɪkli/ | adv. | 江湖骗子式地，装作内行地 | In a way that suggests quackery or deceitful pretense of knowledge. | He charlatanically claimed to be a medical expert, despite having no formal training. 他骗子般地声称自己是医学专家，尽管他没有接受过任何正规训练。|  The salesman charlatanically promoted the product, making exaggerated claims about its effectiveness.  这个推销员像江湖骗子一样推销产品，夸大其词地宣传其功效。 | synonym: fraudulently, deceptively, dishonestly, falsely | antonym: genuinely, honestly, sincerely, truthfully |词性: 副词

Etymology:
From charlatan + -ical + -ly.  Charlatan comes from the Italian word *ciarlatano*, derived from *ciarlare* "to chatter."
