druggies:/ˈdrʌɡiz/
n.
吸毒者，瘾君子
People who regularly use illegal drugs.
The police arrested a group of druggies in the park. 警察在公园里逮捕了一群瘾君子。
近义词：addicts, junkies, users
反义词：n/a
词性：名词

druggies:/ˈdrʌɡiz/
n.
吸毒成瘾的人
Individuals who are addicted to drugs.
The rehabilitation center helps druggies get clean.  康复中心帮助吸毒者戒毒。
近义词：drug abusers, substance abusers
反义词：n/a
词性：名词


词源:  From drug + -ie (a suffix forming diminutive nouns).  "Drug" originates from Middle Dutch "droge vate," meaning "dry barrels" (referring to dried herbs sold by apothecaries).
