nonprogrammable:/ˌnɒnˈprəʊɡræməbl/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˈproʊɡræməbl/| adj.|不可编程的，非程序化的|Not able to be programmed; fixed or permanent in function.|This old washing machine is nonprogrammable, so you have to manually adjust the settings for each wash. 这台旧洗衣机是不可编程的，所以每次洗涤都必须手动调整设置。| The thermostat is nonprogrammable, meaning you can't set different temperatures for different times of day.  这个恒温器是不可编程的，这意味着你无法为一天中的不同时间设置不同的温度。|non-programmable, fixed, hardwired|programmable|adjective
