cyaniding:/ˈsaɪəˌnaɪdɪŋ/ | n. | 氰化法，氰化处理 | The process of extracting gold or silver from ore by dissolving it in a solution of cyanide. |  The cyaniding process is controversial due to its environmental impact. 氰化法由于其对环境的影响而备受争议。 | 近义词: cyanide leaching | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps alternative extraction methods like "gravity separation") | 词性: noun


cyaniding:/ˈsaɪəˌnaɪdɪŋ/ | v. | 用氰化物处理 | To treat with cyanide, especially to extract gold or silver. |  The company is cyaniding the ore to extract the gold.  这家公司正在用氰化物处理矿石以提取黄金。 | 近义词: cyanide leaching, treating with cyanide | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: verb (gerund or present participle)
