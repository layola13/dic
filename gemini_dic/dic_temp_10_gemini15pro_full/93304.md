ill-considered:/ˌɪl kənˈsɪdərd/
adjective
欠考虑的；轻率的
Not thought about carefully; unwise.
例句：
That was an ill-considered remark.
那是一句欠考虑的话。

It was an ill-considered decision that he later regretted.
这是一个他后来后悔的轻率决定。

近义词：
injudicious, unwise, imprudent, rash, hasty, thoughtless, ill-advised, foolhardy, reckless

反义词：
well-considered, prudent, wise, judicious, thoughtful

词性
adjective
