edisons:/ˈɛdɪs(ə)nz/
n.
Edisons (plural)
爱迪生们（复数）
Refers to people like Thomas Edison, known for their inventiveness and ingenuity.  This is usually used figuratively.
The company is looking for the edisons of the 21st century.  这家公司正在寻找21世纪的爱迪生们。
近义词: innovators, inventors, geniuses
反义词:  followers, copycats
词性: 名词

词源:
Proper noun, surname derived from "son of Edward".  Used generically, it refers to people similar to Thomas Edison, the famous American inventor.
