evaders:/ɪˈveɪdərz/ | n. | 逃避者，规避者 | People who avoid something, especially obligations or responsibilities. | The tax evaders were caught and prosecuted.  逃税者被抓获并被起诉。 |  synonyms: avoiders, shirkers, dodgers, escapees | antonyms: conformists, followers, adherents | noun

evades:/ɪˈveɪdz/ | v. | 逃避，规避 | To escape or avoid something, especially by cleverness or trickery. | He evaded the question by changing the subject. 他通过改变话题来回避这个问题。 | synonyms: avoids, dodges, eludes, bypasses | antonyms: confronts, faces, meets, tackles | verb
