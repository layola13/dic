ahoy:/əˈhɔɪ/| int.|喂；啊喂(招呼用语，尤用于船上)|Used to hail a ship or boat.  A nautical call for attention.|Ahoy there, matey!  啊喂，伙计！|近义词：hello, hey (informal)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|interjection
