butylamine:/ˈbjuːtɪləmiːn/ /ˈbjutl̩əˌmin/| n. |丁胺|An organic compound with the formula CH3(CH2)3NH2.  It is a colorless liquid with a fishy odor. It is used in the manufacture of rubber chemicals, pharmaceuticals, and pesticides.|Butylamine is a common precursor for the manufacture of rubber vulcanization accelerators. 丁胺是制造橡胶硫化促进剂的常用前体。|Butylamine can cause burns to skin and eyes. 丁胺会灼伤皮肤和眼睛。|近义词: n-butylamine, 1-aminobutane|反义词: None|词性:noun

etymology->
From butyl + amine.  Butyl refers to the four-carbon alkyl group and amine indicates the presence of an amino group (-NH2).
