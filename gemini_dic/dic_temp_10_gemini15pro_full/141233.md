ooze-out:/uːz aʊt/
verb
渗出，慢慢流出
to flow out slowly or gradually, or to be emitted slowly in drops or small quantities.
The resin oozed out from the tree.  树脂从树上慢慢渗出。
Pus oozed out of the wound. 伤口渗出了脓液。
Synonyms: seep, leak, exude, discharge
Antonyms: absorb, soak up
