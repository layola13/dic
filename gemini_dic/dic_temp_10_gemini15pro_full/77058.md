garnetlike: /ˈɡɑːrnɪtˌlaɪk/
adjective
像石榴石的，石榴石色的
Resembling a garnet, especially in color or deep red transparency.  Having the characteristics of a garnet.
The wine had a garnetlike hue.  这葡萄酒有像石榴石一样的色调。
Her dress was a deep, garnetlike red.  她的裙子是深石榴红色的。
近义词:  garnet-colored, ruby-colored, crimson, deep red
反义词:  colorless, pale
词性: adjective
