nonpromiscuously:/ˌnɑːnprəˈmɪskjuəsli/
adverb|非滥交地；非混杂地|In a way that is not promiscuous; without engaging in many sexual relationships.|She chose her partners nonpromiscuously, valuing commitment and emotional connection.|近义词：selectively, chastely, discreetly, modestly|反义词：promiscuously, indiscriminately|词性: 副词

Etymology:
From non- + promiscuous + -ly.  "Promiscuous" comes from the Latin "promiscuus," meaning "mixed, indiscriminate," from "pro-" (forth, forward) + "miscere" (to mix).
