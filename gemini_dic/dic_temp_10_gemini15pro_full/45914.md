cutouts:/ˈkʌtaʊts/| n.|剪纸；镂空装饰；(服装的)挖剪；删减部分|Shapes cut out of paper or other material, or a part of something that has been removed by cutting.|例句：The children made cutouts of snowflakes. 孩子们做了雪花的剪纸。

例句：The dress had attractive cutouts on the shoulders. 这件连衣裙肩膀上有漂亮的镂空设计。|近义词：shapes, figures, designs, holes, excisions|反义词: additions, inclusions|词性: noun
