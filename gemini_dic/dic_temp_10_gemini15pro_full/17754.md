bedfast:/ˈbedfæst/| adj.|卧床不起的；长期卧床的|Confined to bed by illness or infirmity.  Unable to get out of bed.|She was bedfast for months after the accident. 她事故后卧床不起好几个月了。|近义词：bedridden, confined to bed|反义词: ambulatory, mobile|adjective
