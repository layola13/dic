fell-out:/ˈfɛl aʊt/ | n. | 争吵，不和，掉落物 | a quarrel or disagreement; something that has fallen out |  The two friends had a bitter fell-out over money. 这两个朋友因为钱闹得很不愉快。 | 近义词：argument, disagreement, quarrel, dispute, fallout | 反义词：agreement, reconciliation, accord | 词性：名词

fell-out:/ˈfɛl aʊt/ | adj. | 脱落的，掉落的 | having fallen out |  Her fell-out hair was all over the pillow. 她掉落的头发散落在枕头上。 | 近义词：loose, detached, shed | 反义词：attached, fixed | 词性：形容词
