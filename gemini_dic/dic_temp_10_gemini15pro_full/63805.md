epidemic:/ˌɛpɪˈdɛmɪk/ | n. adj. | 流行病，流行的 | A widespread occurrence of an infectious disease in a community at a particular time. |  The city was struggling to contain the measles epidemic.  这个城市正在努力控制麻疹的流行。| synonyms: outbreak, plague, pandemic | antonyms: isolated case, sporadic | noun

epidemic:/ˌɛpɪˈdɛmɪk/ | adj. | 流行的，盛行的 | Affecting or tending to affect a disproportionately large number of individuals within a population, community, or region at the same time. |  Social media can make rumors epidemic. 社交媒体会让谣言迅速传播。| synonyms: widespread, rampant, prevalent | antonyms: rare, uncommon, isolated | adjective
