housework:/ˈhaʊswɜːrk/| n.|家务劳动，家务事|Regular housework includes cleaning, laundry, and cooking.  家务劳动包括清洁、洗衣和做饭。|例句：She spends most of her Saturdays doing housework. 她大部分的星期六都在做家务。|近义词：chores, domestic work|反义词: leisure, recreation|词性:名词
Etymology:  From "house" (Old English "hūs") + "work" (Old English "weorc"). 字面意思是“房屋的工作”。
