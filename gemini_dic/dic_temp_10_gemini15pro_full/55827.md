dividedness:/dɪˈvaɪdɪdnəs/
n.
分裂，分歧
The state of being divided; disunity.
The dividedness of the country is a serious problem.  这个国家的分裂是一个严重的问题。

The political dividedness of the nation is alarming. 这个国家的政治分歧令人担忧。

近义词:division, disunity, discord, schism, fragmentation
反义词:unity, harmony, agreement, solidarity, cohesion
词性:名词
