guff:/ɡʌf/
guffs:/ɡʌfs/
| n. | 废话；胡说；空话；假话 |  worthless or foolish talk, or ideas that are not true | He gave me a load of guff about needing the money for his sick mother. 他胡说八道说需要钱给他生病的母亲。|近义词：nonsense, rubbish, baloney, hogwash, bunk, claptrap |反义词:sense, truth |词性:名词

guff:/ɡʌf/
guffs:/ɡʌfs/
| v. | 胡说；瞎扯 | to talk foolishly or say things that are not true | Stop guffing and tell me what really happened. 别胡扯了，告诉我到底发生了什么。|近义词：blather, waffle, drivel |反义词:be honest |词性:动词
