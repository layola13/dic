balances:/ˈbælənsɪz/| n. v.|余额，平衡，权衡|The act of comparing or assessing two things to see how they are similar or different.|She checked her account balance. 她查了她的账户余额。|synonyms: equilibrium, stability, symmetry, remainder|antonyms: imbalance, instability, asymmetry|noun

balances:/ˈbælənsɪz/| v.|平衡，权衡，抵消|To keep something in a steady position so that it does not fall.|He balanced the book on his head. 他把书顶在头上保持平衡。|synonyms: stabilize, steady, equalize, counteract|antonyms: destabilize, unbalance, tip|verb
