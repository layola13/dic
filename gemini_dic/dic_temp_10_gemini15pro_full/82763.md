guillemot:/ˈɡɪləmɒt/ (UK), /ˈɡɪləˌmɑːt/ (US)| n. |海鸠|A seabird of northern and Arctic waters, with short wings and a thick body.|例句：A flock of guillemots perched on the cliffs. 一群海鸠栖息在悬崖上。|例句：The guillemot plunged into the icy water to catch a fish. 海鸠跳入冰冷的海水中捕鱼。|近义词：auk, murre|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From French *guillemot*, perhaps from Guillaume "William" (a common personal name), because of the bird's supposed resemblance to a clumsy person.
