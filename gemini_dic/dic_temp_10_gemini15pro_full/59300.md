eanling:/ˈjiːnlɪŋ/| n.|一岁小马; 一岁小牛|A young horse or other equine animal between one and two years old.  A yearling calf.|例句：The eanling frolicked in the pasture. 小马驹在牧场上嬉戏。|例句：The farmer checked on his prize eanling. 农民检查了他的获奖小牛。|近义词：foal, yearling, calf (for cattle)|反义词:|词性：名词
