dermestes:/dərˈmɛstiːz/
n.
皮蠹；皮蠹甲虫
a genus of beetles whose larvae feed on dried animal matter, such as skin, fur, and feathers.
The museum had a problem with dermestes eating the taxidermied animals.  博物馆的动物标本被皮蠹吃掉了，这成了一个问题。
近义词:skin beetle, carpet beetle
反义词:N/A
词性:noun
