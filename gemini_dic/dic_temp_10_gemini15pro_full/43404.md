crankness:/ˈkræŋknəs/| n.|古怪；坏脾气；易怒|The state of being cranky; irritability or ill-temper.|His crankness this morning was unusual. 他今天早上的坏脾气很不寻常。|近义词：irritability, grumpiness, bad temper, peevishness, irascibility|反义词：good humor, cheerfulness, pleasantness|词性:名词
