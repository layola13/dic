divertibility:/ˌdaɪvɜːrtəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可转移性；可娱乐性；可供消遣性 | The quality of being easily diverted; distractibility. |  The child's divertibility made it difficult for him to focus on his studies.  这个孩子的注意力很容易分散，这让他很难集中精力学习。 | synonyms: distractibility, fickleness, volatility, inconstancy | antonyms:  concentration, focus, attentiveness | noun
