germicide: /ˈdʒɜːrmɪsaɪd/ | n. | 杀菌剂；消毒剂 | A substance that kills germs, especially bacteria. |  Hospitals use many different germicides to prevent the spread of infection. 医院使用多种不同的杀菌剂来防止感染传播。|  The cleaner advertised itself as a powerful germicide.  这种清洁剂标榜自己是一种强力杀菌剂。 | synonyms: disinfectant, antiseptic, bactericide | antonyms: None | noun
