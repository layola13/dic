affricating:/ˈæfrɪkeɪtɪŋ/
adjective| 摩擦化的；塞擦音化的|The process of changing a sound into an affricate.  将一个音变成塞擦音的过程。|The sound change involved affricating the dental stop. 这个语音变化包括将齿塞音摩擦化。|近义词: spirantizing, fricativizing|反义词: None|词性: adjective
affricate:/ˈæfrɪkət/
noun| 塞擦音|A consonant sound that begins as a stop and releases as a fricative, such as the "ch" in "church" and the "j" in "judge".|The "ch" sound is an affricate. “ch”音是一个塞擦音。|近义词: None|反义词: None|词性: noun
affricate:/ˈæfrɪkeɪt/
verb| 使变成塞擦音|To change a sound into an affricate. 将一个音变成塞擦音。|Over time, the /t/ sound affricated to become /tʃ/. 随着时间的推移，/t/ 音摩擦化成了 /tʃ/。|近义词: spirantize, fricativize|反义词: None|词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ad- + fricāre ("to rub") + -ing.  来自拉丁语 *ad- + fricāre（“摩擦”）+ -ing。
