off-ramp:/ˈɔːfˌræmp/ (US), /ˈɒfˌræmp/ (UK)| n. |出口匝道；下线坡道|A sloping road that leads a vehicle off a highway.|We took the next off-ramp to get gas. 我们从下一个出口匝道下去加油。|Synonyms: exit ramp, slip road|Antonyms: on-ramp, entrance ramp|noun

off-ramp:/ˈɔːfˌræmp/ (US), /ˈɒfˌræmp/ (UK)| n. |比喻义：摆脱困境的途径|A way to withdraw from a difficult situation or commitment.|The government is looking for an off-ramp from the trade war. 政府正在寻找摆脱贸易战的途径。|Synonyms: exit strategy, escape route|Antonyms: escalation, commitment|noun
