diamylene:/daɪˈæmɪliːn/
noun
二戊烯，癸烯
A branched alkene hydrocarbon with the formula C10H20. It is used as a solvent and in the production of other chemicals.
Diamylene is a useful solvent for resins and polymers. 二戊烯是一种有用的树脂和聚合物溶剂。
Diamylene is also used in the production of synthetic lubricants. 二戊烯也用于生产合成润滑剂。
近义词:decene
