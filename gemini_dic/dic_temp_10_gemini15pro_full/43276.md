crafts:/kræfts/ | n. | 工艺; 手艺; 技艺 | Skill in planning, making, or executing something. |  She sells her crafts at the local market.  她把自己的手工艺品在当地市场出售。

crafts:/kræfts/ | n. |  船; 飞机; 航天器 |  Vehicles, especially those used in war or in a particular trade. |  Enemy crafts were sighted in the area.  在该地区发现了敌人的飞机。


Etymology: From Old English cræft "strength, skill, power, art," from Proto-Germanic *kraftiz.


Synonym: handiwork, skills, trades,  vessels, ships, aircraft

Antonym:  incompetence, inability, clumsiness
