coronet:/ˈkɒrənɪt/ (UK), /ˈkɔːrənɪt/ (US)| n.| 小冠冕，冠状物；花冠 |A small or simple crown, often worn by lesser royalty or nobility. Also, a crown-like ornament or garland. |The princess wore a delicate coronet of pearls. 公主戴着精致的珍珠小冠冕。|Synonyms: crown, diadem, tiara, chaplet, garland|Antonyms: none|noun

coronet:/ˈkɒrənɪt/ (UK), /ˈkɔːrənɪt/ (US)| n.| (马蹄)蹄冠 |The lowest part of a horse's pastern, just above the hoof. |The horse had an injury to its coronet. 这匹马的蹄冠受伤了。|Synonyms: hoof crown, coronary band|Antonyms: none|noun
