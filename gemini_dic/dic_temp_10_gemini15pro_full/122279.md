mitmaps:/ˈmɪtˌmæps/ | n. | 心智图; 思维导图 | Mind maps are a visual way of representing information, often used for brainstorming, note-taking, and problem-solving. They typically involve a central idea surrounded by branching connections to related concepts. | I used mitmaps to organize my thoughts for the presentation. 我用思维导图整理了我的演示文稿思路。| synonyms: mind maps, concept maps, spider diagrams | antonyms: linear notes, outlines | noun
