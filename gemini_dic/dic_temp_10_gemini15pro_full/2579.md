admonishers: /ədˈmɑːnɪʃərz/
n.
劝诫者，警告者
People who admonish; those who give warnings or reprimands.
The admonishers urged the crowd to disperse peacefully.  劝诫者敦促人群和平散去。
The king listened to his admonishers but ultimately made his own decision. 国王听取了劝诫者们的意见，但最终还是自己做出了决定。

近义词: critics, reprovers, censors, warners
反义词: supporters, praisers, encouragers
词性: noun (plural)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *amonestour*, from Anglo-French *amonestur*, from *amonester* ("to admonish"), from Latin *admonēre* ("to remind, advise, warn"), from *ad- ("to") + *monēre* ("to warn").
