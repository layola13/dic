outsonnets:/ˌaʊtˈsɒnɪt/
verb
胜过十四行诗; 写出比十四行诗更好的诗
to surpass in writing sonnets; to write better sonnets than.
Shakespeare outsonneted all his contemporaries.  莎士比亚的十四行诗超过了他所有的同时代人。
近义词: outwrite, surpass
反义词:  None (The concept of being "worse" at sonnet writing than another doesn't have a single word equivalent.)
