carman:/ˈkɑːrmən/ | n. |汽车修理工；电车司机|A person who repairs or maintains cars, or (historically) a driver of a streetcar. |例句：The carman quickly diagnosed the problem with the engine. 汽车修理工很快诊断出了发动机的问题。
