chirp:/tʃɜːrp/ (US), /tʃɜːp/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.|啁啾声；唧唧声；（尤指鸟）鸣叫；愉快地高声说|A short, high-pitched sound, typically made by a bird or insect. Also, to make such a sound, or to speak in a cheerful and lively way. |The chirp of the crickets filled the night air. 蟋蟀的鸣叫声充满了夜空。

She chirped a greeting to her friends. 她愉快地向朋友们打招呼。

He chirped, "Good morning!" 他欢快地说：“早上好！”|近义词：tweet, peep, cheep, twitter|反义词: croak, growl |词性: noun, verb intransitive, verb transitive
