casklike:/ˈkɑːskˌlaɪk/| adj.|像桶的；桶状的|Resembling a cask in shape or size. Shaped like a barrel; barrel-shaped. |The giant sequoia's trunk was casklike, immense and sturdy. 巨大的红杉树干像桶一样，巨大而坚固。

The hobbit's house was small and casklike, nestled into the hillside.  霍比特人的房子又小又像桶状，依偎在山坡上。|近义词：barrel-shaped, barrel-like, tun-like|反义词: slender, thin|词性: adjective
