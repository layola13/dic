insurrectionary:/ˌɪnsəˈrɛkʃəneri/ | adj. n.|暴动的，起义的；暴动者，起义者|Relating to or participating in an insurrection; a person who takes part in an insurrection.|例句：The insurrectionary forces were quickly defeated.  起义军很快就被击败了。

例句：The government labeled him as an insurrectionary. 政府把他贴上了暴动者的标签。

|近义词：rebellious, mutinous, revolutionary, seditious|反义词:loyal, obedient, compliant|词性:形容词, 名词


insurrectionary:/ˌɪnsəˈrɛkʃəneri/ | n.|暴动者，起义者|A person who takes part in an insurrection.|例句：The insurrectionaries stormed the government building. 暴动者冲进了政府大楼。

例句：Several insurrectionaries were arrested after the riot. 骚乱后，几名暴动者被逮捕。|近义词：rebel, mutineer, revolutionary|反义词:loyalist, conformist|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *insurrectionarius, from Latin insurrectio (“rising up”), from insurgere (“to rise up”), from in (“upon, against”) + surgere (“to rise”).
