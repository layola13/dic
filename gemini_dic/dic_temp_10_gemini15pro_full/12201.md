as well:/æz ˈwɛl/ | adv. | 也，还，而且 |  Used to add something to what has already been said. It's similar in meaning to "too" or "also," but it usually comes at the end of a clause or sentence. | I like apples, and bananas as well. 我喜欢苹果，也喜欢香蕉。 | also, too, in addition, besides |  N/A | adverb

as well:/æz ˈwɛl/ | adj. | 健康的 |  Used to describe someone's state of health, meaning they are not ill. Less common than the adverbial use. |  He was ill yesterday, but he's as well as can be expected today. 他昨天病了，但今天他的情况还不错。 | healthy, fine, well | ill, sick | adjective
