hash:/hæʃ/ | n. vt. | 哈希，散列；把…弄乱；推敲；切碎 | A function that converts an input of arbitrary length into a fixed-size string of characters.  It's used in cryptography and data storage for indexing and retrieval.  As a verb, it means to chop into small pieces. |  The password was stored as a hash.  密码被存储为哈希值。 | synonyms: chop, mince, hash out | antonyms: combine, unite | noun, verb

hash:/hæʃ/ | n. | 杂乱，混乱 | A confused mixture or mess. |  The project was a complete hash.  这个项目完全是一团糟。 | synonyms: mess, jumble, muddle | antonyms: order, organization | noun
