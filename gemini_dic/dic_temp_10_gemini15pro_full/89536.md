honorableness:/ˈɑːnərəblnəs/ |n.|荣誉；高尚；正直|The honorableness of his intentions was never in doubt. 他的意图的正直性从未受到怀疑。|例句：The king praised the knight for his honorableness in battle. 国王称赞骑士在战斗中的高尚品德。

例句：Her honorableness was evident in her refusal to accept the bribe. 她拒绝接受贿赂，彰显了她的正直。|近义词：integrity, rectitude, uprightness, nobility, virtue|反义词：dishonor, baseness, ignobility, wickedness, depravity|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *honorablenes, from honorablen +‎ -nes. Equivalent to honorable +‎ -ness.
