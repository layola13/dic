outbeams:/ˌaʊtˈbiːmz/
verb
发出更强的光
to surpass in emitting beams of light; to shine more brightly than.
The new lighthouse outbeams the old one. 新灯塔的光芒胜过旧灯塔。
Synonyms: outshine, eclipse, overshadow
Antonyms: dim, darken
词性: 动词
