microchips:/ˈmaɪkroʊtʃɪps/ | n. |微芯片;集成电路|A microchip is a tiny piece of silicon that has circuits printed on it. It is used in computers and other electronic devices to store and process information.| Modern smartphones contain incredibly powerful microchips. 现代智能手机包含功能强大的微芯片。|  Microchips are essential components in modern electronics. 微芯片是现代电子产品中的重要组成部分。 | synonyms: integrated circuit, chip, semiconductor | antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "discrete components") | noun

microchip:/ˈmaɪkroʊtʃɪp/ | vt. |给…装微芯片|To implant a microchip, especially for identification. |  The vet microchipped my dog so he can be easily identified if he gets lost. 兽医给我的狗植入了微芯片，这样如果它走丢了就能很容易地找到它。|  Farmers are microchipping their livestock for better tracking and management. 农民正在给他们的牲畜植入微芯片，以便更好地跟踪和管理。 | synonyms: chip, tag | antonyms: (No direct antonym) | verb
