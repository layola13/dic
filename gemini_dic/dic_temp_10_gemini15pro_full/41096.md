copra:/ˈkoʊprə/| n.|椰干，椰肉干|The dried meat of the coconut, from which coconut oil is expressed.|Copra is used to extract coconut oil. 椰干用来榨取椰子油。|Synonyms: coconut meat, dried coconut|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps coconut milk/water as a processed form)|noun

Etymology->
From Malayalam കൊപ്ര (kopra), from കൊപ്പ് (koppŭ, “coconut shell”). Ultimately from Proto-Dravidian *koppal- (“coconut shell”).
