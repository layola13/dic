oligandrous:/ˌɒlɪˈɡændrəs/ |adj.| 少雄蕊的；寡雄蕊的 | Having few stamens (fewer than the number of petals or sepals). |  Example: This particular orchid species is oligandrous, with only two stamens.  这种兰花是少雄蕊的，只有两个雄蕊。| Example: Oligandrous flowers are less common than polyandrous ones. 少雄蕊的花比多雄蕊的花少见。 | Synonym:  paucandrous | Antonym: polyandrous | Adjective
