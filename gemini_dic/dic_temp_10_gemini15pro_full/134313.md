noninstinctively:/ˌnɒnɪnˈstɪŋktɪvli/ | adv. | 非本能地，非直觉地 | Not in a way that is based on instinct; in a learned or reasoned way. | He reacted noninstinctively, pausing to think before acting. 他没有本能地反应，而是在行动之前停下来思考。|  She picked up the spider noninstinctively, having learned it wasn't dangerous. 她非本能地拿起蜘蛛，因为她知道它不危险。 | 近义词: deliberately, consciously, rationally | 反义词: instinctively, intuitively | 词性: 副词

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"  来自拉丁语 non
instinct (noun):  meaning "a natural or inherent aptitude, impulse, or capacity" 来自拉丁语 instinctus, 意思是 "impulse, instigation."
-ive (suffix): forming adjectives  来自拉丁语 -ivus
-ly (suffix): forming adverbs  来自古英语 -lice
