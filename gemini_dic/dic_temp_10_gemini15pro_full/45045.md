cubatory:/ˈkjuːbətɔːri/
adjective
孵化的；育雏的
Relating to or resembling an incubator; used for incubation.
例句：The cubatory conditions were carefully controlled for optimal hatching rates.  育雏条件受到仔细控制以获得最佳孵化率。

例句：The zoo built a new cubatory for the penguin chicks.  动物园为企鹅幼雏建造了一个新的育雏室。
近义词：incubating, brooding
反义词：None
词性：adjective
