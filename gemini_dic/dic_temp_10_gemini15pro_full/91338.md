hydropical:/haɪˈdrɒpɪkəl/ (US) /haɪˈdrɑːpɪkl/| adj.|水肿的；患水肿病的|Affected with, or characteristic of, dropsy; dropsical.  Suffering from an abnormal accumulation of serous fluid in the tissues or in a body cavity.|The hydropical patient's legs were swollen and painful.|Synonyms: edematous, dropsical|Antonyms: healthy, normal|adjective
