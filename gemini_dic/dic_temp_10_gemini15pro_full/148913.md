overwillingness:/ˌoʊvərˈwɪlɪŋnɪs/
n.|过度热情，过分愿意|The overwillingness of some people to share their opinions online can be overwhelming. 有些人过度热衷于在网上分享他们的观点，这可能会让人难以承受。|
近义词：eagerness, enthusiasm, zeal
反义词：reluctance, unwillingness, apathy
词性:名词

overwilling:/ˌoʊvərˈwɪlɪŋ/
adj.|过分愿意的，过度热情的|He was overwilling to please his boss, often taking on more work than he could handle. 他过分渴望取悦他的老板，经常承担超过他所能承受的工作量。|
近义词：too eager, excessively keen
反义词：reluctant, unwilling
词性:形容词
