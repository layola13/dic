ETYMOLOGY->
From "barn" + "storm". Originally referred to theatrical troupes performing in barns. Later extended to political campaigning and aviation stunts.  从“谷仓”+“风暴”而来。最初指的是在谷仓里表演的剧团。后来扩展到政治竞选和航空特技。
