exclusively:/ɪkˈskluːsɪvli/| adv.|专门地，独家地，仅仅|Used to indicate that something is restricted to a particular person, group, or area.|This offer is exclusively for our premium members. 这个优惠只针对我们的高级会员。|The resort caters exclusively to families.  这个度假村专门接待家庭游客。|近义词：solely, only, entirely, completely, strictly|反义词: inclusively, generally|词性:副词

词源: From Late Latin exclusus, past participle of excludere (“to shut out”), from ex- (“out of, from”) + claudere (“to shut, to close”).
