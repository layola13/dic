merciful:/ˈmɜːrsɪfʊl/ | adj. | 仁慈的；慈悲的；宽大的 | Showing or characterized by mercy; compassionate. |  The merciful king pardoned the thief. 仁慈的国王赦免了小偷。 |  The judge handed down a merciful sentence. 法官作出了仁慈的判决。 | 近义词: compassionate, forgiving, lenient, clement | 反义词: merciless, cruel, ruthless, pitiless | 词性: 形容词
词源: From Middle English *merciable, *merciful, from Anglo-Norman merciable, from Old French merciable, from merci (“mercy”) + -able. Equivalent to mercy +‎ -ful.
