furores:/fʊˈrɔːriːz/
noun
狂热；轰动；骚动
An outburst of public enthusiasm or excitement.
The movie caused a furore when it was first released. 这部电影首次上映时引起了轰动。
frenzy, uproar, commotion, excitement, stir
calm, peace, tranquility
n.
