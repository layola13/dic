miserere:/ˌmɪzəˈreɪri/ /ˌmɪzəˈrɪəri/ | n. | 忏悔诗; 求怜悯的祈祷 |A prayer or musical setting of Psalm 51, used as a penitential prayer.  It expresses deep remorse and pleas for God's mercy. |  The choir sang a beautiful miserere during the Good Friday service.  唱诗班在耶稣受难日仪式上唱了一首优美的忏悔诗。

The king listened to the prisoner's miserere and granted him clemency. 国王听了囚犯的求饶，便饶恕了他。 | 近义词: lament, supplication, plea | 反义词: celebration, rejoicing | 词性: noun
