nonsubstitutionary:/ˌnɑːnsʌbstɪˈtuːʃəneri/
adjective
非替代性的, 不可替代的
Not able to be substituted; not involving substitution.  Relating to a mutation where one base is changed to another, but the resulting amino acid remains the same, therefore not affecting the protein's function. This is in contrast to a missense mutation.
Example:  The mutation was nonsubstitutionary, therefore the protein function remained unchanged. 这个突变是非替代性的，因此蛋白质功能保持不变。
Example: The scientist explained that a nonsubstitutionary mutation won't alter the protein's amino acid sequence.  科学家解释说，非替代性突变不会改变蛋白质的氨基酸序列。
Synonyms: irreplaceable, essential, indispensable
Antonyms: replaceable, substitutable, dispensable
词性: adjective
