noncommitment:/ˌnɒnkəˈmɪtmənt/
n.
lack of commitment; unwillingness to commit oneself
The government's noncommitment to the project caused delays. 政府对该项目缺乏承诺导致了延误。
His noncommitment to the relationship made her feel insecure. 他对这段关系的漫不经心让她感到没有安全感。
近义词：indecision, reluctance, hesitation, ambiguity
反义词：commitment, dedication, promise, pledge
词性: 名词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + commitment (from commit, from Latin committere "to unite, connect, entrust")
