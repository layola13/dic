alluringly:/əˈlʊərɪŋli/ | adv. | 诱惑地，迷人地 | In a way that attracts or tempts someone by seeming appealing, charming, or exciting. | She smiled alluringly at him across the room. 她妩媚地笑着，从房间的另一头看着他。|  She danced alluringly, capturing everyone's attention. 她跳着迷人的舞蹈，吸引了所有人的目光。 | 近义词: attractively, charmingly, enticingly, seductively, temptingly | 反义词: repulsively, unattractively, off-puttingly | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From allure + -ing + -ly.  Allure comes from Old French alurer "to attract, entice," from a- "to" + lurer "to lure."
