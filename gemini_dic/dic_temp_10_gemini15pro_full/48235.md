decontaminates:/ˌdiːkənˈtæmɪneɪts/
verb
净化，清除污染，消毒
To remove or neutralize contaminants, making something safe to handle or use.
The cleaning crew decontaminates the lab after each experiment.  实验结束后，清洁人员对实验室进行消毒。
The hospital staff decontaminated the operating room. 医院工作人员对手术室进行了消毒。
sterilize, sanitize, purify, cleanse
contaminate, pollute
v.
