gutturalize:/ˈɡʌtərəlaɪz/ | v. | 发喉音；使发喉音 | To pronounce a sound in the throat; to make a sound guttural. |  The singer gutturalized the final note of the song. 歌手用喉音唱了这首歌的最后一个音符。

The actor gutturalized his voice to portray the villain. 演员用喉音说话来刻画这个反派角色。 | 近义词: rasp, growl | 反义词: None | 词性: verb
Etymology: From Late Latin *gutturalis, from Latin guttur ("throat") + -ize.
