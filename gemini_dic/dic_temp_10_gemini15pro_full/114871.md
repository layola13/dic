mammonist: /ˈmæmənɪst/
n.
拜金主义者; 贪财者
A person who is obsessed with wealth and material possessions; a greedy person.
He was a mammonist, caring only for money and power. 他是一个拜金主义者，只在乎金钱和权力。
The mammonist saw an opportunity to exploit the situation for his own financial gain.  这个贪财者看到了一个利用这种情况为自己谋取经济利益的机会。
近义词: materialist, miser, chaser of lucre, money-grubber
反义词: philanthropist, altruist
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Mammon, the Aramaic word for riches, personified in the New Testament as a false god, representing wealth and greed.
