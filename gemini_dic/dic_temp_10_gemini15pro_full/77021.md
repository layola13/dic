garglers:/ˈɡɑːrɡlər/| n.|漱口者；含漱剂；漱口声|A person who gargles; a gargle; the sound of gargling.|The garglers filled the bathroom with the sound of swirling mouthwash.|近义词：rinsers|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps one could consider "swallower")|词性:noun


Etymology:  From the verb "gargle," which comes from the Middle French word "gargouiller," meaning "to gurgle, to gargle."  Ultimately of imitative origin.
