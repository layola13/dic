FHA:/ˌɛfˌeɪˈtʃˈeɪ/
| n. |美国联邦住房管理局 (Federal Housing Administration)|The FHA provides mortgage insurance on loans made by FHA-approved lenders.|美国联邦住房管理局为经其批准的贷款机构提供的贷款提供抵押贷款保险。|近义词: government housing agency|反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑 private lenders)|词性: noun (缩写)

Etymology:  FHA is an initialism that stands for Federal Housing Administration.  "Federal" relates to the central government of the US. "Housing" refers to dwellings or residences. "Administration" signifies a body responsible for managing or directing something.
