assemble:/əˈsɛmbəl/ /əˈsɛmbl/| v. |集合，聚集；装配，组装|To gather together in one place for a common purpose; to fit together the parts of something.|The students assembled in the auditorium for the morning assembly. 学生们聚集在礼堂里参加晨会。|synonyms: gather, congregate, convene, collect, amass, compile, construct, build|antonyms: disperse, scatter, disassemble, dismantle|verb

assemble:/əˈsɛmbəl/ /əˈsɛmbl/| n. |集会；集合体|(rare) The act of assembling or the state of being assembled. |The assemble of students cheered for the winning team. (less common usage) 学生集会为获胜的队伍欢呼。|synonyms: gathering, congregation, convention, collection|antonyms: dispersal, scattering|noun
