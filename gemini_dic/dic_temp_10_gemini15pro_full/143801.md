outorganize:/ˌaʊtˈɔːrɡəˌnaɪz/
verb
胜过组织；在组织方面胜过
To surpass or defeat someone or something through superior organization.
Our team outorganized their team and won the competition. 我们的团队在组织方面胜过了他们的团队，赢得了比赛。
outorganize their opponents. 在组织方面胜过他们的对手
近义词: outmaneuver, outplan, outstrategize
反义词: be disorganized, be outmaneuvered
