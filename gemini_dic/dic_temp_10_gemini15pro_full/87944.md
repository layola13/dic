higgledy-piggledy:/ˌhɪɡəldi ˈpɪɡəldi/| adj. adv.|乱七八糟的，杂乱无章地|in a confused and disordered way|The toys were scattered higgledy-piggledy all over the floor. 玩具乱七八糟地散落在地板上。|The books were piled higgledy-piggledy on the shelf. 书架上的书堆放得乱七八糟。|近义词：disorderly, chaotic, jumbled, messy|反义词：orderly, neat, tidy|词性:形容词,副词

Etymology:
Likely imitative in origin, suggesting a confused and jumbled state.  The repetition of similar-sounding syllables contributes to the sense of disorder.  First recorded in the 16th century.
