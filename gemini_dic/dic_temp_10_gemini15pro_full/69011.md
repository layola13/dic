fattened:/ˈfætnd/ | adj. | 肥育的，养肥的 | Made fat, plump, or fleshy, typically for slaughter. | The farmer fattened the pigs for market.  农民把猪养肥了以备出售。 |  The prize-winning pumpkin was fattened with fertilizer and water.  获奖的南瓜是用肥料和水养肥的。 | 近义词: plump, fleshy, well-fed, overfed | 反义词: lean, thin, emaciated | 词性: 形容词

PHRASE->
fattened calf:  肥牛犊 (often used in the biblical story of the prodigal son)
fattened goose/duck: 肥鹅/鸭 (referring to poultry raised for food)
fattened pig/hog: 肥猪 (referring to pigs raised for food)
fattened livestock: 肥育牲畜 (a general term for farm animals raised for meat)
a fattened wallet: 鼓鼓的钱包 (figurative use, meaning a wallet full of money)


词源:  Past participle of "fatten," which comes from the Old English word "fættan," meaning "to make fat."
