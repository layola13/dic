noncausativeness:/ˌnɑːnkɔːˈzætɪvnəs/ | n. | 非致使性 | The quality of a verb or construction indicating that the subject does not directly cause the action or state expressed by the verb.  | The noncausativeness of the verb "die" is evident in the sentence "The flowers died." | 近义词: inactiveness, inertness | 反义词: causativeness | 词性: noun
