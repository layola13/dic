heartbreaks:/ˈhɑːrtbreɪks/| n. | 心碎，极度悲伤 |Overwhelming sadness, extreme grief, typically caused by romantic loss or other emotional trauma.  A source of intense emotional pain. |She suffered several heartbreaks before finding lasting love. 她经历了几次心碎才找到 lasting love.|近义词：grief, sorrow, anguish, devastation |反义词: joy, happiness | noun
