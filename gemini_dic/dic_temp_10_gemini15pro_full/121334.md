例句：The article misgendered the non-binary activist several times. 这篇文章多次性别错称了这位非二元性别活动家。|近义词：misidentified, mislabeled|反义词：correctly gendered|词性:形容词

词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning wrongly) + "gender" (verb meaning to assign a gender to) + "-ed" (past participle suffix)
