branning:/ˈbrænɪŋ/ | n. |糠化，去除麸皮|The process of removing the bran from grain, typically wheat, to produce refined flour. |The branning process removes the outer layer of the grain. 糠化过程去除了谷物的最外层。|近义词:milling, refining|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 enriching - 浓缩，加料) |词性: noun

词源: From bran (麸皮) + -ing (动名词后缀), indicating the action or process of removing bran.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *brannō.
