dockyard:/ˈdɒkjɑːd/ (UK) /ˈdɑːkjɑːrd/ (US)| n. |船坞；造船厂|A place where ships are built or repaired.|The city's dockyards were bustling with activity.  这座城市的船坞一片繁忙景象。|例句：The massive dockyard employed thousands of workers.  这个大型船坞雇佣了数千名工人。

例句：Ships of all sizes were lined up in the dockyard for repairs. 各种大小的船只都在船坞里排队等候维修。|近义词：shipyard, naval yard|反义词:None|词性:noun

Etymology->
From dock + yard.  "Dock" originally referred to a ditch or trench, later evolving to mean an enclosed area for ships. "Yard" refers to an enclosed area, often for specific work.
