outinventing:/ˌaʊtɪnˈvɛntɪŋ/
verb
胜过；在发明创造方面超越
surpassing in inventiveness; inventing more or better things than
Tesla aimed at outinventing Edison in the field of electricity. 特斯拉的目标是在电力领域超越爱迪生。
近义词: outdo, surpass, excel
反义词: lag behind, fall behind
词性: verb (动词)
