aliturgical:/ˌeɪlɪˈtɜːrdʒɪkəl/
adjective
非礼拜式的，不遵循礼拜仪式的
Characterized by the absence of or departure from established liturgical forms.  Not liturgical; lacking liturgy.
The meeting had an aliturgical feel, focusing on personal sharing rather than formal prayers.  这次会议有一种非礼拜式的氛围，侧重于个人分享而不是正式的祈祷。
The group's aliturgical approach to worship was refreshing for some, but unsettling for others.  这个团体不遵循礼拜仪式的敬拜方式对一些人来说是耳目一新的，但对另一些人来说却令人不安。
informal, non-liturgical, unconventional
liturgical, formal, ritualistic
