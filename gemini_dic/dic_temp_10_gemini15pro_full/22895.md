boteless:/ˈboʊtlɪs/
adjective
无底的，用之不竭的
bottomless; inexhaustible, unfathomable.  Having no bottom; very deep.  Figuratively, boundless or immeasurable.
The ocean seemed boteless as we gazed into its depths.  我们凝视着海洋深处，它似乎无底洞一般。
Synonyms:  inexhaustible, immeasurable, boundless, profound, fathomless
Antonyms:  shallow, finite, limited, measurable
词性: adjective
