canniest:/ˈkæniɪst/| adj.|最精明的，最狡猾的|Shrewd, astute, and having good judgement, especially in business or politics.|例句：He's the canniest negotiator I've ever met. 他是我见过的最精明的谈判者。

例句：She made the canniest investment and tripled her money. 她做了最精明的投资，使她的钱翻了三倍。|近义词：shrewdest, sharpest, cleverest, wisest, most astute|反义词：naive, foolish, gullible|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *canni, from Old English *cannig.  Ultimately related to "can" (know, be able to).  The suffix "-est" indicates the superlative form.
