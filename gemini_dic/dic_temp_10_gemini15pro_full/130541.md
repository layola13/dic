nonacidic:/ˌnɑːnəˈsɪdɪk/
adjective
非酸性的
Not acidic; having a pH of greater than 7.
Soil samples were tested and found to be nonacidic. 土壤样本经过测试，发现是非酸性的。
The solution is nonacidic and safe for skin contact.  这种溶液是非酸性的，可安全接触皮肤。
Synonyms: alkaline, basic, neutral
Antonyms: acidic, acid
词性: adjective
