canzone:/kɑːnˈtsoʊneɪ/ /kænˈzoʊni/
n.
歌曲；小调；民歌
A song, especially a lyrical song or ballad.  Specifically, a popular Italian song, often sentimental or romantic in nature.
Her performance of the canzone moved the audience to tears. 她演唱的这首民歌使观众感动得流泪。
Synonyms: song, ballad, tune, air, melody
Antonyms: silence, noise (in a general sense, not a specific musical piece)
noun
