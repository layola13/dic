abandon: /əˈbændən/ (US) /əˈbændən/ (UK)| vt. n.|放弃，抛弃; 放纵|To leave completely and finally; to give up; to discontinue.|The sailors abandoned ship. = 水手们弃船了。

They abandoned the project due to lack of funding. = 由于缺乏资金，他们放弃了这个项目。|近义词：desert, forsake, relinquish, surrender|反义词：maintain, retain, cherish, support|verb

abandon: /əˈbændən/ (US) /əˈbændən/ (UK)| n.|放任，放纵; 尽情|A complete lack of inhibition or restraint.|He danced with abandon. = 他尽情地跳舞。

She spoke with an abandon that shocked her parents. = 她说话的放纵令她的父母感到震惊。|近义词：unrestraint, recklessness|反义词： restraint, control, inhibition|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French abandoner "surrender, give up," from the phrase a bandon "at one's disposal, in one's power," from a "to" (from Latin ad "to") + bandon "power, jurisdiction" (from Late Latin bandum "proclamation, ban"). The original sense was "to put (something) under someone else's control."


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They abandon their old car. = 他们丢弃了他们的旧车。
Some people abandon their pets when they move. = 有些人在搬家时会抛弃他们的宠物。

一般过去时:
The captain abandoned the sinking ship. = 船长放弃了正在下沉的船。
He abandoned his family for a younger woman. = 他为了一个年轻的女人抛弃了他的家庭。

现在完成时:
The company has abandoned its plans for expansion. = 公司已经放弃了扩张计划。
They have abandoned hope of finding the missing child. = 他们已经放弃了找到失踪孩子的希望。

过去完成时:
The explorers had abandoned their search for the lost city. = 探险家们已经放弃了寻找失落之城的努力。
She had abandoned her career to raise her children. = 她为了抚养孩子而放弃了事业。

将来时:
They will abandon the building if it is not safe. = 如果不安全，他们将放弃这栋建筑。
If the weather gets worse, we will abandon our trip. = 如果天气恶化，我们将放弃我们的旅行。

虚拟语气:
If they were to abandon the project, it would be a disaster. = 如果他们放弃这个项目，那将是一场灾难。
I wish I could abandon all my responsibilities. = 我希望我可以放弃我所有的责任。

被动语态:
The house was abandoned after the fire. = 火灾后，这所房子被遗弃了。
The car had been abandoned by the side of the road. = 这辆车被遗弃在路边。

疑问句:
Why did they abandon the car? = 他们为什么丢弃了这辆车？

否定句:

He shouldn't abandon his dreams. = 他不应该放弃他的梦想。
