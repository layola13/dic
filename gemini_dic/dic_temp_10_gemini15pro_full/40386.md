contradictors:/ˌkɑːntrəˈdɪktərz/ | n. |反驳者，矛盾者| People who contradict or deny something; those who express opposing views. | The contradictors of the new policy voiced their concerns at the meeting.  新的政策的反驳者在会议上表达了他们的担忧。|  opponents, dissenters, challengers | supporters, proponents, advocates | noun

contradictory:/ˌkɑːntrəˈdɪktəri/ | adj. |矛盾的，对立的| Mutually opposed or inconsistent; containing or involving contradiction. | The witness gave contradictory statements to the police. 目击者向警方提供了相互矛盾的陈述。| conflicting, opposing, incompatible | consistent, compatible, harmonious | adjective
