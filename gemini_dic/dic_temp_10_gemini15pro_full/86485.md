heightening:/ˈhaɪtnɪŋ/| n. v. adj.|加高，提高，增强|An increase in intensity, degree, or amount.  The act of making something higher or increasing its intensity.|例句：The heightening tension in the room was palpable. 房间里日益紧张的气氛很明显。|近义词：increasing, intensifying, amplifying, enhancing, escalating|反义词:decreasing, lessening, diminishing, reducing, weakening|词性:Noun (Gerund), Verb (present participle), Adjective (attributive)


Etymology: From heighten + -ing. Heighten comes from Middle English heighten, from hight (“height”) + -en.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *hauhtaz.
