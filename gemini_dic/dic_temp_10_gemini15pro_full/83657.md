haemoscope:/ˈhiːməˌskoʊp/ (US), /ˈhiːməˌskəʊp/ (UK)| n. |血视镜，血液观察器|An instrument for viewing the circulation of the blood, especially in the capillaries.|例句：The doctor used a haemoscope to observe the blood flow in the patient's capillaries. 医生用血视镜观察病人毛细血管中的血流。

例句：The haemoscope revealed a sluggish blood flow, indicating a potential circulatory issue. 血视镜显示血流缓慢，表明可能存在循环问题。

|近义词：capillaroscope, microscope|反义词: None|词性: noun
