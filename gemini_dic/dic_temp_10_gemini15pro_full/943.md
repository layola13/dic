acacine: /ˈækəsaɪn/
noun
金合欢碱; 刺槐精
A crystalline alkaloid, C<sub>15</sub>H<sub>14</sub>N<sub>2</sub>O<sub>2</sub>, obtained from several species of acacia, especially from Acacia farnesiana. It is an isomer of sophorine, and is medicinally similar to nicotine in its properties.
Acacine is found in some species of Acacia.  金合欢碱存在于一些金合欢树种中。
Acacine can have stimulant effects. 金合欢碱可以有兴奋作用。
synonym: sophorine
antonym: N/A
