neopallial:/ˌniːoʊˈpæliəl/ | adj. | 新皮层的 | Relating to the neopallium, the newer, outer layer of the cerebral cortex, responsible for higher-level cognitive functions in mammals and particularly well-developed in humans. | The neopallial regions of the brain are crucial for language processing.  新皮层区域对语言处理至关重要。| Synonyms: isocortical | Antonyms: allocortical | adjective
