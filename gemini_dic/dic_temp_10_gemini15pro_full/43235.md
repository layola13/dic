ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *crakelen, frequentative of craken (“to crack”), from Old English cracian (“to crack”). Cognate with Scots crackle (“to crackle”), West Frisian kreakelje (“to crackle”), Dutch krakelen (“to crackle”), Low German krakeln (“to crackle”), German krachen (“to crack, crash”).
