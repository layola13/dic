emancipator:/ɪˈmænsɪpeɪtər/
n.
解放者，释放者
A person who frees someone from slavery or oppression.
Abraham Lincoln is considered a great emancipator. 亚伯拉罕·林肯被认为是一位伟大的解放者。

近义词:liberator, freer, deliverer
反义词:oppressor, enslaver
词性:名词

emancipators:/ɪˈmænsɪpeɪtərz/
n.
解放者们，释放者们
Plural form of emancipator, referring to multiple people who free others from slavery or oppression.
The emancipators were celebrated as heroes. 这些解放者被誉为英雄。

近义词:liberators, freers, deliverers
反义词:oppressors, enslavers
词性:名词
