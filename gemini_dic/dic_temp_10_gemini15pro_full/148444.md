overstocks:/ˈoʊvərˌstɑːks/ (US) /ˈəʊvəˌstɒks/ (UK)| n.|积压库存，过量库存|An excessive supply of goods beyond what is needed or can be sold.|The warehouse was full of overstocks from last season. 仓库里堆满了上个季度的积压库存。|synonyms: surplus, excess, glut|antonyms: shortage, deficit|noun

overstock:/ˌoʊvərˈstɑːk/ (US) /ˌəʊvəˈstɒk/ (UK)| vt.|过度储备，积压|To supply or have on hand more of something than is needed or can be sold.|The store overstocked on winter coats and had to offer deep discounts to clear them out.  这家商店冬季外套库存过多，不得不提供大幅折扣来清理它们。|synonyms: oversupply, glut|antonyms: understock|verb
