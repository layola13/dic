emulable:/ˈɛmjʊləbl/| adj.|可模仿的，可效仿的|Capable of being emulated; worthy of imitation.|Her dedication and work ethic are emulable qualities. 她的奉献精神和职业道德是值得效仿的品质。|Example: The success of their business model is emulable by other companies.  他们的商业模式的成功是可以被其他公司效仿的。|Synonyms: imitable, copyable, replicable, followable|Antonyms: inimitable, unique, unparalleled|adjective
