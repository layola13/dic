nonsalutations:/ˌnɒnsæljuˈteɪʃənz/ /ˌnɑːnsæljuˈteɪʃənz/
n.
非问候语; 不打招呼
Absence of greetings or salutations.  A deliberate avoidance of conventional greetings.
He began his speech with nonsalutations, immediately diving into the complex issue at hand. 他没有寒暄，直接开始讨论手头的复杂问题。
The email's nonsalutations and abrupt tone were considered rude.  这封电子邮件没有问候语，语气生硬，被认为是不礼貌的。
近义词:  lack of greetings, omission of greetings,  absence of salutations
反义词: greetings, salutations
词性: noun


Etymology:
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "salutation" (from Latin "salutatio," meaning "greeting")
