nonirrigated:/ˌnɒnɪˈrɪɡeɪtɪd/
adjective
未灌溉的
Not irrigated; not supplied with water by artificial means.
The nonirrigated land yielded a smaller crop this year due to the drought. 今年由于干旱，未灌溉的土地产量较低。
Near Antonyms: irrigated, watered
Antonyms: irrigated
