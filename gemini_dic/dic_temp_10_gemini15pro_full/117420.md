mediety:/ˌmiːdiˈɛtɪ/
n.
中间状态；中庸；中道
The state or quality of being mediate; a middle state or degree; mean.
He sought a mediety between extremes. 他在极端之间寻求中庸之道。
Synonyms: medium, mean, average, moderation
Antonyms: extreme, excess, immoderation
