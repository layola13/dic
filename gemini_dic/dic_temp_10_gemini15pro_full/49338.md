delineate:/dɪˈlɪnɪeɪt/| vt.|描绘，描述，勾勒|To describe or portray something precisely, often with words or lines. |例句：The artist delineated the scene with delicate brushstrokes. 艺术家用细腻的笔触描绘了这个场景。

例句：The report clearly delineates the steps involved in the process. 报告清楚地描述了这个过程中的步骤。|近义词：describe, portray, depict, outline, sketch|反义词：obfuscate, confuse|词性：动词

delineation:/dɪˌlɪnɪˈeɪʃn/| n.|描绘，描述，勾勒|The act of describing or portraying something precisely. |例句：The architect's delineation of the building was very detailed. 建筑师对建筑物的描绘非常详细。
