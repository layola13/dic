intermetropolitan:/ˌɪntərˌmɛtrəˈpɒlɪtən/
adjective
都市间的；大都市之间的
Relating to or connecting two or more major cities.
例句：Intermetropolitan trade has increased significantly in recent years. 近年来，都市间贸易显著增长。

例句：The intermetropolitan rivalry between the two cities is well-known. 这两个城市之间的大都市竞争是众所周知的。
近义词：interurban, intercity
反义词: intracity, local
词性: adjective
