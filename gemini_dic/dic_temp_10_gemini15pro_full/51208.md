destituent:/dɪˈstɪtʃuənt/
adjective
贫困的，缺乏的，无依无靠的
Lacking resources or means of subsistence; impoverished.  Also, lacking something essential; deficient.
The destituent family struggled to find food and shelter.  这个贫困的家庭挣扎着寻找食物和住所。
The soil is destituent of nutrients. 土壤缺乏营养。
synonyms: impoverished, indigent, needy, poor, deprived, impecunious
antonyms: wealthy, affluent, rich, prosperous
