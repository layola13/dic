cementing:/sɪˈmentɪŋ/
verb
巩固，加强
the process of making something such as an agreement or friendship stronger
例句：The two countries signed a treaty cementing their alliance.  这两个国家签署了一项条约，巩固了他们的联盟。

例句：Their shared experiences cemented their friendship. 他们的共同经历巩固了他们的友谊。
近义词：strengthening, solidifying, consolidating
反义词：weakening, undermining, dissolving
词性：v.


cementing:/sɪˈmentɪŋ/
noun
胶结，粘合
the process of joining things together using cement
例句：The cementing of the bricks took several hours.  砖块的粘合花了几个小时。

例句：The cementing process is crucial for the stability of the structure. 粘合过程对于结构的稳定性至关重要。
近义词：bonding, joining, gluing
反义词：separating, detaching
词性：n.


词源：From the Latin word "caementum," meaning "rough pieces of stone."  来自拉丁语单词“caementum”，意思是“粗糙的石头碎片”。
