fit-to-be-tied:/ˌfɪt tə bi ˈtaɪd/| adj.|暴跳如雷的，气急败坏的|Extremely angry; furious.  The phrase suggests someone so angry they feel they might need to be restrained.  It doesn't literally mean someone is tied up.|例句：She was fit to be tied when she found out her car had been towed. 她发现她的车被拖走了，气得暴跳如雷。|近义词：furious, livid, enraged, incensed|反义词:calm, tranquil|词性:形容词

fit-to-be-tied:/ˌfɪt tə bi ˈtaɪd/| adj.|气得发疯的|Extremely angry; so angry as to be beside oneself.  The phrase emphasizes the extreme nature of the anger.|例句：He was fit to be tied when his flight was canceled.  当他的航班被取消时，他气得发疯。|近义词：infuriated, beside oneself, apoplectic|反义词:pleased, content|词性:形容词
