ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *lechen, from Old English lǣċan (“to heal, cure, redeem, restore”), from Proto-Germanic *laikijaną (“to leap, jump, play”), from Proto-Indo-European *leyk- (“to jump, play”).  The sexual sense developed from the notion of "playing" or "sporting."  More distantly related to "lick."
