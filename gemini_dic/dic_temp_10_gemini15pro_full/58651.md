dumbness:/ˈdʌmnɪs/| n. |愚蠢，哑口无言|The dumbness of his response surprised everyone. 他的回答愚蠢得让每个人都感到惊讶。|近义词：stupidity, silliness, foolishness, muteness, silence|反义词：intelligence, wisdom, eloquence|词性:名词
Etymology: From Middle English *dombnes*, from Old English *dumbnes*, from *dumb* ("mute, speechless, stupid") + *-nes* (suffix forming nouns of quality or state). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *dumbaz* ("mute, stupid").
