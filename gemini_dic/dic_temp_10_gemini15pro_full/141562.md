oppositely:/ˈɒpəzɪtli/ /ˈɑːpəzɪtli/
adv.
相反地，对立地
in a way that is contrary to or completely different from something else.
The two groups reacted oppositely to the news.  这两个团体对这消息的反应截然相反。

近义词:conversely, contrarily, inversely, counter to
反义词:similarly, likewise, identically
词性:副词
