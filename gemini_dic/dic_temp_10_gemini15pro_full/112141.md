loofah:/ˈluːfə/ (US), /ˈluːfɑː/ (UK)| n.| 丝瓜，丝瓜络，丝瓜瓤|A tropical climbing plant bearing a large, ridged fruit with a fibrous interior. Also refers to the dried fibrous interior of the fruit, used as a sponge.|例句：I use a loofah in the shower every day. 我每天洗澡都用丝瓜络。

例句：The loofah plant grew quickly up the trellis. 丝瓜藤很快爬满了棚架。

|近义词：sponge, washcloth, scrubber|反义词: None|词性: Noun
