mayors:/ˈmeɪərz/| n. |市长们|The plural form of "mayor," referring to the elected leaders of multiple cities or towns.|The mayors of several cities met to discuss common issues. 几位城市的市长开会讨论共同关心的问题。|近义词：city managers, council leaders|反义词:citizens, residents|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *maire, from Latin major "greater, elder."
