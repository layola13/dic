bacteria's:/bækˈtɪəriəz/
noun

Possessive form of bacteria, indicating ownership or association.  It refers to something belonging to or originating from bacteria.
The bacteria's cell wall is composed of peptidoglycan.  细菌的细胞壁是由肽聚糖组成的。

Synonym: bacterial
Antonym: None (possessive forms don't typically have antonyms)
