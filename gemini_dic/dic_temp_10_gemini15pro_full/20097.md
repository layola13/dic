biospheres:/ˈbaɪoʊsfɪrz/
n.
生物圈
The regions of the surface and atmosphere of the Earth or another planet occupied by living organisms.
例句：The Earth's biosphere is incredibly diverse. 地球的生物圈极其多样。

例句：Scientists are studying how pollution affects the biosphere. 科学家正在研究污染如何影响生物圈。

近义词：ecosphere, biota
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps "lifeless zone")
词性：Noun
