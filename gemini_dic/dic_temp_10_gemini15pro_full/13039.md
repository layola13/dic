astride:/əˈstraɪd/| prep. adv.|跨着，骑着，两腿分开着|With one leg on each side of something; straddling. |例句：She sat astride the horse. 她骑在马上。|例句：He stood astride the ditch. 他跨立在沟上。|近义词：straddling, across, over|反义词:|preposition, adverb
