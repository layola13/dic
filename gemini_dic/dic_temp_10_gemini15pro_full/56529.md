domify:/ˈdɑːməfaɪ/ | v. | 使成为DOM元素; DOM化 | To convert (HTML code, often provided as a string) into a set of DOM elements.  This usually involves creating the elements using `document.createElement` and appending them to an existing DOM node. | `const htmlString = '<div>Hello</div>'; const myElement = domify(htmlString); document.body.appendChild(myElement);`  将HTML字符串'<div>Hello</div>'转换为DOM元素，并添加到文档body中. | 近义词: parse, render | 反义词: stringify, serialize | 词性: 动词
