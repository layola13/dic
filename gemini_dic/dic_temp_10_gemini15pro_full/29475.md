cataplectic:/ˌkætəˈplektɪk/
adjective
猝倒症的，患猝倒症的
Relating to or affected by cataplexy, a sudden loss of muscle tone triggered by strong emotions.
He experienced cataplectic attacks whenever he laughed too hard. 他每次大笑的时候都会有猝倒症发作。
Near Synonyms: narcoleptic, narcolepsy-related
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms exist, consider using phrases like "having normal muscle tone" or "not affected by cataplexy")
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *kataplēxis* "a striking down," from *kataplēktos* "struck down," verbal adjective of *kataplēgnynai* "to strike down," from *kata-* "down" + *plēgnynai* "to strike."
