nonintellectualness:/ˌnɑːnˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəlnəs/
n.
lack of intellect; absence of intellectual activity
The nonintellectualness of the entertainment left me bored.  这种娱乐活动的缺乏智性让我感到无聊。
Synonyms: unintelligence, ignorance, simplemindedness, mindlessness
Antonyms: intelligence, intellect, brilliance, smartness
