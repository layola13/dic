dipetalous: /daɪˈpɛtələs/
| adj. |
| 双瓣的；有两片花瓣的 |
| Having two petals.  A flower with two petals in its corolla is described as dipetalous. |
| This rare orchid is dipetalous, a characteristic that distinguishes it from other species.  这种稀有兰花是双瓣的，这一特征使其区别于其他品种。|

| The botanist identified the flower as dipetalous due to its two distinct petals.  植物学家根据其两片不同的花瓣将这种花鉴定为双瓣的。 |
