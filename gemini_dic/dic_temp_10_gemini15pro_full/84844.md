hang-up:/ˈhæŋ ʌp/ (US), /ˈhæŋ ʌp/ (UK)| n.| 障碍，难题；(心理上的) 情结，纠结；停顿，延迟|An emotional problem or inhibition.|He has a hang-up about women. 他对女人有心理障碍。|近义词: problem, difficulty, obstacle, issue, complex, inhibition, block, fixation |反义词: solution, breakthrough|noun

hang-up:/ˈhæŋ ʌp/ (US), /ˈhæŋ ʌp/ (UK)| v. | 挂断（电话）；挂起；搁置|To end a telephone conversation by replacing the receiver.|He hung up on me! 他挂我电话！|近义词: disconnect, terminate (a call), put down the phone|反义词: answer, pick up|verb
