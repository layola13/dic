engining:/ˈɛndʒɪnɪŋ/
|n.|发动机；引擎；发动机制造；给…装发动机|The act of providing with an engine, or the engines collectively.  The science of designing, building, and maintaining engines, especially those that convert heat energy into mechanical work.|The engining of the new aircraft was a complex undertaking.  这架新飞机的发动机制造是一项复杂的工作。|engine, motor, power plant|null|noun
Etymology->From engine + -ing.
