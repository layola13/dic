eschewing:/ɪsˈtʃuːɪŋ/
verb
避免，避开
deliberately avoid using; abstain from.
例句：He eschewed publicity and avoided interviews. 他避免抛头露面，拒绝接受采访。

例句：She eschewed all forms of violence. 她反对一切形式的暴力。

近义词：avoiding, shunning, abstaining from, refraining from, forgoing, foregoing
反义词：embracing, indulging in, courting
词性：verb (动词)

词源：From Old French *eschiver, from Frankish *skiuhan "to shy away, avoid, dread".
