clubber:/ˈklʌbər/ (UK), /ˈklʌbər/ (US)| n. |夜店常客，俱乐部会员|A person who frequents nightclubs, especially one who is a regular attendee of dance clubs or raves.|例句：The clubber danced all night to the electronic music.  这位夜店常客整晚都在随着电子音乐跳舞。

例句：Many clubbers enjoy the social atmosphere of nightclubs. 许多夜店常客喜欢夜总会的社交氛围。


|近义词：raver, partygoer, nightclubber|反义词: homebody|词性: noun
