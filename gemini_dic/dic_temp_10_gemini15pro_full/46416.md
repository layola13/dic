dag:/dæɡ/ | n. | 羊毛上的污垢，泥块；悬挂物；下垂物 |  A clump of matted wool, dirt, or other matter. A piece of something that hangs down. | The sheep's fleece was covered in dags. 这只羊的羊毛上沾满了泥块。 | synonyms: clump, mat, clot, gob, lump | antonyms: cleanliness | noun


dag:/dæɡ/ | n. | (澳大利亚，新西兰俚语)  A funny or eccentric person. | He's a bit of a dag, but he's a good bloke. 他有点古怪，但他是好人。| synonyms: eccentric, character, oddball | antonyms: conformist | noun
