bitters:/ˈbɪtərz/ | n. | 苦酒，苦味剂 | A potent alcoholic preparation flavored with bitter herbs or roots.  Usually used in small quantities to stimulate the appetite or add flavor to cocktails. |  He added a dash of bitters to his Old Fashioned. 他在他的老式鸡尾酒里加了一点苦酒。 | synonyms: tonic, aperitif | antonyms: sweetener | noun

bitters:/ˈbɪtərz/ | n. | 苦涩，辛酸 |  A feeling of resentment or disappointment. (Usually plural) | The bitters of defeat were hard to swallow.  失败的苦涩难以吞咽。 | synonyms: resentment, disappointment, acrimony | antonyms: joy, happiness | noun
