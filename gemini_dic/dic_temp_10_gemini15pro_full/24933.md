bruning:/ˈbruːnɪŋ/ | adj. | 燃烧的；灼热的；强烈的 | Burning describes something on fire, or something very hot or intense.  | The burning building collapsed. 燃烧的建筑物倒塌了。 |  The desert sun was burning hot. 沙漠的太阳灼热难耐。| synonyms: blazing, scorching, fiery, intense | antonyms: cold, freezing, extinguished | adjective
