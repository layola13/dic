abduction:/əbˈdʌkʃən/| n.|绑架，拐骗，外展|The abduction of the child shocked the community.  孩子的绑架震惊了整个社区。|例句：The police are investigating the abduction of a wealthy businessman. 警方正在调查一位富商的绑架案。

The movie was about an alien abduction. 这部电影是关于外星人绑架的。|近义词：kidnapping, seizure, capture|反义词:release|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin abductionem (nominative abductio) "a leading or taking away," noun of action from past-participle stem of abducere "to lead away," from ab- "away" + ducere "to lead."

USAGE->
The abduction case remains unsolved. 这起绑架案仍未解决。

His sudden abduction left everyone bewildered. 他的突然失踪让每个人都感到困惑。


PHRASE->
parental abduction 父母绑架（指父母一方未经另一方同意将子女带走）
abduction of a minor 未成年人绑架
alien abduction 外星人绑架
investigation into the abduction 对绑架案的调查
the abduction of the airplane 劫持飞机
