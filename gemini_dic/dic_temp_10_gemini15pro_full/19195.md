bewitchingnesses:/bɪˈwɪtʃɪŋnɪsɪz/| n. | 迷人之处；魅力；令人着迷的特性 |The quality of being bewitching; the power or ability to bewitch.  It refers to the plural of "bewitchingness," which itself is not a commonly used word.  It emphasizes multiple instances or aspects of something's enchanting nature. |Her bewitchingnesses captivated the entire audience. 她的魅力迷住了全场观众。 |Synonyms: allure, charm, enchantment, fascination, magnetism |Antonyms: repulsiveness, unpleasantness |Noun
