buffoonery:/bəˈfuːnəri/
noun
滑稽可笑的行為；丑角的表演
Foolish or playful behavior, especially intended to make people laugh; the actions or behavior of a buffoon.
His buffoonery may have offended some people. 他的滑稽行为可能冒犯了一些人。
synonyms: clowning, antics, tomfoolery, silliness, absurdity
antonyms: seriousness, solemnity, gravity
词性: 名词
