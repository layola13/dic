canoed:/kəˈn(j)uːd/
verb
乘独木舟，划独木舟
past tense and past participle of canoe, meaning "traveled in or propelled a canoe."
We canoed down the river yesterday. 我们昨天乘独木舟顺流而下。
They canoed across the lake to the island. 他们划独木舟穿过湖泊到达了岛屿。
近义词: paddled, boated, sailed
反义词: hiked, walked, drove
词性: verb
