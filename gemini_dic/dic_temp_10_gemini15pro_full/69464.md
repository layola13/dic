feint:/feɪnt/| v. n.|佯攻，假动作；装作；昏厥，昏倒|To pretend to make an attack in one direction in order to draw the opponent's defense away from the real point of attack; a pretended blow, thrust, or other movement, especially in boxing or fencing.  Can also mean to lose consciousness briefly.|The boxer feinted with his left and then delivered a powerful right hook. 拳击手佯装左勾拳，然后给了对手一个强有力的右勾拳。|近义词：deceive, bluff, sham, pretend, simulate, fake; swoon, faint|反义词： genuine, sincere|词性: verb, noun

feint:/feɪnt/| n.|佯攻，假动作|A deceptive movement; a pretense.|His feint toward the left completely fooled the defender. 他向左的假动作完全骗过了防守队员。|近义词：pretense, sham, ruse|反义词：reality, truth|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *feint, past participle of feindre "to feign, pretend," from Latin fingere "to shape, form, invent, pretend."
