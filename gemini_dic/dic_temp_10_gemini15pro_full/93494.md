ill-willed:/ˌɪlˈwɪld/
|adj.|
|恶意的；怀恨的|
|Having or showing a desire to harm someone.|
|He gave her an ill-willed glare. 他恶狠狠地瞪了她一眼。|

|She spread ill-willed rumors about her colleagues. 她散布关于同事的恶意谣言。|

|malicious, malevolent, spiteful, hostile|
|benevolent, kind, friendly, well-meaning|
|adjective|
