neptunicentric:/ˌnɛptjuːnɪˈsɛntrɪk/| adj.|以海王星为中心的|Relating to a point of view or reference frame centered on Neptune.  Having Neptune as the center.|例句：A neptunicentric orbit would describe a path around Neptune.  以海王星为中心的轨道将描述一条绕海王星运行的路径。|例句：From a neptunicentric perspective, Triton appears to be the largest moon. 从以海王星为中心的视角来看，海卫一似乎是最大的卫星。|近义词:N/A|反义词:heliocentric (以太阳为中心的)|词性:adjective
