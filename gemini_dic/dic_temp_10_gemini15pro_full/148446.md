overstores:/ˌoʊvərˈstɔːrz/ | v. | 过度储存，超量储存 | To stock or supply (a store or market) with more goods than are needed or can be sold. | The supermarket overstores produce during the harvest season, leading to significant waste. 超市在收获季节过度储存农产品，导致了大量的浪费。| Example 2: The company overstores inventory, tying up valuable capital.  这家公司过度储存库存，占用了宝贵的资金。| 近义词: overstock | 反义词: understock | 词性: 动词

overstores: /ˌoʊvərˈstɔːrz/ | n. |  过剩的库存 | An excessive amount of goods stored in a store or warehouse. |  The overstores in the warehouse took up valuable space. 仓库里过剩的库存占用了宝贵的空间。| Example 2:  Dealing with the overstores became a major logistical challenge. 处理过剩的库存成为一个主要的物流挑战。 | 近义词: surplus, excess | 反义词: shortage, deficit | 词性: 名词
