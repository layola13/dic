confer:/kənˈfɜːr/ (US) /kənˈfəːr/ (UK)| v. |授予，赋予；商谈，商议|To grant or bestow (a title, degree, benefit, or honour). To discuss something with someone else; consult; have a conference.|The university conferred an honorary degree on the renowned scientist. 大学授予这位著名科学家荣誉学位。

He conferred with his colleagues before making a decision. 他在做决定之前与同事们商议。|近义词：grant, bestow, award, consult, discuss, deliberate|反义词：revoke, withdraw, withhold|词性:动词

词源: From Latin *conferre*, from *com-* (together) + *ferre* (to bring, bear).
