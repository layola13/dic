homothermic:/ˌhəʊməˈθɜːrmɪk/ /ˌhoʊməˈθɜːrmɪk/| adj.|恒温的；温血的|Maintaining a relatively constant body temperature despite changes in the surrounding environment.  Able to generate and regulate internal body heat.|Birds and mammals are homothermic animals. 鸟类和哺乳动物是恒温动物。|Synonyms: warm-blooded, endothermic|Antonyms: poikilothermic, ectothermic|adjective
