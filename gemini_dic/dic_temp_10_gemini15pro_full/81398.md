grassers:/ˈɡræsərz/ /ˈɡrɑːsərz/ | n. |食草动物|Animals that primarily eat grass.  These animals have digestive systems adapted to process grass and other plant material.|例句：The grassers in the African savanna include zebras and wildebeest. 非洲大草原上的食草动物包括斑马和角马。

例句：Grassers play a vital role in maintaining the ecosystem. 食草动物在维持生态系统中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：herbivores, grazers|反义词:carnivores, predators|词性:noun
