hand-it-to:/ˈhænd ɪt tuː/
idiom
承认(某人)的优点; 佩服(某人)
to admit that someone has a good quality or ability, especially one that you do not have yourself: You have to hand it to her, she's a great organizer.  你不得不佩服她，她真是个很棒的组织者。
You have to hand it to them; they've done a great job. 你必须承认他们的优点；他们做得很好。
give credit to, admire, respect
criticize, disparage
phrase
