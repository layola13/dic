overappraised: /ˌoʊvərəˈpreɪzd/
adjective
估价过高的
Having been appraised at too high a value.
The house was significantly overappraised, leading to difficulties in selling it. 这房子被严重高估了，导致难以出售。
The antique dealer realized the necklace was overappraised and offered a lower price. 古董商意识到这条项链估价过高，于是提出了更低的价格。

近义词: overvalued, overestimated
反义词: undervalued, underestimated
词性: adjective

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively, too much") + "appraise" (verb meaning "to assess the value or quality of")
