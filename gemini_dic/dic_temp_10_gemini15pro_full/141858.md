orchestrates:/ˈɔːrkɪstreɪts/
verb
精心安排；策划；(尤指)精心组织
arranges or directs the elements of (a situation) as if conducting a musical performance.
The CEO orchestrated the merger of the two companies. 首席执行官精心策划了两家公司的合并。
coordinate, manage, direct, arrange, stage-manage
improvise, neglect, disregard
词性:动词

orchestrate:/ˈɔːrkɪstreɪt/
verb
为…谱写管弦乐曲；改编…为管弦乐曲
compose or arrange (a piece of music) for an orchestra.
He orchestrated the symphony beautifully. 他优美地为交响乐谱了曲。
arrange, score, adapt
词性:动词
