oversolemnness:/ˌoʊvərˈsɑːləmnəs/
n.
过度严肃；过分庄严
The state of being excessively solemn or serious.
His oversolemnness made the lighthearted gathering feel like a funeral.  他过分的严肃让原本轻松的聚会感觉像一场葬礼。
The oversolemnness of the occasion was somewhat offset by the colorful decorations.  这个场合过分的庄严被五彩缤纷的装饰稍微抵消了一些。
solemnity, seriousness, gravity
lightheartedness, levity, frivolity
noun
