lignite: /ˈlɪɡnaɪt/ | n. |褐煤|A soft brownish-black coal showing traces of plant structure, intermediate between peat and bituminous coal.|Lignite is used to generate electricity in some power plants. 褐煤在一些发电厂用于发电。|Synonyms: brown coal|Antonyms: anthracite|noun
