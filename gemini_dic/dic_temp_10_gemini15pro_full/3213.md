affectingly:/əˈfɛktɪŋli/
adverb|动人地，感人地|in a way that produces strong feelings, especially sadness or pity|She spoke affectingly about her childhood. 她动情地讲述了自己的童年。|
近义词:touchingly, movingly, poignantly, emotionally
反义词:unfeelingly, coldly, impassively
词性:副词
词源:From affect + -ing + -ly.  Ultimately from the Latin afficere.
