counter-passion:/ˈkaʊntərˌpæʃən/ | n. | 对抗的激情，相反的热情 | An emotion or enthusiasm that counteracts another, opposing passion. |  He channeled his counter-passion for video games into studying computer programming.  他将自己对电子游戏的热情转化为学习计算机编程。

She developed a counter-passion for gardening to combat her stress. 她培养了对园艺的热情来对抗压力。 |  antipathy, aversion, opposing desire  | zeal, fervor, passion | noun
