actinic: /ækˈtɪnɪk/
adjective
光化的; 光化性的; 光致的
Relating to or exhibiting chemical changes caused by radiant energy, especially light.
The actinic rays of the sun can cause premature aging.  阳光中的光化射线会导致过早衰老。

Actinic keratosis is a precancerous skin condition caused by sun exposure. 光化性角化病是一种由阳光照射引起的癌前皮肤病。

近义词:  photosensitive, photochemical
反义词:  insensitive (to light)
词性: adjective

词源: From Greek *aktînos* "ray," related to *aktís* "ray of light."
