giants:/ˈdʒaɪənts/| n.|巨人；伟人；巨头|People of unusually great stature or size.  Figuratively, people of great importance or ability in a particular field.|The San Francisco Giants are a popular baseball team. 旧金山巨人队是一支很受欢迎的棒球队。|synonyms: titans, behemoths, colossi|antonyms: dwarfs, midgets, pygmies|noun
Etymology: From Old French *geant, from Latin gigantem, accusative singular of gigas (“giant”), from Ancient Greek γίγας (gígas).
