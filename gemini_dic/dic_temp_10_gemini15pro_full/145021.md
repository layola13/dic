overadvertising:/ˌoʊvərˈædvərˌtaɪzɪŋ/
n.
过度广告；广告过滥
Excessive advertising; advertising too much.
The overadvertising of fast food contributes to childhood obesity. 快餐的过度广告导致了儿童肥胖。
近义词: excessive advertising, saturation advertising
反义词: underadvertising, insufficient advertising
词性: 名词

词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessive") + advertising (noun from advertise, from Old French *advertiser, from Latin advertere "to turn towards")
