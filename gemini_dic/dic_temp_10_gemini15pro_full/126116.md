mutely:/ˈmjuːtli/ | adv. | 默默地；无声地 | In a way that shows no sound or speech; silently. | She nodded mutely, tears streaming down her face. 她默默地点了点头，眼泪顺着她的脸流了下来。| She stared mutely at the wall, lost in thought. 她默默地盯着墙，陷入了沉思。 | 近义词：silently, soundlessly, speechlessly | 反义词：loudly, vocally, aloud | 词性: 副词

词源:
From mute + -ly.  "Mute" comes from the Old French "mut" (meaning silent), which in turn derives from the Latin "mutus" (also meaning silent).
