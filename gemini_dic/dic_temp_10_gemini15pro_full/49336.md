delimiting:/dɪˈlɪmɪtɪŋ/
adjective
划定的，界定的
Marking or defining the limits or boundaries of something.
The surveyor was delimiting the property lines. 测量员正在划定地产线。
Synonyms: bounding, defining, outlining, circumscribing
Antonyms: expanding, extending, enlarging
词性: 形容词


delimit:/dɪˈlɪmɪt/
verb
划定界限，限定
To fix or define the limits or boundaries of something.
The countries delimited their shared border in the treaty.  这些国家在条约中划定了共同边界。
Synonyms: define, fix, establish, determine
Antonyms: blur, obscure, confuse
词性: 动词

delimitation:/diːˌlɪmɪˈteɪʃn/
noun
划界，界限
The act of fixing or defining limits or boundaries.
The delimitation of the new voting districts caused controversy.  新投票区的划分引起了争议。
Synonyms: demarcation, definition, boundary
Antonyms:  integration, fusion
词性: 名词
