diabolo:/diˈæbəloʊ/ (US), /dɪˈæbələʊ/ (UK)| n. |空竹，扯铃|A juggling toy consisting of two cups or cones joined by an axle, spun and tossed on a string.  It is of Chinese origin. | He skillfully manipulated the diabolo, sending it soaring high into the air. 他熟练地操纵着空竹，让它高高地飞向空中。|The diabolo danced and whirled on the string. 空竹在绳子上飞舞旋转。| 近义词: Chinese yo-yo, devil on two sticks | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) |词性:noun

词源:

From French *diable*, from Late Latin *diabolus*, from Ancient Greek διάβολος (diábolos, “slanderer, accuser, devil”).  The toy's name may derive from its fast, back-and-forth motion, reminiscent of the devil's supposed activity.
