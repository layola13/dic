hygienist:/ˈhaɪdʒɪnɪst/ | n. |保健员，卫生员|A person who is trained to clean teeth and give advice on dental hygiene.|Example: You should see a dental hygienist regularly for cleaning and checkups. 你应该定期去看牙科保健员进行清洁和检查。|Example: The hygienist advised me to floss daily. 保健员建议我每天使用牙线。|Synonym: dental hygienist, oral hygienist|Antonym: N/A|Noun

hygiene:/ˈhaɪdʒiːn/ | n. |卫生，保健|Conditions or practices conducive to maintaining health and preventing disease, especially through cleanliness.|Example: Good hygiene is essential for preventing the spread of germs. 良好的卫生习惯对于预防细菌传播至关重要。|Example: Maintaining personal hygiene is important for overall health. 保持个人卫生对整体健康很重要。|Synonym: cleanliness, sanitation|Antonym: filth, dirtiness|Noun
