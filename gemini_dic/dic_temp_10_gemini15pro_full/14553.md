avertedly:/əˈvɜːtɪdli/
adverb
故意地，有意地
done in a way that is clearly intended and not accidental
He avertidly avoided eye contact. 他故意避免眼神接触。
Synonym: deliberately, intentionally, purposely, knowingly
Antonym: accidentally, unintentionally, unknowingly
词性: 副词
