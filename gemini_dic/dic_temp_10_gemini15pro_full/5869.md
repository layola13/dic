ambassador-at-large:/æmˈbæsədər ət lɑːrdʒ/
n.
特使；大使；无任所大使；巡回大使
An ambassador-at-large is a diplomat of the highest rank who is accredited to represent their country, often focusing on a specific issue or region, and not assigned to a particular country or embassy.
The former president now serves as an ambassador-at-large, promoting global peace initiatives.  前总统现在担任巡回大使，推动全球和平倡议。

The ambassador-at-large addressed the UN on climate change.  这位无任所大使在联合国就气候变化问题发表了讲话。

近义词: roving ambassador, special envoy, diplomat
反义词: (No direct antonym, as it's a specific type of ambassador)
词性: noun
