aculeate: /əˈkjuːliɪt/
adjective
尖刺的，有刺的
Having a sharp point; prickly.
The aculeate leaves of the thistle can cause a painful prick.  蓟的尖刺叶子会引起疼痛的刺痛。
The porcupine is covered in aculeate quills. 豪猪身上覆盖着尖刺。
近义词: prickly, spiny, thorny, barbed
反义词: smooth, blunt
词性: adjective

USAGE->
The aculeate leaves of the holly bush deterred animals from eating them. 冬青灌木的尖叶阻止了动物吃它们。

Cacti have evolved aculeate spines to protect themselves from predators. 仙人掌进化出了尖刺来保护自己免受捕食者的侵害。
