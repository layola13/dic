dodge:/dɑːdʒ/ (US) /dɒdʒ/ (UK)| v. n.|躲避，闪避；伎俩，托词|To avoid something, typically quickly or cleverly.  Also, a clever trick or a way of avoiding something.|He dodged the snowball. 他躲过了雪球。|The boxer dodged his opponent's punch. 拳击手躲过了对手的拳头。 |synonyms: evade, avoid, elude, duck, sidestep|antonyms: confront, face, meet|verb
