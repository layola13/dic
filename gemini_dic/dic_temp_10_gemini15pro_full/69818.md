ferraris:/fəˈrɑːriz/
n.
法拉利（跑车）
plural of Ferrari.  Refers to multiple cars made by the Italian luxury sports car manufacturer, Ferrari.
He owns three Ferraris. 他拥有三辆法拉利。
My dream is to drive a Ferraris one day. 我的梦想是有一天能驾驶一辆法拉利。
近义词：sports cars, luxury cars
反义词：(No direct antonym, but could consider) everyday cars, budget cars
词性: noun
