cichoraceous:/ˌsɪkəˈreɪʃəs/
adjective
菊科植物的；类似菊科植物的
Resembling or related to plants of the chicory family (Cichoriaceae), typically having milky sap and flower heads composed of ray florets.
The dandelion, a common cichoraceous weed, has a bright yellow flower head. 蒲公英是一种常见的菊科杂草，有着鲜黄色的头状花序。
The plant had a cichoraceous appearance, with milky sap and small, ray-like flowers.  这种植物具有菊科植物的外观，带有乳白色的汁液和小小的、射线状的花朵。
synonyms: chicory-like, asteraceous
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, as it describes a botanical classification)
词性: 形容词
