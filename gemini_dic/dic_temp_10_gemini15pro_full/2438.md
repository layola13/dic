adjudicator: /əˈdʒuːdɪkeɪtər/ | n. | 裁决者，评判员 | A person who makes a formal judgment or decision about a problem or disputed matter. | The adjudicator listened carefully to both sides of the argument before making her decision. 裁决者在做出决定之前仔细听取了双方的论点。

The adjudicator awarded first prize to the young pianist. 评判员将一等奖授予了这位年轻的钢琴家。 | 近义词: judge, arbiter, referee, umpire | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *ajugen*, from Latin *adjudicare* "to award," from *ad-* "to" + *judicare* "to judge," from *judex* "judge."
