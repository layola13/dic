goosewinged:/ˈɡuːsˌwɪŋd/
adjective
鹅翼状的，像鹅翅膀一样张开的
Having sails set on opposite sides of the boat, especially with the wind slightly behind; (of a boat) sailing with sails set in this way.  The sails are positioned to catch the wind from opposite sides, similar to how a goose holds its wings when landing on water.
The goosewinged junk sailed gracefully across the bay.  鹅翼状的帆船优雅地驶过海湾。
The small boat, goosewinged with its two sails, skipped lightly over the waves.  那艘小船，两面帆张得像鹅的翅膀，轻快地掠过波浪。
Near Synonyms:  wing and wing, sailing wing and wing
Antonyms:  close-hauled, head to wind
word class: adjective
