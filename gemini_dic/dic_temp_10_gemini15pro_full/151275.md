pareto:/pəˈretəʊ/ (UK) /pəˈreɪtoʊ/ (US)| adj. n.|帕累托，帕累托的|Relating to or denoting the Italian economist Vilfredo Pareto (1848–1923) or his work, especially his observation that a small proportion of causes produces a large proportion of effects.|The Pareto principle suggests that 80% of the effects come from 20% of the causes.|近义词:power law, 80/20 rule|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective, noun

pareto:/pəˈretəʊ/ (UK) /pəˈreɪtoʊ/ (US)| n.|帕累托图，帕累托分析|A Pareto chart, also called a Pareto distribution diagram, is a vertical bar graph in which values are plotted in descending order of relative frequency from left to right.  It is used to identify the most significant factors in a dataset.|The Pareto analysis helped the company identify the key areas for improvement.|近义词:Pareto diagram, Pareto distribution, Pareto analysis|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
