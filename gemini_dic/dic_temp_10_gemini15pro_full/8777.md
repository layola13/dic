antiecclesiastic:/ˌæntiɪˌkliːziˈæstɪk/
adjective
反对教会的；非教会的
opposed to the church or clergy; secular.
His antiecclesiastic views made him unpopular with the local clergy. 他反对教会的观点使他不受当地神职人员的欢迎。
近义词:secular, irreligious, profane
反义词:religious, pious, devout
词性:adjective

antiecclesiastic:/ˌæntiɪˌkliːziˈæstɪk/
noun
反对教会者
a person who opposes the church or clergy.
He was known as an outspoken antiecclesiastic. 他以直言不讳地反对教会而闻名。
近义词:heretic, dissenter, nonconformist
反义词:cleric, churchman, believer
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- (against) + ecclesiastic (relating to the church).  Ultimately from Greek anti- (against) +  ekklēsia (assembly, church).
