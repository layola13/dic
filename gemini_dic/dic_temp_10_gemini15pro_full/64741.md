escort: /ˈeskɔːrt/ (UK) /ˈeskɔːrt/ (US)
n.|护送者，陪同者，护卫队|A person or group of people who accompany someone to protect or guide them or to show them courtesy.|The president's car was surrounded by a police escort.|近义词: companion, guard, attendant|反义词: abandon|词性:名词

escort: /ɪˈskɔːrt/ (UK) /ɪˈskɔːrt/ (US)
vt.|护送，陪同|To accompany someone to protect or guide them or to show them courtesy.|He escorted her to the theater.|近义词: accompany, guide, attend|反义词: abandon|词性:动词
