doctoral:/ˈdɑːktərəl/ (US), /ˈdɒktərəl/ (UK)| adj.|博士的；博士学位的|Of, relating to, or designed to produce a doctor (especially a PhD).|例句：She's pursuing her doctoral studies in physics. 她正在攻读物理学博士学位。

例句：He received his doctoral degree from Harvard University. 他获得了哈佛大学的博士学位。

近义词：PhD, postgraduate|反义词: undergraduate|词性: adjective
