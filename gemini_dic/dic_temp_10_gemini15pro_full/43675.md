credendum:/krɪˈdɛndəm/
n.
信条；要信的东西；基本原则
Something that is believed; a statement of belief or principle.
The Apostles' Creed is a well-known credendum in Christian faith. 使徒信经是基督教信仰中众所周知的信条。
The company's credendum is to put customers first.  这家公司的信条是顾客至上。
synonym: creed, doctrine, dogma, tenet
antonym: disbelief, skepticism, doubt
noun
