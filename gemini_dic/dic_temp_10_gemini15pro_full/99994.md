interpatterning:/ˌɪntərˈpætərnɪŋ/ | n. | 交织模式，交错模式 | The act or process of interweaving or intermingling different patterns or elements.  It refers to a therapeutic technique where different memory networks are stimulated simultaneously to create new neural pathways and facilitate positive change. | Interpatterning different learning strategies can enhance memory consolidation.  交织不同的学习策略可以增强记忆巩固。 | 近义词: interweaving, integration, blending | 反义词: separation, isolation | noun

interpatterning:/ˌɪntərˈpætərnɪŋ/ | v. | 交织，交错 | To interweave or intermingle different patterns or elements.  In a therapeutic context, it involves stimulating multiple memory networks concurrently. |  The therapist interpatterned positive memories with negative ones to reduce the impact of trauma.  治疗师将积极的记忆与消极的记忆交织在一起，以减少创伤的影响。 | 近义词: interweave, integrate, blend | 反义词: separate, isolate | verb
