drivable:/ˈdraɪvəbl/ /ˈdraɪvəbəl/| adj.|可驾驶的，可以开车的|Suitable for driving; able to be driven.  A road or vehicle that is in a condition that allows it to be driven safely.|The roads were icy, but still drivable.  道路很滑，但仍然可以驾驶。|Synonyms: navigable, motorable, passable|Antonyms: impassable, undrivable|adjective
