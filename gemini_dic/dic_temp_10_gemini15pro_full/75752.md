fulmar:/ˈfʊlmɑːr/ (US), /ˈfʊlmə(r)/ (UK)| n. |管鼻藿|A type of seabird found in northern oceans, known for its distinctive tube-like nostrils and its ability to eject a foul-smelling oil at predators.|
例句：The fulmar circled the fishing boat, hoping for scraps. 管鼻藿绕着渔船盘旋，希望能得到一些残羹剩饭。

例句：The cliffs were teeming with nesting fulmars. 悬崖上挤满了筑巢的管鼻藿。
