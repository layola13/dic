deadlining:/ˈdɛdˌlaɪnɪŋ/
n.|设定截止日期;截止日期的制定|The act of setting deadlines, or the process of working towards a deadline.|The project manager is deadlining all the tasks for the next quarter. 项目经理正在为下个季度设定所有任务的截止日期。|近义词: scheduling, timing|反义词: procrastinating
v.|设定截止日期|The act of setting a deadline.|He's deadlining the report for next Friday. 他把报告的截止日期定在下周五。|近义词: setting a deadline, imposing a deadline|反义词: extending a deadline
