abominator: /əˈbɒmɪneɪtər/
n.
憎恶者；痛恨者；可憎之物
One who abominates; a detester; something abominable.
The dictator was seen as an abominator of freedom by the people.  独裁者被人民视为自由的憎恶者。

He considered slavery an abominator. 他认为奴隶制是可憎之物。

近义词: hater, detester, loather, abhorrer
反义词: lover, admirer, adorer
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin abominator, from Latin abominari "to deprecate as ominous, abhor," from ab- "away" + ominari "to forebode," from omen "omen, portent."  Meaning "one who abominates" is from 1520s.
