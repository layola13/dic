bother:/ˈbɒðə(r) (UK), ˈbɑːðər (US)| v. n.|打扰，麻烦；操心；烦恼|To take the trouble to do something; to cause somebody trouble or worry.|例句：Don't bother to call.  不用麻烦打电话了。|近义词：annoy, disturb, trouble, inconvenience|反义词:ignore, neglect|词性:verb

bother:/ˈbɒðə(r) (UK), ˈbɑːðər (US)| n.|麻烦；烦恼；令人烦恼的人或事|Something that causes trouble or worry.|例句：It's a bother having to go to the bank.  不得不去银行真是麻烦。|近义词：nuisance, problem, worry|反义词:convenience, ease|词性:noun
