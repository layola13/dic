hausa:/ˈhaʊsə/| n. |豪萨人；豪萨语|A member of a people of northern Nigeria and neighboring areas of Niger.  The language of the Hausa people, belonging to the Chadic branch of the Afro-Asiatic family.|例句：The Hausa people have a rich cultural heritage. 豪萨人拥有丰富的文化遗产。

hausa:/ˈhaʊsə/| n. |豪萨语|The language of the Hausa people, belonging to the Chadic branch of the Afro-Asiatic family.|例句：Hausa is spoken by millions of people in West Africa. 豪萨语是西非数百万人的语言。
