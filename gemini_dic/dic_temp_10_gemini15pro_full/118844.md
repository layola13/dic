metaphosphate:/ˌmɛtəˈfɒsfɛɪt/ | n. |偏磷酸盐；偏磷酸酯|A salt or ester of metaphosphoric acid.  It is a type of phosphate where the phosphorus atom is bonded to four oxygen atoms, forming a ring or chain structure.|Sodium metaphosphate is used as a food additive. 偏磷酸钠用作食品添加剂。|Synonyms: phosphate, polyphosphate|Antonyms: None|noun
