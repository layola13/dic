anomalistical: /ˌænəməˈlɪstɪkəl/
| adj. |  异常的；不规则的；近点月的 | Relating to an anomaly, deviation from the common rule, or the interval between two successive passages of a planet through its perihelion (closest point to the sun) or of the moon through its perigee (closest point to Earth).  | The anomalistic month is slightly longer than the sidereal month. 近点月比恒星月稍长。| Synonym: aberrant, anomalous, deviant, irregular, unusual | Antonym: normal, regular, standard, typical |词性: 形容词

anomalistically: /ˌænəməˈlɪstɪkli/
| adv. | 异常地；不规则地；近点月地 | In an anomalistical manner. | The moon moves anomalistically around the Earth. 月球异常地绕地球运行。| Synonym: aberrantly, anomalously, deviantly, irregularly, unusually | Antonym: normally, regularly, standardly, typically |词性: 副词
