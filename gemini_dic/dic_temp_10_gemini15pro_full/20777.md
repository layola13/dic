blanketing:/ˈblæŋkɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle of blanket)
覆盖；以毯子覆盖；
covering completely like a blanket.
The snow was blanketing the fields. 雪覆盖着田野。
近义词：covering, enveloping, smothering
反义词：exposing, uncovering, revealing
词性：v.


blanketing:/ˈblæŋkɪtɪŋ/
adjective
像毯子一样的；全面覆盖的；
resembling a blanket in appearance or function.
A blanketing fog rolled in. 一层浓雾滚滚而来。
近义词：covering, pervasive, all-encompassing
反义词：partial, limited, localized
词性：adj.


blanketing:/ˈblæŋkɪtɪŋ/
noun
覆盖物；毛毯；
a covering that resembles a blanket.
A blanketing of snow covered the ground. 一层雪覆盖了地面。
近义词：covering, layer, blanket
反义词：exposure, gap, opening
词性：n.


词源:  From the verb "blanket," which comes from Old North French "blankete," meaning a white woolen cloth, ultimately from "blanc," meaning white.
