heteroecious: /ˌhɛtərˈiːʃəs/ | adj. | 异寄生的 | (of rust fungi) Having two or more hosts during the life cycle. |  Example: The heteroecious rust fungus requires both juniper and hawthorn to complete its life cycle.  这种异寄生的锈菌需要桧柏和山楂才能完成其生命周期。 | Example:  Some rusts are heteroecious, meaning they require two different host plants to complete their life cycle. 一些锈菌是异寄生的，这意味着它们需要两种不同的寄主植物才能完成其生命周期。| Synonym: heteroxenous | Antonym: autoecious | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek  ἕτερος (héteros, “other”) + οἶκος (oîkos, “house”).
