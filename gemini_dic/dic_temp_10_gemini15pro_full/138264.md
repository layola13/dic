not-too-bad:/ˌnɒt tuː ˈbæd/ /ˌnɑːt tuː ˈbæd/| adj.|还不错，过得去|An informal expression meaning fairly good, acceptable, or satisfactory.|例句： "How was the movie?" "Not too bad, actually." “电影怎么样？” “还不错，实际上。”|近义词：decent, alright, okay, tolerable, passable|反义词：terrible, awful, horrible|词性：形容词
