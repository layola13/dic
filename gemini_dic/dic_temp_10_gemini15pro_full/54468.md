disintegratively:/dɪsˈɪntɪɡrətɪvli/
adverb
分裂地，瓦解地
in a way that causes something to break up into small parts or pieces.
The empire disintegratively collapsed after the death of the emperor.  帝国在皇帝死后分裂瓦解了。
The robot disintegratively exploded after being hit by the laser. 机器人在被激光击中后爆炸成碎片。
近义词: destructively, fragmentarily
反义词: constructively, integratively
词性: adverb

词源:
From disintegrate + -ive + -ly. Disintegrate comes from the Latin dis- "apart" + integrare "make whole," from integer "whole."
