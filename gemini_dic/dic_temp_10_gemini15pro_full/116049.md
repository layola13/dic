martialist: /ˈmɑːrʃəlɪst/ | n. | 军国主义者；武力崇拜者 | A person who believes in the importance of military power and the use of force to achieve political goals. |  He's a martialist who believes in solving every problem with military intervention. 他是一个军国主义者，认为可以用军事干预来解决所有问题。

|  The martialist regime quickly crushed any dissent. 军国主义政权迅速镇压了任何异议。 |  近义词: warmonger, militarist, jingoist | 反义词: pacifist, dove | 词性: 名词

词源: martial (好战的) + -ist (表示人的后缀)  Ultimately from Latin "Martialis" (of Mars, the Roman god of war).
