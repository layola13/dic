enliven:/ɪnˈlaɪvən/ /ɛnˈlaɪvən/| v. |使生动；使活泼；使更有趣|To make something more lively, interesting, or enjoyable.|The music enlivened the party.  音乐使派对更加活跃。|近义词：animate, invigorate, stimulate, brighten, cheer|反义词：bore, dull, dampen, deaden|词性:动词
Etymology: From en- + life + -en,  "life" comes from Old English "līf" and "en-" is a prefix meaning "to put into" or "to cause to be." The suffix "-en" turns a noun or adjective into a verb.
