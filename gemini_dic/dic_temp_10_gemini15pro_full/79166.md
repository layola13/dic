glamorizations: /ˌɡlæməraɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 魅力化；美化；渲染 | The act of making something seem more attractive, exciting, or desirable than it really is.  Adding a sense of allure or fascination. | The media's glamorizations of violence desensitize viewers to its real consequences. 媒体对暴力的美化使观众对暴力的真实后果麻木不仁。|  The movie offers a glamorization of the gangster lifestyle. 这部电影美化了黑帮的生活方式。 | Synonyms: idealizations, romanticizations, glorifications | Antonyms: vilifications, denigrations | Noun
