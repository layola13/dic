guipure:/ɡɪˈpjʊr/ | n. |吉浦尔花边；车骨蕾丝 | A heavy lace, the pattern of which is linked by bars or brides rather than by a net ground. |  She wore a beautiful guipure lace dress to the wedding. 她穿着一件漂亮的吉浦尔蕾丝连衣裙去参加婚礼。| The tablecloth was edged with delicate guipure. 桌布边缘镶有精致的吉浦尔花边。| 近义词: lace,  chantilly lace | 反义词:  None | 词性: noun
