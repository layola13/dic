accessed: /ækˈsɛst/ | v. |访问，获取，使用|To gain entry to or make use of something, typically information or a facility.|I accessed the company's database to retrieve the sales figures. 我访问了公司的数据库以获取销售数据。

The files can be accessed remotely. 这些文件可以远程访问。|近义词：obtain, retrieve, reach, gain, utilize|反义词:block, restrict, deny|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Latin "accessus," meaning "approach" or "entrance."  It's the past participle of the verb "access."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He accesses the internet daily for news. = 他每天上网看新闻。
She accesses the files through a secure server. = 她通过安全的服务器访问文件。


一般过去时:
I accessed the information I needed yesterday. = 我昨天访问了我需要的信息。
They accessed the building using a key card. = 他们用钥匙卡进入了大楼。


现在完成时:
We have accessed the system multiple times today. = 我们今天多次访问了该系统。
She has accessed her bank account online. = 她在网上访问了她的银行账户。


过去完成时:
He had accessed the files before the system crashed. = 在系统崩溃之前，他已经访问了文件。
They had accessed the confidential information without authorization. = 他们在未经授权的情况下访问了机密信息。


将来时:
I will access the report tomorrow morning. = 我明天早上会查看报告。
You will be able to access the website once it's launched. = 网站启动后，你就可以访问了。


被动语态:
The database was accessed by an unauthorized user. = 数据库被未授权用户访问了。
The files are being accessed remotely. = 文件正在被远程访问。


PHRASE->
easily accessed = 易于访问
frequently accessed = 经常访问
remotely accessed = 远程访问
illegally accessed = 非法访问
publicly accessed = 公开访问
