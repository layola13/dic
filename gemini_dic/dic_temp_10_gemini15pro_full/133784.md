nongenerative:/ˌnɒnˈdʒɛnərətɪv/
adj.
非生成的，非衍生的
Not generative; not capable of producing or creating something new.
This theory relies on a nongenerative model of language acquisition.  这个理论依赖于一种非生成的语言习得模型。
The artist's recent work has been criticized for being derivative and nongenerative.  这位艺术家最近的作品因缺乏原创性和衍生性而受到批评。
近义词:unproductive, sterile, barren
反义词:generative, productive, creative
词性:形容词

词源:
