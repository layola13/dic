bulkily:/ˈbʌlkɪli/| adv.|笨重地，庞大地|In a bulky or unwieldy manner; taking up much space.|The package was bulkily wrapped and difficult to carry.  这个包裹包装得很笨重，很难携带。|近义词：clumsily, awkwardly, heavily|反义词：compactly, neatly, lightly|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From bulky + -ly.  Bulky itself comes from bulk, from Middle Dutch bulke "cargo, hull of a ship," of uncertain origin.
