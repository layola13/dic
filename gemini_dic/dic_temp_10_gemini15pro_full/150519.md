pantophagist:/pænˈtɒfədʒɪst/ | n. | 杂食动物；什么都吃的人 | An organism that eats both plants and animals.  A person who eats all kinds of food. | Example 1: Bears are pantophagists, consuming berries, fish, and insects. 熊是杂食动物，吃浆果、鱼和昆虫。

Example 2:  My friend is a true pantophagist; he'll try any cuisine at least once. 我的朋友是一个真正的杂食者；他会尝试任何菜系至少一次。 | Synonym: omnivore, generalist | Antonym: specialist, herbivore, carnivore | Noun
