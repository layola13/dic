gargil:/ˈɡɑːrɡɪl/| n.|滴水嘴兽，怪兽状排水口|A gargil is a carved stone grotesque, often in the form of a fantastic animal, projecting from the gutter of a building, typically a church.  滴水嘴兽是一种雕刻的怪诞的石头，通常以奇异动物的形式，从建筑物（通常是教堂）的排水沟中伸出来。|The cathedral's gargils were fascinating.  大教堂的滴水嘴兽令人着迷。|近义词：grotesque, chimera|反义词:None|词性:noun

词源:
From Old French *gargouille "throat, gullet," also "gargoyle," from gargouiller "to gurgle," of imitative origin.  源自古法语 *gargouille “喉咙、食道”，也是“滴水嘴兽”，来自 gargouiller “发出咯咯声”，是拟声词。
