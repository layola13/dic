lankiness:/ˈlæŋkɪnɪs/
noun
瘦长；细高
The state or quality of being lanky; tall and thin.
His lankiness made him look awkward on the basketball court. 他身材瘦长，在篮球场上显得笨拙。
tallness, thinness, slenderness, gangliness
stockiness, stoutness, chubbiness
n.
