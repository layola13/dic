brutalest:/ˈbruːtəlɪst/ | adj. | 最残忍的，最野蛮的 |  Superlative form of brutal: most brutal.  It describes something extremely cruel, violent, and harsh. | The brutalest act of the war was the bombing of the hospital.  战争中最残忍的行为是轰炸医院。|  The brutalest winter storm in decades left the city paralyzed.  几十年来最残酷的冬季风暴使这座城市瘫痪。|  fiercest, savagest, harshest, cruelest, most vicious | kindest, gentlest, most humane, most compassionate | adjective

Etymology: From *brutal* (cruel, savage) + *-est* (superlative suffix).  *Brutal* comes from the Late Latin *brutalis* “of beasts,” from Latin *brutus* “dull, senseless, irrational, brute, beast,”  ultimately related to Greek *baros* “weight, heaviness.”
