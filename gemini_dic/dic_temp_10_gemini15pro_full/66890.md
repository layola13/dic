expediential:/ˌɛkspɪˈrɛnʃəl/| adj.|经验的；体验的；基于经验的|Of or relating to experience or based on experience.|例句：The program offers students an experiential learning opportunity. 这个项目为学生提供了体验式学习的机会。

例句：She described the trip as an incredibly experiential journey. 她形容这次旅行是一次令人难以置信的体验之旅。|近义词：empirical, practical, observed, firsthand|反义词：theoretical, hypothetical, abstract|词性:形容词

词源: Late 19th century: from experience + -ial.
