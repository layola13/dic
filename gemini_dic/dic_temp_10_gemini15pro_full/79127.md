ETYMOLOGY->

From Latin *gladiātōrius* (“of gladiators”), from *gladiātor* (“swordsman, gladiator”), from *gladius* (“sword”) + *-tōr* (agent suffix) + *-ius* (adjective suffix).  The English word is formed with the suffix *-ship*, which denotes status, condition, or skill.
