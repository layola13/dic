caravanist:/ˈkærəvænɪst/
n.
房车旅行者；拖车度假者
A person who travels or vacations in a caravan.
We met several friendly caravanists at the campsite. 我们在营地遇到了几个友好的房车旅行者。
The caravanist parked his vehicle near the beach.  这位房车旅行者把车停在海滩附近。
synonyms: camper, RVer, motorhomer
antonyms:  resident,  settler
noun
