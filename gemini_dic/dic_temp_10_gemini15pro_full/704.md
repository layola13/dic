absent-minded:/ˈæbsəntˈmaɪndɪd/| adj.|心不在焉的，健忘的|Not paying attention to what is happening around you because you are thinking about something else.|He's so absent-minded, he's always losing his keys. 他太心不在焉了，总是丢钥匙。

He was so absent-minded he forgot to turn off the stove. 他心不在焉到忘记关炉子了。

|近义词：forgetful, preoccupied, distracted|反义词：attentive, mindful, alert|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *absens*, present participle of *abesse* "to be away" + "-minded" from Old English *gemyndig*  "mindful."  So literally "away-minded."

USAGE->
He is an absent-minded professor. = 他是一位心不在焉的教授。
She's so absent-minded she left her umbrella on the bus. = 她太心不在焉了，把伞落在公交车上了。

It's absent-minded of him to forget his wallet. = 他真是心不在焉，竟然忘记了钱包。
It was absent-minded of me to leave the keys in the car. = 我真是心不在焉，把钥匙落在车里了。

He has become increasingly absent-minded in recent years. = 近年来他变得越来越心不在焉了。
She has always been a bit absent-minded. = 她一直有点心不在焉。

His absent-mindedness caused him to miss the important meeting. = 他的心不在焉导致他错过了重要的会议。
Her absent-mindedness can be frustrating at times. = 她的心不在焉有时会让人很沮丧。


Don't be so absent-minded! = 别这么心不在焉！
Try not to be absent-minded during the exam. = 考试期间尽量不要心不在焉。
