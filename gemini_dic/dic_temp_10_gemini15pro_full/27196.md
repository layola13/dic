callisthenicses:/ˌkælɪsˈθɛnɪksɪz/| n.|健美体操，徒手体操|Systematic rhythmic bodily exercises performed without apparatus or with light hand apparatus.|She practices callisthenicses every morning to stay fit. 她每天早上做健美操以保持健康。|Callisthenicses are a great way to build strength and flexibility. 健美操是增强力量和柔韧性的好方法。|近义词：exercises, workouts|反义词:(没有明确的反义词，可以考虑 inactivity, laziness)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek καλός (kalós, “beautiful”) + σθένος (sthénos, “strength”).
