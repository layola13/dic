overcontracted:/ˌōvərˈkənˌtræktɪd/ | adj. |过度收缩的；过度紧缩的| Excessively contracted, often referring to muscles.  This can lead to stiffness, pain, and reduced range of motion. | The physiotherapist noted that her hamstring was overcontracted.  她的理疗师注意到她的腿筋过度收缩了。

The overcontracted muscles in his back caused him constant pain. 他背部过度收缩的肌肉使他持续疼痛。 | 近义词: tight, tense, constricted | 反义词: relaxed, loose, extended | 词性: 形容词

词源: over- + contracted (from contract, from Latin contractus, past participle of contrahere "to draw together, conclude, shorten")
