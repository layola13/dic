dapperly:/ˈdæpərli/
adverb
衣冠楚楚地；整洁地；漂亮地
In a neat and stylish way.

He was dapperly dressed in a suit and tie.
他衣冠楚楚地穿着西装打着领带。

She walked dapperly down the street in her new shoes.
她穿着新鞋，神气地走在街上。

近义词:smartly, elegantly, stylishly, neatly
反义词:slovenly, untidily, messily
词性:adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From dapper + -ly.  Dapper itself has uncertain origins, possibly from Dutch.
