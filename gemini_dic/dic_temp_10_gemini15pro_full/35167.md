clownish:/ˈklaʊnɪʃ/ | adj. | 小丑般的，滑稽可笑的 | Resembling or characteristic of a clown, especially in being foolish or ludicrous. |  His clownish behavior embarrassed his family. 他的滑稽行为让他的家人感到尴尬。

| Example: He made a clownish face to amuse the children. 他扮了个鬼脸逗孩子们开心。 | Synonym: foolish, silly, ludicrous, comical, buffoonish | Antonym: serious, solemn, dignified, grave | adjective

clownish:/ˈklaʊnɪʃ/ | adj. |  愚蠢的，滑稽的 |  Acting in a silly or foolish way, often to make people laugh. | Her clownish antics amused the children. 她滑稽的动作逗乐了孩子们。
