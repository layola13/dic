inequation:/ˌɪnɪˈkweɪʒn/ | n. |不等式|A statement that an algebraic expression is not equal to another.  It can use symbols such as ≠ (not equal to), > (greater than), < (less than), ≥ (greater than or equal to), ≤ (less than or equal to).|The inequation x + 2 > 5 is true for x > 3.|近义词:inequality|反义词:equation|词性:noun
