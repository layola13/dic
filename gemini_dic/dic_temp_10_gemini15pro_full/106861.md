labrador:/ˈlæbrədɔːr(r)/ (US) /ˈlæbrədɔː(r)/ (UK)| n.|拉布拉多犬，拉布拉多寻回犬|A breed of retriever known for its short, dense coat, webbed feet, and friendly temperament.
