consorting:/kənˈsɔːrtɪŋ/
verb (present participle of consort)
交往；厮混；鬼混
Associating with, especially in a disapproved way.
He was seen consorting with known criminals.  他被看到与已知的罪犯厮混在一起。
Synonyms: associating, mingling, fraternizing, keeping company
Antonyms: avoiding, shunning, isolating
词性: 动词(现在分词)

consort:/ˈkɑːnsɔːrt/
noun
配偶（尤指君王的）
A spouse, especially of a reigning monarch.
The King and his consort. 国王和他的配偶。
Synonyms: spouse, partner, companion, husband, wife
Antonyms: N/A
词性: 名词

consort:/kənˈsɔːrt/
verb
(与…)交往；(与…)结交
To habitually associate with (someone), typically with the disapproval of others.
He consorts with known criminals. 他与已知的罪犯来往。
Synonyms: associate, mix, mingle, fraternize
Antonyms: avoid, shun, isolate
词性: 动词
