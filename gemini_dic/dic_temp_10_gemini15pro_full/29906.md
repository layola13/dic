cautioners:/ˈkɔːʃənərz/
n.
保证人，担保人
People who vouch for someone else, often in a legal or financial context.
The loan agreement required two cautioners to sign.  贷款协议需要两名担保人签字。
guarantors, sureties
opponents, adversaries
noun
