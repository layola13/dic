hideth:/ˈhaɪdɪθ/
Verb
隐藏，遮蔽
Archaic third-person singular present form of hide.  It means "conceals" or "puts out of sight."
The cat hideth the mouse under the rug. 猫把老鼠藏在地毯下。
conceals, covers, obscures
reveals, exposes, shows
