ghettoed:/ˈɡɛtəʊd/ (UK) /ˈɡɛtoʊd/ (US)| adj.|被限制的，被边缘化的|Confined to or marginalized in a particular area or group, often due to social or economic factors.  Originally referring to the confinement of Jewish people to ghettos, the term now applies to any group facing similar restrictions and disadvantages.|The community felt ghettoed by the lack of resources and opportunities.|近义词：marginalized, isolated, segregated, restricted|反义词：integrated, included, mainstream|词性:形容词
ghettoize:/ˈɡɛtəʊaɪz/ (UK) /ˈɡɛtoʊaɪz/ (US)| vt.|使边缘化，使受限制|To confine or restrict to a particular area or group, often due to social or economic factors.|Policies that ghettoize certain communities can perpetuate inequality.|近义词：marginalize, isolate, segregate, restrict|反义词：integrate, include|词性:动词
