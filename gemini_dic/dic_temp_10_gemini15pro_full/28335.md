caramelize:/ˈkærəməlaɪz/ /ˈkɑːrəməlaɪz/| v. |焦糖化，使变成焦糖色|To heat sugar until it melts and turns brown and syrupy. |例句：She caramelized the onions for the soup. 她把洋葱焦糖化，用来做汤。

例句：The sugar caramelized quickly over the high heat. 糖在高温下迅速焦糖化。


近义词：brown, glaze|反义词:|词性:verb

caramelization:/ˌkærəmələˈzeɪʃn/ /ˌkɑːrəmələˈzeɪʃn/| n. |焦糖化|The process of caramelizing sugar. |例句：The caramelization of the sugar gave the dish a rich flavor. 糖的焦糖化赋予了这道菜浓郁的风味。

例句：The slow caramelization of the onions brought out their natural sweetness.  洋葱的慢速焦糖化使其天然的甜味得以散发。


近义词：browning|反义词:|词性:noun
