feminists:/ˈfɛmɪnɪsts/
n.|女权主义者|People who advocate for the social, political, and economic equality of the sexes.|The feminists at the rally held signs advocating for equal pay. 集会上的女权主义者举着倡导同工同酬的标语牌。|近义词：women's rights advocates, suffragists|反义词：anti-feminists, misogynists|词性：名词

feminist:/ˈfɛmɪnɪst/
adj.|女权主义的|Relating to or supporting feminism.|She identified as a feminist writer and activist. 她认为自己是一位女权主义作家和活动家。|近义词：pro-women, egalitarian|反义词：anti-feminist, sexist|词性：形容词

词源:  From the French word "féministe," derived from "femme" meaning "woman." Ultimately from Latin "femina."
