evince:/ɪˈvɪns/| vt.|表明，显示，表露|To show or express a feeling, quality, or other characteristic clearly.|Her behavior evinced a complete lack of interest. 她的行为表明她完全缺乏兴趣。|The data evinced a clear upward trend. 数据显示出明显的上升趋势。|近义词：demonstrate, exhibit, manifest, reveal, show|反义词：conceal, hide, mask|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *evincere*, from *ex- "out" + vincere "to conquer, overcome," related to victor "victor."*  The original sense was "to overcome in argument, establish beyond dispute," hence "to show clearly, make evident."
