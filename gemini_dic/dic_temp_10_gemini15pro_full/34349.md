classed:/klæst/ | v. | 分类的，归类的 | past participle of "class" meaning categorized or regarded as belonging to a particular group or type | The car is classed as a luxury vehicle. 这辆车被归类为豪华车。| categorized, grouped, ranked |  | verb |
Etymology: From the French word "classe," ultimately derived from the Latin "classis," meaning "fleet," "army," or "division."
