malfunctioned: /ˌmælˈfʌŋkʃənd/ | v. | 故障，失灵 | To fail to function or work properly. | The computer malfunctioned and lost all my data. 电脑故障了，我所有的数据都丢失了。 | The printer malfunctioned during the important presentation. 打印机在重要的演示过程中发生了故障。 | synonyms: failed, broke down, crashed, acted up, went wrong | antonyms: worked, functioned, operated, performed | verb

malfunction: /ˌmælˈfʌŋkʃən/ | n. | 故障，失灵 | A failure to function normally. | The system suffered a major malfunction. 系统出现了重大故障。 | There was a malfunction in the engine. 发动机发生了故障。| synonyms: failure, breakdown, glitch, defect, fault | antonyms: success, operation, working order | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From mal- (badly) + function.  The prefix "mal-" comes from French, ultimately from Latin "male" (badly, ill). "Function" comes from Old French "funcion" and Latin "functio"  (performance, execution).
