de-expropriation:/diːɛkˌsproʊpriˈeɪʃən/
n.
收回；取消征用；归还被征用财产
The act of reversing expropriation, returning property that had been seized by the government to its original owners.
The government initiated the de-expropriation of the land, returning it to the farmers. 政府启动了土地的收回，将其归还给农民。
De-expropriation of the factory allowed the family to resume their business. 工厂的取消征用使这个家庭能够恢复他们的生意。
restitution, return, restoration
expropriation, seizure, confiscation
noun
