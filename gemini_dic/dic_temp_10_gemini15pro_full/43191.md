crabber:/ˈkræbər/| n.|捕蟹人；抱怨者；爱发牢骚的人|A person who catches crabs, or someone who complains a lot, a grumbler.|He's a crabber, always finding something to complain about. 他是个爱抱怨的人，总是能找到东西抱怨。|近义词：complainer, grumbler, whiner|反义词:optimist, enthusiast|noun

crabber:/ˈkræbər/| n.|脾气暴躁的人；爱争吵的人|A person who is easily irritated and quarrelsome.|Don't be a crabber, just try to enjoy the party.  别这么暴躁，试着享受派对吧。|近义词：grouch, curmudgeon|反义词:easygoing person, agreeable person|noun
