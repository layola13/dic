fictionalizer:/ˈfɪkʃənəˌlaɪzər/| n.|小说家；虚构者；小说化者|Someone who creates fictional works, or adapts a true story into a fictional one.|The fictionalizer took liberties with the historical facts to create a more compelling narrative.  这位小说家为了创作更引人入胜的叙事，对史实进行了自由发挥。|Synonyms: novelist, author, writer, storyteller, fabulist|Antonyms: historian, biographer, journalist|noun


fictionalize:/ˈfɪkʃənəˌlaɪz/| v.|使小说化；将…改编成小说|To create a fictional account of something, often based on real events.|They fictionalized his life story for the movie. 他们将他的生平故事改编成电影。|Synonyms: novelize, dramatize, romanticize|Antonyms: document, report, factualize|verb
