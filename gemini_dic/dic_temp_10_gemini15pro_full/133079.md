nonequilibriums:/ˌnɒnˌiːkwɪˈlɪbriəmz/ | n. | 非平衡状态（复数） |  A state where a system is not in equilibrium, meaning it's dynamic and changing, often far from a steady state. | The researcher studies nonequilibriums in complex systems.  研究人员研究复杂系统中的非平衡状态。| Near-equilibrium states, steady states | Equilibrium states, balanced states | noun


nonequilibrium:/ˌnɒnˌiːkwɪˈlɪbriəm/ | adj. | 非平衡的 | Not in a state of balance or equilibrium; dynamic and evolving. |  The physicist specialized in nonequilibrium thermodynamics. 这位物理学家专攻非平衡热力学。 | dynamic, unstable, evolving | equilibrium, balanced, stable | adjective


词源:
"non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "equilibrium" (from Latin "aequus" meaning "equal" and "libra" meaning "balance").
