get-the-jump-on:/ɡet ðə dʒʌmp ɑːn/ | phrasal verb | 抢先一步；占上风；先发制人 | To gain an advantage over someone by acting before they do. |  Our competitors got the jump on us by releasing their product first. 我们的竞争对手抢先一步发布了他们的产品，占了上风。
