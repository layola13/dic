incorrespondency:/ɪnkɒrɪˈspɒndənsi/ | n. |不一致，不符，不相称 |lack of correspondence or agreement; disagreement; disparity| The incorrespondency between the two reports led to confusion.  两份报告的不一致导致了混乱。|Example: There's a marked incorrespondency between his words and his actions. 他的言行明显不一致。| 近义词:disagreement, disparity, variance, discrepancy,inconsistency | 反义词: agreement, correspondence, harmony, consistency | 词性: 名词

incorrespondent:/ɪnkɒrɪˈspɒndənt/ | adj. |不一致的，不符的，不相称的 | not corresponding; not agreeing; not matching |  The figures quoted in the two reports are incorrespondent.  两份报告中引用的数字不一致。| Example:  The witness's testimony was incorrespondent with the physical evidence. 目击者的证词与物证不符。 | 近义词: disagreeing, disparate, variant, discrepant, inconsistent | 反义词: corresponding, agreeing, harmonious, consistent | 词性: 形容词
