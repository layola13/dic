budokan:/ˌbuːdəˈkɑːn/ |n.|武道馆|A large indoor arena in Tokyo, Japan, originally built for the 1964 Summer Olympics and now used for martial arts competitions, concerts, and other events.  It comes from the Japanese words "bu" (martial arts) and "dōkan" (hall or building).|The Beatles played at the Budokan in 1966.  披头士乐队于1966年在武道馆演出。

The Budokan is a famous venue for rock concerts. 武道馆是著名的摇滚音乐会场地。|近义词:arena, stadium, venue|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps open field) |词性:noun
