outdrag:/ˌaʊtˈdræɡ/
verb
胜过；在（某方面）超过
to surpass someone in a particular activity, especially one involving endurance or flamboyance.
RuPaul outdragged everyone on the runway last night.  昨晚鲁保罗在T台上艳压群芳。
synonyms: outdo, surpass, outperform, excel, eclipse
antonyms: lose to, be outdone by
word type: verb
