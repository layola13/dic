词源:
From Old English *ciele, cele (“cold”), from Proto-Germanic *kaliz (“cold”). Cognate with West Frisian kjeld (“cold”), Dutch kil (“chill”), German Kälte (“cold”), dialectal German Kehl (“throat”) (influenced by Kehle (“throat”)). Related also to Old Norse kal (“cold”).  The verb senses developed from the adjective.
