harder:/ˈhɑːrdər/ (US) /ˈhɑːdə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更难的，更坚硬的，更努力的|Comparative form of "hard."  Indicates a greater degree of difficulty, firmness, or effort.|例句：This task is harder than I expected. 这项任务比我预期的要难。|近义词：tougher, more difficult, more challenging, firmer, more solid, more strenuous|反义词：easier, softer, less demanding|词性:形容词
