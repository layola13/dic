detectors:/dɪˈtɛktərz/ | n. | 探测器；检测器 | A device designed to detect the presence of something, such as smoke, metal, or radiation. |  The detectors alerted the firefighters to the presence of smoke.  探测器向消防员发出了烟雾存在的警报。| synonyms: sensors, monitors, scanners | antonyms: concealers, hiders | noun

detectors:/dɪˈtɛktərz/ | n. | 探测器；检测器 |  Instruments used to discover specific substances or conditions. |  The metal detectors at the airport helped ensure passenger safety. 机场里的金属探测器有助于确保乘客安全。 | synonyms: finders, locators, indicators | antonyms:  maskers, camouflagers | noun
