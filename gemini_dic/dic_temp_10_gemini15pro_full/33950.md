cirrostrati:/ˌsɪroʊˈstrɑːtaɪ/ /ˌsɪrəˈstreɪtiː/
noun
卷层云
High altitude sheet-like clouds composed of ice crystals, often producing halo effects around the sun or moon and sometimes indicating approaching rain or snow.
Cirrostrati clouds veiled the sun, creating a halo effect. 卷层云遮蔽了太阳，形成了光晕效应。
