antrorse:/ænˈtrɔːrs/ | adj. | 向上或向前生长的；向前弯曲的 | Directed forward or upward, especially toward the apex or tip. | The plant's antrorse hairs helped it cling to surfaces.  这种植物的向上生长的毛发帮助它附着在表面上。| Example 2:  The antrorse barbs on the fishhook made it difficult to remove.  鱼钩上向前弯曲的倒钩使它很难被取出来。| Synonym: forward-pointing, upward-pointing | Antonym: retrorse | Adjective
