inaptitude:/ɪˈnæptɪtjuːd/
n.
lack of skill or ability; unsuitableness
The student's inaptitude for mathematics was apparent from an early age. 这个学生对数学的无能从很小的时候就显而易见了。
inability, incompetence, lack of skill, unfitness
aptitude, skill, talent, ability
noun
