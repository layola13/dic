halituous:/həˈlɪtʃuəs/| adj.|充满蒸汽的；恶臭的；令人作呕的|Filled with vapor; foul-smelling; nauseating.|The halituous air of the swamp made it difficult to breathe. 沼泽里充满蒸汽的空气使人难以呼吸。|近义词：fetid, noxious, rank, mephitic|反义词:fresh, fragrant, sweet|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Latin *halitus* ("breath, vapor, exhalation"), from *halare* ("to breathe").
