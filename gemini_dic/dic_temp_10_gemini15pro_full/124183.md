mosaiclike:/moʊˈzeɪɪklaɪk/
adjective
像马赛克的，拼凑式的
Resembling a mosaic; composed of diverse elements.
The artist created a mosaiclike pattern on the wall using tiny pieces of colored glass.  艺术家用彩色玻璃碎片在墙上创作了一幅像马赛克一样的图案。
The story is told in a mosaiclike structure, jumping between different characters and timelines.  这个故事以一种类似马赛克的结构讲述，在不同的角色和时间线之间跳跃。
近义词:patchwork, fragmented, composite
反义词:uniform, homogeneous
词性:adjective
