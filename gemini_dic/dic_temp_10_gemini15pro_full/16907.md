basket:/ˈbæskɪt (US), ˈbɑːskɪt (UK)| n.|篮子，筐|A container used for carrying or storing things, typically made of interwoven material such as wicker, cane, or wire.|例句：She put the apples in a basket. 她把苹果放进篮子里。

例句：He carried a basket of flowers. 他提着一篮花。


近义词：container, receptacle, hamper|反义词:|词性:noun

basket:/ˈbæskɪt (US), ˈbɑːskɪt (UK)| n.|一篮之量|The amount contained in a basket.|例句：A basket of laundry. 一篮子待洗衣物。

例句：A basket of grapes. 一篮葡萄。


近义词:batch, load|反义词:|词性:noun

basket:/ˈbæskɪt (US), ˈbɑːskɪt (UK)| n.|(篮球)篮，得分|The hoop through which the ball is thrown in basketball.|例句：He shot the ball into the basket. 他把球投进了篮筐。

例句：The basket was just out of his reach.  篮筐刚好在他的够不着的地方。


近义词：hoop, net|反义词:|词性:noun
