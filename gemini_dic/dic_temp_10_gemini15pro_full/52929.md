din:/dɪn/ | n. | 喧闹声，嘈杂声 | a loud, unpleasant, and prolonged noise | The din of the traffic made it hard to concentrate. 交通的喧嚣声使人难以集中注意力。| synonyms: noise, racket, uproar, clamor | antonyms: silence, quiet | noun

din:/dɪn/ | vt. |  使(某人)反复听到; 向(某人)不停地说 | to make (someone) hear something repeatedly; to repeat something to someone continually | He dinned his complaints into her ears. 他不断地向她抱怨。| synonyms:  bombard, reiterate, harp on | antonyms: whisper, murmur | verb
