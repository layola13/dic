anointers:/əˈnɔɪntərz/
n.|涂油者，加冕者|People who anoint someone or something, typically with oil, often as part of a religious ceremony.|The anointers prepared the sacred oil for the coronation. 涂油者为加冕仪式准备了圣油。|近义词：consecrators, ordinators|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

anointers:/əˈnɔɪntərz/
n.|指定者;任命者|Those who designate or appoint someone to a position, often of authority.|The king's anointers chose his successor. 国王的指定者们选择了他的继任者。|近义词：appointers, nominators|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
