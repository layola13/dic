multiage:/ˌmʌltiˈeɪdʒ/ | adj. | 多年龄段的，混合年龄的 |  Composed of or involving students of several ages or several grade levels. |  The school implemented a multiage classroom where children from different grades learn together. 这所学校实施了多年龄段的课堂，不同年级的孩子一起学习。 | mixed-age, multi-grade | single-age, same-age | adjective

Etymology:
"multi-" (from Latin "multus" meaning "much, many") + "age" (from Old French "age" meaning "age")
