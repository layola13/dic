majapahit:/ˌmɑːdʒəˈpɑːhɪt/ | n. |  满者伯夷 | An Indonesian Hindu–Buddhist empire based on the island of Java (1293–1527). It was an agrarian kingdom with a sophisticated bureaucratic structure. Its influence extended throughout much of present-day Indonesia. | The Majapahit Empire was a powerful force in Southeast Asia. 满者伯夷帝国是东南亚的一支强大力量。|  The Majapahit period is considered a golden age in Javanese history. 满者伯夷时期被认为是爪哇历史上的黄金时代。 | 近义词: kingdom, empire | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
