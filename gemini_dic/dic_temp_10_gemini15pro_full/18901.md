besmircher:/bɪˈsmɜːrtʃər/
n.|诽谤者，玷污者|A person who besmirches or sullies someone's reputation.|The tabloid was accused of being a besmircher of the actor's good name. 这家小报被指控玷污了这位演员的名誉。|Synonyms: slanderer, defamer, calumniator|Antonyms: defender, advocate|Noun

besmirch:/bɪˈsmɜːrtʃ/
vt.|玷污，诽谤|To damage the reputation of (someone or something) in the opinion of others.|The scandal besmirched the reputation of the entire family.  这桩丑闻玷污了整个家族的名誉。|Synonyms: sully, tarnish, smear|Antonyms: enhance, improve, elevate|Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + smirch.  "Smirch" comes from Middle English *smerten, from Old Norse smerkja "to smear," related to Old English smearcian "to smear."  The prefix "be-" intensifies the meaning of the verb.
