hypertechnical:/ˌhaɪpərˈtɛknɪkəl/| adj.|过度技术化的，过于专业的|Excessively or unnecessarily technical; using complex technical language that is difficult for ordinary people to understand.|例句：The manual was so hypertechnical that even the engineers struggled to understand it.  这本手册过于技术化，即使是工程师也很难理解。

例句：Her explanation was hypertechnical, leaving the audience confused. 她的解释过于专业，让听众感到困惑。|近义词：overly technical, esoteric, specialized, jargon-filled|反义词：simple, basic, layman's, accessible|词性:形容词
