dredge:/drɛdʒ/| n. v.| 挖泥机，疏浚；搜索，挖掘|A machine used for deepening or clearing out harbors, rivers, etc. / To search thoroughly, as if by digging.|The boat dredged the harbor. 这艘船疏浚了港口。|Synonyms: excavate, scoop, trawl |Antonyms: fill, bury|noun, verb

dredges:/drɛdʒɪz/| v. (dredge的第三人称单数形式)| 挖泥，疏浚；搜索，挖掘|Third person singular present tense form of "dredge".|He dredges the river every spring. 他每年春天都疏浚河道。|Synonyms: excavates, scoops, trawls |Antonyms: fills, buries|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *draggen*, from Old Norse *draga* (to draw, pull, drag).
