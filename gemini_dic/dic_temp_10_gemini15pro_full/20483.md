bituminization:/bɪˌtjuːmɪnaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /bɪˌtuːmɪnɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
沥青化；沥青处理
The process by which organic matter is transformed into bitumen, a black, viscous substance resembling asphalt.
The bituminization of the wood made it waterproof.  木材的沥青处理使其防水。
bitumenization, asphaltation
