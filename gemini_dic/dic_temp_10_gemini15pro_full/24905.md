bruiters:/ˈbruːtərz/
n.
散布谣言者；造谣者
People who spread rumors or gossip.
The bruiters were spreading false information about the company merger. 那些造谣者散布关于公司合并的虚假信息。
The bruiters were quickly identified and dealt with. 这些造谣者很快就被查明并处理了。
Synonyms: gossips, rumormongers, scandalmongers
Antonyms: truth-tellers
