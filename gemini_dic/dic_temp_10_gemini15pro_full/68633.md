fanzines:/ˈfænˌziːnz/
n.
同人志；爱好者杂志
A fanzine is a non-official magazine or newspaper written and published by fans of a particular band, TV show, etc.
Example 1:  He collects Star Trek fanzines. 他收集《星际迷航》的同人志。

Example 2:  She published her poems in a science fiction fanzine. 她在一个科幻迷杂志上发表了她的诗。
近义词:magazines, journals, publications
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "professional publication")
词性: Noun
