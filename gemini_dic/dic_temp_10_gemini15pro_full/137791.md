nonvenomously:/ˌnɒnˈvɛnəməsli/
adverb
无毒地；不带毒液地
in a nonvenomous manner; without venom.
The snake, though large, nonvenomously slithered past the hiker.  这条蛇虽然很大，却无毒地从徒步旅行者身边滑过。
The constrictor nonvenomously subdued its prey.  蟒蛇无毒地制服了它的猎物。
Synonym: harmlessly, innocuously
Antonym: venomously, poisonously
word class: adverb

Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + venomous + -ly (adverbial suffix)
"non-" comes from Latin, meaning "not."
"venomous" comes from Old French "venimeus," ultimately from Latin "venenum" meaning "poison."
"-ly" is a common suffix forming adverbs.
