count:/kaʊnt/|v. n.|数数，计算；计数；认为；重要|The teacher counted the students in the class. 老师数了数班里的学生。|synonyms: enumerate, calculate, tally, consider, matter|antonyms: disregard, ignore|verb, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *conter*, from Latin *computare* "to count, reckon," from *com-* "together" + *putare* "to prune, to think."  The original sense was "to reckon, calculate."
