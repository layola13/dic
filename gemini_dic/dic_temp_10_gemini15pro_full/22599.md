bootstrappers:/ˈbuːtˌstræpərz/
n.
创业者；自力更生的人
People who start and build a business with limited resources, relying on their own efforts and ingenuity rather than external funding.
Many Silicon Valley startups were founded by bootstrappers who worked out of their garages. 许多硅谷的初创公司都是由在自家车库里工作的自力更生者创立的。
entrepreneurs, self-starters, go-getters
freeloaders, dependents
noun
