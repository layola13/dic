downstroke:/ˈdaʊnˌstroʊk/| n.|下行冲程，向下击键，下划|A downward stroke, as of a piston, or a key on a keyboard; a downward stroke of a pen or brush.|The artist used a single downstroke to create the effect. 艺术家用单一的向下笔触创造了这种效果。|近义词：descent, drop|反义词: upstroke, ascent|noun

downstroke:/ˈdaʊnˌstroʊk/| n.|下划线|A line drawn under a word or phrase for emphasis.|He underlined the title with a strong downstroke. 他用粗重的下划线强调了标题。|近义词：underline, underscore|反义词: N/A|noun

Etymology:  "down" + "stroke"  Both words have Old English origins.  "Down" comes from *dūne, and "stroke" from *strācian.
