coking:/ˈkoʊkɪŋ/| n. |焦化，炼焦|The process of heating coal in the absence of air to produce coke.|The coking plant produces thousands of tons of coke each day. 炼焦厂每天生产数千吨焦炭。|近义词：carbonization|反义词:|n.

coking:/ˈkoʊkɪŋ/| adj. |焦化的，炼焦的|Relating to or used in the process of coking.|The coking coal is heated to high temperatures. 焦煤被加热到高温。|近义词：carbonizing|反义词:|adj.
