empath:/ˈempæθ/ | n. | 共情者，移情者 | A person who has an unusually strong ability to understand and share the feelings of others. | She's an empath and can always sense when someone is upset. 她是个共情者，总是能感觉到别人什么时候难过。| 近义词: compassionate person, sensitive person | 反义词: narcissist, sociopath | 词性: noun

empathic:/ɛmˈpæθɪk/ | adj. | 共情的，有同理心的 | Showing an ability to understand and share the feelings of another. | He gave her an empathic look. 他给了她一个充满同理心的眼神。| 近义词: compassionate, understanding, sympathetic | 反义词: unsympathetic, callous, apathetic | 词性: adjective

词源: From Greek empatheia, from em- 'in' + pathos 'feeling'.
