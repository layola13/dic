importuner:/ɪmˈpɔːrtʃənər/ | n. | 恳求者；不断请求者；纠缠不休者 | A person who makes persistent or troublesome requests or demands. |  The importuner kept bothering the celebrity for an autograph.  那个不断请求者一直缠着名人要签名。 | synonyms: beggar, solicitor, supplicant, pest, nuisance | antonyms: benefactor, giver, donor | noun

importune:/ˌɪmpɔːrˈtjuːn/ | v. |  (不断地)请求；纠缠；强求 | To ask (someone) pressingly and persistently for or to do something. | He importuned the king for a pardon. 他不断请求国王赦免他。 | synonyms: beg, beseech, implore, pester, harass | antonyms: offer, give, grant | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *importuner*, from Late Latin importunari "to make troublesome requests," from Latin importunus "unfit, inconvenient, troublesome," originally "having no harbor," from in- "not" + portus "harbor."
