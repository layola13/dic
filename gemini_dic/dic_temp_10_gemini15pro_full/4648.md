alec:/ˈeɪlɪk/| adj.|狡猾的; 花花公子的|Clever, mischievous, and often dishonest.|He had an alec grin on his face. 他脸上带着狡猾的笑容。|近义词：cunning, sly, wily|反义词:honest, sincere|Adjective
