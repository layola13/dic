mattednesses:/ˈmætɪdnɪsɪz/
noun
纠结；缠结；乱糟糟的状态
The state of being matted, tangled, or clumped together.
The dog's fur was full of mattednesses after rolling in the mud. 这条狗在泥里打滚后，它的毛乱糟糟地缠结在一起。
tangledness, clumpiness, knottedness
smoothness, neatness
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From matted + -ness.  "Matted" comes from the verb "mat," which originates from Old English "meatta" and is related to the Old Norse "motti," meaning "clod."  The suffix "-ness" is used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
