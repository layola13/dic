echinite:/ˈɛkaɪnaɪt/| n. |海胆化石；海胆纲的动物|A fossil sea urchin, or a member of the Echinoidea class.|Example 1: The museum displayed a fascinating collection of echinites from the Jurassic period.  博物馆展出了一批引人入胜的侏罗纪时期海胆化石。

Example 2:  The paleontologist discovered a rare echinite embedded in the limestone. 古生物学家在石灰岩中发现了一块稀有的海胆化石。|近义词：sea urchin, echinoid|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


Etymology: From Modern Latin *echinites, from Greek *echinos (sea urchin) + -ites (suffix indicating fossil).
