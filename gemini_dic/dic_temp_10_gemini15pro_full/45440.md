curacies:/ˈækjərəsiz/| n. |准确性，精确度|The plural form of "accuracy," referring to multiple instances of being accurate or precise. It denotes the quality or state of being correct or precise. | The scientist conducted multiple trials to ensure the accuracies of their findings. 科学家进行了多次试验以确保他们研究结果的准确性。

The accuracies of the measurements were crucial for the success of the experiment. 测量结果的准确性对于实验的成功至关重要。|近义词：precisions, exactnesses, correctness|反义词:inaccuracies, errors, imprecisions|词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin *accuratia*, from Latin *accuratus* (“prepared with care, elaborate”), past participle of *accurare* (“to take care of”), from *ad-* + *cura* (“care”).
