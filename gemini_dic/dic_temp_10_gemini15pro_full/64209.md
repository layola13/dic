equidiurnal: /ˌiːkwɪˈdaɪənl/ /ˌiːkwɪˈdɜːrnl/
adjective
昼夜平分的；等昼夜的
Characterized by equal duration of day and night.  Relating to the equinoxes.
The equidiurnal shadow is shortest at noon.  昼夜平分的影子在中午最短。
The spring and autumn equinoxes mark the equidiurnal points of the year. 春分和秋分标志着一年中昼夜平分的时间点。
近义词: equinoctial
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin *aequidiurnalis, from Latin aequus (“equal”) + diurnus (“daily”).
