disadvantageous:/ˌdɪsədvænˈteɪdʒəs/| adj.|不利(的),有害(的)|Presenting a disadvantage; unfavorable or unprofitable.|Choosing that location proved disadvantageous to the business. 选择那个地点对生意很不利。|近义词：unfavorable, detrimental, adverse, harmful, prejudicial|反义词:advantageous, beneficial, favorable|词性:adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From French *désavantageux*, from *désavantage* (disadvantage), from *des-* (dis-) + *avantage* (advantage), from Late Latin *abante* (from before), from Latin *ab* (from) + *ante* (before).
