cartographer:/kɑːrˈtɒɡrəfər/ /kɑːrˈtɑːɡrəfər/ | n. | 制图师，地图绘制者 | A person who draws or produces maps. | Example 1: The cartographer spent years meticulously charting the coastline.  制图师花了数年时间精心绘制海岸线。

Example 2:  Modern cartographers use sophisticated software to create detailed maps. 现代制图师使用先进的软件来创建详细的地图。 | 近义词: mapmaker, geographer | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French cartographe, from Greek khartēs (map, papyrus leaf) + -graphe (writer).  Ultimately from Egyptian.
