lovelify:/ˈlʌvliˌfaɪ/| v.|使可爱；使美好|To make something lovely or more lovely; to enhance the beauty or appeal of something.|She lovelified the room with flowers and candles. 她用鲜花和蜡烛把房间装饰得更可爱了。|近义词：beautify, enhance, prettify, adorn|反义词：uglify, spoil, mar|词性:动词
词源:  "lovelify" is a relatively modern word formed by adding the suffix "-ify" (meaning "to make or cause to be") to the adjective "lovely."  "Lovely" itself comes from the Old English "luflic," meaning "worthy of love."
