balkers:/ˈbɔːkərz/| n.|拒绝者，犹豫者|People who refuse to proceed or cooperate; those who hesitate or raise objections.|Several balkers refused to sign the agreement, holding up the entire process.  几个拒绝者拒绝签署协议，阻碍了整个进程。|Example: The balkers in the group prevented the project from moving forward.  小组中的犹豫者阻碍了项目的进展。|Near synonyms: objectors, dissenters, resisters, hesitators|Antonyms: cooperators, supporters, enthusiasts|noun

balker:/ˈbɔːkər/| n.|拒绝者，犹豫者|A person who refuses to proceed or cooperate; someone who hesitates or raises objections.|The balker at the back of the room voiced his disapproval loudly.  房间后面的那个反对者大声表达了他的不满。|Example: He was known as a balker, always finding reasons to delay decisions. 他以犹豫不决而闻名，总是能找到拖延决定的理由。|Near synonyms: objector, dissenter, resister, hesitator|Antonyms: cooperator, supporter, enthusiast|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "balk," which originated from Middle English *balken* "to miss, fail to hit," likely related to Old Norse *balka* "to divide into ridges."  The sense of hesitation or refusal developed later.
