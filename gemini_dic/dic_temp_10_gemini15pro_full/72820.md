foliaceous:/ˌfəʊliˈeɪʃəs/ | adj. | 叶状的，多叶的，叶片状的 |  Having a leaf-like structure; consisting of thin sheets or laminae.  Derived from Latin "folium" meaning "leaf." |  The foliaceous structure of the rock made it easy to split. 这块岩石的叶状结构使其易于分裂。| The mineral mica has a foliaceous habit. 云母矿物具有叶状习性。|  laminar, flaky, leafy | massive, compact | adjective
