blusteringly:/ˈblʌstərɪŋli/ | adv. | 猛烈地；咆哮地；狂妄自大地 | In a loud, aggressive, and typically empty or insincere way.  Characterized by bluster; loudly boastful or threatening. | He blusteringly claimed he could do the job single-handedly. 他狂妄自大地声称他可以单枪匹马完成这项工作。|  boisterously, loudly, aggressively, swaggeringly | quietly, meekly, humbly, softly | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From bluster + -ing + -ly.  "Bluster" likely originates from a Scandinavian source, possibly related to Old Norse *blaustra "to blow, puff."
