excommunicating:/ˌɛks kəˈmjuːnɪˌkeɪtɪŋ/
verb
逐出教会，开除教籍
officially excluding someone from participation in the sacraments and services of the Christian Church.
He was excommunicated for his heretical beliefs. 他因为异端信仰被逐出教会。
近义词: ostracizing, banishing, shunning
反义词:  readmitting, reinstating
词性: verb (present participle)
