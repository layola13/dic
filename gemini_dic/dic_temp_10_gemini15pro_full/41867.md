coscoroba:/ˌkɒskəˈroʊbə/ | n. | Coscoroba Swan/疣鼻天鹅 | A large, white, South American swan with a black-tipped bill and pink legs. It is the only member of the genus Coscoroba. |  例句：
The coscoroba gracefully glided across the lake. 疣鼻天鹅优雅地在湖面上滑行。

We observed a pair of coscorobas nesting near the reeds. 我们观察到一对疣鼻天鹅在芦苇附近筑巢。| 近义词：swan, mute swan | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
