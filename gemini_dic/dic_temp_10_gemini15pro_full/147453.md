overoffended: /ˌoʊvərəˈfɛndɪd/ | adj. | 过度冒犯的，过度敏感的 | Easily or excessively offended; too readily taking offense. | Example: He was overoffended by the joke and stormed out of the room. 他因为这个笑话而过度生气，冲出了房间。 | Example:  She's so overoffended these days; it's hard to have a conversation with her. 她最近太敏感了，很难和她聊天。 | 近义词：oversensitive, touchy, thin-skinned | 反义词：thick-skinned, insensitive | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning excessively) + "offended" (past participle of "offend," meaning to cause displeasure or resentment).  "Offend" comes from Old French "offendre," ultimately from Latin "offendere" (to strike against, stumble).
