darkish:/ˈdɑːrkɪʃ/| adj.|暗淡的，有点暗的|Somewhat dark; moderately dark.|The room was darkish, with only a single lamp illuminating the corner. 房间有点暗，只有一盏灯照亮着角落。|It was a darkish grey colour. 它是深灰色的。|近义词：dim, dusky, shadowy|反义词：bright, light|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From dark + -ish.  The suffix "-ish" is used to form adjectives meaning "somewhat" or "slightly."  The word "dark" comes from Old English *deorc.
