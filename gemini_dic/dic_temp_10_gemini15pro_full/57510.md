dragonet:/ˈdræɡənɪt/| n. |小龙，幼龙；鲬科鱼类|A small dragon; a fish of the Callionymidae family, typically brightly coloured and found in shallow coastal waters. |例句：The dragonet darted among the coral. 小龙在珊瑚间飞窜。


例句：The fisherman caught a colorful dragonet in his net. 渔夫的网里捕到了一条色彩艳丽的鲬鱼。

近义词：minnow, tadpole (for young of other creatures), sculpin (for the fish)|反义词:dragon|词性:noun
