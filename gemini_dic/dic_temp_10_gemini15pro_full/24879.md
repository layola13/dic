bruang:/ˈbruːɑːŋ/| n.|马来熊|A sun bear, native to Southeast Asia.  It is the smallest bear species.|The bruang is known for its short, sleek black fur and a distinctive orange patch on its chest. 马来熊以其短而光滑的黑色皮毛和胸部独特的橙色斑块而闻名。|Example: The bruang climbed the tree with surprising agility. 马来熊以惊人的敏捷爬上了树。|Synonym: sun bear, honey bear|Antonym: (No direct antonym for an animal name)|noun
