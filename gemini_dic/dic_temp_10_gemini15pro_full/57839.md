dressily:/ˈdrɛsɪli/
adverb
衣着华丽地；穿着讲究地
in a dressy manner; elegantly or smartly dressed
She was dressily attired for the evening gala. 她为晚会盛装打扮。
近义词:smartly, elegantly, fashionably
反义词:casually, informally, plainly
词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From dressy + -ly. Dressy from dress + -y. Dress (n.) from Old French drescer "to straighten, arrange, prepare," from Vulgar Latin *drectus. Ultimately from Latin directus "straight."
