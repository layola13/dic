eghen:/ˈeɪɡən/| adj.|自己的，自身的; (古语)容易的|Own; (archaic) easy.|He went to his eghen house. 他去了他自己的房子。|近义词:own, personal, individual|反义词:another's, alien|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *eghen, *awen, from Old English āgen (“own, possessed, peculiar, proper, private”), from Proto-Germanic *aiganaz (“possessed, owned”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂eyḱ- (“to possess”). Cognate with Scots awin (“own”), West Frisian eigen (“own”), Dutch eigen (“own”), German eigen (“own”), Danish egen (“own”), Swedish egen (“own”), Icelandic eiginn (“own”). More at owe.
