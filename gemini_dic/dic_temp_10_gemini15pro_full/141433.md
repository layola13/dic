ophthalmologists:/ˌɒfθælˈmɒlədʒɪsts/ | n. |眼科医生|A medical doctor specializing in the diagnosis, medical and surgical treatment of disorders of the eye.|例句：I made an appointment with the ophthalmologists to check my blurry vision. 我预约了眼科医生检查我的视力模糊。|例句：Ophthalmologists perform surgeries like cataract removal and LASIK. 眼科医生会进行白内障摘除和激光手术等手术。|近义词：eye doctor, oculist|反义词:|词性:noun
