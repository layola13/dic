magnetised:/ˈmæɡnɪtaɪzd/ | adj. | 磁化的，带磁性的 | Having been made magnetic; imbued with magnetic properties. |  The magnetised needle pointed north. 磁化的指针指向北方。

The iron filings were magnetised by the strong magnet. 铁屑被强磁铁磁化了。| 近义词：magnetic, polarized | 反义词：demagnetised, unmagnetised | 词性:形容词

magnetize:/ˈmæɡnɪtaɪz/ | v. | 磁化，使具有磁性 | To make something magnetic. | The process magnetizes the iron bar. 这个过程使铁棒磁化。

He magnetized the screwdriver to pick up the screws. 他将螺丝刀磁化以便吸起螺丝。 | 近义词：polarize | 反义词：demagnetize | 词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *magnetizare, from Latin magnes (“magnet”) + -izare.  Ultimately from Greek Μαγνησία (Magnēsía, “Magnesia”), a region in Thessaly, Greece, where magnetic lodestone was found.
