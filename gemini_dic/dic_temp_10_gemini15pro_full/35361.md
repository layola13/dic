co-inhere:/ˌkoʊɪnˈhɪr/| v. |共同存在，相互依存|To exist together or be inherently connected.|The different aspects of the problem co-inhere and cannot be treated in isolation. 这个问题的不同方面相互依存，不能孤立地处理。|The physical and mental aspects of a person co-inhere. 人的生理和心理方面是相互依存的。|近义词：coexist, interrelate, intertwine|反义词: separate, disconnect|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *coinherēre, from Latin co- + inhaerēre "to stick in, cleave to".
