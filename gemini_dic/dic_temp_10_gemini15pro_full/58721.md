ETYMOLOGY->

From dunce + -ical.  "Dunce" originates from the name of John Duns Scotus, a 13th-century philosopher and theologian.  Initially, his followers were called Dunsmen, but later, as his teachings fell out of favor, the term "dunce" came to be associated with stupidity.
