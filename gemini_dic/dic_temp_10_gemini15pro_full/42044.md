costumier:/kɒˈstjuːmiə(r)/ /kɑːˈstuːmiər/
noun
戏服设计师；服装师；服装提供商
A person who makes, supplies, or hires out theatrical costumes.
例句：The costumier created stunning period dresses for the play.  戏服设计师为这部戏剧创作了令人惊艳的时代服装。

例句：The film's costumier won an award for her work.  这部电影的服装设计师凭借她的作品获得了奖项。
近义词：costume designer, wardrobe mistress/master
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps "actor" or "director" as different roles in a production.)
词性: noun
