hammerman:/ˈhæmərˌmæn/| n. |锤工，锻工|A person who works with a hammer, especially a blacksmith.|The hammerman shaped the glowing metal on the anvil. 锻工在铁砧上塑造炽热的金属。|例句：The rhythmic clang of the hammerman's hammer filled the air. 锤工有节奏的锤击声响彻空中。

例句：The experienced hammerman crafted beautiful iron gates.  经验丰富的锤工打造了漂亮的铁门。|近义词：blacksmith, smith, metalworker|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
