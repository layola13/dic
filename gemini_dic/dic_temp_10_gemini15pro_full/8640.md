anticonfederationism:/ˌæntiˌkənˌfɛdəˈreɪʃənɪzəm/
n.
反联邦主义
opposition to confederation, especially to the Canadian Confederation of 1867.
The anticonfederationism movement in Nova Scotia was quite strong.  新斯科舍的反联邦运动相当强大。
近义词:secessionism, separatism
反义词:federalism, unionism
词性:名词
