ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *deturbātus*, past participle of *deturbāre*  "to thrust down, throw down, dislodge,"  from *de-* "down, away" + *turbāre* "to disturb, agitate, confuse," from *turba* "turmoil, crowd, disturbance."
