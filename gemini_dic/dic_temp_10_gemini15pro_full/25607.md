bumpier: /ˈbʌmpiər/ (US), /ˈbʌmpiə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更颠簸的，更崎岖不平的|Comparative form of bumpy: having a surface full of bumps.  Meaning even more uneven or rough than something already considered bumpy.|The road to the cabin was even bumpier than we expected. 通往小屋的道路比我们预想的更加颠簸。|The old cart rattled along the bumpier track.  旧马车沿着更加颠簸的小路嘎吱作响。|近义词：rougher, unevener, more rugged|反义词: smoother, flatter, evener|词性: adjective
