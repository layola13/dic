impastation:/ɪmˌpeɪˈsteɪʃən/ /ɪmˌpɑːˈsteɪʃən/
n.
厚涂颜料; 稠厚; 调色; impasto
The impastation of the oil paint created a textured surface. 油画颜料的厚涂形成了一个有纹理的表面。
The artist's impastation technique gave the painting a three-dimensional quality.  这位艺术家的厚涂技法赋予了这幅画一种三维的质感。
近义词:  impasto, thickness
反义词: thinness

词源: From Italian *impastare* "to knead," from *in- "into" + *pasta* "paste."
