nonaffiliated:/ˌnɑːnəˈfɪliˌeɪtɪd/
adjective
非附属的，无关联的
Not officially attached to or connected with an organization or group.
The nonaffiliated voters could swing the election.  那些无党派选民可能会左右选举结果。
synonyms:independent, unattached, unaffiliated, freelance, free-agent
antonyms:affiliated, attached, connected, related
词性: adjective
