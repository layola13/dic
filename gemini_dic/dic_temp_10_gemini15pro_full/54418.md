disincorporated:/ˌdɪsɪnˈkɔːrpəreɪtɪd/
adjective
解散的，未成立法人的
Deprived of corporate status; no longer existing as a legal entity.
The disincorporated company could no longer conduct business. 这家解散的公司不能再经营业务了。
The club was disincorporated after years of declining membership.  由于会员人数连年下降，俱乐部被解散了。
Synonyms: dissolved, liquidated, defunct
Antonyms: incorporated, established, operating
word type: adjective
