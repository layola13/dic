displaceability:/dɪˌspleɪsəˈbɪləti/ | n. |可取代性，可移动性，易位性| The displaceability of the population makes it difficult to track disease outbreaks. |  The ease with which something can be displaced or moved. This can refer to physical objects, people, or even abstract concepts like market share. |例句：The displaceability of the workforce due to automation is a growing concern. 由于自动化，劳动力的可替代性日益受到关注。

例句：The displaceability of the refugees made it hard to provide aid.  难民的可移动性使得提供援助变得困难。|近义词：mobility, movability, portability, transferability |反义词：immobility, fixity, permanence |词性：noun
