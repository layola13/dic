bonding:/ˈbɑːndɪŋ/ (US), /ˈbɒndɪŋ/ (UK)| n. adj.| 粘合，结合；建立关系，培养感情 |The process of forming a close relationship, especially an emotional one. It can also refer to the physical process of joining materials together.| The bonding between mother and child is very strong. 母子之间的联系非常紧密。| synonyms: connection, attachment, union, link, adhesion | antonyms: separation, detachment, division, estrangement | noun

bonding:/ˈbɑːndɪŋ/ (US), /ˈbɒndɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.| 粘合的，结合的 |Used to describe something that creates a close relationship.|  They went on a bonding trip to the mountains. 他们去山上进行了一次增进感情的旅行。| synonyms:  unifying, cohesive, connective | antonyms:  divisive, separating | adjective
