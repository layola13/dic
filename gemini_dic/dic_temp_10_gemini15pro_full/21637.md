blurriness:/ˈblɜːrinɪs/
noun
模糊不清; 模糊
The blurriness of the image made it hard to see. 图像的模糊使得很难看清。
He experienced blurriness in his vision after the accident. 事故发生后，他的视力变得模糊。
synonyms: fuzziness, haziness, indistinctness, unclarity
antonyms: clarity, sharpness, distinctness, focus
词性:名词

Etymology:
From blurry + -ness.  Blurry itself comes from blur, which likely originates from a Scandinavian source, possibly related to Old Norse *blyrr, meaning "blear-eyed."
