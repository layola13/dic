grasshopper:/ˈɡræshɒpər/ (UK) /ˈɡræshɑːpər/ (US)| n. |蚱蜢，蝗虫|A plant-eating insect with long hind legs which are used for jumping.|The grasshopper hopped from leaf to leaf. 蚱蜢从一片叶子跳到另一片叶子。|近义词：locust|反义词: N/A|noun
