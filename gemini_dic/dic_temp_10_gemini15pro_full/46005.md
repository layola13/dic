cybercrimes:/ˈsaɪbərkraɪmz/
noun
网络犯罪
Criminal activities carried out using computers and the internet.
Cybercrimes are becoming increasingly sophisticated. 网络犯罪正变得越来越复杂。
Cybercriminals often target vulnerable individuals online. 网络犯罪分子经常在网上针对弱势群体。
synonyms:  e-crimes, online crimes, internet crimes
antonyms:  lawful online activities
词性: 名词
