metanotum: /ˈmɛtəˌnoʊtəm/ | n. |后胸背板|The dorsal (upper) sclerite of the metathorax, the third segment of the thorax in insects.  It is the plate on the back of an insect between the base of the wings and the abdomen. |The metanotum of this beetle species is particularly large.  这种甲虫的后胸背板特别大。|Synonym: mesonotum (similar structure but in the second thoracic segment), tergum (general term for a dorsal plate)|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, potentially sternum which is the ventral plate)|noun
