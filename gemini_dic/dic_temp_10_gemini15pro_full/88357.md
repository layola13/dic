hippopotamus:/ˌhɪpəˈpɒtəməs/ (US) /ˌhɪpəˈpɒtəməs/ (UK)| n. |河马|A large, thick-skinned, semiaquatic African mammal with short legs, a massive head, and a large mouth.|例句：We saw a hippopotamus in the river. 我们在河里看到了一只河马。|例句: The hippopotamus opened its enormous mouth. 河马张开了它巨大的嘴。|近义词：river horse, behemoth|反义词: None (no direct opposite for an animal)|名词
