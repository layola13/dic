inaffable:/ɪnˈæfəbl/ | adj. | 不友善的，难以接近的，冷漠的 | Not friendly or approachable; aloof or unsociable. |  He was so inaffable that nobody dared to approach him for help. 他如此冷漠，以至于没有人敢向他寻求帮助。

The CEO's inaffable demeanor intimidated many employees.  这位CEO冷漠的态度吓到了很多员工。

| 近义词：unapproachable, aloof, unfriendly, distant, cold, hostile, reserved, surly, curt | 反义词：affable, approachable, friendly, amiable, genial, warm, sociable, gregarious | 词性：形容词

Etymology:
Late Middle English: from Old French, or from late Latin inaffabilis, from in- ‘not’ + affabilis (see affable).
