palingenetic:/ˌpælɪndʒəˈnɛtɪk/
adjective
再生的，重生，彻底革新的
Relating to or characterized by palingenesis, the rebirth or regeneration of something.  Specifically, in geology, referring to rocks formed from pre-existing rocks that have been completely transformed by metamorphism. Also used more generally to describe a process of radical transformation or renewal.
The palingenetic myth of the phoenix rising from ashes resonates across cultures.  凤凰从灰烬中重生的再生神话在不同文化中引起共鸣。

The geologist studied the palingenetic rocks, trying to understand their complex history.  地质学家研究了再生岩，试图了解它们复杂的历史。

Synonyms:  regenerative, renascent, revolutionary, transformative
Antonyms:  degenerative, declining, stagnant, static
词性: 形容词
