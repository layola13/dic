americanizer:/əˈmɛrɪkəˌnaɪzər/
noun|美国化者，使美国化的人|A person who Americanizes something or someone; one who promotes or imposes American customs, culture, or values.|The americanizer encouraged immigrants to learn English and adopt American customs.|Synonym:assimilator|Antonym:nativist
verb|美国化|To make American in character; to assimilate to American customs and institutions.|The company tried to americanize its products for the US market.|Synonym:assimilate, acculturate|Antonym:localize
