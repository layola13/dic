collage:/kəˈlɑːʒ/ | n. | 拼贴画；拼贴艺术；混合物 | A picture or design created by sticking photographs, pieces of paper, cloth, etc. onto a surface; a collection or combination of various things. | Example 1: She made a collage of holiday photos. 她用假日照片制作了一幅拼贴画。

Example 2: The city is a vibrant collage of cultures. 这座城市是充满活力的文化混合体。 | Synonyms: montage, assemblage, composite, medley | Antonyms: separation, division | Noun
