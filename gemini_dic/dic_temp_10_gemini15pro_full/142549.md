ostensive:/ɒˈstensɪv/ /ɑːˈstensɪv/| adj.|明显的；外表的；以指示方式进行的|Characterized by outward display; readily apparent.  Serving to exhibit or demonstrate.|He gave an ostensive yawn, hoping to signal his boredom.|近义词：apparent, evident, obvious, manifest, demonstrative|反义词：implicit, tacit, veiled, hidden|词性:形容词

词源:
From Late Latin *ostensivus, from Latin ostendere (“to show, exhibit”), from ob- (“towards”) + tendere (“to stretch”).
