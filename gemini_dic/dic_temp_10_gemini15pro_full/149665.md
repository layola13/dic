pais:/pɑːˈiːs/ or /paɪz/ | n. | 伙伴；朋友；老乡；同胞 |  A fellow countryman or person from the same region. From the Spanish word "país" meaning "country." |  He met a pais from his hometown at the conference. 他在会议上遇到了一个家乡的老乡。|  Synonym: compatriot, fellow countryman  | Antonym: foreigner, stranger | noun

pais:/pɑːˈiːs/ or /paɪz/ | n. | 国家；地区 |  (Less common, but still used in some contexts) A country or region. Also from the Spanish word "país." |  He traveled to many different paises in South America. 他去了南美洲许多不同的国家。|  Synonym: country, nation, land | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but consider: city, town, village if referring to smaller areas) | noun
