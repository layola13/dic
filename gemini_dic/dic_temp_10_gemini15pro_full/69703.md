fennel:/ˈfɛnəl/ | n. |茴香 |An aromatic herb (Foeniculum vulgare) in the parsley family, having feathery leaves and yellow flowers. The leaves, stalks, and seeds are used in cooking. |I love the anise-like flavor of fennel in salads. 我喜欢沙拉中茴香的茴芹味。|近义词:anise, dill |反义词: N/A |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *fenol, from Latin faeniculum, diminutive of faenum (“hay”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *kʷʰey- (“grass, hay”).
