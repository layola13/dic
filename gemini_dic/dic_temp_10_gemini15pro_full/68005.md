factitively:/ˈfæktɪtɪvli/
adv.
作补语地，使役地
in a factitive way; by acting as a factitive verb or construction.  A factitive verb is a verb that takes a subject and two objects, one of which is caused to be or become the other.
例句：She painted the wall blue. 她把墙漆成了蓝色。(painted acts factitively, causing the wall to become blue)

例句：He made me angry. 他让我生气了。(made acts factitively, causing "me" to become angry.)
近义词: causatively
反义词: N/A
词性: adverb
