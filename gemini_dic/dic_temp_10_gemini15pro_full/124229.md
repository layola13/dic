moss:/mɒs/ (UK), /mɑːs/ (US)| n.|苔藓|A small, flowerless green plant that grows in clumps or mats in damp or shady locations.|The rocks were covered in moss. 岩石上覆盖着苔藓。|Synonyms: lichen, liverwort|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider flowering plants)|noun

moss:/mɒs/ (UK), /mɑːs/ (US)| v.| (usually be mossed) cover with moss |The north side of the tree was mossed.  树的北侧长满了苔藓。|Synonyms: overgrow|Antonyms: clear, uncover|verb
