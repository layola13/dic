antagonised:/ænˈtæɡənaɪzd/
| v. |使敌对；使对抗；引起…的敌意（或反感）|To cause someone to become hostile or unfriendly.|His aggressive attitude antagonised his colleagues. 他的咄咄逼人的态度引起了同事们的反感。|近义词:alienate, offend, provoke, irritate|反义词:befriend, placate, conciliate|词性:动词

antagonising:/ænˈtæɡənaɪzɪŋ/
| adj. |对抗性的；引起敌意的|Causing hostility or antagonism.|Her antagonising remarks made the situation worse. 她对抗性的言论使情况恶化。|近义词:provocative, irritating, offensive|反义词:conciliatory, placating, friendly|词性:形容词
