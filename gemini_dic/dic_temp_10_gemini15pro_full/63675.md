epauleted:/ɪˈpɔːlətɪd/| adj.|有肩章的，佩带肩章的|Having epaulets or shoulder pieces.  An epaulet is a decorative shoulder piece on a garment, typically on the coat or jacket of a military uniform.|The general wore an epauleted uniform. 将军穿着一件有肩章的制服。|Synonyms: decorated, adorned, ornamented|Antonyms: plain, undecorated|adjective
