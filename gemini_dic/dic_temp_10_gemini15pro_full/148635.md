overthought:/ˌoʊvərˈθɔːt/ |v. (past tense & past participle)|过度思考；想太多|To think about something too much, typically to the point of causing anxiety or stress.|I overthought the interview questions and ended up rambling. 我过度思考了面试问题，最后语无伦次。|

She had overthought the situation and made herself unnecessarily worried. 她把情况想太多了，让自己变得不必要地担心。


近义词：overanalyze, ruminate, dwell on|反义词：dismiss, ignore|词性：动词

overthought:/ˌoʊvərˈθɔːt/ |adj.|过度思考的|Characterized by excessive thinking or analysis, often leading to anxiety or stress.|He felt overthought and exhausted after spending hours analyzing the problem.  在花了几个小时分析这个问题后，他感到过度思考和精疲力竭。|

Her overthought plans often became too complicated to execute. 她过度思考的计划往往变得过于复杂而无法执行。

近义词：overanalyzed, obsessive|反义词：relaxed, carefree|词性：形容词
