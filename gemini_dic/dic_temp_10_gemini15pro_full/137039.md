nonstereotypical:/ˌnɒnˌstɛrɪəˈtɪpɪk(ə)l/ /ˌnɑːnˌstɛrɪəˈtɪpɪk(ə)l/
adj.
非刻板印象的
Not conforming to or exhibiting stereotypes; atypical.
Her nonstereotypical portrayal of a female CEO challenged traditional gender roles in media. 她对女性CEO的非刻板印象的刻画挑战了媒体中传统的性别角色。
The advertisement presented a nonstereotypical image of a family, showcasing diverse ethnic backgrounds and lifestyles. 这则广告展现了一个非刻板印象的家庭形象，展示了不同的种族背景和生活方式。
近义词:unconventional, atypical, unorthodox
反义词:stereotypical, conventional, typical
词性:adjective
