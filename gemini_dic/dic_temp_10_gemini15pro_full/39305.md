conifer:/ˈkɒnɪfər/ (UK) /ˈkoʊnɪfər/ (US)| n. |针叶树|An evergreen tree that bears cones and needle-like or scale-like leaves. |例句：Pines, spruces, and firs are all conifers. 松树、云杉和冷杉都是针叶树。|例句：The slopes were covered in dense conifer forest.  山坡上覆盖着茂密的针叶林。|近义词：evergreen, softwood|反义词:deciduous tree|词性:noun

Etymology: From Latin *conifer, from conus "cone" + -fer "bearing," from ferre "to bear, carry."
