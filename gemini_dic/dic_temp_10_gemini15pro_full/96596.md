inductionless:/ɪnˈdʌkʃənləs/
adj.
无电感的;非电感的
lacking inductance; not inductive
An inductionless resistor is ideal for high-frequency circuits. 无感电阻是高频电路的理想选择。
Synonyms: non-inductive
Antonyms: inductive
词性:形容词
