dunkable:/ˈdʌŋkəbl/| adj.|可扣篮的|Something that is dunkable can be dunked (usually referring to a basketball).  This implies that the object is of a suitable size and weight, and the person performing the action has the necessary skill and height.|That basketball hoop is dunkable even for shorter players.  那个篮球框即使对矮个子球员来说也可以扣篮。|Synonyms: slammable|Antonyms: undunkable|adjective

dunkable:/ˈdʌŋkəbl/| adj.|（食物）可以蘸的|Something that is dunkable can be dipped into a beverage or sauce.|These biscotti are perfect and dunkable in coffee. 这些饼干非常适合蘸咖啡吃。|Synonyms: dippable|Antonyms: undipable|adjective
