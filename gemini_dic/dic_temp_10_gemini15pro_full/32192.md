chibouk: /tʃɪˈbuːk/ | n. | 长烟斗；土耳其烟斗 | A long-stemmed Turkish tobacco pipe. | He puffed contentedly on his chibouk. 他心满意足地抽着他的长烟斗。| 近义词: pipe, hookah | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Turkish *çubuk* meaning "rod," "stick," or "pipe." Ultimately from Proto-Turkic *t͡ʃopuk*.
