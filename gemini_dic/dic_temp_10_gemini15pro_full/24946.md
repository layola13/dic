brush-aside:/brʌʃ əˈsaɪd/
verb
漠视，不理会，对…置之不理
to ignore or dismiss something, often abruptly or dismissively
He brushed aside my concerns about the project. 他对我在项目上的顾虑置之不理。
The CEO brushed aside the criticisms and continued with his plans. 首席执行官对批评置之不理，继续他的计划。
synonyms: dismiss, ignore, disregard, reject, pooh-pooh
antonyms: acknowledge, address, consider, heed
词性:动词


brush-aside:/brʌʃ əˈsaɪd/
noun
漠视，不理会
the act of dismissing or ignoring something
Her brush-aside of his apology hurt his feelings. 她对他的道歉不屑一顾，伤害了他的感情。
The government's brush-aside of public opinion caused widespread protests. 政府对公众舆论的漠视引发了广泛的抗议。
synonyms: dismissal, rejection, disregard
antonyms: acknowledgement, acceptance, consideration
词性:名词
