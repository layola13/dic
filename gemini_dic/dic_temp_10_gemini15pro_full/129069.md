nitrogenize:/ˈnaɪtrədʒənaɪz/
verb
氮化（v.）
To combine or treat with nitrogen; to introduce nitrogen into.
Steel is nitrogenized to increase its hardness.  钢经过氮化处理以增加其硬度。
The process nitrogenizes the surface of the metal. 这个过程使金属表面氮化。
synonyms: nitride
antonyms: denitrify
v.
