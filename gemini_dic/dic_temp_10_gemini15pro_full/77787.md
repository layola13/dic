generalissimo:/ˌdʒɛnərəˈlɪsɪmoʊ/
|n.|最高统帅，大元帅|A generalissimo is the supreme commander of a combined military force, typically in wartime.  This term often implies a higher rank or authority than a typical general. |Example: The generalissimo directed the troops from his headquarters.  大元帅从他的指挥部指挥军队。

|Example: During the war, he was appointed generalissimo of all armed forces.  战争期间，他被任命为所有武装部队的最高统帅。
|近义词:commander-in-chief, supreme commander|反义词:subordinate, private|词性:名词
