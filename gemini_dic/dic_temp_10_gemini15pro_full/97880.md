innardses:/ˈɪnərdzɪz/ | n. | 内脏，内部结构 | The surgeon carefully examined the patient's innardses.  外科医生仔细检查了病人的内脏。 | 例句：The mechanic spread the innardses of the clock all over the table.  机械师把钟表的内部结构都摊在桌子上。| 近义词：viscera, entrails, guts, insides | 反义词: exterior, outside | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From innards + -es (plural suffix). Innards itself comes from Middle English inwardes, from Old English innweard, meaning "internal, inward."
