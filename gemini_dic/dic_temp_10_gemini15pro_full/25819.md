burghers:/ˈbɜːrɡərz/
n.
市民，镇民，资产阶级
A citizen of a town or city, typically a member of the middle class.
The burghers of the town voted to raise taxes.  小镇的市民投票决定提高税收。

The burghers protested the new law. 市民抗议新法律。

citizen, townsman, townspeople
aristocrat, peasant
