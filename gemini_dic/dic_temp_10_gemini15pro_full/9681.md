antitheft:/ˌæntiˈθeft/
noun|防盗装置|A device or measure designed to prevent theft.|The antitheft device prevented the car from being stolen.  这个防盗装置防止了汽车被盗。|近义词：security system, alarm|反义词：N/A|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
