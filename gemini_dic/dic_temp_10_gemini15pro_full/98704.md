insupportableness:/ˌɪnsəˈpɔːrtəbəlnəs/
n.
无法忍受；难以忍受
The state or quality of being insupportable; intolerableness.
The insupportableness of the heat drove them indoors.  酷热难耐，他们只好躲进室内。
近义词:  intolerableness, unbearable, unendurable
反义词:  tolerableness, bearableness, endurable
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From in- (not) + supportable + -ness.  Supportable from support (from Latin supportare "to carry, convey, bring up") + -able.
