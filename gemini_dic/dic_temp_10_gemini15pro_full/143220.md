outcurse: /aʊtˈkɜːrs/
verb
诅咒; 恶毒地咒骂
To curse or denounce vehemently; to surpass in cursing.
He outcursed the sailor with a string of vile epithets. 他用一连串恶毒的绰号咒骂那个水手。
近义词: curse, damn, execrate
反义词: bless
词性: verb

outcurse: /aʊtˈkɜːrs/
noun
诅咒; 咒骂
A vehement curse or denunciation.
His outcurse startled the quiet village. 他的咒骂声惊动了宁静的村庄。
近义词: curse, malediction, imprecation
反义词: blessing
词性: noun
