lunatical: /ˈluːnətɪkl/ | adj. |疯狂的，精神错乱的 |Insane; wildly foolish or unreasonable. |例句：His lunatical behavior frightened everyone. 他的疯狂举动吓坏了所有人。

例句：Investing all your savings in that scheme seemed a bit lunatical. 把你所有的积蓄都投资在那项计划上似乎有点疯狂。

近义词：crazy, mad, insane, demented, deranged |反义词：sane, rational, sensible |词性：形容词

词源:
From Late Latin *lunaticus, from Latin lunaticus (“moonstruck, affected by the moon”), from luna (“moon”).  The belief was that changes in the moon caused intermittent insanity.
