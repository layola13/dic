cerebriform: /səˈriːbrɪfɔːrm/ /səˈriːbrəˌfɔrm/
adjective
脑形的；像脑回的
Resembling the form of the brain, especially in its convoluted surface.  Having a convoluted or wrinkled surface like that of the brain.
The tumor had a cerebriform appearance.  这个肿瘤外观呈脑形。
The surface of the mineral was cerebriform. 这种矿物的表面像脑回一样。
近义词: convoluted, gyrose, sinuous
反义词: smooth, flat
词性: adjective
