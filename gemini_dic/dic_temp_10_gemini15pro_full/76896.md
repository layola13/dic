ganil:/ˈɡɑːnɪl/ | n. | 加尼尔（法国重离子国家实验室） | Ganil (Grand Accélérateur National d'Ions Lourds), the National Large Heavy Ion Accelerator, is a French national research facility located near Caen, Normandy. | The Ganil facility is a leading research center for nuclear physics. 加尼尔装置是核物理研究的领先中心。 | The SPIRAL2 project at Ganil aims to explore exotic nuclei. 加尼尔的SPIRAL2项目旨在探索奇异核。 | 近义词: heavy ion accelerator | 反义词:  (No direct antonym. Could consider "particle decelerator" in a very broad sense.) | 词性: noun


词源: Acronym from French: *Grand Accélérateur National d'Ions Lourds* (Large National Heavy Ion Accelerator)
