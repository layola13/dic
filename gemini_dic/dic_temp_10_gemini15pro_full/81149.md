grand:/ɡrænd/| adj.|宏伟的，壮丽的；重大的，主要的；极好的，很棒的|Magnificent and imposing; of great importance or significance; excellent, wonderful.|例句：They stayed in a grand hotel. 他们在一家豪华酒店下榻。/  It was a grand occasion. 那是一个盛大的场合。|近义词：magnificent, splendid, majestic, imposing, superb, excellent, wonderful|反义词：humble, modest, simple, plain, ordinary|词性:形容词

grand:/ɡrænd/| n.|一千美元；大钢琴|One thousand dollars; a grand piano.|例句：He won ten grand in the lottery. 他中了彩票，赢了一万美元。/  She plays the grand beautifully. 她弹奏大钢琴弹得优美。|近义词：(for $1000) thousand|反义词:|词性:名词
