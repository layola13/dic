diarrhoea:/ˌdaɪəˈriːə/ (UK) /ˌdaɪəˈriːə/ (US)| n. |腹泻|Frequent and watery bowel movements.  A common symptom of gastrointestinal infections.|例句：The contaminated water caused diarrhoea in many people. 受污染的水导致许多人腹泻。

例句：He suffered from diarrhoea after eating the street food.  他吃了街边小吃后腹泻了。|近义词：the runs, loose bowels|反义词: constipation|词性:noun

diarrhea:/ˌdaɪəˈriːə/ (US)| n. |腹泻|Frequent and watery bowel movements. A common symptom of gastrointestinal infections.|例句：The doctor diagnosed her with diarrhea. 医生诊断她腹泻。

例句：He experienced severe diarrhea during his trip. 他在旅途中经历了严重的腹泻。|近义词：the runs, loose bowels|反义词: constipation|词性:noun
