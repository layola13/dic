endpoint:/ˈɛndˌpɔɪnt/ (US), /ˈɛndˌpɔɪnt/ (UK)| n.|端点；终点|A point that marks the end of a line segment or interval; a point at which something terminates, such as a network connection or a communication channel.|The endpoints of the line segment are A and B. 这条线段的端点是A和B。|synonyms: terminus, extremity, termination, conclusion|antonyms: beginning, origin, start|noun

endpoint:/ˈɛndˌpɔɪnt/ (US), /ˈɛndˌpɔɪnt/ (UK)| n.|（通信）终端|A device or node at the edge of a network, such as a computer, phone, or sensor.|The security software protects all endpoints on the network. 该安全软件保护网络上的所有终端。|synonyms: terminal, node, device|antonyms: server, hub|noun
