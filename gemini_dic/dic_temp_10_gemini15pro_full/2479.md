adjust:/əˈdʒʌst/| v.|调整，调节，适应|To change something slightly, especially to make it more correct, effective, or suitable.|She adjusted her glasses and smiled. 她调整了一下眼镜，笑了。|He adjusted the thermostat to 70 degrees. 他把恒温器调到70度。|近义词：adapt, modify, regulate, alter, tune|反义词：misalign, disarrange, upset|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *ajuster "to arrange, bring together," from Vulgar Latin *ad-iuxtare, from Latin iuxta "near, next to."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I adjust the volume on the TV. = 我调整电视的音量。
She adjusts her schedule to fit her new job. = 她调整了她的时间表以适应她的新工作。

一般过去时:
He adjusted his tie before the interview. = 面试前，他调整了一下领带。
They adjusted the recipe to make it healthier. = 他们调整了食谱，使其更健康。

现在完成时:
We have adjusted our plans for the trip. = 我们已经调整了旅行计划。
The company has adjusted its prices due to inflation. = 由于通货膨胀，公司调整了价格。

过去完成时:
After she had adjusted the settings, the picture became clearer. = 在她调整了设置之后，画面变得更清晰了。
They had adjusted their strategy before the game started. = 比赛开始前，他们已经调整了策略。

将来时:
I will adjust the height of the chair later. = 我稍后会调整椅子的高度。
He will adjust his expectations based on the new information. = 他将根据新信息调整他的期望。

虚拟语气:
If I could adjust the temperature, I would make it cooler. = 如果我能调节温度，我会让它更凉快一些。
I wish I could adjust the time. = 我希望我能调整时间。

被动语态:
The settings were adjusted by the technician. = 设置由技术员调整。
The prices will be adjusted next month. = 价格将在下个月调整。

疑问句:
Can you adjust the brightness of the screen? = 你能调整屏幕的亮度吗？
How do you adjust the volume on this radio? = 这台收音机的音量怎么调？

否定句:
I couldn't adjust the focus of the camera. = 我无法调整相机的焦距。
He didn't adjust his behavior despite the warnings. = 尽管受到了警告，他也没有调整自己的行为。


PHRASE->
adjust to = 适应
adjust for = 调整…以适应…
adjust oneself to = 使自己适应于
make adjustments = 做出调整
adjust the sails = 随机应变
