clues:/kluːz/| n.|线索，提示|A fact or a piece of evidence that helps to solve a problem, mystery, etc. |The detective followed the clues to find the murderer.  侦探顺着线索找到了凶手。| synonyms: hints, indications, leads, pointers | antonyms: answers, solutions | noun


/kluːz/| v.|提供线索|To give someone information that helps them to solve a problem, mystery, etc.  |The witness clued the police in on the suspect's whereabouts. 目击者向警方提供了嫌疑人的行踪线索。| synonyms: inform, tip off, enlighten | antonyms: mislead, deceive | verb
