frothers:/ˈfrɒðəz/ (UK) /ˈfrɑːðərz/ (US)| n.|起泡剂；泡沫制造者；矿物浮选机|A substance that forms or promotes the formation of froth or foam, often used in mineral processing or in making foamed drinks.  Also refers to machines that create froth, especially in mineral flotation.|The barista used a milk frother to create a latte.  咖啡师用牛奶起泡器制作拿铁。|Synonyms: foaming agent, aerator|Antonyms: defoamer|noun
