motey:/ˈmoʊti/| adj.|多尘的，有尘埃的|Covered with motes; dusty.  Containing motes or particles of dust.|The old attic was motey and smelled of mildew.|近义词：dusty, dirty, grimy|反义词：clean, spotless|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From mote (a speck of dust) + -y (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Old English *mot (dust particle).
