geopolitical:/ˌdʒiːoʊpəˈlɪtɪkl/
adjective
地缘政治的
Relating to the study of the effects of geography (both human and physical) on politics and international relations.
例句：The geopolitical landscape is constantly shifting. 地缘政治格局在不断变化。

例句：Geopolitical tensions are rising in the region. 该地区的地缘政治紧张局势正在加剧。
近义词：international, global, strategic
反义词:domestic, local, internal
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From geo- (meaning "earth") + political.  The term began to be widely used in the late 19th and early 20th centuries.
