networkable:/ˈnɛtwɜːkəbəl/
adjective
可联网的;可组网的
Capable of being connected to or operated on a network.
This printer is networkable, so you can access it from any computer in the office. 这台打印机可以联网，所以你可以从办公室的任何一台电脑访问它。
Many modern appliances are networkable, allowing for remote control and monitoring. 许多现代电器可以联网，允许远程控制和监控。
近义词:connectable, linkable
反义词:standalone, isolated
词性:adjective
