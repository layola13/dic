multihued:/ˌmʌltiˈhjuːd/ | adj. | 多色的，五彩缤纷的 | Having many colors; multicolored. | The multihued foliage of autumn is a beautiful sight. 秋天五彩缤纷的树叶是一道美丽的风景。 |  The artist used a multihued palette to create the vibrant painting. 艺术家使用了一个多色的调色板来创作这幅充满活力的画作。 | Synonyms: multicolored, polychromatic, varicolored, kaleidoscopic | Antonyms: monochrome, monochromatic | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From multi- + hued, from hue (color).  "Multi-" is a combining form meaning "many," derived from Latin "multus." "Hue" comes from Old English "hīw" (color, appearance, form, shape).
