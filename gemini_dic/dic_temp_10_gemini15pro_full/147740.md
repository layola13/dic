overproduce:/ˌoʊvərprəˈduːs/ /ˌoʊvərprəˈdjuːs/| v. |过度生产；生产过剩|To produce more of a product, commodity, or substance than is needed or wanted.|The factory overproduced and now they have a surplus of goods. 工厂生产过剩，现在他们有多余的货物。|Farmers overproduced milk, leading to a drop in prices. 农民过度生产牛奶，导致价格下跌。|近义词：overmanufacture, glut|反义词: underproduce|词性：动词
