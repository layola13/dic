baksheesh:/bækˈʃiːʃ/ /bɑːkˈʃiːʃ/
n.|小费，赏钱，贿赂|A tip or gratuity, especially one given in countries where it is expected.  It can also refer to a bribe.|例句：He offered the porter a small baksheesh to carry his bags. 他给了搬运工一点小费让他搬行李。|例句：The official hinted that a baksheesh would expedite the process.  这位官员暗示，给点好处费可以加快办理速度。|近义词：tip, gratuity, bribe|反义词:(No direct antonym, possibly penalty if referring to bribe)|词性:noun
