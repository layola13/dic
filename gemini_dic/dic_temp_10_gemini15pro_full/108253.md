launchable:/ˈlɔːntʃəbl/| adj.|可发射的，可启动的，可推出的|Suitable for launching; capable of being launched.  Able to be started or set in motion. Able to be introduced or released (e.g., a product).|The rocket is now launchable. 火箭现在可以发射了。

The new software is launchable next week.  这款新软件下周可以推出。|Synonyms: releasable, startable, deployable, ready, operational|Antonyms: unlaunchable, unusable, inoperable|adjective
