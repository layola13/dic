dhed:/dɛd/| n.|已故的，死的 (主要用于苏格兰英语和方言)|A person who is dead.  Primarily used in Scottish English and dialects.|He visited his dhed's grave. 他去拜祭了他父亲的坟墓。|近义词：deceased, dead, departed|反义词:alive, living|词性:名词

词源:
From Middle English *ded, from Old English dēad (“dead”), from Proto-Germanic *daudaz (“dead”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰewh₂- (“to disappear, cease”). Cognate with Scots deid (“dead”), West Frisian dea (“dead”), Dutch dood (“dead”), German tot (“dead”), Danish død (“dead”), Swedish död (“dead”), Icelandic dauður (“dead”), Latin fūnus (“funeral”), Ancient Greek θάνατος (thánatos, “death”).
