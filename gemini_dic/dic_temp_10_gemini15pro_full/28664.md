carinaria:/ˌkærɪˈnɛəriə/
noun
玻璃翼螺科;玻璃螺
A genus of pelagic marine gastropod molluscs with a thin, transparent, pelagic shell.  They are part of the heteropod group, which means they have a modified foot used for swimming.
The carinaria propelled itself gracefully through the water. 玻璃翼螺优雅地在水中推进。
synonyms: heteropod, sea snail, pelagic snail
antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a specific genus.  Could use "benthic snail" for a snail that lives on the seafloor)
