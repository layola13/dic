conterminant:/kənˈtɜːrmɪnənt/ /kɑːnˈtɜːrmɪnənt/
adjective
相邻的，接壤的；同界限的，同一范围的
Having the same boundaries or extent; contiguous.
例句：The conterminant properties extended for miles.  这些相邻的房产绵延数英里。

例句：The two conterminant nations share a long border. 这两个接壤的国家共享着一条漫长的边界。

近义词：adjacent, adjoining, contiguous, bordering, neighboring
反义词：distant, separate, nonadjacent, discontiguous

词性: adjective


词源: From Latin conterminus, from con- 'together' + terminus 'boundary'.
