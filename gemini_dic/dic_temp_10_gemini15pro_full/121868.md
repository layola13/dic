misrewarded:/ˌmɪsrɪˈwɔːrdɪd/
|adj.|错误地奖励的；奖励不当的；|Given a reward that is not deserved or appropriate, or for the wrong reasons.|The employee felt misrewarded for his hard work when he received a smaller bonus than his colleagues.  这位员工感到自己的辛勤工作受到了不公正的奖励，因为他得到的奖金比同事少。|misapplied, wrongly compensated|appropriately rewarded, justly compensated|adjective
