clarifier:/ˈklærɪfaɪər/| n.|澄清剂，净化器，阐明者|A substance used to remove impurities from a liquid or to make it clear. A person or thing that makes something clearer or easier to understand.|The water treatment plant uses a clarifier to remove sediment from the water.  这家水处理厂使用澄清剂去除水中的沉淀物。|synonyms: purifier, filter, elucidator|antonyms: contaminant, pollutant|noun
