nonsalutariness:/ˌnɒnsæljuːˈtɛrɪnəs/
n.
无益; 不利于健康
The nonsalutariness of smoking is widely acknowledged.
吸烟有害健康是众所周知的。
lack of healthfulness, unwholesomeness, detrimental nature
salubriousness, healthfulness, wholesomeness
noun


Etymology:
From non- + salutary + -ness.  Non- is a prefix meaning "not." Salutary comes from the Latin word "salutaris," meaning "healthful."  The suffix -ness forms nouns from adjectives.
