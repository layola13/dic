overneglect: /ˌoʊvər nɪˈɡlɛkt/
verb
过度忽视，极度疏忽
to neglect to an excessive degree
The city council overneglected the maintenance of the bridge, which led to its eventual collapse. 市议会过度忽视了桥梁的维护，最终导致桥梁倒塌。
The farmer overneglected his crops during the drought, resulting in a poor harvest.  这个农民在干旱期间极度疏忽了庄稼，导致收成不好。
近义词: disregard, ignore, slight
反义词: cherish, nurture, prioritize
词性: verb

词源:
From "over-" (meaning "excessively") + "neglect" (from Latin *neglegere*, meaning "to disregard").
