bespread:/bɪˈsprɛd/ | v. | 覆盖；遍布 | To cover the surface of something completely with something else. | The table was bespread with a white cloth. 桌子上铺着一块白布。

The field was bespread with wildflowers. 田野里遍布野花。

| 近义词：cover, blanket, overspread | 反义词：uncover, bare |词性:动词
