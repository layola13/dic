magnoliaceous:/ˌmæɡˌnoʊliˈeɪʃəs/
|adj.|木兰科的；似木兰的|Of or relating to the magnolia family (Magnoliaceae) of trees and shrubs.|The garden features a stunning collection of magnoliaceous trees.  这个花园以令人惊叹的木兰科树木收藏为特色。|The sweet fragrance filled the air, a telltale sign of the magnoliaceous blooms nearby. 甜美的香味弥漫在空气中，这是附近木兰科植物开花的迹象。|近义词: magnolia-like|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-magnoliaceous)|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From *Magnolia* (the genus name), + *-aceous* (a suffix meaning "of or pertaining to the nature of," "resembling").  *Magnolia* was named after Pierre Magnol, a French botanist.
