nonrequirable:/ˌnɒnrɪˈkwaɪərəbl/
adjective
非必需的，不需要的
Not necessary or required; optional.
This software update is nonrequirable, but it includes several performance improvements. 这个软件更新并非必需，但它包含了一些性能改进。
The extra features are nonrequirable and can be removed if needed. 这些额外功能并非必需，如果需要可以移除。
近义词: optional, unnecessary, dispensable
反义词: required, necessary, essential, mandatory
词性: adjective
词源: non- (not) + requirable (capable of being required)
