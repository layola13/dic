alluvials:/əˈluːviəlz/ | n. | 冲积层；冲积土 | Deposits of earth, sand, and other transported matter left by rivers, floods, etc. |  The fertile alluvials of the Nile Valley have supported agriculture for millennia. 尼罗河谷肥沃的冲积层支撑了数千年的农业。

The farmer enriched his fields with alluvials from the nearby river.  这位农民用附近河流的冲积土来肥沃他的田地。 | 近义词：sediment, deposit, silt | 反义词: bedrock | 词性: 名词

alluvial:/əˈluːviəl/ | adj. | 冲积的 | Relating to or composed of alluvium; deposited by running water. | The alluvial plain is prone to flooding. 冲积平原容易发生洪水.

The alluvial soil is rich in nutrients. 冲积土富含营养物质. | 近义词： sedimentary | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 形容词
