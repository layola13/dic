counter-blast:/ˈkaʊntərˌblæst/ /ˈkaʊntərˌblɑːst/
noun
反击；驳斥；对抗
a forceful reply to an attack or criticism; something done to oppose or counteract something else
例句：The article was a counter-blast to the government's proposals. 这篇文章是对政府提案的有力反驳。

例句：His speech was a counter-blast against the accusations of corruption. 他的演讲是对腐败指控的反击。
synonym: rebuttal, refutation, retort, riposte
antonym: support, agreement, endorsement
n.
