gymnonoti: /ˌdʒɪmnəˈnoʊtaɪ/ | n. | 裸背鱼科; 电鳗目 | Gymnotiformes, also called the Gymnoti, are an order of teleost bony fishes commonly known as the Neotropical or South American knifefishes. They have long bodies and swim using undulations of a long anal fin.  Found exclusively in fresh waters of the Americas. | Electric eels and the black ghost knifefish belong to the order Gymnotiformes.  电鳗和黑魔鬼刀鱼都属于电鳗目。 |  近义词: Neotropical knifefishes, South American knifefishes | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun
