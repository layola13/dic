binominous:/baɪˈnɒmɪnəs/| adj.|双名的，有两个名字的|Having two names, especially a scientific name with two parts (genus and species).|例句：The binominous nomenclature system is used to classify living organisms. 双名命名法用于对生物进行分类。

例句：Linnaeus established the binominous system of naming plants and animals. 林奈建立了动植物的双名命名系统。|近义词：binominal|反义词:|adjective
