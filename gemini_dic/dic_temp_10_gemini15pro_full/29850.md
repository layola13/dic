causatively:/ˈkɔːzətɪvli/
adverb|起因地，因果关系地|In a causative manner; so as to cause something.  表示以引起某事的方式；以便引起某事。|The drug causatively affects brain function. 这种药物会对大脑功能产生影响。| 近义词: effectively, instrumentally | 反义词: passively |词性:副词

causative:/ˈkɔːzətɪv/
adjective|引起…的，成为…原因的|Acting as a cause; causing. 充当原因；引起。|Smoking is a causative factor in lung cancer. 吸烟是导致肺癌的一个原因。|近义词: causal, generating | 反义词: resultant, resulting |词性:形容词
