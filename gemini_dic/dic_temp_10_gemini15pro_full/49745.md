demographics:/ˌdɛm.əˈɡræf.ɪks/|n.|人口统计数据|Statistical data relating to the population and particular groups within it.|The demographics of the town show a predominantly elderly population.  该镇的人口统计数据显示，人口主要为老年人。|synonyms: population statistics, census data|antonyms: N/A|noun

demographic:/ˌdɛm.əˈɡræf.ɪk/|adj.|人口统计学的，人口结构的|Relating to the structure of populations.|The changing demographic trends are having a significant impact on the economy. 不断变化的人口趋势正在对经济产生重大影响。|synonyms: populational|antonyms: N/A|adjective
