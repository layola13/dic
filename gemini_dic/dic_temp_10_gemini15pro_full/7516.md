annealer:/əˈniːlər/| n.|退火炉，退火器，退火的人|A person or device that anneals something, especially metal or glass.  This process softens the material, making it less brittle and easier to work with.|The annealer slowly cooled the glass.  退火炉缓慢地冷却玻璃。|The experienced annealer knew the precise temperature required.  经验丰富的退火工人知道所需的精确温度。|近义词：temperer, softener|反义词：hardener|词性:noun


词源: From the verb "anneal," which comes from Old English *onælan "to set fire to, kindle, burn," later "to heat (metal or glass) and allow it to cool slowly, in order to toughen and reduce brittleness."
