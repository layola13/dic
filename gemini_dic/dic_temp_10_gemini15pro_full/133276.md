词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Old French non- and directly from Latin non "not."
exotic (adjective): From Late Latin exoticus, from Greek exotikos "foreign," from exo "outside."
-ally (suffix): Forming adverbs from adjectives, from Old English -lice, from Germanic *-liko-, meaning "in the manner of, like."
