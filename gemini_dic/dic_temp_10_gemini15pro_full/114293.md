mainprise:/meɪnˈpraɪz/| n.|保释，担保，释放|The act of taking responsibility for someone accused of a crime, promising that they will appear in court when required.|He was released on mainprise, pending further investigation. 他在进一步调查之前被保释了。|近义词：bail, surety, guarantee, pledge|反义词: imprisonment, detention|noun
