deplumate:/diːˈpluːmeɪt/
verb
拔毛；剥去羽毛
To strip of feathers; pluck.
The cruel farmer deplumathed the live goose.  残忍的农民活拔鹅毛。
Synonyms: pluck, defeather
Antonyms: feather, fledge
词性: 动词


deplumate:/diːˈpluːmeɪt/
adjective
被拔毛的；羽毛脱落的
Stripped of feathers; plucked.
The deplumate chicken looked pathetic.  被拔光羽毛的鸡看起来很可怜。
Synonyms: featherless, plucked
Antonyms: feathered, plumed
词性: 形容词
