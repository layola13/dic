omnivore:/ˈɑːmnɪvɔːr/ (US), /ˈɒmnɪvɔː(r)/ (UK)| n.|杂食动物；无所不读的人|An animal or person that eats food of both plant and animal origin.|Bears are omnivores, eating berries and fish.|近义词：generalist, all-eater|反义词: herbivore, carnivore|词性:noun
Etymology: From Latin omnis "all" + vorare "to devour."
