echinodermal:/ɪˌkaɪnəˈdɜːrməl/| adj.|棘皮动物的|Relating to or characteristic of echinoderms, a phylum of marine invertebrates including starfish, sea urchins, and sea cucumbers.|Starfish are echinodermal creatures. 海星是棘皮动物。|The echinodermal skeleton is made of calcite. 棘皮动物的骨骼是由方解石构成的。|近义词:invertebrate, marine|反义词:vertebrate|词性:形容词
词源: From Greek *ekhinos* "sea urchin, hedgehog" + *derma* "skin".
