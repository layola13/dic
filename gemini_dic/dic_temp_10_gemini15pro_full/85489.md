hast:/hæst/|/ v. (archaic)|你拥有 (第二人称单数)|You hast a kind heart. 你拥有一颗善良的心。|近义词:have (modern equivalent)|反义词:lack|词性:动词

词源:
From Middle English *hast*, *havest*, from Old English *hæfst* (second-person singular present indicative of *habban*, "to have").  Cognate with Dutch *hebt*, German *hast*, and Gothic *habst*.
