oosphere:/ˈoʊəsfɪr/| n.| 卵球 |The female gamete of certain algae and fungi; the egg cell in plants.| The oosphere is fertilized by the male gamete to form a zygote. 卵球与雄配子结合形成受精卵。|Synonyms: egg cell, ovum|Antonyms: sperm, male gamete|Noun
