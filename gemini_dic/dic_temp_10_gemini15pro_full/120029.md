millionism:/ˈmɪljənɪzəm/ | n. | 百万富翁主义，百万富翁崇拜 | The belief in or advocacy of the importance of millionaires and their influence.  The idea that the accumulation of wealth by a few is beneficial for society. | Millionism, the belief that millionaires are inherently superior, is a flawed ideology. 百万富翁主义，认为百万富翁天生优越的信念，是一种有缺陷的意识形态。

| The rise of millionism has led to policies that favor the wealthy. 百万富翁主义的兴起导致了有利于富人的政策。 | 近义词: plutocracy,  materialism | 反义词: egalitarianism, socialism | 词性: noun
