dehairing:/diˈhɛərɪŋ/
noun
脱毛，去毛
the process of removing hair from something, especially an animal hide
The dehairing process is crucial in leather production.  脱毛工序在皮革生产中至关重要。

近义词:  hair removal, depilation
反义词:  hairing

词性: noun (名词)
词源:  de- (prefix meaning "remove") + hair + -ing (suffix forming a noun from a verb)
