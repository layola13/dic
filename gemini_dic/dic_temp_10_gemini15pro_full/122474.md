mobloggers:/ˈmoʊblɒɡərz/ (US), /ˈməʊblɒɡəz/ (UK)| n. |移动博客作者，手机博客写手|People who create and maintain blogs using mobile devices, typically posting text, photos, or videos directly from their phones or other portable devices.| Mobloggers were among the first to report the news from the scene of the accident. 移动博客作者是最先从事故现场报道新闻的人。| bloggers, mobile journalists, citizen journalists |  N/A | noun
Etymology:  A blend of "mobile" and "bloggers." "Mobile" traces back to Latin "mobilis," meaning "movable." "Blogger" is derived from "blog," short for "weblog," combining "web" and "log."
