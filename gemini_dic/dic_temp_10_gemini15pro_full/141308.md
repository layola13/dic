open-pit:/ˈoʊpən pɪt/|n.|露天矿，露天采矿场|A large hole or excavation in the ground from which minerals are extracted. |They visited a huge open-pit coal mine. 他们参观了一个巨大的露天煤矿。|近义词:quarry, surface mine|反义词:underground mine, subsurface mine|词性:名词


词源: "open" + "pit". "Open" signifies exposed to the air, while "pit" refers to a large hole in the ground.  The term is descriptive of the mining method, which involves excavating a large open hole to extract minerals.
