efficient:/ɪˈfɪʃənt/| adj.|高效的，能干的，有效的|Performing or functioning in the best possible manner with the least waste of time and effort; competent, capable.|The new machine is very efficient. 这台新机器效率很高。|近义词：effective, productive, competent, capable|反义词：inefficient, incompetent|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin efficiēns, present participle of efficiō (“I bring about, accomplish”), from ex (“out”) + faciō (“I make, do”).
