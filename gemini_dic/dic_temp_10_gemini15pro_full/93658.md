imbrue: /ɪmˈbruː/ /ɪmˈbruː/ | vt. | 浸透；使充满；灌输 | to saturate or inspire with something, as if by dyeing or steeping |  The artist imbued the canvas with vibrant colors. 艺术家给画布注入了鲜艳的色彩。

The air was imbued with the scent of pine needles. 空气中弥漫着松针的气味。 | 近义词：infuse, permeate, saturate, imbibe, steep | 反义词：drain, cleanse, expunge | 词性：动词
