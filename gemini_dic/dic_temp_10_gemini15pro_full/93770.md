immelodious:/ˌɪməˈləʊdiəs/
adjective
不悦耳的，不和谐的
Not melodious; unmusical; harsh to the ear.
Her singing voice, unfortunately, was quite immelodious.  她唱歌的声音，很不幸，非常不悦耳。
The immelodious sound of the construction site made it difficult to concentrate.  建筑工地刺耳的声音让人难以集中注意力。
近义词:discordant, cacophonous, dissonant, inharmonious, unmusical
反义词:melodious, harmonious, euphonious, tuneful
词性:adjective
词源:From in- (not) + melodious (from melody, from Greek melos "song" + -oid "like").
