expostulate:/ɪkˈspɒstjʊleɪt/ /ɛkˈspɑːstʃəleɪt/
verb
抗议；劝诫；告诫；反对
To reason earnestly with someone against something that person intends to do or has done; remonstrate.
She expostulated with him about his decision to quit his job. 她劝诫他不要放弃工作。

expostulated with him about his reckless driving. 她因他不顾后果的驾驶行为而责备他。

synonym: remonstrate, protest, object, argue, reason with, plead with
antonym: agree, consent, approve, support
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin expostulatus, past participle of expostulare (“to demand urgently, remonstrate”), from ex- (“out”) + postulare (“to demand”).
