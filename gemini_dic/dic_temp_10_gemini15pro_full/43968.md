crickets:/ˈkrɪkɪts/| n.|蟋蟀;板球运动; (表示沉默) 吱吱…|Small, jumping insects that make chirping noises, also a popular bat-and-ball game.|The crickets chirped loudly in the night. 夜晚蟋蟀叫声很大。  The children were playing crickets in the park. 孩子们在公园里打板球。 "So, what do you think?" he asked.  *Crickets*.  “那么，你怎么想？”他问。  *一片寂静*。|近义词：(insect) grasshopper, locust; (game) cricket; (silence) silence, hush|反义词: (silence) noise, commotion|词性:名词

crickets:/ˈkrɪkɪts/| int.| (非正式) 用于表示尴尬的沉默|Used informally to indicate an awkward silence.|He told a joke, but all he got was crickets. 他讲了个笑话，但是回应他的却是一片寂静。 She asked a question, but the room was filled with crickets. 她问了一个问题，但房间里一片寂静。|近义词:silence, hush|反义词:laughter, applause|词性:感叹词
