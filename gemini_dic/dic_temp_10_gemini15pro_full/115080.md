maneuverers:/məˈnuːvərərz/
noun
操纵者，调遣者，策略家
People who perform maneuvers; those skilled in maneuvering.
The skilled maneuverers expertly guided their ships through the narrow channel.  熟练的操纵者巧妙地引导他们的船只通过狭窄的航道。
Synonyms: operators, strategists, tacticians, handlers
Antonyms: followers, bystanders, passengers
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "maneuver" + "-er" (agent suffix)
"Maneuver" comes from French "manœuvrer," ultimately from Latin "manu operari" (to work by hand).
