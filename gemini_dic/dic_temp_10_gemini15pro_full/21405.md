bloviate: /ˈbloʊvieɪt/ (US), /ˈbləʊvieɪt/ (UK)| v. | 夸夸其谈；高谈阔论 | To talk at length, especially in an inflated or empty way. | He bloviated for hours about his latest business venture. 他就他最新的商业冒险夸夸其谈了几个小时。

| The politician bloviated on the topic of economic reform.  这位政客就经济改革问题高谈阔论。 | 近义词：rant, rave, ramble, spout, declaim | 反义词：concise, succinct, laconic | 词性：动词
