outpaints:/ˌaʊtˈpeɪnts/
|v.|外绘; 扩展绘制;|To extend the borders of an image by generating new content that blends seamlessly with the original.|The AI model can outpaint images, creating expansive landscapes from small photos.|近义词:extend, expand, extrapolate|反义词:crop, reduce, restrict|动词

outpainting:/ˌaʊtˈpeɪntɪŋ/
|n.|外绘; 扩展绘制; |The act or process of extending an image by generating new content around its original borders.|Outpainting is a powerful technique for creating immersive and imaginative visuals.|近义词:extension, expansion, extrapolation|反义词:cropping, reduction, restriction|名词

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "outside" or "beyond") + "paint" (verb meaning "to apply color to a surface").  The term emerged with the development of AI image generation technology.
