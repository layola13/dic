conjugative:/ˈkɒndʒʊɡətɪv/| adj.| 共轭的，接合的| Relating to or involving conjugation, especially in bacteria.  (尤指细菌的) 接合的|The conjugative transfer of plasmids is an important mechanism for the spread of antibiotic resistance. 质粒的接合转移是抗生素耐药性传播的重要机制。|synonyms: transferable, transmissible|antonyms: non-conjugative|adjective
