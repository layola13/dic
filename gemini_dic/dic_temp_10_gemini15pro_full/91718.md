hypercritical:/ˌhaɪpərˈkrɪtɪkl/| adj.|过度批评的，吹毛求疵的|Inclined to find fault too readily; excessively critical.|He's so hypercritical of everything I do, it's exhausting. 他对我做的每件事都吹毛求疵，真让人筋疲力尽。|The hypercritical reviewer tore the novel to shreds. 这位吹毛求疵的评论家把这部小说批得体无完肤。|近义词：faultfinding, captious, carping, nitpicking, overcritical|反义词：tolerant, forgiving, lenient, uncritical|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- + critical.  "Hyper-" is a prefix meaning "over, beyond, excessively," from Greek hyper (over, beyond). "Critical" comes from the Late Latin criticus, from Greek kritikos (able to discern, judge), from krinein (to separate, decide).
