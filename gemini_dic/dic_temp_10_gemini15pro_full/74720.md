freeness:/ˈfriːnɪs/
noun
自由;大方;慷慨;无拘无束
The state or quality of being free, especially from constraint or limitation; generosity; liberality.
The freeness of her laughter made everyone feel at ease. 她爽朗的笑声让每个人都感到轻松自在。
Synonyms: liberty, generosity, liberality, openness, unrestrictedness
Antonyms: restriction, constraint, stinginess, meanness, tightness
