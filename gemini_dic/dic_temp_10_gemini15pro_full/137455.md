nonthirstiest:/ˌnɒnˈθɜːstiɪst/
adjective
最不渴的
Not the most thirsty; less thirsty than others.
Example: Of the three hikers, John was the nonthirstiest, having just finished a bottle of water.  三个徒步旅行者中，约翰最不渴，因为他刚刚喝完一瓶水。
Example:  Surprisingly, the marathon runner was the nonthirstiest person at the finish line.  令人惊讶的是，马拉松选手是终点线上最不渴的人。
Near synonyms: less thirsty, least thirsty
Antonyms: thirstiest, most thirsty
