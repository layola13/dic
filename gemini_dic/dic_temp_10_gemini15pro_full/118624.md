messiah:/məˈsaɪə/ | n. | 救世主，弥赛亚 | A leader or savior who is expected to deliver their people from difficulty or suffering.  A promised deliverer. |  He believed he was the messiah who would lead his people to freedom. 他相信他是将带领他的人民走向自由的救世主。 |  The group awaited their messiah who they believed would bring about world peace.  这个团体等待着他们的救世主，他们相信他会带来世界和平。| synonyms: savior, redeemer, liberator | antonyms: oppressor, tyrant | noun
