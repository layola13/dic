antilevelling:/ˌæntiˈlɛvəlɪŋ/
| adj. | 反对平均主义的，反对拉平的 | Opposed to levelling, especially in education; opposing policies that aim to reduce disparities in achievement or opportunity. |  The antilevelling movement argues that focusing on raising the floor lowers the ceiling, limiting the potential of high-achieving students. 反对平均主义运动认为，专注于提高底线会降低上限，限制高成就学生的发展潜力。 |  He expressed antilevelling views, believing that competition drives excellence. 他表达了反对平均主义的观点，认为竞争才能推动卓越。| near synonyms:  elitist, meritocratic, hierarchical | antonyms: egalitarian, levelling, equitable | adjective
