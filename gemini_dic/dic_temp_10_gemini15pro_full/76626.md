galloped:/ˈɡæləpt/| v.|飞奔，疾驰|To run or ride a horse at a gallop.  The horse galloped across the field. 马飞奔穿过田野。|例句：The horse galloped across the field. 马飞奔穿过田野。

例句：The children galloped down the beach. 孩子们沿着海滩奔跑。|近义词：ran, raced, sped, dashed|反义词:walked, strolled, ambled|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *galoper, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old Norse *kalpa (“to gallop”).
