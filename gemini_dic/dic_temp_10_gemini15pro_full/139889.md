odd:/ɒd/ (UK) /ɑːd/ (US)| adj.|奇怪的；古怪的；奇数的；剩余的；临时的；零散的|Strange or unusual; not even; remaining; occasional; not regular; various.|例句：He has some odd habits. 他有一些奇怪的习惯。|例句：It's odd that he hasn't called.  他没打电话很奇怪。|近义词：strange, unusual, peculiar, weird, bizarre, uneven|反义词：even, normal, regular, usual|词性:形容词

odd:/ɒd/ (UK) /ɑːd/ (US)| n.|可能性，概率|The probability or likelihood of something happening. (Often used in the plural: odds)|例句：The odds are against him winning. 他赢的机会很小。|例句：What are the odds of that happening? 发生这种情况的概率是多少？|近义词：probability, likelihood, chance|反义词：improbability|词性:名词
