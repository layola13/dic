fabricative:/ˈfæbrɪkeɪtɪv/
adjective
捏造的，虚构的
Relating to, or characterized by fabrication; tending to fabricate; inventive.
She told a fabricative story about her childhood. 她讲述了一个关于她童年的虚构故事。
The evidence presented was clearly fabricative. 提出的证据显然是伪造的。
近义词:fabricated, fictitious, invented, made-up, spurious, untrue, false
反义词:factual, true, real, genuine
词性:adjective

fabricate:/ˈfæbrɪkeɪt/
verb
制造，装配；捏造，编造
(1) invent or concoct (something), typically with deceitful intent.
(2) construct or manufacture (an industrial product), especially from prepared components.
He fabricated a story about losing his wallet. 他编造了一个关于丢钱包的故事。
The company fabricates steel components for bridges.  这家公司制造桥梁用的钢构件。
近义词:construct, manufacture, produce, build, assemble, forge, fake, invent, concoct, lie
反义词:dismantle, destroy, demolish, tell the truth
词性:verb

词源:From Latin fabricatus, past participle of fabricari ‘to build, construct, fashion,’ from fabrica ‘workshop, something skillfully produced,’ from faber ‘workman, craftsman.’
