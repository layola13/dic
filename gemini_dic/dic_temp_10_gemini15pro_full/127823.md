nematocera:/ˌnɛməˈtɒsərə/ (US) /ˌnɛməˈsɪərə/ (UK)| n. |长角亚目；蚊蚋|A suborder of Diptera characterized by long antennae, typically with six or more segments.  These insects include mosquitoes, gnats, midges, and crane flies.|Mosquitoes, which belong to the Nematocera, are known carriers of diseases.|近义词:None (Nematocera is a specific taxonomic term)|反义词:Brachycera (short-horned flies, another suborder of Diptera)|词性:noun
