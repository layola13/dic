condolence:/kənˈdoʊləns/ /ˈkɒndələns/| n.| 哀悼，慰问 | An expression of sympathy, especially on the occasion of a death. |  We offer our sincere condolences to his family. 我们向他的家人表示诚挚的慰问。

condolence:/kənˈdoʊləns/ /ˈkɒndələns/| n.| 慰问 |  Sympathy and sorrow for the misfortunes of others. |  Please accept my condolences on the death of your father. 请接受我对您父亲去世的慰问。


近义词:sympathy, commiseration, solace
反义词:congratulation, indifference
词性:noun
