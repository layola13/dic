detinue:/ˈdɛtɪnjuː/
n.|非法扣留，侵占|The unlawful detention of goods belonging to another.|例句：He brought an action of detinue against the warehouse. 他对仓库提起了非法扣留诉讼。

例句：She was sued for detinue after refusing to return the borrowed jewelry. 她因拒绝归还借来的珠宝而被起诉非法扣留。
近义词：withholding, retention, detention
反义词：release, surrender
词性：noun

detinue:/ˈdɛtɪnjuː/
n.|非法扣留（之诉）|A common-law action to recover personal property wrongfully withheld.|例句：The plaintiff sought the remedy of detinue. 原告寻求非法扣留的救济。

例句：Detinue is a legal remedy for recovering possession of specific chattels. 非法扣留是一种追回特定动产的法律救济。
近义词：replevin, trover
反义词：N/A
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *detenue, past participle of detenir "to detain," from Latin detinere "to hold back, detain," from de- "away, from" + tenere "to hold."
