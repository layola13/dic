nickeliferous:/ˌnɪkəˈlɪfərəs/
adjective
含镍的；产镍的
Containing or yielding nickel.
The nickeliferous ores are found in the Sudbury Basin.  萨德伯里盆地发现了含镍矿石。
The company mined the nickeliferous rock for its valuable metal content.  该公司开采含镍岩石以获取其宝贵的金属含量。

近义词:nickel-bearing, nickel-rich
反义词: (No direct antonym, could use terms like "nickel-poor" or "barren" in specific contexts)
词性:adjective
