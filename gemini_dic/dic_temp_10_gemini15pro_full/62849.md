enjoining:/ɪnˈdʒɔɪnɪŋ/
|v.| 命令，禁止，吩咐 |The judge enjoined the defendant from contacting the victim.| 法官禁止被告与受害者联系。|近义词: commanding, ordering, prohibiting, forbidding, directing, instructing |反义词: allowing, permitting |verb

enjoining:/ɪnˈdʒɔɪnɪŋ/
|adj.| 命令的，禁止的 |His enjoining words resonated with the crowd. |他命令式的话语引起了人群的共鸣。|近义词:  directive, imperative |反义词: permissive |adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French enjoindre, from Latin iniungere "to join into, fasten on; impose, inflict, enjoin," from in- "into, on" + iungere "to join."
