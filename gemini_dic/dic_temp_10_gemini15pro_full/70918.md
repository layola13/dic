firebird:/ˈfaɪərbɜːrd/ (US), /ˈfaɪəbɜːd/ (UK)| n.|火鸟，不死鸟|A mythical bird said to periodically burn itself to ashes and rise again, reborn and rejuvenated.  It's often a symbol of renewal, resurrection, or immortality.|The legend of the firebird is told in many cultures. 火鸟的传说在许多文化中都有流传。|The ballet "The Firebird" by Stravinsky is a famous work. 斯特拉文斯基的芭蕾舞剧《火鸟》是一部著名的作品。|synonyms: phoenix, phenix|antonyms: N/A|noun
