encrisped:/ɪnˈkrɪspt/ | adj. | 卷曲的；波浪起伏的；（尤指头发）卷缩的 | Having a covering of small, tight curls or waves; (especially of hair) curled or crisped.  | The wind encrisped the surface of the lake. 风使湖面起了涟漪。 | synonyms: curled, wavy, frizzy, kinky | antonyms: straight, smooth | adjective


encrisp:/ɪnˈkrɪsp/ | v. | 使卷曲；使起波浪 | To cause to curl or become wavy. | The heat encrisped the edges of the leaves.  高温使叶子的边缘卷曲了。 | synonyms: curl, wave, crimp, crinkle | antonyms: straighten, smooth | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From en- + crisp.  "En-" is a prefix meaning "to put into or onto," and "crisp" refers to something being tightly curled or brittle.
