nonflagrancy:/nɒnˈfleɪɡrənsi/
noun
非公然性；不显著性
The nonflagrancy of the offense led to a lesser charge.  该违法行为不具公然性，因此指控较轻。
The judge noted the nonflagrancy of the violation. 法官注意到违规行为不明显。
Nonflagrancy refers to the lack of blatant or egregious qualities in an action or offense.  非公然性指的是一个行为或犯罪缺乏公然或恶劣的性质。
The defense argued for leniency based on the nonflagrancy of the crime.  辩方以罪行不具公然性为理由，请求从轻处罚。
Near Synonyms:  unobtrusiveness, subtlety, discretion, inconspicuousness
Antonyms: flagrancy, blatancy, obviousness, egregiousness
