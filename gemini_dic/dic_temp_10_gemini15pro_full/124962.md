mufti:/ˈmʌfti/| n.|便服，便装；穆夫提（伊斯兰教法典的解释者）| Civilian clothes, especially when worn by someone who normally wears a uniform; a Muslim legal expert who is empowered to give rulings on religious matters.|The general appeared in mufti at the party. 将军在聚会上穿着便服。|Synonyms: civilian clothes, plain clothes|Antonyms: uniform, regimentals|noun
