cybersquatters:/ˈsaɪbərˌskwɑːtərz/
|n.|域名抢注者|People who register Internet domain names containing trademarks with the intent to sell them to the trademark owners at inflated prices.|The cybersquatters registered the domain name "mcdonalds.pizza" hoping to sell it to McDonald's.|Synonyms: domain grabbers, domain hijackers|Antonyms: legitimate domain owners|Noun
