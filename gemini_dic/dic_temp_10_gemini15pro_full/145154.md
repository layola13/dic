overassessments:/ˌoʊvərəˈsɛsmənts/ | n. | 过高评估；过度评估 | The act of assessing something too highly or too often.  It implies an excessive or unnecessary evaluation, often resulting in an inflated value or perception. | The city's overassessments of property values led to widespread protests. 城市对房产价值的过高评估引发了广泛的抗议。 |  The frequent overassessments of the student's progress created unnecessary anxiety.  对学生进步的频繁过度评估造成了不必要的焦虑。 | overvaluations, overestimations | undervaluations, underestimations | noun
