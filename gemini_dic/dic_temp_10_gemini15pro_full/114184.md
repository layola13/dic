mahumetanism:/məˈhɒmɪtənɪzəm/ /məˈhɑːmɪtənɪzəm/
n.
伊斯兰教（旧称，现已过时且带有冒犯意味）
An outdated and offensive term for Islam, implying a false belief that Muslims worship Muhammad.
The term "mahumetanism" was used in the past to refer to Islam, but it is now considered offensive.  这个词“mahumetanism”过去曾用来指代伊斯兰教，但现在被认为是冒犯性的。
The use of "mahumetanism" reflects an outdated and prejudiced view of Islam.  使用“mahumetanism”反映了对伊斯兰教过时和带有偏见的观点。
近义词：Islam, Mohammedanism (also outdated and offensive)
反义词:  None (as it's a religion, direct opposites don't apply)
词性：noun
