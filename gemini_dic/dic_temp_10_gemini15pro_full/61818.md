empire-building:/ˈempaɪər ˌbɪldɪŋ/| adj.|构建帝国的；扩张势力的|Used to describe actions or policies aimed at creating or expanding an empire.|His empire-building ambitions led to conflict with neighboring countries.|近义词：expansionist, imperialist|反义词: contractionary|adjective
