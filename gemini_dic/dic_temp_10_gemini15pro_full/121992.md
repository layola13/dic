misspecified:/ˌmɪsˈspesɪfaɪd/
adjective
指定错误的，设定错误的
incorrectly specified; wrongly identified or described.
The model is misspecified if it omits a key variable.  这个模型如果忽略了一个关键变量，就是指定错误的。
The regression equation was misspecified, leading to biased results. 回归方程设定错误，导致结果有偏差。
misidentified, inaccurate, incorrect, wrongly specified, flawed
correctly specified, accurate, properly defined
adjective
