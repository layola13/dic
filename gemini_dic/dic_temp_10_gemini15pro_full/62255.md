encumberment:/ɪnˈkʌmbərmənt/ | n. | 阻碍，妨碍；累赘；债务 | Something that encumbers; a burden or impediment. | The high interest rates are an encumberment to economic growth. 高利率是经济增长的一个障碍。| 近义词：hindrance, obstacle, impediment, burden | 反义词：aid, help, assistance | 词性: 名词

词源: From Old French *encombrer (Modern French encombrer), from combre "dam, weir, obstacle," from Latin cumulus "heap."
