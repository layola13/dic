ochreous:/ˈoʊkəriəs/| adj.|赭色的，黄褐色的|Of a pale brownish-yellow color; ochre. |例句：The walls were painted an ochreous yellow. 墙壁被漆成了赭黄色。

例句：The bird had ochreous markings on its wings. 这只鸟的翅膀上有赭石色的斑点。|近义词：yellowish-brown, brownish-yellow, buff, tan, sandy, fawn|反义词:blue, green, black|词性:adjective
词源:From Latin ōchreus, from ōchra (“ochre”).
