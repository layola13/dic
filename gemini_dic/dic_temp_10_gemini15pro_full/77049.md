garmentless:/ˈɡɑːrməntləs/
adjective
无衣的；裸体的
Without any garments; naked.
The garmentless man ran through the streets.  那个没穿衣服的男人跑过街道。
Synonyms: naked, nude, unclothed, bare
Antonyms: clothed, dressed, clad
词性: 形容词
