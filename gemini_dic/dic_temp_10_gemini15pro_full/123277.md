monoecious: /məˈniːʃəs/ /mɑːˈniːʃəs/
| adj. | 雌雄同株的；雌雄同体的 | Having both male and female reproductive organs in the same individual; hermaphroditic. |  Corn is a monoecious plant, with separate male and female flowers on the same stalk. 玉米是雌雄同株的植物，同一株茎上有雄花和雌花。| The earthworm is a monoecious animal. 蚯蚓是雌雄同体的动物。|  hermaphroditic, bisexual | dioecious | adjective
