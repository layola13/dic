demandingnesses:/dɪˈmændɪŋnɪsɪz/
noun
苛求，高要求
The state or quality of being demanding; excessive requirements or expectations.
The demandingnesses of the job led to high staff turnover.  这份工作的高要求导致了员工的高流动率。
The demandingnesses of her parents made her childhood stressful. 她父母的苛刻要求使她的童年充满压力。
Synonym: exactingness, strictness, rigor
Antonym: leniency, laxity, permissiveness
词性:n.
