cajolery: /kəˈdʒoʊləri/ /kəˈdʒəʊləri/
noun
哄骗；劝诱；甜言蜜语
the use of pleasant words or promises to persuade someone to do something
例句：He used cajolery to get what he wanted. 他用甜言蜜语来达到目的。

例句：She wasn't swayed by his cajolery. 她没有被他劝诱动摇。

近义词：persuasion, coaxing, flattery, blandishment
反义词：coercion, force, intimidation
词性：n.
