bulblet:/ˈbʌlb.lət/ | n. | 小鳞茎；球芽；珠芽 |A small bulb or bulb-shaped growth, often produced in the axils of leaves or on flower stalks, that can develop into a new plant. |  The tiger lily forms bulblets along its stem.  虎皮百合的茎上会长出小鳞茎。

The gardener propagated the lilies using the bulblets. 园丁用球芽繁殖百合花。 |  近义词：bulb, offset, clove, bulbil | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

bulblet:/ˈbʌlb.lət/ | n. | 小灯泡 |A small light bulb. (Less common usage) | The miniature Christmas tree was decorated with tiny bulblets.  迷你圣诞树上装饰着小小的灯泡。

The dollhouse had tiny bulblets in the lamps. 娃娃屋的灯里有小灯泡。 | 近义词：light bulb, miniature bulb | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
