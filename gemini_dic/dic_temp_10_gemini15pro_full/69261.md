featherbrained:/ˈfeðərˌbreɪnd/ | adj. | 轻浮的；愚蠢的；缺心眼的 |  Lacking good sense or judgment; frivolous and silly.  It suggests a lack of seriousness and intelligence, as if one's head were filled with feathers instead of brains. |  She made a featherbrained decision to quit her job without having another one lined up. 她轻率地决定辞职，却没有找到另一份工作。 |  She's so featherbrained, she forgot her own birthday. 她太糊涂了，竟然连自己的生日都忘了。 |  silly, frivolous, foolish, dizzy, scatterbrained | sensible, wise, prudent, level-headed | adjective
