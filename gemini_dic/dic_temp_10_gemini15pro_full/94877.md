in-car:/ˈɪnˌkɑːr/ (US) /ˈɪnˌkɑː(r)/ (UK)
adjective
车内的；车载的
Existing or designed for use in a car.
The car has an in-car entertainment system. 这辆车有一个车载娱乐系统。
The in-car navigation system helped us find our way.  车载导航系统帮助我们找到了路。
近义词:automotive, onboard
反义词:external, outside
词性:adjective
