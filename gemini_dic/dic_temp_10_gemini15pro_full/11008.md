arboriculturists:/ˌɑːrbərɪˈkʌltʃərɪsts/
noun
树艺家；树木栽培家
A person who cultivates trees, especially for scientific or commercial purposes.
例句：The arboriculturists carefully pruned the branches of the old oak tree.  树艺家仔细地修剪了老橡树的枝条。

例句：The city employed a team of arboriculturists to manage the trees in the park.  市政府雇佣了一队树艺家来管理公园里的树木。

synonyms: tree surgeon, tree expert, horticulturist
antonyms: logger, lumberjack
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin arbor (tree) + cultura (cultivation) + -ist (one who practices).
