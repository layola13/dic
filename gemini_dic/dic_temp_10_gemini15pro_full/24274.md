brigadier:/brɪɡəˈdɪər/| n.|准将；陆军少将|An officer in the army ranking below major general and above colonel, commanding a brigade. |例句：The brigadier inspected the troops. 准将检阅了部队。

例句：He was promoted to the rank of brigadier. 他被提升为准将。

近义词：general, commander|反义词:private, soldier|词性:名词
