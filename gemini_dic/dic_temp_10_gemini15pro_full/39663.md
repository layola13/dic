conservator:/kənˈsɜːrvətər/ (US), /kənˈsɜːvətə(r)/ (UK)| n.|管理员，保护者，文物修复师|A person who protects, preserves, or restores something, especially works of art, buildings, or other cultural artifacts.|The conservator carefully restored the ancient painting.|近义词: curator, preserver, restorer, protector|反义词: destroyer, vandal|noun

conservator:/kənˈsɜːrvətər/ (US), /kənˈsɜːvətə(r)/ (UK)| n.|（法律）监护人|A person appointed by a court to manage the affairs of someone legally incapable of doing so.|The court appointed a conservator to oversee the finances of the elderly woman.|近义词: guardian, trustee|反义词: ward|noun
