agglutination:/əˌɡluːtɪˈneɪʃən/| n. |凝集，粘合|The process by which particles, such as red blood cells or bacteria, clump together.|The agglutination of the red blood cells indicated a positive reaction to the test.|近义词：clumping, adhesion, coalescence|反义词:separation, dispersion|noun
agglutinate:/əˈɡluːtɪneɪt/| v. |凝集，粘合|To cause particles to clump together.|Certain antibodies can agglutinate red blood cells.|近义词：clump, adhere, coalesce|反义词:separate, disperse|verb
