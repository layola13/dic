capercaillie:/ˌkæpərˈkeɪli/ | n. |西方松鸡| A large, black grouse, Tetrao urogallus, with a fan-shaped tail, found in coniferous forests of Europe and Asia. |  The capercaillie is the largest member of the grouse family. 西方松鸡是松鸡科中体型最大的成员。 |  Synonyms: wood grouse, heath cock | Antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a specific animal) | Noun

caperer:/ˈkeɪpərər/ | n. |雀跃的人; 跳跃的人|  A person who capers; someone who leaps or skips playfully. | The children were caperers in the park, full of energy and excitement. 孩子们在公园里雀跃，充满了活力和兴奋。 | Synonyms: frolicker, gamboler, reveller | Antonyms: dullard, stick-in-the-mud | Noun
