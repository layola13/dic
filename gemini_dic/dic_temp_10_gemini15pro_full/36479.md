coleads:/ˈkoʊˌliːdz/| v.|共同领导；联合领导|To lead jointly with another or others.|They colead the sales team and have achieved remarkable results. 他们共同领导销售团队，并取得了显著的成绩。|synonyms: co-manage, jointly lead|antonyms: follow, obey|verb
