fussify:/ˈfʌsɪfaɪ/| v. |过分讲究；小题大做；使烦恼；使烦躁|To behave or cause someone to behave in a fussy way, characterized by excessive attention to unnecessary details and a tendency to complain or become upset over minor issues.|She fussified over the arrangement of the flowers. 她对花的摆放过于讲究。|近义词：fret, fuss, niggle, worry, bother|反义词：calm, soothe, relax|词性:动词
