nonlegume:/nɒnˈlɛɡjuːm/ /nɑːnˈlɛɡjuːm/ | n. |非豆科植物|A plant that is not a legume (member of the pea family). Nonlegumes typically require nitrogen fertilizer for optimal growth, as they don't fix nitrogen from the atmosphere like legumes do. |Example: Corn is a nonlegume and needs nitrogen supplements. 玉米是非豆科植物，需要补充氮肥。

Example:  Farmers often rotate nonlegumes with legumes to improve soil health. 农民经常将非豆科植物和豆科植物轮作以改善土壤健康。|近义词：nonleguminous plant|反义词:legume|词性:noun
