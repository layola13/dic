endowers:/ɪnˈdaʊərz/| n.| 捐赠者；赋予者 |  People who endow someone or something with a particular quality, ability, or asset. | The endowers of the university made significant contributions to its research programs. 大学的捐赠者为其研究项目做出了重大贡献。 | 近义词：donors, benefactors, patrons | 反义词：recipients, beneficiaries | 词性: Noun (plural)

endows:/ɪnˈdaʊz/| vt.| 捐赠；赋予 | To provide with a quality, ability, or asset. |  Nature endows some animals with camouflage. 大自然赋予一些动物伪装的能力。| 近义词：bestow, grant, furnish, equip | 反义词：deprive, divest | 词性: Verb (third-person singular simple present)
