hypergrammatically:/ˌhaɪpərɡrəˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 过于语法化地；过分注重语法地 | Excessively concerned with the rules of grammar; in a way that focuses too much on grammar. | He spoke hypergrammatically, correcting every minor error in his colleagues' speech. 他说话过于语法化，纠正了他同事的每一个小错误。|  She analyzed the sentence hypergrammatically, dissecting every clause and phrase. 她对这个句子进行了过于语法化的分析，仔细剖析了每个从句和短语。 | 近义词: pedantically, scrupulously | 反义词: ungrammatically, loosely | 词性: 副词
