medicament:/ˈmedɪkəm(ə)nt/| n. |药，药物|A substance used for medical treatment. |例句：The doctor prescribed a medicament for her cough. 医生给她开了一种治咳嗽的药。

例句：The ancient medicament was surprisingly effective.  这种古老的药物竟然出奇地有效。|近义词：medicine, drug, remedy, medication|反义词: poison (Although not a direct opposite, poison represents the harmful counterpart to a medicament's intended healing.)|noun
