ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *alterpece*, from Old French *autel piece* ("altar piece"), equivalent to *autel* ("altar") + *piece* ("piece"). *Autel* from Latin *altare*, from *altus* ("high"); *piece* from Late Latin *petium* ("piece, portion").
