nonbibulousness:/ˌnɒnbɪˈbjʊləsnəs/
n.
戒酒; 不饮酒
the quality or state of being nonbibulous; abstinence from alcoholic drink.
His nonbibulousness was surprising, considering his family history.  他滴酒不沾令人惊讶，考虑到他的家族史。
He maintained his nonbibulousness throughout his life. 他一生都保持着滴酒不沾的习惯。
近义词:abstinence, sobriety, temperance
反义词:intemperance, drunkenness
词性:名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + bibulous (adjective meaning "given to or marked by the consumption of alcoholic beverages") + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality)

The word "bibulous" comes from the Latin "bibulus," meaning "drinking," from "bibere," meaning "to drink."
