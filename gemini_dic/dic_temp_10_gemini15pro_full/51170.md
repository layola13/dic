despume:/dɪˈspjuːm/ /dɪˈspuːm/
verb
去除泡沫；撇去浮沫
To remove scum or foam from the surface of a liquid.
The winemaker despumed the fermenting must.  酿酒师撇去了发酵葡萄汁上的浮沫。
The chef carefully despumed the stock for a clear consommé.  厨师小心地撇去高汤上的浮沫，以便制作清澈的清汤。
近义词:skim, clarify, purify
反义词:foam, froth
词性:verb

词源:
From Latin despūmāre, from de- + spūmāre (“to foam”), from spūma (“foam”).
