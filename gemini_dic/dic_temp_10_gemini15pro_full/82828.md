gulags:/ˈɡuːlɑːɡz/
n.
古拉格，(前苏联的)劳改营
A system of forced labor camps in the former Soviet Union.
Many political prisoners were sent to the gulags. 许多政治犯被送往古拉格。
concentration camps, labor camps
