miasmal:/maɪˈæzməl/ | adj. | 瘴气的；污浊的；令人不快的 | Relating to or resembling miasma (a noxious or poisonous atmosphere). | The miasmal swamp was a breeding ground for mosquitoes. 瘴气弥漫的沼泽是蚊子的滋生地。 |  Synonyms: noxious, pestilential, fetid, foul, unhealthy | Antonyms: pure, clean, fresh, healthy | Adjective

miasma:/maɪˈæzmə/ | n. | 瘴气；臭气；不良影响 |  A highly unpleasant or unhealthy smell or vapor. Also used figuratively to describe a general feeling of gloom or unhealthy influence. |  A miasma of despair hung over the town. 一股绝望的氛围笼罩着整个城镇。 | Synonyms: stench, stink, reek, fetor, odor | Antonyms: fragrance, perfume, aroma | Noun
