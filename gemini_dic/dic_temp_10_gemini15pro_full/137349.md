nontaxer:/ˈnɒnˌtæksər/ (US) /ˈnɒnˌtæksə(r)/ (UK)| n.|非纳税人；免税者|A person or entity that is exempt from paying taxes or does not pay taxes.|Example:  The nontaxers benefit from public services without contributing financially.  非纳税人享受公共服务而不缴纳任何费用。

Example: Some argue that nontaxers are unfairly burdening those who do pay.  有些人认为非纳税人给纳税人带来了不公平的负担。|近义词: tax-exempt individual,  tax dodger (in some contexts, though this implies illegality) |反义词: taxpayer|词性: noun
