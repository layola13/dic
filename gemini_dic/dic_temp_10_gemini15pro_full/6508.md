amused: /əˈmjuːzd/ | adj. | 愉快的，好笑的 | Feeling or showing amusement; entertained. |  She was amused by the clown's antics. 她被小丑的滑稽动作逗乐了。

The children were amused by the playful puppies. 孩子们被顽皮的小狗逗乐了。


近义词: entertained, delighted, cheerful, jolly
反义词: bored, annoyed, displeased, irritated
词性: adjective

词源: From Old French *amuser (verb), from a- (from Latin ad ‘to’) + muser ‘to dream, to muse’, of uncertain origin.
