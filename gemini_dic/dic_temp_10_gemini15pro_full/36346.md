coiling:/ˈkɔɪlɪŋ/ | n. |盘绕，卷绕，线圈|The act of winding something into a coil. |The coiling of the snake around the branch was mesmerizing. 蛇盘绕在树枝上的样子令人着迷。|近义词：winding, spiraling, twisting|反义词:uncoiling, straightening|noun

coiling:/ˈkɔɪlɪŋ/ | adj. |盘绕的，卷曲的|Having the shape or form of a coil. |The coiling wire was difficult to untangle. 盘绕的电线很难解开。|近义词：spiral, twisted, winding|反义词:straight, untwisted|adjective
