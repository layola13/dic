nonactivist:/ˌnɒnˈæktɪvɪst/
adjective
非积极的；不积极参与的
Not taking part in activities, especially political or social protests.
The nonactivist investors were content to simply collect dividends. 那些不积极参与的投资者满足于仅仅收取股息。
Synonyms: passive, inactive, uninvolved
Antonyms: activist, active, involved
词性: 形容词
