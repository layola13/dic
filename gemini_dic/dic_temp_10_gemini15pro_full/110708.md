linebacker:/ˈlaɪnbækər/ | n. | 中后卫，线卫 | A defensive position in American football, typically positioned behind the defensive linemen and in front of the defensive backs.  They are often involved in tackling ball carriers, defending against passes, and blitzing the quarterback. | The linebacker made a crucial interception.  中后卫完成了一次关键的拦截。|  The linebacker sacked the quarterback for a big loss.  线卫擒杀了四分卫，使其损失惨重。| synonyms: defender, tackler | antonyms: quarterback, receiver | noun
