abators:/əˈbeɪtərz/| n.|减轻者，消除者， abatement的复数|Persons or things that abate or lessen something.  Those who put an end to or suppress something.|The city hired abators to deal with the noise pollution. 城市雇佣了消除者来处理噪音污染。

The abators worked tirelessly to reduce the lead levels in the old house.  消除者不知疲倦地工作以降低老房子中的铅含量。|近义词：reducers, mitigators, suppressors, diminishers|反义词: aggravators, intensifiers|词性:Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *abatour, from Anglo-Norman abatre, abater (“to beat down, abate”), from Old French abatre, from a- (“down, away”) + batre (“to beat”).  The suffix -or comes from Anglo-Norman -our, from Old French -eor, -eur, from Latin -or.
