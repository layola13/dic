boiler:/ˈbɔɪlər/ (US), /ˈbɔɪlə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 锅炉；烧水壶；热水器|A closed metal container in which water is heated to provide steam or hot water for central heating or other purposes.|The boiler broke down in the middle of winter.  冬天正中央锅炉坏了。|Synonyms: furnace, heater, kettle (for smaller ones)|Antonyms: cooler, refrigerator|noun
