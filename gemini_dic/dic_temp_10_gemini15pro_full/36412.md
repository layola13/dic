coke:/koʊk/ (US) /kəʊk/ (UK)| n.|可乐，焦炭|A carbonated soft drink flavored with coca extract and kola nut. Originally containing cocaine, it is now typically caffeine-based.|I'd like a coke, please. 我想要一杯可乐。|He put more coke on the fire. 他在火上加了更多焦炭。|近义词：(for the drink) soda, pop, soft drink (for the fuel) coal, charcoal|反义词: (no common opposite)|词性:noun
