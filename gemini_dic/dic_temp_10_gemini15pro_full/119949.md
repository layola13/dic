millable:/ˈmɪləbəl/| adj.|可碾磨的，可 milling 的|Suitable for milling or grinding.  Capable of being shaped or formed by a milling machine.|This type of grain is highly millable and produces excellent flour. 这种谷物非常适合碾磨，可以生产出优质的面粉。|grindable, workable, machinable|unmillable, non-millable|adjective
Etymology->
From mill + -able.  "Mill" comes from Old English *mylen, from Latin mola "millstone," related to molere "to grind."  "-able" is a suffix of Latin origin meaning "capable of being."
