cutable:/ˈkʌtəbl/| adj.|可切割的，可裁剪的|Something that is cutable can be cut.  This fabric is very cutable.  这种布料很容易裁剪。|例句：The cake was dense but cutable.  蛋糕很厚实，但是可以切。

例句：The meat was tender and cutable. 这肉很嫩，可以切。|近义词：severable, divisible, scissorable|反义词: uncuttable, indivisible|词性:形容词
