brutelike:/ˈbruːtlaɪk/| adj.|像野兽的，残忍的|Resembling or characteristic of a brute; savage, cruel, or insensitive.|He displayed brutelike strength in lifting the heavy weight. 他举起重物时展现出了野兽般的力量。| The dictator ruled with brutelike force.  独裁者用野蛮的力量进行统治。 |近义词：brutal, savage, ferocious, barbaric, cruel, inhuman, ruthless|反义词：gentle, kind, compassionate, humane, civilized, refined|词性:形容词
词源: brute (野兽) + like (像) -  From the noun "brute," meaning a beast or animal, combined with the suffix "-like" to form an adjective indicating resemblance.
