environmentalism: /ɪnˌvaɪrənˈmentəlɪzəm/ | n. | 环保主义，环境保护论 | A concern about and action aimed at protecting the environment. | Example 1: Environmentalism is gaining momentum as people become more aware of climate change.  环保主义随着人们对气候变化的认识越来越深入而 gaining momentum。
Example 2:  She dedicated her life to environmentalism, fighting for stricter pollution controls. 她毕生致力于环保事业，争取更严格的污染控制。 | 近义词: conservationism, ecologism | 反义词: anti-environmentalism | 词性: noun


词源: environment + -al + -ism

environment: From Old French environner "to surround," from en- "in" + viron "circle, circuit."

-al: suffix forming adjectives from nouns, meaning "relating to, characterized by."

-ism:  suffix forming nouns meaning "doctrine, belief, practice."
