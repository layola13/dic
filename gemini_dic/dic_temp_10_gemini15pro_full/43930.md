crewman:/ˈkruːmən/ | n. |船员，机组人员|A member of a ship's company or aircraft's crew. |Example 1: The crewman secured the ropes before the storm.  船员在暴风雨来临前固定好了绳索。

Example 2:  A crewman on the flight helped passengers with their luggage.  飞机上的一名机组人员帮助乘客拿行李。|Synonym: sailor, seaman, airman |Antonym: passenger, landsman |noun
