flyspeck:/ˈflaɪˌspɛk/| n.|小污点，小黑点；微不足道的事物|A tiny speck of fly excrement; something insignificant or trivial.|The contract was full of legal flyspecks.  合同里充满了法律上的细枝末节。|Synonyms: speck, dot, mote, trifle|Antonyms:  mountain, something significant|noun


flyspeck:/ˈflaɪˌspɛk/| vt.|挑剔；吹毛求疵|To criticize or find fault with minutely and pettily.|He flyspecked the report, looking for any minor errors. 他仔细检查了报告，寻找任何细微的错误。|Synonyms: nitpick, cavil, quibble|Antonyms:  praise, commend|verb
