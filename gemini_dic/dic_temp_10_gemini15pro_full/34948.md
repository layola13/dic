clocker:/ˈklɒkər/ (UK) /ˈklɑːkər/ (US)| n.|计时员，测速员，（尤指赛马的）速度测定员|A person who times something, especially the speed of racehorses.|The clocker recorded a fast time for the horse's final furlong. 计时员记录了这匹马最后一弗隆的最快时间。|Synonyms: timer, timekeeper|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
