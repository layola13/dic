blitzer:/ˈblɪtsər/| n.|超速驾驶者，测速雷达|A person who drives at a speed considerably faster than the legal limit, often caught by speed traps.  Someone who studies or works intensely, especially to finish something quickly.  A device used to catch speeding drivers, such as a radar gun or speed camera.|The police blitzer caught him going 90 in a 50 zone. 警察的测速雷达测到他在限速50的区域开到了90。 He's such a blitzer; he finished the project in just two days. 他真是个快手，两天就把项目完成了.  The police set up a blitzer on the highway. 警察在高速公路上设置了测速装置。|近义词：speeder, speed demon, road hog (for speeding drivers), workaholic, grinder (for someone who works intensely) |反义词:slowpoke, laggard (for speeding drivers), slacker (for someone who works intensely)|Noun
