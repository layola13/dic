heyh: /heɪh/ | int. | 嘿，你好 | An informal greeting, similar to "hey" or "hi". It's often used online or in text messages. | Heyh, what's up? 嘿，怎么了？ | 近义词: hi, hey, hello | 反义词: goodbye, farewell | 词性: interjection

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃什么？
What does she like to do in her free time? = 她空闲时间喜欢做什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在看什么电视节目？
What is he cooking for dinner tonight? = 他今晚晚餐做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned recently? = 你最近学了什么？
What has she done to help? = 她做了些什么来帮忙？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading this week? = 他这周一直在读什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
