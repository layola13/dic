flintwood:/ˈflɪntˌwʊd/ | n. |燧石木，一种硬木|A very hard wood, typically used for tools or fires in prehistoric times because it can create sparks when struck against flint. | Early humans used flintwood to create fire. 早期人类使用燧石木生火。| The flintwood spear was surprisingly effective. 燧石木矛出奇地有效。 | synonyms: hardwood, ironwood | antonyms: softwood | noun
