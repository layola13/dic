muzzler:/ˈmʌzlər/| n. |口罩；口套；枪口；钳制言论之物|A device, typically made of straps or wires, placed over an animal's snout to prevent it from biting or barking.|The dog wore a muzzler to prevent it from biting strangers.|近义词：gag, silencer, restraint|反义词:microphone, amplifier|词性:名词

muzzle:/ˈmʌzl/| n. |动物的口鼻部；枪口；钳制言论之物|The projecting part of the face, including the nose and mouth, of an animal such as a dog or horse. Also, the open end of the barrel of a gun.|The dog licked its muzzle.|近义词：snout, nose, mouth|反义词:|词性:名词

muzzle:/ˈmʌzl/| vt. |给…戴口套；钳制…的言论|To put a muzzle on (an animal). Also, to prevent (someone) from expressing their opinions freely.|The owner muzzled his aggressive dog.|近义词：gag, silence, censor, restrain|反义词:liberate, free|词性:动词
