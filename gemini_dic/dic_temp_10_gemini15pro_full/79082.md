glaceed:/ɡleɪˈsid/ /ɡlɑːˈseɪd/| adj.|糖衣的，光滑的，冷漠的|Covered with a smooth, glossy surface, as if with icing; having a smooth, polished, or glassy surface; having a cold or unfriendly manner.|例句：The glaceed cherries on top of the cake looked delicious. 蛋糕上糖衣的樱桃看起来很美味。|例句：Her glaceed expression gave no hint of her true feelings. 她冷漠的表情没有流露出她的真实感受。|近义词：icy, frosty, glazed, polished, smooth|反义词：warm, friendly, rough|词性:形容词

词源: From French *glacé*, past participle of *glacer* "to freeze, ice, glaze," from *glace* "ice," from Vulgar Latin *glacia*, from Latin *glacies*.
