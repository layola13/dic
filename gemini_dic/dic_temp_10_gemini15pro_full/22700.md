bored: /bɔːrd/ (UK) /bɔːrd/ (US) | adj. | 无聊的，厌烦的 | Feeling weary because one is unoccupied or lacks interest in one's current activity. | I'm so bored with this movie.  这部电影让我觉得很无聊。| synonyms: uninterested, weary, tired, listless | antonyms: interested, enthusiastic, excited, stimulated | adjective
