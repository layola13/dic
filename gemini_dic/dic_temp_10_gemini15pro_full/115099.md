magnesium:/mæɡˈniːziəm/ | n. |镁|A light, silvery-white, metallic element, the lightest of the structural metals. It burns with a brilliant white light and is used in alloys, flares, and fireworks.|Magnesium is essential for plant growth. 镁对植物生长至关重要。|Synonyms: Mg (symbol), metal|Antonyms: nonmetal|noun
