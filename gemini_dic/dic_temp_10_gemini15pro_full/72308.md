flourished:/ˈflɜːrɪʃt/ |v. | 繁荣，兴旺；茂盛；挥舞；炫耀 | To grow or develop successfully; to thrive.  |  The arts flourished during the Renaissance. 文艺复兴时期艺术蓬勃发展。 | synonyms: thrive, prosper, boom | antonyms: decline, wither, fail | verb

flourish:/ˈflɜːrɪʃ/ | n. | 繁荣；茂盛；兴旺；炫耀；华丽的辞藻 | A bold or extravagant gesture or action, made especially to attract attention. | He finished his speech with a flourish. 他以一个华丽的姿态结束了演讲。| synonyms: embellishment, ornamentation, fanfare | antonyms: understatement, simplicity | noun
