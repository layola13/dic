expeditionary:/ˌɛkspɪˈdɪʃənəri/
adjective
远征的；探险的
Relating to or forming part of an expedition.  Characterized by exploration, discovery, or adventure.
例句：The expeditionary force landed on the island. 远征军登陆了该岛。

例句：The museum displayed artifacts from several expeditionary voyages.  博物馆展出了几次远征航行的文物。

近义词：exploring, pioneering, adventurous
反义词：domestic, settled, stationary
词性: adjective

expeditionary:/ˌɛkspɪˈdɪʃənəri/
noun
远征军士兵
A member of an expeditionary force.
例句：The expeditionaries faced many challenges in the uncharted territory.  远征军士兵们在未知的领域面临着许多挑战。

例句：The expeditionaries were well-equipped for their journey. 远征军士兵们为他们的旅程做好了充分的准备。

近义词：explorer, adventurer, pioneer
反义词：resident, native, local
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *expeditionarius, from Latin expeditio (“a setting free, release; preparation, equipment; military expedition”), from expedire (“to extricate, disengage, set free; prepare, make ready”), from ex- (“out”) + pes (“foot”) + -ire (verbal suffix).
