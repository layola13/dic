packwax:/ˈpækwæks/
n.
包装蜡，封蜡
A wax used for sealing packages.
The shopkeeper used packwax to seal the parcel.  店主用封蜡密封包裹。
近义词：sealing wax
反义词: N/A
词性: 名词

Etymology:
"pack" (verb) from Middle Dutch *pakken, frequentative of *pakken "to seize, pack," from Proto-Germanic *pakkon.
"wax" (noun) from Old English weax, from Proto-Germanic *wakhs-, from Proto-Indo-European *(H)wēk-s- "wax."
