monom:/ˈmɒnəʊm/ /ˈmɑːnəʊm/ | n. |单项式|A single term in mathematics, consisting of a product of numbers and variables raised to non-negative integer powers. |Example: 3x^2y is a monomial.  3x^2y是一个单项式。


Example:  The expression 5ab is a monomial. 表达式5ab是一个单项式。

|近义词: term |反义词: polynomial |词性: noun
