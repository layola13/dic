overattachments:/ˌōvərəˈtætʃmənts/ | n. | 过度依恋，过度依赖 | An excessive or unhealthy emotional dependence on someone or something. |  She struggled with overattachments to her romantic partners. 她对她的恋爱伴侣过度依赖。

He developed an overattachment to his phone. 他对他的手机过度依赖。


| 近义词：dependence, clinginess, neediness | 反义词：independence, detachment | 词性: 名词

overattachment:/ˌōvərəˈtætʃmənt/ | n. | 过度依恋，过度依赖 |  The state of being excessively attached; an excessive emotional dependence on someone or something. |  His overattachment to his mother was a source of concern. 他对他母亲的过度依恋令人担忧。

Her overattachment to material possessions made it difficult for her to downsize. 她对物质财富的过度依赖让她难以精简生活。
