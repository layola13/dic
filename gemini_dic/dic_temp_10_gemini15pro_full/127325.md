nayward:/ˈneɪwərd/| adj.| 顽固的，执拗的；不听话的；难驾驭的；倔强的 | stubbornly refusing to go the desired direction; disobedient; difficult to control; headstrong. | The nayward mule refused to budge. 那头倔强的骡子拒绝移动。| The nayward child threw a tantrum in the store.  那个任性的孩子在商店里大发脾气。| 近义词: stubborn, obstinate, recalcitrant, refractory, unruly, wayward | 反义词: obedient, compliant, docile, tractable | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From "nay" (no) + "-ward" (toward), meaning "turned toward no," or "refusing to go forward."  源自 "nay" (不) + "-ward" (朝向)，意思是“转向不”，或“拒绝前进”。
