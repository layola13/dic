inter-pollinated:/ˌɪntərˈpɒləneɪtɪd/ |v. |异花授粉的| pollinated by pollen from a different plant; cross-pollinated | The apple trees were inter-pollinated with a nearby variety. 这些苹果树与附近的一个品种异花授粉了。| The farmer inter-pollinated the two types of corn to create a hybrid.  这位农民将两种玉米异花授粉以创造杂交品种。|Synonyms: cross-pollinated, crossbred, hybridized | Antonyms: self-pollinated, inbred| verb
