eventlessly: /ɪˈvɛntləsli/
adjective
平淡无奇地；平静地；无事地
Happening without any significant or memorable events.
The days passed eventlessly.  日子平淡无奇地过去了。
The meeting concluded eventlessly. 会议平静地结束了。
Synonyms: uneventfully, monotonously, dully, plainly
Antonyms: eventfully, excitingly, dramatically
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From event + -less + -ly.  Event comes from Old French event, from Latin eventus "outcome, occurrence," past participle of evenire "to come out, happen, result," from ex- "out" + venire "to come."  The suffix -less means "without," and -ly forms adverbs from adjectives.
