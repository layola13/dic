helmeted:/ˈhɛlmɪtɪd/| adj.|戴着头盔的，装有头盔的|Wearing or provided with a helmet.  A helmeted policeman directed traffic. |例句：The helmeted soldier stood guard. 戴着头盔的士兵站岗。

The cyclists were all helmeted.  骑自行车的人全都戴着头盔。|近义词：protected, armored|反义词:bareheaded, unprotected|词性:adjective
词源: From helmet + -ed.  Ultimately from the Old French word "helme."
