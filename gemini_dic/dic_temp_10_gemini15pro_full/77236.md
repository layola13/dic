gastroenteritis:/ˌɡæstroʊˌɛntəˈraɪtɪs/
n.|肠胃炎|Inflammation of the stomach and intestines, typically resulting in vomiting and diarrhea.|She was diagnosed with gastroenteritis after experiencing severe stomach cramps. 她经历了严重的胃痉挛后被诊断出患有肠胃炎。|Synonyms: stomach flu, belly flu|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider "digestive health")|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From gastro- (combining form meaning "stomach") + entero- (combining form meaning "intestine") + -itis (suffix meaning "inflammation").
