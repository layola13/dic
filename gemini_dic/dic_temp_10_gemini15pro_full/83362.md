gyro:/ˈdʒaɪroʊ/ (US) /ˈdʒaɪərəʊ/ (UK)| n. | 陀螺仪；旋转烤肉；希腊卷饼 | A device consisting of a wheel or disk mounted so that it can spin rapidly about an axis that is itself free to alter in direction. Also, a Greek sandwich made with pita bread, filled with roasted meat, vegetables, and tzatziki sauce. |  例句：The gyro in the airplane helps maintain stability. 飞机上的陀螺仪有助于保持稳定。

例句：I ordered a gyro for lunch. 我午餐点了一个希腊卷饼。| 近义词：kebab, shawarma (for the food), gyroscope (for the device) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


词源: From Greek *γῦρος (gûros), from γυρός (gurós, “round, curved, ring”).
