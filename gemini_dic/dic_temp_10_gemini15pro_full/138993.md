nymphical: /ˈnɪmfɪk(ə)l/
adjective
仙女般的；若虫的
Resembling or characteristic of a nymph; relating to or characteristic of the immature stage of certain insects.
The dragonfly displayed its nymphical form before its final metamorphosis.  蜻蜓在最后一次蜕变之前展现了它的若虫形态。
The nymphical stage of a cicada can last for several years.  蝉的若虫阶段可以持续数年。
Synonyms: nymphean, nympholeptic, nymph-like
Antonyms: adult, mature
