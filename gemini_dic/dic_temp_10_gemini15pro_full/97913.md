innocence:/ˈɪnəsəns/| n.|清白，无辜，天真|The state of being innocent of a crime or wrongdoing; lack of experience and guile; purity.|She maintained her innocence throughout the trial. 她始终坚称自己在整个审判过程中是无辜的。|Synonyms: purity, guiltlessness, blamelessness, chastity, naivete|Antonyms: guilt, culpability, wickedness, experience, sophistication|noun
