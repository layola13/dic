overcomingly:/ˌoʊvərˈkʌmɪŋli/
| adv. |压倒性地；不可抗拒地；|in a way that overwhelms or overpowers|Her beauty was overcomingly apparent. 她的美貌令人难以抗拒。|近义词：overpoweringly, irresistibly, overwhelmingly|反义词：weakly, mildly|词性:副词
词源: From overcome + -ing + -ly.  "Overcome" itself comes from Old English *ofercuman "to conquer, surpass," from ofer- "over" + cuman "to come."
