boodies:/ˈbuːdiz/| n. |朋友，伙伴，密友（通常指女性）| Close friends, especially female friends. This is an informal and often affectionate term. |例句：She went shopping with her boodies. 她和她的闺蜜们去购物了。

例句：My boodies and I are planning a girls' trip. 我和我的姐妹们正在计划一次女生旅行。|近义词：friends, pals, mates, besties|反义词:enemies, foes|词性:noun

词源:Likely a variation of "buddies," which itself is a shortened and informal form of "brothers." Over time, "boodies" has taken on a predominantly feminine connotation, though it can sometimes be used in a gender-neutral way among close friends.
