headstanding:/ˈhɛdˌstændɪŋ/| n. |倒立|The act of supporting the body in an inverted vertical position by balancing on the head and hands.|She held a headstanding for a full minute. 她倒立了整整一分钟。|近义词: handstand|反义词: standing upright|词性:名词

headstanding:/ˈhɛdˌstændɪŋ/| adj. |倒立的|Relating to or involving a headstand.|The headstanding yogi amazed the crowd.  倒立的瑜伽士让观众惊叹。|近义词: inverted|反义词: upright|词性:形容词
