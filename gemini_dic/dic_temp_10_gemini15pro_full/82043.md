grisly:/ˈɡrɪzli/| adj.|可怕的，令人毛骨悚然的，恐怖的|Causing horror or disgust; gruesome.|The police reported the grisly details of the murder. 警方报告了这起谋杀案的可怕细节。|Synonyms: gruesome, ghastly, macabre, horrific, appalling|Antonyms: pleasant, delightful, agreeable|adjective

grisly:/ˈɡrɪzli/| adj.|灰色的，灰白的 (古语，罕用)|Gray or grizzly.|The old man had a grisly beard. 老人有一把灰白的胡子。 (This usage is archaic and rarely seen.)|Synonyms: gray, grizzled|Antonyms: colorful, vibrant|adjective
