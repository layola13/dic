what:/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US) | pron. det. adv. int.|什么，多么|Used to ask for information about something.|What is your name? 你叫什么名字？|近义词：which|反义词: (无直接反义词)|pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? = 你怎么想？
What does she want? = 她想要什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing here? = 他在这里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned? = 你学到了什么？
What has she seen? = 她看到了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on? = 你一直在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you eat for dinner? = 你晚饭吃了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about yesterday? = 她昨天在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had she eaten before the movie? = 在看电影之前她吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the storm? = 暴风雨来临之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about? = 他们一直在争论什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? = 你下周末要做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be talking about? = 他们会谈论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by tomorrow? = 到明天你会完成什么？
What will they have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底他们会取得什么成就？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been studying for five years? = 你将已经学习五年什么了？
What will she have been working on by then? = 到那时她将已经做什么了？


虚拟语气:
If I knew what he wanted, I would tell you. = 如果我知道他想要什么，我会告诉你。
I wish I knew what to say. = 我希望我知道该说什么。

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 这个问题正在被如何处理？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是哪里？
What are your plans for the summer? = 你暑假的计划是什么？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
He doesn't understand what she means. = 他不明白她的意思。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What the hell! = 搞什么鬼！
What if we go to the beach? = 如果我们去海滩怎么样？
What for? = 为了什么？
So what? = 那又怎样？
