overdescriptive:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈskrɪptɪv/| adj.|过度描述的，过于详细的|Characterized by excessive or unnecessary description; providing more detail than is necessary or helpful.|The novel was overdescriptive, making it tedious to read. 这部小说描写过于详细，读起来很乏味。|Synonyms: prolix, verbose, long-winded|Antonyms: concise, succinct, terse|adjective
