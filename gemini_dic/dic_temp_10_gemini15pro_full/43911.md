crevassed: /krɪˈvæst/ | adj. | 布满裂缝的；有裂隙的 | Covered with crevasses (deep open cracks, especially in a glacier). | The climbers carefully navigated the crevassed glacier.  登山者小心翼翼地穿过布满裂缝的冰川。 | The crevassed terrain was treacherous.  裂缝遍布的地形非常危险。 |  近义词：fissured, cracked, fractured | 反义词：smooth, unblemished | 词性: adjective

crevasse: /krɪˈvæs/ | n. | 裂缝；裂隙 | A deep open crack, especially one in a glacier. | The crevasse was hidden under a thin layer of snow.  裂缝隐藏在一层薄薄的雪下。 |  The mountaineers had to rope themselves together to avoid falling into crevasses. 登山者必须用绳子把自己绑在一起，以免掉进裂缝里。| 近义词：fissure, crack, chasm, rift | 反义词：closure, seal | 词性: noun
