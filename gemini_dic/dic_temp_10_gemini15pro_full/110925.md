liposome: /ˈlaɪpəˌsoʊm/ (US), /ˈlɪpəˌsəʊm/ (UK) | n. | 脂质体 | A spherical vesicle having at least one lipid bilayer.  Liposomes can be used as drug delivery vehicles. | Liposomes are used in cosmetics to deliver active ingredients to the skin. 脂质体用于化妆品中，将活性成分输送到皮肤。 |  Liposomal doxorubicin is a chemotherapy drug encapsulated in a liposome. 脂质体阿霉素是一种包裹在脂质体中的化疗药物。 | synonyms: vesicle, nanoparticle | antonyms: N/A | noun
