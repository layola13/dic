huffingly:/ˈhʌfɪŋli/| adv.|气呼呼地，气鼓鼓地|In a way that shows annoyance or impatience by breathing heavily.|She turned and walked away huffingly. 她气呼呼地转身走开了。|例句：He huffingly stormed out of the room. 他气呼呼地冲出了房间。|近义词：angrily, indignantly, sullenly, resentfully|反义词：cheerfully, happily, pleasantly|词性：副词

词源: From huff (v.) + -ing + -ly.  "Huff" meaning a fit of anger or resentment dates back to the 15th century.
