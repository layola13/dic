lonesome:/ˈloʊnsəm/ (US), /ˈləʊnsəm/ (UK)| adj.|孤独的，寂寞的| Experiencing or causing a feeling of sadness because one is alone; solitary.|例句：The lonesome whistle of a train echoed through the valley. 火车的孤独汽笛声回荡在山谷中。

例句：She felt lonesome and isolated in the big city. 在大城市里，她感到孤独和孤立。

近义词：lonely, solitary, alone, isolated, desolate|反义词：accompanied, sociable, gregarious|词性:形容词
