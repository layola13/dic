parasiticalness:/ˌpærəˈsɪtɪkəlnəs/ | n. | 寄生性；依赖性 | The state or quality of being parasitical; dependence. |  The parasiticalness of some plants can harm the host organism.  某些植物的寄生性会损害宿主生物。 | synonyms: dependence, parasitism, leeching, freeloading | antonyms: independence, self-sufficiency, autonomy | noun

parasitical:/ˌpærəˈsɪtɪkl/ | adj. |寄生的；依赖的|Of or relating to a parasite; living as a parasite.  |Mistletoe is a parasitical plant. 槲寄生是一种寄生植物。| synonyms: parasitic, dependent, leeching, freeloading | antonyms: independent, self-sufficient, autonomous | adjective
