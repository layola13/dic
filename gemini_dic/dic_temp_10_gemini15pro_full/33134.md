christocentric:/ˌkraɪstoʊˈsɛntrɪk/ | adj. | 以基督为中心的；基督中心论的 | Having Christ as the central focus.  Relating to a theological viewpoint that emphasizes the centrality of Christ in the understanding of Christianity and the world. |  His theology is deeply christocentric. 他的神学是以基督为中心的。|  Christocentric interpretations of the Old Testament.  以基督为中心的旧约圣经解释。| 近义词: Christ-centered | 反义词: anthropocentric | 词性: 形容词
