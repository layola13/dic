homilies:/ˈhɒməliz/ (UK), /ˈhɑːməliz/ (US)| n.|布道；说教；冗长乏味的道德训诫|A religious discourse that is intended primarily for spiritual edification rather than doctrinal instruction; a tedious moralizing discourse.|The priest's homilies were often lengthy and focused on the importance of charity.|Synonyms: sermon, lecture, discourse, preachment|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "diatribe" for a harshly critical speech or "entertainment" for its opposite function)|Noun
