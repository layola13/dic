azymite: /ˈæzɪˌmaɪt/ | n. | 无酵饼, 无酵母面包 | Unleavened bread, especially that eaten at Passover.  A person who eats unleavened bread. |  The azymites were prepared for Passover.  无酵饼已经为逾越节准备好了。| The family only ate azymites during Passover. 这家人在逾越节期间只吃无酵饼。 | synonyms: unleavened bread, matzah, matzo | antonyms: leavened bread | noun
