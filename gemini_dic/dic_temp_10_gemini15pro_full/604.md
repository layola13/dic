abradable:/əˈbreɪdəbl/
adjective
可磨损的；耐磨的
Capable of being abraded or worn away by friction or erosion.  Designed to wear away under contact with another part, often to maintain a tight seal.
The turbine blades are coated with an abradable material to prevent damage to the casing.  涡轮叶片涂有可磨损材料，以防止外壳损坏。

The abradable lining helps to improve engine efficiency. 可磨损衬里有助于提高发动机效率。


近义词: erodible, wearable
反义词: non-abradable, resistant
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From abrade + -able.  "Abrade" comes from Latin abradere, from ab- "away" + radere "to scrape".

USAGE->
The abradable coating protected the engine from damage. 可磨损涂层保护发动机免受损坏。

Abradable seals are commonly used in gas turbines. 可磨损密封件常用于燃气轮机。

PHRASE->
abradable coating = 可磨损涂层
abradable material = 可磨损材料
abradable seal = 可磨损密封
highly abradable = 高度可磨损的
abradable lining = 可磨损衬里
