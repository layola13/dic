fuggier:/ˈfʌɡiər/
adjective
更闷热的，更潮湿的，更憋闷的
More stuffy, more humid, more sultry.  More unclear, more vague, more confused.
It's much fuggier in here than it is outside. 这里面比外面闷热多了。
The details of the plan are still fuggier than I'd like.  这个计划的细节仍然比我想要的更模糊。
近义词：stuffier, muggier, sultrier, hazier, vaguer
反义词：cooler, fresher, clearer, more distinct
