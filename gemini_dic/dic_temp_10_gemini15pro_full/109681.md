leucoryx:/ˌluːkɔːˈrɪks/ | n. | 白羚羊 | A large, heavily built antelope with long, straight, pointed horns, native to the Arabian Peninsula and the dry regions of northeast Africa. |  The leucoryx is an endangered species. 白羚羊是一种濒危物种。 |  The zoo has a successful breeding program for leucoryx. 这家动物园有一个成功的白羚羊繁殖计划。 |  synonyms: Arabian oryx, white oryx | antonyms: N/A | noun
