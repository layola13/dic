emote:/ɪˈmoʊt/ (US), /ɪˈməʊt/ (UK)| v. |表达感情；流露感情|To express emotion, especially in an excessive or theatrical way.|She emoted her distress with loud sobs and dramatic gestures. 她以大声哭泣和夸张的姿势表达她的痛苦。|The actor emoted beautifully, conveying the character's inner turmoil.  这位演员的表演非常出色，传达了角色内心的混乱。|近义词：express, display, convey, manifest|反义词：conceal, suppress, repress|词性:动词

emotes:/ɪˈmoʊts/ (US), /ɪˈməʊts/ (UK)| n. |表情符号（复数）；情感表达（复数）|A small digital image or icon used to express an idea or emotion in electronic communication.|He uses a lot of emotes in his online messages. 他在网上留言中使用了大量的表情符号。|Streamers often use custom emotes to engage with their audience. 主播经常使用自定义表情符号与观众互动。|近义词：emojis, icons, symbols|反义词：-|词性:名词
