morphologization:/mɔːrfəlɒdʒaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /mɔːrfəlɑːdʒɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 形态化，形态学化 | The process by which a word or phrase becomes a morpheme (the smallest meaningful unit in a language).  This often involves a word losing its independent meaning and becoming bound to another word, changing its pronunciation or spelling, or both. | The morphologization of "burger" from "hamburger" is a common example in English. 从 "hamburger" 到 "burger" 的形态化是英语中的一个常见例子。|  grammaticalization, lexicalization | - | noun
