multiwavelength:/ˌmʌltiˈweɪvlɛŋθ/
adjective
多波长的
Utilizing or involving multiple wavelengths of electromagnetic radiation.
例句：
Multiwavelength observations of the galaxy revealed new details about its structure.
对星系的多波长观测揭示了其结构的新细节。

The multiwavelength laser is used in various scientific applications.
多波长激光器用于各种科学应用。

近义词：multispectral
反义词：monochromatic, single-wavelength
词性：adjective
