dishabille:/ˌdɪsæˈbiːl/ /ˌdɪʃəˈbiːl/| n.|穿着随便， négligée；（尤指妇女的）晨衣，睡衣|A state of being only partly or scantily clothed.  The state of being dressed in a casual or lounging attire, typically at home.|She answered the door in dishabille. 她衣衫不整地开了门。|近义词：undress, negligee, nightclothes, pajamas |反义词:formal wear, full dress|noun

dishabille:/ˌdɪsæˈbiːl/ /ˌdɪʃəˈbiːl/| adj.|穿着随便的，衣衫不整的|dressed in a casual or lounging attire, typically at home.|Caught in dishabille, she hurried to put on a robe. 她衣衫不整地被撞见，赶紧披上了一件长袍。|近义词：undressed, informal |反义词:dressed, formal|adjective
