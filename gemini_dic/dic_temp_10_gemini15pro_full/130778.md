nonanemic:/ˌnɒnəˈniːmɪk/ | adj. | 非贫血的 | Not anemic; having a normal hemoglobin level. |  Example:  The patient's blood test results showed a nonanemic red blood cell count. 病人的血检结果显示红细胞计数正常，没有贫血。

Example:  After taking iron supplements, she became nonanemic and felt much healthier.  服用铁补充剂后，她不再贫血，感觉健康多了。| 近义词: healthy, robust | 反义词: anemic | 词性: 形容词
