eupryion:/juːˈpraɪɒn/ | n. |  优普里昂，最适宜的温度 | A state of mildness of temperature, neither too hot nor too cold; a pleasant and agreeable temperature. | The eupryion of the spring weather was perfect for a picnic. 春天天气宜人，非常适合野餐。| 近义词:  balminess, mildness, temperateness | 反义词:  inclement, harsh, severe | 词性: noun
Etymology: From Ancient Greek εὖ (eû, “well”) + πρύων (prúōn, “prow, bow of a ship”).  The connection to ships is obscure, but it may relate to the idea of a favorable wind.
