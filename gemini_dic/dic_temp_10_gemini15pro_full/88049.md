highers:/ˈhaɪərz/ | n. | 更高的东西；更高的人；上级；高年级学生 |  Those in the highers of the organization make the decisions.  组织中的高层人士做出决定。| 例句： The highers in the company decided to implement a new policy. 公司高层决定实施一项新政策。| 近义词：superiors, seniors, elders | 反义词:juniors, inferiors | 词性:名词

Etymology: From "high" + "-er" (comparative suffix) + "-s" (plural suffix).  Ultimately from Old English "hēah".
