calamistration: /kəˌlæmɪˈstreɪʃən/
noun
烫发; 卷发; 烫发的过程
the act or process of curling the hair with heated irons or rollers.
Her elaborate calamistration took hours each morning. 她每天早上都要花好几个小时精心烫发。

calamistration: /kəˌlæmɪˈstreɪʃən/
noun
灾难; 不幸
a disaster or misfortune. (rare, literary)
The calamistration of the flood left the town in ruins. 洪水带来的灾难使这个城镇变成一片废墟。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *calamistratio, from calamistrare "to curl the hair," from Latin calamistrum "curling iron," from Greek kalamis "reed."

SYNONYMS->
(for curling hair):  perming, waving, crimping, styling
(for disaster): catastrophe, calamity, disaster, tragedy

ANTONYMS->
(for curling hair): straightening
(for disaster):  triumph, success, blessing
