nickel-and-diming:/ˈnɪkəl ənd ˈdaɪmɪŋ/
| adj. |斤斤计较的；为小钱操心的；小气抠门的|Charging many small amounts of money, often seen as unfair or excessive.  Refers to the practice of charging small, often seemingly insignificant amounts of money for various things, which can accumulate into a significant expense.|The airline's nickel-and-diming tactics, like charging for checked bags and even water, have angered many customers. 这家航空公司斤斤计较的做法，比如对托运行李甚至水都收费，激怒了许多顾客。|例句：The hotel's nickel-and-diming policy of charging for Wi-Fi and use of the gym really put me off.  这家酒店对Wi-Fi和健身房的使用都要收费，这种斤斤计较的政策真让我反感。|近义词：penny-pinching, stingy, parsimonious|反义词：generous, lavish|词性:形容词

nickel-and-dime:/ˈnɪkəl ənd ˈdaɪm/
| vt. |通过收取许多小额费用来赚钱；为小钱操心|To extract money from someone by charging many small amounts.|The company is nickel-and-diming its customers with hidden fees. 这家公司通过收取隐性费用来盘剥顾客。|例句：I felt like the landlord was trying to nickel-and-dime me with all these extra charges. 我觉得房东想通过这些额外的费用来盘剥我。|近义词：exploit, fleece|反义词：compensate fairly|词性:动词
