beguile:/bɪˈɡaɪl/| v.|欺骗；诱骗；使着迷；消遣|To charm or enchant (someone), sometimes in a deceptive way. To trick or deceive.|例句：The salesman beguiled them into buying a car they didn't need. 推销员诱骗他们买了一辆他们并不需要的汽车。

例句：Her beauty beguiled everyone. 她的美貌迷住了所有人。|近义词：charm, enchant, captivate, deceive, trick|反义词：repulse, disenchant, bore|词性：动词
