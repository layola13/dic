antiscripturist:/ˌæntiˈskrɪptʃərɪst/
noun
敌基督者；反对圣经者
Someone who denies the authority or truth of the Bible, especially the New Testament, or who opposes Christ.
Martin Luther considered the Pope to be the Antichrist. 马丁·路德认为教皇是敌基督。
opponent of scripture, heretic
believer, supporter of scripture
n.
