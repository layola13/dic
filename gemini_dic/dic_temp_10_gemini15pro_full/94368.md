implantable:/ɪmˈplæntəbl/
adjective
可植入的
able to be implanted.
例句：An implantable cardioverter defibrillator can detect life-threatening arrhythmias.  一个可植入式心脏复律除颤器可以检测危及生命的心律失常。

例句：Scientists are developing an implantable device that can monitor blood glucose levels. 科学家们正在开发一种可以监测血糖水平的可植入设备。

近义词：insertable,  graftable
反义词：removable
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From implant + -able.  "implant" comes from the Latin "in-" (in, into) + "plantare" (to plant).
