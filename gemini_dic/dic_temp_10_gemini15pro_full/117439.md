meditate:/ˈmedɪteɪt/ (US), /ˈmedɪteɪt/ (UK)| v.| 冥想，沉思，思考 |To think deeply and quietly about something; to contemplate; to engage in contemplation or reflection.|  He meditated on the problem for hours. 他沉思这个问题好几个小时。

She meditates for 20 minutes every morning. 她每天早上冥想20分钟。

|近义词：contemplate, ponder, reflect, muse|反义词: ignore, neglect |词性: verb (动词)
