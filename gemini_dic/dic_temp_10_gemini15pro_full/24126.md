brezilin:/brəˈzɪlɪn/
noun
巴西木素
A crystalline compound, C16H14O5, extracted from brazilwood and used as a dye and indicator.
Brazilin is a natural dye extracted from brazilwood. 巴西木素是一种从巴西木中提取的天然染料。
The scientist used brazilin as an indicator in the experiment. 科学家在实验中使用巴西木素作为指示剂。
Synonyms: brazilin dye, natural red 24
Antonyms: N/A
