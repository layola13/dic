deceivably:/dɪˈsiːvɪŋli/| adv.|欺骗地；貌似；迷惑地|In a way that is deceptive; so as to mislead or deceive.  Appearing to be one thing when it is actually another.|The path was deceivably long. 这条路看起来不长，其实很长。|近义词：misleadingly, deceptively, falsely|反义词：truthfully, honestly, genuinely|词性:副词
词源: From deceive + -ably.  Ultimately from Latin *dēcipere*, from *dē-* (expressing removal) + *capere* (to take).
