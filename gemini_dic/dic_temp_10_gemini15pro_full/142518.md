osseter:/ˈɒsɪtər/ (UK) /ˈɑːsɪtər/ (US)| n. |鲟鱼|A large, primitive fish with bony plates instead of scales, found in rivers and seas. Some species are valued for their roe (caviar).|例句：The osseter is a source of caviar. 鲟鱼是鱼子酱的来源。

例句：Osseters can grow to enormous sizes. 鲟鱼可以长到非常大。|近义词：sturgeon|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *ostrucier, from Late Latin *ostruciarius, from Latin ostracium "oyster" (due to the resemblance of the scutes to oyster shells), ultimately from Greek óstreon "oyster, bone."  The change from /str/ to /st/ likely occurred under the influence of Germanic languages.
