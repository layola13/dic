The meeting discussed the boundaries of several archbishoprics. 会议讨论了几个大主教管区的边界。 | synonym: archdiocese, see | antonym: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *erchebischopriche*, equivalent to archbishop +‎ -ric.  Ultimately from Greek ἀρχι- (arkhi-, “chief”) +‎ ἐπίσκοπος (epískopos, “overseer, bishop”).
