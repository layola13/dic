noncomprehensive:/ˌnɒnkɒmprɪˈhensɪv/ | adj. |不全面的，不详尽的 | Not including everything; not thorough or complete. |  The report provided a noncomprehensive overview of the situation, omitting key details. 该报告对情况进行了不全面的概述，省略了关键细节。

The study offered a noncomprehensive analysis of the data, focusing only on a limited subset. 这项研究只对数据进行了不全面的分析，仅关注有限的子集。


| 近义词: incomplete, partial, limited, selective, cursory | 反义词: comprehensive, thorough, complete, exhaustive, in-depth | 词性: adjective
