ascococcus:/æskoʊˈkɒkəs/| n. |球菌属；囊球菌|A genus of bacteria in the family Micrococcaceae, characterized by spherical cells that occur in irregular masses.|Ascococci are known for their ability to survive in harsh environments.  囊球菌以其在恶劣环境中的生存能力而闻名。|Ascococcus has been isolated from soil samples.  从土壤样本中分离出囊球菌。|近义词:Micrococcus(微球菌), Staphylococcus(葡萄球菌) |反义词: None|Noun
