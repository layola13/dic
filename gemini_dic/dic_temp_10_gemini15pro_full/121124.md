misdeed:/ˌmɪsˈdiːd/
n.
不端行为，恶行，罪行
An act that is criminal or bad.

He confessed to all his misdeeds.
他坦白了他的所有罪行。

The politician's misdeeds were exposed by the media.
这位政客的罪行被媒体曝光了。

近义词: crime, offense, wrongdoing, transgression, sin
反义词: good deed, virtue, kindness

词性: 名词 (noun)

词源:  From Middle English *misdede*, from mis- + deed.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Germanic origin meaning "wrong," "bad," or "incorrect." "Deed" comes from Old English *dæd*, meaning "act" or "thing done."
