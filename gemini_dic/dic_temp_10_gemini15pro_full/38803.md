conductresses:/kənˈdʌktrɪsɪz/
n.
女售票员；女指挥
a woman who collects fares on a bus or train, or a woman who directs an orchestra or choir.
The conductresses on the old trams were always very helpful. 老式电车上的女售票员总是很乐于助人。
