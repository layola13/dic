distichous:/dɪˈstɪkəs/
adjective
二列的，二行的
arranged in two vertical rows or ranks on opposite sides of a stem, typically one above another.
The distichous arrangement of leaves on the stem is a characteristic feature of this plant.  这种植物茎上的叶子二列排列是其特征。
The plant's distichous leaves gave it a flattened appearance.  这种植物的二列式叶子使它看起来扁平。

近义词: two-ranked
反义词: spiral, alternate, whorled
词性: adjective
