bancal:/bæŋˈkɑːl/
| adj. |倾斜的，不稳的|Sloping; uneven; askew.  Referring to land, especially a terrace or vineyard, built on a slope.  Can also refer to something that is not straight or level.|The farmer cultivated the bancal fields with great care. 农民小心翼翼地耕种着倾斜的田地。|The bancal table wobbled precariously. 那张不稳的桌子摇摇晃晃，很不安全。|
|近义词:sloping, inclined, tilted, uneven, askew|反义词:level, flat, even, straight|词性:形容词|词源:From French *bancal*, ultimately from Old Provençal *banc* ("bench, slope").|
