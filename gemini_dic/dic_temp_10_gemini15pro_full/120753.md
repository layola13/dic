misappellation:/ˌmɪsˌæpəˈleɪʃn/
noun
错误命名；误称
an act or instance of applying a wrong name to a person or thing.
The politician sued the newspaper for misappellation after it consistently referred to him by the wrong name.  这位政治家起诉了这家报纸，因为该报不断地用错误的名字称呼他。
The use of "Eskimo" to refer to Inuit peoples is now considered a misappellation.  用“爱斯基摩人”来指代因纽特人现在被认为是一种误称。
misnaming, wrong name, incorrect designation
