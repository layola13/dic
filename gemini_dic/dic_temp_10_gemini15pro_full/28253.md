captains:/ˈkæptɪnz/| n.|船长，队长，领袖|A person who commands a ship, a sports team, or other group.|The captains of the two teams met for the coin toss. 两队队长碰面进行抛硬币决定开球方。|近义词：leaders, commanders, chiefs|反义词:crew, subordinates|noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *capitayn*, from Old French *capitaine*, from Late Latin *capitāneus*, from Latin *caput* (“head”).
