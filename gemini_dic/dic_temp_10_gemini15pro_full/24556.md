broadview:/ˈbrɔːdvjuː/
noun
广阔的视野；开阔的景色；全面的看法
a wide, extensive view; a comprehensive outlook or perspective
From the mountaintop, we had a broadview of the valley below. 从山顶上，我们看到了山谷的全景。
synonyms: panorama, vista, overview, perspective
antonyms: limited view, narrow perspective
词性: 名词

broadview:/ˈbrɔːdvjuː/
adjective
视野开阔的；全面的
having a wide range or scope; comprehensive
He has a broadview of the political situation. 他对政治形势有全面的了解。
synonyms: wide-ranging, comprehensive, all-encompassing
antonyms: narrow, limited, restricted
词性: 形容词
