agog:/əˈɡɒɡ/ /əˈɡɑːɡ/| adj.|兴奋的，急切的|Full of intense interest or excitement; eager.|例句：The children were agog with excitement as they waited for Santa. 孩子们兴奋地等待着圣诞老人。

例句：She was agog to hear the news. 她急切地想听到这个消息。


|近义词：eager, excited, expectant, impatient, keen|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, uninterested|词性:形容词
