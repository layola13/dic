inactions:/ɪnˈækʃənz/
|n.|不作为，无行动|Lack of action; failure to act.|His inaction cost the company millions. 他的不作为使公司损失了数百万美元。|The government's inaction on climate change is alarming. 政府在气候变化问题上的不作为令人担忧。|近义词：inactivity, idleness, passiveness|反义词:action, activity, movement|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + action.  The prefix "in-" negates the meaning of "action".  "Action" comes from the Latin "actio," meaning "a doing, a driving, an impulse."
