fiorite:/ˈfiːəˌraɪt/ /ˈfɪərɪˌtaɪt/ | adj. | 花状的，花朵似的，繁茂的 | Resembling or adorned with flowers; flowery. | The artist created a fiorite design, reminiscent of a blooming garden. 艺术家创作了一个花状的设计，让人想起一个盛开的花园。| The fabric was covered in a fiorite pattern. 这块布料上覆盖着花朵图案。 | 近义词：floral, flowery, blossoming | 反义词：plain, simple, unadorned | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Italian *fiorito*, past participle of *fiorire* "to blossom, flourish," from Latin *florere* "to blossom, bloom, flourish," from *flos* "flower."
