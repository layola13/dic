cinerary:/ˈsɪnəˌrɛri/ | adj.|骨灰的；用于骨灰的；骨灰瓮的|Relating to ashes, especially the ashes of the dead; used for ashes, typically those of cremated bodies.|The cinerary urn was placed in the family mausoleum. 骨灰瓮被安放在家族陵墓中.|Synonyms:  crematory, funereal|Antonyms:  living, vital|adjective


cinerary:/ˈsɪnəˌrɛri/ | n.|骨灰瓮；盛骨灰的容器|A container for ashes, especially those of a cremated body.|The museum displayed ancient Roman cineraries.  博物馆展出了古罗马的骨灰瓮。|Synonyms: urn, ossuary|Antonyms: N/A|noun
