conchinine:/ˈkɒŋkɪniːn/ /ˈkɑːnkɪniːn/
noun
奎宁碱; 辛可宁
A white crystalline alkaloid, C₂₀H₂₄N₂O₂, obtained from cinchona bark and formerly used to treat malaria. It is an isomer of quinine.

Conchinine is an isomer of quinine, both derived from cinchona bark.

近义词: quinidine, cinchonine
