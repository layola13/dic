congenital:/kənˈdʒɛnɪtəl/| adj.|先天的，天生的|Present at birth; existing from birth.  Relating to a condition present at birth, whether inherited or caused by the environment, especially the uterine environment.|例句：The baby was born with a congenital heart defect.  这个婴儿出生时患有先天性心脏缺陷。|例句：Congenital blindness can be caused by a variety of factors. 先天性失明可能是由多种因素引起的。|近义词：innate, inborn, inherent|反义词：acquired, developed|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *congenitus*  "born together," from *com-* "together" + *genitus*, past participle of *gignere* "to beget."
