ambitionlessly:/æmˈbɪʃən.ləs.li/
adv.
没有雄心地，缺乏抱负地
Without ambition; in a way that shows no desire to succeed or achieve something.
He drifted through life ambitionlessly, content with a simple existence. 他一生浑浑噩噩，缺乏抱负，满足于简单的生活。
Synonyms: halfheartedly, listlessly, apathetically, indifferently
Antonyms: ambitiously, purposefully, determinedly, zealously
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From ambition + -less + -ly.  "Ambition" comes from the Latin "ambitio" meaning "a going around," especially to solicit votes (from "ambire" "to go around"). The suffix "-less" indicates a lack of something, and "-ly" forms an adverb.
