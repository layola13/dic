incorruptibility:/ˌɪnkəˌrʌptɪˈbɪlɪti/
n.
不可腐败性; 廉洁; 正直
The quality of not being susceptible to corruption, especially bribery or moral decay.
His incorruptibility made him an ideal candidate for the position.  他的廉洁使他成为该职位的理想人选。
Near Synonyms: integrity, honesty, probity, uprightness, righteousness
Antonyms: corruptibility, dishonesty, venality
Noun

incorruptible:/ˌɪnkəˈrʌptɪbl/
adj.
不可腐败的; 廉洁的; 正直的
Not susceptible to corruption, especially bribery or moral decay.
She was known for her incorruptible character. 她以正直的品格而闻名。
Near Synonyms: honest, upright, righteous, principled, moral
Antonyms: corruptible, dishonest, venal
