antiquarianism:/ˌæn.tɪˈkweə.ri.ə.nɪ.zəm/
noun
古物研究；古物收藏；对古物的兴趣
The study or collection of antiquities.  A devotion to or interest in antiquities.
He developed an antiquarianism in his later years, amassing a large collection of Roman coins. 他晚年对古物产生了兴趣，收集了大量的罗马硬币。
Synonyms:  archaeology, antiquarian study
Antonyms: modernism, futurism
词性: 名词

antiquarianisms:/ˌæn.tɪˈkweə.ri.ə.nɪ.zəmz/
noun
古物研究（复数）；古物收藏（复数）；对古物的兴趣（复数）
Multiple instances of the study or collection of antiquities or interests in them.  Devotions to or interests in antiquities.
His antiquarianisms ranged from Roman pottery to medieval manuscripts. 他的古物研究范围从罗马陶器到中世纪手稿。
Synonyms:  archaeological pursuits, antiquarian studies
Antonyms: modern trends, futuristic interests
