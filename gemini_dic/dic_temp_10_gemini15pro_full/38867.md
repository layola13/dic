confederationist:/kənˌfedəˈreɪʃənɪst/
n.|邦联主义者；支持邦联制者|A supporter of a confederation, especially a supporter of the Confederate States of America during the American Civil War.|Example: His grandfather was a confederationist during the Civil War. 他的祖父在内战期间是邦联主义者。|Example: Many confederationists believed in states' rights over federal power. 许多邦联主义者相信州权高于联邦权力。|Synonym: secessionist, rebel, Confederate|Antonym: unionist, federalist|noun
