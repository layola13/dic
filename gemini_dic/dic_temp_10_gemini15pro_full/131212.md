nonbuyings:/ˌnɒnˈbaɪɪŋz/ | n. | 不购买，拒购 |The act of not buying something; a boycott.  A collective refusal to buy certain goods or services.|Consumer nonbuyings can significantly impact a company's sales. 消费者拒购会严重影响公司的销售。| The nonbuyings of certain products due to ethical concerns have increased in recent years.近年来，出于伦理方面的担忧，某些产品的拒购有所增加。 | synonyms: boycott, refusal to purchase | antonyms: purchase, buying | noun
