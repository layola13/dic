anticonvulsants:/ˌæntikənˈvʌlsənts/| n.|抗惊厥药|Medicines used to prevent or control seizures (fits or convulsions).|例句：The doctor prescribed anticonvulsants to control her epilepsy. 医生给她开了抗惊厥药来控制她的癫痫。

例句：Anticonvulsants can have various side effects, so it's important to monitor the patient. 抗惊厥药可能有各种副作用，因此监测病人很重要。|近义词：antiepileptics, antiseizure medications|反义词:convulsants|词性:noun
