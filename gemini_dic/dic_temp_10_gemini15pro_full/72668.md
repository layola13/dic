foamed:/foʊmd/| v.|（使）起泡沫|Past tense and past participle of foam.|He foamed the milk for the cappuccino.  他把牛奶打成泡沫做卡布奇诺。|近义词：froth, bubble, lather|反义词:flatten, settle|词性:动词
