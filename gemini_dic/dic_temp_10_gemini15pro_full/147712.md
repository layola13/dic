overprescribing: /ˌoʊvərprɪˈskraɪbɪŋ/ | n. | 过度处方，过度开药 | The act of prescribing too much medication or prescribing medication too frequently. | Overprescribing of antibiotics can lead to antibiotic resistance. 过度使用抗生素会导致抗生素耐药性。| Overmedication, excessive prescription | Underprescribing | noun

Etymology:  "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above, beyond") + "prescribe" (from Latin praescribere "write beforehand, order, direct," from prae- "before" + scribere "to write").
