fazed:/feɪzd/
adjective
烦扰的，担忧的，慌乱的
disturbed, worried, or disconcerted; unsettled.
He didn't seem fazed by the news. 他似乎没有被这个消息吓到。
近义词: bothered, disturbed, flustered, rattled
反义词: unfazed, calm, composed, unflappable
词性: adjective

词源:
From the verb *faze*, a variant of *feeze*, an obsolete word meaning "to drive away; to put to flight; frighten; worry."  Ultimately of unknown origin.
