cv: /ˌsiːˈviː/ (British English), /ˌsiːˈviː/ (American English) | n. | 简历; 履历 | curriculum vitae (the course of one's life) - a written overview of a person's experience and other qualifications for a job, typically including education, employment history, and personal interests. | Example: I updated my CV before applying for the job. 我在申请工作前更新了我的简历。 | Example:  Please send your CV and cover letter to the following address.  请将您的简历和求职信发送到以下地址。 | Synonyms: resume, biodata | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
