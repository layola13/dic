inductive:/ɪnˈdʌktɪv/
adjective
归纳的；诱导的
Characterized by or based on the inference of general laws from particular instances.
例句：Inductive reasoning is a method of reasoning in which the premises are viewed as supplying some evidence for the truth of the conclusion. 归纳推理是一种推理方法，其中前提被视为为结论的真实性提供了一些证据。

例句：Scientists often use inductive reasoning to form hypotheses and theories. 科学家经常使用归纳推理来形成假设和理论。
近义词：empirical, inferential
反义词：deductive
词性:adjective
