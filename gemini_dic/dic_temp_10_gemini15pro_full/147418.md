overnourishing:/ˌoʊvərˈnɜːrɪʃɪŋ/
adjective
营养过剩的，过度滋养的
Providing or containing an excessive amount of nourishment.
Example: Overnourishing a plant can lead to root rot. 给植物过多的营养会导致根腐烂。
Example: The overnourishing diet caused the child to become overweight. 营养过剩的饮食导致孩子超重。
Synonyms: overfeeding, surfeiting
Antonyms: undernourishing, starving
词性: 形容词
