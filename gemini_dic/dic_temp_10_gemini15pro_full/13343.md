atlantean:/ətˈlæn.ti.ən/ | n. | 亚特兰蒂斯人 | An inhabitant of the legendary island of Atlantis. |  He claimed to be a descendant of the atlanteans. 他声称是亚特兰蒂斯人的后裔。| 近义词：Atlantean, inhabitant, resident | 反义词: N/A | 词性:名词

词源: From Atlantis + -ean.  The word "Atlantis" itself comes from Greek Ἀτλαντὶς νῆσος (Atlantìs nêsos), meaning "island of Atlas."
