inconsistentness:/ˌɪnkənˈsɪstənsɪ/| n.|不一致，前后矛盾|The inconsistency between his words and actions made him seem untrustworthy. 他的言行不一让他显得不可信。|例句：The inconsistency in her testimony raised doubts about her credibility.  她在证词中的前后矛盾让人对她的可信度产生了怀疑。

例句：His inconsistentness in applying the rules angered the employees. 他在执行规则上的不一致激怒了员工。|近义词：discrepancy, contradiction, incongruity, variance|反义词：consistency, agreement, harmony|词性:名词

Etymology:
From in- (not) + consistent + -ness (forming nouns expressing a state or quality). Consistent comes from Latin consistere (to stand firm, stand together), from con- (together) + sistere (to stand).
