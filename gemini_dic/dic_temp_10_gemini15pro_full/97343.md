infortune:/ɪnˈfɔːrtʃuːn/ |n.|不幸，厄运|Misfortune; bad luck.  A situation marked by adversity, hardship, or affliction.|He suffered a series of infortunes in his business ventures.  他在生意场上遭遇了一连串的不幸。|Synonyms: misfortune, adversity, hardship, calamity, mishap, setback|Antonyms: fortune, luck, prosperity, success|Noun
