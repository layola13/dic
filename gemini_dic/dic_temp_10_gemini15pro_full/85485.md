hassling:/ˈhæsəlɪŋ/ /ˈhæslɪŋ/
hassle: n. vt.|麻烦，困难; 争论，口角; 烦扰，纠缠|A persistent annoyance.|The children kept hassling me to take them to the park. 孩子们一直缠着我去公园。|近义词：bother, annoy, pester, trouble, harass|反义词:soothe, comfort, please|词性:名词，动词
hassling: v.|烦扰，纠缠（hassle的现在分词）|The act of persistently annoying someone.|I'm tired of being hassled by salespeople. 我厌倦了被销售人员纠缠。|近义词：bothering, annoying, pestering, troubling, harassing|反义词:soothing, comforting, pleasing|词性:动词
