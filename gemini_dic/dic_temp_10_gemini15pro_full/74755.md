freewheelingly:/ˌfriːˈwiːlɪŋli/
adv.
自由自在地，无拘无束地
acting or done in a free and easy way, without concern for rules or the possible results
He freewheelingly dismissed the concerns of his colleagues. 他漫不经心地驳回了同事们的担忧。
Synonyms: casually, carelessly, recklessly, unrestrainedly
Antonyms: cautiously, carefully, deliberately, restrainedly
词性: 副词
