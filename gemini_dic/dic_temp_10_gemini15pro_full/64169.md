equatability:/ˌiːkwəˈtæbɪləti/
n.
公平; 平稳; 均衡
The quality of being equatable or uniform.  The state of being steady and unchanging.
The equatability of his temperament made him a good mediator. 他的性情平和使他成为了一位优秀的调解员。
Synonyms:  uniformity, consistency, stability, evenness, steadiness
Antonyms:  variability, inconsistency, instability, unevenness
词性: 名词
