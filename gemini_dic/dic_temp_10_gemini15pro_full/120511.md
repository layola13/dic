minutemen:/ˈmɪnɪtmɛn/ | n. | 民兵 |  American militiamen who were ready to fight at a minute's notice during the American Revolution. |  The minutemen played a crucial role in the battles of Lexington and Concord.  民兵在莱克星顿和康科德战役中发挥了至关重要的作用。 | synonyms: militia, irregulars | antonyms: regular army, standing army | noun
