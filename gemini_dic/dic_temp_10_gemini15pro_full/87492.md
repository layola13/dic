heterocarpous:/ˌhɛtərəˈkɑːrpəs/
adjective
异果的
Producing two or more distinct types of fruit.  A plant that produces two or more structurally different kinds of fruits.
The heterocarpous plant produced both spiny and smooth fruits.  这种异果植物既结多刺的果实，也结光滑的果实。
近义词:polycarpic
反义词:homocarpous
词性:adjective

Etymology:  From hetero- (different) + -carpous (fruiting), derived from Greek "heteros" (different) and "karpos" (fruit).
