animalish: /ˈænɪməlɪʃ/
adjective|
像动物的；兽性的；粗野的|
Resembling or characteristic of an animal, often in a negative or derogatory sense; beastly, brutish.|
He behaved in an animalish way, snarling and snapping at everyone. 他表现得像野兽一样，对每个人都咆哮着、 snapping着。

She had an animalish fear of the dark. 她对黑暗有一种动物般的恐惧。

近义词: beastly, brutish, bestial, savage, feral, wild, untamed|
反义词: civilized, refined, cultured, humane, gentle, tame|
词性: adjective
