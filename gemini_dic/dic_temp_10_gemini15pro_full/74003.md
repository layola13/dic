fortune:/ˈfɔːrtʃuːn/ (US) /ˈfɔːtʃən/ (UK)| n. |运气，财富，命运|Chance or luck, especially in the way it affects people's lives; a large amount of money or property|She had the good fortune to be born into a wealthy family. 她很幸运地出生在一个富裕的家庭。|synonyms: luck, chance, fate, destiny, prosperity, wealth, riches|antonyms: misfortune, adversity, poverty, hardship|noun

fortune:/ˈfɔːrtʃuːn/ (US) /ˈfɔːtʃən/ (UK)| v. |（常用于被动语态）给…带来好运/厄运|to happen to someone by chance|He was fortuned to escape injury in the accident. 他很幸运地在事故中没有受伤。|synonyms: bless, favor|antonyms: curse|verb
