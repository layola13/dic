girtline:/ˈɡɜːrtlaɪn/ | n. |  腰围；裙长；（尤指船的）水线 | The measurement around the waist or the length of a skirt, particularly referring to the waterline of a ship. |  She measured her girtline to ensure the skirt would fit. 她测量了她的腰围以确保裙子合身。The ship's girtline was low in the water due to the heavy cargo. 由于货物过重，船的水线很低。 | synonyms: waistline, skirt length, waterline | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, could consider terms like "bustline" for contrasting body measurements) | noun
