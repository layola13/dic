albugo:/ælˈbjuːɡoʊ/
n.
白锈菌，白锈病
A genus of parasitic fungi (Oomycetes) causing white rust.  They form a white, blister-like growth on the host plant.
The albugo is a destructive plant pathogen. 白锈菌是一种具有破坏性的植物病原体。
Albugo candida causes white rust in crucifers. 白锈菌引起十字科植物白锈病。
Synonym: white rust fungus
Antonym: N/A (No direct antonym for a genus of fungi)
