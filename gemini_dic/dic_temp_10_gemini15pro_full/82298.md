ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "group," which comes from the Old French "grouper," meaning "to assemble in a crowd."  Ultimately from the Frankish "*kruppo," meaning "lump, rounded mass." The "-ing" suffix transforms the verb into a noun indicating the result or process of the action.
