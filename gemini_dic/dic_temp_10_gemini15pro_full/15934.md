balmily:/ˈbɑːmɪli/| adv.|温和地；馨香地；止痛地|In a balmy manner; soothingly and pleasantly.  Referring to a mild and pleasant climate, often associated with a fragrant scent.|The breeze blew balmily through the open window. 微风轻柔地吹过敞开的窗户。|Synonyms: mildly, gently, soothingly, pleasantly|Antonyms: harshly, roughly, unpleasantly|adverb
