fluoboric:/ˌfluːəˈbɔːrɪk/ | adj. |氟硼酸的；四氟硼酸的| Relating to or containing the fluoborate ion (BF4-), a tetrahedral anion formed from boron trifluoride.|Fluoboric acid is a strong acid. 氟硼酸是一种强酸。| Fluoboric acid is used in electroplating baths. 氟硼酸用于电镀浴。|Synonyms: tetrafluoroborate|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
