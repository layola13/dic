arachnids:/əˈræknɪdz/
n.
蛛形纲动物
A class of arthropods that includes spiders, scorpions, mites, and ticks.
Spiders are arachnids. 蜘蛛是蛛形纲动物。
Arachnids have eight legs. 蛛形纲动物有八条腿。
synonyms:  arachnoids
antonyms: insects, crustaceans
noun
