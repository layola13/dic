painstakingness:/ˈpeɪnzˌteɪkɪŋnəs/ | n. | 认真；一丝不苟； painstaking |The painstakingness of her work is evident in every detail. 她的作品的每个细节都体现了她的认真细致。|近义词：meticulousness, thoroughness, diligence, assiduity | 反义词：carelessness, negligence, sloppiness | noun

painstaking:/ˈpeɪnzˌteɪkɪŋ/ | adj. | 艰苦的；勤勉的；一丝不苟的 |Creating this artwork was a painstaking process. 创作这件艺术品是一个艰苦的过程。|近义词：meticulous, thorough, diligent, assiduous | 反义词：careless, negligent, sloppy | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *panes taking, equivalent to pains +‎ taking.  "Pains" originally referred to efforts, labor, or trouble taken.  "Taking" refers to the act of taking those pains or efforts. Thus, the word literally means the act of taking pains or making great efforts.
