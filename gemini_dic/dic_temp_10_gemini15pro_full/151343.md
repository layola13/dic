parkas:/ˈpɑːrkəz/ (US), /ˈpɑːkəs/ (UK)| n.|派克大衣，风雪大衣|A parka is a hip-length cold-weather jacket, typically made of very warm material, often with a fur-lined hood.|She wore a thick parka to protect herself from the blizzard. 她穿了一件厚厚的派克大衣来抵御暴风雪。|近义词：anorak, coat, jacket|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps shorts, t-shirt)|noun
