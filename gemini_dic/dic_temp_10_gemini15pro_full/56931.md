dotingnesses:/ˈdoʊtɪŋnɪsɪz/
n.
溺爱，过分宠爱
Excessive fondness or affection.
She showered her grandchildren with dotingnesses, spoiling them rotten. 她对孙子孙女们百般溺爱，把他们宠坏了。
Near Synonyms:  indulgence, pampering, fondness, affection, tenderness
Antonyms: neglect, indifference, coldness, harshness

doting:/ˈdoʊtɪŋ/
adj.
溺爱的，过分宠爱的
Showing excessive fondness or affection.
He's a doting father who loves spending time with his children. 他是一位慈爱的父亲，喜欢和孩子们待在一起。
Near Synonyms:  fond, affectionate, loving, tender, indulgent
Antonyms: neglectful, indifferent, cold, harsh
