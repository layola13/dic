misdiagrammed:/ˌmɪsdaɪəˈɡræmd/
|adj.|图表绘制错误的；图解错误的|incorrectly or inaccurately diagrammed.  Representing something with a flawed or incorrect diagram.|The circuit was misdiagrammed, leading to a short circuit. 电路图画错了，导致了短路。|misrepresented, inaccurately drawn|correctly diagrammed|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
mis- (prefix): From Old English *mis-, from Proto-Germanic *missa- "wrongly, amiss," cognate with Old High German missi-, Old Norse mis-, Gothic missa-.  Indicates something done wrongly or incorrectly.
diagram (noun/verb): From Late Latin diagramma "figure, geometrical figure," from Greek diagramma "geometric figure, outline, plan," from diagraphein "mark out by lines, delineate," from dia- "across, through" + graphein "to write, draw."
