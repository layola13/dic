overimport:/ˌōvərˈɪmpɔːrt/ | n. | 过度进口 | The act of importing more goods than a country needs or can readily absorb. | The overimport of cheap textiles has damaged the domestic industry. 过度进口廉价纺织品损害了国内产业。|近义词：excess import, surplus import |反义词: underimport| noun


overimport:/ˌōvərˈɪmpɔːrt/ | v. | 过度进口 | To import more goods than a country needs or can readily absorb. | The government is trying to curb the tendency to overimport luxury goods. 政府正试图遏制过度进口奢侈品的趋势。|近义词：import excessively |反义词: underimport | verb
