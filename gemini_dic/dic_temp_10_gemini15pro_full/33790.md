circumcisions: /ˌsɜːrkəmˈsɪʒənz/ | n. | 割礼，包皮环切术 |The surgical removal of the foreskin of the penis.  It is a common religious rite in Judaism and Islam, and is also practiced for cultural or health reasons in some other societies. | Male circumcisions are performed for religious and cultural reasons in many parts of the world. 在世界许多地方，男性割礼是出于宗教和文化原因进行的。

Circumcisions are less common in some cultures than others.  在某些文化中，割礼不如其他文化中常见。 | 近义词:  excision | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun


词源: Late Middle English: from Old French circumcision or late Latin circumcisio(n-), from circumcidere ‘cut around’, from circum- ‘around’ + caedere ‘cut’.
