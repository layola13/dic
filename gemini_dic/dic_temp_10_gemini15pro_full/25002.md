brutalizing:/ˈbruːtəlaɪzɪŋ/
v.
使残暴；使丧失人性；残酷对待
making someone cruel or violent; treating someone cruelly
The war brutalized many young men. 战争使许多年轻人变得残暴。
近义词:dehumanizing, savaging, battering
反义词:civilizing, humanizing
词性:动词


brutalize:/ˈbruːtəlaɪz/
v.
使残暴；使丧失人性；残酷对待
making someone cruel or violent; treating someone cruelly
Years of abuse brutalized the child. 多年的虐待使这个孩子变得残暴。
近义词:dehumanize, savage, batter
反义词:civilize, humanize
词性:动词


brutal:/ˈbruːtl/
adj.
残忍的；野蛮的；残酷的
cruel, violent, and completely without feelings
He was brutally murdered. 他被残忍地杀害了。
近义词:savage, cruel, vicious, barbaric
反义词:gentle, kind, humane
词性:形容词


词源:
From brute + -al + -ize.  Ultimately from Latin *brūtus "heavy, dull, insensible," of uncertain origin.
