epanthous:/ɛˈpænθəs/
adjective
开花于叶上的; 花在叶上的
Developing or borne on the upper surface of a leaf.  Growing on or out of a leaf.
The fern is characterized by its epanthous inflorescences.  这种蕨类植物的特点是其花序生长在叶片上。
Epiphyllous, foliar
(No common antonym)
adjective
