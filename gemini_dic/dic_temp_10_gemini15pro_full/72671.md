foamflower:/ˈfoʊmˌflaʊər/| n. |泡沫花，假延胡索|A delicate woodland wildflower (Tiarella cordifolia) with small, white, foamy flowers.|例句：The foamflowers are blooming in the woods. 泡沫花正在树林里盛开。

例句：I planted some foamflowers in my shade garden. 我在我的阴凉花园里种了一些泡沫花。|近义词：false miterwort, coolwort|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From *foam* + *flower*.  Foam refers to the appearance of the clusters of tiny white flowers.
