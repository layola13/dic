barometrograph:/ˌbærəˈmɒtrəɡrɑːf/ /ˌbærəˈmɑːtrəɡræf/| n. |气压记录器；自记气压计|An instrument that automatically records variations in atmospheric pressure.  It provides a continuous record of barometric pressure changes over time.|The barometrograph showed a steady drop in pressure, indicating an approaching storm. 气压记录器显示气压持续下降，预示着暴风雨即将来临。|Synonyms: barograph |Antonyms: N/A | noun
