organismically:/ˌɔːrɡəˈnɪzmɪkli/ | adv. | 有机地，完整地 | In a manner that involves or affects the entire organism; holistically. |  Her artwork reflects an organismically interconnected view of nature. 她的艺术作品反映了一种有机互联的自然观。|  holistically, integrally, systematically, comprehensively |  mechanically, fragmentarily | adverb

Etymology:
From organism + -ic + -ally. "Organism" comes from the French organisme, from the Greek organon "instrument, tool, organ." "-ic" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "of, relating to, or characterized by." "-ally" is a suffix forming adverbs from adjectives ending in "-ic."
