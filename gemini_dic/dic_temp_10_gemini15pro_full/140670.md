omening:/ˈəʊmənɪŋ/ | n. | 预兆，征兆 |Something that is believed to be a sign of something good or bad that will happen in the future. |  The dark clouds were an omening of the storm to come.  乌云是暴风雨即将来临的预兆。 | synonyms: portent, presage, augury, foreboding, harbinger | antonyms:  none (the concept itself doesn't have a direct opposite, although specific omens can be positive or negative) | noun
