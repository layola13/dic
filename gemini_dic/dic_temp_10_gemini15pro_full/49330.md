delimitation:/diːˌlɪmɪˈteɪʃn/
n.|界限；限定；划界|The delimitation of the border between the two countries was finally agreed upon. 两国之间的边界划分最终达成一致。|例句：The delimitation of responsibilities between the two departments was unclear.  两个部门之间的职责划分不明确。|近义词：demarcation, definition, boundary, limit|反义词：extension, expansion|词性:名词

delimit:/dɪˈlɪmɪt/
vt.|限定；划定界限|The river delimits the two properties. 这条河划分了两处房产。|例句：We need to clearly delimit the scope of the project. 我们需要明确界定项目的范围。|近义词：define, demarcate, bound, limit|反义词：expand, extend|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dēlimitātiō, from dēlimitātus, past participle of dēlimitāre (“to define, mark off the boundaries of”), from dē (“from, down from”) + līmitāre (“to limit”), from līmis (“limit, boundary”).
