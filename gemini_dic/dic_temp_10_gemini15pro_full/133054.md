nonepiscopalian:/ˌnɒnɪpɪˈskəʊpəlɪən/
adjective
非圣公会的
Not belonging to or connected with the Episcopal Church or Anglican Communion.
He was raised in a nonepiscopalian household. 他在一个非圣公会的家庭长大。
near synonyms: non-Anglican
antonyms: Episcopalian, Anglican
词性: adjective
