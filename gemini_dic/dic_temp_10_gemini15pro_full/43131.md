coyote:/kaɪˈoʊtiː/ /kaɪˈoʊt/ | n. |郊狼，土狼|A wild dog-like animal related to wolves and found in North and Central America. |例句：We saw a coyote running across the field. 我们看见一只郊狼跑过田野。

例句：Coyotes are known for their distinctive howls. 土狼以其独特的嚎叫而闻名。|近义词：wolf, jackal |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps domesticated dog as a conceptual opposite) |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Mexican Spanish *coyote*, from Nahuatl *coyōtl*.
