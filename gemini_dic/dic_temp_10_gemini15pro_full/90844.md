hung:/hʌŋ/
v.|悬挂，吊着；(be hung with)装饰着；垂下；搁置，悬而未决；（陪审团）无法作出裁决|The picture was hung above the fireplace.|这幅画挂在壁炉上方。|近义词：suspend, dangle, hang |反义词: take down|词性:动词(past tense and past participle of hang)

hung:/hʌŋ/
adj.|（尤指男人）身材魁梧的，雄伟的|He was tall, dark, and hung.|他又高又黑，身材魁梧。|近义词：well-endowed, large, big|反义词: small, little|词性:形容词


词源:
From Middle English *hongen, *hangen, from Old English hangian, hōn (“to hang”), from Proto-Germanic *hangōną (“to hang”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kenǵ- (“to hang”). Cognate with Scots hing (“to hang”), West Frisian hingje (“to hang”), Dutch hangen (“to hang”), German hängen (“to hang”), Danish hænge (“to hang”), Swedish hänga (“to hang”), Icelandic hengja (“to hang”).
