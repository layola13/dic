antagonistically:/ænˌtæɡəˈnɪstɪkli/| adv.|敌对地，对抗地|Showing or feeling active opposition or hostility.|The two groups debated antagonistically.  这两个团体进行了激烈的辩论。|Example: He spoke antagonistically to his opponent. 他以敌对的态度对他的对手说话。|Synonyms: hostilely, aggressively, combatively, contentiously|Antonyms: cooperatively, amicably, harmoniously|词性:副词

Etymology:
From antagonist + -ic + -ally.  Antagonist comes from the Late Latin word antagonista, meaning "rival, competitor,"  which in turn comes from the Greek word antagōnistēs, meaning "opponent, competitor in games," from anti- "against" + agōnistēs "competitor."
