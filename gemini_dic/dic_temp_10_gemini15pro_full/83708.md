hagiographists:/ˌhæɡiˈɒɡrəfɪsts/
n.
圣徒传记作者，吹捧者
Writers of hagiographies; those who idealize or promote a person or thing excessively.
Many historians dismiss the work of early hagiographists as overly biased and lacking in objectivity. 许多历史学家认为早期圣徒传记作者的作品过于偏颇，缺乏客观性。

The company's PR department functions as hagiographists, constantly churning out glowing portrayals of the CEO.  这家公司的公关部门就像圣徒传记作者，不断地炮制出对首席执行官的溢美之词。

biographers, idealizers, promoters
critics, detractors
