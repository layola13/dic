changelessness:/ˈtʃeɪndʒləsnəs/| n. |不变性，永恒性|The quality of being unchanging or constant. |The changelessness of the mountains gives a sense of peace.  群山的永恒给人一种平和的感觉。|近义词：immutability, permanence, constancy|反义词：mutability, variability, inconstancy|词性:名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From change + -less + -ness.  Change (from Old French changier) ultimately derives from Latin cambiare "to barter."
