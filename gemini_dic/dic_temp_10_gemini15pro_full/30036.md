cbe:/siː biː iː/ |abbr.| 完全血细胞计数 (Complete Blood Count)|  A laboratory test that measures the number of red blood cells, white blood cells, and platelets in a blood sample. | Example: The doctor ordered a CBE to check for anemia. 医生要求做全血细胞计数来检查贫血。 | 近义词: full blood count (FBC) | 反义词:  None (as it's a medical procedure) | noun
