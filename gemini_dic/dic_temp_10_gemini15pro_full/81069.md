grainiest:/ˈɡreɪniɪst/| adj.|颗粒最粗的；最粒状的|Having the most prominent grains or granules; most granular.|The grainiest photo I've ever seen was taken with a very old camera.|近义词：granular, coarse, gritty|反义词:smooth, fine, refined|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "grainy" + "-est" (superlative suffix). "Grainy" comes from "grain" (referring to a small, hard particle) + "-y" (adjective suffix). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *grāną.
