fuguists:/ˈfjuːɡɪsts/
n.
河豚爱好者；吃河豚的人
A person who enjoys eating fugu (pufferfish), despite the potential risks.
Fuguists appreciate the unique taste and thrill of eating this potentially deadly fish.  河豚爱好者欣赏这种具有潜在致命危险的鱼的独特味道和刺激感。
Experienced fuguists know which restaurants to trust for proper preparation.  经验丰富的河豚爱好者知道哪些餐厅可以信赖，能够正确地处理河豚。
Synonym:  fugu connoisseurs, pufferfish enthusiasts
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps) people who avoid risky foods, risk-averse diners
