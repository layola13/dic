mineralogists:/ˌmɪnərˈɑːlədʒɪsts/ | n. | 矿物学家 | A scientist who studies minerals, their composition, structure, properties, and occurrence. |  Mineralogists study the physical and chemical properties of minerals. 矿物学家研究矿物的物理和化学性质。

Mineralogists are working to discover new minerals. 矿物学家正在努力发现新的矿物。 | 近义词: geologists, geochemists | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun (plural)

Etymology: From French *minéralogiste*, from *minéral* (“mineral”) + *-logiste* (from Greek *-λόγος* (*-lógos*)).
