astrobiologist:/ˌæstrəʊbaɪˈɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 天体生物学家 | A scientist who studies the origin, evolution, distribution, and future of life in the universe. | Example: The astrobiologist was excited about the possibility of finding microbial life on Mars.  天体生物学家对在火星上发现微生物生命的可能性感到兴奋。| Example:  Astrobiologists are searching for signs of life beyond Earth. 天体生物学家正在寻找地球以外生命的迹象。| Synonym: exobiologist, space biologist | Antonym: N/A | Noun
