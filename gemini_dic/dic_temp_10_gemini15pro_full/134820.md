nonminerals:/ˌnɒnˈmɪnərəlz/
|n.| 非矿物质 |Substances that are not minerals, typically organic materials.  These are materials that are not formed by geological processes and do not have a crystalline structure.  |例句：Soil is composed of both minerals and nonminerals. 土壤由矿物质和非矿物质组成。|例句：Nonminerals like peat and compost improve soil structure. 像泥炭和堆肥这样的非矿物质改善了土壤结构。|近义词：organic matter, organic materials|反义词：minerals|词性:noun
