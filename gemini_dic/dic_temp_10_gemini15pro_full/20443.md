bitterer:/ˈbɪtərər/ | adj. | 更苦的，更痛苦的，更怨恨的 | More bitter than something else; comparative form of "bitter". | The coffee was bitterer than I expected. 这咖啡比我想象的更苦。|  The defeat felt even bitterer this time. 这次的失败感觉更加痛苦。 | 近义词:more bitter, harsher, more resentful | 反义词:sweeter, milder, less resentful | 词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *bitterer, comparative of *bitter, from Old English biter (“bitter”), from Proto-Germanic *bitraz (“biting, sharp”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰeyd- (“to split, bite, sharp”).
