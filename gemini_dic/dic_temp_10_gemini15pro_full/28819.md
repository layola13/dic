carousel:/ˌkærəˈsel/ | n. |旋转木马；行李传送带|A merry-go-round, a revolving circular platform with seats shaped like horses or other animals, typically used at amusement parks. Also, a conveyor belt at an airport for passengers to collect their luggage. |例句：The children loved riding the carousel at the fair. 孩子们喜欢在游乐场里坐旋转木马。

例句：I retrieved my suitcase from the baggage carousel. 我从行李传送带取回了我的手提箱。|近义词：merry-go-round, roundabout (for amusement park ride); conveyor belt, baggage claim (for airport usage) |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
