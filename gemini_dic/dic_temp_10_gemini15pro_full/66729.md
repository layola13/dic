exorcisation:/ˈɛksɔːrsaɪzˌeɪʃən/ /ˈɛksɔːrsaɪˌzeɪʃən/ | n. |驱魔，驱邪|The act of expelling an evil spirit from a person, place, or thing. |The priest performed an exorcisation on the possessed man.  神父对被附身的人进行了驱魔。|
|近义词:purification, cleansing, deliverance|反义词:possession, summoning|词性:noun|

exorcise:/ˈɛksɔːrsaɪz/ /ˈɛksɔːrsaɪz/ | v. |驱魔，驱邪;消除(坏念头、记忆等)|To drive out an evil spirit from a person, place, or thing; to rid oneself of something troublesome or unpleasant. |He tried to exorcise the demons from her mind. 他试图驱除她心中的恶魔。|He tried to exorcise the painful memories from his mind. 他努力想忘却痛苦的回忆。|
|近义词:expel, banish, purge|反义词:invoke, summon|词性:verb|
