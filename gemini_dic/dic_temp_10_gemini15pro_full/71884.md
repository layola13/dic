fleur:/flɜːr/ (US), /flɜː(r)/ (UK)| n. |花；精华；精英|A flower, especially one cultivated for its beauty. Also used figuratively to refer to the finest part or example of something.|例句：The fleur-de-lis is a stylized lily.  鸢尾花是一种程式化的百合花。

例句：She is the fleur of Southern womanhood. 她是南方女性的精华。|近义词：flower, blossom, bloom|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly weed, decay)|词性:noun
