overthrong:/ˌoʊvərˈθrɒŋ/ /ˌoʊvərˈθrɑːŋ/| vt. n.|过度拥挤，挤满|to fill a place with too many people or things|The market was overthronged with shoppers during the holiday season.  假日季节，市场里挤满了购物者。|
The narrow streets were overthronged with tourists. 狭窄的街道上挤满了游客。|
近义词： overcrowd, pack, jam, cram|反义词：deserted, empty|词性:动词, 名词

overthrong:/ˌoʊvərˈθrɒŋ/ /ˌoʊvərˈθrɑːŋ/| n.|过度拥挤，人群|a crowd of too many people|The overthrong made it difficult to move. 过度拥挤使得难以移动。|The overthrong at the concert was overwhelming.  演唱会上的人群拥挤不堪。|近义词：crowd, multitude, throng|反义词：emptiness, scarcity|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *overthringen, from Old English *oferþringan, from ofer- (over) + þringan (to press, throng).
