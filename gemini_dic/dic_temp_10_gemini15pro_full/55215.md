disses:/ˈdɪsɪz/
verb
侮辱，贬低，说…的坏话
To speak ill of; criticize, belittle, or disparage.
He disses his rivals in every interview. 他在每次采访中都贬低他的对手。
近义词:criticizes, insults, slams, puts down, badmouths
反义词:praises, compliments, lauds
词性:verb (third person singular present tense)


disses:/ˈdɪsɪz/
noun
侮辱，贬低，批评
An insult, criticism, or disparagement.
The rapper's new song is full of disses aimed at other artists.  这位说唱歌手的新歌充满了针对其他艺术家的侮辱。
近义词:insults, criticisms, put-downs, slams
反义词:compliments, praise
词性:noun (plural)


词源:
Shortened form of disrespect, from dis- (expressing negation) + respect.
