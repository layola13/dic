bonnily:/ˈbɒnɪli/| adv.|美好地，愉快地，漂亮地|In a cheerful, pleasant, or attractive manner.|She smiled bonnily at him. 她愉快地对他笑了笑。|近义词：cheerfully, pleasantly, attractively, prettily|反义词：unpleasantly, disagreeably, gloomily|词性: 副词

词源:
From bonny (attractive) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from the French word *bon* meaning "good".
