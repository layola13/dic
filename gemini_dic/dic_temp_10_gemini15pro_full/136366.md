nonrespondents:/ˌnɑːn.rɪˈspɑːn.dənts/
n.|未答复者，无应答者|People who do not respond to a survey, questionnaire, or request for information are called nonrespondents. |The survey had a high number of nonrespondents, making the results less reliable. 这项调查有很多未答复者，使得结果不太可靠。|Example:  Researchers are trying to understand why some people become nonrespondents in surveys. 研究人员正在试图理解为什么有些人会成为调查中的未回复者。

|Synonyms: non-participants, non-answerers|
|Antonyms: respondents, participants, answerers|
|noun|
