heathenishness:/ˈhiːðənɪʃnɪs/
n.
异教徒的野蛮行为；异教；不信教
The state or quality of being heathen; paganism; barbarity.
Missionaries often described the customs of indigenous peoples as heathenishness.  传教士经常将土著居民的风俗描述为异教徒的野蛮行为。
Their heathenishness offended the devout pilgrims. 他们的异教信仰冒犯了虔诚的朝圣者。
近义词：paganism, barbarity, savagery, ungodliness
反义词：piety, devoutness, religiousness
词性：noun
