aikido:/aɪˈkiːdoʊ/ | n. | 合气道 | A Japanese martial art that uses locks, holds, throws, and other techniques to defend oneself without injuring the attacker. |  Example 1: She has been practicing aikido for five years. 她练习合气道已经五年了。

Example 2: Aikido emphasizes blending with an attacker's movements rather than opposing them directly. 合气道强调与攻击者的动作融合，而不是直接对抗。| 近义词: martial art, jiu-jitsu | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Japanese 合気道 (aikidō), from 合 (ai, "harmony, joining, uniting") + 気 (ki, "spirit, energy, mood, morale") + 道 (dō, "way, path").
