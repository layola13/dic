bicuspidate:/baɪˈkʌspɪdeɪt/| adj.|二尖瓣的，有两尖的|Having two cusps or points, especially referring to a tooth or the mitral valve of the heart.|The bicuspid valve regulates blood flow between the left atrium and left ventricle of the heart.  二尖瓣调节左心房和左心室之间的血流。|The dentist examined my bicuspid teeth. 牙医检查了我的二尖牙。|Synonyms: bicuspid, two-cusped, double-pointed|Antonyms: tricuspid (specifically for valves), multi-cusped|adjective
