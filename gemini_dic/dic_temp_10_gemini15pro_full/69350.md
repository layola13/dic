federally:/ˈfɛdərəli/
adjective
联邦地；由联邦政府
In a federal manner; by the federal government.
The program is federally funded.  这个项目是由联邦政府资助的。
The issue was federally regulated.  这个问题是由联邦政府监管的。
Synonym:nationally, centrally
Antonym:locally, state
