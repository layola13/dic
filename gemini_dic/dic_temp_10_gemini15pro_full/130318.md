non-tenant:/ˌnɒnˈtɛnənt/ | n. | 非租户，非承租人 |  A person or entity that does not hold a tenancy agreement for a particular property.  Someone who does not rent or lease a specific space. | The non-tenant was asked to leave the premises.  非租户被要求离开该场所。

The building owner is responsible for repairs, even for damage caused by non-tenants.  即使是非租户造成的损害，业主也有责任进行维修。 | 近义词: outsider, visitor, guest | 反义词: tenant, lessee, renter | 词性: 名词
