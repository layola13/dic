muffs:/mʌfs/| n.| 手套，保暖套；(尤指棒球、板球或垒球)接球失误 |A pair of thick, soft coverings for the hands, usually worn in cold weather. Also, an act of failing to catch a ball, especially in baseball, cricket, or softball.| She wore a pair of fluffy muffs to keep her hands warm. 她戴着一副毛茸茸的保暖套来保暖。 He made a crucial muff in the final inning. 他在最后一局犯了一个关键的接球失误。|近义词：mittens, gloves (for hand coverings); error, blunder (for dropping a ball) |反义词：catch (for dropping a ball)|名词


muff:/mʌf/| vt.| 弄糟，搞砸；接球失误 |To handle clumsily or badly; to fail to catch a ball, especially in baseball, cricket, or softball.| He completely muffed his lines in the play.  他在剧中完全搞砸了他的台词。The outfielder muffed an easy fly ball. 外野手漏接了一个简单的飞球。|近义词：bungle, botch, fumble|反义词：succeed, achieve, catch|动词


词源:  From Middle Dutch *mof or *muffe, of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to Old French moufle, meaning "mitten."
