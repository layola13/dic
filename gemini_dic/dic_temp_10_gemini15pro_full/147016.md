overlarding: /ˌoʊvərˈlɑːrdɪŋ/ | n. | 过度装饰；过分渲染；添加过多不必要的细节 | The act of adding too much detail or embellishment to something, often to the point of obscuring the main point or making it seem excessive. | His writing style is characterized by overlarding with technical jargon. 他的写作风格特点是充斥着过多的专业术语。 | 近义词： embellishing, exaggerating, overdoing, padding | 反义词: simplifying, streamlining | 词性: noun

overlarding: /ˌoʊvərˈlɑːrdɪŋ/ | v. | 过度装饰；过分渲染；添加过多不必要的细节 | To add too much detail or embellishment to something, often to the point of obscuring the main point or making it seem excessive. | He overlarded his speech with quotations and anecdotes. 他的演讲中引用和轶事过多。| 近义词： embellish, exaggerate, overdo, pad | 反义词: simplify, streamline | 词性: verb


词源: From over- + lard (verb), originally referring to covering meat with lard.  Figurative sense of "overloading" or "over-embellishing" developed later.
