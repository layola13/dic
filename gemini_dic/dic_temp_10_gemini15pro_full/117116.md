|adj.|说话吞吞吐吐的，拐弯抹角的|He gave a mealy-mouthed response to the journalist's question. 他对记者的提问给出了一个含糊其辞的回答。|The mealy-mouthed statement from the company did little to reassure investors.  公司那份拐弯抹角的声明并没有让投资者安心。|近义词：evasiveness, indirectness, equivocation|反义词：directness, frankness, candor|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From mealy (covered with meal, soft) + mouthed.  Implies having a mouth soft as if filled with meal, hence unwilling to speak plainly.  The first recorded use is from the late 16th century.
