parallelogram: /ˌpærəˈlɛləɡræm/ | n. | 平行四边形 | A quadrilateral with two pairs of parallel sides. |  A rhombus is a special type of parallelogram.  菱形是一种特殊的平行四边形。| synonyms: quadrilateral, tetragon | antonyms:  N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *parallelogrammum, from Greek parallēlogrammon, from parallēlos "parallel" + grammē "line."
