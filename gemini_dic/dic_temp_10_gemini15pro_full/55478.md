distracted: /dɪˈstræktɪd/ | adj. | 心烦意乱的；注意力分散的 | Unable to concentrate because one's mind is preoccupied. |  I was so distracted by the noise that I couldn't study. 我被噪音弄得心烦意乱，无法学习。

distracted: /dɪˈstræktɪd/ | adj. | 分心的 | Having one's attention diverted. | The distracted driver nearly caused an accident.  分心的司机差点酿成事故。


近义词: preoccupied, inattentive, diverted, absent-minded
反义词: focused, attentive, concentrated
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *distractus*, past participle of *distrahere*  "to draw apart, divide, separate," from *dis-* "apart" + *trahere* "to draw."
