modelize:/ˈmɒdəlaɪz/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdəlaɪz/ (US)| v. | 建模，模型化| To create a model of something, often a mathematical or computational model, to understand its behavior or predict its future states.  Represent something in the form of a model. |Researchers modelized the spread of the virus to predict its impact. 研究人员对病毒的传播进行了建模，以预测其影响。

The engineers modelized the bridge's structure to test its stability. 工程师们对桥梁的结构进行了建模，以测试其稳定性。

|近义词：model, simulate, represent, emulate|反义词: N/A|词性: verb
