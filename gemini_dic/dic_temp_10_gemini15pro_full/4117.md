aino:/ˈaɪnoʊ/ | n. | 阿伊努人；阿伊努语 | A member of an indigenous people of northern Japan, also formerly of Sakhalin Island and the Kuril Islands. Also, the language of this people. | Example: The Ainu people have a rich cultural heritage.  阿伊努人拥有丰富的文化遗产。

Example:  She is studying the Ainu language. 她在学习阿伊努语。 | Synonym: Ainuic | Antonym: N/A | Noun
