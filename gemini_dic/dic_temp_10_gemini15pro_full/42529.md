countermand:/ˌkaʊntərˈmænd/ | v. | 撤销，取消（命令）；收回（成命）| To revoke or cancel (a command); to recall or order back. | The general countermanded the order to attack. 将军撤销了进攻的命令。| The CEO countermanded the earlier instructions, causing confusion among the staff. 首席执行官撤销了先前的指示，在员工中引起了混乱。 | 近义词: revoke, rescind, cancel, annul, repeal | 反义词: order, command, direct, authorize | 词性: 动词

countermand:/ˌkaʊntərˈmænd/ | n. | 取消；撤回的命令 | An order revoking a previous command. | The countermand arrived just in time to prevent the disastrous mission. 取消命令及时到达，阻止了灾难性的任务。|  The captain received a countermand from headquarters, halting the deployment of troops.  船长收到了总部的取消命令，停止了部队的部署。 | 近义词: revocation, cancellation | 反义词: order, command | 词性: 名词


词源:
From late Middle English: from Old French contremander, from contre- ‘against’ + mander ‘to order’.
