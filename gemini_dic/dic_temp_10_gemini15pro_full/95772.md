incontestable:/ˌɪnkənˈtɛstəb(ə)l/
adjective
无可争辩的; 不容置疑的
not able to be disputed or questioned
Her logic was incontestable. 她的逻辑无可争辩。
The evidence against him was incontestable.  针对他的证据不容置疑。
近义词:undeniable, irrefutable, indisputable, unchallengeable
反义词:disputable, questionable, debatable, challengeable
词性:adjective

incontestably:/ˌɪnkənˈtɛstəbli/
adverb
无可争辩地; 不容置疑地
in a way that cannot be disputed or questioned.
He is incontestably the best player in the world. 他无可争辩地是世界上最好的球员。
The results of the election were incontestably fair. 选举结果无可争辩地公平。
近义词:undeniably, irrefutably, indisputably, unchallengeably
反义词:disputably, questionably, debatably, challengeably
词性:adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + contestable (capable of being contested).  Contestable comes from the Old French contester, and ultimately from the Latin contestari (to call to witness).
