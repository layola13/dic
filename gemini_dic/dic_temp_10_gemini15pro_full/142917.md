out-shaping:/ˌaʊtˈʃeɪpɪŋ/
|v.|胜过；超越；优于；比...更有型|Surpassing or exceeding something or someone in shape, form, or design; being superior in aesthetic form.|The new sports car out-shapes all its competitors. 这款新的跑车比所有竞争对手都更有型。|近义词：surpassing, exceeding, outperforming, outclassing|反义词: inferior to, lagging behind|词性:动词

out-shaping:/ˌaʊtˈʃeɪpɪŋ/
|adj.|（指某物）比...更有型的；优于...的|Having a superior shape, form, or design compared to something else.|The out-shaping design of the new building made it a landmark. 这座新建筑优越的造型使其成为地标。|近义词：superior, better-shaped, more stylish|反义词: inferior, less stylish, poorly designed|词性:形容词


词源: "out-" (prefix meaning exceeding or surpassing) + "shaping" (present participle of the verb "shape")
