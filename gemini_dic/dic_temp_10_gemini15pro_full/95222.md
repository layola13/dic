inbred:/ˈɪnbrɛd/ (US), /ˈɪnbrɛd/ (UK)| adj.|近亲繁殖的；天生的；根深蒂固的|Bred from closely related individuals, especially over many generations; innate; firmly established.|The isolated community developed a number of inbred genetic disorders.  这个与世隔绝的社区出现了一些近亲繁殖的遗传疾病。

The dog's fear of loud noises is inbred. 这只狗对巨大声响的恐惧是天生的。|Synonyms: congenital, inherent, ingrained, innate|Antonyms: acquired, learned|Adjective


inbred:/ˈɪnbrɛd/ (US), /ˈɪnbrɛd/ (UK)| n.|近亲繁殖的动植物|An animal or plant produced by inbreeding.|The inbreds were more susceptible to disease. 这些近亲繁殖的动植物更容易生病。|Synonyms:  (no single perfect synonym, depends on context - could be mutant, hybrid depending on usage) |Antonyms:  (no single perfect synonym, depends on context - could be crossbreed, outcross)|Noun
