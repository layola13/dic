misunderstandingly:/ˌmɪsʌndərˈstændɪŋli/
adv.
误解地，由于误解
Due to a misunderstanding; in a way that shows or results from a misunderstanding.
She misunderstandingly took his silence as disapproval. 她误解了他的沉默，认为他不赞成。
近义词: mistakenly, erroneously
反义词: correctly, accurately
词性: 副词

Etymology:
From misunderstanding + -ly.  Misunderstanding itself comes from mis- (prefix indicating error) + understand + -ing (suffix forming a noun).  Ultimately from Old English understandan, from under + standan "to stand."
