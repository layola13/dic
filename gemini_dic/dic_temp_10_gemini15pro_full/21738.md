boasting: /ˈboʊstɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbəʊstɪŋ/ (UK)
|n./ v./ adj.| 吹嘘，夸耀；自夸；值得夸耀的事物|  act of bragging or boasting; something to be proud of. |He was known for his boasting about his wealth. 他以吹嘘自己的财富而闻名。|近义词：bragging, vaunting, crowing|反义词：modesty, humility|词性: noun/gerund or present participle

boast: /boʊst/ (US) /bəʊst/ (UK)
|v. / n.| 夸口说，吹嘘；拥有（值得自豪的东西）|speak with excessive pride and self-satisfaction about one's achievements, possessions, or abilities. |He boasted that he could speak six languages. 他吹嘘自己会说六种语言。|近义词：brag, vaunt, crow|反义词：deprecate, downplay|词性: verb/noun


Etymology: From Middle English *bosten*, from Old French *boster*, of uncertain origin.
