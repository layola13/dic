coextend:/ˌkoʊɪkˈstɛnd/| v. |共同延伸，同时扩展|To extend over the same space or time period as something else.|例句：The Roman Empire coextended with the Pax Romana. 罗马帝国与罗马和平时期同时存在。

例句：Their influence coextended throughout the region. 他们的影响力遍及整个地区。|近义词：coincide, overlap, synchronize|反义词:diverge, separate|词性:verb
