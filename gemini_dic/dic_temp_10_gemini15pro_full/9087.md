antimodernization:/ˌæntiˌmɑːdərnɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 反现代化 | Opposition to modernization, often involving resistance to technological and social changes. |  The Amish community's antimodernization stance is reflected in their rejection of cars and electricity. 阿米什社区的反现代化立场体现在他们拒绝汽车和电力。

Antimodernization sentiments fueled the protests against the new highway project. 反现代化的情绪加剧了对新高速公路项目的抗议。 | 近义词: traditionalism, conservatism | 反义词: modernization, progress | 词性: 名词
