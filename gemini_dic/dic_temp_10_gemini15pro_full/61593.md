emeriti:/ɪˈmɛrɪtaɪ/
n.
退休人员；荣誉退休人员
retired professors or ministers entitled to retain their title as an honor
Example: The university's emeriti faculty were invited to the commencement ceremony.  大学的退休教授们被邀请参加毕业典礼。

Example: The emeriti professors still contribute to the academic community. 退休的教授们仍然为学术界做出贡献。

近义词:retired, emeritus (singular)
反义词:employed, active
词性:noun (plural)

emeritus:/ɪˈmɛrɪtəs/
adj. n.
退休的，荣誉退休的；荣誉退休者
(of a professor, minister, etc.) retired but allowed to retain their title as an honor.
Example: Professor Smith is now emeritus professor of history. 史密斯教授现在是历史系荣誉退休教授。

Example: He was appointed emeritus director of the museum. 他被任命为博物馆的荣誉退休馆长。

近义词:retired
反义词:active
词性:adjective, noun
