一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I'll go to the store tomorrow.  我明天会去商店。
I'll see you later.  回头见。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I'll be working on this project all day tomorrow.  我明天一整天都会在做这个项目。
This time next week, I'll be lying on a beach. 下周这个时候，我会躺在沙滩上。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By the time you arrive, I'll have finished dinner.  等你到了，我已经吃完晚饭了。
I'll have completed the report by Friday. 我会在周五前完成报告。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next year, I'll have been living here for five years.  到明年，我在这里住了五年了。
By the time the movie starts, I'll have been waiting for an hour.  电影开始的时候，我已经等了一个小时了。


虚拟语气:
I'd go if I could, but I'll be working. 如果可以的话我会去的，但我得工作。
I told him I'll help him if he needs it. 我告诉他如果需要的话我会帮他。


被动语态:
N/A - 'i'll' is not used in passive voice constructions.


疑问句:
(Technically "will I..." is the question form, but 'i'll' can appear in indirect questions)
He asked if I'll be there. 他问我是否会在那里。
She wondered if I'll bring the cake. 她想知道我是否会带蛋糕。


否定句:
I'll not (I won't) be attending the meeting. 我不会参加会议。
I'll not (I won't) tolerate this behavior. 我不会容忍这种行为。

注意事项:
The contraction "i'll" is informal and generally used in spoken language or casual writing.  In formal writing, "I will" or "I shall" is preferred. It originates from the contraction of "I will" or "I shall," with "shall" being more archaic and less common in modern English.
