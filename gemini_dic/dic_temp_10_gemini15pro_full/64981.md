estivation:/ˌɛstɪˈveɪʃən/ | n. | 夏眠；[动]夏蛰 |A state of dormancy or inactivity during the summer, similar to hibernation but taking place in hot, dry periods.  |  Example: The desert tortoise enters estivation during the hottest months of the year. 沙漠龟在一年中最热的几个月进入夏眠状态。

Example:  Estivation helps some animals survive extreme heat and drought. 夏眠帮助一些动物在酷热和干旱中生存。 | 近义词: dormancy, inactivity, torpor | 反义词: activity, hibernation | 词性: noun
