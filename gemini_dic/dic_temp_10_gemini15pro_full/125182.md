multicarinate:/ˌmʌltiˈkærɪneɪt/| adj.|多脊的，多隆线的|Having many carinae or ridges, especially on the shells of mollusks or the bones of vertebrates.|The newly discovered fossil was multicarinate, with several distinct ridges along its shell.|近义词：multiridged, multicostate|反义词:smooth, unridged|adjective
