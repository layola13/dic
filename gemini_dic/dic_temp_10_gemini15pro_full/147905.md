overreflection:/ˌoʊvərɪˈflekʃən/ | n. | 过度反思，过度反省 | Excessive reflection or contemplation, often leading to indecision or anxiety. |  He was paralyzed by overreflection and couldn't make a simple choice. 他因过度反思而不知所措，无法做出一个简单的选择。

She suffered from overreflection, constantly analyzing her past actions. 她饱受过度反思的困扰，不断分析自己过去的行为。 | 近义词：rumination, brooding, introspection | 反义词：impulsiveness, spontaneity | 词性: noun
