abstractedness: /æbˈstræktɪdnəs/ /æbˈstræktɪdnɪs/
noun
抽象性；心不在焉
The state of being abstracted; preoccupation.  The quality of dealing with ideas rather than events.
He stared out the window, lost in abstractedness. 他凝视着窗外，陷入了沉思。
Her abstractedness during the meeting was noticeable.  她在会议期间的心不在焉很明显。
近义词: abstraction, detachment, preoccupation, remoteness, absorption
反义词: concreteness, attention, presence
词性: noun

词源: From abstract + -ed + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin abstractus.
