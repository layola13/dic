borehole:/ˈbɔːrhəʊl/ (UK) /ˈbɔːrhoʊl/ (US)| n. |钻孔，井眼|A hole drilled in the earth, especially to locate or extract water, oil, or other substances.|例句：They drilled a borehole to test for oil. 他们钻了一个钻孔来勘探石油。

例句：The borehole provided a steady supply of water for the village. 这口井为村庄提供了稳定的水源。

近义词：well, shaft, pit|反义词: mound, hill |词性: noun
