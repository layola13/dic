breathalysing:/ˈbrɛθəlaɪzɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈbrɛθəˌlaɪzɪŋ/ (US)| n.| 呼气测试；酒精测试|The act of testing someone's breath to determine the amount of alcohol in their blood.|The police officer conducted a breathalysing test on the driver. 警察对司机进行了呼气测试。|Synonyms: breath test, alcohol test|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider sobriety test)|noun


breathalyse:/ˈbrɛθəlaɪz/ (UK) /ˈbrɛθəˌlaɪz/ (US)| v.| 对…进行呼气测试；对…进行酒精测试|To test someone's breath to determine the amount of alcohol in their blood.|The driver was breathalysed at the scene of the accident.  司机在事故现场接受了呼气测试。|Synonyms: breath test, alcohol test|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym)|verb
