consequent:/ˈkɒnsɪkwənt/ (British), /ˈkɑːnsɪkwent/ (American)| adj. n.|随之发生的，作为结果的；结果，推论|resulting from something that has happened previously; a result or conclusion|例句：The drought had a consequent effect on agricultural production. 干旱对农业生产造成了相应的影响。|近义词：resulting, ensuing, subsequent, resultant|反义词：antecedent, prior, preceding|词性:形容词

consequent:/ˈkɒnsɪkwənt/ (British), /ˈkɑːnsɪkwent/ (American)| n.|结果，推论|a result or conclusion|例句：One consequent of the decision was a drop in sales.  这个决定的一个结果就是销售额下降。|近义词：result, outcome, effect, consequence|反义词：cause, origin, antecedent|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *consequentem, the accusative singular of consequens, present participle of consequi (“to follow closely”), from con- (“with, together”) + sequi (“to follow”).
