along-with:/əˈlɔŋ wɪð/ | prep. | 连同，以及，和...一起 |  Used to indicate accompaniment or inclusion. It means "in company with" or "together with." | I went to the store along with my sister. 我和我的妹妹一起去商店。| 近义词: together with, in addition to, as well as, accompanied by | 反义词: without, excluding, apart from | 词性: 介词
