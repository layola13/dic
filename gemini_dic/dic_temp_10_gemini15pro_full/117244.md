meccans:/ˈmɛkənz/
n.|麦加人；麦加居民|People from Mecca, a city in Saudi Arabia considered the holiest city in Islam.|例句：The Meccans welcomed the pilgrims. 麦加人欢迎朝圣者。|例句：Many Meccans participate in the Hajj each year. 每年都有许多麦加人参加朝圣。|近义词：inhabitants of Mecca, residents of Mecca|反义词:Non-Meccans|词性:noun

Etymology: Derived from Mecca + -an.  "Mecca" itself comes from the Arabic Makkah (مكة).
