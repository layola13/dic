broach:/broʊtʃ/| n. |拉刀，胸针|A pointed tool used for shaping or enlarging a hole.  A decorative pin worn on clothing.|She wore an antique broach on her lapel. 她在外套翻领上佩戴了一枚古董胸针。|synonyms: pin, brooch, ornament|antonyms: N/A|noun
broach:/broʊtʃ/| vt. |提出，开始讨论，开启|To raise (a sensitive or difficult subject) for discussion. To pierce (a cask, etc.) in order to draw the contents; to open for the first time.|He finally broached the subject of their divorce. 他终于提出了他们离婚的话题。|synonyms: raise, introduce, moot, open, initiate|antonyms: avoid, evade, ignore, conceal|verb
