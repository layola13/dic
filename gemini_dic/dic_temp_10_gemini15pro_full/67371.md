extensometer:/ɪkˈstɛnsɒmɪtər/ (US) /ɪkˈstensɒmɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |伸长计，应变仪|An instrument used to measure the elongation or extension of a material under stress or strain. |例句：The extensometer recorded a significant increase in length as the tensile force was applied.  拉伸力施加时，伸长计记录了长度的显著增加。

例句：Engineers use extensometers to determine the material's properties, such as Young's modulus. 工程师使用伸长计来确定材料的特性，例如杨氏模量。|近义词：strain gauge, dilatometer|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
