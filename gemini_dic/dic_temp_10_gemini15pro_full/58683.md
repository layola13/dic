dump-on:/ˈdʌmp ɑːn/ | phrasal verb | 严厉批评；斥责；对…发泄(怒气、不满等) | to criticize someone severely, often unfairly; to treat someone badly |  The boss dumped on me for the mistake, even though it wasn't my fault. 老板因为这个错误严厉批评了我，即使那不是我的错。

| Example: He always dumps on his assistant when things go wrong.  当事情出错时，他总是斥责他的助手。 | Synonym: criticize, berate, lambaste, unload on | Antonym: praise, commend, compliment | verb phrase


dump-on (noun form, less common): /ˈdʌmp ɑːn/ | n. |  斥责；批评 | an instance of harsh criticism | Example: He gave me a real dump-on about the report. 他就这份报告狠狠地批评了我一顿。 | Synonym: criticism, tirade, reprimand | Antonym: praise, commendation | noun
