parlourmaids:/ˈpɑːləmeɪdz/ | n. | 客厅女仆；客厅侍女 | A female servant employed to clean and tidy the parlour (a sitting room or living room) in a large house. |  The parlourmaids dusted the furniture and polished the silver every morning. 客厅女仆每天早上都要掸掉家具上的灰尘并擦拭银器。| synonyms: maids, housemaids, servants | antonyms: masters, employers | noun
