overdo:/ˌoʊvərˈduː/ /ˌəʊvəˈduː/| v.|过度；做过头；夸张|to do something too much or in an extreme way|例句：She really overdid the makeup for the party. 她参加派对的妆容太浓了。

例句：Don't overdo the exercise, or you'll hurt yourself. 不要过度运动，否则会受伤。

近义词：exaggerate, overact, overstate|反义词: underdo|词性:动词

overdo:/ˌoʊvərˈdʌn/ /ˌəʊvəˈdʌn/| adj.|过度的；做得过分的；夸张的|cooked for too long|例句：The steak was overdone and dry. 牛排烤得太久了，很干。

例句：Her acting was a bit overdone in that scene. 她在那一幕的表演有点过火。

近义词：excessive, exaggerated, overblown|反义词: underdone|词性:形容词
