checkerberry:/ˈtʃɛkərˌbɛri/ | n. |冬青果, 冬青, 茶莓|A small creeping evergreen shrub (Gaultheria procumbens) of eastern North America, having white bell-shaped flowers, spicy red berries, and aromatic leaves that yield wintergreen oil. |例句：The checkerberry plant is a common sight in the forests of New England. 冬青植物在新英格兰的森林里很常见。
