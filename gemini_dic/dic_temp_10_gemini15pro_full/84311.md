half-willful:/ˌhæfˈwɪlfl/ | adj. | 半故意的；有些任性的 | Partly or somewhat willful; done with a degree of intention but not fully deliberate. |  He gave her a half-willful, half-appealing look. 他半故意地、半恳求地看了她一眼。| Near synonyms: semivoluntary, semideliberate, partially intentional | Near antonyms: unintentional, accidental, involuntary | adjective
