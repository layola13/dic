comestible:/ˈkɒmɪstɪb(ə)l/ (US) /kəˈmɛstəbəl/| adj. n.|可食用的；食物|Something that is comestible is safe or fit to be eaten.|These berries are comestible, but those are poisonous. 这些浆果可以食用，但那些有毒。|近义词：edible, palatable|反义词：inedible, poisonous|词性: 形容词, 名词
comestible:/ˈkɒmɪstɪb(ə)l/ (US) /kəˈmɛstəbəl/| n. |食物|A comestible is an item of food.|The banquet included a variety of comestibles, from roasted meats to delicate pastries. 宴会上摆满了各种各样的食物，从烤肉到精致的糕点。|近义词：food, victuals, edibles|反义词：None (as a noun)|词性: 名词

词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French, or from late Latin comestibilis, from Latin comesus ‘eaten’, past participle of comedere, from com- ‘together’ + edere ‘eat’.
