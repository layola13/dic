disrespectfully:/ˌdɪsrɪˈspektfəli/ | adv. |不敬地，失礼地 | Showing a lack of respect; impolitely. | He spoke disrespectfully to his elders. 他对长辈说话很不尊重。|  She tossed the invitation aside disrespectfully. 她不尊重地把邀请函扔到一边。 | 近义词: rudely, impolitely, contemptuously, insolently | 反义词: respectfully, politely, courteously | 词性: 副词

disrespectful:/ˌdɪsrɪˈspektfəl/ | adj. | 不尊重的，失礼的| Exhibiting a lack of respect. |  It was disrespectful of him to interrupt the speaker. 他打断演讲者是不尊重的行为。|  Her disrespectful behavior towards the teacher earned her a detention. 她对老师的不尊重行为使她被留校。 | 近义词: rude, impolite, contemptuous, insolent | 反义词: respectful, polite, courteous | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From dis- (expressing negation) + respectful, ultimately from Latin respectus "regard," past participle of respicere "look back at, regard."
