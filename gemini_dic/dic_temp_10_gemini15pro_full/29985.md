cavillous: /ˈkævələs/ | adj. | 吹毛求疵的，挑剔的 | Raising trivial objections; quibbling. |  He made a few cavillous remarks about the food, but overall he enjoyed the meal.  他對食物提出了一些吹毛求疵的意見，但總體來說他很享受這頓飯。 | Synonyms: carping, nitpicking, faultfinding, critical, hypercritical | Antonyms: uncritical, accepting, tolerant,  approving | adjective

Etymology:  From Late Latin *cavillōsus, from Latin cavillārī (“to jest, to scoff”),  frequentative of cavilla (“jest, raillery”).
