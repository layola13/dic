eluding:/ɪˈluːdɪŋ/
verb
逃避; 躲避; 使…无法理解; 使…无法得到
avoiding or escaping from someone or something by cunning or skill; failing to be grasped or remembered.
The cause of the disease has been eluding researchers for years.  这种疾病的病因多年来一直困扰着研究人员。
近义词: evading, escaping, avoiding, dodging
反义词: facing, confronting, meeting, encountering
词性: Verb (present participle of elude)
