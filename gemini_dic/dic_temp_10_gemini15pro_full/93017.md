idiotic:/ˌɪdiˈɒtɪk/ (US) /ˌɪdiˈɑːdɪk/| adj. |愚蠢的，白痴的|extremely stupid or foolish|例句：That was an idiotic thing to do. 那是一件愚蠢的事情。|例句：It was idiotic of him to leave his keys in the car. 他把钥匙留在车里真是太蠢了。|近义词：stupid, foolish, silly, absurd, ridiculous, asinine, moronic|反义词：intelligent, sensible, wise, smart|词性:形容词

词源:
From idiot + -ic.  Ultimately from the Greek *ἰδιώτης (idiṓtēs) meaning "private person, layman, ignorant person".
