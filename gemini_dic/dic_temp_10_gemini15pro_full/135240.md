nonpalatals:/nɒnˈpælətəlz/
|adj.|非腭音的|Sounds that are not produced with the tongue near or touching the hard palate.|The sounds /t/, /d/, and /k/ are nonpalatal consonants. /t/、/d/和/k/都是非腭音辅音。|近义词:velar, guttural|反义词:palatal|词性:adjective
