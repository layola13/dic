fallaciously:/fəˈleɪʃəsli/
adverb
谬误地；虚妄地
in a fallacious manner; based on a fallacy.
He fallaciously argued that because some criminals are poor, all poor people are criminals. 他错误地认为，因为有些罪犯很穷，所以所有穷人都很罪犯。
fallaciously claimed the earth was flat.  他错误地声称地球是平的。
近义词: erroneously, wrongly, falsely, invalidly
反义词: correctly, validly, truthfully, accurately
词性: adverb

词源:
From Late Latin *fallaciosus, from Latin fallacia (“deceit, trickery, a trick”), from fallax (“deceiving, deceitful”), from fallere (“to deceive, trip, cause to fall”).
