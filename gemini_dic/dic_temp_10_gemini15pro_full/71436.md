flak:/flæk/
noun
高射炮; 指责; 批评
anti-aircraft fire. strong criticism.
The pilot was worried about the amount of flak coming from the ground. 飞行员担心来自地面的高射炮火。
He took a lot of flak for his decision. 他的决定受到了很多批评。
Synonyms: criticism, censure, disapproval, condemnation, abuse, attack, fire, barrage
Antonyms: praise, acclaim, approval
n.
