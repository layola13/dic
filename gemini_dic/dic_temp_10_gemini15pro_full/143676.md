outlaughing: /ˌaʊtˈlæfɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的笑;笑得比…更厉害
Outlaughing someone means laughing longer or louder than them.
He outlaughed everyone in the room with his infectious laughter. 他富有感染力的笑声盖过了房间里所有人的笑声。
近义词: outchuckling, outguffawing
反义词: being outdone in laughter (没有直接的反义词)
词性: verb (动词)
