crease: /kriːs/ | v. |  使起皱纹，弄皱；起皱纹 | To make a crease in something. | He creased his brow in concentration. 他皱着眉头专注思考。 | synonym: wrinkle, crinkle, rumple | antonym: smooth, iron, flatten | verb
