obliging:/əˈblaɪdʒɪŋ/
adjective
乐于助人的；恳切的
willing to do favors or be helpful to others.
例句：The staff were very obliging and helpful.  工作人员非常乐于助人，而且很帮忙。

例句：He was obliging enough to show us the way. 他很热心地给我们指路。

近义词：helpful, accommodating, kind, considerate, amiable
反义词：unhelpful, disobliging, unaccommodating, rude

词性
adjective
