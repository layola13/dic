parahormone:/ˈpærəhɔːrmoʊn/| n. |旁泌性激素，类激素|A substance that acts like a hormone but is not produced by an endocrine gland. It is produced by tissues and acts locally.|例句：Histamine is considered a parahormone. 组胺被认为是一种旁泌性激素。

例句：Prostaglandins are important parahormones that regulate various physiological processes. 前列腺素是调节各种生理过程的重要旁泌性激素。|近义词：autacoid, local hormone|反义词:hormone (in the classical sense)|词性:noun
