interjugular:/ˌɪntərˈdʒʌɡjʊlər/| adj.|颈静脉间的|Situated between the jugular veins.|The interjugular notch is a small depression in the superior border of the manubrium of the sternum, between the clavicular notches.|近义词:suprasternal|反义词:None|词性:adjective
Etymology: inter- (Latin, between) + jugular (from Latin jugulum, throat)
