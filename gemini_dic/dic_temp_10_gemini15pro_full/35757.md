cochrane:/ˈkɒkrən/ (UK) /ˈkɑːkrən/ (US)| n. adj.|科克伦；科克伦的|Referring to Archie Cochrane, a Scottish physician and epidemiologist known for his work in evidence-based medicine, or the Cochrane Collaboration, an international non-profit organization that produces systematic reviews of health interventions.|例句：The Cochrane review found no evidence to support the use of this treatment. 科克伦综述没有发现支持使用这种疗法的证据。

例句：He is a member of the Cochrane Collaboration. 他是科克伦协作网的成员。|近义词：systematic review, meta-analysis|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially anecdotal evidence)|词性: noun, adjective
