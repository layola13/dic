deconstruct:/ˌdiːkənˈstrʌkt/
verb
解构，拆解
To analyze (something, such as a text or work of art) by examining its internal elements and their relationships.  To reduce something to its constituent elements in order to reinterpret it.
例句：The professor deconstructed the poem, revealing its hidden meanings. 教授解构了这首诗，揭示了它隐藏的含义。

例句：The critic attempted to deconstruct the film's narrative.  评论家试图解构这部电影的叙事。

近义词：analyze, dissect, examine, interpret
反义词：construct, synthesize, build
词性 v.
