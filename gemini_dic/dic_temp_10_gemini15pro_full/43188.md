crabbedness:/ˈkræbɪdnəs/
|n.|
|暴躁，乖戾，难懂|
|The state or quality of being crabbed; bad temper; peevishness; difficulty; obscurity.|
|His crabbedness made him difficult to work with. 他的暴躁使他很难与人共事。|
|近义词：grumpiness, irritability, irascibility, surliness, cantankerousness, churlishness, ill-temper, crossness, peevishness, acrimony, asperity, sourness, sullenness, moroseness, dourness, gloominess, testiness, petulance, pique, huffiness, touchiness,  fractiousness|
|反义词：good temper, amiability, affability, geniality, good nature, pleasantness, agreeableness, cheerfulness, good humor, joviality,  affability|


crabbed:/ˈkræbd/
|adj.|
|暴躁的，乖戾的，难懂的|
|Bad-tempered and irritable; (of handwriting) cramped and illegible; (of style) complex and obscure.|
|The old man's crabbed handwriting was almost impossible to decipher.  这位老人的潦草笔迹几乎无法辨认。|
|近义词：grumpy, irritable, irascible, surly, cantankerous, churlish, ill-tempered, cross, peevish, acrimonious, asperity, sour, sullen, morose, dour, gloomy, testy, petulant, piqued, huffy, touchy,  fractious|
|反义词：good-tempered, amiable, affable, genial, good-natured, pleasant, agreeable, cheerful, good-humored, jovial|
