cataract:/ˈkætərækt/ (US) /ˈkætərækt/ (UK)| n.|白内障; 瀑布; 洪水; 大雨|A medical condition in which the lens of the eye becomes progressively opaque, resulting in blurred vision.|例句：His eyesight was failing because of cataracts. 他的视力因白内障而衰退。

例句：The majestic cataract thundered down the mountainside. 雄伟的瀑布从山坡上倾泻而下。|近义词：waterfall, cascade (for waterfall); opacity, cloudiness (for medical condition)|反义词: clarity (for medical condition)|词性:noun

例句：The village was flooded after a week of cataracts.  村庄在一周的大雨之后被淹没了。|近义词：downpour, deluge, torrent|反义词: drizzle|词性:noun
