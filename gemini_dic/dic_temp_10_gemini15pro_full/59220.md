dystopianism:/dɪsˈtoʊpiənɪzəm/
n.
反乌托邦主义；反乌托邦思想
An imagined state or society in which people lead wretched and dehumanized lives, typically one controlled by a totalitarian state.  A philosophy or ideology that promotes such a society.
The novel explores themes of dystopianism and control. 这部小说探讨了反乌托邦主义和控制的主题。
近义词:anti-utopianism
反义词:utopianism
词性:noun


dystopian:/dɪsˈtoʊpiən/
adj.
反乌托邦的；反面乌托邦的
Relating to or denoting an imagined place or state in which everything is unpleasant or bad, typically a totalitarian or environmentally degraded one.
The film presents a dystopian vision of the future. 这部电影展现了对未来的反乌托邦式愿景。
近义词:anti-utopian
反义词:utopian
词性:adjective
