gria:/ˈɡriːə/ | n. | 格里奥人（西非的传统吟游诗人、赞颂者、口述历史学家、谱系学家、仲裁者、赞美者或战士，有时是所有这些）| A griot is a West African historian, storyteller, praise singer, poet and/or musician.  The griot is a repository of oral tradition and is often seen as a societal leader due to their position as an advisor to royal families. |  The griot told stories of ancient kings and heroes.  格里奥人讲述了古代国王和英雄的故事。

The griot played the kora and sang praises of the village chief.  格里奥人弹奏科拉琴，歌颂村长的功绩。 | 近义词:bard, storyteller, poet, musician | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
