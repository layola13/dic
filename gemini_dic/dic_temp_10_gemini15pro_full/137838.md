nonvicariousness:/ˌnɒnvaɪˈkɛəriəsnəs/ | n. | 非替代性；不可替代性 | The state or quality of not being vicarious; inability to be experienced or undergone by one person in place of another.  |  The nonvicariousness of suffering means each individual must bear their own pain.  痛苦的不可替代性意味着每个人都必须承受自己的痛苦。| 近义词: nontransferability, incommunicability | 反义词: vicariousness, substitutability | 词性: 名词

词源: non- (not) + vicarious + -ness (noun suffix forming abstract nouns from adjectives)
