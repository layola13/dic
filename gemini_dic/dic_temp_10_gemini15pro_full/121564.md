misnarrated:/ˌmɪsnəˈreɪtɪd/
verb
错误地叙述，讲述不准确
told or recounted incorrectly or inaccurately.
The witness misnarrated the events of the evening, leading to confusion.  目击者错误地叙述了当晚发生的事件，导致了混乱。
The story was misnarrated by the journalist, omitting crucial details.  这个故事被记者错误地叙述了，省略了关键的细节。
misrepresented, misstated, falsified
correctly narrated, accurately recounted
verb
