mistakingly:/mɪˈsteɪkɪŋli/
adverb
错误地；弄错地
in a way that is wrong because you have understood or judged something wrongly.
I mistakenly believed that he was my friend. 我错误地认为他是我的朋友。

He mistakenly deleted the file. 他误删了文件。


Synonym: erroneously, wrongly, incorrectly
Antonym: correctly, rightly, accurately
词性: 副词
