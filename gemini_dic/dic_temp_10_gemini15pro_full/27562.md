canaliculated:/kəˈnælɪkjʊˌleɪtɪd/
adjective
有细管的，有沟渠的
having a small channel or groove, especially in bone or a plant stem
The bone is canaliculated for the passage of blood vessels. 这块骨头上有细管供血管通过。
近义词: grooved, channeled, furrowed
反义词: smooth, flat
词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin *canaliculatus, past participle of canaliculare (“to furnish with a canal”), from canalis (“canal, groove”) + -iculare (frequentative suffix).
