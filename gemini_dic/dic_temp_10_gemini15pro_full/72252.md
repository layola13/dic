florin:/ˈflɔːrɪn/| n. |弗罗林，佛罗林币|A former gold coin of Florence, first struck in 1252 and imitated elsewhere; also, any of various other European gold coins.  A former monetary unit of several European countries, especially the Netherlands (replaced by the euro) and Great Britain (replaced by the pound).|He paid ten florins for the painting.|近义词：coin, currency, money|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific currency)|noun
