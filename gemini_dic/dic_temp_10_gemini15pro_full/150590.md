paperback:/ˈpeɪpərbæk/| n. |平装书；简装本|A book bound in a flexible paper cover, typically a mass-market edition.|I prefer reading paperbacks because they're lighter and easier to carry than hardcovers. 我更喜欢读平装书，因为它们比精装书更轻，更容易携带。|近义词：softcover, softback|反义词：hardcover, hardback|词性:名词

Etymology:
paper + back
