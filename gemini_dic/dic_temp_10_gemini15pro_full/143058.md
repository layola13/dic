outboard motor:/ˈaʊtbɔːrd ˈmoʊtər/ (US), /ˈaʊtbɔːd ˈməʊtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|舷外发动机|A propulsion system for boats, consisting of a self-contained unit that includes an engine, gearbox, and propeller, attached to the outside of the transom.|
The fisherman started his outboard motor and headed out to sea. 渔夫启动了他的舷外马达，驶向大海。

The small boat was powered by a 15-horsepower outboard motor.  这艘小船由一个15马力的舷外发动机提供动力。

近义词:outboard engine, outboard|反义词:inboard motor|词性:名词

outboard motors:/ˈaʊtbɔːrd ˈmoʊtərz/ (US), /ˈaʊtbɔːd ˈməʊtəz/ (UK)| n.|舷外发动机（复数）|Plural form of "outboard motor," referring to more than one such unit.|
