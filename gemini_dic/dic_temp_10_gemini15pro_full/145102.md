overappreciative:/ˌoʊvərəˈpriːʃətɪv/ | adj. | 过于感激的，过分欣赏的 | Showing or expressing too much appreciation; excessively grateful. |  She was overappreciative of his small gesture of kindness. 她对他小小的善举表示过分的感激。 | Example: He was overappreciative of the gift, almost to the point of embarrassment. 他对这份礼物表现得过于感激，几乎到了尴尬的地步。| Synonyms: excessively grateful, overly thankful | Antonyms: ungrateful, unappreciative | word property: adjective
