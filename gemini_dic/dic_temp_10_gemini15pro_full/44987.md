crystallize:/ˈkrɪstəlaɪz/ /ˈkrɪstəˌlaɪz/| v. |结晶；使具体化；使明确|To cause to form crystals or assume crystalline form; to make something definite or clear.|The solution crystallized overnight. 溶液一夜之间结晶了。|The events of that day helped to crystallize his decision. 那一天的事件促使他做出了决定。|近义词：solidify, concretize, materialize|反义词:dissolve, melt|词性:动词

crystallize:/ˈkrɪstəlaɪz/ /ˈkrɪstəˌlaɪz/| n. |结晶；具体化|The process of forming crystals or becoming definite.|The crystallization of his ideas took many years.  他的想法的形成花了许多年。|近义词：solidification, concretion|反义词:dissolution, melting|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *crystallizāre, from Latin crystallum "crystal" (from Ancient Greek κρύσταλλος (krústallos) "ice, crystal") + -izare (forming verbs).
