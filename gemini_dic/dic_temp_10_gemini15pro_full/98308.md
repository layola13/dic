insightful:/ˈɪnsaɪtfʊl/| adj.|有洞察力的；富有见地的|Having or showing an accurate and deep understanding of something.|Her insightful comments helped us understand the problem better. 她富有见地的评论帮助我们更好地理解了这个问题。|近义词：perceptive, astute, discerning, insightful|反义词：unperceptive, obtuse, shallow|词性:形容词
词源: From insight + -ful. Insight comes from the Old English word *insīht*, meaning "inner sight" or "understanding."
