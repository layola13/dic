cogitative:/ˈkɒdʒɪtətɪv/ /ˈkɑːɡɪteɪtɪv/| adj.| 思考的，沉思的|Relating to the process of thinking and reasoning; thoughtful.|She spent a cogitative hour contemplating the meaning of life. 她花了沉思的一个小时思考生命的意义。|近义词：thoughtful, reflective, meditative, contemplative, pensive|反义词：unthinking, impulsive, rash|词性: adjective
词源: From Late Latin *cōgitātīvus*, from Latin cōgitātus, past participle of cōgitāre ‘think over’, from co- ‘together’ + agitāre ‘turn over, discuss’ (from agere ‘do, drive’).
