gipsyism:/ˈdʒɪpsiɪzəm/ | n. |吉卜赛文化；吉卜赛习俗；流浪生活|The state or condition of being a Gypsy, or the customs, practices, or way of life associated with Gypsies. It can also refer to a romanticized or stereotypical view of this way of life.|He embraced gipsyism and travelled the world. 他热爱吉卜赛式的生活，周游世界。|近义词：nomadism, wandering, roving|反义词：sedentarism, settled life|词性:noun

gipsyism:/ˈdʒɪpsiɪzəm/ | n. |吉卜赛精神；流浪癖；漂泊|A spirit of wandering or a love of adventure and freedom from conventional social norms.|Her gipsyism led her to abandon her corporate job and live in a van. 她的流浪癖促使她放弃了公司的工作，住在了一辆货车里。|近义词：wanderlust, adventurousness, free-spiritedness|反义词：conventionality, conformity|词性:noun
