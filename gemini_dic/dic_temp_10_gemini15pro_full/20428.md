bitonal:/baɪˈtoʊnl/
adjective
双音的，有两个调性的
Having or using two tonalities or keys simultaneously.
The composer experimented with bitonal harmonies, creating a dissonant yet intriguing sound. 作曲家尝试了双音和声，创造了一种不和谐却又引人入胜的声音。
bitonal chords created an unsettling effect. 双音和弦营造了一种令人不安的效果。

synonyms:diatonic, bichordal
antonyms:monotonic, monophonic
