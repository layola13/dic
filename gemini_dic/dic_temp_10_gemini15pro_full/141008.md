onesie: /ˈwʌnzi/ (US), /ˈwʌnsi/ (UK)| n. |连体衣，连身衣|A loose-fitting one-piece garment combining a top and trousers, often worn as sleepwear or loungewear, typically made of soft material like cotton or fleece.  It usually has a front zipper or buttons for closure and sometimes includes a hood or feet.|My daughter loves wearing her unicorn onesie. 我女儿喜欢穿她的独角兽连体衣。|synonyms: jumpsuit, romper, coverall, playsuit|antonyms: separates (two-piece clothing)|noun
