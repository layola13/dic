noncollapsible:/ˌnɒnkəˈlæpsəbl/ | adj. | 不可折叠的，不可拆卸的 | Not able to be collapsed or folded. | The frame of the tent is noncollapsible, making it sturdy but difficult to store.  帐篷的框架是不可折叠的，这使得它很坚固，但难以存放。 | The surgeon inserted a noncollapsible stent to keep the artery open.  外科医生插入了一个不可拆卸的支架以保持动脉畅通。 |  近义词：rigid, fixed, inflexible | 反义词: collapsible, foldable | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + collapsible (from collapse, meaning "to fall down or inward suddenly")
