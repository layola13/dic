gravel:/ˈɡrævəl/| n.|碎石，砂砾|Small, rounded stones, often used for paths and roads.|The driveway was covered in gravel. 车道上铺满了碎石。|Synonyms: pebbles, stones, aggregate|Antonyms: sand, dust|noun

gravel:/ˈɡrævəl/| v.|用碎石铺|To cover with gravel.|They graveled the path to the garden. 他们在通往花园的小路上铺了碎石。|Synonyms: pave, surface|Antonyms: excavate, dig up|verb
