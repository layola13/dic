imperialist:/ɪmˈpɪəriəlɪst/ /ɪmˈpɪriəlɪst/ | n. adj. |帝国主义者；帝国主义的|A supporter of imperialism, the policy of extending a country's power and influence through diplomacy or military force.| 例句：The imperialist powers carved up Africa among themselves. 帝国主义列强瓜分了非洲。|近义词：colonialist, expansionist|反义词：anti-imperialist, nationalist|词性: 名词, 形容词

imperialist:/ɪmˈpɪəriəlɪst/ /ɪmˈpɪriəlɪst/| adj. |帝国主义的|Relating to or characteristic of imperialism.| 例句：He condemned the imperialist policies of the government. 他谴责了政府的帝国主义政策。|近义词：colonial, expansionist|反义词：anti-imperialist, nationalist|词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *impérialiste*, from *impérial* (“imperial”) + *-iste* (suffix forming agent nouns).  *Impérial* comes from Latin *imperiālis* (“of the empire, imperial”), from *imperium* (“command, power, empire”).
