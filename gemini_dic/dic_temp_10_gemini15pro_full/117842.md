mellitic:/məˈlɪtɪk/
adjective
蜜的；蜜似的；由蜜制成的；甜如蜜的
Relating to, derived from, or resembling honey; sweet as honey.
Mellic acid is a benzenehexacarboxylic acid.  蜜酸是一种苯六甲酸。
近义词: honeyed, sweet, sugary
反义词: sour, bitter
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *mellīticus, from mel, mellis (“honey”).  源自拉丁语 *mellīticus，来自 mel, mellis（“蜂蜜”）。
