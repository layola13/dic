dishevelled: /dɪˈʃevəld/ | adj. |  凌乱的，蓬乱的 | Being in loose disarray; untidy; disordered.  Characterized by disorder or disarray in clothing, hair, or general appearance. | Her hair was dishevelled and her clothes were torn. 她的头发蓬乱，衣服也破了。|  messy, unkempt, rumpled, disordered | tidy, neat, ordered, groomed | adjective

dishevel: /dɪˈʃevəl/ | vt. |  使蓬乱，使凌乱 | To disarrange or rumple (e.g., hair or clothing). | The wind dishevelled her hair. 风吹乱了她的头发。| mess up, disorder, ruffle | tidy, neaten, arrange | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *descheveler, from des- (expressing reversal) + chevel (hair), from Latin capillus (hair).
