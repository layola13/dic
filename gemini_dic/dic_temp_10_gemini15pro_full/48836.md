defoliation:/ˌdiːˌfoʊliˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 落叶；脱叶；去叶 |The removal of leaves from a plant, either naturally, as in autumn, or artificially, often by chemical means. | The defoliation of the trees by the caterpillars was alarming.  毛毛虫把树叶吃光了，真让人担忧。 | defoliating, leaf fall, leaf shedding | foliation, leafing | noun

defoliate:/ˌdiːˈfoʊliˌeɪt/ | v. |  使落叶；使脱叶；除去…的叶子 | To cause the leaves of a plant to fall off, either naturally or by the use of chemicals. | The harsh winter defoliated many of the trees in the park. 严冬使得公园里许多树木的叶子都掉光了。 | strip, denude | cover, clothe | verb
