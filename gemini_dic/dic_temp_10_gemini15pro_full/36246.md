cognizee:/ˌkɑɡnɪˈziː/ | n. |被认知者；认识对象|A person who is cognized or known, especially in a legal context.  The person who is the object of someone's knowledge or perception.| The judge ruled that the cognizee was not responsible for the damages. 法官裁定被认知者对损害不负责任。| The cognizee in this contract is the party receiving the goods.  在这份合同中，被认知者是接收货物的当事人。 | synonyms: recipient, beneficiary, subject (of perception) | antonyms: cognizer, perceiver | noun
