actualising: /ˈæktʃuəlaɪzɪŋ/
Verb
实现; 实际化; 使现实化
The process of making something real or giving it practical form.
She is actualising her dream of becoming a doctor. 她正在实现她成为一名医生的梦想。

He is finally actualising his potential. 他终于发挥出他的潜力。

近义词: realizing, fulfilling, achieving, manifesting
反义词: neglecting, ignoring, hindering
词性: Verb (present participle)

actualise: /ˈæktʃuəlaɪz/
Verb
实现; 实际化; 使现实化
To make something real or to give it practical form.
He finally actualised his ambition to sail around the world. 他终于实现了环游世界的雄心壮志。

The company needs to actualise its plans for expansion. 公司需要实现其扩张计划。

近义词: realize, fulfill, achieve, manifest
反义词: neglect, ignore, hinder
词性: Verb
