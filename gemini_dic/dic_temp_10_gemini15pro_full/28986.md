cartelisms: /kɑːrˈtɛlɪzəmz/ | n. | 卡特尔主义；卡特尔行为 | The practices and policies characteristic of cartels, especially those involving collusion and price-fixing.  | Example: The government is trying to crack down on cartelisms in the pharmaceutical industry. 政府正在努力打击制药行业的卡特尔行为。 | Example: Cartelisms often lead to higher prices for consumers and reduced competition. 卡特尔行为通常会导致消费者价格上涨和竞争减少。 | Synonyms: collusion, price-fixing, market manipulation, anti-competitive practices | Antonyms: competition, free market | noun
