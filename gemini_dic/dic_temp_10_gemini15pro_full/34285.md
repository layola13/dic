clarets:/ˈklærɪts/| n.|红葡萄酒，深红色|A dry red wine, originally and especially from the Bordeaux region of France, or a similar wine made elsewhere.  Typically a deep purplish-red color.|The sommelier recommended a robust claret to accompany the steak.侍酒师推荐了一种浓郁的红葡萄酒来搭配牛排。|Synonyms: red wine, Bordeaux, Burgundy|Antonyms: white wine|noun
