outface:/ˌaʊtˈfeɪs/ | v. | 胜过；超越 | to surpass or exceed |  She outfaced all the other competitors in the race. 她在比赛中胜过所有其他选手。| synonyms: surpass, exceed, outdo, outperform | antonyms: lose to, be surpassed by | verb
