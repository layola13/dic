extemporises:/ɪkˈstempəraɪzɪz/
verb
即兴创作；即席发言
to compose, perform, or produce something such as music or a speech without preparation; improvise
He extemporises brilliantly. 他即兴创作非常出色。
Synonyms: improvise, ad-lib, wing it, make it up as one goes along, speak off the cuff
Antonyms: prepare, rehearse, plan
词性: 动词
