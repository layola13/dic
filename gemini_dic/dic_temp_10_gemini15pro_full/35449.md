co-tenancy:/koʊˈtɛnənsi/
n.|共同租用，共同租赁，联名租赁|The agreement outlines the terms of co-tenancy, including responsibilities for maintenance and repairs.  该协议概述了共同租赁的条款，包括维护和维修的责任。|Co-tenancy can lead to disagreements if the tenants don't communicate effectively. 如果租户之间没有有效的沟通，共同租赁可能会导致分歧。|joint tenancy, shared tenancy|sole tenancy, individual tenancy|noun
