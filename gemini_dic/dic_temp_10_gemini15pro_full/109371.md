lensing:/ˈlɛnzɪŋ/| n.|透镜效应；透镜作用|The distortion of light caused by a massive object, like a galaxy cluster, bending the path of light from a more distant object.|Astronomers use gravitational lensing to study distant galaxies. 天文学家利用引力透镜效应来研究遥远的星系。|synonyms: refraction, distortion|antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "clarity" in a specific context)|noun
