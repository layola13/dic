dumbass:/ˈdʌmæs/ (US), /ˈdʌmɑːs/ (UK)| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜|A stupid or foolish person.|例句：He's such a dumbass; he locked his keys in the car. 他真是个笨蛋，把钥匙锁在车里了。

例句：Don't be a dumbass, think before you act. 别犯傻了，三思而后行。

近义词：idiot, moron, fool, imbecile, nitwit|反义词: genius, intellectual, smart person|词性:名词
