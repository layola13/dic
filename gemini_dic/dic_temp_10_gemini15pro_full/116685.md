maturative:/ˈmætʃʊˌreɪtɪv/| adj.|成熟的，促使成熟的|Relating to or causing maturation; promoting development to a mature state.|The maturative process of the brain continues throughout adolescence. 大脑的成熟过程贯穿整个青春期。|Synonyms: ripening, developing, maturing|Antonyms: immature, undeveloped, regressing|adjective

Etymology->
From Late Latin *maturativus, from Latin maturare (“to ripen”), from maturus (“ripe”).
