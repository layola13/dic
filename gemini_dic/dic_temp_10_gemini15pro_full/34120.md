clacks:/klæks/| n.|咔哒声，敲击声|A series of sharp, short sounds made by hard objects striking together, like the sound of typing on a mechanical keyboard or the sound of wooden clogs on a hard floor.|The clacks of the typewriter filled the room. 打字机的咔哒声充满了整个房间。|近义词：clatter, click, rattle|反义词:silence|noun
clack:/klæk/| v.|发出咔哒声|To make a short, sharp sound by striking together.|Her heels clacked on the pavement. 她的高跟鞋在人行道上发出咔哒声。|近义词：clatter, click, rattle|反义词:mute|verb
