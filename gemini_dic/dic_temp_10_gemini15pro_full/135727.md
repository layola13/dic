Nonpriority items can often be delegated or postponed. 非优先事项通常可以委派或推迟。
It's important to differentiate between priority and nonpriority tasks. 区分优先任务和非优先任务很重要.

Focusing on nonpriority tasks can distract from more important goals. 专注于非优先任务可能会分散对更重要目标的注意力。
