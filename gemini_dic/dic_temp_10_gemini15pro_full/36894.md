colorimetry:/ˌkʌləˈrɪmətri/ | n. | 比色法，色度学 | The science of measuring and specifying colors.  It involves using instruments and techniques to quantify color attributes and ensure color accuracy and consistency. |  Colorimetry plays a vital role in quality control in industries like textile and paint manufacturing. 比色法在纺织和油漆制造等行业的质量控制中起着至关重要的作用。 |  spectrophotometry, color analysis | N/A | noun

Etymology:  From French *colorimétrie*, from *couleur* (color) + *-métrie* (measurement), from Greek *metron* (measure).
