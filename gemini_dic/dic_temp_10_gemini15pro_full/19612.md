bigotedly:/ˈbɪɡətɪdli/ | adv. | 偏执地，固执地 | In a manner exhibiting intolerance and prejudice towards those different from oneself. | He bigotedly refused to listen to any viewpoints that challenged his own. 他固执地拒绝听取任何挑战他自身观点的意见。 |  She bigotedly dismissed all other religions as false. 她固执地认为所有其他宗教都是假的。 | 近义词: intolerantly, narrowly, prejudicedly, dogmatically | 反义词: open-mindedly, tolerantly, liberally | 词性: 副词

bigot:/ˈbɪɡət/ | n. | 偏执者，顽固者 | A person who is intolerant towards those holding different opinions. |  He's a bigot who refuses to associate with anyone of a different race. 他是一个顽固的种族主义者，拒绝与任何其他种族的人交往。 | The bigot wouldn't even consider the possibility that he was wrong.  这个偏执的人甚至不愿考虑他可能错了。 | 近义词: racist, sexist, chauvinist, fanatic | 反义词: liberal, tolerant | 词性: 名词

bigoted:/ˈbɪɡətɪd/ | adj. | 偏执的，顽固的 | Having or revealing an intolerant or prejudiced belief system. | His bigoted views on immigration made him unpopular. 他在移民问题上顽固的观点使他不受欢迎。 | Her bigoted comments offended many people at the party.  她在聚会上的偏执言论冒犯了很多人。 | 近义词: intolerant, prejudiced, narrow-minded, dogmatic | 反义词: tolerant, open-minded, liberal | 词性: 形容词
