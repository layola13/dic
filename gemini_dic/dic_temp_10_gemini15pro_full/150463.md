pantheistical:/ˌpænθiˈɪstɪkəl/ | adj. | 泛神论的 | Relating to or characterized by pantheism, the belief that God is identical with the universe, or that all things comprise the immanent God. | The poet's works often express a pantheistical view of nature.  这位诗人的作品经常表达一种泛神论的自然观。|  The artist's pantheistical vision is evident in his portrayal of the interconnectedness of all living things.  这位艺术家的泛神论视野在他对所有生物相互联系的描绘中显而易见。 | 近义词: pantheistic | 反义词: atheistic, theistic | 词性: adjective

pantheism:/ˈpænθiˌɪzəm/ | n. | 泛神论 | The belief that God is identical with the universe, or that all things comprise the immanent God. | Pantheism is a philosophical and religious doctrine. 泛神论是一种哲学和宗教教义。 |  Many indigenous cultures hold pantheistic beliefs. 许多土著文化持有泛神论的信仰。 | 近义词: panentheism | 反义词: atheism, theism | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Ancient Greek πᾶν (pân, “all”) + θεός (theós, “god”) + -istic + -al.
