fleshly:/ˈflɛʃli/| adj.|肉体的；肉欲的；世俗的|Relating to the body; carnal; worldly; not spiritual.|She felt a fleshly desire for him. 她对他产生了肉欲。|近义词：carnal, physical, bodily, corporeal, sensual|反义词：spiritual, ethereal, incorporeal|词性:形容词
词源: From Old English *flæsclīc (unattested), from flæsc "flesh" + -līc "-ly." Cognate with Old Saxon flēsklīk, Old Frisian flesklik, Middle Dutch vleeschelijc, Old High German fleisklīh, German fleischlich.
