nonsculptural:/ˌnɒnˈskʌlptʃərəl/
adjective
非雕塑的；非雕刻的
Not of or relating to sculpture; not suitable for sculpture.
The museum's nonsculptural collection includes paintings and photographs.  博物馆的非雕塑藏品包括绘画和照片。
The soft clay was deemed nonsculptural due to its inability to hold a shape.  这种软粘土因其无法保持形状而被认为不适合雕塑。
近义词:non-sculptural, painterly, two-dimensional
反义词:sculptural, three-dimensional
词性:adjective
词源:non- (prefix meaning "not") + sculptural (adjective relating to sculpture)
