munificate: /mjuːˈnɪfɪkeɪt/
verb
慷慨捐赠，给予
to give or bestow (something, especially money) in a generous or lavish way.
He munificated a large sum to the university. 他向大学慷慨捐赠了一大笔钱。
The philanthropist munificated generously to various charities.  这位慈善家慷慨地向各种慈善机构捐款。
Synonyms: donate, bestow, contribute, endow
Antonyms: hoard, withhold, stint, begrudge
词性: 动词
