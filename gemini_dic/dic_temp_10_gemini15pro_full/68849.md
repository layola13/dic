fashionably:/ˈfæʃənəbli/| adv.|时髦地；流行地；时尚地|In a fashionable manner; stylishly.  Being or following the fashion; dressed, behaving, or made according to the prevailing mode.|She was fashionably dressed in a designer suit. 她身着名牌套装，穿着很时髦。|Synonyms: stylishly, trendily, chicly, smartly|Antonyms: unfashionably, dowdily, frumpily|Word Type: Adverb
