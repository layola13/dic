autology:/ɔːˈtɒlədʒi/ | n. | 同义反复，赘述 |A statement that is true by virtue of its logical form, such as "A is A" or "Everything is identical to itself."  A needless repetition of an idea in different words. |例句：Saying "a beginner who has just started" is an autology. 说“一个刚开始的初学者”是同义反复。

例句：The phrase "free gift" is an autology since a gift is inherently free. “免费礼物”这个词组是同义反复，因为礼物本身就是免费的。 | 近义词：redundancy, pleonasm, tautology | 反义词: oxymoron, paradox | 词性: noun
