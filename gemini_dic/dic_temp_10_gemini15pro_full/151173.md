parcelization:/ˌpɑːrsəlɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌpɑːrsələˈzeɪʃən/
n.
分割；细分；地块划分
The act or process of dividing land into smaller parcels or lots.
The parcelization of the farmland led to smaller, less efficient farms.  农田的分割导致农场规模变小，效率降低。
subdivision, fragmentation, partitioning
consolidation, unification
noun
