lye:/laɪ/
n.
碱液；灰汁
A strongly alkaline solution, especially of potassium hydroxide, used for washing or cleansing.
Lye is a highly corrosive chemical used in making soap. 碱液是一种用于制造肥皂的高腐蚀性化学物质。
Traditional soap making involves mixing lye with fats.  传统肥皂制作包括将碱液与脂肪混合。
synonyms: alkali, caustic soda, potash
antonyms: acid
