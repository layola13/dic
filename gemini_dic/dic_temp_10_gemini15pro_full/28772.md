carnot:/kɑːrˈnoʊ/ | adj. n.|卡诺的；卡诺|Relating to or denoting Nicolas Léonard Sadi Carnot (1796–1832), French physicist, or his work, especially the Carnot cycle.|The Carnot efficiency is the theoretical maximum efficiency that a heat engine can achieve during the conversion between heat and work, or conversely, during work extraction through cooling of a heat reservoir.| 近义词: thermodynamic|反义词:|adjective, noun
