heliotrope:/ˈhiːliəˌtroʊp/| n. adj.|香水草，淡紫色|A plant with small fragrant purple flowers. Also, a shade of purple.|例句：The heliotrope in the garden emitted a sweet fragrance. 花园里的香水草散发着甜甜的香味。|例句：She wore a dress of a delicate heliotrope shade. 她穿着一件淡紫色的连衣裙。|近义词：lavender, lilac, violet|反义词: (As a color) yellow, orange|词性: noun, adjective

heliotroper:/ˈhiːliəˌtroʊpər/| n.|栽培香水草的人，追逐太阳的人或事物|Someone who cultivates heliotropes, or something that turns towards the sun.|例句：My grandmother, a dedicated heliotroper, had a whole greenhouse filled with the fragrant plants.  我祖母是一位专注的香水草栽培者，她有一个装满这种芳香植物的温室。|例句：The sunflower, a classic heliotroper, follows the sun's path across the sky. 向日葵是一种典型的追逐太阳的植物，它会跟随太阳的轨迹划过天空。|近义词：(for a person) gardener, cultivator|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
