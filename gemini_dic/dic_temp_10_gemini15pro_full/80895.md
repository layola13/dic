governmentally:/ˌɡʌvərnˈmentəli/
adverb|政府地；以政府方式|In a governmental manner; by or through a government.
例句：The issue was handled governmentally.  这个问题是由政府处理的。

例句：The funds were distributed governmentally. 资金是通过政府渠道分配的。

近义词：publicly, officially, state-sponsored
反义词：privately, independently
