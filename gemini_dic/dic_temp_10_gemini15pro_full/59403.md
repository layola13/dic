earthboard:/ˈɜːrθbɔːrd/ | n. |土滑板，泥滑板|A board used for sliding down a muddy slope.  A less common term for mudsliding or the act of mudsliding.  Sometimes refers to the act of sliding down a hill on cardboard or other improvised sleds on dirt or grass.  | Example:  The kids were having fun on the earthboard after the rain. 孩子们雨后玩土滑板玩得很开心。 Example:  He grabbed an old piece of cardboard and used it as an earthboard. 他抓起一块旧纸板，把它当作土滑板。| 近义词: mudsled, dirtboard, grass sled | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
