hyperthyroidic:/ˌhaɪpərθaɪˈrɔɪdɪk/
adjective
甲状腺功能亢进的; 甲亢的
Relating to or affected with hyperthyroidism, a condition in which the thyroid gland produces too much thyroxine.
Her hyperthyroidic cat needed daily medication. 她的甲亢猫需要每天服药。
The doctor prescribed medication to treat his hyperthyroidic condition. 医生开了药来治疗他的甲亢。
近义词: overactive thyroid, thyrotoxic
反义词: hypothyroid
