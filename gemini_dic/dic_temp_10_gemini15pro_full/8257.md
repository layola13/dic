anti-inflation:/ˈænti ɪnˈfleɪʃən/ |adj.| 反通货膨胀的，遏制通货膨胀的 | Designed to prevent or reduce inflation. |  The government introduced anti-inflation measures to stabilize the economy. 政府推出了反通货膨胀措施来稳定经济。| synonyms: deflationary, counter-inflationary | antonyms: inflationary | adjective
anti-inflations:/(not commonly used as a plural noun) /| n. (rarely used)| 反通货膨胀措施（罕用复数形式）| Measures taken to combat inflation. | The various anti-inflations implemented by the government have had little effect. 政府实施的各种反通货膨胀措施收效甚微。  (This usage is less common. It's more natural to say "anti-inflation measures" or "anti-inflation policies.") | synonyms: deflationary measures, counter-inflationary measures | antonyms: inflationary measures | noun

Etymology:
"anti-" (prefix): from Greek "anti" meaning "against," "opposite of"
"inflation": from Late Latin "inflatio" meaning "a swelling," from Latin "inflare" meaning "to blow into," from "in-" (in) + "flare" (to blow)
