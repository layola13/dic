ascertain:/æsərˈteɪn/
v.
确定；查明；弄清
to find something out for certain; to make sure of
The police are trying to ascertain the facts of the case. 警方正在设法查明案件真相。

They were unable to ascertain the cause of the fire.  他们无法确定火灾的原因。


determine, discover, establish, verify
guess, speculate, assume

词性:动词
