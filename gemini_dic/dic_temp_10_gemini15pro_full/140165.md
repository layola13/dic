offenseless:/əˈfɛnslɪs/ | adj. |无害的，不冒犯的，无攻击性的|Not causing offense or harm; harmless.  |The comment was entirely offenseless and shouldn't have caused any upset. 这评论完全没有恶意，不应该引起任何不满。| The butterfly is an offenseless creature. 蝴蝶是一种无害的生物。| 近义词：harmless, innocent, inoffensive | 反义词：offensive, harmful, aggressive | 词性：形容词

Etymology:
From "offense" (meaning a transgression or wrongdoing) + "-less" (a suffix indicating lack or absence).  The word "offense" comes from the Old French "ofense" and ultimately from the Latin "offendere" meaning "to strike against, stumble."
