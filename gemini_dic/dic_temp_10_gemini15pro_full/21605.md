blunder:/ˈblʌndər/
| n. | 错误，失误，笨拙的行为 | A stupid or careless mistake. | I made a terrible blunder by telling her the secret. 我告诉了她这个秘密，犯了一个可怕的错误。 | mistake, error, gaffe | success, triumph | noun
| vi. | 犯大错，踉跄，跌跌撞撞地走 | To make a stupid or careless mistake; to move clumsily and unsteadily. |  He blundered into the wrong room. 他踉踉跄跄地走进了错误的房间。 | err, stumble, flounder | succeed, excel | verb


Etymology:  From Middle English *blundren*, probably of imitative origin, suggesting clumsy movement.
