nonmoneyer:/ˈnɒnˈmʌniər/ (US) /ˈnɒnˈmʌniə(r)/ (UK)| n.|非货币主义者|A person who does not believe in or advocate for monetarism (the theory that changes in the money supply are the primary cause of economic fluctuations).|例句：The nonmoneyer argued that fiscal policy, not monetary policy, was the key to economic stability.  非货币主义者认为，财政政策，而不是货币政策，才是经济稳定的关键。

|例句：Some nonmoneyers believe that other factors, such as technological innovation, are more important drivers of economic growth. 一些非货币主义者认为，其他因素，例如技术创新，是经济增长的更重要驱动力。|近义词：fiscalist, Keynesian (sometimes)|反义词：monetarist|词性:noun
