gainers:/ˈɡeɪnərz/| n.| 获得者；增益者；上扬股；|People or things that gain something, such as weight, value, or success.  指获得某物（如体重、价值或成功）的人或事物。|The biggest gainers in the stock market today were technology companies. 今天股市的最大赢家是科技公司。|Synonyms: winners, achievers, victors|Antonyms: losers, decliners|noun

gainer:/ˈɡeɪnər/| n.|增益器；增益电路；|An electronic device or circuit that amplifies a signal.  一种放大信号的电子设备或电路。|The audio signal was boosted by a gainer. 音频信号被增益器增强了。|Synonyms: amplifier, booster|Antonyms: attenuator|noun
