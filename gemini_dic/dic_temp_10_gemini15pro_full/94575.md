impoverishers:/ɪmˈpɒvərɪʃəz/ /ɪmˈpɑːvərɪʃərz/| n. |使人贫困的事物/人|Something or someone that makes a person or group poor or poorer. |The war and the resulting displacement were major impoverishers of the local population. 战争和由此造成的流离失所是当地居民贫困的主要原因。|High taxes can be impoverishers for small businesses.  高税收可能会使小型企业陷入困境。|近义词：depleters, weakeners, drains|反义词：enrichers, benefactors|词性：名词(plural)

impoverisher:/ɪmˈpɒvərɪʃə(r)/ /ɪmˈpɑːvərɪʃər/| n. |使人贫困的事物/人|Something or someone that makes a person or group poor or poorer. |Lack of access to education is a significant impoverisher in many developing countries. 缺乏教育机会是许多发展中国家的一个重要致贫因素。|Constant medical bills can be a real impoverisher for families.  持续的医疗费用对家庭来说可能是一个真正的负担。|近义词：depleter, weakener, drain|反义词：enricher, benefactor|词性：名词(singular)
