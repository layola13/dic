maniform:/ˈmænɪfɔːrm/
adjective
多种多样的，多形的
having many forms or shapes.
The artist's maniform sculptures were a highlight of the exhibition.  这位艺术家的多形雕塑是展览的亮点。
近义词:multiform, polymorphic, diverse, varied
反义词:uniform, homogeneous, similar
