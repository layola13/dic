entries:/ˈɛntriz/| n.|条目；入口；登记；参加比赛的人（或物）|A record of something, an item written or printed in a list, diary, etc.; a way in, such as a door, gate, or passage; the act of entering a place or competition.|例句：The entries in her diary revealed her innermost thoughts. 她的日记记录了她内心深处的想法。

例句：The museum has several entries. 这家博物馆有几个入口。|近义词：items, records, entrances, access points|反义词:exits|词性:noun
