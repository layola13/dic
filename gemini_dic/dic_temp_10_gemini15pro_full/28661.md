carillons:/kærɪˈlɒnz/ /kærɪˈlɑːnz/
noun
钟琴，编钟
A set of bells in a tower, played using a keyboard or by an automatic mechanism.
The carillons rang out across the city. 钟声响彻整个城市。

The carillons of the church tower played a familiar hymn. 教堂塔楼的钟琴演奏了一首熟悉的赞美诗。
