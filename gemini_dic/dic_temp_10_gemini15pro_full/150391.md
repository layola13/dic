panoply:/ˈpænəpli/| n. |全套披甲,华丽的服饰，盛装；大量, 炫耀, 陈列|A complete or impressive collection of things; a splendid display.|The knight wore a full panoply of armor. 骑士穿着全套盔甲。|Synonyms: array, collection, display, exhibit, show, spectacle|Antonyms: lack, scarcity|noun

panoply:/ˈpænəpli/| n. |全副武装, 盔甲|A complete suit of armor.|The museum displayed a panoply of medieval weaponry.  博物馆展出了一套中世纪武器。|Synonyms: armor, mail, suit of armor|Antonyms:  disarmament, vulnerability|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French panoplie, from Late Latin panoplia "complete armor," from Greek panoplia, from pan- "all" + hopla "arms, armor."
