embroiderers:/ɪmˈbrɔɪdərərz/
noun
刺绣者；绣花工
People who embroider, creating decorative needlework on fabric.
The embroiderers skillfully stitched intricate designs onto the silk cloth. 刺绣者们熟练地将复杂的图案缝在丝绸上。
近义词: needleworkers, stitchers
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "machine embroiderers" as a contrasting concept)
词性: noun (plural)
