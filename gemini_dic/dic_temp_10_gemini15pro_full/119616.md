midden:/ˈmɪdn/ | n. |垃圾堆; 粪堆; 贝冢|A refuse heap or dunghill.  Specifically, a prehistoric refuse heap which marks an ancient settlement, often containing shells, animal bones, and discarded tools. | Archaeologists excavated the midden and found numerous artifacts. 考古学家挖掘了这个垃圾堆，发现了许多文物。| synonyms:  dump, heap, mound, refuse pile | antonyms:  cleanliness, order | noun
