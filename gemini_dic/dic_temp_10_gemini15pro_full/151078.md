parapsychologies:/ˌpærəsaɪˈkɒlədʒiz/ | n. | 超心理学 | The study of mental phenomena that are excluded from or inexplicable by orthodox scientific psychology (such as hypnosis, telepathy, etc.).  |  He's fascinated by parapsychologies and the possibility of ESP. 他对超心理学和超感官知觉的可能性很着迷。 |  Parapsychologies encompasses various areas of study, including telekinesis. 超心理学包括各种研究领域，包括心灵致动。| Near Synonyms:  metapsychology, psychical research | Antonyms:  conventional psychology | Noun
