creeps:/kriːps/| v.  n.| 爬行；蔓延；毛骨悚然 |  (v.) To move slowly and quietly, especially in a way that seems unpleasant or frightening; (n.) a feeling of disgust or fear. | The spider creeps up the wall. 蜘蛛沿着墙爬上去。| 近义词：(v.) crawl, slither, inch; (n.) shivers, goosebumps | 反义词: (v.) sprint, run; (n.) comfort, ease | 词性: verb (mostly), noun (plural)

creeps:/kriːps/| n. | 毛骨悚然的感觉；讨厌的人 |  A feeling of disgust or fear; (informal) a person who is unpleasant and makes you feel uncomfortable. |  He gives me the creeps. 他让我毛骨悚然。 | 近义词: shivers, goosebumps, weirdo, creep (singular) | 反义词: comfort, ease | 词性: noun (plural)
