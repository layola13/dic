neuralgic:/nʊˈrældʒɪk/ | adj. | 神经痛的；神经衰弱的；极度敏感的 | Relating to or characterized by neuralgia (severe pain along the course of a nerve).  Also used figuratively to describe extreme sensitivity or irritability. |  The patient complained of a neuralgic pain in his face.  病人抱怨他的脸部有神经痛。| synonyms: painful, sharp, shooting, stabbing, agonizing, excruciating | antonyms: painless, numb, comfortable | adjective

neuralgic:/nʊˈrældʒɪk/ | adj. | 神经痛的；神经衰弱的；极度敏感的 |  His writing sometimes displays a neuralgic sensitivity to criticism. 他的写作有时表现出对批评极度敏感。| synonyms: sensitive, touchy, irritable, edgy, nervous | antonyms: insensitive, calm, stoic | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *névralgique*, from Greek *νεῦρον* (neuron, "nerve") + *-αλγία* (-algia, "pain").
