barracker:/ˈbærəkər/ (US), /ˈbærəkə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 支持者；啦啦队员|A person who shouts encouragement and support for their team, especially at a sporting event. |例句：The barrackers were cheering wildly for their team. 啦啦队员们疯狂地为他们的球队欢呼。

例句：The barrackers' chants filled the stadium. 啦啦队员们的呐喊声响彻了整个体育场。

近义词：supporter, fan, rooter, cheerleader|反义词: opponent, critic, detractor|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From barrack (v.) "to jeer, shout against" + -er (agent suffix).  Originally, a barracker could be someone who jeered or cheered.  The negative sense has largely fallen out of use.
