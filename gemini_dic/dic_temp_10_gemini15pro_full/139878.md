oculus:/ˈɑːkjələs/ (US), /ˈɒkjʊləs/ (UK)| n.|眼睛，孔，窥视孔|An eye; a small opening, especially one that resembles an eye. |The oculus in the Pantheon allows sunlight to stream into the building.  万神殿的 Oculus 让阳光倾泻到建筑物内。

The giant squid has the largest oculus of any known animal. 巨型乌贼的眼睛是所有已知动物中最大的。|synonyms: eye, aperture, opening, hole|antonyms: closure, blockage|noun
