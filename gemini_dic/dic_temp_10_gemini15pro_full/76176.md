fussed:/ˈfʌst/ (US), /fʌst/ (UK)| adj.| 焦躁的，不安的，过于讲究的|  Bothered or anxious about something, especially something unimportant; making a great show of being concerned about something, especially something trivial. |She gets so fussed about the house being perfectly clean. 她总是为房子是否一尘不染而焦虑不安。| 近义词：worried, anxious, bothered, concerned, flustered, agitated, overwrought| 反义词：calm, relaxed, unconcerned, indifferent| 词性: 形容词

fussed (past participle of fuss): /ˈfʌst/ (US), /fʌst/ (UK)| v.| 小题大做，过于讲究，忙乱|To show excessive concern about something unimportant; to make a fuss.|He fussed over the arrangements for the party. 他对派对的安排过于讲究。| 近义词：worry, fret, flutter, fidget| 反义词：ignore, neglect, disregard| 词性: 动词
