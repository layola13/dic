hornwork:/ˈhɔːrnwɜːrk/| n. |角堡，角形防御工事|A hornwork is a type of outwork in fortification. It consists of two demi-bastions connected by a curtain wall and two long side walls extending towards the main fortification.|The hornwork provided a strong defense against attacks from the flanks.| 近义词：outwork, fortification, bastion |反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "weakness" in a strategic sense) | 词性:noun
