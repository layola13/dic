harnesser:/ˈhɑːrnɪsər/| n.| 利用者；治理者；驾驭者 |Someone who harnesses something (especially abstract things like energy or emotions).|  The harnesser of solar energy has created a sustainable power source. 太阳能的利用者创造了一个可持续的能源。| Synonyms: utilizer, exploiter, controller | Antonyms: waster, squanderer | noun
Etymology: From the verb "harness," from Old French "harneis" meaning "armor," later "equipment for a horse." Ultimately from Frankish *harnaski.
