givebacks:/ˈɡɪvbæks/| n. |让步，返还，回扣|Something given back, especially wages or benefits previously gained by workers, that are later conceded to management.|The union agreed to givebacks in order to save jobs. 工会同意让步以保住工作岗位。|synonyms: concessions, compromises, sacrifices|antonyms: gains, increases, advancements|noun

givebacks:/ˈɡɪvbæks/| n. |优惠，赠品|Something given back to customers as a promotion, such as a discount or a free gift.|The store offered givebacks to its loyal customers. 这家商店向其忠实顾客提供优惠。|synonyms: discounts, rebates, promotions|antonyms: surcharges, extra charges|noun
