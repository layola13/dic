christmastide:/ˈkrɪsməstaɪd/| n.|圣诞节期，圣诞节期间|The period from Christmas Eve to Epiphany (January 6th).  The twelve days after Christmas Day, traditionally regarded as a festive season.|We sang carols throughout Christmastide. 我们整个圣诞节期间都在唱颂歌。|Synonyms: Yuletide, Christmas season, holiday season|Antonyms: ordinary time|noun
