deconflict:/diːkənˈflɪkt/ | v. | 消除冲突，避免冲突 | To prevent or resolve conflicts or overlaps, especially between military operations or schedules. | The two generals met to deconflict their operations and ensure their troops didn't accidentally engage each other. 两位将军会面以消除他们的行动冲突，并确保他们的部队不会意外交火。

The airline had to deconflict its flight schedule with the other airlines to avoid congestion at the airport.  这家航空公司不得不与其他航空公司协调航班时刻表，以避免机场拥堵。 |  synonyms: coordinate, harmonize, synchronize, integrate, reconcile | antonyms: clash, conflict, interfere, collide | verb
