macrozooespore:/ˈmækroʊˌzoʊ.əˌspɔːr/| n. |大型游动孢子|A large, motile zoospore. It is a type of asexual spore that uses flagella for locomotion, commonly found in certain algae and fungi.|Example: The macrozooespores of the alga were observed swimming actively under the microscope.  该藻类的大型游动孢子在显微镜下被观察到活跃地游动。|Example: The release of macrozooespores is triggered by specific environmental conditions. 大型游动孢子的释放是由特定的环境条件触发的。|近义词：zoospore, megazoospore|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider aplanospore - a non-motile spore)|词性:noun
