nonrejoinder:/ˌnɑːnrɪˈdʒɔɪndər/
n.
不答辩，不反驳，沉默
An instance of not replying or making a rejoinder; silence.
The lawyer's nonrejoinder suggested he agreed with the point.  律师的沉默表明他同意这一点。

近义词: silence, taciturnity, muteness, no reply
反义词: reply, response, rejoinder, rebuttal, answer
词性: noun
