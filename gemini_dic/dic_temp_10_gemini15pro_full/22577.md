bootie's:/ˈbuːtiz/| n.| 短靴；婴儿袜；战利品|This refers to a small boot, often a woman's ankle boot, or a baby's soft shoe. It can also slangily refer to something taken as a prize or trophy.|"She bought a new pair of booties for the winter." 她买了一双新的短靴过冬。

"The baby's booties were knitted by her grandmother."  婴儿的袜子是她祖母织的。

"They considered the stolen jewels their booties." 他们把偷来的珠宝视为他们的战利品。 |近义词：boots, ankle boots, infant shoes, trophy, spoils|反义词:|词性:名词(plural)

注意事项:

bootie本身就是一个缩写词,来源于 booty. bootie's是bootie的所有格形式。由于bootie指代的物品通常是复数形式出现，所以booties比bootie更常见。bootie's则指代某一双或某几双短靴。
