hornwort:/ˈhɔːrnwɜːrt/| n.|金鱼藻；角苔|A submerged aquatic plant with narrow forked leaves, popular in aquariums and ponds.  A type of non-vascular plant (bryophyte) that grows in damp environments.|例句：The hornwort in my aquarium provides good cover for the fish.  我的鱼缸里的金鱼藻为鱼提供了很好的掩护。

例句：Hornworts are an ancient group of plants. 角苔是一组古老的植物。|近义词：coontail, milfoil (for aquatic varieties)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


Etymology: From "horn" + "wort" (Old English "wyrt," meaning "plant," especially one used for food or medicine).  The "horn" part likely refers to the elongated sporophytes that resemble horns.
