leery:/ˈlɪəri/| adj.|谨慎的，怀疑的，警惕的|Suspicious or wary due to past experiences or distrust.|例句：I'm leery of anyone who promises to make me rich quickly.  我对任何承诺让我快速致富的人都持怀疑态度。

例句：She was leery of the stranger's offer of help. 她对陌生人提供的帮助感到警惕。|近义词：wary, cautious, skeptical, suspicious, distrustful|反义词：trusting, gullible, naive|词性:形容词
