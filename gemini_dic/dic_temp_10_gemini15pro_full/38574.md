concomitant:/kənˈkɒmɪtənt/ /kənˈkɑːmɪtənt/| adj. n.|伴随的；共存的；同时发生的；伴随物|Naturally occurring in or associated with something else.  Existing or happening together, especially as a result of something else.|例句：Loss of memory is a frequent concomitant of old age.  记忆力丧失是老年人的常见伴发现象。

concomitant:/kənˈkɒmɪtənt/ /kənˈkɑːmɪtənt/| adj. n.|伴随的；共存的；同时发生的；伴随物|Something that happens at the same time as something else.|例句：She experienced the anxieties concomitant with taking on new responsibilities. 她经历了承担新责任的同时产生的焦虑。|近义词：accompanying, attendant, concurrent, coincident, related|反义词:unrelated, separate, independent|词性:adjective, noun
