electromigration:/ɪˌlɛktroʊmaɪˈɡreɪʃən/ | n. |电迁移|The gradual displacement of atoms in a conductor due to the momentum transfer from moving electrons.  It is a significant concern in microelectronics, where high current densities can cause circuit failure.| High current densities can lead to electromigration and ultimately device failure. 高电流密度会导致电迁移，最终导致器件失效。| 近义词: atomic diffusion, mass transport | 反义词: N/A | noun

electromigration:/ɪˌlɛktroʊmaɪˈɡreɪʃən/ | n. | 电迁移 | The movement of metal atoms in a conductor caused by the flow of electrons.  This phenomenon can cause voids and hillocks in the conductor, eventually leading to circuit failure. | Electromigration is a major reliability concern in integrated circuits. 电迁移是集成电路中一个主要的可靠性问题。| 近义词: metal migration | 反义词: N/A | noun
