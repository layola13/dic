crusades:/kruːˈseɪdz/| n.|十字军东征|A series of religious wars between Christians and Muslims started primarily to secure control of holy sites considered sacred by both groups.|The Crusades had a profound impact on European history. 十字军东征对欧洲历史产生了深远的影响。|近义词：holy wars, expeditions|反义词: peace treaties|词性:noun

crusade:/kruːˈseɪd/| n. vi.|运动，斗争|A vigorous campaign for political, social, or religious change.|She's on a crusade against smoking. 她正在开展一场反对吸烟的运动。|近义词：campaign, movement, struggle|反义词: inaction, surrender|词性:noun, verb
