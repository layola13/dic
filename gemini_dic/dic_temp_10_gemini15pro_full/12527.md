asphodel:/ˈæsfədɛl/ | n. |  水仙,日光百合; (希腊神话) 冥府之花 | A plant of the lily family, typically having long, narrow leaves and a tall stem bearing clusters of small, fragrant, white or yellow flowers.  In Greek mythology, it was associated with the underworld. |  The fields were carpeted with asphodel. 田野里铺满了日光百合。 | Synonyms: daffodil, narcissus, lily | Antonyms: N/A | noun

asphodel:/ˈæsfədɛl/ | n. |  (诗歌用语) 忘忧草 |  Poetically, a flower symbolizing remembrance or forgetfulness, often associated with death and the afterlife. |  She wandered through the fields of asphodel, a symbol of her grief.  她在日光百合花田里徘徊，象征着她的悲伤。| Synonyms: amaranth (in this context) | Antonyms: N/A | noun
