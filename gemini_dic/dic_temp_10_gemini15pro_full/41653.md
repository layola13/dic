correi:/kɔːˈraɪ/ /kəˈraɪ/ | n. |勘误表; 正误表| A list of errors in a printed work, together with their corrections. | Example 1: The publisher issued a correi to address the numerous typos in the first edition of the book. 出版商发布了一份勘误表，以纠正该书第一版中的许多印刷错误。

Example 2:  Check the correi before using the textbook, as there might be some crucial corrections to the formulas. 在使用教科书之前，请检查勘误表，因为公式中可能有一些重要的更正。|Synonym: errata, corrigendum| Antonym:  N/A (Corrections imply the existence of errors; a direct opposite doesn't exist.)| Noun
