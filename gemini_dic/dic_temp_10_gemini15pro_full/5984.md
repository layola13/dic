ambuscado:/ˌæmbʊˈskɑːdoʊ/ /ˌæmbʊˈskeɪdoʊ/
| n.| 伏击，埋伏；陷阱 |An ambush; a place of concealment for the purpose of surprising an enemy. | The soldiers lay in ambuscado, waiting for the enemy patrol. 士兵们埋伏起来，等待着敌人的巡逻队。|
|近义词：ambush, trap, snare|
|反义词：openness, exposure|
|词性：noun|


ambuscado:/ˌæmbʊˈskɑːdoʊ/ /ˌæmbʊˈskeɪdoʊ/
| adj.| 埋伏的，伏击的 |Ambushed; lying in wait. | The ambuscado soldiers surprised the enemy. 埋伏的士兵们奇袭了敌人。|
|近义词：hidden, concealed, lurking|
|反义词：exposed, visible|
|词性：adjective (less common usage)|


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *emboscada*, from *emboscar* "to ambush," from *en* "in" + *bosque* "wood," from Frankish *bosk* "bush," from Proto-Germanic *buskaz*. Related to English *bush*.
