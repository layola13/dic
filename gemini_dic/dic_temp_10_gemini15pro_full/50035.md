denotation's:/ˌdiːnəʊˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 字面意义；指称意义；外延 |  The explicit or direct meaning or set of meanings of a word or expression, as distinguished from the ideas or meanings associated with it or suggested by it; literal significance.  | The denotation of "home" is a place where one lives, but its connotations can include warmth, family, and security. “家”的字面意思是人居住的地方，但它的内涵可以包括温暖、家庭和安全。 | synonyms: meaning, signification, sense, import, gist, drift | antonyms: connotation, implication | noun
