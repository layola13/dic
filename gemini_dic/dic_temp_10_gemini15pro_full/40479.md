contributively:/kənˈtrɪbjətɪvli/
adverb
贡献地，起作用地
in a way that makes a contribution.
Her work contributively led to the project's success. 她的工作对项目的成功起到了作用。
The team members worked contributively to achieve the goal. 团队成员共同努力实现了目标。
Synonyms: jointly, collectively, cooperatively
Antonyms: individually, separately, independently
词性: 副词
