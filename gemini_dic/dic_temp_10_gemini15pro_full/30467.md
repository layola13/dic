| n. | 离心机 | a machine with a rapidly rotating container that applies centrifugal force to its contents, typically to separate fluids of different densities or liquids from solids. |  The lab uses a high-speed centrifuge.  实验室使用高速离心机。| separator | integrator, combiner | noun
