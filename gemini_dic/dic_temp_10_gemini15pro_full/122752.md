moistureless:/ˈmɔɪstʃərləs/
adjective
无水分的；干燥的
lacking moisture; dry
The desert air was hot and moistureless. 沙漠的空气又热又干燥。
近义词:arid, dry, parched, dehydrated
反义词:moist, damp, humid, wet
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From moisture + -less.  Moisture comes from Middle English *moistour, from Old French moisteur, from moiste "moist," ultimately from Latin mucidus "moldy, musty."  The suffix -less is a native English suffix meaning "without."
