collectorship:/kəˈlektərʃɪp/
n.
职位；收税员的职位；收藏家身份
The position or office of a collector.
He was appointed to the collectorship of customs. 他被任命为海关税务员。
synonyms: position, office, post, job, appointment
antonyms: unemployment, dismissal


collectorship:/kəˈlektərʃɪp/
n.
收藏；收藏品
The activity of collecting something.
His collectorship of antique clocks is well-known.  他收藏古董钟表的事迹广为人知。
synonyms: collection, accumulation, gathering, amassment
antonyms: dispersal, distribution
