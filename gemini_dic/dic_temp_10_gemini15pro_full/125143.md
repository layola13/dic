multi-tasking: /ˌmʌltiˈtɑːskɪŋ/ /ˌmʌltiˈtæsking/
| n.| 多任务处理 |The act of performing multiple tasks simultaneously.  It often involves switching quickly between different activities, which can reduce efficiency and increase errors.| She's good at multi-tasking and can handle several projects at once. 她擅长多任务处理，可以同时处理多个项目。| synonyms: multitasking, parallel processing, concurrency | antonyms: single-tasking, serial processing | noun

multi-task: /ˌmʌltiˈtɑːsk/ /ˌmʌltiˈtæsk/
| v. | 多任务处理 |To perform multiple tasks seemingly at the same time.| He tried to multi-task, but ended up making mistakes on both projects. 他试图同时处理多项任务，但最终在两个项目上都犯了错误。| synonyms: juggle, manage multiple tasks concurrently | antonyms: focus on one task | verb
