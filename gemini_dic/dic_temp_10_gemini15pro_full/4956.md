ETYMOLOGY->
From French *alizarine*, from Italian *alizari*, from Arabic *al-ʿaṣāra* (العَصارة, “the juice, sap, extract”). Ultimately from the same Semitic root as Hebrew *עָסִיס*‎ (*ʿāsīs*, “juice”).
