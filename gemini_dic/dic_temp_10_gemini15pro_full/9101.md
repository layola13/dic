antimonate:/ˈæntɪməneɪt/ | n. |锑酸盐|A salt of antimonic acid.  It contains the anion SbO₄³⁻ or SbO₃⁻. |Sodium antimonate is used as a flame retardant. 锑酸钠用作阻燃剂。|Synonyms:  Stibnate (less common)|Antonyms: None (specific chemical term)|noun


antimonite:/ˈæntɪmənaɪt/ | n. |亚锑酸盐|A salt of antimonous acid containing the anion SbO₂⁻ or SbO₃³⁻. |Potassium antimonite is used in some chemical processes. 亚锑酸钾用于某些化学过程中。|Synonyms:  Stibnite (more commonly refers to the mineral)|Antonyms: None (specific chemical term)|noun
