nonbitterest: /nɒnˈbɪtərɪst/
adjective
最不苦的;  苦味最淡的
Not the least bitter; having the least bitterness.
This grapefruit is the nonbitterest I've ever tasted.  这个柚子是我尝过最不苦的。
Near Antonyms: bitterest, most bitter
Antonyms: sweetest
adjective

Etymology:
non- + bitter + -est
