interlocker:/ˈɪntərˌlɑːkər/ | n. |联锁装置，联锁器|A device or system that prevents two or more operations from occurring simultaneously, ensuring safety or proper sequencing. |The interlocker prevented the train from entering the occupied section of track.  这台联锁装置防止火车进入已被占用的轨道段。|Synonyms: safety device, lock, interlocking mechanism |Antonyms:  N/A | noun

interlock:/ˌɪntərˈlɑːk/ | v. |联锁，互锁，扣紧| To connect or join together so that the parts cannot move independently. |The gears interlock to transfer power smoothly. 齿轮相互啮合以平稳地传递动力。 | Synonyms:  mesh, engage, connect, link | Antonyms: disconnect, disengage, separate | verb
