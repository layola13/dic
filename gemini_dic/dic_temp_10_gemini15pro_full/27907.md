cantel:/ˈkæntəl/ | n. | 边缘; 片段;  (尤指面包的) 外皮 | A piece or fragment, especially of bread crust. |  He broke off a cantel of bread and dipped it in the soup. 他掰下一块面包皮，蘸着汤吃。 | 近义词: crust, piece, fragment | 反义词: whole, center | 词性: 名词

cantel:/ˈkæntəl/ | vt. | 分割; 切开 | To divide into parts or sections. | The baker canteled the dough into equal portions. 面包师把面团分成等份。 | 近义词: divide, cut, separate | 反义词: join, combine | 词性: 动词
