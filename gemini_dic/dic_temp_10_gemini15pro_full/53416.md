disannul:/dɪsəˈnʌl/
verb
废除，取消，宣告无效
to officially declare that something such as a law, agreement, or marriage is no longer legally valid.
The marriage was disannuled by the court. 法庭宣布婚姻无效。
synonyms: invalidate, nullify, annul, void, cancel, revoke, repeal
antonyms: validate, ratify, confirm, uphold, establish
