no-shows:/ˌnoʊ ˈʃoʊz/
noun
缺席者；预定后未到场的人
People who make reservations or appointments but fail to appear.
The restaurant had a lot of no-shows last night due to the bad weather. 由于天气恶劣，昨晚这家餐馆有很多预订的客人没有来。
近义词:absentees, defaulters
反义词:attendees, participants
词性:n.

no-show:/ˌnoʊ ˈʃoʊ/
noun
缺席；预定后未到场
The act of not appearing for a reservation or appointment.
There was a no-show fee for the concert tickets.  演唱会门票有缺席费。
近义词:absence, default
反义词:attendance, presence
词性:n.
