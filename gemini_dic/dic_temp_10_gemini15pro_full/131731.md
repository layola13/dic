nonconfiding:/ˌnɒnkənˈfaɪdɪŋ/
adjective
不信任的，不吐露心事的
Not inclined to confide; reserved.
She had a nonconfiding nature, preferring to keep her troubles to herself. 她性格内向，不愿吐露心事，宁愿把烦恼藏在心里。
Synonyms: reserved, reticent, secretive, private, close-mouthed, tight-lipped
Antonyms: open, communicative, trusting, confiding
词性: adjective
