multilateralisms:/ˌmʌltɪˈlætərəlɪzəmz/ | n. | 多边主义 | The principles or practice of cooperation among multiple nations or international actors.  It often involves agreements, treaties, or organizations that facilitate joint action on shared issues. |  The rise of multilateralisms offers hope for addressing global challenges. 多边主义的兴起为应对全球挑战带来了希望。 |  The conference focused on strengthening multilateralisms in trade and security. 会议的重点是加强贸易和安全领域的多边主义。 | synonyms:  multilateral cooperation, international cooperation, global governance | antonyms: unilateralism, isolationism | noun
