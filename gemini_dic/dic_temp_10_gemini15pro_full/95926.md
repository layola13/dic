incroyable:/ɛ̃.kʁwa.jabl/ | adj. | 不可思议的，惊人的 |  Meaning "unbelievable," derived from the French word "croire" (to believe) with the prefixes "in-" (not) and "a-" (not).  Used to express astonishment or admiration. | That concert was incroyable!  那场音乐会真是不可思议！| Synonyms: amazing, astonishing, unbelievable, extraordinary, remarkable | Antonyms: believable, ordinary, unimpressive, mediocre | Word Class: Adjective
