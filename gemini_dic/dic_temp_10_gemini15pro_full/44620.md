crowdstrike:/ˈkraʊdˌstraɪk/| n.|人群打击，群众打击（通常指CrowdStrike公司或其产品）|A cybersecurity technology company that provides endpoint security, threat intelligence, and incident response services.  It refers to proactively stopping breaches and attacks before they occur, rather than reactively responding to them after the fact.|CrowdStrike's Falcon platform is used by many large organizations.  CrowdStrike的Falcon平台被许多大型组织使用。|近义词：cybersecurity, endpoint protection, threat detection|反义词: vulnerability, weakness|名词
