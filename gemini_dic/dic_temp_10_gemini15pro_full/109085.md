legato:/lɪˈɡɑːtoʊ/ (US), /lɪˈɡɑːtəʊ/ (UK)| adj. adv. n.|连奏，流畅地|In a smooth, flowing manner, without breaks between notes.|例句：She sang the melody legato. 她流畅地唱出了这段旋律。|近义词：smoothly, connectedly|反义词：staccato|adverb
