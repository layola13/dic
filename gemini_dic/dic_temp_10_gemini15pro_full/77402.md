gauziness:/ˈɡɔːzinəs/| n. |薄纱感，轻薄，朦胧|The gauziness of the curtains allowed the sunlight to filter through softly. 窗帘的薄纱感让阳光柔和地透进来。|例句：The gauziness of the fabric made it perfect for summer dresses. 这种织物的轻薄使其非常适合制作夏季连衣裙。

例句：A gauziness hung over the distant mountains. 远山上笼罩着一层薄雾。

近义词：thinness, sheerness, transparency, flimsiness, ethereality|反义词:thickness, opacity, density|词性:名词

Etymology->
From gauze + -iness. Gauze itself comes from Middle French gaze, ultimately from Gaza, a city in Palestine where this type of fabric was originally made.
