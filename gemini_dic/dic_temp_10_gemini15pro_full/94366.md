implacentalia:/ˌɪmpləsɛnˈteɪliə/
|n.|无胎盘类|A subclass of mammals that includes marsupials and monotremes, characterized by the absence of a placenta during gestation.|The implacentalia represent a diverse group of mammals with unique reproductive strategies.  无胎盘类代表了具有独特繁殖策略的多样化哺乳动物群体。|
|近义词：Aplacentalia,  Metatheria (partially)|反义词：Placentalia, Eutheria|noun|

implacentalian:/ˌɪmpləsɛnˈteɪliən/
|adj.|无胎盘的|Relating to or characteristic of mammals that lack a placenta.|Implacentalian mammals have shorter gestation periods compared to placental mammals. 与有胎盘哺乳动物相比，无胎盘哺乳动物的妊娠期较短。|
|近义词：Aplacental|反义词：Placental|adjective|
