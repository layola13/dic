epistler:/ɪˈpɪslər/ | n. | 书信作者；写信人 | A person who writes letters, especially long or formal ones. |  Example: He was a prolific epistler, corresponding with many notable figures of his time. 他是一位多产的书信作者，与他那个时代的许多名人都有书信往来。

Example: The epistler expressed his concerns eloquently in his letter to the editor.  这位写信人在给编辑的信中雄辩地表达了他的担忧。| 近义词: letter writer, correspondent | 反义词: recipient | 词性: noun
