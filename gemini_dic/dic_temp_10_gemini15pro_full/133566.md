nonflagellate:/ˌnɒnˈflædʒɪleɪt/ | adj. | 无鞭毛的 | Lacking flagella; not flagellated.  A nonflagellate bacterium, for example, moves by other means, such as gliding. |  Certain nonflagellate bacteria can still move across surfaces.  某些无鞭毛细菌仍然可以在表面移动。| nonmotile, aflagellate | flagellate | adjective

Etymology->
non- (prefix): from Latin *nōn "not"
flagellate (verb/adjective): from Latin flagellum "whip, scourge," diminutive of flagrum "whip."  The biological sense of "whip-like appendage" dates from the 17th century.
