fraîcheur:/freˈʃœʁ/| n.|清新, 凉爽, 新鲜|The air had a delightful fraîcheur.  空气清新宜人。|例句：The fraîcheur of the morning air was invigorating.  早晨的清新空气令人精神焕发。

例句：The salad had a wonderful fraîcheur. 这沙拉有一种很棒的清新感。|近义词：freshness, coolness, crispness|反义词：staleness, warmth, mustiness|词性:noun

词源: From French fraîcheur, from frais (“fresh”) ultimately from Latin *frescus. Related to fresh.
