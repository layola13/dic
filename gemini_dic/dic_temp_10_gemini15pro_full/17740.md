bedesman:/ˈbiːdzmən/| n.|念珠人；受施舍者；（尤指旧时）领取救济金的老人|A person, often elderly and poor, who was given charitable aid and in return prayed for the benefactor.|The aged bedesman shuffled along the cloisters. 那位年迈的念珠人拖着脚步走过修道院.|Synonyms: almsman, pauper, pensioner|Antonyms: benefactor, donor|Noun
