limitlessnesses:/ˈlɪmɪtləsnəsɪz/
noun
无限；无界；无量
The state or quality of being limitless; vastness or infinity.
He spoke of the limitlessnesses of human potential. 他谈到了人类潜力的无限性。
Near Synonyms: boundlessness, endlessness, infinity, vastness, immensity
Antonyms: limitations, restrictions, finiteness, boundaries
