non-idiomatic:/ˌnɒnˌɪdiəˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 非惯用的，不自然的 | Not characteristic of the natural way in which a native speaker would express something; literal. |  His translation was technically correct, but non-idiomatic. 他的翻译在技术上是正确的，但很不自然。 | The sentence sounds non-idiomatic to a native English speaker.  这个句子对于英语母语人士来说听起来很不地道。 | 近义词: unnatural, awkward, stilted, literal | 反义词: idiomatic, natural, fluent | 词性: adjective

词源: "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "idiomatic" (from "idiom," from Greek "idioma" meaning "peculiar phraseology").
