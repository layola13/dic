chimaera:/kaɪˈmɪərə/ /kɪˈmɪrə/
noun
奇美拉；幻想；嵌合体
A fire-breathing female monster with a lion's head, a goat's body, and a serpent's tail. Also, a thing that is hoped or wished for but in fact is illusory or impossible to achieve.  A mythical creature.  In biology, it refers to an organism containing a mixture of genetically different tissues, formed by processes such as fusion of early embryos, grafting, or mutation.
例句：The chimaera of economic stability proved elusive.  经济稳定的幻想难以实现。

例句：Scientists have created a chimaera by combining cells from different species.  科学家们通过结合不同物种的细胞创造了一个嵌合体。
近义词：illusion, fantasy, delusion, fabrication, monster, hybrid
反义词：reality, truth
词性：n.
