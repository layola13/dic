dignify: /ˈdɪɡnɪfaɪ/ | vt. | 使...高贵，使...有尊严 | To give dignity or distinction to; make something seem worthy or impressive. |  He tried to dignify his rather menial tasks by referring to them as "research." 他试图通过将自己相当卑微的任务称为“研究”来使其显得高贵。

| She felt that wearing a suit would dignify the occasion. 她觉得穿西装会使这个场合显得更加庄重。 | 近义词: ennoble, exalt, elevate | 反义词: demean, degrade, humiliate | 词性: 动词

词源: From Late Latin *dignificare, from Latin dignus (“worthy”) + -ficare (from facere (“to make”)).
