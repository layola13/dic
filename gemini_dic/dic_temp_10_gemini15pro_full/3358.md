affricatives:/ˈæfrɪkətɪvz/ | n. | 塞擦音 |  Consonant sounds that begin as stops (complete closure of the airflow) and release as fricatives (partial closure creating friction).  |  Example: The sounds /tʃ/ as in "church" and /dʒ/ as in "judge" are affricatives.  教堂和法官这两个词中的/tʃ/和/dʒ/音是塞擦音。| 近义词: (none, as it's a specific phonetic category) | 反义词: (none, as it's a specific phonetic category) | noun
