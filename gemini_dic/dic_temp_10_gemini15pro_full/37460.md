commissionable:/kəˈmɪʃənəbl/
adjective
可付佣金的；可收佣金的
That which may earn a commission.  A commissionable sale, for example, is one for which a salesperson receives a percentage.
This position offers a generous base salary plus commissionable sales. 这个职位提供丰厚的底薪加上可赚取佣金的销售额。
The company offers a wide range of commissionable products.  这家公司提供各种可赚取佣金的产品。
Synonyms: remunerative, payable, rewardable
Antonyms: non-commissionable, salaried, fixed-income
词性: adjective
