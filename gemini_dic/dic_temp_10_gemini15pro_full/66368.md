excuser: /ɪkˈskjuːzər/ | n. | 借口者，辩解者 | Someone who makes excuses or apologizes, often excessively. | He's a chronic excuser; he always has a reason for not doing his work. 他是一个惯于找借口的人；他总有理由不完成工作。 |  Synonyms: apologizer, justifier | Antonyms:  accuser, blamer | noun

excuser: /ɪkˈskjuːzər/ | n. | 原谅者，宽恕者 (less common) | Someone who pardons or forgives.  | In this context, God is seen as the ultimate excuser. 在这种情况下，上帝被视为最终的宽恕者。 | Synonyms: forgiver, pardoner | Antonyms:  condemner, judge | noun
