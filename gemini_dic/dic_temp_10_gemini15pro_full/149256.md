oyster-like:/ˈɔɪstərˌlaɪk/
adjective
像牡蛎的；沉默寡言的
Resembling an oyster in appearance, texture, or flavor; or, metaphorically, silent and withdrawn.
The rock formations were oyster-like, rough and grey.  这些岩层像牡蛎一样，粗糙而灰暗。
He sat oyster-like, refusing to answer any questions. 他像牡蛎一样坐着，拒绝回答任何问题。
近义词:clam-like, shell-like, taciturn, reserved
反义词:communicative, talkative
词性:adjective

词源:
From oyster + -like.  "Oyster" comes from Old French "oistre," ultimately from Latin "ostrea."  "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
