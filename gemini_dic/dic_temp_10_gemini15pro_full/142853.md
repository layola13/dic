out-greening:/ˌaʊtˈɡriːnɪŋ/
verb
超越绿化;在环保方面胜过
Surpassing someone in environmentally friendly practices or policies.
The company is out-greening its competitors by investing heavily in renewable energy.  这家公司通过大力投资可再生能源，在环保方面胜过了竞争对手。
Tesla is out-greening traditional car manufacturers with its electric vehicles. 特斯拉凭借其电动汽车在环保方面超越了传统的汽车制造商。
Synonym: out-eco, surpass in environmentalism
Antonym: lagging in environmentalism, polluting more
词性:动词
