inexpugnably:/ˌɪnɪkˈspʌɡnəbli/
adverb
攻不可破地，无法征服地
in a way that cannot be overcome or defeated
例句：The fortress was inexpugnably defended.  这座堡垒防守得固若金汤。

例句：Their arguments were inexpugnably logical. 他们的论点逻辑上无懈可击。

近义词：invulnerably, unconquerably, impregnably
反义词：vulnerably, weakly

词性: adverb
