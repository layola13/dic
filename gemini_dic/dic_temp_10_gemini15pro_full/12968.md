asthenia:/æsˈθiːniə/ /æsˈθɛniə/
n.|虚弱，无力|A condition of bodily weakness or debility; loss of strength and energy.|The doctor diagnosed her with asthenia after she complained of persistent fatigue.|近义词:weakness, debility, fatigue, lassitude|反义词:strength, vigor, energy|词性:名词
Etymology: From Greek ἀσθένεια (asthéneia), from ἀσθενής (asthenḗs, “weak, without strength”), from ἀ- (a-, “without”) + σθένος (sthénos, “strength”).
