homages:/ˈhɒmɪdʒɪz/
n.
敬意，致敬，颂词
public expressions of great respect and honor
The film festival opened with a homage to the director's work.  电影节以向导演作品致敬的方式开幕。
The museum exhibition was a homage to the artist's life and work.  博物馆展览是对艺术家生活和工作的致敬。
tributes, respects, salutations
insults, criticisms
noun
