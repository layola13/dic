cc'ing:/ˈsiːsiːɪŋ/ |v. |抄送，发送副本|Adding someone to an email thread by including their email address in the "cc" field.  This allows them to see the conversation without being a direct recipient.| I'm cc'ing Sarah on this email so she's aware of the latest developments. 我把这封邮件抄送给莎拉，让她知道最新的进展。| synonyms: copying, including, looping in | antonyms: excluding, omitting | verb
