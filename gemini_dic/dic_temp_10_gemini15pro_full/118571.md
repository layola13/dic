mesoscutum:/ˌmɛzoʊˈskjuːtəm/| n. |中胸背板|The mesoscutum is the dorsal part of the mesothorax, which is located between the prothorax and metathorax in insects.  It is a major sclerite (hardened plate) that often plays a crucial role in flight and muscle attachment.|The mesoscutum of the dragonfly was iridescent.|近义词:mesonotum (more general term)|反义词:None|词性:noun
