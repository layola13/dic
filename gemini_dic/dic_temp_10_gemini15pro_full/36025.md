codirected:/ˌkoʊdəˈrɛktɪd/
verb
共同执导
jointly directed or managed.
例句：The film was codirected by two talented individuals. 这部电影由两位才华横溢的人共同执导。

例句：They codirected the project and achieved remarkable success. 他们共同指导了这个项目，取得了显著的成功。

近义词：jointly directed, collaboratively directed
反义词：solely directed, independently directed
词性: verb (过去分词)
