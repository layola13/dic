dodecasyllabic: /ˌdoʊˌdɛkəsɪˈlæbɪk/ /ˌdəʊˌdɛkəsɪˈlæbɪk/
adjective
十二音节的
Having twelve syllables.
Example:  The poet employed a dodecasyllabic meter in his latest work. 诗人在他的最新作品中使用了十二音节的韵律。

Example:  Dodecasyllabic lines are less common in English poetry than shorter lines. 十二音节的行在英文诗歌中比短行少见。
近义词: twelve-syllable,
反义词: monosyllabic, disyllabic (and other terms for fewer syllables)
词性: adjective
