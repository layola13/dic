alveolate:/ælˈviːəleɪt/ | adj. n.| 肺泡状的；有小窝的；[生] 窝孔状的 | Having small cavities or pits; resembling alveoli (the tiny air sacs in the lungs). Also used as a noun to refer to an organism belonging to the Alveolata group. | The surface of the diatom was alveolate. 硅藻的表面是肺泡状的。|The paramecium is an example of an alveolate. 草履虫是肺泡生物的一个例子。|  近义词: pitted, porous, cellular | 反义词: smooth, flat | 词性: adjective, noun

alveolate:/ælˈviːəleɪt/ | n. | 肺泡生物 | A single-celled eukaryote belonging to the Alveolata group, characterized by the presence of alveoli (sac-like structures) beneath the cell membrane.  | Dinoflagellates are a type of marine alveolate. 甲藻是一种海洋肺泡生物。 | Malaria is caused by Plasmodium, an alveolate parasite. 疟疾是由疟原虫引起的，它是一种肺泡寄生虫。| 近义词: protist | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin *alveolatus*, from Latin *alveolus* "small cavity, pit, socket," diminutive of *alveus* "hollow, cavity, trough, tub, beehive's cell."
