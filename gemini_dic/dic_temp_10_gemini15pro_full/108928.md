leeching:/ˈliːtʃɪŋ/
n.|水蛭疗法；榨取；依附；(尤指非法)下载|The practice of exploiting or attaching oneself to another for personal gain, often without giving anything in return, like a leech. Also, the process of downloading copyrighted material illegally.|He was accused of leeching off his wealthy parents.  他被指责依赖他富有的父母生活。|exploitation, parasitism, freeloading|contributing, giving, supporting|noun
leeching:/ˈliːtʃɪŋ/
v.|依附；榨取；(尤指非法)下载|(of a leech) suck blood; drain (someone) of money, energy, or another resource.|He's been leeching off his friends for years. 他多年来一直依赖他的朋友。|exploiting, draining,  sponging|giving, supporting, contributing|verb
