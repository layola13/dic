flecked:/flekt/ | adj. | 点缀的，斑点的 | Marked with small spots or streaks of color or other contrasting material. | The horse had a flecked coat of brown and white. 这匹马的毛皮是棕色和白色相间的。 | The bird's wings were flecked with black. 这只鸟的翅膀上点缀着黑色的斑点。 | spotted, speckled, dappled, mottled | solid, plain | adjective

flecked:/flekt/ | v. | 点缀，使…有斑点（fleck的过去式和过去分词）| To mark with small spots or streaks of color or other contrasting material.  | The artist flecked the canvas with paint. 艺术家在画布上点缀颜料。| The baker flecked the cake with chocolate chips. 面包师在蛋糕上撒了巧克力屑。 | spot, speckle, mottle | clear, plain | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *flecken*, from Old Norse *flekkr* ("spot, stain").
