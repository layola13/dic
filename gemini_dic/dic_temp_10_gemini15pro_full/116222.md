masonry:/ˈmeɪsənri/ /ˈmeɪsənri/| n.|石工；石工工艺；砖石结构| Something constructed of such materials, such as a wall or building. 由这种材料建造的东西，如墙壁或建筑物。|例句：The masonry of the old castle was crumbling. 古堡的砖石结构正在崩塌。

例句：The earthquake damaged the masonry of several buildings. 地震损坏了几座建筑物的砖石结构。|近义词：stonework, brickwork|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "woodwork" or "steel frame")|词性:noun
