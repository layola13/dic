beardlike:/ˈbiərdlaɪk/
adjective
像胡须的;似胡须的
Resembling a beard; similar to a beard in appearance or texture.
The old man had long, beardlike moss hanging from his chin.  这位老人的下巴上挂着长长的、像胡须一样的苔藓。
The roots were beardlike, thin and stringy. 这些根像胡须一样，又细又长。
近义词:  bushy, hairy, whiskered
反义词:  smooth, clean-shaven
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From beard + like.  由 beard（胡须）和 like（像）组成。
