mis-citation:/ˌmɪsaɪˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 错误引用，误引 | An act of quoting (something) incorrectly or inaccurately. |  The article contained a miscitation of a famous poem. 这篇文章错误地引用了一首著名诗歌。| misquotation, incorrect citation | correct citation | noun

Etymology:  "mis-" (Old English *mis-, from Proto-Germanic *missa- "wrongly, astray, amiss") + "citation" (from Latin "citatio," from "citare" "to summon, urge, rouse").
