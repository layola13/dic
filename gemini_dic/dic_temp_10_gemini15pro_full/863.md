abstractors:/æbˈstræktərz/| n.|摘要者，提取者|People who write abstracts, summaries of academic papers or legal documents.  They condense information into a concise and informative format. |The abstractors worked diligently to summarize the complex research findings. 摘要员们努力工作，以总结复杂的研究结果。

The abstractors were tasked with creating concise summaries of legal documents. 摘要员的任务是创建法律文件的简明摘要。|近义词：summarizers, condensers|反义词: elaborators, expanders|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "abstract" + "-or" (suffix forming nouns denoting an agent or person who performs an action).  "Abstract" comes from the Latin "abstractus," the past participle of "abstrahere" (to draw away from), from "abs-" (away) + "trahere" (to draw).
