now-now:/naʊ naʊ/ | adv. | 立刻，马上; (南非英语)目前，现在 | right away; at this moment (South African English) | "Now-now, children, settle down!"  孩子们，现在立刻安静下来！ "Things are a bit tough now-now." (South African English) 目前情况有点艰难。 | immediately, right away, at once | later | adverb
Etymology->
From reduplication of *now*.  The doubled form emphasizes immediacy.  The South African usage developed separately.

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you need now-now? = 你现在立刻需要什么？
He wants his payment now-now. = 他现在就要付款。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I'm dealing with it now-now. = 我正在处理它。
She's working on the project now-now. = 她现在正在进行这个项目。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I've just finished it now-now. = 我现在刚刚完成它。
He has arrived now-now. = 他现在已经到了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I've been waiting for you now-now. = 我一直在等你。
They've been working on it now-now. = 他们一直在做这件事。


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He demanded it now-now. = 他当时就要求了。
She left now-now. = 她当时就离开了。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was working on it now-now when you called. = 你打电话时我正在处理它。
She was leaving now-now when the phone rang. = 电话响的时候她正要离开。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
He had already left now-now. = 他当时已经离开了。
She had finished the work now-now. = 她当时已经完成了工作。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been waiting now-now when he finally arrived. = 他终于到达时，我已经等了一会儿了。
They had been working on it now-now when the power went out. = 断电时，他们一直在做这件事。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I'll do it now-now. = 我马上就去做。
She'll be there now-now. = 她马上就到那里。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I'll be finishing it now-now. = 我马上就要完成了。
They'll be leaving now-now. = 他们马上就要离开了。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I will have finished it now-now. = 我马上就要完成了。
She will have arrived now-now. = 她马上就要到了。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
I will have been working on it now-now. = 我马上就要做完了。
They will have been waiting now-now. = 他们马上就要等完了。

PHRASE->
Do it now-now! = 马上做！
Come here now-now! = 马上过来！
Finish it now-now! = 马上完成！
Get ready now-now! = 马上准备好！
Leave now-now! = 马上离开！


注意事项:
*  While "now-now" is common in South African English, it might be considered informal or even childish in other dialects.
* The reduplication emphasizes the immediacy of the action.
