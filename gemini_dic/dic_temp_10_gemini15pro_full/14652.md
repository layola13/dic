avowably:/əˈvaʊəbli/
adverb|公开地；可承认地；无疑地|in a way that can be openly acknowledged or admitted|He avowably supported the new policy. 他公开支持这项新政策。|
近义词:openly, admittedly, undoubtedly, confessedly
反义词:secretly, covertly, privately
词性:副词

词源:
From avow + -ably.  Avow comes from Old French *avouer "to acknowledge, confess, admit, grant," from Latin advocare "to summon, call upon (as witness or advocate)," from ad- "to" + vocare "to call."  The suffix -ably forms adverbs meaning "in a specified manner."
