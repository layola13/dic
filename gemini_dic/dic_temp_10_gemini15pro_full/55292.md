dissolubility:/ˌdɪˌsɒljʊˈbɪləti/ /ˌdɪˌsɑːljʊˈbɪləti/
n.
可溶性，溶解度
The dissolubility of sugar in water is high.  糖在水中的溶解度很高。
The scientist measured the dissolubility of the compound. 科学家测量了该化合物的溶解度。
近义词：solubility
反义词：insolubility
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin dissolubilitās, from dissolūbilis (“that can be dissolved”), from dissolvere (“to dissolve”), from dis- (“apart”) + solvere (“to loosen”).
