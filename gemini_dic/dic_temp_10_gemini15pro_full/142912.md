out-sainting:/ˌaʊtˈseɪntɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的圣洁，比…更像圣人
Trying to appear more virtuous or morally superior than someone else.
She was always trying to out-saint her sister by volunteering for every charitable cause. 她总是试图通过自愿参加每一个慈善事业来胜过她姐姐的圣洁。
outdo, surpass, excel
-, -
词性:v.
