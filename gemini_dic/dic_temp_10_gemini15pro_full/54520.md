disjunctively:/dɪsˈdʒʌŋktɪvli/| adv.| 分离地，不相连地|In logic, two propositions are considered disjunctively when at least one of them is true. |The options were presented disjunctively: either accept the offer or walk away.  这两个选项以不相连的方式提出：要么接受提议，要么走开。|Synonyms: separately, individually, independently|Antonyms: conjunctively, jointly, together|词性:副词
