词源：From Old French *mailer (compare Old North French maillier), from maille “mail, mesh of a net” (from Latin macula “spot, mesh”) + -er (suffix forming verbs).  The original sense was “to wrap in mail, enclose in a bag or container.” This evolved into the modern meaning of "to send by post."
