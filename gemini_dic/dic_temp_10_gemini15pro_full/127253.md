nautiluses:/ˈnɔːtɪləsəz/
n.
鹦鹉螺（复数）
A cephalopod mollusk of the genus Nautilus, having a spiral, chambered shell and numerous short tentacles.
The nautiluses are fascinating creatures. 鹦鹉螺是很迷人的生物。

nautilus:/ˈnɔːtɪləs/
n.
鹦鹉螺（单数）
A cephalopod mollusk of the genus Nautilus, having a spiral, chambered shell and numerous short tentacles.
The nautilus is a living fossil. 鹦鹉螺是一种活化石。

近义词: cephalopod, mollusk
反义词:  (无明确的反义词，可以考虑与之对比的生物，例如：vertebrate 脊椎动物)
词性: noun
