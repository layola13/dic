cloff: /klɒf/ | n. |悬崖，峭壁|A high, steep face of rock, earth, or ice. |例句：The cloff offered a stunning view of the ocean. 峭壁提供了令人惊叹的海景。

例句：He carefully climbed down the cloff. 他小心翼翼地爬下悬崖。

近义词：cliff, precipice, bluff |反义词:plain, valley|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *clof, from Old Norse *klof (“cleft, fissure”). Cognate with Icelandic klof (“cleft”). Related to cleave.
