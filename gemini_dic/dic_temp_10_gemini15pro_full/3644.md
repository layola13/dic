agency:/ˈeɪdʒənsi/|n.|代理；机构；作用；媒介|An agency is a business or organization that provides a particular service, especially on behalf of other businesses or organizations.  An agency can also refer to the capacity, power, or instrumentality of achieving a particular effect.|例句：The travel agency booked our flights and hotels. 旅行社预订了我们的航班和酒店。

例句：The advertising agency created a successful campaign for the new product. 这家广告公司为新产品打造了一场成功的营销活动。|近义词：bureau, office, company, organization, firm|反义词:inaction, inactivity|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *agentia, from Latin agens (present participle of agere, “to do, drive, act”), + -ia.
