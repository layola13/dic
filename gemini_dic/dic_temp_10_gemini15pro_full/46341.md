dacoity: /dəˈkɔɪti/ (UK) /dəˈkɔɪti/ (US)| n.|抢劫；盗匪行为|The act of robbery by a band of armed robbers; banditry.  It typically involves violence or intimidation and is often committed in rural areas.  |The dacoity terrified the villagers. 村民们被这次抢劫吓坏了。|Synonyms: robbery, banditry, brigandage, highway robbery, plundering|Antonyms:  donation, charity|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi डकैती (ḍakaitī), from डकैत (ḍakait, “robber”), ultimately from Sanskrit द्रुघट (drugháṭa, “injurer”).
