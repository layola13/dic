congressionalists:/kənˈɡrɛʃənəlɪsts/
noun
国会议员（统称），国会主义者
Members of a congress, especially the US Congress; advocates of a congressional system of government.
The congressionalists debated the new healthcare bill fiercely. 国会议员们激烈地辩论了新的医疗保健法案。
Congressmen and women met to discuss the budget. 男女国会议员们开会讨论预算。
synonyms:  congressmen, congresswomen, representatives, senators, legislators
antonyms:  citizens, constituents, voters
