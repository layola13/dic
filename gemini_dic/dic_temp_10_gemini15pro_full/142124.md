origenism:/ˈɔːrɪdʒənɪzəm/
n.
奥利金主义，俄利根主义
The doctrines of Origen, an early Christian theologian (c. 185–254), especially his belief in the preexistence of souls and universal salvation.  Origen's teachings, though influential, were later declared heretical.
Origenism was condemned as a heresy. 奥利金主义被判为异端。
Origenism played a significant role in the development of early Christian thought.  奥利金主义在早期基督教思想的发展中扮演了重要的角色。
近义词:heresy, heterodoxy
反义词:orthodoxy
词性:noun
