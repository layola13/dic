habitan:/ˈhæbɪtæn/ (US), /ˈhæbɪtən/ (UK)| n.|居民，居住者|A person or animal that lives in or occupies a place.  A dweller; resident.|例句：The habitans of the island were friendly and welcoming. 岛上的居民友好而热情。

例句：The desert habitans have adapted to the harsh conditions. 沙漠居民已经适应了恶劣的环境。|近义词：resident, dweller, inhabitant, occupant|反义词: visitor, tourist, transient|词性: noun
