baffling:/ˈbæflɪŋ/| adj.|令人困惑的，难以理解的|Something baffling is extremely confusing and difficult to understand.|The disappearance of the plane is baffling investigators.|Synonyms: perplexing, puzzling, bewildering, mystifying|Antonyms: clear, obvious, understandable|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From baffle (v.) + -ing. Baffle (v.) comes from the Old French bafler "to muzzle, deceive," of uncertain origin.
