deliriousnesses:/dɪˈlɪriəsnɪsɪz/
|n.|神志不清；谵妄状态；极度兴奋|A state of mental confusion, often accompanied by hallucinations and disordered speech, typically caused by fever, intoxication, or illness. Also, a state of wild excitement or ecstasy.|He experienced deliriousnesses due to the high fever.|近义词: delirium, frenzy, madness, mania, ecstasy|反义词: lucidity, sanity, coherence|词性: 名词
