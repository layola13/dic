cyatholith:/ˈsaɪəθəˌlɪθ/ |n.|杯状结石|A concretion, usually of calcium carbonate, formed within the cell walls of certain plants, especially algae.  It resembles a small cup or goblet.|Calcium carbonate cyatholiths were observed in the leaves. 在叶片中观察到碳酸钙杯状结石。|Synonyms: concretion, calculus|Antonyms: N/A|noun
