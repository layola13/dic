hostships:/ˈhoʊstʃɪps/
n.
主办权; 东道主身份; 主办活动
The act or privilege of being a host, especially for an event or organization.
Our university won the hostship for the international conference. 我们的大学获得了国际会议的主办权。
The city's hostship of the Olympics brought in millions of tourists.  该城市主办奥运会吸引了数百万游客。
近义词: hosting, sponsorship
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider "participation" or "attendance" as contrasting concepts)
词性: 名词
