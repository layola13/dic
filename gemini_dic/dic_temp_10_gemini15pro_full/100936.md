inunct:/ɪˈnʌŋkt/
verb
涂油，敷油，上油膏
To apply an ointment or oil to; anoint.
The ancient Egyptians inuncted their bodies with fragrant oils. 古埃及人用香油涂抹身体。
近义词: anoint, smear, rub, apply
反义词: cleanse, wash
