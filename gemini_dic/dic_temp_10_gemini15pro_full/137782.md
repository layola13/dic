nonvegetativenesses:/nɒnˌvɛdʒɪˈteɪtɪvnɪsɪz/
|n.| 非植物性状态（复数）|The lack of characteristics or properties typically associated with plants or vegetative growth; the state of being nonvegetative.  This refers to a state where something doesn't exhibit the signs of plant life, like growth, reproduction, or metabolic processes. This word is rarely used.| Due to the extreme cold, the plants entered a state of nonvegetativenesses. 由于极端寒冷，植物进入了一种非植物状态。|Near synonyms: inactivity, dormancy, lifelessness|Antonyms: vegetativeness, growth, life|Noun
