monociliated:/ˌmɒnəˈsɪlieɪtɪd/ | adj. | 单纤毛的 | Having a single cilium.  A cilium is a microscopic, hair-like structure on the surface of some cells, used for movement or sensing. Monociliated refers to having only one such cilium. |  Monociliated cells play a crucial role in embryonic development. 单纤毛细胞在胚胎发育中起着至关重要的作用。|  The researchers identified a new type of monociliated receptor cell in the nose. 研究人员在鼻子中发现了一种新型的单纤毛受体细胞。 | uniciliated, uniflagellate | multiciliated | adjective
