molten:/ˈmoʊltən/ (US) /ˈməʊltən/ (UK)| adj.|熔化的；熔融的|Made liquid by heat; melted.|例句：Molten lava flowed down the side of the volcano. 熔岩顺着火山侧流了下来。

例句：The molten metal was poured into the mold.  熔化的金属被倒入模具中。|近义词：melted, fused, liquefied|反义词：solid, frozen|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *melten, from Old English *molten, past participle of meltan (“to melt”), from Proto-Germanic *meltaną (“to melt”), from Proto-Indo-European *méltei (“melts, softens”). Cognate with West Frisian molten (“melted”), Dutch molten (“to malt”), German Malz (“malt”), Icelandic melta (“to melt”).
