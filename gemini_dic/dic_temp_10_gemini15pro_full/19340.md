bibliological:/ˌbɪbliəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
目录学的；书志学的
Relating to the study of books, especially their history, printing, and binding.
This bibliological study traces the history of the Gutenberg Bible. 这项目录学研究追溯了古腾堡圣经的历史。

The bibliological details of this first edition are fascinating. 这本初版书籍的书志学细节令人着迷。
近义词:bibliographic
反义词:None
词性:adjective
