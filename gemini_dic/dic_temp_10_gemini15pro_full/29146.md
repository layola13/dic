casemented:/ˈkeɪsməntɪd/| adj.|装有窗扇的，有窗框的|Fitted with casements; having casements.|The casemented windows offered a picturesque view of the garden.  装有窗扇的窗户提供了花园如画的景色。|近义词:windowed, glazed|反义词:windowless, unglazed|词性:形容词
Etymology: From casement + -ed.  Casement itself comes from Old French "caser" meaning "to frame."
