allurement:/əˈljʊəmənt/ | n. |诱惑，吸引力 |Something that tempts or attracts; an attraction.| The allurement of the big city drew her away from her small town. 大城市的诱惑让她离开了小镇。|  近义词: attraction, temptation, lure, enticement, charm | 反义词: repulsion, deterrent, discouragement | noun

alluring:/əˈljʊərɪŋ/ | adj. | 诱惑的，迷人的 |Tempting and attractive; enticing.|  She found his smile alluring. 她觉得他的笑容很迷人。| 近义词: attractive, tempting, enticing, captivating, charming | 反义词: repulsive, repellent, unappealing | adjective
