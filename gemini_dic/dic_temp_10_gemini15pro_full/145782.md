overdazzled: /ˌoʊvərˈdæzəld/ | adj. | 目眩的，眼花缭乱的 |  Made unable to see clearly by a very strong light or an excess of something visually stimulating. |  The photographers' flashbulbs overdazzled the actress as she stepped out of the limousine.  女演员走出豪华轿车时，摄影师的闪光灯让她眼花缭乱。 | synonyms: dazzled, blinded, overwhelmed | antonyms: unimpressed, unmoved, unaffected | adjective
