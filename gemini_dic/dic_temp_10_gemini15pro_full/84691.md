handful:/ˈhændfʊl/ (US), /ˈhændfʊl/ (UK)| n.|一把；少量；难以控制的人（或动物）|A small number or amount that can be held in the hand.  Can also refer to a person or animal that is difficult to control.|例句：She grabbed a handful of almonds from the bowl. 她从碗里抓了一把杏仁。

例句：He's a real handful - always getting into trouble. 他真是个麻烦精，总是惹事。|近义词：few, smattering, small number, small amount|反义词:large amount, lot, plenty|词性:名词
