ablution:/əˈbluːʃən/ | n. | 洗礼，净礼；沐浴 | the ritual washing of a priest's hands or of sacred vessels.  The act of washing oneself (especially as a religious ritual). |  The priest performed his ablutions before the Mass.  神父在弥撒前进行了净礼。

Ablutions are an important part of many religious ceremonies.  沐浴是许多宗教仪式的重要组成部分。 | 近义词：washing, cleansing, purification, bath, shower | 反义词：pollution, contamination | 词性: 名词
