anisopleura:/ˌænˌaɪsoʊˈplʊərə/ | n. |异脉蜻属|A genus of dragonflies in the family Euphaeidae, commonly known as pondhawks.|Anisopleura comes from Greek, "anisos" meaning "unequal" and "pleura" meaning "side" referring to the unequal sides of the discoidal cell in the wings.  This genus is found mainly in Asia.|例句：The Anisopleura zhengi is a recently discovered species of pondhawk.  郑氏异脉蜻是最近发现的一种异脉蜻属蜻蜓。

例句：Several species of Anisopleura are known for their bright metallic colours.  一些异脉蜻属的物种以其鲜艳的金属色泽而闻名。|近义词：dragonfly, pondhawk |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
