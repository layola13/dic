outscooped:/ˌaʊtˈskuːpt/
verb
抢先报道，胜过，比...先得到
To get a news story before a competitor; to get something before someone else.
The rival newspaper outscooped us on the mayor's resignation. 对手报社抢在我们前面报道了市长辞职的消息。
The local reporter outscooped all the national newspapers with the story.  当地记者抢在所有全国性报纸之前报道了这个故事。
近义词:beat, forestall, preempt
反义词:be scooped
