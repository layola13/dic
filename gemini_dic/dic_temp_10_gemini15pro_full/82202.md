grossular:/ˈɡrɒsjʊlə(r)/ (US) /ˈɡrɒsjʊlə/ (UK)| n. |绿宝石，醋栗石|A translucent green gemstone, a variety of garnet.  It is also known as gooseberry garnet due to its resemblance to the fruit.|She wore a necklace featuring a large, luminous grossular. 她戴着一条项链，上面有一颗又大又亮的绿宝石。|Synonyms: gooseberry garnet, tsavorite (a bright green variety)|Antonyms: N/A|noun

grossular:/ˈɡrɒsjʊlə(r)/ (US) /ˈɡrɒsjʊlə/ (UK)| adj. |绿宝石的，醋栗石的|Relating to or made of grossular.|The grossular crystals were embedded in the matrix. 绿宝石晶体嵌在基质中。|Synonyms:  gooseberry garnet, tsavorite|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
