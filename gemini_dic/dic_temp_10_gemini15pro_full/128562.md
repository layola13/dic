ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *knibblen, frequentative of *nibben (“to nip, bite”), from Old English *nibban (“to peck, pick, bite”), of imitative origin. Cognate with Dutch knibbelen (“to nibble”), German knibbeln (“to nibble”), Norwegian knibla (“to nibble”).
