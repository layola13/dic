individualizers:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlaɪzərz/
noun
个性化者; 个体化者;
People who promote or create individualized experiences, products, or services.  Those who emphasize or cater to the needs and preferences of individuals.
例句：The individualizers in the education system are pushing for personalized learning plans for each student. 教育系统中的个性化者正在推动为每个学生制定个性化的学习计划。

例句：The company employs individualizers to tailor their marketing campaigns to specific demographics.  该公司雇用了个性化者来根据特定的人口统计数据定制他们的营销活动。

近义词：customizers, personalizers, tailors
反义词：standardizers, generalists

词性: noun (plural)
词源: From individualize + -er (agent suffix), ultimately from Latin individuus "indivisible" + -ize (verb-forming suffix).
