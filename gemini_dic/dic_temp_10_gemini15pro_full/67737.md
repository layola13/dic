eye-opener:/ˈaɪˌoʊpənər/| n. |令人瞠目结舌的事物；使人醒悟的事情|Something surprising or enlightening that changes your view of something.|例句：The documentary was a real eye-opener about the plight of refugees. 这部纪录片让人们真正了解了难民的困境。

例句：Traveling to India was a real eye-opener for me. 去印度旅行对我来说是一次真正的开眼界之旅。|近义词：revelation, surprise, discovery, enlightenment|反义词:disappointment, letdown|词性:noun
