lithotriptic: /ˌlɪθəˈtrɪptɪk/
adjective|碎石的，溶石的|Relating to, or causing lithotripsy; dissolving or crushing stones, especially calculi in the kidneys or bladder.|Lithotriptric medications can help dissolve kidney stones.|近义词:antilithic|反义词: (No direct antonym, but one could consider words related to stone formation, like "calcifying")
noun|碎石剂|A medicine or agent that dissolves or crushes stones, especially calculi in the kidneys or bladder.|The doctor prescribed a lithotriptic to help break down the kidney stones.|近义词:antilithic|反义词: (No direct antonym)
