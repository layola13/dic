commensalities:/kəˌmɛnsəˈlɪtiz/ | n. | 共餐; 共食; 共生性;  共栖; 共食关系 |The act or practice of eating together; fellowship at table.  The state of sharing a table or meal.  A social group that eats together. |The family enjoyed the commensalities of their holiday dinner.  家人享受着节日晚餐的共餐时光。|  The anthropologists studied the commensalities of the tribe, observing their rituals around food.  人类学家研究了该部落的共食习俗，观察了他们围绕食物的仪式。 |synonyms:  communion, fellowship, conviviality, togetherness| antonyms: isolation, solitude| noun
