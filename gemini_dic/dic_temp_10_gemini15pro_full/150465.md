pantheists:/ˈpænθiːɪsts/ | n. | 泛神论者 | People who believe in pantheism, the doctrine that God is everything and everywhere, or that God is identical with the universe. | Many pantheists find spiritual connection in nature. 许多泛神论者在大自然中找到精神联系。| Pantheists believe that the divine permeates all aspects of the universe. 泛神论者相信神性弥漫于宇宙的各个方面。 | 近义词: nature worshippers, | 反义词: atheists, theists | noun
