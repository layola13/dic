condolatory: /kənˈdoʊləˌtɔːri/ /kənˈdɒlətri/ | adj. | 吊唁的，慰问的 | Expressing or offering sympathy or condolence.  |  She sent a condolatory letter to the bereaved family. 她给丧失亲人的家庭寄了一封慰问信。 |  He offered his condolatory remarks. 他表达了慰问之情。 | 近义词: sympathetic, compassionate, consoling | 反义词: unsympathetic, indifferent | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Late Latin *condolatorius, from Latin condolere "to suffer together," from com- "together" (see com-) + dolere "to grieve" (see doleful).
