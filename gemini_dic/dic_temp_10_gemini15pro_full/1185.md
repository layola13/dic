acclimatize: /əˈklaɪmətaɪz/ (UK) /əˈklaɪməˌtaɪz/ (US)| v.| 适应，适应环境；使适应环境| To become or make someone become used to a new climate or situation. |It took him a while to acclimatize to the heat.  他花了一段时间才适应炎热的天气。

The climbers spent a few days acclimatizing themselves to the high altitude. 登山者们花了几天时间来适应高海拔环境。|近义词: adapt, adjust, accustom|反义词: unacclimatize|词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
She acclimatizes quickly to new environments. = 她很快就能适应新环境。
He acclimatizes easily to different cultures. = 他很容易适应不同的文化。

一般过去时:
The team acclimatized to the altitude before climbing the mountain. = 队伍在爬山之前适应了海拔高度。
It took several weeks for the new students to acclimatize to the school. = 新生花了几个星期才适应学校。

现在完成时:
They have acclimatized to the hot weather. = 他们已经适应了炎热的天气。
She has acclimatized herself to the new job. = 她已经适应了新工作。

过去完成时:
The explorers had acclimatized to the cold before they reached the North Pole. = 探险家们在到达北极之前就已经适应了寒冷。
By the time we arrived, she had already acclimatized to the time difference. = 当我们到达时，她已经适应了时差。

将来时:
The plants will acclimatize to the indoor environment. = 植物将适应室内环境。
We will need to acclimatize to the lower oxygen levels at high altitude. = 我们需要适应高海拔地区较低的氧气水平。

虚拟语气:
If I were to move to a colder climate, I would need to acclimatize slowly. = 如果我要搬到一个较冷的气候，我需要慢慢适应。
I wish I could acclimatize to this heat more easily. = 我希望我能更容易地适应这种炎热。

被动语态:
The animals were acclimatized to the new environment gradually. = 动物们逐渐适应了新环境。
The seedlings are being acclimatized to the outdoor conditions. = 幼苗正在适应室外条件。

疑问句:
How long does it take to acclimatize to high altitude? = 适应高海拔需要多长时间？
Are you acclimatized to the time difference yet? = 你适应时差了吗？

否定句:
He did not acclimatize well to the humid climate. = 他不太适应潮湿的气候。
They haven't acclimatized to the new working hours yet. = 他们还没有适应新的工作时间。
