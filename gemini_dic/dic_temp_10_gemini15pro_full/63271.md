enthelminthes:/ˌɛnθɛlˈmɪnθiːz/
| n. |内蠕虫类，线形动物门|A phylum of wormlike animals having a long, slender, unsegmented body with a complete digestive tract. This group includes roundworms, hookworms, and pinworms.|例句：Enthelminthes are a diverse group of animals found in a variety of habitats. 内蠕虫类是种类繁多的动物，生活在各种各样的栖息地。

例句：Some species of enthelminthes are parasitic and can cause diseases in humans and animals. 一些内蠕虫类物种是寄生虫，会导致人类和动物的疾病。|近义词：Nematoda, Nemathelminthes|反义词: None|词性: Noun


词源:
From Greek *en* (in) + *helmins* (worm) + *-thes* (plural suffix)
