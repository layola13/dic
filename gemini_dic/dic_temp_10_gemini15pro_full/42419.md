countercries:/ˈkaʊntərkraɪz/
n.
反驳的呼喊，反对的呼声
An outcry or clamor in opposition to another; shouts or cries in answer to others.
The crowd's countercries drowned out the speaker.  人群的反驳声淹没了演讲者。
countercries filled the air as the verdict was read.  宣读判决时，反对的呼声响彻空中。
Synonyms: protests, objections, retorts, rebuttals
Antonyms: agreements, approvals, endorsements
noun

countercry:/ˈkaʊntərkraɪ/
n.
反驳的呼喊，反对的呼声
A cry uttered in response to another cry, especially one of defiance or opposition.
His countercry was immediate and sharp.  他的反驳迅速而尖锐。
The king's decree was met with a countercry of dissent.  国王的命令遭到了反对的呼喊。
Synonyms: retort, rejoinder, riposte, response
Antonyms: agreement, acceptance, acquiescence
noun



ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- + cry.  Counter- means "against" or "in opposition to".  Cry means "to shout or call out loudly."
