micronodular:/ˌmaɪkrəˈnɒdjʊlə(r)/ | adj. |微小结节的|Characterized by the presence of small nodules.  Micronodular refers to nodules that are small, typically less than 3 mm in diameter, and often numerous.  It is often used in medical contexts, particularly in describing the appearance of tissues or organs. | The liver biopsy revealed micronodular cirrhosis.  肝脏活检显示微小结节性肝硬化。

The radiologist identified micronodular opacities in the lungs. 放射科医生在肺部发现了微小结节性阴影。 |  nodular, small-noduled | large-noduled, macronodular | adjective
