bestowable:/bɪˈstoʊəbl/
adjective
可授予的，可赋予的
capable of being bestowed; that may be conferred as a gift, privilege, etc.
The Queen bestowed a knighthood upon him. 女王授予他爵位。
The medal is bestowable upon any citizen who performs an act of great courage. 这枚奖章可以授予任何做出英勇行为的公民。
近义词:conferrable, grantable, awardable
反义词:unworthy, undeserving
词性:adjective
