coot:/kuːt/| n.| 傻瓜，笨蛋；(北美)一种水鸟，白骨顶鸡|A small black water bird with a white beak and forehead, found in Europe and North America.  Also, an informal term for a foolish or eccentric person. |The lake was full of coots. 湖里有很多白骨顶鸡。|He's a bit of a coot, but he's harmless. 他有点傻，但无害。|近义词：fool, idiot, simpleton (for person); moorhen, grebe (for bird)|反义词:genius, clever person (for person)|noun
