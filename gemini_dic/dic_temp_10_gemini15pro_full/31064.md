chamfron:/ˈtʃæmfrən/ | n. |  马的额甲;  马的面甲 | A piece of armor that protected a horse's head, especially the forehead. | The knight's warhorse wore a gleaming chamfron, protecting it from enemy blows.  骑士的战马戴着闪闪发光的额甲，保护它免受敌人的打击。 | Near synonyms:  headpiece,  armor,  barding | Antonyms: None (lack of armor isn't a direct antonym) | noun
