limelighters:/ˈlaɪmˌlaɪtərz/
n.
聚光灯下的人；引人注目的人；（尤指）演艺人员
People who are in the public eye, especially entertainers.
The limelightters of the 1960s folk music scene.  20世纪60年代民歌界的风云人物。
performers, stars, celebrities
obscurity, anonymity
noun
