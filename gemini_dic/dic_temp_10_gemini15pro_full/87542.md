heterographic:/ˌhɛtərəˈɡræfɪk/
adjective
异形异义的
Differently written (of words in different languages with the same meaning and etymology).  Relating to or being words spelled differently but pronounced the same.
The words "write" and "right" are heterographic.  “write”和“right”是异形异义词。
Heterographic homophones can be confusing for language learners. 异形异义同音词可能会让语言学习者感到困惑。
Near synonyms: homophonous (referring to sound), homographic (referring to writing, opposite meaning)
Antonyms: homographic
词性: adjective
