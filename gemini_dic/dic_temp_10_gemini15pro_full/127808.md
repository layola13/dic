neither-fish-nor-fowl:/ˌnaɪðər ˈfɪʃ nɔːr ˈfaʊl/ | adj. | 不伦不类；非驴非马；四不像 | Something that is neither fish nor fowl doesn't fit neatly into any category; it's hard to classify. |  The movie was a strange mix of comedy and drama – neither fish nor fowl. 这部电影是喜剧和戏剧的奇怪组合——不伦不类。 | synonyms: nondescript, anomalous, ambiguous, indeterminate, unclassifiable | antonyms: typical, characteristic, definite, clear-cut | adjective

neither-fish-nor-fowl:/ˌnaɪðər ˈfɪʃ nɔːr ˈfaʊl/ | n. | 不伦不类的事物；四不像的东西 |  A person or thing that is difficult to classify. | That new restaurant is neither fish nor fowl – it's not quite a cafe, but it's not a formal restaurant either.  那家新餐馆不伦不类——它既不像咖啡馆，也不像正式的餐厅。 | synonyms: oddity, anomaly | antonyms: standard, norm | noun
