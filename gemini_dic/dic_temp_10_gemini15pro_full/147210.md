overmannered:/ˌoʊvərˈmænərd/| adj.|过分礼貌的，拘谨的|Excessively polite or formal in behavior, often to the point of seeming artificial or insincere.| He was so overmannered it was hard to relax around him. 他太拘谨了，让人很难在他身边放松下来。|例句：Her overmannered behavior made her seem insincere. 她过分礼貌的行为让她显得不真诚。|近义词：prim, proper, stuffy, formal, ceremonious|反义词：casual, informal, natural, relaxed, unpretentious|词性:形容词
