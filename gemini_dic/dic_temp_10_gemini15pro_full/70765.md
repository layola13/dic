finesse:/fɪˈnɛs/ /fɪˈnɛs/| n. vt.|技巧；策略；精细；巧妙地处理|Skill and cleverness in handling a situation; to handle (a situation) with skill and cleverness.|She handled the delicate situation with finesse. 她巧妙地处理了这个微妙的局面。|He finessed the deal to his advantage. 他巧妙地操纵这笔交易，使自己获利。|近义词：skill, delicacy, tact, subtlety, diplomacy|反义词：clumsiness, awkwardness, gaucheness|词性:名词, 动词

Etymology:
From French *finesse*, from Old French *fin* (fine, thin, subtle) ultimately from Latin *finis* (end, limit, boundary).  The original meaning referred to refined delicacy and elegant skill in workmanship.
