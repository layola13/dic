concenter:/kənˈsɛntər/ | v. | 集中；聚集；浓缩 | To bring or direct (something, such as attention or resources) to a common center or a single object.  To converge or meet in a common center. | The troops concentered near the border. 部队在边境附近集结。| 近义词: concentrate, focus, converge | 反义词: disperse, scatter, diffuse | verb

concenter:/ˈkɒnsɛntə/ | n. | 中心；集中点 | A point or area at which things converge or are concentrated. | The market square was the concenter of the town's activities. 市场广场是城镇活动的中心。| 近义词: center, focus, core | 反义词: periphery, edge, margin | noun
