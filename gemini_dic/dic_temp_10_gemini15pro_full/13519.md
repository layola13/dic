attainers:/əˈteɪnərz/
n.|获得者，达到者，成功者|People who attain something, especially a particular age, level, or status. |The attainers of high office are often those who have sacrificed much.|近义词：achievers, winners, victors|反义词:losers, failures|词性:名词

Etymology:
From the verb "attain," which comes from Old French "ateindre" (to reach, attain), from Latin "attingere" (to touch upon, reach), from "ad" (to) + "tangere" (to touch).
