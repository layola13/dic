non-ionics: /ˌnɒn aɪˈɒnɪks/ | n. | 非离子型表面活性剂 |  Substances that do not dissociate into ions in solution, often used as detergents, emulsifiers, and wetting agents. |  Non-ionics are used in many cleaning products because they are gentle.  非离子型表面活性剂在许多清洁产品中都有使用，因为它们很温和。 | Synonyms: non-ionic surfactants, non-ionic detergents | Antonyms: ionic surfactants, cationic surfactants, anionic surfactants | noun

non-ionic: /ˌnɒn aɪˈɒnɪk/ | adj. | 非离子型的 | Not ionizing, not dissociating into ions in solution. | This detergent is non-ionic and therefore suitable for sensitive skin.  这种洗涤剂是非离子型的，因此适用于敏感肌肤。 | Synonyms: non-ionized, un-ionized | Antonyms: ionic, ionized | adjective
