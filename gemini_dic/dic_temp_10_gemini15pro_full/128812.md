nightlier:/ˈnaɪt.li.ər/
adjective
更夜的; 更像夜晚的
more nightly; more like night
The forest grew nightlier as the sun set.  随着太阳落山，森林变得更像夜晚了。
The sounds of the owls made the atmosphere nightlier. 猫头鹰的叫声使气氛更像夜晚了。
近义词:darker, gloomier
反义词:brighter, lighter
词性:adjective

词源:
From "nightly" + "-er" (comparative suffix).  "Nightly" comes from "night" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Night" ultimately derives from Proto-Germanic *nahts.
