asteroid:/ˈæstərɔɪd/ | n. |小行星|A relatively small, inactive, rocky body orbiting the Sun. |例句：The asteroid belt lies between Mars and Jupiter. 小行星带位于火星和木星之间。|例句：Scientists are tracking a large asteroid that might pose a threat to Earth. 科学家们正在追踪一颗可能对地球构成威胁的大型小行星。|近义词：planetoid, minor planet|反义词: planet, star |noun
