halloos:/həˈluːz/ | n. | 喊叫声，喧闹声 | A loud cry or shout, especially of excitement or joy. | The halloos of the crowd echoed through the stadium.  人群的喊叫声回荡在体育场内。 | synonyms: shouts, cries, yells, whoops | antonyms: whispers, murmurs | noun


halloos:/həˈluːz/ | v. (third-person singular simple present halloos, present participle hallooing, simple past and past participle hallooed) |  喊叫，大声呼喊 | To shout or call loudly, especially to attract attention. | He hallooed to his friends across the field. 他隔着田野向他的朋友们大声喊叫。 | synonyms: shout, yell, call, whoop | antonyms: whisper, murmur | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *haller "to urge dogs on," from Frankish *halon or some other Germanic source, from Proto-Germanic *hallon "to call."
