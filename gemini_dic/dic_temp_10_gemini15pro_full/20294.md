bisect:/baɪˈsekt/ /baɪˈsɛkt/| vt. n.|二等分，平分；交叉；<生>二分|To divide something into two equal parts.|The road bisects the town. 这条路把城镇一分为二。|近义词：divide, halve, split|反义词:join, unite|verb

bisect:/baɪˈsekt/ /baɪˈsɛkt/| n.|二等分线；<数>二分法|The act of bisecting something; a line that bisects something.|The bisector of the angle divided it into two equal parts. 角的平分线把它分成相等的两部分。|近义词：division, halving|反义词:combination|noun
