what:/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US) |pron. det. adv.|什么，多么|Used to ask for information about something. | What is your name? 你叫什么名字？|which, who | |pronoun/determiner/adverb


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does he do for a living? 他做什么工作？


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner?  她晚饭在做什么？


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has he done to improve his skills? 他做了什么来提高他的技能？


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has she been reading this week? 她这周一直在读什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? 她对这部电影说了些什么？


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you arrived here? 你来这之前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? 她生病前吃了什么？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the accident? 事故发生前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major?  她换专业之前一直在学什么？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after you graduate? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will she study in college? 她大学里要学什么？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be presenting at the conference?  她将在会议上展示什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will she have learned by the time she finishes the course?  到她完成课程时，她将学到什么？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做了什么十年了？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？



虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? 如果你中了彩票，你会做什么？
What would she say if she were here? 如果她在这里，她会说什么？


被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to address the issue? 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？



疑问句:
What time is it? 现在几点？
What is your favorite color? 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？


否定句:
I don't know what to do. 我不知道该做什么。
She doesn't understand what you mean. 她不明白你的意思。
