left-wingism:/ˌleftˈwɪŋɪzəm/ | n. |左翼主义|The belief in or support for liberal or socialist political ideals, typically involving social reform or intervention by the state.  It emphasizes social equality, collective responsibility, and government intervention to address economic and social inequality. |  The party's platform is rooted in left-wingism, advocating for policies like universal healthcare and free college tuition.  这个党的纲领植根于左翼主义，倡导诸如全民医保和免费大学学费之类的政策。|synonyms: liberalism, socialism, progressivism, radicalism| antonyms: right-wingism, conservatism, reactionaryism |noun
