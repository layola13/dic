multi-articular:/ˌmʌlti-ɑːrˈtɪkjʊlər/
| adj. |多关节的| Affecting or involving several joints. | The squat is a multi-articular exercise. 深蹲是一个多关节运动。|  The pain is multi-articular, affecting his knees, hips, and ankles. 疼痛是多关节的，影响了他的膝盖、臀部和脚踝。|近义词：polyarticular|反义词：monoarticular, single-joint|词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From multi- (many) + articular (relating to joints).  "Articular" comes from Latin "articulus" (small joint), diminutive of "artus" (joint).
