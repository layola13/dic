antipestilential:/ˌæntiˌpɛstɪˈlɛnʃəl/
adjective
防瘟疫的，抗疫的
Preventing or protecting against pestilence or plague.
The doctor prescribed an antipestilential medication during the outbreak.  医生在疫情爆发期间开了一种防瘟疫的药物。
The community adopted antipestilential measures to prevent the spread of the disease. 社区采取了防瘟疫措施来防止疾病传播。

近义词:antiepidemic, prophylactic, preventative
反义词:pestilential, contagious, infectious
词性:adjective

词源:
From anti- "against" + pestilential "relating to a pestilence or plague."  Pestilential ultimately comes from the Latin word "pestilentia" meaning "plague, epidemic."
