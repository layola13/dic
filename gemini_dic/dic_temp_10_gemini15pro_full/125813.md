murmurer:/ˈmɜːrmərər (UK), ˈmɜːrmərər (US)| n.|低语者，咕哝者|A person who murmurs; someone who speaks in a low, indistinct voice. |The murmurer in the back of the room was disrupting the lecture.  房间后面低语的人扰乱了讲座。|近义词：whisperer, grumbler, complainer|反义词：shouter, orator|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *murmurere*, from Anglo-Norman *murmurur*, from Old French *murmureor*, from Latin *murmurātor*, agent noun from *murmurāre* "to murmur".
