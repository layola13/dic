blue-on-blue:/ˌbluː ɒn ˈbluː/| adj.|  误伤；友军攻击；| An attack on one's own forces or allies, typically by mistake.  It refers to incidents where friendly forces inadvertently engage and harm each other. The term originates from military exercises where opposing forces were identified by different colored flags or uniforms (blue representing friendly forces). |  The inquiry concluded that the helicopter crash was a tragic case of blue-on-blue.  调查结论是，直升机坠毁是一起友军误伤的悲剧事件。

The general was furious about the blue-on-blue incident that led to the death of several soldiers. 将军对导致几名士兵死亡的友军误伤事件非常愤怒。 | 近义词: friendly fire, accidental attack | 反义词: enemy attack, hostile fire | 词性: 形容词
