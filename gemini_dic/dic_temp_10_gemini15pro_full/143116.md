outbulking:/ˌaʊtˈbʌlkɪŋ/
verb
增大, 使体积增大; 通过增大体积来超越或胜过
The act of surpassing something or someone in bulk or size.
He’s trying to outbulk his competitors by consuming vast quantities of protein. 他正试图通过摄入大量的蛋白质来超越他的竞争对手。
近义词: outgrowing, exceeding, surpassing
反义词: shrinking, reducing, diminishing
词性: v.


ETYMOLOGY->
"out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding, surpassing") + "bulk" (noun meaning "size, mass")
