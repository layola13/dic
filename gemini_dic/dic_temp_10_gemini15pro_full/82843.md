gulfing:/ˈɡʌlfɪŋ/
verb, adjective|吞没，吞噬；打高尔夫球|The flames were gulfing the building.  火焰吞噬了那栋建筑。|例句：The kayaker was gulfed by a huge wave. 那位皮划艇运动员被巨浪吞没了。|近义词：engulfing, swallowing, consuming, overwhelming|反义词: releasing, freeing|词性:v./adj.
gulfing:/ˈɡʌlfɪŋ/
adjective|狂饮的，暴食的|He had a gulfing appetite. 他胃口极大。|例句：His gulfing greed led him to financial ruin. 他贪得无厌导致他破产。|近义词：voracious, ravenous, insatiable|反义词: moderate, controlled|词性:adj.

ETYMOLOGY->
From gulf + -ing.  Ultimately from Greek *kólpos* "bosom, bay, gulf."
