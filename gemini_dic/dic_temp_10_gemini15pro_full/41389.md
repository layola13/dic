cormus:/ˈkɔːməs/| n.|植物体；茎干系统|The cormus of a plant consists of the stem, leaves, and reproductive structures.|例句：The botanist studied the cormus of the rare orchid to understand its growth patterns. 植物学家研究了稀有兰花的茎干系统以了解其生长模式。

例句：Damage to the cormus can significantly affect the plant's ability to photosynthesize. 茎干系统的损害会严重影响植物的光合作用能力。|近义词：shoot system, plant body|反义词: root system|词性:noun
