decrew:/diˈkruː/
verb
卸下船员;使（船）无人驾驶
to remove the crew from (a ship); to leave (a ship) unmanned
The ship was decrewed after the engine failure. 引擎故障后，船员被撤离了。
近义词: abandon, desert
反义词: man, crew
词性: verb

decrew:/diˈkruː/
verb
减少工作人员
to reduce the number of staff
The company was forced to decrew its operations due to budget cuts. 由于预算削减，公司被迫减少了运营人员。
近义词: downsize, reduce staff
反义词: increase staff, hire
词性: verb
