extra-illustrator:/ˌekstrə ˈɪləstreɪtər/
n.
书籍插图补充者；书籍插图外加者
A person who adds extra illustrations to a book, often by inserting prints, drawings, or other materials.
He was a keen extra-illustrator, expanding his copy of Boswell's "Life of Johnson" with hundreds of portraits and views. 他是一位热衷于书籍插图补充的人，用数百幅肖像和风景画扩展了他的《博斯韦尔约翰逊传》副本。
The extra-illustrator meticulously sourced engravings to enhance the narrative.  这位书籍插图补充者精心挑选雕刻画来增强叙事效果。
近义词: illustrator, embellisher
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun
