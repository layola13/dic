intermediate:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdiət/| adj. n.|中级的；中间的；中级学生；中间物|Being or occurring between two extremes, especially a higher and a lower level or degree.|例句：He's taking an intermediate English course. 他正在上中级英语课。
