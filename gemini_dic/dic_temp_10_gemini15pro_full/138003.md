例句：We found a nookier spot in the library, tucked away behind the bookshelves. 我们在图书馆里找到了一个更隐蔽的地方，藏在书架后面。|近义词：cozier, more secluded, more private, more intimate|反义词：exposed, open, public|词性:形容词
