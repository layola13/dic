ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *ynkestande, equivalent to ink + stand.  The word "ink" ultimately comes from the Latin "encaustum," meaning "burnt in," referring to a purple-red ink used in ancient times.  "Stand" comes from Old English "standan."
