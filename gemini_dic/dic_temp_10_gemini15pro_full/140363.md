oil-less:/ˈɔɪl lɪs/
adjective
无油的；不加油的
Not requiring oil or using little oil.
This oil-less fryer uses air to cook food, making it a healthier option.
这款无油炸锅使用空气来烹饪食物，使其成为更健康的选择。
The oil-less compressor is quieter and requires less maintenance.
无油压缩机更安静，需要的维护也更少。
near synonyms: non-oiled, oil-free
opposite: oiled, lubricated
词性: 形容词
