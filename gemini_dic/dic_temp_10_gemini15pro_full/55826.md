dividedly:/dɪˈvaɪdɪdli/
adv.
分裂地，不一致地
in a divided or disunited manner; with disagreement or dissent.
The committee voted dividedly on the proposal. 委员会对这项提议的投票结果存在分歧。
近义词:disunitedly, discordantly, contentiously
反义词:unanimously, harmoniously, concordantly
词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From divide + -ed + -ly.  "Divide" comes from the Latin "dividere," meaning "to force apart, distribute, separate."
