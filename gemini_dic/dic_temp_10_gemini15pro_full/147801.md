overproved:/ˌoʊvərˈpruːvd/| adj.|过度发酵的|Having risen or expanded excessively, especially of dough.|The bread was dense and heavy because it was overproved.|近义词:overrisen, overfermented|反义词:underproved, underfermented|词性:形容词
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "proved" (past participle of "prove," in the sense of testing or allowing dough to rise).  Ultimately from Latin "probare" (to test).
