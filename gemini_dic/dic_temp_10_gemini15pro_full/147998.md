overrighteous:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪtʃəs/| adj.|自以为是的，假正经的，伪善的|Excessively righteous; making an ostentatious display of righteousness; holier-than-thou.|例句：He gave me an overrighteous lecture about the importance of honesty, while he himself was cheating on his taxes. 他给我讲了一大堆关于诚实重要性的大道理，而他自己却在偷税漏税。|近义词：sanctimonious, self-righteous, hypocritical, pietistic|反义词：humble, modest, unpretentious|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From over- + righteous.  "Over-" acts as an intensifier, implying an excess or exaggeration of righteousness. "Righteous" comes from Middle English *rightwys*, from Old English *rihtwīs*, from *riht* "right" + *wīs* "wise, way."
