demonetize:/diːˈmʌnɪtaɪz/| v. | 剥夺货币资格，使货币失效；从…上撤下广告| To deprive of monetary value, especially to withdraw from circulation as legal tender;  to remove advertisements from (something, typically a website or social media platform).|
The government decided to demonetize the old 500 rupee notes. 政府决定废除旧的 500 卢比纸币。

The social media platform demonetized the controversial video. 社交媒体平台从有争议的视频中撤下了广告。

|近义词：invalidate, withdraw,  devalue|反义词:monetize, legalize|词性:verb|

demonetization:/diːˌmʌnɪtaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. | 货币失效；撤下广告| The act of demonetizing something. |The demonetization of the old currency caused chaos in the market. 旧货币的失效导致市场混乱。

The demonetization of his channel resulted in a significant loss of income.  他的频道被撤下广告后，收入大幅减少。


|近义词：invalidation, withdrawal|反义词:monetization|词性:noun|
