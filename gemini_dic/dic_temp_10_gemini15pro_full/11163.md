archetypical:/ˌɑːrkɪˈtɪpɪkl/ | adj. | 原型的；典型的 |  Of or relating to an archetype; constituting or serving as a prototype.  |  The archetypical hero's journey involves facing trials and tribulations.  典型的英雄之旅包括面对考验和磨难。|  prototypical, exemplary, classic | atypical, unusual | adjective

archetype:/ˈɑːrkɪtaɪp/ | n. | 原型；典型 |  An original model or pattern from which copies are made; a prototype. |  The character of the wise old man is an archetype found in many cultures.  智者的角色是许多文化中都能找到的原型。|  prototype, model, exemplar |  antithesis, deviation | noun
