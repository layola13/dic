deforest:/ˌdiːˈfɔːrɪst/ | v. | 砍伐森林，清除树林 | To clear (an area) of forests or trees. |  They are deforesting the Amazon at an alarming rate. 他们正在以惊人的速度砍伐亚马逊森林。

The company was fined for illegally deforesting protected land.  这家公司因非法砍伐受保护的林地而被罚款。 | 近义词: clear-cut, log, denude | 反义词: reforest, afforest | 词性: 动词
