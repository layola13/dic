costly:/ˈkɒstli/ (UK) /ˈkɔːstli/ (US)| adj.|昂贵的，代价高的|Involving or requiring a large amount of money; expensive.  A costly mistake can ruin a company. |例句：The war was a costly mistake. 这场战争是一个代价高昂的错误。

例句：Renovating the old house proved to be a costly undertaking. 翻新老房子证明是一项耗资巨大的工程。|近义词：expensive, dear, exorbitant, pricey, lavish |反义词:cheap, inexpensive, economical, affordable|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *costly*, from *cost* + *-ly*.  Ultimately from Latin *constāre* (“to stand firm, cost”).
