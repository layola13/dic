anglicizing:/ˈæŋɡlɪsaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使英语化；英语化
The process of making something more English in character or form.
Many immigrants anglicized their names to fit in better. 许多移民将他们的名字英语化以便更好地融入。
anglicising (British English)
n/a
v
