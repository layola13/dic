bioethical:/ˌbaɪoʊˈɛθɪkəl/
adjective
生物伦理的
Relating to the moral principles and problems that arise from advances in biology and medicine.
Bioethical concerns surrounding genetic engineering are complex and constantly evolving.  生物伦理学围绕基因工程的担忧是复杂且不断发展的。
The committee discussed the bioethical implications of the new research. 委员会讨论了这项新研究的生物伦理学意义。
近义词:moral, ethical
反义词:unethical, immoral
词性:adjective

Etymology: bio- (life) + ethical (relating to morals)
词源: bio- (生命) + ethical (与道德相关的)
