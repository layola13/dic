metropolises: /məˈtrɒpəlɪsiːz/ (UK) /məˈtrɑːpəlɪsiːz/ (US)| n. |大都市；大城市|A metropolis is a very large city, often the most important city in a large area or country. |例句：Many young people flock to metropolises seeking better job opportunities. 许多年轻人涌向大都市寻找更好的工作机会。

例句：London and New York are two of the world's most famous metropolises.  伦敦和纽约是世界上最著名的两个大都市。

近义词：cities, urban areas, megacities|反义词:rural areas, countryside, villages|词性:noun
