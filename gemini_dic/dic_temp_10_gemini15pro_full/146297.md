overfavored: /ˌoʊvərˈfeɪvərd/
adjective
过分受宠的，偏爱的
Having received too much favor or preferential treatment.
The overfavored child grew up to be spoiled and entitled.  这个过分受宠的孩子长大后被宠坏了，认为自己有特权。
The company was accused of overfavoring certain employees.  该公司被指控偏袒某些员工。
近义词: privileged, preferred, favored, indulged
反义词: neglected, overlooked, disadvantaged, deprived
词性: adjective

词源:
"Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "favored" (past participle of "favor," from Old French "favor" and Latin "favere" - to be favorable to).
