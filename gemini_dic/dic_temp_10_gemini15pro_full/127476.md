necessitating:/nəˈsesɪteɪtɪŋ/
verb
使成为必需, 需要
making something necessary
The increase in population necessitates building more houses. 人口增长使得建造更多房屋成为必需。
synonyms: requiring, demanding, calling for
antonyms: obviating, precluding
