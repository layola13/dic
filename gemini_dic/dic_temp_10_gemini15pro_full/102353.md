iyo:/ˈiːjoʊ/ |interj.| 嘿；哟| An informal greeting, similar to "hey" or "yo." Used primarily in online communication, especially among younger generations. | Example: Iyo, what's up? 嘿，怎么了？

Example: Iyo, wanna play games later? 哟，待会儿想玩游戏吗？ | Near synonyms: hey, yo, hi | Antonyms: (Interjections don't typically have antonyms) |interjection

需要注意的是，"iyo" 并非标准英语词典中的词汇，它更多地出现在网络用语和非正式场合中。它的词源难以追溯，很可能源于对 "hey" 或 "yo" 的变形。
