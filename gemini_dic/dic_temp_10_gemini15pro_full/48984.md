deghosted:/diːˈɡoʊstɪd/
adjective
去除鬼影的，消除重影的
Removed or eliminated ghosting artifacts (visual distortions) from an image or display.
The new monitor boasts deghosted images, providing a much clearer picture. 这款新显示器拥有去鬼影图像，呈现更清晰的画面。
The software effectively deghosts the video, making it more watchable.  这个软件有效地去除了视频的重影，使其更具可观赏性。
近义词:  cleaned, clarified, enhanced
反义词:  ghosted
词性: adjective
