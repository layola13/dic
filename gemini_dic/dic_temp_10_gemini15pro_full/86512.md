heirship:/ˈɛərʃɪp/
n.
继承权；继承资格
The state or condition of being an heir; the right of inheriting property.
He challenged his brother's heirship to the family estate.  他质疑他兄弟对家族财产的继承权。
inheritance, succession
disinheritance, exclusion

词性: noun
