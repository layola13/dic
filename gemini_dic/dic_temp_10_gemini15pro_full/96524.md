noun
印多根，印度尼西亚和基因的合成词
A portmanteau of Indonesia and gene, referring to things related to Indonesian genetics or genetic resources.
Indonesia has a rich biodiversity, making it a treasure trove of indogens for research and development. 印度尼西亚拥有丰富的生物多样性，使其成为研究和开发印多根的宝库。
Indogen research is crucial for understanding the genetic makeup of Indonesia's diverse population. 印多根研究对于了解印度尼西亚不同人群的基因构成至关重要。
