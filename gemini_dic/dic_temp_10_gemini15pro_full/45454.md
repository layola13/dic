curateships:/ˈkjʊərɪtʃɪps/
n.
管理(职位)；策展(工作)
The position of a curator; the work or period of time spent as a curator.
The museum is seeking applications for two new curateships.  博物馆正在招聘两个新的策展职位。
Several curateships are available at the gallery.  画廊有几个策展职位空缺。
近义词: curatorship, curation
反义词:  (No direct antonym)
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "curate" + "-ship."  "Curate" comes from Late Latin "curatus," past participle of "curare" (to care for), from Latin "cura" (care, concern).  "-ship" is an Old English suffix denoting state, condition, office, or skill.
