benched:/bɛntʃt/| adj.|被坐在板凳上的；被撤换下场的；被搁置的|Removed from a game or other activity and relegated to the sidelines; set aside; not being used.|例句：The coach benched him for the rest of the game. 教练让他在比赛的剩余时间里坐在板凳上。|近义词：side-lined, replaced, inactive, shelved|反义词：active, playing, starting, in use|词性:形容词

benched:/bɛntʃt/| v.|使坐在板凳上；撤换下场；搁置|To remove from a game or other activity and relegate to the sidelines; to set aside; to stop using.|例句：He was benched after his poor performance.  由于表现不佳，他被撤换下场。|近义词：side-line, replace, shelve, deactivate|反义词：activate, start, play, use|词性:动词
