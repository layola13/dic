continuator:/kənˈtɪnjuˌeɪtər/| n.|继续者，延续者|Someone or something that continues a process or activity. |The king appointed a continuator to finish the historical chronicles after the original author's death. 国王任命了一位继续者，在原作者去世后完成历史编年史。|近义词：successor, follower, inheritor|反义词:terminator, ender|noun

continuator:/kənˈtɪnjuˌeɪtər/| n.|延续者，继续者|Someone or something that continues a process or activity. |The software acts as a continuator, automatically backing up files and ensuring data integrity.  这款软件充当一个延续者，自动备份文件并确保数据的完整性。|近义词：prolonger, maintainer|反义词:interrupter, disruptor|noun
