macropterous:/ˌmækrɒpˈtɛrəs/| adj.|大翅的，长翅的|Having large or long wings, especially unusually large wings in relation to body size.|例句：The macropterous form of the insect is capable of long-distance flight.  这种昆虫的长翅型能够进行长途飞行。

例句：Macropterous birds are often better fliers. 长翅鸟通常是更好的飞行者。|近义词：long-winged, fully-winged|反义词：brachypterous, micropterous|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek μακρός (makrós, “long”) + πτερόν (pterón, “wing, feather”).
