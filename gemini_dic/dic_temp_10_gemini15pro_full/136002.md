nonrationalist:/ˌnɒnˈræʃənəlɪst/ | adj. n. | 非理性主义的；非理性主义者 | Not based on or guided by reason; appealing to emotions or beliefs rather than logic.  |  例句：His nonrationalist approach to decision-making often led to unpredictable outcomes. 他非理性主义的决策方式常常导致不可预测的结果。

例句：The artist's nonrationalist paintings evoked a sense of mystery and wonder.  这位艺术家的非理性主义绘画唤起了一种神秘和惊奇的感觉。 | 近义词：irrational, emotional, instinctive | 反义词：rational, logical, reasonable | 词性: 形容词，名词

Etymology:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + rationalist (from Latin rationalis, meaning "reasonable")
