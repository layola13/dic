dreggy:/ˈdrɛɡi/| adj.| 渣滓的，沉淀的，污浊的|Containing dregs or sediment; not clear or pure. Appearing or feeling dull and apathetic.|The dreggy bottom of the coffee cup was unappetizing. 咖啡杯底的渣滓令人倒胃口。|近义词：sedimentary, turbid, murky, cloudy, feculent, impure, stale, sluggish|反义词: clear, pure, pristine|词性:形容词

dreggy:/ˈdrɛɡi/| adj.| 无精打采的，沉闷的|Lacking energy or enthusiasm; dull and listless.|He felt dreggy and unmotivated after weeks of unemployment.  几周的失业后，他感到无精打采，毫无动力。|近义词：listless, lethargic, apathetic, sluggish, lackluster|反义词: energetic, vibrant, enthusiastic|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From dregs + -y. Dregs comes from the Old Norse word *dreggjar, meaning "sediment, lees."
