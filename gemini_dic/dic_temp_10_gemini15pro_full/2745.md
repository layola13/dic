adulteration: /əˌdʌltəˈreɪʃn/
n.
掺假，伪造，劣货
The act of making something impure or weaker by adding something of poor quality.
The food inspector found adulteration in the olive oil. 食品检查员发现橄榄油中掺假。
近义词:debasement, contamination, falsification
反义词:purification
词性:名词

adulterate: /əˈdʌltəreɪt/
v.
掺假，掺杂
To make something impure or weaker by adding something of poor quality.
Some companies adulterate their products to increase profits. 一些公司掺假他们的产品以增加利润。
近义词:contaminate, debase, doctor
反义词:purify
词性:动词
