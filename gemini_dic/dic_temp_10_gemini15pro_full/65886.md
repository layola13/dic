evolvable:/ɪˈvɒlvəb(ə)l/ /ɪˈvɑːlvəb(ə)l/| adj.|可进化的，可发展的|Capable of evolving or developing; adaptable.|例句：The company's software is evolvable, allowing for easy updates and new features.  这家公司的软件是可发展的，可以轻松更新和添加新功能。

例句：Evolvable systems are designed to adapt to changing environments.  可进化系统被设计成能适应变化的环境。|近义词：adaptable, flexible, modifiable, dynamic|反义词：fixed, rigid, static, inflexible|词性:adjective

evolve:/ɪˈvɒlv/ /ɪˈvɑːlv/| v.|进化，发展|Develop gradually, especially from a simple to a more complex form.|例句：The company has evolved from a small startup to a multinational corporation.  这家公司已经从一家小型初创企业发展成为一家跨国公司。

例句：Birds are believed to have evolved from dinosaurs.  鸟类被认为是从恐龙进化而来的。|近义词：develop, progress, advance, mature|反义词：devolve, regress, degenerate|词性:verb
