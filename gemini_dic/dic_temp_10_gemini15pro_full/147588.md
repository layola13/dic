overpiteous:/ˌō-vərˈpɪt-ē-əs/| adj.|过度怜悯的，过分同情的|Excessively piteous; too full of pity or compassion, often to an excessive or inappropriate degree.  It suggests an exaggerated or insincere display of pity.|He cast an overpiteous glance at the beggar, clearly more for show than genuine concern. 他怜悯地瞥了一眼乞丐，显然是为了做样子而不是真心关心。|Synonyms: oversympathetic, maudlin, overly compassionate|Antonyms: unsympathetic, callous, indifferent|Word type: adjective
