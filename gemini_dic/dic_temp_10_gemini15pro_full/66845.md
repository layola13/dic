expatriatism:/ˌɛksˈpeɪtriətɪzəm/ |n.|侨居国外; 移居国外|The state of living in a foreign country.|She experienced a sense of expatriatism after living in France for ten years.  她在法国生活了十年后，体验到了一种侨居国外的感觉。|Synonyms: emigration, exile, migration|Antonyms: repatriation, return, immigration|noun

expatriate:/ɛksˈpeɪtriət/ |n. adj.|移居国外者; 外籍人士|A person who lives outside their native country.|Many American expatriates live in Paris. 许多美国侨民居住在巴黎。|Synonyms: emigrant, exile, migrant|Antonyms: repatriate, native, resident|noun/adjective
