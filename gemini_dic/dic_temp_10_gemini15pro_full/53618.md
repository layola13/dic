discase:/dɪsˈkeɪs/ | vt. |剥夺(衣服、覆盖物);使脱光;使赤裸 |To deprive of clothes or covering; to undress; to strip. | He discased himself of his coat and hat. 他脱掉了外套和帽子。| The storm discased the trees of their leaves.  暴风雨把树上的叶子都吹掉了。 | 近义词: undress, strip, divest, denude | 反义词: dress, clothe, cover | 词性: 动词

Etymology:  From Late Latin *discasare, from Latin dis- "apart, away" + casa "house, hut, cottage."  Originally meant "to take out of one's house," later generalized to "to take off (clothing)."
