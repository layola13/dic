manually:/ˈmænjʊəli/ | adv. | 手动地，人工地 | Done or operated by hand rather than automatically. | I had to enter the data manually because the system was down.  我不得不手动输入数据，因为系统瘫痪了。|

例句：
The gate must be opened manually.  这个大门必须手动打开。

例句：
He manually adjusted the settings on the machine. 他手动调整了机器上的设置。


近义词: by hand, physically, personally
反义词: automatically, electronically
词性: 副词
