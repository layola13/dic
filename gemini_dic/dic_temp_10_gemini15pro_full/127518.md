necrobiosis:/ˌnɛkrəbaɪˈoʊsɪs/ | n. | 坏死 | The physiological death of cells or tissues.  The gradual degeneration and death of cells in a living organism, especially as a normal physiological process. |  Necrobiosis of epidermal cells is a normal process. 表皮细胞的坏死是一个正常的过程。|  degeneration, atrophy, decay | growth, regeneration | noun
