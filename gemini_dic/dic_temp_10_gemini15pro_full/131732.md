nonconfined:/ˌnɒnkənˈfaɪnd/ | adj. | 非承压的，无侧限的，自由的 | Not confined; free from restraint or limits. | The groundwater in this area is nonconfined, meaning it is not under pressure from overlying impermeable layers.  这个地区的地下水是非承压的，意味着它不受上覆不透水层的压力。 |  The birds were nonconfined, able to fly freely within the aviary. 鸟儿们没有被限制，可以在鸟舍里自由飞翔。 | Synonyms: free, unrestricted, unbound, unconstrained | Antonyms: confined, restricted, limited, bound | adjective
