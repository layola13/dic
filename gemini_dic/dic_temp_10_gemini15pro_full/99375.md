interdetermined:/ˌɪntərdiˈtɜːrmɪnd/ | adj. |相互决定的，互相制约的 |  Mutually determined; each element influences and is influenced by the others.  This implies a complex relationship of reciprocal causality where various factors are intertwined and affect each other. | The interdetermined nature of social and economic factors makes it difficult to isolate the cause of poverty. 社会和经济因素相互决定的性质使得很难找出贫困的根源。|  interconnected, interdependent, interrelated | independent, separate | adjective
