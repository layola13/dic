incumbency: /ɪnˈkʌmbənsi/ | n. | 任期；现任；责任；义务 | the holding of an office or the period during which one is held. |  The president's incumbency was marked by economic growth. 总统的任期以经济增长为标志。

The incumbent mayor faces a tough challenge in the upcoming election. 现任市长在即将到来的选举中面临严峻挑战。| 近义词：tenure, office, position | 反义词: vacancy | 词性: 名词

incumbent: /ɪnˈkʌmbənt/ | adj. | 现任的；负有义务的 | currently holding an office; obligatory. | The incumbent president is seeking re-election. 现任总统正在寻求连任。

It is incumbent upon all citizens to obey the law. 所有公民都有义务遵守法律。| 近义词：current, present, obligatory, required | 反义词: former, past, optional | 词性: 形容词
