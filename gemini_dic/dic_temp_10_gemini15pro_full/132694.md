nondissipatedness:/ˌnɒndɪˈsɪpeɪtɪdnəs/ | n. | 非耗散性；未耗散状态 | The state of not being dissipated or scattered.  The property of a system or process where energy is not lost or transformed into unusable forms. | The nondissipatedness of the laser beam allowed it to travel long distances. 激光束的非耗散性使其能够传播很长的距离。|  conservation, preservation, integrity | dissipation, dispersion, scattering | noun
