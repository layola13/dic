enable:/ɪˈneɪbl/| v. |使能够，使成为可能|To give someone the authority or means to do something. |The scholarship enabled her to study abroad.  这笔奖学金使她能够出国留学。

|She was enabled to walk again after months of therapy. 经过几个月的治疗，她又能走路了。|近义词：allow, permit, empower, facilitate|反义词：prevent, hinder, disable, prohibit|词性:动词
