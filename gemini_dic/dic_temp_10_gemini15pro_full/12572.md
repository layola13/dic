aspiringness:/əˈspaɪərɪŋnɪs/| n. |抱负，渴望，志向|The state of being aspiring; ambition or strong desire to achieve something.|Her aspiringness to become a CEO was evident in her dedication and hard work. 她渴望成为首席执行官的雄心壮志体现在她的奉献精神和辛勤工作中。|近义词：ambition, aspiration, drive, zeal|反义词：apathy, indifference, lack of ambition|词性:名词
Etymology: From "aspiring" + "-ness". "Aspiring" comes from the Middle English "aspyren," from Old French "aspirer," ultimately from Latin "aspirare"  (to breathe on, to strive for).
