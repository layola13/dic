methionine:/məˈθaɪəˌniːn/ |n.|甲硫氨酸;蛋氨酸|An essential amino acid that is important for normal growth and metabolism in humans and animals. It is also used in dietary supplements and as a flavor enhancer in food. | Methionine is important for protein synthesis. 甲硫氨酸对蛋白质合成很重要。|  Methionine deficiency can lead to health problems. 甲硫氨酸缺乏会导致健康问题。 |  cysteine, homocysteine| N/A |noun
