heer:/hɪər/ | n. | 军队; 大批; 众多 | a large group of people or things |  Example: A heer of tourists descended on the small town.  大批游客涌入小镇。| Synonyms: host, multitude, throng, legion | Antonyms: few, handful, scattering | noun


/hɪər/ | adv. | 这里 | in or at this place | Example: Come heer and sit down. 到这里来坐下。 | Synonyms: here | Antonyms: there | adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *heer, *here, from Old English hēr (“here”), from Proto-Germanic *hēz (“here”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱi- (“this”). Cognate with West Frisian hjir, Dutch hier, German hier, Danish her, Swedish här, Icelandic hér.
