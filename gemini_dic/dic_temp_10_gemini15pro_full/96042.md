indecency: /ɪnˈdiːsnsi/ | n. | 不体面；猥亵；下流 | The act or quality of being indecent; behavior or language that is thought to be morally offensive. |  The comedian was fired for his indecency on stage.  这位喜剧演员因为在舞台上的不雅行为被解雇了。|  The book was banned for its indecency. 这本书因其不雅内容而被禁。 | 近义词: impropriety, immodesty, obscenity | 反义词: decency, propriety, modesty | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Late Latin *indecentia, from Latin indecent-, indecent-, from in- 'not' + decent-, decent- 'fitting, proper'.
