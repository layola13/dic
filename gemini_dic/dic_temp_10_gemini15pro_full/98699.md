ETYMOLOGY->
From insuperable + -ness.  Insuperable comes from Late Latin insuperabilis, from in- + superabilis “that can be overcome,” from superare “to overcome,” from super “above, over.”

SYNONYMS->

ANTONYMS->
vulnerability, weakness, conquerability, surmountable nature
