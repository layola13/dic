erm:/ɜːm/ (UK) /ɝːm/ (US)| interjection | 嗯，呃 | A sound made when hesitating or thinking about what to say. | Example: "Erm, I'm not sure what to say." 嗯，我不确定该说什么。 | Example: "He paused, 'Erm... yes, I think so.'" 他停顿了一下，“嗯……是的，我想是的。” | Synonym: uh, um | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a hesitation sound) | interjection

/ɜːm/ (UK) /ɝːm/ (US)| noun | 嗯，呃 (名词形式较少用) | The act of hesitating or making a sound of hesitation. | Example: "His constant 'erms' and 'ahs' were distracting." 他不停地“嗯”和“啊”让人分心。| Example: "There were a lot of 'erms' during his presentation."  他的演讲中有很多“嗯”。 | Synonym: hesitation, pause | Antonym: fluency | noun
