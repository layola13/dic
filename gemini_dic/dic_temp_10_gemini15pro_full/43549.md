crayonist: /ˈkreɪənɪst/
n.
蜡笔画家；蜡笔画艺术家
An artist who uses crayons as their primary medium.
例句：The crayonist displayed her vibrant artwork at the local gallery. 蜡笔画家在当地画廊展示了她充满活力的艺术作品。

例句：My niece is a budding crayonist, her drawings are full of imagination. 我的侄女是一位崭露头角的蜡笔画家，她的画作充满了想象力。

近义词：artist, drawer, sketcher
反义词: 无
词性: 名词
