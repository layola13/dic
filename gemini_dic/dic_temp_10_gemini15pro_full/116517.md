mateynesses:/ˈmeɪtɪnɛsɪz/
n.
僵局，逼和状态 (复数)
A situation in chess where the player whose turn it is to move is not in check but has no legal moves.  This results in a draw.
The game ended in mateynesses after a long and complicated series of moves.  这场比赛经过一系列漫长而复杂的走法后以逼和告终。
