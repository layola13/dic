liabilities:/ˌlaɪəˈbɪlətiz/
|n.|负债，债务|A company's liabilities are its debts or financial obligations.|The company's liabilities exceeded its assets.  这家公司的负债超过了它的资产。|debts, obligations|assets, credits|noun

liability:/ˌlaɪəˈbɪləti/
|n.|责任，义务；不利条件；倾向|Legal liability for the accident rests with the driver. 事故的法律责任在于司机。|responsibility, obligation, burden|asset, advantage|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *liablete (Modern French liability) < liable + -ity.  Liable is from Old French liable meaning "obliged, responsible, subject to," from lier "to bind," from Latin ligare.
