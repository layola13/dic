nonusance: /ˈnɒnjuːsəns/ (British English) /ˈnɑːnjuːsəns/ (American English)| n.| 无害事物；不构成妨害的事物 |Something that is not harmful or offensive and does not cause a problem. | The overgrown bushes in my yard are a nonusance to my neighbors. 我院子里长得过高的灌木丛对我的邻居来说不是什么麻烦事。|  Near synonyms:  inoffensive thing, harmless thing| Antonyms: nuisance, bother, annoyance | Noun
