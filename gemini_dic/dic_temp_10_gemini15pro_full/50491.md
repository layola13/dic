depressurises:/diːˈprɛʃəˌraɪzɪz/
verb
减压；卸压
To release or reduce the pressure in a sealed container or system, such as an aircraft cabin or a pressure cooker.
The pilot depressurises the cabin before landing.  飞行员在着陆前给机舱减压。
The system automatically depressurises in case of a leak. 如果发生泄漏，系统会自动减压。
近义词: decompress, vent
反义词: pressurize, compress
词性: verb
