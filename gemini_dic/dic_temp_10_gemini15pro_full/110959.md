liquefiable:/lɪkwɪˈfaɪəbl/
adjective
可液化的
Capable of being liquefied; easily converted into a liquid state.
The gas is liquefiable under pressure.  这种气体在压力下可以液化。
近义词:meltable, fusible
反义词:non-liquefiable, solid, unmeltable
词性:adjective

liquefy:/ˈlɪkwɪfaɪ/
verb
液化
To make or become liquid.
The substance liquefies at room temperature.  这种物质在室温下会液化。
近义词:melt, dissolve, fuse
反义词:solidify, freeze
词性:verb

liquefaction:/ˌlɪkwɪˈfækʃən/
noun
液化
The process of making or becoming liquid.
The liquefaction of natural gas allows for easier transportation. 天然气的液化使其更容易运输。
近义词:melting, fusion, dissolving
反义词:solidification, freezing
词性:noun
