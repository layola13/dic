mentioned:/ˈmenʃənd/ | v. | 提及，说起 | To refer to something or someone briefly and without going into detail. |  I mentioned your name to him. 我向他提到了你的名字。

| mentioned:/ˈmenʃənd/ | adj. | 被提及的 | Having been referred to; aforesaid. | The aforementioned incident occurred last week.  上述事件发生在上周。


| 近义词: cited, referred to, noted, alluded to | 反义词: omitted, ignored | 词性: verb, adjective
