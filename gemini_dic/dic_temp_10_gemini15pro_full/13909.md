augustness:/ɔːˈɡʌstnəs/
n.
威严，庄严，崇高
The quality of being inspiring reverence or admiration; majestic dignity.
The augustness of the cathedral filled her with awe.  大教堂的庄严让她充满敬畏。
近义词：majesty, grandeur, dignity, nobility
反义词：humility, insignificance, commonness
词性:名词
