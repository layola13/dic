nonregulated:/ˌnɒnˈreɡjʊleɪtɪd/ |adj.|不受监管的，无管制的|Not regulated; not subject to rules or control.|例句：The nonregulated market allows for more innovation.  无管制的市场允许更多创新。|例句：Nonregulated industries often pose higher risks to consumers. 不受监管的行业通常给消费者带来更高的风险。|近义词：unregulated, uncontrolled, unlicensed|反义词：regulated, controlled, licensed|词性:形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not".
regulated (past participle of regulate): from Late Latin regulatus, past participle of regulare "to control by rule, direct," from Latin regula "rule"
