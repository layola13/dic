enlute:/ɪnˈluːt/ | v. | 涂油泥; 用油泥粘合; 用油泥密封 | To cover or seal with lute. |  The chemist enluted the flask to prevent leakage. 化学家用油泥密封烧瓶以防泄漏。

The workers enluted the pipes to prevent corrosion. 工人们用油泥涂抹管道以防止腐蚀。 | 近义词: seal, cement, caulk | 反义词: open, unseal | 词性: 动词

词源: From Late Latin *inlūtāre, from in- + lūtum ‘mud, clay’ (from lutum ‘mud’).
