bustling:/ˈbʌslɪŋ/| adj.|熙熙攘攘的，热闹的|Full of energetic and noisy activity.|The bustling market was filled with shoppers. 熙熙攘攘的市场挤满了购物者。|近义词：busy, lively, crowded, vibrant|反义词：quiet, calm, deserted, still|词性:形容词
词源: From the verb "bustle," which originated in the late 16th century, possibly from a now obsolete Dutch word "busselen" meaning "to hurry," "to flutter," or "to prepare."
