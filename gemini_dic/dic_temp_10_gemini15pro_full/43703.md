creditor:/ˈkrɛdɪtər/| n. |债权人，贷方|A person or company to whom money is owed.|例句：The company's creditors are demanding payment. 该公司的债权人要求付款。|例句：She had to declare bankruptcy because she couldn't pay her creditors. 她不得不宣布破产，因为她无力偿还债权人。|近义词：lender, debtee|反义词: debtor, borrower|词性:noun
Etymology: From Latin "creditor" (one who trusts or believes), from "credere" (to believe, trust, entrust).  The sense shifted from "one who entrusts something to another" to "one to whom money is owed".
