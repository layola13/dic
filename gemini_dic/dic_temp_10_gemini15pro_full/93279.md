allicin:/ˈælɪsɪn/| n. |大蒜素|An organosulfur compound obtained from garlic.|Garlic is a good source of allicin, which is believed to have health benefits. 大蒜富含大蒜素，它被认为对健康有益。|Synonyms: diallyl thiosulfinate (chemical name)|Antonyms: None|Noun
