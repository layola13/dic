deuterogamist: /ˌdjuːtərˈɒɡəmɪst/ | n. | 再婚者；已二婚的人 | A person who remarries after the death or divorce of their first spouse.  A person who enters into a second marriage. |  Example: After the death of his wife, he became a deuterogamist.  在他妻子去世后，他成了一个再婚者。

Example:  The support group was specifically for deuterogamists who were struggling with blended families. 这个支持小组专门为正在努力适应混合家庭的再婚人士而设。| 近义词: remarried person | 反义词: monogamist | 词性: noun
