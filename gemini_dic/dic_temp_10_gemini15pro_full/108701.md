lean:/liːn/
verb
倾斜；倚靠；倾向；依赖
To incline or bend from a vertical position.
He leaned against the wall, exhausted.  他靠在墙上，筋疲力尽。
The tower leans slightly to the north.  塔身略微向北倾斜。
incline, bend, tilt
stand, straighten

lean:/liːn/
adjective
瘦的；无脂肪的；精干的；简洁的
(of a person or animal) thin and fit; not fat.
Containing little or no fat.
A lean, muscular physique.  精瘦的肌肉体格。
Lean ground beef.  瘦牛肉末。
thin, slim, slender
fat, obese

lean:/liːn/
noun
瘦肉
The part of meat that has little or no fat.
There's not much lean on this chop.  这块排骨上没多少瘦肉。
meat (without fat)
fat

Etymology:
From Old English *hlǣne (attested only in unhlǣne ‘not lean, fat’), of Germanic origin; related to Dutch leen and German lehn, also to loan.
