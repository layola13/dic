fester:/ˈfestər/
verb
溃烂; 恶化
to become worse or more intense, especially because of lack of attention
The wound festered for days.  伤口溃烂了好几天。

近义词:rankle, smolder, worsen, aggravate
反义词:heal, improve, ameliorate
词性:v.


fester:/ˈfestər/
noun
脓疮; 溃疡
a sore that has become infected
The festering wound needed immediate medical attention.  溃烂的伤口需要立即就医。

近义词:sore, ulcer, abscess
反义词:healthy tissue
词性:n.
