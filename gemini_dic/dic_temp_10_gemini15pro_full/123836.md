morales:/məˈrɑːlez/| n.|士气，道德，伦理|principles of right and wrong behavior; the degree to which something is right or wrong.  It can also refer to the morale of a group, meaning their confidence, enthusiasm, and discipline.|High morale among the troops is essential for victory. 部队的士气高昂对于胜利至关重要.|synonyms: ethics, principles, standards, values (for moral principles); spirit, confidence, enthusiasm (for morale)|antonyms: immorality, corruption, wickedness (for moral principles); despair, discouragement, apathy (for morale)|noun
