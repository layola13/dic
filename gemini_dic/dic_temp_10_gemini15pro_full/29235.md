cassate:/kəˈsɑːt/ /kæˈseɪt/
noun
卡萨塔蛋糕（一种意大利传统甜点）
A rich Italian ice cream dessert consisting of layers of ice cream, candied fruit, and nuts, often encased in a shell of marzipan.
The cassate was beautifully decorated with candied cherries and pistachios. 卡萨塔蛋糕用蜜饯樱桃和开心果精美地装饰着。
She served a delicious cassate for dessert. 她用美味的卡萨塔蛋糕作为甜点。
synonyms: iced dessert, frozen dessert, ice cream cake
antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "savory dish")
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *cassata*, from Arabic *qaššāṭah*, meaning “bowl”.  The name originally referred to the bowl in which the dessert was molded.
