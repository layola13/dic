ensnaringly:/ɪnˈsneərɪŋli/
adverb
诱人地；迷人地；圈套似地
in a way that catches someone as if in a trap
Her beauty ensnaringly drew him in. 她的美貌诱人地吸引着他。
Synonyms: captivatingly, alluringly, temptingly, seductively
Antonyms: repellently, off-puttingly, unattractively
词性: 副词


Etymology:
From ensnare + -ingly.  Ensnare comes from en- (meaning "in" or "into") + snare (a trap for catching small animals).
