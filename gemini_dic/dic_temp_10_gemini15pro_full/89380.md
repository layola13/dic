homophones:/ˈhɒməfəʊnz/ /ˈhoʊməfoʊnz/ | n. |同音词|Words that sound the same but have different meanings and spellings. |例句： "To," "too," and "two" are homophones. “to”，“too”和“two”是同音词。|近义词：homonyms |反义词:heteronyms| noun
