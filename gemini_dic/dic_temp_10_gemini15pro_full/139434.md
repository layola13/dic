obsidian:/əbˈsɪdiən/| n.|黑曜石|A hard, dark, glassy volcanic rock formed by the rapid solidification of lava without crystallization.|The ancient Aztecs used obsidian to make knives and tools. 古代阿兹特克人用黑曜石制作刀具和工具。|Synonyms: volcanic glass|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

obsidian:/əbˈsɪdiən/| adj. |黑曜石般的; 漆黑的|Resembling obsidian in color or appearance; very dark or black.|Her obsidian eyes stared back at him. 她黑曜石般的眼睛回瞪着他。|Synonyms: jet-black, inky, ebony|Antonyms: light, bright|Adjective
