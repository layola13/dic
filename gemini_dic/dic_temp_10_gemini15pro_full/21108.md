bliss:/blɪs/
n.
极乐；天赐之福
A state of perfect happiness, great joy.
She closed her eyes and sighed with bliss. 她闭上眼睛，幸福地叹了口气。

近义词:ecstasy, euphoria, rapture, joy, happiness
反义词:misery, unhappiness, sorrow
