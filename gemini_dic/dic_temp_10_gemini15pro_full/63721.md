ephippium:/ɛˈfɪpɪəm/| n. |鞍状物，介壳，（水蚤的）卵鞍|A thickened, chitinized, saddle-like structure on the dorsal side of a cladoceran (water flea), in which the winter eggs are carried.|The ephippium protects the water flea's eggs during harsh conditions.|Synonym: saddle, case, protective covering|Antonym: (No direct antonym, possibly vulnerability) |noun
