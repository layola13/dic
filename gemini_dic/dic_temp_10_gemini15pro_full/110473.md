lilly:/ˈlɪli/| n.|百合花|A flowering plant with large, prominent flowers, typically trumpet-shaped and fragrant, often white or brightly colored.  It symbolizes purity, innocence, and majesty in many cultures.|The lilies in the garden are in full bloom. 花园里的百合花盛开了。|近义词：flower, bloom|反义词: (No direct antonym for a flower. Perhaps "weed" in a metaphorical sense, representing the opposite of beauty and desirability) |词性:noun
