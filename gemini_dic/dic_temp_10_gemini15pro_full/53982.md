discretional:/dɪˈskreʃənəl/
adj.
自由决定的，酌情处理的
Left to individual judgment or choice.
例句：Judges have considerable discretional powers. 法官拥有相当大的酌情决定权。

例句：The use of the fund is entirely discretional. 这笔资金的使用完全是酌情决定的。

近义词：optional, elective, voluntary, discretionary
反义词：obligatory, mandatory, compulsory
词性：adjective

词源：From Late Latin *discretionalis*, from Latin discretio (“separation, distinction, discernment”), from discernere (“to separate, distinguish, discern”).
