martyrdom:/ˈmɑːrtərdəm/ | n. |殉道；殉教；牺牲|The state of being a martyr, especially the suffering of death on account of adherence to a cause, especially one's religious beliefs. | Joan of Arc's martyrdom inspired many.  圣女贞德的殉道激励了很多人。| synonyms: sacrifice, immolation, victimization | antonyms: survival, deliverance | noun

martyrship:/ˈmɑːrtərʃɪp/ | n. |殉道；殉教；为…而牺牲；殉难 | The suffering or death of a martyr. |  He endured martyrdom for his faith. 他因信仰而殉道。| synonyms: martyrdom, sacrifice, immolation, victimization | antonyms: survival, deliverance | noun
