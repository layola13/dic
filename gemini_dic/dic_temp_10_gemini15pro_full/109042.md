legal-sized:/ˈliːɡəl saɪzd/
adjective
法定尺寸的
Of the size prescribed by law for certain documents.
The lawyer requested legal-sized paper for the contract. 律师要求用法律规定的尺寸的纸张来写合同。

non-legal-sized, illegal-sized
