acing: /ˈeɪsɪŋ/
adjective
(非正式)极好的，非常棒的
(informal) very good or excellent
例句：She's acing all her exams. 她所有考试都得了高分。
例句：The band is acing their comeback tour.  这支乐队在他们的复出演唱会上表现出色。
近义词：excellent, outstanding, superb, brilliant, terrific
反义词：terrible, awful, dreadful, poor
词性：形容词

acing: /ˈeɪsɪŋ/
verb
(非正式)在…中取得好成绩；完胜
(informal) to do very well in something; to excel at something
例句：He aced the test. 他在考试中取得了优异的成绩。
例句：She's acing her new job. 她新工作做得非常出色。
近义词：mastering, conquering, excelling in, triumphing in
反义词：failing, flunking, struggling with
词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the noun "ace," meaning "a person who excels at something," ultimately derived from the Old French word "as" (meaning "one"), which referred to the single pip on a die or playing card.  The meaning evolved to signify someone who is "number one" or highly skilled.
