celtic: /ˈkɛltɪk/ (US), /ˈsɛltɪk/ (UK)| adj. n.|凯尔特的；凯尔特人|Relating to the Celts, their languages, or their culture.  A member of any of the peoples inhabiting ancient Gaul and the British Isles.|例句：Celtic music is known for its haunting melodies. 凯尔特音乐以其萦绕的旋律而闻名。

例句：The Celtic tribes once inhabited much of Europe. 凯尔特部落曾经居住在欧洲的大部分地区。|近义词：Gaelic, Irish, Scottish|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-Celtic")|词性: adjective, noun
