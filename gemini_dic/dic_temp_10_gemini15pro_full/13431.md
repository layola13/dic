atoners:/əˈtoʊnərz/
noun
赎罪者; 补偿者
People who make amends for wrongdoings.
The atoners worked tirelessly to rebuild the community after the disaster. 灾难过后，赎罪者们不知疲倦地重建社区。
The film explores the complex psychology of atoners seeking redemption. 这部电影探讨了寻求救赎的赎罪者们的复杂心理。
synonyms: expiators, reconcilers, redressers
antonyms: offenders, culprits, transgressors
词性: 名词
