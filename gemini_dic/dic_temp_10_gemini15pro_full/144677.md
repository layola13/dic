outyells:/ˌaʊtˈjɛl/
verb
大声喊叫，盖过…的喊叫声
to shout louder than someone else
The coach outyelled the referee, arguing the call. 教练大声喊叫，盖过了裁判的声音，争论着判罚。
outcry, outshout, drown out
