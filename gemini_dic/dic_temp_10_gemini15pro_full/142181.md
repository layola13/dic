ornamentally:/ˌɔːrnəˈmentəli/
adverb
装饰地；观赏性地
in a way that adds decoration or beauty; decoratively
The cake was ornamentally iced with intricate patterns.  这个蛋糕装饰性地覆上了复杂的图案。
The room was ornamentally arranged with flowers and candles.  房间用鲜花和蜡烛装饰性地布置。
近义词:decoratively, elaborately, fancifully
反义词:plainly, simply, austerely
词性:adverb
