euclase:/ˈjuːkleɪz/ | n. |  绿柱石 | A rare beryllium aluminium silicate mineral, BeAlSiO4(OH), occurring as transparent blue, green, or colorless prismatic crystals. It is used as a gemstone. | Euclase is a prized gemstone due to its rarity and vibrant colors. 绿柱石因其稀有性和鲜艳的色彩而成为珍贵的宝石。|  Synonyms: None (as it's a specific mineral) | Antonyms: None | noun
