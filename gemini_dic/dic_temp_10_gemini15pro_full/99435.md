interequinoctial:/ˌɪntərˌiːkwɪˈnɒkʃəl/ /ˌɪntərˌiːkwɪˈnɑːkʃəl/
adjective
昼夜平分的；分点之间的；赤道的
Occurring or situated between equinoxes.  Relating to the regions or climate of the equator.
The interequinoctial rains fell heavily. 分点之间的雨下得很大。
