influenzalike:/ˌɪnfluˈɛnzəˌlaɪk/ | adj. |类似流感的|Resembling influenza; having symptoms similar to those of influenza. |  She had influenzalike symptoms, including fever, chills, and muscle aches. 她有类似流感的症状，包括发烧、发冷和肌肉酸痛。| Example: He was feeling influenzalike, so he stayed home from work. 他感觉像是得了流感，所以待在家里没去上班。 |Synonyms: flulike, grippelike | Antonyms: healthy, well | Adjective


Etymology->
From *influenza* + *-like*. *Influenza* comes from Italian *influenza*, meaning "influence," originally referring to the supposed astrological influence of the stars on health. *-like* is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling," "characteristic of," or "similar to."
