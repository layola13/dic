overcuriousness:/ˌoʊvərˈkjʊəriəsnəs/
|n.| 过分好奇，过度好奇 |An excessive eagerness to know about the affairs of others; inquisitiveness.| Her overcuriousness led her to snoop through her neighbor's mail. 她的过度好奇让她偷看了邻居的邮件。| 近义词: inquisitiveness, nosiness, prying | 反义词: indifference, apathy | 词性: noun

overcurious:/ˌoʊvərˈkjʊəriəs/
|adj.| 过分好奇的，过度好奇的 | Excessively eager to know about the affairs of others; inquisitive. | He was an overcurious child, always getting into things he shouldn't. 他是一个过度好奇的孩子，总是去碰他不该碰的东西。| 近义词: inquisitive, nosy, prying | 反义词: indifferent, apathetic | 词性: adjective
