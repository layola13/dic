microdiorite:/ˈmaɪkroʊˈdaɪəˌraɪt/| n. |微闪长岩|A diorite with a fine-grained texture, often containing microscopic crystals.|The geologist identified the sample as a microdiorite due to its fine texture and mineral composition. 地质学家根据其细密的质地和矿物成分将样品鉴定为微闪长岩。|Synonyms: fine-grained diorite|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but could be contrasted with coarse-grained diorite)|noun
