dermaptera:/dɜːrˈmæptərə/
n.
蠼目昆虫;革翅目
An order of insects, also known as earwigs, characterized by their cerci, a pair of forceps-like appendages at the end of their abdomen.
Earwigs, belonging to the order Dermaptera, are nocturnal insects found in damp environments. 蠼目昆虫，也称为蠷螋，是夜行性昆虫，常见于潮湿的环境中。
Dermaptera are known for their distinctive cerci. 蠼目昆虫以其独特的尾须而闻名。
近义词:earwigs
反义词:None
词性:noun
