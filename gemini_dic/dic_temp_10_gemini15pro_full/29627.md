catenary:/ˈkætənɛri/ /ˈkætɪnɛri/| n. adj.|悬链线，悬垂线；悬链线的|A curve formed by a flexible cable or chain hanging freely from two fixed points.  It's a specific mathematical curve.|The cable of the suspension bridge hung in a perfect catenary.  这座吊桥的缆索形成了完美的悬链线。|synonyms:  curve, arc, droop, sag|antonyms: straight, linear|noun

catenary:/ˈkætənɛri/ /ˈkætɪnɛri/| adj.|悬链线的，悬垂的|Resembling or relating to a catenary curve.|The catenary arch of the gateway was impressive.  大门的悬链拱令人印象深刻。|synonyms:  curved, drooping, sagging|antonyms: straight, linear|adjective
