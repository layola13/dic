facilely:/ˈfæsɪlli/ /ˈfæsɪlːi/| adv.|容易地，流畅地|Done or achieved with little effort or difficulty; superficially.  Characterized by ease and fluency, often suggesting a lack of depth or thoroughness.|He facilely dismissed her concerns. 他轻易地打消了她的顾虑。|Synonyms: easily, smoothly, readily, effortlessly, glibly|Antonyms: laboriously, painstakingly, arduously, difficultly|词性:副词
