bedjackets:/ˈbedˌdʒækɪts/
noun
床罩，短上衣
A short, lightweight jacket worn over nightclothes or pajamas, typically by women.
She wore a delicate lace bedjacket over her nightgown. 她穿着睡衣，外面套着一件精致的蕾丝短上衣。
Synonyms: bed jacket, dressing jacket, housecoat, wrapper
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but consider) overcoat, winter coat
词性: 名词
