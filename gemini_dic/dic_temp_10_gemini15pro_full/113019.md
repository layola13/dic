lumbricoid:/ˌlʌmbrɪˈkɔɪd/ | adj. n. | 蛔虫样的；蛔虫 |Resembling or pertaining to a roundworm, especially those of the genus *Ascaris*; a roundworm, especially *Ascaris lumbricoides*.| The lumbricoid worms infested the intestines of the puppy. 这只小狗的肠道里充满了蛔虫。 | The veterinarian identified the parasite as a lumbricoid. 兽医确认这种寄生虫是蛔虫。|Synonym: ascarid, nematode, roundworm | Antonym: None | Adjective, Noun
