例句：She prefers to wear joggers when she's relaxing at home.  她在家放松时更喜欢穿慢跑裤。|近义词：runner, jogger (for pants: sweatpants, track pants)|反义词: sedentary person (for the person), dress pants (for the clothing) |词性: Noun
