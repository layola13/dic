dropsicalness:/ˌdrɒˈpsɪkəlnəs/
noun
水肿; 浮肿;
The state of being dropsical; excessive accumulation of fluid in the body tissues.
His dropsicalness was a clear sign of his failing health. 他的水肿是他健康状况恶化的明显迹象。
近义词:edema, swelling, bloating
反义词: dehydration
