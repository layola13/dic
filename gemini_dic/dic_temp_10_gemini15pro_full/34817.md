clincher:/ˈklɪntʃər/| n.|定论；敲定的事物；决定性因素|A fact, argument, or event that settles a matter conclusively.|Her testimony provided the clincher that convinced the jury of his guilt. 她的证词提供了确凿的证据，使陪审团相信他有罪。|synonyms: deciding factor, conclusive argument, key point,  proof, confirmation|antonyms: disproof, counterargument, rebuttal|noun

clincher:/ˈklɪntʃər/| v.|敲定；决定；取得决定性胜利|To settle (a matter) conclusively.|The new evidence clinched the case for the prosecution.  新的证据确凿地证明了控方的论点。|synonyms: settle, decide, conclude, confirm, seal|antonyms:  unsettle, open up, question|verb
