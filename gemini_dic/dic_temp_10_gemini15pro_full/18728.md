berger:/ˈbɜːrɡər/ (US), /ˈbɜːɡə/ (UK)| n.|汉堡包；牛肉饼|A patty of ground beef, usually served in a bun.  It can also sometimes refer to the entire sandwich.|例句：I ordered a cheeseburger with fries. 我点了一个芝士汉堡和薯条。

例句：He made homemade burgers for the barbecue. 他为烧烤做了自制汉堡。

近义词：hamburger, patty|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps salad or a healthy alternative)|词性:noun
