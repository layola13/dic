bedcovers: /ˈbedˌkʌvərz/
n.
床罩；盖床的覆盖物
a general term for coverings that are used on a bed, including blankets, quilts, duvets, comforters, and bedspreads.
She carefully smoothed the bedcovers before leaving the room. 她离开房间前仔细地抚平了床罩。
The bedcovers were embroidered with intricate floral patterns. 床罩上绣着复杂的图案。
synonyms: blankets, quilts, duvets, comforters, bedspreads
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but one could consider "uncovered" as a contrasting state)
noun
