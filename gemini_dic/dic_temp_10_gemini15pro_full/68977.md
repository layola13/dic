fatigable:/ˈfætɪɡəbl/| adj.|易疲劳的，易疲倦的|Easily fatigued; susceptible to tiredness.  Capable of being fatigued.|The hiker found the steep climb fatigable.  这位徒步旅行者发现陡峭的攀登很容易疲劳。|Synonyms: tireable, exhaustible, wearying|Antonyms: tireless, indefatigable, inexhaustible|adjective
