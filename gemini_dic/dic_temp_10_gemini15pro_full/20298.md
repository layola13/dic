bisectional:/baɪˈsɛkʃənəl/
adjective
二等分的；二分的
Dividing something into two equal sections.
例句：The bisectional line divides the angle into two equal parts.  二等分线将角分成相等的两部分。

例句：The surgeon made a bisectional incision across the patient's abdomen.  外科医生在病人的腹部做了一个二等分的切口。

近义词：bisecting, dividing
反义词：joining, uniting
词性：adjective


bisection:/baɪˈsɛkʃən/
noun
二等分；平分
The act of dividing something into two equal parts.
例句：The bisection of the line segment is a fundamental concept in geometry.  线段的二等分是几何中的一个基本概念。

例句：The bisection of the angle created two congruent angles.  角的二等分创造了两个全等角。

近义词：division, halving
反义词：joining, combination
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *bisection-, from bisect- (past participle stem of bisecare 'to cut in two'), from bi- 'two' + secare 'to cut'.
