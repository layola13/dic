impingement:/ɪmˈpɪndʒmənt/| n.|碰撞，冲击，侵犯|The impingement of the cold air on their faces made them shiver.  冷空气冲击他们的脸，使他们颤抖。|例句：The light impingement of the branches against the window was barely audible.  树枝轻微地撞击窗户，几乎听不见。

例句：The impingement of the new building on their view caused a dispute among the neighbors.  新建筑对他们视野的侵犯引起了邻居之间的争执。|近义词：impact, encroachment, infringement, intrusion|反义词:noninterference, respect|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English *empingen*, from Old French *empeindre* "to infringe on, encroach upon," derived from Latin *impingere* "to push against, strike against, drive into," from *in-* "in, on" + *pangere* "to fix, fasten, drive in."
