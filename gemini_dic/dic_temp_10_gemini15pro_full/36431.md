colander:/ˈkʌləndər/ /ˈkɑːləndər/| n.|漏勺，滤器|A bowl-shaped utensil with holes in it used for draining food, such as pasta or vegetables.|I rinsed the pasta in a colander. 我用漏勺冲洗了意大利面。|She drained the potatoes in a colander. 她用漏勺沥干了土豆。|近义词：strainer, sieve|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "bowl" in the sense of something that retains, not drains)|词性: 名词
