monocotyledon:/ˌmɒnəʊˌkɒtɪˈliːdən/ | n. | 单子叶植物 | A flowering plant with an embryo that bears a single cotyledon (seed leaf). Monocotyledons typically have leaves with parallel veins, flower parts in multiples of three, and fibrous root systems. |  Example 1: Corn is a common monocotyledon. 玉米是一种常见的单子叶植物。

Example 2:  The orchid is a beautiful example of a monocotyledon. 兰花是单子叶植物的一个美丽的例子。 | 近义词: monocot | 反义词: dicotyledon (dicot) | 词性: noun
