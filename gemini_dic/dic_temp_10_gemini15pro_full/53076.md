dioxindol: /daɪˈɒksɪndɒl/ | n. | 二氧吲哚 | A heterocyclic organic compound with the molecular formula C8H5NO2.  It is a bicyclic structure consisting of a six-membered benzene ring fused to a five-membered nitrogen-containing ring.  | Dioxindol derivatives are important building blocks in the synthesis of various pharmaceuticals. 二氧吲哚衍生物是合成各种药物的重要组成部分。|  Isatin can be reduced to dioxindol.靛红可以被还原成二氧吲哚。 | synonyms: 1,3-dihydroindol-2,3-dione | antonyms: N/A | noun
