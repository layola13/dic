bemuse:/bɪˈmjuːz/| v. |使困惑，使茫然|To confuse or bewilder someone.|She was bemused by his sudden change of mood. 她被他突然的情绪变化弄得茫然。|近义词：confuse, bewilder, puzzle, perplex|反义词：clarify, enlighten|词性:动词

Etymology: From be- + muse (v.) "to gaze meditatively," from Old French muser "to ponder, dream, stare fixedly," of uncertain origin.  The original sense was "to cause to be lost in thought," hence "to bewilder, confuse."
