americanizers:/əˈmɛrɪkəˌnaɪzərz/
n.
美国化者，美国化推动者
People who promote or support Americanization, the process of assimilating to American culture and values.
These americanizers sought to instill patriotic values in new immigrants. 这些美国化推动者试图向新移民灌输爱国价值观。
americanizers worked to assimilate immigrants into American society. 美国化推动者致力于让移民融入美国社会。
synonyms: assimilationists, promoters of American culture
antonyms: traditionalists, cultural preservationists
noun
