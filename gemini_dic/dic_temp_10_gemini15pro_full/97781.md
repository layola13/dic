injudicable:/ɪnˈdʒuːdɪkəbl/ | adj. | 不可判定的，无法裁决的 | Not capable of being judged or decided; unsuitable for judicial determination. |  The issue of divine justice is often considered injudicable by human courts.  上帝的正义问题通常被认为是人类法庭无法裁决的。| Synonyms: nonjusticiable, undecidable, unjudgeable | Antonyms: judicable, decidable, determinable | adjective
