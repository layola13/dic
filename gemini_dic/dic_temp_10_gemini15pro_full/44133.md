crissum:/ˈkrɪsəm/| n.|肛门周围的羽毛区域；尾下覆羽|The area of feathers surrounding the cloaca of a bird, especially the undertail coverts.|Ornithologists use the color of the crissum to identify certain bird species. 鸟类学家利用尾下覆羽的颜色来识别某些鸟类。|The bird puffed up its crissum as a threat display. 这只鸟蓬松起它的尾下覆羽以示威胁。|synonyms: undertail coverts, vent, anal region|antonyms: (no direct antonyms, could consider dorsal feathers)|noun
