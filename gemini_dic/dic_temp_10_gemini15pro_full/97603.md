inhalant:/ɪnˈheɪlənt/| n.|吸入剂；由吸入方式进入体内的物质|A substance that is breathed in, often for medicinal or recreational purposes.  It can refer to medications delivered via inhalers or to substances abused for their psychoactive effects.|The dangers of inhalant abuse are serious and can be deadly. 吸入剂滥用的危害很严重，可能是致命的。|
The doctor prescribed an inhalant for her asthma. 医生给她开了治疗哮喘的吸入剂。|synonyms:  aerosol,  vapor,  fume|antonyms:  exhalant|noun
