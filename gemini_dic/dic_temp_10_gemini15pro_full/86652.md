helleborin:/ˈhɛlɪbɔːrɪn/| n. |嚏根草苷;嚏根草毒素|A poisonous glycoside obtained from the Christmas rose (Helleborus niger). It is a cardiac stimulant.|Helleborin is a potent toxin found in certain Helleborus species. 嚏根草苷是一种在某些嚏根草物种中发现的强效毒素。|Synonyms: hellebrin, helleborigenin|Antonyms: None|Noun
