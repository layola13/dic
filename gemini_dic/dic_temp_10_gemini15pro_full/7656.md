annunciative:/əˈnʌnsiˌeɪtɪv/
adjective
宣告的，通告的
Serving to announce something; declarative.
The chairman made an annunciative statement about the company's future. 董事长就公司的未来发表了声明。
近义词:declarative, pronounce, proclaim
反义词: secretive, taciturn
词性:adjective
