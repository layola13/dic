going:/ˈɡoʊɪŋ/ (US) /ˈɡəʊɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. v.|进行的，发生的，即将离去的|Present participle of "go".  Indicates an action in progress, a future plan, or a general tendency. |例句：The party is going well. 派对进行得很顺利。|近义词：progressing, proceeding, leaving, departing|反义词：staying, remaining, arriving|词性:形容词, 动词的现在分词

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
He is going to school every day. 他每天都去上学。
She is going home early today. 她今天早点回家。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am going to the store now. 我现在正要去商店。
They are going to the movies tonight. 他们今晚要去看电影。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
He has gone to London. 他已经去了伦敦。(表示已不在说话的地方)
She has been going to the gym regularly. 她一直定期去健身房。(表示持续一段时间的动作)

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been going to that cafe for years. 我多年来一直去那家咖啡馆。
They have been going to the same doctor. 他们一直去看同一个医生。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He went to the park yesterday. 他昨天去了公园。
She went home early. 她早早回家了。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was going to the store when I saw him. 我正要去商店的时候看到了他。
They were going to the party when the car broke down. 他们正要去参加聚会时，车子抛锚了。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
He had gone to the store before I arrived. 在我到达之前，他已经去了商店。
She had gone home early because she was sick. 她早早回家了，因为她病了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been going to that cafe for years before it closed. 在它关闭之前，我多年来一直去那家咖啡馆。
They had been going to the same doctor for a long time. 他们长期以来一直去看同一个医生。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
He will go to the party tomorrow. 他明天会去参加聚会。
She will go to London next year. 她明年会去伦敦。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be going to the store this time tomorrow. 明天这个时候我将正在去商店的路上。
They will be going to the beach next week. 下周他们将要去海滩。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
He will have gone to London by next year. 到明年，他将已经去了伦敦。
She will have gone home by the time you arrive. 到你到达的时候，她将已经回家了。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
I will have been going to that gym for two years by next month. 到下个月，我将已经去那家健身房两年了。
They will have been going to the same school for five years by the time they graduate. 到他们毕业的时候，他们将已经去同一所学校五年了。
