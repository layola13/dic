half-frowning:/ˌhæf ˈfraʊnɪŋ/
adjective
半皱眉的，略带不悦的
Partly frowning; showing slight displeasure or disapproval.
She gave him a half-frowning, half-amused look. 她给了他一个半皱眉半 amused 的表情。
近义词：scowling, disapproving
反义词：smiling, beaming
词性: adjective
