hoelike:/ˈhoʊlaɪk/
adjective
锄头状的；像锄头的
Resembling a hoe; shaped like a hoe.
The tool had a hoelike blade, perfect for weeding.  这工具有一个像锄头一样的刀片，非常适合除草。
The front of the car had a strangely hoelike shape.  车的前部有一个奇怪的像锄头一样的形状。
Near Synonyms: hoe-shaped, spadelike, shovel-like
Antonyms: rounded, smooth, pointed
