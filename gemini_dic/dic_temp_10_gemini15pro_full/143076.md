outbranches:/ˌaʊtˈbrɑːntʃɪz/
| n. |分支，支流；支派；旁系|Extensions or subdivisions that branch out from a main source or structure.  These can be physical like the branches of a tree or abstract like subcategories of a topic.|The river's outbranches reached far into the countryside. 这条河的支流延伸到很远的乡村。|synonyms: offshoots, branches, tributaries, ramifications|antonyms: main stem, trunk, source, origin|noun

outbranch:/ˌaʊtˈbrɑːntʃ/
| v. |分叉，分支|To extend or divide into branches, typically outwards from a main stem or source.|The company outbranched into new markets.  这家公司扩展到新的市场。|synonyms: branch out, diverge, ramify, spread out|antonyms: converge, consolidate, unite, centralize|verb
