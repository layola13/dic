diaglyphic: /ˌdaɪəˈɡlɪfɪk/

n.|阴刻宝石，阴刻作品| a diaglyphic carving or gem. |  The diaglyphic depicted a mythological scene. 这块阴刻宝石描绘了一个神话场景。|intaglio, carving| embossment, relief
