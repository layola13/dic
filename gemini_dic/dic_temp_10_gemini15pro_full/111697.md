lockjaw:/ˈlɒkdʒɔː/ (UK) /ˈlɑːkˌdʒɔː/ (US)| n.|破伤风; 牙关紧闭症|Lockjaw is a serious bacterial infection that causes painful muscle spasms, particularly in the jaw and neck.  It is also known as tetanus. |Example 1: The doctor diagnosed the patient with lockjaw after noticing the stiffness in his jaw and neck. 医生注意到病人下巴和颈部僵硬后，诊断他患有破伤风。

Example 2:  Lockjaw can be prevented through vaccination.  接种疫苗可以预防破伤风。|Synonym: tetanus|Antonym: N/A|noun
