词源：
Named after the German botanist Leonhart Fuchs (1501-1566).  The flower was named Fuchsia, and the color was later named after the flower.
来源于德国植物学家莱昂哈特·富克斯(Leonhart Fuchs，1501-1566)的名字。这种花被命名为Fuchsia（倒挂金钟），后来这种颜色以这种花命名。
