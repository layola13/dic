nonreimbursement:/ˌnɑːnriːɪmˈbɜːrsmənt/
n.
不报销，未偿付
lack of reimbursement; failure to reimburse.
The policy regarding nonreimbursement of travel expenses caused much dissent.  关于不报销差旅费的政策引起了很多异议。
nonpayment, no refund
reimbursement, compensation
noun
