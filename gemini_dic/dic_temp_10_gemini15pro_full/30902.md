chagrinned:/ʃəˈɡrɪnd/ /ʃəˈɡrind/| adj.|懊恼的，失望的|Feeling or showing disappointment or annoyance at having failed or been humiliated.|He was chagrined to discover he'd sent the email to the wrong person. 他懊恼地发现他把邮件发错了人。|例句：She was chagrined by her failure to win the prize. 她因没能赢得奖品而懊恼。

例句：He felt chagrined when he realized his mistake.  当他意识到自己的错误时，他感到很懊恼。


近义词：disappointed, annoyed, vexed, mortified, humiliated|反义词：pleased, delighted, elated|词性：形容词
