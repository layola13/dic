armlet: /ˈɑːmlɪt/ (US) /ˈɑːmlət/ (UK)
noun
臂环；手镯；袖章
A band worn around the upper arm, typically as ornamentation or an insignia of rank or office.
The princess wore a golden armlet. 公主戴着一个金臂环。
She wore a black armlet as a sign of mourning. 她戴着黑色袖章以示哀悼。
Synonyms: armband, bracelet, bangle
Antonyms: None (lack of an antonym for an item of jewelry)
词性: noun
