corrupted:/kəˈrʌptɪd/ | adj. | 腐败的，堕落的，损坏的 | damaged, spoiled, or made dishonest or immoral | The corrupted file could not be opened.  这个损坏的文件无法打开。 | The officials were corrupted by greed. 官员们因贪婪而腐败。 | synonyms: tainted, contaminated, depraved, polluted, vitiated | antonyms: pure, uncorrupted, incorruptible, untainted, virtuous | adjective

corrupt:/kəˈrʌpt/ | v. | 腐蚀，使堕落；贿赂 | to cause someone or something to become dishonest or immoral; to change something so that it is wrong or not the way it should be | The politician was corrupted by power.  这位政治家被权力腐蚀了。 | He tried to corrupt the judge with a bribe. 他试图用贿赂腐蚀法官。 | synonyms: bribe, subvert, pervert, debauch, deprave | antonyms: purify, cleanse, reform, uplift, edify | verb

corrupt:/kəˈrʌpt/ | adj. | 腐败的，堕落的 |  dishonest or immoral | The corrupt official was arrested.  腐败的官员被逮捕了。 | The data on the hard drive was corrupt.  硬盘上的数据损坏了。 | synonyms: dishonest, unscrupulous, crooked, fraudulent, deceitful | antonyms: honest, upright, ethical, moral, righteous | adjective
