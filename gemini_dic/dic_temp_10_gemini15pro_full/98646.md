insue:/ɪnˈsuː/
verb
发生；接着发生；因而产生
happen or occur afterward, often as a result of something.
After the heavy rains, flooding ensued. 大雨过后，发生了洪水。
The police officer pursued the suspect, and a high-speed chase ensued. 警察追捕嫌疑人，随之而来的是一场高速追逐。
近义词:follow, result, arise, stem, proceed
反义词:precede, cause
词性:v.
