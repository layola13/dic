complexant:/kəmˈplɛksənt/ | n. | 络合剂；螯合剂 | A substance that forms a complex with a metal ion.  A complexant is a molecule that can form a stable complex with a metal ion, often used in chemistry and biology to control metal ion concentrations. | EDTA is a common complexant used in many industrial processes. 乙二胺四乙酸是许多工业过程中常用的络合剂。| synonyms: chelating agent, ligand | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "metal precipitant") | noun

complexing agent:/kəmˈplɛksɪŋ ˈeɪdʒənt/ | n. | 络合剂；螯合剂 |  A substance that forms a complex with a metal ion.  A complexing agent is synonymous with complexant. |  The complexing agent binds to the metal ion, preventing it from reacting with other substances.  这种络合剂与金属离子结合，防止其与其他物质发生反应。| synonyms: chelating agent, ligand, complexant | antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "metal precipitant") | noun
