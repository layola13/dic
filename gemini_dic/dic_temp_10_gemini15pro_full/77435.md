gawked:/ɡɔːkt/| v.| 呆呆地看，瞠目结舌 | to stare stupidly, often with an open mouth |  The tourists gawked at the street performers. 游客们呆呆地看着街头艺人们。| synonyms: stared, gaped, ogled | antonyms: ignored, overlooked | verb

gawked:/ɡɔːkt/| v.| 瞠目结舌 | to be completely surprised and unable to speak |  She gawked at the news of her winning the lottery. 她听到自己中了彩票的消息，惊得目瞪口呆。| synonyms: astonished, amazed, dumbfounded | antonyms: unimpressed, unfazed | verb
