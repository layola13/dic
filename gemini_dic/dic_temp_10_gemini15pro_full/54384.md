disillusioned:/ˌdɪsɪˈluːʒənd/ | adj. | 失望的，幻想破灭的 |  Disappointed in someone or something that one discovers to be less good than one had believed. |  He became disillusioned with politics and left the party. 他对政治感到失望，离开了该党。| synonyms: disappointed, disenchanted, jaded, cynical | antonyms: hopeful, optimistic, enthusiastic | adjective


disillusioned:/ˌdɪsɪˈluːʒənd/ | adj. | 失去幻想的，醒悟的 |  Free from illusion or naive belief. |  She was disillusioned about love after her divorce. 离婚后，她对爱情失去了幻想。| synonyms: disenchanted, disabused, undeceived | antonyms: naive, idealistic, romantic | adjective
