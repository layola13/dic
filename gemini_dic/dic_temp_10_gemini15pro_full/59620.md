ecchymoses:/ˌɛkɪˈmoʊsiːz/| n. |瘀斑（复数）|Plural form of ecchymosis.|The patient presented with multiple ecchymoses on their legs. 病人的腿上有多处瘀斑。|Synonyms: bruises, contusions|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ecchymōsis, from Ancient Greek ἐκχύμωσις (ekchýmōsis, “extravasation”), from ἐκχυμοῦσθαι (ekchumoûsthai, “to extravasate”), from ἐκ- (ek-, “out”) + χυμός (khymós, “juice, humour”).  Meaning "effusion of blood beneath the skin" is from 1770.
