lucidity:/luːˈsɪdəti/| n.| 清晰，明晰，清醒|The lucidity of her writing made it easy to understand complex ideas. 她的写作清晰明了，使复杂的思想易于理解。|例句：He spoke with remarkable lucidity. 他讲话非常清晰。|例句：After the fever broke, she experienced a brief period of lucidity. 退烧后，她短暂地清醒了一会儿。|近义词：clarity, clearness, transparency, perspicuity|反义词：obfuscation, confusion, obscurity, vagueness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *luciditas, from Latin lucidus (“bright, clear, shining”) (from lux (“light”)) + -itas (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality).
