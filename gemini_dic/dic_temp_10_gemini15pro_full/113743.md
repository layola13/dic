macrography:/məˈkrɒɡrəfi/
noun
宏观摄影术；肉眼可见的文字
The study or description of objects large enough to be observed by the naked eye.  Also, the practice of making enlarged photographic images of such objects.
Macrography allows us to see the intricate details of a butterfly's wing. 宏观摄影让我们能够看到蝴蝶翅膀的复杂细节。
This ancient inscription is a perfect subject for macrography. 这段古代铭文是宏观摄影的完美主题。
Synonyms: macrophotography, close-up photography
Antonyms: micrography, microphotography
