ensober:/ɪnˈsoʊbər/
v.
使清醒；使冷静
To make sober; to free from the influence of intoxicating liquor.
He splashed cold water on his face to ensober himself. 他往脸上泼冷水让自己清醒过来。
近义词: sober up, calm down
反义词: intoxicate, inebriate
词性:动词


词源:
From en- + sober, from Old French sobre, from Latin sobrius.
