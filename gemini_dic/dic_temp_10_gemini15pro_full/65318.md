eugenics:/juːˈdʒɛnɪks/
n.
优生学
The study of how to arrange reproduction within a human population to increase the occurrence of heritable characteristics regarded as desirable.
Eugenics was a popular social movement in the early 20th century. 优生学在20世纪早期是一场受欢迎的社会运动。
Many countries enacted eugenics laws in the early 20th century. 许多国家在20世纪早期颁布了优生法。
近义词: genetic engineering, genetic modification
反义词: dysgenics
