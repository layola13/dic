maligner:/məˈlaɪnər/| n.|诽谤者，恶意中伤者|A person who maligns someone, speaking about them falsely and maliciously.|He was known as a maligner of all things good and decent. 他被称为所有美好和正派事物的诽谤者。|Synonyms: slanderer, defamer, libeler, traducer|Antonyms: admirer, supporter, advocate|Noun

malign:/məˈlaɪn/| vt. |诽谤，污蔑，中伤；(疾病)恶化|To speak about someone falsely and maliciously.|The article unfairly maligned the actor's reputation. 这篇文章不公平地诋毁了这位演员的名声。|vt.|(疾病)恶化|The tumor was malign and spread quickly. 肿瘤是恶性的，扩散很快。|Synonyms: slander, defame, libel, traduce, vilify, denigrate|Antonyms: praise, commend, laud, extol|Verb
malign:/məˈlaɪn/| adj.|恶性的，有害的| (of a disease) very virulent or infectious.|The biopsy showed a malign tumor. 活检显示了一个恶性肿瘤。|Synonyms: harmful, evil, cancerous, malignant, noxious|Antonyms: benign, harmless, beneficial|Adjective
