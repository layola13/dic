antimoralistic:/ˌæntiˌmɔːrəˈlɪstɪk/
adj.
反道德主义的
Opposed to or rejecting moral principles or traditional morality.
His antimoralistic stance often put him at odds with the community. 他反道德主义的立场经常使他与社区格格不入。
Synonyms: amoral, nonmoral, unmoral, immoral
Antonyms: moral, moralistic
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix meaning "against, opposite of") + moralistic (adjective meaning "characterized by or expressive of a concern with morality, especially a narrow or conventional morality")
