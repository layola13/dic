mimicked:/ˈmɪmɪkt/ | v. | 模仿，效仿 | To imitate or copy someone's speech, movements, or behavior, often in a playful or satirical way. | The comedian mimicked the president's voice and gestures.  喜剧演员模仿了总统的声音和姿态。| synonyms: imitated, copied, aped, mocked | antonyms: originated, innovated | verb

mimicked:/ˈmɪmɪkt/ | adj. | 被模仿的 | Imitated or copied. | Her mimicked laughter sounded unnatural. 她模仿的笑声听起来很不自然。| synonyms: imitated, copied | antonyms: original, unique | adjective
