deluge:/ˈdeljuːdʒ/ /ˈdɛljuːdʒ/| n. vt.|洪水；暴雨；泛滥；大量|A severe flood; a heavy fall of rain; an overwhelming amount of something.|The town was deluged by heavy rains. 这个小镇被暴雨淹没了。|近义词：flood, inundation, torrent, downpour|反义词:drought, trickle|名词

deluge:/ˈdeljuːdʒ/ /ˈdɛljuːdʒ/| vt.|使泛滥；淹没；压倒|To flood or overwhelm.|We were deluged with applications for the job.  我们收到了大量的求职申请。|近义词：flood, overwhelm, inundate, swamp|反义词:drain|动词
