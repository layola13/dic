morbidly:/ˈmɔːrbɪdli/| adv.|病态地，不正常地，|characterized by or appealing to an abnormal and unhealthy interest in disturbing and unpleasant subjects, especially death and disease.|She has a morbidly fascination with true crime. 她对真实犯罪有着病态的迷恋。|近义词：unhealthily, gruesomely, macabrely|反义词: healthily, cheerfully|词性:副词
词源: From Late Latin *morbidus* "diseased," from Latin *morbus* "disease, sickness" + -ly (suffix forming adverbs).
