overload:/ˌoʊvərˈloʊd/ /ˌəʊvəˈləʊd/| n. v. |超负荷；过载；超载量|An excessive load or amount.  To load with too great a burden or cargo. |The electrical circuit overloaded and caused a fire. 电路超负荷引起火灾。|synonyms: overburden, overtax, overwhelm|antonyms: underload, underwhelm|noun

overload:/ˌoʊvərˈloʊd/ /ˌəʊvəˈləʊd/| v. |使超载；使过载；使负荷过重|To load with too great a burden or cargo. To burden excessively. |Don't overload the washing machine. 不要把洗衣机装得太满。|synonyms: overburden, overtax, overwhelm|antonyms: underload, underwhelm|verb
