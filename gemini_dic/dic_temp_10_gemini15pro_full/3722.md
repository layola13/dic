aggrieved:/əˈɡriːvd/| adj.|委屈的，愤愤不平的|Feeling resentment at having been unfairly treated.|She felt aggrieved at not being invited to the party. 她因为没有被邀请参加聚会而感到委屈。|近义词：resentful, offended, wronged, indignant|反义词：content, satisfied, pleased|adjective

aggrieved:/əˈɡriːvd/| adj.|受委屈的，愤懑的|Experiencing or expressing a sense of injustice or wrongdoing.|The aggrieved customer demanded a refund.  这位感到受委屈的顾客要求退款。|近义词：upset, distressed, troubled|反义词：happy, joyful, cheerful|adjective
