deadpan:/ˈdedpæn/| adj. adv. n.|面无表情的，不动声色的；面无表情地，不动声色地；面无表情|deliberately impassive or expressionless, typically in a humorous way.  Showing no emotion, especially when telling a joke.|He delivered the joke deadpan. 他面无表情地讲了那个笑话。|近义词：expressionless, impassive, blank, poker-faced|反义词： expressive, emotional|词性: adjective, adverb, noun
deadpan:/ˈdedpæn/|词源:  From 'dead' + 'pan' (referring to a face with no expression like a metal pan).|
