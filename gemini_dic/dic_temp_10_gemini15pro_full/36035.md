codiscoverers:/ˌkəʊdɪˈskʌvərərz/| n. |共同发现者|People who discover something together.  Those who share the credit for a discovery. | Marie Curie and her husband, Pierre, were codiscoverers of radium. 居里夫人和她丈夫皮埃尔是镭的共同发现者。|  The two scientists were codiscoverers of the new element. 这两位科学家是新元素的共同发现者。 | collaborators, joint discoverers |  | noun
