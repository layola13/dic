basilisk:/ˈbæsɪlɪsk/ /ˈbæzɪlɪsk/| n.| 蜥蜴; 蛇怪; 一种加农炮 |A legendary reptile reputed to be king of serpents and said to kill by its breath or look. |例句：The basilisk is a mythical creature with a deadly stare. 蛇怪是一种传说中的生物，拥有致命的凝视。

例句：The adventurer bravely faced the basilisk in the dungeon.  冒险家勇敢地在地牢里面对蛇怪。|近义词：cockatrice, wyvern, dragon |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
Etymology: From Old French *basilisc*, from Latin *basiliscus*, from Ancient Greek *βασιλίσκος* (*basilískos*), meaning "little king."  The name refers to the crown-like crest or marking on the head of some species.
