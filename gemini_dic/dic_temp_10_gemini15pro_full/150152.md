pan-frying:/ˈpænˌfraɪɪŋ/| n. |煎; 以少量油煎|The act of cooking food in a frying pan with a small amount of fat or oil.|I love the crispy skin you get from pan-frying chicken. 我喜欢煎鸡肉时脆脆的鸡皮。|近义词：shallow frying, sauteing|反义词: deep-frying|词性: 名词(动名词)
pan-fry:/ˈpænˌfraɪ/| v. |煎; 以少量油煎|To cook food in a frying pan with a small amount of fat or oil.|He pan-fried the fish to perfection. 他把鱼煎得恰到好处。|近义词：saute, shallow fry|反义词: deep-fry|词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "pan" + "frying" (present participle of "fry"). "Pan" comes from Old English "panne," from Late Latin "panna." "Fry" comes from Old French "frire," from Latin "frigere."
