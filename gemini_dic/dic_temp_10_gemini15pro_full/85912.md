heading-off:/ˈhedɪŋ ɒf/ | phrasal verb | 阻止，防止，避免 | To prevent something from happening, especially something unpleasant. |  They built a fence to head off the cattle. 他们建了一道篱笆来阻止牛群进入。 | avert, prevent, forestall, preclude |  allow, permit, encourage | verb phrase

Etymology: "head" (Old English *hēafod) + "off" (Old English of)
