paroophoron:/ˌpærəˈɒfərɒn/| n. |卵旁体|A vestigial structure in the female reproductive system, representing remnants of the mesonephric tubules, located in the broad ligament between the ovary and the fallopian tube.  It is a remnant of the Wolffian body (mesonephros) in the female.|The paroophoron can sometimes be the site of cysts or tumors.|Near synonyms: epoophoron,  |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for an anatomical structure)|Noun
