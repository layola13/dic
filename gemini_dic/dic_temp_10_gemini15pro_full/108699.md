leam:/liːm/| n.| 微光，闪光，光线；火焰，火光|A faint or flickering light; flame.|The dying embers cast a faint leam across the room. 垂死的余烬在房间里投下微弱的光芒。|近义词：gleam, glimmer, flicker, shimmer|反义词：darkness, gloom|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *leme, from Old English lēoma (“light, ray, beam, brightness, splendor”), from Proto-Germanic *lauhmaz (“light, flame”), from Proto-Indo-European *léwkm̥ōn (“light, brightness”). Cognate with Scots leme (“gleam, flash”), West Frisian leam (“flame”), Dutch loom (“flame, blaze”), German Low German Lome (“flame”), German Lohme (“flame”).
