creepered:/ˈkriːpəd/| v. |爬行，蔓延，(感觉)慢慢出现|The ivy creepered up the wall. 常春藤爬满了墙壁。|近义词：crawled, spread, crept|反义词:receded, shrunk|verb
creepered:/ˈkriːpəd/| adj. |感到毛骨悚然的，令人害怕的|The movie gave me a creepered feeling. 这电影让我毛骨悚然。|近义词：creepy, unnerving, scary|反义词:comforting, reassuring|adjective

词源:  From creep (v.) + -er (agent suffix) + -ed (past participle suffix).  Essentially, something that has crept. The adjectival use is a more recent development.
