burnouts:/ˈbɜːrnaʊts/| n.|精疲力竭，倦怠|A state of emotional, physical, and mental exhaustion caused by prolonged or excessive stress.|She's suffering from burnout after working 70-hour weeks for months. 她因连续几个月每周工作70小时而感到精疲力竭。|近义词：exhaustion, fatigue, breakdown|反义词:energy, vigor|词性:名词

burnout:/ˈbɜːrnaʊt/| n.|烧坏，烧毁|The failure of an electrical device or component due to overheating.|The burnout of the motor caused the machine to stop working. 电机的烧毁导致机器停止工作。|近义词：failure, malfunction|反义词:repair, fix|词性:名词
