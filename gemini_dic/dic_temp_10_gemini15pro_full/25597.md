bump:/bʌmp/| n. v.|碰撞，肿块，隆起物；撞，碰，颠簸|A sudden collision, a swelling, a protuberance; to strike or knock against something, to jolt or bounce.|例句：The car hit a bump in the road. 汽车在路上撞到了一个凸起。|近义词：knock, jolt, lump, swelling|反义词:smooth, dip, hollow|词性:名词,动词

bump:/bʌmp/| v.|增加，提高|To increase or raise something, often suddenly.|例句：They bumped up the prices. 他们提高了价格。|近义词：raise, increase, boost|反义词:lower, decrease, reduce|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bumpen*, of imitative origin.  Relates to words like "boom" and "thump."
