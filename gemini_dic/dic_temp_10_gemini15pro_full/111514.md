loanin: /ˈloʊnɪn/
noun
借入; 贷款
The act of receiving something on loan.  This word is likely a non-standard or dialectal form, possibly originating from a blend of "loan" and "in".
He got a loanin from the bank. 他从银行获得了一笔贷款。
近义词: borrowing, loan
反义词: lending
