crossbeak:/ˈkrɒsbik/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsbik/ (US)| n.|交喙鸟; 交嘴雀|A bird of the finch family (Fringillidae) having the tips of the upper and lower mandibles crossing each other.|We saw a red crossbill perched on a pine cone. 我们看到一只红交嘴雀栖息在松果上。|近义词：crossbill|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From cross + beak.  "Cross" refers to the crossed mandibles, and "beak" is the bird's bill.
