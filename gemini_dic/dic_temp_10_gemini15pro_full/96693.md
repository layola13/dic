inedibility:/ˌɪnɛdəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可食用性，不能吃
The inedibility of the mushroom was apparent from its bright color. 这种蘑菇的鲜艳颜色表明它不可食用。
The inedibility of the plant made it useless as a food source.  这种植物的不可食用性使其无法作为食物来源。
Near synonyms: uneatableness, nonedibility
Antonyms: edibility, eatableness
