mesoplast:/ˈmɛzəˌplæst/ | n. | 中胚层；中层 | The mesoplast is the middle layer of a three-layered cell wall in certain algae.  中胚层是某些藻类三层细胞壁的中间层。| The mesoplast of this alga species is particularly thick. 这种藻类的中胚层特别厚。 | middle layer, median layer |  | noun
