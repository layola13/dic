parastatals:/ˌpærəˈsteɪtəlz/ | n. | 半国营公司，准政府机构 | Companies or organizations that are owned or controlled wholly or partly by the government, but operate as independent entities. |  Many developing countries have numerous parastatals in key sectors like energy and transportation. 许多发展中国家在能源和交通等关键部门拥有大量的半国营公司。 | Synonyms: government-owned corporations, state-owned enterprises, public sector undertakings | Antonyms: private companies, privately-owned businesses | noun
