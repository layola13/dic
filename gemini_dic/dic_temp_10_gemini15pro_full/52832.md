diluviate:/daɪˈluːvieɪt/ /dɪˈluːviˌeɪt/| v. | 倾盆大雨；下如瓢泼大雨 | To rain very heavily; to flood. |  The storm clouds gathered, and soon it began to diluviate.  暴雨云聚集，很快倾盆大雨就开始了。| 近义词: downpour, pour, storm, deluge | 反义词: drizzle, sprinkle | 词性: 动词

diluviate:/daɪˈluːvieɪt/ /dɪˈluːviˌeɪt/| n. | 暴雨；洪水 | A heavy downpour; a flood. | The diluviate caused significant damage to the crops. 这场暴雨对农作物造成了重大损害。| 近义词: downpour, deluge, flood | 反义词: drought | 词性: 名词


词源: From Latin *diluviāre, from diluvium (“flood, deluge”), from diluere (“to wash away”), from dis- (“apart, away”) + luere (“to wash”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *lewh₂- (“to wash”).
