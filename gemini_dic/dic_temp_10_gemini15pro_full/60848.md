electrotype: /ɪˈlɛktrəˌtaɪp/ | n. | 电版; 复制品; 铅版 | A metallic copy of something, such as a page of type or a design, made by electrolytic deposition.  A facsimile made by this process.  A person or thing that is exactly like another. |  The museum displayed an electrotype of the ancient gold coin.  博物馆展出了这枚古代金币的电版复制品。 | synonyms: replica, duplicate, copy, facsimile | antonyms: original, prototype | noun

electrotype: /ɪˈlɛktrəˌtaɪp/ | v. | 电铸; 用电版印刷 | To make an electrotype of. | The printing company electrotyped the entire book. 这家印刷公司用电版印刷了整本书。| synonyms: reproduce, copy, duplicate | antonyms: originate, create | verb
