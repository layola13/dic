counter-gobony:/ˈkaʊntər ɡɒbəni/ | adj. | 相反的，逆向的（在纹章学中指与相邻图案颜色相反）| In heraldry, counter-gobony describes a pattern where the tinctures are reversed in adjacent sections, creating a checkered effect with opposite colors. | The shield was counter-gobony, with alternating squares of blue and gold next to squares of gold and blue.  盾牌是反戈博尼图案，蓝色和金色的交替方块旁边是金色和蓝色的方块。 |  Opposite, reversed, inverted (in heraldry) | synonymous (in general usage):  contrary, opposing | antonymous (in general usage):  similar, identical | adjective
