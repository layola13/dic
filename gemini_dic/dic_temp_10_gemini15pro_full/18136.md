behooveful:/bɪˈhuːvfʊl/
adjective
有益的，合适的，应该的
suitable, proper, and fitting; incumbent
It is behooveful for a young man to show respect to his elders.  对年轻人来说，尊敬长辈是应该的。
近义词:fitting, proper, appropriate, suitable, incumbent, apt
反义词:inappropriate, unsuitable, improper, unfit, unbecoming
词性:adjective
