helianthin:/ˌhiːliˈænθɪn/| n. |甲基橙;向日葵红;二甲基氨基偶氮苯磺酸钠|An orange azo dye used as an acid-base indicator, turning red at pH 3.1 and yellow at pH 4.4.  It is also known as methyl orange.|The chemist used helianthin to determine the pH of the solution. 化学家用甲基橙来测定溶液的pH值。|Synonyms: methyl orange|Antonyms: N/A|noun
