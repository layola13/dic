almesse:/alˈmɛsə/ | n. | alms, charity |  An archaic word meaning charitable giving of money or goods to the poor.  Derived from Old French "almosne," ultimately from Late Latin "eleemosyna," from Greek "eleēmosynē" meaning "pity, alms."  |  The knight gave almesse to the beggar.  骑士给了乞丐施舍。 | synonyms: charity, donation, offering, benefaction | antonyms: greed, selfishness, avarice | noun
