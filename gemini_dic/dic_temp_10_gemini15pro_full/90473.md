hubcap:/ˈhʌbkæp/ (US) /ˈhʌbkæp/ (UK)| n. |轮毂罩，轮盖|A metal or plastic cover over the centre of a vehicle's wheel.|

例句：
The hubcap fell off when I hit the pothole.
我的车撞到坑洼时，轮毂罩掉了。

My car needs a new hubcap.
我的车需要一个新的轮毂罩。

近义词：wheel cover, rim cover|
反义词: (无明确的反义词)|
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From *hub* (the central part of a wheel) + *cap* (a protective covering).
