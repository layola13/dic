balata:/bəˈlɑːtə/ | n. | 巴拉塔树胶，巴拉塔树 |A tough, elastic, non-elastic substance resembling rubber, obtained from the milky sap of the South American balata tree (Manilkara bidentata). It is used in belting, golf balls, and other products. | The balata balls offered superior performance compared to the gutta-percha ones. 巴拉塔球比古塔胶球性能更优越。

 The cable insulation is made of balata.  这电缆的绝缘材料是巴拉塔树胶制成的。 | 近义词: gutta-percha, rubber | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

balata:/bəˈlɑːtə/ | n. | 巴拉塔树 | The South American tree (Manilkara bidentata) from which balata gum is obtained. | The balata tree thrives in humid tropical forests. 巴拉塔树生长在潮湿的热带森林中。
