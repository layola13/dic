anaclastic:/ˌænəˈklæstɪk/
adjective
变形表面的，折射的
Relating to or denoting refraction by a surface of uneven curvature.  Specifically relating to lenses with a surface of uneven curvature, used to correct astigmatism.
An anaclastic lens can focus light into a line.  变形透镜可以将光线聚焦成一条线。
An anaclastic curve is formed by the reflection of rays from a curved mirror.  变形曲线是由曲面镜反射的光线形成的。
近义词:refractive, astigmatic
反义词:None
词性:adjective

anaclastics:/ˌænəˈklæstɪks/
noun
变形光学
The study of refraction by uneven surfaces.
Anaclastics involves complex calculations to determine the path of light.  变形光学涉及复杂的计算来确定光线的路径。
He specialized in the field of anaclastics, designing lenses for various applications. 他专门研究变形光学领域，设计用于各种应用的透镜。
近义词:dioptrics
反义词:None
词性:noun

词源: From Greek *anaklastikos*, from *anaklan* "to bend back, reflect."
