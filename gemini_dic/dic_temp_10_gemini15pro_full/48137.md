decollectivize:/diːkəˈlɛktɪvaɪz/
verb
非集体化；取消集体化
To abolish collectivization, especially of farms or businesses.  The process of converting collectively owned property (such as farmland) back to private ownership.
The government decided to decollectivize agriculture and allow farmers to own their own land. 政府决定取消农业集体化，允许农民拥有自己的土地。

After the fall of the Soviet Union, many countries began to decollectivize their economies. 苏联解体后，许多国家开始取消其经济的集体化。

近义词:privatize, individualize
反义词:collectivize, nationalize
词性:v.
