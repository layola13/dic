leans:/liːnz/
verb | 倾斜，倚靠，倾向 | To incline or bend from a vertical position.  | The tower leans slightly to the left.  塔略微向左倾斜。|  incline, bend, tilt | straighten, stand | v.
leans:/liːnz/
noun | 倾向，偏好 | An inclination or preference towards something. |  He has leanings towards socialism. 他倾向于社会主义。|  inclination, tendency, preference | aversion, dislike | n.
