ETYMOLOGY->
From middle + weight.  "Middle" comes from Old English "middel", meaning "mid, midst." "Weight" comes from Old English "wiht" meaning "thing, creature, person, weight".  The combined meaning referring to a weight class in boxing emerged in the late 19th century.
