fritter-away:/ˈfrɪtər əˈweɪ/
verb
浪费，挥霍
to waste time, money, or effort on something unimportant
He frittered away his inheritance on fast cars and expensive holidays. 他把遗产浪费在跑车和昂贵的假期上。
squander, waste, dissipate
save, conserve, preserve
词性: 动词短语

fritter:/ˈfrɪtər/
noun
油炸面糊；油炸食品
a piece of fruit, vegetable, or meat that is covered in batter and deep-fried
apple fritters 苹果油条
snack, treat, fried food
词性: 名词

away:/əˈweɪ/
adverb
离开；离去
from a particular place or position
Go away! 走开！
off, gone, absent
here, present, near
词性: 副词
