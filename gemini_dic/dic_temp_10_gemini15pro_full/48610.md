defectiveness:/dɪˈfɛktɪvnəs/| n.|缺陷；不完美；有缺陷|The defectiveness of the product led to a recall. 该产品的缺陷导致召回。|例句：The defectiveness of the bridge caused it to collapse.  这座桥的缺陷导致它倒塌。

The defectiveness of the argument undermined his whole presentation.  这个论证的缺陷损害了他的整个演讲。|近义词：faultiness, imperfection, flawedness|反义词:perfection, soundness, completeness|词性:noun

Etymology:  From Late Middle English *defectyfnes, from Old French defectif + -nes/-ness.  Ultimately from Latin dēfectivus (“defective, incomplete”).
