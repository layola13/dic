flowable:/ˈfloʊəbl/| adj. n.|流动的，可流动的；流动资产|Something that is flowable is capable of flowing, or being poured easily.  A flowable is something that flows easily, typically a liquid or a substance that behaves like a liquid.|The concrete mix needs to be flowable enough to be pumped easily.  这种混凝土混合物需要有足够的流动性以便于泵送。|The new paint is highly flowable and easy to apply.  这种新油漆流动性很好，很容易涂抹。|近义词：liquid, fluid, pourable|反义词：viscous, thick, solid|词性:adjective, noun
flowable:/ˈfloʊəbl/| n.|流动资产|Assets that can be readily converted into cash.|Liquid assets and flowables are important for a company's short-term financial health.  流动资产对公司的短期财务健康至关重要。|The company had a significant amount of flowables on hand.  该公司手头上有大量的流动资产。|近义词：liquid assets, current assets|反义词：fixed assets, long-term assets|词性:noun
