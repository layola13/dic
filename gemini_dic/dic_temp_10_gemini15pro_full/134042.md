nonidealistic:/ˌnɒnaɪˌdiːəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
非理想主义的
Not idealistic; practical or pragmatic.  Lacking or not based on ideals. Focused on practical considerations rather than on ideals.
He took a nonidealistic approach to politics, focusing on achievable goals rather than grand visions. 他对政治采取了非理想主义的做法，注重可实现的目标而不是宏伟的愿景。
She had a nonidealistic view of marriage, seeing it as a partnership rather than a fairytale romance. 她对婚姻持非理想主义的观点，认为婚姻是一种伙伴关系，而不是童话般的浪漫。
近义词: pragmatic, practical, realistic, down-to-earth
反义词: idealistic, utopian, romantic
词性: adjective
词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + idealistic (from ideal + -istic)
