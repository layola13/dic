composites:/ˈkɒmpəzɪts/ /ˈkɑːmpəzɪts/ | n. |复合材料; 复合物|A material made up of two or more distinct materials, combined to enhance properties.|例句：Carbon fiber composites are used in aircraft construction.  碳纤维复合材料用于飞机制造。|近义词：mixture, compound, blend|反义词: element, component| noun


composites:/ˈkɒmpəzɪts/ /ˈkɑːmpəzɪts/ | adj. |复合的|Made up of separate parts or elements.|例句：The building has a composite structure of steel and concrete. 这座建筑物由钢筋混凝土的复合结构组成。|近义词：combined, mixed, compound|反义词: simple, basic| adjective
