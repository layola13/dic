murk:/mɜːrk/ /mɝːk/| n.  adj.|黑暗；阴沉；朦胧；浑浊|Darkness or gloom, especially that which is thick or heavy; also, something which obscures or makes unclear.|The boat disappeared into the murk.  那艘船消失在黑暗中。|近义词：darkness, gloom, obscurity, haze, fog|反义词：brightness, clarity, light|Noun

murk:/mɜːrk/ /mɝːk/| adj.|黑暗的；阴沉的；朦胧的；浑浊的|Dark and gloomy; obscure or unclear.|The murk water made it difficult to see the bottom. 浑浊的水使人难以看到底部。|近义词：dark, gloomy, obscure, hazy, foggy|反义词：bright, clear, light|Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mirke, from Old Norse *myrkr, from Proto-Germanic *murkaz, from Proto-Indo-European *merg-, *mork- (“dark”). Cognate with Old English mirce (“dark”), Dutch merk (“mark, sign”), German Mark (“mark, borderland”), Gothic 𐌼𐌰𐍂𐌺𐍃 (marks, “border”). Related to murky.
