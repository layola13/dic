monotrematous:/ˌmɒnəˈtriːməs/ /ˌmɑːnəˈtriːməs/| adj.|单孔目的，卵生的|Of or relating to the Monotremata, an order of primitive mammals that lay large yolky eggs and have a common opening for the urogenital and digestive systems.  Characterized by laying eggs rather than bearing live young.|The platypus is a monotrematous mammal.  鸭嘴兽是一种单孔目哺乳动物。|Near synonyms: oviparous, egg-laying|Antonyms: viviparous|adjective

monotreme:/ˈmɒnətriːm/ /ˈmɑːnətriːm/| n.|单孔目动物|A primitive mammal that lays large yolky eggs and has a common opening for the urogenital and digestive systems; the echidnas and the platypus.|Echidnas are fascinating monotremes found in Australia.  针鼹是发现于澳大利亚的迷人的单孔目动物。|Near synonyms: prototherian|Antonyms: placental mammal, marsupial|noun
