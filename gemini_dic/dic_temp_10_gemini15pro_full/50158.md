deny:/dɪˈnaɪ/| vt.|否认，拒绝|To declare untrue; to refuse to admit or accept.|He denied stealing the money. 他否认偷了钱。|近义词: refuse, reject, contradict, disavow|反义词: admit, accept, acknowledge, confess|verb

deny:/dɪˈnaɪ/| vt.|拒绝给予|To withhold something from someone.|The company denied him access to the confidential files. 公司拒绝让他访问机密文件。|近义词: refuse, withhold|反义词: grant, allow, give|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *deneier*, denier "deny, refuse,"  from Latin denegare "to refuse, deny, say no," from de- "down, away" + negare "to deny, refuse," from nec "not" + aio "I say."
