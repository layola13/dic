overambitiously: /ˌoʊvəræmˈbɪʃəsli/ | adv. | 过于雄心勃勃地，野心过大地 |  In a manner that exhibits excessive ambition; in a way that sets goals or pursues objectives that are beyond one's realistic capabilities or resources. | He overambitiously planned to finish the project in a week, but it ended up taking a month. 他过于雄心勃勃地计划在一周内完成这个项目，但最终花了一个月的时间。| Synonyms: excessively ambitiously, impractically ambitiously, unrealistically ambitiously | Antonyms: cautiously, modestly, realistically | 词性: 副词
