oompah-pah:/ˈuːmpɑː pɑː/ | n. | 伴奏音乐(铜管乐队演奏的),  欢快的进行曲|A rhythmic accompaniment often played by a brass band, typically for a polka or other lively march. |The oompah-pah music made everyone want to dance.  伴奏音乐让每个人都想跳舞。|Synonyms: beat, rhythm, accompaniment | Antonyms: silence, quiet | noun

oompah-pah:/ˈuːmpɑː pɑː/ | interj. |  (象声词) 咚咚锵| Used to represent the sound of a oompah-pah accompaniment. | "Oompah-pah," went the tuba.  “咚咚锵，”低音号发出声音。|Synonyms:  (No direct equivalent -  onomatopoeic) | Antonyms: (No direct equivalent - onomatopoeic) | interjection
