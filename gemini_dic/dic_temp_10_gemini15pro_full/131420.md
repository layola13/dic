nonclimactic:/ˌnɒnklaɪˈmæktɪk/
adjective
平淡的，虎头蛇尾的
lacking a climax; failing to come to a satisfactory or exciting conclusion.
The ending of the movie was rather nonclimactic after such a dramatic buildup. 这部电影的结局在如此戏剧性的铺垫之后显得相当平淡。
