harrage:/ˈhærɪdʒ/| n. vt.|骚扰，反复袭击|A prolonged and aggressive attack, criticism, or interrogation.|The press harraged the celebrity relentlessly after the scandal.  媒体在丑闻曝光后对这位名人进行了无情的骚扰。|例句：The politician was harraged with questions about the scandal.  这位政治家被有关丑闻的问题纠缠不休。|近义词：badger, hound, pester, torment|反义词：soothe, comfort|词性:名词,动词


词源: From Old North French *harier “to set dogs on, incite, provoke,” from Frankish *herjan “to cry out, shout”
