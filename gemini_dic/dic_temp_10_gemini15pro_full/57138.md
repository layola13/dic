dovetailer:/ˈdʌvˌteɪlər/| n.|榫接者，吻合者，使…吻合的人|A person or thing that dovetails something, especially one whose job it is to join pieces of wood together using dovetail joints.  A person who makes things fit together neatly or smoothly. |The furniture maker was a skilled dovetailer, creating seamless joints.|Synonyms: joiner, fitter, assembler|Antonyms: separator, divider|noun


dovetail:/ˈdʌvˌteɪl/| n. v.|鸠尾榫，吻合，使…吻合|A type of joint used in woodworking where two pieces of wood interlock. To fit together neatly or smoothly.|The carpenter used a dovetail joint to connect the two pieces of wood.  The schedule dovetailed perfectly with my plans.|Synonyms: interlock, mesh, coincide, harmonize|Antonyms: clash, conflict, diverge|noun, verb
