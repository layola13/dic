megasthenic:/ˌmɛɡəsˈθɛnɪk/
adjective
非常强壮的；力大无比的
possessing unusual strength
Hercules was known for his megasthenic feats.  赫拉克勒斯以其力大无比的功绩而闻名。
近义词: powerful, mighty, Herculean, strong, muscular
反义词: weak, feeble, frail
词性: adjective
词源: From Greek *megasthenes* "of great strength," from *megas* "great" + *sthenos* "strength."
