hemipode:/ˈhɛmɪˌpoʊd/| n.|三趾鹑|A small, ground-dwelling bird of the family Turnicidae, found in warm regions of the Old World.|The hemipode scurried through the undergrowth. 三趾鹑匆匆穿过灌木丛。|近义词：buttonquail, bustard-quail|反义词:|名词

词源:
From French *hémipode*, from Greek *hēmipodos* "having half a foot," from *hēmi-* "half" + *pous* (genitive *podos*) "foot," referring to the apparent three-toed appearance (though they actually have four).
