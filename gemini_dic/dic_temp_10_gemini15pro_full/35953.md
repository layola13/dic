code-names:/ˈkoʊdˌneɪmz/ | n. | 代号；代码名称 |A name given to a person, place, or thing, intended to be secret or used for a particular purpose.  It comes from "code" meaning a system of symbols and "names" meaning the word or words by which a person, place, or thing is known.| The spies used code-names to protect their identities.  间谍使用代号来保护他们的身份。| synonyms: aliases, pseudonyms, codenames | antonyms: real names, true names | noun
