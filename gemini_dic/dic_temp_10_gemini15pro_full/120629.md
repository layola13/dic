misconnecting:/ˌmɪskəˈnɛktɪŋ/
adjective
未连接的，连接错误的
failing to connect properly or as expected.
The misconnecting flight caused me to miss my meeting.  未连接的航班导致我错过了会议。
The cables were misconnecting, causing the internet to go down.  电缆连接错误，导致互联网中断。
近义词: malfunctioning, failing, faulty
反义词: connecting, working, functional
词性: adjective

misconnect:/ˌmɪskəˈnɛkt/
verb
连接错误，接错
to connect wrongly or fail to connect.
I misconnected the wires and blew a fuse. 我把电线接错了，烧断了保险丝。
The phone company misconnected our line. 电话公司把我们的线路接错了。
近义词: misjoin, mislink
反义词: connect, join, link
词性: verb
