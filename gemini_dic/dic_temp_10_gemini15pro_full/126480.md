mythologue:/mɪˈθɒlədʒaɪz/
n.
神话化；将…编成神话
To interpret (something) in terms of, or as if in terms of, myth; to give a mythical interpretation to.  To recount in the form of a myth.
He mythologized his childhood experiences in his novel. 他在小说中将他的童年经历神话化了。
mythologizer, romanticize
demystify, demythologize
noun
