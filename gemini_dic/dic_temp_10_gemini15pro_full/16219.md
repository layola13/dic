banns:/bænz/
noun
结婚预告
a public announcement of a proposed marriage, made in church three Sundays in a row.
The banns of marriage were read in church for three successive Sundays.  教堂连续三个星期天宣读了他们的结婚预告。
近义词：announcement, proclamation
反义词：None
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *banes (plural), from Frankish *ban "proclamation, order, command." Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *bannaną "to summon, proclaim."
