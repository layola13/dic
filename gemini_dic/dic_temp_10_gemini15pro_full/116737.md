maumet:/ˈmɔːmɪt/| n.|偶像，神像（贬义，指异教神的偶像），傀儡|An idol; specifically : a false god; also : an image or representation of a deity used as an object of worship. A puppet or doll.|例句：The crusaders destroyed the maumets of the infidels. 十字军摧毁了异教徒的偶像。

例句：The king was a mere maumet in the hands of his advisors. 国王只是他顾问手中的傀儡。|近义词：idol, fetish, puppet, doll|反义词: god, deity|词性: noun
