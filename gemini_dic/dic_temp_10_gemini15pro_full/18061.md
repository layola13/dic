beguile:/bɪˈɡaɪl/
verb
欺骗；哄骗；使着迷
To charm or enchant (someone), sometimes in a deceptive way.
She was beguiled by his smooth talk. 她被他花言巧语所迷惑。
charm, enchant, captivate, entice
repel, disgust, offend
v.

beguile:/bɪˈɡaɪl/
verb
消磨(时间)
To help (time) pass pleasantly.
The long hours were beguiled with stories and songs. 漫长的时光在故事和歌声中消磨掉了。
while away, pass, spend
rush, hurry
v.
