guilts:/ɡɪlts/| n. |罪恶感，内疚|A feeling of having done wrong or failed in an obligation.|She was overwhelmed by guilts after lying to her parents. 她对父母撒谎后，充满了罪恶感。|近义词：remorse, shame, regret|反义词：innocence, pride|noun

guilt:/ɡɪlt/| n. |罪行，内疚|The fact of having committed a specified or implied offense or crime.|He admitted his guilt in the robbery. 他承认自己在抢劫案中有罪。|近义词：culpability, responsibility|反义词：innocence|noun

guilty:/ˈɡɪlti/| adj. |有罪的，内疚的|Culpable of or responsible for a specified wrongdoing.|He pleaded guilty to the charge of theft. 他承认盗窃罪。|近义词：culpable, responsible|反义词：innocent|adjective
