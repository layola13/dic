misappraised:/ˌmɪsəˈpreɪzd/
verb
估价过低，估价过高，评价错误
having judged the value or quality of something incorrectly.
The house was misappraised by the real estate agent.  这栋房子的估价被房地产经纪人估错了。
The company's potential was misappraised by investors.  这家公司的潜力被投资者错误评估了。
synonyms: undervalued, overvalued, misjudged, misinterpreted, misevaluated
antonyms: accurately assessed, correctly valued, properly evaluated
