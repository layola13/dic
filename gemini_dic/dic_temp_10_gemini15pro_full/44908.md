cryosat:/ˈkraɪoʊsæt/ | n. | 冰星 (卫星) | A satellite designed to monitor Earth's polar ice caps and sea ice. |  例句：CryoSat-2 provides crucial data on changes in polar ice thickness. 冰星二号提供了关于极地冰层厚度变化的关键数据。

例句：The CryoSat mission is vital for understanding the effects of climate change. 冰星计划对于理解气候变化的影响至关重要。 | 近义词：ice-monitoring satellite | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


词源: cryo- (from Greek kryos, meaning "frost" or "ice") + sat (short for "satellite")
