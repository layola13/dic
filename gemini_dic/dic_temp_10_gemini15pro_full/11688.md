armoured:/ˈɑːməd/ (US) /ˈɑːməd/ (UK)| adj.|装甲的，披甲的|Equipped with protective metal covering.|The soldiers rode in an armoured vehicle. 士兵们乘坐装甲车。|The knight wore armoured gloves. 骑士戴着铁手套。|近义词：protected, shielded, fortified|反义词：unarmored, unprotected, vulnerable|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "armer" (to arm), from Latin "armare" (to arm), from "arma" (weapons). The suffix "-ed" indicates the past participle form used as an adjective.
