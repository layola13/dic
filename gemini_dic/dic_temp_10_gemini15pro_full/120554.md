mirage:/məˈrɑːʒ/ /mɪˈrɑːʒ/| n. |海市蜃楼，幻景，妄想|An optical illusion caused by atmospheric conditions, especially the appearance of a sheet of water in a desert or on a hot road caused by the refraction of light from the sky by heated air.  Something that appears real or possible but is not in fact so.|  例句：The oasis in the desert turned out to be a mirage. 沙漠中的绿洲原来是海市蜃楼。

例句：His hopes of winning the lottery were just a mirage. 他想中彩票的希望只不过是妄想。|近义词：illusion, delusion, fantasy, phantom|反义词：reality|词性：名词
