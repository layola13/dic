acrylamide: /əˈkrɪləmaɪd/
n.
丙烯酰胺
a colorless crystalline solid amide used in the synthesis of polymers
French acrylamide, from acryl- + amide
Acrylamide is formed when starchy foods are cooked at high temperatures.  丙烯酰胺是在淀粉类食物高温烹饪时形成的。

Acrylamide is a chemical used in many industrial processes. 丙烯酰胺是一种用于许多工业过程的化学物质。

近义词: 2-propenamide (this is more of a chemical name/synonym)
反义词: None (chemical compounds generally don't have antonyms)
词性: noun
