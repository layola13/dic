doubloons:/dʌˈbluːnz/
n.
双倍金币（旧时西班牙金币）;
A gold coin of Spain, equal to two escudos, current especially in the 17th and 18th centuries.
The pirates buried their chest of doubloons on the deserted island.  海盗们把他们装满双倍金币的箱子埋在了荒岛上。

gold coins, treasure
