bergamot:/ˈbɜːrɡəmɒt/ (UK), /ˈbɜːrɡəˌmɑːt/ (US)| n. |佛手柑，香柠檬|A small citrus tree (Citrus bergamia) with fragrant leaves and pear-shaped fruit, or the fruit itself.  The rind of the fruit yields an essential oil used in perfumery.|The Earl Grey tea is flavored with bergamot.  伯爵茶是用佛手柑调味的。|Synonyms: bergamot orange|Antonyms: N/A|noun
