cushiony:/ˈkʊʃəni/ /ˈkʊʃni/| adj.|缓冲的，减少冲击的|Serving to cushion or lessen the impact of something.|The cushiony soles of the shoes absorbed the shock of each step. 鞋底柔软有弹性，吸收了每一步的冲击力。|Synonyms: shock-absorbing, resilient, protective|Antonyms: jarring, impactful|adjective
