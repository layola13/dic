overassist:/ˌōvərəˈsɪst/ | v. | 过度协助，过分帮助 | To provide excessive assistance, often to the point of hindering independence or development. | The coach overassisted the young players, preventing them from learning to make decisions on their own. 教练过度协助年轻球员，阻止他们学习自己做决定。

The helicopter parent overassisted her child with homework, completing assignments for him instead of guiding him.  这位直升机父母过度帮助她的孩子做作业，代替他完成作业而不是指导他。 | 近义词: coddle, pamper, overprotect | 反义词: neglect, ignore | 词性: 动词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "assist" (verb meaning "to help")
