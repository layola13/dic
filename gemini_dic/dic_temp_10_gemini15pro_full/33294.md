chubbed:/ˈtʃʌbd/| adj.|圆胖的，丰满的|Plump and rounded, typically in a childishly appealing way.  Referring to someone, especially a child, who is slightly fat in a pleasant-looking way.|The baby had cute, chubbed cheeks.  这个婴儿有着可爱的，胖乎乎的脸蛋。|近义词：plump, chubby, portly, rotund|反义词:thin, slender, lean|形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From *chub*, meaning a small, thick-bodied fish, extended to describe a person with a similar build, especially a child.  Ultimately of uncertain origin.
