maoisms: /ˈmaʊɪzəmz/ | n. | 毛泽东思想 | The doctrines of Mao Zedong, often regarded as a variant of Marxism-Leninism.  It emphasizes the importance of the peasantry, of small-scale industry, and of permanent revolution. |  His political speeches were filled with maoisms. 他的政治演讲充满了毛泽东思想。| synonyms: Mao Zedong Thought, Maoism | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider opposing ideologies like Capitalism, Liberalism) | noun
