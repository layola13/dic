escudo:/ɪˈskuːdoʊ/ (US), /ɛˈskuːdəʊ/ (UK)| n. |埃斯库多（葡萄牙货币单位）; 盾牌; 保护; 盾形纹章|A former monetary unit of Portugal and its colonies, replaced by the euro. Also, a shield or protection, or a coat of arms.|例句：The price was 200 escudos. 价格是200埃斯库多。

例句：The family crest was displayed on an escudo. 家族徽章展示在一个盾牌上。|近义词：shield, buckler, protection, currency, monetary unit|反义词: vulnerability|词性:noun
