gralloch: /ˈɡræləx/ | n. | 剖腹；取出内脏（尤指猎获的鹿） | The act of disemboweling a deer or other game animal, especially after it has been hunted. |  The hunter gralloched the stag quickly and efficiently. 猎人快速有效地剖开了雄鹿的肚子。

 The gralloch must be done carefully to avoid puncturing the intestines. 剖腹必须小心进行，以免刺破肠子。| 近义词: disembowel, eviscerate | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially "preserve" in a culinary context) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Scottish Gaelic <i>grallach</i>, meaning "offal," "entrails."
