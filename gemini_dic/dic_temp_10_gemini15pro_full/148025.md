overroasts:/ˌoʊvərˈroʊsts/| v. |烤过头，烤得太久|To roast (food) for too long, resulting in dryness or burning.|She overroasted the chicken and it was dry. 她把鸡肉烤过头了，烤干了。|The chef overroasted the vegetables, making them bitter.  厨师把蔬菜烤过头了，烤苦了。|近义词：burn, scorch, overcook|反义词：undercook|词性：动词
