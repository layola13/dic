parr:/pɑːr/| n.|幼鲑；小鲑鱼|A young salmon or trout between the stages of fry and smolt, marked with dark vertical bars on the sides.|例句：The parr were abundant in the shallows of the stream. 小鲑鱼在溪流的浅滩处大量存在。

例句：The fisherman carefully released the small parr back into the river. 渔夫小心地将小鲑鱼放回河里。|近义词：fingerling, fry, juvenile|反义词:adult, mature|词性:名词

Etymology: From Middle English *par*, of uncertain origin, perhaps related to Welsh *barfog* "bearded," referring to the markings on the fish.
