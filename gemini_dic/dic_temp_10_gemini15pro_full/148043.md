overruler:/ˌoʊvərˈrulər/
n.
统治者; 支配者; 裁决者
One who overrules; one who has overriding authority or power.
The overruler made the final decision. 裁决者做出了最终决定。
The king, as overruler of the land, decreed a new tax. 国王作为这片土地的统治者，颁布了一项新的税收法令。
synonyms: ruler, governor, sovereign, master, lord, commander, chief, director, head, boss, superintendent, supervisor
antonyms: subject, subordinate, follower
noun

overrule:/ˌoʊvərˈruːl/
vt.
否决；驳回；宣布…无效；统治；支配
To set aside by virtue of a higher authority. To reject or disallow by exercising one's superior authority. To prevail over.
The judge overruled the objection. 法官驳回了异议。
His parents overruled his decision to leave school. 他的父母否决了他辍学的决定。
synonyms: override, reject, disallow, dismiss, deny, veto, cancel, reverse, repeal, invalidate, nullify, quash, void, annul, abrogate, countermand, revoke
antonyms: approve, allow, permit, uphold, sustain
verb
