deregulation: /ˌdiːˌreɡjəˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 放松管制，撤销管制 | The removal or reduction of government regulations in a particular industry. | The deregulation of the airline industry led to increased competition and lower fares. 航空业的放松管制导致竞争加剧和票价降低。| Example: The deregulation of the banking sector has had mixed results. 银行业的放松管制产生了复杂的结果。| Synonyms: liberalization, freeing, unleashing | Antonyms: regulation, control, restriction | noun

deregulate: /ˌdiːˈreɡjəleɪt/ | v. | 放松管制，撤销管制 | To remove or reduce government regulations. | The government plans to deregulate the telecommunications industry. 政府计划放宽对电信行业的管制。 | Example:  Many industries were deregulated in the 1980s. 许多产业在20世纪80年代被放松管制。 | Synonyms: liberalize, free, open up | Antonyms: regulate, control, restrict | verb
