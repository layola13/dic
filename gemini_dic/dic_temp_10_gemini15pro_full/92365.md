hypochondriacal:/ˌhaɪpəʊkɒnˈdraɪəkl/ (US) /ˌhaɪpəʊkɑːnˈdraɪəkl/| adj.|疑病的，患疑病症的|Excessively anxious about one's health, often imagining or exaggerating illness.|例句：She's always been a bit hypochondriacal, rushing to the doctor for every little ache and pain. 她一直有点疑病症，每次有点小疼小痛就去看医生。|例句：His hypochondriacal tendencies led him to believe he had a serious illness, despite the doctor's reassurances. 他的疑病症倾向让他相信自己得了重病，尽管医生再三保证他没事。|近义词：neurotic, anxious, obsessive|反义词：healthy, well, relaxed|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Late Latin *hypochondriacus*, from Greek *hupok hondrion*  "under the cartilage (of the breastbone)," the area anciently believed to be the seat of melancholy and hypochondria.  *Hupo* means "under" and *chondros* means "cartilage."
