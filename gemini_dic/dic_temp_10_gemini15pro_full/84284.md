half-tone's:/ˌhɑːfˈtoʊnz/ | n. | 半色调 |  A reprographic technique that simulates continuous tone imagery through the use of dots, varying either in size or in spacing, thus generating a gradient-like effect.  This is commonly used in printing, especially for photographs. | The newspaper printed the photograph using half-tones. 这家报纸用半色调印刷了这张照片。| synonyms: halftone screen, mezzotint | antonyms: continuous tone | noun


half-tone:/ˈhɑːfˌtoʊn/ | adj. | 半色调的 |  Relating to or using a half-tone process. | The image had a distinctive half-tone pattern. 这张图片有明显的半色调图案。 | synonyms: screened | antonyms: continuous tone | adjective
