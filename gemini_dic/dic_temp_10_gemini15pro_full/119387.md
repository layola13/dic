microdont:/ˈmaɪkrəʊdɒnt/ (UK) /ˈmaɪkroʊdɑːnt/ (US)| adj. n.|小齿的，小齿动物|Having small teeth; an animal with small teeth. |例句：The microdont shrew has surprisingly small teeth for its size.  这种小齿鼩鼱的牙齿就其体型而言出奇的小。

例句：Microdont fossils are difficult to identify due to their delicate structure. 小齿动物化石由于其精巧的结构难以辨认。|近义词：small-toothed|反义词：macrodont (large-toothed)|词性：形容词，名词
