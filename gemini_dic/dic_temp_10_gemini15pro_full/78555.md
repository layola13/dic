ghostwriters:/ˈɡoʊstˌraɪtərz/ (US), /ˈɡəʊstˌraɪtəz/ (UK)| n. |代笔作家，捉刀人|A person who writes for someone else, who then takes credit for the work.|Many celebrities use ghostwriters for their autobiographies. 许多名人都用代笔作家写自传。|Synonyms: amanuensis, coauthor (sometimes), collaborator (sometimes)|Antonyms: author, writer|noun
