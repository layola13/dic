confectioner:/kənˈfɛkʃənər/| n.|糖果店老板；糖果制造商；糕点师|A person who makes or sells sweets or confectionery.|例句：The confectioner created a magnificent wedding cake.  糖果制造商制作了一个华丽的结婚蛋糕。

例句：We bought a box of chocolates from the local confectioner. 我们从当地的糖果店老板那里买了一盒巧克力。|近义词：candy maker, pastry chef, baker|反义词:|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *confeccioner, from Anglo-Norman confectioner, confitureor, and Old French confectionaire, confiturier, from Late Latin confectionarius (“one who makes preserves”), from Latin confectio (“a preparing, compounding, making”), from conficere (“to make ready, prepare, complete, finish”), from com- (“together, completely”) + facere (“to do, make”).
