carry-out:/ˈkæriˌaʊt/ (US), /ˈkæriˌaʊt/ (UK)| n. adj.|外卖，外带; 外卖的，外带的|Food that is prepared in a restaurant or shop and taken elsewhere to be eaten. Can also be used as an adjective describing this kind of food.|例句：We ordered Chinese carry-out for dinner. 我们点了中式外卖当晚餐。|近义词：takeaway, takeout, to-go|反义词：dine-in, eat-in|词性:名词，形容词
carry-out:/ˈkæriˌaʊt/ (US), /ˈkæriˌaʊt/ (UK)| n.|执行，实行|The process of completing or fulfilling something.|例句：The carry-out of the plan was flawless.  这个计划的执行完美无瑕。|近义词：execution, implementation, performance|反义词：neglect, failure|词性:名词
