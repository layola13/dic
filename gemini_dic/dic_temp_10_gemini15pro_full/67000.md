expiation:/ˌɛks.piˈeɪ.ʃən/ | n. |赎罪，补偿， atonement|The act of making amends or reparation for a wrong or injury; atonement.  The ceremony was an act of expiation for the sins of the community.  这个仪式是为了弥补社区的罪孽。|近义词：atonement, redemption, reparation, amends |反义词:sin, crime, offense|noun
