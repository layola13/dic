foodlessness:/ˈfuːdləsnəs/
n.
缺乏食物;食物匮乏
The prolonged drought led to widespread foodlessness in the region. 长期的干旱导致该地区普遍缺乏食物。
The foodlessness of the refugees was heartbreaking.  难民缺乏食物的状况令人心碎。
近义词: starvation, famine, hunger
反义词: abundance, plenty, sufficiency
词性: 名词

Etymology: food + -less + -ness
"food" comes from Old English "fōda," meaning "food, fodder." "-less" is a suffix meaning "without" or "lacking," from Old English "-lēas." "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
