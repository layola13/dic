monotreme:/ˈmɒnəˌtriːm/ (US) /ˈmɑːnəˌtriːm/| n.|单孔目动物|An egg-laying mammal, such as the echidna or platypus, of the order Monotremata, characterized by certain primitive features, especially the presence of a cloaca.|The platypus is a fascinating monotreme.  鸭嘴兽是一种迷人的单孔目动物。|
The echidna is another example of a monotreme.  针鼹是单孔目动物的另一个例子。|Synonym: prototherian|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but could be contrasted with marsupials and placental mammals)|Noun
