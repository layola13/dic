overflows:/ˌoʊvərˈfloʊz/
| n. | 溢出；泛滥；超限 | An excess or surplus that flows over.  The river overflows its banks every spring. |例句：The river overflows its banks every spring.  河水每年春天都会泛滥。

overflows:/ˌoʊvərˈfloʊz/
| v. |  (使)溢出；(使)泛滥；充满 | To flow over the brim or bounds; to exceed capacity. The bathtub overflowed and soaked the floor. |例句：The bathtub overflowed and soaked the floor. 浴缸溢水，把地板都弄湿了。


overflows:/ˌoʊvərˈfloʊz/
|词源| From Old English "oferflowan," from "ofer" (over) + "flowan" (to flow).|
overflows:/ˌoʊvərˈfloʊz/

overflows:/ˌoʊvərˈfloʊz/
|词性|  Noun (plural) or Verb (third person singular present) |
