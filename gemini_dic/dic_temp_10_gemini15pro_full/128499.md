newsreel: /ˈnjuːzriːl/ (US), /ˈnjuːzriːl/ (UK)| n. |新闻短片，新闻影片|A short film of news and current affairs, formerly shown in cinemas.| We watched old newsreels of World War II. 我们观看了二战时期的旧新闻短片。| 近义词：newsfilm, documentary footage| 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps fictional film)| 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "news" + "reel" (a spool or cylinder on which film, wire, thread, etc., is wound). The term emerged in the early 20th century with the development of cinema.
