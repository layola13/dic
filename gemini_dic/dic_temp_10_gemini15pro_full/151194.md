parchmenter:/ˈpɑːrtʃməntər/ |ˈpɑːrtʃməntər/| n. |羊皮纸工；羊皮纸制造商|A person who prepares or deals in parchment. |The parchmenter carefully stretched the animal skin to create a smooth writing surface. 羊皮纸工小心地拉伸动物皮，以创造一个光滑的书写表面。|Synonyms: scrivener, clerk, scribe|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider "digital document creator" in a modern context)|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *parchementier, from Old French parcheminter, from parchemin (parchment). Ultimately from the place name Pergamon in Asia Minor, where parchment was first made.
