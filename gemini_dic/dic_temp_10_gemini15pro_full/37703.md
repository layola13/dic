companionway:/kəmˈpænɪənweɪ/ /kəmˈpænɪənˌweɪ/
n.
舷梯; 通往甲板的楼梯; (尤指)通到船舱的扶梯
A stairway from one deck of a ship to another, especially one leading down from the upper deck.
The crew hurried up the companionway to the main deck.  船员们匆匆沿着舷梯登上主甲板。

The captain descended the companionway to his cabin. 船长沿着舷梯下到他的船舱。

近义词:stairway, staircase, steps, ladder
反义词:None (No single antonym exists for a specific type of stairway on a ship)
词性:Noun
