lifeboat:/ˈlaɪfˌboʊt/| n.|救生艇|A small boat kept on a ship for use in emergencies, especially if the ship sinks.|The crew and passengers escaped the sinking ship in lifeboats.|Synonyms: rescue boat, emergency boat|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "ship" in the context of evacuation, or "wreckage" in the context of survival)|noun
