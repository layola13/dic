no-dice:/ˌnoʊ ˈdaɪs/ | idiom | 不行；没门；拒绝 | An informal expression indicating failure, refusal, or rejection.  It originates from gambling, where "dice" are used, and "no dice" means an unsuccessful roll. | "I asked for a raise, but it was no dice." 我要求加薪，但被拒绝了。|Synonyms: no way, no go, not a chance, out of the question | Antonyms:  certainly, definitely, absolutely | interjection
