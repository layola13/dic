nonflatulency:/ˌnɒnˌflætʃʊlənsi/
n.
无胀气；没有肠胃气胀
The state of not experiencing excessive gas in the digestive tract.
After taking the medication, she experienced a welcome period of nonflatulency.  服用药物后，她经历了一段令人欣慰的无胀气时期。
Near synonyms: absence of flatulence, lack of gas
Antonyms: flatulence, gassiness
noun
