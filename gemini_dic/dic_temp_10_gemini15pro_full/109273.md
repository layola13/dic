lemniscus:/lɛmˈnɪskəs/
n.
束，带，(神经解剖学)丘系
A ribbon-like structure, especially a band of nerve fibers in the brain.
The medial lemniscus is a pathway in the brainstem that carries sensory information to the thalamus. 内侧丘系是脑干中的一条通路，将感觉信息传递到丘脑。
The lateral lemniscus carries auditory information. 外侧丘系传递听觉信息。
Synonyms: tract, fasciculus
Antonyms: N/A
