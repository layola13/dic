amalgam:/əˈmælɡəm/ | n. |混合物，汞合金|A mixture or blend; an alloy of mercury with another metal. |例句：The sculptor used an amalgam of different metals to create the statue. 雕塑家使用不同金属的混合物来创作雕像。

例句：Dental amalgams are used to fill cavities. 牙科汞合金用于填充蛀牙。|近义词：mixture, blend, compound, alloy |反义词：element, component |词性: noun

amalgamate:/əˈmælɡəˌmeɪt/ | v. |合并，混合|To combine or unite to form one organization or structure; to blend or mix together. |例句：The two companies amalgamated to form a larger corporation.  两家公司合并成一个更大的公司。

例句：The artist amalgamated various styles in his paintings. 这位艺术家在他的绘画中融合了各种风格。|近义词：merge, combine, unite, blend, consolidate|反义词：separate, divide, split |词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *amalgama, from Medieval Latin amalgama, from Arabic al-malgham, meaning "a soft mass."  Ultimately from Greek malagma "emollient, plaster."
