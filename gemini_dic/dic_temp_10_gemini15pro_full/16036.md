bandala:/bænˈdɑːlə/ | n. | 赃物; 抢劫; 掠夺; (尤指菲律宾的)强制劳动 |  Loot; robbery; plunder; (especially in the Philippines) forced labor.  | The bandits escaped with their bandala. 土匪带着他们的赃物逃跑了。 | Synonyms: loot, plunder, spoils, booty | Antonyms: restitution, return | Noun

bandala:/bænˈdɑːlə/ | n. | (尤指菲律宾的)强制劳动; 强迫征用 | (especially in the Philippines) forced labor; compulsory requisitioning |  The villagers were forced into bandala to build the road. 村民们被迫强制劳动修路。 | Synonyms: forced labor, corvée, impressment | Antonyms: voluntary labor | Noun
