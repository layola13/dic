cosmography:/kɒzˈmɒɡrəfi/ | n. |宇宙志，宇宙学|The study of the universe, especially its structure and origin.  A description of the universe, or of theories about the universe. |例句：Ancient cosmography often placed Earth at the center of the universe. 古代宇宙学常常把地球置于宇宙的中心。

例句：His book offered a detailed cosmography of the known world. 他的书提供了对已知世界的详细宇宙志。|近义词：cosmology, astronomy |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *cosmographia*, from Ancient Greek κοσμογραφία (kosmographía, “description of the world”), from κόσμος (kósmos, “world, universe; order, ornament”) + -γραφία (-graphía, from γράφω (gráphō, “I write”)).
