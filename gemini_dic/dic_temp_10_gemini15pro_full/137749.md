nonvariability:/ˌnɑːnˌværiəˈbɪləti/
n.|不变性，无变异性|The nonvariability of the results indicated the experiment's success.  结果的不变性表明实验成功。|The nonvariability of the star's brightness is remarkable.  这颗恒星亮度的恒定性非常显著。|近义词：constancy, stability, invariance|反义词：variability, fluctuation, change|词性:名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): not, absence of (from Latin)
variable (adjective): able to be changed (from Late Middle English, from Old French)
-ity (suffix): forms nouns indicating a state or quality (from Latin -itas)
