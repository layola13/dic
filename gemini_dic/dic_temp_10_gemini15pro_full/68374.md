falling-out:/ˈfɔːlɪŋ aʊt/
n.
争吵，不和
a disagreement or quarrel between people, causing them to stop being friendly or to end a relationship
例句：They had a falling-out over money. 他们因为钱闹翻了。

例句：The two brothers had a bitter falling-out and haven't spoken in years.  两兄弟发生了激烈的争吵，多年来一直没有说话。

近义词：disagreement, quarrel, argument, dispute, feud, rift
反义词：reconciliation, agreement, harmony
