oscilloscope:/əˈsɪləskəʊp/ (UK), /əˈsɪləskoʊp/ (US)| n. |示波器|An electronic instrument that allows observation of constantly varying electrical signals as a two-dimensional plot of one or more signals as a function of time.|The engineer used an oscilloscope to analyze the circuit's waveform.|Synonyms: cathode-ray oscilloscope (CRO), scope|Antonyms: N/A|noun

oscillograph:/əˈsɪləɡrɑːf/ (UK), /əˈsɪləɡræf/ (US) | n. |示波器|A device for recording or displaying oscillations, especially those of an electric current.|The oscillograph recorded the fluctuations in voltage.|Synonyms: oscilloscope|Antonyms: N/A|noun
