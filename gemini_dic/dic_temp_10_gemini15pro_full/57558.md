dramatically:/drəˈmætɪkli/
adverb
戏剧性地；显著地；引人注目地
In a dramatic manner; to a very noticeable degree.
The landscape changed dramatically as we drove further west.  随着我们向西行驶，风景发生了巨大的变化。

Her health has improved dramatically since she started exercising. 自从她开始锻炼以来，她的健康状况有了显著改善。

近义词:significantly, considerably, markedly, substantially, noticeably, greatly, drastically
反义词:slightly, subtly, gradually, imperceptibly, mildly
词性:adverb
