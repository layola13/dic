morbidity:/mɔrˈbɪdɪti/ /mɔːrˈbɪdɪti/| n. |发病率，病态，死亡率|An abnormally gloomy or unhealthy preoccupation with death and disease; the relative incidence of a particular disease in a specific locality.|The morbidity rate increased significantly after the outbreak.  疫情爆发后，发病率显著上升。|Synonyms: prevalence, incidence, sickness, unhealthiness|Antonyms: health, wellness|noun

morbidity:/mɔrˈbɪdɪti/ /mɔːrˈbɪdɪti/| n. |病态，不健全|A state of being morbid or characterized by morbid thoughts or feelings.|His morbidity was evident in his fascination with gruesome details. 他对可怕细节的迷恋显露出了他的病态。|Synonyms: unhealthiness, sickliness, morbidity|Antonyms: healthiness, wholesomeness|noun
