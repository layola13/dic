liquefacient:/ˌlɪkwɪˈfeɪʃənt/
adjective
液化的；使液化的
Capable of liquefying or becoming liquid.
The liquefacient properties of certain gases allow them to be stored and transported efficiently. 某些气体的液化特性使它们能够被高效地储存和运输。
近义词：liquefying, dissolving
反义词：solidifying, hardening
词性：adjective

liquefacient:/ˌlɪkwɪˈfeɪʃənt/
noun
液化剂
An agent that causes liquefaction.
The company developed a new liquefacient for natural gas. 该公司开发了一种新的天然气液化剂。
近义词：solvent, thinner
反义词：coagulant, thickener
词性：noun


词源：From Latin liquefaciens, present participle of liquefacere "to make liquid," from liquere "to be liquid" + facere "to make."
