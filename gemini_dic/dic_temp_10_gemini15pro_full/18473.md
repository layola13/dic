benchmarking: /ˈbɛntʃˌmɑːrkɪŋ/ /ˈben(t)ʃˌmärkiNG/ | n. | 基准测试，标杆管理 | The process of comparing one's business processes and performance metrics to industry bests or best practices from other companies.  Typically, benchmarking involves quantifying differences in performance and identifying the causes of those differences, with the ultimate objective of improving one's own performance. | Many companies use benchmarking to improve their customer service. 许多公司用基准测试来改进他们的客户服务。|  Benchmarking against competitors helps identify areas for improvement. 与竞争对手进行基准比较有助于发现需要改进的领域。 | synonyms: comparison, evaluation, measuring, assessment | antonyms:  N/A | noun

bench-marking: /ˈbɛntʃˌmɑːrkɪŋ/ /ˈben(t)ʃˌmärkiNG/ | n. | 基准，标杆 | A standard or point of reference against which things may be compared or assessed. |  The company's performance serves as a benchmark for the industry. 该公司的业绩是行业的标杆。 | The new software became the benchmark for the industry.  这款新软件成为了行业的标杆。 | synonyms: standard, yardstick, criterion, gauge | antonyms: N/A | noun
