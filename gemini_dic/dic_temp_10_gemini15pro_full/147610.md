overplease:/ˌoʊvərˈpliːz/| v. |使过分高兴，讨好过头|To please excessively; to cause someone to feel overly pleased or gratified, often to the point of insincerity or discomfort.|He overpleased his boss with constant flattery and gifts. 他不断地奉承和送礼，讨好过头了他的老板。|例句：She overpleased her guests with an excessive amount of food. 她用过多的食物让客人们感到过分高兴了。|近义词：fawn, indulge, pamper|反义词：displease, annoy|词性:动词
