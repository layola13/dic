compositionally:/ˌkɒmpəˈzɪʃənəli/ | adv. | 在组成上，成分上 |  Relating to the composition of something, especially concerning the elements or ingredients that make it up. | The material is compositionally similar to granite. 这种材料的成分与花岗岩相似。 | The two rocks are compositionally distinct. 这两块岩石的成分截然不同。 | 近义词: constitutively, inherently | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 副词

词源:  Derived from "composition" + "-al" + "-ly."  "Composition" comes from Late Middle English via Old French from Latin *compositionem, from componere 'put together'.
