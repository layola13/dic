half-convicted:/ˌhæf kənˈvɪktɪd/
adj.
半信半疑的，不太确定的
partially convinced; not fully persuaded; having lingering doubts.
He had a half-convicted look on his face when I told him the news.  当我告诉他这个消息时，他脸上露出半信半疑的表情。
Synonyms: uncertain, doubtful, hesitant, skeptical, dubious
Antonyms: convinced, certain, sure, positive
词性: 形容词
