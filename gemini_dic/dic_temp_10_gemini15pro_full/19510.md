biello:/biˈɛloʊ/
noun
白色,  亚麻布
White, linen cloth or linen.
The bride wore a beautiful biello dress.  新娘穿了一件漂亮的亚麻连衣裙。
近义词:white, linen, cloth
反义词:black, dark, colored
词性:n.

词源: From Italian *biello*, from Latin *vellus* (fleece, wool).  Related to "veil".
