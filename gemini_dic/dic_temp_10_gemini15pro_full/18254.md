词源：From Middle English *bileven, *bleven, from Old English belēfan (“to believe”), from Proto-Germanic *galauƀijaną (“to believe”).  Related to Old Saxon gilōbian, Old High German gilouben, Old Norse gjálfa (“to enchant”).  More distantly related to Latin libēre (“to please”).
