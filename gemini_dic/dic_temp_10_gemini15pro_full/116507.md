maternities:/məˈtɜːrnɪtiz/
n.
产科病房; 孕妇装; 产妇期
periods of pregnancy and childbirth, or the clothing worn during these periods.
The hospital has a new wing for maternities. 这家医院有一个新的产科病房。
近义词：pregnancies, gestations
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "infertility")
词性: noun (plural)
