conversancy:/kənˈvɜːrsənsi/ /kɑːnˈvɜːrsənsi/
|n.|熟悉，精通，熟练|Familiarity with a particular subject or field, typically gained through study or experience.|He demonstrated a conversancy with the latest technology. 他展现出对最新技术的熟悉。|近义词：familiarity, mastery, expertise, knowledge, proficiency|反义词：ignorance, unfamiliarity, inexperience|词性:名词

conversant:/kənˈvɜːrsənt/ /kɑːnˈvɜːrsənt/
|adj.|熟悉的，精通的，熟练的|Familiar with or knowledgeable about something.|She is conversant in several languages. 她精通几种语言。|近义词：familiar, acquainted, versed, knowledgeable, proficient|反义词：ignorant, unfamiliar, unacquainted|词性:形容词
