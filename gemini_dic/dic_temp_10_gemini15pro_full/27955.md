cantonize:/ˈkæntənaɪz/
verb
划分成行政区；使地方分权
To divide into cantons or districts; to establish a cantonal system of government.
Switzerland is cantonized into 26 cantons. 瑞士被划分为26个州。
The government decided to cantonize the region to give more autonomy to local communities. 政府决定将该地区划分成行政区，赋予地方社区更大的自治权。
Synonym: decentralize, federalize
Antonym: centralize, unify
词性: 动词
