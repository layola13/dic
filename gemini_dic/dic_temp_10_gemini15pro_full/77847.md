generously:/ˈdʒenərəsli/| adv.|慷慨地，大方地|In a generous manner; freely.|He generously donated to the charity. 他慷慨地向慈善机构捐款。|近义词：liberally, bountifully, charitably|反义词：stingily, meanly, selfishly|词性:副词
词源: From Late Latin *generōsus, from Latin generōsus (“of noble birth, magnanimous”), from genus (“birth, descent, race, stock, kind, sort, class”) + -ōsus.
