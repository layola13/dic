antes:/ˈæntiːz/| preposition, adverb|在...之前，以前|before, previously, earlier|例句：Dinner will be served at seven, but we can have drinks on the terrace antes. 晚餐七点钟供应，但我们可以在之前在露台上喝点饮料。|近义词：before, prior to, ahead of|反义词:after, subsequently|词性:介词，副词
Etymology: From Spanish "antes" (before), ultimately from Latin "ante" (before, in front of).
