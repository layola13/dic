This term is now considered archaic and offensive due to its historical association with colonialism and inaccurate representation of Islamic beliefs.  Prefer "Muslim" instead.|例句：The term "Muhammadan" is now considered outdated and offensive. “Muhammadan”这个词现在被认为是过时的和冒犯的。

例句：It's more respectful to refer to someone as a Muslim rather than a Muhammadan.  称某人为穆斯林比称其为Muhammadan更尊重。|近义词：Muslim, Islamic (for adjective usage)|反义词: Non-Muslim|词性: adjective, noun
