outwaited:/ˌaʊtˈweɪtɪd/
verb
等候得比…久; 比…更有耐心
To wait longer than; to be more patient than.
He outwaited his opponents and eventually won the negotiation. 他比他的对手更有耐心，最终赢得了谈判。
The cat outwaited the mouse, eventually catching it.  猫比老鼠更有耐心，最终抓住了它。
Synonyms: outlast, outstay
Antonyms: concede, give up
词性: 动词
