nominor:/ˈnɒmɪnɔːr/ (British English) /ˈnɑːmɪnɔːr/ (American English)| adj.| 提名的；被任命的；指定的 | Designated by nomination; appointed. | Example: He was the nominor for the prestigious award. 他是这个著名奖项的提名人。 | Example: The nominor's decision was final. 提名人的决定是最终决定。| Near synonyms: appointed, designated, nominated, chosen | Antonyms: elected, self-appointed | Adjective
