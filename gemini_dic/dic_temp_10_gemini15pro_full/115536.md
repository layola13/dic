maple: /ˈmeɪpəl/ | n. | 枫树; 枫木 | A tree with five-pointed leaves, grown in northern countries. Its leaves turn bright red or yellow in the autumn.  |  例句：The maple tree in my yard is ablaze with color in the fall.  我家院子里的枫树在秋天色彩鲜艳。

例句：The cabinet is made of solid maple.  这个橱柜是用实心枫木做的。| 近义词：sycamore (梧桐树, especially in UK),  | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a type of tree) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *mapultrēow, from mapul (maple) + trēow (tree).  The word "maple" itself is likely of Germanic origin.
