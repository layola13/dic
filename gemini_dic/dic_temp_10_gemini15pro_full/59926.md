edgebone:/ˈɛdʒˌboʊn/
n.
aitchbone, rump bone
The lower part of the pelvic bone of a quadruped, especially of a cow, sheep, or pig; the aitchbone.  Also more generally, the rump.
The butcher prepared the edgebone for the roast.  屠夫准备了烤肉用的臀骨。
Synonyms: aitchbone, rump bone, pelvic bone
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, could consider terms for opposing skeletal parts like "cranial bone")
