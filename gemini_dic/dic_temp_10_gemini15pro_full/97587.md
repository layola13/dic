inhabitant:/ɪnˈhæbɪtənt/ | n. |居民，居住者|A person or animal that lives in a particular place. |例句：The inhabitants of the island were friendly and welcoming. 岛上的居民友好而热情。

例句：The city has about 2 million inhabitants.  这座城市大约有两百万居民。|近义词：resident, dweller, occupant, denizen |反义词:emigrant, alien |词性:noun
