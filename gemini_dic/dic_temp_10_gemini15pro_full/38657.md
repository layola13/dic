concussant:/kənˈkʌsənt/
adjective
震荡的; 引起震荡的
causing concussion or characterized by concussion.
The concussant force of the explosion shattered nearby windows. 爆炸的震荡力震碎了附近的窗户。
近义词: jarring, shocking, stunning
反义词: soothing, calming
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *concussus*, past participle of concutere "to shake violently," from com- "together" + quatere "to shake."
