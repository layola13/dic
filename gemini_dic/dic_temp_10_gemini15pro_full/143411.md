outflanks:/ˌaʊtˈflæŋk/ | vt. | 包抄，迂回侧翼，智胜 | To go around the side of an enemy or competitor and attack them from the flank; to surpass someone in strategy or maneuvering. | The general outflanked the enemy army by sending troops through the mountains. 将军派军队穿过山脉，包抄了敌军。

The company outflanked its competitors by introducing a new product line. 这家公司通过推出新的产品线，智胜了竞争对手。


| 近义词: bypass, circumvent, surround, outmaneuver, surpass | 反义词: confront, face, meet head-on | 词性: verb
