hospitalizations:/ˌhɑːspɪtələˈzeɪʃənz/ /ˌhɒspɪtəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/
noun
住院人数，住院治疗
The act of admitting someone to a hospital for treatment.  Also refers to the number of people admitted to hospitals.
The recent flu outbreak has led to a surge in hospitalizations. 最近的流感爆发导致住院人数激增。
Hospitalizations due to COVID-19 are declining. 由于新冠肺炎，住院人数正在下降。
synonyms: admissions, inpatient treatments
antonyms: discharges, releases
n.
