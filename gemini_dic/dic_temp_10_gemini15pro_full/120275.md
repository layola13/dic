miniatures:/ˈmɪnɪtʃərz/ /ˈmɪnətʃʊrz/
n.|微型画；缩影；微型复制品；微型人像|A miniature is a very small painting, usually of a person.  微型画是一种非常小的绘画，通常是人物画。|She collects antique miniatures. 她收藏古董微型画。|synonyms: replica, model, reduction, microcosm|antonyms: enlargement, magnification|noun

miniatures:/ˈmɪnɪtʃərz/ /ˈmɪnətʃʊrz/
n.|微型物；微型模型|Miniatures are very small versions of objects. 微型物是物体的非常小的版本。|He built a miniature railway in his garden. 他在花园里建造了一个微型铁路。|synonyms: model, replica, toy, scale model|antonyms: giant, enormous|noun

miniatures:/ˈmɪnɪtʃərz/ /ˈmɪnətʃʊrz/
adj.|微型的；小型的|Miniature means very small. 微型的意思是非常小。|She bought a miniature camera. 她买了一台微型相机。|synonyms: tiny, small, diminutive, minute|antonyms: giant, huge, enormous, massive|adjective
