finback:/ˈfɪnbæk/| n.|长须鲸，脊背鲸|A baleen whale (Balaenoptera physalus) of cold and temperate seas, having a dark back and a white belly. It is second in size only to the blue whale.|例句：The finback whale is a magnificent creature of the ocean. 长须鲸是一种壮丽的海洋生物。

例句：Researchers are studying the migration patterns of finback whales. 研究人员正在研究长须鲸的迁徙模式。

|近义词：rorqual, fin whale|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
