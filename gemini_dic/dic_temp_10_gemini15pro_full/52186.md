diarrheal:/ˌdaɪəˈriːəl/ | adj. | 腹泻的；引起腹泻的 | relating to or characterized by diarrhea |  The doctor diagnosed the patient with a diarrheal illness. 医生诊断病人患有腹泻性疾病。|  The contaminated water caused a diarrheal outbreak. 受污染的水导致了腹泻的爆发。|Synonyms: loose, watery, runny | Antonyms: constipated, firm | adjective


diarrhea:/ˌdaɪəˈriːə/ | n. | 腹泻 | an illness in which the body's solid waste is more liquid than usual and is discharged from the bowels more often than usual | The baby had diarrhea after eating some spoiled food. 宝宝吃了变质的食物后腹泻了。|  He suffered from severe diarrhea during his trip. 他在旅途中患上了严重的腹泻。|Synonyms: the runs, the trots | Antonyms: constipation | noun
