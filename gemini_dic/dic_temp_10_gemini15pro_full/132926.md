nonelucidations:/ˌnɒnɪˌluːsɪˈdeɪʃənz/
| n. |无阐明；缺乏解释|The report was filled with data but offered no elucidations of the findings. 报告充满了数据，但没有对调查结果进行任何阐明。|Absence of explanations or clarifications, lack of elucidation.|The witness's nonelucidations frustrated the jury. 证人的不解释让陪审团很沮丧。|近义词:obscurity, ambiguity, vagueness|反义词:clarification, explanation, elucidation|词性:名词

词源:
non- (prefix):  Latin origin, meaning "not"
elucidation (noun):  From Latin elucidare "to make clear," from e- "out" + lucidus "clear, bright," related to lux "light."
-s (suffix):  Indicates plural form.
