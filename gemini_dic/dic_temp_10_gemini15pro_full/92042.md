hyperphalangism:/ˌhaɪpərˈfæləndʒɪzəm/
noun
多指（趾）畸形
A congenital anomaly characterized by the presence of extra phalanges in the fingers or toes.
Hyperphalangism is a common finding in several syndromes. 多指畸形是几种综合征的常见发现。
Polydactyly, supernumerary digits
Oligodactyly
noun


hyperphalangy:/ˌhaɪpərˈfæləndʒi/
noun
多指（趾）畸形
The condition of having more than the normal number of phalanges in a finger or toe.
Hyperphalangy can cause difficulties with grasping objects. 多指畸形会导致抓握物体困难。
Polydactyly, supernumerary digits
Oligodactyly
