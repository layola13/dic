counterproductive:/ˌkaʊntərprəˈdʌktɪv/
adjective
适得其反的，事与愿违的
Having the opposite of the desired effect; hindering the achievement of a goal.
Working late into the night can be counterproductive if you're too tired the next day.  熬夜到很晚如果第二天太累的话可能会适得其反。

His stubborn attitude was counterproductive to finding a solution. 他固执的态度对找到解决方案适得其反。

近义词:ineffective, detrimental, adverse, unfavorable
反义词:productive, beneficial, helpful, constructive
词性:adjective
