erythroleic:/ɪˌrɪθroʊˈliːɪk/
adjective
赤油酸的; 红油酸的
Relating to or derived from erythroleic acid, an unsaturated fatty acid.
Erythroleic acid is found in some natural oils.  红油酸存在于一些天然油中。
This research focuses on the synthesis of erythroleic acid. 这项研究的重点是红油酸的合成。
Near Synonyms:  oleic, unsaturated
Antonyms: saturated
词性: adjective
