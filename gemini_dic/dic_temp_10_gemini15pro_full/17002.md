bat:/bæt/
noun|蝙蝠|A small, nocturnal, flying mammal with wings formed by a membrane stretched between its elongated fingers.|Bats are nocturnal creatures. 蝙蝠是夜行动物。|近义词:chiropteran|反义词:|n.

bat:/bæt/
noun|球棒|A specially shaped wooden club used for hitting the ball in baseball, cricket, etc.|He swung the bat and hit a home run. 他挥动球棒，打出了一个本垒打。|近义词:club, stick|反义词:|n.

bat:/bæt/
verb|击球|To hit a ball with a bat.|He batted the ball out of the park. 他把球击出了公园。|近义词:hit, strike|反义词:|v.
