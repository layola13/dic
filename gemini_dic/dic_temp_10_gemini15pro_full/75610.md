fugacity:/fjuːˈɡæsɪti/| n.|逸度，挥发性|The fugacity of a gas is a measure of its tendency to escape from a solution.|The fugacity of carbon dioxide in seawater is important for understanding ocean acidification. 海水中二氧化碳的逸度对于理解海洋酸化很重要。|Synonyms: volatility, escaping tendency|Antonyms: stability, inertness|noun
