disfigurements: /dɪsˈfɪɡjərmənts/ | n. | 毁容，缺陷，瑕疵 |  The act of disfiguring something or the state of being disfigured; a blemish or flaw that impairs appearance.  It usually refers to physical appearance, but can sometimes be used metaphorically. | The fire left him with terrible disfigurements.  这场大火给他留下了可怕的毁容。 | The statue suffered several disfigurements over the centuries.  几个世纪以来，这座雕像遭受了几处损坏。 |Synonyms: blemishes, flaws, imperfections, deformities, mutilations | Antonyms: perfections, enhancements, beautifications | noun
