beyond:/bɪˈjɒnd/ (UK), /biˈjɑːnd/ (US)| prep. adv. n. adj.| 超过，越过；在...的那一边；迟于；除...之外| extending or lying on the farther side of something; more distant than; later than; except; in addition to |例句：The town lies beyond the river. 小镇位于河的另一边。|近义词：past, further, over, after, outside, exceeding|反义词：within, inside, before|词性: 介词 (preposition), 副词 (adverb), 名词 (noun), 形容词 (adjective)

Etymology: From Old English *begeondan (attested only as begeondum, begeonde), from be- "by" + geond "yonder, there." Cognate with Old Frisian begeonda, Old Saxon bigundan, Middle Dutch beghenden.
