dezincify:/diːˈzɪŋkɪfaɪ/
|v.|
|去除锌|
|To remove zinc from something, typically an alloy.|
|The process is used to dezincify the brass.  这个过程用于去除黄铜中的锌。|

|The scrap metal merchant dezincified the brass fittings.  废金属商去除了黄铜配件中的锌。|
|近义词:extract zinc, remove zinc|
|反义词:galvanize (镀锌)|
|词性: verb (动词)|


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "remove") + zinc + -ify (suffix meaning "to make or cause to be").
