balefuller:/ˈbeɪlˌfʊlər/ | n. |打包机，捆包机|A machine that compresses and ties material (such as hay or cotton) into bales.|The balefuller efficiently packed the hay into tight bales. 打包机有效地将干草打包成紧密的捆包。|Synonyms: baler, bundler|Antonyms: unpacker, unbinder|noun
