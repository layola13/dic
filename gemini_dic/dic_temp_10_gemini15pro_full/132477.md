nonderogatorily:/ˌnɒndɪˈrɒɡətərɪli/
| adv. |无贬义地；不贬低地|Without expressing any criticism or disapproval; in a way that does not disparage. | He spoke nonderogatorily of his former colleagues, acknowledging their contributions despite their differences. 他没有贬低他的前同事，尽管存在分歧，他仍然认可他们的贡献。| 近义词:respectfully, neutrally, approvingly | 反义词:derogatorily, disparagingly, critically |词性:副词

nonderogatorily:/ˌnɑːndɪˈrɑːɡətɔːrɪli/
| adv. |无贬义地；不贬低地|Without expressing any criticism or disapproval; in a way that does not disparage. | She referred to her competitor's product nonderogatorily, focusing on objective comparisons rather than subjective criticisms.  她没有贬低竞争对手的产品，而是专注于客观比较，而不是主观批评。| 近义词:positively, favorably | 反义词:negatively, unfavorably|词性:副词
