bibliographize:/ˌbɪbliˈɒɡrəfaɪz/
|v.|把…编入目录；为…编制目录|To include in a bibliography; to make a bibliography of.|She bibliographized all the sources she used for her dissertation. 她把她论文中使用的所有资料来源都编入了目录。|
|The librarian bibliographized the rare collection of medieval manuscripts. 图书管理员为珍稀的中世纪手稿集编制了目录。|
|catalog, document, list, index|
