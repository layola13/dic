darwinianism:/dɑːrˈwɪniənɪzəm/ |n.| 达尔文主义；达尔文进化论 |The theory of the evolution of species by natural selection advanced by Charles Darwin.| Example: Darwinianism revolutionized the study of biology. 达尔文主义彻底改变了生物学的研究。 Example:  The impact of Darwinianism extends far beyond the realm of science. 达尔文主义的影响远远超出了科学领域。|近义词:evolutionism, natural selection|反义词:creationism, Lamarckism|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Charles Darwin (1809-1882), British naturalist + -ism.  Darwin published his theory of evolution by natural selection in *On the Origin of Species* in 1859.
