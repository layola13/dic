encumbrancer:/ɪnˈkʌmbrənsər/
n.
阻碍者，妨碍者
a person or thing that encumbers or hinders; someone or something that is a burden or impediment.
The heavy backpack was an encumbrancer to the hiker's progress.  这个沉重的背包是徒步旅行者前进的障碍。
The old regulations are an encumbrancer to innovation.  旧的规章制度阻碍了创新。
Synonym: hindrance, obstacle, impediment, burden
Antonym: aid, assistance, help, facilitator
Noun
