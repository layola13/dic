bacillus:/bəˈsɪləs/ | n. | 杆菌；细菌 | A rod-shaped bacterium. | 例句：Bacillus subtilis is a common soil bacterium.  枯草芽孢杆菌是一种常见的土壤细菌。

例句：The disease is caused by a bacillus. 这种疾病是由一种杆菌引起的。 | 近义词：bacteria, germ, microbe | 反义词: None (Bacillus is a specific type of bacteria; there's no direct opposite.) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From New Latin bacillus, diminutive of Latin baculus "staff, stick."  Referring to the rod-like shape of the bacteria.
