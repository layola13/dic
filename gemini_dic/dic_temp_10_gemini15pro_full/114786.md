maltreat:/ˌmælˈtriːt/ | vt. | 虐待；苛待 | To treat someone cruelly or badly. |  The animals were severely maltreated by their owner. 这些动物受到主人的严重虐待。

The child had been maltreated for years before the authorities intervened.  这个孩子在当局干预之前已经被虐待多年了。| 近义词：abuse, mistreat, ill-treat | 反义词: cherish, nurture, protect | 词性: 动词
