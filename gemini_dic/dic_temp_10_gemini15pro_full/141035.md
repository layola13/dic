onite:/ˈoʊnaɪt/| n. |鲕状岩，鲕状矿石|A sedimentary rock formed from ooids, small round grains coated with concentric layers of calcium carbonate or other minerals.  It can also refer to a mineral occurring in oolitic form.|The geologist examined the onite sample to determine its age.|Near synonyms: oolite (often used interchangeably), oolitic limestone|Antonym: (No direct antonym exists; perhaps "non-oolitic rock")|Noun
