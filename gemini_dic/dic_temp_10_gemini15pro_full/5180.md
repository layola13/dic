alley-cat:/ˈæli kæt/ | n. |流浪猫；野猫|A cat that lives in alleys or streets and doesn't have a home. |例句：The alley-cat scavenged for food in the dumpster.  那只流浪猫在垃圾箱里觅食。

例句：There are a lot of alley-cats in this neighborhood. 这个街区有很多流浪猫。|近义词：stray cat, feral cat|反义词: house cat, pet cat|词性: noun
