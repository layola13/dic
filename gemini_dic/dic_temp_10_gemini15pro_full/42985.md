covetous: /ˈkʌvɪtəs/ (US), /ˈkʌvɪtəs/ (UK)
adjective
贪婪的；垂涎的
Excessively desirous of wealth or possessions; greedy.
She gave him a covetous glance. 她贪婪地看了他一眼。
The covetous eyes of the thief lingered upon the diamond necklace. 小偷贪婪的目光停留在钻石项链上。
近义词: greedy, avaricious, grasping, acquisitive, envious
反义词: generous, charitable, altruistic, content
词性: adjective

covetousness: /ˈkʌvɪtəsnəs/ (US), /ˈkʌvɪtəsnəs/ (UK)
noun
贪婪；贪心
The state or quality of being covetous.
His covetousness led him to steal from his own family. 他的贪婪导致他偷了他自己家人的东西。
Covetousness is one of the seven deadly sins. 贪婪是七宗罪之一。
近义词: greed, avarice, cupidity
反义词: generosity, charity, contentment
词性: noun
