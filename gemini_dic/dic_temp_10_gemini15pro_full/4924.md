alioth:/ˈæliɒθ/ | n. | 天枢星（大熊座ε星） | Alioth is a star in the constellation Ursa Major, also known as the Great Bear. It is one of the seven bright stars that form the Big Dipper. | Alioth is the brightest star in the Big Dipper. 天枢星是北斗七星中最亮的星。|  Synonyms: Epsilon Ursae Majoris | Antonyms: N/A | noun
