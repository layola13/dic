orang-utan:/ɔːˈræŋʊtæn/ | n. | 猩猩 |A large ape with reddish-brown hair, long arms, and no tail, found in the forests of Borneo and Sumatra.|  The orangutan swung effortlessly through the trees.  猩猩在树间轻松地荡来荡去。|  The documentary showed a mother orangutan caring for her young. 这部纪录片展示了一只猩猩妈妈照顾幼崽的画面。 | 近义词:ape, primate | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

词源: From Malay *orang utan*, literally “person of the forest”
