daredevilism:/ˈdɛəˌdɛvɪlɪzəm/| n.| 蛮勇；冒险主义；鲁莽的行为| Reckless behavior; a tendency to take dangerous risks. | He was known for his daredevilism, often performing dangerous stunts. 他以其冒险精神而闻名，经常表演危险的特技。| Her daredevilism led her to climb Mount Everest without oxygen. 她的冒险精神促使她不带氧气攀登珠穆朗玛峰。| 近义词：recklessness, audacity, temerity | 反义词：caution, prudence, timidity | 词性: 名词

词源:  From daredevil + -ism.  "Daredevil" itself is a compound of "dare" and "devil", suggesting someone who would dare the devil.
