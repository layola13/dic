lecterns: /ˈlɛktərnz/
n.
讲台，诵经台
A stand with a sloping top, used to support a book or script, or to hold notes when someone is giving a speech or reading aloud.
The speaker approached the lectern and began her address. 演讲者走到讲台前开始讲话。
The priest read from the lectern.  牧师在诵经台上朗读。
近义词: podium, stand, rostrum
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "floor" in the context of public speaking)
词性: noun
