chromatosphere:/ˈkrəʊməˌsfɪər/ /ˈkroʊməˌsfɪr/| n.|色球|The chromosphere is a layer of the Sun's atmosphere above the photosphere and below the corona. 色球是太阳大气中位于光球层上方和日冕下方的一层。|例句：The chromosphere is visible during a total solar eclipse. 在日全食期间可以看到色球。|例句：Scientists study the chromosphere to learn more about the Sun's activity. 科学家研究色球以了解更多关于太阳活动的知识。|近义词：None (specific layer)|反义词：None (specific layer)|词性：Noun

chromatospheric:/ˌkrəʊməˈsfɪrɪk/ /ˌkroʊməˈsfɪrɪk/| adj.|色球的|Chromatospheric activity refers to the dynamic processes occurring within the Sun's chromosphere. 色球活动指的是发生在太阳色球层内的动态过程。|例句：Chromatospheric flares can release large amounts of energy. 色球耀斑可以释放大量的能量。|例句：Chromatospheric observations provide valuable insights into the Sun's magnetic field. 色球观测为了解太阳磁场提供了宝贵的见解。|近义词：solar (in a general sense)|反义词：None (specific layer)|词性：Adjective

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek χρῶμα (khrôma, “color”) + σφαῖρα (sphaîra, “sphere, ball”).
