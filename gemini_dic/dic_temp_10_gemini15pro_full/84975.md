happenstance:/ˈhæpənˌstæns/| n.|偶然事件，意外事件|An event that happens by chance, especially one with fortunate or unlucky consequences.  A chance occurrence or situation.|例句：By happenstance, I ran into an old friend at the airport. 我在机场偶然遇到了一个老朋友。

例句：It was sheer happenstance that they were both in Paris at the same time. 他们俩同时在巴黎完全是巧合。|近义词：coincidence, chance, accident, fortuity|反义词：intention, plan, design|词性:名词
