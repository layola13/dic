arboreous:/ɑːrˈbɔːriəs/ | adj. | 树木的；栖息在树上的 | Relating to trees; living in trees. |  The monkey is an arboreous animal. 猴子是一种树栖动物。|  The arboreous forest provided a habitat for numerous species of birds.  这片树木繁茂的森林为众多鸟类提供了栖息地。 | 近义词：arboreal, woody | 反义词： terrestrial, ground | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *arboreus, from arbor ("tree") + -eus (adjective suffix).
