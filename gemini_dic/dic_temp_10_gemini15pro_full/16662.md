barraging:/bəˈrɑːʒɪŋ/
| v. | 连续炮击，猛烈攻击 | bombarding intensively with something. | The enemy troops were barraging the city with artillery fire. 敌军正用炮火猛烈攻击这座城市。 |  The reporters barraged the president with questions. 记者们向总统连珠炮似的提出问题。 | 近义词：bombarding, shelling, peppering | 反义词: protecting, shielding | 动词
