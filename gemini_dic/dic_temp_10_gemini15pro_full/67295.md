exsiccator:/ˈɛksɪkeɪtər/ | n. |干燥器，保干器 | A container used for drying substances, especially in laboratories, containing a drying agent such as silica gel or calcium chloride. | The botanist stored the delicate specimens in an exsiccator.  植物学家将精细的标本储存在干燥器中。| Synonyms: desiccator | Antonyms: humidifier | noun

exsiccator:/ˈɛksɪkeɪtər/ | n. |腊叶标本 | A dried herbarium specimen. | The exsiccator was carefully labeled with the date and location of collection. 这份腊叶标本被仔细地标注了采集日期和地点。 | Synonyms: herbarium specimen | Antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *exsiccātōr*, agent noun from *exsiccāre*, "to dry thoroughly," from *ex-* ("out") + *siccāre* ("to dry"). Related to "desiccate."
