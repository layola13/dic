cromlech: /ˈkrɒmleχ/ (UK), /ˈkrɑːmleχ/ (US) | n. | 巨石阵，石圈 | A prehistoric monument consisting of a circle of standing stones, often supporting a large, flat horizontal capstone. |  例句：We visited a cromlech in Wales that was thousands of years old. 我们参观了威尔士的一个有数千年历史的巨石阵。

例句：The cromlech stood silhouetted against the setting sun. 巨石阵在落日的映衬下轮廓分明。

近义词：dolmen, henge | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
