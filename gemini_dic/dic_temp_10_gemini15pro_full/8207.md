anti-estrogen:/ˌæntiˈɛstrədʒən/ | n. adj. | 抗雌激素 | A substance that blocks or reduces the effects of estrogen. |  Example: The doctor prescribed an anti-estrogen medication. 医生开了抗雌激素药物。| Example:  Anti-estrogen therapy can be used to treat some types of breast cancer. 抗雌激素疗法可以用来治疗某些类型的乳腺癌。 | Synonyms: estrogen antagonist, estrogen blocker | Antonyms: estrogen, estrogen agonist | noun, adjective
