biconcave: /baɪˈkɒnkeɪv/ (UK) /baɪˈkɑːnkeɪv/ (US)| adj.|双凹的|Having two concave surfaces, especially describing the shape of red blood cells.|Red blood cells have a characteristic biconcave shape. 红细胞具有典型的双凹形状。|The lenses of some microscopes are biconcave. 一些显微镜的透镜是双凹的。|concave, depressed|convex|adjective


biconcavity: /ˌbaɪkɒnˈkævɪti/ (UK) /ˌbaɪkɑːnˈkævəti/ (US)| n.|双凹性|The state or quality of being biconcave.|The biconcavity of red blood cells allows them to efficiently carry oxygen. 红细胞的双凹性使它们能够有效地携带氧气。|The biconcavity of the lens affects its focal length.  透镜的双凹性会影响其焦距。|concavity, depression|convexity|noun
