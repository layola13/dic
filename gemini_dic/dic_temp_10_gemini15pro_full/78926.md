giraffe:/dʒəˈræf/ (US), /dʒɪˈrɑːf/ (UK)| n.|长颈鹿|A tall African mammal with a very long neck and legs, patterned with brown patches separated by lighter lines.|例句：The giraffe stretched its long neck to reach the leaves. 长颈鹿伸长脖子去够树叶。

例句：We saw a herd of giraffes on safari. 我们在旅行中看到了一群长颈鹿。|近义词：camelopard (archaic)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From the Arabic word *zarāfah* (زرافة), probably from a Somali word *geri*.
