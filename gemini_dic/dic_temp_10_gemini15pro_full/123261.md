monodic: /məˈnɒdɪk/ (UK) /məˈnɑːdɪk/ (US) | adj. | 單音的；單旋律的 | Consisting of a single melodic line without harmony or counterpoint.  Characterized by a single voice or melody.  |  The early Gregorian chants are examples of monodic music. 早期的格里高利圣咏是单音音乐的例子。 | The composer experimented with monodic textures in his latest work.  这位作曲家在他的最新作品中尝试了单音的结构。 | homophonic, unison | polyphonic | adjective
