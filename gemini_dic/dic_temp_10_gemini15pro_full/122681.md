modulus:/ˈmɒdʒʊləs/ (British) /ˈmɑːdʒələs/ (American)| n. |模数，系数，模量|The absolute value of a complex number. In mathematics, the modulus of a number is its absolute value or magnitude. In physics, it refers to a coefficient representing some elastic property of a substance.  |The modulus of elasticity is a measure of a material's stiffness. 弹性模量是衡量材料刚度的指标。The modulus of -5 is 5. -5的模数是5。|近义词：absolute value, magnitude, coefficient|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


moduli:/ˈmɒdʒʊlaɪ/ (British) /ˈmɑːdʒəˌlaɪ/ (American) | n. |模数（复数）|The plural form of modulus. |The moduli of these complex numbers are all greater than 1. 这些复数的模量都大于1。 Young's modulus and shear modulus are important properties of materials. 杨氏模量和剪切模量是材料的重要属性。|近义词：absolute values, magnitudes, coefficients|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *modulus*, a diminutive of *modus* "measure, manner, extent."
