hyetographic:/ˌhaɪɛtəˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. |雨量分布图的；与雨量分布图相关的|Relating to or represented by a hyetograph, which is a graphical representation of rainfall intensity over time.|The hyetographic analysis showed the peak rainfall occurred during the first hour of the storm. 雨量分布图分析显示，暴雨高峰出现在暴雨的第一个小时。|Synonyms: pluviographic, ombrographic|Antonyms: N/A|adjective

hyetograph:/ˈhaɪɛtəɡræf/ | n. |雨量分布图|A graph of rainfall intensity over time.|The hyetograph clearly showed the fluctuating rainfall during the monsoon season. 雨量图清楚地显示了季风季节降雨量的波动。|Synonyms: pluviograph, ombrograph|Antonyms: N/A|noun
