antiexpansionists:/ˌæntiɪkˈspænʃənɪsts/
n.|反扩张主义者|People who oppose expansion, especially territorial expansion.  Those who advocate against increasing a nation's influence or territory.|The antiexpansionists argued that acquiring new territories would lead to unnecessary wars. 反扩张主义者认为，获得新的领土会导致不必要的战争。|opponents of expansion, noninterventionists|imperialists, expansionists|noun
