heathen:/ˈhiːðən/ (US) /ˈhiːðən/ (UK)| n. adj.|异教徒，非基督教徒；没有宗教信仰的人；粗野的人|A person who does not belong to a widely held religion (especially one who is not a Christian, Jew, or Muslim) as regarded by those who do.  Can also be used as an adjective to describe something related to heathens or their practices.  Sometimes used informally to describe someone who is uncivilized or irreligious.|例句：The missionaries tried to convert the heathens.  传教士试图皈依异教徒。

例句：He called them a bunch of heathens because they didn't celebrate Christmas.  因为他没过圣诞节，他称他们为一群异教徒。|近义词：pagan, infidel, unbeliever, gentile|反义词：believer, Christian, religious|词性:noun, adjective
