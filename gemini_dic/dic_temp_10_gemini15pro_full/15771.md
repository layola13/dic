balefulest:/ˈbeɪlfʊlɪst/
adjective
最凶兆的；最恶毒的
Most ominous or foreboding; most menacing.
The balefulest glare he gave her made her shudder. 他那凶恶的一瞥让她不寒而栗。
Synonyms: ominous, menacing, sinister, malevolent, threatening
Antonyms: auspicious, benign, benevolent, propitious
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From bale (evil, woe) + -ful (full of) + -est (superlative suffix).  Ultimately from Old English *balu (evil, woe).
