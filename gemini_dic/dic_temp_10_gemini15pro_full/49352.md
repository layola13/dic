delinquencies:/dɪˈlɪŋkwənsiz/
|n.|拖欠，违约；失职；不良行为|An act of failing to do what is required; the state of being overdue in payment.  A failure in duty; a misdeed or offense.|The rising number of mortgage delinquencies is a cause for concern. 抵押贷款拖欠数量的上升令人担忧。|近义词：defaults, arrears, offenses, misconducts|反义词：payments, compliance, obedience|词性:名词(plural noun)


delinquency:/dɪˈlɪŋkwənsi/
|n.|拖欠，违约；失职；不良行为|An act of failing to do what is required; the state of being overdue in payment. A failure in duty; a misdeed or offense.|His delinquency in paying his rent led to his eviction. 他拖欠房租导致他被驱逐。|近义词：default, arrear, offense, misconduct|反义词：payment, compliance, obedience|词性:名词(singular noun)

词源:  Late 16th century: from late Latin delinquentia ‘offense,’ from delinquent- ‘failing in duty,’ from the verb delinquere, from de- ‘down, away’ + linquere ‘to leave.’
