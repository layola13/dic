excortication: /ɛksˌkɔːrtɪˈkeɪʃən/
noun
剥皮，去皮；严厉谴责
The act of removing the outer layer of something, especially skin or bark; harsh criticism or denunciation.
The excoriation of the patient's skin was caused by the allergic reaction. 病人的皮肤剥落是由过敏反应引起的。

The politician faced excoriation in the media for his controversial remarks.  这位政治家因其有争议的言论而面临媒体的严厉谴责。
近义词: abrasion, denudation, censure, condemnation
反义词: praise, commendation
