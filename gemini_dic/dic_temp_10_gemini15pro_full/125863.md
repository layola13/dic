muscatel:/ˌmʌskəˈtɛl/ | n. |麝香葡萄；麝香葡萄酒|A sweet, aromatic wine made from muscat grapes, or the grapes themselves.|例句：This muscatel has a rich, honeyed flavor.  这款麝香葡萄酒具有浓郁的蜂蜜味。

例句：The muscatel grapes are plump and juicy. 这些麝香葡萄饱满多汁。|近义词：muscat, Moscato|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps a dry wine like Sauvignon Blanc)|词性:noun
