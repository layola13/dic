dollarize:/ˈdɒləraɪz/ (US) /ˈdɒləraɪz/ (UK)| v. |美元化|To adopt the US dollar as the official currency of a country.|Panama dollarized its economy in 1904. 巴拿马在1904年实现了经济美元化。

The country decided to dollarize to stabilize its currency.  这个国家决定美元化以稳定其货币。|Synonyms: monetize, denominate|Antonyms: demonetize|verb
