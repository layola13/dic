bigot:/ˈbɪɡət/| n.|偏执者，顽固者；抱偏见的人|A person who is intolerant towards those holding different opinions.|He was known as a religious bigot, intolerant of those with different beliefs. 他被称为宗教偏执狂，不能容忍那些信仰不同的人。|Synonyms: fanatic, extremist, dogmatist, partisan|Antonyms: tolerant, open-minded|Noun
