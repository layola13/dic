diaphanousness:/ˌdaɪəˈfænəsnəs/ | n. | 透明；精致；清澈 | The quality of being diaphanous; transparency, delicacy, or sheerness. | The diaphanousness of the fabric made it perfect for summer.  这种面料的透明度使它非常适合夏天。| synonyms: transparency, translucency, clarity, fineness, delicacy, sheerness, ethereality | antonyms: opacity, thickness, density, coarseness | noun


diaphanous:/daɪˈæfənəs/ | adj. | 透明的；精致的；清澈的 | (especially of fabric) light, delicate, and translucent. |  Her diaphanous gown shimmered in the candlelight. 她的透明礼服在烛光下闪闪发光。| synonyms: transparent, translucent, sheer, fine, delicate, filmy, gossamer, ethereal | antonyms: opaque, thick, heavy, coarse | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek διαφανής (diaphanḗs)  meaning “transparent,” from διά (diá) meaning "through" + φαίνω (phaínō) meaning "to show, to shine".
