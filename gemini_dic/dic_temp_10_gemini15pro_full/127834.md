nemean:/ˈniːmiən/| adj.|尼米亚的；凶猛的；巨大的|Of or relating to Nemea, a valley in ancient Greece, or to the Nemean Games held there, especially the Nemean lion slain by Heracles as the first of his twelve labors.  Also, by extension, extremely fierce or powerful.|例句：The nemean lion was a fearsome beast. 尼米亚狮子是一头可怕的野兽。

例句：He faced a nemean task. 他面临着一项艰巨的任务。|近义词：fierce, powerful, formidable, monstrous|反义词:weak, gentle, small|词性:adjective
