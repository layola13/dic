knife-like:/ˈnaɪfˌlaɪk/
adjective
像刀一样的；锋利的
Resembling a knife; sharp.
The bird had a knife-like beak.  这只鸟有着像刀一样锋利的喙。
The pain was knife-like, stabbing through his chest. 疼痛像刀割一样，刺穿了他的胸膛。
sharp, keen, piercing, cutting
blunt, dull
adjective
