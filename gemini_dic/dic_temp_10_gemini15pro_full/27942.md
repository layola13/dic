cantle:/ˈkæntl/ | n. | (马鞍的)后桥; (圆形或其他物体的)一部分, 片; | A piece, especially of something round, that has been or seems to have been cut or broken off. |  The sun peeked over the cantle of the hill. 太阳从山顶露出了一角。 | Synonyms: piece, portion, segment, section, fragment | Antonyms: whole, entirety | noun

cantle:/ˈkæntl/ | v. | 切割, 分割; | To cut or divide into parts, especially a cantle. |  The butcher cantled the carcass into smaller portions. 屠夫把尸体分割成小块。 | Synonyms: divide, cut, sever, separate | Antonyms: join, unite, combine | verb
