agrostography:/əˌɡrɒsˈtɒɡrəfi/
n.
农作物地理学; 对草的描述
The scientific description of grasses.  It also refers to the branch of botany concerned with the systematic classification and description of grasses.
Agrostography plays a crucial role in understanding the distribution and characteristics of different grass species. 农作物地理学在理解不同草种的分布和特征方面起着至关重要的作用。
The botanist specialized in agrostography, meticulously documenting each species. 这位植物学家专门研究农作物地理学，一丝不苟地记录每一个物种。
