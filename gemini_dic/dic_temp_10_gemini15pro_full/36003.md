codgers:/ˈkɒdʒəz/ (British), /ˈkɑːdʒərz/ (American)| n. |古怪老头（通常指一群）, 老家伙|An informal, often humorous term for an elderly man, typically used in the plural to refer to a group of old men.|The group of codgers at the park spent their afternoons playing checkers.|近义词：old men, geezers, seniors, elderly men|反义词:youngsters, youths|Noun
