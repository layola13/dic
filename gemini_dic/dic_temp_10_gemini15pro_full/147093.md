overlie:/ˌoʊvərˈlaɪ/ |v. |覆盖；叠加；位于…之上|The map overlies a grid of squares.  地图叠加在一个方格网上。|Synonyms: covers, overlaps, superimposes |Antonyms: underlies, is beneath, is under |verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *overlien, from Old English oferliġan (“to lie over, cover”), from ofer (“over”) + liġan (“to lie”).  The word is related to the German überliegen (“to overlay”) and the Dutch overliggen (“to overlie”).
