horridest:/ˈhɒrɪdɪst/ (UK) /ˈhɔːrɪdɪst/ (US)| adj.|最可怕的，最令人讨厌的|Superlative form of horrid: most horrid.  It means extremely unpleasant, shocking, or repulsive.|The horridest smell emanated from the dumpster. 最可怕的气味从垃圾箱里散发出来。|The movie depicted the horridest scenes of violence I've ever seen. 这部电影描绘了我见过的最可怕的暴力场面。|近义词：ghastliest, most dreadful, most awful, most terrible|反义词：pleasantest, nicest, best, most delightful|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *horride*, from Old French *horride*, from Latin *horridus* ("rough, bristly, shaggy, prickly, frightful"), from *horrēre* ("to bristle, shudder").
