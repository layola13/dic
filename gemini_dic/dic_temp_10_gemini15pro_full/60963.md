elfishly:/ˈɛlfɪʃli/
adverb
自私地；小气地
In a selfish or self-centered manner; characteristic of an elf.
He laughed elfishly as he snatched the last cookie.  他自私地笑着抢走了最后一块饼干。
She elfishly hoarded all the toys for herself. 她自私地把所有玩具都据为己有。
近义词: selfishly, meanly, greedily, stingily
反义词: generously, altruistically, charitably
词性: adverb

词源:
From elf + -ish + -ly.  来自elf（精灵）+ -ish（形容词后缀，表示“…的”）+ -ly（副词后缀）。  最初指像精灵一样，后来引申为精灵常被描述为淘气、自私，因此elfishly也带有自私的含义。
