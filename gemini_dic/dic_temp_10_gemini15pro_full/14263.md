autogamous: /ɔːˈtɒɡəməs/ | adj. | 自花授粉的 | Self-fertilizing; capable of self-fertilization; pertaining to autogamy. |  Many species of plants are autogamous. 许多植物种类是自花授粉的。|  The autogamous flowers ensured seed production even in the absence of pollinators.  即使没有传粉者，自花授粉的花朵也能确保种子的产生。| 近义词: self-fertilizing, self-pollinating | 反义词: allogamous, cross-pollinating | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From auto- (self) + -gamous (marrying), from Greek gamos (marriage).
