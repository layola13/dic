dispiritment:/dɪˈspɪrɪtmənt/
n.
沮丧，灰心，丧气
A feeling of discouragement and loss of hope.
The team's dispiritment was palpable after their crushing defeat. 球队惨败后，沮丧的情绪显而易见。
The constant rain added to their dispiritment.  连绵不断的雨水加剧了他们的沮丧。
近义词：discouragement, dejection, despondency
反义词：encouragement, hope, optimism
词性: 名词

词源:  From dis- + spirit + -ment.  "Dis-" prefixes indicate negation or reversal. "Spirit" refers to morale or enthusiasm. "-ment" forms nouns indicating a state or result.
