messianic:/ˌmɛsiˈænɪk/| adj.|救世主般的，弥赛亚的|Relating to a messiah or to the idea of a messiah, a savior or liberator.  Characterized by hope or belief in a better future, often with a sense of religious or political fervor.|He had a messianic vision for the future of the company. 他对公司的未来抱有救世主般的愿景。|Synonyms: redemptive, salvific, utopian|Antonyms: pessimistic, cynical, despairing|adjective


messianic:/ˌmɛsiˈænɪk/| n.|救世主信徒|A believer in a messiah.|He considered himself a messianic, waiting for the promised leader. 他认为自己是一位救世主信徒，等待着承诺的领袖到来。|Synonyms: believer, follower, disciple|Antonyms: nonbeliever, skeptic, doubter|noun
