hematocrya:/ˌhiːməˈtɒkraɪə/ | n. | 冷血动物 |Cold-blooded animals; animals whose body temperature varies with the surrounding environment.|  Many reptiles, like snakes and lizards, are hematocrya. 许多爬行动物，例如蛇和蜥蜴，都是冷血动物。|  Reptiles are hematocrya and thus rely on external sources of heat for thermoregulation. 爬行动物是冷血动物，因此依靠外部热源来调节体温。 | Synonyms: poikilotherm | Antonyms: homeotherm | noun
