hearkener:/ˈhɑːrkənər/ (US), /ˈhɑːkənə(r)/ (UK)| n. |倾听者；听众|A person who listens attentively; a listener. |例句：The hearkener sat spellbound as the storyteller wove his tale. 倾听者被讲故事的人编织的故事迷住了。

例句：Few hearkeners remained by the end of the lengthy sermon.冗长的布道结束后，几乎没有听众留下来。|近义词：listener, auditor, audience, follower|反义词：speaker, talker, orator|词性:noun
