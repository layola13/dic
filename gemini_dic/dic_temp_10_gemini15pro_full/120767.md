misappoints:/ˌmɪsəˈpɔɪnts/ | v. | 使失望 | To fail to fulfill the hopes or expectations of (someone). | The movie disappointed me; it wasn't as good as the reviews said.  这部电影让我失望；它不如评论说的那么好。 | The team's performance disappointed their fans. 球队的表现让球迷们失望。 |  近义词:disillusions, frustrates, lets down | 反义词: pleases, delights, satisfies | verb
