agroforestries:/ˌæɡroʊˈfɔːrɪstriz/| n. |农林复合经营，农林复合系统|  The integration of trees and shrubs into crop and animal farming systems to create a more integrated and sustainable land use management system. |  Agroforestries are becoming increasingly popular as a way to improve soil health and biodiversity. 农林复合经营正日益成为一种改善土壤健康和生物多样性的方法。|  Synonyms: agroforestry systems, integrated farming systems | Antonyms: monoculture | Noun
