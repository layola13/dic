guidance:/ˈɡaɪdəns/| n.|指导，引导，咨询|The student sought guidance from her professor.  这个学生向她的教授寻求指导。|Synonyms:  direction, advice, counsel, support, leadership|Antonyms:  misguidance, neglect, indifference|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *guidaince, from guider “to guide” (from Frankish *witan, from Proto-Germanic *witanan “to know”), + -ance (suffix forming nouns of action or process).
