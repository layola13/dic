nonsensationalistic:/ˌnɒnsɛnˈseɪʃənəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
非耸人听闻的；不哗众取宠的
Avoiding exaggerated or shocking elements; factual and objective.
The journalist wrote a nonsensationalistic account of the event, focusing on the facts and avoiding hyperbole.  记者对该事件进行了非耸人听闻的报道，专注于事实，避免夸张。
The report provided a nonsensationalistic analysis of the complex political situation.  该报告对复杂的政治局势进行了非耸人听闻的分析。

近义词:objective, factual, unbiased, impartial, unemotional, dispassionate, sober, measured
反义词:sensationalistic, lurid, exaggerated, hyperbolic, dramatic, overblown, hyped-up
词性:adjective

词源:
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "sensationalistic" (derived from "sensation" + "-al" + "-istic")
