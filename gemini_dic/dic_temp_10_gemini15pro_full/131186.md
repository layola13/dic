nonbudding:/ˌnɒnˈbʌdɪŋ/
adjective
非芽殖的
Not reproducing or growing by budding.  A nonbudding yeast reproduces through fission, not by forming buds.
This strain of yeast is nonbudding and reproduces through fission. 这种酵母菌株是非芽殖的，通过裂殖繁殖。
Nonbudding organisms typically reproduce through other methods, like fission or fragmentation. 非芽殖生物通常通过其他方法繁殖，例如裂殖或断裂。
Near synonyms: non-germinating, non-sprouting
Antonyms: budding, germinating, sprouting
词性: adjective
