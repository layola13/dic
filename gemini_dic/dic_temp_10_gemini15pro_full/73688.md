forgivableness:/fərˈɡɪvəblnəs/
n.
可原谅性，宽恕
The forgivableness of a mistake depends on its severity.
一个错误的可原谅性取决于其严重程度。
His forgivableness surprised everyone.
他的宽容让每个人都感到惊讶。
Synonyms: pardonableness, remissible, excusable
Antonyms: unforgivableness, inexcusable
