meconium:/mɪˈkoʊniəm/ /məˈkəʊniəm/
n.
胎粪
A dark green fecal material that accumulates in the fetal intestines and is discharged at or near the time of birth.
The baby passed meconium shortly after birth.  婴儿出生后不久就排出了胎粪。

近义词:
stool, feces

反义词:
None (Meconium is a unique substance with no direct opposite.)

词性:
Noun
