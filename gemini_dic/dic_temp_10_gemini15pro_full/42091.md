cotransductions:/ˌkoʊˌtrænsˈdʌkʃənz/| n. |共转导|The simultaneous transduction of two or more genes from one bacterial cell to another by a single transducing particle.|Cotransduction frequencies can be used to map the relative positions of genes on a bacterial chromosome. 共转导频率可用于绘制细菌染色体上基因的相对位置。|Near synonyms: joint transduction, linked transduction|Antonym: (No direct antonym, but consider "independent transduction")|noun
