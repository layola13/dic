anatomize:/ˈænətəˌmaɪz/ | v. | 解剖，仔细分析 | To dissect or analyze something in detail, typically a text, an idea, or a situation. |  The professor anatomized the poem, exploring its imagery and symbolism. 教授仔细分析了这首诗，探讨了它的意象和象征意义。

The biologist anatomized the frog to study its internal organs.  生物学家解剖了青蛙来研究它的内部器官。 | 近义词: dissect, analyze, examine, scrutinize | 反义词: synthesize, combine, construct | 词性: 动词
