auget:/ˈɔːɡɛt/| n.|水槽，水管，导管|A channel, pipe, or conduit for conveying water.|The Roman aqueduct served as an auget, bringing fresh water to the city.|近义词：channel, conduit, pipe, duct|反义词: blockage|词性: 名词

词源: From Latin *auctus* (increased, enlarged), past participle of *augēre* (to increase). Related to augment.
