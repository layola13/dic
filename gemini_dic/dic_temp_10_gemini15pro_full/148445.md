overstore:/ˌoʊvərˈstɔːr/ /ˌəʊvəˈstɔː(r)/
verb|过度储存，过量供应|To supply with more than is needed or can be sold; to stock to excess.|The warehouse was overstored with unsold merchandise. 仓库里堆满了未售出的商品。|oversupply, glut, saturate|understock, deplete|v.
overstore:/ˈoʊvərˌstɔːr/ /ˈəʊvəˌstɔː(r)/
noun|过度储存，过量供应|An excessive supply of goods; a state of being overstocked.|The overstore of winter coats led to significant price reductions. 冬衣的过量库存导致价格大幅下调。|oversupply, glut, surplus|shortage, deficit|n.
