biracial:/ˌbaɪˈreɪʃəl/| adj.|双人种的，混血的|Of or relating to people whose parents are of different races.|例句：She identified as biracial, having a Black mother and a White father. 她认为自己是混血儿，因为她有一个黑人母亲和一个白人父亲。

例句：The biracial community faces unique challenges. 双人种群体面临着独特的挑战。|近义词：multiracial, mixed-race|反义词：monoracial|词性：形容词
