bladdery:/ˈblædəri/| adj.|膀胱状的；像膀胱的；薄而透明的|Resembling a bladder; thin and membranous.  Covered in or full of bladders.|The seaweed had a bladdery texture.  这种海藻的质地像膀胱一样。|近义词：membranous, vesicular, inflated|反义词: solid, thick|词性:形容词

Etymology: From "bladder" + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Bladder" comes from Old English "blædre," related to Old Norse "blaðra" and Dutch "blaar."
