necessitative:/nəˈsɛsɪˌteɪtɪv/
adjective
必要的；必需的；不可避免的
Of the nature of or implying necessity; expressing necessity.  Creating or imposing a necessity.
例句：The necessitative measures were taken to ensure public safety.  采取了必要的措施以确保公共安全。

例句：The current economic climate makes cost-cutting a necessitative measure. 当前的经济环境使得削减成本成为一项必要的措施。

近义词：necessary, essential, required, indispensable, obligatory, compulsory
反义词：unnecessary, optional, nonessential
词性：adjective
