glutinousnesses:/ˌɡluːtɪnəsnəsɪz/
|n.|粘性；胶粘性|The glutinousnesses of the rice made it perfect for sushi.  这种米饭的粘性使它非常适合做寿司。|The dough's glutinousnesses allowed it to be stretched thinly.  面团的粘性使它可以被拉得很薄。|近义词：stickiness, adhesiveness, viscosity|反义词：slipperiness, dryness|词性:名词

glutinous:/ˈɡluːtɪnəs/
|adj.|粘性的；胶状的|Glutinous rice is used in many Asian dishes.  糯米在很多亚洲菜肴中被使用。|The cake had a strangely glutinous texture.  这个蛋糕的口感出奇地粘稠。|近义词：sticky, gummy, adhesive|反义词：dry, non-sticky|词性:形容词

glutinous: From Late Latin *glūtinōsus, from Latin glūtin-, stem of glūten (“glue”) + -ōsus (suffix forming adjectives meaning ‘full of, abounding in’).
|词源|源自晚期拉丁语 *glūtinōsus，来自拉丁语 glūtin-（glūten “胶水”的词干）+ -ōsus（构成形容词的后缀，意为“充满……的，富含……的”）。
