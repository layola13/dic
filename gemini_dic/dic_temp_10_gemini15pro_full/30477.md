centripetency:/ˌsɛntrɪˈpiːtənsi/
n.|向心力，向心倾向|The tendency of a body to move or be directed toward a center.|The government's policies encouraged centripetency, drawing power and resources towards the capital.|近义词：centralization, concentration|反义词：centrifugality, decentralization|词性:noun
centripetal:/ˌsɛnˈtrɪpɪtl/
adj.|向心的|Relating to or directed toward a center.|The centripetal force keeps the planets in orbit around the sun.|近义词：inward, centralizing|反义词：centrifugal, outward|词性:adjective
