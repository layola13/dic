buff:/bʌf/| n. v. adj.|爱好者；浅黄色；抛光；米黄色；狂热者|A buff is someone who is very interested in and knowledgeable about a particular subject.  As a verb, it means to polish something. As an adjective, it describes a pale brownish-yellow color. |He's a real movie buff. 他是个真正的电影迷。|Synonyms: fan, enthusiast, expert, aficionado, polish, shine|Antonyms: novice, amateur, dull, tarnish|noun, verb, adjective
