disbandments:/dɪsˈbændmənts/
n.
解散，遣散
the act of breaking up or dissolving an organized group, especially a military unit.
The disbandment of the army was a key part of the peace treaty.  军队解散是和平条约的关键部分。
The museum faces closure following the disbandment of its board of trustees.  在董事会解散后，博物馆面临关闭。
dissolutions, breakups, dismissals
formations, establishments
noun
