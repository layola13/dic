donkey:/ˈdɒŋki/ (UK) /ˈdɑːŋki/ (US)| n.|驴；笨蛋；顽固的人|A domesticated hoofed mammal of the horse family with a long, furry tail, large ears, and a braying call. Also used informally to refer to a stubborn or foolish person.|The farmer used a donkey to carry his goods to market. 农民用一头驴把货物运到市场。|近义词:ass, burro|反义词:(no direct antonym, but consider: genius, scholar for the figurative meaning)|noun
