cofeatured:/ˌkoʊˈfiːtʃərd/
adjective
共同推荐的，共同主演的
featured together; presented jointly as a main attraction.
The cofeatured artists brought different styles to the concert. 这两位共同推荐的艺术家为音乐会带来了不同的风格。
The movie cofeatured two of Hollywood's biggest stars.  这部电影由两位好莱坞巨星共同主演。
近义词:jointly featured, co-starring
反义词:solo, individually featured
词性:adjective
