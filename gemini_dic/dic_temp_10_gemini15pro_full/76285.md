gab:/ɡæb/| vi.|闲聊，空谈，饶舌|They gabbed about their families and friends. 他们闲聊着各自的家人和朋友。|近义词：chat, chatter, prattle|反义词:be quiet|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From perhaps a variant of *gabben "to mock," related to Middle Dutch gabben "to mock, jest, lie," Old Norse gabba "to mock, deceive."  The noun is from the verb.
