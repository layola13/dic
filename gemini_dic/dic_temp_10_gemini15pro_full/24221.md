bridgelike:/ˈbrɪdʒˌlaɪk/
adjective
桥一样的；像桥的
resembling a bridge in form or function.
The rock formation was bridgelike, spanning the chasm.  这块岩层像桥一样，横跨着峡谷。
The new highway overpass has a bridgelike structure. 新的高速公路立交桥有一个像桥一样的结构。
近义词:archlike, arched, spanning
反义词:solid, continuous
