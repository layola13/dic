pannus:/ˈpænəs/| n. |(尤指关节的)滑膜肿胀|Inflammation and thickening of the synovial membrane, especially in a joint.|Rheumatoid arthritis can cause pannus formation in the joints. 类风湿性关节炎会导致关节滑膜肿胀。|Synonyms: swelling, inflammation, thickening|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but could consider "healthy synovial membrane")|Noun
