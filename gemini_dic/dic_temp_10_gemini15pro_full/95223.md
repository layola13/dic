inbred:/ˈɪnbrɛd/| adj.|近亲繁殖的；天生的|Produced by inbreeding; inherent.|The inbred strain of mice was particularly susceptible to disease. |近义词：congenital, innate, inherent|反义词:outbred, acquired|形容词
