occupationless:/ˌɑːkjəˈpeɪʃənləs/
adjective
无职业的，失业的
Without an occupation; unemployed.
He was occupationless after the factory closed down.  他工厂倒闭后就失业了。
The occupationless man spent his days wandering the streets.  这个失业男子每天都在街上闲逛。
近义词:jobless, unemployed, idle
反义词:employed, working, occupied
词性:adjective

词源:
From occupation + -less.  由occupation(职业) + -less(无...的)组成。
