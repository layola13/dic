corporatenesses:/ˈkɔːrpərɪtnɪsɪz/
noun
公司特性；企业文化
The quality or state of being corporate; the characteristics of a corporation.
The corporateness of the new venture was evident in its hierarchical structure. 这家新企业的公司特性体现在其等级森严的结构中。

The increasing corporateness of the music industry stifles creativity. 音乐产业日益增长的企业化扼杀了创造力。
Synonyms: corporate culture, corporate identity, corporate nature
Antonyms: individuality, independence, autonomy
词性: 名词
