lexicographically: /ˌlɛksɪkəˈɡræfɪkli/ | adv. | 字典编纂地，按字母顺序地 |  Arranged in the order of the alphabet, as in a dictionary. | The words are listed lexicographically.  这些单词是按字母顺序排列的。| alphabetically, in alphabetical order | reverse alphabetically, inversely | adverb


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What does he do for a living? = 他以什么为生？


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you doing now? = 你现在在做什么？
What is she reading at the moment? = 她这会儿在读什么？


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you done? = 你做了什么？
What has she achieved so far? = 到目前为止她取得了什么成就？


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been doing all day? = 你一整天都在做什么？
What has he been working on lately? = 他最近一直在做什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did he say? = 他说了什么？
What happened yesterday? = 昨天发生了什么事？


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when I called? = 我打电话时你在做什么？
What was she thinking about? = 她当时在想什么？


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had happened before I arrived? = 在我到达之前发生了什么事？
What had they discussed before the meeting? = 会议前他们讨论了什么？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the accident? = 事故发生前你一直在做什么？
What had they been discussing for so long? = 他们一直在讨论什么那么久？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天要做什么？
What will the weather be like next week? = 下周天气会怎样？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time tomorrow? = 明天这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting? = 会议上他们会讨论什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have accomplished by next year? = 到明年你会完成什么？
What will have changed by then? = 到那时会有什么变化？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years? = 到时候你将已经做这件事十年了？
What will she have been studying by graduation? = 到毕业时她将已经学习什么专业多久了？



虚拟语气 (Subjunctive Mood):
If I knew the answer, I would tell you. = 如果我知道答案，我会告诉你。
I wish I were taller. = 我希望我更高。


被动语态 (Passive Voice):
What was decided at the meeting? = 会议上决定了什么？
What is being done to solve the problem? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？


疑问句 (Interrogative Sentence):
What time is it? = 现在几点了？
What are you talking about? = 你在说什么？


否定句 (Negative Sentence):
I don't know what to say. = 我不知道该说什么。
She didn't tell me what she wanted. = 她没有告诉我她想要什么。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What the hell! = 搞什么鬼！
What if it rains? = 如果下雨怎么办？
What for? = 为了什么？
What about going to the cinema? = 去看电影怎么样？


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *what*, from Old English *hwæt*, neuter nominative and accusative singular of *hwā* (“who”, “what”), from Proto-Germanic *hwat*, from Proto-Indo-European *kʷód* (“what”). Cognate with Scots *what* (“what”), West Frisian *wat* (“what”), Dutch *wat* (“what”), German *was* (“what”), Danish *hvad* (“what”), Swedish *vad* (“what”), Icelandic *hvað* (“what”), Latin *quod* (“what”), Sanskrit *कत्* (*kat*) (“who”, “what”).
