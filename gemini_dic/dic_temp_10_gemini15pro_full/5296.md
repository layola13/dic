allotropes:/ˈæləˌtroʊps/ |n.| 同素异形体 | Different forms of the same element in the same physical state, but with different structures and therefore different properties. | Diamond and graphite are allotropes of carbon. 钻石和石墨是碳的同素异形体。| synonyms: polymorphic forms, modifications | antonyms: (No direct antonym, as it's a descriptive term) | noun
