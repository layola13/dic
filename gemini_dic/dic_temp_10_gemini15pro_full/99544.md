intergranular:/ˌɪntərˈɡrænjʊlər/ |adj.|晶间的，颗粒间的|Occurring between or around grains, as of a metal.  Relating to or situated between the grains or crystals of a polycrystalline material.|The intergranular corrosion weakened the metal significantly. 晶间腐蚀使金属严重弱化。|Synonyms: intercrystalline|Antonyms: transgranular|adjective
