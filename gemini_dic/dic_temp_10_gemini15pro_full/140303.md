ogham:/ˈoʊəm/ | n. | 欧甘文 |  An early medieval alphabet used primarily to write the early Irish language, typically consisting of a series of strokes inscribed on the edges of a stone. | The ogham inscription was difficult to decipher. 这段欧甘文铭文很难破译。 | The museum displayed a collection of ogham stones. 博物馆展出了一系列欧甘文字的石头。 | 近义词: Early Irish script | 反义词:  Modern alphabet | 词性: noun
