foryelde: /fɔːrˈjiːld/
verb
放弃，停止，屈服
to give up the control of or responsibility for something, or to stop resisting something
He was forced to foryelde his claim to the throne. 他被迫放弃了对王位的要求。
The rebels were eventually forced to foryelde to the government forces. 叛军最终被迫向政府军投降。
近义词: relinquish, surrender, cede, abandon
反义词: retain, maintain, defend, resist
词性: verb

Etymology:
From Middle English *foryelden, from Old English *forġieldan (“to repay, reward, yield, give up, resign”), from for- + ġieldan (“to yield, pay”).  Cognate with German *vergelten (“to requite, retaliate”).  The modern form is likely influenced by yield.
