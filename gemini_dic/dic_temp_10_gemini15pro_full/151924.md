pasteurizer:/ˈpæstʃəraɪzər/ /ˈpɑːstʃəˌraɪzər/| n. |巴氏灭菌器；巴氏消毒器|A machine used to pasteurize liquids, such as milk or juice, by heating them to a specific temperature for a specific period of time to kill harmful microorganisms without significantly altering the taste or nutritional value.|The dairy farm uses a large pasteurizer to process all of its milk.  这家奶牛场使用大型巴氏灭菌器来处理所有的牛奶。|

The brewery invested in a new pasteurizer to extend the shelf life of its beer.  这家啤酒厂投资了一台新的巴氏灭菌器以延长其啤酒的保质期。|Synonyms: sterilizer, disinfector|Antonyms: N/A|noun
