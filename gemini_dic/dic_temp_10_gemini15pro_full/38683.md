condensate:/ˈkɑːndɛnˌseɪt/ /ˈkɒndɛnseɪt/| n.|冷凝物，凝结物|a liquid that is formed when a gas cools and changes to a liquid state|The air conditioner produced a lot of condensate.|Synonyms: condensation, deposit, precipitate|Antonyms: vapor, gas|noun

condensate:/kənˈdɛnˌseɪt/ /kənˈdɛnseɪt/| v.|冷凝，凝结|to change from a gas or vapor to a liquid|The steam condensed into water droplets on the window.|Synonyms: liquefy, precipitate, solidify|Antonyms: evaporate, vaporize|verb
