debasednesses:/dɪˈbeɪsɪdnəsɪz/
noun
堕落；卑劣；贬低
The state of being debased; degradation, corruption, or depravity.
The debasednesses of the regime were evident in its treatment of political prisoners. 这个政权的堕落体现在其对待政治犯的方式上。
Synonyms: degradations, corruptions, depravities, vitiations, perversions
Antonyms: nobilities, virtues, purities, refinements, elevations
词性: 名词
