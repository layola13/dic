gazebo:/ɡəˈziːboʊ/ (US) /ɡəˈziːbəʊ/ (UK)| n.|凉亭；观景亭|A freestanding, roofed, open-sided structure, often hexagonal or octagonal, providing shade and a place to relax in a garden or park. |例句：We sat in the gazebo and watched the sunset. 我们坐在凉亭里看日落。

例句：The gazebo offered a welcome respite from the midday sun. 凉亭提供了正午阳光下的一个受欢迎的休憩场所。|近义词：pavilion, pergola, summerhouse|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps enclosed building)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the humorous mock-Latin *gazebo, perhaps influenced by gaze (look) + -ebo (future tense ending of Latin verbs).  Essentially, a fanciful word coined in 18th-century English.
