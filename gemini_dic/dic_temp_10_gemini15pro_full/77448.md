gawntree:/ˈɡɔːntriː/| n.|  不毛之地; 荒芜之地 (比喻) | A barren or desolate place, often used figuratively.| The drought turned the once fertile valley into a gawntree.  干旱把曾经肥沃的山谷变成了不毛之地。|Synonyms: wasteland, desert, barren land|Antonyms:  oasis, paradise, fertile land|noun
