abut:/əˈbʌt/| v. n.|邻接，毗邻；紧靠；|To be next to or have a common boundary with.|例句：Their properties abut each other. 他们的房产彼此相邻。

My land abuts onto a public footpath. 我的土地紧邻一条公共人行道。|近义词：adjoin, border, neighbor, verge, touch|反义词:separate, divide|词性:动词,名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *abouter, from *a- "to" + *boter "to push, thrust," from Germanic origin. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *butaną "to strike, push."

USAGE->
及物动词:
The building abuts a busy road. = 这栋建筑毗邻一条繁忙的道路。
Their land abuts ours. = 他们的土地与我们的土地相邻。

不及物动词:
The two properties abut. = 这两处房产相邻。
The forest abuts on the lake. = 森林与湖泊接壤。


PHRASE->
abut on = 毗邻，紧靠
abut against = 紧靠，抵着
