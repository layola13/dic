capitalizer:/ˈkæpɪtəlaɪzər/| n.|大写化工具，大写转换器|A tool or program that converts lowercase letters to uppercase. |Example:  Use the online capitalizer to format your title correctly.  使用在线大写工具正确设置标题格式。|Synonym: uppercase converter, capitalization tool|Antonym: lowercase converter|noun

capitalizer:/ˈkæpɪtəlaɪzər/| n.|资本家，投资者|One who provides capital, especially for business ventures. |Example: The capitalizer invested heavily in the startup.  这位资本家对这家初创公司进行了大量投资。|Synonym: investor, financier, backer|Antonym: borrower, debtor|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From capitalize + -er (agent suffix). The verb "capitalize" comes from Late Latin "capitalizare" meaning "to write with a capital letter," derived from "capitalis" meaning "of the head," hence "chief, principal."  Ultimately from Latin "caput" meaning "head."
