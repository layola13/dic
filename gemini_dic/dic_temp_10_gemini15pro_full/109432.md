leopards: /ˈlepərdz/ (US) /ˈlepədz/ (UK)| n. |豹子|A large, spotted cat found in Africa and Asia, known for its agility and hunting prowess.|例句：Leopards are known for their camouflage. 豹子以它们的伪装而闻名。

例句：The leopard stalked its prey through the tall grass.  这只豹子在高高的草丛中悄悄跟踪猎物。

近义词：panther, jaguar |反义词:  None (No direct opposite for an animal)|词性: Noun
