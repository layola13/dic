nonfinancial:/ˌnɒnfaɪˈnænʃəl/ /ˌnɑːnfaɪˈnænʃəl/
adjective
非财务的；非金融的
Relating to aspects of a company's performance or value that are not measured in monetary terms.
The company is increasingly focused on nonfinancial measures of success, such as customer satisfaction and employee engagement.  这家公司越来越关注非财务方面的成功衡量标准，例如客户满意度和员工敬业度。
synonyms: non-monetary, intangible, qualitative
antonyms: financial, monetary, economic
词性: 形容词
