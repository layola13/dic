misrelates:/ˌmɪsrɪˈleɪts/
verb
错误地联系；不正确地叙述
To show or describe the connection between (two or more things) incorrectly.
He misrelates the events of that night, confusing the order and omitting key details. 他错误地叙述了那晚的事件，混淆了顺序并省略了关键细节。
misrepresents, distorts, falsifies
accurately relates, correctly portrays
词性:v.
