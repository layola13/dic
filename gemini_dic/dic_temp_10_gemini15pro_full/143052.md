outblushing:/ˌaʊtˈblʌʃɪŋ/
| v. | 比...更脸红；使...相形见绌 | Surpassing someone in blushing; causing someone to blush even more by comparison. |  She outblushed him with her sudden, fiery cheeks. 她突然火红的脸颊让他相形见绌，更加脸红。 | 近义词: outshining, surpassing, eclipsing | 反义词: paling, fading | 词性: 动词

outblushing:/ˌaʊtˈblʌʃɪŋ/
| adj. | (rare) 用于描述比另一个人脸红得更厉害的 | Used to describe someone who blushes more intensely than another. | Her outblushing cheeks betrayed her nervousness. 她涨红的脸颊暴露了她的紧张。 | 近义词: excessively blushing | 反义词: composed, unflustered | 词性: 形容词
