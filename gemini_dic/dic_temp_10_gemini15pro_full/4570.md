alcove: /ˈælkəʊv/ (UK) /ˈælkəʊv/ (US)| n. |壁龛；凹室|A small recessed section of a room, or a small room opening off a larger one.|例句：The statue was placed in an alcove in the wall.  雕像被放置在墙上的一个壁龛里。

例句：We had breakfast in a sunny alcove off the kitchen. 我们在厨房外一个阳光明媚的凹室里吃了早餐。|近义词：recess, niche, nook, bay|反义词: protrusion, projection|词性: noun
