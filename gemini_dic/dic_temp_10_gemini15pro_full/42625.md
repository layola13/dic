counterrevolution:/ˌkaʊntərˌrɛvəˈluːʃən/ | n. | 反革命，反革命运动 | A counterrevolution is a movement that opposes a previous revolution. |  The government brutally suppressed the counterrevolution. 政府残酷镇压了反革命。|  The counterrevolution aimed to restore the monarchy. 反革命旨在恢复君主制。| synonyms: reaction, rebellion, revolt | antonyms: revolution | noun
