ledge:/lɛdʒ/| n.|壁架；突出物；矿脉|A narrow, flat surface, especially one that projects from a wall or cliff; a shelf-like projection. Also, a layer of rock or mineral.|The climber rested on a narrow ledge.  登山者在一个狭窄的壁架上休息。|Synonyms: shelf, projection, overhang, rim, brink|Antonyms: abyss, chasm|noun

ledge:/lɛdʒ/| n.|暗礁；沙洲|A ridge of rocks or sand, especially one near the surface of the water.|The ship ran aground on a hidden ledge.  船撞上了一处暗藏的暗礁。|Synonyms: reef, shoal, sandbank|Antonyms: deep, trench|noun
