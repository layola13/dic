indefatigableness:/ˌɪndɪˈfætiɡəb(ə)lnəs/
|n.|不屈不挠，不知疲倦
|The quality of being tireless and persistent.|
|Her indefatigableness in pursuing her goals was admirable. 她对目标孜孜不倦的追求令人钦佩。|
|His indefatigableness led him to climb the mountain despite the blizzard.  尽管暴风雪肆虐，他不知疲倦的精神使他登上了顶峰。|
|tirelessness, persistence, perseverance, tenacity|
|fatigue, weariness, lassitude, apathy|
|noun|

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + Latin *fatigabilis (capable of being tired) < fatigare (to tire) + -ness.
