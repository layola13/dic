astrometeorological:/ˌæstroʊˌmiːtiərəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
天体气象学的
Relating to the branch of meteorology that studies the supposed influence of the stars and planets on the weather.
Astrometeorological forecasts are not scientifically accepted. 天体气象学的预测并不被科学界接受。
Astrometeorological data is not considered reliable. 天体气象学的数据不被认为是可靠的。
近义词: astrological, celestial
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps: scientific, empirical)
词性: adjective
