ozonoscope: /ˈoʊzəʊnəˌskoʊp/ | n. | 臭氧检测仪 | An instrument for detecting and measuring ozone, especially in the atmosphere. |  Scientists used an ozonoscope to measure the ozone levels in the stratosphere. 科学家们使用臭氧检测仪测量平流层的臭氧水平。

The ozonoscope revealed a significant decrease in ozone concentration. 臭氧检测仪显示臭氧浓度显著下降。 | Near Synonyms: ozone meter, ozone sensor | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
