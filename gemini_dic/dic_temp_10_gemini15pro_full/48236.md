decontaminating: /ˌdiːkənˈtæmɪneɪtɪŋ/
verb, present participle
净化，清除污染，消毒
The act of removing contaminants from something, such as a person, object, or area.

例句：
They are decontaminating the area after the chemical spill.
他们在化学品泄漏后对该地区进行净化。

The workers were decontaminating the equipment.
工人们正在对设备进行消毒。


近义词：
cleaning, sterilizing, purifying, sanitizing

反义词：
contaminating, polluting

词性：
Verb (present participle)
