horticulturist:/ˌhɔːrtɪˈkʌltʃərɪst/ | n. | 园艺家；园艺师 | A person who practices horticulture; a gardener, especially one skilled in the science of horticulture. |  My neighbor is a horticulturist and has the most beautiful garden. 我的邻居是一位园艺家，拥有最美丽的花园。

| The horticulturist grafted a new variety of apple onto the old tree.  这位园艺师将一种新品种的苹果嫁接到老树上。 | Near Synonyms: gardener, cultivator, grower | Antonyms: N/A | Noun

horticulture:/ˈhɔːrtɪkʌltʃər/ | n. | 园艺；园艺学 | The art or practice of garden cultivation and management. | She studied horticulture in college and now owns a successful nursery. 她在大学学习园艺学，现在拥有一家成功的苗圃。

| Horticulture is a relaxing and rewarding hobby. 园艺是一项轻松且有益的爱好。 | Near Synonyms: gardening, floriculture, arboriculture | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
