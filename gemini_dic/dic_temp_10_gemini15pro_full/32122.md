chevachie:/ʃəˈvɑːʃi/| n.|骑马; 突袭; 骑兵袭击|A raid or incursion on horseback.|The general led a daring chevachie deep into enemy territory. 将军率领一支大胆的骑兵突袭深入敌后。|例句：The sudden chevachie caught the enemy off guard. 突然的骑兵袭击使敌人措手不及。

例句：The king's chevachie devastated the countryside. 国王的骑兵袭击摧毁了乡村。|近义词：raid, incursion, foray, attack|反义词:defense, retreat|词性:noun
