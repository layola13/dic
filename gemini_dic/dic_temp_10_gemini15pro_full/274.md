abe:/eɪb/| n.| 阿贝（Abraham的昵称）|Abe is a common nickname for Abraham.|例句：Abe Lincoln was the 16th president of the United States. 亚伯拉罕·林肯是美国第16任总统。|近义词：Abraham|反义词: N/A|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
Shortened form of Abraham, a Hebrew name meaning "father of a multitude".
