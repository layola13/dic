cambering:/ˈkæmbərɪŋ/
verb
拱起，弯曲，使…成弧形
giving a slightly arched or curved shape to something
The road was cambered to allow rainwater to run off. 道路略微拱起，以便雨水流走。
arching, curving, bending
flattening, straightening
v.
