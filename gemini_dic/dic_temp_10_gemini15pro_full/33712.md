circadian: /sɜːrˈkeɪdiən/ | adj. |生理节奏的，昼夜节律的 | Relating to or showing a biological process that occurs naturally on a twenty-four-hour cycle, even in the absence of light. |  例句：Circadian rhythms control our sleep-wake cycle.  昼夜节律控制着我们的睡眠-觉醒周期。| 近义词：diurnal, daily | 反义词：N/A | 词性：形容词

circadian: /sərˈkeɪdiən/ | n. |昼夜节律 | A circadian rhythm. |  例句：The circadian clock is influenced by light. 昼夜节律时钟受光线的影响。| 近义词：diurnal rhythm, daily cycle | 反义词：N/A | 词性：名词
