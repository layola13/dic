chebec:/ˈtʃɛbɛk/| n.|小霸鹟，白腹王霸鹟|A small flycatching bird of the genus Empidonax, especially the least flycatcher (E. minimus), native to North America.  These birds are known for their distinctive, sharp "chebec" call.|The chebec darted out from its perch to catch a fly.  小霸鹟从栖木上飞扑而出，捕捉一只苍蝇。|近义词：flycatcher, least flycatcher|反义词:|n.
