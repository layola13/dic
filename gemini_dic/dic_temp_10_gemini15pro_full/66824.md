expansivenesses:/ɪkˈspænsɪvnɪsɪz/
|n.| 扩张性；广阔性；豪爽；健谈|The state or quality of being expansive; breadth, largeness.  The tendency to be open, communicative, and generous.|His expansiveness made him a popular host. 他的豪爽使他成为受欢迎的主人。|近义词：breadth, largeness, generosity, openness, communicativeness|反义词：reticence, reserve, narrowness, stinginess|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From expansive + -ness. Expansive from French expansif, from Latin expansus, past participle of expandere "to spread out".
