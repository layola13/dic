non-portable:/ˌnɒnˈpɔːtəbl/ /ˌnɑːnˈpɔːrtəbl/| adj.|不可携带的，非便携式的|Not easily moved or carried; not suitable for being transported.|This old computer is non-portable; it weighs a ton! 这台旧电脑不可携带；它重达一吨！|The software is non-portable and can only be run on this specific operating system. 这个软件不可移植，只能在这个特定的操作系统上运行。|近义词：immobile, fixed, bulky, cumbersome|反义词:portable, mobile, transportable|词性:形容词

词源:
non- (prefix)  from Latin *non "not"
portable (adjective) from Late Middle English: from Old French portable, from porter ‘to carry’, from Latin portare .
