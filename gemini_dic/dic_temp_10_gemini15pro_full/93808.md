immeshing:/ɪˈmɛʃɪŋ/
|v.|啮合，紧密结合|The gears are immeshing smoothly. 齿轮啮合顺畅。|
|英文解析：The act of engaging or becoming engaged, as gears.  The act of involving or becoming deeply involved.|
|例句：The immeshing of the gears ensured the smooth operation of the machine.  齿轮的啮合保证了机器的平稳运行。|

|例句：His immeshing in the project led to significant breakthroughs. 他对项目的全身心投入带来了重大突破。|
|近义词：engaging, interlocking, meshing, integrating, involving|
|反义词：disengaging, separating, disconnecting|
|词性：动词|
