geomantic:/ˌdʒiːəˈmæntik/
adjective
地气的；风水术的；占卜的
Relating to geomancy (divination by geographic features or lines or figures).
例句：The placement of the building was determined through geomantic principles.  这栋建筑的位置是根据风水原则确定的。

例句：She consulted a geomancer for advice on the best location for her new business. 她咨询了一位风水师，寻求关于新公司最佳选址的建议。

近义词：divinatory, oracular
反义词:  scientific, empirical
词性: adjective
