ignescent:/ɪɡˈnɛsənt/| adj.|发光的，炽热的，闪耀的|Emitting light or fire, especially brightly; luminous; brilliant.|The ignescent meteor streaked across the night sky.  炽热的流星划过夜空。|近义词：luminous, radiant, incandescent, glowing, fiery|反义词：dark, dim, dull|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *ignescentem (nominative ignescens), present participle of ignescere "to take fire," from ignis "fire."
