cacaemia:/kækɪˈmiːə/
noun
恶病质，体液恶化
The presence of faecal matter in the blood.
Cacaemia is a serious condition that requires immediate medical attention. 体液恶化是一种需要立即就医的严重疾病。
Cacaemia can be caused by a ruptured bowel. 体液恶化可能是由肠破裂引起的。
