intermunicipal:/ˌɪntərˌmjuːˈnɪsɪpəl/
adjective
市政当局间的，跨市的
Existing or occurring between municipalities.
The intermunicipal bus service connects several towns in the region.  该跨市巴士服务连接了该地区的几个城镇。
近义词: intercity, regional
反义词: intramunicipal, local
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From inter- + municipal.  "Inter-" is a Latin prefix meaning "between" or "among." "Municipal" comes from the Latin word "municipalis," relating to a town or city with self-government.
