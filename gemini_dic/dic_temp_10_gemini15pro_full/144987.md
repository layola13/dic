overaction:/ˌoʊvərˈækʃən/| n.|过度反应，反应过激|An excessive or exaggerated reaction or response to a stimulus or situation.  It implies doing too much or behaving in a way that is disproportionate to the situation.|He showed a bit of overaction when I told him the news. 他听到这个消息时反应有点过激。|近义词：overreaction, exaggeration, hyperbole|反义词:underreaction, restraint|noun
