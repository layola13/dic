omen:/ˈoʊmən/ (US), /ˈəʊmən/ (UK)| n. |预兆，征兆|Something that is believed to be a sign of something good or bad that will happen in the future. |例句：The black cat crossing their path was seen as an ill omen.  黑猫穿过他们的路被视为不祥之兆。

例句：Many people believe that a broken mirror is an omen of bad luck. 很多人认为破镜子是不祥之兆。|近义词：sign, portent, presage |反义词:  (没有确切的反义词，可以考虑用表示“巧合”或“随机事件”的词) coincidence, chance |词性: noun
