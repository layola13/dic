banish:/ˈbænɪʃ/ /ˈbæniʃ/| vt.|放逐，驱逐；消除，排除|To send someone away from a country or place as an official punishment; to get rid of something completely, especially a feeling or thought.|The king banished his treacherous advisor. 国王放逐了他奸诈的顾问。

He tried to banish the thought from his mind. 他试图把这个念头从脑海中抹去。|Synonyms: exile, expel, deport, oust, dismiss, remove, eradicate, eliminate|Antonyms: welcome, admit, accept, embrace, retain|verb
