merchantabilities:/ˌmɜːrtʃəntəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. |适销性；可销售性| The quality of being fit for sale in the marketplace; the state of being acceptable to buyers.  It implies that goods are of a quality that a reasonable person would consider suitable for the purpose for which they are typically used.|  The car's numerous mechanical problems seriously affected its merchantabilities. 这辆车众多的机械问题严重影响了它的适销性。| Synonyms: salability, marketability | Antonyms: unmarketability, unsalability | noun
