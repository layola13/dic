assapanic: /ˌæsəˈpænɪk/ | adj. | 阿萨帕尼克式的；（指北美土著的仪式）涉及用阿萨帕尼克根进行净化的 | Relating to or designating a ceremony of purification practiced by some North American Indian peoples, involving the use of the root of the assapanic. |  The assapanic ritual was an important part of their spiritual life. 阿萨帕尼克仪式是他们精神生活的重要组成部分。 | The tribe performed an assapanic ceremony to cleanse the village. 部落举行了阿萨帕尼克仪式来净化村庄。 | 近义词: purifying, cleansing | 反义词: contaminating, polluting | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Assapanic, a name given by the Algonquian peoples to the wild ginger (Asarum canadense), whose root was used medicinally and ceremonially.
