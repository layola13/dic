antiobesity:/ˌæntiɔʊˈbiːsəti/
adjective
抗肥胖的
Relating to or intended to prevent or reduce obesity.
例句：The doctor recommended an antiobesity drug. 医生推荐了一种抗肥胖药。

例句：Antiobesity programs are becoming increasingly popular. 抗肥胖计划越来越受欢迎。

近义词：weight-loss, slimming,  anti-fat
反义词：obesogenic, fattening
词性：adjective
