guilty: /ˈɡɪlti/ | adj. | 有罪的；内疚的 | Feeling or showing remorse for having committed a wrong or crime. |  例句：He felt guilty about lying to his mother. 他对向母亲撒谎感到内疚。 | 近义词：ashamed, remorseful, culpable | 反义词：innocent, blameless | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Old English *gyltig, from *gylt ("guilt") + *-ig (adjectival suffix).  The word "guilt" comes from Proto-Germanic *kultiz, meaning "debt, fault."
