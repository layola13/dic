falter:/ˈfɔːltər/ (US) /ˈfɔːltə(r)/ (UK)| v. | 犹豫，迟疑；蹒跚；支吾；衰弱 | to hesitate or waver in action, purpose, or intent; to move unsteadily; to speak hesitantly; to lose strength or momentum |  His voice faltered as he spoke about the accident.  他谈到事故时，声音颤抖了。

Her steps faltered and she almost fell. 她脚步踉跄，差点摔倒。| 近义词：hesitate, waver, stumble, stammer | 反义词： persevere, continue, proceed,  strengthen | 词性: 动词
