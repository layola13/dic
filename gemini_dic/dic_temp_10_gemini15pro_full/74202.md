Fourier:/ˈfʊrieɪ/ /ˈfɔːrieɪ/ | n. | 傅里叶 |  Referring to Jean-Baptiste Joseph Fourier (1768-1830), a French mathematician and physicist known for his work on the representation of functions as a sum of trigonometric functions (Fourier series) and their application to the study of heat flow. | The Fourier transform is a powerful tool in signal processing. 傅里叶变换是信号处理中的强大工具。| Near Synonyms:  Fourier analysis, Fourier transform, harmonic analysis | Antonyms: N/A | proper noun
