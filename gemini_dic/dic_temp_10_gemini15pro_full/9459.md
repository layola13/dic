antireformists:/ˌæntiˈrɪˈfɔːrmɪsts/
n.| 反改革者，反对改革的人士 | People who oppose reforms or changes, especially in political or social systems. | The antireformists blocked the passage of the new law. 反改革者阻止了新法律的通过。| The antireformists clung to the old ways. 反对改革者固守着旧的方式。 |  opponents of reform, conservatives, reactionaries | reformers, progressives | noun
