inquiries:/ɪnˈkwaɪəriz/ /ˈɪŋkwəriːz/| n.|询问，打听，调查|An act of asking for information.|例句：We received numerous inquiries about the new product. 我们收到了大量关于新产品的咨询。|例句：The police are making inquiries into the robbery. 警方正在调查这起抢劫案。|近义词：questions, queries, investigations|反义词:answers, replies|词性:名词(plural)

inquiry:/ɪnˈkwaɪəri/ /ˈɪŋkwəriː/| n.|询问，打听，调查|An act of asking for information.|例句：I made an inquiry about the train schedule. 我询问了火车时刻表。|例句：The inquiry into the cause of the accident is still ongoing. 对事故原因的调查仍在进行中。|近义词：question, query, investigation|反义词:answer, reply|词性:名词(singular)

词源: From Old French enquerre, from Latin inquirere "to seek into, search for," from in- "into" + quaerere "to seek, ask."
