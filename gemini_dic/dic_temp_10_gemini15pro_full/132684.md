nondisqualifying:/ˌnɒndɪsˈkwɒlɪfaɪɪŋ/
adjective
非取消资格的；不使丧失资格的
Not causing someone or something to no longer be suitable or eligible for something.
例句：A nondisqualifying foul in basketball doesn't result in free throws.  篮球比赛中的非取消资格犯规不会导致罚球。

例句：The minor error was nondisqualifying and she was still eligible for the prize.  这个小错误并没有使她失去资格，她仍然有资格获得奖项。
近义词：acceptable, permissible, allowable
反义词：disqualifying
词性：adjective
