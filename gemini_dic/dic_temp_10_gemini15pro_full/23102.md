bountyhood:/ˈbaʊntiˌhʊd/
n.
慷慨；大方；赏金状态
The state or quality of being bountiful or generous; liberality.
He showed great bountyhood in donating to the charity. 他向慈善机构捐款表现出极大的慷慨。
近义词：generosity, liberality, munificence
反义词：stinginess, meanness, miserliness
词性: 名词

词源:  From bounty + -hood.  "Bounty" comes from Old French "bonté" meaning "goodness," ultimately from Latin "bonitas." "-hood" is an Old English suffix denoting "state or condition of being."
