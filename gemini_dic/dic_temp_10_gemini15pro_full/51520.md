detritic:/dɪˈtrɪtɪk/
adjective
碎屑的
Formed from or consisting of detritus; relating to or derived from detritus.
The detritic limestone is composed primarily of shell fragments. 这种碎屑灰岩主要由贝壳碎片组成。
近义词:clastic, fragmental
反义词:non-detrital
