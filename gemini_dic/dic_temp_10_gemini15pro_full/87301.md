hermitism:/ˈhɜːmɪtɪzəm/
n.
隐居；遁世；隐士生活；神秘主义
the practice of living as a hermit; seclusion from society. Also, a system of religious belief and practices based on the writings attributed to Hermes Trismegistus, characterized by mysticism, astrology, and alchemy.
He practiced hermitism in the remote mountains. 他在偏远的山区过着隐居生活。
synonyms: seclusion, solitude, isolation, withdrawal, retirement, asceticism, monasticism, mysticism
antonyms: sociability, gregariousness, worldliness
noun
