butternuts:/ˈbʌtərˌnʌts/ | n. |白胡桃; 冬南瓜; 奶油坚果|A type of squash with a tan, pear-shaped fruit with sweet, orange flesh, or the fruit itself. Also, a type of walnut with a buttery flavor.|
例句：I made butternut squash soup for dinner. 我晚饭做了冬南瓜汤。

例句：Butternut squash is a good source of vitamin A. 冬南瓜是维生素A的良好来源。

近义词：squash, walnut | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


butternut:/ˈbʌtərˌnʌt/ | n. adj. |白胡桃; 冬南瓜; 奶油坚果（形容词：白胡桃木的）| As an adjective, it describes something made from the wood of the butternut tree.|
例句：The table was made of beautiful butternut wood. 这张桌子是用漂亮的胡桃木做的。

例句：He planted a butternut tree in his backyard. 他在后院种了一棵白胡桃树。

近义词：(n.) squash, walnut; (adj.) walnut | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun, adjective
