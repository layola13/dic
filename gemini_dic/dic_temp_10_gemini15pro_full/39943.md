constructing:/kənˈstrʌktɪŋ/
verb
建造，构建
the act of building or creating something
例句：They are constructing a new bridge across the river. 他们正在建造一座横跨河流的新桥。

例句：The company is constructing a new factory in the suburbs. 这家公司正在郊区建造一座新工厂。

近义词：building, erecting, assembling, creating, making, forming, developing
反义词：demolishing, destroying, dismantling
词性：v.


Etymology:
From Latin constructus, past participle of construere "to heap up, build," from com- "together" (see com-) + struere "to pile, build" (see structure (n.)).
