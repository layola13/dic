muddlingly:/ˈmʌdlɪŋli/| adv.|混乱地，笨拙地|In a muddling or confusing manner; clumsily.|He muddlingly explained the complex procedure, leaving everyone more confused than before. 他笨手笨脚地解释了这个复杂的过程，让每个人都更加困惑了。|近义词：confusedly, awkwardly, clumsily, ineptly|反义词：clearly, efficiently, skillfully, adeptly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From the verb "muddle" + "-ing" + "-ly".  "Muddle" comes from Middle English *modlen*, frequentative of *mudden* "to make muddy," from *mud*.
