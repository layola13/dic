capitulatory:/kəˈpɪtʃʊləˌtɔːri/
|adj.|投降的；屈服的；有条件投降的|
Relating to, involving, or characterized by surrender on stipulated terms.  Indicating a disposition to surrender or give up resistance.
|
After a long siege, the city finally made a capitulatory gesture, agreeing to open its gates to the enemy.  在长期围困之后，这座城市最终做出了投降的姿态，同意向敌人打开城门。

The defeated army sent a capitulatory message, requesting terms of surrender.  战败的军队发出了投降的信息，要求投降条件。
|
Near synonyms: surrendering, submissive, yielding, resigned
|
Near antonyms: resistant, defiant, unyielding, resolute
|
