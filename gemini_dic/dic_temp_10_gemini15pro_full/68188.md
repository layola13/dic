faintnesses:/ˈfeɪntnɪsɪz/
n.
昏厥，晕厥; 微弱; 模糊
a sudden loss of consciousness for a short time because of a temporarily insufficient supply of oxygen to the brain.  The state of being faint or weak.  The quality of lacking clarity or distinctness.
She suffered from frequent faintnesses. 她经常昏厥。
The faintnesses of the old photograph made it difficult to identify the people.  这张老照片的模糊让人很难辨认出人。
近义词：dizziness, lightheadedness, weakness, vagueness
反义词：strength, clarity, consciousness
词性：名词
