bum-someone-out:/bʌm ˈsʌmwʌn aʊt/
|phrasal verb|
|使某人沮丧，使某人扫兴|
|To make someone feel sad, disappointed, or depressed.|
|The news of the layoffs really bummed me out.|这个裁员的消息真让我沮丧。
|It bummed her out that she didn't get the promotion.|她没有得到晋升，这让她很沮丧。
|近义词：depress, sadden, disappoint, upset|
|反义词：cheer up, elate, excite|
|词性：动词短语|
