ashiness:/ˈæʃɪnɪs/| n.|灰白；苍白；灰烬状态|The ashiness of her face betrayed her fear. 她苍白的脸色暴露了她的恐惧。|近义词：paleness, pallor|反义词：ruddiness, rosiness|词性:名词
Etymology: From "ashy" + "-ness." "Ashy" comes from Old English "æsc" (ash tree) + "-y."  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
