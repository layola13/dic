noninflammatory:/ˌnɒnɪnˈflæmətɔːri/ (US) /ˌnɒnɪnˈflæmətəri/ (UK)| adj.|非炎性的，非炎症的|Not inflammatory; not tending to cause inflammation.|This cream is noninflammatory and suitable for sensitive skin.  这种乳霜是非炎性的，适合敏感肌肤。|The medication is noninflammatory and won't exacerbate the condition.  这种药物是非炎性的，不会加剧病情。|近义词：non-inflammatory, anti-inflammatory|反义词:inflammatory|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + inflammatory.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "inflammatory" refers to something that causes inflammation.
