The politician's oratorical skills were legendary.  这位政治家的演讲技巧堪称传奇。


Synonym: eloquent, rhetorical, declamatory, bombastic
Antonym: inarticulate, dull, uninspired
Word type: Adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *oratoricalis, from Latin orator + -icalis. Equivalent to orator + -ical.
