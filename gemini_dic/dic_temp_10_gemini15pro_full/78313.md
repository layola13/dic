gerontocracies:/ˌdʒɛrənˈtɒkrəsiːz/
noun
老年统治；老人政治
A state, society, or group governed by old men.
The island nation is essentially a gerontocracy, ruled by a council of elders. 这个岛国本质上是一个老年政治国家，由长老委员会统治。
The company was criticized for being a gerontocracy, resistant to new ideas.  这家公司被批评为老人政治，抵制新思想。
Synonyms:  oligarchy of elders, rule by elders
Antonyms:  youthocracy
