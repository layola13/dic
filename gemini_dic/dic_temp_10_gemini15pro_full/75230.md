frolic:/ˈfrɒlɪk/ (UK) /ˈfrɑːlɪk/ (US)| n. vi. vt. adj.|嬉戏，玩闹；嬉戏的，欢乐的|An act of playful behaviour, especially by children or animals; to behave playfully.|The lambs frolicked in the meadow. 羊羔在草地上嬉戏。|近义词：play, romp, gambol, caper|反义词：mope, sulk|词性:名词，动词，形容词
词源：From Middle Dutch wrolijk "merry, happy," related to Middle High German vrœlich "happy, joyful," from Proto-Germanic *frogjaz.
