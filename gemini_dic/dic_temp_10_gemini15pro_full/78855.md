gingering:/ˈdʒɪndʒərɪŋ/ | v. |给…加姜；使活跃；使兴奋；鞭策|Adding ginger to a dish to enhance its flavor; invigorating or stimulating something; urging someone on. |例句：He was gingering up the crowd with his speech. 他用他的演讲激励着人群。

例句：She's gingering up the sauce with some chili. 她在酱汁里加了些辣椒提味。|近义词：spicing, enlivening, stimulating, invigorating, prodding, urging |反义词：dulling, subduing, dampening |词性:动词

词源: From the noun "ginger," referring to the spice known for its pungent and warming qualities, hence its figurative use for adding zest or stimulation.
