extroversion:/ˈekstrəvɜːrʒən/ /ˈekstroʊvɜːrʒən/
n.
外向性，外向性格
The act, state, or habit of being predominantly concerned with obtaining gratification from what is outside the self.
She displays a high degree of extroversion. 她表现出高度的外向性。

Extroversion is a personality trait characterized by sociability, assertiveness, and a tendency to seek stimulation in the company of others.  外向性是一种人格特质，其特征是善于交际、自信果断，以及倾向于在与他人的交往中寻求刺激。

近义词:outgoingness, sociability
反义词:introversion
词性:noun
