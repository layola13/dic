divested:/dɪˈvɛstɪd/ | adj. | 剥夺的，脱去了…的 | deprived of power, rights, or possessions; rid of something that is regarded as unpleasant or undesirable |  The company divested itself of all its foreign subsidiaries.  这家公司剥离了其所有的外国子公司。 | synonyms: stripped, deprived, rid, freed | antonyms: invested, endowed, furnished | adjective

divest:/dɪˈvɛst/ | vt. | 剥夺，脱去 | deprive someone of power, rights, or possessions; rid something of something that is regarded as unpleasant or undesirable | The court divested him of his parental rights. 法院剥夺了他的父母权利。 | synonyms: strip, deprive, rid, free | antonyms: invest, endow, furnish | verb
