hyperemotionality:/ˌhaɪpərɪˌmoʊʃəˈnælɪti/| n.|过度情绪化；情感过度|A state of heightened emotional reactivity, where an individual experiences and expresses emotions with greater intensity and frequency than is considered typical.|She displayed hyperemotionality after the accident, swinging between fits of laughter and crying.|近义词：emotional lability, mood swings, excessive emotionality|反义词：emotional stability, equanimity|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- (Greek: "over, beyond") + emotionality (from emotion + -ality)
