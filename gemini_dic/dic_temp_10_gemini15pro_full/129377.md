nomads:/ˈnoʊmædz/ (US) /ˈnəʊmədz/ (UK)| n.|游牧民族；流浪者|People who have no permanent home and travel from place to place, usually in search of pasture for their livestock.|The nomads travelled across the desert, searching for water.|Synonyms: wanderers, migrants, travelers, rovers|Antonyms: settlers, residents|Noun

nomadic:/nəʊˈmædɪk/ (UK) /noʊˈmædɪk/ (US)| adj.|游牧的；流浪的|Relating to or characteristic of nomads; wandering.|They lived a nomadic life, moving their herds from one pasture to another.|Synonyms: wandering, roving, migratory, itinerant|Antonyms: settled, sedentary, stationary|Adjective
