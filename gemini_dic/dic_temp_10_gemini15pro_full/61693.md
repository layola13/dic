emollients:/ɪˈmɑːliənts/ /ɪˈmɒliənts/ | n. |润肤剂；软化剂|Substances that soften and soothe the skin, especially dry or chapped skin.  They typically work by forming a barrier on the skin to prevent moisture loss.|Apply the emollients liberally to the affected area.  将润肤剂大量涂抹于患处。|synonyms: moisturizers, lotions, creams, ointments, balms|antonyms: astringents, irritants|noun

emollient:/ɪˈmɑːliənt/ /ɪˈmɒliənt/ | adj. |润肤的；软化性的；缓和的|Having the quality of softening or soothing the skin.|The emollient cream helped to soothe her dry, cracked hands.  润肤霜有助于舒缓她干燥皲裂的双手。|synonyms: softening, soothing, moisturizing, lubricating|antonyms: irritating, drying, astringent|adjective
