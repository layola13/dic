hyperspatiality:/ˌhaɪpərˌspeɪʃiˈælɪti/ | n. |超空间性，多维空间性 |The state or quality of being or relating to hyperspace; the condition of having more than three spatial dimensions.  It often refers to a conceptual or theoretical space with more dimensions than the three we experience in everyday life. |The concept of hyperspatiality is explored in science fiction, allowing for faster-than-light travel.| 近义词: multidimensionality, higher-dimensionality | 反义词: three-dimensionality | 词性: noun
