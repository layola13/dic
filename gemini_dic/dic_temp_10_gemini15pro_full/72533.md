flustered:/ˈflʌstərd/| adj.|慌张的，不安的，混乱的|Agitated and confused.|例句：He became flustered when he realized he had forgotten his notes for the presentation. 当他意识到自己忘记了演讲稿时，他变得慌张起来。

例句：She was so flustered by the unexpected question that she couldn't answer.  突如其来的问题让她不知所措，以至于无法回答。|近义词：agitated, nervous, confused, disconcerted, ruffled|反义词：calm, composed, collected, unperturbed|词性:形容词
词源: From the verb *fluster*, likely of Scandinavian origin, related to Old Norse *flaustra* "to bustle, hurry."
