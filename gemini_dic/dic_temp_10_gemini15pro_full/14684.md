awaiting:/əˈweɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 等待的，期待的 | waiting for something to happen; expecting something |  The package is awaiting shipment. 包裹正在等待发货。| 近义词: pending, expectant, anticipated | 反义词: completed, finished | 词性:形容词

Etymology: From the verb "await," which comes from Middle English *abiden*, from Old English *abīdan* "to wait, remain, endure," from *a-* (intensifying prefix) + *bīdan* "to wait, abide."
