counterbalance:/ˈkaʊntərˌbæləns/ /ˈkaʊntərˌbæləns/| v.|平衡，抵消；抗衡|To oppose or balance with an equal weight or force.|The company's losses in one market were counterbalanced by gains in another. 公司在一个市场的损失被在另一个市场的收益所抵消。|synonyms: offset, compensate, balance, counteract|antonyms: aggravate, worsen, intensify|verb
