astrotheology:/ˌæstroʊθiˈɒlədʒi/ | n. | 星象神学；天体神学 | The study of the astronomical and astrological beliefs and practices of ancient cultures, especially in relation to their religious and mythological systems.  It explores how celestial phenomena influenced the development of religious ideas and rituals. | Astrotheology offers a fascinating glimpse into the minds of our ancestors and their understanding of the cosmos. 星象神学为了解我们祖先的思想以及他们对宇宙的理解提供了迷人的一瞥。|  Astrotheology examines how ancient civilizations incorporated celestial events into their myths and rituals.  星象神学研究古代文明如何将天体事件融入他们的神话和仪式中。 |  cosmology, archaeoastronomy | N/A | noun
