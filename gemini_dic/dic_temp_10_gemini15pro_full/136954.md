nonspecificities:/ˌnɑːnspɛsɪˈfɪsɪtiz/ | n. | 非特异性，非专一性 | The lack of specific targeting or action; referring to something that affects or reacts with a wide range of targets rather than a particular one. | The test showed several nonspecificities, making the diagnosis uncertain.  该测试显示了几种非特异性，使得诊断不确定。 | Synonyms: generality, imprecision, broadness | Antonyms: specificity, precision, selectivity | noun
