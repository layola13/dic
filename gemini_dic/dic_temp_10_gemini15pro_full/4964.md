alkali: /ˈælkəlaɪ/ (US), /ˈælkəli/ (UK)| n. |碱|A chemical substance that neutralizes acids and forms salts.  It has a pH greater than 7. |Sodium hydroxide is a strong alkali. 氢氧化钠是一种强碱。| synonym: base | antonym: acid | noun


alkali: /ˈælkəlaɪ/ (US), /ˈælkəli/ (UK)| adj. |碱性的|Having the properties of an alkali; basic. |Alkali soils are not suitable for all crops. 碱性土壤并不适合所有作物。| synonym: basic | antonym: acidic | adjective
