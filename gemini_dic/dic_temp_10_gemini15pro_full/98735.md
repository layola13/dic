insurmountably:/ˌɪnsərˈmaʊntəbli/| adv.|不可逾越地，无法克服地|In a way that cannot be overcome or dealt with successfully. |The two sides seemed insurmountably divided. 双方似乎存在着不可逾越的分歧。|The task seemed insurmountably difficult. 这项任务看起来难于登天。|近义词: insuperably, unovercomably|反义词: surmountably, easily|词性: 副词

词源:
From in- (not) + surmount (overcome) + -ably (in a manner that can be).  "Surmount" itself comes from Old French "surmonter" (to climb over, surpass), from "sur-" (over) + "monter" (to mount, climb).
