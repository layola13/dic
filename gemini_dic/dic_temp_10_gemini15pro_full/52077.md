dialysis: /daɪˈælɪsɪs/ /daɪˈæləsɪs/
noun
透析
The separation of particles in a liquid on the basis of differences in their ability to pass through a membrane.
Patients with kidney failure undergo dialysis. 肾衰竭患者要进行透析。
hemodialysis, kidney dialysis
---
dialysis: /daɪˈælɪsɪs/ /daɪˈæləsɪs/
noun
分析
A method of separating small molecules from large ones using a semi-permeable membrane.
Dialysis is a crucial process in various scientific fields. 透析是各个科学领域中的一个关键过程。
analysis, separation
---


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek dialusis "separation," from dialuein "to dissolve," from dia- "through" + lyein "to loosen."
