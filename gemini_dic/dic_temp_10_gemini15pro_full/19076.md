betrayal:/bɪˈtreɪəl/| n.|背叛，辜负，暴露|the action of betraying one's country, a group, or a person; treachery.|例句：He felt a sense of betrayal when his friend lied to him. 他感到朋友欺骗他是一种背叛。

例句：The betrayal of secrets to the enemy led to the loss of the war.  向敌人泄露机密导致了战争的失败。


近义词：treachery, treason, disloyalty, perfidy|反义词:loyalty, faithfulness, fidelity|词性:noun
