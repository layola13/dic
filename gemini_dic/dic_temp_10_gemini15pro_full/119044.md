meticulosity:/ˌmɛtɪˈkjʊləsɪti/| n.|一丝不苟，谨小慎微|Extreme care in the consideration or treatment of details; painstaking conscientiousness.|She planned the party with her usual meticulousity. 她以她一贯的细致态度筹划了这次聚会。|近义词：precision, exactitude, scrupulousness|反义词：carelessness, negligence|词性:名词

meticulous:/məˈtɪkjʊləs/| adj.|一丝不苟的，谨小慎微的|Showing extreme care about minute details; very careful and precise.|He is meticulous about his work. 他对工作一丝不苟。|近义词：careful, precise, scrupulous|反义词：careless, sloppy|词性:形容词
