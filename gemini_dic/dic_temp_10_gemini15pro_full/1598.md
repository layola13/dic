aciculiform: /əˈsɪkjʊlɪfɔːrm/
adjective
针状的
shaped like a needle; slender and pointed.
The plant's leaves are aciculiform, adapting it to arid environments.  这种植物的叶子呈针状，使其适应干旱环境。

The pine needles are aciculiform and help retain moisture. 松针呈针状，有助于保持水分。

synonym
needle-shaped, acicular, linear, setaceous
antonym
broad, ovate, rotund
词性
adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *acicula* (small needle, diminutive of *acus*, needle) + *-formis* (having the form of)

USAGE->
The cactus has aciculiform spines for protection. 仙人掌有针状的刺以保护自己。
Many conifers have aciculiform leaves that reduce water loss. 许多针叶树有针状的叶子，以减少水分流失。
