insufficiency:/ˌɪnsəˈfɪʃənsi/ | n. |不足，缺乏，机能不全|The insufficiency of funds prevented the project from being completed. 资金不足导致项目无法完成。|例句：The insufficiency of evidence led to the case being dismissed.  证据不足导致案件被驳回。

例句：His insufficiency as a leader was evident in the team's poor performance. 他作为领导者的不足之处体现在团队糟糕的表现上。|近义词：lack, shortage, deficiency, inadequacy, dearth |反义词: sufficiency, abundance, surplus |词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *insufficientia, from Latin insufficiens (present participle of insufficere “be insufficient”), from in- "not" + sufficere "be sufficient".
