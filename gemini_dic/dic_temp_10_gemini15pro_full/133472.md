nonfeebler:/nɒnˈfiːblər/
adjective
非更弱的；比...更强壮的
Someone who is a nonfeebler is not weaker than someone or something else; they are stronger or at least as strong.  This word is not standard English and is likely a neologism formed by adding the prefix "non-" to "feebler" (comparative form of "feeble").
The champion boxer proved to be a nonfeebler opponent than anticipated.  这位冠军拳击手被证明是一个比预期更强大的对手。
After his recovery, he was a nonfeebler version of himself. 康复后，他比以前更强壮了。
近义词:stronger, more robust, fitter
反义词:feebler, weaker, less robust
词性:adjective
