drillable:/ˈdrɪləbl/| adj.|可钻的，可训练的|Something that is drillable can be drilled into or trained repeatedly.  This word often refers to materials like wood or metal, but can also be used metaphorically for skills or knowledge that can be instilled through repetitive practice. |The rock was too hard and not drillable with our equipment. 这块岩石太硬了，我们的设备无法钻透。|Synonyms: penetrable, permeable, pierceable (for materials); teachable, trainable (for skills/knowledge)|Antonyms: impenetrable, impermeable, resistant (for materials); unteachable, untrainable (for skills/knowledge)|adjective
