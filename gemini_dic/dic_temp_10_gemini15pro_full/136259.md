nonrepellency:/ˌnɒnrɪˈpɛlənsi/ | n. |  无驱避性，不排斥性 | The nonrepellency of the fabric means it won't scare away insects.  这种织物的不排斥性意味着它不会吓跑昆虫。| Example: The nonrepellency of the new coating allowed graffiti to be easily removed.  新的涂层的不排斥性使涂鸦很容易被清除。 | Example:  The nonrepellency of the surface made it susceptible to staining.  表面的不排斥性使其容易沾上污渍。 | Synonym: nonresistance, unresistance, permeability | Antonym: repellency, resistance, impermeability | Noun
