hypotheses:/haɪˈpɒθəsiːz/
noun
假设（复数）
A proposed explanation for a phenomenon made as a starting point for further investigation.
The scientist tested several hypotheses before reaching a conclusion.  科学家在得出结论之前检验了几个假设。
synonyms: theories, assumptions, suppositions, conjectures
antonyms: facts, proofs, certainties
n.
