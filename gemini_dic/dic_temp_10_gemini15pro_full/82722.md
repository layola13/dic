guidebooky:/ˈɡaɪdˌbʊki/| adj.|像导游手册的；充满导游手册信息的|Resembling or characteristic of a guidebook; full of guidebook information.  It felt a bit guidebooky, listing all the "must-see" sights without any personal touch.|例句：The website's description of the city was a little too guidebooky for my taste.  这个网站对这座城市的描述对我来说有点太像导游手册了。

例句：Her travel blog was refreshingly personal and avoided sounding guidebooky. 她的旅行博客令人耳目一新，避免了听起来像导游手册。|近义词：touristy, formulaic, conventional|反义词：original, unique, unconventional|词性:形容词
