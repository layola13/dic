manufacturability:/ˌmænjʊˌfæktʃərəˈbɪləti/
n.
可制造性
The ease with which a product can be manufactured.
The manufacturability of the new design was a key concern for the engineers.  工程师们对新设计的可制造性非常关注。
producibility, feasibility
non-manufacturability
noun
