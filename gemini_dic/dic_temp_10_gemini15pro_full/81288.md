grantor:/ˈɡræntər/ | n. | 设保人，让渡人，授予者 | A person or entity that transfers property or rights to another. | Example 1: The grantor signed the deed, transferring ownership of the house.  让渡人签署了契约，将房屋所有权转让。

Example 2: The grantor of the scholarship wished to remain anonymous.  奖学金的授予者希望保持匿名。 | Synonyms: donor, giver, assigner | Antonyms: grantee, recipient | noun
