audibles:/ˈɔːdəblz/| n.|听得见的声音；有声读物|Sounds that can be heard; audiobooks. |例句：The audibles from the concert were loud and clear. 音乐会上的声音响亮而清晰。

例句：She enjoys listening to audibles during her commute. 她喜欢在通勤途中听有声读物。|近义词：sounds, audio, audiobooks|反义词:inaudible, silent|词性:noun (plural)
Etymology: From "audible" + "-s" (plural marker).  "Audible" comes from the Late Middle English *audible*, from the Old French *audible* and directly from the Latin *audibilis*, meaning "that can be heard," from *audire* "to hear."
