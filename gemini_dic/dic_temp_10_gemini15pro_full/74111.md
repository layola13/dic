fought /fɔːt/
verb
打仗，战斗，搏斗
past tense and past participle of "fight" - engaged in a physical or emotional struggle against someone or something.
They fought bravely for their country. 他们为了国家英勇作战。
The two boys fought over a toy. 两个男孩为了一个玩具打架。
近义词: battled, struggled, contended, competed
反义词: yielded, surrendered, submitted
词性: verb
