grapeshot: /ˈɡreɪpʃɒt/ (UK), /ˈɡreɪpʃɑːt/ (US) | n. | 葡萄弹；霰弹 | A cluster of small iron balls fired from a cannon.  It was used against massed infantry at close range. | The ship fired a volley of grapeshot into the advancing enemy troops.  这艘船向推进的敌军发射了一排葡萄弹。 |  The grapeshot ripped through the ranks of soldiers, causing devastating casualties. 葡萄弹撕裂了士兵的队伍，造成了毁灭性的伤亡。 | Synonym: canister shot | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps a single cannonball) | Noun
