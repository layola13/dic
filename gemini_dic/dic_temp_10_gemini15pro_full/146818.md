overimports:/ˈoʊvərɪmpɔːrts/
n.
过度进口，进口过剩
The overimports of cheap goods harmed domestic industries. 廉价商品的过度进口损害了国内产业。
Excessive imports, imports exceeding demand or a desirable level.  Overimports can lead to trade imbalances and harm domestic producers. The country's overimports contributed to a large trade deficit.  这个国家的过度进口导致了巨额贸易逆差。
例句：The overimports of steel led to a decline in domestic production. 钢铁的过度进口导致国内产量下降。
近义词：excess imports, surplus imports
反义词：undervaluation of imports
