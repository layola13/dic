out-measured:/ˌaʊtˈmeʒərd/
verb
胜过，超过（在计量上）
surpassed in measurement; exceeded in a measured quantity
例句：His performance out-measured all expectations. 他的表现超出了所有预期。

例句：The new building out-measured the old one in height. 新建筑的高度超过了旧建筑。
近义词：exceeded, surpassed, outstripped, outdone
反义词：underperformed, fell short of
词性：v.
