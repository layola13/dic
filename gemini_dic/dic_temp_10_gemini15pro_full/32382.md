chiliasm:/ˈtʃɪliæzəm/| n.|千禧年主义，千年至福说|The belief that Christ will reign on earth for a thousand years before the final judgment.|Many evangelical Christians believe in chiliasm. 很多福音派基督徒相信千禧年主义。|Synonyms: millennialism, millenarianism|Antonyms: none|Noun


chiliasm:/ˈtʃɪliæzəm/| n.|对理想社会的渴望，乌托邦思想|A belief in a future golden age of peace and prosperity.|Her chiliasm led her to join a utopian community. 她对理想社会的渴望使她加入了一个乌托邦社区。|Synonyms: utopianism, idealism|Antonyms: pessimism, cynicism|Noun
