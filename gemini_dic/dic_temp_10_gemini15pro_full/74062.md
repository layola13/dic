fossilate:/ˈfɒsɪleɪt/ /ˈfɑːsɪleɪt/ | v. | 使…成化石；变成化石 | To convert into a fossil; to become a fossil. | The remains of the dinosaur were fossilized over millions of years.  恐龙的遗骸在数百万年中变成了化石。

The ancient tree fossilized over time. 古树随着时间的推移变成了化石。| 近义词：petrify, mineralize | 反义词: decay, decompose | 词性：动词

fossilate:/ˈfɒsɪleɪt/ /ˈfɑːsɪleɪt/ | adj. | 化石的；成为化石的 |  Of, relating to, or resembling a fossil.  | Fossilized remains provide valuable clues about the past. 化石遗骸提供了关于过去的宝贵线索。

They discovered fossilized footprints of early humans. 他们发现了早期人类的化石足迹。| 近义词：petrified, fossilized | 反义词: living, extant | 词性：形容词
