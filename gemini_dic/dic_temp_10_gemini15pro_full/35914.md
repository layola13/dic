cocreating:/ˌkoʊkriˈeɪtɪŋ/
verb
共同创造
The act of creating something together, collaboratively.
We are cocreating a new future for our community. 我们正在共同创造我们社区的新未来。

Companies are cocreating products with their customers. 公司正在与客户共同创造产品。


近义词: collaborating, co-producing, co-designing, jointly creating
反义词: independently creating, solely creating
词性: verb (present participle)

Etymology:
From co- + creating, the present participle of create.  "Co-" is a prefix meaning "together," "jointly," "mutually."  "Create" ultimately derives from the Latin "creare," meaning "to produce," "to bring forth."
