agonizer:/ˈæɡənaɪzər/| n.| 苦恼者，深思熟虑者 |Someone who agonizes or suffers intense mental anguish.| He was an agonizer, constantly second-guessing his decisions. 他是一个苦恼者，总是反复思考他的决定。|近义词：worrier, fretter|反义词：carefree person, optimist|noun


agonize:/ˈæɡənaɪz/| v.|  使苦恼，折磨；苦苦思索 |To suffer intense mental anguish; to worry excessively about something.|  She agonized over whether to accept the job offer. 她为是否接受这份工作而苦恼。|近义词：worry, fret, struggle|反义词：relax, disregard|verb
