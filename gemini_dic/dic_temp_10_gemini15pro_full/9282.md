antiphilosophy:/ˌænti fɪˈlɒsəfi/ | n. | 反哲学 | Opposition to or rejection of traditional philosophy, often characterized by skepticism, cynicism, or a focus on practical concerns. |  Nietzsche's work is often considered a form of antiphilosophy. 尼采的著作常被认为是一种反哲学的形式。 | synonyms:  iconoclasm, nonconformism | antonyms:  philosophical orthodoxy, traditionalism | noun

antiphilosophy:/ˌænti fɪˈlɒsəfi/ | n. | 反哲学 |  A set of ideas or practices that challenge or subvert the fundamental principles of philosophy. | Some see antiphilosophy as a necessary corrective to abstract theorizing.  有些人认为反哲学是对抽象理论化的必要修正。 | synonyms:  heterodoxy, dissidence | antonyms:  conformity,  dogmatism | noun
