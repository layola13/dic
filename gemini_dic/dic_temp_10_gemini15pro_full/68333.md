fall-off-the-roof:/fɔːl ɔːf ðə ruːf/
adjective
暴跌的，急剧下降的
A sudden and dramatic decline, often used to describe prices, sales, or performance.
Sales fell off the roof after the scandal.  丑闻爆出后，销售额暴跌。
近义词:plummet, plunge, crash, tumble
反义词:soar, skyrocket, surge
词性:adjective

fall-off-the-roof:/fɔːl ɔːf ðə ruːf/
noun
暴跌，急剧下降
A sudden and dramatic decline, often used to describe prices, sales, or performance.
There was a fall-off-the-roof in tourism after the earthquake. 地震后，旅游业急剧下滑。
近义词:slump, decline, drop, decrease
反义词:rise, increase, growth, surge
