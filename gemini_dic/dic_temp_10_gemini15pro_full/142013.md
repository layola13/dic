organization:/ˌɔːrɡənɪˈzeɪʃn/ | n. | 组织；机构；团体；安排，筹划 | An organization is a group of people who work together in a structured way to achieve a specific goal. | The World Health Organization is a specialized agency of the United Nations. 世界卫生组织是联合国的一个专门机构。|  The organization of the conference was flawless.  这次会议的组织工作完美无瑕。| synonyms: institution, group, association, arrangement, structure | antonyms: disorganization, chaos, disorder | noun

Etymology:  From Old French *organizacion, from Late Latin organizatio, from Latin organizare “to provide with organs, to organize,” from organum “instrument, organ,” from Greek organon “instrument, tool, organ.”
