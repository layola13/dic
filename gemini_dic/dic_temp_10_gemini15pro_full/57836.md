dresses:/ˈdrɛsɪz/| n.|连衣裙，服装，穿着|A garment typically worn by women or girls that covers the body from the shoulders to a length that is generally above the ankles.|She wore a beautiful red dress to the party. 她穿了一件漂亮的红色连衣裙去参加聚会。|近义词：garments, clothing, attire, gowns|反义词: undresses|noun

dresses:/ˈdrɛsɪz/| v.|穿衣，打扮，包扎（伤口），整理|put clothes on oneself or someone else.|She dresses her children in matching outfits. 她给她的孩子们穿上了配套的服装。|近义词： attire, clothe, garb, array|反义词: undress|verb
