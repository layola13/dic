demosthenic:/ˌdɛmɒˈθɛnɪk/ | adj. | 雄辩的，如德摩斯梯尼的 |  Relating to or characteristic of Demosthenes, a prominent Athenian statesman and orator known for his forceful and persuasive speaking style; marked by vehement oratory. |  His demosthenic delivery captivated the audience. 他的雄辩的演讲深深吸引了观众。 | 近义词：eloquent, impassioned, forceful, fiery | 反义词：ineloquent,inarticulate, hesitant | 词性: 形容词

词源：From Greek *Dēmosthenēs*, the name of the Athenian orator, + *-ikos* “-ic.”
