ocarinists:/ˌɒkərɪˈnɪsts/
n.
陶笛演奏家；吹陶笛的人
A person who plays the ocarina.
The ocarinists gathered for the annual festival.  陶笛演奏家们聚集在一起参加年度节日。
The group of ocarinists played a beautiful melody.  这群陶笛演奏家演奏了一曲优美的旋律。
Synonyms: ocarina players
Antonyms: None.
