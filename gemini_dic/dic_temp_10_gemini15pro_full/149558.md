paginate:/ˈpeɪdʒɪneɪt/| v.|分页，标页码|To divide (a document or book) into pages.|The software can automatically paginate documents. 该软件可以自动为文档分页。|近义词：number, separate|反义词: combine, merge|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *paginatus*, past participle of *paginare* "to arrange in pages," from Latin *pagina* "page."
