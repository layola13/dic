counterpointed:/ˌkaʊntərˈpɔɪntɪd/
|adj.|对照的，形成对比的|Set in opposition or contrast; juxtaposed.|Her vibrant optimism was counterpointed by his deep pessimism. 她的乐观开朗与他深深的悲观形成了鲜明的对比。|The sweet melody was counterpointed by a deeper, more resonant bass line. 甜美的旋律与更深沉、更响亮的低音线形成对比。|contrasted, juxtaposed, opposed|harmonized, consistent, similar|adjective
