anhelose:/ænˈhiːloʊz/
verb
渴望，热望
to long for something very much; to yearn for
She anhelosed for a return to her homeland. 她渴望回到故土。
He anhelosed to see his family again. 他渴望再次见到他的家人。
synonyms: yearn, crave, long for, desire
antonyms: dislike, hate, despise
