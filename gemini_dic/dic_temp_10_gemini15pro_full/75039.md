frigate:/ˈfrɪɡət/ | n. | 护卫舰 | A warship that is smaller than a destroyer and larger than a corvette.  It is a fast, maneuverable warship typically used for escorting larger vessels, patrolling, and independent operations. | The frigate patrolled the coastline. 护卫舰在海岸线巡逻。|  The frigate engaged the enemy submarine. 护卫舰与敌方潜艇交战。 | 近义词:warship, destroyer (although larger), corvette (although smaller) | 反义词:None (ships aren't generally considered to have antonyms) | 词性:noun
词源:From Italian *fregata*, ultimately from Late Latin *fabricata* (“constructed ship”), feminine past participle of *fabricare*, from Latin *fabrica* ("workshop").
