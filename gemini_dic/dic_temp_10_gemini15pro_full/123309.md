monogrammal:/ˌmɒnəˈɡræməl/ | adj. | 字母组合的；用字母组合图案装饰的 |  Relating to or consisting of a monogram (a motif of two or more letters, typically a person's initials, usually interwoven or combined in a decorative design). |  The napkins were embroidered with a monogrammal design. 餐巾上绣着字母组合图案。 | 近义词: initialed, personalized | 反义词: plain, undecorated | 词性: adjective

词源: From *monogram* + *-al*. *Monogram* comes from Greek *mónogramma*, from *mónos* "single" + *gramma* "letter."
