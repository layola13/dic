nitrifying:/ˈnaɪtrɪfaɪɪŋ/
adjective
硝化的
causing nitrification; converting ammonia into nitrates or nitrites.
Nitrifying bacteria play a crucial role in the nitrogen cycle. 硝化细菌在氮循环中起着至关重要的作用。
近义词: oxidizing
反义词: denitrifying
词性: adjective

词源: From nitrify + -ing.  "Nitrify" comes from  "nitre" (now "niter"), meaning potassium nitrate, from Old French, ultimately from Medieval Latin nitrum, from Greek nitron, and "-fy"  meaning "to make" or "cause to be," from French -fier, from Latin -ficare.
