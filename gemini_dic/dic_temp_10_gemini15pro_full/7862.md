antebellum:/ˌæntiˈbɛləm/| adj.|战前的（尤指美国南北战争前的）|Existing before a war, especially the American Civil War.|The antebellum South was known for its large plantations and reliance on slave labor.  战前的南方以其大型种植园和对奴隶劳动的依赖而闻名。|例句：The antebellum architecture of Charleston is renowned for its beauty. 查尔斯顿的战前建筑以其美丽而闻名。
