citation: /saɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 引用，引文，表彰，传票 | a quotation from or reference to a book, paper, or author, especially in a scholarly work. |  Example: The student's essay included citations from several prominent historians.  这个学生的论文引用了几位杰出历史学家的著作。

Example:  He received a citation for bravery. 他因勇敢而受到表彰。 | synonyms: quotation, reference, mention, commendation, award, summons | antonyms: omission, neglect | noun


citation: /saɪˈteɪʃən/ | v. | 引用，引证, 表彰 | to quote (a passage, book, or author) as evidence for or justification of an argument or statement, especially in a scholarly work. | Example: She cited several experts in her presentation. 她在演讲中引用了几位专家的观点。

Example:  He was cited for his outstanding contributions to the community. 他因对社区的杰出贡献而受到表彰。 | synonyms: quote, refer to, mention, commend, award, summon | antonyms: omit, neglect | verb
