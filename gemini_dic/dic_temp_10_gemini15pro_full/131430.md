nonclosely:/nɒnˈkloʊsli/ | adv. | 不密切地，不紧密地 | Not in a close manner; not intimately or tightly. | The houses were nonclosely spaced, giving each family ample privacy.  这些房子间隔不紧密，给每个家庭充足的隐私空间。| The points were nonclosely related, making the argument difficult to follow. 这些观点联系不紧密，使得论证难以理解。 | 近义词: loosely, distantly, sparsely | 反义词: closely, tightly, densely | 词性: 副词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "closely" (adverbial form of "close").  "Close" ultimately derives from the Latin "claudere" meaning "to shut."
