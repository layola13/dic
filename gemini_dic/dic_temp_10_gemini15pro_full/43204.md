crabfaced:/ˈkræbˌfeɪst/| adj.|脾气暴躁的，阴沉的|Having a bad-tempered or sullen facial expression, likened to the perceived grumpiness of a crab.  It suggests someone looks perpetually annoyed or ill-tempered.|He gave me a crabfaced look when I asked him for a favor. 他当我请他帮忙时，给了我一个臭脸。|近义词：sullen, grumpy, surly, ill-tempered|反义词：cheerful, amiable, pleasant|形容词
