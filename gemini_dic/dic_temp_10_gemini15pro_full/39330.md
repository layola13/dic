conjecturally:/kənˈdʒɛktʃərəli/
adverb
推测地；猜想地
based on conjecture; by guessing.
The date of the manuscript can be conjecturally placed in the 14th century. 这份手稿的日期可以推测为14世纪。
Synonyms: hypothetically, presumably, putatively, supposedly, tentatively
Antonyms: certainly, definitely, factually, surely
词性: adverb
