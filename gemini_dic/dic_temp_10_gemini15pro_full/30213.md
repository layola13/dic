cellaress:/ˈselərəs/| n.|女侍酒师；酒吧女招待|A woman who serves alcoholic beverages, especially wine, in a cellar, bar, or restaurant. |She worked as a cellaress in a trendy wine bar.  她在一家时尚的酒吧担任侍酒师。|近义词：bartender, barmaid, sommelier (for wine)|反义词: N/A| noun


Etymology->
From "cellar" (referring to a storage space, often for wine) + "-ess" (a suffix denoting a female).  The word "cellar" ultimately comes from Latin "cellarium."
