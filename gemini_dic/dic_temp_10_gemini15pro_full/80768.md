gossiphood:/ˈɡɒsɪphʊd/| n. | 流言蜚语, 流言蜚语的传播范围 |A social environment characterized by gossip; a community or group where gossip is prevalent.  It refers to the collective world or realm of gossip. |The small town was a hotbed of gossiphood, with every little detail of everyone's life being dissected and discussed. 这个小镇是一个流言蜚语的温床，每个人的生活中的每一个小细节都被剖析和讨论。|近义词：rumormongering,  gossipmongering|反义词:secrecy, discretion|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "gossip" + "-hood". "Gossip" comes from Old English "godsibb," meaning "sponsor at a baptism," later evolving to mean a close friend and then someone who talks idly about others. "-hood" is a suffix denoting a state, condition, or quality.
