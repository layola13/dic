cultivate:/ˈkʌltɪveɪt/| v. |耕作，培养，陶冶，结交|To prepare and use (land) for crops or gardening; to try to acquire or develop (a quality, sentiment, or skill).|He cultivated a small piece of land behind his house. 他在房子后面耕种了一小块地。


She cultivated a taste for opera. 她培养了对歌剧的爱好。|近义词：develop, nurture, foster, grow, refine, improve|反义词：neglect, abandon, ignore|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *cultivatus*, past participle of cultivare "to till, cultivate," from Latin cultus "care, cultivation," originally past participle of colere "to till, cultivate, inhabit, protect, worship."
