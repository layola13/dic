nonbuttressed:/ˌnɒnˈbʌtrɪst/ | adj. | 无扶壁的，无支撑的 | Not supported by buttresses. A buttress is a structure built against a wall to support it. So, nonbuttressed means without this kind of support. | The nonbuttressed wall eventually crumbled under the pressure.  无扶壁的墙最终在压力下倒塌了。| The nonbuttressed design of the building made it vulnerable to earthquakes.  这座建筑的无扶壁设计使其易受地震影响。 | synonyms: unsupported, unreinforced | antonyms: buttressed, supported, reinforced | adjective
