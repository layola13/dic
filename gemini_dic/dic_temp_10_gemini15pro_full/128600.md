niceties:/ˈnaɪsɪtiz/
n.
细节；礼节；微妙之处；繁琐的礼仪
A detail or feature of polite social behavior or etiquette.  The subtle points or distinctions of something.
例句：He was not interested in the niceties of diplomatic protocol. 他对外交礼仪的细节不感兴趣。

例句：She observed the niceties of polite conversation. 她遵守了礼貌谈话的礼节。

近义词：courtesies, formalities, subtleties, nuances
反义词：rudeness, impoliteness, crudeness, bluntness
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

词源:  From "nicety," from Middle English "nicite," from Old French "niceté," from Latin "nicitas" ("exactness, precision"), from "nicus" ("precise, exact, careful, attentive").
