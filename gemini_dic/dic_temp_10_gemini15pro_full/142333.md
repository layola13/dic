orthogeosynclinal: /ˌɔːrθoʊˌdʒiːoʊˈsɪŋklaɪnl/ /ˌɔːθəʊˌdʒiːəʊˈsɪŋklaɪnl/
adjective
正地槽的
Relating to or designating a geosyncline that develops along the margin of a continent, characterized by volcanism and intensive folding and faulting.
The orthogeosynclinal theory explains the formation of mountain ranges. 正地槽理论解释了山脉的形成。
The geologist studied the orthogeosynclinal deposits. 地质学家研究了正地槽沉积物。
Synonyms: miogeosynclinal (somewhat)
Antonyms: None
