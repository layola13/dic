contextualize: /kənˈtekstʃuəlaɪz/ | v. | 将…置于背景中; 上下文化; 联系上下文; | To place (something, such as a word or activity) in a context. | Historians try to contextualize past events by showing how they relate to the current situation. 历史学家试图通过展示过去事件与当前形势之间的关系，将过去事件置于背景中。| The professor contextualized the poem by discussing the poet's life and times. 教授通过讨论诗人的生平和时代，将这首诗置于背景中。 | synonyms: place in context, situate, locate, position, set, frame, interpret, explain, explicate, illuminate, elucidate | antonyms: decontextualize, abstract, isolate | verb
