ducking:/ˈdʌkɪŋ/| n. |躲避，闪避; 蹲下; 快速浸水|The act of lowering the head or body quickly, especially to avoid being hit or seen; plunging briefly into water.|She gave the children a quick ducking in the sea. 她让孩子们在海里快速浸了一下。|近义词：dip, plunge|反义词: - |n.

词源：From the verb "duck," which originated from Old English *dūcan, meaning "to dive, plunge, immerse."
