leno:/ˈliːnoʊ/| n. |勒诺，一种薄而透明的织物，常用作窗帘或服装的一部分|A leno is a thin, transparent fabric, often used for curtains or as part of a garment.  It is characterized by open, fancy weaves. |The curtains were made of a delicate leno. 窗帘是用精致的勒诺布料做的。|近义词：gauze, muslin, net|反义词: -(not commonly used, perhaps consider "heavy fabric")|noun
