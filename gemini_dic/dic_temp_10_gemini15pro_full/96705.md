ineffectible:/ˌɪnɪˈfɛktɪbl/
adjective
无效的，不起作用的
Not capable of being effected; unachievable or impossible to implement.
His plan to solve the traffic problem was ultimately ineffectible due to lack of funding. 他解决交通问题的计划最终因为缺乏资金而无效。
The medicine proved ineffectible against the new strain of the virus.  这种药对新病毒株无效。
近义词:futile, useless, ineffective, inefficacious
反义词:effective, efficacious, potent, powerful
词性:adjective
词源:in- (not) + effectible (capable of being effected), from effect + -ible (capable of being)
