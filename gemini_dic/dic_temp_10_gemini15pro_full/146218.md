overexplain:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspleɪn/| v. |过度解释；解释得过于详细|To explain something in too much detail, often to the point of being confusing or tedious.|例句：I tend to overexplain things when I'm nervous. 我紧张的时候往往会解释得太多。|近义词：belabor, elaborate, overelaborate|反义词: understate, imply |词性:动词

overexplain:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspleɪn/| n. |过度解释|The act of explaining something in too much detail.|例句：Her overexplain made the situation more confusing. 她的过度解释让情况更混乱了。|近义词：overelaboration|反义词: understatement |词性:名词
