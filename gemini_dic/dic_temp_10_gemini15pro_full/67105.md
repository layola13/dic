explores:/ɪkˈsplɔːrz/ /ɪkˈsplɔːrz/
verb
探索，探究，考察
to travel to or through (a place) in order to learn about it or to search for something
例句：The team explores the Amazon rainforest.  团队探索亚马逊雨林。

例句：Scientists are exploring new ways to treat cancer. 科学家们正在探索治疗癌症的新方法。

近义词：investigates, examines, researches, discovers
反义词：ignores, neglects
词性：v.
