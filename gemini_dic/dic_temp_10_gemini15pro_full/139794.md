octavo:/ɒkˈteɪvəʊ/ /ɑːkˈteɪvoʊ/
noun
八开本；八开纸
A size of book page resulting from folding a sheet of paper three times to form eight leaves.
This dictionary is an octavo. 这本字典是八开本的。
近义词:book size
反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific book size)
词性:n.
