evisceration:/ɪˌvɪsərˈeɪʃən/
n.
取出内脏；剖腹；摘除…的精华
the process of removing the internal organs of a body, especially of a person or animal; the process of depriving something of its essential part
The surgeon performed an evisceration of the infected abdominal cavity. 外科医生对受感染的腹腔进行了内脏摘除手术。
disembowelment, gutting
replenishment, filling
noun

eviscerate:/ɪˈvɪsərˌeɪt/
vt.
取出内脏；除去主要部分
to remove the internal organs of a body, especially of a person or animal; to deprive something of its essential part
The fish were eviscerated and cleaned before being grilled.  这些鱼在烧烤前被取出内脏并清洗干净。
disembowel, gut
fill, replenish
verb
