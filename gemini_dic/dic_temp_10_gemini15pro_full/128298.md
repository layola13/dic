neurosecretory:/ˌnʊəroʊˈsiːkrɪtɔːri/
adjective
神经分泌的
Relating to or denoting nerve cells that secrete hormones.
Neurosecretory cells release hormones into the bloodstream.  神经分泌细胞将激素释放到血液中。
Neurosecretory granules contain hormones. 神经分泌颗粒含有激素。
近义词: neuroendocrine
反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly non-secretory)
词性: adjective
