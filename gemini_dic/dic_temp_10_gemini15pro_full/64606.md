eruption: /ɪˈrʌpʃən/ | n. | 爆发，喷发 | A sudden, often violent, outburst or release, as of a volcano, emotions, or a disease. | The volcanic eruption destroyed the nearby village. 火山爆发摧毁了附近的村庄。 | Synonyms: outburst, explosion, burst, discharge | Antonyms: suppression, containment | noun
