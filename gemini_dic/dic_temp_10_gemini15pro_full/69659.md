fencelessness:/ˈfɛnsləsnəs/
n.
无栅栏状态；无围栏状态；无防备状态
The state of being without fences; lack of barriers or enclosure; vulnerability.
The fencelessness of the property made it vulnerable to trespassers.  这处房产没有围栏，很容易被入侵者闯入。
The metaphorical fencelessness of the internet poses challenges to security.  互联网的象征性无围栏状态给安全带来了挑战。
近义词: openness, vulnerability, unprotectedness, exposure
反义词: enclosure, security, protection, safety
词性: noun

Etymology:
From "fenceless" + "-ness".
"Fenceless" is from "fence" (meaning barrier) + "-less" (suffix indicating absence).
"Fence" ultimately derives from the Old French "defense".
