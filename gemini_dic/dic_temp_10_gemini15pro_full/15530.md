bagatelle:/ˌbæɡəˈtɛl/| n.|小事；琐事；不值钱的东西；（尤指）短小精悍的乐曲|Something that is unimportant or insignificant; a trifle.  Also a short, light piece of music, especially for the piano.|The argument was about some bagatelle. 这场争论是关于一些琐事的。|Synonyms: trifle, triviality, nothing, piece of fluff, knick-knack, gimcrack, gewgaw, bauble, trinket|Antonyms: essential, important, significant|noun

bagatelle:/ˌbæɡəˈtɛl/| adj.|无足轻重的；不重要的|Unimportant or insignificant.|It was a bagatelle matter, easily resolved.  这是一个无关紧要的问题，很容易解决。|Synonyms:  trivial, insignificant, unimportant, negligible, trifling, petty, paltry, nugatory|Antonyms: significant, important, crucial, essential|adjective
