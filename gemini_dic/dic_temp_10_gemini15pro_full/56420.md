dollardom: /ˈdɒlərdəm/ (US) /ˈdɒlədəm/ (UK) | n. | 金钱至上；拜金主义 | The state or condition of being dominated by money or the pursuit of wealth.  A social system or environment in which money is considered the most important value. |  The pervasive dollardom of Hollywood can be disheartening for aspiring artists. 好莱坞盛行的拜金主义可能会让有抱负的艺术家感到沮丧。

The family's relentless pursuit of wealth led to a culture of dollardom in their home.  这个家庭对财富的无情追求导致了他们家中拜金主义的文化。| Near synonyms: materialism, consumerism, money-worship, affluenza | Near antonyms: altruism, philanthropy, asceticism | noun
