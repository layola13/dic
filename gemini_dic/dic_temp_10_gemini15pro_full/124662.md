movable:/ˈmuːvəbl/| adj.|可移动的，活动的|Something that is movable can be moved from one place to another.|The furniture in the apartment is all movable. 公寓里的家具都是可移动的。|近义词: mobile, portable, transportable|反义词: fixed, immovable, stationary|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from Old French movable or late Latin movabilis, from movere ‘to move’.
