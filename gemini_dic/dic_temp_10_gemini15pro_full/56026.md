doability:/ˌduːəˈbɪləti/
noun
可行性, 可做性
The doability of the project is still under discussion.  这个项目的可行性仍在讨论中。
The engineer questioned the doability of the design.  工程师质疑设计的可行性。
Synonyms: feasibility, practicability, viability
Antonyms: impossibility, impracticability, unfeasibility
