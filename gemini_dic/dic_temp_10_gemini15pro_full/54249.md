disgorging:/dɪsˈɡɔːrdʒɪŋ/
|v.|排出；吐出；涌出|The volcano disgorged molten lava. 火山喷出了熔岩。|
|例句：The pipe was disgorging sewage into the river. 这根管道正向河里排放污水。|
|例句：The crowd disgorged from the stadium after the game.  比赛结束后人群从体育场涌出。|
|近义词：expelling, emitting, spewing, discharging|
|反义词：absorbing, swallowing, ingesting|
|词性：动词|

disgorge:/dɪsˈɡɔːrdʒ/
|v.|排出；吐出；涌出|to release or discharge something forcefully|
|例句：The company was forced to disgorge its ill-gotten gains.  公司被迫吐出非法所得。|
|例句：He disgorged the contents of his pockets onto the table. 他把口袋里的东西都倒在了桌子上。|
|近义词：expel, emit, spew, discharge|
|反义词：absorb, swallow, ingest|

词源:
From Old French desgorger "to vomit, clear the throat," from des- "out" (see dis-) + gorge "throat" (see gorge (n.)). Figurative sense of "yield up" (money, information, etc.) is first attested 1590s. Related: Disgorged; disgorging.
