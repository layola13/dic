cast-down:/ˌkæst ˈdaʊn/ | adj. | 沮丧的，气馁的 | Feeling sad, discouraged, or depressed. |  He was cast-down after failing the exam. 他考试不及格后很沮丧。| 近义词: dejected, depressed, downcast, disheartened | 反义词: cheerful, happy, elated, joyful | 词性: 形容词

cast down:/ˌkæst ˈdaʊn/ | v. | 使沮丧，使气馁 | To make someone feel sad, discouraged, or depressed. | The news of her friend's illness cast her down.  她朋友生病的消息让她很沮丧。 | 近义词: depress, deject, discourage, dispirit | 反义词: cheer up, encourage, hearten, uplift | 词性: 动词

词源:  From the Middle English *casten doun*, from Old Norse *kasta niðr*, meaning "to throw down."  The figurative sense of "to lower in spirits" developed later.
