concerned:/kənˈsɜːrnd/ /kənˈsɝːnd/| adj.|关心的，担忧的；有关的，涉及的| Worried, anxious, or troubled about something.  Also, related to or involved with something.|例句：I am concerned about your health. 我很担心你的健康。|近义词：worried, anxious, apprehensive, involved, related|反义词：indifferent, unconcerned, apathetic|词性:形容词
concerned:/kənˈsɜːrnd/ /kənˈsɝːnd/| n.|相关人士，有关方面|A person or group of people who are involved in something or affected by it.|例句：The concerned parties met to discuss the issue. 有关各方开会讨论了这个问题。|近义词：stakeholders, interested parties|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

词源: From the Latin "concernere" meaning "to sift together," later evolving to mean "to concern oneself with."
