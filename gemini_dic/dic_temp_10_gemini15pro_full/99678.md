interlining:/ˈɪntərˌlaɪnɪŋ/
n.
衬里，衬布
A layer of material placed between the outer fabric and the lining of a garment, for warmth or stiffness.
The coat has a warm interlining. 这件外套有一层保暖衬里。

interlining:/ˈɪntərˌlaɪnɪŋ/
n.
(印刷)行间加铅
The insertion of extra space between lines of type.
The interlining makes the document easier to read. 行间加铅使文件更容易阅读。


Etymology:
From inter- + lining, from line (v.)

Synonyms:
lining, padding, wadding, filler, interlayer

Antonyms:
outer layer, exterior

n. (noun)
