nonelusive:/ˌnɒnɪˈluːsɪv/
adjective
非难懂的，容易理解的
easily understood; not obscure or difficult to grasp.
The instructions were nonelusive and easy to follow.  说明书浅显易懂，容易遵循。
The professor's explanation of the complex theory was surprisingly nonelusive.  教授对复杂理论的解释出奇地容易理解。
近义词:clear, straightforward, simple, easy to understand, accessible, comprehensible, perspicuous, lucid, unambiguous, plain, transparent
反义词:elusive, obscure, difficult, complex, complicated, intricate, ambiguous, vague, unclear, opaque
词性:adjective
词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "elusive" (from Latin "eludere," meaning "to elude, escape")
