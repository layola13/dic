libertarians:/ˌlɪbərˈtɛəriənz/
n.
自由意志主义者；自由论者
People who believe in libertarianism.  Advocates of a political philosophy that emphasizes individual liberty, limited government, and free markets.
Libertarians believe the government should have minimal involvement in people's lives. 自由意志主义者认为政府应该尽量少地干预人们的生活。
Many libertarians oppose taxation. 许多自由意志主义者反对征税。
Synonyms: individualists, liberals (in the classical sense)
Antonyms: authoritarians, statists
noun
