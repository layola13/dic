noncontemporaneousnesses:/ˌnɒnkənˌtɛmpəˈreɪniəsnɪsɪz/
n.
不同时性；非同时期性
The state of not being contemporaneous; the state of not existing or occurring at the same time.
The historian noted the noncontemporaneousnesses of the two documents, proving they couldn't have influenced each other.  历史学家注意到这两份文件并非同时期的，证明它们不可能互相影响。
noncontemporaneity, asynchrony
contemporaneity, synchronicity
noun
