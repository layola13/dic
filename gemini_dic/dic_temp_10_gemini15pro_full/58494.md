duel's:/ˈduːəlz/
noun
决斗的；决斗
Possessive form of duel, indicating ownership or association with a duel.  It refers to something belonging to or associated with a duel.
The duel's outcome was uncertain.  决斗的结果难以预料。
The duel's location was kept secret. 决斗的地点被保密。
Synonyms:  fight's, contest's, battle's
Antonyms: peace's, agreement's
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French duel, from Latin duellum ("war"), originally duorum bellum ("war of two").
