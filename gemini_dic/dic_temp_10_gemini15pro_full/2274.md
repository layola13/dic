adenitis:/ˌædɪˈnaɪtɪs/
n.
腺炎
Inflammation of a gland, such as a lymph node.
The doctor diagnosed the swelling in her neck as adenitis. 医生诊断她脖子上的肿胀为腺炎。
Lymphadenitis,  glandular swelling
Healthy gland function
noun
