garden-variety:/ˈɡɑːrdn vəˈraɪəti/ | adj. |普通的，常见的，平凡的 |Common or ordinary; not special or unusual. |例句：It was a garden-variety cold, nothing serious.  这是普通的感冒，没什么大不了的。

例句：The bank robbery was a garden-variety heist, nothing spectacular.  这起银行抢劫案很普通，没什么特别的。 |近义词：common, ordinary, typical, mundane, usual |反义词：unusual, uncommon, atypical, rare, exceptional |词性:形容词

词源：This term originates from the idea of the common types of plants one might find in a typical garden, as opposed to rarer, more exotic species. It began to be used figuratively in the mid-20th century.
