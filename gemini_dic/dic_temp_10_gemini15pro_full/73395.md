forehorse:/ˈfɔːrhɔːrs/| n.|先锋，领头马，最重要的事物|A person or thing in the leading position.|The CEO is the forehorse of innovation in the company.  首席执行官是公司创新的先锋。|synonyms: vanguard, leader, pioneer, spearhead|antonyms: follower, laggard|noun

Etymology: From Middle English *forehors*, from Old English *forehors*, a compound of *fore* ("before, in front") + *hors* ("horse").
