moronities:/məˌrɑːˈnɪtiz/ | n. | 愚蠢行为；愚蠢言论 |  Plural form of *moronity*, referring to acts or statements exhibiting extreme foolishness or stupidity. |  His recent moronities have cost the company dearly. 他最近的愚蠢行为让公司付出了高昂的代价。 |  The politician's speech was full of moronities. 这位政客的演讲充满了愚蠢的言论。 | 近义词：stupidities, follies, absurdities | 反义词： wisdom, intelligence, brilliance | 词性: 名词

词源:  Derived from *moron* (a person of low intelligence) + *-ity* (a suffix forming nouns of quality or state).  *Moron* itself comes from Greek *mōros*, meaning "dull" or "foolish."
