conceitedness's:/kənˈsiːtɪdnəsɪz/
noun
自负；自大；骄傲自满
The quality of being excessively proud and self-satisfied.
His conceitedness's obvious display alienated his colleagues. 他明显的自负让同事们疏远了他。
近义词: arrogance, vanity, egotism, pride
反义词: humility, modesty
词性: noun (名词)

ETYMOLOGY->
From conceited + -ness.  Conceited comes from Middle English *conceited, from conceive (imagine, think) + -ed.  Ultimately from Latin *concipere* (to take in, conceive).
