exceedingly:/ɪkˈsiːdɪŋli/
adverb
非常地，极其地
extremely; to a very great extent
The task proved to be exceedingly difficult. 这项任务被证明极其困难。
He was exceedingly polite. 他非常有礼貌。

近义词:extremely, highly, very, exceptionally, remarkably, unusually, extraordinarily
反义词:moderately, slightly, marginally

词性:adverb

词源:From Middle English *excedyngly*, from Old French *excedant* (“exceeding”) + *-ly*, equivalent to *exceed* + *-ingly*.
