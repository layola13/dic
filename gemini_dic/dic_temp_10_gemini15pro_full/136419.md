nonretirements:/ˌnɒn rɪˈtaɪərmənts/ | n. | 不退休，继续工作;  | The increasing trend of nonretirements is changing the workforce demographic. |  Older workers are increasingly choosing nonretirement, opting to stay engaged in their careers for longer. | near synonyms: continued employment, ongoing work,  working past retirement age | antonyms: retirements, cessation of work | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
"non-" (prefix): from Latin "non" meaning "not"
"retirement": from Old French "retirer" meaning "to withdraw"
Thus, "nonretirement" literally means "not withdrawing" from work.
