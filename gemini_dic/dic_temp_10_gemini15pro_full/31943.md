chelidonius: /ˌkɛlɪˈdoʊniəs/
adjective|燕子的，celandine的|Of or relating to swallows, or to the celandine plant (Chelidonium majus).|The chelidonius swallow arrives in early spring. 燕子在早春到达。|近义词: hirundine|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *chelidonius*, from Greek *khelidṓnios* (χελιδώνιος), from *khelidṓn* (χελιδών) "swallow".  The connection to the celandine plant is due to the belief that the plant bloomed when swallows arrived and withered when they departed.
