mileage:/ˈmaɪlɪdʒ/| n.|里程；里程数；好处；利益|the number of miles travelled by a vehicle or person, or the distance that a vehicle can travel on a particular amount of fuel. Also means the advantages or benefits that you can get from a situation or action.|例句：The car has high mileage. 这辆车的里程数很高。|例句：He got a lot of mileage out of the scandal. 他从这桩丑闻中捞到了很多好处。|近义词：distance, range, profit, advantage|反义词:disadvantage, loss|noun
