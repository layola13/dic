-icial 不是一个独立的英文单词。它通常是作为后缀出现在单词的末尾，例如 official, judicial, beneficial 等。因此，无法单独对其进行词义解析。 

以下是以 official 为例的单词解析：

official: /əˈfɪʃ(ə)l/ | adj. n. | 官方的；正式的；官员 | relating to or done by someone in authority; a person who holds a position of authority |  The official announcement was made yesterday. 官方公告昨天发布了。 | formal, authorized | unofficial, informal | adjective, noun

official: /əˈfɪʃ(ə)l/ | n. | 官员 | a person who holds a position of authority | He is a government official. 他是一位政府官员。 | officer, administrator | citizen, civilian | noun
