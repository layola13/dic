mussitation:/ˌmʌsɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 喃喃自语；低声说话 |The act of muttering; a low, indistinct utterance. | He was so nervous he spoke in a low mussitation. 他太紧张了，说话的声音低得像喃喃自语。|  She offered a prayer in a soft mussitation. 她低声喃喃地祈祷。| 近义词: murmur, mutter, mumble | 反义词: shout, yell | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *mussitātiō, from Latin mussitāre (“to mutter, murmur”).
