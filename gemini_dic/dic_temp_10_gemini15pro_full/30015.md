caxton:/ˈkækstən/| n.|卡克斯顿; 印刷机;威廉·卡克斯顿|William Caxton (c. 1422 – c. 1491) was an English merchant, diplomat, writer, printer, and publisher. He is thought to be the first person to introduce a printing press into England, and the first English retailer of printed books.|William Caxton is often credited with printing the first book in English.|近义词:printer, publisher|反义词:|名词
