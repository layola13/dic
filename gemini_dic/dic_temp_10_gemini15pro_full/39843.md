constable:/ˈkɒnstəbl/ (UK), /ˈkɑːnstəbl/ (US)| n.|警官，治安官|A police officer of the lowest rank, typically in a small town.|The constable arrested the thief. 警官逮捕了小偷。|Synonyms: police officer, officer, lawman, peace officer|Antonyms: criminal, lawbreaker|noun

constable:/ˈkɒnstəbl/ (UK), /ˈkɑːnstəbl/ (US)| n.|(古)城堡的指挥官，总管|A high-ranking official in a medieval castle.|The constable was responsible for the castle's defenses.  城堡指挥官负责城堡的防御。|Synonyms: governor, commander, castellan|Antonyms: N/A|noun
