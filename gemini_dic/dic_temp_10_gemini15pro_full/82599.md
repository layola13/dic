guardingly:/ˈɡɑːrdɪŋli/
adverb|谨慎地；小心地；提防地|In a cautious or reserved manner, especially in revealing information or expressing opinions.|She spoke guardingly about her plans, not wanting to give too much away. 她谨慎地谈论她的计划，不想透露太多。|例句：He answered guardingly, revealing little about his true intentions. 他谨慎地回答，几乎没有透露他的真实意图。

例句：She smiled guardingly, unsure of his motives. 她谨慎地笑了笑，不确定他的动机。


近义词：cautiously, carefully, prudently, warily, circumspectly|
反义词：openly, frankly, freely, carelessly, recklessly|
词性：adverb|

词源：From "guarding" + "-ly." "Guarding" comes from the verb "guard," which ultimately derives from the Old French "garder," meaning "to watch, protect, keep."  The suffix "-ly" transforms the word into an adverb.
