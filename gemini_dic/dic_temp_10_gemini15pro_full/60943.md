elevatory: /ˈɛləvətɔːri/ (US), /ˈɛlɪvətəri/ (UK)
adjective
提升的；升高的; 有提高作用的
Tending to elevate; elevating.  Having the power of raising or improving.
Elevatory influences can improve one's mood.  提升的影响可以改善一个人的情绪。
The elevatory music inspired the audience.  振奋人心的音乐鼓舞了观众。
近义词: uplifting, raising, improving, enhancing
反义词: depressing, lowering, degrading
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *elevatorius, from Latin elevatus, past participle of elevare (“to lift up, raise”), from e- (“out, up”) + levare (“to lift, raise”).
