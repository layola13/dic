hearingless:/ˈhɪərɪŋlɪs/
adjective
失聪的，听不见的
Unable to hear; deaf.
The hearingless child communicated through sign language.  这个失聪的孩子用手语交流。
近义词:deaf, hard of hearing
反义词:hearing, auditory
词性:adjective
