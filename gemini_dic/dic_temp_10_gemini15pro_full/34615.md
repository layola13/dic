clee:/kliː/
n.
爪；（尤指鸟兽的）脚爪；(鹰等猛禽的)爪
A sharp, curved nail on the foot of a bird or animal, especially a bird of prey.
The eagle gripped its prey with its powerful clees.  老鹰用它强有力的爪子抓住猎物。
近义词：claw, talon
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑paw（掌）表示结构上的不同)
词性: 名词


clee:/kliː/
n.
(古语)斜坡，悬崖
A steep slope or cliff. (Archaic)
The path led up a rocky clee.  这条小路通向一个岩石峭壁。
近义词：cliff, slope, precipice
反义词:  plain, flat
词性: 名词


词源:  Middle English *cle*, from Old English *clēa*, related to Old High German *klāa* "claw".
