co-operativenesses:/koʊˈɑːpərətɪvnəsəz/ /kəʊˈɒpərətɪvnəsɪz/
n.
合作性，协作性
The quality of being co-operative; willingness to work together.
例句：The team's co-operativenesses were essential to the project's success.  团队的合作精神对项目的成功至关重要。

例句：The co-operativenesses of the various departments allowed for a smooth workflow. 各个部门的协作性使工作流程顺畅。

近义词：collaboration, teamwork, synergy, partnership
反义词：uncooperativeness, antagonism, resistance, obstructionism
词性:名词
