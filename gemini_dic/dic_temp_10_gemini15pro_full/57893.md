drifter:/ˈdrɪftər/| n.|漂流者，流浪汉；漂流物|A person who moves from place to place without a permanent home or job; something that drifts.|He was a drifter, moving from town to town.|近义词：vagabond, wanderer, nomad, rover|反义词: settler, resident|noun

drifter:/ˈdrɪftər/| n.|(尤指植物的)飘落物|A plant part, such as a leaf or seed, that is carried by wind or water.|The beach was covered with drifters after the storm.|近义词: flotsam, jetsam|反义词:|noun
