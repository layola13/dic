maqluba:/mɑːˈkluːbə/
n.
Maqluba (dish)
An Arab dish, mainly Palestinian, consisting of meat, rice and fried vegetables placed in a pot which is flipped upside down when served, hence the name "maqluba" which translates literally as "upside-down".
My grandmother makes the best maqluba. 我祖母做的maqluba最棒。
Synonyms:  upside-down dish, inverted dish

Noun
