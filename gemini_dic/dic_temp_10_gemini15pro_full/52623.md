digests:/daɪˈdʒɛsts/ /dɪˈdʒɛsts/| n. v.|摘要，文摘；消化；吸收|A digest is a shortened version of a piece of writing or a collection of several pieces of writing.  As a verb, it refers to the process of breaking down food in the stomach. |The lawyer prepared a digest of the case for the judge.|近义词：summary, abstract, compendium, condense, absorb, assimilate|反义词:expand, elaborate|noun

digests:/daɪˈdʒɛsts/ /dɪˈdʒɛsts/| v.|消化；吸收|To digest something means to break down food in the stomach or to understand and absorb information. |She couldn't digest the complex scientific theory.|近义词：absorb, assimilate, comprehend, process|反义词:excrete|verb
