cliquish: /ˈkliːkɪʃ/ | adj. | 小集团的；排外的；派系的 | Tending to form or associate in small exclusive groups |  High school can be cliquish, making it hard for new students to fit in.  高中可能会很排外，让新生很难融入。|  She found the office atmosphere rather cliquish and unfriendly. 她觉得办公室气氛很小团体化，很不友好。| 近义词: exclusive, clannish, insular | 反义词: inclusive, welcoming, open | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From clique + -ish.  Ultimately from French *cliquer "to click, clack."
