missang: /mɪˈsæŋ/ | n. |念珠，玫瑰经| A string of beads used for counting prayers, especially in the Roman Catholic Church. | She fingered the missang as she prayed. 她祈祷时拨弄着念珠。 | 近义词: rosary, chaplet | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

词源: From French *missange*, alteration of *messaing*, from Old French *messe* "mass."
