disembogue:/ˌdɪsɪmˈboʊɡ/ /ˌdɪsɛmˈbəʊɡ/| v. |流入，注入，排放|To flow or empty (as a river or stream) into a larger body of water, such as a lake, sea, or ocean.|The Amazon River disembogues into the Atlantic Ocean. 亚马逊河流入大西洋。

The Mississippi River disembogues into the Gulf of Mexico. 密西西比河流入墨西哥湾。


|近义词：discharge, empty, flow, drain|反义词: - |词性：动词
