inexhaustedly:/ˌɪnɪɡˈzɔːstɪdli/
adverb
不知疲倦地，不屈不挠地
in a way that does not become exhausted; tirelessly.
He worked inexhaustedly on the project for months. 他几个月来不知疲倦地致力于这个项目。
The team searched inexhaustedly for the missing child.  救援队不知疲倦地搜寻失踪的孩子。
近义词:tirelessly, relentlessly, unflaggingly, persistently
反义词: wearily, exhaustedly, tiredly
词性: adverb

词源:
From in- "not" + exhaustedly, the adverbial form of exhausted, past participle of exhaust "to use up completely," from Latin exhaustus, past participle of exhaurire "to draw out, drain, empty out, remove completely," from ex- "out" + haurire "to draw, drink."
