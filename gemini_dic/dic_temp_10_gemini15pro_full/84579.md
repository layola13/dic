hammock:/ˈhæmək/| n. |吊床|A hanging bed or couch, usually made of canvas or netting, suspended between two points.|She relaxed in the hammock, reading a book. 她躺在吊床上看书。|例句：The hammock swayed gently in the breeze. 吊床在微风中轻轻摇曳。|近义词：swing, cot|反义词:bed, mattress|词性:noun
Etymology: From Spanish *hamaca*, from Taino *hamaka*.
