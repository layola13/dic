guvs:/ɡʌvz/ | n. | governments (plural) | Governments are responsible for providing services to their citizens. | The guvs are working on a new trade agreement. |  各国政府正在制定一项新的贸易协定。| synonyms: administrations, authorities, regimes | antonyms: anarchy, disorder | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
"Guv" is a shortened, informal form of "governor."  The term "governor" ultimately comes from the Latin word "gubernator," meaning "pilot" or "steersman."  It entered English through Old French "governeur."  The plural, "guvs," simply adds an "s" to the colloquial singular.
