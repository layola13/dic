non-stimulant:/ˌnɒnˈstɪmjʊlənt/ /ˌnɑːnˈstɪmjələnt/
adjective
非兴奋剂的; 非刺激性的
A non-stimulant medication is one that does not contain stimulants.  非兴奋剂药物是不含兴奋剂的药物。
Doctors may prescribe non-stimulant medications for ADHD. 医生可能会为多动症患者开非兴奋剂药物。
近义词: non-addictive, calming
反义词: stimulant, stimulating
词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non "not."
stimulant (noun): From Late Latin stimulant-, stem of stimulans, present participle of stimulare "to prick, urge, incite," from stimulus "goad, prick."
