eloquently: /ˈɛləkwəntli/ | adv. | 雄辩地，流畅地 | In a fluent or persuasive manner. |  She spoke eloquently about the need for social justice. 她雄辩地谈到了社会正义的必要性。

He eloquently defended his client in court. 他在法庭上雄辩地为他的客户辩护。


近义词: articulately, fluently, persuasively, expressively
反义词: inarticulately, haltingly, poorly, vaguely
词性: adverb

词源: From Late Latin *eloquentem, accusative singular masculine of *eloquens, present participle of *eloqui (“to speak out”), from e- (“out”) + loqui (“to speak”).
