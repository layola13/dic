oleander:/ˌoʊliˈændər/ | n. | 夹竹桃 | An evergreen shrub or small tree cultivated for its fragrant white to pink or red flowers. All parts of the plant are poisonous. | The oleander bushes along the driveway were in full bloom.  沿着车道的夹竹桃灌木丛盛开着。| 近义词: rosebay (specifically for Nerium oleander) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific plant) | noun
