budge:/bʌdʒ/| n.|移动; 轻微的变动|A slight movement; a shift.|There hasn't been a budge in the negotiations.  谈判没有取得任何进展。|近义词：shift, movement|反义词:stillness, standstill|名词

词源:
From Old French *boger (compare Walloon bodjî “to stir, to budge”), from Vulgar Latin *bullire “to boil, bubble, seethe," ultimately from Latin bullīre “to boil, bubble”.  The semantic shift from “boil” to “move” may be influenced by the image of something bubbling or agitated.
