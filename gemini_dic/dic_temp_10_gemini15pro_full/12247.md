ascendants:/əˈsɛndənts/ | n. |祖先，祖宗；先人；上升星座| People who are related to you and who lived a long time ago, especially your grandparents, great-grandparents, etc.  A person or animal's ascendant is a person or animal related to it that lived in the past.  In astrology, your ascendant is the sign of the zodiac that was rising in the east at the moment you were born.| My ascendants came from Ireland. 我的祖先来自爱尔兰。| synonyms: ancestors, forebears, forefathers, progenitors | antonyms: descendants, offspring | noun

ascendant:/əˈsɛndənt/ | adj. | 上升的；占优势的；有影响力的 | Becoming more powerful or important; Rising. | The ascendant political party is gaining more support.  这个新兴的政党正在获得越来越多的支持。 The sun is ascendant in the morning sky. 太阳在早晨的天空中冉冉升起。| synonyms: rising, increasing, growing, dominant, influential | antonyms: declining, decreasing, falling, subordinate | adjective
