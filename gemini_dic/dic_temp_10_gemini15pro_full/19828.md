bimuscular:/baɪˈmʌskjʊlər/
adjective
双肌的；有两块肌肉的
Having two muscles; relating to two muscles.
The bimuscular system allows for greater control and precision of movement. 双肌系统允许更大的运动控制和精度。
The surgery repaired his bimuscular flap.  手术修复了他的双肌瓣。
近义词: two-muscled
