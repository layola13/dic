disillusionary:/ˌdɪsɪˈluːʒənəri/
adjective
令人失望的；使幻想破灭的
Causing disillusionment; tending to shatter illusions.
The disillusionary experience of war. 战争令人失望的经历。
近义词:disappointing, disheartening, disillusioning
反义词:encouraging, inspiring, uplifting
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From disillusion + -ary.  Disillusion comes from French désillusionner, from des- "away from" + illusion.
