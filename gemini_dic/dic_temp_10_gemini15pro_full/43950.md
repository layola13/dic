cribellum:/krɪˈbɛləm/
n.
筛疣
a sieve-like plate in front of the spinnerets of some spiders, used to comb out the silk into a hackled band.
The cribellum produces extremely fine fibers that create a woolly texture in the spider's web.  这种蜘蛛的筛疣能产生极其细的纤维，使蜘蛛网呈现出羊毛状的质地。
近义词: spinneret (loosely)
反义词: None
词性: Noun

cribellum:/krɪˈbɛləm/
Etymology: From Latin *cribellum, diminutive of cribrum (“sieve”).
词源：源自拉丁语 *cribellum，cribrum（“筛子”）的小词。
