lobiped:/ˈloʊbɪped/ | n. |瓣蹼足鸟|A bird with lobed feet, such as a grebe or coot.|Grebes and coots are examples of lobipods, using their lobed feet for efficient swimming.  瓣蹼足鸟，例如䴙䴘或骨顶鸡，用它们瓣状的脚有效地游泳。|近义词：grebe, coot|反义词: (no direct antonym, perhaps terrestrial bird) | noun
