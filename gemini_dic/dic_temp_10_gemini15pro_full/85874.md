headbanger:/ˈhedˌbæŋər/| n.| 甩头族；疯狂摇滚乐迷|A person who enjoys heavy metal music and often shakes their head vigorously to the rhythm.|He's a real headbanger; he goes to every metal concert in town. 他是个十足的甩头族，镇上每场金属音乐会他都去。|Synonym: metalhead, rocker, fan, enthusiast|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps) classical music lover, non-fan|noun
