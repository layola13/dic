chisel:/ˈtʃɪzl/| n. v.|凿子，雕刻|A chisel is a tool with a sharp beveled edge used for carving or cutting hard materials such as wood, stone, or metal. As a verb, it means to carve or cut with a chisel.|He used a chisel to shape the wood. 他用凿子来塑形木头。  He chiseled the inscription into the stone. 他把铭文刻在石头上。|近义词:(n.) carving tool, graver, burin (v.) carve, sculpt, engrave|反义词:None|词性:Noun, Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French cisel, from Vulgar Latin *cisellum, from Latin caesellum, diminutive of caedere ("to cut").
