overprogramme:/ˌəʊvəˈprəʊɡræm/ |v.| 过度安排；过量节目 | to schedule too many activities or events. |  They overprogrammed the conference, leaving little time for networking. 他们把会议安排得太满，几乎没有时间进行交流。| overload, overschedule | underprogramme | verb
