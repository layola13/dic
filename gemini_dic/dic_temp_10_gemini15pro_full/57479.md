draftsmen: /ˈdræftsmən/ (US), /ˈdrɑːftsmən/ (UK)| n.| 制图员，绘图员 |A person who makes drawings, especially technical drawings, plans, or designs.| The draftsmen worked diligently on the blueprints for the new skyscraper.  制图员们在新的摩天大楼的蓝图上辛勤工作。| synonyms: draughtsman, designer, drawer, illustrator | antonyms: - | noun

draftsman: /ˈdræftsmən/ (US), /ˈdrɑːftsmən/ (UK)| n.| 起草人 |A person who drafts or writes something, such as a legal document or a speech.|  The draftsman carefully worded the contract to avoid any ambiguity. 起草人仔细地措辞合同，以避免任何歧义。 | synonyms: writer, author, composer | antonyms: - | noun
