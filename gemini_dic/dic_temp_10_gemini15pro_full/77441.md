gawkily:/ˈɡɔːkɪli/| adv.|笨拙地，难看地|In a clumsy, awkward, or ungainly way.  Derived from "gawky," meaning awkward or clumsy, with the suffix "-ly" forming the adverb.|He gawkily stumbled across the stage. 他笨手笨脚地在舞台上绊倒了。|Synonyms: clumsily, awkwardly, ungainly, ineptly|Antonyms: gracefully, elegantly, smoothly|Word type: Adverb

gawky:/ˈɡɔːki/| adj.|笨拙的，难看的|Awkward and clumsy in movement or appearance.  Origin uncertain, possibly related to "gawk," meaning to stare stupidly.|The tall, gawky teenager tripped over his own feet.  这个高个子、笨手笨脚的少年被自己的脚绊倒了。|Synonyms: clumsy, awkward, ungainly, inept|Antonyms: graceful, elegant, coordinated|Word type: Adjective
