indefatigably:/ˌɪndɪˈfætɪɡəbli/
adverb
不知疲倦地
Working or continuing tirelessly.
He worked indefatigably on the project for months.  他不知疲倦地在这个项目上工作了几个月。
She campaigned indefatigably for human rights. 她不知疲倦地为争取人权而奔走。
tirelessly, persistently, relentlessly, unflaggingly
wearily, lazily, listlessly
adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + French defatigable (tiring) < Latin defatigare (to tire out) < de- (down) + fatigare (to weary).
