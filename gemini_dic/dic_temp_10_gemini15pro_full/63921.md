epiphytic:/ˌɛpɪˈfɪtɪk/ | adj. | 附生的 | (of a plant) growing on another plant but not parasitic. |  Many orchids are epiphytic, attaching themselves to tree branches. 许多兰花是附生的，依附在树枝上。 | Example: The epiphytic ferns thrived in the humid rainforest. 这些附生蕨类在潮湿的雨林中茁壮成长。| synonyms: air-plant, aerophytic | antonyms: terrestrial, subterranean | adjective

epiphyte:/ˈɛpɪˌfaɪt/ | n. | 附生植物 | a plant that grows on another plant but is not parasitic. |  Orchids and bromeliads are common epiphytes. 兰花和凤梨是常见的附生植物。 | Example: The tree was covered in epiphytes, creating a miniature hanging garden.  这棵树上覆盖着附生植物，形成了一个微型的空中花园。| synonyms: air-plant, aerophyte | antonyms: terrestrial plant | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *epi- (upon, on) + *phyton (plant)
