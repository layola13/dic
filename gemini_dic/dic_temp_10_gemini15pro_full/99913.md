internationalizes:/ˌɪntərˈnæʃənəlaɪzɪz/
|v.|使国际化|To make something international; to adapt something for international use or markets.|The company internationalizes its products by offering them in multiple languages.  这家公司通过以多种语言提供产品来使其国际化。|
|Example 1: The university is working to internationalize its curriculum. 这所大学正在努力使其课程国际化。|
|Example 2:  They've internationalized their business by opening offices in several countries.  他们通过在几个国家开设办事处，使他们的业务国际化了。|
|Synonym: globalize, universalize|
|Antonym: localize, nationalize|
|Word Type: Verb|
