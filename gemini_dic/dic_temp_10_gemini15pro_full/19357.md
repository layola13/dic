bibliophobia:/ˌbɪblɪəˈfəʊbɪə/ | n. | 惧书症，书籍恐惧症 | An abnormal fear of books. |  Example: His bibliophobia prevented him from entering libraries. 他的惧书症让他无法进入图书馆。

Example:  She suffers from bibliophobia and finds it difficult to even hold a book. 她患有惧书症，甚至拿本书都很困难。 | Near synonyms: book aversion, book anxiety | Antonyms: bibliophilia (love of books) | noun
