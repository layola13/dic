licenser's:/ˈlaɪsənsərz/
n.
许可方的，授权人的
belonging to or associated with the licenser; of the licenser.
The licenser's rights are clearly outlined in the contract. 许可方的权利在合同中明确规定。
近义词: grantor's
反义词: licensee's
词性: possessive noun (所有格名词)
