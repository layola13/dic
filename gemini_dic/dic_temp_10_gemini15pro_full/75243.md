frolicsomenesses: /ˌfrɒlɪksəmnɪsɪz/
noun
嬉戏；欢乐；嬉闹
The quality or state of being frolicsome; playful gaiety.

The puppy's frolicsomenesses were endless, bouncing and tumbling with infectious joy.
小狗的嬉闹无休无止，蹦蹦跳跳，充满了感染力的快乐。

Her youthful frolicsomenesses made her a delight to be around.
她年轻的嬉闹使她成为一个令人愉快的人。

近义词: playfulness, gaiety, merriment, mirth, jollity
反义词: seriousness, solemnity, gloom, dullness
词性: noun

词源：from frolicsome + -ness.  Frolicsome itself comes from frolic (verb and noun), which has uncertain origins, possibly related to Dutch vrolijk (cheerful).
