otherworldly:/ˌʌðərˈwɜːrldli/

超凡脱俗的；非尘世的；天外之境的

例句：Her beauty was almost otherworldly. 她的美貌简直是超凡脱俗。

近义词：unearthly, supernatural, ethereal, spiritual, transcendental
反义词：worldly, earthly, mundane, terrestrial
词性：形容词
