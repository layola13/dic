haliotis:/ˌhælɪˈoʊtɪs/ | n. |鲍鱼|A genus of large edible sea snails with a shallow ear-shaped shell with a row of respiratory holes.|例句：The haliotis is a delicacy in many cultures. 鲍鱼在许多文化中都是一种美味佳肴。

例句：Divers carefully harvested the haliotis from the ocean floor. 潜水员小心翼翼地从海底采集鲍鱼。|近义词：abalone|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a type of shellfish)|词性: Noun
