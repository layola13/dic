informedly:/ɪnˈfɔːrmɪdli/ | adv. | 知情地，见识广博地 | In a way that shows knowledge or understanding; based on information or knowledge. | She spoke informedly about the current political situation. 她对当前的政治局势发表了高明的见解。 | 近义词：knowledgeably, wisely, intelligently | 反义词：ignorantly, foolishly, blindly | 词性：副词

词源:
From "informed" + "-ly."  "Informed" comes from the past participle of the verb "inform," which traces back to Old French "enformer" and ultimately to Latin "informare," meaning "to shape, train, instruct."
