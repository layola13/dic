booming:/ˈbuːmɪŋ/| adj.|繁荣的，迅速发展的；（生意）兴隆的；（声音）深沉响亮的|experiencing a period of great prosperity or rapid economic growth; (of a business) very successful; making a deep resonant sound.|The booming economy created many new jobs. 繁荣的经济创造了许多新的就业机会。|近义词: thriving, flourishing, prosperous, booming, resonant, deep|反义词: declining, failing, weak, quiet, soft|词性:形容词
Etymology: From the verb "boom,"  which originated from imitative origin, referring to the sound of a cannon or explosion.  The meaning extended to a sudden increase or expansion.
