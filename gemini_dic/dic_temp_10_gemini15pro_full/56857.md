dorsiferous:/dɔːrˈsɪfərəs/
adj.
背负的，背上有…的
Bearing or carrying something on the back.
The dorsiferous beetle carried a small twig on its back.  这只背负的甲虫背上扛着一根小树枝。
近义词:  bearing, carrying
反义词:  bare, unburdened
词性: adjective

dorsiferous:/dɔːrˈsɪfərəs/
词源: From Latin *dorsum (back) + -ferous (bearing).
用法: Used to describe something that carries or bears something on its back, particularly in a biological context.  It is often used to describe insects or other invertebrates.
