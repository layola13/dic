helixes:/ˈhiːlɪksiːz/
noun
螺旋线（helix的复数）
A helix is a spiral shape, like a corkscrew or a strand of DNA. Helixes is the plural form of helix.
The DNA molecule is formed by two helixes intertwined. DNA分子由两条相互缠绕的螺旋线组成。

The railing of the spiral staircase formed elegant helixes.  螺旋楼梯的栏杆形成了优雅的螺旋线。
synonym: spirals, coils, whorls
antonym: straight lines
词性: 名词


helix:/ˈhiːlɪks/
