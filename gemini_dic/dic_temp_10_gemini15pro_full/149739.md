palatably:/ˈpælətəbli/ | adv. | 美味地，可口地，合意地 | In a way that is pleasant to taste or acceptable to the mind. | The chef prepared the fish palatably.  厨师把鱼做得美味可口。| Synonyms:  acceptably, agreeably, pleasantly, tastefully | Antonyms: unpleasantly, distastefully, offensively | 词性: 副词

palatably:/ˈpælətəbli/ | adv. | 适口地; 合乎口味地 |  In a way that is easily accepted or tolerated. |  He presented the bad news as palatably as possible. 他尽可能委婉地表达了这个坏消息。 | Synonyms: acceptably, agreeably, pleasantly | Antonyms: unacceptably, disagreeably, unpleasantly | 词性: 副词
