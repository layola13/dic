chuck-out:/ˈtʃʌk aʊt/| n.|丢弃物，废弃物，驱逐|Something that has been discarded or thrown away.  The act of discarding or throwing something away. Also, the act of forcefully removing someone from a place.|例句：The old sofa was a chuck-out.  那张旧沙发被丢掉了。

The chuck-out of the old furniture made space for the new.  丢弃旧家具为新的腾出了空间。|近义词：discard, rubbish, ejection, eviction|反义词：keeper, salvage|词性:名词

chuck-out:/ˈtʃʌk aʊt/| vt.|丢弃，扔掉，驱逐|To discard or throw something away.  To forcefully remove someone from a place.|例句：They chucked out the broken television.  他们把坏掉的电视机扔掉了。

The bouncer chucked him out of the club.  保镖把他赶出了俱乐部。|近义词：discard, jettison, expel, eject|反义词：keep, retain, admit|词性:动词

PHRASE->
chuck something out = 扔掉某物
chuck someone out = 把某人赶出去
get chucked out = 被赶出去
chuck it out = 停止做某事 (通常指停止不当行为)
chuck something out the window = 放弃某事

词源：chuck 源于中古英语 chokken，意为“窒息”，后来演变成“猛推”或“扔”的意思。  out 是一个副词，表示“向外”。
