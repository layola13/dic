outspurt:/ˈaʊtspɜːrt/| n.|突然的迸发，冲刺|A sudden burst of activity, effort, or growth.|He won the race with a dramatic outspurt in the final stretch. 他在最后冲刺阶段突然发力，赢得了比赛。|近义词：burst, spurt, surge|反义词: slowdown, lag, decline|词性:名词
outspurt:/ˈaʊtspɜːrt/| v.|突然发力，冲刺|To show a sudden burst of activity, effort, or growth.|The runner outspurted his rivals in the last few meters.  这名跑步者在最后几米冲刺超过了他的对手。|近义词：outpace, outrun, surpass|反义词: lag behind, fall behind|词性:动词

PHRASE->
a sudden outspurt = 突然的迸发
an outspurt of energy =  能量的迸发
an outspurt of growth =  增长的迸发
an outspurt of activity = 活动的迸发
experience an outspurt =  经历一次迸发


词源：out- + spurt.  "spurt" 意为喷射，涌出。"out-" 则表示超过，胜过。 因此，outspurt 表示比喷射更强烈的动作，即突然的迸发或冲刺。
