enervator:/ˈɛnərveɪtər/| n. |使人衰弱的人或物；泄气的人或事|Something that weakens or depletes vitality. |The constant stress of the job proved to be a powerful enervator.  这份工作持续的压力证明是一种强大的消耗因素。|  The humid heat was an enervator, sapping their strength. 湿热令人衰弱，消耗着他们的体力。|近义词：weaken, exhaust, drain, debilitate, sap|反义词：invigorate, energize, strengthen, fortify|noun
