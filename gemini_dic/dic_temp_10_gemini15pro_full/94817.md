impunctuality:/ˌɪmpʌŋktʃuˈælɪti/
n.
不守时，迟到
lack of punctuality; being late
His impunctuality cost him his job. 他的不守时让他丢了工作。
近义词:lateness, tardiness, untimeliness
反义词:punctuality, promptness
词性:名词

Etymology:
From in- (not) + punctual + -ity.  "Punctual" comes from Late Latin punctum (point), referring to a precise point in time.
