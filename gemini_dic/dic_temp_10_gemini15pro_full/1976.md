activist: /ˈæktɪvɪst/| n.|活动家，积极分子|A person who campaigns to bring about political or social change.|She's a well-known environmental activist. 她是一位著名的环保活动家。

The activists protested against the new law.  这些积极分子抗议新法律。|近义词: campaigner, advocate, reformer|反义词: passivist, bystander|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *activiste*, from activer ‘activate’ + -iste. Ultimately from Latin actus ‘a doing’.

USAGE->
一般现在时:
An activist works tirelessly for their cause. = 一位活动家不知疲倦地为他们的目标工作。
The activist speaks out against injustice. = 这位活动家公开反对不公正。


一般过去时:
The activist organized a protest last week. = 活动家上周组织了一场抗议活动。
The activist was arrested for trespassing. = 活动家因非法侵入被捕。


现在完成时:
The activist has dedicated their life to this cause. = 这位活动家毕生致力于这项事业。
Activists have achieved significant progress in recent years. = 近年来，活动家们取得了重大进展。


过去完成时:
The activist had already left before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，活动家已经离开了。
The activist had been working on this campaign for months. = 这位活动家已经为这场运动工作了几个月。


将来时:
The activist will continue to fight for human rights. = 活动家将继续为争取人权而奋斗。
The activists will hold a press conference tomorrow. = 活动家们明天将举行新闻发布会。


虚拟语气:
If I were an activist, I would fight for climate action. = 如果我是一名活动家，我会为气候行动而战。
I wish the activist could change the world. = 我希望这位活动家能够改变世界。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
