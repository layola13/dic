incomprehensive:/ˌɪnkɑːmprɪˈhensɪv/| adj.| 无法理解的，不全面的 |Not able to be fully grasped or understood; not including all aspects. |The instructions were so incomprehensible that I couldn't assemble the furniture.  说明书太难理解了，我无法组装家具。|近义词：unintelligible, obscure, confusing|反义词: clear, understandable, comprehensive|词性:形容词

Etymology->

From in- (not) + comprehensible (understandable).  Comprehensible comes from Latin comprehendere (to grasp mentally), from com- (together) + prehendere (to seize).
