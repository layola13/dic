caricaturist:/ˌkærɪkəˈtʃʊərɪst/ | n. |漫画家；讽刺画家|An artist who draws caricatures, which are comically exaggerated portraits or depictions of people or things. |例句：The caricaturist at the fair captured her large nose and bright smile perfectly.  这位集市上的漫画家完美地捕捉到了她的大鼻子和灿烂的笑容。

例句：He's a talented caricaturist who can make anyone look both funny and recognizable. 他是一位才华横溢的漫画家，可以让任何人看起来既滑稽又容易辨认。 |近义词：cartoonist, satirist |反义词: portraitist |词性:noun
