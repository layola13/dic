inflating:/ɪnˈfleɪtɪŋ/
v. |膨胀;使充气;抬高(物价);(使)得意|The act of filling something with air or gas, making it larger, or increasing a price or value artificially. |They are inflating the tires. 他们在给轮胎充气。|近义词：expanding, enlarging, pumping up, blowing up, exaggerating|反义词:deflating, contracting, shrinking|词性:动词现在分词

inflating:/ɪnˈfleɪtɪŋ/
adj. |膨胀的;充气的|Being filled with air or gas, or increasing in size or value. |The inflating balloon floated towards the sky.  膨胀的气球飘向天空。|近义词：expanding, swelling|反义词:deflating, shrinking|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *inflāre*, present active infinitive of *inflō*, from *in-* (“in, into”) + *flō* (“blow, breathe”).
