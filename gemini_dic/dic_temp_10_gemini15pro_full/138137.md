northeastwardly:/ˌnɔːrθˈiːstwərdli/
| adv. |向东北|Toward the northeast; in a northeasterly direction.|The ship sailed northeastwardly through the storm. 这艘船向东北方向航行，穿过风暴。|近义词：northeasterly, north by east|反义词:southwestwardly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From northeast + -ward + -ly.  "Northeast" combines "north" and "east," indicating a direction between the two.  "-ward" forms adverbs of direction, and "-ly" further reinforces the adverbial function.
