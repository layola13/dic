noes:/noʊz/| n.|拒绝; 否定; 否决票; 反对票|A negative answer; a refusal.|例句：The proposal received several noes. 这项提议遭到几次否决。|例句：There were more noes than ayes. 反对票多于赞成票。|近义词：refusals, rejections, denials|反义词:ayes, yeses|词性:名词

词源: Middle English *nos, plural of no.
