confusely:/kənˈfjuːzli/ | adv. | 混乱地，迷惑地 | In a way that causes confusion or bewilderment.  | She explained the instructions confusely, leaving everyone unsure of what to do. 她把指示解释得很混乱，让每个人都不知道该做什么。 | Synonyms: bewilderingly, unclearly, vaguely, obscurely | Antonyms: clearly, distinctly, plainly, coherently | 词性: 副词

confuse:/kənˈfjuːz/ | vt. | 使困惑，使糊涂 | To make someone unable to think clearly; to mistake one person or thing for another. | The similar names confused me.  相似的名字把我搞糊涂了。| Synonyms: bewilder, perplex, puzzle, muddle | Antonyms: clarify, enlighten, explain, elucidate | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *confusus, past participle of confundere "to pour together, mix up," from com- "together" + fundere "to pour."
