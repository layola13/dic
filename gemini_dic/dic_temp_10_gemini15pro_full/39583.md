conscript:/ˈkɒnskrɪpt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnskrɪpt/ (US)| adj.|被征召的|Enrolled compulsorily for military service.|例句：Conscript soldiers often lacked the training and experience of regular troops. 被征召的士兵通常缺乏正规部队的训练和经验。|近义词：drafted, enlisted|反义词:volunteer|词性:adj.


Etymology: From Latin *conscriptus*, past participle of *conscribere* "to enroll, enlist," from *com-* "together" + *scribere* "to write."
