bolas:/ˈboʊləs/ (US), /ˈbɒləs/ (UK)| n. |流星锤；捕兽用的流星锤； (南美) 高乔人使用的抛绳|A bolas (also boleadoras or boleadores) is a type of throwing weapon made of weights on the ends of interconnected cords, used to capture animals by entangling their legs.  They are typically constructed with two or three stones or metal balls attached to leather cords.|The gaucho expertly swung the bolas, capturing the runaway calf. 高乔人熟练地挥舞着流星锤，抓住了逃跑的小牛。|Synonyms: boleadoras, boleadores|Antonyms: N/A|noun
