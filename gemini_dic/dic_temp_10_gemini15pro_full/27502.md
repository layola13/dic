The campestrian landscape stretched out before them, mile after mile of flat farmland. 在他们面前，平原的景色绵延数英里，一望无际的平坦农田。|近义词：rural, rustic, commonplace, pedestrian|反义词：urban, sophisticated, imaginative|词性:形容词
