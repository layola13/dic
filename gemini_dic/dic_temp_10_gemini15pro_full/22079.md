bolled:/bəʊld/ | adj. | 去壳的，去荚的，阉割的 | Having had the boll or capsule removed (as cotton); Castrated;  | 例句：The bolled cotton is ready for spinning. 去壳的棉花可以纺纱了。| 近义词：husked, shelled, castrated | 反义词：unbolled, intact | 词性:形容词

词源: From boll (n.) + -ed.  "Boll" refers to the seed pod of a plant, especially cotton or flax.  The suffix "-ed" forms the past participle of verbs, and in this case, creates an adjective meaning "having undergone the process of having the boll removed."
