hydrography:/haɪˈdrɒɡrəfi (British English)/ /haɪˈdrɑːɡrəfi (American English)/
n.|水文学；水道测量学|The science that deals with the description and measurement of the physical features of bodies of water, including oceans, seas, lakes, rivers, and coastal areas. It also encompasses the study of these features' temporal variations.|The hydrography of the region is complex, with numerous small islands and reefs. 这个地区的水文情况很复杂，有很多小岛和礁石。|近义词：hydrology, bathymetry|反义词: None|词性: noun

Etymology:
From French *hydrographie*, from Ancient Greek *ὕδωρ* (*húdōr*, “water”) + *γραφία* (*graphía*, “writing, description”).
