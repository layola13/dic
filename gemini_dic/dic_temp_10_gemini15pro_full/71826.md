fleeting:/ˈfliːtɪŋ/ | adj. | 转瞬即逝的，短暂的 | Passing quickly; ephemeral. |  The fleeting moment of happiness was cherished.  这转瞬即逝的幸福时刻弥足珍贵。 | synonyms: brief, transient, ephemeral, momentary, short-lived | antonyms: enduring, lasting, permanent, prolonged, lengthy | adjective

fleeten: This word is archaic and rarely used in modern English. It means "to pass swiftly," "to vanish," or "to fade away." | v. |  迅速流逝，消散，逐渐消失 |  His youth fleetened away like a dream. 他的青春像梦一样消逝了。  (This is an archaic usage.) | synonyms: vanish, disappear, fade, evaporate | antonyms:  persist, endure, remain | verb
