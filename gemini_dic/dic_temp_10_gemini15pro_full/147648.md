overpollinate: /ˌoʊvərˈpɑːləneɪt/ | v. | 过度授粉 | To pollinate excessively, resulting in reduced fruit or seed set. | The farmer overpollinated his apple orchard, leading to smaller apples than usual. 农民过度授粉了他的苹果园，导致苹果比平常小。| The bees overpollinated the flowers, causing them to wilt prematurely. 蜜蜂过度授粉了花朵，导致它们过早枯萎。 | overfertilize | underpollinate | verb
