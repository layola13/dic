criterial:/kraɪˈtɪəriəl/
| adj.|标准的，准则的|Relating to or serving as a criterion; of the nature of a criterion.  It establishes or serves as a standard by which something is judged or assessed.|The professor explained the criterial attributes of a good research paper. 教授解释了一篇好的研究论文的标准属性。|近义词：standard, normative, decisive|反义词:nonstandard, atypical|词性:adjective
词源:Late 19th century: from criterion + -al.
