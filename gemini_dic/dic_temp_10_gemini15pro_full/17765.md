bedizen:/bɪˈdaɪzən/
verb
装饰；打扮；使炫耀
to dress or decorate gaudily or excessively
The actress was bedizened in jewels.  这位女演员浑身珠光宝气。
近义词: adorn, decorate, ornament, embellish, deck, bedeck
反义词: strip, denude, simplify
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + dizen, from an obsolete verb dizen "to dress," of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old French dise "ten," referring to a set of clothes.
