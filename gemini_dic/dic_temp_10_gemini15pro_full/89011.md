hollowed:/ˈhɒləʊd/ (UK) /ˈhɑːloʊd/ (US)| adj.|空的，掏空的，凹陷的|Having a hole or empty space inside; made hollow.  From the verb "to hollow," meaning to make something hollow or empty.|The tree had a hollowed trunk where animals could shelter. 这棵树有一个掏空的树干，动物可以在里面躲避。|Synonyms: concave, indented, excavated, sunken|Antonyms: convex, solid, filled, full|adjective

hollowed:/ˈhɒləʊd/ (UK) /ˈhɑːloʊd/ (US)| v.|挖空，掏空|Past participle of the verb "to hollow," meaning to make something hollow or empty.|The wind and rain had hollowed out the cliff face. 风雨侵蚀掏空了悬崖表面。|Synonyms: excavate, gouge, scoop|Antonyms: fill, pack, stuff|verb
