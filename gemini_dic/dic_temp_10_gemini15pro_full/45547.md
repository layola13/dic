curiousness:/ˈkjʊəriəsnəs/| n. |好奇心，求知欲|The state of being curious; inquisitiveness.|Her curiousness led her to explore the abandoned house. 她的好奇心驱使她去探索那栋废弃的房子。|近义词：inquisitiveness, curiosity, interest|反义词：apathy, indifference|词性:名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From curious + -ness.  "Curious" traces back to the Latin "curiosus," meaning "careful, inquisitive, meticulous." The suffix "-ness" is used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
