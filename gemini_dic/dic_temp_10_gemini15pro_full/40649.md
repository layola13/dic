conventioneered:/kənˈvɛnʃənɪrd/
verb
参加会议；担任会议代表
acted as a delegate at a convention or meeting.
Example: He conventioneered at the annual marketing conference.  他参加了年度营销会议。

Example:  She has conventioneered for several years, building a strong network. 她已经参加会议好几年了，建立了一个强大的关系网。

近义词: attended, participated, conferred
反义词:  abstained, boycotted
词性: verb
