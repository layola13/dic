flotsam:/ˈflɒtsəm/ (UK), /ˈflɑːtsəm/ (US)| n. |漂浮物，废料，(尤指)海上漂流的货物、残骸| wreckage of a ship or its cargo found floating on the sea or washed ashore, especially after a shipwreck | The beach was littered with flotsam and jetsam after the storm.  暴风雨过后，海滩上到处都是漂浮物和垃圾。| 近义词：debris, wreckage, jetsam | 反义词: N/A | noun
