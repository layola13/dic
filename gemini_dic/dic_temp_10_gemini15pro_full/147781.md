overpromptly: /ˌoʊvərˈprɒmptli/ | adv. | 过于迅速地，过分及时地 |  Done with excessive promptness; too quickly or readily.  | He responded overpromptly, almost interrupting the question. 他回答得太快了，几乎打断了提问。 | She overpromptly agreed to help, later regretting her impulsive decision. 她过于迅速地答应帮忙，后来后悔自己冲动的决定。 | 近义词: too quickly, hastily, prematurely | 反义词: slowly, tardily, late | 词性: 副词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "promptly" (adverb form of "prompt," from Latin "promptus" meaning "ready").
