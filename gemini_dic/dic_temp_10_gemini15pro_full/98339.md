insinuatingness:/ɪnˈsɪnjuːeɪtɪŋnɪs/
n.
暗示性；含蓄性；曲折性
The quality of being insinuating; subtly suggesting something, often something unpleasant or disapproving.
His insinuatingness made her uncomfortable; she felt he was implying something she couldn't quite grasp. 他的暗示性让她感到不舒服；她觉得他在暗示一些她无法完全理解的事情。
insinuation, suggestiveness, implication
directness, frankness
noun
