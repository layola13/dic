beta-tester:/ˈbeɪtə ˈtɛstər/| n.|测试员，试用员|A person who tests a pre-release version of a product (especially software) to identify bugs and provide feedback.|例句：The beta-testers found several bugs in the new software. 测试员在新软件中发现了一些错误。

例句：We rely on beta-testers to help us improve our products. 我们依靠测试员来帮助我们改进产品。|近义词：trial user, early adopter, pre-release tester|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
