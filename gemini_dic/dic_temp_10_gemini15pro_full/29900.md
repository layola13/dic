cauterizing:/ˈkɔːtəraɪzɪŋ/
v.
烧灼; 烙
The process of burning tissue to stop bleeding or prevent infection.
The doctor cauterized the wound to stop the bleeding. 医生烧灼伤口以止血。
synonyms: burning, searing, scorching
antonyms: healing, soothing
verb

cauterize:/ˈkɔːtəraɪz/
v.
烧灼; 烙
To burn tissue to stop bleeding or prevent infection.
The wound was cauterized to prevent infection.  伤口被烧灼以防止感染。
synonyms: burn, sear, scorch
antonyms: heal, soothe
verb

cautery:/ˈkɔːtəri/
n.
烧灼术; 烙术
An instrument or method used to burn tissue.
The doctor used a cautery to stop the bleeding. 医生用烧灼器止血。
synonyms: burning, searing, branding
antonyms: N/A
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *cauterizare, from Latin cauterium, from Ancient Greek καυτήριον (kautḗrion, “branding iron”), from καίω (kaíō, “I burn”).
