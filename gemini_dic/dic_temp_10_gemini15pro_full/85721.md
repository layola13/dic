hawaiians:/həˈwaɪjənz/
noun
夏威夷人
People who are native to or live in the Hawaiian Islands.
Many Hawaiians are proud of their Polynesian heritage. 许多夏威夷人为他们的波利尼西亚血统感到自豪。
