ideologists:/ˌaɪdiˈɒlədʒɪsts/
n.
思想家；理论家；意识形态倡导者
An ideologist is someone who strongly believes in a particular set of ideas and principles, especially political or economic ones, and often tries to persuade other people to accept them.
The ideologists of the party believed in a strict interpretation of their core principles. 该党的理论家们坚持对核心原则的严格解释。

The new CEO is less of an ideologist and more of a pragmatist. 新任首席执行官与其说是一位理论家，不如说是一位实用主义者。

Synonyms: theorist, doctrinaire, proponent, advocate
Antonyms: pragmatist, realist

词性: noun
