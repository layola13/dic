clouded:/ˈklaʊdɪd/ /ˈklaʊdɪd/ | adj. | 阴天的，多云的；模糊的，不清楚的 | Covered with clouds; obscured, unclear. |  The sky was clouded and gray. 天空阴沉沉的，灰蒙蒙的。|  His judgment was clouded by anger.  他的判断力被愤怒蒙蔽了。| 近义词：overcast, hazy, obscure, murky | 反义词：clear, sunny, bright | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From cloud + -ed.  源自名词 cloud（云）加上形容词后缀 -ed。
