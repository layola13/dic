beefy:/ˈbiːfi/| adj.|强壮的，肌肉发达的;|Having a strong, muscular body; brawny.|He was a beefy man with a loud voice. 他是一个有着洪亮嗓音的强壮男人。|近义词：muscular, brawny, burly, husky, strapping|反义词：scrawny, skinny, thin, slight|词性:形容词
词源：From beef + -y.  Originally referred to cattle, later extended to describe people resembling the qualities of well-fed cattle (strength and size).
