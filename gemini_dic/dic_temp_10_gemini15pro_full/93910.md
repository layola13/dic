immovable:/ɪˈmuːvəbl/| adj.|不可移动的，固定的，不动产的|Not able to be moved, either physically or emotionally.|The statue appeared immovable, firmly rooted to its pedestal. 这座雕像似乎一动不动，牢牢地固定在底座上.| The judge was immovable in his decision. 法官的决定不可更改。 |synonyms: fixed, steadfast, unyielding, resolute, inflexible|antonyms: movable, mobile, flexible, yielding|adjective


immovable:/ɪˈmuːvəbl/| n.|不动产|Property, especially land or buildings, that cannot be moved.|The family's immovables included a large house and several acres of land.  这个家庭的不动产包括一栋大房子和几英亩土地。|  He invested heavily in immovables. 他在不动产方面投入了大量资金。|synonyms: real estate, property, land|antonyms: movables, chattels|noun
