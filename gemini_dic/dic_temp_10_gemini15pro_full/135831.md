nonpropitiable:/ˌnɒnproʊˈpɪʃiəbl/
adjective
不可 appeased 的；无法平息的；难以和解的
Not capable of being propitiated; unable to be appeased or appeased.
His nonpropitiable wrath made all who knew him tremble.  他难以平息的愤怒使所有认识他的人都感到害怕。
The dictator's nonpropitiable fury led to the execution of many political prisoners.  独裁者难以和解的愤怒导致许多政治犯被处决。
近义词:implacable, unappeasable, relentless, inexorable
反义词:appeasable, placable, forgiving, merciful
词性:adjective
词源:From non- + propitiable, from Late Latin propitiabilis, from propitiare “to appease,” from propitius "favorable."  由non- + propitiable构成，来自晚期拉丁语propitiabilis，源于propitiare “平息”，来自propitius “有利的”。
