indeterministic:/ˌɪndɪˌtɜːrmɪˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 不确定的，非决定论的 | Not deterministic; having a random or unpredictable element; subject to chance. |  The outcome of this experiment is indeterministic, depending on numerous uncontrolled factors.  这个实验的结果是不确定的，取决于许多不可控因素。| The future of the market is indeterministic and difficult to predict.  市场的未来是不确定的，难以预测。 | Synonyms: random, stochastic, probabilistic, unpredictable, nondeterministic | Antonyms: deterministic, predictable, certain | adjective
