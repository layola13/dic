foretime:/ˈfɔːrtaɪm/| n.| 往昔，过去 |A time long past. |例句：In the foretime, people traveled by horse and carriage. 在过去，人们乘坐马车旅行。|例句：Legends speak of magical creatures from the foretime.  传说讲述了来自远古时代的魔法生物。|近义词：past, olden days, antiquity, yesteryear |反义词:future, present, modern times|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *foretime, from Old English *fōrtima, from fōr (before) + tīma (time).
