bothering:/ˈbɒðərɪŋ/ (UK), /ˈbɑːðərɪŋ/ (US)| v. |打扰，烦扰，使…不安|The noise was bothering me, so I asked them to turn it down. 噪音打扰到我了，所以我让他们把它关小点。|近义词：annoying, disturbing, troubling|反义词：pleasing, soothing|verb (present participle)

bother:/ˈbɒðər/ (UK), /ˈbɑːðər/ (US)| n. v.|麻烦，打扰，费心| I don't want to bother you with my problems. 我不想用我的问题来打扰你。|近义词：trouble, inconvenience, annoyance|反义词：ease, comfort, peace|noun, verb
