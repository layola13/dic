cupping:/ˈkʌpɪŋ/| n.|拔罐；杯状；成杯形|a therapy in which heated glass cups are applied to the skin along the meridians of the body, creating suction|例句：Cupping is a traditional Chinese medicine therapy. 拔罐是一种传统中医疗法。

例句：The athlete underwent cupping therapy to relieve muscle soreness.  这位运动员接受了拔罐疗法来缓解肌肉酸痛。|近义词：acupuncture, moxibustion|反义词: None|词性:noun
