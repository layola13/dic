parsimoniousness:/ˌpɑːrsɪˈməʊniəsnəs/ |n.|吝啬，节俭|The state of being unwilling to spend money or use resources; stinginess. |His parsimoniousness was so extreme that he reused tea bags.| 近义词: stinginess, frugality, thrift, miserliness |反义词: generosity, extravagance, lavishness| noun

parsimonious:/ˌpɑːrsɪˈməʊniəs/ |adj.|吝啬的，节俭的|Unwilling to spend money or use resources; stingy. |She is parsimonious in her spending habits.| 近义词: stingy, frugal, thrifty, miserly |反义词: generous, extravagant, lavish| adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin parsimonia, from Latin parcus ("sparing").
