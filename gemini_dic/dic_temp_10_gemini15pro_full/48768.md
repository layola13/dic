deflagrator:/ˈdefləˌɡreɪtər/ | n. | 爆燃器，燃烧器 | A device used to cause deflagration (rapid burning). |  The chemist used a deflagrator to ignite the mixture.  化学家使用爆燃器点燃混合物。 | synonyms: igniter, combustor | antonyms: extinguisher, suppressant | noun

deflagrator:/ˈdefləˌɡreɪtər/ | n. | 引爆装置 | A device or substance that initiates deflagration. | The deflagrator triggered the explosion in the controlled experiment.  在受控实验中，引爆装置引发了爆炸。 | synonyms: detonator, primer | antonyms: inhibitor, retardant | noun
