corvette:/kɔːrˈvet/| n.|小型护卫舰，轻型跑车|A corvette is a small, fast warship. It is smaller than a frigate and larger than a patrol boat. /  A Corvette is also a sports car manufactured by Chevrolet.|The corvette shadowed the larger vessel. 小型护卫舰跟踪着那艘大船。/ He just bought a brand new Corvette. 他刚买了一辆全新的科尔维特跑车。|近义词：(warship) frigate, destroyer, sloop (car) sports car, roadster|反义词:(warship) battleship, aircraft carrier|noun
