muconate:/ˈmjuːkəneɪt/ /ˈmjuːkənət/| n. |粘康酸盐；粘酸盐|A salt or ester of muconic acid.  A dicarboxylic acid formed by the oxidation of benzene or phenol.|Muconate is a byproduct of benzene degradation in the body.  粘康酸盐是人体内苯降解的副产物。|Synonyms: salt of muconic acid |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific chemical compound) | noun
