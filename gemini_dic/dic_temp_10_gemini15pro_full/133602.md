nonfluent:/nɒnˈfluːənt/ /nɑːnˈfluːənt/| adj.|不流利的，不熟练的|Not speaking or writing a language easily or well.|She is nonfluent in Spanish, but she can understand it. 她西班牙语说不流利，但她能听懂。|近义词：halting, hesitant, faltering, inarticulate|反义词:fluent, articulate, eloquent|词性:形容词

nonfluency:/nɒnˈfluːənsi/ /nɑːnˈfluːənsi/| n.|不流利，不熟练|The lack of fluency in a language.|His nonfluency in English made it difficult for him to communicate. 他英语不流利，使他难以沟通。|近义词：hesitancy, faltering|反义词:fluency, eloquence|词性:名词

词源: non- (not) + fluent (flowing smoothly)
