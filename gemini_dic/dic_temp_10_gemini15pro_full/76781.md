gamier:/ˈɡeɪmiər/| adj.|更刺激的，更有野味的，更腐败的|Having a strong, pungent flavor or smell, often associated with game meat that has been hung; more risky or corrupt.|The pheasant was gamier than the chicken.  野鸡比鸡肉更有野味。| The politics in that city are getting gamier by the day.  那个城市的政治一天比一天腐败。|近义词：stronger, richer, more pungent, riskier, shadier|反义词:milder, blander|词性:形容词
词源：From game (wild animal hunted for food) + -ier (comparative suffix).  来源于game（猎取食物的野生动物）+ -ier（比较级后缀）。
