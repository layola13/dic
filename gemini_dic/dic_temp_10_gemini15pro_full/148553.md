overswinging:/ˌəʊvəˈswɪŋɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度摇摆的; 过度反应的 | Swinging excessively; reacting too strongly. | The pendulum was overswinging, creating a dizzying effect. 钟摆过度摇摆，产生了令人眩晕的效果。| The market overswung in response to the news, causing a sudden crash.  市场对这一消息反应过度，导致突然崩盘。|近义词：overreacting, overcompensating |反义词: underreacting, undercompensating | 词性: 形容词

overswing:/ˌəʊvəˈswɪŋ/ | n. | 过度摇摆; 过度反应 | An excessive swing or reaction. | The overswing of the crane caused the load to topple. 起重机的过度摇摆导致负载倾覆。| There was a significant overswing in public opinion after the scandal.  丑闻发生后，公众舆论出现了明显的过度反应。| 近义词：overreaction, overcompensation |反义词: underreaction, undercompensation | 词性: 名词

overswing:/ˌəʊvəˈswɪŋ/ | v. | 过度摇摆; 过度反应 | To swing excessively; to react too strongly. | The share prices overswung after the announcement.  股价在公告发布后过度波动。| He tends to overswing when faced with criticism. 面对批评时，他往往反应过度。|近义词：overreact, overcompensate |反义词: underreact, undercompensate | 词性: 动词
