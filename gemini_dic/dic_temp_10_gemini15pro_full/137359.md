nonteachablenesses:/ˌnɒnˌtiːtʃəˈbɪlnəsɪz/
n.
不可教性；无法被教导的状态
The state or quality of being unteachable or incapable of being taught.
His nonteachablenesses frustrated the tutor. 他的不可教性让导师感到沮丧。
non-educability, unreceptiveness, indocility, resistance to learning
teachability, educability, receptiveness, docility
noun
