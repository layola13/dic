honoris:/hɒˈnɔːrɪs/ |adj.|荣誉的，名誉的|Latin word meaning "of honor."  Used in academic contexts, often for honorary degrees or titles awarded to someone who has made significant contributions but without fulfilling the usual requirements.  It signifies respect and recognition.|例句：He received an honoris causa doctorate from the university. 他获得了大学的荣誉博士学位。

例句：She was given the title of Professor honoris causa. 她被授予荣誉教授的头衔。|近义词：honorary, titular|反义词：earned, substantive|词性: adjective
