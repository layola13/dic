corallike:/ˈkɔːrəlˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 珊瑚似的，像珊瑚的 | Resembling coral in appearance or form. | The intricate, corallike formations were breathtaking.  这些错综复杂的珊瑚状构造令人叹为观止。 | The corallike structure of the sponge was fascinating. 海绵的珊瑚状结构很吸引人。| 近义词: coral-shaped, coralline | 反义词: (No direct antonym, consider smooth, flat) | 词性: 形容词

词源:  coral + like. "Coral" comes from Old French "coral" and Latin "corallium," ultimately from Greek "korallion." "Like" comes from Old English "gelic."
