concentrative:/ˈkɒnsəntrətɪv/ (US) /ˈkɑːnsəntreɪtɪv/| adj.|专心的，专注的|Tending to concentrate; concentrating.|She has a concentrative mind, ideal for research work. 她心思专注，非常适合做研究工作。|近义词:focused, attentive, absorbed|反义词:distracted, inattentive|词性:形容词|词源:From concentrate + -ive. Concentrate itself comes from Late Latin concentratus, past participle of concentrare "to unite in a center," from com- "together" + centrum "center."
