caving:/ˈkeɪvɪŋ/| n.|洞穴探险；洞穴探险运动|The exploration of caves, especially as a recreational activity.  Exploring caves, often for recreation.|例句：They went caving in the mountains last weekend.  他们上周末去山里探洞了。

例句：Caving can be a dangerous activity if you're not properly equipped.  如果没有合适的装备，洞穴探险可能是一项危险的活动。|近义词：spelunking, potholing|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "surface exploration")|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "cave" + "-ing" suffix.  "Cave" originates from the Old French "cave" (feminine of "caf," meaning "hollow"), ultimately from the Latin "cavus" meaning "hollow."
