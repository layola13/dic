gluepot:/ˈɡluːpɒt/ (UK) /ˈɡluːpɑːt/ (US)| n. |胶壶；困境；混乱|A container for holding glue. Also figuratively used to describe a difficult situation or a mess.|例句：The project became a complete gluepot due to poor planning.  这个项目由于计划不周而变得一团糟。

例句：He kept his gluepot in the shed. 他把胶壶放在棚子里。|近义词：mess, muddle, predicament, fix|反义词: order, organization|词性:noun

Etymology:  From 'glue' + 'pot'.  The figurative meaning likely arose from the idea of being stuck in a sticky situation, like being caught in glue.
