grouch:/ɡraʊtʃ/ | n. vi. | 脾气坏的人；抱怨；抱怨 | A habitually grumpy person; to complain or grumble. |  He's such a grouch in the mornings. 他早上脾气真坏。|  He grouched about the cold weather. 他抱怨天气冷。 | 近义词: grumble, complain, whine, sulk | 反义词:  cheer, rejoice | 词性: noun, intransitive verb

grouch:/ɡraʊtʃ/ | adj. | 脾气坏的；爱抱怨的 | Grumpy or complaining. |  He gave me a grouchy look. 他给了我一个不悦的眼神。 | 近义词: grumpy, bad-tempered, irritable | 反义词:  cheerful, good-natured | 词性: adjective
