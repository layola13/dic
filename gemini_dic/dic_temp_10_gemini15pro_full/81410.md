grassless:/ˈɡrɑːsləs/
adjective
无草的，不长草的
lacking grass; having no grass
The landscape was barren and grassless.  这片土地贫瘠荒芜，寸草不生。
The dusty, grassless field baked under the scorching sun.  尘土飞扬、寸草不生的田地在烈日下烘烤。
近义词：bare, barren, treeless
反义词：grassy, verdant, lush
词性：adjective
