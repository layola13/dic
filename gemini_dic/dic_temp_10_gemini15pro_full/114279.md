maining: /ˈmeɪnɪŋ/
adjective|剩余的，剩下的|remaining means something that is left after other things have been used, taken away, or dealt with. |Only a few tickets remain. 只剩下几张票了。|近义词:left, leftover, residual|反义词:used, gone, complete|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Middle English remainant, remayning, from Anglo-Norman remaindre, Old French remaindre, from Latin remanēre (“to remain, stay behind”), present active infinitive of remaneo, from re- (“back, again”) + maneō (“stay, remain”).
