ensue:/ɪnˈsuː/
v.
发生，接着发生，因而产生

The police officer pulled the driver over, and a high-speed chase ensued. 警察拦下了司机，随后发生了一场高速追逐。
After the heavy rains, flooding ensued.  暴雨过后，发生了洪水。
follow, result, succeed
precede
