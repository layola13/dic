ligated:/ˈlaɪɡeɪtɪd/
adjective
结扎的，绑扎的
tied or bound off, as a blood vessel or duct
The surgeon ligated the artery during the operation.  外科医生在手术期间结扎了动脉。
近义词: tied, bound, fastened
反义词: untied, unbound, loosened
词性: adjective

ligated:/ˈlaɪɡeɪtɪd/
verb
结扎，绑扎 (past participle of ligate)
to tie or bind off, as a blood vessel or duct
The bleeding vessel was quickly ligated.  出血的血管很快被结扎了。
近义词: tied, bound, fastened
反义词: untied, unbound, loosened
词性: verb (past participle)


词源: From Latin *ligatus, past participle of ligare (“to bind, tie”).
