nonclarification:/ˌnɒnˌklærɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 未澄清，未说明 | The lack of nonclarification led to further confusion. 缺乏说明导致了进一步的混乱。|  The committee's report offered no nonclarification on the budget discrepancies. 委员会的报告没有对预算差异做出任何澄清。 | 近义词: ambiguity, obscurity, vagueness | 反义词: clarification, explanation, elucidation | 词性: noun

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + clarification (noun from clarify, meaning "to make clear")
