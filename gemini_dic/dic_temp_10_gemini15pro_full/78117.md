geomagnetic:/ˌdʒiːəʊmæɡˈnɛtɪk/
adjective
地磁的
Relating to the earth's magnetism.
The geomagnetic field protects the earth from harmful solar radiation.  地球磁场保护地球免受有害的太阳辐射。
近义词: terrestrial magnetic
反义词: None
