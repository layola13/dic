competitiveness:/kəmˈpɛtɪtɪvnəs/| n. |竞争力；竞争性|The state or quality of being competitive. The ability of a business, a country, or a person to compete successfully against rivals.|例句：The company's competitiveness has improved significantly in recent years.  这家公司的竞争力近年来显著提高。

例句：The country's competitiveness in the global market is a key concern for the government.  该国在全球市场的竞争力是政府关注的一个关键问题。|近义词：rivalry, competition, ability|反义词:incompetence, weakness|词性:noun
