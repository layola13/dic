metacinnabar:/ˌmɛtəˈsɪnəˌbɑːr/ /ˌmɛtəˈsɪnəˌbɑr/
noun
黑辰砂，β-硫化汞
a black, cubic crystalline form of mercury sulfide, HgS.
Metacinnabar is a less common mineral than cinnabar (the red form of mercury sulfide). 黑辰砂是一种比辰砂（红色的硫化汞）少见的矿物。
Metacinnabar occurs in hydrothermal veins and hot springs deposits. 黑辰砂出现在热液矿脉和温泉沉积物中。
Synonym:  black cinnabar, beta-mercury sulfide
Antonym: cinnabar (in terms of color and crystal structure)
n.
