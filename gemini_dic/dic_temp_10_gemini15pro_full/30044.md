CBSS:/ˌsiː biː ɛs ɛs/| n. |哥伦比亚广播公司 (Columbia Broadcasting System)| An American commercial broadcast television and radio network. It is sometimes referred to as the Eye Network, in reference to the company's iconic eye logo, or simply CBS.  It is one of the "Big Three" television networks in the United States. | CBS aired the Super Bowl this year. 今年超级碗由哥伦比亚广播公司转播。 |Synonyms: network, broadcaster, television station|Antonyms: None|Abbreviation
