cliffhanger:/ˈklɪfˌhæŋər/| n.|悬念，扣人心弦的结尾|A cliffhanger is a plot device in fiction that features a main character in a precarious or difficult dilemma, or confronted with a shocking revelation at the end of an episode or serialized fiction.  |The movie ended on a cliffhanger, leaving the audience eager for the sequel.  这部电影以扣人心弦的方式结尾，让观众渴望看到续集。|Synonyms: suspense, climax, turning point|Antonyms: resolution, conclusion|noun
