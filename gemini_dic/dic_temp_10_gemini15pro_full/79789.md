gluttonous:/ˈɡlʌtənəs/| adj.|贪吃的；贪婪的；暴食的|Excessively greedy; given to overindulgence in food or drink.  Characterized by or exhibiting gluttony. |He has a gluttonous appetite for both food and information. 他对食物和信息都有着贪婪的胃口。|Synonyms: voracious, ravenous, insatiable, greedy, rapacious|Antonyms: abstemious, temperate, moderate|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *gluttonosus, from Latin gluttio (“I gulp down, swallow”), from glutire (“to swallow”), from Proto-Indo-European *gʷel- (“to swallow”).
