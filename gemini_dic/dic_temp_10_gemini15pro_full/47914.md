decentralizer:/diːˈsɛntrəlaɪzər/ | n. | 去中心化者；分散化装置；分权者 |  A person or thing that decentralizes something, especially power or control. | The internet has acted as a decentralizer of information. 互联网已经成为信息去中心化的工具。| synonyms: distributor, disperser | antonyms: centralizer, concentrator | noun


decentralize:/diːˈsɛntrəlaɪz/ | v. | 去中心化；分散；权力下放 | To transfer (authority) from central to local government. To distribute or disperse from a centre. |  The company decided to decentralize its operations. 公司决定将其运营分散化。| synonyms: distribute, disperse, devolve | antonyms: centralize, concentrate | verb
