outbribed:/ˌaʊtˈbraɪbd/
verb
用贿赂胜过
To surpass or defeat someone in bribery; to bribe more effectively than another.
He outbribed his rivals to secure the contract. 他用贿赂击败了他的竞争对手，从而获得了合同。
outbid, outmaneuver
