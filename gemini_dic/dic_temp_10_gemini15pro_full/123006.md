monarchize:/ˈmɒnəkɪzaɪz/ | v. | 使成为君主; 君主化; 以君主身份统治 | To make someone a monarch; to establish a monarchy. |  The general attempted to monarchize the newly formed republic.  将军试图将新成立的共和国君主化。

He sought to monarchize the entire region under his rule. 他试图将整个地区君主化，置于他的统治之下。 | 近义词: crown, enthrone, reign | 反义词: democratize, depose | 词性: 动词

词源: From monarch + -ize.  来自 monarch（君主）+ -ize（动词后缀，表示“使成为…”）。
