dawdled:/ˈdɔːdld/ (US) /ˈdɔːdld/ (UK)| v. |闲逛，磨蹭，拖延|To waste time; to spend time idly; to move slowly and lackadaisically.|例句：He dawdled over his breakfast, reading the newspaper. 他慢吞吞地吃着早餐，读着报纸。|例句：She dawdled on her way to school and arrived late.  她在上学的路上磨磨蹭蹭，结果迟到了。|近义词：linger, loiter, tarry, delay, procrastinate|反义词:hurry, rush, hasten|词性:动词

词源:
From Middle English *dadlen, probably frequentative of *daden (“to play, sport, be foolish”), probably from Old Norse *dæða (“to amuse oneself, play”).
