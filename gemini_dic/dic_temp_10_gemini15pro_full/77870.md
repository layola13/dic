genetically:/dʒəˈnɛtɪkli/
adverb
基因地，遗传地
in a way that relates to genes or genetics
例句：Genetically modified foods are a subject of debate.  转基因食品是一个备受争议的话题。

例句：The twins are genetically identical. 这对双胞胎基因相同。
近义词：hereditarily, congenitally
反义词: environmentally
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From genetic + -ally.  Genetic comes from the Greek word "genesis" meaning "origin" or "creation."
