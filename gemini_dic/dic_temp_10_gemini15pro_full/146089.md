overeducative:/ˌoʊvərˈɛdʒʊkeɪtɪv/ | adj. | 过度教育的；教育程度过高的 | Providing more education than is necessary or useful for a particular job or situation. |  He felt the job market was overeducative, requiring a master's degree for entry-level positions.  他觉得就业市场过度教育化，入门级职位都需要硕士学位。 | 近义词: overqualified, excessively educated | 反义词: undereducated, underqualified | 词性: 形容词
