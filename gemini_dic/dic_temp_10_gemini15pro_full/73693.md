forgivenesses:/fərˈɡɪvnəsɪz/
n.
宽恕，原谅（复数）
The act of forgiving someone or something.  The state of being forgiven.
He asked for forgiveness for his mistakes. 他请求原谅他的错误。
The family finally found forgiveness after years of pain.  这家人在多年的痛苦之后终于得到了宽恕。
近义词:pardons, absolutions, remissions
反义词:vengeance, retribution, resentment
词性:名词
