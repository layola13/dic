gurdwara:/ˈɡʊrdwɑːrə/ (US), /ˈɡʊədwɑːrə/ (UK)| n. |锡克教谒师所，庙宇|A place of worship for Sikhs. It literally means "the Guru's door".|例句：We went to the gurdwara to pray. 我们去谒师所祈祷。

|例句：The gurdwara serves free meals to everyone, regardless of religion. 谒师所向所有人提供免费餐食，不分宗教。|近义词：temple, shrine|反义词:|noun
