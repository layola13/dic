basketwork:/ˈbɑːskɪtwɜːrk/ /ˈbæskɪtwɜːrk/
n.
编织物；柳条制品；篮子工艺
Articles made of interwoven twigs, reeds, or other flexible material.
The picnic basket was a beautiful piece of basketwork.  这个野餐篮子是一件漂亮的编织品。
近义词: wickerwork, wattlework
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "solid construction" or "metalwork" as contrasting concepts)
词性: 名词
