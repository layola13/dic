leatherette:/ˌleðəˈret/
n.
人造革，仿皮
An imitation leather, typically made by coating a fabric base with a plastic.
This sofa is made of a durable leatherette.  这沙发是用耐用的人造革制成的。
近义词：faux leather, imitation leather, synthetic leather, vinyl
反义词：genuine leather, real leather
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From leather + -ette (a suffix forming nouns meaning "small" or "imitation," from French).
