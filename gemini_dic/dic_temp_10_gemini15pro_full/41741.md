corrosives:/kəˈroʊsɪvz/
noun
腐蚀剂
Substances that cause gradual destruction by chemical reaction.
The truck was carrying hazardous corrosives. 这辆卡车装载着危险的腐蚀剂。
synonyms: acids, caustics, corrodents
antonyms: inhibitors, neutralizers
n.

corrosive:/kəˈroʊsɪv/
adjective
腐蚀性的
Having the power to gradually destroy or damage something.
The effect of the acid was highly corrosive.  这种酸的腐蚀性很强。
synonyms: caustic, erosive, destructive
antonyms: protective, preservative
adj.
