pangaea:/pænˈdʒiːə/
n.
盘古大陆
A supercontinent comprising all the continental crust of the earth, postulated to have existed in late Paleozoic and Mesozoic times before it broke up into the present continents.
The breakup of Pangaea led to the formation of the Atlantic Ocean. 盘古大陆的分裂导致了大西洋的形成。

Pangaea:/pænˈdʒiːə/
n.
泛大陆
A hypothetical supercontinent that included all the landmasses of the earth before the Triassic period.
Scientists believe that dinosaurs roamed freely across Pangaea. 科学家们相信恐龙曾在泛大陆上自由漫游。

近义词:supercontinent
反义词:(无)
词性:noun
