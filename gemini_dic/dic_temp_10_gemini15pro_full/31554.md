charts:/tʃɑːrts/ /tʃɑːrts/| n.|图表；曲线图|A visual representation of data, often using lines, bars, or other graphical elements to show trends or comparisons. |The sales charts showed a significant increase in profits this quarter.  这个季度的销售图表显示利润大幅增长。

|近义词：graphs, diagrams, tables|反义词:|词性:noun

charts:/tʃɑːrts/ /tʃɑːrts/| v. (third-person singular simple present charts, present participle charting, simple past and past participle charted)|绘制图表；规划；记录|To make a chart of something; to plan a course of action; to record the progress or development of something.|They charted the course of the ship on a map. 他们在海图上标出了船的航线。

|近义词：map, plot, graph, record|反义词:|词性:verb
