chairlift:/ˈtʃɛərlɪft/ | n. | 缆车，升降椅 | A mechanical lift for carrying skiers up a slope, consisting of a series of chairs suspended from a moving cable. |  We took the chairlift to the top of the mountain. 我们乘坐缆车到达了山顶。

The chairlift offered stunning views of the valley below. 缆车提供了下面山谷的壮丽景色。 | 近义词: ski lift, gondola, cable car | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
