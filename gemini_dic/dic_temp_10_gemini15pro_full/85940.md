headmaster:/ˈhedˌmæstər/ (UK), /ˈhedˌmæstər/ (US)| n.|校长（尤指私立男校）|A headmaster is the principal of a school, typically a private school for boys. |The headmaster addressed the students at the assembly. 校长在集会上向学生们讲话。|
The headmaster expelled the student for cheating. 校长因作弊开除了这名学生。|
Synonyms: principal, head teacher, school principal|Antonyms: student, pupil|noun
