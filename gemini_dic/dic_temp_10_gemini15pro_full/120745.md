misanthropic:/ˌmɪsənˈθrɒpɪk/ (US) /ˌmɪsənˈθrɑːpɪk/| adj.|厌世的；遁世的；不愿与人来往的|Disliking humankind and avoiding human society.|例句：He became increasingly misanthropic in his later years.  他晚年变得越来越孤僻厌世。


例句：Her misanthropic tendencies made it difficult for her to form close relationships. 她厌世的倾向使她难以建立亲密关系。


近义词：antisocial, unsociable, cynical, pessimistic|
反义词：philanthropic, sociable, gregarious|
词性: adjective


Etymology: From Greek *misanthrōpos*  (hating mankind), from *misein* (to hate) + *anthrōpos* (man, human being).
