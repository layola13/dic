inferrer:/ɪnˈfɜːrər/ | n. | 推断者，推论者 |  A person who infers; someone who derives a conclusion from facts or premises. |  The astute inferrer correctly deduced the murderer's identity from the clues.  这位敏锐的推断者从线索中正确地推断出了凶手的身份。 | synonyms: deducer, reasoner, concluder | antonyms:  N/A (Lack of a direct antonym, one might consider words like "misinterpreter" or "misunderstander") | noun
