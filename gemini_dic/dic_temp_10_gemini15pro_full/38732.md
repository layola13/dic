conditioner:/kənˈdɪʃənər/| n.|护发素；空调；调节器|A substance that improves the quality or appearance of something, especially hair or the skin.  A machine that regulates temperature, humidity, etc., especially one that cools the air in a building or vehicle.|I use conditioner after shampooing my hair. 我洗发后使用护发素。|近义词：balm, cream, softener (for hair);  air conditioner, AC unit (for cooling)|反义词: -- |词性:noun

conditioners:/kənˈdɪʃənərz/| n.|护发素；空调；调节器（复数）|Plural form of "conditioner".
