groundedly:/ˈɡraʊndɪdli/
adv.
有根据地；理由充分地
Based on sound reasons or evidence; justifiably.
He argued groundedly that the evidence was insufficient. 他有理有据地辩称证据不足。
近义词: justifiably, reasonably, soundly, validly
反义词: baselessly, groundlessly, unjustifiably, unreasonably
