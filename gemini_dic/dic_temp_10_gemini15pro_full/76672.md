galvanizers:/ˈɡælvənaɪzərz/
n.
镀锌工人；电镀工人
People who galvanize metal, applying a protective zinc coating.
The galvanizers worked diligently to coat the steel beams. 镀锌工人努力地给钢梁镀上锌。
近义词:platers, coaters
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与去除涂层相关的词) strippers


galvanize:/ˈɡælvənaɪz/
vt.
镀锌；激励；刺激
To coat (iron or steel) with zinc. To shock or excite someone, typically into taking action.
The news galvanized them into action.  这消息激励了他们行动起来。
The steel is galvanized to prevent rust. 钢铁镀锌以防生锈。
近义词: stimulate, excite, rouse, spur, coat, plate
反义词: demotivate, discourage, depress, dull
