gimmickers:/ˈɡɪmɪkərz/
noun
耍花招的人；玩噱头的人；骗子
People who use gimmicks, often to deceive or manipulate others.
Those gimmickers tried to sell us a miracle cure, but it was just snake oil. 那些骗子试图向我们兜售一种神奇的疗法，但那只是骗人的玩意儿。
tricksters, swindlers, fraudsters
honest people, straightforward people
n.
