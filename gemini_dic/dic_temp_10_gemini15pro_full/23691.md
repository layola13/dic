brashest:/ˈbreɪʃɪst/| adj.|最无礼的，最鲁莽的|superlative adjective of brash: self-assertive in a rude, noisy, or overbearing way.|He was known for his brashest pronouncements, often offending those around him. 他以其最鲁莽的言论而闻名，经常冒犯周围的人。|近义词：boldest, most impudent, most brazen, most audacious|反义词：shyest, most timid, most reserved, most humble|词性:形容词
词源: From brash + -est (superlative suffix).  Brash itself is of uncertain origin, possibly related to a dialectal word meaning "attack," "rush," or "shatter."
