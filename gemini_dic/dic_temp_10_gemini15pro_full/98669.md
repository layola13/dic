insulate:/ˈɪnsjəleɪt/ (US), /ˈɪnsjʊleɪt/ (UK)| v. |隔离，使绝缘，使隔热|To cover or protect something with a material that prevents the passage of heat, sound, electricity, etc.|They insulated the attic to reduce heat loss. 他们给阁楼做了隔热处理以减少热量散失.|近义词：protect, shield, isolate|反义词:expose|词性:动词

insulation:/ˌɪnsjəˈleɪʃn/ (US), /ˌɪnsjʊˈleɪʃn/ (UK)| n. |隔离，绝缘，隔热|The act of insulating something, or the state of being insulated.|The house has good insulation, so it stays warm in winter. 这房子隔热性能好，所以冬天很暖和.|近义词：protection, shielding|反义词:exposure|词性:名词
