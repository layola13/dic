loaminess:/ˈloʊminəs/
|n.|沃土；肥沃|The quality of being loamy; the richness and fertility of soil.|The loaminess of the soil contributed to the bountiful harvest. 土壤的肥沃促成了丰收。|Synonyms: fertility, richness, fecundity|Antonyms: barrenness, sterility|noun

loamy:/ˈloʊmi/
|adj.|肥沃的；像沃土的|Consisting of or resembling loam; fertile.|The loamy soil was perfect for growing vegetables.  肥沃的土壤非常适合种植蔬菜。|Synonyms: fertile, rich, fecund|Antonyms: barren, sterile|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From loam + -y + -ness + -es.  Loam comes from Middle English *lom, from Old English lām 'clay, mud'.
