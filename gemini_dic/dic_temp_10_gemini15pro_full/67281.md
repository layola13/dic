exsanguious:/ɪkˈsæŋɡwɪəs/
adjective
血色不好的；苍白的；无血色的；没有血的
Lacking blood; bloodless.  Figuratively, drained of vitality or spirit.
The exsanguinous patient needed a blood transfusion.  那个面色苍白的病人需要输血。

The long illness left him looking exsanguinous and weak. 长期患病让他看起来脸色苍白，身体虚弱。

pale, pallid, ashen, anemic
ruddy, flushed, sanguine

adjective
词源: From Latin exsanguis (“bloodless”), from ex (“out of, from”) + sanguis (“blood”).
中文翻译: 血色不好的；苍白的；无血色的；没有血的
英文解析: Lacking blood; bloodless. Figuratively, drained of vitality or spirit.
例句:

近义词: pale, pallid, ashen, anemic, bloodless
反义词: ruddy, flushed, sanguine, full-blooded
