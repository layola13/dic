dry-cleans:/ˈdraɪ kliːnz/
verb
干洗
cleans (something) using a solvent other than water.
My suit dry-cleans well. 我的西装很适合干洗。
近义词: chemically cleans
反义词: washes, launders
词性: verb


dry-cleaned:/ˈdraɪ kliːnd/
adjective
干洗过的
cleaned with solvents rather than water.
This is a dry-cleaned garment. 这是一件干洗过的衣服。
近义词: chemically cleaned
反义词: washed, laundered
词性: adjective


dry-cleaning:/ˌdraɪ ˈkliːnɪŋ/
noun
干洗
the process of cleaning clothes or other textiles using a chemical solvent rather than water.
I took my coat to the dry-cleaning. 我把我的外套送去干洗了。
近义词: chemical cleaning
反义词: washing, laundering
词性: noun


词源: "dry-" + "clean."  "Dry" in this context refers to the absence of water. The term arose in the mid-19th century with the development of non-water-based cleaning solvents.
