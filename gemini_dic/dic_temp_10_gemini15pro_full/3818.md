ago:/əˈɡoʊ/ | adv. | 以前，之前 | Used to refer to a past time, usually with a specified amount of time before the present. | I saw him two days ago. 我两天前见过他。| 近义词: before, earlier, previously | 反义词: later, subsequently | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From Old English *agān (based on *gan "gone"). Related to "go".

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Not applicable. "Ago" refers to the past and is not used with the simple present tense.

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Not applicable. "Ago" refers to a completed past action and is not used with the present continuous tense.


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have seen him two days ago. (Incorrect - should be "I saw him two days ago.")
She has finished the work a week ago. (Incorrect - should be "She finished the work a week ago.")


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
Not applicable.  "Ago" specifies a finished time point, not a duration leading up to the present.


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I visited my grandmother two weeks ago. 我两周前去看望了我的祖母。
He left the country five years ago. 他五年前离开了这个国家。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was living in London ten years ago. 十年前我住在伦敦。
They were playing football an hour ago. 一小时前他们在踢足球。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
He had already left two hours ago when I arrived.  我到的时候他两小时前已经离开了。（ago通常不与过去完成时连用，因为过去完成时已经表示了“过去的过去”。 这个例句比较不自然, 更好的说法是 He left two hours before I arrived.)
She had finished the project a week ago before the deadline. 她一周前在截止日期前完成了这个项目。(同样地, 更好的说法是 She finished the project a week before the deadline.)


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
They had been working on the project for months ago when it was finally cancelled. (Incorrect - ago is not typically used with past perfect continuous)
He had been living in Paris for two years ago when he decided to move back to London. (Incorrect - ago is not typically used with past perfect continuous)

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
Not applicable. "Ago" refers to the past and is not used with the future tense.


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
Not applicable. "Ago" refers to the past and is not used with the future tense.


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
Not applicable. "Ago" refers to the past and is not used with the future tense.


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
Not applicable. "Ago" refers to the past and is not used with the future tense.


虚拟语气:
If I had known that a week ago, I would have done things differently. 如果一周前我知道这件事，我会采取不同的做法。
I wish I had started this project a month ago. 我希望我一个月前就开始做这个项目了。

被动语态:
The decision was made two weeks ago.  这个决定是两周前做出的。
The house was built many years ago. 这房子是很多年前建的。


疑问句:
How long ago did you move here? 你搬到这里多久了？
When did you see him last, a week ago? 你上次见到他是什么时候，一周前吗？


否定句:
I didn't see him a week ago. 我一周前没见过他。
She hasn't been here a month ago (Incorrect - should be "She wasn't here a month ago").


PHRASE->
long ago 很久以前
a while ago 不久前
not so long ago 没多久以前
just a moment ago 就在刚才
ages ago 很久以前


注意事项:
"Ago" always follows a noun phrase expressing a period of time.
