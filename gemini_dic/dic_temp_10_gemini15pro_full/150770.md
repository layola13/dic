paradigm:/ˈpærədaɪm/| n.| 范例，模式，典范 | A paradigm is a widely accepted example, model, or pattern of something. It's a way of thinking or doing things that is considered typical or standard during a particular time period. | The discovery of penicillin created a new paradigm in medicine. 青霉素的发现创造了医学的新范式。| synonyms: model, pattern, example, archetype, template | antonyms: anomaly, exception, irregularity | noun
