circumscissile: /ˌsɜːrkəmˈsɪsaɪl/ | adj. | 横裂的；盖裂的 | (of a fruit or seed capsule) Dehiscing by a transverse circular line, so that the top separates like a lid.  | The fruit of the plant is circumscissile, opening like a little pot with a lid. 这种植物的果实是横裂的，像一个带盖的小罐子一样打开。| Example 2:  Plantain has a circumscissile capsule. 车前草的蒴果是盖裂的。 | Synonym: dehiscent, valvular | Antonym: indehiscent | Word Type: adjective
