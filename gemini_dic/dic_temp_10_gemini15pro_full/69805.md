ferocious:/fəˈroʊʃəs/
|adj.|
|凶猛的；残暴的；激烈的；猛烈的|
|Exhibiting or given to extreme fierceness and unrestrained violence.|
|The storm was ferocious, with winds gusting over 100 miles per hour.|暴风雨非常猛烈，阵风风速超过每小时100英里。

|The competition in the market is ferocious.|市场竞争非常激烈。

|Synonyms: savage, fierce, brutal, violent, aggressive, intense|
|Antonyms: gentle, mild, calm, peaceful, tame|
|词性: 形容词|
