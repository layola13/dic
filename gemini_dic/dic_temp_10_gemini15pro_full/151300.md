partial:/ˈpɑːrʃəl/ | adj. | 非常喜欢的；酷爱的 | having a strong liking for something or someone | I'm partial to a glass of wine in the evening. 我晚上喜欢喝一杯葡萄酒。 | synonyms: fond, keen, inclined | antonyms: averse, indifferent, opposed | adjective
