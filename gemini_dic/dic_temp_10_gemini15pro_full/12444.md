aslant: /əˈslɑːnt/ /əˈslænt/
adjective
倾斜的
in a sloping position; obliquely
The picture hung aslant on the wall.  这幅画歪挂在墙上。

adverb
倾斜地
at an angle or slant
Rain was driving aslant against the windows. 雨斜打在窗户上。

Etymology: a- + slant

Synonyms: sloping, slanted, tilted, inclined, oblique, angled

Antonyms: straight, level, upright, vertical

词性: adjective, adverb
