eye-watering:/ˈaɪ ˈwɔːtərɪŋ/| adj.| 令人瞠目结舌的，贵得离谱的；催人泪下的 | Causing one's eyes to produce tears, either from strong emotion or from containing a pungent substance; also, exorbitantly expensive. | The eye-watering cost of housing in the city makes it difficult for young people to afford to live there.  这座城市高得离谱的房价让年轻人难以负担得起在那里生活。| synonyms: exorbitant, outrageous, extortionate, steep, prohibitive, shocking, breathtaking, astonishing  | antonyms: affordable, cheap, reasonable, inexpensive | adjective

eye-watering:/ˈaɪ ˈwɔːtərɪŋ/| adj.| 催人泪下的 | Evoking strong emotions, especially sadness or empathy, that may cause tears. | The film's eye-watering scenes of loss and resilience moved the audience to tears.  这部电影中关于失去和坚韧的催人泪下的场景让观众感动落泪。| synonyms: moving, touching, poignant, heart-wrenching, tearjerking | antonyms: unemotional, unmoving, apathetic | adjective
