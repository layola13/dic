disassent:/ˌdɪsəˈsɛnt/ | n. vi.| 异议，不同意 |Disagreement or dissent is a strong difference of opinion on a particular subject, especially about an official suggestion or plan or a popular belief. |Two members dissented from the majority decision. 两名成员对多数人的决定表示异议。| 近义词：disagreement, objection, discord, dissent,  opposition | 反义词：agreement, assent, concord, consent |词性：名词，动词

disassent:/ˌdɪsəˈsɛnt/ | vi. | 不同意，持异议|To disagree with the methods, goals, etc., of a political party or government; to express opposition. |Several board members dissented from the chairman's proposal. 几位董事会成员不同意主席的提议。| 近义词：oppose, object, disagree | 反义词：agree, consent, assent, concur |词性：动词
