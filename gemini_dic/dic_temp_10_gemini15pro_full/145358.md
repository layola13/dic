overburdensome: /ˌoʊvərˈbɜːrdənsəm/ | adj. | 繁重的，负担过重的 | Excessively burdensome; imposing too much of a burden. | The overburdensome regulations stifled innovation. 这些繁重的规章制度扼杀了创新。| 近义词: onerous, oppressive, taxing | 反义词: light, easy, manageable | 词性: adjective

overburdensome: /ˌəʊvəˈbɜːdənˌsəm/ | adj. | 繁重的，负担过重的 | Excessively burdensome; imposing too much of a burden. |  He found the paperwork overburdensome and tedious. 他觉得文书工作繁重而乏味。 | 近义词: cumbersome, weighty | 反义词: simple, straightforward | 词性: adjective

词源:  From over- + burdensome.  "Burdensome" itself comes from "burden" (from Old English byrðen, meaning "load, weight") + "-some" (a suffix meaning "characterized by" or "tending to").
