desertified:/dɪˈzɜːrtɪfaɪd/
adjective
荒漠化的，沙漠化的
turned into desert; made barren
例句：The once fertile land was desertified by overgrazing and drought.  曾经肥沃的土地由于过度放牧和干旱而荒漠化了。

例句：Large areas of the region have become desertified due to unsustainable agricultural practices.  由于不可持续的农业实践，该地区的大片土地已经荒漠化。
近义词：barren, arid, desolate
反义词：fertile, lush, verdant
词性：adjective

词源:  From desert + -ify + -ed.  Ultimately from Latin *desertum "abandoned place".
