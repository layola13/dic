nonassenting:/ˌnɑːnəˈsɛntɪŋ/
adjective
不同意的，不赞成的
Withholding assent; not agreeing.
The nonassenting shareholders were offered a buyout. 那些不同意的股东获得了收购要约。

The nonassenting members of the committee filed a formal complaint. 委员会中不同意的成员提交了正式投诉。

Near Synonyms: dissenting, objecting, opposing, disagreeing, unsupportive, resistant
Antonyms: assenting, agreeing, consenting, concurring, approving, supportive
Word Origin:  From non- (not) + assenting (present participle of assent, from Latin assentire "to agree to," from ad- "to" + sentire "to feel").
