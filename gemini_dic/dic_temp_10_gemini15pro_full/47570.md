debarment:/dɪˈbɑːrmənt/
n.
禁止；阻止；排除；取消资格
The act of debarring or the state of being debarred; exclusion, prohibition.
He was given a lifetime debarment from baseball.  他被终身禁止参加棒球比赛。
Synonyms: exclusion, prohibition, ban, bar, preclusion, disqualification
Antonyms: inclusion, admittance, permission, reinstatement
noun
