mult:/mʌlt/ | n. | 罚款，罚金 | A fine or penalty. |  The company had to pay a hefty mult for violating environmental regulations.  这家公司因违反环境法规不得不支付巨额罚款。| synonym: fine, penalty | antonym: reward, bonus | noun

mult:/mʌlt/ | v. | 处以罚款 | To fine or penalize. | The judge multed the defendant for contempt of court. 法官因被告藐视法庭而对其处以罚款。| synonym: fine, penalize | antonym: reward, compensate | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
