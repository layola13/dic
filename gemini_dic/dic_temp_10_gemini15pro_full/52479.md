dietetics:/ˌdaɪəˈtɛtɪks/
n.|营养学；饮食疗法|The science or art of applying the principles of nutrition to the selection and preparation of foods.|She studied dietetics in college. 她在大学学习营养学。
近义词:nutrition, nutriment
反义词:(没有直接的反义词，可以考虑malnutrition 营养不良)
词性:名词

dietitian:/ˌdaɪəˈtɪʃən/
n.|营养师；饮食学家|A person trained in dietetics; one who is skilled in applying the principles of nutrition to food selection and meal preparation, especially in clinical settings.|The dietitian helped him create a meal plan. 营养师帮他制定了膳食计划。
近义词:nutritionist
反义词:(无直接反义词)
词性:名词

词源：From Greek diaita "manner of living," later "diet," from diaitasthai "lead one's life," from diaita "manner of living"
