agar:/ˈeɪɡɑːr/ (US), /ˈeɪɡə/ (UK)| n.| 琼脂，琼脂糖，洋菜|A gelatinous substance obtained from various kinds of red seaweed and used in biological culture media and as a thickener in foods.|The bacteria were cultured on agar. 细菌在琼脂上培养。|Synonyms: agar-agar, China grass|Antonyms: None|noun

agar:/ˈeɪɡɑːr/ (US), /ˈeɪɡə/ (UK)| n.| 培养基|A substance used for growing bacteria or other microorganisms.|The agar was contaminated and had to be discarded. 培养基被污染了，不得不丢弃。|Synonyms: medium, culture medium|Antonyms: None|noun
