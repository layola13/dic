crater:/ˈkreɪtər/| n.|火山口；陨石坑；弹坑|A large, bowl-shaped cavity in the ground or on the surface of a planet or moon, typically one caused by an explosion or impact.|The meteor left a massive crater in the Arizona desert. 这颗流星在亚利桑那沙漠留下了一个巨大的陨石坑。|
|Synonym:cavity, hole, pit, depression|Antonym: mound, hill, elevation|noun|

cratered:/ˈkreɪtərd/ | adj. |布满坑洞的；有弹坑的|Marked with craters.|The moon's surface is heavily cratered. 月球表面布满了坑洞。|Synonym: pitted, pockmarked|Antonym: smooth, even|adjective|
