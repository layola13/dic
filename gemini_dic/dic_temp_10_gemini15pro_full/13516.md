attainder:/əˈteɪndər/| n.|剥夺公民权和财产权；腐败；被剥夺公民权的人|The act of attainting; the state of being attainted; the extinction of civil rights and capacities, and of inheritance, in consequence of sentence of death or outlawry; -- now obsolete except in its consequences.|例句：He suffered attainder for treason. 他因叛国罪而被剥夺公民权和财产权。

例句：The attainder against him was finally reversed.  针对他的剥夺公民权的判决最终被撤销了。|近义词：forfeiture, confiscation, outlawry|反义词: pardon, amnesty|词性:noun
