colored: /ˈkʌlərd/ (US) /ˈkʌləd/ (UK)| adj.|有色的，彩色的|Having a color or colors; dyed, painted, or stained.|例句：She wore a brightly colored dress. 她穿了一件颜色鲜艳的连衣裙。|例句：The walls were colored a soft blue. 墙壁被涂成了柔和的蓝色。|近义词：colourful, bright, vivid, pigmented, tinted, dyed|反义词：colorless, uncolored, plain, white, monochrome|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *coloured*, *colourid*, from *colouren* (“to color”) + *-ed* (suffix forming past participles and adjectives), from Anglo-Norman *colurer*, *colorer*, from Old French *colorer* (“to color”), from Latin *colorāre* (“to color”), from *color* (“color”).
