begone:/bɪˈɡɒn/ /bɪˈɡɔːn/
verb
走开；滚开
Go away; depart at once.
Begone, foul creature! 滚开，你这肮脏的生物！
The evil spirit was told to begone.  恶灵被告知要离开。
近义词:depart, leave, get out, go away
反义词:come, stay, arrive, approach
词性:verb
