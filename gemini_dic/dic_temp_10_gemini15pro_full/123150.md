monitrix: /ˈmɒnɪtrɪks/ (British) /ˈmɑːnɪtrɪks/ (American)| n.|女监视者，女监督员|A female monitor; a woman who admonishes or warns.|The monitrix kept a watchful eye on the students during the exam.  女监视员在考试期间密切关注着学生们。|Example: The queen acted as a monitrix, overseeing the education of her younger siblings. 女王担任监督员，监督她弟弟妹妹们的教育。|Synonyms: supervisor, overseer, invigilator,  warden|Antonyms:  subordinate, student, charge|Noun
