calicular:/kəˈlɪkjʊlər/| adj.|杯状的，萼状的|Relating to or resembling a calyx; cup-shaped.|The calicular structure of the flower was readily apparent.  这朵花的杯状结构很明显。|Example: The mushroom had a distinct calicular shape.  这个蘑菇有一个明显的杯状形状。|Synonyms: cup-shaped, cuplike, calycine, calyciform|Antonyms:  (No clear antonyms exist for this highly specific term)|Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *calicularis*, from *caliculus* (“small cup”), diminutive of *calix* (“cup, calyx”).  The term "calyx" in botany refers to the sepals of a flower, typically forming a whorl that encloses the petals and other flower parts in the bud.
