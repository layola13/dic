crocuses:/ˈkroʊkəsɪz/ |n. |番红花，藏红花（crocus的复数）|A small, brightly coloured flower of the iris family, which blooms in early spring.  The plural form of "crocus".|例句：The crocuses are blooming in the garden. 番红花正在花园里盛开。

|例句：Purple and yellow crocuses heralded the arrival of spring. 紫色和黄色的番红花预示着春天的到来。

|近义词：spring flowers, blooms|反义词:|n.
