gibbered:/ˈdʒɪbərd/ | v. | 急促而不清楚地说，胡言乱语 | to speak rapidly and unintelligibly; to chatter incoherently | The frightened child gibbered incoherently.  受惊的孩子语无伦次地胡言乱语。| synonyms: babble, chatter, prattle | antonyms: articulate, enunciate | verb

gibber:/ˈdʒɪbər/ | n. | 急促而不清楚的话，胡言乱语 | rapid, unintelligible talk; incoherent chatter | His explanation was just a load of gibber. 他的解释只是一堆废话。| synonyms: babble, chatter, prattle | antonyms: articulation, enunciation | noun
