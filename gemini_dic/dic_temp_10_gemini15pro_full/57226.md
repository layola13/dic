downgraded:/ˌdaʊnˈɡreɪdɪd/| adj.|降级的；降低等级的|Reduced in rank, status, or quality.  The word comes from "down" indicating a lowering and "grade" referring to a level or rank.|The software was downgraded due to security concerns.  该软件由于安全问题而被降级。|Synonyms: demoted, reduced, lowered, devalued|Antonyms: upgraded, promoted, elevated|adjective

downgrade:/ˌdaʊnˈɡreɪd/| vt.|降级；降低等级|To reduce in rank, status, or quality.|The company downgraded his position from manager to assistant manager. 公司将他的职位从经理降为助理经理。|Synonyms: demote, reduce, lower, devalue|Antonyms: upgrade, promote, elevate|verb
