freewheeled:/ˈfriːwiːld/| adj.|自由放任的，无拘无束的，随心所欲的|acting in a relaxed and unconcerned way, without planning or worrying about the future.  Letting events take their own course without attempting to control them.|He has a freewheeled approach to life. 他对生活采取一种自由放任的态度。|近义词：easygoing, carefree, uninhibited, relaxed, spontaneous|反义词：controlled, restrained, inhibited, cautious, planned|词性:形容词

freewheel:/ˈfriːwiːl/| v.|自由滑行，自由发展，放任自流|to move forward easily without using any power or effort, or to allow something to develop without trying to control it.|The cyclist freewheeled down the hill. 骑自行车的人顺着山坡自由滑行。|近义词：coast, drift, glide|反义词：pedal, strive, control|词性:动词

freewheel:/ˈfriːwiːl/| n.|飞轮，空转装置|a mechanism, especially on a bicycle, that allows the driven wheel to rotate independently of the pedals.|The bicycle's freewheel was broken. 自行车的飞轮坏了。|近义词：coaster, ratchet|反义词：fixed wheel|词性:名词


词源:  "free" + "wheel"，字面意思是“自由的轮子”。最初指的是自行车上的飞轮，后来引申为比喻意义，指不受约束或控制的状态。
