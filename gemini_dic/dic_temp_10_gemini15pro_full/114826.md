mamma:/ˈmɑːmə/ (US), /ˈmæmə/ (UK)| n.|妈妈；乳房|A term of endearment for mother, or referring to the breast, particularly in a medical context.  Derived from Latin "mamma."|例句：The baby cried for its mamma. 婴儿哭着要妈妈。

例句：The doctor examined the mamma for lumps. 医生检查乳房是否有肿块。|近义词：mom, mother, breast|反义词: (No direct antonym for a term of endearment. For breast, perhaps "chest" in a broader anatomical context, but not a true opposite.)|词性:noun
