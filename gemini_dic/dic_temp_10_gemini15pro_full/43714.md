credo:/ˈkriːdoʊ/ /ˈkreɪdoʊ/
|n.|信条，教义|A statement of the beliefs or aims which guide someone's actions.|His credo is "Less is more." 他的信条是“少即是多”。|
|近义词：creed, doctrine, dogma, tenet, principle|反义词：disbelief, doubt, skepticism|词性:noun|
|词源：From Latin crēdō (“I believe”).|
例句：
The company's credo is to provide excellent customer service.
这家公司的信条是提供优质的客户服务。

Their credo is based on principles of fairness and equality.
他们的信条建立在公平和平等原则之上。
