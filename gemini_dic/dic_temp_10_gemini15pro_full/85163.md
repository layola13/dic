hardwired:/ˌhɑːrdˈwaɪərd/| adj.|硬连线的，固有的，本能的|Genetically determined; present at birth; part of a person's or animal's inherent nature.  Built-in, inherent, innate.|Humans are hardwired to seek connection with others. 人类天生就渴望与他人建立联系。|Babies are hardwired to cry when they are hungry. 婴儿天生就会在饿的时候哭。|近义词：innate, ingrained, inherent, built-in|反义词：learned, acquired|词性:形容词
