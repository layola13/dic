memoriam:/məˈmɔːriəm/
n.
纪念；悼念
in memory of; as a memorial to someone or something.
The service was held in memoriam of the victims of the war. 这场仪式是为了纪念战争的受害者。
近义词：remembrance, commemoration
反义词：oblivion
