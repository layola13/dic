legge: /ledʒeɪ/ | n. | law, regulation (especially in Italian context) | A legge is a law or regulation, often used when referring to Italian law. | Example:  The new legge regarding property taxes was controversial.  新的财产税法引发了争议。

Example:  He studied Italian legge for many years. 他研究意大利法律多年。

| 近义词: law, statute, regulation, ordinance | 反义词: lawlessness, anarchy | 词性: noun
