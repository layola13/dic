antedate:/ˌæntiˈdeɪt/ | v.  n. |先于，早于;  比实际日期早的日期|To exist or happen before something else, or to give something an earlier date than its real one.|The church antedates the Norman Conquest. 这座教堂早于诺曼征服时期。|Synonyms: precede, predate, pre-exist|Antonyms: postdate, follow, succeed|verb

antedate:/ˌæntiˈdeɪt/ | n. |比实际日期早的日期|A date that is earlier than the actual date.|The antedate on the check caused confusion.  支票上的日期早于实际日期，引起了混乱。|Synonyms: earlier date |Antonyms: postdate, later date|noun
