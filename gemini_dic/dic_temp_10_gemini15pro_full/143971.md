outrage:/ˈaʊtreɪdʒ/| n. vt.|愤怒，暴行；激怒|An act of extreme violence or viciousness.  A feeling of righteous anger.|The murder of innocent children is an outrage.  谋杀无辜的儿童是一种暴行。|近义词：fury, indignation, wrath|反义词:calm, peace|noun
outrage:/ˈaʊtreɪdʒ/| vt.|激怒， outrage someone|To arouse intense anger, shock, or indignation in (someone).|The judge's decision outraged many people. 法官的判决激怒了很多人。|近义词：infuriate, enrage|反义词:please, soothe|verb
