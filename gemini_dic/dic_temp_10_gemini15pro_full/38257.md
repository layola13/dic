comradery:/ˈkɒmrɑːdəri/ /ˈkɑːmrɑːdəri/
n.
同志情谊；友情
mutual trust and friendship among people who share a common aim
例句：There was a genuine sense of comradery among the team members. 团队成员之间有一种真诚的同志情谊。

例句：The soldiers developed a strong sense of comradery during the war.  士兵们在战争期间培养了深厚的同志情谊。

近义词：fellowship, companionship, camaraderie, esprit de corps
反义词：enmity, hostility, antagonism
