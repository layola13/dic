antiphrastic:/ˌæntiˈfræstɪk/
adjective
反语的，讽刺的
Using words in a sense opposite to their proper meaning; ironic.
例句：Calling a tall person "Shorty" is an antiphrastic nickname.  把一个高个子叫做“矮子”是一个反语的绰号。

例句：His antiphrastic humor often went unnoticed. 他反语式的幽默常常被人忽视。
近义词：ironic, sarcastic, sardonic
反义词：literal, straightforward
词性: adjective
