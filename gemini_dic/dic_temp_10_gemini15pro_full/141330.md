open-mindedness:/ˌoʊpənˈmaɪndɪdnəs/| n.|开放思想；开明；虚心|The state of being receptive to new ideas or experiences; willingness to consider different perspectives.|例句：Her open-mindedness allowed her to consider perspectives she hadn't thought of before. 她的开明让她能够考虑她以前从未想过的观点。

例句：Open-mindedness is essential for personal growth and understanding. 开放的思想对于个人成长和理解至关重要。|近义词：tolerance, receptiveness, broad-mindedness, impartiality|反义词：close-mindedness, intolerance, prejudice, bigotry|词性:名词
