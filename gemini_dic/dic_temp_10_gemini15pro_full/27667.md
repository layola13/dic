candido:/kænˈdiːdoʊ/ /kænˈdiːdəʊ/| adj.|坦率的，真诚的，直言不讳的|Frank; outspoken; sincere.  Coming from Latin "candidus" meaning "white, pure, sincere."|He gave a candid assessment of the situation. 他坦率地评估了形势。|Synonyms: frank, honest, straightforward, open, forthright|Antonyms:  dishonest, deceitful, evasive, secretive|adjective

candido:/kænˈdiːdoʊ/ /kænˈdiːdəʊ/| n.|白色念珠菌病患者|A person suffering from candidiasis (a yeast infection).  Derived from the genus Candida, which causes the infection.|The doctor diagnosed him as a candido due to the recurring thrush. 医生诊断他为念珠菌病患者，因为他反复出现鹅口疮。|Synonyms: (for the condition)  thrush sufferer (if oral), yeast infection sufferer|Antonyms: N/A (This is a noun referring to a person with a medical condition; it doesn't have a direct opposite.)|noun
