bounties:/ˈbaʊntiz/| n.|赏金，奖励金，慷慨之举|A sum of money offered as a reward, especially for capturing or killing someone or something. Also refers to generous gifts or acts.|The government offered bounties for the capture of the outlaws. 政府悬赏捉拿逃犯。|Synonyms: rewards, premiums, gratuities, subsidies, grants|Antonyms: penalties, fines, punishments|Noun
