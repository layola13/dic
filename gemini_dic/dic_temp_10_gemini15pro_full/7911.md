antenniform:/ænˈtɛnɪfɔːrm/
adjective
触角状的，像触角的
Resembling an antenna in form or function; shaped like an antenna.
The antenniform appendages on the insect's head helped it to sense its surroundings.  这昆虫头部触角状的附肢帮助它感知周围的环境。
The robot had two antenniform sensors that detected changes in air pressure.  这个机器人有两个触角状的传感器，可以探测气压的变化。
近义词: antenna-like, tentacle-like
反义词: (No specific antonym, perhaps "smooth," "unbranched")
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *antenna* (antenna) + *-formis* (-form), meaning "having the form of."
