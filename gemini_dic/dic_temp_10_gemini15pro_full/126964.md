narrowcast: /ˈnæroʊˌkæst/ | n. v. | 窄播;定向广播 |  The transmission of information to a select group of recipients, as opposed to broadcasting to the general public.  It involves targeting a specific audience based on demographics, interests, or other criteria. |  The company uses narrowcasting to reach its target demographic.  这家公司使用窄播来接触其目标受众。 |  The politician used narrowcasting to appeal to specific voter groups.  这位政治家使用窄播来吸引特定的选民群体。 | Synonyms: targeted advertising, niche marketing, specialized broadcasting | Antonyms: broadcast, multicast | noun, verb
