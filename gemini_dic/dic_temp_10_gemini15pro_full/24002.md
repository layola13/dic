breathless:/ˈbrɛθləs/ | adj. | 气喘吁吁的；屏息的；激动的；令人兴奋的 | Lacking breath, especially because of exertion; also used figuratively to describe states of excitement or awe. | She was breathless after running up the stairs. 她跑上楼梯后气喘吁吁。

The breathless beauty of the sunset left them speechless.  令人屏息的日落美景让他们说不出话来。| 近义词: panting, gasping, out of breath, winded, awestruck, amazed | 反义词: calm, composed, relaxed | 词性: 形容词

breathlessness:/ˈbrɛθləsnəs/ | n. | 气喘；呼吸困难 | The state of being breathless. |  The doctor diagnosed his breathlessness as asthma. 医生诊断他的呼吸困难为哮喘。

She experienced a sudden breathlessness after climbing the hill. 爬上山后，她突然感到呼吸困难。 | 近义词: shortness of breath, dyspnea | 反义词: normal breathing | 词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English *brethles, equivalent to breath +‎ -less.
