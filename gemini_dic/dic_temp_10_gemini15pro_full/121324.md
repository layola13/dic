misframing:/ˌmɪsˈfreɪmɪŋ/| n.| 误框; 框架错误; 误导性框架 |The misframing of the issue led to a misunderstanding.| 这个问题的框架错误导致了误解。|Synonyms: misrepresentation, distortion, mischaracterization|Antonyms: accurate representation, truthful portrayal|noun
misframing:|/ˌmɪsˈfreɪmɪŋ/| v.|错误地构建; 歪曲; 误导性地描述|The media misframed the debate, focusing on trivial details.| 媒体歪曲了辩论，关注琐碎的细节。|Synonyms: misrepresent, distort, mischaracterize|Antonyms: accurately represent, truthfully portray|verb
