outdevil:/ˌaʊtˈdɛvəl/
verb
胜过魔鬼; 超过魔鬼的邪恶
to surpass even the devil in wickedness or cunning
He outdevilled the devil himself with his cruel schemes. 他用残忍的诡计胜过了魔鬼。
近义词: outdo, surpass, excel, exceed
反义词:  be outdone, fall short
