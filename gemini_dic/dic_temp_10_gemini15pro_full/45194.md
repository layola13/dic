Culdee
An early Irish or Scottish monk.
The Culdees were an ascetic monastic order in Ireland and Scotland.  They flourished between the 8th and 12th centuries.
The ruins of the Culdee monastery are still visible on the island.  这个岛上仍然可以看到卡尔迪修道院的遗址。
