backg:/ˈbækɡraʊnd/ | n. | 背景；经历；幕后 |The area or scenery behind the main object of contemplation, especially when perceived as a framework for it.  The facts, experiences, and events that make up someone's past. | The painting has a blue background. 这幅画的背景是蓝色的。| He has a background in finance. 他有金融方面的背景。 | synonyms: backdrop, setting, context, history, experience | antonyms: foreground, forefront | noun
