flabbergast:/ˈflæbərɡæst/| vt.|使大吃一惊，使目瞪口呆|To surprise or astonish someone so greatly that they are speechless or bewildered.|The news completely flabbergasted me. 这消息让我大吃一惊。|Synonyms: astound, amaze, bewilder, dumbfound, shock, stun|Antonyms: bore, expect, anticipate|Verb


flabbergasted:/ˈflæbərɡæstɪd/| adj.|大吃一惊的，目瞪口呆的|Extremely surprised and bewildered.|I was flabbergasted by his sudden proposal. 我被他突然的求婚惊呆了。|Synonyms: astonished, amazed, stunned, bewildered|Antonyms: unsurprised, unexcited, indifferent|Adjective
