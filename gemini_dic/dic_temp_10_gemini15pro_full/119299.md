microa: /maɪkroʊˈeɪ/ | n. |微阵列; 微阵列技术|  A microarray is a laboratory tool used to detect the expression of thousands of genes at the same time. DNA microarrays are microscope slides that are printed with thousands of tiny spots in defined areas. Each spot contains a known DNA sequence or gene. | Microarray analysis revealed significant changes in gene expression. 微阵列分析揭示了基因表达的显著变化。| synonym: gene chip, DNA chip | antonym: N/A | noun
