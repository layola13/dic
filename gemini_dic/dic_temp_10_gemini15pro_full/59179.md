dyslexia:/dɪsˈleksiə/
n.
阅读障碍，诵读困难
A learning disorder that involves difficulty reading due to problems identifying speech sounds and learning how they relate to letters and words.
My brother has dyslexia and struggles with reading. 我的兄弟患有阅读障碍，阅读很吃力。
近义词:reading disability, learning disability
反义词:N/A
词性:noun

Etymology:  From Greek *dys- 'bad, difficult' + *lexis 'speech, word, reading'.
