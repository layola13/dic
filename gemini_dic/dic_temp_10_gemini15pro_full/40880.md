cookhouses:/ˈkʊkhaʊsɪz/| n. |炊事房，伙房|A building in a military camp, prison, or other institution where meals are prepared and served.|The soldiers lined up outside the cookhouses, waiting for their dinner. 士兵们在炊事房外排队，等待着他们的晚餐。|Synonyms: kitchen, galley, mess hall|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "dining hall" in some contexts as it emphasizes eating rather than cooking)
