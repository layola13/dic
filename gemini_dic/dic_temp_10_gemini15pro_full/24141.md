briary:/ˈbraɪəri/| adj. n.|荆棘的；多刺的；棘丛|Overgrown with or covered in thorny bushes; a thorny patch or thicket.|例句：The path was briary and difficult to traverse.  这条小路荆棘丛生，难以通行。

例句：We had to fight our way through the briary undergrowth. 我们不得不穿过荆棘丛生的灌木丛。|近义词：thorny, prickly, spiny|反义词:smooth, soft|词性:形容词, 名词

briary:/ˈbraɪəri/| n.|荆棘丛；灌木丛|A thorny patch or thicket.|例句：The bird built its nest deep within the briary. 这只鸟在荆棘丛深处筑巢。

例句：The briary provided excellent cover for the rabbits. 荆棘丛为兔子提供了极好的掩护。|近义词：thicket, brush, scrub|反义词:clearing, meadow|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English brere, from Old English brǣr (“briar”), from Proto-Germanic *brēraz (“thorn bush”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰars- (“point, bristle”). Cognate with Dutch braam (“bramble”), German Brombeere (“blackberry”).
