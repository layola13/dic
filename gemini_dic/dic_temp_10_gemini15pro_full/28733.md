carnallite:/ˈkɑːrnəˌlaɪt/ | n. | 光卤石 | A mineral consisting of hydrated potassium magnesium chloride, used as a source of potassium.  A naturally occurring evaporite mineral composed of hydrated potassium magnesium chloride (KMgCl3·6H2O). It is variably colored yellow to white, reddish, and sometimes colorless or blue. | Carnallite is an important source of potash. 光卤石是钾肥的重要来源。| Carnallite deposits are often found alongside other evaporite minerals like halite and sylvite. 光卤石矿床经常与其他蒸发岩矿物如岩盐和钾盐一起发现。 |  sylvite, potash | N/A | noun
