enablement:/ɪˈneɪblmənt/| n. |授权；赋能；使能|The process of giving someone the authority or means to do something.  The act of making something possible.|例句：The company's focus on employee enablement has led to increased productivity.  公司对员工赋能的重视导致了生产力的提高。

例句：The software provides the enablement for remote working.  该软件为远程工作提供了支持。|近义词：empowerment, facilitation, support|反义词:restriction, hindrance, disablement|词性:noun
