calendaring:/ˈkæləndərɪŋ/
n.
日历安排；日程安排
the process of scheduling appointments, meetings, and other events, typically using a calendar application.
例句：Calendaring software helps me stay organized.  日历软件帮助我保持井井有条。

例句：Effective calendaring is essential for busy professionals.  有效的日程安排对于忙碌的专业人士至关重要。
近义词：scheduling, planning
反义词: disorder, disorganization
词性: noun
