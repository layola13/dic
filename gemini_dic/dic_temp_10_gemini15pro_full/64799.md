esophagi: /ɪˈsɒfədʒaɪ/ /ɪˈsɑːfəɡaɪ/
noun|食管（复数）|The plural form of "esophagus," referring to the muscular tubes connecting the throat to the stomach.|Endoscopes are used to examine the esophagi of patients with swallowing difficulties. 内窥镜用于检查吞咽困难患者的食道。|Synonym:gullets|Antonym:None|n.

esophagus: /ɪˈsɒfəɡəs/ /ɪˈsɑːfəɡəs/
noun|食管|The muscular tube connecting the throat to the stomach.|Food passes down the esophagus to the stomach. 食物通过食道进入胃。|Synonym:gullet|Antonym:None|n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek οἰσοφάγος (oisophágos, “gullet”), from οἴσω (oísō, “I shall carry, bear”) + -φαγος (-phagos, “-eater, -eating”).
