injured: /ˈɪndʒərd/ | adj. | 受伤的；受损害的 |  Harmed, damaged, or wounded, physically or emotionally. |  The injured man was taken to the hospital. 受伤的男子被送往医院。| wounded, hurt, damaged | unharmed, healthy, intact | adjective

injured: /ˈɪndʒərd/ | n. | 受伤者 | People who have been injured. | The injured were given first aid. 受伤人员得到了急救。| casualties, victims | none | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *injuriet, past participle of injurier "to injure," from Latin injuriare "to do wrong to, inflict injury upon," from injuria "wrong, injustice, injury," from in- "not, opposite of" (see in- (1)) + jus (genitive juris) "right, law" (see jurist).  Meaning "damaged or impaired" is from late 14c.
