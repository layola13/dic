gemmipara:/dʒɛˈmɪpərə/ | n. | 芽殖动物，芽殖繁殖的动物 | An animal that reproduces by budding, where new individuals develop from outgrowths of the parent organism. | Example 1: Hydra is a classic example of a gemmipara, reproducing asexually through budding. 水螅是芽殖动物的典型例子，通过出芽进行无性繁殖。

Example 2:  Some species of sponges are also classified as gemmipara. 一些种类的海绵也被归类为芽殖动物。| 近义词: budding animal | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


Etymology: From Latin "gemma" (bud) + "parus" (bearing, producing).  来自拉丁语 "gemma"（芽）+ "parus"（生育，生产）。
