fillet:/ˈfɪlɪt/ (US), /ˈfɪleɪ/ (UK)| n. vt.|鱼片，肉片；（建筑）带形装饰；|A fillet is a boneless piece of meat or fish.  (鱼片是一块无骨的肉或鱼。) |He ordered the salmon fillet. 他点了鲑鱼片。|Synonyms: strip, slice, tenderloin|Antonyms: bone, carcass|noun
fillet:/ˈfɪlɪt/ (US), /ˈfɪleɪ/ (UK)| vt.|把…切成片；给…镶嵌带形装饰|To fillet something is to remove the bones from a piece of meat or fish. (给…切片是指去除一块肉或鱼的骨头。) |The chef filleted the fish with precision.  厨师精确地将鱼切成片。|Synonyms: bone, debone, trim|Antonyms: bone|verb
