abysmally: /əˈbɪzməli/
adverb
极糟地，非常差地
in a terribly bad way; extremely badly.
The film was abysmally dull. 这部电影糟透了，非常无聊。

The team played abysmally in the first half.  该队上半场表现极差。

近义词: awfully, terribly, dreadfully, appallingly, disastrously
反义词: wonderfully, excellently, superbly, magnificently
词性: adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From abyss + -al + -ly. Ultimately from the Greek ἄβυσσος (abyssos, “bottomless”).

USAGE->
修饰动词:
He sang abysmally at the karaoke night. = 他在卡拉OK之夜唱得糟透了。
The project was abysmally managed from the start. =  这个项目从一开始就管理得极差。

修饰形容词:
The restaurant was abysmally overpriced. = 这家餐厅的价格高得离谱。
The conditions in the refugee camp were abysmally poor. =  难民营的条件极其恶劣。

修饰副词 (较少见):
She behaved abysmally badly at the party. =  她在聚会上表现得非常糟糕。
The company performed abysmally poorly in the last quarter. =  公司在上个季度的业绩极其糟糕。

PHRASE->
abysmally low = 极低
abysmally bad = 极其糟糕
abysmally poor = 极其贫困
abysmally ignorant =  极其无知
abysmally boring =  极其无聊
