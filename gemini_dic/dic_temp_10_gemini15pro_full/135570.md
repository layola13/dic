nonpollutant:/ˌnɑːn.pəˈluː.tənt/| n. adj.|无污染物，无污染的|A substance or process that does not pollute the environment.|Electric cars are promoted as nonpollutants.|近义词：clean, green, eco-friendly|反义词:pollutant, contaminant|词性:名词，形容词
nonpolluting:/ˌnɑːn.pəˈluː.tɪŋ/| adj.|无污染的|Not causing pollution.|The company is developing nonpolluting energy sources.|近义词：clean, green, eco-friendly|反义词:polluting, contaminating|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
non-: prefix meaning "not" (from Latin non)
pollutant: from pollute + -ant (suffix forming nouns from verbs)
pollute: from Latin polluere "to defile, soil"
