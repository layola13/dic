crotchetinesses:/ˌkrɒtʃɪtɪnɪsɪz/
|n.|古怪；怪癖；坏脾气|The state of being crotchety; having many crochets or peculiarities of temper; whimsicalness; oddness.  It refers to instances or examples of being crotchety.|His crotchetinesses became more pronounced with age.  他年纪越大，怪脾气越明显。|Synonyms: eccentricities, peculiarities, quirks, idiosyncrasies, oddities|Antonyms: agreeableness, pleasantness, affability|Noun

Etymology:
From crotchety + -ness + -es (plural).  Crotchety itself comes from crochet (a small hook, whim, or fancy) + -y.  Ultimately from French crochet, diminutive of croc "hook."
