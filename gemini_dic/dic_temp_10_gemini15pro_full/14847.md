axles:/ˈæksəlz/
n.
车轴，轴
A rod or spindle (either fixed or rotating) passing through the center of a wheel or group of wheels.
The car's rear axles were damaged in the accident. 汽车的后轴在事故中损坏了。
近义词: shafts, spindles
反义词: (No direct antonym for a physical object like an axle)
词性: 名词(plural)


axle:/ˈæksəl/
n.
车轴，轴
A rod or spindle (either fixed or rotating) passing through the center of a wheel or group of wheels.
The front axle of the bicycle was bent. 自行车的前轴弯了。
近义词: shaft, spindle
反义词: (No direct antonym for a physical object like an axle)
词性: 名词(singular)


词源:
From Old Norse *öxl, from Proto-Germanic *akslaz, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂eḱs- (“axis”). Cognate with Old English eaxl, Old Frisian axle, Old Saxon axla, Old High German ahsa, German Achse, Dutch as, Swedish axel.
