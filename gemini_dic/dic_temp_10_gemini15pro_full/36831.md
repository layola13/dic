colonises:/ˈkɒlənaɪzɪz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːlənaɪzɪz/ (US)| v.|殖民，拓殖；(动植物)移生，繁衍| establishes a colony or colonies in (a place). Also, (of plants or animals) establishes itself in (an area).|例句：The British colonised many countries around the world. 英国在世界各地殖民了许多国家。

例句：The plant easily colonises disturbed ground. 这种植物很容易在受干扰的土地上繁衍。|近义词：settle, populate, inhabit, occupy|反义词：abandon, desert, evacuate|词性:动词

词源: From late Middle English: from Old French coloniser, from medieval Latin colonizare, from Latin colonia ‘colony’.
