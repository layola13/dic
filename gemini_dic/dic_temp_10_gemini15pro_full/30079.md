cd-rom:/ˌsiː diː ˈrɒm/ | n. |光盘只读存储器；只读光盘|A cd-rom is a compact disc used to store computer data that cannot be altered. | I installed the software from the cd-rom. 我从光盘安装了软件。 | The game came on a cd-rom.  这款游戏是光盘版的。| 近义词: optical disc, CD, compact disc | 反义词: CD-RW, DVD-RAM (rewritable media) | noun
