hindrance:/ˈhɪndrəns/| n.|障碍，阻碍|Something that delays or prevents progress; an obstacle.|The heavy traffic was a hindrance to our journey.  大拥堵阻碍了我们的旅程。|

例句：
Lack of funding was a major hindrance to the project. 资金不足是该项目的主要障碍。
Her constant interruptions were a hindrance to my concentration. 她不断的打断阻碍了我的注意力。

近义词：obstacle, impediment, obstruction, barrier, deterrent
反义词：aid, assistance, help, support

词性: noun


词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French *hindrer ‘hold back’, of Germanic origin; related to behind.
