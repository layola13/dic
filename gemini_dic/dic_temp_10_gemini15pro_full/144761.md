over-cunningness:/ˈoʊvərˈkʌnɪŋnɪs/
n.| 过度狡猾，诡计多端 |The fox's over-cunningness led to its capture. 这只狐狸的过度狡猾导致它被捕获。|Over-cunningness can sometimes backfire. 过度狡猾有时会适得其反。|近义词：cunning, craftiness, slyness, guile, trickery |反义词：naivety, innocence, artlessness, honesty, forthrightness
词性

ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (excessive) + "cunning" (clever in a deceitful way) + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition). "Cunning" comes from Old English "cunnan" (to know).
