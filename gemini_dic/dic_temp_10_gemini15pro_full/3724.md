aggrievedness:/əˈɡriːvdnɪs/
n.
委屈，冤屈；愤懑
The state of feeling wronged or unjustly treated.
She expressed her aggrievedness at not being invited to the party. 她表达了因为没有被邀请参加聚会而感到委屈。
近义词：resentment, indignation, umbrage
反义词：contentment, satisfaction
词性: 名词

aggrieved:/əˈɡriːvd/
adj.
感到委屈的；愤懑的
Feeling resentment at having been unfairly treated.
He felt aggrieved at the decision. 他对这个决定感到委屈。
近义词：resentful, indignant, wronged
反义词：content, satisfied
词性: 形容词

词源:
From  aggrieve (v.) + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or condition).  Aggrieve itself comes from Old French agrever, from Latin aggravare "to make heavy, aggravate," from ad- "to" + gravis "heavy."
