mithras:/ˈmɪθrəs/| n.|密特拉, 密斯拉|A pre-Zoroastrian Iranian god of light and truth, worshipped as the mediator between Ahura Mazda and humanity. Later adopted in Roman times as Mithras, a soldier god often depicted slaying a bull. |例句：The cult of Mithras was popular among Roman soldiers. 密特拉崇拜在罗马士兵中很流行。

例句：Archaeologists unearthed a Mithraeum, a temple dedicated to Mithras. 考古学家挖掘出了一座密特拉神庙。|近义词：Sol Invictus (Unconquered Sun), Helios|反义词:Ahriman (in some interpretations)|词性:noun
