gasserian:/ɡəˈsɪəriən/
adjective
加塞尔神经节的;三叉神经节的
Relating to the gasserian ganglion (also known as the trigeminal ganglion).
The gasserian ganglion is a sensory ganglion of the trigeminal nerve (cranial nerve V) responsible for transmitting sensory information from the face. A gasserian injection can be used to treat trigeminal neuralgia.
The surgeon performed a gasserian ganglion block to alleviate the patient's excruciating facial pain.  外科医生进行了加塞尔神经节阻滞以减轻病人剧烈的面部疼痛。
