inseverable:/ɪnˈsɛvərəbəl/| adj.|不可分割的，分不开的|Not able to be separated or broken.|例句：Their fates seemed inseverable. 他们的命运似乎密不可分。|例句：The two friends had an inseverable bond. 这两个朋友有着牢不可破的联系。|近义词：indivisible, inseparable, unbreakable|反义词:severable, divisible, separable|词性:形容词
词源:From Late Latin inseparabilis, from in- ‘not’ + separabilis ‘separable’.
