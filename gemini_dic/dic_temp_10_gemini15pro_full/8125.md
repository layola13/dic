anti-Austrians:/ˈæntiˈɔːstriənz/ | n. | 反奥地利学派者；反对奥地利学派经济理论的人 |  People who oppose the economic theories of the Austrian School, a school of thought that emphasizes individual action, private property, and free markets. |  The anti-Austrians argue that the school's theories are unrealistic and impractical. 反奥地利学派者认为该学派的理论不切实际，不实用。|  Critics of laissez-faire capitalism are sometimes labeled as anti-Austrians. 自由放任资本主义的批评者有时被称为反奥地利学派者。 |  opponents, critics, dissenters | proponents, supporters, advocates | noun
