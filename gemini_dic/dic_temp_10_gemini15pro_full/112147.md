look-after:/lʊk ˈɑːftər/ | phrasal verb |照顾，照料|To take care of someone or something. | I need to look after my younger sister today. 我今天需要照顾我的妹妹。

She looked after the garden while we were away. 我们不在的时候，她照料了花园。

|近义词：care for, take care of, tend, watch over|反义词: neglect, abandon |词性:短语动词
