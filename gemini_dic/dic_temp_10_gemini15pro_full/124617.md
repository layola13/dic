moussaka: /muˈsɑːkə/ /muˈsɑːkɑː/ | n. | 慕莎卡，希腊菜肴 | A Greek dish made of layers of minced meat, eggplant, and a creamy sauce, usually topped with a layer of béchamel sauce. |  My grandmother makes the best moussaka I've ever tasted. 我祖母做的慕莎卡是我吃过最好吃的。

Moussaka is a popular dish in many Mediterranean countries. 慕莎卡是许多地中海国家的流行菜肴。 | 近义词: casserole, gratin | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific dish) | 词性: noun
