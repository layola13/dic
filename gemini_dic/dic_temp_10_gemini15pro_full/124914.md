mudguards:/ˈmʌdˌɡɑːrdz/ (US) /ˈmʌdˌɡɑːdz/ (UK)| n. |挡泥板；挡泥罩|A curved piece of metal, plastic, or rubber fitted above or behind a wheel to protect a vehicle or cyclist from mud or water sprayed up by the wheels. |The car's mudguards were caked in mud after the off-road drive. 汽车的挡泥板在越野行驶后沾满了泥。|近义词：fenders, splash guards|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性: noun
