counterfeiters:/ˈkaʊntərˌfɪtərz/
n.
造假者，伪造者
People who make fake versions of things, especially money or official documents.
The police arrested a gang of counterfeiters who were producing fake banknotes. 警方逮捕了一个制造假钞的造假团伙。
近义词：forgers, fakers, imitators
反义词：authenticators, verifiers
词性：名词(plural)

counterfeiter:/ˈkaʊntərˌfɪtər/
n.
造假者，伪造者
A person who makes fake versions of things, especially money or official documents.
The counterfeiter was skilled at replicating famous paintings.  这个造假者擅长复制名画。
近义词：forger, faker, imitator
反义词：authenticator, verifier
词性：名词(singular)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *contrefaire (“to imitate”), from contre- (“against”) + faire (“to make, to do”).
