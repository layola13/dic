parliamenteering:/ˌpɑːrləmənˈtɪərɪŋ/
n.
议会政治活动；议会斗争
The act or process of engaging in parliamentary debates or political maneuvering.
The prime minister is skilled in the art of parliamenteering.  首相精通议会政治活动。
Synonyms:  politicking, lobbying, maneuvering, wheeling and dealing
Antonyms:  cooperation, compromise, bipartisanship
noun
