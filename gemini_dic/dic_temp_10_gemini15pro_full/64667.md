escalators:/ˈɛskəleɪtərz/
n.
自动扶梯; 电动扶梯
A moving staircase that carries people between different floors of a building or structure.
The shoppers rode the escalators to the second floor. 顾客们乘坐自动扶梯上了二楼。

escalators:/ˈɛskəleɪtəz/
n.
自动扶梯; 电动扶梯
A power-driven set of stairs arranged like an endless belt that ascend or descend continuously.
We took the escalators down to the subway. 我们乘自动扶梯下到地铁站。

近义词:moving staircase, moving stairway
反义词:stairs, elevator, lift
词性:noun (plural)


词源:From escalate + -or.  Escalate itself comes from scala, Latin for "ladder" or "stairs."
