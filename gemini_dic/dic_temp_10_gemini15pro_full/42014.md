costermongery:/ˈkɒstə(r)ˌmʌŋɡəri/ /ˈkɑːstərˌmʌŋɡəri/ | n. | 叫卖；叫卖的货物；粗俗的语言或行为 | The noisy costermongery of the market filled the air.  市场的喧闹叫卖声充斥着空气。 |  The street was filled with the costermongery of the vendors. 街道上到处都是小贩的叫卖声。 | 近义词：hawking, peddling, street vending | 反义词: silence, quiet | 词性: noun

Etymology: From "costermonger" (a person who sells goods, especially fruit and vegetables, from a barrow or stall in the street) + "-y" (suffix forming nouns denoting a place where a particular activity is carried out or a collection of things of a particular kind). "Costermonger" itself is a contraction of "costardmonger," from "costard" (a type of large apple) + "monger" (a dealer or trader).
