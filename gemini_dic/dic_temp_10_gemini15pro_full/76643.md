galore:/ɡəˈlɔːr/| adj.|大量的，丰富的，充足的|in abundance; plentiful.  It is used postpositively (i.e., it follows the word it modifies).|例句：There were prizes galore at the carnival. 嘉年华上奖品琳琅满目。

例句：There will be food and drink galore at the party. 派对上将有大量的食物和饮料。|近义词：abundant, plentiful, copious, ample|反义词：scarce, few, lacking|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Irish Gaelic *go leor meaning "enough, sufficiently".  Borrowed into Scots as galore, meaning "abundance, plenty," and from there into English in the 18th century.
