noncurtailments: /ˌnɒnkɜːrˈteɪlmənts/ | n. | 未削减，未缩减；未限制 | Absence of curtailments; instances where something is not reduced or limited. |  The noncurtailments of renewable energy sources are crucial for a sustainable future. 未削减可再生能源对于可持续的未来至关重要。

The report highlighted the noncurtailments of government spending despite economic hardship.  报告强调了尽管经济困难，但政府支出并未削减。 | 近义词: continuations, maintenances | 反义词: curtailments, reductions, limitations | 词性: 名词 (plural)

Etymology:  "Non-" (prefix denoting negation) + "curtailment" (from Old French "curtailler," meaning "to shorten, dock").
