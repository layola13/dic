annihilate:/əˈnaɪəleɪt/
verb
消灭，歼灭
To destroy completely; to reduce to nonexistence.
The atomic bomb annihilated the city. 原子弹摧毁了这座城市。

近义词:destroy, obliterate, eradicate, exterminate
反义词:create, build, establish
词性:verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin annihilatus, past participle of annihilare "to reduce to nothing," from Latin ad "to" + nihil "nothing."
