litre:/ˈliːtər/ (US) /ˈliːtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |升|A metric unit of capacity, formerly defined as the volume occupied by one kilogram of water under specified conditions, now equal to one cubic decimeter (about 1.76 pints).|The car has a 1.6 litre engine. 这辆车有一个1.6升的引擎。|Synonyms: cubic decimeter, dm³|Antonyms: millilitre, gallon|noun

litres:/ˈliːtərz/ (US) /ˈliːtəz/ (UK)| n. |升（复数）|Plural form of litre.|The tank holds 50 litres. 这个罐子能装50升。|Synonyms: cubic decimeters, dm³s|Antonyms: millilitres, gallons|noun
