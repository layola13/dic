overtaxation:/ˌoʊvərˈtækˈseɪʃən/| n.|过度征税，课税过重|The overtaxation of the population led to widespread unrest.  人民负担过重的税收导致了广泛的动乱。|例句：The overtaxation of small businesses can stifle economic growth.  对小企业的过度征税会扼杀经济增长。|近义词：excessive taxation, overassessment|反义词: undertaxation|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + taxation.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much." "Taxation" refers to the act of levying or imposing a tax.
