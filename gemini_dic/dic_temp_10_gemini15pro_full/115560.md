mara:/ˈmɑːrə/ | n. |  梦魇，折磨，噩梦 | A bad dream, especially one caused by fever or drugs; something that causes great suffering or worry. |  She woke up screaming from a terrible mara. 她从可怕的梦魇中惊醒尖叫。| synonyms: nightmare, torment, ordeal, affliction | antonyms:  comfort, solace, peace, tranquility | noun

mara:/ˈmɑːrə/ | n. | (精神分析)梦魇/梦魔 | In psychoanalysis, an incubus; a demon believed to lie on sleeping persons and cause nightmares. |  Folklore describes the mara as a malevolent spirit that sits on the chest of sleepers. 民间传说将梦魇描述为一种坐在熟睡者胸口的恶灵。 | synonyms: incubus, demon, evil spirit | antonyms: guardian angel, benevolent spirit | noun
