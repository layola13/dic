overmoralize: /ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrəˌlaɪz/ /ˌəʊvəˈmɒrəlaɪz/
verb
过度道德化；过分说教
To moralize excessively; to make moral judgments too readily or severely.
He tends to overmoralize about people's choices, even when they don't affect him. 他倾向于对人们的选择过度道德化，即使这些选择并不影响他。
The critic overmoralized the novel, missing its artistic merit.  这位评论家对这部小说进行了过度道德化的解读，忽略了它的艺术价值。

近义词: preach, sermonize, moralize
反义词:  (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑用  be tolerant/accepting/understanding)
词性: verb
