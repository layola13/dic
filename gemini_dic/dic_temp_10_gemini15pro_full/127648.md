needs:/niːdz/| v. (third-person singular simple present needs, present participle needing, simple past and past participle needed)|需要|Requires something essential or desirable; is necessary. |例句：He needs a new car. 他需要一辆新车。|近义词：requires, wants, demands|反义词:has, possesses|verb

needs:/niːdz/| n. (plural only)|必需品|Things that are essential or very important.|例句：The hospital provides for all of your basic needs. 医院提供你所有的基本需求。|近义词：necessities, requirements, essentials|反义词:luxuries, nonessentials|noun
