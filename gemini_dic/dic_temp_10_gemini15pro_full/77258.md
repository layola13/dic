gastropneumatic: /ˌɡæstrəʊnjuːˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 胃气体的，胃气胀的 | Relating to or involving both the stomach and the lungs or air.  It often refers to the inflation of the stomach with air. | The gastropneumatic function of certain fish allows them to control their buoyancy.  某些鱼类的胃气功能使它们能够控制浮力。 |  近义词: aeroenteric | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: adjective

gastropneumatic: /ˌɡæstrəʊnjuːˈmætɪk/ | n. | 胃气 |  The presence of air or gas in the stomach. |  The doctor diagnosed the patient's discomfort as gastropneumatic. 医生诊断病人的不适是胃气。 | 近义词: stomach gas, flatulence | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun


Etymology:  "gastro-" from Greek *gastēr* (γαστήρ) meaning "belly, stomach," and "-pneumatic" from Greek *pneuma* (πνεῦμα) meaning "breath, air, spirit."
