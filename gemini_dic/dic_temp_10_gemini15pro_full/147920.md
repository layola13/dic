overregulations:/ˌoʊvərˌreɡjuˈleɪʃənz/
n.
过度监管，过度管制
Excessive regulations; too many rules and restrictions.
The overregulations are stifling small businesses. 过度监管扼杀了小型企业。
Overregulation has made it difficult for new businesses to enter the market. 过度监管使得新企业难以进入市场。
近义词: overgovernance, excessive regulation, red tape
反义词: deregulation, underregulation, liberalization
词性: noun
