clearstarch:/ˈklɪrˌstɑːrtʃ/ | n. |透明淀粉，浆 starch| A type of starch that becomes clear when cooked, often used for laundry or cooking. | I used clearstarch to give my shirts a crisp finish. 我用透明淀粉使我的衬衫更挺括。|  The recipe calls for clearstarch to thicken the sauce.  这道菜需要透明淀粉来使酱汁变稠。| Near synonyms: starch, laundry starch | Antonyms: None (lack of starch would be the opposite effect, not a specific substance)| Noun
