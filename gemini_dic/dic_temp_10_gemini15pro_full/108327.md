lautu:/ˈlaʊtuː/
noun
sea, ocean
A large body of salt water.
The lautu is vast and mysterious.  大海浩瀚而神秘。
The ship sailed across the lautu.  船航行过大海。
synonyms: ocean, sea
antonyms: land
