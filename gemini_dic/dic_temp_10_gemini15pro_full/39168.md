congealability:/kənˌdʒiːləˈbɪləti/
n.
凝固性；凝结性
The ability of a substance to congeal; the property of becoming congealed.
The congealability of the oil was tested at low temperatures.  这种油的凝固性在低温下进行了测试。
The recipe relies on the congealability of gelatin.  这个食谱依赖于明胶的凝固性。
coagulability, solidification
fluidity, liquidity
noun
