clione:/ˈklaɪoʊniː/ /ˈklaɪəniː/ | n. | 海天使，裸海蝶 | A small, free-swimming sea slug with wing-like appendages, found in cold Arctic and Antarctic waters.  It is a type of pteropod mollusk. | Clione limacina is a species of sea angel found in the Arctic Ocean.  海天使（蟠虎螺）是一种生活在北冰洋的海天使物种。

Clione are fascinating creatures to observe.  观察海天使是件很迷人的事。 | 近义词：sea angel, pteropod | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From Greek Kleio, one of the nine Muses, in allusion to the graceful, wing-like swimming movements.  源自希腊语 Kleio，九位缪斯女神之一，暗指其优雅、如翅膀般的游泳动作。
