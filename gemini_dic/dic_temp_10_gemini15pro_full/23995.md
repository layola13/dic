breathily:/ˈbreθɪli/| adv.| 气喘吁吁地；呼吸急促地；低声地|In a way that involves breathing quickly or with difficulty; in a soft, whispering voice. |She spoke breathily into the phone, trying not to be overheard. 她气喘吁吁地对着电话说话，尽量不让别人听到。|近义词：gaspingly, pantingly, whisperingly, softly|反义词:loudly, clearly|词性:副词
