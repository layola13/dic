mendings:/ˈmɛndɪŋz/
noun
修补；缝补工作；修补处
the act of repairing something that is broken or damaged; things that have been mended

She was busy with her mendings. 她正忙着缝补衣服。

repairs, fixes, patches
breakages, damages
