dictionary:/ˈdɪkʃəneri/ (US) /ˈdɪkʃənəri/ (UK)| n. |字典；词典；词汇|A book or electronic resource that lists the words of a language (typically in alphabetical order) and gives their meaning, or gives the equivalent words in a different language, often also providing information about pronunciation, origin, and usage.|

例句：
I looked up the word in the dictionary.
我在字典里查了这个词。

I need to buy a new English dictionary.
我需要买一本新的英语词典。


近义词：lexicon, wordbook, glossary|
反义词: None (Dictionaries don't have a true opposite.)|
词性: Noun


词源: Late Middle English: from Old French dictionaire, via medieval Latin from Latin dictio ‘saying, word’.
