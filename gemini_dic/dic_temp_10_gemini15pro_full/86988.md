henlike:/ˈhenˌlaɪk/| adj.|像母鸡的；胆怯的；怯懦的|Resembling a hen, especially in being timid or cowardly.|The child stood henlike in the corner, afraid to speak.  孩子像母鸡一样胆怯地站在角落里，不敢说话。|Synonyms: timid, cowardly, chicken, fearful, apprehensive|Antonyms: brave, courageous, bold, daring, fearless|adjective
