epichorial:/ˌɛpɪˈkɔːriəl/
adjective
绒毛膜的；母体胎盘的
Relating to the maternal part of the placenta, or to the outer layer of fetal membranes, in contact with the lining of the uterus.
The epichorial placenta is found in horses and pigs.  马和猪的胎盘是绒毛膜胎盘。
The veterinarian explained the complexities of the epichorial attachment in the mare. 兽医解释了母马绒毛膜附着的复杂性。
近义词: placental
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider terms related to non-placental birth like oviparous)
词性: adjective
