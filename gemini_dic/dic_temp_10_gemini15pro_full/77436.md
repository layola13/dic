gawker:/ˈɡɔːkər/| n.| 围观者，好奇的旁观者 | Someone who stares rudely or intrusively, especially at something unpleasant or unusual.| The gawkers slowed traffic as they rubbernecked at the accident scene. 围观者们伸长脖子看事故现场，导致交通缓慢。| synonyms: onlooker, spectator, observer, bystander, rubbernecker | antonyms: participant, participant | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "gawk," which means to stare stupidly or openly.  "Gawk" likely originated from a Scandinavian source, possibly related to Old Norse "gaka" (to gape).
