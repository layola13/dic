implicative:/ɪmˈplɪkətɪv/ | n. |  蕴涵式 | A statement that implies another. (Less common usage) | The implicative "If it rains, the ground will be wet" can be represented symbolically. 蕴涵式“如果下雨，地面就会湿”可以用符号表示。 | implication, entailment | - | noun
