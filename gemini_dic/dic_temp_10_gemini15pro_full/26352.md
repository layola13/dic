bwana:/ˈbwɑːnə/| n.|先生，主人，老板 (对非洲男子的称呼)|A term of respect used to address a man, especially of European origin, in East Africa.|例句：The bwana asked for his tea.  先生要了他的茶。|例句：The locals referred to him as "bwana." 当地人称他为“先生”。|近义词：sir, master, boss|反义词:servant, slave|名词
