darker:/ˈdɑːrkər (US) /ˈdɑːkə(r) (UK)| adj.|更暗的，更黑暗的；更深色的|Comparative form of dark.  Meaning more dark in color or having less light.|The room grew darker as the sun set. 房间随着太阳落山而变得更暗了。|Synonyms: dimmer, murkier, gloomier|Antonyms: lighter, brighter, paler|adjective
