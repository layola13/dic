harpooned:/hɑːrˈpuːnd/
|v.|用鱼叉刺；攫取；抨击|
|The whaler harpooned the massive whale. 捕鲸人用鱼叉刺中了那条巨大的鲸鱼。|
|He harpooned a large tuna. 他叉住了一条大金枪鱼。|
|The politician harpooned his opponent's policies.  这位政治家抨击了对手的政策。|
|近义词：speared, caught, criticized, attacked|
|反义词：released, praised|
|词性: 动词|


harpooned:/hɑːrˈpuːnd/
|adj.|被鱼叉刺中的|
|The harpooned whale struggled violently. 被鱼叉刺中的鲸鱼拼命挣扎。|
|近义词：speared, pierced|
|反义词：unharmed, untouched|
|词性: 形容词|


词源: From harpoon + -ed, from Middle French harpon, of uncertain origin.
