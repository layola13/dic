adversarious: /ˌædvərˈsɛəriəs/
adjective
敌对的，反对的
Characterized by or involving conflict or opposition.
The lawyer adopted an adversarious tone during the cross-examination.  律师在交叉盘问时采取了敌对的语气。

The political climate has become increasingly adversarious. 政治气氛变得越来越敌对。

近义词: antagonistic, hostile, opposed, inimical
反义词: friendly, amicable, supportive, cooperative
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *adversarius, from adversus (“turned towards, opposite, hostile”), past participle of advertere (“to turn towards”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + vertere (“to turn”).
