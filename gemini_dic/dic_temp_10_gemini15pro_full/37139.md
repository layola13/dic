come-on:/ˈkʌmɒn/ (UK), /ˈkʌmɑːn/ (US)| n. int. interj.|鼓励；诱惑；勾引；（非正式）开始；快点；得了吧|An encouragement or enticement to do something. Also used to express disbelief or impatience. |"Come on, you can do it!"  加油，你能做到！|"Come on, don't be silly." 得了吧，别傻了.|Synonyms: hurry up, get going, encouragement, enticement|Antonyms: wait, stop, discouragement|noun, interjection
