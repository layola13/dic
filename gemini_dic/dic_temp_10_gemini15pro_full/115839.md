markets:/ˈmɑːrkɪts/ /ˈmɑːrkəts/| n.|市场；股市；行情；销路|A place where people buy and sell goods.  A particular area or country in which a company sells its goods. The activity of buying and selling goods, or the value of goods bought and sold.|
例句：The local markets are full of fresh produce. 当地的市场上满是新鲜农产品。

例句：The company is trying to expand into new markets. 这家公司正试图拓展新市场。

近义词：bazaars, marketplaces, exchanges, stores|反义词:|n.

markets:/ˈmɑːrkɪts/ /ˈmɑːrkəts/| v.|销售；推销|To advertise and offer a product for sale.|
例句：The company is aggressively marketing its new product. 这家公司正在大力推销其新产品。

例句：They marketed the car as a family vehicle. 他们把这款车作为家用汽车进行销售。

近义词：sell, promote, advertise|反义词:|v.
