Etymology:
From Middle English *forlesen, from Old English *forlēosan (“to lose, destroy, forfeit”), from Proto-Germanic *fraleusaną (“to lose”), from Proto-Indo-European *lews- (“to loosen, separate”).  Cognate with Dutch verliezen (“to lose”), German verlieren (“to lose”), Gothic 𐍆𐍂𐌻𐌹𐌿𐍃𐌰𐌽 (fraliusan, “to lose”).
