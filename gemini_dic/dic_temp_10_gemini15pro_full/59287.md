eagling:/ˈiːɡlɪŋ/| n.|小鹰； 雏鹰|A young eagle.  A young eagle in its first year.|The eagling left the nest for the first time.|近义词:fledgling, chick|反义词:adult eagle|词性:noun

词源: From Middle English *eglyng, equivalent to eagle +‎ -ling.  Cognate with German Ägelchen.
