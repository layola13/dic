一般现在时 (Simple Present):
He is always in luck when it comes to gambling.  他赌博的时候总是很幸运。
She is in luck; the store has the shoes she wants. 她很幸运；商店有她想要的鞋子。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
He is being in luck today; everything is going his way. 他今天运气很好；一切都很顺利。
She is being in luck this week; she's won several prizes. 她这周运气很好；她赢得了几个奖项。（*注意：being in luck通常不用于进行时，这两个例句略有不自然，更自然的表达是She's been lucky this week.*）

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
He has been in luck lately; he's found several good deals. 他最近运气很好；他找到了几笔好交易。
She has been in luck with her investments. 她在投资方面一直很幸运。

虚拟语气:
If he were in luck, he would win the lottery. 如果他幸运的话，他会赢得彩票。
I wish I were in luck more often. 我希望我更经常走运。

被动语态:
*in luck不能用于被动语态*

疑问句:
Is he in luck today? 他今天运气好吗？
Was she in luck with the job interview? 她的工作面试顺利吗？

否定句:
He wasn't in luck; he missed the train. 他运气不好；他错过了火车。
She isn't in luck today; everything is going wrong. 她今天运气不好；一切都出了问题。
