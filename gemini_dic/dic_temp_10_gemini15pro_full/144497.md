outtinkled: /ˌaʊtˈtɪŋk(ə)ld/
verb
发出比...更清脆的叮当声
to surpass in tinkling; to produce a more delicate or musical tinkling sound than.
The wind chimes outtinkled the nearby brook. 风铃声比附近的小溪声更清脆悦耳。
Near synonyms: outsound, surpass, excel
Antonyms: be drowned out by, be quieter than
词性: 动词
