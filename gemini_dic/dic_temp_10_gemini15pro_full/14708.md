awardable:/əˈwɔːdəbl/
adjective
可授予的；值得奖励的
Suitable for being given as an award; deserving of an award.
The film was considered awardable for its outstanding cinematography. 这部电影因其出色的摄影而被认为值得获奖。
The committee decided that her contribution was awardable. 委员会认为她的贡献值得奖励。
Synonyms: commendable, praiseworthy, meritorious
Antonyms: unworthy, undeserving
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "award" + "-able".  "Award" comes from Old North French "awarder" (to adjudge, award). "-able" is a suffix meaning "capable of being," "worthy of being," or "tending to."
