greediest:/ˈɡriːdiɪst/| adj.|最贪婪的|Superlative form of greedy: most greedy.  Characterized by having or showing an excessive desire or rapaciousness, especially for wealth or possessions.|He was the greediest man I'd ever met, always wanting more. 他是我见过的最贪婪的人，总是想要更多。|The greediest child grabbed all the candy.  最贪婪的孩子抓走了所有的糖果。|近义词：most avaricious, most covetous, most rapacious|反义词：most generous, most selfless|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English grǣdig "greedy," from grǣd "greed," of uncertain origin.
