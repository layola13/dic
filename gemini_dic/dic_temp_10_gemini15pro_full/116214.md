mason:/ˈmeɪsən/| n.|石匠，泥瓦匠|A person skilled in cutting, shaping, and building with stone or brick.|例句：The mason carefully placed each brick, creating a sturdy wall.  石匠小心翼翼地放置每一块砖，砌成了一堵坚固的墙。

例句：The cathedral was built by skilled masons centuries ago.  这座大教堂是几个世纪前由技艺精湛的石匠建造的。|近义词：bricklayer, stonemason, builder|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用demolition worker -  拆迁工人)|词性:noun
