axe's:/ˈæksɪz/
noun
斧子的；斧头的
belonging to or associated with an axe.  The possessive form of "axe."
The axe's handle was broken. 斧子的柄断了。
近义词:axe-head
反义词: (No direct antonym for a possessive.  Could consider antonyms of "axe" itself, such as "saw" or "knife")
词性:noun (possessive)
