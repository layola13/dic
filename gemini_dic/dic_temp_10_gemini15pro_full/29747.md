catoptrical: /kætˈɒptrɪkəl/ | adj. | 反射的，折射的，镜面的 | Relating to catoptrics; using or involving reflection. |  The catoptrical telescope uses mirrors to gather and focus light.  反射式望远镜使用镜子来收集和聚焦光线。 |  reflective, mirroring | refractive, dioptric | adjective

catoptrics: /kætˈɒptrɪks/ | n. | 反射光学 | The branch of optics dealing with the reflection of light, especially by mirrors. |  Ancient Greeks studied catoptrics to understand how mirrors work.  古希腊人研究反射光学来理解镜子的工作原理。| reflection optics | refraction optics | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek κατοπτρικός (katoptrikós, “of or pertaining to a mirror”), from κάτοπτρον (kátoptron, “mirror”), from κατά (katá, “down”) + ὄπτομαι (óptomai, “I see”).
