dolling:/ˈdɒlɪŋ/ (UK), /ˈdɑːlɪŋ/ (US)| n.|打扮; 装扮; (尤指)打扮得花枝招展|The act of dressing up or adorning, especially in a showy or elaborate way.|She spent hours dolling herself up for the party. 她花了几个小时打扮自己去参加派对。|近义词：adorning, decorating, preening|反义词:undressing|Noun

dolling:/ˈdɒlɪŋ/ (UK), /ˈdɑːlɪŋ/ (US)| adj.|漂亮的; 可爱的 (主要用于英国英语)|Attractive, pretty, lovely (primarily used in British English). |"She looks dolling in that dress," he commented. “她穿那条裙子真漂亮，”他评论道。|近义词:pretty, lovely, charming|反义词:ugly, unattractive|Adjective
