mistrial:/ˈmɪstraɪəl/| n.|无效审判，流审|A trial that is invalid because of some fundamental error in procedure, such as misconduct by the jury or the judge, or because the jury cannot reach a verdict.|The case ended in a mistrial because the jury couldn't agree on a verdict.  案件因为陪审团无法达成一致意见而导致流审。|Synonyms: hung jury, invalid trial, aborted trial|Antonyms: successful trial, fair trial|Noun
