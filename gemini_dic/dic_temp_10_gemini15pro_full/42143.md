cotyledon:/ˌkɒtɪˈliːdən/ | n. | 子叶 |  The primary leaf or one of the first pair or whorl of leaves developed by the embryo of a seed plant and stored in the seed. |  Dicots have two cotyledons. 双子叶植物有两个子叶。|  The cotyledons provide nutrients to the seedling. 子叶为幼苗提供营养。| synonymous: seed leaf, plumule | antonymous: (No direct antonym) | noun
