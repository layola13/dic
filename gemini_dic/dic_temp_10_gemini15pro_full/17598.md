beautifully:/ˈbjuːtɪfli/ | adv. | 美丽地，优美地 | In a beautiful manner; aesthetically pleasing. | She sings beautifully. 她唱歌唱得优美。|  The sunset was beautifully painted across the sky. 日落美丽地映照在天空中。| 近义词: attractively, gracefully, elegantly, exquisitely | 反义词: uglily, horribly, awfully | 词性: 副词

词源: From "beautiful" + "-ly." "Beautiful" comes from Middle English *beautevous*, from Anglo-French *beutevus*, from Old French *bel* (beautiful) + *-eus* (suffix forming adjectives).
