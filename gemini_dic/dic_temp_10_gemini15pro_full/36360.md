coincidences:/koʊˈɪnsɪdənsɪz/
|n.|巧合，巧合事件|A series of events that seem to be related but are not caused by the same thing or person.|It was a strange coincidence that we both chose the same book.  真巧，我们俩选了同一本书。|synonyms: accident, chance, fluke|antonyms: intention, plan, purpose|noun
