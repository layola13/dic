coattails:/ˈkoʊtˌteɪlz/| n.|衣尾，(比喻)权势, 庇护|The popularity of the president helped many lesser-known candidates ride his coattails to victory. 总统的声望帮助许多不太知名的候选人沾他的光而获胜。|例句：He was elected on the coattails of his popular predecessor. 他是沾了他广受欢迎的前任的光而当选的。

例句：She hoped to ride his coattails to success. 她希望沾他的光获得成功。


|近义词：influence, power, patronage|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
