hyperanarchy:/ˌhaɪpərˈænərki/ | n. | 超无政府状态；极端无政府状态 | A state of extreme anarchy or disorder; a theoretical condition of lawlessness brought about by an excess of individualized power or authority, leading to chaos.  | The collapse of the central government resulted in hyperanarchy, with various factions vying for control. 中央政府的崩溃导致了极端无政府状态，各派势力争夺控制权。 |  The film depicts a future dystopia characterized by hyperanarchy and social breakdown. 这部电影描绘了一个以极端无政府状态和社会崩溃为特征的未来反乌托邦。 | Synonyms: lawlessness, chaos, disorder, anomie | Antonyms: order, stability, control, government | noun
