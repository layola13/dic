decked:/dekt/
adjective
装饰的，打扮的
Adorned or decorated, especially festively.  Often used with "out."
The house was decked with lights for the holidays.  房子为了节日装饰了彩灯。
近义词: decorated, adorned, ornamented, embellished
反义词: bare, plain, undecorated
词性: adjective

decked:/dekt/
verb (past tense and past participle of deck)
击倒
Knocked down; floored.
He decked the bully with one punch. 他一拳就把那个恶霸打倒在地。
近义词: floored, knocked down, flattened
反义词: helped up, raised
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *dekken*, from Middle Dutch *decken*, from Old Saxon *thekkian* (to cover), from Proto-Germanic *þakjaną* (to thatch, cover), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)tegʰ- (to cover). Related to thatch. The meaning "to knock down" is a figurative extension of the sense of "to cover completely," as if covering someone with the ground.
