paleography:/ˌpeɪliˈɒɡrəfi/ /ˌpeɪliˈɑːɡrəfi/
n.|古文书学；古文字学|The study of ancient writing systems and the deciphering and dating of historical manuscripts.|She's studying paleography to better understand medieval texts. 她正在学习古文字学，以便更好地理解中世纪的文本。|近义词: epigraphy|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性: noun
