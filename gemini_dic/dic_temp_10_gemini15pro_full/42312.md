counter-pole:/ˈkaʊntərˌpoʊl/
n.
对立面，对立物，相反极
Something diametrically opposed; an antithesis.
The counter-pole of love is hate. 爱的对立面是恨。
近义词：opposite, antithesis, contrary
反义词：complement, counterpart
词性：名词


counter-pole:/ˈkaʊntərˌpoʊl/
n.
(地球的)磁极，极点
(also counter pole) either of the two points on the earth's surface around which a magnetic needle rotates
The north counter-pole. 北磁极
近义词：pole
反义词：N/A
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- + pole.  Counter in this context means "opposite" or "contrary." Pole refers to a point of extremity, like the poles of a magnet or the earth.
