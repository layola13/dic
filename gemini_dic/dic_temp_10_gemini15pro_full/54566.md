dislodging:/dɪsˈlɒdʒɪŋ/ /dɪsˈlɑːdʒɪŋ/
verb
使…移动；取出；逐出
The explosion dislodged several boulders from the cliff face. 爆炸使几块巨石从悬崖面上移动。
The cleaning lady dislodged a wad of dust from under the sofa. 清洁女工从沙发底下清除了一团灰尘。
present participle of dislodge
近义词: removing, shifting, ejecting, ousting, extracting
反义词: embedding, fixing, securing, lodging
