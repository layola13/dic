ministered:/ˈmɪnɪstərd/
verb
服侍，照顾；执行牧师职务
attended to the needs of; performed the duties of a minister
The nurse ministered to the wounded soldiers.  护士照顾受伤的士兵。
The priest ministered to the spiritual needs of his congregation.  牧师满足了他教众的精神需求。
Synonyms: served, aided, assisted, tended, nursed
Antonyms: neglected, ignored
