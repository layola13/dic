crows:/kroʊz/ | n. |乌鸦（复数）|Plural form of crow, a large black bird with a harsh cry. |The crows were circling overhead. 乌鸦在头顶盘旋。| 近义词: ravens, rooks | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for an animal) | Noun

crows:/kroʊz/ | v. | 啼叫（第三人称单数）|Third person singular present tense form of "crow," meaning to utter a loud cry, characteristic of a rooster or, less commonly, a baby. |The rooster crows every morning at dawn. 公鸡每天黎明都啼叫。| 近义词: boasts, brags, gloats | 反义词: whimpers, complains | Verb
