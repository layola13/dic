nibs's: /nɪbzɪz/ | n. | 笔尖 | The possessive form of "nibs," referring to something belonging to or associated with multiple pen nibs. |  These are my nibs's cases. 这些是我的笔尖的盒子。|  synonyms: tips, points | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical object like a nib) | noun

Etymology:
From "nib" + possessive suffix "-'s."  "Nib" originates from Old English *nebb, meaning "beak" or "point," reflecting the pointed shape of a pen nib.
