monkishly:/ˈmʌŋkɪʃli/
adverb
像僧侣一样地；禁欲地；遁世地
In a manner resembling or characteristic of a monk; austerely, ascetically.
He lived monkishly, devoting himself to prayer and study.  他过着僧侣般的生活，致力于祈祷和学习。
The scholar lived monkishly in his small, book-filled apartment.  这位学者在他那间堆满书籍的小公寓里过着禁欲的生活。
近义词: ascetically, austerely, simply, plainly
反义词: luxuriously, extravagantly, hedonistically
词性: adverb

词源:
From monk + -ish + -ly.  来自 monk（僧侣）+ -ish（形容词后缀，表示“像...一样的”）+ -ly（副词后缀）。
