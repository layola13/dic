exegesis:/ˌɛksɪˈdʒiːsɪs/| n. |注释；解释；(尤指)圣经注释|An explanation or critical interpretation of a text, especially of scripture.|The professor's exegesis of the poem was insightful and thought-provoking. 教授对这首诗的注释富有洞察力，引人深思。|例句：Her exegesis of the ancient text brought new understanding to its meaning. 她对古代文本的解释为其含义带来了新的理解。

例句：The rabbi offered a detailed exegesis of the Torah passage. 拉比对托拉经段进行了详细的解释。|近义词：interpretation, explanation, exposition, commentary|反义词:misinterpretation, obfuscation|词性:noun
