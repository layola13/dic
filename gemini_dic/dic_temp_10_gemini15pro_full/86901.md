hemitone:/ˈhemɪˌtəʊn/ (UK) /ˈhemɪˌtoʊn/ (US)| n.|半音|A semitone.  The interval between two adjacent notes in a 12-tone scale.|The piece is played in C major, but with a few interesting hemitones.|Synonym: semitone|Antonym: whole tone|noun
hemitonic:/ˌhemɪˈtɒnɪk/ (UK) /ˌhemɪˈtɑːnɪk/ (US)| adj.|半音的|Relating to or consisting of semitones.|The hemitonic scale is the foundation of Western music.|Synonym: semitonic|Antonym: whole-tone|adjective
