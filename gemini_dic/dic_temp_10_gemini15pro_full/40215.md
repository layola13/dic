context's:/ˈkɒnteksts/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnteksts/ (US)| n. |上下文；语境；背景|The meaning of a word often depends on its context. 一个词的含义通常取决于它的上下文。|例句：You have misinterpreted my words; you have taken them out of context. 你曲解了我的话；你断章取义了。|近义词：background, circumstances, setting|反义词: N/A |Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "contextus" meaning "a joining together," from "con-" (together) + "texere" (to weave).
