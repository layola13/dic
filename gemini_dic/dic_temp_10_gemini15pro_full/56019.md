do-the-honors:/duː ðiː ˈɒnəz/ (British) /duː ðiː ˈɑːnərz/ (American)| v.| 做出表示敬意的举动；给予荣誉；主持；执行 (尤指在正式场合)|To perform a ceremonial or formal act, often involving serving or presenting something.  This phrase often implies a degree of respect or privilege.|例句：Would you do the honors and carve the turkey? 你能切火鸡吗？|例句：The mayor did the honors of cutting the ribbon at the new library. 市长为新图书馆剪彩。|近义词：officiate, preside, perform, conduct|反义词:decline, refuse|词性:verb phrase
