didactical: /dɪˈdæktɪkəl/ /daɪˈdæktɪkəl/
adjective
教诲的; 说教的
intended to teach, particularly in having moral instruction as an ulterior motive
例句：The poem is overly didactic and lacks genuine emotion. 这首诗过于教诲，缺乏真情实感。

例句：His novels tend to be highly didactic, exploring themes of social justice and individual responsibility. 他的小说往往带有很强的说教意味，探讨社会正义和个人责任等主题。

近义词：instructive, educational, pedagogical, moralistic, preachy
反义词：nondidactic, uninformative, entertaining


词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *didacticalis, from Ancient Greek διδακτικός (didaktikós, “apt at teaching”), from διδάσκω (didáskō, “I teach”).
