angularity:/ˌæŋɡjʊˈlærəti/ | n. |棱角；尖锐；生硬 |The angularity of her features gave her a severe look. 她的五官棱角分明，使她看起来很严厉。|例句：The building's angularity made it stand out from the surrounding curves of the landscape. 这座建筑的棱角使其在周围景观的曲线中脱颖而出。

例句：Her angularity of speech made her seem unfriendly. 她说话生硬，让人觉得不友好。 |近义词：sharpness, pointedness, gauntness |反义词:roundness, smoothness, curvature |词性:名词


词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin angularitas, from Latin angulus ‘corner, angle’.
