ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + calculable (able to be calculated) + -ness (forming nouns expressing state or quality).  Ultimately from Latin *incalculabilitas* from *in-* (not) + *calculare* (to calculate) + *-bilis* (capable of) + *-itas* (suffix forming nouns of quality).
