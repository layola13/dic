biomechanical:/ˌbaɪoʊməˈkænɪkəl/
adjective
生物力学的
Relating to the mechanical laws of the structure and movement of living organisms.
Biomechanical analysis of the athlete's gait revealed imbalances that could lead to injury. 对运动员步态的生物力学分析揭示了可能导致受伤的失衡。
biomechanical engineering 生物医学工程
biomechanical analysis 生物力学分析
biomechanical principles 生物力学原理

近义词:biokinetic, physiological
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective


Etymology:
bio- (life) + mechanical
From Greek bios "life" + mechanical
