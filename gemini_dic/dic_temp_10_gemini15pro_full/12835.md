associating:/əˈsoʊʃieɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 联系，交往；联想 | Connecting or joining together; relating in the mind. |  She is associating with a new group of friends. 她正在和一群新朋友交往。 |  He associates the smell of baking bread with his childhood. 他把烤面包的味道和他的童年联系在一起。 | synonyms: connecting, relating, joining, linking | antonyms: separating, disconnecting, isolating | verb

associating:/əˈsoʊsiˌeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 副的；联合的 | Acting together or in conjunction with another.  |  He is the associating director of the company. 他是公司的副主管。 | The associating members of the club have voting rights. 俱乐部联合会员有投票权。| synonyms:  auxiliary, adjunct,  subsidiary, allied  | antonyms: primary, main, principal | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *associatus, past participle of associare "to join to, unite," from Latin ad- "to" + sociare "to join, unite," from socius "companion, ally."
