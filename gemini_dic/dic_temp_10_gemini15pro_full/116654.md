matterless:/ˈmætərlɪs/
adjective
无关紧要的，无足轻重的
Unimportant; of no consequence.
His opinion was matterless in the decision-making process.  他的意见在决策过程中无关紧要。
The details are matterless; the overall result is what counts.  细节无关紧要，重要的是最终结果。
近义词：insignificant, unimportant, trivial, inconsequential
反义词：important, significant, crucial, essential
词性: adjective

词源:  From "matter" (meaning substance or importance) + "-less" (suffix indicating absence).
