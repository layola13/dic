discounting:/ˈdɪskaʊntɪŋ/ (US), /ˈdɪskaʊntɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |贴现，折扣，打折；不重视，低估|The act of reducing the price of something or the act of regarding something as unlikely or unimportant.|例句：The store was offering a 10% discounting on all items.  这家商店所有商品都打九折。|近义词：reduction, deduction, depreciation, minimizing, underestimating|反义词: premium, appreciation, valuing, emphasizing|词性:noun

discounting:/dɪˈskaʊntɪŋ/ (US), /dɪˈskaʊntɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. |贴现的，打折的|Relating to or involving a discount.|例句：The discounting price attracted many customers.  打折的价格吸引了很多顾客。|近义词：reduced, discounted|反义词: full-price, premium|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Old French word "desconter," meaning "to count off, deduct," from "des-" (meaning "off, away") and "conter" (meaning "to count").
