cicatrice: /ˈsɪkətrɪs/ (US), /ˈsɪkətraɪs/ (UK)| n.|疤痕；伤疤|A mark left on the skin or within body tissue where a wound, burn, or sore has not healed completely and fibrous connective tissue has developed.| The surgery left a small cicatrice on her abdomen.  手术在她腹部留下了一个小疤痕。|Synonyms: scar, blemish, mark|Antonyms: clear skin, unblemished skin|Noun
