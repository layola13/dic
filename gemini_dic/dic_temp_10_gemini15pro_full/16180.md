bankroller:/ˈbæŋkˌroʊlər/| n.| 资助者，金主 |A person who provides financial backing for a person or enterprise, especially a gambler.| The young boxer's bankroller believed he could be a champion.  这位年轻拳击手的金主相信他能成为冠军。| Synonyms: financier, backer, sponsor, patron | Antonyms: debtor, borrower | noun

bankroll:/ˈbæŋkˌroʊl/| vt.| 资助，提供资金 |To provide financial backing for a person or enterprise.| A wealthy businessman bankrolled the entire expedition. 一位富有的商人资助了整个探险。| Synonyms: finance, fund, subsidize, support | Antonyms: defund | verb
