dunfish:/ˈdʌnfɪʃ/| n. | 腌鳕鱼 | A codfish cured by splitting open, salting, and drying. It is similar to stockfish but is not dried as thoroughly. | Example 1: The fishermen brought in a large haul of dunfish. 渔民们带回了一大批腌鳕鱼。

Example 2: Dunfish was a staple food in many coastal communities. 腌鳕鱼是许多沿海社区的主食。| 近义词: stockfish, salt cod, clipfish | 反义词: fresh fish | 词性: 名词
