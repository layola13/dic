nonsolubly:/nɒnˈsɒljʊbli/ | adv. | 不可溶地 | Not capable of being dissolved. | The substance reacted nonsolubly with the liquid.  这种物质与液体发生反应，不能溶解。| The compound mixed nonsolubly with water.  该化合物与水混合不溶解。|  近义词: insolubly | 反义词: solubly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  From Latin *non, from nōn "not."
soluble (adjective): From Late Latin solubilis, from Latin solvere "to loosen, dissolve, release, solve."
-ly (suffix): Forms adverbs from adjectives.
