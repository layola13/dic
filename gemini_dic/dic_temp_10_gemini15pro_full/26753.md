caduke:/kəˈdʒuːk/ | adj. |  失效的，落空的，脱落的 | No longer in force; annulled, lapsed, or forfeited. |  The contract became caduque after the company went bankrupt.  合同在公司破产后失效了。 | Synonyms: void, null, invalid, defunct, obsolete | Antonyms: valid, effective, current, applicable | adjective

caduke:/kəˈdʒuːk/ | n. |  (法律)失效，落空 | The state of being no longer in force. (Often used in legal contexts) | The caduque of the treaty led to renewed hostilities.  条约的失效导致敌对行动重新开始。 | Synonyms: lapse, forfeiture, annulment | Antonyms: validation, enactment | noun
