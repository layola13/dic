conciliabule:/kənˌsɪliˈæbjuːl/ /kənˌsɪliˈæbəl/
noun
秘密会议，密谋
a secret meeting of a group of people for the purpose of plotting or intrigue.
The conspirators held a conciliabule in the dead of night to finalize their plans.  那些阴谋家在深夜举行了一次秘密会议，最终确定了他们的计划。
The rebels met in a conciliabule to plan their next move.  叛军们在一个秘密会议上碰头，计划他们的下一步行动。
synonyms: cabal, conspiracy, intrigue, plot, machination
antonyms:  open forum, public meeting, transparency
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin conciliabulum "small assembly," diminutive of concilium "council."
