bedazzled:/bɪˈdæzəld/
adjective
眼花缭乱的，被深深吸引的
greatly impressed; overwhelmed with wonder
例句：I was bedazzled by the sheer size of the stadium. 我被体育场的巨大规模所震撼。

例句：The tourists were bedazzled by the magnificent jewels in the museum. 游客们被博物馆里华丽的珠宝深深吸引。

近义词：dazzled, amazed, astonished, awestruck, fascinated, captivated
反义词：unimpressed, bored, indifferent
词性:adjective


bedazzled:/bɪˈdæzəld/
verb
使眼花缭乱，使着迷
to impress or overwhelm with brilliance or splendor; to dazzle
例句：The fireworks bedazzled the children. 焰火使孩子们眼花缭乱。

例句：He was bedazzled by her beauty and wit. 他被她的美貌和智慧所倾倒。

近义词：dazzle, fascinate, captivate, charm
反义词：bore, repel
词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + dazzle, from obsolete dazzle "to be confused or stupid," probably of Scandinavian origin.
