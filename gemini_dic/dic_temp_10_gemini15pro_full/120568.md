mires:/maɪərz/| n.|泥潭; 困境; 沼泽地|A stretch of swampy or boggy ground.|The car got stuck in the mires.|近义词：bogs, swamps, marshes, quagmires|反义词:solid ground|词性:名词

词源:From Old English *mȳre, from Proto-Germanic *mūsiz (compare West Frisian mjuks “soft, muddy”, Dutch moes “pulp, mash”, German Mus “pap, mush, stewed fruit”). Related to moss.
