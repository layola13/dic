franked:/fræŋkt/
adjective
已付邮资的; 特许邮寄的
Having the postage paid, especially by means of an official mark or stamp.  Officially authorized to be sent free of postage.
The letter was franked and ready to be mailed. 这封信已经付了邮资，可以寄出了。
prepaid, post-free, stamped
unpaid, unstamped
词性: 形容词
