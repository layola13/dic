marginalises:/ˈmɑːrjɪnəlaɪzɪz/ /ˈmɑːrdʒɪnəlaɪzɪz/
verb
边缘化；使处于不重要地位；排斥
treat (a person, group, or concept) as insignificant or peripheral.
例句：The new policy marginalises low-income families.  这项新政策使低收入家庭边缘化。

例句：Society tends to marginalise certain groups based on their background. 社会倾向于根据某些群体的背景将其边缘化。

近义词：exclude, isolate, sideline, ostracize
反义词：include, integrate, centralize, empower
词性: v
