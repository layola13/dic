harems:/ˈhɛərəmz/ (US), /ˈhɑːriːmz/ (UK)| n. |后宫; 妻妾;|A group of women associated with one man, especially as wives and concubines in a Muslim household.  It can also refer to the part of a Muslim house reserved for the women of a family.|The sultan's harems were legendary for their beauty and intrigue. 苏丹的后宫以其美丽和阴谋而闻名。|The tourists were not allowed to visit the harems of the palace.  游客不被允许参观宫殿的后宫。|近义词：seraglio, zenana|反义词: None |Noun
