lipyl:/ˈlɪpɪl/ | adj. | 脂酰的，脂肪酸的 | Relating to or derived from a fatty acid.  Lipyl refers to the acyl radical of a fatty acid. In other words, it's the part of the fatty acid that remains after the carboxyl group (-COOH) is removed. |  Lipyl groups are common components of lipids.  |  Synonyms: fatty acyl, acyl  | Antonyms: N/A | adjective
