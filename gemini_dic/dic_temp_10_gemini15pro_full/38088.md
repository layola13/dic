composting:/ˈkɒmpɒstɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmpoʊstɪŋ/ (US)| n.|堆肥，沤肥|The process of decomposing organic matter, such as leaves and food scraps, to create a nutrient-rich soil amendment.|Example: Composting is a great way to reduce household waste and improve your garden. 堆肥是减少家庭垃圾和改善花园的好方法。|Example: He started composting kitchen waste last year. 他去年开始堆肥厨房垃圾。|近义词:decomposition, rotting|反义词: incineration, landfilling|词性:noun

compost:/ˈkɒmpɒst/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmpoʊst/ (US)| n. v.|堆肥，肥料；把…制成堆肥|Decomposed organic matter used as a soil amendment; to convert organic matter into compost.|Example: We use compost to enrich the soil. 我们用堆肥来肥沃土壤。|Example: She composts all her vegetable scraps. 她把所有的蔬菜残渣都做成了堆肥。|近义词:fertilizer, manure|反义词: - |词性:noun, verb
