dent:/dɛnt/ | n. vt. |凹痕；削弱；减少 | a hollow or depression in a surface, as from a blow;  to make such a hollow or depression in; to diminish or reduce | The car had a dent in the fender. 汽车的挡泥板上有一个凹痕。| synonyms: depression, indentation, hollow, nick | antonyms: bulge, protrusion | noun
dent:/dɛnt/ | vt. |使凹陷；削弱；减少 | to make a dent in something; to damage or weaken something | The hail dented the roof of the car. 冰雹把车顶砸出了凹痕。| synonyms: damage, impair, weaken, diminish | antonyms: enhance, strengthen, improve | verb
