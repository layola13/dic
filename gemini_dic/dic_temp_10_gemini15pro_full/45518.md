curetted:/kjʊəˈrɛtɪd/ |v. |刮除；清除| scraped or cleaned a surface, especially by scraping with a curette. |The dentist curetted the patient's gums to remove plaque and tartar. 牙医刮除了病人的牙龈以去除牙菌斑和牙垢。|The surgeon curetted the infected tissue. 外科医生刮除了感染的组织。|近义词: scraped, cleaned, debrided |反义词: implanted, grafted |词性: 动词

curettage:/kjʊəˈrɛtɑːʒ/ |n. |刮除术|the surgical scraping or cleaning of a body cavity, such as the uterus.|The patient underwent a curettage to remove abnormal tissue from the lining of her uterus. 病人接受了刮宫术以去除子宫内膜上的异常组织。|Curettage is sometimes used to treat heavy menstrual bleeding. 刮宫术有时用于治疗月经过多。|近义词: scraping, cleaning, debridement |反义词: implantation, grafting |词性: 名词

词源:  From French *cureter*, from *curette* "scoop, scraper," diminutive of *cure* "spoon," from Latin *cura* "care, attention, concern." The sense development likely arose from the careful attention required in using the instrument.
