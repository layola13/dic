epopt:/ˈɛpɒpt/| n.|秘仪参祭者，旁观者，目击者|A person initiated into the highest mysteries, especially in the Eleusinian mysteries of ancient Greece.  An eyewitness, a beholder.|He was considered an epopt, having witnessed the sacred rites.|Synonym: initiate, witness, observer|Antonym: outsider, layman|noun
