haemolytic:/ˌhiːməˈlɪtɪk/ (US) /ˌhiːməˈlɪtɪk/ (UK)| adj.|溶血的，溶血性的|Causing the destruction or lysis of red blood cells.|Example: The venom of certain snakes has a haemolytic effect. 某些蛇的毒液具有溶血作用。|Example:  Haemolytic anaemia is a condition in which red blood cells are destroyed more rapidly than the body can replace them. 溶血性贫血是一种红细胞被破坏的速度快于身体替换速度的疾病。|Synonym: hemolytic, lytic|Antonym: non-hemolytic|adjective

haemolysis:/ˌhiːˈmɒlɪsɪs/ (UK) /ˌhiːˈmɑːləsɪs/ (US)| n.|溶血|The destruction or rupturing of red blood cells.|Example: The blood test showed evidence of haemolysis. 血液测试显示有溶血的迹象。|Example:  Bacterial toxins can cause haemolysis. 细菌毒素可引起溶血。|Synonym: hemolysis, erythrocytolysis|Antonym:  N/A (The opposite of cell destruction would be cell creation or growth)|noun
