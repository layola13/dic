filthy:/ˈfɪlθi/| adj.|肮脏的，污秽的；卑鄙的|Extremely dirty and unpleasant; morally objectionable.|The bathroom was absolutely filthy. 浴室脏极了。|近义词：dirty, foul, disgusting, squalid, sordid|反义词：clean, pure, immaculate|词性:形容词
词源: From Old English *fīleþig, from fīlþ (“filth”) +‎ -ig. Cognate with West Frisian fyltich (“filthy”), Dutch vuil (“dirty”) (from Middle Dutch vūle), German faul (“rotten, lazy”).
