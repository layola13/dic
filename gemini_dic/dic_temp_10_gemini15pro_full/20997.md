blepharitis:/ˌblefəˈraɪtɪs/| n. |眼睑炎|Inflammation of the eyelid margins, often involving the eyelashes and their associated glands.|She suffers from blepharitis and has to clean her eyelids daily. 她患有眼睑炎，不得不每天清洁眼睑。|近义词: eyelid inflammation|反义词: None|词性: Noun
Etymology: From Greek *blepharon* "eyelid" + *-itis* "inflammation."
