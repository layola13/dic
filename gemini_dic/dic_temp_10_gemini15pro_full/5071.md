all-ticket:/ˌɔːlˈtɪkɪt/ | adj. | 全票入场的；凭票入场的 |  Meaning that all attendees must have a ticket to enter, no free entry or passes allowed.  This system is often used for popular events to control attendance and ensure revenue. |  This is an all-ticket event, so make sure you buy your tickets in advance.  这是一场全票入场的活动，所以请确保提前购票。

The concert is all-ticket, and tickets are selling fast. 这场音乐会是全票入场的，而且票卖得很快。| 近义词: ticketed | 反义词: general admission, free entry | 词性: adjective
