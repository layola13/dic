ingeneration:/ˌɪnˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 内化；同化；吸收 | The process of absorbing or assimilating something, typically information or knowledge, so that it becomes part of one's understanding or way of thinking. |  The ingeneration of new ideas is crucial for progress.  新思想的吸收对进步至关重要。| assimilation, absorption, internalization | rejection, exclusion | noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *ingenerationem, accusative singular of *ingeneratio, from ingenerare "to implant, engender," from in- "in" + generare "to beget."
