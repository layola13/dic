mesopterygium:/ˌmɛsoʊˈtɛrɪdʒiəm/| n. |中鳍骨|The cartilaginous or bony basal element of the median fin of a fish, especially of a shark, typically bearing one or more radials.|The mesopterygium provides structural support for the shark's dorsal fin.|近义词: basal pterygiophore|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially distal pterygiophore in some contexts)|noun
