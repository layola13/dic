overmine:/ˌoʊvərˈmaɪn/ | v. | 过度开采，破坏性开采 | To extract minerals from (a place) to an excessive and destructive extent. | The company overmined the area, leaving it barren and polluted.  这家公司过度开采了这个地区，使其变得贫瘠和污染。| The coal company overmined the mountain, causing landslides. 这家煤炭公司过度开采了这座山，导致了山体滑坡。 | 近义词: overexploit, deplete | 反义词: conserve, preserve |词性:动词
