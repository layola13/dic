allotment: /əˈlɒtmənt/ (UK), /əˈlɑːtmənt/ (US)
n.
分配；份额；拨款；(尤指在城镇的)小块菜地；
A share or portion of something allotted or granted to someone.  A plot of land rented by an individual for growing vegetables or flowers.
例句：
The allotment of funds for education has been increased this year. 今年教育经费的拨款增加了。
He spends most of his free time tending his allotment. 他大部分的空闲时间都花在照料他的小菜地上。

近义词：allocation, share, portion, plot, patch
反义词：confiscation, seizure
词性：名词
