gentlewomanhood: /ˈdʒɛntlwʊmənhʊd/
n.
the state or quality of being a gentlewoman; gentlewomen collectively.
身份或品质高雅的女性；淑女；名媛
The event celebrated gentlewomanhood and female empowerment.  这个活动颂扬了女性的高贵品质和赋权。
She conducted herself with the utmost gentlewomanhood. 她举止极其优雅得体。
近义词:  ladyhood, refinement, gentility
反义词:  vulgarity, coarseness
