coinventors:/ˌkoʊɪnˈvɛntərz/| n. |共同发明人|People who invent something together.  A coinventor shares the rights and responsibilities of an invention with one or more other individuals.|Example: The two coinventors shared the patent for their new device. 这两位共同发明人共享了他们新设备的专利。|Example: The coinventors worked tirelessly for years to perfect their invention. 共同发明人孜孜不倦地工作多年以完善他们的发明。|近义词：collaborators, joint inventors|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "sole inventor")|词性:Noun
