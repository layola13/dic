overcheap:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃiːp/| adj.|太便宜的，过分便宜的|Excessively cheap; cheaper than is reasonable or usual.  Bought at a price below the usual or expected cost.|I found some overcheap vegetables at the market. 我在市场上发现了一些非常便宜的蔬菜。|The land was bought overcheap. 这块地买得太便宜了。|近义词：cheap, inexpensive, bargain, cut-rate|反义词：expensive, costly, dear, overpriced|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *overchep, equivalent to over- + cheap.  Compare Dutch overkoop (“overcheap”).
