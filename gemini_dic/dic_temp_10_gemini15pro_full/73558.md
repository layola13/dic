forestalling:/fɔːrˈstɔːlɪŋ/
v. |阻止，先发制人|Preventing something from happening by acting first.|The government forestalled a potential crisis by increasing interest rates. 政府通过提高利率阻止了一场潜在的危机。| The company forestalled the competition by releasing their product early.  这家公司通过提前发布产品来抢占市场先机。|preempt, prevent, anticipate, obviate| allow, permit, facilitate
verb
