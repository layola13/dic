entrails:/ˈɛntrəlz/ | n. | 内脏；肠子；内部；（尤指）最深处 | The internal organs, especially the intestines; the innermost parts (especially of something unpleasant). |  The hunter cleaned the entrails of the deer. 猎人清理了鹿的内脏。| Synonyms: intestines, guts, viscera, bowels | Antonyms: exterior, outside | Noun

entrails:/ˈɛntrəlz/ | n. |  (比喻) 内部；深处 |  (figuratively) The innermost parts. | The entrails of the earth are full of molten rock. 地球的深处充满了熔岩。 | Synonyms: depths, core, heart | Antonyms: surface, exterior | Noun
