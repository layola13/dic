injunction's:/ɪnˈdʒʌŋkʃənz/
n.
禁令，命令
A court order requiring a person to do or refrain from doing a particular act.
The judge issued an injunction against the company, preventing them from selling the product.  法官对该公司发布了禁令，禁止他们销售该产品。
Synonyms: order, decree, command, mandate, ruling
Antonyms: allowance, permission, authorization
