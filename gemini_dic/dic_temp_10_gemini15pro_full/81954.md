griming:/ˈɡraɪmɪŋ/
Verb
使肮脏,弄脏
Making something dirty or covered with grime.
The factory chimneys were griming the sky. 工厂的烟囱弄脏了天空。

近义词:dirtying, soiling, blackening
反义词:cleaning, washing, purifying
词性:v.
