Example 2:  Musketoons were favored by sailors in close-quarters combat during boarding actions.  在登船行动中的近距离战斗中，水手们更喜欢使用短筒滑膛枪。 | 近义词: blunderbuss, carbine | 反义词: rifle | 词性: 名词
