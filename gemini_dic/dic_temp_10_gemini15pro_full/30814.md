一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she do for a living? 她做什么工作？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? 他晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has she bought at the store? 她在商店买了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading this week? 他这周一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say? 你说了什么？
What did she do yesterday? 她昨天做了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 pm last night? 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 开会的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had she eaten before the dinner party? 晚宴前她吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the phone rang? 电话响之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major?  在她换专业之前她一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? 你下周末要做什么？
What will she study in college? 她大学要学什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time tomorrow? 明天这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be presenting at the conference?  她在会议上将要展示什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by the end of the day? 你到今天结束前会完成什么？
What will she have accomplished by the time she retires?  她退休时会完成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for the past ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到毕业时她将已经学习什么多久了？
