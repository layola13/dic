The barracan kept him warm during the cold desert nights.  巴拉坎呢让他在寒冷的沙漠夜晚保持温暖。| 近义词:  camel hair cloth, coarse wool, baize, frieze | 反义词: silk, satin, linen (in terms of texture and fineness) | 词性: noun
