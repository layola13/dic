bulletined:/ˈbʊlɪtɪnd/
|v.| 公布；用公告宣布；简要报道|The news was bulletined on the radio. 这条新闻已在收音机中广播。| 近义词: announced, reported, broadcast | 反义词: concealed, suppressed |词性: 动词(past tense and past participle of bulletin)

bulletin:/ˈbʊlətɪn/
|n.| 公告；简报 |The company issued a bulletin about the merger. 公司发布了关于合并的公告。|近义词: announcement, notice, report |反义词:  None (A bulletin is a way to communicate information; there's no direct opposite to the act of communicating.) |词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *bulletin*, diminutive of *bulle* (“papal bull, official document”), from Latin *bulla* (“bubble, round object, seal”). The original sense was a sealed official document.
