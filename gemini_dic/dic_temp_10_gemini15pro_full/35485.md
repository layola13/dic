coacting:/koʊˈæktɪŋ/
adjective
共同作用的，互相影响的
acting or working together
The coacting forces of wind and rain eroded the cliff face. 风雨的共同作用侵蚀了悬崖表面。
近义词: interacting, synergistic, cooperating, collaborative
反义词: independent, separate, opposing, antagonistic
词性: adjective

词源:
From co- + acting, present participle of act. Co- signifies "together," "jointly," or "mutually."  Thus, coacting describes actions performed together or with mutual influence.
