expectorate: /ɪkˈspɛktəreɪt/ | v. | 吐痰，咳出 | To cough or spit out phlegm or other matter from the lungs or throat. |  He expectorated a large wad of phlegm into the tissue. 他往纸巾里吐了一大口痰。

The doctor asked him to expectorate into the cup. 医生让他把痰吐到杯子里。 | 近义词: cough up, spit, hawk | 反义词: swallow, ingest | 词性: 动词
