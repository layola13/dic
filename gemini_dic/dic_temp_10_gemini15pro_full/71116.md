fishery:/ˈfɪʃəri/ (US), /ˈfɪʃəri/ (UK)| n. |渔场；渔业；水产业|A place where fish are raised for commercial purposes or where fish are caught; the occupation or industry of catching fish.|例句：The coastal fishery is an important part of the local economy.  沿海渔业是当地经济的重要组成部分。


例句：The new regulations aim to protect the salmon fishery. 新规旨在保护鲑鱼渔业。


近义词：fishing grounds, fishing industry, aquaculture|反义词: None|词性: noun
