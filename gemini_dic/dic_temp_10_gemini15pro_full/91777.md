hyperenthusiastically:/ˌhaɪpərɪnˌθuːziˈæstɪkli/
adverb
极度热情地; 过分热情地
with extreme enthusiasm; excessively enthusiastic
She hyperenthusiastically described her trip to Disneyland. 她极度热情地描述了她的迪士尼乐园之旅。
He hyperenthusiastically greeted every customer that walked into the store. 他过分热情地迎接走进商店的每一位顾客。
Synonym: excitedly, fervently, passionately, exuberantly
Antonym: apathetically, indifferently, listlessly
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
hyper- (prefix): from Greek, meaning "over, beyond, excessive"
enthusiastic: from Greek "enthousiasmos" meaning "inspiration, divine possession," from "enthousiazein" meaning "to be inspired or possessed by a god," from "en" (in) + "theos" (god).
-ally (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives.
