detonatability:/ˌdiːtɒnəˈtəbɪləti/ /ˌdeɪtənəˈtəbɪləti/
noun
可爆性
The quality of being able to be detonated; explosiveness.
例句：The detonatability of the new compound was higher than expected.  这种新化合物的可爆性比预期的要高。

例句：Researchers are studying the detonatability of various fuels. 研究人员正在研究各种燃料的可爆性。


近义词：explosiveness,  inflammability
反义词：stability, inertness
词性：noun

detonate:/ˈdɛtəneɪt/
verb
引爆，爆炸
explode or cause to explode.
例句：The bomb detonated at 6:00 am. 炸弹在早上6点钟爆炸。

例句：The demolition team detonated the building.  拆迁队引爆了大楼。

近义词：explode, blow up
反义词：defuse, disarm
词性：verb
