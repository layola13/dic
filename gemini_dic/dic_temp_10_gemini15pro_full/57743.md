dreamer:/ˈdriːmər/| n.|梦想家，空想家|Someone who spends a lot of time thinking about or imagining pleasant things that are not likely to happen.  A person who dreams.|例句：He's a dreamer, always lost in thought. 他是个梦想家，总是沉浸在遐想中。

例句：She's a dreamer, not a doer. 她是个空想家，而不是实干家。|近义词：idealist, visionary, romanticist, fantasist|反义词：realist, pragmatist|词性:noun

词源: From Middle English *dremer, from Old English drēamere (“dreamer”), equivalent to dream +‎ -er. Cognate with West Frisian dreamer (“dreamer”), Dutch dromer (“dreamer, visionary”), German Träumer (“dreamer”).
