antipasti:/æntiˈpɑːsti/ | n. | 开胃菜，前菜 |  A small dish of food served before the main part of a meal, typically consisting of olives, cheeses, or meats.  It is the Italian word for appetizers. |  We started our meal with a delicious platter of antipasti. 我们以一盘美味的开胃菜开始用餐。

The restaurant offers a wide selection of antipasti. 这家餐厅提供各种各样的开胃菜。 | 近义词: appetizers, starters, hors d'oeuvres | 反义词: dessert, main course | 词性: noun
