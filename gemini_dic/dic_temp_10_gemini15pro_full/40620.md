conveniency:/kənˈviːniənsi/
n.
便利;方便;合宜;便利设施
Something that makes life easier or more comfortable.
The hotel offers a range of conveniences, including a swimming pool and a fitness center.  这家酒店提供一系列便利设施，包括游泳池和健身中心。
convenience, amenity, facility, comfort
inconvenience, difficulty, hardship
noun
