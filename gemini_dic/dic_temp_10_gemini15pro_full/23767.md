bravura:/brəˈvʊərə/ /brəˈvjʊrə/
n.
精湛的技巧; 华丽的表演
great technical skill and brilliance shown in a performance or activity.
She sang the aria with tremendous bravura. 她以惊人的技巧演唱了这首咏叹调。
skill, brilliance, virtuosity, mastery, talent, flair, panache, style
awkwardness, clumsiness, mediocrity
noun
