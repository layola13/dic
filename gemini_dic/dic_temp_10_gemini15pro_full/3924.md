centrifuge: /ˈsɛntrɪfjuːdʒ/ (US) /ˈsɛntrɪfjuːʒ/ (UK)
n.|离心机|A machine with a rapidly rotating container that applies centrifugal force to its contents, typically to separate fluids of different densities or liquids from solids.|The lab technician used a centrifuge to separate the blood components.|近义词: separator, extractor|反义词: None|词性: 名词

centrifuge: /ˈsɛntrɪfjuːdʒ/ (US) /ˈsɛntrɪfjuːʒ/ (UK)
vt.|离心分离|To rotate something at high speed in a centrifuge to separate its components.|They centrifuged the sample to isolate the DNA.|近义词: spin, whirl, separate|反义词: None|词性: 动词
