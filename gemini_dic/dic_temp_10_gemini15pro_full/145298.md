overbraveness:/ˈoʊvərˌbreɪvnəs/
n.
过度勇敢，鲁莽
Excessive bravery; recklessness.
His overbraveness led him into a dangerous situation. 他的过度勇敢使他陷入了危险的境地。
近义词: recklessness, audacity, foolhardiness
反义词: caution, prudence, timidity
词性: 名词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "braveness" (noun from "brave")
