marrowy:/ˈmærəʊi/ (US) /ˈmæroʊi/ (UK)| adj.|多骨髓的；充满精髓的；滋养的|Full of marrow; pithy; nutritious.  Relating to or resembling marrow.|The marrowy bones of the oxtail provided a rich and flavorful broth. 牛尾的多骨髓骨头熬制出了一锅浓郁鲜美的肉汤。|近义词：pithy, succulent, nutritious, rich|反义词: insipid, bland|词性:形容词

Etymology: From marrow + -y.  Marrow comes from Old English mearg, from Proto-Germanic *mazgaz.
