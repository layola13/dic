daffier: /ˈdæfiər/ | adj. | 更愚蠢的，更傻的 | More foolish or silly; sillier. | He's become even daffier in his old age. 他年纪大了，变得更傻了。 |  She made a daffier suggestion than usual. 她提出了一个比平常更愚蠢的建议。 | 近义词: sillier, goofier, crazier | 反义词: wiser, saner, more sensible | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Comparative form of "daffy," which is likely a shortening and alteration of "daffadowndilly," a playful name for the daffodil flower. The association with foolishness may stem from the narcotic properties of the daffodil bulb.
