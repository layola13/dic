incautious:/ɪnˈkɔːʃəs/| adj.|不谨慎的，轻率的|Not cautious; heedless of potential danger or risks. | He made an incautious remark that offended his boss. 他说了一句不谨慎的话，冒犯了他的老板。|  She was incautious in her investments and lost a lot of money. 她在投资上很不谨慎，损失了很多钱。|近义词：rash, reckless, imprudent, careless, heedless|反义词：cautious, prudent, careful, circumspect|词性:形容词

词源:
From in- (not) + cautious (from Latin cautus, past participle of cavere "to beware").
