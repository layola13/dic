capricorn: /ˈkæprɪˌkɔːrn/ (US), /ˈkæprɪˌkɔːn/ (UK)
|n.|摩羯座；摩羯宫|A constellation (the Goat) and the tenth sign of the zodiac, which is entered about December 22.|例句：My birthday is in late December, so I'm a Capricorn. 我的生日在十二月下旬，所以我是摩羯座。

|例句：Capricorn is an Earth sign known for being practical and ambitious. 摩羯座是一个土象星座，以务实和雄心勃勃而闻名。

|近义词：Goat (as a zodiac sign)|反义词:|词性:noun
