gigantism: /ˈdʒaɪɡənˌtɪzəm/| n. |巨人症；巨大发育；巨大化|A condition characterized by excessive growth and height significantly above average, usually caused by overproduction of growth hormone in childhood.|The boy's gigantism was diagnosed early, allowing doctors to intervene and manage his growth.  这个男孩的巨人症很早就被诊断出来了，医生得以介入并控制他的生长。|Synonyms: macrosomia, overgrowth|Antonyms: dwarfism|noun

gigantism: /ˈdʒaɪɡənˌtɪzəm/| n. |巨大；庞大；巨大化|The sheer gigantism of the project overwhelmed the small team.  这个项目的庞大规模让这个小团队不堪重负。|Synonyms: immensity, hugeness, vastness|Antonyms: smallness, tininess|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek "gigas" (giant) + "-ism" (suffix denoting a state or condition).
