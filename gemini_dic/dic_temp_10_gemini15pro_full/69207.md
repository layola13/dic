fear:/fɪr/ (US); /fɪə(r)/ (UK)| n. v. |恐惧，害怕；担忧；敬畏|An unpleasant emotion caused by the perception of danger, pain, or harm.|She has a fear of spiders. 她很怕蜘蛛。|近义词：terror, dread, fright, anxiety, apprehension|反义词：courage, bravery, confidence, boldness|noun

fear:/fɪr/ (US); /fɪə(r)/ (UK)| v. |害怕，恐惧；担忧；敬畏|To be afraid of; to expect or anticipate with anxiety.|He feared that he would lose his job. 他担心会失去工作。|近义词：dread, be afraid of, worry about|反义词：be brave, be bold|verb
