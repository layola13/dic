epitaphize: /ɪˈpɪtəfaɪz/
verb
以墓志铭的形式概括；成为…的缩影
to serve as an epitaph for; to epitomize
The inscription on the tombstone epitaphizes the deceased’s life of service to others. 墓碑上的铭文概括了死者一生为他人的服务。
The ruined city epitaphizes the destructive nature of war. 这座被毁坏的城市是战争破坏性的缩影。
synonyms: epitomize, summarize, encapsulate, typify
antonyms: expand, elaborate, amplify
词性: 动词
