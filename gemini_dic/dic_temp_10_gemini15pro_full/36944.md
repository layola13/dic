coloured: /ˈkʌləd/ (British) /ˈkʌlərd/ (American)| adj.|有色的，彩色的，着色的|Having a colour or colours; dyed, painted, or stained.|例句：She wore a brightly coloured dress. 她穿着一件颜色鲜艳的连衣裙。|例句：The children enjoyed playing with coloured pencils. 孩子们喜欢玩彩色铅笔。|近义词：colorful, dyed, painted, tinted|反义词：colorless, uncolored, plain, white|词性:形容词

词源:
From the verb "colour" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Colour" itself comes from the Old French "color" (modern French "couleur"), ultimately from the Latin "color".
