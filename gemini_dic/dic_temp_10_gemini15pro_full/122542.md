modelers:/ˈmɑːdələrz (US) / ˈmɒdələz (UK)| n.| 建模者，建模人员| People who create models, often using computer software, to simulate real-world systems or phenomena.  They can work in various fields such as engineering, finance, or science.|例句：The modelers created a simulation of the traffic flow in the city. 建模人员创建了城市交通流量的模拟。

例句：Financial modelers use software to predict market trends. 金融建模人员使用软件预测市场趋势。|近义词：simulators, designers, analysts|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
