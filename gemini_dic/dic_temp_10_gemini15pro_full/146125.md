ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + emphasizing, the present participle of emphasize.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," "too much," and comes from Old English *ofer. "Emphasize" comes from the Late Latin word emphasis, derived from the Greek word emphasis meaning "significance, appearance."
