minatorily:/ˈmɪnətɔːrɪli/ | adv. | 专横地，盛气凌人地 | In a domineering or overbearing manner; imperiously. | He spoke minatorily to the staff, demanding immediate obedience. 他专横地对员工说话，要求立即服从。|  Synonym: domineeringly, imperiously, authoritatively, dictatorially, overbearingly | Antonym: humbly, submissively, meekly | Part of Speech: Adverb

Etymology:
From Late Latin *minatorius (“threatening”), from Latin minari (“to threaten”) + -ly (adverbial suffix).
