drosophilas:/drəˈsɒfɪləz/
|n.|果蝇（复数）
|Small flies belonging to the family Drosophilidae, commonly used in biological research, especially genetics.|
|Drosophilas are often used in genetic experiments. 果蝇常用于遗传实验。|
|Drosophilas are vital for research due to their short lifespan and easily observable traits.  由于果蝇寿命短且性状易于观察，它们对研究至关重要。|
