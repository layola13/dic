make a dent:/meɪk ə dɛnt/
|v. phr.|取得进展；产生影响；产生效果|To make some progress or have a noticeable impact on something, especially something large or difficult.| The donation didn't even make a dent in the enormous debt.  这笔捐款甚至都没能让巨额债务减少分毫。|
| |We tried to clean the stain, but we couldn't make a dent in it. 我们试图清洗污渍，但一点效果都没有。|
| |Synonyms: make progress, make headway, make an impact, have an effect|
| |Antonyms: make no difference, have no effect, be ineffective|
