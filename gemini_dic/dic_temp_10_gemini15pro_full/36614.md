collectible:/kəˈlektəbl/ | adj. n. | 可收集的；收藏品 |  Something worth collecting or suitable for collecting. | This antique vase is highly collectible.  这个古董花瓶很有收藏价值。 | synonyms: collectable, desirable, sought-after | antonyms: worthless, undesirable | adjective

collectible:/kəˈlektəbl/ | n. | 收藏品 | An item worth collecting. |  She has a large collection of baseball collectibles. 她收藏了大量的棒球纪念品。| synonyms: collectable, curio, antique | antonyms: junk, trash | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Late Latin *collectabilis, from Latin colligere "to gather," from com- "together" + legere "to gather, select, read."
