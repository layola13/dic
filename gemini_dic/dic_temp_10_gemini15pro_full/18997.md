bestowments:/bɪˈstoʊmənts/
|n.|
|捐赠物，天赋，才能|
|Something bestowed, a gift or talent.  It refers to things that are given or conferred, often of significant value or importance.|
|The university received several generous bestowments from alumni.|大学收到了几位校友的慷慨捐赠。
|Her natural bestowments of intelligence and charm made her a successful diplomat.|她天生的智慧和魅力使她成为一名成功的外交官。
|gifts, endowments, donations, talents, attributes|
|withdrawals, confiscations, deprivations|
|noun|
