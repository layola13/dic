lusciousnesses:/ˈlʌʃəsnɪsɪz/
n.
美味；甘美；芬芳；华丽
The state or quality of being luscious; deliciousness; richness.
The lusciousnesses of the ripe peaches were tempting.  成熟桃子的美味很诱人。
近义词:deliciousness, richness, sweetness, luxuriance
反义词:blandness, insipidity, dullness
词性:名词(plural of lusciousness)

lusciousness:/ˈlʌʃəsnəs/
n.
美味；甘美；芬芳；华丽
The state or quality of being luscious; deliciousness; richness.
The lusciousness of the chocolate cake was irresistible. 巧克力蛋糕的美味令人无法抗拒。
近义词:deliciousness, richness, sweetness, luxuriance
反义词:blandness, insipidity, dullness
词性:名词

词源:
From luscious + -ness.  Luscious comes from Middle English *luscious, from Old French *luscios (“delicious”), from *lus (“pleasure, delight”), of uncertain origin.
