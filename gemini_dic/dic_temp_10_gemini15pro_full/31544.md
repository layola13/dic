chartist: /ˈtʃɑːtɪst/| n.|图表分析师，图表学家|A person who uses charts of past stock prices and trading volumes to predict future price movements.  A person who uses charts and graphs to interpret data, especially in technical analysis of financial markets. |例句：The chartist predicted a rise in the stock market. 图表分析师预测股市会上涨。

例句：Many chartists believe that patterns in price charts can predict future market behavior. 许多图表分析师认为，价格图表中的模式可以预测未来的市场行为。|近义词：technical analyst, market technician|反义词:|n.
