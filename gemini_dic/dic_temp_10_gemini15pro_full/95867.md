incorruptly:/ˌɪnkəˈrʌptli/
| adv. |廉洁地，正直地|In a manner that is free from corruption or dishonesty; with integrity.|He served the public incorruptly for many years. 他多年来廉洁为公。|近义词：honestly, uprightly, ethically, virtuously|反义词：dishonestly, corruptly, venally|词性:副词
Etymology: From "in-" (not) + "corrupt" (from Latin "corruptus," past participle of "corrumpere" - to break up, destroy, spoil, bribe) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).
