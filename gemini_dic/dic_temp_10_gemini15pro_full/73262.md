fore-spurrer:/fɔːrˈspɜːrər/
n.|先行者; 刺激者;|someone or something that encourages or stimulates an activity or process.|The new tax laws acted as a fore-spurrer to investment.|近义词：pioneer, instigator, catalyst, stimulant|反义词:inhibitor, deterrent|词性:名词

Etymology:
From "fore-" (meaning "before" or "ahead") + "spur" (meaning "to urge forward").  The word combines the idea of being ahead and urging others forward.
