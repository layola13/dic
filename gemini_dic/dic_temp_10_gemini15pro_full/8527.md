anticensoriously:/ˌæntiˌsensɔːriəsli/
adverb
反审查地，不审查地
Without censorship; in a manner that opposes or avoids censorship.
He spoke anticensoriously about the government's new media policies.  他毫不避讳地谈论了政府新的媒体政策。
The journalist reported anticensoriously on the sensitive topic.  这位记者毫不避讳地报道了这个敏感话题。
Synonym:  uncensored, freely
Antonym:  censored, restricted
词性: 副词
