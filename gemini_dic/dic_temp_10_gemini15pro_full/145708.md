overcourtesies:/ˌoʊvərˈkɜːrtəsiːz/
noun
过度的礼节; 过分的客气
Excessive politeness or courtesies.
His overcourtesies made her feel uncomfortable. 他过分的客气让她感到不舒服。
近义词:excessive politeness, overpoliteness
反义词:rudeness, impoliteness
词性:n.


Etymology:
From "over" (meaning excessive) + "courtesies" (plural of courtesy).  The prefix "over-" indicates an excess or superfluity of the base word.  "Courtesy" itself comes from Old French "corteisie," ultimately deriving from Latin "curia" (court).
