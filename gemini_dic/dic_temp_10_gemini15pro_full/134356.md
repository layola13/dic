noninterchangeable:/ˌnɒnɪntətˈtʃeɪndʒəbl/ | adj. | 不可互换的，不可交换的 | Not able to be exchanged or used in place of something else. | These parts are noninterchangeable, so make sure you order the correct one. 这些零件不可互换，所以请确保订购正确的零件。| The original documents are noninterchangeable and must be returned. 原始文件不可互换，必须归还。| 近义词：irreplaceable, nonsubstitutable, unique | 反义词：interchangeable, replaceable, substitutable | 词性: adjective

词源: non- (not) + interchangeable (able to be exchanged)
