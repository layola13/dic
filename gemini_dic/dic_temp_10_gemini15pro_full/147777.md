overpromoted:/ˌoʊvərprəˈmoʊtɪd/
adjective
过度晋升的
Given a position too high for one's abilities or experience.
He seemed a little overpromoted, struggling to manage the team effectively. 他似乎有点过度晋升，难以有效地管理团队。
The overpromoted manager made a series of disastrous decisions.  这位被过度提拔的经理做出了一系列灾难性的决定。

近义词: out of one's depth, in over one's head
反义词: underpromoted, qualified


词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "promoted" (past participle of "promote")
