consider:/kənˈsɪdər/
v.
考虑，认为
1. Think carefully about (something), typically before making a decision.
He considered the offer carefully before accepting.  他在接受之前仔细考虑了这个提议。
2. Believe to be.
I consider him a friend. 我把他当作朋友。

近义词: contemplate, ponder, deem, regard
反义词:disregard, ignore
词性:动词

considerate:/kənˈsɪdərɪt/
adj.
体贴的，考虑周到的
Careful not to cause inconvenience or hurt to others.
It was very considerate of you to offer me a ride. 你能提出让我搭车真是太体贴了。

近义词:thoughtful, kind, attentive, caring, compassionate
反义词:inconsiderate, thoughtless, selfishly, rude
词性:形容词

词源:
From Late Middle English *considerat, from Latin consideratus, past participle of considerare "to consider," perhaps originally meaning "to observe the stars," from com- "together" + sidus (genitive sideris) "star, constellation."
