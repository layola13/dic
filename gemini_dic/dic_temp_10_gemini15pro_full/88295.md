hip-pier:/ˈhɪpiər/ (US), /ˈhɪpɪə/ (UK)| n. |嬉皮士；嬉皮风格的人|A person, typically young, who rejects established social customs, especially in dress and lifestyle, and often embraces unconventional beliefs and practices, originally as a member of a countercultural movement in the 1960s.|He lived like a hippie in his youth, embracing peace and free love. 他年轻时像个嬉皮士一样生活，崇尚和平和自由恋爱。|近义词：bohemian, nonconformist, counterculturalist|反义词：conformist, traditionalist|词性: 名词

hip-pier:/ˈhɪpiər/ (US), /ˈhɪpɪə/ (UK)| adj. |嬉皮士的；嬉皮风格的|Characteristic of or relating to hippies or their culture.|She wore a hippie dress and sandals. 她穿着一件嬉皮士风格的连衣裙和凉鞋。|近义词：bohemian, unconventional|反义词：conventional, traditional|词性: 形容词
