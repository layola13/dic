half-democratic:/ˌhɑːf ˌdɛməˈkrætɪk/ | adj. | 半民主的 | Partially democratic; having some features of democracy but not fully democratic.  |  The country's half-democratic system allows for some elections, but real power remains concentrated in the hands of a few. 这个国家的半民主制度允许一些选举，但真正的权力仍然掌握在少数人手中。|  The newly formed government established a half-democratic system, balancing traditional authority with limited public participation. 新成立的政府建立了一个半民主的制度，在传统权威和有限的公众参与之间取得平衡。 | Near synonyms: semi-democratic, quasi-democratic | Antonyms: authoritarian, totalitarian, undemocratic | adjective
