autofill:/ˈɔːtəʊfɪl/ (British) /ˈɔːtəfɪl/ (American)| n. v.|自动填充|The act of automatically completing a form or field with information that has been previously entered or is readily available.|例句：The autofill feature saved me a lot of time when filling out the online form.  自动填充功能在填写在线表格时节省了我很多时间。

例句：I enabled autofill for my passwords on my computer. 我在电脑上启用了密码自动填充功能。|近义词：autocomplete, prefill|反义词: manual entry|词性:名词, 动词
