lay waste:/leɪ weɪst/
verb phrase
摧毁，使一片荒芜
to completely destroy a place or area
例句：The invading army laid waste to the city. 入侵的军队把这座城市夷为平地。

例句：The hurricane laid waste to the coastal towns. 飓风摧毁了沿海城镇。

近义词：devastate, ruin, destroy, ravage
反义词：build, construct, create, restore
词性：动词短语
