nightman:/ˈnaɪtmæn/ | n. | 夜行人；夜间工作者；（虚构）夜晚出没的男子 | A person who works at night or is active at night. Also, a fictional character, often villainous, associated with the night. |  He is a nightman, working the graveyard shift at the factory. 他是一个夜班工人，在工厂上夜班。

The children were frightened by stories of the nightman who haunted the woods. 孩子们被夜晚出没在树林里的夜行人的故事吓坏了。 | 近义词: night worker, night owl | 反义词: day worker | 词性: noun
