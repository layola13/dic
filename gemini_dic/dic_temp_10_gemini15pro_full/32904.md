choo-choo:/ˈtʃuːtʃuː/
n.
火车(儿童用语)
the sound of a train, especially as imitated by children.
例句：The little boy loves playing with his toy choo-choo train.  小男孩喜欢玩他的玩具火车。

例句：My nephew calls trains "choo-choos." 我的侄子把火车叫做“啾啾”。

近义词：train, locomotive
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps car, airplane for alternative modes of transportation)
词性: Noun
