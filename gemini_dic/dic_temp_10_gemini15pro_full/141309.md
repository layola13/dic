open-plan:/ˈoʊpən plæn/ (US) /ˈəʊpən plæn/ (UK)| adj.|开放式的|Characterized by the absence of internal walls, creating a single large space.|例句：They work in an open-plan office. 他们在开放式办公室工作。|例句：We knocked down a wall to create an open-plan kitchen/diner. 我们拆掉了一堵墙，打造了一个开放式厨房/餐厅。|近义词：open, spacious|反义词：closed, partitioned|词性:形容词

open-plan:/ˈoʊpən plæn/ (US) /ˈəʊpən plæn/ (UK)| n.|开放式布局|A design or layout characterized by the absence of internal walls.|例句：The open-plan design maximizes natural light. 开放式设计最大限度地利用了自然光。|例句：The company adopted an open-plan for its new headquarters.  这家公司在新总部采用了开放式布局。|近义词：open layout, open space|反义词：closed plan, partitioned space|词性:名词
