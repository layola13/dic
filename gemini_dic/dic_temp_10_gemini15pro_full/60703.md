electro-acoustic:/ɪˌlektrəʊ əˈkuːstɪk/| adj.|电声的；电声学的|Relating to or involving a combination of electronic and acoustic elements, typically in musical instruments or sound reproduction systems.|Electro-acoustic guitars are popular among modern musicians. 电声吉他很受现代音乐家的欢迎。|Near synonyms: electronic, amplified|Antonyms: acoustic, unamplified|adjective

electro-acoustic:/ɪˌlektrəʊ əˈkuːstɪk/| n.|电声乐器；电声系统|An electro-acoustic instrument or system.|The band used a complex electro-acoustic setup for their live performances.  这支乐队在他们的现场表演中使用了一套复杂的电声系统。|Near synonyms: sound system, audio equipment|Antonyms: acoustic instrument|noun
