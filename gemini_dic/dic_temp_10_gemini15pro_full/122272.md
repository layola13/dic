mitigations:/ˌmɪtɪˈɡeɪʃənz/| n. | 缓解措施，减轻，缓和 |Actions taken to reduce the severity or impact of something negative. | The government implemented several mitigations to address the economic crisis. 政府实施了几项措施来应对经济危机。| synonyms: remedies, solutions, alleviations, palliatives | antonyms: exacerbations, aggravations | noun

mitigation:/ˌmɪtɪˈɡeɪʃən/| n. | 缓解，减轻，缓和 |The act of reducing the severity or impact of something negative. | The mitigation of the risk of flooding involved building higher levees.  降低洪水风险的缓解措施包括建造更高的堤坝。| synonyms: reduction, alleviation, lessening | antonyms: intensification, aggravation | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *mitigationem, from Latin mitigatus, past participle of mitigare "to soften, alleviate," from mitis "mild, soft" + -igare (from agere "to do, drive, act").
