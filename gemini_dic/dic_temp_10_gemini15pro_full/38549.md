conclusible:/kənˈkluːzɪbl/
adjective
可推断的；可下结论的
Capable of being concluded or inferred; deducible.
The evidence is conclusible, pointing towards the suspect's guilt. 证据确凿，指向嫌疑人的罪行。
The detective found conclusible proof of the suspect's involvement.  侦探找到了嫌疑人涉案的确凿证据。

近义词:deducible, inferable, demonstrable
反义词:inconclusive, ambiguous, uncertain
词性:adjective
