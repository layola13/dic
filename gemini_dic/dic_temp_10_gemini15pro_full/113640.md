machine:/məˈʃiːn/ (US) /məˈʃiːn/ (UK)| n. |机器，机械装置|A device that uses power to apply forces and control movement to perform an intended action.  A system or organization likened to a complex machine because of its intricate workings.|The sewing machine is quite old. 这台缝纫机很旧了。|Synonyms: apparatus, appliance, device, mechanism, engine, motor|Antonyms: human, manual, natural|noun

machine:/məˈʃiːn/ (US) /məˈʃiːn/ (UK)| vt. |用机器加工|To make or operate on with a machine.|The parts were machined to precise tolerances. 这些零件是用机器精密加工的。|Synonyms: manufacture, fabricate, produce|Antonyms: handcraft|verb
