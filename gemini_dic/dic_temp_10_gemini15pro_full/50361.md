depopularized:/diːˈpɒpjələraɪzd/
verb
使不受欢迎；使失势
To make something less popular or less supported.
The new tax policies depopularized the government. 新的税收政策使得政府 unpopular。
The mayor's controversial decision depopularized him with voters. 市长有争议的决定使他在选民中失去了 popularity。
近义词:marginalize, alienate, ostracize
反义词:popularize
词性:verb
