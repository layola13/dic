budge:/bʌdʒ/
| v. | 移动; 让步; 改变主意 | to move slightly; to change an opinion or decision | The dog wouldn't budge from the doorway. 狗不肯从门口挪开。 | move, shift, stir | resist, stay put | Verb

budge:/bʌdʒ/
| n. | 预算 | an estimate of income and expenditure for a set period | The company has a tight budget this year. 今年公司的预算是很紧张的。| allowance, allocation, appropriation | debt | Noun
