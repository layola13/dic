mcc:/ɛm siː siː/ |abbr.|移动国家代码（Mobile Country Code）|A three-digit number that identifies a specific country in mobile networks. It is used in combination with the Mobile Network Code (MNC) to uniquely identify a mobile operator.|Example: The MCC for the United States is 310. 美国的MCC是310。|近义词: country code|反义词: N/A|Abbreviation
