discordant:/dɪˈskɔːrdənt/ | adj. | 不和谐的，不一致的 | Disagreeing or incongruous. | The discordant sounds of the construction site made it difficult to concentrate.  建筑工地的不和谐声音使人难以集中注意力。|  The paint color was discordant with the rest of the room's decor. 油漆颜色与房间的其他装饰不协调。 | synonyms: clashing, conflicting, dissonant, jarring, inharmonious | antonyms: harmonious, agreeable, concordant, consonant | adjective

discordant:/dɪˈskɔːrdənt/ | n. | 不和谐音 | A discordant note or sound. | The musician accidentally struck a discordant note, disrupting the melody. 音乐家不小心敲出了一个不和谐的音符，扰乱了旋律。 |  There was a discordant in the otherwise peaceful symphony. 在原本平静的交响乐中出现了一个不和谐音。| synonyms: dissonance, cacophony | antonyms: harmony, consonance | noun
