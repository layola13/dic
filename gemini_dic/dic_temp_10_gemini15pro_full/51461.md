detonations:/ˌdetəˈneɪʃənz/
| n. |爆炸，引爆|An explosion caused by a detonating substance.|The detonations shook the entire city. 爆炸震动了整个城市。|近义词：explosions, blasts|反义词：implosions|名词
词源：From the Late Latin "detonationem,"  noun of action from past-participle stem of "detonare" "to thunder down," from "de-" (down) + "tonare" (to thunder).
