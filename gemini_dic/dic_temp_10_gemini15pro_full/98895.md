intenser:/ɪnˈtɛnsər/ | adj. | 更强烈的，更紧张的 | comparative form of intense: having a characteristic to a greater degree |  The pain became intenser as the hours passed. 随着时间的推移，疼痛变得更加剧烈。 | 近义词: stronger, greater, heightened | 反义词: milder, weaker | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *intensour, from Old French *intenseur (compare modern French intensifieur), ultimately from Latin intendere (to stretch out, strain).  The English word is a back-formation from intensity.
