noncorrespondences:/ˌnɒnkɒrɪˈspɒndənsɪz/ | n. |不一致，不符，不相符 |Lack of agreement or conformity; instances where things do not match or align.| There are several noncorrespondences between the two versions of the document. 这两个版本的文档之间存在几个不一致的地方. |近义词：discrepancies, inconsistencies, disparities, variations|反义词：correspondences, agreements, similarities, consistencies|词性: 名词

词源: non- + correspondence + -s
     non-:  prefix meaning "not"
     correspondence:  from Old French *correspondance,  from Latin correspondentia "agreement, sympathy," from correspondentem (nominative correspondens) "answering, agreeing," present participle of correspondere "to correspond," from com- "with" + respondere "to answer, promise"
     -s: plural suffix
