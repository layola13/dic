dinkier: /ˈdɪŋkiər/ (US), /ˈdɪŋkɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更小的，更矮小的|Comparative form of dinky: smaller and less impressive than other things of the same type.|例句：She traded in her SUV for a dinkier car to save on gas. 她把她的SUV换成了一辆更小的车以节省汽油。|例句：He prefers dinkier apartments because they're more affordable. 他更喜欢小一点的公寓，因为它们更便宜。|近义词：smaller, tinier, more compact|反义词：larger, bigger, more spacious|词性：形容词

dinky: /ˈdɪŋki/| adj.|小的，小巧玲珑的，微不足道的|Small, neat, and attractive; small and insignificant.|例句：She drove a dinky little sports car. 她开着一辆小巧玲珑的跑车。|例句：The company started in a dinky office above a shop. 这家公司最初在一个商店上面的小办公室里成立。|近义词：small, tiny, miniature, compact|反义词：large, big, huge, enormous|词性：形容词
