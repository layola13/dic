impelling:/ɪmˈpɛlɪŋ/
adjective
推进的，驱使的，激励的
driving or urging forward; motivating
例句：An impelling desire for knowledge drove her to enroll in night classes.  对知识的强烈渴望驱使她报名参加夜校。

例句：The impelling force of the hurricane uprooted trees and damaged buildings.  飓风的强大推动力将树木连根拔起，并损坏了建筑物。

近义词：driving, motivating, compelling, urging, prompting
反义词：restraining, hindering, deterring
词性：adjective
