glee-clubs:/ˈɡliː klʌbz/
noun
合唱团
A group of people who sing together, especially for pleasure.  It particularly refers to groups, often associated with schools or colleges, that sing in a close-harmony style.
My brother joined his college glee club and they perform at local events. 我的兄弟加入了他大学的合唱团，他们在当地活动中表演。
Glee clubs were popular in the early 20th century. 合唱团在20世纪早期很流行。
synonyms: choir, chorus, singing group
antonyms: solo act, individual performer
