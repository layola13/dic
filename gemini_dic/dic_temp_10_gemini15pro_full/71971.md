flimflammery:/ˈflɪmˌflæməri/
n.
欺骗；花言巧语；胡言乱语
nonsense intended to deceive; deceitful or misleading talk
The salesman's promises were nothing but flimflammery.  那个推销员的承诺只不过是花言巧语。
He tried to sell me some flimflammery about how his product would make me rich. 他试图用一些花言巧语来向我推销他的产品，说他的产品会让我变得富有。

近义词: deception, trickery, fraud, chicanery, humbug
反义词: honesty, truthfulness, sincerity
词性: noun


flimflam:/ˈflɪmˌflæm/
n.
欺骗；花言巧语；胡言乱语
nonsense intended to deceive; deceitful or misleading talk
The whole scheme was a flimflam. 整个计划就是一个骗局。
Don't fall for his flimflam. 不要相信他的鬼话。
vt.
欺骗
to deceive or trick someone
He flimflammed me out of my money. 他骗走了我的钱。
They flimflammed the old lady into signing over her house.  他们骗那位老太太签字放弃了她的房子。
近义词:  deceive, trick, swindle, defraud, hoodwink
反义词: be honest, tell the truth
词性: noun, verb


词源:  The origin of "flimflam" is uncertain, but it likely emerged in the mid-19th century. Some believe it might be imitative, suggesting the sound of rapid or nonsensical talk.
