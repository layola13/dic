fixture:/ˈfɪkstʃər/| n.|固定装置，设备；固定家具；比赛日程；常客|a piece of equipment or furniture that is fixed in position in a building or vehicle. A sports event that has been arranged to take place on a particular date and at a particular time.  Someone who is well known for regularly going to a particular place.|例句：The light fixtures were quite modern.  这些照明设备很现代。

例句：Next week's fixture against Manchester United has been postponed.  下周对阵曼联的比赛已被推迟。

例句：He's become a fixture in the local pub. 他成了当地酒吧的常客。|近义词：fitting, appliance, apparatus, equipment, appointment, regular|反义词: - |词性:noun
