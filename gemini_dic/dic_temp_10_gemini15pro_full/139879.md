ocupate:/oʊkjuˈpeɪt/ /ˈɑːkjupeɪt/| v. |占用，占据；忙于| to reside or dwell in; to be busy or engaged with |例句：She occupies a small apartment in the city. 她在这座城市里住着一间小公寓。

例句：He occupies himself with gardening. 他忙于园艺。|近义词：inhabit, reside, dwell, engage, busy|反义词:vacate, abandon, idle|词性:动词
