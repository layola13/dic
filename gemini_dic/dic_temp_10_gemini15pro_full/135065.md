nonnotional:/nɒnˈnəʊʃənl/ | adj. | 非概念的，非词汇的 |  Relating to grammatical elements (like articles, prepositions, conjunctions) that primarily express grammatical relationships rather than semantic content.  They don't refer to concrete entities or concepts like nouns or verbs do. | The word "the" is a nonnotional word.  这个词"the"是非概念词。 |  grammatical, functional | lexical, content | adjective
