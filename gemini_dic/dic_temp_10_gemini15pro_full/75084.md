frilled:/frɪld/
adjective
有褶边的，有饰边的
decorated with a frill or frills.
The dress had a frilled collar. 这件连衣裙有一个褶边领子。

近义词: ruffled, pleated, gathered, trimmed
反义词: plain, simple, unadorned
词性: adjective


frill:/frɪl/
noun
褶边，饰边
a strip of gathered or pleated material sewn by one edge to a garment, curtain, etc., as a trimming.
The curtains had delicate lace frills. 窗帘上有精致的蕾丝褶边。
近义词: ruffle, flounce, furbelow, peplum
反义词: plainness, simplicity
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"frill," late 14c., "to wrinkle, curl," of uncertain origin, perhaps from a Scandinavian source (compare Old Norse fryja "to wrinkle"). The noun meaning "a wrinkled or curled edge" is from 1570s; the meaning "something superfluous, a showy extra" is from 1826.
