henry:/ˈhenri/ (US), /ˈhɛnri/ (UK)| n.|亨利（男子名）; 亨利（电感的单位）|A unit of inductance, equal to the inductance in a closed circuit in which an electromotive force of one volt is produced when the electric current in the circuit varies uniformly at the rate of one ampere per second.  Also, a male given name.|Henry is a common name in English-speaking countries. 亨利在英语国家是一个常见的名字。
