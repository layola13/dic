gephyrea:/dʒɪˈfɪriə/| n. |星虫动物门|A phylum of marine worms, also known as peanut worms, characterized by a cylindrical body, a retractable introvert (proboscis), and a U-shaped digestive tract.|Gephyrea are found in a variety of marine habitats, from shallow waters to the deep sea. 星虫动物门发现于各种海洋栖息地，从浅水区到深海区。|近义词:Sipuncula (星虫纲，有时被认为是独立的门)|反义词:None|词性:noun

gephyrean:/dʒɪˈfɪriən/| adj. n. |星虫动物门的，星虫动物|Relating to or belonging to the phylum Gephyrea; a worm of the phylum Gephyrea.|The gephyrean worm burrows in the sand.  这种星虫动物在沙子里挖洞。| The museum has a fascinating collection of gephyrean specimens.  博物馆收藏了引人入胜的星虫动物标本。|近义词:sipunculan|反义词:None|词性:adjective, noun
