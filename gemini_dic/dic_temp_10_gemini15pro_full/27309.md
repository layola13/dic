calicular: /kəˈlɪkjʊlər/ | adj. |  杯状的; 萼状的 | Relating to or resembling a calyx (the sepals of a flower, typically forming a whorl that encloses the petals and forms a protective layer around a flower in bud). |  The calicular appendages were small and inconspicuous.  萼状附属物小而不起眼。| Synonym:  calycine, cup-shaped, cuplike | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, consider describing the opposite shape, e.g., linear, acicular) | Word Type: Adjective
