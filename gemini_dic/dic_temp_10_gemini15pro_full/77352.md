gaudinesses:/ˈɡɔːdinəsɪz/
|n.|俗丽，华而不实|The gaudinesses of the decorations were overwhelming.  装饰的俗丽令人眼花缭乱。|
|n.|炫耀，招摇|She disliked the gaudinesses of the new rich. 她不喜欢暴发户的炫耀。|
|近义词:|garishness, flashiness, tawdriness|
|反义词:|simplicity, elegance, understatement|
|词性:|noun (plural)|
|词源:|From gaudy + -ness + -es. Gaudy comes from Middle English gaude, from Old North French gaudir "to rejoice, be joyful," ultimately from Latin gaudere "to rejoice."|
