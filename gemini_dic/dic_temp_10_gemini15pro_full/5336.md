alloy:/ˈælɔɪ/ (US), /ˈælɔɪ/ (UK)| n. |合金|A metal made by combining two or more metallic elements, especially to give greater strength or resistance to corrosion.|例句：Steel is an alloy of iron and carbon. 钢是铁和碳的合金。

例句：The jewelry is made of a gold alloy. 这件珠宝是由黄金合金制成的。

近义词：composite, mixture, blend|反义词：pure metal|词性：名词

alloy:/əˈlɔɪ/ (US), /əˈlɔɪ/ (UK)| vt. |使成合金，掺杂|To mix (a metal) with another metal or other substance to make an alloy.|例句：Copper is often alloyed with tin to make bronze. 铜通常与锡合金化制成青铜。

例句：The metal was alloyed with other elements to increase its strength.  这种金属与其他元素合金化以提高其强度。


近义词：mix, combine, blend|反义词：separate, purify|词性：动词
