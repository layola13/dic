fatuity:/fəˈtjuːɪti/ /fəˈtuːɪti/| n. |愚蠢；愚昧；昏庸；愚蠢的行为；愚蠢的想法|lack of good sense; foolishness; a foolish act or idea|Her fatuity was evident in her decision to invest all her savings in a pyramid scheme. 她的愚蠢表现在她把所有积蓄都投资于传销计划。|Synonyms: foolishness, stupidity, idiocy, absurdity, inanity|Antonyms: wisdom, intelligence, sagacity, prudence, sensibleness|noun

fatuous:/ˈfætʃuəs/| adj. |愚蠢的；昏庸的；愚昧的|silly and pointless|He made a fatuous remark about the weather. 他对天气做了一个愚蠢的评论。|Synonyms: silly, foolish, stupid, inane, asinine|Antonyms: intelligent, sensible, wise, astute, perceptive|adjective
