encamp:/ɪnˈkæmp/ | v. |扎营，宿营 | To set up a camp; to pitch tents for a stay. | The soldiers encamped near the river. 士兵们在河边扎营。|  The travelers encamped for the night in a clearing in the forest.  旅行者们在森林中的一块空地上扎营过夜。|近义词：camp, bivouac, tent |反义词: decamp |词性:动词

encampment:/ɪnˈkæmpmənt/ | n. |营地，宿营地|The act of encamping or the place where a camp is set up. | The refugees set up an encampment on the outskirts of the city. 难民们在城市郊区建立了一个营地。| The summer encampment for the scouts lasted a week. 童子军的夏季营地持续了一周。|近义词：camp, campsite, bivouac |反义词: N/A |词性:名词
