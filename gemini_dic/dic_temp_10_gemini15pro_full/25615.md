bumpkinet:/ˈbʌmpkɪnɪt/
noun
乡巴佬，土包子
A bumpkinet is a derogatory term for a naive, unsophisticated person from the countryside, often considered awkward or foolish.
He called her a bumpkinet, unaware of her extensive education and world travels. 他叫她土包子，却不知道她受过良好的教育，而且周游过世界。
Synonyms: yokel, rustic, hick, country bumpkin
Antonyms: sophisticate, cosmopolitan
词性: 名词


Etymology:
From "bumpkin" (meaning an awkward, unsophisticated country person) + the diminutive suffix "-et".  "Bumpkin" itself likely derives from the Dutch word "boomken" meaning "little tree" or "blockhead."
