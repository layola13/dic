overprovoking:/ˌoʊvərprəˈvoʊkɪŋ/
adjective
过度刺激的，过分挑衅的
Tending to provoke excessively; causing undue provocation.
His overprovoking behavior led to a fight. 他的过分挑衅的行为导致了一场打斗。
近义词:infuriating, exasperating, irritating
反义词:soothing, calming, placating
词性:adjective

overprovoke:/ˌoʊvərprəˈvoʊk/
verb
过度刺激，过分挑衅
To provoke excessively; to cause undue provocation.
The comedian overprovoked the audience with his controversial jokes.  这位喜剧演员用他备受争议的笑话过度刺激了观众。
近义词:infuriate, exasperate, irritate
反义词:soothe, calm, placate
词性:verb


词源:
"Over-" (prefix) + "provoke" (verb)
"Over-" signifies "excessively" or "too much."
"Provoke" comes from the Latin "provocare," meaning "to call forth, challenge."
