barnstormer:/ˈbɑːrnstɔːrmər/| n.|特技飞行员；巡回演出者；雄辩家|A pilot who travels around the country giving exhibition flights and taking passengers for rides, often performing stunts; a person who travels around giving theatrical performances, especially political speeches, in small towns or rural areas.|The barnstormer looped the loop, thrilling the crowd below.  特技飞行员翻了个筋斗，使下面的观众兴奋不已。|
The presidential candidate barnstormed across the state, giving rousing speeches in small towns.  这位总统候选人在全州巡回演讲，在小城镇发表了激动人心的演讲。|Synonyms: stunt pilot, itinerant performer, orator|Antonyms:  settled performer, resident artist|noun
