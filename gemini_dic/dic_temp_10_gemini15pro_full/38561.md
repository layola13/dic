concoct:/kənˈkɒkt/ (UK) /kənˈkɑːkt/ (US)| v.|调制，配制；编造，捏造|To create or devise something, typically a dish or story, by combining various ingredients or elements.|例句：She concocted a delicious soup from leftover vegetables. 她用剩菜调制了一道美味的汤。

例句：He concocted a story about being lost in the woods. 他编造了一个自己在森林里迷路的故事。

近义词：devise, invent, fabricate, formulate, create, prepare, mix|反义词:discover, find|词性:动词

concocter:/kənˈkɒktə(r)/ (UK) /kənˈkɑːktər/ (US)| n.|调制者；捏造者|Someone who concocts something, especially food or a story.|例句：He was a master concocter of cocktails. 他是一位鸡尾酒调制大师。

例句：The journalist was accused of being a concocter of false news. 这名记者被指控捏造假新闻。

近义词：inventor, creator, fabricator|反义词:N/A|词性:名词
