arval:/ˈɑːrvəl/| adj.|耕地的，农事的|Relating to plowing or arable farming. |The arval brethren were an ancient Roman priesthood devoted to the fertility of the fields. 阿尔瓦兄弟会是一个古老的罗马祭司团体，致力于田地的肥沃。|近义词：agricultural, agrarian|反义词：industrial, urban|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *arvalis*, from *arvum* ("plowed field"). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂erh₃- ("to plow").
