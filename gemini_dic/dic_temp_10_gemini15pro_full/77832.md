generator:/ˈdʒɛnəˌreɪtər/ | n. | 发电机；发生器；生成器 | A person or thing that originates or causes something. |  He is a generator of innovative ideas. 他是一个创新思想的创造者。| synonyms: originator, initiator, source | antonyms: imitator, follower | noun
