non-liquidating:/nɒnˈlɪkwɪdeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 非清算的 | Not involving or requiring the liquidation (selling off of assets) of a business or investment.  |  Example: The company offered non-liquidating financing options to its investors.  公司向其投资者提供非清算融资方案。 | Example: They sought a non-liquidating loan to avoid selling their family home. 他们寻求一笔非清算贷款以避免出售他们的家庭住宅。 |  Near synonyms: ongoing, continuing, solvent |  Antonyms: liquidating, dissolving, winding-up | adjective
