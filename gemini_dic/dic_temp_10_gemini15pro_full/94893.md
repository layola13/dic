in-fact:/ɪn ˈfækt/| adv.|事实上，实际上|Used to introduce a statement that contradicts or adds to a previous statement. It emphasizes the truth or importance of the following statement.|I thought he was poor, but in fact, he's quite wealthy. 我以为他很穷，但实际上他很富有。|Synonyms: actually, indeed, as a matter of fact, in reality|Antonyms: supposedly, seemingly, ostensibly|adverb

in-fact:/ɪn ˈfækt/| adv.|事实上，实际上|Used to emphasize a particular point or fact.|He was, in fact, the only person who knew the truth. 事实上，他是唯一知道真相的人。|Synonyms: actually, indeed, as a matter of fact, in reality|Antonyms: supposedly, seemingly, ostensibly|adverb
