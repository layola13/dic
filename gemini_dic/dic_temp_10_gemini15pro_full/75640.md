fugues:/fjuːɡz/
n.
赋格曲；神游症发作
A musical form in which a theme or subject is introduced by one voice or instrument and is imitated by others, each in turn. A state of temporary amnesia during which a person may wander away from their usual surroundings.
The composer's final work included a complex series of fugues.  这位作曲家的最后一部作品包括一系列复杂的赋格曲。
He experienced fugues as a result of the trauma. 他由于创伤经历了神游症发作。
近义词：canon, round (for music); amnesia, flight (for medical condition)
反义词：(No direct antonym for musical form)  lucidity (for medical condition)
词性: Noun
