notchweed:/ˈnɒtʃwiːd/| n.| 缺刻草，戟形蓼|A type of weed characterized by notches on its leaves, specifically Persicaria lapathifolia (synonyms: Polygonum lapathifolium, Lapathum lapathifolium). It's a common weed of arable land and disturbed ground.|The farmer struggled to control the notchweed in his field.|近义词：curlytop knotweed, pale persicaria, willow weed, smartweed (These are related species, not exact synonyms)|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific weed)|n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From "notch" (referring to the leaf shape) + "weed" (a plant considered undesirable).
