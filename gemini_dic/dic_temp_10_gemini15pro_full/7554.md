/əˈnaɪəˈleɪʃənɪst/ | n. | 灵魂毁灭论者 | A person who believes in annihilationism, the doctrine that the wicked will ultimately cease to exist rather than suffer eternal punishment. | Some theologians argue that annihilationism is a more merciful interpretation of scripture than eternal damnation. 一些神学家认为，灵魂毁灭论比永恒的诅咒是对圣经更仁慈的解释。| 近义词: destructionist | 反义词: eternalist | 词性: 名词

/əˌnaɪəˈleɪʃənˈɪstɪk/ | adj. | 灵魂毁灭论的 | Relating to or characteristic of annihilationism. | The preacher delivered an annihilationistic sermon, arguing that the wicked will simply cease to be. 牧师发表了一篇关于灵魂毁灭论的布道，认为恶人只会消失。| 近义词: destructionist | 反义词: eternalist | 词性: 形容词


词源: From Late Latin *annihilation-, from Latin annihilatus, past participle of annihilare ‘reduce to nothing,’ from ad- ‘to’ + nihil ‘nothing.’
