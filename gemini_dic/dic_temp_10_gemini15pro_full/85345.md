harquebuse:/ˈhɑːrkɪbʌs/| n.|火绳枪；早期的一种步枪|A firearm of the 15th and 16th centuries, the first true handheld gun.  It was fired by a matchlock, later by a wheellock.|The soldiers were armed with harquebuses and pikes. 士兵们装备了火绳枪和长矛。|Synonyms:arquebus, matchlock, handgun|Antonyms: N/A (lack of a direct opposite for a specific historical weapon)|noun
