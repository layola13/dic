cerebrifugal:/ˌsɛrɪˈbrɪfjuːɡəl/
adjective
离脑的；自中枢神经系统向外传导的
Conducting or conveying away from the brain; efferent.
Cerebrifugal pathways carry impulses away from the brain. 离脑通路将神经冲动从大脑传输出去。
Cerebrifugal fibers transmit motor impulses from the brain to muscles. 离脑纤维将运动神经冲动从大脑传递到肌肉。
近义词:efferent
反义词:cerebripetal
词性:adjective
