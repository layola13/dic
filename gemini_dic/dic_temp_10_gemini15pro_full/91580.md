hyperactives:/ˌhaɪpərˈæktɪvz/ | n. |多动症患者；过度活跃的人| People who are excessively active, often used to refer to children with ADHD. |The teacher struggled to manage the hyperactives in her classroom. 老师很难管理她班上那些多动症的孩子。|Near Synonyms: overactive children, restless individuals|Antonyms: calm individuals, placid children|Noun

hyperactive:/ˌhaɪpərˈæktɪv/ | adj. |过度活跃的；多动的| Excessively active; exhibiting hyperactivity. |The hyperactive puppy wouldn't stop jumping and running. 这只精力过剩的小狗不停地跳来跳去。|Near Synonyms: restless, energetic, fidgety|Antonyms: calm, quiet, still|Adjective
