colloidality:/ˌkɒlɔɪˈdælɪti/ | n. | 胶体性，胶体状态 | The state of being a colloid; the degree to which a substance exhibits the properties of a colloid. | The colloidality of the solution was determined by light scattering experiments.  该溶液的胶体性是通过光散射实验测定的。| synonyms: colloidal state, colloidal nature | antonyms: non-colloidal state, non-colloidal nature | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From colloid + -ality.  "Colloid" comes from the Greek "kolla" meaning "glue" and "-oid" meaning "resembling".
