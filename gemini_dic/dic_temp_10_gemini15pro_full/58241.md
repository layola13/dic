drum-out:/drʌmˈaʊt/| v. | 驱逐，开除，轰走 | To expel someone from a group or organization, usually with disgrace or public shame. | The soldier was drummed out of the army for desertion.  这名士兵因逃兵役而被开除军籍。

The corrupt official was drummed out of the government.  这位腐败官员被政府开除。| 近义词: expel, dismiss, discharge, cashier | 反义词: reinstate, admit, welcome | 词性: 动词

drum-out:/ˈdrʌmˌaʊt/| n. | 驱逐，开除，轰走 | The act of expelling someone with disgrace. | His drum-out from the club was a humiliating experience. 他被俱乐部开除是一次屈辱的经历。

The drum-out ceremony was witnessed by the entire regiment. 整个军团都目睹了开除仪式。 | 近义词: expulsion, dismissal, discharge | 反义词: reinstatement, admission | 词性: 名词
