disindividualize:/diːˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlaɪz/
verb
使失去个性；使丧失个性
To deprive of individuality, to make impersonal or anonymous.
The anonymity of the internet can disindividualize people, leading to online disinhibition.  互联网的匿名性会使人们失去个性，导致网络去抑制化。
The soldiers were disindividualized by their uniforms and shaved heads. 士兵们因穿着制服和剃光头而失去了个性。
Synonyms: depersonalize, anonymize, dehumanize
Antonyms: individualize, personalize, distinguish
词性:动词
