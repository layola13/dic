naphtha:/ˈnæpθə/ | n. | 石脑油，粗汽油 |A flammable liquid hydrocarbon mixture obtained from petroleum distillation, lighter than kerosene and used as a solvent, fuel, and in the manufacture of various chemicals.| Naphtha is used as a raw material for producing plastics. 石脑油被用作生产塑料的原料。| The paint thinner contains naphtha.  这油漆稀释剂含有石脑油。 | synonyms: petroleum, gasoline, benzine | antonyms: water | noun
