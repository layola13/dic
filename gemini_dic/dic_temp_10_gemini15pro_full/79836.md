glyph:/ɡlɪf/ | n. | 象形文字，符号 | A symbolic figure or character, typically carved or incised. | Example: The ancient Egyptians used glyphs to write their language. 古埃及人使用象形文字书写他们的语言。 | Example: The software can convert digital text into glyphs.  这款软件可以将数字文本转换成象形文字。 | Synonym: symbol, character, sign, mark | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps "plain text" in a computing context) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *glyphe*, from Greek *gluphē* "carving," from *gluphein* "to carve."
