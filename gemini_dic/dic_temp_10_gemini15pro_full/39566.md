It's rarely used as "consciousness" usually encompasses the concept of multiple facets of awareness. |  He studied the various consciousnesses of the patients after the trauma. 他研究了病人在创伤后的各种意识状态。| 近义词: awareness, sentience | 反义词: unconsciousness | 词性: 名词
