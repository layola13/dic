casein:/ˈkeɪsiːn/ (US), /ˈkeɪsiːɪn/ (UK)| n.|酪蛋白；干酪素|A milk protein used in making cheese and glue.|Casein is a major component of cheese. 酪蛋白是奶酪的主要成分。|Milk solids are made up of roughly 80% casein and 20% whey protein. 牛奶固体大约由80%的酪蛋白和20%的乳清蛋白组成。|近义词：milk protein|反义词:None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *caséine*, from Latin *caseus* "cheese".
