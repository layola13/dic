hobbyism:/ˈhɒbiɪzəm/ | n. | 业余爱好主义；沉迷于业余爱好 | The excessive pursuit of hobbies, sometimes to the detriment of other responsibilities. | He devoted himself to hobbyism, neglecting his family and work. 他沉迷于业余爱好，忽略了家庭和工作。|  近义词: dilettantism, amateurism | 反义词: professionalism | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From hobby + -ism.  "Hobby" originates from the Middle English "hobi," a small horse or pony, later referring to a favorite pastime. "-ism" is a suffix forming nouns meaning a practice, system, doctrine, etc.
