cough up:/ˈkɒf ʌp/ (British) /ˈkɔːf ʌp/ (American)| phrasal verb |咳出;  勉强说出(尤指钱); 提供(尤指不情愿地)|To expel something from the lungs or throat by coughing. Also, to reluctantly give something, especially money.|He coughed up some phlegm. 他咳出了一些痰。|Synonyms: expectorate, relinquish, fork over, shell out|Antonyms: keep, retain|verb phrase
