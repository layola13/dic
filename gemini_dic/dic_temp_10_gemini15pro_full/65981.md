exaltate: /ɪɡˈzɔːlˌteɪt/ | v. |  高举；提升；赞扬；使兴奋 | To praise highly; glorify. To raise in rank, power, or character. To intensify or heighten (a feeling or quality). | The king was exalted by his loyal subjects after the great victory. 国王在大胜后受到忠诚臣民的拥戴。 The poet exalted the beauty of nature in his verses. 诗人在他的诗句中赞美了大自然的美丽。 | 近义词: praise, glorify, elevate, extol, laud | 反义词: demean, degrade, humble, criticize | 词性: 动词
词源: From Late Latin exaltatus, past participle of exaltare "to lift up, elevate," from Latin ex- "up" + altus "high."
