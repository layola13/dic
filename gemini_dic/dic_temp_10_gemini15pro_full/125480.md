multipresence:/ˌmʌltiˈprɛzəns/
n.|多重存在，多地存在|The state of being present in multiple locations, either physically through telepresence technology or virtually through online avatars or social media profiles.|She maintained a multipresence through various social media platforms, engaging with different audiences simultaneously. 她通过各种社交媒体平台保持多重存在，同时与不同的受众互动。|近义词：ubiquity, omnipresence|反义词：absence|词性：名词

Etymology:
multi- (prefix): from Latin multus "much, many"
presence: from Old French presence, from Latin praesentia "a being present," from praesent- (stem of praesens) "present, at hand," from prae- "before" + essent- (stem of esse) "to be."
