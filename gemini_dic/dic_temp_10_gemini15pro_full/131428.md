noncloistered:/nɒnˈklɔɪstəd/ | adj. | 非隐居的，非与世隔绝的 | Not cloistered; living and working among ordinary people, rather than being secluded in a monastery or convent. |  Example: The noncloistered nuns work in schools and hospitals. 这些非隐居的修女在学校和医院工作。

Example: He chose a noncloistered life, preferring to engage with the world. 他选择了一种非隐居的生活，更喜欢与世界接触。 | 近义词: worldly, secular, lay | 反义词: cloistered, monastic, secluded | 词性: 形容词
