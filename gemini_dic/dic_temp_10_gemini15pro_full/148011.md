overrigorousnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈrɪɡərəsnəsɪz/
|n.| 过于严谨；过分严格 |The overrigorousnesses of the rules stifled creativity. 规则的过分严谨扼杀了创造力。|
|n.| 过于苛刻 |The overrigorousnesses of the training regime led to several injuries.  过于苛刻的训练方案导致了几人受伤。|
|近义词:|strictness, severity, rigidity, inflexibility|
|反义词:|leniency, laxity, flexibility, tolerance|
|词性:|noun|


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + rigorousness.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," and "rigorousness" is the noun form of "rigorous," meaning "strict" or "severe."  "Rigorous" ultimately comes from the Latin word "rigor," meaning "stiffness" or "harshness."
