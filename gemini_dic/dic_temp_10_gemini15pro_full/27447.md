camouflageable:/ˌkæməˈflɑːʒəbl/
adjective
可伪装的，可掩饰的
able to be camouflaged, or disguised to blend in with the surroundings.
The chameleon is a highly camouflageable animal. 变色龙是一种高度可伪装的动物。
The soldiers wore camouflageable uniforms in the jungle. 士兵们在丛林中穿着迷彩服。
近义词:disguisable, concealable
反义词:conspicuous, noticeable
词性:adjective

词源:
From camouflage + -able.  Camouflage itself comes from French camoufler, "to disguise," from the slang term camouflet, meaning "a whiff of smoke in the face."
