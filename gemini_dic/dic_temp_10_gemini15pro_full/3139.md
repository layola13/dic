aestheticism:/iːsˈθetɪsɪzəm/ | n. | 审美主义; 唯美主义 | A doctrine that the principles of beauty are central to other, especially moral, principles; devotion to beauty in art, literature, etc.  |  The artist's work was a clear example of aestheticism, prioritizing beauty above all else.  这位艺术家的作品是审美主义的鲜明例证，将美置于一切之上。 |  Synonyms:  art for art's sake, aesthetic movement, beauty worship | Antonyms:  philistinism, utilitarianism | noun
