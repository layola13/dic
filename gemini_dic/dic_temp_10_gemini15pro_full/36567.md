collapsing:/kəˈlæpsɪŋ/ | adj. | 倒塌的，崩溃的，瓦解的 |  present participle of collapse, meaning falling down, failing suddenly, or shrinking. | The collapsing building filled the street with dust.  倒塌的建筑物使街道上充满了灰尘。| synonyms: crumbling, failing, disintegrating, shrinking | antonyms: rising, growing, expanding, strengthening | verb (present participle)

collapsing:/kəˈlæpsɪŋ/ | n. | 倒塌，崩溃 |  the act of falling down, failing, or shrinking. | The collapsing of the bridge cut off access to the town.  桥梁的倒塌切断了通往城镇的通道。| synonyms: downfall, failure, disintegration, shrinkage | antonyms: rise, growth, expansion, strengthening | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *collapsus*, past participle of *collabī* ("to fall together, fall down"), from *com-* ("together") + *labī* ("to slip, slide, fall").
