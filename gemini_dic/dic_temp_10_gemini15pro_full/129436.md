nomothetical:/ˌnɒməˈθɛtɪk(ə)l/ | adj. |  立法式的；有关规律制定的；普遍适用的 | Relating to or based on the study or establishment of general laws or principles. | Psychology is a nomothetic science, seeking general laws of behavior. 心理学是一门法则性的科学，它寻求行为的普遍规律。 |  例句：Nomothetic research aims to establish general principles that apply to a population. 法则性研究旨在建立适用于一个群体的普遍原则。

例句：The nomothetic approach contrasts with the idiographic approach, which focuses on individual cases. 法则性方法与个案方法形成对比，个案方法侧重于个别案例。 | 近义词：general, universal, law-based, principled | 反义词：idiographic, particular, individual | 词性：形容词
