palmitin:/ˈpæl.mə.tɪn/ | n. | 甘油三棕榈酸酯；棕榈精 | A white, crystalline fat, C₃H₅(C₁₅H₃₁COO)₃, the glyceryl ester of palmitic acid, found in many natural fats and oils.  It is used in making soaps, candles, and lubricants. | Palmitin is a major component of palm oil. 棕榈精是棕榈油的主要成分。| Palmitin is also found in butterfat. 棕榈精也存在于乳脂中。 | synonym: tripalmitin, glyceryl tripalmitate | antonym:  (No direct antonym for a specific chemical compound.  One could consider unsaturated fats as a broader contrasting category.) | noun
