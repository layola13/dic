cafeterias:/ˌkæfəˈtɪəriəz/| n. |自助餐厅|A restaurant or dining room in a school, factory, office building, etc., where people serve themselves or are served from a counter and pay before eating. | The school cafeterias are always crowded during lunch break. 学校自助餐厅在午餐时间总是很拥挤。| The company has two cafeterias on site. 公司有两个内部自助餐厅。| synonyms: canteen, lunchroom, dining hall, refectory | antonyms: restaurant, cafe | noun
