hyperperfections:/ˌhaɪpər.pərˈfekʃənz/ | n. | 超完美，极度完美 | A state or quality of being beyond perfect, often in a theoretical or exaggerated sense.  It implies an excessive or unattainable level of perfection. |  The artist strove for hyperperfections in his work, obsessing over every minute detail.  艺术家在他的作品中追求极致的完美，对每一个细微的细节都痴迷不已。| Synonyms: flawlessness, impeccability, faultlessness | Antonyms: imperfection, flaw, defect | noun
