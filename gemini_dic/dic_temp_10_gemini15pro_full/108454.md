lay-a-course:/ˌleɪ ə ˈkɔːrs/| phrasal verb | 设定航线；规划路线| To set a ship's course or direction; to plan a route or direction. |The captain laid a course for the nearest port. 船长设定了前往最近港口的航线。|例句：They laid a course for the island. 他们设定了前往岛屿的航线。|近义词：chart a course, plot a course, set a course, navigate |反义词: drift, wander |词性: 短语动词

lay-a-course:/ˌleɪ ə ˈkɔːrs/| phrasal verb | 采取行动；计划行动步骤| To take a course of action; to plan the steps to achieve something. |The company laid a course for expansion into new markets. 公司制定了进军新市场的扩张计划。 |例句：He laid a course to improve his health. 他制定了改善健康的计划。|近义词：plan, strategize, prepare, devise |反义词: improvise, react |词性: 短语动词
