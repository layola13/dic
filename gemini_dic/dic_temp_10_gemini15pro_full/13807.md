audit-trail:/ˈɔːdɪt treɪl/ | n. | 审计跟踪，审计线索 | A chronological record of system activities that enables the reconstruction and examination of the sequence of events and/or changes in an event. |  The company maintains a detailed audit trail of all financial transactions.  该公司保留所有金融交易的详细审计线索。 | synonyms: audit log, audit record, activity log, transaction log | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but the lack of an audit trail implies obscurity and potential for unaccountability) | noun

audit-trails:/ˈɔːdɪt treɪlz/ | n. | 审计跟踪（复数） | Multiple chronological records of system activities. |  Regularly reviewing the audit trails helps to ensure data integrity. 定期审查审计跟踪有助于确保数据完整性。 | synonyms: audit logs, audit records, activity logs, transaction logs | antonyms: (No direct antonyms) | noun
