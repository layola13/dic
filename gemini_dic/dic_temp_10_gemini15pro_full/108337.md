lavatic:/ləˈvætɪk/ | adj. |熔岩的；火山产生的；|Of or pertaining to lava; formed by lava. | The geologist studied the lavatic rock formations. 地质学家研究了熔岩岩层。| The volcano spewed lavatic flows for miles. 火山喷出的熔岩流绵延数英里。 | 近义词:volcanic, igneous | 反义词:sedimentary | 词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *lavatus*, past participle of *lavare* "to wash," referring to the molten rock "washing" over the land.
