chaunter:/ˈtʃɔːntər/ (UK), /ˈtʃæntər/ (US)| n. |吟诵者；诵唱者；街头歌手|A person who chants, especially a street singer or seller who uses a chant to advertise their wares.|The chaunter sang a ballad in the marketplace.  市场上的吟游诗人唱了一首民谣。|Synonyms: singer, vocalist, minstrel, performer|Antonyms: listener, audience|noun

chaunter:/ˈtʃɔːntər/ (UK), /ˈtʃæntər/ (US)| v. |吟诵；反复唱；叫卖|To sing or intone, often in a monotonous or repetitive way, especially to advertise goods.|The fishmonger chaunted his wares loudly to attract customers.  鱼贩大声叫卖他的货物以吸引顾客。|Synonyms: chant, intone, sing, call, hawk|Antonyms: whisper, listen|verb
