open-mouthedly:/ˌoʊpənˈmaʊðɪdli/ /ˌəʊpənˈmaʊθɪdli/
|adv.|张大着嘴地，目瞪口呆地|With mouth wide open, expressing surprise or astonishment. |She stared open-mouthedly at the magician as he made the rabbit disappear. 她目瞪口呆地看着魔术师变走兔子。|
|open-mouthedly:|He listened open-mouthedly to the incredible story. 他张大着嘴听着这难以置信的故事。|
|近义词:|agape, astounded, amazed, awestruck, dumbfounded, speechless|
|反义词:|unimpressed, bored, indifferent|
|词性:|adverb|
|词源:|From "open-mouthed" + "-ly."  "Open-mouthed" is a compound adjective formed from "open" and "mouthed."  "Open" traces back to Old English "open," and "mouth" to Old English "mūþ." The suffix "-ly" transforms adjectives into adverbs.|
