goffer:/ˈɡɒfər/ (US) /ˈɡɒfə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 蜂窝; 蜂窝煤; 华夫饼; (某些动物挖的)洞穴; 地鼠|A gopher is a burrowing rodent with fur-lined pouches on the outside of its cheeks.  A gopher is also a honeycomb or a waffle, or a burrow made by certain animals.|例句：The gopher had dug a network of tunnels under the lawn. 地鼠在草坪下挖了一条隧道网络。

例句：She made delicious gophers (waffles) for breakfast. 她早餐做了美味的华夫饼。|近义词：waffle (for the food), burrow (for the hole), groundhog, prairie dog (for the animal)|反义词:  (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
