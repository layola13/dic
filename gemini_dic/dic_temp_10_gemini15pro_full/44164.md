criticist: /ˈkrɪtɪsɪst/ | n. | 批评家；评论家 | A person who judges the merits of something, especially works of art, literature, or music. |  The critic praised the film's innovative cinematography.  这位影评人称赞了这部电影的创新性摄影。| synonym: reviewer, judge, evaluator | antonym: supporter, admirer | noun

criticist: /ˈkrɪtɪsɪst/ | n. | 吹毛求疵的人；爱挑剔的人 | A person who habitually finds fault or makes harsh judgments. | He's such a criticist, nothing ever pleases him. 他真是个爱挑剔的人，什么都让他不满意。| synonym: faultfinder, nitpicker, carper | antonym:  optimist, Pollyanna | noun
