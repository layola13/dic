dnn:/ˌdiː ɛnˈɛn/ | n. | 深度神经网络 (Deep Neural Network)|A deep neural network (DNN) is an artificial neural network (ANN) with multiple layers between the input and output layers. |DNNs are typically used for complex tasks such as image recognition and natural language processing.| 近义词: ANN (Artificial Neural Network),  deep learning model | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "shallow neural network" in specific contexts) | 词性: noun
