hyposulphuric:/ˌhaɪpoʊsʌlˈfjʊərɪk/ | adj. | 连二硫酸的;焦硫酸的 | relating to or containing hyposulphuric acid (also called dithionic acid), an unstable strong dibasic acid H2S2O6 known only in solution | Hyposulphuric acid is a powerful oxidizing agent. 连二硫酸是一种强氧化剂。 | 近义词: dithionic | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially reducing agents in a chemical context) | 词性: adjective

hyposulphate:/ˌhaɪpoʊˈsʌlfeɪt/ | n. | 连二硫酸盐;焦硫酸盐 | a salt of hyposulphuric acid (also called dithionic acid) | Sodium hyposulphate is a relatively stable compound. 连二硫酸钠是一种相对稳定的化合物。| 近义词: dithionate | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From hypo- (Greek: 'under', 'less than') + sulphur + -ic (suffix forming adjectives).  The term reflects the lower oxidation state of sulfur in hyposulphuric acid (dithionic acid) compared to sulphuric acid.  Similarly, "hyposulphate" (dithionate) is derived by replacing the -ic ending with -ate, denoting a salt of the acid.
