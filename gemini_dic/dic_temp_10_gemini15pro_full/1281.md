accoucheuse: /ˌækʊˈʃɜːz/ (UK), /ˌɑːkuˈʃɜːz/ (US)| n.|助产士；接生婆|A woman who assists at childbirth.|例句：The accoucheuse helped deliver the baby safely. 接生婆帮助安全地接生了婴儿。

例句：The experienced accoucheuse calmed the anxious mother. 经验丰富的助产士使焦虑的母亲平静下来。

近义词：midwife, birth attendant|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French accoucheuse, from accoucher "to deliver a child," from à "to" + coucher "to put to bed." Ultimately from Latin collocare "to place."
