nonexcusably:/ˌnɒnɪkˈskjuːzəbli/
adverb
不可原谅地
in a way that cannot be excused
His behavior was nonexcusably rude. 他的行为粗鲁得不可原谅。
近义词: unforgivably, unpardonably, inexcusably
反义词: excusably, justifiably, pardonably
词性: adverb
词源: non- (not) + excusable + -ly (adverb suffix). From "excusable," which comes from Old French "excusable" and ultimately from Latin "excusare" (to excuse).
