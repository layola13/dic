carpophagous:/kɑːrˈpɒfəɡəs/
adjective
食果的
Feeding on fruit; frugivorous.
The carpophagous bat feeds primarily on figs and other soft fruits.  这种食果蝙蝠主要以无花果和其他软水果为食。
Synonyms: frugivorous, fruit-eating
Antonyms: carnivorous, herbivorous
词性: adjective
