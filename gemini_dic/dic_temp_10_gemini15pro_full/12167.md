aryanism: /ˈɛəriənɪzəm/ | n. | 雅利安主义，雅利安人至上论 | The ideology that asserts the superiority of the Aryan race.  A racist ideology that holds that Aryans are a superior race. |  The Nazis' belief in Aryanism led to the Holocaust. 纳粹对雅利安主义的信仰导致了大屠杀。|  Hitler's regime promoted Aryanism as a justification for its expansionist policies. 希特勒政权宣扬雅利安主义，以此作为其扩张政策的理由。 | 近义词: racism, white supremacy | 反义词: equality, racial tolerance | 词性: noun

词源: From Aryan + -ism.  Aryan comes from Sanskrit *arya- "noble."
