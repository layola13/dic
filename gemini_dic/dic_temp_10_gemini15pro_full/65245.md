etwee:/eɪˈtwiː/
noun
小盒子；化妆盒；针线盒
A small ornamental case, often used to hold sewing implements or toiletries.
She took a tiny pair of scissors from her etwee. 她从她的小盒子里拿出一把小剪刀。
Synonyms: case, box, container, compact
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps bulk storage)
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French étui 'case', from Old French estuier 'to keep safe', from Latin studium 'zeal, application'.
