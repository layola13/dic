geode:/ˈdʒiːoʊd/| n. |晶洞； geodes(复数)|A hollow rock inside which crystals have grown.|例句：I found a beautiful geode while hiking in the mountains. 我在山上徒步旅行时发现了一个美丽的晶洞。

例句：The geode was filled with sparkling amethyst crystals.  这个晶洞里充满了闪耀的紫水晶。|近义词：vug, cavity, druse|反义词: - |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French géode, from Latin geōda, from Greek geōdēs 'earthlike', from gē 'earth'.
