cataphoras:/kəˈtæfərəz/ | n. | 前指 | A cataphor is a word or expression that refers forward to another word or expression that is used later in the text. |  Here's the deal: we're going to the beach. (Here's the deal refers forward to we're going to the beach.) | 近义词:forward reference | 反义词: anaphora | 词性:noun
