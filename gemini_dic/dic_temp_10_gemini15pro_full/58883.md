durative:/ˈdjʊərətɪv/ /ˈdʊrətɪv/| adj.|持续的，延续的|Lasting; continuing for a long time.  Referring to an action or state that extends over a period of time.|The durative aspect of the verb indicates an ongoing process.|近义词：continuous, prolonged, enduring, persistent, sustained|反义词：brief, short, momentary, transient|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dūrātīvus, from Latin dūrātus (past participle of dūrāre ‘to harden, last’), from dūrus ‘hard’.
