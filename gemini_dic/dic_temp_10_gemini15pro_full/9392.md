antiquenesses:/ænˈtiːkwɪtnɪsɪz/
n.
古代; 古老; 古董; 古物
the quality of being ancient; ancient things
例句：The museum is full of antiquities from Egypt.  这家博物馆里收藏着许多来自埃及的古物。

例句：She was fascinated by the antiquity of the ruins. 她被这些遗迹的古老所吸引。

近义词：ancientness, oldness, relics, artifacts
反义词：modernity, newness

词性: 名词 (plural of antiquity)

词源: From Middle French *antiquete*, from Latin antiquitas (“old age, antiquity”), from antiquus (“old, ancient”).
