manoeuvre:/məˈnuːvər/ (UK) /məˈnuːvər/ (US)| n. v.|策略，调动，操控|近义词: tactic, strategy, operation, maneuver (US), ploy|反义词: inaction, standstill|词性: 名词, 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *manoeuvrer*, from Old French *manovrer* "to work by hand," from Latin *manu operari* "to work by hand," from *manu*, ablative of *manus* "hand," + *operari* "to work."
