dispensatively:/dɪˈspɛnsətɪvli/
| adv. |分配地；施予地| In a dispensing manner; distributively.  Relating to or characteristic of dispensing. | The funds were dispensatively allocated among the various departments.  这些资金被分配给了各个部门。|近义词：distributively, proportionately|反义词: collectively,  indiscriminately|词性:副词

词源:
From dispense + -ative + -ly.  Dispense comes from Latin *dispensare, from dis- "apart, asunder" + pensare "to weigh, pay, compensate."
