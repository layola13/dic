fork-over:/fɔːrk ˈoʊvər/ | phrasal verb | 交出，付出（尤指不情愿地） | To hand over something, especially money, reluctantly. |  例句：I had to fork over a lot of money for that car. 我不得不为那辆车付出一大笔钱。

例句：He finally forked over the cash he owed me. 他最终交出了欠我的钱。

近义词：hand over, cough up, shell out, pay up | 反义词：keep, withhold | 词性：动词短语
