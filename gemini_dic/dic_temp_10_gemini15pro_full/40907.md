coolants:/ˈkuːlənts/ | n. | 冷却剂；冷却液 | Substances used to reduce or regulate the temperature of a system or object, often in engines or industrial processes. |  The car's engine overheated because the coolants leaked out. 汽车发动机过热是因为冷却液泄漏了。

The nuclear reactor uses water as a coolant. 核反应堆用水作冷却剂。 | 近义词:refrigerants, antifreeze | 反义词:heating agents | 词性:名词
