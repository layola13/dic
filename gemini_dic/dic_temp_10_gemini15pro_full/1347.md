acculturates: /əˈkʌltʃəreɪts/
verb
使适应文化，使同化
to adapt or adjust to the cultural traits or patterns of another group
Many immigrants acculturate to the customs of their new country. 许多移民适应了他们新国家的习俗。
Immigrants often struggle to acculturate to a new society.  移民通常很难适应新的社会。

近义词: assimilate, adapt, adjust, integrate
反义词: isolate, segregate
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin 'ad-' (to) + 'cultura' (culture).  Meaning "to adapt to a culture."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He acculturates quickly to new environments. = 他很快适应新环境。
She acculturates by learning the local language. = 她通过学习当地语言来适应环境。

一般过去时:
They acculturated to the new culture within a few years. = 他们在几年内适应了新的文化。
He acculturated more easily than his family. = 他比他的家人更容易适应。

现在完成时:
The family has acculturated to life in the city. = 这家人已经适应了城市生活。
She has acculturated well to her new job. = 她已经很好地适应了她的新工作。

过去完成时:
By the time he started school, he had already acculturated to the local customs. = 到他上学的时候，他已经适应了当地的习俗。
They had acculturated to the new language before moving abroad. = 在移居国外之前，他们已经适应了新的语言。

将来时:
The students will acculturate to university life quickly. = 学生们将很快适应大学生活。
He will acculturate to the new team dynamics over time. = 他会随着时间的推移适应新的团队动态。


被动语态:
The children were acculturated into the dominant culture. = 孩子们被同化到主流文化中。
New employees are being acculturated into the company culture. = 新员工正在适应公司文化。


PHRASE->
acculturate to (a new environment/culture/society) = 适应（新环境/文化/社会）
acculturate quickly/slowly = 快速/缓慢适应
acculturate successfully = 成功适应
process of acculturating = 适应过程
the difficulty of acculturating = 适应的困难
