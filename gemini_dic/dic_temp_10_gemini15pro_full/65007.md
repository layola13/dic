estrepement:/ɪˈstriːpmənt/| n.| 损毁，破坏（尤指土地或财产）|The act of stripping land of its trees or other resources, causing damage or deterioration.  It often refers to waste or damage to property, especially during tenancy.|The landlord sued the tenant for estrepement after discovering the clear-cutting of trees on the property. 房东发现租客在房产上砍伐树木后，起诉租客损毁财产。| 近义词: damage, waste, deterioration, despoilment, devastation | 反义词: improvement, restoration, repair | 词性: noun

estrepement:/ɪˈstriːpmənt/| n.| 废弃物，垃圾| Waste material or refuse, especially that which is left behind after something has been destroyed or damaged.|The estrepement left behind after the demolition of the building was considerable.  建筑物拆除后留下的废弃物相当多。| 近义词: debris, rubble, waste, refuse | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun
