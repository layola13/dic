angstiest:/ˈæŋstiɪst/| adj.|最焦虑的，最不安的|Superlative form of "angsty," meaning filled with or exhibiting angst, a feeling of deep anxiety or dread, typically an existential one.|例句：She wrote the angstiest poems in the entire class. 她写了全班最焦虑的诗。
