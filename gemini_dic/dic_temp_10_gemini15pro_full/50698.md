dermis:/ˈdɜːrmɪs/ /ˈdɝːmɪs/
n.
真皮
The dermis is the layer of skin beneath the epidermis.
真皮是表皮下面的皮肤层。

The dermis contains blood vessels, nerves, and hair follicles.
真皮包含血管、神经和毛囊。

Synonym: corium, cutis
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider epidermis as a contrasting layer)
Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek δέρμα (dérma, “skin, hide”).
