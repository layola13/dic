overtints:/ˌoʊvərˈtɪnts/
verb
过度着色
To tint or color excessively, resulting in a shade that is too dark or intense.
The artist overtinted the canvas, creating a muddy effect. 艺术家过度着色画布，造成浑浊的效果。
overcolor, overdye
undertint
v.
