oversystematized: /ˌoʊvərˈsɪstəmətʌɪzd/
| adj. | 过度系统化的，过度组织的 | Excessively organized or structured, often to the point of inefficiency or rigidity.  Applying a system or structure too rigidly or excessively. |  The oversystematized approach stifled creativity and led to inflexible solutions.  这个过度系统化的方法扼杀了创造力，导致解决方案僵化。| Near Synonyms: overorganized, overstructured, rigid, inflexible | Antonyms: disorganized, unstructured, flexible, adaptable
词性: 形容词
