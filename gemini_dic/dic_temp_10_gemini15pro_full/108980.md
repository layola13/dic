一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Let's eat lunch now. 我们现在吃午饭吧。
Let's begin the meeting.  我们开始开会吧。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Let's not be wasting any more time. 我们别再浪费时间了。  (Technically, this uses "be" + present participle, which is part of the continuous aspect, even though the meaning is similar to a simple present.)
Let's be starting now, we're late. 我们现在开始吧，我们迟到了。

虚拟语气:
Let's pretend we're on a tropical island.  让我们假装我们在一个热带岛屿上。
Let's suppose, for a moment, that we win the lottery. 让我们假设一下，我们中了彩票。


被动语态:
N/A ("Let's" cannot form a passive construction.)



疑问句:
Let's go, shall we? 我们走吧，好吗？ (Technically, this adds a tag question, not a change to "let's" itself.)
Let's not argue, okay? 我们别争论了，好吗？


否定句:
Let's not go there.  我们别去那儿。
Let's not forget to call Mom. 我们别忘了给妈妈打电话.




PHRASE->
Let's face it = 面对现实吧
Let's get started = 我们开始吧
Let's get this straight = 我们把话说清楚
Let's get to the point = 我们开门见山吧
Let's hope for the best = 让我们抱最好的希望


注意事项:
"Let's" is always followed by the base form of the verb.



词源: "Let's" is a contraction of "let us."  "Let" comes from Old English *lætan, meaning "to allow" or "to cause." "Us" is the objective form of the pronoun "we."
