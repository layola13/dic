grosser:/ˈɡroʊsər/ (US) /ˈɡrəʊsə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 食品杂货商；批发商|A person who buys and sells goods in large quantities, especially food or other household goods. | 例句：The grosser supplies fresh produce to local restaurants.  这家批发商向当地餐馆供应新鲜农产品。

例句：He started his career as a grosser's assistant. 他以食品杂货商助理的身份开始了他的职业生涯。

近义词：wholesaler, distributor, supplier, merchant, trader|反义词：retailer, consumer|词性：名词

词源：From Middle English *grossere, from Anglo-Norman grossour, grossier, from Old French grossier (“wholesale merchant”), from gros (“large, big; coarse”), from Late Latin grossus (“thick, coarse, fat”).
