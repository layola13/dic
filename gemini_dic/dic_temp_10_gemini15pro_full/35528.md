coagulate:/koʊˈæɡjʊleɪt/
|v. | 凝结，凝固|Blood coagulates to form a clot. 血液凝结形成血块。|The milk coagulated when we added the vinegar. 当我们加入醋时，牛奶凝结了。|近义词：clot, thicken, congeal|反义词：liquefy, thin, dissolve|词性: Verb

Etymology: From Late Latin coagulātus, past participle of coagulāre, from Latin co- + agulāre “to curdle,” from agulum “rennet.”
