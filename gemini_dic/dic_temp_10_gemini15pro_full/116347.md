masterworks:/ˈmæstərˌwɜːrks/ /ˈmɑːstərˌwɜːrks/| n.|杰作；名作|A masterpiece or a collection of masterpieces, especially in art, literature, or music.  A work of outstanding artistry or skill.|例句：The museum houses a collection of masterworks by Renaissance painters.  这家博物馆收藏了文艺复兴时期画家们的杰作。

例句：Her latest novel is considered one of the masterworks of contemporary literature. 她最新的小说是当代文学的杰作之一。|近义词：masterpieces, classics, chef-d'oeuvres|反义词:junk, trash|词性:名词
