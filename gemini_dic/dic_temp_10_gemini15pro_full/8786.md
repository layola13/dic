antiegoism:/ˌæntiˈiːɡoʊɪzəm/| n. |反自我主义; 利他主义 |The doctrine or practice of altruism; the opposite of egoism.| He demonstrated antiegoism by donating all his earnings to charity. 他将所有收入捐给慈善机构，展现了反自我主义精神。 | 近义词: altruism, selflessness | 反义词: egoism, selfishness | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek _anti_)
egoism (noun):  excessive concern for oneself; selfishness (from Latin _ego_ "I" + -ism)
