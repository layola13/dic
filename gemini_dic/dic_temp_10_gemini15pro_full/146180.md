overexcited:/ˌōvərɪkˈsaɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过于兴奋的，过度激动的 | Too excited; in a state of excessive excitement. | The children were overexcited about their trip to Disneyland. 孩子们对他们的迪士尼乐园之旅感到非常兴奋。|  The overexcited puppy ran in circles, barking excitedly. 过于兴奋的小狗兴奋地吠叫着，绕着圈跑。| 近义词: thrilled, exhilarated, ecstatic | 反义词: calm, composed, tranquil | 词性: 形容词

词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessively") + excited (past participle of excite, from Latin excitare "to rouse, stir up")
