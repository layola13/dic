gravitation:/ˌɡrævɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. |重力，引力；地心吸力 | The force of gravitation pulls objects towards the center of the Earth.  | 例句：Newton's law of universal gravitation explains the force of attraction between any two objects with mass. 牛顿万有引力定律解释了任何两个有质量的物体之间的吸引力。

例句：The gravitation between the Earth and the moon causes the tides. 地球和月球之间的引力引起了潮汐。

近义词：gravity, attraction | 反义词: repulsion | 词性: noun
