oversleep: /ˌōvərˈslēp/ (US), /ˌəʊvəˈsliːp/ (UK)| v. |睡过头；睡得太久|To sleep longer than intended or necessary.|I overslept this morning and missed my train. 我今天早上睡过头了，错过了火车。|He overslept and was late for work. 他睡过头了，上班迟到了。|近义词：sleep in, sleep late|反义词：wake up early, get up early|词性:动词

oversleeping: /ˌōvərˈslēpɪŋ/ (US), /ˌəʊvəˈsliːpɪŋ/ (UK) | n. |睡过头；睡得太久|The act of sleeping past the intended time.|Oversleeping can make you feel groggy. 睡过头会让你感到昏昏沉沉的。|My oversleeping caused me to miss the meeting. 我的睡过头导致我错过了会议。|近义词：sleeping in, late sleeping|反义词：early rising|词性:名词

词源: From Old English *ofer-slæpan, from ofer (over) + slæpan (to sleep).
