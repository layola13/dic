antelope:/ˈæntɪloʊp/ (US), /ˈæntɪləʊp/ (UK)| n.|羚羊|A swift-running deer-like ruminant with smooth hair and upward-pointing horns, native to Africa and Asia.|
The antelope grazed peacefully on the savanna. 羚羊在热带草原上平静地吃草。

The cheetah chased the antelope across the plains. 猎豹在平原上追逐羚羊。

近义词：gazelle, springbok, hartebeest|
反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑predator 捕食者)|
词性: noun

词源：From Old French *antelop (modern French antilope), from Medieval Latin antalopus, from Byzantine Greek ανθόλοψ, anthólops, of uncertain origin.  It may be related to the Greek words anthos (flower) and ops (eye), referring to the animal's beautiful eyes.  However, the origin remains debated.
