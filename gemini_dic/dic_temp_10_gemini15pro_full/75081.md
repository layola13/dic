frijol:/friˈxoʊl/ (US), /friˈhɔl/ (UK)| n. |豆子，菜豆|A bean, especially a kidney bean, commonly used in Latin American cuisine.  It refers to the edible seeds of various bean plants.|例句：Frijoles are a staple in Mexican cuisine.  菜豆是墨西哥菜的主食。

例句：She made a delicious pot of frijoles negros. 她做了一锅美味的黑豆。|近义词：bean, kidney bean, pinto bean (depending on the specific type)|反义词: N/A (No direct antonym for a type of food)|词性:noun
