metamictness:/ˌmɛtəˈmɪktnɪs/ | n. | 非晶质；变晶质；蜕晶质 | The state of being metamict, where a crystalline mineral becomes amorphous due to radiation damage. 指晶体矿物由于辐射损伤而变成非晶态的状态。|  The metamictness of the zircon crystals allowed scientists to date the ancient rock formation.  锆石晶体的非晶质状态使科学家能够确定古代岩层的年代。 | synonyms: amorphism, noncrystallinity | antonyms: crystallinity | noun

metamict:/ˈmɛtəmɪkt/ | adj. | 非晶质的；变晶质的；蜕晶质的 |  A crystalline material that has become amorphous due to radiation damage, typically from alpha decay of incorporated radioactive elements. 指由于辐射损伤（通常是由于掺入的放射性元素的α衰变）而变成非晶态的晶体材料。 | The metamict mineral was no longer transparent. 这种非晶质矿物不再透明。| synonyms: amorphous, noncrystalline, glassy | antonyms: crystalline | adjective
