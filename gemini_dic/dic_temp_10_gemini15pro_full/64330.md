eradicant:/ɪˈrædɪkənt/
adjective| 根除的，消除的| That which eradicates; eradicative. | The new drug proved to be an effective eradicant for the disease.  这种新药被证明是治疗这种疾病的有效根除剂。|近义词：destructive, eradicative, exterminative|反义词: constructive,  preservative|词性: adjective

eradicant:/ɪˈrædɪkənt/
noun| 根除剂，消除剂 | Something that eradicates, such as a chemical or medicine. |  Scientists are searching for an eradicant for the invasive species. 科学家们正在寻找一种根除入侵物种的药剂。|近义词：cure, remedy,  antidote|反义词:  stimulant|词性: noun


词源：
From Latin ērādīcāns, present participle of ērādīcāre (“to root out, eradicate”), from ex (“out of, from”) + rādīx (“root”).
