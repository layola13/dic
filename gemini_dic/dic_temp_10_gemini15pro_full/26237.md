buttermilks:/ˈbʌtərˌmɪlks/ | n. |酪乳，白脱牛奶|A fermented dairy drink, traditionally the liquid left behind after churning butter from cream, but now usually made by adding bacteria cultures to milk. | I used buttermilk to make pancakes. 我用酪乳做煎饼。|Synonyms: cultured milk, soured milk|Antonyms: cream, whole milk|Noun
