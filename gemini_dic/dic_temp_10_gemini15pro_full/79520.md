globs:/ɡlɑːbz/| n.|团块，水滴，小球|small, rounded masses of a thick or sticky substance|The batter fell in globs onto the pan. 面糊成团地掉进锅里。|近义词：lumps, blobs, clumps, dollops|反义词:drops, specks|noun

globs:/ɡlɑːbz/| v.(informal) |贪婪地吃,狼吞虎咽|to eat something quickly and greedily|He globbed down his dinner in five minutes. 他五分钟内就把晚饭狼吞虎咽地吃完了。|近义词：gobble, devour, gulp|反义词:nibble, savor|verb
