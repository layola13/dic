evolutionism:/ˌɛvəˈluːʃənɪzəm/ | n. | 进化论，演化论 | The theory that living things develop gradually from simple to more complex forms over time, primarily through natural selection. |  Darwin's work revolutionized biology with its comprehensive theory of evolutionism. 达尔文的研究以其全面的进化论彻底改变了生物学。

Evolutionism is a cornerstone of modern biological understanding. 进化论是现代生物学理解的基石。


| 近义词: Darwinism, transformism | 反义词: creationism | 词性: noun
