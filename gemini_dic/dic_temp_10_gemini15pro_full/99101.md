intercampus:/ˌɪntərˈkæmpəs/| adj.|校际的，校园间的|Existing or occurring between two or more campuses of a university or college.|例句：The intercampus bus service is very convenient. 校际巴士服务非常方便。|例句：There is an intercampus rivalry between the two schools. 这两所学校之间存在着校际竞争。|近义词：intercollegiate|反义词：intracampus|词性：adjective
