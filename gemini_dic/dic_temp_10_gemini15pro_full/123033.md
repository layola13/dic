mondo:/ˈmɒndoʊ/ (US), /ˈmɒndəʊ/ (UK)| adj.|非凡的，极好的，巨大的|Extraordinary; impressive; huge.  Derived from Italian, meaning "world." Originally used in jazz slang, later adopted into general slang.|That concert was absolutely mondo! 那场音乐会真是太棒了！|近义词:huge, enormous, fantastic, amazing, incredible|反义词:small, tiny, insignificant, ordinary|adjective
