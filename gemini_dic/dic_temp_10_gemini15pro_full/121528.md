mismanaged:/ˌmɪsˈmænɪdʒd/
Verb (past participle)|管理不善的，处置失当的|Handled poorly or inefficiently; badly administered.|The project was mismanaged from the start, leading to significant delays and cost overruns.  这个项目从一开始就管理不善，导致严重的延误和成本超支。|例句：The company was mismanaged and eventually went bankrupt.  这家公司管理不善，最终破产了。|近义词：mishandled, bungled, botched|反义词：well-managed, efficient, competent|词性:v.
