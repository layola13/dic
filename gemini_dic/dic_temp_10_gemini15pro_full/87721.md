hexed:/hɛkst/| adj.|被施了魔法的，被诅咒的，倒霉的|Affected by witchcraft or bad luck; jinxed.|例句：He felt hexed after a series of unfortunate events.  他经历了一连串不幸的事件后，感觉自己被诅咒了。

例句：The team seemed hexed, losing every game despite playing well.  这支球队似乎被施了魔法，尽管打得很好，却每场都输。|近义词：cursed, jinxed, bewitched, doomed|反义词：blessed, fortunate, lucky|词性:形容词

词源: From Pennsylvania German *hext, from hexen "to practice witchcraft," from Old High German hagzissa "witch."
