deniable:/dɪˈnaɪəbl/| adj.|可否认的，可抵赖的|Something that is deniable can be denied.  It often implies that something is being denied even though it is likely true.|例句：His involvement in the scandal was deniable. 他对这起丑闻的参与是可以否认的。

例句：The government issued a deniable authorization for the covert operation. 政府对秘密行动发出了可否认的授权。|近义词：refutable, contestable, disputable|反义词：undeniable, irrefutable, indisputable|词性: adjective

词源: From deny (v.) + -able.  Ultimately from Old French denier, from Latin denegare "to deny, refuse."
