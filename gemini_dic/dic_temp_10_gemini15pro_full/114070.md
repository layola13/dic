magneto:/mæɡˈniːtəʊ/ (UK), /mæɡˈniːtoʊ/ (US)| n.|磁电机，磁发电机|A small electrical generator that uses a permanent magnet to produce an alternating current, especially one used in the ignition system of a petrol engine.|The old tractor wouldn't start because the magneto was faulty.  这辆旧拖拉机发动不起来，因为磁电机坏了。|Synonym: alternator, generator|Antonym: N/A|noun

magneto:/mæɡˈniːtəʊ/ (UK), /mæɡˈniːtoʊ/ (US)| adj. (informal)|磁的，与磁有关的|Relating to or using magnetism.|He's working on a magneto engine. 他正在研究一种磁力发动机。|Synonym: magnetic|Antonym: non-magnetic|adjective
