nonimmunities:/ˌnɒnɪˈmjuːnɪtiz/
noun
缺乏免疫力；无豁免权
The state of not possessing immunity or exemption from something, especially from a disease or legal obligation.
The refugees' nonimmunities to local diseases made them vulnerable.  这些难民对当地疾病缺乏免疫力，使他们很脆弱。
Their nonimmunities to prosecution shocked the public. 他们没有豁免被起诉的权利，这震惊了公众。
近义词: vulnerabilities, susceptibilities
反义词: immunities, exemptions
词性: noun (plural)

nonimmunity:/ˌnɒnɪˈmjuːnɪti/
noun

The state of not possessing immunity or exemption from something, especially from a disease or legal obligation.

近义词: vulnerability, susceptibility
反义词: immunity, exemption
词性: noun


词源:
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "immunity" (from Latin "immunitas," meaning "freedom from service, obligation, or burden")
