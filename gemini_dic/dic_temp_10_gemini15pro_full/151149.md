parats:/ˈpæræts/ | n. |伞兵；空降部队 (通常用复数形式) | soldiers trained to be dropped from aircraft by parachute | The parats landed behind enemy lines. 伞兵降落在敌后。|  近义词: paratroopers, airborne troops | 反义词: infantry (步兵 - 非绝对反义词，只是对比) | 词性: noun


词源:  Shortened from "paratroopers."  First recorded in the early 20th century.  From "parachute" + "troops."
