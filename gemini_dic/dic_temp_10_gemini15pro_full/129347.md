noisy:/ˈnɔɪzi/ (US), /ˈnɔɪzi/ (UK)| adj.|吵闹的，嘈杂的|Making or characterized by a lot of noise.|The street was noisy with traffic. 街上车水马龙，十分嘈杂。|The children were being very noisy. 孩子们非常吵闹。|近义词：loud, boisterous, clamorous, raucous|反义词：quiet, silent, peaceful|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *noyous, *noious, from Anglo-Norman nuisous, from Old French noisous (“harmful, troublesome”), from noise (“noise, quarrel”), from Latin nausea (“seasickness”), from Ancient Greek ναυσία (nausía, “seasickness”), from ναῦς (naûs, “ship”).
