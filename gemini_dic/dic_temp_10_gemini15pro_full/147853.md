overrank:/ˌoʊvərˈræŋk/| vt.|超越；居…之上；给予过高的级别|To give someone a higher rank than they deserve or are qualified for.|He was overranked in the tournament and quickly eliminated. 他在比赛中被给予过高的级别，很快就被淘汰了.|近义词：outrank, promote unduly|反义词：demote, downgrade|动词

overrank:/ˌoʊvərˈræŋk/| n.|过高的级别；级别过高的人|The state of having a higher rank than deserved or merited.|His overrank was obvious to everyone on the team.  他在团队中的级别过高，这一点每个人都显而易见。|近义词：excessive rank, undue promotion|反义词：low rank, demotion|名词
