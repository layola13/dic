heathenism: /ˈhiːðənɪzəm/ | n. |异教，异教信仰|The state of being a heathen; barbaric morals or behavior; paganism.  A lack of moral or religious principles.|  Early missionaries worked to convert people from heathenism to Christianity. 早期传教士致力于将人们从异教信仰转变为基督教信仰。|Synonyms: paganism, idolatry, barbarism, irreligion|Antonyms: Christianity, Islam, Judaism, monotheism|Noun
