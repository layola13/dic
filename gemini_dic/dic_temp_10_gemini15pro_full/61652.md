emission:/ɪˈmɪʃən/ | n. | 排放；散发；发出；（纸币的）发行 | The act of sending out gas, heat, light, etc. |  The emission of greenhouse gases is contributing to climate change. 温室气体的排放正在加剧气候变化。| synonyms: discharge, release, ejection | antonyms: absorption, suppression | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin emissionem, accusative of emissio (“a sending forth”), from Latin emittere (“send forth”), from e- (“out of”) + mittere (“to send”).
