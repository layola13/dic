baksheeshing:/bækˈʃiːʃɪŋ/
n.| 给小费，贿赂 |The act of giving a baksheesh (a tip or bribe).|He was baksheeshing the guards to let him through. 他给守卫塞小费让他们放他过去。|近义词:tipping, bribing|反义词:None|词性:名词/动名词

Etymology: From the Persian word *بخشش* (bakhshesh), meaning "gift" or "donation."  Borrowed into English via Hindi and Urdu.
