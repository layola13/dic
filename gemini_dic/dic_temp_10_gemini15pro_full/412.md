abjuration: /ˌæbdʒʊˈreɪʃən/
noun
放弃；发誓断绝；正式放弃
a formal renunciation of a belief or claim.
He signed an abjuration of his former radical views.  他签署了一份声明，放弃了他以前的激进观点。

The defendant made a complete abjuration of his previous testimony. 被告完全放弃了他先前的证词。

renunciation, repudiation, denial, rejection
affirmation, acceptance, embrace
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin abjuratio, from Latin abjurare (“to deny on oath”), from ab- (“away from”) + jurare (“to swear”).
