bums:/bʌmz/
n.|流浪汉，游民；懒汉|People who have no homes and live on the streets are often called bums.  无家可归流落街头的人通常被称为流浪汉。|例句：The bums were sleeping under the bridge. 流浪汉们睡在桥下。|近义词：vagrants, tramps, hobos, loafers|反义词：workers, employed|词性:名词

bums:/bʌmz/
v.|闲逛，虚度光阴；乞讨|To spend time idly or to beg. 闲逛或乞讨。|例句：He bummed around Europe for a year after college. 大学毕业后，他在欧洲闲逛了一年。|近义词：loaf, loiter, scrounge|反义词：work, strive|词性:动词

词源：bum (n.) "loafer, idler," 1860, American English, perhaps a shortened form of bummer "idle, worthless fellow" (1855), itself of uncertain origin.  可能是 bummer "游手好闲的家伙" 的缩写形式。


注意事项：bum 可以被认为是冒犯性的词语，尤其是在指代无家可归的人时。 使用时需谨慎。
