obduce:/əbˈdjuːs/ | vt. | 遮盖，掩盖；引出，推断 | To cover over; to draw over; to lead or draw away, especially by persuasion or moral influence.  |  The heavy curtains obduced the windows, blocking out the sunlight. 厚重的窗帘遮住了窗户，挡住了阳光。 | Synonyms: cover, conceal, obscure, veil | Antonyms: reveal, uncover, expose | verb

obduce:/əbˈdjuːs/ | vt. |  推断，演绎 | To infer or deduce from evidence or reasoning. | From the evidence, the detective obduced that the suspect was lying.  从证据来看，侦探推断嫌疑人在撒谎。 | Synonyms: deduce, infer, conclude, gather | Antonyms:  assume, suppose (without evidence) | verb
