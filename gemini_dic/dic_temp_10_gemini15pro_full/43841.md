crepitus:/ˈkrɛpɪtəs/| n. |噼啪声，爆裂声，捻发音|A grating or crackling sound or sensation produced by the rubbing together of fractured bone or cartilage.|The doctor detected crepitus in the patient's knee. 医生在病人的膝盖处发现了捻发音。|近义词：crackling, grinding, grating|反义词: silence, quiet|词性: noun
Etymology: From Latin *crepitus*, past participle of *crepare* "to rattle, creak, crackle".
