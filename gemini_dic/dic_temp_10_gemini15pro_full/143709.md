outlimb:/ˌaʊtˈlɪm/ | n. | 外延部分，边缘，险境 | An outlying or extended part, especially of something branching out from a central core; a precarious or dangerous position. |  The company expanded to several outlimbs in the market.  这家公司在市场上扩展到几个边缘领域。| synonym: extremity, fringe, periphery, edge | antonym: center, core, heart | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + limb.  "Out-" signifies outward or beyond, and "limb" refers to a projecting part, like a branch of a tree.  Thus, "outlimb" refers to something that extends outwards.
