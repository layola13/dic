fountains:/ˈfaʊntənz/
noun
喷泉
a structure that sends water up into the air as a decoration, or a natural spring of water
The Trevi Fountain in Rome is a popular tourist attraction. 罗马特莱维喷泉是一个热门的旅游景点。
springs, jets, cascades
taps, faucets
