glitziness:/ˈɡlɪtsɪnəs/
noun
华丽；浮华；炫目
The quality of being glitzy; ostentatious glamour and showiness.
The glitziness of Hollywood attracts many young people. 好莱坞的浮华吸引了很多年轻人。
glitz, glamour, flashiness, showiness
simplicity, modesty, understatement
