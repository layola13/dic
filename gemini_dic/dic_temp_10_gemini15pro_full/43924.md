crewed:/kruːd/
| adj.|载人的，配备人员的|Having a crew; operated by a crew.  A crewed spacecraft, unlike a robotic probe, has people on board to operate it.|例句：The mission requires a crewed spacecraft.  这次任务需要一艘载人飞船。|例句：They launched a crewed mission to Mars. 他们发射了一个载人火星任务。|近义词：manned, staffed|反义词：uncrewed, unmanned, robotic|词性:形容词
词源:From crew + -ed.  The word "crew" ultimately comes from Old French "creue," meaning "growth" or "increase," later referring to a group of soldiers or sailors.
