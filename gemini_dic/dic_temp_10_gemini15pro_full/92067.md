hyperprophetic:/ˌhaɪpərprəˈfɛtɪk/| adj.|过度预言的，极端预言的|Excessively or extremely prophetic; characterized by excessive or extreme prophecy.  It combines "hyper-" (meaning "over, excessive") with "prophetic" (relating to prophecy).|The preacher's hyperprophetic pronouncements about the end of the world caused widespread panic.|近义词：apocalyptic, millenarian, doomsday|反义词：realistic, pragmatic|词性:adjective
