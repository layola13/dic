dogfights:/ˈdɒɡfaɪts/ (UK) /ˈdɔːɡfaɪts/ (US)| n.|空战，混战；（尤指政治上的）激烈争斗|A dogfight is a close-range aerial combat between fighter aircraft.|The two planes engaged in a fierce dogfight high above the clouds. 两架飞机在云层高空进行了一场激烈的空战。|synonyms: aerial combat, air battle, close-range fight, skirmish, battle, clash, struggle, tussle|antonyms: truce, peace, ceasefire|noun

dogfight:/ˈdɒɡfaɪt/ (UK) /ˈdɔːɡfaɪt/ (US)| v.|进行空战；（尤指政治上）激烈争斗|To dogfight is to engage in a close-range aerial combat.|The pilots dogfought fiercely for control of the skies. 飞行员们为了控制天空而激烈地空战。|synonyms: battle, clash, struggle, contend, fight, combat|antonyms: yield, surrender, concede|verb
