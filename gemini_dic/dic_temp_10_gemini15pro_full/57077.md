doucine:/ˈduːsiːn/| n.| 反曲线; 凹圆线; 勺形线; |A concave moulding, the opposite of an ovolo.  A molding with an S-shaped profile, concave at the top and convex at the bottom.|The architect incorporated a doucine molding into the cornice.|近义词: cyma reversa, talon|反义词: ovolo, echinus|词性:noun

Etymology:
From French *doucine*, from Italian *doccino*, diminutive of *dosso* "back," from Latin *dorsum*.
