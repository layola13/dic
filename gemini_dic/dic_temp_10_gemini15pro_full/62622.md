enforceability:/ɪnˌfɔːrsəˈbɪləti/ |n.|可执行性；可强制性|The quality of being enforceable, especially of a law, rule, or contract, meaning it can be legally enforced.|The enforceability of the contract is in question due to a loophole.  由于存在漏洞，合同的可执行性受到质疑。| enforceability of a law: 法律的可执行性|synonym: validity, legality, binding power|antonym: invalidity, illegality, unenforceability|noun
