lobelike:/ˈloʊblaɪk/| adj.|叶状的，像耳垂的|Resembling a lobe, typically in shape or form.  A lobe is a rounded projection or division, often found in organs like the brain, lungs, or liver.  Lobelike can describe anything that shares this rounded, projecting form.|The plant had lobelike leaves.  这种植物有叶状的叶子。|The cloud formation was lobelike, extending outwards from the main mass. 云的形状像耳垂，从主云团向外延伸。|similar: lobed, rounded, projecting|opposite: linear, pointed|adjective
