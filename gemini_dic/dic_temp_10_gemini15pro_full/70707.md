financially:/faɪˈnænʃəli/ /fɪˈnænʃəli/
Adverb
财政上地；经济上地
In a way that relates to money or how money is managed.
例句：The project is not financially viable.  这个项目在财务上不可行。

例句：He's financially independent. 他经济上独立。

近义词：economically, monetarily
反义词：N/A
词性：adverb
