entrepreneurialisms:/ˌɑːntrəprəˈnɜːriəˌlɪzəmz/
n.
创业精神（复数）
The processes, practices, and mindsets associated with being entrepreneurial, often used in the plural to refer to multiple instances or types of entrepreneurial activity.
The city fostered a culture of entrepreneurialisms, leading to the creation of many new businesses.  这座城市培养了一种创业文化，促成了许多新企业的创建。
Many different entrepreneurialisms have emerged in the tech industry.  科技行业出现了许多不同的创业模式。
近义词：entrepreneurship, enterprise, initiative
反义词：inaction, passivity
词性: noun

词源: From entrepreneurial + -ism.  "Entrepreneurial" comes from French "entrepreneur," meaning "one who undertakes."  "-ism" is a suffix forming nouns of action, practice, principle, etc.
