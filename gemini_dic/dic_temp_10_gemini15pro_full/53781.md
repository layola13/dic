discommunity:/ˌdɪskəˈmjuːnɪti/
n.
缺乏共同体; 分裂;  不和谐
A lack of community or a state of disunity and disharmony.
The increasing polarization of politics has led to a sense of discommunity in the nation.  日益两极分化的政治导致了国家分裂感。
Social media can sometimes foster discommunity rather than connection. 社交媒体有时会助长分裂而不是联系。
近义词: disunity, discord, division, fragmentation
反义词: community, unity, harmony, cohesion
词性: 名词
