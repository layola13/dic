humilities:/hjuːˈmɪlətiz/
n.
谦逊，谦卑；谦虚的行为
the quality or state of not thinking you are better than other people; humbleness.  A display of humility.

He spoke with great humility about his role in the project.
他非常谦虚地谈论了他在项目中的作用。

She accepted the award with humility and grace.
她谦逊而优雅地接受了奖项。

Near Synonyms: humbleness, modesty, meekness, lowliness
Antonyms: arrogance, pride, haughtiness, conceit
词性: 名词 (plural noun)
