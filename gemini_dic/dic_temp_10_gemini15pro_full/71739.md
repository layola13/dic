flaxes:/ˈflæksɪz/
n.
亚麻（植物）；亚麻纤维；亚麻制品
Flax is a plant that is grown for its seeds and its fibers, which are used to make linen cloth.
The farmer grows flax for the production of linseed oil.  农民种植亚麻以生产亚麻籽油。

Flax is used to make linen. 亚麻用来制作亚麻布。


近义词:linen, linseed
反义词: (No direct antonym for a plant/material)
词性:noun (plural)
