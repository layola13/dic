aidful:/ˈeɪdfəl/| adj.|有帮助的；有效的|Providing or able to provide help or assistance; effective.|例句：The teacher's aidful comments helped me improve my essay. 老师有帮助的评论帮助我改进了我的文章。|近义词：helpful, assisting, supportive, useful|反义词：unhelpful, useless, hindering|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From aid + -ful.  "Aid" comes from Old French "aide" (help, assistance), ultimately from Latin "adjutare" (to help). "-ful" is a suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by."
