electives:/ɪˈlɛktɪvz/| n.|选修课|A course that a student can choose to take, but is not required to graduate.|Students have a wide range of electives to choose from, including photography and creative writing. 学生们有广泛的选修课可供选择，包括摄影和创意写作。|Synonyms: optional courses, non-required courses|Antonyms: required courses, compulsory courses|noun

elective:/ɪˈlɛktɪv/| adj.|选修的|Allowing a choice; optional.|She decided to take an elective course in history. 她决定选修一门历史课。|Synonyms: optional, voluntary|Antonyms: required, compulsory, mandatory|adjective
