counterslope:/ˈkaʊntərˌsləʊp/ (UK) /ˈkaʊntərˌsloʊp/ (US)| n.|反斜坡，逆坡|A slope descending away from a fortified position, designed to deflect projectiles or minimize their effect; a reverse slope.|The counterslope offered some protection from enemy fire. 反斜坡提供了一些躲避敌人炮火的保护。|The engineers built a counterslope to reduce the impact of the explosion. 工程师们建造了一个反斜坡来减少爆炸的冲击。|近义词: reverse slope, back slope|反义词: forward slope, glacis|词性:noun
