gulag:/ˈɡuːlɑːɡ/
noun
古拉格，苏联劳改营
A system of forced labor camps in the Soviet Union.
His grandfather perished in the gulag. 他的祖父死于古拉格。
Synonyms: labor camp, concentration camp, prison camp
Antonyms: freedom, liberty
