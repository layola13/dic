allenarly:/əˈlɛnərli/ | adv. | only, solely, exclusively | solely; exclusively; only.  Derived from the Scots word "al(l)anerlie" meaning "only," ultimately from Old English "all" + "anerly," from "an" (one) + "-lic" (like) + "-e" (adverbial suffix). | He allenarly focused on finishing the project. 他只专注于完成这个项目。|  She was allenarly responsible for the error. 她独自对这个错误负责。 | 近义词: only, solely, exclusively | 反义词: wholly, completely, entirely | 词性: 副词
