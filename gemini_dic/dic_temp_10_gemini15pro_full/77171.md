gasohol: /ˈɡæsəhɔːl/ (US) /ˈɡæsəhɒl/ (UK)| n. |汽油醇|A mixture of gasoline and alcohol (usually ethanol), used as an alternative fuel.|Brazil is a major producer and consumer of gasohol. 巴西是汽油醇的主要生产国和消费国。|Gasohol can be used in most gasoline-powered vehicles. 汽油醇可以在大多数汽油动力车辆中使用。|近义词：biofuel, alternative fuel|反义词: gasoline, petrol|词性: noun

Etymology:
gasohol = gas(oline) + (alc)ohol
