fraternal:/frəˈtɜːrnl/
adj.
兄弟般的；友好的
Of or like a brother or brothers; brotherly.
例句：He felt a fraternal bond with his teammates. 他感觉与队友之间有兄弟情谊。

例句：The fraternal organizations raised money for charity.  兄弟会组织为慈善事业筹款。

近义词：brotherly, friendly
反义词：unfriendly, hostile
词性：形容词


fraternity:/frəˈtɜːr.nə.ti/
n.
兄弟会；友爱
A group of people, especially men, sharing a common interest.
例句：He joined a fraternity in college. 他在大学时加入了一个兄弟会。

例句：There's a strong sense of fraternity among the firefighters. 消防队员之间有很强的兄弟情谊。

近义词：brotherhood, fellowship
反义词：sorority
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *fraternālis, from Latin frāter (“brother”).
