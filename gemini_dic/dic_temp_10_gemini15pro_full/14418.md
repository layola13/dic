autotypography:/ˌɔːtəʊtaɪˈpɒɡrəfi/ | n. |自动排版，自动印刷|The process or result of text being automatically formatted or typeset, often by a computer program.  This includes features like automatic hyphenation, justification, and font selection. |Modern desktop publishing software relies heavily on autotypography. 现代桌面出版软件很大程度上依赖于自动排版。|Autotypography saved the publisher a lot of time and effort. 自动排版为出版商节省了大量时间和精力。| 近义词: automated typesetting, computer-aided typesetting | 反义词: manual typesetting | 词性: noun

Etymology:  "auto-" (self) + "typography" (the art or process of printing with type)
