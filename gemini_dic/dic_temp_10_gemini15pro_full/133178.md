nonevangelically:/ˌnɒnɪˌvænˈdʒɛlɪkəli/
adv.
非福音派地，不以福音派的方式
in a way that is not characteristic of or associated with evangelical Christianity.
The book presented a nonevangelically Christian perspective. 这本书提出了一个非福音派基督教的观点。
Near Antonyms: evangelically
