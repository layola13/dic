benefitted:/ˈbɛnɪfɪtɪd/ | v. | 受益，得益 |past tense and past participle of benefit.  Means to receive an advantage; profit; gain. | The company benefitted from the new tax laws.  这家公司从新的税法中受益。| Synonyms: profited, gained, improved, advanced | Antonyms: suffered, lost, declined | verb

benefitted:/ˈbɛnɪfɪtɪd/ | v. | 受益，得益 |She benefitted greatly from her time abroad. 她从在国外的时间里获益匪浅。| Synonyms: profited, gained, improved, advanced | Antonyms: suffered, lost, declined | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French bienfet (modern French bienfait), from bien ("well") + fait ("done, deed").  Ultimately from Latin bene factum.
