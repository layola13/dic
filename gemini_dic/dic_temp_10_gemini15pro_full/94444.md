implosion:/ɪmˈploʊʒən/ | n. | 内爆；崩溃；剧减 | A sudden collapse inwards.  The act of imploding; a bursting inward. A sudden decrease or failure. | The demolition experts imploded the old building. 爆破专家将旧大楼内爆拆除。| synonyms: collapse, cave-in, bursting inward | antonyms: explosion, eruption, expansion | noun

implosion:/ɪmˈploʊʒən/ | v. | 内爆；崩溃 | To collapse violently inwards. | The star imploded at the end of its life cycle.  这颗恒星在其生命周期结束时发生了内爆。 | synonyms: collapse, cave-in | antonyms: explode, erupt, expand | verb
