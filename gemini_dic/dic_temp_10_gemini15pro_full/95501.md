inclinational:/ˌɪnklɪˈneɪʃənəl/
adjective
倾向的；倾斜的
Relating to or characterized by inclination; having a tendency or disposition towards something.
The inclinational angle of the ramp was too steep for the wheelchair.  坡道的倾斜角度对轮椅来说太陡了。
Her inclinational preference was for classical music. 她倾向于喜欢古典音乐。
近义词:  inclined, prone, disposed, biased
反义词:  disinclined, averse, opposed
词性:  adjective
