palisaded:/ˌpælɪˈseɪdɪd/ | adj. | 用栅栏围成的，栅栏状的 | Enclosed or fortified with palisades; resembling a palisade. | The garden was palisaded with tall bamboo.  花园用高大的竹子围了起来。 | The palisaded village was well-protected from invaders. 这个用栅栏围起来的村庄受到了很好的保护，免受入侵者的侵扰。 |  近义词: fenced, walled, fortified | 反义词: open, exposed | 词性: adjective

词源: From the noun "palisade," which comes from French "palissade," ultimately from Late Latin "palitiata," from "palus" (stake).
