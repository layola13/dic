monkeys:/ˈmʌŋkiz/ |n.|猴子|Plural form of monkey, referring to more than one monkey.  A primate mammal generally smaller than an ape, typically with a long tail and distinguished by its intelligence and agility.|The monkeys swung through the trees. 猴子在树上荡来荡去。|Near synonyms: primates, simians|Antonyms: None (Animals don't typically have direct antonyms).|Noun
