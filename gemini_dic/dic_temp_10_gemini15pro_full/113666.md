machoism:/ˈmɑːtʃoʊɪzəm/ /ˈmætʃəʊɪzəm/ | n. | 大男子主义；男子气概 |  A strong sense of masculine pride and exaggerated masculinity, often characterized by an aggressive, assertive, and sometimes domineering attitude.  It involves valuing traditionally masculine traits like strength, dominance, and stoicism, often to an excessive degree. | He displayed his machoism by refusing to ask for directions. 他通过拒绝问路来展现他的大男子主义。 |  His machoism prevents him from showing any vulnerability. 他的大男子主义让他无法表现出任何脆弱。 | synonyms: masculinity, bravado, swagger, cockiness | antonyms: femininity, meekness, timidity | noun
