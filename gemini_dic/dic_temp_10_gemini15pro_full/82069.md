grittiness:/ˈɡrɪtɪnɪs/
n.
坚韧不拔，毅力，沙砾感
The quality of being gritty; courage and resolve; strength of character.
The boxer showed incredible grittiness, getting up after every knockdown.  这位拳击手表现出惊人的毅力，每次被击倒后都能站起来。
grit, resilience, toughness, determination, resolve
weakness, fragility, softness, indecision, hesitation
noun

grit:/ɡrɪt/
n.
沙砾，勇气，毅力
Small, loose particles of stone or sand.  Courage and resolve; strength of character.
The road was covered in grit. 这条路被沙砾覆盖。
She showed true grit in the face of adversity.  面对逆境，她展现了真正的勇气。
gravel, sand, courage, determination, resolve
cowardice, fear, timidity, weakness, indecision
noun

gritty:/ˈɡrɪti/
adj.
坚韧不拔的，有沙砾的，粗糙的
Containing or covered with grit; showing courage and resolve.
The photographer is known for his gritty portrayal of urban life. 这位摄影师以其对城市生活的真实写照而闻名。
The beach was gritty and unpleasant to walk on.  沙滩上满是沙砾，走起来很不舒服。
tough, resilient, determined, resolute, courageous, coarse, rough
smooth, refined, delicate, weak, cowardly
adjective
