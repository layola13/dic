anaerobiotic:/ˌænɛrəʊbaɪˈɒtɪk/ |adj.|厌氧的，无氧的|Relating to or requiring an absence of free oxygen.  Characterized by the absence of free oxygen.|例句：Anaerobiotic bacteria thrive in environments without oxygen. 厌氧细菌在无氧环境中 thrive。|例句：Anaerobiotic respiration produces less energy than aerobic respiration. 无氧呼吸产生的能量比有氧呼吸少。|近义词：anoxic, anaerobic|反义词：aerobic, oxygenic|词性:形容词
anaerobiosis:/ˌænɛrəʊbaɪˈəʊsɪs/ |n.|厌氧生活，无氧生活|Life in the absence of oxygen; the process by which certain organisms, such as bacteria and yeast cells, derive energy from nutrients in the absence of oxygen.|例句：Some bacteria can survive through anaerobiosis. 一些细菌可以通过厌氧生活生存。|例句：Yeast undergoes anaerobiosis during fermentation. 酵母在发酵过程中进行厌氧生活。|近义词：anoxia|反义词：aerobiosis|词性:名词
