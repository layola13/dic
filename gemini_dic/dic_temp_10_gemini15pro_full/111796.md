lofty:/ˈlɔːfti/ (US) /ˈlɒfti/ (UK)| adj.|崇高的，高尚的；高傲的；巍峨的，高耸的|of imposing height; of a noble or elevated nature.  Also, haughty and aloof.|The cathedral has lofty ceilings.  这座大教堂有很高的天花板。|Synonyms: high, towering, noble, elevated, exalted, majestic, grand, imposing, arrogant, haughty, disdainful|Antonyms: low, humble, modest, base, lowly|adjective
