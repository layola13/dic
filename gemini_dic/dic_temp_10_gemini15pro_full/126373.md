myrioscope:/ˌmɪriəˈskəʊp/ | n. | 万花筒；千变万化的景象 | An instrument that exhibits an endless variety of beautiful colors and symmetrical forms, often by the reflection of pieces of colored glass in mirrors.  A constantly changing scene or pattern. |  The sunset over the ocean was a true myrioscope of color. 海洋上的日落是一幅名副其实的万花筒般的色彩景象。

The city at night, with its myriad lights, is like a myrioscope.  夜晚的城市，有着无数的灯光，就像一个万花筒。 | 近义词: kaleidoscope | 反义词: monotony | 词性: noun
