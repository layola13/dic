baksheeshed:/bækˈʃiːʃt/
verb
给小费，赏钱
past tense and past participle of baksheesh, meaning "tipped" or "having given a baksheesh."
The porter was handsomely baksheeshed for his help.  搬运工因他的帮助而得到了丰厚的赏钱。
The guide expected to be baksheeshed at the end of the tour. 导游期望在旅行结束时得到小费。
近义词: tipped, rewarded, gratified
反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly) stiffed, cheated
词性: verb
