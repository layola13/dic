fugate:/ˈfjuːɡeɪt/| v.|逃亡；驱逐；|To flee or escape, especially from danger or persecution; to cause to flee or escape.|例句：The family fugated the war-torn country.  这家人逃离了饱受战争蹂躏的国家。|例句：The rebels were fugated from the city. 叛军被驱逐出城。|近义词：flee, escape, exile, banish|反义词:remain, stay|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fugatus, past participle of fugare "to put to flight," from fugere "to flee"
