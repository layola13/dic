delegations:/ˌdelɪˈɡeɪʃənz/ | n. | 代表团，授权，委托 | A group of people who are chosen to represent a country, government, or organization | The UN sent several delegations to the peace talks. 联合国派了几个代表团参加和平谈判。 | synonyms: missions, representatives, committees | antonyms: individuals, single person | noun

delegation:/ˌdelɪˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. | 代表团，授权，委托 | The act of giving someone the authority to do something for you, or a group of people who are chosen to represent a country, government or organization. | The delegation from China arrived yesterday. 中国代表团昨天抵达。 | synonyms: assignment, commission, deputation, missions, representatives, committees | antonyms: retention, revocation, individuals, single person | noun
