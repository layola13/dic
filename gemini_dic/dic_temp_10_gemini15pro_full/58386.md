dub-out:/dʌbˈaʊt/| v. |删除（音轨）; 替换（音轨）; 配音; |To remove or replace a section of an audio or video recording, especially a soundtrack or dialogue.  It can also mean to add a new soundtrack or dialogue in a different language.|They dubbed out the offensive language in the movie. 他们在电影中删除了冒犯性的语言。|synonyms: replace, substitute, redub, revoice|antonyms: retain, keep, preserve|verb

dub-out:/dʌbˈaʊt/| n. |删除（音轨）; 替换（音轨）; 配音; |The act of removing or replacing a section of an audio or video recording.  It can also refer to the new soundtrack or dialogue that is added.|The dub-out of the original soundtrack was poorly done.  原声轨的替换做得不好。|synonyms: replacement, substitution, redubbing, revoicing|antonyms: retention, keeping, preservation|noun
