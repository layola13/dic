chinless:/ˈtʃɪnlɪs/| adj.|没下巴的；懦弱的；缺乏决断力的|Lacking a prominent chin; weak-willed or lacking resolve.  Originally referred to a physical characteristic (lack of a chin), but evolved to describe a perceived lack of strength or character associated with that feature.|He played the part of a chinless aristocrat. 他扮演了一个懦弱的贵族。|近义词：weak, timid, irresolute, spineless|反义词:strong, resolute, determined|词性:形容词
词源:chin + -less (suffix meaning "without")
