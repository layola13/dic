ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mercurie, from Old French mercurie, from Latin Mercurius (the Roman god Mercury, associated with the planet and the element due to his swiftness), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *merkʷ- (“to shimmer; flash”).
