heyday:/ˈheɪdeɪ/ | n. | 全盛时期，鼎盛时期 | The period of greatest prosperity or vigor. |  例句：In its heyday, the company employed thousands of workers.  在其鼎盛时期，这家公司雇佣了数千名工人。

例句：The 1920s were the heyday of jazz.  20世纪20年代是爵士乐的黄金时代。 | 近义词：prime, peak, bloom, golden age | 反义词：decline, downfall, nadir | 词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English *heyday, from hey ("interjection of joy or encouragement") + day.  源自中古英语 *heyday，由 hey（表示喜悦或鼓励的感叹词）+ day 组成。
