dispirit:/dɪˈspɪrɪt/
verb
使沮丧，使气馁
To discourage or depress; to make someone lose enthusiasm or hope.
The team was dispirited by their recent loss.  这支队伍因最近的失利而感到沮丧。
The constant rain dispirited the hikers.  持续的降雨使徒步旅行者感到沮丧。
近义词:deject, demoralize, discourage, dampen
反义词:encourage, hearten, inspire, uplift
词性:v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation) + spirit (meaning "courage, vigor, soul").  源自 dis-（表示否定）+ spirit（意为“勇气、活力、灵魂”）。
