misstart:/ˌmɪsˈstɑːrt/ | n. | 错误的开始，不好的开端 | A bad or unsuccessful beginning. | The team's misstart to the season put them in a difficult position.  该队赛季初的不利开局使他们陷入了困境。 | Example 2:  Her misstart in the race cost her valuable seconds. 她在比赛一开始的失误让她损失了宝贵的几秒钟。 |  近义词：false start, bad start, stumble, blunder | 反义词: good start, strong start | 词性: 名词

misstart:/ˌmɪsˈstɑːrt/ | v. |  开始得不好，错误地开始 | To begin badly or unsuccessfully. |  He misstarted the engine several times before it finally caught. 他几次启动引擎都失败了，最后才打着火。 | Example 2: The project misstarted due to a lack of planning.  由于缺乏规划，该项目一开始就出了问题。 | 近义词：flounder, stumble, falter | 反义词: succeed, begin well | 词性: 动词
