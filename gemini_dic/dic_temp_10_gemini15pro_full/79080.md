glabrous:/ˈɡleɪbrəs/| adj.|光滑的，无毛的|Smooth, having no hairs or projections.|The cactus had a glabrous surface, unlike other prickly varieties.  这种仙人掌表面光滑，不像其他品种那样多刺。|例句：The leaves of the plant are glabrous and shiny. 这种植物的叶子光滑且有光泽。|近义词：smooth, hairless, bald|反义词:hairy, pubescent, downy|词性:形容词
glabrous:/ˈɡleɪbrəs/| adj.|光滑的，无毛的|Free from projections or unevenness; smooth.|The dolphin's skin felt glabrous and cool to the touch. 海豚的皮肤摸起来光滑而凉爽。|近义词：sleek, even|反义词:rough, uneven|词性:形容词
