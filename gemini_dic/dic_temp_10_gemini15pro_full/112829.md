luciferously:/luːˈsɪfərəsli/ | adv. | 恶魔般地；邪恶地；充满恶意地 |  In a manner reminiscent of Lucifer, often suggesting a wicked, mischievous, or brilliant but malevolent quality.  Characterized by a combination of brilliance, pride, and rebelliousness. | He smiled luciferously, revealing a hint of cruelty in his eyes.  他恶魔般地笑着，眼中流露出一丝残忍。| Near synonyms: diabolically, fiendishly, malevolently, wickedly, satanically | Antonyms: angelically, divinely, virtuously | 词性: 副词
