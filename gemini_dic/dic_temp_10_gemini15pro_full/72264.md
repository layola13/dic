flosh:/flɒʃ/ | n.| 涌流，奔流；一阵感情的迸发；脸红；(尤指因兴奋、羞愧等)突然涌上心头的感觉| A sudden surge or rush, especially of emotion; a blush; a sudden feeling, especially of excitement or shame. |  She felt a flosh of anger when she heard the news. 当她听到这个消息时，她感到一阵怒火涌上心头。| synonyms: surge, rush, flush, blush | antonyms: calmness, tranquility | noun

flosh:/flɒʃ/ | v. | (使)(液体)涌出，涌流；(脸)发红，(因羞愧、激动等)脸发热| (Of liquid) to surge or gush; (of a person's face) to redden or become hot (with shame, excitement, etc.) | The blood floshed to her cheeks when he complimented her. 当他赞美她时，她的脸颊涨得通红。| synonyms: gush, surge, flush, blush | antonyms: drain, pale | verb
