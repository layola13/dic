drowningly:/ˈdraʊnɪŋli/
adv.
溺水般地，快要窒息地
in a manner resembling drowning; overwhelmingly
She stared drowningly into his eyes. 她眼神迷离地注视着他的眼睛。

近义词: overwhelmingly, suffocatingly, intensely
反义词: calmly, serenely

词性: adverb
词源: From drown + -ing + -ly.  "Drown" comes from Old English *druncnian, related to Old Frisian drunkna, Old Saxon drunknon, Middle Dutch dronkenen, Old High German trunkanēn "to drown," all from a Germanic base *drunk- "to drink." The original sense was likely "to be so drunk as to drown."
