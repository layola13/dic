estray:/ɪˈstreɪ/
n.|走失的动物（尤指家畜）; 无主财产|A domestic animal found wandering and whose owner is unknown.|The farmer impounded the estray sheep he found in his field.|近义词:stray, lost animal|反义词:owned animal|词性:名词
estray:/ɪˈstreɪ/
adj.|走失的|Straying or having strayed, especially of domestic animals.|Estray dogs are a common problem in the city.|近义词:straying, wandering, lost|反义词:found, owned|词性:形容词
estray:/ɪˈstreɪ/
v.|走失; 迷路|To stray or wander, especially of domestic animals.|The calf estrayed from the herd.|近义词:stray, wander, roam|反义词:return, stay|词性:动词
