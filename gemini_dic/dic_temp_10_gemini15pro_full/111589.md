lobotomy:/ləˈbɒtəmi/ (US) /ləʊˈbɒtəmi/ (UK)| n.|脑叶切除术|A surgical operation involving incision into the prefrontal lobe of the brain, formerly used to treat mental illness.|Example 1: The patient underwent a lobotomy in an attempt to alleviate his severe depression.  病人接受了脑叶切除术，试图减轻他严重的抑郁症。

Example 2: Lobotomy was a controversial procedure with often devastating side effects.  脑叶切除术是一个备受争议的程序，经常会产生破坏性的副作用。|近义词：leucotomy, psychosurgery|反义词: None (no direct opposite for a surgical procedure)|词性:noun
