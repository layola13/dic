distributees:/ˌdɪstrɪˈbjuːtiːz/| n. |受益人，分配对象|Persons who receive something that is distributed.  Those to whom something is allocated, especially property or money from an estate or trust.|The distributees of the estate will receive their inheritance next month.  遗产的受益人下个月将收到他们的遗产。|synonyms: beneficiaries, recipients, heirs|antonyms: distributors, grantors|noun
