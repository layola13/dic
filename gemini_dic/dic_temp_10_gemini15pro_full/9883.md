apartments:/əˈpɑːrtmənts/| n.|公寓，住宅|A set of rooms for living in, especially on one floor of a building.|例句：I'm looking for apartments for rent in the city center. 我正在市中心寻找出租公寓。

例句：The apartments in this building have stunning views of the park. 这栋楼里的公寓可以欣赏到公园的壮丽景色。

近义词：flats, residences, accommodations, lodgings|反义词:house, villa|词性:noun
