myriological:/ˌmɪriəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ |adj.| 万物的，包罗万象的；无数的，数不清的| Relating to myriology, the study of a vast number of things, or innumerable; countless. |The library contained a myriological collection of books, encompassing every imaginable subject.  图书馆藏书浩如烟海，涵盖所有你能想到的主题。| The universe presents a myriological array of stars and galaxies. 宇宙呈现出无数的恒星和星系。|近义词: innumerable, countless, myriad, vast, extensive|反义词: limited, few, scarce|词性: adjective

词源:  From Greek *myrios* (μυρίος) "ten thousand, countless" + *-logos* (-λογος) "one who speaks (in a certain way), one who deals (with a certain subject)"
