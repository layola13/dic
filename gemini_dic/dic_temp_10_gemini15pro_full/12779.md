assimilate: /əˈsɪməleɪt/ (US), /əˈsɪmɪleɪt/ (UK)
|v. |吸收; 同化; 消化;  理解,掌握|To absorb and integrate (people, ideas, or culture) into a wider society or culture. To fully understand something.|例句：The immigrants quickly assimilated into their new community.  移民们很快就融入了他们的新社区。

| | |例句：It took me a while to assimilate all the new information.  我花了一段时间才理解所有新信息。
|近义词：absorb, integrate, incorporate, digest, grasp, comprehend|反义词: reject, exclude, segregate|词性: verb
