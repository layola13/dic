even-out:/ˈiːvən aʊt/
verb
变得平坦，变得均匀；使平坦，使均匀
to become level or regular; to make something level or regular
The ground evens out further up the hill.  山坡再往上走地势就平坦了。
He evened out the mixture with a spatula. 他用铲子把混合物弄平。
近义词: level, smooth, balance
反义词: roughen, uneven
词性: verb
