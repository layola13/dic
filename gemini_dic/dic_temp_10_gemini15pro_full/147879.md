overreactions:/ˌoʊvərriˈækʃənz/
noun
过度反应
excessive reactions to something, often disproportionate to the actual situation
例句：The market's overreactions to the news caused a sudden drop in stock prices.  市场对新闻的过度反应导致股价暴跌。

例句：His overreactions to minor criticisms made it difficult to work with him. 他对一些小批评的过度反应让人很难和他共事。

近义词：overresponses, exaggerations
反义词：underreactions, measured responses
词性：noun (plural)
