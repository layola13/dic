noninstitutions:/ˌnɒnɪnstɪˈtjuːʃənz/
|n.|非机构；非公共机构| Entities that are not established, formal organizations like governments, schools, or hospitals.  Refers to settings or groups outside of traditional institutional frameworks.|
The survey collected data from both institutions and noninstitutions.  这项调查收集了来自机构和非机构的数据。

Noninstitutions play a vital role in community development. 非机构在社区发展中起着至关重要的作用。

|近义词：non-institutional, independent entities, informal groups|
|反义词：institutions, organizations|
|词性：noun|
