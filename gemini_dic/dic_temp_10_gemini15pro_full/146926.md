overintense:/ˌōvərɪnˈtens/| adj.| 过于强烈的，过度的 | Excessively intense; too strong or extreme. | Her overintense focus on the details made her miss the bigger picture. 她对细节过度关注，导致她忽略了全局。 | The overintense light burned my eyes. 过于强烈的光线刺痛了我的眼睛。 | 近义词：excessive, extreme, overpowering, overwhelming | 反义词：mild, moderate, gentle, subtle | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: "over-" (Old English *ofer-, from Proto-Germanic *ubar "over, above") + "intense" (from Late Latin intensus, past participle of intendere "to stretch out, strain," from in- "in" + tendere "to stretch").  Thus, "stretched too far" or "strained excessively."
