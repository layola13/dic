ministries:/ˈmɪnɪstriz/
n.
(政府的)部门，部; 牧师的职责; 牧师的职务
a government department that is responsible for a particular area of activity  (or) the work or office of a minister of religion.
The Ministry of Defence has announced cuts in spending. 国防部宣布削减开支。
He felt a call to the ministry. 他感到一种宗教使命的召唤。
synonyms: departments, agencies, bureaus, offices, services (for government ministries); priesthood, clergy, pastorate (for religious ministry)
antonyms: (For government ministries) None direct antonyms; context-dependent opposites like private sector, corporations, etc. could be used.
