butcherers:/ˈbʊtʃərərz/
n.
屠夫们；刽子手们
People who slaughter animals for meat, or, figuratively, those who kill cruelly and indiscriminately.
The butcherers prepared the meat for the market. 屠夫们为市场准备肉。
The dictator's soldiers were nothing more than butcherers. 独裁者的士兵只不过是刽子手。
synonyms: slaughterers, killers, murderers
antonyms: protectors, savers, rescuers
noun
