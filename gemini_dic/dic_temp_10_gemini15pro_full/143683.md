outlawries:/ˈaʊtlɔːriz/
noun
非法行为；放逐；剥夺法律保护
The act of outlawing someone or something; the state of being outlawed.
The outlawries imposed on the rebels meant they could not own property or access legal protection.  对叛乱分子施加的非法行为意味着他们不能拥有财产或获得法律保护。
synonyms:  banishments, proscriptions, exclusions
antonyms: pardons, amnesties
