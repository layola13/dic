ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *hieroglyphicus, from Ancient Greek ἱερογλυφικός (hieroglyphikós, “hieroglyphic, priestly, sacred”), from ἱερός (hierós, “sacred”) + γλύφειν (glýphein, “to carve”).
