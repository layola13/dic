baste:/beɪst/| vt. |涂油；浇卤汁；公开痛斥|To moisten (meat) with pan drippings, butter, or other fat during cooking to prevent drying and add flavor; to criticize harshly. |例句：She basted the turkey with butter every twenty minutes. 她每二十分钟就用黄油涂抹一次火鸡。

例句：The critics basted the new play. 评论家们严厉批评了这部新剧。|近义词：drizzle, brush, lambaste, castigate|反义词:dry, praise|verb

baste:/beɪst/| n. |粗线；粗缝；痛斥|A coarse thread or stitch used for temporary tacking or sewing; a harsh criticism.|例句：She used a baste to hold the seams together before machine stitching. 她用粗线在缝纫机缝合之前把接缝固定在一起。

例句：He received a thorough baste from his boss for his careless work. 他因粗心大意受到了老板的严厉批评。|近义词：tacking, scolding|反义词: N/A|noun
