magazinist:/ˌmæɡəˈziːnɪst/ | n. |杂志编辑；杂志撰稿人；军械士|A person who writes for or edits magazines, or a soldier responsible for storing and distributing ammunition and weapons.| He's a well-known magazinist for several popular science publications. 他是一位为几家流行科学刊物撰稿的著名杂志撰稿人。

The magazinist carefully reviewed the submitted articles. 这位杂志编辑仔细审查了提交的文章。

The army magazinist ensured all weapons were properly stored. 这位军械士确保所有武器都妥善存放。|Synonym: journalist, writer, editor, armourer (military context) |Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps reader, non-writer) | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *magazinist*, from *magazine* + *-ist*. Ultimately from Arabic *makhāzin* (مَخَازِن), plural of *makhzan* (مَخْزَن) "storehouse".
