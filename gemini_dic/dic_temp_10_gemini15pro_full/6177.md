amiss:/əˈmɪs/| adj. adv.|错误的，不正确的；不妥当的；出差错地|Something is wrong, inappropriate, or out of place.|例句：Something was clearly amiss. 显然出了什么问题。|例句：He sensed something was amiss and went to investigate. 他感觉到有些不对劲，就去调查了。|近义词：wrong, faulty, awry, improper|反义词：right, correct, proper|词性:形容词/副词
Etymology: From Old Norse *á mis "astray," from á "on" + mis "wrongly."
