harmonising:/ˈhɑːmənaɪzɪŋ/ /ˈhɑːrmənaɪzɪŋ/
verb
协调，使和谐，使一致
Bringing different elements into agreement or making them compatible.
The government is trying to harmonise tax laws across the EU. 政府正试图协调整个欧盟的税法。
harmonizing (美式拼写)
reconciling, unifying, coordinating
conflicting, clashing
v.
