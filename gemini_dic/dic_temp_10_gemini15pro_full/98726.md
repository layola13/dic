insurgent:/ɪnˈsɜːrdʒənt/ | n. | 叛乱者，起义者 | A person who rises in opposition to established authority, especially a person who takes part in an armed rebellion against the constituted government. | The insurgents attacked the government building. 叛乱分子袭击了政府大楼。 | synonyms: rebel, revolutionary, mutineer, insurrectionist | antonyms: loyalist, conformist | noun

insurgent:/ɪnˈsɜːrdʒənt/ | adj. | 叛乱的，起义的 | Rising in active revolt; characteristic of an insurgent. |  The insurgent group threatened to overthrow the government.  叛乱组织威胁要推翻政府。| synonyms: rebellious, revolutionary, mutinous, insurrectionary | antonyms: loyal, obedient | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English (as an adjective): from Latin insurgent- ‘rising up’, from the verb insurgere, from in- ‘against, upon’ + surgere ‘to rise’.
