frolicly:/ˈfrɒlɪkli/ (US) /ˈfrɒlɪkli/ (UK)| adv.|嬉戏地，欢乐地|In a playful and lively manner; merrily.|The lambs frolicked frolicly in the meadow. 羊羔在草地上嬉戏玩耍。|Near Synonyms: playfully, merrily, joyfully, sportively, blithely|Near Antonyms: seriously, solemnly, gloomily|Part of Speech: Adverb

frolic:/ˈfrɒlɪk/ (US) /ˈfrɒlɪk/ (UK)| n. vi. |嬉戏，玩耍|A playful action or movement; to play and move about cheerfully; to have fun.|The children's frolic in the park was a joy to watch. 孩子们在公园里嬉戏玩耍，真是令人高兴。|Near Synonyms: play, romp, gambol, caper, frisk|Near Antonyms: work, toil, labor|Part of Speech: Noun, Verb (Intransitive)
