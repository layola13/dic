hypothesis:/haɪˈpɒθəsɪs/ (US) /haɪˈpɒθɪsɪs/ (UK)| n. |假设，假说|A proposed explanation made on the basis of limited evidence as a starting point for further investigation.| Scientists are testing the hypothesis that the universe is expanding. 科学家们正在检验宇宙正在膨胀的假说。|近义词：theory, assumption, supposition, conjecture|反义词：fact, proof, reality|词性:noun

hypothesis:/haɪˈpɒθəsɪs/ (US) /haɪˈpɒθɪsɪs/ (UK)| n. |前提|A proposition, or set of propositions, set forth as an explanation for the occurrence of some specified group of phenomena, either asserted merely as a provisional conjecture to guide investigation (working hypothesis) or accepted as highly probable in the light of established facts.|The hypothesis of the Big Bang is widely accepted. 大爆炸假说是被广泛接受的。|近义词：premise, postulate|反义词：conclusion, result|词性:noun
