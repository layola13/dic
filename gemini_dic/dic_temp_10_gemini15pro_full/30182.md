celesta:/səˈlɛstə/ /sɪˈlɛstə/ | n. |钢片琴|A keyboard instrument similar to a small upright piano, but with hammers that strike metal plates instead of strings, producing a bell-like sound.|The celesta adds a magical shimmer to the orchestra.|Synonyms: glockenspiel, bell-lyra |Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific instrument) | noun
