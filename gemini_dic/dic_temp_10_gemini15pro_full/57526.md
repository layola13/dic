dragoons:/drəˈɡuːnz/| n.|龙骑兵；|A type of cavalry soldier, originally one who fought on horseback but also served on foot.|The dragoons charged the enemy line. 龙骑兵向敌人的防线冲锋。|近义词：cavalry, troopers, mounted soldiers|反义词: infantry|noun


dragoon:/drəˈɡuːn/| vt.|强迫；迫使；|To force someone to do something they do not want to do.|He was dragooned into helping with the move. 他被迫帮忙搬家。|近义词：coerce, compel, force, press, oblige|反义词: persuade|verb
