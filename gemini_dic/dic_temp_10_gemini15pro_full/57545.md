draintile:/ˈdreɪnˌtaɪl/
noun
排水瓦，排水管
A pipe, usually made of clay or plastic, used to drain water from the soil.
The farmer installed draintiles in his fields to prevent waterlogging.  农民在他的田里安装了排水瓦以防止涝灾。

The draintiles helped to improve the soil drainage. 排水瓦有助于改善土壤排水。

近义词:drain pipe, drainage tile, agricultural tile
反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps) irrigation pipe
词性: noun
