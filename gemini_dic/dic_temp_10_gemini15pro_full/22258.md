bone-up:/bəʊn ˈʌp/ | phrasal verb |  临时抱佛脚，突击学习 | To study hard, usually before a test or exam, in order to quickly learn a lot of information. | I need to bone up on my Spanish before my trip to Madrid. 我需要在去马德里之前突击学习一下西班牙语。

bone-up:/bəʊn ˈʌp/ | phrasal verb |  做好准备 | To prepare oneself thoroughly for something. |  She boned up on the company's history before the job interview. 她在面试前认真准备了公司的历史。


bone-up:/bəʊn ˈʌp/ | phrasal verb | 恶补 | To quickly acquire knowledge or skill in a particular area. | He's been boning up on his computer skills lately. 他最近一直在恶补他的计算机技能。

bone-up:/bəʊn ˈʌp/ | phrasal verb | 专攻 | To focus one's studies or efforts on a particular subject or skill. |  I'm boning up on my presentation skills for the conference. 我正在为会议专攻我的演讲技巧。


bone-up:/bəʊn ˈʌp/ | phrasal verb |  复习 | To review or refresh one's knowledge of something. | I need to bone up on my algebra before the final exam. 我需要在期末考试前复习一下我的代数。


近义词: cram, revise, review, study, swot (British), brush up
反义词: neglect, ignore
