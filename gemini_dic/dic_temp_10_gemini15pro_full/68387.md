fallower: /ˈfɒləʊər/ (UK) /ˈfɑːloʊər/ (US) | n. | 追随者，信徒，拥护者 | Someone who follows a particular person, belief, or interest. |  例句：The politician has a large following of dedicated followers.  这位政治家有一大批忠实的追随者。

例句：She is a keen follower of fashion. 她是一位热衷于时尚的追随者。

近义词：supporter, adherent, devotee, fan, admirer | 反义词：opponent, critic, detractor | 词性：名词
