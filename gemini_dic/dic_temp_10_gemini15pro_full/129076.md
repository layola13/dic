nitroleum:/naɪˈtroʊliəm/ | n. |硝化甘油, 炸药|A highly explosive liquid, also known as nitroglycerin. It is used in dynamite and some other explosives. |  He handled the nitroleum with extreme caution. 他小心翼翼地处理硝化甘油。| The nitroleum blast shattered the rock face. 硝化甘油爆炸震碎了岩壁。 |Synonyms: nitroglycerin, glyceryl trinitrate | Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for an explosive substance, but one could consider "inert material") | Noun
