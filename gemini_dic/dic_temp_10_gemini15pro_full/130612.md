nonadhesive:/ˌnɒnədˈhiːsɪv/ /ˌnɑːnədˈhiːsɪv/
adjective
不粘的；非粘合性的
Not sticky; not adhering.
The bandage is nonadhesive, so it won't stick to the wound.  这种绷带是不粘的，所以不会粘在伤口上。
The nonadhesive backing made it easy to remove the label.  不粘的背衬使得标签很容易撕下来。
近义词:nonsticky, non-sticking
反义词:adhesive, sticky
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From non- + adhesive.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "adhesive" comes from the Latin "adhaerere" meaning "to stick to."
