coadjustment:/ˌkoʊəˈdʒʌstmənt/ |n.| 共同调整，相互调节 |The coadjustment of economic policies between countries is crucial for global stability. 各国经济政策的相互协调对全球稳定至关重要。|Coadjustment requires a willingness to compromise.  相互调整需要妥协的意愿。|近义词: adaptation, adjustment, accommodation, harmonization|反义词: discord, conflict, imbalance|词性: 名词

词源: co- (together) + adjustment (the process of adapting or being adapted)
