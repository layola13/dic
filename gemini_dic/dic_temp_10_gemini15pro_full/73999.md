fortunately:/ˈfɔːrtʃənətli/ (US) /ˈfɔːtʃənətli/ (UK)| adv.|幸运地，幸亏|Happily; luckily; by good fortune.  It implies a positive outcome or avoidance of a negative one.  It modifies the whole sentence rather than a specific word.|例句：Fortunately, the rain stopped before the picnic. 幸好，野餐前雨停了。|近义词：luckily, happily, thankfully|反义词:unfortunately, sadly, unluckily|词性:adverb

词源:
From Late Middle English *fortunatli, from Old French fortunalement, from fortunal (“fortunate”) + -ment (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Latin fortuna (“fortune, chance, luck”).
