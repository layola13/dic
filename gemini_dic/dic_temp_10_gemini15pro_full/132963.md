nonempiricisms:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpɪrɪsɪzəmz/
|n.|非经验主义|Theories that are not based on observation or experience, but rather on pure reason, intuition, or other non-empirical sources.|His philosophy leaned towards nonempiricisms, prioritizing abstract thought over sensory data.|近义词:abstractions, rationalisms, apriorisms|反义词:empiricisms, observations, experiences|noun
