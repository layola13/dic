ostentatiousness:/ˌɒstɛnˈteɪʃəsnəs/ /ˌɑːstɛnˈteɪʃəsnəs/
|n.| 炫耀，卖弄，招摇 |The quality of being ostentatious; excessive display of wealth, knowledge, etc., intended to impress others.|Her ostentatiousness made her unpopular with her colleagues. 她的炫耀让她在同事中不受欢迎。|Synonyms: showiness, pretentiousness, flashiness, gaudiness|Antonyms: modesty, humility, unpretentiousness|noun
