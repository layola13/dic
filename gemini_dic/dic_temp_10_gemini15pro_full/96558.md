indow: /ˈɪndəʊ/ (UK) /ˈɪndoʊ/ (US) | n. |窗;  (尤指商店的)橱窗; |A framed opening, especially in a wall, that allows light and air to enter and allows people to see out.  A display area in a shop, often enclosed in glass. |例句：The cat sat in the window watching the birds. 猫坐在窗边看着鸟儿。

例句：The shop window was decorated for Christmas. 商店橱窗装饰着圣诞节的装饰。|近义词：pane, casement, display |反义词: wall, door |词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *vindauga, from vindr ("wind") + auga ("eye"). Cognate with Swedish vindöga, Danish vindue and Icelandic vindauga.
