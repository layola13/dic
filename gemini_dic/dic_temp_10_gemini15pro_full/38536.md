conclave:/ˈkɒŋkleɪv/ (US) /ˈkɑːŋkleɪv/| n. |秘密会议；教皇选举会议|a private meeting; a meeting of cardinals to elect a new pope|The cardinals held a conclave to elect the new pope.  红衣主教们举行秘密会议选举新教皇。|Synonyms: meeting, assembly, gathering, conference|Antonyms: public forum, open meeting|noun

conclave:/ˈkɒŋkleɪv/ (US) /ˈkɑːŋkleɪv/| n. |(尤指为特定目的而聚集的)一群人；集会|a group of people, especially one gathered for a specific purpose|A conclave of art experts gathered to appraise the painting.  一群艺术专家聚集在一起评估这幅画。|Synonyms: group, assembly, gathering, collection|Antonyms: dispersal, scattering|noun
