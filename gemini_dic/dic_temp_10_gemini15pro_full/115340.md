manoeuvrability:/məˌnuːvərəˈbɪləti/ /məˌnuːvərəˈbɪlədi/| n. | 机动性，操控性 |The ability of something, especially a vehicle or aircraft, to be moved easily and accurately. |The car's manoeuvrability was impressive on the winding mountain roads. 这辆汽车在蜿蜒的山路上表现出令人印象深刻的机动性。|Synonyms: agility, flexibility, mobility, nimbleness |Antonyms: clumsiness, awkwardness, rigidity |noun

manoeuvre:/məˈnuːvər/ /məˈnuːvər/ | n. v. |策略，调动；演习，调遣|A movement or series of moves requiring skill and care.  A large-scale military exercise involving the movement of troops and equipment.|The pilot executed a daring manoeuvre to avoid the other plane. 飞行员做了一个大胆的机动动作以避开另一架飞机。|Synonyms: tactic, operation, strategy, ploy |Antonyms: inaction, stillness |noun/verb
