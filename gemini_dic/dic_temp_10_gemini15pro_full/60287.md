eggheads:/ˈeɡˌhedz/| n.|书呆子，知识分子|A person who is highly intellectual or academic, often seen as impractical or out of touch with everyday life.  Someone who is very studious and spends a lot of time reading and thinking.|例句：The eggheads at the university are developing a new type of computer. 大学里的那些书呆子正在开发一种新型计算机。

例句：He was always considered an egghead in high school, preferring books to sports. 他在高中时总是被认为是个书呆子，喜欢书胜过运动。|近义词：intellectual, academic, scholar, brainiac, nerd|反义词：dummy, simpleton, airhead|词性:noun
