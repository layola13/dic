antiauthoritarianisms:/ˌæntiɔːˌθɔːrɪˈtɛəriənɪzəmz/
|n.|反权威主义（的各种形式）|The various doctrines, beliefs, and practices opposed to authoritarianism, emphasizing individual freedom and challenging established power structures. |The rise of antiauthoritarianisms across the globe reflects a growing discontent with centralized control. 全球反权威主义的兴起反映了人们对中央集权日益增长的不满。|近义词：dissidence, nonconformity, rebelliousness|反义词：authoritarianism, conformity, obedience|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- (prefix meaning "against") + authoritarianism (from authoritarian + -ism).  Authoritarian from Latin auctoritas "authority,"  from auctor "author, originator, founder."
