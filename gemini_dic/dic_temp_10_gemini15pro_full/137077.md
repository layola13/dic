nonstructural:/ˌnɒnˈstrʌktʃərəl/
adjective
非结构的；非承重的
Not related to or forming part of a structure, especially of a building.
The nonstructural damage from the earthquake was minimal. 地震造成的非结构性损害很小。
Synonyms: decorative, ornamental, cosmetic
Antonyms: structural, load-bearing
词性: 形容词
