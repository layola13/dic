galumphed:/ɡəˈlʌmft/| v.|笨拙地行走，踉跄|To move in a clumsy, ungainly, or noisy manner.|He galumphed across the room, knocking over a chair. 他笨手笨脚地走过房间，撞翻了一把椅子。|近义词：lumber, clump, stomp, stumble|反义词：glide, float, tiptoe|词性:动词

词源:  Likely a blend of "gallop" and "lump," suggesting a heavy, awkward gait. 可能由“gallop”（飞奔）和“lump”（块）混合而成，暗示着一种沉重、笨拙的步态。
