ethicalnesses:/ˈɛθɪkəlnəsɪz/
n.
道德; 伦理; 道义 (plural of ethicalness)
The state or quality of being ethical.  This word is rarely used, and "ethics" is generally preferred.
His ethicalnesses were questioned after the scandal.  他的道德在丑闻之后受到了质疑。(This sentence is grammatically correct but sounds unnatural.  It would be better to say "His ethics were questioned...")
近义词：morality, integrity, rectitude, righteousness, virtue
反义词：immorality, dishonesty, corruption, wickedness, vice
词性: 名词 (plural)

词源:  From ethical + -ness.  "Ethical" comes from the Greek "ethikos" meaning "of or for character, moral."
