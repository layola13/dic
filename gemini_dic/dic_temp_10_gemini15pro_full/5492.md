alopecia: /ˌæləˈpiːʃə/ (US), /ˌæləˈpiːʃɪə/ (UK) | n. | 脱毛，秃头症 |The medical term for hair loss is alopecia.  脱发的医学术语是秃头症。 | Alopecia can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics and stress. 秃头症可能由多种因素引起，包括遗传和压力。| synonyms: baldness, hair loss | antonyms: hair growth | noun
