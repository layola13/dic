canonicals:/kəˈnɒnɪkəlz/
noun
规范；经典；准则
Relating to or conforming to a canon or canons; following or in agreement with accepted, traditional, or authoritative rules or standards.
The canonicals of taste have changed throughout history.  口味的规范随着历史的变迁而改变。
synonyms: standard, orthodox, authorized, established, accepted, conventional, traditional
antonyms: unconventional, unorthodox, heterodox
