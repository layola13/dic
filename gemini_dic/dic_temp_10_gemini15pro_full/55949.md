django:/ˈdʒæŋɡoʊ/| n.| 姜戈 (一个Python Web框架)|A high-level Python Web framework that encourages rapid development and clean, pragmatic design.  It takes care of much of the hassle of Web development, so you can focus on writing your app without needing to reinvent the wheel. It’s free and open source.|Example: I'm using Django to build my new website. 我正在使用Django构建我的新网站。|近义词: Flask, Pyramid, web2py|反义词: None|Noun
