antic:/ˈæntɪk/
n.|古怪举动；滑稽动作；噱头|A playful trick or action, often silly or mischievous.|He was always up to some antic or other. 他总是搞些恶作剧。|synonym: prank, caper, shenanigan|antonym: seriousness, solemnity|noun

antic:/ˈæntɪk/
adj.|古怪的；滑稽的；奇特的|Grotesque, bizarre, or fantastically absurd.|The house was filled with antic furniture and decorations. 房子里摆满了古怪的家具和装饰品。|synonym: bizarre, grotesque, peculiar|antonym: normal, ordinary, conventional|adjective
