disconsolate:/dɪsˈkɒnsələt/ /dɪsˈkɑːnsəleɪt/| adj.| 悲痛欲绝的，极度沮丧的 | Without consolation or comfort; hopelessly sad. |  She was disconsolate after the death of her beloved pet. 她心爱的宠物去世后，她悲痛欲绝。

The team was disconsolate after losing the championship game.  球队输掉了冠军赛后非常沮丧。| 近义词：heartbroken, devastated, inconsolable, dejected, despondent | 反义词：cheerful, happy, joyful, elated | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *disconsolatus, past participle of disconsolari (“to discomfort, make sad”), from dis- (“not, un-”) + consolari (“to console, comfort”).
