misarranged:/ˌmɪsəˈreɪndʒd/| adj.|排列错误的，紊乱的|Placed or arranged in a wrong or improper order.  From mis- (wrongly) + arrange (to put in order).|The books on the shelf were misarranged, making it difficult to find what I needed. 书架上的书摆放得很乱，很难找到我需要的。|The flowers in the vase were misarranged, looking haphazard and unappealing.  花瓶里的花摆放得不好，看起来杂乱无章，没有吸引力。|近义词：disordered, jumbled, cluttered, chaotic|反义词：arranged, ordered, organized, tidy|词性:形容词
