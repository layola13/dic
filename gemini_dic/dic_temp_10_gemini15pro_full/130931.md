nonaspirated:/ˌnɒnˈæspəreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 非送气的 | Not aspirated; pronounced without an audible puff of breath after the initial stop consonant. | The /p/ in "spin" is nonaspirated, while the /p/ in "pin" is aspirated.  "spin"中的/p/是非送气的，而"pin"中的/p/是送气的。 | 近义词: unaspirated | 反义词: aspirated | 词性: adjective
