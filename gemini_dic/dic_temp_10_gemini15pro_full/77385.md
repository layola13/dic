It emphasizes that the gauntness belongs to someone or something.|The refugee camp was marked by the gauntness's of its inhabitants.  难民营的特点是其居民的消瘦。|近义词:thinness, leanness, emaciation, boniness|反义词: stoutness, plumpness, robustness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From gaunt + -ness + -'s.  "Gaunt" comes from Middle English *gant, perhaps from Old Norse gand "magic wand," with the notion of being bewitched and thus thin and haggard.
