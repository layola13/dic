hydrostatically:/ˌhaɪdrəˈstætɪkli/
adv.
流体静力学地，静水地
Relating to or involving hydrostatics, the branch of physics that deals with the characteristics of fluids at rest.
The submarine is hydrostatically balanced to remain at a specific depth. 潜水艇通过流体静力学平衡来保持在特定深度。
The dam was designed to withstand hydrostatically induced pressures.  大坝的设计是为了承受静水压力。
近义词: hydraulically,  under fluid pressure
反义词: pneumatically
词性: adverb (副词)

词源:
From hydro- + static + -ally. Hydro- comes from Greek "hydōr" (water). Static comes from Greek "statikos" (causing to stand).
