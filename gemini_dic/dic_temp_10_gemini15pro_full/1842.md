acrolein: /əˈkroʊliɪn/ (US) /əˈkrəʊliːɪn/ (UK)| n.|丙烯醛|A colorless, volatile, flammable aldehyde with a pungent, disagreeable odor. It is produced industrially as a by-product of certain chemical processes and is also formed when fats or oils are heated to high temperatures.|The burning fat released acrid acrolein into the air.  燃烧的脂肪向空气中释放出刺鼻的丙烯醛。

Acrolein is a major component of cigarette smoke. 丙烯醛是香烟烟雾的主要成分。|近义词: propenal|反义词: None|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ācer, acri- (“sharp, pungent”) + oleum (“oil”).
