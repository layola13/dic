nonimmanency:/ˌnɒnɪˈmænənsi/
|n.|非内在性，非固有性|The state or quality of not being immanent; transcendence.|The concept of God's nonimmanency suggests a separation between the divine and the created world. 上帝非内在性的概念表明神圣与被造世界之间存在分离。|
|近义词：transcendence, externality|反义词：immanence, inherence|词性：名词
