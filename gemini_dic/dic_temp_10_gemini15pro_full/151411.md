paroccipital:/ˌpærɒkˈsɪpɪtəl/| adj.|枕骨旁的，枕骨附近的|Located near or beside the occipital bone (the bone that forms the back and base of the skull).|The paroccipital fissure is a prominent landmark on the primate brain. 灵长类动物大脑上的枕骨旁裂是一个显著的标志。|The doctor examined the patient's paroccipital region for any abnormalities. 医生检查了病人的枕骨旁区域是否有异常。|近义词:  occipital, juxtaoccipital|反义词:  (No direct antonym, but could consider terms relating to opposite areas of the skull, such as frontal)|词性: adjective
