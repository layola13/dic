outgrow:/ˌaʊtˈɡroʊ/
verb
长得太大穿不下（衣物）；长得太大而不适用（某物）；长大成熟而不再（做某事，喜欢某事）
to grow too large for (something, such as clothing); to grow too mature for (something); to lose interest in or stop doing or enjoying (something) as you grow older.
My son has outgrown all his clothes. 我的儿子已经长得所有衣服都穿不下了。
She has outgrown her childhood fears. 她已经长大，不再有童年时的恐惧了。
近义词: outstrip, surpass, transcend
反义词: shrink, diminish
