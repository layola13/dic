avellane:/ˌævəˈleɪn/ | adj. |浅褐色的；榛子色的 |Of a light brown color, like that of a hazelnut. | The curtains were an elegant avellane, complementing the wooden furniture. 窗帘是优雅的浅褐色，与木制家具相得益彰。| The avellane walls created a warm and inviting atmosphere. 浅褐色的墙壁营造出一种温暖宜人的氛围。 | 近义词:hazel, hazelnut, light brown, beige, fawn | 反义词:dark brown, black, white | 词性:形容词

词源: From Old French *avelain, from aveline (“hazelnut”), from Latin avellāna (“hazelnut”), from Abella, a town in Campania, Italy, famous for its hazelnuts.
