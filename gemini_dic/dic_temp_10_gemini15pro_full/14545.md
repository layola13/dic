averse:/əˈvɜːrs/ | adj. | 厌恶的，反对的 | Having a strong dislike of or opposition to something. |  He was averse to any change. 他反对任何改变。

She is averse to taking risks. 她厌恶冒险。

近义词:opposed, disinclined, reluctant, loath | 反义词:keen, eager, inclined, favorable | 词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin 'aversus', past participle of 'avertere'  ("to turn away from"), from 'ab' ("from, away") + 'vertere' ("to turn").
