infantilistic:/ˌɪnfənˈtɪlɪstɪk/
adjective
幼稚的，孩子气的
Showing or characterized by a reversion to immature behavior or thoughts.
His infantilistic behavior embarrassed everyone at the party.  他幼稚的行为让派对上的每个人都感到尴尬。
The man's infantilistic tantrum in the store was shocking.  这个男人在商店里像孩子一样发脾气，令人震惊。
近义词:childish, immature, puerile
反义词:mature, adult, grown-up
词性:adjective
词源: From infant (婴儿) + -ile (形容词后缀) + -istic (形容词后缀). Ultimately from Latin infans (unable to speak).
