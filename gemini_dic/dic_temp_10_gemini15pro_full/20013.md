bioluminescences:/ˌbaɪoʊˌluːmɪˈnɛsənsɪz/
n.
生物发光现象
the production and emission of light by a living organism
例句：Bioluminescences create a spectacular underwater light show. 生物发光创造了壮观的水下灯光秀。

例句：Researchers are studying the bioluminescences of deep-sea creatures. 研究人员正在研究深海生物的生物发光现象。

近义词：phosphorescence, luminescence
反义词：(No direct antonym, possibly darkness or blackness)
词性: noun
