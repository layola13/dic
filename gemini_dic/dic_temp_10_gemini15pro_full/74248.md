foxhole:/ˈfɒkshəʊl/ (UK) /ˈfɑːkshəʊl/ (US)| n. |散兵坑，避弹坑|A hole dug in the ground as a shelter for one or two soldiers against enemy fire or bombardment.|The soldiers huddled in their foxhole, waiting for the shelling to stop. 士兵们挤在散兵坑里，等待炮击停止。|Synonyms: trench, dugout, bunker|Antonyms: fortress, castle|noun
