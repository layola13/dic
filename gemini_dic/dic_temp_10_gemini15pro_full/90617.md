humanest:/ˈhjuːmənɪst/ | adj. | 最人道的，最富人情味的 |  Most humane; characterized by the greatest degree of compassion and concern for the welfare of others. | He took the humanest course of action, sparing the animal further suffering. 他采取了最人道的行动，避免了动物的进一步痛苦。|  The vet always chooses the humanest way to euthanize an animal.  这位兽医总是选择最人道的方式对动物实施安乐死。| 近义词: compassionate, merciful, kind, benevolent | 反义词: cruel, inhumane, barbaric | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From "human" + "-est" (superlative suffix).  Ultimately from Latin "humanus".
