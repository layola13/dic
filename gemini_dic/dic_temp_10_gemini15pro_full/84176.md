half-proved:/ˌhæfˈpruːvd/
| adj. | 半证实的，未完全证实的 | Partially proven; not fully substantiated.  Not yet completely established or confirmed. |  The evidence against the suspect is half-proved, so the police are continuing their investigation. 警方掌握的证据只能部分证明嫌犯有罪，所以他们还在继续调查。|  The theory is half-proved, needing further research to confirm it. 这个理论只被证实了一半，需要进一步的研究来证实。| Near synonyms: partially substantiated, partly confirmed, incompletely proven | Antonyms: fully proven, completely substantiated, conclusively demonstrated | adjective
