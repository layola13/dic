customising:/ˈkʌstəˌmaɪzɪŋ/
verb
定制，个性化
The act of making or altering something to meet individual needs or preferences.
She is customising her car with new paint and accessories. 她正在用新的油漆和配件定制她的汽车。
personalising, tailoring, adapting, modifying
standardising, generalising
