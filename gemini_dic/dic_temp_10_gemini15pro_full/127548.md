necropoleis:/nɪˈkrɒpəliːz/ | n. | 大墓地，墓葬群 | A large cemetery, especially one of ancient times.  A city of the dead. | The ancient necropoleis of Egypt are fascinating to explore.  探索埃及古代的墓葬群令人着迷。 | 近义词：cemeteries, burial grounds, graveyards | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "cities of the living") | Noun

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek νεκρόπολις (nekrópolis, “city of the dead”), from νεκρός (nekrós, “dead body, corpse”) + πόλις (pólis, “city”).
