exangulous:/ɛksˈæŋɡjʊləs/
adjective
没有棱角的；没有外角的
Having no projecting corners or angles; lacking angles.
The exangulous design of the furniture made it safe for children. 这款家具没有棱角的设计，对儿童来说很安全。
近义词: rounded, smooth, curved
反义词: angular, sharp, pointed
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *exangulus, from ex- "out of" + angulus "angle, corner".
