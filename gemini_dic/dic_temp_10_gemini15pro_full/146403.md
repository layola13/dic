overfly:/ˌoʊvərˈflaɪ/| v. |飞越，从…上空飞过| to fly over something, especially in an aircraft |例句：The plane overflew the city. 飞机飞越了这座城市。|例句：Military aircraft overflew the disputed territory. 军用飞机飞越了争议地区。|近义词：fly over, soar over |反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "land in" or "touch down")|词性:动词

overfly:/ˈoʊvərˌflaɪ/| n. |（飞机的）飞越| an act of flying over a place, especially in an aircraft |例句：The overfly of the presidential plane caused a security alert. 总统专机的飞越引发了安全警报。|例句：The frequent overflies by military jets disturbed residents. 军用喷气式飞机频繁的飞越打扰了居民。|近义词：flyover |反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:名词
