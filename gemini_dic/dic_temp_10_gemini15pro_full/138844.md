nur:/nuːr/| n.| 护士; 保育员 |A person trained to care for the sick or infirm, especially in a hospital. | She is a registered nur. 她是一位注册护士。|  The nur took the patient's temperature. 护士测量了病人的体温。| 近义词:nurse, caregiver | 反义词:patient | 词性:名词


词源:

From German *Nur*, a shortened form of *Amme*, meaning "wet-nurse."  Related to Old English *nos*, meaning "nourishment." Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *nōþiz*, meaning "need, necessity."  The word evolved in German to specifically refer to someone who provides care, especially to children and the sick.
