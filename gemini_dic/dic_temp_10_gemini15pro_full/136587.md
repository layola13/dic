nonsanctimonious:/ˌnɒnsæŋktɪˈməʊniəs/
adjective
不虚伪的，不矫揉造作的
Not displaying or marked by sanctimony; not hypocritical.
She was refreshingly nonsanctimonious about her wealth. 她对自己的财富表现得令人耳目一新地不矫揉造作。
synonyms: genuine, sincere, honest, straightforward, down-to-earth, unaffected, natural, unpretentious
antonyms: sanctimonious, hypocritical, self-righteous, pious, holier-than-thou
