counterfired:/ˌkaʊntərˈfaɪərd/| v. |反击，还击|To return fire; to fire back at an enemy.  The troops counterfired against the sniper's position. 部队向狙击手的位置进行反击。|例句：The artillery battery counterfired immediately. 炮兵连立即进行反击。|例句：They counterfired when they came under attack. 他们在遭到攻击时进行了还击。|近义词：return fire, fire back, retaliate|反义词: surrender, yield|词性:动词
