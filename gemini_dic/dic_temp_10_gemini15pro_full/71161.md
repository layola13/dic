fissioned:/ˈfɪʃənd/| v. |分裂的，裂变的|Having undergone fission; split or broken apart, especially by a nuclear process.|The uranium atom fissioned, releasing a tremendous amount of energy.|近义词：split, divided, cleaved, ruptured|反义词：fused, merged, joined|verb (past participle)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fissionem*, noun of action from past participle stem of *findere* "to split, cleave".
