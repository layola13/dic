outbids:/ˌaʊtˈbɪdz/ | v. | 出价比...高;  胜过 | To offer a higher price than someone else in an auction or bidding process; to surpass or defeat someone by offering more. | She outbid all the other collectors at the auction and won the antique vase. 她在拍卖会上出价比其他所有收藏家都高，赢得了那只古董花瓶。| outdoes, surpasses, trumps | is outbid by, loses to | verb
