cherishers:/ˈtʃɛrɪʃərz/
noun
珍爱的人；珍惜者
People who hold something dear and treat it with affection and care.
The cherishers of traditional crafts are working hard to keep these skills alive.  传统工艺的珍视者正在努力保持这些技能的活力。
supporters, protectors, guardians
abandoners, neglecters
