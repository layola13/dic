emancipation:/ɪˌmænsɪˈpeɪʃən/ | n. | 解放，释放 |The act of freeing someone from slavery, control, or the power of another.  The freeing of someone from legal, social, or political restrictions. |  Lincoln's Emancipation Proclamation freed many slaves. 林肯的《解放黑奴宣言》解放了许多奴隶。

The emancipation of women is a long and ongoing process.  妇女解放是一个漫长而持续的过程。 | liberation, freedom, release |  captivity, enslavement, subjugation | noun
