belligerent:/bəˈlɪdʒərənt/| adj. n.|好战的；交战的；交战国|Inclined to or exhibiting assertiveness, hostility, or combativeness.  Waging war.|例句：His belligerent attitude made it difficult to work with him.  他好斗的态度使人很难与他共事。

例句：The two belligerent nations finally signed a peace treaty.  这两个交战国最终签署了和平条约。|近义词：aggressive, hostile, warlike, combative|反义词：peaceful, conciliatory, amicable|词性:adjective, noun
Etymology:  From Latin *belligerant-(em)*, present participle of *belligerare* "to wage war," from *bellum* "war" + *gerere* "to wage, carry on."
