dissipators:/ˈdɪsɪpeɪtərz/| n. |散热器；消散器；挥霍者|A person or thing that dissipates something, especially heat or energy.  A device that absorbs or disperses unwanted energy, especially heat.|The computer's CPU requires a large heat dissipator. 这台电脑的中央处理器需要一个大型散热器。|Synonyms: heat sink, radiator, diffuser, disperser|Antonyms: accumulator, concentrator, collector|noun
