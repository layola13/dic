inextinguishably:/ˌɪnɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbli/
adv.
不能熄灭地，不可磨灭地
in a way that cannot be extinguished
His name is inextinguishably etched in the annals of history. 他的名字不可磨灭地刻在了历史的史册上。
近义词:permanently, indelibly
反义词:temporarily, fleetingly
词性:副词

inextinguishable:/ˌɪnɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbəl/
adj.
不能熄灭的，不可磨灭的
that cannot be extinguished
She has an inextinguishable passion for learning. 她对学习有着永不熄灭的热情。
近义词:unquenchable, enduring
反义词:extinguishable, temporary
词性:形容词

extinguish:/ɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃ/
v.
熄灭，扑灭；压制，消灭
to stop a fire or a light burning
Firefighters worked hard to extinguish the blaze. 消防队员努力扑灭大火。
近义词:put out, quench
反义词:ignite, kindle
词性:动词


词源:
From Latin *extinguere, from ex- "out" + stinguere "to quench, extinguish."
