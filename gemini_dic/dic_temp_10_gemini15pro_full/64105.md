epos:/ˈiːpɒs/ /ˈepɒs/| n. |史诗; 叙事诗|A long poem, typically one derived from ancient oral tradition, narrating the deeds and adventures of heroic or legendary figures or the history of a nation.|Homer's Odyssey is a classic example of an epos. 荷马的《奥德赛》是史诗的经典范例。|Synonym: epic, saga, narrative poem|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps short story, lyric poem)|noun
