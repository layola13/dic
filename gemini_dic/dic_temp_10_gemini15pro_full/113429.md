lymphoma:/lɪmˈfoʊmə/ /lɪmˈfəʊmə/| n. |淋巴瘤|A type of cancer that develops from lymphocytes, a type of white blood cell.  淋巴瘤是一种由淋巴细胞（一种白细胞）发展而来的癌症。|例句：My uncle was diagnosed with lymphoma last year.  我叔叔去年被诊断出患有淋巴瘤。

例句：The doctor explained the different types of lymphoma. 医生解释了不同类型的淋巴瘤。|近义词：cancer, tumor, neoplasm|反义词:  (No direct antonym for a disease) |词性:noun
