clampdown:/ˈklæmpˌdaʊn/ | n. |压制，取缔，严厉打击|A sudden action taken by a government or other authority to stop or control something, usually related to illegal or undesirable activity. |The government imposed a clampdown on illegal gambling. 政府对非法赌博进行了严厉打击。| synonyms: crackdown, suppression, repression | antonyms: liberalization, deregulation | noun

clampdown:/ˈklæmpˌdaʊn/ | n. |取缔，制裁|A sudden restrictive measure or series of measures. |There has been a clampdown on public spending. 公共支出受到严格控制。| synonyms: curb, restriction, restraint | antonyms: allowance, permission, freedom | noun
