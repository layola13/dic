cudded:/ˈkʌdɪd/
verb
反刍后休息, 挤作一团休息
past tense of cud.  After chewing their cud, the cows cudded down in the field.
The sheep cudded together for warmth in the cold night.
近义词:rested, huddled
反义词:active, dispersed
词性:verb


cudded:/ˈkʌdɪd/
adjective
(of an animal) having chewed the cud.
The cudded cow lay peacefully in the shade.
The farmer checked on the cudded sheep in the barn.
近义词:ruminated
反义词:unruminated
词性:adjective
