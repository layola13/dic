overtightens:/ˌoʊvərˈtaɪtənz/
verb
过度拧紧；拧得太紧
to tighten something too much, exceeding the necessary or recommended level of tightness.
He overtightened the screws and stripped the threads. 他把螺丝拧得太紧，弄坏了螺纹。
overtighten, tighten excessively
loosen, slacken
v.
