mediatrix:/ˌmiːdiˈeɪtrɪks/
n.
女调解人，女仲裁人
a woman who mediates, especially between parties at variance
例句：The mediatrix helped the two warring families reach a settlement. 女调解人帮助这两个敌对的家族达成了和解。

例句：She acted as mediatrix in the dispute between the two companies. 她在这两家公司的纠纷中担任调解人。

近义词：mediator, arbitrator, negotiator, peacemaker
反义词：agitator, instigator
词性：noun
