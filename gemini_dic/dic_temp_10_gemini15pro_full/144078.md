outruns:/ˌaʊtˈrʌnz/| v. |超过，跑得比…快| to run faster than someone or something else and leave them behind | The cheetah outruns its prey. 猎豹比它的猎物跑得快。| The newer model outruns all its competitors in fuel efficiency.  新款车型在燃油效率方面超过了所有竞争对手。 |近义词：outpaces, surpasses, exceeds, overtakes|反义词: lags behind, trails|词性:动词

词源:  From Old English *ūtinnan ("to run out, escape by running"), equivalent to out +‎ run.
