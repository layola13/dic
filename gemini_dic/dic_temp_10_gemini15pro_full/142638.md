ostmark: /ˈɔːst.mɑːrk/ | n. | 东部马克 | The currency of East Germany before reunification.  It was also the name of the currency used in Austria after World War I. |  East German citizens had to exchange their ostmarks for Deutsche Marks after reunification.  东德公民在统一后不得不将他们的东部马克兑换成德国马克。| synonym: East German mark,  Reichsmark (historically in Austria) | antonym: Deutsche Mark (after reunification), Schilling (historically in Austria) | noun

ostmark: /ˈɔːst.mɑːrk/ | n. | 奥地利先令 | The currency used in Austria after World War I and before the introduction of the Schilling and later the Euro. | After the collapse of the Austro-Hungarian Empire, Austria adopted the ostmark as its currency. 奥匈帝国崩溃后，奥地利采用奥地利先令作为其货币。 | synonym: Austrian mark | antonym: Schilling, Euro | noun
