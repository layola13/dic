allegiance:/əˈliːdʒəns/ | n. | 忠诚，忠贞；效忠 |  Loyalty or commitment to a superior or to a group or cause. |  The knight swore allegiance to the king. 骑士向国王宣誓效忠。 | synonyms: loyalty, fidelity, devotion, commitment | antonyms: treachery, disloyalty, betrayal | noun

allegiant:/əˈliːdʒənt/ | adj. | 忠诚的，忠实的 | Loyal and faithful. |  She remained allegiant to her principles. 她始终忠于自己的原则。 | synonyms: loyal, faithful, true, devoted | antonyms: disloyal, unfaithful, treacherous | adjective
