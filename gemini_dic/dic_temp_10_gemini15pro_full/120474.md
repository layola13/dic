minsters:/ˈmɪnɪstərz/
n.
部长们；大臣们；牧师们
A group of ministers, especially in a government.  Can also refer to a group of Protestant clergy.
The prime minister met with his minsters to discuss the new policy. 首相与他的部长们会面讨论新政策。
Several minsters attended the conference. 几位牧师出席了会议。
近义词:secretaries, clergymen, pastors (for religious sense)
反义词:congregations (for religious sense, somewhat loosely)
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French ministre (“servant, minister, agent”), from Latin minister (“servant, attendant, assistant, agent, helper”), from minus (“less”). Doublet of minor.
