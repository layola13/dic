critter:/ˈkrɪtər/| n.|小动物，生物，家伙|A general term for an animal, especially a small or unfamiliar one.  A creature.|例句：The woods are full of critters. 树林里到处都是小动物。|例句：Look at that cute little critter! 看看那只可爱的小动物！|近义词：creature, animal, beast, varmint|反义词: (无明确的反义词)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Shortened form of "creature," which comes from Old French "creature"  and ultimately from Latin "creatura" meaning "thing created,"  from "creare" meaning "to create."
