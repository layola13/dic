hydrographic:/ˌhaɪdrəˈɡræfɪk/
adjective
水文地理学的；水道测量的
relating to hydrography; the science of surveying and charting bodies of water.
Hydrographic surveys are essential for safe navigation. 水文测量对于安全航行至关重要。
Synonyms: bathymetric, nautical, charting
Antonyms: terrestrial, land-based
词性: adjective
