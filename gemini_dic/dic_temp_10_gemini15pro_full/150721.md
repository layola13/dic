paracentesis:/ˌpærəsɪnˈtiːsɪs/
noun
腹穿术；腹腔穿刺术
surgical puncture of a body cavity, especially the abdomen, to draw out fluid.
The doctor performed a paracentesis to relieve the pressure from the ascites. 医生进行了腹穿术以缓解腹水带来的压力。
近义词: abdominocentesis, celiocentesis
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "infusion" as an opposite process)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek para- (beside, alongside) + kentēsis (piercing).
