buttoner's:/ˈbʌtənərz/
n.
纽扣工的 (工作场所、工具等)；装纽扣的人的 (物品)
Belonging to or associated with a buttoner (a person who sews buttons onto garments).  This word is a possessive form.
The buttoner's tools were neatly arranged on the workbench. 纽扣工的工具整齐地摆放在工作台上。
The buttoner's workshop was small but well-lit.  纽扣工的工作室很小但光线充足。
Synonyms:  (No direct synonym for this possessive.  Synonyms for "buttoner" would be seamstress, tailor, garment worker.)
Antonyms: (No antonym)
词性: noun (possessive)
