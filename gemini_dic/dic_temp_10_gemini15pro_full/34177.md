clamming-up:/ˈklæmɪŋ ʌp/
|phrasal verb|
|闭嘴，沉默，不愿说话|
|Becoming suddenly quiet and refusing to talk, often because of shyness or fear.|
|He clammed up when the police started asking questions. 他在警察开始问问题时闭口不言。|
|fall silent, shut up, refuse to speak, become uncommunicative|
|open up, speak freely, be talkative, be communicative|
|verb phrase|
