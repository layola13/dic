frogs:/frɒɡz/ (UK) /frɑːɡz/ (US)| n. |青蛙|The plural form of "frog," referring to more than one frog.  A small, tailless amphibian with smooth skin, long legs for jumping, and webbed feet.|
例句：The frogs croaked loudly in the pond. 池塘里的青蛙大声地叫着。

例句：We dissected frogs in biology class. 我们在生物课上解剖了青蛙。


近义词：toads, amphibians|反义词:|词性:noun
