hindering:/ˈhɪndərɪŋ/| adj. |阻碍的，妨碍的|present participle of hinder, meaning "acting as an obstacle to something"|The hindering factor in the project was the lack of funding.  这个项目的阻碍因素是缺乏资金。|Synonyms: obstructing, impeding, hampering|Antonyms: aiding, assisting, facilitating|verb (present participle)

hinder:/ˈhɪndər/| v. |阻碍，妨碍|To create difficulties for someone or something, resulting in delay or obstruction.|Heavy snow hindered travel. 大雪阻碍了出行。|Synonyms: obstruct, impede, hamper|Antonyms: aid, assist, facilitate|verb
