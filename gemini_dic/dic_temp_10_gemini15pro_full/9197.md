antioptimist:/ˌæntiˈɒptɪmɪst/ | n. |反乐观主义者，悲观主义者|A person who expects things to turn out badly; a pessimist.|例句：He's such an antioptimist, always expecting the worst. 他真是个反乐观主义者，总是期待最坏的结果。

例句：Despite the positive news, the antioptimist in her still feared the project would fail. 尽管有好消息，她内心的悲观主义者仍然担心项目会失败。

近义词：pessimist, cynic, doubter|反义词: optimist, positivist |词性：名词
