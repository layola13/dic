oversentimentalized: /ˌoʊvərˌsɛntɪˈmɛntəlaɪzd/
| adj. | 过于感伤的，过分多愁善感的 |  Excessively sentimental; characterized by an exaggerated display of emotion.  Making something seem more sentimental than it actually is. | The movie was oversentimentalized, filled with cheesy music and predictable plot twists.  这部电影过于煽情，充满了俗气的音乐和可预测的情节转折。 | 近义词：mawkish, saccharine, cloying, overemotional | 反义词：unsentimental, pragmatic, realistic | 词性: adjective
Etymology: over- + sentimentalize + -ed.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "too much" or "excessive". "Sentimentalize" is a verb meaning "to make sentimental," derived from "sentiment" (feeling) and "-ize" (to make). The "-ed" suffix creates the past participle form, used here as an adjective.
