nonuniformist:/ˌnɒnˈjuːnɪfɔːmɪst/ | n. | 非均变论者 | A person who believes in nonuniformism, the theory that changes in the earth's crust during geological history have resulted from the action of continuous and uniform processes. |  The nonuniformist argued that the canyon was carved slowly over millions of years.  非均变论者认为峡谷是经过数百万年缓慢雕刻而成的。|  proponent of uniformitarianism, gradualist | catastrophist | noun

nonuniform:/ˌnɒnˈjuːnɪfɔːm/ | adj. | 非均匀的，不一致的 | Not uniform; varying or differing, especially in quantity, degree, or rate. | The nonuniform distribution of resources caused economic disparity. 资源分布不均导致了经济差距。 | uneven, irregular, heterogeneous | uniform, consistent, homogeneous | adjective
