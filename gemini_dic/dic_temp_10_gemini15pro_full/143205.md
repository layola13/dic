outcreeps:/ˌaʊtˈkriːps/
verb
蔓延，潜滋暗长
to spread slowly and stealthily
例句：A sense of dread outcrept into her heart.  恐惧感慢慢地潜入她的心中。

例句：The vine outcrept along the wall. 藤蔓沿着墙壁蔓延开来。

近义词：spread, creep, extend, inch
反义词：recede, withdraw, contract, shrink
词性：v.
