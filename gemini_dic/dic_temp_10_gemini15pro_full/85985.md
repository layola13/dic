headspace:/ˈhɛdspeɪs/| n.|心理空间，精神状态|Mental or emotional capacity; room to think or feel comfortable.|I need some headspace to think about this decision. 我需要一些心理空间来思考这个决定。|Synonyms: mental space, room to think, breathing room|Antonyms: stress, pressure, anxiety|noun

headspace:/ˈhɛdspeɪs/| n.|净空，顶部空间|The unoccupied space in a container.|Make sure there's enough headspace in the jar for the contents to expand. 确保罐子里有足够的顶部空间让内容物膨胀。|Synonyms: clearance, free space|Antonyms: fullness, capacity|noun
