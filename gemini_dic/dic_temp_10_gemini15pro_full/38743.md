condolement:/kənˈdoʊlmənt/
n.
慰问; 吊唁
an expression of sympathy with someone who is grieving
Example: He offered his condolences to the bereaved family.  他向死者家属表示慰问。

Example:  She received many condolences on the death of her husband.  她丈夫去世后，她收到了很多慰问。

近义词:sympathy, commiseration, solace
反义词:congratulation, indifference
词性:noun
