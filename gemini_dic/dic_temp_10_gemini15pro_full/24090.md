brevis:/ˈbriːvɪs/ | adj. | 短的; 简洁的 | Short or brief.  This word is more commonly used in scientific or technical contexts. |  The plant has a brevis stem. 这种植物的茎很短。| synonyms: short, brief, concise, succinct | antonyms: long, lengthy, protracted, extended | adjective
