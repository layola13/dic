indentment:/ɪnˈdɛntmənt/| n.|缩进；凹陷；订货单|An indentment is a space left at the beginning of a line of writing or printing.  It can also refer to an official order for goods.|The first line of each paragraph has an indentment. 每段的第一行都有缩进。|The company issued an indentment for 100,000 tons of steel. 公司签发了10,000吨钢材的订货单。|synonyms: indentation, recess, order, requisition|antonyms: projection, protrusion|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *endenten, from Old French endenter (“to notch, tooth, jag”), from en- (“in”) + dent (“tooth”), from Latin dens, dentis (“tooth”).  The meaning shifted from "notching" to "setting back a line of text".
