air-dried:/ˈeərˌdraɪd/
adjective
风干的，晾干的
Dried by exposure to the air, rather than by artificial heat.
The air-dried flowers retained their vibrant colors.  风干的花朵保留了它们鲜艳的颜色。
Air-dried ham is a delicacy in some regions.  风干火腿在一些地区是一道美味佳肴。
近义词: sun-dried, naturally dried
反义词: oven-dried, dehydrated
