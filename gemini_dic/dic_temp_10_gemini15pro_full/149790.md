palea:/ˈpeɪliə/ (US), /ˈpɑːliə/ (UK)| n. |颖片，内稃；托片|A small membranous bract, especially one of the inner pair of bracts enclosing the flower in grasses.|The palea of the rice flower is delicate.|近义词:bract, glume, lemma|反义词:None|Noun

palea:/ˈpeɪliə/ (US), /ˈpɑːliə/ (UK)| n. |(解剖学)苍白球|A major component of the basal ganglia, a group of nuclei in the brains of vertebrates.  It is involved in motor control and learning.|Damage to the palea can cause movement disorders.|近义词:None|反义词:None|Noun
