incommensurately:/ˌɪnkəmˈɛnʃərətli/ | adv. | 不可通约地，不相称地 | Not commensurable; having no common measure or standard of comparison; disproportionately. |  The two projects were incommensurately different in scope and budget. 这两个项目在规模和预算上相差悬殊。| Synonyms: disproportionately, excessively, vastly, extremely | Antonyms: proportionately, comparably, similarly | 词性: 副词

incommensurable:/ˌɪnkəmˈɛnʃərəbl/ | adj. | 不可通约的，不能比较的 | Not able to be judged by the same standard; having no common standard of measurement. | The benefits of art are incommensurable with monetary value. 艺术的益处无法用金钱来衡量。| Synonyms: incomparable, disparate, incompatible | Antonyms: comparable, compatible, similar | 词性: 形容词
