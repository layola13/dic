nmt:/ɛn ɛm tiː/ |abbr.|神经机器翻译 (Neural Machine Translation)|A sub-field of computational linguistics that investigates the use of neural networks for machine translation.  It often produces more natural-sounding translations than traditional statistical machine translation methods.|例句：NMT has revolutionized the field of machine translation. 神经机器翻译彻底改变了机器翻译领域。

例句：Modern NMT systems often utilize deep learning techniques. 现代神经机器翻译系统通常使用深度学习技术。|近义词：machine translation (MT), AI translation|反义词:  (NMT doesn't have a direct antonym.  Perhaps "human translation" or "statistical machine translation" could be considered contrasting concepts.)|abbr.
