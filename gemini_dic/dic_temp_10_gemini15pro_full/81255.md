granita:/ɡrəˈniːtə/
n.
冰沙，刨冰
A coarse, granular ice dessert, typically flavored with fruit juice or other sweeteners.
We cooled off with a refreshing lemon granita. 我们吃了一份清爽的柠檬冰沙来解暑。
granita is a semi-frozen dessert made from sugar, water, and various flavorings. 冰沙是一种半冷冻的甜点，由糖、水和各种调味料制成。
synonyms: shaved ice, Italian ice, sorbet
antonyms: (No direct antonym, but could consider) hot chocolate, soup
noun
