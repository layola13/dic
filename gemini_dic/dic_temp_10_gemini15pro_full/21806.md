bobsleighs: /ˈbɒbsleɪz/ (British) /ˈbɑːbsleɪz/ (American)| n. |雪橇, 平底雪橇 |A racing sled for two or four people, used for sliding down an ice-covered run.|
The Jamaican bobsleigh team was a popular underdog story. 牙买加雪橇队是一个受欢迎的劣势逆袭故事。

The bobsleighs hurtled down the icy track. 雪橇飞速冲下冰道。

近义词：sled, toboggan|反义词:|名词


词源: "bob" (a short, jerky movement) + "sleigh" (a vehicle on runners for traveling over snow or ice)
