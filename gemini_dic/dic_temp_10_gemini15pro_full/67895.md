facade:/fəˈsɑːd/ | n. | 外观，表面，正面 |  The outward appearance or face of something, especially a building, often designed to create a favorable impression. | The building's grand facade hides a much more humble interior. 这栋建筑宏伟的外观掩盖了其内部的简陋。| synonyms: front, exterior, face, surface, mask, veneer | antonyms: interior, inside, reality, truth | noun

facade:/fəˈsɑːd/ | n. | 虚假的外表，假象 | A deceptive outward appearance or manner. | He maintained a facade of calm, but inside he was panicking. 他保持着平静的假象，但内心却在恐慌。| synonyms: pretense, charade, mask, disguise, show | antonyms: genuineness, authenticity, sincerity, reality | noun
