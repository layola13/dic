mesmerise:/ˈmezməˌraɪz/ | v. | 迷住，催眠 | to hold the attention of (someone) entirely; fascinate. | The magician mesmerised the children with his tricks. 魔术师用他的戏法迷住了孩子们。 | synonyms: captivates, enthralls, spellbinds, hypnotizes | antonyms: bores, repels, disgusts | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Franz Mesmer (1734-1815), an Austrian physician who developed a theory of animal magnetism, later called mesmerism.
