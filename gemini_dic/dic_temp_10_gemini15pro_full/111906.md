loincloth:/ˈlɔɪnklɒθ/ (US) /ˈlɔɪnklɒθ/ (UK)| n. |缠腰布，遮羞布|A piece of cloth worn around the loins or hips, especially as the sole article of clothing in some hot climates.|The tribesmen wore only loincloths.|Synonyms:  loin covering, breechcloth, breechclout|Antonyms:  trousers, pants, shorts|noun
