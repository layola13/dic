eyereach:/ˈaɪriːtʃ/ |n.| 视线范围，视野，眼界|The product is designed to be within easy eyereach of the customer. 该产品的设计使其易于顾客看到。|例句：The sign was placed within easy eyereach of drivers.  标志牌放置在司机容易看到的地方。

例句：The mountain peak was just within eyereach. 山峰就在视野范围内。|近义词：view, sight, visibility, range of vision|反义词：obscurity, invisibility|词性：名词

词源：eye + reach.  "eye" 指眼睛，"reach" 指达到的范围。
