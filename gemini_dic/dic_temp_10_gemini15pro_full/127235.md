nauseates:/ˈnɔːzieɪts/ | v. | 使恶心，使作呕 | To cause someone to feel nausea, an unpleasant sensation in the stomach often accompanied by the urge to vomit. |  The smell of the rotting food nauseates me.  腐烂食物的气味让我恶心。| synonyms: sickens, disgusts, repulses | antonyms: pleases, delights | verb

nauseated:/ˈnɔːzieɪtɪd/ | adj. | 感到恶心的，作呕的 | Affected with nausea; feeling sick to the stomach. | I felt nauseated after the long car ride. 长途车程后我感到恶心。| synonyms: sick, queasy, nauseous | antonyms: well, healthy | adjective
