antiritual:/ˌæntiˈrɪtʃuəl/
adjective
反仪式的，反对仪式的
Opposing or rejecting ritual or rituals.  Characterized by the absence or avoidance of ritual.
The antiritual movement within the church sought to simplify worship.  教会内部的反仪式运动试图简化礼拜。
近义词:  ceremony-less, informal, unconventional, iconoclastic
反义词:  ritualistic, ceremonial, formal, traditional
词性: adjective


Etymology:
"anti-" (prefix meaning "against, opposite of") + "ritual" (from Latin "ritualis," meaning "relating to rites")
