cutwork: /ˈkʌtwɜːrk/ (US), /ˈkʌtwɜːk/ (UK)
|n.|镂空刺绣，透雕细工|Ornamental openwork needlework in which pieces of a fabric are cut away and the remaining fabric is decorated with embroidery.|The tablecloth was edged with delicate cutwork.|近义词: lacework, openwork, broderie anglaise|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "solid fabric")|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "cut" + "work".  "Cut" meaning to incise or remove a piece, and "work" referring to the process of creating something.
