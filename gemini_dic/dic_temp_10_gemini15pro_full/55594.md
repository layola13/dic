disuniter:/dɪsjuˈnaɪtər/ | n. |分裂者，不团结的人| someone or something that causes disunity or division | The politician was labeled a disuniter for his divisive rhetoric.  这位政治家因其分裂的言论而被称为分裂者。| synonyms: divider, separatist, schismatic | antonyms: unifier, joiner, conciliator | noun


Etymology:  From dis- + unite + -er.  "Dis-" is a prefix meaning "apart," "asunder," or expressing negation.  "Unite" comes from the Old French "unir" and ultimately from the Latin "unus" meaning "one." "-er" is a suffix denoting a person who performs an action.
