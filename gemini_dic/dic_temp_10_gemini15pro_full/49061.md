dehumanizations:/ˌdiːhjuːmənaɪˈzeɪʃənz/
n.
非人化，去人性化
The process of depriving a person or group of positive human qualities.
The propaganda campaign engaged in systematic dehumanizations of the enemy.  宣传活动对敌人进行了系统的非人化。
dehumanization, degradation, brutalization
humanization, personalization
noun
