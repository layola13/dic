archangelic: /ˌɑːrkænˈdʒɛlɪk/ | adj. | 大天使的；似大天使的；崇高的 | Resembling or befitting an archangel; sublime. |  Her archangelic beauty captivated everyone in the room. 她天使般的美丽吸引了房间里所有的人。|  The painting depicted an archangelic figure with outstretched wings. 这幅画描绘了一个展开翅膀的大天使形象。| 近义词：divine, celestial, heavenly, seraphic | 反义词：demonic, infernal, satanic | 词性: adjective

archangel: /ˈɑːrkeɪndʒəl/ | n. | 大天使 | An angel of high rank. |  Michael is considered an archangel in many religious traditions.  在许多宗教传统中，米迦勒被认为是大天使。|  The archangel Gabriel appeared to Mary. 大天使加百列出现在玛丽面前。| 近义词: seraph | 反义词: demon | 词性: noun

词源:  From Late Latin *archangelicus, from Greek arkhangelos (archangel) + -icus. Ultimately from Greek arkhi- (chief) + angelos (messenger).
