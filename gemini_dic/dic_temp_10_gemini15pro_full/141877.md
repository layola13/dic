orchitis:/ɔːrˈkaɪtɪs/
noun

Orchitis can be caused by a bacterial or viral infection. 睾丸炎可能由细菌或病毒感染引起。
He was diagnosed with orchitis after experiencing severe testicular pain. 他在经历了严重的睾丸疼痛后被诊断出患有睾丸炎。
