clawer:/ˈklɔːər/| n.|爪子；抓取工具；爬虫程序|A clawer is a sharp, curved nail on the foot of a bird or animal, or a tool or program designed to grab or scrape something.  A web crawler is a program that automatically browses the internet to collect information.|The eagle sunk its clawers into its prey.  这只鹰把爪子深深地插进了猎物体内。

The robot arm used a clawer to pick up the delicate component.  机械臂使用抓取器拾取精密的组件。|近义词：claw, grabber, scraper, crawler (for programs)|反义词:None|词性:Noun
