adware:/ˈædwer/| n. |广告软件|Software that automatically displays or downloads advertising material (often unwanted) when a user is online.|My computer is running slowly because it's infected with adware. 我的电脑运行缓慢，因为它感染了广告软件。

Adware can be difficult to remove. 广告软件可能很难删除。

|近义词：malware, spyware|反义词:antivirus software|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From advertisement + software, by analogy with shareware.  First recorded in the early 1990s.

USAGE->
一般现在时:
Adware displays unwanted advertisements on your computer. = 广告软件会在你的电脑上显示不需要的广告。
Adware often comes bundled with free software. = 广告软件通常与免费软件捆绑在一起。
