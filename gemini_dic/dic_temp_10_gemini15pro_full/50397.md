deposit:/dɪˈpɒzɪt/ (UK), /dəˈpɑːzɪt/ (US)| vt.|存放，储存，沉积|To put or set down something in a place.|She deposited the book on the table. 她把书放在桌子上。|近义词：place, put, store, leave|反义词:remove, withdraw|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *deposer*, from Latin *depositus*, past participle of *deponere* "to put down," from *de-* "down" + *ponere* "to put."
