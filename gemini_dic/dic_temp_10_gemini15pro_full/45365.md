cuneiformist:/ˌkjuːniˈɪfɔːrmɪst/ | n. |楔形文字学家|A scholar specializing in the study of cuneiform script. |例句：The cuneiformist deciphered the ancient tablet.  楔形文字学家破译了这块古代石板。

例句：The cuneiformist spent years studying the intricacies of Akkadian. 这位楔形文字学家花费了数年时间研究阿卡德语的复杂性。|近义词：assyriologist, epigrapher |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

Etymology: From cuneiform + -ist.  Cuneiform comes from Latin *cuneus* (wedge) + *forma* (form).
