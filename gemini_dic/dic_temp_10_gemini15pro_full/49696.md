demob:/ˌdiːˈməʊb/ /diːˈmɑːb/| n.  v. |复员，退伍；复员军人|The process of releasing members of the armed forces from service, especially after a war. Also, a person who has been demobilized.|例句：Thousands of soldiers were demobbed after the war.  战争结束后，数千名士兵复员了。|近义词：discharge, muster out|反义词：enlist, recruit|词性:名词/动词

demob:/ˌdiːˈməʊb/ /diːˈmɑːb/| n. |复员军人|A person who has been demobilized from the armed forces.|例句：The demobs found it difficult to adjust to civilian life.  复员军人发现很难适应平民生活。|近义词：veteran, ex-serviceman|反义词：recruit|词性:名词

demob:/ˌdiːˈməʊb/ /diːˈmɑːb/| v. |复员，退伍|To release (someone) from military service.|例句：The government plans to demob thousands of troops next year. 政府计划明年让数千名士兵复员。|近义词：discharge, muster out|反义词：enlist, recruit|词性:动词

词源：Shortening & alteration of *demobilize*, from *de-* "from, out of" + *mobilize* "make ready for war," from French *mobiliser*.
