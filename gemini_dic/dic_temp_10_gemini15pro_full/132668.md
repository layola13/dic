nondisparately:/ˌnɒndɪˈspærɪtli/| adv.|无差别地，公平地|Without discrimination; in a way that does not favor one group over another.|The bank was required to lend money nondisparately to all qualified applicants.  银行被要求无差别地向所有合格的申请人提供贷款。|例句：The policy aims to treat all employees nondisparately, regardless of their background. 这项政策旨在公平对待所有员工，不论其背景如何。

例句：The judge ruled that the law must be applied nondisparately. 法官裁定，该法律必须无差别地适用。|近义词：equally, fairly, impartially, evenhandedly|反义词：disparately, unfairly, discriminatorily|词性:副词
