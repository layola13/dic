habitualize:/həˈbɪtʃuəlaɪz/
v.
使成为习惯；习惯于
to make something a habit; to become used to something
He tried to habitualize the practice of writing in a journal every day. 他试图养成每天写日记的习惯。
accustom, inure
disaccustom
verb
