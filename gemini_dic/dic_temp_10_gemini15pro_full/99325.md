intercurl:/ˌɪntərˈkɜːrl/ | v. |相互卷曲，互相缠绕| To curl or twist together; to become intertwined. | The vines intercurled around the trellis. 藤蔓相互缠绕在格子上。|The smoke and fog intercurled, creating an eerie atmosphere. 烟雾和雾气相互缠绕，营造出一种诡异的氛围。|近义词：intertwine, entwine, interweave|反义词：untangle, separate, unravel|词性:动词


词源:
From inter- + curl.  "Inter-" is a Latin prefix meaning "between, among, mutually, reciprocally." "Curl" comes from Middle English *curlen, of uncertain origin; perhaps akin to Old Norse *krulla “to twist, curl," and Middle Dutch *crullen.
