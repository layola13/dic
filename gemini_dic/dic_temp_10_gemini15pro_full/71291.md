fixer-upper:/ˈfɪksər ˈʌpər/
noun
需要修理的房子；待修缮的房屋
A house or other property that needs repairs but is available at a low price, offering the potential for improvement and increased value.
They bought a fixer-upper in the countryside and spent months renovating it. 他们在乡下买了一栋需要修理的房子，花了几个月的时间翻新。
handyman special, renovation project
turnkey property, move-in ready home
n.


fixer-uppers:/ˈfɪksər ˈʌpərz/
noun, plural
需要修理的房子（复数）
Houses or other properties that need repairs.
They specialize in flipping fixer-uppers. 他们专门翻新需要修理的房子。
renovation projects, handyman specials
turnkey properties, move-in ready homes
n.
