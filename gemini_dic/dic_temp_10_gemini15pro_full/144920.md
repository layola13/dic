over-teaches:/ˌoʊvərˈtiːtʃɪz/
| v. |过度教学；教得过多| To teach something too thoroughly or too much, often resulting in confusion or boredom for the student.  |The teacher over-teaches grammar, confusing the students with too many rules.|近义词：over-explain, over-instruct|反义词: under-teach, neglect|verb
