galleons:/ˈɡæliənz/
n.
大型帆船；(尤指15至18世纪的)西班牙大帆船
Large sailing ships, especially those used by Spain from the 15th to 18th centuries.
The Spanish galleons were laden with gold and silver from the New World.  西班牙大帆船满载着来自新大陆的金银。
ship, vessel

词源:
From Spanish *galeón*, augmentative of *galea* (“galley”), from Old French *galie*, from Medieval Greek *γαλέα* (*galéa*), from Late Latin *galea*, alteration of Latin *galēa*, from Ancient Greek *γάλεος* (*gáleos*) (“dogfish, shark; helmet”).
来自西班牙语 *galeón*，是 *galea*（“桨帆船”）的增大词，源自古法语 *galie*，来自中世纪希腊语 *γαλέα* (*galéa*)，来自晚期拉丁语 *galea*，拉丁语 *galēa* 的变体，源自古希腊语 *γάλεος* (*gáleos*)（“角鲨，鲨鱼；头盔”）。
