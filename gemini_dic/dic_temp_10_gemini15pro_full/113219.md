词源:
From Middle English *lusshe, *lusch, from Old French lasche, lache (“lax, loose, slack”), from Latin laxus (“loose, wide, spacious”).  The sense of "luxurious" arose from the idea of loose, flowing garments.  The sense of "drunk" likely arose from the idea of being loose and relaxed.
