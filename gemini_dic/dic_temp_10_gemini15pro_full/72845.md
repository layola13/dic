ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English folc "people, nation, tribe, multitude," also "common people, laity," from Proto-Germanic *fulka- (source also of Old Frisian folk, Old Saxon folc, Old Norse folk, Middle Dutch volc, Dutch volk, Old High German folc, German Volk, Gothic fulks), a word of unknown origin.

注意事项:
The word "folk" can be both a noun and an adjective.  "Folks" is the plural form of the noun.
