blurbists:/ˈblɜːrbɪsts/
noun
吹捧者；广告撰稿人；简介作者
A person who writes blurbs, especially for promotional purposes.
The blurbists have done a great job in making this book sound appealing.  吹捧者们在使这本书听起来很吸引人方面做得很好。
The blurbists crafted a compelling summary that captured the essence of the novel.  广告撰稿人精心制作了一个引人入胜的摘要，抓住了小说的精髓。
synonyms: publicists, promoters, marketers
antonyms: critics, detractors
