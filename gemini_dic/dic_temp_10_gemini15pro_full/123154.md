monkey:/ˈmʌŋki/ (US), /ˈmʌŋki/ (UK)| n.|猴子|A primate mammal typically with a long tail and distinguished by its intelligence and ability to climb.  A playful or mischievous person.|例句：The monkey swung from tree to tree. 猴子在树间荡来荡去。

例句：He's such a little monkey! 他真是个小淘气！|近义词：ape, primate|反义词:  (No direct antonym for the animal.  For the informal meaning, perhaps "angel")|词性:noun
