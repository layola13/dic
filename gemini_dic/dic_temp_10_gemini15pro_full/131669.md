nonconcentricity:/ˌnɒnkɒnˈsɛntrɪsɪti/ /ˌnɑːnkɑːnˈsɛntrɪsɪti/| n. |非同心度，偏心度|The state or condition of not being concentric; the amount or degree by which objects are not concentric.  The radial distance between the center points of two circles or cylinders that nominally share the same center point. |The nonconcentricity of the two circles caused vibrations in the machine.  这两个圆的非同心度导致机器振动。|Synonyms: eccentricity, off-center, misalignment|Antonyms: concentricity, alignment|noun
