haunting:/ˈhɔːntɪŋ/ (US), /ˈhɔːntɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|萦绕心头的，难以忘怀的；令人难忘的|Evoking a poignant sense of sadness or regret; difficult to forget, often in a slightly disturbing way.  It suggests something beautiful yet melancholic that stays in one's thoughts.|The haunting melody of the old song brought tears to her eyes. 那首老歌萦绕心头的旋律让她热泪盈眶。|Synonyms: unforgettable, memorable, poignant, evocative, lingering|Antonyms: forgettable, unmemorable, insignificant|adjective

haunting:/ˈhɔːntɪŋ/ (US), /ˈhɔːntɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|萦绕；(鬼魂)出没|The act or fact of haunting a place, as by a ghost.  Also refers to the quality of being haunting.|The haunting of the old house was the talk of the town. 那栋老房子闹鬼的事成了镇上的热门话题。|Synonyms: apparition, visitation, specter|Antonyms: - |noun
