dry-roast:/ˈdraɪ roʊst/
v.|干烤，烘烤|To roast (nuts, coffee beans, etc.) without oil or fat. |I dry-roasted the almonds until they were fragrant. 我干烤杏仁直到它们散发出香味。|近义词:roast, bake|反义词:boil, fry
词性:动词

dry-roast:/ˈdraɪ roʊst/
n.|干烤，烘烤|The process of roasting something without oil or fat; the product of such roasting. |The dry-roast of the peanuts gave them a unique flavor. 花生的干烤赋予了它们独特的风味。|近义词:roasting|反义词:boiling, frying
词性:名词

词源:  Compound of *dry* and *roast*.  The meaning "to criticize harshly" is a figurative extension of the literal meaning.
