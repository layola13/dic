feverwort:/ˈfiːvərˌwɜːrt/| n.|藿香蓟，胜红蓟|A plant of the genus Eupatorium, especially E. perfoliatum (boneset), traditionally used to treat fevers.|The Native Americans used feverwort to reduce fevers.|Synonyms: boneset, thoroughwort, agueweed|Antonyms: N/A|noun

feverwort:/ˈfiːvərˌwɜːrt/| n.|香薷|A plant of the genus Conoclinium, especially C. coelestinum (blue mistflower), sometimes also referred to as feverwort.|The blue mistflower, also known as feverwort, attracts butterflies.|Synonyms: blue mistflower, wild ageratum|Antonyms: N/A|noun
