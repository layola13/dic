microcalorimetry: /ˌmaɪkroʊˌkæləˈrɪmətri/ | n. | 微量热法 | The measurement of heat changes associated with chemical or physical changes, especially in very small samples. | Microcalorimetry is a sensitive technique used to study the thermodynamics of biological processes. 微量热法是一种用于研究生物过程热力学的灵敏技术。 |  Microcalorimetry is used to study the stability of proteins. 微量热法用于研究蛋白质的稳定性。 |  calorimetry, thermal analysis | N/A | noun

Etymology:
micro- (small) + calorimetry (measurement of heat)
From Greek *mikrós* (small) + Latin *calor* (heat) + -metry (measurement)
