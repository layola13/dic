dweebiest:/ˈdwiːbiɪst/
adjective
最呆子气的，最书呆子气的
most dweeby; most characteristic of a dweeb (a person who is perceived as socially awkward, uninteresting, or excessively intellectual).
He was voted the dweebiest kid in school. 他被选为学校里最呆的孩子。
近义词: nerdiest, geekiest, dorkiest
反义词: coolest, hippest, most popular
词性: adjective

词源:  From dweeb + -iest (superlative suffix).  "Dweeb" is of uncertain origin, possibly influenced by words like "feeble" or "dwarf."  First recorded in the 1960s.
