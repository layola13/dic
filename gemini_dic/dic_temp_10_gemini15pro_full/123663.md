mooch:/muːtʃ/
mooch: v. | 蹭，揩油，闲逛 | to get something from someone without paying for it; to wander around without any particular purpose |  He's always mooching off his friends. 他总是蹭朋友的吃喝。| synonyms: sponge, scrounge, bum, loaf | antonyms: provide, give, earn, work
mooch: n. | 蹭，揩油，闲逛 | the act of mooching | He went for a mooch around town. 他在城里闲逛。| synonyms: stroll, wander | antonyms: hustle, work
