overmellownesses:/ˌoʊvərˈmɛloʊnəsnɪsɪz/
|n.| 过熟，过度成熟（尤指水果或酒），过度伤感|The overmellownesses of the peaches made them mushy and unappetizing.  桃子过熟了，变得软烂不好吃。|The overmellownesses of the film's score made it seem overly sentimental.  电影配乐的过度伤感让它显得过于多愁善感。|近义词：overripeness, mushiness, sentimentality|反义词：tartness, firmness, unsentimentality|词性: 名词

Etymology:
"overmellowness" comes from "over-" (meaning "excessive") + "mellow" + "-ness" (noun-forming suffix). "Mellow" itself comes from Old English "mearu" meaning "soft, tender, ripe."
