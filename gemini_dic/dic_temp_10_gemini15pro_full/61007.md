elimination:/ɪˌlɪmɪˈneɪʃn/ | n. |消除，淘汰，排除|the act of removing or getting rid of something|The elimination of poverty is a global challenge. 消除贫困是一项全球性挑战。|近义词：removal, eradication, exclusion|反义词：inclusion, addition, creation|词性:名词

Etymology:  From Late Latin *eliminationem,  from Latin eliminatus, past participle of eliminare "to turn out of doors, expel," from ex- "out" + limen "threshold."
