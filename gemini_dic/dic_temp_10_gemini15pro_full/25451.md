bulldogger:/ˈbʊldɒɡər/ (US) /ˈbʊldɔːɡər/ (UK)| n.|牛仔竞技摔牛者；顽强的人|A person who wrestles steers in rodeos. Also, a tenacious or determined person.|例句：The bulldogger quickly wrestled the steer to the ground. 牛仔竞技摔牛者迅速将小公牛摔倒在地。

例句：He's a real bulldogger when it comes to negotiations.  在谈判方面，他是个真正顽强的人。|近义词：steer wrestler, cowboy, cowpuncher, rustler (in some contexts), tenacious person, determined person|反义词: quitter, coward |词性: noun
