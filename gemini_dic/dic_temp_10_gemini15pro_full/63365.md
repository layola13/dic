entomologist:/ˌɛntəˈmɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 昆虫学家 | A scientist who studies insects. |例句：The entomologist discovered a new species of beetle in the rainforest.  这位昆虫学家在雨林中发现了一种新的甲虫。

例句：My uncle is an entomologist and he has a fascinating collection of butterflies. 我叔叔是一位昆虫学家，他收藏了很多迷人的蝴蝶。| 近义词：insect scientist | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French entomologiste, from Greek éntomon "insect" + -logos "one who speaks (of), student of."
