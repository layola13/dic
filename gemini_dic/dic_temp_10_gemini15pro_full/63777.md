epicurean:/ˌɛpɪˈkjʊəriən/ /ˌɛpɪˈkjʊriən/ | adj. n. |  享乐主义的；享乐主义者 |  Devoted to the pursuit of pleasure; fond of good food, comfort, and ease; a person devoted to sensual enjoyment.  |  He lived an epicurean lifestyle, indulging in fine wines and gourmet meals. 他过着享乐主义的生活方式，沉迷于美酒和美食。

The restaurant caters to an epicurean clientele. 这家餐馆迎合享乐主义的顾客。 |  近义词：hedonistic, sybaritic, luxurious, gourmet, gourmand | 反义词：ascetic, spartan, abstemious | 词性: adjective, noun

epicurean:/ˌɛpɪˈkjʊəriən/ /ˌɛpɪˈkjʊriən/ | n. | 享乐主义者; 美食家 | A person who takes particular pleasure in fine food and drink. | He is a true epicurean, always seeking out the best restaurants and most exquisite dishes. 他是一位真正的美食家，总是寻找最好的餐厅和最精致的菜肴。

She considers herself an epicurean, appreciating the finer things in life. 她自认为是一个享乐主义者，欣赏生活中美好的事物。 | 近义词：gourmet, gourmand, connoisseur | 反义词：ascetic | 词性: noun
