cloisonné:/ˌklwɑːzəˈneɪ/ /ˌklɔɪzəˈneɪ/ | n. adj.|景泰蓝；景泰蓝制的|Enamelwork in which colored areas are separated by thin metal strips.|例句：The museum has a beautiful collection of cloisonné vases.  这家博物馆收藏了漂亮的景泰蓝花瓶。

例句：She wore a cloisonné pin on her lapel. 她在外套翻领上佩戴了一枚景泰蓝胸针。|近义词：enamel, enamelwork|反义词: (No direct antonym exists.  Perhaps "plain metalwork" could be considered a loose antonym.)|词性: noun, adjective
