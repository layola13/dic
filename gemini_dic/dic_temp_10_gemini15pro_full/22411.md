book: /bʊk/ | n. vt. | 书; 预订; 登记 | A written or printed work consisting of pages glued or sewn together along one side and bound in covers.  To reserve (accommodations, a place, etc.); make a reservation. | I read a book every week. 我每周读一本书。|  I booked a flight to London. 我预订了去伦敦的航班。 | 近义词: volume, publication, reserve | 反义词: cancel | 词性: noun, verb
