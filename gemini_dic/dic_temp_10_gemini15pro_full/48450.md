deeds:/diːdz/
|n.|行为；事迹；功绩；契约|An action that is performed intentionally or consciously.  A notable achievement. A legal document that transfers ownership of property.|
His brave deeds were celebrated throughout the land. 他的英勇事迹举国称颂。

The deeds to the house are in the safe. 房屋的契约在保险箱里。

|近义词:acts, actions, exploits, feats, achievements|
|反义词:inaction, omissions|
|词性: 名词|

deeds:/diːdz/
|v.|立契转让（尤指房地产）|(Usually of property) Transfer by deed.  This is less common usage.|He deeded the property to his son. 他将财产立契转让给了他的儿子。|
|近义词:transfer, convey|
|反义词:retain, keep|
|词性: 动词 (较少用)|
