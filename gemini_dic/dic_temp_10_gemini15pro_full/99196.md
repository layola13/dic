interclashing:/ˌɪntərˈklæʃɪŋ/
adjective
相互冲突的；相互碰撞的
Characterized by clashes or conflicts between different elements or groups.
The interclashing opinions of the committee members made it difficult to reach a decision. 委员会成员相互冲突的意见使得很难达成决议。
The interclashing colors in the painting created a vibrant and dynamic effect. 画作中相互冲突的颜色营造出充满活力和动感的视觉效果。
Synonyms: conflicting, clashing, opposing, colliding
Antonyms: harmonious, agreeing, consistent, compatible
词性: 形容词
