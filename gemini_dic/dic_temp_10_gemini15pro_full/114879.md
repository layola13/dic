mammoth:/ˈmæməθ/| n. adj.|猛犸象；巨大的，庞大的|A large, extinct elephant of the Pleistocene epoch, typically hairy with a sloping back and long curved tusks. Also used to describe something huge or enormous.|例句：Scientists are studying the DNA of a mammoth found frozen in Siberia. 科学家正在研究在西伯利亚发现的冰冻猛犸象的DNA。

例句：The company faced a mammoth task in rebuilding after the hurricane. 这家公司在飓风过后面临着重建的艰巨任务。|近义词：gigantic, colossal, enormous, huge, immense|反义词：tiny, small, miniature|词性:名词、形容词
