defuel:/diːˈfjuːəl/| v. |卸除燃料，去除燃料|To remove fuel from something, such as a vehicle or aircraft.|例句：The aircraft was defueled after the emergency landing.  飞机紧急着陆后被卸除了燃料。

例句：They defueled the rocket before beginning maintenance. 他们在开始维护之前卸除了火箭的燃料。|近义词：drain, empty|反义词：fuel, refuel|词性:动词
