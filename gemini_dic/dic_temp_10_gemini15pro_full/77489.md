gazellelike: /ɡəˈzɛlˌlaɪk/
adjective
像瞪羚一样的
Resembling a gazelle, especially in grace, speed, or agility.  Characterized by elegant, swift, and agile movements, reminiscent of a gazelle.
She moved with gazellelike grace across the dance floor. 她以瞪羚般的优雅姿态在舞池中移动。
The athlete’s gazellelike leaps carried him over the hurdles. 运动员像瞪羚一样的跳跃让他越过了障碍。
近义词: graceful, agile, nimble, swift, elegant
反义词: clumsy, awkward, slow, lumbering
词性: adjective
