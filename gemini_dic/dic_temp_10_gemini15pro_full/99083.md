interbrachial: /ˌɪntərˈbreɪkiəl/ | adj. |肱间的；臂间的|Located between the arms or forelimbs.  Relating to the region between the upper arms. |The monkey had an interbrachial membrane that allowed it to glide between trees.  猴子有一个肱间膜，可以让它在树木之间滑翔。| 近义词: axillary | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective


Etymology:
From Latin *interbrachiālis*, from inter- (“between”) + brachium (“arm”).
