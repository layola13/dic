marriers:/ˈmæriərz/
n.
结婚的人, 主婚人
People who are getting married or those who perform marriages.
The two marriers exchanged vows.  两位结婚的人交换了誓言。
The licensed marriers conducted the ceremony.  持有执照的主婚人主持了仪式。
Synonyms:  newlyweds, spouses, celebrants, officiants
Antonyms: divorcees, singles
词性: 名词
