burgomaster:/ˈbɜːrɡəˌmæstər/ | n. | 市长；镇长 | A mayor or chief magistrate of a town in some European countries, especially the Netherlands, Germany, and Flanders. |  The burgomaster welcomed the visiting dignitaries.  镇长欢迎来访的贵宾。| synonyms: mayor, chief magistrate, town council leader | antonyms: citizen, resident | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *burgemeester, from burger "citizen" + meester "master".  Cognate with German Bürgermeister.
