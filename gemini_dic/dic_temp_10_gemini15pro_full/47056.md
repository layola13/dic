date: /deɪt/ (US) /deɪt/ (UK) | n. v. | 日期；约会；枣；过时；注明日期；确定年代 | A particular day of the month or year.  An appointment to meet someone socially or romantically. |

例句：
The date on the letter is July 10th.  信上的日期是7月10日。

What is your date of birth?  你的出生日期是什么？

I have a date with him tonight.  我今晚和他有个约会。

This dress looks a bit dated. 这条裙子看起来有点过时了。

Please date and sign this document. 请注明日期并在这份文件上签名。

Archaeologists dated the fossil to be over 2 million years old. 考古学家确定这块化石的年代超过200万年。


近义词：
(n.) appointment, rendezvous, engagement, day, time
(v.) timestamp, mark, age

反义词:
(adj.) modern, contemporary, current


词性: noun, verb
