actualnesses:/ˈæktʃuəlnəsɪz/
noun
真实性；现实性；实际情况
The state or quality of being actual; reality.  The plural form of "actualness," emphasizing multiple instances or aspects of reality.
The actualnesses of the situation were far more complex than he had anticipated.  这种情况的实际情况比他预期的要复杂得多。
The report detailed the actualnesses of daily life in the war zone. 这份报告详细描述了战区日常生活的真实情况。

unreality, fiction

ETYMOLOGY->
From actual + -ness.  "Actual" comes from Late Latin "actualis" meaning "active, practical," from Latin "actus" ("act, action").  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns expressing state, quality, or condition.
