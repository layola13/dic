anticonservatism:/ˌæntiˈkənˌsɜːrvətɪzəm/ |n.|反保守主义|Opposition to conservative principles; the beliefs and actions of someone who is not conservative. |The rise of anticonservatism among young people is a significant trend. 年轻人中反保守主义的兴起是一个重要的趋势。|The government's new policies were seen as a sign of anticonservatism. 政府的新政策被视为反保守主义的标志。|Near synonyms:  liberalism, progressivism, radicalism|Antonyms: conservatism, traditionalism|noun
