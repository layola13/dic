camels:/ˈkæməlz/| n. |骆驼|A large, long-necked ungulate mammal of arid regions of Africa and Asia, having either one or two humps on its back and adapted to desert conditions.|We saw a caravan of camels crossing the desert. 我们看到一队骆驼穿越沙漠。|
camels:/ˈkæməlz/| n. |骆驼| Camels are known for their ability to survive for long periods without water. 骆驼以其长时间不喝水的能力而闻名。|近义词：dromedary (单峰骆驼)|反义词:  (无直接反义词) |词性: noun
