locally:/ˈloʊkəli/ /ˈləʊkəli/| adv.|当地地，本地地|In many areas, produce is shipped in from elsewhere, even though it could be grown locally. 在许多地区，农产品都是从其他地方运来的，即使它可以在当地种植。|例句：The fruit is grown locally. 这种水果是本地种植的。

例句：The newspaper is distributed locally. 这份报纸在当地发行。|近义词：nearby, regionally|反义词：nationally, internationally|词性:副词
