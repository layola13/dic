antilibertarian:/ˌæntiˌlɪbərˈtɛəriən/
adjective| 反自由主义的；反对自由意志主义的| Opposed to libertarianism; believing that individual liberty should be limited for the good of society. |  Many social programs are seen as antilibertarian because they involve government intervention. 许多社会项目被视为反自由主义的，因为它们涉及政府干预。| anti-freedom, authoritarian, statist | libertarian, liberal, individualist| adjective

antilibertarian:/ˌæntiˌlɪbərˈtɛəriən/
noun| 反自由意志主义者 | A person who opposes libertarianism. | He's a staunch antilibertarian, believing in strong government regulation. 他是一个坚定的反自由意志主义者，相信强有力的政府监管。| authoritarian, statist | libertarian, liberal, individualist| noun
