musks:/mʌsks/| n.|麝香，雄香鹿|A strong-smelling reddish-brown substance which is produced by the male musk deer and used in perfumes.|The perfume had a strong scent of musks. 这款香水有浓烈的麝香味。|Synonyms: fragrances, scents, aromas|Antonyms: odorlessness|Noun

musk:/mʌsk/| n. vt.|麝香，香；使…充满麝香气味|A strong-smelling reddish-brown substance which is produced by the male musk deer and used in perfumes. / To perfume or scent with musk.|The hunter sought the elusive musk deer. 猎人寻找难以捉摸的麝香鹿。|He musked his clothes with the pungent scent. 他用刺鼻的气味给他的衣服熏香。|Synonyms: fragrance, scent, aroma (n.); perfume, scent (vt.)|Antonyms: odorlessness (n.); deodorize (vt.)|Noun, Verb
