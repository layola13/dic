monomaniacal: /ˌmɒnəməˈnaɪəkl/ (UK) /ˌmɑːnəməˈnaɪəkl/ (US) | adj. | 偏执的，着魔的 | Excessively or obsessively focused on a single thing or idea.  Characterized by an intense preoccupation with one subject or interest, often to the exclusion of all others. | He has a monomaniacal focus on his research. 他对他的研究到了着魔的地步。 |  She pursued her artistic vision with monomaniacal intensity. 她以着魔般的热情追求她的艺术理想。 | 近义词: obsessive, fixated, fanatical | 反义词: balanced, moderate, reasonable | 词性: adjective
Etymology: From Greek *monos* (single, alone) + *mania* (madness, frenzy).
