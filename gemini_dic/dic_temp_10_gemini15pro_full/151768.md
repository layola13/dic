pask:/pæsk/ /pɑːsk/| n.| Easter, Passover |A variant of *Pasch*, derived from Old English *Pascha*, ultimately from Aramaic *pasḥā* meaning "Passover". Refers to both Easter and Passover. |例句：
They celebrated Pask with a feast.  他们用盛宴庆祝了复活节/逾越节。

The family gathered for Pask dinner.  这家人聚在一起吃复活节/逾越节晚餐。
