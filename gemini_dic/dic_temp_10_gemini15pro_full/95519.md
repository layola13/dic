include: /ɪnˈkluːd/ (UK) /ɪnˈkluːd/ (US)| vt.|包含，包括|to contain something as a part of something else, or to make something a part of something else| The price includes flights and accommodation.  价格包含机票和住宿。

The tour included a visit to the museum. 旅行包括参观博物馆。|近义词：comprise, contain, encompass, incorporate, involve|反义词:exclude, omit|词性:动词
