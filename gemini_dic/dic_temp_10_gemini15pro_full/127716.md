neglectors:/nɪˈɡlɛktərz/| n. |疏忽者，忽略者|A person who neglects someone or something; a person who fails to care for someone or something properly.|The child's neglectors were brought to justice. 孩子的疏忽照顾者被绳之以法。|synonyms: derelict, slacker, shirker, noncarer|antonyms: caretaker, guardian, custodian|noun

neglect:/nɪˈɡlɛkt/| v. n.| 忽视，忽略；疏忽；玩忽职守 | to fail to care for properly.|He neglected his studies and failed the exam. 他忽视了学习，考试不及格。|synonyms: disregard, ignore, overlook, slight|antonyms: attend to, heed, mind, cherish|verb/noun
