oratorial:/ˌɔːrəˈtɔːriəl/ /ˌɔːrəˈtɔːriəl/ |adj.|演说的；雄辩的；口才好的|Relating to the art of public speaking, or characteristic of an orator.  Suitable for orators or oratory; eloquent.|He delivered an oratorial masterpiece, captivating the audience with his powerful voice and persuasive arguments. 他发表了一篇精彩的演说，用他雄浑的声音和有说服力的论证吸引了观众。|近义词：eloquent, rhetorical, articulate, silver-tongued|反义词：inarticulate, tongue-tied, halting|词性: adjective

Etymology->
From Latin *ōrātōrius*, from *ōrātor* (“speaker, pleader”), from *ōrāre* (“to speak, plead, pray”), from *ōs* (“mouth”).
