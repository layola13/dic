glossarially:/ˌɡlɒsəˈrɪəli/ | adv. | 以词汇表的方式；逐字地；详细地 |  In the dictionary, the word is defined glossarially, providing every nuance of its meaning. 在词典中，这个词是以词汇表的方式定义的，提供了其含义的每一个细微差别。 |  He explained the contract glossarially, ensuring everyone understood each clause. 他逐字逐句地解释了合同，确保每个人都理解每个条款。 | 近义词:  lexically, verbatim | 反义词: briefly, summarily | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From "glossary" + "-ally". "Glossary" comes from the Late Latin "glossarium," meaning "a collection of glosses," from "glossa," meaning "a gloss, explanation of a difficult word."  "-ally" is a suffix forming adverbs from adjectives.
