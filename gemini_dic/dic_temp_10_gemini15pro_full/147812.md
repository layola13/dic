overproving: /ˌoʊvərˈpruːvɪŋ/
| n. |过度发酵|Allowing dough to prove for too long, resulting in a collapsed or dense texture. |The bread didn't rise properly because of overproving.|Synonyms: over-rising, over-fermenting|Antonyms: underproving, under-rising|noun
overprove: /ˌoʊvərˈpruːv/
| v. |过度发酵|To allow dough to prove for too long. |I think I overproved the dough, as the bread is quite flat.|Synonyms: over-rise, over-ferment|Antonyms: underprove, under-rise|verb
