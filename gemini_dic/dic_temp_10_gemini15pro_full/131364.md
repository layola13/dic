nonchurches:/ˈnɑːnˌtʃɜːrtʃɪz/
noun
非教堂；非教会组织
Places or organizations that offer spiritual or community-based activities outside of traditional church settings.
Many young people are finding community in nonchurches. 许多年轻人在非教会组织中找到了归属感。
Synonyms: community centers, secular gatherings, alternative spiritual groups
Antonyms: churches, temples, synagogues
