part-with:/pɑːrt wɪð/ | phrasal verb | 放弃，割爱，与...分开 | To relinquish possession of something, often reluctantly. | I finally parted with my old car. 我终于把我的旧车卖了。 | He was reluctant to part with his collection of stamps. 他舍不得放弃他的邮票收藏。|近义词：give up, relinquish, surrender, let go of |反义词：keep, retain, hold onto |词性:动词短语

词源: "part" 来自古法语 "partir" (分离, 分开), 最终源自拉丁语 "partiri" (分享, 分配). "with" 来自古英语 "wiþ" (对抗, 反对; 和...一起).  "part with" 的含义演变是从“与...分开”到“放弃对...的拥有”。

PHRASE->
part with something for (a price/an amount of money):  He parted with the car for a thousand dollars. 他以一千美元的价格卖掉了这辆车。
be hard/easy to part with: This old teddy bear is hard to part with.  这个旧泰迪熊很难割舍。
be willing/unwilling to part with something: He was unwilling to part with his inheritance. 他不愿放弃他的遗产。
part with something to someone: She parted with her old books to the library. 她把她的旧书捐给了图书馆。
never part with something: I will never part with this photograph. 我永远不会放弃这张照片。
