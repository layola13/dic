agonistic:/ˌæɡəˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 争斗的，好斗的; 激动性的，兴奋的 | Relating to or resembling contest, struggle, or rivalry.  Also, relating to the production of an effect (such as stimulation or inhibition) by a drug or other agent that mimics the action of a naturally occurring substance. | The two rams locked horns in an agonistic display. 两只公羊用角互顶，展现出好斗的姿态。
