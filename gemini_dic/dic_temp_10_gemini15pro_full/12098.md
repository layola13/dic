artistically:/ɑːrˈtɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 艺术地，富有艺术性地 | In a way that relates to art, or in a skillful and attractive way. | She danced artistically, expressing her emotions through graceful movements. 她翩翩起舞，用优雅的动作表达着自己的情感。| The cake was artistically decorated with intricate frosting designs.  蛋糕装饰精美，糖霜图案错综复杂。 | synonym: creatively, imaginatively, skillfully, aesthetically | antonym: clumsily, awkwardly, artlessly, crudely |词性:副词

art:/ɑːrt/ | n. | 艺术 | The expression or application of human creative skill and imagination, typically in a visual form such as painting or sculpture, producing works to be appreciated primarily for their beauty or emotional power. |  She studied art in college. 她在大学学习艺术。 |  He's a true connoisseur of art. 他是一位真正的艺术鉴赏家。 | synonym: fine art, visual art, creative activity | antonym: science, nature |词性:名词
