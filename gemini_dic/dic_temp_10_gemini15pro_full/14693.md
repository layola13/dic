awakeners:/əˈweɪkənərz/
noun
唤醒者；觉醒者
Those who cause a revival of interest or awareness in something.
The awakeners of the civil rights movement fought tirelessly for equality.  民权运动的唤醒者们为平等 tirelessly 地奋斗。
The new marketing campaign acted as an awakener for the brand. 新的营销活动唤醒了这个品牌。
synonyms: catalysts, instigators, pioneers, inspirers
antonyms: suppressors, inhibitors, deterrents
词性: 名词
