nonconjugalities: /ˌnɒnˌkɒndʒʊˈɡælɪtiz/ | n. | 非共轭性 | The state or quality of not being conjugated.  This often refers to mathematical or chemical properties that are not related by a conjugation operation. | The nonconjugalities of the two systems led to unexpected results. 这两个系统的非共轭性导致了意想不到的结果。 | 近义词: dissimilarity, difference, non-identity | 反义词: conjugacy, similarity, identity | 词性: noun

词源:  "Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "conjugate" (from Latin "conjugatus," past participle of "conjugare," meaning "to join together") + "-ity" (suffix forming abstract nouns).
