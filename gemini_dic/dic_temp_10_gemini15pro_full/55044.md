disquiets:/dɪsˈkwaɪəts/ | n.  |不安；焦虑；忧虑|A feeling of worry or unease.|The news of the layoffs caused widespread disquiet among the employees. 公司裁员的消息在员工中引起了广泛的不安。|Synonyms: anxiety, uneasiness, worry, concern, apprehension |Antonyms: calmness, tranquility, peace, serenity |noun

disquiets:/dɪsˈkwaɪəts/ | v.  |使不安；使焦虑；使忧虑|To make someone feel worried or uneasy.|The economic downturn disquieted investors. 经济衰退使投资者感到不安。|Synonyms: worry, disturb, upset, bother, perturb |Antonyms: calm, soothe, reassure, comfort |verb
