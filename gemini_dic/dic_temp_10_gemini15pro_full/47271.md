de-anglicizing:/diːˈæŋɡlɪsaɪzɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
去英语化，去除英语的影响
The process of removing English influence, especially from language, culture, or institutions.
The government is de-anglicizing street names. 政府正在去除街道名称中的英语影响。
De-anglicizing place names is a complex issue. 将地名去英语化是一个复杂的问题。
近义词:localize, vernacularize
反义词:anglicize
