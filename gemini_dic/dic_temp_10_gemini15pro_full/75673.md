fulfill:/fʊlˈfɪl/ /fʊlˈfɪl/| vt.|履行，实现，完成|to achieve or realize something desired, promised, or predicted.|She fulfilled her dream of becoming a doctor. 她实现了成为医生的梦想。|近义词：accomplish, achieve, realize, execute, complete|反义词:fail, neglect, abandon|verb

fulfill:/fʊlˈfɪl/ /fʊlˈfɪl/| vt.|满足，使满意|to satisfy a need, desire, or requirement.|The movie fulfilled my expectations. 这部电影满足了我的期望。|近义词：satisfy, meet, gratify|反义词:disappoint, frustrate|verb

fulfill:/fʊlˈfɪl/ /fʊlˈfɪl/| vt.|完成（任务、职责等）|to carry out a task, duty, or role.|He fulfilled his obligations as a soldier. 他履行了作为一名士兵的义务。|近义词：perform, discharge, complete|反义词:neglect, shirk|verb
