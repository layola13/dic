hitlerian:/hɪtˈlɪəriən/
adjective
希特勒的；纳粹的；极权主义的
Of or relating to Adolf Hitler or Nazism; characteristic of Hitler or his doctrines.
His policies were described as Hitlerian. 他的政策被描述为希特勒式的。
近义词:Nazi, fascist, totalitarian, tyrannical, despotic, autocratic
反义词:democratic, liberal, egalitarian
词性:形容词

hitlerian:/hɪtˈlɪəriən/
noun
希特勒主义者；纳粹分子
A supporter of Adolf Hitler or Nazism.
He was denounced as a Hitlerian. 他被谴责为希特勒主义者。
近义词:Nazi, fascist
反义词:democrat, liberal
词性:名词
