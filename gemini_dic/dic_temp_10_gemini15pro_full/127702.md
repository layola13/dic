neglected:/nɪˈɡlektɪd/| adj.|被忽视的，疏忽的|Failing to care for properly.|The garden was neglected and overgrown. 花园无人照料，杂草丛生。|近义词：abandoned, forsaken, ignored, overlooked|反义词：cherished, nurtured, cared for|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *neglectus*, past participle of *neglegere* "disregard, neglect," from *nec-* "not" (from PIE root *ne-* "not") + *legere* "to gather, select, choose, read."
