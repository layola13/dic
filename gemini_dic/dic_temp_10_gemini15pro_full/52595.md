diagenesis:/ˌdaɪəˈdʒenɪsɪs/
n.|成岩作用|The physical and chemical changes occurring during the conversion of sediment to sedimentary rock.|Diagenesis transforms loose sediment into hard rock. 成岩作用将松散的沉积物转化为坚硬的岩石。|lithification, cementation|metamorphism|noun
