gobbler:/ˈɡɑːblər/ (US), /ˈɡɒblər/ (UK)| n.|火鸡（尤指雄火鸡），贪吃的人|A male turkey, especially a large one.  Informally, it can also refer to a person who eats a lot.|The farmer raised several gobblers for Thanksgiving.  农民养了几只火鸡过感恩节。|近义词：turkey, glutton|反义词:|名词
gobbler:/ˈɡɑːblər/ (US), /ˈɡɒblər/ (UK)| v.|狼吞虎咽地吃|To eat something quickly and greedily.|He gobbled down his dinner in five minutes. 他五分钟内就把晚饭狼吞虎咽地吃完了。|近义词：devour, guzzle|反义词: nibble|动词
