bignoniaceous:/ˌbɪɡnoʊniˈeɪʃəs/ | adj. | 紫葳科的 | Belonging to or resembling the Bignoniaceae family of plants, characterized by often showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. |  The jacaranda is a bignoniaceous tree known for its vibrant purple blossoms. 蓝花楹是一种紫葳科树木，以其鲜艳的紫色花朵而闻名。| Synonyms: trumpet-creeper-like, catalpa-like | Antonyms: N/A | adjective

bignoniaceous:/ˌbɪɡnoʊniˈeɪʃəs/ | n. | 紫葳科植物 | A plant belonging to the Bignoniaceae family. |  Many bignoniaceous are popular ornamental plants in tropical and subtropical gardens. 许多紫葳科植物是热带和亚热带花园中受欢迎的观赏植物。| Synonyms: trumpet creeper, catalpa | Antonyms: N/A | noun
