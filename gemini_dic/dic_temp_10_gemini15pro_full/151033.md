parapectin:/ˌpærəˈpɛktɪn/| n. |副果胶|A water-insoluble substance produced during the ripening of fruit, especially pectin-rich fruits.  It is an intermediary product in the hydrolysis of protopectin to pectin.|Parapectin is formed as fruit ripens. 果实成熟时会形成副果胶。| The parapectin content increases as the fruit softens. 随着果实软化，副果胶含量增加。|近义词: protopectin (in a sense, as a precursor), pectinic acid (as a related substance)|反义词: pectin (in a sense, as a product of its hydrolysis)|Noun
