degenerationist:/dɪˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃənɪst/
n.|退化论者；堕落论者|A believer in the theory of degeneration, especially of the human race or society.|The degenerationist believed that society was in decline.|近义词:decadent, declining|反义词:progressive, reformist|词性:名词

degenerationistic:/dɪˌdʒɛnəreɪʃəˈnɪstɪk/
adj.|退化的；堕落的|Relating to or characteristic of degeneration or degenerationism.|He held a degenerationistic view of modern art.|近义词:decadent, declining|反义词:progressive, evolutionary|词性:形容词
