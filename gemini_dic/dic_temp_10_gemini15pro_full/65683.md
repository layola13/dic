evenest:/ˈiːvənɪst/
adjective
最平坦的; 最均匀的
superlative adjective of even: most even.  Having the least variation in height, depth, or similar dimension.
The evenest surface I could find was the tabletop. 我能找到的最平坦的表面是桌面。
The baker spread the frosting in the evenest layer possible. 面包师将糖霜尽可能均匀地涂抹开来。
近义词: flattest, smoothest, most uniform, most level
反义词: unevenest, roughest, most irregular, most bumpy
词性: adjective

词源:
From Old English *efen, from Proto-Germanic *ebnaz, from Proto-Indo-European *h₁ep- (“even”). Cognate with Old Frisian even, Old Saxon eban, Old High German eban, German eben, Gothic 𐌹𐌱𐌽𐍃 (ibns).
