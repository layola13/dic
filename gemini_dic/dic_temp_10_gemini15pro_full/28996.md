carthaginians:/kɑːrθəˈdʒɪniənz/
n.
迦太基人
People from the ancient city-state of Carthage, located in modern-day Tunisia.
The Carthaginians were renowned seafarers and traders. 迦太基人以航海和贸易而闻名。
Hannibal, a Carthaginian general, led his army across the Alps. 迦太基将军汉尼拔率领他的军队翻越阿尔卑斯山。
近义词: Carthaginian people
反义词: Romans (often considered their primary rivals)
词性: Noun (plural)
