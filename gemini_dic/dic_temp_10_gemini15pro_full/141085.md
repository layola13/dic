onp: /ɒnp/ |abbr.| osteonecrosis of the jaw (颌骨骨坏死)| ONP is a rare but serious side effect of some medications, particularly those used to treat osteoporosis. |  ONP can cause pain, swelling, and infection in the jaw. 颌骨骨坏死是一种罕见但严重的药物副作用，尤其是在治疗骨质疏松症的药物中。颌骨骨坏死会导致颌骨疼痛、肿胀和感染。| 近义词: BONJ (Bisphosphonate-related osteonecrosis of the jaw) | 反义词: N/A | abbr.


onp: /ɒnp/ |n.|  operating net profit (营业净利润)| ONP is a measure of a company's profitability after all operating expenses have been deducted. | The company's ONP increased by 10% this year.  该公司的营业净利润今年增长了10%。 | 近义词: operating income, EBIT | 反义词: operating net loss | n.
