ecthyma:/ekˈθaɪmə/ | n. |脓疱疮|A skin infection characterized by deep ulcers with crusts. It is a more severe form of impetigo.|The doctor diagnosed the child's skin condition as ecthyma.  医生诊断孩子的皮肤病为脓疱疮。|Synonyms: pyoderma, ulcerative impetigo|Antonyms: healthy skin|noun
