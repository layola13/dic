graphomania:/ˌɡræfəˈmeɪniə/| n. |书写癖，写作狂|An irresistible urge to write excessively.  A psychological condition characterized by an obsessive desire to write. |He suffers from graphomania and fills notebooks with his rambling thoughts. 他患有书写癖，在笔记本上写满了杂乱无章的想法。|近义词：hypergraphia|反义词: writer's block|noun
