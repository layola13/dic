damnedest:/ˈdæmədɪst/| adj.|最奇怪的；最令人惊讶的；最该死的|A superlative adjective, informally used to emphasize something surprising, remarkable, or frustrating. It originates from "damnedest," a euphemism for "damned."|That's the damnedest thing I've ever heard!  那是我听过的最奇怪的事情！|He tried his damnedest to win the race. 他拼尽全力想赢得比赛。|Synonyms: strangest, oddest, most surprising, darnedest|Antonyms: ordinary, common, usual|adjective
