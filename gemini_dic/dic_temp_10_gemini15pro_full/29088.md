caraway:/ˈkærəweɪ/
n.
香菜，葛缕子，香芹
An aromatic herb (Carum carvi) of the parsley family, with feathery leaves, small white flowers, and long, ridged, aromatic fruits.
The bread is flavored with caraway seeds.  这种面包用葛缕子籽调味。
近义词:cumin, fennel
反义词: (No direct antonym for a spice)
词性: noun


caraway:/ˈkærəweɪ/
adj.
香菜味的，葛缕子味的
Flavored with caraway seeds.
Caraway seed cake is a traditional treat. 香菜籽蛋糕是一种传统美食。
近义词:(No clear adjective synonym, perhaps "cumin-flavored" or "fennel-flavored" depending on context)
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: adjective (less common usage)
