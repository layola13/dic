hi-res:/ˌhaɪ ˈrez/ | adj. | 高分辨率的 |  Short for "high resolution," referring to images, displays, or audio that have a high level of detail and clarity due to a large number of pixels or a high sampling rate. | This camera takes hi-res photos. 这款相机可以拍摄高分辨率的照片。| synonyms: high-definition, high-quality | antonyms: low-res, low-resolution | adjective
hi-resolution:/ˌhaɪ ˌrezəˈluːʃən/ | adj. | 高分辨率的 | Having or producing images or sound of high quality, usually because of having many pixels or a high sampling frequency. | This monitor has a hi-resolution display. 这台显示器是高分辨率的。| synonyms: high-definition, high-quality | antonyms: low-res, low-resolution | adjective
