beseechingness:/bɪˈsiːtʃɪŋnɪs/
|n.|恳求，乞求，哀求|The beseechingness in her eyes made it hard to refuse her request. 她眼神里的恳求让人难以拒绝她的请求。|The dog looked at its owner with utter beseechingness, hoping for a treat.  这只狗满怀恳求地看着主人，希望得到一块零食。|近义词: supplication, entreaty, imploration, pleading|反义词: demand, command, insistence|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From beseeching + -ness.  Beseeching comes from the verb beseech, which is from Middle English besechen, from Old English besēcan "to seek, implore, beseech," from be- "thoroughly" + sēcan "to seek."  The suffix -ness forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
