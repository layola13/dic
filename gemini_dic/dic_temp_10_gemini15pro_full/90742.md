hummed:/hʌmd/ | v. | 哼唱，发出嗡嗡声 |  To make a low, continuous sound like a bee or a motor, or to sing a tune with closed lips. | She hummed a cheerful tune as she cooked dinner. 她做饭时哼着欢快的曲子。|  sing softly, croon, murmur | shout, yell | verb

hummed:/hʌmd/ | adj. (rarely used) |  | Used in combination, usually indicating the sound described. |  a hummed lullaby  一首哼唱的摇篮曲 (This usage is less common and often considered informal.) | - | - | adjective (used as past participle)
