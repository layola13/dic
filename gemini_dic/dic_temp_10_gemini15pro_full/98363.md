例句：She was insistive that she had seen him at the party. 她坚持说她在聚会上见过他。|近义词：persistent, insistent, pertinacious, tenacious|反义词：yielding, submissive, compliant|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English: from French *insistant, from Latin insistent- ‘standing upon’, from the verb insistere (see insist).
