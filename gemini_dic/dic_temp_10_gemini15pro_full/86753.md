helpfully:/ˈhɛlpfəli/| adv.|有帮助地，有用地|In a helpful manner; in a way that provides assistance or benefit. |She helpfully pointed out the mistake in my report. 她好心地指出了我报告中的错误。|近义词：usefully, beneficially, kindly, supportively|反义词：unhelpfully, uselessly, detrimentally|词性:副词

Etymology:
From "helpful" + "-ly."  "Helpful" comes from "help" (Old English "helpan") + "-ful" (Old English "-full").
