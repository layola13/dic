counter-mobilization:/ˌkaʊntərˌmoʊbɪləˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 反动员，反调动，对动员的对抗 | The act of organizing and preparing forces or resources in opposition to a mobilization.  This could be a military mobilization, a social mobilization, or a political mobilization. |  The government's counter-mobilization efforts effectively quelled the protests. 政府的反动员工作有效地平息了抗议活动。

The counter-mobilization of the opposition party stalled the legislative process. 反对党的反动员行动阻碍了立法进程。 | 近义词：counter-measures, counter-offensive | 反义词：mobilization | 词性：名词

Etymology:
counter- (prefix): from Old French *contre- and directly from Latin contra "against, opposite"
mobilization: from mobilize + -ation,  mobilize from French mobiliser "to make mobile," from mobile "movable," from Latin mobilis.
