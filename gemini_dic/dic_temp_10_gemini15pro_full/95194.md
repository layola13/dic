inauguration: /ɪˌnɔːɡjəˈreɪʃn/ | n. | 就职典礼；开幕式；落成典礼 |  A ceremony in which the president of a country or other leader officially takes office or a building or project is officially opened. | The inauguration of the new president was a grand affair. 新总统的就职典礼是一场盛大的活动。| The museum's inauguration attracted many visitors. 博物馆的开幕式吸引了许多游客。 | 近义词：commencement, initiation, opening | 反义词：conclusion, ending | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *inauguratio*, from Latin inaugurare "to take omens from the flight of birds," from in- "in, on" + augurare "to predict, foretell," from augur "priest who divines by the flight of birds."
