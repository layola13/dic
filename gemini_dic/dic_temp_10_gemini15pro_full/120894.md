miscalculate:/ˌmɪskælkjʊˈleɪt/ | v. | 计算错误，估算错 | To calculate or judge something incorrectly. |  I miscalculated the distance and arrived late. 我错误地计算了距离，所以迟到了。

|  She miscalculated the cost of the project. 她错误估计了项目的成本。

| 近义词: misjudge, underestimate, overestimate | 反义词: calculate correctly, estimate accurately | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (wrongly) + calculate (from Latin calculāre, "to reckon, compute," from calculus, "pebble used in reckoning").
