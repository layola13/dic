一般现在时 (Simple Present):
You hear the birds singing. 你听到鸟儿在唱歌。
Do you hear what I'm saying? 你听到我在说什么吗？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am hearing a strange noise. 我正在听到一个奇怪的声音。(通常用listen to)
Are you hearing me clearly? 你能清楚地听到我说话吗？(通常用listen to)


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Have you heard the news? 你听到这个消息了吗？
I have heard that song before. 我以前听过那首歌。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been hearing good things about that restaurant. 我一直听说那家餐馆不错。 (通常用"I have been hearing"较少见)
She has been hearing rumors about her boss. 她一直听到关于她老板的谣言。(通常用"She has been hearing"较少见)

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I heard a knock at the door. 我听到敲门声。
Did you hear the thunder last night? 你昨晚听到雷声了吗？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was hearing a strange noise when the phone rang.  电话响的时候，我正听到一个奇怪的声音。(通常用listening to)
We were hearing the concert when the power went out.  停电时我们正在听音乐会。(通常用listening to)

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had heard the news before you told me. 在你告诉我之前，我已经听到这个消息了。
She had heard the song many times before she learned the lyrics. 在学会歌词之前，她已经听过这首歌很多次了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been hearing good things about that movie before I finally saw it. 在我最终看到那部电影之前，我一直听说它很好。(通常用"I had been hearing"较少见)
They had been hearing rumors about the company merger for months. 几个月来，他们一直听到有关公司合并的谣言。(通常用"They had been hearing"较少见)

一般将来时 (Simple Future):

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):

By the end of the year, she will have been hearing complaints about the new policy for months. 到年底，她将已经听了好几个月的关于新政策的投诉。

虚拟语气:
If thou hearest him, tell him to come home. 如果你听到他的消息，告诉他回家。
I wish I hearest  the music better. 我希望我能更好地听到音乐。 (Hearst 用于虚拟语气非常古老和罕见)


被动语态: (hear 通常不用于被动语态， 这里用be heard)
The music is heard throughout the house.  音乐在整个房子里都能听到。
His voice was heard above the noise. 他的声音盖过了噪音。


疑问句:
Hearest thou the wind?  你听到风声了吗？（古英语用法）
Do you hear what I hear? 你听到我听到的声音了吗？

否定句:
I hear thee not. 我听不到你。（古英语用法）
I do not hear anything. 我什么也听不到。
