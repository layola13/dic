grandmastership:/ˌɡrændˈmæstərʃɪp/| n.|大师级，大师地位|The status, rank, or skill of a grandmaster.  The attainment of grandmaster status requires exceptional skill and dedication.|He achieved grandmastership in chess at a young age. 他在很小的时候就获得了国际象棋大师的头衔。|Synonyms: mastery, expertise, proficiency|Antonyms: incompetence, ineptitude|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From grandmaster + -ship.  "Grandmaster" comes from the French "grand maître," meaning "great master."  The suffix "-ship" denotes a state, condition, or quality of being.
