outpromises: /ˌaʊtˈprɒmɪsɪz/ | v. | 做出比...更多的承诺; 比...承诺得更多 |  To make more or greater promises than someone else. | The candidate outpromised his opponent, vowing to lower taxes and increase spending.  这位候选人比他的对手承诺得更多，誓言要降低税收和增加支出。 |近义词: outbid, surpass, exceed | 反义词: underpromise |词性: 动词

outpromises: /ˌaʊtˈprɒmɪsɪz/ | n. | 做出的超过别人的承诺 |  Promises that exceed those made by someone else. |  His outpromises ultimately led to his downfall when he couldn't deliver. 他过多的承诺最终导致了他的垮台，因为他无法兑现。 |近义词: exaggerations, hyperbole | 反义词: understatements |词性: 名词
