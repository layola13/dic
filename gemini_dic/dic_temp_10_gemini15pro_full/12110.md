artocarpous:/ˌɑːrtɪˈkɑːrpəs/
adjective
结聚花果的；多花果的
(of a flower) having the pistils united so as to form a compound ovary.  Bearing multiple fruits fused together.
例句：The breadfruit is an artocarpous plant.  面包果是一种结聚花果的植物。

例句：Artocarpous trees are common in tropical regions.  结聚花果的树木在热带地区很常见。

近义词：multiple-fruited, syncarpous
反义词：apocarpous
词性：adjective
