disregarders:/ˌdɪsrɪˈɡɑːrdərz/
n.
漠视者；不理会者；不尊重者
People who disregard rules or advice are disregarders.
那些无视规则或建议的人是不尊重者。
He is one of those disregarders of tradition.
他是那些漠视传统的人之一。
Synonym: ignorers, scoffers, despisers
Antonym: respecters, observers, adherents
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From disregard + -er (agent suffix).  Disregard itself comes from dis- (expressing negation) + regard (from Old French regarder, "to look at, watch"). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *wardōną ("to watch").
