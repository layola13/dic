hardboard:/ˈhɑːrdbɔːrd/| n. |硬纸板，纤维板|A stiff board made from compressed wood fibers. |例句：The back of the cupboard is made of hardboard.  橱柜的背面是由硬纸板制成的。

例句：He used hardboard to protect the floor during renovations. 他用硬纸板在装修期间保护地板。|近义词：fiberboard, masonite|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps softwood) |词性:noun
