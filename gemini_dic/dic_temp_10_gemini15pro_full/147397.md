overnighted:/ˌoʊvərˈnaɪtɪd/ | v. |过夜发送；加夜班；过夜|The documents were overnighted to the head office. 文件被连夜送到总部。|例句：I overnighted the package to ensure it arrived on time. 我连夜寄送了包裹以确保它能准时到达。

例句：The lawyer overnighted in the office to finish the case. 律师在办公室里过夜以完成这个案子。|近义词：sent overnight, rushed, expedited|反义词:delayed|词性:动词

overnight:/ˈoʊvərˌnaɪt/ | adj. adv. |一夜间的；突然的；过夜地；在晚上；通宵|Her success was not overnight; it took years of hard work. 她的成功并非一蹴而就，而是花费了多年的努力。|例句：We took an overnight flight to London. 我们乘坐夜间航班去了伦敦。

例句：The temperature dropped overnight.  温度一夜之间下降了。|近义词：sudden, unexpected, all-night|反义词:gradual, expected|词性:形容词/副词


词源: From "over" + "night."  "Over" in this context means "during the course of."
