henpecking:/ˈhenˌpekɪŋ/| adj.| 惧内的，怕老婆的，妻管严的 |Continually nagging and criticizing one's husband or male partner.  Constantly finding fault with and criticizing (a husband or other male partner).|例句：He is constantly henpecked by his wife for his messy habits. 他总是因为他邋遢的习惯被他妻子数落。

例句：He felt henpecked whenever his wife criticized his driving. 每当他妻子批评他的驾驶技术时，他都感到很窝囊。|近义词：browbeaten, bullied, dominated|反义词：dominant, assertive|词性: adjective

henpeck:/ˈhenˌpek/| v.| 责骂（尤指妻子对丈夫），对…唠叨| To continually nag and criticize (one's husband or male partner).|例句：She henpecks him about leaving his socks on the floor. 她因为他把袜子扔在地板上而责骂他。

例句：He was tired of being henpecked about every little thing. 他厌倦了因为每件小事都被唠叨。|近义词：nag, carp, criticize|反义词：praise, support|词性: verb

词源:  From hen + peck, referring to a hen's habit of pecking.  字面意思是“母鸡啄”，引申为妻子像母鸡一样不停地“啄”丈夫，也就是唠叨、责骂。
