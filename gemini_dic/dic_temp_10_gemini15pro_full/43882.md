crestfallennesses:/ˈkrɛstˌfɔːlənɪsɪz/
n.
沮丧，垂头丧气
The state of being crestfallen; dejection.
His crestfallenness was evident after he lost the match.  他在比赛失利后显得很沮丧。
近义词:dejection, despondency, discouragement
反义词:elation, joy, happiness
词性:名词


词源:
From crestfallen + -ness.  Crestfallen originally referred to a cock with drooping crest, a sign of defeat.  The suffix "-ness" turns the adjective "crestfallen" into a noun, indicating the state or quality of being crestfallen.
