clinometry:/klɪˈnɒmɪtri/ | n. | 测斜术，测斜学 | The science of measuring slopes or angles of elevation or depression, especially as applied to determining the heights of objects or the dip of geological strata. |  Geologists use clinometry to measure the dip of rock strata.  地质学家使用测斜术来测量岩层的倾角。

Clinometry is an important tool in surveying. 测斜术是测量学中的一个重要工具。 | 近义词: inclinometry | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *κλίνειν (klinein, “to lean, incline”) +‎ *-μετρία (-metria, “-metry”).
