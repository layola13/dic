blanker:/ˈblæŋkər/| n.|空白表格，空白处|A form or space that needs to be filled in. |Please fill in all the blankers on the application. 请填写申请表上的所有空白处。| synonym: blank, space, void | antonym: filled, complete | noun

blanker:/ˈblæŋkər/| v.|使...空白，使...茫然|To make something blank or empty; to cause someone to become confused or unable to think clearly. |The shock blankered his mind.  震惊使他头脑一片空白。| synonym: erase, clear, confuse | antonym: fill, complete, clarify | verb
