bots:/bɑːts/ | n. | 机器人；网络机器人 | An internet bot is a software application that runs automated tasks over the Internet. |  These bots can crawl websites and gather information. 这些机器人可以抓取网站并收集信息。

| Examples:
Social media bots are often used to spread misinformation. 社交媒体机器人经常被用来传播虚假信息。
Many companies use chatbots to handle customer service inquiries. 许多公司使用聊天机器人来处理客户服务咨询。

| Synonyms: robots, crawlers, spiders, internet agents
| Antonyms: humans
