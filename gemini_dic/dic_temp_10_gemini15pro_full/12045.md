articulating:/ɑːrˈtɪkjʊleɪtɪŋ/
adjective
清晰表达的，清楚吐字的
expressing oneself readily, clearly, and effectively; having joints or jointed segments
She gave a clear and articulating presentation. 她做了一个清晰且表达流畅的演讲。
The skeleton is an articulating framework. 骨骼是一个关节连接的框架。
近义词: eloquent, fluent, coherent, expressive, lucid
反义词: inarticulate, mumbled, unclear, incoherent, garbled
词性: adjective


articulate:/ɑːrˈtɪkjʊleɪt/
verb
清晰表达，清楚吐字
to express (something, such as an idea or feeling) fluently and coherently
He articulated his concerns about the project. 他清晰地表达了他对这个项目的担忧。
She struggled to articulate her feelings. 她难以表达自己的感受。
近义词: express, voice, pronounce, enunciate, communicate
反义词: mumble, slur, mutter, stammer, falter
词性: verb


Etymology:
From Late Latin *articulatus, past participle of articulare (“to utter distinctly, divide into joints”), from Latin articulus (“a small joint, division”), diminutive of artus (“joint, limb”).
