nonperpetuation:/ˌnɑːnpərˌpetʃuˈeɪʃən/ |n.| 不延续，不永久化 |The nonperpetuation of outdated traditions is essential for societal progress. 不延续过时的传统对于社会进步至关重要。| The museum is dedicated to the nonperpetuation of harmful stereotypes.  这家博物馆致力于消除有害的刻板印象。 |近义词：cessation, discontinuation, termination |反义词：perpetuation, continuation, preservation |词性: 名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
perpetuation (noun): from Late Latin perpetuatio, from perpetuatus (past participle of perpetuare "to make perpetual"), from perpetuus "continuous," from per- "through" + -petuus (akin to petere "to seek, go to").
