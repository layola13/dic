hydroplane:/ˈhaɪdrəpleɪn/ | n. vi.|水上飞机；滑水板；|A fast motorboat designed to skim over the surface of the water.  一种设计用来掠过水面的快速摩托艇。|The hydroplane skipped across the waves. 水上飞机掠过波浪。|近义词：seaplane, floatplane, hydrofoil|反义词:submarine|noun
hydroplane:/ˈhaɪdrəpleɪn/ | vi. |（车辆）在积水上打滑，水上滑行；|To slide uncontrollably on a wet road. 在潮湿的路面上失控滑行。|The car hydroplaned on the wet pavement. 汽车在湿漉漉的路面上打滑。|近义词：aquaplane, skid|反义词:brake, grip|verb
