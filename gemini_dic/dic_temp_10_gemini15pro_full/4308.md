aitchless:/ˈeɪtʃləs/
adjective
无H音的；不发H音的
lacking the sound of the letter 'h'
例句：His aitchless pronunciation made it difficult to understand him. 他不发H音的口音让人很难听懂。

例句：She has an aitchless accent, dropping the 'h' in words like 'house'. 她有不发H音的口音，在“house”这样的单词中不发“h”音。

近义词：non-rhotic (referring specifically to the 'r' sound), unaspirated (referring to the lack of aspiration in a consonant)
反义词：aspirated (referring specifically to the presence of aspiration)
词性:adjective

词源:  A blend of "aitch" (a name for the letter 'h') + "-less" (suffix meaning "without").
