matriculate:/məˈtrɪkjʊleɪt/
verb
入学；注册
to enroll as a member of a college or university.
She matriculated at the University of Oxford. 她就读于牛津大学。
He matriculated at Harvard in the fall. 他在秋季入学哈佛大学。
enroll, register, admit
n/a
v


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin "matriculatus," past participle of "matriculare" "to enroll," from "matricula" "register, roll," diminutive of "matrix" "list, register."
