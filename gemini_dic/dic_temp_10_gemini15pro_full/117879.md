melodramatically:/ˌmelədrəˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 夸张地；戏剧性地；感情过分强烈地 |  In a way that is exaggerated, emotional, or sentimental, like a melodrama. | She clutched her chest and gasped melodramatically. 她捂住胸口，夸张地喘着气。| He melodramatically declared that he would rather die than betray his friends.  他戏剧性地宣称，他宁愿死也不愿背叛朋友。| 近义词： theatrically, dramatically, histrionically, exaggeratedly | 反义词： calmly, subtly, realistically, understatedly | 词性: 副词

词源:
From melodrama + -ically.  Melodrama itself comes from French mélodrame, from Greek melos "song" + French drame "drama."
