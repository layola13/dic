agribusinesses:/ˈæɡrɪˌbɪznɪsɪz/
|n.|农业综合企业|Businesses involved in the production, processing, and distribution of agricultural products.|Many agribusinesses are vertically integrated, controlling every step from farm to table. 许多农业综合企业都是垂直整合的，控制着从农场到餐桌的每一步。|
| |例句：The agribusinesses in this region are facing challenges due to climate change. 该地区的农业综合企业正因气候变化而面临挑战。|
| |例句：Several large agribusinesses dominate the global food market.  几家大型农业综合企业主导着全球食品市场。|
| |近义词：agricultural industries, food companies, farming corporations|
| |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) small farms, family farms|
| |词性: noun (plural)|
