gyre:/dʒaɪər/ | n. | 旋转，环流，螺旋 | A circular or spiral motion or form; a vortex. | The ocean currents move in gyres. 洋流以环流的形式运动。| The gyre of the hawk's flight was mesmerizing. 鹰盘旋飞行的轨迹令人着迷。 | synonym: vortex, swirl, spiral, whirl, eddy | antonym: straight line, linear motion | noun

gyre:/dʒaɪər/ | v. |  旋转，盘旋，环绕 | To move in a circle or spiral; to whirl. | The leaves gyred in the wind. 叶子在风中旋转。 | The smoke gyred upwards from the chimney. 烟从烟囱里盘旋上升。| synonym: whirl, swirl, spin, rotate, revolve | antonym: stop, halt, still | verb
