expressionistic:/ɪkˌspreʃənˈɪstɪk/| adj.|表现主义的；表现派的|Relating to or characteristic of expressionism, an artistic style that seeks to express emotional experience rather than impressions of the external world.|The painting was done in a vividly expressionistic style, full of distorted figures and bold colors. 这幅画是以一种鲜明的表现主义风格完成的，充满了扭曲的人物和大胆的色彩。|Synonyms: intense, passionate, dramatic, evocative|Antonyms: realistic, objective, naturalistic|adjective
