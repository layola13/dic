cygnus:/ˈsɪɡnəs/| n. |天鹅座|A constellation in the northern sky, representing a swan.  It contains the bright star Deneb.|例句：Cygnus is easily recognizable by its cross shape. 天鹅座很容易通过它的十字形状辨认出来。

例句：The constellation Cygnus is also known as the Northern Cross. 天鹅座也被称为北十字星。
