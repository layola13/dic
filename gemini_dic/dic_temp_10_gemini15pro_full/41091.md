coppled:/ˈkɒpəld/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpəld/ (US)| adj.|倾覆的，倒塌的；摇摇欲坠的|Overturned, tumbled over; unstable and likely to collapse.|The coppled statue lay in pieces on the ground.  倾覆的雕像碎成几块躺在地上。|The old barn, weakened by years of neglect, looked coppled and ready to fall.  由于多年失修，这座老谷仓看起来摇摇欲坠，随时可能倒塌。|Near synonyms: overturned, toppled, fallen, unstable, precarious, rickety|Antonyms: upright, stable, secure|Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->

From the verb *copple* (to overturn or cause to fall), which is likely related to Middle English *cupelen* and ultimately of uncertain origin.  Possibly influenced by or related to words suggesting a rounded or humped shape, which might easily topple.
