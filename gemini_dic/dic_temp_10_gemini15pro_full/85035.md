harborage:/ˈhɑːrbərɪdʒ/ | n. | 停泊处，避难所 |A place of shelter or refuge, especially for ships; a harbor. | The small cove offered harborage from the storm. 小海湾提供了躲避风暴的场所。| 近义词: shelter, refuge, haven, asylum | 反义词: exposure, danger | 词性: 名词

harborage:/ˈhɑːrbərɪdʒ/ | n. | 窝藏，庇护 |The act of harboring or sheltering someone or something. | The barn provided harborage for the escaped convicts.  谷仓为逃犯提供了藏身之处。| 近义词: protection, concealment, shelter | 反义词: exposure, betrayal | 词性: 名词


词源:
From Middle English *herberage, borrowed from Anglo-Norman herbergage, from herberger (“to lodge, shelter”), from Frankish *haribergōn (“to shelter an army”), from *hari (“army”) + *bergōn (“to protect, save”). Cognate with Old High German heriberga (“army shelter”), German Herberge (“hostel, inn”). More at here, borough.
