naive:/naɪˈiːv/ /nɑːˈiːv/| adj.|天真的，幼稚的，轻信的|Showing a lack of experience, wisdom, or judgment.|She was very naive to believe he would change. 她很天真，竟然相信他会改变。|Synonyms: innocent, ingenuous, guileless, artless, unsophisticated|Antonyms: sophisticated, worldly, experienced, cynical|adjective
