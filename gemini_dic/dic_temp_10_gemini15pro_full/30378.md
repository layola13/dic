centesimo:/tʃɛnˈteːzɪmoʊ/ /tʃɛnˈtɛzɪmoʊ/
|n.|分，百分之一|A centesimo is a monetary unit equal to one hundredth of a basic monetary unit.  It is used in several countries, particularly those that formerly used currencies based on the Latin Monetary Union.|I found a few centesimi on the ground. 我在地上发现了几分钱。|Synonyms: cent, penny|Antonyms: euro, dollar|Noun

centesimo:/tʃɛnˈteːzɪmoʊ/ /tʃɛnˈtɛzɪmoʊ/
|adj.|百分之一的|Relating to or constituting one hundredth part of a whole.|A centesimo donation is still appreciated. 百分之一的捐赠仍然值得赞赏。|Synonyms: hundredth|Antonyms: whole, entire|Adjective
