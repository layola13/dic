internment:/ɪnˈtɜːnmənt/ (US) /ɪnˈtɜːn.mənt/ (UK)| n. |拘留，扣押，收容|The state of being confined as a prisoner, especially for political or military reasons.|  Japanese Americans faced internment during World War II.  二战期间，日裔美国人面临拘留。|近义词：detention, confinement, imprisonment|反义词：release, liberation|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From French *internement*, from *interner* "to confine, intern," from Late Latin *internāre* "to shut up, confine," from Latin *inter* "within" + *nāre* "to put, place."
