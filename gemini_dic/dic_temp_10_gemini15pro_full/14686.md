awake:/əˈweɪk/ | adj. v. | 醒着的，唤醒 | not asleep; roused from sleep. |  She was awake at 3 a.m. 她凌晨三点就醒了。| synonyms: conscious, alert, aware | antonyms: asleep, unconscious | adjective

awake:/əˈweɪk/ | v. | 唤醒，醒来 | to stop sleeping; to make someone stop sleeping. | The noise awoke me. 噪音把我吵醒了。| synonyms: awaken, rouse, stir | antonyms: sleep, doze | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *awacan "to wake up," from Proto-Germanic *wakjanan, from Proto-Indo-European *weg- "to be lively, awake."
