cross-platform:/ˌkrɔːsˈplætˌfɔːrm/| adj.|跨平台的|Designed to work on multiple operating systems or computer architectures.|This new software is cross-platform, so it can be used on both Windows and Mac computers. 这个新软件是跨平台的，所以它可以在Windows和Mac电脑上使用。|Synonyms: multi-platform, platform-independent|Antonyms: platform-specific, platform-dependent|adjective

cross-platform:/ˌkrɔːsˈplætˌfɔːrm/| n.|跨平台|The ability of a software application to run on different operating systems or computer architectures.|Cross-platform development is becoming increasingly important in today's market. 跨平台开发在当今市场变得越来越重要。|Synonyms: multi-platform compatibility, platform independence|Antonyms: platform dependence, platform specificity|noun
