misintelligence:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns/ | n. |错误情报，失实情报|Incorrect or misleading information, often due to faulty analysis, misinterpretation, or deliberate deception. |The misintelligence led to a disastrous military decision. 错误情报导致了灾难性的军事决策。| The campaign was hampered by misintelligence about the opponent's strength.  这场运动因对对手实力的错误情报而受阻。 |Near synonyms: misinformation, disinformation, false intelligence, bad intel |Antonyms: accurate intelligence, good intel |noun
