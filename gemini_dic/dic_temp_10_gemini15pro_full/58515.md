dues:/djuːz/
noun
会员费，应付款
fees or charges that someone owes to an organization
例句：He failed to pay his club dues. 他没有缴纳俱乐部会费。
例句：Union dues are deducted from the workers' paychecks. 工会会费从工人的工资中扣除。
近义词：fees, charges, subscriptions
反义词: credits, refunds
词性:n.


dues:/djuːz/
noun
应得之物，权利
something that someone deserves or is entitled to
例句：She finally received her dues after years of hard work.  经过多年的努力，她终于得到了她应得的。
例句：He felt he hadn't received his dues from the company. 他觉得他没有从公司得到他应得的。
近义词：rights, entitlements, deserts
反义词: penalties, forfeitures
词性:n.
