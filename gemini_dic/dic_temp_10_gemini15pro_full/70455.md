figured:/ˈfɪɡərd/ | adj. |  有图案的；计算出的；预料到的；(身材)匀称的 | Having a pattern or design; calculated; expected; (of a person's body) well-proportioned |  She wore a figured silk dress. 她穿着一件有图案的丝绸连衣裙。 | patterned, decorated, ornamented | plain, simple | adjective

figured:/ˈfɪɡərd/ | v. |  计算；认为；料想；(在…中)起重要作用  | Calculated; thought; supposed; played an important role (in…) | I figured it would rain. 我料想会下雨. | calculated, computed, estimated, believed, assumed | ignored, disregarded | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *figuren, from Old French figurer, from Latin figūrāre (“to form, shape, represent”), from figūra (“shape, form, figure”).
