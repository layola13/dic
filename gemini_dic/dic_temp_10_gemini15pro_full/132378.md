nondeluded:/ˌnɒndɪˈluːdɪd/
adjective
清醒的，不妄想的
Not suffering from or characterized by delusions; sane.
She had a nondeluded view of her own limitations. 她对自己的局限性有着清醒的认识。

近义词:sane, rational, lucid, clear-headed, realistic
反义词:deluded, irrational, insane, unrealistic, fantastical
词性:adjective
