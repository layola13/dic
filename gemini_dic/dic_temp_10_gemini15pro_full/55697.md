diureticalness:/ˌdaɪjʊˈrɛtɪkəlnɛs/ | n. | 利尿性，排尿性 | The diureticalness of this medication helps reduce water retention.  这种药物的利尿性有助于减少水分滞留。| This herb is known for its diureticalness. 这种草药以其利尿性而闻名。 | 近义词: diuretic property, diuretic effect | 反义词: antidiuretic property | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From diuretic + -al + -ness.  "Diuretic" comes from  the Greek "diouretikos" meaning "promoting urine," from "dia-" (through) + "ourein" (to urinate). "-al" forms an adjective, and "-ness" forms a noun indicating a state or quality.
