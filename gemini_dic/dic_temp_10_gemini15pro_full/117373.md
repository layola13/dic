mediatization:/ˌmiːdiətaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 媒介化 | The process by which media become increasingly influential in and integrated with various aspects of society and culture. |  The increasing mediatization of politics has led to a focus on image over substance. 政治日益媒介化导致人们注重形象而非实质。

The mediatization of religion has resulted in televised sermons and online worship services. 宗教的媒介化导致了电视布道和在线礼拜服务的出现。 | 近义词：media influence, media saturation | 反义词：  (No direct antonym, consider "de-mediatization" as a conceptual opposite) | 词性：noun
