innervate:/ɪˈnɜːveɪt/
verb
支配；使受神经支配；激励；赋予活力
to supply with nerves; to stimulate to action
例句：The nerves that innervate the skin of the arm originate in the spinal cord. 支配手臂皮肤的神经起源于脊髓。

例句：The inspiring speech innervated the crowd to take action. 这场鼓舞人心的演讲激励了人群采取行动。
近义词：stimulate, energize, activate, invigorate
反义词：enervate, weaken, debilitate
词性:v.
