词源：
From outdated + -ness.  Outdated itself comes from out- + dated.  Date in this context means "to mark with a date," and by extension, "to assign to a particular period."  The prefix out- implies something is beyond or no longer within a relevant period.  The suffix -ness forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
