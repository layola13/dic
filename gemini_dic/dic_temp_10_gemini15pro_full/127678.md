Nagapatnam (formerly Negapatam):/ˌnɑːɡəpəˈtɪnəm/| n.|纳加帕蒂南（旧称内加帕坦）|A city and port in Tamil Nadu, India, on the Coromandel Coast.  The name is derived from Tamil and means "City of the Snake King". The port has a long history, dating back to the Chola dynasty. |The Dutch East India Company established a trading post in Negapatam in the 17th century.  Nagapatnam was significantly impacted by the 2004 Indian Ocean tsunami.|近义词：port city, coastal town|反义词: inland city
