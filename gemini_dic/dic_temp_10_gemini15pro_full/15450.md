bada:/ˈbɑːdə/| adj. n.|坏的，糟糕的; 坏蛋，恶棍|Bad can describe something unpleasant, of poor quality, or morally wrong. It can also be used as a noun to refer to a villain or troublemaker.  It originates from the Old English word 'bædling' meaning 'effeminate man'. Over time, its meaning broadened to include various negative connotations. |The food was really bad. (食物真的很糟糕。) He's a bad influence on his friends. (他对他的朋友有不良影响。)  The movie's villain was a real bada. (这部电影的反派是个十足的坏蛋。)|近义词：awful, terrible, evil, wicked, villain, scoundrel|反义词:good, excellent, virtuous, hero|词性:adjective, noun
