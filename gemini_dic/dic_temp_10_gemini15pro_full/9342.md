antipriesthood:/ˌæntiˈpriːsthʊd/
|n.|反僧侣制度；反神权主义|Opposition to the priesthood or priestly power; the principle of opposing the priesthood.|The rise of antipriesthood sentiment led to the reformation. 反僧侣情绪的兴起导致了宗教改革。|The book explored the historical roots of antipriesthood. 这本书探讨了反神权主义的历史根源。|近义词: anticlericalism|反义词: pro-clericalism|词性: 名词

Etymology:
anti- (Greek) "against, opposite of" + priesthood (Old English preosthad "state of being a priest")
