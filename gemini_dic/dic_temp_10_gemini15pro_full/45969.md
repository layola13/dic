cyanic: /saɪˈænɪk/ | adj. | 氰的，含氰的 | Containing or relating to cyanide. | Potassium cyanate is a cyanic salt. 氰酸钾是一种氰酸盐。|  Example: Cyanic acid is a highly toxic compound. 氰酸是一种剧毒化合物。 | Synonyms: cyanide-containing | Antonyms: non-cyanic | adjective


cyanic: /saɪˈænɪk/ | n. | 氰酸 | A colorless, volatile, poisonous liquid acid, HOCN.  | Cyanic acid is unstable. 氰酸不稳定。 | Example: The reaction produced a small amount of cyanic acid. 反应产生少量氰酸。 | Synonyms: isocyanic acid | Antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cyanique*, from Greek *kuanos* (dark blue), because of the blue color of Prussian blue, a cyanide-containing pigment.
