henges:/ˈhendʒɪz/| n. |巨石阵（henge的复数形式）| A prehistoric monument consisting of a circle of large upright stones, typically surrounding a smaller circle or horseshoe of stones.  It refers specifically to sites like Stonehenge, and similar structures.|例句：The henges of prehistoric Britain are fascinating. 史前英国的巨石阵令人着迷。

例句：Researchers are still studying the purpose of these ancient henges.  研究人员仍在研究这些古代巨石阵的用途。


|近义词：megaliths, monuments|反义词: (No direct antonym.  One could consider "modern structures" a loose antonym.)|词性:noun
