levo:/ˈliːvoʊ/| adj.|左旋的，左边的|Relating to or being the isomer of an optically active compound that rotates the plane of polarized light to the left, counterclockwise as viewed by an observer facing the incoming light.|Levo-dopa is a medication used to treat Parkinson's disease. 左旋多巴是一种用于治疗帕金森病的药物。|Example: The levo isomer is more effective than the dextro isomer. 左旋异构体比右旋异构体更有效。|Synonym: left, sinistral|Antonym: dextro, right|adjective
