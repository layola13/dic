niacin:/ˈnaɪəsɪn/ | n. | 烟酸，尼克酸，维生素B3 | A specific form of vitamin B3, important for the body's metabolism. | Example 1:  He takes niacin supplements to help lower his cholesterol. 他服用烟酸补充剂来帮助降低胆固醇。

Example 2: Niacin deficiency can lead to pellagra.  缺乏烟酸会导致糙皮病。 | Synonym: nicotinic acid, vitamin B3 | Antonym: None (Vitamins don't typically have antonyms) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From nicotinic acid, shortened to niacin to avoid confusion with nicotine.  "Nicotinic" refers to nicotine, from which nicotinic acid can be derived, and "acid" refers to its chemical properties. The "ni-" part was retained, and "-acin" was added as a general suffix for vitamins.
