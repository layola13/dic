equivalvular:/ˌiːkwɪˈvælvjʊlər/
adjective
等瓣的；同瓣的
Having valves of equal size or of similar form and function.
例句：The equivalvular clam shells were perfectly symmetrical. 这只等瓣蛤蜊的壳完全对称。

例句：Equivalvular bivalves are common in shallow marine environments. 等瓣双壳类在浅海环境中很常见。

近义词: isovalvular, equibivalved
反义词: inequivalve, inequivalvular
词性: adjective


词源:
From equi- (equal) + valve + -ular (forming adjectives).  The prefix "equi-" comes from Latin "aequus" meaning "equal," and "valve" also comes from Latin "valva" meaning "leaf of a folding door," which was later used to describe the shell parts of mollusks.
