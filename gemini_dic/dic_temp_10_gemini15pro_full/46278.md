czaristic:/zɑːrˈɪstɪk/
adjective
沙皇的；沙皇制度的；专横的
Of or relating to a czar; characteristic of a czar or czarist Russia.  Also, autocratic or domineering.
The czaristic regime was overthrown in 1917.  沙皇政权在1917年被推翻。
The CEO's czaristic management style alienated many employees.  这位CEO专横的管理风格疏远了许多员工。
近义词:imperial, autocratic, dictatorial, tyrannical
反义词:democratic, egalitarian
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From czar + -istic.  Czar ultimately derives from Caesar, the title of Roman emperors.
