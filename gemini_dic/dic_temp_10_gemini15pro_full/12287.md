ascidian: /əˈsɪdiən/ | n. | 海鞘 | A marine invertebrate animal of the subphylum Urochordata, enclosed in a tough outer covering.  Ascidians are filter feeders, drawing water in through one siphon and expelling it through another. | The ascidian clung to the rock, filtering plankton from the water.  海鞘 clinging在岩石上，从水中过滤浮游生物。 | synonyms: sea squirt, tunicate | antonyms: N/A | noun

ascidian: /əˈsɪdiən/ | adj. | 海鞘的 | Relating to or characteristic of ascidians. |  The ascidian larva is free-swimming. 海鞘幼虫是自由游动的。 | synonyms: N/A | antonyms: N/A | adjective
