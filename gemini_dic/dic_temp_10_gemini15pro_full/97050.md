infeoffment:/ɪnˈfiːfmənt/ |n.|封地；授予封地；封地证书|The act of granting land in fee, especially in feudal times.  The legal conveyance of land or property. |The king granted him infeoffment of the entire valley. 国王将整个山谷的封地授予他。|近义词：enfeoffment, investiture, endowment|反义词：divestiture, deprivation|noun
