dative: /ˈdeɪtɪv/ | adj. n. | 与格的，与格 | relating to or denoting a case of nouns (typically marking the indirect object of a verb), or a word in that case.  A noun or pronoun in the dative case. |  She gave him the book. (indirect object 'him' is in the dative case conceptually, though not marked distinctly in English)  / The dative case is less common in modern English. | Near synonyms: indirect object | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a grammatical case) | noun, adjective
