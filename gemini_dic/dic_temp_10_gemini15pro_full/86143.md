hearties:/ˈhɑːrtiz/| n.|伙伴，朋友们，同志们|A group of loyal and enthusiastic companions, especially sailors.  Originally used as a term of address to a group of sailors.|Ahoy, hearties! Let's set sail!  喂，伙伴们！我们启航吧！|近义词：friends, comrades, mates, pals|反义词:enemies, foes|Noun
