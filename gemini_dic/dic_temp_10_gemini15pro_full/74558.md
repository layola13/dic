fraudsters:/ˈfrɔːdstərz/
noun
诈骗犯，骗子
A person who commits fraud; a swindler.
The fraudsters were caught and sentenced to prison. 诈骗犯被抓获并被判处监禁。
The online auction site is trying to crack down on fraudsters. 该在线拍卖网站正在努力打击诈骗者。
synonyms: swindlers, cheats, con artists, scammers, impostors
antonyms: honest people, law-abiding citizens
