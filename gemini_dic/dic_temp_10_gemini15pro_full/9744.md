antiwear:/ˌæntiˈwɛər/| adj.|抗磨的，耐磨的|Preventing or reducing wear, especially in machinery.  Designed to resist or minimize wear and tear.|This engine oil has excellent antiwear properties.  这种机油具有优异的抗磨性能。|Synonyms: wear-resistant, abrasion-resistant|Antonyms: abrasive|adjective

antiwear:/ˌæntiˈwɛər/| n.|抗磨添加剂|A substance added to lubricants to reduce wear.  A substance that prevents or reduces wear, especially in machinery.|The antiwear in this grease helps protect moving parts.  这种润滑脂中的抗磨添加剂有助于保护运动部件。|Synonyms: wear inhibitor, anti-friction agent|Antonyms: abrasive|noun
