gunrunners:/ˈɡʌnˌrʌnərz/
n.
军火走私犯，武器贩子
People who illegally smuggle or sell guns.
The gunrunners were arrested at the border.  这些军火走私犯在边境被逮捕了。
Synonyms: arms traffickers, weapons smugglers
Antonyms: law enforcers


Etymology:
gun + runner
gun (n.) Old English *gunna, a woman's name, later the name of a large siege engine, perhaps from Gunnhildr, a Scandinavian female personal name.
runner (n.) from run + -er.  run (v.) Old English rinnan "to flow, melt; to run, go quickly,"
