exilic:/ˈeksɪlɪk/ /ɛɡˈzɪlɪk/
adjective
流亡的；放逐的；与流放有关的
Of or relating to exile; characterized by exile.
The exilic community maintained their cultural traditions.  流亡社群保持着他们的文化传统。
The poet's exilic experience profoundly influenced his work.  诗人的流放经历深刻地影响了他的作品。
近义词:banished, exiled, displaced
反义词:native, indigenous, repatriated
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *exilicus, from Latin exilium (“exile”).
