haste:/heɪst/| n. v.|急忙，匆忙；催促|hurry; accelerate; rush.  A state of urgency or rush. As a verb, it means to hurry or rush something.|例句：He made haste to finish the project. 他急忙完成这个项目。|近义词：hurry, rush, speed, expedite|反义词:delay, slowness, procrastination|词性:名词, 动词

hasted:/ˈheɪstɪd/| v.|急忙，匆忙(haste的过去式和过去分词)|past tense and past participle of "haste"; hurried; rushed.|例句：They hasted to the airport to catch their flight. 他们匆忙赶往机场赶飞机。|近义词：hurried, rushed, sped, expedited|反义词:delayed, slowed, procrastinated|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *hasten, from Old French haster, from Frankish *haifstijan (“to urge on, incite”), from Proto-Germanic *haifstijaną (“to hasten”), from *haifsts (“violent”). Cognate with Old High German heistijan, heistid (“vehement”). More at hasty.
