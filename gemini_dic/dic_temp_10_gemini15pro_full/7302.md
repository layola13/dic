sanguine:/ˈsæŋɡwɪn/| adj.|乐观的，充满希望的；面色红润的|Optimistic or positive, especially in an apparently bad or difficult situation.  Also, having a healthy reddish color.|例句：He remained sanguine about the prospects for peace. 他对和平的前景依然乐观。|例句：Her sanguine complexion radiated health. 她红润的脸色焕发着健康的光彩。|近义词：optimistic, cheerful, confident, hopeful, rosy, bullish|反义词：pessimistic, gloomy, despondent, despairing, pallid|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: from Old French sanguin, from Latin sanguineus ‘of blood’, from sanguis, sanguin- ‘blood’.  The original sense ‘blood-red’ gave rise to ‘ruddy’ (of complexion), hence ‘cheerful’ (late 16th century).
