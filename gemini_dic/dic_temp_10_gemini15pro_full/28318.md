caracol:/kɑːrəˈkɒl/ (UK), /kɑːrəˈkoʊl/ (US)| n. |蜗牛; 海螺; 螺旋形楼梯|A terrestrial mollusk with a spiral shell.  A marine mollusk with a spiral shell. A spiral staircase.|例句：The snail, or caracol, left a silvery trail across the patio.  那只蜗牛，或者说caracol，在露台上留下了一条银色的痕迹。

例句：She found a beautiful caracol shell on the beach. 她在海滩上发现了一个漂亮的海螺壳。|近义词：snail, conch, spiral staircase, helix|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *caracol*, from Old Spanish *caracol*, from Vulgar Latin *cochleolus*, diminutive of Latin *cochlea* (“snail, screw”), related to Greek *κόχλος* (kókhlos, “spiral shell”).
