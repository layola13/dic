chanceries: /ˈtʃɑːnsərɪz/ /ˈtʃænsərɪz/
n.
法院, 大法官法庭，档案馆
A court of equity, as distinguished from a common-law court.  The office or department of a chancellor. A record office for public archives or other important documents.
The case was heard in the chanceries. 这个案件在大法官法庭审理。
The chanceries held records dating back centuries. 档案馆保存着几个世纪以前的记录。
Synonyms: court, tribunal, office, department, archives
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but consider) street, field, outdoors
词性: noun
