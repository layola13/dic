overhaul:/ˌoʊvərˈhɔːl/
|n.|彻底检修；大修|A thorough examination and repair of a machine or system.|The car needs a complete overhaul. 这辆车需要彻底检修。|
|vt.|彻底检修；大修；改造|To examine and repair thoroughly; to renovate or transform completely.|The government plans to overhaul the education system. 政府计划彻底改革教育系统。|
|近义词：renovate, repair, restore, improve|
|反义词：neglect, damage|
|词性：名词，动词|


词源：
From "overhale," meaning to haul or pull over, later evolving to mean a thorough examination and repair.  The word originates from the early 16th century.
