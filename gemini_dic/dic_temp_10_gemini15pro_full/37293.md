commandingly:/kəˈmɑːndɪŋli/
adverb
威严地，命令式地
In a manner that evokes obedience or respect; authoritatively.
The officer spoke commandingly to the troops.  这位军官威严地对部队讲话。
She commandingly directed the team to begin the project. 她命令式地指示团队开始这个项目。
近义词: authoritatively, imperiously, assertively, decisively
反义词: meekly, submissively, timidly, hesitantly
词性: adverb

词源:
From the adjective "commanding" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Commanding" comes from the verb "command," which has roots in the Old French "comander" and ultimately the Latin "commendare" (to entrust, recommend).
