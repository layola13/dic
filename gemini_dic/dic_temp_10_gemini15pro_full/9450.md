antireflection:/ˌæntiｒɪˈflɛkʃən/ |adj.| 抗反射的，防反射的 | Reducing or preventing reflection, especially of light. |  The camera lens has an antireflection coating. 相机镜头有抗反射涂层。| synonyms: nonreflective, non-reflecting | antonyms: reflective | adjective

antireflection:/ˌæntiｒɪˈflɛkʃən/ |n.| 抗反射涂层，防反光措施 | A coating or other measure that reduces or prevents reflection. | The antireflection on my glasses really helps when I'm driving at night. 我眼镜上的抗反射涂层在夜间开车时真的很有帮助。| synonyms: antireflective coating, anti-glare coating | antonyms: reflective coating | noun


ETYMOLOGY->

reflection (noun): the act of reflecting, something reflected (from Latin "reflectere" - to bend back)
