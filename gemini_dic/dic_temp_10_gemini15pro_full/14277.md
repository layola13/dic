autographic:/ˌɔːtəˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. |亲笔的；自传的|Relating to or produced by one's own handwriting; autobiographical.  A document written entirely in the author's own hand. |Example:  The museum displayed an autographic manuscript of the famous scientist.  博物馆展出了这位著名科学家的亲笔手稿。

Example:  Her autographic letter revealed her true feelings.  她的亲笔信揭示了她的真实感受。 | 近义词: handwritten, holograph |反义词: typed, printed | 词性: adjective

autograph:/ˈɔːtəɡrɑːf/ | n. |亲笔签名；手稿|A signature, especially that of a celebrity written as a memento for an admirer. A manuscript in the author's own handwriting.  |Example: Could I have your autograph, please?  请问可以给我你的签名吗？

Example: The auction house sold a rare autograph of the poet. 拍卖行出售了这位诗人的珍贵手稿。| 近义词: signature, handwriting |反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以用copy, reproduction表示非原件) | 词性: noun
