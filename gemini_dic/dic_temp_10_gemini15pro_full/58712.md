dunam:/ˈduːnɑːm/ /ˈduːnəm/
n.
度南（土地面积单位）
A unit of land area equal to 1,000 square meters (approximately 0.25 acre).  It is often used in Israel and other parts of the Middle East.
The farm covers an area of 50 dunams.  这个农场占地50度南。

近义词:acre, hectare
反义词:(No direct antonym for a unit of measurement)
词性:noun
