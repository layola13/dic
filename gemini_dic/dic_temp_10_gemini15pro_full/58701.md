dumpling:/ˈdʌmplɪŋ/| n.|饺子；汤圆；团子|A small ball of dough (= flour and water mixed together) cooked by boiling or steaming, sometimes containing meat or fruit.|例句：She made delicious pork dumplings for dinner. 她晚饭做了美味的猪肉饺子。

例句：I love chicken and vegetable dumplings. 我喜欢鸡肉蔬菜馅的饺子。|近义词：potsticker, ravioli, pierogi|反义词: None|词性: noun
