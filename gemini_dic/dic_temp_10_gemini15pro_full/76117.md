furthersome:/ˈfɜːðərsəm/
adjective
推进的；促进的；有帮助的
Tending to further or promote something; helpful.
His furthersome actions helped the project move forward quickly. 他促进性的行动帮助项目快速推进。
Her furthersome advice proved invaluable to the team. 她有益的建议对团队来说非常宝贵。
近义词: helpful, beneficial, advantageous, conducive, promoting
反义词: hindering, obstructive, detrimental, counterproductive
词性: adjective

词源:
From "further" (verb, meaning to advance or promote) + "-some" (suffix, meaning tending to or causing).  "Further" itself comes from Old English "furthor," comparative of "fore" (before).
