entrepot:/ˈɑːntrəpoʊ/ (US) /ˈɒntrəpəʊ/ (UK)| n. |转口港，贸易中心，仓库|A port, city, or other center to which goods are brought for import and export, and for collection and distribution.|Hong Kong has long been a major entrepot for trade between East and Asia.|Synonyms: trading post, distribution center, warehouse, depot|Antonyms: backwater, isolated area|Noun
