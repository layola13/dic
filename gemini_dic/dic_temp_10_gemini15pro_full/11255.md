archtreasurer: /ˌɑːrtʃˈtrɛʒərər/ | n. | 大司库，财务主管 | A chief treasurer, especially of a cathedral, monastery, or other religious institution. |  The archtreasurer oversaw the abbey's vast holdings and endowments. 大司库监管修道院的庞大财产和捐赠。 | Synonyms: treasurer, cashier, bursar | Antonyms: debtor, borrower | noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From Old French *archetresorier*, from *arche-* (chief) + *tresorier* (treasurer), which ultimately comes from Latin *thesaurius* (treasure).
