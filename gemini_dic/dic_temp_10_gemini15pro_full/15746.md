balderdash:/ˈbɔːldərdæʃ/| n. |胡说八道；废话；无稽之谈|Nonsense; senseless talk or writing.|He dismissed their claims as utter balderdash. 他认为他们的说法完全是胡说八道。|近义词：nonsense, rubbish, gibberish, twaddle|反义词：sense, truth|词性:名词

Etymology:
From the 16th century. Origin uncertain; possibly a blend of words suggesting meaningless, noisy talk.  Some speculate a connection to "bald" (meaning empty or worthless) and "dash" (implying something thrown together carelessly).
