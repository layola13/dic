colloidal:/kəˈlɔɪdəl/| adj.|胶体的，胶质的|Relating to or of the nature of a colloid; a substance microscopically dispersed evenly throughout another substance.|例句：Milk is a colloidal solution. 牛奶是一种胶体溶液。|例句：The paint has a colloidal base. 这油漆以胶体为基料。|近义词：gelatinous, viscous|反义词：crystalline, solution|词性:形容词
Etymology: From colloid + -al.  "Colloid" comes from the Greek kólla (glue) + -eidos (form, shape).
