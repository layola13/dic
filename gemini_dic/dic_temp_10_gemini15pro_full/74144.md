foundered:/ˈfaʊndərd/ (US) /ˈfaʊndəd/ (UK)| v. |沉没；失败；垮掉；(马等)蹄叶炎|To sink below the surface of the water; to fail or collapse completely; (of a horse, etc.) to suffer from laminitis.|The ship foundered in a storm. 这艘船在暴风雨中沉没了。|Synonyms: sink, fail, collapse, stumble|Antonyms: succeed, thrive, float|verb

foundered:/ˈfaʊndərd/ (US) /ˈfaʊndəd/ (UK)| adj. |(马等)患蹄叶炎的|Suffering from laminitis.|The foundered horse was in great pain. 患有蹄叶炎的马非常痛苦。|Synonyms: lame|Antonyms: sound, healthy|adjective
