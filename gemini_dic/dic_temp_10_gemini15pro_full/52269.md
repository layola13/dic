dicer:/ˈdaɪsər/| n.|切丁器; 骰子;|A kitchen tool used to cut food into small cubes.  A person who cuts things into dice. |She used a dicer to chop the vegetables for the soup. 她用切丁器把蔬菜切成丁，准备做汤。|The experienced chef diced the onions with impressive speed.  经验丰富的厨师以惊人的速度将洋葱切成丁。|近义词：chopper, mincer|反义词: N/A|名词
