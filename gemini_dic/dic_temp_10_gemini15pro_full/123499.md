monotheisms: /ˈmɒnəʊθiːɪzəmz/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnəθiːɪzəmz/ (US)
|n.|一神教，一神论|The belief in a single god.|例句：The three major monotheisms are Judaism, Christianity, and Islam.  三大一神教是犹太教、基督教和伊斯兰教。
|例句：Many scholars study the origins of monotheisms. 许多学者研究一神教的起源。|近义词：monotheistic religions|反义词: polytheisms, paganism
|词性: 名词(plural)
