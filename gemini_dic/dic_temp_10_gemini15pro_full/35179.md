cloyless:/ˈklɔɪləs/| adj.|不甜腻的，不会生腻的，不令人厌倦的|Not cloying; not overly sweet, rich, or sentimental; not causing disgust or boredom through excess.|Her cloyless prose kept me engaged until the very end. 她清新自然的散文让我一直读到最后。|近义词:agreeable, pleasant, refreshing, appetizing|反义词:cloying, sickening, nauseating, tedious|词性:形容词

词源:
From cloy + -less.  Cloy comes from Old French encloyer "to nail up, plug, block, stop up," from en- "in" + cloer "to nail, close," from Latin claudere "to close." The original sense was of clogging or satiating.
