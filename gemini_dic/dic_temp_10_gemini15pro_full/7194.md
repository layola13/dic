angiospermous:/ˌændʒiəˈspɜːməs/| adj.|被子植物的|Relating to or denoting a plant having its seeds enclosed in an ovary; a flowering plant.|例句：Roses are angiospermous plants. 玫瑰是被子植物。|近义词：flowering|反义词：gymnospermous|词性：形容词
Etymology: From Ancient Greek ἀγγεῖον (angeîon, “vessel, receptacle”) + σπέρμα (spérma, “seed”)
