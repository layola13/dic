magnetist:/ˈmæɡnətɪst/
n.
磁疗师；磁学研究者；被磁吸引的人
A person who studies magnetism or believes in the healing power of magnets.
例句：The magnetist claimed he could cure her arthritis with magnets.  磁疗师声称他可以用磁铁治好她的关节炎。

例句：He was a renowned magnetist in the 19th century, contributing significantly to the understanding of electromagnetism. 他是19世纪著名的磁学家，对电磁学的理解做出了重大贡献。

近义词：magnetic healer,  magnetizer, physicist (if studying magnetism scientifically)
反义词:  (No direct antonym exists.  One might consider a "skeptic" regarding magnetic healing.)
