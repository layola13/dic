obsessing:/əbˈsɛsɪŋ/
v.
痴迷于，念念不忘
Being excessively preoccupied with someone or something.
He's been obsessing over the same problem for weeks.  他几周来一直对同一个问题耿耿于怀。
I can't stop obsessing about the mistake I made. 我无法停止对犯的错误耿耿于怀。
近义词: fixating, dwelling, brooding
反义词: ignoring, neglecting
词性: 动词 (Present Participle of obsess)
词源: From Late Latin obsessus, past participle of obsidere "to besiege," from ob- "against" + sedere "to sit."  The original sense was "besieged by an evil spirit," later generalized to "preoccupy."
