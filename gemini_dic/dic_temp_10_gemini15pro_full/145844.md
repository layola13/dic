overdemandingly: /ˌoʊvərˌdɪˈmændɪŋli/ | adv. | 过分苛求地，过分要求地 | in a way that demands too much from someone |  She pushed her employees overdemandingly, leading to high turnover. 她对员工的要求过高，导致了高离职率。| The coach pushed the team overdemandingly, resulting in several injuries. 教练对队员的要求过高，导致多人受伤。| 近义词: excessively, unreasonably, exorbitantly | 反义词: reasonably, moderately, leniently | 词性: 副词

词源: over- (prefix, meaning "excessively") + demanding (adjective, from demand (verb), from Old French demander, from Latin demandare) + -ly (adverb suffix)
