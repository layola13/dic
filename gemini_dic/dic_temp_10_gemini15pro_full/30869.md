chaetopod: /ˈkiːtəpɒd/ (US) /ˈkiːtəpɒd/ (UK)| n. |环节动物|A segmented worm of the phylum Annelida, such as an earthworm or a leech.|Earthworms are common chaetopods found in gardens. 蚯蚓是花园里常见的环节动物。| Annelid, segmented worm | invertebrate | noun
Etymology: From Greek khaitē (hair) + pous (foot)
