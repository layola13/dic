delegitimize:/ˌdiːləˈdʒɪtɪmaɪz/ /ˌdiːləˈdʒɪtəˌmaɪz/
verb
使失去合法性；使不合法；剥夺合法地位
To take away the legitimacy of; to make something appear not to be legitimate; to deprive of legal status or authority.
The propaganda campaign sought to delegitimize the government. 这场宣传运动试图使政府失去合法性。
synonyms:invalidate, undermine, discredit, deauthorize
antonyms:legitimize, authorize, validate, empower
词性: 动词(verb)

delegitimize:/ˌdiːləˈdʒɪtɪmaɪz/ /ˌdiːləˈdʒɪtəˌmaɪz/
verb
使…不被接受；使…被认为不合理
To make something seem unacceptable or unreasonable.
The opposition party tried to delegitimize the president's policies. 反对党试图使总统的政策不被接受。
synonyms:discredit, disparage, denigrate, vilify
antonyms:legitimize, validate, support, endorse
词性: 动词(verb)

词源:
From de- (meaning "remove" or "reverse") + legitimize (from Latin legitimus "lawful").
