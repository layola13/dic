imperfectly:/ɪmˈpɜːrfɪktli/ | adv. | 不完美地，有缺陷地 | Not perfectly; in a flawed or incomplete manner.  |  The drawing was imperfectly executed, with several noticeable errors. 这幅画画得不太完美，有几个明显的错误。 |  The musician played the piece imperfectly, hitting a few wrong notes.  这位音乐家演奏得不太完美，弹错了几个音符。 | 近义词: faultily, defectively, inadequately | 反义词: perfectly, flawlessly, impeccably | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From im- "not" + perfectly.  "Perfectly" comes from Old French "parfaitement," ultimately from Latin "perfectus" (past participle of "perficere" - "to complete").
