martius:/ˈmɑːrtiəs/ /ˈmɑːrʃəs/| adj. n.|三月, 三月的, 战神玛尔斯的|Of or relating to March; dedicated to Mars, the Roman god of war.  Originally the first month of the Roman calendar.|The ides of Martius were marked by several religious observances. 三月十五日有一些宗教仪式。|Near synonyms: March, martial|Antonyms: None|adjective, noun
