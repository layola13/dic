futilize: /ˈfjuːtɪlaɪz/
verb
使无效；使无用
to make futile; to render useless or ineffective.
The government's efforts to control inflation were futilized by the rising cost of oil. 政府控制通货膨胀的努力因石油价格上涨而失效了。
The heavy rain futilized our plans for a picnic.  大雨使我们的野餐计划泡汤了。
Synonyms: frustrate, thwart, foil, defeat, neutralize
Antonyms: facilitate, aid, assist, support
词性: 动词
