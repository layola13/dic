lure:/lʊr/ (US), /ljʊə(r)/ (UK)| n. v. |诱惑；诱饵；吸引力|Something that tempts or attracts, especially with the promise of pleasure or reward.  A device used for catching fish, typically consisting of a hook, line, and bait.|
The fisherman used a brightly colored lure to attract the trout. 渔夫用颜色鲜艳的诱饵来吸引鳟鱼。

She was lured to the city by the promise of a better life.  她被更美好生活的承诺吸引到这座城市。

近义词: entice, tempt, attract, allure, bait, decoy
反义词: repel, deter, discourage
词性: noun, verb


lure:/lʊr/ (US), /ljʊə(r)/ (UK)| v. |引诱；诱惑|To tempt or attract with the promise of pleasure or reward.|
The company tried to lure customers with discounts and special offers.  公司试图用折扣和特价来吸引顾客。

The smell of freshly baked bread lured me into the bakery.  刚出炉面包的香味把我吸引进了面包店。

近义词: entice, tempt, attract, allure, seduce
反义词: repel, deter, discourage
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *luerre "bait, decoy," of uncertain origin. Possibly related to a Germanic source meaning "to deceive."
