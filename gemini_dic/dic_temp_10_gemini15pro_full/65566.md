euxenite:/juːˈzɛnaɪt/
noun
 euxenite, euxinite
A brownish-black mineral containing rare earth elements.
Euxenite is a complex oxide containing niobium, titanium, yttrium, and other rare earth elements.
Euxenite is found in granite pegmatites.
近义词:polycrase, priorite
反义词: None (Euxenite is a specific mineral; there is no direct opposite.)
词性:noun
