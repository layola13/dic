glockenspiel:/ˈɡlɒkənʃpiːl/ (US) /ˈɡlɒkənʃpiːl/ (UK)| n. |钟琴|A percussion instrument consisting of a set of tuned metal bars arranged in the fashion of a keyboard, played with small hammers.|The glockenspiel added a bright, shimmering tone to the orchestra.  钟琴为管弦乐队增添了明亮闪烁的音调。|Synonyms: metallophone, bell lyre, carillon|Antonyms: None|noun
