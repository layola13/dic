bezpopovtsy:/bɛspɐˈpɔftsɪ/ | n. |无神父派| Members of a sect of Old Believers who rejected the priesthood after the reforms of Patriarch Nikon in the 17th century.  They believed that the true church and priesthood had ceased to exist after the reforms. |The bezpopovtsy faced persecution for their beliefs. 无神父派因其信仰而面临迫害。| The bezpopovtsy community maintained their traditions in secret. 无神父派社群秘密地保持着他们的传统。|Synonyms: Old Believers (in a broader sense), priestless Old Believers|Antonyms: Popovtsy (priestly Old Believers)|noun
