irresoluteness:/ɪˈrezəluːtnəs/ | n. |优柔寡断，犹豫不决 |The state of being irresolute; inability to decide or take a firm course of action. |His irresoluteness cost him the opportunity. 他的优柔寡断让他错失了良机。|Near Synonyms: indecision, vacillation, hesitancy, wavering |Antonyms: decisiveness, determination, resolve |noun

irresolute:/ɪˈrezəluːt/ | adj. |优柔寡断的，犹豫不决的 |Showing or feeling hesitancy in acting or speaking; unable to decide; uncertain. |He was irresolute about what to do next. 他对下一步该做什么犹豫不决。|Near Synonyms: hesitant, indecisive, wavering, vacillating |Antonyms: decisive, determined, resolute |adjective
