de-democratizing:/diːˌdɪˈmɒkrətaɪzɪŋ/
Verb
去民主化
The act of weakening or undermining democratic institutions, processes, or principles.
The military coup led to the de-democratizing of the country.  军事政变导致了该国去民主化。
The spread of disinformation is de-democratizing public discourse.  虚假信息的传播正在损害公众讨论的民主性。
erosion of democracy, weakening of democratic institutions, undermining of democratic principles
strengthening of democracy, promoting democracy, democratization
