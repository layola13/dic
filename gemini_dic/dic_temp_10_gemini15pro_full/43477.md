例句：The crassness of the comedian's jokes offended many in the audience.  这位喜剧演员的笑话粗俗，冒犯了许多观众。|近义词：insensitivity, vulgarity, crudeness, coarseness|反义词: refinement, sensitivity, tact, politeness|词性:名词

Etymology->
From crass + -ness.  Crass itself comes from Late Latin crassus "thick, fat, gross," of uncertain origin.
