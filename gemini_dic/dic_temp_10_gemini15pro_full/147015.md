overlarded: /ˌoʊvərˈlɑːrdɪd/ | adj. | 过于油腻的；过分修饰的；过分渲染的 |  Covered with too much fat or lard; excessively embellished or elaborated; overdone. | The steak was overlarded and quite unpleasant to eat. 牛排太油腻了，很难吃。| 近义词：overdone, excessive, embellished, ornate, flamboyant | 反义词：simple, plain, understated, austere | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From "over" + "lard" (verb), meaning to cover or dress with lard.  The figurative meaning of excessive embellishment developed later. 词源：由“over”（过度）+“lard”（动词，用猪油覆盖或装饰）组成。后来发展出过度修饰的比喻意义。
