cavalries:/ˈkævəlriz/| n. |骑兵部队；装甲部队|A group of soldiers who fight on horseback, or in armored vehicles.|The general led his cavalries into battle. 将军率领他的骑兵部队投入战斗。|例句：The kingdom's cavalries were renowned for their swiftness and skill.  这个王国的骑兵以速度和技巧而闻名。

例句：Modern cavalries utilize tanks and other armored vehicles. 现代骑兵部队使用坦克和其他装甲车辆。|近义词：troops, armored forces, cavalry|反义词: infantry|词性: 名词
