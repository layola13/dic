overtraining:/ˌoʊvərˈtreɪnɪŋ/
noun
过度训练
Excessive training that leads to diminished performance and various negative physical and psychological symptoms.
She suffered from overtraining and had to take a break from competition. 她过度训练，不得不暂停比赛。
He recognized the signs of overtraining in his athletes and adjusted their training schedules accordingly. 他发现了运动员过度训练的迹象，并相应地调整了他们的训练计划。
Synonyms: burnout, overwork, exhaustion
Antonyms: undertraining, rest, recovery
