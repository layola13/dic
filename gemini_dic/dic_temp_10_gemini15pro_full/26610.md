cachet:/kæˈʃeɪ/ /kæˈʃeɪ/
n.
威望，声望；（尤指来自法国的）印章，封印
A distinctive mark or seal, as of official approval or authentication. Also, prestige or distinction.
The product had a certain cachet, being endorsed by a popular celebrity.  这款产品有一定的声望，因为它是由一位受欢迎的名人代言的。
Synonyms: prestige, distinction, status, kudos, mark, seal
Antonyms: disrepute, ignominy, obscurity

cachet:/kæˈʃeɪ/ /kæˈʃeɪ/
n.
(药)胶囊；糯米纸，淀粉纸
A flat capsule enclosing a dose of unpleasant-tasting medicine.
He took his daily medication in a cachet to mask the bitter taste. 他把他的日常药物装在胶囊里服用，以掩盖苦味。
Synonyms: capsule, wafer, tablet
Antonyms: N/A


词性: noun
