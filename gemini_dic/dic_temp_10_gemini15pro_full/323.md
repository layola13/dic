abettors: /əˈbɛtərz/
n.
教唆犯；帮凶；煽动者
Someone who abets a crime or wrongdoing, especially by encouraging or assisting another person.
The abettors of the rebellion were arrested and imprisoned.  这些叛乱的教唆者被逮捕和监禁。

The police are searching for the abettors of the bombing. 警方正在搜捕这起爆炸案的帮凶。

近义词:accomplices, assistants, collaborators, instigators, supporters
反义词:opponent, adversary, enemy
词性:名词(plural)


词源:
From Anglo-French abeter, from Old French abeter meaning "to bait," from à ("to") + beter ("to bait").  The meaning shifted from "to incite (an animal)" to "to incite (a person)" and then to "to encourage or assist in wrongdoing."
