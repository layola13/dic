agoraphobia:/ˌæɡərəˈfoʊbiə/ | n. | 广场恐惧症；旷野恐惧症 | An anxiety disorder characterized by a fear of being in situations or places where escape might be difficult or embarrassing, or in which help might not be available in the event of having a panic attack or panic-like symptoms. |  She suffers from agoraphobia and rarely leaves her house. 她患有广场恐惧症，很少出门。

Agoraphobia can be a very debilitating condition. 广场恐惧症可能是一种非常使人衰弱的疾病。


|近义词：anxiety, fear, panic |反义词: calmness, composure |词性: noun
