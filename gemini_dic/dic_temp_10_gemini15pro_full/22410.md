booing:/ˈbuːɪŋ/| n.|嘘声，倒彩|The booing from the crowd was deafening.  人群的嘘声震耳欲聋。|The performance was met with loud booing. 表演遭到了大声的嘘声。|近义词：jeering, hissing, catcalling|反义词：cheering, applause|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "boo," which is imitative of the sound.  源自动词 "boo"，是对声音的模仿。
