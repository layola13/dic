cuter:/ˈkjuːtər/| adj.|更可爱的|Comparative form of "cute," meaning more adorable or charming.|That puppy is cuter than the other one. 那只小狗比另一只更可爱。|The kitten's playful antics are even cuter than before. 小猫顽皮的动作比以前更可爱了。|近义词：more adorable, more charming, sweeter|反义词：uglier, less attractive|词性:形容词
cute:/kjuːt/| adj.|可爱的|Having an attractive or endearing quality; charming.|The baby has a cute smile.  这个婴儿笑起来很可爱。|She wore a cute dress to the party. 她穿了一件可爱的连衣裙去参加聚会。|近义词：adorable, charming, lovely, pretty|反义词：ugly, unattractive, hideous|词性:形容词
