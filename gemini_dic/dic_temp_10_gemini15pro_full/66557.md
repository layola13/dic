exhibitional:/ˌɛksɪˈbɪʃənl/
adjective
展览的，展出的
Suitable or intended for exhibition; serving to exhibit.
The gallery has a large collection of exhibition materials, including photographs and videos from past shows.  画廊收藏了大量的展览资料，包括过去展览的照片和视频。
The museum's exhibitional space has been expanded to accommodate larger installations. 博物馆的展览空间已经扩大，以容纳更大的装置艺术。
近义词:exhibitory, display, expositional
反义词:concealed, hidden
词性:adjective
