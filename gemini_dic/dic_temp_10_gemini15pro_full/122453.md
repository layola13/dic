mobiles:/ˈmoʊbəlz/ (US), /ˈməʊbaɪlz/ (UK)| n.| 移动设备；手机；动态雕塑|A mobile is a type of kinetic sculpture constructed to take advantage of the principle of equilibrium. Mobiles are popular decorative items, and some are produced as works of art.|The baby stared at the colourful mobile hanging above his crib.|Synonyms: cell phones, smartphones, handsets (for cell phones); kinetic sculptures, hanging sculptures (for art)|Antonyms: landlines (for cell phones); static sculptures (for art)|noun


mobiles:/ˈmoʊbəlz/ (US), /ˈməʊbaɪlz/ (UK)| adj.|可移动的；活动的|Something that is mobile can be easily moved.|Mobile libraries bring books to people in remote areas.|Synonyms: movable, portable, transportable|Antonyms: immobile, fixed, stationary|adjective
