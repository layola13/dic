ceroplasty: /ˈsɪərəˌplæsti/ | n. | 蜡疗法；蜡模型制作；耳垢成形术 | The art of modelling in wax; also, the modelling or formation of wax; specifically, in surgery, the formation of a wax model to replace a lost or damaged part, or for cosmetic purposes, especially in rhinoplasty (nose surgery). |  Ceroplasty was used in ancient times to create lifelike figures.  蜡疗法在古代被用来制作逼真的雕像。

Ceroplasty can be a delicate procedure in reconstructive surgery.  蜡疗法在重建手术中可能是一个精细的程序。 | 近义词: wax modelling, wax sculpture | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词) | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Greek *kēroplastēs* (modeler in wax), from *kēros* (wax) + *plassein* (to mold).
