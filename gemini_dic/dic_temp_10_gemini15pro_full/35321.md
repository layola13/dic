cnidaria:/naɪˈdɛəriə/
n.
刺胞动物门
aquatic invertebrate animals characterized by stinging cells called cnidocytes.  These cells are used for capturing prey and defense. Examples include jellyfish, corals, and anemones.
Jellyfish, a type of cnidaria, are beautiful but can deliver a painful sting. 水母是一种刺胞动物，美丽但会带来痛苦的刺痛。
Corals, members of the cnidaria phylum, build reefs that support diverse ecosystems. 珊瑚是刺胞动物门的一员，它们建造的珊瑚礁支持着多样化的生态系统。
近义词:coelenterate (腔肠动物)
词性:noun
