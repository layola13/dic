globalizer:/ˈɡloʊbəˌlaɪzər/ | n. | 全球化推动者；促使全球化的人或事物 | A person or thing that promotes or accelerates the process of globalization. |  The internet is a powerful globalizer, connecting people and cultures worldwide. 互联网是一个强大的全球化推动者，连接着世界各地的人们和文化。

|Example: Multinational corporations are often seen as key globalizers. 跨国公司通常被视为主要的全球化推动者。|Synonym: internationalist, cosmopolitan, integrator | Antonym: localist, nationalist, isolationist | noun
