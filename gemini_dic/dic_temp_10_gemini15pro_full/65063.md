eternally:/ɪˈtɜːnəli/ /ɪˈtɝːnəli/
adverb
永远地，永恒地
In a way that lasts forever.

She will be eternally grateful for his kindness. 她将永远感激他的好意。

The question of what happens after death will eternally be debated. 人死后会发生什么这个问题将永远被争论下去。


近义词:forever, always, perpetually, everlastingly
反义词:temporarily, briefly, never
词性:adverb
