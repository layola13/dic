cutest:/ˈkjuːtɪst/| adj.|最可爱的|Superlative form of cute: most cute.  It means extremely charming, attractive, or endearing, often in a youthful or innocent way.|That puppy is the cutest thing I've ever seen! 那只小狗是我见过的最可爱的东西！|The kitten's cutest pose was when it was sleeping curled up in a ball. 小猫最可爱的姿势是蜷成一团睡觉的时候。|近义词：adorable, charming, delightful, precious|反义词：ugliest, hideous, repulsive|词性:形容词
词源:From *cute* + *-est*.  *Cute* is of uncertain origin, possibly a shortening of *acute*, meaning sharp or clever, which acquired a sense of attractive or pretty.
