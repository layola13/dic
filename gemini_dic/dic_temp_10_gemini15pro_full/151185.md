parchedness:/ˈpɑːrtʃɪdnəs/ |n.| 干燥，焦渴 |The parchedness of the desert made travel difficult. 沙漠的干燥使得旅行困难。|例句：The parchedness of the soil made it impossible to grow anything. 土壤的干燥使得无法种植任何东西。

The prolonged drought led to extreme parchedness in the region. 长时间的干旱导致该地区极度干燥。|近义词：dryness, aridity, desiccation|反义词：wetness, humidity, moisture|词性: 名词

Etymology: From "parched" + "-ness". "Parched" comes from the Middle English "parchen," from Old French "parcher," ultimately from Latin "torrere" (to parch, roast).  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns denoting a state, condition, or quality.
