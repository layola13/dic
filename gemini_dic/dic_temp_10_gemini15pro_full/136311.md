nonrepudiable:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈpjuː.di.ə.bəl/ /ˌnɑːn.rɪˈpjuː.di.ə.bəl/
adj.
不可否认的，无法抵赖的
Unable to be denied or disputed.  Relating to a service that provides proof of the integrity and origin of data, which can be verified by a third party.  It prevents an individual or entity from denying that they performed a particular action.
Example:  The digital signature provides nonrepudiable evidence that the document was signed by the claimed sender.  数字签名提供了不可否认的证据，证明该文件是由声称的发送者签署的。

Example:  Nonrepudiable transactions are crucial in online banking. 不可否认的交易在网上银行中至关重要。
Synonym: undeniable, irrefutable, incontestable
Antonym: deniable, disputable, refutable
词性: adjective
