ETYMOLOGY->
From French *basalte*, from Late Latin *basaltes*, perhaps a variant of Latin *basanites* “touchstone”, from Greek *basanitēs (lithos)* ‘basanite (stone)’. Ultimately from Egyptian *bʒn*.
