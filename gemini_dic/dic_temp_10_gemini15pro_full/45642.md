cursorial:/kɜːrˈsɔːriəl/
adjective
适于奔跑的，善跑的
Adapted for running.
The cheetah is a cursorial animal, built for speed. 猎豹是一种善跑的动物，生来就是为了速度。
Cursorial adaptations in birds include long legs and reduced toes. 鸟类适于奔跑的适应性特征包括长腿和减少的脚趾。
synonym:running, fleet, swift
antonym:sessile, sedentary
adjective
