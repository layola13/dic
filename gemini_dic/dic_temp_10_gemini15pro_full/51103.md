desperate:/ˈdespərət/| adj.|绝望的，拼命的|Feeling or showing hopelessness.|She made a desperate attempt to reach the shore. 她拼命地想要游到岸边。|Near synonyms: frantic, hopeless, dire|Near antonyms: hopeful, calm, composed|adjective
