arrosion:/əˈroʊʒən/ | n. | 侵蚀，腐蚀 |The gradual wearing away or disintegration of something by chemical action, or by the action of wind, rain, or water. | The arrosion of the coastline by the sea is a serious problem. 海水对海岸线的侵蚀是一个严重的问题。| synonyms: erosion, corrosion, decay, disintegration | antonyms: accretion, deposition, accumulation | noun

arrosion:/əˈroʊʒən/ | n. | (比喻)削弱，逐渐丧失 |The gradual destruction or weakening of something non-physical, such as power or authority.  | The constant criticism led to the arrosion of her confidence.  不断的批评导致她的自信心逐渐丧失。| synonyms: undermining, weakening, deterioration, decline | antonyms: strengthening, reinforcement, bolstering | noun
