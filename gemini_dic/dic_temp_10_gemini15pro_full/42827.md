couriered:/ˈkʊriərd/ | v. |递送，专人送递|The documents were couriered to the head office. 文件被专人送到了总部。|例句：The contract was couriered to the lawyer's office yesterday. 合同昨天被专人送到了律师事务所。|近义词：sent, shipped, delivered, dispatched|反义词: received, collected|词性: 动词

词源: From the noun "courier" (a messenger), ultimately from French "courrier" (runner), from Old French "correor" (to run).
