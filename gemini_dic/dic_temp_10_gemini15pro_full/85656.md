haustellate:/ˈhɔːstəˌleɪt/ /ˈhɑːstəˌleɪt/| adj.|吸食的，喙吸式的|Adapted for sucking; having a haustellum or proboscis for sucking fluids.|Butterflies have haustellate mouthparts for sipping nectar. 蝴蝶有喙吸式口器来吸食花蜜。|近义词：sucking, proboscidiform|反义词: chewing, biting|词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *haustellātus*, past participle of *haustellāre* (“to draw water, drink”), from *haustus* (“a drawing, draught, scoop”), from *hauriō* (“I draw, drain, drink”).
