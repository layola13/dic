pallbearer:/ˈpɔːlˌberər/| n.|抬棺人，护柩者|A person who helps to carry a coffin at a funeral.|例句：He served as a pallbearer at his grandfather's funeral. 他在他祖父的葬礼上担任护柩者。

例句：Six pallbearers carried the coffin into the church. 六名抬棺人将棺材抬进了教堂。

近义词： coffin bearer|反义词: 無|词性:noun
