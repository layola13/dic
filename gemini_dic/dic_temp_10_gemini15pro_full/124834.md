muckiness:/ˈmʌkɪnɪs/
noun
污秽；泥泞；肮脏
the state of being dirty, muddy, or filled with muck.
The muckiness of the stable made it difficult to walk. 马厩的污秽使得行走困难。
近义词: dirtiness, filthiness, messiness, squalor
反义词: cleanliness, tidiness, neatness
词性: noun


muckiness:/ˈmʌkɪnɪs/
noun
下流；卑鄙
moral filth or corruption.
He was disgusted by the muckiness of the tabloid's reporting. 他对小报报道的下流感到恶心。
近义词: sleaziness, sordidness, depravity
反义词: decency, morality, purity
词性: noun


词源: From muck + -iness.  Muck itself comes from Old Norse *myki, meaning "manure, dung."
