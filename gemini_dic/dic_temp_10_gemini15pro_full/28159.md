capnometer:/kæpˈnɒmɪtər/ | n. | 二氧化碳测量仪，二氧化碳浓度测定计 | An instrument for measuring the amount of carbon dioxide in a gas, especially the level of carbon dioxide in expired air. | The anesthesiologist used a capnometer to monitor the patient's breathing during surgery. 麻醉师在手术期间使用二氧化碳测量仪监测患者的呼吸。|  The capnometer reading indicated a sudden drop in exhaled CO2. 二氧化碳测量仪读数显示呼出的二氧化碳突然下降。 | synonyms: CO2 monitor, carbon dioxide analyzer | antonyms:  (No direct antonym exists for a device.
