emetically:/ɪˈmɛtɪkli/
adverb
催吐地，引起呕吐地
Causing vomiting; in a way that induces vomiting.
The drug acted emetically, causing the patient to vomit repeatedly.  这种药起到了催吐的作用，导致病人反复呕吐。
The toxin worked emetically. 这种毒素会引起呕吐。
Synonym:vomitively
Antonym:anti-emetically
词性: adverb
