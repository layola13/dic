confucianists: /kənˈfjuːʃənɪsts/
| n. | 儒家学者，儒士 |  Followers or advocates of Confucianism, a system of philosophical and ethical teachings founded by Confucius and developed by Mencius. | Many Confucianists believe in the importance of filial piety and social harmony. 许多儒家学者相信孝顺和社会和谐的重要性。 |  synonyms: disciples of Confucius, followers of Confucius, Confucian scholars | antonyms:  Daoists, Legalists (representing contrasting schools of thought in ancient China) | noun

confucianist: /kənˈfjuːʃənɪst/
| adj. | 儒家的，儒教的 | Relating to or characteristic of Confucianism.  |  Confucianist principles emphasize education and moral development. 儒家原则强调教育和道德发展。| synonyms: Confucian | antonyms: Daoist, Legalist | adjective
