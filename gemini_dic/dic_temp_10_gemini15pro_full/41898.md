cosiness:/ˈkoʊzinəs/ (US) /ˈkəʊzinəs/ (UK)| n. |舒适，温馨，惬意|A feeling of warmth, comfort, and relaxation.|例句：The cosiness of the fire made us sleepy. 壁炉的温暖让我们昏昏欲睡。|例句：She loved the cosiness of her small apartment. 她喜欢她小公寓的温馨舒适。|近义词：comfort, warmth, snugness, hominess|反义词：discomfort, coldness, bleakness|词性:名词

cosy:/ˈkoʊzi/ (US) /ˈkəʊzi/ (UK)| adj. |舒适的，温暖的，惬意的|Warm, comfortable, and inviting.|例句：We spent a cosy evening by the fire. 我们在炉火旁度过了一个舒适的夜晚。|例句：The room was cosy and inviting. 房间温暖舒适，引人入胜。|近义词：comfortable, snug, warm, homely|反义词：uncomfortable, cold, bleak, austere|词性:形容词
