hamper:/ˈhæmpər/ (US), /ˈhæmpə(r)/ (UK)| vt.|阻碍，妨碍|To prevent someone from easily doing or achieving something.|例句：The heavy rain hampered our progress.  大雨阻碍了我们的前进。|近义词：obstruct, hinder, impede|反义词:aid, assist, facilitate|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old North French *hanapier "container for a drinking vessel," related to Old French hanap "goblet, drinking vessel." The meaning shifted from "container for goblets" to "large basket" in the 16th century.
