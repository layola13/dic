conceptacle:/kənˈsɛptəkl/| n. | 容器；囊；（藻类等的）生殖窝|A specialized cavity or chamber in some lower plants and fungi, containing reproductive organs.|The brown algae release their gametes from conceptacles within the receptacles.|近义词：cavity, chamber, receptacle|反义词: None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *conceptaculum* "receptacle," from Latin *concipere* "to take in, conceive," from *com-* "together" + *capere* "to take."
