anticatarrhal:/ˌæntiˌkætəˈrɑːl/
adjective
抗卡他性的，治疗卡他性的
Reduces or relieves the symptoms of catarrh (inflammation of the mucous membranes, especially of the nose and throat).
This herbal tea has anticatarrhal properties and can help soothe a sore throat. 这种凉茶具有抗卡他性，可以舒缓喉咙痛。
Synonyms: decongestant, expectorant
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider words like "inflammatory")
Word type: adjective
