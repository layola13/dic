auguries: /ˈɔːɡjʊriz/ | n. | 预兆，征兆 |  Signs or omens, especially those believed to foretell the future.  Derived from Latin "augurium," meaning "divination from birds." |  The auguries for the new business venture were not good. 这家新企业的预兆并不好。 | synonyms: omens, portents, signs, forebodings | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider) certainties, guarantees | noun
