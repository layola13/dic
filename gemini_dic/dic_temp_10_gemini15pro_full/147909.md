overreflectiveness:/ˌoʊvərˌriːflɛkˈtɪvnəs/
|n.|过度反思|The act of thinking too much about oneself, one's actions, and their meanings, often leading to paralysis or anxiety.  It involves excessive introspection and rumination.|Her overreflectiveness often prevented her from making decisions. 她过度反思常常让她难以做决定。|近义词：introspection, rumination, self-analysis|反义词：impulsiveness, spontaneity|词性: noun

词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "reflective" (adjective meaning "characterized by reflection") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns from adjectives).  Ultimately from Latin "reflectere" (to bend back).
