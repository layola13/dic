nonderelict:/ˌnɒnˈdɛrɪlɪkt/ | adj. | 非废弃的，维护良好的 | Not derelict; in good repair or use; not neglected. | The nonderelict building stood in stark contrast to the dilapidated structures surrounding it. 这栋维护良好的建筑与周围破败的建筑形成鲜明对比。 | The park was nonderelict and well-maintained, a popular spot for families. 这个公园维护良好，没有被废弃，是家庭聚会的热门地点。 | 近义词: well-maintained, preserved, sound, intact | 反义词: derelict, dilapidated, neglected, abandoned | 词性: 形容词

词源: From non- + derelict.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "derelict" means abandoned or neglected.
