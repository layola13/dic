misphrase:/ˌmɪsˈfreɪz/ | v. |  表达错误，措辞不当 | To express something incorrectly or awkwardly, especially by using the wrong words. | I misphrased my question and confused everyone. 我措辞不当，把大家都弄糊涂了。| synonyms: misspeak, misstate, misword | antonyms: articulate, express clearly | verb
