abat-voix: /ˌæbɑːˈvwɑː/
noun
lampshade; sound baffle
A covering, often conical or cylindrical, placed over a light source to diffuse or direct the light.  Also, a device used to deflect or absorb sound waves.
The abat-voix over the bedside lamp cast a warm glow. 床头灯上的灯罩发出温暖的光芒。

The recording studio used abat-voix panels to minimize echo. 录音棚使用吸音板来最大限度地减少回声。
Synonym: shade, diffuser, baffle
Antonym: amplifier (for sound context)
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From French, literally "down-voice," from abattre (to lower) + voix (voice).

USAGE->
The abat-voix is a crucial element in creating a cozy atmosphere. 灯罩是营造舒适氛围的关键元素。

The musician positioned the abat-voix to control the sound projection. 音乐家放置了吸音板来控制声音的投射。
