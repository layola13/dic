orbital:/ˈɔːrbɪtl/| adj. n.|轨道的，眼窝的；轨道|Relating to an orbit or orbits.  A region of space around the nucleus of an atom where an electron is likely to be found.|Electrons occupy orbitals around the nucleus. 电子占据原子核周围的轨道。|The orbital bone was fractured. 眼眶骨骨折了。|synonyms: circling, revolving|antonyms: static, stationary|adjective, noun
