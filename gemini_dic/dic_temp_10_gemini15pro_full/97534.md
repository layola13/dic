ingrained:/ɪnˈɡreɪnd/ /ɪnˈɡrend/
adjective
根深蒂固的；牢固的
firmly fixed or established; difficult to change
例句：The belief that hard work is virtuous is deeply ingrained in our culture.  努力工作是美德的信念在我们文化中根深蒂固。

例句：His childhood experiences left him with a deeply ingrained fear of the dark. 他童年时的经历使他对黑暗有着根深蒂固的恐惧。

近义词：deep-rooted, embedded, fixed, established, entrenched
反义词：superficial, shallow, fleeting, transient
词性：adjective
