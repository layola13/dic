hijacking:/ˈhaɪdʒækɪŋ/| n.|劫持，挟持|The act of illegally seizing an aircraft, ship, or vehicle while in transit and forcing it to go to a different destination or using it for one's own purposes.|The hijacking of the plane resulted in the deaths of all on board.  飞机被劫持导致机上所有人死亡。|近义词：seizure, theft, appropriation|反义词:release, return|noun
