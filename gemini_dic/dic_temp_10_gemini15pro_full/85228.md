harlequinesque:/ˌhɑːrlɪkɪˈnɛsk/
adj.
滑稽的，小丑般的，五颜六色的
Resembling or characteristic of a harlequin; in the manner of a harlequin, especially in being brightly colored, fantastic, or grotesquely comic.
The harlequinesque costumes of the circus performers delighted the children.  马戏团表演者滑稽的小丑服装让孩子们很开心。

The building's harlequinesque facade, with its mismatched colors and patterns, was certainly eye-catching.  这栋建筑滑稽的五颜六色的外墙，色彩和图案不协调，确实很引人注目。

近义词:clownish, comical, grotesque, bizarre, flamboyant
反义词:serious, somber, plain, understated
词性:adjective
