masculinizing:/ˈmæskjʊləˌnaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
男性化的；使男性化的
causing someone or something to have or develop more typically male qualities
例句：
The hormone treatment had a masculinizing effect on her voice.
荷尔蒙治疗使她的声音男性化。

Certain exercises can have a masculinizing effect on the body.
某些运动会使身体男性化。

近义词：virilizing,  making male, making masculine
反义词：feminizing
词性: adjective
