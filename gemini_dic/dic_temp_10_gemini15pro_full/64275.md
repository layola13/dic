equisetum:/ˌɛkwɪˈsiːtəm/ | n. | 木贼；问荆 | A genus of vascular plants, the only living genus in Equisetaceae, a family of ferns.  They are commonly known as horsetails. | Equisetum is a common plant found near wetlands.  木贼是一种常见于湿地附近的植物。| Equisetum hyemale is used for scouring pots and pans.  木贼冬草被用来擦洗锅碗瓢盆。| synonyms: horsetail, scouring rush | antonyms: N/A | noun
