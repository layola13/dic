normalizable:/ˈnɔːrməlaɪzəbl/| adj.|可规范化的，可标准化的|Able to be normalized; capable of being made to conform to a norm or standard.  This often appears in mathematical or technical contexts, particularly in physics and computer science.|The wave function is normalizable, ensuring a valid probability distribution.  这个波函数是可规范化的，确保了有效的概率分布。|synonyms: standardizable, regularizable|antonyms: non-normalizable, irregular|adjective
