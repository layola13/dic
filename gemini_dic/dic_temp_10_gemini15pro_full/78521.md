ghibelline:/ˈɡɪbəlɪn/ | n. | 吉贝林；皇帝党 |  A member of the imperial and aristocratic party in medieval Italy and Germany, opposed to the Guelphs who supported the Pope. | During the Italian Renaissance, the Ghibellines were often associated with the Holy Roman Emperor. 在意大利文艺复兴时期，吉贝林派通常与神圣罗马帝国皇帝联系在一起。| synonyms: imperialist, antipapal | antonyms: Guelph | noun
