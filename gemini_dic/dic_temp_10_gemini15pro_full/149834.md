paleosaurus:/ˌpeɪliəˈsɔːrəs/ | n. |古蜥蜴|An extinct reptile of the Triassic period, typically having a large head, powerful jaws, and short limbs.|Paleosaurus fossils have been found in Europe.  古蜥蜴化石在欧洲被发现。|Synonym: thecodont (a broader classification)|Antonym:  N/A (extinct animals don't have direct antonyms)|noun
