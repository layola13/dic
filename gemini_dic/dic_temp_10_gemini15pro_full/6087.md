americanises:/əˈmɛrɪkənaɪzɪz/
verb
使美国化
To cause to become American in character; to assimilate to the customs and institutions of the United States.
Many immigrants americanise their names when they move to the US.  许多移民移居美国后，会把自己的名字美国化。
The company americanised its marketing strategy to appeal to a wider audience.  这家公司将其营销策略美国化，以吸引更广泛的受众。
Synonyms: westernize, assimilate
Antonyms: localize, nationalize
词性:动词
