condemn:/kənˈdɛm/| vt.|谴责，判刑，宣告…不适用|to express strong disapproval of; to pronounce a punishment on; to officially declare (something) to be unfit for use|The government condemned the terrorist attack. 政府谴责了恐怖袭击。|近义词：denounce, criticize, sentence, doom|反义词: praise, commend, acquit, exonerate|词性:动词
Etymology: From Old French condemner, from Latin condemnare "to condemn, sentence, disapprove, blame," from com- "together" + damnare "to inflict loss upon," from damnum "loss, damage."
