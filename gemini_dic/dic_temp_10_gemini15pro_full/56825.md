dormitories: /ˈdɔːrmɪtɔːriz/ (US) /ˈdɔːmɪtriz/ (UK)| n. |宿舍（复数）|A large room containing several beds, especially in a school or institution, or a building containing such rooms.|The university has several dormitories for its students. 大学有几栋学生宿舍楼。|

The dormitories are always noisy during exam week.  考试周宿舍总是很吵。

|Synonyms: dorms, residence halls, hostels, accommodation|Antonyms: private residences, apartments, homes|noun|
