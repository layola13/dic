conoidical:/kəˈnɔɪdɪkəl/
adjective
圆锥形的；圆锥体的
Resembling a cone; cone-shaped.
The shell has a conoidical shape.  这个贝壳呈圆锥形。
近义词: conical, cone-shaped
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "spherical" or "cubical" depending on context)
词性: adjective


conoid:/ˈkəʊnɔɪd/
noun
圆锥体；圆锥面
A surface or solid generated by a straight line moving so that it always intersects a fixed plane curve and passes through a fixed point not in the plane of the curve.
The architect incorporated a conoid into the roof design.  建筑师将一个圆锥体融入到屋顶设计中。
近义词: cone
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun


Etymology:
From Greek *kōnoeides* "cone-shaped," from *kōnos* "cone" + *-eides* "having the form of."
