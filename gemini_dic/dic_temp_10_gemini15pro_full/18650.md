benzoline:/ˈbenzəliːn/ /ˈbɛnzəˌlin/
noun
汽油；轻质石油
A colorless, flammable liquid distilled from petroleum, used as a motor fuel and as a solvent.
Benzoline was commonly used in early automobiles. 早期的汽车普遍使用汽油。
synonyms: gasoline, petrol
antonyms: none (no direct opposite for a specific fuel type)
n.
