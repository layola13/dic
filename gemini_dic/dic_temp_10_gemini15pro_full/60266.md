egad:/ɪˈɡæd/ |int.|哎呀；天哪|An exclamation of surprise or annoyance.|Egad! I've forgotten my keys! 天哪！我忘记带钥匙了！|近义词: gosh, good heavens, my goodness, oh dear |反义词: (No direct antonym for an exclamation) |词性: Interjection

词源:  Shortening of "ye gods," a mild oath.
