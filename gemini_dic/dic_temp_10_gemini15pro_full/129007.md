nirvanas:/nɪərˈvɑːnəz/ | n. |涅槃，极乐世界|A state of perfect happiness and peace; a transcendent state in which there is neither suffering, desire, nor sense of self, and the subject is released from the effects of karma and the cycle of death and rebirth. It represents the final goal of Buddhism.|He finally reached nirvana after years of meditation. 他经过多年的冥想，终于达到了涅槃。|Synonyms: paradise, heaven, bliss, utopia|Antonyms: hell, suffering, torment|noun
