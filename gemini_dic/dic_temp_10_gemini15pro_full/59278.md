eagle: /ˈiːɡəl/ (US) /ˈiːɡl/ (UK)| n.|鹰|A large bird of prey with a hooked beak and powerful talons, known for its sharp eyesight and soaring flight. |The eagle soared high above the mountains.  鹰在群山上空翱翔。|
近义词: hawk, falcon, raptor|反义词: prey, dove|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *egle*, from Old French *aigle*, from Latin *aquila* ("eagle").
