giddiest:/ˈɡɪdiɪst/ | adj. | 最头晕的；最轻浮的；最令人眼花缭乱的 |  Superlative adjective of giddy.  Means feeling or causing a sensation of whirling and a tendency to fall or stagger; frivolous and lighthearted; exciting. | The giddiest heights of the rollercoaster made my stomach drop. 过山车最令人眩晕的高度让我的胃往下坠。| The giddiest rumours swirled around the celebrity couple. 关于这对名人夫妇最荒谬的谣言四处流传。| synonyms:dizziest, lightheaded, flightiest, most frivolous | antonyms:serious, solemn, sober, grounded | adjective
