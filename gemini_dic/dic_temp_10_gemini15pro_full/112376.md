loser: /ˈluːzər/ (US) /ˈluːzə(r)/ (UK)| n.|失败者，输家|A person or thing that loses or has lost something, especially a competition or game.  A person who is generally unsuccessful or inept.|He felt like a loser after failing the exam. 他考试不及格后感觉自己像个失败者。|Synonyms: failure, non-winner, also-ran|Antonyms: winner, victor, champion|noun

loser: /ˈluːzər/ (US) /ˈluːzə(r)/ (UK)| adj. (informal, disapproving)|失败的，差劲的|Characterized by losing or failing; unsuccessful.  (Used informally and often disapprovingly)|That was a loser idea from the start.  那从一开始就是一个糟糕的主意。|Synonyms: failing, unsuccessful, ineffective|Antonyms: winning, successful, effective|adjective
