horus:/ˈhɔːrəs/| n.|荷鲁斯|Horus is one of the most significant ancient Egyptian deities. He was worshipped as the god of the sky, war, and protection.  His image is typically depicted as a falcon or a falcon-headed man.|Horus was a powerful god in ancient Egypt. 荷鲁斯是古埃及一位强大的神。

Horus was often depicted with a falcon's head. 荷鲁斯通常被描绘成隼头人身。|近义词:Ra, god|反义词:Set|词性:noun
