gnashing:/ˈnæʃɪŋ/| n.|咬牙切齿，磨牙|the act of grinding or striking the teeth together, typically as a sign of anger, frustration, or pain.|He sat there, gnashing his teeth in frustration. 他坐在那里，气得咬牙切齿。|Synonyms: grinding, clenching, gritting|Antonyms: relaxing, calming|noun
