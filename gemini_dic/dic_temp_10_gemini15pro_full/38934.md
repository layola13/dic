confide:/kənˈfaɪd/ | v. | 吐露；委托；信赖 | to tell someone about a secret or private matter while trusting them not to repeat it to others. To trust someone with information or a task. | I confided in her about my problems. 我向她倾诉了我的问题。| synonyms: reveal, disclose, divulge, admit, share, impart | antonyms: conceal, hide, suppress | verb

confiding:/kənˈfaɪdɪŋ/ | adj. |  信任的；易于相信人的 | showing trust and intimacy. | She gave me a confiding smile. 她给了我一个信任的微笑。| synonyms: trusting, trustful, open, frank, candid | antonyms: suspicious, distrustful, wary | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "confidere"  (to trust fully), from "con-" (with) + "fidere" (to trust).
