exotery:/ˈeksətɛri/ | n. | 外传；通俗易懂的教义 |  The knowledge or doctrines that are openly taught or revealed, as opposed to esoteric or secret ones. |  His lectures focused on the exotery of Buddhist philosophy, accessible to everyone. 他讲座的重点是佛教哲学的通俗易懂的部分，每个人都能理解。 | 近义词: popularization, vulgarization | 反义词: esotery, mystery | 词性: noun

exoteric:/ˌeksəˈtɛrɪk/ | adj. | 外传的；通俗易懂的 |  Suitable for or communicated to the general public; not esoteric or secret. | The exoteric teachings of the group were designed to attract new members.  该组织的通俗易懂的教义旨在吸引新成员。 | 近义词: public, accessible, common, understandable | 反义词: esoteric, secret, hidden, occult | 词性: adjective
