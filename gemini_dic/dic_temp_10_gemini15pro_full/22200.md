bombycinous:/ˌbɒmbɪˈsaɪnəs/ | adj. | 蚕丝的；柔软光滑的；华丽的 |  Made of or resembling silk; soft and lustrous; splendid. |  The actress wore a bombycinous gown that shimmered in the spotlight.  这位女演员穿着一件在聚光灯下闪闪发光的丝绸长袍。 | 近义词：silken, silky, satiny, luxurious | 反义词：coarse, rough, plain | 词性：形容词

Etymology: From Latin *bombycinus, from bombyx (“silkworm”), from Ancient Greek βόμβυξ (bómbux, “silkworm”).
