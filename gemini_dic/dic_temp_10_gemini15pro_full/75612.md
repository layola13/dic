fugal:/ˈfjuːɡəl/| adj.|赋格的；逃亡的|Of, relating to, or resembling a fugue, especially in being complex and intricate.  Pertaining to or resembling flight; fleeing.|The composer's later works often incorporated fugal elements.  作曲家后期的作品经常融入赋格元素。 The family made a fugal escape from the war-torn country.  这家人逃离了饱受战争蹂躏的国家。|近义词：contrapuntal, polyphonic (for musical sense); fugitive, escaping (for fleeing sense)|反义词：homophonic (for musical sense); settled, staying (for fleeing sense)|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *fugalis, from Latin fuga (“flight”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰug- (“to flee, escape”).
