brim:/brɪm/| v.|充满，溢出|Her eyes brimmed with tears. 她的眼里充满了泪水。|The glass brimmed with champagne. 杯子里盛满了香槟。|近义词：overflow, fill, teem|反义词：empty, drain|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *brymme*, from Old English *brymm* (“brim, edge, surf, shore”), from Proto-Germanic *bremmaz (“edge, rim”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰrem- (“to swell, be pregnant”). Cognate with Old Norse *barmr* (“edge, rim, bosom, lap”), Dutch *brim* (“surf”), German *Bräme* (“border, hem”), *Bremse* (“gadfly”).
