frail:/freɪl/| adj.| 脆弱的；虚弱的；易碎的 | Easily broken or damaged, physically weak, or delicate. | The frail old woman needed help crossing the street.  这位虚弱的老妇人需要帮助才能过马路。| synonyms: weak, delicate, fragile, feeble | antonyms: strong, robust, sturdy | adjective

frails:/freɪlz/| n.| 脆弱的东西；缺点；弱点 (通常用复数) | Weaknesses or flaws in character or a system.  |  We all have our own frails. 我们都有自己的弱点。 | synonyms: weaknesses, shortcomings, flaws, defects | antonyms: strengths, virtues | noun
