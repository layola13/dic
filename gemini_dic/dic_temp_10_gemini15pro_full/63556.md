enviable:/ˈɛnviəbl/| adj.|令人羡慕的；值得羡慕的|Something that is enviable is so desirable that it causes others to feel envy.  It inspires envy in others.|Her enviable figure made other women jealous. 她令人羡慕的身材让其他女人嫉妒。|近义词：desirable, coveted, admirable|反义词：undesirable, pitiable, unfortunate|词性:adjective
Etymology:  From Late Latin *invidiabilis, from Latin invidere "to envy" + -abilis "-able".
