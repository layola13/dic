complanar:/kəʊˈpleɪnə(r)/ /koʊˈpleɪnər/ | adj. |共面的，位于同一平面的 |Lying or acting in the same plane. |The points A, B, and C are complanar.  A、B、C三点共面。|

The two lines are complanar. 这两条线共面。


|近义词：coplanar |反义词：noncoplanar, skew (for lines) |词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->

From Latin com- (together) + planus (flat, level)
