leaching:/ˈliːtʃɪŋ/ | n. | 沥滤，浸出 | the process of removing a substance from something by washing it with a liquid | The leaching of minerals from the soil can be caused by acid rain. 土壤中矿物质的流失可能是由酸雨造成的。 |  synonyms: dissolving, extraction, filtration | antonyms: absorption, deposition | noun

leach:/liːtʃ/ | v. | 沥滤，浸出 | to remove a substance from something by washing it with a liquid |  Rainwater leached the nutrients out of the soil. 雨水将土壤中的养分冲走了。| synonyms: dissolve, extract, filter | antonyms: absorb, deposit | verb
