logger:/ˈlɒɡər/ (UK) /ˈlɔːɡər/ (US)| n.|伐木工人；记录器|A person who cuts down trees for timber; a device or program that records events, data, or messages.|The logger felled the giant redwood. 伐木工人砍倒了巨大的红杉树。 The system uses a logger to track user activity.  该系统使用记录器来跟踪用户活动。|近义词:lumberjack (for person), recorder, monitor|反义词:None (for person), None (for device)|noun
