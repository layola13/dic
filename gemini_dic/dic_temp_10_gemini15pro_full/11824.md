arrearage:/əˈrɪərɪdʒ/| n. |欠款，拖欠款项|Something that is in arrears; the state of being behind in the discharge of obligations, as the payment of money.|The company is facing legal action due to its arrearage in rent payments.  该公司因拖欠租金而面临法律诉讼。|Synonyms: arrears, debt, default, delinquency|Antonyms: prepayment, credit, advance|noun
