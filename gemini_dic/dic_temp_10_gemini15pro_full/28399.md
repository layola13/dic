carbonado:/ˌkɑːrbəˈnɑːdoʊ/ /ˌkɑːbənˈɑːdəʊ/
noun
碳酸盐矿石，黑金刚石
A black, opaque, and granular aggregate of microscopic diamond crystals.
The jeweler examined the rare carbonado with a loupe.  珠宝商用放大镜检查了这颗稀有的黑金刚石。
Synonyms: black diamond,  
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could be contrasted with colorless diamond)
n.

carbonado:/ˌkɑːrbəˈnɑːdoʊ/ /ˌkɑːbənˈɑːdəʊ/
verb
(葡萄牙语) 烧焦，烤焦
To char or scorch. (Primarily used in Portuguese and Spanish, less common in English)
The chef carbonadoed the peppers to bring out their smoky flavor.  厨师烤焦了辣椒，以带出它们的烟熏味。
Synonyms: char, scorch, sear
Antonyms: cool, chill
v.
