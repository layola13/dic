hysteric:/hɪˈstɛrɪk/ | adj. n. |歇斯底里的，癔病的；歇斯底里的人，癔病患者 | Characterized by or exhibiting excessive or uncontrollable emotion, such as fear or panic. Also, a person suffering from hysteria. | She burst into hysterics when she heard the news. 她听到这个消息后歇斯底里地哭了起来。 | Synonyms: frantic, frenzied, distraught | Antonyms: calm, composed, serene | adjective

hysteric:/hɪˈstɛrɪk/ | n. |歇斯底里发作，癔病患者| A fit of uncontrollable laughter or crying; a person prone to such fits. | The comedian sent the audience into hysterics.  这位喜剧演员让观众笑得歇斯底里。 | Synonyms: fit, outburst, convulsion | Antonyms: composure, tranquility | noun
