abrogable:/ˈæbrəɡəbl/| adj.|可废除的，可取消的|That law is abrogable by the legislature. 该法律可由立法机关废除。|The contract contains several abrogable clauses.  该合同包含几个可废除的条款。|近义词：repealable, revocable, annullable, rescindable|反义词：irrevocable, unalterable|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *abrogabilis, from Latin abrogare (“to repeal”), from ab- (“away from”) + rogare (“to ask, propose a law”).

USAGE->
The treaty is abrogable under certain conditions. = 在某些情况下，该条约可以废除。

The agreement is abrogable by mutual consent. = 经双方同意，该协议可以废除。

PHRASE->
an abrogable law = 一项可废除的法律
an abrogable contract = 一项可废除的合同
abrogable rights = 可废除的权利
abrogable clauses = 可废除的条款
abrogable agreements = 可废除的协议
