historiography:/ˌhɪstəˈriˈɑːɡrəfi/ | n. | 历史编纂学 | The study of the writing of history, including its methods, sources, and interpretations.  |  Example:  A course in historiography might examine how historical narratives have changed over time. 历史编纂学课程可能会研究历史叙述是如何随着时间推移而变化的。

Example:  Feminist historiography has challenged traditional historical narratives. 女性主义历史编纂学对传统的史学叙事提出了挑战。 |  Synonyms: historical writing, historical method, historical analysis  | Antonyms:  None (It's a field of study, not a concept with an opposite.) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *historiographie*, from Ancient Greek *ἱστοριογραφία (historíographía)*  *ἱστοριογράφος (historíográphos)* "historian", from  *ἱστορία (historía)*  "inquiry, knowledge acquired by investigation; learning, narrative" + *γράφειν (gráphein)* "to write".
