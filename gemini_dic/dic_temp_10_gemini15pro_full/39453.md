connector:/kəˈnektər/ (US) /kəˈnɛktə/ (UK)| n.|连接器，连接物，接头|A device or fitting that joins two or more things together.|The connector on the cable wasn't compatible with the port on the computer.  电缆上的连接器与计算机上的端口不兼容。|synonyms: coupler, adapter, link, joint, plug, socket|antonyms: separator, divider|noun

connector:/kəˈnektər/ (US) /kəˈnɛktə/ (UK)|n.|连接体，连接件；连接词|Something that connects or joins; a person or thing that establishes communication between different groups or fields.|He acted as a connector between the two warring factions. 他充当了两个交战派别之间的连接体。|synonyms: liaison, intermediary, bridge|antonyms:  barrier, obstacle|noun
