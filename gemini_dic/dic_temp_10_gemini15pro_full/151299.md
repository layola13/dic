pariah: /pəˈraɪə/ | n. |贱民，被社会遗弃者|A person who is rejected from society or community.|例句：He was treated like a pariah after the scandal.  丑闻之后，他被像贱民一样对待。

例句：She became a pariah in her own family after revealing their secrets.  在泄露了家里的秘密后，她成了家里的贱民。


近义词：outcast, undesirable, leper|反义词: insider,  elite |词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Tamil பறையர் (paṟaiyar), plural of பறையன் (paṟaiyan, “drummer; member of a low caste”). Originally referred to a specific low caste in Southern India who were traditionally drummers, leatherworkers, and scavengers.  The term's meaning evolved to encompass anyone who is marginalized and ostracized.
