fibred:/ˈfaɪbəd/| adj.|纤维状的，有纤维的|Composed of or resembling fibers; containing fibers.|例句：The plant has a fibred root system.  这种植物有纤维状的根系。|近义词：fibrous, filamentous, stringy|反义词:smooth, non-fibrous|词性:形容词
fibred:/ˈfaɪbəd/| v.|形成纤维|To form into fibers.|例句：The material fibred easily when pulled.  这种材料拉扯时很容易形成纤维。|近义词：separate, fray, unravel|反义词:compact, solidify|词性:动词

词源: From fibre + -ed.  From Middle English *fibre, from Old French fibre, from Latin fibra (“fiber, filament”).
