finish-coats: /ˈfɪnɪʃ koʊts/ (US) /ˈfɪnɪʃ kəʊts/ (UK)
|n.|面漆，罩面漆|The final layers of paint or other coating applied to a surface to provide protection and a desired appearance.  These coats are usually designed to be durable, weather-resistant, and aesthetically pleasing.|The carpenter applied two finish-coats of varnish to the table. 木匠在桌子上涂了两层面漆。|Synonyms: topcoats, final coats|Antonyms: base coats, primer coats|noun
finish-coat: /ˈfɪnɪʃ koʊt/ (US) /ˈfɪnɪʃ kəʊt/ (UK)
|n.|面漆，罩面漆|A single layer of the final paint or other coating.|He applied the finish-coat with a fine brush. 他用一把细毛刷涂上了面漆。|Synonyms: topcoat, final coat|Antonyms: base coat, primer coat|noun
