de-automate:/diːˈɔːtəmeɪt/ | v. | 去自动化，减少自动化 | To reduce or eliminate the use of automation in a process or system.  |  Example 1: The factory decided to de-automate some production lines to increase flexibility and create more jobs. 工厂决定去自动化一些生产线，以增加灵活性并创造更多就业机会。

Example 2: Some experts argue that de-automating certain tasks can improve worker engagement and job satisfaction. 一些专家认为，去自动化某些任务可以提高员工的参与度和工作满意度。 | Near Synonyms: manualize, humanize (in the context of work) | Antonyms: automate, mechanize | Verb
