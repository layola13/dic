comforters:/ˈkʌmfərtərz/
n.
安慰者；慰藉物；棉被；羽绒被
a person or thing that comforts someone.  Something used to provide warmth, especially a quilt or duvet.
例句：The children snuggled under their comforters. 孩子们依偎在被窝里。

例句：She was a true comforter to her grieving friend. 她对悲痛的朋友来说是一个真正的安慰者。
近义词：consoler, soother, quilt, duvet, comforter
反义词：afflicter, tormentor
词性:noun
