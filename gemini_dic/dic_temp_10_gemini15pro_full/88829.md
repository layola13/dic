hogging:/ˈhɒɡɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈhɑːɡɪŋ/ (US)| n. v.| 垄断，独占;  (车辆、船只)首尾下垂，中间拱起 |The act of taking or using more than one's share of something; taking or using something greedily, selfishly, or unfairly.  In nautical/automotive contexts, it refers to a condition where the ends of a vessel or vehicle droop lower than the middle.|He was accused of hogging the bathroom. (他被指责霸占了浴室。) |  The ship was hogging badly in the heavy seas. (这艘船在汹涌的大海中严重拱起。) |Synonyms: monopolizing, dominating, hoarding| Antonyms: sharing, distributing | Noun/Verb
