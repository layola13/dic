fanciers:/ˈfænsiərz/| n.|爱好者；饲养者；鉴赏家|People who have a special interest in something, especially a particular breed of animal.|例句：Pigeon fanciers from all over the country came to the show. 全国各地的鸽子爱好者都来参加了展览会。

例句：He is a fancier of antique clocks. 他是一位古董钟表的爱好者。|近义词：enthusiasts, aficionados, connoisseurs, devotees|反义词:critics, detractors|词性:noun (plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "fancy" (meaning to like or admire) + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting a person who does something).  "Fancy" itself comes from Old French "fantaisie" meaning "fantasy, imagination," ultimately from Greek "phantasia."
