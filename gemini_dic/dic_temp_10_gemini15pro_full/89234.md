homies:/ˈhoʊmiz/| n.|哥们儿，老铁，伙伴（复数）|Close friends, pals, buddies.  It's slang, often used in African American Vernacular English (AAVE).  It can also be used more broadly, particularly among young people.|例句：I'm hanging out with my homies tonight. 今晚我要和我的哥们儿们出去玩。|近义词：friends, pals, buddies, mates, dudes|反义词:enemies, foes|noun
