exemplarity:/ɪɡˌzɛmplˈærɪti/ | n. | 模范性；典范性； ejemplaridad (Spanish) |The quality of being exemplary; the state of serving as a desirable model. |Her leadership and dedication are qualities that demonstrate her exemplarity. 她的领导才能和奉献精神展现了她的模范性。

The teacher praised the student's exemplarity in completing the assignment. 老师称赞了学生完成作业的模范性。| 近义词：model behaviour, ideal behaviour, perfection, excellence | 反义词：inadequacy, imperfection, mediocrity | 词性: 名词
