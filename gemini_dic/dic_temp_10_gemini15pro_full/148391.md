oversqueamishness:/ˌoʊvərˈskwiːmɪʃnəs/
n.
过度洁癖; 过分拘谨; 神经过敏
Excessive fastidiousness or sensitivity, especially in matters of taste, propriety, or morality.
She couldn't watch the surgery due to her oversqueamishness. 她因为过度洁癖而无法观看手术。
His oversqueamishness prevented him from enjoying the horror movie. 他过分拘谨使他无法享受这部恐怖电影。

近义词:fastidiousness, prudishness, squeamishness, daintiness
反义词:tolerance, insensitivity, robustness

词性:noun

词源: From over- + squeamish + -ness.  "Squeamish" comes from the Old English *squeamish, perhaps from a Scandinavian source related to Norwegian *skvima "to be nauseated."
