nordicism:/ˈnɔːrdɪˌsɪzəm/ /ˈnɔr-/| n.|北欧种族主义；北欧中心论|The belief that the Nordic race is superior to other races and that Nordic people should have a dominant role in society.  This ideology often emphasizes traits like fairness, tall stature, and intelligence as inherently Nordic and superior.  It has historically been associated with racist and supremacist movements.|He was accused of promoting nordicism in his writings.  他被指控在他的著作中宣扬北欧种族主义。|近义词：racism, racialism, white supremacy|反义词：racial equality, multiculturalism|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Nordic + -ism.  Nordic refers to the people and cultures of Scandinavia and surrounding areas.  The suffix -ism denotes a doctrine, belief system, or ideology.
