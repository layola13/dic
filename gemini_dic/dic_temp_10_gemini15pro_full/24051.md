bregma:/ˈbrɛɡmə/| n. |前囟|The point on the top of the skull where the sagittal and coronal sutures meet; the junction of the coronal and sagittal sutures in the infant skull.|The surgeon made an incision just behind the bregma.  外科医生在前囟后方切开了一个口子。|Synonym: anterior fontanelle|Antonym: N/A|noun
Etymology: From Late Latin bregma, from Ancient Greek βρέγμα (brégma, “top of the head”), from βρέχω (brékhō, “I wet, moisten”).  The word's origin likely refers to the fontanelle, the "soft spot" on a baby's head.|
