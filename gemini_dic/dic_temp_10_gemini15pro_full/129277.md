nodosarine:/ˌnəʊdəʊsəˈriːn/ | adj. | 结节状的；瘤状的 | Resembling or pertaining to Nodosaria, a genus of foraminifera characterized by chambers arranged in a straight or slightly curved line, often resembling a string of beads. |  The microfossils found in the sample were predominantly nodosarine in form. 样本中发现的微化石主要呈结节状。| Near synonyms: nodular, moniliform | Antonym: smooth | Adjective

nodosarine:/ˌnəʊdəʊsəˈriːn/ | n. | 结节虫 | Any foraminifer belonging to the genus Nodosaria. | The nodosarines were abundant in the Cretaceous period.  结节虫在白垩纪时期数量丰富。 | Near synonyms: foraminifer, foraminiferan |  | Noun
