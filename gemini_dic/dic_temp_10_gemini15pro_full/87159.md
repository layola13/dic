herblike:/ˈɜːrblaɪk/
adjective
像草本植物的；草本植物似的
Resembling or characteristic of a herb; herbaceous.
The plant has a delicate, herblike fragrance. 这种植物有一种清淡的草本香味。
The garden is filled with herblike plants. 花园里种满了像草本植物一样的植物。
Synonyms: herbaceous, grassy, weedy
Antonyms: woody, shrubby, treelike
词性: 形容词
