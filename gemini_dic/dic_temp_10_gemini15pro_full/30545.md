cephalopod:/ˈsefələpɒd/ (US) /ˈkɛfələpɒd/ (UK)| n. |头足纲动物|A marine mollusk of the class Cephalopoda, such as an octopus or squid, characterized by well-developed eyes, a ring of sucker-bearing tentacles around the mouth, and the ability to squirt ink.|例句：The giant squid is a fascinating cephalopod. 巨型乌贼是一种迷人的头足纲动物。

例句：Cephalopods are highly intelligent invertebrates. 头足纲动物是高智商的无脊椎动物。


近义词：mollusk, invertebrate|反义词: vertebrate|词性: noun
