disavouch:/ˌdɪsəˈvaʊtʃ/
| vt. |否认，拒绝承认，|To deny responsibility for or connection with; disclaim.|He disavouched any knowledge of the plot. 他否认对该阴谋有任何了解。|
|近义词：deny, repudiate, disclaim, renounce|
|反义词：acknowledge, admit, avow, endorse|
|词性：动词|


disavow:/ˌdɪsəˈvaʊ/
| vt. |否认，拒绝承认，|To deny responsibility for or connection with; disclaim.|The government disavowed the actions of the rogue agent. 政府否认了这个流氓特工的行为。|
|近义词：deny, repudiate, disclaim, renounce|
|反义词：acknowledge, admit, avow, endorse|
|词性：动词|

词源:  Late Middle English: from dis- (expressing reversal) + avowen ‘avow’.  Disvouch is a less common variant of disavow.
