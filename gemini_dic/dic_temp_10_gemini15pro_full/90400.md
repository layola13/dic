howlet:/ˈhaʊlɪt/| n.|猫头鹰（尤指幼小的）|A young owl, especially a barn owl.|例句：The howlet perched silently on the branch.  幼小的猫头鹰静静地栖息在树枝上。

例句：The farmer heard the howlet's mournful cry in the night. 农夫在夜里听到了小猫头鹰悲伤的叫声。|近义词：owlet, barn owl|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *houlet, howlet, from Old English *ūlet, a diminutive of ūle (“owl”), from Proto-Germanic *uwwalǭ (“owl”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂uh₁welo-, *h₂uh₁wleh₂- (“owl”). Cognate with Scots howlet (“owl”), Saterland Frisian Uule (“owl”), West Frisian ûle (“owl”), Dutch uil (“owl”), German Eule (“owl”), Danish ugle (“owl”), Swedish uggla (“owl”), Icelandic ugla (“owl”).
