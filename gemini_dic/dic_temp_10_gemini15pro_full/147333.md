overmournful: /ˌōvərˈmôrnfəl/ | adj. | 过度悲伤的，悲痛欲绝的 | Excessively mournful; grieving too much.  Showing or expressing too much grief. | She was overmournful after the death of her cat. 她对猫的死过度悲伤。 | The overmournful widow wore black for years.  这位悲痛欲绝的寡妇穿了多年的黑衣。 | 近义词: grief-stricken, heartbroken, inconsolable | 反义词: cheerful, joyous, happy | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "mournful" (from "mourn," from Old English *murnan, related to Old High German mornēn "to grieve, mourn," and ultimately of imitative origin)
