colorate: /ˈkʌləreɪt/
verb
着色，给...上色
to add color to something
She colorated the drawing with bright crayons. 她用鲜艳的蜡笔给图画上了色。
近义词: color, tint, dye, stain
反义词: bleach, decolorize
词性: verb

colorate: /ˈkʌləreɪt/
adjective (rarely used)
彩色的，着色的
having color
The butterfly's wings were colorate and vibrant. 蝴蝶的翅膀色彩鲜艳。
近义词: colorful, colored, chromatic
反义词: colorless, achromatic
词性: adjective
