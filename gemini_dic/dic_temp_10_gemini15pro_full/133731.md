nonfugitives:/ˌnɑːnˈfjuːdʒətɪvz/
noun
非逃犯；未逃亡者
People who have not fled from justice or persecution.
The police are questioning several nonfugitives in connection with the robbery.  警方正在就这起抢劫案讯问几个非逃犯。
The program aims to help nonfugitives who are struggling with legal issues.  该计划旨在帮助那些在法律问题上苦苦挣扎的非逃犯。
synonyms: residents, civilians, inhabitants
antonyms: fugitives, escapees, runaways
