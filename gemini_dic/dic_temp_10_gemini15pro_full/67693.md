exuding:/ɪɡˈzjuːdɪŋ/
verb
散发;流露;充分显露
displaying (an emotion or quality) strongly and openly.
The flowers were exuding a sweet fragrance. 这些花散发着甜甜的香味。
Synonyms: emanate, radiate, ooze
Antonyms: absorb, conceal, suppress
verb

exude:/ɪɡˈzjuːd/
verb
散发;流露;充分显露
discharge (moisture or a smell) slowly and steadily.
The tree exuded a sticky resin. 这棵树分泌出粘稠的树脂。
Synonyms: release, emit, secrete
Antonyms: absorb, withhold, retain
verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin exsūdāre (to sweat out), from ex- (out) + sūdāre (to sweat).
