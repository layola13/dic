brick:/brɪk/| n. |砖; 砖块; 积木; 一块状物|A rectangular block of hard material used for building walls and houses. |例句：The house is built of brick. 这房子是用砖砌的。

例句：He threw a brick through the window. 他用砖头砸碎了窗户。|近义词：block, stone|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用materials like wood, steel等)| noun
