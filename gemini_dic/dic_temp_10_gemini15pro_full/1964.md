activates: /ˈæktɪveɪts/ | v. | 激活，启动，触发 |  To make something active or operational; to cause something to start working. |  The enzyme activates the chemical reaction.  这种酶激活了化学反应。

Sunlight activates the solar panels. 阳光激活了太阳能电池板。 | 近义词: trigger, stimulate, start, initiate, launch, switch on | 反义词: deactivate, disable, stop, halt | 词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *activaten, from Late Latin activātus, past participle of activāre "to make active," from Latin activus "active," from actus "act, action" (from the past participle stem of agere "to do, drive, act").

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The button activates the alarm. =  这个按钮启动警报。
The enzyme activates the process. =  这种酶激活这个过程。

一般过去时:
The security guard activated the fire alarm. =  保安启动了火灾警报。
The scientist activated the experiment. =  科学家启动了实验。

现在完成时:
The system has been activated. = 系统已被激活。
The program has activated automatically. = 程序已自动激活。

过去完成时:
The bomb had been activated before the police arrived. =  炸弹在警察到达之前就已经被启动了。
He had activated the system before leaving the building. = 他在离开大楼之前启动了系统。

将来时:
The system will activate automatically at midnight. = 系统将在午夜自动激活。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

The switch didn't activate the light. =  开关没有打开灯。
