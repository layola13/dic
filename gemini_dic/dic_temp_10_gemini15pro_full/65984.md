exalted:/ɪɡˈzɔːltɪd/| adj.|崇高的，高贵的；兴奋的，得意洋洋的|Placed in a high and respected position; elevated in rank, character, or status.  Also, filled with or expressing great joy or triumph.|He felt exalted after winning the championship. 他在赢得冠军后感到无比兴奋。|Synonyms: lofty, noble, elevated, glorified, elated, jubilant|Antonyms: lowly, base, humble, dejected, depressed|adjective

exalt:/ɪɡˈzɔːlt/| vt.|提升，提拔；赞扬，歌颂|To raise in rank, power, or character; to praise highly; glorify.|The king exalted his loyal knight to a higher position. 国王将忠诚的骑士提升到更高的职位。|Synonyms: elevate, promote, praise, laud, glorify|Antonyms: degrade, demote, criticize, condemn|verb
