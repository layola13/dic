fact-checking:/ˈfæktˌtʃekɪŋ/| n.|事实核查，事实查证|The process of verifying the accuracy of information presented as facts, often in news reports or other media.|The journalist spent hours on fact-checking before publishing the story. 记者在发表这篇报道前花了几个小时进行事实核查。|Synonyms: verification, corroboration, authentication|Antonyms: fabrication, falsification|noun

fact-check:/ˈfæktˌtʃek/| vt.|对…进行事实核查|To verify the factual accuracy of something.|Reporters are expected to fact-check their sources. 记者应该核实他们的消息来源。|Synonyms: verify, corroborate, authenticate|Antonyms: fabricate, falsify|verb
