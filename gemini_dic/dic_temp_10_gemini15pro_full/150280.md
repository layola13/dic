panegyrist:/ˈpænɪdʒərɪst/ | n. | 颂扬者，赞美者 | A person who delivers a panegyric; a eulogist.  A person who bestows praise, especially one who does so publicly and formally. |  The panegyrist delivered a moving tribute to the fallen hero.  这位颂扬者向牺牲的英雄致以感人的悼词。| synonyms: eulogist, encomiast, praiser, lauder | antonyms: critic, detractor, censurer | noun

panegyric:/ˌpænɪˈdʒɪrɪk/ | n. adj. | 颂词，赞词；赞美的，颂扬的 |  (n.) A formal speech or piece of writing that praises someone or something highly. (adj.) Of, relating to, or expressing praise. | The poet wrote a panegyric for the king's birthday. 诗人写了一首颂词献给国王的生日。 | synonyms: eulogy, encomium, tribute, accolade | antonyms: diatribe, denunciation, philippic | noun, adjective
