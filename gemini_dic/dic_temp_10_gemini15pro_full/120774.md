misappreciate:/ˌmɪsəˈpriːʃieɪt/
|v.|误解，低估，轻视|To fail to appreciate; to undervalue or underestimate.|She misappreciated his kindness and thought he was trying to manipulate her. 她误解了他的好意，以为他想操纵她。|
|v.|未正确评价|To not value correctly; to have a wrong estimation of.|The critics misappreciated the true value of the artwork.  评论家们错误地估计了这件艺术品的真正价值。
|近义词：undervalue, underestimate, misunderstand, misinterpret, slight|
|反义词：appreciate, value, overestimate, understand|
|词性：verb|


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (meaning "wrongly, badly") + appreciate (from Latin appretiat- "valued, estimated," past participle of appretiare, from ad- "to" + pretium "price").
