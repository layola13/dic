mixup:/ˈmɪksʌp/| n.|混乱，混合，错误|a mistake that causes confusion or difficulty|There was a mixup with our hotel reservation and they didn't have a room for us. 我们的酒店预订出了点差错，他们没有我们的房间。|近义词：confusion, muddle, mess, bungle|反义词:organization, order|名词

mixup:/ˌmɪks ˈʌp/| vt.|混合，弄混|to combine different things together, often by mistake|I mixed up the sugar and the salt. 我把糖和盐弄混了。|近义词：confuse, muddle, jumble, blend|反义词:separate, distinguish|动词
