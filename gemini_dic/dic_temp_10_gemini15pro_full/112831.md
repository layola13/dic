lucific:/luːˈsɪfɪk/ | adj. | Luciferian, bringing light; pertaining to the morning star |  Bringing light, especially relating to the planet Venus as the morning star. Often associated with Lucifer, the fallen angel, and thus sometimes carries connotations of evil or rebellion. | The lucific glow of Venus heralded the dawn. 金星的光芒预示着黎明的到来。|  radiant, luminous, bright | dark, shadowy | adjective
