fireweed:/ˈfaɪəˌwiːd/| n. |火草，柳兰|A tall herbaceous plant (Epilobium angustifolium) with pink-purple flowers, often abundant in areas recently cleared by fire.|The hillsides were covered in fireweed after the forest fire. 山火过后，山坡上长满了柳兰。|近义词：rosebay willowherb|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:
From fire + weed, so named because it is one of the first plants to colonize burned areas. 源于fire（火）+ weed（杂草），因其是最先在火烧区域生长的植物之一而得名。
