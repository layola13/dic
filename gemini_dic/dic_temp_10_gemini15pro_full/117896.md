melopiano:/məˈləʊpiˈænəʊ/ | n. |美乐钢琴; 自动钢琴 |A mechanical piano, typically a player piano, that produces music resembling that of a small orchestra. | The melopiano filled the room with the sounds of a waltz.  美乐钢琴让房间里充满了华尔兹的声音。|Near synonyms: player piano, pianola, orchestrion | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
