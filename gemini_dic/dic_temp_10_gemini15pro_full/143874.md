outpoisoned:/ˌaʊtˈpɔɪzənd/
verb
用毒药胜过；毒害更甚于
to poison someone or something more effectively or to a greater degree than someone or something else.
例句：The Borgias outpoisoned their rivals in Renaissance Italy.  博吉亚家族在文艺复兴时期的意大利用毒药胜过了他们的对手。

例句：The spy was outpoisoned by his own agency.  这名间谍被自己的机构毒害了。
近义词：outdo, surpass, defeat
反义词：succumb to, be defeated by
词性：v.
