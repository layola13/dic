hoopoe:/ˈhʊpuː/ (US) /ˈhuːpuː/ (UK)| n.|戴胜|A bird with a long, pointed bill, a pinkish-brown body, black and white wings, and a large erectile crest.|例句：The hoopoe is a striking bird with a distinctive crest. 戴胜是一种引人注目的鸟，有着独特的羽冠。

例句：We saw a hoopoe foraging for insects in the grass. 我们看到一只戴胜在草地上觅食昆虫。|近义词：None (highly distinctive bird)|反义词:None|词性:noun

Etymology: From Old French *upupa*, from Latin upupa, of imitative origin.
