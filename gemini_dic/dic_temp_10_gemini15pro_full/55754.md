diverless:/ˈdaɪvərlɪs/
adjective
无潜水员的；无需潜水的
Without the need for divers; performed without divers.
例句：Diverless underwater hull cleaning is becoming increasingly popular.  无潜水员的水下船体清洁越来越受欢迎。

例句：They offer diverless shipwreck tours using remotely operated vehicles. 他们提供使用远程操作车辆的无潜水员沉船之旅。

近义词：unmanned, remotely operated
反义词：manned, requiring divers
词性：adjective
