deemphasizing:/ˌdiːˈɛmfəsaɪzɪŋ/
verb
减少…的强调；淡化
reducing the emphasis on something; playing something down
The new CEO is deemphasizing the importance of quarterly profits. 新任首席执行官正在淡化季度利润的重要性。
近义词: downplaying, minimizing, understating
反义词: emphasizing, highlighting, stressing
词性: Verb (present participle of deemphasize)

deemphasize:/ˌdiːˈɛmfəsaɪz/
verb
减少…的强调；淡化
to reduce the emphasis on something; to play something down
The report deemphasized the negative aspects of the project.  这份报告淡化了该项目的负面方面。
近义词: downplay, minimize, understate
反义词: emphasize, highlight, stress
词性: Verb

词源:  "de-" (prefix meaning "down" or "away from") + "emphasize" (from Greek "emphasis" meaning "significance").  The word "emphasize" itself entered English in the 16th century.
