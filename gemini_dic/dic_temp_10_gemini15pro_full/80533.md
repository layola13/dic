goodish:/ˈɡʊdɪʃ/| adj.|相当好的，还不错的|Moderately good; fairly good.  It describes something that is good, but not excellent.|The food was goodish, but not great. 食物还不错，但不算很好。|近义词：decent, respectable, fair, tolerable, passable|反义词:bad, poor, awful, terrible|词性:形容词

词源: good + -ish.  The suffix "-ish" is used to form adjectives meaning "somewhat," "rather," or "approximately."
