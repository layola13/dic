countertraditions:/ˈkaʊntərtrəˈdɪʃənz/
n.
反传统，对立传统
Traditions that oppose or challenge established norms and customs.
The countertraditions of the punk rock movement challenged the mainstream music industry. 朋克摇滚运动的反传统挑战了主流音乐产业。
Synonyms: counterculture, subculture, nonconformity
Antonyms: traditions, customs, norms
noun
