distastefully:/dɪsˈteɪstfəli/
adverb
令人厌恶地，讨厌地
in a way that shows a lack of good taste and causes offence
He spoke distastefully about his ex-wife. 他用令人厌恶的口吻谈论他的前妻。
Synonyms: offensively, objectionably, vulgarly, crudely, indelicately
Antonyms: tastefully, respectfully, politely
词性: 副词
