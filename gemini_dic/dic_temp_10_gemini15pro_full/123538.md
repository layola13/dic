monotype:/ˈmɒnətaɪp/ (US) /ˈmɑːnətaɪp/| n. adj.|单一字体，单版印刷|A single letter or character, or a typeface or font designed as a single unit, not as a set of separate characters.  Also, a print made using a monotype process.|The artist created a beautiful monotype using oil-based inks.|近义词:single typeface, unique print|反义词: typeface, font|noun, adjective

monotype:/ˈmɒnətaɪp/ (US) /ˈmɑːnətaɪp/| n.|单版印刷|A print made using a monotype process.|The museum acquired a rare monotype by Degas.|近义词:unique print|反义词: editioned print|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From mono- (one) + -type (type).  "Mono-" comes from Greek "monos" (single, alone). "-type" ultimately derives from Greek "typos" (impression, mark).
