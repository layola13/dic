equidistantly:/ˌiːkwɪˈdɪstəntli/
adverb
等距地
at equal distances.

The trees were planted equidistantly along the avenue.
这些树沿着林荫道等距种植。

The points are equidistantly spaced along the line.
这些点沿着线等距分布。

近义词: evenly spaced, uniformly
反义词: unevenly, irregularly
词性: adverb

词源:
From Latin *aequidistant- (stem of aequidistans) 'equidistant,' from aequi- 'equally' + distant- (stem of distans) 'distant,' present participle of distare 'stand apart.'
