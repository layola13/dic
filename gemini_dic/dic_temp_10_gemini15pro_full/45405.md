cuplike:/ˈkʌplaɪk/| adj.|杯状的，像杯子的|Shaped like a cup; resembling a cup.|The flower had delicate, cuplike petals.  这朵花有着精致的杯状花瓣。|The fruit has a hard, cuplike shell. 这种水果有一个坚硬的杯状外壳。|近义词：cup-shaped, concave|反义词：convex|词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "cup" + "-like". "Cup" comes from Old English "cuppe," from Late Latin "cuppa," of uncertain origin, possibly related to "cupa" (tub, cask). "-like" is a suffix meaning "resembling," from Old English "-lic."
