concisest:/kənˈsaɪsɪst/| adj.|最简洁的，最简明的|Expressing much in few words; brief and to the point. |This is the concisest explanation I can give. 这是我能给出的最简洁的解释。|近义词：brief, succinct, terse, compact|反义词：verbose, wordy, prolix, lengthy|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *concisus*, past participle of *concidere* "to cut off, cut short," from *com-* "together" + *caedere* "to cut."
