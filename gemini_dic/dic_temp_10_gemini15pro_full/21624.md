blurbing:/ˈblɜːrbɪŋ/ | n. | 吹捧；大肆宣传；推荐 | The act of writing or providing blurbs, short promotional descriptions, typically for a book, film, or other product. |  Example 1: The author spent hours blurbing his new novel on social media.  作者花了几个小时在社交媒体上宣传他的新小说。

 Example 2: The publisher asked several famous authors for blurbing to help promote the book.  出版商请了几位著名作家为这本书写推荐语。 | synonyms: promoting, publicizing, hyping, praising, commending | antonyms: criticizing, panning, condemning | noun

blurbs:/ˈblɜːrbz/ | n. |  推荐语；简介；吹捧性广告 | A short promotional description, as found on the back of a book or in a film advertisement. | Example 1: The blurbs on the back of the book made it sound incredibly exciting. 书背面的推荐语让这本书听起来非常精彩。

Example 2:  The movie's blurbs promised a thrilling adventure. 这部电影的宣传语承诺了一场惊险的冒险。 | synonyms: endorsements, testimonials, puffs, plugs | antonyms: criticisms, slates | noun
