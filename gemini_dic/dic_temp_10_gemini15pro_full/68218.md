fairhood:/ˈfeərhʊd/
noun
公平；正直
The quality of being fair and just.
He acted with fairness and fairhood throughout the process. 他在整个过程中行事公平公正。
近义词：fairness, justice, equity, impartiality
反义词：unfairness, injustice, bias, partiality
词性:n.

Etymology:
From "fair" (meaning just and equitable) + "-hood" (a suffix denoting a state, condition, or quality).  "Fair" comes from Old English "fæger" (beautiful, pleasing), which evolved to also mean morally good and just. "-hood" comes from Old English "-hād".
