arboreally:/ɑːrˈbɔːriəli/
adjective
树栖地；在树上
Living in trees; of or relating to trees.
Monkeys are arboreal creatures, spending most of their lives in the trees. 猴子是树栖动物，一生中的大部分时间都在树上度过。
Sloths are adapted for an arboreal existence.  树懒适应了树栖生活。
近义词:tree-dwelling, tree-living
反义词:terrestrial, ground-dwelling
词性:adjective
词源: From Latin *arboreus, from arbor "tree."
