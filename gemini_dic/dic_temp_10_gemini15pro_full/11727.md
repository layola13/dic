aroint:/əˈrɔɪnt/ | interj. | 走开；滚开；去你的 |  An archaic exclamation used to drive away or ward off evil spirits or unwanted persons.  It expresses a strong command to depart. | Aroint thee, witch! 滚开，女巫！| Get away, begone, depart | approach, welcome | interjection
Etymology->
From Middle English *aroynten*, probably from Old English *āryman*  "to drive out," related to *ūt* "out."
