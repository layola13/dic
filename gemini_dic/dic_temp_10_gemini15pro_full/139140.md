obesenesses:/oʊˈbiːsɪtɪz/ /əʊˈbiːsɪtɪz/
n.
肥胖症，过度肥胖（通常指复数形式，表示不同类型的或多例肥胖症）
The study examined the genetic factors contributing to childhood obesenesses. 这项研究调查了导致儿童肥胖症的遗传因素。
obesenesses is the plural form of obesity, referring to the condition of being excessively overweight.  肥胖症是肥胖的复数形式，指的是过度超重的状态。
例句：
Childhood obesenesses are on the rise in many developed countries.  许多发达国家的儿童肥胖症正在上升。

The conference addressed the growing problem of obesenesses worldwide.  会议讨论了全球日益严重的肥胖问题。

近义词：obesities, excessive weight problems, morbid obesity (for extreme cases)
反义词：thinness, slenderness, leanness
词性：名词
