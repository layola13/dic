frostinesses:/ˈfrɔːstɪnɪsɪz/
n.
寒冷；冷淡；霜冻
The state or quality of being frosty, in any sense of the word.
There was a distinct frostiness in her voice. 她的声音里明显带着冷淡。
chilliness, coldness, iciness
warmth, cordiality
