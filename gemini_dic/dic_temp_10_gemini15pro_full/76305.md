gabbro:/ˈɡæbroʊ/| n.|辉长岩|A dark, coarse-grained igneous rock, composed essentially of plagioclase and pyroxene.|Gabbro is often used in construction. 辉长岩常用于建筑。|Gabbro forms much of the oceanic crust. 辉长岩构成了大部分的海洋地壳。|近义词：dolerite, basalt|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific rock type)|词性:noun
