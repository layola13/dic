macro:/ˈmækroʊ/ (US) /ˈmækrəʊ/ (UK)| adj. n.|宏观的，宏指令；宏|A macro is something that relates to large units or processes, or is large in scale.  A macro is also a single instruction that expands automatically into a set of instructions to perform a particular task.|The government's macro economic policy is designed to control inflation. 政府的宏观经济政策旨在控制通货膨胀。|He wrote a macro to automate the process. 他编写了一个宏来使这个过程自动化。|近义词：large-scale, overall, global|反义词：micro, small-scale|词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From Greek *makrós* meaning "long," "large."
