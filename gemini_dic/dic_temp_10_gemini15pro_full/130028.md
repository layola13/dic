non-osmotic:/ˌnɒn ɒzˈmɒtɪk/ | adj. | 非渗透性的 | Not relating to or involving osmosis, the process by which solvents pass through a semipermeable membrane from a less concentrated solution into a more concentrated one, equalizing the concentrations on each side of the membrane. | The transport of some ions across the cell membrane is non-osmotic.  一些离子穿过细胞膜的运输是非渗透性的。 | non-diffusible, non-permeable | osmotic | adjective
