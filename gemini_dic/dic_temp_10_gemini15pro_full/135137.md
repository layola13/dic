nonoffer:/ˈnɒnˌɒfər/ |n.| 非报价; 未提出要约;  |A statement or action that does not constitute a formal offer, especially in a legal or business context. It might be an invitation to treat, an expression of interest, or preliminary negotiations.| The seller's initial price quote was considered a nonoffer, merely an invitation to negotiate. 卖方最初的报价被认为是非报价，仅仅是邀请谈判。 | synonyms:  no bid, no proposal, declination, rejection | antonyms: offer, bid, proposal | noun
