comforted: /ˈkʌmfərtɪd/
comforted: v. |安慰; 慰藉| To make someone feel less sad, worried, or disappointed. |例句：She comforted him with a hug. 她用拥抱安慰他。|近义词：solaced, consoled, reassured, cheered|反义词：distressed, upset, saddened|词性: 动词(过去分词)

comforted: /ˈkʌmfərtɪd/
comforted: adj. |感到安慰的|Feeling less sad, worried, or disappointed. |例句：I felt comforted by her words. 她的话让我感到安慰。|近义词：solaced, consoled, reassured, cheered|反义词：distressed, upset, saddened|词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *comforten*, from Old French *conforter*, from Late Latin *confortāre*, from Latin *com-* (“together”) + *fortāre* (“to strengthen”), from *fortis* (“strong”).
