cordal:/ˈkɔːrdl/| adj.|衷心的，诚挚的；亲切的|Relating to the heart; heartfelt; warm and sincere.|例句：She sent a cordal message of support. 她发了一条诚挚的支持信息。

例句：He offered a cordal welcome to the visitors. 他对来访者表示了热烈的欢迎。|近义词：hearty, warm, sincere, heartfelt, genuine|反义词：cold, insincere, unfriendly|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *cordalis, from Latin cord-, cor heart.
