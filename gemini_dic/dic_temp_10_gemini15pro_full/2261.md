adelocodonic: /ˌædɪˌloʊkəˈdɒnɪk/ /ˌædɪˌloʊkəˈdɑːnɪk/
adjective
腺齿的
Relating to or possessing adelocodonts (teeth with rounded or conical cusps).  Specifically refers to a type of molar tooth structure found in certain mammals, particularly some extinct groups.
The adelocodonic molars of the early mammal provided a crushing surface for its diet.  早期哺乳动物的腺齿臼齿为其饮食提供了压碎表面。

The paleontologist identified the fossil as belonging to an adelocodonic mammal.  古生物学家将这块化石鉴定为属于一种腺齿哺乳动物。
