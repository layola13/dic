overcoming:/ˌoʊvərˈkʌmɪŋ/| n. / adj.|克服；战胜|The act of successfully dealing with a problem or difficulty.  The process of getting the better of a feeling or condition.|例句：Overcoming his shyness, he finally asked her to dance. 克服了他的害羞，他终于邀请她跳舞。|近义词：conquering, defeating, surmounting, mastering|反义词:succumbing, yielding, surrendering|词性: Noun/Adjective (present participle of 'overcome')

overcome:/ˌoʊvərˈkʌm/| vt.|克服；战胜|Successfully deal with a problem or difficulty. Get the better of a feeling or condition.|例句：She overcame her fear of flying. 她克服了对飞行的恐惧。|近义词：conquer, defeat, surmount, master|反义词:succumb, yield, surrender|词性: Verb

词源: From Middle English *overcomen, from Old English *ofercuman (“to overcome, surpass, conquer, prevail over”), from ofer (“over”) + cuman (“to come”).  Cognate with Dutch overkomen (“to happen, befall”), German überkommen (“to receive, get”), Swedish överkomma (“to overcome”).
