howsoever:/ˌhaʊsoʊˈevər/| adv.| 无论如何；不管怎样|  No matter how; in whatever manner or way. |He declared his intention to go on with his research howsoever long it took. 他宣称无论要花多长时间，他都要继续他的研究。| 近义词: however, nevertheless, nonetheless, anyhow, anyway | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，表示限制或特定条件的词可以视为相对反义，例如: specifically, particularly) | 词性: 副词

PHRASE-> (Howsoever is not typically used in phrases. It's a formal and somewhat archaic word.  The following are examples of phrases using "however" which is more common.)
However much it costs, I'll buy it. = 无论它多少钱，我都会买。
However long it takes, I will finish it. = 无论需要多长时间，我都会完成它。
However you look at it, it's a problem. = 无论你怎么看，这都是个问题。
However hard I try, I can't seem to do it. = 无论我多么努力，我似乎都做不到。
However, I must disagree with you on this point. = 但是，在这一点上我必须不同意你的看法。
