evocativeness:/ɪˈvɑːkətɪvnəs/
n.
唤起性；引起回忆的特性；令人回味的特质
The quality of evoking strong images, memories, or feelings.
The evocativeness of the old house made her feel nostalgic. 老房子的怀旧气息让她感到伤感。
近义词：suggestiveness, reminiscence, resonance
反义词：blandness, dullness
词性：名词

Etymology:
From evocative + -ness.  Evocative comes from Late Latin ēvocātīvus, from ēvocāre "to call out," from ex- "out" + vocāre "to call."
