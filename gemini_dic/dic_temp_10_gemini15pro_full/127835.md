nemertean:/nɪˈmɜːrtiən/ | adj. n. | 纽形动物的；纽形动物 |Relating to or denoting the nemerteans, a phylum of invertebrate worms, also called ribbon worms. | Nemertean worms are found in marine, freshwater, and terrestrial environments.  纽形虫存在于海洋、淡水和陆地环境中。|  The nemertean stretched out its proboscis to capture prey. 这只纽形虫伸出它的吻来捕捉猎物。 |  近义词: ribbon worm | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From Modern Latin *Nemertina*, from *Nemertes*, the name of a sea nymph in Greek mythology.
