ETYMOLOGY->
From institution + -ize + -ed.  "Institution" comes from the Latin "institutio" meaning "establishment" or "instruction." The suffix "-ize" forms verbs meaning "to make or become," and "-ed" creates the past participle form, used here as an adjective.
