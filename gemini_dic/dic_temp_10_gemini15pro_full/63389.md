entosternum:/ɛntoʊˈstɜːrnəm/
noun
内胸骨，腹内骨板
A skeletal plate on the ventral side within the body cavity of certain arthropods.
Insects have an entosternum that serves as an attachment point for muscles. 昆虫的内胸骨是肌肉的附着点。
The entosternum provides structural support for the insect's thorax. 内胸骨为昆虫的胸部提供结构支撑。
synonym: endosternite, furca
antonym: (No direct antonym, as it's a specific anatomical feature)
n.
