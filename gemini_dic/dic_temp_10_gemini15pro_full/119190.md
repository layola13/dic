mezzotinto:/ˌmetsəˈtɪntoʊ/| n.|美柔色调版画；美柔色调版画复制品|A method of engraving on copper or steel by roughening the entire surface and then scraping and burnishing the areas intended to show as light. A print made using this method. |例句：The artist was known for his masterful mezzotints.  这位艺术家以其精湛的美柔色调版画而闻名。

例句：The gallery exhibited a collection of 18th-century mezzotints.  画廊展出了一批18世纪的美柔色调版画。|近义词：engraving, etching|反义词:|词性:noun
