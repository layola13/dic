cristallology:/ˌkrɪstəˈlɒlədʒi/
n.
晶体学
The study of crystals, their forms, structures, and properties.
Crystallography plays a vital role in understanding the properties of materials. 晶体学在理解材料特性方面起着至关重要的作用。
The scientist dedicated his life to the study of cristallology.  这位科学家毕生致力于晶体学研究。
近义词: crystallography
反义词: None
