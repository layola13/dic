malnutritious:/ˌmælnuˈtrɪʃəs/ | adj. | 营养不良的 | Suffering from malnutrition; not receiving enough nourishment. |  The malnutritious child was thin and weak.  营养不良的孩子又瘦又弱。| Synonyms: undernourished, underfed, starved | Antonyms: well-nourished, healthy, nourished | adjective

malnutritious:/ˌmælnuˈtrɪʃəs/ | adj. | 营养不良的 | Lacking proper nutrition; not providing adequate nourishment. | The malnutritious diet caused health problems.  营养不良的饮食导致了健康问题。 | Synonyms: unhealthy, inadequate, deficient | Antonyms: nutritious, wholesome, healthy | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From mal- (bad, ill) + nutritious.  "Mal-" comes from Latin "malus" meaning "bad." "Nutritious" comes from Latin "nutrire" meaning "to nourish."
