papillomatoses: /ˌpæpɪləʊməˈtəʊsiːz/ /ˌpæpɪloʊməˈtoʊsiːz/
noun
乳头状瘤病；乳头瘤病变
The presence of multiple papillomas.  A condition characterized by the development of numerous papillomas.
Example:  Cutaneous papillomatoses can be caused by various viral infections. 皮肤乳头状瘤病可能是由各种病毒感染引起的。
Example:  He was diagnosed with respiratory papillomatoses. 他被诊断患有呼吸道乳头状瘤病。
Near Synonyms: papillomas, warts, polyposis
Opposite:  absence of papillomas, healthy tissue
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From papilla (Latin: nipple) + -oma (Greek: tumor, swelling) + -tosis (Greek: condition, formation) + -es (English plural suffix).
