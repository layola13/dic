bamboozle:/bæmˈbuːzl/ | v. | 欺骗，迷惑，哄骗 | To trick or deceive someone, often by confusing them. |  The magician bamboozled the audience with his clever tricks. 魔术师用他巧妙的戏法迷惑了观众。

The salesman bamboozled her into buying a car she couldn't afford.  推销员哄骗她买了一辆她买不起的车。| 近义词: deceive, trick, hoodwink, dupe, confound, bewilder | 反义词: enlighten, clarify | 词性: 动词
