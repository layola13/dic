orthotectonic: /ˌɔːrθoʊˈtɛktɒnɪk/ /ˌɔːrθoʊˈtɛktɑːnɪk/
orthotectonic: adj.
orthotectonic: 正构造的，地壳上升运动的
orthotectonic: Relating to or designating tectonic movements of the earth's crust that tend to elevate the land surfaces.  Characterized by vertical uplift rather than folding or compression.
orthotectonic: The orthotectonic movements in the region resulted in the formation of high plateaus.  该地区的正构造运动导致了高原的形成。
orthotectonic: 近义词:  uplifting, elevational
orthotectonic: 反义词:  downdropping, subsiding
orthotectonic: 词性: adjective
