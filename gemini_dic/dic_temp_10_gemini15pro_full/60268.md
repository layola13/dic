egalitarian: /ɪˌɡælɪˈtɛəriən/ | adj. n. | 平等主义的；平等主义者 | Believing in the principle that all people are equal and deserve equal rights and opportunities. |  Example:  She holds egalitarian views on marriage. 她对婚姻持有平等主义的观点。| Example: The movement promotes an egalitarian society.  这场运动提倡一个平等主义的社会。 | Synonyms: equitable, fair, just, impartial | Antonyms: elitist, hierarchical, discriminatory | adjective, noun

egalitarian: /ɪˌɡælɪˈtɛəriən/ | n. | 平等主义者 | A person who advocates or supports egalitarian principles. | Example: He is a staunch egalitarian. 他是一位坚定的平等主义者。| Example: Egalitarians believe in equal opportunities for everyone. 平等主义者相信每个人都应该有平等的机会。| Synonyms:  equalitarian | Antonyms: elitist, snob | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *égalitaire*, from *égal* (“equal”) (from Latin *aequalis*) + *-itaire* (from Latin *-itarius*).
