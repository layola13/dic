downturn:/ˈdaʊntɜːrn/| n.|衰退，下降，低迷|A decline in economic, business, or other activity.|The recent economic downturn has led to many job losses. 最近的经济衰退导致许多人失业。|近义词：decline, slump, recession|反义词:upturn, boom, increase|词性:名词
Etymology: down (adv.) + turn (n.)  From "down" meaning a descending direction and "turn" signifying a change in direction or condition, hence a turn towards a lower state.
