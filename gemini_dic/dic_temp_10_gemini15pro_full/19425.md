bickerment: /ˈbɪkərmənt/
noun
争吵，口角
The act of bickering; a quarrel.
Example: The constant bickerment between the siblings was driving their parents crazy.  兄弟姐妹之间不断的争吵让他们的父母快要疯了。

Example:  The bickerment over who would do the dishes lasted longer than the actual washing up. 关于谁洗碗的争吵比实际洗碗的时间还要长。

近义词: arguing, quarreling, squabbling, wrangling
反义词: agreement, harmony, concord, peace
词性: noun


词源: From the verb "bicker" + "-ment" (suffix forming nouns of action or result). "Bicker" comes from  probably of imitative origin, suggesting repeated sharp sounds.
