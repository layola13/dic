nerved:/nɜːrvd/
| adj. | 勇敢的，有勇气的；紧张的 | Having courage or determination; strengthened and prepared to face a challenge or ordeal; also, tense or nervous. | She felt nerved for the upcoming competition. 她对即将到来的比赛感到充满了勇气。 |  He was nerved by the support of his family. 他因家人的支持而鼓起勇气. | synonyms: emboldened, strengthened, fortified, braced, steeled, resolute, determined; tense, anxious, edgy  | antonyms: cowardly, timid, fearful, apprehensive, daunted, disheartened; relaxed, calm, composed | adjective
