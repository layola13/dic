leapfrogged:/ˌliːpˈfrɒɡd/ /ˌliːpˈfrɑːɡd/| v. |跳跃式发展，超越|To surpass or overtake someone or something suddenly, often by a large margin.  It implies a quick and significant advancement, often bypassing intermediate steps or stages.|例句：Company A leapfrogged its competitors by investing heavily in new technology.  A公司通过大力投资新技术，实现了对竞争对手的跨越式发展。

例句：The small startup leapfrogged the established giants in the market. 这家小型初创公司在市场上超越了老牌巨头。|近义词：surpass, overtake, outstrip, bypass|反义词: lag behind, fall behind|词性:动词

leapfrogging:/ˌliːpˈfrɒɡɪŋ/ /ˌliːpˈfrɑːɡɪŋ/| n. |跳跃式发展，超越（名词形式）|The act of leapfrogging, or progressing rapidly past others.|例句：The company's leapfrogging of its rivals surprised many industry analysts. 该公司对其竞争对手的超越让许多行业分析师感到惊讶。

例句：Leapfrogging in technological development is crucial for emerging economies.  对于新兴经济体而言，技术发展的跳跃式发展至关重要。|近义词：surpassing, overtaking, outstripping|反义词: lagging, falling behind|词性:名词
