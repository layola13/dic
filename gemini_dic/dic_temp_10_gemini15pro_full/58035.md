droil:/drɔɪl/| v.|苦干，辛苦工作|To work hard and steadily; to toil.|He droiled in the fields all day. 他整天在田里辛苦劳作。|近义词: toil, slave, labor, drudge|反义词: rest, relax|词性: 动词

Etymology:
From Middle English *droilen, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old Norse *drýgja (“to delay, prolong”), or to Dutch *druilen (“to be slow, to dawdle”). The meaning shifted from "to delay" or "to be slow" to "to work slowly and steadily" and then to "to work hard."
