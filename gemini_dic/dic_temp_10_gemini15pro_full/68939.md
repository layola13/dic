fated:/ˈfeɪtɪd/| adj.|注定的，命中注定的|Governed by fate; destined. |例句：Their love was fated to end tragically. 他们的爱情注定以悲剧收场。|例句：The fated ship sank to the bottom of the ocean. 那艘注定要沉的船沉到了海底。|近义词：destined, doomed, preordained|反义词：fortuitous, accidental|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *faten, from Old English *fatian (“to decree, destine, assign”), from Proto-Germanic *fatōną (“to grasp, seize”), from Proto-Indo-European *peh₂- (“to protect, shepherd”).  Related to Old English fetian (“to fetch”). More at fetch. The sense development is from “assigned by fate” to “doomed.”
