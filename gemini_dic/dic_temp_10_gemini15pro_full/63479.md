entrusting:/ɪnˈtrʌstɪŋ/
|v.|委托；交付|The act of assigning responsibility or authority to someone.|例句：He is entrusting his children to his sister's care while he's away.  他离家期间把孩子托付给姐姐照顾。

例句：Entrusting the project to such a young team was a risk, but they proved themselves capable.  把这个项目委托给这么年轻的团队是一个冒险，但他们证明了自己有能力。|近义词：assigning, committing, confiding, delegating|反义词：withholding, keeping, retaining|词性：动词

Etymology:
From Middle English *entristen*, from Old French *entraistre*, from *en-* + *traistre* (“to hand over”), from Latin *trādere* (“to hand over, deliver, surrender”), from *trans-* (“across”) + *dare* (“to give”).
