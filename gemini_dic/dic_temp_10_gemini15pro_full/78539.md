ghostings:/ˈɡoʊstɪŋz/
noun
故意不回复信息，玩失踪
The act of suddenly ceasing all communication with someone (such as a former romantic partner) without explanation.
She was upset by his ghostings and wondered what she had done wrong. 她被他玩失踪感到很不安，想知道自己做错了什么。
Ignoring someone's calls and messages is a form of ghosting.  不接别人的电话，不回信息是一种玩失踪的表现。
synonyms: ignoring, blanking, cold-shouldering, silent treatment
antonyms: communicating, responding, engaging
