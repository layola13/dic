consulate: /ˈkɒnsjʊlət/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsələt/ (US)| n. |领事馆；领事职务；领事任期|A consulate is the office of a consul, especially in a foreign city, dealing with the affairs of their own citizens there. |The US consulate in Chengdu was closed in 2020. 美国驻成都领事馆于2020年关闭。

The consulate issued a new visa for her. 领事馆给她签发了新的签证。|近义词:embassy |反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:noun


consular: /ˈkɒnsjʊlə(r)/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsələr/ (US) | adj. |领事的；领事馆的|Relating to a consul or consulate. |Consular services are available to citizens abroad.  领事服务面向海外公民。

He holds a consular position in the embassy. 他在使馆担任领事职务。|近义词: diplomatic |反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:adjective



ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *consulatt, from Old French consulate or Medieval Latin consulatus, from Latin consul  "consul" + -atus suffix.
