outbringing:/ˈaʊtbrɪŋɪŋ/| n.|教养，养育，出身|The way in which a child is raised and educated, and the effects this has on their character and beliefs. |Her privileged upbringing gave her a distinct advantage in life. 她优越的出身给了她明显的生活优势。|

例句：
His rural upbringing instilled in him a strong work ethic.  他乡村的成长环境使他养成了强烈的职业道德。

His upbringing shaped his entire life. 他的教养塑造了他的一生。


近义词：upbringing, raising, rearing, education, background, nurture
反义词: neglect

词性: noun
