ETYMOLOGY->
From addictive + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  "Addictive" comes from the Latin "addictus," meaning "assigned to" or "bound to," which later evolved to mean "devoted or addicted to."  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality. "-es" is a plural suffix.
