cochleae:/ˈkɒkliːiː/
noun
耳蜗（复数）
The plural form of cochlea, referring to the spiral cavity of the inner ear that contains the organ of Corti, which is responsible for hearing.
The cochleae of mammals are highly developed. 哺乳动物的耳蜗高度发达。
Near synonyms: inner ears (general), auditory organs
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a body part)
