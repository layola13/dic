disincarceration:/ˌdɪsɪnˌkɑːrsəˈreɪʃən/
n.
释放，出狱，减少监禁
The act of releasing someone from prison or the policy of reducing the number of people in prison.
The report advocated for policies of disincarceration to address the overcrowded prisons.  这份报告主张采取减少监禁的政策来解决监狱人满为患的问题。
The disincarceration of non-violent offenders has been a topic of debate.  释放非暴力犯罪者一直是一个争论的话题。
近义词：release, liberation, freeing
反义词：imprisonment, incarceration, confinement
词性：名词

词源：
dis- (prefix meaning "reversal, removal") + incarcerate (verb meaning "to imprison") + -ion (suffix forming nouns of action or condition)
