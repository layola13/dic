convulse:/kənˈvʌls/| v.| 抽搐，痉挛，剧烈震动 |To shake violently and uncontrollably; to cause a sudden, violent disturbance.|The earthquake convulsed the city. 地震使整个城市剧烈震动。|Synonyms: shake, tremble, shudder, quake, spasm|Antonyms: calm, soothe, steady, stabilize|verb

convulsed:/kənˈvʌlst/| adj.| 震动的，痉挛的 |Affected by convulsions; thrown into violent spasms.|His body was convulsed with laughter. 他笑得浑身抽搐。|Synonyms: shaken, trembling, shuddering, quivering|Antonyms: calm, composed, still, steady|adjective
