morbidity:/mɔːrˈbɪdɪti/ /mɔːrˈbɪdəti/
n.
发病率，病态，不健全
The state of being diseased or unhealthy; the incidence of disease in a population.
The morbidity rate for diabetes is increasing. 糖尿病的发病率正在上升。

近义词:sickness, illness, unhealthiness
反义词:health, wellness

词性:noun

词源:Late Middle English: from late Latin morbiditas, from morbidus ‘diseased’ (see morbid).
