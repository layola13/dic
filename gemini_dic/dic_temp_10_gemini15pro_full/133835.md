nongovernance: /ˌnɒnˈɡʌvənəns/ | n. | 非治理；缺乏治理 | The absence or lack of governance; a state where rules, norms, or structures are missing or ineffective. |  The collapse of the regime led to a period of nongovernance and widespread chaos. 政权的垮台导致了一段时间的非治理和普遍的混乱。

The nongovernance in the region allowed criminal organizations to flourish. 该地区的非治理使得犯罪组织得以猖獗。 | Near synonyms: misgovernance, lack of governance, anarchy, lawlessness | Antonyms: governance, order, rule of law | noun
