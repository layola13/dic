bandoliered:/ˌbændəˈlɪrd/
adjective
佩带子弹带的，斜挎子弹带的
wearing a bandolier or bandoliers; equipped with a bandolier.
The bandoliered soldier stood guard.  佩带子弹带的士兵站岗。
The revolutionary, bandoliered and armed, stood defiantly.  佩带子弹带、全副武装的革命者，傲然挺立。
Synonyms: armed, equipped, accoutered
Antonyms: unarmed, unequipped
词性: adjective
