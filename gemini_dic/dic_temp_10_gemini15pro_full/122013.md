misstate:/ˌmɪsˈsteɪt/
|v.|错误陈述；谎报|To state something wrongly or inaccurately, often unintentionally.|He misstated the company's earnings in the report. 他在报告中错误地陈述了公司的收益。|Synonyms: misrepresent, falsify, distort|Antonyms: state correctly, represent accurately|verb

misstatement:/ˌmɪsˈsteɪtmənt/
|n.|错误的陈述；谎报|A statement that is wrong or inaccurate, often made unintentionally.|The financial report contained a serious misstatement.  财务报告中包含一个严重的错误陈述。|Synonyms: misrepresentation, falsehood, inaccuracy|Antonyms: correct statement, accurate representation|noun
