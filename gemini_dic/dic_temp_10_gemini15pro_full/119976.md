miller:/ˈmɪlər/ | n. | 磨坊主；经营磨坊的人；铣床操作工 | A person who owns or operates a mill, especially a flour mill or a grain mill.  Also, a person who operates a milling machine. | The miller ground the wheat into flour. 磨坊主把小麦磨成了面粉。| The miller adjusted the settings on the milling machine. 铣床操作工调整了铣床的设置。 | 近义词: grinder, mill operator | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

词源:
From Middle English *millere*, from Old English *mylnere*, from *mylen* (“mill”) + *-ere* (agent suffix). Cognate with Dutch *molenaar*, German *Müller*, Danish *møller*. Ultimately from Latin *mola* (“millstone”).
