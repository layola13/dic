countertradition:/ˈkaʊntərtrəˈdɪʃən/
noun
反传统，对抗传统
A set of beliefs or practices that challenge or oppose established traditions.
The artist's work represents a countertradition to the prevailing artistic norms of the time.  这位艺术家的作品代表了对当时主流艺术规范的反传统。
Her lifestyle is a countertradition to the expectations of her family. 她的生活方式与她家人的期望反传统。
Synonyms: antithesis, opposition, rebellion, subversion
Antonyms: tradition, convention, orthodoxy
