overbragged: /ˌoʊvərˈbræɡd/
verb
过度吹嘘，夸大其词
bragged excessively about one's accomplishments or qualities.
He overbragged about his new car, making everyone feel uncomfortable. 他过度吹嘘他的新车，让每个人都感到不舒服。
She overbragged about her cooking skills, and the dish turned out to be quite bland. 她过度吹嘘自己的厨艺，结果这道菜却很淡而无味。
近义词: boasted, exaggerated, gloated
反义词: downplayed, understated
词性: verb (past tense of overbrag)
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning excessively) + "brag" (verb meaning to boast)
