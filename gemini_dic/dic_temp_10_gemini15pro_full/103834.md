joying:/ˈdʒɔɪɪŋ/| v.|享受，喜爱|Derived from the verb "joy," meaning to experience great pleasure or happiness.  "Joying" is the present participle form, suggesting an ongoing state of enjoyment.|She was joying her new freedom after graduating college. 她大学毕业后享受着新的自由。|近义词：enjoying, relishing, savoring|反义词:disliking, hating|词性:动词
