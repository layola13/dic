corollary: /ˈkɔːrəˌlɛri/ (US), /kəˈrɒləri/ (UK)| n. |推论，必然结果|A proposition that follows from (and is often appended to) one already proved. A natural consequence or result.|例句：The high cost of housing is a corollary of limited land supply.  高昂的房价是土地供应有限的必然结果。

例句：One corollary of his theory is that we're all ultimately responsible for our own unhappiness. 他的理论的一个推论是我们最终都要为自己的不幸负责。

近义词：consequence, result, deduction, inference|反义词: cause, origin, source |词性: noun
