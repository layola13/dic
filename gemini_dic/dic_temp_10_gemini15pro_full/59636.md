ecclesiologist:/ɪˌkliːziˈɒlədʒɪst/
n.
教会学家，教堂建筑学家
A specialist in the study of church architecture and history, especially Christian churches.
The ecclesiologist pointed out the unique features of the medieval cathedral's stained glass.  教会学家指出了中世纪大教堂彩色玻璃的独特之处。
近义词:church historian, architectural historian
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun
