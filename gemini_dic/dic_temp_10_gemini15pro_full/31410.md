charging:/ˈtʃɑːrdʒɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈtʃɑːrdʒɪŋ/ (US)
noun|充电；收费；冲锋；装料|the process of supplying electrical power to a battery or battery-powered device. Also, the act of demanding an amount of money for goods or services.|例句：The charging of electric vehicles is becoming more convenient. 电动汽车的充电变得越来越方便了。|近义词：loading, fueling, powering, billing|反义词：discharging, draining, depletion|n.

charging:/ˈtʃɑːrdʒɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈtʃɑːrdʒɪŋ/ (US)
adjective|充满活力的；令人兴奋的；收费的| exciting; thrilling; relating to the act of charging a fee.|例句：The charging rhinoceros was a terrifying sight.  狂奔的犀牛令人望而生畏。|近义词：exciting, thrilling, stimulating, invigorating|反义词：boring, dull, tedious, unexciting|adj.
