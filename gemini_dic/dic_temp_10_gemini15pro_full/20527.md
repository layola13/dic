bizarres:/bɪˈzɑːrz/| adj.|古怪的，奇异的，异乎寻常的|Very strange or unusual.|The shop sold bizarre masks and other curiosities. 这家商店出售奇异的面具和其他古玩。|近义词：strange, odd, peculiar, eccentric, unusual, weird, outlandish|反义词：normal, ordinary, conventional, common|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From French *bizarre*, from Italian *bizzarro* "irascible," probably from Basque *bizar* "beard." The semantic shift may be due to the association of unusual beards with foreigners or eccentrics.
