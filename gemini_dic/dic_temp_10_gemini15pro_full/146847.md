overindividualism: /ˌoʊvərˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlɪzəm/
n.
过度个人主义
Excessive emphasis on individual rights and self-reliance, often at the expense of community and social responsibility.
Overindividualism can lead to a breakdown of social cohesion. 过度个人主义会导致社会凝聚力的崩溃。
The rise of overindividualism has been linked to declining voter turnout.  过度个人主义的兴起与选民投票率下降有关。
近义词:  egoism, narcissism, self-centeredness
反义词: collectivism, communitarianism
词性: noun
