fireworks:/ˈfaɪərˌwɜːrks/| n.| 烟花；烟火；焰火|A device containing gunpowder and other combustible chemicals that causes a spectacular explosion when ignited, used typically for display or celebration. |例句：The fireworks display on the Fourth of July was spectacular. 七月四日的烟火表演非常壮观。

例句：Children are fascinated by fireworks. 孩子们对烟花很着迷。|近义词：pyrotechnics, firecrackers|反义词: None|词性: Noun
