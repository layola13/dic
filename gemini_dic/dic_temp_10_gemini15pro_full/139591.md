obvolute:/ˈɒbvəluːt/| adj.|包卷的，重叠的|Having margins rolled over and covering the surface beneath; used especially of leaves or petals in bud.|The plant's obvolute leaves protect the delicate bud.|近义词:convolute, imbricate, supervolute|反义词:revolute, involute|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin obvolūtus, past participle of obvolvere (“to wrap around, cover”), from ob (“around, upon”) + volvere (“to roll, wrap”).
