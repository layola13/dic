bugbear:/ˈbʌɡˌber/ (US), /ˈbʌɡˌbeə(r)/ (UK)| n.|讨厌的事物；令人烦恼的人或事|A bugbear is a cause of obsessive fear, anxiety, or annoyance. |例句：Traffic jams are a real bugbear of mine. 交通堵塞真让我烦。

例句：His biggest bugbear is people who talk loudly on their phones in public. 他最讨厌的是那些在公共场合大声打电话的人。|近义词：pet peeve, annoyance, bête noire, bane|反义词：delight, pleasure|词性：名词
