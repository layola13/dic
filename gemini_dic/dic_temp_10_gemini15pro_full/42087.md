cotransduce:/ˌkoʊtrænsˈdjuːs/
verb
共同转导
To transfer genetic material from one bacterium to another along with other genetic material.
Bacteriophage P1 can cotransduce genetic markers located near each other on the bacterial chromosome.  噬菌体P1可以共同转导位于细菌染色体上彼此靠近的遗传标记。
The experiment demonstrated that the two genes were close enough to be cotransduced.  该实验表明这两个基因足够接近以至于可以共同转导。
synonyms: cotransfer
antonyms: (No direct antonym, but consider "individually transfer," "separately transduce")
词性:v.
