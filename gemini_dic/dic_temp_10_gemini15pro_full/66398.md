executors:/ˈɛksɪˌkjuːtərz/| n. |执行者；遗嘱执行人|A person or institution appointed by a testator to carry out the terms of their will.|The executors of his estate are selling his property. 他的遗产执行人正在出售他的财产。|近义词：administrator, trustee, enforcer|反义词: N/A|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English executour, from Old French executeur, from Latin executor (“performer”), agent noun from exsequor (“I follow out, execute”).
