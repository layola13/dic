absorbability:/əbˌzɔːrbəˈbɪləti/ /əbˌsɔːrbəˈbɪlədi/
n.
吸收性；吸收能力
The ability of a substance to absorb another substance.
The absorbability of the sponge is impressive.  这块海绵的吸水性令人印象深刻。
The researcher studied the absorbability of different types of soil. 研究人员研究了不同类型土壤的吸收能力。
synonyms: absorption capacity, absorptive capacity,  permeability, porosity
antonyms: impermeability, impenetrability
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From absorb + -ability.  Absorb comes from Latin absorbēre, from ab- "away" + sorbēre "to suck up."


USAGE->
The absorbability of the new material is being tested. =  这种新材料的吸收性正在被测试。

The high absorbability of the paper towels made them ideal for cleaning up spills. =  这种纸巾的高吸收性使它们成为清理溢出物的理想选择。

PHRASE->
high absorbability = 高吸收性
