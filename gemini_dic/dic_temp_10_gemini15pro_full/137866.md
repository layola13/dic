nonvirtues:/ˌnɒnˈvɜːrtʃuːz/
n.
缺点，非美德
lack of virtues;  bad qualities or habits.
His nonvirtues include laziness and dishonesty. 他的缺点包括懒惰和不诚实。
Synonyms: vices, failings, shortcomings, defects, flaws
Antonyms: virtues, merits, strengths, assets
noun
