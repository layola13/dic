duskly:/ˈdʌskli/
adjective
昏暗地；朦胧地
in a way that is dusky; dimly
The room was duskly lit by a single candle. 房间里只有一根蜡烛发出昏暗的光。
近义词：dimly, faintly, obscurely
反义词：brightly, clearly
词性: adjective
词源: From dusk + -ly.  Dusk itself comes from Old English *duxsc, related to Dutch duister and German duster.
