arrowing:/ˈærəʊɪŋ/
verb
形成箭头状; 呈箭头状移动; 变窄;
moving swiftly and directly, like an arrow.  The plane was arrowing towards the runway. 飞机正朝着跑道飞去。
The road arrowed through the valley. 公路像箭一样穿过山谷。
近义词: darting, shooting, zooming, streaking
反义词: meandering, wandering
词性: verb


Etymology: From arrow + -ing.  The verb form derives from the noun "arrow," referring to its swift and direct motion.
