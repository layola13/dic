chickening:/ˈtʃɪkənɪŋ/| v. | 畏缩，退缩 | To lose one's courage or resolve; to back out of something due to fear. |  He was going to ask her out, but he chickened out at the last minute. 他本来要约她出去，但在最后一刻退缩了。| 近义词：backing out, wussing out, flinching, balking | 反义词: braving, facing, confronting | 词性: 动词

chickening (out):/ˈtʃɪkənɪŋ aʊt/| v. | 畏缩，退缩 (通常与 "out" 连用) | To decide not to do something because you are afraid. |  I was going to bungee jump, but I chickened out. 我本来打算蹦极跳的，但我退缩了。| 近义词：backing out, wussing out, flinching, balking | 反义词: braving, facing, confronting | 词性: 动词短语

词源：
"Chicken" 最初是指小鸡，后来引申为“胆小鬼”，因为小鸡被认为是胆小的动物。 "Chickening out" 则表示像小鸡一样胆怯退缩。
