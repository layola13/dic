consolidated:/kənˈsɒlɪdeɪtɪd/ (British), /kənˈsɑːlɪdeɪtɪd/ (American)| adj.| 加固的，统一的，合并的|Combined into a single or unified whole; strengthened.|The company consolidated its operations by merging several smaller branches into a single headquarters.  这家公司通过将几个较小的分支机构合并到一个总部来巩固其业务。|Synonyms: combined, merged, unified, strengthened, reinforced|Antonyms: separated, divided, weakened, fragmented|adjective

consolidate:/kənˈsɒlɪdeɪt/ (British), /kənˈsɑːlɪdeɪt/ (American)| v.| 巩固，加强，合并|To combine into a single or unified whole; to strengthen or make something more solid.|The two companies consolidated to form a larger corporation.  两家公司合并组建了一个更大的公司。|Synonyms: combine, merge, unite, strengthen, reinforce|Antonyms: separate, divide, weaken, fragment|verb
