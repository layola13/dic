budders:/ˈbʌdərz/
noun
大麻浓缩物, 一种质地柔软、类似于黄油的大麻提取物
A cannabis concentrate, a soft, butter-like cannabis extract.
The dispensary offered a variety of budders, each with a distinct flavor profile. 这家药房提供各种不同口味的大麻浓缩物。
Synonyms:  wax, shatter, crumble, badder, dabs
Antonyms:  None (no direct opposite for a specific type of cannabis concentrate)
