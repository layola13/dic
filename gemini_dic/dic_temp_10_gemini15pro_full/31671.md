chattel:/ˈtʃæt(ə)l/
n.
动产，财产（尤指奴隶）
A chattel is an item of personal property which is movable, as opposed to real property or real estate. Historically, the term was also often used to refer to slaves.
The farmer regarded his livestock as mere chattels.  这位农民把他的牲畜仅仅看作是动产。
Synonyms: property, possession, asset, commodity, belonging
Antonyms: real estate, land
