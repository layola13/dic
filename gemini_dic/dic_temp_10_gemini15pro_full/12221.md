asbestic: /æsˈbɛstɪk/
adjective
石棉的；含石棉的
Relating to, containing, or resembling asbestos.
Asbestic materials were commonly used in construction until the dangers of asbestos became known.  石棉材料在人们认识到石棉的危害之前普遍用于建筑。
The old building was riddled with asbestic insulation.  这栋老建筑充满了石棉隔热材料。
近义词: asbestos-containing, asbestine
反义词: asbestos-free
词性: adjective
词源: From asbestos + -ic.  来自asbestos（石棉）+ -ic（形容词后缀）。
