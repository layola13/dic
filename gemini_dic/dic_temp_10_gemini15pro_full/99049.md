interannular:/ˌɪntərˈæn.jʊ.lər/
adjective
环间的，环状的
Situated between rings, especially between annual rings of wood.
The interannular space in the piston was damaged. 活塞的环间空间损坏了。
Synonyms: inter-ring
Antonyms: N/A
