ambitious:/æmˈbɪʃəs/| adj.|有雄心的，野心勃勃的|Having or showing a strong desire and determination to succeed.|She has ambitious plans for her future. 她对自己的未来有雄心勃勃的计划。|近义词:aspiring, determined, driven|反义词:unambitious, lazy, apathetic|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin ambitiōsus "full of ambition," from ambitiō (see ambition).
