duct tape:/ˈdʌkt teɪp/ (US) /ˈdʌkt teɪp/ (UK)| n.|管道胶带，防水胶布|A strong, flexible, adhesive tape used for sealing ducts, repairs, and various other purposes.|I used duct tape to fix the broken pipe. 我用管道胶带修好了破裂的管道。|近义词：gaffer tape, duck tape|反义词: None|noun

duct tape:/ˈdʌkt teɪp/ (US) /ˈdʌkt teɪp/ (UK)| v.|用管道胶带封住或粘住|To seal or adhere something using duct tape.|He duct-taped the box shut. 他用管道胶带把盒子封上了。|近义词：tape up, seal|反义词: unseal, detach|verb
