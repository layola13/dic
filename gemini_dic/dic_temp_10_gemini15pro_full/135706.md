nonprevalently:/nɒnˈprɛvələntli/
| adv. |不普遍地，不流行地|Not in a prevalent or widespread manner; infrequently.|The disease occurs nonprevalently in this region.  这种疾病在这个地区不普遍。|Synonym: uncommonly, rarely, infrequently|Antonym: prevalently, commonly, widely|Word Type: Adverb

Etymology:
From non- + prevalent + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," "prevalent" comes from the Latin "praevalēre" meaning "to be very strong" or "to prevail," and "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs.
