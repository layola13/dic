aircrafts:/ˈɛəkrɑːfts/ /ˈɛərkræfts/| n.|飞机（复数）|Aircraft is a term used to refer to any machine capable of flight, such as airplanes, helicopters, gliders, etc. Although grammatically "aircraft" is both singular and plural, "aircrafts" is sometimes used informally, particularly in technical contexts, to refer to multiple aircraft of different types or in lists and inventories. |Several aircrafts were damaged in the storm.  几架飞机在暴风雨中受损。|  The company manufactures various aircrafts, including helicopters and drones.  这家公司生产各种飞机，包括直升机和无人机。|synonyms: airplanes, planes, helicopters, flying machines| antonyms: N/A (Aircraft don't have a direct opposite.)| noun
