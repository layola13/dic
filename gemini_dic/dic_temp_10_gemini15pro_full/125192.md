multicellularity:/ˌmʌltiˌsɛljʊˈlærəti/ | n. | 多细胞性 | The state of being composed of multiple cells, forming a complex organism. | Multicellularity evolved independently in several lineages.  多细胞性在几个谱系中独立进化。|  Multicellularity allows for specialization of cells within an organism. 多细胞性允许生物体内细胞特化。 | 近义词:cellular complexity | 反义词:unicellularity | 词性: noun


Etymology: multi- (Latin, "many") + cell + -ularity (suffix forming abstract nouns)
