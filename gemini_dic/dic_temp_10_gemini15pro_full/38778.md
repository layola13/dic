conducively:/kənˈduːsɪvli/
|adv.|
|有利地，有助于地，有益地|
|In a way that contributes favorably to something; helpfully.|
|The soft music played conducively to a relaxing atmosphere.  柔和的音乐有助于营造轻松的氛围。|
|The quiet environment is conducive to studying. 安静的环境有利于学习。|
|近义词: helpfully, beneficially, favorably, positively|
|反义词: detrimentally, harmfully, negatively, adversely|
|词性: adverb (副词)|
|词源: From conducive + -ly.  Conducive comes from Late Latin conducivus "leading, helpful," from Latin conducere "to lead together, contribute."|
