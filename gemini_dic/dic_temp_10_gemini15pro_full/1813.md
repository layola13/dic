acritan: /ˈækrɪtæn/
noun
疑源类
Any of a group of fossil unicellular micro-organisms, probably algal, that occur in Precambrian rocks.
Acritarchs are a diverse group of organic microfossils known from the Precambrian to the recent.
近义词: microfossil, protist
反义词: (无明确的反义词，可以考虑 macroscopic organism)
词性: n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *akritos* (of uncertain origin) + -an.
