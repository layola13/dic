paracrostic:/ˌpærəˈkrɒstɪk/ | n. | 藏头诗，嵌字诗 | A poem in which the first letter of each line spells out a word, name, or phrase. | Example: My love for you is deep,
Eternal and true,
Radiant like the sun,
I cherish every moment with you. (The first letters spell "MERI") 我的爱很深，
永恒而真实，
像太阳一样光芒四射，
我珍惜与你在一起的每一刻。（第一个字母拼写“MERI”）

Example: An angel watched me as I slept,
Never leaving my side,
Guiding me through the night,
Each breath I took was blessed,
Love surrounded me. (The first letters spell "ANGEL")
一个天使在我睡觉时看着我，
从未离开我的身边，
指引我度过黑夜，
我的每一次呼吸都受到祝福，
爱包围着我。（第一个字母拼写“ANGEL”） |  acrostic | - | noun
