palmitate: /ˈpæl.mɪ.teɪt/ | n. | 棕榈酸盐；软脂酸盐 | A salt or ester of palmitic acid.  It is a saturated fatty acid found in animals and plants. | Sodium palmitate is a common ingredient in soap.  棕榈酸钠是肥皂中的常见成分。|  Palmitate is stored in the body as fat. 棕榈酸盐在体内以脂肪的形式储存。 | synonym: hexadecanoate | antonym:  (No direct antonym for a specific chemical compound.  Could consider unsaturated fatty acids as a broader contrasting category, e.g., oleate) | noun
