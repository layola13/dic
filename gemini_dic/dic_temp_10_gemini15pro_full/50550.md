depute:/dɪˈpjuːt/| v.|委托；委派；指派|to give someone else the authority to represent you or do something for you|The manager deputed his assistant to deal with the complaint. 经理指派他的助手处理投诉。|synonyms: delegate, assign, commission, authorize|antonyms: retain, keep, withhold|verb

depute:/ˈdepjʊt/| n.|代表；代理人|a person appointed to act for or represent another|He served as a depute for the company at the conference. 他在会议上担任公司的代表。|synonyms: delegate, representative, agent, proxy|antonyms: principal|noun
