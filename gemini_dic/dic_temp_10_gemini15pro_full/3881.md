agree:/əˈɡriː/ | vi. vt.| 同意，赞成；一致；商定 | To have the same opinion or feeling as someone else; to say that you will do something that someone else wants or suggests. | We agreed to meet on Sunday. 我们同意星期天见面。| synonyms: concur, consent, assent | antonyms: disagree, dissent, object | verb

agree:/əˈɡriː/ | vi.| (气候、食物等)适合 |  To be suitable or beneficial for someone's health. | The climate here doesn't agree with me.  这里的气候不适合我。| synonyms: suit, be good for | antonyms: disagree with, be bad for | verb
