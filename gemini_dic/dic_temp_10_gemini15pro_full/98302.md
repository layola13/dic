例句：The insidiousness of the marketing campaign slowly influenced consumer behavior.  这个营销活动的潜伏性慢慢地影响了消费者的行为。

近义词：treachery, deceit, cunning, stealth, surreptitiousness|反义词: openness, honesty, forthrightness|词性:noun

Etymology:  From insidious + -ness.  Insidious comes from Late Latin insidiosus "deceitful, cunning," from Latin insidiae "ambush, snare, treachery," from insidere "to sit in, occupy," from in- "in" + sedere "to sit."
