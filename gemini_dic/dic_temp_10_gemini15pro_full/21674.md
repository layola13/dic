USAGE->
Simple Present:
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she do for a living? 她以什么为生？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? 他晚饭在做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? 她用这笔钱做了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on recently? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has she been reading lately? 她最近一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you say? 你说了什么？
What happened last night? 昨晚发生了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing at 8 pm yesterday? 昨天晚上8点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? 我见到她时她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you left? 你走之前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? 她生病前吃了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I called? 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major?  她转专业之前一直在学什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气会怎么样？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting tomorrow? 明天的会议上他们会讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will they have finished by next month? 到下个月他们将完成什么？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？
