co-ossify:/kəʊˈɒsɪfaɪ/
verb
共同骨化，融合
to ossify together; to become united by the growth of bone
The bones of the skull co-ossify as a child grows. 随着孩子的成长，头骨的骨头会融合在一起。
The two bones gradually co-ossified over time.  两块骨头随着时间的推移逐渐融合在一起。
fuse, unite, join
separate, divide
v.
