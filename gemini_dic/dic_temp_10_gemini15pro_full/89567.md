honourer:/ˈɒnərə(r) (UK), ˈɑːnərər (US)| n.|尊敬者，给予荣誉者|A person who honours someone or something. |例句：He was a great honourer of tradition. 他是一位非常尊重传统的人。

例句：The Queen is an honourer of brave soldiers. 女王很尊敬勇敢的士兵。


近义词：admirer, respecter, reverer|反义词: detractor, critic, disparager|词性:noun

honour:/ˈɒnər (UK), ˈɑːnər (US)| n. vt.|荣誉，尊敬，给…以荣誉|High respect; great esteem. To regard with great respect. |例句：They fought for the honour of their country. 他们为国家的荣誉而战。

例句：We honour her for her bravery. 我们敬佩她的勇敢。


近义词：respect, esteem, reverence|反义词: dishonour, disrespect, contempt|词性:noun, verb
