annihilator:/əˈnaɪəˌleɪtər/ | n. |歼灭者，毁灭者 |Something that annihilates.  |The asteroid was the annihilator of the dinosaurs. 这颗小行星是恐龙的毁灭者。| The conquerors were the annihilators of the ancient civilization. 征服者是古代文明的毁灭者。 | synonyms: destroyer, exterminator, eradicator | antonyms: creator, preserver, savior | noun

annihilators:/əˈnaɪəˌleɪtərz/ | n. |歼灭者们，毁灭者们 |Plural form of annihilator. |The annihilators of the forest were identified as illegal loggers. 森林的毁灭者被确定为非法伐木者。| The annihilators of the city's culture were apathy and neglect.  这座城市文化的毁灭者是冷漠和忽视。 | synonyms: destroyers, exterminators, eradicators | antonyms: creators, preservers, saviors | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin annihilator, from Latin annihilatus, past participle of annihilare "to reduce to nothing," from ad- "to" + nihil "nothing."
