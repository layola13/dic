fawnlike:/ˈfɔːnlaɪk/| adj.|像小鹿的，温顺的，讨好的|Resembling or characteristic of a young deer, especially in being gentle, graceful, or timid; often suggesting a desire to please or gain favor.|The actress gave a fawnlike smile to the director, hoping to land the lead role.  这位女演员对导演露出了小鹿般的微笑，希望能获得主角。|近义词：timid, meek, docile, submissive, ingratiating|反义词：bold, assertive, aggressive, confident|词性:形容词

词源:  From "fawn" (a young deer) + "-like" (suffix meaning resembling or characteristic of).  "Fawn" ultimately comes from the Old French "faon," from the Latin "fetus" (offspring, young animal).
