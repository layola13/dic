outwaste:/ˌaʊtˈweɪst/
verb
浪费；挥霍
to spend or use something carelessly or extravagantly
He outwasted his inheritance in a year of reckless spending. 他一年挥霍的开销就浪费了遗产。
squander, dissipate, lavish
conserve, save
