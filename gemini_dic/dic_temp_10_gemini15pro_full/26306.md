buxine:/ˈbʌksiːn/
noun
贝壳杉碱
An alkaloid derived from the bark of the boxwood tree (Buxus sempervirens).
Buxine can be used as a heart stimulant. 贝壳杉碱可以用作心脏兴奋剂。
Synonyms: bebeerine, pelosine
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a specific chemical compound.  Could consider antonyms for its effect, like "depressant")
