blooped:/bluːpt/| v. |发出噗通声；搞砸；犯错|To make a short, low, dull sound, like something dropping into water; to make a mistake, often in a clumsy or embarrassing way. |例句：The fish blooped as it jumped out of the water. 鱼跳出水面时发出噗通声。

例句：I really blooped that presentation – I forgot half my lines! 我真的搞砸了那次演讲——我忘了一半的台词！|近义词：blundered, botched, messed up, flubbed|反义词:succeeded, aced|词性:动词
