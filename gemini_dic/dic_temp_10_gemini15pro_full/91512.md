hyne:/haɪn/ | n. | 仆人；乡下人；卑贱之人 | A servant; a rustic; a base person.  A term of contempt. |  "The haughty lord sneered at the hyne."  傲慢的领主嘲笑那个卑贱的人。 | 近义词: peasant, serf, churl | 反义词: lord, nobleman | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Old English *hīna, from Proto-Germanic *hīnaz, meaning "member of a household."  Cognate with Old High German *hīno and Old Norse *hein.
