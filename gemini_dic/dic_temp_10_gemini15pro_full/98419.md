insourcing:/ˈɪnˌsɔːrsɪŋ/ | n. | 内包；内部采购 | The practice of using an organization's own personnel or other resources to do work rather than outsourcing it. |  Many companies are now considering insourcing to reduce costs and improve quality control. 许多公司现在正在考虑内包以降低成本并改进质量控制。 | Example: The company decided on insourcing their IT department after experiencing problems with their outsourced provider.  该公司在与外包供应商出现问题后决定内包其IT部门。 | Synonyms: backsourcing, onshoring, vertical integration | Antonyms: outsourcing, offshoring | noun
