nontreatment:/ˌnɒnˈtriːtmənt/ | n. | 不治疗，非治疗 |  The nontreatment of the infection led to serious complications.  |  The nontreatment of his condition resulted in further deterioration. | 近义词: neglect, inaction | 反义词: treatment, therapy | 词性: 名词

Etymology: non- + treatment. "Non-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "not." "Treatment" comes from the Old French word "traitement," meaning "the act of treating."
