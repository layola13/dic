driest:/ˈdraɪɪst/| adj.|最干的|Superlative form of dry; having the least moisture.|The Atacama Desert is one of the driest places on Earth. 阿塔卡马沙漠是地球上最干燥的地方之一。|The driest month of the year is usually July. 一年中，七月通常是最干燥的月份。|近义词：arid, parched, barren|反义词：wettest, dampest, most humid|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *driegste, *dryeste, from Old English drīgeste, drygeste (“driest”), superlative form of drīg (“dry”). Cognate with West Frisian drûchste (“driest”), Dutch droogste (“driest”), German trockenst (“driest”). Related to dry.
