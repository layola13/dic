argue: /ˈɑːrɡjuː/ (US) /ˈɑːɡjuː/ (UK)| v.|争论，辩论|To give reasons for or against something; to contend or disagree in words.|
They argued about the best way to solve the problem. 他们就解决问题的最佳方案进行了辩论。

He argued that the evidence was insufficient. 他辩称证据不足。

近义词: debate, dispute, contend, quarrel
反义词: agree, concur, concede
词性: verb (动词)
