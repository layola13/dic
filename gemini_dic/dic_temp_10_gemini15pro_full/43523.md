craw:/krɔː/
noun|鸟类的嗉囊；胃|The bird stored food in its craw.|鸟把食物储存在嗉囊里。
|近义词:crop, gizzard|反义词: (无直接反义词)|词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *kraga, from Proto-Germanic *kraʒōn, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)ker- (to cut, separate).  Cognate with Danish kro, Swedish kräva, Old English crawe. Originally referred to the neck or throat.
