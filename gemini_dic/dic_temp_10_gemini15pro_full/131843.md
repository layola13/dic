nonconstat:/nɒnˈkɒnstæt/| adv.|不必然；未必如此；|It does not follow that because A is true, B is also true.  |例句：Just because he's quiet doesn't nonconstat that he's shy.  他安静并不一定意味着他害羞。

例句：The presence of poverty nonconstat the absence of happiness. 存在贫困并不一定意味着没有幸福。|近义词：it doesn't follow, not necessarily|反义词:necessarily, certainly|词性:副词
