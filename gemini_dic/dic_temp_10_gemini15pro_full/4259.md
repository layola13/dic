airsick:/ˈɛərsɪk/ | adj. | 晕机的；患航空病的 | Feeling nauseous or vomiting as a result of the motion of an aircraft. |  I always get airsick on long flights. 我在长途飞行中总是会晕机。| nauseated, queasy, sick | well, healthy | adjective

Etymology: air + sick.  "Sick from being in the air," 1919, from air (n.) + sick (adj.).
