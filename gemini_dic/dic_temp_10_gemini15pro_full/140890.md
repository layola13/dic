on your mark:/ˌɑːn jər ˈmɑːrk/ | phrase | 各就各位 | Used to signal the start of a race.  It's the first command in the starting sequence "On your mark, get set, go." | The starter said, "On your mark, get set, go!" 发令员说：“各就各位，预备，跑！” | 近义词: ready, set | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "stop" or "finish") | phrase
