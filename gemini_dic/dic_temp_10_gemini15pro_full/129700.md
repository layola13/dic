non-domesticated:/ˌnɒn dəˈmɛstɪkeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 未驯化的，野生的 | Not tamed or domesticated; wild. |  Example: The non-domesticated cat hissed at me. 这只野猫冲我嘶嘶叫。 | Example: Researchers are studying the behavior of non-domesticated animals. 研究人员正在研究野生动物的行为。 | Synonyms: wild, feral, untamed | Antonyms: domesticated, tame, domestic | Adjective

domesticate: /dəˈmɛstɪkeɪt/ | v. | 驯养，驯化 | To tame (an animal) and keep it as a pet or for farm produce. | Example:  Humans domesticated dogs thousands of years ago.  人类在几千年前就驯化了狗。 | Example:  They tried to domesticate the wild horses. 他们试图驯化野马。 | Synonyms: tame, train, break | Antonyms: release, free, wild | Verb
