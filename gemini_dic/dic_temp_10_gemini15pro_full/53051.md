diopside:/ˈdaɪəpsaɪd/ | n. |透辉石|A mineral of the pyroxene group, typically occurring as granular green crystals. It is a silicate of calcium and magnesium, sometimes containing iron or chromium.  |Diopside is a common mineral in metamorphic rocks. 透辉石是变质岩中常见的矿物。| Diopside can be a beautiful gemstone, ranging in color from light green to deep emerald. 透辉石可以是一种美丽的宝石，颜色从浅绿色到深祖母绿不等。 |  Synonyms: pyroxene, silicate| Antonyms: None | Noun
