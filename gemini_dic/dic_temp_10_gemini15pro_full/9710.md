antitrochanter:/ˌæntiˈtroʊkæntər/ | n. | 反转子 | A process of the femur that projects from the posteromedial aspect of the proximal end of the shaft and acts as an insertion point for the quadratus femoris muscle. | The antitrochanter is a key landmark in hip replacement surgery. 反转子是髋关节置换手术中的一个关键标志。| Synonym:  (no exact synonym, but related terms include lesser trochanter, intertrochanteric crest) | Antonym: (no antonym) | noun
