gutturalities:/ˌɡʌtəˈrælɪtiz/| n.|喉音; 粗哑的声音;|Sounds made in the throat, often harsh or grating.  These sounds are typically produced in the back of the throat and can sound rough or unpleasant.|His speech was full of gutturalities, making him difficult to understand. 他的讲话充满了喉音，使人难以理解。|近义词：growls, grunts, rasps|反义词: melodious sounds, smooth sounds|noun
