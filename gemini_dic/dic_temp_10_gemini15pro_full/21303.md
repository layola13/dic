bloodstains:/ˈblʌd steɪnz/
noun
血迹
Marks left by blood.
The detective examined the bloodstains on the carpet.  侦探检查了地毯上的血迹。
近义词:blood marks, gore
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "cleanliness")
词性:n.
