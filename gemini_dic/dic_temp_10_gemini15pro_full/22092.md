bolo:/ˈboʊloʊ/| n.|大砍刀; 菲律宾短剑 |A large, heavy single-edged knife, similar to a machete, originating in the Philippines. |He used a bolo to clear the brush. 他用大砍刀清理灌木丛。|Example:  The farmer sharpened his bolo before heading to the fields. 农民在去田里之前磨快了他的大砍刀。|近义词：machete, knife, sword|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


bolo:/ˈboʊloʊ/| n.|一种领结，通常是细绳或皮革制成，带有装饰性滑块或尖端 |A type of necktie, typically a thin cord or leather strip fastened with an ornamental slide or tips. |He wore a bolo tie with his western shirt. 他穿着西部衬衫，戴着波洛领带。|Example: The bolo tie is a popular accessory in the southwestern United States. 波洛领带是美国西南部流行的配饰。|近义词：necktie, tie|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

词源: (Etymology)

From Tagalog *bolo*, from Spanish *bolo*, "short, thick dagger." The word's ultimate origin is uncertain, possibly related to Proto-Malayo-Polynesian *buel*, meaning "round."
