ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *cokewold, from Old French cucuault, from cucu "cuckoo," referring to the cuckoo's habit of laying its eggs in other birds' nests.  The cuckoo bird was seen as a symbol of the deceived husband whose nest (home and marriage) is invaded by another.
