outquarters:/ˈaʊtˌkwɔːrtərz/
noun
外部住所；临时住所
A place of lodging away from one's usual residence, especially for troops.
The soldiers were billeted in outquarters during the maneuvers.  士兵们在演习期间被安置在外部住所。
synonyms:  billet, barracks, lodgings, accommodations
antonyms:  home, residence, domicile
n.
