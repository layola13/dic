n.
华丽俗气；炫耀；浮华
The state or quality of being excessively bright or showy, typically in a way considered vulgar or lacking in good taste.
Her gaudy dress displayed a certain gauirishness that was inappropriate for the occasion.  她那件俗艳的连衣裙展现出某种华丽俗气，不适合这个场合。

The gauirishness of the Las Vegas strip can be overwhelming. 拉斯维加斯大道的浮华可能会让人不知所措。
近义词:flashiness, gaudiness, showiness, ostentation, flamboyance
反义词:understatement, subtlety, refinement, simplicity, modesty
词性:noun
