earworms:/ˈɪərwɜːrmz/
n.
耳虫，洗脑神曲
A catchy tune or song that continuously plays in one's head.
The song from that commercial is a total earworm; I've been humming it all day. 那广告里的歌真是洗脑，我一整天都在哼。
Synonyms: brainworm, stuck song, catchy tune
Antonyms: N/A
noun
