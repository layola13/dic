boroughmongering:/ˈbʌrəˌmʌŋɡərɪŋ/
n.
选区操纵；控制议会席位
The manipulation of the boundaries of parliamentary boroughs, or the corrupt purchase of seats in Parliament, in order to gain political influence.
18th and 19th century British politics was rife with boroughmongering. 十八、十九世纪的英国政治充斥着选区操纵。

Boroughmongering was a common practice before the Reform Act of 1832. 选区操纵在1832年改革法案之前是一种常见的做法。

近义词:gerrymandering, political corruption, electoral manipulation
反义词:fair elections, democratic representation
