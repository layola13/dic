bravenesses:/ˈbreɪvnəsɪz/ |n.|勇敢的行为（罕用，通常用bravery）|Instances or displays of courage; acts of bravery.  This word is rarely used; "bravery" is much more common.|He recounted tales of his grandfather's bravenesses in the war. 他讲述了他祖父在战争中的英勇事迹。|Near Synonyms: bravery, courage, valor, heroism, boldness, daring, intrepidity|Near Antonyms: cowardice, timidity, fearfulness|noun

Etymology: From brave + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  The root "brave" comes from the Italian "bravo" meaning "wild, savage," later evolving to mean "courageous."
