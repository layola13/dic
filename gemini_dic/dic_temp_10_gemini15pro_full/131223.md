noncalculating:/nɒnˈkælkjʊleɪtɪŋ/
adjective
不精明的；不计较的；无私的
Not shrewd or calculating; unselfish.
She had a noncalculating generosity. 她有着不计较的慷慨。
近义词: artless, ingenuous, naive, unsophisticated, guileless, unworldly, innocent, childlike, simple, trusting, unwary, uncritical
反义词: calculating, shrewd, scheming, manipulative, designing, devious, cunning, crafty, wily, artful, guileful
