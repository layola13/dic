nondepletions:/ˌnɒndɪˈpliːʃənz/| n.|非消耗，非耗竭（通常指资源）|The report focused on nondepletions of renewable energy sources.  这份报告关注的是可再生能源的非耗竭性。|The study highlighted the importance of nondepletions in sustainable forestry. 这项研究强调了非耗竭性在可持续林业中的重要性。|近义词:conservation, preservation, renewal|反义词:depletion, exhaustion, consumption|词性:名词

词源:
non- (prefix):  not, absence of.  来自拉丁语 non.
depletion (noun): the reduction in the number or quantity of something. 来自拉丁语 deplere, 意思是 "to empty out."
-s (suffix): plural marker.
