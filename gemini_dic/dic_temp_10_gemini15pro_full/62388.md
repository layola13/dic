endocrine: /ˈɛndəkrɪn/ (US), /ˈɛndəkraɪn/ (UK) | adj. n. | 内分泌的；内分泌腺 |  Relating to or denoting glands which secrete hormones or other products directly into the blood. | The endocrine system plays a vital role in regulating various bodily functions. 内分泌系统在调节各种身体机能中起着至关重要的作用。 |  The doctor specializes in endocrine disorders. 这位医生专攻内分泌失调。 | 近义词: hormonal,  glandular | 反义词: exocrine | 词性: adjective, noun

endocrine: /ˈɛndəkrɪn/ (US), /ˈɛndəkraɪn/ (UK) | n. | 内分泌腺 | An endocrine gland. | The pancreas is both an exocrine and an endocrine gland. 胰腺既是外分泌腺也是内分泌腺。| The endocrine glands produce hormones. 内分泌腺产生激素。 | 近义词: hormone gland | 反义词: exocrine gland | 词性: noun
