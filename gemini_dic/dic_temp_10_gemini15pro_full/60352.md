egotize:/ˈiːɡətaɪz/ |v. |自我化，以自我为中心|To make something about oneself, often excessively or inappropriately; to center or focus something around one's own ego or self-importance. |She egotized the entire conversation, making it all about her recent promotion. 她把整个谈话都自我化了，全都在谈她最近的升职。|He egotized his accomplishments, boasting about them to anyone who would listen.  他自吹自擂地炫耀自己的成就，逢人就说。|Synonyms: boast, brag, vaunt, show off|Antonyms: humble oneself, downplay|Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From ego (Latin, 'I') + -ize (suffix forming verbs).
