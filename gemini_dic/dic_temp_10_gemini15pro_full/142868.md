out-measuring:/ˌaʊtˈmɛʒərɪŋ/
verb
胜过，超过（在测量方面）
surpassing in measurement; exceeding in size, quantity, or degree.
The new building is out-measuring the old one by a significant margin. 这座新建筑在规模上大大超过了旧的那座。
The company is out-measuring its competitors in sales. 这家公司的销售额超过了竞争对手。
近义词: outstripping, exceeding, surpassing, bettering
反义词: falling short of, lagging behind
词性: verb
