heydays:/ˈheɪdeɪz/| n. |全盛时期，鼎盛时期|The period of greatest prosperity, vigor, or success.|例句：The company's heyday was in the 1990s, when it dominated the market.  这家公司的鼎盛时期是在20世纪90年代，当时它在市场上占据主导地位。

例句：She reminisced about the heydays of her youth, filled with carefree adventures. 她回忆起她年轻时的黄金时代，充满了无忧无虑的冒险。|近义词：prime, bloom, peak, golden age|反义词:decline, downfall|词性:名词
