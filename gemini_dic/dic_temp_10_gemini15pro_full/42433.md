countercurrent:/ˈkaʊntərˌkʌrənt/| adj. n.|逆流的，反向的；逆流|A countercurrent is a current flowing in the opposite direction to another.|Countercurrent exchange is an important mechanism in biological systems. 逆流交换是生物系统中的一个重要机制。|Synonyms: reverse flow, backflow|Antonyms: direct flow, forward flow|adjective, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- (against, opposite) + current (flowing stream).  The word "counter" ultimately derives from the Latin word "contra" meaning "against."  "Current" comes from the Old French "corant" meaning "running."
