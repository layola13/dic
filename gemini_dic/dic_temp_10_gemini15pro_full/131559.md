noncommunally:/ˌnɑːnkəˈmjuːnəli/ | adv. | 非公共地；不以社区为单位地；不集体地 |  In a way that is not shared by all members of a community; individually or separately, rather than collectively.  |  The land was owned noncommunally, with each family having its own plot.  土地并非集体所有，每个家庭都有自己的地块。 | Synonyms: individually, separately, privately, independently | Antonyms: communally, collectively, jointly, shared | adverb
