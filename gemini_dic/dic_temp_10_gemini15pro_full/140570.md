oligarchy: /ˈɒlɪɡɑːki/ (UK) /ˈɑːlɪɡɑːrki/ (US)| n. |寡头政治；寡头统治集团|A small group of people having control of a country or organization.|例句：The country is ruled by an oligarchy of wealthy families. 这个国家由富裕家族的寡头统治。

例句：The oligarchy controlled all aspects of the society.  这个寡头集团控制了社会的所有方面。


近义词：autocracy, tyranny, despotism |反义词: democracy |词性: noun
