alienating: /ˈeɪliəneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 疏远的，使人反感的 | Causing someone to feel isolated or estranged. |  Her constant criticism was alienating her friends. 她不断的批评使她的朋友们疏远了她。

The alienating experience of being the only foreigner in the village made him homesick.  在这个村庄里作为唯一的外国人，这种疏离的经历让他想家。 | 近义词：isolating, estranging, distancing, off-putting | 反义词：uniting, connecting, endearing | 词性: 形容词

词源: From the verb "alienate," which comes from the Latin word "alienare," meaning "to estrange."  源自动词"alienate"，该词来自拉丁语"alienare"，意思是"疏远"。
