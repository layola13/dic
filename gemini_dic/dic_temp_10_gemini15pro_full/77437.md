gawkers:/ˈɡɔːkərz/
noun
观望者；围观者
People who stare openly and stupidly, especially at something unusual or unpleasant.
The gawkers slowed traffic as they craned their necks to see the accident. 围观者伸长脖子看事故，造成了交通堵塞。
The celebrity was surrounded by gawkers as she left the restaurant. 名人离开餐厅时被一群围观者包围。
synonyms: onlookers, spectators, rubberneckers, voyeurs
antonyms:  participants,  ignorers
n.
