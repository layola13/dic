clubbism: /ˈklʌbɪzəm/
n.
俱乐部主义，俱乐部精神
A strong attachment or loyalty to one's club.  The tendency to socialize primarily or exclusively within one's club.
例句：Clubbism can sometimes lead to exclusion of those outside the group.  俱乐部主义有时会导致群体外的人被排斥。

例句：Their clubbism prevented them from interacting with people from other social circles.  他们的俱乐部精神阻碍了他们与其他社交圈的人互动。
近义词：cliquishness, parochialism, insularity
反义词：inclusiveness, open-mindedness
词性：名词
