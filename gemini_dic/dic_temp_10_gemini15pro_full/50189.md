deoxidation:/diːˌɒksɪˈdeɪʃən/
n.|脱氧，去氧|The process of removing oxygen from a substance, typically a metal or ore.|例句：Deoxidation is a crucial step in steelmaking.  脱氧是炼钢的关键步骤。

例句：The winemaker used a deoxidation process to improve the quality of the wine.  酿酒师使用脱氧工艺来提高葡萄酒的质量。
|近义词：reduction, oxygen removal|反义词：oxidation|词性：名词
