greenliest:/ˈɡriːnɪst/
adjective
最绿的
superlative adjective of green; having the most intense green color.
The greenliest leaves were at the top of the tree.  树顶的叶子最绿。
近义词:verdant, lush
反义词:brown, withered
词性:adjective

词源:
From Old English grēne (green) + -est (superlative suffix).
