anodized: /ˈænəˌdaɪzd/ | adj. | 阳极氧化的 | Coated with a protective oxide layer by an electrolytic process.  |  例句：The anodized aluminum frame is resistant to corrosion.  阳极氧化铝框架耐腐蚀。 | 近义词：oxidized, coated | 反义词: uncoated, bare | 词性: 形容词

anodize: /ˈænəˌdaɪz/ | vt. | 阳极氧化 | To coat (a metal, especially aluminum) with a protective oxide layer by an electrolytic process. | 例句：The company anodizes aluminum parts for various industries.  这家公司为各行各业阳极氧化铝零件。 | 近义词：oxidize, coat | 反义词: strip, bare | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From anode + -ize.  Anode refers to the positive electrode used in electrolysis.
