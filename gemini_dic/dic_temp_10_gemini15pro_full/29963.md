cavers:/ˈkeɪvərz/
n.
探洞者；洞穴探险者
People who explore caves, often for recreation or scientific study.
例句：The cavers descended into the darkness, equipped with headlamps and ropes. 探洞者们带着头灯和绳索下降到黑暗中。

例句：A group of experienced cavers mapped the extensive cave system. 一群经验丰富的探洞者绘制了广阔的洞穴系统地图。

近义词：spelunkers, potholers
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "surface dwellers")
词性: Noun (plural)


Etymology:
From "cave" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Cave" comes from Old French "cave"  and Latin "cava" (hollow place, cave).
