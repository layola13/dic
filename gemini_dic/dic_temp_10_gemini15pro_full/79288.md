glaucous:/ˈɡlɔːkəs/ | adj. | 灰绿色的，苍白色的 | Of a dull grayish-green or blue-green color. | The glaucous leaves of the succulent shimmered in the sunlight.  多肉植物灰绿色的叶子在阳光下闪闪发光。| Near synonyms: sea-green, pale green, bluish-green | Near antonyms: bright green, vibrant green | adjective

glaucous:/ˈɡlɔːkəs/ | adj. | 被白霜的 | Covered with a powdery bloom that gives a frosted appearance. | The glaucous grapes hung heavy on the vine.  挂在藤蔓上的葡萄覆盖着一层白霜。| Near synonyms: frosted, powdery, dusty | Near antonyms: glossy, shiny | adjective

Etymology: From Latin *glaucus, from Greek glaukós "gleaming, silvery," of uncertain origin.
