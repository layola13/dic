dissembled:/dɪˈsɛmbəld/
verb (past tense) | 伪装的，掩饰的 | concealed or disguised; pretended | He dissembled his true feelings with a smile. 他用微笑掩饰了他真实的感受。 | synonyms: disguised, masked, feigned, pretended | antonyms: revealed, exposed, honest, forthright | v.

dissemble:/dɪˈsɛmbəl/
verb | 掩饰，伪装 | to conceal one's true motives, feelings, or beliefs | She dissembled her anger with a forced smile. 她强颜欢笑以掩饰她的愤怒。 | synonyms: disguise, mask, feign, pretend | antonyms: reveal, expose, be honest | v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French dissembler, from Latin dissimulare, from dis- "apart, away" + simulare "to simulate, copy, represent, feign," from similis "like."
