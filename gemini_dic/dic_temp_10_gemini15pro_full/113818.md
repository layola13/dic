madbrain:/ˈmædbreɪn/| n. |疯狂的想法；疯狂的计划；鲁莽的人|A madbrain scheme or idea is very silly or likely to fail. A madbrain person acts impulsively or recklessly.|He had a madbrain scheme to get rich quick. 他有一个快速致富的疯狂计划。|近义词：crackpot, lunatic, daredevil|反义词: cautious person, sane person|词性:名词

词源:  Compound of 'mad' (meaning insane or foolish) and 'brain' (referring to the mind or intellect).  Relatively modern, likely originating in the late 19th or early 20th century.
