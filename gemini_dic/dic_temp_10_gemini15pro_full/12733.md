assets:/ˈæsɛts/
n.
资产,财产
Something valuable that an entity owns, benefits from, or has use of, in generating income.
The company's assets include cash, investments, and property.  这家公司的资产包括现金、投资和房产。
synonyms: possessions, holdings, resources, wealth
antonyms: liabilities, debts
