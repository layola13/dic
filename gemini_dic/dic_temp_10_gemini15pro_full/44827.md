crushproof:/ˈkrʌʃpruːf/
adjective
防压的;抗压的
Able to withstand crushing or being flattened by pressure.
This suitcase is crushproof, so you don't have to worry about your belongings getting damaged.  这个行李箱是防压的，所以你不必担心你的行李会被损坏。
The packaging is crushproof to protect the delicate items inside.  包装是防压的，以保护里面的易碎物品。
近义词:resistant, durable, strong, sturdy
反义词:fragile, delicate, breakable
词性:adjective
