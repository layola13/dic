feeble-minded:/ˈfiːblˈmaɪndɪd/
| adj. |低能的，愚笨的|Having below average intelligence; mentally deficient.  Lacking in judgment or good sense. |He was considered feeble-minded and unable to care for himself.|Synonyms: simpleminded, foolish, unintelligent, witless, slow-witted, half-witted, imbecilic, moronic, cretinous (offensive), stupid, foolish, silly|Antonyms: intelligent, bright, smart, clever, astute, sharp, brilliant|adjective


feeble-mindedness:/ˈfiːblˈmaɪndɪdnəs/
| n. |低能，愚笨|The state of being feeble-minded; mental deficiency.|Feeble-mindedness was once considered a valid medical diagnosis.|Synonyms: mental deficiency, intellectual disability, simplemindedness, stupidity|Antonyms: intelligence, brilliance, cleverness|noun
