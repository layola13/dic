comprobate:/ˈkɒmprəʊbeɪt/ | n. | 遗嘱认证; 认证书; 遗嘱检验 | An official document that proves that a will is valid. |  The executor applied for the comprobate of the will. 执行人申请了遗嘱认证。| synonyms: probate, testamentary letters | antonyms: N/A | noun

comprobate:/ˈkɒmprəʊbeɪt/ | vt. | 认证（遗嘱）| To officially prove that a will is valid. | The court comprobated the will. 法院认证了这份遗嘱。| synonyms: probate, validate, authenticate | antonyms: invalidate, reject | verb
