disbeliever:/ˌdɪsbɪˈliːvər/| n.|不信者，怀疑者|A person who does not believe something, especially someone who does not believe in a particular religion.|He was a disbeliever in the power of prayer. 他不相信祈祷的力量。|近义词：skeptic, doubter, cynic|反义词：believer, adherent|noun
