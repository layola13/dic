facilitator:/fəˈsɪlɪteɪtər/| n.|推动者，促进者，协调人|Someone who helps a group of people understand their common objectives and assists them to plan to achieve them without taking a particular position on the outcome himself.  A person who makes an action or process easy or easier.|The facilitator guided the discussion, ensuring everyone had a chance to speak.|Synonyms: moderator, coordinator, helper, assistant, guide, leader, enabler|Antonyms: obstructor, hinderer, inhibitor|noun
