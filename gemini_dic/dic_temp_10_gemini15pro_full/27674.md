candite:/ˈkændɪt/| n.| 结晶糖; 冰糖 |A crystalline form of sugar, often used in confectionery.|The candite sparkled on the cake. 蛋糕上的冰糖闪闪发光。|近义词：sugar crystal, rock candy|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

词源: From French *candi*, from Arabic *qandī*, ultimately from Sanskrit *khaṇḍa* (meaning "piece, fragment, candied sugar").
