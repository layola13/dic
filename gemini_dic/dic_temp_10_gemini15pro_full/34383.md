classifieds:/ˈklæsɪfaɪdz/
noun
分类广告，分类广告栏
a section of a newspaper or magazine, or a website, containing advertisements for goods and services
I found a used car for sale in the classifieds. 我在分类广告里找到了一辆二手车出售。
synonyms: advertisements, listings, notices
antonyms: N/A (This word doesn't have a direct antonym.)
