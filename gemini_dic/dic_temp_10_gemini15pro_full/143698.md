outlearn: /ˌaʊtˈlɜːrn/ | v. | 超越，在学习上胜过 | To surpass someone in learning or skill; to learn more or better than someone else. |  He outlearnt his classmates in mathematics. 他在数学方面胜过他的同学们。| Synonyms: surpass, outstrip, excel, outdo | Antonyms: lag behind, fall behind | verb

outlearn: /ˌaʊtˈlɜːrn/ | v. |  忘却（通过学习新事物）| To forget (something learned) by learning something new or different. | She outlearnt her bad habits and adopted healthier ones. 她改掉了坏习惯，养成了更健康的习惯。| Synonyms: unlearn, supersede, replace | Antonyms: retain, remember | verb
