luft:/lʊft/
n.
air, atmosphere
The fresh luft invigorated him. 新鲜的空气让他精神焕发。
例句：The mountain luft is thin and cold. 山上的空气稀薄而寒冷。
例句：Open the window and let in some fresh luft. 打开窗户，让新鲜空气进来。
近义词：air, atmosphere, breeze
反义词：vacuum, void
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Luft*, meaning "air".  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *luftuz, related to Old English *lyft* (air, sky), and further back to Proto-Indo-European *lewp- (to peel, to fly).  The connection likely comes from the idea of something light or buoyant, like air.
