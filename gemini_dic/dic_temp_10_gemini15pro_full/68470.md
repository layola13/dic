familiarly:/fəˈmɪliərli/| adv.|熟悉地，亲切地|in a familiar way; intimately|She spoke to him familiarly, calling him by his first name. 她亲切地和他说话，直呼他的名字。|Synonyms: intimately, closely, personally, informally|Antonyms: formally, distantly, coldly, stiffly|词性:副词

familiarly:/fəˈmɪliərli/| adv.|随便地，放肆地|in a too familiar or forward manner|He put his arm around her familiarly, which made her uncomfortable.  他随便地搂住她，让她感到不舒服。|Synonyms: presumptuously, impertinently, impudently, boldly|Antonyms: respectfully, politely, reservedly, shyly|词性:副词
