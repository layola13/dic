ethionic:/ˌiːθaɪˈɒnɪk/ | adj. |乙硫的|Relating to or derived from ethionic acid (H₂S₂O₇).| Ethionic acid is an intermediate in the formation of sulfuric acid. 乙硫酸是硫酸形成过程中的中间体。|Ethionic acid plays a crucial role in the industrial production of sulfuric acid. 乙硫酸在硫酸的工业生产中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词:sulfoacetic (somewhat related in chemical structure)|反义词: N/A |词性: adjective
