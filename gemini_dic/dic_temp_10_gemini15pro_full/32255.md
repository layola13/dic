chicle: /ˈtʃɪkəl/ | n. |天然口香糖，糖胶|A gumlike substance obtained from the sapodilla tree, used as the basis of chewing gum.|例句：The ancient Mayans chewed chicle. 古代玛雅人嚼糖胶。

例句：Chicle is the main ingredient in natural chewing gum.  糖胶是天然口香糖的主要成分。|近义词：gum, chewing gum|反义词: None|词性: Noun
