equate:/ɪˈkweɪt/| v. |使相等；等同|To consider something to be the same as or equivalent to something else.|例句：He equates wealth with happiness. 他把财富等同于幸福。|近义词：equalize, compare, liken, associate|反义词:differentiate, distinguish|verb
equated:/ɪˈkweɪtɪd/ | v. |使相等；等同（equate的过去式和过去分词）|Considered something to be the same as or equivalent to something else.|例句：The study equated the two groups in terms of education level. 这项研究在教育水平方面将两组等同起来。|近义词：equalized, compared, likened, associated|反义词:differentiated, distinguished|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *aequatus, past participle of aequare (“to make equal”), from Latin aequus (“even, level, equal”).
