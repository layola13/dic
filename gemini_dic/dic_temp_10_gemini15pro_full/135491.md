nonphonetic:/ˌnɒn.fəˈnet.ɪk/ /ˌnɑːn.fəˈnet̬.ɪk/ | adj. | 非语音的，不表音的 | Not relating to or based on the sounds of speech; not representing the sounds of speech. |  This writing system is nonphonetic, making it difficult to learn.  这种文字系统是非语音的，因此很难学习。| synonyms: non-phonemic, unphonetic | antonyms: phonetic, phonemic | adjective
