dubbed:/dʌbd/ |adj. | 配音的；称为；授予…称号 | used to describe something that has been given a particular name, especially describing what you think of it | This is the dubbed version of the film. 这是电影的配音版。| synonyms: named, called, entitled, christened | antonyms: unnamed | adjective
