ectal:/ˈɛktəl/
adjective
外胚层的; 体壁的
Relating to the ectoderm (the outermost of the three primary germ layers of an embryo).  Also, relating to the outer wall of a body cavity.
The ectal layer of the embryo develops into the skin and nervous system.  胚胎的外胚层发育成皮肤和神经系统。
The polyp's ectal surface is covered in tiny cilia.  息肉的外壁表面覆盖着微小的纤毛。
近义词: outer, external
反义词: endodermal, internal
词性: adjective

词源: From Greek *ektos* meaning outside.
