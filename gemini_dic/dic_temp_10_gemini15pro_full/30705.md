certify:/ˈsɜːrtɪfaɪ/ (US) /ˈsɜːtɪfaɪ/ (UK)| v.|证明; 证实; 保证; 颁发证书|to officially state or prove that something is true or correct|例句：The document certifies his birth in New York.  这份文件证明他出生在纽约。

例句：The software is certified for use on all Apple products. 该软件经认证可在所有苹果产品上使用。


近义词：confirm, attest, verify, guarantee, endorse|反义词：deny, refute, contradict|词性：动词

certify:/ˈsɜːrtɪfaɪ/ (US) /ˈsɜːtɪfaɪ/ (UK)| n.|证明; 证实; 保证; 颁发证书 (较少用作名词)|The act of certifying.|例句：The certification of the document is crucial.  文件的认证至关重要。

例句：He received his teaching certification last year. 他去年获得了教师资格证书。


近义词：confirmation, attestation, verification|反义词：denial, refutation, contradiction|词性：名词 (rare)

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French certifier, based on Latin certus ‘certain’.
