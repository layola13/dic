out-passes:/ˈaʊtˌpæsɪz/
noun
外出许可；出境证；（尤指比赛中）传球
Authorization or permit to leave a place, especially a pass in a game.
The prisoners were given out-passes to visit their families. 囚犯们获得了外出许可证去看望他们的家人。
The team's out-passes were accurate and effective. 球队的传球准确有效。
synonyms: permits, exits, clearances, passes
antonyms:  entries, denials, restrictions
