monera:/məˈnɪərə/
| n. |原核生物界|A kingdom that contains unicellular organisms without a nucleus, such as bacteria. | Example: Monera was once considered a kingdom that included all prokaryotic organisms. 原核生物界曾被认为是一个包含所有原核生物的界。| Synonym: prokaryote, bacteria (in older classifications) | Antonym: eukaryote | Noun
