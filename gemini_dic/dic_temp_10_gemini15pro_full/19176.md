bewilderednesses:/bɪˈwɪldərdnəsɪz/| n. |困惑，迷茫，慌乱|The state of being completely confused or perplexed.|例句：His bewilderednesses at the sudden change of plans was evident. 他对计划突然改变的困惑显而易见。|例句：She experienced a series of bewilderednesses upon entering the unfamiliar city.  进入陌生的城市后，她经历了一系列的迷茫。|近义词：confusion, perplexity, disorientation, bewilderment|反义词：clarity, understanding, composure|词性: 名词

词源: From bewildered + -ness. Bewildered comes from the Middle English *bewildren, meaning "to lead astray as if in a wilderness."  Ultimately from Old English *wilddeoren, "wild animals."
