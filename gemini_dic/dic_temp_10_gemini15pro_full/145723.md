overcredulities: /ˌoʊvərkrɪˈduːlətiz/ | n. | 过于轻信，过度信任 |  A tendency to believe things too readily, especially those that are improbable or based on insufficient evidence.  It refers to a state of excessive gullibility or naivete. |  His overcredulities led him to fall for numerous scams. 他的过于轻信导致他上了很多骗子的当。|  naivete, gullibility, credulity | skepticism, cynicism | Noun
