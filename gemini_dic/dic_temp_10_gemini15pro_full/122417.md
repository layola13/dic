moa:/ˈmoʊə/ (US) /ˈməʊə/ (UK)| n. |恐鸟；（射击运动的） MOA（minute of angle, 分角）|A large extinct flightless bird of New Zealand, similar to the emu and ostrich.  A unit of angular measurement equal to one sixtieth of a degree, used in adjusting sights on firearms. |例句：The moa was hunted to extinction by the Maori. 毛利人将恐鸟猎杀至灭绝。|近义词：ratite (for the bird), arcminute (for the unit of angle)|反义词: N/A (no direct antonym for either meaning) |词性: noun
