cataleptic:/ˌkætəˈlɛptɪk/| adj.|僵直性昏厥的，紧张性精神分裂症的|Relating to or resembling catalepsy, a condition characterized by muscular rigidity and a trance-like state of consciousness.|The patient exhibited cataleptic symptoms, remaining motionless for hours. 病人表现出僵直性昏厥症状，一动不动地保持了几个小时。|Cataleptic rigidity can be a frightening symptom. 紧张性精神分裂症的僵硬会是一个令人恐惧的症状。|Synonyms: rigid, immobile, trance-like|Antonyms: flexible, mobile, responsive|Adjective
