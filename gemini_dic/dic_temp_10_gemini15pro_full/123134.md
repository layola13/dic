monistic:/məˈnɪstɪk/
adjective
一元的；一元论的
Characterized by the doctrine of monism.  Relating to or characterized by the belief that reality consists of a single basic substance or element.
Monistic theories propose a single ultimate substance or principle as the ground of reality.
一元论理论提出单一的终极实体或原理作为现实的基础。
Spinoza's philosophy is often described as monistic.
斯宾诺莎的哲学常被描述为一元论。
近义词: unitary, holistic
反义词: dualistic, pluralistic
词性: adjective
