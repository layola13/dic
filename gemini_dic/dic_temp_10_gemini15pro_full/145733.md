overcriticism: /ˌoʊvərˈkrɪtɪˌsɪzəm/
noun
过度批评
The act of criticizing someone or something too much or too harshly.
He faced overcriticism from his parents for his academic performance. 他因为学习成绩受到了父母的过度批评。
The constant overcriticism demoralized the team.  不断的过度批评打击了团队的士气。

近义词: excessive criticism, hypercriticism, nitpicking
反义词: praise, encouragement, support
词性: noun

Etymology:
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "criticism" (from "criticize"  ultimately from Greek "kritikos" meaning "able to judge").
