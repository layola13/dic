febrile:/ˈfiːbraɪl/ /ˈfebraɪl/ | adj. | 发烧的，发热的 | Marked by fever; feverish. |  She became febrile and delirious. 她开始发烧并说胡话。

The child was febrile and listless.  孩子发烧了，并且无精打采。

近义词: feverish, pyretic | 反义词: afebrile, healthy | 词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *febrilis*, from *febris* "fever". Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\*gʷʰer-* "warm".
