gauntlet:/ˈɡɔːntlɪt/ | n. |  金属手套; 挑战; 严峻考验 | A glove with a long, flaring cuff, as worn by a knight in armor. A challenge. An ordeal or trial. |  The knight threw down his gauntlet, challenging the other to a duel. 骑士扔下他的金属手套，向另一个人挑战决斗。He had to run the gauntlet of hostile reporters. 他不得不面对充满敌意的记者们的严峻考验。 | Synonyms: challenge, trial, ordeal | Antonyms: peace, comfort, ease | noun


gauntlet:/ˈɡɔːntlɪt/ | v. |  戴金属手套; 挑战 | To challenge someone. | The young knight gauntleted the champion. 年轻的骑士向冠军发起了挑战。| Synonyms: challenge, defy | Antonyms: surrender, yield | verb
