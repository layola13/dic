metamorphically:/ˌmɛtəˈmɔːrfɪkli/ | adv. | 比喻地，象征性地 | In a way that involves metamorphosis; figuratively; symbolically.  It describes something that changes in a significant way, often used figuratively to describe a dramatic personal transformation. |  Her life metamorphically changed after she moved to a new city.  她搬到新城市后，生活发生了翻天覆地的变化。| synonym: figuratively, symbolically, allegorically | antonym: literally | word type: adverb
