harage:/ˈhærɪdʒ/ | n. | 车库，汽车修理厂 | A building where a car is kept, or where cars are repaired. |  例句：He keeps his classic car in the harage. 他把他的老爷车放在车库里。

例句：The car is being repaired at the harage. 这辆车正在汽车修理厂修理。| 近义词：garage, carport | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

词源:  Likely a misspelling of "garage," which comes from the French word "garer" meaning "to shelter."
