analogousnesses:/əˈnæləɡəsnəsɪz/
noun
相似性，类似性
The state or quality of being analogous; resemblance of relations; correspondence of functions.
The analogousnesses between the two systems are striking. 这两个系统之间的相似性令人震惊。
analogies, similarities, resemblances, parallels
differences, dissimilarities, disparities

Etymology:
From analogous + -ness.  Analogous comes from the Late Latin analogus, from the Greek analogos "proportionate," from ana- "according to" + logos "ratio, word."
