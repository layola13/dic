overgrieve: /ˌoʊvərˈɡriːv/
verb
过度悲伤；过分悲痛
To grieve excessively or for too long a period.
She overgrieved the loss of her pet hamster. 她为失去宠物仓鼠而过度悲伤。
The family overgrieved the death of their patriarch.  这个家庭为他们族长的去世而过度悲痛。

近义词: mourn excessively, grieve too much
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑) celebrate, rejoice
词性: verb
