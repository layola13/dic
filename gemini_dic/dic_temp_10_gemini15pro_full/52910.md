dimorph:/ˈdaɪmɔːrf/| n. adj.|二形体，二态性|An organism that exists in two distinct forms.|Example 1: The butterfly is a dimorph, existing as a caterpillar and then a winged adult. 蝴蝶是一种二形体，以毛毛虫和带翅膀的成虫两种形式存在。

Example 2:  Sexual dimorphism is common in birds, with males often having brighter plumage.  鸟类中性二态性很常见，雄性通常有更鲜艳的羽毛。|近义词：polymorph|反义词:monomorph|词性: 名词, 形容词

Etymology: From Greek *dimorphos*, from *di-* "two" + *morphē* "form".  源自希腊语 *dimorphos*，由 *di-* “两个” + *morphē* “形式” 组成。
