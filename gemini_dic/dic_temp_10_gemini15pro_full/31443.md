charityless: /ˈtʃærɪtilɪs/
adjective
无情的；缺乏仁慈的；吝啬的
Lacking charity; uncharitable.  Devoid of kindness and compassion.
He gave a charityless stare at the beggar. 他无情地盯着那个乞丐。
近义词: uncharitable, unkind, merciless, heartless, ruthless, cruel, inhumane, pitiless
反义词: charitable, kind, compassionate, benevolent, humane, merciful
词性: adjective

charityless: /ˈtʃærɪtilɪs/
adjective
无慈善心的；无同情心的
Not giving to charity; not inclined to give to charity.
The charityless millionaire hoarded his wealth.  那个吝啬的百万富翁囤积着他的财富。
近义词: miserly, stingy, tight-fisted, parsimonious, ungenerous
反义词: generous, philanthropic, altruistic
词性: adjective
