outstolen:/ˌaʊtˈstəʊlən/
verb
超过偷窃; 比...偷得更多
Having surpassed someone in stealing; stolen more than.
The notorious art thief bragged about having outstolen all his rivals.  这个臭名昭著的艺术窃贼吹嘘自己偷窃的艺术品比所有竞争对手都多。
近义词:outsmarted, outmaneuvered
反义词:surrendered, yielded
词性:verb
