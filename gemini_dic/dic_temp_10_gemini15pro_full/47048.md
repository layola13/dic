datadriven:/ˈdeɪtəˌdrɪvən/| adj.|数据驱动的|Driven by or based on data; decisions or actions determined by the analysis of data.|例句：Our marketing strategy is datadriven, using analytics to inform our campaigns. 我们的营销策略是数据驱动的，使用分析来指导我们的活动。

例句：Datadriven decision making is crucial for success in today's business world.  在当今的商业世界中，数据驱动的决策对于成功至关重要。|近义词：analytical, empirical, evidence-based|反义词：intuitive, subjective, anecdotal|词性:形容词
