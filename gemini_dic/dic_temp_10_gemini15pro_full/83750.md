hailstorms:/ˈheɪlstɔːrmz/ (US), /ˈheɪlstɔːmz/ (UK)| n. |冰雹|A storm with hail (pellets of frozen rain).|The hailstorm damaged several cars. 冰雹损坏了了几辆汽车。|Example: The sudden hailstorm caught everyone by surprise. 突如其来的冰雹让每个人都措手不及。|Synonyms: hail, ice storm|Antonyms: sunshine, clear skies|Noun
