idealizer:/aɪˈdiːəlaɪzər/ | n. | 理想化者；完美化者 |  A person who idealizes someone or something; someone who attributes exaggeratedly positive qualities to another person or thing. | She was an idealizer, always seeing the best in everyone, even when they didn't deserve it. 她是一个理想化者，总是看到每个人最好的一面，即使他们不值得。

He is an idealizer of the past, remembering only the good times and forgetting the hardships. 他是一个过去理想化者，只记得美好的时光，忘记了艰辛。| 近义词: romanticizer, optimist | 反义词: critic, cynic, realist | 词性: noun
