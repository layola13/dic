foreshadows:/fɔːrˈʃædoʊz/ /fɔːrˈʃædəʊz/
verb
预示，预兆
indicates or suggests something that is going to happen in the future.
The dark clouds foreshadow a storm.  乌云预示着暴风雨即将来临。
His early interest in airplanes foreshadowed his later career as a pilot. 他早期对飞机的兴趣预示着他后来成为一名飞行员。
近义词: presage, portend, augur, forebode
反义词: belie
词性: verb
