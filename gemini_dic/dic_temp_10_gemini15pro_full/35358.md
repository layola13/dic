co-host:/ˈkoʊ hoʊst/ (US), /ˈkəʊ həʊst/ (UK)| n. vt.|共同主持人，共同主办|A person who hosts an event, such as a television show or a conference, alongside another host.|Ellen DeGeneres co-hosted the Oscars with Neil Patrick Harris. 艾伦·德杰尼勒斯和尼尔·帕特里克·哈里斯共同主持了奥斯卡颁奖典礼。|They co-hosted a fundraising gala. 他们共同主办了一场筹款晚会。|synonyms: joint host, co-presenter|antonyms: guest, attendee|noun, verb
