grieves:/ɡriːvz/| v. |悲痛，悲伤，使悲伤|To feel or express deep sadness, especially because of someone's death.|例句：She still grieves for her lost child. 她仍然为失去的孩子感到悲痛。

例句：The news of his death grieved us all. 他去世的消息使我们所有人都感到悲伤。|近义词：mourns, sorrows, laments|反义词：rejoices, celebrates|词性:动词

grieving:/ˈɡriːvɪŋ/| adj. |悲痛的，悲伤的|Feeling or expressing deep sadness, especially because of someone's death.|例句：The grieving family gathered at the funeral. 悲痛的家人聚集在葬礼上。

例句：She offered her condolences to the grieving widow. 她向悲痛的寡妇表示慰问。|近义词：mourning, sorrowful, heartbroken|反义词：joyful, happy|词性:形容词

词源: From Old French *grever, from Latin gravare "to burden," related to gravis "heavy."
