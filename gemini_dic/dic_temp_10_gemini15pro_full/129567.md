non-challenging:/ˌnɒnˈtʃælɪndʒɪŋ/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˈtʃælɪndʒɪŋ/| adj. |没有挑战性的，不费力的|Not demanding; easy to do or deal with.  Requiring little effort or skill.|This course is non-challenging and suitable for beginners. 这门课程没有挑战性，适合初学者。|Synonyms: easy, simple, undemanding, straightforward, effortless|Antonyms: challenging, difficult, demanding, strenuous, arduous|adjective
