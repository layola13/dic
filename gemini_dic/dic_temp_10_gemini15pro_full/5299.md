allotropicity:/ˌæləˈtrɒpɪsɪti/ |n.|同素异形性|The ability of a chemical element to exist in two or more different forms, in the same physical state.|Diamond and graphite exhibit the allotropicity of carbon. 钻石和石墨展现了碳的同素异形性。|Near synonyms: allotropy, polymorphism|Antonym: N/A|noun
