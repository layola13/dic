autosoteric:/ˌɔːtəʊˈsɒtərɪk/
adjective
深奥的，秘传的; 只为少数人所知的
Relating to esoteric knowledge or traditions known only to a select group.
Autosoteric teachings are only revealed to initiated members of the society.  这个社团的秘传教义只向入会成员透露。
The autosoteric nature of the ritual made it even more fascinating.  仪式的秘传性质使它更加引人入胜。
近义词: esoteric, arcane, occult, mystic
反义词: exoteric, public, common
词性: adjective

词源:
From auto- (self) + esoteric (intended for or likely to be understood by only a small number of people with specialized knowledge or interest).
