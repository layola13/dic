ETYMOLOGY->
From "graphical" + "-ness". "Graphical" comes from "graphic," which originates from the Greek "graphikos" (γραφικός), meaning "of or for writing, drawn, painted," from "graphein" (γράφειν), meaning "to write, draw, paint."  The suffix "-ness" is used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
