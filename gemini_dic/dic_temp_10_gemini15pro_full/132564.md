nondiaphanousnesses:/ˌnɒndaɪəˈfænəsnɪsɪz/ | n. |不透明性；不透光性|The state or quality of being nondiaphanous; opacity.  The plural form of nondiaphanousness. |The multiple layers of fabric contributed to the nondiaphanousnesses of the curtains, ensuring complete privacy.  The artist experimented with different pigments to achieve varying degrees of nondiaphanousnesses in their paintings.| 近义词: opacities, opaqueness | 反义词: transparencies, translucencies | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From non- + diaphanous + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  "Diaphanous" comes from the Greek "diaphanēs" meaning "transparent, translucent."
