mounties: /ˈmaʊntiz/
noun
骑警，加拿大皇家骑警
Members of the Royal Canadian Mounted Police, a national police force.
例句：The Mounties are known for their red serge uniforms.  加拿大骑警以他们的红色制服而闻名。

例句：The mounties arrived quickly at the scene of the accident. 骑警迅速抵达事故现场。


近义词：police, RCMP, officers
反义词: civilians, criminals
词性: noun
