ensurers:/ɪnˈʃʊrərz/
n.
担保人，保证人;保险商
People or organizations that make sure something happens or is definite.  Those who guarantee or provide insurance.
The insurers will cover the cost of the damage. 保险公司将承担损失费用。
近义词: guarantors, underwriters, sponsors
反义词:  N/A
词性: 名词

ensurers:/ɪnˈʃʊrərz/
Etymology: From the verb "ensure," which comes from Old French "enseurer," meaning "to make certain."  Ultimately derived from Latin "in-" (in, into) + "securus" (secure, safe).
词源: 源于动词 "ensure"，来自古法语 "enseurer"，意为“确保”。最终源于拉丁语 "in-" (在...内，进入) + "securus" (安全的)。
