outdo:/ˌaʊtˈduː/
verb
胜过，超过
to be better or more successful than someone or something else
He outdid all the other runners and won the race. 他超过了所有其他选手，赢得了比赛。
surpass, excel, exceed, beat, outstrip
fall behind, be inferior to
词性: 动词

outdo:/ˌaʊtˈduː/

做得比…更好
to do something better than someone else
