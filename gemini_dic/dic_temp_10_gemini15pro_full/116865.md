maxing:/ˈmæksɪŋ/ | v. | 最大化，达到最大值 |  The act of maximizing something, often referring to a skill, statistic, or resource in a game or activity. | He's maxing out his credit cards. 他把信用卡都刷爆了 (达到额度上限). | She's been maxing her stats in the game. 她在游戏中一直在提升自己的各项数值到最高. | synonyms: maximizing, optimizing, boosting | antonyms: minimizing, reducing | verb (present participle)

maxing (out):/ˈmæksɪŋ aʊt/ | v. |  达到极限，用尽 |  Reaching the maximum limit or capacity of something. |  I maxed out my credit card buying Christmas presents. 我买圣诞礼物把信用卡刷爆了. | The weightlifter maxed out at 300 pounds. 举重运动员最大举重达到300磅. | synonyms: reaching the limit, topping out | antonyms: starting, beginning | verb phrase
