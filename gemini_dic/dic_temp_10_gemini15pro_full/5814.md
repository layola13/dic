amasses:/əˈmæsɪz/
verb
积聚，积累
gathers together or accumulates, especially in large quantities.
He amassed a fortune in real estate. 他在房地产领域积累了巨额财富。
Synonyms: accumulate, gather, collect, stockpile
Antonyms: disperse, scatter, dissipate, squander
词性:动词
