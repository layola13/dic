bottlescrew:/ˈbɑːt(ə)lˌskruː/ | n. | 瓶塞起子；螺旋拉紧器 | A device for tightening wires or ropes, consisting of a screw between two eyes or hooks.  It can also be a corkscrew used for opening wine bottles. |  She used a bottlescrew to tighten the guy ropes of the tent. 她用螺旋拉紧器拉紧了帐篷的拉绳。

The old sailor opened the wine with a bottlescrew. 老水手用瓶塞起子打开了葡萄酒。 | 近义词:turnbuckle, corkscrew, wine opener | 反义词: N/A | noun
