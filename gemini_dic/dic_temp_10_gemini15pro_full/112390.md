lossiest:/ˈlɔːsiɪst/
adj.
损失最大的，损耗最大的
Having or causing the greatest loss or waste, especially of energy or data.
This is the lossiest compression method, resulting in the smallest file size but the poorest quality. 这是损耗最大的压缩方法，会导致文件大小最小，但质量最差。
The old pipes were the lossiest part of the irrigation system.  这些旧管道是灌溉系统中损耗最大的部分。
近义词:least efficient, most wasteful, most dissipative
反义词:most efficient, least wasteful,  most conservative
词性:形容词

词源:
From loss + -y + -est.  "Loss" comes from Old Norse "los" meaning destruction.
