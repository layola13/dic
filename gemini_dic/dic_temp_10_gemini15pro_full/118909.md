metayage:/mɛˈteɪɪdʒ/ | n. |分成制，分佃制 | A system of land tenure in which the tenant pays the landlord a portion (usually one-half) of the produce. | Example 1: The farmer worked the land under a metayage agreement, sharing half his harvest with the landowner.  这位农民根据分成制协议耕种土地，将一半的收成与地主分享。

Example 2:  Metayage was a common practice in pre-industrial France. 分成制在工业化前的法国是一种常见的做法。 | Synonym: sharecropping | Antonym: freehold | noun
