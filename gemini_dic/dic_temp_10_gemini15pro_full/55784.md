divert:/daɪˈvɜːrt/, /dɪˈvɜːrt/| v. | 转移；使分心；娱乐 | To turn aside from a course or direction; to distract; to amuse | The loud noise diverted my attention from my work.  喧闹的声音转移了我工作的注意力。| synonyms: redirect, reroute, distract, entertain | antonyms: focus, concentrate | verb


diversion:/daɪˈvɜːrʒən/, /dɪˈvɜːrʒən/| n. | 转移；消遣；娱乐 | The act of diverting or turning aside; something that distracts or amuses |  Reading is my favorite diversion. 阅读是我最喜欢的消遣。| synonyms: distraction, entertainment, pastime | antonyms: focus, concentration | noun
