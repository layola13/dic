colour-blind: /ˈkʌlə blaɪnd/ (UK) /ˈkʌlər blaɪnd/ (US)| adj.|色盲的|Unable to distinguish certain colours, especially red and green.|例句：My uncle is colour-blind and can't tell the difference between red and green. 我叔叔是色盲，分不清红色和绿色。

例句：The test revealed that he was colour-blind. 测试显示他是色盲。

近义词：colorblind (US), color deficient|反义词：N/A|词性: adjective
