enlighteners: /ɪnˈlaɪtənərz/ | n. | 启蒙者；教化者 | People who bring intellectual or spiritual enlightenment.  Those who educate others, often challenging traditional beliefs and promoting reason and knowledge. | The Enlightenment thinkers, the enlighteners of the 18th century, championed reason and individual liberty. 十八世纪的启蒙思想家，启蒙运动的倡导者，拥护理性与个人自由。|  The enlighteners of the Renaissance period rediscovered classical knowledge and sparked a renewed interest in art and science.  文艺复兴时期的启蒙者重新发现了古典知识，并激发了人们对艺术和科学的新兴趣。 | synonyms: educators, teachers, mentors, illuminators | antonyms: obscurantists, reactionaries | noun
