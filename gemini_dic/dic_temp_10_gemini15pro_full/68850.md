fashioned:/ˈfæʃənd/| adj.|过时的，老式的；（以特定方式）制作的|Made or shaped in a particular way.  Created, designed, or made in a skilled way.|She was wearing an old-fashioned dress. 她穿着一件老式的连衣裙。|The table was fashioned from a single piece of wood. 这张桌子是用一块木头做成的。|近义词：outdated, old, styled, crafted, made|反义词：modern, contemporary, new|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *fascioner (modern French façonner), from Latin factionem, accusative of factio ("a making, doing"), from facere ("to make, do").
