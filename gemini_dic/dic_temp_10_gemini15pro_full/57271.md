downpouring:/ˌdaʊnˈpɔːrɪŋ/| adj.|倾盆大雨的；暴雨倾盆的|A heavy downpour of rain.  A heavy fall of rain.|The rain was downpouring, making it difficult to see the road ahead.  雨倾盆而下，很难看清前方的路。|Synonyms: torrential, pouring, pelting|Antonyms: drizzling, sprinkling|adjective
