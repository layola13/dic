futuristically:/ˌfjuːtʃəˈrɪstɪkli/
adverb|未来式地，未来主义地|In a way that suggests the future, especially in appearance or design.  以一种暗示未来的方式，尤其是在外观或设计上。|例句：The building was futuristically designed with sleek lines and metallic surfaces.  这座建筑采用未来主义设计，线条流畅，表面呈金属质感。

例句：Her clothes were always so futuristically styled. 她的服装总是那么具有未来主义风格。
近义词：advanced, modern, futuristic, visionary|反义词：old-fashioned, outdated, archaic, traditional|词性：副词
