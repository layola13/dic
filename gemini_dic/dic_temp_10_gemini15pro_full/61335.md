embay:/ɪmˈbeɪ/ | vt. |嵌入，插入，使融入| To fix something firmly into a surrounding mass. | The archaeologist found a coin embedded in the rock. 考古学家发现一枚嵌入岩石中的硬币。

The bullet embedded itself in the wall. 子弹嵌入了墙里。 | 近义词：embed, insert, implant, encase | 反义词：extract, remove, dislodge | 词性: 动词
