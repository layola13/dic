centralistic:/ˌsɛntrəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
中央集权的
Relating to or characterized by centralism; advocating the concentration of power or control in a central authority.
The government implemented a centralistic policy, taking power away from local municipalities. 政府实施了一项中央集权政策，剥夺了地方市政当局的权力。
The party's centralistic approach alienated many voters who preferred more local autonomy. 该党的中央集权主义做法疏远了许多倾向于地方自治的选民。

近义词:centralized, unitary, top-down
反义词:decentralized, federal, distributed
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From central + -istic.  "Central" ultimately derives from the Latin "centrum" meaning "center."  The suffix "-istic" forms adjectives meaning "relating to" or "characteristic of."
