misleadingnesses:/ˌmɪsˈliːdɪŋnɪsɪz/
noun
误导性（复数）
The quality of being misleading; deceptiveness.  This word is rarely used; "misleading nature" or similar phrasing is more common.
The misleadingnesses of the advertisements eventually led to a class-action lawsuit.  这些广告的误导性最终导致了一场集体诉讼。
The numerous misleadingnesses in the politician's speech angered many voters.  这位政治家演讲中的众多误导性言论激怒了许多选民。
Synonyms: deceptiveness, deceptivenesses, fallaciousness, spuriousness
Antonyms: truthfulness, accuracy, honesty
