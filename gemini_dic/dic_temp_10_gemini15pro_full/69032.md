fatuous:/ˈfætʃuəs/ | adj. | 愚蠢的；傻的；昏庸的 | Showing a lack of intelligence or good judgment; silly and pointless. |  The politician made a fatuous remark about the economy.  这位政客对经济发表了愚蠢的评论。 |  He gave a fatuous grin. 他傻傻地咧嘴一笑。| Synonyms: foolish, silly, inane, asinine, vapid | Antonyms: sensible, intelligent, wise, astute | adjective
