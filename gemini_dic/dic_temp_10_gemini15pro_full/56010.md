do-in:/ˈduːɪn/ | n. | 凶杀，谋杀；(尤指)暗杀 | An act of killing, especially secretly. | The police suspected a do-in. 警方怀疑这是一起谋杀。 | 近义词: murder, assassination, killing | 反义词: resuscitation, revival | 词性: 名词

词源: From Japanese 同 Japanese どういん (dōin)  <  どう (dō, “same, together, jointly”) + いん (in, “temple, institution”). Literally meaning "same institution" or "temple gathering". It originally referred to a form of self-massage and exercise practiced in Zen Buddhism, but in English it has acquired the meaning of "murder".
