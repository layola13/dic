insulations:/ˌɪnsəˈleɪʃənz/
n.
绝缘，隔热；绝缘材料；隔离
Material used to prevent the passage of heat, sound, or electricity.
The house has good insulation, so it stays warm in the winter. 这房子隔热性能好，所以冬天很暖和。
insulation materials, thermal insulation, soundproofing
conduction, connection

词性: 名词 (Noun)
