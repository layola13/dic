deafening:/ˈdɛfənɪŋ/ (US), /ˈdɛfnɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|震耳欲聋的，极喧闹的|So loud as to make it impossible to hear anything else.|The music at the concert was deafening. 音乐会上的音乐震耳欲聋。|Synonyms: earsplitting, thunderous, overpowering, loud, booming|Antonyms: quiet, silent, hushed, faint, inaudible|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From deafen + -ing.  Deafen comes from Middle English defen, from Old English deafian, from deaf.
