nightlike:/ˈnaɪtlaɪk/
adjective
像夜晚的；夜间的
Resembling or characteristic of night; nocturnal.
The owl's nightlike vision allowed it to hunt effectively in the darkness. 猫头鹰的夜视能力使它能够在黑暗中有效地捕猎。
The forest had a nightlike quality even during the day. 即使在白天，森林也有一种夜晚般的气氛。
Near synonyms: nocturnal, night, dark
Opposite: daylike, diurnal
词性: adjective
