counterblast:/ˈkaʊntərbæst/ | n. | 反击；猛烈抨击；对抗性的言论或行动 | a strong reply or attack, especially in words |  The politician issued a counterblast against the accusations of corruption.  这位政治家对腐败指控进行了猛烈抨击。 | synonyms: retort, rebuttal, riposte, rejoinder | antonyms: agreement, acceptance, concession | noun
counterblast:/ˈkaʊntərbæst/ | n. | 响亮的爆炸声; 巨响 | a loud, explosive sound | The demolition of the building created a mighty counterblast.  建筑物的拆除产生了巨大的爆炸声。 | synonyms: explosion, detonation, blast | antonyms: silence, whisper | noun
