mastology:/mæˈstɑːlədʒi/
n.
乳腺学；乳房学
The study of the breasts, particularly their structure, function, and diseases.
Mammography is an important tool in mastology for early detection of breast cancer. 乳房 X 射线照相术是乳腺学中早期发现乳腺癌的重要工具。
近义词:mammology
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun

Etymology:
From Greek mastos (breast) + -logy (study of).
