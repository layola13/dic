discubitory:/dɪsˈkʌbɪtɔːri/
adjective
斜倚的，躺着的
Relating to reclining or lying down.
He preferred a discubitory posture while reading. 他喜欢斜倚着看书。
The Roman feast featured discubitory dining, with guests reclining on couches. 罗马盛宴以斜倚用餐为特色，客人们斜躺在沙发上。
近义词:recumbent, reclining, prone
反义词:upright, standing, sitting
词性:adjective

词源:
From Latin discubitōrius (“pertaining to lying down”), from discumbō (“I lie down”), from dis- (“apart, asunder”) +‎ cubō (“I lie down”).
