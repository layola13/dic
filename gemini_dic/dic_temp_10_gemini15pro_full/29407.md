cataclysmically:/ˌkætəˈklɪzmɪkli/
adverb
灾难性地;剧烈地
In a manner that involves or results in a catastrophe.
The city was cataclysmically destroyed by the earthquake.  这座城市被地震灾难性地摧毁了。
The economy collapsed cataclysmically. 经济灾难性地崩溃了。
近义词:disastrously, catastrophically, devastatingly
反义词:beneficially, favorably, advantageously
词性:adverb

词源:
From Ancient Greek κατακλυσμός (kataklusmós, “deluge, flood”) + -ically.
