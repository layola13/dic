antihistoricalnesses:/ˌæntiˌhɪˈstɔːrɪkəlnəsɪz/
|n.|反历史性；违反历史性的状态|The book was criticized for its antihistoricalnesses, particularly its disregard for established chronology.|例句：The author's antihistoricalnesses led to several factual errors in his account of the war.  作者的反历史性导致了他的战争记录中的一些事实错误。

例句：The film's many antihistoricalnesses made it difficult for historians to take it seriously.  这部电影的许多反历史性使得历史学家很难认真对待它。
|近义词： ahistoricity, anachronism|反义词：historicity, historical accuracy|词性: noun
