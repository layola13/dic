entrappers:/ɪnˈtræpərz/| n. |设陷阱者，诱捕者|People who trap others, either literally or figuratively, by tricking or deceiving them into a difficult situation.|The police used undercover officers as entrappers to catch the drug dealers. 警方利用卧底警员设陷阱抓捕毒贩。|Synonyms: trappers, ensnarers, deceivers|Antonyms: rescuers, liberators|noun
