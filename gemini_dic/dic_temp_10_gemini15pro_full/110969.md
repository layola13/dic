liqueurs:/lɪˈkɜːrz/ (US), /lɪˈkjʊəz/ (UK)
n.
利口酒，甜酒
Sweet alcoholic drinks, often flavored with fruits, herbs, or spices.
She offered him a choice of liqueurs after dinner. 她晚饭后给他提供了各种利口酒。
After dinner, we enjoyed some coffee and liqueurs. 晚饭后，我们享用了咖啡和利口酒。
synonyms: cordials, aperitifs, digestifs
antonyms: non-alcoholic beverages
