antlered:/ˈæntlərd/| adj.|长角的，有鹿角的|Having antlers; bearing antlers.  Describes an animal, typically a deer, elk, or moose, that has antlers.|例句：The antlered stag stood majestically in the clearing. 长角的雄鹿雄赳赳地站在空地上。|近义词：horned, antler-bearing|反义词:antlerless|词性:形容词
