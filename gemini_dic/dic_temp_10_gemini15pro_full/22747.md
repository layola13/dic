boroughmaster: /ˈbʌrəˌmæstər/ | n. | 市政官，镇长 | An officer in some boroughs or towns having similar duties to a mayor.  A boroughmaster is a chief magistrate or executive officer of a borough. | The boroughmaster announced the new town square project. 市政官宣布了新的城镇广场项目。| The boroughmaster called a town meeting to discuss the budget. 市政官召集了镇民大会讨论预算。| synonym: mayor, burgomaster | antonym: N/A | noun


Etymology: From *burh* ("fortified place") + *-gerefa* ("official"). Cognate with Dutch *burgemeester*, German *Bürgermeister*.
