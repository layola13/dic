escalop: /ɪˈskæləp/ /ɛˈskɑːləp/ | n. | 薄肉片；扇贝 | A thin boneless slice of meat, often veal or pork, usually coated in breadcrumbs and fried. Also, a large edible bivalve mollusk with a ribbed fan-shaped shell. |  例句：
He ordered veal escalopes with lemon sauce. 他点了一份柠檬酱小牛肉片。

The restaurant served fresh escalops seared to perfection. 这家餐厅供应鲜嫩的、煎至完美的扇贝。

近义词：cutlet, scallop (for shellfish), fillet | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
