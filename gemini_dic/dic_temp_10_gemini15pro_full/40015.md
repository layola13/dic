consumable:/kənˈsuːməbl/ | adj. n. | 可消耗的；消耗品 | Something that can be used up and needs to be replaced.  As a noun, it refers to goods that are intended to be used up and then replaced. |  The printer requires consumable ink cartridges. 这台打印机需要可消耗的墨盒。 | synonyms: expendable, disposable, single-use | antonyms: durable, lasting, permanent | adjective

consumable:/kənˈsuːməbl/ | n. | 消耗品 | Goods that are intended to be used up and then replaced. | Office consumables include paper, pens, and staples. 办公室消耗品包括纸张、笔和订书钉。 | synonyms: supplies, provisions, goods | antonyms: durables, capital goods | noun
