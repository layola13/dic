cathartic:/kəˈθɑːrtɪk/
adjective
宣泄的；净化心灵的
Providing psychological relief through the open expression of strong emotions; relating to catharsis.
Crying is a cathartic release. 哭泣是一种情感的宣泄。
近义词: cleansing, purifying, purging, relieving
反义词: repressive, suppressive
词性: adjective

catharsis:/kəˈθɑːrsɪs/
noun
情感宣泄；净化
The process of releasing, and thereby providing relief from, strong or repressed emotions.  
Writing poetry can be a form of catharsis. 写诗可以是一种情感宣泄的方式。
近义词: release, purging, cleansing
反义词: repression, suppression
词性: noun
