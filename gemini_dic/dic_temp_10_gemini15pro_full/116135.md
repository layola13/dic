maryolatry:/ˌmɛəriˈɒlətri/ | n. | 圣母崇拜；对圣母玛利亚的过度崇拜 | Excessive veneration of the Virgin Mary. |  The Catholic Church distinguishes between proper veneration of Mary and the excessive devotion that constitutes mariolatry. 天主教会区分对玛丽的适当崇敬和构成圣母崇拜的过度虔诚。

Mary's powerful intercession is seen by some as bordering on mariolatry.  有些人认为玛丽强有力的代祷近乎于圣母崇拜。| 近义词:  Marianism, excessive devotion to Mary | 反义词:  iconoclasm (对圣像的破坏), rejection of religious images | 词性: noun
