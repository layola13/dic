leely:/ˈliːli/| adj.|可爱的，讨人喜欢的|Charming, appealing, often used to describe something small and delicate.  Derived from "lee," meaning sheltered, plus the diminutive suffix "-ly."  Originally meant "delicate" and evolved to describe something pleasant and attractive. |The child had a leely smile.  这个孩子笑起来很可爱。|synonyms: sweet, charming, delightful, cute, winsome |antonyms: unpleasant, unattractive, repulsive|adjective
