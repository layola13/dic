nonvegetative: /ˌnɒnˈvɛdʒɪtətɪv/ /ˌnɑːnˈvɛdʒɪteɪtɪv/ | adj. | 非植物性的，非营养性的；非植物生长的 | Not relating to or characteristic of plant life or growth; not involving or concerned with growth or reproduction; relating to or denoting reproduction by asexual means rather than by seeds or spores. |  The nonvegetative reproduction of some fungi involves the production of spores. 一些真菌的非植物性繁殖涉及孢子的产生。

|  The study focused on the nonvegetative components of the soil. 这项研究的重点是土壤中的非植物成分。 | Near synonyms:  abiotic, nonbiological, inanimate | Antonyms: vegetative, plant, biological
