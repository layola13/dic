misadventure:/ˌmɪsədˈvɛntʃər/| n.|不幸的遭遇，灾难|An unfortunate or unlucky accident; a mishap.|例句：Their camping trip was filled with misadventure, from a flat tire to a sudden rainstorm. 他们的露营之旅充满了不幸，从爆胎到突如其来的暴雨。

例句：He recounted the misadventures of his youth with a wry smile. 他苦笑着讲述了他年轻时的不幸遭遇。|近义词：mishap, misfortune, accident, calamity|反义词：fortune, luck, success|词性：名词
