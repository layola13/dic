deontology:/ˌdiːɒnˈtɒlədʒi/ /ˌdiːɑːnˈtɑːlədʒi/| n.|义务论，道义论|The study of the nature of duty and obligation.|例句：Deontology emphasizes the inherent rightness or wrongness of actions, regardless of their consequences.  义务论强调行为本身的对错，而不管其后果如何。

例句：Kantian deontology is a prominent example of this ethical framework. 康德义务论是这种伦理框架的一个突出例子。|近义词：ethics, moral philosophy|反义词:consequentialism, teleology|词性:noun
