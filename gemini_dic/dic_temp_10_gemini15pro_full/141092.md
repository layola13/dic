onscreen:/ˈɒnˌskriːn/ (UK), /ˈɑːnˌskriːn/ (US)| adj. adv.|在屏幕上的，屏幕上地| Appearing or existing on a screen, such as a television, computer, or movie screen.|
The onscreen graphics were impressive.  屏幕上的图形令人印象深刻。

The director told the actor to move slightly onscreen. 导演告诉演员在屏幕上稍微移动一下。


|近义词：displayed, visible, projected|反义词：offscreen|词性：adjective, adverb
