nonaffiliating:/ˌnɒnəˈfɪliˌeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非附属的，无关联的
Not officially attached to or connected with a larger organization or group.
Many nonaffiliating voters are likely to support the independent candidate. 许多无党派选民可能会支持这位独立候选人。
Synonyms: independent, unaffiliated, unattached, freelance
Antonyms: affiliated, associated, connected, related
词性: adjective
