galvanical:/ɡælˈvænɪk(ə)l/
adjective
电流的，电击的，惊人的，震撼的
of or relating to galvanism; produced by or producing galvanism.  Also, startling, shocking.
The galvanical reaction caused the muscles to twitch. 电流反应导致肌肉抽搐。

The galvanical performance left the audience breathless.  震撼的表演让观众屏住呼吸。


近义词:electric, shocking, startling, electrifying
反义词:soothing, calming, uneventful


词性:adjective
词源:From French *galvanique*, from *galvanisme* (“galvanism”) (see galvanism) + *-ique* (English *-ic*).
