meannesses:/ˈmiːnɪsnɪsɪz/
noun
卑鄙行为；刻薄；吝啬
The state of being mean or ungenerous.  Acts of meanness or spite.
He was shocked by the meannesses of his supposed friends. 他对他那些所谓朋友的卑鄙行为感到震惊。
synonyms: unkindness, nastiness, spitefulness, maliciousness, viciousness, cruelty, brutality, barbarity, inhumanity, savagery, mercilessness, ruthlessness, heartlessness, callousness, coldness, hardness, harshness, severity, stringency,  pettiness, small-mindedness, stinginess, miserliness, niggardliness, parsimony, illiberality, avarice, cupidity, greed, rapacity, close-fistedness, tight-fistedness
antonyms: kindness, generosity, compassion, benevolence
