mockney:/ˈmɒkni/ (UK) /ˈmɑːkni/ (US)| n. |伦敦东区模仿口音; 仿伦敦东区口音的人 |A type of accent and speech associated with working-class London, often seen as an affectation when adopted by those from other social backgrounds. It blends elements of Cockney and Received Pronunciation. | He put on a mockney accent to try and blend in with the locals. 他故意操起一种模仿伦敦东区口音，试图融入当地人。| 近义词: Estuary English | 反义词: Received Pronunciation | 词性: noun
