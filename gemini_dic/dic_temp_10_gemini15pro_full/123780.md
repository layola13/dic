mooted:/ˈmuːtɪd/ | adj. | 已提出的，被讨论的，悬而未决的 | Raised or brought up for discussion, especially for consideration; open to debate or discussion; mentioned or suggested as a possibility.  |  The possibility of merging the two companies was mooted at the meeting.  合并两家公司的可能性在会议上被提出。 | Synonyms:  discussed, debated, suggested, proposed, raised, broached, mentioned | Antonyms:  unmentioned, undiscussed, unresolved, settled, decided | adjective

mooted:/ˈmuːtɪd/ | v. | 提出，讨论（尤指供考虑）| To raise (a question or topic) for discussion; suggest (something) for consideration.  | They mooted the idea of a merger. 他们提出了合并的想法。 | Synonyms:  propose, suggest, raise, broach, mention, introduce | Antonyms:  withdraw, retract, suppress, conceal | verb
