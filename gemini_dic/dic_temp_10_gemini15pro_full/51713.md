devises:/dɪˈvaɪzɪz/ | n. |  (法律)遗赠的财产(尤指不动产) | (law) a gift of real property by will | He left all his devises to his children. 他把所有不动产都遗赠给了他的孩子们。 | synonyms: bequest, legacy | antonyms: inheritance (can be acquired other ways than by will) | noun (plural devises)
