deadly:/ˈdedli/| adj. adv.|致命的，极度的|Causing or able to cause death.|例句：The cobra's venom is deadly. 眼镜蛇的毒液是致命的。

例句：A deadly silence fell over the room. 房间里一片死寂。|近义词：lethal, fatal, mortal, dangerous|反义词：harmless, safe, beneficial|adjective

deadly:/ˈdedli/| adv.|非常，极其|To a very great degree; extremely.|例句：The meeting was deadly dull.  这次会议非常无聊。

例句：She's deadly serious about her career. 她对自己的事业非常认真。|近义词：extremely, very, exceedingly|反义词：slightly, mildly|adverb
