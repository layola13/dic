nothing-doing:/ˈnʌθɪŋ ˈduːɪŋ/
interj.|不行；没门；绝对不行|An emphatic refusal or rejection.| "Can I borrow your car?" "Nothing doing!"  “我能借你的车吗？” “不行！” |Synonyms: no way, absolutely not, not a chance, out of the question|Antonyms: certainly, definitely, by all means|interjection

nothing-doing:/ˈnʌθɪŋ ˈduːɪŋ/
adj.|徒劳的；无用的|Unsuccessful; futile.|All his efforts were nothing doing. 他所有的努力都是徒劳的。|Synonyms: fruitless, futile, useless, vain|Antonyms: successful, fruitful, effective|adjective
