dismissive:/dɪsˈmɪsɪv/| adj.|轻蔑的，不屑一顾的|Showing a lack of respect and interest; tending to dismiss or reject something or someone.|She gave a dismissive wave of her hand. 她轻蔑地挥了挥手。|Synonyms: contemptuous, disdainful, scornful|Antonyms: respectful, attentive, interested|adjective
