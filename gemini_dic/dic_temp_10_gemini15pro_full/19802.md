Biltmore: /ˈbɪltmɔːr/
proper noun
比尔特莫庄园
A large private estate and tourist attraction in Asheville, North Carolina, built by George Vanderbilt II between 1889 and 1895.  It is the largest privately owned house in the United States.
Example: We toured Biltmore Estate last summer and were amazed by its grandeur.  去年夏天我们参观了比尔特莫庄园，它的宏伟壮丽让我们惊叹。
Synonym: mansion, estate
Antonym:  hut, shack
word type: noun
