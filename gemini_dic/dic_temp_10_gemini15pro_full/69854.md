ferrocyanate:/ˌfɛroʊˈsaɪəneɪt/ | n. |亚铁氰化物；六氰合铁(II)酸盐 |A salt containing the anion [Fe(CN)₆]⁴⁻.  It forms stable complexes with various metal ions. | Potassium ferrocyanate is used as an anticaking agent in table salt. 亚铁氰化钾在食盐中用作抗结块剂。| synonym: hexacyanoferrate(II) | antonym: ferricyanate | noun

ferrocyanide:/ˌfɛroʊˈsaɪənaɪd/ | n. |亚铁氰化物；六氰合铁(II)酸盐 |The anion [Fe(CN)₆]⁴⁻, or a salt containing it.  It is a coordination complex of iron(II) with cyanide ligands. |Sodium ferrocyanide is a common reagent in analytical chemistry. 亚铁氰化钠是分析化学中常用的试剂。| synonym: hexacyanoferrate(II) | antonym: ferricyanide | noun
