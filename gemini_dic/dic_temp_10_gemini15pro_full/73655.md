forgave:/fərˈɡeɪv/
verb
原谅，饶恕
past tense of "forgive," meaning to grant pardon for an offense; to cease to feel resentment against.
She forgave him for his mistake. 她原谅了他的错误。
He forgave her for lying. 他原谅了她撒谎。
近义词：pardoned, excused, absolved
反义词：condemned, blamed, resented
词性：verb
