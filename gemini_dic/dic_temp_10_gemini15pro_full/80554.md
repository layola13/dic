goodwill:/ɡʊdˈwɪl/| n.| 商誉；友好；善意| the established reputation of a business regarded as a quantifiable asset and calculated as the difference between the company's market value and the sum of its tangible assets.|  The company's goodwill is its most valuable asset. 该公司的商誉是其最有价值的资产。| synonyms: benevolence, kindness, amity | antonyms: hostility, malice, ill will | noun
