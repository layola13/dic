catsuits:/ˈkætˌsuːts/ | n. |紧身连衣裤 |A close-fitting, one-piece garment that covers the torso and legs, often made of stretchy material like spandex or lycra.  |例句：The actress wore a dazzling red catsuit in the movie.  这位女演员在电影中穿了一件耀眼的红色紧身连衣裤。

|例句：Catsuits are popular choices for athletes, dancers, and performers. 紧身连衣裤是运动员、舞蹈演员和表演者的热门选择。

|近义词：bodysuit, jumpsuit, unitard |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps loose clothing) |词性: Noun
