nondesisting:/ˌnɒndɪˈsɪstɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːndɪˈsɪstɪŋ/| adj.|不停的，持续的|Characterized by continuous or persistent action without ceasing.|The nondesisting rain caused severe flooding.  不停的雨造成了严重的洪水。|近义词：incessant, continuous, unrelenting, persistent, unremitting|反义词：intermittent, sporadic, occasional|词性:形容词

词源:
From non- + desisting (present participle of desist).  Desist comes from Old French desister, from Latin desistere "to stand off, leave off," from de- "off, away" + sistere "to cause to stand, place, set," frequentative of stare "to stand."
