nonregressive:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈɡrɛs.ɪv/ | adj. | 非递减的，非倒退的 | Not regressive; not decreasing or returning to a previous, usually worse, state.  | The tax system is nonregressive, meaning higher earners pay a greater percentage of their income.  这个税收制度是非递减的，这意味着高收入者支付的收入比例更大。| Synonyms: progressive, increasing, advancing | Antonyms: regressive, decreasing, declining | adjective
