Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + symbolical + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives)
From "non-" + "symbolical" + "-ness".  "Non-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "not". "Symbolical" comes from the Late Latin "symbolicus" and Greek "sumbolikos," meaning "pertaining to a symbol." "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns expressing state or condition.
