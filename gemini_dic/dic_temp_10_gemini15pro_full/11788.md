aragonite:/əˈræɡənaɪt/
n.
霰石
A crystalline form of calcium carbonate, CaCO3, often found in caves and shells.
Aragonite is a common polymorph of calcite. 霰石是方解石的一种常见的多形体。
Aragonite forms the skeletons of many corals. 霰石构成了许多珊瑚的骨骼。
近义词:calcite 方解石
反义词: (No direct antonym as it's a specific mineral)
词性:noun
