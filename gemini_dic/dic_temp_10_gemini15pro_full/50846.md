desecrators:/ˈdɛsɪˌkreɪtərz/ | n. |亵渎者，污损者|  A person who desecrates; someone who treats something sacred or highly valued with disrespect or contempt. | The desecrators of the ancient temple were condemned by all. 那些亵渎古庙的人受到了所有人的谴责。| The vandals were arrested for desecrating the cemetery. 这些破坏者因亵渎墓地而被捕。| synonyms: profaner, violator, defiler | antonyms: protector, guardian, reverer | noun
