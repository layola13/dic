metrologically:/ˌmɛtrəˈlɒdʒɪkli/
adjective
计量地；计量学上地
Relating to the science of measurement.
The experiment was conducted metrologically soundly, ensuring accurate results.  这个实验在计量学上进行得很完善，确保了结果的准确性。
The equipment was metrologically calibrated.  该设备经过计量校准。
近义词: accurately, precisely, quantitatively
反义词: imprecisely, inaccurately, qualitatively
词性: adjective
词源: From "metrology" + "-ically". "Metrology" comes from the French "métrologie", ultimately from Greek "metron" (measure) + "-logia" (study).
