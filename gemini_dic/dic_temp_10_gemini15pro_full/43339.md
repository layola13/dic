crampiest:/ˈkræmpiest/ | adj. | 最狭窄的；最拥挤的；最受限制的 |  Superlative form of "crampy," meaning extremely confined, restricted, or overcrowded, causing discomfort. |  This is the crampiest airplane seat I've ever been in.  这是我坐过的最狭窄的飞机座位。|  The attic room was crampiest, barely enough space to stand. 阁楼房间是最狭窄的，几乎没有站立的空间。 |  confined, cramped, restricted, tight | spacious, roomy, comfortable | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From "cramp" (verb), meaning to restrict or confine, from Old English "crampian" (to draw together, contract).  The "-y" suffix forms an adjective, and "-est" indicates the superlative form.
