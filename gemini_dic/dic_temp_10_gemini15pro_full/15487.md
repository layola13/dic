badlands:/ˈbædˌlændz/| n.|荒地，劣地|A region of barren, eroded land with roughly sculpted ridges and pinnacles.|We drove through the badlands of South Dakota. 我们开车穿过南达科他州的荒地。|近义词：wasteland, desert|反义词:fertile land, grassland|词性:名词
词源:From bad + lands. Originally referring to a difficult terrain for travel, specifically applied to the White River Badlands of South Dakota.
