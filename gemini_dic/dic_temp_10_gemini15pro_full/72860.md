folks:/foʊks/ (US), /fəʊks/ (UK)| n.|人们，家属，亲属，大伙儿|People in general or one's family. |例句：How are your folks doing?  你家里人怎么样？

例句：Folks around here are friendly. 这附近的人都很友好。

近义词：people, family, relatives, parents|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "strangers," "enemies")|词性:Noun


folks:/foʊks/ (US), /fəʊks/ (UK)| n. |各位，伙计们 (Informal address)| Used as a form of address to a group of people.| 例句：Okay, folks, let's get started. 好了，各位，我们开始吧。

例句：Thanks for coming, folks!  谢谢各位的光临！

近义词：everyone, everybody, you all, guys|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:Noun
