correctibility:/kəˌrɛktəˈbɪləti/| n. |可纠正性，可改正性|The quality of being able to be corrected or rectified.  It refers to the potential for errors or mistakes to be identified and fixed.|The software's correctibility is a key feature, allowing users to easily amend any input errors.  该软件的可纠正性是一个关键特性，允许用户轻松修改任何输入错误。|Synonyms: amendability, rectifiability, revisability|Antonyms: incorrigibility, unrectifiability, irreparability|noun
