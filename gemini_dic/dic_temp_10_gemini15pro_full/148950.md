overzealously: /ˌoʊvərˈzɛləsli/ | adv. | 过分热情地，过分积极地 |  Characterized by excessive zeal; with too much enthusiasm or eagerness. | He overzealously cleaned the house until it was spotless, even scrubbing the grout with a toothbrush. 他过分热情地打扫房子，直到一尘不染，甚至用牙刷擦洗水泥浆。|  The security guard overzealously checked everyone's bags, causing a long delay. 保安人员过分热情地检查每个人的行李，导致了长时间的延误。 | 近义词: excessively, fanatically, immoderately | 反义词: apathetically, halfheartedly, indifferently | 词性: 副词

词源: From "over-" (excessive) + "zealous" (full of zeal) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Zealous" ultimately derives from Greek "zelos" meaning "zeal, ardor."
