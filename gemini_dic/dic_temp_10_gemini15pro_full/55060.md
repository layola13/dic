disrealize:/ˌdɪsrɪːəˈlaɪz/ | v. | 使失去真实感；使觉得不真实；使感到陌生 | To make (something) unreal or to cause (someone) to feel that something is unreal. | The intense heat disrealized the landscape, making it shimmer like a mirage.  酷热使眼前的景物失去了真实感，看起来像海市蜃楼一样闪烁。


The medication disrealized her, making her feel detached from her surroundings.  药物让她感到不真实，感觉与周围环境脱节。 | 近义词：alienate, estrange, disorient | 反义词: realize, actualize | 词性: 动词
