ambages:/ˈæmbəˌdʒiːz/
noun
迂回曲折的言辞，拐弯抹角的话
roundabout or indirect language; circumlocution.
He told us the bad news without ambages.  他直截了当地告诉了我们这个坏消息。
Synonyms: circumlocution, periphrasis, indirectness, evasiveness, equivocation
Antonyms: directness, straightforwardness, frankness, candor, openness
n.
