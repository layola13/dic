break-camp:/breɪk kæmp/ | v. | 拆营；拔营；停止露营 | To pack up tents and other camping gear and leave a campsite. | We broke camp early in the morning to avoid the midday heat. 我们一大清早就拔营了，以免在中午的酷热中行走。|  We broke camp at dawn and headed home. 我们在黎明时分拔营回家。 | synonyms: decamp, pack up, move on | antonyms: set up camp, make camp, pitch camp | verb
