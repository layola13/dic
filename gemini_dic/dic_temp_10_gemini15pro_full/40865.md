cook-the-books:/kʊk ðə bʊks/
|v.|做假账，篡改账目|To falsify financial records, typically to make a company appear more profitable than it is.|The accountant was caught cooking the books to hide the company's losses.  会计被抓到做假账以掩盖公司的损失。|Synonyms: falsify, manipulate, doctor, embezzle|Antonyms: report accurately, be honest|verb

cook-the-books:/kʊk ðə bʊks/
|n.|做假账的行为|The act of falsifying financial records.|The cook-the-books scheme went undetected for years. 做假账的阴谋多年未被发现。|Synonyms: fraud, embezzlement, accounting irregularities|Antonyms: accurate accounting, financial transparency|noun
