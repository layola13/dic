murmuration:/ˌmɜːməˈreɪʃən/ /ˌmɝːməˈreɪʃən/| n. |低语，喃喃声；（尤指椋鸟的）群飞，群集|A large group of starlings flying together in swirling patterns is called a murmuration.  一大群椋鸟以漩涡状的图案一起飞行被称为椋鸟群飞。|例句：The murmuration of starlings was a mesmerizing sight. 椋鸟群飞是一个迷人的景象。|近义词：flock, swarm, mass, throng|反义词: scattering, dispersal|noun
