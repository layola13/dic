belatedly:/bɪˈleɪtɪdli/
adv.
迟来地，晚些时候
done or happening late
She belatedly realized she had left her keys at home. 她后来才意识到她把钥匙落在家里了。
近义词：late, tardily, after the fact
反义词：promptly, early, timely
词性：副词

词源：From *belate* (from *be-* + *late*) + *-ed* + *-ly*.
