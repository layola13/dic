overtly:/ˈəʊvɜːtli/ (US) /oʊˈvɜːrtli/| adv.|公开地，明显地|In an open and obvious way, without any attempt to hide something.|He overtly criticized the government's policies. 他公开批评了政府的政策。|Synonyms: openly, publicly, plainly, explicitly, blatantly|Antonyms: secretly, covertly, privately, subtly|adverb

overt:/ˈəʊvɜːt/ (US) /oʊˈvɜːrt/| adj.|公开的，明显的|Done or shown openly; not secret or hidden.|His overt hostility towards his colleagues surprised everyone.  他对同事公开的敌意让每个人都感到惊讶。|Synonyms: open, apparent, evident, manifest, unconcealed|Antonyms: covert, hidden, secret, concealed|adjective
