cadge:/kædʒ/| v.| 乞讨，骗取，|He always cadges cigarettes from me. 他总是从我这儿骗烟。|近义词：beg, sponge, scrounge|反义词：earn, give, provide|词性:动词

词源:  Possibly from Scottish and Northern English *cadge 'to carry', also 'peddler, hawker', perhaps related to 'cad' (meaning a dishonest or disreputable man).  The original meaning was likely 'to hawk or peddle goods', which later evolved to mean 'to beg or obtain by imposing on another'.
