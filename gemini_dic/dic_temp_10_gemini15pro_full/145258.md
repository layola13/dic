overboastfully: /ˌoʊvərˈboʊstfəli/
adverb
过度夸耀地，自吹自擂地
Excessively boastful; in a manner characterized by extreme bragging or self-praise.
He overboastfully recounted his supposed achievements, boring everyone in the room.  他过度夸耀地讲述了他所谓的成就，让房间里的每个人都感到厌烦。
She overboastfully described her new car, making it sound much more impressive than it actually was. 她自吹自擂地描述她的新车，说得比实际情况要好得多。
近义词: boastingly, braggingly, vaingloriously
反义词: humbly, modestly, unassumingly
词性: adverb
词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessively") + boastful (adjective) + -ly (adverb suffix)
