insecty:/ˈɪnˌsɛkti/ | adj. | 昆虫似的；多昆虫的；像昆虫的 | Resembling or suggestive of an insect, especially in being small, spindly, or numerous. | The room had an insecty smell, like something had crawled in and died.  房间里有一股昆虫的气味，好像有什么东西爬进来死了。 |The dark corners were filled with insecty clicking sounds.  黑暗的角落里充满了昆虫的咔哒声。|近义词：buggy, creepy-crawly |反义词: (No direct opposite, but consider: clean, pristine) |词性: adjective
