hurrier:/ˈhɜːriər/ (US), /ˈhʌriər/ (UK)| n.| 匆忙的人；赶时间的人|Someone who is in a hurry; someone who rushes.| He's a real hurrier; he always seems to be rushing around. 他真是个急性子，好像总是匆匆忙忙的。|The hurrier bumped into me as he rushed through the station. 那位赶时间的人冲过车站时撞到了我。|近义词：rusher, speeder|反义词：dawdler, lingerer|词性:noun

词源: From the verb "hurry" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Hurry" comes from the Middle English "hurrien," of uncertain origin, perhaps related to the Middle Dutch "horren," meaning "to shake, tremble."
