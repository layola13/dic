enigmatography:/ˌɛnɪɡməˈtɒɡrəfi/ |n.|谜语写作，谜语创作|The art or practice of constructing or solving enigmas; the composition of enigmatical writings or discourse.|His book is a masterpiece of enigmatography, challenging even the most seasoned puzzle solvers.|近义词:puzzle-making, riddle-making|反义词:plain speaking, clarity|词性:名词

Etymology: From enigma (meaning riddle) + -graphy (meaning writing or recording)
