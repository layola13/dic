expander:/ɪkˈspændər/ | n. |扩张器，扩展器|A device or substance that expands something.|Chest expanders are used to build up the chest muscles. |Synonyms: dilator, stretcher, inflater, widener |Antonyms: compressor, constrictor, reducer, shrinker |noun

expander:/ɪkˈspændər/ | n. |（电路）扩展器|A device that increases the capacity or capability of a circuit.|The memory expander allows you to add more RAM to your computer. |Synonyms: enhancer, augmenter, booster |Antonyms: limiter, restrictor |noun
