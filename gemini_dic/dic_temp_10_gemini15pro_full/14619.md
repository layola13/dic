avocational:/ˌævəˈkeɪʃənəl/ | adj. |  副业的；嗜好的；业余爱好的 | Relating to a hobby or pastime pursued in addition to one's regular work. |  She is an avocational photographer, taking stunning nature pictures in her spare time.  她是一位业余摄影师，在业余时间拍摄令人惊叹的自然照片。| synonyms: hobbyist, amateur, recreational | antonyms: professional, vocational, occupational | adjective

avocation: /ˌævəˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 副业；嗜好；业余爱好  | A hobby or minor occupation, especially one pursued for pleasure rather than financial gain. |  His avocation is collecting antique clocks. 他的爱好是收藏古董钟。| synonyms: hobby, pastime, leisure pursuit | antonyms:  profession, vocation, occupation | noun
