continuances: /kənˈtɪnjuənsɪz/
| n. | 延期，继续， 持续|The judge granted several continuances in the case. 法官准予了此案的几次延期。|  The lawyer requested a continuance to gather more evidence. 律师请求延期以收集更多证据。| synonyms: adjournments, postponements, extensions | antonyms: cancellations, terminations | noun

continuances: /kənˈtɪnjuənsɪz/
| n. | 诉讼的延期|The repeated continuances frustrated the plaintiff. 反复的延期使原告感到沮丧。| The judge warned against further continuances. 法官警告不要再延期。| synonyms: deferrals, delays | antonyms:  expediting, speeding up | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French continuance, from continuer "to continue," from Latin continuare "to make continuous," from continuus "continuous."
