emenagogue: /ɪˈmenəɡɒɡ/ (UK) /ɪˈmenəɡɑːɡ/ (US) | n. | 通经药，催经药 | A medicine or other agent that stimulates or increases menstrual flow. |  Example 1: The doctor prescribed an emmenagogue to help regulate her menstrual cycle. 医生给她开了一种通经药来帮助调节她的月经周期。

Example 2:  Certain herbs are believed to act as emmenagogues.  某些草药被认为具有通经的作用。 | 近义词:  menstruum,  | 反义词:  emmenostatic (止经药) | 词性: noun
