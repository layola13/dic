feathered:/ˈfeðərd/ | adj. | 有羽毛的；羽毛状的 | Covered with feathers; resembling feathers. | The feathered bird sang a beautiful song.  这只带羽毛的鸟唱了一首优美的歌。 | Synonyms: plumed, plumy, downy | Antonyms: featherless, bare | adjective

feathered:/ˈfeðərd/ | adj. |  (指箭头、桨等)有翼片的；(指滑雪板)有边刃的 | (of an arrow, oar, etc.) having a thin wing or wings; (of skis) having edges. | He used a feathered arrow for hunting. 他用带翼片的箭去打猎。 | Synonyms: winged, fletched, edged | Antonyms: unfeathered, bladeless | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *feþerod, from feþer (feather) + -od (suffix forming adjectives). Cognate with Old High German fetharot, Old Norse fjaðraðr.
