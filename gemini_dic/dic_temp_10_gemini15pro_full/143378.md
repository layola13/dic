outfenced:/ˌaʊtˈfɛnst/
verb
用篱笆围起来; 比...的篱笆好
To enclose with or as if with a fence, especially a better fence.
The farmer outfenced his neighbor to keep the cows from wandering. 农民用篱笆围住了他的邻居，以防止牛乱跑。
近义词: enclose, fence in
反义词: open up
词性: verb

outfenced:/ˌaʊtˈfɛnst/
verb
(体育)在击剑比赛中胜过
To defeat in a fencing match.
She outfenced her opponent in the final round. 她在最后一轮击剑比赛中击败了对手。
近义词: defeat, beat
反义词: lose to
词性: verb
