guardful:/ˈɡɑːrdfəl/| adj.|警惕的，谨慎的|Watchful and cautious; vigilant.|She cast a guardful glance around the empty street. 她警惕地环顾空荡荡的街道。|近义词：watchful, vigilant, cautious, wary, alert|反义词：careless, heedless, negligent, reckless|词性:形容词

词源: From "guard" (meaning to protect or watch over) + "-ful" (a suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by").  The word emphasizes being full of or characterized by guarding.
