ambassadors-at-large:/æmˈbæsədərz æt lɑːrdʒ/
|n.|巡回大使；特使|An ambassador-at-large is a diplomat of high rank who is appointed by a head of state to deal with special diplomatic assignments. They are not assigned to any particular country and often travel extensively. |The president appointed her as an ambassador-at-large to promote peace in the region. 总统任命她为巡回大使，以促进该地区的和平。

| | |  The former senator served as an ambassador-at-large for human rights.  这位前参议员担任了人权巡回大使。|
| | | |近义词: roving ambassador, special envoy|反义词: resident ambassador|Noun|
