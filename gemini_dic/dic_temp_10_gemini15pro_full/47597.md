debaters:/dɪˈbeɪtərz/
n.
辩论者，辩论员
People who engage in formal debates.  Those who argue a point or discuss opposing viewpoints.
The debaters presented their arguments clearly and persuasively.  辩论者们清晰而有说服力地陈述了他们的论点。
The debaters skillfully refuted each other's points. 辩论者巧妙地反驳了彼此的观点。
synonyms: arguers, disputants, discussants
antonyms:  agreers, mediators


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "debate," which comes from Old French "debatre"  meaning "to fight, struggle, contend," ultimately from Latin "battuere" meaning "to beat, fight."  The "-er" suffix denotes a person who performs the action of the verb.
