adjournal:/ˈædʒɜːrnl/| n.|法院日志，法院期刊|A periodical publication containing legal notices and official court business.|The adjournal contained the details of the upcoming court cases. 法院日志包含了即将进行的法庭案件的细节。|synonyms: court record, legal journal, docket|antonyms: N/A|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Scottish legal usage, combining "ad" (Latin, "to") and "journal" (from Old French "journal," meaning "daily"). Originally referred to the daily record of court proceedings.
