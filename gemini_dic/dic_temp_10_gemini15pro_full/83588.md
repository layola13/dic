haematogenic:/ˌhiːmətəˈdʒɛnɪk/ /ˌhemətəˈdʒɛnɪk/
adjective
造血的; 促进造血的
Relating to or promoting the formation of blood.
Haematogenic foods are rich in iron and other nutrients essential for blood production.  造血食物富含铁和其他对血液生成至关重要的营养素。
Iron supplements can be haematogenic, helping the body produce more red blood cells.  铁补充剂可以促进造血，帮助身体产生更多的红细胞。
近义词:haematopoietic, blood-forming
反义词:haemolytic (Although not a direct opposite, it relates to the *destruction* of blood cells)
词性:adjective
