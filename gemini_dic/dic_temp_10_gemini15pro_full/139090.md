obbligato:/ˌɑːblɪˈɡɑːtoʊ/ (US), /ˌɒblɪˈɡɑːtəʊ/ (UK)| n. |强制段落； obbligatos (plural)|A musical passage, usually melodic and elaborate, that is required to be played exactly as written and cannot be omitted or altered.  It often features a solo instrument accompanying a main melody.|The flute obbligato in the second movement was breathtaking. 第二乐章的长笛强制段落令人叹为观止。|近义词：solo, accompaniment|反义词: improvisation, ad lib|名词

obbligato:/ˌɑːblɪˈɡɑːtoʊ/ (US), /ˌɒblɪˈɡɑːtəʊ/ (UK)| adj. |强制的；必须的|Required; obligatory.  Used especially in music.|The concerto includes an obbligato part for the cello. 这首协奏曲包含一段大提琴的强制演奏部分。|近义词：obligatory, required, essential|反义词: optional, discretionary|形容词
