galantine:/ˈɡælənˌtiːn/ | n. | 肉冻；禽肉冻；脱骨填馅烧禽 | A dish of boned stuffed poultry, usually chicken or turkey, served cold, coated with aspic. |  She prepared a galantine of pheasant for the Christmas feast. 她为圣诞盛宴准备了一道野鸡肉冻。| 近义词: ballotine, aspic | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "roast" as a contrasting hot dish) | noun

galantine:/ˈɡælənˌtiːn/ | n. | （旧）冒险故事；传奇故事 |  (archaic) A romantic adventure story; a tale of chivalry. | He regaled the children with galantines of brave knights and fair maidens.  他用勇敢骑士和美丽少女的冒险故事让孩子们着迷。| 近义词: saga, romance, tale | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | noun
