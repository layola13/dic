installs:/ɪnˈstɔːlz/
verb
安装，设置
puts (something) in position or connects (something) so that it is ready for use.
The technician installs the new software on my computer.  技术人员在我的电脑上安装了新软件。
近义词: sets up, puts in, fits, mounts
反义词: uninstalls, removes
词性: verb

installs:/ɪnˈstɔːlz/
noun
安装，设置
the act of installing something.
The installs of the new security system took several hours. 新安全系统的安装花了几个小时。
近义词: setups, installations
反义词: removals, uninstallations
词性: noun
