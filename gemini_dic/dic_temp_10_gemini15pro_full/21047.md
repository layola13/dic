blimpery:/ˈblɪmpəri/| adj.|浮夸的，华而不实的，空洞的|Characterized by pompous or inflated language; bombastic.  Often used to describe speech or writing that sounds impressive but lacks substance.|His blimpery speech impressed no one who knew the facts. 他浮夸的演讲没有打动任何了解真相的人。|Synonyms: pompous, bombastic, inflated, pretentious, ostentatious|Antonyms: simple, modest, unpretentious, understated|adjective

blimpery:/ˈblɪmpəri/| n.|浮夸，华而不实，空洞|Pompous or inflated language; bombast.|The blimpery of the politician's promises was evident to everyone.  这位政客承诺的空洞之处对每个人来说都是显而易见的。|Synonyms: pomposity, bombast, inflation, pretentiousness, ostentation|Antonyms: simplicity, modesty, unpretentiousness, understatement|noun
