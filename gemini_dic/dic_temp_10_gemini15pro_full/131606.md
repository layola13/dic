noncomplementary:/ˌnɒnkɒmplɪˈmɛntəri/ | adj. | 非互补的，不互补的 | Not complementary; not supplementing or completing something else. |  The two strands of DNA are noncomplementary in this region, preventing proper base pairing. 这段DNA的两个链是非互补的，阻止了正常的碱基配对。 | The noncomplementary colors clashed horribly.  这些不互补的颜色冲突得很厉害。 | 近义词: dissimilar, incompatible, conflicting | 反义词: complementary, matching, compatible | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- + complementary.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "complementary" refers to something that completes or makes perfect.
