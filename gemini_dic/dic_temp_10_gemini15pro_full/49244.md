deleterious:/ˌdɛlɪˈtɪəriəs/
adjective
有害的
causing harm or damage
Smoking is deleterious to your health. 吸烟有害健康。

The chemical has deleterious effects on the environment.  这种化学物质对环境有有害影响。

近义词:harmful, damaging, injurious, detrimental, adverse
反义词:beneficial, advantageous, helpful, salutary
词性:adjective
