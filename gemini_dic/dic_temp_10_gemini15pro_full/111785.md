lofted:/ˈlɒftɪd/ (UK) /ˈlɔːftɪd/ (US)| adj.|阁楼式的，挑高的，高耸的|Having a high ceiling or roof, or having an upper floor like a loft.  Relating to or played with a lofted shot (in sports like golf and tennis).|The apartment had a lofted bedroom area, making it feel spacious. 这套公寓有一个阁楼式的卧室区域，使它感觉很宽敞。

He hit a perfectly lofted shot over the defender's head. 他打出一个完美的挑高球，越过了防守队员的头顶。|近义词：high-ceilinged, elevated, airy, raised, (for shots) high, arcing|反义词：low-ceilinged, low, ground-level|词性:adjective
