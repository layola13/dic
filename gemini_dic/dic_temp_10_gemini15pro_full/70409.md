例句：The cake was dense and figgy, perfect with a cup of tea.  蛋糕口感扎实，充满无花果的香味，配上一杯茶真是完美。|近义词：fig-filled|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "plain" if referring to taste)|词性:adjective
