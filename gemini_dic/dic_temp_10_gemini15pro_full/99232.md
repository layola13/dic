intercombination:/ˌɪntərˌkɒmbɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. |  相互结合，混合，（量子力学）禁跃迁 | A transition between two atomic states that have different total spin angular momenta. | The phosphorescence is due to an intercombination transition from a triplet excited state to the singlet ground state. 磷光是由三重激发态到单重基态的禁跃迁引起的。 | synonyms: transition, change, shift | antonyms: stability, constancy | noun

intercombination:/ˌɪntərˌkɒmbɪˈneɪʃən/ | adj. |  相互结合的，混合的 | Relating to or involving a combination of different things. | The painting uses an intercombination of techniques. 这幅画使用了多种技巧的结合。 | synonyms: combined, mixed, blended | antonyms: separate, individual | adjective
