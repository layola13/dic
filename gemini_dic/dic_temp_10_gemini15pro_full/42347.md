counteractors:/ˌkaʊntərˈæktərz/
noun
对抗者，抵消者
Those who act in opposition to something else, neutralizing or mitigating its effects.
The new drug has several counteractors that can lessen its side effects. 这种新药有几种可以减轻其副作用的对抗剂。
synonyms: neutralizers, mitigators, inhibitors, antagonists
antonyms:  catalysts, enhancers, promoters
