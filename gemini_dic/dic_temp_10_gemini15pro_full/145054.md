overallegiance:/ˌoʊvərəˈliːdʒəns/
noun
过度忠诚，过分效忠
Excessive loyalty or dedication, often to the detriment of other interests or values.
He showed overallegiance to his company, neglecting his family and friends. 他对公司表现出过度的忠诚，忽略了他的家人和朋友。
loyalty, devotion, fealty
disloyalty, treachery, infidelity
