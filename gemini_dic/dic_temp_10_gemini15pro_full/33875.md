circumspect:/ˈsɜːrkəmˌspekt/ | adj. | 谨慎的，慎重的，周到的 | Careful to consider all circumstances and possible consequences; prudent. | She was circumspect in her choice of words, knowing the issue was sensitive. 她措辞谨慎，因为她知道这个问题很敏感。|  He made a circumspect withdrawal from the volatile stock market. 他谨慎地从动荡的股市中撤出。 | 近义词: cautious, prudent, wary, discreet | 反义词: reckless, impulsive, careless, rash | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From Latin circumspectus, past participle of circumspicere "to look around, consider carefully," from circum- "around" + specere "to look."
