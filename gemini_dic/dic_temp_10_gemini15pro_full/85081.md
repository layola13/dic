hard-wearing:/ˌhɑːrdˈwerɪŋ/ | adj. | 耐磨的，经穿的 | Able to withstand continued use or wear without damage or deterioration. | This jacket is made of hard-wearing material and will last for years. 这件夹克是用耐磨材料制成的，可以使用很多年。| These boots are hard-wearing and perfect for hiking.  这些靴子很耐磨，非常适合徒步旅行。|近义词：durable, long-lasting, tough, resilient |反义词：fragile, flimsy, delicate |词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From hard + wearing. "Hard" meaning resistant to pressure or damage, and "wearing" referring to the act of being worn or used.  由 hard + wearing 组成。“hard”表示抵抗压力或损坏，“wearing”表示穿着或使用的行为。
