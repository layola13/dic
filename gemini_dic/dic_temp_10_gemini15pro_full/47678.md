debtorship:/ˈdɛtərʃɪp/
n.
债务人身份，负债状态
The state of owing money; the condition of being a debtor.
He declared bankruptcy, ending his years of debtorshhip. 他宣布破产，结束了多年的负债状态。
近义词：indebtedness, liability
反义词：creditor status, solvency
词性: noun

词源:  From "debtor" + "-ship". "Debtor" comes from Old French "deteur" ultimately from Latin "dēbitor" (one who owes), from "dēbēre" (to owe). "-ship" is an Old English suffix denoting state, condition, or office.
