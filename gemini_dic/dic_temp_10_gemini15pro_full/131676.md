nonconcessive:/ˌnɑːnkənˈsesɪv/| adj.|不退让的，不妥协的，不让步的|Not making concessions; although.  Used to introduce a clause which contrasts with the main clause but does not contradict it.|Although it was raining, we still went for a walk. 尽管下雨了，我们还是去散步了。|Synonyms:  uncompromising, unyielding, inflexible|Antonyms:  concessive, yielding, accommodating|Adjective
