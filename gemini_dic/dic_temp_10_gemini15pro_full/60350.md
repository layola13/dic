egotistical: /ˌiːɡəˈtɪstɪkəl/ | adj. | 自我中心的；自负的 |  Excessively conceited or absorbed in oneself; self-centered.  Characterized by egotism. | He was so egotistical, always talking about himself and his achievements. 他太自负了，总是谈论自己和自己的成就。|  She's incredibly egotistical and has little regard for others' feelings.  她非常自负，很少顾及别人的感受。 | 近义词: self-centered, narcissistic, conceited, arrogant, vain | 反义词: humble, modest, self-effacing | 词性: 形容词

词源: From egotism + -ical.  Egotism comes from ego + -ism. Ego is from Latin, meaning "I".
