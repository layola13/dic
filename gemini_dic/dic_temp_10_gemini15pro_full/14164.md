autism: /ˈɔːtɪzəm/ (UK) /ˈɑːtɪzəm/ (US)| n. |自闭症|A developmental disorder of variable severity that is characterized by difficulty in social interaction and communication and by restricted or repetitive patterns of thought and behavior.  It is now considered to be part of a broader category of neurodevelopmental disorders called autism spectrum disorder (ASD).|My nephew was diagnosed with autism at the age of three.|近义词: autism spectrum disorder (ASD)|反义词: neurotypicality|词性: noun
