admitted:/ədˈmɪtɪd/
verb
承认的；被录取的
past participle of admit
He admitted his guilt. = 他承认了自己的罪行。
She was admitted to the hospital. = 她被送进了医院。
acknowledged, confessed
denied, refuted
verb

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He admits his mistake. = 他承认他的错误。
She admits she was wrong. = 她承认她错了。

一般过去时:
They admitted their defeat. = 他们承认了失败。
He admitted he had lied. = 他承认他撒谎了。

现在完成时:
The hospital has admitted several new patients. = 医院接收了几名新病人。
She has admitted her involvement in the crime. = 她承认自己参与了犯罪。

过去完成时:
He had admitted his guilt before the trial began. = 在审判开始前，他就承认了自己的罪行。
They had admitted their mistake before the damage was done. = 在造成损害之前，他们就承认了错误。

将来时:
He will admit his error eventually. = 他最终会承认他的错误。
They will admit the new students in September. = 他们将在九月录取新生。

被动语态:
He was admitted to the bar. = 他被律师协会录取了。
New evidence was admitted in court. = 新证据被法庭采纳。

疑问句:
Did he admit his fault? = 他承认他的错误了吗？
Has she been admitted to the university? = 她被大学录取了吗？

否定句:
He didn't admit his guilt. = 他没有承认他的罪行。
She wasn't admitted to the program. = 她没有被该项目录取。


PHRASE->
admitted to + (doing something) = 承认做某事
admitted to + (noun/place) = 被准许进入某地
admitted into + (noun/group/organization) = 被接纳进入某团体/组织
be admitted as + (noun/role) = 被接纳为某种身份/角色
openly admitted = 公开承认
