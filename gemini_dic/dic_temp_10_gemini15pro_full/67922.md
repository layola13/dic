facelessnesses:/ˈfeɪsləsnəsɪz/
n.
匿名性；无人格性；无个性
the state of lacking individual characteristics or identity, especially in a large organization or society.
The facelessnesses of the bureaucracy made it difficult to get anything done. 官僚机构的无人格性使得很难做成任何事情。
anonymity, impersonality
individuality, personality
noun
