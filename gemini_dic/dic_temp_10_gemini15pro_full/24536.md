broadleaved:/ˈbrɔːdliːvd/ | adj. |阔叶的|Having broad leaves; not needle-leaved or scale-leaved.|例句：Broadleaved trees are dominant in this forest. 这片森林以阔叶树为主。|近义词：deciduous, hardwood |反义词: needle-leaved, coniferous |词性: 形容词

broadleaved:/ˈbrɔːdliːvd/ | n. |阔叶树|A tree or shrub having broad leaves.|例句：The broadleaved shed their leaves in the autumn. 这些阔叶树在秋天落叶。|近义词：deciduous tree, hardwood tree |反义词: conifer, needle-leaved tree |词性: 名词
