decanting:/dɪˈkæntiŋ/
n.|倾析，滗析|The act of pouring a liquid from one container to another, typically to separate it from sediment or impurities.|He carefully began decanting the wine into a carafe. 他小心翼翼地开始将葡萄酒滗入玻璃水瓶中。|近义词：pouring, transferring, separating|反义词:mixing, combining
词性:名词

decant:/dɪˈkænt/
vt.|倾析，滗析|To pour a liquid from one container to another, typically to separate it from sediment or impurities.|She decanted the soup into bowls. 她把汤倒进碗里。|近义词：pour, transfer, separate|反义词:mix, combine
词性:动词
