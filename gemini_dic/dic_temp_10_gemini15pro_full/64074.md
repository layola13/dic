epizoic:/ˌɛpɪˈzoʊɪk/ | adj. | 附生的，体外寄生的 | Living or growing on the exterior of a living animal, but not parasitic. | Barnacles are epizoic on whales. 藤壶附生在鲸鱼身上。| 近义词：ectozoic, commensal | 反义词：endoparasitic, parasitic | 词性: adjective

epizoic:/ˌɛpɪˈzoʊɪk/ | n. | 体外寄生生物 | An organism that lives on the exterior of a living animal, but is not parasitic. |  Certain protozoa are epizoic on aquatic invertebrates.  某些原生动物是水生无脊椎动物的体外寄生生物。 | 近义词：ectozoan, commensal | 反义词：endoparasite, parasite | 词性: noun
