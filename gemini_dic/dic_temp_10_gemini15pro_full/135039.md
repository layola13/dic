nonnitrogenous:/ˌnɒn naɪˈtrɒdʒɪnəs/
adjective
非氮的；不含氮的
Not containing nitrogen.  Specifically, referring to organic compounds that do not contain nitrogen atoms.
例句：
The plant requires both nitrogenous and nonnitrogenous fertilizers for healthy growth.
这种植物需要含氮和不含氮的肥料才能健康生长。

Nonnitrogenous organic compounds, such as carbohydrates and fats, are important sources of energy.
非氮有机化合物，例如碳水化合物和脂肪，是重要的能量来源。


近义词:nitrogen-free
反义词:nitrogenous
