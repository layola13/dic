luminously:/ˈluːmɪnəsli/ | adv. | 明亮地，光辉地 | in a way that produces or reflects bright light; shiningly | The moon shone luminously in the clear night sky. 月亮在晴朗的夜空中闪耀着光芒。|  The fireflies blinked luminously in the dark forest. 萤火虫在黑暗的森林里发出明亮的闪烁。 | 近义词: brightly, brilliantly, radiantly, shiningly | 反义词: dimly, faintly, darkly | 词性: 副词

词源:  From Latin *luminosus*, from lumen ("light").
