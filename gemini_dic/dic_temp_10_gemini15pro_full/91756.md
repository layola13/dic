hyperemotional:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈmoʊʃənəl/
adjective
过度情绪化的，过于敏感的
Excessively emotional; readily displaying or prone to strong emotions.
She's been very hyperemotional lately, crying at the drop of a hat. 她最近情绪非常不稳定，动不动就哭。
He gave a hyperemotional speech that moved many in the audience to tears.  他发表了一篇充满激情澎湃的演讲，让许多观众感动落泪。

近义词:overemotional,oversensitive, sentimental, dramatic
反义词: unemotional, apathetic, stoic, calm
词性:adjective
