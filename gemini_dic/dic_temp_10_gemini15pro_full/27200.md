callose:/ˈkæləʊs/| n. |胼胝质；愈伤组织|A carbohydrate that forms a callus in sieve elements at wound sites or in response to stress.  一种碳水化合物，在伤口部位或应激反应中在筛管分子中形成愈伤组织。|Callose deposition is a common plant response to wounding. 胼胝质沉积是植物对伤口的常见反应。|Synonyms: callus, tylose|Antonyms: None|Noun

callose:/kəˈloʊs/| adj. |硬结的；起老茧的|Hardened and thickened, as skin.|Her hands were calloused from years of gardening.  她的手因多年的园艺工作而变得粗糙。|Synonyms: hard, thickened, toughened|Antonyms: soft, smooth|Adjective
