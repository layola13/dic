matte:/mæt/ | n. | 无光泽表面；无光照片；遮罩 | A matte surface is one that is not shiny.  A matte photograph has a dull finish. In photography and special effects, a matte is a mask used to combine different image elements. | The painting had a matte finish. 这幅画的表面没有光泽。| synonym: dull, flat | antonym: glossy, shiny | noun

mattes:/mæts/ | n. | 无光泽表面（复数）；无光照片（复数）；遮罩（复数）| Plural form of "matte." Refers to multiple matte surfaces, photographs, or masks. |  She prefers mattes to glossy prints. 她更喜欢无光泽的照片而不是光面照片。 | synonym: dull finishes, flat surfaces | antonym: glossy finishes, shiny surfaces | noun
