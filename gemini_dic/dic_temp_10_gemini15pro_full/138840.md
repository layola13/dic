nupson:/ˈnʌpsən/| n.|傻瓜，笨蛋|A foolish or incompetent person.  Derived from "nup" (meaning to hit on the head) + "son." Implies someone who has been hit on the head so often they've become stupid.|He's such a nupson, he locked his keys in the car again.|近义词：fool, idiot, dolt, dimwit, nincompoop|反义词:genius, intellectual|Noun
