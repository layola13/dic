mismanagement:/ˌmɪsˈmænɪdʒmənt/
n.
管理不善，经营失误
The company's downfall was due to years of mismanagement.
这家公司的倒闭是由于多年的管理不善造成的。
The project's failure was a direct result of mismanagement by the team leader.
该项目的失败是团队领导管理不善的直接结果。
近义词：misconduct, mishandling, misadministration
反义词：management, administration, good governance
词性：名词

词源：
From mis- + management.  "mis-" is a prefix of Latin origin, meaning "bad, wrong, or wrongly." "Management" comes from Old French "manegement" meaning "the act of managing."
