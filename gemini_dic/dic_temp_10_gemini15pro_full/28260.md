captioner:/ˈkæpʃənər/| n.|字幕员，字幕制作人|A person who writes captions for television programs, films, or videos, especially for the deaf or hard-of-hearing.|The captioner worked diligently to ensure the subtitles were accurate and synchronized with the dialogue.|Synonyms: subtitler, transcriber|Antonyms: None|Noun
