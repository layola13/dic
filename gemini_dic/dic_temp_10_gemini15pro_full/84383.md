hall:/hɔːl/ (US) /hɔːl/ (UK)| n.|大厅，礼堂，走廊，会堂|A large room or building used for public gatherings, meetings, performances, etc.  A corridor or passageway in a building.|例句：The wedding reception was held in the grand hall. 婚礼招待会在富丽堂皇的大厅举行。

例句：The students gathered in the entrance hall. 学生们聚集在入口大厅。


近义词：auditorium, lobby, corridor, foyer|反义词:room, chamber|词性:noun
