antipole:/ˈæn.tɪ.poʊl/ | n. |对跖点；正相反的事物|A place on Earth diametrically opposite to another place.  Something that is the complete opposite of something else.|例句：The antipole of love is hate. 爱的对立面是恨。

例句：New Zealand is roughly the antipole of Spain. 新西兰大致与西班牙位于地球的相对两端。|近义词：opposite, contrary, antithesis|反义词：counterpart, equivalent|词性:名词
