delegacy:/ˈdɛlɪɡəsi/ | n. | 代表团；授权；委托 | A group of people chosen to represent a larger group or organization; the act of entrusting a task or responsibility to another person. | The company sent a delegacy to the international conference. 公司派了一个代表团参加国际会议。|  He gave me the delegacy to sign the contract. 他授权我签署合同。| synonyms: delegation, mission, deputation, commission | antonyms: retention, refusal, denial | noun

delegacy:/ˈdɛlɪɡəsi/ | v. | 授权；委托 | To entrust a task or responsibility to another person. | The CEO delegated the task of hiring new staff to the HR department. 首席执行官将招聘新员工的任务委托给人力资源部门。 | The manager delegated authority to his assistant. 经理将权力委托给他的助手。| synonyms: entrust, assign, commit, authorize | antonyms: retain, withhold, keep, reserve | verb
