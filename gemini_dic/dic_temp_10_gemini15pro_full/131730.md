nonconfidently:/ˌnɒnˈkɒnfɪdəntli/ (British) /ˌnɑːnˈkɑːnfɪdəntli/ (American)| adv. |不自信地，缺乏自信地|In a manner lacking confidence or self-assurance.|She spoke nonconfidently, her voice barely above a whisper. 她说话很不自信，声音几乎听不见。|He nonconfidently approached the podium, fidgeting with his notes. 他很不自信地走近讲台，不安地摆弄着他的笔记。|近义词：hesitantly, timidly, nervously, uncertainly|反义词：confidently, assertively, boldly|词性：副词

词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non.
confident (adjective): From Latin confident-, confidentem, present participle of confidere "to trust firmly, rely on," from com- "with" + fidere "to trust."
-ly (suffix):  Forms adverbs from adjectives, meaning "in a ... manner."
