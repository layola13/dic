cresting:/ˈkrɛstɪŋ/ | adj. |  达到顶峰的；形成顶冠的；| reaching a peak or forming a crest | The hikers cheered as they reached the cresting ridge.  登山者们到达山脊顶峰时欢呼起来。| synonyms: peaking, culminating, topping, climaxing | antonyms: declining, descending, falling, ebbing | adjective

cresting:/ˈkrɛstɪŋ/ | n. |  波峰；山顶；| the act of reaching a crest; the highest point of something | The cresting of the wave sent spray high into the air.  波峰将水雾高高地抛向空中。| synonyms: peak, summit, apex, culmination | antonyms: trough, bottom, base, nadir | noun

cresting:/ˈkrɛstɪŋ/ | v. |  到达顶峰；形成顶冠；| to reach a peak or form a crest | The river was cresting after the heavy rains.  暴雨过后，河水暴涨。| synonyms: peaking, culminating, topping, climaxing | antonyms: declining, descending, falling, ebbing | verb


Etymology: From the verb "crest," which comes from Old French "creste" and ultimately from Latin "crista," meaning "tuft," "comb," or "crest."
