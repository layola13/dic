choking-up: /ˈtʃoʊkɪŋ ʌp/ (US) /ˈtʃəʊkɪŋ ʌp/ (UK)
|phrasal verb|哽咽；激动得说不出话来|Becoming unable to speak because of strong emotion, typically sadness.|例句：He started choking up when he talked about his father.  他谈到父亲时开始哽咽。|近义词：welling up, getting emotional, becoming teary-eyed|反义词：composed, calm, unemotional|verb phrase

choking-up: /ˈtʃoʊkɪŋ ʌp/ (US) /ˈtʃəʊkɪŋ ʌp/ (UK)
|adjective|哽咽的；激动得说不出话来的|Describing the state of being unable to speak due to strong emotion.|例句：She gave a choking-up speech about her experiences. 她哽咽着讲述了她的经历。|近义词：emotional, teary, choked with emotion|反义词：composed, calm, unemotional|adjective
