cercal:/ˈsɜːrkl/| adj.|尾须的，尾毛的|Of or relating to cerci, a pair of appendages at the end of the abdomen of some insects.|The cercal organs of the cricket are sensitive to air currents. 蟋蟀的尾须器官对气流很敏感。|近义词: caudal, appendicular |反义词:  (No clear antonym exists, perhaps 'headless' in a very broad sense) |词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *cercus*, from Greek *kerkos* "tail".
