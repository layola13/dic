bayonetted:/ˌbeɪəˈnɛtɪd/
verb|用刺刀刺；装上刺刀|
英文解析:Past participle of bayonet. Having been stabbed or attacked with a bayonet. Also, equipped with a bayonet.|
例句：The soldier bayonetted the enemy.士兵用刺刀刺杀了敌人。

例句：He was bayonetted during the charge. 他在冲锋中被刺刀刺伤了。

近义词：stabbed, pierced, impaled, skewered
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 protected, defended, shielded)
词性:v.
