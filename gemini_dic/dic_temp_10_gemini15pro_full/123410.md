monophyllous:/ˌmɒnəˈfɪləs/ | adj. | 单叶的；独叶的 | Having only one leaf.  Specifically used in botany to describe plants or parts of plants (like a flower) with a single leaf or leaf-like structure. |  The orchid is monophyllous, producing a single, large leaf each year.  这株兰花是单叶的，每年只长出一片大叶子。|  Monophyllous orchids are highly prized by collectors.  单叶兰花深受收藏家的青睐。 | synonym: unifoliate | antonym: polyphyllous | adjective
