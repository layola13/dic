boozed:/buːzd/
| adj. | 喝醉的 | intoxicated; drunk. | He came home boozed last night. 他昨晚喝醉回家了。 | tipsy, drunk, intoxicated, inebriated | sober, clear-headed | adjective

boozed:/buːzd/
| v. |  (非正式) 豪饮，狂饮 | (informal) To drink alcohol heavily. |  They boozed all night long. 他们整晚都在狂饮。 | drink heavily, get drunk, carouse, binge drink | abstain, be sober | verb

Etymology:
From *booze* (n.), which is of uncertain origin, possibly from Middle Dutch *būsen* "to drink heavily".
