multiplanetary:/ˌmʌltiˈplænɪtəri/ | adj. | 多行星的；涉及多个行星的 | Involving or relating to more than one planet.  |  例句：A multiplanetary species is one that can survive and thrive on more than one planet. 一个多行星物种是指能够在多个行星上生存和繁衍的物种。 | 例句：The future of humanity may lie in becoming a multiplanetary civilization. 人类的未来可能在于成为一个多行星文明。| 近义词：interplanetary | 反义词：N/A | 词性：形容词

Etymology->
From multi- (meaning "many") + planetary (relating to planets).  "Multi-" comes from Latin "multus" meaning "much, many". "Planetary" ultimately derives from the Greek word "planētēs" meaning "wanderer".
