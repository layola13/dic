complainer:/kəmˈpleɪnər/ | n. | 抱怨者，爱抱怨的人 | A person who complains frequently or habitually. |  She's a constant complainer, always finding something to criticize. 她总是抱怨，总能找到批评的东西。

He's known as the office complainer, never happy with anything. 他是办公室里有名的爱抱怨的人，对什么都不满意。


近义词: whiner, grumbler, faultfinder | 反义词: optimist, enthusiast | 词性: noun
