monotremata:/ˌmɒnəˈtriːmə-/ /ˌmɑːnəˈtriːmə-/| n. |单孔目动物|An order of primitive mammals that lay large yolky eggs and have a common opening for the urogenital and digestive systems.  They are represented by the echidnas and the platypus.|例句：The platypus and echidnas are the only surviving members of the Monotremata.  鸭嘴兽和针鼹是单孔目仅存的成员。

例句：Monotremata are unique among mammals for laying eggs. 单孔目动物在哺乳动物中独一无二，因为它们会产卵。|近义词：prototheria|反义词: (No direct antonym, but could be contrasted with)  Metatheria (marsupials), Eutheria (placental mammals)|词性: noun
