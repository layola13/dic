antievolutionism:/ˌæntiˌɛvəˈluːʃənɪzəm/ |n.| 反进化论|The belief that the theory of evolution is false and that other explanations, often religious, are needed to explain the origins and diversity of life.|  He's a staunch advocate of antievolutionism and believes the earth is only a few thousand years old. 他是反进化论的坚定拥护者，相信地球只有几千年的历史。|  Antievolutionism is often based on literal interpretations of religious texts. 反进化论通常基于对宗教文本的字面解释。 |近义词: creationism |反义词: evolutionism |词性: 名词

词源:
anti- (prefix) against, opposed to  + evolution (n.) + -ism (suffix) indicating a doctrine, belief, or system of thought
