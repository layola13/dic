incommutability:/ɪnkəmˌjuːtəˈbɪləti/| n.|不可改变性;不变性|The incommutability of the laws of physics is a fundamental principle. 物理定律的不可改变性是一个基本原则。|例句：The incommutability of his decision caused a rift in the family. 他的决定不可更改，导致了家庭的裂痕。

例句：The judge stressed the incommutability of the sentence. 法官强调了判决的不可更改性。

|近义词：immutability, unchangeableness, permanence|反义词: mutability, changeability|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + commutable (capable of being changed) + -ity (noun suffix).  "Commutable" ultimately derives from the Latin "commutare" (to change thoroughly).
