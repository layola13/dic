nondigestibility:/ˌnɒndaɪˌdʒɛstɪˈbɪlɪti/
n.|不消化性，难消化性|The nondigestibility of certain fibers contributes to their health benefits. 某些纤维的难消化性有助于其健康益处。|indigestibility, non-absorption|digestibility, assimilability|noun


Etymology->
From non- + digestibility.  Non- is a prefix meaning "not." Digestibility comes from the verb "digest," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "digerere," meaning "to separate, distribute, dissolve, digest."
