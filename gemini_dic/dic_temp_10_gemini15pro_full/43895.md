cretic:/ˈkriːtɪk/ | n. adj. | 长短长格, 克里特岛的 |A metrical foot consisting of a long syllable followed by a short syllable and then another long syllable (in quantitative verse) or of a stressed syllable followed by an unstressed syllable and then another stressed syllable (in accentual verse). Also occasionally (as adjective): of or relating to Crete or its ancient inhabitants. |The poem is written in cretic meter. 这首诗是用长短长格写的。|Synonym:  amphimacer (less common) |Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a metrical foot) | noun, adjective
