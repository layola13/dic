castigation:/ˌkæstɪˈɡeɪʃn/| n. | 惩罚，严惩；责骂，斥责 | Severe criticism or punishment. |  The teacher's castigation of the student for cheating was swift and severe. 老师对学生作弊行为的惩罚迅速而严厉。

The politician faced public castigation for his controversial remarks.  这位政治家因其有争议的言论而面临公众的谴责。| 近义词：punishment, reprimand, rebuke, censure, chastisement | 反义词：praise, commendation, reward | 词性: 名词
