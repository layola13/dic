clop:/klɑːp/ /klɑp/| n. vi. |（马蹄）得得声，（缓慢沉重地）走|The sound of a horse's hooves striking a hard surface, or the action of walking with a slow, heavy step.|The horse clopped down the cobblestone street. 马沿着鹅卵石街道得得地走。|近义词：clatter, thump|反义词:|n. vi.
