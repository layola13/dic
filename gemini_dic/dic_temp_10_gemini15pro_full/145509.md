overclutter:/ˌoʊvərˈklʌtər/ | v. |  使过度杂乱，堆满 | To fill a space or area with too many things, making it messy and disorganized. |  She overcluttered her apartment with furniture and knick-knacks. 她把自己的公寓堆满了家具和小摆设。 |  clutter, overcrowd | organize, tidy | Verb
overclutter:/ˌoʊvərˈklʌtər/ | n. | 过度杂乱，堆积 |  A state of being excessively cluttered. | The overclutter in the garage made it difficult to find anything. 车库里堆积如山的东西使得很难找到任何东西。 | clutter, mess | order, tidiness | Noun
