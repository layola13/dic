antispeculatively:/ˌæntiˈspɛkjʊlətɪvli/
|adv.| 反投机地；非投机性地|Acting or done in a way that discourages or avoids speculation.  It refers to actions taken to prevent or mitigate speculation, often in financial markets.|The government acted antispeculatively to curb inflation. 政府采取反投机措施来抑制通货膨胀。|Example: The central bank implemented antispeculatively measures to stabilize the currency. 中央银行实施了反投机措施以稳定货币。|Synonyms: cautiously, prudently, conservatively|Antonyms: speculatively, recklessly, rashly|adverb
