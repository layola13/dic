nonsedentarily:/nɑːnˈsɛdənˌtɛrɪli/
adverb
非久坐地；不固定地
in a nonsedentary manner; actively.  Engaging in physical activity rather than sitting or remaining still for extended periods.
She works nonsedentarily, frequently walking around the office and taking the stairs. 她工作时不常坐着，经常在办公室里走动，走楼梯。
He lives nonsedentarily, incorporating exercise and movement into his daily routine.  他的生活方式不固定，将锻炼和运动融入到他的日常生活中。
近义词: actively, dynamically, energetically
反义词: sedentarily, inactively, passively
词性: adverb

Etymology:
From non- + sedentary + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," "sedentary" refers to sitting or remaining in one place for extended periods, and "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs.
