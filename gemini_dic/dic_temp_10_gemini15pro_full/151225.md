parelcon:/ˈpærəlˌkɒn/ /ˈpærəlˌkɑːn/ | n. | 平行会议，并行会议 | A meeting or conference held at the same time as another, often related, meeting or conference. | The parelcon focused on emerging technologies while the main conference addressed current market trends.  平行会议的重点是新兴技术，而主会议则讨论了当前的市场趋势。

| Example: Several parelcons were organized to discuss specific issues related to the main conference theme.  几个平行会议被组织起来讨论与主会议主题相关的具体问题。| Synonyms: concurrent session, parallel session, breakout session | Antonyms: plenary session, main session | noun
