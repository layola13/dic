mystagogy: /ˈmɪstəɡɒdʒi/ /ˈmɪstəɡoʊdʒi/ | n. | 入门，奥秘的解释，秘传，基督教圣礼的解释 |  The introduction or explanation of mysteries, especially religious ones.
