foddering:/ˈfɒdərɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈfɑːdərɪŋ/ (US)| n.|饲料，秣料;  (比喻)素材,燃料|The process of providing fodder (food, especially dried hay or straw, for cattle and other livestock) or material for a specific purpose.  It can also refer metaphorically to providing "fuel" for something, like a discussion or a negative emotion.|The farmer spent the morning foddering the cows.  农民早上都在喂牛。|近义词：feed, forage, provender|反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 starvation, deprivation  指缺乏食物的状态)|noun
