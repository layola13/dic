enervators:/ˈɛnərˌveɪtərz/
n.|使人衰弱的人或物|People or things that cause someone to feel drained of energy or vitality. |The humid heat and lack of sleep were powerful enervators.|近义词:weakeners, debilitators|反义词:energizers, invigorators|词性:名词(plural)

enervate:/ˈɛnərˌveɪt/
v.|使衰弱，使无力|To deprive of force or strength; weaken. |The long illness had enervated him.|近义词:weaken, debilitate, sap, exhaust|反义词:energize, invigorate, strengthen|词性:动词

enervated:/ˈɛnərˌveɪtɪd/
adj.|衰弱的，无力的|Deprived of force or strength; weakened. |She felt enervated by the heat.|近义词:weakened, debilitated, exhausted|反义词:energized, invigorated, strengthened|词性:形容词


USAGE->
