nonadaptable:/ˌnɑːnəˈdæptəbl/ | adj. | 不可适应的，不能适应的 | Not able to adapt or be adapted.  |  He found himself in a nonadaptable work environment. 他发现自己在一个无法适应的工作环境中。 | 近义词：inflexible, rigid, unadaptable | 反义词：adaptable, flexible, versatile | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- + adaptable.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "adaptable" comes from the verb "adapt," which ultimately derives from the Latin "adaptare," meaning "to fit."
