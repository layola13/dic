enteron: /ˈentərɒn/ (UK) /ˈentəˌrɑːn/ (US)
n.
肠
The digestive tract, especially the small intestine.
The doctor examined the patient's enteron for signs of inflammation. 医生检查了病人的肠道是否有炎症迹象。
The enteron plays a vital role in nutrient absorption. 肠道在营养吸收中起着至关重要的作用。

近义词: intestine, gut
