outsider:/ˌaʊtˈsaɪdər/ | n. | 外地人；局外人；门外汉 |  A person who does not belong to a particular group, organization, or place; a stranger. |  He felt like an outsider in the close-knit community.  他感觉在这个联系紧密的社区里像个局外人。| synonyms: foreigner, stranger, alien, newcomer | antonyms: insider, native, local | noun

outsider:/ˌaʊtˈsaɪdər/ | n. | （比赛中的）冷门选手，爆冷选手 |  In a competition, a person or animal that is not expected to win. | The outsider surprised everyone by winning the race.  这匹爆冷的马赢得了比赛，让大家大吃一惊。 | synonyms: underdog, dark horse | antonyms: favorite, front-runner | noun
