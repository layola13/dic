neoplatonist:/ˌniːoʊˈpleɪtənɪst/
n.
新柏拉图主义者
A follower of Neoplatonism, a philosophical system originating in the 3rd century AD that combined elements of Plato's philosophy with other philosophical and religious systems.
Plotinus is considered the founder of Neoplatonism, and many prominent Neoplatonists followed him.  普罗提诺被认为是新柏拉图主义的创始人，许多杰出的新柏拉图主义者都追随他。

The neoplatonist believed in the existence of a single, supreme being.  这位新柏拉图主义者相信存在着一个至高无上的存在。

近义词:idealist, mystic
反义词:materialist, realist
词性:noun
