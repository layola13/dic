chavo:/ˈtʃɑːvoʊ/ (US), /ˈtʃævəʊ/ (UK)| n.|小伙子，孩子（主要用于墨西哥西班牙语）|A young boy or child.  This word is primarily used in Mexican Spanish. | Example: El chavo del ocho era muy gracioso. The kid from the eight was very funny.

Example: Ese chavo es muy inteligente. That kid is very smart.| 近义词: chico (boy), niño (child), muchacho (boy, lad),  kid (English), youngster (English) | 反义词: adulto (adult), hombre (man),  anciano (elderly man)|词性:noun
