brieriest:/ˈbraɪəriɪst/
adjective
最简短的，最简洁的
shortest, most concise
The editor insisted on the briefest possible summary of the news story.
编辑坚持要尽可能简短地概括这篇新闻报道。
近义词:shortest, tersest, most concise, most succinct
反义词:longest, wordiest, most verbose, most prolix
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English 'brær' (thorn, briar) + '-y' (suffix forming adjectives) + '-est' (superlative suffix).  The sense of "short" or "concise" likely developed from the idea of something being prickly and therefore unpleasant to linger on.
