eschew:/ɪsˈtʃuː/| vt.| 避免，避开，回避|deliberately avoid or refrain from (something).|She eschews publicity and avoids social media. 她避免抛头露面，也回避社交媒体。| He eschewed all forms of violence. 他避免一切形式的暴力。|近义词：avoid, shun, abstain from, forgo, renounce|反义词： embrace, indulge in, welcome|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Old French *eschiver "to shun, avoid, eschew," from Frankish *skiuhan "to frighten away, avoid," from Proto-Germanic *skeuhwijanan "to make shy, frighten away."  Cognate with Old High German sciuhen "to avoid, shun," German scheuen "to shy away from, be afraid of."
