effectualities:/ɪˌfɛktʃuˈælɪtiz/| n. |有效性；实际结果；实力|The effectualities of the new policy are yet to be seen. 新政策的有效性还有待观察。|The general outlined the effectualities of the new weapons system. 将军概述了新武器系统的实际效力。|近义词：effectiveness, efficacy, potency, validity|反义词：ineffectiveness, inefficacy, impotence, invalidity|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *effectualte*, from Late Latin *effectualitas*, from Latin *effectuālis* (“producing an effect”) + *-itās* (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality). *Effectuālis* is derived from *effectus* (“effect”) (from *efficiō* (“I bring about, accomplish, perform, complete”), from *ex-* (“out”) + *faciō* (“I do, make”)) + *-ālis* (suffix forming adjectives).
