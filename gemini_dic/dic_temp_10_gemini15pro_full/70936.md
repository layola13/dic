firecrackers:/ˈfaɪərkrækərz/ (US) /ˈfaɪəkrækəz/ (UK)| n. |爆竹，鞭炮|Small explosive devices primarily used for producing a loud noise and bright flashes of light, especially during celebrations.|例句：The children were excited to light the firecrackers on New Year's Eve. 孩子们很兴奋在除夕夜点燃爆竹。

例句：Firecrackers are a traditional part of many cultures' celebrations. 爆竹是许多文化庆祝活动的传统组成部分。|近义词：fireworks, bangers, crackers|反义词: None (no direct opposite)|词性: Noun
