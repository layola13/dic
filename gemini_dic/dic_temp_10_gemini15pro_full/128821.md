nightmare:/ˈnaɪtˌmer/ (US), /ˈnaɪtmeə(r)/ (UK)| n.|噩梦，可怕的经历|A dream that causes a strong feeling of fear, horror, distress, or anxiety. Also, a very unpleasant or frightening experience.|例句：I had a terrible nightmare last night. 我昨晚做了一个可怕的噩梦。

例句：The traffic jam was a nightmare. 交通堵塞真是一场噩梦。|近义词：bad dream, horror, ordeal|反义词：daydream, fantasy|词性:名词
