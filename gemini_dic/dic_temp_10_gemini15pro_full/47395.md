deadest:/ˈdedɪst/| adj.|最死的，死得最透的|superlative form of dead; most dead|例句：Of all the houseplants, this one looks the deadest.  在所有室内植物中，这株看起来最死了。

例句：His eyes were the deadest I'd ever seen. 他的眼睛是我见过的最没有生气的。|近义词：lifeless, inanimate, deceased|反义词:alive, living, animate|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old English *dēad, from Proto-Germanic *daudaz ("dead").  Cognate with Old Saxon dōd, Old Frisian dād, Old High German tōt, Gothic 𐌳𐌰𐌿𐌸𐍃 (dauþs), Old Norse dauðr, Dutch dood, German tot. Related also to die.
