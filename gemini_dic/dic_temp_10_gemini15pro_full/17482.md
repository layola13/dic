bearhound:/ˈberˌhaʊnd/| n.|猎熊犬|A dog of a large breed, originally used for hunting bears.|The bearhound bayed loudly as it chased the bear through the forest. 猎熊犬在追赶穿过森林的熊时大声吠叫。|近义词：hound, hunting dog|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑pet dog作为宠物犬与猎犬的功能性对比)|noun
