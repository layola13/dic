musk:/mʌsk/| n.|麝香；麝香鹿；麝香味|A strong-smelling reddish-brown substance which is produced by a male musk deer and used in perfumes.|The perfume had a strong musk scent. 这款香水有浓烈的麝香味。|Synonyms: aroma, fragrance, scent|Antonyms: odor, stench|noun

musk:/mʌsk/| n.|麝香鹿|A small deer that produces musk.|Musk deer are endangered due to poaching. 麝香鹿因偷猎而濒临灭绝。|Synonyms: deer|Antonyms: N/A|noun

musky:/ˈmʌski/| adj.|麝香的；有麝香味的|Having a smell or taste of musk.|The room was filled with a musky aroma. 房间里充满了麝香味。|Synonyms: aromatic, fragrant, scented|Antonyms: odoriferous, stinky|adjective
