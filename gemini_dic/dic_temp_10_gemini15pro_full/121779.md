misreceive:/ˌmɪsrɪˈsiːv/
verb
错误地接收；收错
To receive incorrectly or misunderstand.
I misreceived the package intended for my neighbor. 我收错了寄给我邻居的包裹。
The message was misreceived and caused a lot of confusion. 这条消息被误解了，造成了很多混乱。
misinterpret, misunderstand
receive correctly, understand correctly
