antenor:/ænˈtiːnɔːr/ | n. | 安特诺尔 | A Trojan nobleman who advocated making peace with the Greeks and returning Helen.  He is often portrayed as a traitor in later accounts, but Homer presents him as a wise and thoughtful counselor. | Antenor urged the Trojans to end the war. 安特诺尔敦促特洛伊人结束战争。| Antenor advised Priam to return Helen to the Greeks. 安特诺尔建议普里阿摩斯把海伦送回希腊。 | synonym: counselor, advisor | antonym: warmonger, belligerent | noun
