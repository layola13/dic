aphid: /ˈeɪfɪd/ (US), /ˈeɪfɪd/ (UK)| n. |蚜虫|A small, soft-bodied insect that sucks the sap from plants. |Example: Aphids can cause significant damage to crops. 蚜虫会对农作物造成重大损害。

Example:  The roses were covered in aphids. 玫瑰上爬满了蚜虫。

|近义词：greenfly, blackfly, plant louse|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin *aphis, probably from Greek ἀφρός (aphros, “foam”), perhaps referring to the honeydew they secrete.
