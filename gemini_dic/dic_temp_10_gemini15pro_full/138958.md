NVQ:/ˌɛn viː ˈkjuː/ | n. | National Vocational Qualification (国家职业资格证书) | A qualification in a specific occupational area, awarded in England, Wales and Northern Ireland, based on competence in the workplace. | Example: He gained an NVQ Level 3 in plumbing. 他获得了三级管道工程NVQ证书。| Example:  She's working towards her NVQ in childcare. 她正在努力获得儿童保育方面的NVQ证书。 |Synonym: qualification, certification, diploma | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps lack of qualification) | noun
