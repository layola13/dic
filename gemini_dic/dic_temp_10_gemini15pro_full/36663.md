colleterium:/kɒlɪˈtɪəriəm/ | n. | 粘腺，胶腺 | A gland, usually found in insects, that secretes a sticky substance used for various purposes such as egg protection or nest building. |  The colleterium of the female cockroach secretes a substance that forms the ootheca. 雌性蟑螂的粘腺分泌一种物质形成卵鞘。

The colleterium of some bees produces a glue-like substance used in nest construction.  一些蜜蜂的粘腺产生一种用于筑巢的胶状物质。 | synonyms: gland, secretory organ | antonyms: N/A | noun
