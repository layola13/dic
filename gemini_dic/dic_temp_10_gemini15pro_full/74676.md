freeboots:/ˈfriːbuːts/
n.
海盗，掠夺者，冒险家
A pirate, especially one who preys on Spanish ships and settlements in the West Indies.
例句：The freeboots plundered the coastal towns, taking everything of value.  海盗们洗劫了沿海城镇，带走了所有值钱的东西。

例句：Tales of daring freeboots filled the taverns. 酒馆里充满了关于大胆海盗的故事。

近义词：pirate, buccaneer, corsair, privateer
反义词: lawman, officer


词性: noun


词源:
From Dutch vrijbuiter, literally "free plunderer/looter", from vrij "free" + buit "booty, loot".  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *fraujaz "lord, master," and *beutaną "to improve, profit, benefit".
