incalescence:/ˌɪnkəˈlɛsəns/| n.|炽热，白热化;温度突然上升|A sudden increase in temperature, especially of a metal or alloy during heating, often accompanied by a change in physical properties.|The incalescence of the metal was a sign that it was reaching its melting point. 金属的白热化表明它正在达到熔点。|近义词：heating, warming, incandescence|反义词:cooling, chilling|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *incalescere, from in- "in, into" + calescere "to grow warm," from calere "to be warm."
