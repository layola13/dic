corklike:/ˈkɔːrklaɪk/
adjective
像软木的；软木似的
Resembling cork in texture, appearance, or lightness.
The bark of the tree was corklike and easy to peel.  这棵树的树皮像软木一样，很容易剥落。
The material had a corklike buoyancy.  这种材料具有像软木一样的浮力。
近义词:  spongy, porous, buoyant
反义词:  dense, compact, solid
词性: adjective
