chimpanzees:/ˌtʃɪmpænˈziːz/ | n. |黑猩猩|A great ape with large ears, mainly black coloration, and lighter skin on the face, native to the forests of western and central Africa.| Chimpanzees are highly intelligent animals. 黑猩猩是非常聪明的动物。|  Chimpanzees use tools like sticks to fish for termites. 黑猩猩会使用像棍子这样的工具来钓白蚁。| 近义词: ape, primate | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

Etymology: From Tshiluba *kivili-chimpenze, meaning "mockman" or possibly derived from a personal name.
