outthought:/ˌaʊtˈθɔːt/
verb
智胜；想得比…周到
To defeat or surpass someone in thinking or planning; to anticipate and counter someone's actions or strategies by superior thinking.
She outthought her opponents and won the negotiation. 她智胜了对手，赢得了谈判。
He outthought the police and escaped capture. 他比警察棋高一着，逃脱了抓捕。
outwit, outmaneuver, outsmart
be outthought by
v.
