adjusted:/əˈdʒʌstɪd/| adj.|调整过的，调节过的|Adapted or modified to suit a particular purpose or condition.  Brought into a proper or desired relationship.|The adjusted figures reflect the current market value. 调整后的数字反映了当前的市场价值。
The recipe can be adjusted to suit individual tastes.  这个食谱可以根据个人口味进行调整。|近义词：modified, altered, adapted, corrected, amended|反义词：unaltered, unadjusted, original|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English *adjusten*, from Old French *ajuster* "to arrange, put in order," from Vulgar Latin *adjustare*, from Latin *ad* "to" + *iuxta* "near, next to."  The meaning shifted from "put in order" to "make fit or suitable" in the 17th century.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The thermostat is adjusted automatically. = 恒温器会自动调节。
He adjusts his tie before the interview. = 他在面试前整理了一下领带。

一般过去时:
The company adjusted its prices to reflect the increased costs. = 公司调整了价格以反映增加的成本。
She adjusted the mirror so she could see better. = 她调整了镜子以便看得更清楚。

现在完成时:
They have adjusted their plans due to the bad weather. = 由于天气恶劣，他们已经调整了计划。
He has adjusted well to his new job. = 他已经很好地适应了他的新工作。

过去完成时:
After he had adjusted the settings, the picture was much clearer. = 在他调整设置之后，画面清晰多了。
They had adjusted their strategy before the competition began. = 他们在比赛开始前调整了策略。

将来时:
We will adjust the schedule if necessary. = 如果需要，我们将调整时间表。
The airline will adjust its flight schedule for the holiday season. = 航空公司将调整假期的航班时刻表。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would adjust my attitude. = 如果我是你，我会调整我的态度。
I wish they had adjusted their plans earlier. = 我希望他们早点调整计划。

被动语态:
The interest rate was adjusted by the central bank. = 中央银行调整了利率。
The equipment is being adjusted for the experiment. = 设备正在为实验进行调整。

疑问句:
Has the time been adjusted for daylight saving time? = 时间是否已根据夏令时进行了调整？
Why did you adjust the volume? = 你为什么调整音量？

否定句:
The figures have not been adjusted for inflation. = 这些数字尚未根据通货膨胀进行调整。
He doesn't adjust well to change. = 他不太适应变化。
