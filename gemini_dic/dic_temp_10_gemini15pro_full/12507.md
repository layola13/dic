aspersion:/əˈspɜːrʒən/
n.|诽谤，中伤|An aspersion is an attack on someone's reputation or integrity.|The politician faced aspersions on his character during the election campaign.  这位政治家在竞选期间面临对其品格的攻击。|synonyms: defamation, slander, calumny, vilification|antonyms: praise, commendation, acclaim, applause|noun

aspersion:/əˈspɜːrʒən/
n.|洒水，喷水|Aspersion can also refer to the act of sprinkling, typically of water in a religious context. |The priest performed the aspersion of holy water.  牧师进行了圣水的洒水仪式。|synonyms: sprinkling, spraying|antonyms: N/A|noun
