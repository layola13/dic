PHRASE->
lock up:  锁好；关押  Lock up the house before you leave. 离开前锁好房子。
lock in:  锁住；困住 The rising tide locked the boat in the harbor. 上涨的潮水把船困在了港口里。
lock out:  锁住不让…进入；把…关在外面  She locked herself out of her apartment. 她把自己锁在了公寓外面。
lock horns:  争吵；角力 The two candidates locked horns over the issue of healthcare. 两位候选人在医疗保健问题上争执不休。
lock, stock, and barrel: 全部地；所有的一切 They sold the business lock, stock, and barrel. 他们把生意全部卖掉了。
