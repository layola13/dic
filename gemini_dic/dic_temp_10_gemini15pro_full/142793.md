ounded:/ˈaʊndɪd/| adj.|圆形的，浑圆的；（声音）圆润的，洪亮的；大量的，充足的|Having a curved or rounded shape; (of a sound) full and resonant; large and plentiful|The valley was surrounded by rounded hills.  山谷被圆形的山丘包围着。  The singer had a beautifully rounded voice. 歌手的声音优美而圆润。 He received a rounded education. 他接受了全面的教育。|近义词：circular, curved, full, rich, comprehensive|反义词：flat, sharp, thin, incomplete|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *roundid, *roundede, from round +‎ -ed. Cognate with Scots roundit (“rounded”).
