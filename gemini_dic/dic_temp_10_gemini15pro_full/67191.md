expressible:/ɪkˈsprɛsəbl/| adj.|可表达的，可言喻的|Able to be expressed or conveyed, especially in words.  Something that is expressible can be put into words or communicated effectively.|Her gratitude was too deep to be expressible in words. 她的感激之情太深了，无法用言语表达。|近义词：communicable, utterable, articulable|反义词：inexpressible, unutterable, incommunicable|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Middle English: from late Latin *expressabilis, from Latin express- ‘pressed out’ (see express).
