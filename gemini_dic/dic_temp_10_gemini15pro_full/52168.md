diaphragm: /ˈdaɪəfræm/ (US) /ˈdaɪəfræɡm/ (UK)| n. |膈膜，光圈，膜片| A dome-shaped, muscular partition separating the thorax from the abdomen in mammals. It plays a major role in breathing, as its contraction increases the volume of the thorax and so inflates the lungs.  Also refers to a thin sheet of material forming a partition.| The diaphragm contracts and relaxes as we breathe.  我们的呼吸过程中，膈膜会收缩和放松。| synonyms: membrane, septum | antonyms: none | noun

diaphragms: /ˈdaɪəfræmz/ (US) /ˈdaɪəfræɡmz/ (UK)| n. |膈膜（复数），光圈（复数），膜片（复数）| The plural form of "diaphragm". Refers to multiple diaphragms. | The diaphragms of the cameras were adjusted for the low light.  这些相机的 diaphragms 已针对低光进行了调整。| synonyms: membranes, septa | antonyms: none | noun
