mutiny:/ˈmjuːtɪni/ | n. vi. | 叛乱，兵变；反抗，不服从 | An open rebellion against constituted authority, especially by sailors or soldiers against their officers. | The crew mutinied and took control of the ship. 船员们发动叛乱，控制了这艘船。|  The soldiers staged a mutiny against their cruel commander. 士兵们发动兵变反抗他们残暴的指挥官。 | 近义词: rebellion, revolt, uprising, insurrection | 反义词: obedience, submission | 词性: 名词, 动词

mutiny:/ˈmjuːtɪni/ | vi. |  发动叛乱，进行兵变；反抗，不服从 | To engage in mutiny. | The sailors mutinied over poor food and low pay.  水手们因为食物差、工资低而发动了叛变。| The workers mutinied against the new factory regulations. 工人们反抗新的工厂规章制度。 | 近义词: rebel, revolt, rise up | 反义词: obey, submit | 词性: 动词
