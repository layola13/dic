nonabstractedness:/nɒnæbˈstræktɪdnəs/ | n. | 非抽象性；具体性 | The quality of being nonabstract; concreteness.  The state of being focused on concrete details or specific instances rather than general concepts or principles. | The nonabstractedness of the legal language made it easy to understand.  法律语言的具体性使它易于理解。 |  concreteness, specificity, particularity | abstractness, generality | noun
