balachong:/ˈbɑːlətʃɒŋ/ (UK), /ˈbɑːlətʃɔːŋ/ (US)| n.|峇拉煎，马来盏|A pungent, fermented shrimp paste used as a condiment in Southeast Asian cuisine, particularly in Malaysia, Singapore, and Indonesia. It is made from small shrimp or krill that are fermented, dried, and then usually ground into a paste.  It is typically used in small quantities due to its strong flavor.|例句：She added a spoonful of balachong to the sambal. 她往参峇里加了一匙峇拉煎。

例句：The aroma of balachong filled the kitchen. 厨房里充满了峇拉煎的香味。|近义词：shrimp paste, terasi (Indonesian)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
