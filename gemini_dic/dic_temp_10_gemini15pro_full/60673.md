electrepeter: /ɪˌlɛktriˈpiːtər/ | n. | 电复读机 | An electronic device that records and plays back audio, often used for language learning. |  Example: The student used an electrepeter to practice his pronunciation. 学生用电复读机练习发音。 Example: Electrepeters were popular before digital audio players became common. 在数字音频播放器普及之前，电复读机很流行。 | 近义词: tape recorder, audio recorder, language learning device | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
