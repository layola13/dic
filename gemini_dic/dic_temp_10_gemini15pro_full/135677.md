nonprepositional:/ˌnɑːnˌprɛpəˈzɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 非介词的，不使用介词的 | Not using or requiring a preposition.  This term usually describes a verb that takes a direct object without a preposition. | The verb "enter" is nonprepositional; you enter a room, not enter into a room.  进入这个动词是非介词的；你进入一个房间，而不是进入到一个房间。|近义词: prepositionless|反义词: prepositional|词性:形容词
