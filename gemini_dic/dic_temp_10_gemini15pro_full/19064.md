betokened:/bɪˈtoʊkənd/
verb
预示，表示
indicated, signified.
The dark clouds betokened a storm.  乌云预示着暴风雨。
His smile betokened his satisfaction. 他的笑容表明他很满意。
Synonyms: presage, portend, foreshadow, augur, indicate, signify
Antonyms: conceal, mask, hide
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bitokenen, from be- + tokenen (“to signify”).  The prefix be- intensifies the meaning of the verb.  Token itself comes from Old English tācen, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *taiknaz.
