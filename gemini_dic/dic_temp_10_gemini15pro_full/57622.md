draughtier:/ˈdrɑːftiər/ /ˈdræftiər/| adj.|更通风的，更透风的|More draughty than something else; having more currents of air passing through.|The old house was draughtier in the winter than in the summer. 冬天老房子比夏天更透风。|Synonyms: airier, breezier, more ventilated|Antonyms: stuffier, less ventilated, airtight|adjective
