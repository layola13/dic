outshriek: /ˌaʊtˈʃriːk/ | v. |  大声喊叫，盖过…的喊叫声 | to shout louder than someone or something else |  She outshrieked the other fans at the concert. 她在音乐会上盖过了其他粉丝的喊叫声。| 近义词: outshout, outyell | 反义词: whisper, murmur | 词性: 动词

outshriek: /ˌaʊtˈʃriːk/ | n. |  大声喊叫，尖叫声  | a loud shout or shriek | The outshriek of the crowd was deafening. 人群的尖叫声震耳欲聋。| 近义词: yell, scream, shriek | 反义词: whisper, murmur | 词性: 名词

词源:  From out- + shriek.  "Out-" prefix indicating surpassing or exceeding.  "Shriek" meaning a loud, sharp cry.
