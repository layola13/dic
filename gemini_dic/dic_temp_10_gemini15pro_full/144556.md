outwale: /ˈaʊtweɪl/ | n. | 外龙骨；舷缘材 | A thick, longitudinal timber bolted to a ship's frames along the gunwale, strengthening the side and serving as a fender. | The outwale of the small boat was damaged in the collision. 小船的外龙骨在碰撞中受损了。| The outwale protected the hull from damage as the ship came alongside the dock.  当船靠码头时，外龙骨保护了船体免受损坏。 | synonyms: gunwale strake, sheerstrake | antonyms: keelson (internal strengthening member) | noun
