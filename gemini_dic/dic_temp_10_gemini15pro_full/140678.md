omigod:/oʊ maɪ ˈɡɑːd/ | interj. | 我的天哪；哦，我的上帝 | An exclamation of surprise, shock, or dismay.  A shortening of "Oh my God". | Omigod, I forgot my keys! 我的天哪，我忘记带钥匙了！| Omigod, did you see that? 我的天哪，你看到了吗？ | 近义词: oh my god, oh my gosh, good heavens, good grief | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for an exclamation) | 词性: interjection
