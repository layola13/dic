catalysis:/kəˈtælɪsɪs/| n.|催化(作用); 触媒作用|The reaction takes place through catalysis. 这个反应是通过催化作用发生的。|Example: Enzymes are essential for catalysis in living organisms. 酶对于生物体内的催化作用至关重要。

Example: The platinum in the catalytic converter promotes the catalysis of harmful gases into less harmful ones. 催化转化器中的铂促进了有害气体向低害气体的催化转化。|近义词：acceleration, facilitation|反义词: inhibition, retardation|词性:noun

catalyses:/ˈkætəlaɪzɪz/| v.|催化|The enzyme catalyses the breakdown of starch into sugars. 这种酶催化淀粉分解成糖。|Example:  A catalyst catalyses a chemical reaction without being consumed itself. 催化剂催化化学反应而自身不被消耗。

Example:  Sunlight catalyses the production of vitamin D in our skin. 阳光催化我们皮肤中维生素D的产生。|近义词：accelerate, facilitate, promote|反义词: inhibit, hinder, retard|词性:verb
