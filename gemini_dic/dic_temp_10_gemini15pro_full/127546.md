necrophobia:/ˈnɛkrəˌfoʊbiə/| n. |恐惧死亡；尸体恐惧症|An abnormal and persistent fear of death or dead things.  It's a specific phobia, characterized by excessive and unreasonable anxiety triggered by reminders of mortality.|She couldn't visit her grandmother in the hospital due to her severe necrophobia. 她因为严重的尸体恐惧症而无法去医院探望她的祖母。|Synonym: thanatophobia|Antonym: None|Noun
