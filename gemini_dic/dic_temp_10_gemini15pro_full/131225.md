noncallable:/ˌnɑːnˈkɔːləbl/ | adj. | 不可赎回的，不可提前偿还的 | Not able to be called or redeemed before maturity, especially of bonds or loans.  | Example: Investors are attracted to noncallable bonds because they offer a guaranteed interest rate for a specific period. 投资者被不可赎回债券所吸引，因为它们在特定时期内提供有保证的利率。 | Example:  A noncallable loan ensures the borrower has access to the funds for the entire loan term. 不可提前偿还的贷款确保借款人在整个贷款期限内可以使用资金。 | Synonyms: irredeemable, non-redeemable | Antonyms: callable, redeemable | word property: adjective
