chlorofluorocarbons: /ˌklɔːrəʊˈflʊərəʊˌkɑːrbənz/ /ˌklɔːroʊˈflʊərəʊˌkɑːrbənz/ | n. |氯氟烃|  Synthetic compounds containing carbon, chlorine, and fluorine, typically gases used in refrigerants and aerosols. They are harmful to the ozone layer. | Chlorofluorocarbons were widely used in refrigerators until scientists discovered their damaging effects on the ozone layer. 氯氟烃曾被广泛用于冰箱，直到科学家发现它们对臭氧层的破坏作用。| Synonyms: CFCs, freons | Antonyms: None | Noun
