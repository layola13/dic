nontraversable:/ˌnɒntrəˈvɜːsəbl/ /ˌnɑːntrəˈvɜːrsəbl/| adj.|不可穿越的，无法通行的|Not able to be traversed or crossed; impassable.|The dense jungle was nontraversable.|近义词：impassable, inaccessible, impenetrable|反义词：traversable, passable, accessible|词性:形容词
Etymology: From non- + traversable.  "Traverse" comes from Old French "traverser" meaning "to cross," ultimately from Latin "trans-" (across) + "vertere" (to turn).
