diuretic:/ˌdaɪjʊˈrɛtɪk/| adj. n.|利尿的；利尿剂|Increasing the volume of urine excreted.  A medicine that promotes the production of urine.|
The doctor prescribed a diuretic to help reduce the fluid buildup in his legs. 医生给他开了利尿剂以帮助减少腿部积液。|
This medication is a powerful diuretic. 这种药物是一种强效利尿剂。|
近义词：water pill|
反义词：antidiuretic|
词性：adjective, noun|

diuretical:/ˌdaɪjʊˈrɛtɪkəl/| adj.|利尿的|Having the effect of increasing the excretion of urine.|
Diuretical medications can be helpful for people with high blood pressure. 利尿药物可以帮助高血压患者。|
The diuretical properties of this herb are well known. 这种草药的利尿特性是众所周知的。|
近义词：diuretic|
反义词：antidiuretic|
词性：adjective|

From French *diurétique*, from Greek *διουρητικός (diourētikos)*, from *διά (dia, “through”)* + *οὖρον (ouron, “urine”)*.
