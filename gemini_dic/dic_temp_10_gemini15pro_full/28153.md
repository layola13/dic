caple:/ˈkeɪpəl/| n.| 挽具，马匹，劣马|A horse, especially a worn-out or inferior one.  Originally, a workhorse, especially one used with a plow.  Later, often used in a derogatory sense.|The farmer sold the old caple at the market.|近义词：nag, jade, hack|反义词: steed, charger|noun
