fantasias:/fænˈteɪʒəz/| n. |幻想曲；狂想曲；幻想作品（fantasia的复数）|A musical composition of a free and whimsical form.  A work of art, such as a novel or a film, that is based on imagination and not on fact.|The orchestra performed several fantasias by Mozart.  管弦乐队演奏了莫扎特的多首幻想曲。|Synonyms: fantasies, daydreams, reveries|Antonyms: realities, facts|Noun
