e-toll:/ˈiːtəʊl/ | n. | 电子收费 | Electronic toll collection, a system where drivers pay tolls electronically, usually without stopping. | Example: The e-toll system makes commuting much faster. 电子收费系统让通勤变得更快。
Example:  I paid the e-toll online. 我在网上支付了电子通行费。 | 近义词: electronic toll collection, automatic toll collection | 反义词: manual toll collection | 词性: noun
