palmerworm:/ˈpɑːmərˌwɜːrm/ (US) /ˈpɑːməˌwɜːm/ (UK)| n. |毛毛虫，尺蠖，尤其指侵害农作物的|A hairy caterpillar, especially one that is considered a pest to crops.  Specifically, it often refers to the larvae of various moths.|例句：The palmerworms devoured the entire field of crops. 毛毛虫吞噬了整片农作物。

例句：The farmer sprayed insecticide to protect his crops from palmerworms.  农民喷洒杀虫剂以保护他的作物免受毛毛虫侵害。

近义词：caterpillar, larva, grub|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a larval stage insect)|词性: Noun
