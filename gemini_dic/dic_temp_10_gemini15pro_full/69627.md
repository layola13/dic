feminisms:/ˈfɛmɪnɪzəmz/
noun
女权主义（复数）
Theories and movements advocating for women's rights and equality.
例句：Many feminisms exist, each with its own perspective on gender inequality. 许多女权主义存在，每一种都有自己对性别不平等的看法。

例句：Intersectionality is a key concept in many contemporary feminisms.  交叉性是许多当代女权主义中的一个关键概念。

近义词：women's liberation movements, feminist movements
反义词：anti-feminism, sexism, patriarchy
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From French féminisme, from féminin (feminine) + -isme (-ism). Ultimately from Latin femina (woman).
