guiltiest:/ˈɡɪltiɪst/ | adj. | 最内疚的，最有罪的 |  Superlative form of "guilty," meaning experiencing or showing remorse for a wrongdoing. |  例句：He felt like the guiltiest person in the world. 他觉得自己是世界上最内疚的人。

例句：Of all the siblings, she felt the guiltiest about neglecting their aging parents. 在所有的兄弟姐妹中，她对忽视年迈的父母感到最内疚。 | 近义词：most culpable, most remorseful | 反义词：least culpable, most innocent | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *gilti, from Old English gyltig (“guilty, sinful, wicked, criminal”), from Proto-Germanic *gultigaz (“guilty”). Related to guilt. The suffix -est is the superlative suffix.
