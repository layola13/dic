interclasping:/ˌɪntərˈklɑːspɪŋ/ /ˌɪntərˈklæspɪŋ/
adjective
互相扣紧的，交握的
The action or state of being clasped or intertwined together.
Her hands were interclasping in her lap. 她的双手交握在膝上。
近义词：intertwined, interlaced, clasped
反义词：separated, unclasped
词性：adjective

interclasping:/ˌɪntərˈklɑːspɪŋ/ /ˌɪntərˈklæspɪŋ/
noun
交握，扣紧
The act of clasping or intertwining together.
The interclasping of their fingers symbolized their unity.  他们手指的交握象征着他们的团结。
近义词：intertwining, interlacing, clasping
反义词：separation, unclasping
词性：noun


词源：
From inter- + clasping, present participle of clasp.
