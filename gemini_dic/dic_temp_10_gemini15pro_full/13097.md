astronomies: /əˈstrɒnəmiz/
n.
天文学(的多个分支); 各个天文学领域
the various branches of astronomy; different astronomical systems or fields of study.
She studied the astronomies of ancient civilizations. 她研究了古代文明的天文学。
近义词: astronomy, astrophysics, cosmology
反义词:  (无直接反义词，可以考虑一些非科学领域，例如 astrology 占星术)
词性: 名词(复数)

Etymology:
From Old French astronomie, from Latin astronomia, from Ancient Greek ἀστρονομία (astronomía), from ἄστρον (ástron, “star”) + νόμος (nómos, “law” or “culture”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂stḗr (“star”).
