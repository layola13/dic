goodwood:/ˈɡʊdwʊd/| n.|古德伍德（地名）|Goodwood is a country estate and historic sporting venue located near Chichester, West Sussex, England.|The Goodwood Festival of Speed is a popular event. 古德伍德速度节是一个很受欢迎的活动。|近义词：(N/A - 地名通常没有近义词，可以考虑 Chichester 作为附近的城镇)|反义词：(N/A - 地名没有反义词)|词性:noun

词源:  'Goodwood' is a place name.  It likely derives from 'Godu' (an Old English personal name) + 'wudu' (Old English for 'wood').  Therefore, it probably originally meant 'Godu's wood'.
