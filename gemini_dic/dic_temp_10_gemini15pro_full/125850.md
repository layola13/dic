musar:/ˈmuːzɑːr/ (US), /ˈmjuːzɑːr/ (UK)| n.|沉思，冥想；(尤指道德方面的)教训，箴言|A careful and thoughtful consideration of a subject, often involving moral or ethical issues.  It can also refer to a moral lesson or precept.|例句：He spent hours in quiet musar, pondering the meaning of life. 他花了几个小时静静地沉思，思考人生的意义。

例句：The rabbi's musar offered guidance on how to live a righteous life. 拉比的箴言为如何过上正直的生活提供了指导。|近义词：reflection, contemplation, meditation, rumination, precept, maxim|反义词：frivolity, levity|词性:noun
