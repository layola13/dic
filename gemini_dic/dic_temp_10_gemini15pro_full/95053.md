inactuation:/ˌɪnˌæktʃuˈeɪʃən/
n.|启动失效，不启动|The inactuation of the safety system caused the accident.|安全系统失效导致了这起事故。
例句：The inactuation of the airbags contributed to the severity of the injuries.  安全气囊未启动加剧了伤势的严重程度。

例句： Investigators are looking into the inactuation of the emergency brakes.  调查人员正在调查紧急制动器失效的原因。
近义词：failure, malfunction, non-activation, non-operation
反义词：activation, operation, functioning
词性: 名词


词源:  in- (not) + actuate (to put into action) + -ion (noun suffix)  来自拉丁语 actus, 意为“行动”。
