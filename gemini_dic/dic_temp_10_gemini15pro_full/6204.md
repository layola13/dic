ammoniated:/əˈmoʊniˌeɪtɪd/
adjective
氨化的；用氨处理的
treated with ammonia; containing ammonia.
例句：Ammoniated mercury is a white, odorless powder. 氨化汞是一种白色、无味的粉末。

例句：Farmers use ammoniated fertilizers to enrich the soil.  农民使用氨化肥料来肥沃土壤。

近义词：ammoniacal
反义词：N/A
词性：adjective
