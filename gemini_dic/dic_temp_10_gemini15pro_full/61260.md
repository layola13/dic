emancipatory:/ɪˌmænsɪˈpeɪtəri/
adjective
解放的，使人获得自由的
Relating to or promoting emancipation; setting free from restraint, control, or power.
The Civil Rights Act of 1964 was an emancipatory piece of legislation.  1964年的《民权法案》是一项解放性的立法。

The invention of the printing press had an emancipatory effect on the spread of knowledge. 印刷术的发明对知识的传播具有解放作用。

近义词:liberating, freeing, releasing
反义词:oppressive, restrictive, enslaving
词性:adjective

词源:
From emancipate + -ory.  Emancipate comes from Latin emancipare "to set free," from ex- "out" + mancipare "to transfer ownership," from manus "hand" + capere "to take."
