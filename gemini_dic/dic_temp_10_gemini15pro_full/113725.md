macrocystis: /ˌmækroʊˈsɪstɪs/ | n. |巨藻属，巨藻 |A genus of large brown algae, commonly known as giant kelp, found in cool, nutrient-rich waters.| Example: Macrocystis pyrifera is the largest species of kelp. 巨藻是最大的海藻种类。| Example:  Forests of Macrocystis provide habitat for many marine animals. 巨藻林为许多海洋动物提供了栖息地。| 近义词: kelp, seaweed | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek  *μακρός (makrós, “long, large”) + *κύστις (kústis, “bladder, pouch”).
