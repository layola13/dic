metosteon:/ˌmɛtəˈstiːən/| n. |额骨|The metosteon is a bone in the forehead of some animals, particularly ungulates, situated between the frontal bones and the nasal bones.  It's often referred to as the "interfrontal".|The paleontologist identified the fossil by its distinct metosteon.  古生物学家通过其独特的额骨识别了这块化石。|Synonyms: interfrontal (more common in scientific literature)|Antonyms:  (None.  This is a specific anatomical term without a direct opposite.)|noun
