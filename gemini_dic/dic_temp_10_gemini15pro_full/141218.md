oopses:/ˈuːpsiːz/| n.| 失误，错误（复数）| An exclamation of surprise or mild apology, typically used when one has made a small mistake.  It is the plural form of "oops."|Oops, I spilled my coffee.  哎呀，我洒了咖啡。Oops, I forgot your birthday. 哎呀，我忘了你的生日。|近义词：mistakes, errors, blunders|反义词: successes, achievements|noun
