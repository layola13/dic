adds:/ædz/
verb
增加；补充；附带说明
increases the number or amount of something.

The fire adds to the coziness of the room.
火增添了房间的舒适感。

Extra charges add up quickly.
额外的费用很快就增加了。

近义词:increases, supplements, appends
反义词:subtracts, deducts, removes
词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English adden, from Old French adier, from Latin addere (to add), from ad- (to) + dare (to give).

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He adds sugar to his coffee. = 他在咖啡里加糖。
She adds a personal touch to her artwork. = 她在她的艺术作品中添加了个人风格。

一般过去时:
They added a new wing to the building last year. = 去年他们给大楼增加了一个新的侧翼。
I added some salt to the soup. = 我在汤里加了些盐。

现在完成时:
The company has added several new products to its line. = 公司在其产品线中增加了几个新产品。
We have added more staff to the team. = 我们在团队中增加了更多员工。

过去完成时:
She had added the finishing touches to the painting before the gallery opened. = 在画廊开幕之前，她已经为这幅画添加了最后的润色。
He had added an extra chapter to the book before it was published. = 在这本书出版之前，他添加了一章。

将来时:
I will add your name to the list. = 我会把你的名字添加到名单上。
They will add more features to the software next year. = 明年他们将在软件中添加更多功能。

虚拟语气:
If I were to add anything, I would add more spices. = 如果我要添加任何东西，我会添加更多香料。
I wish I could add more time to my day. = 我希望我能为我的一天增加更多时间。

被动语态:
A new chapter was added to the book. = 这本书增加了一章。
More security measures are being added to the building. = 大楼正在增加更多的安全措施。

否定句:
I didn't add anything to the recipe. = 我没有在食谱中添加任何东西。
She doesn't add salt to her food. = 她不在食物里加盐。


PHRASE->
add up = 合计；加起来
add up to = 总计达；意味着
add in = 包括；添加
add on = 添加；附加
add to = 增加；增添
