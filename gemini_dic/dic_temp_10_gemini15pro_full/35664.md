cobaltous:/koʊˈbɔːltəs/| adj.|钴的；二价钴的|Containing or relating to cobalt, especially in its +2 oxidation state.  Characterized by cobalt, especially divalent cobalt.|Cobaltous chloride is a common laboratory reagent.|近义词:cobalt(II)|反义词:cobaltic|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cobalteux*, from *cobalt* (see cobalt) + *-eux* (suffix forming adjectives meaning 'characterized by').
