acotyledonous: /ˌeɪˌkɒtɪˈliːdənəs/ /ˌeɪˌkɑːtɪˈliːdənəs/
adjective
无子叶的
Lacking cotyledons; (of a plant) not having or producing cotyledons. Also, relating to or characteristic of such plants.
Ferns and horsetails are acotyledonous plants.  蕨类植物和木贼是无子叶植物。

Acotyledonous plants reproduce through spores. 无子叶植物通过孢子繁殖。

synonym
acotyledon, cryptogamous
antonym
cotyledonous, phanerogamous
词性
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From a- (not) + cotyledon (seed leaf) + -ous (adjective suffix).  Cotyledon comes from the Greek kotylēdōn, meaning "cup-shaped hollow."
