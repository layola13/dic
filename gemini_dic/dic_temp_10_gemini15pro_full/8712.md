anticults:/ˈæntiˌkʌlts/
n.|反邪教组织，反邪教运动|Groups or movements that actively oppose cults and their practices.  These organizations often focus on educating the public about the dangers of cults and providing support for former cult members.| The anticults helped many people leave destructive organizations. 反邪教组织帮助许多人离开了具有破坏性的组织。|近义词：cult awareness groups, countercult movement|反义词:pro-cult groups|词性:noun
