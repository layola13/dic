gretch:/ɡrɛtʃ/ | n. | 怨言，抱怨 | A complaint or grievance. |  "She aired her gretches about the new policy." 她表达了对新政策的不满。 | 近义词:complaint, grievance, gripe | 反义词:praise, compliment | 词性:名词

Etymology: From Middle English *grecchen, from Old North French *grechier (“to complain, grumble”), from Frankish *grōtiōn (“to cry out, groan”), from Proto-Germanic *grōtijaną (“to weep, lament”).
