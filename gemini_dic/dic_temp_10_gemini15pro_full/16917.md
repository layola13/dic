basking:/ˈbæskɪŋ/ | v. | 晒太阳，取暖，沉浸于 | lie exposed to warmth and light, typically from the sun, for relaxation and pleasure; revel in something that produces feelings of well-being or gratification | The cat was basking in the sun. 猫在晒太阳。 |  The team basked in the glory of their victory. 团队沉浸在胜利的荣耀中。 | synonyms: luxuriating, reveling, enjoying, soaking up | antonyms: shivering, freezing, suffering | verb

basking:/ˈbæskɪŋ/ | adj. | 享受的，舒适的 |  Experiencing or displaying enjoyment or comfort |  He had a basking smile on his face. 他脸上带着享受的笑容。 | synonyms: comfortable, relaxed, content | antonyms: uncomfortable, stressed, anxious | adjective
