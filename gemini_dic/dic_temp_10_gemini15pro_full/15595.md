bail:/beɪl/ | n. v. | 保释金，保释，舀水 | Money paid to release someone from jail until their trial.  To remove water from a boat. | The judge set bail at $10,000. 法官将保释金定为1万美元。 He bailed water from the sinking boat. 他从下沉的船里舀水。 |  近义词：(n.) bond, security, (v.) scoop, ladle | 反义词: (v.) flood | 词性: 名词，动词

bail:/beɪl/ | n. v. | 横木，栅栏，放弃，退出 | A horizontal bar or a series of bars.  To abandon a difficult situation or activity. | The cows jumped over the bail. 奶牛跳过了栅栏。 He bailed on the project at the last minute. 他在最后一刻放弃了这个项目。 | 近义词: (n.) rail, bar, (v.) abandon, quit | 反义词: (v.) persist, continue | 词性: 名词，动词
