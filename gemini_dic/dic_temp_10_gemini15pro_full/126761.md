nankeen:/nænˈkiːn/
noun|南京布，淡黄褐色|A durable buff-colored cotton cloth, originally from Nanjing, China.  Also, the yellowish-brown color of this cloth.|She wore a dress made of nankeen. 她穿着一件南京布做的连衣裙。|Synonyms: buff, beige, khaki|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a color/fabric. Could consider contrasting colors like navy, black)|n.

nankeen:/nænˈkiːn/
adjective|淡黄褐色的|Of a yellowish-brown color, like nankeen cloth.|He had a nankeen jacket. 他有一件淡黄褐色的夹克。|Synonyms: buff, beige, khaki|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a color. Could consider contrasting colors like navy, black)|adj.
