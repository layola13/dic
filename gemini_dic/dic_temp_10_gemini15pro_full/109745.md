leverwood: /ˈlɛvərˌwʊd/ | n. | 铁木;美洲朴 | A deciduous tree (Ostrya virginiana) of eastern North America, having very hard wood and bearing hoplike fruit clusters. |  The leverwood tree is known for its exceptionally hard wood, making it difficult to work with but excellent for tool handles. 铁木以其异常坚硬的木材而闻名，因此难以加工，但非常适合制作工具手柄。 | The early settlers used leverwood to make levers and other tools. 早期的定居者用铁木制作杠杆和其他工具。 | Synonyms: ironwood, hophornbeam | Antonyms: N/A | noun
