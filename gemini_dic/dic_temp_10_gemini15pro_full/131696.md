noncondescendingly:/ˌnɒnkɒndɪˈsendɪŋli/ | adv. | 不居高临下地，没有优越感地 |  In a manner that does not treat someone as if they are less intelligent or less important. | She explained the complex topic noncondescendingly, making sure everyone understood. 她不居高临下地解释了这个复杂的话题，确保每个人都理解。|  respectfully, courteously, graciously | condescendingly, patronizingly | adverb

词源:
From non- (not) + condescending (showing a feeling of superiority) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Condescend" comes from Latin "condescendere" (to stoop down to).
