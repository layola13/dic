bilker:/ˈbɪlkər/| n. |诈骗者，赖账者|A person who cheats or swindles someone, especially by avoiding payment of a debt. |The bilker skipped town after racking up huge debts at the local stores.  这个骗子在当地商店欠下巨额债务后逃之夭夭。|Synonyms: cheat, swindler, fraudster, defaulter, dodger|Antonyms: honest person, payer|noun


bilker:/ˈbɪlkər/| n. |逃避责任者|A person who evades responsibility or duty. |He was a bilker who never fulfilled his promises. 他是一个逃避责任的人，从不履行自己的承诺。|Synonyms: shirker, evader, dodger|Antonyms: responsible person|noun
