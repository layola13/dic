newsgroup:/ˈnjuːzɡruːp/| n.|新闻组；讨论组|A newsgroup is an online discussion forum, part of a wider system called Usenet.  It is used for the public exchange of opinions and information on a particular topic.|I regularly contribute to several photography newsgroups.|Synonyms: forum, discussion group, online community, bulletin board|Antonyms: blog (arguably, as blogs can be one-person entities), personal website|Noun
