de-pressingly:/dɪˈprɛsɪŋli/
| adv. | 沮丧地；令人沮丧地 |  In a way that causes or expresses sadness and lack of hope. | The rain fell depressingly. 雨令人沮丧地下着。|Synonyms: sadly, gloomily, dishearteningly, miserably | Antonyms: cheerfully, joyfully, encouragingly, hopefully | 词性: 副词

de-pressed:/dɪˈprɛst/
| adj. | 沮丧的；萧条的 | (of a person) in a state of general unhappiness and low spirits. | She felt depressed after losing her job. 她失业后感到沮丧。| Synonyms: sad, unhappy, down, dejected | Antonyms: happy, cheerful, elated, joyful | 词性: 形容词

press:/prɛs/
| v. | 按，压；逼迫；催促 | to exert steady force against (something), typically by means of something else. | He pressed the button to open the door. 他按下按钮打开门。| Synonyms: push, squeeze, compress | Antonyms: pull, release, loosen  | 词性: 动词
