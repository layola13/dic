overroughly:/ˌoʊvərˈrʌfli/
adverb|粗略地，大致地| done in a rough or approximate way, without attention to detail. |He overroughly estimated the cost of the project. 他粗略地估计了项目的成本。|例句：The land was overroughly surveyed.  这块土地的测量很粗略。|近义词：roughly, approximately, imprecisely|反义词：precisely, accurately, meticulously|词性：副词
词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "roughly" (adverb meaning "in a rough manner").  The prefix intensifies the meaning of "roughly".
