doily: /ˈdɔɪli/ (US) /ˈdɔɪli/ (UK)| n. |小垫子，装饰垫|A small ornamental mat, often made of lace or paper, placed under dishes, vases, or other objects on a table or other surface. |She placed the cake on a delicate lace doily. 她把蛋糕放在一个精致的蕾丝小垫子上。|The antique shop had a collection of crocheted doilies.  这家古董店收藏了一批钩针编织的小垫子。|近义词：mat, placemat, coaster|反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:noun
