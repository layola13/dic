overtrusted:/ˌoʊvərˈtrʌstɪd/ | adj. | 过于信任的，被过度信赖的 |  Having been trusted excessively; placed too much trust in.  It implies a situation where someone or something has been given more trust than they deserved, often leading to negative consequences. |  The overtrusted employee embezzled millions from the company.  那个被过度信任的员工挪用了公司数百万美元。|  The overtrusted security system failed to prevent the breach. 被过度信赖的安全系统未能阻止入侵。 | synonyms: overly trusted, excessively relied upon | antonyms: distrusted, suspected, doubted | adjective
