factoidal:/fækˈtɔɪdəl/| adj.| 事实般的；貌似真实的；近乎事实的| Resembling or having the appearance of fact; quasi-factual.  While the story sounds factoidal, there's no evidence to support it.|例句：The article presented a factoidal account of the historical event. 这篇文章对这一历史事件进行了事实性的描述。

例句：His factoidal arguments were persuasive, but lacked real substance. 他的论点看似真实，但却缺乏实质内容。|近义词：factual, fact-based, truth-like|反义词：fictional, fabricated, untrue|词性：形容词
