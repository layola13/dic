babbles:/ˈbæbəlz/ (US) /ˈbæbəlz/ (UK)| v. n.|胡言乱语，牙牙学语；喋喋不休；(水)潺潺作响|To talk rapidly and continuously in a foolish, excited, or incomprehensible way; to utter sounds like a baby.|The baby babbles happily in his crib. 婴儿在婴儿床里高兴地牙牙学语。|近义词：chatters, prattles, murmurs, gurgles|反义词：articulates, enunciates|词性:动词 (verb), 名词 (noun)
