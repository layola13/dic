inarching:/ɪnˈɑːrtʃɪŋ/
n.|压条繁殖，靠接|The process of grafting by approach, where two plants are joined while both are still growing on their own roots.|We used inarching to propagate the rare apple variety.|近义词：grafting, layering|反义词:cutting, division
词性:名词
