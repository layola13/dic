humored:/ˈhjuːmərd/| adj.|幽默的，诙谐的；迁就的，顺从的|Having or showing a sense of humor; indulging or complying with someone's whims or moods.  Inclined to be amused or tolerant.|He seemed good-humored despite the long wait.  尽管等了很久，他看起来还是心情很好。|Synonyms:  amiable, cheerful, good-natured, indulgent, tolerant|Antonyms:  bad-tempered, grumpy, ill-humored, irritable, sullen|adjective
