on-board:/ˌɒnˈbɔːd/ /ˌɑːnˈbɔːrd/| adj. adv.|在船上，在飞机上，在火车上；内置的，集成的|Existing or carried out while on a vehicle, such as a ship, train, or aircraft; integrated into a system or structure.|例句：The aircraft has on-board entertainment systems. 飞机上有机载娱乐系统。|近义词：aboard, built-in, integrated|反义词：off-board, external|词性：形容词/副词

on-board:/ˌɒnˈbɔːd/ /ˌɑːnˈbɔːrd/| adv.|上船，上飞机，上火车|To go onto a vehicle, such as a ship, train, or aircraft.|例句：Passengers are now invited to on-board the aircraft.  现在请乘客登机。|近义词：embark, board|反义词：disembark|词性：副词

词源: on + board.  "Board" originally referred to the side of a ship, and later expanded to mean any vehicle.
