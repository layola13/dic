half-contentedness:/ˌhæf kənˈtɛntɪdnəs/ |n.| 半满足；略感满足 |A state of being partially or moderately content; not fully satisfied but not unhappy either. |She felt a sense of half-contentedness, pleased with what she had achieved but still yearning for more. 她感到一种半满足感，对自己所取得的成就感到高兴，但仍然渴望更多。|例句：He lived a life of half-contentedness, never truly happy but never truly sad either.  他过着半满足的生活，从未真正快乐过，但也从未真正悲伤过。

例句：There was a half-contentedness in her smile, suggesting a lingering dissatisfaction. 她的笑容里带着一丝半满足，暗示着挥之不去的不满。| 近义词: partial contentment, moderate satisfaction, mild happiness | 反义词: discontentment, dissatisfaction, unhappiness |词性: 名词
