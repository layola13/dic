densest:/ˈdensɪst/| adj.|最密集的|Superlative form of dense: most dense.  Having the most people, things, or plants in a small area.|The densest fog I've ever driven through. 我开车经过过的最浓的雾。| The densest population is in the city center.  市中心的人口最稠密。| 近义词: thickest, most compact, most crowded|反义词: thinnest, sparsest, least dense|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *densest, superlative of dens, from Old French dens, from Latin densus (“thick, dense, close, compact”).
