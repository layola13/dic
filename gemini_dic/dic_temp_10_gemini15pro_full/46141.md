cymograph:/ˈsaɪməɡræf/| n. |记纹鼓，描记器|An instrument for recording variations in pressure, as of blood pressure, or muscular contractions.|The cymograph recorded the subtle changes in the patient's pulse. 记纹鼓记录了病人脉搏的细微变化。|Synonyms: recorder, kymograph|Antonyms: None|noun

cymographic:/ˌsaɪməˈɡræfɪk/| adj. |记纹鼓的，描记器的|Relating to or produced by a cymograph.|The cymographic recording showed a steady increase in activity.  描记器的记录显示活动持续增加。|Synonyms: recording, inscribing|Antonyms: None|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek κύμα (kúma, “wave”) + γράφω (gráphō, “to write”).
