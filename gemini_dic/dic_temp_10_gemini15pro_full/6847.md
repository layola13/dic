anathemize:/əˈnæθəˌmaɪz/ | vt. | 诅咒，咒骂，强烈谴责 | To pronounce a curse upon; to denounce or condemn someone or something as evil or wicked. | The priest anathematized the heretics.  神父诅咒了异教徒。

The government anathematized the rebels. 政府强烈谴责了叛乱分子。 | 近义词：curse, condemn, denounce, excommunicate | 反义词：bless, praise, commend | 词性：动词
