misunderstanders:/ˌmɪsʌndərˈstændərz/| n. |误解者；不理解的人|People who misunderstand something or someone are misunderstanders.  误解某事或某人的人是误解者。|The misunderstanders took offense at her comment, even though she meant no harm. 误解者对她的评论感到生气，即使她没有恶意。|近义词：misinterpreters, misconstruers|反义词: understanders, interpreters|词性:noun

词源:
From "misunderstand" (verb) + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting a person who performs the action of the verb).  "Misunderstand" comes from "mis-" (prefix indicating wrong or bad) + "understand" (from Old English "understandan," meaning "to comprehend").
