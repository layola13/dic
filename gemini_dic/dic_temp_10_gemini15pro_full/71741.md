flaxiest:/ˈflæksiɪst/| adj.|最易弯曲的，最柔韧的|Superlative form of "flaxy," meaning resembling flax in its flexibility or looseness.  It describes something that bends or yields very easily.|The gymnast was the flaxiest of all the competitors.|近义词：supplest, most pliable, bendy, most flexible|反义词:stiffest, rigidest, firmest|词性:adjective
Etymology: From flax + -y + -est. Flax refers to a plant with long, flexible fibers used to make linen.  The "-y" suffix forms adjectives meaning "having the quality of," and "-est" is the superlative suffix.
