close-ups:/ˈkloʊs ʌps/ (US), /ˈkləʊs ʌps/ (UK)| n.|特写镜头|A photograph, movie, or television shot taken at close range. |The film uses a lot of close-ups to show the actors' emotions.  这部电影使用了大量的特写镜头来展现演员的情绪。|Synonyms: close shot, close view|Antonyms: long shot, wide shot|noun

close-ups:/ˈkloʊs ʌps/ (US), /ˈkləʊs ʌps/ (UK)| n.|特写镜头|Detailed examination. (Figurative) |The documentary provided close-ups of the lives of ordinary people. 这部纪录片详细展现了普通人的生活。|Synonyms: detailed view, close examination|Antonyms: overview, general view|noun
