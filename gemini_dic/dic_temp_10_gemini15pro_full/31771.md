checkable:/ˈtʃɛkəbl/| adj.|可检查的，可核实的|Able to be checked or verified.|例句：The information is easily checkable online.  这些信息很容易在网上查到。|近义词：verifiable, confirmable|反义词:unverifiable, unconfirmable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "check" (meaning to examine or verify) + "-able" (suffix indicating capability).  "Check" ultimately derives from Old French "eschec," referring to a chessboard and by extension, control or restraint.
