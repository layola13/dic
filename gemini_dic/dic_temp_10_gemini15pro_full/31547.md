cartographic:/ˌkɑːrtəˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. | 制图的；地图绘制的 | Relating to the making of maps or charts. |  This cartographic representation shows the distribution of population density.  这幅地图描绘了人口密度的分布。| cartographical, mapmaking | non-cartographic | adjective

Etymology:
From French *cartographique*, from Greek *khártēs* (paper, map) + *-graphikos* (relating to writing or drawing).
