omphalopsychite:/ˈɒmfələʊˈsaɪkaɪt/ | n. | 肚脐凝视者；冥想者 | A person who engages in omphaloskepsis (contemplation of one's navel as an aid to meditation).  A mystic who practices navel-gazing as a form of religious contemplation. |  He was known as an omphalopsychite, always lost in deep meditation. 他被称为一个肚脐凝视者，总是沉浸在深度冥想中。|  The omphalopsychite spent hours in quiet contemplation.  这位冥想者花了几个小时静静地沉思。| Synonym: mystic, contemplative, meditator | Antonym:  extrovert, materialist | Noun

From Greek *omphalos* (navel) + *psychē* (soul, mind)
