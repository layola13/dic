metastannic:/ˌmɛtəˈstænɪk/ | adj. |偏锡酸的；变锡酸的| Relating to or designating a less soluble form of stannic acid, probably a polymerized form of the ordinary acid.|  Metastannic acid is formed by the action of nitric acid on tin. | 近义词: stannic (in some contexts) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, depends on the specific chemical context) | 词性: adjective

词源:
From meta- + stannic.  "Meta-" in this context indicates a "changed" or "modified" form.  Stannic refers to tin(IV) compounds.
