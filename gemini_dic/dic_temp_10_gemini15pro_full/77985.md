gentilitial:/ˌdʒɛntɪˈlɪʃəl/
adjective
家族的；民族的；国籍的
Relating to or denoting a person's place of origin or nationality.
The gentilitial adjective for someone from Canada is "Canadian." 加拿大人的家族形容词是“加拿大人”。
Gentilitial names often end in -an, -ian, -ese, or -ish. 家族名称通常以-an、-ian、-ese 或-ish 结尾。
synonyms: national, native, ethnic
antonyms: foreign, alien
词性: adjective
