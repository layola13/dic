hamose:/ˈhæmoʊs/| adj.| 好战的，嗜杀的 | Inclined to fighting; warlike; bloodthirsty. |  The hamose rhetoric of the dictator further inflamed tensions.  独裁者的好战言辞进一步加剧了紧张局势。 | Example: The hamose tribe was known for its fierce warriors. 这个好战的部落以其凶猛的战士而闻名。| 近义词: aggressive, belligerent, pugnacious, truculent | 反义词: peaceful, pacifistic, dovish | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *hamosus (“hooked”), from Latin hamus (“hook”).  The semantic shift likely occurred through the association of hooks with weapons and thus with fighting.
