detectivelike:/dɪˈtɛktɪvˌlaɪk/
adjective

Resembling or characteristic of a detective; observant and inquisitive.
She had a detectivelike eye for detail. 她有着侦探般敏锐的观察力，对细节一丝不苟。
His detectivelike questioning made her nervous. 他像侦探一样的盘问让她感到紧张。
近义词: observant, inquisitive, shrewd, perceptive, astute
反义词: unobservant, inattentive, oblivious, careless
