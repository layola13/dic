nonintelligences: /nɒnɪnˈtɛlɪdʒənsɪz/
|n.| 非智力因素；非情报信息|Lack of intelligence or information not related to intelligence. |The report focused on the nonintelligences impacting the mission's success, such as logistical failures and communication breakdowns. 这份报告关注的是影响任务成功的非智力因素，例如后勤失误和沟通中断。| Synonyms: non-cognitive factors, extraneous factors | Antonyms: intelligences, cognitive abilities | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix):  From Latin *non "not."
intelligence (noun): From Old French intelligence (12c.), from Latin intelligentia "understanding, power of discerning; art, skill, taste," from intelligentem (nominative intelligens) "intelligent, understanding," present participle of intelligere "to understand, comprehend, perceive," from inter "between" (from PIE root *en "in") + legere "choose, gather, read" (from PIE root *leg- (1) "to collect, gather," with derivatives meaning "to speak (to 'pick out words')")
