curtailment:/kərˈteɪlmənt/
noun
缩减；削减
the act of reducing or restricting something
例句：The government announced the curtailment of public spending. 政府宣布削减公共开支。

例句：Budget curtailments have forced the company to lay off workers. 预算削减迫使公司裁员。

近义词：reduction, decrease, cutback, restriction
反义词：increase, expansion, extension
词性: n.
