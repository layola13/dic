bookable:/ˈbʊkəbəl/| adj.|可预订的，可预约的|That hotel room is bookable online. 那家酒店的房间可以在线预订。|例句：The tickets are bookable through our website.  门票可以通过我们的网站预订。|近义词：reservable, available|反义词:unavailable, unbookable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "book" (v.) + "-able" (suffix meaning "capable of being"). "Book" comes from Old English "bōc" (noun) and "bōcian" (verb), ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*bōk-" (beech tree, writing tablet). The "-able" suffix comes from Old French "-able" and Latin "-abilis".
