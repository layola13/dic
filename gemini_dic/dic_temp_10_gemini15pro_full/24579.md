brochure:/broʊˈʃʊr/ (US) /ˈbrəʊʃə(r)/ (UK)| n. |小册子，宣传册|A small booklet or pamphlet containing information about a product, service, or place.|We picked up some brochures at the tourist office. 我们在旅游局拿了一些宣传册。|leaflet, pamphlet, booklet|book, novel|noun


brochure:/broʊˈʃʊr/ (US) /ˈbrəʊʃə(r)/ (UK)| n. |宣传册|A small book or magazine containing pictures and information about a product or service.|The travel agent gave us a brochure about holidays in Greece. 旅行社给了我们一本关于希腊度假的宣传册。|pamphlet, leaflet, flyer|book, magazine|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French "brochure" (stitched work), from "brocher" (to stitch), from Old French "brochier," ultimately from Vulgar Latin "*broccare" (to prick, stitch).
