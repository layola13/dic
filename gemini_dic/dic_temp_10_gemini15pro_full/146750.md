overhumanizing:/ˌoʊvərˈhjuːmənaɪzɪŋ/
|adj.|过度拟人化|Attributing excessively human qualities, emotions, or motivations to non-human entities, often animals or AI.|例句：Overhumanizing pets can lead to unrealistic expectations of their behavior.  过度拟人化宠物会导致对其行为不切实际的期望。

例句：Some critics argue that the movie overhumanizes the robot characters, making them too relatable. 一些评论家认为这部电影过度拟人化了机器人角色，使它们过于容易产生共鸣。|近义词：anthropomorphizing|反义词:objectifying, dehumanizing|词性:adjective
