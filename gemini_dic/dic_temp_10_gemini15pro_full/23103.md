bountyless:/ˈbaʊntilɪs/
adjective
无赏金的；没有报酬的；贫瘠的
Without bounty; unrewarded; unproductive.
The bountyless desert stretched before them, offering no sustenance.  在他们面前，荒凉贫瘠的沙漠延伸开来，没有任何食物。
The bountyless hunter returned home empty-handed.  一无所获的猎人空手而归。
Synonyms: unrewarding, barren, unproductive, sterile, infertile, meager, scanty
Antonyms: bountiful, fruitful, fertile, productive, rewarding, lucrative
