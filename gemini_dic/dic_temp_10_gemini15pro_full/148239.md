oversimplistic:/ˌoʊvərˌsɪmplˈɪstɪk/ | adj. | 过于简化的，过分简单化的 |Characterized by extreme simplicity; presenting a complex issue in a way that makes it seem much simpler than it actually is. | The explanation was oversimplistic and failed to address the nuances of the problem.  这个解释过于简单化，没有涉及到问题的细微之处。|  The politician's oversimplistic view of the economy ignored several key factors. 这位政治家对经济的过度简化观点忽略了几个关键因素。| synonyms: simplistic, reductive, naive, shallow | antonyms: nuanced, complex, sophisticated, in-depth | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (excessively) + "simplistic" (characterized by excessive simplicity).  "Simplistic" is derived from "simple" (easy to understand or do) + "-istic" (forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Latin "simplex" (single, simple).
