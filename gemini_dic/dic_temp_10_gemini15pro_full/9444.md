antireducing:/ˌæntiɹɪˈduːsɪŋ/ | adj. | 抗还原的 |Preventing or inhibiting reduction (a chemical reaction involving the gain of electrons). This term describes a substance or process that opposes reduction.| The antireducing agent protected the metal from corrosion.  抗还原剂保护金属免受腐蚀。| near synonyms: antioxidant, reducing inhibitor | near antonyms: reducing, pro-oxidant | adjective
