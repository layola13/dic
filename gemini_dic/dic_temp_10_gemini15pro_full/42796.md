couplet:/ˈkʌplɪt/
n.
对句; 对联; 双行诗
Two lines of verse, usually in the same meter and joined by rhyme, that form a unit.
Shakespeare's sonnets always end in a couplet.  莎士比亚的十四行诗总是以对句结尾。

The poet concluded with a rhyming couplet. 诗人以押韵的对句结尾。

synonym: distich, pair, duo
antonym: N/A
