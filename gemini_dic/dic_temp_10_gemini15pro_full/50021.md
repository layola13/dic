denominational:/dɪˌnɑːməˈneɪʃənəl/
adjective
宗派的；教派的
Relating to or characteristic of a particular religious denomination.
The school is nondenominational, accepting students of all faiths. 这所学校不分宗派，接受所有信仰的学生。
denominational differences have led to conflicts in the region.  教派分歧导致了该地区的冲突。

近义词: sectarian, religious
反义词: nondenominational, secular, ecumenical
词性: adjective
