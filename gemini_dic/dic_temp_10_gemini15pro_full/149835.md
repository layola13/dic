paleotechnic:/ˌpeɪlɪoʊˈtɛknɪk/ | adj. | 旧石器时代的；使用简单工具的；前工业时代的 | Relating to or designating the earliest phase of industrial development, characterized by the use of water and wind power and by the burning of wood as fuel. |  The paleotechnic era saw the rise of watermills and windmills.  旧石器时代见证了水磨坊和风车的发展。

The museum's exhibit showcased paleotechnic tools and machinery.  博物馆的展览展示了旧石器时代的工具和机械。| 近义词: pre-industrial, primitive | 反义词: neotechnic, modern | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Greek *palaios* "old" + *tekhnikos* "of art, skillful."
