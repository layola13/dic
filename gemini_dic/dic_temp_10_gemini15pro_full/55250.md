dissimulating:/dɪˈsɪmjʊleɪtɪŋ/
| adj. | 掩饰的，假装的 | concealing or disguising one's true thoughts, feelings, or intentions |  He was dissimulating his true intentions with a false smile. 他用虚假的微笑掩饰着他真正的意图。 |  dissembling, feigning, masking, pretending | revealing, honest, candid | adjective
dissimulate:/dɪˈsɪmjʊleɪt/
| v. | 掩饰，假装 | to conceal or disguise one's true thoughts, feelings, or intentions | She dissimulated her anger with a forced smile. 她强颜欢笑以掩饰她的愤怒。| dissemble, feign, mask, pretend | reveal, expose, be honest | verb
