nonremembrance:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈmɛmbrəns/
n.
遗忘; 不记得; 没被想起的状态
The state of being forgotten or not remembered.
The anniversary of her death passed by in nonremembrance. 她逝世的周年纪念日在无人记起中过去了。
近义词:forgetfulness, oblivion, amnesia
反义词:remembrance, memory, recollection
词性:名词
