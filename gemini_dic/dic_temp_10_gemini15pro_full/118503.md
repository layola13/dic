mesne:/miːn/
adjective
中間的；間接的；intermediate; intervening
The mesne profits were calculated from the date of the breach of contract.  中间收益是从违约之日起计算的。
He holds the mesne tenancy between the freeholder and the subtenant. 他持有自由持有人和分租人之间的中间租赁权。
Synonyms: intermediate, intervening, middle, median
Antonyms: extreme, ultimate, direct
词性: adjective


mesne:/miːn/
noun
中间领主；中间产业；intermediate lord; intermediate estate
The mesne lord held the land between the king and the tenant. 中间领主持有国王和佃户之间的土地。
The property was held in mesne. 该财产由中间人持有。
Synonyms: intermediary, go-between, mediator
Antonyms: principal, owner
词性: noun
