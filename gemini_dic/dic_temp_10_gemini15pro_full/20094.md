biosociological:/ˌbaɪoʊˌsoʊsiəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
生物社会学的
Relating to the interaction of biological and social factors in determining individual or group behavior.
Biosociological research suggests that both genetics and environment play a role in human aggression. 生物社会学研究表明，遗传和环境都对人类的攻击性起作用。
Biosociological factors can influence health outcomes. 生物社会因素会影响健康结果。
Synonyms: biosocial
Antonyms: None
词性: adjective
