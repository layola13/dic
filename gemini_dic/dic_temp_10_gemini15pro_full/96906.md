inexpiable:/ɪnˈɛkspiəbl/| adj.|不可饶恕的，无法补偿的|That which cannot be expiated; that for which no atonement can be made.|His crime was so heinous, so inexpiable, that he was banished from the kingdom. 他的罪行如此滔天，如此不可饶恕，以至于他被驱逐出王国。|Synonyms: unforgivable, unpardonable, irredeemable|Antonyms: forgivable, pardonable, excusable|adjective
