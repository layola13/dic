dorkiest:/ˈdɔːrkiɪst/
adjective
最呆的，最傻的
superlative adjective of dorky; most dorky.  Describing someone as extremely socially awkward, unfashionable, or obsessed with niche interests.
He wore the dorkiest glasses I've ever seen. 他戴着我见过的最傻的眼镜。
近义词:nerdiest, geekiest, goofiest
反义词:coolest, hippest, trendiest
词性:adjective
