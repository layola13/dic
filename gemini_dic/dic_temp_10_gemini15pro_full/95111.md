inanimate:/ɪˈnænɪmət/ | adj. |无生命的；无生气的 |Not alive, especially not in the manner of animals and humans. |例句：Rocks and minerals are inanimate objects.  岩石和矿物质是无生命的物体。|例句：She looked at the inanimate statue with a sense of awe. 她敬畏地看着那座毫无生气的雕像。|近义词：lifeless, inert, dead |反义词：animate, living, alive |词性:形容词

词源:
From Late Latin *inanimatus, from in- ‘not’ + animatus ‘having breath, alive.’
