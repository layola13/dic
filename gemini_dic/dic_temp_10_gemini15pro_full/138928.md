nutritiously:/njuˈtrɪʃəsli/
adv.
营养地，滋养地
in a way that provides the substances that people, animals, and plants need to live and grow healthily.
She always makes sure her family eats nutritiously. 她总是确保她的家人吃得营养。
Eating nutritiously is important for maintaining good health.  营养饮食对保持健康很重要。
近义词:healthily, healthfully
反义词:unhealthily, poorly
词性:adverb
