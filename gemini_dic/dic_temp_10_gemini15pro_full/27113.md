calligraphic: /ˌkælɪˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. | 书法体的，书法的 | relating to calligraphy; written in a beautiful script |  例句：The wedding invitations were written in a calligraphic script. 婚礼请柬是用书法字体写的。| 近义词：cursive, elegant, artistic | 反义词：printed, typed, plain | 词性：形容词
Etymology: From Greek *kalligraphikos*, from *kallos* "beauty" + *graphein* "to write."
