internally:/ɪnˈtɜːnəli/ | adv. | 内部地，在内部；内在地 |  Happening or existing inside something, especially the body or a building. | The company discussed the issue internally. 公司内部讨论了这个问题。| internally:/ɪnˈtɜːnəli/ | adv. | 内部地，在内部；内在地 |  Relating to the inner workings or structure of something. | The software works internally to process data.  这个软件在内部运作以处理数据。 | 近义词: inwardly, within, inside | 反义词: externally, outwardly | 词性: 副词

词源: Late Middle English: from medieval Latin *internalis, from Latin internus ‘inward’.
