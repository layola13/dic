arabesque:/ˌærəˈbɛsk/ | n. |蔓藤花纹，阿拉伯式花纹，（芭蕾舞）阿拉贝斯克舞姿 |An ornamental design consisting of intertwined flowing lines, typically based on floral forms and often used in Islamic art.  Also, a position in ballet in which one leg is extended backward at right angles to the body, especially while standing on the other leg. |The wallpaper featured a delicate arabesque pattern.  壁纸上装饰着精致的蔓藤花纹图案。

She held the arabesque pose with effortless grace. 她轻松优雅地保持着阿拉贝斯克舞姿。 |近义词： ornamentation, flourish, decoration (for design); attitude (for ballet) |反义词: plainness, simplicity (for design)| 词性: noun
