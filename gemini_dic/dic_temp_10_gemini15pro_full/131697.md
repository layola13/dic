noncondescendingness:/ˌnɒnkɒndɪˈsendɪŋnəs/ | n. |  不屈尊俯就；没有优越感；平易近人 | The quality of not behaving in a condescending way; lack of condescension.  It implies treating others with respect and as equals, regardless of their social standing, intelligence, or other perceived differences. |  Her noncondescendingness put everyone at ease. 她平易近人的态度让每个人都感到轻松自在。| synonyms: humility, modesty, respectfulness, egalitarianism | antonyms: condescension, arrogance, haughtiness, superciliousness | noun
