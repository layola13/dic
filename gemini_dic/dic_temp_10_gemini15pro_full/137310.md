nontactically:/nɒnˈtæktɪkli/
adjective
非战术地，非策略地
Not in a tactical or strategic manner; without regard to tactics or strategy.
The general nontactically deployed his troops, leading to a chaotic battle. 将军非战术性地部署了他的部队，导致了一场混乱的战斗。
Near synonyms:  unstrategically, haphazardly, randomly, carelessly
Antonyms:  tactically, strategically, deliberately, planned
词性: adjective
