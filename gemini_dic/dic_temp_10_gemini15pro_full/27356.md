cambrasine: /kæmˈbrəˌsiːn/ | n. |  剑麻碱; 剑麻毒素 |  A toxic alkaloid found in sisal (Agave sisalana). It is a steroidal sapogenin.  |  Cambrasine can be extracted from sisal leaves.  剑麻碱可以从剑麻叶中提取。| Sisal leaves contain cambrasine, a toxic substance. 剑麻叶含有剑麻碱，一种有毒物质。 | Synonyms:  sisalagenin | Antonyms:  None | Noun
