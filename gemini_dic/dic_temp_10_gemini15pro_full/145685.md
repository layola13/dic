overcopiousness:/ˌəʊvəˈkəʊpiəsnəs/| n. | 过于丰富，过多，冗长 | The overcopiousness of his writing made it difficult to read.  他过于丰富的写作风格使得文章难以阅读。 | Example: The overcopiousness of detail in the report made it tedious.  报告中过多的细节使其冗长乏味。

Example:  Her overcopiousness of language obscured the main point she was trying to make. 她过于丰富的语言掩盖了她想要表达的要点。 | 近义词：prolixity, verbosity, redundancy, superfluity | 反义词: conciseness, brevity, succinctness | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + copiousness.  "Over-" signifies "excessive" or "too much." "Copiousness" comes from the Latin "copiosus," meaning "plentiful" or "abundant."
