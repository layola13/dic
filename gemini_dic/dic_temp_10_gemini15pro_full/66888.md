expediency:/ɪkˈspɪːdiənsi/ | n. |权宜之计，方便，利己 | The quality of being convenient and practical despite possibly being improper or immoral; advantageousness. |  He acted out of expediency rather than principle. 他出于权宜之计而不是原则行事。| synonyms: convenience, practicality, advantage, opportunism | antonyms: principle, morality, ethics | noun

expedient:/ɪkˈspɪːdiənt/ | adj. |权宜的，方便的，有利的 | (of an action) convenient and practical although possibly improper or immoral. | It is expedient to use a smaller car in the city. 在城市里使用小型汽车比较方便。| synonyms: convenient, practical, advantageous, opportune | antonyms: inexpedient, inconvenient, impractical, disadvantageous | adjective
