extracurricular: /ˌekstrəkəˈrɪkjələr/ | adj. | 课外的，业余的 | Relating to activities or subjects not included in the usual school or college course. | She's involved in many extracurricular activities, including sports and music. 她参与许多课外活动，包括体育和音乐。|  He participated in various extracurriculars to broaden his horizons. 他参加各种课外活动来拓宽视野。 | 近义词：nonscholastic, outside, spare-time | 反义词：curricular, academic | 词性: 形容词

词源:  extra- (outside) + curricular (relating to a curriculum) 来自拉丁语 "extra" (outside) 和 "currere" (to run),  "curriculum" 原意指 "a course to be run".
