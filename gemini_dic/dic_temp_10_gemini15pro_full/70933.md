firebug:/ˈfaɪərbʌɡ/| n. |纵火犯；放火者；消防员（旧称）；（美）萤火虫|A person who deliberately sets fire to property; an arsonist. Also, an archaic term for a firefighter, or (US) a firefly.|The police caught the firebug responsible for the string of recent arsons. 警方抓获了对最近一系列纵火案负责的纵火犯。|Synonyms: arsonist, incendiary, pyromaniac|Antonyms: firefighter, fireman|noun

firebug:/ˈfaɪərbʌɡ/| n. |萤火虫（美式用法）|A firefly (chiefly US).|Children love to catch firebugs on summer nights. 孩子们喜欢在夏夜捉萤火虫。|Synonyms: firefly, lightning bug|Antonyms: None|noun
