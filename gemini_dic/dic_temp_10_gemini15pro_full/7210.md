anglesite:/ˈæŋɡləˌsaɪt/| n. |角铅矿|A mineral consisting of lead sulfate, PbSO4, and crystallizing in the orthorhombic system. It is usually colorless, white, or gray.  It forms as a secondary mineral from the oxidation of galena.|Anglesite is a common secondary mineral found in lead deposits.|近义词:lead sulfate, cerussite|反义词:None|词性:noun

Etymology: Named after the locality of its discovery, Anglesey, Wales, in 1783.
