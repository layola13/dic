embolite:/ˈɛmbəˌlaɪt/| n.|氯溴银矿|A silver halide mineral composed of silver chloride and silver bromide.|Embolite is a valuable ore of silver found in arid regions. 氯溴银矿是一种在干旱地区发现的珍贵的银矿石。|Synonyms: bromargyrite, chlorargyrite|Antonyms: N/A|noun

embolite:/ˈɛmbəˌlaɪt/| n.|氯溴银矿|A naturally occurring mineral form of silver halide, specifically a solid solution of silver chloride (AgCl) and silver bromide (AgBr).|The embolite specimen displayed a greenish-gray hue. 展出的氯溴银矿标本呈绿灰色。|Synonyms:  silver chlorobromide|Antonyms: N/A|noun
