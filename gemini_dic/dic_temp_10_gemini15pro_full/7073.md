anencephalous:/ˌænənˈsɛfələs/ | adj. | 无脑的；无脑畸形的 | Born without a brain, or with a very underdeveloped brain.  A serious birth defect in which a baby is born without parts of the brain and skull. |  The anencephalic infant tragically died shortly after birth.  这个无脑婴儿出生后不久就悲惨地夭折了。 | Synonyms: brainless | Antonyms: normal, healthy | adjective
