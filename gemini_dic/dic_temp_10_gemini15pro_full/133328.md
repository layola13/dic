nonexpressionistic:/ˌnɒnɪkˌspreʃəˈnɪstɪk/
adjective
非表现主义的
Not characteristic of or related to expressionism, an artistic style that emphasizes the artist's subjective feelings and emotions rather than objective reality.
Her nonexpressionistic paintings focused on realistic portrayal of everyday objects. 她的非表现主义绘画侧重于对日常物品的逼真描绘。
His nonexpressionistic style contrasted sharply with the work of his contemporaries. 他的非表现主义风格与同时代的作品形成鲜明对比。
近义词:realistic, objective, representational
反义词:expressionistic
词性:adjective
