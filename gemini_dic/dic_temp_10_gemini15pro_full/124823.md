mucked:/mʌkt/ | v. | 弄脏，搞砸 | to make something dirty or messy, or to spoil something |  I mucked up the recipe and the cake was a disaster. 我把食谱搞砸了，蛋糕做成了灾难。| synonyms: messed up, ruined, botched | antonyms: cleaned, fixed, improved | verb

mucked:/mʌkt/ | adj. | 脏的，混乱的 | (informal) dirty or messy |  My boots were mucked after walking through the mud. 我的靴子在泥里走过后很脏。| synonyms: dirty, messy, filthy | antonyms: clean, tidy, spotless | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mukken*, from Old Norse *myki* (dung, manure).
