cloakedly:/ˈkloʊkɪdli/
adverb
秘密地，隐秘地
in a cloaked manner; secretly, concealed.
He cloakedly slipped the note into her hand. 他偷偷地把纸条塞到她手里。
Synonyms: secretly, covertly, stealthily, surreptitiously
Antonyms: openly, publicly, visibly
词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From cloak + -ed + -ly.  "Cloak" comes from Old French "cloke," ultimately from Late Latin "clocca" (bell, cloak).  The "-ed" suffix forms the past participle (and sometimes adjective form) of verbs.  The "-ly" suffix turns adjectives into adverbs.
