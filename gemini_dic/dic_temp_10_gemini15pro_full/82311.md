grouser:/ˈɡraʊzər/| n.|抱怨者；履带板；防滑装置|A person who complains habitually; a metal cleat attached to the track of a vehicle such as a bulldozer or tank to improve its traction on soft ground. |The construction workers attached grousers to the bulldozer's tracks to prevent it from slipping in the mud. 建筑工人在推土机的履带上安装了防滑装置，以防止它在泥泞中打滑。|Synonyms: complainer, grumbler, whiner, cleat, tread|Antonyms: optimist, enthusiast, Pollyanna|noun

grouser:/ˈɡraʊzər/| v.|抱怨；发牢骚|To complain or grumble.|He's always grousing about the weather. 他总是抱怨天气。|Synonyms: complain, grumble, whine, moan, gripe|Antonyms: praise, commend, applaud|verb
