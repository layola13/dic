oversparingnesses: /ˌoʊvərˈspɛərɪŋnəsɪz/ | n. | 过度节俭，极度吝啬 |  The state of being excessively frugal or stingy; extreme parsimony. |  His oversparingnesses bordered on miserliness, refusing to spend even on necessities. 他的过度节俭近乎吝啬，甚至连必需品也不愿花钱。 |  frugality, parsimony, stinginess, miserliness | extravagance, generosity, lavishness | noun

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above") + "sparing" (from Old English spær "sparing, frugal") + "-ness" (Old English -nes(s), a suffix forming nouns expressing state, quality, or condition)
