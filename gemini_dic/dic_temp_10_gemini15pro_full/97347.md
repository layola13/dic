infotainments: /ˌɪnfəˈteɪnmənts/
| n. |信息娱乐|A blend of "information" and "entertainment," referring to systems that provide both information and entertainment, typically in vehicles.|The car's infotainment system includes navigation, music streaming, and hands-free calling. 汽车的信息娱乐系统包括导航、音乐流媒体和免提通话。|Synonyms: multimedia system, in-car entertainment|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
