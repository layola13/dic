adjudicate: /əˈdʒuːdɪkeɪt/
verb
裁定；判决；宣判
To formally judge and settle a dispute or contest.
The case was adjudicated in the High Court.  这个案件在高等法院审理。

The panel of judges adjudicated the art competition. 评审团裁定了艺术比赛的结果。

近义词: judge, decide, determine, rule, settle
反义词:  defer, postpone, suspend
词性: verb

adjudicated: /əˈdʒuːdɪkeɪtɪd/
adjective
已裁定的；已判决的
Having been formally judged or settled.
The adjudicated amount was $10,000. 已判定的金额为1万美元。

The adjudicated case set a legal precedent.  已判决的案件确立了法律先例。


近义词: decided, settled, resolved, determined
反义词: pending, undecided, unresolved, disputed
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY ->
From Late Latin *adjudicare, from Latin ad- + judicare "to judge," from judex "judge."
