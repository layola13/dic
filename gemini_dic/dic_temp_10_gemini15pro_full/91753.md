hyperelegantly:/ˈhaɪpərˈɛlɪɡəntli/
adverb
极其优雅地；非常优美地
in a way that is extremely elegant.
She was hyperelegantly dressed in a flowing silk gown. 她身穿飘逸的丝绸长袍，极其优雅。
Hyperelegantly appointed rooms awaited the guests.  极其优雅的房间等待着客人们。
近义词: exquisitely, supremely elegantly
反义词: inelegantly, clumsily
词性: adverb

词源:
"hyper-" (Greek: ὑπέρ, meaning "over, beyond") + "elegant" (from Latin: elegans, meaning "choice, tasteful, fine") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix)
