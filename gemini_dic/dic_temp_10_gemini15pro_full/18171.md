beknow:/bɪˈnoʊ/
verb
承认；公开宣布
to admit or acknowledge something publicly
The king beknowed his illegitimate son. 国王承认了他的私生子。
He beknowed his guilt. 他承认了自己的罪行。
acknowledge, admit, confess
deny, repudiate
