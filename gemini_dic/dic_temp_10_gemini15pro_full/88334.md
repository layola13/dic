hippocratic:/ˌhɪpəˈkrætɪk/| adj.|希波克拉底的；医的；有关医学的|Of or relating to Hippocrates, a Greek physician of the 5th century BC, or to the school of medicine that he founded, especially in its emphasis on careful observation and clinical practice.  Also, relating to the oath taken by physicians to observe medical ethics.|The Hippocratic Oath is still taken by medical graduates today. 希波克拉底誓言至今仍被医学毕业生宣誓。|近义词：medical, clinical|反义词: None|词性: adjective
