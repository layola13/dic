oaks:/oʊks/| n.|橡树，橡木|A large tree that bears acorns and has strong wood.  Many species exist worldwide.|例句：The oaks in the park are hundreds of years old. 公园里的橡树有数百年的历史了。

例句：The carpenter used oak to build the table. 木匠用橡木做了桌子。|近义词：tree, hardwood|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 softwood 作为木材方面的反义)|词性:名词

词源:From Old English *āc, from Proto-Germanic *aikō, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂eyǵ-. Cognate with Dutch eik, German Eiche, Latin aesculus.
