aubade:/oʊˈbɑːd/ | n. | 晨歌；黎明曲 | A song or poem greeting the dawn. |  She wrote an aubade for her lover, filled with the sounds of birdsong. 她为她的爱人写了一首晨歌，充满了鸟鸣的声音。

| Example: The poet's aubade captured the beauty of the sunrise perfectly. 这位诗人的晨歌完美地捕捉到了日出的美景。 | Synonym:  matin, morning song, dawn song | Antonym: serenade, nocturn | noun
