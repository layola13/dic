bulges:/ˈbʌldʒɪz/| n. v.| 膨胀，凸起；暴涨，激增(复数)|Plural form of "bulge".  Rounded swellings or protuberances that distort a flat or even surface.  To swell or protrude outwards (plural form).|例句：His pockets bulged with candy. 他的口袋里塞满了糖果。|近义词：swells, protrusions, expansions, distensions|反义词:contractions, shrinkages, flattenings, recessions|词性:名词,动词

词源: From Old French *bouge (Modern French bouge 'small room, hovel'), perhaps from a Germanic source related to English "belly".
