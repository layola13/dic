exigendary:/ɛksɪˈdʒɛndəri/ | adj. | 要求高的，苛刻的 |  Relating to or containing demands; requiring immediate attention or action.  | The professor's exigendary grading policy made the course notoriously difficult. 教授苛刻的评分政策使这门课程出了名的难。 | The company's exigendary demands for increased productivity led to employee burnout. 公司对提高生产力的苛刻要求导致员工倦怠。 |近义词：demanding, exacting, stringent, rigorous |反义词：lenient, undemanding, lax |词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *exigendus*, the future passive participle of exigō (“I demand, require, exact, enforce”), from ex (“out”) + agō (“I drive, lead, act”).
