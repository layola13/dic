lifeward:/ˈlaɪfwərd/| adj.|朝向生命的，朝向生长的|Directed or tending towards life or growth; giving or preserving life.|The lifeward slope of the mountain received more sunlight, fostering a vibrant ecosystem.  山坡朝阳的一面阳光更多，从而形成了一个充满活力的生态系统。|近义词：life-giving, life-sustaining, vital|反义词：deathward, deadly, lethal|词性:形容词

词源: life + -ward.  "-ward" 是一个古英语后缀，表示“方向”或“朝向”。
