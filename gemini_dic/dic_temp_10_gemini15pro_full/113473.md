lyrebird:/ˈlaɪəbɜːd/
|n.|琴鸟|A bird of the family Menuridae, found in eastern Australia, the male of which has a long, elaborate tail and is noted for its remarkable ability to mimic sounds.|The male lyrebird uses its tail in courtship displays. 雄琴鸟在求偶炫耀时会使用它的尾巴。|近义词:mimic, songbird|反义词:|词性:名词
