abhor:/əbˈhɔːr/ /æbˈhɔːr/| vt.|憎恶，厌恶，痛恨|regard with disgust and hatred.|She abhors cruelty to animals. 她厌恶虐待动物。

I abhor the smell of cigarettes. 我讨厌香烟的味道。|近义词：detest, loathe, despise, hate|反义词：love, adore, admire|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French abhorrer, from Latin abhorrēre “to shrink back from, shudder at,” from ab- “away from” + horrēre “to bristle, shudder.”

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He abhors violence. = 他憎恶暴力。
She abhors any form of discrimination. = 她厌恶任何形式的歧视。


一般过去时:
They abhorred the idea of leaving their home. = 他们痛恨离开家的想法。
He abhorred the taste of the medicine. = 他讨厌这药的味道。


现在完成时:
She has always abhorred dishonesty. = 她一直痛恨不诚实。
I have abhorred spiders since I was a child. = 我从小就讨厌蜘蛛。


过去完成时:
He had abhorred the thought of war ever since he was a young boy. = 他从小就痛恨战争的想法。
They had abhorred the dictator's policies for years. = 他们多年来一直憎恶独裁者的政策。


将来时:
I will always abhor betrayal. = 我将永远憎恶背叛。
She will abhor having to work with him. = 她会讨厌不得不和他一起工作。


虚拟语气:
If I were to abhor anything, it would be injustice. = 如果我要憎恶什么东西，那就是不公正。
I wish I didn't abhor public speaking so much. = 我希望我没有那么讨厌公开演讲。


被动语态:
Such behavior is abhorred by all decent people. = 所有正派的人都憎恶这种行为。
His actions were abhorred by the community. = 他的行为受到了社区的憎恶。


疑问句:
Do you abhor the smell of durian? = 你讨厌榴莲的味道吗？
Why does she abhor him so much? = 她为什么这么讨厌他？


否定句:
I don't abhor all insects. = 我并不讨厌所有昆虫。
She doesn't abhor him, she just dislikes him. = 她不讨厌他，只是不喜欢他。



PHRASE->
abhor the thought of = 痛恨…的想法
abhor the sight of = 痛恨看到…
abhor the very idea of = 痛恨…的念头
utterly abhor = 完全憎恶
deeply abhor = 深深憎恶
