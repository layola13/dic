electroactive:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈæktɪv/| adj.|电活性的，电化学活性的| Exhibiting electrochemical activity; capable of participating in electrochemical reactions.|Electroactive polymers can change their volume or shape in response to electrical stimulation. 电活性聚合物可以响应电刺激而改变其体积或形状.|Synonyms: redox-active, electroresponsive|Antonyms: electroinactive, inert|adjective
