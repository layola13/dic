indifferentism:/ɪnˈdɪfərəntɪzəm/| n. |冷漠主义；无差别主义|The state of being indifferent, especially in matters of religion or morality.  Apathy toward or lack of interest in important matters, especially those of religion or morality.|He was accused of indifferentism because he didn't seem to care which political party won the election.|近义词：apathy, unconcern, indifference, nonchalance|反义词：zeal, fervor, passion, enthusiasm|noun
