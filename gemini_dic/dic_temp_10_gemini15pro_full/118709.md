metacrolein:/ˈmetəˈkroʊliɪn/ | n. | 甲基丙烯醛 | An unsaturated aldehyde, CH2=C(CH3)CHO, obtained by the dehydration of glycerol or by the oxidation of isobutylene.  It is a highly reactive and flammable liquid with a pungent odor.  | Metacrolein is an intermediate in the production of methacrylic acid. 甲基丙烯醛是生产甲基丙烯酸的中间体。 | Metacrolein can cause irritation to the eyes, skin, and respiratory tract. 甲基丙烯醛会刺激眼睛、皮肤和呼吸道。 |Synonyms: methacrolein| Antonyms: N/A | noun
