cogitate:/ˈkɒdʒɪteɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːdʒɪteɪt/ (US)| v. |思考，思索，沉思|To think deeply and carefully about something.|She sat alone, cogitating on the problem. 她独自坐着，思考着这个问题。|例句：He cogitated over the problem for several days before reaching a solution. 他对这个问题思考了几天才找到解决办法。|近义词：ponder, contemplate, meditate, ruminate, deliberate, muse|反义词：ignore, neglect, disregard|词性:动词
Etymology: From Latin cogitatus, past participle of cogitare "to think," frequentative of coagitare "to consider, turn over in the mind," from co- "together" + agitare "to drive, discuss, consider."
