innutritious:/ˌɪnjuˈtrɪʃəs/ | adj. | 缺乏营养的，不健康的 | Not providing the body with the nutrients it needs for good health.  Lacking in nutritive value. | Example 1:  The child's diet was innutritious, consisting mainly of sugary drinks and processed snacks.  这孩子的饮食缺乏营养，主要由含糖饮料和加工零食组成。

Example 2:  Eating innutritious food can lead to health problems.  吃缺乏营养的食物会导致健康问题。 | Synonym: unhealthy, unwholesome, nutrient-deficient, poor | Antonym: nutritious, healthy, wholesome, nourishing | word property: adjective
