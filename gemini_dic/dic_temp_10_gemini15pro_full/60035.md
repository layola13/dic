educatedly:/ˈɛdʒʊkeɪtɪdli/
adverb|有教养地；受过教育地|In a manner that reflects education and knowledge.  Showing signs of having been well educated.|She spoke educatedly about the complex political situation. 她用有教养的方式谈论了复杂的政治局势。|近义词：intelligently, knowledgeably, wisely|反义词：ignorantly, foolishly|词性: adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From educated + -ly.  "Educated" comes from the Latin "educatus," the past participle of "educare," meaning "to bring up, to rear, to educate."  The suffix "-ly" is a common adverbial ending.
