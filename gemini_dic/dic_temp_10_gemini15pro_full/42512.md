counterinterpretations:/ˌkaʊntərɪnˌtɜːrprɪˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 反释; 对抗性解释 | Explanations that oppose or challenge existing interpretations.  It involves offering a different understanding or meaning, often in contrast to a dominant or accepted perspective. | Historians offer various counterinterpretations of the Civil War's causes. 历史学家对内战的起因提出了各种不同的解释。

The defense presented counterinterpretations of the forensic evidence. 辩方对法医证据提出了反驳性的解释。 | 近义词: alternative interpretations, opposing interpretations, reinterpretations | 反义词: standard interpretations, accepted interpretations | 词性: noun
