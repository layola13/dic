n.
增厚，粗化;扩大;总括
The process of making something grosser or larger.  This can refer to a physical thickening or enlargement, or a more figurative sense of making something coarser or less refined.
The grossification of the artist's style over time is evident in his later works.  这位艺术家风格随着时间的推移而粗犷化，这在他的后期作品中很明显。

refinement, thinning
