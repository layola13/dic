chlorometer:/ˌklɔːˈrɒmɪtər/ | n. | 氯量计；余氯测定仪 | An instrument used to measure the amount of chlorine, especially free chlorine, in a substance, particularly water. | 例句：The chlorometer indicated a safe level of chlorine in the pool water.  泳池水中的氯含量计显示氯含量安全。

例句：The water treatment plant uses a chlorometer to monitor chlorine levels. 水处理厂使用氯量计来监测氯含量。| 近义词：chlorimeter | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
