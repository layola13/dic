onion-like:/ˈʌnjənˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 像洋葱的；多层的；层层叠叠的 | Resembling an onion in structure, appearance, or having multiple layers. | The bacteria formed onion-like concentric rings in the petri dish. 细菌在培养皿中形成了像洋葱一样的同心圆环。| The software has an onion-like security system, with multiple layers of protection.  这款软件有一个像洋葱一样的安全系统，有多层保护。 | layered, concentric, multi-layered, stratified | monolithic, simple, uniform | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "onion" + "-like".  "Onion" comes from Old French " oignon," ultimately from Latin "unio" meaning "single, unique" (referring to the single bulb).  "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
