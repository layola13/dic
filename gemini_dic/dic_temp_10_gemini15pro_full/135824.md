nonpronunciations:/ˌnɑːnprəˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃənz/ | n. | 非发音；不发音的字母（或字母组合） | The word "knight" contains several nonpronunciations. |  This dictionary marks nonpronunciations in red. 这本词典用红色标出不发音的字母。| silent letters, unpronounced sounds | pronounced sounds | noun


Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "pronunciation" (the act or manner of pronouncing something). "Pronunciation" comes from Late Middle English: from late Latin pronunciatio(n-), from pronunciare ‘proclaim’, from pro- ‘forth’ + nuntiare ‘announce’.
