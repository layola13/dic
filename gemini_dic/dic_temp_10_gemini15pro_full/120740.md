verb
错误分析的，分析错误的
incorrectly analyzed; subjected to faulty analysis
The data was misanalyzed, leading to incorrect conclusions.  数据被错误地分析了，导致了不正确的结论。
The scientist misanalyzed the results of the experiment. 科学家错误地分析了实验结果。

Synonym: misinterpreted, misconstrued, misjudged
Antonym: correctly analyzed, accurately interpreted
