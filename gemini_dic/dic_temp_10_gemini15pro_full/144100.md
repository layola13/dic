outscent:/ˌaʊtˈsent/ | v. |胜过…的香味；发出比…更浓的香味| To surpass in scent; to emit a stronger scent than. | The roses outscent the lilies in the garden.  花园里的玫瑰花比百合花更香。| 近义词: outperfume, overpower (in scent) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider "be outscent by") | 词性: 动词

词源:  From 'out-' (meaning surpassing) + 'scent' (meaning smell).
