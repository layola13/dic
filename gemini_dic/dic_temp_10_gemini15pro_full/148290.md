oversocializing: /ˌoʊvərˈsoʊʃəlaɪzɪŋ/
|n.|过度社交|The act of socializing excessively, often to the point of exhaustion or neglecting other important aspects of life.|She admitted to oversocializing as a way to avoid dealing with her personal problems. 她承认过度社交是为了逃避处理个人问题。|Synonyms: overdoing it (socially), socializing too much|Antonyms: isolating, withdrawing, being a recluse|Noun

oversocialized: /ˌoʊvərˈsoʊʃəlaɪzd/
|adj.|过度社交化的|Having socialized excessively, often leading to feelings of exhaustion or burnout.|He felt oversocialized after attending three parties in one weekend.  参加了周末的三场派对后，他感觉过度社交了。|Synonyms: burnt out (socially), socially exhausted|Antonyms: undersocialized, withdrawn|Adjective
