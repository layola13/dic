dux:/dʌks/ | n. |领袖，首领，第一名|A leader; a head; the top student in a class. |例句：He was the dux of his class. 他是班上的第一名。

例句：The dux of the school gave a speech at graduation. 学校的第一名在毕业典礼上发表了演讲。|近义词：leader, chief, head, top student, valedictorian |反义词：follower, last place |词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dux* (leader, guide, commander), from *ducere* (to lead).  Cognate with duke.
