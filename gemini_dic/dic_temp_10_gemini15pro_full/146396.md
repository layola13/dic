overfluent:/ˌoʊvərˈfluːənt/| adj.| 过于流利的，过分流畅的|Characterized by excessive fluency of speech, often to the point of being unnatural or difficult to understand.  Speaking too quickly and smoothly, sometimes without pausing for breath or thought.|He spoke with an almost overfluent rapidity, making it hard to follow his train of thought. 他说话速度快得令人难以置信，让人很难跟上他的思路。|The presentation was overfluent and lacked substance.  这个演讲过于流利，缺乏实质内容。|近义词: glib, voluble, verbose|反义词: hesitant, halting, inarticulate|词性: adjective
