antimaterialistic:/ˌænti məˌtɪəriəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
反物质主义的; 非物质主义的
Opposed to or rejecting materialism.  Characterized by a lack of emphasis on material possessions.
She lives an antimaterialistic lifestyle, preferring experiences over possessions. 她过着一种反物质主义的生活方式，更注重体验而不是物质财富。
He made an antimaterialistic choice to work for a non-profit. 他做了一个反物质主义的选择，去为非营利组织工作。
近义词:non-materialistic, spiritual, ascetic
反义词:materialistic, consumerist, acquisitive
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix): against, opposing  (from Greek anti-)
materialistic (adjective): excessively concerned with material possessions; relating to materialism (from materia "matter")
