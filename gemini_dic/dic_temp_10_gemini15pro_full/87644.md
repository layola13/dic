hexabasic:/ˌhɛksəˈbeɪsɪk/
| adj. |六元的，六碱基的| Containing six replaceable hydrogen atoms or their equivalent.  A hexabasic acid has six hydrogen atoms that can be replaced by basic atoms or radicals.|Phosphoric acid is a tribasic acid, whereas ethylenediaminetetraacetic acid is a tetrabasic acid and benzenehexacarboxylic acid is a hexabasic acid.|近义词: sexbasic|反义词: monobasic, dibasic|词性: adjective
