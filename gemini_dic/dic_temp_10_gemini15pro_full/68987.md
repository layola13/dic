fatiguingly:/fəˈtiːɡɪŋli/
adverb|令人疲倦地，使人疲劳地|in a way that makes you feel very tired|The journey was fatiguingly long. 这趟旅程累人地漫长。|近义词:exhaustingly, tiringly|反义词:refreshingly, invigoratingly|词性:副词
Etymology: From fatigue + -ing + -ly.  Fatigue comes from the French word "fatigue," which itself is derived from the Latin word "fatigare," meaning "to tire out."
