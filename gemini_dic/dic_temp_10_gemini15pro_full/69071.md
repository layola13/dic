faultlessness:/ˈfɔːltləsnəs/
noun
完美无瑕；无过失
The state or quality of being without fault or defect.
Her faultlessness in playing the piano was remarkable. 她弹钢琴的完美无瑕令人惊叹。
Synonyms: impeccability, flawlessness, perfection, immaculateness
Antonyms: imperfection, defectiveness, flawedness
