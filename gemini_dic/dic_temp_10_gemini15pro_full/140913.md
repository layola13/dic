oncograph:/ˈɒŋkəɡrɑːf/ /ˈɑːŋkəɡræf/
noun
肿瘤描记器
An instrument for recording variations in the dimensions of a part of the body, especially a tumor, during respiration.
The oncograph provided valuable data on the tumor's growth rate. 肿瘤描记器提供了关于肿瘤生长速度的宝贵数据。
synonyms: tumor recorder, oncometer
antonyms: N/A
