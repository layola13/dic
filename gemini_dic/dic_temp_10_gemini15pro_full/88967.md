holidayers:/ˈhɒlədeɪəz (UK), ˈhɑːlədeɪərz (US)| n.|度假者，休假者|People who are on holiday.|例句：The holidayers crowded the beaches. 度假者挤满了海滩。|例句：Many holidayers choose to stay in all-inclusive resorts. 许多度假者选择住在全包度假村。|近义词：vacationers, tourists, travelers|反义词:residents, locals|词性:noun

词源: From "holiday" + "-er" (a suffix forming nouns denoting persons or things that perform the action of the verb, or are associated with or characterized by the basic noun). "Holiday" comes from the Old English "hāligdæg" (holy day).
