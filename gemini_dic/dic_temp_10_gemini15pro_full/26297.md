butyric:/bjuːˈtɪrɪk/
adjective
丁酸的；酪酸的
Relating to or containing butyric acid.
Butyric acid is a carboxylic acid. 丁酸是一种羧酸。
Butyric acid has an unpleasant smell. 丁酸有一种难闻的气味。
近义词: butanoic
反义词: (No common direct antonym, but perhaps "non-butyric" could be used in a specific context)
词性: adjective
