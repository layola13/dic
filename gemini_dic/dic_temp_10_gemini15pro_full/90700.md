humectant:/hjuːˈmɛktənt/
n.|保湿剂，润湿剂|A substance that absorbs or helps another substance retain moisture.|This lotion contains several humectants to keep your skin hydrated. 这款乳液含有多种保湿剂，可保持皮肤水分。|
synonyms: moisturizer, emollient
antonyms: desiccant, drying agent
词性:noun
