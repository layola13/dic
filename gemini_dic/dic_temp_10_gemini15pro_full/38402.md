conceptualizes:/kənˈsɛptʃuəˌlaɪzɪz/
v.
概念化；使形成概念
forms a concept or idea of something.
The author conceptualizes the history of the region as a series of power struggles.  作者将该地区的历史概念化为一系列权力斗争。
conceptualize, conceive, envision, imagine
n/a
