motordom:/ˈməʊtərdəm/| n. |汽车统治；汽车狂热|A prevalence or dominance of motor vehicles; an obsession with motor vehicles.  It refers to a society or culture heavily influenced by cars, often to the detriment of other forms of transportation or the environment.|The vast parking lots and multi-lane highways are a testament to the city's motordom.|近义词: car culture, automania|反义词: pedestrianism, cycling culture|词性: noun

Etymology:  "Motor" from Latin "motor" (mover) + "dom" from Old English "-dōm" (state, condition, domain).
