micrometer: /maɪˈkrɒmɪtər/ (UK) /maɪˈkrɑːmɪtər/ (US)| n.|千分尺，测微计|An instrument for measuring small distances, objects, or angles with precision, often having a calibrated screw. |例句：The machinist used a micrometer to ensure the part was the correct size.  机械师用千分尺确保零件尺寸正确。

例句：The scientist measured the thickness of the sample with a micrometer. 科学家用千分尺测量样品的厚度。|近义词：caliper, gauge|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


micrometer: /ˈmaɪkroʊˌmiːtər/ (US)| n.|微米|One millionth of a meter.|例句：Bacteria are typically a few micrometers in length.  细菌通常有几微米长。

例句：The diameter of the human hair is about 75 micrometers.  人类头发的直径约为75微米。|近义词：micron|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
