internalness:/ɪnˈtɜːnlnəs/ /ɪnˈtɜːrnlənəs/| n. |内在性，内在化|The quality or state of being internal; inwardness; subjectivity.  The process or result of internalizing something, such as values or beliefs.|He struggled with a sense of internalness, finding it difficult to express his emotions. 他难以表达自己的情感，内心充满挣扎。|近义词：inwardness, subjectivity, internality|反义词：externality, outwardness|词性:名词

词源:
From internal + -ness.  "Internal" comes from Late Latin "internus" meaning "inward, inner, internal," from "inter" meaning "within, between, among."
