overthronged:/ˌoʊvərˈθrɒŋd/ | adj. | 过于拥挤的；挤满的 | Filled to excess; overcrowded. | The market was overthronged with shoppers during the holiday season.  假日季节，市场里挤满了购物者。| Synonyms: overcrowded, packed, jammed, congested | Antonyms: empty, deserted, sparse | Adjective

overthrong:/ˌoʊvərˈθrɒŋ/ | verb |  过度拥挤；挤满 | To fill to excess; overcrowd. | Tourists overthronged the small island during the summer months.  夏季的几个月里，游客挤满了这个小岛。| Synonyms: overcrowd, pack, jam, congest | Antonyms: empty, desert, thin out | Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + throng. Over- means "excessively," and throng means "crowd."  So, overthronged literally means "excessively crowded."
