noncoalescing: /ˌnɒnkəʊəˈlɛsɪŋ/ | adj. | 不聚结的，不合并的 | Not coming together or merging into a single unit or mass. |  The noncoalescing droplets of oil formed a slick on the water's surface.  油的不聚结的液滴在水面上形成了一层浮油。 | 近义词：nonmerging, separate, discrete | 反义词：coalescing, merging, uniting | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From "non-" (not) + "coalesce" (from Latin "coalescere," meaning "to grow together") + "-ing" (present participle suffix). 词源：来自"non-"（不）+"coalesce"（来自拉丁语"coalescere"，意思是“一起生长”）+ "-ing"（现在分词后缀）。
