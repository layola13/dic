fixability:/ˌfɪksəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可修复性，可固定性 | The fixability of the problem depends on the availability of spare parts.  这个问题的可修复性取决于备件的可用性。| Example: The software's fixability is a key factor in its long-term success.  该软件的可修复性是其长期成功的关键因素。 | Synonym: repairability, mendability | Antonym: irreparability, unfixability | noun

fixability:/ˌfɪksəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可解决性 | The fixability of the issue depends on several factors. 这个问题的可解决性取决于几个因素。 | Example:  The committee is assessing the fixability of the current economic crisis. 委员会正在评估当前经济危机的可解决性。 | Synonym: solvability, resolvability | Antonym: insolvability, intractability | noun
