hyperawareness:/ˌhaɪpərəˈwɛrnɪs/| n.|过度警觉，过度意识|An abnormally heightened state of awareness, often accompanied by anxiety and a feeling of being overwhelmed by sensory input.|例句：Her hyperawareness made her jump at every little sound. 她的过度警觉使她一听到任何细小的声音就跳起来。

例句：Living with PTSD, he experienced constant hyperawareness of his surroundings. 患有创伤后应激障碍的他，对周围环境一直保持着高度警觉。|近义词：hypersensitivity, oversensitivity|反义词: unawareness, obliviousness|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- + awareness.  "Hyper-" is a prefix derived from Greek meaning "over, above, excessive." "Awareness" comes from Old English -war  meaning "attentive, watchful, cautious."
