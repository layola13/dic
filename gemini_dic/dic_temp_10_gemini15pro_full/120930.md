miscegenation:/ˌmɪsɪdʒɪˈneɪʃən/ /ˌmɪsəˌdʒəˈneɪʃən/
| n. |种族混合，混血|The mixing of different racial groups through marriage, cohabitation, sexual relations, or procreation.|例句：Laws against miscegenation were common in the United States until the mid-20th century.  美国在20世纪中期以前普遍存在反对种族通婚的法律。

例句：The novel explores the complexities of miscegenation in a post-colonial society. 这部小说探讨了后殖民社会中种族融合的复杂性。
|近义词：intermarriage, interracial marriage, racial mixing|反义词:racial segregation, racial purity|noun
