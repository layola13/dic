airdropped:/ˈɛərdɹɒpt/ (UK) /ˈɛrdɹɑːpt/ (US)| v. |空投；空降；通过空投发送（尤指加密货币）| To deliver or send something, especially cryptocurrency, by airdrop.  It also refers to the act of transferring files wirelessly between devices, like from a phone to a computer. | The humanitarian aid was airdropped to the refugees. 人道主义援助被空投给了难民。| synonyms: parachuted, delivered, transmitted, transferred | antonyms: received, collected, uploaded | verb (past tense)
