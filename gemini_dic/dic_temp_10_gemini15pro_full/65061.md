eternalizes:/ɪˈtɜːrnəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使永恒；使不朽
Makes something eternal or everlasting; immortalizes.
The artist eternalizes the fleeting beauty of nature in his paintings. 艺术家在他的绘画中使稍纵即逝的自然美永恒。
immortalizes, perpetuates, enshrines
annihilates, destroys, obliterates
词性: 动词
