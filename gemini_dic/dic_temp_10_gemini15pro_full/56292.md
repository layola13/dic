doggiest: /ˈdɒɡiɪst/ (UK) /ˈdɑːɡiɪst/ (US) | adj. | 最像狗的；最狗的 | Most like a dog; having the most characteristics of a dog, often in a negative or exaggerated way.  | That's the doggiest-looking cat I've ever seen. 那是我见过的最像狗的猫。|  He had a doggiest grin on his face. 他脸上露出了谄媚的笑容。 | 近义词: canine, dog-like | 反义词: feline, cat-like | 词性: 形容词

词源: From "dog" + "-y" (forming adjectives meaning 'having the qualities of') + "-est" (superlative suffix).
