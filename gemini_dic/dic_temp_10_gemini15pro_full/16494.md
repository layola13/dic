bargepole:/ˈbɑːdʒˌpoʊl/ (US) /ˈbɑːdʒˌpəʊl/ (UK)| n. |驳船撑杆；(比喻)绝不，绝对不|A long pole used to propel or guide a barge. Figuratively used to express strong aversion or refusal.|I wouldn't touch that with a bargepole. 我绝对不会碰那个东西。|近义词:pole, staff (literal);  not at all, no way, under no circumstances (figurative)|反义词: readily, willingly (figurative) | noun
