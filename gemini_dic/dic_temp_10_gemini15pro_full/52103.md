diamides:/ˈdaɪəˌmaɪdz/ | n. |二酰胺|Compounds containing two amide groups.  A diamide is an organic compound containing two amide functional groups (-CONH2).| Nylon, a common synthetic polymer, is a polydiamide. 尼龙，一种常见的合成聚合物，是一种聚二酰胺。|  Kevlar, known for its strength, is also a type of aromatic diamide.  凯夫拉尔，以其强度而闻名，也是一种芳香族二酰胺。 |synonyms: bisamide | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a chemical class) | noun
