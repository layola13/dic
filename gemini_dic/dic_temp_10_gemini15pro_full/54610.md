dismayful:/dɪsˈmeɪfʊl/
adjective
令人沮丧的，令人惊慌的
Causing or filled with dismay; alarming.
The news of the accident was dismayful.  事故的消息令人沮丧。
The team's dismayful performance led to their elimination from the tournament.  球队令人沮丧的表现导致他们被淘汰出局。
近义词:dismaying, alarming, distressing, disheartening, shocking
反义词:encouraging, heartening, cheering, comforting
词性:adjective

dismay:/dɪsˈmeɪ/
noun
沮丧，惊慌
A feeling of alarm and consternation.
She felt a deep sense of dismay at the news. 她听到这个消息感到非常沮丧。
The sudden drop in sales caused widespread dismay among investors. 销售额的突然下降引起了投资者们的普遍恐慌。
verb
使沮丧，使惊慌
Cause (someone) to feel consternation and distress.
The team was dismayed by the sudden injury to their star player.  明星球员的突然受伤使球队感到沮丧。
The news of the impending storm dismayed the residents. 即将到来的暴风雨的消息使居民们感到惊慌。
近义词:alarm, consternation, distress, shock
反义词:encouragement, comfort, relief
词性:noun, verb


词源:
From Old French *desmayer (Modern French démayer), from des- "away" + Vulgar Latin *exmagare "to deprive of one's might", from Latin ex- "out" + magis "more".
