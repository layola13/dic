etymon:/ˈɛtɪmən/| n. |词源；词根|A word or morpheme from which a later word is derived.|The word "etymology" has the etymon "etymon" itself, referring to the original form of a word.|Synonyms: root, origin, source, derivation|Antonyms: derivative, offspring|noun
