decalling:/diːˈkælɪŋ/
n.
贴花；移印；去花
The act of removing decals from a surface.  The process of applying decals.
The company specializes in aircraft decaling. 这家公司专门从事飞机贴花。
近义词:transfer printing, waterslide decal application
反义词:N/A
词性:noun


decalling:/diːˈkælɪŋ/
v.
贴花；移印；去花
Applying or removing decals from a surface.
He was decaling model cars. 他正在给汽车模型贴花。
近义词:applying transfers, removing stickers
反义词:N/A
词性:verb
