finance-companies:/ˈfaɪnæns ˈkʌmpəniz/ |n.|金融公司；财务公司|Companies that provide financial services, such as loans and credit.  They are distinct from banks, though some larger finance companies may offer similar services. |Example 1: Many small businesses rely on finance companies for short-term loans. 许多小企业依靠金融公司获得短期贷款。

Example 2:  The finance company offered a competitive interest rate on the car loan. 这家金融公司为汽车贷款提供了具有竞争力的利率。|Synonyms: lending institutions, credit companies, loan companies|Antonyms:  N/A (No direct antonym; perhaps "savings institutions" in a loose sense)|Noun
