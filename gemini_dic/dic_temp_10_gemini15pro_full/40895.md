cookware:/ˈkʊkwɛːr/
n.|厨具|Pots, pans, and other utensils used for cooking.|She invested in high-quality stainless steel cookware.|近义词: kitchenware, utensils|反义词: None|词性: Noun
Etymology: cook + ware. "Ware" comes from Old English "waru," meaning "articles of merchandise."  It refers to manufactured goods or products.
