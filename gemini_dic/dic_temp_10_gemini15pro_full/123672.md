moodiest:/ˈmuːdiɪst/ | adj. | 最喜怒无常的 | Superlative form of moody: most moody.  Having or expressing a lot of moods, especially unhappy or annoyed ones. | The moodiest character in the film was the misunderstood artist.  电影中最喜怒无常的角色是被误解的艺术家。|  gloomiest, sullenest, grumpiest | cheerful, happy | adjective

Etymology->
From mood + -y + -est.  "Mood" comes from Old English "mōd" meaning mind, heart, feeling.
