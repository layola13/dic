numerologist:/ˌn(j)uːməˈrɒlədʒɪst/ /ˌnuːməˈrɑːlədʒɪst/| n. | 命理学家；数字命理学家 | A person who practices numerology, the study of the purported mystical relationship between numbers and events. |  She consulted a numerologist to determine her lucky number. 她咨询了一位命理学家来确定她的幸运数字。 |  The numerologist predicted a great year for her based on her birth date. 命理学家根据她的生日预测她会有一个很棒的一年。 | 近义词: fortune teller, soothsayer | 反义词: (无直接反义词) | 词性: noun


Etymology->
From number + -ology + -ist.  Number comes from Old French nombre, ultimately from Latin numerus.  -ology signifies "study of". -ist denotes "one who practices or is skilled in".
