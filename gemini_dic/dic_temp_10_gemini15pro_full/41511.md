corollate:/ˈkɒrəleɪt/ /ˈkɔːrəleɪt/ | adj. v.|联合的，合生的；使联合，使合生|Having petals united at the base, or joined into one body; said of gamopetalous corollas.  (Botany) United; joined; connected. |The petals of the flower are corollate. 这朵花的花瓣是合生的。|近义词：united, joined, fused, connate|反义词: separate, distinct|词性:adjective, verb

corollate:/ˈkɒrəleɪt/ /ˈkɔːrəleɪt/ | v.|联合，使合生|To unite or grow together; to connect intimately. |He correlated the information from various sources. 他把来自不同来源的信息联系起来。|近义词：unite, join, fuse, combine|反义词: separate, divide|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *corollatus, past participle of *corollare, from corolla (“small crown, garland, corolla”), diminutive of corona (“crown, garland”).
