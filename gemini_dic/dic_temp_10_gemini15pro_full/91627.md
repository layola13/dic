hyperbarically:/ˌhaɪpərˈbærɪkli/ | adv. |高压地，在高压下|In a way that involves high pressure, especially higher than atmospheric pressure.  This typically refers to medical treatments or environments. |The wound was treated hyperbarically.  这个伤口在高压下进行了治疗。| The diver ascended too quickly, failing to decompress hyperbarically.  潜水员上升太快，没有进行高压减压。 | 近义词: under high pressure | 反义词: hypobarically (在低压下) | 词性: adverb

Etymology:
From hyperbaric + -ally.  Hyperbaric comes from Greek *hyper-* (over, above) + *baros* (weight, pressure).
