overassumes:/ˌōvərəˈsuːmz/
verb
过度假设，过分假设
To assume too much; to make presumptions that are not based on sufficient evidence.
She overassumes that everyone agrees with her political views. 她过分假设每个人都同意她的政治观点。
He overassumed his authority and made decisions without consulting the team. 他滥用职权，未与团队协商就做出了决定。
presume, presuppose, overestimate
underestimate, question
v.
