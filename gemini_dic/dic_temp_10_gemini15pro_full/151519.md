parsec:/ˈpɑːrˌsek/ | n. | 秒差距 | A unit of distance used in astronomy, equal to about 3.26 light years. It is the distance at which a star would have a parallax of one second of arc. |  The nearest star to the Sun, Proxima Centauri, is about 1.3 parsecs away. 比邻星，离太阳最近的恒星，大约距离1.3秒差距。

The Andromeda Galaxy is approximately 770 kiloparsecs from Earth. 仙女座星系距离地球大约770千秒差距。 | 近义词:  (none - it's a unit of measurement) | 反义词: (none - it's a unit of measurement) | 词性: noun
