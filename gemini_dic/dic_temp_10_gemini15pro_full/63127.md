enslaves:/ɪnˈsleɪvz/
verb
奴役，使成为奴隶
to cause (someone) to become a slave : to put (someone) in a state of slavery
The plantation owners enslaved thousands of Africans. 种植园主奴役了数千名非洲人。
近义词: subjugate, oppress, dominate, control
反义词: liberate, free, emancipate
词性: verb

enslaves:/ɪnˈsleɪvz/
词源: From en- + slave + -s.  The prefix "en-" means "to put into or on," and "-s" marks the third-person singular present tense form of the verb.
词性: verb
