forefoot:/ˈfɔːrfʊt/ | n. |前脚掌|The part of the foot between the toes and the arch.  The forefoot bears a significant portion of the body's weight during walking and running.|She landed awkwardly on her forefoot and sprained her ankle. 她笨拙地落在前脚掌上，扭伤了脚踝。|The dancer balanced delicately on the forefoot of her right foot.  舞者巧妙地用右脚前脚掌保持平衡。|Synonyms: ball of the foot, metatarsus|Antonyms: heel |noun
