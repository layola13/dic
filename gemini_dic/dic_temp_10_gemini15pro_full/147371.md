overneglectfully:/ˌoʊvər nɪˈɡlɛktfəli/
| adv. | 过度疏忽地，极度忽略地 |  in a way that shows excessive neglect; extremely neglectfully | He overneglectfully left his pet alone for weeks. 他极度疏忽地把他的宠物单独留了好几个星期。|  The garden was overneglectfully maintained and became overgrown. 花园被过度疏忽地维护，变得杂草丛生。 | negligently, carelessly, irresponsibly | attentively, carefully, diligently | adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
over- (prefix): from Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi (meaning "over, above, beyond")
neglect (verb/noun): from Latin neglectus, past participle of neglegere ("to disregard, neglect"), from nec ("not") + legere ("to gather, choose, read")
-fully (suffix): from Old English -ful, from Proto-Germanic *-fullaz (meaning "full of")
