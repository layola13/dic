overmobilized: /ˌoʊvərˈmoʊbəˌlaɪzd/ | adj. |过度动员的| Excessively mobilized, usually referring to military forces or resources, beyond what is necessary or sustainable.  This can lead to strain on resources, logistical problems, and potential economic instability. | The country was overmobilized for a war that never happened, leaving its economy severely weakened.  这个国家为一场从未发生的战争过度动员，导致其经济严重衰退。|  The general realized they had overmobilized troops and began sending some back to their bases. 将军意识到他们过度动员了军队，于是开始将一些部队遣送回基地。 | overextended, overprepared | undermobilized, unprepared | adjective
