mains:/meɪnz/| n.|主电源，电力网，城市煤气管道|The power outage affected the mains, leaving thousands of homes without electricity. 停电影响了主电源，导致数千户家庭断电。|Synonyms: power supply, power lines, gas lines|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider "backup generator")|noun

mains:/meɪnz/| n.|主菜|The restaurant's mains include steak, fish, and pasta. 这家餐厅的主菜包括牛排、鱼和意大利面。|Synonyms: main course, entrée|Antonyms: appetizer, starter, dessert|noun
