misclicked:/ˌmɪsˈklɪkt/ | v. |  错误点击，误点击 | To click a computer mouse unintentionally or on the wrong button/link. | I misclicked and accidentally deleted the file. 我错误点击，不小心删除了文件。| I misclicked the link and ended up on a spam website. 我点错了链接，结果到了一个垃圾网站。 | 近义词: clicked wrongly, clicked accidentally | 反义词: clicked correctly | 词性: 动词
