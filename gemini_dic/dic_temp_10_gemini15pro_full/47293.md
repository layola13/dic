de-frozen:/ˌdiːˈfrəʊzn/
adjective
解冻的
thawed, no longer frozen; having been thawed.
The de-frozen chicken was ready to be cooked. 解冻后的鸡肉可以烹饪了。
The scientist examined the de-frozen mammoth remains. 科学家检查了已解冻的猛犸象遗骸。

近义词:thawed, defrosted
反义词:frozen

词性:adjective
词源: "de-" (prefix meaning "removal or reversal") + "frozen" (past participle of "freeze")
