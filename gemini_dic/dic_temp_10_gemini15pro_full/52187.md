diarrhetic:/ˌdaɪəˈrɛtɪk/ |  n. | 泻药 | A medicine that causes diarrhea |  He took a diarrhetic to relieve his constipation. 他服用了泻药来缓解便秘。 | Synonyms: laxative, purgative, aperient | Antonyms: antidiarrheal, constipating | noun


Etymology: From Late Latin *diarrheticus, from Greek diarrhoetikos, from diarrhoia "diarrhea."
