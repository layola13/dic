clamor:/ˈklæmər/ (American)| n. v.|喧嚷，吵闹；大声要求|A loud and confused noise, especially of people shouting vehemently.  To shout loudly and insistently.|The crowd clamored for an encore.  人群喧闹着要求再来一次。|近义词：shout, yell, cry, demand, uproar, hubbub|反义词: silence, whisper, peace, quiet|词性:名词, 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
