exploitations:/ˌɛksplɔɪˈteɪʃənz/| n. |开发，利用，剥削|The act of using something or someone in a way that helps you unfairly.|The company's exploitation of its workers led to a strike. 该公司对工人的剥削导致了罢工。|Near synonyms: abuse, misuse, profiteering|Near antonyms: conservation, preservation|noun

exploit:/ɪkˈsplɔɪt/| n. vt. |功绩，勋绩；利用，剥削|A bold or daring feat; to make full use of a resource; to use a person or situation in a way that is considered unfair.|He exploited his connections to get the job. 他利用他的关系得到了这份工作.  His wartime exploits were legendary. 他的战时功绩是传奇性的。|Near synonyms: accomplishment, feat, utilize, capitalize on|Near antonyms: neglect, waste|noun, verb
