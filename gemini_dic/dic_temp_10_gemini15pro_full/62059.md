encases:/ɪnˈkeɪsɪz/| v.| 包裹，封装| to enclose or cover something completely| The jeweler encased the diamond in a velvet box. 珠宝商把钻石装在一个天鹅绒盒子里。| The protective covering encases the delicate instrument.  保护罩包裹着精密仪器。|近义词：enclose, envelop, surround, sheathe|反义词：uncover, expose, release|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From en- (meaning "in" or "into") + case (meaning "a container or covering").  源自en-（意为“在...里面”或“进入”）+ case（意为“容器或覆盖物”）。
