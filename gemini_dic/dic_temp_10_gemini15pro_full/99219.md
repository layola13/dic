intercolonial:/ˌɪntərˈkəˈloʊniəl/ | adj. |殖民地间的；跨殖民地的|Existing or occurring between colonies. |例句：Intercolonial trade boosted the economies of all involved. 殖民地间的贸易促进了所有参与方的经济。

例句：The intercolonial railway connected the eastern provinces of Canada.  殖民地间的铁路连接了加拿大东部各省。|近义词：interterritorial, interstate |反义词:intracolonial, domestic|词性:adjective
