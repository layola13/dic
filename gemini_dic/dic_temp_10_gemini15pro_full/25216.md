budgie:/ˈbʌdʒi/| n. |虎皮鹦鹉 (budgerigar的非正式说法)|A small Australian parrot (Melopsittacus undulatus), often kept as a pet, also called a budgerigar. |例句：I taught my budgie to whistle a tune. 我教我的虎皮鹦鹉吹口哨。

例句：The budgie flew around the room, chirping excitedly.  虎皮鹦鹉兴奋地在房间里飞来飞去，唧唧喳喳地叫着。|近义词：parakeet, budgerigar|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


Etymology: From Gamilaraay *budgery "good" + *gaar "cockatoo".
