logons:/ˈlɒɡɒnz/ (UK), /ˈlɑːɡɑːnz/ (US)| n. |登录，登录次数|The system recorded over 1000 logons yesterday. 系统昨天记录了超过1000次登录。|A logon is an act of accessing a computer system or network by entering a user ID and password.  It refers to the process of identifying and verifying a user's credentials to grant them access. |The server experienced a surge in logons during peak hours.  服务器在高峰时段经历了登录激增。|Near synonyms: logins, sign-ins, accesses|Antonyms: logoffs, sign-outs, disconnections|Noun
