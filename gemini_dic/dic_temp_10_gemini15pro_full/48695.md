defiantly:/dɪˈfaɪəntli/
adverb
挑战地；对抗地；坚决地
In a manner that shows open resistance or bold disobedience.
She defiantly refused to apologize. 她坚决拒绝道歉。
He defiantly crossed his arms and glared at her. 他挑衅地交叉双臂，怒视着她。

defiantly:/dɪˈfaɪəntli/
词性: 副词
中文翻译: 挑战地；对抗地；坚决地
英文解析: In a manner that shows open resistance or bold disobedience.
例句:
The little boy defiantly refused to eat his vegetables. 小男孩坚决不吃蔬菜。
Despite the warnings, she defiantly continued down the forbidden path. 尽管受到了警告，她还是执意沿着禁路走去。


近义词: rebelliously, audaciously, boldly, resolutely
反义词: obediently, submissively, meekly

ETYMOLOGY->
From defiant + -ly. Defiant from Old French defiant, present participle of defier "to defy, challenge," from Latin diffidare "to distrust, despair of, renounce," from dis- "apart, away" + fidare "to trust," from fides "faith."
