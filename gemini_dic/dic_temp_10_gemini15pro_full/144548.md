outvoyaged:/ˌaʊtˈvɔɪɪdʒd/
verb
航行得比…更远；胜过…的航行
To sail farther or longer than; to surpass in voyaging.
Magellan outvoyaged all other explorers of his time. 麦哲伦的航行超过了他那个时代的所有其他探险家。
outstrip, surpass, exceed
lag behind, fall behind
词性:动词
