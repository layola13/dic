futurologies:/ˌfjuːtʃəˈrɒlədʒiz/
n.
未来学；未来研究；对未来社会形态和趋势的预测
studies of or writings about the future, especially about what is likely to happen
例句：His latest book delves into the futurologies of artificial intelligence.  他最新的著作深入探讨了人工智能的未来。

例句：The conference explored various futurologies, from optimistic technological utopias to dystopian warnings.  这次会议探讨了各种各样的未来学，从乐观的技术乌托邦到反乌托邦的警告。

近义词：forecasts, predictions, projections, speculations
反义词：histories, past
词性: 名词
