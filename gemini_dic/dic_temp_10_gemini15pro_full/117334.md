medevac:/ˈmɛdɪvæk/ /ˈmiːdɪvæk/
n. |医疗后送，空中救护|The emergency medevac transported the injured climber to the nearest hospital.  紧急医疗后送将受伤的登山者送往最近的医院。|
vt. |用直升机等医疗后送|They medevacked the soldier out of the combat zone. 他们用直升机将这名士兵从战区后送出去。|
近义词：airlift, medical evacuation|
反义词:|None|
词性：名词, 动词|
词源：Shortening of medical evacuation|
