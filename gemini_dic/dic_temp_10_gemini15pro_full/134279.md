noninfringement:/ˌnɒnɪnˈfrɪndʒmənt/| n.|不侵权，未侵权|The state of not violating or infringing upon someone else's intellectual property rights, such as patents, copyrights, or trademarks.| The company ensured noninfringement by conducting a thorough patent search.  该公司通过进行彻底的专利检索来确保不侵权。|Synonyms: non-violation, observance, compliance|Antonyms: infringement, violation, breach|noun
