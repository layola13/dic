|n.|宣福礼，荣列真福品；册封真福礼|The act or ceremony of beatifying someone, especially by the Roman Catholic Church. ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *beatificatio*, from Latin beatificare "to make happy," from beatus "blessed" + -ficare, combining form of facere "to make."
