bow-wow:/ˈbaʊ waʊ/
noun
狗叫声
The sound a dog makes.
The puppy made a small bow-wow. 小狗轻轻地叫了一声。
bark, woof, yelp
meow, purr
n.


bow-wow:/ˈbaʊ waʊ/
interjection
汪汪
Used to represent the sound of a dog barking.
"Bow-wow," barked the dog. “汪汪，”狗叫道。
bark, woof, arf
meow, purr
int.


ETYMOLOGY->

From imitative origin.  The word directly mimics the sound of a dog barking.
