holohedral:/ˌhɒləʊˈhiːdrəl/ | adj. |全对称的；全面的| Exhibiting all the faces required by the full symmetry of the crystal system.  A crystal possessing all the faces required by its symmetry. | The garnet crystal exhibited holohedral symmetry, displaying all the possible faces for its cubic structure.| 近义词:  isotropic (in some contexts)| 反义词: merohedral |词性: adjective
