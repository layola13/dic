outstole:/ˌaʊtˈstoʊl/
verb
超过，胜过（在偷窃方面）
surpassed in stealing; stole more than.  This word is archaic and rarely used.
He outstole his rival thief and became the most notorious pickpocket in the city. 他在偷窃方面胜过他的对手，成为城里最臭名昭著的扒手。
近义词: outdid, surpassed, excelled
反义词:  was surpassed by, was outdone by
词性: verb
