cirrhous:/ˈsɪrəs/| adj.|卷云状的，触须状的，有卷须的|Characterized by thin, wispy filaments or tendrils, like a cirrus cloud.  Relating to or resembling a cirrus cloud, often used to describe the appearance of a tumor or growth.|The doctor noted the cirrhous appearance of the cancerous growth. 医生注意到癌性增生的卷云状外观。|Synonyms: wispy, filamentous, tendrilous, feathery|Antonyms: dense, compact, solid|adjective
