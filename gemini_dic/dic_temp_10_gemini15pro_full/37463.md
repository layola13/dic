commissional: /kəˈmɪʃənəl/
adjective
佣金的; 由佣金支付的; 与佣金有关的
Relating to, or paid by commission.
He receives a commissional payment for each sale. 他每销售一件商品就得到一笔佣金。
近义词: commissioned, commission-based
反义词: salary-based, fixed-income
词性: adjective
