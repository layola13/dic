excursion:/ɪkˈskɜːrʒn/ /ɪkˈskɜːrʃn/
noun
短途旅行，远足
A short journey or trip, especially one engaged in for pleasure or education.
例句：We took a day excursion to the beach. 我们去海滩一日游。

例句：The school organized an excursion to the museum. 学校组织了一次去博物馆的参观。

近义词：trip, journey, outing, expedition, tour
反义词：stay, homebody

词性:n.
