funniness:/ˈfʌninəs/| n. |滑稽，有趣|The quality of being funny; humor.|Her stories are always full of funniness. 她的故事总是充满了趣味。|The funniness of the situation made everyone laugh.  这个场景的滑稽让每个人都笑了。|近义词：humor, comicality, amusement, hilarity|反义词：seriousness, solemnity|词性: noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From funny + -ness.  "Funny" originates from the early 18th century, meaning "strange, odd, peculiar," possibly influenced by "fun."  The suffix "-ness" is used to form nouns expressing a state or quality.
