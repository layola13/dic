lipase:/ˈlaɪpeɪs/| n.|脂肪酶|An enzyme that catalyzes the hydrolysis of fats (lipids).  It breaks down fats into fatty acids and glycerol.|Pancreatic lipase is essential for the digestion of fats. 胰脂肪酶对脂肪的消化至关重要。|Lipase is used in some laundry detergents to help remove grease stains.  脂肪酶用于一些洗衣液中以帮助去除油渍。|synonym: esterase|antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps inhibitor)|noun
