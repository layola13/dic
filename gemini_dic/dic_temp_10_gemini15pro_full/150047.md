palpitant: /ˈpælpɪtənt/ | adj. | 心跳的，悸动的，兴奋的 | Beating rapidly, throbbing; exciting.  |  Her heart was palpitant with fear. 她的心因恐惧而怦怦直跳。 |  The palpitant rhythm of the music heightened the suspense.  激动人心的音乐节奏加剧了悬念。 | 近义词: throbbing, pulsating, fluttering, exciting, trembling | 反义词: calm, steady, still | 词性: 形容词
词源:  Late 16th century: from Latin palpitant- ‘throbbing’, from the verb palpitare, frequentative of palpare ‘to stroke, touch gently’.
