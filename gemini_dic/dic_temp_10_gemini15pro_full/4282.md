airway:/ˈɛə(r)weɪ/ /ˈɛrˌweɪ/| n.|呼吸道，航线|The smoke made it difficult to breathe, and his airway was constricted.  烟雾使他呼吸困难，呼吸道收缩了。|The plane was cleared for landing and entered the designated airway.  飞机获准着陆并进入指定的航线。|近义词： respiratory tract,  flight path, route|反义词: blockage (for respiratory sense)|n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From air + way.  "Air" comes from Old French "air" ultimately from Greek "aer" (air). "Way" comes from Old English "weg" (road, path).
