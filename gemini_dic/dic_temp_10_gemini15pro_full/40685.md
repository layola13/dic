conversazione:/ˌkɒnvərsætˈsjoʊneɪ/ /ˌkɑːnvərsəˈtsoʊni/
noun
社交聚会，座谈会
a social gathering for conversation, especially about literature, the arts, or science, or one at which a lecture or short addresses are given.
The countess held a conversazione at her palace. 女伯爵在她的宫殿里举行了一场社交聚会。
近义词：gathering, assembly, meeting, reception
反义词：isolation, solitude
词性：n.
