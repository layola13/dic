dismastment: /dɪsˈmɑːstmənt/ /dɪsˈmæstmənt/
n.
拆卸桅杆; 失去桅杆; 灾难; 严重损坏
The act of removing a mast from a ship or the loss of a mast.  Also, a catastrophic loss or severe damage.
The ship suffered dismastment in the storm.  这艘船在暴风雨中失去了桅杆。
The earthquake caused the dismastment of several buildings. 地震导致几座建筑物严重损毁。
近义词: destruction, ruin, wreckage
反义词: construction, repair, restoration
词性: 名词
