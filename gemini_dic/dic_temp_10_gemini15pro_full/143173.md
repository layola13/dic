outclerk: /ˌaʊtˈklɜːrk/
verb
比...办事效率更高;胜过
to be a more efficient clerk than
Example: She consistently outclerked her colleagues, processing twice as many orders. 她办事效率一直比同事高，处理的订单数量是他们的两倍。

Example: He aimed to outclerk everyone in the department and earn a promotion. 他的目标是比部门里所有人的办事效率都高，以此获得晋升。

近义词: outperform, surpass, excel
反义词: underperform


词性: verb (动词)
