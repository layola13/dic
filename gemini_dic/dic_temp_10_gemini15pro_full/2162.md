addedly:/ˈædɪdli/ | adv. | 此外，另外 | In addition; furthermore; as an additional factor.  It implies something is being added to what has already been said or considered. | He was tired, and addedly, he was very hungry. 他很累，而且也很饿。

The trip was expensive, and addedly, it was quite dangerous.  这次旅行很昂贵，而且相当危险。 | 近义词: additionally, furthermore, moreover, besides | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider using "however," "nevertheless," or "despite this") | 词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "added" (past participle of "add") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  The word emphasizes the act of adding something further to a statement or situation.
