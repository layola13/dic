darners:/ˈdɑːrnərz/
n.
缝补者；织补者；蜻蜓类
A person who darns socks, stockings, or other clothes. Also, a common name for dragonflies of the family Aeshnidae.
She's a skilled darners, always mending our socks. 她是一位熟练的缝补者，总是帮我们缝补袜子。
The darners flitted across the pond.  蜻蜓掠过池塘。
近义词：mender, repairer (for person); dragonfly (for insect)
反义词： (No direct antonym)
词性：名词

darning:/ˈdɑːrnɪŋ/
n.
缝补；织补
The act of repairing holes or worn areas in fabric by weaving thread across them.
She spent the evening doing her darning. 她晚上一直在缝补衣物。
The darning on his socks was neat and almost invisible. 他袜子上的补丁很整齐，几乎看不出来。
近义词：mending, patching, repair
反义词： (No direct antonym)
词性：名词


词源:  From Middle English *darner, from darnen + -er, equivalent to darn + -er.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *darnōną (“to mend, cover, conceal”).
