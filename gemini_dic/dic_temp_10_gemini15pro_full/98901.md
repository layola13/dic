intensifier:/ɪnˈtɛnsɪˌfaɪər/| n.|增强剂；加强词；修饰语|A word, especially an adverb or adjective, that has little meaning itself but is used to emphasize another word or phrase.|The word "very" is an intensifier in the sentence "I'm very happy."  我很高兴。“非常”这个词在句子“我很高兴”中是一个加强词。|synonyms: booster, amplifier, intensifying word|antonyms: weakener, diminisher|noun


intensify:/ɪnˈtɛnsɪˌfaɪ/| v.|加剧，加强；增强|To make or become more intense; increase, strengthen, or magnify.|The conflict intensified after the bombing. 轰炸后，冲突加剧了。|synonyms: escalate, heighten, boost, amplify, strengthen|antonyms: weaken, diminish, reduce, lessen|verb
