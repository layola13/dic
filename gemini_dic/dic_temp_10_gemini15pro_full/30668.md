ceroplastic:/ˌsɪərəˈplæstɪk/
adjective
蜡塑的；蜡制的
Relating to the art of modelling in wax; made of wax.
The museum displayed several ceroplastic anatomical models.  博物馆展出了几个蜡制的人体解剖模型。
The ceroplastic figures were incredibly lifelike.  这些蜡像栩栩如生。
Near Synonyms: waxen, wax
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps consider materials like "wooden," "clay," "plastic" as contrasting materials)
Etymology:  From Greek *kēro- 'wax' + *plastikos 'able to be molded'
Word Type: adjective
