examinees:/ɪɡˌzæmɪˈniːz/
noun
考生，应试者
People who are taking an examination.
The examinees were asked to remain silent during the test. 考生被要求在考试期间保持安静。
All examinees must arrive 30 minutes before the exam starts. 所有考生必须在考试开始前30分钟到达。
synonyms: candidates, test-takers, students
antonyms: examiners, proctors, invigilators
