depopulating:/ˌdiːˈpɒpjʊleɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
减少人口
reducing the population of a place
例句：The war is depopulating the country. 战争正在使这个国家人口减少。

例句：Disease and famine were depopulating the region. 疾病和饥荒正在使该地区人口减少。

近义词：diminishing, lessening, depleting
反义词：populating, increasing

词性:v.


词源:  From de- + populate, from Latin populus "people".
