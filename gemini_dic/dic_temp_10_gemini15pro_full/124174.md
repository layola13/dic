mosa:/ˈmoʊsə/| n.|马赛克，镶嵌图案|A picture or pattern produced by arranging together small colored pieces of hard material, such as stone, tile, or glass.|例句：The artist created a beautiful mosa using tiny pieces of colored glass. 艺术家用彩色玻璃碎片创作了一幅美丽的马赛克。|近义词：mosaic, inlay, tessellation|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "monochrome" if contrasting the variety of colors)|词性:noun
