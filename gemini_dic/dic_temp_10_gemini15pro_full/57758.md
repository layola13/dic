dreamland: /ˈdriːmlænd/ (US) /ˈdriːmlænd/ (UK)| n. | 梦境，梦幻世界 |A state of sleep or of reverie; a place or state of unrealistic or fantastic imagery or experience.  A place or state that is ideal or perfect.|  He drifted off to dreamland.  他进入了梦乡。|Synonyms:  fantasyland, never-never land, wonderland, cloud nine, utopia|Antonyms: reality, actuality|Noun
