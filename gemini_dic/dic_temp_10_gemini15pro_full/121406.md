misimagination:/ˌmɪsɪˌmædʒɪˈneɪʃən/
|n.| 错觉；误想；错误的想象 |The story was a product of his misimagination.  这个故事是他错误想象的产物。| English explanation: The act or process of imagining incorrectly; a false or mistaken idea. | Example:  His fear was based on misimagination, not reality. 他的恐惧是基于错觉，而不是现实。|Synonyms: delusion, hallucination, illusion, fantasy|Antonyms: reality, truth, fact|noun
