词源:
From Middle English *morwe, from Old English morgen (“morning”), from Proto-Germanic *murganą (“morning”), from Proto-Indo-European *merǵ- (“short; small; little”). Cognate with Scots morrow (“morning, tomorrow”), West Frisian moarn (“morning, tomorrow”), Dutch morgen (“morning, tomorrow”), German Morgen (“morning”), Danish morgen (“morning”), Swedish morgon (“morning”), Icelandic morgunn (“morning”). Related to morn.
