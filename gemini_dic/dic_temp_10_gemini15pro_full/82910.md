gumless:/ˈɡʌmlɪs/
adjective
无牙龈的；没有粘性的
lacking gums; without gum
The old dog was gumless and couldn't chew his food properly.  这只老狗没有牙龈，无法好好咀嚼食物。
The adhesive was gumless and relied on static cling.  这种粘合剂不含胶，依靠静电粘附。
Near synonyms: toothless, gummy (when referring to lacking gums, though gummy usually means having excessive gum),  adhesive-free
Antonyms: gummy (when referring to having gum), adhesive
