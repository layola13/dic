mansard:/ˈmænsɑːrd/ (US), /ˈmɑːnzɑːd/ (UK)| n. |屋顶房间，阁楼， мансардная крыша (Russian)|A roof having two slopes on all four sides, the lower slope being steeper than the upper.|The old house had a mansard roof that gave it a distinctive look.  这栋老房子有一个斜屋顶，这使它看起来与众不同。|Synonym: attic, garret, loft|Antonym: cellar, basement|noun


mansard:/ˈmænsɑːrd/ (US), /ˈmɑːnzɑːd/ (UK)| adj. | мансардный (Russian)|Of or relating to a mansard roof.|The mansard windows provided a charming view of the city.  阁楼的窗户提供了迷人的城市景观。|Synonym: sloping, pitched|Antonym: flat|adjective
