nutritional:/njuːˈtrɪʃənəl/| adj.|营养的，与营养有关的|Relating to the process of providing or obtaining the food necessary for health and growth.|A healthy diet provides all your nutritional needs. 健康的饮食能提供你所有所需的营养。|近义词：nourishing, nutritive|反义词：unhealthy, detrimental|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *nutritionālis*, from Latin *nutritiō* (“nourishment”), from *nutrire* (“to nourish”).
