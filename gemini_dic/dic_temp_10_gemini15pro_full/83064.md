gurmy: /ˈɡɜːrmi/ | adj. | 黏糊糊的；甜腻的；感伤的 | Sticky, sweet, and sentimental.  Often used to describe overly sentimental or saccharine things, especially music or stories.  Sometimes can also describe something literally sticky or sweet. | The movie was so gurmy I almost couldn't finish it.  这部电影太甜腻了，我差点没看完。 |  The gurmy love song made her cringe. 这首腻歪的爱情歌曲让她感到尴尬。 | 近义词: saccharine, sentimental, maudlin, cloying, sticky, syrupy | 反义词: unsentimental, dry, austere | 词性: 形容词


Etymology: Likely a blend of "gummy" and "murky," suggesting something sticky, unclear, and potentially unpleasant in its sweetness.  Not a formally recognized word in most dictionaries, considered informal or slang.
