guerdon:/ˈɡɜːrdən/ | n. | 酬谢，报酬，奖赏 | something given as a reward for service or merit; recompense |  The knight received a guerdon of gold for his bravery. 这位骑士因其英勇而获得了黄金的赏赐。 | synonyms: reward, recompense, prize, bounty | antonyms: penalty, punishment | noun

guerdon:/ˈɡɜːrdən/ | vt. (literary) | 酬谢，奖赏 | to give a reward to; recompense | The king guerdoned the poet for his beautiful verses. 国王因诗人优美的诗句而赏赐他。 | synonyms: reward, recompense, compensate | antonyms: penalize, punish | verb
