interhabitation:/ɪntərˌhæbɪˈteɪʃən/
n.
同居，共栖
The interhabitation of different species in an ecosystem.  不同物种在同一个生态系统中的共栖。
The interhabitation of humans and animals can lead to the spread of diseases. 人类和动物的同居会导致疾病传播。
coexistence, cohabitation
segregation, separation
noun
