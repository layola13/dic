coordinates:/koʊˈɔːrdɪnɪts/ /kəˈɔːrdɪnəts/| n. |坐标；方位；配套服装|A set of numbers that show the exact position of a point on a map, graph, or other system.  Also refers to clothes, colours, etc. that are designed to be worn together and to look good together.|
例句：The coordinates of the treasure are marked on the map. 宝藏的坐标标在地图上。

例句：She bought a new pair of shoes to coordinates with her dress. 她买了一双新鞋来搭配她的裙子。

近义词：position, location, match, complement|反义词: - |词性:noun


coordinates:/koʊˈɔːrdɪneɪt/ /kəˈɔːrdɪneɪt/| v. |协调；配合|To make different things work together effectively.|
例句：We need to coordinate our efforts to achieve the best results. 我们需要协调我们的努力以达到最佳效果。

例句：They coordinated their outfits for the family photo. 他们为全家福搭配了服装。

近义词：organize, harmonize, synchronize, match|反义词: clash, conflict |词性:verb


coordinates:/koʊˈɔːrdɪnɪtɪd/ /kəˈɔːrdɪneɪtɪd/| adj. |协调的；配合的|Working together effectively.|
例句：Their coordinated movements on the dance floor were impressive. 他们在舞池里协调的动作令人印象深刻。

例句：The charity event was a well-coordinated effort. 慈善活动是一次协调良好的努力。

近义词：organized, harmonious, synchronized, matched|反义词: disorganized, chaotic |词性:adjective
