federalized:/ˈfɛdərəˌlaɪzd/
adjective
联邦化的
Brought under the control of a federal government.
The government federalized the National Guard. 政府将国民警卫队联邦化。
Synonyms: nationalized, centralized
Antonyms: localized, decentralized
词性: 形容词
