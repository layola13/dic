mendacious:/mɛnˈdeɪʃəs/
adjective
虚假的，说谎的
Given to lying; habitually dishonest.
例句：The mendacious politician was eventually exposed by the media.  这个虚伪的政客最终被媒体曝光了。

例句：Her mendacious statements damaged her reputation. 她虚假的陈述损害了她的名誉。

近义词：lying, dishonest, deceitful, untruthful, false
反义词：truthful, honest, veracious

词性: adjective
