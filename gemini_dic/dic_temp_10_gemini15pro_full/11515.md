aright:/əˈraɪt/
adv.
correctly; properly; rightly 正确地；恰当地；合适地
All assumptions being correct, the conclusions are alright. 所有假设都正确，结论也没错。
近义词:correctly, properly, rightly, duly
反义词:wrongly, incorrectly, improperly
词性:副词

aright:/əˈraɪt/
adj. (archaic or dialect)
In a satisfactory state; all right 满意；良好
Is everything alright with you? 你一切安好吗？
近义词: satisfactory, all right, fine, OK
反义词: unsatisfactory, wrong, bad
词性:形容词
