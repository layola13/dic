leanly:/ˈliːnli/ | adv. | 瘦削地，精简地，简洁地 |  In a thin or lean manner; also, without any extra or unnecessary elements. |  He was leanly built, with a sharp, intelligent face. 他身材瘦削，面容尖刻而聪明。|  The report was written leanly, focusing only on the essential facts.  这份报告写得简洁，只关注基本事实。 | 近义词: thinly, sparsely, simply, efficiently | 反义词:  fatly, thickly, elaborately, excessively | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From lean (adjective) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  The adjective "lean" comes from Old English "hlæne," meaning "thin, meager." The suffix "-ly" is a common adverbial suffix in English.
