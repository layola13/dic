cajolers:/kəˈdʒoʊlərz/| n. |哄骗者，劝诱者|People who use flattery or deceit to persuade someone to do something.  Those who attempt to coax or wheedle.|The cajolers tried to convince her to invest in their scheme. 那些劝诱者试图说服她投资他们的计划。|Synonyms: flatterers, wheedlers, coaxers, persuaders|Antonyms: discouragers, dissuaders|Noun
