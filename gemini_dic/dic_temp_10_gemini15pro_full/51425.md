detersiveness:/dɪˈtɜːrdʒəntnəs/
|n.|去污力，清洁力|The quality of being detersive; the ability to cleanse or remove dirt and impurities.|This detergent boasts superior detersiveness, easily lifting grease and grime. 这种洗涤剂具有优异的去污力，轻松去除油脂和污垢。|近义词：cleansing power, cleaning ability|反义词：inertness, inactivity|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From detersive + -ness.  Detersive comes from Late Latin *dētersīvus, from dētersus, past participle of dētergēre "to wipe off," from dē- "off" + tergēre "to wipe, polish."
