overabstemiousnesses:/ˌoʊvəræbstˈiːmiəsnəsɪz/
|n.|过度节制; 过度禁欲|The overabstemiousnesses of the monks led to health problems.  僧侣们过度节制导致了健康问题。|  Excessive abstinence, extreme temperance | He practiced overabstemiousness in his youth, which might have contributed to his later health issues. 他年轻时非常禁欲，这可能导致了他后来的健康问题。|近义词: asceticism, austerity, self-denial|反义词: gluttony, indulgence, excess|词性: 名词 (plural)

Etymology:
From over- + abstemiousness.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," and "abstemiousness" comes from the Latin "abstemius," meaning "temperate, abstaining from wine."
