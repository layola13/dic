hexadecapole:/ˌhɛksəˈdɛkəˌpoʊl/ | n. |十六极|A distribution of electric charge or magnetic strength which has a particular configuration in three dimensions; it can be permanent, as in a magnetized material, or time-varying, as in the transmitting antenna of a radio or television station.| The hexadecapole moment of a nucleus influences the energy levels of its electrons.| 近义词:multipole | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps monopole could be considered a contrasting term) | noun


hexadecapole:/ˌhɛksəˈdɛkəˌpoʊl/ | adj. |十六极的| Relating to a hexadecapole.| Hexadecapole interactions are important in some molecular systems. | 近义词:multipolar | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps monopolar could be considered a contrasting term) | adjective
