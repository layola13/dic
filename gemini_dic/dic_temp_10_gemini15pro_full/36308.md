cohesively:/kəʊˈhiːsɪvli/| adv.|有凝聚力地；连贯地|in a way that forms a united whole.|The team worked cohesively to achieve their goal.  团队团结协作实现了他们的目标。|Synonyms: unitedly, conjointly, harmoniously|Antonyms: disjointedly, incoherently, separately|词性:副词

cohesion:/kəʊˈhiːʒən/| n.|凝聚；凝聚力；团结|the action or fact of forming a united whole.|The cohesion of the group was remarkable.  这个群体的凝聚力非常引人注目。|Synonyms: unity, solidarity, togetherness|Antonyms: division, disunity, fragmentation|词性:名词

cohesive:/kəʊˈhiːsɪv/| adj.|有凝聚力的；团结的|forming a united whole.|The presentation was cohesive and well-organized.  这个演讲很有凝聚力，而且组织得很好。|Synonyms: united, integrated, connected|Antonyms: disjointed, incoherent, fragmented|词性:形容词
