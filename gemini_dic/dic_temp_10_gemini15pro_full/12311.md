ascribed: /əˈskraɪbd/ | v. | 归因于，归功于；认为…是…所说(或所为) |  Considered to be the origin of or belonging to someone or something. | The painting was ascribed to Leonardo da Vinci. 这幅画被认为是莱昂纳多·达·芬奇的作品。 | Synonyms: attributed, credited, assigned | Antonyms: denied, refuted | verb

ascribed: /əˈskraɪbd/ | adj. |  归因的，归属的 | Attributed to a specified cause, source, or origin. |  The commonly ascribed motive for the crime was jealousy. 通常认为这起犯罪的动机是嫉妒。 | Synonyms: attributed, imputed | Antonyms: unattributed, unascribed | adjective
