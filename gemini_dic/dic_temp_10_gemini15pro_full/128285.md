neuropter:/ˈnjʊərɒptər/ (US) /ˈnʊərɒptə(r)/ (UK)| n. |神经翅目昆虫; 脉翅目昆虫 |An insect of the order Neuroptera, such as a lacewing, alderfly, or antlion.| Lacewings are common neuropters that help control garden pests. | 近义词: lacewing, alderfly, antlion, dobsonfly | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From  neuro-  "nerve" + -ptera "wing," from Greek *neuron "sinew, tendon, nerve" + *pteron "wing, feather."  So literally, "nerve-winged."
