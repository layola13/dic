parietine:/ˌpærɪˈtaɪn/ /ˌpærɪˈtiːn/ | n. adj.|墙生植物；墙藓|A plant that grows on walls, especially a kind of pellitory. Also used as an adjective to describe something relating to or growing on walls.|例句：The old wall was covered in parietine.  古老的墙壁上覆盖着墙藓。|近义词：wall plant, pellitory, mural|反义词:N/A|词性:noun, adjective

parietine:/ˌpærɪˈtaɪn/ /ˌpærɪˈtiːn/ | adj.|墙壁的|Relating to or growing on walls.|例句：Parietine ferns thrived in the humid crevice.  潮湿的裂缝里生长着茂盛的墙生蕨类植物。|近义词：mural, wall-growing|反义词:N/A|词性:adjective
