geocaching:/ˈdʒiːoʊˌkæʃɪŋ/ /ˈdʒiːəʊˌkæʃɪŋ/
n.
寻宝游戏；地理藏宝
A recreational activity in which participants use a Global Positioning System (GPS) receiver or mobile device and other navigational techniques to hide and seek containers, called "geocaches" or "caches", at specific locations marked by coordinates all over the world.
例句：Geocaching is a fun way to explore new places. 地理藏宝是一种探索新地方的有趣方式。

例句：We went geocaching in the mountains last weekend. 上周末我们去山里玩地理藏宝游戏了。

近义词：treasure hunting, letterboxing
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "staying indoors")
词性: noun
