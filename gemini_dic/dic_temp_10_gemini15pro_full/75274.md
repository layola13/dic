front-end:/ˌfrʌntˈend/ | adj. n. |前端的，前端|Relating to or denoting the initial part of a process or system.  The part of a computer system or application with which the user interacts directly. |Our company is looking for a skilled front-end developer. 我们公司正在寻找一名熟练的前端开发人员。|The front-end of the website needs a redesign.  网站的前端需要重新设计。|近义词：client-side, user interface, front |反义词: back-end, server-side |词性: adjective, noun
