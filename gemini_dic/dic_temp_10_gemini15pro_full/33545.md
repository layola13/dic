cierge:/kɔ̃ˈsjɛʁʒ/ | n. | 门房，礼宾员 | A hotel employee whose job is to assist guests with various tasks, such as making reservations, arranging transportation, and providing information about local attractions. | Example 1: The concierge helped us book a table at the best restaurant in town.  门房帮我们预订了城里最好的餐厅的位子。

Example 2:  I asked the concierge for directions to the museum. 我向礼宾员询问去博物馆的路线。 | Synonym: porter, doorman, attendant | Antonym: N/A | Noun
