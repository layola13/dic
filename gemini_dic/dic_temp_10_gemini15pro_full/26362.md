by-election:/ˌbaɪ ɪˈlekʃn/ | n. | 补选 | A special election held between general elections to fill a vacancy, typically caused by the death or resignation of an elected official. |  The by-election was called after the sitting MP resigned. 补选是因为现任议员辞职而举行的。| Synonyms: special election, supplementary election | Antonyms: general election | noun
