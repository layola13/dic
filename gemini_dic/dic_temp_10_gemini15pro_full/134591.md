nonlubricating: /ˌnɒnˈluːbrɪkeɪtɪŋ/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈluːbrɪkeɪtɪŋ/ (US) | adj. | 非润滑的，不润滑的 | Not providing lubrication; not designed to reduce friction. | This nonlubricating gasket is ideal for high-temperature applications.  这种非润滑垫片非常适合高温应用。|  The coating is nonlubricating and wear-resistant.  这种涂层不润滑且耐磨。 |  Synonyms: non-oiled, dry, friction-increasing (less common) | Antonyms: lubricating, oiled, greasy | adjective
