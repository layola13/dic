encolor:/ɪnˈkʌlər/ | v. | 给...着色，染上颜色 | To imbue with color; to color. |  She decided to encolor the old black and white photographs. 她决定给那些黑白老照片上色。

The artist encolored the canvas with vibrant hues. 艺术家用鲜艳的色彩给画布着色。 | 近义词: color, tint, dye, stain | 反义词: bleach, decolorize |词性: 动词

encolor evenly (均匀地着色): Make sure to encolor the surface evenly. 确保表面着色均匀。
