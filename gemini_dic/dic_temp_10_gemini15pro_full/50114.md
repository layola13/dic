dentirostrate:/ˌdɛntɪˈrɒstreɪt/
adjective
齿喙的，具齿喙的
Having a toothed beak or bill.
Dentirostrate birds, like some extinct species, had tooth-like projections on their beaks.  一些已经灭绝的齿喙鸟类，它们的喙上有类似牙齿的突起。
The fossil remains suggest a dentirostrate adaptation for catching fish. 化石遗骸表明其齿喙适应捕捉鱼类。
近义词: toothed-beaked
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "smooth-beaked")
词性: adjective
