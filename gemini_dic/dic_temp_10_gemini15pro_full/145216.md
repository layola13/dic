overbear:/ˌoʊvərˈber/| v. |压倒，制服，专横跋扈|To overwhelm or dominate by superior force or authority; to be overbearing or domineering.|His forceful personality tends to overbear others in meetings. 他强势的个性在会议上往往会压倒其他人。|The loud music overbore all other sounds. 响亮的音乐盖过了所有其他声音。|近义词：overpower, subdue, dominate, overwhelm, browbeat|反义词：submit, yield, concede|词性：动词

overbearing:/ˌoʊvərˈberɪŋ/| adj. |专横的，傲慢的，压倒性的|Domineering, haughty, or dictatorial in manner; tending to overwhelm or suppress.|Her overbearing attitude made it difficult for her colleagues to work with her. 她专横的态度使她的同事很难与她共事。|The overbearing heat made it impossible to work outdoors.  压倒性的高温使得无法在户外工作。|近义词：domineering, arrogant, haughty, imperious, dictatorial|反义词：humble, modest, meek, submissive|词性：形容词


词源: From Middle English *overberen, from Old English oferberan (“to carry over, transport, bring over, afflict, oppress”), from ofer (“over”) + beran (“to bear”).
