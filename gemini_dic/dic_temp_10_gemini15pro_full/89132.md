homebrew:/ˈhoʊmˌbruː/ (US), /ˈhəʊmˌbruː/ (UK)| n. adj.| 自酿啤酒；自酿的；自制的| Something, especially beer, that is made at home.  It can also refer to something made locally or on a small scale, often suggesting a non-professional or amateur production.|  例句：He offered me a glass of his homebrew. 他给了我一杯他的自酿啤酒。

|例句：The software is a homebrew application designed for a specific purpose. 这个软件是一个为特定目的设计的自制应用程序。|近义词：homemade, craft, artisanal|反义词：commercial, mass-produced|词性: 名词, 形容词
