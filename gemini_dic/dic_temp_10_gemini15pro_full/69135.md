n.|恩惠，帮助；偏袒；赠品；纪念品|acts of kindness or generosity; preferential treatment; small gifts or souvenirs|She did me a great favor by lending me her car. 她把车借给我，真是帮了我大忙。|近义词: kindnesses, courtesies, privileges, gifts, souvenirs|反义词: disfavors, prejudices, hindrances|词性:名词(plural)

favor:/ˈfeɪvər/ (US), /ˈfeɪvər/ (UK)
n.|恩惠，帮助；偏袒；赠品；纪念品|an act of kindness or generosity; preferential treatment; a small gift or souvenir|Could you do me a favor and pick up some milk? 你能帮我个忙，买点牛奶吗？|近义词: kindness, courtesy, privilege, gift, souvenir|反义词: disfavor, prejudice, hindrance|词性:名词

favor:/ˈfeɪvər/ (US), /ˈfeɪvər/ (UK)
vt.|赞同，支持；偏爱，偏袒；有利于|to support or prefer something; to be advantageous to something|The warm weather favors outdoor activities.  温暖的天气有利于户外活动。|近义词: support, prefer, promote, encourage|反义词: oppose, disfavor, hinder|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *faveur* (modern French *faveur*), from Latin *favor* "goodwill, kindness, favor, partiality," related to *favēre* "to be well-disposed, to favor."
