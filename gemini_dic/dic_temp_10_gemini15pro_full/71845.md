flemishes:/ˈflɛmɪʃɪz/
noun
瑕疵，斑点
A mark or imperfection, especially on the skin, such as a spot or pimple.
The teenager was embarrassed by the blemishes on his face.  这个青少年对他脸上的斑点感到尴尬。
synonyms: imperfections, spots, pimples, acne, marks, defects, flaws
antonyms: perfections, clear skin

flemishes:/ˈflɛmɪʃɪz/
noun
污点，缺点
A small fault or weakness that spoils something.
The otherwise perfect painting had a few blemishes.  这幅本来完美的画有一些瑕疵。
synonyms: faults, weaknesses, defects, flaws, imperfections
antonyms: perfections, strengths, virtues
