overvehemently:/ˌoʊvərˈviːəməntli/
adverb
过度热情地；过分激动地
With excessive vehemence; too intensely or passionately.
He argued his case overvehemently, alienating the jury. 他过于激动地辩护，疏远了陪审团。
Synonyms: passionately, fervently, intensely, excitedly, wildly, furiously, fanatically
Antonyms: calmly, quietly, mildly, moderately, apathetically
词性: 副词
