enged: /ɪnˈɡeɪdʒd/ | adj. | 已订婚的，忙碌的，从事…的 | involved in something, especially in a busy or serious way; having formally agreed to marry |  She is engaged to be married next spring. 她明年春天就要结婚了。| 近义词: betrothed, occupied, busy | 反义词: free, unoccupied, single | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Old French *engaier (meaning "to pledge, promise"), derived from the prefix en- ("in, into") and gage ("pledge").
