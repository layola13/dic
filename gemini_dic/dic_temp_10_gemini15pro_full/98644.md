insuccation:/ɪnsʌˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 灌输; 浸渍; 浸泡 | The act of soaking or steeping something in a liquid.  The term can also refer to the act of instilling or imbuing something, such as a principle or quality. | The insuccation of the herbs in the oil infused it with their medicinal properties.  草药在油中的浸泡使其充满了药用特性。 | Near synonyms: immersion, steeping, infusion, inculcation | Antonyms: dehydration, desiccation | noun
