familistic:/ˌfæmɪˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
家庭的；家族主义的
Oriented or focused on the family and its values; emphasizing the importance of family relationships.
例句：Their culture is very familistic, with strong emphasis on family ties and obligations. 他们的文化非常重视家庭，强调家庭关系和义务。

例句：Familistic societies often prioritize the needs of the family over individual desires.  家族主义社会通常将家庭的需求置于个人愿望之上。

近义词：family-oriented, family-centered, patriarchal, matriarchal
反义词：individualistic, egocentric


词性: adjective
