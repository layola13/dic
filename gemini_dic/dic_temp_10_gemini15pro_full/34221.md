clangous:/ˈklæŋɡəs/| adj.|发出铿锵声的；响亮的|Characterized by a loud, resonant, metallic sound.|The clangous bells of the church echoed through the valley. 教堂响亮的钟声在山谷中回荡。|近义词：resonant, sonorous, clangorous|反义词：muted, soft, quiet|词性: 形容词
Etymology: From clang + -ous (suffix forming adjectives).  "Clang" itself comes from imitative origins, echoing the sound it represents.
