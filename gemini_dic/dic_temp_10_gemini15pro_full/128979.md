nip-off:/nɪp ˈɒf/ (UK) /nɪp ˈɔːf/ (US)| v. |掐掉，摘掉; 阻止…的发展|To remove something, such as a bud or a flower, by pinching or snipping it off. Also, to stop something from developing or continuing.|She nipped off the dead leaves from the plant. 她把植物上的枯叶掐掉了。|Synonyms: pinch off, snip off, remove, detach|Antonyms: attach, grow, develop|Verb

nip-off:/nɪp ˈɒf/ (UK) /nɪp ˈɔːf/ (US)| n. |掐掉，摘掉 (的动作)|The act of nipping something off.|The nip-off of the flower buds prevented the plant from flowering. 掐掉花蕾阻止了植物开花。|Synonyms: pinching off, snipping off, removal, detachment|Antonyms: attachment, growth, development|Noun
