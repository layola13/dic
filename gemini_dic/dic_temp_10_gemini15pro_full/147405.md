overnormal:/ˌoʊvərˈnɔːrməl/ | adj. | 超常的，异常的，超乎寻常的 | Exceeding or surpassing what is normal or usual; abnormally large, intense, or effective. |  The overnormal profits attracted the attention of regulators.  超常的利润引起了监管机构的注意。|  The athlete demonstrated overnormal strength and endurance.  这位运动员展现出超乎寻常的力量和耐力。 | 近义词: exceptional, extraordinary, phenomenal, preternatural | 反义词: normal, usual, ordinary, typical | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From "over-" (meaning "above, beyond") + "normal."  "Over-" has roots in Old English "ofer," and "normal" comes from Late Middle English via Old French from Late Latin "normalis" (according to a carpenter's square).
