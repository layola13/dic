astrolithology:/ˌæstrəʊlɪˈθɒlədʒi/
n.
星石学
The study of rocks from space, specifically meteorites and lunar samples.
Astrolithology helps us understand the formation and evolution of the solar system.  星石学帮助我们理解太阳系的形成和演化。
Astrolithology provides valuable insights into the composition of other celestial bodies. 星石学为了解其他天体的组成提供了宝贵的见解。
近义词:meteoritics, lunar geology
反义词:N/A
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From astro- (Greek ἄστρον astron, "star") + litho- (Greek λίθος lithos, "stone") + -logy (Greek -λογία -logia, "study of").
