demoniacally:/ˌdiːməˈnaɪkəli/ | adv. | 恶魔般地; 疯狂地 | In a way that suggests possession by a demon; frenziedly, wildly. |  He laughed demoniacally. 他恶魔般地大笑。|  The wind howled demoniacally outside.  风在外面疯狂地呼啸。| 近义词：fiendishly, satanically, diabolically, hellishly, insanely, maniacally | 反义词: angelically, divinely | 词性: 副词

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin *demoniacus + -al + -ly.
