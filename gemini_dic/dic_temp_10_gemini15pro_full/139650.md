occultness:/əˈkʌltnɪs/
n.
神秘; 奥秘; 玄妙; 隐秘
the state or quality of being occult; supernaturalness.
例句：The occultness of the ancient ritual fascinated the historian.  古代仪式的奥秘让历史学家着迷。

例句：She was drawn to the occultness of the tarot cards. 她被塔罗牌的神秘所吸引。

近义词：mystery, secrecy, secretiveness, hiddenness, obscurity, enigma
反义词：obviousness, clarity, openness, transparency
词性：名词

词源: From Late Middle English *occulnesse, from Old French occulteté, from Latin occultus (“hidden, secret”), past participle of occulere (“to cover over, conceal”), from ob (“over, before, against”) + calere (“to hide”) (whence Latin celare (“to hide”)).
