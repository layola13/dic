discerpible:/dɪˈsɜːrpɪbl/ /dɪˈsɜːr.pə.bəl/
adjective
可辨别的, 可识别的
Capable of being discerned; distinguishable.
The subtle differences between the two wines are discerpible to a trained palate.  两种葡萄酒之间的细微差别对于训练有素的味觉是可以辨别的。
The faint outline of a ship was discerpible through the fog.  透过雾，依稀可见一艘船的轮廓。
近义词: perceptible, distinguishable, noticeable, detectable
反义词: imperceptible, indistinguishable, unnoticeable, undetectable
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *discerpibilis, from discerpere "to tear apart, separate," from dis- "apart" + carpere "to pluck, seize."  源自拉丁语 *discerpibilis，来自 discerpere “撕开，分开”，来自 dis- “分开” + carpere “采摘，抓住”。
