eozooenal:/iːoʊzoʊˈiːnəl/ | adj. | 曙光动物区的; 与曙光动物群有关的 | Relating to the eozooen, a hypothetical faunal realm proposed by some biogeographers to encompass the animal life of the Holarctic region during the early Cenozoic Era. |  The discovery of fossil remains suggests an eozooenal distribution for this extinct species.  该化石遗骸的发现表明这个灭绝物种的曙光动物区分布。

The eozooenal hypothesis is now largely superseded by more nuanced biogeographic models.  曙光动物区假说现在已被更细致的生物地理模型所取代。 | 近义词: Holarctic (in a broader sense) | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective
