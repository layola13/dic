bumbarge:/ˈbʌmbɑːrdʒ/ | n. |驳船，大型平底船|A large, flat-bottomed boat used for carrying heavy loads, especially on canals and rivers. |例句：The bumbarge slowly made its way down the river, laden with coal. 这艘驳船载着煤，缓缓地沿着河流而下。

例句：The bumbarge was moored alongside the wharf, waiting to be unloaded. 这艘驳船停泊在码头边，等待卸货。|近义词：barge, lighter, flatboat |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'speedboat' for contrast in size and speed) |词性: noun
