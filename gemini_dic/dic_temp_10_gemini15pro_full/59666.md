echinodermata:/ɪˌkaɪnəˈdɜːrmətə/ | n. | 棘皮动物门 |A phylum of marine invertebrates characterized by a hard, spiny covering or skin. They include starfish, sea urchins, and sea cucumbers.| Starfish are a well-known example of echinodermata. 海星是棘皮动物的一个著名例子。| Sea urchins are also part of the echinodermata phylum. 海胆也属于棘皮动物门。 |synonyms: none (as it's a taxonomic classification)| antonyms: none (as it's a taxonomic classification)| noun
