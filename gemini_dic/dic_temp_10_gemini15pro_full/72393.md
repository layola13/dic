fluctuational:/ˌflʌktʃuˈeɪʃənəl/
adjective
波动的，起伏的
Characterized by fluctuation; tending to fluctuate.
The fluctuational stock market makes investors nervous. 波动的股票市场让投资者感到紧张。
The company's fluctuational profits caused concern among the board members. 公司利润的波动引起了董事会成员的担忧。
synonyms: variable, unstable, erratic, volatile, changeable
antonyms: stable, steady, constant, fixed, unvarying
词性: adjective
