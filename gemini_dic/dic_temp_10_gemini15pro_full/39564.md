conscious:/ˈkɒnʃəs/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnʃəs/ (US)| adj.|有意识的；清醒的；察觉到的；故意的|Aware of and responding to one's surroundings; awake.|例句：He became conscious of someone watching him. 他意识到有人在看着他。|近义词：aware, awake, sentient|反义词：unconscious, unaware, insensible|词性:形容词

conscious:/ˈkɒnʃəs/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnʃəs/ (US)| adj.|关注的；关心的；刻意的；慎重的|Having knowledge of something; deliberate.|例句：She made a conscious effort to be more friendly. 她有意识地努力让自己更友好。|近义词：intentional, deliberate, purposeful|反义词：unintentional, accidental, inadvertent|词性:形容词
