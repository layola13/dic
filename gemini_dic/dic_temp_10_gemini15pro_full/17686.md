bed's:/bedz/
noun
床的；床铺的
Indicates possession or association with a bed.  It is a contraction of "bed is" or "bed has."
My bed's very comfortable. 我的床很舒服。
The cat is sleeping on the bed's edge. 猫正睡在床边。
synonyms: belonging to the bed
antonyms: N/A
POS: determiner (possessive)
