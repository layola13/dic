granivores:/ˈɡrænɪvɔːrz/
n.
食谷动物
Animals that primarily eat seeds or grain.
Granivores, such as certain rodents and birds, play an essential role in seed dispersal. 食谷动物，例如某些啮齿动物和鸟类，在种子传播中起着至关重要的作用。
Squirrels are granivores and often bury nuts for later retrieval. 松鼠是食谷动物，经常埋藏坚果以备日后取回。
Synonyms: seed-eaters, grain-eating animals
Antonyms: carnivores, herbivores
