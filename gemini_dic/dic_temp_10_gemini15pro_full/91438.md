hygienism:/ˈhaɪdʒiːənɪzəm/
n.|卫生主义; 健康主义|A system of health principles; belief in the importance of cleanliness and sanitation for preserving health.|The hygienism movement of the 19th century advocated for improved sanitation to prevent disease.|Synonym: sanitationism, cleanliness|Antonym: uncleanliness, filth|noun
