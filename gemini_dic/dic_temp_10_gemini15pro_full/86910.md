hemlock:/ˈhɛmlɒk/ (UK) /ˈhɛmlɑːk/ (US)| n.|铁杉；毒芹；由毒芹制成的毒药|A highly poisonous European plant of the parsley family, with a ribbed stem, small white flowers, and finely divided leaves.  Also, a poisonous drink prepared from this plant, or any of several related poisonous plants of the carrot family.|Socrates was forced to drink hemlock. 苏格拉底被迫喝下毒芹汁。| 近义词: poison, conium | 反义词: antidote, remedy |词性: noun
