n.
剩余物，遗留物，延期的事物；（电影）延期放映的影片；留任者；延期住宿的旅客
Something remaining from an earlier time; something postponed or continued.  A film continuing its run in a cinema after the general release has ended. A person or thing remaining from a previous time, especially a person who continues in office after their term has ended. A person staying on longer than originally planned, especially in a hotel.
The hotel was full of holdovers from the convention. 这家酒店住满了会议的延期住宿旅客。
The old regulations are a holdover from the previous administration. 这些旧规章是前一届政府遗留下来的。
