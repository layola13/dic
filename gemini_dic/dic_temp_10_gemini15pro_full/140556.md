olid:/ˈɒlɪd/ | adj. | 坚固的，实心的，可靠的；固体的，液体的反义词 | firm and stable in shape; not hollow or containing spaces; dependable; not in the form of a gas or liquid |  The table is made of solid oak. 这张桌子是用实心橡木做的。| firm, stable, sturdy, robust, sound | hollow, flimsy, unstable, liquid, gaseous | adjective

olid:/ˈɒlɪd/ | n. | 固体 | a substance that is not a liquid or a gas | Ice is water in its solid state. 冰是水的固态。 | solid material, substance | liquid, gas, fluid | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *olid, from Latin solidus (“dense, compact, firm, whole, entire”), from Proto-Italic *sollos (“whole”), from Proto-Indo-European *solh₂ós (“whole, complete”). Cognate with Old Irish sollus (“clear, manifest”), Sanskrit sárva (“whole, all”).
