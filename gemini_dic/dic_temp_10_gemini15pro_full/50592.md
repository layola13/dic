deranged:/dɪˈreɪndʒd/
adjective
精神错乱的，疯狂的
Mad; insane.  Driven to madness.  Seriously mentally disturbed.
例句：The deranged man wandered the streets, talking to himself.  这个精神错乱的男人在街上游荡，自言自语。

例句：The killer was described as a deranged individual with a history of violence.  凶手被描述为一个有暴力史的精神错乱的个人。

近义词：insane, mad, lunatic, demented, unhinged
反义词：sane, rational, lucid, composed
词性: adjective
