hyperexcitable:/ˌhaɪpərɪkˈsaɪtəbl/ |adj.|过度兴奋的，极易兴奋的|Easily or excessively excitable; responding too readily to stimuli.|The hyperexcitable puppy barked at every sound. 这只过度兴奋的小狗对每一个声音都吠叫。|
The child was hyperexcitable after eating too much sugar.  这个孩子吃了太多糖后变得非常兴奋。
|synonyms: overexcitable, jumpy, nervous, jittery, restless, fidgety|antonyms: calm, placid, tranquil, serene, relaxed, composed|adjective


Etymology:
hyper- (prefix): from Greek, meaning "over, above, excessive"
excitable (adjective): from Late Middle English, from Old French excitable, from Late Latin excitabilis, from Latin excitare "to rouse, excite"
