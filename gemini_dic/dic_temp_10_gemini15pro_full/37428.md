comminatory:/ˈkɒmɪnətəri/ /ˈkɑːmɪnətɔːri/| adj.|威胁的，恐吓的，预示灾祸的| Expressing or containing a threat or warning of punishment. |The king delivered a comminatory speech against his enemies. 国王向他的敌人发表了恐吓性的演讲。

The teacher's comminatory tone made the students nervous. 老师恐吓的语气让学生们感到紧张。|近义词：threatening, menacing, minatory|反义词： encouraging, reassuring, comforting|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *comminatorius, from Latin comminari (“to threaten repeatedly”), from com- (“together”) + minari (“to threaten”).
