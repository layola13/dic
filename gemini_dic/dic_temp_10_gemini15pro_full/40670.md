conversably:/kənˈvɜːsəbli/
adverb|健谈地，交谈地|In a manner suitable for conversation; in a way that encourages or facilitates conversation.|They chatted conversably about the weather. 他们轻松地聊着天气。|近义词:sociably, affably, genially|反义词:unsociably, taciturnly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From the adjective "conversable," which comes from the verb "converse" + "-able."  "Converse" ultimately derives from the Latin "conversari," meaning "to associate with," frequentative of "convertere," "to turn around, be together."
