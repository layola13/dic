nonhumanists:/ˌnɒnˈhjuːmənɪsts/ |/ˌnɑːnˈhjuːmənɪsts/ | n. |非人类中心主义者；非人本主义者| People who do not subscribe to humanist philosophy, which emphasizes human values and agency.  They may prioritize other values, such as animal rights, environmentalism, or religious beliefs that decenter humans. |Many nonhumanists believe that humans should not exploit animals for their own benefit. 许多非人类中心主义者认为人类不应该为了自身利益而剥削动物。

|Nonhumanists often advocate for environmental protection. 非人类中心主义者经常倡导环境保护。| 近义词:anti-humanists, posthumanists| 反义词:humanists| 词性:名词
