dbms:/ˈdiː biː emˈes/| n.|数据库管理系统 (Database Management System)|A software system used for creating and managing databases. It provides a systematic way to create, retrieve, update and manage data.|The company uses a robust DBMS to handle its large customer database. 这家公司使用强大的数据库管理系统来处理其庞大的客户数据库。|近义词：database system, database software|反义词: None|名词
