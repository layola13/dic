mandarining:/ˈmændərɪnɪŋ/| n. |用官话交谈；摆官架子|The act of speaking Mandarin Chinese or behaving in an officious or condescending manner, like a mandarin (a high-ranking official in Imperial China).|例句：His mandarining alienated his colleagues. 他那副官腔官调疏远了他的同事们。

例句：The mandarining of the new manager made everyone uncomfortable. 新经理的官僚作风让每个人都不舒服。|近义词：officiousness, pomposity, condescending|反义词:humility, modesty|词性:名词
