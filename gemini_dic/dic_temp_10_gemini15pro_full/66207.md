excitably:/ɪkˈsaɪtəbli/
adv.
兴奋地
in an excited manner
She talked excitably about her trip to Paris. 她兴奋地谈论着她的巴黎之旅。
The children ran excitably towards the playground. 孩子们兴奋地跑向操场。
近义词: eagerly, enthusiastically, animatedly
反义词: calmly, quietly, apathetically
词性: 副词

词源: From the adjective "excitable" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Excitable" comes from the verb "excite" + "-able" (suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being"). "Excite" comes from Latin "excitare" meaning "to rouse, stir up, call forth".
