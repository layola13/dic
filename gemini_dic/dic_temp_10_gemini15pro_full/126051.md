mustang:/ˈmʌstæŋ/| n.|野马，(尤指美国西部)未驯服的小马; 特指福特野马跑车|A small, hardy horse of the American west, descended from horses brought by the Spaniards.  Also, specifically, the Ford Mustang sports car. |例句：He rode a wild mustang across the plains.  他骑着一匹野马穿越平原。

例句：She just bought a brand new Mustang convertible. 她刚买了一辆全新的野马敞篷跑车。|近义词：bronco, wild horse|反义词:domestic horse|词性:noun
