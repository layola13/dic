词源:  "fish" (Old English "fisc") + "farm" (Old English "feorm," originally meaning "food, provisions," later evolving to mean "a fixed payment, rent," then "land leased for farming"). The compound "fish-farm" emerged later, with the increasing practice of aquaculture.
