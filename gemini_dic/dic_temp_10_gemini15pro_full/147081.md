overliberalized: /ˌōvərˈlɪbərəˌlaɪzd/ | adj. | 过度自由化的 | Having been made excessively liberal, often in terms of economic or social policies.  Characterized by an excessive relaxation of regulations or controls. | The overliberalized banking sector led to a financial crisis. 过度自由化的银行业导致了金融危机。 | The overliberalized lending practices of the bank eventually led to its collapse.  该银行过度自由化的贷款行为最终导致其倒闭。 | synonyms: deregulated, laissez-faire | antonyms: regulated, controlled | adjective
