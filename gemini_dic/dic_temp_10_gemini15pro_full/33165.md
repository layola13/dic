chromatype:/ˈkrəʊməˌtaɪp/ | n. |染色体组型; 染色体组; 核型 | A photographic representation of the chromosomes of a single cell, arranged in order of decreasing size. | Example: The chromatype of a person with Down syndrome shows an extra copy of chromosome 21.  唐氏综合症患者的染色体组型显示21号染色体多了一个拷贝。| Example:  Analysis of the chromatype revealed several abnormalities. 染色体组型分析显示出几个异常。|Synonym: karyotype|Antonym: N/A| noun
