non-subsistence:/ˌnɒn səbˈsɪstəns/ | adj. | 非维持生计的；非必需的 |  Relating to goods, activities, or income that are not necessary for basic survival. It refers to things beyond the necessities of life, like luxuries or discretionary spending. | Many families in developed countries spend a significant portion of their income on non-subsistence items like entertainment and travel. 许多发达国家的家庭将收入的很大一部分用于娱乐和旅行等非维持生计的项目。| 近义词: non-essential, discretionary, luxury | 反义词: subsistence, essential, necessary | 词性: adjective

non-subsistence:/ˌnɒn səbˈsɪstəns/ | n. | 非维持生计（的状态或活动）| The state or condition of not being focused on basic survival needs. This can refer to a lifestyle that includes leisure, luxury, and pursuits beyond meeting basic needs. |  Their lifestyle of non-subsistence allowed them to pursue their passions for art and music. 他们的非维持生计的生活方式使他们能够追求对艺术和音乐的热情。| 近义词: affluence, prosperity | 反义词: poverty, deprivation | 词性: noun
