noncapillary:/nɒnˈkæpɪləri/ | adj. | 非毛细血管的 | Not relating to or involving capillaries (tiny blood vessels). |  The larger arteries and veins are noncapillary blood vessels.  较大的动脉和静脉是非毛细血管的血管。| synonyms:  amacrine (specifically for retinal neurons), larger vessel | antonyms: capillary | adjective

Etymology:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "capillary" (from Latin "capillaris" meaning "hair-like," referring to the tiny size of capillaries).
