frigefaction:/ˌfrɪdʒɪˈfækʃən/ | n. | 冷却，冷冻 |The process of making something cold; cooling or refrigeration. |The frigefaction of the food preserved it for weeks.  食物的冷冻使其保存了数周。| 近义词：cooling, refrigeration, chilling | 反义词：heating, warming | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Latin *frīgefactus, past participle of frīgefacere "to make cold," from frīgus "cold" + facere "to make."
