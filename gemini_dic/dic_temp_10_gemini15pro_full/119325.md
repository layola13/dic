microbiologist:/ˌmaɪkroʊbaɪˈɑːlədʒɪst/ | n. | 微生物学家 | A scientist who studies microorganisms such as bacteria, viruses, and fungi. |  Example 1: The microbiologist studied the bacteria under a powerful microscope.  微生物学家在高倍显微镜下研究细菌。

Example 2:  She's a leading microbiologist in the field of infectious diseases.  她是传染病领域的顶尖微生物学家。 | Synonym: bacteriologist (for those specializing in bacteria), virologist (for viruses), mycologist (for fungi) | Antonym: N/A (no direct opposite for a profession) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
micro-: from Greek "mikros" meaning "small"
bio-: from Greek "bios" meaning "life"
-logist: from Greek "-logos" meaning "one who studies" or "study of"
