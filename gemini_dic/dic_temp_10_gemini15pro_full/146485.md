overfurthest:/ˌoʊvərˈfɜːrðɪst/ | adj. | 最远的；最遥远的 |  Located at the most distant point beyond a specified place or limit.  Implies being even further than what is already considered furthest.  Often used hyperbolically or for emphasis. | The overfurthest reaches of the galaxy are still largely unknown.  银河系最遥远的地方仍然很大程度上不为人知。

The hiker ventured to the overfurthest point on the trail.  徒步旅行者冒险前往小径上最远的地方。| 近义词: farthest, remotest, utmost | 反义词: nearest, closest | 词性: 形容词
