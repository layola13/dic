friller:/ˈfrɪlər/| n. |褶边，饰边，荷叶边；追求浮华的人；爱打扮的人|A piece of gathered or pleated material used as a decoration or trimming on clothing.|The dress had a pretty friller around the neckline. 这件连衣裙领口有一圈漂亮的褶边。|近义词：ruffle, flounce, furbelow|反义词:plainness, simplicity|noun

frilly:/ˈfrɪli/| adj. |有褶边的，饰边的；浮华的，花哨的|Decorated with frills or ruffles; characterized by excessive ornamentation.|She wore a frilly pink dress to the party. 她穿着一件有褶边的粉色连衣裙去参加聚会。|近义词：ruffled, flounced, ornate|反义词:plain, simple, unadorned|adjective
