lyencephala:/laɪɛnˈsɛfələ/
n.
rhombencephalon (hindbrain)
The posterior portion of the brain, composed of the metencephalon (cerebellum and pons) and myelencephalon (medulla oblongata).
The lyencephala plays a crucial role in regulating vital functions such as breathing and heart rate. 菱脑在调节呼吸和心率等重要功能中起着至关重要的作用。
The doctor explained that damage to the lyencephala can result in severe neurological deficits. 医生解释说，菱脑的损伤会导致严重的神经功能缺损。
Synonym: hindbrain
Antonym: prosencephalon (forebrain)
