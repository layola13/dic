conventionalizing:/kənˈvɛnʃənəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使…惯例化，使…俗套化
Making something conform to convention or accepted standards; becoming conventional or commonplace.
The artist was accused of conventionalizing his style to appeal to a wider audience.  这位艺术家被指责为了迎合更广泛的观众而使自己的风格俗套化。
