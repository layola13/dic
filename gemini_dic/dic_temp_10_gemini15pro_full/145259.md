overboastfulness:/ˌoʊvərboʊstfəlnəs/ | n. | 过度自夸，自吹自擂 | The quality of boasting excessively about oneself or one's achievements.  It implies an arrogance and a need to impress others. | His overboastfulness alienated his colleagues. 他的过度自夸疏远了他的同事。| Synonyms: arrogance, conceit, vainglory, egotism, bragging | Antonyms: humility, modesty, self-deprecation | noun

Etymology:
From 'over' (excessive) + 'boast' (to brag) + 'ful' (characterized by) + 'ness' (state of being).  'Boast' comes from Old English *bōgan "to boast," of uncertain origin.
