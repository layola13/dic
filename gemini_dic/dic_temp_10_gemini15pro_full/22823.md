boss-ship:/ˈbɒsʃɪp/ /ˈbɔːsʃɪp/
n.
领导地位；老板身份；老板的职位
The state or position of being a boss.
He assumed the boss-ship of the company after his father retired.  他父亲退休后，他接管了公司的领导地位。
She handled the boss-ship with grace and efficiency. 她以优雅和高效的方式处理老板的职位。
近义词：leadership, management, superintendency,  authority, control
反义词：subordination,  subservience
