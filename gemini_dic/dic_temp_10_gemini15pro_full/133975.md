nonhesitant:/nɒnˈhezɪtənt/ (British), /nɑːnˈhezɪtənt/ (American)| adj.|毫不犹豫的，果断的|Without hesitation; resolute and decisive.|She gave a nonhesitant answer to the question. 她毫不犹豫地回答了这个问题。|He took nonhesitant action to address the crisis. 他毫不犹豫地采取行动来应对危机。|近义词：resolute, decisive, unhesitating, prompt, immediate|反义词：hesitant, indecisive, wavering, reluctant|词性:形容词

nonhesitant:|Etymology:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "hesitant" (from Latin "haesitare" - to stick fast, hesitate).|词源：non-（拉丁语前缀，意为“不”）+ hesitant（源自拉丁语“haesitare” - 粘住，犹豫）。
