ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *nippen*, from Old English *knippan* (“to pinch, nip”), from Proto-Germanic *knippaną* (“to pinch, nip”).  Cognate with Dutch *knippen* (“to cut, snip”), German *knippen* (“to pinch, nip”).
