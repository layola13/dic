googs:/ɡuːɡz/ | n. |护目镜; 泳镜 | Goggles are protective eyewear that usually enclose or protect the area surrounding the eye. | Example: He wore googs while swimming. 他游泳时戴着护目镜。| Example:  She needs new googs for her skiing trip. 她滑雪旅行需要一副新的护目镜。| 近义词:eyewear, glasses, spectacles| 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

词源:  Likely a playful alteration of "goggles."  The term "goggles" itself has an uncertain origin, possibly related to the verb "goggle" meaning to stare with wide eyes.
