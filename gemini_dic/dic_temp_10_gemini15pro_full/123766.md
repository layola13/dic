moorland:/ˈmɔːrlənd/ (UK), /ˈmʊərlənd/ (US)| n. |荒野，高沼地，旷野|A type of open, uncultivated upland, often covered with heather, coarse grasses, and peat bogs.  Characterized by its acidic soil and low-growing vegetation.|例句：The hikers traversed the vast moorland.  徒步旅行者穿越了广阔的荒野。

例句：Sheep grazed peacefully on the moorland.  羊群在荒野上平静地吃草。|近义词：heath, moor, upland, fen|反义词:meadow, grassland|词性:noun
