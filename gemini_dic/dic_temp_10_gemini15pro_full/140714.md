omnific:/ɒmˈnɪfɪk/| adj.|无所不能的；全能的|Having unlimited power to create; all-powerful.  From Latin *omnifex* "all-making," from *omni-* "all" + *-fex* "maker."|God is often described as omnific. 上帝常被描述为无所不能的。|例句：The omnific power of the creator is beyond human comprehension.  造物主的全能力量是人类无法理解的。

例句：The artist attempted to capture the omnific nature of the universe in his painting.  这位艺术家试图在他的画作中捕捉宇宙的全能本质。|近义词：almighty, omnipotent, all-powerful|反义词：powerless, impotent, weak|词性:形容词
