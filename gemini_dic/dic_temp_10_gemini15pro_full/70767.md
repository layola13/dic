finesseful:/fɪˈnɛsfʊl/
adjective
巧妙的；精巧的；技艺高超的
Having or showing great skill and sensitivity.
She handled the delicate situation with finesseful diplomacy. 她以巧妙的技巧处理了这个微妙的局面。
近义词: skillful, adept, adroit, deft, subtle, artful
反义词: clumsy, awkward, inept, maladroit
词性: adjective

finesseful:/fɪˈnɛsfʊl/
adjective
优雅的；精致的
Characterized by refinement and elegance.
The dancer's finesseful movements captivated the audience.  舞者优雅的动作吸引了观众。
近义词: elegant, refined, graceful, polished
反义词: crude, rough, unrefined
词性: adjective
