noncosmopolitanism:/ˌnɒnkɒzməˈpɒlɪtənɪzəm/
n.
缺乏世界性；狭隘主义
The state of not being cosmopolitan; lack of interest in or understanding of other cultures.
His noncosmopolitanism was evident in his refusal to try any foreign food.  他的狭隘主义体现在他拒绝尝试任何外国食物。
insularity, parochialism
