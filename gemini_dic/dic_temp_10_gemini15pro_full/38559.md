concoct:/kənˈkɒkt/ (UK) /kənˈkɑːkt/ (US)| v. |调制，配制；编造，捏造|To make something, usually food or drink, by mixing different ingredients together; to invent a story or explanation, especially one that is not true.|She concocted a delicious soup from leftover vegetables. 她用剩下的蔬菜调制了一碗美味的汤。|近义词：devise, fabricate, invent, formulate, create|反义词：discover, find, reveal|词性:动词

concoction:/kənˈkɒkʃn/ (UK) /kənˈkɑːkʃn/ (US)| n. |调制品，混合物；编造的故事|Something, especially food or drink, made by mixing different ingredients together; an invented story or explanation, especially one that is not true.|The drink was a strange concoction of fruit juices and spices. 这种饮料是用果汁和香料调制成的奇怪混合物。|近义词：mixture, blend, combination, fabrication|反义词: component, element |词性:名词
