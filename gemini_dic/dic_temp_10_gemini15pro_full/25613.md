bumpingly:/ˈbʌmpɪŋli/
adverb
颠簸地，崎岖不平地
in a bumpy manner; with much jolting or shaking.
The car drove bumpingly along the dirt road. 汽车在土路上颠簸地行驶。
近义词: roughly, unevenly, jerkily
反义词: smoothly, evenly
词性: 副词
