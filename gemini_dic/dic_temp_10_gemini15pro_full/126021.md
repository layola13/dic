mussed:/ˈmʌst/| adj.|凌乱的，蓬乱的|Disarranged and untidy, typically of hair.|Her hair was mussed from the wind. 她的头发被风吹乱了。|近义词：disheveled, rumpled, tousled, untidy|反义词：neat, tidy, groomed, kempt|词性:形容词
词源:From muss (v.) + -ed, from Middle Dutch *musen "to make dirty," from *mose "marsh, bog."
