overtoil: /ˌoʊvərˈtɔɪl/ | n. | 过度操劳；过度工作 | the act of working too hard or too long; excessive work | The overtoil took a toll on his health. 过度操劳损害了他的健康。| 近义词: overwork, exhaustion | 反义词: relaxation, rest | 词性: 名词

词源:  From over- + toil. "Over-" is an Old English prefix meaning "above," "across," or "excessively." "Toil" comes from Old French "toillier," meaning "to stir, mix, or disturb," later evolving to mean "to work hard."
