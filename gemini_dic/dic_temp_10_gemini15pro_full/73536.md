foresightless:/ˈfɔːrsaɪtləs/| adj.|缺乏远见的，目光短浅的|Lacking foresight; without the ability to anticipate future needs or consequences.|The foresightless government failed to prepare for the economic crisis. 缺乏远见的政府未能为经济危机做好准备。|近义词:shortsighted, myopic, improvident, imprudent|反义词:foresighted, provident, prudent|词性:形容词
词源:foresightless 源于 foresight + -less。foresight 来自 fore- (before) + sight (vision)。 -less 是一个后缀，表示“没有”或“缺乏”。
