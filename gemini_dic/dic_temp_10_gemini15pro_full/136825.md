nonsidereal: /ˌnɒnsaɪˈdɪəriəl/ | adj. | 非恒星的 |  Relating to or measured by a method other than the movement of fixed stars; not sidereal.  Specifically, relating to or measured by the apparent diurnal motion of the Sun.  |  Nonsidereal time is based on the Sun's position. 非恒星时间是基于太阳的位置的。 |  The nonsidereal day is slightly longer than the sidereal day. 非恒星日比恒星日稍长。 |  solar, apparent | sidereal | adjective
