comparably:/ˈkɑːmpərəbli/ /ˈkɒmpərəbli/| adv.|类似地，可比地|In a way that is similar to something else, or that can be compared to something else.|House prices in the two areas are comparably high. 这两个地区的房价差不多高。|近义词：similarly, likewise, equivalently|反义词：differently, dissimilarly|词性:副词
词源: From the adjective "comparable" + the adverbial suffix "-ly". "Comparable" comes from the Late Latin "comparabilis", from "comparare" (to compare), from "com-" (together) + "parare" (to prepare, make ready).
