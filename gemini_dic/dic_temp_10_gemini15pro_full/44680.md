cruciformities:/ˌkruːsɪˈfɔːrmɪtiz/| n. |十字形物，十字形饰物|The architect incorporated several cruciformities into the design of the cathedral.  这位建筑师将几个十字形饰物融入到大教堂的设计中。|例句：The stained-glass windows featured intricate cruciformities.  彩色玻璃窗上有复杂的十字形图案。|近义词：crosses, crucifixes|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider shapes like circles, squares)|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *cruciformitas, from Latin cruciformis (“cross-shaped”), from crux (“cross”) + -formis (“-shaped, -formed”).
