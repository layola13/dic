assure:/əˈʃʊr/ (US), /əˈʃɔːr/ (UK)| vt.|向…保证；使确信；|To tell someone something positively to remove any doubt.|I assure you that I will be there. 我向你保证我会在那里的。|近义词：guarantee, promise, convince|反义词：doubt, question|verb

assure:/əˈʃʊr/ (US), /əˈʃɔːr/ (UK)| vt.|使（某事）确定；确保；|To make (something) certain to happen.|The company assured the safety of its products. 公司确保了其产品的安全性。|近义词：ensure, secure, guarantee|反义词：endanger, jeopardize|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English: from Old French *assurer*, based on Latin ad- ‘to’ + securus ‘secure’.
