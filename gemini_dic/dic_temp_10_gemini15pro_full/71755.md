flea-marketeer:/ˌfliː ˈmɑːrkɪˌtɪər/
n.
跳蚤市场摊贩，在跳蚤市场卖东西的人
A person who sells goods at a flea market.
She's a seasoned flea-marketeer, always knowing how to get the best deals. 她是一位经验丰富的跳蚤市场摊贩，总是知道如何获得最划算的交易。
He became a flea-marketeer after retiring, selling his old collections. 他退休后成了跳蚤市场摊贩，卖掉了他以前的收藏品。
vendor, seller, trader
buyer, customer
noun
