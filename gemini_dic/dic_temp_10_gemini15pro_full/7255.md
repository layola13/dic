anglocentric:/ˌæŋɡloʊˈsɛntrɪk/
adjective
以英国为中心的；英格兰中心的
Focused on England or the English, often to the exclusion or detriment of other cultures.
Many older history books have a very anglocentric view of the world. 很多古老的历史书都带有非常以英国为中心的视角来看待世界。

The curriculum was criticized for its anglocentric bias.  这门课程因其以英国为中心的偏见而受到批评。

近义词:  British-centered, Anglo-Saxon-centered
反义词:  multicultural, global, cosmopolitan
词性: adjective
