monk:/mʌŋk/|n.|僧侣，修道士|A man who has withdrawn from the world to live a religious life, especially as a member of a community.|The monk devoted his life to prayer and meditation.  这个僧侣毕生致力于祈祷和冥想。|synonyms: friar, brother, religious|antonyms: layman, secular|noun
