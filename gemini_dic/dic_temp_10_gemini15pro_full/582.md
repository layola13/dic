aboundingly:/əˈbaʊndɪŋli/
adverb
极其丰富地；大量地
in a way that shows there is a very large quantity of something
Examples abound of this phenomenon.  这种现象的例子比比皆是。
Opportunities abound for skilled workers in the computer industry.  计算机行业熟练工人的机会比比皆是。

近义词: profusely, copiously, plentifully, abundantly
反义词: sparsely, scarcely, meagerly
词性: adverb

词源:
From abound + -ing + -ly.  Abound comes from Old French abonder, from Latin abundare "to overflow, abound," from ab- "from, away" + undare "to surge, overflow," related to unda "wave."

USAGE->
Fish aboundingly in this river. = 这条河里鱼很多。
The garden was aboundingly filled with colorful flowers. = 花园里开满了五颜六色的花。


PHRASE->
(While not common in phrases, it can be used with intensifying adverbs):
Most aboundingly: 最大量地
Quite aboundingly: 非常丰富地
Truly aboundingly: 的确非常多地
Exceptionally aboundingly: 异常丰富地
Remarkably aboundingly: 显著地大量
