devisen:/dəˈviːzən/ (German pronunciation)| n. |外汇；外币|Foreign currency; foreign exchange.  This word is primarily used in German.  It is less common in English, where "foreign currency" or "foreign exchange" are preferred.|例句：Die Firma handelt mit Devisen. (German) The company trades in foreign currency.|近义词：foreign exchange, foreign currency, currency|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps domestic currency)|词性:noun
