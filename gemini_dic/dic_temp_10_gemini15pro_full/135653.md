nonpredictive: /ˌnɒnprɪˈdɪktɪv/ | /ˌnɑːnprɪˈdɪktɪv/ | adjective | 不可预测的 |  Formed from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the adjective "predictive" (meaning "able to be predicted").
