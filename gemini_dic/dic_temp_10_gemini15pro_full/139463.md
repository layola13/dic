obstetrical: /əbˈstet.rɪ.kəl/ | adj. | 产科的；助产的 | Relating to childbirth and the processes associated with it. |  Obstetrical care is crucial for a healthy pregnancy. 产科护理对健康怀孕至关重要。

The obstetrical ward of the hospital was very busy. 医院的产科病房非常繁忙。

近义词: childbirth,  maternity, birthing, parturition | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective


词源: From Late Latin *obstetricalis, from Latin obstetrix "midwife," from obstare "stand before, stand opposite to, assist," from ob "before, toward" + stare "to stand."
