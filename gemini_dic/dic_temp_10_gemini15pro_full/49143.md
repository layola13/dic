deject:/dɪˈdʒɛkt/
verb
使沮丧，使灰心
To make sad or disheartened; depress.
The news of her friend's illness dejected her. 她朋友生病的消息使她沮丧。
He was dejected by his failure to get the job.  他因未能得到这份工作而感到沮丧。
近义词:depress, dishearten, dispirit, sadden
反义词:elate, cheer, encourage
词性:verb
