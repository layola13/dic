agria:/ˈæɡriə/| adj.|野生的，野蛮的，粗野的|Wild, uncultivated, growing wild;  (of plants) growing wild, especially as opposed to cultivated;  (of animals) wild, especially as opposed to domesticated;  (of people or their behaviour) rough, uncivilized, or unrestrained.|The agria rose had a more vibrant color than the cultivated variety. 野玫瑰比栽培品种的颜色更加鲜艳。|Synonyms: wild, feral, untamed, savage, ferocious|Antonyms: cultivated, domesticated, tame, civilized, refined|adjective

agria:/ˈæɡriə/| n.|野生植物或动物|A wild plant or animal.|The agria in the forest provided food for the local tribes. 森林里的野生动植物为当地部落提供了食物。|Synonyms: wildling, feral|Antonyms: cultivar, domesticate|noun
