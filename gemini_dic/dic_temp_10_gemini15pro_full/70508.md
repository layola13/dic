fileable:/ˈfaɪləbl/| adj.|可归档的，适于归档的|Suitable for filing; capable of being filed.|例句：The document is fileable and should be stored appropriately.  这份文件可以归档，应该妥善保存。|例句：These receipts are all fileable for tax purposes. 这些收据都可以归档用于税务目的。|近义词：recordable, storable, archivable|反义词: unorganizable, unmanageable|词性:形容词

词源:  From "file" (verb, meaning "to arrange systematically") + "-able" (suffix meaning "capable of being").  来源于动词 "file"（意为“系统地排列”）+ 后缀 "-able"（意为“能够被...”）。
