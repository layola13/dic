attunement:/əˈtjuːnmənt/
n.|协调，一致，调谐|a state of being in harmony; a feeling of being "on the same wavelength" as someone else. |She felt a deep attunement with nature. 她感到与自然深深的协调。|synonyms: harmony, resonance, sympathy, rapport|antonyms: discord, dissonance, conflict|noun

attunement:/əˈtjuːnmənt/
n.|调音，校音|the process of making a musical instrument play at the correct pitch. |The piano tuner performed an attunement of the instrument. 钢琴调音师对乐器进行了调音。|synonyms: tuning, adjustment, calibration|antonyms: detuning|noun
