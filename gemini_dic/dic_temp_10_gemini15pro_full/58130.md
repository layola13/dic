dropt:/drɒpt/ | v. | dropped（drop的过去式和过去分词）| dropped is the past tense and past participle of the verb "drop." It means to fall or allow something to fall, to reduce or decrease, or to stop doing or participating in something. | I dropped my phone on the floor. 我把手机掉在地上了。| Synonyms: fallen, lowered, decreased, discontinued | Antonyms: risen, increased, continued | verb

dropt:/drɒpt/ | adj. |  obsolete form of dropped |  An archaic form of dropped, no longer in common use. | He hath dropt his blade. 他已经丢下了他的刀。（古英语用法，现已过时）| Synonyms: dropped | Antonyms: raised | adjective
