endosymbionts:/ˌɛndoʊˌsɪmbaɪˈɑːnts/ | n. | 内共生体 | An organism that lives within the body or cells of another organism (the host) in a mutually beneficial relationship. |  Mitochondria and chloroplasts are believed to be ancient endosymbionts of eukaryotic cells. 线粒体和叶绿体被认为是真核细胞的古代内共生体。| synonyms: symbiont, mutualist | antonyms: parasite, pathogen | noun
