couchlike:/ˈkaʊtʃlaɪk/
adjective
像沙发的；类似躺椅的
Resembling a couch; like a couch in form, appearance, or comfort.
The beanbag chair was large and couchlike, perfect for lounging.  豆袋椅又大又像沙发，非常适合 lounging。
The pile of cushions formed a couchlike seat. 这堆垫子形成了一个像沙发一样的座位。
synonyms:sofa-like, settee-like, lounge-like
antonyms:chairlike, upright, rigid
词性:adjective
