imitatrix:/ˈɪmɪteɪtrɪks/
n.
女模仿者；女演员
a female imitator, especially an actress.
She was a brilliant imitatrix of famous actresses. 她是一位出色模仿著名女演员的人。
近义词：mimic, impersonator, copycat
反义词：original, innovator
词性：noun

Etymology:
From Latin *imitator* + *-trix* (feminine agent suffix).  *Imitator* itself derives from *imitari* "to imitate."
