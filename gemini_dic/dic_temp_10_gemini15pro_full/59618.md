eccentric:/ɪkˈsɛntrɪk/ | adj. n. |古怪的，反常的；古怪的人| deviating from the recognized or customary character, practice, etc.; peculiar; odd;  a person of odd or unconventional habits|例句：His eccentric behavior amused everyone. 他的古怪行为逗乐了大家。|近义词：odd, peculiar, strange, unusual, bizarre, unconventional|反义词：normal, conventional, ordinary, regular|词性:形容词,名词
eccentrics:/ɪkˈsɛntrɪks/ | n. |古怪的人|  people of odd or unconventional habits (plural of eccentric)|例句：The town was known for its eccentrics.  这个小镇以其古怪的居民而闻名。|近义词：oddballs, nonconformists, characters|反义词：conformists|词性:名词
