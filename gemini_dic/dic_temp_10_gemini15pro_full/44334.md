croquette:/kroʊˈkɛt/ | n. |炸丸子，可乐饼|A small roll of minced meat, fish, or vegetables, coated in breadcrumbs and deep-fried.|例句：I love potato croquettes. 我喜欢土豆可乐饼。

例句：The chef prepared salmon croquettes as an appetizer.  厨师准备了鲑鱼可乐饼作为开胃菜。

|近义词：fritter, rissole, patty|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps soup or stew as a contrasting dish)|词性:noun
