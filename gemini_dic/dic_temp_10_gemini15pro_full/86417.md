heedfully:/ˈhiːdfəli/ | adv. | 注意地，小心地 | In a careful and attentive manner, paying close attention to details and potential risks. | She heedfully crossed the busy street, looking both ways twice. 她小心翼翼地穿过繁忙的街道，左右看了看两次。|  He heedfully followed the instructions, ensuring he didn't miss a step. 他小心翼翼地按照说明操作，确保没有漏掉任何一个步骤。| 近义词: carefully, attentively, cautiously, prudently | 反义词: carelessly, negligently, inattentively | 词性: 副词

词源: From "heedful" + "-ly."  "Heedful" comes from the Old English "hēdan" (to take heed, observe), from Proto-Germanic "*hauzijan" (to hear, perceive).
