creational:/kriˈeɪʃənl/| adj.|创建的，创造性的|Relating to creation or the act of creating something; having the power to create.|例句：Creational design patterns provide ways to instantiate objects. 创建型设计模式提供了实例化对象的方法。|近义词：creative, generative, productive|反义词：destructive, uncreative|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Late Latin *creationale*, from Latin *creatio* (creation) + *-alis* (suffix forming adjectives of relation).  The English word "creational" is derived from "creation," which ultimately comes from the Latin verb *creare* "to create, produce, bring forth."
