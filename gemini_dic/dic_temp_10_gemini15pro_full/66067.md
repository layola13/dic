excalceation: /ˌɛkskælˈseɪʃən/
n.
脱鞋; 去除鞋子的行为;  
The act of taking off one's shoes.
The monk practiced excalceation before entering the temple.  僧侣在进入寺庙前进行了脱鞋仪式。
Removal of footwear, especially as a sign of respect.  脱鞋，尤指作为尊敬的标志。
The museum requires excalceation before entering certain exhibits.  博物馆要求在进入某些展品前脱鞋。
近义词:  barefootness, discalceation
反义词:  shoeing, footwear
