bayonet:/ˈbeɪənɪt/ | n. |刺刀|A blade that can be attached to a rifle for use in hand-to-hand combat. |The soldier fixed his bayonet to his rifle. 士兵把刺刀装到步枪上。|
bayonet:/ˈbeɪənɪt/ | vt. |用刺刀刺|To stab or kill someone with a bayonet. |The enemy soldier was bayoneted during the charge. 敌方士兵在冲锋中被刺刀刺死。|
bayonet:/ˈbeɪənɪt/ | n. |卡口式连接（如某些灯泡、镜头）|A type of fastening or connection, as for some light bulbs or lenses. |The camera lens uses a bayonet mount. 这个相机镜头使用卡口式安装。|
bayonet:/ˈbeɪənɪt/ | vt. |用卡口式连接固定|To fasten or connect using a bayonet fitting. |He bayoneted the lens onto the camera. 他把镜头卡在相机上。|

近义词:blade, knife, dagger (for the weapon sense), mount, fitting (for the connection sense)
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun, verb
