imagesetter:/ˈɪmɪdʒˌsɛtər/
noun
照排机，图像输出机
A machine that produces very high-quality text and graphics, usually on photographic paper, using a laser.
The imagesetter produced crisp, clear output for the brochure.  照排机为小册子制作了清晰的输出。
The newspaper used an imagesetter to create the film for the printing plates. 报社使用照排机制作印刷版的胶片。
Synonyms: typesetter, output device, laser printer
Antonyms: typewriter, manual typesetting machine
词性: 名词
