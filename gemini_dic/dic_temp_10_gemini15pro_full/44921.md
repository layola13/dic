cryptobranchiate: /ˌkrɪptəˈbræŋkiət/ | n. | 隐鳃动物 | An animal belonging to the Cryptobranchiidae family, characterized by hidden gills. | Cryptobranchiates are found in Asia and North America. 隐鳃动物分布在亚洲和北美。 | Synonym:  giant salamander, hellbender | Antonym: (No specific antonym exists) | noun
