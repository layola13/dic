nonsusceptiblenesses:/ˌnɒnsəˌsɛptɪˈbɪlɪtɪz/
noun
非易感性；不易受影响的状态
The state or quality of being nonsusceptible; lack of susceptibility.
The researchers studied the nonsusceptiblenesses of certain plants to specific diseases. 研究人员研究了某些植物对特定疾病的非易感性。
Near Synonyms: immunities, resistances, invulnerabilities
Near Antonyms: susceptibilities, vulnerabilities, sensitivities
词性: 名词
