methodization:/ˌmɛθədaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 方法化；系统化 | The act of reducing to method; systematic arrangement or systematization. | The methodization of the company's procedures increased efficiency. 公司程序的系统化提高了效率。| synonyms: systematization, organization, arrangement, ordering, structuring | antonyms: disorganization, chaos, disorder | noun

method:/ˈmɛθəd/ | n. | 方法；方式 | A particular form of procedure for accomplishing or approaching something, especially a systematic or established one. |  The new teaching method is very effective. 新的教学方法很有效。| synonyms: procedure, process, technique, system, approach, way, manner | antonyms: randomness, aimlessness | noun
