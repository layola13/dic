bisection:/baɪˈsɛkʃən/| n. |二等分；平分；二分法|The bisection of a line segment creates two equal parts.  一条线段的二等分将其分成两等份。|The bisection method is a root-finding algorithm that repeatedly bisects an interval and then selects the subinterval in which a root must lie for further processing. 二分法是一种求根算法，它反复地将一个区间二等分，然后选择包含根的子区间进行进一步处理。|近义词：division, halving|反义词: combination, unification|noun
