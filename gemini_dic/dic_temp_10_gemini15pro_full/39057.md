conformate:/kənˈfɔːmət/ | adj. n. | 整形的，顺形的；整合面 |  (adj.) Corresponding in form or shape; adapted; (n.) A geological formation that lies between older and younger formations and is parallel to them. |例句：The sedimentary rocks are conformate with the underlying strata. 这些沉积岩与下伏地层整合。| 近义词: conformable, agreeable, consistent | 反义词: unconformable, discordant | 词性: adjective, noun

conformate:/kənˈfɔːmeɪt/ | v. | 使一致，使遵守 | To bring into conformity; to comply with. |例句：He conformed his behavior to the company's rules. 他使自己的行为符合公司的规定。| 近义词: comply, adapt, adjust | 反义词: disobey, resist, defy | 词性: verb
