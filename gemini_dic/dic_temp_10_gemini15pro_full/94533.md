|n.|强加，负担，税款，（书籍、杂志等的）排版|The impositions of the new tax were felt by everyone. 新税的负担人人都有感觉。|Synonyms: burdens, levies, taxes, constraints, demands|Antonyms: reliefs, exemptions, freedoms|noun

imposition:/ˌɪmpəˈzɪʃən/
|n.|强加，负担，税款，（书籍、杂志等的）排版|Asking for help felt like an imposition. 请求帮助感觉像是一种强加于人。|Synonyms: burden, levy, tax, constraint, demand|Antonyms: relief, exemption, freedom|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English imposicioun, from Old French imposicion, from Latin impositio (“an imposing, infliction”), from imponere (“to impose, place upon”), from in- (“in, on”) + ponere (“to put, place”).
