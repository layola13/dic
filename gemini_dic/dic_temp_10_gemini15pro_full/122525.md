modable:/ˈmɒdəbl/ (US) /ˈmɒdəbəl/ (UK)| adj.|可修改的，可改装的|Able to be modded or modified; adaptable.  Something that can be easily changed or adjusted, often referring to video games or software where users can alter the original content.|例句：The game is highly modable, allowing players to create their own levels and characters.  这个游戏具有很高的可修改性，允许玩家创建自己的关卡和角色。

例句：The car is modable, with many aftermarket parts available for customization. 这辆车可以改装，有许多售后市场零件可用于定制。|近义词：modifiable, adaptable, customizable, alterable|反义词：unmodifiable, fixed, unalterable|词性: adjective
