anigh:/əˈnaɪ/
adverb
靠近，附近
near, close (in place or time)
The ship was anchored anigh the shore. 这艘船锚定在靠近海岸的地方。
近义词:near, close, nearby, nigh
反义词:far, distant, remote
词性:副词

Etymology:
From Middle English aneigh, anigh, from Old English on nēawiste (“in nearness”), from on (“on”) + nēawest (“nearest”).  More at on, nigh.
