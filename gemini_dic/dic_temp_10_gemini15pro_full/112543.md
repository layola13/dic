loved:/lʌvd/
verb|爱过；喜爱过|past tense and past participle of love|
She loved her children dearly. 她深爱她的孩子们。
He loved playing football when he was young. 他年轻时喜欢踢足球。
近义词:adored, cherished, liked
反义词:hated, despised, disliked
词性:verb

loved:/lʌvd/
adjective|被爱的；心爱的|Used to describe someone or something that is loved very much.|
My loved ones are always in my thoughts. 我的亲人一直在我心中。
This is my loved teddy bear from childhood. 这是我童年时期心爱的泰迪熊。
近义词:beloved, cherished, adored
反义词:hated, despised, disliked
词性:adjective
