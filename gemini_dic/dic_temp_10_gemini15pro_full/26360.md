by-ear:/baɪ ˈɪr/ | adv. | 凭听觉；不看乐谱 | By listening to the music and imitating it, without reading musical notation. | She can play the piano by ear. 她可以不看乐谱弹钢琴。 | Near synonyms: aurally, by hearing | Antonyms: by sight, visually | Adverb

by-ear:/baɪ ˈɪr/ | adj. | 凭听觉的；不看乐谱的 | Played or learned by listening to the music and imitating it, without reading musical notation. | She has a by-ear knowledge of music. 她对音乐的了解是凭听觉的。 | Near synonyms: aural, auditory | Antonyms: visual | Adjective
