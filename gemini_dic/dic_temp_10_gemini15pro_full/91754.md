hyperemesis:/ˌhaɪpərˈɛməsɪs/| n.|妊娠剧吐|Severe nausea and vomiting during pregnancy, much more extreme than typical morning sickness. |She suffered from hyperemesis throughout her first trimester. 她整个孕早期都患有妊娠剧吐。| 近义词:severe vomiting, excessive nausea| 反义词: None (lack of vomiting would be the opposite condition, but there's no single word for that related to pregnancy)|词性:名词

morning sickness:/ˈmɔːrnɪŋ ˌsɪknɪs/| n.|孕吐|Nausea and vomiting during pregnancy, typically in the morning. |Many women experience morning sickness in the first few months of pregnancy. 很多女性在怀孕的头几个月都会出现孕吐。| 近义词:nausea, vomiting in pregnancy| 反义词: None|词性:名词
