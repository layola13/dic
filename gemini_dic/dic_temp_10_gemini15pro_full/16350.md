barbastel: /bɑːrˈbæstəl/ | n. | 手工艺品；小玩意儿 |  A small object, especially one made by hand, often considered a trinket or a piece of craftwork.  It often implies a playful or whimsical nature. This word is primarily used in German. |  She made some charming barbastel for the Christmas market. 她为圣诞市场制作了一些迷人的小工艺品。 | synonyms: craft, trinket, knick-knack, bauble, gadget | antonyms:  masterpiece, essential, necessity | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Barbastel*, a blend of *Barbara* (a common German female name, often associated with traditional crafts) and *Basteln* (to craft, to tinker).
