mirthlessness:/ˈmɜːrθləsnəs/
noun
缺乏欢乐, 缺乏乐趣, 严肃
lack of mirth; seriousness.
Her mirthlessness made the party feel somber. 她的严肃让派对气氛沉闷。
近义词:somberness, seriousness, gravity, glumness
反义词:mirth, cheerfulness, gaiety, joviality
词性:n.

Etymology:
From mirthless + -ness.  Mirthless from mirth + -less. Mirth from Old English *myrgþu (unattested), from myrge "pleasant, merry, sweet, agreeable" (from Proto-Germanic *murgijaz) + -þu.
