chintziest:/ˈtʃɪn.t͡si.ɪst/
adjective
最便宜的，最吝啬的
superlative adjective of chintzy: cheaply made or of poor quality; also : stingy, miserly
Example:  She wore a chintziest dress to the party. 她穿着一条最便宜的裙子去参加派对。

Example: He gave the chintziest tip I've ever seen. 他给的小费是我见过最少的。

近义词: cheapest, stingiest, miserliest
反义词: most expensive, most generous, most lavish
词性: adjective
