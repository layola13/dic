discommonize:/dɪskəˈmɒnaɪz/
v.
使不常见；使不普通；废除
To make uncommon; to deprive of common rights or status.
The king discommonized the grazing lands, depriving the peasants of their traditional rights. 国王废除了牧场的公共权利，剥夺了农民的传统权利。

discommonize:/dɪskəˈmɒnaɪz/
verb
使不常见；使不普通；废除
To make uncommon; to deprive of common rights or status.
New technologies have discommonized traditional skills like weaving. 新技术使得像编织这样的传统技能变得不再常见。
近义词: abolish, revoke, repeal, annul, invalidate
反义词: authorize, establish, legalize, validate
词性: verb
