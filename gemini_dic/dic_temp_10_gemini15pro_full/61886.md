emptier:/ˈemptiər (US), ˈemtiər (UK)| adj.|更空的，更空虚的|Comparative form of empty: more empty than another.  Having less inside; containing a smaller quantity.|The cookie jar was emptier than I expected.  饼干罐比我想象的要空。|Near Synonyms: more vacant, more barren, more desolate|Near Antonyms: fuller, more replete|adjective

empty:/ˈempti (US), ˈemti (UK)| adj. n. v.|空的，空虚的；空的东西；使…变空|Adj: Containing nothing; not filled or occupied. Noun: An empty container, space, etc. Verb: To remove all the contents of something.|Adj: The room was empty. 房间是空的。 Noun: Return the empties to the store.  把空瓶子退回商店。 Verb:  Empty the trash, please. 请把垃圾倒掉。|Near Synonyms: Adj: vacant, void, bare; Noun: vacuum; Verb: evacuate, clear|Near Antonyms: Adj: full, replete; Verb: fill|adjective, noun, verb
