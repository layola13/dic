agilely:/ˈædʒɪlli/| adv.|敏捷地，灵活地|In a quick and nimble manner; easily.|The dancer moved agilely across the stage. 舞者在舞台上敏捷地移动。|Synonyms: nimbly, deftly, quickly, swiftly|Antonyms: clumsily, awkwardly, slowly|adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From agile + -ly. Agile comes from French, ultimately from Latin agilis "nimble, quick," from agere "to drive, lead, act, do."
