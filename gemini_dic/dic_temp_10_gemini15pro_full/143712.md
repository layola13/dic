outlined:/ˈaʊtlaɪnd/ /ˈaʊtˌlaɪnd/ | v. adj.|概述，描绘；轮廓分明的|Outlined is the past participle and past tense of the verb "outline." As an adjective, it means having a clearly defined shape or form. | He outlined the plan to the board. 他向董事会概述了这个计划。|Synonyms: sketched, summarized, delineated|Antonyms: detailed, elaborated|verb

outlined:/ˈaʊtlaɪnd/ /ˈaʊtˌlaɪnd/ | adj.|轮廓分明的，概述的|Outlined describes something with a clearly defined shape or form, often referring to something that has been drawn or described in a general way. |The mountains were outlined against the sky. 群山在天空中轮廓分明。|Synonyms: defined, distinct, clear-cut|Antonyms: blurred, indistinct, vague|adjective
