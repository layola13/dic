aphasy: /əˈfeɪʒə/ (US), /əˈfeɪziə/ (UK)| n. |失语症|Loss of ability to understand or express speech, caused by brain damage.|Example: After the stroke, he suffered from aphasia and struggled to communicate. 中风后，他患上了失语症，难以沟通。

Example:  Her aphasia made it difficult for her to participate in conversations. 她的失语症使她难以参与对话。|近义词：dysphasia, speechlessness|反义词: fluency|词性: noun
