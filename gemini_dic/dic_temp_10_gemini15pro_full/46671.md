dampener:/ˈdæmpənər/ (US) /ˈdæmpnər/ (UK)| n.|抑制因素；扫兴的人或事|Something that reduces enthusiasm, excitement, or enjoyment.|The rain put a dampener on our picnic.  这场雨给我们的野餐扫了兴。|Synonyms: downer, wet blanket, killjoy, spoiler|Antonyms: stimulant, boost, encouragement|Noun

dampener:/ˈdæmpənər/ (US) /ˈdæmpnər/ (UK)| n.|减震器；阻尼器|A device that reduces vibrations or oscillations.|The car's dampeners need replacing. 这辆车的减震器需要更换。|Synonyms: shock absorber, damper|Antonyms: amplifier, intensifier|Noun
