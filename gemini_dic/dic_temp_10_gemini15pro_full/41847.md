corymbed: /ˈkɔːrɪmbd/ | adj. |伞状花序的，成伞房状的 | Arranged in or resembling a corymb (a flat-topped or convex flower cluster in which the outer flowers open first). | The corymbed flowers of the yarrow plant attract many butterflies.  蓍草的伞房状花序吸引了许多蝴蝶。| 近义词: corymbose, umbellate | 反义词: (No common direct antonym, possibly racemose for a different inflorescence type) | 词性: adjective
corymb: /ˈkɔːrɪmb/ | n. |伞房花序 | A flat-topped or convex inflorescence in which the individual flower stalks arise at different points on the main stem but reach approximately the same level. | The elderberry bush has a beautiful corymb of white flowers. 接骨木灌木有一束美丽的白色伞房花序。| 近义词: corymbose inflorescence, flat-topped cluster | 反义词: (No common direct antonym, possibly raceme) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *corymbus, from Latin corymbus "cluster of ivy berries," from Greek korymbos "top, cluster of flowers or fruit," perhaps related to korys "helmet."
