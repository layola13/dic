boraces: /ˈbɔːreɪsiːz/
noun
硼酸盐
borates, salts of boric acid.
Boraces are commonly used in detergents and fertilizers. 硼酸盐通常用于洗涤剂和肥料中。
synonyms: borate salts
antonyms: N/A
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From borax + -ace, ultimately from Arabic بورق (būraq, “borax”).
