circuiter:/ˈsɜːrkɪtər/ | n. |巡回者；巡回演出者；巡回法官 | A person who travels a circuit, especially a judge or a performer. | The circuiter visited several towns during his tour.  巡回法官在他的巡回期间访问了几个城镇。| Example: The comedy circuiter performed in small clubs across the country.  这位喜剧巡回演员在全国各地的小俱乐部演出。 | Synonyms: traveler, itinerant, journeyman, tourist, wayfarer | Antonyms: resident, settler, stay-at-home | noun
