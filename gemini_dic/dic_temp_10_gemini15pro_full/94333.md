impierceable:/ɪmˈpɪərsəbl/
adj.
不可穿透的；无法刺穿的；不能渗透的
Not able to be pierced or penetrated.
The knight's armor was impierceable. 骑士的盔甲坚不可摧。
The fortress boasted impierceable walls.  这座堡垒拥有坚不可摧的城墙。

近义词: impenetrable, impervious, invulnerable
反义词: penetrable, permeable, vulnerable
词性: adjective

词源:
From im- (not) + pierce + -able (capable of being).  Ultimately from Latin *pertusiāre, frequentative of pertūsāre “to beat through, pierce.”
