drop-in:/ˈdrɒpɪn/ (British) /ˈdrɑːpɪn/ (American)| adj. n.| 顺便拜访的；即兴的；无需预约的；顺便来访者；即兴表演|A drop-in is someone who visits another person without having arranged the visit in advance. As an adjective, it describes something that can be attended or used without making an appointment.  It comes from the phrasal verb "drop in" meaning to visit casually or unexpectedly.|例句：I'm having a drop-in clinic this afternoon. 我今天下午有个即时门诊。|近义词：casual, informal, impromptu, unscheduled, unexpected|反义词：scheduled, planned, by appointment|词性: adjective, noun
