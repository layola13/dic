calligraphically:/ˌkælɪˈɡræfɪkli/
adv.
书法地，用书法写成地
in a way that relates to calligraphy; beautifully and carefully.
The invitation was calligraphically addressed. 请柬上的地址是用书法写成的。
近义词: elegantly, artistically, beautifully
反义词: crudely, sloppily, messily
词性: 副词

词源:  From calligraphy + -ically. Calligraphy comes from Greek kalligraphia "beautiful writing," from kallos "beauty" + graphein "to write."
