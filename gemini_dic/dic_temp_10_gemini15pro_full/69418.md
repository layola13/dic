feelers:/ˈfiːlərz/| n.|触角；试探；探子|Tentative inquiries or suggestions to ascertain someone's views or intentions.  They are often used to gauge public opinion or test the waters before making a decision.|例句：I put out some feelers to see if there was any interest in my proposal. 我试探了一下，看看是否有人对我的提议感兴趣。

例句：The government sent out feelers to gauge public reaction to the proposed policy change. 政府试探公众对拟议的政策变化的反应。|近义词：antennae, probes, inquiries, overtures|反义词:declarations, pronouncements|词性:noun
