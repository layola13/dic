freeholds:/ˈfriːhoʊldz/
noun
永久产权；不动产
An estate in land, a building, etc., held for life or with the right to pass it on through inheritance.
Example 1: They own the freehold of the property. 他们拥有该房产的永久产权。
Example 2:  The family sold the freehold to a developer. 这家人把永久产权卖给了一位开发商。

近义词:freehold property, fee simple
反义词:leasehold
词性:noun
