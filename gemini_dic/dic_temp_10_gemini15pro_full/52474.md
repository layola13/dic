dieter:/ˈdaɪətər/| n.|节食者，减肥者|A person who is trying to lose weight by eating less food or following a special diet.|例句：The dieter carefully tracked their calorie intake.  这位节食者仔细记录了他们的卡路里摄入量。

例句：The dieter avoided sugary drinks and processed foods.  这位节食者避免含糖饮料和加工食品。|近义词：slimmer, weight watcher|反义词:glutton, overeater|词性:noun
