half-minded:/ˌhæfˈmaɪndɪd/ | adj. | 三心二意的，优柔寡断的，意志薄弱的 | Lacking in resolution or determination; irresolute; wavering. | He was half-minded about accepting the job offer.  他对接受这份工作犹豫不决。 |  Example: She was half-minded about going to the party, torn between wanting to see her friends and needing to study. 她对于去参加派对三心二意，在想见朋友和需要学习之间左右为难。|Synonyms: hesitant, indecisive, irresolute, wavering, ambivalent | Antonyms: resolute, decisive, determined, committed | 词性: 形容词

half-minded:/ˌhæfˈmaɪndɪd/ | adj. | 缺乏热情的，不专心的 | Lacking enthusiasm or wholeheartedness. | His half-minded attempts at studying resulted in poor grades. 他不专心的学习导致成绩很差。 | Example: He gave a half-minded performance, clearly not invested in the play. 他的表演缺乏热情，显然对这部戏并不投入。 | Synonyms: unenthusiastic, apathetic, lukewarm, indifferent | Antonyms: enthusiastic, passionate, dedicated, committed | 词性: 形容词
