lumpectomies:/ˌlʌmˈpɛktəmiz/
| n. |乳房肿块切除术|The surgical removal of a lump, typically a tumor, from the breast, preserving as much healthy breast tissue as possible.  It is commonly performed to diagnose or treat breast cancer. |She underwent two lumpectomies before opting for a mastectomy. 她做了两次肿块切除术后才选择乳房切除术。|Synonym: partial mastectomy, tylectomy |Antonym: mastectomy
|noun
