disestablishment:/dɪsɪˈstæblɪʃmənt/
n.
撤销，废除（机构、法律、习俗等）
The act of officially ending a system, practice, or institution.
The disestablishment of the Church of England is a complex issue.  英国教会的废除是一个复杂的问题。
abolition, termination, removal
establishment, institution
