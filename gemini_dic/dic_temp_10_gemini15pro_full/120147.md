mincemeat:/ˈmɪnsˌmiːt/| n. |肉馅，碎肉；（喻）惨败，彻底失败|A mixture of chopped dried fruit, spices, suet, and sometimes meat, typically used in mince pies. (字面意思：一种由切碎的干果、香料、板油，有时还有肉混合而成的馅料，通常用于肉馅饼。)  Figuratively, a state of utter defeat or ruin. (比喻义：彻底失败或毁灭的状态)|例句：The chef prepared the mincemeat for the pies.  厨师准备了馅饼的肉馅。

例句：After the scandal, his reputation was mincemeat.  丑闻之后，他的名声彻底败坏。|近义词：ground meat, hash; ruin, destruction|反义词: victory, success|词性:noun
