fumed:/fjuːmd/| adj.|熏制的；冒烟的；愤怒的| filled with or smelling of smoke; very angry|例句：The fumed oak cabinets gave the kitchen a rustic feel. 熏制的橡木橱柜给厨房增添了乡村气息。

例句：She was absolutely fumed when she heard the news.  听到这个消息她非常生气。|近义词：angered, enraged, infuriated, smoky, smoke-filled|反义词：calm, pleased, delighted|词性:形容词

fume:/fjuːm/| n. v.|烟；烟雾；熏蒸；冒烟；发怒|gas, smoke, or vapor, especially if harmful or irritating; to emit fumes; to be very angry|例句：The fumes from the car exhaust filled the air. 汽车尾气的烟雾弥漫在空气中。

例句：He was fuming about the unfair treatment he received. 他对受到的不公平待遇感到愤怒。|近义词：smoke, vapor, rage, rant|反义词：calm, soothe|词性:名词；动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *fumen, from Anglo-Norman fumer, Old French fumer (“to smoke, steam, exhale”), from Latin fumus (“smoke, steam, vapor”).
