dankly:/ˈdæŋkli/| adv.|潮湿地，阴冷地|in a damp, chilly, and unpleasant way|The air hung dankly in the cellar.  空气在地窖里阴冷潮湿。|例句：The dungeon smelled dankly of mildew. 地牢里散发着霉菌的潮湿气味。
