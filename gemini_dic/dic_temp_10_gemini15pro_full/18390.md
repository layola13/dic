belomancy: /ˈbeləˌmænsi/ | n. | 占卜箭术；箭卜 | Divination by means of arrows, typically involving observing the direction or position of arrows shot or drawn at random.  |  Example:  Belomancy was practiced in ancient times to predict the outcome of battles.  占卜箭术在古代被用来预测战争的结果。

Example:  Though belomancy is rarely practiced today, historical accounts describe its use in various cultures. 虽然箭卜现在很少有人使用了，但历史记载描述了它在不同文化中的使用。 | Synonym: divination, augury | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but could consider "scientific prediction") | noun
