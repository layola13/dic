pasturing:/ˈpɑːstʃərɪŋ/ /ˈpæstʃərɪŋ/
n.|放牧|The act of grazing livestock on pasture.|The farmer was pasturing his cattle in the field. 农民正在田里放牧他的牛。|grazing, herding|confining, enclosing
v.|放牧|To graze livestock on pasture.|They pasture their sheep on the hillsides. 他们在山坡上放牧羊群。|graze, herd|confine, enclose


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *pasturen*, from Old French *pasturer*, from Late Latin *pasturare* "to feed," from Latin *pastus*, past participle of *pascere* "to feed, pasture."
