cymar:/ˈsaɪmɑːr/| n.|透明薄衫，女睡衣|A woman's light, loose, often transparent robe or nightdress.|She wore a delicate cymar of silk. 她穿着一件精致的丝绸睡衣。|近义词：nightgown, negligee, robe|反义词: outerwear, daywear|noun

Etymology:
From French _cymar_, from Italian _zimarra_, from Arabic *sammur*  meaning "weasel," referring to a fur garment. Over time, the meaning shifted to a light robe.
