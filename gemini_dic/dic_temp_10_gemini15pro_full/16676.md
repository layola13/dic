barrelled:/ˈbærəld/ | v. |  (barrel 的过去式和过去分词) 装桶;飞驰;高速行驶 | past tense and past participle of barrel. To put into barrels; to move very fast | He barrelled down the hallway. 他沿着走廊飞奔而去。 | Synonyms: barreled, sped, rushed, raced, flew | Antonyms: crawled, crept, dawdled | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
