dodecagynian:/ˌdoʊˌdekəˈɡɪniən/
adjective
十二雌蕊的
Having twelve pistils or carpels.
The botanist identified the rare flower as dodecagynian due to its twelve distinct pistils.  植物学家根据这种稀有花朵的十二个不同的雌蕊将其鉴定为十二雌蕊的。
polycarpellary, multipistillate
-
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *dōdeka- (twelve) + gynē (woman, pistil)
