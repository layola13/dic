bravo: /ˈbrɑːvoʊ/ (US) /ˈbrɑːvəʊ/ (UK) | interj. n. adj. | 好极了！/喝彩/ 勇敢的 | An exclamation of praise or approval, especially at a performance. Also used as a noun referring to a shout of "bravo," or as an adjective meaning courageous or excellent. |  The audience shouted "Bravo!" after the performance.  演出结束后，观众高喊“好极了！” | 近义词: well done, excellent, hurrah, cheers | 反义词: terrible, awful | 词性: interjection, noun, adjective

Etymology: From Italian *bravo*, literally "brave, wild, fierce," from Latin *bravus* "wild, savage."
