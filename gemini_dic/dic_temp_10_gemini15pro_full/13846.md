augean:/ɔːˈdʒiːən/
adjective
奥吉安的；（任务）极其艰巨的；非常肮脏的
Requiring Herculean effort to accomplish or cleanse; extremely difficult; requiring great strength or effort to clean.  Relating to the Augean stables, which were cleaned by Hercules.
The CEO faced an Augean task in trying to reform the corrupt corporation. 首席执行官在试图改革这家腐败的公司时面临着一项艰巨的任务。
Herculean, monumental, formidable, colossal, arduous
Simple, easy, manageable
adjective
