discontenting:/ˌdɪskənˈtɛntɪŋ/
adjective
令人不满的；令人不快的
Causing discontent; dissatisfying.
The discontenting news of the budget cuts spread quickly through the office.  办公室里关于预算削减的令人不满的消息迅速传播开来。
The food was cold and discontenting.  食物很冷，令人不快。
近义词:unsatisfying, disappointing, displeasing
反义词:satisfying, pleasing, gratifying
词性:adjective


discontent:/ˌdɪskənˈtɛnt/
noun
不满；不满足
Lack of contentment; dissatisfaction.
There is widespread discontent among the workers about the new policy. 工人们对新政策普遍不满。
The survey revealed growing public discontent with the government.  调查显示公众对政府的不满情绪日益加剧。
近义词:dissatisfaction, unhappiness, disaffection
反义词:contentment, satisfaction, happiness
词性:noun

discontent:/ˌdɪskənˈtɛnt/
verb
使不满；使不满足
To make discontented; to dissatisfy.
The new regulations discontented many of the employees. 新规定使许多员工感到不满。
The poor working conditions discontented the staff.  恶劣的工作条件使员工不满。
近义词:dissatisfy, disappoint, displease
反义词:satisfy, please, gratify
词性:verb

词源:
From dis- (expressing negation) + content (satisfied, pleased). Ultimately from Latin contentus, past participle of continēre "to hold together, contain."
