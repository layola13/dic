drink-driving:/ˈdrɪŋk ˈdraɪvɪŋ/
noun
酒后驾驶
The act of driving a vehicle after consuming alcohol above the legal limit.
He was arrested for drink-driving. 他因酒后驾驶被捕。
The police are cracking down on drink-driving over the holiday period.  警察正在假期期间严厉打击酒后驾驶行为。
近义词: drunk driving, driving under the influence (DUI), driving while intoxicated (DWI)
反义词: sober driving
词性: noun
