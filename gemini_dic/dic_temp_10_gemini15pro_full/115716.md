margraviate: /ˈmɑːrɡreɪviət/ | n. |边疆伯爵领地；女边疆伯爵的爵位或领地|A territory ruled by a margrave or margravine.  The margraviate bordered the kingdom to the east.  这个边疆伯爵领地与东边的王国接壤。|近义词: march, county, principality |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


Etymology:
From Old French *marchegraviat, from marchegravié (“margrave”) + -at (suffix forming abstract nouns).  Ultimately from Frankish *marka (“borderland”) + *graf (“count”).
