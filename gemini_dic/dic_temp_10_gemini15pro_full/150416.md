panshon:/ˈpɑːnʃən/ (UK), /ˈpɛnʃən/ (US)| n.|膳宿公寓，寄宿学校；（尤指法国的）小型旅馆|A small hotel or boarding house, especially one in France, offering full or partial board.|We stayed at a charming panshon in the Loire Valley. 我们在卢瓦尔河谷住在一个迷人的膳宿公寓。|The panshon offered comfortable rooms and delicious meals. 这家小型旅馆提供舒适的房间和美味的餐点。|近义词:boarding house, guest house, inn, hotel|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑) private residence, home|词性:noun

Etymology:
Borrowed from French *pension* "boarding house, board, boarding school," from Old French *pension*, from Latin *pensionem* (nominative *pensio*) "payment, weight, rent, tax," from *pendere* "to weigh, pay."
