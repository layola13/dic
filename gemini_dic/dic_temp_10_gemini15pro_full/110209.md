life-saving:/ˈlaɪfˌseɪvɪŋ/
adjective
救命的；救生的
designed to save or protect people's lives.
例句：The doctor's quick thinking and life-saving actions saved the patient.  医生的快速思考和救命的行动挽救了病人的生命。

例句：The life-saving equipment on the boat was essential during the storm.  暴风雨期间，船上的救生设备至关重要。

近义词：essential, crucial, critical, vital
反义词：life-threatening, fatal, deadly
词性: adjective
