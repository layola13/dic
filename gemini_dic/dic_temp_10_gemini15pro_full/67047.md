expletively: /ɪkˈsplɛtɪvli/ | adv. | 咒骂地；粗俗地；用感叹词地 | Used to describe language that contains swear words or offensive expressions. | He cursed expletively at the driver who cut him off. 他咒骂着那个超他车的司机。|  She muttered expletively under her breath. 她低声咒骂着。| synonyms: profanely, obscenely, abusively | antonyms: politely, respectfully | adverb

expletive: /ˈɛksplətɪv/ | n. | 咒骂语；感叹词 | A swear word or exclamation, especially one that is offensive. | He shouted expletives at the referee. 他对着裁判大喊脏话。|  The song contains several expletives. 这首歌包含几个脏话。| synonyms: swear word, curse, oath, obscenity | antonyms: euphemism, polite term | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *expletīvus, from Latin explēre (“to fill out, complete”), from ex- (“out, from”) + plēre (“to fill”).
