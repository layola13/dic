outreaching:/ˌaʊtˈriːtʃɪŋ/
adjective
外展的，延伸的
reaching out or extending beyond the normal limits.
The library has an outreaching program to serve the homeless population. 图书馆有一个外展项目为无家可归的人群服务。
近义词: expansive, extending, far-reaching
反义词: limited, restricted, confined
词性: adjective

outreaching:/ˌaʊtˈriːtʃɪŋ/
noun
外展活动，延伸服务
the activity of providing services to people who are not able to access them easily.
The church's outreaching program provides food and clothing to the needy. 教会的延伸服务项目为有需要的人提供食物和衣物。
近义词: outreach, community service, social work
反义词: isolation, withdrawal
词性: noun
