contradictoriness:/ˌkɒntrəˈdɪktərɪnɪs/ | n. |矛盾性，前后矛盾|The contradictoriness of his statements made it difficult to trust him. 他的陈述自相矛盾，让人难以信任他。|The inherent contradictoriness of the theory eventually led to its downfall. 该理论内在的矛盾性最终导致了它的失败。|近义词：inconsistency, paradox, conflict, discrepancy|反义词：consistency, agreement, harmony|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From contradictory + -ness.  "Contradictory" comes from Late Latin *contradictorius, from Latin contradicere "to speak against," from contra "against" + dicere "to say."
