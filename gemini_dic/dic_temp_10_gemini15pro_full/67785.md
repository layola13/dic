eyeliner:/ˈaɪlaɪnər/ | n. |眼线笔；眼线（膏/液/粉）|A cosmetic used to draw lines around the eyes, enhancing or changing their shape. |She applied black eyeliner to her upper lids. 她把黑色眼线涂在了上眼皮上。|Synonyms: eye pencil, kohl |Antonyms: makeup remover |noun
