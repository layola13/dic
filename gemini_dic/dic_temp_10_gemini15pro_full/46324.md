dabblingly:/ˈdæblɪŋli/
| adv. | 浅尝地，业余地 | in a dabbling manner; superficially or intermittently | He dabbled in painting, but never seriously pursued it. 他涉猎绘画，但从未认真追求过。| synonym: superficially, dilettantishly, amateurishly | antonym: professionally, expertly, thoroughly |词性:副词
