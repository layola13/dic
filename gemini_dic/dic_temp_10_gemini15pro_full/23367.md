bracer:/ˈbreɪsər/| n. |护腕，腕甲；牙套；支柱；振奋精神之物|Something that braces or supports something else. Can also refer to an orthodontic appliance or something that invigorates.|He needed a bracer after the bad news. 他在听到坏消息后需要一些振奋精神的东西。|Synonyms: support, reinforcement, tonic|Antonyms: depressant, weakening agent|noun
