lovelornnesses:/ˈlʌvlɔːrnɪsɪz/
|n.|失恋的痛苦，单恋的苦恼（复数）
|The state of being lovelorn, especially with regard to multiple instances or a great degree of such sadness.  It refers to the feelings of sadness and loneliness that come from being unloved or rejected by someone you love.|
|Her lovelornnesses were a constant theme in her poetry. 她的诗歌中始终贯穿着失恋的痛苦。|
|近义词: heartache, heartbreak, lovesickness|
|反义词: happiness, joy, contentment|
|词性: noun (plural)|
|词源: From lovelorn + -ness (suffix denoting a state or quality), further from love + lorn (meaning "abandoned" or "forlorn").|
