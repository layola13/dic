half-informingly:/ˌhæf ɪnˈfɔːrmɪŋli/
| adv. | 半知半解地；提供部分信息地 | Providing information in a way that is incomplete or only partially revealing. |  He spoke half-informingly about the situation, leaving us with more questions than answers.  他半遮半掩地谈论了情况，留给我们的问题比答案还多。| Near synonyms: vaguely, partially, incompletely, suggestively | Antonyms: completely, fully, thoroughly, comprehensively |词性:副词

half-informingly:/ˌhæf ɪnˈfɔːrmɪŋli/
|词源| "half-" (meaning partly) + "informing" (present participle of "inform," meaning to give knowledge) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix) |  The word combines "half" with the verb "inform" to create an adverb that describes an action done in a way that only partially informs. |  No specific historical origin beyond the combination of its constituent parts. | 词源
