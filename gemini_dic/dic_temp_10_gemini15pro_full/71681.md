flaunt:/flɔːnt/ | v. | 炫耀，卖弄 | To display (something) ostentatiously, especially in order to provoke envy or admiration or to show defiance. | She flaunted her new diamond ring. 她炫耀着她新买的钻戒。 |  Example: He flaunted his wealth by driving a luxury car. 他开着豪车炫耀他的财富。| Synonyms: show off, parade, display, boast, brandish | Antonyms: hide, conceal, downplay | verb

flaunter:/ˈflɔːntər/ | n. | 炫耀者，卖弄者 | A person who flaunts. | He's such a flaunter, always showing off his expensive clothes. 他是个爱炫耀的人，总是炫耀他昂贵的衣服。 | Example: The flaunter constantly boasted about his achievements.  这个炫耀者不断吹嘘他的成就。| Synonyms: show-off, braggart, boaster | Antonyms: modest person, humble person | noun
