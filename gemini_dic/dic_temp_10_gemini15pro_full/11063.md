archaeolithic:/ˌɑːrkiːəˈlɪθɪk/ |adj.| 旧石器时代的 |Relating to the earliest period of the Stone Age, characterized by the use of rudimentary chipped stone tools. |The archaeologist discovered archaeolithic tools at the dig site. 考古学家在挖掘现场发现了旧石器时代的工具。| The museum's collection includes numerous archaeolithic artifacts, offering a glimpse into the lives of early humans.  博物馆的藏品包括许多旧石器时代的文物，让我们得以一窥早期人类的生活。 | Paleolithic, Early Stone Age | Neolithic |adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From archaeo- + -lithic, from Greek archaios "ancient" + lithos "stone."
