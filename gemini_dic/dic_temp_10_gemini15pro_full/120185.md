mindlessnesses:/ˈmaɪndləsnɪsɪz/
n.
心不在焉，无意识，漫不经心
The state of being mindless; lack of attention or awareness.
Her mindlessnesses led to several mistakes on the test. 她考试时心不在焉导致犯了几个错误。

He often acts in mindlessness, forgetting important appointments. 他经常漫不经心，忘记重要的约会。

近义词：inattentiveness, absent-mindedness, distraction, heedlessness, thoughtlessness
反义词：mindfulness, awareness, attentiveness, concentration, focus
词性：名词(plural)
