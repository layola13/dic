hunkerousness:/ˈhʌŋkərəsnəs/
n.
蹲伏的状态; 蹲伏的倾向; 
A state of being hunkered down; a tendency to hunker.
The soldiers displayed remarkable hunkerousness during the prolonged siege, enduring weeks in cramped, uncomfortable conditions. 士兵们在漫长的围困中表现出了惊人的蹲伏状态，在狭窄、不舒服的环境中忍受了数周。
Facing the blizzard, the family's hunkerousness kept them safe and warm indoors. 面对暴风雪，这家人蹲伏在家中，确保了安全和温暖。
近义词：hunkering, hunkeredness
反义词： restlessness, activity
