nondiscursivenesses:/ˌnɑːndɪˈskɜːrsɪvnɪsɪz/
noun
非散漫性；非离题性
The state or quality of being nondiscursive; lack of discursiveness.  This refers to a style of communication or thought that is concise, direct, and to the point, avoiding digressions or unnecessary elaborations.
His nondiscursivenesses in the meeting were appreciated, as everyone was short on time.  他在会议上的简洁扼要受到赞赏，因为每个人都时间紧迫。
The artist's nondiscursivenesses in his latest work reflect a new focus on essential forms.  这位艺术家在他最新作品中的简洁性反映了他对基本形式的新关注。
