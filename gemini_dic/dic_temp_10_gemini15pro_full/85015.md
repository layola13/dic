harappan:/həˈræpən/| adj. n.|哈拉帕的；哈拉帕人|Relating to the ancient Indus Valley Civilization, specifically the city of Harappa.  Also, a person belonging to this civilization.|The Harappan civilization was known for its advanced urban planning.|近义词：Indus Valley,|反义词:|adjective, noun

Etymology->
From Harappa, the name of a major archaeological site of the Indus Valley Civilization in modern-day Pakistan.
