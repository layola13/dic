indulgency:/ɪnˈdʌldʒənsi/ | n. | 放纵；纵容；沉溺；嗜好；宽容；包容 | the act of allowing yourself or another person to have or do something enjoyable, especially more than is good for you |  She allows herself the occasional indulgence of a glass of wine. 她偶尔会喝一杯葡萄酒，让自己享受一下。 | synonyms: gratification, extravagance, luxury, treat | antonyms: abstinence, self-denial | noun

indulgence:/ɪnˈdʌldʒənsi/ | n. | 宽恕；迁就；包涵 | willingness to ignore the weaknesses of others | His indulgence towards his children's behaviour is understandable. 他对孩子们行为的纵容是可以理解的。| synonyms: tolerance, forbearance, leniency | antonyms: strictness, severity | noun
