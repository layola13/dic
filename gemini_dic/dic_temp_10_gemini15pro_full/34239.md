clans:/klænz/
noun
家族，氏族，宗族
A group of families or households, as among the Scottish Highlanders, claiming descent from a common ancestor and having a common surname.  A group of people united by a common interest or characteristic.
The clan gathered for its annual reunion.  这个家族聚在一起举行年度聚会。
