brutes:/bruːts/| n.|野兽；暴徒；残忍的人|A brute is a cruel, violent, and unfeeling person.  A brute can also refer to an animal, especially a large or wild one.|The dictator was a brute who ruled through fear and intimidation.  那个独裁者是个靠恐惧和恐吓进行统治的暴徒。|The lion, king of the jungle, is a magnificent brute.  狮子，丛林之王，是一种雄伟的野兽。|synonyms: savage, beast, monster, barbarian, ruffian|antonyms: civilized person, gentleman, humanitarian|noun

brutal:/ˈbruːtl/| adj.|野蛮的；残忍的；无情的|Brutal describes something cruel, violent, and without feeling. It can also mean harsh and severe.|The brutal attack left the victim with serious injuries.  这次残忍的袭击使受害者受了重伤。|The winter was brutal, with record-breaking low temperatures.  这个冬天异常寒冷，气温创历史新低。|synonyms: savage, cruel, vicious, ruthless, merciless|antonyms: gentle, kind, humane, merciful, compassionate|adjective
