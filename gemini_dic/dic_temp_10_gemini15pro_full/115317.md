manness:/ˈmænnɪs/
n.
男子气概，男人特质
The quality or state of being manly; manliness.
He displayed great manness in the face of danger.  他在面对危险时表现出极大的男子气概。
近义词:masculinity, virility
反义词:femininity, effeminacy
词性:名词


词源:
From Middle English *mannys, equivalent to man + -ness.  来自中古英语 *mannys，相当于 man + -ness。
