eruginous:/ɪˈruːdʒɪnəs/ | adj. | 生锈的；铜绿色的；绿色的 | Of the greenish-blue colour characteristic of copper rust. | The old statue had an eruginous patina. 这座古老的雕像有一层铜绿色的光泽。| 近义词:rusty, verdigris, copper-green | 反义词:shiny, polished, bright | 词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *aeruginosus*, from aerugo ("rust of copper, verdigris").  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ey-es- ("copper, bronze").
