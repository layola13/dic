half-successfulnesses:/ˌhæf səkˈsɛsfʊlnəsɪz/ | n. | 半成功；部分成功 |  A state of being only partially or moderately successful; instances of limited success.  This is a rare and somewhat awkward word, formed by adding the prefix "half-" and the plural suffix "-es" to "successfulness." | The project's half-successfulnesses were a source of frustration for the team.  这个项目的半成功状态让团队感到沮丧。| Near synonyms: partial successes, limited successes, qualified successes | Antonyms: complete successes, total failures | noun
