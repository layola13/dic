conceptualizer:/kənˈsɛptʃʊəˌlaɪzər/ | n. | 概念化者；构思者 |  Someone who forms concepts or ideas, especially in a creative or abstract way. | The lead conceptualizer for the project envisioned a futuristic city powered by renewable energy.  项目首席构思者设想了一个由可再生能源驱动的未来城市。 | synonyms:  thinker, innovator, ideator, visionary, designer, planner | antonyms:  imitator, follower, copier | noun


conceptualize:/kənˈsɛptʃʊəˌlaɪz/ | v. |  概念化；构思；使形成概念 | To form a concept or idea of something. |  I can't conceptualize how such a tiny device can hold so much information. 我无法想象这么小的设备怎么能容纳这么多信息。| synonyms: envision, imagine, conceive, visualize, ideate | antonyms: misunderstand, misinterpret, misconceive | verb
