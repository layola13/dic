overlusciousness:/ˌoʊvərˈlʌʃəsnəs/| n. | 过于甜美，过于腻，过于华丽 |  The state of being excessively luscious; an overabundance of richness or sweetness. | The overlusciousness of the dessert made it difficult to finish.  这道甜点过于甜腻，让人难以吃完。| 近义词：excessiveness, richness, sweetness, opulence | 反义词：plainness, simplicity, austerity | 词性: noun

词源: From over- + luscious + -ness.  "Over-" 表示"过度"， "luscious" 表示"美味的，甜美的"， "-ness"是名词后缀，表示状态或性质。
