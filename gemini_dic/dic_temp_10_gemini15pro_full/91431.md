hygienist:/ˈhaɪdʒiənɪst/ | n. |保健员；卫生学家；卫生员|A person who works in the field of hygiene, often specializing in dental hygiene.  | Example:  The dental hygienist cleaned my teeth. 牙科保健员给我洗了牙。

Example: The industrial hygienist inspected the factory for safety hazards. 工业卫生学家检查了工厂的安全隐患。

|Synonym:  sanitarian, health professional |Antonym: N/A |noun
