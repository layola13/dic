intemperate:/ɪnˈtɛmpərɪt/| adj.|放纵的，无节制的，(气候)恶劣的|Lacking moderation or self-restraint; excessive. Also, not mild or moderate; inclement.|例句：His intemperate outbursts of anger made him difficult to work with. 他无节制的愤怒爆发让他很难相处。|近义词：excessive, immoderate, unrestrained, extreme, inordinate, extravagant|反义词：temperate, moderate, restrained, controlled|词性:adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin intemperatus "immoderate," from in- "not" + temperatus "moderate, temperate."
