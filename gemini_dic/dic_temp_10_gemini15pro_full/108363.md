lavishly: /ˈlævɪʃli/ | adv. | 奢侈地，慷慨地 | in a way that is very generous or extravagant; giving or using a large amount of something |  They entertained lavishly. 他们盛情款待。 | 近义词: extravagantly, profusely, generously, abundantly | 反义词: sparingly, frugally, meagerly | 词性: 副词

词源: From lavish + -ly.  Lavish itself comes from Middle English *lavisshe, from Old French lavasse (“deluge, downpour”), from laver (“to wash”).  The original sense was "profuse, overflowing."
