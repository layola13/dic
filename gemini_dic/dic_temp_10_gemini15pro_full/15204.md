backcountry:/ˈbækˌkʌntri/| n.|偏远地区；穷乡僻壤|A remote, undeveloped area, typically far from towns and cities and characterized by wilderness.|We went hiking in the backcountry of Yosemite National Park. 我们去优胜美地国家公园的偏远地区徒步旅行了。|Synonyms: wilderness, outback, hinterland|Antonyms: city, urban area|noun
backcountry:/ˈbækˌkʌntri/| adj.|偏僻的；荒凉的|Of or relating to the backcountry.|The backcountry roads were rough and difficult to navigate. 偏僻的道路崎岖不平，难以通行。|Synonyms: remote, isolated, undeveloped|Antonyms: urban, developed, accessible|adjective
