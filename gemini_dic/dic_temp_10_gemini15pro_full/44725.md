cruised:/kruːzd/
|v.|巡航，漫游，轻松完成|to travel at a moderate speed, especially over water or in the air for pleasure; to move easily and confidently;|We cruised along the coast. 我们沿着海岸航行。|近义词: sailed, toured, drifted, glided, coasted |反义词: raced, sped, labored, struggled|verb
cruised:/kruːzd/
|v.|（车辆）缓慢行驶|to drive a car slowly, especially so that you can see what is happening or look for someone or something;|The police car cruised the streets. 警车在街上巡逻。|近义词: patrolled, surveyed|反义词: sped, raced|verb
