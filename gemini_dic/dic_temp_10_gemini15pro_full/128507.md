newsworthier:/ˈnjuːzwɜːrðiər/ |adj.|更具新闻价值的|More worthy of being reported as news; more significant or interesting as news.|The scandal involving the mayor was far newsworthier than the local bake sale. 市长卷入的丑闻比当地烘焙义卖更具新闻价值。|Synonyms: more important, more significant, more relevant, more interesting|Antonyms: less important, less significant, trivial, mundane|adjective

newsworthy:/ˈnjuːzwɜːrði/ |adj.|有新闻价值的|Worthy of being reported as news; sufficiently interesting or important to the public to warrant reporting.|The local dog show wasn't newsworthy enough for the national media. 当地狗展对国家媒体来说没有足够的新闻价值。|Synonyms: notable, remarkable, significant, important|Antonyms: unimportant, insignificant, trivial|adjective
