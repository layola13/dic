corvin:/ˈkɔːrvɪn/ | adj. n. |乌鸦的，黑色的；乌鸦| Relating to a raven; black as a raven.  Also used as a noun referring to a raven, particularly in poetic or literary contexts. | The corvin feathers shimmered in the moonlight.  月光下乌鸦的羽毛闪烁着光芒。|  The old poet described the bird as a dark corvin, a symbol of ill omen. 老诗人把这只鸟描述成一只黑色的乌鸦，是不祥之兆的象征。 | synonyms: raven, black, ebony, dark | antonyms: white, light, bright | adjective, noun
