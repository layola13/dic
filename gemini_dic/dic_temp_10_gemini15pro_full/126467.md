mythographer: /mɪˈθɒɡrəfər/ (British) /mɪˈθɑːɡrəfər/ (American)
n.|神话作者，神话编纂者|A person who writes or compiles myths. |Robert Graves was a renowned mythographer, exploring Greek myths in depth. 罗伯特·格雷夫斯是一位著名的神话作者，深入探索了希腊神话。| mythologist, myth-writer | N/A | noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek *μυθογράφος (muthográphos)  "myth-writer," from *μῦθος (mûthos) "myth, story, tale" + *γράφω (gráphō) "I write, scratch, draw."
