anticausotic:/ˌæntikaːˈstɔːtɪk/
adjective
抗腐蚀的，防腐蚀的
Preventing or counteracting burning or corrosion, especially by a caustic substance.
This new paint is anticausotic and protects the metal from rust. 这种新油漆具有抗腐蚀性，可以保护金属免受生锈。
The anticausotic lining of the tank prevents chemical damage.  储罐的抗腐蚀内衬可防止化学损坏。
synonyms:  anticorrosive, rustproof, protective
antonyms: corrosive, caustic
word type: adjective
