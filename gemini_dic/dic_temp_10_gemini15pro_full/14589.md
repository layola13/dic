avidiously:/əˈvɪdiəsli/| adv.|贪婪地；渴望地；热切地|showing great enthusiasm for or interest in something, especially something regarded as valuable or desirable|He avidiously collected stamps. 他热切地收集邮票。|She avidiously reads every new book by her favorite author. 她如饥似渴地阅读她最喜欢的作者的每一本新书。|近义词：eagerly, greedily, enthusiastically, voraciously|反义词：apathetically, indifferently, reluctantly|词性:副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *avidiosus*, from avidus (“eager, greedy”) + -osus (suffix forming adjectives).
