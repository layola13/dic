indigenities:/ˌɪndɪˈdʒɛnɪtiz/
n.
侮辱，轻蔑；无礼的言行
Acts that violate the dignity or respect of a person or group, especially indigenous people.
The report documented the numerous indigenities suffered by the native population.  这份报告记录了土著居民遭受的无数侮辱。
The museum apologized for past indigenities towards First Nations artifacts.  博物馆为过去对原住民文物的轻蔑行为道歉。
近义词: insults, offences, affronts, indignities
反义词: respect, honor, courtesy
词性: 名词
