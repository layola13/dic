agent:/ˈeɪdʒənt/| n.|代理人，代理商；特工；媒介；作用物|A person who acts on behalf of another person or group.|The travel agent booked our flights. 旅行社帮我们订了机票。|synonyms: representative, deputy, broker, intermediary, factor, operative, spy|antonyms: principal, client|noun

agent:/ˈeɪdʒənt/| adj.|起作用的；有效的|Having the power to produce an effect; active.|The detergent is the active agent in removing stains.  这种洗涤剂是去除污渍的有效成分。|synonyms: active, effective, operative|antonyms: inactive, passive|adjective
