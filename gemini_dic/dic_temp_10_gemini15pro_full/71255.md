five-spice:/ˈfaɪv spaɪs/
noun
五香粉
A mixture of ground spices, typically including star anise, cloves, cinnamon, Sichuan pepper, and fennel seeds.
This recipe calls for a teaspoon of five-spice.  这个食谱需要一茶匙五香粉。
Synonyms: Chinese five-spice, five-spice powder
