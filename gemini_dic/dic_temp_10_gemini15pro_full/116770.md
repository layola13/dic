mauvaniline:/ˌmɔːvəˈnɪlɪn/| n. |苯胺紫；毛凡尼林；玫瑰苯胺|A synthetic purple dye, similar to mauveine, but less stable. It is a by-product in the manufacture of rosaniline.|Mauvaniline was once used to dye silk. 毛凡尼林曾被用来染丝绸。|Synonyms: mauveine, aniline violet, Perkin's mauve|Antonyms: N/A|noun
