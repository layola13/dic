fossores:/fɒˈsɔːriːz/
| n. |穴居动物；掘土动物|Burrowing animals, typically mammals, that dig and live in burrows.  They are adapted for a subterranean lifestyle.|Fossores, such as badgers and moles, play an important role in soil aeration. 穴居动物，例如獾和鼹鼠，在土壤通气中起着重要作用。|近义词:burrowers, diggers|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly surface dwellers)|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fossor*, meaning "digger," from the verb *fodere*, "to dig."
