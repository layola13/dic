decorement:/ˈdɛkərmənt/
n.
装饰;装潢;装饰品
An embellishment or ornamentation; something used to decorate.
The decorement of the Christmas tree brought joy to the children.  圣诞树的装饰给孩子们带来了欢乐。
Synonyms: ornamentation, adornment, embellishment, decoration, garnishment
Antonyms: plainness, simplicity
