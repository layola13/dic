dystopianisms:/dɪsˈtəʊpiənɪzəmz/
|n.|反乌托邦主义，反乌托邦的特征|The novel explores various dystopianisms, such as government surveillance and loss of individual freedom. 这部小说探讨了各种反乌托邦主义，例如政府监控和个人自由的丧失。|
|English Explanation:  Plural form of "dystopianism," referring to the characteristics or elements that constitute a dystopia.  A dystopia is an imagined place or state in which everything is unpleasant or bad, typically a totalitarian or environmentally degraded one.|
|例句：The film presents several disturbing dystopianisms, including a social hierarchy based on genetic engineering. 这部电影呈现了几个令人不安的反乌托邦特征，包括基于基因工程的社会等级制度。|

|例句：Many young adult novels explore themes of dystopianisms and rebellion against oppressive regimes. 许多青少年小说都探讨了反乌托邦主义和反抗压迫政权的主题。|

|近义词：dystopias, anti-utopias, |
|反义词:utopias, ideal societies|
|词性: Noun (plural)|
