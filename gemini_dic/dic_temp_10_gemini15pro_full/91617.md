hyperaware:/ˌhaɪpərəˈweər/| adj.|过度警觉的，高度敏感的|Excessively or acutely aware; highly sensitive.  Being very conscious of something, often to an excessive degree.|She was hyperaware of every sound in the room after the power went out.  停电后，她对房间里的每一个声音都高度警觉。|例句：He was hyperaware of his surroundings after the incident. 事件发生后，他对周围环境非常警觉。|近义词：oversensitive, conscious, mindful|反义词：oblivious, unaware, insensitive|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From hyper- + aware.  "Hyper-" is a prefix meaning "over, above, beyond," deriving from Greek "huper."  "Aware" comes from Old English "ġewær," meaning "watchful, cautious."
