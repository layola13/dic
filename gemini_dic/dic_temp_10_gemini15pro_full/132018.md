noncorrelatively:/ˌnɒnkɒrɪˈleɪtɪvli/ | adv. | 非相关地 |  In a way that is not correlated; without correlation.  It describes actions, processes, or data that don't exhibit a statistical relationship or connection. | The two variables changed noncorrelatively over time. 这两个变量随着时间的推移非相关地变化。|  The price of gold and the number of sunny days in a year change noncorrelatively. 黄金的价格和一年中晴天的数量非相关地变化。 | independently, separately, unrelatedly | correlatively, jointly | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + correlatively.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Correlatively" is the adverbial form of "correlative," which comes from the Latin "correlativus," meaning "having mutual relation."
