misnumbering:/ˌmɪsˈnʌmbərɪŋ/
n.|错误编号；编号错误|The misnumbering of the pages caused confusion. 页码编号错误导致混乱。|
例句：The misnumbering of the invoices led to a significant accounting error. 发票编号错误导致了严重的会计错误。
例句：There was a misnumbering of the exhibits in the museum. 博物馆展品的编号有误。
近义词：mislabeling, miscalculation, error
反义词：correct numbering, accurate numbering
词性：名词

misnumber:/ˌmɪsˈnʌmbər/
vt.|错误编号；给…编错号|I misnumbered the chapters in my dissertation. 我把论文的章节编号弄错了。|
例句：She misnumbered the files, making them difficult to find. 她把文件的编号弄错了，很难找到。
例句：He misnumbered the questions on the quiz. 他把测验题的编号弄错了。
近义词：mislabel, miscalculate
反义词：number correctly, number accurately
词性：动词

词源：mis- (prefix meaning "wrong" or "badly") + number (verb) + -ing (suffix forming present participle or gerund)
