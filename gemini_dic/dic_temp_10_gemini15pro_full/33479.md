chylous:/ˈkaɪləs/| adj.|乳糜的，乳糜状的|Relating to or resembling chyle; milky.  Containing chyle.|The patient presented with chylous ascites.  病人出现乳糜性腹水。|Synonyms: milky, chyle-like|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider "clear" or "serous" in a medical context)|adjective


chyle:/kaɪl/| n.|乳糜|A milky fluid consisting of fat droplets and lymph that is formed in the small intestine during digestion and taken up by lymph vessels.|The chyle is transported through the lymphatic system.|Synonyms: intestinal lymph|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|noun
