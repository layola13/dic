contemptibility:/kənˌtɛmptɪˈbɪlɪti/
n.
可鄙, 卑劣, 令人轻蔑
The quality of being contemptible; deserving contempt.
His contemptibility was evident in his dishonest dealings. 他的卑劣体现在他不诚实的交易中。
近义词:despicableness, vileness, baseness
反义词:admirability, respectability, worthiness
词性:名词

contempt:/kənˈtɛmpt/
n.
轻蔑, 蔑视, 耻辱
The feeling that a person or a thing is beneath consideration, worthless, or deserving scorn.
She looked at him with contempt. 她轻蔑地看着他。
近义词:scorn, disdain, disrespect
反义词:respect, admiration, esteem
词性:名词

-ible:/ɪbl/
suffix
可...的，能...的
Forms adjectives meaning "capable of, worthy of, or tending to."
Visible - capable of being seen. 可见的 - 能够被看到的。
近义词: -able
反义词: None
词性:后缀

contemptible:/kənˈtɛmptɪbl/
adj.
可鄙的, 卑劣的, 令人轻蔑的
Deserving contempt; despicable.
His behavior was contemptible. 他的行为令人不齿。
近义词:despicable, vile, base
反义词:admirable, respectable, worthy
词性:形容词
