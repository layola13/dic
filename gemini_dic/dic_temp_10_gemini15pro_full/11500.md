aridest:/ˈærɪdɪst/ | adj. | 最干旱的 |  Superlative form of arid: most arid; having little or no rain; too dry or barren to support vegetation. | The Atacama Desert is one of the aridest places on Earth. 阿塔卡马沙漠是地球上最干旱的地方之一。| The aridest regions of the country suffer from frequent droughts. 这个国家最干旱的地区经常遭受干旱。|  driest, barrenest |  wettest, most humid | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
