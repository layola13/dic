boulework: /ˈbuːlwɜːrk/ | n. | 壁垒；防御工事；保障；支柱 | A defensive wall; any protection against external danger; something serving as a defense or safeguard. |  The castle's high walls formed a strong boulework against attack. 城堡的高墙构成了强大的防御工事，抵御攻击。

The First Amendment serves as a boulework against government censorship.  第一修正案是抵御政府审查制度的保障。 | 近义词: rampart, bulwark, bastion, safeguard, defense | 反义词: vulnerability, weakness | 词性: 名词

词源: From Middle French *bollewerc, from Middle Dutch bolwerc, from bol "rounded timber, tree trunk" + werc "work".  源自中古法语 *bollewerc，来自中古荷兰语 bolwerc，由 bol “圆木，树干” + werc “工作” 组成。
