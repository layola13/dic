khakis:/ˈkɑːkiz/
noun
卡其裤；卡其色军服
Loose-fitting trousers made of khaki cloth.  A pair of khakis.
例句：He wore a pair of khakis and a polo shirt. 他穿了一条卡其裤和一件polo衫。

例句：She bought a new pair of khakis for the summer. 她为夏天买了一条新的卡其裤。

近义词：trousers, pants
反义词：(无明确反义词)
词性：noun
