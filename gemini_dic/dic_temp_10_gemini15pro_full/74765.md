freeze-dried:/ˈfriːz draɪd/
adjective|冻干的|preserved by freezing and then drying in a vacuum, typically to preserve perishable food|例句：Freeze-dried coffee is convenient for camping. 冻干咖啡便于露营。|例句：The freeze-dried strawberries retained their original flavor. 冻干草莓保留了原有的风味。|近义词：dehydrated, desiccated|反义词:fresh, hydrated|词性:adjective

freeze-dried:/ˈfriːz draɪd/
verb|冻干|to preserve (something) by freezing and then drying in a vacuum|例句：They freeze-dried the meat for preservation. 他们冻干肉类以便保存。|例句：The company freeze-dries fruits and vegetables for long-term storage. 这家公司冻干水果和蔬菜以长期保存。|近义词：dehydrate, desiccate|反义词:hydrate, moisten|词性:verb (past participle)
