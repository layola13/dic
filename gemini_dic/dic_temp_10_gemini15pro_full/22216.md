bonapartism:/ˈbəʊnəpɑːtɪzəm/ /ˈboʊnəpɑːrtɪzəm/
n.
波拿巴主义
A political ideology associated with Napoleon Bonaparte, characterized by authoritarian rule, military glory, and nationalistic sentiment.  It often involves a charismatic leader centralizing power and appealing to popular support through promises of order and prosperity.
Bonapartism emerged in 19th-century France following the rule of Napoleon Bonaparte.

Napoleon's reign was a classic example of Bonapartism. 拿破仑的统治是波拿巴主义的典型例子。

The resurgence of Bonapartism in the 20th century was a surprising political development.  20世纪波拿巴主义的复兴是一个令人惊讶的政治发展。

authoritarianism, Caesarism, despotism, one-man rule
democracy, republicanism
