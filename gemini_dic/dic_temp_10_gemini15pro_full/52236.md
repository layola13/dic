diazepam:/daɪˈæzɪpæm/
noun
安定; 地西泮
A benzodiazepine tranquilizer drug used chiefly to relieve anxiety, muscle spasms, and seizures, and to control agitation caused by alcohol withdrawal.
例句：Diazepam is often prescribed for anxiety.  地西泮常用于治疗焦虑。

例句：He was given diazepam to help him relax before the surgery.  手术前他服用了地西泮来帮助他放松。
近义词：Valium (brand name), lorazepam, alprazolam
反义词: stimulant
词性:n.
