oomph:/ʊmf/ | n. |活力，魅力，吸引力 |Energy, vigor, attractiveness, especially sex appeal.|  She's got lots of oomph. 她很有魅力。| 近义词: pizzazz, charisma, vigor, energy, appeal | 反义词: dullness, lifelessness | 词性: 名词

词源:  Likely imitative of a grunt of exertion.  Possibly influenced by German _umpf_ (interjection expressing exertion).
