alforja: /ælˈfɔːrhɑː/ (Spanish pronunciation)| n. |鞍囊；褡裢|A pair of bags, traditionally made of leather or cloth, slung across the back of a horse or other animal, or carried over a person's shoulder, used for carrying provisions or personal belongings.  Derived from the Arabic "al-ḵurǧa," meaning "saddlebag."|The horseman carried his provisions in an alforja. 骑士把他的粮食放在褡裢里。|Synonyms: saddlebag, pannier|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

alforjas: /ælˈfɔːrhɑːs/ (Spanish pronunciation)| n. |鞍囊；褡裢（复数）|Plural form of "alforja," referring to a pair of bags. | The alforjas were overflowing with fresh produce from the market. 这对褡裢里装满了从市场买来的新鲜农产品。|Synonyms: saddlebags, panniers|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
