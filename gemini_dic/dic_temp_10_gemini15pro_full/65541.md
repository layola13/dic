euthanises:/ˌjuːθəˈnaɪzɪz/ |v. | 安乐死 | To kill (a person or animal) suffering from an incurable and painful disease or in an irreversible coma.  The act is usually performed by a doctor or veterinarian. | The vet euthanised the dog to end its suffering. 兽医对这只狗实施了安乐死以结束它的痛苦。| synonyms: put down, put to sleep | antonyms: resuscitate, revive | verb
