eh:/eɪ/ /ɛ/ | interj. | 呃，啊，是吗？ | An interjection expressing a range of emotions including surprise, doubt, questioning, and seeking confirmation. | Example: "Eh, what was that?"  呃，那是什么？ | Example: "It's a nice day, eh?" 天气不错，是吗？ | Synonym: huh, right, what | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for an interjection) | interjection

词源:
Likely originated as a variant of "hey" or "ay," possibly influenced by similar interjections in other Germanic languages.  Its precise origin is uncertain.
