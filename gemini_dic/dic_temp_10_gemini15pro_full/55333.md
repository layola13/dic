dissuasion:/dɪˈsweɪʒən/
n.
劝阻，阻止
The act of persuading someone not to do something.
His friends’ dissuasion didn’t stop him from going. 他的朋友的劝阻并没有阻止他去。
近义词：deterrence, discouragement
反义词：persuasion, encouragement
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin dissuasio, from dissuasus, past participle of dissuadere "to advise against," from dis- "apart, away" + suadere "to advise."
