idolises:/ˈaɪdəlaɪzɪz/ | v. | 崇拜；把…奉为偶像 |  To admire or love someone or something very much, often to an excessive degree. | She absolutely idolises her older brother. 她非常崇拜她的哥哥。|  worships, adores, reveres | criticizes, condemns | verb

idolises:/ˈaɪdəlaɪzɪz/ | v. | 偶像化；将…理想化 | To regard or represent someone or something as perfect or much better than they really are. | The media tends to idolise successful businesspeople. 媒体倾向于把成功的商人偶像化。| romanticizes, glorifies, exalts | vilifies, denigrates | verb
