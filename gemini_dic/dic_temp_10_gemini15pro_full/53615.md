discards:/dɪˈskɑːrdz/ | n. | 丢弃物，废弃物 | Something that is discarded or rejected |  The factory safely disposes of its hazardous discards.  这家工厂安全地处理其危险废弃物。| synonyms: rejects, refuse, trash, waste, debris | antonyms: keeps, treasures, valuables | noun

discards:/dɪˈskɑːrdz/ | v. | 丢弃，抛弃 |  To throw away or get rid of something | He discarded the old newspapers. 他把旧报纸丢掉了。 | synonyms: jettison, scrap, dump, ditch, reject | antonyms: keep, retain, save, preserve | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "descarter" (to discard a card), from "des-" (dis-) + "carte" (card).
