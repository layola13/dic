hurraying:/həˈreɪɪŋ/
| v. |欢呼，喝彩|The crowd was hurraying as the team scored the winning goal. |人群在球队攻入制胜一球时欢呼雀跃。|近义词：cheering, applauding, acclaiming|反义词: booing, jeering|词性:动词
Etymology: From the interjection "hurray," which is of uncertain origin, possibly related to "huzza."
