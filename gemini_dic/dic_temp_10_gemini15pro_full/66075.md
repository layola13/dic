excambium:/ɛksˈkeɪmbiəm/ | n. |交换层；形成层|A layer of meristematic cells, responsible for producing secondary tissues such as xylem and phloem in plants.| Example: The excambium is essential for the growth of woody plants.  形成层对木本植物的生长至关重要。| Example:  Damage to the excambium can hinder a tree's ability to transport water and nutrients. 对形成层的损害会阻碍树木输送水和养分的能力。|Synonym: cambium|Antonym: N/A|Noun
