fibrospongiae:/ˌfaɪbroʊˈspʌndʒiaɪ/ | n. | 纤维海绵纲 |A class of marine sponges characterized by a skeleton made of spongin fibers with or without siliceous spicules.|  The fibrospongiae are a diverse group of sponges. | 纤维海绵纲是种类繁多的海绵群体。| 近义词: demosponges (although not all demosponges are fibrospongiae) | 反义词:  (No specific antonym, potentially other sponge classes like Calcarea or Hexactinellida depending on context) | 词性: noun

fibrospongia:/ˌfaɪbroʊˈspʌndʒiə/ | n. | 纤维海绵 |A sponge belonging to the class Fibrospongiae. | This fibrospongia was found in shallow waters. | 这个纤维海绵是在浅水中发现的。| 近义词: demosponge (although not all demosponges are fibrospongiae) | 反义词: (No specific antonym, potentially sponges from other classes like Calcarea or Hexactinellida depending on context) | 词性: noun
