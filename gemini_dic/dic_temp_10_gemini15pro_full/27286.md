calumnying: /kəˈlʌmniɪŋ/
verb
诽谤，造谣
the act of uttering false charges or misrepresentations maliciously calculated to harm another's reputation
He was accused of calumnying his opponent. 他被指控诽谤他的对手。

近义词: defaming, libeling, slandering, vilifying, maligning, traducing
反义词: praising, lauding, extolling, commending
