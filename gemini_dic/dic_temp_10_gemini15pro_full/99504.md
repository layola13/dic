interfold:/ˌɪntərˈfoʊld/
noun|夹页，折页|A folded section inserted into a publication, often used for maps or charts.|The magazine included a large interfold showcasing the new car model.|Synonyms: insert, leaflet, supplement|Antonyms: main section, body (of publication)|n.
