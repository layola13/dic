impulsivity:/ɪmˌpʌlˈsɪvɪti/| n. |冲动，冲动性|The tendency to act on a whim, without forethought or consideration of the consequences.|  His impulsivity often got him into trouble. 他的冲动经常让他陷入困境。|synonyms: rashness, recklessness, impetuousness | antonyms: caution, prudence, deliberation | noun

impulsivities:/ɪmˌpʌlˈsɪvɪtiz/| n. |冲动性(复数)|Plural form of impulsivity, referring to multiple instances or types of impulsive behavior. |  The therapist helped him understand the roots of his impulsivities.  治疗师帮助他理解他各种冲动行为的根源。| synonyms: rashnesses, recklessnesses, impetuousnesses (less common) | antonyms: cautions, prudences, deliberations (less common) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *impulsivitas, from Latin impulsus (past participle of impellere “to push, drive”), from in- “in” + pellere “to push, drive”.
