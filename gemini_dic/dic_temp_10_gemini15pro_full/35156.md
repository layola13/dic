clovers:/ˈkloʊvərz/| n.|三叶草，苜蓿|A small plant with usually three leaves on each stem, often considered to be a sign of good luck.  Clover is often grown as food for farm animals. |例句：The field was covered in clovers.  这片田野上长满了三叶草。

例句：He found a four-leaf clover. 他找到了一株四叶草。|近义词：trefoil|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
