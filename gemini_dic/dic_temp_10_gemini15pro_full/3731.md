aggroup:/əˈɡruːp/
verb
聚集，组合
To form or gather into a group or cluster.
Farmers sometimes aggroup their products for marketing.  农民有时会把他们的产品集合起来销售。
The data were aggroupped according to region. 数据按地区分组。
近义词:  assemble, gather, collect, congregate, cluster
反义词: disperse, scatter, separate
词性: verb

Etymology:
From French *aggrouper*, from *a-* (to) + *grouper* (to group), from Old French *grope* (group). Ultimately from Germanic origin.
