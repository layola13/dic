classis:/ˈklæsɪs/| n.|经典作品；(动植物)纲；阶级；舰队|A group of things or persons having common characteristics, a class.  A grouping of organisms having common characteristics above the order and below the phylum or division.|The epic poem is considered a classis of ancient literature. 这部史诗被认为是古代文学的经典之作。|Synonyms: category, group, order, rank, division|Antonyms:  individual, singularity|noun
