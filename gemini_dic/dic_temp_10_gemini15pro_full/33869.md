circumscriptive:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈskrɪptɪv/ | adj. |限制性的，限定的|serving to circumscribe; restrictive.  Defining something precisely, often by setting boundaries or limits.|  The circumscriptive nature of the town's curfew kept teenagers at home after dark.  小镇宵禁的限制性使得青少年天黑后都待在家里。 |  The lawyer used circumscriptive language in the contract to avoid any ambiguity.  律师在合同中使用了限制性语言，以避免任何歧义。|  limiting, restrictive, confining, defining, delimiting | expansive, broad, unlimited, general, inclusive | adjective
