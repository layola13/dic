heliographies:/ˌhiːliˈɒɡrəfiz/
noun
日光照片；日光照相术
The process of making photographs by the action of sunlight on a prepared surface, or a photograph made by this process.
Early heliographies were often blurry and lacked detail. 早期的日光照片通常很模糊，缺乏细节。
