ops:/ɒps/ | interj. |哎哟; 糟糕 | Used to express mild apology, surprise, or dismay. | Oops, I spilled my coffee. 哎哟，我洒了咖啡。| 近义词: whoops, uh-oh | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: interjection
ops:/ɑːps/ | n. | (Informal) operations |  Short for "operations," referring to the practical work or activities of an organization. | The ops team is working on fixing the server issue.  运营团队正在努力修复服务器问题。| 近义词: operations, procedures | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
