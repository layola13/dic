counter-bill:/ˈkaʊntərˌbɪl/ | n. | 反议案，对案 | A bill introduced in a legislature in opposition to another bill under consideration.  It's designed to offer an alternative course of action. | The senator introduced a counter-bill that proposed tax cuts instead of tax increases. 参议员提出了一项反议案，建议减税而不是增税。

The opposition party presented a counter-bill to address the issue of climate change. 反对党提出一项反议案来解决气候变化问题。 | 近义词: alternative bill, competing bill | 反义词: original bill, primary bill | 词性: noun
