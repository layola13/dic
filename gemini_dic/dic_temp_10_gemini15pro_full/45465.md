curatorial:/kjʊəˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
策展的；馆长的
Relating to the selection and organization of a collection, especially in a museum or art gallery.
The museum's curatorial staff is highly respected.  博物馆的策展人员备受尊敬。

The curatorial decisions were controversial. 策展决定引起了争议。


近义词:  museum, exhibition, gallery
反义词: None

词性: adjective
