ctenoidei:/ˌsɪnɔɪˈdiːaɪ/| n.|栉鳞鱼类|A large order of bony fishes characterized by ctenoid scales, which have a comb-like edge. This order includes many familiar fishes such as perch, bass, and sunfish.| Perch, bass, and sunfish belong to the Ctenoidei order.  鲈鱼、鲈鱼和翻车鱼都属于栉鳞鱼目。|近义词:Acanthopterygii (棘鳍总目 - a broader classification)|反义词:Cycloidei (圆鳞鱼类)|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek κτείς (kteís, “comb”) + εἶδος (eîdos, “form, shape”) + -ei (plural suffix).
