imprecations:/ˌɪmprɪˈkeɪʃənz/
|n.|诅咒，咒骂|A spoken curse.  The act of invoking evil or misfortune on someone or something.|The witch muttered imprecations against her enemies.|近义词: curses, maledictions, anathemas|反义词: blessings, benedictions|词性:名词

imprecations:/ˌɪmprɪˈkeɪʃənz/
|n.|咒骂，恶毒的咒语|The expression of a wish that misfortune, evil, etc., befall a person or thing.|He showered imprecations upon the driver who cut him off.|近义词: execrations, denunciations|反义词: praises, commendations|词性:名词
