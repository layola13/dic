degenerated:/dɪˈdʒɛnəreɪtɪd/
adjective
堕落的，退化的，变质的
Having lost the physical, mental, or moral qualities considered normal and desirable; deteriorated.
例句：
The once vibrant city had degenerated into a slum.
曾经充满活力的城市已经沦为贫民窟。

He had degenerated into a gambler and a drunkard.
他已经堕落成一个赌徒和酒鬼。

近义词：declined, deteriorated, worsened, decayed, corrupted
反义词：improved, enhanced, ameliorated, elevated, refined
词性: adjective


degenerated:/dɪˈdʒɛnəreɪt/
verb
退化，堕落，变质
To decline or deteriorate physically, mentally, or morally.
例句：
His health degenerated rapidly after the diagnosis.
确诊后，他的健康状况迅速恶化。

The discussion degenerated into a shouting match.
讨论变成了争吵。

近义词：decline, deteriorate, worsen, decay, corrupt
反义词：improve, enhance, ameliorate, elevate, refine
词性: verb


词源：
From Latin degeneratus, past participle of degenerare (“to depart from one's kind, to deteriorate”), from de- (“from, away from”) + genus (“birth, race, kind”).
