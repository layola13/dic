hoofed:/huːft/ /huːvd/
adjective
有蹄的
having hooves
例句：Horses, cows, and pigs are hoofed animals. 马、牛和猪都是有蹄动物。

例句：The hoofed mammal grazed peacefully in the meadow.  这只有蹄的哺乳动物在草地上平静地吃草。

近义词：ungulate
反义词：(no single antonym, consider "paw-footed," "soft-footed")
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hōffod, past participle of *hōfian "to provide with hooves," from hōf "hoof."
