misbehavior:/ˌmɪsbɪˈheɪvjər/ | n. |  不良行为，不端行为 | The teacher reprimanded the student for his misbehavior in class. |  The child's misbehavior was a result of his frustration. | 近义词：misconduct, delinquency, naughtiness | 反义词：good behavior, obedience | 词性: 名词

词源:  From mis- + behavior.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "wrong" or "bad." "Behavior" comes from the Old French "behaviour," ultimately derived from the verb "behave."
