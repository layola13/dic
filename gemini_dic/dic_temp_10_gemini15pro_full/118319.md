meresman:/ˈmɛərzmən/| n.|邊界管理員，邊界標誌管理員|A person who maintains or manages boundaries, especially between properties.  This term is archaic and rarely used in modern English. |Example: The meresman walked the length of the boundary, checking for any damage to the markers. 邊界管理員沿著邊界走了一圈，檢查標記是否有任何損壞。

Example:  In olden times, the meresman was an important figure in the community, resolving disputes over land ownership.  在古代，邊界管理員是社區中的重要人物，負責解決土地所有權糾紛。|近义词：boundary rider, land surveyor|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *mæremann (not directly attested), from mære ("boundary, landmark") + mann ("man").  The word "mere" in modern English, meaning "lake" or "pond," comes from a different Old English word.
