overdedicate: /ˌoʊvərˈdɛdɪˌkeɪt/
verb
过度奉献；过分投入
To dedicate excessively or beyond what is reasonable or healthy.
He overdedicated himself to his work and neglected his family. 他过度投入工作，忽略了家人。
She overdedicated herself to the cause, sacrificing her own well-being. 她过分投入这项事业，牺牲了自己的健康。
近义词: overwork, overdo, overextend
反义词: neglect, underwork
词性: verb

overdedicated: /ˌoʊvərˈdɛdɪˌkeɪtɪd/
adjective
过度投入的；过分专注的
Excessively or unreasonably dedicated.
He was overdedicated to his job and rarely took time off.  他过度投入于工作，很少休息。
She felt overdedicated and burnt out from constantly volunteering. 她觉得自己过度投入志愿工作，感到精疲力竭。
近义词: overworked, overzealous, driven
反义词: relaxed, laid-back
词性: adjective
