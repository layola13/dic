melanochroite:/məˌlænəˈkroʊaɪt/ | n. |红铅矿，铬铅矿 |A rare mineral consisting of lead chromate. It is usually found as bright red, translucent crystals. |Melanochroite is a sought-after mineral by collectors. 红铅矿是收藏家梦寐以求的矿物。| The museum's collection includes a beautiful specimen of melanochroite.  博物馆的收藏包括一件美丽的红铅矿标本。| 近义词: crocoite, phoenicochroite | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
