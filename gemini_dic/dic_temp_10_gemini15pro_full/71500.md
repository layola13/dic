flangers:/ˈflændʒərz/| n. |镶边器，加法兰者|A device, especially one used to create a flanging effect on an electric guitar or other electronic instrument.  It creates a swirling, whooshing sound by mixing a slightly delayed signal with the original signal. |The guitarist used flangers to create a psychedelic sound. 吉他手使用镶边器制造迷幻的声音。|近义词：phaser, chorus|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "clean sound" or "dry signal")|noun


flange:/flændʒ/| n. |凸缘，法兰|A projecting flat rim, collar, or rib on an object, serving to strengthen or attach or (as on a wheel) to maintain position on a rail.|The flange of the pipe prevented leaks. 管道的凸缘防止泄漏。|近义词：rim, edge, lip|反义词: (No direct antonym) |noun

flange:/flændʒ/| v. |给…装凸缘，给…装法兰|To provide with a flange.|The workers flanged the steel beams together. 工人们将钢梁用凸缘连接在一起。|近义词：join, connect, attach|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "detach" or "separate")|verb
