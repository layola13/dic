leg-up:/ˈleɡ ʌp/
noun
帮助；提携；优势
a boost or assistance that helps someone achieve something.
My uncle gave me a leg-up when I started my business. 我的叔叔在我创业时给了我很多帮助。
The internship gave her a leg-up in her career.  这份实习经历让她在职业生涯中获得了优势。
help, assistance, boost, advantage, support
disadvantage, hindrance, obstacle
