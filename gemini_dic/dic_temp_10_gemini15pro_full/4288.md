airworthiest:/ˈɛərwɜːrðiɪst/| adj.|最适航的|Most suitable for safe flight; in the best condition for flying.|例句：This plane is the airworthiest in the fleet.  这架飞机是机队中最适航的。

例句：They chose the airworthiest aircraft for the long journey. 他们选择了最适航的飞机进行长途旅行。|近义词：safest, most reliable, soundest|反义词:unairworthy, unsafe, rickety|词性:形容词


词源: airworthy + -est (superlative suffix). Airworthy comes from air + worthy. Air refers to the atmosphere, and worthy means having merit or value.
