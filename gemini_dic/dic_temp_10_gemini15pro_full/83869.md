half-abandoned:/ˌhæf əˈbændənd/
adjective
半废弃的，几乎废弃的
partially abandoned; not completely deserted but showing signs of neglect.
The half-abandoned factory stood silhouetted against the twilight sky.  半废弃的工厂在暮色中映衬出来。
The half-abandoned house was a haven for stray cats. 半废弃的房子成了流浪猫的避难所。
near synonyms: semi-abandoned, partially deserted, neglected, derelict
antonyms: occupied, inhabited, thriving, flourishing
词性: 形容词
