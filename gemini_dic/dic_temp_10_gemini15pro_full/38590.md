concordatory:/kənˈkɔːrdətɔːri/
adjective
和谐的，一致的
Relating to or involving concord or agreement; tending to promote concord or agreement.
The government proposed a concordatory solution to the labor dispute. 政府提出了一个旨在解决劳资纠纷的和谐方案。
The two sides adopted a concordatory approach to negotiations. 双方采取了和谐一致的谈判方式。
近义词:harmonious, agreeable, conciliatory, pacific
反义词:discordant, antagonistic, conflicting
词性:adjective

concordatory:/kənˈkɔːrdətɔːri/
noun
协调者，和解人
A person who promotes concord or agreement. (Less common usage)
He acted as a concordatory between the warring factions. 他在交战派别之间充当了协调人。(不太常用)
The concordatory helped the two sides reach a compromise.  协调者帮助双方达成了妥协。(不太常用)
近义词:mediator, arbitrator, conciliator
反义词:agitator, instigator
词性:noun
