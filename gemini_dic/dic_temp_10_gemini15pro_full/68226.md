fairish:/ˈfeərɪʃ/| adj.| 尚可的，过得去的，还可以的 | Moderately fair; somewhat or rather fair. |  The food was fairish, not great but not bad. 食物还可以，不算好也不算坏。|  The weather was fairish, a little cloudy but mostly sunny.  天气还可以，有点多云但大部分时间晴朗。|Synonyms:  tolerable, decent, passable, adequate, average, so-so | Antonyms: excellent, superb, wonderful, terrible, awful |词性: 形容词
