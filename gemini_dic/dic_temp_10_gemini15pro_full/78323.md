gerry:/ˈdʒɛri/| v. n.|划分选区以谋求私利；不公正地改划选区；杰里蝾螈|To manipulate the boundaries of (an electoral constituency) so as to favor one party or class.|例句：The opposition party accused the government of gerrymandering the districts to maintain its power. 反对党指责政府为了维持其权力而对选区进行不公正的划分。

例句：The oddly shaped district is a result of gerrymandering. 这个形状奇怪的选区是不公正划分选区的结果。|近义词：manipulate, rig|反义词:fairly allocate|词性：动词、名词
