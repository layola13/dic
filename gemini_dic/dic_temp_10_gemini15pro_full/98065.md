inquietude:/ɪnˈkwaɪəˌtjuːd/
n.
不安，焦虑，担忧
A feeling of worry, nervousness, or unease.
She felt a growing inquietude about the future. 她对未来感到越来越不安。
The news of the approaching storm filled them with inquietude. 即将到来的暴风雨的消息使他们充满了焦虑。
近义词: anxiety, uneasiness, worry, apprehension, disquiet, perturbation
反义词: calmness, tranquility, serenity, peace, composure
词性: 名词

inquietude:/ɪnˈkwaɪəˌtjuːd/
词源:  From French *inquiétude*, from Late Latin *inquietudo*, from Latin *inquietus* ("uneasy, restless"), from *in-* ("not") + *quietus* ("quiet, calm").
