duplicate:/ˈduːplɪkət/ (US), /ˈdʒuːplɪkət/ (UK)| adj. n. vt.|复制的，副本；复制，重复|A duplicate is an exact copy of something.  To duplicate something is to make an exact copy of it.|例句：She made a duplicate of the key. 她复制了钥匙。|近义词：copy, replica, reproduce, clone|反义词：original, unique|词性: adjective

duplicate:/ˈduːplɪkət/ (US), /ˈdʒuːplɪkət/ (UK)| n.|复制品，副本|An exact copy of something.|例句：He kept the duplicate and gave me the original. 他保留了副本，把原件给了我。|近义词：copy, replica|反义词：original|词性: noun

duplicate:/ˈduːplɪkeɪt/ (US), /ˈdʒuːplɪkeɪt/ (UK)| vt.|复制，使加倍|To make an exact copy of something.|例句：The document was duplicated and sent to all the members. 文件被复制并发送给了所有成员。|近义词：copy, reproduce, replicate|反义词：n/a|词性: verb
