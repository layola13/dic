crystallized:/ˈkrɪstəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 结晶的；明确的；固定的 |  Formed into crystals; having become definite or fixed. |  The honey had crystallized in the jar.  蜂蜜在罐子里结晶了。|  crystallized sugar 结晶糖 | synonyms:  crystalized, crystalline, set, fixed, solidified, concrete, definite, formed  | antonyms: amorphous, fluid, indefinite, vague | adjective

crystallized:/ˈkrɪstəlaɪzd/ | v. | 结晶；使明确；使固定 | (Of a substance) form or cause to form crystals;  make or become definite or fixed. |  The events of that day crystallized her determination to become a doctor. 那天的事件使她坚定了要成为一名医生的决心。 |  crystallize out 析出 | synonyms: solidify, harden, congeal, coalesce,  | antonyms: melt, dissolve, liquefy | verb
