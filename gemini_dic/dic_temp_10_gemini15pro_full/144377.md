outstormed:/ˌaʊtˈstɔːrmd/
verb
战胜风暴，经受住风暴
To have weathered or survived a storm; to have surpassed or outdone in storminess.
The ship outstormed the hurricane and arrived safely in port. 这艘船经受住了飓风，安全抵达了港口。
近义词: weather, endure, survive, withstand
反义词: succumb

词性:v.
