awe:/ɔː/ | n. | 敬畏，惊叹 | a feeling of reverential respect mixed with fear or wonder | The vastness of the universe filled me with awe.  浩瀚的宇宙让我充满了敬畏。 | synonyms: wonder, amazement, reverence | antonyms: disdain, contempt | noun

awe:/ɔː/ | vt. | 使敬畏，使惊叹 | inspire with awe | The sight of the Grand Canyon awed us all. 大峡谷的景象使我们所有人都感到敬畏。 | synonyms: amaze, astound, impress | antonyms: bore, disappoint | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *agi, meaning "fear, terror," from Proto-Germanic *agō, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ogʰos, meaning "trouble, distress, fear."
