concettism: /kənˈsɛtɪzəm/ | n. | 巧思，巧妙的措辞，尤指意大利式的 | A stylistic device characterized by the use of elaborate and often strained conceits, especially prevalent in 17th-century Italian literature. |  The poet's work was full of concettism, making it difficult to understand at times. 这位诗人的作品充满了巧思，有时难以理解。 |  Conceit, witticism,  wordplay | - | noun
