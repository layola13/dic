fossulate:/ˈfɒsjʊleɪt/ (US) /ˈfɒsʒʊleɪt/ (UK)| v. | 挖洞，掘洞；（尤指动物）用鼻子或爪子拱土 | To dig or burrow, especially with the nose or paws, as an animal does. | The pig fossulated in the mud, searching for roots. 猪在泥里拱土，寻找根茎。| 近义词：burrow, dig, root, grub | 反义词: fill, cover | 词性: 动词

词源:  From Latin *fossulāre, frequentative of fossulāre “to dig little ditches,” from fossa “ditch.”
