/ˈhɔːlʃtætɪən/ | /ˈhɔːlstætɪən/ | adj. n. | 哈尔施塔特文化的；哈尔施塔特文化时期的人 | Relating to or denoting the prehistoric Iron Age culture of central Europe, characterized by richly furnished burials and the development of ironworking.  A person of the Hallstatt culture. | Example: The Hallstattian period saw significant advancements in metalworking. 哈尔施塔特时期见证了金属加工技术的重大进步。

| Example:  Archaeologists unearthed a Hallstattian sword in remarkable condition. 考古学家出土了一把保存完好的哈尔施塔特时期的剑。 | Synonym: Iron Age (broadly), La Tène (succeeding culture) | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but could consider) Bronze Age, Neolithic | word type: Adjective, Noun
