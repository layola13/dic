avocate:/ˈævəkət/ (US) /ˈædvəkət/ (UK)| v. n.|提倡，主张；拥护者，支持者|To publicly recommend or support a particular cause or policy; a person who publicly supports or recommends a particular cause or policy.|He advocated for stricter gun control laws. 他主张更严格的枪支管制法。 She is a strong advocate for animal rights. 她是一位坚定的动物权利倡导者。|近义词：support, champion, proponent, backer|反义词：oppose, opponent, critic|词性: verb, noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *advocatus*, past participle of *advocare* "to summon," from *ad-* "to" + *vocare* "to call."
