levanter:/ləˈvæntər/| n.|地中海东部强东风；黎凡特人|A strong easterly wind in the eastern Mediterranean.|The levanter blew fiercely, whipping the sea into a frenzy. 地中海东部强东风猛烈地吹着，把大海搅得狂怒。|近义词:easterly, east wind|反义词:westerly, west wind|词性:noun

Etymology: From Spanish *levante* "east," ultimately from Latin *levāre* "to raise."  The wind is so named because it appears to rise from the east.
