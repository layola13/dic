-omy:/-əmi/ | suffix | -学，-术 | A suffix used to form nouns that refer to a particular branch of knowledge, study, or practice. |  Derived from Greek -omia, from -omos "law, custom." |

例句：
Astronomy is the study of celestial objects.
天文学是对天体的研究。

The economy is struggling at the moment.
目前经济正处于困境。

近义词：-ology, -ics | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: suffix
