flummoxing:/ˈflʌməksɪŋ/| adj.|令人困惑的，令人迷惑的|Making you confused and unable to think clearly. |The flummoxing question left him speechless. 这个问题让他困惑得说不出话来。|Synonyms: bewildering, perplexing, confusing, baffling|Antonyms: clarifying, enlightening|adjective

flummox:/ˈflʌməks/| vt.|使困惑，使不知所措|To confuse someone so much that they do not know what to do. |The sudden change in plans completely flummoxed me. 计划的突然改变让我完全不知所措。|Synonyms: bewilder, perplex, confuse, baffle|Antonyms: clarify, enlighten|verb
