ornamentalize: /ˌɔːrnəˈmentəlaɪz/ | v. |装饰；美化|To make something ornamental; to decorate or embellish. |例句：They ornamentalized the cake with elaborate frosting. 他们用精致的糖霜装饰蛋糕。

例句：The architect ornamentalized the building with intricate carvings.  建筑师用复杂的雕刻装饰了这栋建筑。


近义词：decorate, adorn, embellish, beautify, garnish |反义词:strip, simplify|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From ornament + -al + -ize.  "Ornament" comes from the Old French "ornement," ultimately from the Latin "ornare" meaning "to equip, adorn."  "-al" forms adjectives, and "-ize" creates verbs meaning "to make or cause to be."
