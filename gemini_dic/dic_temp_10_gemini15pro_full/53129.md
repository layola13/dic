diploidal:/dɪˈplɔɪdəl/
adjective
二倍体的
Having two sets of chromosomes, one from each parent.
Most animal cells are diploid.  大多数动物细胞是二倍体。
The diploid number of chromosomes in humans is 46. 人类的二倍体染色体数是46。
近义词: euploid
反义词: haploid, monoploid
词性: adjective
词源: From Greek diplóos ‘double’ + -oid.
