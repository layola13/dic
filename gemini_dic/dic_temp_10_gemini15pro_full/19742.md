billbug:/ˈbɪlˌbʌɡ/| n. |象鼻虫|A type of weevil (snout beetle) whose larvae feed on the stems and roots of grasses, including cereal crops and lawns.  They are considered pests in agriculture and turfgrass.|The billbug infestation destroyed a significant portion of the wheat crop.|近义词：weevil, snout beetle|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
