excerptive:/ɪkˈsɜːrptɪv/
adjective
摘录的；选录的
Relating to or characterized by excerpting; tending to excerpt.
This anthology is an excerptive work, drawing together key passages from a range of philosophical texts. 这部选集是一部摘录作品，汇集了各种哲学文本中的关键段落。
The professor assigned an excerptive essay, requiring students to select and analyze important quotes from the novel. 教授布置了一篇摘录性论文，要求学生从小说中选择并分析重要的引文。
近义词: extractive, selective
反义词: inclusive, comprehensive
词性: adjective

词源:  From the Latin excerptus, past participle of excerpere "to pick out, pluck out," from ex- "out" + carpere "to pluck, gather."
