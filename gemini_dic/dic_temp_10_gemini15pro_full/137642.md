nonubiquitousness:/ˌnɒnjuːˈbɪkwɪtəsnəs/
| n. | 非普遍性；非无处不在 | The nonubiquitousness of internet access is still a problem in many developing countries.  互联网接入的非普遍性在许多发展中国家仍然是一个问题。 |  The study highlighted the nonubiquitousness of the species, showing it was not found in all habitats. 这项研究强调了该物种的非普遍性，表明它并非在所有栖息地都能找到。 | rarity, scarcity, infrequency | ubiquity, universality, omnipresence | noun

Etymology:
non- (prefix) from Latin meaning "not"
ubiquitous (adjective) from Latin ubique "everywhere"
-ness (suffix) forming nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality
