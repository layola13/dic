multisonous:/ˌmʌltɪˈsoʊnəs/| adj.|多音的，声音丰富的|Having many sounds; producing many sounds at once; full of sound.  Characterized by a variety of sounds, often harmonious or resonant.|The multisonous choir filled the cathedral with beautiful music. 多音的合唱团用优美的音乐充满了大教堂。|Synonyms: polyphonic, multi-toned, resonant, sonorous|Antonyms: quiet, silent, monotonous|adjective
