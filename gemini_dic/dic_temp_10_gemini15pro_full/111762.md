lodgeable:/ˈlɒdʒəbl/ (US) /ˈlɑːdʒəbl/| adj.|可住宿的，适于住宿的|Suitable for lodging; affording lodging; capable of being lodged.|The hotel offers lodgeable rooms at reasonable prices. 这家酒店以合理的价格提供可住宿的房间。|The apartment is small but lodgeable. 这间公寓很小，但可以住宿。|近义词：habitable, livable, occupiable|反义词：uninhabitable, unlivable|词性:形容词

词源:
From lodge + -able.  "Lodge" comes from Old French "loge," meaning a covered walkway or small house, ultimately from Frankish *lauba "shelter, foliage."  "-able" is a suffix indicating capability or suitability.
