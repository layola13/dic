monotonicity:/ˌmɒnəˈtɒnɪsɪti/ /ˌmɑːnəˈtɑːnɪsiːti/| n.|单调性，单调乏味|The monotonicity of the landscape made the journey tedious.  这片景色的单调乏味让旅程变得枯燥无味。|The function exhibits strict monotonicity. 这个函数呈现严格单调性。|近义词：dullness, tediousness, uniformity|反义词: variety, diversity|词性:noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *monotonia, from Ancient Greek μονότονος (monótonos, “monotonous”), from μόνος (mónos, “single, alone”) + τόνος (tónos, “tone, pitch”).
