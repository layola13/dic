out-boxed:/ˌaʊtˈbɒkst/ | adj. | 超越限制的，跳出条条框框的 |  Defeated in a competition or argument by someone using superior or unexpected tactics; thinking or behaving in a way not constrained by conventional rules or expectations.  It's a combination of "out-" (meaning surpassing or exceeding) and "boxed" (referring to being confined or limited). |  He felt completely out-boxed by his opponent's innovative strategy. 他感觉完全被对手创新的策略击败了。

The company was out-boxed by a smaller, more agile competitor.  这家公司被一家规模较小、更灵活的竞争对手击败了。| 近义词: outmaneuvered, outsmarted, surpassed | 反义词: confined, restricted, limited | 词性:形容词
