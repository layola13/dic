debarments:/dɪˈbɑːrmənts/
n.
禁止；停职；除名；阻止
The act of debarring someone, officially preventing someone from doing something, especially from entering a place or taking part in an activity.
The company faced debarments from several government contracts due to ethical violations.  由于违反道德，该公司面临多项政府合同的禁令。
Synonyms: exclusions, prohibitions, disqualifications, suspensions, bans
Antonyms: permissions, allowances, authorizations, approvals, inclusions
noun

debar:/dɪˈbɑːr/
vt.
禁止；阻止；妨碍
To officially prevent someone from doing something, especially from entering a place or taking part in an activity.
He was debarred from holding public office. 他被禁止担任公职。
Synonyms:  prohibit, prevent, preclude, forbid, exclude
Antonyms:  permit, allow, authorize, enable, include
verb
