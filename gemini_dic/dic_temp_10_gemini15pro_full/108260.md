launchpad:/ˈlɔːntʃpæd/ (US), /ˈlɔːntʃpæd/ (UK)| n.|发射台，起点，平台|A platform from which a rocket or spacecraft is launched. Also used metaphorically to describe a starting point for a project or career.|例句：The rocket blasted off from the launchpad. 火箭从发射台上发射升空。

例句：The internship served as a launchpad for her career in journalism.  这份实习是她新闻事业的起点。|近义词：springboard, platform, base, starting point|反义词: culmination, end point|词性:名词
