ambusher:/ˈæmbʊʃər/| n. |埋伏者；伏击者|A person who ambushes someone or something.  One who lies in wait to attack by surprise.|The ambushers were hidden in the trees. 埋伏者藏在树林里。|Synonyms: attacker, assailant, waylayer|Antonyms: victim, prey|noun
