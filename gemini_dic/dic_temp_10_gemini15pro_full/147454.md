overoffending:/ˌoʊvərəˈfɛndɪŋ/
adjective
过度冒犯的，过分得罪人的
The act of offending too much or too often; excessive offending.
He was known for his overoffending jokes that alienated many people. 他因其过分冒犯的笑话而疏远了很多人。
近义词:overly offensive, excessively offending
反义词:respectful, considerate
词性:adjective
