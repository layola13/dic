interestingly:/ˈɪntrɪstɪŋli/ | adv. | 有趣地，引人入胜地 |  In a way that provokes interest or curiosity. | Interestingly, the results of the study contradicted previous findings. 有趣的是，这项研究的结果与之前的发现相矛盾。| Interestingly, he never mentioned the incident again.  有趣的是，他再也没有提过这件事。| 近义词: remarkably, surprisingly, curiously, fascinatingly | 反义词: boringly, dully, tediously | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From interesting + -ly.  "Interesting" comes from the late Middle English "enteressen" meaning "to concern, be of importance," which evolved from the Old French "interesser." The suffix "-ly" is a common adverbial ending.
