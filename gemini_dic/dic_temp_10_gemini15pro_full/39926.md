constricted:/kənˈstrɪktɪd/
adjective
收缩的，狭窄的，受限制的
Made narrower or tighter.
例句：The constricted blood vessels reduced blood flow. 血管收缩减少了血流量。

例句：His breathing was constricted by the tight bandage. 绷带太紧，他的呼吸受到了限制。

近义词：narrowed, tightened, compressed, restricted
反义词：expanded, dilated, widened, enlarged
词性: adjective

constrict:/kənˈstrɪkt/
verb
收缩，压缩，限制
To make narrower or tighter, to squeeze.
例句：Fear constricted her throat.  恐惧让她喉咙发紧。

例句：The government's policies have constricted the economy. 政府的政策限制了经济发展。

近义词：compress, squeeze, tighten, narrow
反义词：expand, dilate, widen, enlarge
词性: verb

词源: From Latin *constrictus*, past participle of *constringere*  "to bind together," from *com-* "together" + *stringere* "to draw tight."
