gerontologist:/ˌdʒɛrənˈtɒlədʒɪst/ | n. |老年学家；老年医学专家|A gerontologist is a specialist who studies aging and the problems of old people.  老年学家是研究衰老和老年人问题的专家。|例句：The gerontologist spoke about the importance of maintaining social connections in later life. 老年学家谈到了在晚年保持社会联系的重要性。

例句：My grandmother consults a gerontologist for advice on healthy aging.  我的祖母咨询了一位老年学家，寻求健康老龄化的建议。

|近义词：geriatrician|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
