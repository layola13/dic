exstipulate: /ɛkˈstɪpjʊleɪt/
verb
明确规定；约定；讲明
To specify explicitly as a condition of an agreement.
The contract exstipulates that the work must be completed within six months.  合同明确规定，工作必须在六个月内完成。
The lease exstipulates no pets are allowed. 租约明确规定不允许养宠物。
近义词: specify, stipulate, prescribe, define
反义词: imply, suggest, insinuate
词性: verb
词源: From Latin ex- (out) + stipula (stalk, straw), originally referring to breaking a straw as a way of making an agreement.
