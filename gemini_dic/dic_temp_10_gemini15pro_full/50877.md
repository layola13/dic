desensitize:/ˌdiːˈsɛnsɪtaɪz/ |vt.|使脱敏，使麻木|To make someone less sensitive to something, especially something unpleasant, by repeated exposure to it.  |Repeated exposure to violence in the media can desensitize people to real-life suffering.  媒体上反复出现的暴力内容会使人们对现实生活中的苦难麻木。|近义词:numb, dull, inure|反义词:sensitize, sharpen|verb

desensitizes:/ˌdiːˈsɛnsɪtaɪzes/ |vt.|使脱敏，使麻木（第三人称单数形式）|The third-person singular present tense form of "desensitize," meaning to make someone less sensitive to something through repeated exposure. |Playing violent video games desensitizes him to real-world violence. 玩暴力电子游戏使他对现实世界的暴力麻木了。 |近义词:numbs, dulls, inures|反义词:sensitizes, sharpens|verb
