despiting: /dɪˈspaɪtɪŋ/ | preposition | 尽管，虽然 | This word is not standard English. The correct form is "despite."  It means "in spite of" or "notwithstanding." |  (Incorrect usage) Despiting the rain, they went for a walk. | 近义词: despite, notwithstanding, in spite of, regardless of | 反义词: because of, due to, owing to | 词性: (incorrectly used as a) preposition


词源:  "Despite" comes from the Old French word "despit" meaning "contempt" or "scorn."  There is no etymologically correct form "despiting."
