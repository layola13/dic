pasquin:/ˈpæskwɪn/ |n.| 讽刺诗；匿名诽谤；讽刺的场所或媒介|A satire or lampoon; a place or medium for posting or publishing satires. |Example: The statue became a pasquin for anonymous political commentary. 这座雕像成了匿名政治评论的场所。

Example:  He used his blog as a pasquin to criticize the government.  他用自己的博客作为讽刺政府的工具。 | Synonym:  lampoon, satire, libel | Antonym:  eulogy, panegyric | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *pasquino*, name of a statue in Rome to which satirical verses were affixed, perhaps from the name of a cobbler or tailor noted for his wit.  Ultimately of uncertain origin.
