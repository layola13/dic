animatingly:/ˈænɪmeɪtɪŋli/
| adv. |活泼地，生动地|In a way that shows excitement and enthusiasm.|She spoke animatingly about her new project. 她充满激情地谈论着她的新项目。|Synonyms: lively, spirited, vivaciously, energetically|Antonyms: listlessly, dully, apathetically|词性:副词

Etymology: From animate + -ing + -ly.  "Animate" comes from the Latin "animare," meaning "to give life to."
