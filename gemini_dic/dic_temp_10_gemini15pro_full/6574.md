anabar:/ˈænəbɑːr/
noun
辰砂，朱砂，硫化汞
a bright red mineral consisting of mercury sulfide, used as the principal ore of mercury.
Cinnabar, or ananar, is a toxic mercury sulfide mineral.  辰砂，或称朱砂，是一种有毒的硫化汞矿物。
The ancient Egyptians used ananar as a pigment. 古埃及人使用朱砂作为颜料。
synonyms: cinnabar, vermillion
antonyms: None (Anabar is a specific mineral, not a concept with an opposite.)
