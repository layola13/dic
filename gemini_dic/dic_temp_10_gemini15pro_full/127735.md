negotiable:/nɪˈɡoʊʃiəbl/ /nɪˈɡəʊʃəbl/| adj.|可协商的，可转让的|Open to discussion or modification; able to be transferred by endorsement or delivery.|The price is negotiable.  价格是可以协商的。|synonyms: discussable, adjustable, transferable|antonyms: non-negotiable, fixed, firm|adjective

negotiable:/nɪˈɡoʊʃiəbl/ /nɪˈɡəʊʃəbl/| n.|可转让票据|A document, such as a check or promissory note, that can be transferred by endorsement or delivery.|The company issued negotiable bonds. 公司发行了可转让债券。|synonyms: instrument, security|antonyms: N/A|noun
