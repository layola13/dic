gayness:/ˈɡeɪnɪs/
n.
快乐，高兴; 同性恋
The state or quality of being gay.  This can refer to happiness or to homosexuality.

He radiated a youthful gayness. 他浑身散发着年轻人的快乐。

His gayness was something he kept hidden from his family. 他的同性恋是他对家人隐瞒的事情。

近义词：happiness, cheerfulness (for happiness); homosexuality (for sexuality)
反义词：sadness, unhappiness (for happiness); heterosexuality (for sexuality)
词性: 名词
