anti-predator:/ˈæntiˌprɛdətər/| adj.|反捕食者的，反掠食者的|Relating to or denoting behavior by prey animals aimed at avoiding detection or capture by predators.|The gazelle's speed is an example of an anti-predator adaptation.|近义词：defensive, protective|反义词:predatory|词性:adjective

anti-predator:/ˈæntiˌprɛdətər/| n.|反捕食者，反掠食者（指生物体或其特征）|An adaptation or behavior that helps an organism avoid being preyed upon.|Camouflage is a common anti-predator.|近义词：defense, protection|反义词:predator|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix): from Greek anti- "against, opposite of, instead of, in place of"
predator: from Late Middle English predatour, from Old French predateur, from Latin praedator "plunderer," from praedari "to plunder, rob," from praeda "booty, plunder"
