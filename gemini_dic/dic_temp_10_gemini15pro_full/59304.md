ear-splitting:/ˈɪərˌsplɪtɪŋ/| adj.|震耳欲聋的，刺耳的|Extremely loud and harsh, as if capable of splitting the eardrums.|The ear-splitting sound of the explosion made everyone jump. 爆炸声震耳欲聋，吓了所有人一跳.|近义词：deafening, piercing, loud, shrill|反义词:quiet, soft, faint|词性:形容词
Etymology:  'ear' (Old English *ēare) + 'splitting' (present participle of 'split' - Old English *splītan - to divide lengthwise) - literally "dividing the ear".
