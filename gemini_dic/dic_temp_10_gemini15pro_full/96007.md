incursive:/ɪnˈkɜːrsɪv/| adj.|草书的，连笔的；侵略的，入侵的|Characterized by running or flowing together; aggressively entering another's territory.|Her cursive handwriting was elegant and difficult to read. 她的草书优雅但难以辨认。|The army made an incursive maneuver into enemy territory.  军队对敌方领土进行了入侵行动。|近义词:flowing, running, aggressive, invasive|反义词:disjointed, separate, defensive, retreating|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Late Latin *incursīvus*, from Latin incursus (“a running into, an attack, invasion”), past participle of incurro (“run into, attack”), from in- (“into, in, on, against”) + curro (“run”).
