bled:/bled/ | v. | 流血; 失血; 悲痛; 大量赔钱 | past tense and past participle of *bleed* | 
He bled profusely from the wound. 他伤口流血不止。
The company bled money for years. 这家公司多年来一直在亏损。| 近义词: hemorrhaged, leaked, lost | 反义词: healed, clotted | 词性: 动词

词源:
From Middle English *bleden*, from Old English *blēdan*, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch *bloeden* and German *bluten*.
