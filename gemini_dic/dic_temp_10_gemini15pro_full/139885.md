odal:/ˈoʊdəl/| adj. n.|祖传的，祖传财产|Of or relating to inherited land, especially in Norway or other Scandinavian countries.  Also, the land itself.  Refers to a system of land ownership where the title has been held by the same family for generations and cannot be alienated. |Example:  The family's odal farm had been in their possession for centuries.  这个家族的祖传农场已经拥有几个世纪了。

Example:  He claimed odal rights to the property. 他声称对这处房产拥有祖传权利。|近义词：hereditary, ancestral, inherited|反义词：acquired|词性：adjective, noun
