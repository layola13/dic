frere:/frɛər/| n. |兄弟；修士；<古>同僚|A male member of a religious order, not a priest; a friar.  In older English, it could also mean "brother" or "colleague". |He joined the order as a young frere. 他年轻时作为修士加入了教会。|近义词：friar, brother, monk (in some contexts)|反义词:sister, nun (in some contexts)|noun
