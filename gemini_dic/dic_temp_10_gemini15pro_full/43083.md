cowriters:/ˈkoʊˌraɪtərz/| n. |合著者，共同撰稿人|A person who writes something with another person or other people.|例句：The song had two cowriters. 这首歌有两个共同的作者。|例句：She and her husband are cowriters on the new play. 她和她的丈夫是这部新剧的共同编剧。|近义词：collaborators, joint authors|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly sole author)|词性:noun
