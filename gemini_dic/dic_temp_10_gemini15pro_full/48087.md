declassified: /diːˈklæsɪfaɪd/ | adj. |解密的，撤销机密的 |  No longer officially secret.  It refers to information or documents that were previously classified (secret) but have been made available to the public. | The government declassified the documents related to the Cold War. 政府解密了与冷战相关的文件。| The declassified report revealed new information about the incident. 解密后的报告揭露了关于该事件的新信息。|  disclosed, revealed, public | classified, confidential, secret | adjective

declassify: /diːˈklæsɪfaɪ/ | vt. |解密，撤销机密 | To remove the security classification from (information, a document, etc.), making it available to the public. | The agency decided to declassify the files after 50 years.  该机构决定在50年后将这些文件解密。| They declassified the mission details after its successful completion. 任务成功完成后，他们解密了任务细节。| disclose, reveal, release | classify, conceal, protect | verb
