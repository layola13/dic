miscataloging:/ˌmɪskætəlɔːɡɪŋ/
n.
错误分类，编目错误
The act of incorrectly classifying or categorizing something, especially in a library or archive.
The miscataloging of the book led to its being shelved in the wrong section. 这本书的错误分类导致它被放在了错误的区域。
The museum's miscataloging resulted in several artifacts being lost for years.  博物馆的错误分类导致几件文物丢失了多年。
misclassification, misfiling
correct cataloging
noun
