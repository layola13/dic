dysphonia:/dɪsˈfoʊniə/ /dɪsˈfəʊniə/
n.
发音困难，声音障碍
Difficulty in speaking due to a physical disorder of the mouth, tongue, throat, or vocal cords.
The singer suffered from dysphonia after a severe cold.  这位歌手在一次严重的感冒后患上了发音困难。
Dysphonia can be a symptom of various medical conditions. 发音困难可能是各种疾病的症状。
近义词:hoarseness, voice disorder
反义词:euphonia (优美的声音), clear voice (清晰的声音)
词性:noun

dysphonia:/dɪsˈfoʊniə/ /dɪsˈfəʊniə/
Etymology: From Greek *dys- “bad, difficult” + *phōnē “voice, sound”
词源：来自希腊语 *dys- “坏的，困难的” + *phōnē “声音”
