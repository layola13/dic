micrometer:/maɪˈkrɒmɪtər (UK) / maɪˈkrɑːmɪtər (US)| n.|千分尺，测微计|An instrument for measuring small distances or thicknesses.|The machinist used a micrometer to measure the thickness of the metal sheet.|近义词: caliper, gauge|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "ruler" for a less precise instrument)|noun
micron:/ˈmaɪkrɒn (UK) / ˈmaɪkrɑːn (US)| n.|微米，百万分之一米|A unit of length equal to one millionth of a meter.|The diameter of a human hair is about 75 microns.|近义词: micrometer (as a unit of length)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "kilometer" for a much larger unit)|noun
