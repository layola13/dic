注意事项: 
* afterwork can be both an adjective and a noun. As an adjective, it describes something that happens after work. As a noun, it refers to activities or socializing that takes place after work.
* While less common, "after work" (two words) can also be used.  When used as an adverbial phrase,  "after work" is the preferred form (e.g.,  I go to the gym after work).
