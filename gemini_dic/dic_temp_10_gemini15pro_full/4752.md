alfenide: /ˈælfɪnaɪd/
noun
磺胺嘧啶银
silver sulfadiazine; a topical antibacterial agent used in the treatment of burns.
Silver sulfadiazine cream is commonly used to prevent and treat infections in burn wounds. 磺胺嘧啶银乳膏通常用于预防和治疗烧伤创面的感染。
Synonyms: silver sulfadiazine, SSD cream
Antonyms: None (lack of direct antonym for a specific medication)
