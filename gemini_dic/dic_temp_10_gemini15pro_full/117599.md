megametre:/ˈmɛɡəˌmiːtər/ | n. |百万米，兆米|A unit of length equal to one million metres.|例句：The distance between the two cities is several megametres. 这两个城市之间的距离有几百万米。

例句：A megametre is a thousand kilometres. 一兆米等于一千公里。


|近义词：million meters|反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement)|词性:noun
