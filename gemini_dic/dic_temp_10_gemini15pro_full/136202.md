ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + reliable + -ness + -es.  "Reliable" comes from the French "relier" (to tie, bind) and the Latin "ligare" (to bind), suggesting something that can be depended upon.  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.  The suffix "-es" creates the plural form.
