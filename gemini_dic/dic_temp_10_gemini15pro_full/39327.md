conjecturably:/kənˈdʒɛktʃərəbli/
adverb
推测地，猜想地
Based on conjecture; by guesswork.  It describes something that is possibly true based on guesswork or incomplete information.
Conjecturably, the painting dates back to the 17th century.  这幅画推测可以追溯到17世纪。
Conjecturably, he will arrive tomorrow, but we haven't heard from him. 他大概明天会到，但我们还没有收到他的消息。
Synonyms: presumably, possibly, probably, likely, supposedly
Antonyms: certainly, definitely, undoubtedly, assuredly
词性: 副词
