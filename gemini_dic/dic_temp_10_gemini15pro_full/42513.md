counterintuitive:/ˌkaʊntərɪnˈtjuːɪtɪv/ /ˌkaʊntərɪnˈtuːɪtɪv/| adj.|违反直觉的；反直觉的|Contrary to what intuition or common sense would suggest.|例句：It may seem counterintuitive, but sometimes less is more.  这似乎违反直觉，但有时少即是多。

例句：The findings of the study were counterintuitive. 这项研究的结果出人意料。

近义词：unexpected, surprising, illogical, irrational|反义词：intuitive, logical, rational|词性:adjective
