disquieted:/dɪsˈkwaɪətɪd/
adjective
不安的，焦虑的
feeling or showing worry and anxiety.
例句：She felt disquieted by the news. 她被这消息弄得心神不安。

例句：The strange silence disquieted him.  奇怪的寂静让他感到不安。

近义词：anxious, uneasy, worried, apprehensive, perturbed
反义词：calm, tranquil, peaceful, serene, composed
词性: adjective
