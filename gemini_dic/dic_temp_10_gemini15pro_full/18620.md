benthamite:/ˈbɛnθəˌmaɪt/ | n. adj. | 边沁主义者；功利主义者 | A follower of the philosophy of Jeremy Bentham, especially in his advocacy of utilitarianism. |  Example: As a Benthamite, she believed in the greatest good for the greatest number. 作为一名边沁主义者，她相信最大多数人的最大利益。| Example: Benthamite principles influenced 19th-century social reform. 边沁主义原则影响了19世纪的社会改革。 | Synonym: utilitarian, pragmatist | Antonym: altruist, idealist | noun
benthamite:/ˈbɛnθəˌmaɪt/ | n. adj. | 边沁主义的；功利主义的 | Relating to or characteristic of the philosophy of Jeremy Bentham, especially his utilitarianism. | Example:  The government adopted a Benthamite approach to penal reform. 政府对刑罚改革采取了功利主义的做法。 | Example: His Benthamite views on education emphasized practical skills. 他对教育的功利主义观点强调实用技能。 | Synonym: utilitarian, pragmatic | Antonym: altruistic, idealistic | adjective
