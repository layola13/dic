effortless:/ˈefərtlɪs/| adj.|毫不费力的，轻松的|Achieved without apparent effort; easy.|She danced with effortless grace. 她舞姿优美，毫不费力。|近义词：easy, simple, smooth, natural, facile|反义词：difficult, laborious, strenuous, arduous|词性:形容词
词源：From effort + -less.  Effort comes from Old French esforcier, ultimately from Latin ex- 'out' + fortis 'strong'.
