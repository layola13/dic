misbestows:/ˌmɪsbɪˈstoʊz/
verb
错误地给予；滥施；浪费
To give something, especially love, affection, or praise, to an undeserving recipient.
He misbestowed his affections on a woman who did not love him. 他把感情错付给了一个不爱他的女人。
synonyms: misapply, misuse, squander, waste
antonyms: bestow properly, use wisely, allocate correctly
词性: 动词
