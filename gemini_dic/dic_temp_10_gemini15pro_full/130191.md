non-satiation:/ˌnɒn seɪˈʃeɪʃən/ | n. | 不满足；不知足 |The state of not being satisfied; the inability to be satisfied.  It is often used in economics to describe the assumption that people always want more of a good or service. |  His non-satiation for knowledge led him to read hundreds of books.  他对知识的不知足使他读了数百本书。| insatiability, greed, avarice | contentment, satisfaction | noun

non-satiety:/ˌnɒn səˈtaɪəti/ | n. |不满足；不知足 | A synonym for non-satiation, emphasizing a lack of fullness or satisfaction. | Her non-satiety with material possessions was a constant source of stress. 她对物质财富的不知足是她持续压力来源。 | insatiability, greed, unsatedness | contentment, satiety, satisfaction | noun
