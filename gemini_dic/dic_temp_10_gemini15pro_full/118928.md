metemptosis:/ˌmɛtɛmpˈtoʊsɪs/
n.
灵魂转世;  再生
The transmigration of souls; reincarnation.
例句：Some religions believe in metemptosis, where the soul is reborn into a new body after death.  一些宗教相信灵魂转世，即灵魂在死后重生到一个新的身体里。

例句：The concept of metemptosis offers comfort to those who mourn the loss of loved ones. 灵魂转世的概念为那些哀悼失去亲人的人提供了安慰。

近义词：reincarnation, transmigration, rebirth
反义词：death, demise
