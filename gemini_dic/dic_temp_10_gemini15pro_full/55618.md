diswarren:/dɪsˈwɒrən/
verb
使脱离保证; 使丧失资格
To deprive of a warren or the right of warren; to throw open a warren.
The lord of the manor diswarrened the common land.  庄园主取消了公共土地的围猎权。
disfranchise, disqualify
authorize, entitle
