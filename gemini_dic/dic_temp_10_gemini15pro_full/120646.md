mis-routed:/ˌmɪsˈruːtɪd/
adjective
中文翻译：路由错误的，传送错误的
英文解析：incorrectly routed; sent to the wrong destination.  Formed by adding the prefix "mis-" (meaning "wrong" or "badly") to the verb "route" (meaning to send something along a particular path).
例句：The mis-routed package finally arrived a week late. 路由错误的包裹最终晚了一周才到达。

例句：A mis-routed email landed in my inbox by mistake. 一封传送错误的电子邮件错误地进入了我的收件箱。

近义词：misdirected, wrongly routed, incorrectly addressed
反义词：correctly routed, properly addressed
词性：adjective
