nonfixations:/ˌnɒn.fɪkˈseɪ.ʃənz/ | n. | 非固定, 非固着 |  The absence of fixations, or the state of not being fixed or attached.  It refers to the lack of firm attachments or the state of being unfixed or unattached, often in a psychological or emotional context, but can also be used in a physical sense. |  His nonfixations on material possessions allowed him to live a simpler life. 他对物质财富的非执着让他过上了更简单的生活。

The artist's nonfixations on a single style allowed for a diverse body of work.  这位艺术家不拘泥于单一风格，创作了丰富多样的作品。 | Near Antonyms: fixations, attachments | Antonyms: fixations | noun
