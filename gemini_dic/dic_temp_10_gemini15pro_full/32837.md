cholecystotomy:/ˌkoʊləˌsɪsˈtɑːtəmi/ /ˌkɒləˌsɪsˈtɒtəmi/
noun
胆囊切开术
Surgical incision into the gallbladder.
The patient underwent a cholecystotomy to remove gallstones.  病人接受了胆囊切开术以移除胆结石。
cholecystectomy, cholecystostomy
