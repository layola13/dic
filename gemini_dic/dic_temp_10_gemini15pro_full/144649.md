outwrangled:/ˌaʊtˈræŋɡəld/ |v. |胜过，辩论赢过，比...更胜一筹| To have outdone someone in wrangling; to have surpassed or defeated someone in argument or dispute. |例句：She outwrangled him in the debate and secured the victory. 她在辩论中胜过他，取得了胜利。|近义词：outargued, outdebated, bested, defeated, surpassed|反义词：defeated, lost to, succumbed to|词性：动词

词源:
From "out-" (meaning "exceeding," "surpassing") + "wrangle" (meaning "to argue or dispute noisily and persistently").
