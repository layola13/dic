angles:/ˈæŋɡəlz/ | n. |角，角度|The sum of the angles in a triangle is 180 degrees. 三角形的内角和是180度。|Example: The architect carefully measured the angles of the roof. 建筑师仔细测量了屋顶的角度。|Synonyms: corners, bends|Antonyms: curves, rounds|noun


angle:/ˈæŋɡəl/ | n. |角度,立场,观点|He approached the problem from a different angle. 他从不同的角度来处理这个问题。|Example:  The journalist tried to get a fresh angle on the story. 记者试图从一个新的角度报道这个故事。|Synonyms: perspective, viewpoint, standpoint|Antonyms: - |noun

angle:/ˈæŋɡəl/ | v. |钓鱼,谋取|He was angling for a promotion. 他在谋求晋升。|Example: She angled her chair so she could see the screen better. 她调整了椅子的角度以便更好地看到屏幕。|Synonyms: fish, scheme, maneuver|Antonyms: - |verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French angle, from Latin angulus "corner, angle,"  related to angustus "narrow."  The geometric sense is the primary meaning.
