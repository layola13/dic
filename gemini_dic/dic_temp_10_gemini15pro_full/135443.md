nonpersuadable:/ˌnɒnpərˈsweɪdəbl/ | /ˌnɑːnpərˈsweɪdəbl/
adjective
不可说服的；固执的
Not able to be persuaded; unyielding.
He held a nonpersuadable belief in his own righteousness. 他对自己正直的信念坚不可摧。
Near Synonyms:  intransigent, unyielding, immovable, inflexible, obstinate, stubborn, headstrong
Antonyms: persuadable, amenable, susceptible, receptive, flexible
词性: 形容词
