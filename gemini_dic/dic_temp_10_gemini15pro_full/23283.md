boxfish:/ˈbɒksfɪʃ/ (UK) /ˈbɑːksfɪʃ/ (US)| n. |箱鲀|A type of fish encased in a bony shell, typically square or triangular in shape.|The boxfish is a slow swimmer but its bony shell protects it from predators. 箱鲀游得很慢，但它的骨质外壳保护它免受捕食者的侵害。|近义词：cowfish, trunkfish|反义词: None|名词

boxfish:/ˈbɒksfɪʃ/ (UK) /ˈbɑːksfɪʃ/ (US)| adj. (rare) |箱鲀似的|Resembling or characteristic of a boxfish, especially in shape.|Its boxfish-like shape made it difficult to maneuver in tight spaces. 它箱鲀似的外形使得它在狭窄的空间里难以移动。|近义词：cubic, boxy|反义词: streamlined|形容词
