artily:/ˈɑːrtɪli/ | adv. | 巧妙地；狡猾地；做作地 | In a manner that suggests artificiality or affected refinement; with contrived sophistication. | She laughed artily, trying to appear amused. 她装腔作势地笑着，试图显得开心。 |  Synonyms: affectedly, theatrically, pretentiously, artificially, ostentatiously | Antonyms: naturally, genuinely, sincerely, unpretentiously | adverb

artily:/ˈɑːrtɪli/ | adj. (obsolete, rare) |  Skilled in art; artistic.  |  He was an artily crafted sword.  这是一把工艺精湛的剑。(这个用法已过时)  | Synonyms: artistic (in the obsolete sense of skilled) | Antonyms: artless, clumsy  | adjective
