navigates:/ˈnævɪɡeɪts/| v.|航行，导航，浏览，设法应对|He expertly navigates the complex world of international finance. 他熟练地应对了复杂的国际金融世界。|例句：The captain skillfully navigates the ship through the narrow channel. 船长熟练地驾驶船只通过狭窄的航道。|近义词：travels, steers, directs, manages, handles|反义词:loses, wanders|词性:动词

Etymology: From Latin *navigatus*, past participle of *navigare* "to sail, sail over, navigate," from *navis* "ship" + *agere* "to drive, lead, act."
