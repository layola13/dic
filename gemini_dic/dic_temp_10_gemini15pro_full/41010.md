coparcenary:/kəʊˈpɑːsənəri/
n.
共同继承；共同继承权；共同继承人
Joint heirship; the joint inheritance itself; joint heirs
The property was held in coparcenary by the three sisters. 这处房产由三姐妹共同继承。
近义词: joint tenancy, joint inheritance
反义词: sole ownership, severalty
