lurcher:/ˈlɜːrtʃər/ (UK), /ˈlɜːrtʃər/ (US)| n.|猎兔犬;杂种猎犬|A dog, especially a cross-bred sighthound, noted for its speed and keen eyesight, often used for hunting rabbits and other small game.|The farmer used his lurcher to control the rabbit population. 农民用他的猎兔犬来控制兔子的数量。|Synonyms: sighthound, hound, hunting dog|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific breed, could use "pet dog" for a contrasting concept)|Noun
