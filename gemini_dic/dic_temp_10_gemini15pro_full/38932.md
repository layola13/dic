confidante:/ˌkɒnfɪˈdænt/ /ˌkɑːnfɪˈdænt/| n. |知己，密友（尤指女性）| a person, especially a woman, who you trust and share your feelings and secrets with |例句：She was her closest friend and confidante. 她是她最亲密的朋友和知己。

例句：He needed a confidante to share his worries with. 他需要一个知己来分担他的忧虑。

近义词：close friend, intimate, confidant |反义词:enemy, foe|词性:noun
