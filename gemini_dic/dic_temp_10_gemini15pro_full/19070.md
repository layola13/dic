betook:/bɪˈtʊk/
verb
过去式
(literary or archaic) Go to
前往；离去
He betook himself to the library. 他去了图书馆。
近义词: went, repaired, proceeded
反义词: remained, stayed
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bitaken, from Old English *betācan (“to commit, entrust, hand over, give, deliver, assign, commend, assign, show, point out, declare, announce”), from be- + tācan (“to teach, show”). Cognate with Dutch betaken (“to betoken, signify”) and German bezeigen (“to show, demonstrate”).  More literally, "to take oneself to a place."
