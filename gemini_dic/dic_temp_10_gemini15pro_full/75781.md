fumade: /fjuːˈmeɪd/ | n. | 熏制的食物;  烟熏制品 |  A food that has been preserved and flavored by exposure to smoke. |  The restaurant specializes in fumade meats and cheeses.  这家餐厅专门经营烟熏肉类和奶酪。 | smoked, cured | fresh | noun


Etymology:  "fumade" likely comes from the French word "fumé," past participle of "fumer" (to smoke),  ultimately deriving from the Latin "fumus" (smoke).
