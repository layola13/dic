inkling:/ˈɪŋklɪŋ/| n.|暗示；略知；模糊的概念|a slight knowledge or suspicion; a hint|例句：I had an inkling that something was wrong. 我隐约觉得有些不对劲。

例句：She had no inkling of his true feelings. 她一点也不知道他的真实感受。

近义词：hint, suspicion, clue, suggestion, intimation|反义词：certainty, knowledge, assurance|词性：noun
