diathermometer:/ˌdaɪəθərˈmɒmɪtər/ | n. | 透热计 | An instrument for measuring the heat-conducting power of materials. | The diathermometer was used to compare the thermal conductivity of different types of insulation. 透热计用于比较不同类型绝缘材料的导热率。 | The scientist carefully calibrated the diathermometer before starting the experiment. 科学家在开始实验前仔细校准了透热计。 |  synonyms: calorimeter, thermometer | antonyms: N/A | noun

Etymology:
From dia- (through) + thermo- (heat) + -meter (measuring instrument).
