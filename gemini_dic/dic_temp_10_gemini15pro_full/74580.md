fraying:/ˈfreɪɪŋ/
fraying|n.|磨损，磨损处；争吵，冲突|the process of becoming worn at the edge and separating into threads|The fraying edges of the rug showed its age.  地毯磨损的边缘显示出它的年代。|近义词：unraveling, wearing, deteriorating|反义词:mending, repairing|词性:名词

fraying:/ˈfreɪɪŋ/
fraying|adj.|磨损的；紧张的|becoming worn at the edge and separating into threads; showing signs of strain or tension|The fraying rope needed to be replaced. 磨损的绳子需要更换。|近义词：worn, tattered, strained|反义词:intact, strong|词性:形容词


词源: From Middle English *freien, from Old French freier, froier (“to rub, fray”), from Frankish *frijōn, from Proto-Germanic *frijōną (“to rub”). Cognate with Old High German rīban (“to rub”), Old English frēon (“to free”).
