dannebrog:/ˈdænəˌbrɔːɡ/| n.|丹麦国旗|The national flag of Denmark, a white Scandinavian cross on a red background. It is the oldest continuously used national flag.|The Dannebrog flew proudly over the embassy. 丹麦国旗在使馆上空骄傲地飘扬。|近义词:Danish flag|反义词:None|词性:noun

Etymology: From Danish *Dannebrog*, literally "Danish cloth" or "Danes' cloth", from *Daner* "Danes" + *brog* "cloth". The *brog* element is related to Old English *bræc* "cloth", from Proto-Germanic *brekaną*.
