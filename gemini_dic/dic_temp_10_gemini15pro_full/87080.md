heptateuch:/ˈhɛptətjuːk/
n.
七经
The first seven books of the Old Testament (Genesis, Exodus, Leviticus, Numbers, Deuteronomy, Joshua, and Judges).
The scholar devoted years to studying the Heptateuch.  这位学者多年致力于研究七经。
近义词:Pentateuch (五经, 指的是前五卷), Septuagint (七十士译本)
反义词:None (没有明确的反义词)
词性:Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *hepta* (seven) + *teuchos* (tool, container, book).
