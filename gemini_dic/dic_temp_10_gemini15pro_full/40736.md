conveyancing:/kənˈveɪənsɪŋ/ | n. | 财产转让；产权过户 | The process of transferring legal ownership of property from one person to another. | The conveyancing on the house took longer than expected.  这栋房子的产权过户比预期的要长。 | 近义词: property transfer, title transfer | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

conveyanced: /kənˈveɪənst/ | adj. |  已转让的 (罕用) |  Having been conveyed or transferred, particularly in reference to property. | The conveyanced property is now legally owned by Mr. Smith. 已转让的房产现在由史密斯先生合法拥有. | 近义词: transferred | 反义词: untransferred | 词性: 形容词


词源: From the verb "convey" + "-ance" (suffix forming nouns denoting an action, process, or state).  "Convey" comes from Old French "conveier," from Latin "convehere" (to carry together).
