flectional /flɛkʃənəl/ | adj. | 屈折的，词尾变化的 | Relating to inflection; characterized by inflection.  A flectional language uses changes in word endings to express grammatical relations. |  English is a flectional language, unlike Mandarin Chinese. 英语是一种屈折语，不像汉语普通话。|  The flectional endings of verbs indicate tense and person. 动词的屈折词尾表示时态和人称。 |  inflectional, derivational | analytical, isolating | adjective
