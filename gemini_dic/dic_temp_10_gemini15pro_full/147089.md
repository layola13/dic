overlick:/ˈoʊvərˌlɪk/| v.| 过分修饰; 过分润色; (动物) 过度舔舐| To lick or groom excessively, often to the point of detriment.  Can also refer to overworking or refining something excessively, such as a piece of writing or a musical performance.| The cat overlicked its kitten, removing patches of fur.  这只猫过度舔舐它的小猫，舔掉了一块块的毛。|The editor felt the author had overlicked the prose, making it sound artificial. 编辑觉得作者把文章润色得太过头了，听起来很不自然。|近义词：overdo, overwork, overrefine, overpolish|反义词: neglect, underwork|词性:动词
