anti-idealisms:/ˈænˌtɪˌaɪˈdiːəlɪzəmz/ | n. | 反理想主义 |  The rejection of or opposition to idealisms, especially those that are considered unrealistic or impractical. |  The rise of anti-idealisms in the 20th century reflected a growing disillusionment with utopian visions. 20世纪反理想主义的兴起反映了人们对乌托邦愿景日益增长的幻灭。 |  cynicism, pessimism, realism | idealism, optimism | noun

Etymology:
anti- (prefix) from Greek anti- "against, opposite" + idealism from ideal + -ism
