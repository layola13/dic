hummable:/ˈhʌməbl/| adj.|可以哼唱的，易上口的|Something that is hummable is easy to hum, usually because it has a simple, pleasant tune.|The melody was simple and hummable. 这旋律简单易上口。|近义词：catchy, memorable, singable|反义词：unmemorable, forgettable|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From *hum* + *-able*.  The suffix *-able* forms adjectives meaning "capable of being" or "worthy of being".  *Hum* itself comes from imitative origin.
