blissom:/ˈblɪsəm/| n. vi.|花，开花，兴旺发达|A state of great happiness or joy; flourishing condition.  The trees are covered in blossom. 树上开满了花。  She watched her children blossom into successful adults. 她看着她的孩子们成长为成功的成年人。|The cherry trees are in full blossom. 樱花盛开。 The business is blossoming. 生意兴隆。|近义词：bloom, flower, flourish, thrive|反义词：wither, fade, decline|词性:名词/动词

Etymology:
From Old English *blīðsum "joyous, blithe, merry, kind, gracious, friendly," from blīðe "blithe, happy" (see blithe) + -sum. The sense of "blossom" developed from the notion of flowers as symbols of happiness and prosperity.
