carcinogenecities:/ˌkɑːrsɪnəʊdʒəˈnɪsɪtiz/ | n. | 致癌性；致癌力 | The plural form of carcinogenicity, referring to the potential of multiple substances or factors to cause cancer. |  Exposure to asbestos is one of the carcinogenecities that increases the risk of lung cancer. | 近义词: cancer-causing properties, oncogenic potential | 反义词:  anticarcinogenic properties | 词性: 名词


carcinogenicity:/ˌkɑːrsɪnəʊdʒəˈnɪsɪti/ | n. | 致癌性；致癌力 | The potential of a substance or agent to cause cancer. | The carcinogenicity of certain chemicals is well documented.  某些化学物质的致癌性已被充分证明。|  The International Agency for Research on Cancer (IARC) evaluates the carcinogenicity of various substances. 国际癌症研究机构 (IARC) 评估各种物质的致癌性。 | 近义词: cancer-causing potential, oncogenicity | 反义词:  anticarcinogenicity | 词性: 名词

词源:  From carcinogenic + -ity. Carcinogenic comes from  Greek karkínos "crab, cancer" + -genic, from Greek -genes "producing".
