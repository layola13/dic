gymnocarpous:/ˌdʒɪmnoʊˈkɑːrpəs/
adjective
裸果的，裸子果实的
Having naked fruits; specifically, (of fungi) having the hymenium exposed, rather than enclosed in a fruiting body.
The gymnocarpous nature of the fungus allowed for easy spore dispersal.  这种真菌裸果的特性使其孢子易于传播。
This species is distinguished by its gymnocarpous fruiting structures.  这个物种以其裸果的子实体结构而闻名。
近义词:achlamydeous, gymnospermous
反义词:angiocarpous, cleistocarpous
词性:adjective
