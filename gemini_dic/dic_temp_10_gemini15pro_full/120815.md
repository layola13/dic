misassigns:/ˌmɪs əˈsaɪnz/
verb
错误分配；分配错误
to assign wrongly or incorrectly.
The algorithm misassigns data to the wrong category.  该算法将数据错误地分配到错误的类别。
The teacher misassigned the essays, giving the wrong ones back to the students. 老师发错了作文，把错误的作文发回给了学生。
misallocate, misclassify
assign correctly
v.
