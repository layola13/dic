dangersome:/ˈdeɪndʒərsəm/ | adj. | 危险的，不安全的 | Full of danger; perilous.  This word is now largely archaic and replaced by "dangerous." | That dangersome path along the cliff should be avoided. 那条沿着悬崖的危险小路应该避开。 | 近义词: dangerous, perilous, hazardous, risky | 反义词: safe, secure | 词性: adjective
词源: From Middle English *dangerouse, *daungerous, from Anglo-Norman dangerus, dangerose, from Old French dangereux (“dangerous”), from danger (“danger”). Surface analysis is danger +‎ -some.
