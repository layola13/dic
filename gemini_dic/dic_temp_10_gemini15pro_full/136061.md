nonreception:/ˌnɑːnrɪˈsɛpʃən/
n.
未收到;接收不良;冷淡
Lack of reception or the fact of not being received; a failure to receive something expected. The act of not receiving or accepting something.  A cool or indifferent reception.
The nonreception of the broadcast caused frustration among viewers.  广播没有被接收到，这使得观众们感到沮丧。
近义词:nonacceptance, rejection, failure to receive
反义词:reception, acceptance
词性:名词
