nonfeverishnesses:/nɒnˈfiːvərɪʃnɪsɪz/
|n.| 无热状态；不发烧的状态 |The doctor noted the patient's nonfeverishness in his chart. 医生在病历上记录了病人不发烧的状态。|  The child's nonfeverishness was a good sign.  孩子不发烧是个好兆头。|近义词: absence of fever, afebrility |反义词: feverishness, febrility | 词性: 名词


Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin, meaning "not"
feverish: from Old English "fefer" (fever) + "-ish" (suffix forming adjectives)
-ness (suffix):  forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality
