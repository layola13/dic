go-in-for:/ɡəʊ ˈɪn fɔːr/ /ɡoʊ ˈɪn fɔːr/
phrasal verb
参加；从事；爱好；追求
to enter a competition, exam, etc. / to do something regularly as a hobby, sport, job, etc.
He went in for the 100-meter race.  他参加了100米赛跑。
She goes in for swimming and tennis. 她爱好游泳和网球。
synonyms: enter, participate in, take part in, engage in, pursue, indulge in
antonyms: abstain from, avoid, refrain from
verb phrase
