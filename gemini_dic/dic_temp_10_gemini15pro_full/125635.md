mumblings:/ˈmʌmblɪŋz/ | n. | 咕哝；喃喃自语；含糊不清的话 | Low, indistinct, and often unclear speech. |  He answered my question with some indistinct mumblings. 他含糊地咕哝了几句回答我的问题。 | synonyms: murmurs, mutterings, grumbles | antonyms: articulations, pronouncements | noun

mumbling:/ˈmʌmblɪŋ/ | adj. | 咕哝的；喃喃的；含糊不清的 | Speaking in a low, indistinct, and often unclear way. |  The mumbling beggar asked for spare change.  那个含糊不清的乞丐要零钱。| synonyms: inarticulate, unclear, indistinct | antonyms: articulate, clear, distinct | adjective
