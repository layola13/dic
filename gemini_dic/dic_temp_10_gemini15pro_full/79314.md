gleaming:/ˈɡliːmɪŋ/| adj.|闪闪发光的，闪亮的|Shining brightly; reflecting light.|The gleaming swords of the knights reflected the sunlight. 骑士们闪闪发光的剑反射着阳光。|近义词：shining, sparkling, glittering, radiant, luminous|反义词：dull, matte, dim, lackluster|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *glemynge, present participle of glemen  "to gleam," from Old English glǣm "gleam, brightness," from Proto-Germanic *glaimaz (source also of Old High German gleimo "gleam"), from PIE root *ghel- (1) "to shine, flash, glitter" (source also of Sanskrit jvalati "burns, flames," Latin helus, Greek selas "brightness, flash," Middle Irish glan "pure, bright").
