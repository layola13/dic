aid-climb:/ˈeɪd klaɪm/
noun
器械攀登
Climbing a rock face with the help of equipment, rather than by free climbing.
The climber used aid-climbing techniques to ascend the difficult cliff face.  攀登者使用器械攀登技术登上了这处难爬的峭壁。
近义词:  assisted climbing, technical climbing
反义词: free climbing
n.

/ˈeɪd klaɪm/
verb
进行器械攀登
To climb a rock face using equipment, rather than by free climbing.
They aid-climbed El Capitan in Yosemite. 他们在优胜美地国家公园进行了酋长岩的器械攀登。
近义词: ascend with aid
反义词: free climb
