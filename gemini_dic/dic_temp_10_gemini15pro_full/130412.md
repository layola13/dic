non-winner:/ˌnɒnˈwɪnər/ | n. | 非赢家；失败者 |  Someone or something that does not win a competition or prize.  A person who generally does not succeed in life. | He felt like a non-winner after failing the exam. 他考试不及格后感觉自己像个失败者。 | Synonyms: loser, failure, also-ran | Antonyms: winner, victor, champion | Noun

non-winner:/ˌnɒnˈwɪnər/ | adj. | 非赢家的；失败的 |  Characterized by not winning or succeeding. |  His non-winner attitude held him back from trying new things. 他失败者的态度阻碍了他尝试新事物。 | Synonyms: losing, unsuccessful, failing | Antonyms: winning, successful, triumphant | Adjective
