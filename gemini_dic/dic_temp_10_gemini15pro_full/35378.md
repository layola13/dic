co-occurs:/ˌkoʊ əˈkɜːrz/ | v. | 共现，同时出现 |  This word means that two or more things happen or exist together at the same time or in the same place. It often describes the relationship between linguistic features or events. | "The words 'bright' and 'sunny' often co-occur."  明亮和阳光明媚这两个词经常同时出现。 | synonyms: coexists, coincides, appears with | antonyms: avoids, excludes, separates | verb
