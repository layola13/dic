incompatible:/ˌɪnkəmˈpætəbl/ | adj. | 不相容的，不兼容的，合不来的 | Not able to exist or work together successfully because of basic differences. |  This software is incompatible with older operating systems. 这个软件与较旧的操作系统不兼容。

The two personalities were totally incompatible. 这两种性格完全不相容。 | 近义词: conflicting, clashing, discordant, antagonistic, irreconcilable | 反义词: compatible, harmonious, consistent | 词性: adjective
