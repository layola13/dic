hypertext:/ˈhaɪpərˌtekst/| n.|超文本，超链接|A system of storing and retrieving information by means of interlinked electronic documents.|Example 1:  Clicking on the hypertext link took me to a different webpage. 点击超文本链接把我带到了另一个网页。

Example 2: The internet is built on the concept of hypertext, allowing users to easily navigate between related information. 互联网建立在超文本的概念之上，允许用户轻松地在相关信息之间导航。|近义词：link, hyperlink|反义词: plain text|词性:noun
