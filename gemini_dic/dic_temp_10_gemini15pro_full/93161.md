ignited: /ɪɡˈnaɪtɪd/
verb (past participle)|点燃的，激起的|set (something) on fire.|The match ignited the bonfire. 火柴点燃了篝火。|近义词：kindled, lit, sparked, inflamed|反义词：extinguished, quenched, smothered
adjective|点燃的，激起的|having been set on fire; roused to strong emotion or action.|Her passion for music was ignited by her teacher. 她对音乐的热情被她的老师点燃了。|近义词：burning, blazing, fiery, aroused, impassioned|反义词：extinguished, quenched, apathetic

词性:
verb (past tense & past participle of ignite)
adjective

词源:
From Latin *ignītus*, past participle of *ignīre* (“to set on fire”), from *ignis* (“fire”).
