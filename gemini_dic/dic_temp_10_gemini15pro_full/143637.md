outjuts:/ˌaʊtˈdʒʌts/
verb
突出；伸出
projects or extends outward beyond a line or surface; protrudes
The rocky cliff outjuts into the sea.  岩石峭壁伸入海中。
近义词:protrude, project, stick out, jut out
反义词:recede, retract, sink in
词性:v.
