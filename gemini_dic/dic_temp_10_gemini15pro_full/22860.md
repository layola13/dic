bot's: /bɑːts/ | possessive pronoun |机器人的；聊天机器人的|  Contraction of "bot" (robot or internet bot) and the possessive clitic 's.  Indicates ownership or association with a bot.  |  The bot's responses were surprisingly human-like.  聊天机器人的回复出奇地像人类。| synonyms: robot's, chatbot's | antonyms: user's, human's | noun
