forkedly:/ˈfɔːrkɪdli/
adv.
分叉地，像叉子一样地
in a forked manner; resembling a fork.
The lightning struck forkedly across the sky. 闪电像叉子一样划过天空。
近义词:  branchingly, dividingly
反义词: straightly, linearly
词性: 副词

Etymology: From "forked" + "-ly".  "Forked" comes from the Old English "forcod," past participle of "forcian" (to fork).  "-ly" is a suffix used to form adverbs from adjectives.
