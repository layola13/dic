overlarge:/ˌoʊvərˈlɑːrdʒ/| adj.|过大的，特大的|Excessively large; larger than necessary or desirable.|The box was overlarge and difficult to carry. 这个盒子太大了，很难搬运。|The company car felt overlarge for just one person. 这辆公司配的车对一个人来说太大了。|近义词：oversized, huge, enormous, gigantic|反义词：small, tiny, miniature|词性:形容词

词源:
"over-" (prefix) + "large" (adjective)
"over-" comes from Old English "ofer," meaning "above, beyond, across."
"large" comes from Old French "large," ultimately from Latin "largus," meaning "abundant, copious, plentiful."
