Example:  He agnized his old friend in the crowd. 他在人群中认出了他的老朋友。

近义词: recognize, acknowledge, admit, concede, avow
反义词: deny, disavow, ignore, repudiate
词性: verb (动词)

词源: From Late Latin *agnōscere, from Latin ad- + gnōscere "to know," related to nōscere "to come to know"
