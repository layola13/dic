overdistorts:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈstɔːrts/
verb
过度扭曲，过分歪曲
to distort excessively; to twist out of shape or proportion to an excessive degree
The media overdistorted the politician's words, making it seem like he supported something he didn't. 媒体过度歪曲了这位政治家的话，使他看起来像是支持了他并不支持的事情。
The funhouse mirror overdistorted my reflection, making me look incredibly tall and thin.  哈哈镜过度扭曲了我的倒影，让我看起来又高又瘦。

近义词: exaggerate, misrepresent, warp
反义词: represent accurately, portray truthfully
词性: verb
