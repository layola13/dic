drugger:/ˈdrʌɡər/| n.|瘾君子；毒贩；药剂师(旧时用法)|A person who habitually uses narcotic drugs.  A person who sells illegal drugs.  (Archaic) A pharmacist or apothecary.|He was a known drugger in the neighborhood. 他是附近有名的瘾君子。|近义词：addict, junkie, dealer, pusher (for drug seller) |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

词源:  From drug + -er.  The suffix "-er" is used to form nouns denoting a person or thing that performs a specified action or is associated with a particular thing. In this case, it denotes someone associated with drugs, either using or selling them.
