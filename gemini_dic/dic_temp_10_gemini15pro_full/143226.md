outcuts: /ˈaʊtkʌts/
n.
外景拍摄; 外景片段; 剪辑掉的片段
Segments of film edited out of a finished movie, often scenes shot outdoors.
The director reviewed the outcuts to see if any could be used in the final edit. 导演审查了剪辑掉的片段，看看是否有任何片段可以用在最终剪辑中。
近义词: deleted scenes, offcuts, trims
反义词:  included footage
