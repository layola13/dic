decorated: /ˈdekəreɪtɪd/
adjective
装饰的，修饰的
Made more attractive by being decorated.
The Christmas tree was decorated with colorful lights and ornaments.  圣诞树装饰着五颜六色的灯和饰品。
The cake was decorated with fresh strawberries. 蛋糕用新鲜草莓装饰。

近义词: adorned, embellished, ornamented
反义词: plain, undecorated, bare
词性: adjective


词源:
From the Latin word "decorare," meaning "to adorn" or "to embellish."
