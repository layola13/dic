brigand:/ˈbrɪɡənd/| n.|土匪，强盗|A robber, especially one of a band of robbers in mountain or forest regions.|The brigands held the travelers hostage for ransom. 土匪挟持旅客为人质以索取赎金。|Synonyms: bandit, outlaw, robber, highwayman|Antonyms: lawman, police officer|Noun
