europeanizations:/ˌjʊərəpiːənaɪˈzeɪʃənz/
|n.|欧洲化（过程）|The process by which countries or regions become increasingly integrated into the European Union, adopting its laws, policies, and cultural norms.  This can involve political, economic, and social changes.|
例句：The europeanizations of Eastern European countries after the fall of the Soviet Union was a complex process.  东欧国家在苏联解体后的欧洲化进程是一个复杂的过程。

例句：Some scholars argue that europeanizations can lead to a loss of national identity.  一些学者认为欧洲化会导致民族认同的丧失。
|近义词：westernization, integration, assimilation|反义词：nationalization, isolation|词性: 名词
