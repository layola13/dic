bluebirds:/ˈbluːbɜːrdz/
n.
蓝鸟（复数）
A small North American thrush with bright blue plumage.
例句：The bluebirds returned to their nest in the spring.  春天，蓝鸟回到了它们的巢穴。

例句：I saw a flock of bluebirds flitting among the branches. 我看到一群蓝鸟在树枝间飞来飞去。

近义词：thrush, songbird
反义词：(无直接反义词，可以考虑 crow, raven 等颜色暗沉的鸟类)
词性: noun
