nonrestrictions:/ˌnɑːnrɪˈstrɪkʃənz/| n.|无限制，无约束|The absence of restrictions; a state of being unrestricted.|例句：The government has lifted all nonrestrictions on travel. 政府已经取消了所有旅行限制。|例句：Nonrestrictions on trade have boosted the economy.  贸易自由化促进了经济增长。|近义词：freedom, liberty, deregulation|反义词: restrictions, limitations, constraints|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + restriction (from Latin restrictus, past participle of restringere "to restrict").
