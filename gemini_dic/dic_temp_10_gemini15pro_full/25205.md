buddy-up:/ˈbʌdi ʌp/
verb
结成伙伴，成为搭档
To pair up with someone, usually for a specific purpose or activity.
例句：The teacher asked the students to buddy up for the science project. 老师让学生们结成小组做科学项目。

例句：They buddied up to share the cost of the taxi. 他们结伴而行以分摊出租车的费用。
近义词：pair up, team up, partner up
反义词：split up, separate
词性：verb
