mayoresses:/ˈmeɪərɪsɪz/
noun
女市长们; 女镇长们
The plural form of mayoress, referring to multiple female mayors or the wives of multiple mayors.
The mayoresses of several cities attended the conference.  几位女市长出席了会议。
The mayoresses were invited to a charity gala.  各位市长夫人被邀请参加慈善晚会。
Synonyms: female mayors, wives of mayors
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could use male mayors or husbands of mayors depending on context)
词性: noun
