berg:/bɜːɡ/ (UK), /bɜːrɡ/ (US)| n.|冰山，山|A large mass of ice floating in the sea, or a mountain.|例句：The ship struck an iceberg and sank. 这艘船撞上冰山沉没了。

例句：We climbed the steep berg. 我们爬上了陡峭的山峰。|近义词：mountain, glacier, iceberg|反义词:valley|noun
