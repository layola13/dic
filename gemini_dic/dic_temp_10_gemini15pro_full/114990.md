mandalic:/mænˈdælɪk/
adjective
曼陀罗的；曼荼罗的
Relating to or resembling a mandala.
例句：The therapist used mandalic art therapy to help the patient explore her inner self.  治疗师使用曼陀罗艺术疗法来帮助病人探索她的内心世界。

例句：The intricate mandalic patterns on the Tibetan sand painting were mesmerizing.  西藏沙画上复杂的曼陀罗图案令人着迷。

近义词:circular, geometric, symbolic
反义词:formless, shapeless
词性:adjective
