incursion:/ɪnˈkɜːrʒən/ /ɪnˈkɜːrʃən/| n. |入侵，袭击，侵犯|An invasion or attack, especially a sudden or brief one.  A hostile entrance into or invasion of a place or territory, especially a sudden one; a raid.|例句：The army launched an incursion into enemy territory.  军队对敌方领土发动了袭击。|例句：The incursion of social media into our lives has changed how we communicate. 社交媒体对我们生活的入侵改变了我们的沟通方式。|近义词：invasion, raid, attack, foray, encroachment|反义词: retreat, withdrawal|词性: noun
