braillists:/ˈbreɪlɪsts/
noun
盲文使用者；精通盲文的人
People who read and write Braille.  These individuals are skilled in using the tactile writing system.
Many braillists advocate for greater accessibility in public spaces. 许多盲文使用者倡导在公共场所提供更好的无障碍设施。
Braillists can read books and other materials using their sense of touch. 盲文使用者可以通过触觉阅读书籍和其他材料。

近义词: Braille readers, Braille users
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly sighted individuals if contrasting reading methods)
词性: noun
