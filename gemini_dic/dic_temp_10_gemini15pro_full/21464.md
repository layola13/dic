blowtorch: /ˈbləʊtɔːtʃ/ (UK) /ˈbloʊtɔːrtʃ/ (US)| n.| 喷灯 |A portable device producing a very hot flame by burning a mixture of fuel (usually a gas) and oxygen, used for brazing, soldering, or cutting metal.| He used a blowtorch to melt the ice on the lock. 他用喷灯融化了锁上的冰。|Synonyms: torch, burner, flamethrower|Antonyms: extinguisher, fire suppressant|noun


blowtorch: /ˈbləʊtɔːtʃ/ (UK) /ˈbloʊtɔːrtʃ/ (US)| v.| 用喷灯烧 |To apply the flame of a blowtorch to something.|He blowtorched the paint off the metal gate. 他用喷灯烧掉了金属门上的油漆。|Synonyms: sear, scorch, burn|Antonyms: cool, extinguish|verb
