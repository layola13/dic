endosmotic:/ˌɛndoʊzˈmɑːtɪk/ | adj. | 内渗的 | Relating to or involving endosmosis, the inward passage of a fluid through a semipermeable membrane toward a solution of higher concentration. |  The endosmotic pressure caused the cell to swell. 内渗压使细胞膨胀。| synonym: inward-osmotic | antonym: exosmotic | adjective

endosmosis:/ˌɛndoʊzˈmoʊsɪs/ | n. | 内渗 | The inward passage of a fluid through a semipermeable membrane toward a solution of higher concentration. | Endosmosis is the process responsible for the absorption of water by plant roots. 内渗是植物根系吸收水分的过程。| synonym: inward osmosis | antonym: exosmosis | noun
