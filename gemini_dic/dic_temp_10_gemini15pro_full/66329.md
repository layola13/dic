excretory:/ˈɛkskrɪtɔːri/ /ɪkˈskriːtəri/
adjective
排泄的；分泌的
Relating to or concerned with the excretion of waste products from the body.
The excretory system removes waste from the body.  排泄系统清除体内的废物。
The kidneys play a vital role in the excretory process. 肾脏在排泄过程中起着至关重要的作用。
近义词: eliminatory, secretory
反义词: retentive
词性: adjective
