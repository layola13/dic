homeworker:/ˈhoʊmˌwɜːrkər/ (US), /ˈhəʊmˌwɜːkə(r)/ (UK)| n.|在家工作者，家庭工人|A person who works from home, often using a computer and internet connection.|例句：Many companies are now employing homeworkers to reduce office costs. 许多公司现在雇佣在家工作者以降低办公室成本。

例句：She enjoys being a homeworker as it allows her flexibility. 她喜欢在家工作，因为这样可以让她更灵活地安排时间。

近义词：telecommuter, remote worker, freelancer|反义词:office worker, commuter|词性:noun
