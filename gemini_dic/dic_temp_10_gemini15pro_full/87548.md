heteromerous:/ˌhɛtərˈɒmərəs/| adj.|异节的，异构的|Having unequal or dissimilar parts or elements, especially applied to a flower with an unlike number of parts in each whorl (e.g., petals and sepals). It can also refer to structures composed of different subunits.|The heteromerous flower of the cactus has numerous petals and sepals.|Near synonyms: dissimilar, heterogeneous, varied, diverse|Antonym: homomerous|adjective
