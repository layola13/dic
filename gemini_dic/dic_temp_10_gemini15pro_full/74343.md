fragmented:/ˈfræɡˌmɛntɪd/ | adj. |碎片化的，片段式的|Broken into small parts or pieces; disconnected.|The report was fragmented and difficult to understand.  报告支离破碎，难以理解。| The ancient vase was fragmented beyond repair. 这只古代花瓶碎裂得无法修复。|近义词：broken, disjointed, fractured, piecemeal, segmented|反义词：whole, complete, unified, integrated|词性:形容词

词源: From the Latin word "fragmentum,"  meaning "a fragment" or "a piece broken off."
