hymning:/ˈhɪmɪŋ/ | n. | 赞美歌; 赞歌; 赞美; 歌颂 |The act of singing hymns or songs of praise.  |  The hymning of heroes is a common theme in epic poetry.  英雄赞歌是史诗中常见的主题。 | synonyms: praising, extolling, lauding, celebrating | antonyms: criticizing, condemning, denouncing | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *hymnynge*, from Old English *hymnian* (“to hymn, celebrate in song”), from *hymn* (“hymn”) + *-ian* (suffix forming verbs).  Cognate with West Frisian *himje* (“to hymn”), Dutch *hymnen* (“to intone a hymn”), German *hymnen* (“to hymn”).
