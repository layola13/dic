panhandler:/ˈpænˌhændlər/ (US), /ˈpænˌhændlə(r)/ (UK)| n. |乞丐；沿街乞讨者|A person who begs, especially one who approaches people in public places and asks for money.|The panhandler held out a cup and asked for spare change. 沿街乞讨者伸出一个杯子乞讨零钱。|近义词：beggar, mendicant, vagrant|反义词: philanthropist, donor|noun
