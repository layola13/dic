canopus:/kəˈnoʊpəs/ |n.|老人星；南极老人星|The brightest star in the constellation Carina, Canopus, is a supergiant. |老人星，船底座中最亮的恒星，是一颗超巨星。|Synonyms: Alpha Carinae, Suhail|Antonyms: N/A|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *Canōpus*, from Ancient Greek Κάνωβος (*Kánōbos*).  The name's origin is uncertain.  One possibility connects it to the mythological Canopus, the pilot of Menelaus's ship in Homer's *Odyssey*. Another suggests an Egyptian origin, perhaps meaning "Golden Earth".
