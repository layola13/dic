non-prejudicial:/ˌnɒnˌpredʒʊˈdɪʃəl/ | adj. | 无损害的，不带偏见的 | Not harmful or detrimental to someone or something; not biasing or prejudicing any party involved. | The investigation was conducted in a non-prejudicial manner. 调查以不带偏见的方式进行。|  The interim agreement is non-prejudicial to the final settlement.  临时协议不损害最终的和解。| Synonyms: impartial, unbiased, neutral, objective | Antonyms: prejudicial, biased, unfair, partial | adjective
