boulderized: /ˈboʊldəraɪzd/
verb
巨石化的，用巨石加固的
Modified or fortified with boulders.  Made more difficult, especially in rock climbing, by the addition of large handholds or footholds.
The route was boulderized to make it safer for beginners. 这条路线被巨石化了，以便让初学者更安全。
近义词: reinforced, fortified, augmented
反义词: weakened, diminished
词性: verb


boulderized: /ˈboʊldəraɪzd/
adjective
巨石化的，用巨石加固的
Modified or fortified with boulders.  Made more difficult, especially in rock climbing, by the addition of large handholds or footholds.
The boulderized cliff face looked unnatural. 巨石化的悬崖表面看起来很不自然。
近义词: reinforced, fortified, augmented
反义词: weakened, diminished
词性: adjective


词源:  From "boulder" (large rock) + "-ize" (to make or become).
