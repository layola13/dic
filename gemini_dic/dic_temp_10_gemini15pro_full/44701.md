crudités:/kruː.di.ˈteɪz/ | n. |  生鲜蔬菜拼盘; 蔬菜开胃小点 | A platter of raw vegetables, typically served as an appetizer with a dipping sauce. |  We started with a plate of crudités and hummus. 我们先吃了一盘蔬菜拼盘和鹰嘴豆泥。 |  appetizers, hors d'oeuvres, finger foods, snacks, starters | cooked dishes, main courses | noun

Etymology->
From French crudité, from cru "raw", from Latin crudus "raw".  The -ités ending is the French plural feminine suffix.
