maggiore:/mɑdˈdʒɔːre/ | adj. | greater; major; older (Italian) |  Used in music to indicate a major key or interval. Also used to mean "older" or "elder" (especially of siblings) or more generally, "greater" or "larger". |  The symphony is in D maggiore. (The symphony is in D major.) | Synonyms: greater, larger, elder, older, senior | Antonyms: minore (minor), younger, smaller, lesser | Adjective
