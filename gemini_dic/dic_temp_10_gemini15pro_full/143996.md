outrage:/ˈaʊtreɪdʒ/| n. vt.|暴行，侮辱；激怒|An act that violates accepted standards of behavior or morality.  To arouse intense anger, shock, or indignation in (someone).|The outrage committed against the innocent civilians will not be forgotten.  对无辜平民犯下的暴行不会被忘记。|He was outraged by the judge's decision. 他对法官的判决感到愤怒。|近义词：offense, insult, affront, infuriate, enrage|反义词:honor, respect, please, soothe|词性:名词，动词
