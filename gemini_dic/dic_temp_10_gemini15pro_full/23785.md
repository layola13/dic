brawnier:/ˈbrɔːnɪər/| adj.|更强壮的，更结实的|Comparative adjective of brawny.  More physically strong and muscular.|He's become much brawnier since he started working out. 他自从开始健身后变得强壮多了。|Near synonyms: stronger, more muscular, sturdier, huskier, beefier|Near antonyms: weaker, frailer, thinner, slighter|adjective

brawny:/ˈbrɔːni/| adj.|强壮的，结实的|Physically strong and muscular.|The brawny lumberjack easily lifted the heavy log.  强壮的伐木工人轻松地举起了沉重的原木。|Near synonyms: strong, muscular, sturdy, husky, beefy|Near antonyms: weak, frail, thin, slight|adjective

Etymology: From Middle English *brauni, from Old English *brūne "brown," of Germanic origin. The semantic shift from "brown" to "muscular" likely occurred through the association of a tanned, sun-weathered appearance with outdoor labor and physical strength.
