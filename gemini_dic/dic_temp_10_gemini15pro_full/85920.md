headlessnesses:/ˈhɛdlɪsnɪsɪz/
n.
无头状态；缺乏领导；无政府状态
The state of being headless; lack of a leader or guidance; anarchy.
The company experienced a period of headlessnesses after the CEO resigned unexpectedly.  公司在首席执行官意外辞职后经历了一段时间的群龙无首状态。
The headlessnesses of the revolutionary movement ultimately led to its downfall.  革命运动的无政府状态最终导致了它的失败。
近义词:leaderlessness, anarchy, disorder, chaos
反义词:leadership, order, organization
