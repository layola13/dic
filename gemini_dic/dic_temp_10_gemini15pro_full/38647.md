concurrences:/kənˈkʌrənsɪz/
n.
并发；同时发生；一致同意；共存
The simultaneous occurrence of events or circumstances. Agreement in opinion; concurrence of opinion.
The project suffered from a concurrence of unfortunate events that led to its delay.  这个项目由于一系列不幸事件的同时发生而导致延期。
We need the concurrence of all members to proceed with the plan. 我们需要所有成员的一致同意才能继续执行该计划。
synonyms: coincidence, conjunction, agreement, accord, consent, approval
antonyms: disagreement, conflict, dissent, opposition
noun
