dignity:/ˈdɪɡnɪti/ | n. |尊严，高贵，庄重|The state or quality of being worthy of honor or respect.  A composed or serious manner or style.|She maintained her dignity throughout the trial.  她在整个审判过程中都保持着自己的尊严。|Synonyms:  honor, respect, nobility, grace, decorum|Antonyms:  humiliation, degradation, shame|noun

dignified:/ˈdɪɡnɪfaɪd/ | adj. |有尊严的，高贵的，庄重的|Having or showing a composed or serious manner that deserves respect.|He has a very dignified manner. 他举止非常庄重。|Synonyms: stately, noble, majestic, imposing|Antonyms: undignified, shameful, lowly|adjective
