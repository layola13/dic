amortise: /ˈæmərtaɪz/ (US) /əˈmɔːtaɪz/ (UK)| v. |摊销，分期偿还|To gradually reduce or write off the cost of an asset over a period of time.|The company amortised the cost of the new equipment over five years.  公司将新设备的成本在五年内摊销。|Synonyms: depreciate, write off|Antonyms: appreciate, increase|verb

amortise: /ˈæmərtaɪz/ (US) /əˈmɔːtaɪz/ (UK)| n. |摊销，分期偿还（名词形式较少使用）|The process of gradually reducing or writing off the cost of an asset over a period of time.|The amortise of the loan will take 10 years.  贷款的摊销将需要10年。|Synonyms: depreciation, write-off|Antonyms: appreciation, increase|noun
