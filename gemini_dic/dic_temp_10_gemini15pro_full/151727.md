partywalled:/ˈpɑːrtiwɔːld/ | adj. | 共墙的，共用墙壁的 | Having a common wall with an adjoining building or buildings; separated from an adjoining building or buildings by a common wall. |  Our house is partywalled with our neighbors' on both sides. 我们的房子两边都和邻居的房子共用墙壁。| synonyms: attached, adjoining, connected | antonyms: detached, separate, freestanding | adjective

partywalled:/ˈpɑːrtiwɔːld/ | v. | 用墙隔开，用共用墙连接 | To build a wall between (properties); to separate or connect by a party wall. | The developer partywalled the new townhouses. 开发商用墙隔开了新的联排别墅。| synonyms: divide, separate, connect | antonyms: detach, separate, disconnect | verb


ETYMOLOGY->

From party + wall.  "Party" in this context refers to a separating element or division.  The term "party wall" dates back to the 16th century.
