charumati: /tʃɑːruːˈmɑːti/ | n. |  妙音天女，辩才天女 | A Buddhist deity associated with eloquence, music, learning, and the arts, often depicted playing a lute-like instrument called the biwa.  | Charumati is revered for her wisdom and artistic skills. 妙音天女因其智慧和艺术技能而受到崇敬。 |  In some traditions, Saraswati is identified with Charumati.  在一些传统中，萨拉斯瓦蒂被认为是妙音天女。 | Synonyms: Saraswati, Benzaiten | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
