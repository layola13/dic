octocoral:/ˈɒktəʊkɒrəl/ /ˈɑːktoʊkɔːrəl/| n. |八放珊瑚|A coral of the subclass Octocorallia, characterized by eight tentacles around the mouth of each polyp.  These corals include soft corals, sea fans, and sea pens.|Example 1: The divers marveled at the vibrant colors of the octocoral garden. 潜水员惊叹于八放珊瑚花园的鲜艳色彩。

Example 2: The research team studied the octocoral's symbiotic relationship with algae. 研究小组研究了八放珊瑚与藻类的共生关系。|近义词：alcyonarian, soft coral|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially stony coral as a contrasting group)|词性:noun
