blastodermic:/ˌblæstəˈdɜːrmɪk/
adjective
胚盘的
Relating to or derived from the blastoderm.  The blastoderm is a layer of cells formed early in embryonic development, forming the embryonic disk.
The blastodermic vesicle is a crucial stage in development.  胚盘囊泡是发育的关键阶段。
The researchers studied the blastodermic cells to understand early differentiation. 研究人员研究了胚盘细胞以了解早期分化。
近义词: embryonic, germinal
反义词: None
