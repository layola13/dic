matron:/ˈmeɪtrən/| n. |女舍监，护士长，已婚妇女|A woman who is in charge of domestic and medical arrangements in a boarding school, hospital, prison, etc. or A married woman, usually one who is mature and dignified.|例句：The matron of the hospital was a kind and caring woman. 医院的护士长是一位善良且关心他人的女性。

例句：She was a matron of considerable dignity and experience. 她是一位相当有尊严和经验的已婚妇女。|近义词：supervisor, superintendent, wife, lady|反义词: bachelor, spinster|词性:noun

matrons:/ˈmeɪtrənz/| n. |女舍监们，护士长们，已婚妇女们|Plural form of matron.  Refers to multiple women who are in charge of domestic and medical arrangements in institutions, or multiple married women, usually mature and dignified.|例句：The matrons of the various dormitories met to discuss student issues. 各宿舍的女舍监们开会讨论学生问题。

例句：The matrons of the town organized a charity event. 镇上的已婚妇女们组织了一场慈善活动。|近义词：supervisors, superintendents, wives, ladies|反义词: bachelors, spinsters|词性:noun
