majorly:/ˈmeɪdʒərli/ | adv. | 主要地，大部分地 |  In a major or significant way; predominantly. | The success of the project was majorly due to her hard work.  这个项目的成功主要归功于她的辛勤工作。 | 近义词: mainly, chiefly, primarily, largely, mostly | 反义词: minimally, slightly, insignificantly | 词性: 副词

词源:  From "major" (greater, larger) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Latin "maior" (greater).
