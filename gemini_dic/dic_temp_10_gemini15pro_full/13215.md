at-work:/æt ˈwɜːrk/
adjective
在工作的，上班的
engaged in one's regular employment; on the job.
例句：He's at work right now. 他现在正在工作。
例句：She spends most of her time at work. 她大部分时间都在工作。
近义词：working, on duty, on the job
反义词：off work, off duty, unemployed
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Old English æt + weorc.  "æt" meaning "at, by, near" and "weorc" meaning "work." The two words combined to form the phrase "at work" denoting the state of being engaged in work.
