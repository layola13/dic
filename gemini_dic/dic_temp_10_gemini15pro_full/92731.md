ichnoscopy:/ɪkˈnɒskəpi/ | n. | 足迹学，遗迹学 | The study of fossilized footprints and other traces of ancient organisms, such as trails, burrows, and borings. |  Ichnoscopy can provide valuable insights into the behavior and ecology of extinct animals.  足迹学可以为了解已灭绝动物的行为和生态提供宝贵的见解。 |  Near synonyms:  paleoichnology, trace fossil analysis | Antonyms: None | noun
