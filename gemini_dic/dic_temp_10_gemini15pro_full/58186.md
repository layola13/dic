drowsiness:/ˈdraʊzinəs/ | n. | 困倦，睡意 | A feeling of tiredness and sleepiness. |  I felt a wave of drowsiness after lunch.  午饭后我感到一阵困倦。| synonyms: sleepiness, somnolence, lethargy | antonyms: alertness, wakefulness | noun


drowsy:/ˈdraʊzi/ | adj. | 困倦的，昏昏欲睡的 | Sleepy or half asleep. | The warm room made me feel drowsy. 温暖的房间让我感到昏昏欲睡。| synonyms: sleepy, lethargic, somnolent | antonyms: alert, awake, wakeful | adjective
