exclusioner:/ɪkˈskluːʒənər/| n.|排除者; 排斥者; 驱逐者|A person or thing that excludes or has the power to exclude.  Something that excludes something else. |The bouncer acted as the exclusioner, barring entry to anyone without a ticket.  保安充当了驱逐者的角色，禁止任何没有票的人进入。

The new law acted as an exclusioner, preventing small businesses from competing. 这项新法律成了一道屏障，阻止了小型企业参与竞争。|近义词：expeller, ejector, rejecter, ostracizer|反义词：includer, welcomer|词性:noun
