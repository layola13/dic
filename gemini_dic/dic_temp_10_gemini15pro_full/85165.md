hardwoods:/ˈhɑːrdwʊdz/| n.|硬木|The wood from broad-leaved, mostly deciduous trees. Characterized by their density and durability.|例句：The furniture is made of beautiful hardwoods. 这件家具是用漂亮的硬木制成的。

例句：Hardwoods are often preferred for flooring due to their durability. 硬木因其耐用性而常被用于地板。|近义词：timber, lumber|反义词:softwoods|词性:名词
