atopic:/əˈtɒpɪk/ | adj.  n.|特应性的；过敏体质的；特应性个体|Relating to or caused by atopy, a genetic tendency to develop allergic diseases such as eczema, asthma, and hay fever.  Also, a person with this tendency.|例句：Atopic dermatitis is a common skin condition. 特应性皮炎是一种常见的皮肤病。

例句：He is an atopic individual, prone to allergies. 他是过敏体质，容易过敏。|近义词：allergic, hypersensitivity|反义词: non-atopic, non-allergic|词性: adjective, noun

Etymology:  From French *atopique*, from Greek *a-* (not, without) + *topos* (place).  The term refers to the unusual or misplaced reaction of the immune system.
