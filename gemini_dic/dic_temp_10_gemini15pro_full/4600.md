alcove: /ˈælkoʊv/ (US) /ˈælkəʊv/ (UK)| n.| 壁龛；凹室|A small recessed area in a room, often used for shelves or seating.|The alcove held a small statue of Buddha.  壁龛里放着一尊小佛像。|例句：
The alcove provided a cozy reading nook. 这个凹室提供了一个舒适的阅读角落。
They placed a small table and chairs in the alcove. 他们在凹室里放了一张小桌子和几把椅子。|近义词：recess, niche, nook|反义词:|noun
