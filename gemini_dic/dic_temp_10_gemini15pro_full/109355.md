lenni:/ˈlɛni/| adj.| being, existing (Finnish)|Lenni is a Finnish word meaning "being" or "existing". It's typically used as an adjective or a part of a compound word.|Olen lenni olen. (I am being.)|近义词:olemassa (existing) |反义词:olematon (non-existent)|adjective

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Onko hän lenni? = Is he/she being?
Se on lenni asia. = It is an existing thing.

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Hän on oleva lenni. = He/she is being. (This tense is less common with "lenni")
Asiat ovat tulevia lenni. = Things are coming into being.

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Hän on ollut lenni. = He/she has been.
Asia on ollut lenni jo pitkään. = The thing has existed for a long time.

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
Hän on ollut oleva lenni koko päivän. = He/she has been being all day. (Awkward, less common)
Asia on ollut oleva lenni jo pitkään. = The thing has been existing for a long time.  (Awkward, less common)


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
Hän oli lenni. = He/she was being.
Asia oli lenni. = The thing existed.


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
Hän oli oleva lenni. = He/she was being.
Asia oli oleva lenni. = The thing was existing. (less common)



过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
Hän oli ollut lenni. = He/she had been.
Asia oli ollut lenni jo kauan. = The thing had existed for a long time.


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
Hän oli ollut oleva lenni pitkään. = He/she had been being for a long time. (Awkward, less common)
Asia oli ollut oleva lenni jo kauan. = The thing had been existing for a long time. (Awkward, less common)



一般将来时 (Simple Future):
Hän tulee olemaan lenni. = He/she will be.
Asia tulee olemaan lenni. = The thing will exist.



将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
Hän tulee olemaan oleva lenni. = He/she will be being. (Awkward, less common)
Asia tulee olemaan oleva lenni. = The thing will be existing. (Awkward, less common)


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
Hän tulee olemaan ollut lenni. = He/she will have been.
Asia tulee olemaan ollut lenni. = The thing will have existed.



将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
Hän tulee olemaan ollut oleva lenni. = He/she will have been being. (Very awkward, avoid)
Asia tulee olemaan ollut oleva lenni. = The thing will have been existing. (Very awkward, avoid)



虚拟语气:
Jos hän olisi lenni, hän tekisi sen. = If he/she were being, he/she would do it.
Toivon, että olisin lenni siellä. = I wish I were being there.


被动语态:
(Not applicable to "lenni" as it's not an action verb.)



疑问句:
Onko hän lenni? = Is he/she being?
Mikä on lenni? = What is being?



否定句:
Hän ei ole lenni. = He/she is not being.
Asia ei ole lenni. = The thing does not exist.




PHRASE->

(Lenni doesn't readily form common phrases in the way English words do. It's more of a grammatical element. These examples show it in context.)

Lenni ihminen = To be human.
Lenni elossa = To be alive.
Lenni läsnä = To be present.
Lenni poissa = To be absent.
Lenni hereillä = To be awake.



注意事项:

Finnish has only one standard pronunciation.  The /ɛ/ sound in "lenni" is similar to the "e" in "bed".


The continuous forms with "lenni" often sound awkward and unnatural in Finnish.  It's best to stick to simpler constructions where possible.  Finnish expresses ongoing states in other ways, not always with a direct equivalent of the English continuous aspect.
