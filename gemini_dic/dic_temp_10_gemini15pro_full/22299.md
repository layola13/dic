bonhomie:/ˌbɒnəˈmiː/ |n.|友好；和蔼；温厚 |Good-natured, easy friendliness. |The annual picnic was always an occasion of great bonhomie.  年度野餐总是一个充满友好的场合。|近义词：affability, amiability, cordiality, geniality |反义词：hostility, animosity, coldness |词性:名词

Etymology:
From French bonhomie, from bonhomme "good fellow," from bon "good" + homme "man."
