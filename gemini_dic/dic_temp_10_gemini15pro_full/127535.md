necromorphs:/ˈnɛkrəmɔːrfz/
| n. |尸变体|Fictional reanimated corpses that appear in the Dead Space series of video games. They are created by an alien artifact known as the Marker, which transmits a signal that reanimates dead tissue and transforms it into grotesque, monstrous forms.|The necromorphs overwhelmed the ship's crew.|Synonyms: mutants, monsters, abominations|Antonyms: humans, living beings|Noun
