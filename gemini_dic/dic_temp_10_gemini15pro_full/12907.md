assuredly:/əˈʃʊrədli/ | adv. | 肯定地，无疑地 | Certainly; without a doubt.  |  He will assuredly win the election. 他肯定会赢得选举。 | She assuredly knew the answer. 她肯定知道答案。 | 近义词: certainly, definitely, undoubtedly, indeed, surely | 反义词: doubtfully, uncertainly, possibly, perhaps | 词性: 副词

词源:
From Middle English *assuredli, equivalent to assured +‎ -ly.
