gimmickry: /ˈɡɪmɪkri/ | n. |  噱头，花招，欺骗 |  The use of gimmicks, especially attention-getting devices, to sell or promote something.  Tricks or devices used to attract attention, publicity, or business. | The magician's performance was full of gimmickry.  魔术师的表演充满了噱头。|  The advertising campaign relied heavily on gimmickry. 这场广告宣传活动严重依赖于各种噱头。| synonyms: trickery, chicanery, subterfuge, artifice, deception | antonyms: genuineness, authenticity, sincerity | noun
