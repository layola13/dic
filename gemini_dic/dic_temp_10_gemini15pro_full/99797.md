intermessage's:/ˌɪntərˈmɛsɪdʒɪz/ | adj. |  消息间的，信息间的 |  Relating to or occurring between messages.  It describes something that happens or exists in the space or time between messages.  This word is derived from "inter-" (between) and "message". The apostrophe-s indicates possession, so it literally translates to "belonging to the intermessage." | The intermessage's delay was causing frustration. 消息间的延迟令人沮丧。| Near synonyms:  intercommunication, between-messages (less common) | Antonyms:  intramessage | word type: adjective
