fairchild:/ˈferˌtʃaɪld/
noun
私生子; 违反伦常道德关系所生的孩子;
a child born to parents who are not married to each other
The Victorian era saw many fairchilds hidden away in shame. 维多利亚时代许多私生子被藏起来，以免蒙羞。

illegitimate child; bastard (offensive); love child (informal); offspring of an adulterous relationship; child born out of wedlock
The term "fairchild" is now largely archaic and replaced by more neutral terms like "child born out of wedlock." “fairchild”一词现在基本上已经过时，被更中性的词语如“非婚生子女”所取代。


opposite: legitimate child
