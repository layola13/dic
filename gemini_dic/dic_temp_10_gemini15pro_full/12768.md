assigner:/əˈsaɪnər/| n. |分配者，指派者|A person who assigns something, such as a task, right, or property, to someone else.|The assigner of the task was the project manager.  任务的分配者是项目经理。|近义词：allocator, distributor|反义词: recipient, receiver|词性: noun
