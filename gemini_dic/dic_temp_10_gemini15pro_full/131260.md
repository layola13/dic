noncasual:/ˌnɒnˈkæʒuəl/ | /ˌnɑːnˈkæʒuəl/ | adj. | 非正式的，非便装的 | Not casual; formal or designed for a special occasion. |  She wore a noncasual dress to the wedding. 她穿着一件非便装的连衣裙去参加婚礼。|  He opted for noncasual attire for the business meeting.  他选择非便装出席商务会议。 | 近义词：formal, dressy, smart | 反义词：casual, informal | 词性：形容词

词源：
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not" or "opposite of."  From Latin *non, from Old Latin *noenum, from ne "not" + oinom "one."
casual (adjective): From Latin *casualis, from casus "a chance, accident, event," from cadere "to fall, happen."
