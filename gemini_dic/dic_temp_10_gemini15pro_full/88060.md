highjacked:/ˌhaɪˈdʒækt/ | v. | 劫持；不正当控制 | To seize or commandeer (a vehicle, aircraft, or ship) in transit by force or threat of force; to take control of something illicitly or by force. | The plane was highjacked by terrorists.  这架飞机被恐怖分子劫持了。

The company's project was highjacked by a rival firm.  这家公司的项目被竞争对手不正当地控制了。 | synonyms: seize, commandeer, hijack, appropriate, usurp | antonyms: release, surrender, relinquish | verb


highjacked:/ˌhaɪˈdʒækt/ | adj. | 被劫持的；被不正当控制的 |  Having been seized or commandeered illegally, especially while in transit; taken control of illicitly. | The highjacked plane landed in a different country.  被劫持的飞机降落在另一个国家。

The highjacked project ultimately failed. 被不正当控制的项目最终失败了。| synonyms: seized, commandeered, hijacked, appropriated, usurped | antonyms: released, surrendered, relinquished | adjective
