misunderstand:/ˌmɪsʌndərˈstænd/ | v. | 误解，误会 | To interpret something incorrectly or wrongly; to not understand something correctly. |  I think you misunderstood my instructions. 我认为你误解了我的指示。

She completely misunderstood his intentions. 她完全误解了他的意图。

| 近义词: misinterpret, misconceive, misapprehend | 反义词: understand, comprehend, grasp | 词性: 动词
