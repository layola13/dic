decurion:/dɪˈkjʊəriən/
n.
十夫长，队长
a commander of a decuria, a subdivision of a Roman legion, originally numbering ten men.
The decurion led his men into battle. 十夫长带领他的士兵投入战斗。

The decurion was responsible for the training and discipline of his men.  十夫长负责训练和纪律他的士兵。
近义词:leader, commander, captain
反义词:subordinate, follower
