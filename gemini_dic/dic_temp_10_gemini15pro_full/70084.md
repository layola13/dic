feuillants:/fœˈjɑ̃/ (French pronunciation - approximate English equivalent: fuh-YAHn)
|n.| Feuillants (members of a short-lived French political club) |Members of a French political club during the French Revolution known for their moderate stance.  They were named after the former monastery of Feuillants where they met. |The Feuillants advocated for a constitutional monarchy. | 近义词:  moderates, constitutionalists | 反义词:  Jacobins, radicals | noun
