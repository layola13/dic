have:/hæv/ (US), /həv/ (UK)|v. aux. |有; 持有; 经历; 必须; 吃; 允许|I have a car. 我有一辆车。|possess, own|lack|verb, auxiliary verb

have:/hæv/ (US), /həv/ (UK)|v. aux. |已经|I have finished my work. 我已经完成我的工作了。|complete, finish|start, begin|verb, auxiliary verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *haven*, from Old English *habban* (“to have, hold, possess”), from Proto-Germanic *habjaną* (“to have, hold”), from Proto-Indo-European *keh₂p-* (“to grasp, take, hold”).

Present Perfect Continuous:

Past Perfect:

PHRASE->
have to: 必须  I have to go to work. 我必须去上班。
have a look: 看一看  Have a look at this picture. 看一看这张照片。
have a good time: 玩得开心  I hope you have a good time at the party. 我希望你在派对上玩得开心。
have breakfast/lunch/dinner: 吃早饭/午饭/晚饭  I usually have breakfast at 7am. 我通常早上7点吃早餐。
have a word with: 和…说句话  Can I have a word with you in private? 我可以私下和你谈谈吗？
