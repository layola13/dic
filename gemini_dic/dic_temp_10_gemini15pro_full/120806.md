misassertion:/ˌmɪsəˈsɜːrʃn/ | n. | 歪曲；虚报；谎称 | An untrue or misleading assertion.  A statement that is not true. |  The politician's speech was filled with misassertions about his opponent's record.  这位政治家的演讲充满了对他对手记录的歪曲。| Synonyms: falsehood, lie, untruth, fabrication, misrepresentation, distortion | Antonyms: truth, fact, accuracy, veracity | noun
