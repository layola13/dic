nonthermoplastics:/ˌnɒnˌθɜːrməˈplæstɪks/ | n. | 非热塑性塑料 |  Nonthermoplastics are polymers that do not soften when heated and cannot be reshaped. They undergo irreversible chemical changes when heated, often charring or decomposing rather than melting. |  Epoxy resins are common examples of nonthermoplastics. 环氧树脂是常见的非热塑性塑料的例子。|  Thermosetting polymers, thermosets | Thermoplastics | noun
