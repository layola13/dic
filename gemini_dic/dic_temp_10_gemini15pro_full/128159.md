nettle:/ˈnetl/ (US), /ˈnetəl/ (UK)| n. v.|荨麻；激怒|A herbaceous plant which has jagged leaves covered with stinging hairs.  As a verb, it means to irritate or annoy someone.|
例句：The nettles stung my legs as I walked through the long grass.  走在高高的草丛中，荨麻刺痛了我的腿。

例句：His constant criticism nettled her. 他不断的批评激怒了她。


近义词：irritate, annoy, vex, provoke (v), stinging nettle (n)|
反义词：soothe, please, calm (v)|
词性：noun, verb|

nettles:/ˈnetlz/ (US), /ˈnetəlz/ (UK)| n.|荨麻（复数）|The plural form of nettle.|
例句：Be careful of the nettles in the garden. 小心花园里的荨麻。

例句：I got stung by nettles while weeding. 我除草时被荨麻刺痛了。

近义词：stinging nettles|
反义词：N/A|
词性：noun|
