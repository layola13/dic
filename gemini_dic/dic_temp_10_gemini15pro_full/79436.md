glitch's:/ɡlɪtʃɪz/| n.|小故障，小毛病，小差错|A glitch is a small problem or fault in a system or machine, especially in electronics or computers, that prevents it from working correctly for a short time.|The computer program had a glitch that caused it to crash. 电脑程序出现了一个小故障，导致它崩溃了。|Synonyms: bug, error, fault, malfunction, problem|Antonyms: perfection, flawlessness|noun
