assessor:/əˈsesər/| n.|评估员，估价员，评税员|A person who evaluates the quality or value of something or someone.|The assessor determined the value of the property for tax purposes. 评估员确定了该房产的纳税价值。|近义词：evaluator, appraiser, judge|反义词: N/A|noun

assessor:/əˈsesər/| n.|（陪审法官的）助理，技术顾问|An expert who advises a judge on technical matters.|The judge consulted with the assessor before making a ruling on the patent dispute.  法官在对专利纠纷作出裁决之前咨询了技术顾问。|近义词：advisor, consultant|反义词: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French assessour, from Late Latin assessor, from Latin assessus (“one who sits beside”), past participle of assidere (“to sit beside”), from ad- (“to, at”) + sedere (“to sit”).
