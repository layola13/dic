anguiform:/ˈæŋ.ɡwɪ.fɔːrm/| adj.|鳗形的，像鳗鱼的|Resembling an eel in shape; elongated and snakelike.  Having a long, slender body like an eel.|The anguiform fish slithered through the coral. 这种鳗形鱼滑过珊瑚。|Synonyms: eel-like, serpentine, sinuous|Antonyms:  short, stout, thick|adjective

anguiform:/ˈæŋ.ɡwɪ.fɔːrm/| n.|鳗形鱼|A fish that has a long, slender body like an eel.|Several anguiforms were observed in the deep sea study. 在深海研究中观察到了几种鳗形鱼。|Synonyms: eel, elver|Antonyms: None (Specific animal, not a shape)|noun
