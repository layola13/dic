naturalisms:/ˈnætʃrəlɪzəmz/
n.
自然主义（艺术、文学、哲学流派）
A style and theory of representation based on the accurate depiction of detail.  It is a belief that only natural laws and forces operate in the world.
The novel's stark naturalisms shocked some readers. 这部小说的赤裸裸的自然主义风格震惊了一些读者。

近义词:realism, materialism
反义词:idealism, spiritualism
词性:noun


naturalism:/ˈnætʃrəlɪzəm/
n.
自然主义（艺术、文学、哲学流派）
The principles and methods of natural science used in other fields, such as the arts, philosophy, and the humanities.
His approach to acting was rooted in naturalism. 他的表演方法植根于自然主义。

近义词:realism, positivism
反义词:supernaturalism, mysticism
词性:noun
