etiologists:/ˌiːtiˈɒlədʒɪsts/
n.
病原学家，病因学家
Scientists and etiologists are working hard to understand the origins of the disease. 科学家和病原学家正在努力研究这种疾病的起源。
Etiologists are studying the causes of the recent outbreak. 病原学家正在研究最近疫情爆发的原因。
近义词: researchers, scientists, specialists
反义词:  None (No direct antonym for a profession)
词性: Noun
