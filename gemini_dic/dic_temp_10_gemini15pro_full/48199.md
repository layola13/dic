decompressor:/ˌdiːkəmˈprɛsər/ | n. | 减压器；解压缩程序 | A device or program that reduces pressure or expands compressed data. |  The diver needed a decompressor to safely ascend from the depths. 潜水员需要一个减压器才能安全地从深处上升。

The file was too large to send, so I used a decompressor to shrink it.  这个文件太大无法发送，所以我用了一个解压缩程序来缩小它。 | 近义词: extractor, expander | 反义词: compressor | 词性: 名词

decompress:/ˌdiːkəmˈprɛs/ | v. |  减压；解压缩 | To reduce pressure or expand compressed data. | After a long day at work, I like to decompress with a hot bath. 下班后漫长的一天后，我喜欢洗个热水澡来放松一下。

I needed to decompress the file before I could open it. 我需要先解压缩文件才能打开它。| 近义词: extract, expand | 反义词: compress | 词性: 动词
