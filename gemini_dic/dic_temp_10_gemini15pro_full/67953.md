facies:/ˈfeɪʃiːz/ /ˈfeɪsiːs/
n.
面貌；相；外观；[地质]相；[生态]群落相
The general appearance or aspect of something.  In geology, the overall appearance of a rock unit, reflecting its origin and depositional environment. In ecology, the characteristic appearance of a plant community.
The limestone facies of the quarry are clearly visible.  这个采石场的石灰岩相清晰可见。
