gowany:/ˈɡoʊəni/ | adj. | 糟糕的，差劲的；令人不快的；腐烂的 |  Scottish slang, often considered vulgar, used to describe something of poor quality, unpleasant, or rotten.  Derived from "gowan," a type of daisy, but with a negative connotation. | That gowany film was a complete waste of money. 那部烂电影完全是浪费钱。|  awful, terrible, rubbish, rotten, lousy | excellent, wonderful, great | adjective
gowan:/ˈɡaʊən/ | n. | 雏菊 | A wildflower, specifically a daisy.  Primarily Scottish usage. | The field was covered in white gowans. 田野里开满了白色的雏菊。 | daisy, wildflower | cultivated flower | noun
