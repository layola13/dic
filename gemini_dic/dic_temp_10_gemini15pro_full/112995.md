lumbago:/lʌmˈbeɪɡoʊ/
n.
腰痛，腰背痛
a sudden pain in the muscles and joints of the lower back.
例句：He suffered from lumbago after lifting heavy boxes. 他搬了重箱子后腰疼。

例句：Gardening often gives me lumbago. 园艺活常让我腰疼。

近义词：backache, lower back pain
反义词：(No direct antonym for a medical condition)
词性：noun
