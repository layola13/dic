apexes:/ˈeɪpɪsiːz/
n.
顶点，最高点
the highest point of something
The apex of the pyramid is sharp. 金字塔的顶点很尖。

apexes:/ˈeɪpɪsiːz/
n.
(复数)顶点，极点
the highest points of something
The apexes of the mountains were covered in snow. 山峰的顶点被雪覆盖。

Etymology:
From Latin apex (nominative apex, genitive apicis) "peak, summit, tip, top, extremity, point; head, chief person".

Synonyms:
peak, summit, pinnacle, climax, acme, zenith, culmination, vertex, tip, top

Antonyms:
base, bottom, nadir, trough
