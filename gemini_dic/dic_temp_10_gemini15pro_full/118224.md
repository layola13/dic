mercantilistic: /ˌmɜːrkənˈtɪlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 重商主义的 | Relating to the economic theory and system of mercantilism, which advocates maximizing a nation's wealth through trade, with a focus on exports and accumulation of precious metals. |  The government adopted mercantilistic policies to boost the nation's economy. 政府采取了重商主义政策来促进国家经济。 | The East India Company was a powerful force in the mercantilistic era. 东印度公司在重商主义时代是一股强大的力量。 |  近义词: protectionist, nationalist (in economics) | 反义词: free-trade, laissez-faire | 词性: adjective | 词源: From French *mercantiliste*, from *mercantile* 'mercantile', from Italian *mercantile*, from *mercante* 'merchant'.
