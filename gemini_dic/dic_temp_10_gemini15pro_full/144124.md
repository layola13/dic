outsourcing:/ˈaʊtsɔːrsɪŋ/| n.|外包，业务外包|The act of contracting with another company to perform some of the company's functions, such as manufacturing or accounting.|Many companies are outsourcing their IT operations to save costs. 许多公司为了节省成本，将IT业务外包。|Example 1: The company decided on outsourcing its customer service operations to India.  这家公司决定将其客户服务业务外包给印度。

Example 2: Outsourcing can be a cost-effective strategy for businesses. 外包对企业来说可能是一种具有成本效益的策略。|Synonyms: subcontracting, offshoring, contracting out|Antonyms: insourcing, in-house production|noun
