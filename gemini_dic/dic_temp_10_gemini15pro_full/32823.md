chokers:/ˈtʃoʊkərz/
n.|窒息物；噎住的人；(尤指女性佩戴的)贴颈项链，短项链|A choker is a close-fitting necklace.  It can also refer to something that chokes or causes choking, or a person who is choking.|She wore a velvet choker. 她戴着一条天鹅绒短项链。|Near synonyms: necklace, necklet, collar. |Antonyms: loose necklace, long necklace
词性: 名词


chokers:/ˈtʃoʊkərz/
n.|压制者；令人窒息的东西|Something that restricts or suppresses.|The pollution levels were a choker on the city's development. 污染水平抑制了城市的发展。|Near synonyms: obstacle, impediment, restriction. |Antonyms: aid, help, assistance
词性: 名词
