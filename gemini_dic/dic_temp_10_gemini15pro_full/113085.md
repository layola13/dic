lunated: /ˈluːneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 新月形的，月牙形的 | Shaped like a crescent moon; having a crescent shape. |  The lunated edges of the design gave it a mystical feel.  设计的新月形边缘赋予它一种神秘感。 | The lunated moon hung low in the sky. 新月低垂在天空中。 | 近义词: crescent, crescentic | 反义词: gibbous (凸月形的) | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Latin *lunatus*, from luna (“moon”).
