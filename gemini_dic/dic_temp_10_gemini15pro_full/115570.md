maral:/ˈmɑːrəl/| n.|马鹿（主要产于中亚地区的一种大型鹿）|A large deer (Cervus elaphus maral) of central Asia, having large branched antlers.|The maral is prized for its antlers, which are used in traditional medicine. 马鹿因其鹿角而备受珍视，鹿角被用于传统医学。|Synonyms: red deer, elk (in North America)|Antonyms: N/A|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Russian марал (maral), from Turkic *maral (“deer”). Ultimately from Proto-Turkic *maral.
