non-polar:/ˌnɒnˈpəʊlə(r)/ /ˌnɑːnˈpoʊlər/ | adj. | 非极性的 |  Lacking polarity; lacking a dipole moment.  A nonpolar molecule has no separation of charge, so no positive or negative poles are formed. | Oil and water don't mix because water is polar and oil is nonpolar.  油和水不能混合，因为水是极性的，而油是非极性的。

Oil is a nonpolar substance and doesn't dissolve in water. 油是一种非极性物质，不溶于水。| 近义词:hydrophobic | 反义词: polar | 词性:形容词
