over-enthusiastically: /ˌoʊvərɪnˌθuːziˈæstɪkli/ /ˌəʊvərɪnˌθjuːziˈæstɪkli/
| adv. | 过于热情地；过分热情地 | With excessive enthusiasm; in a way that shows too much excitement or eagerness. | She clapped over-enthusiastically, nearly knocking her drink over. 她过于热情地鼓掌，差点把饮料打翻。| He greeted me over-enthusiastically, which made me a little uncomfortable. 他过于热情地跟我打招呼，让我有点不舒服。 | 近义词: excessively, immoderately, too eagerly | 反义词: apathetically, indifferently, half-heartedly | 词性: 副词

词源:  From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "enthusiastic" (adjective from Greek "enthousiasmos," meaning "divine inspiration" or "possession by a god") + "-ally" (suffix forming adverbs).
