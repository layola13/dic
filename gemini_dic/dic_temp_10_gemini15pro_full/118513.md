mesocaecum:/ˌmɛzoʊˈsiːkəm/| n. |中盲肠|The mesocaecum is a fold of peritoneum that attaches the cecum to the posterior abdominal wall.  It's part of the mesocolon, specifically related to the ascending colon's initial portion.|The surgeon carefully examined the mesocaecum during the appendectomy.  外科医生在阑尾切除术中仔细检查了中盲肠。|The mesocaecum was inflamed due to the infection. 由于感染，中盲肠发炎了。|Synonyms: mesoappendix (sometimes used interchangeably, but technically refers to the mesentery of the appendix)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
Etymology->
meso- (Greek μέσος mesos "middle") + caecum (Latin caecum "blind gut")
