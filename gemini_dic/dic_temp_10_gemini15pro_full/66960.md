experimental:/ɪkˌsperɪˈmentl/ | adj. | 实验的；试验性的；经验的 | based on or involving experiment; used for or connected with scientific experiments |  例句：The drug is still at the experimental stage.  这种药物仍处于试验阶段。| 近义词：trial, test, pilot | 反义词：proven, established, theoretical | 词性: adjective

experimental:/ɪkˌsperɪˈmentl/ | n. | 实验性的东西；试验性的东西 | something experimental | 例句：The new teaching method is still an experimental.  这种新的教学方法仍然是试验性的。| 近义词：trial, test | 反义词：standard, established | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin experimentalis, from Latin experimentum "a trial, proof, experiment," from experiri "to try, test"
