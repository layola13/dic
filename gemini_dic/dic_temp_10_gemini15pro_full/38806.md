conduit:/ˈkɒndjuɪt/ (UK), /ˈkɑːnduɪt/ (US)| n.|管道，导管；渠道，途径|A conduit is a pipe or channel for conveying liquids, gases, or wires.  It can also refer to a means by which something is transmitted.|The electrical wires were run through a metal conduit. 电线穿过金属管道。|Synonyms: channel, pipe, duct, passage, medium|Antonyms: barrier, obstacle|noun

conduit:/ˈkɒndjuɪt/ (UK), /ˈkɑːnduɪt/ (US)| n.|渠道，途径|A conduit can also refer to a means by which something is transmitted, often used figuratively.|The newspaper served as a conduit for political propaganda. 这家报纸成了政治宣传的渠道。|Synonyms: channel, medium, vehicle|Antonyms: barrier, obstacle|noun
