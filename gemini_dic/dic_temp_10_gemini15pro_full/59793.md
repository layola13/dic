ecotype:/ˈiːkəʊtaɪp/ (US) /ˈiːkətaɪp/ (UK)| n. |生态型|A distinct form or race of a plant or animal species occupying a particular habitat and adapted to local environmental conditions.|The harsh conditions of the desert have led to the evolution of a unique cactus ecotype.  沙漠的恶劣条件导致了一种独特的仙人掌生态型的进化。|近义词：subspecies, variety, race|反义词: N/A|noun
