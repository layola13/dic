deflagrability:/dɪˌflæɡrəˈbɪləti/| n. |可燃性，易燃性|The quality of being easily set on fire and burning rapidly.|The deflagrability of dry leaves makes them a fire hazard. 干树叶易燃，容易引发火灾。|Synonyms: flammability, combustibility|Antonyms: incombustibility, nonflammability|noun
