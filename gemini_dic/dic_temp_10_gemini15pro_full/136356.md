nonresolutions:/ˌnɑːnˌrɛzəˈluːʃənz/
noun
未完成的决心；未实现的目标；未履行的承诺
Things that someone intended to do or achieve but did not.
This year, my list of nonresolutions is longer than my list of resolutions. 今年，我的未完成目标清单比我的新年决心清单还要长。
Many people find their nonresolutions piling up year after year. 许多人发现他们的未完成目标年复一年地堆积起来。
synonyms: unfulfilled goals, abandoned intentions, broken promises
antonyms: achievements, accomplishments, successes
