fied:/faɪd/ | adj. |  恐惧的，害怕的 |  Suffix indicating fear or aversion. Often used in combination with other words to create adjectives describing specific phobias. Primarily found in older or more technical texts.  | He has always been mystified by snakes. 他一直对蛇感到恐惧。 |  afraid, scared, terrified | unafraid, bold, fearless | suffix
