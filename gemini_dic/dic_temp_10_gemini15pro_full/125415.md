multipath:/ˈmʌltɪˌpæθ/ | adj. n. |多路径的；多路径 | Relating to or denoting a communications system that uses multiple paths between transmitter and receiver. | The multipath propagation can cause signal fading. 多径传播会导致信号衰落。| The multipath channel is a challenging environment for wireless communication. 多径信道对无线通信来说是一个充满挑战的环境。| synonyms: multichannel, multiple-path | antonyms: single-path | adjective, noun

multipath:/ˈmʌltɪˌpæθ/ | n. |多路径 | A propagation phenomenon that results in radio signals reaching the receiving antenna by two or more paths. | Multipath is a common problem in urban areas. 多路径是城市地区的常见问题。| Multipath can cause interference and distortion in radio signals. 多路径会导致无线电信号的干扰和失真。| synonyms: multipath propagation, multipath interference | antonyms: line-of-sight propagation | noun
