aerostatical:/ˌɛərəˈstætɪkəl/
adjective
空气静力的；航空学的
Relating to aerostatics, the branch of physics that deals with the equilibrium of gases and of bodies immersed in them.
Aerostatical calculations are crucial for the design of hot air balloons.  空气静力学计算对于热气球的设计至关重要。
The aerostatical properties of the gas determined its buoyancy.  这种气体的空气静力特性决定了它的浮力。
