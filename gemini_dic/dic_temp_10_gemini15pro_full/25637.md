bunchy:/ˈbʌn(t)ʃi/
adjective
成束的；成群的；臃肿的
Growing in or forming bunches; tending to form bunches or clusters.  (of clothing)  Appearing bulky or puffy.
The flowers were bunchy and bright yellow. 这些花成束生长，呈鲜艳的黄色。
She wore a bunchy, ill-fitting coat. 她穿着一件臃肿不合身的衣服。
synonyms: bulky, puffy, clustered, bunched
antonyms: smooth, streamlined, flat, slender
词性: 形容词
