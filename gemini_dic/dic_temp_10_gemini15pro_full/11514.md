ariette:/ˌærɪˈɛt/ | n. |小咏叹调，抒情短歌|A short aria, typically one that is light in character and less elaborate than a formal aria. It's often found in operas, oratorios, and cantatas, and sometimes as a standalone piece.|She sang a beautiful ariette from a forgotten opera. 她演唱了一首来自一部被遗忘的歌剧的优美小咏叹调。|近义词：aria, song, melody|反义词:|noun
