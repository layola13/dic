euphemisms:/ˈjufəmɪzəmz/ | n. |委婉语，委婉说法 |  A mild or indirect word or expression substituted for one considered to be too harsh or blunt when referring to something unpleasant or embarrassing. |  "Senior citizen" is a euphemism for "old person."  “老年人”是“老人”的委婉说法。| synonyms: circumlocutions, indirect expressions, polite terms, substitutes | antonyms: dysphemisms, blunt language, direct terms | noun
