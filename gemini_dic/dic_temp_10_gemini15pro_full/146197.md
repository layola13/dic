overexpands:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspændz/ /ˌəʊvərɪkˈspændz/ | v. | 过度扩张，过度膨胀 | to expand beyond safe or reasonable limits | The company overexpanded during the boom years and subsequently went bankrupt.  这家公司在繁荣时期过度扩张，随后破产了。| synonyms: overextend, overreach | antonyms: contract, shrink | verb

overexpands:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspændz/ /ˌəʊvərɪkˈspændz/ | n. | 过度扩张，过度膨胀（名词形式较少使用） | the act of overexpanding | The overexpands of the market led to a crash. 市场过度扩张导致了崩盘。 | synonyms: overextension, overgrowth | antonyms: contraction, shrinkage | noun
