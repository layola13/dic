lutist:/ˈluːtɪst/ | n. | 琵琶演奏家；鲁特琴演奏家 | A musician who plays the lute. | The lutist played a beautiful melody. 琵琶演奏家演奏了一首优美的旋律。|  Synonym: lute player | Antonym: None | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *lutiste*, from Old French *leutist*, from *leut* (lute) + *-ist*.  Ultimately from Arabic *al-ʿūd* (العود, "the wood," referring to the instrument's wooden soundboard).
