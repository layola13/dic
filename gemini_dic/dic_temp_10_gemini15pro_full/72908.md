fomenter:/ˈfoʊˌmɛntər/ (US), /ˈfəʊˈmɛntə/ (UK)| n.|煽动者；教唆者| someone who stirs up trouble or encourages bad feelings | The fomenter of the rebellion was eventually caught and imprisoned.  叛乱的煽动者最终被捕入狱。| 近义词：agitator, instigator, provocateur | 反义词: peacemaker, mediator, conciliator | noun

foment:/foʊˈmɛnt/ (US), /fəˈmɛnt/ (UK)| vt.|煽动；助长；激起| to stir up or incite (trouble or rebellion); to promote the growth or development of something undesirable |  The politician's speeches fomented unrest among the people.  这位政治家的演讲煽动了民众的不安。| 近义词：incite, instigate, provoke | 反义词: quell, suppress, allay | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *fomenter*, from Latin *fomentum* "poultice, lotion; incentive, encouragement," from *fovēre* "to warm, cherish, promote."
