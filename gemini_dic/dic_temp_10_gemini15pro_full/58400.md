dubious:/ˈdjuːbiəs/ (US), /ˈdʒuːbiəs/ (UK)| adj.|可疑的，不确定的，犹豫不决的|Hesitant or doubtful; not to be relied upon; suspect.|The salesman's claims about the product seemed dubious.  推销员关于该产品的说法似乎很可疑。|例句：The evidence against him was dubious at best.  针对他的证据充其量也是可疑的。|近义词：doubtful, questionable, suspect, uncertain|反义词:certain, sure, definite|词性:adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dubius*, from *duo* "two" + *-bius* (akin to habēre "to hold, have").  Literally, "wavering between two."  The sense of "morally suspect" developed later.
