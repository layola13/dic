eurocentricity:/ˌjʊərəʊˈsɛntrɪsɪti/ | n. |欧洲中心主义|The belief that European culture and values are superior to those of other cultures, and that European experiences and perspectives are the most important or valid ones.  A worldview that centers Europe and considers it the norm or standard against which all other cultures are measured.|例句：The history book displayed a strong eurocentricity, focusing primarily on European contributions to world civilization. 这本历史书展现了强烈的欧洲中心主义，主要关注欧洲对世界文明的贡献。

例句：Her research challenges the eurocentricity of traditional art history. 她的研究挑战了传统艺术史的欧洲中心主义。|近义词：Western-centrism, Occidentalism|反义词:Multiculturalism, Afrocentrism|词性:noun


Etymology->
From Euro- (combining form meaning "Europe") + -centric (meaning "centered") + -ity (noun suffix).  "Euro-" ultimately derives from Greek "Europa," the name of a Phoenician princess in Greek mythology.
