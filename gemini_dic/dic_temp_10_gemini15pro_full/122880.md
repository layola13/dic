molluscan:/məˈlʌskən/ | adj. n. |  软体动物的；软体动物 | Relating to or belonging to the Mollusca phylum, a large group of invertebrate animals including snails, slugs, mussels, and octopuses.  |  Molluscan shells are often collected for their beauty. 软体动物的壳经常因为它们的美丽而被收集。 | 近义词:  invertebrate, shellfish (for some molluscs) | 反义词: vertebrate | 词性: adjective, noun

molluscan:/məˈlʌskən/ | n. | 软体动物 | An animal belonging to the Mollusca phylum. |  The octopus is a highly intelligent molluscan. 章鱼是一种非常聪明的软体动物。 | 近义词:  invertebrate, shellfish (for some molluscs) | 反义词: vertebrate | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *molluscus* (“soft”), from *mollis* (“soft”).
