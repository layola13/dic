babes:/beɪbz/| n.|宝贝们，婴儿们|A term of endearment, often used to address women or children affectionately, or informally to refer to attractive young women.  Can also refer to literal babies.|例句：Look at those adorable babes playing in the park. 看看那些在公园里玩耍的可爱宝贝们。

例句：He called all the women in the office "babes," which some found offensive. 他称呼办公室里所有的女人为“宝贝”，有些人觉得这很冒犯。|近义词：babies, darlings, sweethearts, honeys (informal), chicks (slang, can be offensive)|反义词:  (No direct antonym, depends on context.  Could be 'old people' if referring to age, or 'unattractive people' if referring to appearance, but these are not true antonyms.)|词性:Noun
