gemmeous:/ˈdʒɛmiəs/
adjective
宝石般的；似宝石的
Resembling a gem; having the nature or brilliance of a gem.
The beetle’s gemmeous carapace shimmered in the sunlight.  甲虫宝石般的甲壳在阳光下闪闪发光。
The gemmeous hues of the stained glass window captivated the visitors. 彩色玻璃窗宝石般的色调吸引了游客。
Synonyms: jeweled, gemlike, brilliant, sparkling, glittering
Antonyms: dull, lackluster, opaque
Etymology: From Latin *gemmeus, from gemma "bud, gem, precious stone."
