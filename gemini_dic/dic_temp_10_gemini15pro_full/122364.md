mizzen:/ˈmɪzən/| n. adj.|后桅；后帆；小帆船|The mizzen mast is the third mast from forward on a sailing vessel. 后桅是帆船上从船头算起的第三根桅杆。|例句：The mizzen sail was torn in the storm. 后帆在暴风雨中被撕裂了。|近义词：jigger, spanker|反义词:foremast, mainsail|词性:名词，形容词

词源: From Old French *misen, from Italian mezzana "middle, mizzen," from Late Latin medianus "middle," from Latin medius.  最终源自拉丁语 medius，意为“中间的”。
