cystolith: /ˈsɪstəlɪθ/ | n. | 膀胱结石；钟乳体 | A small, hard, usually calcareous body found in the epidermal cells of certain plants, especially in the leaves of some members of the families Urticaceae and Moraceae. |  Example: Cystoliths are readily visible under a microscope in the leaves of the fig tree.  无花果树的叶子里用显微镜很容易看到膀胱结石。

Example: The presence of cystoliths can be a useful characteristic for plant identification. 膀胱结石的存在可以作为植物鉴定的有用特征。 | Synonym: concretion | Antonym: N/A | noun
