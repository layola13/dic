mosaics:/moʊˈzeɪɪks/ | n. | 马赛克，镶嵌图案 | A picture or pattern produced by arranging together small colored pieces of hard material, such as stone, tile, or glass. | The artist created beautiful mosaics using tiny pieces of colored glass.  艺术家用彩色玻璃碎片创作了美丽的马赛克。 | synonyms: inlay, tessellation | antonyms: whole, solid | noun

mosaics:/moʊˈzeɪɪks/ | n. | 综合体，混合体 | A combination of diverse elements forming a more complex whole. |  Her novel is a mosaics of different genres and styles. 她的小说是不同体裁和风格的综合体。 | synonyms: blend, mixture, composite | antonyms: uniformity, homogeneity | noun
