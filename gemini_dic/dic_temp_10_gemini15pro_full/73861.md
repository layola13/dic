formulation:/ˌfɔːrmjəˈleɪʃn/ | n. | 配方；公式化；构想；表达 |  The act or process of formulating something, such as a plan, theory, or product.  The way in which something is stated or expressed. |  The chemist worked on the formulation of a new drug.  化学家致力于研发一种新药的配方。

The formulation of the policy took several months.  这项政策的制定花了几个月的时间。| 近义词：development, creation, production, expression, articulation | 反义词：dissolution, destruction | 词性: noun


formulate:/ˈfɔːrmjəleɪt/ | v. | 构想，规划；明确表达；系统地阐述 | To create or devise methodically (a strategy, policy, theory etc.). To express (an idea or thought) clearly and effectively. |  The committee formulated a plan to raise money.  委员会制定了一个筹款计划。

She struggled to formulate her thoughts coherently.  她努力使自己的想法表达得连贯。| 近义词：develop, devise, frame, articulate, express | 反义词：destroy, dissolve | 词性: verb
