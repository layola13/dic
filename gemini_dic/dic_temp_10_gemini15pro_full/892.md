abuelas:/aˈβwelas/ | n. | grandmothers; maternal grandmothers (specifically in some dialects) | Plural form of abuela (Spanish for "grandmother").  It refers to two or more grandmothers. | My abuelas are wonderful cooks. 我的祖母们都是很棒的厨师。

Mis abuelas me visitan cada verano.  My grandmothers visit me every summer. | 近义词: grandmothers, nanas (informal) | 反义词: grandsons, granddaughters | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish "abuela", which comes from Latin "avia" (grandmother).
