inefficient:/ˌɪnɪˈfɪʃənt/| adj.|效率低的，无能的|Not achieving maximum productivity; wasting or failing to make the best use of time or resources.|The inefficient system wasted both time and money.  这个低效的系统既浪费时间又浪费金钱。|The company's inefficient use of resources led to financial losses.  该公司对资源的低效使用导致了财务损失。|Synonyms: unproductive, ineffective, incompetent, wasteful|Antonyms: efficient, effective, productive, competent|adjective
