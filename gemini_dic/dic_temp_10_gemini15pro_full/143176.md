outclimbing:/ˌaʊtˈklaɪmɪŋ/
verb
胜过攀登，在攀登方面超越
Surpassing someone in climbing ability.
The young climber outclimbed his more experienced partner.  年轻的登山者在攀登能力上超过了他更有经验的伙伴。
outclimbed (past tense)
outclimbs (third person singular present)
outclimbing (present participle)
surpass in climbing, excel in climbing
better, defeat
be outclimbed by, lose to in climbing
