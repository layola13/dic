ambulatorily:/ˌæmbjʊləˈtɔːrɪli/
adverb|走动地，不卧床地|In a manner that involves walking or moving about.|The patient was ambulatorily treated, meaning they didn't need to stay overnight in the hospital. 患者接受了门诊治疗，这意味着他们不需要在医院过夜。|例句：He was able to move ambulatorily after the surgery.  手术后他能够走动。|近义词：walking, mobile, on foot|反义词:bedridden, immobile, stationary|词性:adverb

Etymology:
From Latin *ambulatorius*, from ambulare ("to walk") + -orius (adjective suffix).
