clowe: /kloʊ/
noun |爪子; 抓住|A clowe is a sharp, curved nail on the foot of an animal, especially a cat, dog, or bird.  It can also refer to the whole foot of these animals.  In older usage, it could also refer to a clove of garlic.|The cat sharpened its clowe on the scratching post. 猫在抓板上磨爪子。|Synonym: claw, talon (for birds of prey)|Antonym: paw (softer, without claws)|n.
