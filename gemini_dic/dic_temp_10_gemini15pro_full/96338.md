indigofera:/ˌɪndɪɡəˈfɛrə/ | n. | 槐蓝属; 木蓝; |A large genus of shrubs and herbs of the pea family, many of which yield indigo. |例句：Indigofera tinctoria is the main source of natural indigo dye.  槐蓝是天然靛蓝染料的主要来源。

|例句：Many species of Indigofera are used in traditional medicine. 许多种槐蓝属植物用于传统医学。|近义词：indigo plant |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *indicum (indigo) + -fera (bearing).  "Indicum" itself comes from Greek "indikon", meaning "Indian (dye)".  The "-fera" suffix is commonly used in botanical names to indicate "bearing" or "carrying."
