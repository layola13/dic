halfcoaxingly:/ˌhæfkoʊˈæksɪŋli/
halfcoaxingly| adv. |半哄半骗地|In a manner that is partly coaxing and partly some other unspecified means, often implying a gentle or slightly manipulative approach.|She halfcoaxingly persuaded him to try the spicy dish. 她半哄半骗地劝他尝尝那道辣菜。|近义词：persuasively, gently, subtly|反义词：forcefully, aggressively|词性:副词

halfcoaxingly|词源:  "half-" (meaning partly) + "coaxingly" (derived from "coax," meaning to persuade by gentle urging or flattery).  The "-ingly" suffix transforms the verb "coax" into an adverb.|词源解析：由“half-”（意为部分地）和“coaxingly”（源自“coax”，意为通过温和的劝说或奉承来说服）组成。“-ingly”后缀将动词“coax”转换为副词。
