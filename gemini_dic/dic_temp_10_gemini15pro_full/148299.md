oversoftnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈsɒftnəsɪz/ /ˌoʊvərˈsɔːftnəsɪz/
noun
过度柔软；极度的柔软
extreme softness; excessive softness
The oversoftnesses of the cushions made it difficult to sit up straight.  这些垫子太软了，很难坐直。
The artist captured the oversoftnesses of the clouds in the painting.  这位艺术家在画中捕捉到了云朵的极致柔软。
近义词:flabbiness, sponginess, mushiness
反义词:firmness, hardness, rigidity
词性:noun


Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above") + "soft" (Old English softe, from Proto-Germanic *samftijaz) + "-ness" (Old English -nes(s), suffix forming nouns of state or quality)
