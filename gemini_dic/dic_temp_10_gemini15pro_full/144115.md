outscoop:/ˌaʊtˈskuːp/
verb
抢先报道；比…先得到独家新闻
To get a news story before (a competitor); to publish a story before someone else.
The rival newspaper outscooped them on the latest scandal.  竞争对手的报纸抢在他们前面报道了最新的丑闻。
The local paper was outscooped by a national daily. 地方报纸被一家全国性的日报抢先报道了。
Synonyms: beat, scoop, forestall, preempt
Antonyms: be scooped, be beaten to the punch
词性:v.
