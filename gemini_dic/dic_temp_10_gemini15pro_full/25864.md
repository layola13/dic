burkhas: /ˈbɜːrkəz/ | n. | 布卡，罩袍 | A long, loose garment covering the whole body from head to toe, worn in public by some Muslim women. |  Many women choose to wear burkhas as an expression of their religious beliefs. 许多女性选择穿布卡作为表达她们宗教信仰的一种方式。

Many countries have debated the legality of wearing burkhas in public spaces. 许多国家就公共场所穿布卡的合法性进行了辩论。

| 近义词：niqab, chador, hijab | 反义词: Western clothing, revealing clothing | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Urdu بُرقع (burqaʿ), from Arabic بُرْقُع (burquʿ, “face veil”). Ultimately from the root ب ر ق ('baraqa) "to shine, flash, glitter, lightning" referring to the thin material.
