galvanically:/ɡælˈvænɪkli/
adjective
电镀地，电流地
in a way that relates to or involves electric currents, especially ones produced by chemical activity.
The two metals were galvanically connected. 这两种金属用电流连接。
近义词：electrically
反义词：none
词性：adjective
