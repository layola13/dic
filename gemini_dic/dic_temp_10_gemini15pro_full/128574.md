niblike:/ˈnɪblaɪk/| adj.|像笔尖的，尖细的|Resembling a pen nib; pointed.|The bird had a niblike beak. 这只鸟有一个像笔尖一样的喙。|近义词：pointed, sharp, fine|反义词:blunt, rounded|词性:形容词

词源:  From 'nib' (the pointed writing end of a pen) + '-like' (suffix indicating resemblance). 'Nib' ultimately comes from Old English *nebb, meaning beak or nose.
