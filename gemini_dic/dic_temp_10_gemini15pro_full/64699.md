escapological:/ˌɛskəpəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
逃避主义的，逃避现实的
Relating to or providing a means of escape, especially from reality or routine.
例句：She enjoys reading escapological fiction to forget her troubles. 她喜欢阅读逃避现实的小说来忘记她的烦恼。

例句：The film provides a welcome escapological fantasy for viewers tired of everyday life.  这部电影为厌倦日常生活的观众提供了一个受欢迎的逃避现实的幻想。

近义词： escapist
反义词：realistic, practical
词性：adjective
