cosplaying:/ˈkɒspleɪɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːspleɪɪŋ/ (US)| n. |角色扮演|The act of dressing up and acting as a fictional character, often from anime, manga, video games, or movies.|例句：Cosplaying is a popular hobby at conventions. 角色扮演是漫展上很受欢迎的爱好。
