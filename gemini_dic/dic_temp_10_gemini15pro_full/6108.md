amerindians:/əˌmɛrɪnˈdiənz/ | n. |美洲印第安人|A term used to refer to the indigenous peoples of the Americas, excluding the Inuit and Aleut.  This term is considered outdated and potentially offensive by many, and "Indigenous peoples of the Americas" is generally preferred. | Many museums now display artifacts made by Amerindians. 许多博物馆现在展出美洲印第安人制作的文物。 |  Indigenous peoples of the Americas, Native Americans, First Nations (in Canada), American Indians |  None (as the term refers to a group of people) | Noun
