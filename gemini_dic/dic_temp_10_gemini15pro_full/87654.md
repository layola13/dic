hexadactylism:/ˌhɛksəˈdæktɪlɪzəm/ | n. | 六指（趾）畸形 |  A congenital anomaly characterized by the presence of six fingers or toes on one or both hands or feet. |  Hexadactylism is a relatively common genetic condition. 六指（趾）畸形是一种相对常见的遗传性疾病。| Polydactyly |  - (No direct antonym, could consider pentadactyly which is the normal five-digit state) | noun
