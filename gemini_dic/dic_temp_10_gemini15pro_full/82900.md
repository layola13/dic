gumball:/ˈɡʌmˌbɔːl/ | n. | 泡泡糖球，口香糖球 | A chewy, brightly colored candy, typically spherical, that is dispensed from a gumball machine. |  I spent all my allowance on gumballs. 我把所有的零花钱都花在了泡泡糖球上。

| The gumball machine was empty. 泡泡糖机器是空的。 | synonyms: chewing gum, bubble gum | antonyms: None | noun
