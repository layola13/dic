grottolike: /ˈɡrɒtəʊlaɪk/ | adj. | 像洞穴的，洞穴般的 | Resembling a grotto; cave-like. | The restaurant had a grottolike atmosphere, with dim lighting and rough stone walls.  这家餐厅有一种像洞穴般的氛围，灯光昏暗，石墙粗糙。|  The narrow, winding passage felt grottolike and mysterious. 这条狭窄蜿蜒的通道感觉像洞穴般神秘。 | 近义词: cavernous, cavelike | 反义词: open, airy | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From "grotto" (Italian *grotta*, from Late Latin *grupta*, from Greek *kryptē* "a vault, crypt") + "-like" (Old English *līc*, meaning "having the form or appearance of").
