boogy:/ˈbuːɡi/| vi. n.|布吉舞，跳布吉舞|To dance to boogy music; a lively, rhythmical dance, typically to rock and roll or blues music.|He was boogying on the dance floor. 他在舞池里跳布吉舞。|近义词：dance, groove, jive|反义词:stand still, sit|verb, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From earlier boogie-woogie, a style of blues piano music, of unknown origin.
