hanked:/hæŋkt/ | v. | 渴望，热切盼望 | To have a strong, restless desire or craving for something. |  I hankered for a piece of chocolate cake. 我渴望一块巧克力蛋糕。

|  She hankered after a simpler life in the countryside. 她渴望乡村更简单的生活。 | 近义词：yearn, crave, long for, desire | 反义词: dislike, hate | 词性: 动词

词源:
From Middle Dutch *hancken or Middle Low German *hanken  "to hang, dangle," also "to linger, tarry," hence "to hang about," then figuratively "to yearn for."
