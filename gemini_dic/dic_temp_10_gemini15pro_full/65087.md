ethenes:/ˈɛθiːnz/ | n. |乙烯类|A group of unsaturated hydrocarbons with a carbon-carbon double bond. Specifically, it refers to alkenes with the general formula CnH2n.|Ethenes are important building blocks in the petrochemical industry. 乙烯类是石油化工行业重要的组成部分。|Near synonyms: alkenes, olefins|Antonyms: alkanes|Noun
