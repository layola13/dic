indecent:/ɪnˈdiːsnt/| adj.|不雅的，下流的，猥亵的|Not conforming to accepted standards of morality or decency, especially in relation to sexual behavior or appearance.|The comedian told an indecent joke.  那个喜剧演员讲了一个下流的笑话。|The photograph was considered indecent. 这张照片被认为是不雅的。|近义词：obscene, lewd, improper, immodest, offensive|反义词：decent, proper, modest|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from Latin indecent- ‘unbecoming’, from in- ‘not’ + decent- ‘fitting’, present participle of decere.
