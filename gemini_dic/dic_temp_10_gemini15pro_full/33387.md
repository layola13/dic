PHRASE->

What's up? (怎么了？/你好吗？)  -  Not directly related to "chuntered" but used in conversational context.

What the hell! (搞什么鬼！) - Expressing frustration, sometimes preceding or following a chunter.

what if (如果...会怎么样) - Can be used in conjunction with a chunter, e.g., "What if he keeps chuntering?"

what for (为了什么)  - Can be used in response to a chunter, e.g., "What are you chuntering about, what for?"

no matter what (无论如何) -  Not directly related but can be used in the context of persistent chuntering.
