committal: /kəˈmɪtl/ (US), /kəˈmɪtəl/ (UK)| n. adj.| 承诺，保证；拘禁令；委托；承担义务 | An act of committing, or the state of being committed; a pledge or engagement; the act of sending a person to prison by warrant or order; consignment to custody; the act of referring something to a committee. |  The judge signed the committal order for the suspect. 法官签署了对嫌疑人的拘禁令。| synonyms: pledge, undertaking, consignment, referral, imprisonment | antonyms: release, freedom, discharge, revocation | noun

committal: /kəˈmɪtl/ (US), /kəˈmɪtəl/ (UK)| adj.| 承担义务的；需要承担义务的；不坦率的，含糊其辞的| Having the nature of a commitment; obligating; noncommittal. |  Her response to the proposal was surprisingly committal. 她对这个提议的回应出人意料地表示了承诺。| synonyms: binding, obligatory, decisive | antonyms: noncommittal, evasive, unbinding | adjective
