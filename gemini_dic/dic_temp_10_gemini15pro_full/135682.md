nonprescriber:/ˌnɑːnprɪˈskraɪbər/ | n. | 非处方药师；非开处方者 |  A healthcare professional who is not authorized to prescribe medication. | Patients often consult with nonprescribers like nurses or dieticians before seeing a doctor.  病人经常在看医生之前咨询护士或营养师等非处方药师。| synonyms: non-physician, allied health professional | antonyms: prescriber, physician, doctor | noun
