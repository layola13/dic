miniaturistic:/ˌmɪnɪəˈtʃʊərɪstɪk/
adjective
微型的，小型化的，微缩模型的
extremely small; resembling a miniature
例句：She creates miniaturist portraits of birds. 她创作鸟类的微型肖像。

例句：The dollhouse was filled with miniaturist furniture. 玩具屋里摆满了微型家具。

近义词：miniature, tiny, diminutive, small, minute
反义词：large, huge, gigantic, enormous, massive
词性：adjective
