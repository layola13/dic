hutchinson:/ˈhʌtʃɪnsən/ | n. | 哈钦森（姓氏；地名） | A surname, derived from a patronymic meaning "son of Hutchin," where Hutchin is a diminutive of Hugh. Also, a place name in several locations, most notably in Kansas, USA. |  Example 1: Anne Hutchinson was a prominent figure in the Antinomian Controversy in the Massachusetts Bay Colony. 安妮·哈钦森是马萨诸塞湾殖民地反律法主义论战中的杰出人物。

Example 2:  We're driving through Hutchinson, Kansas, on our way to Denver. 我们开车途经堪萨斯州的哈钦森，前往丹佛。 | Synonym: (for surname - no direct synonym, perhaps family name, last name)  (for place name - town, city depending on the place) | Antonym: (None) | noun
