numskulled:/ˈnʌmskʌld/
adjective
愚蠢的，笨的
lacking intelligence; stupid.
He made a numskulled decision to invest all his money in a failing business. 他做了一个愚蠢的决定，把所有的钱都投资于一家失败的企业。
Synonyms: stupid, foolish, idiotic, dense, witless, brainless
Antonyms: intelligent, smart, bright, clever, wise
词性: 形容词
