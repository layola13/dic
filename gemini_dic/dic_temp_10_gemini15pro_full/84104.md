half-Irish:/ˌhæf ˈaɪrɪʃ/
adjective
有一半爱尔兰血统的
Of or relating to a person having one parent of Irish descent.
My friend is half-Irish; his mother emigrated from Dublin. 我的朋友有一半爱尔兰血统；他的母亲是从都柏林移民过来的。
My colleague is half-Irish and half-German. 我的同事一半爱尔兰血统，一半德国血统。
Synonyms: part-Irish
Antonyms: full-blooded Irish
