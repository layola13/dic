garlandless:/ˈɡɑːrləndləs/
adjective
没有花环的，无装饰的
Without garlands; lacking festive decorations.
The Christmas tree looked sadly garlandless this year.  今年的圣诞树看起来很凄凉，没有花环装饰。
The stage was garlandless, giving the performance a somber feel. 舞台上没有花环，使演出有一种忧郁的感觉。
Synonyms: undecorated, unadorned, bare
Antonyms: decorated, adorned, festooned
词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From garland + -less.  Garland comes from Old French *garlande, of uncertain origin.  The suffix -less is a common Old English suffix indicating absence or lack.
词源：来自 garland + -less。Garland 源于古法语 *garlande，起源不确定。后缀 -less 是一个常见的古英语后缀，表示没有或缺乏。
