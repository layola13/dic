oversalting:/ˌoʊvərˈsɔːltɪŋ/ | n. | 过度加盐，加盐过多 | The act of adding too much salt to food. |  The oversalting ruined the flavor of the soup. 汤加盐过多了，味道都坏了。| Near synonyms: overseasoning | Antonym: underseasoning | noun

oversalted:/ˌoʊvərˈsɔːltɪd/ | adj. | 咸的，加盐过多的 | Containing too much salt. | The steak was oversalted and inedible. 牛排太咸了，没法吃。| Near synonyms: salty, overseasoned | Antonym: underseasoned | adjective
