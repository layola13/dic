misperforms:/ˌmɪspərˈfɔːrmz/ /ˌmɪspərˈfɔːrms/
verb
执行不佳，表现欠佳
To perform below expectations or standards; to fail to achieve the desired or expected results.
The company misperformed last quarter due to declining sales.  由于销售额下降，该公司上个季度的业绩不佳。
underperforms, fails, falters
outperforms, excels, succeeds
v.
