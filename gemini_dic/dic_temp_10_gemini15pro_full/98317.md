insignificance:/ˌɪnsɪɡˈnɪfɪkəns/| n. |无足轻重，微不足道|The insignificance of the event was surprising.  这件事的微不足道令人惊讶。|例句：He felt a sense of insignificance in the vast universe. 他在浩瀚的宇宙中感到一种渺小的感觉。|近义词：unimportance, triviality, paltriness|反义词: significance, importance, consequence|词性:名词

insignificant:/ˌɪnsɪɡˈnɪfɪkənt/| adj. |无足轻重的，微不足道的|The amount of money was insignificant compared to the total cost. 与总成本相比，这笔钱微不足道。|例句：The differences between the two versions were insignificant.  两个版本之间的差异微不足道。|近义词：unimportant, trivial, negligible, paltry|反义词: significant, important, consequential|词性:形容词
