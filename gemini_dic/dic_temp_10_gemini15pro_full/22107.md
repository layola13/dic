bolshevist:/ˈbɒlʃəvɪst/ (UK) /ˈboʊlʃəvɪst/ (US)| n. adj.|布尔什维克，布尔什维克主义者|A member of the majority faction of the Russian Social Democratic Party, which seized power in the October Revolution of 1917.  Also used as an adjective to describe things relating to Bolshevism.|
例句：He was denounced as a Bolshevist. 他被谴责为布尔什维克。
例句：The Bolshevist revolution changed the course of history. 布尔什维克革命改变了历史进程。

近义词：communist, revolutionary, Marxist, socialist|
反义词：Menshevik, Tsarist, anti-communist|
词性: noun, adjective|


bolshevik:/ˌbɒlʃəˈvɪk/ (UK) /ˌboʊlʃəˈvɪk/ (US)| n. adj.|布尔什维克，布尔什维克主义者| Alternative spelling of Bolshevist, generally considered less common in modern usage.  Carries the same meanings as Bolshevist.|
例句：The Bolshevik uprising led to the creation of the Soviet Union. 布尔什维克起义导致了苏联的建立。
例句：He held strong Bolshevik beliefs. 他持有坚定的布尔什维克信仰。

近义词：communist, revolutionary, Marxist, socialist|
反义词：Menshevik, Tsarist, anti-communist|
词性: noun, adjective|

词源: From Russian  большевик (bol'ševik), meaning "member of the majority", derived from большинство (bol'šinstvo), "majority". This refers to the majority faction of the Russian Social Democratic Labour Party led by Vladimir Lenin.
