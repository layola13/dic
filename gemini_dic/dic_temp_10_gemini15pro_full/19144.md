bevile: /ˈbɛvəl/
noun|斜面，斜角；|A sloping surface or edge.|The picture frame had a subtle bevile.|Synonym: slant, slope, bevel, cant|Antonym: straight, level|n.

verb|使成斜角，使成斜面；|To cut or shape to a sloping edge.|He beveled the edges of the wooden plank.|Synonym: slant, slope, bevel, cant|Antonym: straighten, level|v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *bevel*,  probably from a Vulgar Latin *biuellus*, diminutive of Latin *bis*, "twice".  The original sense likely referred to a carpenter's tool with two arms or blades.
