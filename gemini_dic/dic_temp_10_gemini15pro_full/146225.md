overexploit:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈsplɔɪt/| vt.|过度开发，过度利用|To make excessive use of something, especially natural resources, to the point of depletion or damage.|The fishing industry has overexploited the ocean's resources, leading to a decline in fish populations.|近义词：overuse, deplete, exhaust|反义词：conserve, protect, preserve|verb
