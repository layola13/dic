dottily:/ˈdɒtɪli/ (US) /ˈdɑːtɪli/| adv.|糊涂地，愚蠢地，古怪地|In a scatterbrained or slightly crazy manner. Characterized by foolish or eccentric behavior.|She dottily wandered around the house looking for her glasses, which were perched on her head. 她糊里糊涂地在房子里四处游荡，寻找她的眼镜，而眼镜就戴在她的头上。

The old woman dottily insisted that she was a princess.  这位老妇人糊涂地坚持说她是一位公主。


近义词:absently, distractedly, foolishly, eccentrically, crazily|反义词:sensibly, rationally, wisely|词性:副词
