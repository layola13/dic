atomically:/əˈtɒmɪkli/| adv.|原子地，不可分割地|Performed as a single, uninterruptible unit.  It ensures that all parts of an operation complete successfully or none do, preventing partial updates and data corruption.|The database transaction was processed atomically, ensuring data integrity.|Synonyms: indivisibly, inseparably, all at once|Antonyms: partially, piecemeal|adverb
