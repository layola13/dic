curries:/ˈkʌriz/ | n. | 咖喱菜肴；咖喱粉 | A dish or dishes flavored with curry powder. |  She made a delicious chicken curry. 她做了一道美味的鸡肉咖喱。

curries:/ˈkʌriz/ | v. |  给…加咖喱粉；用咖喱粉调味（curry的第三人称单数） | To flavor (food) with curry powder. | He curries the lamb with a blend of spices. 他用混合香料给羊肉加咖喱味。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Tamil கறி (kaṟi, “sauce, relish for rice”).

SYNONYM->
(n.) curry dish, curry powder
(v.) spice, season

ANTONYM->
(n.) bland dish (for curry dish)
