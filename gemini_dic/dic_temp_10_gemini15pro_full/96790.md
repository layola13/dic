inergetical:/ˌɪnərˈdʒɛtɪkəl/| adj.|惰性的，不活动的|Lacking energy or activity; inert.|The inergetical mass remained motionless despite the force applied. 惰性质量尽管受到力的作用，仍然保持静止。|近义词：inert, inactive, passive, sluggish|反义词：active, energetic, dynamic|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *inergeticus*, from iners (idle, inactive) + -eticus (relating to).
