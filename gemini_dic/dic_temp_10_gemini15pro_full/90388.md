however:/haʊˈevər/| adv. conj.|然而，但是，无论如何|Used to introduce a statement that contrasts with or seems to contradict something that has been said previously.|The weather was bad; however, we still went out. 天气很糟糕；然而，我们还是出去了。|近义词：nevertheless, nonetheless, still, yet, but|反义词:therefore, consequently|词性:副词/连词
词源:From Middle English *hou ever*, equivalent to *how* + *ever*.
