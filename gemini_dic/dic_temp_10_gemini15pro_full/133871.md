nongravitationally:/nɒnˌɡrævɪˈteɪʃənəli/
adverb
非重力地
Not relating to or caused by gravity; by means other than gravity.
The particles were dispersed nongravitationally. 颗粒是非重力分散的。
The satellite was propelled nongravitationally using a solar sail. 这颗卫星使用太阳帆进行非重力推进。
Synonym:  non-gravitationally (less common, essentially the same)
Antonym: gravitationally
词性: adverb
