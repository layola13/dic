fancifully:/fænˈsɪfəli/
adverb
异想天开地，奇特地
In a fanciful or imaginative way.
She described her dream trip fancifully, with talking animals and floating castles. 她异想天开地描述了她的梦想之旅，里面有会说话的动物和漂浮的城堡。
The author fancifully depicted a world where gravity worked in reverse. 作者奇特地描绘了一个重力反向运行的世界。
Synonyms: imaginatively, whimsically, fantastically, elaborately
Antonyms: realistically, practically, plainly, simply
词性: 副词
