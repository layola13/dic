micromere: /ˈmaɪkrəˌmɪər/ | n. | 小裂球，微球 | A small blastomere, especially one formed by unequal cleavage of a fertilized ovum. |  The micromeres at the vegetal pole of the sea urchin embryo are essential for its development. 海胆胚胎植物极的小裂球对其发育至关重要。

The micromeres give rise to the skeleton of the larva. 小裂球形成幼虫的骨骼。 | 近义词: blastomere (in some contexts) | 反义词: macromere | 词性: noun
