bizarro:/bɪˈzɑːroʊ/ (US), /bɪˈzɑːrəʊ/ (UK)| adj. n.|古怪的，奇异的；怪人|Very strange or unusual.|例句：That's a bizarro story! 那真是个怪诞的故事！|例句：He's got some bizarro ideas about politics. 他对政治有一些古怪的想法。|近义词：weird, strange, odd, eccentric, bizarre|反义词：normal, ordinary, conventional|词性:形容词，名词

bizarro:/bɪˈzɑːroʊ/ (US), /bɪˈzɑːrəʊ/ (UK)| n.|怪人|A bizarre or eccentric person.|例句：He's a real bizarro. 他真是个怪人。|例句：The party was full of bizarros. 派对上满是怪人。|近义词：eccentric, oddball, weirdo|反义词：normal person|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *bizarro*, originally meaning 'brave, gallant'.  The current meaning of 'strange, unusual' developed in American slang, possibly influenced by the character Bizarro from DC Comics' Superman, a flawed and reversed duplicate of Superman.
