calyx:/ˈkeɪlɪks/ (US), /ˈkælɪks/ (UK)| n. |花萼|The sepals of a flower, typically forming a whorl that encloses the petals and forms a protective layer around a flower in bud.|The calyx of the hibiscus flower is a deep red.|近义词:sepals|反义词:corolla|noun
