hyksos:/ˈhɪksɒs/| n.|喜克索斯人|A group of people of mixed Semitic and Asiatic origin who ruled Egypt during the Second Intermediate Period (c. 1650-1550 BC).  They are believed to have originated in the Levant.|The Hyksos introduced the horse and chariot to Egypt. 喜克索斯人将马和战车引入了埃及。|Synonyms:  Shepherd Kings|Antonyms:  Native Egyptians|Noun
