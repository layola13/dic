bistort:/ˈbɪstɔːrt/| n.|拳参;|A perennial herb (genus Bistorta, especially B. officinalis syn. Polygonum bistorta) of the dock family, having spikes of pink flowers and a twisted rootstock used medicinally.|The bistort's twisted root is said to have medicinal properties.|近义词:snakeweed, knotweed|反义词:|noun
