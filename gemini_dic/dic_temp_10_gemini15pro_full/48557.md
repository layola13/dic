defamers:/dɪˈfeɪmərz/
n.
诽谤者，中伤者
People who defame someone; those who attack the reputation of another.
The defamers were sued for libel. 诽谤者被控告诽谤罪。
synonyms: slanderers, libelers, maligners, traducers, vilifiers
antonyms: admirers, praisers, eulogizers
noun
