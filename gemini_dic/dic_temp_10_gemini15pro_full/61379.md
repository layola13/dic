emblazon:/ɪmˈbleɪzən/ | vt. | 装饰；颂扬；炫耀 | To adorn or decorate richly, especially with heraldic devices; to celebrate or extol publicly. |  The knight's shield was emblazoned with a golden lion. 骑士的盾牌上装饰着一头金色的狮子。

The family's history was emblazoned on a tapestry hanging in the hall.  家族的历史被绣在一幅挂在大厅里的挂毯上。 | 近义词：adorn, decorate, ornament, celebrate, extol | 反义词：deface, obscure, conceal | 词性：动词
