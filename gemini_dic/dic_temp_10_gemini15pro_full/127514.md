neckwear:/ˈnekˌwer/| n.|领带，领结，围巾等颈部服饰|Articles of clothing worn around the neck, such as ties, scarves, and cravats.|He chose a silk neckwear to complement his suit. 他选择了一条丝绸领带搭配他的西装。|Synonyms: neckcloth, accessory|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps casual wear)|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From neck + wear.  "Wear" in this context comes from Old English *werian "to wear, carry, bear," related to Old High German werien "to adorn, clothe," Gothic wasjan "to clothe," from PIE root *wes- (1) "to clothe."
