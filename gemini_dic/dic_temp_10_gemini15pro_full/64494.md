erode:/ɪˈroʊd/ (US) /ɪˈrəʊd/ (UK)| v. |侵蚀，腐蚀；削弱，损害|To gradually wear away or destroy something by natural forces (such as water, wind, or ice) or by chemical action; to gradually diminish or destroy something.|The coastline is slowly being eroded by the sea. 海岸线正慢慢地被海水侵蚀。|近义词：wear away, corrode, abrade, deteriorate, undermine|反义词：build up, strengthen, enhance, augment|词性:动词
