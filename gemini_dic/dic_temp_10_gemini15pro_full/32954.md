choragic:/kəˈrædʒɪk/ | adj. | 合唱的，合唱队的，与合唱有关的 | Relating to a chorus or choir, especially in ancient Greek drama; of or relating to a choragus. | The choragic monument of Lysicrates is a well-preserved example of ancient Greek architecture.  利西克拉特合唱纪念碑是古希腊建筑保存完好的典范。 | The play featured elaborate choragic odes. 这部戏剧以精心制作的合唱颂歌为特色。 | 近义词: choral | 反义词:  (No clear antonym) | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Greek *khoragikos*, from *khoragos* "chorus leader," from *khoros* "chorus, dance" + *agein* "to lead."
