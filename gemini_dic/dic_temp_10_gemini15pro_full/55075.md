disremember:/ˌdɪsrɪˈmɛmbər/| v. |忘记，记不起|To fail to remember; forget.|I disremember where I put my keys. 我不记得我把钥匙放哪儿了.|近义词：forget, misremember|反义词：remember, recall|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- + remember.  The prefix "dis-" indicates negation or reversal, so "disremember" literally means "to un-remember" or "to not remember."
