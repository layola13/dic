devouring:/dɪˈvaʊərɪŋ/
adjective
吞食的，贪婪的
consuming eagerly
The devouring flames engulfed the entire building. 吞噬一切的火焰吞没了整座建筑。
近义词：consuming, engulfing, voracious
反义词： nibbling, sparing

devour:/dɪˈvaʊər/
verb
吞食，贪婪地吃，毁灭
eat (food or prey) hungrily or quickly.
He devoured the entire plate of spaghetti. 他狼吞虎咽地吃完了整盘意大利面。
近义词：consume, gobble, gulp
反义词： nibble


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French devourer, from Latin devorare "to swallow down, gulp down, devour," from de- "down, completely" + vorare "to swallow, devour."
