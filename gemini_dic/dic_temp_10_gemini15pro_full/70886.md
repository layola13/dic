finway:/ˈfɪnweɪ/ | n. | 鱼路，鳍路 | A path or route followed by fish, especially when migrating or spawning.  It is not a standard English word but a  neologism, likely formed by analogy with "pathway" or "highway", using "fin" to indicate its relation to fish. | The salmon found their finway back to their spawning grounds. 鲑鱼找到了返回产卵地的鱼路。 | The young trout followed the ancient finway through the rapids. 幼小的鳟鱼沿着古老的鱼路穿过急流。 | 近义词: migration route, fish passage | 反义词:  (No established antonym) | 词性: 名词
