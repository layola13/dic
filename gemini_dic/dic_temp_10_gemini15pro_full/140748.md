omnisexuality:/ˌɒmniˌsɛkʃʊˈælɪti/ | n. | 全性恋 |  Attraction to people of all genders and sexes, with the gender and sex of the other person not being a limiting factor.  It emphasizes the potential for emotional, romantic, or sexual connections with individuals regardless of their gender identity or sexual orientation. |  Example 1:  "Omnisexuality acknowledges the spectrum of gender and allows for attraction based on individual characteristics rather than strict categories."  全性恋承认性别的多样性，并允许基于个人特征而非严格类别来产生吸引力。

Example 2: "She identifies as omnisexual because she finds herself attracted to people of various genders, including men, women, transgender, and non-binary individuals." 她认同自己是全性恋，因为她发现自己会被各种性别的人所吸引，包括男性、女性、跨性别者和非二元性别者。 | Near Synonyms: pansexual, polysexual | Antonyms: monosexual | noun
