nontyrannically:/ˌnɒntɪˈrænɪkli/ | adv. | 非专制地，不残暴地 | In a way that is not tyrannical; without exercising oppressive power. | The leader governed nontyrannically, respecting the rights of his people. 领导人以非专制的方式进行统治，尊重人民的权利。| Example: He ruled nontyrannically, always consulting with his advisors. 他以非专横的方式统治，总是咨询他的顾问。|Synonyms: democratically, fairly, justly, equitably, liberally | Antonyms: tyrannically, despotically, dictatorially, oppressively, autocratically |词性:副词
