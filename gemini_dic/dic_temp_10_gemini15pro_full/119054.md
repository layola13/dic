metises:/ˈmɛtiːsiːz/ /məˈtiːsiz/
|n.|混血儿（尤指欧裔加拿大人与原住民的混血后代）; 混血人（尤指欧洲人和非欧洲人的混血儿）|People of mixed race, especially the offspring of a European Canadian and a First Nations person; a person of mixed race, especially one having European and non-European parentage.|The community is made up of metises, descendants of European fur traders and indigenous women. 这个社区由混血儿组成，他们是欧洲皮毛商人和土著妇女的后裔。|
|synonyms: mixed-race people, hybrids, half-breeds (offensive), half-castes (offensive)|antonyms: purebreds (sometimes used for people in an offensive way)|noun|

metis:/ˈmɛtiːs/ /məˈtiːs/
|n.|混血儿（尤指欧裔加拿大人与原住民的混血后代）; 混血人（尤指欧洲人和非欧洲人的混血儿）|A person of mixed race, especially the offspring of a European Canadian and a First Nations person; a person of mixed race, especially one having European and non-European parentage.|Louis Riel was a prominent Métis leader in the 19th century. 路易·里尔是19世纪著名的梅蒂斯人领袖。|
|synonyms: mixed-race person, hybrid, half-breed (offensive), half-caste (offensive)|antonyms: purebred (sometimes used for people in an offensive way)|noun|
