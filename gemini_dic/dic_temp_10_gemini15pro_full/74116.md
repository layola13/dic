foul-up:/ˈfaʊlˌʌp/| n.|混乱，搞砸，失误|A situation that has gone wrong or been muddled.|The airline strike caused a major foul-up with our travel plans. 航空公司的罢工导致我们的旅行计划一片混乱。|近义词：mess, blunder, snafu, screw-up|反义词：success, triumph|词性:名词

foul-up:/ˈfaʊlˌʌp/| v.|搞砸，弄糟|To make a mistake; to cause something to go wrong.|I completely foul-up the presentation. 我完全搞砸了演讲。|近义词：mess up, bungle, spoil|反义词：succeed, fix|词性:动词

词源: "foul" (犯规，不好的) + "up" (向上，完全地)  组合而成，表示事情变得糟糕或混乱。
