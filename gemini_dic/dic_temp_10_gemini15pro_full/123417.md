monoplast:/ˈmɑːnəˌplæst/ | n. |单层塑料；单层膜|A thin, flexible sheet of plastic, typically used for packaging or covering. | The strawberries were covered with a monoplast to protect them. 草莓上覆盖着一层单层膜以保护它们。| The delicate pastries were individually wrapped in monoplast.  这些精致的糕点分别用单层塑料包装。 | synonym: plastic film, plastic sheet, plastic wrap | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps thick plastic sheeting) | noun
