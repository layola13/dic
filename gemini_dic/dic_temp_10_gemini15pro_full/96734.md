inelegance:/ɪnˈɛlɪɡəns/
n.
不优雅，粗俗，不得体
lack of elegance; awkwardness; crudeness
Her inelegance in handling the situation caused embarrassment. 她处理这种情况时的不得体令人尴尬。

近义词:awkwardness, clumsiness, gaucheness, gracelessness, uncouthness
反义词:elegance, grace, refinement, sophistication
词性:名词
