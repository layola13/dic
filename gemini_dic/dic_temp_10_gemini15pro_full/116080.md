martlet:/ˈmɑːtlɪt/| n.|燕子（尤指没有后爪的一种传说中的鸟），纹章上的燕子图案|A small, swift bird, typically a swallow or swift, often depicted without feet in heraldry. It symbolizes constant activity and readiness for action. The martlet is a common charge in English heraldry.|例句：The crest features three martlets in flight.  徽章上有三只正在飞翔的燕子。

例句：The martlet, legless by tradition, is a symbol of the landless younger sons of nobility.  按照传统，无腿的燕子象征着没有土地的贵族幼子。|近义词：swallow, swift|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
