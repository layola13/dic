anemophilous:/ˌænɪˈmɒfɪləs/ | adj. |风媒的|Pollinated by wind; wind-loving. |例句：Oak trees are anemophilous, relying on the wind to carry their pollen to other trees. 橡树是风媒的，依靠风把花粉带到其他树上。

例句：Anemophilous plants often have inconspicuous flowers because they don't need to attract insects for pollination. 风媒植物的花通常不显眼，因为它们不需要吸引昆虫来授粉。|近义词：wind-pollinated|反义词：entomophilous (insect-pollinated), hydrophilous (water-pollinated) |词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ánemos* (“wind”) + *phílos* (“loving”).
