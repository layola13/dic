armoires:/ɑːrˈmwɑːrz/
n.
衣柜；橱柜
A large wardrobe or movable cupboard, typically one that is ornate or antique.
The bedroom was furnished with an antique bed, a dresser, and two large armoires. 卧室里摆放着一张古董床、一个梳妆台和两个大衣柜。
synonyms: wardrobe, cupboard, closet, cabinet
antonyms: shelf, drawer
noun
