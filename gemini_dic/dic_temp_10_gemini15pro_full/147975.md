overrestriction:/ˌoʊvərɪˈstrɪkʃən/
n.|过度限制，过度约束|The act of restricting something too much.  It implies an excessive limitation that can be harmful or counterproductive.|His diet seemed like overrestriction to me – he barely ate anything.|近义词：limitation, constraint, confinement|反义词:freedom, liberation, permissiveness|词性:名词
overrestrict:/ˌoʊvərɪˈstrɪkt/
v.|过度限制，过度约束|To restrict something too much, to impose excessive limitations.|They overrestricted their child's access to the internet, which made him resentful.|近义词：confine, limit, curb|反义词:free, liberate, loosen|词性:动词
overrestrictive:/ˌoʊvərɪˈstrɪktɪv/
adj.|过度限制的，过度约束的|Characterized by excessive restriction.|The school's dress code was considered overrestrictive by many students.|近义词：limiting, constraining, confining|反义词:liberal, permissive, freeing|词性:形容词
