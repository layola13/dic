clearer:/ˈklɪrər/ (US), /ˈklɪərə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更清晰的，更清楚的|Comparative form of "clear."  Means more easily seen, heard, or understood.|The picture is clearer now that I've cleaned the screen.  现在我把屏幕擦干净了，图片更清晰了。|Synonyms: more distinct, more obvious, more transparent, more lucid|Antonyms: more obscure, more vague, more ambiguous, murkier|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *clerer*, comparative of *cler* (“bright, shining, clear”), from Old French *cler* (compare modern French *clair*), from Latin *clārus* (“clear, bright, loud”).
