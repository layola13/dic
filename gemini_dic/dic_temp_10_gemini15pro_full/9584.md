antisocialities:/ˌæntiˌsoʊʃiˈælɪtiz/
n.
反社会行为（复数）
Plural form of antisociality, referring to multiple instances or types of behavior that are harmful or disruptive to society.
His antisocialities, including vandalism and theft, led to his expulsion from school. 他的反社会行为，包括故意破坏和偷窃，导致他被学校开除。
近义词：deviances, misconducts
反义词：prosocial behaviors
词性：名词


antisociality:/ˌæntiˌsoʊʃiˈælɪti/
n.
反社会性
Behavior that is harmful or disruptive to society.
His antisociality made it difficult for him to maintain relationships. 他的反社会性使他难以维持人际关系。
近义词：unsociability, misanthropy
反义词：sociability
词性：名词
