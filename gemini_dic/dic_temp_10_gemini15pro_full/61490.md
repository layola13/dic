embroider:/ɪmˈbrɔɪdər/ /ɛmˈbrɔɪdər/| v. | 刺绣，修饰，渲染| To decorate fabric with needlework; to embellish a story or account. |She embroidered a beautiful design onto the pillowcase. 她把一个漂亮的设计绣在枕套上。

He embroidered his tales of adventure with fanciful details. 他用奇特的细节渲染了他冒险的故事。|近义词：decorate, adorn, embellish, stitch |反义词: simplify, strip down| verb
