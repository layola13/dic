crossflow:/ˈkrɒsfləʊ/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsfloʊ/ (US)| n. adj.|横流，交叉流，错流|The flow of a fluid, such as a liquid or gas, across another fluid or surface, often at a right angle.|
The crossflow in the heat exchanger maximizes efficiency.  热交换器中的横流使效率最大化。

The crossflow prevented the two liquids from mixing thoroughly.  横流阻止了两种液体充分混合。


|近义词：transverse flow, countercurrent flow|反义词:parallel flow, cocurrent flow|词性:名词，形容词|

crossflow:/ˈkrɒsfləʊ/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsfloʊ/ (US)| adj.|横流的，交叉流的|Relating to or characterized by a crossflow.|
A crossflow filtration system was used to separate the particles.  使用了一种错流过滤系统来分离颗粒。

The crossflow design of the ventilation system ensured even distribution of air.  通风系统的横流设计确保了空气的均匀分布。


|近义词：transverse, countercurrent|反义词:parallel, cocurrent|词性:形容词|
