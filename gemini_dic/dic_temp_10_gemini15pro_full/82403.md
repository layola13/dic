grudgeful: /ˈɡrʌdʒfʊl/ | adj. | 怨恨的，吝啬的 | Feeling or showing resentment or ill will because of a past insult or injury; unwilling to give or share something. | He gave a grudgeful look at the other driver who had cut him off. 他怨恨地看了一眼超车的司机。 |  She was grudgeful about sharing her toys. 她不愿意分享她的玩具。| 近义词: resentful, begrudging, envious, unwilling | 反义词: forgiving, generous, magnanimous | 词性: 形容词

词源: From grudge + -ful.
