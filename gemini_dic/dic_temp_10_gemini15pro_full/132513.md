nondetergent:/ˌnɑːndɪˈtɜːrdʒənt/
adjective
非清洁剂的；非去垢剂的
Not containing detergent; not having the properties of a detergent.
This nondetergent soap is gentle on sensitive skin.  这种非清洁剂的肥皂对敏感肌肤很温和。
近义词:soapless, detergent-free
反义词:detergent
词性:adjective
