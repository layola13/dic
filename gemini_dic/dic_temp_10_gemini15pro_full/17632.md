becalm:/bɪˈkɑːm/
verb
使平静；使停滞
To make calm or still; to deprive of wind
The ship was becalmed in the doldrums. 这艘船在赤道无风带一动不动。
近义词: soothe, pacify, quiet, still
反义词: agitate, excite, rouse, stir
词性:v.

Etymology:
From be- + calm.  The prefix "be-" adds intensive force or has a causative meaning (making something into a certain state).
