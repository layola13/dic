glasshouses:/ˈɡlɑːsˌhaʊzɪz/ | n. | 温室；玻璃房 |A structure with walls and roof made chiefly of glass, used for growing plants that need warmth and protection. |  People who live in glasshouses shouldn't throw stones.  住在玻璃房子里的人不应该扔石头。| Synonyms: greenhouse, conservatory, hothouse | Antonyms: open field | noun
