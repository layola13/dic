castoreum:/kæsˈtɔːriəm/ | n. | 海狸香 | A yellowish-brown, unctuous substance with a strong odor, secreted by beavers. It is used in perfumery as a fixative. |  Beavers use castoreum to mark their territory. 海狸用海狸香来标记它们的领地。 |  Perfume makers prize castoreum for its fixative properties. 香水制造商珍视海狸香的定香特性。 | synonyms: beaver scent, beaver musk | antonyms: N/A | noun
