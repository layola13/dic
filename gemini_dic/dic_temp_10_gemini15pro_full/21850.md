bodice-rippers:/ˈbɒdɪs ˌrɪpəz/| n.|情色浪漫小说（尤指历史背景，包含强迫性情节）|A genre of historical romance novels characterized by melodramatic plots featuring a vulnerable heroine and a powerful, often brooding hero.  These stories often involve abduction, forced seduction, and other sensational elements.|例句：She devoured bodice-rippers all summer. 她整个夏天都在读情色浪漫小说。

例句：Bodice-rippers are often criticized for their romanticized portrayal of non-consensual relationships. 情色浪漫小说经常因其对非自愿关系的浪漫化描述而受到批评。|近义词：historical romance, romance novel|反义词:literary fiction|词性:名词
