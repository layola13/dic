exclusionary:/ɪkˈskluːʒənəri/
adjective
排斥的；排他的；专属的
Tending to exclude or shut out others, especially from a society or group.
例句：The club's exclusionary membership policies have been criticized.  这个俱乐部的排他性会员政策受到了批评。

例句：Exclusionary zoning practices can make it difficult for low-income families to find housing. 排斥性的分区规划使得低收入家庭很难找到住房。


近义词：restrictive, exclusive, discriminatory
反义词：inclusive, open, welcoming
词性: adjective
