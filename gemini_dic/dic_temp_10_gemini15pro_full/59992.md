editions:/ɪˈdɪʃənz/
n.
版本；版次
A particular form or version of a published text.
This is the first edition of the book. 这是这本书的第一版。

近义词:versions, prints, issues
反义词:None (Editions implies multiple versions, so a direct opposite doesn't exist)
词性:Noun
