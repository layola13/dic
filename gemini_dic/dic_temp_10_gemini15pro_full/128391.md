new lease on life:/nuː liːs ɑːn laɪf/ /nuː liːs ɑːn laɪf/
noun phrase
新生/重获新生
A chance to start again or to live more fully, as if one's life had begun anew.  A fresh start; a revitalization.
After his surgery, he felt like he had a new lease on life.  手术后，他感觉自己重获新生。
synonyms: fresh start, second chance, renaissance, revival, rebirth
antonyms: decline, deterioration, end of life, demise
