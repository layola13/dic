do-it-up-brown:/ˌduː ɪt ˈʌp ˈbraʊn/
verb phrase
做得彻底；做得出色；做得过火
To do something thoroughly, completely, and often excessively or ostentatiously.
He really did it up brown for his wife's birthday party – hired a band, catered food, and decorated the whole house.  他为他妻子的生日派对做得非常出色——雇了一个乐队，提供餐饮，并装饰了整个房子。
The decorators really did the house up brown for Christmas.  装潢师们把房子装饰得非常华丽来迎接圣诞节。
近义词: go all out, pull out all the stops, go to town
反义词: do a half-baked job, cut corners
词性: verb phrase
