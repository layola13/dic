contritely:/kənˈtraɪtli/ | adv. | 悔恨地，懊悔地 | Showing or expressing remorse; feeling or showing sorrow and regret for having done wrong. | He apologized contritely for his mistake.  他懊悔地为自己的错误道歉。|  apologetically, remorsefully, penitently | unapologetically, impenitently | adverb

contrite:/kənˈtraɪt/ | adj. | 悔恨的，懊悔的 | Feeling or expressing remorse; feeling or showing sorrow and regret for having done wrong. | She had a contrite expression on her face. 她脸上带着悔恨的表情。| remorseful, penitent, repentant | unrepentant, impenitent | adjective

词源:
From Late Middle English *contrit, from Old French contrit, from Latin contrītus, past participle of conterere (“to grind, bruise, rub together; wear away; weaken; destroy”), from com- (“together”) + terere (“to rub”).
