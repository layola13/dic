elaidic:/ˌɛleɪˈɪdɪk/| adj.| 反油酸的，反式十八烯酸的|Of or relating to elaidic acid, a trans isomer of oleic acid.|Elaidic acid is a trans unsaturated fatty acid. 反油酸是一种反式不饱和脂肪酸。|The elaidic form is more stable than the oleic form. 反式构型比顺式构型更稳定。|Synonym: trans-oleic|Antonym: oleic|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From elaidin + -ic. Elaidin from French élaïdine, from Greek elaia olive + -in.
