acropetal: /əˈkrɒpɪtəl/
adjective
向顶的，向基部渐狭的
Developing or maturing from the base toward the apex or tip, as in a flower cluster.  The development of tissues or organs from the base towards the apex.
The plant exhibits acropetal flowering.  这种植物展现出向顶开花的特性。
Acropetal leaf development is common in ferns. 蕨类植物的向顶叶发育很常见。
近义词: basifugal
反义词: basipetal
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek akros (topmost, outermost) + Latin petere (to seek, go towards).

USAGE->
Acropetal maturation is a key characteristic of this plant. =  这种植物的关键特征是向顶成熟。

The acropetal development of the flower spike is clearly visible. = 花穗的向顶发育清晰可见。
