flagrantly:/ˈfleɪɡrəntli/
adverb
公然地；明目张胆地
in a way that is so obvious that it is shocking
He flagrantly disregarded the rules. 他公然无视规则。
flagrantly violated the law  公然违反法律
The judge was flagrantly biased. 法官明显带有偏见。
Synonyms: openly, blatantly, conspicuously, notoriously, brazenly
Antonyms: secretly, discreetly, subtly, covertly
词性: 副词
