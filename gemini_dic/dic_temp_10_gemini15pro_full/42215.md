counselable:/ˈkaʊnsələbəl/
adjective
可劝导的，可接受劝告的
Capable of being counseled; open to or receptive of advice or guidance.
She was in a counselable state of mind, willing to listen to reason. 她心态平和，愿意听取意见。
Synonyms: receptive, advisable, open-minded, willing to listen
Antonyms: unreceptive, closed-minded, intractable, stubborn
词性: 形容词

counsel:/ˈkaʊnsəl/
noun, verb
劝告，忠告；律师
(n.) Advice or guidance, especially that given formally. (v.) To give advice or guidance to.
The lawyer offered his client counsel. 律师向他的客户提供咨询。
He counseled the young man to stay in school. 他劝告那个年轻人继续上学。
Synonyms: (n.) advice, guidance, recommendation; (v.) advise, guide, recommend
Antonyms: (v.) misguide, misdirect
词性: 名词, 动词
