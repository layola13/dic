grounded:/ˈɡraʊndɪd/| adj.|接地的；有理由的；被禁足的；沉稳的；务实的|Based on facts or reality; sensible and practical.  Also:  (of a person) not allowed to go out as a punishment; (of a ship or aircraft) unable to fly or sail because of a problem.|例句：He's very grounded, even though he's so successful. 即使他如此成功，他仍然很务实。|近义词：realistic, practical, sensible, down-to-earth, stable|反义词：unrealistic, impractical, flighty, unstable|词性:形容词

grounded:/ˈɡraʊndɪd/| v.|使接地；使搁浅；惩罚…不准外出；基于；根据|past tense and past participle of "ground," meaning to connect (an electrical device) to the ground; to prevent (a ship or aircraft) from flying or sailing; to punish (especially a child) by not allowing them to go out.|例句：The airline grounded all its flights because of the storm. 由于暴风雨，航空公司停飞了所有航班。|近义词：restrict, confine, base, establish|反义词：release, free|词性:动词
