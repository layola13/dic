busboy:/ˈbʌsbɔɪ/| n.|餐馆勤杂工，打杂工，收拾餐桌的人|A busboy is a person who works in a restaurant, clearing tables, setting tables, and doing other similar tasks. |The busboy quickly cleared the dirty dishes from our table. 餐馆勤杂工迅速地清理了我们桌上的脏盘子。

The busboy refilled our water glasses. 餐馆勤杂工给我们添满了水。|近义词：busser, dining room attendant|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps restaurant manager or chef as they oversee the busboy)|noun
