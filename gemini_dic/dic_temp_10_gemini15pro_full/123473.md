monostich: /ˈmɒnəstɪk/ /ˈmɑːnəstɪk/ | n. | 单行诗; 一行诗 | A poem consisting of a single line. |  Example: "The moon is a silver coin in the velvet sky." 月亮是天鹅绒天空中的银币。

Example:  "Hope is the thing with feathers." 希望是长着羽毛的东西。| 近义词: single line, one-liner | 反义词:  poem, couplet, tercet, quatrain, etc. (any poem with more than one line) | 词性: noun
