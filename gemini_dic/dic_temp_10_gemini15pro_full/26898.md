calaboose:/ˌkæləˈbuːs/| n.|监狱，拘留所|A jail, especially a small local jail.|The outlaw was thrown in the calaboose for the night.  歹徒被关进了拘留所过夜。|Synonyms: jail, lockup, prison, hoosegow|Antonyms: freedom, liberty|noun
