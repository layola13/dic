bismuthic:/bɪzˈmʌθɪk/
adjective
铋的；五价铋的
Containing or relating to bismuth, especially with a valency of five.
Bismuthic oxide is a compound of bismuth and oxygen. 氧化铋是铋和氧的化合物。
Bismuthic sulfide is a brownish-black compound. 硫化铋是一种棕黑色的化合物。
Near Synonyms: bismuth, bismuthous
Antonyms: None
词性: adjective
