frumper:/ˈfrʌmpər/| n.| 过时的老古董（尤指衣着老旧的女人）,古板的人|A person, especially a woman, who wears old-fashioned clothes or is considered stuffy or dowdy.|例句：She was seen as a frumper by her more fashionable colleagues.  她被她更时尚的同事视为老古董。

例句：He's such a frumper, always complaining about the modern world. 他真是个老顽固，总是抱怨现代世界。|近义词：dowdy, old-fashioned, fuddy-duddy, prig, prude|反义词:trendy, stylish, fashionable, modern|词性:名词
