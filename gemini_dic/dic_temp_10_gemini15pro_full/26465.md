byways:/ˈbaɪweɪz/
n.
小路；僻径；冷门学科
A byway is a small road, especially in a rural area, or an area of study that is not commonly known or pursued.
We drove along winding byways through the countryside. 我们沿着乡间蜿蜒的小路行驶。
He was an expert in the byways of medieval history. 他是中世纪历史冷门学科的专家。
synonyms: back roads, side roads, lanes, paths, tracks, secluded spots, out-of-the-way places, lesser-known areas, obscure subjects, unusual fields
antonyms: highways, main roads, popular subjects, mainstream fields
noun
