noble:/ˈnoʊbl/ (US) /ˈnəʊbl/ (UK)| adj. n.|高贵的，贵族的；高尚的；宏伟的|Of high social rank, especially by descent; aristocratic.  Having or showing fine personal qualities or moral principles.  Impressive in size or appearance.|例句：He came from a noble family. 他出身贵族。  She made a noble sacrifice for her country. 她为了国家做出了崇高的牺牲。|近义词：aristocratic, dignified, honorable, grand, stately|反义词：base, ignoble, common, vulgar|词性:形容词, 名词
