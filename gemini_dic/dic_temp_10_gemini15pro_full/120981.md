mischoose:/ˌmɪsˈtʃuːz/
verb
错误地选择；选错
to choose badly or wrongly
He mischose his career and ended up unhappy. 他选错了职业，最终很不快乐。
近义词: select poorly, choose wrongly, make a bad choice, make a wrong selection
反义词: choose wisely, select correctly
词性: verb

词源:
From mis- + choose.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Old English and Germanic origin meaning "wrong," "bad," or "wrongly." "Choose" comes from Old English *cēosan, of Germanic origin.
