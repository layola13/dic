dampen:/ˈdæmpən/ (US), /ˈdæmpən/ (UK)| v. |使潮湿；抑制；减弱；使沮丧|To make something slightly wet. To make something less strong or intense.|The rain dampened the ground. 雨水打湿了地面。|synonyms: moisten, dampen, stifle, repress, curb, subdue|antonyms: dry, encourage, intensify, strengthen|verb

dampened:/ˈdæmpənd/ (US), /ˈdæmpənd/ (UK)| adj. |潮湿的；沮丧的|Made slightly wet. Made less strong or intense.|Her dampened spirits made it hard to enjoy the party. 她沮丧的情绪让她很难享受派对。|synonyms: moist, damp, depressed, dejected|antonyms: dry, cheerful, elated|adjective
