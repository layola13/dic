astrocyte: /ˈæstrəˌsaɪt/ | n. | 星形胶质细胞 | A star-shaped glial cell in the brain and spinal cord that provides support for neurons, regulates the extracellular environment, and contributes to the blood-brain barrier. |  Astrocytes play a vital role in maintaining the health of the central nervous system. 星形胶质细胞在维持中枢神经系统的健康方面起着至关重要的作用。|  Recent research suggests that astrocytes may also play a role in information processing. 最新研究表明，星形胶质细胞也可能在信息处理中发挥作用。 | synonyms: glial cell, astroglia | antonyms: neuron (although not a strict opposite, it highlights a functional distinction) | noun
