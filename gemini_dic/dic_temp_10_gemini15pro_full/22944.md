bottleneck:/ˈbɑːtlˌnek/ (US) /ˈbɒtlnek/ (UK)| n.  |瓶颈，障碍，窄路，高翻领毛衣|A bottleneck is a point of congestion or blockage in a process, system, or flow, causing delays or preventing smooth progress.  It can also refer to a type of high-necked sweater.|The traffic bottleneck on the highway caused a long delay.  高速公路上的交通瓶颈造成了长时间的延误。|synonyms: obstruction, hindrance, impediment, constriction, snag, problem, difficulty|antonyms: solution, advantage, aid, opening, expansion|noun

bottleneck:/ˈbɑːtlˌnek/ (US) /ˈbɒtlnek/ (UK)| v. |造成瓶颈，阻碍|To bottleneck something means to cause a blockage or delay in its progress.|Construction work is bottlenecking traffic in the city center.  市中心的建筑工程造成了交通瓶颈。|synonyms: obstruct, hinder, impede, restrict, hamper|antonyms: expedite, facilitate, accelerate, aid|verb
