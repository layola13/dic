observership:/əbˈzɜːrvərʃɪp/ | n. | 见习;观摩;实习 | A period of time spent observing the work of a specialist or organization, typically in a hospital, for professional development. |  She completed a three-month observership in pediatric cardiology. 她完成了为期三个月的小儿心脏病学见习。|  He gained valuable experience during his observership at the renowned hospital. 他在著名医院的见习期间获得了宝贵的经验。| internship, apprenticeship, clerkship, practicum |  - | noun
