cambodian:/kæmˈboʊdiən/ /kæmˈbəʊdiən/ | adj. n.|柬埔寨的；柬埔寨人；柬埔寨语|Relating to Cambodia, its people, or their language.  A native or inhabitant of Cambodia; the Khmer language.|例句：She is a Cambodian citizen. 她是一位柬埔寨公民。

例句：Cambodian cuisine is known for its fresh flavors. 柬埔寨菜以其新鲜的味道而闻名。|近义词：Khmer|反义词: N/A|词性:形容词, 名词
Etymology: From French *Cambodgien*, from *Cambodge* "Cambodia", ultimately from Sanskrit *Kambujadeśa* "land of the Kambujas".
