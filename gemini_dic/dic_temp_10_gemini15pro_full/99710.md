interloted:/ɪntərˈloʊtɪd/ | adj. |  插入的，干预的 |  Inserted or introduced from another source; interfering.  Derived from "interlot," an obsolete word meaning "to mix different kinds of things."  While "interlot" itself is no longer used, the past participle form "interloted" occasionally appears, often in legal or historical contexts. | The manuscript contained several interloted pages from a different text.  这份手稿包含几页来自不同文本的插入页。 |  The interloted clause caused confusion in the contract's interpretation. 这条插入的条款导致了对合同解释的混淆。| synonyms: inserted, interpolated, intruded, interfering | antonyms: original, inherent, intrinsic | adjective
