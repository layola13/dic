coguardian:/koʊˈɡɑːrdiən/| n. |共同监护人|A person who shares the legal responsibility for the care and upbringing of a child, typically with another adult.|例句：Both parents were listed as coguardians of the child.  父母双方都被列为孩子的共同监护人。

例句：The coguardians disagreed on the child's education. 共同监护人在孩子的教育问题上意见不一致。|近义词：joint guardian, shared guardian|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
