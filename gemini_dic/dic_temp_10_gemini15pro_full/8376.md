anti-racism:/ˈæntiˈreɪsɪzəm/
n.
反种族主义
The active opposition to racism and racial discrimination.
Many organizations are dedicated to promoting anti-racism. 许多组织致力于推广反种族主义。
Colleges are now incorporating anti-racism into their curriculum.  大学现在正将反种族主义纳入他们的课程。
近义词：racial equality, racial justice
反义词：racism, racial discrimination
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix) + racism (noun)

"anti-" comes from Greek and means "against" or "opposite".

"racism" originated in the early 20th century from "racist" + "-ism". "Racist" comes from French "raciste", ultimately from Italian "razza" (race). "-ism" is a suffix forming nouns of action, practice, or condition.
