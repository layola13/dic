occupiers:/ˈɒkjʊpaɪəz/ (UK) /ˈɑːkjʊpaɪərz/ (US)
n.
占用者，居住者，占领者
Those who occupy a space, building, or territory.
The occupiers of the apartment were asked to evacuate.  公寓的居住者被要求撤离。
The occupiers of the disputed territory refused to leave.  争议领土的占领者拒绝离开。
Synonyms: residents, inhabitants, tenants, possessors
Antonyms: evacuees, refugees
