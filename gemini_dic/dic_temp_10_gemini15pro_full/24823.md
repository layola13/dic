browdyng: /ˈbraʊdɪŋ/
n.| 刺绣; 华丽的衣服 |Embroidery; richly ornamented clothing, particularly in the context of medieval or historical settings.|The queen's browdyng was exquisite, woven with gold and silver thread. 女王的刺绣精美绝伦，用金银线织成。|Synonyms: embroidery, needlework, embellishment, ornamentation|Antonyms: plainness, simplicity|noun

Etymology: From Middle English *browdynge*, from *browden*, to embroider, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old Norse *broddr*, a sharp point, suggesting the use of a needle.
