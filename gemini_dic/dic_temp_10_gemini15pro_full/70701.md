financeable:/faɪˈnænsəbl/ /fɪˈnænsəbl/
adjective
可融资的，可筹资的
Able to be financed; capable of being funded.
The project is financeable, provided we can secure a loan.  这个项目是可融资的，只要我们能获得贷款。
The new car is financeable through several different lenders. 这辆新车可以通过几家不同的贷款机构融资。
近义词:fundable, bankable
反义词:unfinanceable
词性:adjective
