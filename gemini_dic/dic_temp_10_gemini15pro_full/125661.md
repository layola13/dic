mumpish:/ˈmʌmpɪʃ/| adj.|闷闷不乐的，愠怒的|Sullen and ill-tempered; sulky.|He's been mumpish all day since he lost his favorite toy.  他自从丢了他最喜欢的玩具后，一整天都闷闷不乐的。|例句：She gave me a mumpish look. 她没好气地瞪了我一眼。|近义词：sulky, grumpy, sullen, morose|反义词：cheerful, happy, jovial|词性:形容词
Etymology: From the verb "mump," meaning "to grimace or sulk," possibly of imitative origin.
