abkhazia:/æbˈkeɪʒə/ /æbˈkɑːziə/
n.

A partially recognized state on the eastern coast of the Black Sea, internationally considered to be part of Georgia.  It declared independence from Georgia in 1992 following a war.
The government of Abkhazia is largely unrecognized by the international community. = 阿布哈兹政府在很大程度上未得到国际社会的承认。

Abkhazia has a subtropical climate. = 阿布哈兹属于亚热带气候。
