hyperdrive:/ˈhaɪpərˌdraɪv/| n.|超光速引擎；超速运转|A fictional propulsion system that allows spacecraft to travel faster than light.|The Millennium Falcon made the Kessel Run in less than twelve parsecs thanks to its hyperdrive. 千年隼号凭借其超光速引擎在不到十二秒差距的时间内完成了 Kessel Run。|近义词：warp drive, faster-than-light travel|反义词:sub-light speed|词性:noun

Etymology:  "Hyper-" (from Greek "hyper," meaning "over, beyond") + "drive" (from Old English "drīfan," meaning "to push, compel").  The term gained popularity through science fiction, notably Star Wars.
