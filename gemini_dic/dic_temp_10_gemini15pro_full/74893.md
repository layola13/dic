refreshing:/rɪˈfrɛʃɪŋ/ | adj. |清爽的，提神的，令人耳目一新的 | Making one feel less tired or hot; pleasantly new or different. |例句：A refreshing breeze blew in from the sea. 一阵清爽的海风吹来。


例句：It's refreshing to hear some good news. 听到一些好消息真是令人耳目一新。


近义词：invigorating, stimulating, reviving, bracing |反义词：tiring, exhausting, stale |词性：形容词


词源：From Middle English refreshen, from Anglo-Norman refrescher, Old French refreschir, from re- + freis (“fresh”).
