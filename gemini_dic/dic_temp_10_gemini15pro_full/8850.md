antifashions:/ˌæntiˈfæʃənz/
|n.|非主流时尚；反时尚| Styles and trends that deliberately reject or oppose mainstream fashion, often unconventional or provocative. |  They embraced antifashions like ripped jeans and oversized sweaters. 他们喜欢像破牛仔裤和超大号毛衣这样的反时尚。|Near synonyms: counter-fashion, alternative fashion |Antonyms: mainstream fashion, high fashion|noun
