ornate:/ɔːrˈneɪt/
adjective
华丽的，装饰的
elaborately or excessively decorated
The palace gates were ornate, covered in gold leaf and intricate carvings. 宫殿的大门装饰华丽，覆盖着金箔和复杂的雕刻。
ornate:/ɔːrˈneɪt/
adjective
（文辞）华丽的，夸张的
(of literary style) using unusual words and complex constructions
His writing style is too ornate for my taste; I prefer simpler language.  他的写作风格对我来说过于华丽; 我更喜欢简单的语言。

近义词:elaborate, decorated, embellished, flamboyant, ostentatious
反义词:plain, simple, austere, unadorned
词性:adjective
