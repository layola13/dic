excruciating:/ɪkˈskruːʃieɪtɪŋ/| adj.|极度的，极大的|Used to emphasize the intensity or degree of something unpleasant.|The meeting was excruciatingly long and boring. 会议冗长乏味，令人难以忍受。|Synonyms: intensely, extremely, exceedingly|Antonyms: mildly, slightly, moderately|adjective
