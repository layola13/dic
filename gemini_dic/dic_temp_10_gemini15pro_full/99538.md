intergossipped:/ˌɪntərˈɡɑːsɪpt/ | v. | 交头接耳；互相八卦 | To gossip amongst each other; to spread rumors or talk about private matters between individuals within a group. | The women intergossipped while waiting for the meeting to start. 女士们在等待会议开始时互相八卦。|  The students intergossipped about the upcoming exam. 学生们互相八卦即将到来的考试。 | 近义词: whispered, chatted, gossiped | 反义词: kept silent, refrained from speaking | 词性: 动词

词源:  "inter-" (between) + "gossip" (idle talk)
