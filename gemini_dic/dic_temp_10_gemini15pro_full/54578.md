dismal:/ˈdɪzməl/ | adj. | 阴沉的；凄凉的；忧郁的；令人沮丧的 | causing gloom or depression; dreary; cheerless; depressing | The weather was dismal, with heavy rain and gray skies. 天气阴沉，下着大雨，天空灰蒙蒙的。|  The economic forecast is dismal. 经济预测令人沮丧。 | gloomy, dreary, bleak, depressing, somber | cheerful, bright, sunny, optimistic | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *dismal, from dis- (prefix indicating separation, negation) + mal (evil, bad), ultimately from Latin malus (bad, evil).  The original sense was "evil days," referring to unlucky days in medieval calendars.
