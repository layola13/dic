disworship:/dɪsˈwɜːrʃɪp/
|v. |不崇拜；不再崇拜；亵渎|To refuse to worship; to treat with disrespect or contempt something considered sacred.|He disworshipped the gods of his ancestors. 他不再崇拜他祖先的神灵。|近义词:profane, desecrate, blaspheme|反义词:worship, revere, venerate|词性:动词


disworship:/dɪsˈwɜːrʃɪp/
|n. |不崇拜；亵渎|The act of refusing to worship; the act of treating with disrespect or contempt something considered sacred.|His disworship of the traditional values shocked the community. 他对传统价值观的亵渎震惊了社区。|近义词:profanation, desecration, blasphemy|反义词:worship, reverence, veneration|词性:名词
