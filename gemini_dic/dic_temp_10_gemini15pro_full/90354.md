hovel:/ˈhɒvəl/ (British), /ˈhʌvəl/ (American)| n. |小屋，棚屋；肮脏简陋的住所|A small, mean dwelling; a wretched hut. |They lived in a hovel with a dirt floor. 他们住在一个泥土地板的小屋里。

The refugees were forced to live in hovels. 难民被迫住在棚屋里。

近义词：hut, shack, shanty, slum, cabin|反义词: mansion, palace|词性:名词
