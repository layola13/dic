outbellow:/ˌaʊtˈbɛloʊ/| v.|大声吼叫，盖过…的声音|To shout louder than someone or something else, so that their voice cannot be heard.|The angry captain outbellowed the storm, giving orders to his crew. 愤怒的船长大声吼叫，盖过了风暴的声音，向他的船员发出命令。|近义词：outshout, drown out, overpower|反义词:whisper, murmur|词性:动词

词源:  From "out-" (meaning exceeding or surpassing) + "bellow" (meaning to roar).
