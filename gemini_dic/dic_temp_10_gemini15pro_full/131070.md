nonbaryonic:/ˌnɒnˈbæriɒnɪk/ | adj. | 非重子的 | Not composed of baryons (protons and neutrons).  Relating to matter that is not composed of baryons. | Dark matter is thought to be primarily nonbaryonic. 暗物质被认为主要是由非重子物质构成的。 |  Nonbaryonic dark matter candidates include neutrinos and hypothetical particles like axions and WIMPs. 非重子暗物质候选者包括中微子以及像轴子和弱相互作用重粒子这样的假设粒子。 | non-hadronic | baryonic | adjective
