dramas:/ˈdrɑːməz/ /ˈdræməz/
n.
戏剧，剧本；戏剧性事件；戏剧艺术
plays, especially serious plays, written for performance on stage, television, or radio; exciting or emotional events or situations
She has starred in several TV dramas. 她主演过几部电视剧。
近义词: plays, shows, performances, productions
反义词: comedies (specifically for comedic dramas)
词性: noun (plural)
