astraddle:/əˈstræd(ə)l/
adv. prep.|跨着，骑着，两腿分开着|With a leg on each side; extending across or over.|例句：She sat astraddle the motorcycle. 她跨坐在摩托车上。|近义词：astride, across|反义词：beneath, below
词性：副词，介词

astraddle:/əˈstræd(ə)l/
adj.|跨着的，两腿分开着的|Extending across or on both sides of something.|例句：The town is astraddle the border.  这座小镇横跨边境。|近义词：spanning, straddling|反义词：contained, within
词性：形容词

词源: From a- + straddle, from Middle English *straddle, from Old English *stridan "to stride", of Germanic origin.
