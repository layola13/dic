antisubversive:/ˌæntiˈsəbvɜːrsɪv/
adjective
反颠覆的；防止颠覆的
Opposing or intended to prevent subversion (the undermining or overthrowing of a government or other institution).
The government implemented antisubversive measures to protect national security. 政府实施了反颠覆措施以保护国家安全。
The antisubversive legislation was criticized for limiting free speech.  反颠覆立法因限制言论自由而受到批评。
近义词: counter-subversive, anti-revolutionary
反义词: subversive, revolutionary
词性: adjective


antisubversive:/ˌæntiˈsəbvɜːrsɪv/
noun
反颠覆分子；反颠覆措施
A person or measure intended to prevent subversion.
He was labeled an antisubversive for his outspoken views. 他因直言不讳的观点而被贴上反颠覆分子的标签。
The agency developed new antisubversives to combat terrorism.  该机构开发了新的反颠覆措施来打击恐怖主义。
近义词: counterrevolutionary
反义词: revolutionary, subversive
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + subversive.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to." "Subversive" comes from the Latin "subvertere" meaning "to overturn" or "to overthrow."
