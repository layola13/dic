palimpsest:/ˈpælɪmpsɛst/| n. | 重写本，羊皮纸手稿，(历史的)痕迹|Something reused or altered but still bearing visible traces of its earlier form.  A manuscript or piece of writing material on which the original writing has been effaced to make room for later writing but of which traces remain.|The city is a palimpsest of different cultures and historical periods. 这座城市是不同文化和历史时期的重写本。|Synonyms: manuscript, parchment, tablet, record, document |Antonyms: blank slate, tabula rasa|noun
