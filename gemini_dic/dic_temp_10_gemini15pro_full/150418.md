pansies:/ˈpænziːz/
n.
三色堇；胆小鬼，懦夫
A small garden plant with brightly coloured flowers.  Also a derogatory term for a weak or effeminate man.
The pansies in her garden were a riot of colour. 她花园里的三色堇五彩缤纷。
He called the other boy a pansy for refusing to fight. 他因为另一个男孩拒绝打架而叫他胆小鬼。
近义词：violet, viola (for the flower); coward, wimp (for the derogatory term)
反义词：brave, courageous (for the derogatory term)
词性: noun


Etymology: From Middle French *pensées*, plural of *pensée* "thought," from *penser* "to think."  The name refers to the flower's resemblance to a pensive face.
