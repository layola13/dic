belated:/bɪˈleɪtɪd/| adj.|迟来的，晚到的|Coming or happening later than should have been the case; delayed.|例句：A belated birthday greeting. 迟来的生日祝福。|近义词：delayed, overdue, late, tardy|反义词：early, timely, prompt|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *bellatus, from bellus "beautiful, fine" + -atus (past participle suffix). The original meaning was "made fine, improved," later shifting to "delayed." The connection might be through the idea of taking extra time to perfect something.
