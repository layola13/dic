defuse:/dɪˈfjuːz/ /diːˈfjuːz/| vt.|拆除(炸弹的)引信；缓和，平息|To remove the fuse from (an explosive device) and thus render it harmless; to make a situation less tense or dangerous. |The police were able to defuse the bomb before it exploded. 警察在炸弹爆炸前成功拆除了引信。

The mayor's calming words helped to defuse the tense situation. 市长安慰的话语帮助缓和了紧张的局势。|近义词：deactivate, neutralize, disarm, alleviate, mitigate|反义词：activate, escalate, aggravate|词性: verb
