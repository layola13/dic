apetalous:/əˈpɛtələs/
adjective
无瓣的；无花瓣的
Lacking petals; having no petals.
The apetalous flowers are pollinated by the wind. 这些无瓣的花朵靠风授粉。
synonyms:petaless
antonyms:petalous
adjective
