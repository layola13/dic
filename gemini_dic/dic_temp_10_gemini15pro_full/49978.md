词源:
From French *serge de Nîmes (“serge from Nîmes”), referring to the sturdy twill fabric originally made in Nîmes, France.  The term later shortened to "denim."  来自法语 *serge de Nîmes（“尼姆的斜纹布”），指的是最初在法国尼姆制造的坚固斜纹布。该术语后来缩写为“牛仔布”。
