hypermetrical:/ˌhaɪpərˈmetrɪkl/
adjective
超韵律的
Exceeding the requirements of regular meter; having an extra syllable or syllables.  Relating to or composed in hypermeter.
This poem employs a hypermetrical line for emphasis. 这首诗运用超韵律的诗行来强调。
The use of hypermetrical syllables creates a sense of irregularity. 超韵律音节的使用营造了一种不规则感。
近义词: irregular, uneven
反义词: regular, metrical
词性: adjective
