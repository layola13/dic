nondeciduously:/ˌnɒndɪˈsɪdʒuəsli/
adjective
非落叶地
not deciduous; evergreen.  Remaining attached to the tree or shrub throughout the year; not falling off seasonally.
The pine trees stand nondeciduously, providing green throughout the winter. 松树常青，整个冬天都保持绿色。
近义词: evergreen, persistent
反义词: deciduous, caducous
词性: adjective
