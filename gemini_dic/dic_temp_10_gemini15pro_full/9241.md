antipasto:/ˌæntiˈpɑːstoʊ/ (US), /ˌæntiˈpæstoʊ/ (UK)| n.|开胃菜，前菜|An appetizer typically consisting of an assortment of meats, cheeses, fish, vegetables, and olives.  It is derived from the Italian word "antipasto," which literally translates to "before the meal."|We started our meal with a delicious antipasto of cured meats and cheeses. 我们以美味的腌肉和奶酪开胃菜开始了我们的用餐。|synonyms: appetizer, starter, hors d'oeuvre|antonyms: dessert, after-dinner drink|noun
