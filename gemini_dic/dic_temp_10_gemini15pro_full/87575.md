heteroplastic:/ˌhɛtərəˈplæstɪk/
adjective
异质成形的，异种移植的
Formed from tissue of a different individual of the same or a different species.
Example: Heteroplastic transplantation of bone marrow can be used to treat certain blood disorders.  异种骨髓移植可以用来治疗某些血液疾病。
Example: Scientists are exploring the potential of heteroplastic grafts in regenerative medicine. 科学家们正在探索异塑性移植在再生医学中的潜力。

近义词: allogeneic, xenogeneic
反义词: autoplastic, isoplastic
词性: adjective
