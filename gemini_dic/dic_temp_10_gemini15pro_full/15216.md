backdropped:/ˈbækdrɒpt/ /ˈbækdrɑːpt/| v. |作为背景|To provide a backdrop for something means to be the setting or background against which it happens or is seen.|The mountains backdropped the serene valley. 群山是宁静山谷的背景。|近义词：background, set against, behind|反义词:foreground|词性:动词

backdropped:/ˈbækdrɒpt/ /ˈbækdrɑːpt/| adj. |以...为背景的|Having something as a background.|The backdropped stage created a dramatic effect.  以...为背景的舞台营造了戏剧性的效果。|近义词：backgrounded|反义词:foregrounded|词性:形容词
