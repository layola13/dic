nonsigners:/ˌnɒnˈsaɪnərz/ | n. | 未签署者，未签约者 | People who have not signed a particular document or agreement. | Example: The nonsigners of the petition were not included in the final count.  未签署请愿书的人未被计入最终人数。| Example: The nonsigners of the treaty were not bound by its terms. 未签署该条约的国家不受其条款约束。 | Synonyms: objectors, dissenters, abstainers | Antonyms: signatories, subscribers | Noun
