humbling:/ˈhʌmblɪŋ/| adj.|令人谦卑的；使人感到卑微的|Making you feel less proud or important.|例句：It was a humbling experience to meet the survivors of the earthquake.  见到地震的幸存者是一次令人谦卑的经历。

例句：Losing the competition was a humbling experience. 输掉比赛是一次令人羞愧的经历。


近义词：humiliating, chastening, humbling, mortifying|反义词:exalting, elevating|词性:形容词
