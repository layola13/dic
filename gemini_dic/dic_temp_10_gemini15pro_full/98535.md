instigation:/ˌɪnstɪˈɡeɪʃn/| n.|煽动，怂恿，教唆|The riot was started at the instigation of a small group of agitators. 这场骚乱是在一小撮煽动者的唆使下开始的。|例句：The investigation revealed that the crime was committed at the instigation of a rival gang. 调查显示，这起犯罪是在敌对帮派的唆使下犯下的。

例句：She joined the protest at the instigation of her friends.  她在朋友的鼓动下参加了抗议活动。


近义词：incitement, encouragement, goading, prompting|反义词:dissuasion, deterrence, prevention|词性:名词
