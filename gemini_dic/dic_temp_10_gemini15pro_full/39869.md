constellate:/ˈkɒnstəleɪt/ (UK), /ˈkɑːnstəleɪt/ (US)| v. |聚集，形成星座；点缀|To form into a constellation or cluster; to gather or assemble together.  To adorn with stars or other bright objects.|The stars constellated in the night sky. 星星在夜空中形成了星座。|The ceiling was constellated with tiny lights. 天花板上点缀着点点灯光。|Synonyms: cluster, gather, assemble, congregate|Antonyms: disperse, scatter|verb

constellate:/ˈkɒnstəleɪt/ (UK), /ˈkɑːnstəleɪt/ (US)| adj. |(罕见)布满星星的，像星座的|Resembling a constellation; studded with stars.|The constellate sky was breathtaking. 布满星星的天空令人叹为观止。 (This usage is less common.) |Synonyms: starry, star-studded|Antonyms: dark, dim|adjective
