loyalism:/ˈlɔɪəlɪzəm/ | n. | 忠诚；忠义；效忠 |  The steadfast devotion and allegiance to a person, government, or cause.  |  Their loyalism to the crown was unwavering. 他们对王室的忠诚坚定不移。 | 近义词：allegiance, fidelity, devotion | 反义词：treachery, disloyalty, betrayal | 词性: 名词

词源:  Late 18th century: from loyal + -ism.
