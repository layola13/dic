chaetopoda:/ˌkiːtəˈpoʊdə/ /ˌkiːtəˈpɒdə/| n. |环节动物门|A phylum of invertebrate animals commonly called segmented worms, including earthworms, leeches, and marine worms. They are characterized by a segmented body, bristles called chaetae, and a well-developed coelom.|例句：Earthworms are a common example of chaetopoda. 蚯蚓是环节动物门的一个常见例子。

例句：Chaetopoda play a vital role in soil health. 环节动物在土壤健康中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：Annelida|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
