denationalize:/diːˈnæʃənəlaɪz/ |v.|  去国有化；使失去国籍 | The government plans to denationalize several key industries. 政府计划将几个关键产业私有化。|  Many people were denationalized during the war. 许多人在战争期间被剥夺了国籍。| privatize, divest | nationalize | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "reverse" or "remove") + nationalization (the process of bringing industries or businesses under state control).  The word "nationalization" itself comes from "national," ultimately derived from the Latin "natio" (nation, race, stock).
