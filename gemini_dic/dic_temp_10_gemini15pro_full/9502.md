antiseptic:/ˌæntiˈsɛptɪk/| adj. n.|防腐的，抗菌的；防腐剂，抗菌剂|Preventing the growth of disease-causing microorganisms.|例句：She cleaned the wound with an antiseptic solution. 她用防腐溶液清洗了伤口。

例句：The antiseptic properties of garlic are well known. 大蒜的防腐特性是众所周知的。|近义词：disinfectant, germicidal, antimicrobial|反义词：septic, infected|词性:adjective, noun

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek ἀντί (anti, “against”) + σηπτικός (sēptikos, “putrefactive”).
