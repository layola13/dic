helminthology:/ˌhɛlmɪnˈθɒlədʒi/ /ˌhɛlmɪnˈθɑːlədʒi/| n. |蠕虫学；寄生虫学|The study of parasitic worms (helminths).|Helminthology is a crucial field for understanding and combating parasitic infections in both humans and animals.|近义词:parasitology|反义词: None|词性:noun

etymology->
From Ancient Greek ἕλμινς (hélmins, “worm”) + -λογία (-logía, “study of”).
