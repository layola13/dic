betterer:/ˈbɛtərər/ | adj. | 更好的（非标准用法）| A nonstandard or humorous comparative form of "better."  While grammatically incorrect, it is sometimes used for emphasis or comedic effect. | Example: My cake is way betterer than yours! 我的蛋糕比你的好多了！（幽默用法）| Example:  He thinks he's betterer than everyone else. 他认为自己比其他所有人都好。（非标准用法）| 近义词: better, superior, finer | 反义词: worse, inferior | 词性: adjective

词源: "betterer" is a double comparative formed by adding the "-er" suffix twice to the adjective "good."  The correct comparative form is "better," and the superlative form is "best."  "Betterer" is considered nonstandard and is generally avoided in formal writing or speech.
