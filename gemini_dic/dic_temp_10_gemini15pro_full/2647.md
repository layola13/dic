adorableness:/əˈdɔːrəblənəs/
noun
可爱;讨人喜欢
The quality of being adorable; extreme cuteness or charm.
The puppy's adorableness was undeniable.  这只小狗的可爱程度是不可否认的。

The kitten's adorableness made me want to take it home.  这只小猫的可爱让我很想把它带回家。

近义词:charm, sweetness, cuteness, loveliness
反义词:unattractiveness, ugliness, hideousness
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From adorable + -ness.  Adorable comes from the French word "adorer" (to adore) + the suffix "-able".  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.

USAGE->
The adorableness of the baby made everyone smile. =  婴儿的可爱让每个人都笑了。
She couldn't resist the adorableness of the puppies. = 她无法抗拒小狗们的可爱。
