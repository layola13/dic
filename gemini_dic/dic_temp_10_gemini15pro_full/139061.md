oasis:/oʊˈeɪsɪs/ /əʊˈeɪsɪs/| n. |绿洲；舒适的地方；(困境中的)令人宽慰的事物|A fertile spot in a desert where water is found.|例句：The oasis offered a welcome respite from the scorching desert sun.  绿洲提供了躲避炎炎沙漠阳光的舒适场所。

例句：The library was an oasis of calm in the bustling city.  图书馆是喧嚣城市中一片宁静的绿洲。|近义词：haven, refuge, sanctuary, retreat|反义词：desert, wasteland|词性: 名词
