hmd:/eɪtʃ em diː/ |abbr.| 头戴式显示器（Head-Mounted Display）| A head-mounted display (HMD) is a display device, worn on the head or as part of a helmet, that has a small display optic in front of one (monocular HMD) or each eye (binocular HMD). |  I just bought a new VR hmd. 我刚买了一个新的VR头戴式显示器。 | 近义词: VR headset, AR headset | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | noun
