instigator:/ˈɪnstɪɡeɪtər/| n. |煽动者，教唆者，发起人|A person who initiates or encourages a course of action, especially one that is undesirable or illicit. |例句：The police are still searching for the instigators of the riot. 警方仍在搜寻骚乱的煽动者。

例句：She was the instigator of the whole affair. 她就是整件事的始作俑者。

近义词：agitator, provoker, inciter, rabble-rouser |反义词: peacemaker, mediator, conciliator |词性: noun


词源：From Late Latin instigator, from Latin instigare "to incite," from in- "in" + stigare "to prick, goad."
