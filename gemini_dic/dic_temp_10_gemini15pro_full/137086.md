nonstudious:/ˌnɒnˈstjuːdiəs/ /ˌnɑːnˈstuːdiəs/| adj. |不爱学习的，不用功的|Not diligent in studies; not studious.  Lacking interest in or devotion to study.| He was a bright but nonstudious child. 他是个聪明但不用功的孩子。|Synonyms: indolent, lazy, slothful, idle, unschoolish|Antonyms: studious, diligent, scholarly, academic|adjective
