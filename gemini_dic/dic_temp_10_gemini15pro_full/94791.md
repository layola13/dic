impugn: /ɪmˈpjuːn/ | v. | 质疑，攻击，抨击 | To challenge the truth, validity, or honesty of (a statement or motive); to call into question. |  The lawyer impugned the witness's testimony. 律师质疑证人的证词。

The politician's integrity was impugned by his opponents.  这位政治家的正直被他的对手抨击了。 | 近义词: challenge, question, dispute, attack, criticize | 反义词: support, defend, uphold, endorse | 词性: 动词
