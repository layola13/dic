bishopess:/ˈbɪʃəpɪs/
n.
主教夫人；女主教
A female bishop, or the wife of a bishop.
The bishopess presided over the ceremony.  主教夫人主持了仪式。
The new bishopess was known for her compassion. 新任女主教以她的同情心而闻名。
synonyms:  wife of a bishop, female bishop (in some denominations)
antonyms:  (No direct antonym.  Could consider bishop (male) in some contexts, but not a true opposite.)
