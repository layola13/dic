culvert: /ˈkʌlvərt/ (US), /ˈkʌlvət/ (UK)| n.|涵洞，排水管，暗渠|A transverse drain under a road or railway.|The culvert beneath the road diverted the floodwaters. 路下的涵洞分流了洪水。|Synonyms: conduit, channel, drain, duct, pipe|Antonyms: bridge, overpass|noun
