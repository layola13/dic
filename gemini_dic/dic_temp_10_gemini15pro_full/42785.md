coupe:/kuːp/ | n. | 双门轿跑车 | A closed, two-door car, often sporty in style. |  例句：He just bought a sleek new coupe. 他刚买了一辆时尚的新款双门轿跑车。| 近义词: sports car, two-door | 反义词: sedan, four-door | noun

coupes:/kuːps/ | n. | 双门轿跑车（复数） | Plural form of "coupe," referring to multiple two-door cars. | 例句：The car show featured a variety of coupes. 车展上展出了各种各样的双门轿跑车。 | 近义词: sports cars, two-doors | 反义词: sedans, four-doors | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *coupé*, past participle of *couper* "to cut," from Latin *colare* "to filter, strain," later "to cut, prune, lop."  Originally referred to a horse-drawn carriage with the front section cut away.
