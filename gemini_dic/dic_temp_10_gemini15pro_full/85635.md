haunching:/hɔːntʃɪŋ/
verb
蹲伏; 蜷缩
The act of crouching or squatting, especially with the haunches bent.
The cat was haunching in the corner, watching the mouse. 猫蹲在角落里，注视着老鼠。
crouching, squatting, hunkering
standing, stretching
v.
