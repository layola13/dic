ophiuran:/ˌɒfiˈjʊərən/ | n. | 蛇尾纲动物；海蛇尾 | Any of various echinoderms of the class Ophiuroidea, having a body with a central disk and five slender, flexible arms. |  例句：The ophiuran quickly retreated under the rock when touched.  海蛇尾被触碰后迅速缩回岩石下。

例句：Ophiurans are closely related to starfish. 海蛇尾和海星是近亲。 | 近义词：brittle star, serpent star | 反义词:  (无明显的反义词，可以考虑其他纲的棘皮动物，例如海胆 sea urchin) | 词性: noun
