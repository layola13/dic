hoity-toity:/ˈhɔɪti ˈtɔɪti/| adj. n.|傲慢的，自命不凡的；傲慢自大的人|Someone who is hoity-toity behaves in a way that shows they think they are very important and better than other people.  It can also be used as a noun to refer to such a person.|例句：She gave me a hoity-toity look. 她傲慢地看了我一眼。

例句：He's become all hoity-toity since he got promoted. 自从升职后，他就变得非常傲慢自大。

近义词：snobbish, arrogant, haughty, supercilious, pompous|反义词:humble, modest, down-to-earth|词性:adjective, noun
