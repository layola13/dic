hearses:/ˈhɜːrsɪz/ /ˈhɝːsiz/| n. |灵车；丧葬车|A vehicle for conveying a coffin to a church or cemetery.|The hearse slowly made its way to the cemetery.|近义词：funeral car, casket coach|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Middle English *herse*, from Old French *herce* "harrow," referring to a triangular frame used to hold candles at funerals. Later, the term was applied to the vehicle carrying the coffin.
