analysing (analyzing - 美式拼写) /ˈænəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
分析；分析中
The process of examining something methodically and in detail in order to explain and interpret it.
They are analysing the data from the experiment. 他们正在分析实验数据。
The government is analysing the causes of the recent economic downturn. 政府正在分析最近经济衰退的原因。
近义词: examining, investigating, studying, interpreting
反义词: synthesizing, combining
