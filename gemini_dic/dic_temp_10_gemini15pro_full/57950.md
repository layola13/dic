drip-fed:/ˈdrɪpˈfɛd/ | adj. | 滴注式的，少量多次提供的 |  Given in small amounts over a long period of time, often to prolong the effect or create a sense of anticipation. | The information was drip-fed to the public over several months.  这些信息在几个月内被一点一点地透露给公众。|  gradual, piecemeal, incremental | sudden, all at once | adjective


drip-fed:/ˈdrɪpˈfɛd/ | v. (past participle) |  (be drip-fed something) 被少量多次地提供 |  To be given something in small amounts over a long period of time. |  We were drip-fed information about the project. 我们被一点一点地告知了有关项目的信息。 | supplied gradually,  meted out |  given all at once, delivered completely | verb (past participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
"drip" (v.) + "fed" (past participle of "feed").  "Drip" refers to the slow falling of liquid drop by drop, and "feed" refers to supplying something, especially food.  The combined meaning refers to something being supplied in small, slow amounts like dripping liquid.
