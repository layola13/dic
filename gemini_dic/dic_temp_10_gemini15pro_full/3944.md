agrobiotec:/ˌæɡroʊbaɪoʊˈtɛk/ | n. | 农业生物技术 | The application of biotechnology to agricultural processes. This includes things like genetic engineering of crops, development of biopesticides, and use of microorganisms to improve soil health. |  Agrobiotec has revolutionized farming practices. 农业生物技术彻底改变了耕作方式。| synonyms: agricultural biotechnology, agrotech, agribiotechnology | antonyms: traditional farming | noun
