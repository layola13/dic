assorted:/əˈsɔːrtɪd/ /əˈsɔːr.t̬ɪd/| adj.|各种各样的；混杂的；组合的|Consisting of various types mixed together.|例句：The box contained an assorted collection of chocolates. 盒子里装着各种各样的巧克力。|例句：She wore an assorted collection of jewelry. 她佩戴着各种各样的珠宝。|近义词：various, diverse, miscellaneous, mixed|反义词：uniform, homogeneous, identical|词性:形容词

词源: From the verb "assort," from Old French "assortir" (to match, sort), from "as-" (to) + "sort" (kind, species).  Ultimately from Latin "sors" (lot, fate).
