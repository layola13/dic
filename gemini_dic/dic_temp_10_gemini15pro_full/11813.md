arraught:/əˈrɔːt/| adj.|发狂的，心烦意乱的|Being in a state of mental distress, typically as a result of worry or suffering.|例句：She was distraught with worry when her son didn't come home. 当她的儿子没回家时，她担心得发狂。|近义词：distressed, frantic, agitated, beside oneself|反义词：calm, composed, serene|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Middle English *araughte, *arawhte, from Old English *ārǣht, past participle of ārēcan (“to afflict, trouble, oppress”), from ā- (“on, onto, in, into, to, up”) + rēcan (“to reach, stretch out, extend”).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *raikijaną (“to reach, stretch”).
