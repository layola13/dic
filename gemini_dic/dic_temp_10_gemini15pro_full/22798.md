boskage:/ˈbɒskɪdʒ/ (US) /ˈbɑːskɪdʒ/| n.|灌木丛，树丛，|A mass of trees and shrubs; woods; a thicket.|We walked through the dense boskage, hoping to find a path. 我们穿过茂密的灌木丛，希望能找到一条路。|The deer disappeared into the boskage. 鹿消失在树丛中。|近义词：thicket, undergrowth, underbrush, shrubbery, copse, woodland|反义词：clearing, meadow, grassland|词性:noun

boskage:| n.|灌木丛，树丛，|From Middle English *boscage, borrowed from Old French boscage, from Old North French boscage (“wood, grove, thicket”), from Vulgar Latin *boscāticum (“woody place”), from Late Latin boscus (“wood, bush”).Ultimately from Pre-Germanic *bʰoskós (“bush”). Cognate with Italian bosco (“wood”), Spanish and Portuguese bosque (“forest”).| | | |noun
