coreplasty:/ˈkɔːrəˌplæsti/ | n. |核心成形术|Surgical repair of the pupil.  It involves the reconstruction of the pupil, often after trauma or certain eye diseases.  This procedure aims to restore the pupil to a more normal shape and size to improve vision and/or cosmetic appearance. | The patient underwent coreplasty after suffering a penetrating eye injury.  该病人眼球穿透伤后接受了瞳孔成形术。

The ophthalmologist recommended coreplasty to improve the patient's distorted pupil.  眼科医生建议进行瞳孔成形术来改善病人扭曲的瞳孔。 | 近义词: pupilloplasty | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, as it's a surgical procedure) | 词性: noun
