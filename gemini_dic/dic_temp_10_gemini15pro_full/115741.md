marigenous:/məˈrɪdʒɪnəs/
adjective
海洋生的，海产的
Produced in or by the sea; native to the sea.
Marigenous organisms are adapted to saltwater environments. 海洋生物适应咸水环境。
Many commercially important fish are marigenous. 许多重要的商业鱼类都是海生的。
Synonyms: marine, oceanic, pelagic
Antonyms: terrestrial, freshwater
