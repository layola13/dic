demiurge:/ˈdɛmiˌɜːrdʒ/ | n. | 造物主；创造者 |A being responsible for the creation of the universe, in particular one subordinate to the Supreme Being.  A powerful creative force or personality. |  Gnosticism viewed the demiurge as an imperfect creator of the material world.  He was the demiurge of his own successful company, building it from the ground up. | Synonyms: creator, maker, architect, author, founder | Antonyms: destroyer, ruiner | noun

demiurges:/ˈdɛmiˌɜːrdʒɪz/ | n. | 造物主们；创造者们 | Plural form of demiurge. |  Ancient myths often feature demiurges responsible for different aspects of creation. | Synonyms: creators, makers, architects, authors, founders | Antonyms: destroyers, ruiners | noun
