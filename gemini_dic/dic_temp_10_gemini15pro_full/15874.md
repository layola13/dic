ballistician:/ˌbælɪˈstɪʃən/ | n. | 弹道学家；弹道专家 | An expert in ballistics, the science of projectiles and firearms. |  The ballistician examined the bullet to determine the type of gun used. 弹道学家检查了子弹以确定所使用的枪支类型。

The police consulted a ballistician to help with the murder investigation. 警方咨询了一位弹道专家来协助谋杀案的调查。 | 近义词: firearms expert, ballistics expert | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *ballisticien*, from *ballistique* ("ballistics") + *-ien* (suffix forming agent nouns).  *Ballistique* is from Greek *βαλλιστικός (ballistikós)*, from *βάλλω (bállō)* meaning "to throw".
