epenthetic:/ˌɛpənˈθɛtɪk/| adj.|插音的，附加的|Relating to or characterized by epenthesis, the insertion of a sound or letter within a word.|The "b" in "thimble" is an epenthetic consonant.|近义词：inserted, intruded, added|反义词：deleted, omitted|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Greek epenthesis "insertion," from epentithenai "to put in," from epi "in addition" + en "in" + tithenai "to put."
