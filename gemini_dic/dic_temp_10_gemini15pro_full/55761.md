diversifiability:/dɪˌvɜːrsɪfaɪəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 多样化能力，可分散化 | The quality of being able to be diversified; the capability of something being diversified or varied. |  A portfolio with high diversifiability can reduce risk. 一个具有高度多样化能力的投资组合可以降低风险。 | synonyms: diversifiableness, variability, variety | antonyms: uniformity, homogeneity | noun

diversify:/dɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪ/ | v. | 使多样化，分散 | To make or become more diverse or varied; to extend (business activities) into new areas. | The company decided to diversify its product line. 公司决定使其产品线多样化。| synonyms: vary, expand, broaden, branch out | antonyms: concentrate, focus, specialize | verb
