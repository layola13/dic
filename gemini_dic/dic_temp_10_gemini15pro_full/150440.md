pantagraph:/ˈpæn.tə.ɡræf/ | n. | 缩放仪，比例缩放器，受电弓 | An apparatus, consisting of four jointed bars, used to copy a drawing or plan on a different scale or to transmit motion. It is also used on electric trains to collect power from overhead wires. | The artist used a pantagraph to enlarge the sketch for the mural. 艺术家用缩放仪放大草图以绘制壁画。 The train's pantagraph made contact with the overhead wire. 火车的受电弓与架空电线接触。 | Synonyms: pantograph, scale rule | Antonyms: N/A | noun
