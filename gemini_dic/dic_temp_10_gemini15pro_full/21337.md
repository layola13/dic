bloomerism:/ˈbluːmərɪzəm/| n. |女式灯笼裤; 女式灯笼裤运动;  (妇女争取平等权利的)早期女权运动 |A style of women's clothing consisting of loose trousers gathered at the ankles, worn under a short skirt. Also refers to the movement advocating for this style of dress as part of women's rights in the mid-19th century. | In the 1850s, bloomerism was seen as a radical statement for women's rights.  在19世纪50年代，穿灯笼裤被视为争取妇女权利的激进宣言。

Bloomerism quickly became a symbol of women's suffrage and dress reform.  灯笼裤迅速成为妇女选举权和服装改革的象征。 |近义词: women's rights movement, dress reform |反义词: traditional dress, conservatism |词性: noun
