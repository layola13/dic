emphatical:/ɪmˈfætɪkəl/| adj.|强调的，着重的|Done or expressed in a way that gives emphasis.  Characterized by emphasis; forceful and definite.|She was emphatic that she had not seen him. 她强调说她没有见过他。|近义词：forceful, assertive, insistent, decided, firm, positive, definite, resounding, unequivocal|反义词：hesitant, tentative, irresolute|adjective
