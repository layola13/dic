navigation-weirs:/ˌnævɪˈɡeɪʃən wɪəz/
|n.| 导航堰,通航堰|A low dam built across a river to raise the water level upstream, making the river deeper and easier for boats to navigate.|Navigation weirs ensure a minimum depth for boats to pass.|近义词:navigation dams, lock and dam|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "rapids" or "shallow")|noun
