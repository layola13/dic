groove:/ɡruːv/| n. |沟，槽；最佳状态；惯例|A long, narrow cut or depression in a surface.  A settled routine. A state of being comfortable, natural, or at one's best.|The needle followed the groove of the record.唱针沿着唱片的凹槽移动。|近义词：furrow, channel, rut, routine, habit|反义词：ridge, peak|noun

groove:/ɡruːv/| v. |开槽于；形成沟槽|To make a groove in something.|He grooved the wood for the inlay. 他在木头上刻出凹槽以便镶嵌。|近义词：furrow, channel, gouge|反义词：smooth|verb
