lloyds:/lɔɪdz/ | n. | 劳合社；劳埃德船级社 |  A British insurance market and provider of specialist insurance and reinsurance. It is also known for Lloyd's Register, a classification society for ships.  | Lloyd's of London is a major player in the global insurance industry. 劳合社是全球保险业的主要参与者。 | Lloyd's Register certified the seaworthiness of the vessel. 劳埃德船级社证明了这艘船的适航性。| synonyms: insurance market,  classification society | antonyms: N/A | noun
