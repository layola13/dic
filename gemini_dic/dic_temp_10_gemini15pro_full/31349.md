characterizable:/ˈkærɪktəraɪzəbl/ | adj. | 可描述的，可刻画的 | Able to be described or characterized; capable of being depicted or distinguished by a particular feature. |  The behavior is characterizable as aggressive.  这种行为可以被描述为侵略性的。|Synonyms: describable, definable, typifiable, classifiable | Antonyms: indefinable, indescribable, vague, ambiguous | word property: adjective

characterizable:/ˈkærɪktəraɪzəbl/ | adj. | 可表征的，可特征化的 | Able to be distinguished or identified by specific characteristics. | The plant is characterizable by its large, fragrant flowers.  这种植物的特点是它的大而芬芳的花朵。 | Synonyms: distinctive, typical, representative | Antonyms: atypical, nondescript, generic | word property: adjective
