astronomically:/ˌæstrəˈnɒmɪkli/
adverb
天文数字地；极其巨大地
Relating to astronomy or to the vastness of space; extremely large.
The cost of the project has risen astronomically.  这个项目的成本已经天文数字般地上升了。
The universe is astronomically large. 宇宙浩瀚无垠。
近义词: immensely, enormously, hugely, vastly
反义词: minutely, slightly, insignificantly
词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From astronomy + -ical + -ly.  Astronomy from Greek *astronomia* "science of the stars," from *astron* "star" + *nomos* "arrangement, law."
