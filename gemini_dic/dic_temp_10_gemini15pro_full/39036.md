confliction: /kənˈflɪkʃən/
n.
冲突，矛盾
a state of disagreement or argument between people, groups, countries, etc.
例句：There was a confliction of interest between the two companies.  两家公司之间存在利益冲突。

例句：The confliction between her work and family life caused her a lot of stress. 工作和家庭生活之间的冲突给她带来了很大的压力。

近义词：conflict, clash, disagreement, discord, friction
反义词：agreement, harmony, concord, accord
词性: 名词
