overseed:/ˌoʊvərˈsiːd/
verb
播撒过量种子; 补播
to sow seed thickly or to sow (an area) again with seed.
例句：
The lawn was overseeded in the fall to improve its density. 秋天的时候，草坪上播撒了过量的种子以增加密度。

I decided to overseed my lawn with ryegrass to keep it green during the winter. 我决定在我的草坪上补播黑麦草，以使其在冬天保持绿色。

近义词: resow, reseed
反义词: None

词性: verb
