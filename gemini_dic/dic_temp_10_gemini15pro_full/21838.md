bodess: /ˈbɒdəs/ (UK) /ˈbɑːdəs/ (US)| n. |女神; 女菩萨|A female deity or powerful female figure, often with spiritual or supernatural connotations.  Derived from "bodhisattva," a term in Buddhism referring to someone on the path to enlightenment.  While "bodhisattva" is typically gender-neutral, "bodess" explicitly denotes a female figure. |She was hailed as a bodess of the silver screen. 她被誉为银幕女神。|近义词：goddess, deity, diva, icon|反义词:mortal, human|词性:名词
