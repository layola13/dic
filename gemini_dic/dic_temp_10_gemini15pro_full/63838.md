epigeous:/ɛˈpɪdʒiəs/| adj.|地上生的；表生的|Growing or occurring above ground; not subterranean. |The epigeous germination of a bean is easily observed. 豆子的地上萌发很容易观察到。|Epigeal, aboveground|Subterranean, hypogeous|adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἐπίγειος (epígeios, “on the earth”), from ἐπί (epí, “on, upon”) + γῆ (gê, “earth, land”).
