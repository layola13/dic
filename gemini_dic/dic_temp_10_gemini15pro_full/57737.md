dreads:/drɛdz/| n.|恐惧，害怕；令人恐惧的事物；脏辫|A strong feeling of fear or apprehension.|She had dreads of failing her exams. 她很害怕考试不及格。|Synonyms: fears, anxieties, apprehensions|Antonyms: hopes, confidence, calmness|noun

dread:/drɛd/| v.|害怕，恐惧；担心|To feel extremely worried or frightened about something that is going to happen or that might happen.|He dreaded going to the dentist. 他很害怕去看牙医。|Synonyms: fear, be afraid of, apprehend|Antonyms: look forward to, anticipate with pleasure|verb

dread:/drɛd/| adj.|可怕的，令人恐惧的；令人敬畏的|Causing fear or dread; extremely bad or serious.|The dreaded moment had finally arrived.  可怕的时刻终于到来了。|Synonyms: terrible, awful, frightful|Antonyms: wonderful, pleasant, delightful|adjective
