generatrix:/ˈdʒɛnəreɪtrɪks/| n.|母线; 生成线|A straight line that generates a surface by moving according to a given rule.  A point, line, or plane whose motion generates a line, surface, or solid.|The generatrix of a cone is a straight line.|近义词：generator, generating line|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From Latin *generatrix*, feminine form of *generator*, from *generāre* "to beget, produce".
