hanap:/ˈhɑːnɑːp/| n.|寻找，搜索 (Tagalog)|A Tagalog word meaning "search" or "look for." It describes the act of seeking or finding something.|Example:  Naghahanap ako ng trabaho. (I am looking for a job.)

Example:  Hanap ka ng ibang solusyon. (Look for a different solution.)|近义词：seek, search, look for|反义词:ignore, neglect|词性:noun (Tagalog)
