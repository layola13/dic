elicitations:/ɪˌlɪsɪˈteɪʃənz/
|n.|启发；引出；探询|The elicitation of requirements is crucial in software development.  启发需求在软件开发中至关重要。|The teacher's skillful elicitations encouraged students to participate actively. 老师巧妙的启发鼓励学生积极参与。|
|近义词：promptings, evocations, solicitations|
|反义词：suppressions, concealments|
|词性：名词|


elicit:/ɪˈlɪsɪt/
|v.|引出；探出；诱出|The police tried to elicit a confession from the suspect. 警察试图从嫌疑人那里诱导出供词。|The comedian's jokes elicited laughter from the audience.  喜剧演员的笑话引来了观众的笑声。|
|近义词：evoke, draw out, obtain|
|反义词：suppress, conceal, hide|
|词性：动词|
