paltriest:/ˈpɔːltriɪst/ | adj. | 最微不足道的，最不重要的 | Of the smallest amount or value; insignificant. | He offered the paltriest of excuses for his behavior. 他为自己的行为提出了最微不足道的借口。|  The reward for such hard work was paltriest.  对于如此辛苦的工作，回报却是微不足道的。 |  insignificant, negligible, trifling, petty, measly | substantial, significant, considerable, important | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *paltriste* (unattested), from Old French *palte*, *paultet* (“piece of old cloth, rag”), from Latin *pallium* (“cloak, coverlet”).  The sense development is from "made of rags" to "worthless."
