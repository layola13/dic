eloquent:/ˈɛləkwənt/ | adj. | 雄辩的；有口才的；流利的 | fluent or persuasive in speaking or writing |  She delivered an eloquent speech that moved the entire audience. 她发表了一场感动了全场观众的雄辩演讲。 | synonyms: articulate, expressive, fluent, persuasive | antonyms: inarticulate, tongue-tied | adjective

eloquence:/ˈɛləkwəns/ | n. | 雄辩；口才；流畅 | fluent or persuasive speaking or writing |  The eloquence of his prose captivated readers. 他散文中的雄辩深深吸引了读者。| synonyms:  articulateness, expressiveness, fluency, persuasiveness | antonyms: inarticulateness | noun
