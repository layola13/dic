intendancy:/ɪnˈtɛndənsi/| n. |监督；管理；管辖区域|The position or territory over which an intendant has authority.  Originally a French administrative district.  Later adopted in other countries, often with modified meanings. |The intendant had complete intendancy over the province.| 近义词：administration, management, supervision, jurisdiction, governance | 反义词:  lack of control, disorder, anarchy | 词性: noun

Etymology: From French *intendance*, from *intendant* "one who intends," from Latin *intendere* "to stretch out, turn one's attention to".
