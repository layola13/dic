colinear:/ˌkoʊˈlɪniər/| adj.|共线的，同线的|Lying on the same straight line.|例句：The points A, B, and C are collinear.  A、B、C三点共线。|例句：These three stars appear collinear to the naked eye.  这三颗星肉眼看起来是在一条直线上。|近义词：aligned, linear|反义词:noncollinear|词性:adjective
co-linear:/ˌkoʊˈlɪniər/| adj.|共线的，同线的|Formed by combining co- meaning "together" + linear "pertaining to a line".|例句：The points A, B, and C are collinear.  A、B、C三点共线。|例句：These three stars appear collinear to the naked eye.  这三颗星肉眼看起来是在一条直线上。|近义词：aligned, linear|反义词:noncollinear|词性:adjective
