epipetalous:/ˌɛpɪˈpɛtələs/| adj.|花瓣生的，附生于花瓣上的|Having petals attached to the petals, or, of stamens, attached to the petals.  The stamens arise from the petals.|The primrose has epipetalous stamens. 报春花具有附生于花瓣上的雄蕊。|Synonyms: petaliferous|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps hypogynous or epigynous depending on the specific contrasting feature)|adjective
