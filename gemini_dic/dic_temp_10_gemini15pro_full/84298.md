half-vexed: /ˌhɑːf ˈvekst/ | adj. | 半恼怒的，略感恼火的 | Slightly annoyed; irritated, but not intensely so.  A mixture of annoyance and some other emotion, often amusement or affection. | She gave him a half-vexed, half-amused look. 她给了他一个半恼火半好笑的眼神。| Near synonyms:  slightly annoyed, irritated, irked, piqued, miffed | Antonyms: pleased, delighted, content, satisfied | Word Origin:  "half-" + "vexed" (past participle of "vex," from Middle English *vexen*, from Old French *vexer*, from Latin *vexare* "to shake, agitate, trouble") | Adjective
