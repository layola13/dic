de-power:/diːˈpaʊər/
verb
使失去动力; 使丧失权力; 剥夺权力
To deprive of power, authority, or influence.  To make something or someone less powerful.
The new law will de-power local governments.  新法律将削弱地方政府的权力。
The aim is to de-power the central bureaucracy. 目的是削弱中央官僚机构的权力。
synonyms: weaken, disable, disempower, emasculate
antonyms: empower, strengthen, enable
词性: 动词

de-power:/diːˈpaʊər/
noun
丧失权力; 剥夺权力
The act of depriving of power.
The de-power of the monarchy was a gradual process. 君主立宪制的削弱是一个渐进的过程。
The report details the de-power of the trade unions. 该报告详细描述了工会的权力被削弱的情况。
synonyms: weakening, disabling, disempowering
antonyms: empowerment, strengthening, enabling
词性: 名词
