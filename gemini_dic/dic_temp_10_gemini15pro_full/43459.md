crash-test:/ˈkræʃ test/ | adj. n. | 碰撞测试 | A test in which a vehicle is deliberately crashed to assess its safety features. | The new car received a five-star rating in its crash-test. 这款新车在碰撞测试中获得了五星评级。|  |  | adjective/noun


crash-test:/ˈkræʃ test/ |  v. | 碰撞测试 | To subject (a vehicle) to a crash test. | They crash-tested the new model to ensure its safety. 他们对新模型进行了碰撞测试以确保其安全性。| test, evaluate |   | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From "crash" (violent collision) + "test" (trial).  "Crash" originates from imitative origins, likely Scandinavian. "Test" comes from Old French "test" (earthen pot used in assaying metals), ultimately from Latin "testum" (earthen vessel).
