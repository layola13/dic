outstripped:/ˌaʊtˈstrɪpt/ | v. | 超过，胜过 |  To surpass or exceed, especially in performance or achievement.  |  The new model of the car has outstripped all its rivals in sales. 这款新车型在销量上超过了所有竞争对手。 | Examples: The athlete outstripped his competitors in the final race.  这名运动员在决赛中超过了他的竞争对手。  | Synonyms: surpassed, exceeded, outdone, overtaken, eclipsed | Antonyms: lagged behind, trailed, fell short of | verb
