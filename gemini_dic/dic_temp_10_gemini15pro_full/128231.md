neurodivergent:/ˌnʊərəʊdaɪˈvɜːdʒənt/ /ˌnʊroʊdaɪˈvɜːrdʒənt/
adjective
神经多样性，神经分歧的
Differing in mental or neurological function from what is considered typical or normal.  Relating to a person whose brain functions in ways that are different from what is considered typical.
Example:  Many neurodivergent individuals find traditional classroom settings challenging. 许多神经多样性的人觉得传统的课堂环境具有挑战性。

Example: The school offers support programs tailored to the needs of neurodivergent students. 学校提供针对神经多样性学生需求的支援计划。

近义词: atypical, unconventional
反义词: neurotypical, typical
词性: adjective
