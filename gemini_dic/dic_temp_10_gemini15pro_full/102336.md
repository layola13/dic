ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English _hwæt_, neuter singular nominative and accusative of _hwā_  ("who", "what"), from Proto-Germanic *_hwat_, from Proto-Indo-European *_kwod_. Cognate with Scots _what_, West Frisian _wat_, Dutch _wat_, Low German _wat_, German _was_, Danish _hvad_, Swedish _vad_, Icelandic _hvað_, Gothic _ƕa_. Related also to who.
