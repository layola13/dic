mouldery:/ˈmoʊldəri/ (US), /ˈməʊldəri/ (UK)| adj.|发霉的，霉烂的|Covered with or smelling of mould; musty.|The bread was mouldery and had to be thrown away. 面包发霉了，只好扔掉。|近义词:moldy, musty, mildewy|反义词:fresh, clean|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mowldri*, from Old English *molde* (dust, mould) + *-ig*.  Cognate with German *muldig* (mouldy).
