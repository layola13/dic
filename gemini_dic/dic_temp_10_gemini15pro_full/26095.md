businesslike:/ˈbɪznəsˌlaɪk/| adj.|高效率的；有条理的；公事公办的|Efficient and practical, like a person who is good at business.|She adopted a businesslike tone on the phone. 她在电话里采取了公事公办的语气。|近义词：efficient, practical, professional, organized, methodical|反义词：inefficient, unprofessional, disorganized, casual|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From business + -like.  The suffix "-like" means "resembling" or "characteristic of".  Therefore, "businesslike" means resembling or characteristic of business, specifically in efficiency and practicality.
