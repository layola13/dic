frisked:/frɪskt/ | v. |搜身；快速地翻查 |To search (a person) for concealed weapons or other contraband, usually by running the hands quickly over the clothing. |The police officer frisked the suspect for weapons. 警察搜查了嫌疑犯身上的武器。| The security guard frisked him before he entered the building.  保安在他进入大楼前搜了他的身。| synonyms: search, pat down, check | antonyms: ignore, overlook |verb

frisky:/ˈfrɪski/ | adj. |活泼的；欢闹的|Playful and full of energy. |The frisky puppy ran around the yard. 活泼的小狗在院子里跑来跑去。 | The lambs were frisky in the spring sunshine. 春天的阳光下，小羊羔们欢蹦乱跳。 |synonyms: lively, playful, energetic |antonyms: lethargic, sluggish | adjective
