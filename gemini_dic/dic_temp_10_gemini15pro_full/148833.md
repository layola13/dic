例句：Her overvividly imagined fears kept her awake all night. 她过分鲜明的想象恐惧让她整夜无法入睡。|近义词：intensely, graphically, excessively, dramatically|反义词：dully, faintly, blandly|词性:副词

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "vivid" (adjective meaning "producing powerful feelings or strong, clear images in the mind") + "-ly" (adverb suffix)
