differentness:/ˈdɪfrəntnəs/
n.
差异，不同
the quality or state of being different
The differentness of their cultures made communication difficult.  他们文化上的差异使得沟通很困难。
近义词:dissimilarity, distinctness, divergence, unlikeness, variation
反义词:similarity, likeness, sameness, resemblance
词性:名词

词源:  From different + -ness.  源自 different 加后缀 -ness.
