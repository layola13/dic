asthma: /ˈæzmə/ (US) /ˈæsmə/ (UK)| n. |哮喘|A chronic respiratory condition marked by spasms in the bronchi of the lungs, causing difficulty in breathing. It is usually accompanied by wheezing and coughing and often triggered by such things as allergic reactions or strenuous exercise.|She suffers from severe asthma. 她患有严重的哮喘。| My son's asthma flared up during the pollen season.  我儿子的哮喘在花粉季节发作了。 |近义词: respiratory disease, bronchospasm |反义词: None (Asthma is a medical condition, not a descriptive quality with a direct opposite)|词性: noun
