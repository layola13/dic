handshake: /ˈhændʃeɪk/ | n. | 握手 | The act of grasping and shaking another person's hand as a greeting or sign of agreement. | We sealed the deal with a firm handshake. 我们以一个坚定的握手达成了协议。 |  The two leaders exchanged a warm handshake. 两位领导人热情地握手。 | synonym: greeting, clasp | antonym: refusal, rejection | noun
