mismeasures:/ˌmɪsˈmɛʒərz/| n. |错误的衡量；失策；失误| Actions or decisions based on incorrect assessments or judgments.  A plural form of mismeasure.| The government's economic mismeasures led to a recession. 政府的经济失策导致了经济衰退。|近义词：mistakes, errors, blunders, miscalculations, misjudgments|反义词: successes, achievements, triumphs| noun

mismeasure:/ˌmɪsˈmɛʒər/| v. |错误地衡量；错误地估计| To measure or estimate incorrectly.|  They mismeasured the room and the carpet was too small. 他们把房间量错了，地毯太小了。|近义词：miscalculate, misjudge, underestimate, overestimate|反义词: calculate correctly, judge accurately, measure accurately | verb
