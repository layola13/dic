aerating:/ˈeəreɪtɪŋ/
v.
通气，充气
Introducing air into something.
The soil needs aerating to improve drainage. 土壤需要通气以改善排水。
synonyms: oxygenate, ventilate, carbonate
antonyms: suffocate, stifle
verb
