eighths:/eɪtθs/
|n.|八分之一|One of eight equal parts of a whole.  It represents the fraction 1/8.|Cut the pizza into eighths. 把披萨切成八等份。|Synonym: one-eighth|Antonym: whole, eight|noun

eighths:/eɪtθs/
|n.|八分音符|A musical note with a time value of one eighth of a whole note.|The melody is full of eighths and sixteenths. 这首曲子充满了八分音符和十六分音符。|Synonym: quaver (British)|Antonym: whole note|noun
