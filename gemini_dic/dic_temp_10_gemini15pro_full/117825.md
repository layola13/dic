mellific:/məˈlɪfɪk/| adj.|甜蜜的；产生蜂蜜的；像蜂蜜的|Making or producing honey; flowing with honey; honey-sweet.|The air was mellific with the scent of honeysuckle. 空气中弥漫着甜蜜的金银花香味。|近义词：sweet, honeyed, sugary|反义词：bitter, sour|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *mellificus*, from mel (genitive mellis) "honey" + -ficus "-making," related to facere "to make, do."
