households:/ˈhaʊshəʊldz/ (UK) /ˈhaʊshəʊldz/ (US)| n. |家庭，户|A house and its occupants regarded as a unit.  A group of people, often a family, who live together.| The government sent out a survey to 2,000 households. 政府向2000户家庭发放了调查问卷。| synonyms: families, homes, residences | antonyms: individuals, businesses, institutions | noun
