externality:/ˌɛkstɜːˈnælɪti/ | n. | 外部性，外部效应 |  A side effect or consequence of an industrial or commercial activity that affects other parties without this being reflected in the cost of the goods or services involved. |  Pollution is a negative externality that affects everyone, not just those who produce or consume the polluting goods. 污染是一种负外部性，它影响到每个人，而不仅仅是那些生产或消费污染商品的人。|  The construction of a new airport brought positive externalities such as increased tourism and job creation. 新机场的建设带来了积极的外部效应，例如旅游业的增长和就业机会的创造。 | synonym: spillover effect, side effect, consequence | antonym: internality | noun
