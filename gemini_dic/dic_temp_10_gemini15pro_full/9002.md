antilittering:/ˌæntiˈlɪtərɪŋ/
adjective
反乱扔垃圾的; 防止乱扔垃圾的
Preventing or discouraging littering.
The city council implemented new antilittering measures. 市议会实施了新的反乱扔垃圾措施。
The antilittering campaign was successful in reducing waste. 反乱扔垃圾运动成功减少了垃圾。
近义词:anti-litter,  cleanup
反义词:littering, polluting
词性:adjective
