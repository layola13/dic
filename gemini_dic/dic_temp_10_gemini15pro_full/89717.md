hopefully:/ˈhoʊpfəli/ (US) /ˈhəʊpfʊli/ (UK)| adv.|但愿；抱有希望地；如果一切顺利的话|Used to express what you hope will happen|Hopefully, the weather will be good tomorrow. 但愿明天天气好。|Hopefully, I'll be finished by next week. 如果一切顺利的话，我下周就能完成。|近义词:ideally, with any luck, all being well|反义词:unfortunately, sadly|词性:副词

词源:
From "hopeful" + "-ly."  "Hopeful" comes from "hope" (Old English hopian) + "-ful" (Old English -full).
