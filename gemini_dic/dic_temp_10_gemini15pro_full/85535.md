hatchbacks:/ˈhætʃˌbæks/| n.|掀背车|A car with a sloping back door that opens upwards, often including the rear window.  The back seats can usually be folded down to increase the luggage space.|She traded her sedan for a more practical hatchback. 她把她的轿车换成了一辆更实用的掀背车。|近义词：hatchback cars, five-door cars|反义词：sedans, coupes, convertibles|noun
