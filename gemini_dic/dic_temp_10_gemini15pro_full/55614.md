disvest:/dɪsˈvɛst/ /daɪˈvɛst/| vt. |剥夺，脱去，解除，撤资|To deprive someone of rights, powers, or possessions; to sell off an asset or subsidiary. |The company is divesting its assets to raise capital.  该公司正在剥售资产以筹集资金。|Synonyms: sell off, dispose of, liquidate, relinquish, forfeit, surrender |Antonyms: invest, acquire, obtain, assume|verb


disvest:/dɪsˈvɛst/ /daɪˈvɛst/| n. |撤资|The act of selling off assets or subsidiaries. |The company's divestment of its non-core businesses was a strategic move. 该公司剥离非核心业务是一项战略举措。|Synonyms: divestiture, sale, disposal |Antonyms: investment, acquisition|noun
