paragenesis:/pærəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
n.|共生,伴生|The formation of minerals in association with each other within the same rock or deposit.  The study of the origin and relationships of minerals found together.|例句：The paragenesis of the ore deposit suggests a complex history of hydrothermal activity.  该矿床的共生关系表明其热液活动历史复杂。

例句：Geologists study the paragenesis of rocks to understand their formation. 地质学家研究岩石的共生关系以了解它们的形成过程。|近义词：association, assemblage, coexistence|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
