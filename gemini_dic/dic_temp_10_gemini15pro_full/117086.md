meads:/miːdz/
n.
草地；蜂蜜酒
A mead is a field habitat vegetated by grass and other non-woody plants. Mead can also refer to an alcoholic beverage made from fermented honey and water.
The wildflower meads were buzzing with bees. 野花草地上蜜蜂嗡嗡作响。
He brewed a potent mead. 他酿造了一种浓烈的蜂蜜酒。
近义词：meadow, grassland (for field); honey wine (for beverage)
反义词：(No direct antonym for mead as a field.  For the beverage, perhaps "non-alcoholic drink")
词性: noun
