intercession:/ˌɪntərˈseʃən/ | n. | 祈求；调解；说情；斡旋 | The act of pleading on behalf of another person, especially to a person in authority. |  The teacher's intercession saved the student from expulsion. 老师的求情使得该学生免于被开除。

My intercession with the boss got my colleague a raise. 我替同事向老板求情，为他争取到了加薪。 | 近义词: mediation, intervention, arbitration, plea, appeal | 反义词: indifference, apathy, inaction | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *intercessiōn*, from Latin intercēdō (“I intercede”), from inter (“between”) + cēdō (“I go, yield”).
