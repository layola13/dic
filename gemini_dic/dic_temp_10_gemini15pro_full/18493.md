bend-toward:/bɛnd ˈtɔːrd/| phrasal verb |倾向，偏向| To incline or show a tendency or preference for something or someone. |The company's strategy seems to bend toward innovation.  这家公司的战略似乎偏向创新。| 近义词: incline towards, lean towards, gravitate towards | 反义词: resist, oppose, shy away from |词性: 动词短语


bend:/bɛnd/ | v. | 弯曲；屈服；倾向 |To force something straight into a curved or angular position or to become curved or angular.  |He bent the wire into a circle. 他把铁丝弯成一个圆圈。| 近义词: curve, flex, bow | 反义词: straighten, rigidify |词性: 动词

toward:/tɔːrd/ | prep. | 向，朝；对于；接近 | In the direction of; approaching. |She walked toward the door. 她走向门口。| 近义词: towards, to | 反义词: away from |词性: 介词
