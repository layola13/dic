mongolia:/mɒŋˈɡəʊliə/ (US) /mɑːŋˈɡoʊliə/| n. |蒙古|A landlocked country in East Asia, bordered by Russia to the north and China to the south.  It is known for its vast, rugged expanse of desert, steppes, and mountains.|Example: Mongolia is famous for its nomadic culture. 蒙古以其游牧文化而闻名。|Example:  We travelled across the vast steppes of Mongolia. 我们穿越了蒙古广阔的草原。|Synonym: Outer Mongolia (historical)|Antonym: N/A|noun
