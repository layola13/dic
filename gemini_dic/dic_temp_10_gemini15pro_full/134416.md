noninvincible:/ˌnɒnɪnˈvɪnsɪbl/
adjective
非无敌的，可战胜的
Not invincible; capable of being defeated or overcome.
The Roman army, though powerful, proved noninvincible.  罗马军队虽然强大，但最终证明并非不可战胜。
The champion's noninvincible status was exposed after his shock defeat.  这位冠军在令人震惊的失败后暴露出并非不可战胜。
近义词:defeatable, conquerable, vulnerable, surmountable
反义词:invincible, unconquerable, unbeatable
词性:adjective

词源:
From non- + invincible.  "non-" 表示否定，"invincible" 源自拉丁语 "invincibilis"，意为“不可征服的”。
