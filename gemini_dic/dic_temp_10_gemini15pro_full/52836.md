dimble:/ˈdɪmbəl/| n.|小凹陷，酒窝，小坑|A small natural indentation in the flesh, especially in the cheek or chin.|She had a dimple in her left cheek when she smiled. 她笑的时候左脸颊上有一个酒窝。|近义词：dent, hollow, indentation|反义词:bulge, protrusion|noun

Etymology: From Middle English *dimpul, probably from a frequentative form of *dimpen “to indent,” perhaps of imitative origin.
