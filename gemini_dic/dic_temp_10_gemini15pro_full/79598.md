glorification:/ˌɡlɔːrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 荣耀化；颂扬；赞美 | the act of making something seem more important or better than it really is | The film was criticized for its glorification of violence. 这部电影因美化暴力而受到批评。 | 近义词：exaltation, praise, celebration | 反义词：vilification, condemnation, denigration | 词性: 名词

词源: From Late Latin glorificatio, from Latin glorificare “to glorify,” from gloria "glory" + -ficare, combining form of facere "to make, do."
