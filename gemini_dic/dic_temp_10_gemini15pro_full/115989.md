marshalcies:/ˈmɑːrʃəlsiz/
n.
元帅职位；军管区域；司法行政管辖区
The office or jurisdiction of a marshal.  Areas controlled by a marshal, often referring to law enforcement or military control.
The marshalcies were reorganized after the war. 战后，军管区域被重新划分。
The president appointed new marshals to several marshalcies. 总统任命了几个司法行政管辖区的新执法官。
