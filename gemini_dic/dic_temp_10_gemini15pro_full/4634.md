aldine:/ˈɔːldaɪn/ | adj. | 阿尔丁的；印刷精美的 | Relating to Aldus Manutius, a Venetian printer of the late 15th and early 16th centuries, or to the books he published, characterized by elegant and correct typography. | This is a rare aldine edition of Virgil. 这是一本罕见的阿尔丁版本的维吉尔诗集。 | 近义词: elegant, fine, beautiful | 反义词: crude, coarse, ugly | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Aldus Manutius (Latin: Aldus Manutius), Venetian printer (1449-1515), + -ine.
