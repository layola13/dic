headmastership:/ˌhedˈmɑːstərʃɪp/ | n. | 校长职位；校长身份 | The headmastership of the prestigious school was a highly sought-after position. 这所著名学校的校长职位是一个备受追捧的职位。 |  The headmastership carries a great deal of responsibility. 校长职位责任重大。 |  principalship, leadership | subordination | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From headmaster + -ship.  "Headmaster" itself comes from "head" (chief, principal) + "master" (teacher, leader).  The suffix "-ship" denotes state, condition, or office.
