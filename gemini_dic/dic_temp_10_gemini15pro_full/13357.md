atman:/ˈɑːtmən/| n.|真我，灵魂，自我|The atman is the individual self or soul in Hinduism and other Indian religions. 在印度教和其他印度宗教中，真我是指个体自我或灵魂。|例句：In Hinduism, the atman is believed to be ultimately identical with Brahman. 在印度教中，真我最终被认为与梵天相同。

例句：The concept of atman is central to understanding the cycle of reincarnation. 真我的概念对于理解轮回的循环至关重要。|近义词：soul, spirit, self, essence|反义词:body, physical form|词性:noun
