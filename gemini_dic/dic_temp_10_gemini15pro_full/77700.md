geminid: /ˈdʒɛmɪnɪd/
n.
双子座流星雨
A meteor shower that occurs annually in December, peaking around December 13-14.  It is associated with the asteroid 3200 Phaethon.
The Geminids are considered to be one of the best and most reliable meteor showers every year. 双子座流星雨被认为是每年最好最可靠的流星雨之一。

The Geminid meteor shower produced a spectacular display last night. 昨晚双子座流星雨呈现了壮观的景象。

近义词: meteor shower (流星雨)
反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific meteor shower)
词性: noun
