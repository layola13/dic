panderage:/ˈpændərɪdʒ/ | n. | 拉皮条；怂恿；迎合；煽动 | The act of pandering, especially to base desires or tastes.  The exploitation or promotion of something that caters to low tastes or immoral desires. |  The film was criticized for its blatant panderage to the lowest common denominator. 这部电影因公然迎合最庸俗的趣味而受到批评。| synonyms: pimping, procuring, exploitation, indulgence, gratification | antonyms: restraint, disapproval, condemnation | noun

pander:/ˈpændər/ | v. | 拉皮条；迎合；怂恿 | To gratify or indulge (an immoral or distasteful desire or taste). To cater basely. |  He pandered to the rich tourists. 他迎合富有的游客。| synonyms: indulge, gratify, cater to, exploit | antonyms: deny, refuse, resist | verb
