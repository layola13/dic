blessed:/ˈblesɪd/| adj.|被祝福的；神圣的；幸福的；该死的（用于加强语气）|Made holy; consecrated. Bringing happiness or good fortune. (Used for emphasis) |例句：The blessed event is coming soon.  幸福的时刻即将到来。

例句：Blessed are the meek, for they shall inherit the earth. 温柔的人有福了，因为他们必承受地土。|近义词：sacred, holy, fortunate, lucky, happy|反义词：cursed, damned, unhappy, unfortunate|词性:形容词

blessed:/ˈblesɪd/| n.|受祝福的人|Those who are blessed. |例句：The blessed shall inherit the kingdom of heaven.  蒙福的人必承受天国。

例句：Only the blessed will enter paradise. 只有受祝福的人才能进入天堂。|近义词：saint, fortunate one|反义词：cursed, damned|词性:名词
