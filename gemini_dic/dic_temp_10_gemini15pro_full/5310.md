allotting:/əˈlɒtɪŋ/
verb
分配;拨给
the action of allotting something.
例句：The government is allotting $10 million for education research. 政府正在拨款1千万美元用于教育研究。

例句：They are allotting seats on a first-come, first-served basis. 他们按先到先得的原则分配座位。

近义词：allocating, assigning, distributing, apportioning
反义词：withholding, denying

词性:动名词(gerund)


allot:/əˈlɒt/
verb
分配;拨给
give or apportion (something) to someone.
例句：The teacher allotted each student a different task. 老师给每个学生分配了不同的任务。

例句：We were allotted a small room. 我们被分配到一个小房间。

近义词：allocate, assign, distribute, apportion
反义词：withhold, deny

词性:动词(verb)
