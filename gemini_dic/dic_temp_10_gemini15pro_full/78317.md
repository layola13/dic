gerontocrats: /ˌdʒɛrənˈtɒkræts/ | n. |  老年统治者；老人政治 | A group of rulers who are significantly older than most of the adult population. |  The country is ruled by a group of gerontocrats who refuse to give up power.  这个国家由一群拒绝放弃权力的老年统治者统治。| synonyms: elders, oligarchy (of elders), old guard | antonyms: youth, young generation | noun

gerontocracy: /ˌdʒɛrənˈtɒkrəsi/ | n. |  老人政治；老人统治 | A form of social organization in which the elderly hold the most power and influence. |  The gerontocracy in the village makes all the important decisions.  村里的老人政治决定了所有重要的事情。| synonyms: rule by elders | antonyms: youthocracy | noun
