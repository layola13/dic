constitutivity:/ˌkɒnstɪtjuːˈtɪvɪti/ | n. | 构成性，组成性 | The property of being constitutive; the way in which something is constituted.  | The constitutivity of language is a complex issue. 语言的构成性是一个复杂的问题。 |  The constitutivity of social norms is a central concern in sociology. 社会规范的构成性是社会学的核心关注点。 | synonyms: constitution, composition, formation | antonyms:  decomposition, dissolution | noun
