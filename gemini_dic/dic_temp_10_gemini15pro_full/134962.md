nonnationalizations:/ˌnɒnˌnæʃənəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/
noun
非国有化
The act of reversing nationalization, returning previously nationalized industries or assets to private ownership.
The government pursued a policy of nonnationalizations, selling off state-owned enterprises. 政府奉行非国有化政策，出售国有企业。

The nonnationalizations of the railway system led to improved efficiency. 铁路系统的非国有化提高了效率。
Synonym: privatization, denationalization
Antonym: nationalization
