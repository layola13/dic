corroborant: /kəˈrɒbərənt/ | n. adj. | 滋补剂；滋补的 | something that strengthens or confirms; strengthening or confirming |  The doctor prescribed a corroborant tonic to help her regain her strength. 医生给她开了一种滋补的补药，帮助她恢复体力。|  The witness's testimony was corroborant, confirming the defendant's alibi.  证人的证词是确凿的，证实了被告的不在场证明。 | 近义词: tonic, restorative, invigorating, confirming | 反义词: weakening, debilitating | 词性: noun, adjective

corroborate: /kəˈrɒbəreɪt/ | vt. | 证实；确证 | to confirm or give support to (a statement, theory, or finding) |  The evidence corroborated his story. 证据证实了他的说法。| Further research corroborated the initial findings.  进一步的研究证实了最初的发现。| 近义词: confirm, verify, substantiate | 反义词: contradict, refute | 词性: verb

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin corroborant- ‘strengthening’, from the verb corroborare, from cor- ‘together’ + roborare ‘to strengthen’, from robur ‘strength’.
