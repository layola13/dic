galvanographic:/ˌɡæl.və.nəˈɡræf.ɪk/ | adj. | 电流图的，电流记录法的 | Relating to galvanography, a technique of electroplating to produce a design. |  The galvanographic process creates intricate patterns on metal surfaces. 电流图工艺在金属表面形成复杂的图案。| Near Synonyms: electrographic, electroplating | Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps "manual etching" as a contrasting process) | Word type: Adjective

galvanography:/ˌɡæl.vəˈnɒɡ.rə.fi/ | n. | 电流图法，电镀术 | An electrolytic process for copying engraved plates, or producing plates for printing; electrotypy.  A method of electrical recording. | Galvanography was used to create duplicates of intricate engravings. 电流图法被用于复制复杂的雕刻作品。| Near Synonyms: electrography, electrotypy | Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, perhaps "traditional engraving" as a contrasting process) | Word type: Noun
