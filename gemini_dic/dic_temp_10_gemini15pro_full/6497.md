amok:/əˈmʌk/ | adv. n. | 狂乱地，杀人狂 |  In a violently raging, murderous, or uncontrolled manner; a frenzied or uncontrolled outburst.  Derived from the Malay word "amuk," meaning "engaging furiously in battle."  Originally referred to a culturally specific dissociative episode characterized by a period of brooding followed by an outburst of violent, aggressive, or homicidal behavior directed at people and objects. The term has broadened in English to describe any uncontrolled or frenzied outburst. | The man ran amok in the streets, attacking anyone in his path. 那个人在街上狂奔，攻击他遇到的任何人。| Synonyms: berserk, frenzied, wild, uncontrolled, raging | Antonyms: calm, peaceful, controlled, restrained | adverb
