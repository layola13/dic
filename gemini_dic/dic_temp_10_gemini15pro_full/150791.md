parados:/ˈpærədɒs/ (UK), /ˈpærədɑːs/ (US)| n.|舞台后方的通道；演员出入通道；古希腊剧场舞台背后的通道或建筑物|A passage behind the stage or seating area of an ancient Greek theatre, or the building containing it.  The parados served as an entrance and exit for the chorus and sometimes actors.  It also provided a backdrop for the action.|The chorus entered the orchestra through the parados.|Synonyms: passage, entrance, exit|Antonyms: N/A|noun
