etymologist:/ˌɛtɪˈmɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 词源学家 | A person who studies the origin and history of words. |  例句：The etymologist traced the word back to its Latin roots.  这位词源学家追溯了这个词的拉丁语词根。

例句：She's a renowned etymologist who has written several books on the history of the English language. 她是一位著名的词源学家，写过几本关于英语历史的书。

| 近义词：lexicographer, philologist | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
