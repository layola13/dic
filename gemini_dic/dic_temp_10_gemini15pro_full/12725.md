assessorship:/əˈsɛsərʃɪp/
n.
评估员的职位；估价员的职位
The position or office of an assessor.
He held the assessorship for five years.  他担任了五年的估价员。
position, office, post
N/A
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From assessor + -ship.  Assessor comes from Old French assessour, from Late Latin assessor, from Latin assessus, past participle of assidere "to sit beside," from ad- "to" + sedere "to sit."
