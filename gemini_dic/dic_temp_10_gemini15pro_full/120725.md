misalloted:/ˌmɪsəˈlɒtɪd/
adjective
错误分配的，分配不当的
incorrectly or inappropriately allotted
The funds were misalloted, resulting in a shortage for the intended project. 资金分配不当，导致预定项目的资金短缺。
The misalloted resources caused delays in the project's completion.  资源分配不当导致项目完成延误。
misallocated, wrongly distributed
correctly allocated, properly distributed
