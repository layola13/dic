docilities:/dɒˈsɪlətiz/
noun
温顺，顺从，可教性
the quality of being docile; ready compliance or submissiveness
The trainer praised the dog's docilities.  训练员称赞了这只狗的温顺。
Synonyms:  tractability, manageability, obedience, compliance, submissiveness
Antonyms:  wilfulness, obstinacy, intractability, defiance, rebelliousness
