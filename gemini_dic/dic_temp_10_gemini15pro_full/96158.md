indenturing:/ɪnˈdɛntʃərɪŋ/
|n.|契约约束，签订契约|The act of binding someone by an indenture, a formal legal agreement or contract, often for a fixed period of service.|The indenturing of young apprentices was common in the 18th century.  18世纪，年轻学徒的契约约束是很常见的。|近义词: binding, contracting|反义词: releasing, freeing|noun

indenturing:/ɪnˈdɛntʃərɪŋ/
|v.|以契约约束|Binding someone by an indenture, especially for a fixed period of service.|The company indentured many workers from overseas.  这家公司以契约约束了许多来自海外的工人。|近义词: binding, contracting|反义词: releasing, freeing|verb
