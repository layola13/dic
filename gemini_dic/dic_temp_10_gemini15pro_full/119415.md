microfilming: /ˈmaɪkrəʊfɪlmɪŋ/ | n. |缩微胶卷拍摄；缩微胶片；缩微摄影|The process of photographing documents, drawings, etc., at a reduced size onto microfilm. |Libraries are increasingly using microfilming to preserve old newspapers.  图书馆越来越多地使用缩微胶卷来保存旧报纸。| 近义词: microphotography | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps enlargement) | 词性: noun

microfilm: /ˈmaɪkrəʊfɪlm/ | n. |缩微胶卷；缩微胶片|A strip or roll of photographic film containing microphotographs of documents, newspapers, etc., for storage or retrieval. |The microfilm can be viewed on a special reader.  这缩微胶卷可以在特殊的阅读器上观看。| 近义词: microfiche | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun

microfilm: /ˈmaɪkrəʊfɪlm/ | vt. |用缩微胶卷拍摄；缩微拍摄|To photograph documents, drawings, etc., at a reduced size onto microfilm. |The library microfilmed its collection of rare manuscripts. 图书馆将其珍稀手稿收藏缩微拍摄。| 近义词: microphotograph | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: verb
