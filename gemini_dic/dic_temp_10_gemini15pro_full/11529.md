variolation:/ˌværiˈəʊleɪʃn/
noun
种痘；人痘接种
The deliberate infection with smallpox (or occasionally other diseases) in order to create immunity.
Variolation was practiced for centuries before the development of vaccination.  种痘在疫苗出现前的几个世纪里就已实行。
