overwinded:/ˌoʊvərˈwaɪndɪd/| adj.|喘不过气的，呼吸困难的|Excessively out of breath; gasping for air, often due to strenuous exertion.  The word suggests more than just being tired; it implies a struggle to breathe.|例句：After sprinting up the hill, he was completely overwinded.  跑上山后，他完全喘不过气来了。

例句：The singer was overwinded after performing a particularly demanding piece.  这位歌手在表演了一首特别费力的曲目后，呼吸困难。|近义词：breathless, winded, gasping, puffed, out of breath|反义词: rested, relaxed, calm|词性:形容词
