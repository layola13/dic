freedoms:/ˈfriːdəmz/
noun
自由（复数）
The state of being free or at liberty rather than in confinement or under physical restraint.
People enjoy basic freedoms such as freedom of speech and religion. 人们享有言论自由和宗教自由等基本自由。
liberties, rights
restrictions, limitations
