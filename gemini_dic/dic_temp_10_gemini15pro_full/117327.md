meddlesomeness:/ˈmedlˌsʌmnɪs/| n.|爱管闲事，干涉，多管闲事|The quality of being meddlesome; interfering in others' affairs.|Her meddlesomeness made her unpopular with her colleagues. 她爱管闲事的行为让她在同事中不受欢迎。|Synonyms: interference, intrusion, intervention|Antonyms: noninterference, respect, aloofness|noun


meddle:/ˈmedl/| v.|干涉，干预，插手|To interfere in something that is not one's concern.|Don't meddle in other people's business. 不要干涉别人的事。|Synonyms: interfere, tamper, intervene|Antonyms: ignore, neglect, abstain|verb
