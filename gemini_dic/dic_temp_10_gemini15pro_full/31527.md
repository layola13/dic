chartaceous:/tʃɑːrˈteɪʃəs/
adj.
纸质的；像纸的
Resembling paper in texture; papery.
The mushroom had a chartaceous cap. 这种蘑菇的菌盖像纸一样。
Synonyms: papery, papyraceous
Antonyms: leathery, thick
adjective
