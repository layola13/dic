coelacanthine:/ˌsiːləˈkænθaɪn/| adj. n.|腔棘鱼的；腔棘鱼目动物|Relating to or characteristic of the coelacanths, a group of primitive lobe-finned fishes.|The coelacanthine fish is a living fossil. 腔棘鱼是一种活化石。|近义词：crossopterygian|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "modern fish")|词性: adjective, noun


coelacanth:/ˈsiːləkænθ/| n.|腔棘鱼|A type of fish that was thought to be extinct, but living specimens have been found.|The discovery of the coelacanth astonished the scientific world. 腔棘鱼的发现震惊了科学界。|近义词： Latimeria|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性: noun


词源: From Greek *koilos*  (hollow) + *akantha* (spine), referring to the hollow spines in the fish's fins.
