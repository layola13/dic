gazing:/ˈɡeɪzɪŋ/| v.|凝视，注视|Looking steadily and intently, especially in admiration, surprise, or thought.|She was gazing out the window, lost in thought. 她凝视着窗外，陷入了沉思。|近义词：staring, watching, observing|反义词：glancing, ignoring|词性: Verb, present participle of gaze.
Etymology: From Middle English gasen, from Old Norse gausa (“to gape, stare”).
