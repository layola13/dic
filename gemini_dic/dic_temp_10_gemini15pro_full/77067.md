garnishes:/ˈɡɑːrnɪʃɪz/
n.|装饰物，配菜|A small amount of food used to decorate a dish or drink.|The chef added a few garnishes of parsley to the soup. 厨师在汤里加了一些欧芹作为装饰。|近义词：decoration, trimming, adornment|反义词:plainness|词性:名词

garnishes:/ˈɡɑːrnɪʃɪz/
v.|装饰，加配菜|To decorate a dish or drink with a small amount of food.|She garnished the cake with fresh strawberries. 她用新鲜草莓装饰蛋糕。|近义词：decorate, adorn, embellish|反义词:strip, denude|词性:动词

词源:
From Old French *garniss-, stem of garnir "to furnish, equip, supply, provide, garnish," from Frankish *warnjan "to provide, protect," or some other Germanic source, from Proto-Germanic *warnijaną "to warn, take care, provide."
