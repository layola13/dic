fuzziness:/ˈfʌzinəs/ | n. | 模糊性；绒毛状；不清楚 | The fuzziness of the old photograph made it difficult to identify the people.  这张老照片的模糊性使得很难辨认出照片中的人。 | The camera's autofocus struggled with the fuzziness of the subject. 相机的自动对焦功能难以处理拍摄对象的模糊性。 | 近义词：blurriness, vagueness, indistinctness, ambiguity | 反义词：clarity, sharpness, distinctness, precision | 词性: 名词

词源:  From fuzzy + -ness.  Fuzzy 源自 fuzz,  指细小的纤维或绒毛，-ness 是名词后缀，表示状态或性质。
