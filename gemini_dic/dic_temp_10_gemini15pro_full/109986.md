libertys:/ˈlɪbərtiz/
n.
自由（liberty的复数形式，不常用）
Plural form of liberty, referring to instances or types of freedom.  This form is less common than the singular "liberty."
They fought for their liberties. 他们为他们的自由而战。
近义词：freedoms, rights
反义词：restrictions, constraints
词性：名词

liberty:/ˈlɪbərti/
n.
自由
The state of being free within society from oppressive restrictions imposed by authority on one's way of life, behavior, or political views.
The statue is a symbol of liberty. 这座雕像是自由的象征。
近义词：freedom, independence
反义词：captivity, oppression
词性：名词
