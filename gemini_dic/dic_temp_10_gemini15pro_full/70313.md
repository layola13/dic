fie:/faɪ/
interjection
呸; 啐; 哎呀（表示不赞成、厌恶或不耐烦）
An exclamation of disgust or disapproval.
Fie! You shouldn't say such things! 呸！你不应该说这样的话！
Fie upon this world!  呸！这糟糕的世界！
近义词: shame, for shame, pshaw
反义词: bravo, well done
词性:感叹词
