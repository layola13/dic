deploredness:/dɪˈplɔːrɪdnəs/
n.
悲惨；凄惨；可悲
The state of being deplored; wretchedness.
The deploredness of the situation moved her to tears.  这种情况的悲惨使她感动得流下了眼泪。
近义词: misery, wretchedness, sadness, sorrow, pitifulness
反义词: happiness, joy, blessedness, felicity
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From *deplored* + *-ness*.  *Deplored* comes from the French *déplorer*, from Latin *dēplōrāre* "to bewail, lament," from *dē-* "down, completely" + *plōrāre* "to weep, cry, wail."  *-ness* is a native English suffix forming nouns from adjectives, expressing state or condition.
