commiseratingly:/kəˈmɪzəreɪtɪŋli/
adverb
同情地，怜悯地
in a way that shows or expresses commiseration.
He patted her shoulder commiseratingly. 他同情地拍了拍她的肩膀。
近义词: sympathetically, empathetically, compassionately
反义词: unsympathetically, heartlessly, callously
词性: adverb

Etymology:
From commiserate + -ing + -ly.  Commiserate comes from Late Latin commiseratus, past participle of commiserari "to pity," from com- "with" + miserari "to pity," from miser "wretched, pitiable."
