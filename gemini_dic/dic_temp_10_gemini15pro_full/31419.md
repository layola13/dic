chariness:/ˈtʃɛrɪnɪs/| n.|谨慎,小心,吝啬|Carefulness; caution; reluctance to speak or act freely.|She approached the new project with chariness, unsure of its potential success. 她小心翼翼地开始了这个新项目，不确定它是否有成功的潜力。|近义词:caution, wariness, hesitancy, reluctance, reserve|反义词:recklessness, carelessness, boldness, generosity, extravagance|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *cherie*, from Old French *chier* (to hold dear, cherish), from Late Latin *cārus* (dear). The sense of "carefulness" developed from the idea of treating something as precious and therefore handling it with caution.
