montanans:/mɒnˈtænənz/
noun
蒙大拿州人；蒙大拿居民

Many Montanans enjoy outdoor activities like hiking and fishing. 蒙大拿州的许多居民都喜欢远足和钓鱼等户外活动。
Montanans are known for their independent spirit. 蒙大拿州人以其独立精神而闻名。
synonyms: residents of Montana, citizens of Montana
