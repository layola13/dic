excusability:/ɪkˌskjuːzəˈbɪləti/| n. |可原谅性，可辩解性|The excusability of his actions is debatable. 他的行为是否可以原谅还有待商榷。|例句：The court accepted the excusability of his absence due to illness. 法庭接受了他因病缺席的可辩解性。

The excusability of her lateness, given the traffic, was understandable. 考虑到交通状况，她迟到的理由是可以理解的。|近义词：justification, forgiveness, acceptability|反义词:unforgivability, culpability|词性:noun
词源:From "excusable" + "-ity." "Excusable" comes from Late Middle English "excusable," from Old French "excusable," from Latin "excusabilis," from "excusare" (to excuse).
