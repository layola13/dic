ghyll:/ɡɪl/ | n. |峡谷，溪流|A deep, narrow ravine, especially one with a stream running through it. |例句：We hiked through the ghyll, marveling at the waterfalls. 我们徒步穿越峡谷，惊叹于瀑布的美景。

例句：The ghyll was carved out by glaciers centuries ago.  几个世纪前，冰川雕刻出了这条峡谷。|近义词：ravine, gorge, canyon, gully |反义词:ridge, peak |词性:n.

Etymology: From the dialectal word *ghyll*, ultimately from Old Norse *gil* "ravine, cleft".
