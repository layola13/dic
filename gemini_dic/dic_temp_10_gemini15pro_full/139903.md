odds:/ɑdz/ | n. |  可能性，几率；不利条件；不平等；优势 | the probability of a specified outcome | The odds are against him winning the election. 他赢得选举的可能性很小。 | synonyms: chances, probability, likelihood | antonyms: certainty, improbability | noun

odds:/ɑdz/ | n. |  赔率 | the ratio between the amounts staked by the parties to a bet, based on the expected probability either way | The odds are 2 to 1 that the horse will win. 这匹马赢的赔率是2比1。 | synonyms: ratio, betting odds | antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *oddr, meaning "point, triangle."  Originally referred to a pointed object or a triangle formed by three dice.  The sense of inequality or difference arose from the uneven points or sides of a triangle.  The gambling sense developed from the use of dice in games of chance.
