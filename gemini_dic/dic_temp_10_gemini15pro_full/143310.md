outduelling:/ˌaʊtˈdʒuːəlɪŋ/ | v. | 比…更擅长决斗；在决斗中胜过 | Defeating someone in a duel; surpassing someone in skill at duelling. |  He outduelled his opponent with a swift move. 他以迅雷不及掩耳之势在决斗中击败了对手。 |  The young knight outduelled the champion, shocking the crowd.  年轻的骑士在决斗中胜过了冠军，震惊了全场。 | 近义词: outfighting, vanquishing, defeating | 反义词: losing to, being defeated by | 词性: 动词
