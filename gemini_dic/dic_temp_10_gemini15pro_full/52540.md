diffidentness:/ˈdɪfɪdəntnəs/| n.|缺乏自信，胆怯，羞怯|The state of being diffident; lack of self-confidence.|Her diffidentness prevented her from speaking up in class. 她因为缺乏自信而在课堂上不敢发言。|近义词：shyness, timidity, hesitancy, insecurity, self-doubt|反义词：confidence, assertiveness, boldness, self-assurance|词性:名词
Etymology: From Late Latin *diffīdentia, from Latin diffīdens "distrustful, lacking confidence," present participle of diffīdere "to distrust, lack confidence," from dis- "away from" + fīdere "to trust."
