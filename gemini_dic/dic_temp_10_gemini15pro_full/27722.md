canes:/keɪnz/| n.|拐杖；（尤指某些植物的）茎，秆；藤条|Long, thin pieces of wood or other material used for support or punishment, especially the stems of certain plants such as bamboo or sugar cane.|He walks with a cane. 他拄着拐杖走路。|近义词：sticks, staffs, rods|反义词：(没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 crutches 作为功能上的反面例子) |Noun
