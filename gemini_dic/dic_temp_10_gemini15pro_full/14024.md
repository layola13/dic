auscultate:/ˈɔːskəlˌteɪt/| v.|听诊|To listen to the sounds within (a patient's body) to detect abnormalities, especially in the heart or lungs.|The doctor auscultated the patient's chest. 医生听诊了病人的胸部。|近义词：examine, listen to|反义词:ignore|verb

auscultation:/ˌɔːskəlˈteɪʃən/| n.|听诊|The act of listening to sounds within the body as a method of diagnosis.|Auscultation of the heart revealed a murmur. 心脏听诊发现杂音。|近义词：examination, listening|反义词: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin auscultāre "to listen to," from auscultare "to listen attentively," from auris "ear."
