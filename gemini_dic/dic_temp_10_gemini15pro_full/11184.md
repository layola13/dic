archimandrite:/ˌɑːrkɪˈmændraɪt/| n.|大修道院院长；大修道院的男院长|A superior abbot in certain Eastern Orthodox churches.  An archimandrite is a title of a higher rank than hegumen, roughly equivalent to abbot in Western Christianity.|The archimandrite presided over the elaborate ceremony.|近义词：abbot, superior|反义词:monk (although not a direct opposite, it represents a lower rank)|noun
