chuckleheaded:/ˈtʃʌkəlˌhɛdɪd/| adj.|傻头傻脑的，愚蠢的|A chuckleheaded person is silly or stupid.|He made a chuckleheaded decision to invest all his money in that failing company. 他傻乎乎地把所有钱都投资到那家快倒闭的公司了。|Synonyms: foolish, stupid, silly, dim-witted, simpleminded|Antonyms: intelligent, smart, bright, clever, wise|adjective

chucklehead:/ˈtʃʌkəlˌhɛd/| n.|傻瓜，笨蛋|A chucklehead is a stupid person.|Don't be such a chucklehead! 别这么傻！|Synonyms: fool, idiot, dolt, nitwit, simpleton|Antonyms: genius, intellectual, sage, savant|noun
