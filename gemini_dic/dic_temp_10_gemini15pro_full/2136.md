adaptivities:/əˌdæpˈtɪvɪtiz/
n.|适应性，适应能力|The adaptivities of this species are remarkable, allowing it to thrive in diverse environments.  这个物种的适应能力非常出色，使其能够在不同的环境中繁衍生息。
The company demonstrated its adaptivities by quickly adjusting to the changing market. 该公司通过快速适应变化的市场展示了其适应能力。
|近义词：adaptability, flexibility, resilience|反义词：inflexibility, rigidity|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From adapt + -ivity + -ies.  Adapt comes from Latin adaptare "to fit, adjust," from ad- "to" + aptare "to fit."  The suffix -ivity denotes the state or quality of, and -ies marks the plural form. 由 adapt + -ivity + -ies 组成。Adapt 源自拉丁语 adaptare，意为“适应、调整”，由 ad- “到” + aptare “适合”组成。后缀 -ivity 表示状态或性质，-ies 标记复数形式。
