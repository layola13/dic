mvp:/ˌem viː ˈpiː/| n.|最有价值球员 (Most Valuable Player)|A player who is considered the most important or effective member of a team. | LeBron James was named the MVP of the finals. 勒布朗·詹姆斯被评为总决赛最有价值球员。| synonyms: star player, key player, all-star | antonyms: benchwarmer, reserve player | noun

mvp:/ˌem viː ˈpiː/| n.|最小可行产品 (Minimum Viable Product)|A product with just enough features to attract early-adopter customers and validate a product idea early in the development cycle. |  The startup launched an MVP to gather user feedback. 这家初创公司发布了一个最小可行产品以收集用户反馈。| synonyms: prototype, test product, beta version | antonyms: finished product, final version | noun
