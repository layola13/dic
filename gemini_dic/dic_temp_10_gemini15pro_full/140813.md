on his part:/ɒn hɪz pɑːt/ /ɑːn hɪz pɑːrt/
|phrase|就他而言；在他方面|Concerning or with respect to him; from his point of view or as his contribution.|He apologized, but there was no corresponding expression of regret on her part. 他道歉了，但她方面却没有相应的表示遗憾。|For his part, he wished the matter had never come up. 就他而言，他希望这件事从未发生过。|Synonym: as far as he is concerned, as for him, from his side|Antonym: on her part, on their part
|phrase|
