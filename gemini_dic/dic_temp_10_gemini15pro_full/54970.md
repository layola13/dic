disproportionateness:/ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃənətnəs/ | n. | 不成比例，不相称 |  The state or quality of being disproportionate; lack of proportion.  | The disproportionateness of the punishment to the crime shocked many.  对罪行处罚的不相称让许多人感到震惊。 | synonyms: imbalance, disparity, inequality, asymmetry | antonyms: balance, proportionality, symmetry | noun

disproportionateness:/ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃənətnəs/ | n. | 不均衡，不协调 | The condition of being out of proportion or balance. | There is a disproportionateness between the resources available and the needs of the community.  可用资源与社区需求之间存在不均衡。| synonyms: unevenness, lopsidedness, incongruity | antonyms:  evenness, harmony, balance | noun
