butters:/ˈbʌtərz/| v. |涂黄油于；用黄油烹调；讨好；奉承|To spread butter on something; to cook with butter; to flatter someone excessively.|She buttered the bread. 她给面包涂了黄油。|近义词：grease, flatter, sweet-talk|反义词:|verb
