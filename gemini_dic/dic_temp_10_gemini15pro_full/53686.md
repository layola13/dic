disciplinary:/ˈdɪsɪplɪˌnɛri/ /ˈdɪsəplɪˌnɛri/| adj.|纪律的；惩戒性的；训练的|Relating to discipline; designed to correct or punish breaches of discipline.|例句：The teacher took disciplinary action against the disruptive student. 老师对扰乱课堂秩序的学生采取了纪律处分。|近义词：corrective, punitive, penal|反义词: lenient, permissive|词性:形容词

disciplinary:/ˈdɪsɪplɪˌnɛri/ /ˈdɪsəplɪˌnɛri/| n.|纪律委员；惩戒人员|A person who enforces discipline.|例句：The school's disciplinary board met to discuss the student's expulsion. 学校的纪律委员会开会讨论了该学生的开除问题。|近义词：enforcer, regulator|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
