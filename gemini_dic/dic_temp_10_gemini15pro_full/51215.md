destituting:/ˈdɛstɪˌtjuːtɪŋ/
verb
使贫困；使丧失；剥夺
depriving someone of resources or means of support, making them destitute.
The economic downturn is destituting many families. 经济衰退使许多家庭陷入贫困。
Synonyms: impoverishing, ruining, bankrupting
Antonyms: enriching, supporting, providing for
verb

destitute:/ˈdɛstɪˌtjuːt/
adjective
贫困的；缺乏的；赤贫的
without the basic necessities of life; lacking something needed or desirable
The earthquake left many people destitute. 地震使许多人一无所有。
Synonyms: impoverished, penniless, needy
Antonyms: wealthy, affluent, prosperous
adjective
