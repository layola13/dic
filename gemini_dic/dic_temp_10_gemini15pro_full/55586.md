disuniform:/ˌdɪsˈjuːnɪfɔːrm/
adjective
不统一的，不一致的
Not uniform; lacking uniformity; dissimilar; diverse.
The quality of the products was disuniform, ranging from excellent to subpar. 产品的质量参差不齐，从优秀到劣质都有。
The soldiers' disuniform heights made the formation look uneven. 士兵们身高不一，使得队形看起来不整齐。
近义词:nonuniform, inconsistent, uneven, irregular, disparate, varying, diverse
反义词:uniform, consistent, even, regular, homogeneous
词性:adjective
词源:dis- (not) + uniform
