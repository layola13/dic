grasp: /ɡræsp/ | v. | 抓住，理解，掌握 | To grip something firmly; to comprehend or understand something. | He grasped the concept quickly. 他很快理解了这个概念。| synonyms: grip, clutch, seize, comprehend, understand | antonyms: release, loosen, misunderstand, misinterpret | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *grapsen, *graspen, from Old Norse *graspa (“to grasp, grip”), from Proto-Germanic *graspaną (“to grasp, grip”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰrebʰ- (“to seize, grasp”).
