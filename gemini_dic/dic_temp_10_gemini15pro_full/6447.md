amplificate:/æmˈplɪfɪkeɪt/
v.
放大，增强，详述
to make larger or greater; increase; extend; to expand (something, such as a statement) by the addition of details
The lawyer amplificated his point by providing several case studies. 律师通过提供几个案例研究来详细阐述他的观点。
近义词:expand, enlarge, amplify, augment, elaborate
反义词:reduce, decrease, condense, abridge
词性:动词
