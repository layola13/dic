agribusiness:/ˈæɡrɪˌbɪznɪs/| n.|农业综合企业; 农业产业|The agribusiness sector is facing increasing challenges due to climate change. 农业综合企业部门正因气候变化面临越来越多的挑战。|例句：The agribusiness company invests heavily in research and development. 这家农业综合企业在研发方面投入巨资。|例句：Agribusiness plays a vital role in the global economy. 农业综合企业在全球经济中扮演着至关重要的角色。|近义词：agricultural industry, farming industry|反义词: (No clear antonym exists, potentially "subsistence farming")|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From *agriculture* + *business*, coined in 1957 by John Davis and Ray Goldberg. *Agriculture* comes from Latin *agricultūra*, from *ager* "field" + *cultūra* "cultivation". *Business* comes from Middle English *bisynes*, meaning "state of being busy," ultimately from Old English *bisig*.
