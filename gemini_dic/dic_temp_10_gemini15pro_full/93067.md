idolizations:/ˌaɪdələˈzeɪʃənz/
n.
偶像化，崇拜，盲目崇拜
The act of idolizing; excessive admiration or reverence.
Her idolizations of the movie star bordered on obsession. 她对这位电影明星的崇拜近乎痴迷。
worship, adoration, reverence
criticism, condemnation
noun

idol:/ˈaɪdl/
n.
偶像，神像
An image or representation of a god used as an object of worship.
The ancient tribe worshipped a wooden idol.  这个古老的部落崇拜一个木制偶像。
icon, hero, star
critic, enemy
noun

idolize:/ˈaɪdəˌlaɪz/
v.
崇拜，把…当作偶像
To admire, reverence, or love someone or something to excess.
He idolized his older brother. 他崇拜他的哥哥。
worship, adore, revere
criticize, condemn
verb
