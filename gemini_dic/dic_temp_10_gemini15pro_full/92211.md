hypertechnicalness:/ˌhaɪpərˈtɛknɪkəlnəs/| n.|过度技术性，过分技术化|The hypertechnicalness of the manual made it difficult for the average user to understand.  这本手册的过度技术性使得普通用户难以理解。|例句：The lawyer's argument was marred by its hypertechnicalness, obscuring the core issues. 律师的论证因其过分的技术性而受损，掩盖了核心问题。

The hypertechnicalness of the presentation lost the audience.  演讲的过度技术性让听众感到困惑。|近义词：technicality, complexity, intricacy|反义词:simplicity, clarity|词性:noun
