icon:/ˈaɪkɒn/ (UK) /ˈaɪkɑːn/ (US)| n.|图标，象征，偶像|A small picture or symbol on a computer screen that represents a program, file, or function.  A person or thing regarded as a representative symbol of something.|例句：Click on the printer icon to print the document. 点击打印机图标打印文档。|例句：He became a fashion icon in the 1960s. 他在20世纪60年代成为了时尚偶像。|近义词：symbol, emblem, image|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
