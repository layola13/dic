miscasualty:/ˌmɪsˈkæʒuəlti/
noun
误伤；意外伤亡；非战斗伤亡
An injury or death caused by accident or unintentionally, especially in war or other violent situations.
The general reported several misc casualties during the retreat. 将军报告说在撤退期间有几起意外伤亡。
Synonyms: accident, mishap, unintentional injury, non-battle casualty
Antonyms: intentional injury, combat casualty
