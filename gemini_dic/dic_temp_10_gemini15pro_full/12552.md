aspirating:/ˈæspəreɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 吸入的，送气的 | drawing in by suction or breathing; pronounced with an audible puff of breath |  The doctor was aspirating fluid from the patient's lung. 医生正在吸出病人肺部的液体。 | synonyms: inhaling, sucking, breathing in | antonyms: exhaling, blowing out | adjective

aspirating:/ˈæspəreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 吸入，送气 | the act of drawing in by suction or breathing; pronouncing with an audible puff of breath | He aspirated some water while swimming. 他游泳时吸入了一些水. | synonyms: inhale, suck, breathe in | antonyms: exhale, blow out | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *aspirare*, frequentative of *aspirare* "to breathe on, blow at, breathe towards, strive for," from *ad-* "to, towards" + *spirare* "to breathe."
