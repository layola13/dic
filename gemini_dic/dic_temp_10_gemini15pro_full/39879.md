constipations: /ˌkɒnstɪˈpeɪʃnz/ (UK) /ˌkɑːnstɪˈpeɪʃnz/ (US) | n. |便秘（复数）| Multiple instances of the condition of being unable to easily release solid waste from the body.  This is less commonly used than the singular form. | The patient reported recurring constipations.  这位病人报告说反复便秘。| 近义词:  (plural of) costiveness, dyschezia | 反义词: diarrheas (less common) | 词性: 名词
