multifarious:/ˌmʌltɪˈfɛəriəs/ | adj. |多种多样的，各式各样的 | Having many different parts or forms; numerous and varied. | The city offers multifarious attractions for visitors.  这座城市为游客提供了各种各样的景点。| The museum boasts a multifarious collection of artifacts from around the world.  这家博物馆拥有来自世界各地的各种各样的文物。| synonyms: diverse, varied, manifold, numerous | antonyms: limited, few, uniform, homogeneous | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *multifarius*, from *multus* (many) + *-farius* (perhaps related to *fari* "to speak," suggesting "speaking in many ways").
