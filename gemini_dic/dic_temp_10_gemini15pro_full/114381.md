makavelli:/ˌmɑːkəˈvɛli/ | n. | 马基雅维利，权谋家 |  Refers to a cunning, amoral, and opportunistic person, often associated with Niccolò Machiavelli, author of "The Prince," a treatise on acquiring and maintaining political power.  It is often used in hip-hop culture to represent a shrewd and manipulative persona. | Tupac Shakur adopted the stage name Makaveli after reading Machiavelli's "The Prince."  图帕克·沙库尔在读了马基雅维利的《君主论》之后，取了艺名“马基雅维利”。 |  strategist, manipulator, schemer |  moralist, idealist | noun
