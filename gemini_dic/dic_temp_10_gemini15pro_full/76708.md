gambier:/ˈɡæmbiər/| n.|  gambier, 棕榈儿茶, 槟榔膏|A southeast Asian climbing plant (Uncaria gambir) whose leaves and twigs yield an extract containing catechu.  Also: the extract itself, used for tanning and dyeing and medicinally as an astringent.|Gambier is used in traditional medicine to treat diarrhea.|近义词：catechu|反义词:|n.
