milldam:/ˈmɪlˌdæm/ | n. | 水坝，磨坊坝 | A dam built to create a millpond, providing power for a watermill. | The old milldam had fallen into disrepair and was no longer holding back the water.  旧磨坊坝年久失修，再也挡不住水了。| synonyms: dam, barrage, weir | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "breach" if referring to the action of destroying a dam) | noun
