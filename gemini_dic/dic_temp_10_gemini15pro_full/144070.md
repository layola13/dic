outroom: /ˈaʊtruːm/
n.
户外房间，室外房间
An outroom is a room that is attached to the outside of a house, often used for storage or as a workshop.
The old tools were kept in the outroom. 旧工具都放在外面的房间里。
近义词: outhouse, shed, annex
反义词: main room, inside room
词性: 名词
