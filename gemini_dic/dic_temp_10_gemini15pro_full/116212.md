masochistically:/ˌmæsəˈkɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 受虐狂地；自我折磨地 |  In a way that suggests a deriving pleasure from one's own pain or humiliation. | He masochistically replayed the recording of his humiliating defeat. 他自我折磨般地反复播放他那场耻辱性失败的录音。 |  sadistically, perversely |  mercifully, kindly | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From masochist + -ically.  Masochist is from Leopold von Sacher-Masoch (1836–1895), Austrian novelist, whose novels described such behavior + -ist.
