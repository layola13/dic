depreciative:/dɪˈpriːʃiətɪv/ /dɪˈpriːʃətiv/
adjective
贬低的；轻视的；贬值的
Expressing disapproval; tending to diminish or disparage.
He made a depreciative remark about her work. 他对她的工作做了贬低的评论。

近义词:disparaging, derogatory, belittling, demeaning, negative
反义词:complimentary, praising, laudatory, appreciative, positive
词性:adjective

词源: From Late Latin *depreciativus, from depreciare "to lower the price of," from de- "down" + pretium "price."
