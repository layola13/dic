garba:/ˈɡɑːrbə/ (US), /ˈɡɑːrbɑː/ (UK)| n.| 加尔巴舞，一种古吉拉特邦的民间舞蹈|A folk dance and music style from Gujarat, India, often performed during the nine-day Navaratri festival.|We danced garba all night during Navaratri.  我们在九夜节期间整晚都跳加尔巴舞。| The vibrant music and colorful costumes make garba a truly festive experience.  充满活力的音乐和色彩缤纷的服装使加尔巴舞成为真正的节日体验。|Synonyms:  dance, folk dance|Antonyms: N/A|noun
