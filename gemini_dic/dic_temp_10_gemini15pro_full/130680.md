nonaffectingly:/ˌnɒnəˈfɛktɪŋli/ | adv. |  不矫揉造作地，自然地 |  In a way that is not intended to impress or produce an emotional effect; naturally or genuinely. | She spoke nonaffectingly about her experiences. 她自然地讲述了她的经历。 | He nonaffectingly expressed his gratitude. 他真诚地表达了他的感激之情。 | Synonyms: naturally, genuinely, sincerely, unpretentiously, artlessly | Antonyms: affectedly, artificially, pretentiously, theatrically | 词性: 副词
