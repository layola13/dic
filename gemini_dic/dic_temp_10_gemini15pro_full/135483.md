nonphilosophers:/ˌnɒn fɪˈlɒsəfəz/ /ˌnɑːn fɪˈlɑːsəfərz/
noun
非哲学家；非哲学家们
People who are not philosophers; those who do not study or practice philosophy.
Nonphilosophers often struggle with abstract concepts.  非哲学家们经常难以理解抽象概念。
Many nonphilosophers find metaphysics confusing. 许多非哲学家觉得形而上学令人困惑。
Synonyms:laypeople, non-specialists
Antonyms:philosophers, thinkers
n.
