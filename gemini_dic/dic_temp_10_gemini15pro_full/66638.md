existentialism:/ˌɛɡzɪˈstɛnʃəlɪzəm/
| n.|存在主义|A philosophical theory or approach that emphasizes the existence of the individual person as a free and responsible agent determining their own development through acts of the will.|例句：Existentialism emphasizes personal responsibility. 存在主义强调个人责任。|近义词：absurdism, nihilism|反义词:essentialism|词性:noun
