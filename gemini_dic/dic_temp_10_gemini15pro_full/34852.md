clinkering:/ˈklɪŋkərɪŋ/
n.|熔渣化，烧结|The process of forming clinkers, hard masses of fused material.  熔渣形成的过程，即熔融材料的硬块。|The clinkering of the ash caused problems in the furnace.  灰烬的熔渣化给熔炉带来了问题。|sintering, agglomeration|fusion, melting
clinker:/ˈklɪŋkər/
n.|熔渣，烧结块|A hard, stony residue formed during combustion.燃烧过程中形成的坚硬的石质残留物。|The blacksmith removed the clinker from the forge. 铁匠从锻炉中取出熔渣。|slag, cinder|ash, dust
clinker:/ˈklɪŋkər/
v. (intr., often foll by: together, up)|烧结，熔合|To become fused or solidified into clinkers.熔合或凝固成熔渣。|The bricks clinkered together in the intense heat.  砖块在高温下熔合在一起。|fuse, solidify|separate, crumble
