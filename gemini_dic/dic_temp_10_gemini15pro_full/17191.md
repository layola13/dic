bauhaus:/ˈbaʊˌhaʊs/| n. |包豪斯（设计学院）|A German art school operational from 1919 to 1933 that combined crafts and the fine arts, and was famous for the approach to design that it publicized and taught.  It is now regarded as one of the most important art schools of the 20th century.|The Bauhaus philosophy emphasized function over form. 包豪斯的哲学强调功能重于形式。|The new building's design was heavily influenced by the Bauhaus. 这座新建筑的设计深受包豪斯的影响。|近义词: school, academy, institution |反义词: None|noun
