infernal:/ɪnˈfɜːrnl/ (US) /ɪnˈfɜːnəl/ (UK)| adj.|地狱般的；恶魔般的；可恶的；讨厌的|Relating to hell or the underworld; extremely unpleasant or annoying.|The infernal heat made it impossible to work.  这地狱般的高温让人无法工作。|Synonyms: hellish, diabolical, fiendish, cursed, damnable|Antonyms: heavenly, celestial, divine|Adjective

infernal:/ɪnˈfɜːrnl/ (US) /ɪnˈfɜːnəl/ (UK)| n.|地狱的居民|An inhabitant of hell.|The poet described the infernals suffering in the underworld. 诗人描述了地狱居民在地狱中的苦难。|Synonyms: devil, demon, fiend|Antonyms: angel, saint|Noun (rarely used)
