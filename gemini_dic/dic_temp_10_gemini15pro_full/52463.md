dieselization:/ˌdiːzəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.|柴油化|The process of converting something to use diesel fuel, or the increased use of diesel engines.|The dieselization of the fishing fleet has led to significant cost savings. 渔船队的柴油化带来了巨大的成本节约。|Synonyms: conversion to diesel, adoption of diesel power|Antonyms: electrification, gasoline conversion|noun

dieselization:/ˌdiːzəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.|柴油机化|The conversion of an engine or vehicle to run on diesel fuel.|The dieselization of locomotives revolutionized the railroad industry.  机车的柴油机化彻底改变了铁路行业。|Synonyms: diesel conversion|Antonyms: electrification, gasoline conversion|noun
