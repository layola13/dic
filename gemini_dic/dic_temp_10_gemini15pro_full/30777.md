cestodean:/sɛsˈtoʊdiən/| adj. n.|绦虫的，绦虫纲动物|Relating to or belonging to the class Cestoda, a group of parasitic flatworms, commonly known as tapeworms.  Also, a member of this class.|例句：The cestodean parasite was identified in the dog's intestines.  这条狗的肠道里发现了绦虫寄生虫。

例句：The biologist specialized in the study of cestodeans.  这位生物学家专门研究绦虫。|近义词：tapeworm|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "host" in a biological context)|词性：adjective, noun

词源：From New Latin Cestoidea, from Greek kestos 'girdle' + -odea.
