pairing:/ˈpɛərɪŋ/| n.|配对，配对物，组合|The pairing of wine and cheese can be a delicate art.  葡萄酒和奶酪的搭配可以是一门精致的艺术。|例句：The pairing of the two players proved successful. 这两位运动员的组合证明是成功的。

The restaurant offers wine pairings with each dish. 这家餐厅每道菜都提供葡萄酒搭配。|近义词：matching, coupling, combination|反义词:separation, division|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From pair + -ing.  来自 pair（一对） + -ing（名词后缀）。
