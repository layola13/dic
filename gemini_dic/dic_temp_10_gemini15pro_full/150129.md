pamphleteered:/ˌpæmflɪˈtɪrd/
v.
写小册子；散发小册子
distributed or wrote pamphlets
He pamphleteered against the new law. 他散发小册子反对新法律。
近义词: propagandized, publicized
反义词: suppressed, censored
词性: 动词
