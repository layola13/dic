biomap:/ˈbaɪoʊˌmæp/ | n. |生物地图；生物分布图 | A visual representation showing the distribution of living organisms in a specific geographic area, often incorporating environmental data. |  Researchers created a biomap of the Amazon rainforest to track the impact of deforestation. 研究人员创建了一份亚马逊雨林的生物地图，以追踪森林砍伐的影响。| synonym: biological map, distribution map | antonym: N/A | noun

biomap:/ˈbaɪoʊˌmæp/ | n. |生物特征图谱 | A representation of an individual's biological characteristics, like fingerprints or DNA. |  The security system uses a biomap of each employee's retina for access. 该安全系统使用每位员工的视网膜生物特征图谱进行访问。 | synonym: biometric profile, biological profile | antonym: N/A | noun
