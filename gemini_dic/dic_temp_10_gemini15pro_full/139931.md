odium:/ˈoʊdiəm/ | n. | 憎恨，厌恶 | Intense hatred or dislike, especially toward a person or thing regarded as contemptible, despicable, or repugnant. | The politician was held in odium by the public after the scandal.  这位政客在丑闻曝光后遭到公众的憎恨。|  The odium he incurred made him a social outcast. 他招致的憎恨使他成了社会的弃儿。|Synonyms: abhorrence, loathing, detestation, aversion, antipathy | Antonyms: love, admiration, esteem, respect | noun
