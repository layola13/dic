nearctic:/nɪərˈɑːktɪk/ | adj. n. | 近北极区的；近北极区 | Relating to or denoting the biogeographic region covering most of North America, including Greenland and the highlands of Mexico, but excluding southern Florida. |  The Nearctic realm shares many animal and plant families with the Palearctic. 近北极区与古北极区共享许多动植物科。| The grizzly bear is a prominent species in the Nearctic region. 灰熊是近北极地区的一个重要物种。 | 近义词: Holarctic (partly) | 反义词: Neotropical, Palearctic | 词性: adjective, noun
