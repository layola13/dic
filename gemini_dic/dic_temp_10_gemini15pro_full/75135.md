friskingly:/ˈfrɪskɪŋli/
adverb
嬉戏地，活泼地
in a frisky manner; playfully.
The lambs were friskingly in the meadow. 羊羔在草地上嬉戏玩耍。
The puppies friskingly chased each other's tails. 小狗们互相追逐着尾巴嬉戏。
近义词:playfully, merrily, sportively
反义词:sluggishly, listlessly, apathetically
词性:adverb

词源:
From frisk + -ing + -ly.  Frisk comes from Middle French frisque "lively, brisk," of uncertain origin.
