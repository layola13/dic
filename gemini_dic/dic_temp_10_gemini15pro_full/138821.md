nuncius: /ˈnʌn.si.əs/ | n. | 使者，信使，报信者 | A messenger; one who announces or reports something. | The nuncius delivered the king's decree to the people. 使者向人民宣读了国王的法令。 | Synonyms: messenger, envoy, emissary, herald | Antonyms: recipient, receiver | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *nūntius*, from *nūntiāre* "to announce," related to *novus* "new."
