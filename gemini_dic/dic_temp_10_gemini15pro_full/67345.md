extender:/ɪkˈstɛndər/| n.|延长器，扩展器|A device or substance used to increase the length or duration of something.  It can also refer to a person who extends something.|例句：He used a range extender to boost the Wi-Fi signal. 他用了一个范围扩展器来增强Wi-Fi信号。

例句：The baker used a dough extender to make the loaf larger.  面包师用面团延长剂使面包更大。|近义词：lengthener, prolonger, expander|反义词:shortener, reducer|词性:noun

extender:/ɪkˈstɛndər/| n.|（证券）延期支付者|A person or entity who extends the payment period for a security.|例句：The bond extender agreed to postpone the maturity date. 债券延期支付者同意推迟到期日。

例句：The company acted as an extender for several investors. 公司为几个投资者担任了延期支付者的角色。|近义词：deferrer, postponer|反义词:collector, demander|词性:noun
