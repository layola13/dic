dragoon:/drəˈɡuːn/| n. v. |龙骑兵；强迫；骑兵|A soldier originally mounted on horseback but later also trained to fight on foot.  To persecute or coerce someone into doing something. |例句：The dragoons charged across the battlefield. 龙骑兵冲过战场。

例句：He was dragooned into helping with the move. 他被迫帮忙搬家。|近义词：cavalryman, trooper, coerce, compel, press, force|反义词: volunteer, release, liberate |词性:名词, 动词
