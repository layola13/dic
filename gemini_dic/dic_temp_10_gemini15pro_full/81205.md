grandmasterly:/ˌɡrændˈmɑːstərli/| adj.|大师级的，精湛的，出神入化的|Showing the skill or understanding of a grandmaster (a person of the highest rank in chess, or a person with great skill or knowledge of a particular activity or subject).|His performance on the piano was grandmasterly. 他在钢琴上的演奏堪称大师级。|Synonyms: masterful, expert, proficient, virtuoso, consummate, superb, excellent, brilliant|Antonyms: amateurish, unskilled, incompetent, clumsy, inept|adjective

grandmasterly:/ˌɡrændˈmɑːstərli/| adv.|大师地，精湛地，出神入化地|In a grandmasterly manner.  This usage is less common than the adjective form.|He played the piece grandmasterly. 他以大师级的水准演奏了这首曲子。|Synonyms: masterfully, expertly, proficiently, consummately, superbly, excellently, brilliantly|Antonyms: amateurishly, unskillfully, incompetently, clumsily, ineptly|adverb
