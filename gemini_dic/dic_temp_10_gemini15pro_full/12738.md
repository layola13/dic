asseverative:/əˈsɜːvərətɪv/
adjective
断言的，肯定的
Characterized by or expressing positive assertion; affirming confidently.
He spoke in an asseverative tone, leaving no room for doubt. 他以断言的语气说话，不容置疑。
近义词：affirmative, positive, emphatic, declaratory
反义词：hesitant, uncertain, doubtful, questioning
词性：adjective

asseveration:/əˌsɛvəˈreɪʃən/
noun
断言，肯定
A positive assertion; an emphatic affirmation.
The defendant's asseveration of innocence was not believed by the jury. 被告的无罪断言没有被陪审团相信。
近义词：affirmation, declaration, assertion, avowal
反义词：denial, retraction, disavowal
词性：noun

词源：From Late Latin *asseverativus, from asseverare "to assert earnestly," from ad- "to" + severus "serious."
