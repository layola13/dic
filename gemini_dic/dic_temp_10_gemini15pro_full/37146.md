come-to-a-head:/kʌm tuː ə hɛd/
verb phrase
达到高潮；到达紧要关头；爆发
reach a crisis or critical point
The situation came to a head when the workers went on strike.  情况在工人罢工时达到了顶点。
After weeks of escalating tension, the conflict finally came to a head.  经过数周的紧张局势升级，冲突终于爆发了。
近义词:culminate, climax, reach a crisis
反义词:subside, diminish, de-escalate
词性:动词短语
