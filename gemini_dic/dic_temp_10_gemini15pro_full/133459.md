nonfeasibilities:/ˌnɒnˌfiːzəˈbɪlətiz/
noun
不可行性；不可行的事项
The report highlighted the nonfeasibilities of the proposed project due to budget constraints. 这份报告强调了由于预算限制，拟议项目不可行。
The nonfeasibilities of implementing the plan became apparent after careful consideration.  经过仔细考虑，实施该计划的不可行性变得显而易见。
Synonym: impracticalities, impossibilities
Antonym: feasibilities, possibilities
n.
