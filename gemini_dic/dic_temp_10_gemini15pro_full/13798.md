audiophile:/ˈɔːdiəʊfaɪl/| n.|发烧友；音响发烧友|A person who is enthusiastic about high-fidelity sound reproduction.|He's a true audiophile, with a collection of vintage speakers and amplifiers. 他是一个真正的音响发烧友，收藏了大量的古董扬声器和放大器。|近义词：music lover, hi-fi enthusiast|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps someone indifferent to sound quality)|词性:noun
