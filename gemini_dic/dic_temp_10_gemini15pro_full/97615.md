inharmonic:/ˌɪnhɑːrˈmɒnɪk/ | adj. |不和谐的，非谐的|Lacking harmony; discordant.  Not consisting of simple multiples of a fundamental frequency.|The inharmonic sounds of the untuned piano made her cringe.|Synonyms: discordant, dissonant, jarring, cacophonous|Antonyms: harmonic, consonant, euphonious|adjective

inharmonicity:/ˌɪnhɑːrˈmɒnɪsɪti/ | n. |不和谐性，非谐性|The state or quality of being inharmonic.|The inharmonicity of the instrument made it difficult to play in tune.|Synonyms: discordance, dissonance|Antonyms: harmony, consonance|noun
