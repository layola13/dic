agonizers:/ˈæɡəˌnaɪzərz/| n. |痛苦者，折磨者|People who cause agony or intense suffering.  Those who struggle with difficult decisions or problems.|The agonizers paced the floor, wrestling with the impossible dilemma.|近义词：tormentors, sufferers, worriers|反义词:comforters, soothers|noun


agonize:/ˈæɡəˌnaɪz/| v. | (使)痛苦，(使)苦恼，挣扎|To suffer intense mental or physical pain; to struggle with a difficult decision or problem. |She agonized over whether to accept the job offer.|近义词：suffer, struggle, worry|反义词:rejoice, celebrate|verb
