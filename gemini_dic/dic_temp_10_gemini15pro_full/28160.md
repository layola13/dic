capo:/ˈkɑːpoʊ/ (US), /ˈkæpoʊ/ (UK)| n.|变调夹，弦枕|A device used on stringed instruments, especially guitars and banjos, to raise the pitch of all the strings by shortening their effective vibrating length.|He used a capo on the third fret to play the song in a different key. 他在第三品使用变调夹来以不同的调演奏这首歌。|Synonyms: clamp, mute|Antonyms: N/A|noun
