hunky-dory:/ˌhʌŋkiˈdɔːri/ | adj. | 惬意的，一切顺利的 |  Satisfactory and pleasant; in good condition.  Origin uncertain, possibly from a Japanese place name or a Dutch or English slang term.  First recorded in the mid-19th century. | Everything is hunky-dory at work. 工作上一切顺利。|  近义词: fine, good, excellent, satisfactory, pleasant, agreeable, peachy, swell | 反义词: unsatisfactory, unpleasant, bad, awful, terrible | 词性: adjective
