laurates:/ˈlɔːreɪts/ /ˈlɑːreɪts/| n.|月桂酸盐；月桂酸酯|Salts or esters of lauric acid, a saturated fatty acid.  They are commonly used in soaps, shampoos, and other personal care products.|Sodium laurate is a common ingredient in soap. 月桂酸钠是肥皂中的常见成分。

Potassium laurate is used in some shampoos. 月桂酸钾用于一些洗发水中。|近义词: dodecanoates (more technical)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
