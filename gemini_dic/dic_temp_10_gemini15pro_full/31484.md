charmed:/tʃɑːmd/ | adj. | 着迷的；被施魔法的；快乐的 |  Pleasantly attracted or delighted; fascinated; under a magic spell. |  I was charmed by her beauty and wit. 我被她的美貌和智慧迷住了。 | synonyms: captivated, delighted, enchanted, fascinated | antonyms: repulsed, disgusted, disenchanted | adjective

charmed:/tʃɑːmd/ | adj. | 幸运的 | Protected from harm by magic; lucky. | He led a charmed life, always escaping danger. 他过着幸运的生活，总是能逃脱危险。 | synonyms: fortunate, lucky, blessed | antonyms: unfortunate, unlucky, cursed | adjective
