flimsily:/ˈflɪmzɪli/| adv.|脆弱地；不牢固地；轻薄地；浅薄地|in a flimsy or unsubstantial way; weakly; superficially.|The tent was flimsily constructed and collapsed in the wind.  帐篷搭建得很单薄，被风吹倒了。|The evidence against him was rather flimsy. 对他不利的证据相当薄弱。|近义词：weakly, thinly, slightly, superficially|反义词：sturdily, strongly, robustly|词性: adverb

flimsy:/ˈflɪmzi/| adj.|脆弱的；不牢固的；轻薄的；浅薄的|easily broken or damaged; not strong or solid; (of a pretext or argument) weak and unconvincing.|She wore a flimsy summer dress. 她穿着一件薄薄的夏装。|He offered a flimsy excuse for his absence. 他为自己的缺席找了个站不住脚的借口。|近义词：fragile, weak, thin, slight, insubstantial, tenuous|反义词：strong, sturdy, robust, substantial|词性: adjective
