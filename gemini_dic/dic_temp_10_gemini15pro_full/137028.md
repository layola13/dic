nonstationaries:/ˌnɒnˈsteɪʃənəriːz/ | n. | 非平稳性，非平稳过程 |  Refers to data or processes that do not have a constant statistical distribution over time, meaning their statistical properties like mean and variance change.  Often used in the plural to refer to multiple nonstationary time series. | The analysis of nonstationaries in financial markets is crucial for accurate forecasting.  金融市场中非平稳性的分析对于准确预测至关重要。 | Synonyms: non-stationarity (singular), volatile processes, unstable processes | Antonyms: stationarity, stationary processes | noun

nonstationary:/ˌnɒnˈsteɪʃənəri/ | adj. | 非平稳的 | Describes a time series or process whose statistical properties change over time. | The economist identified a nonstationary trend in the housing market data.  经济学家从房地产市场数据中发现了一个非平稳的趋势。 | Synonyms: volatile, unstable, fluctuating | Antonyms: stationary, stable, constant | adjective
