aureola:/ɔːˈriːələ/ /ɔˈriːələ/ | n. | 光环，晕轮，圣像上的光环 |A bright circle of light; a halo appearing around the sun or moon or a luminous area surrounding a religious figure in a painting.| The painting depicted the saint with a golden aureola around her head. 这幅画描绘了这位圣人，她的头部周围有一个金色的光环。| 近义词:halo, nimbus, corona, aura | 反义词:None | Noun
