noncontemptibilities:/ˌnɒnkənˌtɛmptɪˈbɪlɪtiz/
noun
不可轻视性；不鄙视性；不蔑视性
The state or quality of not being contemptible; the fact of not deserving contempt.  This word is rarely used.
Due to their bravery and resilience, the noncontemptibilities of the firefighters were evident to all. 由于他们的勇敢和韧性，消防员的不可轻视性对所有人来说都是显而易见的。
Near synonyms:  respectability, admirability, worthiness
Near antonyms:  contemptibility, despicability, worthlessness
