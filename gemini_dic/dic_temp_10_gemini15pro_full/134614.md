nonlustrous:/ˌnɑːnˈlʌstrəs/| adj.|无光泽的，暗淡的|Not shiny; dull.  Lacking luster or brightness.|The nonlustrous surface of the rock indicated it was not a precious gemstone. 这块岩石黯淡无光的表面表明它不是一颗珍贵的宝石。|Synonyms: dull, matte, lackluster, tarnished|Antonyms: lustrous, shiny, glossy, gleaming|adjective
