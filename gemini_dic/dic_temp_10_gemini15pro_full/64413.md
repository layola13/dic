ergograph:/ˈɜːrɡəɡrɑːf/
n.
测力计，握力计
An instrument for measuring and recording the work done by a group of muscles.
The ergograph measured the contractions of his finger muscles.  测力计测量了他手指肌肉的收缩。
synonyms: dynamometer, myograph
antonyms: None. (It's a specific instrument; there isn't a direct opposite.)
noun
