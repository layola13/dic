neglective:/nɪˈɡlɛktɪv/| adj.| 疏忽的，忽略的 | Habitually neglecting duties or responsibilities; characterized by neglect.  Showing neglect. |  She was neglective in her duties as a mother. 她作为母亲疏忽了她的职责。 |  The neglective landlord allowed the building to fall into disrepair.  这位疏忽的房东任由房屋破败不堪。 | negligent, remiss, careless, inattentive | attentive, careful, diligent, responsible | adjective
