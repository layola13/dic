nonfunctional:/ˌnɒnˈfʌŋkʃənəl/ /ˌnɑːnˈfʌŋkʃənəl/
adjective
无功能的；不工作的；不运行的
Not performing or able to perform its regular function.
The old radio is nonfunctional.  旧收音机坏了。
non-working, inoperative, broken, unusable, out of order
functional, working, operational
adjective
