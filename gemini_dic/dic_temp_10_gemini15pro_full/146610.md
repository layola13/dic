overground:/ˈoʊvərɡraʊnd/ (US), /ˈəʊvəɡraʊnd/ (UK)| adj. adv. n.|地上铁路，地面交通|A railway system that is above ground level.|The London Overground connects various parts of the city. 伦敦地上铁路连接着城市的各个地区。|近义词：surface railway|反义词:underground, subway|词性:名词


词源:  'Over' (above) + 'ground' (the surface of the earth).  The word emerged in the late 19th century, initially primarily in a technical context relating to mining and later broadening to general usage.
