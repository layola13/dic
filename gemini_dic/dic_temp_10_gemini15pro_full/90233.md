house-broken:/ˈhaʊsˌbroʊkən/
adjective
驯养好的；习惯于室内大小便的
trained to urinate and defecate outdoors or in a designated area inside, typically used for pets.
My dog is completely house-broken and never has accidents inside. 我的狗完全驯养好了，从不在屋里大小便。
The new puppy isn't house-broken yet and still needs training. 这只新的小狗还没有驯养好，还需要训练。
synonyms: house-trained, potty-trained
antonyms: untrained, not house-trained
词性: adjective
