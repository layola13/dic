alliances:/əˈlaɪənsɪz/
n.
联盟，同盟；结盟，联姻
a union or association formed for mutual benefit, especially between countries or organizations.
例句：The two countries formed a military alliance. 这两个国家结成了军事同盟。

例句：The company has formed several strategic alliances with other businesses.  这家公司已经与其他企业建立了几项战略联盟。

近义词：coalition, partnership, league, union, federation
反义词：division, separation, disunity, rivalry

词性: 名词 (plural)
