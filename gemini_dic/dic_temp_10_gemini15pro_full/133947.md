词源:  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not". "Heathen" comes from Old English "hæðen" meaning "inhabitant of uncultivated land," later associated with those not adhering to a major religion, particularly Christianity. Therefore, "nonheathen" refers to someone who is not a heathen.
