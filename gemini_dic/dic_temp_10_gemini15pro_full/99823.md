intermingled:/ˌɪntərˈmɪŋɡld/| adj. v. (past tense & past participle)|混合的，交织的|mixed or mingled together|例句：Her laughter was intermingled with tears. 她的笑声夹杂着泪水。|近义词：interwoven, intertwined|反义词：segregated, disentangled|词性:形容词/动词

词源: From inter- + mingle.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," and "mingle" means to mix or combine.
