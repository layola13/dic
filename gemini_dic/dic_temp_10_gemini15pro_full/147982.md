overrewarding:/ˌoʊvər rɪˈwɔːrdɪŋ/
adjective
过度奖励的；回报过高的
giving too much reward; more rewarding than is justified or deserved.
The job is overrewarding considering how little work is involved.  这份工作考虑到涉及的工作量很少，回报过高。
He felt overrewarded for his contribution to the project. 他觉得他对这个项目的贡献得到了过多的回报。
近义词: excessive, exorbitant, lavish
反义词: underrewarding, inadequate
词性: adjective
