asilus:/əˈsaɪləs/| n. |盜蠅|A predatory fly of the family Asilidae, also called a robber fly.  These flies are known for their aggressive hunting behavior and are often seen perched on vegetation, waiting to ambush other insects.| The asilus snatched a bee mid-flight. 盜蠅在半空中抓到一隻蜜蜂。|近义词：robber fly|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider prey insects like houseflies, bees)|noun
