ethiopianism:/ˌiːθiˈoʊpiənɪzəm/
n.
埃塞俄比亚主义;泛非主义;黑人民族主义
A political and cultural movement emphasizing the unity and empowerment of people of African descent worldwide.  It often draws inspiration from the history and culture of Ethiopia, which was one of the few African countries to remain independent during the period of European colonization.
Ethiopia has long been a symbol of independence and resistance to colonial rule, inspiring pan-Africanist and black liberation movements across the globe. 埃塞俄比亚长期以来一直是独立和抵抗殖民统治的象征，激励着全球泛非主义和黑人解放运动。

The rise of Ethiopianism in the late 19th and early 20th centuries played a significant role in the anti-colonial struggles across Africa. 19世纪末20世纪初埃塞俄比亚主义的兴起在整个非洲的反殖民斗争中发挥了重要作用。

近义词:Pan-Africanism, Black Nationalism
反义词:Colonialism, Imperialism
词性:noun
