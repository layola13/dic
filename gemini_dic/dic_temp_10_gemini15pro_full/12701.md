assertional:/əˈsɜːʃənəl/
adjective
断言的，断定的
Of, relating to, or characterized by assertion.  Making a statement as if it were true.
例句：His assertional tone made it clear he wasn't open to debate. 他断言的语气表明他不想辩论。

例句：The assertional nature of the claim required strong evidence.  这项声明的断言性质需要强有力的证据。

近义词：affirmative, declarative, positive
反义词：questioning, tentative, hesitant
词性: adjective
