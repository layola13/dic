outslides:/ˈaʊtslaɪdz/
verb
(第三人称单数) 超越滑行; 滑出…之外
surpasses in sliding; slides out beyond something else.
The experienced skier outslides the beginner on the steep slope.  经验丰富的滑雪者在陡峭的斜坡上滑得比初学者快。
The car outslides the guardrail and plunges down the embankment. 汽车滑出护栏，坠入路堤。
近义词: outpaces, surpasses, overtakes
反义词: lags behind, falls behind
词性: v.


词源:  A combination of "out-" (meaning "beyond" or "exceeding") and "slides" (from the verb "to slide").
