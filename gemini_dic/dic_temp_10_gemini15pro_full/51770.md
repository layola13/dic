devotional:/dɪˈvoʊʃənəl/ | adj. n. | 虔诚的；祈祷的；灵修的；祈祷式；灵修书 |  Relating to or used in religious worship; characterized by or expressing devotion. A short worship service, especially one held at home. | She reads a devotional every morning. 她每天早上读灵修书籍。 | Near synonyms: devout, pious, religious, prayerful, reverent | Near antonyms: irreligious, impious, profane, irreverent | adjective

devotional:/dɪˈvoʊʃənəl/ | n. | 祈祷式；灵修书 | A short worship service, especially one held at home; a book of prayers or meditations for private use. | The pastor published a book of devotionals. 牧师出版了一本灵修书籍。 | Near synonyms: prayer book, hymnal | Near antonyms:  (No direct antonym for the noun form) | noun
