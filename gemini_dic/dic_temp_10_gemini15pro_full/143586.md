outhunt:/ˌaʊtˈhʌnt/
verb
胜过捕猎；在狩猎中胜过
to hunt more successfully than someone else
例句：He outhunted his rivals and brought home the biggest deer. 他比对手捕猎到更多猎物，带回了最大的鹿。

例句：She consistently outhunts her brother, much to his chagrin. 她总是比她哥哥捕猎到更多猎物，这让他很懊恼。

近义词：outdo, surpass, excel
反义词：be outdone, be surpassed


词性：v.
