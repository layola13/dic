assiduously:/əˈsɪdjuəsli/
| adv. |勤奋地，刻苦地|With constant and careful effort; diligently.|She worked assiduously on her project, determined to finish it on time. 她勤奋地做她的项目，决心按时完成。|近义词：diligently, industriously, painstakingly, tirelessly|反义词：lazily, carelessly, negligently|词性:副词
Etymology: From Latin *assiduus* (sitting close to, unremitting, constant) < *assidēre* (to sit near to, sit by, attend constantly) < *ad-* (to, near) + *sedēre* (to sit).
