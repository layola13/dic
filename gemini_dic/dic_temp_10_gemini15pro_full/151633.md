particolored:/ˌpɑːrtɪˈkʌlərd/ | adj. | 多彩的，杂色的 | Having various colours; variegated.  | The jester wore a particolored outfit. 小丑穿着一件五颜六色的衣服。 | The particolored wings of the butterfly were stunning. 蝴蝶色彩斑斓的翅膀令人惊叹。 | 近义词: multicolored, polychromatic, variegated | 反义词: monochrome, monochromatic | 词性: adjective

词源:
From "parti" (partly) + "colored".  "Parti" comes from the Old French "parti" meaning "divided, shared," ultimately from Latin "partīre" meaning "to divide, share."
