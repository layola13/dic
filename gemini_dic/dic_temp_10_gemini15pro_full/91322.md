hydrophane:/ˈhaɪdrəfeɪn/| n. |水胆矾，水蛋白石|A translucent opal that becomes transparent or more translucent when immersed in water.|The museum displayed a rare hydrophane that shimmered when submerged.|Near synonyms: opal, hyalite|Antonym: None specific to this mineral, perhaps opaque stone|noun
