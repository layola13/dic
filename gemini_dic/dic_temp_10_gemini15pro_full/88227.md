hindgut:/ˈhaɪndɡʌt/| n. |后肠|The hindgut is the posterior part of the alimentary canal.  后肠是消化道的后部。|The hindgut of insects plays a crucial role in water reabsorption. 昆虫的后肠在水分重吸收中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词:rectum, large intestine|反义词:foregut|名词
