muskellunge:/ˈmʌskəlʌndʒ/ (US), /ˈmʌskɪlʌndʒ/ (UK)| n. | 麝香鱼 | A large freshwater fish of North America, of the pike family, prized as a game fish. |例句：The angler landed a huge muskie, a true trophy muscallonge.  这位钓鱼者钓到了一条巨大的麝香鱼，这是一条真正的战利品麝香鱼。

例句：Muscallonge are apex predators in their freshwater ecosystems. 麝香鱼在其淡水生态系统中是顶级掠食者。|近义词：muskie, muskellunge, great pike |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

etymology: From Ojibwe language *maashkinoozhe*  "ugly pike" from *maashk* "ugly, deformed" + *kinoozhe* "pike".
