diamante:/ˌdiːəˈmænti/ /ˌdaɪəˈmɑːnteɪ/ | n. |钻石；金刚石；菱形 |A very hard, valuable stone that is used in jewellery and also in industry for cutting hard things. Also, a shape with four straight sides of equal length, forming two opposite acute angles and two opposite obtuse angles (a rhombus).| She wore a diamante necklace. 她戴着一条钻石项链。 The pattern was made up of small diamantes.  这个图案由小菱形组成。| 近义词：diamond, brilliant, rhinestone | 反义词: - | noun
