concentric:/kənˈsɛntrɪk/| adj.|同心的，共中心的|Having a common center, as circles or spheres.|The target was a series of concentric circles.  靶子是一系列同心圆。|synonyms: coaxial, homocentric|antonyms: eccentric, off-center|adjective

concentrical:/kənˈsɛntrɪkəl/| adj.|同心的，共中心的|A less common variant of "concentric."  It carries the same meaning.|The architect designed a building with concentrical arches.  建筑师设计了一座带有同心拱门的建筑。|synonyms: coaxial, homocentric|antonyms: eccentric, off-center|adjective
