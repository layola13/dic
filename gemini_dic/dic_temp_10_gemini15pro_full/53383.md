disagreeably:/dɪsəˈɡriːəbli/ | adv. | 令人不愉快地，讨厌地 | in a manner that is unpleasant or offensive | The man coughed disagreeably throughout the concert.  那个男人在整个音乐会期间都令人讨厌地咳嗽。|  unpleasantly, offensively, nastily | pleasantly, agreeably | adverb

Etymology:
From dis- (expressing negation) + agreeable + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Agreeable" ultimately derives from Latin "agere" (to do, act) via French "agréable".
