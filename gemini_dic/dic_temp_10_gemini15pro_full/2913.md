advisedly:/ədˈvaɪzɪdli/| adv.|深思熟虑地，故意地|After much deliberation, she acted advisedly.|She advisedly chose to remain silent.|近义词：deliberately, purposely, intentionally, knowingly|反义词：unintentionally, accidentally, thoughtlessly, rashly|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From advise + -ed + -ly.  "Advise" comes from Old French "aviser," meaning "to consider, reflect."  The "-ed" suffix creates the past participle form, which can function as an adjective.  The "-ly" suffix transforms the adjective into an adverb.

USAGE->
She advisedly chose not to comment on the situation. = 她有意地选择不对情况发表评论。

He advisedly invested his money in a diverse portfolio. = 他经过深思熟虑后将资金投资于多元化的投资组合。
