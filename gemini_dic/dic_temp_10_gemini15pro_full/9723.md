antiuniversity:/ˌæntiˌjuːnɪˈvɜːrsəti/
n.|反大学，另类大学|An institution offering unconventional or experimental courses and workshops, often outside the traditional academic structure, focused on radical, experimental, or practical learning.|例句：The antiuniversity offered courses in activism and community organizing. 反大学开设了激进主义和社区组织的课程。

例句：Many artists and activists found a home at the antiuniversity, exploring creative expression and social change. 许多艺术家和活动家在反大学找到了归宿，探索创造性表达和社会变革。|近义词：free school, alternative school, open university|反义词：university, college|词性：名词
