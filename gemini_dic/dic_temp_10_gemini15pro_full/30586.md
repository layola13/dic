cercopod:/ˈsɜːrkəpɒd/| n. |尾附器；尾肢；尾节|A cercopod is one of a pair of appendages at the end of the abdomen in some arthropods, such as springtails.|Springtails use their cercopods for jumping. 弹尾虫用它们的尾节跳跃。|近义词: appendage, furcula |反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps body/trunk) |词性:noun
