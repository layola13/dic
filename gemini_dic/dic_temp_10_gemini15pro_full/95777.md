incontinence: /ɪnkɒntɪnəns/ (British) /ˌɪnkɑːntɪnəns/ (American) | n. | 失禁，不能自制 | inability to control urination or defecation |  She suffers from incontinence after the surgery.  她手术后患有失禁。| synonyms: inability to hold, lack of control, involuntary urination/defecation | antonyms: continence, self-control | noun

incontinent: /ɪnkɒntɪnənt/ (British) /ˌɪnkɑːntɪnənt/ (American) | adj. | 失禁的，不能自制的 |  unable to control urination or defecation; lacking self-restraint | He was incontinent after the stroke. 他中风后失禁了。 | synonyms: uncontrolled, unrestrained, immoderate | antonyms: continent, restrained, moderate | adjective
