dockyard: /ˈdɒkjɑːd/ (UK) /ˈdɑːkjɑːrd/ (US)| n.|船坞，造船厂|An area with docks and equipment for building and repairing ships.|The dockyard was busy with activity as workers prepared the ship for its maiden voyage. 造船厂里一片繁忙，工人们正准备着这艘船的首航。|近义词: shipyard, naval yard|反义词: None|词性: noun

Etymology:
From *dock* (an enclosed area of water in a port for loading, unloading, and repair of ships) + *yard* (an enclosed area of ground).  The word *dock* ultimately comes from Middle Dutch *docke*, probably related to *dok* meaning "cage, enclosed space."
