interlabeling:/ˌɪntərˈleɪbəlɪŋ/
n.
相互标记，互相标注
The process of assigning labels to each other within a group or between groups.
Researchers used interlabeling to understand how individuals in a group perceived each other's roles. 研究人员使用相互标记来理解群体中个人如何看待彼此的角色。
labeling, tagging, marking
