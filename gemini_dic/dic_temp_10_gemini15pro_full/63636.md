一般现在时 (Simple Present):
An eon represents a vast expanse of time. 一个宙代表着一段漫长的时间。
Geologists use eons to divide Earth's history. 地质学家使用宙来划分地球的历史。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
The Earth is constantly changing, even over eons.  地球在不断变化，即使是跨越数个宙。
Scientists are studying rock formations from past eons. 科学家正在研究来自过去宙的岩层。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Life has evolved significantly over eons.  生命在数个宙中发生了显著的进化。
The Earth has experienced numerous ice ages during past eons. 地球在过去的数个宙中经历了无数的冰河时代。

The continents have been drifting for eons.  大陆一直在漂移了亿万年。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
The dinosaurs lived during the Mesozoic eon. 恐龙生活在中生代。
The first eon in Earth's history was the Hadean eon. 地球历史上的第一个宙是冥古宙。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
The Earth was cooling during the early eons. 在早期宙，地球正在冷却。
Volcanoes were erupting frequently during the Archean eon. 在太古宙，火山频繁喷发。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
Life had not yet evolved during the Hadean eon. 在冥古宙时期，生命还没有进化。
The Earth had already formed by the beginning of the Archean eon. 到太古宙开始时，地球已经形成了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
The Earth had been cooling for eons before life appeared. 在生命出现之前，地球已经冷却了亿万年。
The solar system had been forming for eons before the Earth existed. 在地球存在之前，太阳系已经形成了亿万年。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
Future eons will likely see further changes to the Earth's climate. 未来的亿万年里，地球的气候可能会发生进一步的变化。
Scientists will continue to study past eons to understand Earth's history. 科学家们将继续研究过去的亿万年，以了解地球的历史。

Scientists will be studying the effects of this change for eons to come.  科学家们将在未来的亿万年里研究这种变化的影响。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By the end of the next eon, the Earth may have become uninhabitable. 到下一个宙结束时，地球可能已经变得不适宜居住。
Scientists will have learned much more about the universe by then. 到那时，科学家们将对宇宙有了更多的了解。
