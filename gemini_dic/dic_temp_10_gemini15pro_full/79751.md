glumnesses:/ˈɡlʌmnɪsɪz/
noun
闷闷不乐，忧郁，沮丧
A state of being glum; sullenness or gloominess.
The glumnesses of the long winter nights were starting to affect everyone's mood. 漫长冬夜的沉闷开始影响每个人的心情。
Synonyms: dejection, depression, despondency, gloom, melancholy, sadness
Antonyms: cheerfulness, happiness, joy, lightheartedness
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From glum + -ness.  "Glum" originates from the Middle Low German or Middle Dutch word "glummen," meaning "to stare sullenly."
