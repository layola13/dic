fibulae:/ˈfɪbjʊliː/
n.
腓骨（复数）
The slender outer bone of the lower leg, parallel to the tibia.
The doctor pointed to the X-ray and showed me where the fibulae were fractured. 医生指着X光片，告诉我腓骨骨折的位置。

fibulae:/ˈfɪbjʊliː/
n.
（古代）别针，扣针 (复数)
A clasp or brooch used in antiquity.
The archaeologist unearthed several fibulae crafted from bronze. 考古学家挖掘出几枚青铜制成的别针。


近义词：(针对骨骼) calf bone (小腿骨),  (针对扣针) brooch, clasp, pin
反义词：(无直接反义词，针对骨骼可以考虑 tibia 胫骨, 作为相对的骨骼)
词性: noun
