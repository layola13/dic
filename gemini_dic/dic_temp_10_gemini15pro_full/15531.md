bagatelle:/ˌbæɡəˈtɛl/ | n. | 小事；琐事；不值钱的东西；（尤指）短小精巧的乐曲 | A bagatelle is something that is unimportant or of little value, especially a short, light piece of music. |  She dismissed his concerns as mere bagatelles. 她认为他的担忧只是些琐事。| synonyms: trifle, triviality, trinket, knick-knack | antonyms: essential, necessity, important matter | noun

bagatelles:/ˌbæɡəˈtɛlz/ | n. | 小事；琐事；不值钱的东西；（尤指）短小精巧的乐曲（bagatelle的复数）|  Multiple bagatelles occupied his time. 许多琐事占据了他的时间。| synonyms: trifles, trivialities, trinkets, knick-knacks | antonyms: essentials, necessities, important matters | noun
