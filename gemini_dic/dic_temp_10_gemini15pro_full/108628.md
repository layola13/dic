leading-edge:/ˌliːdɪŋ ˈedʒ/| adj.| 领先的；尖端的；前沿的 | At the very leading edge of scientific discovery.  在科学发现的最前沿。|例句：The company is at the leading edge of mobile technology. 这家公司处于移动技术的前沿。|近义词：cutting-edge, advanced, innovative, pioneering, state-of-the-art|反义词：outdated, obsolete, backward, lagging|词性:形容词


leading-edge:/ˌliːdɪŋ ˈedʒ/| n.| 前沿；领先地位 |  Maintaining a leading edge in the market.  保持在市场上的领先地位。|例句：They are determined to maintain their leading edge in the industry. 他们决心保持在行业中的领先地位。|近义词：forefront, vanguard, cutting edge|反义词：rear, backwater|词性:名词
