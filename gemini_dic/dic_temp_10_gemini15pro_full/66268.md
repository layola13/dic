exclusivistic:/ɪkˌskluːsɪˈvɪstɪk/
adj.
排外的；独有的；势利的
Catering or pertaining to an exclusive group or class; snobbish.  Characterized by exclusiveness or snobbery.
She belongs to an exclusivistic country club that doesn't allow just anyone to join. 她属于一个排外的乡村俱乐部，不是谁想加入就能加入的。
The artist cultivated an exclusivistic image, rarely giving interviews or appearing in public.  这位艺术家刻意营造了一种高冷的形象，很少接受采访或公开露面。
近义词:exclusive, snobbish, elitist, restrictive
反义词:inclusive, open, egalitarian, democratic
词性:adjective
