extrorse:/ˈɛkstrɔːrs/| adj.|外向的，向外弯曲的|Directed or turned outwards, especially away from the axis.  This term is primarily used in botany to describe the direction of anthers or other floral parts.|The extrorse anthers of the flower released pollen outwards.  这朵花外向的花药向外释放花粉。|Near Synonyms: outward, extroverted (for behavior),  excurved|Antonyms: introrse, introverted (for behavior), incurved|Adjective
