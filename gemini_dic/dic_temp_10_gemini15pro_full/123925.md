moreover:/mɔːrˈoʊvər/
adverb
此外，而且
in addition to what has already been said; further; besides.
Moreover, the price is very reasonable. 此外，价格也很合理。
The food is excellent; moreover, the service is outstanding. 食物很棒；而且，服务也很出色。
Synonyms: furthermore, besides, also, in addition, additionally
Antonyms: however, nevertheless, on the contrary, conversely
词性: 副词
