coveter:/ˈkʌvɪtər/| n.|贪婪者，觊觎者|Someone who covets something desires it intensely, often something that belongs to another person.|例句：She was a coveter of her neighbor's possessions. 她觊觎邻居的财产。

例句：The coveter eyed the expensive sports car with envy.  这个贪婪的人羡慕地盯着那辆昂贵的跑车。


|近义词：envier, desirer, yearner|反义词:philanthropist, altruist|词性:noun


covet:/ˈkʌvɪt/| vt.|贪图，觊觎|To covet something is to yearn to possess or have something.|例句：He coveted his brother's new car. 他贪图他哥哥的新车。

例句：She coveted the prestigious award. 她渴望获得那个久负盛名的奖项。


|近义词：desire, crave, envy|反义词:disdain, despise|词性:verb
