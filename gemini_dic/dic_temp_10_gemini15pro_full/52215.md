diathermanism:/ˌdaɪəˈθɜːrmənɪzəm/| n.|透热性；热穿透性|The property of transmitting radiant heat; transparency to heat rays.  The ability of a substance to transmit infrared radiation.|Rock salt exhibits high diathermanism. 岩盐表现出很高的透热性。|Synonyms:  transmissivity, diathermancy|Antonyms:  athermanism, opacity to heat|noun
