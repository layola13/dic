calendar:/ˈkælɪndər/ (US), /ˈkælɪndə(r)/ (UK)| n.|日历；日程表|A system for organizing days into weeks, months, and years; a chart showing this system.|I marked the date on my calendar. 我在我的日历上标记了日期。|synonyms: schedule, timetable, almanac|antonyms: N/A|noun

calendar:/ˈkælɪndər/ (US), /ˈkælɪndə(r)/ (UK)| v.|安排；预定|To schedule an event or activity.|The meeting is calendared for next Tuesday. 会议安排在下周二。|synonyms: schedule, book, arrange|antonyms: cancel, postpone|verb
