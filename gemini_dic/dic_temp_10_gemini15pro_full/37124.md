combustive:/kəmˈbʌstɪv/ | adj. | 易燃的；燃烧性的；易激动的 |  Relating to or used in combustion; easily aroused or excited. | The warehouse stored highly combustive materials.  仓库储存了高度易燃的材料。|  inflammable, flammable, incendiary | nonflammable, fire-retardant | adjective

combustible:/kəmˈbʌstɪbl/ | n. | 易燃物；可燃物 | A substance that can be burned. | Gasoline is a highly combustible liquid. 汽油是一种高度易燃的液体。| fuel, flammable material | fire retardant | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *combustivus, from Latin combustus, past participle of comburere "to burn up," from com- "completely" + burere "to burn."
