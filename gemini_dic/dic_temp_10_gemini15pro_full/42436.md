counterdefense:/ˈkaʊntər dɪˈfɛns/| n.|反防御，对抗防御|An action or actions taken to oppose or nullify an enemy's defense or attack.|The general planned a swift counterdefense to the enemy's surprise offensive. 将军计划迅速反击敌人的突然袭击。|Synonyms: counterattack, counteroffensive, countermeasure|Antonyms: defense, offense|noun
