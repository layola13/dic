metalliferous:/ˌmɛtəlˈɪfərəs/
adjective
含金属的；产金属的
Yielding or containing metal; producing metal.
The area is known for its metalliferous deposits. 这个地区以其金属矿床而闻名。
The explorers searched for metalliferous rocks. 探险者们寻找含金属的岩石。
近义词: ore-bearing, metal-bearing, metallic
反义词: non-metallic, barren

词性: adjective
