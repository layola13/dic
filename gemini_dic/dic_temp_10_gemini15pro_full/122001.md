misspending:/mɪsˈspɛndɪŋ/
n.
浪费，滥用
The misspending of public funds is a serious issue. 
公众资金的滥用是一个严重的问题。
The report criticized the government's misspending on defense.
该报告批评了政府在国防上的浪费。
近义词: waste, squandering, extravagance
反义词: saving, thrift, economy
词性:名词

misspend:/ˌmɪsˈspɛnd/
v.
浪费，滥用
He misspent his youth chasing after fleeting pleasures.
他浪费了自己的青春去追求转瞬即逝的快乐。
The company misspent millions on a failed marketing campaign.
该公司在一个失败的营销活动中浪费了数百万美元。
近义词: waste, squander, fritter away
反义词: save, invest, economize
词性:动词

词源:
From mis- + spend.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Germanic origin meaning "wrong" or "badly." "Spend" comes from Old English spendan, meaning "to expend, pay out."
