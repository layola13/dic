draughtswomen:/ˈdrɑːftsˌwɪmɪn/
noun
女绘图员，女制图员
A woman who makes detailed technical drawings, plans, or sketches, especially of machinery or buildings.
The draughtswomen meticulously rendered the blueprints for the new skyscraper.  女绘图员一丝不苟地绘制了新摩天大楼的蓝图。
Synonyms: draftswomen, draftsperson, drafter, designer, illustrator
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps) laborer, builder
词性: noun
