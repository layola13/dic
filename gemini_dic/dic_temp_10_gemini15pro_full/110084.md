lichenologist: /laɪkəˈnɒlədʒɪst/ (British) /ˈlaɪkənɑːlədʒɪst/ (American)
noun
地衣学家
A scientist who studies lichens.
The lichenologist spent years in the field, cataloging different species.  这位地衣学家在野外待了数年，对不同种类的地衣进行编目。
The lichenologist discovered a new species of lichen on the expedition.  这位地衣学家在考察中发现了一种新的地衣。
synonyms:  lichen specialist,  expert in lichenology
antonyms: N/A (no direct antonym for a profession)
n.
