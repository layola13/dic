manks: /mæŋks/
n.
曼克斯猫; 曼岛人; 曼岛语;
A tailless cat breed originating on the Isle of Man. Also, a native or inhabitant of the Isle of Man, or the language spoken there.
例句：The manks cat is known for its lack of tail.  曼克斯猫以没有尾巴而闻名。

例句：My neighbor is a Manks, and he speaks the Manks language fluently. 我的邻居是曼岛人，他能说一口流利的曼岛语。
近义词：Manxman, Manxwoman (for people);  (对于人来说)曼岛男人，曼岛女人
反义词: N/A
词性: noun (主要); can also be used as an adjective (less common)  (名词(主要);也可以用作形容词(较少见))
