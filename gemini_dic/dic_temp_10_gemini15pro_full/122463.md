mobilized:/ˈmoʊbəˌlaɪzd/ | adj. |动员的，已动员的 |  Made ready for action or use, especially for war or a similar emergency. |  The country mobilized its troops along the border.  这个国家沿着边境动员了军队。 |  activated, deployed, mustered |  demobilized, inactive | adjective

mobilize:/ˈmoʊbəˌlaɪz/ | v. |动员，调动 | To prepare and organize (troops, resources, etc.) for active service or use. | The government mobilized the army for the war. 政府动员军队参战。 |  assemble, rally, marshal | demobilize, disband | verb
