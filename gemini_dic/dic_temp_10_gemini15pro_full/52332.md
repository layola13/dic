dicrotic:/daɪˈkrɒtɪk/ | adj. | 双脉的，重搏的 | Relating to or characterized by a double pulse or beat, especially a double beat in the artery with each heartbeat. | The doctor detected a dicrotic pulse in the patient. 医生在病人身上检测到重搏脉。|  Near Synonyms:  bisferious | Antonyms:  monocrotic | Adjective
