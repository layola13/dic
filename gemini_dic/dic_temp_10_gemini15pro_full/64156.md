equalizing:/ˈiːkwəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb|使均衡，使相等，补偿|The government is trying to equalize opportunities for all citizens. 政府正在努力使所有公民机会均等。|
present participle of equalize|
例句：
The team is equalizing the playing field by providing training to all members.
该团队正在通过为所有成员提供培训来平衡竞争环境。

They are equalizing the pressure in the two containers.
他们正在使两个容器中的压力相等。


近义词：balancing, leveling, evening out
反义词：differentiating, unbalancing
词性: verb (present participle)


equalize:/ˈiːkwəlaɪz/
verb|使均衡，使相等，补偿|The new tax laws will equalize the wealth distribution. 新的税法将平衡财富分配。|
From Late Middle English *equale, from Latin aequālis (“equal, like, uniform”) + -ize, from -ize. |
例句：
The company aims to equalize pay across all departments.
该公司旨在使所有部门的工资均等。

The score was equalized in the final minutes of the game.
比分在比赛的最后几分钟被扳平。

近义词：balance, level, even out
反义词：differentiate, unbalance
词性: verb
