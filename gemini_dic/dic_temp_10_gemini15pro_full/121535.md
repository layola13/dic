mismark:/ˌmɪsˈmɑːrk/
verb
错误地标记；标错
to mark incorrectly
The examiner mismarked my paper, giving me a lower grade than I deserved.  考官把我的试卷批错了，给我的分数比我应得的低。
近义词: mislabel, misgrade
反义词: mark correctly, grade accurately
词性: verb

mismark:/ˌmɪsˈmɑːrk/
noun
错误的标记；标错
a mark that is incorrect
The mismark on the product led to it being sold at a discounted price. 产品上的错误标记导致它以折扣价出售。
近义词: mislabeling, error
反义词: correct mark, accurate label
词性: noun
