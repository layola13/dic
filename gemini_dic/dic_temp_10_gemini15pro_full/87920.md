hierolatry:/ˌhaɪərˈɒlətri/ | n. | 圣物崇拜；偶像崇拜 | The worship of sacred things or idols. |  The museum's exhibit on ancient Egypt explored the complexities of their hierolatry, showcasing artifacts related to the worship of various gods and goddesses.  埃及博物馆的古埃及展览探索了他们圣物崇拜的复杂性，展示了与各种神灵崇拜相关的文物。

The tribe's hierolatry centered around a sacred tree believed to possess healing powers.  这个部落的圣物崇拜以一棵被认为拥有治愈能力的神树为中心。 | 近义词：idolatry, iconolatry | 反义词: iconoclasm | 词性: noun
