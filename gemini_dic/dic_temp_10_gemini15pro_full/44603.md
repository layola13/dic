crowd-pleasing:/ˈkraʊdˌpliːzɪŋ/
adjective
取悦大众的；迎合大众的
Designed to gain popular approval, often at the expense of artistic merit.
Her crowd-pleasing performance earned her a standing ovation. 她迎合大众的表演为她赢得了全场起立鼓掌。
crowd-pleasing:/ˈkraʊdˌpliːzɪŋ/
adjective
迎合大众的
Designed to gain popular approval.
The movie's crowd-pleasing ending left audiences satisfied.  这部电影迎合大众的结局让观众很满意。

crowd-pleasing: /ˈkraʊdˌpliːzɪŋ/
noun
取悦大众的行为；迎合大众的策略
The act or strategy of seeking popular approval.
The politician's speech was pure crowd-pleasing. 这位政治家的演讲纯粹是为了迎合大众。


crowd-pleasing: /ˈkraʊdˌpliːzɪŋ/
noun
迎合大众的行为
The act of seeking popular approval.
The band's setlist was full of crowd-pleasing hits.  这支乐队的歌单里全是迎合大众的热门歌曲。

Etymology:  Compound of "crowd" (from Old English *crūdan "to press, crowd") + "pleasing" (present participle of "please," from Old French plaisir "to please").
近义词:popular, commercial, mainstream, accessible
反义词:highbrow, esoteric, avant-garde, niche
词性: adjective, noun
