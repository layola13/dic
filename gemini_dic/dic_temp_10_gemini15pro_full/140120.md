off-side:/ˌɒfˈsaɪd/ /ˌɔːfˈsaɪd/| adj. n.|越位的，越位位置|Being in a position in a game (especially soccer and hockey) where a player is nearer to the opponent's goal line than the ball when the ball is passed to them by a teammate, and there are fewer than two opposing players between them and the goal line, resulting in an infringement of the rules.|The referee blew the whistle for offside. 裁判吹哨判罚越位。|Synonyms: illegal position,  ahead of the play|Antonyms: onside|adjective

off-side:/ˌɒfˈsaɪd/ /ˌɔːfˈsaɪd/| n.|越位|The state of being offside.|The striker was caught in an offside position.  前锋被抓到越位。|Synonyms: offside position|Antonyms: onside position|noun
