client's: /ˈklaɪənts/| n. (possessive)|客户的，委托人的|Something that belongs to or is associated with a client.|例句：The lawyer reviewed the client's documents. 律师审查了客户的文件。|例句：Protecting the client's confidentiality is crucial.  保护客户的机密至关重要。|近义词：customer's, patron's|反义词: N/A|词性: possessive pronoun/adjective

Etymology:
From client + 's (possessive suffix).  "Client" comes from the Old French "client" and ultimately from the Latin "cliens," meaning "dependent."
