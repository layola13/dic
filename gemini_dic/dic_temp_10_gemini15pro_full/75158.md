frittering:/ˈfrɪtərɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
浪费，挥霍
the act of wasting time or money on unimportant things.
He frittered away his inheritance on fast cars and gambling. 他把遗产浪费在跑车和赌博上。
synonyms: wasting, squandering, dissipating
antonyms: saving, conserving, hoarding
verb

fritter:/ˈfrɪtər/
verb
浪费，挥霍
to waste time or money on unimportant things.
She frittered away the whole afternoon watching TV. 她浪费了整个下午看电视。
synonyms: waste, squander, dissipate
antonyms: save, conserve, hoard
verb

fritter:/ˈfrɪtər/
noun
油炸馅饼
a small cake made from batter (= a mixture of flour, eggs and milk) and containing fruit or other food, fried in hot fat.
