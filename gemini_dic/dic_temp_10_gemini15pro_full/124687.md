movie-theaters:/ˈmuːvi θiːətərz/| n. |电影院|A building or place where movies are shown.  It is generally considered the plural of "movie theater" or "movie theatre" (American and British English spellings respectively).|We went to the movie-theaters to see the new action film.|Synonyms: cinemas, cineplexes, multiplexes|Antonyms:  (There isn't a direct antonym, perhaps "home theater" as a place where movies are watched but not publicly shown)|noun
