agglomerated:/əˈɡlɒməreɪtɪd/
agglomerated:|adj.|聚集的，凝聚的|Gathered into a mass or ball; clustered.|The city is an agglomerated mass of buildings and streets. 这座城市是由建筑物和街道聚集而成的.|近义词：clustered, amassed, concentrated, accumulated|反义词：dispersed, scattered|adjective

agglomerate:/əˈɡlɒməreɪt/
agglomerate:|v.|聚集，凝聚|To gather into a mass or ball; to cluster.|Dust particles agglomerate to form larger clumps. 灰尘颗粒聚集形成更大的团块.|近义词：cluster, amass, concentrate, accumulate|反义词：disperse, scatter|verb

agglomerate:/əˈɡlɒməreɪt/
agglomerate:|n.|聚集体，凝聚物|A mass or collection of things gathered together.|The rock is an agglomerate of various minerals. 这块岩石是各种矿物的聚集体.|近义词：cluster, mass, collection, accumulation|反义词：dispersion|noun
