devours:/dɪˈvaʊərz/| v.|吞食，狼吞虎咽；贪婪地阅读；热切地倾听；（火灾、疾病等）毁坏，吞噬|The fire quickly devoured the entire building. 大火迅速吞噬了整栋建筑。|近义词：consume, engulf, gobble, absorb|反义词: nibble, taste|词性:动词
Etymology: From Old French *devorer, from Latin devorare ("to swallow down, devour"), from de- ("down") + vorare ("to swallow").
