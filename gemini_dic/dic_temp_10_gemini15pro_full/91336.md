hydrophytology:/ˌhaɪdrəʊfaɪˈtɒlədʒi/
n.
水生植物学
The study of aquatic plants, their physiology, ecology, and distribution.
Hydrophytology plays a crucial role in understanding aquatic ecosystems.  水生植物学在理解水生生态系统中起着至关重要的作用。
Hydrophytology research can help us manage and conserve our water resources.  水生植物学研究可以帮助我们管理和保护水资源。
Near Synonyms: aquatic botany, water plant biology
Antonyms: xerophytology (study of desert plants)
