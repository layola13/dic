governableness:/ˈɡʌvərnəbəlnəs/
n.
可 управляемость, 可统治性
The quality or state of being governable; the capacity to be governed.
The governableness of a nation depends on the cooperation of its citizens. 一个国家的可治理性取决于其公民的合作。
The new leader questioned the governableness of the rebellious province.  新领导人质疑叛乱省份的可治理性。

近义词: controllability, manageability, tractability, docility
反义词: ungovernableness, intractability, unruliness, rebelliousness
词性: noun

词源: From govern + -able + -ness.  Govern comes from the Old French governer, ultimately from the Latin gubernare "to steer, guide, govern."
