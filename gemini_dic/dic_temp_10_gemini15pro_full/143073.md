outbrags:/ˌaʊtˈbræɡz/
verb
吹嘘胜过；夸口比…强
boasts more extravagantly than; outdoes in bragging
He outbragged everyone else at the party about his new car.  他在聚会上吹嘘他的新车比其他任何人都好。
近义词: outboasts, surpasses, excels
反义词: downplays, minimizes
