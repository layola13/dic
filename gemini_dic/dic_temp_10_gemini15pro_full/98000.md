inogen:/ˈɪnədʒən/| n. |便携式制氧机|A portable oxygen concentrator. Inogen is a brand name that has become synonymous with the product itself.  It provides oxygen therapy for people with respiratory conditions.|例句：My grandmother uses an Inogen One G5 to help her breathe when she travels. 我祖母旅行时使用Inogen One G5来帮助她呼吸。

例句：The Inogen is much lighter and easier to carry than traditional oxygen tanks.  相比传统的氧气罐，Inogen更轻便，更容易携带。|近义词：portable oxygen concentrator, POC|反义词:oxygen tank|词性:noun
