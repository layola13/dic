elongative:/ɪˈlɒŋɡətɪv/ |adj.|伸长的，拉长的|Having the property of elongating or lengthening; causing elongation.  Characterized by lengthening or stretching.|例句：The elongative phase of muscle contraction involves the sliding of filaments. 肌肉收缩的伸长阶段涉及肌丝的滑动。

例句：The plant hormone auxin plays a crucial role in the elongative growth of plant cells.  植物激素生长素在植物细胞的伸长生长中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：lengthening, extending, stretching, protracting|反义词：shortening, contracting, compressing|词性：形容词
词源：From elongate + -ive.  Elongate itself comes from Latin ēlongātus, past participle of ēlongāre "to lengthen," from ex- "out" + longus "long."
