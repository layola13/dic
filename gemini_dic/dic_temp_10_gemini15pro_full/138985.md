nymphal:/ˈnɪmfəl/| adj.|蛹的，若虫的|Relating to or characteristic of a nymph, the immature form of certain insects.|The butterfly is in its nymphal stage. 这只蝴蝶处于若虫阶段。|近义词：larval, pupal, immature|反义词：adult, mature|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin "nymphālis," from "nympha" (nymph) + "-ālis" (adjectival suffix).  Ultimately from Greek "νύμφη" (nýmphē).
