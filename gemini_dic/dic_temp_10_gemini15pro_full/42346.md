counteractor:/ˈkaʊntərˌæktər/| n.|抵消物，中和剂，对抗者|Something that counteracts something else; a neutralizing agent. |The medicine acted as a counteractor to the poison.  这种药作为一种解毒剂来抵消毒药的作用。|synonyms: neutralizer, antidote, remedy, opponent, adversary|antonyms:  agonist, catalyst, enhancer|noun

counteractor:/ˈkaʊntərˌæktər/| n.|抵消物，中和剂，对抗者|A person or thing that opposes or counteracts another. |He acted as a counteractor to the dominant political power. 他对抗占统治地位的政治力量。|synonyms:  opponent, adversary, rival, foe |antonyms: ally, supporter, friend|noun
