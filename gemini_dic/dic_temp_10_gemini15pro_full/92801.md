icon's:/ˈaɪkɒnz/ (UK), /ˈaɪkɑːnz/ (US)| n.|图标; 象征; 圣像|A small picture or symbol on a computer screen that represents a program, file, or other object.  A person or thing regarded as a representative symbol of something.|The app's icon is a blue bird.  这个应用程序的图标是一只蓝鸟。|synonyms: symbol, emblem, image, representation|antonyms: nonentity, nobody|noun
