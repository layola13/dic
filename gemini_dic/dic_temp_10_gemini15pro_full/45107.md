cuckoo-clocks: /ˈkʊkuː klɒks/ (UK), /ˈkʊkuː klɑːks/ (US) | n. | 布谷鸟钟 | A clock, typically originating from the Black Forest region of Germany, that marks the hours with a sound like a cuckoo's call and often has a small, carved wooden cuckoo bird that emerges from a door above the clock face. |  My grandmother has a beautiful cuckoo-clock that she inherited from her mother.  我祖母有一个她从母亲那里继承下来的漂亮的布谷鸟钟。

The cuckoo-clock in the living room chimed three times. 客厅里的布谷鸟钟敲了三下。 | 近义词: pendulum clock (摆钟) | 反义词: digital clock (数字钟) | 词性: noun
