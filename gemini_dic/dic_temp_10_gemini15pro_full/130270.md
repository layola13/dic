non-structure:/ˈnɒnˈstrʌktʃə(r)/ |n.| 非结构；非结构体；非结构化 |A non-structure is something that lacks formal organization or a defined framework.  |The data was a non-structure, making it difficult to analyze.  这些数据是非结构化的，因此难以分析。| synonyms:  disorganization, formlessness | antonyms: structure, organization | noun

non-structural:/ˌnɒnˈstrʌktʃərəl/ |adj.| 非结构的；非承重的 |Not relating to or forming part of the structure of something, especially a building. |The non-structural walls could be easily removed.  这些非承重墙可以很容易地拆除。| synonyms: decorative, ornamental, non-load-bearing | antonyms: structural, load-bearing | adjective
