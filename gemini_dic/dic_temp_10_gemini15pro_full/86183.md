heat-proof:/ˈhiːtˌpruːf/
adj.
防热的，耐热的
Impervious to damage by heat.  Able to withstand high temperatures without being damaged.
This oven uses heat-proof glass.  这个烤箱使用耐热玻璃。
The firefighters wore heat-proof clothing. 消防队员穿着防热服。
近义词:heat-resistant, fireproof, fire-resistant
反义词:flammable, combustible
词性:adjective


heat-proof:/ˈhiːtˌpruːf/
noun.
防热材料，耐热材料
A material that is heat-proof.
We used a heat-proof material to line the fireplace. 我们用一种耐热材料来砌壁炉。
New heat-proofs have been developed for spacecraft.  为航天器开发了新的耐热材料。
近义词:insulation
反义词:N/A
词性:noun
