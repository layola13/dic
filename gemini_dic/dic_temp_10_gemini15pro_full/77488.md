gazelle:/ɡəˈzɛl/ | n. |瞪羚|A small, swift antelope of Africa and Asia, noted for its graceful movements and lustrous eyes.|A gazelle leaped across the savannah. 一只瞪羚跃过大草原。|Near synonyms: antelope, springbok|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for an animal, could use slow-moving animal as a contrasting concept, e.g., sloth)|Noun
