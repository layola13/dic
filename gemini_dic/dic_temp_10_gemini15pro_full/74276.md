fps:/ˌɛf piː ˈɛs/| n. | 帧每秒；每秒传输帧数 | Frames per second. A unit of frequency used to describe the rate at which sequential images called frames appear in a display, typically in a video game or film. | The game runs smoothly at 60 fps.  这款游戏以每秒60帧的速率流畅运行。| synonyms: frame rate | antonyms: N/A | noun
