daguerreotypists:/dəˈɡɛrətaɪpɪsts/
n.
达盖尔摄影师
A person who takes daguerreotypes, an early type of photograph.
Many daguerreotypists traveled the country, capturing portraits of everyday people. 许多达盖尔摄影师走遍全国，拍摄普通人的肖像。
