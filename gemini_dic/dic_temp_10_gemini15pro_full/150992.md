paramountcies:/ˈpærəmaʊntsiz/| n. | 至高无上，最高权力(地位) | The state of being paramount; supreme power or authority.  It refers to a collection of paramountcies or instances of supreme authority.  While not commonly used, it is grammatically correct. | The various paramountcies within the ancient empire often clashed.  古代帝国内的各种最高权力经常发生冲突。| The shifting paramountcies made stable governance difficult.  不断变化的最高权力使稳定的统治变得困难。 | synonyms: supremacies, sovereignties, dominions | antonyms: subordinations, subservience | noun
