beings:/ˈbiːɪŋz/| n. |生物，存在，人|Plural form of "being". Refers to living creatures, often with a sense of existence or consciousness. |例句：All living beings need water. 所有生物都需要水。

例句：Mythical beings populate many ancient stories.  许多古老的故事中都有神话生物。|近义词：creatures, entities, individuals|反义词:non-beings, inanimate objects|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *beinges*, plural of *beinge*, from Old English *bēing*, verbal noun from *bēon* "to be". Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *bēōną*, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰuH-*.
