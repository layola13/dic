airinesses:/ˈɛərɪnɪsɪz/
noun
轻盈；空灵；通风良好
The quality of being light and airy; open or well-ventilated spaces.
The airinesses of the chiffon curtains added to the room's ethereal beauty.  轻盈的雪纺窗帘增添了房间空灵的美感。
The architect designed the building with large windows to maximize the airinesses of the space.  建筑师设计了带有大窗户的建筑，以最大限度地提高空间的通风性。
Synonyms: lightnesses, etherealness,  airiness, delicacy, flimsiness
Antonyms: heaviness, density, stuffiness, solidity, oppressiveness
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From airy + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  Airy comes from Old English *ærig, from ær "air" (from Proto-Germanic *airaz, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂éh₂r- "air, mist") + -ig (suffix forming adjectives).
