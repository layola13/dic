compost:/ˈkɒmpɒst/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmpəʊst/ (US)| n. vt.|堆肥，混合肥料；把…制成堆肥|A mixture of decayed organic matter used as fertilizer.  |例句：She added compost to the garden. 她往花园里加了堆肥。|近义词：fertilizer, manure, humus|反义词:|n. & vt.

compost:/ˈkɒmpɒst/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmpəʊst/ (US)| vt.|把…制成堆肥|To convert (organic matter) into compost. |例句：He composts all his kitchen waste. 他把所有的厨房垃圾都做成了堆肥。|近义词：rot, decompose|反义词:|vt.
