executress:/ɪɡˈzɛkjətrɪs/ | n. |女遗嘱执行人|A woman appointed by a testator to carry out the terms of their will. |  Example 1:  The executress distributed the assets according to the will. 女遗嘱执行人根据遗嘱分配了资产。

Example 2: My aunt was named the executress of my grandmother's estate.  我的姑姑被指定为我祖母的遗产执行人。|Synonym: female executor, administratrix| Antonym: (No direct antonym, but perhaps beneficiary) | Noun
