affectively:/əˈfɛktɪvli/
adverb
情感地；情绪化地
In a manner relating to or influenced by emotions.  Pertaining to the affections or emotions, especially to pleasurable ones.
She responded affectively to the music, tears welling up in her eyes. 她对音乐产生了强烈的情感反应，眼里涌出了泪水。
emotionally, feelingly
cognitively, rationally
词性: 副词
