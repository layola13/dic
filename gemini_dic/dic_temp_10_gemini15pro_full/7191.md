angioscope:/ˈændʒiəˌskoʊp/
noun
血管镜
An instrument for examining the interior of blood vessels.
The surgeon used an angioscope to examine the patient's artery.  外科医生使用血管镜检查病人的动脉。
endoscope, fiberscope

ETYMOLOGY->
From  angio- (relating to blood vessels) + -scope (instrument for viewing).  Angio- comes from Greek *angeîon* (vessel). -scope comes from Greek *skopeîn* (to look at).
