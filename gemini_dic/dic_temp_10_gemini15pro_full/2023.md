actuation:/ˌæktʃʊˈeɪʃən/| n.|启动，驱动，激励|The actuation of the valve is controlled electronically.  阀门的启动是由电子控制的。|The actuation of the emergency brake prevented a disaster.  紧急刹车的启动避免了一场灾难。|近义词：activation, operation, triggering|反义词:deactivation, cessation|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin actuatus, past participle of actuare (“to put into action, drive, incite”), from Latin actus (“a doing, a driving, impulse”), from agere (“to do, drive, act”).

USAGE->
The actuation of the system requires a complex sequence of events.  该系统的启动需要一系列复杂的事件。

The precise actuation of the robotic arm is crucial for the success of the surgery.  机械臂的精确驱动对于手术的成功至关重要.
