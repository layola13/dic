ebonize:/ˈɛbəˌnaɪz/| v. |使呈乌木色，使变黑|To make black or dark like ebony.  To give a surface a deep, lustrous black finish, often by staining or painting.|They ebonized the wood to give it a rich, dark look. 他们把木头染黑，使其呈现出一种浓郁的黑色。|The craftsman ebonized the piano keys. 工匠把钢琴键染成了黑色。|blacken, darken|whiten, lighten|verb

ebonize:/ˈɛbəˌnaɪz/| adj. |乌木制的，黑色的|Made of ebony; black. |She wore an ebonized necklace. 她戴着一条乌木项链。 |The room was decorated with ebonized furniture. 房间里装饰着乌木家具。|black, dark|white, light|adjective
