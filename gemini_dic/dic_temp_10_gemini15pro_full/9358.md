antiprotectionists:/ˌæntiprəˈtɛkʃənɪsts/
|n.|反保护主义者|People who oppose protectionism, advocating for free trade and open markets.  They believe that protectionist policies hinder economic growth and limit consumer choice.|The antiprotectionists argued that tariffs would harm consumers.|近义词:free traders, advocates of free trade|反义词:protectionists|词性:名词

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek *anti-)
protectionist: one who advocates or practices protectionism
-ist (suffix): forms agent nouns
