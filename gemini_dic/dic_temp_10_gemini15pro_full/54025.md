discursively:/dɪsˈkɜːrsɪvli/
adv.
散漫地，离题地，推理地
in a manner that rambles from topic to topic
He spoke discursively about his travels, jumping from one anecdote to another. 他漫无边际地讲述他的旅行，从一个轶事跳到另一个轶事。
近义词: ramblingly, digressively, circuitously
反义词: concisely, succinctly, directly
词性: 副词

discourse:/ˈdɪskɔːrs/
n.
演讲，论述，论文
written or spoken communication or debate
The academic discourse on climate change is complex and multifaceted.  关于气候变化的学术讨论是复杂和多方面的。
近义词: discussion, conversation, talk
反义词: silence, quiet
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *discursīvus, from Latin discursus (“a running about”), from the past participle stem of discurrere (“to run about, run to and fro, digress”), from dis- (“apart, in different directions”) + currere (“to run”).
