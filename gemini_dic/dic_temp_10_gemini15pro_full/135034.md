nonnews:/ˈnɒnˌnjuːz/ (US) /ˈnɒnˌnjuːz/ (UK)| n.|非新闻; 无关紧要的消息|Information that is not considered newsworthy or of public interest.  It often refers to trivial or unimportant events, publicity stunts, or promotional material disguised as news.|The press release was full of nonnews about the company's latest products.|近义词: fluff, filler, trivia|反义词: news, hard news|词性: 名词
