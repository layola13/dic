magnetic:/mæɡˈnetɪk/ | adj. |磁的，有磁性的；有吸引力的| Having the properties of a magnet; capable of being magnetized or attracted by a magnet; also, figuratively, powerfully attractive or alluring. |例句：The magnetic field of the Earth protects us from harmful radiation. 地球的磁场保护我们免受有害辐射。

例句：Her magnetic personality drew everyone to her. 她富有魅力的个性吸引了每个人。|近义词：attractive, alluring, charismatic |反义词：repellent, unattractive|词性:形容词
