overthin:/ˌoʊvərˈθɪn/
adjective
过瘦的，太瘦的
Excessively thin; thinner than is desirable or healthy.
The model was criticized for being overthin.  这个模特因为太瘦而受到批评。
The farmer worried about his overthin cattle.  农场主担心他的牛太瘦了。
近义词:emaciated, gaunt, skinny, underweight
反义词:overweight, obese, plump
词性:adjective

词源: "over-" (excessively) + "thin" (having little flesh or fat)  由"over-" (过度地) + "thin" (瘦的) 组成
