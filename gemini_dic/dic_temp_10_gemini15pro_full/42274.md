counter-criticism:/ˌkaʊntərˈkrɪtɪsɪzəm/ | n. | 反批评，反驳 |  An argument made in opposition to criticism; a response to criticism. |  The author offered some counter-criticism in response to the negative reviews of his book. 作者针对其著作的负面评论提出了一些反驳。| synonyms: rebuttal, refutation, defense, rejoinder | antonyms: criticism, censure, condemnation | noun

counter-criticism:/ˌkaʊntərˈkrɪtɪsɪzəm/ | n. | 反批评，反驳 | An act of criticizing someone or something that has criticized you. | He launched into a lengthy counter-criticism of his opponent's claims. 他对对手的主张进行了长篇幅的反驳。| synonyms: riposte, reply, response | antonyms: acceptance, concession | noun
