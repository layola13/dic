disastrousness:/dɪˈzæstrəsnəs/
n.
灾难性，严重性
The disastrousness of the flood was evident in the widespread destruction. 洪水的灾难性在广泛的破坏中显而易见。
The report detailed the disastrousness of the economic policies. 报告详细说明了这些经济政策的灾难性。
近义词:catastrophe, calamity, devastation, ruin
反义词:success, benefit, advantage
词性:noun

Etymology: From disastrous + -ness.  "Disastrous" comes from the French "désastreux," ultimately from the Italian "disastro," meaning "ill-starred" or "unlucky," from "dis-" (bad) and "astro" (star).  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
