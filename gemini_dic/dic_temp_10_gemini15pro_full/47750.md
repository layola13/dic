decalitre:/ˈdekəˌliːtər/ /ˈdekəˌliːtɚ/
noun
十升
A metric unit of volume or capacity equal to ten liters.
The farmer harvested 50 decalitres of wheat this year. 今年农民收获了50十升小麦。
Synonyms: dekaliter, 10 liters
Antonyms: milliliter, microliter
n.


decalitres:/ˈdekəˌliːtərz/ /ˈdekəˌliːtɚz/
noun
十升（复数）
The plural form of decalitre, a metric unit of volume or capacity equal to ten liters each.
The vats held hundreds of decalitres of wine. 这些大桶装了几百十升的葡萄酒。
Synonyms: dekaliters, 10 liters (plural)
Antonyms: milliliters, microliters
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From French décalitre, from Greek deka "ten" + litron "a measure of capacity."
