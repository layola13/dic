misclaiming:/mɪsˈkleɪmɪŋ/
verb
错误地声称；不正当地要求
Making a false or incorrect claim.
He was misclaiming ownership of the land. 他错误地声称拥有这块土地。
近义词: misrepresenting, falsely claiming
反义词: rightfully claiming, acknowledging
词性: verb (动名词形式)

misclaim:/mɪsˈkleɪm/
verb
错误地声称；不正当地要求
To make a false or incorrect claim.
She misclaimed the expenses on her tax return. 她在纳税申报表上错误地申报了费用。
近义词: misrepresent, falsely claim
反义词: rightfully claim, acknowledge
词性: verb


词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning "wrong" or "bad") + "claim" (from Old French "claimer," meaning "to call, cry out, proclaim").
