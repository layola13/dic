alcyonoid:/ælˈsaɪənɔɪd/ | adj. n. |  海鸡冠目的；软珊瑚目的；海鸡冠目动物的；软珊瑚目动物的 |  Relating to or denoting an order (Alcyonacea) of corals that form colonies with fleshy, often brightly coloured skeletons, including soft corals, sea fans, and sea pens.  Also, a coral of this order.  |  Example: The alcyonoid corals create vibrant reefs in tropical waters.  这些海鸡冠珊瑚在热带水域形成了充满活力的珊瑚礁。

Example:  Divers marveled at the diverse array of alcyonoid species.  潜水员对海鸡冠物种的多样性感到惊叹。 | 近义词: soft coral, octocoral | 反义词: hard coral, scleractinian | 词性: adjective, noun
