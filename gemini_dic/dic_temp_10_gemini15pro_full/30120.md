ceder:/ˈsiːdər/| n.|雪松|A large evergreen tree with spreading branches and fragrant wood.|The cedars of Lebanon are famous for their size and longevity. 黎巴嫩的雪松以其体积和寿命而闻名。|近义词:conifer, evergreen|反义词:deciduous tree|词性:名词

Etymology: From Old French *cedre*, from Latin *cedrus*, from Greek *kédros*. Ultimately from a Semitic source.
