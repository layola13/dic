grandfathering:/ˈɡrændˌfɑːðərɪŋ/ /ˈɡrænˌfɑːðərɪŋ/| n.|祖父条款；豁免条款；旧有权利保留|The grandfather clause allowed existing businesses to continue operating under the old regulations.  这项祖父条款允许现有企业继续按照旧的规定运营。|exempting, protecting|restricting, limiting|noun
grandfathering:| n.| 允许已存在的状况继续存在，即使新的规则或法律已生效|Grandfathering is a provision in which an old rule continues to apply to some existing situations while a new rule will apply to all future cases. 祖父条款是指一项规定，其中旧规则继续适用于某些现有情况，而新规则将适用于所有未来情况。|exemption, safeguarding|prohibition, restriction|noun
