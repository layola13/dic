painture:/pɛ̃ˈtyʁ/ (French pronunciation)  No standard English pronunciation.| n.|绘画; 水彩画; 油画|A painting, especially one done in watercolor or oil.  Primarily used in older or more formal texts, often in art history contexts.|The painture depicted a pastoral scene. 这幅画描绘了一幅田园风光。|近义词：painting, picture, artwork|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Old French *peinture*,  from Latin *pictura* ("a painting"), from *pingere* ("to paint").
