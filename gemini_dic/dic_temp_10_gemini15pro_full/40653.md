conventionist:/kənˈvɛnʃənɪst/| n.|因循守旧者，墨守成规者；拘泥传统者；参加会议者|A person who adheres to conventional practices or beliefs, especially in art or literature; a person who attends a convention.|The conventionist refused to embrace new technologies, preferring the old ways of doing things. 这位墨守成规者拒绝接受新技术，更喜欢旧的做事方式。|The hotel was filled with conventionists attending the annual technology conference. 酒店里挤满了参加年度科技会议的与会者。|synonyms: traditionalist, conformist, attendee, delegate|antonyms: innovator, radical, nonconformist|noun
