commiserator:/kəˈmɪzəˌreɪtər/| n.|同情者，慰问者|A person who expresses or feels sympathy or pity for someone.|She found a commiserator in her best friend after losing her job.  她在失业后从她最好的朋友那里找到了慰问。|Synonyms: sympathizer, condoler|Antonyms: critic, detractor|noun

commiserate:/kəˈmɪzəˌreɪt/| v.|同情，怜悯，慰问|To express or feel sympathy or pity for someone.|I commiserated with her over the loss of her pet. 我对她的宠物去世表示同情。|Synonyms: sympathize, condole|Antonyms: criticize, condemn|verb
