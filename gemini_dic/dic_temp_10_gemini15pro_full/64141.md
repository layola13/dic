equaling:/ˈiːkwəlɪŋ/ | v. | 等于；相当于 | Being equal to something; amounting to the same value. | The total cost, equaling $500, was more than we expected. 总费用为 500 美元，比我们预期的要多。| synonyms: matching, corresponding, totaling, amounting to | antonyms: exceeding, surpassing, falling short of | verb

equaling:/ˈiːkwəlɪŋ/ | adj. | 相等的；等同的 | Being equal to something; having the same value or amount. |  They contributed equaling amounts to the project. 他们为这个项目贡献了相同的金额。 | synonyms: equal, equivalent, identical, same | antonyms: unequal, different, dissimilar | adjective
