lectrice: /lɛkˈtris/ (French pronunciation)| n.|女读者；女讲师|A female reader or lecturer, especially in a university context.  This word is borrowed directly from French.|例句：The lectrice offered insightful commentary on the poem.  女讲师对这首诗做了富有洞察力的评论。

例句：She was a renowned lectrice at the Sorbonne. 她在索邦大学是一位著名的女讲师。|近义词：reader, lecturer, instructor|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider student, listener)|词性:noun
