intercommunion:/ˌɪntər kəˈmjuːnjən/ | n. | 共同圣餐; 相互往来; 交流 |The act or practice of sharing communion between different Christian churches.  The sharing or exchanging of ideas, feelings, etc.| 例句：Intercommunion between the two churches was a sign of growing unity. 这两个教会之间的共同圣餐是团结日益增长的标志。

例句：The internet has facilitated intercommunion between people across the globe. 互联网促进了世界各地人们之间的交流。| 近义词：fellowship, communion, exchange, interaction | 反义词: isolation, separation | 词性: 名词
