annealer:/əˈniːlər/| n.|退火炉，退火器，韧化剂|A device or substance used for annealing.|The annealer slowly cooled the glass to prevent cracking. 退火炉缓慢地冷却玻璃以防止破裂。|Synonyms: tempering furnace, softening agent|Antonyms: hardener|noun

anneal:/əˈniːl/| v.|退火，韧化（金属），使（玻璃、塑料等）缓冷，使变坚韧|To heat (metal or glass) and allow it to cool slowly, in order to remove internal stresses and toughen it.  To reduce brittleness and improve toughness.|The glass was annealed to increase its strength. 玻璃经过退火以增加其强度。|Synonyms: temper, toughen, soften|Antonyms: harden, embrittle|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *onǣlan, from on- + *ǣlan "to burn, bake," of uncertain origin.
