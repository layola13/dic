blundered:/ˈblʌndərd/| v. |犯大错，踉跄，失策|To make a stupid or careless mistake; to move clumsily and awkwardly.|He blundered into the wrong room. 他误闯入了错误的房间。|Synonyms: erred, stumbled, floundered, miscalculated|Antonyms: succeeded, excelled, triumphed|verb

blunder:/ˈblʌndər/| n. |错误，失误|A stupid or careless mistake.|His biggest blunder was not investing in the stock market. 他最大的错误是没有投资股市。|Synonyms: mistake, error, gaffe, faux pas|Antonyms: success, triumph|noun
