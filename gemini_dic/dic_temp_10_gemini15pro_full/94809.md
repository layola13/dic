impulsiveness:/ɪmˈpʌlsɪvnəs/| n. |冲动，易冲动|The state of being impulsive; acting or done without forethought.|Her impulsiveness led her to buy a plane ticket to Hawaii on a whim. 她一时冲动，心血来潮地买了张去夏威夷的机票。|Synonyms: rashness, recklessness, impetuousness|Antonyms: caution, prudence, deliberation|noun

impulsive:/ɪmˈpʌlsɪv/| adj. |冲动的，易冲动的|Acting or done without forethought.|He made an impulsive decision to quit his job. 他一时冲动决定辞职。|Synonyms: rash, reckless, impetuous|Antonyms: cautious, prudent, deliberate|adjective
