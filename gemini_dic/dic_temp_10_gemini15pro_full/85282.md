harmonite:/ˈhɑːrməˌnɪt/ | n. |谐波，泛音 |A component frequency of a complex wave that is an integer multiple of the fundamental frequency. A sound wave can be broken down into its fundamental frequency and a series of harmonics, each with a progressively higher frequency and lower amplitude.|The musician could hear the faint harmonics resonating in the concert hall. 音乐家可以听到音乐厅里微弱的泛音在回响。| synonyms: overtone, partial | antonyms: fundamental, inharmonic | noun

harmonite:/ˈhɑːrməˌnɪt/ | adj. |谐波的 |Relating to or characterized by musical harmony or harmonics. | The harmonic series is a divergent infinite series. 谐波级数是一个发散的无穷级数。 | synonyms: harmonic, consonant | antonyms: inharmonic, dissonant | adjective
