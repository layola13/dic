clodhopper:/ˈklɑːdˌhɑːpər/| n. |乡巴佬，土包子|A clumsy, awkward, or unsophisticated person, especially one from a rural area.  A large, heavy shoe.|例句：He's a bit of a clodhopper, but he's got a good heart. 他有点土，但他心地善良。

例句：She wore her clodhoppers to the barn dance. 她穿着她笨重的鞋子去参加谷仓舞会。|近义词：yokel, rustic, bumpkin, oaf, lout|反义词:sophisticate, cosmopolitan|词性:noun
