enforest:/ɪnˈfɔːrɪst/ /ɛnˈfɔːrɪst/ | vt. | 植树造林；使成为森林 | To establish a forest on land that is not forested; to cover with trees. |  The government plans to enforest the barren hills. 政府计划在荒山上植树造林。| synonyms: afforest, plant, reforest | antonyms: deforest, clear, log | verb

enforest:/ɪnˈfɔːrɪst/ /ɛnˈfɔːrɪst/ | n. |  (罕用) 造林；森林化 | The act of planting trees in an area to create a forest. | The enforest of the area provided a habitat for various species.  该地区的造林为各种物种提供了栖息地。| synonyms: afforestation, reforestation | antonyms: deforestation | noun
