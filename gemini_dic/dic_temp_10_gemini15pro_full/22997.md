bougainvillea:/ˌbuːɡənˈvɪliə/ (US), /ˌbuːɡɪnˈvɪliə/ (UK)| n. |九重葛，叶子花|A tropical climbing plant with bright, colourful bracts which resemble flowers.  The actual flowers are small and white, surrounded by these showy bracts.|The bougainvillea climbed up the trellis, creating a vibrant splash of color. 九重葛爬上了棚架，营造出一片鲜艳的色彩。|Synonyms: paperflower (less common)|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

bougainvillea:/ˌbuːɡənˈvɪliə/ (US), /ˌbuːɡɪnˈvɪliə/ (UK)| adj. |九重葛色的，叶子花色的|Having the color of bougainvillea, typically a vibrant shade of pink, purple, or magenta.|She wore a bougainvillea-colored dress to the summer party. 她穿着一件九重葛色的连衣裙去参加夏季派对。|Synonyms: magenta, fuchsia, purplish-pink|Antonyms: N/A|Adjective
