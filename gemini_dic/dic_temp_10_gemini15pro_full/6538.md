amylaceous:/ˌæmɪˈleɪʃəs/ | adj. | 淀粉的，含淀粉的 | Containing or resembling starch; starchy. | The potatoes have an amylaceous texture.  土豆有淀粉质地。| Synonyms: starchy, farinaceous | Antonyms: non-starchy | adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *amylaceus*, from *amylum* "starch" (from Greek *ámylon* "starch," literally "not ground at a mill," from *a-* "not" + *mýlē* "mill") + *-aceus* "-aceous."
