parsonish:/ˈpɑːrsənɪʃ/| adj.|牧师似的；一本正经的；假正经的|Resembling or characteristic of a parson, especially in being sanctimonious or excessively serious.|He had a parsonish air about him, always ready with a moralizing comment.|近义词：sanctimonious, pious, preachy, self-righteous|反义词：irreverent, impious, secular|词性: adjective
词源: From parson + -ish.
