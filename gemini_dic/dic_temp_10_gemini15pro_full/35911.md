cocreate:/ˌkoʊkriˈeɪt/| v. |共同创造，协同创造|To create something jointly with others.|We cocreated this art project with the local community. 我们与当地社区共同创作了这个艺术项目。|例句：The team cocreated a new marketing strategy.  团队共同制定了一个新的营销策略。

例句：The software allows users to cocreate and share documents. 该软件允许用户共同创建和共享文档。|近义词：collaborate, cooperate, joint create|反义词：dictate, impose|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From co- + create. Co- is a prefix meaning "jointly," "together," or "mutually."  Create comes from the Latin "creare," meaning "to produce" or "to bring forth."
