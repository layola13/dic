injudiciously:/ˌɪndʒʊˈdɪʃəsli/
adverb
不明智地；不审慎地
in a way that shows poor judgment; unwisely.
He injudiciously chose to argue with his boss. 他不明智地选择与他的老板争论。
She spent her money injudiciously. 她不明智地花掉了她的钱。
Synonyms: unwisely, imprudently, foolishly, rashly, thoughtlessly
Antonyms: wisely, prudently, judiciously, carefully, thoughtfully
part of speech: adverb

injudicious:/ˌɪndʒʊˈdɪʃəs/
adjective
不明智的；不审慎的
showing very poor judgment; unwise.
It was an injudicious remark that cost him his job. 正是一句不明智的话让他丢了工作。
Making injudicious investments can lead to financial ruin.  不明智的投资会导致经济崩溃。
Synonyms: unwise, imprudent, foolish, rash, thoughtless
Antonyms: wise, prudent, judicious, careful, thoughtful
part of speech: adjective
