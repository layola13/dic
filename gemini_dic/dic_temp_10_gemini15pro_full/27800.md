cannonball:/ˈkænənˌbɔːl/| n.|炮弹；实心弹；高速跳水（双腿并拢，双手抱膝）|A large, heavy ball, typically made of iron, fired from a cannon.  A type of dive in which the diver enters the water head first with their legs straight and their arms wrapped around their knees. |The cannonball crashed through the wall. 炮弹击穿了墙壁。

He did a cannonball into the pool. 他做了一个炮弹式跳水。|近义词：projectile, bombshell (figurative), shell|反义词: (No direct antonym.  Could consider "peace offering" as a figurative antonym in certain contexts) |词性:noun
