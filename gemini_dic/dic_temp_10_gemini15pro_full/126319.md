myopathic:/ˌmaɪəˈpæθɪk/| adj.|肌病的|Relating to or affected with myopathy, a disease of muscle tissue.|例句：The patient exhibited myopathic symptoms, such as muscle weakness and atrophy. 病人表现出肌病的症状，例如肌肉无力和萎缩。

例句：Myopathic disorders can be inherited or acquired. 肌病可以是遗传的，也可以是后天获得的。|近义词：muscular, myogenic|反义词: healthy, robust|词性:形容词
