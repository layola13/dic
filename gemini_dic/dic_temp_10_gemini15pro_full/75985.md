funnest:/ˈfʌnɪst/ | adj. | 最有趣的 |  Superlative form of fun (informal).  While grammatically correct, "funnest" is often considered less formal than "most fun." It's more commonly used in casual conversation or writing, particularly by children. |  That was the funnest party I've ever been to! 那是我参加过的最有趣的派对！|  The funnest game we played was hide-and-seek. 我们玩得最开心的游戏是捉迷藏。 |  Synonyms: most fun, most enjoyable, most entertaining | Antonyms: least fun, most boring, least enjoyable | adjective
