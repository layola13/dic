globe-like:/ˈɡloʊbˌlaɪk/| adj.|球状的，地球仪似的|Resembling a globe; spherical.  Having the form of a globe; spherical or nearly so.|例句：The hot air balloon was globe-like as it ascended into the sky.  热气球升上天空时呈球状。|例句：The artist sculpted a globe-like figure out of clay.  艺术家用粘土雕刻了一个球状的雕像。|近义词：spherical, round, globular, orbicular|反义词：flat, planar|词性: adjective

Etymology: globe (from Latin globus "sphere, ball") + -like (suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling")
