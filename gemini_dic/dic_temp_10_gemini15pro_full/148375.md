overspiced: /ˌoʊvərˈspaɪst/ | adj. | 过度调味的，加太多香料的 | Containing too much spice. | The curry was overspiced and I couldn't finish it. 咖喱太辣了，我吃不完。|  The chili was a bit overspiced for my taste.  这辣椒对我来说有点太辣了。 | 近义词：over-seasoned, too spicy | 反义词: bland, under-seasoned | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above") + "spiced" (past participle of "spice," from Old French espice "spice, species, kind," from Latin species "appearance, form, kind").
