mediator:/ˈmiːdieɪtər/| n.|调解人，调停人，斡旋者|A person who attempts to make people involved in a conflict come to an agreement; a go-between.|The mediator helped the two sides reach a compromise. 调解员帮助双方达成了妥协。|近义词：arbitrator, negotiator, conciliator, intermediary|反义词:agitator, instigator|noun
