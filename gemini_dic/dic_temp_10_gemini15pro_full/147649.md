overpollinated:/ˌoʊvərˈpɑːləˌneɪtɪd/
adjective
过度授粉的
pollinated excessively, resulting in a reduced fruit or seed set.
The squash plants were overpollinated, leading to misshapen fruit.  这些南瓜植物授粉过度，导致果实畸形。
