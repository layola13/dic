amblingly:/ˈæmblɪŋli/
adverb
漫步地，悠闲地
in a leisurely strolling manner
He walked amblingly along the beach, enjoying the sunset. 他悠闲地沿着海滩漫步，欣赏日落。
She amblingly made her way through the crowded market. 她慢悠悠地穿过拥挤的市场。
近义词: leisurely, slowly, casually
反义词: quickly, rapidly, hurriedly
词性: adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From amble + -ly.  Amble comes from Old French ambler "to go at a slow pace," from Latin ambulare "to walk."
