communionist: /kəˈmjuːnjənɪst/
n.
圣餐信徒，参加圣餐者
a person who receives Holy Communion.
Example: The communionists knelt at the altar rail. 圣餐信徒跪在祭坛栏杆前。

Example:  The priest offered the bread and wine to the communionists.  神父把面包和葡萄酒递给圣餐者。

近义词: communicant
反义词: None (No direct opposite for a person receiving communion)
词性: Noun
