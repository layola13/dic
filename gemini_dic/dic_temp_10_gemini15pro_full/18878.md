beside:/bɪˈsaɪd/| prep. adv.|在...旁边，除...之外|At or by the side of; next to. Also, in addition to; apart from.|例句：The dog sat beside its owner. 狗坐在它的主人旁边。|近义词：next to, alongside, by, near|反义词：away from, far from|词性:介词, 副词
beside:/bɪˈsaɪd/| prep. adv.|除...之外|In addition to; apart from.|例句：Beside the cost, there are other factors to consider. 除了成本之外，还有其他因素需要考虑。|近义词：in addition to, apart from, as well as|反义词：excluding, except|词性:介词, 副词
