overpresumptive:/ˌoʊvərprɪˈzʌmptɪv/
adjective
过于自信的；自以为是的；放肆的
Excessively presumptuous; unduly confident or bold.
He was overpresumptive in assuming he would get the promotion without even applying. 他自以为是地认为不用申请就能升职。
She made some overpresumptive remarks about the boss's personal life. 她对老板的私生活发表了一些放肆的言论。
Synonyms: arrogant, presumptuous, conceited, overconfident, bold, audacious
Antonyms: humble, modest, diffident, reserved, timid
词性: adjective
