nondepartmental:/ˌnɒndɪˌpɑːtˈmɛntəl/
adjective
非部门的，不属于任何部门的
Not belonging to or controlled by any particular government department.
例句：A nondepartmental public body is an organization that is not part of a government department but still carries out public functions.  一个非部门公共机构是不属于任何政府部门但仍然履行公共职能的组织。

例句：The nondepartmental nature of the task force allowed for greater flexibility and collaboration.  这个工作组的非部门性质使其具有更大的灵活性和协作性。
近义词：independent, autonomous, cross-functional
反义词：departmental
词性：adjective
