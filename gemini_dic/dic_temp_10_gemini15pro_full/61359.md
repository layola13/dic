embezzle:/ɪmˈbezəl/ | v. | 盗用，挪用，侵吞 | To illegally take money that you are responsible for, usually from a company or organization. |  He was caught embezzling funds from the company. 他被抓到挪用公司资金。

The former CEO embezzled millions of dollars from investors.  这位前首席执行官从投资者那里盗用了数百万美元。 | 近义词: misappropriate, pilfer, defraud, steal | 反义词: donate, contribute | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *embesillier "to make away with," from en- "in" + besillier "to destroy, damage, plunder," of Germanic origin.
