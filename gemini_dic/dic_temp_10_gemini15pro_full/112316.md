lordosis:/lɔːrˈdoʊsɪs/
noun
脊柱前凸，腰椎前凸
An abnormal inward curvature of the spine, typically in the lumbar region.
Lordosis can cause lower back pain and stiffness.  脊柱前凸会导致下背部疼痛和僵硬。
The gymnast's extreme lordosis was concerning.  体操运动员的极度脊柱前凸令人担忧。
近义词: swayback, hyperlordosis
反义词: kyphosis
