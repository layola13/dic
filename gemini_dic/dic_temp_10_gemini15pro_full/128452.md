newscastings: /ˈnjuːzkɑːstɪŋz/
n.
新闻广播；新闻播送
the action or process of broadcasting news on television or radio.
The newscastings were interrupted by a special announcement. 新闻广播被一则特别通告打断了。
The evening newscastings covered the developing crisis. 晚间新闻播报了正在发展的危机。
synonyms: news broadcasts, news reports, news bulletins, news programs, news coverage
antonyms: silence, censorship

词性: 名词
