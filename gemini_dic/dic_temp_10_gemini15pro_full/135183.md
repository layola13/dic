nonorbiting:/ˌnɒnˈɔːbɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非轨道的，不绕轨道运行的
Not orbiting; not revolving around a celestial body in a fixed path.
The nonorbiting probe sent data back to Earth before crashing into the asteroid.  这个非轨道探测器在撞向小行星之前将数据传回了地球。
The nonorbiting space debris poses a threat to satellites. 非轨道运行的太空碎片对卫星构成威胁。
Near Antonyms: orbiting, revolving
Antonyms: stationary
词性: adjective
