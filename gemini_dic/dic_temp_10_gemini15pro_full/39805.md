consortism:/kənˈsɔːrtɪzəm/
n.
共同体；集合体；联营；合伙
An association or partnership, typically of businesses or organizations, for the purpose of engaging in a joint venture.
The consortism of several European companies allowed them to bid on the large construction project.  几个欧洲公司的联合体使他们能够投标这个大型建筑项目。

The universities formed a consortism to share resources and research.  这些大学组成了一个联盟来共享资源和研究。
syndicate, alliance, consortium, partnership, collaboration
competition, rivalry
