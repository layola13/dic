lungfish:/ˈlʌŋfɪʃ/| n. |肺鱼|A fish that breathes air and has a lung-like swim bladder. |例句：The lungfish can survive in dry conditions by burrowing into the mud. 肺鱼可以通过钻入泥土在干燥的环境中生存。

例句：Lungfish are considered to be living fossils. 肺鱼被认为是活化石。|近义词：dipnoi|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps other fish types like "bony fish" or "cartilaginous fish" could be considered contrasting groups) |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
