montaine:/mɒnˈteɪn/ | adj. | 山的；山区的；住在山里的 | Of or relating to mountains; inhabiting mountains. |  The montaine forests are home to a variety of unique species. 山地森林是各种独特物种的家园。|  Montaine gorillas are adapted to high altitudes. 山地大猩猩适应了高海拔地区。| 近义词: mountain, alpine, upland | 反义词: lowland, valley | 词性: 形容词

词源: From French *montain*, from Old French *montaigne*, from *mont* (“mountain”) + *-agne*. Ultimately from Latin *montāneus*.
