bashfulness:/ˈbæʃfʊlnəs/| n.|害羞，羞怯|The state of being shy or timid.  It often involves feelings of self-consciousness and discomfort in social situations. |Her bashfulness prevented her from speaking up in class. 她的害羞使她不敢在课堂上发言。|Synonyms: shyness, timidity, modesty, diffidence|Antonyms: boldness, confidence, assertiveness|noun
