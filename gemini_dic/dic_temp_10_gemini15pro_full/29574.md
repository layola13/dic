catchword:/ˈkætʃˌwɜːrd/ (US), /ˈkætʃˌwɜːd/ (UK)| n.| （演员提示下一位演员台词的）提示语 |A cue word used in a play.| The actor missed his catchword and the scene fell apart. 演员错过了他的提示语，导致整个场景垮掉。| synonyms: cue, prompt | antonyms:  (No direct antonym) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "catch" (to grasp or seize) + "word" (a unit of language). Originally referred to the first word of a page printed at the bottom of the preceding page as a guide for printers, later broadened to mean any memorable word or phrase.
