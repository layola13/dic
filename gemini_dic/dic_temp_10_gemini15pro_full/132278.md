nondefectively:/nɒndɪˈfɛktɪvli/
adverb
无缺陷地；完美地
without defect; perfectly.
The product was manufactured nondefectively and passed all quality control tests.  该产品生产无缺陷，并通过了所有质量控制测试。
Synonyms: perfectly, flawlessly, impeccably
Antonyms: defectively, imperfectly, faultily
词性: 副词

Etymology:
From non- + defectively.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "defectively" is derived from "defect," from the Latin word "defectus," meaning "lack, failure, fault."
