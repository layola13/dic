bouquetier:/ˌbuːkeɪˈtɪeɪ/
n.
花瓶，花束架，插花筒
A vase or other receptacle for holding a bouquet of flowers.
She placed the bouquet in a delicate crystal bouquetier. 她把花束放在一个精致的水晶花瓶里。
The antique bouquetier was a beautiful addition to the dining room.  古董花瓶是餐厅里一件漂亮的装饰品。
synonyms: vase, flower holder, flower pot
antonyms: N/A
