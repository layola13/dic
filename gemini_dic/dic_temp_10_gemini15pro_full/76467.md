gaiters:/ˈɡeɪtərz/| n. |绑腿，护腿|A garment worn over the shoe and lower leg for protection, often used in hiking or other outdoor activities.  They can be made of various materials like canvas, nylon, or leather.|例句：I wore gaiters to prevent snow from getting into my boots. 我穿了护腿，防止雪进入我的靴子。

例句：The hikers used gaiters to protect their legs from thorns and bushes.  徒步旅行者使用护腿来保护他们的腿免受荆棘和灌木的伤害。|近义词：leggings, spats|反义词: None|词性:noun


gaiter:/ˈɡeɪtər/| n. |绑腿，护腿|Singular form of gaiters.  A covering for the lower leg, often extending over the shoe. |例句：He lost one gaiter in the thick mud. 他在厚厚的泥里丢了一只护腿。
