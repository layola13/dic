overembroidering: /ˌoʊvərɪmˈbrɔɪdərɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度刺绣，过分装饰
Embroidering something excessively, adding too much decoration.
The dress was overembroidered with sequins, making it look gaudy.  这件衣服绣了太多的亮片，看起来很俗气。
She overembroidered the cushion with intricate floral patterns.  她在垫子上绣了过分复杂的图案。
近义词: overdecorating, embellishing excessively
反义词: understating, simplifying

词性:v.
