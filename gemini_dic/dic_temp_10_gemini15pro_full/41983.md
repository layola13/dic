cost-effective:/ˌkɒst ɪˈfektɪv/ /ˌkɑːst ɪˈfektɪv/
adjective
划算的，成本效益高的
Producing something in a way that does not cost a lot of money compared to the profit or benefit that is received.
This is a very cost-effective way of marketing our products. 这是一种非常划算的营销我们产品的方式。
Buying in bulk is more cost-effective. 批量购买更划算。
Synonyms: economical, efficient, affordable, budget-friendly, value-for-money
Antonyms: expensive, costly, uneconomical, inefficient, wasteful
词性: adjective
