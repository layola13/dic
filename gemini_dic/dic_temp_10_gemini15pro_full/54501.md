disinvigorate:/ˌdɪsɪnˈvɪɡəreɪt/
verb
使衰弱；使失去活力
To deprive of vigor or energy; to weaken.
Long hours of overtime can disinvigorate even the most dedicated employees.  长时间的加班会让即使是最敬业的员工也感到疲惫不堪。
The humid heat disinvigorated the tourists. 湿热的天气使游客们感到疲惫不堪。
Synonyms: weaken, enervate, debilitate, sap, exhaust
Antonyms: invigorate, energize, strengthen, revitalize
词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation) + invigorate (from Latin *invigoratus, past participle of *invigorare, from in- (into) + vigor (strength) + -ate (to make)).
