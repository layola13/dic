contemporaneous:/kənˌtɛmpəˈreɪniəs/
adjective
同时发生的，同时代的
existing, occurring, or originating during the same time
The two events were contemporaneous, both happening in the summer of 1969. 这两件事是同时发生的，都发生在1969年的夏天。
The contemporaneous accounts of the battle differed wildly.  关于这场战斗的同时期记载大相径庭。

近义词:concurrent, simultaneous, synchronous
反义词:non-concurrent, asynchronous

词性:adjective
词源:From Late Latin contemporaneus, from Latin con- 'together with' + tempor- 'time' + -aneus.
