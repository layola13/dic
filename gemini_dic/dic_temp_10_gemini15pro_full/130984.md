nonatmospheric:/ˌnɒnˌætməsˈfɪrɪk/
adjective
非大气的
Not relating to, existing in, or caused by the atmosphere.
The experiment was conducted in a nonatmospheric chamber to eliminate the effects of air pressure.  这个实验是在一个非大气舱内进行的，以消除气压的影响。
The spacecraft's nonatmospheric propulsion system allowed it to maneuver in the vacuum of space.  航天器的非大气推进系统使其能够在真空的太空中进行机动。
Synonyms: extra-atmospheric, ex-atmospheric
Antonyms: atmospheric
词性: 形容词
