macadamed:/məˈkædəmd/ | adj.|用碎石铺成的|Covered with small, broken stones, especially as a road surface.  Derived from the process invented by John Loudon McAdam.| The macadamed road wound through the countryside.  碎石路蜿蜒穿过乡村。 | surfaced, paved | dirt, unpaved | adjective
