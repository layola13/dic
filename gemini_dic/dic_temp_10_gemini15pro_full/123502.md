monotheistically:/ˌmɒnəʊθiːˈɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 一神论地 | In a way that relates to or characterized by the doctrine or belief that there is only one God. |  The ancient Israelites were the first people to worship God monotheistically. 古代以色列人是第一个一神论崇拜上帝的民族。|  They believe monotheistically in one God. 他们一神论地信仰一个上帝。| 近义词: religiously, piously | 反义词: polytheistically | 词性: 副词

词源:
From monotheist + -ically.  "Monotheist" comes from Greek "monos" (one) + "theos" (god).  "-ically" is a suffix forming adverbs.
