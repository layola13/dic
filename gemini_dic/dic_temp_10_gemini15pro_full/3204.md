affectedness:/əˈfɛktɪdnəs/ | n. |做作，矫揉造作 |  The quality of being affected; artificiality or unnaturalness of manner. | Her affectedness was evident in her overly dramatic gestures and pronouncements. 她的做作在她夸张的手势和言辞中显露无疑。 | synonyms: artificiality, pretension, mannerism, ostentation, airs, unnaturalness | antonyms: naturalness, genuineness, sincerity, simplicity, artlessness | noun

Etymology:
From affect + -ed + -ness.  "Affect" ultimately derives from the Latin "afficere," meaning "to act on, influence."  "-ed" forms the past participle, and "-ness" creates a noun denoting a state or quality.
