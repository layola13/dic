hibernation:/ˌhaɪbərˈneɪʃn/| n.|冬眠|A state of inactivity and metabolic depression in endotherms.  A long period of sleep to avoid cold weather conditions. |Bears go into hibernation during the winter. 熊在冬天进入冬眠。|The dormouse’s hibernation lasted for several months. 睡鼠的冬眠持续了几个月。|近义词：dormancy, sleep, torpor|反义词: activity, wakefulness|noun
