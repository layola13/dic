dodgeball:/ˈdɑːdʒˌbɔːl/ | n. | 躲避球 | A game in which players on two teams try to throw balls at each other while avoiding being hit themselves. |  The kids were playing dodgeball in the gym. 孩子们在体育馆里玩躲避球。| synonyms:  no exact synonym, similar games include bombardment, prisonball | antonyms:  no direct antonym, perhaps cooperative games | noun


dodge:/dɑːdʒ/ | v. | 躲避，闪避 | To avoid (someone or something) by a sudden quick movement. | He dodged the snowball thrown at him. 他躲开了扔向他的雪球。| synonyms: evade, avoid, duck, shirk, sidestep | antonyms: confront, face, meet | verb
