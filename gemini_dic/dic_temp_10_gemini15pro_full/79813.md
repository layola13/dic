glycolic:/ɡlaɪˈkɒlɪk/ /ɡlaɪˈkɑːlɪk/
adjective|乙醇酸的；羟基乙酸的|Relating to or derived from glycol; containing glycol.  Specifically refers to hydroxyacetic acid, the simplest alpha-hydroxy acid (AHA).|Glycolic acid is often used in skincare products.|近义词:hydroxyacetic|反义词:(No direct antonym, but some ingredients with opposing effects in skincare might be retinoids or certain oils)|词性:adjective
