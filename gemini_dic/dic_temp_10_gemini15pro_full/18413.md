bel:/bɛl/| n.|贝尔 (响度单位)|A bel (symbol B) is a unit of measurement of sound intensity or power level, a logarithmic measure relative to a reference level.  A decibel (dB), one tenth of a bel, is more commonly used.|The sound was measured at 100 bels, an incredibly high level.|近义词:decibel (more common)|反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement)|noun
