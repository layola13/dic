beavering:/ˈbiːvərɪŋ/| n. | 忙碌的工作，勤奋的活动 | The action of working hard like a beaver. | The beavering of the construction workers ensured the project finished on time. 建筑工人的辛勤工作确保了项目按时完成。| work, toil, labor, effort | idleness, inactivity | noun
