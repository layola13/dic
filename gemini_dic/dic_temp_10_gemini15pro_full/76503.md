galbanum: /ˈɡælbənəm/ | n. | 白松香，松脂 | A gum resin with a strong, somewhat musky odor, obtained from certain Asian plants of the genus *Ferula*, used in perfumery and formerly in medicine. | The perfume had a distinctive note of galbanum.  这款香水有一种独特的松脂香味。|  The ancient Egyptians used galbanum in incense. 古埃及人用松脂做香。 | synonyms: resin, gum resin, oleoresin | antonyms: N/A | noun
