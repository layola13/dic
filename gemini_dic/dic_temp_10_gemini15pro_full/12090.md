artisan:/ˈɑːrtɪzən/ /ˈɑːrtɪzn/ | n. |工匠，手艺人|A skilled worker who makes things by hand, typically one that involves a particular skill.|例句：The artisan crafted a beautiful wooden bowl.  这位工匠制作了一个漂亮的木碗。

例句：The local artisans displayed their wares at the market. 当地的工匠在市场上展示他们的商品。


|近义词：craftsman, craftsperson, skilled worker, artisan, maker|反义词:amateur, unskilled worker|词性:noun
