hunte:/hʌnt/ |v. |狩猎，打猎|To pursue wild animals for food or sport; to search diligently for something or someone.|He hunted deer in the forest. 他在森林里猎鹿。|近义词: pursue, stalk, chase, seek|反义词: protect, conserve|动词

hunted:/ˈhʌntɪd/ |adj.|被追捕的，惶惶不安的|Feeling or looking as if one is being hunted or persecuted.|He had a hunted look in his eyes. 他的眼神惶惶不安。|近义词:  pursued, persecuted, harassed|反义词: safe, secure|形容词
