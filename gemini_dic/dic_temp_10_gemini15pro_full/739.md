absinthin: /ˈæbsɪnθɪn/
noun
苦艾素; 苦艾精
A bitter crystalline compound, C30H40O8, present in wormwood.
The absinthin in wormwood gives it its distinctive bitter taste.  苦艾中的苦艾素赋予了它独特的苦味。
Absinthin is extracted from wormwood. 苦艾素是从苦艾中提取的。
Synonyms:  bitter principle,  lactone
Antonyms:  sweetener
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From French absinthine, from absinthe (“wormwood”), from Latin absinthium, from Ancient Greek ἀψίνθιον (apsínthion). Ultimately from a reconstructed Proto-Indo-European root *h₂epsin- (“wormwood”).
