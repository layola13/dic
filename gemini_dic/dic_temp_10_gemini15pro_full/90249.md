housebound:/ˈhaʊsbaʊnd/| adj.|居家不出的，足不出户的|Confined to one's house, typically by illness or old age.|例句：She has been housebound for weeks due to a broken leg.  她因为腿骨折已经在家待了好几个星期了。

例句：The heavy snow made many people housebound.  大雪使许多人无法出门。|近义词：homebound, confined, restricted|反义词:mobile, active|词性:形容词

husband:/ˈhʌzbənd/| n. vt.|丈夫；节俭使用|A married man considered in relation to his spouse. /  Use (resources) economically.|例句：Her husband is a doctor. 她丈夫是个医生。

例句：We need to husband our resources during this difficult time.  在这段困难时期，我们需要节俭地使用资源。|近义词：(n.)spouse, partner, mate; (vt.)conserve, save|反义词:(n.)wife; (vt.)squander, waste|词性:名词，动词
