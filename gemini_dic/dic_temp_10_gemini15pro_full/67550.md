extraneous:/ɪkˈstreɪniəs/ /ɛkˈstreɪniəs/| adj.|外来的，无关的，不重要的|Not essential; irrelevant; coming from outside.|例句：Extraneous information can make it harder to focus on the main points.  无关的信息会让人更难集中注意要点。|例句：The book contained a lot of extraneous material. 这本书包含了很多无关紧要的材料。|近义词：irrelevant, superfluous, unnecessary, external|反义词：essential, relevant, intrinsic, inherent|词性:形容词

extraneous:/ɪkˈstreɪniəs/ /ɛkˈstreɪniəs/| n.|无关紧要的事物|Something that is not essential or relevant.|例句：We need to eliminate all the extraneous details and focus on the core issue. 我们需要剔除所有无关紧要的细节，专注于核心问题。|例句：Please remove any extraneous objects from the stage. 请把舞台上所有无关的物品移除。|近义词：irrelevancy, superfluity|反义词：essential, necessity|词性:名词

From Latin extraneus "external, foreign," from extra "outside."
