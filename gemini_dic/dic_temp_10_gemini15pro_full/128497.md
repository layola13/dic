newsreaders:/ˈnjuːzˌriːdərz/
n.
新闻播报员
A person who reads the news on television or radio.
The newsreaders announced the breaking news live from the scene.  新闻播报员从现场直播突发新闻。

The newsreader delivered the news with a somber tone.  新闻播报员以沉重的语气播报了这条新闻。

近义词:anchor, announcer, broadcaster, commentator
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "audience" or "viewer")
词性: noun
