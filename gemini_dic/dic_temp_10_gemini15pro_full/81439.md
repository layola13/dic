gratification:/ˌɡrætɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/
n.
满足;满意;喜悦
The feeling of pleasure or satisfaction that you get from achieving something or from having something that you wanted.
She felt a deep sense of gratification at having completed the marathon. 她完成马拉松后感到深深的满足感。
satisfaction, pleasure, fulfillment, enjoyment
disappointment, dissatisfaction, frustration
noun
