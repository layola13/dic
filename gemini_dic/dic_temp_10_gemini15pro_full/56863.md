dorsy:/ˈdɔːrsi/| adj.|背部的，背侧的|Relating to the back or dorsal surface, especially of an animal or plant.|The dorsy fin of a shark helps it to maintain balance. 鲨鱼的背鳍帮助它保持平衡。|近义词：dorsal, posterior|反义词：ventral, anterior|词性：形容词

词源：From Latin *dorsālis*, from *dorsum* (“back”).
