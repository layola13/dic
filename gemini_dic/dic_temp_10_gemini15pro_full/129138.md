no-better-than-one-should-be:/noʊ ˈbɛtər ðæn wʌn ʃʊd bi/| adj. | 不比应有的好；不过如此；名声不好 |  Used to describe someone whose behavior is morally questionable or not as good as it should be.  Implies a mild disapproval or disappointment. |  He's no-better-than-one-should-be, always getting into trouble with the law. 他不比应有的好，总是惹上官司。| Synonyms: disreputable, shady, questionable | Antonyms: respectable, honorable, upright | adjective
