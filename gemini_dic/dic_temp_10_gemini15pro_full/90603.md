hulotheism:/ˌhjuːləʊˈθiːɪzəm/
n.
万物有灵论;泛灵论
The belief that all things are imbued with a divine or spiritual essence.
Hulotheism sees divinity in every aspect of nature, from rocks and rivers to trees and animals. 万物有灵论认为自然界的每一个方面，从岩石、河流到树木和动物，都充满了神性或灵性。
Indigenous cultures often practice a form of hulotheism, revering the spirits of the land and its creatures.  许多土著文化都实行某种形式的万物有灵论，崇敬土地及其生物的精神。
近义词:animism, pantheism
反义词:atheism, materialism
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *hūlos* (ὕλη) "matter, wood, timber, material" + *theos* (θεός) "god".  The term is a relatively modern coinage, likely first appearing in the late 19th or early 20th century.
