not-on-your-life:/ˌnɒt ɒn jɔːr ˈlaɪf/ /ˌnɑːt ɑːn jʊr ˈlaɪf/
|idiom|绝对不行；没门；不可能|An emphatic way of saying "no." It expresses strong disagreement or refusal.| "Can I borrow your car?" "Not on your life!"  “我能借你的车吗？” “绝对不行！”|Synonyms: absolutely not, no way, never, under no circumstances, fat chance|Antonyms: definitely, certainly, by all means|phrase
