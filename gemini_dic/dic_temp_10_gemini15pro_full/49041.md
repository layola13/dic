deh:/deɪ/ |interj.| (表示轻蔑、不耐烦或怀疑) 呸; 切; 得了| An exclamation expressing scorn, impatience, or disbelief.  Essentially, it's a way of dismissing something as insignificant or untrue. |"Deh, you don't really expect me to believe that, do you?"  “得了，你不会真指望我相信吧？” |Near synonyms: pshaw, bah, humph|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but words expressing belief or agreement could be considered contrasting, such as "Indeed," "Absolutely," or "Certainly")|interjection
