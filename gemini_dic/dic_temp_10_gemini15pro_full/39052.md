ETYMOLOGY->
From conform + -ableness.  "Conform" comes from the Old French "conformer," meaning "to form in accordance with," ultimately from the Latin "conformare," meaning "to shape together."  "-ableness" is a suffix used to form nouns expressing a state or quality.
