desolators:/ˈdesəleɪtərz/
n.
破坏者，使荒芜的人或物
Those who cause widespread destruction or devastation, leaving a place barren or desolate.
The desolators swept through the city, leaving nothing but ruin in their wake.  破坏者席卷了这座城市，只留下废墟。
The war was a desolator of lives and hopes.  战争摧毁了生命和希望。
synonyms: destroyers, devastators, ravagers, wreckers
antonyms: creators, builders, preservers, protectors
noun
