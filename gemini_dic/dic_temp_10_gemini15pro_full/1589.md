achroodextrin: /ˌeɪkroʊˈdɛkstrɪn/
noun
消色糊精
A type of dextrin that is not colored by iodine. It is formed by the hydrolysis of starch.
The solution tested positive for achroodextrin.  该溶液的消色糊精测试呈阳性。
The enzyme breaks down starch into achroodextrin. 这种酶将淀粉分解成消色糊精。
synonyms:  limit dextrin
antonyms:  starch, amylose, amylopectin
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From a- (not) + chromo- (color) + dextrin.
