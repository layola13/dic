panniered:/ˈpænɪəd/| adj.|穿着裙撑的，膨大的|Having wide, projecting sides, like panniers (baskets formerly carried on either side of a pack animal or bicycle).  Resembling or suggesting the full shape of a skirt supported by panniers.|Her panniered gown swept across the ballroom floor. 她的穿着裙撑的礼服扫过舞池。|近义词：billowing, bouffant, full-skirted|反义词：slim, narrow, tight-fitting|词性:形容词
词源: From French *pannier* (basket), from Latin *pānārium* (bread basket).
