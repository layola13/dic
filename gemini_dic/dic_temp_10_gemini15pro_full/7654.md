annunciate: /əˈnʌnsieɪt/ (US) /əˈnʌnʃɪeɪt/ (UK)
verb
清晰地发音；宣布；通报
to pronounce words clearly and distinctly; to proclaim or announce something publicly or officially
She annunciated each syllable of the word carefully. 她仔细地念出了这个单词的每个音节。
The spokesperson annunciated the company's new policy. 发言人宣布了公司的新政策。
近义词: pronounce, articulate, declare, proclaim
反义词: mumble, mutter
词性: verb
