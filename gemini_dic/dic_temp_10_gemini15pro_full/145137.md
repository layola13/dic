overarticulation: /ˌoʊvərɑːrˌtɪkjʊˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 过度清晰的发音；过度发音 |  The act of pronouncing sounds or words more distinctly than is natural or necessary, often for emphasis or clarity, but sometimes perceived as affected or artificial. |  Her overarticulation made her sound like she was talking to a child. 她过度清晰的发音让她听起来像是在跟孩子说话。| synonyms: over-enunciation, hyperarticulation | antonyms: mumbling, slurring | noun

overarticulate: /ˌoʊvərɑːrˈtɪkjʊleɪt/ | v. |  过度清晰地发音；过度发音 | To pronounce sounds or words more distinctly than is natural or necessary. | He overarticulated every syllable, making it difficult to understand him. 他每个音节都发音过度清晰，反而让人难以理解。| synonyms: over-enunciate | antonyms: mumble, slur | verb
