biangulate:/baɪˈæŋɡjʊleɪt/| adj.|双角的，有两角的|Having two angles or corners.  Specifically referring to a geometric shape or a part of an organism having two angles or points. It's a technical term rarely used in everyday conversation.|The biangulate leaf had a distinctive shape. 这片双角的叶子形状独特。|近义词：two-angled, bicornuate|反义词: rounded, smooth|词性: adjective

biangulate:/baɪˈæŋɡjʊleɪt/| v.|使成两角|To make something have two angles or corners. This usage is even less common than the adjective form.|The artist biangulated the sculpture's base for stability.  艺术家将雕塑的底座做成两角以保持稳定。|近义词：angle, shape|反义词: round, smooth|词性: verb

词源: From the Latin prefix "bi-" (meaning "two") and the Latin "angulus" (meaning "angle" or "corner").
