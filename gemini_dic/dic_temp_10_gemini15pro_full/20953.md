/blɪːpt/ | adj. | 消音的，被屏蔽的 | Censored or obscured, typically with a bleeping sound, to remove offensive or sensitive material. |  The swear words in the broadcast were bleeped out.  广播中的脏话被消音了。 | censored, muted, expurgated | uncensored, unbleeped | adjective
