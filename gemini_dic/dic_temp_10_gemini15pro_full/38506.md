conciliate: /kənˈsɪlieɪt/ | v. | 安抚，调解，调停 | To stop someone from being angry or discontented; placate; to gain (esteem, good will, or favor) by deliberate effort. |  The company tried to conciliate the angry customers by offering them refunds. 公司试图通过提供退款来安抚愤怒的顾客。 | The mediator conciliated the two warring factions. 调解员调解了两个交战派别。 | synonyms: appease, placate, pacify, soothe, mollify | antonyms: antagonize, provoke, aggravate, incite | verb
