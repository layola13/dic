counterretaliation:/ˌkaʊntər rɪˌtæliˈeɪʃən/ | n. |报复；反报复；反击|an act of retaliation in response to an initial act of retaliation| The government's counterretaliation against the trade sanctions was swift and decisive. 政府对贸易制裁的反击迅速而果断。|  The general ordered counterretaliation after the enemy's surprise attack. 将军在敌人突袭后下令反击。 | synonyms: revenge, reprisal, retribution | antonyms: forgiveness, pardon | noun
