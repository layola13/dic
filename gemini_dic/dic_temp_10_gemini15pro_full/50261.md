depersonalises:/diːˈpɜːrsənəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使失去个性，使非人格化
To deprive of personality or individuality; to make impersonal or anonymous.
The hospital system often depersonalises patients, treating them as numbers rather than individuals.  医院系统常常使病人失去个性，把他们当作数字而不是个人。
Bureaucratic procedures can depersonalise the learning experience. 官僚程序会使学习体验失去个性。
近义词: dehumanise, objectify, anonymise
反义词: personalize, individualize
词性: verb
