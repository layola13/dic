hyperweak:/ˈhaɪpərwiːk/| adj.|超弱的，极弱的|Extremely weak; weaker than normal.|The hyperweak interaction is a fundamental force in physics.|例句：The hyperweak force is much weaker than the weak force. 超弱力比弱力弱得多。

例句：The signal was hyperweak and barely detectable. 信号极弱，几乎检测不到。|近义词：feeble, faint, very weak|反义词：strong, powerful|词性:形容词
