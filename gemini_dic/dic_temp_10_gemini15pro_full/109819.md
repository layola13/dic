lexeme: /ˈlɛksiːm/ | n. | 词位 | A lexeme is a unit of lexical meaning that exists regardless of any inflectional endings it may have or the number of words it may contain. It is the abstract unit underlying a set of forms related through inflection.  | The lexeme "run" includes the forms "runs," "running," and "ran." | 近义词: word, vocabulary item | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
