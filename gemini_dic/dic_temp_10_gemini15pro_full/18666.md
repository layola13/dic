beplumed:/bɪˈpluːmd/
adjective
有羽毛装饰的；戴着羽毛的
adorned or decorated with feathers, often in a showy or elaborate way
The knight's helmet was beplumed with bright red feathers. 这位骑士的头盔上装饰着鲜红色的羽毛。
The beplumed dancer moved gracefully across the stage.  这位戴着羽毛装饰的舞者优雅地穿过舞台。
近义词: feathered, plumed, adorned
反义词: bare, unadorned
词性: adjective

词源:
From *be- + plumed, past participle of plume (“to decorate with feathers”).  由前缀 *be- 和 plume 的过去分词 plumed 构成，plume 的意思是“用羽毛装饰”。
