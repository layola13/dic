nitwittery:/ˈnɪtwɪtəri/
noun
愚蠢的行为；傻事；胡闹
Frivolous or silly behavior or activities; foolishness.
Her constant nitwittery was starting to annoy everyone. 她不断的胡闹开始惹恼所有人。
Synonyms: foolishness, silliness, tomfoolery, absurdity, idiocy
Antonyms: seriousness, wisdom, sense, prudence
词性: 名词
