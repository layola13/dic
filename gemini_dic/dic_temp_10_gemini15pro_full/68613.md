fantastically:/fænˈtæstɪkli/ | adv. | 极好地，奇妙地，非常地 |  In a fantastic manner; wonderfully; extraordinarily.  | The party was fantastically decorated. 派对装饰得非常棒。| She played fantastically well. 她演奏得非常好。 | 近义词: wonderfully, marvelously, incredibly, superbly, brilliantly | 反义词: terribly, awfully, poorly, badly | 词性: 副词

词源: From fantastic + -ally. "Fantastic" comes from the Late Latin *fantasticus, from the Greek phantastikos "able to present to the mind," from phantazein "to make visible, present to the mind," from phantasma "appearance, phantom, image."
