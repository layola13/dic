dionysus:/daɪəˈnaɪsəs/| n.|狄俄尼索斯; 酒神|The Greek god of wine, vegetation, festivity, and theatre.  He is associated with ecstasy, madness, and resurrection.| Dionysus was worshipped with wild abandon. 狄俄尼索斯被狂热地崇拜着。| The play explored the themes of revelry and abandon associated with Dionysus.  这出戏探讨了与狄俄尼索斯相关的狂欢和放纵的主题。|Synonyms: Bacchus, Liber|Antonyms: None|Noun
