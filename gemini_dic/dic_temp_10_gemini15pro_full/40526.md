controllership:/kənˈtrəʊləʃɪp/ | n. |主计长职位；主计长职责；财务控制|The position and duties of a controller, especially in a company or organization.  It encompasses the overall management and supervision of accounting, financial reporting, internal controls, and other related financial activities. | The company is searching for a new controllership to oversee financial operations.  这家公司正在寻找一位新的主计长来监督财务运作。|  The controllership plays a vital role in ensuring the financial health of the organization.  主计长在确保组织的财务健康方面发挥着至关重要的作用。 | synonyms: comptrollership, financial control, financial management | antonyms: N/A | noun
