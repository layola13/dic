nonindustry:/nɒnˈɪndəstri/ | n. | 非产业; 非工业部门 |  Referring to sectors of the economy that are not involved in manufacturing or production of goods.  It encompasses services, government, education, healthcare, etc. | The growth of the nonindustry sector is outpacing that of manufacturing.  非工业部门的增长超过了制造业。|  The government is investing heavily in the nonindustry sector to create more jobs. 政府正在大力投资非工业部门以创造更多就业机会。| Synonyms: service sector, tertiary sector | Antonyms: industry, manufacturing sector | noun
