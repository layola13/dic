balladic:/bəˈlædɪk/
adjective
叙事诗的；民谣的
Resembling or characteristic of a ballad; suitable for a ballad.  Relating to or suggestive of ballads.
The song had a balladic quality, telling a story of love and loss.  这首歌具有叙事诗的性质，讲述了一个关于爱与失去的故事。
The musician played a balladic piece on the guitar.  音乐家在吉他上演奏了一首民谣风格的曲子。
近义词:narrative, story-like, lyrical, poetic
反义词:prosaic, mundane
