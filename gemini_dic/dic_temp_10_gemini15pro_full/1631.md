aciform:/ˈæsɪfɔːrm/
adjective
针状的，针形的
Shaped like a needle; needle-shaped.
The aciform leaves of the pine tree help it conserve water. 松树针状的叶子有助于其保存水分。

The mosquito has an aciform proboscis for piercing skin. 蚊子有一个针状的喙用来刺穿皮肤。
近义词:needle-shaped, acicular, acerose
反义词:obtuse, rounded
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin acus (needle) + -formis (shaped like).

USAGE->
The aciform beak of the bird allowed it to access nectar deep within flowers.  这只鸟针状的喙使它能够吸取花朵深处的花蜜。

Aciform crystals were observed under the microscope. 在显微镜下观察到针状晶体。
