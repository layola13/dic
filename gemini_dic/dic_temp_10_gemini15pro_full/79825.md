glycosometer:/ˌɡlaɪkɒˈsɒmɪtər/ | n. | 血糖计 | An instrument for measuring the amount of sugar (glucose) in the blood. |  Example 1: The diabetic patient used a glycosometer to monitor their blood sugar levels. 糖尿病患者使用血糖计监测他们的血糖水平。
