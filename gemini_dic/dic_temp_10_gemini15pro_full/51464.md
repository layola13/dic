detonator: /ˈdɛtəneɪtər/ (US), /ˈdɛtəneɪtə/ (UK)| n. |引爆器，雷管|A device used to set off an explosive charge, such as dynamite. |例句：The detonator triggered the explosion. 雷管引爆了炸药。

例句：He carefully inserted the detonator into the explosive. 他小心地将雷管插入炸药中。

近义词：fuse, primer, trigger|反义词: None|词性: Noun


detonators: /ˈdɛtəneɪtərz/ (US), /ˈdɛtəneɪtəz/ (UK)| n. |引爆器（复数）,雷管（复数）|Plural form of "detonator".|例句：The workers discovered a box of detonators. 工人们发现了一箱雷管。


例句：The police confiscated several detonators from the suspect. 警察从嫌疑人那里没收了几个雷管。
