lavish: /ˈlævɪʃ/ | adj. vt. | 奢华的；丰富的；慷慨的；浪费；挥霍 |  marked by profusion or excess; extravagant; to expend or bestow profusely | The hotel was lavishly decorated for the holidays.  这家酒店为了节日装饰得非常奢华。 |  She lavished attention on her guests. 她对客人关怀备至。 | 近义词: luxurious, opulent, extravagant, profuse, generous | 反义词: meager, sparse, stingy, frugal | 词性: 形容词，动词 | 词源: From Middle French *lavasse (“deluge of rain”), from laver (“to wash”), from Latin lavō.  Initially meant "profuse, overflowing".
