fellest:/ˈfɛlɪst/
adjective
最凶狠的，最残酷的
most fierce, cruel, or savage
例句：He delivered the fellest blow to his opponent. 他给了对手最狠毒的一击。

例句：The fellest of the storms raged through the valley.  山谷里肆虐着最猛烈的暴风雨。

近义词：fiercest, cruelest, savagest, most brutal
反义词：kindest, gentlest, most merciful
词性：adjective (形容词)


词源:
From Middle English *fellest, superlative of fel (“fierce, cruel, terrible, dreadful, savage, ruthless, intense, strong, great, mighty, hard, severe, harsh, sharp, keen, painful, deadly, destructive, fatal, sinister, evil, wicked, bad, difficult, arduous, heavy, serious, grave, weighty”).
