grizelin:/ɡrɪzəlɪn/ | n. | 灰黄霉素 | An antifungal antibiotic derived from various species of *Penicillium*. Used to treat fungal infections of the skin, hair, and nails. |  Grizelin is effective against dermatophytes. 灰黄霉素对皮肤癣菌有效。|  She was prescribed grizelin for a persistent nail infection.  她因持续的指甲感染开了灰黄霉素。 | Synonyms: griseofulvin | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider using terms like "pro-fungal" or names of specific antifungal medications that have opposing mechanisms of action) | noun
