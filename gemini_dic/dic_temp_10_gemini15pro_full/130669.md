nonadvocate:/ˌnɒnˈædvəkət/ | n. | 非倡导者；不提倡者 | Someone who does not advocate or support a particular cause or policy. |  The politician was a nonadvocate for the proposed tax increase. 这位政治家不提倡拟议中的增税方案。|  He was a nonadvocate of violence as a means of resolving conflict. 他不提倡使用暴力作为解决冲突的手段。 | 近义词: opponent, critic, dissenter | 反义词: advocate, supporter, proponent | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From non- (not) + advocate (from Latin *advocatus, past participle of advocare "to summon, call to one's aid," from ad- "to" + vocare "to call").
