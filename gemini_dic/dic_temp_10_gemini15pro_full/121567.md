misnavigate: /ˌmɪsnˈævɪɡeɪt/
verb
错误导航，迷航
To navigate incorrectly; to get lost or go the wrong way, especially while sailing or flying.
The captain misnavigated and the ship ran aground. 船长导航错误，导致船搁浅了。
The pilot misnavigated and the plane ended up in the wrong city. 飞行员导航错误，飞机最终到了错误的城市。

近义词: get lost, lose one's way, go astray
反义词: navigate correctly, find one's way
