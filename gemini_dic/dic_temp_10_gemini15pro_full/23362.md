braced: /breɪst/ | adj. | 支撑的，加固的；准备好的，做好心理准备的 | Prepared for something difficult or unpleasant; strengthened or reinforced. | The building was braced for the hurricane.  这栋建筑已做好应对飓风的准备。 | 近义词: prepared, ready, reinforced, fortified, strengthened | 反义词: unprepared, vulnerable, weak | 词性: 形容词

braced: /breɪst/ | v. | 支撑，加固；（使）做好准备 | past participle of *brace* meaning to prepare (oneself) for something difficult or unpleasant; make (something) stronger or firmer with wood, iron, or other forms of support. | He braced himself for the impact. 他做好了承受撞击的准备。 | 近义词: prepare, steady, support, strengthen | 反义词: weaken | 词性: 动词

词源: From Middle English *bracen*, from Old French *bracier* "to embrace, enclose, fasten," from Vulgar Latin *brachiare*, from Latin *bracchium* "arm."
