hypomixolydian:/ˌhaɪpoʊmɪksoʊˈlɪdiən/
adjective
次混合利底亚调式
A musical mode derived from the Mixolydian mode by lowering the second degree by a semitone.  It is a mode with a minor seventh and a major third.
The guitarist used a hypomixolydian scale in his solo. 吉他手在他的独奏中使用了次混合利底亚音阶。
近义词: Mixolydian (somewhat), Dorian (somewhat)
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective

Etymology:
"Hypo-" (Greek:  ὑπο) meaning "under" or "lowered" + "Mixolydian" (Greek name of a musical mode).
