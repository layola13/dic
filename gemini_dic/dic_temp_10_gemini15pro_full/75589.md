fuehrer:/ˈfʊərər/| n.|元首，领袖 (尤指纳粹德国的希特勒)|A ruthless, tyrannical leader. A term most strongly associated with Adolf Hitler.  It translates literally from German as "leader" or "guide".|Hitler was known as the Fuehrer. 希特勒被称为元首。|Synonym: leader, dictator, chief, head|Antonym: follower, subordinate|noun
