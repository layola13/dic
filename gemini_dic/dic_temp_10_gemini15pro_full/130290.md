non-sustainable:/ˌnɒn səˈsteɪnəbl/ | adj. | 不可持续的 | Not able to be maintained at the current rate or level.  |  例句：The current rate of deforestation is non-sustainable.  当前的森林砍伐速度是不可持续的。| 近义词：unsustainable, ruinous, depleting | 反义词：sustainable, renewable, enduring | 词性: 形容词
