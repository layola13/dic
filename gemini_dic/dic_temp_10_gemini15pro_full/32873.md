chomping:/ˈtʃɒmpɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈtʃɑːmpɪŋ/ (US)| v. | 大声咀嚼，咬; (非正式)渴望，急切地期待 |  The horse was chomping on its bit.  马大声地嚼着嚼子。| 近义词：munching, gnawing, chewing, champing;  eager for, keen on | 反义词：nibbling, sipping | 词性: 动词 (present participle)

词源:  Chomping comes from the verb "chomp," which is imitative of the sound of loud chewing.  It's related to "champ."
