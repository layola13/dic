abaiser:/abɛze/| vt.|降低，贬低; 屈尊俯就|To lower or debase; to condescend. |He abaises himself by associating with such people. 他自贬身份与这些人交往。|近义词：lower, debase, degrade, humble, stoop|反义词：elevate, raise, exalt|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *abaissier*, from *a-* (to) + *baissier* (to lower), from Vulgar Latin *bassare*, from Late Latin *bassus* (low). Ultimately from a pre-Indo-European source.
