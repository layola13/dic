calcography:/kælˈkɒɡrəfi/| n. |铜版雕刻术；铜版画|The art of engraving on copper or other metal plates, typically with a burin, and printing the design onto paper. |例句：The museum boasts a remarkable collection of 18th-century calcography. 这家博物馆收藏了大量的18世纪铜版画。

例句：He studied calcography under a renowned master. 他在一位著名的师傅指导下学习铜版雕刻术。|近义词：engraving, etching|反义词: None|词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *calcographie*, from Italian *calcografia*, from Ancient Greek *χαλκός* (khalkós, “copper, bronze”) + *-γραφία* (-graphía, “writing”).
