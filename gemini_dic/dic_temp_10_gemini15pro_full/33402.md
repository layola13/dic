churchiest:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃiɪst/
adj.
最像教堂的;最虔诚的
Most resembling a church; most devoutly religious.
例句：He had the churchiest voice I'd ever heard. 他拥有我听过的最像教堂唱诗班的嗓音。

例句：She was the churchiest woman in the whole town, attending services every single day.  她是全镇最虔诚的女人，每天都参加礼拜。

近义词：devout, pious, religious
反义词：irreligious, impious, secular
词性: 形容词

词源: church + -y + -est.  From "church" meaning a building for public Christian worship. The suffix "-y" forms adjectives meaning "having the quality of" or "full of". The suffix "-est" forms the superlative degree of adjectives.
