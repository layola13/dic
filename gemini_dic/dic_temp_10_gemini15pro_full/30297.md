ceno:/ˈsiːnoʊ/
n.
dinner, supper
The main meal of the day, typically eaten in the evening.
We had a delicious ceno at a local restaurant. 我们在当地一家餐馆吃了一顿美味的晚餐。
The family gathered for ceno around the large table. 全家人围坐在大桌子旁吃晚餐。
Synonyms: dinner, supper, meal, repast
Antonyms: breakfast, lunch, brunch
noun


Etymology->
From Latin *cena, from Proto-Italic *kenā, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰekʷ- (“to take, accept”). Cognate with Ancient Greek δείκνυμι (deíknumi, “I show”), Sanskrit दिशति (diśati, “he shows, points out”).
