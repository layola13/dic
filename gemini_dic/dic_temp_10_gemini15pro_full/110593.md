limitlessly:/ˈlɪmɪtləsli/
adverb
无限地；无限制地
Without any limits; boundlessly.
He believed he could improve limitlessly. 他相信自己可以无限进步。
limitlessly:/ˈlɪmɪtləsli/
Synonyms: boundlessly, endlessly, infinitely, unrestrainedly
Antonyms: finitely, restrictedly
词性: 副词
