non-premium:/nɒnˈprɪːmiəm/ | adj. | 非溢价的；非高级的；普通的；标准的 | Not involving or requiring an extra fee or charge; standard or basic. |  This is the non-premium version of the software, with limited features. 这是该软件的非高级版本，功能有限。| The hotel offers both premium and non-premium rooms. 这家酒店提供高级客房和普通客房。 | 近义词: standard, basic, regular, ordinary | 反义词: premium, high-end, luxury | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + premium (from Latin praemium, meaning "reward, prize")
