overtechnicalities:/ˌoʊvərˌtɛknɪˈkælɪtiz/ | n. | 过于技术性的细节；过分拘泥于技术细节 | Excessive focus on technical details, often to the detriment of clarity or practicality. | The lawyer lost the jury in a bog of overtechnicalities. 律师在一堆过于技术性的细节中失去了陪审团的支持。| The manual was so full of overtechnicalities that the average user couldn't understand it.  这本手册充满了过于技术性的细节，以至于普通用户无法理解。 | 近义词：jargon, technicalities, minutiae | 反义词：simplicities, basics | 词性：名词

词源:
"over-" (prefix) + "technicalities" (plural of "technicality")

"over-" 表示“过度”、“过分”

"technicality" 源于 "technical,"  最终源于希腊语 "tekhnikos" (τεχνικός),  意思是 "of art," "skillful,"  与 "tekhnē" (τέχνη)  "art," "skill" 相关。
