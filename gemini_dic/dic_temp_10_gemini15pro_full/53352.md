disadvantaging:/ˌdɪsədˈvæntɪdʒɪŋ/
verb
使处于不利地位，使吃亏
putting someone or something in a worse position compared to others
The new policy is disadvantaging low-income families. 新政策使低收入家庭处于不利地位。
synonyms: hindering, handicapping, impairing
antonyms:  advantaging, benefiting, aiding
