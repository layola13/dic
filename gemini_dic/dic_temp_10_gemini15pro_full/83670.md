hafter:/ˈhæftər/| n.|柄，把手；(尤指刀、剑、斧的)柄|A handle, especially of a knife, sword, or axe.|He grabbed the hafter of the axe and swung it.|近义词：handle, hilt, grip|反义词:blade|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hæfter, from Proto-Germanic *haftō, from Proto-Indo-European *kep- (“to seize, hold”). Cognate with Dutch heft (“heft, handle”), German Heft (“heft, handle, booklet”), Old Norse haft (“haft, handle”).
