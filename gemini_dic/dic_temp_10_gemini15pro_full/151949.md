pastorale:/ˌpɑːstəˈrɑːl/ | n. adj. | 田园曲，田园诗；田园景色；田园的 | A musical composition that evokes a rural scene; a literary or artistic work describing rural life; relating to rural life. |  例句：The composer's pastorale evoked images of rolling hills and sheep grazing in the meadow. 作曲家的田园曲唤起了连绵起伏的群山和绵羊在草地上吃草的景象。

例句：The painting was a beautiful pastorale, depicting a tranquil countryside scene. 这幅画是一幅美丽的田园景色，描绘了宁静的乡村景象。| 近义词：bucolic, idyllic, rustic | 反义词：urban, metropolitan | 词性: 名词, 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *pastorale*, from Late Latin *pastoralis* "of shepherds," from Latin *pastor* "shepherd," from *pascere* "to feed, graze."
