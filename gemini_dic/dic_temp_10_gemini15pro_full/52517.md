|n.|区别，分化，变异|The process of distinguishing or making different; the state of being distinguished or different. |Cell differentiation is essential for the development of complex organisms. 细胞分化对于复杂生物体的发育至关重要。|
|近义词: distinction, variation, modification|
|反义词: similarity, uniformity, identity|

differentiate:/ˌdɪfəˈrenʃiˌeɪt/

|反义词: confuse, conflate|
