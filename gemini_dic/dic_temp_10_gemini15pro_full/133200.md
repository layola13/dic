nonevolutionist:/ˌnɒnˌiːvəˈluːʃənɪst/
n.|非进化论者|A person who does not believe in the theory of evolution.|The nonevolutionist argued against the fossil record.|近义词:creationist|反义词:evolutionist|词性:名词
Etymology:  "non-" (not) + "evolution" (the process of gradual development) + "-ist" (one who believes in or advocates a particular theory).  "Evolution" comes from Latin "evolutio," meaning "an unrolling."
