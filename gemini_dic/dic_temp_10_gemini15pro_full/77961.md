genteeler:/dʒɛnˈtiːlər/
adjective
更文雅的，更有教养的
More refined or polite in manner, more aristocratic.
She has become much genteeler since she moved to the city.  她搬到城市后变得更有教养了。
He spoke in a genteeler accent than his brothers. 他说话的口音比他的兄弟们更文雅。
近义词:refined, polished, cultured, sophisticated, courteous
反义词:coarse, vulgar, rude, unrefined, uncouth
词性:adjective

词源:
From genteel + -er (comparative suffix).  Genteel comes from Old French gentil, ultimately from Latin gentilis "of the same clan or family."
