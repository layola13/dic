hexamethylenediamine:/ˌhɛksəmɛθɪˈliːndiːəˌmiːn/
noun
六亚甲基二胺
A colorless, crystalline solid with a fish-like odor, used in the production of nylon.
Hexamethylenediamine reacts with adipic acid to form nylon 66. 六亚甲基二胺与己二酸反应生成尼龙66。
近义词: 1,6-diaminohexane, 1,6-hexane diamine
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a chemical compound)
词性: n.
