noncircumstantial:/ˌnɒnsɜːrkəmˈstænʃəl/
adjective
非间接的；直接的；非依据情况的
Based on direct observation or inherent qualities rather than on circumstances or evidence.
The witness gave noncircumstantial evidence of the crime, saying she saw the suspect commit the act.  证人提供了犯罪的直接证据，说她亲眼看到嫌疑人实施了犯罪行为。

The lawyer presented noncircumstantial proof of his client's innocence. 律师提供了其客户无罪的直接证据。

近义词:direct, straightforward, conclusive
反义词:circumstantial, indirect, inferential
词性:adjective
