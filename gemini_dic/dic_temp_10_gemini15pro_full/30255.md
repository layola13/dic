celsius: /ˈselsiəs/ /ˈsɛlsiəs/ | adj. n.| 摄氏的；摄氏度 |A scale of temperature in which water freezes at 0° and boils at 100° under standard conditions. |例句：The temperature is 25 degrees Celsius. 温度是25摄氏度。|近义词：centigrade |反义词: Fahrenheit|词性: adjective, noun
