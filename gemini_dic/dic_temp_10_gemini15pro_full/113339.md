luxuriate: /lʌɡˈʒʊərieɪt/ | v. | 沉溺；尽情享受；生长茂盛 | to enjoy oneself in a luxurious way; to grow well |  She luxuriated in a hot bath after a long day. 她劳累了一天后，享受了一个热水澡。| The plants luxuriated in the warm sunshine. 植物在温暖的阳光下茂盛生长。 | 近义词: indulge, revel, bask, flourish, thrive | 反义词: deprive, suffer, wither | 词性: 动词

词源: From Latin luxuriatus, past participle of luxuriare ("to be luxuriant, to abound"), from luxus ("excess, luxury").
