contrariwise:/ˌkɒntrəˈraɪwaɪz/ | adv. | 相反地，反之 | On the contrary; in the opposite way. | He thought the medicine would make him better, but contrariwise, it made him worse. 他以为这药会让他好转，但相反，它让他更糟了。|  The price didn't go up; contrariwise, it went down. 价格没有上涨；相反，它下降了。 |Synonyms: conversely, inversely, on the contrary, vice versa | Antonyms: similarly, likewise | Adverb
