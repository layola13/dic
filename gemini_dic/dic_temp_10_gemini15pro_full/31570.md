chasidism:/ˈhɑːsɪdɪzəm/ /ˈkɑːsɪdɪzəm/
n.
哈西德主义，哈西德教派
A Jewish religious movement emphasizing mystical piety, joy, and the leadership of charismatic rabbis.
Chasidism is a branch of Orthodox Judaism. 哈西德主义是正统犹太教的一个分支。
The Chasidim are known for their distinctive dress. 哈西德派以其独特的服饰而闻名。
近义词: Hasidism, Hasidic Judaism
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps secular Judaism or Reform Judaism could be considered contrasting ideologies.)
词性: noun
