expound:/ɪkˈspaʊnd/| v. |阐述，详细解释|To present and explain (a theory or idea) systematically and in detail.|She expounded her views on the subject to the committee. 她向委员会阐述了她对这个问题的看法。|The professor expounded on the theory of relativity. 教授详细讲解了相对论。|近义词：explain, elaborate, interpret, clarify|反义词：summarize, condense|词性:动词
