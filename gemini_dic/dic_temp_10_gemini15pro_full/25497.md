bullheaded:/ˈbʊlˌhɛdɪd/| adj.|顽固的，固执的，倔强的|Stubbornly unyielding; unreasonably persistent in an opinion or course of action, refusing to listen to advice or reason.  Like a bull charging ahead without thinking.|He was bullheaded about the project, refusing to consider any other approaches. 他对这个项目很固执，拒绝考虑任何其他方法。|synonyms: obstinate, stubborn, headstrong, pigheaded, inflexible, intractable|antonyms: compliant, amenable, flexible, open-minded|adjective

bullheaded:/ˈbʊlˌhɛdɪd/| adj.|愚笨的，鲁莽的|Stupidly obstinate; foolishly persistent.|It was a bullheaded decision to invest all his money in one stock.  把所有钱都投资在一支股票上是一个愚蠢的决定。|synonyms: foolish, unwise, reckless, imprudent|antonyms: wise, prudent, sensible, judicious|adjective
