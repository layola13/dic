overveil:/ˌoʊvərˈveɪl/
verb|遮盖，掩盖|To cover or conceal something, as with a veil.|A light mist overveiled the valley. 薄雾笼罩着山谷。| 近义词: conceal, obscure, shroud, cover, hide | 反义词: reveal, uncover, expose |词性: 动词

overveil:/ˌoʊvərˈveɪl/
noun|遮盖物，面纱|Something that covers or conceals, like a veil.|The overveil of secrecy surrounding the project made it difficult to understand.  围绕该项目的保密面纱使其难以理解。|近义词: veil, covering, screen, cloak, mask | 反义词: exposure, revelation |词性: 名词
