decalcification:/diːˌkælsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
脱钙，去钙
The process of removing calcium salts from bone or other tissue.
The archaeologist used a decalcification solution to remove the calcium deposits from the ancient bones. 考古学家使用脱钙溶液去除古代骨骼上的钙沉积物。
demineralization, softening
calcification, mineralization
noun
