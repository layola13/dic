hybridises:/ˈhaɪbrɪdaɪzɪz/
verb
杂交，使杂交
causes (different varieties of plant or animal) to breed with each other.
例句：
Scientists are hybridising different species of plants to create new varieties.
科学家们正在将不同种类的植物杂交以创造新品种。

The roses have hybridised with the wild roses in the hedgerow.
这些玫瑰与树篱中的野生玫瑰杂交了。

近义词：crossbreed, interbreed, cross-fertilize
反义词：inbreed
