nigglingly:/ˈnɪɡlɪŋli/
adverb|琐碎地；吹毛求疵地；烦扰地|in a niggling way; in a petty or annoying manner|The problem has been nigglingly bothering me all day.  这个问题一整天都在烦扰着我。|The pain in his shoulder nigglingly persisted. 他肩膀的疼痛一直持续地困扰着他。|近义词：persistently, constantly, annoyingly, pettily|反义词：significantly, importantly, considerably|词性: 副词

词源: From the verb "niggle" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Niggle" comes from Middle English "nigelen," probably of imitative origin, suggesting a slight, repetitive action.
