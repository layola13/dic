benightedness:/bɪˈnaɪtɪdnəs/| n.|愚昧，无知|A state of ignorance or intellectual darkness; lack of enlightenment.|The benightedness of the villagers led them to believe the charlatan's false promises. 村民的愚昧使他们相信了骗子的虚假承诺。|Example 2:  Her benightedness about current affairs was surprising. 她对时事的无知令人惊讶。|Synonyms: ignorance, unenlightenment, backwardness, primitiveness|Antonyms: enlightenment, knowledge, sophistication, wisdom|noun

benighted:/bɪˈnaɪtɪd/| adj.|愚昧的，无知的|Intellectually or morally ignorant; unenlightened.|The benighted travelers were lost in the forest. 愚昧的旅行者在森林里迷路了。|Example 2:  His benighted views on race relations were offensive. 他在种族关系上的愚昧观点令人反感。|Synonyms: ignorant, unenlightened, backward, primitive|Antonyms: enlightened, knowledgeable, sophisticated, wise|adjective
