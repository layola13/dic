She has an umbrella. 她有一把伞。

Present Continuous:

She is reading an interesting book. 她在读一本有趣的书。

Present Perfect:
I have seen an elephant at the zoo. 我在动物园里见过一头大象。
She has written an email to her boss. 她已经给她老板写了一封电子邮件。

Present Perfect Continuous:

Simple Past:

Past Continuous:

Past Perfect:

She had already seen an elephant. 她已经见过一头大象了。


Past Perfect Continuous:
