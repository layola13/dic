crestlike:/ˈkrɛstlaɪk/
adjective
像冠的；似山顶的
Resembling a crest, such as the top of a wave or a mountain.
The snow-covered peaks had a crestlike appearance.  白雪覆盖的山峰看起来像冠状。
The waves formed crestlike shapes as they crashed against the rocks. 海浪撞击岩石时形成了像山顶一样的形状。
crest-shaped, crownlike
troughlike
adjective
