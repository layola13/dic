coordinated:/koʊˈɔːrdɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
协调的，配合的
harmonized, integrated, synchronized, aligned, organized
uncoordinated, disorganized, chaotic, disjointed, rambling
adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From the Latin word "coordinatus," past participle of "coordinare" ("to arrange together"), from "co-" ("together") + "ordinare" ("to arrange").

DEFINITION->
(adjective) Brought into a common action, movement, or condition; harmoniously organized or integrated; operating together smoothly and efficiently.

EXAMPLE->
The dancers' movements were perfectly coordinated.  舞者们的动作完美协调。

EXAMPLE->
She wore a coordinated outfit of skirt and jacket. 她穿着一套协调的裙子和夹克。


SYNONYM->
harmonized, integrated, synchronized, aligned, organized


ANTONYM->
uncoordinated, disorganized, chaotic, disjointed, rambling
