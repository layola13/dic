hydrotherapy:/ˌhaɪdrəˈθɛrəpi/| n.|水疗法|The use of water (hot, cold, steam, or ice) in the treatment of disease or injury.|例句：Hydrotherapy can be beneficial for arthritis sufferers. 水疗法对关节炎患者有益。

例句：She underwent hydrotherapy after her knee surgery. 她在膝盖手术后接受了水疗。|近义词：aquatic therapy, water cure|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From hydro- +‎ therapy, from Ancient Greek ὕδωρ (húdōr, “water”) + θεραπεία (therapeía, “healing, medical treatment”).
