nonjuridic:/ˌnɒnˈdʒʊərɪdɪk/
adjective
非法律的; 非司法性的
Not pertaining to law or jurisprudence.  Relating to matters outside the scope of legal proceedings or judicial authority.
The club's nonjuridic internal dispute resolution process helped avoid costly litigation.  俱乐部内部的非司法纠纷解决程序帮助避免了昂贵的诉讼。
The committee made a nonjuridic decision, choosing to focus on ethical rather than legal implications. 委员会做出了一个非法律的决定，选择关注道德而非法律含义。
Synonyms: extralegal, nonlegal
Antonyms: legal, judicial
词性: 形容词
