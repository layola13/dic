inter-sets:/ˈɪntəˌsɛts/ | n. | 组间休息 |The period of rest or low-intensity exercise taken between sets of weightlifting or other resistance training exercises. | I usually take a 60-second inter-set rest. 我组间休息通常是60秒。 |  He kept his inter-sets short to maintain an elevated heart rate. 他缩短组间休息时间以保持较高的心率。 | 近义词: rest period, between-sets break | 反义词: set, exercise | 词性: 名词
