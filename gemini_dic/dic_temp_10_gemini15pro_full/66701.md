exonerating:/ɪɡˈzɑːnəreɪtɪŋ/
|v. (present participle of exonerate)|免除…的责任；证明…的清白|clearing someone of blame or suspicion; showing that someone is not guilty of wrongdoing.|The evidence presented was exonerating, proving his innocence in the crime. 提出的证据为他洗清了罪名，证明了他在犯罪事件中的清白。|近义词：absolving, clearing, vindicating|反义词：incriminating, condemning|词性: Verb (present participle)

exonerate:/ɪɡˈzɑːnəreɪt/
|vt.|免除（某人）的责任；证明…的清白|to clear from blame or accusation; to prove innocent.|The jury's verdict exonerated the defendant. 陪审团的裁决证明了被告无罪。|近义词：absolve, acquit, clear, vindicate|反义词：incriminate, condemn, convict|词性: Verb

词源:
From Latin exoneratus, past participle of exonerare "to free from a burden," from ex- "out of" + onus "burden."
