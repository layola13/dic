misbounding:/ˌmɪsˈbaʊndɪŋ/
|v.|错误地划定界限；跳跃失误|The surveyor misbounded the property, leading to a dispute with the neighbor.  测量员错误地划定了房产的界限，导致了与邻居的纠纷。|
|v.|错误估计；判断失误|He misbounded the horse's ability and fell off during the jump. 他错误估计了马的能力，在跳跃时摔了下来。|
|近义词：miscalculate, misjudge, misestimate|
|反义词：calculate correctly, judge accurately, estimate correctly|
|词性：动词|


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + bounding (from bound, meaning "to leap" or "to set limits").  源自 mis-（意为“错误地”或“糟糕地”）+ bounding（来自 bound，意为“跳跃”或“设定界限”）。
