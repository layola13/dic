hornpipe:/ˈhɔːrnpaɪp/ (US); /ˈhɔːnpaɪp/ (UK)| n. |角笛舞; 角笛舞曲|A lively dance, traditionally popular with sailors, or the music for this dance. Originally, the hornpipe was played on a wind instrument made from an animal's horn.|The sailors danced a hornpipe on deck. 水手们在甲板上跳起了角笛舞。|近义词：jig, reel|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'slow dance' or 'ballad')|noun

hornpipe:/ˈhɔːrnpaɪp/ (US); /ˈhɔːnpaɪp/ (UK)| n. |角笛|A simple wind instrument originally made from an animal's horn.|The musician played a lively tune on his hornpipe. 音乐家用他的角笛吹奏了一首欢快的曲子。|近义词：recorder, flute|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
