microglia:/maɪˈkroʊɡliːə/ | n. | 小胶质细胞 | A type of glial cell that acts as the first and main form of active immune defense in the central nervous system (CNS).  | Microglia are crucial for maintaining the health of the brain. 小胶质细胞对维持大脑健康至关重要。 | Microglia play a vital role in synaptic pruning during development.  小胶质细胞在发育过程中对突触修剪起着至关重要的作用。 | 近义词:  None (Microglia are a specific cell type) | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From micro- + -glia.  "Micro-" comes from Greek "mikrós" meaning "small". "-glia" comes from Greek "glía" meaning "glue".
