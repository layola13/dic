cinctured:/ˈsɪŋktʃərd/
adjective
束带的，环绕的
Encircled or bound with a belt or girdle.  Having a belt or girdle-like band.
The knight was cinctured with a sword belt. 骑士腰间束着剑带。
Near Synonyms: belted, girdled, encompassed, surrounded
Antonyms: unbound, unbelted, loose
词性: adjective


cincture:/ˈsɪŋktʃər/
noun
腰带，带状物
A belt, girdle, or something that encircles like a belt. The act of encircling.
The monk's cincture was a simple rope.  和尚的腰带是一根简单的绳子。
Near Synonyms: belt, girdle, band, sash
Antonyms: gap, opening
