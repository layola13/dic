crestfallenness:/ˈkrɛstˌfɔːlənɪs/| n.|沮丧，垂头丧气|The state of being crestfallen; dejection.  A feeling of disappointment and discouragement.|His crestfallenness was evident after he lost the match. 他输了比赛后，沮丧之情溢于言表。|Synonyms: dejection, despondency, discouragement, downheartedness|Antonyms: elation, joy, happiness|noun
