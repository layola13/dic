biphasic:/baɪˈfeɪzɪk/
adjective
双相的，两相的
Having two phases or stages.
例句：
The biphasic sleep pattern of the elderly often involves a nap in the afternoon.
老年人的双相睡眠模式通常包括下午的小睡。

The medication is delivered in a biphasic manner, with an initial dose followed by a booster.
这种药物以双相方式递送，初始剂量后再追加一剂。

近义词：two-phase, two-stage
反义词：monophasic
词性：adjective
