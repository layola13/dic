nondelegation:/ˌnɒndɛlɪˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. | 不可委托；不授权 | The principle that one to whom power is delegated cannot further delegate that power. | The court upheld the principle of nondelegation. 法院维持了不可委托原则。| The president's nondelegation of authority frustrated his staff. 总统不授权的行为让他的员工感到沮丧。 |  Synonym: non-assignment,  reservation | Antonym: delegation, assignment | noun
