disoblige:/ˌdɪsəˈblaɪdʒ/ | v. | 怠慢，使不高兴，拒绝帮助 | To refuse a request or demand of someone; to slight or offend |  I would never intentionally disoblige a friend. 我绝不会故意怠慢朋友。

I was sorry to disoblige her, but I couldn't agree to her request. 我很抱歉拒绝她的请求，但我不能同意。 | 近义词：offend, slight, snub, refuse, decline | 反义词：oblige, accommodate, please, gratify | 词性: 动词
