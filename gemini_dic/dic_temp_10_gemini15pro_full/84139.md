half-mumbled:/ˌhæfˈmʌmbəld/
adjective
含糊不清的，咕哝的
uttered in a low, indistinct voice; partly mumbled.
She gave a half-mumbled apology and quickly left the room. 她含糊不清地说了句道歉，然后迅速离开了房间。
The half-mumbled words were barely audible over the noise of the crowd.  在人群的喧闹声中，那些含糊不清的话几乎听不见。
near: muttered, murmured, indistinct, unclear
opposite: clear, distinct, articulate, enunciated
