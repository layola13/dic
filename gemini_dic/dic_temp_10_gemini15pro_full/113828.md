madder:/ˈmædər/ (US) /ˈmædə(r)/ (UK)| n. adj.|茜草，更疯狂的|A Eurasian plant with whorls of leaves and small yellow flowers, formerly widely cultivated for the red dye obtained from its roots.  Also, more mad; angrier or more insane.|

例句：
The madder root was used to dye the cloth a vibrant red.
茜草根被用来将布料染成鲜艳的红色。

He became madder with each passing moment.
他每过一刻就变得更加疯狂。

近义词：
(n.) rubia, dyestuff
(adj.) angrier, more insane, crazier, wilder, furious

反义词:
(adj.) calmer, saner, more rational

词性:
noun, adjective
