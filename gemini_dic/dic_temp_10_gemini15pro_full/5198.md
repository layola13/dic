alliant:/əˈlaɪənt/| adj. |结盟的，联盟的|Joined by or relating to a treaty or agreement, especially of a military or political nature.|例句：The two countries were allied during the war. 这两个国家在战争期间是联盟的。|例句：Alliant forces pushed back the enemy. 盟军击退了敌人。|近义词：allied, confederated, united|反义词：enemy, hostile, opposed|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Old French *aliant (present participle of alier "to ally, bind, unite"), from Latin alligare "to bind to, tie up, fasten," from ad- "to" + ligare "to bind."
