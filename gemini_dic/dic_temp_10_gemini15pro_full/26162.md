but's:/bʌts/ | n. | 借口，推托 |  But's are excuses or reasons given for not doing something.  It's an informal contraction of "buts." | "He had a lot of but's about why he couldn't finish the project." 他有很多借口解释为什么他不能完成这个项目。| 近义词: excuses, reasons, justifications | 反义词: agreements, acceptances | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "but" (conjunction) + plural suffix "-s."  Originally used in phrases like "no ifs, ands, or buts," the plural form "buts" eventually became a noun in informal usage.
