foxgloves: /ˈfɒksˌɡlʌvz/ (UK) /ˈfɑːksˌɡlʌvz/ (US)| n.|毛地黄；洋地黄|A tall, slender plant with clusters of bell-shaped flowers, typically purplish or white, native to Europe and widely cultivated as an ornamental and medicinal plant.
