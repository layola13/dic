attends:/əˈtɛndz/
verb
出席；参加；照顾；护理
to be present at (an event, meeting, etc.); to go regularly to (a place such as a church or school); to deal with; to take care of
He attends church every Sunday. 他每个星期天都去教堂。
The nurse attended to the patient's needs.  护士照顾病人的需要。
synonyms:  participate in, be present at, go to, take care of, look after
antonyms:  miss, avoid, neglect, ignore
词性: 动词
