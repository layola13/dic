betorn:/bɪˈtɔːrn/| adj.|破烂的，衣衫褴褛的|Torn or tattered; in a dilapidated condition.  This word is archaic and rarely used in modern English. |The betorn beggar huddled in the doorway. 衣衫褴褛的乞丐蜷缩在门口。|近义词：tattered, ragged, shabby, dilapidated|反义词：pristine, immaculate, new, mint|词性：形容词
词源：From Middle English *bitorne, from Old English *bitornen, past participle of bitēran (“to tear”). Cognate with Dutch betorn (“tattered”), German zerrissen (“torn”).
