crumpled:/ˈkrʌmpəld/| adj.|皱的，弄皱的|Crushed or wrinkled, as by folding or squeezing.|The paper was crumpled in his fist.  他的拳头里攥着一张皱巴巴的纸。|近义词：wrinkled, creased, rumpled, crushed|反义词:smooth, ironed, flat|词性:形容词
Etymology: From the verb "crumple," which originated in the late 16th century, likely as a frequentative form of "crump" (meaning to bend or curl).
