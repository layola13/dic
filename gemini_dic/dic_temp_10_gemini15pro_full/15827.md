balladeers:/ˌbæləˈdɪərz/
noun
民谣歌手；民谣诗人
A balladeer is a singer or writer of ballads.
The balladeers entertained the crowds in the town square. 民谣歌手在城镇广场上娱乐群众。
The old balladeer sang a mournful song of lost love. 老民谣歌手唱了一首悲伤的失恋歌曲。
singers, musicians, poets, minstrels, troubadours
