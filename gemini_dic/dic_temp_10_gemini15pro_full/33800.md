circumference: /səˈkʌmfərəns/ (US) /səˈkʌmf(ə)rəns/ (UK)| n. |周长，圆周；外围，周围|The circumference of the Earth is about 40,000 kilometers. 地球的周长大约是40,000公里。|The fence marks the circumference of the property. 篱笆标示了这处房产的周长。|近义词：perimeter, periphery|反义词: diameter, radius|词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *circumferentia, from Latin circumferens (present participle of circumferre, "to carry around"), from circum ("around") + ferre ("to carry").
