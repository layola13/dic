haematoplastic:/ˌhɪmətəˈplæstɪk/ /ˌhimætəˈplæstɪk/
adjective
造血的; 促进造血的
Pertaining to the formation of blood; haematopoietic.
例句：Haematoplastic tissues are essential for maintaining a healthy blood supply. 造血组织对于维持健康的血液供应至关重要。

例句：The doctor recommended a haematoplastic diet to improve the patient's red blood cell count. 医生建议采用促进造血的饮食来提高病人的红细胞计数。
近义词：haemopoietic, hematopoietic
反义词: (No direct antonym, but potentially) blood-destructive, haemolytic
词性: adjective
