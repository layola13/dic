firmament: /ˈfɜːrməmənt/ (US) /ˈfɜːməmənt/ (UK)
n.
天空，苍穹
The vast expanse of the sky, especially when considered as a dome.
The stars twinkled brightly in the firmament.  星星在苍穹中闪耀。
He looked up at the firmament and wondered about the mysteries of the universe. 他仰望苍穹，思考着宇宙的奥秘。

近义词: sky, heavens, vault, celestial sphere
反义词: earth, ground

词性: noun

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French firmament, or from late Latin firmamentum ‘support, prop’, from Latin firmare ‘strengthen’.
