chloroplatinic:/ˌklɔːroʊpləˈtɪnɪk/ | adj. |氯铂酸的| Containing or relating to both chlorine and platinum.  Specifically, refers to hexachloroplatinic acid (H2PtCl6).| Chloroplatinic acid is used as a catalyst. 氯铂酸用作催化剂。 | Near Synonyms: platinic chloride, hexachloroplatinic| Antonyms: N/A | Adjective

chloroplatinate: /ˌklɔːroʊˈplætɪneɪt/ | n. |氯铂酸盐| A salt of chloroplatinic acid. |  Sodium chloroplatinate is a reddish salt. 氯铂酸钠是一种微红色的盐。| Near Synonyms:  hexachloroplatinate | Antonyms: N/A | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From chloro- (combining form meaning "chlorine") + platinic (relating to or containing platinum, especially in its higher oxidation state).  Ultimately from Greek khlōros (pale green) and Spanish platina (little silver).
