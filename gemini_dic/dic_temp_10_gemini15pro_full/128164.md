nettlier:/ˈnɛtlɪər/ | adj. | 更易怒的，更刺人的 | More readily angered; more prickly or stinging. | The nettlier the plant, the less likely animals are to eat it.  植物越刺人，动物就越不可能吃它。|  The security guard became nettlier as the night wore on.  随着夜幕降临，保安越来越暴躁。| 近义词：pricklier, touchier, more irritable, more irascible | 反义词：gentler, calmer, more placid, more amiable | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From *nettle* (n.) + *-ier* (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *natilō*.
