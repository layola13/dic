imbreed:/ɪnˈbriːd/ | v. | 近亲繁殖；同系交配 | To breed from closely related animals or people. |  The royal families of Europe had a history of inbreeding. 欧洲皇室有近亲繁殖的历史。

The farmer tried to avoid inbreeding his cattle.  这位农场主试图避免他的牛近亲繁殖。| 近义词：interbreed, crossbreed (sometimes) | 反义词：outbreed, outcross |词性：动词
