massacred:/ˈmæsəkərd/ | v. |屠杀，残杀；彻底击败|To kill a large number of people violently, especially in a war or for political reasons; to defeat someone very badly in a game or competition.|例句：Thousands of civilians were massacred during the war.  战争期间成千上万的平民被屠杀。

例句：Our team was massacred in the final. 我们队在决赛中惨败。|近义词：slaughter, butcher, decimate, annihilate, rout|反义词：protect, save, defend|词性：动词

massacre:/ˈmæsəkər/ | n. |大屠杀，残杀；惨败|The killing of a large number of people, especially in a war or for political reasons; a complete defeat in a game or competition.|例句：The massacre of innocent civilians shocked the world.  无辜平民的大屠杀震惊了世界。

例句：The game ended in a massacre for the home team. 这场比赛以主队的惨败告终。|近义词：slaughter, carnage, bloodbath, rout, debacle|反义词：victory, triumph|词性：名词

词源：From Middle French *massacrer*, from Old French *macecrer* "to slaughter, butcher,"  probably from *maçacre* "slaughterhouse, butchery," of uncertain origin.
