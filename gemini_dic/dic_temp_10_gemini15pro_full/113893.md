madreporite:/ˌmædrəˈpɔːraɪt/
| n. | madreporite;筛板 |A perforated plate by which the vascular system of echinoderms communicates with the exterior. |The starfish draws water into its vascular system through the madreporite. 海星通过筛板将水吸入其血管系统。| 近义词:sieve plate | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists as it's a specific anatomical feature) | noun

madreporian:/ˌmædrəˈpɔːrɪən/
| adj. | madreporic;筛板的|Relating to or resembling a madrepore or madreporite.  |The madreporian plate is a crucial part of the starfish's anatomy. 筛板是海星解剖结构的关键部分。|近义词: madreporic | 反义词: (No direct antonym exists as it's a specific anatomical feature) | adjective
