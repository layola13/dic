outworks:/ˈaʊtwɜːrks/
noun
外围工事，外围防御
external fortifications of a place, especially a fort or city
The outworks of the castle were breached by the enemy's cannons. 城堡的外围工事被敌人的大炮攻破了。
synonyms: ramparts, bulwarks, defenses, fortifications
antonyms: interior, inside, citadel
