commonweal: /ˈkɒmənwiːl/ (US) /ˈkɒmənwiːl/ (UK)| n. |公益；公共福利；国家；共和国|The general good or welfare of the public.  It refers to the well-being of all members of a community or society.  It can also be used synonymously with "commonwealth."|The politician dedicated his life to the commonweal.|Synonyms: common good, public good, general welfare, commonwealth|Antonyms: private interest, individual gain|noun
