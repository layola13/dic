overanalyses:/ˌoʊvərəˈnæləsiːz/| n.|过度分析|An excessive or unnecessary amount of analysis, often leading to a misinterpretation or complication of a situation.  It's when you think about something too much, reading into things that aren't really there.|例句：His overanalyses of her text messages led him to believe she was angry, when in reality, she was just busy. 他过度分析她的短信，认为她生气了，而实际上，她只是很忙。

例句：The team's overanalyses of the data paralyzed them from making a quick decision.  团队对数据的过度分析使他们无法快速做出决定。|近义词：overthinking, overinterpretation, rumination|反义词:underestimation, simplification|词性:名词
