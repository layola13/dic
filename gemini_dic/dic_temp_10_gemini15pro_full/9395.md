antiques:/ænˈtiːks/
n.
古董；古玩
objects that are old and often valuable
She collects antiques. 她收藏古董。
近义词: relics, artifacts, vintage items
反义词: modern pieces, contemporary items
词性: 名词(plural noun)

antique:/ænˈtiːk/
n.
古董；古玩
an object that is old and often valuable
This antique is worth a fortune. 这件古董价值连城。
近义词: relic, artifact, vintage item
反义词: modern piece, contemporary item
词性: 名词(noun)

antique:/ænˈtiːk/
adj.
古老的；古董的
old and often valuable
She has an antique desk in her study. 她的书房里有一张古董书桌。
近义词: old, vintage, ancient
反义词: modern, new, contemporary
词性: 形容词 (adjective)


词源: From French *antique*, from Latin *antiquus* "old, ancient".
