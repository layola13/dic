colloquies:/ˈkɒləkwiz/ /kəˈloʊkwiz/
|n.|对话，谈话；(正式的)讨论，会谈|A formal conversation or discussion, often on a serious subject.|例句：The two professors engaged in learned colloquies about the nature of the universe. 两位教授就宇宙的本质进行了学术性的对话。

例句：Their colloquies often lasted late into the night. 他们的谈话经常持续到深夜。|近义词：discussions, conversations, dialogues, conferences|反义词：arguments, disputes, disagreements|noun
