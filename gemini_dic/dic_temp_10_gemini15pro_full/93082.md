idrialine:/ɪˈdriəˌlaɪn/ | n. | 矿脂；伊德利尔沥青 | A white, crystalline hydrocarbon found in Idria, formerly Austria, now Italy. It is fluorescent under UV light. |  Idrialine is a rare mineral found in mercury deposits. 伊德利尔沥青是一种稀有矿物，发现于汞矿床中。 | Synonyms: idrialite | Antonyms: N/A | noun
