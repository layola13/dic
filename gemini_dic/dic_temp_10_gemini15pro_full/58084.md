drooping:/ˈdruːpɪŋ/| adj. |下垂的，萎靡的| hanging down; weak and tired | The flowers were drooping in the heat. 花朵在高温下都垂了下来了。| synonyms: sagging, wilting, declining | antonyms: upright, erect, flourishing | adjective

droop:/druːp/| v. |下垂，萎靡| to bend or hang down heavily |  His shoulders drooped with tiredness. 他的肩膀因疲倦而下垂。 | synonyms: sag, wilt, decline | antonyms: rise, lift, perk up | verb

Etymology: From Middle English *drupen, from Old Norse *drūpa (“to droop, drip”), from Proto-Germanic *drūpōną (“to drip, droop”).
