braver:/ˈbreɪvər/| adj.|更勇敢的|Comparative form of "brave".  Indicates a greater degree of courage or fearlessness than someone or something else.|He was braver than I expected. 他比我想象的更勇敢。|近义词：more courageous, bolder, more valiant|反义词:more cowardly, more timid, more fearful|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *bravere*, comparative of *braf* (“brave”), from Old French *brave* (“brave, excellent, splendid”)
