consists:/kənˈsɪsts/
verb
组成，构成
be composed or made up of.
The team consists of four Europeans and two Americans.  这个团队由四个欧洲人和两个美国人组成。
The house consists of six rooms. 这座房子由六个房间组成。
synonym:comprise, be composed of, be made up of
antonym:exclude, lack, omit
词性:动词
