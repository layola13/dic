blindless:/ˈblaɪndləsnɪs/
n.
失明，盲目
The state of being unable to see.
His sudden blindness shocked his family and friends. 他的突然失明震惊了他的家人和朋友。
近义词:sightlessness, vision impairment
反义词:sight, vision
词性:名词
