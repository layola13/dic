gluteus:/ˈɡluːtiəs/ | n. | 臀肌 | Any of the three large muscles in each buttock. | The gluteus maximus is the largest muscle in the human body. 臀大肌是人体最大的肌肉。| Synonyms: buttock muscle | Antonyms: None | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *glūt-, stem of glūten ("glue") + -al (adjective suffix).  Relates to the sticky nature of the muscles.  The term "gluteus" is the Latin word for buttock.
