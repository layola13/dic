lithologically:/ˌlɪθəˈlɒdʒɪkəli/
adverb
岩性地
In terms of rock type or composition.  Relating to lithology (the study of the general characteristics of rocks).
The area is lithologically diverse, with sandstone, shale, and limestone formations.  这个地区岩性多样，有砂岩、页岩和石灰岩地层。
Near synonyms:  petrographically, geologically
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, consider using phrases like "non-lithologically" or focusing on specific rock properties for contrast, e.g., "chemically," "structurally")
词性: 副词
