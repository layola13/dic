nonrecoils:/nɒnˈriːkɔɪlz/ | n. | 无后坐力武器（通常用复数形式） |  Weapons (usually plural) that are designed to minimize or eliminate recoil when fired.  This is typically achieved through mechanisms that redirect propellant gases or counter the rearward force of the projectile. | The army deployed nonrecoils to support the infantry advance.  军队部署了无后坐力炮以支援步兵前进。|  The nonrecoils proved effective against the enemy fortifications.  这些无后坐力武器证明对敌人的防御工事很有效。 | 近义词: recoilless rifles, recoilless guns | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) high-recoil weapons | 词性: 名词

nonrecoil:/nɒnˈriːkɔɪl/ | adj. | 无后坐力的 | Having the characteristic of not recoiling.  Describing a weapon or other device designed to minimize or eliminate recoil. | The nonrecoil rifle was easy to handle even for inexperienced soldiers. 无后坐力步枪即使对没有经验的士兵来说也很容易操作。|  The development of nonrecoil artillery significantly changed modern warfare. 无后坐力炮的发展极大地改变了现代战争。| 近义词: recoilless | 反义词: recoiling | 词性: 形容词

词源： "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "recoil" (from Old French "recoillir," meaning "to draw back").
