estrus: /ˈɛstrəs/ (US), /ˈiːstrəs/ (UK)| n.| 发情期 |A recurring period of sexual receptivity and fertility in many female mammals; heat.| The lioness was in estrus and attracting the attention of all the males. 母狮正处于发情期，吸引了所有雄狮的注意。|Synonyms: heat, rut, season|Antonyms: anestrus|noun
