pamprodactylous:/ˌpæmprəˈdæktɪləs/
adjective
全蹼足的，指所有趾间都有蹼的
having all the toes connected by a web, like a duck's foot.
The coot is a pamprodactylous bird, enabling it to swim efficiently.  白骨顶是一种全蹼足的鸟类，这使得它能够高效地游泳。
The pamprodactylous feet of the grebe allow it to navigate the water with ease.  鸊鷉的全蹼足使其能够轻松地在水中航行。
近义词: web-footed
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider "unwebbed" or "separate-toed")
词性: adjective
