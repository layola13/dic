furibundal:/fjʊərɪˈbʌndl/| adj.|狂暴的，激烈的，暴怒的|Characterized by or expressing extreme anger or fury; raging.  Full of or characterized by fury; violently angry.|The furibundal storm raged through the night, tearing trees from their roots.  狂暴的风暴整夜肆虐，将树木连根拔起。|例句：His furibundal reaction surprised everyone. 他暴怒的反应让每个人都感到惊讶。|近义词：furious, enraged, wrathful, tempestuous, vehement|反义词：calm, peaceful, tranquil, serene|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *fūribundus, from *fūribundia, from furor (“rage, madness”) +‎ -bundus (suffix forming adjectives indicating a state of being or becoming).
