nondepletion:/ˌnɒndɪˈpliːʃən/
n.|非消耗，非损耗|The state or quality of not being depleted; not reducing in quantity or quality. |The report highlighted the nondepletion of natural resources due to sustainable practices.|近义词: conservation, preservation, maintenance|反义词: depletion, exhaustion, reduction|词性:名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin "non" meaning "not"
depletion (noun): from deplete (verb), from Latin "deplēre" meaning "to empty out," from "de-" (down, away) + "plēre" (to fill)
