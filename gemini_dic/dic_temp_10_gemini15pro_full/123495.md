monothalamous:/ˌmɒnəˈθæləmʌs/
adjective
单室的；单囊的
Having or consisting of a single chamber or locule.  Specifically used in botany to describe a fruit or ovary having only one cavity.
The monothalamous fruit contained a single seed.  这个单室的果实包含一颗种子。
Near Synonyms: unilocular, single-chambered
Antonyms: multilocular, plurilocular
词性: adjective
