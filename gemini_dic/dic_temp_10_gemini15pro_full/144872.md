over-ready:/ˌoʊvərˈrɛdi/
adjective
过于准备好的；过分积极的；操之过急的
Too eager or prepared; excessively prompt.
She was over-ready to offer advice, even when it wasn't asked for. 她总是过分积极地提出建议，即使别人并没有寻求她的意见。
The soldiers, over-ready for combat, jumped at the slightest sound. 士兵们过度准备战斗，一有轻微的声音就跳了起来。
近义词: eager, keen, impulsive, impatient, premature
反义词: hesitant, reluctant, cautious, unprepared
词性: adjective

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "ready" (adjective)
