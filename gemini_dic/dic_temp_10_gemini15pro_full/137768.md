nonvascularly:/nɒnˈvæskjʊləli/
词性: adverb
中文翻译: 无血管地；非维管地
英文解析: In a nonvascular manner; without the involvement of vascular tissue (xylem and phloem, which transport water, minerals, and nutrients in plants).
例句: Some plants, like mosses, reproduce nonvascularly.  一些植物，例如苔藓，以无血管的方式繁殖。

例句: The nutrient solution was absorbed nonvascularly.  营养液被无血管地吸收了。
近义词:  (No direct single-word synonym.  Could use phrases like "without blood vessels" or "without xylem and phloem" depending on context.)
反义词: vascularly
词性: adverb
