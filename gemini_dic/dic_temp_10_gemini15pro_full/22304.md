boniest:/ˈboʊniɪst/| adj.|最瘦骨嶙峋的|Superlative form of bony: most bony.  Having prominent bones, thin.|例句：He was the boniest horse I'd ever seen. 他是我见过的最瘦骨嶙峋的马。|近义词：gauntest, skinniest, thinnest|反义词:fleshiest, plumpest, chubbiest|词性:形容词
词源: From bone + -y + -est.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *bainam.
