cacolet:/ˈkækəˌlɛt/ | n. | 驮篮，驮椅；（尤指用于运送伤员的）双人座 | A chair or litter secured on either side of a mule, especially for carrying wounded. |  The wounded soldier was transported down the mountain in a cacolet.  受伤的士兵被用驮篮运下山。| synonyms: litter, pannier, howdah | antonyms: N/A | noun
