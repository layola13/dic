bird-ringer:/ˈbɜːrd rɪŋər/ | n. |鸟类环志员| A person who catches wild birds, puts identification rings on their legs, and releases them for tracking and research purposes. | Example: The bird-ringer carefully attached a lightweight aluminum band to the robin's leg. 鸟类环志员小心翼翼地将一个轻质铝环系在知更鸟的腿上。

Example: Bird-ringers play a vital role in monitoring bird populations and migration patterns. 鸟类环志员在监测鸟类种群和迁徙模式方面发挥着至关重要的作用。 | 近义词: ornithologist (鸟类学家, 更广义), bander (主要指北美用法) | 反义词:  (无直接反义词) | 词性: noun


bird-ringing:/ˈbɜːrd rɪŋɪŋ/ | n. |鸟类环志| The activity of catching wild birds, putting identification rings on their legs, and releasing them for tracking and research purposes. | Example: Bird-ringing provides valuable data on bird longevity and breeding success. 鸟类环志提供了关于鸟类寿命和繁殖成功率的宝贵数据。

Example:  The researchers conducted bird-ringing in the forest reserve to study the local bird population.  研究人员在森林保护区进行了鸟类环志，以研究当地的鸟类种群。| 近义词: bird banding (主要指北美用法) | 反义词: (无直接反义词) | 词性: noun
