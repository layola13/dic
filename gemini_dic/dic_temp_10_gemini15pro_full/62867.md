enkindle:/ɪnˈkɪndl/ /ɛnˈkɪndl/| vt.|点燃；激起；使燃烧|To arouse or inspire (a feeling, emotion, or idea).|The coach's speech enkindled the team's desire to win. 教练的讲话激起了队员们获胜的渴望。

The sunset enkindled the sky with vibrant colors.  日落用鲜艳的色彩点燃了天空。|近义词：ignite, inflame, arouse, inspire, stimulate|反义词：extinguish, quench, stifle, suppress|词性：动词
