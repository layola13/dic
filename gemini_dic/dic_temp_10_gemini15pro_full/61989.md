enacts:/ɪˈnækts/
|v.|制定，颁布；扮演|
|To make a bill or other proposal into law. To perform a role or represent a character in a play, film, or other dramatic presentation.|
|Congress enacts laws. 国会制定法律。|
|The students enacted a play about the American Revolution. 学生们演了一出关于美国独立战争的戏。|

|近义词:legislate, perform, portray, depict|
|反义词:repeal, abolish|
|词性:verb|
