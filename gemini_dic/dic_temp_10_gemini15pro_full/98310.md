insightfulness:/ˈɪnsaɪtflnəs/| n.|洞察力；深刻的理解|The insightfulness of her analysis impressed everyone. 她的分析的深刻见解给每个人都留下了深刻的印象。|近义词：perspicacity, acumen, discernment|反义词：obtuseness, shallowness|词性:名词

词源: From insightful + -ness.  "insightful" comes from "insight," which originated from the Old English word "insīht," meaning "the action of looking in." The suffix "-ful" means "full of" or "characterized by," while "-ness" transforms an adjective into a noun denoting a state or quality.
