affirmant:/əˈfɜːrmənt/
n.|宣誓人，立誓词的人|A person who makes an affirmation instead of taking an oath, especially in a legal context.|The affirmant signed the document in front of a notary. 宣誓人在公证人面前签署了文件。|近义词：deponent, witness|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
affirmant:/əˈfɜːrmənt/
adj.|肯定的，断言的 (Rare usage as adjective) |Making an affirmation; affirming. |His affirmant statement confirmed his belief. 他肯定的声明证实了他的信念。 |近义词:assertive, positive|反义词:negative, denying|词性:adjective
