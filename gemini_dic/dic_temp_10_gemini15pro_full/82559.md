guarana:/ˌɡwɑːrəˈnɑː/ | n. |瓜拉纳，瓜拉纳果，瓜拉纳提取物 | A climbing plant native to the Amazon basin, whose seeds are used to make a stimulant drink. |  Brazil is a major producer of guarana. 巴西是瓜拉纳的主要产地。

Guarana is a popular ingredient in energy drinks. 瓜拉纳是能量饮料中的一种流行成分。 | 近义词: (for the effect) caffeine, stimulant | 反义词: depressant | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Portuguese guaraná, from the Tupian word waraná.
