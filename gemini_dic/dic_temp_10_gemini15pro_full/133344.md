nonextensional:/ˌnɒnɪkˈstɛnʃənl/
adjective
非外延的
Not determined solely by the objects it denotes; intensional.  A nonextensional context is one in which the truth value of a statement containing an expression depends on the specific meaning of the expression, and not just on the things to which the expression refers.
例句：Belief is a nonextensional context.  相信是一种非外延语境。
例句：The statement "John believes that Hesperus is the Evening Star" is nonextensional.  “约翰相信赫斯珀勒斯是昏星”这个陈述是非外延的。
近义词：intensional
反义词：extensional
词性：adjective
