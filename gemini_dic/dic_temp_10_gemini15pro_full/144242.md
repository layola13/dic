outsmell:/ˌaʊtˈsmɛl/| vt.| 比...难闻；发出比...更强烈的气味 | To have a stronger or more unpleasant smell than something else. | The durian outsmelled everything else in the market.  榴莲的气味盖过了市场里所有其他的东西。

The skunk outsmelled the garbage. 臭鼬的气味比垃圾更难闻。| 近义词: outstink, overpower (in smell) | 反义词: be outsmelled by |词性:动词
