chancing:/ˈtʃɑːnsɪŋ/ /ˈtʃænsɪŋ/| v. |冒险，碰运气；试图做（尤指明知不大可能成功的事）|Taking a risk; attempting to do something, especially when the likelihood of success is low. |He was chancing his luck by driving without a license. 他无证驾驶，是在碰运气。|近义词：risking, gambling, venturing|反义词：playing it safe, avoiding risk|词性:动词

词源: 来自chance (n.) 机会；运气，源自古法语 cheance.
