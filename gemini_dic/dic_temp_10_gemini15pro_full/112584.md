loves:/lʌvz/| v.|爱，喜欢|Third-person singular present tense form of the verb "love".  It expresses affection, fondness, or deep caring for someone or something.|She loves her family very much. 她非常爱她的家人。|He loves playing basketball. 他喜欢打篮球。|近义词：adores, cherishes, likes|反义词：hates, dislikes, despises|verb

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
She loves to read books. 她喜欢读书。
He loves his job. 他热爱他的工作。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
She is loving the attention she's getting. 她很享受她正在得到的关注。（注意：love 通常不用于进行时，除非表示短暂的、强烈的喜爱）
He is loving this new movie. 他很喜欢这部新电影。（同上）

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
She has loved him since they were children.  她从小就爱他。
He has loved that band for years. 他喜欢那个乐队很多年了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
She has been loving her new life in the city. 她一直很喜欢她在城市的新生活。（同上）
He has been loving the warm weather lately. 他最近一直很喜欢温暖的天气。（同上）

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
She loved the gift he gave her. 她喜欢他送给她的礼物。
He loved playing in the park as a child. 他小时候喜欢在公园里玩耍。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
She was loving the concert until it started to rain.  在开始下雨之前，她一直很喜欢这场音乐会。（同上）
He was loving the attention. 他当时很喜欢这种关注。（同上）


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
She had loved him for years before they finally got together. 在他们最终在一起之前，她已经爱了他很多年。
He had loved that car since he first saw it. 自从第一次看到那辆车，他就喜欢上了它。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
She had been loving her job until her boss changed. 在她的老板换人之前，她一直很喜欢她的工作。（同上）
He had been loving the quiet life until his family came to visit. 在他的家人来访之前，他一直很喜欢平静的生活。（同上）

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
She will love this present. 她会喜欢这份礼物的。
He will love the new restaurant. 他会喜欢这家新餐厅的。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
She will be loving the beach this time next week. 下周这个时候她将会在海滩享受。（同上）
He will be loving the peace and quiet when the kids go back to school.  孩子们开学后他将会享受宁静。（同上）


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By the time she's thirty, she will have loved and lost many times. 到她三十岁的时候，她将会经历很多次爱与失去。
He will have loved that car for ten years by next summer. 到明年夏天，他拥有那辆车就十年了。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next year, she will have been loving that dog for five years. 到明年，她养那只狗就五年了。
He will have been loving his job for two years by the time he gets a promotion. 到他升职的时候，他就已经喜欢他的工作两年了。


虚拟语气:
If she loved him, she would marry him. 如果她爱他，她就会嫁给他。
I wish she loved me. 我希望她爱我。

被动语态:
(Love is rarely used in the passive voice.  爱很少用于被动语态。)


疑问句:
Does she love him? 她爱他吗？
What does he love about his job? 他喜欢他工作的哪些方面？

否定句:
She doesn't love him anymore. 她不再爱他了。
He doesn't love spicy food. 他不喜欢辣的食物。
