ostracizable:/ˈɒstrəsaɪzəbəl/ (US) /ˈɒstrɪsaɪzəbl/ (UK)
adjective
可放逐的；可排斥的
Capable of being ostracized; liable to be excluded from a group or society.
He was ostracizable due to his radical views.  他因为激进的观点而被排斥。
The unpopular politician was ostracizable by his own party.  这位不受欢迎的政治家被自己的党派排斥。
近义词: excludable, banishable, shunning
反义词: acceptable, welcome, included
词性: adjective
