calculatedness:/ˈkælkjuleɪtɪdnəs/| n. |精明；算计；深思熟虑|The calculatedness of her moves suggested she had a plan. 她的举动经过精心策划，表明她有一个计划。|例句：The businessman's calculatedness ensured his success.  这个商人的精明确保了他的成功。

例句：Her calculatedness in choosing her words made it difficult to argue with her. 她措辞的谨慎让人很难和她争论。

近义词：cunning, shrewdness, artfulness, craftiness|反义词:naiveté, artlessness, ingenuousness|词性:名词

calculate:/ˈkælkjuleɪt/| v. |计算；估计；预测；计划；打算|例句：We calculated the cost of the trip. 我们计算了旅行的费用。

例句：I calculate that we'll arrive around noon. 我估计我们会在中午左右到达。|近义词：compute, estimate, reckon, gauge|反义词:guess, miscalculate|词性:动词
