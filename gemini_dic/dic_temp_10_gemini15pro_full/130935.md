nonaspiring:/ˌnɒnəˈspaɪərɪŋ/
adjective
没有抱负的；不求上进的
Lacking or showing no ambition or desire to progress.
He seemed content with his nonaspiring lifestyle, happy to simply exist. 他似乎满足于自己不求上进的生活方式，乐于简单的存在。
She was described as a nonaspiring student, more interested in socializing than studying. 她被描述成一个没有抱负的学生，对社交比学习更感兴趣。
Synonyms: unambitious, unmotivated, lackadaisical
Antonyms: ambitious, driven, motivated
词性: 形容词
