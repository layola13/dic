hop's:/hɒps/ | n. |啤酒花; 跳跃;  |Belonging to or associated with hop. Usually refers to the flowers of the hop plant used in brewing beer. It can also, less commonly, be a possessive form of "hop," the verb meaning "to jump."|The hop's bitterness balances the sweetness of the malt. 啤酒花的苦味平衡了麦芽的甜味。| The hop's aroma is crucial to the beer's character.  啤酒花的香气对啤酒的特性至关重要。 | synonyms:  (for the plant) hop vine, (for the flavor)  bitter, tangy | antonyms: (for the flavor) sweet, mild | noun

注意事项:  The use of "hop's" is limited and often sounds unnatural. Using "the hop" or "the hops" followed by a noun is usually preferred.  For instance, "the hop aroma" is better than "the hop's aroma," and "the hops' bitterness" is generally preferable to "the hop's bitterness" when referring to the collective bitterness from multiple hop flowers.
