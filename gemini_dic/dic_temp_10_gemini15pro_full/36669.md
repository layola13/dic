collider:/kəˈlaɪdər/| n.|对撞机；碰撞器；碰撞者|A collider is a machine that accelerates particles to very high speeds and then makes them collide.  对撞机是一种将粒子加速到非常高的速度然后使它们碰撞的机器。|The Large Hadron Collider is the world's largest and most powerful particle accelerator. 大型强子对撞机是世界上最大、功能最强的粒子加速器。|Synonym: accelerator, smasher|Antonym: N/A|Noun
