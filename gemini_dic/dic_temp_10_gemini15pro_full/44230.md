crocky:/ˈkrɒki/ (UK) /ˈkrɑːki/ (US)| adj.|破旧的，快要散架的；虚弱的；不舒服的|Something that is crocky is old, broken, or in bad condition; feeling ill or unwell.|例句：That chair looks a bit crocky, I wouldn't sit on it. 那把椅子看起来有点破旧，我不会坐在上面。

例句：I'm feeling a bit crocky today, I think I'm coming down with a cold. 我今天感觉有点不舒服，我想我快要感冒了。|近义词：rickety, shaky, dilapidated, unwell, ill|反义词：sturdy, robust, healthy, well|词性:形容词
