disbind:/dɪsˈbaɪnd/
verb
解除绑定，拆散
To unbind; to separate something that was bound.
The librarian disbound the old book for restoration. 图书管理员为了修复而拆散了那本旧书。
The contract was disbound after both parties agreed to terminate it.  双方同意终止合同后，合同被解除了。
synonyms: unbind, detach, loose, separate, undo
antonyms: bind, fasten, attach, connect
