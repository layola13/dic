diverge:/daɪˈvɜːrdʒ/ /dɪˈvɜːrdʒ/| v. | 分歧，偏离，相异 | To separate and go in different directions; to differ in opinion or character. |  The paths diverged in the woods.  两条小路在树林里分岔了。| synonyms: deviate, separate, branch off | antonyms: converge, meet, unite | verb

diverged:/daɪˈvɜːrdʒd/ /dɪˈvɜːrdʒd/| v. | 分歧了，偏离了，相异了 | past tense of "diverge," meaning separated and went in different directions, or differed in opinion or character. |  Their opinions diverged on the best course of action. 他们的意见在最佳行动方案上存在分歧。 | synonyms: deviated, separated, branched off | antonyms: converged, met, united | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dīvergō* (“I bend apart, I separate”), from *dī-* (“apart”) + *vergō* (“I incline, bend, turn”).
