miraculousness:/mɪˈrækjʊləsnəs/| n. |神奇；奇迹般|The miraculousness of her recovery astonished everyone. 她的康复令人惊叹。|近义词：wonder, marvel, magic, supernaturalness |反义词：ordinariness, commonness| noun

Etymology: From miraculous + -ness.  "Miraculous" comes from Late Latin *miraculosus, from Latin miraculum "wonder, marvel, miracle," from mirari "to wonder at," from mirus "wonderful, astonishing, strange, causing wonder."
