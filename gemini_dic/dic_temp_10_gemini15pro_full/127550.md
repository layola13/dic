necropolis: /nəˈkrɒpəlɪs/ /nəˈkrɑːpəlɪs/
noun
大墓地；陵墓
A large cemetery, especially one of ancient times.
The Valley of the Kings is a vast necropolis containing the tombs of many pharaohs.  帝王谷是一个巨大的陵墓，里面埋葬着许多法老。
The ancient city became a necropolis after the plague.  瘟疫过后，这座古城变成了一个大墓地。
synonyms: cemetery, graveyard, burial ground
antonyms:  city, town (places of life)
n.
