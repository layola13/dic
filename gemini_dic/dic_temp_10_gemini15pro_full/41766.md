corruptiblenesses:/kəˈrʌptɪblənəsɪz/
noun
可腐败性；腐败倾向
The inherent susceptibility to moral decay or corruption.
The corruptiblenesses of the officials led to the downfall of the empire.  官员们的腐败导致了帝国的灭亡。
corruptibility, venality
incorruptibility, integrity
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From corruptible + -ness.  Corruptible from Late Latin corruptibilis, from corrumpere "to destroy, spoil, corrupt," from com- "together" + rumpere "to break."
