inaneness:/ɪˈneɪnnəs/ | n. | 空洞；无意义；愚蠢 |  Lack of meaning or substance; emptiness. |  The sheer inaneness of the conversation left me feeling drained.  这场对话的空洞无物让我感到精疲力竭。 | synonyms: emptiness, hollowness, futility, pointlessness, senselessness | antonyms: meaningfulness, significance, substance, importance | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From inane (lacking sense or meaning) + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives).  Inane itself comes from Latin inanis (empty, vain).
