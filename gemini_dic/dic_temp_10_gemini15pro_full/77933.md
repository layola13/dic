genocidal: /ˌdʒɛnəˈsaɪdl/
adjective
种族灭绝的
Relating to or involving genocide.
例句：The genocidal campaign against the minority group was condemned by the international community.  针对少数群体的种族灭绝运动受到了国际社会的谴责。

例句：The dictator's genocidal policies led to the deaths of thousands of innocent people.  独裁者的种族灭绝政策导致成千上万无辜人民丧生。

近义词：murderous, destructive, exterminatory
反义词：protective, preserving
词性：adjective
