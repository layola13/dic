nonanatomic:/ˌnɑːnənəˈtɑːmɪk/ | adj. | 非解剖的，非解剖学上的 | Not related to anatomy or the structure of the body. |  Example: The pain was described as nonanatomic, suggesting a psychological origin. 疼痛被描述为非解剖性的，表明是心理原因。 | Example: Nonanatomic factors can also influence a patient's experience of pain. 非解剖因素也会影响患者的疼痛体验。 |  Synonyms: nonstructural, functional | Antonyms: anatomic, structural | adjective
