blur:/blɜːr/ (US) /blɜː(r)/ (UK)| n. v. |模糊，模糊的东西；使…模糊|Something that you cannot see clearly.  To make something difficult to see clearly.|The rain blurred the view from the window. 雨水模糊了窗外的景色。|近义词：smear, smudge, haze, fog|反义词：clarify, sharpen, focus|词性:名词,动词

词源: From Middle English *blurren, likely from *blouren, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old Norse blunda (“to close one's eyes”).
