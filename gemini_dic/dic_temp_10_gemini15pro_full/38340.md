conceive:/kənˈsiːv/ /kənˈsiv/| v.|构想，设想；怀孕|To form an idea, plan, or thought in the mind; to become pregnant.|She conceived of a plan to start her own business. 她构想了一个创业计划。|Synonyms: imagine, envision, devise, formulate, think up|Antonyms: misunderstand, misinterpret|verb

conceived:/kənˈsiːvd/ /kənˈsivd/| v. (past tense and past participle of conceive)|构想出的，设想出的；怀孕的|Having been formed in the mind; having become pregnant.|The project was conceived by a group of young entrepreneurs.  这个项目是由一群年轻的企业家构思出来的。|Synonyms: imagined, envisioned, devised, formulated|Antonyms:  None (as a past participle)|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *conceveir*, from Latin *concipere* "to take in, conceive, become pregnant," from *com-* "together" + *capere* "to take."
