melodramas: /ˈmɛləˌdrɑːməz/ | n. | 情节剧;通俗剧 | A play, film, or other narrative form characterized by exaggerated emotions, stereotypical characters, and interpersonal conflicts. |  The new TV series is a melodrama about a rich family and their secrets. 这部新的电视剧是一部关于富裕家庭及其秘密的情节剧。

| Example: She loves watching melodramas, especially those with complicated love stories. 她喜欢看情节剧，尤其是那些爱情故事复杂的。 | Synonyms: soap operas, tearjerkers, potboilers | Antonyms: documentaries, realism, satire | noun
