gryphon:/ˈɡrɪfən/ /ˈɡrɪfɪn/| n. |狮鹫；格里芬|A mythical creature with the body, tail, and back legs of a lion; the head and wings of an eagle; and an eagle's talons as its front feet. |例句：The gryphon guarded the treasure. 狮鹫守护着宝藏。

例句：Legends say a gryphon is stronger than eight lions. 传说狮鹫比八头狮子还要强壮。|近义词：griffin, hippogriff|反义词: None (mythical creature)|词性: Noun
