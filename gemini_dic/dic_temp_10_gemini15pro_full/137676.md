nonuniting:/ˌnɒnjuːˈnaɪtɪŋ/
adjective
不联合的，不愈合的
Not uniting; not healing or closing.
The doctor was concerned about the nonuniting fracture in his patient's leg. 医生很担心病人腿部不愈合的骨折。
non-healing, ununited
uniting, healing, joining
词性: 形容词
