paper-cutting:/ˈpeɪpər kʌtɪŋ/
n.
剪纸
The act or art of cutting designs from paper.
She learned the art of paper-cutting from her grandmother. 她跟她祖母学习了剪纸艺术。

The delicate paper-cutting decorated the windows. 精致的剪纸装饰着窗户。


近义词：papercraft, découpage
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑"paper-folding" 折纸 作为一种对比)
词性: noun
