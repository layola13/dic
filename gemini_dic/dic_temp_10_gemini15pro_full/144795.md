over-flowed:/ˌoʊvərˈfloʊd/| v.|溢出，泛滥|To flow over the brim or bounds of a container; to flood; to exceed capacity.|The river over-flowed its banks after the heavy rain. 河水在大雨过后漫过了河岸。|Synonyms: flooded, inundated, swamped|Antonyms: drained, emptied|verb

over-flowed:/ˌoʊvərˈfloʊd/| adj.|溢出的，泛滥的|Filled beyond capacity.|The over-flowed bathtub created a puddle on the bathroom floor. 溢出的浴缸在浴室地板上形成了一滩水。|Synonyms: overflowing, flooded, inundated|Antonyms: empty, drained|adjective
