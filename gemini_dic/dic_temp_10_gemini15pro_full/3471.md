afterbirth: /ˈɑːftərbɜːrθ/ (US) /ˈɑːftərbɜːθ/ (UK)
n.
胎盘；胞衣
The placenta and membranes that are expelled from the uterus after the birth of a baby.
The nurse checked to make sure the afterbirth had been completely expelled.  护士检查以确保胎盘已完全排出。
The afterbirth was delivered shortly after the baby.  婴儿出生后不久，胎盘也娩出了。
Synonyms: placenta, secundines
