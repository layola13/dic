palettes:/ˈpæləts/| n. |调色板，颜色系列，（事物的）范围，种类|A thin board or slab on which an artist lays and mixes colors.  A range or selection of colors or qualities. |The artist squeezed paint onto his palette.  艺术家把颜料挤到调色板上。 The film offers a rich palette of emotions. 这部电影展现了丰富的情感。| synonyms: range, spectrum, array, gamut, variety | antonyms: limitation, restriction | noun
