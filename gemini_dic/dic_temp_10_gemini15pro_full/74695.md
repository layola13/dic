freehand:/ˌfriːˈhænd/| adj. adv.|徒手的，不用工具的；随意地，即兴地|done or drawn without using any tools or aids for guidance; extemporaneously or without preparation|He drew a freehand circle on the whiteboard. 他在白板上徒手画了一个圆。|Synonyms: unaided, unassisted, impromptu, extempore|Antonyms: guided, assisted, planned, prepared|adjective/adverb

freehand:/ˌfriːˈhænd/| n.|徒手画|a drawing or something else made or done freehand|Her freehand was remarkably accurate. 她的徒手画非常精确。|Synonyms: sketch, doodle|Antonyms: blueprint, measured drawing|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From free + hand.  Meaning "done by hand, without instruments" is from the late 18th century.
