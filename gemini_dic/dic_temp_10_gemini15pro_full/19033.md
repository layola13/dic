betas:/ˈbeɪtəz/ (US), /ˈbiːtəz/ (UK)| n.|测试版，贝塔系数|A pre-release version of software distributed to a select group of users for testing and feedback before the official release.  In finance, beta is a measure of a stock's volatility in relation to the overall market.|The company released the betas of their new operating system to a limited number of testers.  这家公司向有限数量的测试人员发布了他们新操作系统的测试版。|Synonyms: trial version, preview, test version|Antonyms: final version, release version|noun

betas:/ˈbeɪtəz/ (US), /ˈbiːtəz/ (UK)| n.|贝塔系数|In finance, a measure of a stock's volatility in relation to the overall market. A beta of 1 indicates that the stock's price will move with the market, while a beta greater than 1 indicates that the stock is more volatile than the market.|The stock has a beta of 1.5, indicating it is more volatile than the market. 这支股票的贝塔系数为1.5，表明它比市场更具波动性。|Synonyms: volatility measure, market risk indicator|Antonyms: N/A|noun
