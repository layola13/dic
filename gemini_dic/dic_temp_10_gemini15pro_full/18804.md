berserker:/bərˈsɜːrkər/ | n. | 狂战士；暴怒的人 | A fierce warrior, especially one in the ancient Scandinavian military, who fought with frenzied rage.  A person behaving in a wildly violent or destructive way. |  He fought like a berserker, ignoring the pain of his wounds. 他像狂战士一样战斗，无视伤口的疼痛。

The football hooligans turned into berserkers after their team lost.  足球流氓在他们的球队输球后变成了狂战士。 | 近义词:  wildman, maniac,  madman, fanatic | 反义词: pacifist,  calm person | 词性: 名词

词源: From Old Norse *berserkr, from *ber- 'bear' + *serkr 'shirt, coat'.  Originally referring to warriors who wore bearskins in battle.  来自古诺尔斯语 *berserkr，源自 *ber- “熊” + *serkr “衬衫、外套”。最初指的是在战斗中穿着熊皮的战士。
