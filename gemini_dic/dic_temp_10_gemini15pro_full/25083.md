bubbles:/ˈbʌbəlz/ (US), /ˈbʌblz/ (UK)| n. |泡泡；气泡；泡沫；幻想；（漫画中的）对话气泡|Small, round balls of air or gas in a liquid or a solid substance. Can also refer to a transient state of affairs.|The children were blowing bubbles in the park. 孩子们在公园里吹泡泡。|synonyms: foams, froths, spheres, orbs |antonyms: solids, liquids|noun

bubbles:/ˈbʌbəlz/ (US), /ˈbʌblz/ (UK)| v. |冒泡；起泡；发出汩汩声；兴奋；洋溢|To form bubbles or to cause bubbles to form.|The champagne bubbled in the glass. 香槟在杯子里冒泡。|synonyms: fizz, effervesce, foam |antonyms: flatten, deflate|verb
