balded:/ˈbɔːldɪd/ | v. | 变秃，秃顶 | To become bald or make something bald; to lose hair.  | The chemotherapy balded him. 化疗让他秃顶了。|近义词：hairless, bald, depilated |反义词: hairy, hirsute |词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From bald + -ed.  "Bald" comes from Old English "bæld," meaning "shining, white, pale."  The connection to hairlessness likely arose from the appearance of a shiny, pale scalp.
