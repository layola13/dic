hunkerish:/ˈhʌŋkərɪʃ/| adj.|不舒服的，不自在的；（尤指因寒冷或潮湿）蜷缩的|Inclined to hunker or squat; having a crouching posture, especially from cold or dampness. Also, generally uncomfortable or uneasy.|例句：The damp weather made everyone feel hunkerish. 潮湿的天气让每个人都感到不舒服。|例句：He sat hunkerish in the corner, trying to stay warm. 他蜷缩在角落里，试图保持温暖。|近义词：uncomfortable, uneasy, restless, fidgety, cramped|反义词：comfortable, relaxed, at ease|词性:形容词|词源:From hunker + -ish.|
