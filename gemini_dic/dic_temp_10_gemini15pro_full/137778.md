nonvegetation:/ˌnɒnˌvɛdʒɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. |非植被，非植物覆盖区|Areas of bare ground, rock, or other surfaces lacking plant life.  The study focused on the effects of erosion in nonvegetation areas.|例句：The researchers measured the soil temperature in both vegetation and nonvegetation plots. 研究人员测量了植被区和非植被区的地温。

例句：Nonvegetation surfaces such as roads and parking lots contribute to urban heat island effect.  像道路和停车场这样的非植被表面会导致城市热岛效应。|近义词：barren land, bare ground,  unvegetated area|反义词: vegetation, plant cover |词性: noun
