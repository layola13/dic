outsuffering:/ˌaʊtˈsʌfərɪŋ/
|n.|超越苦难，胜过苦难；比…更痛苦|The act of suffering more than someone or something else, or enduring greater hardship.|Her outsuffering during the war years was a testament to her resilience.  她战争年代所承受的巨大苦难证明了她的坚韧。|例句：His outsuffering touched the hearts of many.  他所承受的巨大苦难触动了许多人的心。|近义词：outdoing, surpassing, exceeding|反义词：succumbing, yielding|词性：名词

词源: "out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding, surpassing") + "suffering" (noun from the verb "suffer")
