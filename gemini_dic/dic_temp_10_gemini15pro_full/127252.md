nautilus: /ˈnɔːtɪləs/ (US), /ˈnɔːtɪləs/ (UK)| n. | 鹦鹉螺 | A cephalopod mollusk of the order Nautilida, having a spiral, chambered shell.  It is the only extant cephalopod with an external shell. |  The nautilus is a living fossil. 鹦鹉螺是一种活化石。

The chambered nautilus is found in the Indo-Pacific region.  腔室鹦鹉螺生活在印度洋-太平洋地区。| 近义词: cephalopod, ammonite (extinct)| 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps land snail for contrasting habitat) | 词性: Noun


nautilus: /ˈnɔːtɪləs/ (US), /ˈnɔːtɪləs/ (UK)| adj. |鹦鹉螺似的| Resembling or characteristic of a nautilus. | The submarine had a nautilus-like shape.  这艘潜艇的形状像鹦鹉螺。| 近义词: spiral, coiled | 反义词: straight, linear | 词性: Adjective (rare usage)
