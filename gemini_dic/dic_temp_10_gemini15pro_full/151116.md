paraskier:/ˈpærəˌskiːər/| n. |残疾人滑雪运动员；伞降滑雪者|A person who skis, typically competitively, despite having a physical disability, often using adaptive equipment.  Alternatively, someone who skis with a parachute. |例句：The paraskier navigated the course with incredible skill.  这位残疾人滑雪运动员以惊人的技巧完成了赛道。

例句：The paraskier sped down the slope, their specialized skis carving through the snow.  这位残疾人滑雪运动员高速滑下斜坡，他们的专用滑雪板在雪地上刻出一道道痕迹。|近义词：disabled skier, adaptive skier (for those with disabilities); parachuting skier, sky skier (for those using parachutes)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps able-bodied skier in the context of disability)|词性:noun
