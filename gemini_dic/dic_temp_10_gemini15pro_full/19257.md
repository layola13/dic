bhistee: /bɪˈstiː/
noun
水夫，送水人
A person in India who carries water, especially one employed to supply water to travelers or households.
The bhistee filled the earthenware pots with fresh water from the well. 水夫从井里把陶罐装满了清水。
The bhistee sprinkled water on the dusty road to cool it down. 水夫在尘土飞扬的道路上洒水降温。
Synonyms: water carrier, waterman
Antonyms: N/A
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi bhisti, from Persian bihishti "of paradise; heavenly, of Paradise," because water carriers were believed to bring a heavenly relief in hot climates.
