bananas:/bəˈnænəz/| n.|香蕉|A long curved fruit with a yellow skin that grows in bunches on a tropical plant.  The fruit can be eaten raw or cooked.|I ate two bananas for breakfast. 我早餐吃了两根香蕉。|近义词:plantains (cooking bananas)|反义词:(No direct antonym for a fruit)|noun
