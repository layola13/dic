catechistical:/ˌkætɪˈkɪstɪkəl/
adjective
教义问答式的；问答教学法的
Relating to, resembling, or consisting of questions and answers, especially for religious instruction; like a catechism.
The priest used a catechistical method to teach the children about the sacraments.  牧师用问答教学法向孩子们讲解圣礼。
Catechistical instruction is common in religious education.  问答式教学在宗教教育中很常见。
近义词: didactic, instructive, Socratic
反义词: n/a
词性: adjective

catechism:/ˈkætɪkɪzəm/
noun
教义问答；问答集；问答教学法
A summary of the principles of Christian religion in the form of questions and answers, used for the instruction of Christians.  
He learned the catechism as a child. 他小时候学习了教义问答。
The catechism provided a structured approach to religious education. 这本问答集提供了一种结构化的宗教教育方法。
近义词: creed, dogma, doctrine
反义词: n/a
词性: noun
