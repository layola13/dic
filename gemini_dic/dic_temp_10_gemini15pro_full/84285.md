half-trained:/ˌhæfˈtreɪnd/ | adj. | 半培训的，训练不足的 |  Partially trained; not fully or adequately trained.  | Example: The half-trained dog kept jumping on visitors. 这只未完全训练的狗不停地跳到访客身上。

Example:  Employing half-trained staff can be risky. 雇佣未经充分培训的员工可能会有风险。 | Synonyms:  undertrained, poorly trained, incompletely trained | Antonyms: well-trained, fully trained, expertly trained | word type: adjective
