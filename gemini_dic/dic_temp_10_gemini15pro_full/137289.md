nonsynonymous:/ˌnɒnsɪˈnɒnɪməs/| adj.|非同义的|Referring to a mutation in a gene that changes the amino acid sequence of the protein it encodes.|A nonsynonymous mutation can have a significant effect on protein function. 非同义突变会对蛋白质功能产生显著影响。|Example: The researchers identified several nonsynonymous mutations in the gene associated with the disease. 研究人员在与该疾病相关的基因中发现了几个非同义突变。|Synonyms: missense, replacement|Antonyms: synonymous, silent|adjective
