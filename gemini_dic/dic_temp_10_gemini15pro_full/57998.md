driver:/ˈdraɪvər/ (US) /ˈdraɪvə(r)/ (UK)| n.|驾驶员，司机；驱动程序|A person who drives a vehicle.|例句：The bus driver is very experienced. 公共汽车司机经验非常丰富。

例句：He's a terrible driver; he's always getting into accidents. 他开车技术很差，总是出事故。

近义词：operator, chauffeur, motorist|反义词:passenger|词性:noun

driver:/ˈdraɪvər/ (US) /ˈdraɪvə(r)/ (UK)| n.|驱动器，驱动程序|A program that controls a particular type of device attached to your computer.|例句：I need to update my graphics driver. 我需要更新我的显卡驱动程序。

例句：The printer driver isn't working properly. 打印机驱动程序工作不正常。

近义词：software, program|反义词:None|词性:noun
