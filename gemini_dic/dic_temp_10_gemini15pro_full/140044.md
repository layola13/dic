oenophiles:/ˈiːnəˌfaɪlz/ | n. | 酒 connoisseur; 爱好者 | A person who is knowledgeable about and enjoys wine. |  Oenophiles will appreciate the subtle notes of oak in this vintage.  葡萄酒爱好者会欣赏这款葡萄酒中橡木的微妙味道。 | 近义词: wine lover, connoisseur, wine buff | 反义词: teetotaler, abstainer | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Greek *oinos* (wine) + *philos* (loving).
