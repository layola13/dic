commissaries:/ˈkɒmɪsəriz/ (UK), /ˈkɑːmɪsəriz/ (US)| n.|军粮供应站，小卖部；(尤指电影制片厂的)食品杂货店；代表，委员|A store that sells food and supplies, especially to members of the armed forces or to employees of a large organization, at reduced prices. Also, a person authorized to perform certain duties or tasks, often in a diplomatic or military context.|The soldiers stocked up on supplies at the commissary. 士兵们在军需供应站储备了物资。|The film crew grabbed lunch at the studio commissary.  电影摄制组在工作室的食品杂货店吃了午饭。|Synonyms: store, canteen, shop, grocery, representative, agent, deputy, delegate|Antonyms:  (For the store meaning:) None. (For the representative meaning:) principal, leader|noun
