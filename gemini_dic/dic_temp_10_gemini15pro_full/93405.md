illiterateness:/ɪˈlɪtərətnəs/
n.
文盲;不识字;缺乏教育
the state of being illiterate; a lack of ability to read and write.
The high rate of illiterateness in the country is a major obstacle to economic development. 这个国家的高文盲率是经济发展的主要障碍。
近义词: illiteracy, ignorance, unlearnedness
反义词: literacy, education, knowledge
词性: noun


illiterate:/ɪˈlɪtərət/
adj.
文盲的;不识字的;缺乏教育的
unable to read or write.
Many adults in developing countries are still illiterate. 许多发展中国家的成年人仍然是文盲。
近义词: unlettered, uneducated, ignorant
反义词: literate, educated, knowledgeable
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *illitteratus, from Latin in- (“not”) + litteratus (“lettered”).
