nonnarration:/nɒnˌnæreɪʃən/ | n. | 非叙述，非叙事 |  The absence of narration, often used in film or other media to describe a style that focuses on visual storytelling rather than spoken or written exposition. |  The film employed nonnarration, relying heavily on imagery and symbolism to convey its message. 这部电影采用非叙事手法，主要依靠图像和象征来传达其信息。

The director's use of nonnarration created a sense of mystery and intrigue. 导演对非叙事手法的运用营造了一种神秘和阴谋的气氛。 | 近义词：visual storytelling, showing (not telling) | 反义词：narration, exposition | 词性: 名词
