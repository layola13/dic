imaginational:/ɪˌmædʒɪˈneɪʃənəl/
adjective
富有想象力的；虚构的
Relating to or produced by the imagination.
例句：The child's drawing was highly imaginational, full of fantastical creatures and vibrant colors.  这孩子的画充满了想象力，画满了奇异的生物和鲜艳的色彩。

例句：The author created an imaginational world filled with magic and wonder.  作者创造了一个充满魔法和奇迹的想象世界。

近义词：imaginative, fanciful, creative, visionary
反义词：realistic, practical, unimaginative
词性: adjective
