openly:/ˈoʊpənli/| adv.|公开地，坦率地|In an open manner; without secrecy or concealment.  Publicly and unreservedly.|She openly admitted her mistake. 她公开承认了自己的错误。|近义词：publicly, frankly, candidly|反义词:secretly, privately, covertly|词性:副词
Etymology: From 'open' + '-ly' (suffix forming adverbs). 'Open' comes from Old English 'open', of Germanic origin.
