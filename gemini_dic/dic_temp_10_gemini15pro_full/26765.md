caenozoic: /ˌsiːnəˈzoʊɪk/ /ˌsenəˈzoʊɪk/
| adj. n.|新生代的；新生代|Relating to or denoting the most recent era of geological history, following the Mesozoic era and comprising the Paleogene and Neogene periods (and extending from about 66 million years ago to the present). Also used as a noun, referring to the Cenozoic Era itself.|The Cenozoic era saw the rise of mammals. 新生代见证了哺乳动物的兴起。|Synonyms: Recent, latest|Antonyms: Mesozoic, Paleozoic|adjective, noun
