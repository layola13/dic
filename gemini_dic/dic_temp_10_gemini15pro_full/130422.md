nonabiding:/ˌnɒnəˈbaɪdɪŋ/
adj.
不遵守的，不持久的
Not abiding; not obeying or enduring.
The nonabiding nature of temporary tattoos makes them popular for events.  临时纹身的短暂性使其在活动中很受欢迎。
The contract is nonabiding after one year.  一年后，合同失效。
近义词: disobedient, lawless, rebellious, unruly, deviant, noncompliant
反义词: obedient, law-abiding, compliant, conforming
词性: adjective
