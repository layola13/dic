devote:/dɪˈvoʊt/| vt.| 致力于；奉献；|To give all or a large part of one's time or resources to a person, activity, or cause.|She devoted her life to helping others. 她一生致力于帮助他人。|近义词：dedicate, commit, apply|反义词：neglect, abandon|词性:动词
devoter:/dɪˈvoʊtər/| n.| 献身者；爱好者；|A person who is very interested in and enthusiastic about someone or something.|He is a devoter of classical music. 他是古典音乐的爱好者。|近义词：enthusiast, fan, aficionado|反义词：critic, detractor|词性:名词

词源:
From Late Latin *dēvōtāre, from Latin dēvōvēre (“to dedicate by a vow, devote, curse”), from dē (“down, from, away”) + vōvēre (“to vow, promise solemnly”).
