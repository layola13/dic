blottesque: /blɒˈtɛsk/ | adj. | 墨迹般的，污点状的；丑陋怪诞的 | Resembling or suggestive of a blot or stain; grotesquely ugly or misshapen. | The painting was a blottesque mess of colors. 这幅画是一团污迹般的色彩.| 近义词：blotchy, stained, grotesque, malformed | 反义词：pristine, immaculate, beautiful, shapely | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From French "blottesque," from "blotte" (blot) + "-esque" (like). Ultimately from Frankish *blotto "lump, piece."
