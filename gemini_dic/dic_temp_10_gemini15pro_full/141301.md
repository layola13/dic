open-mindedness:/ˌoʊpənˈmaɪndɪdnəs/ |n.| 开放思想；心胸开阔|The scientist approached the new theory with open-mindedness.| She demonstrated remarkable open-mindedness in considering different perspectives. | 近义词: receptiveness, tolerance, broad-mindedness | 反义词: closed-mindedness, intolerance, prejudice | 词性: 名词

open-mindednesses: /ˌoʊpənˈmaɪndɪdnəsɪz/ |n.|  开放思想；心胸开阔（复数形式，较少使用）| The open-mindednesses of the participants allowed for a productive discussion. |  The various open-mindednesses present in the group led to a diverse range of solutions. | 近义词: receptivenesses, tolerances (较少使用复数) | 反义词: closed-mindednesses, intolerances (较少使用复数) | 词性: 名词


词源:  open-mindedness 源于形容词 open-minded，由 open (开放的) + minded (有…思想的) 构成。"-ness" 是一个名词后缀，用于将形容词转换为名词，表示状态或性质。
