dulcamara:/ˌdʌlkəˈmɑːrə/ /ˌdʌlkəˈmɛərə/ | n. | 欧白英；苦乐参 | A climbing, shrubby nightshade (Solanum dulcamara) with purple flowers and poisonous bright red berries, native to Eurasia and naturalized in North America.  It is also called bittersweet nightshade. | The dulcamara twined around the old fence post. 欧白英缠绕着旧栅栏柱。| 近义词：bittersweet nightshade, woody nightshade | 反义词: N/A | noun
