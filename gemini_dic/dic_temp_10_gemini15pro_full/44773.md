crumpler:/ˈkrʌmplər/ | n. |打包工，包装工；(尤指)装苹果的人；褶皱机|A person who crumples something, especially apples for packing.  A machine that crumples something. |He worked as a crumpler in the apple orchard. 他在苹果园里当打包工。|近义词：packer, wrapper|反义词：unpacker, unwrapper|词性:名词

crumple:/ˈkrʌmpl/ | v. |弄皱，压皱；(因外力)变形，垮塌；倒下，瘫倒|To crush something into wrinkles or folds; to become wrinkled or crushed; to collapse or fall down suddenly.|The car crumpled in the crash. 汽车在撞车事故中变形了。He crumpled to the ground in pain.  他痛苦地瘫倒在地上。|近义词：crush, crease, collapse|反义词：smooth, straighten, unfold|词性:动词

词源:
From Middle English *crumpelen, frequentative of crumpen (“to bend, curve”), from Old English *crump (“crooked, bent”). Cognate with Dutch krompen (“to shrink, shrivel”), German krump (“crooked”).
来自中古英语 *crumpelen，是 crumpen（“弯曲，卷曲”）的重复形式，源于古英语 *crump（“弯曲的，弯的”）。 与荷兰语 krompen（“收缩，萎缩”）、德语 krump（“弯曲的”）同源。
