instinct:/ˈɪnstɪŋkt/ | n. | 本能，直觉 | An innate, typically fixed pattern of behavior in animals in response to certain stimuli.  A natural or inherent aptitude or capacity. | Birds build nests by instinct. 鸟类筑巢是出于本能。 He had an instinct for knowing where the best deals were. 他天生就知道哪里有最好的交易。 | 近义词： intuition, hunch, reflex | 反义词：reasoning, logic | noun
