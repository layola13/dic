brackened:/ˈbrækənd/ | adj. |蕨类植物覆盖的；长满蕨类的|Covered with bracken (a large, coarse fern).|The hills were brackened and desolate.  这些山丘长满了蕨类植物，一片荒凉。|例句：The path led through a brackened hillside.  这条小路穿过长满蕨类的山坡。|近义词：fern-covered, overgrown |反义词: clear, barren |词性:形容词
词源: From bracken + -ed.  Bracken comes from Old Norse *brakni.
