overimposed:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈpoʊzd/
| adj. |重叠的，叠加的| Placed or laid over something else, typically so as to obscure it partly or wholly. | The overimposed images created a confusing blur. 重叠的图像造成了一片混乱的模糊。|The artist overimposed layers of paint to create texture. 艺术家叠加了几层颜料来创造质感。| superimposed, overlaid | underlying | adjective

overimpose:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈpoʊz/
| vt. |叠加，重叠| To place or lay (something) over something else, typically so as to obscure it partly or wholly. |  He overimposed one image onto another. 他把一个图像叠加到另一个图像上。 |The new regulations are overimposed on the existing ones. 新规章制度叠加在现有的规章制度之上。| superimpose, overlay | remove, separate | verb
