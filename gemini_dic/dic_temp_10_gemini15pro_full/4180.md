airdrop:/ˈɛərdrɒp/ (US) /ˈeədrɒp/ (UK)| n. v.|空投；空投物资；空投行动；(通过无线网络)隔空投送|An act of dropping supplies or personnel from an aircraft, or the supplies or personnel dropped in this way. Also refers to the wireless transfer of files between devices.|The military performed an airdrop of supplies to the stranded refugees. 軍方向受困的難民空投了物資。|Synonyms: airlift, paradrop, delivery|Antonyms: pickup, ground transportation|noun, verb
