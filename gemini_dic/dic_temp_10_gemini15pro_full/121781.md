misrecite:/ˌmɪsrɪˈsaɪt/ | v. | 错误地背诵，背错 | To recite something incorrectly, such as a poem, passage, or lines in a play. |  He misrecited his lines in the school play and ruined the scene. 他在学校戏剧中背错了台词，毁了整个场景。

| The nervous actor misrecited several lines during the audition.  紧张的演员在试镜时背错了好几句台词。| 近义词: fumble, misquote, misspeak | 反义词: recite correctly | 词性: 动词
