hydrosulphite: /ˌhaɪdrəˈsʌlfaɪt/ | n. | 连二亚硫酸钠; 氢硫酸盐 | a white crystalline salt, Na2S2O4, used as a reducing agent in dyeing and bleaching. |  Sodium hydrosulphite is commonly used in the textile industry. 连二亚硫酸钠常用于纺织工业。 | synonym: sodium dithionite, dithionite | antonym: oxidizing agent | noun
