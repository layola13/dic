mesoblastic:/ˌmezəˈblæstɪk/
adjective
中胚层的;由中胚层发育而来的
Relating to or derived from the mesoblast, the middle germ layer of an embryo.
The mesoblastic layer gives rise to the circulatory system, muscles, and skeleton. 中胚层会发育形成循环系统、肌肉和骨骼。
The scientist studied the mesoblastic origin of the heart.  这位科学家研究了心脏的中胚层起源。

词性:adjective


mesoblast: /ˈmezəˌblæst/
noun
中胚层
The middle germ layer of an embryo, lying between the ectoderm and endoderm.
The mesoblast differentiates into the skeleton, muscles, and circulatory system. 中胚层分化成骨骼、肌肉和循环系统。
The scientist observed the development of the mesoblast in the chicken embryo.  科学家观察了鸡胚胎中中胚层的生长发育。
近义词:mesoderm
反义词: (无明确的反义词)
词性:noun


词源:
From meso- + -blast.  "meso-" comes from Greek mesos "middle". "-blast" comes from Greek blastos "germ, sprout."
