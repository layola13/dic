chemistries:/ˈkɛmɪstriz/
n.
化学过程; 化学作用; 化学成分;  相互吸引的性质
The complex chemistries of the human brain. 人脑的复杂化学过程。
The two have a special chemistry. 两人之间有种特殊的相互吸引。
synonyms: reactions, interactions, makeup, affinity, rapport
antonyms: incompatibility, discord
