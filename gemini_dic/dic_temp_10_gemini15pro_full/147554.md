overpenalizes: /ˌoʊvərˈpiːnəlaɪzɪz/
verb
过度惩罚，惩罚过重
to penalize someone too harshly or excessively.  The referee overpenalized the team for minor infractions.
The new law overpenalizes small businesses.  新的法律过度惩罚了小型企业。
Synonyms: overpunishes, oversanctions
Antonyms: underpenalizes, underpunishes
词性: 动词

Etymology:
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "penalize" (from "penal" ultimately from Latin "poena" meaning "punishment").
