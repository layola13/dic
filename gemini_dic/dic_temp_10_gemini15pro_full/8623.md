anticommercialism:/ˌænti kəˈmɜːrʃəlɪzəm/
|n.|反商业主义|Opposition to commercialism or the pursuit of profit as a dominant value in society.  It often emphasizes ethical considerations, social responsibility, and the prioritization of values other than monetary gain. |The anticommercialism movement gained traction during the 1960s. 反商业主义运动在20世纪60年代获得了关注。| The festival organizers embraced anticommercialism by refusing corporate sponsorships.  节日组织者拒绝了企业赞助，拥护反商业主义。|anticonsumerism, anti-materialism|commercialism, materialism|noun
