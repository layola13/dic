auto-suggestible:/ˌɔːtəsəˈdʒɛstəbl/ | adj. | 易受暗示的，易被自我暗示的 | Easily influenced by suggestion, especially self-suggestion; readily accepting and acting on suggestions, particularly those originating within oneself. | Example:  He was an auto-suggestible patient who readily embraced the placebo effect. 他是一位易受暗示的病人，很容易接受安慰剂效应。

Example: Her auto-suggestible nature made her susceptible to various fad diets. 她易受暗示的性格使她容易受到各种流行减肥方法的影响。| 近义词: suggestible, impressionable, susceptible | 反义词: resistant, skeptical, critical | 词性: adjective


Etymology:
auto- (prefix): from Greek autos "self"
suggest (verb): from Latin suggerere "to bring up, supply," from sub- "under" + gerere "to carry, bear"
-ible (suffix): from Latin -ibilis, meaning "capable of being"
