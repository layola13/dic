driveways:/ˈdraɪvˌweɪz/
n.
车道；私家车道
A private road providing access to a house or garage.
The delivery truck was parked in the driveway. 送货卡车停在车道上。

The long driveway wound through the trees. 长长的车道蜿蜒穿过树林。

近义词:access road, private road, approach
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "street" or "road" in certain contexts)
词性:noun (plural)

词源: From 'drive' + 'way'.  'Drive' originally referred to driving livestock, then expanded to mean driving vehicles. 'Way' simply means a path or road.
