nucleoplasmic: /ˌnjuːkliːoʊˈplæzmɪk/ | adj. | 核质的；细胞核基质的 | Relating to or occurring in the nucleoplasm, the substance of the cell nucleus except for the chromosomes and nucleolus. |  The nucleoplasmic concentration of this protein is higher than in the cytoplasm.  这种蛋白质在核质中的浓度高于细胞质。| synonyms: nuclear, karyoplasmic | antonyms: cytoplasmic | adjective
