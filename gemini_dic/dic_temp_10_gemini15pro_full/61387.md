emblazon:/ɪmˈbleɪzən/
verb
中文翻译：纹章装饰；宣扬；使显著
英文解析：To adorn or decorate with prominent markings, especially heraldic devices; to celebrate or proclaim publicly.
例句：The knight's shield was emblazoned with a golden lion. 骑士的盾牌上饰有金色的狮子。

例句：The company's logo was emblazoned on all their products.  公司的标志装饰在他们所有的产品上。


近义词：adorn, decorate, embellish, celebrate, proclaim
反义词：conceal, hide, obscure
词性：v.
