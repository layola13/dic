moesogothic: /ˌmoʊsoʊˈɡɒθɪk/ | adj. |  萌系哥特 | A subgenre of gothic fashion and aesthetics characterized by a combination of gothic elements (dark colors, crosses, etc.) with cute and childlike elements (pastel colors, toys, stuffed animals, etc.).  It originated in Japan and often incorporates elements of anime and manga. |  Her style could be described as moesogothic, with a black dress adorned with lace and a Hello Kitty backpack. 她风格可以被描述为萌系哥特，穿着饰有蕾丝的黑色连衣裙，背着Hello Kitty的背包。 |  Synonyms: sweet lolita, decora, fairy kei | Antonyms: traditional gothic, punk | Adjective
