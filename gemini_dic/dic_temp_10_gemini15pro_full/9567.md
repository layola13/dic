antiskeptics:/ˌæntiˈskeptɪks/
| n.|反怀疑论者|Those who oppose or argue against skepticism are called antiskeptics.  那些反对或驳斥怀疑论的人被称为反怀疑论者。|Antiskeptics often argue for the possibility of certain knowledge. 反怀疑论者经常论证确定知识的可能性。|近义词：believer, optimist|反义词: skeptic, doubter|词性:noun

antiskeptic:/ˌæntiˈskeptɪk/
| adj.|反怀疑论的|An antiskeptic viewpoint emphasizes the reliability of our senses. 反怀疑论的观点强调我们感官的可靠性.|His antiskeptic stance made him a target for the philosophers. 他的反怀疑论立场使他成为哲学家的攻击目标。|近义词：credulous, trusting|反义词: skeptical, doubting|词性:adjective
