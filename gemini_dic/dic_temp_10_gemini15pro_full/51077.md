despair:/dɪˈspɛr/| n. vi.|绝望，丧失信心|The situation filled him with despair.  这种情况让他充满了绝望。|例句：He was overcome by despair when his wife died.  妻子去世后，他陷入了绝望。

despair:/dɪˈspɛr/| vi.|感到绝望|Don't despair! Things will get better.  别绝望！事情会好转的。|例句：She despaired of ever seeing him again.  她对再次见到他已经绝望了。


近义词：hopelessness, despondency|反义词:hope, optimism|词性:名词, 动词
