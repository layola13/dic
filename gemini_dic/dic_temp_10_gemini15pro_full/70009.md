fetid:/ˈfɛtɪd/ (US), /ˈfiːtɪd/ (UK)| adj.|恶臭的，腐臭的|Having a strong, unpleasant smell; stinking.|The fetid odor of the rotting meat filled the air.  腐烂的肉发出恶臭，弥漫在空气中。|The fetid swamp was a breeding ground for mosquitoes.  这个恶臭的沼泽是蚊子的滋生地。|Synonyms: foul, putrid, rank, noisome|Antonyms: fragrant, aromatic, sweet-smelling|Adjective
