combated:/kəmˈbætɪd/
verb
斗争过，战斗过
past participle of combat, meaning "fought against" or "struggled with."
The firefighters combated the blaze for hours. 消防队员与大火搏斗了几个小时。
近义词:fought, opposed, resisted, withstood
反义词:aided, assisted, supported, yielded
词性:verb (past participle)
