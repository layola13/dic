clocks:/klɒks/ (British) /klɑːks/ (American)| n.|钟，时钟|instruments for measuring and indicating time, especially ones that are not worn on the wrist.|The grandfather clocks chimed midnight. 祖父的钟敲响了午夜。|synonyms: timepieces, chronometers, timers|antonyms: sundials (arguably, as they don't measure time mechanically)|noun
