lumen:/ˈluːmən/| n. |流明（光通量单位）|A unit of luminous flux in the International System of Units (SI), that is equal to the amount of light given out through a solid angle by a source of one candela intensity radiating equally in all directions.|The lamp emits 1000 lumens.|近义词: lux, candlepower|反义词: darkness|词性: noun
