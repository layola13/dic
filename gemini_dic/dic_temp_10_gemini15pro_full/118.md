abalone:/ˈæbəˌloʊni/ (US) /ˌæbəˈləʊni/ (UK)| n.|鲍鱼|A shellfish that has a flat, oval shell with a row of holes along one side. Its flesh is eaten as food.|例句：We had abalone for dinner last night. 昨晚我们晚餐吃了鲍鱼。

Abalone is considered a delicacy in many cultures. 在许多文化中，鲍鱼被认为是一种美味佳肴。|近义词：ear-shell, ormer, sea-ear|反义词: None|词性: Noun
