deficient:/dɪˈfɪʃənt/| adj.|缺乏的，不足的| lacking some element or characteristic; insufficient or inadequate.|例句：Their diet is deficient in vitamin C.  他们的饮食缺乏维生素C。|近义词：lacking, insufficient, inadequate, short, scarce|反义词：sufficient, adequate, ample, plentiful|词性:形容词
deficients:/dɪˈfɪʃənts/| n.|有缺陷的人或物（复数）| plural form of deficient, referring to people or things that are lacking in something.|例句：The deficients in the program were given extra support.  项目中有缺陷的人得到了额外的支持。|近义词：incompetents, inadequates|反义词：experts, professionals|词性:名词

词源:
Late Middle English: from Latin deficient- ‘failing, lacking’, from the verb deficere, from de- ‘down, away’ + facere ‘to do’.
