outplan:/ˌaʊtˈplæn/
verb
胜过…的计划; 比…计划更周密
to plan better or more thoroughly than someone else
例句：They were outplanned and outmaneuvered by the enemy. 他们在计划和策略上都被敌人胜过了。
例句：We were completely outplanned by the other team, which is why we lost. 我们完全被另一支队伍的计划所击败，这就是我们失败的原因。
近义词：outsmart, outmaneuver, outthink
反义词：be outplanned, be outsmarted
