mucopurulent:/ˌmjuːkoʊˈpjʊərələnt/| adj.|粘脓性的|Containing or consisting of both mucus and pus.|The patient presented with a mucopurulent discharge from the nose. 病人鼻子流出粘脓性分泌物。|近义词：purulent, suppurative|反义词: non-purulent, clear|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From mucopurlent, from muco- (combining form meaning "mucus") + purulent (containing or consisting of pus).  The word "mucus" comes from Latin "mucus" (meaning "snot, slime"). "Purulent" derives from Latin "purulentus," from "pus" (meaning "pus, matter").
