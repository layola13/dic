de-stress:/diːˈstrɛs/
verb
中文翻译：缓解压力，减轻压力
英文解析：To reduce or eliminate stress or tension.
例句：I de-stress by listening to music. 我通过听音乐来减压。

例句：She took a yoga class to de-stress after a long day at work.  在下班后，她上了一节瑜伽课来缓解压力。

近义词：relax, unwind, calm down, chill out
反义词：stress, tense, worry
词性：verb


de-stress:/diːˈstrɛs/
noun
中文翻译：减压，缓解压力
英文解析：The act of reducing or eliminating stress or tension.
例句：Yoga is a great form of de-stress. 瑜伽是一种很好的减压方式。

例句：Taking a vacation is a good way to experience de-stress. 度假是体验减压的好方法。


近义词：relaxation, unwinding
反义词：stress, tension
词性：noun
