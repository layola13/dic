demurrage:/dɪˈmɜːrɪdʒ/ | n. |滞期费，延滞费|  A charge payable to the owner of a chartered ship on account of undue detention of the vessel beyond the time allowed for loading, unloading, or sailing. | The charterer had to pay a hefty demurrage due to unforeseen delays in port.  承租人由于港口不可预见的延误而不得不支付巨额滞期费。 | synonyms: detention charges, late fees | antonyms: dispatch money, early completion bonus | noun

demurrage:/dɪˈmɜːrɪdʒ/ | n. |滞留,延误| Any undue detention or delay. | The demurrage of the train caused us to miss our connecting flight. 火车的延误导致我们错过了转机航班。| synonyms: delay, detention | antonyms: expedition, acceleration | noun
