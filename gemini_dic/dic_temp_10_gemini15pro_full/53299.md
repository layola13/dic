dirtyish:/ˈdɜːrtiɪʃ/ | adj. | 稍微脏的，有点脏的 | Somewhat dirty; slightly unclean. |  The tablecloth was dirtyish after the picnic. 野餐后，桌布有点脏。|  The floor was a bit dirtyish, needing a sweep. 地板有点脏，需要扫一下。 | 近义词:  grubby, soiled, messy | 反义词: clean, spotless, immaculate | 词性: 形容词

词源:  dirty + -ish.  The suffix "-ish"  means "somewhat" or "approximately."  It is added to adjectives to create a new adjective indicating a lesser degree of the original quality.  "Dirty" comes from Old English "dihtig."
