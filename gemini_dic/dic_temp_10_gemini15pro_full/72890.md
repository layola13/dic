followed: /ˈfɒləʊd/ (British) /ˈfɑːloʊd/ (American) | v. | 跟随，接着，理解，关注 | Followed by a loud bang, the lights went out.  一声巨响之后，灯灭了。| synonyms: pursued, succeeded, ensued, adhered to, observed | antonyms: preceded, led, ignored, disobeyed | verb

followed: /ˈfɒləʊd/ (British) /ˈfɑːloʊd/ (American) | adj. | 接着的，后面的 | The followed chapters will discuss this issue in more detail. 后面的章节将更详细地讨论这个问题。| synonyms: subsequent, ensuing, next | antonyms: preceding, previous, prior | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *folewen, from Old English folgian (“to follow, pursue, persecute”), from Proto-Germanic *fulgijaną (“to follow”), from Proto-Indo-European *pl̥kʷ- (“to follow”). Cognate with West Frisian folgje (“to follow”), Dutch volgen (“to follow”), German folgen (“to follow”), Danish følge (“to follow”), Swedish följa (“to follow”), Icelandic fylgja (“to follow”).
