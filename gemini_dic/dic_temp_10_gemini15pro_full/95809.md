incooerdination:/ɪnkoʊˌɔːrdɪˈneɪʃən/
n.|不协调，失调|lack of coordination; inability to make different parts of the body work smoothly together.|His incoordination made it difficult for him to play sports. 他的不协调让他很难进行体育运动。|incoherence, awkwardness, clumsiness|coordination, dexterity, skill|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + coordination (harmonious functioning of parts).  The word "coordination" comes from the Latin "coordinare," meaning "to arrange in order," from "co-" (together) + "ordinare" (to arrange).
