nonsustainability:/ˌnɒnsəˌsteɪnəˈbɪlɪti/ | n. | 不可持续性 |The state or quality of not being sustainable; inability to be maintained at a certain rate or level.| The nonsustainability of current farming practices is a major concern.  当前农业实践的不可持续性是一个主要问题。 | 近义词: unsustainability | 反义词: sustainability | 词性: 名词

Etymology: non- + sustainability.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "sustainability" comes from "sustain" (from Old French "sustenir," meaning "to hold up"), combined with the suffix "-ability."
