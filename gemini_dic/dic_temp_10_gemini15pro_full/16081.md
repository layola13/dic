bandoleers:/ˌbændəˈlɪərz/
n.
子弹带，弹药带
A belt worn over the shoulder and across the chest, typically used to carry ammunition.
The soldier adjusted his bandoleers before heading into battle. 士兵在进入战场前调整了他的子弹带。

Synonyms: ammunition belt, cartridge belt
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps "holster" for a single firearm)
词性: 名词


bandoleer:/ˌbændəˈlɪər/
n.

A belt worn over the shoulder and across the chest, typically used to carry ammunition.
The revolutionary wore a bandoleer across his chest.  这位革命者胸前斜挎着一条子弹带。

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *bandoulière*, from *bande* "band, strip". Ultimately from Frankish *binda*.
