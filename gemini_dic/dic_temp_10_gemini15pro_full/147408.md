overnormalization:/ˌoʊvərˌnɔːrmələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度正常化，过度规范化 | The act of applying grammatical rules too rigidly, often resulting in an incorrect form.  It frequently occurs in language acquisition, where children apply regular grammatical patterns to irregular words. |  Young children sometimes exhibit overnormalization, saying "goed" instead of "went". 小孩子有时会过度规范化，说“goed”而不是“went”。 |  Overregularization, hypercorrection | Underregulation | noun
