microsurgeries:/ˈmaɪkroʊsɜːrdʒəriːz/
noun
显微外科手术
Surgical procedures performed using a microscope and specialized instruments, typically involving very small structures such as blood vessels and nerves.
Microsurgeries are often used to reattach severed limbs. 显微外科手术常用于断肢再植。
Microsurgeries can repair damaged nerves and restore function. 显微外科手术可以修复受损的神经并恢复功能。
synonyms: microsurgical procedures, microprocedures
antonyms: macrosurgeries, conventional surgeries
n.
