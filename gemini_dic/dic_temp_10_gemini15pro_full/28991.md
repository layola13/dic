cartesianism:/kɑːrˈtiːʒənɪzəm/
n.
笛卡尔主义，笛卡尔哲学
The philosophical system of René Descartes. It is characterized by its dualism of mind and matter and its emphasis on reason and deduction.
Descartes's influence on Western thought is profound, and Cartesianism remains a significant philosophical tradition. 笛卡尔对西方思想的影响是深远的，笛卡尔主义仍然是一个重要的哲学传统。
rationalism, dualism
empiricism, idealism
noun
