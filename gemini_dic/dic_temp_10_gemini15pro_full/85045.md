harbourer:/ˈhɑːrbərər/ (US) /ˈhɑːbərə(r)/ (UK)| n.|窝藏者，庇护者|A person who shelters or hides someone, especially a criminal or fugitive. |The police were searching for the harbourer of the escaped convict. 警方正在搜捕窝藏逃犯的人。|近义词：protector, shelterer, concealer|反义词: betrayer, informant|noun

harbourer:/ˈhɑːrbərər/ (US) /ˈhɑːbərə(r)/ (UK)| n.|怀有…的人|A person who has or keeps (a thought, feeling, or belief), especially a negative one. |He was a harbourer of resentment towards his former employer. 他对他的前任雇主怀恨在心。|近义词：keeper, holder|反义词: releaser|noun
