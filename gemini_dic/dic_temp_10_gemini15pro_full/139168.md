obituary: /əˈbɪtʃuːeri/ (US), /əˈbɪtʃʊəri/ (UK)| n. |讣告，讣闻|A notice of a person's death, usually including a short biography.|The newspaper published a lengthy obituary for the renowned scientist.|Synonyms: death notice, necrology|Antonyms: birth announcement|noun


obituarial: /əˈbɪtʃuːeriəl/ (US), /əˈbɪtʃʊˈɛəriəl/ (UK)| adj. |讣告的，讣闻的|Relating to or in the style of an obituary.|The obituarial tone of the article made it clear the subject had passed away.|Synonyms: elegiac, valedictory|Antonyms: celebratory, congratulatory|adjective
