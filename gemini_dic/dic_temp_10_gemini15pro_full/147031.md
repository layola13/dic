Characterized by or given to excessive praise. |  His overlaudatory remarks about the mediocre painting embarrassed everyone. 他对那幅平庸的画作的过分赞扬让每个人都很尴尬。| Synonyms:  effusive, gushing, extravagant, fulsome | Antonyms:  critical, disparaging, understated, measured | Word Type: Adjective
