hazardable:/ˈhæzərdəbəl/
adjective
危险的，有危险的
Capable of posing a hazard; dangerous.
The chemicals are hazardable and must be handled with care. 这些化学品有危险，必须小心处理。
The old building was deemed hazardable and slated for demolition. 这栋旧楼被认为是危险的，预定要拆除。
近义词:dangerous, risky, perilous, hazardous
反义词:safe, secure
词性:adjective

词源:  From hazard + -able.
