nontariff: /ˌnɒnˈtærɪf/ /ˌnɑːnˈtɛrɪf/ | adj. n.| 非关税的，非关税壁垒 |  Relating to or involving regulations that restrict trade but do not involve import duties.  |  Many countries use nontariff barriers to protect domestic industries. 许多国家使用非关税壁垒来保护国内产业。| synonym: protectionist, restrictive | antonym: free trade, tariff | adjective, noun

nontariff: /ˌnɒnˈtærɪf/ /ˌnɑːnˈtɛrɪf/ | n. | 非关税壁垒 | A nontariff barrier is a form of restrictive trade where barriers to trade are set up and take a form other than a tariff.  |  The government imposed a nontariff barrier on imported agricultural products. 政府对进口农产品施加了非关税壁垒。| synonym: trade restriction, trade barrier | antonym: tariff, free trade | noun
