nonfeverishly:/nɒnˈfiːvərɪʃli/ /nɑːnˈfiːvərɪʃli/
|adv.| 非狂热地，冷静地 |Without fever; in a calm and controlled manner, not frantic or frenetic.|She worked nonfeverishly, taking her time to ensure accuracy. 她工作起来不慌不忙，花时间确保准确性。|例句：He addressed the issue nonfeverishly, presenting the facts calmly. 他冷静地陈述了事实，不带任何情绪地处理了这个问题。|近义词：calmly, coolly, composedly, sedately, peacefully|反义词：feverishly, frantically, excitedly, hysterically|词性: 副词

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"  来源于拉丁语
feverish (adjective): characterized by fever; intensely excited or active. 来源于古英语 "fefor"
