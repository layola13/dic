gallomania:/ˌɡæləˈmeɪniə/ | n. | 狂热的爱法国文化；亲法狂 | An excessive admiration of French customs and culture. | He suffers from gallomania, constantly praising French wine and fashion. 他患有亲法狂，不断赞扬法国的葡萄酒和时尚。| 近义词: Francophilia | 反义词: Francophobia | 词性: noun

词源: From French *gallomanie*, from *gallo-* (Gaul, referring to France) + *-manie* (mania).
