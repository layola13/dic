barracks:/ˈbærəks/ (US) /ˈbærɪks/ (UK)| n. |兵营；营房|A building or group of buildings used to house soldiers.|The soldiers returned to their barracks after a long day of training. 士兵们经过一天的训练后回到了兵营。|
例句：
The barracks were overcrowded and unsanitary. 营房拥挤不堪，而且不卫生。
New barracks are being built to accommodate the increasing number of soldiers.  新的兵营正在建造中，以容纳越来越多的士兵。|
近义词：quarters, accommodations, housing|反义词：home, residence|词性：名词
