ingorge:/ɪnˈɡɔːrdʒ/ /ɪnˈɡɔːrdʒ/
verb
狼吞虎咽；(使)充满；塞满
to eat a large amount of food greedily; to fill something completely; to stuff
The boy ingorged himself with cake. 这个男孩狼吞虎咽地吃蛋糕。
The river had ingorged itself with melted snow. 这条河充满了融化的雪。
近义词: gorge, devour, stuff, cram
反义词: abstain, fast
词性: verb
