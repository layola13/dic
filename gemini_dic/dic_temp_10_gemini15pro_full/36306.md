cohesion:/koʊˈhiːʒən/ /kəʊˈhiːʒən/| n. |凝聚力，团结，结合|The cohesion of the group was remarkable, despite their differing backgrounds.  这个群体的凝聚力很强，尽管他们的背景各不相同。|例句：The team showed great cohesion in the face of adversity.  面对逆境，团队展现了强大的凝聚力。

cohesion:/koʊˈhiːʒən/ /kəʊˈhiːʒən/| n. |内聚性，粘结性|The cohesion of the molecules is what gives the substance its strength. 分子的内聚性赋予了这种物质强度。|例句：The cohesion of the soil is important for preventing erosion. 土壤的粘结性对于防止侵蚀很重要。|近义词：unity, solidarity, coherence, adhesion|反义词:division, disunity, fragmentation|词性:名词
