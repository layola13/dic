herbier:/ɛʁ.bje/ (French), /ɜːr.bi.eɪ/ (anglicized)| n.| 植物标本集，腊叶标本集|A collection of dried plant specimens mounted on sheets of paper, often with accompanying information such as the plant's name, habitat, and date of collection.  It's used for scientific study and identification.|例句：The botanist consulted the herbier to identify the unknown species.  植物学家查阅了植物标本集以鉴定未知物种。

例句：She carefully arranged the pressed flowers in her herbier. 她小心翼翼地将压好的花排列在她的植物标本集中。|近义词：herbarium|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
