myth:/mɪθ/ | n. | 神话，虚构的故事，荒诞的说法 | A traditional story, especially one concerning the early history of a people or explaining some natural or social phenomenon, and typically involving supernatural beings or events. |  The myth of Icarus is a cautionary tale about the dangers of hubris. 伊卡洛斯的神话是一个关于傲慢危险的警示故事。

The myth that older workers are less productive needs to be dispelled.  老年工人效率较低的说法需要被消除。 | 近义词：legend, fable, fiction, falsehood | 反义词：reality, truth, fact | 词性: noun
