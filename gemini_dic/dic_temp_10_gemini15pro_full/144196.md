outsifted:/aʊtˈsɪftɪd/
verb
筛选出来的，仔细挑选出来的
having been sifted out; separated by sifting.  The archaeologist outsifted the dirt looking for artifacts. 考古学家筛选泥土寻找文物。
近义词:  separated, filtered, winnowed, culled
反义词: combined, mixed, included
词性:  Verb (past participle)

词源：
From out- + sift + -ed.  "Out-" prefix indicating removal or separation, and "sift" meaning to separate finer particles from coarser ones.
