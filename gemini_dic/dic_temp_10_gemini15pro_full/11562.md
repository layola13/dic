aristocrats:/ˈærɪstəkræts/ (US) /ˌærɪstəkræts/ (UK)
n.
贵族
People of high social rank who typically have titles, inherited wealth, and land.
The aristocrats lived in opulent mansions and enjoyed a life of privilege.  贵族们住在豪华的宅邸里，享受着特权生活。
The aristocrats attended the royal ball.  贵族们参加了皇家舞会。

近义词：nobles, gentry, elite, patricians
反义词：commoners, peasants, proletariat
词性：noun (plural)

aristocrat:/ˈærɪstəkræt/ (US) /ˌærɪstəkræt/ (UK)
n.
贵族
A member of the aristocracy.
He behaved like an aristocrat, even though his family had modest means.  他表现得像个贵族，尽管他的家庭并不富裕。
She married an aristocrat and became a duchess. 她嫁给了一位贵族，成了一位公爵夫人。

近义词：noble, nobleman, gentlewoman
反义词：commoner, peasant
词性：noun


词源: From French *aristocrate*, from Greek *aristokratēs* "rule of the best," from *aristos* "best" + *kratēs* "power, rule."
