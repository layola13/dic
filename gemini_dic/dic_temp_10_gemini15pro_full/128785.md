nightclothes: /ˈnaɪtˌkloʊðz/ /ˈnaɪtˌkləʊðz/
n.
睡衣
Garments worn for sleeping.
She changed into her nightclothes and went to bed. 她换上睡衣上床睡觉了。

近义词: pajamas, pyjamas, sleepwear
反义词: daywear, street clothes
词性: noun
