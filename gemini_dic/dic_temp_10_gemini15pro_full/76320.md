gabioned:/ˈɡeɪbiənd/
adjective
用石笼加固的;装在石笼里的
Enclosed or fortified with gabions.
The riverbank was gabioned to prevent erosion.  河岸用石笼加固以防止侵蚀。
The gabioned walls protected the fortress.  石笼墙保护着堡垒。
近义词:reinforced, fortified
反义词:unprotected, exposed
词性:adjective

词源:From French *gabionner*, from Italian gabbione (“large cage”), from gabbia (“cage”), from Latin cavea (“cage, enclosure”).
