misery:/ˈmɪzəri/| n. |痛苦，悲惨，不幸|A state of extreme unhappiness and suffering.|She lived a life of misery after the accident. 事故发生后，她过着悲惨的生活。|近义词：distress, suffering, anguish, torment, woe|反义词:happiness, joy, bliss|noun

miserable:/ˈmɪzrəbl/| adj. |悲惨的，痛苦的，可怜的|Wretchedly unhappy or uncomfortable.|The rainy weather made me feel miserable.  下雨天让我感到很痛苦。|近义词：unhappy, wretched, sorrowful, dejected|反义词:happy, cheerful, joyful|adjective
