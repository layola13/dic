line-out:/ˈlaɪn aʊt/ | n. |线路输出; 排队; 划线;  |A connection on a piece of audio equipment that sends a signal to another device. The process of removing a player from a game or lineup. The act of marking or drawing a line. |例句：The line-out on my amplifier is broken. 我的放大器的线路输出坏了。|例句：The coach decided on a line-out for the injured player. 教练决定让受伤的球员下场。| 近义词：output, withdrawal, removal, marking | 反义词: input, insertion | 词性: 名词

line-out:/ˈlaɪn aʊt/ | v. |划线; 排队;  |To mark or draw a line through something, often to indicate deletion or correction.  To arrange or form a line. |例句：Please line-out the incorrect answers on the test. 请在测试中划掉错误的答案。|例句：The students lined-out in the hallway for lunch. 学生们在走廊里排队吃午饭。| 近义词：cross out, delete, queue, line up | 反义词: insert, include | 词性: 动词
