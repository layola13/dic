boastings: /ˈboʊstɪŋz/
n.
吹嘘；自夸；夸耀
acts of bragging or boasting about something
His boastings about his wealth became tiresome. 他不断吹嘘自己的财富，令人厌烦。
synonyms: bragging, vaunting, boasting, crowing, bragging rights
antonyms: modesty, humility
noun
