aide-de-camp:/ˌeɪd də ˈkɑːmp/ /ˌeɪd də ˈkæmp/
noun
副官；侍从武官
A military officer acting as a confidential assistant to a senior officer.
The general's aide-de-camp delivered the message. 将军的副官递送了消息。
Synonyms: adjutant, assistant
Antonyms: superior, commander
