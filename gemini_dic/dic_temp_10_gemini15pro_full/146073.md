overeating:/ˌoʊvərˈiːtɪŋ/

overeat:/ˌoʊvərˈiːt/
v.|吃得过多，暴食|To eat too much food.|I tend to overeat during the holidays. 我在假期里往往会吃得过多。|gorge, stuff oneself|starve, undereat|verb
