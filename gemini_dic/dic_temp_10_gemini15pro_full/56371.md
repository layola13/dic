dojos:/ˈdōˌjōz/| n. |道场；练习场所|A dojo is a hall or space for immersive learning in a variety of fields, traditionally martial arts. It is a place for practice and training.|Example 1: He practices karate at a local dojo. 他在当地的道场练习空手道。

Example 2:  Many tech companies have adopted the dojo model for training their employees. 许多科技公司采用道场模式来培训员工。|近义词：gym, studio, training center, school|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
