obliteratingly: /əˈblɪtəreɪtɪŋli/
adverb
毁灭性地，彻底地
in a way that destroys something completely
The bomb obliterated the city obliteratingly.  这颗炸弹彻底摧毁了这座城市。
The evidence was obliteratingly damning. 证据确凿得无可辩驳。
近义词: devastatingly, destructively, completely, utterly
反义词: slightly, mildly, gently
词性: adverb

obliterate: /əˈblɪtəreɪt/
verb
消灭；摧毁；抹去
destroy utterly; wipe out
The bomb obliterated the city. 炸弹摧毁了这座城市。
All traces of the ancient city were obliterated by time.  这座古城的所有痕迹都被时间抹去了。
近义词: destroy, annihilate, eradicate, erase
反义词: create, build, establish, preserve
词性: verb

词源: From Latin obliteratus, past participle of obliterare (“to erase, blot out, efface, destroy”), from ob- (“over, against”) + littera (“letter”).
