grogginess:/ˈɡrɒɡɪnəs/ /ˈɡrɑːɡinəs/| n. |昏沉，迷糊，不清醒|The state of being groggy; a feeling of disorientation and sluggishness, often due to lack of sleep or the effects of medication.|I woke up with a terrible grogginess after the long flight. 长途飞行后，我醒来时感到非常昏沉。|Near synonyms: drowsiness, sleepiness, lethargy, stupor|Near antonyms: alertness, wakefulness, vigilance|noun

groggy:/ˈɡrɒɡi/ /ˈɡrɑːɡi/| adj. |昏沉的，迷糊的，不清醒的|Dazed, weak, or unsteady, especially from lack of sleep, intoxication, or illness.|I felt groggy after taking the cold medicine.  吃了感冒药后，我感到昏昏沉沉的。|Near synonyms: drowsy, sleepy, lethargic, dazed|Near antonyms: alert, awake, lively, energetic|adjective
