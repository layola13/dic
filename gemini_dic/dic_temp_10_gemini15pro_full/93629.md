imbody:/ɪmˈbɑːdi/ /ɪmˈbɒdi/| v. | 体现，使具体化，包含 | To give a concrete or visible form to an abstract idea or quality; to include or contain as a constituent part. | The statue embodies the spirit of liberty. 这座雕像体现了自由精神。

The new car embodies the latest technology. 这款新车体现了最新的科技。

近义词：represent, personify, epitomize, incorporate, include | 反义词：exclude, omit | 词性：动词
