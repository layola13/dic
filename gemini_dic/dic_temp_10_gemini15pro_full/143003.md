outbelched: /ˌaʊtˈbɛltʃt/
verb
大声地喷出; 比...更大声地喷出
to belch more loudly than someone or something else
The volcano outbelched a plume of ash and smoke. 火山喷出大量的火山灰和烟雾。

The dragon outbelched the other dragons. 这条龙比其他龙喷出的声音更大。
近义词: erupt, explode, spew
反义词: inhale, absorb
词性: verb
