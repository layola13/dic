manus:/ˈmɑːnʊs/| n.|手，手稿|A manuscript is a handwritten or typewritten document, especially a book, article, or piece of music. 手稿是手写或打字的文件，尤指书籍、文章或乐曲。|The author submitted the manuscript to the publisher. 作者将手稿提交给出版商。|Synonym: document, text, script|Antonym: print, published work|Noun
