clinchingly:/ˈklɪntʃɪŋli/
adverb
决定性地; 令人信服地
Serving as a clincher; decisively; conclusively.
Her testimony provided the clinchingly evidence needed to convict the defendant. 她的证词提供了给被告定罪所需的决定性证据。

The debate was clinchingly won by the challenger's well-researched arguments.  挑战者凭借充分的研究论据，最终赢得了辩论。

近义词:decisively, conclusively, definitively
反义词:indecisively, inconclusively, ambiguously
词性:adverb

词源:
From clinch + -ing + -ly.  Clinch itself comes from Middle English clenchen, from Old English *clencan, of uncertain origin.
