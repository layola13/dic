ectoplasmic:/ˌɛktəʊˈplæzmɪk/ | adj. | 外质的；灵魂的；幽灵似的 |  Relating to ectoplasm, a supernatural viscous substance supposedly exuded by mediums.  It can also be used figuratively to describe something ghostly or ethereal. | The medium claimed to produce ectoplasmic manifestations during the séance.  灵媒声称在降神会上产生了外质的表现。 |  The band's music had an ethereal, almost ectoplasmic quality. 乐队的音乐有一种空灵的，几乎是幽灵般的气质。 | ghostly, spectral, ethereal, phantom | material, corporeal | adjective
