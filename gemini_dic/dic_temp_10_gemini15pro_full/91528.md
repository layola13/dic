hyosternum:/ˌhaɪoʊˈstɜːrnəm/| n. |舌骨体|The middle part of the hyoid bone, also called the body of the hyoid. It provides attachment for muscles involved in swallowing and tongue movement.|The hyosternum is crucial for proper tongue function. 舌骨体对于正常的舌头功能至关重要。|Synonym: body of hyoid|Antonym: N/A|noun
