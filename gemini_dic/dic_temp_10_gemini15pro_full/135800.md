nonprolifically:/ˌnɒnprəˈlɪfɪkli/
adverb
非多产地，不丰富地
Not producing much fruit, offspring, or work; unproductively.
The artist, struggling with a creative block, worked nonprolifically for months.  这位艺术家与创作瓶颈作斗争，几个月来创作不多。
The drought caused the crops to grow nonprolifically this year.  干旱导致今年农作物产量不高。
Synonym: sparsely, sparingly, infrequently, rarely
Antonym: prolifically, abundantly, copiously
词性: 副词
