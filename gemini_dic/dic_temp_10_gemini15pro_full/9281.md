antiphilosophies: /ˌænti fɪˈlɒsəfiz/ /ˌænti fɪˈlɑːsəfiz/
| n. | 反哲学，非哲学 | Systems of thought or beliefs that oppose or reject the fundamental principles of philosophy, often by emphasizing practicality, skepticism, or religious dogma. |  Many religious fundamentalists hold antiphilosophies that prioritize faith over reason. 许多宗教原教旨主义者持有反哲学，将信仰置于理性之上。|  His antiphilosophies led him to reject any form of intellectual inquiry. 他的反哲学让他拒绝任何形式的知识探究。 | synonyms:  counter-philosophies,  non-philosophies | antonyms: philosophies | noun
