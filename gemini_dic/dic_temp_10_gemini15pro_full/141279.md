OPEC:/ˈoʊpɛk/ | n. | 石油输出国组织 | Organization of the Petroleum Exporting Countries.  An intergovernmental organization of oil-producing nations that coordinates and unifies the petroleum policies of its Member Countries. |  OPEC's decision to cut oil production sent prices soaring. 石油输出国组织决定减产导致油价飙升。 | synonym: cartel | antonym: consumer group | noun
