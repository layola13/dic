barnacle:/ˈbɑːrnəkəl/ (US) /ˈbɑːnəkl/ (UK)| n.|藤壶；难以摆脱的人或物|A barnacle is a small crustacean that attaches itself to rocks, ships' bottoms, etc. 藤壶是一种小型甲壳类动物，附着在岩石、船底等处。|The ship's hull was covered with barnacles. 船体上覆盖着藤壶。|Synonyms: shellfish, crustacean |Antonyms: N/A|noun
