overhearers:/ˌoʊvərˈhɪrərz/ | n. | 窃听者；无意中听到的人 | People who unintentionally or secretly listen to a conversation. |  I didn't realize there were overhearers in the room when I told my secret. 我不知道房间里有人在偷听我讲秘密。| synonyms: eavesdroppers, listeners | antonyms: speakers, talkers | noun

overhearers:/ˌoʊvərˈhɪrərz/ | n. | 无意听到谈话的人 | People who happen to hear a conversation they are not part of. |  The overhearers at the next table looked shocked at our conversation. 邻桌无意听到我们谈话的人看起来很震惊。 | synonyms: bystanders, onlookers | antonyms: participants, speakers | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "overhear" + "-ers" (suffix forming nouns of agency). "Overhear" comes from Old English "oferhieran," from "ofer-" (over) + "hieran" (to hear).
