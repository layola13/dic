namely:/ˈneɪmli/| adv.|也就是，即|Used to introduce a list of items that elaborates on something just mentioned.  It specifies or identifies something previously referred to in a more general way.|Several countries participated, namely France, Germany, and Italy.  几个国家参与了，即法国、德国和意大利。|Synonyms: that is, specifically, to wit, viz.|Antonyms: generally, broadly|adverb
