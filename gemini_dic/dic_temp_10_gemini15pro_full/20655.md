blackshirt:/ˈblækʃɜːrt/ /ˈblækʃɝːt/| n.| 黑衫党（成员）; (尤指20世纪20-40年代意大利或其他国家的)法西斯党员|A member of a fascist organization, especially (historical) a member of the Italian Fascist party in the period 1920–40, or a member of a similar organization in another country.|Mussolini's Blackshirts terrorized the population.  墨索里尼的黑衫党恐吓民众。|近义词: fascist, Nazi|反义词: anti-fascist, democrat|noun

blackshirt:/ˈblækʃɜːrt/ /ˈblækʃɝːt/| adj.| 黑衫党的|Relating to or characteristic of Blackshirts or fascism.|The blackshirt regime was brutal. 黑衫党政权是残酷的。|近义词: fascist|反义词: anti-fascist, democratic|adjective
