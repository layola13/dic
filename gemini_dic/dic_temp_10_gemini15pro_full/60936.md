elevates:/ˈɛlɪveɪts/| v.|提升，举起，提高；使情绪高昂；|The platform elevates the actors above the audience.  这个平台使演员高于观众。|近义词：raise, lift, boost, uplift, enhance|反义词:lower, depress, degrade|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *elevate*, from Latin *ēlevātus*, past participle of *ēlevāre* (“to lift up, raise”), from *ē-* (variant of *ex-*) + *levāre* (“to lift, lighten”), from *levis* (“light”).
