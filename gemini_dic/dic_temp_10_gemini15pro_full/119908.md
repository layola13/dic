militating:/ˈmɪlɪteɪtɪŋ/
verb
不利于，阻碍，产生不利影响
acting or having the effect of working against; opposing or hindering
Her poor health militated against her career. 她糟糕的健康状况阻碍了她的事业发展。
Synonyms: hindering, obstructing, deterring, working against, counteracting, prejudicing, damaging, harming, injuring
Antonyms: helping, aiding, assisting, supporting, promoting, furthering, advancing, benefiting
词性: 动词 (verb)
