did:/dɪd/| v.|做(do的过去式)|A form of the verb "do" used in the past tense. It can be used as an auxiliary verb or a main verb.|I did my homework yesterday. 我昨天做了我的家庭作业。|近义词:completed, performed, executed, carried out|反义词:failed, neglected|verb

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Do you like apples? 你喜欢苹果吗？
They do their homework every day. 他们每天都做作业。


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am doing my homework now. 我现在正在做我的作业。
She is doing the dishes. 她正在洗碗。


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have done my homework. 我已经完成了我的作业。
He has done a great job. 他干得棒极了。


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been doing my homework for two hours. 我已经做了两个小时的作业了。
They have been doing exercises all morning. 他们整个上午都在做运动。


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I did my homework last night. 我昨晚做了我的作业。
She did not go to school yesterday. 她昨天没去上学。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was doing my homework when you called. 你打电话时我正在做作业。
He was doing the dishes while she was cooking. 她做饭时他正在洗碗。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had done my homework before dinner. 晚饭前我已经完成了我的作业。
She had done everything she could. 她已经尽力了。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been doing my homework for two hours before you arrived. 你来之前我已经做了两个小时的作业了。
They had been doing research for months before they published their findings.  他们在发表研究结果之前已经做了几个月的研究。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will do my homework tomorrow. 我明天会做我的作业。
He will do his best. 他会尽力而为的。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be doing my homework this time tomorrow. 明天这个时候我会在做作业。
They will be doing research next year. 他们明年将会做研究。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I will have done my homework by tomorrow evening. 明天晚上之前我会完成我的作业。
He will have finished the project by next week. 他下周之前会完成这个项目。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
I will have been doing my homework for three hours by the time you arrive. 你来的时候我将已经做了三个小时的作业了。
They will have been working on this project for a year by next month. 到下个月，他们在这个项目上将已经工作了一年。


虚拟语气:
If I did my homework, I would get a better grade. 如果我做了作业，我会得到更好的成绩。
I wish I did my homework last night. 我希望我昨晚做了作业。



被动语态:
The work was done quickly. 这项工作很快就完成了。
The dishes were done by my brother. 碗是我弟弟洗的。



疑问句:
Did you do your homework? 你做作业了吗？
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？



否定句:
I did not do my homework. 我没做作业。
She didn't do anything wrong. 她什么也没做错。
