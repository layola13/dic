beyondness:/biˈjɒndnɪs/ |n.|超越性，超然性|The concept of beyondness encompasses a state or quality of being beyond the ordinary, known, or perceived limits. 超越性的概念包含了一种超越普通、已知或感知极限的状态或品质。|He spoke of a certain beyondness he experienced during meditation. 他谈到他在冥想中体验到的一种超越感。|近义词：transcendence, sublimity|反义词：immanence, mundanity|词性: 名词

词源:  From "beyond" + "-ness". "Beyond" comes from Old English *beġeondan (from be- "by" + ġeond "yonder").  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
