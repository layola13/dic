dispatching:/dɪˈspætʃɪŋ/
n.|派遣，调度，发送|The dispatching of troops to the border was swift. 部队迅速被派往边境。|近义词：sending, shipping, forwarding|反义词：receiving, collecting, withholding|词性:名词

dispatching:/dɪˈspætʃɪŋ/
adj.|迅速的，高效的|The dispatching officer handled the situation with remarkable speed. 调度员以惊人的速度处理了情况。|近义词：efficient, quick, rapid|反义词：slow, inefficient, sluggish|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French despescher "hasten, expedite," from des- "out, away" + pescher "to fish out, pull out." Originally meaning to get rid of something quickly.
