alluvium:/əˈluːviəm/ | n. | 冲积层；冲积土 |  Deposits of clay, silt, sand, and gravel left by flowing streams in a river valley or delta, typically producing fertile soil. | The fertile alluvium of the Nile delta has supported agriculture for millennia. 尼罗河三角洲肥沃的冲积土已经支持农业数千年了。 |  sediment, deposit, silt, | bedrock,  | noun

alluvial:/əˈluːviəl/ | adj. | 冲积的 | Relating to or derived from alluvium. | The alluvial plain is prone to flooding. 冲积平原容易发生洪水。| sedimentary, deposited |  | adjective
