obduct:/əbˈdʌkt/ | vt. | 推覆，逆掩 | To thrust or push over or against.  In geology, refers to the process where older rocks are thrust over younger rocks. | The oceanic plate was obducted onto the continental margin. 海洋板块被推覆到大陆边缘。| The mountain range was formed by the obduction of tectonic plates.  山脉是由构造板块的推覆形成的。 | 近义词：thrust, overthrust, shove, impose | 反义词：subduct | 动词

obduction:/əbˈdʌkʃən/ | n. | 推覆，逆掩 | The process of thrusting or pushing over or against, especially in geology where older rocks are thrust over younger rocks. | The obduction of the oceanic plate created a complex geological structure. 海洋板块的推覆作用形成了一个复杂的地质结构。 | The geologists studied the obduction zone to understand the mountain building process. 地质学家研究了推覆带以了解造山过程。| 近义词：thrusting, overthrusting | 反义词：subduction | 名词
