overbraced: /ˌoʊvərˈbreɪst/ | adj. | 过度支撑的，过度加固的 | Having too many braces or supports; excessively reinforced. | The bridge was overbraced, making it unnecessarily heavy and expensive. 这座桥过度加固了，使其不必要地笨重和昂贵。 |The building felt overbraced, with supports everywhere. 这栋建筑感觉过度支撑，到处都是支架。 | 近义词: oversupported, overreinforced | 反义词: underbraced, weakly supported | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "braced" (past participle of "brace," meaning "to support or strengthen").  The word "brace" ultimately comes from the Old French "brace" meaning "two arms."
