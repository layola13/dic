continually:/kənˈtɪnjuəli/
adv.
持续地，频繁地
happening repeatedly or at regular intervals over a long period of time
The phone was continually ringing. 电话铃声不断地响着。
近义词: constantly, frequently, repeatedly, incessantly
反义词: intermittently, occasionally, rarely, sporadically
词性: 副词

词源: From Late Latin *continualis, from Latin continuus (“continuous”).
