anthozoic:/ˌænθəˈzoʊɪk/
adjective
食花的；以花为食的
Feeding on flowers; flower-eating.
Certain insects are anthozoic, relying on flowers for their sustenance.  某些昆虫以花为食，依赖花朵维持生命。
Anthozoic insects play a vital role in pollination. 食花的昆虫在授粉中起着至关重要的作用。
近义词:nectarivorous, flower-feeding
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider herbivorous in a broader sense)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ánthos* (flower) + *zōikós* (of animals, pertaining to animals).
