chafer:/ˈtʃeɪfər/ (US), /ˈtʃeɪfə(r)/ (UK)| n. |金龟子；兜虫|A beetle of the Scarabaeidae family, typically having shiny wing cases and clubbed antennae.  Many chafers are plant pests.|The rose chafers were devastating her prize-winning roses. 玫瑰金龟子正在摧毁她获奖的玫瑰花。|近义词：beetle, scarab |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ċeafor, from Proto-Germanic *kafuzą, of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to the verb "to chafe" (rub).
