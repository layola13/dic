imposed: /ɪmˈpoʊzd/ (US) /ɪmˈpəʊzd/ (UK)| adj. |强加的，施加的，征收的|Forced or applied from the outside; not chosen by the person or thing affected. |例句：The government imposed a new tax on imported goods. 政府对进口商品征收了新的税。|例句：She imposed her will on the rest of the group. 她把自己的意愿强加于小组的其他成员。|近义词：forced, levied, inflicted, prescribed, dictated|反义词：optional, voluntary, elective|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *impositus*, past participle of imponere "to put on, place in, insert," from Latin in- "in" + ponere "to put, place."
