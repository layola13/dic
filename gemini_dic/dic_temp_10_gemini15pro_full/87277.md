hermaphroditish:/hɜːrˌmæfrəˈdɪtɪʃ/ | adj. | 雌雄同体的，阴阳体的 | Having both male and female reproductive organs. |  The flowers of this plant are hermaphroditish, containing both stamens and pistils.  这种植物的花是雌雄同体的，既有雄蕊也有雌蕊。 | The snail is a hermaphroditish creature. 这只蜗牛是雌雄同体的生物。 | Synonyms: androgynous, bisexual, monoecious | Antonyms: unisexual, dioecious | adjective
