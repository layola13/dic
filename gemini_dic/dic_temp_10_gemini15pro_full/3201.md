affectation:/ˌæfɛkˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 做作，矫揉造作;  装腔作势; 假装 | An affectation is behavior, speech, or writing that is artificial and designed to impress. | She spoke with a ridiculous affectation of a French accent. 她说话时故意装腔作势地模仿法国口音。| synonyms: pretension, artificiality, mannerism, pose, airs | antonyms: naturalness, genuineness, sincerity | noun

affectation:/ˌæfɛkˈteɪʃən/ | n. |  (尤指对衣着、举止等的) 过分讲究，刻意追求 |  An affectation can also refer to an exaggerated display or fondness for something, often in clothing or manners. | His love of opera was just an affectation. 他对歌剧的热爱只是一种装腔作势。 | synonyms:  pretentiousness, ostentation | antonyms:  simplicity, modesty | noun
