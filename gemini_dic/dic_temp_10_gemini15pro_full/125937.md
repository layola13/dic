mushroomer:/ˈmʌʃˌrumər/ (US), /ˈmʌʃˌruːmər/ (UK)| n.| 采蘑菇的人；迅速发展的事物|A person who gathers mushrooms, especially as a hobby or for food; something that grows or expands rapidly.|例句：He's an avid mushroomer and spends his weekends foraging in the woods. 他是一个狂热的采蘑菇爱好者，周末都在树林里觅食。

例句：The town became a mushroomer of new businesses after the highway was built.  高速公路建成后，这个小镇迅速发展，涌现出许多新企业。|近义词：forager, collector (for people); booming, burgeoning, expanding (for things)|反义词:shrinking, declining (for things)|词性:noun
