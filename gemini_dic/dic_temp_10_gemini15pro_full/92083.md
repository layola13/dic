hyperreactivity:/ˌhaɪpərˌriːækˈtɪvɪti/ | n. | 过度反应，过敏反应 |  The state of being excessively reactive or sensitive to stimuli.  |  The child's hyperreactivity to certain foods made it difficult to find things he could eat.  这个孩子对某些食物的过度反应使得很难找到他能吃的东西。 | hypersensitivity, oversensitivity | hyposensitivity, unresponsiveness | noun

hyperreactivity:/ˌhaɪpərˌriːækˈtɪvɪti/ | n. | 过敏反应，高度反应性 |  The tendency to react excessively or with greater intensity than normal to stimuli. | His hyperreactivity to noise made city life unbearable. 他对噪音的过度反应让他无法忍受城市生活。 | hypersensitivity, excitability |  inertness, unresponsiveness | noun
