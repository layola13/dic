biparietal: /baɪpəˈraɪɪtəl/ | adj. | 双顶的；两顶骨间的 | Relating to the two parietal bones, or the parietal eminences, of the skull.  |  The biparietal diameter is a measurement of the fetal head obtained by ultrasound.  双顶径是通过超声波获得的胎儿头部测量值。 |  Synonyms: transparietal | Antonyms: N/A | adjective

biparietal: /baɪpəˈraɪɪtəl/ | n. | 双顶径 | The distance between the two parietal eminences of the skull. | The doctor measured the baby's biparietal diameter during the ultrasound. 医生在超声波检查中测量了婴儿的双顶径。 | Synonyms: BPD | Antonyms: N/A | noun
