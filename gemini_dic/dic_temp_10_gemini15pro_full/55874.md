divisibleness:/dɪˌvɪzəˈbɪlnəs/ | n. |可分性；可除性| The quality of being divisible; the capacity of being divided without a remainder. |The divisibility of 12 by 3 is clear. 12可以被3整除是显而易见的。| 近义词: divisibility | 反义词: indivisibility | 词性: noun

Etymology: From divisible + -ness.  Divisible comes from Late Middle English: from late Latin divisibilis, from dividere ‘to divide’.
