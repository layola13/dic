nepenthe:/ˈnɛpənθiː/
n.
忘忧药; 解忧物;
A drug or drink that makes people forget their sorrows or troubles.
例句：She sought nepenthe in the bottle to ease the pain of her broken heart. 她借酒消愁，以减轻心碎之痛。

例句：The music acted as a nepenthe, allowing him to escape his worries for a while. 音乐像一剂忘忧药，让他暂时摆脱了烦恼。

近义词：oblivion, solace, relief
反义词：misery, sorrow, pain
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek *nēpenthēs* (νηπενθής), from *nē-* (νη-) meaning "not" + *penthos* (πένθος) meaning "grief, sorrow".
