mammalians:/məˈmeɪliənz/
noun
哺乳动物
Animals that are warm-blooded, have hair or fur, and feed their young with milk produced by mammary glands.
例句：Mammals, including humans, are a diverse group of animals. 哺乳动物，包括人类在内，是一个种类繁多的动物群体。

例句：Whales and dolphins are marine mammals.  鲸鱼和海豚是海洋哺乳动物。

近义词：mammals (no direct single-word synonym, but phrases like "mammalian species" can be used)
反义词：(no direct antonym, but one could use "non-mammalian vertebrates" or "invertebrates")
词性:noun

mammal:/ˈmæməl/
noun
哺乳动物
An animal that is warm-blooded, has hair or fur, and feeds its young with milk produced by mammary glands.
例句：The blue whale is the largest mammal on Earth. 蓝鲸是地球上最大的哺乳动物。

例句：Bats are the only mammals capable of true flight.  蝙蝠是唯一能够真正飞行的哺乳动物。
