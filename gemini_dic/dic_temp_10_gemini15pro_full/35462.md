coachable:/ˈkoʊtʃəbl/| adj.|可指导的，可训练的|Easy to coach or train; receptive to guidance or instruction.  Someone who is coachable is willing to learn and improve.  |Example 1: He is a very coachable athlete and always listens to his coach. 他是一个非常容易指导的运动员，总是听教练的话。

Example 2:  She's a coachable employee, eager to learn new skills. 她是一位可教的员工，渴望学习新技能。|近义词：trainable, receptive, teachable, malleable, guidable|反义词：uncoachable, intractable, stubborn, resistant, obstinate|词性:形容词
