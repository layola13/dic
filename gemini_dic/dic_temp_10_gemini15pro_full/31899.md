cheesemonger: /ˈtʃiːzmʌŋɡər/ | n. | 奶酪商，干酪商 | A person who sells cheese. |  例句：The cheesemonger recommended a sharp cheddar.  奶酪商推荐了一种浓郁的切达干酪。

例句：I asked the cheesemonger for advice on pairing cheese with wine. 我向奶酪商咨询了奶酪和葡萄酒的搭配建议。

近义词：cheese seller, cheese merchant | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
