beelzebubs: /biːˈɛlzɪbʌbz/ | n. |别西卜（魔王名）；苍蝇之王|Beelzebub, a name derived from a Philistine god, is a powerful demon in Christian demonology, often associated with Satan and depicted as the "lord of the flies".  It is sometimes used figuratively to refer to a very wicked or powerful person.|The preacher railed against Beelzebub and his influence on the world.  牧师痛斥别西卜及其对世界的影响。

The superstitious villagers blamed Beelzebub for the recent misfortunes. 迷信的村民们把最近的不幸归咎于别西卜。|近义词：Satan, devil, Lucifer|反义词: God, angel|词性: noun
