incomprehensibilities: /ɪnˌkɒmprɪˌhensɪˈbɪlɪtiz/ | n. | 不可理解性；费解的事物 |  The state or quality of being impossible or difficult to understand.  | The sheer incomprehensibilities of quantum physics can be daunting. 量子物理的绝对不可理解性可能令人望而生畏。 | The incomprehensibilities of his behavior baffled everyone. 他行为的费解之处让每个人都感到困惑。 | 近义词: complexities, intricacies, obscurities, enigmas | 反义词: clarities, simplicities, transparencies | 词性: 名词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French incomprehensible (from in- ‘not’ + comprehensible ) + -ity.
