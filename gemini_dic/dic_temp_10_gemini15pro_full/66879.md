expectorations: /ɪkˌspɛktəˈreɪʃənz/ /ɪkˌspɛktəˈreɪʃnz/ | n. |咳出物；痰；（引申）排泄物；分泌物|  Matter coughed up from the respiratory tract, typically containing mucus, phlegm, or other substances. It can also refer to the act of coughing up such matter. | The doctor examined the patient's expectorations for signs of infection. 医生检查了病人的痰液，寻找感染的迹象。| synonyms: sputum, phlegm, mucus, discharge | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider "inhalation," "inspiration") | noun
