inalienablenesses:/ɪnˈeɪlɪənəbl̩nəsɪz/ | n. | 不可剥夺性；不可分割性 | The state or quality of being inalienable; something that is inalienable.  This refers to rights or qualities that cannot be taken away or given up. |  These inalienablenesses are central to our understanding of human rights. 这些不可剥夺性是我们理解人权的核心。The declaration stressed the inalienablenesses of life, liberty, and the pursuit of happiness.  宣言强调了生命、自由和追求幸福的不可剥夺性。 | synonyms:  inviolability, unassailability,  indispensaility | antonyms: transferability, dispensability | noun
