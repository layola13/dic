bayou: /ˈbaɪ.uː/ (US), /ˈbaɪ.uː/ (UK)
n.
河口, 沼泽, 支流
A marshy outlet of a lake or river. A slow-moving creek or a swampy section of a river or a lake, typically found in the southeastern United States.
The alligator slipped silently into the murky bayou.  短吻鳄悄悄地滑进了黑暗的河口。
The cajun music drifted across the bayou.  卡津音乐飘过河口。

近义词: creek, swamp, marsh, slough
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider opposites like 'mountain' or 'desert')
词性: noun
