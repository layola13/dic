decertation: /ˌdiːsərˈteɪʃən/
n.
论文；专题讨论；学术演讲
A long piece of writing on a particular subject, especially one that is done for a higher university degree.
例句：She is writing her dissertation on the history of feminist literature. 她正在写关于女性主义文学史的论文。

例句：He gave a brilliant dissertation on the philosophy of Kant. 他就康德哲学发表了一篇精彩的学术演讲。

近义词：thesis, essay, treatise, monograph, study
反义词: None (No direct antonym for a piece of academic writing)
词性: Noun
