mistunes:/ˌmɪsˈtjuːnz/ | n. vt.| 失调，走调，不和谐 |A state of being out of tune; a discordant sound.  The act of putting something out of tune. |The piano mistunes easily in this humid weather.  这架钢琴在这种潮湿的天气里很容易走调。 |He mistuned the guitar on purpose for a special effect. 他故意把吉他弄走调，以达到一种特殊效果。|近义词：discord, dissonance, cacophony |反义词: harmony, tune, concord|词性:名词, 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + tune.
