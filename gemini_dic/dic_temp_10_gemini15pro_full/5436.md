almonership: /ˈɑːməˌnərʃɪp/ | n. |施舍，施舍者的职位；施舍物|The act of distributing alms, or the position of someone who distributes alms, especially as a charitable duty.  It can also refer to the alms themselves.|The king's almonership provided food and clothing to the poor. 国王的施舍为穷人提供了食物和衣服。|Synonyms: charity, philanthropy, benefaction|Antonyms: stinginess, miserliness|noun
