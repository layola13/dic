aftercrop:/ˈæftərkrɒp/ (US) /ˈɑːftərkrɒp/ (UK)| n.|后续作物，再生作物|A second crop grown on the same land in the same year after the first crop has been harvested.|The farmer planted an aftercrop of turnips after harvesting the wheat.|Synonyms: catch crop, second crop|Antonyms: main crop|noun
