maybes:/ˈmeɪbiz/| n. |可能的事情；不确定的事情|Something that is uncertain or only a possibility. |There are a few maybes on the guest list.  宾客名单上有一些待定的人。| There are several maybes we need to consider. 我们需要考虑几个不确定因素。 |近义词：possibilities, uncertainties|反义词: certainties|词性:名词

词源:  From "maybe" (adverb & adjective), with the plural "-s" added to indicate multiple possibilities.  "Maybe" itself originates from "it may be."
