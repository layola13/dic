habiture:/ˈhæbɪtʃər/
n.
habitual residence; dwelling place
The place where someone lives; a dwelling.
He established his habiture in a small village nestled in the mountains.  他在一个依偎在山里的小村庄里安了家。
dwelling, residence, abode, domicile, home
nomadic, itinerant
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French habiture, from Latin habitūra (“dwelling”), from habitō (“I dwell, inhabit”), frequentative of habeō (“I have, hold”).
