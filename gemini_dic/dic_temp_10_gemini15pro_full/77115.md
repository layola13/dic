garth:/ɡɑːrθ/| n.|院子；围场；庭院；（尤指）庄园内的围地|An enclosed area, especially one beside a house or within the walls of a castle; a yard or courtyard.|例句：The children played in the garth. 孩子们在院子里玩耍。

例句：The king held court in the castle garth. 国王在城堡庭院里举行朝会。|近义词：yard, courtyard, enclosure, close|反义词:open field|词性:noun
