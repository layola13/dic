ochrey:/ˈoʊkri/| adj.|赭色的，赭石色的|Of a light brownish-yellow color, like ochre.|The walls were painted an ochrey yellow.|近义词：yellowish-brown, ochreous, buff|反义词:blue, green|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From ochre + -y.  Ochre comes from the Old French ocre, ultimately from the Greek ōkhra, meaning "pale yellow."
