bluntest:/ˈblʌntɪst/
adjective
最钝的；最直率的
Superlative form of blunt: most blunt.  Having a dull edge or point; not sharp.  Speaking frankly and directly, even if it seems rude or abrupt.
The bluntest knife in the drawer couldn't cut the butter.  抽屉里最钝的刀也切不开黄油。

His bluntest comment was that the project was a complete waste of time. 他最直率的评论是，这个项目完全是浪费时间。
synonym: dullest, frankest, most direct
antonym: sharpest, most subtle, most diplomatic
词性: 形容词


blunt:/blʌnt/
adjective, verb
钝的；直率的；使变钝
Having a dull edge or point; not sharp.  Speaking frankly and directly, even if it seems rude or abrupt.  To make something less sharp.
The knife was too blunt to cut the vegetables. 这把刀太钝了，切不了菜。

Her blunt words offended some people. 她直言不讳的话冒犯了一些人。

The impact blunted the tip of the spear. 撞击使矛尖变钝了。
synonym: dull, frank, direct (adj); weaken, soften (verb)
antonym: sharp, subtle, diplomatic (adj); sharpen (verb)
词性: 形容词, 动词
