amylate: /ˈæmɪleɪt/ | n. v. | 淀粉酶; 使淀粉化 |  An enzyme that hydrolyzes starch to sugar.  A salt or ester of amylic alcohol. To treat or convert into starch. |  The enzyme amylase breaks down starches into sugars. 淀粉酶将淀粉分解成糖。 |  The chemist amylated the compound.  化学家使该化合物淀粉化。 | 近义词: diastase | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps inhibitor) | 词性: noun, verb


amyl: /ˈæmɪl/ | n. adj. | 戊基 | The univalent radical, C5H11, of amyl alcohol. Pertaining to or derived from amyl alcohol.  | Amyl acetate is a common solvent. 乙酸戊酯是一种常见的溶剂。 |  Amyl alcohol has a distinct odor. 戊醇有一种独特的气味。 | 近义词: pentyl | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun, adjective
