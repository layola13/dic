bouquetin:/ˌbuːkɪˈtɛ̃/ | n. | 野山羊（尤指阿尔卑斯野山羊） | A wild goat, especially the ibex of the Alps. |  例句：The bouquetin nimbly scaled the steep cliffs. 野山羊灵活地攀登陡峭的悬崖。

例句：We spotted a bouquetin perched high on a rocky ledge. 我们发现一只野山羊栖息在岩石峭壁上。

近义词：ibex, wild goat | 反义词: (无直接反义词) | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *bouquetin*, from Franco-Provençal *bouquetin*, from Vulgar Latin *bucētinus* "wild goat," diminutive of Latin *bucca* "cheek, mouth" (perhaps in reference to the animal's thick cheeks or jutting lower jaw).
