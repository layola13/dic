guaniferous:/ˌɡwɑːˈnɪfərəs/
adjective
含鸟粪的；产鸟粪的
Containing or yielding guano.
The guaniferous islands are rich in phosphates. 这些产鸟粪的岛屿富含磷酸盐。
The expedition explored the guaniferous caves. 探险队探索了盛产鸟粪的洞穴。
synonyms: guano-bearing, guano-producing, guano-rich
antonyms: barren, infertile
