deuced:/ˈdjuːsɪd/ /ˈduːsɪd/| adj. adv.|非常，极其；讨厌的，该死的|Used for emphasis, especially to express annoyance.  Originates as a euphemism for "devilish."|It's deucedly cold today. 今天非常冷。|Near synonyms: extremely, awfully, terribly, very, damned|Near antonyms: slightly, mildly|Adjective/Adverb
