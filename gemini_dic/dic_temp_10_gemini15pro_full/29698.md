cathodically:/kəˈθɒdɪkli/ | adv. | 阴极地; 以阴极方式 |  In electrolysis or electroplating, something done cathodically occurs at or relating to the cathode, the negatively charged electrode. | The metal was cathodically protected from corrosion.  金属被阴极保护以防止腐蚀。| Synonyms:  electrolytically (at the cathode), negatively (in terms of charge) | Antonyms: anodically | 词性: 副词
