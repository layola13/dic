despair-of:/dɪˈspɛər əv/
verb phrase
放弃希望；对…感到绝望
to lose all hope that something will happen or that someone will improve
例句：
They began to despair of ever finding their dog.
他们开始对找到他们的狗不抱希望了。

例句：
The doctors despaired of saving his life.
医生们对他能否活下来感到绝望。

近义词：give up on, lose hope in
反义词：hope for, have faith in
词性：动词短语
