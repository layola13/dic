nonglossiest:/nɒnˈɡlɒsiɪst/
adjective
最不光滑的
Having the least gloss; most matte.  Lacking a shiny or polished surface.
The nonglossiest paper is best for sketching with charcoal.  最不光滑的纸最适合用木炭素描。
Near Synonyms:  matte, dull, flat
Antonyms: glossy, shiny, polished, lustrous
词性: adjective
