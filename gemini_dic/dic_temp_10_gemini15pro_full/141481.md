opinionator:/əˈpɪnjəneɪtər/| n. |评论员，持强硬意见的人|A person who expresses strong opinions, often in public.  Someone who is very vocal about their beliefs, sometimes to the point of being overbearing. |例句：He's a well-known opinionator on political matters. 他是一位著名的政治评论员。

例句：She's become quite the opinionator on social media.  她在社交媒体上成了一个相当爱发表意见的人。|近义词：pundit, commentator, columnist, critic, advocate|反义词: listener, follower |noun
