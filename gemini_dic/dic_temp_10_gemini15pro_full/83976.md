half-crazy:/ˈhæfˈkreɪzi/| adj.|半疯的，有点疯狂的|Somewhat insane; not entirely sane but exhibiting eccentric or irrational behavior.|He had a half-crazy look in his eyes after staying up all night.  他熬了一整夜后，眼神看起来有点疯狂。|She has some half-crazy ideas about starting a business with no money. 她有一些疯狂的想法，想在没有钱的情况下创业。|近义词：eccentric, deranged, unhinged, unbalanced|反义词：sane, rational, balanced|词性:形容词
