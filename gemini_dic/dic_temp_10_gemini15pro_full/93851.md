immobility:/ˌɪməˈbɪləti/
n.|不动性，静止，固定|The immobility of the patient was a concern for the doctors. 病人的不动让医生很担心。|
例句：His immobility after the accident was a major setback in his recovery. 事故后他的无法行动是他康复的主要障碍。
例句：The immobility of the statue contrasted sharply with the bustling city around it. 雕像的静止与周围熙熙攘攘的城市形成鲜明对比。
近义词：inaction, stillness, paralysis
反义词：mobility, movement, activity
词性:名词

Etymology: From Late Latin *immobilitas, from Latin immobilis ("immovable"), from in- ("not") + mobilis ("movable").  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *meue- ("to move, to push away").
