flautists:/ˈflɔːtɪsts/ /ˈflaʊtɪsts/
n.
长笛演奏家，笛手
A person who plays the flute.
The orchestra has three flautists.  这支管弦乐队有三名长笛演奏家。
The flautists played a beautiful melody. 长笛演奏家们演奏了一段优美的旋律。
synonyms: flute player, flutist
antonyms: (No direct antonym for a person playing an instrument)
