nonpatented: /ˌnɒnˈpeɪtəntɪd/ /ˌnɑːnˈpeɪtntɪd/
adjective
未申请专利的，未取得专利的
Not patented; not protected by a patent.
The company decided to market the nonpatented technology.  这家公司决定将未申请专利的技术推向市场。
Generic drugs are often nonpatented versions of brand-name drugs.  非专利药通常是品牌药的未申请专利版本。
近义词: unpatented, generic
反义词: patented
词性: adjective
