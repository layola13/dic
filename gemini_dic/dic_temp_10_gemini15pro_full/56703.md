doominess:/ˈduːminəs/| n.|厄运; 沉闷; 阴郁|The doominess of the situation was palpable.  情况的黯淡显而易见。|例句：A sense of doominess pervaded the room. 一种厄运感弥漫了整个房间。  The film's doominess reflected the director's pessimistic worldview.  这部电影的阴郁反映了导演悲观的世界观。|近义词：gloom, pessimism, bleakness, despair, despondency|反义词：optimism, cheerfulness, hopefulness|词性:名词
Etymology: From *doom* (n.) + *-iness*, a suffix forming abstract nouns from adjectives, expressing state or quality. *Doom* comes from Old English *dōm* "judgment, statute, decree, destiny," from Proto-Germanic *dōmaz* "judgment," related to *dēmi* "judge."
