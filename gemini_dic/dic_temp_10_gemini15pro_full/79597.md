glorifiable:/ˈɡlɔːrɪfaɪəbl/ |adj.| 可荣耀的，可颂扬的 |Something that is glorifiable is worthy of being glorified or praised.  |Her selfless dedication to helping others is a glorifiable act. 她无私奉献帮助他人的行为是值得颂扬的。

The artist's skill in capturing the beauty of nature is truly glorifiable.  这位艺术家捕捉自然之美的技巧 truly 值得称颂。| 近义词: praiseworthy, commendable, laudable, admirable | 反义词:  inglorious, shameful, disgraceful | 词性: adjective


词源:

From "glorify" + "-able".  "Glorify" comes from the Late Latin "glorificare," from Latin "gloria" (glory) + "-ficare" (making, doing).  "-able" is a suffix that forms adjectives meaning "capable of being" or "worthy of being."
