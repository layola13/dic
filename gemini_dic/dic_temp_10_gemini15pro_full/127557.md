necroscopic:/ˌnɛkrəˈskɒpɪk/ /ˌnɛkrəˈskɑːpɪk/| adj.|尸检的，关于尸体解剖的|Relating to or characteristic of a necropsy; obtained or revealed by a necropsy.|The necroroscopic findings confirmed the initial diagnosis. 尸检结果证实了初步诊断。|Synonyms: postmortem, autoptic|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider "ante-mortem" or "pre-mortem" as contrasting terms)|adjective
