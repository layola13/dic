paper-pushing: /ˈpeɪpərˌpʊʃɪŋ/
adjective
文书工作的； paperwork
Relating to or involving routine office work, especially that which is perceived as bureaucratic and unproductive.
He complained about the paper-pushing aspects of his job. 他抱怨他工作中文书工作的部分。
近义词: bureaucratic, administrative, clerical
反义词: hands-on, practical
