attentional:/əˈtɛnʃənəl/| adj.|注意的，注意力的|Relating to or involving attention; characterized by attention.  It describes something related to the cognitive process of selectively focusing on one aspect of the environment while ignoring other things.|例句：The attentional demands of the task were very high. 这项任务对注意力的要求非常高。|近义词：attentive, observant, focused, heedful|反义词：inattentive, unobservant, distracted, heedless|词性:adjective
