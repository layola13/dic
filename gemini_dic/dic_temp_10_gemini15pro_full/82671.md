guesstimate:/ˈɡɛstɪmət/| n. v. |估计；估测|An estimate based on a combination of guesswork and calculation.  A rough calculation based on incomplete information.|  We can only guesstimate how long it will take.|近义词：estimate, approximation, rough calculation, conjecture, surmise|反义词: precise calculation, accurate measurement|词性:名词,动词

Etymology:
guesstimate (v.) 1934, American English, from guess (v.) + estimate (v.). As a noun from 1936.
