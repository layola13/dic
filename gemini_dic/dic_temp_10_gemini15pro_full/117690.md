melaconite:/məˈlækəˌnaɪt/| n. |黑铜矿|A black mineral consisting of copper(II) oxide, CuO.  It is a secondary mineral formed by the weathering of other copper ores.|Melaconite is a valuable ore of copper. 黑铜矿是一种有价值的铜矿石。|The geologist identified the black mineral as melaconite. 地质学家将这种黑色矿物鉴定为黑铜矿。|Synonyms: tenorite (a more common synonym), cupric oxide|Antonyms: N/A (Minerals typically don't have direct antonyms)|noun
