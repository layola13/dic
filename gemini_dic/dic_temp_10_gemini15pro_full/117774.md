melds:/meldz/
| v. (third-person singular simple present melds, present participle melding, simple past and past participle melded) | 混合，融合 | To blend or merge; to combine or unite. | The flavors of the different spices melded together beautifully. 各种香料的味道完美地融合在一起。|
|
|近义词：blend, merge, combine, fuse, unite|
|
|反义词：separate, divide, disconnect|
|词性：动词|


meld:/meld/
| n. | 混合物，融合 | A blend or mixture. | The final product was a meld of different styles and influences.  最终产品是不同风格和影响的融合。|
|
|近义词：blend, mixture, combination, fusion|
|
|反义词：separation|
|词性：名词|


词源: From Middle English *melden, from Old English meltan (“to melt”), of Germanic origin. Related to Dutch smelten and German schmelzen.  The sense of "blend, merge" arose from the image of melting things together.
