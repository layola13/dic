nitrifier:/ˈnaɪtrɪfaɪər/
n.|硝化菌|An organism, especially a bacterium, that converts ammonia into nitrites or nitrates.|Nitrifiers play a crucial role in the nitrogen cycle. 硝化菌在氮循环中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词:nitrogen-fixing bacteria|反义词:denitrifier|词性:noun

Etymology:
From nitrify + -er.  Nitrify comes from  nitr-  "nitrogen" + -ify.
