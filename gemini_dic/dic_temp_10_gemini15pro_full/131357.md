nonchromatic:/ˌnɒnkrəˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 非彩色的，消色的 | Not chromatic; having no hue; achromatic.  Lacking color; not of or pertaining to color. | The artist used a nonchromatic palette of grays and blacks for the charcoal drawing.  这位艺术家用灰色和黑色的非彩色调色板来画木炭画。|  The scientist studied the nonchromatic properties of light.  这位科学家研究了光的非彩色特性。| 近义词: achromatic, colorless, grayscale | 反义词: chromatic, colorful | 词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non "not."
chromatic (adjective): From Greek chrōmatikos "relating to color," from chrōma "color."
