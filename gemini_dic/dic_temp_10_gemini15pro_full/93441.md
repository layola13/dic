illuminatism:/ɪˌluːmɪˈnɪzəm/ | n. |启蒙主义，光照派 |A philosophical movement of the 18th century that emphasized reason and individualism rather than tradition. It advocated the use of reason to examine accepted doctrines and traditions and to reform society based on rational principles. | The French Revolution was heavily influenced by the ideals of illuminatism. 法国大革命深受启蒙主义思想的影响。|The spread of illuminatism led to significant social and political changes. 启蒙主义的传播导致了重大的社会和政治变革。| 近义词: enlightenment, rationalism | 反义词: obscurantism, mysticism | 词性: noun

Etymology: From French *illuminisme*, from *illuminer* "to illuminate," from Latin *illuminare*, from *in-* "in" + *lumen* "light."
