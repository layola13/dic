exarch:/ˈeksɑːrk/ | n. | 外主教；总督；地方行政长官 | A bishop or ruler of a province or other major region in the early Christian church, especially in the Eastern Church, or a secular official with similar responsibilities. | The exarch of Ravenna represented the Byzantine emperor in Italy. 拉文纳的外主教代表拜占庭皇帝在意大利。 | The exarch levied taxes and administered justice. 外主教征税并执行司法。 | synonyms: governor, viceroy, prefect, deputy, legate | antonyms: subject, subordinate | noun
