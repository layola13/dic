eador:/eɪˈdɔːr/ | n. | 崇拜者；爱慕者 | A person who adores someone or something. | She is a passionate eador of the arts. 她是一位充满热情地艺术爱好者。 |  He is a devoted eador of his wife. 他是一位深爱妻子的丈夫。 | 近义词: admirer, devotee, fan, worshipper | 反义词: critic, detractor | 词性: 名词

词源:  From the Late Latin *adorator, from Latin adorare "to adore," from ad- "to" + orare "to pray, speak, beseech."
