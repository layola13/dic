auditorships:/ˈɔːdɪtərʃɪps/
n.
审计职务；审计员的工作；审计员的资格
The position or responsibilities of an auditor.  The qualification of being an auditor.
Our firm has secured several new auditorships this year. 今年我们公司获得了几个新的审计项目。
synonyms: audit engagements, audit assignments, audit practices
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider: client relationships, business management)
noun
