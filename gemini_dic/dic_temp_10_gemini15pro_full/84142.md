half-naked:/ˌhɑːf ˈneɪkɪd/ | adj. | 半裸的，衣衫单薄的 | Partially clothed, wearing very little clothing. |  The children were playing in the garden half-naked. 孩子们衣衫单薄地在花园里玩耍。

The half-naked man ran out of the burning building.  那个半裸的男人从着火的建筑物里跑了出来。

| 近义词：scantily clad, partially clothed, underdressed | 反义词：fully clothed, dressed | 词性：形容词

half:/hæf/ | n. adj. adv.| 一半，半个 | One of two equal or approximately equal parts of a divisible whole. | I ate half the pizza. 我吃了一半的披萨。

She gave me half of her sandwich. 她给了我一半的三明治。

| 近义词： moiety, one-half | 反义词： whole, double | 词性：名词，形容词，副词

naked:/ˈneɪkɪd/ | adj. | 裸体的，赤裸的 | Without clothes or covering. |  The baby was completely naked. 婴儿一丝不挂。

They swam in the lake naked. 他们在湖里裸泳。

| 近义词： nude, bare, unclothed | 反义词： clothed, dressed | 词性：形容词


词源:
"half" comes from Old English "healf," related to Dutch "half," German "halb," from Proto-Germanic *halbaz.
"naked" comes from Old English "nacod," from Proto-Germanic *nakwaz.  The prefix "half-" simply combines the meaning of "half" with the adjective "naked."
