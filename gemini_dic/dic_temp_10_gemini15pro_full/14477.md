avar:/ˈævər/| n.|贪婪的人; 守财奴|A person who is excessively greedy for wealth or possessions. | He was known as a miser, an avar, hoarding his wealth and refusing to share it. 他被称为守财奴，一个贪婪的人，囤积财富，拒绝分享。|近义词: miser, skinflint, tightwad, hoarder|反义词: philanthropist, altruist|Noun
