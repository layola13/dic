ghostship:/ˈɡoʊstʃɪp/| n. |鬼船，幽灵船|A ship with no living crew aboard, often seen as a harbinger of doom or a metaphor for something lost or aimless. |The Mary Celeste became a famous ghostship after being found adrift and deserted.|Synonyms: derelict, phantom ship|Antonyms: crewed ship|noun
