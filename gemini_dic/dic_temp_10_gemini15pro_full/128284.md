neuropsychology:/ˌnʊəroʊsaɪˈkɑːlədʒi/ /ˌnjʊərəʊsaɪˈkɒlədʒi/
n.|神经心理学|The study of the relationship between brain function and behavior.  It explores how brain structures and processes influence cognitive abilities, emotions, and behavior.|Neuropsychology plays a crucial role in understanding the effects of brain injury. 神经心理学在理解脑损伤的影响方面起着至关重要的作用。|cognitive psychology, behavioral neuroscience|N/A|noun
Etymology->
From neuro- + psychology.  "Neuro-" comes from the Greek "neuron" meaning "nerve" and "psychology" comes from Greek "psyche" meaning "soul" or "mind" and "-logia" meaning "study".
