mimosa:/mɪˈmoʊzə/ /mɪˈməʊzə/| n.|含羞草；含羞草属植物；含羞草鸡尾酒|A tropical American tree or shrub with small pink or white ball-shaped flowers.  A cocktail made with champagne and orange juice.|例句：The mimosa tree is known for its delicate leaves that close when touched. 含羞草以其娇嫩的叶子而闻名，触摸时会闭合。

例句：She enjoyed a mimosa at brunch. 她在早午餐时享用了一杯含羞草鸡尾酒。|近义词：sensitive plant, shameplant (for the plant); Bellini (for the cocktail)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
