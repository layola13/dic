nonoccupant:/ˌnɒnˈɒkjʊpənt/ /ˌnɑːnˈɑːkjəpənt/
noun|非居住者；非占用者|a person who does not occupy a particular place, especially a house or other building|The nonoccupant owner is responsible for property taxes.|近义词:absentee, nonresident|反义词:occupant, resident|n.
