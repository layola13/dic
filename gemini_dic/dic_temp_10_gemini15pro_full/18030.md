begnaw:/bɪˈnɔː/
verb
啃，咬，折磨
to bite or chew on persistently; to worry or torment
The dog begnawed the bone. 狗啃着骨头。
Anxiety begnawed at her. 焦虑折磨着她。
近义词:gnaw, nibble, erode, corrode
反义词:soothe, comfort
词性:v.
