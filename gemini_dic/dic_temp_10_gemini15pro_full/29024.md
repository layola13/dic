cartoned:/ˈkɑːrtənd/| adj.|装在纸盒里的，盒装的|Packaged in a carton.  The milk is sold cartoned for convenience. 牛奶为了方便起见，以盒装出售。|例句：The eggs were cartoned and ready for shipping. 鸡蛋已装箱，准备发货。The cartoned juice is easier to store. 盒装果汁更容易储存。|近义词：boxed, packaged|反义词: unpackaged, loose|词性:形容词
carton:/ˈkɑːrtn/| n.|纸盒，纸箱|A box or container usually made of cardboard, for holding goods.  |词源：From French carton, from Italian cartone, from carta 'card, paper'. Ultimately from Latin charta.|
