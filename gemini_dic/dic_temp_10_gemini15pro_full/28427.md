carbonizes:/ˈkɑːrbənaɪzɪz/
verb
使碳化；烧焦
To convert or be converted into carbon or a carbon residue by partial burning or destructive distillation.
The intense heat carbonizes the wood, turning it into charcoal.  高温使木材碳化，将其变成木炭。

The wood carbonized in the fire. 木头在火中烧焦了。

近义词:char, scorch, burn
反义词: extinguish, put out
词性:v.
