indelibility:/ˌɪndɪˈlɪbɪlɪti/
n.
不可磨灭性，难以忘却
The quality or state of being indelible.
The indelibility of the memory made it hard for him to move on. 这段记忆的不可磨灭让他难以释怀。
Synonyms: permanence, durability, lastingness
Antonyms: ephemerality, transience, temporariness
noun
