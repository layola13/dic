gablelike:/ˈɡeɪblˌlaɪk/| adj.|像山墙的，像三角墙的|Resembling a gable, typically having a triangular shape.|The roofline was distinctly gablelike, giving the house a charming cottage feel. 屋顶轮廓明显像山墙，赋予了房子迷人的小屋感觉。|近义词：gabled, triangular, peaked|反义词:flat, rounded|词性:形容词

词源:  From "gable" + "-like."  "Gable" comes from Old Norse "gafl," meaning "fork."
