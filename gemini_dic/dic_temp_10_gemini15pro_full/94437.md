implorer:/ɪmˈplɔːrər/
n.|恳求者；哀求者；|A person who implores or begs earnestly. |The implorer knelt before the king, begging for mercy. 恳求者跪在国王面前，乞求怜悯。|Synonym:  supplicant, petitioner, beggar, pleader|Antonym:  demander, commander|noun
