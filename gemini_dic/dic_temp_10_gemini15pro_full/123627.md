monticulate:/mɒnˈtɪkjʊleɪt/ /mɑːnˈtɪkjʊleɪt/
adjective
多小丘的，微凸的；具小颗粒的
Covered with or characterized by small elevations; minutely hilly; granular.
The bacteria formed small, monticulate colonies on the agar plate. 细菌在琼脂平板上形成了小的、多小丘的菌落。
近义词: bumpy, granular, hillocky, knobby, lumpy
反义词: smooth, flat
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *monticulus (small hill), diminutive of mons (mountain).
