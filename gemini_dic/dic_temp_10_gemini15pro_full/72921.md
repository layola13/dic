fonded:/ˈfɒndɪd/ | adj. | 喜爱的；深情的；宠爱的；溺爱的 | Having a strong liking or affection for. |  She had grown quite fonded of the little dog. 她已经非常喜欢这只小狗了。|  He's very fonded of his grandchildren. 他非常疼爱他的孙子孙女们。 | 近义词: affectionate, loving, attached, devoted | 反义词: indifferent, apathetic, uncaring | 词性: 形容词
词源: From the verb "fond," which comes from the Middle English "fonnen," meaning "to be foolish or act like a fool," later evolving to mean "to dote on" or "be foolishly affectionate."
