amnestic: /æmˈnɛstɪk/ | adj. n.| 遗忘的，失忆的；遗忘症的；健忘症患者 | Relating to or causing amnesia; a person suffering from amnesia. |  The patient exhibited amnestic symptoms after the accident. 事故发生后，病人表现出失忆症状。| The doctor prescribed an amnestic drug. 医生开了健忘症的药。 |Synonyms: forgetful, oblivious, memory-impaired | Antonyms: mindful, remembering | adjective

amnestic: /æmˈnɛstɪk/ | n. | 健忘症患者 | A person suffering from amnesia. | The amnestic couldn't remember his own name.  这位健忘症患者不记得自己的名字了。 | He was an amnestic wandering the streets. 他是一个在街上游荡的失忆症患者。| Synonyms: amnesiac | Antonyms: N/A | noun
