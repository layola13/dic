Etymology: From Marathi महाराष्ट्र (mahārāṣṭra), ultimately from Sanskrit महाराष्ट्र (mahārāṣṭra), meaning "Great Maharashtra" or possibly "the great country" or "the country of the Mahars" (an ancient tribe).
