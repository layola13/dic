embalmments:/ɪmˈbɑːmənts/
n.
防腐处理；尸体保存
the process of preserving a dead body by treating it with chemicals or other substances to prevent decay.
The museum houses several ancient Egyptian embalmments.  博物馆收藏了几具古埃及的木乃伊。
preservation, mummification
decay, decomposition
noun
