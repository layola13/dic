literate: /ˈlɪtərət/ (US), /ˈlɪtrət/ (UK)| adj. n.|有读写能力的；识字的；精通文学的；博学的；有文化的人|Able to read and write; having knowledge or competence.|例句：A literate population is essential for a thriving democracy. 受过教育的民众对于一个繁荣的民主国家至关重要。|例句：She is literate in both English and French. 她精通英语和法语。|近义词：educated, learned, knowledgeable|反义词：illiterate, ignorant|词性:形容词, 名词

词源: From Late Middle English literate, from Latin lītterātus, past participle of lītterāre (“to mark with letters, instruct in letters”), from lītter (“letter”).
