outwasting:/ˌaʊtˈweɪstɪŋ/
verb
过度消耗,过度浪费
Exceeding or surpassing in wasting; wasting more than.
The company was outwasting its competitors by a significant margin, leading to financial difficulties. 这家公司过度消耗远超其竞争对手，导致财务困难。
The prodigal son was outwasting his inheritance on frivolous pursuits. 那个挥霍无度的儿子把遗产浪费在轻浮的追求上。
近义词:squandering, dissipating, overspending
反义词:saving, conserving, preserving
词性:v.
