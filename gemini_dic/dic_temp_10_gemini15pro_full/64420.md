ergonomics:/ˌɜːɡəˈnɒmɪks/ (US) /ˌɜːrɡəˈnɑːmɪks/| n. |人体工程学|The study of people's efficiency in their working environment.|Example: The office was redesigned with ergonomics in mind to reduce strain on employees. 这间办公室根据人体工程学进行了重新设计，以减少员工的压力。|Example:  Good ergonomics can help prevent repetitive strain injuries. 良好的人体工程学可以帮助预防重复性劳损。|Synonym: human factors, human engineering|Antonym: N/A|Noun
