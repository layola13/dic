booties:/ˈbuːtiz/ /ˈbuːt̬iz/| n.|婴儿袜；短靴；战利品|Small, soft footwear for babies or infants, often knitted or crocheted. Also refers to ankle-high boots for women or, informally, to spoils or prizes.|The baby's booties were knitted by her grandmother. 婴儿的袜子是她祖母织的。

She bought a new pair of stylish suede booties. 她买了一双时髦的绒面革短靴。

The soldiers divided the booties after the battle. 士兵们在战斗后瓜分了战利品。|近义词：baby socks (for baby booties), ankle boots, spoils, loot|反义词: (No direct antonym for the general meaning.  Could be losses for the "spoils" meaning)|词性:noun
