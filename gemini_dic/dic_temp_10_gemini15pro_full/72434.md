fluidize:/ˈfluːɪdaɪz/| v. |流化，使…流化|To make (a solid material, such as sand or coal dust) behave like a fluid by suspending it in a moving gas or liquid.|The process fluidizes the catalyst particles, allowing them to circulate freely.  这个过程使催化剂颗粒流化，使其能够自由循环。|The engineers fluidized the coal dust before injecting it into the furnace. 工程师们在将煤粉注入熔炉之前将其流化。|liquefy, suspend|solidify|verb
fluidization:/ˌfluːɪdaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |流化，流态化|The process of making a solid material behave like a fluid by suspending it in a moving gas or liquid.|Fluidization is a key process in many industrial applications. 流态化是许多工业应用中的关键过程。|The fluidization of the sand created a quicksand-like effect. 沙子的流化产生了类似流沙的效果。|liquefaction, suspension|solidification|noun
