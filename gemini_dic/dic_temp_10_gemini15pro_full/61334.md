embattlement:/ɪmˈbætəlmənt/
noun
城垛；防御工事；(象征意义上的)防御姿态
A parapet, usually battlemented, on a castle, fort, or town wall.  A position of defence.
The castle's embattlements provided a good vantage point for archers. 城堡的城垛为弓箭手提供了一个很好的制高点。
He took an embattlement against the criticism. 他摆出防御姿态来应对批评。
synonyms: fortification, rampart, parapet, battlement, bulwark, bastion
antonyms: vulnerability, weakness
