infuriating:/ɪnˈfjʊərieɪtɪŋ/| adj.|令人愤怒的，使人恼火的|Causing intense annoyance, frustration, or anger.|Her constant complaining was infuriating.|近义词：annoying, exasperating, maddening, frustrating|反义词：pleasing, soothing, calming|词性:形容词|词源: From the verb "infuriate," which comes from the Latin "infuriare" (to make furious), from "in-" (in) + "furia" (fury).
