feudalism: /ˈfjuːdəlɪzəm/ | n. |封建主义|A social system that existed in Europe during the Middle Ages in which people worked and fought for nobles who gave them protection and the use of land in return.|The history professor lectured on the decline of feudalism. 历史教授讲述了封建制度的衰落。|Synonyms: vassalage, manorialism |Antonyms: democracy, republicanism |noun

feudal: /ˈfjuːdəl/ | adj. |封建的|Relating to or characteristic of feudalism.|The feudal system was based on land ownership. 封建制度是建立在土地所有制的基础上的。|Synonyms: medieval, aristocratic |Antonyms: modern, democratic |adjective
