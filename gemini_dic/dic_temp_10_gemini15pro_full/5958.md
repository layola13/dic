ambrosial:/æmˈbroʊʒəl/ | adj. | 美味的，芳香的；神食的 | Exceptionally pleasing to taste or smell; worthy of the gods; divine. | The ambrosial aroma of the freshly baked bread filled the kitchen.  新鲜出炉的面包的美味香气弥漫了整个厨房。 | The chef created an ambrosial feast that delighted all the guests.  这位厨师创造了一场让所有客人都感到愉悦的美味盛宴。 | 近义词: delicious, heavenly, divine, fragrant | 反义词: unappetizing, unpleasant, foul | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Late Latin *ambrosialis, from Latin ambrosia (“food of the gods”), from Ancient Greek ἀμβροσία (ambrosía, “immortality, food of the gods”), from ἄμβροτος (ámbrotos, “immortal”).
