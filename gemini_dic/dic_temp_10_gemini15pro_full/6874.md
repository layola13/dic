ancestor:/ˈænˌsɛstər/ | n. |祖先，祖宗|A person from whom one is descended; a forebear.| My ancestors came from Ireland. 我的祖先来自爱尔兰。|Synonyms: forebear, progenitor, antecedent|Antonyms: descendant, offspring|Noun

ancestor:/ˈænˌsɛstər/ | n. |原型，先驱| something that precedes and influences the development or evolution of something else, such as a prototype or earlier model | This car is the ancestor of most modern SUVs. 这辆车是大多数现代SUV的原型。|Synonyms: precursor, prototype, forerunner|Antonyms: successor, descendant|Noun
