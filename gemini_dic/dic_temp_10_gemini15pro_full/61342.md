embedding:/ɪmˈbedɪŋ/ | n. | 嵌入; 嵌入物; 镶嵌 |  The act of embedding something, or something that is embedded. embedding:/ɪmˈbedɪŋ/ | adj. | 嵌入的 |  Used to describe something that is embedded. |  The embedded journalist sent back daily reports. 嵌入式记者每天发回报道。 | synonyms: inserted, implanted, incorporated, integrated | antonyms: extracted, removed | adjective
