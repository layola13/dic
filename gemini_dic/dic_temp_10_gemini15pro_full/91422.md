hyetal:/ˈhaɪɪtəl/| adj.|雨的，降水的|Relating to rain or rainfall.  Characterized by rain.  |例句：The hyetal regime of the region is characterized by heavy monsoonal rains.  该地区的降水状况以季风性强降雨为特征。

例句：Hyetal charts can be used to visualize rainfall patterns over time.  降水量图表可以用来可视化一段时间内的降雨模式。|近义词：pluvial, rainy|反义词：arid, dry|词性:adjective
