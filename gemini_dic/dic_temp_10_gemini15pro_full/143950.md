outquarters:/ˈaʊtˌkwɔːrtərz/
noun
外宿；外营房；外部住所
A place of lodging away from one's usual residence, especially for military personnel.
The soldiers were assigned to outquarters during the training exercise.  士兵们在训练演习期间被分配到外营房。
The construction crew lived in temporary outquarters near the building site. 建筑工人们住在工地附近的临时住所。
synonyms: barracks, billet, lodgings, accommodation
antonyms: headquarters, home, residence
