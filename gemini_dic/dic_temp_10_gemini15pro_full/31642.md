chatrooms:/ˈtʃætˌruːmz/ | n. |聊天室| A room, or a virtual space online, where people can communicate with each other in real time by typing messages. |  I met some interesting people in online chatrooms. 我在网上聊天室里遇到了一些有趣的人。

|  Many teenagers spend hours in chatrooms. 很多青少年在聊天室里待上几个小时。
| 近义词: chat groups, forums, message boards | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "face-to-face meeting") | 词性: noun
