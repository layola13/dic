oversure:/ˌoʊvərˈʃʊr/ | adj. | 过于自信的；自负的；过于确信的 | Too confident; excessively certain. | He was oversure of his ability to win the race. 他对赢得比赛的能力过于自信。 | He was oversure about his investment strategy and ended up losing a lot of money. 他对自己的投资策略过于自信，最终损失了很多钱。 | 近义词: arrogant, cocky, presumptuous, conceited | 反义词: modest, humble, diffident, unsure | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From over- + sure.  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning of "sure," indicating an excessive degree of certainty.
