caricous:/kəˈrɪkəs/ | adj. | 螨虫引起的 | Caused by mites; infested with mites. | The vet diagnosed the dog's skin condition as caricous mange.  兽医诊断这只狗的皮肤病是由螨虫引起的疥癣。 | The farmer noticed caricous lesions on the chickens. 农民注意到鸡身上有螨虫引起的病灶。 | 近义词:  mite-ridden, acarine | 反义词: healthy, mite-free | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Latin *carīx* (genitive *carīcis*), meaning "mite".
