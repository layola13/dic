epicarp:/ˈɛpɪkɑːrp/ | n. | 外果皮 | The outermost layer of the pericarp, i.e., the skin of a fruit. | The epicarp of the peach is fuzzy. 桃子的外果皮毛茸茸的。| The tough, leathery epicarp of the coconut protects the seed inside.  坚韧的椰子外果皮保护着里面的种子。|  exocarp, rind | endocarp, mesocarp | noun
