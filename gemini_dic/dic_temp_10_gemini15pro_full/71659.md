flattery:/ˈflætəri/| n.|奉承；恭维；阿谀|Excessive flattery can be insincere. 过度的奉承可能是不真诚的。|She used flattery to get what she wanted. 她用奉承来得到她想要的东西。|近义词：praise, adulation, compliment|反义词：criticism, condemnation|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *flaterie* (Modern French *flatterie*), from *flater* "to flatter," from Frankish *flaton* "to stroke, caress," from Proto-Germanic *flatojaną*. Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European root *pleh₁-*, "to be broad, flat, spread."
