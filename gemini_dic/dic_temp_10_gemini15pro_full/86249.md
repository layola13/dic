heaved:/hiːvd/| v.| 举起；发出（叹息等）；呕吐；起伏；波动|lift or haul (a heavy thing) with great effort.|She heaved the heavy box onto the truck. 她吃力地把沉重的箱子搬到卡车上。|近义词：lifted, hoisted, raised, pulled, hauled, threw up, vomited|反义词:lowered, dropped|词性:动词
词源：From Old English *hēafian, from Proto-Germanic *hafjaną (“to lift, raise”), from Proto-Indo-European *keh₂p- (“to seize, grasp”). Cognate with Dutch heffen (“to lift”), German heben (“to lift”).
