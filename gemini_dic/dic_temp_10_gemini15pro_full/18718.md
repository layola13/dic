bereft:/bɪˈrɛft/ /bəˈrɛft/| adj.| 丧失的，被剥夺的；失去亲人的 | Deprived of or lacking something, especially a nonmaterial possession.  Suffering the death of a loved one; bereaved. |  She was bereft of all hope after the diagnosis.  她确诊后失去了所有希望。

The family was bereft after the loss of their father.  这家人在失去父亲后悲痛欲绝。| 近义词：deprived, bereaved, lacking, destitute | 反义词：replete, full, rich, possessing | 词性：形容词
