lippiest:/ˈlɪpiɪst/
adjective
最爱抱怨的；最爱发牢骚的
Having a tendency to complain a lot; given to whimpering or complaining.
例句：He was the lippiest child in the class, always whining about something. 他是班上最爱抱怨的孩子，总是哼哼唧唧的。

例句：She's known as the lippiest customer in the store, always finding fault with something. 她被认为是店里最爱抱怨的顾客，总是挑毛病。
近义词：complaining, whining, grumbling, querulous
反义词：content, satisfied, cheerful
词性: adjective
