nonfoods:/ˈnɑːnfuːdz/
n.
非食品
Items that are not edible or intended for consumption.
The store sells a variety of nonfoods, including cleaning supplies and pet food. 这家商店出售各种非食品，包括清洁用品和宠物食品。
近义词:inedibles, non-edibles
反义词:foods, foodstuffs
词性:名词


Etymology:
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "foods" (plural of "food")
