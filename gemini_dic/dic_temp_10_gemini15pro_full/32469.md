chinan:/ˈtʃaɪnən/ | n. |瓷器; 瓷; |A hard, white, translucent ceramic material made by firing a pure clay and then glazing it.  Derived from "China" where it was first made. | The antique chinan was delicately painted with flowers.  这件古董瓷器上精细地绘着花朵。 | synonym: porcelain, china, ceramic | antonym: earthenware | noun
