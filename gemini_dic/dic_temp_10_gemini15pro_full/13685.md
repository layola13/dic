attributable:/əˈtrɪbjutəbl/ | adj. | 可归因于…的；可归属于…的 |  Capable of being attributed to a particular cause or source. | The increase in crime is attributable to several factors, including poverty and unemployment. 犯罪率的上升可归因于多种因素，包括贫困和失业。|  ascribable, imputable, creditable | unattributable, unascribable | adjective

attributable:/əˈtrɪbjutəbl/ | adj. | 归于…的，归功于…的 |  Belonging to or appropriate to something. (This meaning is less common.) |  The success of the project is attributable to the hard work of the team.  项目的成功归功于团队的辛勤工作。 |  ascribable, imputable, creditable | unattributable, unascribable | adjective
