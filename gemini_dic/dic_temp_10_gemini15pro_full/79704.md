glucina:/ɡluːˈsaɪnə/| n.|氧化铍,铍土|A sweet-tasting, highly toxic, white or colorless, amorphous powder, BeO, used in ceramics and as a moderator in nuclear reactors.  It is the oxide of beryllium.|Beryllium oxide (glucina) was used in the early days of nuclear technology.|近义词:beryllium oxide|反义词: (No direct antonym for a chemical compound)|noun
