bickern:/ˈbɪkərn/| n.|铁砧, 尖嘴铁砧|A bickern, also called a beakiron or beak iron, is a type of anvil with a pointed beak-like projection, used by blacksmiths for shaping metal. |The blacksmith used the bickern to create a decorative curl on the wrought iron gate. 铁匠用尖嘴铁砧在锻铁门上制作了一个装饰性的卷曲。|Synonyms: anvil, beakiron, beak iron|Antonyms: N/A|noun

bickern:/ˈbɪkərn/| v.|用尖嘴铁砧加工|To shape or work metal using a bickern.|He bickered the red-hot metal into a delicate leaf shape. 他用尖嘴铁砧将炽热的金属加工成精美的叶子形状。|Synonyms: forge, hammer, shape|Antonyms: N/A|verb
