词源: From Middle English *morcellen, from Old French morcel, morsel (“a bite, morsel”), diminutive of mors (“a bite”), from Latin morsus (“a bite”), past participle of mordēre (“to bite”).

注意事项:  虽然morsel主要用作名词，但在某些情况下也可以用作动词，表示“切成小块”或“少量给予”。
