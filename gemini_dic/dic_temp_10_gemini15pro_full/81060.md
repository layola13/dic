grained:/ɡreɪnd/ | adj. | 根深蒂固的，深入人心的 | Firmly fixed or established. |  His ingrained prejudices made it difficult for him to accept new ideas. 他根深蒂固的偏见使他难以接受新思想。 | Synonyms: deep-rooted, entrenched, inherent | Antonyms: superficial, fleeting | adjective
