overbreeds:/ˌoʊvərˈbriːdz/ | v. | 过度繁殖，繁殖过多 | To breed (animals) excessively, leading to an overpopulation. |  They overbred the dogs, resulting in many sickly puppies. 他们过度繁殖了这些狗，导致许多幼犬体弱多病。|  overpopulate, proliferate | underbreed | verb
overbreeds:/ˌoʊvərˈbriːdz/ | n. | 过度繁殖的动物 | Animals that are the result of overbreeding. |  The overbreeds were susceptible to various health problems. 这些过度繁殖的动物容易患上各种健康问题。 |  overpopulation, surplus |  shortage, scarcity | noun
