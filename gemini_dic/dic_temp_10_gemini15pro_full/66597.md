exhume: /ɪɡˈzjuːm/ /ɛksˈhjuːm/ | vt. | 掘出；发掘 | To dig out (something buried, especially a corpse) from the earth; to unearth. | The archaeologists exhumed the ancient king's remains. 考古学家掘出了古代国王的遗骸。

The police exhumed the body to conduct further forensic tests. 警方掘出尸体进行进一步的法医检验。


近义词: disinter, unearth, uncover | 反义词: bury, inter | 词性: 动词
