debauchee:/ˌdɛbɔːˈʃiː/ /ˌdɛbɔːˈtʃiː/
noun
放荡者；酒色之徒
a person given to excessive indulgence in sensual pleasures.
He was known as a debauchee who frequented the city's most notorious establishments. 他被称为一个放荡者，经常光顾这座城市最臭名昭著的场所。

The young debauchee quickly squandered his inheritance on extravagant parties and illicit pleasures.  这个年轻的放荡者迅速地把他的遗产挥霍在奢靡的派对和不正当的享乐上。


近义词:libertine, profligate, rake, roué
反义词:ascetic, puritan
词性:n.
