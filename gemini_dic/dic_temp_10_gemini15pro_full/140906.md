一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I exercise once a week.  我每周锻炼一次。
He visits his grandmother once a month. 他每月去看望他的祖母一次。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I'm only saying this once. 我只说这一次。
He's explaining it once, so listen carefully. 他只解释一次，所以仔细听。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have only seen that movie once. 我只看过那部电影一次。
She has visited Paris once. 她去过巴黎一次。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I've been thinking about it once and again. 我一直在反复思考这件事。 (Note: This usage is slightly less common, but acceptable)
He's been saying that once too often. 他一直在重复说那句话。( Note: This usage emphasizes repetition, though technically only one instance is being referred to within a specific timeframe. It is a more idiomatic usage.)


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I went there once last year. 我去年去过那里一次。
She met him once at a party. 她在一次聚会上见过他。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was only saying it once. 我只是说了一次。
He was explaining it once, so I listened carefully. 他在解释一次，所以我仔细听了。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had only seen that movie once before. 我以前只看过那部电影一次。
She had visited Paris once before she moved to France.  她在搬到法国之前去过巴黎一次。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been considering it once before. 我之前考虑过一次。( Note: Similar to present perfect continuous, this is less common but acceptable)
He had been mentioning it once before he decided. 他在决定之前提到过一次。(Note: Similar to present perfect continuous, this highlights repeated mentioning within a past timeframe.)

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will go there once next year. 我明年会去那里一次。
She will meet him once she arrives. 她一到就会见他一次。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be explaining this once tomorrow. 我明天会解释一次。 (Note: Slightly unusual but grammatically correct. It suggests a planned single explanation.)
He will be mentioning it once during the meeting. 他会在会议期间提到一次。(Note: Again, emphasizes a single instance within a future timeframe.)

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I will have been there once by next summer. 到明年夏天，我就去过那里一次了。
She will have seen him once by the time she leaves. 到她离开的时候，她就见过他一次了。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
(This tense is rarely used with "once" due to the inherent contradiction between the continuous aspect and the single instance implied by "once.")


虚拟语气:
If I had gone there once, I would know the way. 如果我去过那里一次，我就知道路了。
I wish I could do it over again, even just once. 我希望我能再做一次，哪怕只有一次。

被动语态:
It was done once and never again. 它只做了一次，再也没有了。
The song was played only once. 这首歌只播放了一次。

疑问句:
Have you been there once? 你去过那里一次吗？
Did she say that once? 她说过一次吗？

否定句:
I haven't been there once. 我一次也没去过那里。
She didn't say that once. 她一次也没说过那样的话。
