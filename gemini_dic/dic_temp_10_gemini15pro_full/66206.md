excitablenesses:/ɪkˌsaɪtəbəlˈnɛsɪz/| n. |兴奋性，易激动性|The state or quality of being easily excited.|The excitablenesses of the fans reached a fever pitch during the final seconds of the game.  比赛的最后几秒钟，球迷们的兴奋达到了顶点。|近义词：irascibility, irritability, sensitivity|反义词：calmness, placidity, tranquility|词性:名词

Etymology:  From "excitable" + "-ness".  "Excitable" comes from Late Middle English: from late Latin excitabilis, from excitare (see excite). The suffix "-ness" forms nouns expressing state or condition.
