off-roaders:/ˌɒfˈroʊdərz/ (US), /ˌɒfˈrəʊdərz/ (UK)
n.
越野者；越野车；非公路车辆
People who drive vehicles off-road, or the vehicles themselves designed for off-road driving.
例句：Many off-roaders enjoy exploring the desert. 许多越野爱好者喜欢探索沙漠。

例句：The off-roaders gathered for the annual competition. 越野车爱好者们聚集在一起参加年度比赛。

近义词：off-road vehicles, 4x4s, SUVs
反义词：on-road vehicles, cars, sedans
词性：名词

off-roader:/ˌɒfˈroʊdər/ (US), /ˌɒfˈrəʊdər/ (UK)
n.
越野者；越野车；非公路车辆
A person who drives a vehicle off-road, or a vehicle designed for off-road driving.
例句：He's a keen off-roader and spends his weekends in the mountains. 他是一位热衷于越野的人，周末都在山里度过。

例句：That off-roader is equipped with special tires for muddy terrain. 那辆越野车配备了用于泥泞地形的特殊轮胎。

近义词：off-road vehicle, 4x4, SUV
反义词：on-road vehicle, car, sedan
词性：名词
