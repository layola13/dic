gibibit:/ˈɡɪbiˌbɪt/
n.
吉比特（信息量单位）
A gibibit is a unit of information or computer storage equal to 2^30 (1,073,741,824) bits.  It is a member of a set of units with binary prefixes, defined by the International Electrotechnical Commission (IEC) in 1998.
A network connection with a speed of 1 Gigabit per second can transfer 1 gibibit of data approximately every 8 seconds.  一个每秒1千兆位的网络连接大约每8秒可以传输1吉比特的数据。
Modern hard drives can store several tebibits of data. 现代硬盘可以存储数太比特的数据。
近义词:mebibit, kibibit, tebibit (other units of digital information)
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
