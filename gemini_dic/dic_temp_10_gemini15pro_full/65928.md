exacerbates:/ɪɡˈzæsərbeɪts/ /ɛkˈsæsərbeɪts/
verb
加剧，恶化
Makes a problem, bad situation, or negative feeling worse.
The humid weather exacerbated his asthma. 潮湿的天气加剧了他的哮喘。
近义词:worsen, aggravate, intensify, compound
反义词:alleviate, improve, mitigate, relieve
词性:v.


exacerbation:/ɪɡˌzæsərˈbeɪʃn/ /ɛkˌsæsərˈbeɪʃn/
noun
恶化，加剧
The act of making something worse, especially a problem or a disease.
Stress can lead to an exacerbation of his condition. 压力会导致他的病情恶化。
近义词:worsening, aggravation, intensification
反义词:improvement, alleviation, mitigation
词性:n.

词源:
From Late Latin exacerbatus, past participle of exacerbare “to irritate, exasperate,” from ex- “out” + acerbare “to make bitter,” from acerbus “bitter, harsh.”
