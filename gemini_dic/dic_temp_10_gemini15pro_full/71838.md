fleets:/fliːts/
n.|舰队，车队，机群|A group of ships, vehicles, or aircraft operating together or under the same ownership.|The company owns a large fleet of trucks. 这家公司拥有一支庞大的卡车车队。|synonyms: armada, flotilla, navy (for ships), convoy, motorcade (for vehicles), squadron (for aircraft)|antonyms: single ship/vehicle/aircraft, individual unit|noun

fleets:/fliːts/
v. (third-person singular simple present fleets, present participle fleeting, simple past and past participle fleeted).|快速移动，飞逝|To move swiftly; to pass quickly.|Time fleets by. 时光飞逝。|synonyms: flit, dart, flash, rush, hurry, speed|antonyms: crawl, dawdle, linger|verb
