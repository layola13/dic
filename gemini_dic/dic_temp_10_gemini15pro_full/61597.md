emersed:/ɪˈmɜːrst/ | adj. | 露出水面的，挺水植物的 | Growing above the surface of the water, especially of the leaves of an aquatic plant. | Example: The emersed leaves of the water lily floated on the pond's surface.  睡莲的挺水叶漂浮在池塘的水面上。| Example:  Emersed plants are common in wetlands. 挺水植物在湿地很常见。 | 近义词: aerial, above-water, emergent | 反义词: submerged, immersed | 词性: adjective

emerge:/ɪˈmɜːrdʒ/ | v. | 浮现，出现 | To come forth into view or notice, as from concealment or obscurity. | Example: The sun emerged from behind the clouds. 太阳从云层后面出现。 | Example: The facts emerged after a thorough investigation. 经过彻底调查，事实真相浮出水面。 | 近义词: appear, surface, arise | 反义词: disappear, vanish, submerge | 词性: verb
