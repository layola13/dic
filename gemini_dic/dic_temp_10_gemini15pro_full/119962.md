millennial:/mɪˈlɛniəl/| n. adj.|千禧一代；千禧年的|A person reaching young adulthood around the year 2000; relating to this generation.|例句：Millennials are known for their tech-savviness. 千禧一代以精通科技而闻名。

例句：The millennial generation faces unique economic challenges.  千禧一代面临着独特的经济挑战。|近义词：Generation Y, Gen Y|反义词: Baby Boomer, Generation X|词性: noun, adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *millennium* "a thousand years," from *mille* "thousand" and *annus* "year."  The term "millennial" specifically refers to the generation born roughly between 1981 and 1996.
