cordiality:/kɔːrˈdʒæləti/ /kɔːrˈdiːæləti/ | n. | 热情, 友好, 诚恳 |  Sincere affection and kindness; warm and friendly behavior. | The hostess greeted us with cordiality and offered us refreshments.  女主人热情地迎接我们，并为我们提供点心。|  friendliness, warmth, geniality, affability | coldness, hostility, unfriendliness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From Middle French *cordialité*, from Late Latin *cordiālitās*, from Latin *cordiālis* (“hearty, sincere”), from *cor*, *cord-* (“heart”).
