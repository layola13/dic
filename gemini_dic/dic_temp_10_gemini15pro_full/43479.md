procrastination: /proʊˌkræstɪˈneɪʃən/ /prəˌkræstɪˈneɪʃn/ | n. | 拖延，拖延症 |  The act of delaying or postponing something, especially something requiring immediate attention. | I know I should start working on the project, but procrastination keeps getting the better of me. 我知道我应该开始做这个项目了，但拖延症总是占了上风。|  delay, postponement, stalling |  action, decisiveness, diligence | noun
