defenselessness:/dɪˈfɛnsləsnəs/| n.|无助，无力自卫，毫无防御能力|The defenselessness of the young animals made them easy prey. 幼小动物的无力自卫使它们容易成为猎物。|例句：The refugees' defenselessness against the attacks was heartbreaking.  难民们无力抵抗袭击，这令人心碎。

例句：Her defenselessness in the face of the bully was apparent. 她面对恶霸时的无助显而易见。

|近义词：vulnerability, helplessness, weakness, impotence|反义词:strength, power, security, protection|词性:名词
