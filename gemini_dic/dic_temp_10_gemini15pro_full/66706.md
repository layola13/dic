exonerators:/ɪɡˈzɑːnəˌreɪtərz/ | n. | 免罪者，证明无罪者 |  A person or group of people who exonerate someone, meaning they clear them of blame or accusation. | The exonerators presented compelling evidence that proved the defendant's innocence. 免罪者提供了令人信服的证据，证明了被告的清白。 |  vindicators, defenders, champions | accusers, prosecutors, condemners | noun

exonerate:/ɪɡˈzɑːnəˌreɪt/ | v. | 免除…的罪责；证明…的清白 | To clear someone of blame or accusation. | The new evidence exonerated the suspect. 新证据证明了嫌疑人无罪。| absolve, acquit, vindicate | accuse, incriminate, condemn | verb
