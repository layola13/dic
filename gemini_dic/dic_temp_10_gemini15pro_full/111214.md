lithophyte:/ˈlɪθəˌfaɪt/ | n. | 岩生植物；石生植物 | A plant that grows on rock.  A lithophyte derives its nutrients from rain, dust, decaying organic matter, and its own photosynthesis. |  The steep cliffs were covered in lithophytes, clinging tenaciously to the bare rock. 峭壁上覆盖着岩生植物，紧紧地 clinging to the bare rock。 | The tiny lithophyte thrived in the harsh, exposed environment.  这株小小的岩生植物在恶劣的、裸露的环境中茁壮成长。 |Synonyms: rock plant, chasmophyte | Antonyms:  hydrophyte (water plant)| noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek λίθος (líthos, “stone”) + φυτόν (phutón, “plant”).
