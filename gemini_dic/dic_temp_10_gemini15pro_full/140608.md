olivine: /ˈɒlɪˌvɪn/ (UK) /ˈɑːləˌviːn/ (US) | n. |橄榄石|A magnesium iron silicate mineral, (Mg, Fe)2SiO4, occurring widely in igneous and metamorphic rocks and varying in color from olive-green to brown. |Olivine is a common mineral in Earth's mantle. 橄榄石是地球地幔中的一种常见矿物。

Olivine is the primary component of the gemstone peridot. 橄榄石是宝石橄榄绿的主要成分。|近义词:peridot (when gem quality), chrysolite (archaic term)|反义词: N/A |词性:noun

olivine: /ˈɒlɪˌvɪn/ (UK) /ˈɑːləˌviːn/ (US) | adj. |橄榄石的，橄榄绿色的|Of or relating to olivine; having the color of olivine. |The olivine sand on this beach is beautiful. 这个沙滩上的橄榄绿沙子很漂亮。

The geologist identified the olivine crystals in the rock sample. 地质学家在岩石样本中识别出了橄榄石晶体。|近义词:olive-green, olive-colored|反义词: N/A |词性:adjective
