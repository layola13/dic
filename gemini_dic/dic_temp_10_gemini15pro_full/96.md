ab:/æb/| prefix.|脱离, 从...离开|A prefix meaning "away from," "off," or "departing from."|Example: abnormal (not normal, deviating from the standard).|近义词: off, away, de-|反义词: ad, to, toward|prefix


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This plant's growth is abnormal. = 这株植物的生长异常。
His behavior is aberrant. = 他的行为反常。

一般过去时:
The doctor noticed an aberrant heartbeat. = 医生注意到心跳异常。
The spaceship abjured its course. = 宇宙飞船偏离了航线。

现在完成时:
He has abdicated his responsibilities. = 他已经放弃了他的责任。
The company has absconded with the funds. = 这家公司携款潜逃了。

过去完成时:
The king had abdicated the throne before the revolution. = 国王在革命前已经退位。
The suspect had absconded before the police arrived. = 嫌疑人在警察到达之前已经逃跑了。

将来时:
If he continues like this, he will abjure his faith. = 如果他继续这样下去，他将放弃他的信仰。
The CEO will abdicate next year. = 首席执行官明年将辞职。


PHRASE->
ab initio = from the beginning 从一开始
ab extra = from outside 从外部
ab intra = from within 从内部
ab origine = from the origin 从起源
ab ovo = from the egg; from the beginning 从头开始


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ab*, meaning "from," "away from," "off."  It is related to the Greek *apo*. This prepositional prefix appears in many English words borrowed directly or indirectly from Latin.
