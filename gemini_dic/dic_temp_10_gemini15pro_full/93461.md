illusionistically:/ɪˌluːʒənˈɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 以幻觉方式；逼真地 |  In a manner that creates an illusion, often of depth, space, or reality. | The artist painted the scene illusionistically, making the flat canvas appear three-dimensional.  艺术家以幻觉的方式描绘了场景，使平坦的画布呈现出三维效果。| synonym: deceptively, realistically, convincingly | antonym: unconvincingly, unrealistically, plainly | adverb
