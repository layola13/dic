olein:/ˈoʊliɪn/ | n. |油酸甘油酯；油精 | A naturally occurring triglyceride found in various animal and vegetable fats and oils.  It is the main constituent of olive oil. |  Olein is used in the production of soaps and cosmetics. 油酸甘油酯用于生产肥皂和化妆品。|  Olive oil is rich in olein. 橄榄油富含油酸甘油酯。 | synonyms: triolein, glyceryl trioleate | antonyms:  N/A (Chemical compounds generally don't have direct antonyms.) | noun
