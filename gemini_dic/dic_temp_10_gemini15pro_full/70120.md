feyness:/ˈfeɪnɪs/
n.
仙气；空灵；优雅
The quality of being ethereal or otherworldly, especially in appearance or manner.
She moved with an effortless feyness, as if floating on air. 她行动起来带着毫不费力的仙气，仿佛漂浮在空中。
ethereality, delicacy, grace, charm
clumsiness, awkwardness, gaucheness
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From fay + -ness.  Fay meaning fairy or elf, ultimately from Latin *fata, fate.
