cuz: /kʌz/| informal contraction| 因为 |Cuz is a shortened, informal version of "because". It's used primarily in casual conversation, text messages, and online communication.| Cuz I'm tired, I'm going to bed early. 因为我累了，我要早点睡觉。 | 近义词: because, 'cause, as, since | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a conjunction) | conjunction

PHRASE->
What's up, cuz?  怎么了，兄弟/姐妹？
No worries, cuz.  没事儿，兄弟/姐妹。
I got you, cuz.  我懂你，兄弟/姐妹。
Let's go, cuz!  我们走吧，兄弟/姐妹！
Thanks, cuz.  谢谢，兄弟/姐妹。

注意事项:  Cuz is highly informal and should be avoided in formal writing or professional settings.  It's generally used among close friends or family members.
