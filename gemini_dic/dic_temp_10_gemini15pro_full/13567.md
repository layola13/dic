attended: /əˈtɛndɪd/ | v. | 参加，出席；照料，护理；伴随 |  to be present at (an event, meeting, etc.); to go regularly to (a place); to take care of someone or something |  I attended the conference last week. 我上周参加了会议。| 近义词: present, participate, join, be at, go to, look after, care for, tend | 反义词: absent, miss, neglect | 词性: 动词

词源: From Old French atendre "to wait for, expect, attend, pay attention to," from Latin attendere "to give heed to, apply oneself to, await," literally "to stretch toward," from ad- "to" (see ad-) + tendere "to stretch, extend, direct, aim" (from PIE root *ten- "to stretch").
