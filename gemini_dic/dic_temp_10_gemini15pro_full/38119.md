comprehend:/ˌkɒmprɪˈhɛnd/ (UK) /ˌkɑːmprɪˈhɛnd/ (US)| v. |理解，领会|To understand something fully.|I couldn't comprehend what she was saying. 我无法理解她在说什么。|近义词：understand, grasp, perceive|反义词：misunderstand, misinterpret|词性：动词

词源: From Late Latin comprehendere "to grasp mentally, understand," from com- "together" (see com-) + prehendere "to seize, grasp" (see prehensile).
