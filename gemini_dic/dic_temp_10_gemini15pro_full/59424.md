earthliness:/ˈɜːrθlinəs/
n.
世俗,尘世,现世
The quality of being earthly or worldly; preoccupation with material things.
Her earthliness contrasted sharply with her husband's spiritual aspirations. 她对世俗的专注与她丈夫的精神追求形成了鲜明的对比。
近义词: worldliness, secularity, materialism
反义词: spirituality, otherworldliness
