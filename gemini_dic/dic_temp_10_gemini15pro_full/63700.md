ephemera's:/ɪˈfɛmərəz/| n.|蜉蝣的；短命事物的；易消逝事物的| Belonging to or associated with ephemera, things that exist or are used or enjoyed for only a short time.|  These are my grandmother's ephemera's, including old postcards and ticket stubs. 这些是我祖母的蜉蝣，包括旧明信片和票根。|Synonyms:  transients, short-lived items, temporary things|Antonyms:  permanents, durables, lasting things|Noun (Possessive form)
