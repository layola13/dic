methadone:/ˈmɛθəˌdoʊn/ | n. |美沙酮|A synthetic opioid drug used as a pain reliever and as a treatment for heroin addiction. |The doctor prescribed methadone to help him overcome his heroin addiction. 医生给他开了美沙酮来帮助他克服海洛因成瘾。|Synonyms: Dolophine (brand name), opioid analgesic |Antonyms:  naloxone (antagonist) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Methadon*, a contraction of *Methylaminopropionon* (methylaminopropanone), from *Methyl* (methyl) + *Amino* (amino) + *Propion* (propionyl) + *-on* (ketone suffix).  Coined in Germany during World War II.
