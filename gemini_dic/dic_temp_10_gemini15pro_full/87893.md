hierarchical: /ˌhaɪəˈrɑːrkɪkəl/ (US), /ˌhaɪəˈrɑːkɪkəl/ (UK)| adj.|等级的，分层的|arranged in order of rank or importance|例句：The company has a hierarchical structure, with the CEO at the top.  这家公司有一个等级森严的结构，首席执行官位居顶层。

例句：Feudal society was rigidly hierarchical. 封建社会等级森严。

近义词：stratified, layered, graded, ranked, ordered|反义词：non-hierarchical, egalitarian|词性: adjective
