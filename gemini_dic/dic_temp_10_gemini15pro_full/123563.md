monsoon: /mɒnˈsuːn/ (UK) /mɑːnˈsuːn/ (US)| n.|季风；雨季|A seasonal prevailing wind in the region of South and Southeast Asia, blowing from the southwest between May and September and bringing rain (the wet monsoon ), or from the northeast between October and April (the dry monsoon ).|The monsoon season brings heavy rainfall to India.  印度的季风季节带来了大量的降雨。|The heavy monsoon rains caused widespread flooding. 暴雨季风造成了大范围的洪水。|近义词: rainy season|反义词: dry season|词性:noun

monsoons: /mɒnˈsuːnz/ (UK) /mɑːnˈsuːnz/ (US)| n.|季风（复数）|Plural form of monsoon.|The monsoons are vital for agriculture in many parts of Asia. 季风对亚洲许多地区的农业至关重要。|The changing patterns of the monsoons are a cause for concern. 季风模式的变化令人担忧。|近义词: rainy seasons|反义词: dry seasons|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Portuguese monção, from Arabic mawsim ‘season’, perhaps influenced by Malay musim.
