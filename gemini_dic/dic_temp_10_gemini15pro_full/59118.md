dynamic:/daɪˈnæmɪk/ | adj. n. |动态的，有活力的；动力，动态 | characterized by constant change, activity, or progress; a force that stimulates change or activity within a system or process |  The city has a dynamic and vibrant nightlife.  这座城市拥有充满活力和生机的夜生活。 | synonyms: energetic, vigorous, active, forceful, powerful | antonyms: static, inert, passive, inactive | adjective

dynamic:/daɪˈnæmɪk/ | n. |动力，动态 |  a force that stimulates change or activity within a system or process | The dynamics of the team were affected by the new manager. 团队的活力受到了新经理的影响。 | synonyms: force, power, energy, momentum | antonyms: stagnation, inertia | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
