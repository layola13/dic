nominees:/ˌnɑːmɪˈniːz/
n.
被提名者；候选人
A person who has been nominated for an award, position, or honor.
The nominees for Best Picture were announced. 最佳影片的提名名单已经公布。
The nominees for the award all gave impressive speeches.  该奖项的提名者都发表了令人印象深刻的演讲。
近义词: candidates, contenders, applicants
反义词: electors, voters
