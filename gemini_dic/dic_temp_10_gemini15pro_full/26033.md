bushelers:/ˈbʊʃələrz/
n.
计量员（复数）,按蒲式耳计量者（复数）
People who measure or deal with bushels of grain or other produce.
The bushelers weighed the wheat before loading it onto the truck. 计量员在将小麦装上卡车之前称了重。
The bushelers were busy during the harvest season.  在收获季节，计量员们都很忙。
