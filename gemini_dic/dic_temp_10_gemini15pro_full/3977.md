aguaje:/ɑːˈɡwɑːxeɪ/ | n. |  阿瓜赫果，莫鲁iche棕榈果 | A fruit from the Mauritia flexuosa palm tree, native to the Amazon rainforest, known for its oily pulp and distinct flavor.  It is often used in juices, ice cream, and other desserts, and is believed to have various health benefits. |  I tried aguaje ice cream for the first time and it was surprisingly delicious. 我第一次尝试了阿瓜赫冰淇淋，味道出奇的好。
