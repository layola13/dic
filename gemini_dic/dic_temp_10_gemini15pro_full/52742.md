dilacerate: /dɪˈlæsəreɪt/ /daɪˈlæsəreɪt/
verb
撕裂；割碎；使…极为痛苦
To tear or rend apart violently; to distress deeply.
The lawyer dilacerated the witness's testimony. 律师驳斥了证人的证词，使其支离破碎。
His heart was dilacerated by grief.  他悲痛欲绝。
近义词:mangle, tear, rip, rend, torture, torment
反义词:soothe, comfort, heal, mend
词性:v.

词源: From Latin dilacerāre (“to tear to pieces”), from di- (“apart”) + lacerāre (“to tear, rend”).
