cryingly:/ˈkraɪɪŋli/| adv.|哭着地，含泪地|in a way that involves crying.  She told him cryingly that she had lost her job.|例句：She walked away cryingly. 她哭着走开了。

例句：He spoke cryingly about his lost dog.  他哭着诉说着他丢失的爱犬。|近义词：tearfully, weepingly, sobbingly|反义词：smilingly, laughingly|词性:副词
