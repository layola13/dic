| Example 2: The politician's cacodoxical stance on the issue confused many voters.  这位政治家在这个问题上自相矛盾的立场让许多选民感到困惑。 | Synonyms: paradoxical, contradictory, inconsistent, illogical, self-contradictory | Antonyms: logical, consistent, harmonious, orthodox | 词性: 形容词
