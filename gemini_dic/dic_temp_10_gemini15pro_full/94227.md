imperialize:/ɪmˈpɪəriəlaɪz/ | v. | 帝国化，使帝国主义化 | To extend the rule or authority of an empire or nation over foreign countries, or to acquire and hold colonies and dependencies. | The British imperialized much of Africa in the 19th century.  19世纪英国将非洲的大部分地区帝国主义化了。|  colonize, conquer, subjugate | liberate, free | verb

imperialize:/ɪmˈpɪəriəlaɪz/ | v. |  给…强加…的观点，使…带有帝国主义色彩 | To impose the beliefs, values, or way of life of one country or group onto another, often by force or coercion. |  Some critics argue that Western nations are trying to imperialize the developing world with their economic policies. 一些批评家认为西方国家正试图用其经济政策将发展中国家帝国主义化。 | impose, dictate, enforce | respect, accommodate | verb
