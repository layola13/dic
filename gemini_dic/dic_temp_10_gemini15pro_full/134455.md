nonjailable:/ˌnɒnˈdʒeɪləbl/
adjective
不可监禁的
Not subject to imprisonment; that cannot or should not be punished by imprisonment.
The offense was deemed nonjailable, resulting in a fine.  这个罪行被认为是不可监禁的，因此处以罚款。
His actions, while unethical, were nonjailable. 他的行为虽然不道德，但不足以构成监禁。
Synonym:non-custodial, non-imprisonable
Antonym:jailable, imprisonable
词性:形容词
