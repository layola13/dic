longing:/ˈlɒŋɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈlɔːŋɪŋ/ (US)| n. adj.|渴望，向往；渴望的事物|A strong feeling of wanting something or someone.|She felt a longing for her homeland. 她对家乡充满了渴望。|近义词：yearning, craving, desire, wish|反义词：indifference, apathy|noun

longing:/ˈlɒŋɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈlɔːŋɪŋ/ (US)| adj.|渴望的，热切的|Having or expressing a strong desire for something or someone.|He gave her a longing look. 他给了她一个渴望的眼神。|近义词：yearning, wistful|反义词：indifferent, apathetic|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *langung, from langian "to yearn," from Proto-Germanic *langōną, from the root *langaz "long." The sense development is from "to grow long" to "to stretch out after something" to "to yearn."
