nonperpetuities:/ˌnɒnpərˈpɛtʃuɪtiz/
|n.| 非永久性财产; 非永续年金|A nonperpetuity is a stream of cash flows that does not continue forever, unlike a perpetuity which is a theoretical stream of cash flows that continues indefinitely.|The company's investments in short-term bonds are considered nonperpetuities. 该公司对短期债券的投资被认为是非永久性财产。| Nonperpetuities are essential to understand when valuing certain financial instruments. 理解非永久性财产对于评估某些金融工具至关重要。|Synonyms: finite annuities, temporary annuities|Antonyms: perpetuities|noun
