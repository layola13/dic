devastate:/ˈdevəsteɪt/| vt.| 摧毁，毁灭；使震惊|To destroy or ruin something completely; to cause someone to feel extreme emotional pain.|The hurricane devastated the coastal town.  飓风摧毁了沿海城镇。|近义词：ruin, destroy, wreck, shatter|反义词：build, construct, create|verb

devastation:/ˌdevəˈsteɪʃn/| n.| 毁灭，破坏；极度悲伤|Great destruction or damage; overwhelming grief.|The earthquake caused widespread devastation. 地震造成了大范围的破坏。|近义词：destruction, ruin, havoc|反义词：construction, creation|noun

devastated:/ˈdevəsteɪtɪd/| adj.| 震惊的，极度悲伤的|Extremely upset and shocked.|She was devastated by the news of her friend's death. 她被朋友去世的消息震惊了。|近义词：heartbroken, grief-stricken, shattered|反义词：elated, joyful|adjective
