diacope:/daɪˈækəpiː/ | n. |间隔反复|A rhetorical device involving the repetition of a word or phrase with one or more intervening words.|  "Free at last, free at last, thank God almighty, free at last." - Martin Luther King Jr.| 近义词:repetition, epizeuxis, conduplicatio | 反义词:N/A | 词性:noun

Etymology: From the Greek διακοπή (diakopē) meaning "a cutting in two, interruption, break," from διακόπτειν (diakoptein), "to cut through, interrupt."
