guest:/ɡɛst/| n.|客人，宾客；旅客，房客|A person who is invited to visit or stay in someone's home or other place.|例句：We have guests coming for dinner tonight. 今晚我们有客人来吃晚饭。

例句：She was a guest at the wedding. 她是婚礼上的客人。|近义词：visitor, company|反义词：host|noun
