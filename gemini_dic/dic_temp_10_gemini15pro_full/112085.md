Longinus: /lɒnˈdʒaɪnəs/ | n. | 隆吉努斯 |  Referring to Cassius Longinus, a Roman soldier who pierced the side of Jesus Christ with a spear.  It can also refer to the pseudo-Longinus, the unknown author of *On the Sublime*. |  Longinus is mentioned in the Gospel of John. 隆吉努斯在约翰福音中被提到。| Synonyms:  Centurion (in some contexts), Spear-bearer (in some contexts) | Antonyms: N/A | noun
