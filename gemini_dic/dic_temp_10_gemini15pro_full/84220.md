half-russian:/ˌhɑːfˈrʌʃən/ | adj. | 半俄罗斯的；一半是俄罗斯血统的 |  Someone who is half-Russian has one parent who is Russian and one parent of another nationality.  | Example: He's half-Russian, his mother is from Moscow. 他有一半俄罗斯血统，他母亲来自莫斯科。

Example:  She's a half-Russian actress who speaks fluent English and Russian.  她是一位有一半俄罗斯血统的女演员，能说流利的英语和俄语。 | 近义词: part-Russian | 反义词: full-blooded Russian | 词性: adjective
