depoliticization:/diːpəˌlɪtɪsaɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.|去政治化，非政治化|The process of removing political influence or considerations from a subject or institution.  The depoliticization of education is crucial for fostering objective learning.|The depoliticization of the central bank is essential for its credibility. 中央银行的非政治化对其信誉至关重要。|近义词:neutralization, objectification|反义词:politicization|词性:名词

Etymology:  From de- (prefix meaning "removal, reversal") + politicization (from politic + -ization).  Ultimately from Greek "politikos" (of citizens or the state).
