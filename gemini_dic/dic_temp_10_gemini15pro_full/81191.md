grandiose:/ˈɡrændiəʊs/ (UK), /ˈɡrændioʊs/ (US)| adj.|宏伟的，堂皇的，浮夸的|Impressive and imposing in appearance or style, especially pretentiously so.|例句：He had grandiose plans to build a huge mansion. 他有一个宏伟的计划，要建造一座巨大的豪宅。|近义词：magnificent, imposing, pompous, ostentatious|反义词：humble, modest, simple, understated|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From French *grandiose*, from Italian *grandioso*, from *grande* "large, great" (from Latin *grandis*) + *-oso* (from Latin *-osus*).
