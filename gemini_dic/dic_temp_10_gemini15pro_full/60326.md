egoical:/ˌiːɡoʊˈɪkəl/ | adj. | 自我中心的，自负的 |  Centered on the self; conceited; selfish.  Characterized by egoism. | He was so egoical, always talking about his own achievements. 他太自负了，总是谈论自己的成就。 |  He made an egoical display of his wealth. 他炫耀性地展示了他的财富。| Near Synonyms:  self-centered, narcissistic, conceited, vain, arrogant, self-absorbed, egotistic | Antonyms: humble, modest, selfless, altruistic | Adjective
