gazunderer:/ɡæzˈʌndərər/
|n.| 降价购房者；压低房价的买主 |Someone who reduces the amount of their offer for a house just before the exchange of contracts, often causing the seller significant problems.| The gazunderer managed to knock £10,000 off the asking price.  这个压低房价的买主设法将要价降低了1万英镑。| 近义词: bargain hunter | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "overbidder") | 词性: noun


gazunder:/ˌɡæzˈʌndər/
|vt.| 压低房价；临时杀价| To reduce the price offered for a house just before contracts are exchanged. | The buyer tried to gazunder the seller at the last minute. 买家试图在最后一刻压低卖家的价格。|近义词: undercut, lowball | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "overbid") |词性: verb
