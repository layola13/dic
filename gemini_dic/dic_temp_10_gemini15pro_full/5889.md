ambery: /ˈæmbəri/ (US), /ˈæmbəri/ (UK) | adj. | 琥珀色的，琥珀味的 | Of a yellowish-orange colour, like that of amber; having a smell or taste suggestive of amber. | The beer had a rich, ambery glow.  这啤酒有着浓郁的琥珀色光泽。 | The room was lit by the ambery light of the setting sun.  房间里洒满了落日余晖的琥珀色光芒。 | 近义词: golden, yellow, amber-coloured | 反义词: dark, colourless | 词性: 形容词
词源: From amber + -y.  源自 amber（琥珀） + -y（形容词后缀）。
