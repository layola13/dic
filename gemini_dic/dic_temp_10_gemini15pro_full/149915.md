palladiums:/pəˈleɪdiəmz/
noun
钯（金属元素），保护物，保障
a rare silvery-white metallic element of the platinum group, used in jewellery and as a catalyst.  Something that provides protection or security.
Palladiums are used in catalytic converters in cars. 钯用于汽车的催化转化器。
The country's constitution is a palladium of liberty.  这个国家的宪法是自由的保障。
synonyms: safeguard, bulwark, protector, shield
antonyms: danger, threat, risk
