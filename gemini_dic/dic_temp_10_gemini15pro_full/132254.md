nondecoration:/ˌnɒndekəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 非装饰;  无装饰;  朴素 | Absence of decoration; plainness. | The architect opted for a nondecoration style, emphasizing functionality over ornamentation.  这位建筑师选择了非装饰风格，强调功能性而非装饰性。 | The room's nondecoration gave it a minimalist feel.  房间的朴素风格给人一种极简的感觉。 | 近义词: plainness, simplicity, austerity | 反义词: decoration, ornamentation, embellishment | 词性: 名词

Etymology: non- (prefix meaning "not") + decoration (from Latin decorare "to adorn")
