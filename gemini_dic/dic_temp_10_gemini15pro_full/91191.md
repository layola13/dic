hydraulic:/haɪˈdrɔːlɪk/ | adj. n.|水力的，液压的；液压系统|Operated by the pressure of a fluid, typically water or oil.  Relating to or using hydraulics.  A piece of machinery or equipment operated by the pressure of a liquid. |例句：The car's brakes are hydraulic. 这辆车的刹车是液压的。|近义词：pneumatic, fluid-powered|反义词：manual, mechanical|词性：adjective, noun

hydraulic:/haɪˈdrɔːlɪk/ | n.|液压装置；液压系统|A piece of machinery or equipment operated by the pressure of a liquid.  The system of using pressurized liquid to operate machinery.|例句：The hydraulics in the crane failed, causing a serious accident. 起重机的液压系统失灵，导致了严重事故。|近义词：fluid power system|反义词：N/A|词性：noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *hydraulique*, from Latin hydraulicus, from Greek hydraulikos (“of water organ”), from hydr-, stem of hydōr (“water”) + aulos (“pipe, tube”).
