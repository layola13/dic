confoundingly:/kənˈfaʊndɪŋli/ | adv. | 令人困惑地，使人惊讶地 | in a way that causes surprise or confusion; surprisingly |  The results were confoundingly inconclusive. 结果令人困惑地没有定论。| synonym: surprisingly, bafflingly, bewilderingly, puzzlingly | antonym: clearly, understandably, plainly | Word type: Adverb

confound:/kənˈfaʊnd/ | vt. | 使困惑，使惊讶，挫败 | to cause surprise or confusion in (someone), especially by acting against their expectations |  The sudden change in policy confounded investors. 政策的突然变化使投资者感到困惑。| synonym: confuse, bewilder, baffle, frustrate | antonym: clarify, enlighten, explain | Word type: Verb
