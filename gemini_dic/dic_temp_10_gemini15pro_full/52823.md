diluter:/daɪˈluːtər/ /dɪˈluːtər/| n.|稀释剂，稀释器|A substance used to dilute something, typically a solvent.|The chemist added a diluter to the concentrated acid. 化学家在浓酸中加入了稀释剂。|近义词：thinner, weakening agent|反义词:concentrator, thickener|词性:noun
dilute:/daɪˈluːt/ /dɪˈluːt/| v. |稀释，冲淡|Make (a liquid) thinner or weaker by adding water or another solvent to it.|She diluted the juice with water. 她用水稀释了果汁。|近义词：thin, weaken, water down|反义词:concentrate, thicken|词性:verb
dilute:/daɪˈluːt/ /dɪˈluːt/| adj.|稀释的，冲淡的|Made thinner or weaker by having had water or another solvent added.|The paint was too dilute, so I added more pigment. 油漆太稀了，所以我加了更多颜料。|近义词：thin, weak, watered-down|反义词:concentrated, thick|词性:adjective
