billfish:/ˈbɪlfɪʃ/| n. |长嘴鱼，尖嘴鱼|A fish having a long projecting upper jaw, such as a marlin, sailfish, or spearfish.  A predatory fish with a long, pointed bill.|例句：The fisherman landed a huge billfish after a long struggle. 渔夫经过长时间的搏斗，钓到了一条巨大的长嘴鱼。

例句：Billfish are prized game fish. 长嘴鱼是珍贵的垂钓鱼。|近义词：marlin, sailfish, spearfish, swordfish|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps small fish, or bottom feeder)|词性:noun
