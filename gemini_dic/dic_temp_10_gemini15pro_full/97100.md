infestive:/ɪnˈfɛstɪv/
adjective
感染性的; 引起感染的; 寄生虫性的
Causing or transmitting infection; parasitic.
例句：The infestive stage of the parasite is when it enters the host.  寄生虫的感染阶段是它进入宿主的时候。

例句：The contaminated water supply proved infestive, leading to a widespread outbreak of illness. 受污染的水源证明具有感染性，导致疾病的广泛爆发。
近义词：infectious, contagious, parasitic
反义词：non-infectious, non-contagious
词性：adjective
词源：From Latin infestus ("hostile, troublesome") + -ive (forming adjectives).
