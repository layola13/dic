inclusiveness:/ɪnˈkluːsɪvnəs/| n.|包容性，包容度|The quality of including all groups of people and treating them fairly and equally. |Our goal is to create a culture of inclusiveness where everyone feels welcome. 我们的目标是创造一种包容的文化，让每个人都感到受欢迎。|近义词：acceptance, tolerance, diversity, integration|反义词:exclusiveness, discrimination, intolerance|noun
