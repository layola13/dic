oscillating:/ˈɒsɪleɪtɪŋ/ /ˈɑːsɪleɪtɪŋ/| adj.|振荡的，摆动的，摇摆不定的，犹豫不决的|Moving back and forth repeatedly between two points, or varying regularly in magnitude or position. Also, fluctuating between two different emotional states or opinions.|The oscillating fan provided a welcome breeze.  振荡风扇带来了一阵凉风。|fluctuating, swinging, vibrating, wavering|fixed, stable, steady|adjective


oscillate:/ˈɒsɪleɪt/ /ˈɑːsɪleɪt/| v.|振荡，摆动，摇摆不定，犹豫不决|To move back and forth repeatedly between two points, or vary regularly in magnitude or position. Also, to fluctuate between two different emotional states or opinions.|The pendulum oscillated back and forth. 钟摆来回摆动。|fluctuate, swing, vibrate, waver|fix, stabilize, steady|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *oscillāre*, frequentative of *oscillare* "to swing," from *oscillum* "swing."
