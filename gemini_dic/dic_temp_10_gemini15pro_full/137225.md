nonsurgical:/ˌnɒnˈsɜːdʒɪkəl/ /ˌnɑːnˈsɜːrdʒɪkəl/
adjective
非手术的
Not involving surgery; performed without cutting open the body.
She opted for a nonsurgical cosmetic procedure. 她选择了一种非手术的美容方法。
The doctor recommended nonsurgical treatment for his back pain. 医生建议对他背痛进行非手术治疗。
近义词:nonoperative, medical
反义词:surgical, operative
词性:adjective
