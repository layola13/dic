lychee:/ˈliːtʃiː/ /ˈlaɪtʃiː/
n.
荔枝
A small rounded fruit with a tough, red rind, sweet white flesh, and a large central stone.
例句：I love the sweet taste of lychee. 我喜欢荔枝的甜味。

例句：Lychees are a popular fruit in Southeast Asia. 荔枝在东南亚是一种很受欢迎的水果。

近义词：litchi
反义词: (No direct antonym for a fruit)
词性: noun
