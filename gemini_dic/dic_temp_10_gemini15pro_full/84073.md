half-hollow:/ˌhæf ˈhɒləʊ/ (UK) /ˌhæf ˈhɑːloʊ/ (US)| adj.|半空的，半凹的|Partially hollow; having a cavity or indentation that doesn't go all the way through.  It refers to something that is not completely hollow but has a hollowed-out section or a reduced thickness in part of its structure. |The guitar had a half-hollow body, giving it a warm, resonant tone. 这把吉他有一个半空心的琴身，赋予它温暖而洪亮的音色。

The half-hollow design of the surfboard made it lighter and more maneuverable.  冲浪板的半空心设计使其更轻，更易操作。|Near synonyms: semi-hollow, partially hollow|Near antonyms: solid, filled|adjective
