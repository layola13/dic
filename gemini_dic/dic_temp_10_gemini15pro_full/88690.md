Example 2: He's become a real hobbers when it comes to woodworking. 他成了一个真正的木工爱好者。 | Near synonyms: enthusiasts, aficionados, devotees | Antonyms:  professionals (if the hobby is their profession), dilettantes (if they are not serious) | noun
