bureaucrats: /ˈbjʊərəˌkræts/ | n. | 官僚 | Officials who work in a large administrative system; often used with a negative connotation, suggesting excessive adherence to rules and procedures. | The bureaucrats were slow to respond to the public's needs. 官僚们对公众的需求反应迟缓。| The city is run by bureaucrats. 这个城市由官僚们管理。 |  synonyms: officials, administrators, civil servants | antonyms:  entrepreneurs, innovators | noun

Etymology:
From French *bureaucrate*, from *bureau* (desk, office) + *-crate* (from Greek *kratēs*, power, rule).
