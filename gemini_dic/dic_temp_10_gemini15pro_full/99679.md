interlink:/ˌɪntərˈlɪŋk/ | v. n. |互相连接，互连；连接，环环相扣|to connect different parts of something together, or to be connected in this way| The chapters in the book interlink to form a cohesive whole. 这本书的章节相互关联，形成了一个紧密的整体。|Synonyms: connect, link, join, network|Antonyms: separate, disconnect, divide|verb

interlink:/ˌɪntərˈlɪŋk/ | n. |互相连接，互连；连接，环环相扣|a connection between different parts of something|The interlink between the two companies has strengthened their market position.  两家公司之间的联系加强了它们的市场地位。|Synonyms: connection, link, linkage|Antonyms: separation, division|noun
