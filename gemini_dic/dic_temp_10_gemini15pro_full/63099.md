enshrined:/ɪnˈʃraɪnd/| v. adj.|珍藏；铭记；奉为神圣|preserved or cherished as sacred|The right to free speech is enshrined in the First Amendment. 言论自由权被载入美国宪法第一修正案。|近义词：preserved, cherished, enshrined, protected, sacred, hallowed|反义词：forgotten, neglected, violated, desecrated|词性: 动词 (v.)，形容词 (adj.)

词源: From en- (meaning "in") + shrine (a holy place).  Ultimately from Latin *scrinarium "chest for books or papers," from scrinium "case, box."
