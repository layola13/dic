casually:/ˈkæʒuəli/| adv.|随意地，漫不经心地|In a relaxed or informal manner; without much thought or effort.|She dressed casually in jeans and a t-shirt. 她穿着随意，牛仔裤和T恤衫。|近义词：informally, nonchalantly, effortlessly|反义词：formally, deliberately, carefully|词性:副词
Etymology: From casual + -ly.  "Casual" comes from the Late Latin *casuālis, from Latin cāsus "a chance, accident, event".
