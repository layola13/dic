bookstores:/ˈbʊkstɔːrz/ (US), /ˈbʊkstɔːz/ (UK)
n.
书店（复数）
Shops that sell books.
Many independent bookstores have closed due to competition from online retailers. 许多独立书店由于来自网络零售商的竞争而倒闭。
Independent bookstores offer a unique browsing experience. 独立书店提供独特的浏览体验。

近义词:bookshops
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 library 图书馆)
词性:noun
