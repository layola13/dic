oliva:/oʊˈliːvə/| n.|橄榄，橄榄树，橄榄色|An evergreen tree cultivated for its edible fruit. Also refers to the fruit itself, which is a small, oval drupe with a hard stone, a bitter taste when fresh, and used as food and a source of oil.|She added a few olives to the salad. 她往沙拉里加了几颗橄榄。|近义词：olive tree|反义词:|noun
