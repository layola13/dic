back-up:/ˈbækʌp/ (US), /ˈbækʌp/ (UK)| n. adj. v.|备份，支持，后援|a copy of computer data made in case the original is lost or damaged; support or help; a traffic jam; (adjective) used to support or replace something else|例句：Remember to back up your files regularly. 记得定期备份你的文件。|近义词：reserve, copy, support, substitute|反义词:original, primary|词性:noun, adjective, verb

back up:/bæk ˈʌp/ (US), /bæk ˈʌp/ (UK)| v.|备份，倒车，支持|to make a copy of computer data; to move a vehicle backwards; to support or help someone|例句：Could you please back up your car a little?  你能把你的车往后倒一点吗？|近义词：reverse, support, second|反义词:advance, oppose|词性:verb

词源: "back" (meaning "rear" or "reverse") + "up" (indicating direction or completion).  The combined meaning relates to creating a secondary copy located "behind" or "in reserve" of the original.
