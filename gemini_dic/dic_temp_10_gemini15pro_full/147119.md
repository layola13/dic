overlively: /ˌoʊvərˈlaɪvli/ | adj. | 过于活泼的，过分活跃的 | Excessively lively; too energetic or spirited.  Characterized by an abundance of energy that can be disruptive or overwhelming. | The overlively puppy kept jumping on the guests. 这只过于活泼的小狗不停地跳到客人身上。

The overlively children were difficult to manage in the classroom. 这些过于活泼的孩子在教室里很难管教。| 近义词: boisterous, rambunctious, exuberant, energetic | 反义词: calm, quiet, subdued, placid | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "above, over") + "lively" (Middle English lifli, from Old English liflic, meaning "living, vital").  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning of "lively" to signify an excessive degree of activity.
