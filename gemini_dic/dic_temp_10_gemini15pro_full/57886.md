dries:/draɪz/
verb
变干，干燥
becomes dry; loses all moisture
The paint dries quickly in the sun.  油漆在阳光下干得很快。

近义词: evaporates, dehydrates
反义词: moistens, wets
词性: verb (third person singular present tense of dry)

dry:/draɪ/
adjective
干的；干燥的
free from moisture or liquid; not wet or moist
The ground is dry after the long drought.  长期的干旱之后，地面很干燥。

近义词: arid, parched
反义词: wet, damp
词性: adjective

dry:/draɪ/
verb
使…变干；弄干
to make something free from moisture or liquid
Please dry your hands before touching the book.  请在触摸书之前把手擦干。

近义词: dehydrate, desiccate
反义词: wet, soak
词性: verb

dry:/draɪ/
noun
干燥；干旱
a period of time during which there is little or no rain
The long dry has killed many crops.  长期的干旱导致许多农作物死亡。

近义词: drought, aridity
反义词: flood, downpour
词性: noun
