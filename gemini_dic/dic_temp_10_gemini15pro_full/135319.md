nonpaying:/ˌnɑːnˈpeɪɪŋ/ | /ˌnɒnˈpeɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 不支付的，不给报酬的 | Not paying; not providing any payment or financial return. |  Many landlords are struggling with nonpaying tenants. 许多房东都在为不付房租的租客而苦恼。| Synonyms: unpaid, delinquent, defaulting | Antonyms: paying, remunerative | adjective

nonpaying:/ˌnɑːnˈpeɪɪŋ/ | /ˌnɒnˈpeɪɪŋ/ | n. | 不付款的人或物 | A person or thing that does not pay.  (This usage is less common than the adjective form.) | The nonpaying customers were asked to leave the restaurant.  不付钱的顾客被要求离开餐厅。| Synonyms: defaulter, deadbeat | Antonyms: payer | noun
