gift-vouchers:/ˈɡɪft ˈvaʊtʃəz/
n.
礼品券；购物券
A gift voucher is a piece of paper that can be exchanged for goods in a particular shop.
She received a gift voucher for her birthday. 她生日收到了一张礼品券。
I'm going to use this gift voucher to buy a new dress. 我打算用这张礼品券买一件新裙子。
近义词:gift certificate, gift card
反义词:cash
