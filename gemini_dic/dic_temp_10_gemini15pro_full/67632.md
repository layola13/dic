extremist:/ɪkˈstriːmɪst/
n.|极端主义者；极端分子|A person who holds extreme political or religious views, especially one who resorts to or advocates extreme action.|The extremist group claimed responsibility for the bombing. 极端主义组织声称对爆炸事件负责。|近义词：fanatic, radical, zealot|反义词：moderate, centrist|词性: 名词

extremist:/ɪkˈstriːmɪst/
adj.|极端的；过激的|Holding extreme political or religious views.|His extremist views alienated many of his former supporters.  他的极端观点疏远了许多他以前的追随者。|近义词：radical, extreme, fanatical|反义词：moderate, reasonable|词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *extrémiste*, from *extrême* (extreme) + *-iste* (-ist). Ultimately from Latin *extrēmus* (outermost).
