macadamia: /ˌmækəˈdeɪmiə/ | n. |澳洲坚果，澳洲坚果树|A tree native to Australia, bearing edible nuts with a hard shell.  The nut of this tree, rich in oil and having a delicate flavour.|例句：Macadamia nuts are expensive but delicious. 澳洲坚果很贵，但很好吃。

例句：She loves to snack on macadamia nuts. 她喜欢吃澳洲坚果作为零食。|近义词：Queensland nut, bush nut |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
