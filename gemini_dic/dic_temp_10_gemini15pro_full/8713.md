anticultural:/ˌæntiˈkʌltʃərəl/
adj.
反文化的，反对文化的
Opposing or hostile to the prevailing culture or social norms.
His anticultural attitudes often led to clashes with his family. 他的反文化态度经常导致与家人发生冲突。
The punk rock movement was seen as anticultural by many in the 1970s.  在20世纪70年代，朋克摇滚运动被许多人视为反文化运动。

近义词:countercultural,  iconoclastic,  subversive,  nonconformist
反义词:pro-cultural,  conventional,  traditional,  orthodox
词性:adjective
