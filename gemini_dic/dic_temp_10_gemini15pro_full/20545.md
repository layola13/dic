blabbiest:/ˈblæbiɪst/| adj.|最饶舌的，最爱唠叨的|Superlative form of blabby: most blabby.  Meaning excessively talkative, especially about unimportant matters.|例句：She was the blabbiest person in the office, always gossiping about everyone. 她办公室里最饶舌，总是说每个人的闲话。

例句：He's the blabbiest guy I know; he can't keep a secret to save his life. 他是我认识的最爱唠叨的人；他根本守不住秘密。|近义词：talkative, garrulous, loquacious, voluble|反义词：quiet, reserved, reticent|词性:形容词
词源: From blab + -iest (superlative suffix).  "Blab" originates from imitative Middle English  "blabbe" meaning to chatter.
