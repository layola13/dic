expugnation:/ˌekspʌɡˈneɪʃən/| n. |攻克，征服，夺取|The act of taking a city or fortress by force; conquest.|The expugnation of the enemy's stronghold took months of planning and execution. 攻克敌人的要塞耗费了数月的计划和执行。|近义词：capture, conquest, seizure, overthrow, subjugation|反义词：surrender, liberation, relinquishment|词性:名词

expugnation:/ˌekspʌɡˈneɪʃən/| n. |（从书籍等中）删除，删去|The act of removing or deleting something, especially from a book or other written work.|The expugnation of controversial passages from the textbook sparked outrage among scholars. 从教科书中删除有争议的段落激起了学者们的愤怒。|近义词：deletion, removal, excision, erasure, obliteration|反义词：insertion, addition, inclusion|词性:名词
