flayed:/fleɪd/
| v. |剥皮，严厉批评|To strip off the skin or outer covering of something; to criticize harshly. |The hunter flayed the deer. 猎人剥了鹿的皮。|The critic flayed the new movie in his review.  评论家在他的评论中严厉批评了这部新电影。|skin, excoriate, castigate|cover, praise|verb


flayed:/fleɪd/
| adj. |被剥皮的，被严厉批评的|Having had the skin or outer covering stripped off; having been harshly criticized.|The flayed carcass of the animal lay in the field. 动物被剥皮的尸体躺在田野里。|The author felt flayed after the scathing review. 作者在受到严厉的评论后感到被剥皮了一样。|skinned, excoriated, castigated|covered, praised|adjective
