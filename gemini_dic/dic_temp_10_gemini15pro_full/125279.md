multifetation: /ˌmʌltɪfɪˈteɪʃən/
|n.|多胎妊娠；多胎分娩|The development of two or more fetuses in the same uterus.|She had a multifetation pregnancy and gave birth to triplets.|近义词: multiple pregnancy, multiple birth|反义词: singleton pregnancy|词性: 名词

词源:  From multi- (many) + fetus (offspring) + -ation (forming nouns of action or process).
