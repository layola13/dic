misvaluing:/ˌmɪsˈvæljuɪŋ/
verb
低估；错误估价
Underestimating or incorrectly assessing the worth or value of something.
The company was misvaluing its assets, leading to financial difficulties.  这家公司低估了其资产，导致了财务困难。
He misvalued her friendship, taking it for granted. 他低估了她的友谊，认为这是理所当然的。
synonyms: undervaluing, underestimating, underappreciating, slighting
antonyms: overvaluing, overestimating, appreciating, valuing
词性: 动词
