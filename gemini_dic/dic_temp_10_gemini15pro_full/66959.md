experiment:/ɪkˈspɛrɪmənt/ (US), /ɪkˈspɛrɪmənt/ (UK)| n. v. |实验，试验；尝试|A scientific procedure undertaken to make a discovery, test a hypothesis, or demonstrate a known fact. |例句：Scientists conducted an experiment to test the new drug. 科学家们进行了一项实验来测试这种新药。|近义词：test, trial, research|反义词：observation, theory|noun

experiment:/ɪkˈspɛrɪmənt/ (US), /ɪkˈspɛrɪmənt/ (UK)| v. |做实验，进行试验；尝试|To carry out a scientific procedure, especially in a laboratory, to make a discovery, test a hypothesis, or demonstrate a known fact. |例句：They experimented with different materials to find the strongest one.  他们用不同的材料进行试验，以找到最坚固的材料。|近义词：test, try, explore|反义词：observe, theorize|verb
