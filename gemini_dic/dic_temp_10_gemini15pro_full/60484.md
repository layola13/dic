如果指的是 "what"，则解析如下：

what: /wɒt/ (British) /wʌt/ (American) | pron. det. adv. | 什么，多么 | Used to ask for information about something. |
例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

例句：What do you want for dinner? 你晚饭想吃什么？

近义词：which, how, whatever | 反义词: (无明确的反义词) | 词性: 代词 (pronoun), 限定词 (determiner), 副词 (adverb)

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What does he do for a living? = 他做什么工作？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? = 她正在做什么晚餐？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has he done with the money? = 他用这笔钱做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has she been studying at university? = 她一直在大学里学习什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 聚会上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? = 她参加聚会时穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you left? = 你离开之前做了什么？
What had they discussed before the meeting? = 会议前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I arrived? = 我到达之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been working on before she quit her job? = 她辞职前一直在做什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? = 你下周末要做什么？
What will happen if I press this button? = 如果我按下这个按钮会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time tomorrow? = 明天这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting? = 会议上他们会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你会完成什么？
What will have changed by then? = 到那时会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
