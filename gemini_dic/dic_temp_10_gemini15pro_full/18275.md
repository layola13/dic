believe: /bɪˈliːv/ (UK) /bəˈliːv/ (US)| v. |相信，认为|To accept as true or real; to have confidence in the truth or existence of something or someone.| I believe you. 我相信你。| synonyms: trust, accept, credit | antonyms: doubt, disbelieve, question | verb

Etymology: From Middle English _bileven_, from Old English _belēfan_  ("to believe, trust, have confidence in"), from _be-_ (an intensifying prefix) + _lēfan_ ("to allow, permit, believe").  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *laubjaną ("to allow, believe").
