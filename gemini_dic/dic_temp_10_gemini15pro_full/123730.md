moonset:/ˈmuːnˌset/| n.|月落|The time when the moon sets below the horizon.|We watched the moonset over the ocean. 我们看着月亮落入海平面。|近义词: moon sinking, lunar setting|反义词: moonrise|词性:名词

Etymology:
From moon + set (verb).  The verb "set" in this context means to descend below the horizon.
