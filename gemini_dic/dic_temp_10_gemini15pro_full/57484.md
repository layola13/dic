drag:/dræɡ/| v. n.|拖，拉；缓慢移动；乏味的事；阻力|To pull something along the ground, often with difficulty; something that is tedious and boring; something that slows down progress.|例句：She dragged the heavy suitcase across the floor. 她吃力地把沉重的行李箱拖过地板。|近义词：pull, haul, tow, tug, draw|反义词：push, shove, carry|词性：动词，名词

drag:/dræɡ/| n.|阻力;累赘;令人厌烦的事物|Something that slows down movement or progress; a boring or tedious activity or person.|例句：The heavy traffic was a real drag. 繁忙的交通真让人心烦。|近义词：burden, hindrance, obstacle|反义词：help, advantage, boost|词性：名词
