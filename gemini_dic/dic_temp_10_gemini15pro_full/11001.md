arboretums:/ɑːrbəˈriːtəmz/
n.
植物园；树木园
A place where many different trees, shrubs, and other plants are grown for scientific or educational purposes.
The university maintains a beautiful arboretum with hundreds of species of trees. 大学维护着一个美丽的植物园，里面有数百种树木。
gardens, parks
zoos, museums
noun
