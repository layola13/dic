emigration: /ˌemɪˈɡreɪʃn/ | n. | 移民，移居国外 | The act of leaving one's own country to settle permanently in another; moving abroad. |  Many Irish people chose emigration during the potato famine.  许多爱尔兰人在马铃薯饥荒时期选择移民。| synonyms: migration, exodus, departure | antonyms: immigration, repatriation, return | noun
