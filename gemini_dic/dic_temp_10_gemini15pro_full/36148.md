coexistent:/ˌkoʊɪɡˈzɪstənt/| adj.|共存的，同时存在的|Existing at the same time or in the same place.|The coexistent belief systems created a diverse culture. 这些共存的信仰体系创造了多元的文化。|Coexisting indigenous tribes maintained peaceful relations.  共存的土著部落保持着和平的关系。|synonym: concurrent, simultaneous, contemporary|antonym: separate, distinct, nonconcurrent|adjective

coexistence:/ˌkoʊɪɡˈzɪstəns/| n.|共存，共处|The state of existing at the same time or in the same place.|Peaceful coexistence between different ethnic groups is essential. 不同种族群体之间的和平共处至关重要。|The coexistence of traditional and modern values shaped the society. 传统价值观与现代价值观的共存塑造了这个社会。|synonym: concurrence, simultaneity, contemporaneity|antonym: separation, distinction, nonconcurrence|noun
