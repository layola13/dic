gomuti:/ɡəˈmuːti/| n.| 桄榔树，桄榔纤维|A palm tree (Arenga pinnata) of Southeast Asia, yielding a strong fiber, a sweet sap, and a starchy pith.|The gomuti palm is a valuable source of fiber. 桄榔树是宝贵的纤维来源。|The bridge was built using strong gomuti ropes. 这座桥是用结实的桄榔绳建造的。|近义词：sugar palm, aren palm|反义词: N/A|noun
