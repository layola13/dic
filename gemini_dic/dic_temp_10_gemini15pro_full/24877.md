brrr:/brɜːr/ (US) /brɜː/ (UK)| interjection | 寒冷的声音；（表示寒冷的）打颤声 |  An exclamation used to indicate one is cold. |  Brrr, it's freezing out here!  天啊，外面冷死了！| 近义词: shiver, shudder | 反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider words expressing warmth like "ahh" or "ooh" when referring to a pleasant temperature) | 词性: interjection
