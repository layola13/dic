cosovereign:/ˈkoʊˈsɑːvərɪn/
adjective
共同主权的; 共同拥有主权的
Sharing sovereign power; jointly possessing supreme authority.
The two nations acted as cosovereign entities in the disputed territory.  这两个国家在争议领土上作为共同主权实体行事。
The cosovereign states agreed on a joint defense policy.  这些共同拥有主权的国家同意了一项联合防御政策。
近义词: joint sovereign, shared sovereign
反义词: subordinate, dependent
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "co-" (together, jointly) + "sovereign" (possessing supreme power).  "Co-" comes from the Latin "com-" (with, together). "Sovereign" comes from the Old French "soverain," ultimately from the Latin "superanus" (supreme).
