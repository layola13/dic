coopering:/ˈkuːpərɪŋ/
n.
制桶业，桶匠工艺
The art or craft of making or repairing barrels, casks, and other wooden containers.
He learned coopering from his father. 他从他父亲那里学到了制桶工艺。
近义词：barrel-making, cask-making
反义词: 无
词性: 名词


cooper:/ˈkuːpər/
n.
桶匠
A person who makes or repairs barrels, casks, and other wooden containers.
The cooper carefully sealed the wine barrel. 桶匠小心地封住了酒桶。
近义词：barrel-maker, cask-maker
反义词: 无
词性: 名词

词源：
From Middle English *couper, from Old English *cūpere, from Late Latin *cūpārius (“barrel-maker”), from Latin cūpa (“tub, cask, vat, barrel”).


注意事项：
虽然cooper既可以指桶匠，也可以指制桶业，但通常用coopering指制桶业，用cooper指桶匠。
