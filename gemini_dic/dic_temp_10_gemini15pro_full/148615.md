overtheatrically: /ˌoʊvərθiˈætrɪkli/
adverb
过于戏剧化地；装腔作势地
in a way that is too theatrical; exaggeratedly dramatic.
She reacted overtheatrically to the news, clutching her chest and gasping for air. 她对这个消息反应过于戏剧化，捂着胸口，大口喘气。
Synonyms: melodramatically, histrionically, dramatically, exaggeratedly
Antonyms: subtly, understatedly, naturally
词性: 副词
