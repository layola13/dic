outfiguring:/ˌaʊtˈfɪɡərɪŋ/
verb
胜过，在…方面超过
surpassing in calculation or ingenuity; exceeding in figuring or calculating.  This implies outsmarting or exceeding someone in a way that involves planning, strategy, or cleverness. It's not just about being better, but about being more cunning or resourceful.
He outfigured his opponents in the complex negotiations, securing a much better deal. 他在复杂的谈判中智胜对手，达成了更有利的协议。
The chess master outfigured his young challenger, forcing him into a checkmate.  这位国际象棋大师以智取胜，迫使年轻的挑战者将死。
近义词: outsmarting, outmaneuvering, surpassing, exceeding
反义词: being outwitted, being outsmarted, being surpassed
