optimise:/ˈɒptɪmaɪz/ (UK) /ˈɑːptɪmaɪz/ (US)| v. |优化，使最优化|To make something as good as possible.  To make the best or most effective use of (a situation, opportunity, or resource).|We need to optimise our website for mobile devices. 我们需要优化我们的网站以适应移动设备。

The company seeks to optimise its production processes. 该公司试图优化其生产流程。


|近义词：improve, enhance, refine, perfect, maximize|反义词: worsen, impair, degrade|词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *optimus ("best") + -ize.
