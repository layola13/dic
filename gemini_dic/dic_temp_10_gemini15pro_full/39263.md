congregators:/ˈkɒŋɡrɪɡeɪtəz/ (US) /ˈkɒŋɡrɪɡeɪtəz/ (UK)
noun
集会者，会众
People who gather together, especially for religious worship.
The church welcomed new congregators to the service. 教堂欢迎新的会众参加礼拜。
synonyms: worshippers, parishioners, attendees, assembly, gathering
antonyms: individuals, loners, hermits
词性:n.
