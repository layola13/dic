as-large-as-life:/ˌæz ˈlɑːrdʒ əz ˈlaɪf/
adjective
栩栩如生的；原原本本的；和真人一样大小的
Exactly as one really is; behaving in a very typical way.  Appearing to be full of energy and confidence.  Sometimes also means literally as big as in real life.
例句：There she was, as large as life, standing in the doorway. 她就在那儿，活生生地站在门口。

例句：The statue of the president was as large as life. 总统的雕像和真人一样大小。

近义词：real, genuine, authentic, true-to-life, lifelike, in person, in the flesh
反义词：unrealistic, artificial, subdued, quiet
词性：adjective
