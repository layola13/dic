nonarithmetically:/ˌnɒnˌærɪθˈmetɪkli/
adverb|非算术地，非算法地|Not in an arithmetical or algorithmic way; in a way that does not involve calculation or numerical computation. |The problem is solved nonarithmetically, using logical reasoning.  这个问题是用逻辑推理非算术地解决的。|The system arranges data nonarithmetically. 系统以非算术方式排列数据。| 近义词:logically, qualitatively|反义词: arithmetically, computationally|词性:副词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin, meaning "not"
arithmetic: from Late Latin arithmetica, from Greek arithmētikē (tekhnē) "(art) of counting," from arithmos "number"
