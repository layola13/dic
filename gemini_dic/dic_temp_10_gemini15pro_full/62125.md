enchylemma:/enˈkaɪˌlɛmə/ | n. | 乳糜管 | The thin-walled lymphatic vessel in the intestinal villus that absorbs fats is called the enchylemma. |  The enchylemma plays a vital role in the absorption of dietary fats.  肠绒毛中吸收脂肪的薄壁淋巴管称为乳糜管。 | 近义词: lacteal | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Ancient Greek ἐν (en) "in" + χυλός (khulos) "juice, chyle" +  -lemma "husk, sheath, covering"
