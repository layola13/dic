copulatory:/ˈkɒpjʊlət(ə)ri/ /ˈkɑːpjəleɪtɔːri/
adjective
交配的；交尾的
Relating to or involving copulation; of or for copulation.
The male frog grasps the female in a copulatory embrace. 雄蛙以交配的姿势抱住雌蛙。
Near Synonyms:  mating,  reproductive,  sexual,  venereal
Antonyms:  asexual, non-reproductive
词性: adjective
