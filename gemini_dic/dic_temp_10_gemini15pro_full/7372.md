animalcular: /ˌænɪˈmælkjələr/
adjective
微小的，由微生物组成的
Relating to, or consisting of, minute organisms called animalcules.
Example:  The scientist peered through his microscope at the animalcular life teeming in the drop of pond water. 科学家透过显微镜观察一滴池塘水中丰富的微生物。

Example:  The 18th-century theory proposed that all life was generated from animalcular seeds. 18世纪的理论认为所有生命都是由微生物种子产生的。

近义词: microscopic, tiny, minute
反义词: macroscopic, huge, giant
词性: adjective

词源: From French *animalcule*, diminutive of animal, from Latin animal, meaning “living being.”  The suffix -cular derives from Latin -culus, a diminutive suffix.
