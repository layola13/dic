anglomanias:/ˌæŋɡləˈmeɪniəz/
noun
崇英症；对英国文化的过度喜爱
An excessive admiration or imitation of English customs, institutions, or manners.
Many upper-class Russians in the 19th century suffered from anglomanias, adopting English fashion and language. 19世纪许多俄罗斯上层阶级患有崇英症，采用英国的时尚和语言。
synonyms: Anglomania, Anglophilia
antonyms: Francophobia, Germanophobia
