in-transit:/ˌɪn ˈtrænzɪt/
adjective
运输中的，在途的
Being transported; in the process of being moved from one place to another.
The package is currently in-transit and is expected to arrive tomorrow. 包裹目前正在运输中，预计明天到达。
Goods in-transit are insured against damage or loss. 运输途中的货物已投保了损坏或损失险。
近义词:in transit, on the way, en route, being shipped, being transported
反义词:delivered, received, arrived, stationary
词性:adjective


in-transit:/ˌɪn ˈtrænzɪt/
adverb
在运输中，在途中
While being transported; during the process of being moved from one place to another.
The goods were damaged in-transit. 货物在运输途中损坏了。
The documents are in-transit from the head office. 文件正在从总部运送过来。
近义词:en route, on the way, mid-journey, in the process of being shipped
反义词:at rest, stationary, delivered, received
词性:adverb


in transit:/ˌɪn ˈtrænzɪt/
noun
在途，运输中
The state of being transported; the process of being moved from one place to another.
The goods were held in transit due to customs issues. 由于海关问题，货物被扣留在运输途中。
The company monitors all its shipments in transit. 公司监控所有在途货物。
近义词:transit, shipment, transportation, conveyance
反义词:storage, warehouse, depot, arrival
词性:noun


词源: From Latin "in transitu" (in passage).
