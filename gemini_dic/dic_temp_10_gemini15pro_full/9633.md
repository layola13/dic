antistriker:/ˈæntiˌstraɪkər/
noun
反罢工者，反对罢工的人
Someone who is opposed to strikes.
The antistriker spoke out against the union's planned walkout. 反罢工者公开反对工会计划的罢工。
The company hired antistrikers to keep production going. 公司雇佣了反罢工者来维持生产。
synonyms: strikebreaker, scab
antonyms: striker, unionist, labor activist
