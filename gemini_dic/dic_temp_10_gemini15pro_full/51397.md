determinedness:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪndnɪs/
n.
决心；坚定；果断
The state of being determined; firmness of purpose.
She showed great determinedness in pursuing her goals. 她追求目标表现出极大的决心。
近义词：determination, resolve, firmness, tenacity
反义词：hesitation, indecision, vacillation
词性: 名词

Etymology: From determined + -ness.  "Determined" comes from the Middle French *determiner*, from Latin determinare "to limit, fix, bound," from de- "completely" + terminare "to mark the end or boundary."
