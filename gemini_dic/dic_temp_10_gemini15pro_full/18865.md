beseen:/biː ˈsiːn/
verb phrase
被看见；被注意到
to be noticed or observed by other people
例句：She wants to be seen as a strong leader. 她希望被视为一位强有力的领导者。

例句：It's important to be seen to be doing something about the problem.  重要的是要让人们看到你正在为解决这个问题做些事情。

近义词：noticed, observed, perceived
反义词：ignored, overlooked, unseen
词性：动词短语
