dampers:/ˈdæmpərz/ (US), /ˈdæmpəz/ (UK)| n.|减震器; 阻尼器; 制音器; (情绪的)抑制因素|A damper is a device that reduces vibrations or oscillations, or something that subdues or restrains.|The car's dampers need replacing. 这辆车的减震器需要更换。|synonyms: shock absorber, muffler, mute, suppressor, restraint|antonyms: amplifier, accelerator, stimulant|noun

dampers:/ˈdæmpərz/ (US), /ˈdæmpəz/ (UK)| n.| (炉灶等的)风门; 气流调节器|A damper is a valve or plate that regulates the draft in a stove, furnace, or other heating device.|Close the damper to reduce the heat.  关上风门以减少热量。|synonyms: vent, regulator|antonyms: none|noun

dampers:/ˈdæmpərz/ (US), /ˈdæmpəz/ (UK)| n.| (钢琴等的)制音器|A damper is a device in a piano or other musical instrument that stops the strings from vibrating.|The piano's dampers need adjusting. 这架钢琴的制音器需要调整。|synonyms: mute, silencer|antonyms: none|noun
