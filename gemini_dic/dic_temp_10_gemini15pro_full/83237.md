gymnasiarch: /ˌdʒɪmˈneɪziɑːrk/ | n. | 体育馆长；运动会主管 | An official in ancient Greece in charge of a gymnasium or athletic exercises.  The gymnasia played a central role in Greek society, and the gymnasarch held a position of considerable importance. |  The gymnasia offered various sports and exercises, and the gymnasarch was responsible for overseeing the facility and its daily operations. 体育馆提供各种体育运动和锻炼，体育馆长负责监督设施及其日常运作。


The gymnasarch was often a wealthy and respected member of the community.  体育馆长通常是社区中富有且受人尊敬的成员。|Synonym: athletic director, sports administrator | Antonym:  N/A (No direct antonym) | Noun
