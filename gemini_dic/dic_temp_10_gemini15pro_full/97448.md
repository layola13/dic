infusibility:/ˌɪnfjuːzəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可熔性；不能注入性
The quality of being infusible; incapable of being fused or melted.
The infusibility of the material makes it ideal for high-temperature applications.  这种材料的不可熔性使其成为高温应用的理想选择。
The scientist tested the infusibility of the new alloy. 科学家测试了这种新合金的不可熔性。
近义词:non-fusibility, incombustibility, resistance to melting
反义词:fusibility, meltability
词性:noun

Etymology:
From in- (not) + fusible + -ity (suffix forming nouns of quality or state)
Ultimately from Latin fundere (to melt, pour).
