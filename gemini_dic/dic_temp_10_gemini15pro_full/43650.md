creatress:/kriˈeɪtrɪs/
n.
女创造者；女性创始人
A female who creates something; a female author, artist, or founder.
Emily Dickinson was a prolific creatress of poetry. 艾米莉·狄金森是一位多产的诗歌创作者。
The creatress of the company stepped down after 20 years. 公司的女创始人20年后卸任了。
近义词: creator, maker, author, originator, founder
反义词: destroyer
