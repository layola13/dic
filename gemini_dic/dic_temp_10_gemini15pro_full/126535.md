nabobisms:/ˈneɪbɒbɪzəmz/ | n. |  奢华行为；暴发户作风 |  Extravagant behavior or habits characteristic of a nabob; ostentatious display of wealth.  Derived from "nabob," referring to a person of great wealth, especially one who has returned from India after making a fortune. | He was known for his nabobisms, such as driving a gold-plated car and throwing lavish parties. 他以其奢华行为而闻名，例如驾驶镀金汽车和举办奢华派对。 | synonyms: extravagance, ostentation, lavishness, prodigality | antonyms: frugality, modesty, simplicity, austerity | noun
