conflictual: /kənˈflɪktʃuəl/
adjective
冲突的，充满冲突的
Characterized by conflict; involving or causing conflict.
The team dynamic was highly conflictual, with constant disagreements and power struggles.  团队的动态充满了冲突，不断出现分歧和权力斗争。
The two countries have a long and conflictual history. 这两个国家有着漫长而充满冲突的历史。
近义词:contentious, antagonistic, hostile,  disputatious
反义词:harmonious, peaceful, cooperative, amicable
词性:adjective

Etymology: From conflict + -ual.  "Conflict" comes from the Latin "conflictus," past participle of "confligere," meaning "to strike together."
