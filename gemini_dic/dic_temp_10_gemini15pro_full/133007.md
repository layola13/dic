nonentailed:/ˌnɒnɪnˈteɪld/
adjective
非继承的，非限定继承的
Not entailed or necessarily following as a consequence.  This property is nonentailed to the main estate. 这处房产不属于主要遗产的限定继承范围。
The judge ruled that the terms of the contract were nonentailed. 法官裁定合同条款并非限定继承。
近义词:unlinked, unconnected, separate
反义词:entailed, inherited, bequeathed
词性:adjective
