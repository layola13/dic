doming:/ˈdoʊmɪŋ/| n.|穹顶，圆顶，蛋壳形|The process of forming a dome shape, often in jewelry or on a watch crystal.|The watchmaker expertly performed the doming of the crystal, giving it a smooth, curved surface.  制表师熟练地完成了水晶的穹顶制作，使其表面光滑弯曲。|近义词：arching, rounding, crowning|反义词:flattening|noun

doming:/ˈdoʊmɪŋ/| v.|使成圆顶状|The act of shaping something into a dome.|They are doming the roof of the new stadium. 他们正在把新体育场的屋顶做成圆顶状。|近义词：arching, rounding, crowning|反义词:flattening|verb
