misestimate:/ˌmɪsˈɛstɪmeɪt/ | v. | 误判，错误估计 | To estimate incorrectly; underestimate or overestimate. |  I misestimated the time it would take to complete the project. 我错误地估计了完成这个项目所需的时间。

|  She misestimated his reaction to the news. 她错误地估计了他对这个消息的反应。 | 近义词: miscalculate, misjudge, underestimate, overestimate | 反义词: estimate correctly, judge accurately | verb
