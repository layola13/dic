omniety: /ɒmˈnɪɪti/
noun
无所不知; 全知
The state of knowing everything.  This word is not in common usage and is considered archaic.
He aspired to a state of omniety, believing he could one day possess all knowledge. 他渴望达到无所不知的状态，相信有一天他能拥有所有的知识。
Near Synonyms: omniscience, all-knowingness
Antonyms: ignorance, nescience
词性: noun
