indulgentness:/ɪnˈdʌldʒəntnəs/| n. |放任，沉溺|The act of indulging; gratification of desire.|Her indulgentness in rich desserts led to weight gain. 她对甜点的沉溺导致体重增加。|Synonyms: gratification, indulgence, pampering, self-indulgence|Antonyms: self-denial, abstinence, restraint, moderation|noun
