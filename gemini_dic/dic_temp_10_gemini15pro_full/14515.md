aventine:/ˈævənˌtaɪn/| n.|阿文廷山，阿文廷的|One of the seven hills of Rome, south of the Palatine.  Also used to refer to a withdrawal or seclusion, especially from political affairs.|He retreated to his country estate, seeking an Aventine peace from the turmoil of the city.|近义词：seclusion, retreat, withdrawal|反义词：engagement, involvement|noun
