imponderable:/ɪmˈpɒndərəbl/ (British), /ɪmˈpɑːndərəbl/ (American)| adj. n.|无法衡量的，难以估量的；不可估量的事物|Something that is imponderable is impossible to estimate or assess with certainty.|The imponderables of the situation made it difficult to predict the outcome.  这种情况的难以预料因素使得很难预测结果。|The future is full of imponderables. 未来充满了不确定性。|近义词:unforeseeable, unpredictable, incalculable, uncertain|反义词:predictable, calculable, certain, definite|词性:形容词, 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin imponderabilis, from in- 'not' + ponderabilis 'weighable', from pondus 'weight'.
