encrinitic:/ˌɛŋkrɪˈnɪtɪk/ | adj. | 海百合的；由海百合化石组成的 | Composed of or containing crinoid remains; resembling a crinoid. | The encrinitic limestone was full of fossilized sea lilies.  这块海百合灰岩充满了海百合化石。 | Synonyms: crinoidal | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider non-fossiliferous or non-calcareous) | word type: adjective

encrinitic:/ˌɛŋkrɪˈnɪtɪk/ | n. | 海百合灰岩 | A limestone composed largely of crinoid remains. | The quarry yielded a large quantity of encrinitic. 这个采石场出产大量的海百合灰岩。 | Synonyms: crinoidal limestone | Antonyms: (No direct antonym) | word type: noun
