exprobratory:/ɪkˈsprɑːbrətɔːri/
adjective
责备的；斥责的；表示谴责的
Expressing strong disapproval or reproach.
His exprobratory tone made it clear he was deeply disappointed. 他的责备语气清楚地表明他非常失望。
The teacher gave the student an exprobratory look for disrupting class. 老师因学生扰乱课堂秩序而给了他一个谴责的眼神。
近义词:reproachful, condemnatory, censuring, critical, disapproving
反义词:laudatory, complimentary, praising, approving
词性:adjective

词源:
From Latin *exprobrātōrius, from exprobrō (“I reproach”) + -ātōrius.
