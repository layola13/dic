buntine:/ˈbʌntɪn/| n.|彩旗；（尤指船上用的）信号旗；（澳大利亚俚语）吵闹的聚会；喧闹的庆祝活动|A flag, especially a signal flag, typically used on ships. Also (Australian slang) a noisy party or celebration.|例句：The sailors hoisted the buntine to signal their arrival. 水手们升起彩旗，示意他们已经到达。

例句：They had a real buntine to celebrate their victory. 他们举行了一个真正的狂欢来庆祝他们的胜利。|近义词：flag, banner, pennant, celebration, party, festivity|反义词:|noun
