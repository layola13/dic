outgushing:/ˌaʊtˈɡʌʃɪŋ/
adjective
迸发的，涌出的，喷涌而出的
Flowing out rapidly and in large quantities.
Her outgushing enthusiasm was contagious. 她喷涌而出的热情很有感染力。
Synonyms: effusive, gushing, overflowing, torrential
Antonyms: restrained, reserved, controlled
词性: 形容词

outgush:/ˌaʊtˈɡʌʃ/
verb
涌出，喷出
To flow out suddenly and in large quantities.
Lava outgushed from the volcano.  熔岩从火山中喷涌而出。
Synonyms: erupt, spurt, gush, spew
Antonyms: seep, trickle, drip
词性: 动词

词源:
From out- + gush.  "Out-" prefix indicating outward movement or direction. "Gush" meaning to flow out suddenly and copiously.
