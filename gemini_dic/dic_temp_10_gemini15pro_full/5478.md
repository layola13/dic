alonenesses:/əˈloʊnnɪsɪz/
n.
孤独，寂寞
The state of being alone; instances or experiences of being alone.
She experienced profound alonenesses after her husband's death.  她丈夫去世后，她经历了深深的孤独。
The book explores the alonenesses of modern life. 这本书探讨了现代生活的孤独感。
近义词: loneliness, isolation, solitude
反义词: togetherness, companionship
