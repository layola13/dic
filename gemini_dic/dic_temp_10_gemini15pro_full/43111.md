coxic:/ˈkɒksɪk/| adj.|尾骨的，髋骨的|Relating to the coccyx (tailbone) or hip. |The doctor diagnosed coxic pain. 医生诊断为尾骨疼痛。|Synonym: coccygeal|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps cranial)|Etymology: From Latin "coccyx," ultimately from Greek "kokkyx" meaning "cuckoo" (due to the bone's resemblance to a cuckoo's beak).|Part of Speech: Adjective
