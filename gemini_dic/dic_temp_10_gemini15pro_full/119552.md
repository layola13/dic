microsporic:/ˌmaɪkrəˈspɔːrɪk/
adjective
小孢子的；产生小孢子的
Relating to or producing microspores.  A microspore is a small spore that develops into a male gametophyte.
Microspores are found in plants that have heterospory.
The microsporic plants released clouds of pollen. 这些小孢子植物释放出花粉云。
Microspores develop into the male gametophyte, which produces sperm cells.  小孢子发育成雄配子体，产生精子细胞。

近义词:heterosporous
反义词:megasporic

词性:adjective
