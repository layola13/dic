antiquities:/ænˈtɪkwɪtiz/
| n. | 古物；古董；古代文物 | objects, buildings, or works of art from the past | The museum has a vast collection of Roman antiquities.  这家博物馆收藏了大量的罗马文物。 | synonyms: relics, artifacts, ancient objects | antonyms: modern artifacts, contemporary objects | noun

antiquity:/ænˈtɪkwɪti/
| n. | 古代；古物；古董 | the ancient past, especially the period of classical and other human civilizations before the Middle Ages |  The vase is of great antiquity.  这个花瓶年代久远。| synonyms: ancient times, the past, old times | antonyms: modernity, present day, contemporary times | noun
