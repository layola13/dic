dismisser:/dɪˈsmɪsər/ | n. | 解雇者，驳回者 |  A person who dismisses someone or something (from employment, consideration, etc.). | The dismisser of the claim refused to offer any explanation.  解雇索赔的人拒绝提供任何解释。|  Synonyms: terminator, discharger | Antonyms: hirer, employer | noun

dismisser:/dɪˈsmɪsər/ | n. | 轻视者，不予重视者 | A person who treats something as unimportant or unworthy of attention. | He was a dismisser of all new ideas, preferring the traditional ways. 他对所有新想法都不屑一顾，更喜欢传统的方式。| Synonyms:  belittler, scoffer | Antonyms:  supporter, advocate | noun
