butyrone:/ˈbjuːtɪˌroʊn/ | n. | 二丙酮, 4-庚酮 |A colorless liquid ketone, C7H14O, having a peppermint-like odor, used as a solvent and in organic synthesis. | Butyrone is used as a solvent for nitrocellulose, resins, and gums. 二丙酮被用作硝化纤维素、树脂和树胶的溶剂。 | Butyrone is a precursor to other chemicals. 二丙酮是其他化学品的前体。 | synonym:  dipropyl ketone, 4-heptanone | antonym: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From butyro- + -one.  Butyro- comes from Latin butyrum "butter," from Greek boutyron.  -one is a suffix denoting a ketone.
