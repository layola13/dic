scissors: /ˈsɪzərz/ | n. | 剪刀 | A cutting instrument consisting of two blades joined by a pivot, typically used for cutting cloth, paper, or hair. |  I used scissors to cut the paper. 我用剪刀剪纸。| synonyms: shears, clippers, snips | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a tool. Could consider "joiner," "glue," or "tape" as opposites in function) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *cisoires, plural of cisoire (“cutting instrument”), from Latin cisorium (“cutting instrument”), from cisus, past participle of caedere (“to cut”).
