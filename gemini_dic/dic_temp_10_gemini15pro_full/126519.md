naal:/nɑːl/ | adv. prep.|与...一起，偕同，用...|with, along with, using (especially in Punjabi and other South Asian languages). It indicates accompaniment or the instrument used for an action.|例句：He went to the market naal his brother. 他和他兄弟一起去市场了。|近义词：with, along with, alongside|反义词:without, apart from|词性:adverb, preposition

naal:/nɑːl/ | n.|鞋钉，蹄铁|a metal plate nailed to the bottom of a shoe or boot to protect it, especially a horseshoe.|例句：The blacksmith fitted new naals to the horse's hooves. 铁匠给马蹄装上了新的蹄铁。|近义词：horseshoe, shoe plate|反义词:None|词性:noun
