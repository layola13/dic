beret:/ˈbɛreɪ/ (US), /ˈbɛreɪ/ (UK)| n.|贝雷帽|A soft, round, flat-crowned hat, typically made of felt or knitted wool.|She wore a bright red beret. 她戴着一顶鲜红色的贝雷帽。|近义词：cap, hat|反义词: None|词性:noun
Etymology: From French *béret*, from Béarnese *berret*, from Vulgar Latin *birrus* (hooded cloak), from Gaulish *birros*.
