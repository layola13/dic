frontest: /ˈfrʌntɪst/
| adj. (non-standard) | 最前面的，最前方的 |  A non-standard and generally considered incorrect superlative form of "front." It attempts to express the idea of being furthest forward, but "frontmost" or simply "front" are preferred. | *"I wanted the frontest seat in the bus," he said, incorrectly.*  “我想坐在公交车的最前面，”他说的不对。 | 近义词: frontmost, foremost, leading | 反义词: rearmost, hindmost, last | 词性: adjective
