frescoers:/ˈfrɛskoʊərz/
noun
壁画画家（复数）
Painters who create frescoes.  Those who work with or restore frescoes.
The frescoers carefully prepared the wall before applying the pigments. 壁画画家在涂颜料之前仔细地准备了墙壁。
The team of frescoers meticulously restored the damaged artwork. 壁画修复团队一丝不苟地修复了受损的艺术品。
Synonyms: muralists, artists, painters
Antonyms: N/A (lack of a direct opposite)
