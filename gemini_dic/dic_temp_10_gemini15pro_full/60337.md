egomania:/ˌiːɡəʊˈmeɪniə/ /ˌiːɡoʊˈmeɪniə/| n.| 自我迷恋，极端利己主义| An obsessive preoccupation with oneself and one's own needs; excessive vanity and self-importance. | He suffers from egomania and believes he is the most important person in the world. 他患有极端利己主义，认为自己是世界上最重要的人。

|例句：His egomania prevented him from seeing the flaws in his plan. 他的极端自负让他看不到自己计划中的缺陷。
|例句：Her egomania alienated her from her friends. 她的极端自我迷恋使她与朋友疏远。


|近义词：narcissism, self-centeredness, vanity, conceit|反义词: humility, modesty|词性:noun
