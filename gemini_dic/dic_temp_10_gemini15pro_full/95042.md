inactivate: /ɪnˈæktɪveɪt/
verb|使不活动，使失效|to make something inactive or no longer effective|The vaccine inactivates the virus. 疫苗使病毒失效。|synonyms: deactivate, disable, neutralize|antonyms: activate, enable|词性:动词

词源:
in- (prefix): not, opposite of  (Latin)
activate (verb): to make active  (Latin: *activatus*, past participle of *activare* "to make active," from *activus* "active")
-ing (suffix): forms present participles and gerunds
