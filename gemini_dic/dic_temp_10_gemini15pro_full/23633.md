branchiostoma:/bræŋkiˈɒstəmə/
n.
文昌鱼
A small, translucent, fish-like marine animal that is a member of the subphylum Cephalochordata, also called an amphioxus.  It is considered a primitive chordate.
Branchiostoma are important for studying the origins of vertebrates. 文昌鱼对于研究脊椎动物的起源很重要。
Branchiostoma floridae is a common species found in the waters off Florida. 佛罗里达文昌鱼是在佛罗里达附近水域发现的常见物种。
