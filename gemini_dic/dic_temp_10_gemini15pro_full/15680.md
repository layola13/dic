baksheesh:/ˈbækʃiːʃ/ /bɑːkˈʃiːʃ/| n.|小费，赏钱，贿赂|A tip or gratuity, especially one given in countries where it is customary; also, a bribe.|例句：He offered the border guard a small baksheesh to speed up the process. 他给了边防警卫一点小费，以加快通关速度。

例句：The official accepted a hefty baksheesh to approve the project.  这位官员收受了巨额贿赂才批准了这个项目。
