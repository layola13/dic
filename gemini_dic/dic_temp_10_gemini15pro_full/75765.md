fulminic:/fʊlˈmɪnɪk/| adj.|雷酸的；易爆炸的；迅猛的|Relating to or containing fulminic acid, readily explosive; occurring suddenly and with great intensity.|例句：Fulminic acid is a highly unstable compound. 雷酸是一种极不稳定的化合物。

例句：His fulminic outburst surprised everyone. 他暴跳如雷的爆发让每个人都感到惊讶。|近义词：explosive, volatile, sudden, violent|反义词：stable, inert, gradual, calm|词性：形容词

Etymology: From French *fulminique*, from Latin *fulmin-*, stem of *fulmen* "lightning, thunderbolt," from *fulgere* "to flash, shine."
