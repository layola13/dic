| n.|分心；分散注意力|The act of distracting or the state of being distracted.|The noise was a constant distraction. 噪音一直让人分心。|synonyms: interruption, disturbance, diversion|antonyms: concentration, focus|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *distractio, from Latin distractus, past participle of distrahere (“to pull apart, divide, distract”), from dis- (“apart”) + trahere (“to draw, pull”).
