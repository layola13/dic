diaphonics:/ˌdaɪəˈfɑːnɪks/
n.
复音; 和声; 多音
the simultaneous sounding of two or more notes, especially in woodwind instruments, by altering the breath and lip pressure.
The piece explored the diaphonics of the bass clarinet. 这首曲子探索了低音单簧管的复音技巧。
近义词:multiphonics, overtones
反义词:monophonics
词性:名词

diaphonic:/ˌdaɪəˈfɑːnɪk/
adj.
复音的; 和声的; 多音的
relating to or characterized by diaphonics.
He experimented with diaphonic vocal techniques. 他尝试了复音的声乐技巧。
近义词:multiphonic
反义词:monophonic
词性:形容词
