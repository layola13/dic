dominance:/ˈdɒmɪnəns/ (British) /ˈdɑːmɪnəns/ (American)| n. |支配，优势，统治|A position of power, authority, or control over something or someone. |例句：The company's dominance in the market is unquestionable.  这家公司在市场上的主导地位无可置疑。

例句：Lions exhibit dominance through roaring and territorial marking. 狮子通过咆哮和领地标记来展现其统治地位。

近义词：supremacy, ascendancy, predominance, control, authority|反义词:subservience, subordination, weakness, inferiority|词性:noun
