blandishment:/ˈblændɪʃmənt/ | n. |  奉承；甜言蜜语；哄骗 | something that is said or done to persuade someone to do something, often with flattery |  She used every blandishment she could think of to get him to agree. 她用尽了她能想到的所有甜言蜜语来让他同意。 | synonyms: flattery, cajolery, coaxing, persuasion, allurement, enticement | antonyms: criticism, reprimand, condemnation, reproach | noun
