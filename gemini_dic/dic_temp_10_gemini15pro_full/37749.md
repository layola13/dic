compassed:/ˈkʌmpəst/ /kəmˈpæst/| v. |包围，围绕；完成，实现；理解，领会|To form a circle around; to enclose; to bring about; to achieve; to grasp mentally.|例句：The soldiers compassed the city. 士兵们包围了这座城市。|近义词：encircled, surrounded, achieved, accomplished, understood, grasped|反义词:failed, missed, misunderstood|verb

compassed:/ˈkʌmpəst/ /kəmˈpæst/| adj. (古语，罕用) |圆形的，环绕的|Having a circular or encompassing shape.|例句：The compassed shape of the ancient fort was still visible. 古堡的圆形轮廓仍然可见。|近义词：circular, rounded, encompassed|反义词:straight, linear|adjective
