cattles: /ˈkætəlz/ | n. (non-standard)|牛 (复数) |"Cattles" is a non-standard plural form of "cattle." "Cattle" itself is already plural, referring to a group of bovine animals.  Use "cattle" instead of "cattles." |The farmer had many cattle.  这个农民有很多牛。|近义词：cows, bulls, oxen, livestock|反义词: None (Cattle is a collective noun)|Noun
