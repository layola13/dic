organism:/ˈɔːrɡənɪzəm/ | n. |生物，有机体|A living thing, such as a plant, animal, or bacterium, that can function independently.|例句：The human body is a complex organism. 人体是一个复杂的生物体。

|例句：Single-celled organisms are the smallest forms of life. 单细胞生物是最小的生命形式。|近义词：being, creature, life form, individual|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑inanimate object 无生命物体) |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French organisme, from Greek organon "instrument, tool, organ,"  related to ergon "work."  The biological sense emerged in the late 18th century.
