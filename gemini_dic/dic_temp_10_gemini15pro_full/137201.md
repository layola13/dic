nonsuperimposable:/ˌnɒnˌsuːpərɪmˈpəʊzəbl/ /ˌnɑːnˌsuːpərɪmˈpoʊzəbl/| adj.| 不可重叠的 | Not able to be superimposed; not capable of being placed or laid over something else, typically a mirror image, so that all like parts coincide. |  Chiral molecules are nonsuperimposable on their mirror images. 手性分子与其镜像不可重叠。| 近义词: chiral, asymmetric | 反义词: superimposable, achiral | 词性: adjective
