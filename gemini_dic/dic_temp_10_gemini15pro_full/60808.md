electropainting:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈpeɪntɪŋ/
n.
电泳涂漆
The process of coating a conductive object with paint by immersing it in a tank of paint and applying an electric current.
The car parts went through the electropainting process for a uniform coat. 汽车零件经过电泳涂漆工艺以获得均匀的涂层。
Electrocoating, E-coating
--- (No direct antonym)
noun

electropainting:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈpeɪntɪŋ/
v.
电泳涂漆
To coat (a conductive object) with paint by immersing it in a tank of paint and applying an electric current.
We electropaint all our metal components for corrosion resistance. 我们对所有金属部件进行电泳涂漆以提高耐腐蚀性。
Electrocoat, E-coat
--- (No direct antonym)
verb
