antifundamentalists:/ˌæntiˌfʌndəˈmɛntəlɪsts/
n.
反基要主义者
Those who oppose religious fundamentalism.
Anti-fundamentalists advocate for a more liberal interpretation of religious texts.  反基要主义者主张对宗教文本进行更自由的解释。

The rise of anti-fundamentalist movements often coincides with periods of social change. 反基要主义运动的兴起通常与社会变革时期相吻合。
近义词:liberal, progressive, reformist
反义词:fundamentalist, traditionalist, orthodox
词性:noun
