paraterminal:/ˌpærəˈtɜːrmɪnəl/ | adj. |近端的；近末端的| Situated near the end or extremity, but not quite at the end.  It often refers to a location close to the terminal part of a structure, especially in biology or anatomy.|The paraterminal bodies are located near the posterior end of the insect's abdomen.|近义词:subterminal, juxtaminal |反义词: proximal, basal|词性:形容词

Etymology:
"para-" (Greek: beside, alongside) + "terminal" (Latin: terminus, boundary, limit)
