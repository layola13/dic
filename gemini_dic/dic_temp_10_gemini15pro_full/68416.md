falsifiabilities:/ˌfɔːlsɪfaɪəˈbɪlɪtiz/
|n.|可证伪性（复数）
|The quality of being able to be proven false; the susceptibility of a theory or hypothesis to being disproved by observation or experiment.|
|The scientist stressed the importance of falsifiabilities in scientific theories.|科学家强调了科学理论中可证伪性的重要性。
|falsifiability, testability, refutability|
|verifiability, confirmability|
|noun|
