fittest:/ˈfɪtɪst/| adj.|最合适的，最健康的|Most suitable or best adapted, especially for a particular purpose or environment; in the best physical condition.|Only the fittest survive in this harsh environment. 只有最适应环境的才能在这个恶劣的环境中生存下去。|Synonyms: strongest, healthiest, most capable, most qualified|Antonyms: weakest, unhealthiest, least suitable, least capable|adjective

fittest:/ˈfɪtɪst/| n.|最强者，最适合者|The most suitable or best adapted individual or thing.|The fittest of the species will pass on their genes.  该物种中最适应的将传递它们的基因。|Synonyms: champion, victor, winner, best|Antonyms: loser, weakest, failure|noun
