grubber: /ˈɡrʌbər/ | n. | 挖土机; 挖掘者; 搜寻者; 矮壮结实的人 | A tool or machine used for digging or grubbing up roots, weeds, etc.  A person who digs or searches diligently, often for food or other necessities. A short, stocky person. | The gardener used a grubber to remove the stubborn weeds. 园丁用挖土机清除顽固的杂草。|  The hungry children grubbed for food in the trash. 饥饿的孩子们在垃圾里寻找食物. | synonyms: digger, excavator, searcher, forager, prospector | antonyms: planter, sower, giver  | noun


grubber: /ˈɡrʌbər/ | v. | 挖; 搜寻; 翻找 | To dig or search diligently, especially for food or other necessities. |  He grubbed around in his pockets for some loose change. 他在口袋里翻找零钱. | synonyms: dig, search, forage, rummage, root | antonyms: plant, sow, bury | verb
