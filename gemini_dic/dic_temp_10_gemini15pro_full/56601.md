donatistic:/ˌdɒnəˈtɪstɪk/
adjective
多纳图斯的，多纳图斯派的
Relating to Donatism, a heresy in the early Christian Church in North Africa that maintained that the validity of sacraments depended on the moral character of the minister.
The Donatist controversy deeply divided the Christian community in the 4th century.  多纳图斯派的争论在四世纪深深地分裂了基督教社区。
The scholar specialized in Donatistic writings and their historical context.  这位学者专门研究多纳图斯派的著作及其历史背景。
Synonyms: schismatic, heretical, sectarian
Antonyms: orthodox, catholic
词性: adjective
