macerator: /ˈmæsəreɪtər/ | n. |浸渍器，研磨机| A machine that reduces something to a pulp or liquid by soaking or crushing.  It is often used in waste disposal and food processing.| The macerator quickly broke down the food waste.  浸渍器迅速分解了食物垃圾。| The hospital uses a macerator to dispose of medical waste. 医院用研磨机处理医疗废物。 | synonyms: grinder, pulverizer, crusher, mincer | antonyms:  -- | noun
