customizers:/ˈkʌstəˌmaɪzərz/
n.
定制者，自定义者
People or companies who customize products or services according to individual needs.
Many online stores offer product customizers that allow shoppers to personalize their purchases. 许多网店都提供产品定制工具，让顾客可以个性化他们的购买。
The car customizers transformed the old vehicle into a stylish hot rod. 汽车定制者将这辆旧车改装成了一辆时尚的hot rod。
