demiurgically:/ˌdɛmɪɜːrˈdʒɪkli/ | adv. | 以创世神的方式;像造物主一样 | In a manner resembling a demiurge; creatively, formatively. |  He demiurgically shaped the narrative, weaving together disparate threads into a compelling whole. 他以创世神般的方式塑造了叙事，将不同的线索编织成一个引人入胜的整体。 |  creatively, inventively, formatively | imitatively, derivatively | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From demiurge + -ically. Demiurge comes from Late Latin demiurgus, from Greek dēmiourgos "craftsman, artisan," from dēmios "of the people" (from dēmos "people") + -ergos "working" (from ergon "work"). -ically is a suffix forming adverbs from adjectives ending in -ic or -ical.
