distinguished:/dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃt/| adj.|卓越的，著名的，杰出的，高贵的|Having qualities that set one apart as superior or important; easily noticeable or recognized.|例句：He is a distinguished scientist. 他是一位杰出的科学家。|近义词：eminent, prominent, noted, celebrated, renowned, illustrious|反义词：ordinary, undistinguished, common, mediocre|词性:形容词
词源:Late Middle English: past participle of distinguisshen, from Old French distinguer, from Latin distinguere ‘separate by pricking’, from dis- ‘apart’ + stinguere ‘prick, extinguish’.
