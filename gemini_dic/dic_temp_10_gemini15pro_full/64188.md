equestrian:/ɪˈkwɛstriən/ /ɪˈkwɛstriən/| adj. n.|骑马的；骑术的；骑手|Of or relating to horseback riding.  A person who rides horses.|例句：She has always had an equestrian interest. 她一直对骑马很感兴趣。|例句：He is a skilled equestrian. 他是一位技艺娴熟的骑手。|近义词：rider, horseman, horsewoman|反义词:pedestrian|词性:adjective, noun
词源:From Latin *equester, from equus "horse".
