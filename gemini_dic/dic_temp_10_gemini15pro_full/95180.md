inaudibly:/ɪnˈɔːdəbli/| adv.|听不见地|Not able to be heard.|She whispered inaudibly, and I had to lean closer to hear. 她悄声细语，我不得不凑近才能听见。|The music was so soft as to be almost inaudibly.  这音乐太轻柔了，几乎听不见。|近义词：silently, quietly, softly, imperceptibly|反义词：audibly, loudly, clearly|词性:副词

词源:
From in- (not) + audibly (able to be heard).  "Audibly" comes from the Latin "audire" (to hear).
