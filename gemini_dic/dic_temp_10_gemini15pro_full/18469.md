benal:/bɪˈneɪl/ /ˈbiːn(ə)l/
adjective
平庸的；陈腐的；老套的
Lacking originality, freshness, or novelty; trite.
The plot of the movie was banal and predictable. 这部电影的情节平庸且可预料。
Her conversation was so banal that I nearly fell asleep. 她的谈话太陈腐了，我差点睡着了。
synonyms: trite, commonplace, hackneyed, clichéd, stereotyped, unoriginal, stale, vapid, insipid, jejune, pedestrian
antonyms: original, fresh, novel, creative, imaginative
词性: 形容词
