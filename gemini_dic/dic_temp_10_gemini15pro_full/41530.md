coronation:/ˌkɒrəˈneɪʃn/ (UK), /ˌkɔːrəˈneɪʃn/ (US)| n. |加冕，加冕礼|The ceremony of crowning a sovereign.|例句：The coronation of King Charles III was a grand spectacle. 查尔斯三世的加冕典礼是一场盛大的场面。

例句：The coronation will be held at Westminster Abbey. 加冕典礼将在威斯敏斯特教堂举行。|近义词：crowning, enthronement|反义词:abdication, deposition|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Old French *coronacion, from Latin coronationem, accusative of coronatio (“crowning, coronation”), from coronare (“to crown”), from corona (“crown”).
