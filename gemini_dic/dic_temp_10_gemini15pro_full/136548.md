nonsaccharinity:/ˌnɒnsækəˈrɪnɪti/ /ˌnɑːnsækəˈrɪnɪti/| n. |无糖性，不含糖|The absence of sugar or sweetness; not containing sugar.|The nonsaccharinity of the beverage made it a suitable choice for diabetics.  这种饮料的无糖特性使其成为糖尿病患者的合适选择。|Example: The nonsaccharinity of stevia makes it a popular sugar substitute. 甜菊糖的无糖特性使其成为一种流行的糖替代品。|Synonym: sugarlessness, unsweetened|Antonym: sweetness, saccharinity|noun
