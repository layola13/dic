fanaticalnesses:/fəˈnætɪkəlnəsɪz/
n.
狂热；极端；入迷
The state or quality of being fanatical; excessive enthusiasm or zeal.
His fanaticalnesses about the football team was well-known. 他对足球队的狂热是众所周知的。
The various fanaticalnesses of the cult members worried authorities.  该邪教成员的各种狂热行为令当局担忧。
近义词: fanaticism, zealotry, extremism
反义词: apathy, indifference, moderation
词性: 名词

Etymology: From fanatic + -al + -ness + -es (plural).  "Fanatic" originates from the Latin "fanaticus," meaning "inspired by a deity, frenzied, enthusiastic," ultimately derived from "fanum," meaning "temple."
