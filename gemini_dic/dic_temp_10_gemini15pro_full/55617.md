diswarn:/dɪsˈwɔːrn/
verb
取消警告，撤销警告
To retract a warning or caution.
The authorities diswarned the public after the threat subsided.  当局在威胁消退后取消了对公众的警告。
The meteorologist diswarned about the hurricane after its path changed. 气象学家在飓风路径改变后撤销了有关飓风的警告。
近义词: retract, revoke, rescind, cancel
反义词: warn, caution, alert
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing reversal) + warn.
