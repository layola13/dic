abus:/əˈbjuːs/| n. |滥用；虐待；辱骂|The abuse of power is a serious issue. 权力滥用是一个严重的问题。|例句：The child suffered terrible abuse. 这个孩子遭受了可怕的虐待。

The verbal abuse was hurtful and unacceptable. 言语辱骂是伤人的，不可接受的。|近义词：misuse, mistreatment, maltreatment, insult, vituperation|反义词:care, respect, kindness|词性:noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Drug abuse is a serious problem. = 滥用药物是一个严重的问题。
He abuses his position of power. = 他滥用职权。

一般过去时:
The children were abused by their caregivers. = 孩子们被他们的照顾者虐待。
He abused his privileges. = 他滥用了他的特权。

现在完成时:
She has been abused emotionally for years. = 她多年来一直遭受情感虐待。
He has abused his access to confidential information. = 他滥用了接触机密信息的权限。

过去完成时:
The witness testified that she had been abused as a child. = 证人作证说她小时候曾遭受过虐待。
He had abused his authority before he was fired. = 他在被解雇之前滥用了职权。

将来时:
If he continues to abuse his employees, he will be fired. = 如果他继续虐待他的员工，他将被解雇。
The system will be abused if proper safeguards are not put in place. = 如果没有适当的保障措施，该系统将被滥用。

被动语态:
The animals were abused and neglected. = 这些动物被虐待和忽视。
The system is being abused by corrupt officials. = 该系统正被腐败官员滥用。
