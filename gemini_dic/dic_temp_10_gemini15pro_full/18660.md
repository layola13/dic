beond: /biˈjɒnd/ /biˈɑːnd/ | prep. adv.| 超越，超过；在...的那边；除...之外| beyond repair 无法修理 |例句：The task is beyond my abilities. 这项任务超出了我的能力。|近义词：past, over, exceeding, outside, further than|反义词: within, inside|词性:介词，副词


Etymology:
From Middle English *behinde, from Old English begeondan (“beyond, on the other side of, further, more, after”), from be- (“by, near, at”) + geond (“yonder, there, beyond”). Cognate with Old Frisian biionda (“beyond”), Old Saxon bigundan (“beyond”), Middle Dutch bejonden (“beyond”), Old High German bigundan (“beyond”), German jenseits (“beyond”).
