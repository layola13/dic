antisophistry:/ˌæntiˈsɒfɪstri/
n.
驳斥诡辩；反诡辩术
The act of refuting or opposing sophism.  It refers to arguments or techniques used to expose and counter deceptive reasoning.
Her essay was a brilliant piece of antisophistry, dismantling the flawed logic of her opponent's argument. 她写的短文精彩地驳斥了诡辩，拆穿了对手论证中错误的逻辑。
Synonyms: counterargument, refutation, rebuttal
Antonyms: sophism, fallacy, casuistry
