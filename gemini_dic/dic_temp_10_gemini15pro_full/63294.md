enthusiasm:/ɪnˈθuːziæzəm/ /ɛnˈθuːziæzəm/| n.|热情，热忱，热心|A feeling of energetic interest in a particular subject or activity and an eagerness to be involved in it.|She has a great enthusiasm for music. 她对音乐充满热情。|近义词：passion, zeal, fervor, eagerness|反义词：apathy, indifference, coldness|词性:noun

enthusiastic:/ɪnˌθuːziˈæstɪk/ /ɛnˌθuːziˈæstɪk/| adj.|热情的，热心的|Having or showing intense and eager enjoyment, interest, or approval.|He gave an enthusiastic speech. 他发表了一场热情洋溢的演讲。|近义词：eager, ardent, passionate, zealous|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic|词性:adjective

enthusiastically:/ɪnˌθuːziˈæstɪkəli/ /ɛnˌθuːziˈæstɪkəli/| adv.|热情地，热心地|In a way that shows intense and eager enjoyment, interest, or approval.|The audience applauded enthusiastically. 观众热情地鼓掌。|近义词：eagerly, ardently, passionately, zealously|反义词：apathetically, indifferently, unenthusiastically|词性:adverb
