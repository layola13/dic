deforcement:/dɪˈfɔːrsmənt/
n.
非法剥夺; 强占; 驱逐; 阻止
The unlawful withholding of property from the rightful owner.
例句：The deforcement of the farmer from his land caused him great hardship.  农民被强占土地，这给他带来了巨大的困难。

例句：The king punished the baron for the deforcement of the castle. 国王惩罚了男爵对城堡的强占。

近义词：eviction, disseisin, ouster, deprivation
反义词：restitution, restoration

词性: 名词

词源: From Old French *deforcement*, from deforcer "to force away," from de- "away" + force "force."
