functionally:/ˈfʌŋkʃənəli/| adv.|功能上地，实际上，从功能上讲|In terms of its use or purpose; in effect, practically. |The system is functionally similar to the old one, but with updated technology. 该系统在功能上与旧系统相似，但技术更新了。|The design is aesthetically pleasing but functionally flawed.  这个设计美观但功能上有缺陷。|Synonyms: practically, effectively, essentially, in effect, operationally|Antonyms:  dysfunctionally, impractically, ineffectively|adverb

functionally:/ˈfʌŋkʃənəli/| adj.|功能性的|Designed to be practical and useful, rather than attractive. |The furniture was functionally designed, prioritizing comfort and storage. 这套家具以功能性设计为主，注重舒适性和存储空间。| This is a functionally furnished apartment. 这是一间陈设实用而不是为了好看的公寓。|Synonyms: practical, useful, utilitarian, pragmatic|Antonyms: decorative, ornamental, impractical|adjective
