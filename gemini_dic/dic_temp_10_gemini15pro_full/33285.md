what:/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US)|pron. det. adv.|什么，多么|Used to ask for information about something.|例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

例句：What a beautiful day! 多么美好的一天！|近义词：which|反义词: (无直接反义词)|pronoun, determiner, adverb

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? = 你怎么想？
What does she want for her birthday? = 她生日想要什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing this weekend? = 他这周末在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she eaten for lunch? = 她午饭吃了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 聚会上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you left? = 你离开之前做了什么？
What had they discussed before the decision was made? = 决定做出之前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们会在下次会议上讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by the end of the day? = 到今天结束时你将完成什么？
What will they have achieved by next year? = 到明年他们将取得什么成就？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for the past ten years by 2033? = 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时她将已经学习什么多久了？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
What would have happened if I hadn't been there? = 如果我不在那里会发生什么？

被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该做什么。
She doesn't understand what you're saying. = 她不明白你在说什么。
