albano:/ælˈbɑːnoʊ/ /ælˈbeɪnoʊ/
n.
阿爾巴諾（意大利城市名）; 阿尔巴尼亚人（尤指居住在意大利的）; 阿尔巴诺葡萄酒
A person from Albano Laziale, Italy, or of Albanian descent, especially one living in Italy.  A type of wine produced in the area of Albano Laziale.
He bought a bottle of Albano wine.  他买了一瓶阿尔巴诺葡萄酒。
The Albano family has lived in Italy for generations.  阿尔巴诺家族在意大利生活了好几代。

synonyms:Albanian (for the person),  wine (for the wine)
antonyms: None (for a place name or ethnicity).  For wine, perhaps non-alcoholic beverage.
