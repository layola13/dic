fretworks:/ˈfrɛtwɜːrks/
noun
镂空细工，格状装饰，复杂精细的装饰
Ornamental work consisting of intricate designs, often resembling latticework.  Typically made of wood or metal, characterized by elaborate and interlaced patterns.
The antique cabinet featured beautiful fretworks on its doors.  古董柜的门上有着美丽的镂空细工。
The guitar's soundhole was decorated with delicate fretworks. 吉他的音孔装饰着精致的格状装饰。
Synonyms: latticework, tracery, filigree, openwork
Antonyms: solid, plain, unadorned
