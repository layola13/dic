arioso:/ˌɑːriˈoʊsoʊ/ /ˌæriˈoʊsoʊ/
n. adj. adv.|抒情的；咏叹调的；如歌的|A short vocal piece, resembling an aria, often forming part of a larger work, or a passage or movement in this style.|The baritone launched into a rich arioso.|近义词：melodic, lyrical, cantabile|反义词：dissonant, jarring|noun, adjective, adverb
