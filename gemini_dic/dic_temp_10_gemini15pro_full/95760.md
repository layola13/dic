inconstancy:/ɪnˈkɒnstənsi/ | n. |反复无常，易变，不定性|Lack of firmness of purpose; changeability, especially in affections; fickleness.|Her inconstancy made it difficult to maintain a stable relationship. 她的反复无常使得维持一段稳定的关系变得很困难。|Synonyms: fickleness, volatility, capriciousness, instability|Antonyms: constancy, stability, steadfastness|noun


inconstant:/ɪnˈkɒnstənt/ | adj. |反复无常的，易变的，不定的|Not constant; subject to change; fickle.|The inconstant weather made it difficult to plan a picnic.  变化无常的天气使得计划野餐变得很困难。|Synonyms: fickle, volatile, capricious, erratic|Antonyms: constant, stable, steady, steadfast|adjective
