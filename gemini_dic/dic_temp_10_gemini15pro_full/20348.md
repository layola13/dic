bismuthal:/ˈbɪzməθəl/
adjective
铋的；含铋的
Containing or relating to bismuth.
Bismuthal compounds are often used in medicine. 铋化合物常用于医药。
近义词: bismuth-containing
反义词: (No specific antonym)
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From bismuth + -al.  Bismuth itself comes from the German "Wismut," likely derived from "weiße Masse" meaning "white mass."
