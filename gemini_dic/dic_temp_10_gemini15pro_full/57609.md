drastically:/ˈdræstɪkli/| adv.|彻底地，剧烈地|radically, severely, extremely; in a drastic manner.  Signifying a significant and often sudden change.|例句：The company downsized drastically, laying off half its workforce.  公司大幅裁员，解雇了一半员工。|近义词：severely, radically, extremely, significantly|反义词：slightly, moderately, gradually|词性:副词
词源: From the Greek word "drastikos," meaning "active" or "effective," via Late Latin "drasticus."
