lobotomize:/ˌloʊbəˈtɑːmaɪz/ | v. | 切除脑白质；实施脑白质切除术 | To perform a lobotomy on; to remove or sever the connections of the prefrontal lobes of the brain.  |  The doctor lobotomized the patient to treat his severe mental illness.  医生对病人进行了脑白质切除术以治疗他的严重精神疾病。 |  Example:  His aggressive behavior led doctors to consider lobotomizing him.  他的攻击性行为导致医生考虑对他进行脑白质切除术。 | Synonyms:  operate on, excise, sever, disable  |  Antonyms:  heal, cure, rehabilitate | verb

lobotomy:/ləˈbɒtəmi/ | n. | 脑白质切除术 |  A surgical operation involving incision into the prefrontal lobe of the brain, formerly used to treat mental illness. | The lobotomy left the patient with permanent brain damage.  脑白质切除术使病人永久性脑损伤。 | Example:  Lobotomies were a common practice in the mid-20th century.  脑白质切除术在20世纪中期是一种常见的做法。 | Synonyms:  psychosurgery, leucotomy | Antonyms:  therapy, treatment | noun
