noncontemplativeness:/ˌnɒnkənˈtɛmplətɪvnəs/| n.|非沉思性，不沉思的状态|lack of contemplation; absence of meditative thought or reflection. |She was known for her noncontemplativeness and decisive action. 她以不沉思和果断的行动而闻名。| The frenetic pace of modern life often leaves little room for noncontemplativeness. 现代生活的快节奏通常很少留给人们不沉思的空间。|近义词：unreflectiveness, thoughtlessness, mindlessness|反义词: contemplativeness, reflectiveness, thoughtfulness|词性:名词

Etymology:
non- + contemplativeness
non-: prefix meaning "not"
contemplative: from Late Latin contemplativus, from contemplari "to contemplate"
-ness: suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality
