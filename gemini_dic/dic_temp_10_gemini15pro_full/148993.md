ovoviviparism:/ˌoʊvəˌvaɪvɪˈpɛərɪzəm/| n. |卵胎生|A mode of reproduction in which embryos develop inside eggs that are retained within the mother's body until they hatch.  The embryos receive nourishment from the yolk of the egg, not from the mother directly.|例句：Sharks exhibit ovoviviparism, with the eggs hatching inside the female's body.  鲨鱼表现出卵胎生，卵在雌性体内孵化。

例句：Ovoviviparism is a reproductive strategy intermediate between oviparity and viviparity. 卵胎生是一种介于卵生和胎生之间的繁殖策略。|近义词：aplacental viviparity|反义词: oviparity, viviparity|词性: noun
