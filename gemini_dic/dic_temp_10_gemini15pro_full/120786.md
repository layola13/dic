misappropriation:/ˌmɪsəˌproʊpriˈeɪʃən/| n.| 挪用；滥用；盗用|The misappropriation of company funds is a serious offense. 公司资金的挪用是严重的违法行为。|例句：

The investigation revealed the misappropriation of millions of dollars.
调查揭露了数百万美元的挪用。

He was accused of misappropriation of funds.
他被指控挪用资金。

|近义词：embezzlement, theft, misuse, defalcation|反义词: proper use, allocation|词性:noun
