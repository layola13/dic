indians:/ˈɪndiənz/ | n. | 印第安人；印度人 | People who are from India or indigenous peoples of the Americas (although this term is now often considered offensive). | Many indigenous people prefer to be called by their specific tribal names rather than "Indians." 许多原住民更喜欢被称为他们特定的部落名称，而不是“印第安人”。| Indians are known for their rich cultural heritage. 印度人以其丰富的文化遗产而闻名。| synonyms: Native Americans (for indigenous peoples of the Americas - use with caution), Indian people, people of India (for people from India) | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, depends on context) | noun
