habilatory:/həˈbɪləˌtɔːri/ /həˈbɪlɪtəri/
adj.
康复的，复原的，使恢复能力的
Helping or intended to help someone to improve their skills and abilities after they have been ill or injured.
The habilatory program helped him regain his speech after the stroke.  这个康复项目帮助他在中风后恢复了语言能力。
近义词:rehabilitative, restorative, remedial, therapeutic
反义词:debilitating
词性:adjective
