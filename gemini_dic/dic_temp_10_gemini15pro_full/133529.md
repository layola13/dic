nonfidelity:/ˌnɑːnfɪˈdelɪti/ | n. | 不忠诚，不贞;失真 | Lack of faithfulness or loyalty, especially to a sexual partner; inaccuracy in reproduction, as of sound or color. |例句：His nonfidelity led to the end of their marriage. 他的不忠导致了他们婚姻的结束。

例句：The nonfidelity of the recording disappointed audiophiles.  录音的失真让音响发烧友们失望。|近义词：infidelity, disloyalty, unfaithfulness, treachery; distortion, inaccuracy |反义词：fidelity, loyalty, faithfulness; accuracy, precision | 词性: 名词
