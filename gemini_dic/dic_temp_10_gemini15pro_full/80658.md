Gorbachevs: /ˈɡɔːrbətʃɔːvz/, /ˈɡɔːrbətʃɑːvz/
| n. |戈尔巴乔夫们；戈尔巴乔夫一家| Refers to Mikhail Gorbachev, the last leader of the Soviet Union, and potentially his family members.  It's a plural form, suggesting reference to more than one person related to him. | The Gorbachevs visited the United States after his resignation. 戈尔巴乔夫一家在他辞职后访问了美国。|Synonyms:  (For Gorbachev himself) Soviet leader, reformer | Antonyms:  (Ideologically) hardliner, conservative | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Derived from the Russian surname "Горбачёв" (Gorbachyov), a patronymic surname meaning "son of Gorbach," where "Gorbach" is a nickname meaning "hunchback."
