marliest:/ˈmɑːrliɪst/ | adj. | 最早的，最先的 | Earliest; first.  Relatively rare and somewhat archaic. Formed by adding the superlative suffix "-est" to "marly," which itself is not commonly used.  While "marly" might refer to something resembling or containing marl (a type of soil), "marliest" usually refers to time. | The marliest bloom of the apple tree was a welcome sight. 苹果树最早的花开是一个受欢迎的景象。 |  The farmer planted the marliest variety of corn. 农民种植了最早的玉米品种。| 近义词: earliest, first, foremost | 反义词: latest, last | 词性: 形容词
