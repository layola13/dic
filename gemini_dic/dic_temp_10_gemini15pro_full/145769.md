overcuriously: /ˌoʊvərˈkjʊəriəsli/
adverb|过度好奇地，过分好奇地|In a way that shows excessive curiosity; too curiously.|He overcuriously peeked into the neighbor's window.|Synonyms: inquisitively, nosily, pryingly|Antonyms: indifferently, apathetically|词性: 副词

overcurious: /ˌoʊvərˈkjʊəriəs/
adjective|过度好奇的，过分好奇的|Excessively curious; too curious.|She was overcurious about her friend's personal life.|Synonyms: inquisitive, nosy, prying|Antonyms: indifferent, apathetically|词性: 形容词

词源:
From over- + curious.  "Over-" acts as an intensifier, meaning "excessively" or "too much." "Curious" comes from the Latin "curiosus," meaning "careful, inquisitive, diligent."
