navigators:/ˈnævɪɡeɪtərz/| n. |航海家，领航员，导航器|A person who plans and directs the route of a journey, especially by sea or air.  Someone who guides others or helps them find their way.|例句：The navigators used the stars to guide their ships. 航海家利用星星来引导他们的船只。

例句：Modern aircraft rely on sophisticated navigators. 现代飞机依靠精密的导航器。|近义词：explorer, guide, pilot, helmsman, director|反义词:follower, passenger|词性:noun
