impunity:/ɪmˈpjuːnɪti/
n.
免于惩罚，免责
freedom from punishment, harm, or loss
The dictator ruled with impunity, committing human rights abuses without fear of consequence.  这个独裁者肆无忌惮地统治，犯下侵犯人权的罪行而不用担心后果。

He continued to break the law with impunity. 他继续逍遥法外地违法。


近义词:immunity, exemption, freedom from punishment
反义词:liability, accountability, responsibility
词性:名词
