overgratitudes:/ˌoʊvərˈɡrætɪˌtjuːdz/
n.
过度的感激，过分的感谢
Excessive expressions of gratitude; an instance of being overly thankful.
She showered him with overgratitudes for a simple act of kindness. 她对一个简单的善举表达了过分的感激。
近义词：effusive thanks, excessive gratitude
反义词：ingratitude, thanklessness
词性：名词
