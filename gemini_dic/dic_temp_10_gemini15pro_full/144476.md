outthreatened:/ˌaʊtˈθrɛtnd/
verb
以威胁胜过，威胁超过
to threaten more than someone or something else
The new company outthreatened its competitors with aggressive marketing tactics. 这家新公司以咄咄逼人的营销策略胜过其竞争对手的威胁。
outmatch, surpass, outdo
