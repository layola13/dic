outscorns:/ˌaʊtˈskɔːrnz/
verb
胜过，超过（在得分方面）
outperforms in scoring; surpasses in points earned
The rookie basketball player outscored the veteran in the final game.  这位新秀篮球运动员在决赛中得分超过了老将。
The home team outscored their rivals by a significant margin. 主队以显著优势超过了他们的对手。
synonym: outperforms, surpasses, beats, trumps
antonym: loses to, is defeated by, falls behind
词性: 动词
