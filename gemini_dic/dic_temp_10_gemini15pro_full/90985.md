hurtfully:/ˈhɜːrtfəli/ | adv. | 伤害地，令人痛苦地 | In a way that causes emotional pain or distress. | She spoke hurtfully about his past mistakes. 她伤人地谈论了他过去的错误。

He looked at her hurtfully when she rejected his offer.  当她拒绝他的提议时，他痛苦地看着她。 | 近义词：cruelly, unkindly, painfully | 反义词：kindly, gently, compassionately | 词性：副词

Etymology: From "hurtful" + "-ly."  "Hurtful" comes from "hurt" (from Old French "hurter,"  ultimately of uncertain origin) + "-ful."
