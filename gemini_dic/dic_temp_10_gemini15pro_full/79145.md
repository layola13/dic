gladship: /ˈɡlædʃɪp/
noun
高兴；喜悦；令人高兴的事
The state of being glad; gladness.  A thing that causes gladness.
例句：His gladship was evident in his beaming smile. 他的喜悦从他灿烂的笑容中显露出来。

例句：The birth of her child was a gladship beyond measure.  她孩子的出生是一件无比喜悦的事情。

近义词：joy, happiness, delight, pleasure, elation
反义词：sadness, sorrow, grief, misery, despair
词性: n.
