armorican:/ɑːrˈmɒrɪkən/
adjective|
Armorican, of or relating to Armorica|
Relating to or characteristic of Armorica, an ancient region in northwest Gaul, roughly corresponding to Brittany.|
例句：The Armorican peninsula is known for its rugged coastline.  布列塔尼半岛以其崎岖的海岸线而闻名。

例句：Armorican folklore is rich with tales of mythical creatures. 布列塔尼的民间传说充满了神话生物的故事。

近义词：Breton, Celtic|
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "non-Armorican" or referencing a specific contrasting region)|
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *Armoricanus, from Armorica (“Armorica”), from Gaulish *aremorica (“coastal region”), from are- (“at, near”) + mor- (“sea”) + *-ika (adjective suffix).
