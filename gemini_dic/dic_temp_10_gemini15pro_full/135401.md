nonpermission:/ˌnɒnpərˈmɪʃən/
n.|未经许可，未经授权|The act of not granting or having permission. |He accessed the files without nonpermission, leading to his dismissal.|近义词:unauthorization, prohibition, forbiddance|反义词:permission, authorization|词性:名词
Etymology: "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "permission" (from Old French "permission," from Latin "permissio," from "permittere" - to allow)
