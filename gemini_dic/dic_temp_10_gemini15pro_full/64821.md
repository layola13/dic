espalier:/ɪˈspælɪər/ /ɛˈspæljər/ | n. vt.|棚架；树篱；以支架整形树|A framework for training fruit trees or ornamental shrubs to grow flat against a wall or trellis.  To train (a plant) to grow flat against a wall or trellis.|例句：The gardener carefully pruned the espaliered apple trees. 园丁仔细地修剪了棚架上的苹果树。

espalier:/ɪˈspælɪər/ /ɛˈspæljər/ | n.|以支架整形树|A tree or shrub trained to grow flat against a wall or trellis.|例句：The espalier formed a beautiful living screen in the garden.  这棵整形树在花园里形成了美丽的活屏风。
