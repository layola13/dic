murphy's law(s):/ˈmɜːrfiz lɔː(z)/ | n. | 墨菲定律 |If anything can go wrong, it will.  A humorous adage that anything that can go wrong will go wrong. | Example 1: I was late for the meeting because of traffic – Murphy's Law! 我因为交通堵塞开会迟到了——墨菲定律！

Example 2:  The printer always jams when I have a deadline, it's Murphy's Law in action. 打印机总是在我有截止日期的时候卡纸，这就是墨菲定律在起作用。 | 近义词: sod's law | 反义词: serendipity | noun
