grasping:/ˈɡræspɪŋ/ /ˈɡrɑːspɪŋ/| adj.|贪婪的，渴望的；理解的，抓紧的| grasping tightly onto the rope 紧紧抓住绳子|近义词：greedy, avaricious, acquisitive, covetous; understanding, comprehending|反义词：generous, altruistic; uncomprehending, obtuse|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *graspen*, from Old Norse *graspa* (“to grasp, grip”)
