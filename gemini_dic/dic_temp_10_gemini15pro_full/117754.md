melanterite:/ˈmɛləntəˌraɪt/
noun
水绿矾
A hydrated iron sulfate mineral (FeSO₄·7H₂O), typically pale green to bluish-green in color, often found in areas associated with pyrite oxidation.
Melanterite is often found as a coating on pyrite. 水绿矾常在黄铁矿表面形成涂层。
Melanterite is a relatively unstable mineral and can dehydrate easily. 水绿矾是一种相对不稳定的矿物，很容易脱水。
synonym:copperas, iron sulfate, green vitriol
antonym:N/A (Minerals rarely have direct antonyms)
