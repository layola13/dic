lenovo: /ləˈnoʊvoʊ/ | n. | 联想 | A Chinese multinational technology company.  Lenovo designs, develops, manufactures, and sells personal computers, tablet computers, smartphones, workstations, servers, electronic storage devices, IT management software, and smart televisions. |  例句：I bought a new Lenovo laptop. 我买了一台新的联想笔记本电脑。

|  例句：Lenovo is a major player in the global PC market.  联想是全球个人电脑市场的主要参与者。 | 近义词:  (As a brand, it doesn't have direct synonyms, but in terms of product category) Dell, HP, Acer, Asus | 反义词:  None | 词性: noun


词源:  The name "Lenovo" is a portmanteau of the words "Le" (from "Legend", the company's former name) and "novo", Latin for "new".
