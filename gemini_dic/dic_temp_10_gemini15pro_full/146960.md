overirrigated: /ˌoʊvərˈɪrɪɡeɪtɪd/
adjective
过度灌溉的
Having received too much irrigation.  The land becomes waterlogged and often salty.
The overirrigated fields yielded a surprisingly poor crop.  这些过度灌溉的田地出人意料地歉收。
overwatered, waterlogged
underwatered, arid
