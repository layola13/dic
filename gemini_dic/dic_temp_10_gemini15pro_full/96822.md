inescapably:/ˌɪnɪˈskeɪpəbli/
adverb
不可避免地
in a way that cannot be avoided
例句：The conclusion is inescapably that we must act.  结论是，我们必须采取行动，这是不可避免的。

例句：He was inescapably drawn to her. 他不可避免地被她吸引。

近义词：unavoidably, inevitably, necessarily
反义词：avoidably, preventably
词性：adverb

词源：From in- + escapable + -ly.  "in-" 表示否定，"escapable" 表示可避免的，"-ly" 构成副词。
