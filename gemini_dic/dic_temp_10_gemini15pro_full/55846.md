divinatory:/ˈdɪvɪnətɔːri/ |adj.|占卜的；预言的|Used to describe something related to divination or prophecy, the practice of seeking knowledge of the future or the unknown by supernatural means.|The ancient Romans used divinatory rituals before making important decisions. 古代罗马人在做出重要决定之前会进行占卜仪式。| The psychic gave a divinatory reading about my future.  灵媒对我的未来做了一个预言性的解读。|Synonyms: prophetic, oracular, predictive, prognostic, mantic|Antonyms:  mundane, ordinary|adjective
