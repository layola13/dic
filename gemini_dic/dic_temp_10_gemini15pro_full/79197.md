glanced:/ɡlænst/ /ɡlænst/ | v. | 一瞥，扫视，匆匆一看 | to look quickly or briefly at something or someone |  I glanced at my watch and realized I was late. 我看了一眼手表，意识到我迟到了。

He glanced up from his book as I entered the room. 当我进入房间时，他从书本上抬起头瞥了我一眼。

近义词：glimpse, peek, skim, scan,  | 反义词: stare, gaze, scrutinize | 词性: 动词
