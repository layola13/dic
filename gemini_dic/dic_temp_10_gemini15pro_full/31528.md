charte:/ʃɑːrt/| n.|宪章，特许状，许可证|A written document outlining the rights and privileges granted by a ruler or government to a community, class of people, or organization.|The king granted a charte to the city allowing it to govern itself. 国王授予这座城市一份宪章，允许其自治。|近义词：charter, constitution, statute|反义词:prohibition, restriction|名词

charte:/ʃɑːrt/| v.|特许，包租|To grant a charter to; to hire or lease (a vehicle, especially an aircraft or ship).|They charted a plane to fly to the island. 他们包租了一架飞机飞往岛屿。|近义词：lease, hire, rent|反义词:revoke, rescind|动词
