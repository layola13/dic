dinghy: /ˈdɪŋi/ (US), /ˈdɪŋɡi/ (UK)| n.|小艇，橡皮艇|A small open boat, often carried or towed by a larger vessel, or used for recreation. |We took the dinghy ashore. 我们乘小艇上岸。

The dinghy capsized in the rough seas. 小艇在汹涌的海浪中翻了。|近义词：rowboat, skiff, inflatable boat|反义词: ship, yacht|词性: noun
