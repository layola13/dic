biodynamic:/ˌbaɪoʊdaɪˈnæmɪk/ | adj. | 生物动力法的 | Relating to or characterized by biodynamics, a system of organic farming that emphasizes the holistic development and interrelationships of the soil, plants, and animals as a self-regulating ecosystem. |  The farmer uses biodynamic methods to cultivate his vineyard.  这位农民使用生物动力法耕作他的葡萄园。 |  organic, holistic | conventional, chemical | adjective

biodynamics:/ˌbaɪoʊdaɪˈnæmɪks/ | n. | 生物动力法 | A system of organic farming that treats the farm or garden as a self-regulating ecosystem, emphasizing the holistic development and interrelationships of the soil, plants, and animals. | He studied biodynamics in order to improve the health of his soil. 他学习生物动力法是为了改善土壤健康。 | organic farming, permaculture | conventional agriculture, industrial farming | noun
