carpoolings:/ˈkɑːrˌpuːlɪŋz/
n.
拼车，合伙用车
The practice of sharing a car with other people, especially for commuting to work.
We organized carpoolings to reduce traffic congestion. 我们组织了拼车以减少交通拥堵。
