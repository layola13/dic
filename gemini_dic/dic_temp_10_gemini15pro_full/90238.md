house-to-house:/ˌhaʊs tə ˈhaʊs/| adj. adv.|挨家挨户的，逐户的|Going from one house to another in a particular area, especially in order to sell something or to ask people for something.  Visiting each house in turn.| The police conducted a house-to-house search. 警察进行了挨家挨户的搜查。|Synonyms: door-to-door|Antonyms: N/A|adjective/adverb

house-to-house:/ˌhaʊs tə ˈhaʊs/| n.|挨家挨户的访问/销售|The act of going from one house to another, typically for sales or canvassing.|The charity conducted a house-to-house collection.  慈善机构进行了挨家挨户的募捐。|Synonyms: door-to-door canvassing, door-to-door selling|Antonyms: N/A|noun
