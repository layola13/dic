inexactness:/ˌɪnɪɡˈzæktnəs/| n. |不精确性，不准确性|The state or quality of being inexact; lack of precision or accuracy.|The inexactness of the measurements led to flawed results. 测量的不准确导致了有缺陷的结果。|近义词：inaccuracy, imprecision, vagueness|反义词：accuracy, precision, exactness|词性:名词
Etymology: From in- (not) + exactness (the quality of being exact).  "Exact" comes from the Latin "exactus," past participle of "exigere" (to drive out, demand, ascertain), from "ex" (out) + "agere" (to drive, lead).
