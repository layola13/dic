cantoon:/kænˈtuːn/
n.
宿营地；营房
A temporary or makeshift shelter, typically used by soldiers.
The soldiers set up their cantoons in the forest. 士兵们在森林里搭建了宿营地。

词源: From French canton, ultimately from Vulgar Latin *canto "corner, angle," from Latin cantus "rim of a wheel, tire." The military sense developed from the division of troops into separate quarters.

近义词: encampment, bivouac, camp
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider words like "city," "home," "barracks" for contrasting locations)
词性: noun
