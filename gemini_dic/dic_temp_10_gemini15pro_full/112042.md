longanimity:/ˌlɒŋɡəˈnɪməti/ /ˌlɔːŋɡəˈnɪməti/| n. |耐心；沉着；宽宏大量|The state of being long-suffering; patient endurance of hardship or provocation. |She bore the constant interruptions with remarkable longanimity. 她以非凡的耐心忍受着不断的打扰。|Synonyms: patience, forbearance, tolerance, composure, equanimity|Antonyms: impatience, irritability, irascibility|noun

Etymology->
From Latin longanimitas, from longanimis (“patient, long-suffering”), from longus (“long”) + animus (“mind, spirit, courage”).
