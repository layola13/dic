accomplishing: /əˈkʌmplɪʃɪŋ/
verb
完成，实现
present participle of accomplish, meaning in the process of completing or achieving something.
She is accomplishing her goals one by one. 她正在逐步实现她的目标。
He is accomplishing a difficult task. 他正在完成一项艰巨的任务。

近义词: achieving, fulfilling, realizing, executing
反义词: failing, neglecting, abandoning
词性: Verb (present participle)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French acomplir, from a- (intensive prefix) + complir "to fulfill," from Latin complēre "to fill up, complete," from com- "together" + plēre "to fill."

USAGE->
Present Continuous Tense:
She is accomplishing her dreams by working hard. = 她正通过努力工作来实现她的梦想。
They are accomplishing the project ahead of schedule. = 他们正在提前完成这个项目。


Present Perfect Continuous Tense:
He has been accomplishing great things in his field. = 他一直在他的领域取得巨大的成就。
We have been accomplishing our tasks efficiently. = 我们一直在高效地完成我们的任务。


Past Continuous Tense:
They were accomplishing their mission when the storm hit. = 当暴风雨来袭时，他们正在完成他们的任务。
She was accomplishing her daily workout routine when she got the call. = 当她接到电话时，她正在完成她的日常锻炼计划。


Future Continuous Tense:
We will be accomplishing our goals by the end of the year. = 我们将在年底前完成我们的目标。
They will be accomplishing the first phase of the project next month. = 他们将在下个月完成项目的第一阶段。


PHRASE->
