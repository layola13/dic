out-boxes:/ˈaʊtˌbɒksɪz/ (UK) /ˈaʊtˌbɑːksɪz/ (US)
n.
发件箱；已发送邮件箱
A folder or location where copies of emails or other messages that have been sent are stored.
I always check my out-boxes to make sure my emails were sent successfully. 我总是检查我的发件箱以确保我的电子邮件已成功发送。
He deleted all the messages in his out-boxes to clear up space. 他删除了发件箱中的所有邮件以清理空间。
synonyms: sent folder, sent items, sent mail
antonyms: in-boxes, inbox
noun
