outscheme:/ˌaʊtˈskiːm/
verb
胜过…的诡计；以智取胜
To surpass or defeat in scheming or contriving; to outwit.
He outschemed his rivals and won the contract. 他以智取胜，赢得了合同。
outscheme:/ˌaʊtˈskiːm/
verb
策划得比…更好
To devise a scheme that is more effective or elaborate than another.
She outschemed her competitors by offering a lower price and better service. 她通过提供更低的价格和更好的服务，策划得比竞争对手更好。


近义词: outwit, outmaneuver, outsmart, circumvent
反义词: be outwitted, be outsmarted

词性: verb
