ensnaring:/ɪnˈsneərɪŋ/
adjective
诱捕的；使人入迷的
catching and holding something, like a trap; captivating or alluring
The hunter set an ensnaring trap for the rabbit. 猎人为兔子设下了一个诱捕的陷阱。
Her beauty was utterly ensnaring. 她的美貌令人着迷。
Synonyms: trapping, captivating, alluring, enticing, beguiling
Antonyms: freeing, liberating, releasing, repellent, repulsive
word class: adjective
