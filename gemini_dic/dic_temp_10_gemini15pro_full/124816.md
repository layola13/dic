mucilaginous:/ˌmjuːsɪˈlædʒɪnəs/| adj.|粘液质的；粘性的|Covered with or containing mucilage; slimy. |The okra pods were mucilaginous and slippery.  秋葵荚粘糊糊的，滑溜溜的。|Synonyms: slimy, viscous, sticky, gooey, gummy|Antonyms: dry, non-viscous, smooth|adjective


mucilage:/ˈmjuːsɪlɪdʒ/| n.|胶水，粘液|A thick, gluey substance produced by plants and some microorganisms.|The cactus stores water in its mucilage. 仙人掌把水分储存在它的粘液里。|Synonyms: glue, paste, adhesive, gum|Antonyms: solvent, thinner|noun
