encystment:/ɪnˈsɪstmənt/
n.
包囊形成，被囊现象
The process of forming a cyst or becoming enclosed in a cyst.
The encystment of the parasite allows it to survive harsh conditions.  寄生虫的包囊形成使其能够在恶劣的条件下生存。
encystation, encapsulation
