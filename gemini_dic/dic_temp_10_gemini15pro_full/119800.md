migratory:/ˈmaɪɡrəˌtɔːri/| adj.|迁徙的，洄游的|Relating to migration; used of animals that migrate regularly.|Many birds are migratory. 许多鸟类是迁徙的。|近义词:migrant, nomadic, wandering|反义词:resident, settled, stationary|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *migratorius*, from migrat- , past participle stem of migrare ‘to migrate’.
