episodic:/ˌɛpɪˈsɒdɪk/ (UK) /ˌɛpɪˈsɑːdɪk/ (US)| adj.|间歇性的；分集的；情节式的|Occurring in episodes; not continuous or regular.  Containing or consisting of a series of loosely connected parts or events.|The pain she felt was episodic, coming and going without warning. 她感觉到的疼痛是间歇性的，来来去去没有预兆。|The television show had an episodic format, with each episode telling a self-contained story. 这部电视剧采用分集形式，每一集都讲述一个独立的故事。|近义词：intermittent, sporadic, occasional|反义词：continuous, constant, persistent|词性:形容词
