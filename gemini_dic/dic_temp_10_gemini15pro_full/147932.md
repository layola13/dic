overremiss: /ˌoʊvərɪˈmɪs/
adjective
过度疏忽的，非常懈怠的
Excessively negligent or remiss; very neglectful.
He was overremiss in his duties and was subsequently fired. 他玩忽职守，后来被解雇了。
The government was overremiss in its response to the crisis. 政府对危机的反应过于懈怠。
近义词: negligent, derelict, lax, careless
反义词: diligent, careful, attentive, responsible
词性: adjective

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "remiss" (adjective meaning "negligent").  "Remiss" comes from the Latin word "remissus," past participle of "remittere," meaning "to slacken, let go."
