devil-may-care:/ˌdɛvəlˌmeɪˈkɛər/
adjective
满不在乎的；漫不经心的；无所顾忌的
Cheerfully unconcerned; reckless; irresponsible.
He has a devil-may-care attitude towards life. 他对生活抱有一种满不在乎的态度。
devil-may-care attitude
carefree, reckless, nonchalant, irresponsible, insouciant
cautious, careful, responsible, concerned, anxious
词性: adjective
