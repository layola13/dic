bazillion:/bəˈzɪljən/
n.
无数，极大量
An extremely large, indefinite number.
There are a bazillion stars in the sky. 天空中有无数星星。
I've told you a bazillion times not to do that. 我告诉你无数次不要那样做。
近义词:zillion, gazillion, jillion, trillion, countless, innumerable
反义词:few, little
词性:名词
