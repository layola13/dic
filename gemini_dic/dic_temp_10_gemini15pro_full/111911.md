loiter:/ˈlɔɪtər/ (US), /ˈlɔɪtə(r)/ (UK)| vi.| 闲逛，徘徊，游荡|To stand or wait around idly or without apparent purpose.|Example:  A group of teenagers were loitering outside the shop. 一群青少年在商店外面闲逛。

Example:  He was loitered in the park after dark.  天黑后他在公园里徘徊。|近义词: linger, dawdle, hang around, mill about|反义词: hurry, rush|词性: 动词
