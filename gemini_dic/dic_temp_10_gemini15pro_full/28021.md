capacify:/kəˈpæsɪfaɪ/| v. |使能够，使有能力|To give someone the ability or power to do something. |例句：The training program will capacify employees to take on new responsibilities.  这个培训项目将使员工能够承担新的责任。

例句：The new law capacifies the government to regulate the industry. 新法律使政府能够监管该行业。

近义词：empower, enable, equip|反义词:disable, weaken|词性:动词
