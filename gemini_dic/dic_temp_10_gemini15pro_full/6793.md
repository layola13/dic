anaphylaxis:/ˌænəfɪˈlæksɪs/| n. |过敏性休克|A severe, potentially life-threatening allergic reaction.  It can occur within seconds or minutes of exposure to an allergen. Symptoms include difficulty breathing, swelling, hives, and a drop in blood pressure.|例句：The child went into anaphylaxis after eating a peanut.  这个孩子在吃了花生后发生了过敏性休克。

例句：He carries an EpiPen in case of anaphylaxis. 他随身携带一支肾上腺素笔以防过敏性休克。|近义词：allergic reaction, hypersensitivity|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "tolerance")|词性:noun
