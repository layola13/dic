amoa: /əˈmoʊə/ | n. | 阿莫阿（一种灭绝的不会飞的鸟）| An extinct flightless bird of the Hawaiian Islands, related to the rails. | Example: The amoa, once abundant, is now sadly extinct. 阿莫阿，曾经数量众多，现在可悲地灭绝了。 | Example:  Museum exhibits showcase the skeletal remains of the amoa.  博物馆展出了阿莫阿的骨骼遗骸。| 近义词: (As a flightless bird) moa, dodo, kiwi | 反义词:  (Implied: flying bird) eagle, hawk | 词性: noun
