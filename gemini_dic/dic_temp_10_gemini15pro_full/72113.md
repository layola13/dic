flocky:/ˈflɒki/ (UK) /ˈflɑːki/ (US)| adj.|絮状的，绒毛状的，像羊群的|Covered with or resembling small tufts of wool or flock.  Resembling or suggestive of a flock of sheep.|The material had a flocky texture, soft and fuzzy.  这种材料具有絮状的质地，柔软且毛茸茸的。  The hills were dotted with flocky sheep.  山上点缀着像羊群般的羊群。|近义词：fluffy, downy, woolly|反义词：smooth, flat|词性:形容词
