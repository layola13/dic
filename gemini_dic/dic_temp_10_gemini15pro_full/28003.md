caoutchouc:/ˈkaʊtʃʊk/ | n. | 橡胶，弹性树胶 |A tough, elastic substance made from the latex of various tropical plants.  It is the basic constituent of natural rubber.|  The tyres are made of caoutchouc. 轮胎是由橡胶制成的。 | synonyms: rubber, latex, elastic, gum | antonyms: rigid, inflexible | noun
