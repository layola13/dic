moodish:/ˈmuːdɪʃ/| adj.|喜怒无常的；易怒的；情绪化的|Subject to unpredictable changes of mood; given to whims.  Characterized by fits of temper or sullenness.|She's been very moodish lately, snapping at everyone for no reason. 她最近情绪很反复，动不动就无缘无故地对每个人发脾气。|Synonyms: temperamental, moody, fickle, erratic, capricious|Antonyms: stable, even-tempered, cheerful, steady|adjective
