hundredth:/ˈhʌndrədθ/ (US), /ˈhʌndrɪθ/ (UK)| adj. n.|第一百的，百分之一，第一百个|Representing one of a hundred equal parts or the last item in a series of a hundred.|She finished in hundredth place. 她获得了第一百名。|Synonyms: centesimal|Antonyms: first|adjective, noun

hundredth:/ˈhʌndrədθ/ (US), /ˈhʌndrɪθ/ (UK)| n.|第一百个|The ordinal number matching the cardinal number one hundred.|This is the hundredth time I've told you!  这是我第一百次告诉你！|Synonyms: centennial|Antonyms: first|noun
