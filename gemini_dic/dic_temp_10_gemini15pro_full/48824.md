defog:/diːˈfɒɡ/ |v.| 除雾，使清晰 |To remove fog or condensation from something, such as a car windshield or a pair of glasses.  It can also metaphorically refer to clarifying something confusing. |I had to defog my windshield before I could drive. 我开车前必须先清除挡风玻璃上的雾。 The new information helped to defog the situation. 新的信息帮助澄清了情况。 |近义词:clear, clarify, demist|反义词:fog, obscure, confuse|词性:动词

词源: de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + fog (noun)
