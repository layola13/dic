overbravado:/ˌoʊvərbrəˈvɑːdoʊ/| n.|过分的虚张声势，逞强|An excessive display of bravado; swaggering self-confidence.  It's an instance of going beyond simple confidence and entering the realm of boastfulness and arrogance.| His overbravado alienated his colleagues, who found his constant boasting tiresome. 他过度的虚张声势疏远了他的同事，他们觉得他不断的吹嘘很烦人。|近义词：swagger, bluster, bombast, ostentation|反义词：humility, modesty, diffidence|noun
