fray:/freɪ/
n.|磨损，争吵，喧嚷|The fraying rope finally snapped. 磨损的绳子终于断了。|近义词:fight, quarrel, battle, clash, scuffle|反义词:peace, harmony, calm|词性:名词
fray:/freɪ/
v.|磨损，磨破，烦扰，使紧张|The edges of the carpet are starting to fray. 地毯的边缘开始磨损了。|近义词:wear, erode, unravel, chafe, irritate|反义词:mend, repair, strengthen|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *freien, from Old French *freier, from Frankish *fraitan, from Proto-Germanic *fraitaną (“to rub”), from Proto-Indo-European *preh₁y- (“to rub”). Cognate with Old High German rītan (“to rub, scratch”).
