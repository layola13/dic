handicapping:/ˈhændɪˌkæpɪŋ/| n.|让步，不利条件，障碍；（体育比赛中）根据参赛者实力的差异，预先给予较弱一方一定的让步或加重较强一方的负担，以平衡双方获胜的机会；预测（比赛结果）；评估（获胜的可能性）|The process of assigning handicaps in a competition to equalize the chances of winning.|  Handicapping in horse racing is designed to make the race more competitive. 赛马中的让步是为了使比赛更具竞争性。

|近义词：disadvantage, impediment, obstacle, burden, hindering, hampering, assessment, evaluation, prediction, forecasting|反义词:advantage, aid, assistance, help, benefit|词性:noun
