absorbent:/əbˈzɔːrbənt/ /əbˈsɔːrbənt/| adj. n.|吸收性的，吸收剂|Having the ability to absorb or soak up something (liquid or gas); a substance that absorbs or soaks up something.|例句：Sponges are highly absorbent. 海绵吸水性很强。|近义词：porous, permeable, spongy|反义词：impermeable, waterproof|词性：形容词
absorbent:/əbˈzɔːrbənt/ /əbˈsɔːrbənt/| n.|吸收剂|A substance that absorbs or soaks up something.|例句：This new absorbent can clean up oil spills effectively.  这种新型吸收剂能有效清理漏油。|近义词：sponge, blotter|反义词：-|词性：名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin absorbēns, present participle of absorbēre (“to swallow down, suck up”), from ab- (“from, away from”) + sorbēre (“to suck up”).

USAGE->
作为形容词:

The towel was soft and absorbent. = 毛巾柔软且吸水性强。
An absorbent cloth is essential for cleaning up spills. = 一块吸水的布对于清理溢出物至关重要。


作为名词:

The chemist developed a new absorbent for industrial use. = 化学家开发了一种新的工业用吸收剂。
Paper towels are a common household absorbent. = 纸巾是一种常见的家庭吸收剂。


PHRASE->
highly absorbent = 高吸水性的
an absorbent material = 一种吸收性材料
absorbent cotton = 脱脂棉
absorbent paper = 吸水纸
absorbent powder = 吸水粉
