explanation:/ˌɛkspləˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 解释，说明 | a statement or account that makes something clear. |  I owe you an explanation for my behavior. 我欠你一个关于我行为的解释。

| Example: The scientist gave a detailed explanation of the experiment.  科学家对实验进行了详细的解释。 | Synonyms: clarification, elucidation, interpretation | Antonyms: mystification, confusion | noun


/ˌɛkspləˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 解释，说明 | a statement or account that makes something clear. | She demanded an explanation for his rudeness. 她要求他对自己的粗鲁行为做出解释。

| Example: His explanation for the delay was unconvincing.  他对延误的解释令人难以信服。 | Synonyms: account, exposition, rationale | Antonyms:  obscurity, enigma | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *explanacioun*, from Late Latin *explanātiō*, from Latin *explānātus*, past participle of *explānō* (“to explain, make plain”), from *ex- (“out”) + *plānus* (“flat, level, plain”).
