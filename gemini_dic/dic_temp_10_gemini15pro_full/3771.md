agitative:/ˈædʒɪteɪtɪv/
adjective
煽动的；使人不安的；刺激性的
Causing or tending to cause agitation.
His agitative speeches roused the crowd to a frenzy. 他的煽动性言论使人群陷入疯狂。
近义词:inflammatory, provocative, exciting, disturbing, unsettling
反义词:calming, soothing, tranquilizing
词性:adjective
词源:From Latin agitatus, past participle of agitare "to drive, stir, shake, agitate," frequentative of agere "to drive, lead, act, do."
