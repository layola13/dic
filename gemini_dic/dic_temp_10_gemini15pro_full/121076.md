misconvey:/ˌmɪskənˈveɪ/
verb
错误地传达；表达不清
To communicate (information) wrongly or inaccurately.
The spokesperson misconstrued the CEO's statement, which misconveyed the company's position to the public. 发言人曲解了首席执行官的声明，这向公众错误地传达了公司的立场。
He misconstrued her silence as agreement. 他把她的沉默误解为同意。
miscommunicate, misrepresent, distort
communicate clearly, convey accurately, represent correctly
v.
