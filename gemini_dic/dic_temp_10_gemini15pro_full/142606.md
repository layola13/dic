osteopath:/ˈɒstiəpæθ/ (UK) /ˈɑːstiəpæθ/ (US)| n.|整骨医生；整骨疗法医师|A practitioner of osteopathy, a system of complementary medicine involving the treatment of medical disorders through the manipulation and massage of the skeleton and musculature.|例句：I visited an osteopath for my back pain. 我去看整骨医生治疗我的背痛。

例句：The osteopath manipulated the joints in her neck. 整骨医生按摩了她脖子上的关节。|近义词：chiropractor, physical therapist|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps conventional doctor/physician)|词性:noun
