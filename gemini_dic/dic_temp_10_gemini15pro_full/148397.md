overstand:/ˌoʊvərˈstænd/| v. |理解, 彻底明白; 容忍, 宽恕 (非正式)|To understand something completely and thoroughly, often implying a deeper level of comprehension than simply knowing the facts. Can also mean to tolerate or forbear.|例句：He overstood the complexities of the situation. 他彻底明白了情况的复杂性。

例句：She overstood his rudeness because he was under a lot of stress. 她容忍了他的粗鲁，因为他压力很大。|近义词：comprehend, grasp, understand, tolerate, forbear|反义词：misunderstand, misinterpret|词性:动词
