necktieless:/ˈnɛktaɪlɪs/
adjective
没有戴领带的
Not wearing a necktie.
He showed up to the formal event necktieless, much to everyone's surprise. 他没戴领带就出席了正式活动，让每个人都感到惊讶。
The groom's casual, necktieless attire raised a few eyebrows. 新郎休闲的、不戴领带的装扮引起了一些人的侧目。
Near Synonyms: tieless, open-necked, casually dressed
Opposite: wearing a necktie, formally dressed
