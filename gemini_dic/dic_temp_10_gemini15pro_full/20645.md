blacknesses:/ˈblæknəsɪz/
n.
黑暗, 漆黑; 黑色物质
The intense blacknesses of the night sky were dotted with stars. 夜空的浓浓黑暗中点缀着星星。
The artist used varying blacknesses to create depth in the painting.  艺术家使用不同的黑色来创作画的深度。
Etymology: From Middle English *blaknes, from Old English *blæcnes (“blackness”), equivalent to black +‎ -ness. Cognate with Scots blackness, blackness (“blackness”), West Frisian blakens (“blackness”), Dutch zwartenis (“blackness”), German Schwärze (“blackness”). More at black.
Synonyms: darkness, obscurity, inkiness
Antonyms: brightness, lightness, whiteness
词性: 名词
