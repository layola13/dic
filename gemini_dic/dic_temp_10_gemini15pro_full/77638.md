gelatin:/ˈdʒɛlətɪn/ /ˈdʒɛləˌtin/| n.|明胶，凝胶|A translucent, colorless, brittle, flavorless foodstuff, derived from collagen obtained from various animal body parts.|例句：The dessert was made with gelatin.  这道甜点是用明胶做的。

例句：Gelatin is used in many food products. 明胶用于许多食品中。


|近义词：jelly, agar, collagen|反义词:|noun
