gobemouche:/ˈɡoʊbəˌmuːʃ/ /ˈɡɒbəmʊʃ/| n.|轻信的人；容易受骗的人|A gobemouche is a person who is easily tricked or fooled into believing something.  They are gullible and readily accept anything they are told.|He's such a gobemouche; he believed that story about the flying pigs. 他真是个容易受骗的人；他居然相信了关于飞猪的故事。|Synonyms: gullible, credulous, naive, unsuspecting|Antonyms: skeptical, cynical, discerning|Noun
