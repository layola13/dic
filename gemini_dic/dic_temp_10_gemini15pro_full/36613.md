collectednesses:/kəˈlɛktɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
镇静；沉着；冷静；自制力
The state of being collected; composure.
She maintained her collectedness during the emergency.  她在紧急情况下保持镇静。
Near Synonyms: composure, calmness, self-possession, equilibrium, aplomb
Antonyms: agitation, anxiety, nervousness, panic, distress
