ichnographical: /ˌɪknəˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
平面图的；地形图的
Representing a ground plan or layout, as of a building or site.  Relating to the representation of a ground plan.
The ichnographical layout of the ancient city revealed a complex network of streets and buildings.  这座古城的平面图展示了一个复杂的街道和建筑网络。
The architect provided an ichnographical drawing of the proposed house. 建筑师提供了一份拟建房屋的平面图。
Synonyms:  diagrammatic, schematic, planimetric
Antonyms:  pictorial, three-dimensional
词性: adjective
