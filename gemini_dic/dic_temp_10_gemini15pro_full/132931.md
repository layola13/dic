nonelusivenesses:/ˌnɒnɪˈluːsɪvnəsɪz/
n.
无法逃避；不可避免性；显而易见
The quality or state of being nonelusive; inability to be avoided or escaped.
The nonelusivenesses of the evidence made it impossible for the defendant to deny his guilt.  这些证据的显而易见性使得被告无法否认他的罪行。
Synonyms: unavoidability, inescapability, inevitability, obviousness, clarity
Antonyms: elusiveness, evasiveness, obscurity, vagueness
