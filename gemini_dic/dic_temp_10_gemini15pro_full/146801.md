overimitate: /ˌoʊvərˈɪmɪteɪt/
verb
过度模仿
To copy or mimic someone's actions or behaviors, even when those actions are unnecessary or irrelevant to achieving a goal.
Children often overimitate the actions of adults, even when they don't understand the purpose.  孩子们经常过度模仿成年人的行为，即使他们不明白这样做的目的。
Demonstrations designed to teach a task are sometimes overimitated by the learner. 为教授一项任务而设计的演示有时会被学习者过度模仿。
近义词: ape, mimic, copy, emulate
反义词: ignore, disregard, neglect
词性: v.


Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively, beyond") + "imitate" (from Latin "imitari," meaning "to copy, portray")
