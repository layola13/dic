economize:/iˈkɒnəmaɪz/ (US) /ɪˈkɑːnəmaɪz/| v. |节省，节约；有效利用|To spend less money than usual; to reduce expenses. |例句：We had to economize on heating to keep the bills down. 我们不得不节约暖气以减少开支。

例句：She economized by making her own clothes. 她通过自己做衣服来节省开支。|近义词：save, cut back, retrench, conserve|反义词：spend, waste, squander|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *économiser*, from *économie* ("economy"), from Ancient Greek οἰκονομία (oikonomía, "household management"), from οἶκος (oîkos, "house") + νέμω (némō, "manage, distribute").
