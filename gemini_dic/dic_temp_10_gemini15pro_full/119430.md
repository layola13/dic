micrography: /maɪˈkrɒɡrəfi/ | n. |显微照相术；显微摄影；微型书写|The art or practice of writing or drawing in extremely small characters, or the study of such writing. It also refers to the production of extremely small photographs, especially of documents, using a special camera. |He was a master of micrography, able to fit entire books onto a single sheet of paper. 他是显微书写的专家，能够将整本书的内容写在一张纸上。|  Micrography was used to create detailed images of insects for scientific study. 显微照相术被用于为科学研究创建昆虫的详细图像。 | synonyms: microphotography, miniaturization (for writing), microcalligraphy | antonyms: macrography, enlargement | noun
