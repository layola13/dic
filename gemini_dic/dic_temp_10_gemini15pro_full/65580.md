evacuatory:/ɪˈvækjʊətɔːri/
adjective
排泄的；疏散的
Relating to or used for evacuation, especially of waste products from the body or people from a dangerous place.
The building has several evacuatory exits.  这栋建筑有几个疏散出口。
The nurse explained the evacuatory procedure after the surgery.  护士解释了手术后的排泄流程。
近义词: excretory, purgative (for bodily waste); clearing, emptying (for people)
反义词: retentive (for bodily waste); filling (for people)
词性: adjective

evacuate:/ɪˈvækjueɪt/
verb
疏散；撤离；排泄
To remove someone from a place of danger to a safer place; to discharge waste matter from the body.
The residents were evacuated before the hurricane hit.  飓风来临前居民们被疏散了。
The patient needed medication to help evacuate his bowels.  病人需要药物帮助排便。
近义词: clear, empty, withdraw, remove, discharge
反义词: fill, occupy, retain
词性: verb

evacuation:/ɪˌvækjuˈeɪʃn/
noun
疏散；撤离；排泄
The act of removing someone or something from a dangerous place; the discharge of waste matter from the body.
The evacuation of the city was completed before the storm.  风暴来临前城市疏散完毕。
The doctor recommended medication to assist with bowel evacuation.  医生建议用药物来帮助排便。
近义词: clearance, emptying, removal, withdrawal, discharge
反义词: filling, occupation, retention
词性: noun
