metathorax:/ˌmetəˈθɔːræks/ | n. | 后胸 | The metathorax is the posterior segment of the thorax of an insect, bearing the hindwings and the hindlegs. | The grasshopper's powerful metathorax allows it to jump long distances. 蚱蜢强壮的后胸使其能够跳跃很长的距离。| The beetle's metathorax was damaged, preventing it from flying. 甲虫的后胸受损，无法飞行。 |  synonym: posterior thorax | antonym: prothorax | noun
