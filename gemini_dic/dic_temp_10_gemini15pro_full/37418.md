commercially:/kəˈmɜːʃəli/| adv.|商业上地，通商上地|In a way that relates to or is involved in commerce or trade.  Used to describe activities or products that are intended to make a profit.|The product was not commercially successful.  这个产品在商业上并不成功。|Synonyms: profitably, gainfully,  mercantilely|Antonyms: unprofitably, non-commercially|adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From commercial + -ly.  "Commercial" comes from the Late Latin "commercialis," meaning "of or pertaining to trade," from "commercium" meaning "trade, commerce."  This in turn is derived from "com-" (with, together) + "merx" (merchandise).
