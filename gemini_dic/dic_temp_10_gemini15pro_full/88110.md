hijab:/hɪˈdʒɑːb/ | n. |头巾，盖头 | A head covering worn in public by some Muslim women. |  She chose to wear a hijab after she turned 16.  她16岁以后选择戴头巾。

|近义词: headscarf, veil |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps uncovered hair) | 词性: noun


hijab:/hɪˈdʒɑːb/ | n. |希贾布（穆斯林妇女戴的面纱或头巾） |  A veil worn by Muslim women in the presence of any male outside of their immediate family, usually covering the head and chest. | The hijab is an important part of her religious practice. 希贾布是她宗教实践的重要组成部分。


|近义词: veil, headscarf, niqab (more covering) |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps uncovered hair) | 词性: noun
