hexene:/ˈhɛksiːn/| n. |己烯|An alkene with the formula C6H12.  There are several isomers of hexene depending on the location of the double bond.|1-Hexene is used as a comonomer in the production of polyethylene.  己烯用作聚乙烯生产中的共聚单体。| synonym: olefin, alkene| antonym: alkane| noun
