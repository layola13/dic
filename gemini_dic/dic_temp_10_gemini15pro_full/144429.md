outsumming:/ˌaʊtˈsʌmɪŋ/
verb
超过总数；累计超过
surpassing in total; exceeding in sum.
The team's score outsummed their opponents' by a significant margin.  该队的得分大大超过了对手。
Recent donations have outsummed last year's total. 最近的捐款总额超过了去年。
近义词: exceeding, surpassing, outstripping, outdoing
反义词: falling short of, lagging behind
词性: verb
