alveolar:/ælˈviːələr/ (US) /ælˈviːələ(r)/ (UK)| adj. n.| 肺泡的；齿龈音的|Relating to or resembling alveoli (tiny air sacs in the lungs) or formed by the tongue touching the alveolar ridge (the ridge behind the upper teeth).|The alveolar ridge is important for pronouncing certain consonants.|近义词:pulmonary (for lung-related meaning), dental (for sound production)|反义词:None|词性:adjective, noun

alveolus:/ælˈviːələs/ (US) /ælˈviːələs/ (UK)| n.| 肺泡；小窝|A tiny air sac in the lungs where the exchange of oxygen and carbon dioxide takes place; a small cavity, pit, or socket.|The alveoli are essential for respiration.|近义词:air sac|反义词:None|词性:noun
