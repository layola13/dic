delectably:/dɪˈlɛktəbli/ | adv. | 美味地；怡人地；令人愉快地 |  In a highly pleasing or enjoyable manner, especially regarding taste or smell. | The cake was delectably moist and flavorful. 蛋糕美味可口，湿润而香浓。| The warm breeze felt delectably cool on her skin.  温暖的微风拂过她的肌肤，感觉凉爽宜人。 | synonym: deliciously, delightfully, enjoyably, pleasantly | antonym: unpleasantly, displeasingly, distastefully | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *dēlectabiliter, from Latin dēlectabilis (“delightful”) + -iter (adverbial suffix).  The Latin dēlectabilis is derived from dēlectāre (“to delight”), which in turn comes from dē- (“from, down, away”) + *lactāre, a frequentative of laciō (“to entice, allure”).
