ichthyopterygia:/ˌɪkθiɒpˈtɛrɪdʒɪə/
n.
鱼龙目
An extinct order of marine reptiles resembling dolphins, that flourished during the Mesozoic Era.
Ichthyopterygia were the dominant marine reptiles of the Mesozoic. 鱼龙是中生代占统治地位的海洋爬行动物。
The discovery of a new species of Ichthyopterygia provided valuable insights into the evolution of marine reptiles.  新鱼龙物种的发现为海洋爬行动物的进化提供了宝贵的见解。
近义词: ichthyosaurs, fish-lizards (less formal)
反义词:  None (since it refers to an extinct order, there's no direct opposite)
词性: Noun
