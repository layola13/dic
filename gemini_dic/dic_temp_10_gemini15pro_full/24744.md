broodinesses:/ˈbruːdinɪsɪz/
noun
孵蛋状态；沉思状态；郁郁寡欢
The state of being broody; inclined to sit on eggs (of a hen); or, figuratively, moodily or sullenly thoughtful.
The farmer noticed the broodinesses of several hens and prepared nesting boxes.  农场主注意到几只母鸡有孵蛋的迹象，就准备了几个巢箱。
She retreated into long broodinesses after the death of her husband. 她丈夫去世后，她陷入了长期的忧郁之中。
近义词: pensiveness, contemplation, melancholy, sullenness
反义词: cheerfulness, lightheartedness, sociability
