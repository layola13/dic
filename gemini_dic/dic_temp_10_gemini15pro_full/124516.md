mount-up:/maʊnt ˈʌp/| phrasal verb |上马；增加；增长|To get on a horse or other animal for riding; to increase gradually in amount or degree. |例句：The cowboys mounted up and rode off into the sunset. 牛仔们上马，朝着夕阳骑去。|近义词：climb on, get on, increase, rise|反义词:dismount, decrease, decline|词性:动词短语

PHRASE->
mount up on =  骑上…
mount up to =  总计达…；合计达
mount up against =  不利于…；对…越来越不利
mount up in favour of = 有利于…；对…越来越有利
mount up with =  与…一起骑马


注意事项:
"mount up"主要用于指骑马上动物，尤其指马。虽然它也可以用来指数量的增加，但这种用法不如"increase," "rise," "accumulate," 等词常用。
