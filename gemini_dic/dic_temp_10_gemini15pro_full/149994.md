palmiped:/ˈpælmɪˌpɛd/ | n. | 蹼足鸟；有蹼足的鸟 | A bird with webbed feet, adapted for swimming. |例句：Ducks and geese are examples of palmipeds. 鸭子和鹅是蹼足鸟的例子。

例句：The palmiped glided effortlessly across the water. 这只蹼足鸟毫不费力地在水面上滑行。| 近义词：waterfowl, web-footed bird | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps terrestrial bird) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *palmipes, from palma ("palm of the hand") + pes ("foot").  Literally, "palm-footed."
