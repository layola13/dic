auntly: /ˈɑːntli/ | adj. |像阿姨的；慈爱的| Resembling, behaving like, or suitable for an aunt; kindly, solicitous. | She had a very auntly manner, always offering advice and cookies. 她有一种很像阿姨的风范，总是提供建议和饼干。|  She gave me an auntly hug and told me everything would be alright. 她给了我一个像阿姨一样的拥抱，告诉我一切都会好起来的。| 近义词: motherly, maternal, caring | 反义词: uncaring, indifferent | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From aunt + -ly.  The word "aunt" comes from Old French "ante" which in turn derives from Latin "amita," meaning "father's sister."
