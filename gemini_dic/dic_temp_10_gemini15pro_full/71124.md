fishify:/ˈfɪʃɪfaɪ/
verb
使鱼化；使像鱼；变成鱼；充满鱼
To make something fish-like or to turn something into a fish, or to fill with fish.
The artist fishified the human figure in his painting, giving it scales and fins.  艺术家在他的画作中将人物鱼化，赋予它鳞片和鱼鳍。
They fishified the pond by introducing several new species. 他们在池塘里引进了几个新品种的鱼，使它充满了鱼。
