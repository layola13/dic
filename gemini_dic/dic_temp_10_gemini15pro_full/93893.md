immortalities: /ˌɪmɔːrˈtælɪtiz/
n.
不朽的事物；永生；不朽的名声
The state of living forever; never-ending life or fame.

He wrote about the search for immortality. 他写了关于寻找永生的文章。

The poet achieved immortality through his works. 这位诗人通过他的作品获得了不朽的名声。

Synonyms:  eternity, perpetuity, deathlessness
Antonyms: mortality, finiteness, ephemerality
词性: noun (plural)
