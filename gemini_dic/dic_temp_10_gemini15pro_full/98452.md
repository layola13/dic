inspirations:/ˌɪnspəˈreɪʃənz/| n. |灵感，鼓舞人心的人或事物|Something that makes someone want to do something or that gives someone an idea about what to do or create.  It can also refer to the people or things that inspire someone.|例句：Her travels were a rich source of inspiration for her paintings. 她的旅行是她绘画灵感的丰富来源。

例句：He is an inspiration to all of us. 他是我们所有人的榜样。|近义词：influences, motivations, stimulus, muse|反义词：deterrents, hindrances|词性: Noun (plural)


ETYMOLOGY->

From Late Middle English: from Old French, or from late Latin inspiratio(n-), from the verb inspirare (see inspire).
