onside:/ˈɒnsaɪd/ | adj. |在界内的；未越位的；合法的| Within the prescribed limits or rules, especially in sports; not offside. | The referee signaled that the player was onside. 裁判示意球员没有越位。|  The investment was deemed onside according to regulations. 这项投资根据规定被认为是合法的。|近义词：legal, legitimate, valid |反义词：offside, illegal, illegitimate |词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From on + side.  Originally used in sports, particularly football (soccer), to describe a player's position as being on the correct side of the line, allowing them to legally receive a pass. The meaning extended to more general usage to indicate something being within the rules or limits.
