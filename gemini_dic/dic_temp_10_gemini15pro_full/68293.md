falcidian:/fɔːlˈsɪdiən/
adjective
法尔奇迪亚的；（指遗嘱）保留四分之一遗产给继承人的
Relating to or denoting the Falcidian law, a Roman law that guaranteed heirs at least one-quarter of their inheritance, preventing testators from leaving too much of their estate to other parties.
The falcidian portion refers to the minimum share guaranteed to an heir. 法尔奇迪亚部分是指继承人保证获得的最低份额。
The lawyer explained the implications of the falcidian law on the will. 律师解释了法尔奇迪亚法律对遗嘱的影响。
近义词:testamentary,  inheritance-related
反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider words related to disinheritance, such as "disinheriting," "exheredating.")


词性: adjective
