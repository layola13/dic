druggist:/ˈdrʌɡɪst/| n. |药剂师，药商|A person who prepares and sells medicines; a pharmacist. |例句：The druggist carefully measured out the prescribed medication. 药剂师仔细地量取了处方药。

例句：In the old days, people relied on the local druggist for health advice. 在过去，人们依靠当地的药剂师提供健康建议。|近义词：pharmacist, apothecary, chemist |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
