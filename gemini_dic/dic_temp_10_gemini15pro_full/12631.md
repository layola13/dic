assassination:/əˌsæsɪˈneɪʃn/
|n.|暗杀|The assassination of the president shocked the nation. 总统的遇刺震惊了全国。|Example: The assassination attempt failed. 暗杀企图失败了。|Synonyms: murder, killing, execution|Antonyms: protection, rescue|Noun


Etymology: From the Old French word *assassinacion*, derived from *assassin* (assassin).  Ultimately from the Arabic word *ḥashshāshīn* (hashish-users), referring to a medieval Ismaili Muslim sect known for targeted killings.
