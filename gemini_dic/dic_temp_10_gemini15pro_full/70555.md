filings:/ˈfaɪlɪŋz/
n.
文件; 备案; 存档; 锉屑
Small pieces of metal, wood, etc. removed by a file.  Also, documents formally submitted to a court or government agency.
The carpenter swept up the metal filings. 木匠扫掉了金属屑。
The company's filings with the SEC are available online.  该公司提交给美国证券交易委员会的文件可在网上查阅。
synonyms: documents, records, papers, shavings, fragments, particles
antonyms: (For documents) withdrawals, retractions; (for shavings) whole, complete
