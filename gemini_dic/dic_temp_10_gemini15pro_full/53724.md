discoblastic:/ˌdɪskəˈblæstɪk/ | adj. |盘状卵裂的|Having a discoidal cleavage pattern, where the cell divisions occur only in a small disc of cytoplasm at the animal pole of the egg, as in birds and reptiles.|The discoblastic cleavage is characteristic of telolecithal eggs. 盘状卵裂是端黄卵的特征。|The zebrafish embryo, unlike the discoblastic chick embryo, develops as a blastodisc overlying a large yolk cell.  斑马鱼胚胎不像盘状卵裂的鸡胚胎，而是作为一个覆盖在一个大卵黄细胞上的胚盘发育。|近义词:meroblastic (部分卵裂的)|反义词:holoblastic (完全卵裂的)|词性:形容词

词源:
disco- (disk) + -blastic (relating to a blastula or early embryonic stage)
