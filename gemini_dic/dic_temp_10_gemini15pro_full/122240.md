misyoke: /mɪsˈjoʊk/
noun
错误的结合，不匹配，错误的轭
an incorrect or unsuitable joining or pairing; a mismatch; a yoke that is wrongly fitted
The misyoke of the two departments caused inefficiency and confusion.  这两个部门的不匹配导致效率低下和混乱。
The marriage was a misyoke from the start, with their differing values and lifestyles. 这段婚姻从一开始就是个错误的结合，他们的价值观和生活方式截然不同。
mismatch, misalliance, mispairing
suitable pairing, good match
n.
