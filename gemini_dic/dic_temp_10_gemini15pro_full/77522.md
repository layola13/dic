gazundered:/ˌɡæzˈʌndərd/
verb
被压价的；被低价出售的
(of a seller of property) forced to accept a lower offer than previously agreed just before the exchange of contracts.
The seller was gazundered just before completion.  卖方在交易完成前被压价了。
近义词: undercut, lowball
反义词: outbid, inflate
词性: verb
