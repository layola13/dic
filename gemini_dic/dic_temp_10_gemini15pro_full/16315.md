barasi: /bəˈrɑːsi/ | n. | Barasi (a type of fabric/clothing) |  A fine, lightweight, usually transparent cotton fabric of plain weave, often decorated with gold or silver threads, originally made in India.  It can also refer to garments made from this fabric. | She wore a beautiful barasi sari to the wedding. 她穿着一件漂亮的巴拉西纱丽去参加婚礼。 | The barasi curtains filtered the sunlight beautifully. 巴拉西窗帘漂亮地过滤了阳光。 | 近义词: muslin, gauze, voile | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps something like "heavy cloth") | 词性: noun
