biomolecules:/ˌbaɪ.oʊˈmɑː.lə.kjuːlz/ | n. | 生物分子 |  Molecules that are present in living organisms. These include large macromolecules such as proteins, carbohydrates, lipids, and nucleic acids, as well as small molecules such as primary metabolites, secondary metabolites, and natural products. | Proteins and carbohydrates are essential biomolecules for human life. 蛋白质和碳水化合物是人类生命必需的生物分子。 | synonyms: biological molecules, organic molecules | antonyms: inorganic molecules | noun
