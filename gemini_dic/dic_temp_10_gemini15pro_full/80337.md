goldfinger:/ˈɡoʊldˌfɪŋɡər/| n.|金手指; 金匠; (尤指)贪婪的人;  |A person who is obsessed with gold or money, often in a greedy or unscrupulous way.  It can also refer to a goldsmith or a skilled worker with gold. The term gained popularity from the James Bond novel and film of the same name, featuring a villain named Auric Goldfinger.|例句：He's such a goldfinger, always looking for ways to make a quick buck. 他真是个贪财鬼，总想着快速赚钱。

例句：The goldfinger crafted a beautiful necklace. 金匠制作了一条漂亮的项链。|近义词：miser, skinflint, profiteer|反义词: philanthropist, altruist|词性:noun
