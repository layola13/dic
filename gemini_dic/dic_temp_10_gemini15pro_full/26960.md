calcific: /kælˈsɪfɪk/ | adj. |钙化的；含钙的| Relating to or containing calcium, especially calcium salts deposited in body tissues.|  The doctor found calcific deposits in the patient's shoulder joint. 医生在病人的肩关节发现了钙化沉积物。 |Synonyms: calcareous, hardened, ossified | Antonyms: soft, decalcified | adjective


calcify: /ˈkælsɪfaɪ/ | v. |钙化；硬化| To harden by the deposition of calcium salts.| The cartilage in his knee had begun to calcify. 他膝盖里的软骨已经开始钙化了。| Synonyms: harden, ossify, petrify | Antonyms: soften, decalcify | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *calcificāre, from calx (“lime, limestone”) + -ficāre (suffix forming verbs meaning “to make, to cause to become”).
