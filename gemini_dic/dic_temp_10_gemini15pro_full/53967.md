discreetest:/dɪˈskriːtɪst/
adj.
最谨慎的，最慎重的
showing great care and caution in one's speech or actions, especially in order to avoid causing offense or revealing private information.
She was always very discreetest about her personal life. 她对自己的私生活总是非常谨慎。
近义词: most cautious, most prudent, most circumspect, most reserved
反义词: indiscreet, careless, imprudent, reckless
词性: adjective

discreet:/dɪˈskriːt/
adj.
谨慎的，慎重的
showing great care and caution in one's speech or actions, especially in order to avoid causing offense or revealing private information.
He was very discreet about his relationship with the actress. 他对与那位女演员的关系非常谨慎。
近义词: cautious, prudent, circumspect, reserved
反义词: indiscreet, careless, imprudent, reckless
词性: adjective


词源:
From Middle French *discret, from Latin discretus (“separated, distinct”), past participle of discernere (“to separate, distinguish”).
