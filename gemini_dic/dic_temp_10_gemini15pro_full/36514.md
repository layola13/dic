colicroot:/ˈkɑːlɪkruːt/ | n. | 腹痛根，阿利吉尼亚蛇根草 | A North American plant (Aletris farinosa) with bitter roots formerly used as a tonic and treatment for colic.  It's also known as stargrass. |  The colicroot was once a popular remedy for various ailments.  腹痛根曾经是一种治疗各种疾病的流行药物。 | Early settlers used colicroot to treat colic in babies. 早期定居者用腹痛根治疗婴儿的腹痛。 | synonyms: stargrass, ague root, agueweed, crow corn | antonyms: N/A | noun
