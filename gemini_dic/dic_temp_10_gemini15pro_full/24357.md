brineless:/ˈbraɪnlɪs/| adj.|无盐的，淡的|Lacking salt or brine; not salty.  Not stimulating; insipid.|The brineless lake was teeming with freshwater fish.|近义词：fresh, unsalted, insipid|反义词:salty, briny, saline|词性:形容词
词源: brine + -less.  Brine comes from Old English *brýne, from Proto-Germanic *brunjo, related to Dutch brijn and German Brine. The suffix -less is a common Old English suffix meaning "without."
