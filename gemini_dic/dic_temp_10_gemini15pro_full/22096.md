bolognas:/bəˈloʊnjəz/ /bəˈləʊnjəz/
noun
大香肠，博洛尼亚香肠
A large seasoned sausage of mixed meats, especially beef and pork.
例句：
I made a sandwich with bolognas and cheese.
我做了一个夹着博洛尼亚香肠和奶酪的三明治。

例句：
Fried bolognas are a popular snack.
油炸博洛尼亚香肠是一种很受欢迎的小吃。

近义词：sausage, salami, luncheon meat
反义词: None (No direct opposite for a type of food)
词性: noun
