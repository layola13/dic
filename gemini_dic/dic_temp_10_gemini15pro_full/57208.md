downbeats:/ˈdaʊnˌbiːts/| n.|低落的情绪；弱拍；不顺利的事情|A downbeat is the first beat of a musical measure, often emphasized. It can also refer to a pessimistic or depressing feeling, or a period of reduced activity. |The music had a slow, steady downbeat.  这音乐节奏缓慢而稳定。|The team was in a downbeat mood after losing the game. 球队输掉比赛后情绪低落。|Synonyms: pessimistic, gloomy, depressing, discouraging, disheartening|Antonyms: upbeat, optimistic, cheerful, encouraging|noun


downbeat:/ˈdaʊnˌbiːt/| adj.|悲观的；消沉的；低沉的|Downbeat describes something pessimistic, depressing, or characterized by low spirits. |He gave a downbeat assessment of the company's future. 他对公司的前景作出了悲观的评估。|The movie had a surprisingly downbeat ending. 这部电影的结局出人意料地令人沮丧。|Synonyms: pessimistic, gloomy, depressing, discouraging, disheartening|Antonyms: upbeat, optimistic, cheerful, encouraging|adjective
