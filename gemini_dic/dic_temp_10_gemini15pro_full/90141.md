hotcockles:/ˈhɒtkɒkəlz/ (UK) /ˈhɑːtkɑːkəlz/ (US)| n.| 热手套游戏; 打手心游戏|A children's game in which one player kneels blindfolded and places their hand, palm up, behind their back. Another player strikes the kneeling player's hand, and the blindfolded player must guess who struck them.|Example 1:  We played hotcockles at the birthday party. 我们在生日派对上玩了打手心游戏。

Example 2: The children shrieked with laughter during a game of hotcockles. 孩子们在玩打手心游戏时发出尖叫般的笑声。|近义词:hand-slap, blind man's buff (similar in involving blindfolds)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
