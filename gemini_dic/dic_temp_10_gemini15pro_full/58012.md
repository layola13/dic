drizzle:/ˈdrɪzəl/ (US), /ˈdrɪzl/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.|细雨，毛毛雨；下毛毛雨|A light rain falling in very fine drops. |例句：A light drizzle was falling.  下着毛毛雨。|近义词：sprinkle, mist|反义词:downpour, torrent|词性:名词, 动词

drizzle:/ˈdrɪzəl/ (US), /ˈdrɪzl/ (UK)| vi. vt.|下毛毛雨；少量地倾倒|To rain lightly; to pour something in a thin stream.|例句：It began to drizzle as we left. 我们离开时开始下毛毛雨。|近义词：sprinkle, shower|反义词:pour, downpour|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *driselen, frequentative of *drislen, from Old English *drīsan (“to fall in drops”), from Proto-Germanic *drīsaną (“to drip, trickle”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰris- (“to drip, flow”). Cognate with Dutch driesen (“to drizzle”), German triesen (“to drip”), Old Norse drjása (“to drizzle”).
