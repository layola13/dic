braky:/ˈbreɪki/| adj.| 摇摇晃晃的，不稳定的，快散架的 |Something that is braky is likely to break or fall apart because it is old or badly made.  It's often used to describe vehicles or machinery.| The old car was so braky, I was afraid to drive it.| 近义词：rickety, ramshackle, shaky, unstable, dilapidated| 反义词： sturdy, robust, stable, sound |词性: 形容词
