cayuse:/kaɪˈjuːs/| n.|卡尤斯马；印第安马；印第安小马|A small, strong horse, originally bred by the Cayuse Indians of the northwestern United States. It often refers to any horse of the western plains. |例句：The cowboy skillfully rode his cayuse across the rugged terrain. 牛仔熟练地骑着他的卡尤斯马穿过崎岖的地形。

例句：The cayuse was surefooted and agile, perfect for navigating the steep trails. 这匹卡尤斯马脚步稳健，行动敏捷，非常适合在陡峭的小路上行走。|近义词：horse, pony, steed|反义词:|n.
