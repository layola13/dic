cufic: /ˈkuːfɪk/ | adj. n.| 库法体的，库法体书法 |An angular style of Arabic script, used primarily in decorative inscriptions.|The museum has a fascinating collection of cufic manuscripts.  博物馆收藏了引人入胜的库法体手稿。|Near synonyms: angular, geometric| Antonyms: cursive, flowing|adjective, noun

Etymology->
From Kufa, a city in Iraq where this style of calligraphy originated.  源自伊拉克城市库法，这种书法风格起源于此。
