glanduliferous: /ˌɡlændjʊˈlɪfərəs/
adjective
有腺的；具腺体的
Bearing glands or gland-like structures.
The glanduliferous trichomes of the plant secrete a sticky resin.  这种植物的具腺毛分泌一种粘性树脂。
The frog's skin is glanduliferous and secretes toxins.  这种青蛙的皮肤有腺体，会分泌毒素。
Synonyms: glandular
Antonyms: aglandular


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *glandulifer*  (bearing glands), from *glandula* (gland) + *-fer* (bearing).  *-fer* is related to the verb *ferre* (to bear, carry).
