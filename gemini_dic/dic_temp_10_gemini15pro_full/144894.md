over-serves:/ˌoʊvərˈsɜːrvz/
verb
过度服务；提供过多的服务
To provide more service than is necessary or desired.
The restaurant over-served us with bread; we were too full for our entrees.  这家餐厅给我们提供了过多的面包；我们都吃饱了，主菜都吃不下了。
The waiter over-served the wine, filling the glasses to the brim. 服务员倒酒倒太多了，酒都溢出来了。
over-serve, oversupply
underserve
