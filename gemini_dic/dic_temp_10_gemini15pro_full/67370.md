extensivity:/ɪkˌstɛnˈsɪvɪti/ | n. |广延性；扩展性；广泛性 |The property of being extensive, or having a value proportional to the size of the system.  It refers to a property that is additive for independent, noninteracting subsystems. |The extensivity of entropy is a fundamental concept in thermodynamics. 熵的广延性是热力学中的一个基本概念。|The extensivity of the land allowed for large-scale farming.  这片土地的广阔性允许大规模耕作。|近义词：extent, expanse, vastness, scope, range |反义词：intensivity, concentration |词性: noun
