functionary:/ˈfʌŋkʃəneri/| n.|官员，公务员，职员|A person who holds or performs a particular function within an organization or government.|The government functionaries were slow to respond to the crisis. 政府官员对危机的反应迟缓。|Synonyms: official, bureaucrat, administrator, officer|Antonyms: civilian, non-official|Noun

functionary:/ˈfʌŋkʃəneri/| adj.|职能的，公务的|Relating to a function or position within an organization or system.|The report detailed the functionary roles of various departments. 这份报告详细说明了各部门的职能作用。|Synonyms: official, administrative, bureaucratic|Antonyms: unofficial, non-administrative|Adjective
