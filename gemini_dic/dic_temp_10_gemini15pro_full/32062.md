cherty:/ˈtʃɜːrti/| adj.|燧石质的，含燧石的|Resembling chert; containing chert.  Made of or containing chert, a hard, fine-grained sedimentary rock consisting of microcrystalline or cryptocrystalline quartz.|The soil in this area is cherty, making it difficult to cultivate.|近义词：flinty, siliceous|反义词:soft, loose|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From chert + -y. Chert itself comes from Old French chert or cherte, of uncertain origin, possibly related to Latin quartzum, "quartz."
