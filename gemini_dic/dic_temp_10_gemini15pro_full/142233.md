orogenies:/ɔːrəˈdʒɛniːz/
| n. |造山运动；造山作用|A process in which a section of the earth's crust is folded and deformed by lateral compression to form a mountain range.|The Himalayas are the result of ongoing orogenies.|近义词：mountain building, tectonic uplift|反义词: erosion, subsidence|noun
