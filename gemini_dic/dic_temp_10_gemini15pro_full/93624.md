imbitterer:/ɪmˈbɪtərər/
n.|使人怨恨的人或事物|Something or someone that causes someone to become bitter.
|The constant criticism from her boss was the imbitterer of her work life.|她老板不断的批评是她工作生活中让她感到痛苦的根源。
|近义词: bane, curse, plague, affliction|
|反义词: comforter, solace, relief|
|词性: 名词|

imbitter:/ɪmˈbɪtər/
vt.|使怨恨；使苦恼|To make someone feel bitter or resentful.
|The loss of his job imbittered him towards the company.|失业让他对公司充满了怨恨。
|近义词: sour, alienate, disaffect, antagonize|
|反义词: sweeten, appease, mollify|
|词性: 动词|

词源： From im- + bitter + -er.  im-作为前缀表示"into"，bitter表示“苦的，痛苦的”，-er是名词后缀，表示“人或事物”。  所以imbitterer表示“使人变苦或痛苦的人或事物”。 动词 imbitter 则由bitter加上前缀im-构成, im-在此处表示“使...”的意思。
