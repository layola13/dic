fuddies:/ˈfʌdiz/| n. |老古董，守旧者|Old-fashioned or conservative people, often disapproving of anything new or different.|Those fuddies at the golf club wouldn't even consider letting women join.|近义词：conservatives, traditionalists, diehards|反义词:modernists, progressives, radicals|词性:名词

词源:  Likely a humorous alteration of "fogies," which itself is of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to "fusty" meaning stale or old-fashioned.
