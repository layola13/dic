imposement:/ɪmˈpoʊzmənt/| n. |强加；施加；负担|The imposement of new taxes angered the citizens.  新税的征收激怒了市民。| English Explanation: The act of imposing something, such as a tax, rule, or punishment. It often implies forcing something unwanted or burdensome on someone or something. |例句：The imposement of a curfew restricted people's freedom.  宵禁的实施限制了人们的自由。|近义词：imposition, levy, burden|反义词:relief, exemption|词性:noun
