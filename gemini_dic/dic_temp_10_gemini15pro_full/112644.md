lowballing: /ˈloʊˌbɔːlɪŋ/
| n./v. (gerund)|低价报价；压低价格|The act of offering a price or value that is significantly lower than what is considered fair or reasonable.  It can also refer to underestimating something.|He accused the company of lowballing its bid to win the contract. 他指责该公司为了赢得合同而压低报价。|近义词：underbidding, underselling, undervaluing|反义词: overbidding, overestimating|noun/verb

lowball: /ˈloʊˌbɔːl/
| v. /n.|低价报价；低估|To offer a price or value that is significantly lower than what is considered fair or reasonable. It can also mean to underestimate something.|The dealer lowballed me on my trade-in.  经销商低估了我的以旧换新价值。|近义词：underbid, undersell, undervalue|反义词: overbid, overestimate|verb/noun
