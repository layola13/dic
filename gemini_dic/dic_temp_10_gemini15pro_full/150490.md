panto:/ˈpæntoʊ/ (UK), /ˈpæntoʊ/ (US)| n.| 童话剧，哑剧 |A theatrical entertainment, mainly for children, based on a fairy tale or nursery story, usually produced around Christmas, and featuring songs, dances, and jokes. | Example: We took the kids to see the panto at the local theatre. 我们带孩子们去当地剧院看了童话剧。

Example:  The panto this year is Cinderella. 今年的童话剧是灰姑娘。

| 近义词: pantomime, Christmas show | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps serious drama) | 词性: noun
