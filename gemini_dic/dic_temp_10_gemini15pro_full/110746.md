lingers:/ˈlɪŋɡərz/ (US), /ˈlɪŋɡəz/ (UK)| v. |徘徊，逗留，流连；（香味、感觉等）持续，萦绕|To stay in a place longer than necessary or expected, often because of reluctance to leave.  To remain or persist, as of a smell, feeling, or sound.| The scent of roses lingered in the air. 玫瑰的香味在空气中萦绕。|Synonyms: remain, stay, tarry, persist, continue|Antonyms: leave, depart, vanish, disappear|verb
