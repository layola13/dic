contradictory:/ˌkɒntrəˈdɪktəri/ (UK), /ˌkɑːntrəˈdɪktɔːri/ (US)| adj.|矛盾的，对立的，不一致的|Containing or characterized by contradiction; mutually opposed or inconsistent.|The witness gave contradictory statements to the police.  证人向警方提供了相互矛盾的陈述。|Synonyms: conflicting, opposing, inconsistent, incompatible, clashing, discrepant|Antonyms: consistent, compatible, harmonious, agreeing, concordant|adjective

contradictory:/ˌkɒntrəˈdɪktəri/ (UK), /ˌkɑːntrəˈdɪktɔːri/ (US)| n.|矛盾因素，矛盾事物|A proposition, statement, or phrase that contradicts another or itself.|His actions are a contradictory to his words. 他的行为与他的言辞相矛盾。|Synonyms: paradox, inconsistency, contradiction|Antonyms: consistency, agreement, harmony|noun
