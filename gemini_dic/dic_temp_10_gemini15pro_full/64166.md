equanimous:/ˌiːkwəˈnɪməs/ | adj. | 平静的；镇定的；沉着的 |  calm and composed, especially in a difficult situation | He remained equanimous in the face of disaster.  他面对灾难仍然保持镇定。| 近义词: calm, composed, serene, tranquil, imperturbable | 反义词: agitated, anxious, disturbed, frantic, upset | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *aequanimus*, from aequus (“even, level, equal, calm”) + animus (“mind, spirit”).
