overspending:/ˌoʊvərˈspɛndɪŋ/
n.|过度消费，超支|The act of spending more money than one can afford or more than is budgeted.  |Example: Overspending on credit cards can lead to serious debt. 用信用卡过度消费会导致严重的债务。|Synonyms: extravagance, profligacy, overexpenditure |Antonyms: saving, thrift, frugality|noun
