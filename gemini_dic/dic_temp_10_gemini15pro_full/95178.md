inaudible:/ɪnˈɔːdəbl/ | adj. |听不见的，无法听见的 | Not able to be heard. | Example: The whisper was inaudible over the loud music.  在喧闹的音乐声中，耳语听不见。 | Example:  Due to the poor connection, her voice was inaudible. 由于连接不好，她的声音听不见。 | Synonyms: unheard, imperceptible, faint, indistinct | Antonyms: audible, clear, loud, distinct | adjective
