interjection:/ˌɪntərˈdʒekʃən/ | n. |感叹词|A word or phrase that expresses a strong feeling or emotion, such as surprise, pain, or pleasure. It is often followed by an exclamation mark. | Ouch! That hurt! 哎哟！好痛！| Synonyms: exclamation, cry, ejaculation | Antonyms: N/A | noun

interject:/ˌɪntərˈdʒekt/ | v. |插话，打断|To say something abruptly, especially as an interruption.| "But wait," he interjected, "there's more to the story." “但是等等，”他插嘴道，“故事还有更多。”| Synonyms: interrupt, interpose, chime in | Antonyms: listen, refrain | verb
