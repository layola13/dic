gesticulate:/dʒɛˈstɪkjʊleɪt/
verb
做手势；用手势表达
To make gestures, especially when speaking, to emphasize or clarify one's words.
The speaker gesticulated wildly, trying to emphasize his point.  演讲者疯狂地做着手势，试图强调他的观点。
She gesticulated towards the door, indicating he should leave. 她指着门做手势，示意他应该离开。
近义词:gesture, signal, motion
反义词:be still

词性:v.
