cashmere:/ˈkæʃmɪr/| n. adj.|羊绒，开司米；|A soft wool that comes from a type of goat.  一种来自山羊的柔软羊毛。|例句：She wore a beautiful cashmere sweater. 她穿着一件漂亮的羊绒衫。
|近义词：pashmina, wool, angora|反义词: synthetic fiber, acrylic|词性:noun

cashmere:/ˈkæʃmɪr/| adj.|羊绒制的；|Made from cashmere wool. 由羊绒制成。|例句：This scarf is made of pure cashmere. 这条围巾是纯羊绒的。
|近义词：woolen, pashmina|反义词: synthetic, cotton|词性:adjective
