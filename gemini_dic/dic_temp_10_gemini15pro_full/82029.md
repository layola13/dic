gripsack: /ˈɡrɪpsæk/ | n. | 背包，旅行袋 | A bag, often of canvas or other sturdy material, carried on the back or over the shoulder, used for carrying clothes, food, or other supplies, especially by hikers or campers. | He packed his gripsack with essentials for the weekend hike. 他把周末远足的必需品装进了背包里。 |  She slung her gripsack over her shoulder and set off on her journey. 她把背包挎在肩上，开始了她的旅程。 | 近义词: backpack, rucksack, knapsack | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps suitcase for a different type of luggage) | 词性: noun
