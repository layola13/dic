complementarily:/ˌkɒmplɪmənˈtærɪli/ | adv. | 补充地，互补地 | In a complementary way, enhancing or emphasizing each other's qualities. | The two approaches work complementarily to provide a comprehensive solution. 这两种方法互补地工作，以提供一个全面的解决方案。|  The flavors of the wine and cheese complement each other complementarily.  葡萄酒和奶酪的味道相得益彰。 | synonym: reciprocally, mutually | antonym: independently, separately | 词性: 副词

complementarity:/ˌkɒmplɪmɛnˈtærɪti/ | n. | 互补性 | The state or quality of being complementary; a relationship or situation in which two or more different things improve or emphasize each other's qualities. | The complementarity of their skills made them a strong team.  他们的技能互补性使他们成为一支强大的团队。| The report highlighted the complementarity between the two organizations.  报告强调了两个组织之间的互补性。| synonym: synergy, interdependency | antonym: incompatibility, conflict | 词性: 名词
