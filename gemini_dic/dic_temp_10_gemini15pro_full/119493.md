micropayment: /ˈmaɪkroʊˌpeɪmənt/ | n. |小额支付，微支付 | A very small payment made online, usually for digital content or services. | Example 1: Micropayments are becoming increasingly common for online articles and music downloads.  小额支付在在线文章和音乐下载中变得越来越普遍。

Example 2: The website uses a micropayment system for accessing premium content. 该网站使用小额支付系统来访问高级内容。 | Synonyms: small payment, mini-payment | Antonyms: large payment, macropayment | noun
