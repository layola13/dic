obstruction:/əbˈstrʌkʃən/ | n. | 阻碍；障碍物；阻塞；梗阻 |Something that blocks or impedes progress or movement. |The fallen tree was an obstruction in the road.  倒下的树是道路上的障碍物。|  The obstruction in the pipe caused the water to back up. 管道里的阻塞物导致水倒流。 |  synonym: blockage, obstacle, impediment, barrier | antonym: clearance, opening, aid, assistance | noun
