inexplicit:/ˌɪnɪkˈsplɪsɪt/
adjective
不明确的，含蓄的
not clearly or directly expressed; not explicit
The instructions were inexplicit and led to confusion. 说明书不明确，导致了混乱。
His response was inexplicit, leaving me unsure of his true feelings. 他的回答不明确，让我不确定他的真实感受。
Synonyms: ambiguous, vague, unclear, indirect, implied
Antonyms: explicit, clear, direct, specific, unambiguous
词性: 形容词
