dash-down:/ˈdæʃ daʊn/ | v. | 迅速写下；猛冲向下 | To write something quickly; to rush downwards. |  He dashed down a few notes before the meeting. 他在会议前快速记下了一些笔记。

She dashed down the stairs when she heard the doorbell. 她听到门铃声后冲下楼。 | 近义词：jot down, scribble, plunge, descend rapidly | 反义词: ascend, climb | 词性: 动词

dash-down:/ˈdæʃ daʊn/ | n. |  向下猛冲；俯冲 | A rapid downward movement or plunge. | The dash-down of the eagle towards its prey was breathtaking.  老鹰向猎物俯冲的景象令人叹为观止。

The skier's dash-down the slope was impressive. 滑雪者俯冲下坡的景象令人印象深刻。 | 近义词：plunge, swoop, descent | 反义词: ascent, climb | 词性: 名词
