oxidizabilities:/ˌɒksɪdaɪzəˈbɪlɪtiz/ | n. | 氧化性，可氧化性 | The susceptibility of a substance to undergo oxidation. It refers to how easily a substance loses electrons and reacts with oxygen or other oxidizing agents.  | The oxidizabilities of different metals vary greatly. 不同金属的氧化性差异很大。|  The oxidizabilities of the compounds were determined by cyclic voltammetry. 这些化合物的氧化性通过循环伏安法测定。 | synonyms: oxidation susceptibility, oxidation potential | antonyms: reduction potential, reducibility | noun
