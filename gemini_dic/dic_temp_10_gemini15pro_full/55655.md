dithyramb:/ˈdɪθɪræm/| n.|狂热赞美诗，酒神赞歌|A wild, enthusiastic, or ecstatic expression of feeling; an impassioned, irregular poem or song of exaltation.  Originally, a choral hymn in honor of Dionysus.|The poet penned a dithyramb celebrating the beauty of nature. 诗人写了一首热情赞颂大自然之美的诗。|近义词：eulogy, panegyric, encomium, accolade|反义词：criticism, condemnation, censure|noun
