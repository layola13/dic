doddering:/ˈdɒdərɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈdɑːdərɪŋ/ (US)| adj.|步履蹒跚的，老态龙钟的|Moving in a feeble or unsteady way, especially because of old age.  Characterized by trembling, especially from old age. |例句：The doddering old man shuffled slowly down the street.  老态龙钟的老人在街上缓慢地拖着脚步。

例句：His doddering hands fumbled with the buttons. 他颤抖的双手笨拙地摸索着纽扣。|近义词：frail, feeble, shaky, unsteady, tottering|反义词：vigorous, robust, steady, strong|词性:形容词
