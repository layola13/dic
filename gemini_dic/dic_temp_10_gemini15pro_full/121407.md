misimprove:/ˌmɪsɪmˈpruːv/
verb
弄巧成拙，越改越坏
to make something worse by trying to improve it
He misimproved his essay by adding too many complicated words. 他在文章里加了太多复杂的词，反而弄巧成拙了。
近义词: worsen, aggravate, exacerbate
反义词: improve, enhance, ameliorate
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (meaning "badly" or "wrongly") + improve.  First recorded in the early 17th century.
