nonthreatening:/ˌnɒnˈθrɛtnɪŋ/ | adj. | 无威胁的，不构成威胁的 | Not posing a threat; harmless. | The dog's tail was wagging, indicating a nonthreatening posture. 狗的尾巴摇着，表明它没有威胁性。 |  She gave him a nonthreatening smile. 她给了他一个没有威胁性的微笑。 |  近义词：harmless, innocuous, safe, benign | 反义词：threatening, menacing, intimidating | 词性: 形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
threatening (adjective): from threaten (verb), from Old English *þreatnian "to compel by threats," from þreat "threat," from Proto-Germanic *thrautaz "crowd, oppression."
