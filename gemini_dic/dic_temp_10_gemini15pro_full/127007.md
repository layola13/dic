nasby:/ˈnæzbi/| n. |纳斯比（姓氏；地名）| A surname and a place name.  It is primarily known for its association with Petroleum V. Nasby, a fictional character created by American writer David Ross Locke. This character was a satirical representation of a Copperhead, a faction of Democrats in the North who opposed the American Civil War and wanted immediate peace with the Confederacy.|The town of Nasby, OH was named after the fictional character. | 近义词: (For the place name)  village, town, hamlet| 反义词: (None, as it is a proper noun) |词性:noun
