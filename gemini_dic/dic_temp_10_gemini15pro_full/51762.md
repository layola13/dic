devotee:/ˌdevəˈtiː/ /ˌdəvəʊˈtiː/
noun
信徒；爱好者；热心者
A person who is very interested in and enthusiastic about someone or something.
例句：He is a devotee of science fiction. 他是科幻小说的爱好者。

例句：She is a devotee of the opera. 她是歌剧的爱好者。


近义词：fan, enthusiast, admirer, follower, supporter
反义词：critic, detractor, opponent
词性：n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *dévoté*, past participle of *dévoter* "to dedicate, devote," from Latin *dēvōtus*, past participle of *dēvovēre* "to vow, dedicate, devote," from *dē-* "down, away" + *vovēre* "to vow."
