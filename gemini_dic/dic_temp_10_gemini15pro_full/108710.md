leaner:/ˈliːnər/| adj. n.|更瘦的，更精简的；学习者|Someone or something that is leaner is thinner or contains less fat than before. A learner is someone who is learning a skill or a subject. |例句：She's looking much leaner after her diet.  她节食后看起来瘦多了。


例句：He's a quick learner and picked up the new software easily. 他学东西很快，轻松地学会了新的软件。|近义词：(adj.) thinner, slimmer, slender; (n.) student, pupil, novice|反义词:(adj.) fatter, heavier, obese; (n.) expert, professional|词性:形容词，名词
