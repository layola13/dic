devastations:/ˌdevəˈsteɪʃənz/
|n.|毁灭，破坏，蹂躏|Great acts of destruction, typically widespread.|The earthquake caused widespread devastations across the region. 地震在整个地区造成了广泛的破坏。|

|反义词：construction, creation, restoration|

devastation:/ˌdevəˈsteɪʃən/
|n.|毁灭，破坏，蹂躏|Great destruction or damage.|The hurricane left a scene of utter devastation. 飓风过后留下了一片满目疮痍的景象。|
|近义词：destruction, ruin, wreckage, havoc|
|反义词：construction, creation, restoration|
|词性：名词（单数）|

devastate:/ˈdevəsteɪt/
|v.|摧毁，破坏，使震惊|Destroy or ruin.|The fire devastated the forest. 大火摧毁了森林。/  The news devastated her. 这个消息让她悲痛欲绝。|
|近义词：destroy, ruin, wreck, shatter|
|反义词：build, create, restore|
|词性：动词|


词源：From Latin *devastare*, from de- 'down, completely' + vastare 'lay waste'.
