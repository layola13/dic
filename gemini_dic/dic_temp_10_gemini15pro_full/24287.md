brigandism:/ˈbrɪɡəndɪzəm/
n.
土匪行为；盗匪行径；拦路抢劫
The act of being a brigand; highway robbery; banditry.
The travelers feared brigandism in the remote mountain pass. 旅行者们害怕在偏僻的山路上遇到土匪抢劫。

近义词: banditry, robbery, highway robbery, outlawry, piracy
反义词: lawfulness, order, security

词性: noun

词源: From French brigandisme, from Italian brigante. Ultimately from a Germanic source related to Old High German brekan “to break”.
