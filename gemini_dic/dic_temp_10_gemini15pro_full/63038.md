enrapturing:/ɪnˈræptʃərɪŋ/
adjective
令人陶醉的；使人入迷的
Causing someone to feel great pleasure and admiration.
Her performance was absolutely enrapturing, leaving the audience speechless. 她的表演绝对令人陶醉，让观众哑口无言。
The enrapturing beauty of the sunset captivated everyone on the beach.  令人陶醉的日落美景迷住了海滩上的每一个人。

近义词: captivating, enchanting, mesmerizing, spellbinding, fascinating
反义词: boring, dull, tedious, uninteresting, repulsive
词性: adjective

词源: From enrapture + -ing.  Enrapture comes from  en- + rapture. Rapture ultimately comes from the Latin *raptura, from rapere "to seize, carry off."
