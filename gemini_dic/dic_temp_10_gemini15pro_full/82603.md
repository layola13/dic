guardrails:/ˈɡɑːrdreɪlz/ /ˈɡɑːrdˌreɪlz/| n.|护栏，防护轨|A barrier set up along the edge of something dangerous to prevent people from falling.|The hiking trail has guardrails along the steepest sections. 这条步道最陡峭的路段设有护栏。|Synonyms: railings, barriers, fences, balustrades|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "open edge")|noun

guardrail:/ˈɡɑːrdreɪl/ /ˈɡɑːrdˌreɪl/| n.|保护措施，预防措施 (比喻义)|A measure taken to protect against an undesirable or dangerous occurrence.|The company implemented strict guardrails to prevent financial fraud.  这家公司实施了严格的防护措施，以防止金融欺诈。|Synonyms: safeguards, precautions, protections, safety measures|Antonyms: risks, dangers, hazards|noun
