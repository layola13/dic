The omniprevalent fear of failure can paralyze people from pursuing their dreams.  普遍存在的对失败的恐惧会让人们不敢追逐梦想。 | 近义词: ubiquitous, pervasive, widespread, rife | 反义词: rare, scarce, uncommon, localized | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin *omnipraesent- (omni- "all" + praesent- "present").
