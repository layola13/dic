hollering:/ˈhɑːlərɪŋ/ /ˈhɒlərɪŋ/| n. vi. |叫喊，大声喊叫|A loud cry or shout.  The act of shouting loudly.|例句：The children were hollering in the playground. 孩子们在操场上大喊大叫。|近义词：shouting, yelling, screaming, bawling|反义词: whispering, murmuring|词性：noun, verb


hollering:/ˈhɑːlərɪŋ/ /ˈhɒlərɪŋ/| vi. |叫喊，大声喊叫|To shout loudly.|例句：He was hollering at the referee. 他对着裁判大喊大叫。|近义词：shout, yell, scream, bawl|反义词: whisper, murmur|词性: verb
