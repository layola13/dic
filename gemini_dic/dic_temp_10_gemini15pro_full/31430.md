charism: /ˈkærɪzəm/ | n. | 魅力，感召力，天赋 | A special power that some people have naturally that makes them able to influence other people and attract their attention and admiration. |  She has a natural charism that draws people to her. 她有一种天生的魅力，吸引着人们靠近她。

She lacked the charisma to inspire her followers. 她缺乏激励追随者的魅力。 |  近义词: charm, allure, magnetism, appeal | 反义词:  repulsiveness, unattractiveness | 词性: 名词
