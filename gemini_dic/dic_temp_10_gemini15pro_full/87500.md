heterochromous:/ˌhɛtərəˈkrəʊməs/ |adj.|异色的，不同色的|Having parts of different colors, especially as applied to the iris of the eye or parts of plants.|The heterochromous flowers ranged in color from deep purple to pale lavender.|Synonyms: varicolored, multicolored, polychromatic, variegated|Antonyms: homochromous, monochromatic|adjective

heterochromous:/ˌhɛtərəˈkrɑːməs/ |adj.|异色的，不同色的|Having parts of different colors, especially as applied to the eye or parts of plants.|Her cat has heterochromous eyes - one blue and one green.|Synonyms: varicolored, multicolored, polychromatic, variegated|Antonyms: homochromous, monochromatic|adjective
