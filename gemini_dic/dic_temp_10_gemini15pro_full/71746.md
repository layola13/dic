flay:/fleɪ/
verb
剥皮；严厉批评
To strip off the skin of; to criticize harshly.
The butcher flayed the deer.  屠夫剥了鹿的皮。
The critic flayed the director for his latest film.  评论家严厉批评了导演的最新电影。
近义词: skin, excoriate, criticize, condemn
反义词: praise, commend
词性: verb

flay:/fleɪ/
noun
剥皮；严厉批评 (较少用作名词)
The act of stripping off the skin; a harsh criticism.
The flay of the animal was quick and efficient. 对动物的剥皮快速而有效。 (较少用)
His review was a merciless flay of the novel. 他的评论是对这部小说的无情抨击。(较少用)
近义词: excoriation, condemnation
反义词: praise, commendation
词性: noun
