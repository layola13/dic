hyperotreta:/ˌhaɪpərəˈtriːtə/| n. |盲鳗|Hagfish, also known as hyperotreti, are eel-like, slime-producing marine fish. They are the only known living animals that have a skull but no vertebral column, although hagfish do have rudimentary vertebrae.|例句：The hyperotreta produced copious amounts of slime when threatened.  盲鳗在受到威胁时会产生大量的粘液。

例句：Hyperotreta are considered a delicacy in some cultures. 在某些文化中，盲鳗被认为是一种美味佳肴。|近义词：hagfish|反义词: N/A |词性:noun


Etymology:
From  Ancient Greek  ὑπέρ (hupér, “above, over”) + τρετός (tretós, “perforated”).  Referring to the nasohypophyseal duct that opens into the pharynx.
