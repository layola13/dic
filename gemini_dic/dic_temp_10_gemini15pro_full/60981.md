elicitor:/ɪˈlɪsɪtər/| n.|诱导物；引发剂|Something that elicits a particular response or reaction.|The interviewer used various elicitors to encourage the interviewee to speak freely.  面试官使用了各种诱导方法来鼓励受访者畅所欲言。|Synonyms: stimulus, trigger, cue|Antonyms: suppressant, inhibitor|noun
