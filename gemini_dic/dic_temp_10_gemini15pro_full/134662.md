nonmanipulatory:/ˌnɒn məˈnɪpjʊlətɔːri/ /ˌnɑːn məˈnɪpjʊləˌtɔːri/
adjective
非操控性的；非操纵性的
Not designed or intended to manipulate or control others; not involving manipulation.
The therapist employed a nonmanipulatory approach, allowing the patient to guide the session.  这位治疗师采用了一种非操控性的方法，让病人引导治疗过程。
The teacher created a nonmanipulatory learning environment where students felt safe to express their opinions. 老师创造了一个非操控性的学习环境，让学生感到可以安全地表达自己的观点。
Synonyms: noncontrolling, noninterfering, nonintrusive, respectful, impartial
Antonyms: manipulative, controlling, domineering, coercive
词性: 形容词
