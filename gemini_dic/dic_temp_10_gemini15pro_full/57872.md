dribble:/ˈdrɪbəl/ (US), /ˈdrɪbl/ (UK)| n.|运球，滴下，细流 |The act of moving a ball by bouncing it or kicking it slightly, or a small quantity of liquid that falls or flows in drops.|The baby's dribble ran down his chin. 婴儿的口水顺着下巴流了下来。  His dribble was too slow and the defender stole the ball. 他的运球太慢了，防守队员把球抢走了。|近义词：trickle, drip|反义词：gush, pour|词性:名词

词源:  From Middle English *driblen, frequentative of drippen (“to drip”). Cognate with Dutch dribbelen, German tröpfeln.
