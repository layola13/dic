claqueur:/klæˈkɜːr/
noun
喝彩者；拍手党
A person employed to applaud a performer or performance, especially to encourage a favorable reception.
The claqueur led the applause after the aria.  喝彩者在咏叹调结束后带头鼓掌。
Synonyms: hired applauder, clapper, applauder
Antonyms: critic, detractor
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *claqueur*, from *claquer* "to clap," of imitative origin.
