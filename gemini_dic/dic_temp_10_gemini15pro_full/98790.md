integrating: /ˈɪntɪɡreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 整合，结合，合并；使…成为一体 |  The company is integrating its new acquisitions into its existing operations.  该公司正在将其新收购的企业整合到现有业务中。 |  例句：The school is integrating students with disabilities into regular classrooms. 学校正在将残疾学生融入普通教室。 | 例句：They are integrating the new software into the system.  他们正在将新软件集成到系统中。 | 近义词：combining, merging, unifying, incorporating | 反义词：separating, dividing, segregating | 词性: 动词

词源:  From Latin integratus, past participle of integrare "to make whole," from integer "whole, complete"
