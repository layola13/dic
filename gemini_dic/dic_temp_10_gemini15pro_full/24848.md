browning:/ˈbraʊnɪŋ/| n. | 褐变；变成褐色；烤焦 |The browning of the apples indicated they were overripe. 苹果的褐变表明它们熟过头了。|近义词：discoloration, darkening|反义词：whitening, lightening|词性:n.
browning:/ˈbraʊnɪŋ/| v. | (使)变成褐色；(使)烤焦 |The toast is browning nicely. 吐司烤得恰到好处，颜色很漂亮。|近义词：roasting, scorching|反义词：bleaching|词性:v.
