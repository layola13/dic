checkerwork:/ˈtʃekərwɜːrk/ (US), /ˈtʃɛkəwɜːk/ (UK)| n.|格子图案; 格子细工; 方格装饰|A pattern of squares, typically of contrasting colors, resembling a checkerboard.  It can also refer to the material or structure formed in this pattern.|The floor was laid in a beautiful checkerwork of black and white marble. 地板铺着黑白相间的大理石格子图案。|synonyms: checkerboard pattern, tessellation, grid, mosaic|antonyms: solid color, plain, unpatterned|noun
