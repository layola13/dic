contribute:/kənˈtrɪbjuːt/ | v. | 贡献，捐助；投稿；促成 | To give something, especially money, in order to help achieve or provide something. To write articles or other material for a newspaper, magazine, or other publication. To be one of the factors that cause something to happen. |  He contributed a large sum of money to the charity. 他向慈善机构捐了一大笔钱。 She regularly contributes to the magazine. 她定期为该杂志投稿。 Stress can contribute to heart disease. 压力会导致心脏病。 | 近义词: donate, give, subscribe, furnish, provide | 反义词: withhold, detract | verb
