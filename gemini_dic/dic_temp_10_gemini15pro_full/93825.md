immigrants:/ˈɪmɪɡrənts/| n. |移民；侨民|People who have come to a new country to live there permanently.|Many immigrants come to this country seeking better opportunities. 许多移民来到这个国家寻求更好的机会。|The new immigrants quickly adapted to the local customs. 新移民很快适应了当地的风俗习惯。|近义词：newcomers, settlers, migrants|反义词:emigrants, natives|词性:noun
Etymology: From Latin "immigrantem" (nominative immigrans), present participle of immigrare "to move into, migrate to," from in- "into" + migrare "to depart, wander, change one's abode".
