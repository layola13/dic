halved:/hɑːvd/ |v. |减半，二等分|To reduce something by 50%; to divide something into two equal parts.|The company halved its workforce due to the recession. 由于经济衰退，公司裁员一半。|Synonyms: reduced, bisected|Antonyms: doubled, increased|verb
halved:/hɑːvd/ |adj. |减半的，二等分的|Reduced by 50%; divided into two equal parts.|The halved apple sat on the table. 被切成两半的苹果放在桌子上。|Synonyms: halved, bisected|Antonyms: doubled, whole|adjective
