expropriate: /ɛksˈproʊprieɪt/ (US) /ɛksˈprəʊprieɪt/ (UK)
verb
征用，没收，剥夺
To take property from its owner for public use, often with compensation.
The government expropriated the land for the construction of a new highway. 政府征用了这块土地来修建一条新的高速公路。
The dictator expropriated the assets of his political rivals. 独裁者没收了其政敌的资产。
Synonyms: seize, confiscate, appropriate, commandeer
Antonyms: return, restore, give back
