basely:/ˈbeɪsli/| adv.|卑鄙地，下流地|in a low and mean way; dishonorably.|He acted basely towards his friends, betraying their trust for his own gain. 他对他的朋友们卑鄙地行事，为了自己的利益背叛了他们的信任。|Synonyms: dishonorably, meanly, despicably, vilely, shamefully|Antonyms: nobly, honorably, virtuously, admirably|Word Type: Adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *basely*, from base + -ly.  Ultimately from Latin basis "base, foundation."
