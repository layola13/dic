illiberal:/ɪˈlɪbərəl/ | adj. | 不开明的，狭隘的，吝啬的 | Not liberal; narrow-minded, bigoted; stingy or mean. |  He held some illiberal views on the role of women in society. 他对女性在社会中的角色持有一些不开明的观点。

The charity criticized the government's illiberal immigration policy.  该慈善机构批评了政府狭隘的移民政策。 | 近义词: narrow-minded, bigoted, intolerant, prejudiced, ungenerous, mean | 反义词: liberal, tolerant, open-minded, generous | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin illiberalis, from in- "not" + liberalis "befitting a free person, noble, generous."
