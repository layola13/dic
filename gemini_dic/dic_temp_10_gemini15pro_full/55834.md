divider:/dɪˈvaɪdər/| n.|分隔物，分隔线，分配器|Something that divides or separates.|The room divider created two separate spaces.  这个房间隔板创造了两个独立的空间。|synonyms: separator, partition, barrier|antonyms: uniter, connector|noun

divider:/dɪˈvaɪdər/| n.|除数|A number by which another number is divided.|In the equation 10 ÷ 2 = 5, 2 is the divider. 在等式 10 ÷ 2 = 5 中，2 是除数。|synonyms: divisor|antonyms: dividend, quotient|noun
