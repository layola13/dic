oversacrificial: /ˌoʊvərˌsækrɪˈfɪʃəl/ | adj. | 过度牺牲的，牺牲过度的 | Characterized by sacrificing too much, often to one's own detriment.  Excessively willing to give up one's own needs and desires for others. |  She was oversacrificial in her relationship, always putting his needs before her own. 她在这段关系中过度牺牲，总是把他的需求放在自己的前面。|  He made an oversacrificial effort to save the company, working day and night and neglecting his health. 他为了挽救公司做出了过度牺牲的努力，夜以继日地工作，忽略了自己的健康。| 近义词: self-sacrificing, selfless, altruistic | 反义词: selfish, self-centered, egoistic | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "sacrificial" (adjective derived from "sacrifice," from Latin "sacrificium")
