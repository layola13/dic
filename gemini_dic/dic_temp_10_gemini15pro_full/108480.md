lay-to:/leɪ ˈtuː/
| phrasal verb |停航，使（船）停航；停止工作；停下 |To bring a sailing vessel to a standstill with the head turned slightly toward the wind and the sails arranged to keep the vessel in place. To stop working. |The captain decided to lay-to until the storm had passed. 船长决定停航，直到暴风雨过去。| 近义词：heave to, stop, cease, halt | 反义词: proceed, continue | 词性: 短语动词

lay-to:/leɪ ˈtuː/
| n. |停航 |The act or state of laying to.  |The lay-to lasted several hours while repairs were made. 停航持续了几个小时，期间进行了维修。| 近义词：stoppage, halt | 反义词: continuation | 词性: 名词
