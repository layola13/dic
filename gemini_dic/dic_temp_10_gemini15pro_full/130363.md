non-uniformity:/ˌnɒnˌjuːnɪˈfɔːmɪti/ /ˌnɑːnˌjuːnɪˈfɔːrməti/| n.|不均匀性，不一致性|The lack of uniformity in the data made it difficult to analyze.  数据的不均匀性使其难以分析。|Example: The non-uniformity of the paint job was noticeable. 油漆工作的不均匀性很明显。|Synonyms:  inconsistency, irregularity, unevenness, disparity, variation|Antonyms: uniformity, consistency, homogeneity, regularity|noun

non-uniform:/ˌnɒnˈjuːnɪfɔːm/ /ˌnɑːnˈjuːnɪfɔːrm/| adj.|不均匀的，不一致的|The non-uniform distribution of resources caused problems. 资源分配不均造成了问题。|Example: The fabric had a non-uniform texture. 这块布料的质地不均匀。|Synonyms: uneven, irregular, inconsistent, disparate, varying|Antonyms: uniform, consistent, homogeneous, regular|adjective
