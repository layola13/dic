dispark:/dɪsˈpɑːrk/
verb
剥夺…的公园权利; 取消…的停车权
To deprive (a place) of its status or privileges as a park.  To deprive (someone) of the right to park (a vehicle).
The city council voted to dispark the green space and build a parking lot. 市议会投票决定取消这片绿地的公园地位，并建造一个停车场。
The new regulations dispark residents who do not have a parking permit. 新规定取消了没有停车许可证的居民的停车权。
近义词:deprive, divest, strip
反义词:grant, award, bestow
词性:v.
