edged:/ɛdʒd/
| adj. | 有边的，锋利的，紧张的 | Having a sharp edge; sharpened; Also: tense, nervous. | The knife was edged and dangerous. 这把刀很锋利，很危险。| 近义词：sharp, keen, pointed, honed, tense, nervous | 反义词：blunt, dull, rounded, calm, relaxed | 词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ecgde, past participle of ecgian (“to provide with an edge”), from Proto-Germanic *agjōną (“to sharpen; to provide with a point”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂eǵʰ- (“sharp; point”).  Cognate with Old Norse eggja (“to sharpen; to provide with a point”), Old Frisian eggia (“to sharpen”), Dutch eggen (“to sharpen”), German eggen (“to harrow”), Gothic 𐌰𐌲𐌾𐌰𐌽 (agjan, “to frighten, terrify”).
