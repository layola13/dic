astrictive:/əˈstrɪktɪv/
adjective
收敛的;止泻的
Causing contraction, especially of organic tissues; styptic.  Tending to reduce or restrict.
The doctor prescribed an astrictive medication to control the patient's diarrhea. 医生给病人开了收敛性药物来控制腹泻。
synonyms:styptic, binding, contracting, constricting
antonyms:relaxing, dilating, loosening
