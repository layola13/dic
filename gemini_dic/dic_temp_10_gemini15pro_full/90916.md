huracán:/ˌhurəˈkɑːn/ | n. |飓风；暴风雨|A violent, tropical, cyclonic storm of the West Indies, with winds of 74 miles (119 kilometers) per hour or greater. |例句：The huracán devastated the coastal town. 飓风摧毁了沿海城镇。

例句：The huracán season is approaching. 飓风季节即将来临。|近义词：cyclone, typhoon, hurricane|反义词:calm, breeze|词性:noun
