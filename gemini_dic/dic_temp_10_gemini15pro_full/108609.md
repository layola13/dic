leadened:/ˈlɛd(ə)nd/| adj.|沉重的，沉闷的，呆滞的|Depressed and slow; made heavy, as if with lead.  It can describe both physical and emotional heaviness.|Her footsteps were leadened with grief. 她悲伤的脚步沉重。|Synonyms: heavy, weighted, dull, sluggish, listless|Antonyms: light, buoyant, energetic, lively|adjective
