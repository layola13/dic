fug:/fʌɡ/
noun
闷热，浑浊的空气
a warm, stuffy or smoky atmosphere.
例句：The fug in the room was unbearable after the party.  派对结束后，房间里的闷热空气令人难以忍受。

例句：Opening the windows helped clear the fug of cigarette smoke. 打开窗户有助于清除香烟烟雾的浑浊空气。

近义词：stuffiness, closeness, mustiness, smokiness
反义词：freshness, coolness, clarity
