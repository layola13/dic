banteringly:/ˈbæntərɪŋli/
adverb
开玩笑地，打趣地
In a playful and teasing way.
He banteringly suggested that she try wearing a clown suit to the party. 他开玩笑地建议她穿小丑服去参加派对。
She banteringly accused him of being afraid of spiders. 她打趣地指责他害怕蜘蛛。
Synonyms: playfully, teasingly, jokingly, humorously
Antonyms: seriously, earnestly, solemnly
词性: 副词
