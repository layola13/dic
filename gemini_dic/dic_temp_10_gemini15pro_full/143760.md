outmarched:/ˌaʊtˈmɑːrtʃt/
verb
超过…的行军速度；在行军中胜过
surpass in marching; to march faster or further than
例句：The smaller army outmarched the larger one and surprised them at dawn.  这支规模较小的军队在行军速度上超过了规模较大的军队，并在黎明时分突袭了他们。

例句：The experienced hikers easily outmarched the beginners.  经验丰富的徒步旅行者轻松地超过了初学者。
近义词：outrun, outstrip, surpass
反义词：lag behind, fall behind
词性：v.
