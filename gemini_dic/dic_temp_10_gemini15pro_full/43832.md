creosote: /ˈkriːəsəʊt/ (UK) /ˈkriːəsoʊt/ (US) | n. | 木馏油；杂酚油 | A dark brown oily liquid obtained from coal tar, used as a wood preservative. |  The fence posts were treated with creosote.  篱笆桩用杂酚油处理过。| synonyms: wood preservative, tar | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "natural sealant" in some contexts) | noun

creosotes: /ˈkriːəsəʊts/ (UK) /ˈkriːəsoʊts/ (US) | n. | 木馏油；杂酚油（复数）| Plural form of creosote, referring to multiple types or instances of the oily liquid. |  Different creosotes are produced depending on the type of coal used.  不同的煤会产生不同的杂酚油。| synonyms: wood preservatives, tars | antonyms: (No direct antonym) | noun
