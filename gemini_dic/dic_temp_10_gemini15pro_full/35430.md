co-owning:/kəʊˈəʊnɪŋ/
| adj. | 共同拥有 | Sharing ownership of something with another person or entity. |  Example: They are co-owning a vacation home in the mountains. 他们共同拥有一栋山间的度假别墅。 | Example:  Co-owning a business can be challenging but rewarding. 共同拥有一个企业可能充满挑战但也回报丰厚。| Synonym: joint ownership, shared ownership | Antonym: sole ownership, individual ownership | gerund/adjective
