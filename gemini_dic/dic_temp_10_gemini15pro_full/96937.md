inextinguishable:/ˌɪnɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbl/
adjective
不可熄灭的；无法扑灭的；不磨灭的
Not able to be extinguished, quenched, or suppressed.

Her inextinguishable spirit kept her fighting even when things seemed hopeless.
她不屈不挠的精神使她即使在事情看似毫无希望的时候也继续战斗。

The inextinguishable flames consumed the entire building.
无法扑灭的大火吞噬了整栋建筑。

Synonyms: unquenchable, ineradicable, enduring, persistent
Antonyms: extinguishable, quenchable, suppressible, temporary
词性: adjective
