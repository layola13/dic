at-large:/ət ˈlɑːrdʒ/ | adj. adv.| 全体地，普遍地；自由的，未被捕的；（官员）由全体选民选举的 | Representing a whole area or group, not just a part of it;  not confined or in custody; (of an official) elected to represent the whole of a political unit rather than a subdivision.| The mayor is elected at large. 市长是由全体市民选举产生的。 | Synonyms: general, widespread, overall, free, unconfined | Antonyms: local, confined, restricted | adjective, adverb

at-large:/ət ˈlɑːrdʒ/ | n.| 全体选民选举产生的官员 | An official elected to represent the whole of a political unit rather than a subdivision. |  Two at-larges were elected to the city council.  两位由全体选民选举产生的官员被选入了市议会。| Synonyms: representative, delegate | Antonyms: N/A | noun
