bargemaster:/ˈbɑːrdʒˌmæstər/| n.|驳船主，驳船船长|A person in charge of a barge or a fleet of barges.  This person is responsible for the safe and efficient operation of the vessel(s), including navigation, cargo handling, and crew management.|The bargemaster skillfully navigated the river. 驳船船长熟练地驾驶着驳船在河上航行。| The bargemaster oversaw the loading of the cargo. 驳船船长监督货物的装载。|Synonyms: captain, skipper, boatman|Antonyms: passenger, crewman|noun
