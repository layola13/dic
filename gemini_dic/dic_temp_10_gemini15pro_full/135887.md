nonprovocation:/ˌnɑːnˌprɑːvəˈkeɪʃən/| n. |不挑衅，无故|Absence of provocation; the state of not provoking or inciting. |The attack was an act of unprovoked aggression, a clear case of nonprovocation on our part.|近义词:peacefulness, nonaggression, pacifism|反义词:provocation, aggression, incitement|词性:名词

Etymology:
From non- + provocation.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "provocation" comes from the Late Latin *provocātiōn-, from the Latin provocāre "to call forth, challenge."
