obdurate:/ˈɒbdjʊrət/ (UK) /ˈɑːbdərət/ (US)| adjective| 顽固的，执拗的，冷酷无情的|Stubbornly persistent in wrongdoing, hardened in feelings; resistant to persuasion or softening influences.|He remained obdurate, refusing to admit his mistake. 他仍然顽固不化，拒绝承认自己的错误。|Synonyms: stubborn, inflexible, unyielding, adamant, intractable|Antonyms: yielding, compliant, flexible, amenable|adjective
