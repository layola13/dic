over-hurriedly:/ˌoʊvərˈhɜːrɪdli/ | adv. | 过于匆忙地 | Done too quickly and with too much haste. |  She over-hurriedly packed her bags, forgetting her passport. 她过于匆忙地收拾行李，忘记了护照。|  He over-hurriedly signed the contract without reading it carefully. 他过于匆忙地签了合同，没有仔细阅读。 | hastily, precipitately, too quickly | slowly, deliberately, carefully | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (excessive) + "hurriedly" (in a hurried manner).  "Hurried" comes from the verb "hurry," which has uncertain origins, possibly related to Middle Dutch "horren" (to shake, move quickly).
