cocomposer:/ˌkoʊkəmˈpoʊzər/ | n. | 合作者；共同作曲家 | A person who composes something, especially music, with another person. | Elton John and Bernie Taupin were frequent cocomposers. 艾尔顿·约翰和伯尼·陶平是经常合作的作曲家。|  John Lennon and Paul McCartney were cocomposers for many of the Beatles' most famous songs.  约翰·列侬和保罗·麦卡特尼共同创作了许多披头士乐队最著名的歌曲。 | collaborator, joint composer |  (No common antonym) | noun
