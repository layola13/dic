desirableness:/dɪˌzaɪərəblənəs/
n.
可取性；值得向往；合意
The quality of being desirable; desirability.
The desirableness of a quiet life in the countryside appealed to her.  她向往宁静的乡村生活。
近义词：desirability, attractiveness, appeal
反义词：undesirability, unattractiveness, repulsion
词性：名词
