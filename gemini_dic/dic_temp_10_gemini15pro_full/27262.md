calotte:/kəˈlɒt/ /kəˈlɑːt/| n.|小帽；头盖；冰冠|A small, close-fitting cap or skullcap, especially one worn by Catholic clergy. Also, a spherical cap or segment of a sphere, such as the ice cap of a glacier.|The cardinal's calotte was scarlet.  红衣主教的小帽是猩红色的。

The calotte of the glacier was slowly melting. 冰川的冰冠正在慢慢融化。|近义词：skullcap, cap, beanie, ice cap|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "base" if referring to a glacier)|noun
