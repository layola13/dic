inauspiciousness:/ˌɪnɔːˈspɪʃəsnəs/ | n. | 不祥，不吉利 | The state or quality of being inauspicious; unfavorableness.  It refers to a lack of promise or a suggestion of bad luck. | The inauspiciousness of the dark clouds gathering overhead made us postpone the picnic.  头顶乌云密布的不祥之兆让我们推迟了野餐。 | synonyms: unfavorableness, ominousness, adversity, misfortune | antonyms: auspiciousness, favorableness, prosperity, fortune | noun
