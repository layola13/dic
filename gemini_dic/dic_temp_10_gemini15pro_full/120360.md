minimizes:/ˈmɪnɪmaɪzɪz/
verb
最小化;使减到最小
reduces something to the least possible level or amount
The company minimizes its costs by outsourcing production.  公司通过外包生产来最大限度地降低成本。
Streamlining the process minimizes the risk of errors. 简化流程最大限度地减少了错误的风险。
minimize, decrease, reduce, lessen
maximize, increase, amplify, enlarge
verb
