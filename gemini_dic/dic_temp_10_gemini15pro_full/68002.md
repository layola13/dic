factitiousness:/ˌfækˈtɪʃəsnəs/
n.
人为性；不自然；矫揉造作
the quality of being produced artificially rather than naturally.
Her elaborate politeness was pure factitiousness. 她过分的礼貌纯粹是装出来的。
factitiousness: artificiality, unnaturalness, contrivance, affectedness, pretense
factitiousness: genuineness, authenticity, naturalness, sincerity, artlessness
