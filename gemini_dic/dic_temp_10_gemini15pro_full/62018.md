enantiopathy:/ɪˌnæntiˈɒpəθi/ | n. | 对抗疗法; 对抗治疗 | A system of treating disease by producing a condition incompatible with or antagonistic to the condition to be cured or alleviated. |  Enantiopathy is a medical philosophy that suggests treating diseases with remedies that produce opposite effects to the symptoms. | 近义词: allopathy, heteropathy | 反义词: homeopathy, isopathy | noun
