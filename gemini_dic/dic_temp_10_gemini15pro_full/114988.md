mandalas:/ˈmændələz/ (US), /ˈmændələz/ (UK)| n.|曼荼罗;坛场|A mandala (Sanskrit: मण्डल, maṇḍala, literally "circle") is a geometric configuration of symbols, typically in the form of a circle divided into sections, often including a square and various other geometric patterns. It is used in various spiritual traditions, particularly Hinduism and Buddhism, as a tool for meditation, visualization, and spiritual growth.  The mandala represents the universe in its totality and is often seen as a microcosm of the macrocosm.|She creates intricate mandalas using colored sand. 她用彩沙创作复杂的曼荼罗。|

The museum houses a collection of Tibetan mandalas.  这家博物馆收藏了藏族曼荼罗。|近义词:diagram, symbol, pattern|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, potentially "chaos" or "disorder" in a philosophical sense)|Noun
