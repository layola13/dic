hastened:/ˈheɪsənd/| v.| 加速，催促，赶紧|to make something happen sooner or more quickly|例句：The bad news hastened his departure. 坏消息促使他赶紧离开。|近义词：accelerated, expedited, hurried, rushed|反义词:delayed, slowed, hindered|verb

hastened:/ˈheɪsnd/| v.| 加速，催促，赶紧|to move or act with speed; to hurry|例句：He hastened to assure her that everything was alright. 他急忙向她保证一切都没事。|近义词：sped, hurried, rushed|反义词:delayed, slowed, dawdled|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *hasten, from Old Norse *hasta (“to hasten”), from Proto-Germanic *haistjaną (“to urge on, incite”).  Cognate with Icelandic hesta (“to urge on”), Swedish hasta (“to hasten”).
