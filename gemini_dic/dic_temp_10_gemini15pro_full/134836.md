nonmitigation:/ˌnɒnmɪtɪˈɡeɪʃən/
n.|不缓解，不减轻|The nonmitigation of the problem led to a disaster. 这个问题没有得到缓解，导致了一场灾难。|Absence of mitigation, failure to mitigate.|例句：The nonmitigation of greenhouse gas emissions will have dire consequences.  不减少温室气体排放将造成可怕的后果。

例句：His nonmitigation of his anger led to a heated argument. 他没有缓和自己的愤怒，导致了激烈的争吵。

近义词：nonreduction, exacerbation|反义词:mitigation, alleviation|词性:名词
