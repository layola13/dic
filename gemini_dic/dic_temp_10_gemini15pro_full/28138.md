capitulants:/kəˈpɪtʃʊlənts/
n.
capitulants (plural)
地方分会会长；地方分会会员
Members of a chapter, especially of a religious order.
The capitulants gathered for their annual meeting. 地方分会会员聚集在一起召开年度会议。
Synonyms: members, delegates, representatives
Antonyms: non-members
noun
