inroads:/ˈɪnˌroʊdz/
n.
进展， inroads (通常用复数)
an advance or penetration, especially into something new or formerly untouched.
例句：The company has made significant inroads into the Chinese market.  这家公司已经在中国市场取得了重大进展。

例句：They made inroads into enemy territory. 他们攻入了敌方领土。

近义词：advances, gains, progress, penetration
反义词：retreats, losses, setbacks
词性: 名词
