elongation:/iːlɒŋˈɡeɪʃən/ (US) /ˌiːlɒŋˈɡeɪʃn/ (UK)| n.|伸长; 延长; 拉长|The act of making something longer, or the state of being made longer.|例句：The elongation of the metal rod was caused by heating. 金属棒的伸长是由加热引起的。|例句：Muscle elongation is important for flexibility.  肌肉拉伸对柔韧性很重要。|近义词：lengthening, extension, stretching|反义词：shortening, contraction, compression|词性:名词

elongate:/iːˈlɒŋɡeɪt/ (US) /iːˈlɒŋɡeɪt/ (UK)| v.|伸长; 延长; 拉长|To make something longer, or to become longer.|例句：The shadows elongated as the sun set.  随着太阳落下，影子变长了。|例句：Regular stretching can help elongate your muscles.  经常拉伸有助于拉长你的肌肉。|近义词：lengthen, extend, stretch|反义词：shorten, contract, compress|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin ēlongātiōn- (nominative ēlongātiō), from ēlongāre ‘to lengthen’, from e- (variant of ex-) ‘out’ + longus ‘long’.
