mistletoe: /ˈmɪs(ə)ltəʊ/ (UK), /ˈmɪsəlˌtoʊ/ (US) | n. |槲寄生|A leathery-leaved parasitic plant that grows on apple, oak, and other broadleaf trees and bears white berries in winter.  It is traditionally hung at Christmas, and people kiss under it. | We hung mistletoe in the doorway. 我们在门口挂了槲寄生。|  A kiss under the mistletoe is a Christmas tradition. 在槲寄生下亲吻是一个圣诞节传统。 | 近义词: None (in the sense of the plant itself).  Figuratively:  Christmas decoration| 反义词: None | Noun
