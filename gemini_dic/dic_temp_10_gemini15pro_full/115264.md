manipulus:/məˈnɪpjʊləs/ | n. | 一把；一小队；一撮；（古罗马）百人队 | A handful, a small band, a maniple (a unit in the Roman army of about 120 men). |  The centurion commanded a manipulus of soldiers. 百夫长指挥着一支百人队。| synonym: handful, band, troop, company | antonym:  legion, army, horde | noun

manipulus:/məˈnɪpjʊləs/ | n. | （古罗马）一把（谷物）；一束（药草） | A handful (of grain or herbs) in ancient Rome. | The farmer brought a manipulus of wheat to the market. 农民带了一把小麦到市场上去卖。| synonym: handful, bunch, sheaf | antonym:  load, stack | noun
