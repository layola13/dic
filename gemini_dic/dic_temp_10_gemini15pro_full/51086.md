despecification:/ˌdɪˌspesɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
取消规格;  去除详细说明;  泛化
The process of removing or reducing the level of detail in a specification, often for the purpose of abstraction, generalization, or simplification.
The despecification of the product design allowed for a wider range of manufacturers to bid on the project.  产品设计的取消规格允许更广泛的制造商参与项目竞标。

The despecification of the job requirements led to a larger pool of applicants. 简化工作要求导致了更大的申请人群体。
近义词: generalization, simplification, abstraction
反义词: specification, detailing, elaboration
词性: noun
