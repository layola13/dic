envisagement:/ɪnˈvɪzɪdʒmənt/ | n. | 设想，展望，构想 | The act of forming a mental picture or concept of something. |  Example 1:  The architect's envisagement of the new building was breathtaking.  这位建筑师对新建筑的构想令人叹为观止。

Example 2:  Her envisagement of the future was optimistic and full of hope. 她对未来的展望是乐观且充满希望的。 | 近义词: conception, vision, imagination, perception | 反义词:  None (lack of envisagement might be considered an opposite concept) | 词性: 名词

词源:
