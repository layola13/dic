hyperpigmented:/ˌhaɪpərˈpɪɡmɛntɪd/| adj.|色素沉着的，色素过多的|Characterized by hyperpigmentation, having an abnormally high concentration of melanin in the skin, resulting in darker patches or areas.|Her skin was hyperpigmented in patches after sun exposure.  她的皮肤在日晒后出现斑块状色素沉着。|Synonyms: darkened, discolored, melanized|Antonyms: hypopigmented, depigmented|Adjective
