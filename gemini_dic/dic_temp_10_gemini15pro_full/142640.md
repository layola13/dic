ostosis:/ɒˈstəʊsɪs/ /ɑːˈstəʊsɪs/
n.
骨质增生，骨化
The formation of new bone tissue.
Example: The doctor diagnosed the patient with ostosis in his knee. 医生诊断病人膝盖骨质增生。

Example:  The X-ray revealed a significant area of ostosis on the femur. X光显示股骨上有明显的骨质增生区域。

Synonym:  ossification, calcification
Antonym:  osteolysis, bone resorption
noun
