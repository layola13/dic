astrologian:/əˈstrɒlədʒən/ | n. | 占星家 | A person who practices astrology; one who predicts the supposed influences of the stars and planets on human destinies. |  例句：The astrologian predicted a favorable outcome. 占星家预测结果会很顺利。

例句：She consulted an astrologian about her future. 她向一位占星家咨询她的未来。| 近义词：astrologer, stargazer | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "scientist" in a contrasting context) | 词性: noun
