maxest: /ˈmæksɪst/
adjective
最大的, 最多的 (non-standard, humorous or informal)
A superlative form of "max" (meaning maximum), used humorously or informally to indicate the greatest possible extent or degree.  It's not considered standard English.
He gave it his maxest effort. 他尽了最大的努力。
近义词: maximum, greatest, utmost, highest, biggest
反义词: least, smallest, minimum, lowest
词性: adjective
