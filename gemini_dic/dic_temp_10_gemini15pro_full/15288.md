backlog:/ˈbæklɒɡ/ (US) /ˈbæklɒɡ/ (UK)| n.|积压的工作，待办事项|An accumulation of unfinished work or matters needing attention.|例句：The company has a huge backlog of orders. 这家公司积压了大量订单。

例句：She's facing a backlog of emails after her vacation. 她休假回来后要处理一大堆电子邮件。

近义词：arrears, accumulation, buildup|反义词:completion, clearance|词性:名词
