nonambitiously:/ˌnɒnæmˈbɪʃəsli/ | adv. | 没有野心地，不求上进地 |  In a manner lacking ambition or a strong desire to achieve success. | He nonambitiously accepted a low-paying job. 他不求上进地接受了一份低薪工作。| Synonyms: unambitiously, half-heartedly, lackadaisically | Antonyms: ambitiously, zealously, eagerly | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + ambitious + -ly (adverb suffix).  "Ambitious" comes from the Latin "ambitiosus," meaning "going around, canvassing for office," from "ambitio" ("a going around").
