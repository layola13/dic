overcerebral:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛrəbrəl/
adjective
过度思考的; 过于理性的
excessively intellectual; prone to overthinking.  Characterized by excessive intellectual activity at the expense of  feelings and emotions.  
He was so overcerebral that he couldn't make a simple decision without analyzing it to death. 他过度思考，以至于不把一个简单的决定分析到极致就无法做出决定。

She tends to be overcerebral, often getting lost in thought and missing out on the present moment. 她倾向于过度思考，经常沉浸在思考中而错过当下。

近义词: intellectual, analytical, cerebral, highbrow, bookish, studious
反义词: instinctive, intuitive, visceral, emotional, impulsive, unthinking
词性: adjective
