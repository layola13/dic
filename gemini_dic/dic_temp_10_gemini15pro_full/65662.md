evasive:/ɪˈveɪsɪv/ /iˈveisiv/| adj.|逃避的，回避的，托辞的|Tending to avoid commitment or self-revelation, especially by responding only indirectly.|The politician gave an evasive answer to the journalist's question.  这位政客对记者的提问给出了一个回避的答案。|Synonyms: elusive, ambiguous, equivocal, vague|Antonyms: direct, forthright, honest, open|adjective

evasive:/ɪˈveɪsɪv/ /iˈveisiv/| n.|回避，逃避|The act of avoiding commitment or self-revelation.|His evasiveness made her suspicious. 他的逃避让她起了疑心。|Synonyms: avoidance, elusion, equivocation|Antonyms: directness, forthrightness, honesty, openness|noun
