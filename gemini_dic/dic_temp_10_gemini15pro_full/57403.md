dpi:/diː piː ˈaɪ/| n.|每英寸点数|dots per inch, a measure of printing or video display resolution. It refers to the number of individual dots that can be placed in a line within the span of one inch.|The printer has a resolution of 600 dpi. 这台打印机的分辨率为600 dpi.|近义词：resolution|反义词: (No direct antonym, but a lower dpi indicates lower resolution)|词性:noun
