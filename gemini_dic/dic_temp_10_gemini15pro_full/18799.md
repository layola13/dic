berrying:/ˈber.i.ɪŋ/
verb
采摘浆果
The act of gathering berries.
例句：We spent the afternoon berrying in the woods. 我们下午在树林里采摘浆果。

例句：Berrying is a popular summertime activity. 采摘浆果是一项受欢迎的夏季活动。

近义词：gathering, harvesting, picking
反义词：(没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 planting 种植)
词性：动词 (present participle of berry)
