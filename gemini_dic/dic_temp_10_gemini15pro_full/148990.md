ovology:/oʊˈvɒlədʒi/
n.
卵子学，蛋类学
The study of eggs, especially bird eggs.
Ovology plays a crucial role in ornithological research. 卵子学在鸟类学研究中起着至关重要的作用。
Scientists specializing in ovology are studying the effects of pollution on bird eggs.  专门研究卵子学的科学家正在研究污染对鸟蛋的影响。
