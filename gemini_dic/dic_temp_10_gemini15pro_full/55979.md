dmg:/ˈdiː ɛm ˈdʒiː/ | n. | damage（的缩写）|  Short for "damage".  It's used informally, especially in gaming and online contexts, to refer to the amount of damage something inflicts. | The new weapon does a lot of dmg.  这新武器能造成很大的伤害。| 近义词: damage, harm, injury | 反义词: repair, heal | abbreviation
