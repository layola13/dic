inimitably:/ɪˈnɪmɪtəbli/| adv.|无法模仿地，独特地|In a way that cannot be imitated; uniquely. |Her performance was inimitably brilliant. 她的表演才华横溢，无人能及。|He delivered the speech with his inimitable wit and charm.  他以其独特的智慧和魅力发表了演讲。|近义词：uniquely, matchlessly, peerlessly|反义词：imitably, ordinarily, commonly|词性: 副词

词源: From Late Latin *inimitabilis, from in- ‘not’ + imitabilis (from imitari ‘to imitate’).
