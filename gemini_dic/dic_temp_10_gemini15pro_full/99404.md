interdiscipline:/ˌɪntərˈdɪsəplɪn/ | n. | 交叉学科，跨学科 |  A field of study that draws upon two or more disciplines.  It combines or involves two or more academic disciplines or fields of study. |  Environmental science is a classic example of an interdiscipline. 环境科学是交叉学科的典型例子。

The development of new technologies often spurs the creation of new interdisciplines. 新技术的开发常常会促进新的交叉学科的产生。| 近义词: cross-discipline, multidisciplinary, transdisciplinary | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun
