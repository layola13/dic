concordant:/kənˈkɔːrdənt/| adj.|一致的，和谐的，符合的|Being in agreement or harmony; corresponding.|The jury returned a concordant verdict. 陪审团作出一致的裁决。|近义词：harmonious, consistent, agreeable, compatible|反义词：discordant, conflicting, disagreeing, incompatible|词性:形容词
词源:Late Middle English: from Latin concordant- ‘agreeing’, from the verb concordare (see concord).
