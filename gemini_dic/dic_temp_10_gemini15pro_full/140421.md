okay:/oʊˈkeɪ/ /ˈəʊkeɪ/| adj. adv. int. n.|好的，可以，顺利|Used to express agreement or acceptance.|Okay, I'll do it. 好的，我会做的。|近义词:all right, fine, good, alright|反义词:no, bad, awful|词性:adjective, adverb, interjection, noun

词源: Origin uncertain, possibly from Choctaw *okeh, "it is so." Popularized in the 1830s in Boston as an abbreviation of "oll korrect" (all correct), a humorous misspelling.
