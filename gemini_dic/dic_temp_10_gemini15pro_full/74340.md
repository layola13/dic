fragmentarinesses:/ˌfræɡmənˈtɛrɪnəsɪz/
|n.|碎片化，片段性|The state of being fragmentary; consisting of fragments.  The quality of being incomplete or disconnected.|The fragmentarinesses of her memories made it difficult to piece together a coherent narrative. 她记忆的碎片化使得很难拼凑出一个连贯的故事。|Near synonyms:  disjointedness, disconnectedness, incompleteness|Antonyms: wholeness, completeness, unity
|noun
