marsebanker:/ˈmɑːrsˌbæŋkər/| n.|火星银行家 (比喻意义上的)|A person who makes promises or predictions about Mars colonization or development that are considered unrealistic, overly optimistic, or impossible.  Someone who speculates or invests in ventures related to Mars that are highly speculative or unlikely to yield returns.  Often used derisively.|例句：He's just another marsebanker promising lunar vacations by 2030. 他只是另一个承诺到2030年就能去月球度假的火星银行家。

例句：Investors should be wary of marsebankers peddling schemes that sound too good to be true. 投资者应该警惕那些兜售好得令人难以置信的火星计划的“火星银行家”。|近义词：dreamer, speculator, visionary (sometimes)|反义词: realist, pragmatist|词性:noun
