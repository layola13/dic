bubonic:/bjuːˈbɒnɪk/ (US) /bjuːˈbɑːnɪk/| adj.|腺鼠疫的；腹股沟淋巴结肿大的|Relating to or denoting bubonic plague, which is characterized by painful swellings of the lymph nodes, especially in the groin or armpit.|The bubonic plague devastated medieval Europe. 腺鼠疫摧毁了中世纪的欧洲。|Synonyms: pestilential, contagious, infectious|Antonyms: healthy, non-contagious|adjective
