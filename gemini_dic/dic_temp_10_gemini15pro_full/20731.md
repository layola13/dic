blames:/bleɪmz/ | v. | 责备，归咎 | to say or think that someone or something did something wrong or is responsible for something bad happening | He blames his mother for his shyness. 他把自己的害羞归咎于他的母亲。| synonyms: accuses, reproaches, criticizes | antonyms: praises, commends, credits | verb (third-person singular simple present)

blames:/bleɪmz/ | n. | 责备，指责  | an expression of disapproval; a rebuke | He took the blame for the accident. 他承担了事故的责任。| synonyms: criticism, reproach, censure | antonyms: praise, commendation, credit | noun (plural)
