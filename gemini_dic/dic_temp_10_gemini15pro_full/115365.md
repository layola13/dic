manpower:/ˈmænpaʊər/| n.|人力；劳动力；人手|The project was delayed due to a lack of manpower.  这个项目由于缺乏人力而被推迟了。|例句：The factory needs more manpower to increase production.  这家工厂需要更多的人力来提高产量。

例句：There is a shortage of skilled manpower in the construction industry. 建筑行业缺乏熟练的劳动力。

近义词：workforce, labor force, personnel, staff|反义词: automation, machinery |词性: 名词

Etymology: From man + power.  Attested from the late 16th century.
