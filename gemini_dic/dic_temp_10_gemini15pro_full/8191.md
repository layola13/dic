anti-dogmatical:/ˌænti-dɒɡˈmætɪkl/ | adj. | 反教条的，反对教条主义的 | Opposing or rejecting dogma; characterized by a lack of adherence to rigid doctrines or beliefs.  |  He took an anti-dogmatical approach to religion, preferring personal experience over strict adherence to doctrine. 他对宗教采取了反教条主义的态度，更喜欢个人体验而不是严格遵守教义。 | synonyms: open-minded, liberal, tolerant, flexible, undogmatic | antonyms: dogmatic, doctrinaire, rigid, inflexible, orthodox | adjective
