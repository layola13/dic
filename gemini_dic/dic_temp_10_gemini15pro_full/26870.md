cajoling:/kəˈdʒoʊlɪŋ/
verb
哄骗，劝诱
persuading someone to do something by sustained coaxing or flattery.
He cajoled his parents into letting him go to the concert. 他哄骗父母让他去听音乐会。
近义词: coaxing, persuading, wheedling, enticing
反义词:  coercing, forcing, demanding
词性: Verb (动词)
