cottoner:/ˈkɒtnər (UK) / ˈkɑːtnər (US)| n.|拥护奴隶制者，轧棉工人|A person who supports or profits from slavery, especially in the production of cotton.  Alternatively, it can refer to a worker in a cotton mill. |He was a wealthy cottoner who owned vast plantations. 他是一个拥有广阔种植园的富有的奴隶主。| 近义词：slaveholder, plantation owner, mill worker (in the second sense) | 反义词：abolitionist |词性: 名词

cottoner:/ˈkɒtnər (UK) / ˈkɑːtnər (US)| n.|轧棉工人|A worker in a cotton mill.|She worked as a cottoner for many years. 她做了多年的轧棉工人。| 近义词：mill worker, textile worker | 反义词：None (for this sense) |词性: 名词
