consultive:/kənˈsʌltɪv/| adj.|咨询的，顾问的|Providing advice or recommendations as a consultant.  Relating to or characteristic of consultation.|例句：She has a consultive role in the project. 她在项目中担任咨询角色。|近义词：advisory, consulting|反义词:executive, decisive|词性:adjective
consultive:/kənˈsʌltɪv/| adj.|协商的|Decided or done after consultation with others.|例句：The decision was made after a consultive process.  这个决定是在协商程序之后作出的。|近义词：collaborative, cooperative|反义词:unilateral, autocratic|词性:adjective
