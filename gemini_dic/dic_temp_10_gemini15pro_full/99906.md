internationalists:/ɪntərˈnæʃənəlɪsts/
n.|国际主义者|A person who advocates or believes in internationalism.|例句：The internationalists hoped for a world without borders. 国际主义者希望建立一个没有国界的世界。

例句：Many internationalists advocate for global cooperation to address climate change. 许多国际主义者主张通过全球合作来应对气候变化。
|近义词：cosmopolitans, globalists|反义词：nationalists, isolationists|词性:Noun

Etymology:
From international + -ist.  "International" comes from French *international*, from inter- (between) + national (national). The suffix "-ist" forms agent nouns.
