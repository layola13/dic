hyperbarbarously:/ˌhaɪpərˈbɑːrbərəsli/
| adv. |极其野蛮地，极端残暴地 |  In an extremely barbarous or cruel manner; with excessive savagery. | The prisoners were treated hyperbarbarously, subjected to unspeakable tortures.  这些囚犯受到了极端残暴的对待，遭受了难以言喻的折磨。| 近义词：savagely, brutally, cruelly, inhumanly | 反义词: humanely, kindly, compassionately | 词性: 副词
