chestiness:/ˈtʃɛstɪnɪs/
n.
丰满的胸部；傲慢自大
The quality of having a large or prominent chest, often associated with arrogance or self-importance.
She exuded a certain chestiness that intimidated some people. 她流露出某种傲慢自大的气质，让一些人感到害怕。
近义词：haughtiness, arrogance, pomposity
反义词：humility, modesty
词性：名词

chestiness:/ˈtʃɛstɪnɪs/
n.
（尤指男子）胸部发达
The condition of having a well-developed chest, especially in men.
His chestiness was evident beneath his tight shirt.  他发达的胸肌透过紧身衬衫显露出来。
近义词：muscularity, burliness
反义词：frailty, weakness
词性：名词
