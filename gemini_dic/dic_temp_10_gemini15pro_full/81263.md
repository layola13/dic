granitiform:/ɡræˈnɪtɪfɔːrm/
adjective
花岗岩状的；似花岗岩的
Resembling granite in texture or appearance.
The rock displayed a granitiform texture, with interlocking crystals of various minerals.  这块岩石呈现出花岗岩状的质地，各种矿物晶体相互交错。
The solidified magma had a granitiform structure. 凝固的岩浆具有花岗岩状结构。
granitic, phaneritic
n/a
adjective
