disuniting:/dɪsjuːˈnaɪtɪŋ/
verb, adjective|使分裂，不团结的|Causing separation or disharmony; divisive.  Derived from "dis-" (not, apart) + "unite" (to join together).|The disuniting forces of prejudice and discrimination are tearing our community apart. 偏见和歧视的分裂力量正在撕裂我们的社区。|近义词：dividing, separating, alienating, estranging|反义词：uniting, joining, connecting, reconciling
word type: verb, adjective
