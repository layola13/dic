deculassement:/deɪˈklɑːsmɑːn/  /dəˈklɑːsmɑːŋ/
noun
降级，下层化
A decline in social status.  The process by which a person or group moves down the social scale.
例句：
He experienced a sense of declassement after losing his job and having to move to a smaller apartment.
他失业后不得不搬到更小的公寓，经历了一种社会地位下降的感觉。

例句：
The economic recession led to the declassement of many middle-class families.
经济衰退导致许多中产阶级家庭社会地位下降。

近义词：
downgrading, demotion, social decline
反义词：
upward mobility, social advancement, promotion
