pannings:/ˈpænɪŋz/| n. | 淘洗；淘出的东西；严厉批评；抨击 | The act of washing loose earth, sand, gravel, etc., in a pan to separate out heavier minerals, especially gold. Also, harsh criticism or censure. | The gold miner's pannings yielded a small amount of gold dust. / The play received harsh pannings from several critics. | 近义词：criticism, censure, reviews, panning down (for criticism) | 反义词：praise, acclaim, accolades | noun
