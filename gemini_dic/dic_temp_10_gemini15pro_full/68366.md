fallible:/ˈfæləbl/| adj.|易犯错误的；可能出错的|Liable to err, especially in being deceived or mistaken. |Humans are fallible creatures. 人类是易犯错误的生物。

Even the most experienced doctors are fallible. 即使是最有经验的医生也可能犯错。

近义词：errant, flawed, imperfect, liable to err|反义词：infallible, perfect, unerring|词性：形容词
