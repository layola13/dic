hurl:/hɜːrl/ (US) /hɜːl/ (UK)| n.|猛投，猛掷；叫骂|The act of throwing something with great force.|The hurl of the stone broke the window. 石头的猛烈投掷打破了窗户。|近义词：throw, fling, cast, pitch, toss|反义词:catch|词性:名词

词源：From Old French *hurler*, of Germanic origin. Related to Dutch horlen (“to whirl, roll”).
