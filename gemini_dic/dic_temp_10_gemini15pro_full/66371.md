excusingly:/ɪkˈskjuːzɪŋli/| adv.|辩解地，抱歉地，原谅地|In a way that offers an excuse or apology.  It implies a slight sense of guilt or regret, often accompanied by a mitigating explanation.|He smiled excusingly and explained that he was late due to traffic. 他带着歉意笑了笑，解释说他因为交通堵塞而迟到了。|近义词：apologetically, regretfully|反义词: unapologetically, defiantly|词性: 副词
词源: From "excuse" (v.) + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Excuse" comes from Old French "excuser," from Latin "excusare" meaning "to free from blame."
