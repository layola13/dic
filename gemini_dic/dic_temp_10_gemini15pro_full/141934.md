ordination:/ˌɔːrdɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. |任命，授职；排序，排列；协调 |The act of ordaining someone into a religious office, or the act of arranging things in a particular order or sequence. |例句：The ordination of women as priests is still a controversial issue.  女性被任命为神父仍然是一个有争议的问题。


|例句：The ordination of the data allowed for easier analysis.  数据的排序使得分析更加容易。 |近义词：appointment, commissioning, installation, inauguration, ordering, arrangement, organization |反义词：dismissal, removal, chaos, disorder |词性:noun
