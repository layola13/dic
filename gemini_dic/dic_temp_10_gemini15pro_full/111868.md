logjammed:/ˈlɒɡdʒæmd/ (US), /ˈlɒɡdʒæmd/ (UK)| adj.| 堵塞的，停滞不前的|A situation that resembles a logjam, especially one in which progress is blocked.|The construction project was logjammed by disagreements between the city and the developers.  该建设项目由于市政府和开发商之间的分歧而陷入停滞。|近义词：blocked, stalled, gridlocked, congested|反义词：flowing, progressing, moving, unblocked|词性:形容词

Etymology:  From log + jam (n.), referring to logs jamming together in a river, hindering passage.  来源于log（原木） + jam（堵塞），指的是原木在河流中堵塞在一起，阻碍通行。
