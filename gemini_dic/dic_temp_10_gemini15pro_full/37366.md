commensurable:/kəˈmɛnʃərəbl/ /kəˈmɛnsərəbəl/
adjective
可公度的，相称的
able to be measured by the same standard; proportionate, corresponding in size or degree.
例句：The two lengths are commensurable. 这两段长度是可公度的。
例句：Their contributions to the project were commensurable. 他们对这个项目的贡献是相称的。
近义词：proportionate, comparable, commensurate, similar, equivalent
反义词：incommensurable, disproportionate, disparate, unequal, dissimilar
词性: adjective

commensurate:/kəˈmɛnʃərɪt/ /kəˈmɛnʃərət/
adjective
相称的，相当的
corresponding in size or degree; proportionate.
例句：Her salary is commensurate with her experience. 她的薪水与她的经验相称。
例句：The punishment should be commensurate with the crime. 惩罚应该与罪行相称。
近义词：proportionate, comparable, commensurable, appropriate, fitting
反义词：incommensurate, disproportionate, inadequate, unsuitable, unfit
词性: adjective
