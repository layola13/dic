bedhead: /ˈbedˌhed/ (US), /ˈbedhed/ (UK)| n.|乱蓬蓬的头发；床头板|A tousled and messy hairstyle resulting from sleep, or the headboard of a bed.|例句：I woke up with terrible bedhead this morning. 我今天早上醒来时头发乱糟糟的。

例句：She leaned against the bedhead and read a book. 她靠在床头板上看书。

|近义词：sleep hair, messy hair, headboard|反义词: combed hair, styled hair|词性:noun
