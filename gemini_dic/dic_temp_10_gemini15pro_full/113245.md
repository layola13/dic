lustiest:/ˈlʌstiɪst/| adj.|最强壮的；最健康的；最旺盛的；最热烈的|Having or showing great vigor, health, and enthusiasm; hearty.|例句：He gave the lustiest cheer of all. 他发出了最热烈的欢呼。|近义词：vigorous, robust, hearty, enthusiastic|反义词：weak, feeble, frail, listless|词性：形容词
词源：From Middle English *lusti, from Old English *lustī, from Proto-Germanic *lustijaz (“pleasing, desirable, joyful”), from Proto-Indo-European *lewdʰ- (“to grow, be green”). Cognate with Dutch lustig (“cheerful, merry”), German lustig (“funny, merry”), Swedish lustig (“funny, merry”). More at lust.
