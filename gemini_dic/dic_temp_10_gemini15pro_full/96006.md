incursion:/ɪnˈkɜːrʒən/ |/ɪnˈkɜːrʒn/| n. |入侵，袭击，侵犯|An invasion or attack, especially a sudden or brief one.  A harmful intrusion. |例句：The enemy made several incursions into our territory. 敌人多次入侵我国领土。

例句：The incursion of technology into traditional industries has led to significant changes. 科技对传统产业的侵入带来了巨大的变化。

|近义词：invasion, raid, attack, foray, intrusion|反义词：retreat, withdrawal|词性：名词
