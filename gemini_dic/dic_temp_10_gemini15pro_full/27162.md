calla:/ˈkælə/| n. |马蹄莲；水芋|A plant of the arum family, with a large, typically white, spathe enclosing a yellow spadix.|例句：The calla lilies in the garden were in full bloom. 花园里的马蹄莲盛开了。|例句：She carried a bouquet of white callas at her wedding.  她在婚礼上拿着一束白色的马蹄莲。|近义词：arum lily, lily of the Nile|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
calla:/ˈkælə/| n. |水芋|A plant with arrow-shaped leaves and small greenish flowers, growing in water or on marshy ground.|例句：Callas thrive in wet, boggy areas. 水芋在潮湿沼泽的地区生长茂盛。|例句：The pond was edged with callas and reeds. 池塘边长满了水芋和芦苇。|近义词：bog arum, marsh calla|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
