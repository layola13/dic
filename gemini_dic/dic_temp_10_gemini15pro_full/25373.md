bulbaceous:/bʌlˈbeɪʃəs/| adj.|球根状的，球茎状的，膨胀的|Resembling a bulb, especially in roundness and swelling.  Having a bulb-like shape. Can also refer to something that is intellectually or emotionally underdeveloped. |The plant has a bulbaceous root system.  这种植物有球根状的根系。|The comedian's bulbaceous nose was a frequent target of his jokes.  这位喜剧演员球根状的鼻子经常成为他笑话的目标。|近义词：bulbous, swollen, rounded|反义词：slender, thin, narrow|词性:形容词

词源:
From Late Latin *bulbaceus, from Latin bulbus (“bulb, onion”) + -aceus (suffix forming adjectives meaning “of or pertaining to; resembling; having the nature of”).
