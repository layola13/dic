anticyclones:/ˌæntiˈsaɪkloʊnz/ (US), /ˌæntiˈsaɪkləʊnz/ (UK)| n. |反气旋|A weather system with high atmospheric pressure at its center, around which air slowly circulates in a clockwise direction in the Northern Hemisphere and counterclockwise in the Southern Hemisphere.  Anticyclones are generally associated with fair weather.|Anticyclones often bring sunny skies. 反气旋经常带来晴朗的天空。

The anticyclone is expected to persist for several days. 预计反气旋将持续几天。|近义词：high-pressure system, high|反义词:cyclone, low-pressure system|Noun
