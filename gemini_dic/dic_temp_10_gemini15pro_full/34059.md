civic:/ˈsɪvɪk/ /ˈsɪvɪk/ | adj. | 市民的，公民的；城市的，城镇的 | Relating to a city or town and its citizens; municipal.  |  The civic center hosts many community events. 市民中心举办许多社区活动。 |  municipal, urban, community |  rural, private | adjective

civic:/ˈsɪvɪk/ /ˈsɪvɪk/ | n. | 市民课程 | A social science course dealing with the duties, privileges, and responsibilities of citizens. |  Many high schools require students to take civics. 许多高中要求学生学习公民课程。 | citizenship education, government class | N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *civicque*, from Latin *civicus* ("relating to a citizen, civil"), from *civis* ("citizen").
