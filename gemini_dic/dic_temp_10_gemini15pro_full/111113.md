literalized:/ˈlɪtərəlaɪzd/ | adj. |字面化的，逐字的| Made exactly according to the literal sense; not figurative or metaphorical. |The poem was literalized in the translation, losing its poetic nuances. 这首诗在翻译中被字面化了，失去了诗意的细微差别。|  The director literalized the stage directions, resulting in a stiff and unnatural performance. 导演对舞台指示进行了字面化的处理，导致表演僵硬而不自然。 | literal, verbatim | figurative, metaphorical | adjective
