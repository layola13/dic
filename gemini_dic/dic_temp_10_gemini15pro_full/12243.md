ascendancy:/əˈsɛndənsi/
|n.|优势;支配地位;权势|A position of dominant power or influence. |The family's ascendancy in local politics lasted for generations.  这个家族在当地政坛的统治地位持续了几代人。| dominance, supremacy, control | subordination, weakness | noun

ascendancies:/əˈsɛndənsiz/
|n.|优势;支配地位;权势(复数)|Plural form of ascendancy, referring to multiple instances of dominant power or influence. |The various political ascendancies throughout history have shaped the world we live in today. 历史上各种政治优势地位塑造了我们今天生活的世界。|dominances, supremacies, controls|subordinations, weaknesses|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English *ascendancie, from Old French ascendance, from ascendant, present participle of ascendre "to ascend," from Latin ascendere "to climb up, mount, ascend," from ad- "to" + scandere "to climb."
