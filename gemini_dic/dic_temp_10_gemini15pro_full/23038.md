boulier:/bu.lje/| n.|算盘, 计算器|A frame with beads sliding on rods, used for calculating.  A calculating device, especially an abacus.|例句：The shopkeeper used a boulier to calculate the total cost.  店主用算盘计算总价。

例句：She learned to use a boulier at a young age. 她很小的时候就学会了使用算盘。|近义词：abacus, calculator, counting frame|反义词:computer|词性:noun
