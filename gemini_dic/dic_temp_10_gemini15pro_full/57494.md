dragee:/drɑːˈʒeɪ/ /drɑːˈʒiː/
n.
糖衣药丸；糖衣果仁；糖衣杏仁
A small rounded sweet consisting of a sugared almond or other center.
例句：The cake was decorated with colorful dragees.  蛋糕上装饰着五颜六色的糖衣杏仁。

例句：She offered him a dragee from a small silver box. 她从一个小银盒里拿出一个糖衣杏仁递给他。

近义词：confection, sweet, candy
反义词:  None (No direct opposite for a specific type of candy.)
词性: noun
