biosphere:/ˈbaɪoʊsfiər/ | n. | 生物圈 | The regions of the surface, atmosphere, and hydrosphere of the earth (or analogous parts of other planets) occupied by living organisms. |  The biosphere is delicate and easily disrupted by human activity.  生物圈很脆弱，很容易被人为活动破坏。

The Amazon rainforest plays a vital role in the Earth's biosphere. 亚马逊雨林在地球生物圈中起着至关重要的作用。 | 近义词：ecosphere, living world | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *biosphère*, from bio- +‎ -sphère (“sphere”).  Bio- from Greek βίος (bíos, “life”). -sphere from Greek σφαῖρα (sphaîra, “ball, globe, sphere”).
