containerized:/kənˈteɪnəraɪzd/
adj.
集装箱化的，容器化的
packed and transported in a container; adapted for container transport.
The containerized cargo was shipped across the Pacific. 集装箱货物被运过太平洋。
近义词: packaged, pre-packed, boxed, crated
反义词: unpackaged, loose
