masted:/ˈmɑːstɪd/ /ˈmæstɪd/
adjective
有桅杆的；装有桅杆的
having a mast or masts.
The masted ship sailed gracefully across the bay.  这艘装有桅杆的船优雅地航行过海湾。
The small, masted boat bobbed gently on the waves.  这艘小的，有桅杆的船在波浪上轻轻地摇摆。
Synonyms: rigged, sparred
Antonyms: mastless, unmasted
词性: adjective
