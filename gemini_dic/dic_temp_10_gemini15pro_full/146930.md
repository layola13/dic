overintensification: /ˌoʊvərɪnˌtɛnsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度强化，过度加剧 |  The act of intensifying something excessively, often leading to negative consequences.  It refers to an exaggerated or amplified response or action beyond what is considered normal or appropriate. |  Overintensification of agriculture can lead to soil degradation. 过度强化农业会导致土壤退化。

Overintensification of emotions can be detrimental to mental health. 过度强化情绪可能对心理健康有害。 | 近义词: exaggeration, amplification, escalation | 反义词: moderation, reduction, understatement | 词性: noun


词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "intensification" (from "intensify" -  from Late Latin *intensificare, from Latin *intens(us) "stretched, strained, intense" + -ficare "-fy").
