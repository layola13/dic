dubek:/ˈduːbɛk/| n.|杜贝克（一种羊皮大衣）|A dubek is a type of sheepskin coat, often worn in Eastern Europe.|He wore a warm dubek during the harsh winter. 他在严冬穿了一件暖和的杜贝克。|近义词：sheepskin coat, fur coat|反义词: N/A|名词

词源: From Polish *dubel*, ultimately from Proto-Slavic *dublъ* (“double, twofold”), referring to the doubled or folded sheepskin used in making the coat.
