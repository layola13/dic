nonfeelingly:/nɒnˈfiːlɪŋli/
adv.
无情地，冷漠地
Without feeling or emotion; in a heartless manner.
She spoke nonfeelingly about the tragedy, as if it didn't affect her at all. 她冷漠地谈论着这场悲剧，好像它完全没有影响到她一样。
近义词: callously, heartlessly, indifferently, apathetically
反义词: compassionately, empathetically, sympathetically
词性: 副词

Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + feeling + -ly (adverbial suffix)
