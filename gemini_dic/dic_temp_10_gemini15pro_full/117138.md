meaning:/ˈmiːnɪŋ/| n.|意思，含义，意义|Something that is meant by a word, text, concept, or action.|例句：The meaning of the word "love" is difficult to define. “爱”这个词的含义很难定义。|近义词：significance, sense, import, gist|反义词：meaninglessness, insignificance|noun

/ˈmiːnɪŋ/| v.|意味着，意思是|To have a particular meaning.|例句：Smiling can mean different things in different cultures.  微笑在不同的文化中可能意味着不同的事情。|近义词：signify, denote, indicate, imply|反义词：misrepresent|verb
