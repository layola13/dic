cubbyhole: /ˈkʌbihoʊl/ (US) /ˈkʌbihəʊl/ (UK)| n. |小房间，隔间，壁橱|A small enclosed space, often used for storage or as a private workspace. |例句：He keeps his tools in a cubbyhole under the stairs. 他把工具放在楼梯下的一个小隔间里。

例句：She worked in a tiny cubbyhole of an office. 她在一个狭小的办公室隔间里工作。

近义词：alcove, nook, compartment, closet|反义词:hall, large room|词性:noun

cubby: /ˈkʌbi/ (US) /ˈkʌbi/ (UK)| n. |小房间，隔间，壁橱|(Informal) Same as cubbyhole. |例句：The children each have their own cubby for their coats and bags. 孩子们每个人都有自己的小柜子放外套和书包。

例句：My cubby at work is cluttered with papers. 我在办公室的小隔间里堆满了文件。
