what: /wɑːt/ (US) /wɒt/ (UK) | pron. det. adv. int. | 什么，多么，何等 |

英文解析: "What" is a versatile word in English, functioning as a pronoun, determiner, adverb, and interjection. It's primarily used to ask questions about things, qualities, or identities.

例句:

What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？

What time is it? = 现在几点了？


What color is the sky? = 天空是什么颜色？

What a beautiful day! = 多么美好的一天！
