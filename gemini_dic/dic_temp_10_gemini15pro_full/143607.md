outintriguing:/ˌaʊtɪnˈtriːɡɪŋ/
adjective
更加引人入胜的，格外有趣的
surpassing in intrigue; more intriguing than others.
Her new novel is even more outintriguing than her last. 她的新小说比上一部更引人入胜。
近义词: fascinating, captivating, compelling, absorbing
反义词: boring, tedious, dull, uninteresting
词性: adjective

词源:
"Out-" (prefix) + "intriguing" (adjective).  "Out-" acts as an intensifying prefix, meaning "to surpass or exceed." "Intriguing" comes from the French "intriguer" (to plot, scheme, puzzle), ultimately from the Latin "intricare" (to entangle).
