ophiurioidea:/ˌɒfiˌjʊəriˈɔɪdiə/ | n. | 蛇尾纲 |A class of marine echinoderms that includes brittle stars and basket stars, characterized by five slender, flexible arms radiating from a central disk.|  The ophiurioidea are closely related to starfish. 蛇尾纲与海星关系密切。| 近义词: brittle stars, basket stars | 反义词: None | Noun
