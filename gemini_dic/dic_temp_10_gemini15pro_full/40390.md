contradistinction:/ˌkɒntrədɪˈstɪŋkʃn/ /ˌkɑːntrədɪˈstɪŋkʃn/| n. |区别，对比，对照|The state of being strikingly different from something else, typically something in juxtaposition or close association.|He drew a contradistinction between domestic and foreign policy. 他对比了国内政策和外交政策。|近义词：difference, contrast, comparison, distinction|反义词：similarity, resemblance|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *contradistinctio*, from contra- + distinctio "distinction."
