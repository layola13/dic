beshow:/bɪˈʃoʊ/
verb
赠予，授予，给予
To present or confer (something, such as a favor or quality)
The king beshowed honors on his loyal subjects. 国王赐予他忠诚的臣民以荣誉。
bestow, confer, grant, award
deny, withhold
词性:动词
