pasture:/ˈpæstʃər/ (US) /ˈpɑːstʃə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.| 牧场，牧草；放牧|A field covered with grass and other low plants suitable for grazing animals, or the act of grazing livestock.|The cows were put out to pasture. 奶牛被放出去吃草。|近义词：field, meadow, grassland, grazing land|反义词：barren land, desert|n.
pasture:/ˈpæstʃər/ (US) /ˈpɑːstʃə(r)/ (UK)| v.| 放牧|To put animals in a pasture to graze.|The farmer pastures his sheep on the hillside. 农夫在山坡上放牧他的羊。|近义词：graze|反义词：stable, pen|v.
