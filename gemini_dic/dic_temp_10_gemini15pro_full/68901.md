fastish:/ˈfɑːstɪʃ/ | adj. | 稍快的，相当快的 | Relatively fast; somewhat fast.  It implies a speed that is above average but not exceptionally so. |  He walked at a fastish pace to catch the bus. 他快步走着去赶公共汽车。

The car was going at a fastish speed, but not too fast. 这辆车开得相当快，但也不是太快。| 近义词: quick, speedy, brisk | 反义词: slow, sluggish | 词性:形容词

词源:  From "fast" + "-ish" (suffix indicating somewhat or slightly).  "Fast" comes from Old English "fæst," meaning firm, fixed, or quick. The "-ish" suffix is also from Old English "-isc."
