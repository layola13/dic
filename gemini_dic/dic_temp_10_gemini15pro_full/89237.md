homilete: /ˈhɒmɪlɛt/ | n. | 布道；讲道；说教 | A sermon or a tedious moralizing discourse. |  Example: The priest delivered a lengthy homilete on the importance of forgiveness. 牧师就宽恕的重要性发表了冗长的布道。

Example: His after-dinner speech turned into a rather dull homilete. 他的餐后演讲变成了一场相当枯燥的说教。 | Synonym: sermon, preachment, lecture, discourse | Antonym:  None (lack of a direct antonym, but could consider "entertainment," "diversion") | noun
