mayflower:/ˈmeɪˌflaʊər/| n.|五月花（指植物或船名）|A flowering plant, especially any of various spring-blooming plants of the hawthorn family. Also refers to the ship that transported the Pilgrims to America in 1620.|The mayflower is a symbol of spring. 五月花是春天的象征。 The Mayflower carried the Pilgrims to the New World. 五月花号载着清教徒前往新大陆。|近义词：hawthorn, ship (depending on the context)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
