abidingness:/əˈbaɪdɪŋnɪs/| n. |持久性，永恒性|The quality of being lasting or enduring.  It refers to something that remains constant and unwavering over time.|His abidingness to his principles is admirable. 他对原则的坚持令人钦佩。|近义词：permanence, constancy, steadfastness, persistence|反义词：transience, impermanence, fleetingness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From abiding + -ness.  Abiding comes from the Middle English abiden, from Old English ābīdan, meaning "to remain, wait, endure."  The suffix -ness forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state, quality, or condition.
