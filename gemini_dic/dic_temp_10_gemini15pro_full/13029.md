astrakhans:/ˈæstrəkænz/
noun
阿斯特拉罕羔羊皮，阿斯特拉罕羔羊皮衣
tightly curled karakul lambskin, used for coats, hats, etc.  The word can also refer to garments made from this material.
She wore a luxurious astrakhan coat. 她穿着一件奢华的阿斯特拉罕羔羊皮大衣。
Synonyms: karakul, persian lamb
Antonyms: (No direct antonym for the material itself; perhaps "synthetic fur" in the context of material)
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Astrakhan, a city in Russia, formerly a major trading center for this type of lambskin.
