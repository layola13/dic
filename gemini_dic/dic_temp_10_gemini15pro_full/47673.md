debted:/ˈdɛtɪd/| adj.|负债的，感激的|Owing gratitude or recognition to another, especially for help or support; beholden.|例句：I am deeply indebted to my parents for their support. 我非常感激父母的支持。|近义词：obliged, grateful, beholden|反义词：ungrateful|词性:形容词
词源：From Middle English *detted, from Old French *dette, ultimately from Latin dēbita, feminine past participle of dēbeō (“I owe”).
