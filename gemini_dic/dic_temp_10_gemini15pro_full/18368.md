belly-ache:/ˈbeliˌeɪk/ (US), /ˈbɛliˌeɪk/ (UK)| n. vi.| 肚子痛；抱怨|A pain in the belly; a complaint.|例句：I ate too much candy and now I have a belly-ache. 我吃了太多糖果，现在肚子疼。

例句：He's always belly-aching about something. 他总是抱怨个不停。|近义词：stomachache, tummy ache, gripe, complain|反义词:  (For the pain) well-being, comfort (For the complaining)  contentment, satisfaction |词性: noun, verb
