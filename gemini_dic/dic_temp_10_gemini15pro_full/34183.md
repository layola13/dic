clamorous:/ˈklæmərəs/ /ˈklæmɔːrəs/| adj.|吵闹的，喧嚷的|Characterized by a loud uproar; noisy.|The clamorous crowd cheered for their team. 喧闹的人群为他们的队伍欢呼。|例句：The children were clamorous for their supper. 孩子们吵着要吃晚饭。

例句：The clamorous geese took flight. 喧闹的鹅群飞了起来。


|近义词：noisy, loud, vociferous, boisterous|反义词：quiet, silent, peaceful, tranquil|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin clamorosus (“noisy”), from Latin clamor (“shout”).
