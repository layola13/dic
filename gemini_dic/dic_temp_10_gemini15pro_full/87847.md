ETYMOLOGY->
From "hide" (verb) + "away" (adverb).  "Hide" traces back to Old English "hīdan," meaning "to conceal," of Germanic origin. "Away" comes from Old English "onweg," meaning "on the way," from "on" (on) and "weg" (way).
