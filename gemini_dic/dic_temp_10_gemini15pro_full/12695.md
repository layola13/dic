asserted:/əˈsɜːrtɪd/
|v.|断言；声称；维护；坚持|state a fact or belief confidently and forcefully|He asserted that he was innocent. 他坚称自己无辜。|近义词：claimed, declared, maintained, affirmed|反义词：denied, refuted|verb

asserted:/əˈsɜːrtɪd/
|adj.|声称的；宣称的；被断言的|(used especially about a right or quality) stated to be true or existing, but not necessarily so|his asserted right to speak freely 他所主张的言论自由的权利|近义词：claimed, alleged, professed|反义词：unclaimed, undeclared|adjective
