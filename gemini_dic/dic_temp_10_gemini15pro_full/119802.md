migratory:/ˈmaɪɡrəˌtɔːri/ /ˈmaɪɡrəˌtəri/| adj.|迁徙的，迁移的|Relating to migration; habitually migrating.|例句：Many birds are migratory, flying south for the winter. 许多鸟类是迁徙的，冬天飞往南方。|例句：The migratory patterns of butterflies are fascinating. 蝴蝶的迁徙模式令人着迷。|近义词：nomadic, wandering, mobile, itinerant|反义词：settled, resident, stationary|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *migratorius, from Latin migrat-, past participle stem of migrare "to move from one place to another, depart, migrate," from migrare "to change one's place of residence" (see migrate).
