learnt:/lɜːnt/ /lɝːnt/ | v. | 学会，学习(learn的过去式和过去分词) | past tense and past participle of "learn" | I learnt to swim when I was five. 我五岁时学会了游泳。 | learned, studied, acquired | forgotten, unlearned | Verb


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
He learns quickly.  他学得很快。
She learns a new language every year. 她每年都学一门新的语言。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
He is learning to drive. 他正在学开车。
They are learning about the solar system. 他们在学习太阳系。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have learnt a lot from this experience. 我从这次经历中学到了很多。
She has learnt how to play the piano. 她学会了弹钢琴。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been learning Spanish for two years. 我一直在学西班牙语两年了。
He has been learning to cook recently. 他最近一直在学做饭。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I learnt to ride a bike when I was six. 我六岁的时候学会了骑自行车。
She learnt French in high school. 她在高中时学了法语。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was learning to play the guitar when you called. 你打电话的时候我正在学弹吉他。
They were learning about ancient history. 他们当时正在学习古代历史。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had learnt to drive before I bought a car. 我在买车之前就学会了开车。
She had learnt everything she needed to know before the exam. 她在考试前已经学会了她需要知道的一切。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been learning Japanese for five years before I visited Japan. 在我去日本之前，我已经学了五年日语。
He had been learning to fly for several months before his first solo flight. 在他第一次单飞之前，他已经学习飞行好几个月了。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will learn to speak Italian one day.  总有一天我会学说意大利语。
They will learn about the dangers of smoking. 他们将了解吸烟的危害。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be learning to ski next winter. 明年冬天我将学习滑雪。
They will be learning about renewable energy. 他们将学习可再生能源。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next year, I will have learnt enough Spanish to travel comfortably. 到明年，我将学会足够的西班牙语，可以轻松旅行。
By the time he graduates, he will have learnt everything he needs to know. 到他毕业的时候，他将学会他需要知道的一切。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By the time I retire, I will have been learning languages for over twenty years. 到我退休的时候，我将学习语言超过二十年了。
By next month, she will have been learning to code for six months. 到下个月，她将学习编程六个月了。

虚拟语气:
If I had learnt to swim earlier, I would have gone with you to the beach. 如果我早点学会游泳，我就会和你一起去海滩了。
I wish I had learnt to play the piano when I was younger. 我希望我年轻的时候学会了弹钢琴。

被动语态:
Many valuable lessons were learnt during the project. 在这个项目中学到了很多宝贵的经验。
A new skill is being learnt every day. 每天都学习一项新技能。

疑问句:
What did you learn in school today? 你今天在学校学到了什么？
Have you learnt anything new lately? 你最近学到了什么新东西吗？

否定句:
I haven't learnt much about quantum physics. 我对量子物理学了解不多。
He didn't learn his lesson from the experience. 他没有从这次经历中学到教训。
