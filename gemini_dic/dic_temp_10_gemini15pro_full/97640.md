inheritance:/ɪnˈhɛrɪtəns/ | n. | 继承；遗产；遗传 |Something that is received from a predecessor or from the past.| She received a large inheritance from her grandmother. 她从祖母那里继承了一大笔遗产。|  The house is part of his inheritance. 这房子是他遗产的一部分。 | synonyms: legacy, heritage, bequest, patrimony | antonyms: deprivation, forfeiture | noun


inheritance:/ɪnˈhɛrɪtəns/ | n. | 遗传；基因 |The process by which characteristics are transmitted from parents to offspring.|  The study of genetics helps us understand inheritance. 基因研究帮助我们了解遗传。 | His height is due to his inheritance. 他的身高源于遗传。 | synonyms: heredity, genetics, descent | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but could consider "mutation" as a contrasting concept) | noun
