nonfidelities: /ˌnɒn.fɪˈdɛl.ɪ.tiz/ | n. | 不忠行为，不贞行为 | Acts of being unfaithful, especially in a romantic relationship.  It refers to actions that break a promise of loyalty or faithfulness, typically in the context of a marriage or other committed relationship. |  His repeated nonfidelities eventually led to their divorce.  他屡次不忠最终导致他们离婚。 | Synonyms: infidelities, betrayals, unfaithfulness | Antonyms: loyalty, fidelity, faithfulness | Noun
