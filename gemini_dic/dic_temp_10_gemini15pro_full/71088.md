fish-farm:/ˈfɪʃ fɑːrm/ | n. | 养鱼场；鱼类养殖场 | A place where fish are raised commercially, typically in enclosures in rivers, lakes, or the sea. |  The fish-farm produces thousands of trout each year.  这家养鱼场每年生产数千条鳟鱼。| Synonyms: aquaculture, fish hatchery, pisciculture | Antonyms:  wild fishery | noun


fish-farm:/ˈfɪʃ fɑːrm/ | v. | 养殖鱼类 | To raise fish commercially in enclosures. | They fish-farm salmon in the coastal waters. 他们在沿海水域养殖鲑鱼。 | Synonyms: cultivate fish, raise fish, breed fish | Antonyms: wild-catch | verb
