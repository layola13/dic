helminthiasis:/ˌhɛlmɪnˈθaɪəsɪs/| n. |蠕虫病，寄生虫病|An infestation of or infection with parasitic worms.|Example 1: The doctor diagnosed the child with helminthiasis after finding parasitic worms in his stool.  医生在孩子的粪便中发现了寄生虫后，诊断他患有蠕虫病。

Example 2:  Helminthiasis is a common problem in areas with poor sanitation.  在卫生条件差的地区，蠕虫病是一个常见问题。|Synonym: worm infestation, parasitic worm infection|Antonym:  N/A (absence of helminthiasis is implied by good health) |noun
