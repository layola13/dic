illustrated:/ˈɪləstreɪtɪd/
adjective
插图的；图解的
explained or made clear with examples, pictures, etc.
The book is beautifully illustrated with color photographs. 这本书配有精美的彩色照片插图。
synonyms: pictured, visualized, depicted
antonyms: unillustrated, plain, bare
词性:形容词


illustrated:/ˈɪləstreɪtɪd/
verb
（用图、实例等）说明，阐明
to make the meaning of something clearer by giving examples
His lecture was illustrated with slides. 他的讲座配有幻灯片。
synonyms: demonstrate, exemplify, show
antonyms: confuse, obscure, muddle
词性:动词 (过去分词)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin illustratus, past participle of illustrare (“to illuminate, brighten, make clear”), from in- (“in, on”) + lustrare (“to brighten, purify”), from lustrum (“purification”).
