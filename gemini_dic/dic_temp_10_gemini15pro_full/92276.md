hypervoluminously:/ˌhaɪpərˌvɑːljuːˈmɪnəsli/
adv.
极大量地，超级大量地
in a hypervoluminous manner; to a hypervoluminous degree.  Characterized by extreme volume or size.
The author hypervoluminously documented every detail of the historical event. 作者极其详尽地记录了这一历史事件的每一个细节。
The river flowed hypervoluminously after the heavy rain.  暴雨过后，河水汹涌澎湃。
Near Synonyms: excessively, profusely, copiously, abundantly
Antonyms: sparsely, minimally, meagerly
