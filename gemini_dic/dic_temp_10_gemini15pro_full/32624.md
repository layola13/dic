chirruppers:/ˈtʃɪrəpərz/
n.
啁啾者；唧唧喳喳叫的人或物
Those making chirping sounds, typically birds or insects.
The chirruppers in the trees woke me up early this morning. 树上的鸟儿唧唧喳喳地叫，一大早就把我吵醒了。
chirpers, singers
silent creatures
