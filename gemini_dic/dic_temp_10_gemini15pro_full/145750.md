overculls: /ˌoʊvərˈkʌlz/
| n. | 过度剔除，过度淘汰 | The act of culling too many animals or items, exceeding the intended or necessary amount. |  Due to a miscalculation, the farmer overculled his herd, leaving him with too few breeding animals. 由于计算错误，农民过度剔除了他的牛群，导致他剩下的繁殖动物太少。| 近义词：overkill, excessive culling | 反义词: undercull | 词性: 名词

词源:  "Over-" (meaning "excessively") + "cull" (from Old French "cuillir," meaning "to collect, gather, choose").  "Cull" originally meant to select the best, but later evolved to include selecting for removal or destruction.  "Overcull" thus means to select too many for removal.
