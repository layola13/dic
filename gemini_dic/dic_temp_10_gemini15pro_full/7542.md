annexionist:/ˌænekˈʃənɪst/ | n. adj. |吞并主义者；主张吞并的| A person who advocates or supports annexation, especially political annexation of territory. |例句：The annexationist policies of the country led to international condemnation.  这个国家的吞并主义政策导致了国际社会的谴责。

例句：He was labeled an annexationist for his aggressive stance on territorial expansion. 他因在领土扩张问题上的强硬立场而被称为吞并主义者。|近义词：imperialist, expansionist |反义词:pacifist, anti-imperialist |词性: noun, adjective

Etymology: From annexation + -ist. Annexation comes from the Late Latin annexionem, meaning "a joining or adding," from the Latin annexus, past participle of annectere "to bind or tie to."
