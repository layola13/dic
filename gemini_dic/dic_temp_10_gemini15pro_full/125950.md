musicalness:/ˈmjuːzɪkəlnəs/
n.
音乐性；旋律美；音感
The quality of being musical or melodious.
Her playing has a wonderful musicalness. 她的演奏很有音乐性。

近义词:musicality, melodiousness, tunefulness
反义词:unmusicality, dissonance, discordance
词性:noun
