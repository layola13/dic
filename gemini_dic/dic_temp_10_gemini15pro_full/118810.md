metamorphism:/ˌmɛtəˈmɔːrfɪzəm/| n. |变质; 变形; 变态|A change of the form or nature of a thing or person into a completely different one, by natural or supernatural means. |The rock has undergone metamorphism. 这块岩石经历了变质作用。|Synonyms: transformation, alteration, mutation, conversion, transmutation|Antonyms: stasis, stagnation|noun
