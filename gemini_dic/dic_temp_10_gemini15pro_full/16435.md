bareback:/ˈbɛərbæk/ | adj. adv.| 赤裸的，无鞍的 | Riding a horse without a saddle. |  She rode bareback through the fields. 她赤身裸体地骑马穿过田野。|  He performed a bareback riding stunt. 他表演了一个无鞍骑马的特技。 | 近义词: saddleless, naked (when referring to a person) | 反义词: saddled | 词性: 形容词, 副词
