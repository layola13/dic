cast-iron:/ˌkæst ˈaɪərn/| adj. n.|铸铁的；坚强的；不易改变的|Made of cast iron; very strong and rigid; unyielding.|例句：He has a cast-iron stomach and can eat anything. 他有一个铁打的胃，什么都能吃。|近义词：inflexible, rigid, unyielding|反义词：flexible, yielding|词性：形容词

cast-iron:/ˌkæst ˈaɪərn/| n.|铸铁|A hard, relatively brittle alloy of iron and carbon that can be readily cast in a mold and contains a higher proportion of carbon than steel.|例句：The skillet is made of cast iron.  这个煎锅是铸铁做的。|近义词：pig iron|反义词：steel, wrought iron|词性：名词
