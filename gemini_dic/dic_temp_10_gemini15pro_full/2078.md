ad-out:/ˈædˌaʊt/| n.|广告支出，广告投放|The marketing team discussed the ad-out for the new product launch. 市场营销团队讨论了新产品发布的广告支出。|The ad-out for this campaign was significantly higher than last year's.  这次活动的广告支出明显高于去年。|近义词: advertising expenditure, ad spend|反义词:|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "advertisement" (shortened to "ad") + "out" (indicating expenditure or disbursement).  由“advertisement”（缩写为“ad”）+“out”（表示支出或支付）组成。


USAGE->
The company increased its ad-out to reach a wider audience. = 公司增加了广告支出以覆盖更广泛的受众。

Their ad-out for the holiday season was very effective. = 他们的假期广告投放非常有效。

ad-out plan = 广告支出计划
ad-out budget = 广告支出预算
ad-out optimization = 广告支出优化
