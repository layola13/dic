calendulin: /kəˈlɛndjʊlɪn/ | n. |钙调蛋白| A calcium-binding protein involved in regulating a variety of cellular processes, including muscle contraction, cell division, and signal transduction. | Calmodulin plays a crucial role in muscle contraction. 钙调蛋白在肌肉收缩中起着至关重要的作用。|  Calmodulin binds to calcium ions and activates other proteins. 钙调蛋白与钙离子结合并激活其他蛋白质。 | synonyms: calcium-modulated protein | antonyms: None | noun

Etymology: From "calcium-modulated protein".  It was named for its ability to modulate cellular activities in response to changes in calcium concentration.
