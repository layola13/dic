mosaic:/məʊˈzeɪɪk/ (UK) /moʊˈzeɪɪk/ (US)| n. adj.|马赛克，镶嵌图案；混合物，综合体|A picture or pattern produced by arranging together small colored pieces of hard material, such as stone, tile, or glass.|The artist created a beautiful mosaic using tiny pieces of colored glass. 艺术家用彩色玻璃碎片创作了一幅美丽的马赛克画。|近义词:collage, patchwork, medley|反义词:uniformity, homogeneity|noun
