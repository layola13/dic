outquoting:/ˌaʊtˈkwəʊtɪŋ/
|v.|过度引用|The act of quoting excessively, often to the point of obscuring one's own original thoughts or contributions.|He was outquoting everyone in the debate, relying heavily on famous thinkers rather than formulating his own arguments. 他在辩论中过度引用，严重依赖著名思想家的言论，而不是形成自己的论点。|overquoting, citing excessively|underquoting|verb
