amorphozoic:/əˌmɔːrfəˈzoʊɪk/
adj.
无定形的，非晶质的；无生物的
Relating to or denoting the geological eon before the earliest known fossils, lasting from the formation of Earth about 4.5 billion years ago to 4 billion years ago.  Characterized by the absence of life or definite form.
The amorphozoic rocks contain no fossils. 这些无定形岩层中不含化石。
The scientist studied the amorphozoic period of Earth's history.  这位科学家研究了地球历史上的无生物时期。
近义词:abiotic, lifeless, inanimate, inorganic
反义词:biotic, living, animate, organic
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From a- (not, without) + morpho- (form) + zoic (pertaining to animal life). Ultimately from Greek *ἀ- (a-, not, without) +  μορφή (morphḗ, form, shape) +  ζῷον (zōion, animal, living being).
