fayalite:/ˈfeɪəˌlaɪt/| n. |铁橄榄石|An olive-green to brownish-black mineral of the olivine group, consisting of iron silicate.  It is a common constituent of mafic igneous rocks.|Fayalite is a common mineral found in volcanic rocks. 铁橄榄石是一种常见的火山岩矿物。

Fayalite crystals can be quite beautiful. 铁橄榄石晶体可以非常漂亮。|近义词：iron silicate, olivine|反义词: N/A|名词
