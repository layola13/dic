harpsichords:/ˈhɑːrpsɪkɔːrdz/
n.
大键琴（复数）
A keyboard instrument that produces sound by plucking strings with quills or leather plectra.
The museum has a collection of antique harpsichords.  博物馆收藏了一些古董大键琴。

近义词:
clavichord, virginal (These are related keyboard instruments, but not identical.)

反义词:
(No direct antonym, but could contrast with modern instruments like) piano, synthesizer

词性:
Noun
