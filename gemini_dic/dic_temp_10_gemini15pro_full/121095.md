miscreance:/mɪsˈkriːəns/
noun
不端行为，恶行
An act of wickedness or wrongdoing.
His miscreance shocked the entire community. 他的恶行震惊了整个社区。

miscreance:/mɪsˈkriːəns/
noun
不信；不忠
Lack of belief or faith; infidelity.
Her miscreance led her to question the teachings of the church. 她的不信让她质疑教会的教义。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *mescreance* "disbelief, unbelief, wicked conduct," from *mescreant* "unbelieving, infidel" (see miscreant).

SYNONYMS->
wickedness, wrongdoing, evil, sin, iniquity, depravity, villainy, transgression, offense, crime, misdemeanor, felony, infidelity, disbelief, unbelief, apostasy, heresy, nonconformity, dissidence

ANTONYMS->
righteousness, virtue, goodness, piety, faith, belief, loyalty, conformity, orthodoxy
