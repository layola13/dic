obdurate:/ˈɒbdjʊrət/ (UK) /ˈɑːbdərət/ (US)| adj.|固执的，顽固的，执拗的|Stubbornly refusing to change one's opinion or course of action.|He remained obdurate, refusing to admit his mistake. 他依然固执，拒绝承认自己的错误。|Synonyms: stubborn, inflexible, adamant, unyielding, intractable|Antonyms: compliant, amenable, yielding, flexible|adjective
