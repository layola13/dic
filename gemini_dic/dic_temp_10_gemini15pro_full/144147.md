outshames:/ˌaʊtˈʃeɪmz/
verb
使蒙羞，使相形见绌
To cause someone to feel ashamed by behaving in a better or more impressive way than they do.
She completely outshamed him with her generosity. 她以她的慷慨大方使他完全感到羞愧。
近义词:humiliates, embarrasses, mortifies
反义词:praises, commends, honors
词性:v.
