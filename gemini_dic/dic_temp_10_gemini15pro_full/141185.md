oolite:/ˈoʊəlaɪt/ | n. |鲕状岩，鱼卵石 |A sedimentary rock consisting of small spherical grains (ooliths) cemented together. Each grain is made up of concentric layers of calcium carbonate deposited around a nucleus, such as a sand grain or shell fragment.| The cliffs are composed primarily of oolite. 这些悬崖主要由鲕状岩构成。| synonym:  roe stone | antonym: N/A | noun
