groin:/ɡrɔɪn/| n.|腹股沟；(建筑)交叉拱|The area between the abdomen and the thigh, often referring to the crease where they meet.  Also, in architecture, the curved edge formed by the intersection of two vaults.|He pulled a muscle in his groin while playing soccer. 他踢足球时腹股沟拉伤了。|The groins of the vaulted ceiling were beautifully decorated.  拱形天花板的交叉拱装饰精美。|synonyms: inguinal region (medical), crotch|antonyms: N/A|noun

groin:/ɡrɔɪn/| vt.|建成(拱顶)|To build or construct with groins (arches).|They groined the ceiling of the cathedral. 他们在教堂的天花板上建造了交叉拱。|The arches were groined to create a strong and visually appealing structure.  拱门被建成交叉拱，以创造一个坚固且美观的结构。|synonyms: vault, arch|antonyms: N/A|verb
