athy:/ˈeɪθi/ | n. | 冷漠，无情 | A lack of feeling or emotion; impassiveness. | He showed a surprising athy towards her suffering. 他对她遭受的痛苦表现出令人惊讶的冷漠。| 近义词：apathy, indifference, unfeelingness | 反义词：empathy, compassion, sensitivity | 词性: noun

词源: From Greek *ἀπάθεια (apátheia), from ἀ- (a-, "not, without") + πάθος (páthos, "suffering, feeling").
