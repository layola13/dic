ginsengs:/ˈdʒɪnseŋz/
noun
人参（复数）
A slow-growing plant with fleshy roots, prized in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
Ginsengs are often used in traditional Asian medicine. 人参常用于亚洲传统医学。
He bought some ginsengs at the herbal shop. 他在草药店买了一些人参。
synonyms: ginseng root (singular form is preferred), panax
antonyms: (No direct antonym for a plant.  One could consider something detrimental to health, but it wouldn't be a direct opposite.)
