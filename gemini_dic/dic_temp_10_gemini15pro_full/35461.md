coachability:/ˌkoʊtʃəˈbɪləti/
noun
可指导性，可教练性
The quality of being open to and receptive to coaching or instruction; the capacity to learn from feedback and improve one's performance.
Her coachability made her a valuable asset to the team. 她很强的可指导性使她成为团队的宝贵财富。
synonyms: receptiveness, teachability, malleability, trainability
antonyms: intractability, stubbornness, resistance, unresponsiveness
