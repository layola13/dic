crapshoot:/ˈkræpʃuːt/
noun
掷骰子赌博;  冒险的事情;  碰运气的事情
a situation in which something could have a good or a bad result, and you have little or no control over it
Investing in the stock market is a bit of a crapshoot. 投资股市有点像赌博。
gamble, risk, venture
certainty, sure thing
n.
