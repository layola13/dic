noninstructional:/ˌnɑːnɪnˈstrʌkʃənəl/
adjective
非教学的；非指导性的
Not related to or involved in the formal process of teaching or instruction.
The school provides noninstructional services like lunch and counseling. 学校提供午餐和咨询等非教学服务。
The noninstructional staff includes custodians and secretaries. 非教学人员包括管理员和秘书。
近义词: nonacademic, non-teaching
反义词: instructional, academic, teaching
词性: adjective
