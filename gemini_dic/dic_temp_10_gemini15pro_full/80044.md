goad:/ɡoʊd/ (US) /ɡəʊd/ (UK)| n. vt.|刺激；煽动；驱使；刺棒|a pointed stick used to urge animals onward; something that urges or stimulates into action|The farmer used a goad to move the oxen. 农民用刺棒驱赶牛。  The promise of a bonus was a goad to greater effort. 奖金的承诺是更加努力的动力。|近义词：stimulus, incentive, prod, spur|反义词：deterrent|词性:名词，动词
