lithophytous:/lɪˈθɒfɪtəs/
adjective
石生的；岩生的
Growing on rocks.  An organism that grows on rocks.
Example:  Lithophytous algae are common in intertidal zones.  石生藻类在潮间带很常见。

Example:  Certain lithophytous plants have adapted to survive in harsh, exposed environments.  某些石生植物已经适应了在恶劣的暴露环境中生存。
近义词:rock-dwelling, saxicolous, rupestral
反义词:aquatic, terrestrial (in a broader sense)
词性:adjective

Etymology:  From Greek *líthos* (stone) + *phutón* (plant).
