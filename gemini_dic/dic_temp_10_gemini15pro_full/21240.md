blokes: /bləʊks/ (UK) /bloʊks/ (US) | n. |家伙们，伙计们 | An informal term for men, often used in a friendly or familiar way. |  例句：
The blokes down at the pub are a friendly bunch. 酒馆里的那些家伙都很友好。

A couple of blokes were having a chat outside the shop. 几个家伙在商店外聊天。

| 近义词：guys, fellas, chaps (UK) | 反义词: women, ladies | 词性: 名词
