forthward:/ˈfɔːθwərd/| adj.|前进的，向前的|Characterized by progress or movement forward.|With a forthward glance, she stepped onto the stage. 她向前看了一眼，走上了舞台。|近义词：progressive, advancing|反义词:backward, regressive|词性:形容词
