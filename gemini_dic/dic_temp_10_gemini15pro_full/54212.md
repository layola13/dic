disfiguration:/dɪsfɪɡjəˈreɪʃn/
n.
毁容；外形的损毁；变形
The state of being disfigured; a blemish or deformity that mars the appearance of something.
The accident resulted in permanent disfiguration of his face.  这场事故导致他脸部永久毁容。
deformity, mutilation, damage
beauty, perfection
noun
