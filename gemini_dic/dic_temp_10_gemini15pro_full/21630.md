blurredly:/ˈblɜːrdli/ | adv. | 模糊地，朦胧地 | In a blurred or indistinct manner; vaguely. | The streetlights shone blurredly through the fog. 街灯透过雾气发出朦胧的光芒。| The camera captured the moving car blurredly. 相机模糊地捕捉到了移动的汽车。| 近义词：indistinctly, hazily, vaguely, fuzzily | 反义词：clearly, sharply, distinctly | 词性：副词

词源：From "blurred" + "-ly." "Blurred" comes from the verb "blur," which likely originated as an imitative word.
