n.
琉璃苣（复数）
An annual herb (Borago officinalis) in the borage family, native to the Mediterranean region and having bright blue star-shaped flowers and hairy leaves.  It is cultivated for its edible flowers and leaves and for its seed oil, which is rich in gamma-linolenic acid.
