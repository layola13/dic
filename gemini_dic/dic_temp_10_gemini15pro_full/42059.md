cote:/kəʊt/ (British English), /koʊt/ (American English)| n.|小屋，棚；羊圈；鸽棚；海岸，山坡|A small shelter for animals, especially sheep or birds; also, a slope or hillside, especially by the sea. |例句：The shepherd led his flock to the cote for the night. 牧羊人晚上把羊群赶到羊圈。

例句：The vines grew on the sunny cote above the village. 葡萄藤生长在村庄上方的阳光充足的山坡上。|近义词：shed, shelter, pen, enclosure, hillside, slope|反义词:plain, flat|词性:名词

cote:/kəʊt/ (British English), /koʊt/ (American English)| n.|行情，报价；标价|A quotation or price, especially one listed on a stock exchange. |例句：The cote for the company's shares rose sharply today.  该公司股票的报价今天大幅上涨。

例句：He checked the latest cote for gold on his phone. 他用手机查看了最新的黄金报价。|近义词：quotation, price, listing|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
