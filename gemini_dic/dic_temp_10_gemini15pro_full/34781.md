climatical:/klaɪˈmætɪkl/ | adj. | 气候的；与气候有关的 | Relating to climate; climatic conditions. |  例句：Climatical changes are affecting the planet. 气候变化正在影响地球。

例句：The climatic conditions in the region are harsh.  该地区的气候条件恶劣。| 近义词：meteorological, atmospheric | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From climate + -ical.  "Climate" comes from Old French "climat," ultimately from Greek "klima," meaning "inclination, region, zone,"  referring to the angle of the sun's rays.
