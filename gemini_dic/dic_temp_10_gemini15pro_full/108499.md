laying-back:/ˈleɪɪŋ bæk/| adj.| 悠闲的，放松的，随和的| Relaxed and easy-going; not easily upset or worried. |  He has a very laying-back attitude towards life. 他对生活有一种非常悠闲的态度。|  She's so laying-back, nothing seems to faze her. 她很随和，似乎没有什么能让她烦恼。| 近义词: relaxed, easygoing, casual, laid-back | 反义词: uptight, stressed, anxious | 词性:形容词

词源： "laying-back" 是由动词短语 "lay back" 演变而来的。 "Lay" 在这里的意思是 “to place in a resting position,”  "back" 指的是向后的方向。 因此， "laying back" 字面意思是向后躺下，引申为放松、悠闲的状态。
