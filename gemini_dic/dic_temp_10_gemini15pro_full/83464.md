habitus:/ˈhæbɪtəs/| n.|习惯，习性；气质，姿态；(社会学的)惯习|A person's habitus is the total set of learned habits, tastes, and dispositions that they possess by virtue of their long-term occupation of a particular social position.  一个人的惯习是指他们由于长期占据某个特定社会地位而拥有的全部习得习惯、品味和性情。|Her aristocratic habitus was immediately apparent. 她的贵族气质显而易见。|Synonyms: demeanor, bearing, disposition, tendency, characteristic |Antonyms:  None (difficult to find a direct antonym for this nuanced term)|Noun
