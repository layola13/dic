exostosis:/ˌɛksɒˈstəʊsɪs/ (US) /ˌɛɡzɑːˈstoʊsɪs/| n. |骨疣；外生骨疣|A benign bony outgrowth projecting outward from the surface of a bone.|Example: The doctor diagnosed the lump on his finger as an exostosis. 医生诊断他手指上的肿块是骨疣。

Example:  Multiple exostoses can cause pain and limit movement. 多发性骨疣会导致疼痛并限制活动。|Synonym: osteoma, bone spur|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, as it's a physical condition.  Could consider "normal bone growth" as a contrasting concept.)|noun
