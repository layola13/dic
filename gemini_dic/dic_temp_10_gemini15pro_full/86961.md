henchwoman:/ˈhen(t)ʃˌwʊmən/ | n. | 女帮凶，女打手 | A woman who assists a criminal, especially with violent or illegal activities. |例句：The villain's henchwoman carried out his dirty work.  这个恶棍的女帮凶为他干脏活。

例句：She was the henchwoman responsible for disposing of the evidence. 她是负责处理证据的女帮凶。| 近义词：accomplice, assistant, minion | 反义词：opponent, adversary, enemy | 词性：名词
