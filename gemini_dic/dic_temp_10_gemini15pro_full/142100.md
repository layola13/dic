orientating:/ˈɔːriənˌteɪtɪŋ/ |v.|使适应；使熟悉；确定方向|The program is designed to orientate new employees.  这个项目旨在帮助新员工适应环境。|近义词: familiarizing, acclimating, adapting, adjusting, orienting |反义词: disorienting, confusing |词性: 动词 (present participle of orientate)

orientate:/ˈɔːriənˌteɪt/ |v.|使适应；使熟悉；确定方向|She orientated herself by looking at the map. 她看着地图确定了自己的方向。|近义词: familiarize, acclimate, adapt, adjust, orient |反义词: disorient, confuse |词性: 动词

Etymology: From Late Latin *oriēntāre, from oriēns (“rising, east”), present participle of orīrī (“to rise”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂er- (“to rise, to stir”).
