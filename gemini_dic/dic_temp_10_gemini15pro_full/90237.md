house-sitter:/ˈhaʊsˌsɪtər/ | n. | 房屋看护人；代人看管房屋者 | Someone who stays in someone else's house to look after it while they are away. |  We hired a house-sitter to take care of our plants and pets while we were on vacation. 我们雇了一个房屋看护人，在我们度假期间照顾我们的植物和宠物。| Example 2: The house-sitter made sure to collect the mail and water the garden every day. 房屋看护人确保每天收取邮件并浇灌花园。 | Synonym: house watcher, caretaker | Antonym: N/A (No direct antonym) | noun
