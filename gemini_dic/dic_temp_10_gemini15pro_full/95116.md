inanimation:/ˌɪnænɪˈmeɪʃn/
n.
无生命; 无生气; 
lack of life or spirit; lifelessness.
The statue stood in stark inanimation.  这尊雕像毫无生气地立着。
近义词：lifelessness, inertia, torpor
反义词：animation, life, vitality
词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *inanimatio, from Latin inanimatus (“lifeless”), from in- (“not”) + animatus (“animate”).
