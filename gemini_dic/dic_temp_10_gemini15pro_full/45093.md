cubs:/kʌbz/
n.
幼崽;幼兽;幼禽;（尤指熊、狮、狼、狐狸等的）崽
Young animals, especially of bears, lions, wolves, and foxes.
The mother bear protected her cubs fiercely. 母熊凶猛地保护着她的幼崽。

young members of a sports team, especially in baseball
（尤指棒球队的）年轻队员
The Chicago Cubs are a famous baseball team. 芝加哥小熊队是一支著名的棒球队。

Etymology:
From Middle English *cubbe*, probably of Scandinavian origin, related to Old Norse *kubbi*.

近义词:
young, offspring, whelps (for dogs), kittens (for cats), pups (for seals), chicks (for birds)
反义词:
adult, mature

词性:
noun (plural)
