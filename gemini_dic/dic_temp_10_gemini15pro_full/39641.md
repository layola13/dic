consequent:/ˈkɒnsɪkwənt/ /ˈkɑːnsɪkwent/| adj. n.|随之发生的，作为结果的；结果，后果|Something that is consequent happens as a result of something else.|The war and its consequent refugees created a humanitarian crisis. |战争及其随之而来的难民造成了一场人道主义危机。|近义词:resulting, ensuing, subsequent, resultant|反义词:prior, antecedent, preceding|词性:形容词/名词

consequents:/ˈkɒnsɪkwənts/ /ˈkɑːnsɪkwents/| n.|结果，后果；【逻辑学】后件，后项|Things that happen as a result of something else; In logic, the second half of a hypothetical proposition. |He accepted the job and all its consequents. |他接受了这份工作以及随之而来的所有后果。|近义词:results, outcomes, effects, ramifications|反义词:antecedents, premises|词性:名词

词源:
From Late Latin *consequentem, the accusative singular of consequēns (“following, ensuing”), present participle of consequī (“to follow closely”), from com- (“with, together”) + sequī (“to follow”).
