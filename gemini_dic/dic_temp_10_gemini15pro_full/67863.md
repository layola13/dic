fablers:/ˈfeɪblərz/
n.
寓言家；讲故事的人
People who tell fables, especially writers of fables.
Aesop and La Fontaine are among the most famous fablers in history.  伊索和拉封丹是历史上最著名的寓言家之一。
The fablers of old often used animals to represent human virtues and vices. 古老的寓言家经常用动物来代表人类的美德和恶习。
storytellers, writers of fables
liars, deceivers
noun
