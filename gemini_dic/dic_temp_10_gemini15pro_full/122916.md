moly:/ˈmoʊli/| n. |魔法草,  Moly (植物名)|A mythical herb with magical powers, identified with a species of garlic, said to have white flowers and black roots and to have been given by Hermes to Odysseus as a charm against Circe's witchcraft.|Odysseus used moly to protect himself from Circe's magic. 奥德修斯用魔法草来保护自己免受瑟茜的魔法。|近义词:charm, amulet|反义词:bane, curse|noun
