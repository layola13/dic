guv'nor:/ˈɡʌvənə(r)/| n.|老板; 经理; 爸爸; 统治者|A colloquial and often respectful term of address for a man in a position of authority, especially a boss or manager. Also used as a term of endearment, especially for one's father.  Derived from "governor".| "Morning, Guv'nor!" said the cab driver.  早上好，老板！出租车司机说道。|Synonyms: boss, manager, chief, father, dad, old man|Antonyms: employee, subordinate, son|noun
