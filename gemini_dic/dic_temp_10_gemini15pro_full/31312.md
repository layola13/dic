chapped:/tʃæpt/| adj.|皴裂的，裂开的|rough, cracked, and sore, especially from cold weather|例句：My lips are chapped from the cold wind. 我的嘴唇被冷风吹裂了。|例句：The worker's hands were chapped and raw. 工人的手皴裂且粗糙。|近义词：cracked, dry, rough, sore|反义词:smooth, soft|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old English *ceappian "to buy, sell, bargain," later "to split, crack."  The sense development likely arose from the idea of something splitting or cracking open like a bargain being struck.
