empanelment:/ɪmˈpænəlmənt/| n.|陪审团的选任；登记；列入名单|The empanelment of the jury took several hours. 陪审团的选任花了几个小时。|近义词：selection, listing, enrollment|反义词: dismissal, discharge|noun

empanel:/ɪmˈpænəl/| vt.|选任（陪审团）；登记；列入名单|The judge empaneled a jury of twelve people. 法官选任了一个由十二人组成的陪审团。|近义词：select, list, enroll|反义词: dismiss, discharge|verb
