brainstorming: /ˈbreɪnˌstɔːrmɪŋ/ | n. | 头脑风暴，集体讨论 | the process of getting a group of people together and asking them to think of as many ideas as possible to solve a problem or to create a new product/service | We had a brainstorming session to come up with a name for the new product. 我们进行了一场头脑风暴，为新产品想出一个名字。| synonyms: idea generation, spitballing | antonyms: inaction, procrastination | noun

brainstorm: /ˈbreɪnˌstɔːrm/ | v. | 集思广益，集体讨论 | to hold a brainstorming session |  We brainstormed some ideas for the party. 我们集体讨论了一些派对的主意。| synonyms:  generate ideas, collaborate | antonyms: suppress, stifle | verb
