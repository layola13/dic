compartment:/kəmˈpɑːrtmənt/ /kəmˈpɑːrtmənt/| n. |隔间，车厢|A separate section or division of a larger space, such as a train carriage, a piece of furniture, or an area within the body.|例句：I left my bag in the overhead compartment on the train. 我把包落在火车上的行李架隔间里了。|例句：The human heart is divided into four compartments. 人的心脏分成四个腔室。|近义词：section, chamber, bay, cubicle|反义词: whole
