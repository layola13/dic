ace's:/ˈeɪsɪz/| n.|王牌的；高手的东西；一流的|Something that belongs to or is characteristic of an ace; something excellent.|That serve was an ace's serve.  那个发球是王牌发球。|近义词：expert's, master's, champion's|反义词:beginner's, amateur's|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From ace (n.) + -'s (possessive suffix).  Ace itself originates from the Old French "as," meaning "one," referring to the single pip on the playing card.
