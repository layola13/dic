ossean:/ˈɒsiən/| adj.|奥西恩的；吟游诗人奥西恩的；像奥西恩诗歌的；有关吟游诗人奥西恩或其诗歌的|Relating to or characteristic of the legendary Ossian or his poetry.  Characterized by melancholy, romantic grandeur, and a preoccupation with ancient Celtic legends.|The rugged landscape evoked an Ossean sense of melancholy.|近义词：Gaelic, Celtic, bardic|反义词:modern, contemporary|词性:形容词

词源: From Ossian, the name of a legendary Gaelic bard whose poetry, supposedly translated from ancient manuscripts by James Macpherson, was published in the late 18th century.  Although the poems were later shown to be largely Macpherson's own work, they had a significant influence on Romantic literature and art.
