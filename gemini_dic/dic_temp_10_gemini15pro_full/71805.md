fleeceless:/ˈfliːslɪs/
adjective
无毛的，无绒的
lacking a fleece; without wool
The fleeceless sheep shivered in the cold wind.  无毛的绵羊在寒风中瑟瑟发抖。
The newly bred dog was fleeceless, unlike its furry parents.  这个新培育的狗没有绒毛，不像它毛茸茸的父母。
近义词: hairless, bald, smooth
反义词: fleecy, woolly, hairy
词性: adjective

词源:  "fleece" (羊毛) + "-less" (无，没有)
