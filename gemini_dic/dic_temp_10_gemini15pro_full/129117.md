nixiwaka: /ˌnɪksɪˈwɑːkə/ | n. | 尼克西瓦卡（一种虚构的生物）|  Nixi Waka is a fictional creature, often described as a mischievous water spirit or goblin in some indigenous cultures. There is no single, universally agreed-upon definition, and its characteristics can vary. | The old storyteller told tales of the nixi waka that lured children into the river.  老故事讲述者讲述了尼克西瓦卡引诱孩子们进入河里的故事。|  Near synonyms:  water sprite, river goblin, kelpie (depending on specific depiction) | Near antonyms: angel, benevolent spirit | noun
