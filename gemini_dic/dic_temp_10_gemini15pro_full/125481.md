multipresent:/ˌmʌltiˈprɛzənt/| adj.|同时存在于多处的；遍在的|Existing in many places at the same time; ubiquitous.|例句：With the advent of social media, people can be multipresent online, engaging in multiple conversations at once. 随着社交媒体的出现，人们可以在网上同时存在，同时进行多次对话。

例句：The CEO seemed multipresent, attending meetings in different cities on the same day via video conferencing.  这位首席执行官似乎无处不在，同一天通过视频会议参加了不同城市的会议。|近义词：omnipresent, ubiquitous|反义词：localized, confined|词性:形容词
