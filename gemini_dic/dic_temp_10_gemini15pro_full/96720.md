inefficacious:/ˌɪnɪfɪˈkeɪʃəs/
adjective
无效的；无用的
not producing the desired effect
The medicine proved inefficacious in treating the disease.  这种药治疗这种疾病证明无效。
ineffective, useless, futile, unproductive
effective, potent, efficacious
词性: 形容词
