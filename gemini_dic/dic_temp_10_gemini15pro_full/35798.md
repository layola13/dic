cockerel:/ˈkɒkərəl/ (UK) /ˈkɑːkərəl/ (US)|n.|小公鸡，未满一岁的公鸡|A young domestic cock, before it reaches maturity and becomes able to crow properly. |例句：The cockerel strutted proudly around the farmyard. 小公鸡神气地在农场里走来走去。

例句：The farmer sold all his cockerels at the market. 农夫在市场上卖掉了所有的小公鸡。|近义词：rooster (mature male chicken), chick (young chicken) |反义词:hen (female chicken)|词性:名词
