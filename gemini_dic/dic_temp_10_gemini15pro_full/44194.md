croaks:/kroʊks/ /krəʊks/| n. v. |呱呱叫声；（青蛙或乌鸦等）叫声；临终遗言|The sound a frog or crow makes. Also can refer to a person's last words before death.|The frogs croaked loudly in the pond. 池塘里的青蛙大声呱呱叫。|近义词：caws, squawks, rasps|反义词:chirps, tweets|词性:名词，动词

croaks:/kroʊks/ /krəʊks/| v. |呱呱叫；（人）沙哑地说；死|To make a deep hoarse sound like a frog or crow. Also means to die.|He croaked his last words. 他用沙哑的声音说出临终遗言。|近义词：rasps, whispers, dies|反义词:shouts, sings|词性:动词
