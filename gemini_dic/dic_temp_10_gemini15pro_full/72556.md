fluttered:/ˈflʌtərd/ /ˈflʌtəd/ | v. | 颤动，飘动，挥动，拍动 |  moved lightly and quickly; trembled; flapped (wings) quickly and lightly | The butterfly fluttered its wings. 蝴蝶拍动着翅膀。 | synonyms: flapped, trembled, quivered, waved | antonyms:  still, motionless | verb

fluttered:/ˈflʌtərd/ /ˈflʌtəd/ | adj. |  (rare) 激动的，慌乱的 | excited and nervous |  She felt a flutter of excitement. 她感到一阵激动。 (This uses the noun form) | synonyms: agitated, nervous, flustered | antonyms: calm, composed | adjective (This usage is less common than the verb form)
