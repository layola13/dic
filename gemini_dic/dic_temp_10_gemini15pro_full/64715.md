eschatologist:/ˌɛskəˈtɒlədʒɪst/| n. | 末世学家，末世论者 | A person who studies eschatology, the part of theology concerned with death, judgment, and the final destiny of the soul and of humankind. |  The eschatologist predicted the end of the world was near.  末世学家预言世界末日即将到来。| synonyms: millennialist, apocalypticist | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "secularist" or "materialist" in a philosophical sense) | noun


Etymology:  From Greek *eskhatos* (last, furthest, uttermost) + *-logia* (speaking, discourse, study).
