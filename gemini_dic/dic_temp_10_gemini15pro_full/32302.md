chiffon:/ʃɪˈfɒn/ (US) /ʃiːˈfɒn/ (UK)| n. adj.|雪纺绸，薄纱；雪纺绸制的|A sheer, lightweight fabric, typically made of silk or nylon.|She wore a beautiful chiffon dress to the party. 她穿了一件漂亮的雪纺连衣裙去参加派对。|近义词：gauze, voile, georgette|反义词: (As a fabric, it doesn't have a direct opposite, perhaps something heavy like) velvet, tweed|词性: noun, adjective


chiffon:/ʃɪˈfɒn/ (US) /ʃiːˈfɒn/ (UK)| adj.|雪纺绸制的|Made of chiffon fabric.|The chiffon curtains billowed in the breeze. 雪纺窗帘在微风中飘动。|近义词：gauzy, sheer|反义词: thick, opaque|词性: adjective
