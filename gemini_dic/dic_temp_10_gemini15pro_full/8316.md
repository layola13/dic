anti-papist:/ˌæntiˈpeɪpɪst/
adjective
反教皇的；反对天主教教皇的
Opposed to the authority of the Pope, especially in political matters; often with implications of hostility towards Roman Catholics in general.
He delivered an anti-papist speech, railing against the influence of the Vatican. 他发表了一篇反教皇的演讲，抨击梵蒂冈的影响力。
The anti-papist laws were designed to limit the power of the Catholic Church. 这些反教皇的法律旨在限制天主教教会的权力。

近义词: anti-Catholic, anti-Roman Catholic
反义词: pro-Catholic, papist
