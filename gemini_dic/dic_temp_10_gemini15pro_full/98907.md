intensitive:/ɪnˈtɛnsɪv/ | adj. | 集中的，密集的，加强的 | Concentrated on a single area or subject or a small group; characterized by a high degree of concentration. |例句：The farmer practiced intensive agriculture on his small plot of land. 农民在他那小块土地上进行集约农业。 | 例句：The hospital has an intensive care unit for critically ill patients. 这家医院有一个重症病房，专门收治危重病人。| 近义词：concentrated, focused, thorough | 反义词: extensive, superficial | 词性:形容词

词源: Late 16th century: from French *intensif*, from medieval Latin *intensivus*, from Latin *intendere* ‘to stretch out, strain’.
