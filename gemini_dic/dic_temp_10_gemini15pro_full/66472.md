exercisable:/ˈɛksərˌsaɪzəbl/| adj.|可执行的，可行使的|Able to be exercised; capable of being put into action or effect.|The company has exercisable warrants that allow it to purchase additional shares.  这家公司拥有可行使的认股权证，允许其购买更多股票。|例句： The options are exercisable after one year.  这些期权一年后可行使。|近义词：enforceable, applicable, usable, valid|反义词:unenforceable, unusable, invalid|词性:adjective
Etymology: From exercise + -able.  Ultimately from Latin *exercēre* "to keep busy, train, practice."
