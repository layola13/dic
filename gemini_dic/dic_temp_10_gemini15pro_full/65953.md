exactnesses:/ɪɡˈzæktnəsɪz/
n.
精确性；准确性；严谨性
The exactnesses of the measurements were crucial to the success of the experiment. 这些测量的精确性对于实验的成功至关重要。
例句：The engineer insisted on the exactnesses of the calculations.  工程师坚持计算的精确性。

例句：She admired the exactnesses of his work, every detail perfectly in place. 她钦佩他工作的精确性，每个细节都完美到位。

近义词：precision, accuracy, meticulousness, strictness
反义词：inexactness, inaccuracy, imprecision, vagueness
词性: 名词 (plural)


词源:
From Middle English *exactnes, borrowed from Old French exactitude, from Latin exactus (“exact, precise”), past participle of exigō (“demand, require, weigh, measure, complete”), from ex- (“out, from”) + agō (“drive, lead, act”).
