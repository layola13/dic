forswore:/fɔːrˈswɔːr/
verb
发誓放弃；正式放弃
renounce or give up (something) under oath.
He forswore all worldly pleasures. 他发誓放弃一切世俗的享乐。
近义词：renounce, abjure, repudiate
反义词：affirm, embrace, uphold
词性：动词(past tense of forswear)

词源:
From Middle English *forswēren, from Old English forswęrian (“to swear falsely, perjure oneself, abjure, renounce on oath”), from for- (“away, without”) + swęrian (“to swear”). Cognate with Old Frisian forswēria (“to forswear”), Old Saxon forswērian (“to forswear”), Old High German firstwerian (“to forswear, abjure, renounce”), Old Norse forsverja (“to forswear”).
