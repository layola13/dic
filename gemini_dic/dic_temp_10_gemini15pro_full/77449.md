gawp:/ɡɔːp/| vi.| 呆呆地看，瞠目结舌|To stare openly and stupidly, often with the mouth open. |She gawped at the celebrity, speechless. 她呆呆地看着那位名人，说不出话来。|Example:  He just stood there gawping at the wreckage. 他只是站在那里呆呆地看着残骸。

|Synonyms: stare, gape, ogle|Antonyms: glance, ignore|Verb
