deforms:/dɪˈfɔːrmz/
verb
使变形；使残废；丑化；扭曲；
Makes something lose its normal or original shape, often in an unattractive way; spoils the appearance of something.
The intense heat deformed the plastic.  高温使塑料变形。
His face was deformed by a scar. 他的脸被一个疤痕弄得变形了。
distort, contort, warp, twist
beautify, enhance, improve
v.
