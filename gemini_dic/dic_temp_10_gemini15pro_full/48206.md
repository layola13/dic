decongestant:/ˌdiːkənˈdʒɛstənt/| n. adj.|减充血剂，解充血的|A drug that reduces congestion, especially in the nose and sinuses. | I took a decongestant to relieve my stuffy nose. 我服用了减充血剂来缓解鼻塞。|Synonyms: antihistamine, nasal spray|Antonyms: none|noun, adjective

decongestant:/ˌdiːkənˈdʒɛstənt/| adj.|解充血的|Having the effect of reducing congestion. | The decongestant properties of the medication helped me breathe easier.  这种药物的解充血特性帮助我呼吸更顺畅。|Synonyms: relieving, clearing|Antonyms: congesting, blocking|adjective
