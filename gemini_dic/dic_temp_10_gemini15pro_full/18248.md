believe: /bɪˈliːv/ (US) /bɪˈliːv/ (UK)| v.|相信，认为|To accept that something is true or that someone is telling the truth; to think or suppose; to have religious faith.|I believe his story. 我相信他的故事。|synonyms: trust, accept, credit, deem|antonyms: disbelieve, doubt|verb

believe: /bɪˈliːv/ (US) /bɪˈliːv/ (UK)| n.|相信，信仰|An acceptance that something is true or that someone is telling the truth; trust, faith, or confidence in someone or something.|His belief in God is strong. 他对上帝的信仰很坚定。|synonyms: faith, trust, conviction|antonyms: disbelief, doubt|noun

Simple Past:
I believed him when he said he was sorry. 当他说对不起的时候，我相信了他。
She believed in Santa Claus when she was a child. 她小时候相信圣诞老人。


Past Continuous:
I was believing his story until I saw the evidence. 我一直相信他的故事，直到我看到了证据。
They were believing the rumors until they heard the truth.  他们一直相信那些谣言，直到他们听到了真相。


Past Perfect:
I had believed him until he lied to me. 我之前一直相信他，直到他对我撒谎。
She had believed in his innocence until the trial.  她一直相信他是无辜的，直到审判开始。


Past Perfect Continuous:
I had been believing his lies for years before I realized the truth.  多年来我一直相信他的谎言，直到我意识到真相。
They had been believing that story for generations before it was debunked.  几代人以来他们一直相信那个故事，直到它被揭穿。


Simple Future:
I will believe it when I see it. 我眼见为实。
He will believe you if you tell him the truth. 如果你告诉他真相，他会相信你的。


Future Continuous:
I will be believing your story until I hear otherwise. 我会一直相信你的故事，直到我听到其他说法。
They will be believing the forecast until the weather changes. 他们会一直相信天气预报，直到天气变化。


Future Perfect:
By the time the evidence is presented, I will have believed his story for weeks. 到证据提交的时候，我将已经相信他的故事好几个星期了。
By next year, she will have believed in herself enough to start her own business. 到明年，她将已经足够相信自己，可以开始自己的事业了。


Future Perfect Continuous:

Subjunctive Mood:
If I were to believe everything I hear, I would be very gullible. 如果我相信我听到的一切，我会很容易上当受骗。
I wish I could believe him. 我希望我能相信他。


Passive Voice:
He is believed to be innocent. 他被认为是无辜的。
It is believed that the earth is round. 人们相信地球是圆的。


Interrogative Sentence:

Negative Sentence:
I don't believe you. 我不相信你。
She doesn't believe in ghosts. 她不相信鬼魂。
