overtreated:/ˌoʊvərˈtriːtɪd/
adjective
过度治疗的
Having received excessive or unnecessary medical treatment.
The patient was overtreated with antibiotics, leading to complications.  这个病人过度使用了抗生素，导致了并发症。
Overtreated skin can become dry and irritated.  过度护理的皮肤会变得干燥和 раздраженный。
Synonyms: overmedicated, overmanaged
Antonyms: undertreated, neglected
词性: 形容词
