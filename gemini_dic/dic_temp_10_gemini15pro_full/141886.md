ordainer:/ɔːrˈdeɪnər/
noun
任命者；授予圣职者；制定者
someone who ordains someone else (especially someone who ordains someone as a priest)  a person who establishes or decrees something
The ordainer laid his hands on the new priest's head.  任命者将他的手放在新神父的头上。
The city council is the ordainer of the new traffic laws. 市议会是新交通法的制定者。
Synonyms: appointer, commissioner, establisher, institutor
Antonyms:  remover, deposer
