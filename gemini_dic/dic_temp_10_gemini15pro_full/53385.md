disagreed:/ˌdɪsəˈɡriːd/ | v. | 不同意，意见不合 | to have a different opinion from someone else; to not agree |  They disagreed about where to go for dinner. 他们对于去哪里吃晚饭意见不一。|Synonyms: differ, dissent, object | Antonyms: agree, concur, consent | verb

disagreed:/ˌdɪsəˈɡriːd/ | v. | 争论，争执 | to argue or quarrel | The two politicians disagreed fiercely on the new policy.  两位政治家就新政策展开了激烈的争论。|Synonyms: argue, dispute, quarrel | Antonyms: agree, harmonize, compromise | verb
