lineage:/ˈlɪniɪdʒ/| n.|血统，世系，家系|A sequence of biological entities, such as people, organisms, or cells, each of which is considered to have originated from an earlier member of the sequence.|She is very proud of her royal lineage. 她对自己的皇室血统感到非常自豪。|Synonyms: ancestry, descent, genealogy, heritage, pedigree|Antonyms: N/A|noun
