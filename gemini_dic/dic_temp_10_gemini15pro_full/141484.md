opinionless:/əˈpɪnjənlɪs/
adjective
无意见的；没有主见的
Lacking opinions or having no definite opinions.
He seemed opinionless on the matter, simply agreeing with whatever was suggested. 他在这个问题上似乎没有意见，只是同意任何建议。
近义词:neutral, indifferent, apathetic, passive
反义词:opinionated, decisive, assertive
词性:adjective
