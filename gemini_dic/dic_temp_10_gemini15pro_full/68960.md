fatherlinesses:/ˈfɑːðərlɪnɪsɪz/
n.
父爱，慈父般的品质（复数）
The qualities of a kind and loving father.
He displayed great fatherlinesses towards his children and their friends. 他对他的孩子和他们的朋友表现出极大的父爱。
近义词：paternalism, protectiveness
反义词：neglect, indifference
词性：名词

fatherliness:/ˈfɑːðərlɪnɪs/
n.
父爱，慈父般的品质
The quality of being fatherly; kindness and protectiveness.
His fatherliness made him a beloved figure in the community.  他的父爱使他成为社区中受爱戴的人物。
近义词：paternalism, protectiveness
反义词：neglect, indifference
词性：名词

fatherly:/ˈfɑːðəli/
adj.
父亲的；慈父般的
Like a father; kind and protective.
He gave me some fatherly advice. 他给了我一些父亲般的建议。
近义词：paternal
反义词：unfatherly
词性：形容词

father:/ˈfɑːðər/
n.
父亲
A male parent.
My father taught me how to ride a bike. 我的父亲教我如何骑自行车。
近义词：dad, papa
反义词：mother
词性：名词
father:/ˈfɑːðər/
vt.
成为…的父亲；创立，创建
To be the father of; to originate or create something.
He fathered three children.  他是三个孩子的父亲。
近义词：sire, beget
反义词：N/A
词性：动词

词源：From Old English fæder, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch vader and German Vater, from an Indo-European root shared by Latin pater and Greek patēr.
