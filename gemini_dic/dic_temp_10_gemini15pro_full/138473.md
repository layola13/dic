novellas:/nəˈvɛləz/ | n. | 中篇小说 | A short novel, typically between 17,500 and 40,000 words. |  Example: She published two novels and three novellas. 她出版了两部长篇小说和三部中篇小说。 | Example:  He is known for his captivating novellas. 他以其引人入胜的中篇小说而闻名。 | Synonyms: novelette, short novel | Antonyms: novel, epic | noun
