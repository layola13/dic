ascented:/əkˈsɛntɪd/ | adj. | 带口音的，有重音的 | Pronounced with a noticeable accent; emphasized. |  She spoke in accented English. 她说着带口音的英语。

The accented syllable is the second one. 重读音节是第二个。 | 近义词：stressed, emphasized | 反义词：unaccented, unstressed | 词性: adjective
