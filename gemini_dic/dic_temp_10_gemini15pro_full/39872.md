constellations:/ˌkɒnstəˈleɪʃənz/ (US) /ˌkɑːnstəˈleɪʃənz/| n. |星座|A group of stars forming a recognizable pattern that is traditionally named after its apparent form or identified with a mythological figure.|例句：The ancient Greeks identified many constellations. 古代希腊人识别出了许多星座。

例句：She was fascinated by the constellations in the night sky. 她被夜空中的星座迷住了。|近义词：asterisms, clusters|反义词: (无直接反义词)|名词
