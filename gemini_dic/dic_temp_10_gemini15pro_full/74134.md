foundation's:/faʊnˈdeɪʃənz/
noun
基金会的；地基的
Possessive form of "foundation," indicating ownership or association.
The foundation's mission is to support education.  基金会的使命是支持教育。
The building's foundation's cracks are a serious concern.  这栋建筑的地基裂缝是一个严重的问题。
synonyms: base's, groundwork's, underpinning's
antonyms: superstructure's, top's
