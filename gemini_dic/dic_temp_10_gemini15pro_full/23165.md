bovinely:/ˈboʊvaɪnli/| adv.|像牛一样地；迟钝地；愚蠢地|In a manner resembling that of cattle; sluggishly; stupidly.  |He stared bovinely at the television. 他呆呆地盯着电视。|近义词：stupidly, dully, vacantly, blankly|反义词：intelligently, sharply, astutely|词性:副词
词源: Late 16th century: from late Latin bovinus (from bos, bov- ‘ox, cow’) + -ly.
