oversprung:/ˌoʊvərˈsprʌŋ/
adjective
过度弹簧的，弹簧过强的
having springs that are too strong or too tightly coiled, resulting in a bouncy or unstable ride.
The car's suspension was oversprung, making for a jarring ride on rough roads. 这辆车的悬架弹簧过强，在崎岖不平的道路上行驶颠簸得很厉害。

The oversprung mattress made it difficult to get a good night's sleep. 弹簧过强的床垫让人难以睡个好觉。

近义词: stiff, bouncy
反义词: soft, undersprung
词性: adjective
