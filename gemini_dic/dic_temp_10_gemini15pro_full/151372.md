parlayed:/pɑːrˈleɪd/ |adj. |被充分利用的| having been used to achieve something further| Her parlayed skills in negotiation helped her secure the deal. 她充分利用的谈判技巧帮助她达成了这笔交易。|synonyms: leveraged, maximized | antonyms: wasted, squandered |adjective
