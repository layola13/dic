nonzealously:/ˌnɒnˈzɛləsli/
adverb|不热心地，冷淡地|Without zeal; indifferently; apathetically. |He completed the task nonzealously, clearly lacking enthusiasm.|近义词:apathetically, indifferently, half-heartedly, perfunctorily|反义词:zealously, enthusiastically, eagerly, fervently|词性:副词
Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "zealously" (from "zeal" + "-ously" adverb suffix). "Zeal" comes from the Late Latin "zelus," borrowed from the Greek "zēlos" meaning "emulation, rivalry, zeal."
