chide:/tʃaɪd/| vt. |责骂，斥责|To scold or rebuke someone gently or reproachfully. | She chided him for his carelessness. 她责备他的粗心。|Synonyms:  reproach, scold, rebuke, reprimand|Antonyms:  praise, commend, approve|verb
