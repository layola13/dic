overhelpfulness:/ˌoʊvərˈhɛlpfʊlnəs/| n.| 过分热心，过度帮助|The state of being excessively helpful, often to the point of being intrusive or counterproductive.|Her overhelpfulness can be smothering at times. 她的过分热心有时让人感到窒息。|Synonyms: officiousness, intrusiveness, meddling|Antonyms: neglect, indifference|Noun
