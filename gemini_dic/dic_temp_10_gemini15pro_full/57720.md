dreadfulness:/ˈdrɛdflnəs/| n.|可怕; 恐怖; 令人恐惧|The dreadfulness of the situation was overwhelming.  这种情况的可怕程度令人难以承受。|The film conveyed the dreadfulness of war with unflinching realism. 这部电影以毫不畏惧的现实主义手法展现了战争的恐怖。|Synonyms: awfulness, terribleness, frightfulness, ghastliness|Antonyms: pleasantness, delightfulness|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From dreadful + -ness.  Dreadful comes from Middle English *dredeful, from Old English *drēadfull, from drēad (dread) + -full.
