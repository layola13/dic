nonalphabetically:/ˌnɒnˌælfəˈbetɪkli/ | adv. | 非字母顺序地 | Not in alphabetical order, arranged in a way other than according to the conventional order of letters. | The books were shelved nonalphabetically, making them difficult to find.  这些书不是按字母顺序排列的，很难找到。| The files are arranged nonalphabetically by date. 这些文件不是按字母顺序排列，而是按日期排列的。 | synonym: out of alphabetical order | antonym: alphabetically | adverb
