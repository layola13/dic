declaredness:/dɪˈklɛərdnɪs/| n.|公开声明的状态；明确性|The declaredness of his intentions left no room for doubt. 他的意图表达得很明确，不容置疑。|近义词：explicitness, clarity, openness|反义词：vagueness, ambiguity, obscurity|词性:名词

词源:  Derived from the verb "declare" (from Old French *declarer, from Latin declarare "to make clear, reveal, explain") + the suffix "-ness" (forming nouns denoting a state or quality).
