moneyed:/ˈmʌnid/ | adj. | 富有的，有钱的 | Having a lot of money; wealthy. |  The moneyed elite control a large portion of the country's wealth.  富有的精英阶层控制着这个国家的大部分财富。| Synonyms: wealthy, affluent, rich, prosperous | Antonyms: poor, impoverished, destitute, penniless | adjective

moneyed:/ˈmʌnid/ | adj. | 资金雄厚的 |  Supported or financed by substantial financial resources. | The museum launched a moneyed campaign to acquire the rare artifact.  博物馆发起了一场资金雄厚的运动来收购这件稀有文物。| Synonyms: well-funded, well-financed, deep-pocketed | Antonyms: underfunded, poorly-financed | adjective
