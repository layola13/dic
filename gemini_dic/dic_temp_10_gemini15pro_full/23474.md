bragging:/ˈbræɡɪŋ/ /ˈbræɡɪŋ/| n. |吹嘘，炫耀|The act of boasting about oneself or one's achievements.|He couldn't stop bragging about his new car. 他不停地吹嘘他的新车。|近义词：boasting, vaunting|反义词：modesty, humility|词性:noun

braggart:/ˈbræɡərt/ /ˈbræɡərt/| n. |吹牛大王，自夸者|A person who boasts about achievements or possessions.|He's such a braggart; he can't stop talking about himself. 他真是个吹牛大王，他没完没了地谈论自己。|近义词：boaster, show-off|反义词：humble person|词性:noun

brag:/bræɡ/ /bræɡ/| v. |吹嘘，吹牛|To talk boastfully about oneself or one's achievements.|He bragged about his golfing skills. 他吹嘘他的高尔夫球技术。|近义词：boast, vaunt|反义词：be modest|词性:verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *braggen*, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old Norse *bragr* ("best, foremost").
