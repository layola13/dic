bohriums:/bɔːriəm/| n. |钅波|A synthetic radioactive chemical element with the symbol Bh and atomic number 107. It is an extremely radioactive metal. Bohrium is named after Niels Bohr, a Danish physicist. |Bohriums are very unstable and decay quickly. 钅波非常不稳定，很快就会衰变。|The synthesis of bohriums was a major achievement in nuclear chemistry. 钅波的合成是核化学的一项重大成就。|近义词:transactinide (a general term for elements beyond actinium), synthetic element|反义词: None (as it's a specific element, it doesn't have a direct opposite)|词性:noun
