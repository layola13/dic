half-enforced:/ˌhɑːf ɪnˈfɔːrst/
adj.
半强制的，部分实施的
Partially or incompletely enforced; enforced to some extent but not fully.
The new regulations were half-enforced, leading to confusion and inconsistency. 新规章制度执行不力，导致混乱和不一致。
The half-enforced policy created a loophole for many businesses. 这项执行不力的政策为许多企业创造了漏洞。
partially enforced, weakly enforced
fully enforced, strictly enforced
adjective
