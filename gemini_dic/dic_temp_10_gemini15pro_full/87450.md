hesp:/hɛsp/| n.|扣环，搭扣；门闩；把手|A fastening, clasp, latch, or handle.  Primarily a Scottish and Northern English term.|He lifted the hesp and opened the gate. 他 поднял засов и открыл ворота. (He lifted the latch and opened the gate.)|近义词：latch, clasp, fastener, handle|反义词:(None -  lacks a direct opposite.  Consider "unfastened" as an antonymic *state*)|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hæps*, related to Old Norse *hespa*. Ultimately from a Proto-Germanic root meaning "to grasp, seize."
