fellers:/ˈfeləz/| n.|伙计们，家伙们|Informal, often humorous term of address for a group of men or boys.  A variant of "fellows."|"Well, fellers, let's get this show on the road!" 伙计们，咱们开始吧！|近义词：guys, chaps, lads, blokes|反义词: (No direct antonym.  Consider "ladies" if referring specifically to a group of women)|n.
