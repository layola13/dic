cliche:/kliːˈʃeɪ/ /kliːˈʃeɪ/
noun
陈词滥调；老生常谈
a phrase or opinion that is overused and betrays a lack of original thought.
例句：The plot of the movie was full of cliches.  这部电影的情节充满了陈词滥调。

例句：He used the cliche "time heals all wounds". 他用了“时间治愈一切创伤”这个陈词滥调。

近义词：platitude, banality, stereotype, commonplace
反义词：originality, freshness, novelty
词性：noun
