nongrain:/ˈnɒnˌɡreɪn/ (US) /ˈnɒnˌɡreɪn/ (UK)| n.|非谷物，非粮食作物|A crop that is not a cereal grain, such as a fruit, vegetable, or oilseed.|Soybeans are a common nongrain crop. 大豆是一种常见的非谷物作物。|Synonyms: non-cereal, non-grain crop|Antonyms: grain, cereal|noun
