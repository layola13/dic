hermaphroditism:/hərˈmæfrədɪtɪzəm/ | n. |雌雄同体;两性畸形 |The condition of having both male and female reproductive organs. |Hermaphroditism is rare in mammals. 哺乳动物中雌雄同体是罕见的。| Hermaphroditism can occur in some plant species.  某些植物物种中会发生雌雄同体现象。| synonyms: intersexuality, bisexuality (in biology), androgyny | antonyms: unisexuality, gonochorism | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *hermaphroditismus, from Greek Hermaphroditos, the name of a mythological character who became united in one body with the nymph Salmacis.
