fleeter:/ˈfliːtər (US), ˈfliːtə(r) (UK)| adj.|更快速的，更敏捷的|faster; more rapid|The newer model is fleeter than the old one.  这款新车型比旧车型更快。|The gazelle is a fleeter animal than the rhinoceros. 瞪羚比犀牛更加敏捷。|近义词:faster, quicker, swifter, speedier|反义词:slower, more sluggish|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *fleter, comparative of *flet "swift, nimble," from Old English flēot "swift, quick," from Proto-Germanic *fleutiz "swift, nimble," from Proto-Indo-European *plewd- "to flow, float, swim."  The comparative form -er is a standard English suffix indicating a higher degree of a quality.
