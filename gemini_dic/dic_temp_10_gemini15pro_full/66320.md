excreted:/ɪkˈskriːtɪd/
| v. (past participle)|排泄；分泌| discharged from the body as waste; separated or eliminated. | The kidneys excrete waste products from the blood. 肾脏排泄血液中的废物。|  The sweat glands excrete perspiration. 汗腺分泌汗液。|近义词：eliminated, discharged, expelled, voided|反义词: absorbed, ingested, retained|词性:动词

Etymology: From Latin <em>excretus</em>, past participle of <em>excernere</em> ("to sift out, separate"), from <em>ex-</em> ("out") + <em>cernere</em> ("to distinguish, separate, sift").
