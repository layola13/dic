bull:/bʊl/| n.|公牛；(股市)上涨；胡说|A male bovine animal, typically large and strong, used for breeding and as a draught animal. Also, a rising trend in the stock market, or nonsense.|The bull lowered its horns and charged. 公牛 lowered its horns and charged.

The market is bullish today. 今天股市看涨。

That's a load of bull! 那是一派胡言！|近义词：steer, ox (for male bovine); rise, increase (for stock market); nonsense, rubbish (for informal meaning)|反义词：cow (for male bovine); bear, decline (for stock market)|noun
