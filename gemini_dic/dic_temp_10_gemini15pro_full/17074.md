batikked:/bæˈtiːkt/ | adj. | 用蜡染装饰的；蜡染的 | Decorated with a batik design; made using the batik technique. |  The artist displayed her batikked scarves. 艺术家展示了她蜡染的围巾。| synonyms: dyed, printed, patterned | antonyms: plain, undyed | adjective


Etymology: From batik (noun) + -ed (suffix forming adjectives). Batik itself comes from the Javanese word "ambatik" which means "a cloth with a dot".
