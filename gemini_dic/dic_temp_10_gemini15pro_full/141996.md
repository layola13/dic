organise:/ˈɔːɡənaɪz/ (British) /ˈɔːrɡənaɪz/ (American)| v. |组织，安排|To arrange or put in order.|例句：She organised a surprise party for her husband. 她为丈夫组织了一个惊喜派对。|例句：He needs to organise his thoughts before the presentation. 他需要在演讲前整理一下思路。|近义词：arrange, coordinate, plan, manage, structure|反义词：disorganise, mess up|词性:动词

organise:/ˈɔːɡənaɪz/ (British) /ˈɔːrɡənaɪz/ (American)| n. |组织，机构 (较少用作名词)|A structure or system.|例句：The organise of the human body is incredibly complex.  人体的构造非常复杂。 (这个例句中organise更像是organisation的缩写，实际使用中很少见)|例句：The organise of the event was chaotic. 活动的组织混乱不堪。(这个例句中organise更像是organisation的缩写，实际使用中很少见)|近义词：structure, system, organization|反义词：disorder, chaos|词性:名词 (较少用)
