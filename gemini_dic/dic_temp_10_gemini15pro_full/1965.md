activating: /ˈæktɪveɪtɪŋ/
adjective
激活的，活化的
Making something active or operative.  Causing something to start working.
The activating enzyme plays a crucial role in the metabolic process.  这种激活酶在新陈代谢过程中起着至关重要的作用。

The company is activating its emergency plan. 公司正在启动应急预案。

近义词: stimulating, triggering, energizing, starting, initiating
反义词: deactivating, inhibiting, suppressing, stopping
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *activatus, past participle of activare "to make active," from Latin activus "active," from actus, past participle of agere "to do, drive, lead, act."


USAGE->
Present Participle:
The activating signal triggers the release of hormones. 激活信号触发激素的释放。
He is activating the security system right now. 他现在正在启动安全系统。


Past Participle:
The newly activated system is now online.  新激活的系统现在已经上线。
The gene was activated by exposure to radiation. 该基因因暴露于辐射而被激活。


Adjective:
The activating agent in the detergent removes stains effectively. 洗涤剂中的活性剂能有效去除污渍。
The software has an activating code that must be entered. 该软件有一个必须输入的激活码。


PHRASE->
activating enzyme = 激活酶
activating agent = 活化剂
activating signal = 激活信号
activating function = 激活函数 (in computer science, especially neural networks)
activating current = 激活电流
