depaint:/diːˈpeɪnt/
verb
去除绘制; 去除绘画;
To remove paint or other coloring from a surface, digitally or otherwise. This can refer to the act of removing paint physically or using software to remove elements from an image.
The artist decided to depaint the canvas and start over. 艺术家决定把画布上的颜料去除，重新开始。
The software can depaint the unwanted objects from the photo.  这个软件可以从照片中去除不需要的物体。

近义词:erase, remove, delete, obliterate
反义词:paint, color, draw
词性:verb

depaint:/diːˈpeɪnt/
noun
去除绘制（的行为或结果）
The act or result of removing paint or other coloring, especially digitally.
The depaint of the graffiti took several hours. 去除涂鸦花了几个小时。
The software allows for precise depaint of specific areas in an image.  该软件允许精确去除图像中特定区域的内容。

近义词:removal, erasure, deletion
反义词:painting, coloring, drawing
词性:noun
