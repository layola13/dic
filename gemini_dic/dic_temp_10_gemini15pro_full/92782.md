iciest:/ˈaɪsiɪst/| adj.|最冰冷的|Superlative form of icy: most icy.  Covered with or consisting of ice; extremely cold.|The iciest winds blew across the frozen plains.  狂风裹挟着冰雪吹过冰冻的平原。|近义词：coldest, frostiest, chilliest|反义词: warmest, hottest|词性:形容词
Etymology: From ice + -y + -est.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *isaz.
