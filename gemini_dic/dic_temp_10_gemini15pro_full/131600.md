noncomplacencies:/ˌnɒnkəmˈpleɪsənsiz/
n.
不满足，不懈怠，不安于现状
lack of complacency; the state of being noncomplacent.  A feeling of unease or dissatisfaction with the current situation, often driving a desire for improvement or change.
The coach stressed the importance of noncomplacencies, reminding the team that even after a string of victories, they couldn't afford to let their guard down. 教练强调了不懈怠的重要性，提醒球队即使在取得一连串胜利之后，也不能放松警惕。
Her noncomplacencies about her current job led her to pursue further education. 她对目前工作的不安于现状促使她继续深造。
近义词: vigilance, alertness, dissatisfaction, unease
反义词: complacency, satisfaction, contentment
词性: noun (plural)
