flightlessness:/ˈflaɪtləsnəs/
n.
无法飞行;飞翔力丧失
The inability to fly.
例句：The flightlessness of the dodo contributed to its extinction. 渡渡鸟的无法飞行导致了它的灭绝。

例句：Penguins evolved flightlessness to better adapt to their aquatic lifestyle.  企鹅进化出不会飞的能力是为了更好地适应它们的水生生活方式。

近义词：inability to fly, winglessness
反义词：flight, ability to fly
词性：noun
