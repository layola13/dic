compensations:/ˌkɒmpənˈseɪʃənz/ | n. | 补偿；赔偿金；报酬 | something, typically money, awarded to someone as a recompense for loss, injury, or suffering. | Example: He received compensation for the injuries he suffered in the accident. 他因事故受伤获得了赔偿。

Example:  She demanded compensation for the damage to her car. 她要求赔偿汽车的损失。

近义词: payment, recompense, restitution, remuneration, indemnity

反义词: penalty, forfeiture, loss

词性: noun (plural)

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French compensacion, from late Latin compensatio(n-), from compensare ‘weigh together’, from com- ‘together’ + pensare ‘weigh’.
