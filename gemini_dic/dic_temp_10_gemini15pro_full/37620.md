communique:/kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪ/ /kəˈmjuːnɪˌkeɪ/
n.
公报；官方声明；简报
an official announcement or statement, especially one made to the media
The government issued a communique following the meeting. 政府在会后发布了一份公报。
synonym:announcement, statement, bulletin, report, dispatch
antonym:none
noun
