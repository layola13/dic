diligentnesses:/ˈdɪlɪdʒəntnəsɪz/| n. |勤奋，勤勉|The state or quality of being diligent; persistent effort; assiduity.|Her diligentnesses in her studies resulted in excellent grades. 她学习上的勤奋使她取得了优异的成绩。|近义词：assiduity, industry, perseverance|反义词：laziness, negligence, apathy|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *diligentnesse, from Old French diligence, from Latin dīligentia (“carefulness, attentiveness”), from dīligēns (“careful, attentive”), present participle of dīligō (“esteem highly, love; attend to carefully”), from dī- (“apart, thoroughly”) + legō (“choose, gather”).
