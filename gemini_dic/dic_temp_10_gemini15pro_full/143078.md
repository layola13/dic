outbrave:/aʊtˈbreɪv/
verb
胜过；比…更勇敢
to be braver than someone or something
例句：She outbraved the storm and reached safety. 她冒着暴风雨到达了安全的地方。

例句：He outbraved his fear and spoke out against the injustice. 他克服了恐惧，公开反对不公正。

近义词：outdo, surpass, excel, defeat
反义词：cower, yield, surrender
词性：v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + brave.  "Out-" acts as an intensifier, meaning "to exceed or surpass."  "Brave" comes from the Old French "brave," meaning "courageous" or "splendid," ultimately of uncertain origin, possibly from Italian "bravo."
