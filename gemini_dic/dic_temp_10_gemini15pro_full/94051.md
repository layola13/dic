impartible:/ɪmˈpɑːrtɪbl/
adjective
不可分的
not capable of being divided or shared
The estate was impartible, so it passed entirely to the eldest son.  这片地产是不可分割的，所以它全部传给了长子。
近义词：indivisible, inseparable
反义词：divisible, separable
词性: adjective

impartible:/ɪmˈpɑːrtɪbl/
noun
不可分割的事物
something that cannot be divided or shared
The crown jewels are considered an impartible inheritance.  王冠珠宝被认为是不可分割的遗产。
近义词：unit, whole
反义词：portion, part
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin impartibilis, from in- "not" + partibilis "divisible," from partire "to divide," from pars "part."
