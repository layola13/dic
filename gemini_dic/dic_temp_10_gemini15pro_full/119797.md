migrative:/ˈmaɪɡrətɪv/| adj.|迁徙的，迁移的|Relating to or characterized by migration.  Describing something that involves or is related to the act of moving from one place to another, often periodically.|The swallows exhibit migrative behavior, flying south for the winter. 燕子表现出迁徙行为，冬天飞往南方。|Synonyms: migratory, nomadic, wandering, mobile|Antonyms: sedentary, resident, stationary|adjective

migrative:/ˈmaɪɡrətɪv/| adj.|迁移的|Relating to transferring data or systems from one platform to another.|The company is undergoing a migrative process to upgrade their servers. 该公司正在进行迁移过程以升级其服务器。|Synonyms: transitional, shifting, porting|Antonyms: static, fixed|adjective
