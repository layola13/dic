milds:/maɪldz/| n.| 淡味啤酒; 低烈度香烟 |A type of beer or cigarette that is not strong.|He prefers milds to stronger beers. 他更喜欢淡啤酒而不是烈性啤酒。|近义词:lights (for cigarettes), light beer|反义词:strongs, full-flavored|词性:名词

Etymology:  From the adjective "mild" + plural suffix "-s".  "Mild" comes from Old English "mildel" meaning "gentle, kind, merciful," ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*mildjaz."
