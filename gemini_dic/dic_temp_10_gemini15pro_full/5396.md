almagra:/ælˈmɑːɡrə/ | n. |  红赭石，代赭石 | A deep red ochre used as a pigment. | The artist used almagra to create the warm tones in the painting. 艺术家用红赭石来创作画作中温暖的色调。 | 近义词: red ochre, ochre, hematite | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider contrasting colors like azure or white) | 词性: noun

词源: From Spanish *almagra*, from Arabic *al-maḡra* ( المغرة ), meaning "the ochre."
