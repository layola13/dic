disinfection:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfekʃən/ | n. |消毒，杀菌|The process of cleaning something, especially with a chemical, in order to destroy bacteria.|例句：Regular disinfection of surfaces is important to prevent the spread of illness. 定期对表面进行消毒对于预防疾病传播很重要。

例句：The disinfection of the surgical instruments was thorough.  手术器械的消毒很彻底。


近义词：sterilization, sanitization |反义词:contamination, infection| 词性: 名词
