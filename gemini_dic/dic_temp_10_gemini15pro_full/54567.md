dislodgment:/dɪsˈlɒdʒmənt/
n.
移动；移位；排出
The act of removing something from a fixed position.
The earthquake caused the dislodgment of several boulders. 地震导致几块巨石移位。
removal, displacement, eviction
fixation, attachment
noun


dislodge:/dɪsˈlɒdʒ/
vt.
逐出；取出；移动
To remove something from a fixed position.
The heavy rains dislodged several trees.  暴雨冲走了几棵树。
remove, displace, eject
fix, secure, attach
verb
