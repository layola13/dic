oedematous:/ɪˈdɛmətəs/
adjective
水肿的，浮肿的
marked by edema; swollen
例句：The patient's legs were oedematous due to heart failure. 病人的双腿由于心力衰竭而水肿。

例句：The oedematous tissue was biopsied to determine the cause of the swelling.  对水肿组织进行活检以确定肿胀的原因。

近义词：swollen, puffy, bloated
反义词：normal, healthy
