non-theatrical:/ˌnɑːn θiˈætrɪkəl/
| adj. |非剧场的，非戏剧的；非供剧场放映的| Intended for distribution or exhibition in venues other than traditional movie theaters. | The film had a successful non-theatrical run in schools and community centers.  这部电影在学校和社区中心的非剧场放映非常成功。| Synonyms: non-commercial, private, independent | Antonyms: theatrical, commercial, public | adjective

non-theatrical:/ˌnɑːn θiˈætrɪkəl/
| n. |非剧场放映；非剧场发行| The distribution or exhibition of films or videos outside of traditional movie theaters. | The studio earned significant revenue from non-theatricals. 这家制片厂从非剧场发行中获得了可观的收入。| Synonyms: private screening, non-commercial distribution | Antonyms: theatrical release, commercial distribution | noun
