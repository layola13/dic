anisotrope:/ˌænɪˈsɒtrəpi/ /ˌænɪˈsɑːtrəpi/ | n. | 各向异性 |  The property of being directionally dependent, as opposed to isotropy.  This means a material's properties vary depending on the direction in which they are measured. | The anisotropy of the wood made it split easily along the grain. 木头的各向异性使得它很容易沿着纹理裂开。|  Synonym: inhomogeneity, nonuniformity | Antonym: isotropy, uniformity | noun

anisotropic:/ˌænɪsəˈtrɒpɪk/ /ˌænɪsəˈtrɑːpɪk/ | adj. | 各向异性的 | Exhibiting properties with different values when measured along axes in different directions. | The anisotropic nature of liquid crystals makes them useful in displays. 液晶的各向异性使得它们在显示器中很有用。| Synonym: inhomogeneous, nonuniform | Antonym: isotropic, uniform | adjective
