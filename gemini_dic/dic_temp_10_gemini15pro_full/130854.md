nonapproval:/ˌnɒnəˈpruːvəl/ | /ˌnɑːnəˈpruːvəl/ | n. | 不批准，不认可 | The lack of official nonapproval doesn't mean they condone the activity. 官方的不批准缺乏并不意味着他们纵容这种活动。 | The project met with nonapproval from the board.  这个项目未得到董事会的批准。 | 近义词：disapproval, rejection, veto | 反义词：approval, acceptance, sanction | 词性: 名词

词源: non- + approval.  "non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "approval" comes from the Old French "aprover" meaning "to prove, approve."
