dirtier:/ˈdɜːrtiər (US), ˈdɜːtɪər (UK)| adj.|更脏的，更下流的|Comparative form of "dirty," meaning more dirty or more indecent.|The playground is dirtier after the rain.  雨后操场更脏了。|The comedian's jokes got dirtier as the night went on.  随着夜色渐深，喜剧演员的笑话越来越下流。|近义词：filthier, grubbier, messier|反义词:cleaner, purer|词性:形容词

词源: From Old English *dyriht "foul, unclean," from Proto-Germanic *drutiz.  The comparative form "-ier" comes from Old English "-ra," "-re."
