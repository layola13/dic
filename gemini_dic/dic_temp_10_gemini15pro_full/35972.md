codeines:/ˈkoʊdiːnz/ (US), /ˈkəʊdiːnz/ (UK)| n.|可待因（复数）|Plural form of codeine, referring to multiple doses or types of codeine.|The doctor warned against taking too many codeines. 医生警告不要服用过多的可待因。|Synonyms: opiates, narcotics, analgesics|Antonyms: stimulants|noun
