danced:/dɑːnst/ | v. | 跳舞（dance的过去式和过去分词） | Past tense and past participle of "dance". | They danced all night. 他们整晚都在跳舞。|  She danced with joy when she heard the news. 她听到这个消息高兴得跳了起来。 | 近义词:jigged, pranced | 反义词:stood still| verb

词源:
From Middle English *dauncen, from Old French dancier, of uncertain Germanic origin.
