darings:/ˈdeərɪŋz/
n.
大胆的行为；冒险的事情
Acts of courage or boldness; risky undertakings.
She recounted her wartime darings. 她讲述了她战时的冒险经历。
近义词: adventures, exploits, risks, ventures
反义词: cautions, hesitations, timidities
词性: 名词

词源:  From the verb "dare," from Old English *durran, from Proto-Germanic *durzan, meaning "to dare."  The "-ing" suffix forms a gerund or present participle, and the "-s" indicates plural.
