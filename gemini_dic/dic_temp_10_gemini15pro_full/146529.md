overgird:/ˌoʊvərˈɡɜːrd/| vt.|围绕；环绕；包围|To encircle or surround, especially with something that binds or confines.|例句：The giant sequoia was overgird with a measuring tape.  巨大的红杉树被卷尺环绕。

例句：The city was overgird by a high wall. 这座城市被高墙环绕。|近义词：encircle, surround, encompass, enclose|反义词: expose, uncover|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->

From over- + gird.  "Over-" signifies "above" or "completely," while "gird" means "to encircle or bind."  The combination creates the meaning of completely encircling or surrounding something.
