cinematographer: /ˌsɪnəməˈtɒɡrəfər/ (British) /ˌsɪnəməˈtɑːɡrəfər/ (American)| n. |电影摄影师，摄影指导|A person responsible for the art and technique of film photography, including lighting, camera angles, and shot composition.  They collaborate closely with the director to achieve the desired visual aesthetic of the film.|例句：Roger Deakins is a highly acclaimed cinematographer. 罗杰·狄金斯是一位备受赞誉的电影摄影师。

例句：The cinematographer used a wide-angle lens to capture the vast landscape. 摄影师用广角镜头捕捉了广阔的景色。|近义词：director of photography (DP), cameraman|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
