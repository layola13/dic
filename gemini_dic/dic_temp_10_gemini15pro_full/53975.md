discrepant:/dɪˈskrepənt/ | adj. | 差异的，不一致的，相差的 | Differing, inconsistent, or disagreeing. |  The witness's testimony was discrepant with the physical evidence.  证人的证词与物证不符。

The two reports gave discrepant accounts of the accident.  两份报告对事故的描述不一致。

| 近义词：different, divergent, conflicting, inconsistent, incompatible | 反义词：consistent, concordant, agreeing, similar, identical | 词性: adjective
