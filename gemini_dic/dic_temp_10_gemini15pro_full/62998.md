enormous:/ɪˈnɔːrməs/| adj.|巨大的，庞大的|extremely large in size, extent, or amount|The elephant was enormous. 这头大象体型庞大。|The castle is enormous and dominates the town. 这座城堡巨大无比，俯瞰着整个城镇。|近义词：huge, immense, gigantic, vast, massive|反义词：tiny, small, minute, minuscule|词性：形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from Latin enormis ‘out of rule, irregular, immense’, from e- (variant of ex-) ‘out of’ + norma ‘rule’. The original sense ‘deviating from the norm’ is now obsolete; the current sense dates from the mid 17th century.
