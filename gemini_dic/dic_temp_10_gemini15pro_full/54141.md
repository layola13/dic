disengaged:/dɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒd/
adjective
脱离的，解脱的，不感兴趣的
separated, released, uninterested
The clutch is disengaged when you press the pedal. 你踩下踏板时，离合器就分离了。
He seemed emotionally disengaged from his family. 他似乎在情感上与家人疏离了。
近义词：detached, aloof, indifferent, uninvolved, withdrawn
反义词：engaged, involved, interested, connected
