graveolent: /ˈɡreɪvəʊlənt/ (UK) /ˈɡreɪvələnt/ (US)
adjective
有强烈气味的；（尤指）难闻的
Having a strong smell, especially one that is unpleasant.
The graveolent odor of the durian filled the market.  榴莲浓烈的气味弥漫着整个市场。
The locker room emitted a graveolent aroma after the game.  比赛结束后，更衣室里散发出一股难闻的气味。
近义词: fetid, noxious, putrid, rank, stinking
反义词: fragrant, aromatic, sweet-smelling
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *graveolens, from gravis (“heavy”) + olens (“smelling”).
