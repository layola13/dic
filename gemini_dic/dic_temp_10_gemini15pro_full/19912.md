bio-gas:/ˈbaɪoʊɡæs/| n. |沼气|A mixture of gases, primarily methane and carbon dioxide, produced by the anaerobic decomposition of organic matter, such as manure or sewage.  It can be used as a fuel.| Bio-gas is a renewable energy source. 沼气是一种可再生能源。| Bio-gas can be used to generate electricity. 沼气可以用来发电。|Synonyms: methane, biogas, landfill gas|Antonyms: natural gas (somewhat)|noun
