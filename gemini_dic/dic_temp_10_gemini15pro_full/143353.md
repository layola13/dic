outfables:/ˈaʊtˌfeɪblz/
|n.|杜撰的故事; 夸张的故事；胜过寓言的故事|Stories that surpass fables in their imaginative or incredible qualities.  These stories are often exaggerated or entirely fictional.|The storyteller spun outfables about a land where the rivers flowed with chocolate and the mountains were made of marshmallows.|近义词：tall tales, myths, legends, fabrications|反义词：truths, facts, realities|词性:名词
