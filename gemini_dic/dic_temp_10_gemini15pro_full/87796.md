hibiscuses:/hɪˈbɪskəsiz/ /haɪˈbɪskəsiz/
n.
芙蓉花；木槿花
A shrub or small tree cultivated in warm climates for its large, showy flowers.  Hibiscus flowers are often used in teas and as decorations.
The hibiscuses in the garden were in full bloom. 花园里的芙蓉花盛开了。
synonyms: hibiscus, rose mallow
antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a flower. Could consider antonyms for qualities like "showy" - e.g., "inconspicuous," "unobtrusive")
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "hibiscus," borrowed from Greek "hibiskos," denoting a type of mallow.
