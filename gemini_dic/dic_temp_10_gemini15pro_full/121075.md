miscontinuance:/ˌmɪskənˈtɪnjuəns/
noun
中止，中断，停止
The act of discontinuing something; cessation.
The miscontinuance of the treatment led to a relapse. 中止治疗导致病情复发。
近义词:discontinuation, cessation, interruption, suspension
反义词:continuance, continuation, resumption
词性:名词

Etymology:
From mis- + continuance.  "Mis-" is a prefix indicating something wrong, amiss, or bad. "Continuance" comes from the Old French "continuance," meaning continuation.
