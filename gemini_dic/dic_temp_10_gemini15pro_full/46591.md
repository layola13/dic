damas:/ˈdɑːməs/ | n. | ladies, women; checkers (Spanish) |  A Spanish term meaning "ladies," often used in the context of the game of checkers. It can also more generally refer to women. |  Las damas están jugando en el parque. (The ladies are playing in the park.) | Synonyms: mujeres, señoras (Spanish), women, ladies | Antonyms: hombres, caballeros (Spanish), men, gentlemen | Noun

damas:/ˈdæməz/ | n. | damascene, damascened fabric or object (archaic) | A term, mostly archaic now,  referring to items made of or decorated with damascene, a type of inlaid metalwork originating in Damascus. |  The museum displayed a collection of damas. (The museum displayed a collection of damascene objects.)  | Synonyms: damascene work, damascened objects | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym) | Noun
