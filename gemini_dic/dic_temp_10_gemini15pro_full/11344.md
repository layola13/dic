arenas:/əˈriːnəz/
n.
竞技场；舞台；活动场所
a large enclosed area used for sports or entertainment.  A place or scene of activity, debate, or conflict.
The gladiators fought in the arena.  角斗士在竞技场战斗。
Politics is a tough arena. 政坛是一个残酷的竞技场。
stadium, theater, venue
battlefield, domain
noun
