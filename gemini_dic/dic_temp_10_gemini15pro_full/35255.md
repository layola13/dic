clunkiest: /ˈklʌŋkiɪst/ | adj. | 最笨重的，最粗陋的 |  Superlative form of clunky: most clunky.  It describes something that is awkward, heavy, and inefficient in movement or design. |  That's the clunkiest piece of software I've ever used. 那是我用过的最笨重的软件。 | The car's clunkiest feature was its outdated navigation system. 这辆车最笨重的特点是其过时的导航系统。 |  awkward, cumbersome, ungainly, unwieldy | elegant, graceful, streamlined | adjective
