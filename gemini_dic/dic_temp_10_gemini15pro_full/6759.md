analyzes:/ˈænəˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
分析，解析
examines methodically and in detail the constitution or structure of (something, especially information), typically for purposes of explanation and interpretation.
The scientist analyzes the data from the experiment. 科学家分析了实验的数据。
The software analyzes network traffic to identify security threats.  该软件分析网络流量以识别安全威胁。
synonyms: examines, investigates, scrutinizes, assesses, evaluates
antonyms: synthesizes, combines, integrates
词性:动词
