lophobranchii:/ˌlɒfəˈbræŋkiaɪ/ | n. |海龙鱼目|An order of bony fish that includes seahorses, pipefish, and seadragons, characterized by their elongated bodies, fused jaws forming a tube-like snout, and bony plates or rings covering their skin.  They are typically found in shallow coastal waters and are known for their unique reproductive strategy where the male carries the eggs in a pouch or on his body. |例句：Lophobranchii are a fascinating group of fish with unusual adaptations. 海龙鱼目是一类具有非凡适应性的迷人鱼类。

例句：The seahorse, a member of the Lophobranchii order, is known for its upright posture. 海马，作为海龙鱼目的一员，以其直立的姿势而闻名。 |近义词：Syngnathiformes (sometimes used synonymously) |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
