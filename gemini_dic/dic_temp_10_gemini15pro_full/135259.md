nonparalyses:/ˌnɒnpəˈrælɪsiːz/
noun
非麻痹; 无瘫痪
Absence of paralysis; the state of not being paralyzed.
The patient showed signs of nonparalyses after the treatment. 治疗后，病人显示出无瘫痪的迹象。
Near Antonyms: paralysis, palsy
Antonyms: paralysis
