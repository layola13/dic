aphanitic:/ˌæfəˈnɪtɪk/| adj.|隐晶质的；非显晶质的|Having crystals too small to be seen with the naked eye.  Relating to or denoting a rock texture in which the crystals are too small to be distinguished with the naked eye.|The aphanitic texture of the basalt indicated rapid cooling.|近义词:fine-grained, microcrystalline|反义词:phaneritic, coarse-grained|词性:形容词
