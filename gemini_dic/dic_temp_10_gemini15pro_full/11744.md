aromatics:/ˌærəˈmætɪks/
n.|芳香剂，芳香物质|Substances or plants that have a pleasant and distinctive smell.|The room was filled with the aromatics of baking bread.|近义词:fragrances, scents, perfumes|反义词:malodors, stenches|词性:名词

aromatic:/ˌærəˈmætɪk/
adj.|芳香的|Having a pleasant and distinctive smell.|The aromatic spices filled the kitchen with a warm, inviting scent.|近义词:fragrant, scented, perfumed|反义词:malodorous, stinking|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *aromaticus*, from Greek *arōmatikos* (“fragrant, spicy”), from *arōma* (“spice, fragrant herb”), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂er- (“to fit together, join”).
