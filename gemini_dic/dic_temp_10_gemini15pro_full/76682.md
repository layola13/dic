galvanology:/ˌɡælvəˈnɒlədʒi/ | n. |电疗法；电疗学|The study of the therapeutic application of electricity to the body.  It involves the use of electric currents to treat various medical conditions. |Galvanology was a popular treatment in the 19th century. 电疗法在19世纪是一种流行的疗法。|Synonyms: electrotherapy, electrotherapeutics |Antonyms: None | noun

galvanology:/ˌɡælvəˈnɒlədʒi/ | n. |电流学|The study of the production and effects of electric currents, especially in relation to chemical action. |His research in galvanology led to new discoveries about the properties of metals. 他在电流学方面的研究带来了关于金属特性的新发现。|Synonyms: electrochemistry |Antonyms: None | noun
