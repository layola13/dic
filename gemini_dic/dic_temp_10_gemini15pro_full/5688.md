alumina:/əˈluːmɪnə/| n. |氧化铝|A white crystalline oxide of aluminum, Al₂O₃, occurring naturally as corundum, and widely used in its hydrated form as a mordant, in the production of aluminum, and in the manufacture of abrasives, refractories, ceramics, and electrical insulators.|Aluminum is extracted from alumina. 从氧化铝中提取铝。|近义词:aluminum oxide|反义词: (No direct antonym for a chemical compound)|词性:noun
