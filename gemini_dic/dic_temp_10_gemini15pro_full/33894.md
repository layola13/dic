circumstellar: /ˌsɜːrkəmˈstelər/ (US), /ˌsɜːkəmˈstelə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|环绕恒星的，星周的|Surrounding a star; relating to the region around a star.|例句：Astronomers discovered a circumstellar disk around the young star. 天文学家在这颗年轻的恒星周围发现了一个星周盘。

例句：The circumstellar dust is believed to be the remnants of planet formation. 星周尘埃被认为是行星形成的残余物。|近义词：peristellar|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective

Etymology:  From Latin *circum* ("around") + *stellar* (from *stella* "star").
