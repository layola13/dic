nonprominences:/ˌnɒnˈprɒmɪnənsɪz/
n.
不突出；不显眼之处；低洼处
Lack of prominence; areas or features that are not prominent or noticeable.
The map highlighted the nonprominences in the terrain, showing the valleys and depressions.  地图突出了地形的低洼处，显示了山谷和洼地。
The nonprominences of the lunar surface are often shadowed. 月球表面的低洼处经常被阴影笼罩。
synonyms: depressions, hollows, concavities, indentations
antonyms: prominences, projections, protuberances, elevations
noun


Etymology:
From non- + prominence.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "prominence" comes from the Latin "prominentia," meaning "a projecting part."
