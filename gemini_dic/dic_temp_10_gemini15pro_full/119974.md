millepore:/ˈmɪlɪpɔːr/ /ˈmɪləˌpɔːr/| n.|千孔珊瑚；苔藓虫；|A small marine invertebrate animal that forms colonies and secretes a calcareous skeleton.  一种小型海洋无脊椎动物，形成群落并分泌钙质骨骼。|例句：The millepore colonies were vibrant and colorful. 千孔珊瑚群落充满活力，色彩斑斓。

例句：Millepores are often mistaken for corals. 千孔珊瑚常被误认为是珊瑚。|近义词：bryozoan, coral|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
