conversing:/kənˈvɜːsɪŋ/
v.
交谈，谈话
engaging in conversation.
They were conversing in hushed tones. 他们在低声交谈。
近义词：talking, chatting, discussing, speaking
反义词：silent, mute
词性：动词 (verb)

conversing:/kənˈvɜːrsɪŋ/
adj.
交谈的
related to or engaged in conversation.  (Less common usage)
A conversing group of friends. 一群正在交谈的朋友。
近义词：talking, chatting
反义词：silent
词性：形容词 (adjective - less common)

词源: From Late Latin conversari "to live with, keep company with," from Latin conversus, past participle of convertere "to turn around, turn about, be turned, be changed," from com- "together" (see com-) + vertere "to turn" (from PIE root *wer- (2) "to turn, bend"). Meaning "to hold intercourse, commune, talk familiarly" is from 1590s.
