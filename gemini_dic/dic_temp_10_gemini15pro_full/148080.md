oversaw:/ˌoʊvərˈsɔː/ | v. |监督，监管|To watch over and direct; to supervise.|例句：The manager oversaw the project from beginning to end. 经理从头到尾监督了这个项目。

例句：She oversaw the training of the new recruits. 她监督新员工的培训。


近义词：supervised, monitored, directed, managed|反义词: neglected, ignored |词性:动词
