discoid:/ˈdɪskɔɪd/| adj. n.|盘状的；盘状体|Shaped like a disc; a disc-shaped object.|

例句：
The doctor noticed a discoid rash on the patient's arm.
医生注意到病人手臂上有一块盘状的皮疹。

The fossilized remains were discoid in shape, suggesting they might be from an ancient sea creature.
这些化石遗骸呈盘状，表明它们可能来自一种古老的海洋生物。


近义词：disc-shaped, discoidal|
反义词:spherical, globular|
词性:adjective, noun|

近义词：disc, disk|

ETYMOLOGY->
