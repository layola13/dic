hypersensuously:/ˌhaɪpərˈsɛnsjuəsli/
adverb
过度敏感地;感觉过分敏锐地
in a hypersensuous manner; with excessive sensitivity.
She reacted hypersensuously to the slightest touch. 她对最轻微的触碰都反应过度敏感。
近义词:overly sensitively, excessively sensitively
反义词:insensitively, numbly
词性:adverb

hypersensitive:/ˌhaɪpərˈsɛnsɪtɪv/
adjective
过度敏感的
excessively sensitive; abnormally susceptible to something.
He is hypersensitive to criticism. 他对批评过度敏感。
近义词:oversensitive, touchy
反义词:insensitive, indifferent
词性:adjective

hypersensitivity:/ˌhaɪpərˌsɛnsɪˈtɪvɪti/
noun
过度敏感
excessive sensitivity.
Her hypersensitivity to noise made city life difficult. 她对噪音的过度敏感让城市生活变得困难。
近义词:oversensitivity, touchiness
反义词:insensitivity, indifference
词性:noun


词源:
From hyper- + sensuous.  "Hyper-" is a prefix meaning "over, excessive," derived from Greek. "Sensuous" relates to the senses, derived from Late Latin "sensus."
