fragmentist:/ˈfræɡməntɪst/| n.|碎片主义者；分裂主义者|A person who supports fragmentation, especially political fragmentation, or who focuses on small details or parts rather than the whole.|The fragmentist's narrow focus on individual issues prevented him from seeing the bigger picture.|近义词:separatist, schismatic, splitter, sectarian|反义词:unifier, integrator, centralist|词性:noun

Etymology:
From fragment + -ist.  "Fragment" comes from the Latin "fragmentum,"  meaning "a piece broken off."
