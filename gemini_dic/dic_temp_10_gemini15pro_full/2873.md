advertising:/ˈædvərtaɪzɪŋ/
noun
广告; 广告活动; 广告业
The process or business of promoting products or services.
The company launched a new advertising campaign.  这家公司发起了一场新的广告活动。

Many people find online advertising intrusive.  许多人觉得网络广告很烦扰。

近义词:promotion, publicity, marketing
反义词:n/a
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From advertise + -ing.  Ultimately from Latin advertere "to turn towards".

USAGE->
Present Simple:
The company is advertising its new product on TV. = 这家公司正在电视上宣传它的新产品。
She advertises her services online. = 她在网上宣传她的服务。

Present Continuous:
They are currently advertising for a new marketing manager. = 他们目前正在招聘一位新的市场营销经理。
We are advertising our products through social media. = 我们正在通过社交媒体宣传我们的产品。

Past Simple:
They advertised the job vacancy in the local newspaper. = 他们在当地报纸上刊登了招聘广告。
The company advertised its products extensively last year. = 这家公司去年对其产品进行了广泛的宣传。

Past Continuous:

Present Perfect:
