flytrap:/ˈflaɪˌtræp/| n. |捕蝇草；捕蝇器|A plant that traps insects, especially flies, by closing its leaves quickly when they land on it. |例句：The Venus flytrap is a carnivorous plant.  捕蝇草是一种食肉植物。

例句：He set up a flytrap in the kitchen. 他在厨房里放了一个捕蝇器。|近义词：Venus flytrap, insect trap|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
