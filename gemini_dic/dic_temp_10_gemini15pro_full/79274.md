glauberite:/ˈɡlɔːbəˌraɪt/| n.|芒硝|A colorless or pale yellow monoclinic mineral consisting of a sulfate of sodium and calcium.  It is typically found in evaporite deposits.|Glauberite is a common mineral in salt lakes.|近义词：bloedite, thenardite|反义词: N/A|名词
