expressionists:/ɪkˈspreʃənɪsts/
n.
表现主义者
artists, writers, or musicians who practice expressionism
例句：The Expressionists sought to convey the intense emotions of the human experience. 表现主义者试图传达人类经验的强烈情感。

例句：Many early 20th-century German artists were Expressionists. 许多20世纪早期的德国艺术家都是表现主义者。
近义词：modernists, avant-gardists
反义词：traditionalists, classicists
词性：noun
