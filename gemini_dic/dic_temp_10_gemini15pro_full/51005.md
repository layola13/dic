desisting:/dɪˈzɪstɪŋ/
v.
停止，中止
ceasing to do something
The company is desisting from the practice of animal testing.  这家公司正在停止动物试验的做法。
He desisted from further argument. 他停止了进一步的争论。

近义词: ceasing, stopping, abstaining, forbearing
反义词: continuing, persisting, persevering
词性: 动词 (present participle)
