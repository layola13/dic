homaloid:/ˈhoʊməlɔɪd/| adj.|类平面的，近似平面的|Resembling a plane; approximately flat.  Relating to or being a homaloidal surface or system. |The insect's wings had a homaloid appearance.  昆虫的翅膀看起来近似平面。|Near-plane, planiform|Curved, uneven|adjective
Etymology-> From Greek *ὁμαλός (homalós, “even, level”) + *-ειδής (-eidḗs, “-like, resembling”).
