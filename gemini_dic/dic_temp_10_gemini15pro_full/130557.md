nonacquisitively:/ˌnɒnækˈwɪzɪtɪvli/
adverb
非获取性地；不求获取地
In a way that does not involve acquiring something.
She lived nonacquisitively, valuing experiences over possessions. 她生活简朴，重视体验而非物质 possessions.
近义词:  unpossessively, simply, austerely
反义词: acquisitively, greedily, materialistically
词性: adverb

nonacquisitive:/ˌnɒnækˈwɪzɪtɪv/
adjective
非获取性的；不求获取的
Not seeking to acquire things.
He had a nonacquisitive nature, content with what he had. 他生性淡泊，满足于现状.
近义词: unpossessive, unmaterialistic, content
反义词: acquisitive, greedy, materialistic
词性: adjective

词源:
From non- + acquisitive.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "acquisitive" comes from the Latin "acquisitus," past participle of "acquirere," meaning "to acquire."
