ammonia:/əˈmoʊniə/ | n. |氨; 氨水|A colorless pungent gas (NH3) soluble in water, which forms a weakly alkaline solution. |Ammonia is used in many cleaning products. 氨水用于许多清洁产品中。|synonyms: azane, volatile alkali |antonyms: N/A |noun
