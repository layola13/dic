illimitable:/ɪˈlɪmɪtəbl/ | adj. | 无限的，无边无际的 | Without limits; boundless. |  Her energy seemed illimitable. 她似乎有着无限的精力。 |  The universe appears illimitable. 宇宙看起来无边无际。 | 近义词: boundless, infinite, immeasurable, vast, endless | 反义词: limited, finite, bounded, restricted | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + limit (boundary) + -able (capable of being).  Ultimately from Latin *limit- (boundary, threshold).
