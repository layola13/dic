legitimized: /lɪˈdʒɪtɪmaɪzd/
verb
使...合法化；使...正统化
to make something legal or acceptable
The government legitimized the use of force. 政府使武力的使用合法化。
synonyms: validated, justified, sanctioned, authorized
antonyms: invalidated, prohibited, banned
词性: 动词


legitimate: /lɪˈdʒɪtɪmət/
adjective
合法的；正当的；合理的
conforming to the law or to rules
The president is the legitimate ruler of the country. 总统是国家的合法统治者。
synonyms: lawful, legal, valid, justifiable
antonyms: illegitimate, illegal, invalid, unlawful
词性: 形容词


legitimacy: /lɪˈdʒɪtɪməsi/
noun
合法性；正当性；合理性
the quality or state of being legitimate
The legitimacy of the election results is being questioned. 选举结果的合法性受到了质疑。
synonyms: lawfulness, legality, validity, rightfulness
antonyms: illegitimacy, illegality, invalidity
词性: 名词


词源:
From Late Latin legitimatus, past participle of legitimare "to make lawful," from Latin legitimus "lawful," from lex (genitive legis) "law."
