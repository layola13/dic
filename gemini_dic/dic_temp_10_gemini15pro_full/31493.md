charmingest:/ˈtʃɑːrmɪŋɪst/
adjective
最迷人的，最有魅力的
Superlative form of charming: most charming.  While grammatically correct, it's considered archaic or nonstandard and "most charming" is generally preferred.
例句：She had the charmingest smile I'd ever seen. 她有着我见过的最迷人的笑容。
例句：He told the charmingest stories of his travels. 他讲述了他旅行中最迷人的故事。

近义词：most charming, most delightful, most appealing, most captivating
反义词：least charming, most unpleasant, most repulsive, most repellent
词性：adjective
