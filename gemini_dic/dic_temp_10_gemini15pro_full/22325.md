bonneted:/ˈbɒnɪtɪd/ /ˈbɑːnɪtɪd/
adjective
戴着帽子的；头巾包裹的
wearing a bonnet; covered with a bonnet.
The bonneted woman walked down the street. 戴着帽子的女人走在街上。
The child was bonneted and ready to go outside. 孩子戴上了帽子，准备出门。
Synonyms: hooded, capped, covered
Antonyms: bareheaded, uncovered
词性: adjective
