out-cue: /ˈaʊtˌkjuː/
noun
提示演员退场的信号; 出场提示
A signal to a performer to leave the stage or to a technician to change a scene or lighting.
The stage manager gave the actor his out-cue.  舞台监督给了演员退场的提示。
The out-cue for the lighting change was a line of dialogue.  灯光变化的提示是一句台词。
synonyms: exit cue, cue out
antonyms: entrance cue, cue in
n.
