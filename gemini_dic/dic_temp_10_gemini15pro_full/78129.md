geometers:/dʒiˈɒmɪtəz/ | n. |几何学家|A person skilled in geometry, the branch of mathematics concerned with the properties and relations of points, lines, surfaces, solids, and higher dimensional analogs.|例句：The geometers of ancient Greece made significant contributions to mathematics. 古希腊的几何学家对数学做出了重大贡献。

例句：Modern geometers explore complex shapes and spaces beyond our everyday experience. 现代几何学家探索超越我们日常经验的复杂形状和空间。|近义词：mathematicians, |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
