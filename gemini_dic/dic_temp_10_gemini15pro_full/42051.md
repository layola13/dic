cosy:/ˈkoʊzi/ (US) /ˈkəʊzi/ (UK)| adj.|舒适的，温暖的，惬意的|Giving a feeling of comfort, warmth, and relaxation.|例句：We spent a cosy evening by the fire. 我们在火炉边度过了一个舒适的夜晚。

例句：The room was small and cosy. 房间小而舒适。

近义词：comfortable, snug, warm, homely|反义词：uncomfortable, cold, austere|词性：adjective


cozier (comparative) /ˈkoʊziər/ (US) /ˈkəʊziər/ (UK) | adj.|更舒适的|More cosy.|例句：The new armchair is even cozier than the old one.  这把新扶手椅比旧的更舒服。

例句：Let's make the living room cozier with some blankets and pillows.  让我们用一些毯子和枕头让客厅更舒适。

近义词：more comfortable, snugger, warmer|反义词：more uncomfortable, colder|词性：adjective


cosiest (superlative) /ˈkoʊziest/ (US) /ˈkəʊziest/ (UK) | adj.|最舒适的|Most cosy.|例句：This is the cosiest corner in the whole house. 这是整个房子里最舒适的角落。

例句：The attic bedroom was the cosiest place to be on a cold winter night. 阁楼卧室是寒冷冬夜里最舒适的地方。

近义词：most comfortable, snuggest, warmest|反义词：most uncomfortable, coldest|词性：adjective


cosily /ˈkoʊzɪli/ (US) /ˈkəʊzɪli/ (UK)| adv.|舒适地，温暖地，惬意地|In a cosy manner.|例句：They sat cosily by the fire, reading books. 他们舒适地坐在火炉旁看书。

例句：The cat curled up cosily on the rug. 猫舒适地蜷缩在地毯上。

近义词：comfortably, snugly, warmly|反义词：uncomfortably, coldly|词性：adverb


cosiness /ˈkoʊzinəs/ (US) /ˈkəʊzinɪs/ (UK)| n.|舒适，温暖，惬意|The state of being cosy.|例句：The cosiness of the room made me feel sleepy. 房间的舒适让我感到困倦。

例句：I love the cosiness of a log fire in winter. 我喜欢冬天壁炉的温暖舒适。

近义词：comfort, snugness, warmth|反义词：discomfort, coldness|词性：noun
