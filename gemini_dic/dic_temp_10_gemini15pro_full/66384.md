execrate:/ˈɛksɪkreɪt/| v. |痛斥，憎恶，咒骂|To express great loathing for; denounce.|例句：He execrated the politicians who had led the country into war. 他痛斥将国家拖入战争的政客们。

例句：She execrated the cruel treatment of animals. 她憎恶虐待动物的行为。|近义词：denounce, condemn, abhor, detest, loathe|反义词：praise, admire, commend, adore|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *exsecrārī*, from *ex* ("out") + *sacrāre* ("to devote to a deity, to curse").  Originally meant "to curse by devoting to evil spirits."
