hijacked:/ˌhaɪˈdʒækt/ | v. |劫持，绑架|To seize or take control of (a vehicle, aircraft, or ship) by force. |The plane was hijacked by terrorists. 这架飞机被恐怖分子劫持了。|Synonyms: seize, commandeer, usurp|Antonyms: release, surrender|verb

hijacked:/ˌhaɪˈdʒækt/ | adj. |被劫持的|Having been seized or taken control of by force. |The hijacked plane landed in a different country. 被劫持的飞机降落在另一个国家。|Synonyms: seized, captured, commandeered|Antonyms: liberated, freed|adjective
