buoyed:/ˈbɔɪd/ | v. |使浮起，使振奋，支撑 |lifted, supported, or encouraged|  The team was buoyed by their recent win.  该队因最近的胜利而受到鼓舞。| 近义词: encouraged, uplifted, heartened, sustained, supported | 反义词: discouraged, depressed, disheartened, sunk |词性: 动词

buoy:/ˈbuːi/ | n. |浮标，救生圈 | a floating object anchored in water to mark a location| The ship steered clear of the buoy. 船避开了浮标。| 近义词: float, marker, beacon | 反义词: anchor, weight |词性: 名词
