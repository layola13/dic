draughtiness:/ˈdrɑːftɪnɪs/
n.
通风；有穿堂风；冷飕飕
The state or quality of being draughty; exposure to draughts of air.
The draughtiness of the old house made it difficult to heat.  这栋老房子的通风性使它很难被加热。
The draughtiness of the room made her shiver.  房间里的冷风让她打了个寒颤。

近义词: airiness, chilliness, coldness
反义词: cosiness, snugness, warmth
词性: 名词


draughty:/ˈdrɑːftɪ/
adj.
有穿堂风的；通风的
Exposed to draughts of air; letting in draughts.
The draughty windows made the room cold.  通风的窗户使房间很冷。
It was a draughty old house, full of creaking floorboards.  这是一栋通风的老房子，到处都是吱吱作响的地板。
近义词: airy, breezy, chilly
反义词: airtight, cosy, snug
词性: 形容词
