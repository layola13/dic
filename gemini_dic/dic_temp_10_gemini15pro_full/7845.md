antarctica:/ænˈtɑːrktɪkə/ | n. | 南极洲 | A continent surrounding the South Pole, almost entirely covered by ice and snow. |  Antarctica is the coldest, driest, and windiest continent. 南极洲是地球上最冷、最干燥、风最大的大陆。

Antarctica is home to penguins and seals. 南极洲是企鹅和海豹的家园。 | synonym: South Pole | antonym: Arctic | noun
