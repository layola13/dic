miffed:/mɪft/| adj.|恼怒的，生气的|Slightly annoyed or irritated. |例句：He was miffed that she didn't call him back. 他因为她没回电话而生气。

例句：She was miffed about not getting the promotion. 她因为没有得到晋升而恼火。

近义词：annoyed, irritated, irked, peeved, disgruntled|反义词：pleased, happy, delighted|词性：形容词
