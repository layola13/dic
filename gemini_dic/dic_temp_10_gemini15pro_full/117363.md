mediate:/ˈmiːdieɪt/| v. adj.|调解，斡旋；居间的|To act as an intermediary between two or more parties to help them reach an agreement or resolve a dispute.  Also, situated between two extremes. |The UN attempted to mediate between the warring factions. 联合国试图在交战各派之间进行调解。|Synonyms: arbitrate, intercede, negotiate, reconcile, intermediate |Antonyms: agitate, instigate, worsen|verb

mediate:/ˈmiːdieɪt/| adj.|居间的，间接的|Situated between two extremes or acting through an intermediary. |The hippocampus plays a mediate role in memory formation. 海马体在记忆形成中起着中介作用。|Synonyms: intermediary, indirect, intervening |Antonyms: direct, immediate|adjective
