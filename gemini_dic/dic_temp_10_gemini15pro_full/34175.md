ETYMOLOGY->
From clam + -y.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *kleimaz ("clay"). Related to Dutch klem ("sticky"), German kleben ("to stick, adhere"). The sense evolution likely progressed from "sticky" to "cold and sticky" to "damp and unpleasant".
