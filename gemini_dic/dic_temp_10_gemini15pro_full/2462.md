adjustable:/əˈdʒʌstəbl/| adj.|可调节的，可调整的|Able to be changed to suit particular needs or conditions.|The height of the steering wheel is adjustable. 方向盘的高度是可以调节的.

The straps on this backpack are adjustable.  这个背包的带子是可以调节的.|近义词:adaptable, flexible, modifiable|反义词:fixed, rigid, unchangeable|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From adjust + -able.  "Adjust" comes from Old French "ajuster"  meaning "to arrange, put in order".  "-able" is a suffix meaning "capable of being".  Therefore, adjustable literally means "capable of being adjusted".
USAGE->
The adjustable wrench is a handy tool.  可调节扳手是一个方便的工具。

This chair has an adjustable backrest. 这把椅子有一个可调节的靠背。
