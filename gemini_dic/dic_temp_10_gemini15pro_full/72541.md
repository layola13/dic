fluted:/ˈfluːtɪd/| adj.|有凹槽的，有笛声的|Having vertical grooves or channels, resembling the flutes of a column or the sound of a flute.|The fluted columns gave the building a classical look.  这些有凹槽的柱子赋予了建筑物古典的外观。|The fluted notes of the bird filled the air. 鸟儿笛子般的鸣叫声充满了空气。|近义词：grooved, channeled, ribbed|反义词:smooth, plain|词性:形容词

词源: From flute + -ed.  Ultimately from Old French flaüte and Latin flatus (“a blowing”).
