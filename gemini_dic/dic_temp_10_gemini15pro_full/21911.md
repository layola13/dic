boer:/ˈbuːər/ (US) /ˈbɔːr/ (UK)| n.|农夫，农民（尤指南非的荷兰裔农民）|A farmer, especially a Dutch farmer in South Africa. |例句：The boer worked hard on his land.  这个布尔人辛勤地耕作着他的土地。

例句：Many boers migrated north during the Great Trek.  在大迁徙期间，许多布尔人向北迁移。

近义词：farmer, peasant, husbandman|反义词:city-dweller|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Dutch *boer* "farmer," from Middle Dutch *boer*, from Old Dutch *bur*, from Proto-Germanic *buraz* "dweller, inhabitant," from Proto-Indo-European *(s)bʰuH- "to dwell." Cognate with German *Bauer* "farmer, peasant."
