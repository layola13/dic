optimizer:/ˈɒptɪmaɪzə(r)/ (US) /ˈɑːptɪmaɪzər/ (UK)| n.|优化器，优化程序|A person or thing that optimizes.  A program or algorithm designed to improve the efficiency or effectiveness of a system, process, or design. |The optimizer improved the database query performance significantly. 优化器显著提高了数据库查询性能。|Synonyms: improver, enhancer, perfector, streamliner|Antonyms: degrader, impairer, complicator|Noun
