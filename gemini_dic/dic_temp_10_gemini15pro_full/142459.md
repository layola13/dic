oscinine:/ˈɑːsɪnaɪn/ /ˈɒsɪnaɪn/
adjective
鸣禽的；属于鸣禽亚目的
Relating to the oscines; songbirds.  A large suborder (Oscines) of passerine birds (order Passeriformes) characterized by complex vocal muscles and syrinx.
例句：The oscinine birds are known for their complex songs. 鸣禽以其复杂的鸣叫而闻名。

例句：Oscinine vocalizations are often used in territorial displays. 鸣禽的鸣叫常用于领地展示。

近义词：passerine
反义词：(No specific antonym exists, but one could use "non-oscine" to refer to birds outside this suborder.)
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *oscen, from os(cinis) (“singing bird”)  + -ine (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately related to the verb canō (“to sing”).
