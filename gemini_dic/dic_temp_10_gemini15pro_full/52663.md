digitizations:/ˌdɪdʒɪtɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n. |数字化，数字化过程，数字化产物|The process of converting analog information into digital form.  The products of digitizing something.|例句：The library's digitizations of its rare books made them accessible to a wider audience.  图书馆将其珍稀书籍数字化，使更多读者可以阅读它们。

例句：Recent digitizations have improved access to historical archives.  近期的数字化工作改善了对历史档案的访问。|近义词：digitalization, digital conversions, encoding, scanning|反义词:analogization|词性:名词
