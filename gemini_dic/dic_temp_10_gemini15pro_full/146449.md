overfreighted:/ˌoʊvərˈfreɪtɪd/
adjective
过度装载的；货物过多的
carrying too much freight; overloaded.
The ship was overfreighted and sank in the storm. 这艘船货物装载过多，在暴风雨中沉没了。
overloaded, overburdened
underloaded, lightly loaded
adjective
