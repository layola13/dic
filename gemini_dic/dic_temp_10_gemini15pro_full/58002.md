drives:/draɪvz/| v. (third person singular simple present)|驾驶，驱使，迫使|To operate and control the movement of a vehicle or animal; to compel or urge somebody to do something; to cause something to happen.|He drives a truck. 他驾驶一辆卡车。|The hunger drives him to steal. 饥饿迫使他偷窃。|近义词：operates, controls, propels, compels, forces|反义词：stops, halts, follows|词性:动词

drives:/draɪvz/| n. (plural)|驱车旅行，私人车道|A journey in a car; a private road or driveway.|We took several scenic drives during our vacation. 我们在假期中进行了几次风景优美的驾车旅行。|The house has a long, winding drive. 这房子有一条长长的蜿蜒的私人车道。|近义词：journeys, trips, roads, paths|反义词：walks, hikes|词性:名词
