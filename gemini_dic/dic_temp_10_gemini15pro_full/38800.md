conductorships:/kənˈdʌktərʃɪps/
n.
指挥的职位; 乐队指挥的职位
The position or tenure of a conductor, especially of an orchestra or choir.
He held several prestigious conductorships during his career.  他职业生涯中担任过几个享有盛誉的指挥职位。
synonyms: directorship, leadership, management, control
antonyms: subordination, followership
