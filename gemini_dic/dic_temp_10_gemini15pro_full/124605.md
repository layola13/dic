mousey:/ˈmaʊsi/ | adj. | 胆小的，羞怯的；（颜色）灰褐色的，鼠灰色的 | resembling or characteristic of a mouse, especially in being timid or quiet; of a drab greyish brown colour | She has a mousey personality and rarely speaks up in meetings. 她性格胆小，在会议上很少发言。 The walls were painted a dull, mousey brown. 墙壁被漆成了单调的灰褐色。| 近义词: timid, shy, meek, mousy, drab, dull | 反义词: bold, confident, bright, vibrant | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From mouse + -y.  Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European *mus-, meaning "mouse."
