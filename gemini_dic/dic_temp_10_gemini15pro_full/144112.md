outscoop:/ˌaʊtˈskuːp/
verb
抢先报道；比…先得到独家新闻
To get a news story before a competitor.
The local newspaper outscooped the national media on the story. 当地报纸比全国媒体抢先报道了这则新闻。
synonyms: beat, best, outdo, surpass, trump
antonyms: be scooped, lose
词性: 动词

outscoop:/ˌaʊtˈskuːp/
noun
抢先报道；独家新闻
An instance of getting a news story before a competitor.
Getting the outscoop on the mayor's resignation was a major win for the small-town paper. 小镇报纸抢先报道了市长辞职的消息，这是一个重大胜利。
synonyms: scoop, exclusive
antonyms:  N/A
词性: 名词
