bibliophilies: /ˌbɪbliəˈfɪliz/
| n. |藏书癖；爱书癖；书籍收藏爱好 |A great love of books; bibliophilism.  | He indulged his bibliophilies by collecting first editions. 他通过收集初版书籍来满足他的藏书癖。 | 近义词: bibliophilism, book-collecting | 反义词: bibliophobia | 词性: 名词

词源:
From French *bibliophilie*, from biblio- + -philie (from Greek *philía* “love”).
