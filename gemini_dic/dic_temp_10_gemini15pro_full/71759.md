flea-pit:/ˈfliːpɪt/| n.|跳蚤窝；廉价、肮脏的场所（尤指电影院或剧院）|A run-down and often dirty place, especially a cinema or theatre.  It evokes a sense of shabbiness and low quality.|The old cinema was a real flea-pit.|Synonyms: dive, dump, hole, rathole|Antonyms: palace, luxury cinema, high-class theatre|noun
