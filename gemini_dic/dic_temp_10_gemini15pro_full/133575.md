nonflakiest: /nɒnˈfleɪkiɪst/ | adj. | 最可靠的，最不薄片的 |  Superlative form of "nonflaky," meaning least likely to flake or be unreliable.  It describes someone or something as being extremely dependable and consistent. |  She's the nonflakiest friend I have; she always shows up when she says she will. 她是我最可靠的朋友；她说她会出现就一定会出现。|  He's known for being a nonflakiest employee, always meeting deadlines and delivering quality work.  他以是最可靠的员工而闻名，总是能按时完成任务并交付高质量的工作。 | Near Synonyms:  reliable, dependable, consistent, steadfast, steady | Antonyms: flaky, unreliable, undependable, inconsistent, erratic | adjective
