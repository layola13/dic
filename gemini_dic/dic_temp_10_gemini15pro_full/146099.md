overelaborated: /ˌoʊvərɪˈlæbəreɪtɪd/
adjective
过度阐述的；过于精细的
Having been elaborated or explained in excessive detail; too complicated or fussy.
The plot of the movie was overelaborated and confusing. 这部电影的情节过于复杂，令人困惑。
Her costume was overelaborated and ostentatious. 她的服装过于精细，显得炫耀。
Synonyms: overdone, overwrought, fussy, convoluted, complex
Antonyms: simple, concise, straightforward
词性: 形容词
