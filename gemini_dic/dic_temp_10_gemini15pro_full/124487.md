mouldier:/ˈmoʊldiər (US) / ˈməʊldiə(r) (UK)| adj.|更发霉的|Comparative form of mouldy: more covered with mould. |The bread in the back of the cupboard was mouldier than the bread in the front.  橱柜后面的面包比前面的面包更发霉了。

The basement was much mouldier after the flood. 洪水过后，地下室更加发霉了。|近义词：mustier, fustiest, damper, danker|反义词：fresher, cleaner|词性：形容词

mouldy:/ˈmoʊldi (US) / ˈməʊldi (UK)| adj.|发霉的|Covered with or smelling of mould. |The cheese had gone mouldy.  奶酪发霉了。

The old books in the attic smelled mouldy. 阁楼里的旧书闻起来有霉味。|近义词：musty, fusty, damp, dank|反义词：fresh, clean|词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mowledy, *moldy, from Old English *molig, *moligeac, from mold (“mould”) +‎ -ig.  Cognate with Dutch muf (“musty”).
