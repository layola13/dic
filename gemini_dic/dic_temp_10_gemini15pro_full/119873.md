miliolite:/ˈmɪlioʊlaɪt/| n. |鲕状岩，粟粒岩|A kind of limestone formed by the accumulation of miliolid foraminifera tests, often used as a building stone.|The ancient city walls were constructed from miliolite, a durable and locally sourced material.  古代城墙是用鲕状岩建造的，这是一种耐用且当地采购的材料。|Near synonyms:  oolite, roe stone|Antonyms: None|Noun


miliolite:/ˌmɪliəˈlaɪtɪk/| adj. |鲕状的，由鲕粒组成的|Composed of or resembling miliolite.|The miliolitic limestone gave the building a distinctive texture. 鲕状灰岩赋予建筑独特的质感。|Near synonyms: oolitic|Antonyms: None|Adjective
