nonclassic:/nɒnˈklæsɪk/ | adj. | 非经典的，非传统的 | Not conforming to the traditional or established style or form; not classical. |  Example: The museum's exhibit showcased nonclassic art forms.  博物馆的展览展示了非经典艺术形式。 | Example:  His nonclassic approach to management surprised everyone. 他非传统的管理方法让每个人都感到惊讶。 | Synonyms: unconventional, unorthodox, modern, contemporary, avant-garde | Antonyms: classic, traditional, conventional, orthodox | Adjective
