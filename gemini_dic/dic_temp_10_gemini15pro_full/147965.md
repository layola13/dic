overresponded:/ˌoʊvərɪˈspɑːndɪd/ | v. |反应过度；过度回应| To react or respond to a stimulus or situation excessively or disproportionately.| The immune system overresponded to the allergen, causing anaphylaxis. 免疫系统对过敏原反应过度，导致过敏性休克.|overreacted, overcompensated| underreacted|verb
overresponded:/ˌoʊvərɪˈspɑːndɪd/ | adj. |（指人或系统）反应过度的| Having reacted or responded to a stimulus or situation excessively or disproportionately.  This usage is less common than the verb form, and often implies a continuing state of overreaction. | His overresponded immune system made him susceptible to various illnesses. 他反应过度的免疫系统使他容易患上各种疾病.|oversensitive, hyperreactive| undersensitive, hyporeactive|adjective
