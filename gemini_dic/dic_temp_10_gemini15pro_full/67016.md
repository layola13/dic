expirer:/ɪkˈspaɪərər/ | n. | 到期者；失效者；期满者 |  Someone or something that expires. One whose term of office or other appointment expires. | The membership card labels each member as an 'expirer' after one year.  会员卡将每个会员在一年后标记为“过期者”。| Synonyms: non-renewer, lapsed member | Antonyms:  renewer, continuing member | noun

expire:/ɪkˈspaɪər/ | v. | 到期；失效；期满；终止；死亡；呼气 | To come to an end; to cease to be valid. To die. To breathe out.  | My passport is due to expire next month. 我的护照下个月到期。| Synonyms: terminate, cease, conclude, finish, perish, die, exhale | Antonyms: renew, begin, commence, live, inhale | verb
