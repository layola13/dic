nudging:/ˈnʌdʒɪŋ/
n.
轻推，推动，鼓励
The act of gently pushing or encouraging someone to do something.
He gave her a gentle nudging to get her attention. 他轻轻推了她一下以引起她的注意。
Synonyms: prodding, prompting, encouraging, urging
Antonyms: discouraging, hindering, obstructing
noun

nudging:/ˈnʌdʒɪŋ/
v.
轻推，推动，鼓励 (现在分词)
Gently pushing or encouraging someone to do something.
She was nudging him to finish his work.  她一直在鼓励他完成工作。
Synonyms: prodding, prompting, encouraging, urging
Antonyms: discouraging, hindering, obstructing
verb (present participle)
