inclin:/ɪnˈklaɪn/ /ɪnˈklɪn/ | v. | 倾向，倾斜；使倾向于 | To have a tendency or disposition towards something; to lean or slope. | She is inclined to agree with you. 她倾向于同意你的观点。

The land inclines towards the river.  这块地向河边倾斜。 | 近义词：tend, lean, slope, dispose | 反义词： straighten, level | 词性: 动词
