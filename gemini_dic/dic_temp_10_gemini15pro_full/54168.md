disentitle:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtaɪtl/
verb
剥夺权利；使无资格
To deprive of a right, privilege, or entitlement.
His criminal record disentitled him from voting. 他的犯罪记录使他失去了投票权。
近义词: disqualify, preclude, bar
反义词: entitle, qualify, authorize
词性: verb
