oxidic:/ɒkˈsɪdɪk/
adjective
氧化的；含氧化物的
Relating to or containing oxides.  Formed from or containing oxygen.
Example 1: The oxidic layer on the metal protected it from further corrosion. 金属表面的氧化层保护它免受进一步腐蚀。
Example 2:  The mineral sample contained several oxidic compounds.  该矿物样本包含几种氧化合物。
近义词: oxidized
反义词: reduced
词性: adjective
