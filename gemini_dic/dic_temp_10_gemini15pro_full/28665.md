carinatae:/kærɪˈneɪtiː/ | n. | 龙骨突类 |A subclass of birds characterized by a prominent keel on the sternum (breastbone), which provides attachment for flight muscles. This group includes most flying birds. |例句：Carinatae includes a vast diversity of bird species, from tiny hummingbirds to large eagles. 龙骨突类包括种类繁多的鸟类，从微小的蜂鸟到大型的鹰。

例句：The strong keel of the carinatae allows for powerful wing strokes essential for flight.  龙骨突类强壮的龙骨可以进行强有力的翅膀拍打，这对飞行至关重要。| 近义词: Neornithes (sometimes used interchangeably), flying birds | 反义词: Ratites (flightless birds) | 词性: Noun
