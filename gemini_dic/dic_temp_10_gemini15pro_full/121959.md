missioner:/ˈmɪʃənər/| n. |传教士；特使|A person sent on a mission, especially one sent to propagate religion or perform charitable work.|例句：The missionaries traveled to remote villages to spread their faith. 传教士们前往偏远村庄传播他们的信仰。

例句：He was appointed as a special missioner to negotiate a peace treaty. 他被任命为特使去谈判和平条约。|近义词：envoy, emissary, delegate, representative, apostle, preacher|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 antagonist 对立者，或 non-believer 非信徒，视语境而定)|词性: 名词
