good:/ɡʊd/| adj.|好的，优秀的，令人满意的|Something that is positive, desirable, or satisfactory.|例句：She's a good student. 她是一个好学生。|例句：The food was very good. 食物非常好。|近义词：excellent, fine, great, wonderful|反义词：bad, poor, terrible|词性：形容词

词源：From Middle English *god, *gode, from Old English gōd (“good, virtuous, desirable, valid, worthy, considerable, kind, benevolent, ample, fit, sound, unimpaired, honorable, upright, pious, devout, gentle, well-born, of good quality”), from Proto-Germanic *gōdaz (“good, fit”), from Proto-Indo-European *gʰedʰ- (“to unite, be associated, suitable”). Cognate with West Frisian goed (“good”), Dutch goed (“good”), German gut (“good”), Danish god (“good”), Swedish god (“good”).
