minatoriness:/ˈdɒmɪnətərɪnəs/ /ˈdɑːmɪnətɔːrɪnəs/
noun
专横；跋扈；盛气凌人
The quality of being excessively domineering or assertive.
Her minatoriness made it difficult for her colleagues to feel comfortable sharing their ideas. 她专横跋扈的态度让她的同事们很难自在地分享他们的想法。

Antonyms: submissiveness, meekness, humility, deference
