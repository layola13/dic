legitimism:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmɪzəm/
n.
正统主义，合法主义
The belief that a monarch has a legitimate right to rule by virtue of birthright.  A political doctrine that holds that the only legitimate form of government is monarchy based on hereditary right.
The exiled prince argued for legitimism, claiming he was the rightful heir to the throne.  这位流亡王子主张正统主义，声称他是王位的合法继承人。
近义词: monarchism, traditionalism
反义词: republicanism, revolution
词性: noun


legitimate:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmət/
adj.
合法的，正当的
Conforming to the law or to rules.  Able to be defended with logic or justification; valid.
They had a legitimate claim to the property.  他们对这处房产拥有合法的所有权。
近义词: lawful, valid, legal, justifiable
反义词: illegal, illegitimate, invalid, unjustified
词性: adjective


legitimate:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmeɪt/
v.
使合法，认可
Make legitimate; justify or make lawful.
The government legitimized the rebel group by allowing them to participate in elections. 政府通过允许叛军组织参加选举使其合法化。
近义词: legalize, validate, authorize, sanction
反义词: outlaw, criminalize, invalidate
词性: verb
