hitchhikes:/ˈhɪtʃˌhaɪks/ | v. | 搭便车旅行，免费搭车 | To travel by getting free rides from passing vehicles. | He hitchhiked across Europe last summer. 他去年夏天搭便车游遍了欧洲。|  She hitchhiked to California with just a backpack. 她只背着一个背包就搭便车去了加利福尼亚。 | synonyms: thumb a ride, bum a ride | antonyms: drive, pay for transportation | verb
