dratting:/ˈdrætɪŋ/
verb
诅咒; 咒骂
Expressing annoyance or impatience.
Dratting! I've lost my keys again.  该死！我又把钥匙弄丢了。
近义词: cursing, damning, swearing
反义词: blessing, praising
词性: verb

drat:/dræt/
interjection
(表示恼怒或不耐烦)该死，讨厌
Expressing mild annoyance.
Oh, drat! I forgot my wallet.  哦，讨厌！我忘了我的钱包了。
近义词: darn, blast, hang
反义词:  (无明确的反义词，表达的是一种情绪)
词性: interjection

词源:  "drat" likely originated as a euphemism for "damn" or other stronger curses.  "Dratting" is simply the present participle form of "drat" used as a verb.
