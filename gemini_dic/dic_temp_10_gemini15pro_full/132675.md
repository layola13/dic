nondispensational:/ˌnɒndɪspɛnˈseɪʃənəl/ | adj. |非时代论的| Not adhering to dispensationalism, a theological system that divides history into distinct periods or dispensations, each characterized by a different way God interacts with humanity. |  Many theologians prefer a nondispensational approach to biblical interpretation. 许多神学家更喜欢非时代论的圣经诠释方法。|  Nondispensational theologians tend to see more continuity in God's dealings with humanity throughout history. 非时代论神学家倾向于认为上帝在整个历史中与人类的交往更加具有连续性。 |  covenant, unified | dispensational | adjective
