circumstantiable:/sərˈkʌmstænʃiəbl/ /sərˈkʌmstæntiəbl/| adj.|可证实的，可提供详细情况的|Able to be supported or proven with details and evidence relating to the circumstances. |例句：The alibi was circumstantiable, with several witnesses placing him elsewhere.  他的不在场证明是可以证实的，有几个证人证明他在别处。

例句：Her claims were circumstantiable, backed up by documented evidence and eyewitness accounts. 她的说法是可以证实的，有文件证据和目击者证词的支持。|近义词：verifiable, provable, attestable, confirmable|反义词：unprovable, unverifiable|词性:adjective
