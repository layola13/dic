elderships:/ˈeldərʃɪps/
n.
长辈的职位或任期
the office or term of an elder
His elderships in the church lasted for twenty years. 他在教会的长老职位持续了二十年。


elderships:/ˈeldərʃɪps/
n.
（尤指宗教组织的）长老统治的集体
a group of elders, especially governing a religious body
The elderships decided to build a new church. 长老会决定建造一座新教堂。

近义词: leadership, seniority, authority
反义词: youth, juniority, subordination
词性: noun
