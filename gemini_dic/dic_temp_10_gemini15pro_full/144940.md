overabsorbs:/ˌoʊvərəbˈzɔːrbz/ /ˌəʊvərəbˈsɔːbz/| v. |过度吸收，吸收过多| To absorb more of something than is necessary or desirable, especially in accounting where costs are allocated based on a predetermined rate that is higher than the actual rate.| The company overabsorbed overhead costs in the last quarter, resulting in a higher profit margin.  公司在上个季度过度吸收了管理费用，导致利润率更高。| The sponge overabsorbed the water and dripped everywhere. 海绵吸收了过多的水，到处滴水。|近义词：overestimate, inflate|反义词: underabsorb, underestimate|词性:动词
