coprinces:/koʊˈprɪnsɪz/| n. |共同王子，共治王子|Two or more princes who rule jointly over a principality or kingdom.  A person who shares the role and responsibilities of a prince with another.|The coprinces signed the treaty, ensuring peace between the two regions. 这两位共治王子签署了条约，确保了两地之间的和平。|Synonyms: joint rulers, coregents|Antonyms: subject, commoner|Noun
