adhere:/ədˈhɪr/| vi.|粘附，坚持，遵守|To stick firmly to a surface or substance; to believe in and follow the practices of.|The tiles won't adhere properly to the wall.  这些瓷砖无法牢固地粘在墙上。

The glue adheres well to most surfaces.  这种胶水能很好地粘附在大多数表面上。|近义词：stick, cling, comply, conform|反义词:detach, separate|verb

adherence:/ədˈhɪrəns/| n.|坚持，遵守，依附|The fact of adhering to a particular rule, belief, or agreement.|Strict adherence to the rules is mandatory. 严格遵守规则是强制性的。

His adherence to the diet was remarkable. 他对饮食的坚持令人瞩目。|近义词：compliance, conformity, observance|反义词:violation, breach|noun

adherent:/ədˈhɪrənt/| n.|追随者，拥护者|A person who supports a particular party, person, or set of ideas.|The party's adherents campaigned tirelessly. 该党的追随者不知疲倦地进行竞选活动。

She is a strong adherent of natural remedies. 她是自然疗法的坚定拥护者。|近义词：follower, supporter, advocate|反义词:opponent, detractor|noun

adhesive:/ədˈhiːsɪv/| n. adj.|粘合剂，粘性的|A substance used for sticking objects or materials together; able to stick fast to something.|The adhesive is not strong enough for this material.  这种粘合剂对这种材料来说不够牢固。

Use an adhesive tape to seal the package. 使用胶带密封包装。|近义词：glue, sticky, adherent|反义词:non-adhesive|adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adhaerere*, from *ad-* (to) + *haerere* (to stick).

USAGE->
