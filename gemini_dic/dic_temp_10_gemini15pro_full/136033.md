nonreasonabilities:/nɒnˌriːzənəˈbɪlətiz/
noun
不合理性；无理性；不合理之处
The plural form of nonreasonability, referring to multiple instances of something being unreasonable or illogical.
The lawyer pointed out the numerous nonreasonabilities in the contract.  律师指出了合同中许多不合理之处。
Due to the nonreasonabilities of the demands, the negotiations broke down. 由于要求不合理，谈判破裂了。
synonyms: irrationalities, absurdities, illogicalities
antonyms:  rationalities,  justifications,  validities
