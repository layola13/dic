dining-out:/ˈdaɪnɪŋ aʊt/
n.
外出就餐
The act of eating a meal at a restaurant or other establishment, rather than at home.
We enjoy dining-out on weekends.  我们喜欢在周末外出就餐。
eating out, going out to eat
eating in, cooking at home
noun
