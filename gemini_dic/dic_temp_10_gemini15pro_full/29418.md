catadrome:/ˈkætəˌdroʊm/
n.
逆流洄游;逆流产卵的鱼;溯河产卵鱼
A fish that migrates down rivers to spawn in the sea.
Salmon are catadromous, migrating downriver to spawn. 鲑鱼是逆流洄游的，它们会向下游迁移产卵。
Catadromous fish swim downstream to spawn. 逆流洄游的鱼向下游游去产卵。
synonym: diadromous, oceanodromous
antonym: anadromous
