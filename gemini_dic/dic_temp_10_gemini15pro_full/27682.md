candlelighted:/ˈkændəlˌlaɪtɪd/| adj.|烛光的，被烛光照亮的|Lit by the light of candles.  The room was candlelighted and romantic. 房间里点着蜡烛，很浪漫。|例句：We had a candlelighted dinner. 我们吃了一顿烛光晚餐。

例句：The candlelighted room created a warm and intimate atmosphere. 烛光照亮的房间营造出一种温暖而私密的气氛。|近义词：candlelit|反义词:brightly lit, fluorescently lit|词性:形容词

词源:  From "candle" + "light" + "-ed".  "Candle" comes from Old English "candel," from Latin "candela." "Light" comes from Old English "leoht," of Germanic origin.  "-ed" is a suffix forming past participles and adjectives.
