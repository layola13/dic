brandishes:/ˈbrændɪʃɪz/
verb
挥舞；炫耀
waves or flourishes something, especially a weapon, as a threat or in anger or excitement.
He brandished a knife at them. 他向他们挥舞着刀子。
近义词：wave, flourish, wield, display
反义词：conceal, hide
词性:v.


brandish:/ˈbrændɪʃ/
verb
挥舞；炫耀
waves or flourishes something, especially a weapon, as a threat or in anger or excitement.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *brandiss-, present participle stem of brandir, from Frankish *brandōn "to swing a sword," from Proto-Germanic *brandōną "firebrand, sword".
