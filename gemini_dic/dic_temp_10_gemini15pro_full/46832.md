daphnetin:/ˈdæfnɪtɪn/| n. |瑞香素; 7,8-二羟基香豆素|A dihydroxycoumarin derivative found in some plants, notably Daphne mezereum. It has antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties and is used in some medicinal preparations.|Daphnetin has been shown to inhibit the growth of certain cancer cells in laboratory studies. 瑞香素在实验室研究中已被证明可以抑制某些癌细胞的生长。|Synonyms: 7,8-dihydroxycoumarin|Antonyms: None.|noun
daphnetin:/ˈdæfnɪtɪn/| n. |瑞香素; 7,8-二羟基香豆素|Daphnetin is a natural compound extracted from Daphne odora Thunb. and is used in traditional Chinese medicine. 瑞香素是从瑞香中提取的天然化合物，用于中药。|Synonyms: 7,8-dihydroxycoumarin|Antonyms: None.|noun
