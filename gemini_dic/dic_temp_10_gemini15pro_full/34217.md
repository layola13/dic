clangor:/ˈklæŋər/ (US), /ˈklæŋɡə(r)/ (UK)| n. vi.|铿锵声，喧闹声；鸣叫|A loud, resonant, metallic sound, like that of a trumpet or clashing cymbals.  A loud outcry or uproar.|The clangor of the fire alarm woke everyone in the building. 消防警报的铿锵声吵醒了大楼里的所有人。|The geese took flight with a loud clangor.  鹅群大声鸣叫着飞走了。|近义词：din, clamor, noise, ringing, resonance|反义词：silence, quiet, hush|noun
