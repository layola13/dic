clavichordists:/ˈklævɪˌkɔːrdɪsts/
noun
古钢琴演奏者；击弦古钢琴演奏家
People who play the clavichord, a European stringed keyboard instrument.
The clavichordists gathered for a competition in Bruges.  古钢琴演奏家们聚集在布鲁日参加比赛。
Several famous clavichordists emerged during the Baroque period.  巴洛克时期出现了几位著名的击弦古钢琴演奏家。
Synonyms:  clavichord players
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "non-musicians" in a broad sense)
词性: noun
