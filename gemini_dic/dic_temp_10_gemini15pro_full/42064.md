**词源:**

"Coten" is an archaic or dialectal variant of "cottage," coming from the Old French "cote," meaning "hut" or "shelter," and ultimately from the Vulgar Latin "cota."


注意事项:

"Coten" is not a common word in modern English.  "Cottage" is the standard and preferred term.
