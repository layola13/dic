disanimate:/dɪsˈænɪmeɪt/
|v.|使失去生气；使沮丧；使灰心|To deprive of spirit, animation, or courage; discourage.|The bad news disanimated the team. 坏消息使团队士气低落。|
|近义词：depress, demoralize, discourage, dispirit|
|反义词：encourage, hearten, inspire, animate|
|词性：动词|


disanimate:/dɪsˈænɪmeɪt/
|adj.|无生命的；无生气的|Not living; inanimate.|Rocks and minerals are disanimate objects.  岩石和矿物是无生命的物体。|
|近义词：inanimate, lifeless|
|反义词：animate, living|
|词性：形容词|

词源:
From Latin *disanimatus*, past participle of *disanimare* (“to deprive of life, make lifeless”), from *dis-* (“apart, away”) + *anima* (“breath, life, soul”).  源自拉丁语 *disanimatus*，是 *disanimare*（“剥夺生命，使无生命”）的过去分词，来自 *dis-*（“分开，离开”）+ *anima*（“呼吸，生命，灵魂”）。
