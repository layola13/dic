coincidentally:/ˌkoʊˌɪnsɪˈdɛntəli/ /ˌkəʊˌɪnsɪˈdɛntəli/| adv.|巧合地，碰巧地|happening by chance or at the same time by accident; in a way that is not planned or expected|I coincidentally ran into my old friend at the airport. 我在机场碰巧遇到了我的老朋友。|Synonyms: accidentally, fortuitously, unexpectedly, surprisingly|Antonyms: deliberately, intentionally, purposely|adverb

coincidence:/koʊˈɪnsɪdəns/ /kəʊˈɪnsɪdəns/| n.|巧合，巧合的事|the fact of two or more things happening at the same time by chance, in a surprising way|It was pure coincidence that we were both at the same concert. 我们在同一场音乐会上纯属巧合。|Synonyms: accident, chance, fluke|Antonyms: intention, plan|noun
