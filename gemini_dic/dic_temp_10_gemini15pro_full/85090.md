hardballing:/ˈhɑːrdbɔːlɪŋ/
n.|强硬手段，强硬策略|Employing ruthless, aggressive, or uncompromising tactics in negotiations or business dealings.|The company is known for its hardballing tactics in negotiations.  这家公司以其在谈判中强硬的策略而闻名。|Example: They played hardball to get what they wanted. 他们采取强硬手段得到了他们想要的。|Synonyms: aggressive negotiation, tough bargaining, no-holds-barred negotiation|Antonyms:  compromising, conciliatory, cooperative negotiation|noun
