monotheists:/ˈmɒnəθiːɪsts/ (US) /ˈmɑːnəθiːɪsts/| n.|一神教徒，一神论者|People who believe in only one God are called monotheists.  相信只有一个神的人被称为一神教徒。|例句：The three major monotheistic religions are Judaism, Christianity, and Islam.  三大一神教是犹太教、基督教和伊斯兰教。

|近义词：believers (in one God) |反义词: polytheists, pagans|词性:noun
