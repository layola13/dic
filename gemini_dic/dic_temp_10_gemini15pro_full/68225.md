fairings:/ˈferɪŋz/| n. |整流罩，导流罩|A structure whose primary function is to produce a smooth outline and reduce drag, as on an aircraft or motorcycle.|The motorcycle's fairings were damaged in the crash. 摩托车的整流罩在事故中受损了。|近义词：cowling, cladding, shell |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "exposed components") |noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "fare," meaning "to go" or "to travel," referring to the fairing's role in smoothing airflow and improving travel.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *faraną.
