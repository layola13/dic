词源:

"Inter-" (Latin) meaning "between, among, mutually" + "grapple" (from the Old French "grappil" meaning "hook, claw").  "Inter-"（拉丁语）意思是“在…之间，在…之中，相互地” + "grapple"（来自古法语 "grappil"，意思是“钩子，爪子”）。
