gauging:/ˈɡeɪdʒɪŋ/| n. v.|测量，估计，判断|The act of measuring or estimating something, especially depth, size, or amount.  Also, the act of assessing or judging something.|例句：The mechanic was gauging the oil level in the engine.  技工正在测量发动机内的油位。

例句：They were gauging public opinion on the new policy. 他们在评估公众对新政策的看法。|近义词：measuring, assessing, estimating, judging, appraising|反义词:misjudging, miscalculating|词性:noun (the act of gauging); verb (present participle of gauge)
