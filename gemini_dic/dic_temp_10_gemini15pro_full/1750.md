acquire:/əˈkwaɪər/ (US) /əˈkwaɪə(r)/ (UK)| vt.|获得，取得，学到|To gain possession of something; to learn or develop a skill or habit.|She acquired a taste for fine wines during her time in France.  她在法国期间培养了对高级葡萄酒的品味。

He acquired a new car last week. 他上周买了一辆新车。|近义词：obtain, gain, learn, earn, achieve|反义词:lose, forfeit, abandon|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *aqueren*, from Old French *aquérir*, from Latin *acquirere* ("to get in addition to, acquire, gain, earn, obtain"), from *ad* ("to, towards") + *quaerere* ("to seek, search for, ask").

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He acquires knowledge quickly. = 他学东西很快。
She acquires new clients through networking. = 她通过人际关系网获得新客户。


一般过去时:
They acquired the company in 2020. = 他们在2020年收购了这家公司。
He acquired a valuable painting at the auction. = 他在拍卖会上获得了一幅珍贵的画作。


现在完成时:
She has acquired a reputation for honesty. = 她获得了诚实的声誉。
We have acquired several new properties this year. = 我们今年购置了几处新房产。


过去完成时:
By the time he was 30, he had acquired a substantial fortune. = 到他30岁的时候，他已经积累了大量的财富。
She had acquired a good understanding of the subject before the exam. = 她在考试前对这门学科有了很好的理解。


将来时:
The company will acquire new technology to improve efficiency. = 公司将获得新技术来提高效率。
He will acquire the necessary skills for the job through training. = 他将通过培训获得这项工作所需的技能。


虚拟语气:
If I were to acquire a superpower, I would choose teleportation. = 如果我要获得一项超能力，我会选择瞬间移动。
I wish I could acquire fluency in another language. = 我希望我能流利地说另一种语言。


被动语态:
The company was acquired by a larger corporation. = 这家公司被一家更大的公司收购了。
New skills can be acquired through practice and dedication. = 通过练习和奉献可以获得新的技能。


疑问句:
How did they acquire so much wealth? = 他们是如何获得这么多财富的？
Where did she acquire that beautiful dress? = 她在哪里买的这件漂亮的衣服？


否定句:
He did not acquire the property legally. = 他没有合法地获得这处房产。
She hasn't acquired the necessary skills for the job yet. = 她还没有获得这项工作所需的技能。
