half-refined:/ˌhɑːf rɪˈfaɪnd/
adjective
半精炼的
Partially refined; not fully processed or purified.
The half-refined sugar still contained some impurities. 半精炼的糖仍然含有一些杂质。
The factory produced half-refined oil for further processing. 这家工厂生产半精炼油供进一步加工。
Near synonyms: partially refined, semi-refined
Antonyms: fully refined, completely refined, highly refined
词性: adjective
