backfill:/ˈbækˌfɪl/ | n. vt.|回填，回填土；补充；（广播、电视节目）临时节目|The process of refilling an excavated hole or trench with material, often the original material that was removed. Also, the act of filling a vacancy or gap, especially in a schedule or workforce. |They used gravel to backfill the trench around the new pipe. 他们用碎石回填了新管道周围的沟渠。|Synonyms: refill, replace, replenish, fill in|Antonyms: excavate, dig, empty|noun
