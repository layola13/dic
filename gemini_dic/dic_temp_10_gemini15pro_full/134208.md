nonindictable:/ˌnɒnɪnˈdaɪtəbl/
adjective
非起诉的，轻罪的
Not subject to indictment; relating to a minor offense tried summarily before a magistrate or judge without a jury.
Shoplifting is usually a nonindictable offense.  商店行窃通常是一种非起诉罪行。
This is classified as a nonindictable offence. 这被归类为非起诉罪行。
近义词:summary, minor
反义词:indictable, felony
词性:adjective

词源:
From non- + indictable.  "Indictable" comes from the Old French "enditer" meaning "to dictate, write down, accuse".  The prefix "non-" simply negates the meaning.
