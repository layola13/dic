anti-Asian:/ˌæntiˈeɪʒn/
adjective
反亚裔的；歧视亚裔的
Discriminatory or prejudiced against people of Asian descent.
Example:  Anti-Asian hate crimes have risen sharply in recent years.  反亚裔仇恨犯罪近年来急剧上升。

Example:  The politician's comments were deemed anti-Asian by many.  这位政客的言论被许多人认为是反亚裔的。
近义词:racist, discriminatory, prejudiced, bigoted
反义词:pro-Asian, inclusive, tolerant
词性:adjective
