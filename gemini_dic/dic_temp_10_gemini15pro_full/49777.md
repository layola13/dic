demonianism:/ˌdiːməˈnaɪɪzəm/
n.
恶魔崇拜；极端邪恶；残暴
The worship of demons; extreme wickedness or cruelty.
The accusations of demonianism during the Salem witch trials led to many innocent people being executed.  塞勒姆女巫审判期间对恶魔崇拜的指控导致许多无辜的人被处决。
His acts of cruelty and violence were seen as pure demonianism. 他的残忍和暴力行为被视为纯粹的恶魔行径。
近义词:devil worship, satanism, wickedness, evil, cruelty
反义词:goodness, benevolence, kindness
