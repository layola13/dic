intensifier:/ɪnˈtɛnsɪfaɪər/| n.|增强剂；强化词|A word, especially an adverb or adjective, that has little semantic content of its own but that serves to intensify the meaning of another word or phrase.|The word "very" is an intensifier in the sentence "I am very happy."|近义词：amplifier, booster, enhancer|反义词:mitigator, softener|词性:noun
