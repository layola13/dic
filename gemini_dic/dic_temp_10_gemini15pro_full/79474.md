glob:/ɡlɑb/ (US) /ɡlɒb/ (UK)| v.|凝聚成球状|To form into a glob.|The melted cheese globbed together in the pan.  融化的奶酪在锅里凝结成一团。|近义词：clump, coagulate|反义词: N/A|verb
