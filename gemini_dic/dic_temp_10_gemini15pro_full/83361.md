gyrland: /ˈɡɪərlənd/ | n. | 花环，花圈， garlands (plural) | A circular decoration made of flowers, leaves, or other materials.  It is often worn on the head or hung as a decoration. |  She wore a garland of roses in her hair. 她头上戴着玫瑰花环。

The hall was decorated with garlands of flowers. 大厅用花环装饰。 | synonyms: wreath, chaplet, festoon | antonyms: N/A | noun
