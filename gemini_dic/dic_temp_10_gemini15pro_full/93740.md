immaterially:/ˌɪməˈtɪəriəli/
adverb
无关紧要地；非实质性地
In a way that is not important or significant.
The judge ruled that the evidence was immaterially and could not be considered. 法官裁定该证据无关紧要，不能予以考虑。
The error affected the outcome immaterially.  这个错误对结果没有实质性影响。
Synonyms: insignificantly, trivially, unimportantly, negligibly
Antonyms: significantly, materially, substantially, importantly
词性: 副词
