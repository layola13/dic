objectlessness:/ˈɒbdʒɪktlɪsnəs/| n.|无目标性，无客体性|The state of lacking an object, aim, or purpose.  A sense of objectlessness can lead to feelings of emptiness and apathy. |His objectlessness made it difficult for him to find a fulfilling career.  他缺乏目标感，很难找到一份称心如意的职业。|近义词:aimlessness, purposelessness, futility|反义词:purposefulness, direction, focus|词性:noun
