non-igneous:/ˌnɒnˈɪɡniəs/
adjective
非火成的
Not formed from molten rock; sedimentary or metamorphic.
Limestone is a non-igneous rock. 石灰岩是一种非火成岩。
近义词: sedimentary, metamorphic
反义词: igneous
词性: adjective
