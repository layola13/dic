contriving:/kənˈtraɪvɪŋ/
verb
谋划，设计；设法做到
devise or plan cleverly or intricately.
He was contriving a way to escape. 他正在谋划逃跑的办法。
近义词:planning, scheming, plotting, devising, engineering
反义词:failing, neglecting

词性:
present participle of contrive

词源:
From Middle English *controven, *contreven, from Old French controver, contrever (“to invent, compose, feign, forge, fabricate”), from Latin contrō (“against”) + trover (“to find”).
