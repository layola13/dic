overafflicting: /ˌōvərəˈflɪktɪŋ/
adjective
过度折磨的，苦恼不堪的
Causing excessive affliction or suffering; overwhelming with grief or distress.
The overafflicting news of her brother's death left her inconsolable. 她哥哥去世的噩耗让她悲痛欲绝。
Overafflicting poverty plagued the region.  极度贫困困扰着这个地区。
近义词: distressing, harrowing, tormenting, agonizing
反义词: comforting, soothing, relieving, assuaging
词性: adjective

词源：
"Over-" (prefix) + "afflicting" (present participle of "afflict")

"Over-" comes from Old English "ofer," meaning "above, beyond, across."
"Afflict" comes from Latin "affligere," meaning "to strike down, dash to the ground," from "ad-" (to) + "fligere" (to strike).
