dust:/dʌst/ | v. |  除去灰尘；掸掉灰尘；撒上（粉末） | Remove dust from a surface; sprinkle (powder) over something. | She dusted the shelves carefully. 她仔细地掸了掸架子。| synonyms: clean, wipe, sprinkle | antonyms: soil, dirty | verb
