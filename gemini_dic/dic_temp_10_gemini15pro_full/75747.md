full-scale:/ˌfʊlˈskeɪl/ | adj. | 全面的，大规模的，完全的 | Involving all aspects or elements; complete, comprehensive; large-scale, extensive. | The company launched a full-scale investigation into the incident. 公司对该事件展开了全面调查。 |  The army commenced a full-scale invasion. 军队开始了全面入侵。 | 近义词: comprehensive, extensive, thorough, complete, large-scale | 反义词: limited, partial, small-scale | 词性: adjective

full-scale: /ˌfʊlˈskeɪl/ | n. |  (通常不用作名词) |  (Generally not used as a noun) |  N/A | N/A |  近义词: N/A | 反义词: N/A | 词性: (rarely) noun


词源: "full" (Old English "full") + "scale" (from Old Norse "skal" meaning "bowl, shell, scale").  The combined meaning implies the entirety of a scale or range.
