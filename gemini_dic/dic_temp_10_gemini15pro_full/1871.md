acrospire: /ˈækrəˌspaɪər/
n.
嫩芽，(尤指大麦的)胚芽，麦芽根
The first sprout of a germinating grain, especially barley.
The acrospire of the barley had already begun to grow. 大麦的胚芽已经开始生长了。
近义词: sprout, germ, plumule
反义词: (无明确反义词)
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *acrispira, from Greek akris, akros "highest, topmost" + Latin spira "coil, sprout."  Meaning shifted from "first sprout" to specifically the sprout of barley in malting.

USAGE->
The acrospire is a key indicator of the malting process.  麦芽根是麦芽制作过程中的关键指标。

The length of the acrospire determines the degree of modification in the barley kernel. 麦芽根的长度决定了大麦粒的改性程度。

The brewer checked the acrospire to ensure proper germination.  酿酒师检查了麦芽根，以确保适当的发芽。

During the malting process, the acrospire grows within the barley grain. 在麦芽制作过程中，麦芽根生长在大麦粒内。

The acrospire should reach about two-thirds to three-quarters the length of the grain for optimal brewing. 为了获得最佳酿造效果，麦芽根应达到谷物长度的三分之二到四分之三左右。


PHRASE->
(acrospire does not typically appear in common phrases.  It is a technical term primarily used in brewing and agriculture.)
