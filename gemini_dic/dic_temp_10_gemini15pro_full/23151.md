boutiques:/buːˈtiːks/ (US), /buːˈtiːks/ (UK)| n. |精品店；专卖店|A small shop selling fashionable clothes or other goods. |例句：She likes to shop in the chic boutiques of Paris. 她喜欢在巴黎别致的精品店购物。

例句：The street is lined with boutiques selling designer clothes. 这条街上排列着许多出售名牌服装的精品店。|近义词：shops, stores, outlets|反义词：department store, supermarket|noun
