nerita:/nəˈraɪtə/ | n. | 海螺；蜒螺| A genus of small, mostly tropical marine snails, typically having a thick, rounded shell with a semi-circular operculum.  They are commonly found in intertidal zones. |  例句：The nerita clung tightly to the rock. 海螺紧紧地粘在岩石上。

例句：She collected various nerita shells on the beach. 她在海滩上收集了各种海螺壳。 | 近义词：snail, periwinkle | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
