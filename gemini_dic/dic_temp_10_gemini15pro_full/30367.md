centerboards: /ˈsɛntərbɔːrdz/
n.
中心板；活动龙骨
A retractable keel or fin used on sailboats to provide lateral resistance and reduce leeway.  It can be raised or lowered depending on the sailing conditions.
The centerboards were lowered as the boat sailed into deeper water.  船驶入深水区时，中心板被放了下来。
keel, fin, daggerboard
