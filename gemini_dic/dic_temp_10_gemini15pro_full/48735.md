一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you usually eat for breakfast? 你早餐通常吃什么？
What does she think about the new policy? 她对新政策有什么看法？


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she been working on recently? 她最近一直在做什么？


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been doing all afternoon? 你整个下午都在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? 他最近一直在读什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

Etymology:
From Old English _hwæt_, neuter singular nominative and accusative of _hwā_  "who,"  from Proto-Germanic *hwat, from Proto-Indo-European *kwod.  Related to who, how, why.
