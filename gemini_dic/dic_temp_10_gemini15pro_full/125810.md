murmur:/ˈmɜːrmər/ (US), /ˈmɜːmə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|低语，咕哝；潺潺声|a low, continuous sound, like the sound of people talking quietly or the sound of a stream|The crowd murmured their approval. 人群低声表示赞同。|synonyms: whisper, mutter, drone|antonyms: shout, yell|noun
murmur:/ˈmɜːrmər/ (US), /ˈmɜːmə(r)/ (UK)| v.|低声说，咕哝；发出潺潺声|to speak or say something very quietly|He murmured an apology. 他低声地道歉。|synonyms: whisper, mutter, drone|antonyms: shout, yell|verb
