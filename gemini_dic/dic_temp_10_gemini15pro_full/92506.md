hypothesize:/haɪˈpɒθəsaɪz/ (UK) /haɪˈpɑːθəsaɪz/ (US)| v. |假设，假定|To suggest a possible explanation for something, but without knowing whether it is really true.|Scientists hypothesize that the universe began with a big bang. 科学家们假设宇宙起源于一次大爆炸。|Synonyms: postulate, theorize, conjecture, suppose|Antonyms: prove, demonstrate|verb

hypotheses:/haɪˈpɒθəsiːz/ (UK) /haɪˈpɑːθəsiːz/ (US)| n. |假设，假说|A proposed explanation for something that can be tested.|Several hypotheses have been put forward to explain the extinction of the dinosaurs.  已经提出了几个假说来解释恐龙的灭绝。|Synonyms: theory, supposition, conjecture, postulation|Antonyms: proof, fact|noun
