omnibuses:/ˈɒmnɪbʌsɪz/ (UK) /ˈɑːmnɪbʌsɪz/ (US)| n. |公共汽车（尤指旧时的大型公共汽车）; 总括的; 汇编的|A vehicle used for public transportation, especially a large motor bus, typically one of an earlier type. Also refers to something that includes many different items or aspects.|The museum has several vintage omnibuses on display.  博物馆展出了几辆老式公共汽车。|近义词：buses, coaches|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly private car)|名词

omnibus:/ˈɒmnɪbʌs/ (UK) /ˈɑːmnɪbʌs/ (US)| adj. |总括的; 汇编的|Including many different items or aspects.|An omnibus bill containing several proposals.  一项包含多项提案的综合法案。|近义词：comprehensive, inclusive|反义词: specific, individual|形容词
