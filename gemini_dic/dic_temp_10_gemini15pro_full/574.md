abot:/əˈbɒt/| adv. | 大约，关于 | About how much will it cost?  大约要多少钱？ | About ten people came to the party. 大约有十个人来参加了聚会。 | 近义词: approximately, roughly, around, concerning, regarding | 反义词: exactly, precisely | 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English "onbūtan," meaning "around the outside of," from "on" (on) + "būtan" (outside).  The meaning shifted over time to include "approximately" and "concerning."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He talks about his work a lot. = 他经常谈论他的工作。
The book is about the history of China. = 这本书是关于中国历史的。

一般过去时:
We talked about our childhood memories. = 我们谈论了我们童年的回忆。
The movie was about a young boy and his dog. = 这部电影是关于一个小男孩和他的狗的故事。

现在完成时:
I have learned a lot about this topic. = 我已经学习了很多关于这个主题的知识。
She has been thinking about changing her career. = 她一直在考虑换工作。

过去完成时:
They had already talked about the problem before I arrived. = 在我到达之前，他们已经讨论过这个问题了。
He had been worried about his exam results for weeks. = 他几周来一直担心他的考试成绩。

将来时:
I will tell you about my trip when I get back. = 我回来后会告诉你我的旅行情况。
We will talk about the next steps in the project tomorrow. = 我们明天将讨论该项目的后续步骤。

虚拟语气:
If I knew more about it, I would tell you. = 如果我对此了解更多，我会告诉你的。
I wish I had known about this earlier. = 我希望我早点知道这件事。

被动语态:
The incident was talked about for days. = 这件事被谈论了好几天。
The new policy is being talked about everywhere. = 新政策到处都在被谈论。

疑问句:
What are you talking about? = 你在说什么？
What is this book about? = 这本书是关于什么的？

否定句:
I don't know anything about it. = 我对此一无所知。
She didn't say anything about her plans. = 她没有说关于她计划的任何事情。


PHRASE->
about to = 即将
talk about = 谈论
know about = 了解
think about = 考虑
worry about = 担心
