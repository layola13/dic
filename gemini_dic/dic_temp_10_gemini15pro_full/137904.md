nonviviparousness:/ˌnɒnˌvɪvɪˈpærəsnəs/
n.
非胎生
The state or condition of not being viviparous; not bringing forth living young.
Most insects exhibit nonviviparousness, laying eggs instead of giving birth to live young. 大多数昆虫都表现出非胎生性，它们产卵而不是产下幼虫。
Nonviviparousness is a common reproductive strategy in reptiles. 非胎生是爬行动物中常见的繁殖策略。
近义词: oviparity, ovoviviparity
反义词: viviparity
词性: noun

Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + viviparous (adjective meaning "bringing forth living young") + -ness (suffix forming nouns denoting a state or condition)
