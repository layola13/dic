hop-to-it:/ˌhɒpˈtuːɪt/ | idiom | 开始行动，赶快，加油 | To begin working on something quickly and energetically; to get busy. | Example: The boss told us to hop to it and finish the project by Friday. 老板告诉我们要赶紧完成这个项目，在星期五之前做完。

Example: We've got a lot to do today, so let's hop to it! 我们今天有很多事情要做，所以让我们赶快开始吧！ | 近义词: get cracking, get going, get started, get a move on, buckle down | 反义词: procrastinate, dawdle, delay | 词性: idiom
