leucopathy:/ˌluːˈkɒpəθi/ /ˌljuːˈkɑːpəθi/| n. |白斑病，皮肤色素脱失|A condition characterized by the localized or generalized absence of pigment in the skin, resulting in white patches or spots.| Vitiligo is a form of leucopathy. 白癜风是一种白斑病。|Synonyms: leukopathy, hypopigmentation|Antonyms: hyperpigmentation, melanosis|noun
