inter-pollinating:/ˌɪntərˈpɑːləˌneɪtɪŋ/ |ˌɪntəˈpɒləˌneɪtɪŋ/
| adj. |异花授粉的|The act of transferring pollen from one plant to another, typically of a different variety, leading to cross-fertilization.|例句：The bees were busy inter-pollinating the apple blossoms. 蜜蜂正忙着给苹果花进行异花授粉。|近义词：cross-pollinating, hybridizing|反义词：self-pollinating|词性:形容词

inter-pollinate:/ˌɪntərˈpɑːləˌneɪt/ |ˌɪntəˈpɒləˌneɪt/
| v. |异花授粉|To transfer pollen from one plant to another, typically of a different variety.|例句：Different varieties of corn can inter-pollinate. 不同品种的玉米可以异花授粉。|近义词：cross-pollinate, hybridize|反义词：self-pollinate|词性:动词

词源：
From inter- + pollinate.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among." "Pollinate" comes from the Latin word "pollen," meaning "fine flour" or "dust."
