dogship:/ˈdɒɡʃɪp/| n.|狗的地位；（尤指）狗的主人身份；爱狗人士|A playful or humorous term referring to the status or role of being a dog owner, or more generally, a dog lover.  It highlights the close bond and affection between dogs and their humans.|She embraced her dogship with pride, showering her furry companions with love and attention. 她自豪地接受了自己的“狗主人”身份，对她的毛茸茸的伙伴们倾注了爱和关注。|Dogs embraced their dogship on the beach. 狗狗们在沙滩上享受着它们的“狗”生活。|近义词:dog ownership, dog lover, canine companionship|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly cat ownership or a dislike of dogs)|词性:noun
