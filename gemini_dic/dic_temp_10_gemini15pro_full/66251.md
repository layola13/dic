excluders:/ɪksˈkluːdərz/
|n.|排除者，排斥者|People or things that exclude others.  Those who prevent others from entering or participating.|The excluders at the club wouldn't let anyone in without a membership card.  俱乐部的把关人不允许任何人没有会员卡就进入。|excluders kept the general public out of the private event. 把关者将公众拒之于私人活动之外。|near synonyms:  gatekeepers, bouncers, restrictors|near antonyms: includers, welcomers|noun
