hypercriticise:/ˌhaɪpərˈkrɪtɪsaɪz/| v. |过度批评；吹毛求疵|To criticize excessively, finding fault with even minor details. |例句：He tends to hypercriticise everything she does. 他总是对她做的每件事都吹毛求疵。

例句：The art critic hypercriticised the painting, focusing on insignificant brushstrokes.  这位艺术评论家对这幅画进行了过度批评，专注于无关紧要的笔触。

近义词：nitpick, cavil, carp, overcriticize|反义词: praise, commend, laud |词性: verb
