crore:/krɔːr/ /kroʊr/
n.
千万; 一亿(印度英语)
Ten million; one hundred lakhs (Indian English).
India has a population of over a crore. 印度人口超过一亿。
The film earned crores of rupees. 这部电影赚了数千万卢比。
synonyms:ten million (in Indian English), myriad, multitude
antonyms:few, little
noun
