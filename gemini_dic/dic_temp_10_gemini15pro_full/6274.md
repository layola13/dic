amoralisms:/ˌeɪˈmɔːrəlɪzəmz/
n.
不道德言论；非道德论；与道德无关的言论
Doctrines or principles that disregard or reject morality.
His amoralisms shocked the conservative community. 他的不道德言论震惊了保守的社区。
Near Synonyms: immoralities, improprieties, indecencies
Antonyms: morals, ethics, principles
Noun
