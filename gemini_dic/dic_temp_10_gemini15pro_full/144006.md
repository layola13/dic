outreasoning:/ˌaʊtˈriːzənɪŋ/
verb
以理服人; 通过推理胜过
defeating someone by reasoning; surpassing in reasoning
He outreasoned his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中以理服人了他的对手。

近义词: outarguing, outthinking
反义词: losing an argument
