allophylic:/ˌæləˈfɪlɪk/
adjective
亲外族的; 异族喜好的
Attracted to or preferring what is foreign or different, especially with regard to cultural or ethnic groups.
She demonstrated allophylic tendencies in her embrace of different cultures and customs. 她对不同文化和习俗的喜爱展现了她亲外族的倾向。
近义词:xenophilic, exophilic
反义词:allophobic, xenophobic
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From allo- (Greek, "other, different") + -philic (Greek, "loving").
