culler:/ˈkʌlər/| n.|剔除者，拣选者|A person or machine that culls or selects items from a group, especially those of inferior quality.|The culler quickly removed the damaged fruit from the conveyor belt. 剔除者快速地从传送带上移走了损坏的水果。|近义词：selector, picker, sorter|反义词:keeper, includer|noun
