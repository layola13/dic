benzenes:/ˈbɛnˌziːnz/ | n. |苯|Aromatic hydrocarbon with the chemical formula C6H6, consisting of a ring of six carbon atoms each bonded to a hydrogen atom.  It is a colorless, flammable liquid with a sweet odor.|Benzene is used to make other chemicals that are used to make plastics, resins, nylon and synthetic fibers. 苯用于制造其他化学品，这些化学品用于制造塑料、树脂、尼龙和合成纤维。| synonyms: aromatic hydrocarbon, benzol | antonyms: None. | noun
