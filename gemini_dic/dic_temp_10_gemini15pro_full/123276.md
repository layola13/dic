monoecious: /məˈniːʃəs/ | adj. | 雌雄同株的；雌雄同体的 | Having both male and female reproductive organs in the same individual; hermaphroditic. |  Corn is a monoecious plant, with separate male and female flowers on the same stalk. 玉米是雌雄同株的植物，同一植株上有不同的雄花和雌花。

Monoecious species can self-pollinate, ensuring reproduction even in isolation.  雌雄同株的物种可以自花授粉，即使在隔离的情况下也能保证繁殖。 | Near synonyms: hermaphroditic, bisexual, cosexual | Near antonyms: dioecious | Word type: adjective
