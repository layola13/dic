bogda:/ˈbɒɡdə/ | n. | 博格达 |  A mountain range in Xinjiang, China, and also a peak within that range, known for its glaciers and scenic beauty.  It's also a Mongolian word meaning "holy" or "sacred." | Example: The Bogda Peak is covered with snow all year round. 博格达峰终年积雪。 | Example:  The Bogda Mountains are a popular destination for hikers. 博格达山脉是徒步旅行者的热门目的地。 | 近义词: mountain, peak | 反义词: valley | noun
