developability:/dɪˌvɛləpəˈbɪləti/| n.|可开发性，发展性|The developability of this land is high due to its proximity to the city center.  这块土地的可开发性很高，因为它靠近市中心。|The software has good developability, allowing for easy addition of new features.  这款软件具有良好的可开发性，可以轻松添加新功能。|Synonyms: potential, capacity, feasibility|Antonyms: undevelopability, impossibility|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From develop (v.) + -ability (suffix).  Develop comes from Old French desveloper "to unfold, unwrap" from des- "un-, out" + voloper "to wrap".  -ability is a suffix forming nouns of quality or state from adjectives ending in -able, or from verbs.
