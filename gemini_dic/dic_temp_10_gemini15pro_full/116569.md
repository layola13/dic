matins:/ˈmætɪnz/ | n. |晨祷，早课|A service of morning prayer, especially in the Anglican Church. |例句：The monks gathered in the chapel for matins. 修士们聚集在小教堂做晨祷。

例句：She woke early to attend matins. 她早起去参加晨祷。


|近义词：morning prayer, lauds |反义词:vespers, evensong |词性:noun
