cockroach:/ˈkɒkrəʊtʃ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːkroʊtʃ/ (US)| n.|蟑螂|A large, brown insect with a flat body and long antennae, commonly found in dirty places. It is considered a pest.|例句：I saw a cockroach scurry across the kitchen floor. 我看到一只蟑螂飞快地穿过厨房地板。

例句：Cockroaches are known to carry diseases.  众所周知，蟑螂携带疾病。

近义词：pest, vermin|反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性: noun

词源:  The word "cockroach" comes from the Spanish word "cucaracha," which originally referred to a woodlouse.  The word eventually came to be used for the insect we know today in English around the beginning of the 17th century.
