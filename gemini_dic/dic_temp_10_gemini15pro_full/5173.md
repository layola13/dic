alleviation:/əˌliːviˈeɪʃən/
n.
减轻，缓和
the act of making something bad such as pain or problems less severe
The alleviation of poverty is a global challenge. 减轻贫困是一项全球性挑战。

近义词:relief, mitigation, easement
反义词:aggravation, exacerbation
词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin alleviatio(n-), from the verb alleviat- ‘lightened’, from the classical Latin prefix ad- ‘to’ + levis ‘light’.
