elevator: /ˈɛləˌveɪtər/ (US) /ˈɛlɪveɪtə/ (UK)| n.|电梯；升降机|A machine used for carrying people or goods up and down to different levels in a building.|I took the elevator to the tenth floor. 我乘电梯到了十楼。|近义词：lift (UK), escalator (for moving staircases)|反义词: stairs, escalator(sometimes, depending on direction) | noun


elevator: /ˈɛləˌveɪtər/ (US) /ˈɛlɪveɪtə/ (UK)| v.|提升；提高；举起|To raise or lift something to a higher level, either literally or figuratively.|The crane elevated the building materials to the top floor. 起重机将建筑材料吊到顶层。|This win elevated the team's morale. 这场胜利提升了团队的士气。|近义词：raise, lift, boost, upgrade|反义词: lower, depress | verb
