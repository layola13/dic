facsimiled:/ˈfækˈsɪmɪld/ |v. |复制；摹写| To make a facsimile of something; to copy or reproduce something exactly. | He facsimiled the ancient manuscript. 他复制了这份古代手稿。|  The contract was facsimiled and sent to both parties. 合同被复制后发送给了双方。 |Synonyms: copied, reproduced, duplicated, replicated | Antonyms: original, unique| verb


facsimile:/ˈfækˈsɪmɪli/ |n. |复制品；摹本；传真| An exact copy or reproduction of something, such as a document, signature, or work of art. | The museum displayed a facsimile of the Declaration of Independence. 博物馆展出了一份《独立宣言》的复制品。| Please send me a facsimile of the signed contract. 请给我发一份签名合同的传真件。| Synonyms: copy, replica, duplicate, reproduction | Antonyms: original, authentic| noun


Etymology: From Latin fac simile "make similar", imperative of facere "to make" + simile "similarly".
