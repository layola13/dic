expectorant: /ɪkˈspɛktərənt/ /ɛkˈspɛktərənt/ | n. adj. |祛痰药，祛痰的 |A medicine that helps to bring up mucus from the lungs or throat. |例句：The doctor prescribed an expectorant for her cough. 医生给她开了祛痰药治咳嗽。|近义词：mucolytic, antitussive (when used to suppress coughs associated with mucus) |反义词:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider antidiarrheal as an antonym by analogy - one loosens secretions in the respiratory tract, the other firms stool in the digestive tract.) |词性: noun, adjective


expectorants: /ɪkˈspɛktərənts/ /ɛkˈspɛktərənts/ | n. |祛痰药 |Plural form of expectorant.  Medicines that help to bring up mucus from the lungs or throat. |例句：Many over-the-counter expectorants are available.  很多非处方祛痰药都可以买到。 |近义词：mucolytics |反义词: (Same as expectorant - no direct antonym) |词性: noun
