misreaders:/ˌmɪsˈriːdərz/
n.
误读者，曲解者
People who misread or misinterpret something, such as a text or situation.
The author was frustrated by the misreaders who twisted his words.  作者对那些歪曲他话的人感到沮丧。
近义词: misinterpreters, distorters
反义词: interpreters
