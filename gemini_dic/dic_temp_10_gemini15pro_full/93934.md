immunizes:/ˈɪmjʊˌnaɪzɪz/
verb
免疫，使免疫
makes someone immune to a disease, usually by injecting them with a vaccine.
The doctor immunizes children against measles. 医生为孩子们接种麻疹疫苗。
近义词: vaccinates, protects
反义词: exposes, weakens
词性: verb
