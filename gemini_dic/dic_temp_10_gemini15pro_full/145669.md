overcontrolled: /ˌoʊvərˌkənˈtroʊld/ | adj. | 过度控制的，抑制过度的 | Excessively controlled or inhibited, often referring to emotions or behavior.  Characterized by an excessive degree of self-control, often leading to repression of feelings and difficulty expressing oneself. |  He seemed overcontrolled, never showing any emotion. 他看起来过度克制，从不表现出任何情绪。 | The child's overcontrolled behavior made it difficult for him to make friends.  这个孩子过度控制的行为让他难以交友。 | 近义词: repressed, inhibited, restrained, subdued | 反义词: uninhibited, unrestrained, impulsive, expressive | 词性: adjective

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "controlled" (past participle of the verb "control")
