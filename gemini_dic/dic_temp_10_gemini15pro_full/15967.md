balsamy:/ˈbɔːlsəmi/ | adj. | 香脂的，芳香的，有香脂味的 | Having the pleasant smell of balsam; resinous. | The air was balsamy with the scent of pine. 空气中弥漫着松树的香脂味。 | The room was filled with a balsamy fragrance. 房间里充满了香脂的香味。| 近义词: aromatic, fragrant, resinous | 反义词: acrid, fetid, odorless | 词性: adjective

balsamy:/ˈbɔːlsəmi/ | adj. | (指树木)产生香脂的 | (of a tree) yielding balsam. |  The balsamy fir trees lined the path. 香脂冷杉排列在小路两旁。 |  They tapped the balsamy bark to collect the resin. 他们轻敲产生香脂的树皮来收集树脂。| 近义词: resinous, balsam-producing | 反义词: (no specific antonym, could use non-resinous) | 词性: adjective
