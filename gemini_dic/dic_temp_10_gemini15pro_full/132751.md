nondomestic:/ˌnɒndəˈmɛstɪk/ /ˌnɑːndəˈmɛstɪk/| adj.|非家庭的，非国内的|Not related to the home or family; foreign.|例句：The company is expanding its nondomestic markets.  这家公司正在拓展其海外市场。|近义词：foreign, international, overseas|反义词：domestic, home, local|词性:形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not".
domestic: from Latin domesticus "belonging to the house," from domus "house."
