ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *capriciosus, from Italian capriccioso (“whimsical”), from capriccio (“sudden start or motion; whim”), from capo (“head”) + riccio (“hedgehog”).  The notion is of a head bristling like a hedgehog, suggesting a sudden impulsive movement.
