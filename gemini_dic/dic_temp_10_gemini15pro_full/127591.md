neddy:/ˈnɛdi/| n.|驴；笨蛋；傻瓜|A donkey.  Informally, it can also refer to a foolish or stupid person.|例句：The old farmer rode his neddy into town. 老农夫骑着他的驴进城。

例句：He's a bit of a neddy sometimes. 他有时有点傻。|近义词：donkey, ass, fool, idiot|反义词:genius, clever person|词性:noun
