accountant: /əˈkaʊntənt/ | n. | 会计，会计师 | A person whose job is to keep or inspect financial accounts. |  My father is an accountant for a large company.  我父亲是一家大公司的会计。

I need to talk to my accountant about my taxes. 我需要和我的会计师谈谈我的税务问题。| 近义词: bookkeeper, auditor | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "client" in a business context) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *acontant* "accountant," present participle of *aconter* "to count, render account," from Latin *ad* "to" + *computare* "to count, sum up." The spelling with -cc- arose by analogy with *account*.
