hittites:/ˈhɪtaɪts/
n.
赫梯人
A member of an ancient people who established an empire in Asia Minor and Syria c. 1700–1200 BC.
The Hittites were known for their advanced use of iron. 赫梯人以其先进的铁器使用而闻名。
Hittite kings ruled over a vast empire. 赫梯国王统治着一个庞大的帝国。
ancients, Anatolians
