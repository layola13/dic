insure:/ɪnˈʃʊr/ (US), /ɪnˈʃɔːr/ (UK)| vt.|投保，保险，确保|To protect against risk of loss by regularly paying a sum of money to an insurance company, or to guarantee that something will happen.|例句：I insured my car against theft. 我为我的汽车投了盗窃险。|例句：The company insured the shipment against damage. 公司为这批货物投保了损坏险。|近义词：guarantee, protect, assure|反义词：endanger, jeopardize|词性：动词
insure:/ɪnˈʃʊr/ (US), /ɪnˈʃɔːr/ (UK)| vt.|确保|To make certain that something will happen.|例句：We took steps to insure the success of the project. 我们采取措施确保项目的成功。|例句：The company took out a loan to insure against bankruptcy. 公司贷款以确保不会破产。|近义词：ensure, guarantee, secure|反义词：jeopardize, endanger|词性：动词
