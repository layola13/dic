convocants:/kənˈvəʊkənts/
n.
被召集者；与会者
Those summoned to a meeting or assembly.
The convocants gathered in the hall for the annual meeting.  被召集者聚集在大厅里参加年会。
The university convocants included graduating students and their families. 大学的与会者包括毕业生及其家人。
synonyms: attendees, participants, assembly, gathering
antonyms: absentees, non-participants
noun
