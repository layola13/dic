palladian:/pəˈleɪdiən/| adj.|帕拉第奥式的，帕拉第奥风格的|Relating to or characteristic of the style of Andrea Palladio, a 16th-century Italian architect, typically featuring classical elements such as columns, pediments, and domes.|例句：The Palladian villa is a masterpiece of Renaissance architecture. 这座帕拉第奥风格的别墅是文艺复兴建筑的杰作。|例句：The architect incorporated Palladian elements into the design of the new library.  这位建筑师将帕拉第奥风格的元素融入到新图书馆的设计中。|近义词：neoclassical, classical|反义词:modern, contemporary|词性:形容词

palladian:/pəˈleɪdiən/| n.|帕拉第奥主义者，帕拉第奥风格的拥护者|An admirer or follower of the architectural style of Andrea Palladio.|例句：Many prominent architects of the 18th century were Palladian. 18世纪许多杰出的建筑师都是帕拉第奥主义者。|例句：He is a Palladian who believes in the timeless beauty of classical architecture. 他是一位帕拉第奥主义者，相信古典建筑的永恒之美。|近义词：classicist|反义词:modernist|词性:名词
