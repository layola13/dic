have-a-shot-at:/hæv ə ʃɒt æt/ | v. | 尝试；试一试；争取 | To attempt something, typically something challenging or ambitious.  |  例句：I've never played golf before, but I'll have a shot at it. 我以前从未打过高尔夫球，但我会尝试一下。

例句：He decided to have a shot at applying for the promotion. 他决定试着申请升职。| 近义词：try, attempt, give it a go, take a crack at, have a go at | 反义词：avoid, abstain from, refrain from | 词性: 动词短语
