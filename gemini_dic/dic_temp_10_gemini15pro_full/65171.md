ethnographies:/ɛθˈnɒɡrəfiz/
n.
民族志；人种志
A detailed description of a particular culture or group, based on fieldwork and observation.
The anthropologist conducted extensive fieldwork in the Amazon to compile her ethnographies of indigenous tribes.  这位人类学家在亚马逊河流域进行了广泛的田野调查，以汇编她对土著部落的民族志。
Ethnographies provide valuable insights into different ways of life. 民族志为了解不同的生活方式提供了宝贵的见解。
近义词:studies, accounts, reports
反义词:fiction
