outpoliticked:/ˌaʊtˈpɑːləˌtɪkt/
verb
以政治手腕胜过
to defeat someone by being more clever or skillful in political maneuvering
The young senator outpoliticked his more experienced opponent.  这位年轻的参议员以更老练的政治手腕击败了他的对手。
outsmart, outmaneuver
be outpoliticked
