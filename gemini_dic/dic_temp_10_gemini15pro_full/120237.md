mingle:/ˈmɪŋɡl/| n.|混合，交际，混合物|The act of mixing or associating with others.|The party was a mingle of different cultures.  这个派对是不同文化的交融。|近义词：mixture, blend, combination|反义词:separation, isolation|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *minglian, frequentative of mengan "to mix," from Proto-Germanic *mangjan, from PIE root *meik- "to mix."
