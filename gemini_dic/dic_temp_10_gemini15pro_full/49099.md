deify: /ˈdiːɪfaɪ/ (US) /ˈdeɪɪfaɪ/ (UK)| vt.|神化，奉若神明|To treat or regard someone or something as a god.|例句：The Romans used to deify their emperors. 罗马人过去常常神化他们的皇帝。

例句：Some fans deify their favorite musicians. 一些歌迷把他们最喜欢的音乐家奉若神明。

近义词：exalt, glorify, worship, idolize|反义词：vilify, denigrate, disparage|词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: *deifien*, from Old French *deifier*, from Latin *deificare*, from *deus* ‘god’ + *-ficare* (from *facere* ‘to make’).
