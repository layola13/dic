chandry:/ˈtʃændri/| n.|杂货店；食品储藏室|A chandry is a place where provisions and supplies, especially food, are stored or sold, often on a ship or in a port.|The ship's chandry was well-stocked for the long voyage. 船上的食品储藏室为这次长途航行准备了充足的物资。|The sailors visited the chandry in the port to buy fresh provisions. 水手们去了港口的杂货店购买新鲜的食品。|synonyms: grocery store, provision store, larder|antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "empty storehouse" or "pantry") |noun
