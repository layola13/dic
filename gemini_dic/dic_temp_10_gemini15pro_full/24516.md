broadbrim:/ˈbrɔːdbrɪm/| n.| 宽边帽 |A hat with a wide brim, often worn to protect from the sun. | The Amish man wore a traditional broadbrim. 阿米什人戴着一顶传统的宽边帽。| synonym: wide-brimmed hat | antonym: narrow-brimmed hat | noun
Etymology:  Compound of "broad" (wide) and "brim" (the edge of a hat).  "Broad" comes from Old English "brād," and "brim" comes from Middle English "brymme."
