microcosmic:/ˌmaɪkrəˈkɒzmɪk/ (US) /ˌmaɪkrəʊˈkɒzmɪk/ (UK)| adj.|微观的; 小宇宙的; 缩影的|A microcosm is a small thing that is seen as a small version of something much larger.|The village is a microcosm of the whole country.  这个村庄是整个国家的一个缩影。|synonyms: miniature, epitome, model, representation|antonyms: macrocosm, universe|adjective
