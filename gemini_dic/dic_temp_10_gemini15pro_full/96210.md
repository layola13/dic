indexed:/ˈɪndɛkst/| adj.|已索引的，已编入索引的|Having an index or indexes; included in an index.  Referring to something that has been entered into an index, a system for organizing and locating information. It can also refer to something adjusted or measured according to an index, such as a price or wage.|The library's collection is fully indexed.  图书馆的馆藏已全部编入索引。|近义词:cataloged, listed, classified|反义词:unindexed, uncataloged|词性:形容词
