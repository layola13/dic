clade:/kleɪd/| n.|进化枝，分支|A group of organisms believed to have evolved from a common ancestor, according to the principles of cladistics.|Birds form a clade within the larger group of vertebrates. 鸟类在更大的脊椎动物群体中形成了一个进化枝。|The clade of theropod dinosaurs includes the ancestors of modern birds. 兽脚亚目恐龙的进化枝包括现代鸟类的祖先。|近义词：group, taxon, branch|反义词:|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek κλάδος (kládos, “branch, shoot”).
