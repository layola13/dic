apery:/ˈeɪpəri/
n.
模仿; 仿效;  东施效颦; (尤指)滑稽模仿
An imitation or mimicking, often in a humorous or satirical manner, especially of the behavior or mannerisms of another person or group.
The comedian's apery of the president was hilarious.  这位喜剧演员对总统的滑稽模仿令人捧腹。
mimicry, imitation, parody, mockery
originality, authenticity, genuineness
noun
