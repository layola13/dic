gromet:/ˈɡrɒmɪt/ (UK) /ˈɡrɑːmɪt/ (US)| n.|索环，金属圈，橡胶圈|A grommet is a ring of rubber, metal, or plastic that is used to reinforce a hole in a piece of material, such as cloth or leather. It is also used to protect electrical wires or ropes that pass through the hole.|The grommets in the sails were rusting. 帆上的索环生锈了。|Synonyms: eyelet, ring, bushing|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

gromet:/ˈɡrɒmɪt/ (UK) /ˈɡrɑːmɪt/ (US)| v.|装索环|To fit a grommet into something.|He grometted the new sails yesterday. 他昨天给新帆装了索环。|Synonyms:  fit, install|Antonyms: remove|Verb
