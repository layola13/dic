goldberg: /ˈɡoʊldbɜːrɡ/ (US), /ˈɡəʊldbɜːɡ/ (UK)| n.|戈德堡; 戈德堡装置(指一种过度设计的装置，用以执行一项本来可以简单完成的任务); 戈德堡变奏曲|A Goldberg is a needlessly complex apparatus that performs a very simple task, named after cartoonist Rube Goldberg, who drew such contraptions.  It can also refer to the Goldberg Variations, a musical work by Johann Sebastian Bach.|He built a Goldberg to turn on the lights.|近义词: contraption, device, invention|反义词: simple tool, straightforward solution|词性:noun

goldbergian: /ɡoʊldˈbɜːrɡiən/ (US), /ɡəʊldˈbɜːɡiən/ (UK)| adj.|戈德堡式的; 复杂的; 精巧的|Describing something that is unnecessarily complex, like a Rube Goldberg machine.|The machine was a goldbergian contraption of levers and pulleys.|近义词: convoluted, intricate, complex|反义词: simple, straightforward, easy|词性:adjective
