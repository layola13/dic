folksong: /ˈfoʊksɒŋ/ (US) /ˈfəʊksɒŋ/ (UK) | n. | 民歌 | A song that originates in traditional popular culture or that is written in such a style. |  This concert features several Appalachian folksongs. 这场音乐会将表演几首阿巴拉契亚民歌。| synonym: traditional song, folk music, ballad | antonym: pop song, art song | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From folk + song.  "Folk" meaning "people" or "nation" has roots in Old English "folc." "Song" traces back to Old English "sang," from Proto-Germanic "*sangaz."
