confesser:/kənˈfɛsər/ | n. |忏悔者; 告解者; 承认者|A person who confesses something, especially sins to a priest. |例句：The confesser knelt before the priest. 忏悔者跪在牧师面前。

例句：He was a confesser to his crimes. 他是罪行的承认者。|近义词：penitent, admitter|反义词：denier, liar|词性:名词
