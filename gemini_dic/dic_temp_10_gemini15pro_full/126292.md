myocarditis:/ˌmaɪoʊkɑːrˈdaɪtɪs/ /ˌmaɪəʊkɑːˈdaɪtɪs/| n. |心肌炎|Inflammation of the myocardium, the muscular tissue of the heart.  This inflammation can be caused by infections, autoimmune diseases, or reactions to medications.| He was diagnosed with myocarditis after experiencing chest pain and shortness of breath. 他在经历胸痛和呼吸急促后被诊断出患有心肌炎。|近义词:carditis|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From  myo-  +  -cardi-  +  -itis.
myo- :  Greek *mys*, muscle.
-cardi- :  Greek *kardia*, heart.
-itis :  Greek *-itis*, denoting inflammation.
