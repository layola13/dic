/ˈleɪbl/ /ˈleɪbəl/ | n. vt.| 标签，标记；称号，绰号 |A piece of paper, cloth, or other material that is attached to something and gives information about it.  To attach a label to something.| The label on the bottle says "milk." 瓶子上的标签写着“牛奶”。|  He labeled the box with her name. 他在盒子上贴上了她的名字。| tag, sticker, brand, title,  designation| slur, smear |noun, verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *label, diminutive of *labe "strip, ribbon," from Frankish *lappa, from Proto-Germanic *lappō.
