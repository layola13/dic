outquibbled:/ˌaʊtˈkwɪbəld/
verb
胜过诡辩, 在诡辩中胜过
To surpass or defeat someone in quibbling; to argue more cleverly or subtly than someone else.
He outquibbled his opponent in the debate and won the argument. 他在辩论中以诡辩胜过了对手，赢得了辩论。
近义词: outwit, outsmart, outmaneuver
反义词: be outwitted, be outsmarted
