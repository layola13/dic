outsourcing:/ˈaʊtsɔːrsɪŋ/
n.|外包，外部采购|The practice of obtaining goods or services from an outside or foreign supplier, especially in place of an internal source.|Many companies are outsourcing their IT operations to reduce costs. 许多公司将IT业务外包以降低成本。|offshoring, subcontracting|insourcing|noun

outsourced:/ˈaʊtsɔːrst/
adj.|外包的|Obtained from an outside or foreign supplier, especially in place of an internal source.|The company's customer service is outsourced to a call center in India. 该公司的客户服务外包给印度的一个呼叫中心。|offshored, subcontracted|insourced|adjective

outsources:/ˈaʊtsɔːrsɪz/
v.(outsource的第三人称单数形式)|外包，外部采购|Obtains goods or services from an outside or foreign supplier, especially in place of an internal source.|The company outsources its manufacturing to a factory in China.  这家公司将其制造业务外包给中国的一家工厂。|offshores, subcontracts|insources|verb
