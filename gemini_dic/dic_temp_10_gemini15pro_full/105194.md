keychains:/ˈkiːtʃeɪnz/
n.
钥匙链，钥匙圈
A small chain or ring used to hold keys.
She always carries a bunch of keychains on her bag. 她总是随身带着一大串钥匙链在她的包上。
I bought a souvenir keychain from my trip. 我从旅行中买了一个纪念品钥匙链。
key rings, fobs
(无)
