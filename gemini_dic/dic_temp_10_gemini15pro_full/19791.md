billycock:/ˈbɪlikɒk/ (UK) /ˈbɪliˌkɑːk/ (US)| n. |圆顶硬礼帽，小礼帽|A hard felt hat with a rounded crown and a narrow brim, typically worn by men in the 19th and early 20th centuries.  It's a type of bowler hat.| He wore a billycock at a jaunty angle. 他戴着一顶斜戴的圆顶硬礼帽。|近义词：bowler hat, derby hat|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "soft hat" as a general contrast) |词性:noun
