commutative:/kəˈmjuːtətɪv/ | adj. | 交换的，可交换的 |  Relating to or involving quantities whose product or sum is independent of the order in which they are multiplied or added.  | Addition is commutative: 5 + 3 = 3 + 5. |  interchangeable, permutable | noncommutative, non-interchangeable | adjective
