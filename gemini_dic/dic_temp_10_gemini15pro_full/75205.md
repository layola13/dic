froebelian:/froʊˈbiːliən/
adjective
adj. 弗雷贝尔的；幼儿园教育的
Of or relating to the educational theories of Friedrich Froebel, especially those concerning  kindergartens.
The school employed a Froebelian approach to early childhood education.  这所学校采用弗雷贝尔式的幼儿教育方法。
近义词:kindergarten, preschool
反义词:None
词性:adjective


froebelian:/froʊˈbiːliən/
noun
n. 弗雷贝尔的信徒；幼儿园教师
A follower of or advocate of the educational theories of Friedrich Froebel.
She was a dedicated Froebelian and believed in learning through play. 她是一位忠诚的弗雷贝尔信徒，相信通过游戏学习。
近义词:kindergarten teacher, preschool teacher
反义词:None
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Friedrich Fröbel (1782-1852), German educator + -ian.
