graylag:/ˈɡreɪlæɡ/| n.|灰雁|A large gray goose (Anser anser) native to Eurasia, the ancestor of most domestic geese.|例句：The graylag goose is a common sight in wetlands. 灰雁在湿地很常见。

例句：We observed a flock of graylags taking flight. 我们观察到一群灰雁起飞。|近义词：goose, wild goose|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

Etymology->
From Old English *grālag, from grā "gray" + *lag "slow, sluggish," referring to the bird's less agile flight compared to other geese.
