athleticism: /æθˈlɛtɪˌsɪzəm/
n.|运动能力，体育素质，运动天赋|The quality of being athletic, especially in terms of physical strength, agility, and stamina.|She displayed impressive athleticism on the playing field. 她在运动场上展现了令人印象深刻的运动能力。|近义词：fitness, athletic ability, prowess, physicality|反义词：clumsiness, awkwardness|词性:名词

词源： From athletic + -ism.  "athletic" comes from the Greek "athletikos," meaning "pertaining to athletes or contests." "-ism" is a suffix forming nouns of action, state, or quality.
