beanbag:/ˈbiːnˌbæɡ/| n.|豆袋椅，豆袋|A soft seat consisting of a large fabric bag filled with dried beans, polystyrene beads, or similar material.|She relaxed on the beanbag chair, reading a book. 她舒服地躺在豆袋椅上看书。|Synonyms: beanbag chair,  sac, cushion|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "rigid chair")|noun

beanbag:/ˈbiːnˌbæɡ/| adj.(rarely used)|像豆袋的|Resembling or characteristic of a beanbag. |The toy had a beanbag-like texture. 这个玩具有着类似豆袋的质感。|Synonyms: beanbaggy|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|adjective
