agleam:/əˈɡliːm/
adjective
闪闪发光的；闪亮的
Shining brightly; gleaming.
The knight's armor was agleam in the sunlight. 骑士的盔甲在阳光下闪闪发光。
Her eyes were agleam with excitement. 她的眼睛闪耀着兴奋的光芒。

近义词:
gleaming, shining, sparkling, glittering, radiant, luminous

反义词:
dull, dim, lackluster, dark


词性:
adjective

词源:
From a- + gleam.  "A-" acts as an intensifier.
