graininesses:/ˈɡreɪninɪsɪz/
n.
粒状，颗粒状;粗糙
The quality of appearing grainy.  The state of being composed of or resembling grains.
The photograph suffered from excessive graininess. 这张照片颗粒感太强了。
granularity, coarseness
smoothness, fineness
noun
