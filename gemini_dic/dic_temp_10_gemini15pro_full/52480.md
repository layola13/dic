dietitian:/ˌdaɪəˈtɪʃən/ | n. | 营养师；饮食学家 | A health professional who gives people advice about what to eat in order to be healthy. |  A dietitian helped her create a meal plan. 一位营养师帮助她制定了膳食计划。 | synonyms: nutritionist, dietician | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "glutton" or someone who promotes unhealthy eating, but these are not true opposites) | noun

dietitian:/ˌdaɪəˈtɪʃən/ | n. | 营养师；饮食学家 |  A person who is an expert on nutrition and advises people on what to eat. | The dietitian recommended a balanced diet. 营养师建议均衡饮食。| synonyms: nutritionist, dietician | antonyms:  (No direct antonym) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *diététicien*, from *diète* ("diet"), from Latin *diaeta*, from Ancient Greek *δίαιτα* (*diaita*, "manner of living").
