dextrer:/ˈdɛkstər/ | adj. | 灵巧的，熟练的 | Skillful and adept in the use of hands; right-handed. | He is dextrer with a needle and thread. 他针线活很灵巧。| 近义词: adroit, deft, skillful, adept | 反义词: clumsy, awkward, inept | 词性: 形容词
词源: From Latin *dexter* "right, favorable," also "skillful."  The connection with "right" likely stems from the observation that most people are right-handed and therefore more skillful with that hand.
