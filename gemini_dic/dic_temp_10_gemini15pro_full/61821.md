empirical:/ɪmˈpɪrɪkəl/| adj.|经验主义的，以经验为依据的|Based on, concerned with, or verifiable by observation or experience rather than theory or pure logic.|例句：Empirical evidence suggests that regular exercise can reduce the risk of heart disease. 经验表明，经常锻炼可以降低患心脏病的风险。|近义词：observed, experimental, practical, factual|反义词：theoretical, hypothetical, conjectural|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *empiricus*, from Greek *empeirikós* "experienced," from *empeiría* "experience," from *en* "in" + *peîra* "trial, experiment."
