nymph-like:/ˈnɪmfˌlaɪk/| adj.|仙女般的，像仙女的|Resembling or characteristic of a nymph, especially in grace, beauty, or delicate form.  This evokes a sense of youthful beauty, often associated with nature and mythology.|Her nymph-like appearance captivated everyone in the room. 她仙女般的外貌迷住了房间里的每一个人。|Synonyms: ethereal, graceful, delicate, fairy-like|Antonyms: coarse, clumsy, awkward|adjective
