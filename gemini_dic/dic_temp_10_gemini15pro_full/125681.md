mundaneness:/mʌnˈdeɪnnəs/
|n.| 平凡；世俗；单调|The mundaneness of everyday life can sometimes be overwhelming. 日常生活的平淡无奇有时会让人难以承受。|
|例句：He found escape from the mundaneness of his job in writing poetry. 他在写诗中找到了逃避平淡工作的途径。|
|例句：She yearned for a life less marked by mundaneness. 她渴望过一种不那么平淡的生活。|
|近义词：ordinariness, commonness, banality, dullness|
|反义词：excitement, extraordinariness, uniqueness|
|词性：名词|
|词源：From mundane + -ness.  Mundane comes from Old French mondain, from Late Latin mundānus "belonging to the world," from Latin mundus "world, universe."|
