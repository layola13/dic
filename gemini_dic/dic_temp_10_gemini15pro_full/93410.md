illiteracy:/ɪˈlɪtərəsi/
n.|文盲；无知；缺乏教育|The high rate of illiteracy in the country is a major obstacle to development.  这个国家的高文盲率是发展的主要障碍。|Illiteracy can make it difficult to find employment. 文盲会使找工作变得困难。|近义词：ignorance, unlearnedness|反义词：literacy, education|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *illiteratus* "unlettered, uneducated," from in- "not" + literatus "lettered," past participle of literare "to instruct in letters," from litera/littera "letter."
