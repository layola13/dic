crimsony:/ˈkrɪmzəni/| adj.|深红色的，绯红色的|Of a rich deep red color inclining to purple.|The sunset painted the sky in crimsony hues. 夕阳将天空染成了深红色。|近义词：scarlet, ruby, crimson, vermilion|反义词:pale, light|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *crimsin*, from Old French *cremesin*, from Medieval Latin *cremesinus* (whence also Italian *cremesino*, Spanish *carmesí*), from Arabic *qirmiz* (“kermes”), from Sanskrit *kṛmi* (“worm, insect”).  The source is the kermes insect, from which a red dye was obtained.
