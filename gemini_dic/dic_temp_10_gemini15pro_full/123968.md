morisk: /ˈmɔːrɪsk/ | n. | 摩尔式舞; 摩尔人的服饰; | A Moorish dance performed by men in the 15th and 16th centuries, often with blackened faces and bells on their legs. A type of cloth or tapestry with a Moorish design.  |  The morisk was a popular entertainment at court. 摩尔式舞是宫廷里流行的娱乐活动。  The noble wore a robe of rich morisk. 这位贵族穿着一件华丽的摩尔式长袍。 | synonym: Morris dance, Moorish dance, moresque | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "European dance" in a specific context) | noun
