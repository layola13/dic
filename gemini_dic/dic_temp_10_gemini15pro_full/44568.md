crouch:/kraʊtʃ/| v. n.|蹲伏，蜷缩；奉承，卑躬屈膝|To stoop or bend low, especially with the knees bent.|The cat crouched in the grass, ready to pounce. 猫蹲伏在草丛中，准备突袭。|近义词：squat, stoop, bend, cower|反义词：stand, rise, straighten|词性:动词/名词
crouch:/kraʊtʃ/| n.|蹲伏的姿势|The act of crouching.|He adopted a crouch behind the wall. 他在墙后采取了蹲伏的姿势。|近义词：squat, stoop|反义词：standing position|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *crouchir* (modern French *crochir*), from a Vulgar Latin source akin to Latin *croccus* "hook," perhaps from the image of a bent posture resembling a hook.
