fumado:/fuˈmɑːdoʊ/ (Brazilian Portuguese)
adjective
烟熏的；熏制的；吸烟的
smoked; cured by smoking; a person who smokes
例句：The restaurant is known for its fumado meats. 这家餐厅以其烟熏肉类而闻名。

例句：Ele é um fumado inveterado. 他是一个老烟枪。(Brazilian Portuguese)

近义词：smoked, cured, smoker (as a noun)
反义词：unsmoked, fresh (for food) , non-smoker (as a noun)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Portuguese *fumar* ("to smoke"), from Latin *fumus* ("smoke").

注意事项：
* "Fumado" is primarily used in Brazilian Portuguese. In other varieties of Portuguese, "defumado" is more common.
*  While "fumado" can technically describe a person who smokes,  "fumante" is more commonly used for this meaning in Brazilian Portuguese.
