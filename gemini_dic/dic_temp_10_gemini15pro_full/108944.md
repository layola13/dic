leenan: /ˈliːnən/ | n. | 斜纹亚麻布；亚麻制品；亚麻线 | Linen is a fabric made from the fibers of the flax plant. | She wore a cool linen suit. 她穿着一套凉爽的亚麻套装。 | 近义词: flax, cloth | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *linen, from Old English *līnen (attested only in līnen hrægl (“linen garment”)), from Proto-West Germanic *līnīn, from Proto-Germanic *līniną (“linen, made of flax”), from *līną (“flax”), from Proto-Indo-European *līnom (“flax, linen”). Cognate with West Frisian linnen (“linen”), Dutch linnen (“linen”), German Leinen (“linen”), Danish linned (“linen”), Swedish linne (“linen”).
