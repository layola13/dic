bidentate: /baɪˈdɛnˌteɪt/ | adj. | 双齿的，二齿的 | Having two teeth or tooth-like projections, especially used in chemistry to describe a ligand that can bond to a central metal atom through two donor atoms. |  Ethylenediamine is a bidentate ligand. 乙二胺是双齿配体。|  The bidentate ligand formed a stable complex with the metal ion.  这个双齿配体与金属离子形成了稳定的络合物。|  didentate, chelating | monodentate | adjective
