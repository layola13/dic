insidious:/ɪnˈsɪdiəs/| adj.|阴险的；暗中为害的；潜伏的；(疾病)隐袭的|Proceeding in a gradual, subtle way, but with harmful effects.  Developing so gradually as to be well established before becoming apparent.|The insidious effects of stress can take years to show up. 压力带来的潜在危害可能需要数年才会显现出来。|Synonyms: treacherous, stealthy, cunning, subtle, deceptive|Antonyms: obvious, overt, benign, harmless|adjective


insidious:/ɪnˈsɪdiəs/| adj.|阴险的；暗中为害的；潜伏的；(疾病)隐袭的|Working or spreading harmfully in a subtle or stealthy manner. |The disease is insidious and often goes undetected for years. 这种疾病是隐袭的，而且经常多年未被发现。|Synonyms:  surreptitious, artful, wily|Antonyms:  honest, forthright, direct|adjective
