boxy:/ˈbɒksi/ (UK) /ˈbɑːksi/ (US)| adj.|四四方方的；像盒子的；矮胖的；（尤指汽车）笨重的|Resembling a box in shape; square or squat.  Often used to describe cars that are angular and not aerodynamic.|The car was a bit boxy and not very stylish. 这辆车有点四四方方，不太时尚。|synonyms: square, blocky, chunky, squat, dumpy|antonyms: sleek, streamlined, aerodynamic|adjective
