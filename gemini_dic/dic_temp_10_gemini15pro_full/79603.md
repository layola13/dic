glorifies:/ˈɡlɔːrɪfaɪz/| v. |赞美，颂扬，美化|To describe or represent something as admirable, especially in a way that is exaggerated.|The movie glorifies violence and makes it seem heroic. 这部电影美化暴力，使暴力看起来很英勇。|Synonyms: extol, praise, laud, celebrate, commend, exalt|Antonyms: denigrate, disparage, belittle, criticize, condemn|verb
