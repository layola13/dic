paralogism: /ˌpærəˈlɒdʒɪzəm/ (US) /ˌpærəˈlɒdʒɪzəm/ (UK)| n. |谬论；诡辩|A flaw in reasoning that makes an argument invalid, often appearing superficially sound.|例句：His argument was full of paralogisms and therefore unconvincing. 他的论点充满了谬误，因此没有说服力。

例句：The student's essay was riddled with paralogisms, demonstrating a lack of understanding of the subject.  这个学生的文章充满了谬论，表明他对这个主题缺乏理解。

|近义词：fallacy, sophism, error in reasoning|反义词: sound argument, valid reasoning|词性:noun
