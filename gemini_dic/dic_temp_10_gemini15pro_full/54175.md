disequality:/ˌdɪsɪˈkwɒləti/ /ˌdɪsɪˈkwɑːləti/ | n. | 不平等，不均等 | Lack of equality; difference.  A noticeable and unfair difference between people or things. |The report highlights the disequality in access to healthcare between urban and rural areas. 这份报告强调了城乡之间医疗保健机会的不平等。|synonyms: disparity, imbalance, inequality, unevenness, disproportion, asymmetry | antonyms: equality, parity, balance, uniformity | noun

disequilibrium:/ˌdɪsɪˈkwɪlɪbriəm/ /ˌdɪsɪˈkwɪˈlɪbriəm/ | n. | 不平衡，失衡 | A loss or lack of equilibrium or stability; instability. A state of imbalance. |The sudden change in temperature caused a disequilibrium in the ecosystem. 温度的骤变导致了生态系统的不平衡。| synonyms: imbalance, instability, unsteadiness, precariousness | antonyms: equilibrium, balance, stability, steadiness | noun
