couch:/kaʊtʃ/
|v.|表达；措辞|She couched her response in carefully chosen words. 她用精心挑选的词语表达了她的回应。|例句：He couched his complaint in strong terms. 他以强烈的措辞表达了他的抱怨。

例句：The message was couched in simple language so everyone could understand. 这条信息是用简单的语言表达的，以便每个人都能理解。

|近义词：express, phrase, word, frame|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑其含义的反面，例如：garble 使含糊不清, misstate  错误陈述)|词性:动词


词源: From Old French coucher "to lie down," from Latin collocare "to place, arrange," from com- "together" + locare "to place." Originally meaning "to lay down," the sense extended to "express in a certain manner" because of the notion of embedding or laying something within a particular framework.
