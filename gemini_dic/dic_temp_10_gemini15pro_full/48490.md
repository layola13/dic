deepeningly:/ˈdiːpənɪŋli/
adverb
加深地，更深入地
in a manner that becomes increasingly deep or profound.
The crisis is deepeningly affecting the economy. 这场危机正在日益加深地影响着经济。
The conversation grew deepeningly personal.  谈话变得越来越私密。
近义词: increasingly, progressively, further
反义词: superficially, shallowly
词性: adverb
词源: From "deepen" + "-ing" + "-ly". "Deepen" comes from Old English "dēopian," meaning "to make deep."
