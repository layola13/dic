cavally:/ˈkævəli/ /kəˈvæli/ | n. |鲯鳅（鱼）; 军马，骑兵（古语）| A large marine fish (Caranx hippos) related to the jacks, found in warm Atlantic waters.  Also, an archaic term for cavalry (mounted soldiers). |例句：The fisherman landed a huge cavally. 渔夫钓到了一条巨大的鲯鳅。

例句：The cavally made a powerful run, stripping line from the reel.  这条鲯鳅用力挣扎，把卷轴上的线都拉走了。


近义词：crevalle jack, horse mackerel (for the fish); cavalry, horsemen (archaic meaning) |反义词:  None (for the fish); infantry, foot soldiers (archaic contrasting term)|词性:noun
