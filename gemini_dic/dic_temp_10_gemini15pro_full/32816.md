chokeable:/ˈtʃoʊkəbəl/
adjective
易窒息的；容易噎住的
Something that is chokeable is small enough to be swallowed or inhaled and block the airway, causing choking.
Grapes are chokeable for young children. 葡萄对幼儿来说容易造成窒息。
synonyms: hazardous, dangerous, risky
antonyms: safe, harmless
词性: 形容词
