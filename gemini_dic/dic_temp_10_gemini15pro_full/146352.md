overfire:/ˌoʊvərˈfaɪər/ | v. | 过度燃烧，烧过头 | To burn fuel excessively, especially in a furnace or boiler, resulting in wasted fuel and potential damage. | The boiler overfired and damaged the firebricks. 锅炉过度燃烧，损坏了耐火砖。|  The steak was overfired and tasted burnt. 牛排烤过头了，尝起来有烧焦的味道。| 近义词: overheat, scorch | 反义词: underfire |词性: 动词
