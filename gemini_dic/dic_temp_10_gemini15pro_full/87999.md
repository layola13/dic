high-rises:/ˈhaɪˌraɪzɪz/
n.
高楼大厦
Tall buildings; buildings having many stories.
The city skyline is dominated by high-rises.  这座城市的天际线被高楼大厦所占据。
Many high-rises have been built in the city center recently. 最近市中心建起了许多高楼大厦。
近义词: skyscrapers, towers, multistory buildings
反义词: low-rises, bungalows


ETYMOLOGY->
From 'high' + 'rise' (noun, meaning an upward slope or increase). The compound 'high-rise' emerged in the early 20th century with the development of skyscraper technology.
