cucurbitaceous:/ˌkjuːkərˈbɪteɪʃəs/
adjective
葫芦科的
Relating to or belonging to the gourd family, Cucurbitaceae, which includes plants such as cucumbers, pumpkins, and melons.
Pumpkins, cucumbers, and watermelons are all cucurbitaceous plants.  南瓜、黄瓜和西瓜都是葫芦科植物。
Synonyms: gourd-like, cucurbit
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could use terms like non-cucurbitaceous or belonging to a different plant family)
词性: adjective
