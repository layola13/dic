betterments:/ˈbetərmənts/
n.
改进，改良；[常用复数]房屋等的改建；[常用复数]设备的添置
improvements or additions to something, especially to a building or piece of land.

The company invested heavily in betterments to its infrastructure.
该公司在其基础设施的改进上投入了巨资。

New betterments, such as the addition of a swimming pool, increased the value of the property.
新的改进，例如增加游泳池，增加了房产的价值。

synonyms: improvements, enhancements, upgrades, ameliorations
antonyms: deterioration, decline, worsening, decay
noun
