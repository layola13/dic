nonfricative:/ˌnɑːnˈfrɪkətɪv/ /ˌnɒnˈfrɪkətɪv/| adj. n.|非摩擦音|A nonfricative is a speech sound, such as a vowel or nasal, produced without audible friction.  It's also called a sonorant.|The sounds /m/ and /n/ are nonfricatives.|近义词:sonorant, resonant|反义词:fricative|词性:adjective, noun

nonfricative:/ˌnɑːnˈfrɪkətɪv/ /ˌnɒnˈfrɪkətɪv/| n.|非摩擦音|A nonfricative sound.|The word "bat" begins with a nonfricative.|近义词:sonorant, resonant|反义词:fricative|词性:noun
