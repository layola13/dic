oylet: /ˈɔɪlɪt/
noun
金属扣眼;索环;穿孔
A small hole, edged with metal, in leather, cloth, etc., for a lace or cord to pass through.
The canvas cover was secured to the frame with brass oylets. 帆布罩用黄铜扣眼固定在框架上。
Synonyms: grommet, eyelet
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical object like an eyelet)
词性: 名词
