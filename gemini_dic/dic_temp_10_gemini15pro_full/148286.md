oversocial:/ˌōvərˈsōʃəl/
adjective
过分社交的，过度社交的
Excessively social; seeking or enjoying social interaction to an excessive degree.  Characterized by a strong desire for social contact, sometimes to the point of being intrusive or inappropriate.  Often used in the context of individuals with autism or other neurodevelopmental conditions who might struggle with social boundaries.
He's a bit oversocial, always needing to be around people. 他有点过度社交，总是需要和人们在一起。
She found the party overwhelming because it was so oversocial. 她觉得聚会让人难以承受，因为太社交了。
近义词: gregarious, outgoing, sociable, extroverted
反义词: antisocial, withdrawn, introverted, reclusive
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive, too much") + "social" (adjective from Latin "socialis" meaning "companionable")
