charmeress:/ˈtʃɑːməres/| n. |女巫; 迷人的女人; |A woman who uses charm to influence others, often in a manipulative way. |例句：She was a charmeress, able to get anyone to do what she wanted. 她是一个迷人的女人，能够让任何人做她想做的事。

例句：The charmeress used her wit and beauty to deceive the king.  这个女巫用她的智慧和美貌欺骗了国王。

近义词：siren, temptress, enchantress, seductress|反义词:bore, shrew|词性:noun

charm:/tʃɑːrm/| n. vt. |魅力;咒语;吸引;迷住|The power or quality of pleasing or attracting others.|例句：He was charmed by her beauty and wit. 他被她的美貌和智慧迷住了。

例句：The snake charmer used his charm to control the cobra.  耍蛇人用他的咒语控制眼镜蛇。

近义词：appeal, charisma, allure, magic, spell|反义词:repulsion, unpleasantness|词性:noun, verb
