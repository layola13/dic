indefensibility:/ˌɪndɪˌfɛnsɪˈbɪləti/
n.|不可辩护，无法辩护|The indefensibility of his position became clear. 他的立场站不住脚，这一点变得很明显。|例句：The indefensibility of their actions led to widespread condemnation. 他们的行为不可辩护，导致了广泛的谴责。|近义词：untenable, unjustifiable, inexcusable|反义词：justifiable, defensible, excusable|词性:名词
Etymology: From in- (not) + defensible + -ity (noun suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *defendere* (to defend).
