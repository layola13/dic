desolder:/dɪˈsɑːldər/ /diːˈsɒldə(r)/| vt.|拆焊，去焊|To remove solder from (something), especially in order to disconnect electronic components.|I had to desolder the connection before replacing the faulty capacitor. 我不得不先拆焊连接点，然后再更换有故障的电容器。|Synonyms: unsolder|Antonyms: solder|Verb

desolder:/dɪˈsɑːldər/ /diːˈsɒldə(r)/| n.|拆焊剂|A substance used to remove solder.|I used a desoldering pump and some desolder to remove the chip. 我用了一个拆焊泵和一些拆焊剂来移除芯片。|Synonyms: unsoldering flux, solder remover|Antonyms: solder|Noun
