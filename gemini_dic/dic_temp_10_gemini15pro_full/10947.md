aray:/əˈreɪ/ | n. | 队列，数组 | An ordered arrangement of objects, numbers, or other items, typically in rows and columns. |  The aray of colorful flowers was breathtaking.  五彩缤纷的花卉队列令人叹为观止。

| synonyms: array, arrangement, collection, formation, line, order, sequence, series, set | antonyms: disorder, chaos, mess | noun


Etymology: From Old French *areiier "to arrange, put in order," from Vulgar Latin *adregiare, from Latin ad- "to" + regere "to direct, guide, keep straight."
