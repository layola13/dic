frequent:/frɪˈkwent/ (US), /frɪˈkwent/ (UK)| v.|常去，常到|to visit a place often|例句：He frequents the local pub. 他经常光顾当地的酒吧。|近义词：patronize, haunt|反义词: avoid, shun|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *frequent*, from Latin *frequēns*, *frequent-* (“crowded, numerous, frequent, often”), from *farciō* (“I stuff, cram”).
