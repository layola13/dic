disseverment:/dɪˈsɛvəmənt/
n.|分裂，分离；切断；割裂|The disseverment of the empire resulted in several smaller, independent nations.  帝国的分裂导致了几个更小的独立国家的出现。|The surgical disseverment of the artery was a delicate procedure.  外科手术切断动脉是一个精细的程序。|
近义词:separation, division, rupture, detachment, disjunction
反义词:union, connection, juncture, fusion
词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *disseveramentum, from disseverāre “to sever completely,” from dis- “apart” + severāre “to separate, sever,” from severus “serious, grave, strict, severe.”
