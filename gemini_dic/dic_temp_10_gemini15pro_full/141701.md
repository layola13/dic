or:/ɔːr/ (ɔr in some dialects)| conj.|或者，还是|Used to connect two or more possibilities or choices.  It indicates alternatives.|Do you want tea or coffee? 你想喝茶还是咖啡？|Synonyms: alternatively, otherwise|Antonyms: and, nor|Conjunction

or:/ɔːr/ (ɔr in some dialects)| conj.|或者，否则（用于否定句后）|Used after a negative statement to introduce a second negative statement that adds to the first, expressing the result of not doing the first thing.|Hurry up, or you'll be late.  快点，否则你会迟到。|Synonyms: otherwise, else|Antonyms: and|Conjunction
