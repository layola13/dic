chyluria:/kaɪˈljʊəriə/
noun
乳糜尿
The presence of chyle in the urine.  This gives the urine a milky appearance.
Chyle is a lymphatic fluid that contains fats absorbed from the small intestine.
例句：The patient's chyluria was caused by a blockage in the lymphatic system. 病人的乳糜尿是由淋巴系统阻塞引起的。

例句：The doctor diagnosed chyluria after observing the milky appearance of the patient's urine sample. 医生观察到病人尿液样本呈乳白色后，诊断为乳糜尿。
