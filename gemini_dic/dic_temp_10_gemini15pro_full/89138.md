homecoming:/ˈhoʊmkʌmɪŋ/| n.| 返乡；回家；校友返校日|A homecoming is the return of someone to their home after a long absence.  It can also refer to an annual event held by a high school, college, or university for alumni.|例句：The town held a big homecoming celebration for the returning soldiers.  小镇为返回的士兵举行了盛大的欢迎仪式。

例句：The high school homecoming dance is next weekend.  高中校友返校舞会就在下个周末。|近义词：return, arrival|反义词：departure, leaving|词性: 名词
