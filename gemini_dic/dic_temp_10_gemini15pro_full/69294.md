features:/ˈfiːtʃərz/ (US) /ˈfiːtʃəz/ (UK)| n. (plural)| 特征，特点；容貌；面貌；（报纸或杂志的）特写；专题节目|A distinctive attribute or aspect of something; a prominent part or characteristic of the face.|The car has some interesting new features. 这辆车有一些有趣的新特性。|Synonyms: characteristics, traits, attributes, qualities, aspects, properties|Antonyms: flaws, defects|Noun

features:/ˈfiːtʃərz/ (US) /ˈfiːtʃəz/ (UK)| v. (third-person singular simple present)| 以…为特色；是…的特色；突出；由…主演|To have as a prominent or distinctive feature or aspect.|The film features several well-known actors. 这部电影由几位著名演员主演。|Synonyms: highlight, showcase, spotlight, star, present|Antonyms: downplay, minimize|Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *faiture (“fashion, shape”), from Latin factūra (“a making, a doing”), from faciō (“I make, I do”).
