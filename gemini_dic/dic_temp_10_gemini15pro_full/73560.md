forestall:/fɔːrˈstɔːl/ /fɔːrˈstɑːl/
verb
预先阻止，先发制人
to prevent something from happening by acting first
The company forestalls complaints by providing excellent customer service.  这家公司通过提供优质的客户服务来防止投诉发生。

The police forestall the riot by increasing patrols. 警察通过增加巡逻来防止骚乱。

近义词: prevent, preclude, obviate, avert, anticipate
反义词:  precipitate, hasten, expedite, promote, encourage
词性: verb
