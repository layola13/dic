dishevel:/dɪˈʃevəl/| v. |使蓬乱，使凌乱|To make (someone's hair or clothes) untidy.|The wind disheveled her hair. 风吹乱了她的头发。|近义词：rumple, tousle, disorder|反义词:tidy, neaten, groom|词性:动词

disheveled:/dɪˈʃevəld/| adj. |蓬乱的，凌乱的| (of a person's hair, clothes, or appearance) untidy; disordered.|He looked disheveled after his long journey.  长途旅行后，他看起来很邋遢。|近义词：untidy, messy, rumpled|反义词:tidy, neat, orderly|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *descheveler, from des- (expressing reversal) + chevel (hair), from Latin capillus (hair).
