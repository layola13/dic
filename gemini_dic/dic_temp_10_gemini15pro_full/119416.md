microfilms: /ˈmaɪkrəʊfɪlmz/ | n. |缩微胶片|A film on which materials are photographed at greatly reduced size.| The library has a large collection of historical newspapers on microfilm.  图书馆收藏了大量缩微胶卷形式的历史报纸。|近义词：microcopies, microphotographs |反义词: enlargements| noun


microfilm: /ˈmaɪkrəʊfɪlm/ | v. | 拍摄缩微胶片|To photograph or copy onto microfilm.|  They microfilmed the old records for safekeeping.  他们把旧记录拍成缩微胶卷保存。|近义词：microphotograph |反义词: enlarge| verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From micro- + film. "Micro-" comes from Greek "mikros" meaning "small". "Film" comes from Old English "fylmen" meaning "membrane, skin".
