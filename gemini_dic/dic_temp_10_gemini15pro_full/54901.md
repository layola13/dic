disportment:/dɪˈspɔːrtmənt/
n.|娱乐，嬉戏|An activity that creates enjoyment and amusement.  A way of behaving, comportment.|She watched the children's disportment in the park with a smile. 她笑着看着孩子们在公园里嬉戏。|近义词：play, amusement, recreation, pastime, diversion, entertainment, merriment, frolic, gambol, romp, revelry, festivity; comportment, demeanor, conduct|反义词:work, labor, toil|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Middle English disporten, from Old French desporter, deporter (“to divert, amuse, entertain, cheer, gladden, comfort, solace”), from des- (intensive prefix) + porter (“to carry, bear, bring, wear”).
