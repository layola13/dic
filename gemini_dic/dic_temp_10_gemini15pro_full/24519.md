broadcaster:/ˈbrɔːdkɑːstər/ (US) /ˈbrɔːdkɑːstə(r)/ (UK)| n.|广播员；播音员；广播公司|A person or organization that transmits radio or television programs.|The BBC is a well-known broadcaster.  英国广播公司是一家著名的广播公司。|Example: The broadcaster announced the breaking news. 播音员播报了突发新闻。|Synonyms: announcer, commentator, newscaster, station|Antonyms: receiver, audience|noun

broadcaster:/ˈbrɔːdkɑːstər/ (US) /ˈbrɔːdkɑːstə(r)/ (UK)| n.|撒播者；散播者|Someone or something that spreads something widely.|The farmer is a broadcaster of seeds. 农民是种子的播种者。|Example: The internet is a powerful broadcaster of information. 互联网是一个强大的信息传播者。|Synonyms: disseminator, spreader|Antonyms: collector, gatherer|noun
