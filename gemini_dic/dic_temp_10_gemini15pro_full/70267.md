fidalgo:/fɪˈdælɡoʊ/
n.
贵族（尤指葡萄牙或西班牙的）
A nobleman, especially of Portuguese or Spanish origin.
Don Quixote, though impoverished, considered himself a fidalgo. 堂吉诃德虽然贫困，却认为自己是一位贵族。
nobleman, gentleman, aristocrat
commoner, peasant
noun
