amusements:/əˈmjuːzmənts/
|n.|娱乐活动，消遣，娱乐场所|Activities or places that provide entertainment or enjoyment.|The park offered a variety of amusements for children. 公园为孩子们提供了各种各样的娱乐活动。|synonyms: entertainments, diversions, recreations|antonyms: boredom, monotony|noun

amusement:/əˈmjuːzmənt/
|n.|娱乐，乐趣，消遣|The feeling of being entertained or enjoying something.|She watched the performance with great amusement. 她饶有兴致地观看了演出。|synonyms: entertainment, pleasure, enjoyment|antonyms: boredom, displeasure|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "amuser" (to entertain, divert), from "a-" (to) + "muser" (to muse, loiter).  The "-ment" suffix turns the verb into a noun.
