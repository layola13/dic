felled:/feld/
|v. (past tense and past participle of fell)|砍倒；打倒；击倒|To cut down (a tree). To knock someone down, especially in a fight or sporting contest.|The storm felled several large trees in the park.  暴风雨把公园里的几棵大树刮倒了。|The boxer felled his opponent with a single punch. 拳击手一拳就把对手击倒了。|近义词：cut down, knock down, topple, prostrate|反义词:raise, erect|词性:动词

词源:
From Old English *fellan (attested only in *afellan "to fell"), from Proto-Germanic *fallaną (source also of Old Saxon fellian "to fell", Old Norse fella "to fell, hew down", Middle Dutch vellen "to fell", Old High German fallan "to fall", German fällen "to fell"), from Proto-Indo-European *pol- "to fall".  The original sense was "to cause to fall".
