clubbish:/ˈklʌbɪʃ/| adj.|小集团的，排外的，抱团的|Suggesting or characterized by the attitudes and behavior of a private club; exclusive.|例句：They have a rather clubbish atmosphere in that office. 他们在办公室里的氛围相当抱团。

例句：The all-male club had a very clubbish feel, making it hard for outsiders to feel welcome.  这个纯男性的俱乐部有一种很强的抱团感，让外人很难感到受欢迎。|近义词：cliquish, exclusive, clannish|反义词：inclusive, welcoming, open|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From club + -ish.  Meaning "like or characteristic of a club," especially in the sense of being exclusive or restricted.
