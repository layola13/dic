faddiest:/ˈfædiɪst/
adjective
最时尚的；最流行的；最时髦的
Most fashionable; most trendy; most stylish.
例句：She always wears the faddiest clothes. 她总是穿着最时髦的衣服。
例句：That style is so faddiest; it'll be gone next week. 那种款式太流行了，下周就过时了。
近义词：trendiest, most fashionable, stylish, chic
反义词：classic, timeless, unfashionable, outdated
词性: adjective

fad:/fæd/
noun
一时流行的狂热；时尚；风尚
A craze or enthusiasm for something, especially one that is short-lived; a fashion.
例句：The latest fad is to wear brightly colored socks. 最新的时尚是穿颜色鲜艳的袜子。
例句：He's always following the latest fads. 他总是追逐最新的潮流。
近义词：trend, craze, fashion, vogue
反义词：tradition, custom, standard

词源：
From the early 20th century, possibly a shortening of "fadaise," meaning a trifle or foolish notion.
