cryptogamic:/ˌkrɪptəˈɡæmɪk/ | adj. | 隐花植物的；隐孢子的 | Relating to cryptogams, plants that reproduce by spores, without flowers or seeds. |  Cryptogamic plants include ferns, mosses, and algae. 隐花植物包括蕨类植物、苔藓和藻类。 | synonyms: spore-bearing, flowerless | antonyms: flowering, phanerogamic | adjective

cryptogamic:/ˌkrɪptəˈɡæmɪk/ | n. | 隐花植物 | A cryptogam; a plant that reproduces by spores, without flowers or seeds. | The forest floor was covered in cryptogamics. 森林地面上覆盖着隐花植物。 | synonyms: cryptogam | antonyms: phanerogam | noun
