antistatism:/ˌæntiˈsteɪtɪzəm/ | n. |反国家主义，反国家干预主义 | Opposition to state intervention in economic and social affairs, often advocating for individual liberty and limited government. |  Example 1:  The libertarian's antistatism led him to believe that the government should have minimal involvement in the economy.  自由主义者的反国家主义使他相信政府应该尽量减少对经济的干预。

Example 2: Her antistatism was rooted in her belief that individuals are best equipped to manage their own lives. 她的反国家主义根植于她认为个人最有能力管理自己生活的信念。| 近义词: libertarianism, individualism, anarchism | 反义词: statism, collectivism, authoritarianism | 词性: noun
