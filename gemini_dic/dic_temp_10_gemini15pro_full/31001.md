chalkiest: /ˈtʃɔːkiɪst/
adjective
最像粉笔的；极度苍白的
most resembling chalk; extremely pale
Her face was chalkiest I’d ever seen, drained of all color. 她的脸是我见过最苍白的，毫无血色。
The walls were painted a chalkiest white. 墙壁被漆成了极度苍白的白色。
近义词: pale, ashen, pallid
反义词: flushed, ruddy
词性: adjective

词源:
From chalk + -y + -est.  The word "chalk" comes from Old English *cealc (attested in compounds), from Proto-Germanic *kalkaz, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱalḱ- (“lime, chalk”).
