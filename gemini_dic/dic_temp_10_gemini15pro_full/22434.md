bookending:/ˈbʊkˌɛndɪŋ/
n./v./
以...开始和结束; 首尾呼应; 夹住;
to start and end something with particular events or items; to place something at both ends of something else.
The film bookends with scenes of the protagonist's childhood.  这部电影以主人公童年的场景开头和结尾。
He bookended his speech with a joke. 他以一个笑话作为演讲的开头和结尾。
近义词: bracketing, flanking, enclosing
反义词: separating, dividing
词性: noun/verb (present participle)

bookend:/ˈbʊkˌɛnd/
n.
书挡;
a support placed at the end of a row of books to keep them upright.
I need a pair of bookends to hold up my heavy dictionaries. 我需要一对书挡来支撑我沉重的字典。
近义词: book support
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun


词源:  "Bookend" originated in the late 19th century, referring to a physical support for books. The verb "bookend" derived from the noun, appearing in the mid-20th century.
