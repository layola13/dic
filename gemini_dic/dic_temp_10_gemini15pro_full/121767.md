misquoters:/ˌmɪsˈkwoʊtərz/| n. |错误引用者，歪曲引用者|People who misquote others, either deliberately or accidentally, are misquoters.  故意或偶然错误引用他人话语的人是错误引用者。|The journalist was accused of being a misquoter after several subjects claimed he twisted their words.  这名记者被指控歪曲引用，此前有几人声称他歪曲了他们的话。|近义词：distorters, misinterpreters|反义词: interpreters,  paraphrasers|noun
