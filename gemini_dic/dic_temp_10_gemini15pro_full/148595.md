overtechnical:/ˌoʊvərˈtɛknɪkəl/ | adj. | 过于技术性的，过于专业的 | Excessively technical; using too much technical language or detail, making something difficult to understand for a general audience. | The manual was so overtechnical that even the engineers struggled to understand it. 这本手册过于技术化，即使是工程师也很难理解。|  The explanation was overtechnical for the average consumer.  这个解释对于普通消费者来说太过于专业了。 | 近义词: esoteric, abstruse, jargonistic, convoluted | 反义词: simple, straightforward, clear, accessible | 词性: 形容词
