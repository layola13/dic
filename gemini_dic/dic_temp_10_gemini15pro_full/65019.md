estuary: /ˈestʃʊəri/ (US) /ˈestjuəri/ (UK)| n. | 河口；江口 |A partly enclosed coastal body of water with one or more rivers or streams flowing into it, and with a free connection to the open sea.  It's a transition zone between river environments and maritime environments. |The Chesapeake Bay is the largest estuary in the United States. 切萨皮克湾是美国最大的河口。


The Thames estuary is home to a variety of wildlife. 泰晤士河口是各种野生动物的家园。|近义词: inlet, firth, bay |反义词:upstream, source |词性:noun
