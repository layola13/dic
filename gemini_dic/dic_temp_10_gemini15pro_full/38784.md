conductances:/kənˈdʌktənsɪz/
n.|电导；电导率；电导系数|The measure of a material's ability to conduct electric current.  It is the reciprocal of resistance.|The conductances of the wires were carefully measured.  这些电线的电导率经过仔细测量。|synonyms: conductivity, admittance|antonyms: resistance, resistivity|noun
conductance:/kənˈdʌktəns/
n.|电导；电导率；电导系数|The measure of a material's ability to conduct electric current.  It is the reciprocal of resistance.|The conductance of the wire was too low. 这根导线的电导率太低了。|synonyms: conductivity, admittance|antonyms: resistance, resistivity|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *conductantia*, from conductare "to lead, conduct," from con- "together" + ductare "to lead," frequentative of ducere "to lead."
