fatlike:/ˈfætˌlaɪk/| adj.|类似脂肪的；脂肪状的|Resembling fat in appearance, texture, or properties.  Having a greasy or oily consistency.|The substance had a fatlike consistency. 这种物质具有类似脂肪的稠度。|Synonyms: greasy, oily, fatty, adipose, lipidic|Antonyms: lean, thin, watery|adjective
