breakfast: /ˈbrɛkfəst/ (US) /ˈbrekfəst/ (UK)| v.| 吃早餐 |To eat breakfast.| We breakfasted at the hotel. 我们在酒店吃了早餐。| synonyms: have breakfast, eat breakfast| antonyms: fast, skip breakfast| verb
