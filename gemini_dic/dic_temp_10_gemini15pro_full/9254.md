antipatriarchal:/ˌæntiˌpeɪtriˈɑːrkəl/
adjective
反父权制的
Opposed to or challenging patriarchy, a social system in which men hold primary power and predominate in roles of political leadership, moral authority, social privilege and control of property.
Her antipatriarchal stance was evident in her writing. 她的反父权立场在她的作品中显而易见。
The group promoted antipatriarchal values and beliefs.  这个团体提倡反父权的价值观和信仰。
