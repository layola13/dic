bah:/bɑː/
interjection
呸; 表示轻蔑或厌恶
an exclamation of contempt or disgust.
Example: "Bah! That's ridiculous!" 呸！太荒谬了！
近义词: pshaw, humph, pooh, tut
反义词:  (No direct antonym for an interjection expressing disgust)
