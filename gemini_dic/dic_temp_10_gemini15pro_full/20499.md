bivalvous:/baɪˈvælvəs/
adjective
双壳的；有两瓣壳的
Having two valves or shells hinged together, like those of a clam or oyster.
The bivalvous mollusk closed its shell tightly when touched.  这双壳软体动物被触碰时紧紧地合上了壳。
The pearl was found inside a bivalvous oyster. 这颗珍珠是在一个双壳牡蛎里发现的。
近义词:  clamshell, two-shelled
反义词: univalve
词性: adjective


bivalve:/ˈbaɪvælv/
noun
双壳类动物
An aquatic mollusk that has a compressed body enclosed within a hinged shell, such as an oyster, clam, or mussel.
Bivalves are filter feeders, straining plankton from the water. 双壳类动物是滤食动物，从水中过滤浮游生物。
We found several bivalves buried in the sand. 我们在沙子里发现了几个双壳类动物。
近义词:  pelecypod, lamellibranch
反义词:  gastropoda (a class that includes snails and slugs - not a direct opposite, but a different class of mollusk)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *bivalvis*  (two-valved), from *bi-* (two) + *valva* (leaf of a folding door, valve).
