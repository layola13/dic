in-the-clouds:/ˌɪn ðə ˈklaʊdz/ | adj. | 心不在焉的，不切实际的，想入非非的 | Not paying attention to what is happening around you because you are thinking about something else; having unrealistic or impractical ideas. | Example: He’s always been a bit in-the-clouds. 他总是有点心不在焉。| Example:  She had in-the-clouds ideas about changing the world. 她对于改变世界有一些不切实际的想法。 | 近义词: dreamy, absent-minded, impractical, unrealistic | 反义词: realistic, practical, down-to-earth | 词性: 形容词
