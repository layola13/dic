croze:/kloʊz/| n.|填字游戏，克漏字|A cloze test, also cloze deletion test or occlusion test, is an exercise, test, or assessment consisting of a portion of text with certain words removed (cloze text), where the participant is asked to replace the missing words. Cloze tests require the ability to understand context and vocabulary in order to identify the correct words or type of words that belong in the deleted passages of a text.|The teacher gave the students a croze test to assess their reading comprehension.|近义词：gap-fill, fill-in-the-blank|反义词:|名词
