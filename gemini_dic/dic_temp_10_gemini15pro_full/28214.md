caproic: /kəˈproʊɪk/ | adj. |  己酸的，羊油酸的 | relating to or derived from caproic acid (a fatty acid found in animal fats and oils). | Caproic acid gives goat cheese its characteristic aroma. 羊奶酪特有的香味来自于己酸。 |  近义词: hexanoic | 反义词: None | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *caprōicus (“of a goat”), from capra (“goat”) + -ōicus.  己酸因其在山羊奶酪中的存在而得名，其拉丁语词根capra意为“山羊”。
