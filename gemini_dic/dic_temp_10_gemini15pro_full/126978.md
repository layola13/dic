narwhal:/ˈnɑːrwəl/ (US), /ˈnɑːwəl/ (UK)| n.|独角鲸|A medium-sized toothed whale (Monodon monoceros) that possesses a large "tusk" from a protruding canine tooth, mostly found in Arctic waters.|The narwhal's tusk can grow up to 10 feet long. 独角鲸的獠牙可以长到10英尺长。|
|Synonyms: unicorn whale, narwhale|Antonyms: N/A|noun|

narwhal:/ˈnɑːrwəl/ (US), /ˈnɑːwəl/ (UK)| n.|独角鲸的獠牙|The long, spiraled tusk of a narwhal.|The narwhal's narwhal is actually a tooth. 独角鲸的獠牙实际上是一颗牙齿。|Synonyms: tusk|Antonyms: N/A|noun|
