cycloidian:/ˌsaɪklɔɪˈɪdiən/| adj. n.|摆线的，旋轮线的；摆线|Relating to or resembling a cycloid; a curve traced by a point on the circumference of a circle as it rolls along a straight line.|The cycloidian curve is fundamental in the study of mechanics. 摆线曲线在力学研究中至关重要。|The artist used a cycloidian pattern in his design.  这位艺术家在他的设计中使用了摆线图案。|近义词：trochoidal|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, noun
Etymology: From Modern Latin *cycloīdēs*, from Ancient Greek κύκλος (kúklos, “circle”) + -oid.
