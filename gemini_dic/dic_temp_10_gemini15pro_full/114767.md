maltha:/ˈmɔːlθə/ | n. | 矿物沥青，地沥青 | A viscous, semi-solid hydrocarbon.  A bituminous material, like mineral tar or asphalt. | The ancient Egyptians used maltha to seal their tombs. 古埃及人用地沥青来密封他们的坟墓。|Example: The maltha seeped slowly from the cracks in the rock. 地沥青从岩石的裂缝中慢慢渗出。 | Synonym: asphalt, bitumen, pitch, tar | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps something like "water" in the context of sealing) | noun
