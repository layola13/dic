amorphousnesses:/əˈmɔːrfəsnɪsɪz/
n.
无定形；无组织；不确定性
The state or quality of being amorphous; lack of definite form or shape.
The artist captured the amorphousnesses of the clouds in his painting.  这位艺术家在他的画作中捕捉到了云朵的无定形。
The amorphousnesses of the organization made it difficult to manage.  该组织的无组织性使其难以管理。
synonym:formlessness, shapelessness, indefiniteness, vagueness
antonym: definiteness, clarity, structure, organization
noun
