loggan:/ˈlɒɡən/ | n. | logo, emblem, device | A loggan is a graphic symbol, emblem, or device used to identify an organization, product, or brand.  It's often synonymous with "logo." |  The company's loggan was a stylized bird in flight. 公司的标志是一只正在飞翔的程式化鸟。| synonym: logo, emblem, symbol, trademark, device | antonym:  (Loggan doesn't have a direct antonym.  You could consider "blank," "unmarked," or "anonymous" as contrasting concepts.) | noun
