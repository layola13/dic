mimicker:/ˈmɪmɪkər/| n.|模仿者；学样的人；效颦者|A mimicker is someone who imitates or copies the behavior, speech, or appearance of another person, often for amusement or mockery.|He's a talented mimicker and can impersonate almost anyone. 他是一个有才华的模仿者，几乎可以模仿任何人。

The child is a mimicker; she copies everything her older sister does.  这个孩子是个模仿者；她模仿她姐姐所做的一切。|近义词：imitator, impersonator, copier, parrot|反义词: innovator, originator|词性: noun
