obstructionism:/əbˈstrʌkʃənɪzəm/ | n. | 阻挠主义，故意阻挠 |  The deliberate act of hindering progress or preventing something from being done, especially in politics. |  The senator was accused of obstructionism for blocking the bill.  参议员因阻挠该法案而被指控阻挠主义。 |  impediment, hindrance, resistance, opposition |  cooperation, facilitation, support | noun

obstruct:/əbˈstrʌkt/ | v. | 阻碍，阻塞 | To block or close up with an obstacle; to impede or hinder. |  A fallen tree obstructed the road. 一棵倒下的树挡住了道路。 | block, hinder, impede | aid, assist, help | verb
