anapestical:/ˌænəˈpɛstɪk(ə)l/| adj.|抑抑扬格的，抑抑扬格的诗句的|Of or relating to an anapest, a metrical foot consisting of two short or unstressed syllables followed by one long or stressed syllable.  Characterized by the use of anapests.|The poem had a light, anapestical rhythm. 这首诗有一种轻快的抑抑扬格节奏。|Near Synonyms: anapestic|Antonyms: iambic|Adjective
