barbecued:/ˈbɑːrbɪˌkjud/ /ˈbɑːrbiˌkjud/| adj.|烧烤的，烤肉的|Cooked on a barbecue.  The process involves cooking food, usually meat, over a fire or grill, often with a smoky flavor from wood or charcoal.|例句：We had barbecued chicken for dinner. 我们晚饭吃了烤鸡。|近义词：grilled, roasted|反义词:boiled, steamed|词性:形容词
词源: From barbecue (n.) + -ed.  "Barbecue" itself comes from the Spanish "barbacoa," originally from the Taino word "barabicu," referring to a framework of sticks used for cooking meat.
