disbowel:/dɪsˈbaʊəl/
verb
取出内脏；剖腹
To remove the intestines or bowels from; disembowel.
The rebels disbowelled their captives. 叛军将俘虏开膛破肚。
Synonyms: eviscerate, disembowel, gut
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for this violent act.  One might consider "heal" or "mend" in a figurative sense, but these are not true antonyms.)
词性: verb
