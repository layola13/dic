aphesis:/ˈæfɪsɪs/ /əˈfiːsɪs/
n.
省略首音,首音消失
The loss of an unstressed vowel or syllable at the beginning of a word.
'Twas is an aphesis of It was.
'Twas是It was的省略首音形式。

'bout is an aphesis of about.
'bout是about的省略首音形式。
近义词:elision, apocope
反义词:prothesis
词性:noun
