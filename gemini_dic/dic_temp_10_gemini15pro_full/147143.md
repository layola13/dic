overloop:/ˈoʊvərˌfluː/ (US), /ˌəʊvəˈfluː/ (UK)| n. |溢流；溢流口；溢流管；（旅馆）走廊|An overloop is a structure or area where excess water or other liquid can flow over, or a passageway, especially in a hotel or other large building.|The bath overlooped and soaked the carpet. 浴缸里的水溢了出来，把地毯都弄湿了.|Synonym: overflow, spillway|Antonym: underflow|noun


overloop:/ˈoʊvərˌfluː/ (US), /ˌəʊvəˈfluː/ (UK)| v. |溢出；泛滥|To overloop is to flow over the brim or edge of something.|The river overlooped its banks during the flood. 河水在洪水期间漫过了河岸。|Synonym: overflow, flood|Antonym: drain|verb
