hominians:/ˈhɒmɪnɪnz/ |n.| 人族，人科成员 |A primate of the family Hominidae, of which Homo sapiens is the only extant species.  It includes extinct members of the human lineage. |Early hominians used simple tools. 早期人族使用简单的工具。| synonyms: hominids, humans | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, potentially other primates or mammals) | noun
