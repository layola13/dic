nonreceivable:/ˌnɑːnrɪˈsiːvəbl/
adjective
不可收的；无法收回的
Not able to be received, especially of debts or payments that are unlikely to be paid.
The company wrote off $5 million in nonreceivable debts.  这家公司注销了500万美元的无法收回的债务。
The bank classified the loan as nonreceivable after the borrower declared bankruptcy.  在借款人宣布破产后，银行将该贷款列为不可收回的贷款。
近义词: uncollectible, irrecoverable, bad (debt)
反义词: receivable, collectible
