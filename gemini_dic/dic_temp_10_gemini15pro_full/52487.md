dieting:/ˈdaɪətɪŋ/ | n. |节食，控制饮食|The practice of restricting oneself to small amounts or special kinds of food in order to lose weight.|She's been dieting for months to try and fit into her wedding dress. 她为了穿上婚纱已经节食好几个月了。|Synonyms:  weight loss, slimming, reducing |Antonyms:  feasting, gorging, overeating |noun


dieting:/ˈdaɪətɪŋ/ | adj. |节食的|Relating to or characteristic of a diet.|She's following a strict dieting regime. 她正在遵循严格的节食方案。|Synonyms:  weight-reducing, slimming, dietary |Antonyms:  gorging, indulgent, overeating |adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *diete* (modern French *diète*), from Latin *diaeta* "prescribed diet, way of life," from Greek *diaita* "manner of living, diet, regimen," from *diai* "through, thoroughly" + *atos* "year."  The original sense was "way of life," including both food and exercise.
