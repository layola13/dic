clearstory: /ˈklɪəˌstɔːri/ (US), /ˈklɪəˌstɔːri/ (UK)| n.|天窗，高侧窗，透光高窗|A high section of wall containing windows that admit light to the central space of a building, especially a church or hall.|The church's clearstory windows filled the nave with light.|Synonyms: clerestory, high window, skylight|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "solid wall")|Noun

clearstory: /ˈklɪəˌstɔːri/ (US), /ˈklɪəˌstɔːri/ (UK)| adj.|高侧窗的，透光高窗的|Having or relating to a clearstory.|The clearstory windows provided ample illumination.|Synonyms: clerestoried|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|Adjective
