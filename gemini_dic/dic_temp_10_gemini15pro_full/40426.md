contrarily:/kənˈtrɛərɪli/ | adv. |相反地，反之|In a contrary manner; oppositely; on the other hand. |例句：He expected to be praised for his work; contrarily, he was criticized. 他期望自己的工作受到表扬；相反，他受到了批评。

例句：She claimed to be innocent, but contrarily, all the evidence pointed to her guilt. 她声称自己是无辜的，但相反，所有证据都指向她的罪行。|近义词：conversely, inversely, oppositely, on the contrary |反义词：similarly, likewise, correspondingly |词性:副词

词源:
From Late Latin contrarius (“opposite, contrary”), from Latin contra (“against”).
