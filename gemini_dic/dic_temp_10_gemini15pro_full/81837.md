greylag:/ˈɡreɪˌlæɡ/| n. |灰雁|A large grey goose with an orange bill and pink legs, native to Europe and Asia.  |例句：The greylag is the ancestor of most domestic geese. 灰雁是大多数家鹅的祖先。

例句：We saw a flock of greylag geese flying overhead. 我们看到一群灰雁从头顶飞过。|近义词：goose, wild goose|反义词:|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *greyleg, from grey + lag, meaning "slow" or "sluggish" (in reference to its flight compared to other geese).
