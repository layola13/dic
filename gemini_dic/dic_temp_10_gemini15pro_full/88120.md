This term is primarily used in South Asia. |  Example: In some parts of India, hijras are considered a third gender.  在印度的一些地区，海吉拉被认为是第三性别。

Example: Hijras have a long and complex history in South Asian culture. 海吉拉在南亚文化中有着悠久而复杂的历史。 | 近义词: transgender person, trans woman (in some cases), kinnar (sometimes used interchangeably but with different cultural connotations) | 反义词: cisgender (though not a direct opposite, it represents the absence of the transgender identity that defines hijra) | 词性: noun
