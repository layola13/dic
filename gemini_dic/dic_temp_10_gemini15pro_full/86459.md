hegelian:/heɪˈɡeɪliən/ | adj. n.|黑格尔主义的；黑格尔哲学的；黑格尔主义者|Of or relating to the German philosopher Georg Wilhelm Friedrich Hegel, his philosophical system, or his followers. |His work presents a hegelian interpretation of history. 他的作品提出了对历史的黑格尔主义诠释。|The prominent philosopher is a Hegelian.  这位著名的哲学家是一位黑格尔主义者。|近义词：idealistic, dialectical|反义词：empirical, materialistic|词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From Georg Wilhelm Friedrich Hegel + -ian.
