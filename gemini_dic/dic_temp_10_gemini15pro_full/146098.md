overegg:/ˌoʊvərˈɛɡ/
verb
过分装饰；过分渲染；做得过火
to spoil something by elaborating it too much; to exaggerate
例句：
The director overegged the fight scene, making it unrealistic and comical.
导演把打斗场景做得太过火了，反而显得不真实和滑稽。

例句：
He overegged his description of the fish he caught, claiming it was twice the size it actually was.
他夸大了他钓到的鱼的大小，声称它实际上是两倍大。

近义词：exaggerate, overstate, embellish, overdo
反义词：understate, downplay
词性：v.
