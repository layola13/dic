dodecastyle: /ˌdoʊdəˈkaɪstaɪl/ | adj. n. | 十二柱式的；十二根柱子的 | Having twelve columns in front, as a portico.  A building or portico having twelve columns in front. | The temple was a magnificent dodecastyle structure, inspiring awe in all who beheld it. 这座寺庙是一座宏伟的十二柱式建筑，所有看到它的人都感到敬畏。 The architect's design featured a dodecastyle facade. 建筑师的设计以十二柱式的外观为特色。 | 近义词: twelve-columned | 反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly tetrastyle - four-columned, hexastyle - six-columned, octastyle - eight-columned depending on context) | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From Greek *dōdeka* (twelve) + *stylos* (column).
