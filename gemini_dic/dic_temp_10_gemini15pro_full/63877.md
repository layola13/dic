epilogical:/ˌɛpɪˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
结尾的；后记的；结语的
Relating to or resembling an epilogue; concluding.
The epilogical chapter tied up all the loose ends of the story.  结尾的章节把故事的所有未交代的情节都串联起来了。
The film's epilogical scene showed the main character years later.  电影结尾的场景展现了主角多年后的生活。
近义词: concluding, final, closing
反义词: introductory, opening, preliminary
词性: adjective

词源: From Greek *epilogikos*, from epilogos (epilogue) + -ikos (relating to).  来自希腊语 *epilogikos*，由 epilogos（结语）+ -ikos（与...相关）组成。
