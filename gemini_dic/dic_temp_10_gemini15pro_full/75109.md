fringy: /ˈfrɪndʒi/ | adj. | 边缘的；不重要的；非主流的 |  Relating to or characteristic of a fringe; not mainstream; unconventional or extreme. |  Her fringy political views were not popular with the mainstream voters. 她的边缘政治观点并不受主流选民的欢迎。 |  The band had a fringy, experimental sound.  这支乐队的音乐风格边缘化，具有实验性。 | Synonyms: marginal, peripheral, unconventional, alternative, avant-garde, offbeat | Antonyms: mainstream, conventional, orthodox, central | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From fringe + -y.  "Fringe" originally referred to the decorative border of a garment, and later came to mean the outer edge or margin of something.  The suffix "-y" is used to form adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "full of".
