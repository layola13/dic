brattishness:/ˈbrætɪʃnəs/| n. |幼稚任性；孩子气；讨厌的行为|The state of being bratty; childish misbehavior.|His brattishness was embarrassing to his parents. 他的孩子气让他的父母很尴尬。|近义词：immaturity, childishness, petulance, churlishness|反义词：maturity, politeness, courtesy|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From brat + -ish + -ness.  "Brat" originally referred to a raggedly dressed child, and later came to mean a spoiled or ill-mannered child.  The suffix "-ish" turns it into an adjective (brattish), and "-ness" turns the adjective into a noun (brattishness).
