nonhappening:/ˌnɒnˈhæpənɪŋ/
noun
未发生的事；沉闷的事件
an event that is not exciting or interesting; something that doesn't happen
The party was a total nonhappening.  派对非常沉闷。
The predicted riot was a nonhappening. 预计中的暴乱并没有发生。
Synonym: nonevent, dud, flop
Antonym: event, happening, success
n.
