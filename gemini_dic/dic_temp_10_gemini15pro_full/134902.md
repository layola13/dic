nonmultiplications:/ˌnɒnˌmʌltɪplɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 非倍数，不进行乘法运算 |  The process of addition instead of multiplication; instances where multiplication isn't used or doesn't apply.  This word is not commonly used and might be considered jargon in specific contexts. |  The calculations involved nonmultiplications, focusing on sums and differences rather than products.  这些计算不涉及乘法，侧重于加减而不是乘积。| Near synonyms: non-products, additions | Antonyms: multiplications, products | noun
