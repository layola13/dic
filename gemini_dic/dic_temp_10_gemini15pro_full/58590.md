dulcoration:/ˌdʌlkəˈreɪʃən/ /ˌdʌlkəˈreɪʃn/
noun
甜化;使变甜;加糖;减轻痛苦
The act of sweetening or making something more agreeable.
The dulcoration of the medicine made it easier for the child to swallow.  这药加糖后孩子更容易吞咽了。

The dulcoration of the harsh truth with kind words made it easier to bear. 用善意的话语来缓和残酷的事实，使其更容易被接受。

近义词:sweetening, mitigation, alleviation, assuagement
反义词:exacerbation, aggravation, worsening

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin dulcorātiō, from Latin dulcorāre (“to sweeten”), from dulcis (“sweet”).
