leasebacks:/ˈliːsˌbæks/| n.|售后回租；售後租回|A transaction in which an owner sells an asset, typically real estate, and immediately leases it back from the buyer.|The company opted for a sale-and-leaseback agreement to free up capital.  这家公司选择了售后回租协议来释放资金。|The hospital sold its building and entered into a leaseback arrangement.  医院卖掉了它的建筑，并签订了回租协议。|synonyms: sale-and-leaseback, lease-purchase|antonyms: N/A|noun
