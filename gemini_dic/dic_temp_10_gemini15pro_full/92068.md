hyperprophetical:/ˌhaɪpərprəˈfɛtɪk(ə)l/
adjective
超预言的，极度预言的
Excessively or extremely prophetic; characterized by exaggerated or extravagant prophecy.
His hyperprophetical pronouncements about the future of artificial intelligence seemed more like science fiction than reasoned analysis. 他关于人工智能未来的极度预言的论断，听起来更像是科幻小说，而不是理性的分析。
The cult leader's hyperprophetical pronouncements about the end of the world attracted a devoted following.  这位邪教领袖关于世界末日的极度预言吸引了一批忠实的追随者。

近义词: apocalyptic, prophetic, visionary
反义词: realistic, pragmatic

词性: adjective
