distractedly:/dɪˈstræktɪdli/
adverb
心不在焉地，注意力分散地
in a way that shows that you are not paying attention to what is happening
She looked at him distractedly, her mind elsewhere. 她心不在焉地看着他，心思飘到了别处。
Synonyms: absentmindedly, inattentively, vaguely
Antonyms: attentively, carefully, focusedly
词性: 副词

distract:/dɪˈstrækt/
verb
使分心，分散(注意力)
to prevent someone from giving their attention to something by making them look at or listen to something else
The noise from the street distracted me from my work. 街上的噪音分散了我的工作注意力。
Synonyms: divert, sidetrack
Antonyms: focus, concentrate
词性: 动词

distracted:/dɪˈstræktɪd/
adjective
心烦意乱的；注意力不集中的
anxious and unable to think clearly
He seemed very distracted and kept looking out of the window. 他似乎很心烦意乱，不停地望着窗外。
Synonyms: preoccupied, distressed, troubled
Antonyms: focused, attentive, concentrated
词性: 形容词
