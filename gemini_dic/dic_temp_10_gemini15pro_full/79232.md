glaringness:/ˈɡlɛərɪŋnɪs/
n.
刺眼; 耀眼; 显眼
The quality of being glaring; obviousness.
The glaringness of the error was apparent to everyone.  这个错误的显眼程度每个人都看得出来。
近义词: brightness, obviousness, conspicuousness, gaudiness
反义词: dimness, faintness, subtlety, inconspicuousness
词性: 名词

glare:/ɡlɛr/
v.
怒目而视; 发强光; 耀眼; 闪耀; 瞪视
To shine with a strong, harsh light.  发出强烈的刺眼的光。
The sun glared down on the desert. 太阳炙烤着沙漠。
近义词: stare, scowl, frown, blaze, shine
反义词: squint, glance, glimmer, soften
词性: 动词

glaring:/ˈɡlɛrɪŋ/
adj.
耀眼的; 刺眼的; 怒目而视的; 显眼的; 昭然的
Shining with a strong, harsh light; very obvious or conspicuous.
a glaring error  一个明显的错误
近义词: obvious, conspicuous, blatant, flagrant
反义词: subtle, inconspicuous, hidden, obscure
词性: 形容词
