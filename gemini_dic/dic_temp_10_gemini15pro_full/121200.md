misdrive:/ˌmɪsˈdraɪv/
verb
错误地驾驶，打偏（球）
To drive incorrectly or to hit (a ball) off-center.
He misdrove his car into a ditch. 他把车错误地开进了沟里。
The golfer misdrove the ball into the rough. 高尔夫球手把球打偏到了长草区。
misdirect, mishit
properly drive, hit accurately
