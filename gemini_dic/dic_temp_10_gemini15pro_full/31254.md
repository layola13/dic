chaos:/ˈkeɪɒs/ (UK) /ˈkeɪɑːs/ (US)| n. |混乱，混沌|A state of complete confusion and disorder.|例句：The room was in utter chaos after the party. 房间在派对后一片狼藉。

例句：The sudden change in leadership caused chaos in the company. 领导层的突然变化导致公司一片混乱。

近义词：disorder, confusion, anarchy, turmoil, pandemonium|反义词：order, harmony, peace, stability|词性: 名词
