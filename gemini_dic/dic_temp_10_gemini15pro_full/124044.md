morpheus:/ˈmɔːrfiəs/ /ˈmɔːrfɪəs/| n.|梦神; 麻醉剂; 睡眠|Morpheus is the Greek god of dreams.  Morpheus is also sometimes used poetically to refer to sleep itself or a drug that induces sleep. |例句：He fell into the arms of Morpheus. 他进入了梦乡。

例句：Morpheus, the bringer of dreams, visited him in his sleep.  梦神Morpheus在睡梦中拜访了他。|近义词：Hypnos, Somnus|反义词:wakefulness|noun
