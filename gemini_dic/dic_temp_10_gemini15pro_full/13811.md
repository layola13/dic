auditable:/ˈɔːdɪtəbl/| adj.|可审计的|Able to be audited; suitable for auditing.  This means that something can be checked or examined officially to see if it is correct and legal.|The company's financial records are auditable. 该公司的财务记录是可审计的。|The system must be auditable to ensure compliance with regulations.  为了确保符合规定，该系统必须是可审计的。|verifiable, inspectable, accountable|unauditable|adjective
