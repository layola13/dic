gynandromorphism:/ˌɡaɪnˈændrəˌmɔːrfɪzəm/ |n.|雌雄同体，雌雄嵌合体|The condition of having both male and female characteristics in an individual, typically seen in insects and other invertebrates, where an organism has both male and female tissues or organs.|Researchers documented a case of gynandromorphism in a rare butterfly species. 研究人员记录了一个罕见蝴蝶物种的雌雄同体案例。|Synonyms: hermaphroditism, intersex|Antonyms: unisexualism|noun

gynandromorphic:/ˌɡaɪnˈændrəˈmɔːrfɪk/ |adj.|雌雄同体的|Having both male and female characteristics.|The gynandromorphic butterfly exhibited distinct male and female wing patterns. 这只雌雄同体的蝴蝶展现出明显的雄性和雌性翅膀图案。|Synonyms: hermaphroditic, intersex|Antonyms: unisexual|adjective
