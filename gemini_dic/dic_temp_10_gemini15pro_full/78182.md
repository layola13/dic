geoselenic:/ˌdʒiːoʊsɪˈliːnɪk/
adjective
含硒的；硒地的
Relating to or containing selenium, especially in the soil.
Geoselenic soils can be toxic to livestock.  含硒土壤可能对牲畜有毒。
Geoselenic areas are often associated with certain types of vegetation.  富硒地区通常与某些类型的植被有关。
synonyms:seleniferous

词性: adjective
