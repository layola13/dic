complemented:/ˈkɒmplɪmentɪd/ (US) /ˈkɑːmplɪmentɪd/| v. |补充；完善；补足|To make something else seem better or more attractive when combined with it. |The red wine perfectly complemented the steak.  这红酒与牛排相得益彰。|Synonyms: enhance, supplement, augment, enrich|Antonyms: detract from, diminish, worsen|verb

complemented:/ˈkɒmplɪmentɪd/ (US) /ˈkɑːmplɪmentɪd/| adj. |补充的，完善的|Existing or occurring together in such a way as to enhance or emphasize the qualities of each other or another.|The two approaches are complementary and can be used together. 这两种方法是互补的，可以一起使用。|Synonyms: complementary, supplementary|Antonyms: conflicting, contradictory|adjective
