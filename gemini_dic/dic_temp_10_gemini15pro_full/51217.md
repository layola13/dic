destock:/diːˈstɒk/ /diːˈstɑːk/| v. |减少库存，清仓|To reduce the level of stock or inventory, typically by selling goods at a reduced price.|The company decided to destock its summer clothing line to make way for the autumn collection.  这家公司决定清仓夏季服装系列，为秋季系列腾出空间。

The retailer destocked by offering significant discounts.  这家零售商通过提供大幅折扣来减少库存。|近义词：liquidate, clear out, sell off, reduce inventory|反义词：stock up, accumulate, build up|词性:动词
