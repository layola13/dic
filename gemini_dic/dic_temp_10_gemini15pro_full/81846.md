gribble:/ˈɡrɪbəl/| n. |蛀木水虱; 船蛆|A small, marine crustacean that bores into submerged wood, causing damage to wooden structures.|例句：The gribble has caused significant damage to the pier. 蛀木水虱对码头造成了严重的破坏。

例句：Gribble are a threat to wooden boats. 船蛆对木船是一种威胁。|近义词：woodworm, shipworm|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *griblen, frequentative of *griben (“to dig, burrow”), from Old Norse gryfja (“to dig”), from Proto-Germanic *grubaną (“to dig”). Cognate with Dutch graven (“to dig”), German graben (“to dig”).
