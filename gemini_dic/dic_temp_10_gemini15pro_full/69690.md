fenestration:/ˌfenɪˈstreɪʃən/
n.|开窗，窗户布置，穿孔|The fenestration of the building was designed to maximize natural light.  这栋建筑的开窗设计是为了最大限度地利用自然光。|例句：The architect carefully planned the fenestration to create a sense of openness. 建筑师精心规划了窗户布置，以营造一种开放感。
|近义词：glazing, windows, apertures|反义词：walling|词性：名词
