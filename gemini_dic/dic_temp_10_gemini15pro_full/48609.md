defectively:/dɪˈfɛktɪvli/
adverb
有缺陷地；不完美地
in a way that is faulty or imperfect
The product was defectively manufactured.  这个产品制造得有缺陷。
The machine was working defectively.  这台机器运转不良。
Synonym:faultily, imperfectly, inadequately, deficiently
Antonym:perfectly, flawlessly, impeccably
词性: 副词
