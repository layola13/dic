aesthetic:/ɛsˈθɛtɪk/ /iːsˈθɛtɪk/
|adj. n.|美的，美学的；审美的|Concerned with beauty or the appreciation of beauty.|例句：The building has a pleasing aesthetic quality. 这座建筑有一种令人愉悦的美感。|近义词：artistic, beautiful, creative|反义词：ugly, unattractive|词性: adjective

aesthetic:/ɛsˈθɛtɪk/ /iːsˈθɛtɪk/
|n.|美学；审美观|A set of principles underlying and guiding the work of a particular artist or artistic movement.|例句：The artist's aesthetic is minimalist and modern.  这位艺术家的审美观是极简主义和现代主义的。|近义词：taste, style, principles|反义词：None|词性: noun
