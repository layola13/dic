hooty:/ˈhuːti/| adj.|猫头鹰叫声般的，古怪的，滑稽的|Resembling or characteristic of the sound of an owl, often used to describe something strange, quirky, or comical.|例句：The old house had a hooty atmosphere.  那栋老房子有一种古怪的气氛。|例句：He made a hooty noise, trying to imitate an owl. 他发出猫头鹰般的叫声，试图模仿一只猫头鹰。|近义词：owl-like, eerie, strange, quirky, comical|反义词:normal, ordinary|词性:形容词

词源:  From "hoot" (the cry of an owl) + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Old English *hōtan*.
