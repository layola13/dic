fitchy:/ˈfɪtʃi/| adj.|尖的，(纹章学)尖底的|Having a pointed end, especially of a cross in heraldry, where the lower limb tapers to a point. |The shield bears a fitchy cross.  盾牌上有一个尖底十字。|近义词：pointed, sharp|反义词:blunt, rounded|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Old French *fichée, feminine past participle of ficher (“to fix, stick, drive in”).  Ultimately from Frankish *fikōn, from Proto-Germanic *fikōną (“to stick, insert”).  Cognate with Middle Dutch vichen, vicken (“to stick, pierce”), Old High German fihhan (“to attach, connect”), German ficken (“to copulate with”).
