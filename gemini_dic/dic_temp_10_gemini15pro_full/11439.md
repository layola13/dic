argotic:/ɑːrˈɡɑːtɪk/
adjective
行话的；隐语的
relating to or using the special vocabulary or informal slang of a particular group of people.
例句：The argot of the underworld is often difficult for outsiders to understand.  黑社会的黑话通常外人很难理解。

例句：She peppered her conversation with argot, making it hard for her grandparents to follow. 她讲话中夹杂着许多行话，使她的祖父母难以理解。

近义词：slangy, colloquial, informal, jargon, cant
反义词：formal, standard, literary
词性：adjective
