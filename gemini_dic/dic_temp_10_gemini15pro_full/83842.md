halal:/həˈlɑːl/ /hɑːˈlɑːl/| adj. n.|清真的，符合伊斯兰教律法的；清真食品|Permitted or lawful according to Islamic law, especially referring to food.|例句：This restaurant serves halal meat. 这家餐厅供应清真肉类。|近义词：kosher, permissible, lawful|反义词：haram, forbidden, unlawful|词性:形容词, 名词

halal:/həˈlɑːl/ /hɑːˈlɑːl/| n.|清真食品|Food that is permitted or lawful according to Islamic law.|例句：They only eat halal. 他们只吃清真食品。|近义词：kosher food|反义词：haram food|词性:名词
