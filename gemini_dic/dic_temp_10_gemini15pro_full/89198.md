homeshoring:/ˈhoʊmˌʃɔːrɪŋ/ | n. | 家庭办公；居家外包 | The practice of transferring work typically done in a company's office to employees' homes. |  Many companies adopted homeshoring during the pandemic. 许多公司在大流行期间采用了家庭办公模式。|  telecommuting, working from home | offshoring, outsourcing | noun
