cheaping:/ˈtʃiːpɪŋ/
verb
讨价还价;杀价
The act of bargaining for a lower price, often aggressively or unfairly.
He was cheaping with the street vendor over the price of the fruit. 他在和街头小贩就水果的价格讨价还价。
The tourists were cheaping down the price of souvenirs.  游客们正在杀纪念品的价格。
近义词: bargaining, haggling, negotiating
反义词:  splurging, overpaying
词性:v.
