eductive:/ɪˈdʌktɪv/
adjective
教育性的；由教育引起的；启发的
Drawing out, eliciting, or developing something potential or latent, especially by education.  Relating to or characterized by education.
例句：The teacher used eductive methods to encourage critical thinking in her students. 老师运用启发式教学方法鼓励学生进行批判性思维。

例句：The museum offers a range of eductive programs for children and adults.  这家博物馆为儿童和成人提供一系列教育项目。
近义词：educational, instructive, enlightening, formative
反义词：uneducative, unenlightening, stultifying
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin ēdūcō ("I lead forth, I raise up, I educate"), from ē- ("out, forth") + dūcō ("I lead").
