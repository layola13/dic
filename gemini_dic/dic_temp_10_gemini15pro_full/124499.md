moulinet:/ˌmuːlɪˈneɪ/ | n. | 水车；旋转；转轮；（钓鱼）绕线轮 | A revolving part of a machine; a fishing reel; a whirling or circling movement, especially in fencing. |  He expertly cast his line using a lightweight moulinet. 他熟练地使用轻便的绕线轮抛线。| synonyms: reel, whirl, spin, rotation | antonyms: stillness, immobility | noun

moulinet:/ˌmuːlɪˈneɪ/ | v. |  （使）快速旋转；耍花剑 | To perform a whirling movement, especially with a sword in fencing. | The fencer moulinetted his foil with dazzling speed.  击剑运动员以令人眼花缭乱的速度旋转着他的花剑。| synonyms: whirl, spin, twirl, rotate | antonyms: stop, halt | verb
