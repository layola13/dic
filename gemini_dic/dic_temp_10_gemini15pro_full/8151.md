anti-constitution:/ˌæntiˌkɑːnstɪˈtuːʃən/ | adj. |违宪的，反宪法的| Opposed to or in violation of a constitution. |  The group's anti-constitution activities led to their arrest.  该组织的违宪活动导致他们被捕。

The proposed law was deemed anti-constitution. 这项拟议的法律被认为是违宪的。


|近义词：unconstitutional, illegal, unlawful|反义词:constitutional, legal, lawful |词性:adjective
