incidentalist:/ˌɪnsɪˈdɛntəlɪst/ | n. | 附带事件论者；偶然主义者 |  Someone who believes that historical events are primarily determined by a series of accidents and coincidences rather than by underlying trends or forces. | Historians debate whether Hitler's rise was inevitable or the work of incidentalists.  历史学家们争论希特勒的崛起是必然的还是偶然主义者的杰作。| synonyms: contingentist | antonyms: determinist, inevitabilist | noun


incidental:/ˌɪnsɪˈdɛntəl/ | adj. | 附带的；偶然的；非主要的 | Happening as a natural result of something else.  | The plot of the movie felt incidental to the stunning visuals.  这部电影的情节相对于其惊人的视觉效果而言显得次要。| synonyms: secondary, minor, ancillary, subordinate, peripheral | antonyms: essential, primary, main, crucial | adjective
