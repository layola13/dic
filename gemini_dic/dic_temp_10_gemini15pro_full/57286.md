n.|缩减规模，降低规模|The downscaling of the project resulted in job losses.  该项目的规模缩减导致了失业。|reduction, decrease|expansion, upscaling|noun
downscaling:/ˈdaʊnˌskeɪlɪŋ/
v.|缩减…的规模，降低…的规模|The company is downscaling its operations in Asia. 该公司正在缩减其在亚洲的业务规模。|reduce, decrease|expand, upscale|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "down-" (indicating reduction or decrease) + "scaling" (from "scale," meaning to adjust or measure according to a scale).
