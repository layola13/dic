astrologers:/əˈstrɒlədʒəz/
n.
占星家
A person who studies astrology; a person who practices astrology.
Many ancient kings employed astrologers. 许多古代的国王都雇佣占星家。
Astrologers predicted that the new year would be prosperous. 占星家预测新年将会繁荣昌盛。

近义词:stargazers, soothsayers, diviners
反义词:scientists, skeptics
词性:noun (plural)
