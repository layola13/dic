faradays:/ˈfæˌrəˌdeɪz/| n.|法拉第（电容单位，或指迈克尔·法拉第）|A unit of capacitance equal to the capacitance of a capacitor that has an equal and opposite charge of 1 coulomb on each plate and a potential difference of 1 volt between the plates. Also refers to Michael Faraday, a British scientist who contributed significantly to the study of electromagnetism and electrochemistry.|The capacitance of the capacitor is measured in faradays.  这个电容器的电容是用法拉第来衡量的。|Michael Faraday discovered electromagnetic induction. 迈克尔·法拉第发现了电磁感应。|近义词：(for the unit) capacitance unit|反义词: (no direct antonym for the unit or the person)|名词
