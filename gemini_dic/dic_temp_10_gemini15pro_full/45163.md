cued:/kjuːd/ | v. | 提示，暗示，给…提示 | to give someone a signal to do something | The director cued the actor to begin his lines. 导演提示演员开始他的台词。 | prompted, signaled, indicated | dissuaded, deterred | verb

cued:/kjuːd/ | adj. | （戏剧、电影等）有提示的，按提示进行的 | given a cue or signal | The actors were all cued and ready to perform.  演员们都得到了提示，准备好了表演。| prompted, prepared | unprepared, unrehearsed | adjective

PHRASE->
cue sb in (on sth)  给某人提供（关于某事的）信息;  Can you cue me in on what happened at the meeting? 你能告诉我会议上发生了什么吗？
cue up  准备播放;  I've cued up the next song. 我已经准备好播放下一首歌了。
take a cue from sb  效仿某人；学某人的样子; I took a cue from my mentor and started networking more. 我效仿我的导师，开始更多地建立人脉。
right on cue  就在那时；恰好在这个时候; Right on cue, the phone rang.  就在那时，电话响了。
miss a cue  错过提示；错过暗示; The actor missed his cue and ruined the scene.  演员错过了他的提示，毁了这场戏。
