connascency:/kɒˈnæsənsi/ /kəˈnæsənsi/
n.
先天性, 同源, 同族
The state of being born together or of having a common origin. A close relationship or connection.
This unusual flower demonstrates the connascency of its petals, fused together at the base. 这种不寻常的花展示了其花瓣的先天性，在基部融合在一起。
The connascency of the twins was evident in their similar features and mannerisms.  这对双胞胎的同族性体现在他们相似的特征和举止上。
近义词: kinship, affinity, relatedness, connection
反义词: unrelatedness, disconnection, separateness
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *connascentia, from Latin connasci (“to be born together”), from con- (“together”) + nasci (“to be born”).
