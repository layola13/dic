adjudicating: /əˈdʒuːdɪkeɪtɪŋ/
verb
裁决；判决；评判
The act of making a formal judgment or decision about a problem or disputed matter.
The judge is adjudicating a complex legal case.  法官正在审理一个复杂的法律案件。

The committee is adjudicating on the entries for the competition.  委员会正在评判比赛的参赛作品。

近义词: judging, deciding, arbitrating, refereeing, umpiring
反义词: mediating, negotiating
词性: Verb (present participle)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adjudicare* "to award," from *ad-* "to" + *judicare* "to judge," from *judex* "judge."
