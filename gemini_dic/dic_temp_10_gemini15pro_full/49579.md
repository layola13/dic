demera:/dɪˈmɪərə/| n.| 住宅，房屋，住所 |A house or dwelling place.  This word is primarily used in Trinidad and Tobago and some other Caribbean islands. |例句：My demera is near the beach. 我的房子在海滩附近。

例句：They are building a new demera on the hill. 他们在山上建一座新房子。|近义词：house, home, dwelling, residence, abode |反义词: None (lacks a direct opposite, consider perhaps "homelessness")|词性:noun
