outswaggering:/ˌaʊtˈswæɡərɪŋ/
|v.|以更嚣张的态度胜过；在气势上压倒|Outswaggering someone means behaving in a more swaggering or arrogant manner than them, effectively outdoing them in terms of boastful display.|He outswaggered his rivals, striding confidently into the room. 他以更嚣张的气势压倒了他的对手，自信地大步走进了房间。|Synonyms: outbragging, outboasting, outposing|Antonyms:  being humble, being modest|Verb

outswaggering:/ˌaʊtˈswæɡərɪŋ/
|adj.|胜过…的嚣张气焰的；在气势上压倒…的|Used to describe someone who surpasses others in swaggering or arrogant behavior.|His outswaggering attitude intimidated everyone in the room. 他那不可一世的态度吓坏了房间里的每一个人。|Synonyms: domineering, overbearing, arrogant|Antonyms: humble, modest, unassuming|Adjective
