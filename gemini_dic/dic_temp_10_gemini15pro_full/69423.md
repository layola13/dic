feeling-up-to:/ˈfiːlɪŋ ʌp tuː/| phrasal verb | 感觉能够胜任，有心情做 | Feeling up to something means having the physical or emotional energy and willingness to do it.  It implies a sense of capability and desire. | I'm not feeling up to going out tonight. 我今晚不想出去。| 近义词: inclined to, in the mood for, capable of | 反义词: unable to, not in the mood for | 词性: 动词短语
