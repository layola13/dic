antifascism:/ˌæntiˈfæʃɪzəm/
n.
反法西斯主义
A political movement opposed to fascism.
The rise of antifascism in Europe during the 1930s was a response to the growing threat of totalitarian regimes.  20世纪30年代欧洲反法西斯主义的兴起是对极权主义政权日益增长的威胁的回应。

The museum's exhibit on antifascism highlighted the bravery of those who resisted oppression.  博物馆的反法西斯主义展览突出了那些抵抗压迫者的勇气。

Synonyms: anti-fascism
Antonyms: fascism
