bawdily: /ˈbɔːdɪli/ | adv. | 下流地，淫秽地 | In a lewd or indecent manner; obscenely. | He spoke bawdily about his conquests. 他下流地谈论他的艳遇。|  The comedian told a bawdily joke. 这名喜剧演员讲了一个下流的笑话。| 近义词: lewdly, indecently, obscenely, vulgarly | 反义词: chastely, modestly, decently | 词性: 副词

bawd: /bɔːd/ | n. | 老鸨，妓院老板；淫媒 | A woman who runs a brothel or who procures prostitutes.  A person who arranges sexual liaisons. | The bawd was arrested for running a brothel. 老鸨因经营妓院被捕。|  The play featured a cunning bawd who manipulated everyone. 这部戏的主角是一位狡猾的老鸨，她操纵着所有人。| 近义词: madam, procurer, pimp (for a male) | 反义词: None | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bawde*, from Old French *baude*, of uncertain origin. Possibly from Frankish *bald*, bold, brave, or from a Germanic source meaning "to procure."
