indicas:/ˈɪndɪkəz/ | n. |大麻的籼型品种; (尤指)印度大麻|  A subspecies of *Cannabis sativa*, originally from India, known for its relaxing and sedative effects.  It is often used medicinally to relieve pain, insomnia, and anxiety. | This strain is one of the indicas known for its potent effects. 这个品种是大麻的籼型品种之一，以其强效作用而闻名。| synonyms: Cannabis indica, Indian hemp | antonyms: sativas | noun
