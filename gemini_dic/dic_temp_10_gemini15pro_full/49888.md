ETYMOLOGY->

From Middle French *demour*, from Old French *demorer* "to linger, stay, remain, dwell," from Latin *demorari* "to delay, linger, tarry," from *de-* "down, completely" + *morari* "to delay." The sense evolution went from "sober, grave" to "reserved, modest," especially of women.
