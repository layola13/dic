nonrenewable:/ˌnɑːn.rɪˈnuː.ə.bəl/ /ˌnɒn.rɪˈnjuː.ə.bəl/| adj.|不可再生的|Not able to be renewed or replaced once used.|Fossil fuels are nonrenewable resources. 化石燃料是不可再生资源。|Synonyms: irreplaceable, inexhaustible, finite|Antonyms: renewable, sustainable, replenishable|adjective

nonrenewable:/ˌnɑːn.rɪˈnuː.ə.bəl/ /ˌnɒn.rɪˈnjuː.ə.bəl/| n.|不可再生资源|A resource that cannot be renewed or replaced once used.|Coal is a nonrenewable. 煤炭是一种不可再生资源。|Synonyms: nonrenewable resource, finite resource|Antonyms: renewable resource, sustainable resource|noun
