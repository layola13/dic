例句：The surgeon's expertness saved the patient's life.  这位外科医生的精湛技艺挽救了病人的生命。

近义词：skill, proficiency, mastery, expertise | 反义词：ineptitude, incompetence, clumsiness | 词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From expert + -ness.  Expert ultimately derives from Latin expertus, past participle of experiri "to try, test."
