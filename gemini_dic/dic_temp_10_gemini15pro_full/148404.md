overstated:/ˌoʊvərˈsteɪtɪd/
adjective
夸大的；言过其实的
Exaggerated, represented as greater than is actually the case.
The benefits of the new policy have been greatly overstated. 这项新政策的好处被过分夸大了。
synonyms: exaggerated, inflated, amplified, overblown, overemphasized
antonyms: understated, downplayed, minimized
词性: 形容词


overstate:/ˌoʊvərˈsteɪt/
verb
夸大；夸张；对…言过其实
To exaggerate, to describe something as larger, more important, or more serious than it really is.
It's easy to overstate the importance of money.  很容易夸大金钱的重要性。
synonyms: exaggerate, amplify, embellish, inflate
antonyms: understate, downplay, minimize
词性: 动词
