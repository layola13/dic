morphines:/ˈmɔːrfiːnz/ | n. | 吗啡（复数）| Morphine is a powerful opioid used to treat severe pain. It exists in the plural form as morphines, usually referring to multiple doses or types of morphine. | The doctor prescribed morphines for the patient's post-operative pain. 医生给病人开了吗啡来治疗术后疼痛。| Synonyms: opiates, narcotics | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a pain reliever; consider "stimulants" as a contrasting class of drugs) | Noun
