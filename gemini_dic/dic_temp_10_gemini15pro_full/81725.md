greenockite:/ˈɡriːnəˌkaɪt/ | n. | 绿硫镉矿 | A rare yellow mineral, cadmium sulfide (CdS), occurring as earthy coatings or hexagonal crystals. It is a minor ore of cadmium. | Greenockite is often found as a bright yellow coating on sphalerite. 绿硫镉矿经常以鲜黄色的涂层形式出现在闪锌矿上。| Greenockite is named after Lord Greenock. 绿硫镉矿以格里诺克勋爵的名字命名。 |Synonyms: cadmium sulfide | Antonyms: None | Noun
