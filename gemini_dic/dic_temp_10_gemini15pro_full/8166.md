anti-culture:/ˈæntiˌkʌltʃər/ | n. | 反文化 |  A system of values and beliefs that rejects or opposes the dominant or mainstream culture.  It often involves a critique of societal norms, conventions, and institutions. |  The punk rock movement was a powerful expression of anti-culture in the 1970s.  朋克摇滚运动是 20 世纪 70 年代反文化的有力表达。

The rise of social media has given a voice to various anti-culture movements. 社交媒体的兴起为各种反文化运动提供了发声的平台。 | 近义词: counterculture, subculture | 反义词: mainstream culture, dominant culture | 词性: 名词
