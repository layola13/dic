chopine:/tʃoʊˈpiːn/ /ˈtʃɒpiːn/| n. |高底木屐；威尼斯高脚杯；（诗歌）一种五音步抑扬格诗行|A woman's shoe with a very high thick sole. A kind of high-heeled clog or overshoe worn in Venice in the 16th century; also, a high-heeled shoe.  A large drinking glass. |She wore chopines to make herself appear taller. 她穿着高底木屐让自己看起来更高。|近义词：clog, platform shoe, overshoe, high-heeled shoe|反义词:flat shoe|noun

chopine:/tʃoʊˈpiːn/ /ˈtʃɒpiːn/| n. |大酒杯|A large drinking glass, often elaborately decorated.|He raised his chopine and toasted the new couple. 他举起大酒杯，向新婚夫妇敬酒。|近义词：goblet, tankard, beaker|反义词:shot glass|noun
