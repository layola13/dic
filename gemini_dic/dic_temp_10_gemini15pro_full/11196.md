architecting:/ˈɑːrkɪˌtektɪŋ/| v.|架构，设计，策划|The process of designing and planning the structure of something, especially a computer system or software.|例句：They are architecting a new cloud-based platform. 他们在构建一个新的基于云的平台。|例句：She's architecting a new marketing campaign. 她正在策划一个新的营销活动。|近义词：designing, planning, structuring, engineering, developing, constructing|反义词: dismantling, deconstructing|词性:动词

词源:
From architect + -ing.  Ultimately from Greek arkhitektōn "chief builder," from arkhi- "chief" + tektōn "builder, craftsman."
