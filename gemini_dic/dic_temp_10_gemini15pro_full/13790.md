audiological:/ˌɔːdiəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ |adj.|听力学的，听觉学的|Relating to the study of hearing, balance, and related disorders.|例句：She pursued an audiological career. 她从事听力学方面的工作。|例句：Audiological tests are crucial for diagnosing hearing loss. 听力学测试对于诊断听力损失至关重要。|近义词：aural, auditory|反义词：(No direct antonym, but consider "non-audiological")|词性:adjective

audiologist:/ˌɔːdiˈɒlədʒɪst/ |n.|听力学家，听觉学家|A healthcare professional specializing in the diagnosis, treatment, and management of hearing and balance disorders.|例句：The audiologist performed a hearing test. 听力学家进行了听力测试。|例句：I made an appointment with an audiologist to discuss my tinnitus. 我和听力学家预约了讨论我的耳鸣问题。|近义词：hearing specialist|反义词：(No direct antonym)|词性:noun
