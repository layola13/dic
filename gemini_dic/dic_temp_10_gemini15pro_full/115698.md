marginalize: /ˈmɑːrdʒɪnəlaɪz/ /ˈmɑːrɡənəˌlaɪz/ | vt. |边缘化；排斥；忽视|To treat (a person, group, or concept) as insignificant or peripheral.|例句：The new policy marginalized the elderly. 新政策边缘化了老年人。|例句：They are trying to marginalize the opposition parties. 他们试图排斥反对党。|近义词：exclude, isolate, ostracize, sideline, downplay, minimize |反义词: centralize, empower, include, integrate |词性：动词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *marginalis, from Latin margo (margin).
