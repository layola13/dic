luminances:/ˈluːmɪnənsɪz/
noun
亮度（复数）
The brightness of a surface or light source.  It's a physical measure of the luminous intensity per unit area of light travelling in a given direction.
The luminances of the different areas of the screen varied considerably.  屏幕不同区域的亮度差异很大。
synonyms: brightness, brilliance, intensity, radiance
antonyms: darkness, dimness
n.
