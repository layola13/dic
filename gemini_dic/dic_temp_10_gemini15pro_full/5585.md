alter:/ˈɔːltər/ (US) /ˈɒltə(r)/ (UK)| v. |改变，修改|To change something, usually slightly.|例句：He altered his plans to suit her. 他改变了计划以适应她。

例句：The tailor altered the dress to fit perfectly. 裁缝修改了裙子，使其非常合身。

近义词：change, modify, adjust, amend|反义词：maintain, preserve, keep|词性：动词
