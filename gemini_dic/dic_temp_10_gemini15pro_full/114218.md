maieutic:/maɪˈuːtɪk/ |adj.|助产的；苏格拉底式的|Relating to the method of eliciting new ideas from others by skillful questioning, modeled on the approach of Socrates.  | Socrates' maieutic method helped his students arrive at their own conclusions. 苏格拉底的助产术帮助他的学生得出自己的结论。|近义词：Socratic, dialectical|反义词：didactic, lecturing|词性: adjective
