capsizing: /kæpˈsaɪzɪŋ/ | n. | 翻船，倾覆 | The act of a boat or ship turning over in the water. |  The capsizing of the small boat was caused by strong winds. 小船的倾覆是强风造成的。| 近义词: overturning, upsetting | 反义词: righting | 词性: 动名词 (Gerund)


capsize: /kæpˈsaɪz/ | v. |  (使)翻船，(使)倾覆 | To (cause a boat or ship to) turn over in the water. |  The strong winds capsized the sailboat.  强风使帆船翻了。|  The canoe capsized, throwing its occupants into the cold water. 独木舟翻了，把里面的人抛进了冰冷的水里。| 近义词: overturn, upset | 反义词: right | 词性: 动词
