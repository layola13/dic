draftsman's:/ˈdræftsmənˌz/ |adj. |制图员的；绘图员的| Belonging to or associated with a draftsman (a person who makes detailed technical drawings).| The draftsman's tools were neatly arranged on his desk.  绘图员的工具整齐地摆放在他的桌子上。|  drafter's | architect's |adjective


Etymology:
From "draftsman" + "'s" (possessive suffix).
"Draftsman" comes from "draft" (meaning "a preliminary version of a writing or drawing") + "-man" (suffix indicating a person who performs the action).  "Draft" ultimately derives from the Old Norse word "draga" meaning "to draw" or "to pull."
