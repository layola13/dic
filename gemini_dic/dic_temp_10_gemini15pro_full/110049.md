licensing:/ˈlaɪsənsɪŋ/
n.
授权，许可
The act of granting a license.
Software licensing is a complex issue.  软件授权是一个复杂的问题。

近义词:authorization, permitting
反义词:prohibition, forbiddance
词性:noun


licensing:/ˈlaɪsənsɪŋ/
n.
特许
The business of granting licenses.
The city council handles all licensing for businesses. 市议会处理所有企业的许可证颁发。

近义词:franchising, chartering
反义词:restriction, banning
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From license (n.) + -ing (suffix forming nouns of action).  License comes from Old French licence "liberty, permission" from Latin licentia "freedom, permission," from licentem (nominative licens), present participle of licere "to be permitted."
