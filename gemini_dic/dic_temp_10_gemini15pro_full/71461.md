flameproof:/ˈfleɪmpruːf/| adj.|防火的，阻燃的|Treated so as to be resistant to catching fire.|例句：The curtains are flameproof.  这些窗帘是防火的。|例句：Flameproof clothing is essential for firefighters. 对于消防员来说，阻燃服装是必不可少的。|近义词：fireproof, fire-resistant, nonflammable|反义词：flammable, inflammable|词性:形容词
词源: flame (火焰) + proof (防...的)
