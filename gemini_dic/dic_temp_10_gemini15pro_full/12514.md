asphalt:/ˈæsˌfɔːlt/ (US), /ˈæsˌfælt/ (UK)| n.  |沥青; 柏油路|A dark, bituminous substance, used for paving and roofing.|The asphalt on the road was melting in the heat. 路上的沥青在高温下融化了。|近义词:bitumen, tarmac, pavement|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与其用途相反的物质，例如) gravel, concrete|noun


asphalt:/ˈæsˌfɔːlt/ (US), /ˈæsˌfælt/ (UK)| vt. |用沥青铺设|To cover with asphalt.|They asphalted the driveway last week.  他们上周用沥青铺设了车道。|近义词:pave, surface|反义词: (没有直接的反义词)|verb
