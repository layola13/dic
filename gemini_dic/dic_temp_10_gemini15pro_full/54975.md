disproportionate: /ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃənət/ | adj. | 不成比例的，不相称的 | Too large or too small in comparison with something else. |  The punishment was disproportionate to the crime.  惩罚与罪行不相称。 |  excessive, inordinate, unequal |  proportionate, commensurate, balanced | adjective

disproportionately: /ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃənətli/ | adv. | 不成比例地，不相称地 | To a disproportionate degree. |  Minority groups are disproportionately affected by unemployment. 失业对少数群体的影响尤为严重。 | excessively, unduly, unevenly | proportionately, equally, fairly | adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (not, opposite of) + proportionate (proportional).  The word "proportionate" comes from the Latin "proportionatus," past participle of "proportionare" (to make proportional), from "proportio" (proportion).
