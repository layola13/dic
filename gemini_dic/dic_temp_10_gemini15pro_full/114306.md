mainswear:/ˈmeɪnˌswɛər/| n.| 맹세, 서약 (主要用于苏格兰法律)|A solemn oath; an oath required of a party in a legal process in Scotland. | He gave his mainswear in court. 他在法庭上进行了庄严宣誓。|Synonyms: oath, vow, pledge, affirmation|Antonyms: perjury, renunciation, recantation|noun
Etymology: From Scots *manswear, from man + swear.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *mann- ("man") and *swaraną ("to swear").|
