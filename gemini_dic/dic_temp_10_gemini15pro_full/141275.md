opaquing:/oʊˈpeɪkɪŋ/
verb
使不透明，遮盖
The artist is opaquing the background of the painting.  艺术家正在使画作的背景不透明。
The varnish opaqued the wood, hiding the grain.  清漆使木头变得不透明，遮盖了木纹。
synonyms: obscuring, clouding, covering
antonyms: clarifying, revealing, transparenting
