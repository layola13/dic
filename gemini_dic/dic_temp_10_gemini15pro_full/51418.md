deterrer:/dɪˈtɜːrər/ (US), /dɪˈtɛrər/ (UK)| n.| 威慑物；制止物；阻吓因素|Something that deters people from doing something.|The high price of the tickets was a major deterrer for many fans.  高昂的票价是许多球迷的主要障碍。|The presence of police acted as a deterrer to potential criminals. 警察的存在对潜在罪犯起到了威慑作用。| synonyms: deterrent, discouragement, obstacle, impediment| antonyms: encouragement, incentive, inducement| noun
