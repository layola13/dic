mundic:/ˈmʌndɪk/ | adj. n. | 黄铁矿的；黄铁矿 |  Relating to or consisting of iron pyrites (a common sulfide mineral with a metallic luster). Also used as a noun to refer to iron pyrites itself.  | This mine is rich in mundic veins. 这个矿藏富含黄铁矿矿脉。 | The miners extracted a large quantity of mundic. 矿工们提取了大量的黄铁矿。 |Synonyms: pyrite, iron pyrite | Antonyms: None (specific mineral, no direct opposite) | Adjective, Noun
