nean:/niːən/ | adj. | 低下的，卑鄙的；吝啬的 | low, mean, base; stingy |  He made a nean remark about her dress. 他对她的衣服说了句刻薄的话。 | 近义词：base, vile, despicable, contemptible, miserly, stingy | 反义词：noble, generous, kind | 词性：形容词

词源：From Middle English *nene, from Old English *nēane (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *naiwiniz (“small, insignificant”), from Proto-Indo-European *noy- (“new”). Cognate with German *nein (“no”).  The meaning "stingy" developed from "small, insignificant".
