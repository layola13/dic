historian:/hɪˈstɔːriən/ | n. | 历史学家；史学工作者 | A scholar who studies and writes about history.  A person who studies and writes about the past. |  The historian dedicated her life to researching ancient civilizations.  这位历史学家毕生致力于研究古代文明。

The historian analyzed primary sources to understand the events of the war.  这位历史学家分析了主要来源，以了解战争的事件。 |  Synonyms: chronicler, annalist, archivist | Antonyms:  N/A (No direct antonym for a profession) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *istoryen*, from Old French *historieur, historieur*, from Medieval Latin *historiator*, from Latin *historia* ("history") + *-tor* (agent suffix). Ultimately from Ancient Greek *ἱστορία (historía, “inquiry; knowledge acquired by investigation; account, narrative, history”)*.
