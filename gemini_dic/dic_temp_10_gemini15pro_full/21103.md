blipped:/blɪpt/ | v. | 短暂出现；发出哔哔声；迅速移动；忽略 | To appear briefly and suddenly; to make a short, high-pitched electronic sound; to move quickly; to ignore or disregard something. | The radar blip bleeped, indicating an approaching aircraft.  雷达发出哔哔声，显示一架飞机正在接近。

The dot bliped on the screen and then vanished.  那个点在屏幕上短暂出现然后消失了。

He bliped through the channels, looking for something to watch. 他快速地切换频道，寻找想看的节目。

She bliped over the uncomfortable part of the conversation. 她忽略了谈话中令人不适的部分。 | 近义词：flash, beep, dart, skip, overlook | 反义词：linger, remain, address | 词性：动词

词源：
Likely an onomatopoeia, imitating a short, high-pitched sound. 可能是一个拟声词，模仿短促而尖锐的声音。
