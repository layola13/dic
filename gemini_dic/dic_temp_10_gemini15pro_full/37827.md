comperendinate:/ˌkɒmprɪˈhɛndɪneɪt/
verb
概括，总结，包含
to comprehend; to summarize; to include
This textbook comperendinates all the essential information for the exam. 这本教科书总结了考试的所有重要信息。
Synonyms: summarize, encapsulate, epitomize, abridge, condense
Antonyms: expand, elaborate, detail, amplify
词性: 动词
