linking:/ˈlɪŋkɪŋ/| adj. v. n.|连接的，链接；连接，联系；环节，纽带|A linking verb connects the subject of a sentence with a word or phrase that describes or identifies the subject.  A linking road connects two larger roads. |The bridge serves as a linking structure between the two cities. 这座桥是连接两个城市的纽带。|近义词：connecting, joining, uniting, associating, relating|反义词：separating, dividing, disconnecting, isolating|词性: adjective, verb, noun
