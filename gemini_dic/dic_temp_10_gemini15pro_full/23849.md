breadlessness:/ˈbredlɪsnəs/
noun
无面包；缺面包；贫困
Lack of bread; a state of having no bread or insufficient bread, often implying poverty or deprivation.
The breadlessness of the Great Depression forced many families to rely on soup kitchens. 大萧条时期的缺面包迫使许多家庭依靠施粥场。
Near synonyms: poverty, destitution, deprivation, indigence, penury
Antonyms: affluence, wealth, prosperity
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From bread + -less (suffix indicating lack of).  Bread itself comes from Old English *brēad.
