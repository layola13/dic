frowzy: /ˈfraʊzi/ (US) /ˈfraʊzi/ (UK) | adj. | 邋遢的，不整洁的；（房间）闷的，有霉味的 | Appearing unkempt or messy; (of a room) stuffy and stale-smelling. |  Her hair was frowzy and her clothes were wrinkled. 她的头发乱蓬蓬的，衣服也皱巴巴的。|  The frowzy attic smelled of dust and mothballs. 闷热的阁楼里散发着灰尘和樟脑丸的气味。 | 近义词: unkempt, disheveled, messy, slovenly, musty, stuffy | 反义词: neat, tidy, clean, fresh | 词性: 形容词


词源: From earlier *frowy, from frow (obsolete) meaning brittle or fragile, of unknown origin.  The original sense referred to hair or clothing being dry, brittle, and easily disarranged, later extending to a general sense of untidiness and stuffiness.
