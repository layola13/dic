mineralize:/ˈmɪnərəlaɪz/ | v. | 使矿化；变成矿物；使骨骼钙化 | To convert into a mineral or a mineral deposit; to supply with minerals. |  The bones of the dinosaur skeleton have been mineralized over millions of years. 恐龙骨骼经过数百万年已经矿化了。

The farmer mineralized his fields to improve crop yields. 农民在他的田里施加矿物质以提高作物产量。 | 近义词: petrify, fossilize | 反义词: decompose, decay | 词性: 动词

mineralize:/ˈmɪnərəlaɪz/ | n. | 矿化；矿化作用 | The process of converting into a mineral or a mineral deposit. | The mineralization of the wood made it incredibly hard. 木材的矿化作用使其变得异常坚硬。 | 近义词: petrification, fossilization | 反义词: decomposition, decay | 词性: 名词
