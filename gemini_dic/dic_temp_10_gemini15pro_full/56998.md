double-faulting:/ˈdʌbəl ˈfɔːltɪŋ/ | n. | 双误；两次发球失误 |  The act of faulting twice in a row in tennis, resulting in the loss of the point.  This happens when a player's first serve is out, and then their second serve is also out or hits the net. |  She lost the game because of her persistent double-faulting. 她因为持续的双误输掉了比赛。| Synonym: two faults, serving faults | Antonym: ace, successful serve | noun

double-fault:/ˈdʌbəl ˈfɔːlt/ | n. vi. | 双误；两次发球失误 | A double fault in tennis, where both serves are illegal.  | He double-faulted on a crucial point. 他在一个关键点上双误了。 | Synonym: two faults, serving faults | Antonym: ace, successful serve | noun, intransitive verb
