learner:/ˈlɜːrnər/ (US) /ˈlɜːnə(r)/ (UK)| n. |学习者，学生|Someone who is learning something, especially a skill or a language. |例句：He's a quick learner and picked up the new software easily. 他学东西很快，轻松掌握了新软件。

例句：She's a visual learner and prefers diagrams and charts. 她是一个视觉型学习者，喜欢图表。


近义词：student, pupil, apprentice, novice, beginner, trainee|反义词：teacher, instructor, expert, master|词性：noun
