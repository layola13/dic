enslaver: /ɪnˈsleɪvər/ | n. | 奴役者，使人成为奴隶的人 |  A person who enslaves another person or group of people. |  The enslavers treated their captives with brutality.  奴隶主残酷地对待他们的俘虏。 | Synonyms: oppressor, subjugator, tyrant | Antonyms: liberator, emancipator | noun

enslaver: /ɪnˈsleɪvər/ | v. | 奴役，使成为奴隶 | To make someone a slave; to deprive someone of freedom and force them to work for nothing. | The colonists enslaved the indigenous population.  殖民者奴役了土著居民。| Synonyms: subjugate, oppress, dominate | Antonyms: liberate, free, emancipate | verb
