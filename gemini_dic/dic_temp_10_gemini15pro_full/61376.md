embitters:/ɪmˈbɪtərz/| v.|使更苦；使怨恨；加剧|Makes something more bitter; causes someone to feel resentful or hostile; intensifies (usually negative feelings or situations).|例句：The constant criticism embittered their relationship.  不断的批评加剧了他们关系的恶化。
