nonconfrontation:/ˌnɒnkɒnfrənˈteɪʃn/ | n. |不对抗，不冲突|The act of avoiding confrontation; a peaceful or non-aggressive approach to a situation.  |Example:  The government opted for a policy of nonconfrontation. 政府选择了不对抗的政策。 |Example:  Her nonconfrontation made her an easy target for bullies. 她不爱对抗的性格使她很容易成为恃强凌弱者的目标。| 近义词: avoidance, appeasement, compromise, diplomacy, pacifism | 反义词: confrontation, aggression, hostility, conflict, antagonism |词性:名词

nonconfrontational:/ˌnɒnkɒnfrənˈteɪʃənl/ | adj. |不具对抗性的，不爱争论的|Not involving or seeking confrontation; tending to avoid conflict. |Example: He has a nonconfrontational style of management. 他有一种不具对抗性的管理风格。|Example:  She took a nonconfrontational approach to the disagreement. 她对这次分歧采取了不对抗的态度。| 近义词: passive, peaceable, diplomatic, conciliatory, nonaggressive | 反义词: confrontational, aggressive, hostile, belligerent, combative | 词性:形容词
