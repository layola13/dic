overinforming: /ˌōvərɪnˈfɔːrmɪŋ/ | v. (present participle) | 过度告知；提供过多信息 | Providing an excessive amount of information, often to the point of confusion or boredom. |  He was overinforming the client with unnecessary details about the product, causing them to lose interest. 他向客户提供了过多关于产品的非必要细节，导致他们失去了兴趣。
