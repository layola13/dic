carvene:/ˈkɑːrviːn/ | n. | 香芹烯 | A colorless liquid hydrocarbon, C10H16, found in caraway oil and used as a flavoring agent and fragrance.  |  Carvene is a key component in the distinct aroma of caraway seeds. 香芹烯是葛缕子籽独特香味的关键成分。 | Carvene is used in some perfumes for its fresh, herbal scent. 香芹烯因其清新、草本的香味而用于某些香水。 | synonyms: limonene, terpene | antonyms: N/A | noun
