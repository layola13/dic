dolphinaria:/ˌdɒlfɪˈneəriə/ /ˌdɑːlfɪˈnɛəriə/| n. |海豚馆；海豚表演场|A dolphinarium is a public marine mammal park or aquarium where dolphins are kept and displayed. |例句：The dolphinarium is a popular attraction for families. 海豚馆是深受家庭欢迎的景点。

例句：Protesters gathered outside the dolphinarium to advocate for the release of the dolphins. 抗议者聚集在海豚馆外，倡导释放海豚。|近义词：aquarium, marine park|反义词: None (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用ocean, sea 表示自然环境)|词性:noun
