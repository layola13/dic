parasailing: /ˈpærəseɪlɪŋ/ | n. | 滑翔伞运动；帆伞运动 | The activity of being towed through the air while attached to a parachute-like canopy and towed by a motorboat. | We went parasailing on our vacation. 我们在度假时玩了滑翔伞。|  例句：Parasailing offers a thrilling bird's-eye view of the coastline.  滑翔伞运动可以提供海岸线激动人心的鸟瞰图。| 近义词: paragliding | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps swimming/diving) | 词性: noun
