bookseller:/ˈbʊkˌsɛlər/| n. | 书商；书店老板 | A person who sells books, especially one who owns or runs a bookshop. |  I bought this rare first edition from a bookseller in London. 我从伦敦的一位书商那里买了这本罕见的初版。| Synonyms: book dealer, book merchant | Antonyms: book buyer | noun

Etymology:
From book + seller. "seller" comes from the Middle English "seller," from Old English "sellan" (to give, sell)  and ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*saljaną" (to offer for sale).
