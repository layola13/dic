florist:/ˈflɔːrɪst/| n.|花商，花店老板；花卉研究者|A florist is someone who sells and arranges flowers.|例句：I bought these roses from the florist down the street. 我从街那头的花店买了这些玫瑰。

例句：The florist created a beautiful bouquet for the wedding. 花商为婚礼制作了一个漂亮的花束。|近义词：flower seller, flower arranger|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From French *fleuriste*, from fleur "flower" (from Latin *florem*, accusative of *flos*) + -iste.
