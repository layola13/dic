hetchel:/ˈhɛtʃəl/| v. n.|梳理（亚麻），梳麻机|To comb or dress (flax or hemp) with a hetchel.|The farmer hetcheled the flax before spinning it. 农民在纺纱前先用梳麻机梳理亚麻。|近义词：comb, dress, card|反义词:tangle, snarl|词性:动词, 名词
Etymology: From Middle Dutch *hekelen, from Proto-Germanic *hakōną (“to hack, chop”), from Proto-Indo-European *keg- (“to sharpen”).  Related to "hackle."
