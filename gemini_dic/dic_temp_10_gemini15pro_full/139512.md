obstruent:/əbˈstruːənt/| n. adj.|阻塞音；阻塞的|A sound made by obstructing airflow in the vocal tract (such as /p/, /b/, /t/, /d/, /k/, /ɡ/, /tʃ/, /dʒ/, /f/, /v/, /θ/, /ð/, /s/, /z/, /ʃ/, /ʒ/, /h/).  As an adjective, it describes sounds produced in this manner. |The sounds /p/, /b/, /t/, and /d/ are all obstruents. /p/、/b/、/t/ 和 /d/ 都是阻塞音。

The vocal cords vibrate for voiced obstruents.  声带振动发出浊阻塞音。|近义词：stop, fricative (for specific types of obstruents)|反义词:sonorant|词性: noun, adjective
