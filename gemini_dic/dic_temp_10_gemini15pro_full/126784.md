nanoparticle:/ˈnænoʊˌpɑːrtɪkəl/| n. |纳米颗粒，纳米粒子|A microscopic particle whose size is measured in nanometers (typically 1-100 nanometers).|Scientists are exploring the use of nanoparticles in medicine. 科学家正在探索纳米颗粒在医学中的应用。|近义词：nanomaterial|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "bulk material")|noun

nanoparticle:/ˈnænəʊˌpɑːtɪk(ə)l/| n. |纳米颗粒，纳米粒子|A microscopic particle whose size is measured in nanometers (typically 1-100 nanometers).|Gold nanoparticles are used in some diagnostic tests.  金纳米颗粒用于某些诊断测试。|近义词：nanomaterial|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "bulk material")|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From nano- (prefix meaning "one billionth") + particle. "Nano-" comes from the Greek word "nanos," meaning "dwarf."
