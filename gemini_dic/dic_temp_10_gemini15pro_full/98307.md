insighted:/ɪnˈsaɪtɪd/ | adj. | 有洞察力的，有见识的 | Having or showing deep understanding and insight.  |  She made some insightful comments about the current political situation. 她对当前的政治局势发表了一些富有洞察力的评论。 | insightful, perceptive, discerning, perspicacious | unperceptive, obtuse, ignorant | adjective

Etymology:  From "insight" + "-ed." "Insight" comes from the Old English word "insīht," meaning "inspection, examination."
