atwirl:/əˈtwɜːrl/
adjective
旋转的，快速旋转的
spinning rapidly; whirling.
Her skirt was atwirl as she danced. 她跳舞时裙子飞舞着。
近义词: whirling, spinning, rotating, revolving
反义词: still, motionless
词性: adjective

词源: a- (prefix meaning "in a state of") + twirl (verb meaning "to spin rapidly")
