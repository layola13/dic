fateful:/ˈfeɪtfəl/| adj.|重大的；决定性的；宿命的；致命的|Having an important, often very bad, effect on future events; controlled or caused by fate.|例句：The fateful day arrived when she had to make a decision.  决定性的一天到来了，她必须做出决定。|例句：A fateful encounter that changed her life forever. 一次命中注定的相遇永远地改变了她的人生。|近义词：decisive, critical, crucial, momentous, significant, destined|反义词：insignificant, trivial|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fatful, from Old English *fætfyl, from fæt ("fate") + -ful ("full of").  The word "fate" itself comes from Latin *fātum* ("fate, destiny, what has been spoken").
