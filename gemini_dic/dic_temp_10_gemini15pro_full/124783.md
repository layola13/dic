abbreviation|名词|MSX|MSX is an acronym for MicroSoft eXtended Basic, a standardized home computer architecture announced by Microsoft and ASCII Corporation in June 1983. It was popular in Europe, South America, and the Middle East, particularly in the 1980s and early 1990s.|The MSX standard brought some order to the 8-bit home computer market.|近义词:home computer, 8-bit computer|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps modern PC or console)|n.
