barbre:/ˈbɑːrbrə/| n.| 倒钩，鱼钩，芒刺，(金属丝上的)毛刺|A small projection, point, or bristle, typically on a wire or plant. |The barbre on the wire caught on my clothes. 金属丝上的倒钩钩住了我的衣服。|近义词：barb, prickle, thorn, bristle|反义词:smoothness|词性:名词

词源:  From Old French *barbre, from Latin barba "beard," also "fishhook barb," from PIE *bhardha- "point, edge, bristle."
