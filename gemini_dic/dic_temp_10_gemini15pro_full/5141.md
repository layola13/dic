allelic:/əˈliːlɪk/
adjective
等位基因的
Relating to or determined by alleles.  Pertaining to one of two or more alternative forms of a gene that arise by mutation and are found at the same place on a chromosome.
Example: The allelic variation at this locus is associated with disease susceptibility.  该基因座上的等位基因变异与疾病易感性相关。

Example:  Researchers studied the allelic frequencies in different populations.  研究人员研究了不同人群中的等位基因频率。
Synonym:allelomorphic
Antonym:non-allelic
词性:adjective
