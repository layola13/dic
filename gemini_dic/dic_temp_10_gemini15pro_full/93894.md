immortality: /ˌɪmɔːrˈtælɪti/ | n. | 不朽，永生 | The state of living forever or being remembered forever. |  He achieved immortality through his works of art. 他通过他的艺术作品获得了不朽。 |  His music has ensured his immortality. 他的音乐确保了他的不朽。 | 近义词: eternity, permanence, deathlessness | 反义词: mortality, finiteness | 词性: noun

词源: From Late Latin *immortalitatem, from Latin immortalis (not subject to death) + -itatem.  The Latin immortalis comes from in- (not) + mortalis (mortal).
