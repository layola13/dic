massora:/məˈsɔːrə/ | n. |  马索拉文本，马索拉学| The traditional body of critical notes on the text of the Hebrew Bible, its vocalization, and accentuation.  It defines the canonical text and preserves its transmission. |  Scholars meticulously studied the massora to ensure the accuracy of biblical texts. 学者们一丝不苟地研究马索拉文本，以确保圣经文本的准确性。|  近义词: Masoretic text | 反义词:  None | 词性: Noun

massorah:/məˈsɔːrə/ | n. |  马索拉学 | The study of the Massora, encompassing the system of vowel points, accents, and other markings added to the Hebrew Bible. |  His expertise in massorah allowed him to decipher the complex vocalization of the ancient text. 他在马索拉学方面的专业知识使他能够破译古代文本中复杂的元音。 | 近义词: Masoretics | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun
