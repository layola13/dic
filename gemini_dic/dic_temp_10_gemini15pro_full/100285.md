interseminal:/ˌɪntərˈsɛmɪnəl/
adjective
位于两粒种子之间的；种子间的
Situated between seeds or seminiferous tubules.
The interseminal scales of the pine cone are quite small.  松果的种子间鳞片非常小。

近义词:interjacent, intervening
反义词:None

词性:adjective
