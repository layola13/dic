overgreatness:/ˌəʊvəˈɡreɪtnəs/ | n. | 过分的伟大; 过度的伟大 | Excessive greatness; greatness that is too much or beyond what is appropriate. | His overgreatness became his downfall, as it blinded him to his own flaws. 他的过分的伟大导致了他的垮台，因为它使他对自己的缺点视而不见。| The movie portrayed an overgreatness in the character that felt unrealistic.  这部电影描绘了角色的一种过度的伟大，让人感觉不真实。 | synonyms:  excessiveness, immoderation, exorbitance | antonyms:  modesty, humility, insignificance | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive, too much") + "greatness" (noun derived from "great").  "Over-" has its roots in Old English "ofer," while "great" comes from Old English "grēat."
