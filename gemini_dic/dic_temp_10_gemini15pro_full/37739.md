compartmentalized:/kəmˌpɑːrtˈmentəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 分隔的，划分成各自独立部分的 | Divided into separate sections or categories, often with the implication that interaction or communication between them is limited or nonexistent. |  The company's operations are highly compartmentalized, with each department working independently.  这家公司的运营高度分隔，每个部门都独立运作。

The human brain is compartmentalized, with different areas responsible for different functions. 人脑是分隔的，不同的区域负责不同的功能。 | 近义词: categorized, classified, segmented, partitioned | 反义词: integrated, unified, holistic | 词性: 形容词

compartmentalize:/kəmˌpɑːrtˈmentəlaɪz/ | v. | 分隔，划分 | To divide something into separate sections or categories. | He compartmentalized his life, keeping his work and personal life strictly separate. 他把自己的生活划分开来，将工作和个人生活严格分开。

She tries to compartmentalize her emotions to cope with the stress. 她试图将自己的情绪分隔开来以应对压力。| 近义词: categorize, classify, segment, partition | 反义词: integrate, unify | 词性: 动词

词源: From compartment + -ize.  Compartment (隔间) comes from the French word compartiment.
