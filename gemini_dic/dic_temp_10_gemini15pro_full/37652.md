comorans:/ˈkɒmərənz/ (US) /ˈkɒmərænz/ (UK)| n. |鸬鹚|A large, dark-colored waterbird with a long neck, hooked bill, and webbed feet, often trained to catch fish.|例句：The comorans dived into the water with impressive speed. 鸬鹚以惊人的速度潜入水中。

例句：We watched the comorans fishing in the river. 我们看着鸬鹚在河里捕鱼。|近义词：cormorant|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Old French cormaran, from Vulgar Latin *corvus marinus ("sea crow"), from Latin corvus ("raven") + marinus ("marine").
