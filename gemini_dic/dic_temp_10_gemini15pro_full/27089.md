calico:/ˈkælɪkoʊ/ (US) /ˈkælɪkəʊ/ (UK)| n. adj.|印花棉布; 花斑猫|A plain-weave cotton cloth, typically with a bright printed design.  A cat with a coat of three colours, typically white with patches of orange and black.|例句：She wore a calico dress. 她穿着一件印花棉布连衣裙。|例句：My grandmother had a calico cat named Patches. 我祖母有一只名叫Patches的花斑猫。|近义词：(for fabric) chintz, gingham; (for cat) tortoiseshell, tricolour|反义词: (no direct opposite for either meaning)|名词, 形容词

calico:/ˈkælɪkoʊ/ (US) /ˈkælɪkəʊ/ (UK)| adj.|印花棉布的; 花斑的|Made of calico; having a spotted or parti-coloured coat like a calico cat.|例句：The calico curtains brightened up the room.  印花棉布窗帘使房间亮了起来。|例句：The calico kitten played with a ball of yarn. 花斑小猫玩着毛线球。|近义词：(for fabric) printed, patterned; (for cat) mottled, piebald|反义词: (no direct opposite for either meaning)|形容词
