manzanilla:/ˌmænzəˈniːjə/ /ˌmænzəˈniːlə/| n. |甘菊茶，甘菊，(西班牙)一种干雪利酒|A pale, dry type of sherry from Spain. Also, an infusion made from chamomile flowers.|例句：I ordered a glass of manzanilla. 我点了一杯甘菊酒。

例句：She drinks manzanilla tea every night before bed. 她每晚睡前都喝甘菊茶。|近义词：chamomile tea (for the infusion), fino sherry (for the wine)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
