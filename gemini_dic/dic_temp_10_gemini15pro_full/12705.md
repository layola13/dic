assertiveness:/əˈsɜːtɪvnəs/
n.
自信，坚定，果断
The quality of being able to express your opinions and needs in a confident way without being aggressive.
She showed assertiveness in dealing with the difficult customer. 她应对难缠顾客时表现出自信果断。
synonyms: confidence, firmness, self-assurance, decisiveness
antonyms: timidity, submissiveness, meekness, passivity
noun
