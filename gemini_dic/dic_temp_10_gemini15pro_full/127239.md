nauseously: /ˈnɔːʃəsli/ /ˈnɔːziəsli/ | adv. | 恶心,作呕地 |  In a way that causes nausea; sickeningly.  | The smell of the rotting food made me feel nauseously sick.  腐烂食物的气味让我感到恶心。| The room spun nauseously. 房间令人作呕地旋转。 | 近义词: sickeningly, disgustingly | 反义词: pleasantly, appealingly | 词性: 副词

nauseous: /ˈnɔːʃəs/ /ˈnɔːziəs/ | adj. | 恶心的,作呕的 | Affected with nausea; inclined to vomit. |  I felt nauseous after eating the spoiled food. 我吃了变质的食物后感到恶心。| The sight of blood made him nauseous.  看到血他感到恶心。| 近义词: sick, queasy | 反义词: well, healthy | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *nauseosus*, from nausea (“seasickness”), from Greek ναυσία (nausía, “seasickness”), from ναῦς (naûs, “ship”).
