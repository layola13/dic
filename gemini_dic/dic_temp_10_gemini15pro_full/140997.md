onerary: /ˈɒnərəri/ (US) /ˈɒnərəri/ (UK)| adj.|名誉的；荣誉的|Conferred as an honor, without the usual requirements or duties.|She received an honorary doctorate from Harvard University. 她获得了哈佛大学的荣誉博士学位。|The university awarded him an honorary degree. 大学授予他荣誉学位。|近义词：titular, nominal|反义词：earned, substantive|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *honorarius*, from Latin honor (“honor”).
