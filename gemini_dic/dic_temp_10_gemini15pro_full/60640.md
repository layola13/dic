electary:/ɪˈlɛktəri/
noun
糖剂；药糖；蜜剂
A medicinal confection, formerly used as a vehicle for unpleasant-tasting drugs.  A pasty or semi-liquid preparation consisting of a powdered drug mixed with honey or syrup.
The doctor prescribed an electary to soothe the child's sore throat. 医生给孩子开了一种药糖来舒缓喉咙痛。
