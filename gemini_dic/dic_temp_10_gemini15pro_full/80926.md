gdp:/dʒiː diː ˈpiː/ | n. | 国内生产总值 (Gross Domestic Product) | The total value of goods produced and services provided in a country during one year. |  The country's GDP grew by 2% last year.  这个国家的国内生产总值去年增长了2%。| synonyms: national output, national income | antonyms: N/A | noun
