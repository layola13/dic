fabulists:/ˈfæbjəlɪsts/
n.
寓言家；说谎者
A person who writes or tells fables; a liar, especially a habitual liar.
Aesop was a renowned fabulist.  伊索是一位著名的寓言家。
The politician was known as a fabulist, constantly embellishing the truth. 这位政客被称为说谎者，经常添油加醋。
近义词:liar, storyteller, prevaricator, fibber
反义词:truth-teller, honest person
词性:noun
