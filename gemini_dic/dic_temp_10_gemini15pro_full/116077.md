martite: /ˈmɑːrtaɪt/
n.
赤铁矿
A form of hematite (Fe2O3) pseudomorphously replacing magnetite (Fe3O4), typically with preservation of the octahedral crystal form of the original magnetite.
Martite is a common alteration product of magnetite. 赤铁矿是磁铁矿常见的蚀变产物。
Martite is found in many iron ore deposits. 赤铁矿存在于许多铁矿床中。
Synonym: hematite (in this context)
Antonym: magnetite (as its precursor mineral)
noun
