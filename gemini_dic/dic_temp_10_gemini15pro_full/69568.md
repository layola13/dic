feloniously:/fəˈloʊniəsli/
adverb
重罪地；罪恶地
in a way that involves a serious crime
He was feloniously assaulted. 他遭到恶意攻击。
Synonym: criminally, illegally, unlawfully
Antonym: legally, lawfully
词性: 副词
