ermit:/ˈɜːmɪt/|n.|隐士，遁世者|A person who lives a solitary life and avoids other people, especially for religious reasons. |例句：The hermit lived in a small cabin deep in the woods. 这位隐士住在森林深处的一个小木屋里。

例句：He lived as a hermit for many years, seeking spiritual enlightenment. 他多年来过着隐士般的生活，寻求精神上的启迪。|近义词：recluse, anchorite, solitary|反义词：socialite, extrovert|词性: 名词
