misfeature:/ˌmɪsˈfiːtʃər/
n.
缺陷，缺点，毛病
A fault or defect in a product or design.
This phone has a few misfeatures, such as poor battery life. 这个手机有一些缺点，比如电池续航能力差。
The software was riddled with misfeatures.  这个软件充满了缺陷。
Synonyms: defect, flaw, bug, glitch, imperfection, shortcoming
Antonyms: feature, advantage, strength, merit
noun
