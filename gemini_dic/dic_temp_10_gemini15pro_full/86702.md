helly:/ˈhɛli/ | n. |  地狱; 阴间; 喧嚣嘈杂的场所 | A place of torment and misery; a chaotic or unpleasant situation. |  例句：The traffic jam was a complete helly. 交通堵塞简直就是地狱。 | 近义词：hell, inferno, pandemonium | 反义词: heaven, paradise | 词性:名词


词源:
From hell + -y.  A diminutive of hell.

用法说明:
"Helly" is an informal and less common term than "hell."  It's used to describe a situation that is extremely unpleasant, chaotic, or difficult. It can also refer to a place that is noisy and crowded.
