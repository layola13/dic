biased: /ˈbaɪəst/ | adj. | 有偏见的；偏向的 | unfairly preferring one person or group over others | The referee was biased towards the home team. 裁判偏袒主队。| synonyms: prejudiced, partial, one-sided, unfair, unjust | antonyms: impartial, unbiased, neutral, objective, fair | adjective

biased: /ˈbaɪəst/ | adj. | 偏置的，偏移的 (technical) | exhibiting bias; systematically deviating from the true value | The results of the study were biased due to a flaw in the methodology. 由于方法上的缺陷，研究结果有偏差。| synonyms: skewed, distorted, warped, weighted | antonyms: accurate, precise, correct | adjective
