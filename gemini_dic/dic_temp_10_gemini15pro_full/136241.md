nonrenunciations:/ˌnɒnˌrɪˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃənz/
n.|放弃的否定；不放弃；不宣布放弃|The act of not renouncing or giving something up.  This word emphasizes the continued holding or maintaining of something, especially rights, claims, or beliefs. |His nonrenunciation of his claim to the throne caused further political instability. 他不放弃王位继承权导致了进一步的政治动荡。|persistence, adherence, retention|renunciation, abandonment|noun
