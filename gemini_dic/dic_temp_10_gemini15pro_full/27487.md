campanulaceous: /ˌkæmpənjuˈleɪʃəs/
adjective
桔梗科的；钟形花冠的
Relating to or characteristic of the bellflower family (Campanulaceae), typically having bell-shaped flowers.
The garden boasted a variety of campanulate, or campanula-like, flowers.  花园里种植着各种各样的钟形花，或者说类似桔梗的花。
Campanulaceous plants thrive in this region. 桔梗科植物在这个地区生长茂盛。
Synonyms: bell-shaped, campanulate
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, could use descriptions like "non-campanulaceous" or list shapes that are not bell-like, e.g., "tubular," "funnel-shaped")
词性: adjective
