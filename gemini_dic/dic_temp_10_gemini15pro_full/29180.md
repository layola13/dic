PHRASE->
cash in = 获利, 利用
cash in on =  利用...赚钱, 从...中获利
cash out =  兑现, 结算
cash on delivery = 货到付款
cash cow = 摇钱树


词源: From Old French *casse (unattested), from Italian cassa "money box", from Latin capsa "box, case".

注意事项: cash作名词时，单数形式指“现金”，复数形式cashes指“多笔现金”或“多处现金”。
