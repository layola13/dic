countercriticism:/ˈkaʊntərˌkrɪtɪsɪzəm/
n.|反批评，反驳|An act of criticizing someone or something that has criticized you. |例句：Her countercriticism of the review was well-received. 她对评论的反驳很受欢迎。|例句：He offered a strong countercriticism to the accusations. 他对这些指控提出了强有力的反驳。|近义词：rebuttal, refutation, response|反义词：criticism, attack|词性：名词
