decompartmentalization:/ˌdiːkəmpɑːrtˌmɛntələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 去隔间化；去部门化；打破隔阂 | The act or process of breaking down barriers between separate departments or groups, often to improve communication and collaboration. | Example 1: The decompartmentalization of government agencies has led to more efficient use of resources. 政府机构去隔间化使得资源利用更高效。

Example 2:  Decompartmentalization of knowledge is crucial for innovation.  知识去隔间化对创新至关重要。 | Near Synonyms: integration, unification, consolidation, desegregation | Antonyms: compartmentalization, segregation, division, fragmentation | noun
