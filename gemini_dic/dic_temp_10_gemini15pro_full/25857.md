burion:/ˈbʊəriən/ | n. | 埋葬; 埋藏物 | The act of burying something, or something that has been buried. |  The burion of the time capsule marked the centenary of the town. 时间胶囊的埋藏标志着该镇的百年诞辰。| 近义词: interment, burial, inhumation | 反义词: exhumation, unearthing | 词性: 名词

词源: From Old French *buiron, from Late Latin *buriōnem, from Latin burīre “to bury”.
