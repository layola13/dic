commutabilities:/kəˌmjuːtəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |可交换性；交换性；对易性|The property of operations or quantities being commutable, meaning their order can be interchanged without affecting the result.  This is often seen in mathematics, particularly with matrices and operators.|The commutabilities of the matrices were analyzed. 矩阵的可交换性得到了分析。|synonyms: interchangeability, exchangeability |antonyms: non-commutability, non-interchangeability|noun
