anticritical:/ˌæntiˈkrɪtɪkəl/
adjective
反对批评的；抑制批评的
Opposed to or discouraging criticism; tending to stifle or suppress critical thinking or expression.
The government's anticritical stance stifled public debate. 政府反对批评的立场扼杀了公众辩论。
His anticritical attitude made it difficult for his team to provide honest feedback.  他反对批评的态度使他的团队难以提供诚实的反馈。
近义词:uncritical, censorious, repressive
反义词:critical, receptive to criticism
词性:adjective
