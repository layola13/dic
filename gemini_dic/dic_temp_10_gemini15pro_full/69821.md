ferrate:/ˈfɛreɪt/ (US), /ˈfɛrɪt/ (UK)| n.|铁酸盐|A salt containing the anion FeO₄²⁻.  It contains iron in a high oxidation state, usually +6. |Potassium ferrate is a powerful oxidizing agent. 高铁酸钾是一种强氧化剂。|Synonyms:  iron(VI) oxide salt|Antonyms: N/A|noun


ferrate:/fəˈreɪt/ (US), /fəˈreɪt/ (UK)| v.|用铁酸盐处理|To treat or combine with a ferrate.|The wastewater was ferrated to remove organic pollutants.  废水用铁酸盐处理以去除有机污染物。|Synonyms: oxidize (in the context of using ferrates)|Antonyms: reduce|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ferrātus*, from ferrum ("iron") + -ātus (past participle suffix).
