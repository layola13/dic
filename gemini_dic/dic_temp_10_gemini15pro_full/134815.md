nonmimetic:/ˌnɒnˌmɪˈmɛtɪk/ | /ˌnɑːnˌmɪˈmɛtɪk/ | adj. | 非模仿的，非拟态的 | Not imitative of anything else; not representing natural forms or sounds accurately. |  His art is abstract and nonmimetic, focusing on color and form rather than depicting real-world objects. 他的艺术是抽象的和非模仿的，专注于颜色和形式，而不是描绘现实世界的物体。 |  The composer's nonmimetic music relies on complex rhythms and harmonies rather than melodies that evoke natural sounds.  这位作曲家的非模仿性音乐依赖于复杂的节奏和和声，而不是唤起自然声音的旋律。 |  abstract, nonrepresentational | mimetic, representational | adjective
