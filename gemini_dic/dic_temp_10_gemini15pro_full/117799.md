meline:/ˈmɛlaɪn/ |n.|黑色素；黑色素瘤|A black or dark-brown pigment occurring in the hair, skin, and iris of the eye in people and animals. It is responsible for tanning of skin exposed to sunlight.|Melanin is produced by specialized cells called melanocytes. 黑色素是由称为黑素细胞的特殊细胞产生的。|Melanin protects the skin from the harmful effects of ultraviolet radiation. 黑色素保护皮肤免受紫外线辐射的 harmful effects.|近义词:pigment|反义词:None|词性:noun
