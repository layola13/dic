drums:/drʌmz/| n.|鼓，鼓声|A percussion instrument consisting of a hollow cylinder with a membrane stretched over one or both ends, played by striking with sticks or the hands. |The drummer kept a steady beat on the drums. 鼓手保持着稳定的鼓点。|Synonyms: percussion, timpani|Antonyms: silence, quiet|noun

drums:/drʌmz/| v.|击鼓，敲击|To play the drums. |He drums his fingers on the table nervously. 他紧张地用手指敲击桌子。|Synonyms: beat, tap|Antonyms: stop, cease|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *drumme*, from Middle Dutch *tromme*, from Middle Low German *trumme*, of imitative origin.
