off-the-shelf:/ˌɒf ðə ˈʃɛlf/ (UK), /ˌɔːf ðə ˈʃɛlf/ (US)| adj.|现成的，非定制的|Available immediately and without the need for special modification or customization.| We bought an off-the-shelf software package. 我们购买了一个现成的软件包。|Example: The company decided to use an off-the-shelf solution rather than developing custom software.  这家公司决定使用现成解决方案，而不是开发定制软件。|Synonyms: ready-made, pre-packaged, stock, standard|Antonyms: custom-made, bespoke, tailor-made|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
This term originates from the practice of selecting readily available products from a store shelf, as opposed to ordering custom-made items.  "Off the shelf" literally means taking something directly from the shelf where it is displayed for sale.
