orchards:/ˈɔːrtʃərdz/
n.
果园；果树林
An enclosed area of land planted with fruit trees.
The farmer owns several apple orchards.  这位农民拥有几个苹果园。

近义词:fruit farm, grove
反义词:barren land, wasteland
词性:名词
