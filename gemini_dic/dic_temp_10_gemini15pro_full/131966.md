noncontumaciousnesses:/ˌnɒnkɒntjuːˈmeɪʃəsnəsɪz/
|n.|  不抗拒；顺从；服从|The noncontumaciousnesses of the defendants surprised the prosecution. 被告的顺从让控方感到惊讶。|
|英文解析: The state or quality of not being contumacious; obedience, compliance.|
|例句：Their noncontumaciousnesses facilitated the legal proceedings. 他们的顺从使得法律程序得以顺利进行。|
|例句：The judge commended the witnesses for their noncontumaciousnesses. 法官称赞了证人的配合。|
|近义词：obedience, compliance, submission, acquiescence|
|反义词：contumacy, defiance, resistance, rebellion|
|词性: noun|
