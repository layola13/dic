moe: /moʊ/ (US), /məʊ/ (UK)| n. | 萌 | A Japanese slang term used to describe feelings of strong affection mainly for fictional characters (especially in anime, manga, and video games) but also for real people. It is often associated with cuteness, innocence, or vulnerability. |  She has a real moe for that anime character. 她对那个动漫角色很有萌感。 | 近义词: cuteness, charm | 反义词: dislike, aversion | 词性: noun
