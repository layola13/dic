conjuncture's:/ˈkʌndʒʌŋktʃərz/
|n.| 机遇；时机；关头；（一系列事件的）结合，同时发生|A particular point in time, especially one considered in relation to the circumstances that exist at that time; a combination of events.|The government's decision came at a difficult conjuncture. 政府的决定是在一个困难的关头作出的。|近义词： juncture, moment, occasion, time, situation, circumstance, convergence, confluence|反义词: disjunction, separation|词性:名词
