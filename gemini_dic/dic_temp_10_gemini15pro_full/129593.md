non-communicant: /ˌnɒn kəˈmjuːnɪkənt/ (UK) /ˌnɑːn kəˈmjuːnɪkənt/ (US)
non-communicant: adjective
non-communicant: 中文翻译：未领圣餐的；不交流的；不传染的
non-communicant:  Not having received Holy Communion, especially within a particular church. Also more generally: not communicating; uncommunicative.  Relating to a disease: not transmissible from one person to another.
non-communicant: 例句1：The non-communicant members of the church were not allowed to partake in the sacrament. 教堂里未领圣餐的成员不允许领受圣礼。

non-communicant: 例句2：He remained non-communicant throughout the interrogation, refusing to answer any questions.  在整个审讯过程中，他保持沉默，拒绝回答任何问题。
non-communicant: 近义词：uncommunicative, reserved, silent, withdrawn, introverted, non-contagious (for diseases)
non-communicant: 反义词：communicative, outgoing, sociable, contagious (for diseases)
non-communicant: 词性：形容词
