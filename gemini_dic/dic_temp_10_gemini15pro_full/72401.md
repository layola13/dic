fluencies:/ˈfluːənsiz/| n. |流利度，流畅度|The state of being fluent, especially in speaking or writing a language.  The ability to express oneself easily and articulately. |She demonstrated great fluency in French. 她展现了极佳的法语流利度。|近义词：articulateness, eloquence, facility, smoothness|反义词:hesitancy, stammering|Noun
