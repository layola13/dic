exsputory: /ɛkˈspjʊtəri/ | adj. | 赎罪的，补偿的 | Relating to or serving as expiation or atonement; making amends for a wrong. |  The judge ordered him to perform exsputory community service. 法官命令他进行赎罪性质的社区服务。 |  The family offered an exsputory sacrifice to appease the gods. 这家人献上了赎罪的祭品来安抚神明。| 近义词: atoning, compensatory,  propitiatory | 反义词: unforgiving, unrepentant | 词性: adjective | 词源: From Latin *exsputorius, from exsputare "to spit out, reject," figuratively "to atone for."  The word is rare and sometimes misspelled as "expiatory," which has a similar meaning.
