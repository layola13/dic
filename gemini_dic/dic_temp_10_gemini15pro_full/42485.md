counterfort: /ˈkaʊntərfɔːrt/ (US) /ˈkaʊntəfɔːt/ (UK)| n.|支撑结构，扶壁；支持物，精神支柱|A structure built against a wall to strengthen it, especially one of sloping masonry or brickwork built against the foot of a retaining wall or terrace.|The cathedral walls are supported by massive counterforts.|近义词:buttress, support, reinforcement|反义词:weakness|词性:名词

counterfort: /ˈkaʊntərfɔːrt/ (US) /ˈkaʊntəfɔːt/ (UK)| vt.|加固，支持|To strengthen or support something.|The government's policies are counterforted by strong public opinion.|近义词:strengthen, support, reinforce|反义词:weaken|词性:动词
