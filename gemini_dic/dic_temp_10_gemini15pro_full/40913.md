cooled-down:/kuːld daʊn/
adjective|冷却的；平静的；镇定的|Having lost heat and become cooler; calm and composed after a period of anger or excitement.| The cooled-down lava formed interesting rock formations. 冷却后的熔岩形成了有趣的岩层。| synonyms: calmed, composed, relaxed, serene, tranquil | antonyms: heated, agitated, excited, frenzied | adjective

cooled-down:/kuːld daʊn/
verb | 冷却；平静下来；镇定下来|(Of a person or their feelings) become less strong.|  She needed a few minutes to cool down after the argument. 争吵后她需要几分钟冷静下来。| synonyms: calm down, settle down, compose oneself, relax | antonyms: heat up, get excited, get worked up | verb
