indulgently:/ɪnˈdʌl.dʒənt.li/
adverb
放纵地，纵容地
in a way that allows someone to have or do what they want, even if this is not good for them
He smiled indulgently at his little daughter. 他对着他的小女儿纵容地笑了笑。

indulgently:/ɪnˈdʌl.dʒənt.li/
adverb
溺爱地
in a way that shows you love and care for someone very much, sometimes too much
She looked at him indulgently. 她溺爱地看着他。

indulgently:/ɪnˈdʌl.dʒənt.li/
adverb
宽容地
in a way that is kind and patient, especially towards someone's weaknesses or mistakes
The teacher indulgently overlooked the student's late arrival. 老师宽容地忽略了学生的迟到。


近义词: leniently, permissively, tolerantly, forgivingly
反义词: strictly, severely, harshly, austerely


词源: From the Latin word "indulgere" meaning "to be kind or tender to," "to yield to," or "to give oneself up to."  The word evolved through French as "indulgent" and eventually to the adverb form "indulgently."
