nonliquidly:/nɒnˈlɪkwɪdli/
adverb
非流动地；不流通地
in a way that is not readily convertible into cash.  The assets were nonliquidly held.
The company's assets are tied up in real estate and are therefore nonliquidly held. 公司的资产被房地产套牢，因此流动性很差。
近义词: illiquidly, unliquidly
反义词: liquidly
词性: adverb
