exauthorize:/ɛksˈɔːθəˌraɪz/ |  v. | 取消授权，撤销授权 | To revoke or cancel someone's authorization or permission to do something. | The company exauthorized his access to confidential files after he resigned.  公司在他辞职后取消了他访问机密文件的授权。|  revoke, rescind, cancel, withdraw | authorize, empower, commission | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From ex- (meaning "out of" or "from") + authorize (meaning "to give authority to").  The prefix "ex-" indicates removal or reversal of the authorization.
