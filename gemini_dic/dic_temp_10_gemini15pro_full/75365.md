froufrou:/ˈfruːfruː/ (US), /ˈfruːfruː/ (UK)| n. adj.| 褶饰；繁复的装饰；华而不实的东西|Ornamental frills or flounces, typically on women's clothing; showy or ostentatious display.|The dress was covered in froufrou and sequins. 这件衣服上满是褶饰和亮片。|近义词：frills, flounces, ornamentation, embellishment, furbelow|反义词：plainness, simplicity|词性:名词, 形容词

froufrou:/ˈfruːfruː/ (US), /ˈfruːfruː/ (UK)| adj.| 华而不实的；繁琐的|Showy or ostentatious; excessively elaborate.|She dismissed the new designs as too froufrou. 她认为这些新设计过于华而不实。|近义词：ostentatious, showy, fussy, elaborate|反义词：simple, understated, plain|词性:形容词
