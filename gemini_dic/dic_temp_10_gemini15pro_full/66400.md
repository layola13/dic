executing:/ˈeksɪkjuːtɪŋ/| v.|执行，实行，处决|Carrying out a plan, order, or course of action; putting to death.|The company is executing a new marketing strategy. 公司正在执行新的营销策略。|synonyms: implementing, performing, carrying out, enacting, administering, enforcing|antonyms: neglecting, abandoning, delaying, postponing|verb

executing:/ˈeksɪkjuːtɪŋ/| adj.|执行中的，履行中的|Currently being carried out or performed.|The executing program encountered an error. 执行中的程序遇到了错误。|synonyms: ongoing, in progress, current, active|antonyms: finished, completed, stopped, halted|adjective
