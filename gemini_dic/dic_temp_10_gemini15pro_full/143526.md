outground:/ˈaʊtɡraʊnd/
n.
外场; 郊区运动场
An area of land, typically in the suburbs, used for sports, especially cricket.
The cricket club has a beautiful outground where they host matches.  这个板球俱乐部有一个漂亮的郊区运动场，在那里他们举办比赛。
The outground was perfect for a friendly game of football.  这块外场非常适合进行一场友好的足球比赛。
Synonyms: playing field, sports ground, recreation ground
Antonyms: indoor arena, stadium

词性: 名词
