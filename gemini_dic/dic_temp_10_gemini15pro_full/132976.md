nonencyclopaedic:/ˌnɒnɪnˌsaɪkləˈpiːdɪk/ | adj. | 非百科全书式的；不适合百科全书的 | Not suitable for or characteristic of an encyclopaedia; not containing comprehensive information on a wide range of topics. | This blog post is interesting, but rather nonencyclopaedic. 这篇博客文章很有趣，但不太像百科全书式的。| Near Synonyms: informal, unscholarly, subjective, anecdotal | Antonyms: encyclopaedic, comprehensive, scholarly, objective | adjective
