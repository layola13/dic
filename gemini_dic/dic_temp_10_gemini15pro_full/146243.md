overexpressivenesses:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈsprɛsɪvnɪsɪz/
|n.| 过度表达；过度表现 |The state of being excessively expressive; the tendency to express oneself too much or too intensely.|Her overexpressivenesses often led to misunderstandings. 她的过度表达经常导致误解。|近义词：exaggeration, effusiveness|反义词：reticence, reserve|词性:名词

词源: over- (prefix) + expressive (adjective) + -ness (suffix) + -es (plural suffix)

"over-" 表示“过度”、“超过”。 "expressive" 表示“富于表现力的”。 "-ness" 将形容词转换为名词，表示状态或性质。 "-es" 构成名词的复数形式。
