blasting:/ˈblæstɪŋ/ /ˈblɑːstɪŋ/| n. v. adj.|爆破，爆炸；疾风，强风；（非正式）痛快，极好的|The blasting of the rock face sent debris flying everywhere. 岩石表面的爆破使碎屑飞得到处都是。|近义词：explosion, detonation, blowing up, gale, blast|反义词：implosion, building, construction|词性:名词，动词，形容词
词源：From Middle English *blastinge, verbal noun from blasten (“to blow, blast”). Equivalent to blast + -ing.
