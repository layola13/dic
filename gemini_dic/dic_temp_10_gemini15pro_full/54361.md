dishonoured:/dɪsˈɒnəd/ (UK) /dɪsˈɑːnərd/ (US)| adj.|拒付的，不兑现的；蒙羞的，丢脸的|Not paid or not fulfilled; brought shame or disgrace upon.|The dishonoured cheque bounced. 这张拒付的支票被退票了。|Synonyms: unpaid, rejected, disgraced, shamed|Antonyms: honoured, respected, paid|adjective

dishonour:/dɪsˈɒnə(r)/ (UK) /dɪsˈɑːnər/ (US)|  vt. n.|拒付，不兑现；使蒙羞，玷辱|To refuse to pay (a cheque or bill); to bring shame or disgrace upon.|He dishonoured his family by his actions. 他的行为使他的家族蒙羞。|Synonyms: disgrace, shame, default, decline|Antonyms: honour, respect, pay|verb/noun

词源: From Old French *deshonorer*, from des- (expressing negation) + honorer (to honour).
