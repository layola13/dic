born-agains:/ˌbɔːrn əˈɡɛnz/ | n. | 重生者，更生信徒 |  A person who has experienced a spiritual rebirth or conversion, typically to evangelical Christianity.  They often describe this as being "born again". |例句：The born-agains held a revival meeting. 重生信徒举行了一场复兴聚会。


例句：Many born-agains feel a strong connection to their church community. 许多重生信徒感觉与他们的教会群体有着强烈的联系。

近义词：convert, believer | 反义词：non-believer, atheist | 词性：名词

词源：born (past participle of bear) + again (adv.) -  The term originates from the biblical passage in John 3:3, where Jesus tells Nicodemus that he must be "born again" to enter the kingdom of God.
