administratorship: /ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪtərʃɪp/
n.
管理职位；遗产管理权
The position or office of an administrator. The legal right to administer the estate of a deceased person.
He was granted administratorship of his late father's estate. 他被授予了已故父亲的遗产管理权。

The administratorship of the charity was given to a qualified professional.  这个慈善机构的管理职位交给了合格的专业人士。

近义词: administration, management, directorship, stewardship
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps) subordination, dependency
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From administrator + -ship.  "Administrator" ultimately comes from Latin "administrare" (to manage, serve), which is derived from "ad" (to) + "ministrare" (to serve).  "-ship" is an Old English suffix denoting state, condition, or office.
