dentiform:/ˈdɛntɪfɔːrm/
adjective
齿状的，牙形的
Shaped like a tooth.
The dentiform leaves of the plant are unique.  这种植物的齿状叶子很独特。

The fossilized remains showed a dentiform structure. 这块化石残骸显示出一种齿状结构。
synonym: tooth-shaped, odontoid
antonym: N/A
词性: adjective
