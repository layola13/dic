exodic:/ɛkˈsɒdɪk/ | adj. | 外生的，向外的 |  Relating to or characterized by an outward flow or migration; proceeding outward; (of a crystalline rock) formed by crystallization from a magma that has been intruded into surrounding rocks. | The exodic eruption sent lava flowing down the mountainside. 火山向外的喷发使熔岩流下山坡。| 近义词: outward, external, efferent | 反义词: endodic, internal, afferent | 词性: 形容词
词源: From Greek *exodos* "a going out," from *ex* "out" + *hodos* "way".
