contradicters:/ˌkɒntrəˈdɪktəz/ | n. | 反驳者，驳斥者 |  People who contradict, or deny the truth of, something. | The contradicters were quickly silenced by the overwhelming evidence. 反驳者很快就被压倒性的证据驳倒了。|  The contradicters presented alternative theories. 反驳者提出了替代的理论。 | synonyms: dissenters, opponents, challengers, gainsayers, refuters | antonyms: supporters, proponents, advocates, confirmers | noun
