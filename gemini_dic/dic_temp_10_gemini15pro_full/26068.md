bushmen:/ˈbʊʃmən/| n.|布须曼人|A member of any of several indigenous hunter-gatherer peoples of southern Africa.|例句：The Bushmen have lived in the Kalahari Desert for thousands of years. 布须曼人在卡拉哈里沙漠生活了数千年。

例句：The documentary explored the traditional way of life of the Bushmen. 这部纪录片探索了布须曼人的传统生活方式。

|近义词：San people, hunter-gatherers|反义词: (No direct antonym, could use "settled agriculturalists")|词性:noun
