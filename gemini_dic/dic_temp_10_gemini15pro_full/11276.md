arctic:/ˈɑːrktɪk/ (US) /ˈɑːktɪk/ (UK)| adj. n.|北极的，北极地区|Relating to the region around the North Pole.  Also, the Arctic region itself.|The arctic fox has thick fur to survive the cold. 北极狐有厚厚的皮毛以抵御寒冷。|The Arctic is a fragile environment. 北极是一个脆弱的环境。|近义词：polar, frigid, northern|反义词:antarctic, tropical|词性:adjective, noun
词源:From Late Middle English *artick, from Old French artique and directly from Late Latin arcticus, from Ancient Greek ἀρκτικός (arktikós, “of the north, northern”), from ἄρκτος (árktos, “bear, Great Bear, north”). The name refers to the constellation Ursa Major, the Great Bear, which is prominent in the northern sky.
