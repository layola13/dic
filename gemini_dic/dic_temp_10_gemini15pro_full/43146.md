cozier:/ˈkoʊziər (US) /ˈkəʊziər (UK)| adj.|更舒适的，更惬意的|Comparative form of cozy: more cozy.  Meaning warm, comfortable, and inviting, often suggesting a small and intimate space.  It implies a greater degree of coziness than the base word "cozy".|The new armchair makes the living room even cozier.|近义词：snug, comfortable, warm, homey|反义词：uncomfortable, cold, stark, austere|词性:形容词

词源：From cozy + -er (comparative suffix). Cozy is from Scots cose, cosie "snug, sheltered," perhaps from Middle Low German kose "a hollow; choice morsel," or related to Old Norse kása "to doze, sleep."
