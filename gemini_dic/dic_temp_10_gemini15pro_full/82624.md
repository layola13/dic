governance:/ˈɡʌvərnəns/ | n. | 治理；管理；统治 | The process of governing something or someone; the way that power is used to control a country, organization, etc. | The company's governance is under scrutiny after the recent scandal.  这家公司在最近的丑闻后，其治理受到了审查。 | synonyms: administration, management, rule, control | antonyms: misrule, anarchy, chaos | noun

governance:/ˈɡʌvərnəns/ | n. | 统治方式；管理方法 | The system or manner of governing. | The new governance structure has proven to be more efficient. 新的治理结构已被证明更加有效。| synonyms: system, method, approach, procedure | antonyms: disorder, disorganization | noun
