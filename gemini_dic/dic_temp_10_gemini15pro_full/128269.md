neuropathy:/nʊˈrɒpəθi/ /nʊˈrɑːpəθi/| n. |神经病，神经障碍|A disorder of the peripheral nerves, usually causing weakness, numbness, and pain.|Example: Diabetic neuropathy is a common complication of diabetes. 糖尿病神经病变是糖尿病的常见并发症。|Example: He suffers from peripheral neuropathy in his legs. 他的腿患有周围神经病变。|近义词：neuralgia, nerve damage|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "nerve health")|词性:noun


词源:
From Greek neuro- "nerve" + -pathy "suffering"
