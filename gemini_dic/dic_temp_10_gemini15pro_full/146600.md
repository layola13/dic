overgrieves:/ˌoʊvərˈɡriːvz/| v. |过度悲伤，过分悲伤|To grieve excessively or beyond what is considered normal or healthy.  It implies an excessive or prolonged period of mourning.|She overgrieved the loss of her pet hamster, refusing to leave her room for days.| 近义词:mourn excessively, lament excessively|反义词:underreact, be apathetic|词性:动词
