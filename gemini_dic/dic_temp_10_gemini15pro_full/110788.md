lining: /ˈlaɪnɪŋ/ | n. | 衬里，内衬；里子 |A layer of material covering the inner surface of something. | The coat had a warm fur lining. 这件外套有一层温暖的毛皮衬里。

The lining of the curtains was faded. 窗帘的衬里褪色了。

近义词: inner layer, covering, inside, interior
反义词: exterior, outside

词性: noun

词源: From Middle English *linynge, from Old English *līning, from Proto-Germanic *līningō, from the verb *līnijaną (“to cover, to line”), from Proto-Indo-European *ley- (“to smear, to stick”).  Cognate with Dutch voering (“lining”), German Futter (“lining, fodder”), Swedish foder (“lining, fodder”).
