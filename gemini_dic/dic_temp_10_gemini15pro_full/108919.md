leech:/liːtʃ/| n. v.|水蛭；吸血鬼；榨取他人钱财的人；依附他人过活者|A bloodsucking worm that lives in water.  A person who extorts money from others or lives off others. |例句：The leech attached itself to my leg. 水蛭吸附在我的腿上。


例句：He's a leech on his family, always borrowing money. 他像个吸血鬼一样依附着他的家人，总是借钱。|近义词：parasite, bloodsucker, freeloader|反义词:host, benefactor|词性:名词, 动词

leech:/liːtʃ/|v.|吸附；依附；榨取|To cling to and suck blood from; to exploit or drain resources from.|例句：He leeched off his friends for years. 他多年来一直依赖朋友生活。


例句：The company leeched money from its investors. 这家公司榨取了投资者的钱财。|近义词：sponge, exploit, drain|反义词:give, support|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *lēce, from Proto-Germanic *lēkaz. Cognate with German Leech.
