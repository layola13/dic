germogen:/ˈdʒɜːrmədʒɛn/ | n. | 萌发素，萌发层 | A layer of meristematic cells below the epidermis of certain lichens, capable of developing into a new lichen thallus. |  Lichens propagate vegetatively by soredia and isidia which are formed from the germinal layer or germinative zone of the thallus. 地衣通过粉芽和裂芽进行营养繁殖，这些粉芽和裂芽是由菌体的萌发层或萌发区形成的。| synonym: generative layer, embryonic layer | antonym:  (No direct antonym, potentially differentiated tissue) | noun
