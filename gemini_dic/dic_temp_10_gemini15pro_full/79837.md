glyphic:/ˈɡlɪfɪk/| adj.|雕刻的；象形文字的|Relating to carving or engravings, especially hieroglyphics.|The ancient temple walls were covered in glyphic inscriptions. 古老的寺庙墙壁上覆盖着雕刻的铭文。|近义词：carved, engraved, inscribed|反义词:smooth, plain|词性:形容词
glyph:/ɡlɪf/| n.|雕刻符号；象形文字|A carved or incised figure or symbol; a hieroglyph.|The glyphs on the Rosetta Stone helped scholars decipher ancient Egyptian writing. 罗塞塔石碑上的象形文字帮助学者们破译了古埃及文字。|近义词：symbol, character, hieroglyph|反义词: |词性:名词
