avid-for:/ˈævɪd fɔːr/ | adj. phrase |渴望的，热衷于...的 | Having a keen interest in or enthusiasm for something. | He is avid for knowledge. 他渴望知识。 |  She's an avid reader of mystery novels. 她热衷于阅读推理小说。 | 近义词: eager, keen, enthusiastic, passionate | 反义词: apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic | 词性: 形容词短语

词源: "avid" comes from the Latin "avidus," meaning "eager" or "greedy."  "For" indicates the target of the avidity.
