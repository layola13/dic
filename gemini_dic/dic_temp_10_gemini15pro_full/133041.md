nonenviousnesses:/ˌnɒnˈɛnviəsnɪsɪz/
|n.| 无嫉妒；不羡慕 |The nonenviousnesses of the team members contributed to their success.  团队成员之间没有嫉妒，这促成了他们的成功。|
|英文解析: The state or quality of being nonenvious; lack of envy.  It refers to the absence of the feeling of wanting what someone else has.|
|例句: Her nonenviousness was admirable, even when her colleagues received promotions. 即使她的同事升职了，她不嫉妒的态度也令人钦佩。

|例句:  He displayed a remarkable nonenviousness towards his more successful brother.  他对更成功的兄弟表现出一种非凡的不嫉妒。|
|近义词: lack of envy,  generosity, magnanimity|
|反义词: envy, jealousy, covetousness|
|词性: noun|
