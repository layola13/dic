dystrophy:/ˈdɪstrəfi/| n. |营养不良; 肌营养不良|A progressive degeneration and weakening of the muscles.  It is a disorder in which an organ or tissue of the body wastes away.|Muscular dystrophy is a debilitating disease. 肌营养不良是一种使人衰弱的疾病.|Synonyms: degeneration, atrophy, wasting|Antonyms: growth, development|noun
