hallelujahs:/ˌhælɪˈluːjəz/ | n. |哈利路亚；赞美上帝之语| An exclamation of praise to God. It is a transliteration of the Hebrew word הַלְלוּיָהּ (hallelu-Yah), meaning "Praise Yah" (Yah being a shortened form of Yahweh, the name of God).  | The choir sang hallelujahs at the end of the service.  唱诗班在礼拜结束时唱起了哈利路亚。 | synonyms: praise, acclamation, worship, adoration | antonyms: blasphemy, curse, condemnation | noun
