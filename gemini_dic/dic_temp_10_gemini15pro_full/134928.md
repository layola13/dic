nonmutinousness:/ˌnɒnˌmjuːtɪˈnəsnəs/
noun
不叛逆，不暴动，顺从
The state or quality of not being mutinous; obedience, loyalty.
The crew demonstrated their nonmutinousness by continuing to work despite harsh conditions.  船员们尽管条件恶劣，仍然继续工作，表明他们没有叛变。
Near synonyms: obedience, loyalty, compliance, submissiveness, docility
Antonyms: mutinousness, rebelliousness, sedition, insurrection, defiance
