overrealisms: /ˌoʊvərˈriːəlɪzəmz/ | n. | 超现实主义（复数） |  A style or genre characterized by an exaggerated depiction of reality, often to the point of absurdity or dreamlike quality, emphasizing the subconscious or irrational. It builds upon the foundations of surrealism but pushes the boundaries further, intensifying the bizarre and fantastical elements. |  The artist's latest works explore the unsettling and dreamlike landscapes of overrealisms. 艺术家最新的作品探索了超现实主义令人不安和梦幻般的风景。|  His paintings, filled with melting clocks and distorted figures, exemplify overrealisms. 他的画作充满了融化的时钟和扭曲的人物，是超现实主义的典范。 | Near Synonyms: surrealisms, hyperrealisms,  | Antonyms: realism, naturalism | noun
