conservatorship:/kənˈsɜːrvətərʃɪp/ | n. | 监护权，保护权 |  A legal right given to a person to manage the property and financial affairs of someone deemed incapable of doing so themselves, such as a minor, an incapacitated adult, or an estate. |  The court placed Britney Spears under a conservatorship controlled by her father.  法院将布兰妮·斯皮尔斯置于她父亲控制的监护之下。 | guardianship, trusteeship, custodianship | autonomy, independence | noun

conservatorship:/kənˈsɜːrvətərʃɪp/ | n. | 保护状态 |  The state of being under a conservatorship. |  The conservatorship lasted for thirteen years.  保护状态持续了十三年。| protection, custody, guardianship | freedom, liberty | noun
