bumpkin:/ˈbʌmpkɪn/| n.|乡巴佬，土包子|A bumpkin is a person from the countryside who is considered to be awkward and unsophisticated.|He's a bumpkin who's never been to a big city. 他是个从未去过大城市的乡巴佬。|The bumpkin was amazed by the skyscrapers. 这个乡巴佬被摩天大楼惊呆了。|近义词：yokel, rustic, hick|反义词：sophisticate, cosmopolitan|词性：名词
