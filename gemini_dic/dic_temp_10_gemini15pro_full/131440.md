noncoalescence: /ˌnɒnˌkəʊəˈlɛsəns/ | n. | 不聚结，不合并 | The noncoalescence of the oil droplets in the emulsion was surprising. |  The failure of two or more things to join or merge together. | Example 1: The noncoalescence of the water droplets on the waxed surface demonstrated the hydrophobic properties of the wax. 水滴在蜡质表面上的不聚结证明了蜡的疏水性。

Example 2:  The noncoalescence of the political factions led to further division within the country.  政治派系的不合并导致了该国进一步的分裂。 | Near synonyms: separation, disunion, division, non-fusion | Antonyms: coalescence, fusion, union, merging | noun
