besets:/bɪˈsɛts/
verb
困扰，围攻
trouble persistently; assail.
例句：The team was beset by injuries all season.  这个团队整个赛季都受到伤病困扰。

例句：Doubts beset her as she considered the offer. 当她考虑这份工作时，疑虑困扰着她。
近义词：trouble, plague, harass, beleaguer, afflict
反义词：aid, assist, help
词性：v.

Etymology:
From Middle English *bisetten*, from Old English *besettan* (“to set around, surround, beset”), from *be-* (“around”) + *settan* (“to set, place”).  The sense of "to attack on all sides" arose from the image of being surrounded by enemies.
