farrowed:/ˈfæroʊd/
verb
产仔（猪）; 生（小猪）
past tense and past participle of farrow.  A sow farrowed eight piglets. 母猪产下了八只小猪。
The sow farrowed a large litter. 这头母猪产了一窝小猪。
近义词: delivered, birthed, whelped (用于其他动物)
反义词:  N/A
词性: verb


farrow:/ˈfæroʊ/
verb
(指母猪)产仔; 生（小猪）
The sow is expected to farrow next week. 预计这头母猪下周会产仔。
Farmers carefully monitor their sows when they are about to farrow.  农民们在母猪即将分娩时会仔细观察它们。
近义词: give birth (to piglets), deliver (piglets), whelp (用于其他动物)
反义词: N/A
词性: verb

farrow:/ˈfæroʊ/
noun
一窝小猪
The farmer proudly showed off his new farrow. 农民自豪地展示了他新出生的一窝小猪。
The farrow was healthy and playful. 这窝小猪很健康，也很活泼。
近义词: litter (of piglets)
反义词: N/A
词性: noun



词源:  Old English *fearh, from Proto-Germanic *farxaz "young pig".  Related to Old High German farah, German Ferkel.
