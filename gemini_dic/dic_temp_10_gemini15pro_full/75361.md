frothing:/ˈfrɒθɪŋ/ (UK), /ˈfrɔːθɪŋ/ (US)| adj.| 起泡沫的；愤怒的；狂暴的 | Covered with or resembling froth; (figuratively) extremely angry or agitated. | The dog was frothing at the mouth. 狗的嘴里都是白沫。| synonyms: foaming, bubbling, spuming, raging, furious | antonyms: calm, peaceful | adjective

froth:/frɒθ/ (UK), /frɔːθ/ (US)| n. v. | 泡沫；(v.)起泡沫 | A mass of small bubbles formed on or in a liquid; (v.) to form or exude froth. |  The beer had a thick head of froth. 啤酒上面有一层厚厚的泡沫。| synonyms: foam, lather, suds, effervescence (n.); foam, bubble, fizz (v.) | antonyms: flatness, stillness (n.) | noun, verb
