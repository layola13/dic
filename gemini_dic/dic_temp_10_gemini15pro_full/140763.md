omnivorous:/ɒmˈnɪvərəs/ | adj. | 杂食的 | Eating or able to eat both plants and animals. | Example 1: Pigs are omnivorous animals, consuming a variety of foods. 猪是杂食动物，吃各种各样的食物。

Example 2: Humans are omnivorous and can thrive on a diet of both meat and vegetables.  人类是杂食动物，可以靠肉类和蔬菜的饮食茁壮成长。| Near synonyms: all-devouring, polyphagous | Antonyms: herbivorous, carnivorous | adjective
