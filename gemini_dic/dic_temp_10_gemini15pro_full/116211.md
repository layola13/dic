masochistic:/ˌmæsəˈkɪstɪk/ | adj. | 受虐狂的；自虐的 | Deriving from Masoch, the name of Leopold von Sacher-Masoch, an Austrian novelist whose fictional characters derived sexual pleasure from their own pain or humiliation.  Relating to or characterized by masochism. | He took a masochistic pleasure in working long hours. 他从长时间工作中获得了一种自虐式的快感。 | Synonyms: self-punishing, self-destructive, self-abusing | Antonyms: masochistic doesn't have a direct antonym, but consider pleasure-seeking, hedonistic | adjective
