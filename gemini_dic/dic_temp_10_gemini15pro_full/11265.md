arciform:/ˈɑːrsɪfɔːrm/
arciform| adj.|弓形的，拱形的|Having the shape of a bow or arch; curved like a bow.|The bridge had beautiful arciform arches supporting its structure. 这座桥有美丽的弓形拱支撑着它的结构。|近义词：arched, curved, bow-shaped|反义词:straight, linear|adjective
