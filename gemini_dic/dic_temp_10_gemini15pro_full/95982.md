incumbrancer:/ɪnˈkʌmbrənsər/| n.| 妨碍者，累赘，阻碍物|A person or thing that encumbers or hinders; a burden or impediment.|The mortgage was an incumbrancer on the property. 这笔抵押贷款是这处房产的负担。|Synonyms: burden, hindrance, obstacle, impediment, obstruction|Antonyms: aid, assistance, help, support|noun
