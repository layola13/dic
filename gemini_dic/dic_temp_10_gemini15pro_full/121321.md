misframe:/ˌmɪsˈfreɪm/| n.|歪曲；曲解|An inaccurate or unfair presentation or interpretation of something.|The politician claimed the report was a misframe of his actual position on the issue.  这位政治家声称该报告歪曲了他在这个问题上的实际立场。|misrepresentation, distortion, misinterpretation|accurate representation, truthful portrayal|noun
