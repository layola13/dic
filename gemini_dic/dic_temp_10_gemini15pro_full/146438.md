overfoulness:/ˌoʊvərˈfaʊlnəs/ | n. | 过度污秽；极度肮脏 | Excessive foulness; extreme filthiness. | The overfoulness of the abandoned building made it uninhabitable.  这栋废弃建筑物极其污秽，无法居住。 |  The overfoulness of the stable was overwhelming. 马厩里极其肮脏，令人难以忍受。 | 近义词：filthiness, squalor, dirtiness | 反义词：cleanliness, purity | 词性: 名词

词源:  "over" (Old English *ofer) + "foul" (Old English *fūl) + "-ness" (Old English -nes(s), a suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality).
