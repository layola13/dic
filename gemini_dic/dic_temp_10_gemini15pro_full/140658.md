ombudsmanship: /ˈɒmbʊdzmənʃɪp/ (UK) /ˈɑːmbədzmənʃɪp/ (US)
n.
申诉专员制度; 监察员制度;  申诉专员的职务或职权
The role of investigating complaints against an organization.  The quality of being an effective ombudsman.
The university has a strong tradition of ombudsmanship. 这所大学有着悠久的申诉专员制度传统。
Her experience in customer service made her an ideal candidate for the ombudsmanship. 她丰富的客户服务经验使她成为申诉专员的理想人选。
Synonym: mediation, advocacy, arbitration
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider: suppression,  ignorance, neglect)
noun
