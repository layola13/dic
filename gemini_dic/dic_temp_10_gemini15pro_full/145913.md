overdilate: /ˌoʊvərdaɪˈleɪt/
verb
过度扩张；过度膨胀
to cause to dilate excessively.  To expand beyond normal or healthy limits.
The doctor warned against using eyedrops that might overdilate the pupils. 医生警告不要使用可能导致瞳孔过度扩大的眼药水。

The medication caused the blood vessels to overdilate, leading to a drop in blood pressure.  药物导致血管过度扩张，引起血压下降。

近义词: overexpand
反义词: constrict, shrink
词性: verb
