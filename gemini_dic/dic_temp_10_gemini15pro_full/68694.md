fardage:/ˈfɑːrdɪdʒ/
n.
额外包装重量; 额外包装材料; 衬垫材料
The weight or measurement of extra packing material allowed to protect fragile goods.
The fardage added to the shipment protected the delicate porcelain.  额外包装保护了易碎的瓷器。
近义词：packing, stuffing, padding
反义词：None
词性：noun

fardage:/ˈfɑːrdɪdʒ/
n.
（为使货物达到规定重量而添加的）压舱物; 垫舱物
Ballast or packing material used to fill a ship's hold or make up a cargo to a minimum weight.
The ship required fardage to ensure stability in rough seas. 这艘船需要压舱物以确保在波涛汹涌的海面上保持稳定。
近义词：ballast
反义词：cargo
词性：noun
