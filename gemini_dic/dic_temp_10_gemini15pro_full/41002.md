cop-out:/ˈkɒpˌaʊt/ (US) /ˈkɑːpˌaʊt/| n.|逃避；推诿；借口|An act of withdrawing or an excuse for withdrawing from something difficult, demanding, or unpleasant.|He always has a cop-out when it's his turn to do the dishes.  他轮到洗碗时总是有借口推脱。|synonyms: excuse, evasion, dodge, pretext|antonyms: commitment, engagement, confrontation|noun


cop-out:/ˈkɒpˌaʊt/ (US) /ˈkɑːpˌaʊt/| adj.|逃避的；推诿的|Characterized by withdrawing or making excuses to withdraw from something difficult, demanding, or unpleasant.|His cop-out attitude towards chores frustrates everyone.  他对家务事的推诿态度让每个人都很沮丧。|synonyms: evasive, dodging|antonyms: committed, engaged, responsible|adjective
