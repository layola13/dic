detassel:/diːˈtæsəl/ | v. | 去雄；去除玉米的雄穗 | To remove the tassels from (a plant, especially corn) to prevent self-pollination and promote cross-pollination. |  Corn is detasseled to control pollination. 玉米去雄是为了控制授粉。|Synonyms: emasculate, unsex | Antonyms: pollinate | verb

detasseling:/diːˈtæsəlɪŋ/ | n. | 去雄；去除玉米的雄穗（的过程） | The act or process of removing tassels from a plant, typically corn. | Detasseling corn is a common summer job for teenagers. 去除玉米的雄穗是青少年常见的暑期工作。| Synonyms: emasculation | Antonyms: pollination | noun
