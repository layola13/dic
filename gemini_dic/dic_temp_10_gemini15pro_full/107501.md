lanciferous:/lænˈsɪfərəs/
adjective
持矛的；似矛的
bearing a lance or lances; shaped like a lance.
The lanciferous knight charged into battle. 持矛的骑士冲入战场。
近义词: spear-bearing, lancelike
反义词: unarmed
