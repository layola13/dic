collagenase:/kəˈlɑːdʒɪneɪs/| n. |胶原酶|An enzyme that breaks down collagen.|Collagenase is used in some medical treatments to break down scar tissue. 胶原酶在一些医学治疗中被用来分解疤痕组织。|Synonyms: protease, enzyme|Antonyms: inhibitor|noun

collagenase:/kəˈlɑːdʒɪneɪs/| n. |胶原蛋白酶|An enzyme that catalyzes the hydrolysis of collagen.|Bacterial collagenase is an important virulence factor.  细菌胶原酶是一种重要的毒力因子。|Synonyms:  matrix metalloproteinase, peptidase|Antonyms:  anticollagenase|noun
