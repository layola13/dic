epithalamium: /ˌɛpɪθəˈleɪmiəm/ | n. | 婚礼颂歌，结婚颂 | A song or poem celebrating a marriage. |  Edmund Spenser wrote a famous epithalamium for his own wedding.  埃德蒙·斯宾塞为他自己的婚礼写了一首著名的婚礼颂歌。

|Synonyms:  nuptial song, wedding poem, marriage hymn | Antonyms:  elegy, dirge, lament | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *epithalamion, from Ancient Greek ἐπιθαλάμιον (epithalámion, “nuptial song, wedding song”), from ἐπί (epí, “upon”) + θάλαμος (thálamos, “bridal chamber”).
