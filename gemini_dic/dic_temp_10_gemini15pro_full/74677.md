freebooty:/ˈfriːˌbuːti/| n.|战利品；掠夺物；非法所得|Goods or property seized illegally, especially by pirates or privateers. |The pirates divided the freebooty amongst themselves. 海盗们把战利品分了。|Synonyms: spoils, loot, plunder, pillage, booty|Antonyms:  earnings, wages|noun

freebooty:/ˈfriːˌbuːti/| n.|海盗行为；掠夺|The practice of engaging in robbery or piracy.|The captain was known for his freebooty and disregard for the law.  这位船长以其海盗行径和对法律的漠视而闻名。|Synonyms: piracy, buccaneering, privateering|Antonyms: lawfulness, legality |noun
