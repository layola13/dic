closen:/ˈkloʊzən/| v. |关闭，结束，终止|To bring to an end; conclude.|The chairman closen the meeting at 5 p.m. 主席在下午5点结束了会议。|近义词：close, conclude, finish, terminate|反义词:open, begin, start|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *closenen, from Old English *clōsian, from Proto-Germanic *klōzijaną, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱleh₂w- (“to hook, bend”). Cognate with Scots closen (“to close”), West Frisian klozje (“to lock, close”), Dutch kluizen (“to lock up, shut up”), German klusen (“to lock up, shut up”), Danish kluse (“to shut up”), Swedish klusa (“to shut up”).  Related to close.
