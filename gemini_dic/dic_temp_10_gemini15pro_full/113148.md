lunule:/ˈluːnjuːl/ | n. | 月牙，新月形，半月形 | A crescent-shaped figure or body; a lunula.  Specifically, the white, crescent-shaped area at the base of a fingernail. |  The lunule on my thumb is particularly prominent. 我的拇指上的月牙特别明显。 | synonyms: crescent, half-moon | antonyms: full moon | noun


lunula:/ˈluːnjʊlə/ | n. | 小月亮，月牙，(尤指指甲根部的)半月形 | A small moon or crescent-shaped area. In anatomy, a lunula is the whitish, half-moon shaped area at the base of a fingernail or toenail. |  The lunula of each fingernail is clearly visible. 每个指甲的月牙都清晰可见。| synonyms: crescent, half-moon | antonyms: full moon | noun
