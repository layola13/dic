electrofocusing:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈfoʊkəsɪŋ/
noun
电聚焦
The separation of molecules, such as proteins, by their isoelectric points in a stable pH gradient created by an electric field.
Electrofocusing is a powerful technique for separating proteins. 电聚焦是一种分离蛋白质的有效技术。
Isoelectric focusing is used to separate proteins based on their isoelectric point (pI). 等电聚焦用于根据蛋白质的等电点 (pI) 分离蛋白质。
synonyms: isoelectric focusing
antonyms: None (This is a specific technique; there isn't a direct opposite.)
