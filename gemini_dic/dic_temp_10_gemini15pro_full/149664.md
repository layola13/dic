pairwise:/ˈpɛərwaɪz/| adj. adv.| 成对的，逐对的|Considering or comparing each member of a set with every other member.|We performed a pairwise comparison of all the candidates. 我们对所有候选人进行了两两比较。|近义词：individually, separately, one-on-one |反义词: group, collective|词性:形容词/副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "pair" (meaning two) + "-wise" (meaning in the manner of). "Pair" comes from Old French "paire" and ultimately from Latin "paria" (equal things). "-wise" is a native English suffix.
