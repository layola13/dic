curvaciously:/kɜːrˈveɪʃəsli/

Etymology: From curve + -acious + -ly.  "Curve" comes from the Latin "curvus" meaning "bent, curved." "-acious" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "full of" or "given to." "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs.
