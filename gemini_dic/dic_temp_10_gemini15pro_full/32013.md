chepster:/ˈtʃɛpstər/ | n. | 廉价商品爱好者；爱占便宜的人 |  A person who seeks out cheap or discounted items, often to an excessive degree.  This term often carries a slightly negative connotation, implying that the person is overly focused on saving money, sometimes at the expense of quality or other considerations. | He's a real chepster, always looking for the best deals and coupons. 他是个十足的廉价商品爱好者，总是寻找最优惠的交易和优惠券。| Example:  She's such a chepster, she'll drive across town to save a few cents on gas. 她真是个爱占便宜的人，为了省几美分油钱，她会开车穿过整个城市。|Synonyms: bargain hunter, cheapskate, tightwad | Antonyms: spendthrift, big spender | noun
