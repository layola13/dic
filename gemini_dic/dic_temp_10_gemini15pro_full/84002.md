half-deservedly: /ˌhɑːf dɪˈzɜːrvɪdli/ /ˌhæf dɪˈzɜːrvɪdli/
|adv.|一半应得地；部分理所当然地|Meaning "in a way that is only partly deserved." It combines "half" with "deservedly," an adverb meaning "in a manner that is deserved."|He half-deservedly won the award; while he put in some effort, luck also played a significant role. 他赢得这个奖一部分是应得的；虽然他付出了一些努力，但运气也起了重要作用。|Partially deservedly, partly deservedly, somewhat deservedly|Undeservedly, completely undeservedly|adverb
