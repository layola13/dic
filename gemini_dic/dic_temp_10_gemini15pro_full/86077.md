ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *herknen*, from Old English *heorcnian* (“to listen, hearken”), from Proto-Germanic *hauzijan* (“to hear”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱeus-*. Cognate with Dutch *horken* (“to listen”), German *horchen* (“to listen”). Related to hear.
