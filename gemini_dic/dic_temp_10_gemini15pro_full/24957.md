brushing-aside:/ˈbrʌʃɪŋ əˈsaɪd/| adj.|漠视，不屑一顾，置之不理|The CEO brushed aside the concerns of the employees about the new policy. |例句：He brushed aside my objections. 他对我的反对意见置之不理。|例句：She brushed aside her tears and continued working. 她擦干眼泪，继续工作。|近义词：dismissing, ignoring, disregarding|反义词：addressing, acknowledging, considering|词性:现在分词 (present participle)  用作形容词

词源:  From "brush" (meaning to sweep lightly) + "aside" (to the side).  The original meaning related to physically brushing something to the side.  The metaphorical meaning of dismissing or ignoring something developed later.
