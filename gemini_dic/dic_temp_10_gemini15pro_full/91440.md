hygienists:/ˈhaɪdʒɪənɪsts/
n.
卫生员；保健员
A person who works in the field of hygiene, typically focusing on preventative care and promoting healthy habits.
例句：Dental hygienists clean teeth and provide other preventative dental care.  牙科保健员清洁牙齿并提供其他预防性牙科护理。

例句：The industrial hygienists inspected the factory for safety hazards.  工业卫生学家检查了工厂的安全隐患。

近义词：sanitarians, health professionals
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From hygiene + -ist. Hygiene comes from  Greek hygieinē (tekhnē) "(art of) health," from hygies "healthy, sound," perhaps related to hygieia "health," originally an epithet of Athena Hygieia, goddess of health, literally "living well."
