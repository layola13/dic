evasive:/ɪˈveɪsɪv/| adj.|逃避的，回避的，托辞的|Tending to avoid commitment or self-revelation, especially by responding only indirectly.|He gave an evasive answer when asked about his plans. 他在被问及计划时，给出了一个含糊其辞的答案。|近义词：elusive, ambiguous, equivocal|反义词：direct, forthright, candid|词性:形容词
evasive:/ɪˈveɪsɪv/| adj.|难以捉摸的，难以获得的|Difficult to find or catch.|The fox was too evasive for the hounds to catch.  这只狐狸太狡猾了，猎犬抓不到它。|近义词：elusive, slippery|反义词：accessible, attainable|词性:形容词

词源: Late 16th century: from late Latin evas- ‘escaped’, from the verb evadere, from e- (variant of ex- ) ‘out of’ + vadere ‘to go’.
