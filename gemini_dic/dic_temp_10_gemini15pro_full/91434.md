hygienes:/ˈhaɪdʒiːnz/
|n.|卫生；卫生学；保健法|Conditions or practices conducive to maintaining health and preventing disease, especially through cleanliness.|例句：Good hygiene can prevent the spread of infection. 良好的卫生习惯可以预防感染的传播。

例句：Dental hygiene is important for healthy teeth and gums.  牙齿卫生对牙齿和牙龈的健康很重要。|近义词：cleanliness, sanitation, purity|反义词：dirtiness, filth, uncleanliness|词性:名词
