nonblinding:/ˌnɒnˈblaɪndɪŋ/
adjective
非盲法的
Not blinded; without blinding.  This refers to a study or experiment where both the participants and the researchers are aware of the treatment being administered.
The nonblinding nature of the study introduced potential bias.  这项研究的非盲法性质引入了潜在的偏见。
The nonblinding trial allowed both doctors and patients to know who was receiving the placebo.  这个非盲试验让医生和病人都知道谁接受了安慰剂。
Synonyms: open-label, unblinded
Antonyms: blinded, double-blinded
词性: 形容词
