inacquaintance: /ˌɪnəˈkweɪntəns/
n.
不熟悉，不认识
lack of familiarity; the state of not knowing someone.
He showed his inacquaintance with the subject by his confused answers.  他对这个主题的不熟悉从他混乱的回答中显露出来。
近义词: unfamiliarity, ignorance, strangeness
反义词: familiarity, acquaintance, knowledge
词性: 名词

词源:
From in- (not) + acquaintance (familiarity with a person or subject).  由in- (不) + acquaintance (与人或事物的熟悉) 组成。
