fluty:/ˈfluːti/| adj.|像笛子似的，笛音的，清脆的|Resembling the sound of a flute; clear and high-pitched.|Her voice was high and fluty. 她的声音又高又像笛声。|近义词：melodious, sweet, high-pitched|反义词：raspy, hoarse, deep|词性:形容词
Etymology: From flute + -y.  Ultimately from Old French "flaüte," from Provençal "flauta." Related to Old High German "floita."
