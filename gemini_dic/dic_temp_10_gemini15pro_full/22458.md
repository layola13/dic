bookland:/ˈbʊklænd/| n.|图书世界；书籍的国度|A metaphorical realm or world inhabited by books and their characters.|He was lost in bookland, oblivious to the world around him. 他沉浸在书的海洋中，对外界的一切浑然不觉。|近义词: literary world, world of books|反义词: reality, real world|词性:noun

Etymology:
"book" (n.) Old English "boc" (beech tree, writing tablet, book) + "land" (n.) Old English "land" (territory, region).  The term evokes a metaphorical "land" or "world" composed of books and their contents.
