nonvolublenesses:/ˌnɒnvɒˈljuːbləsnɪsɪz/ /ˌnɑːnvəˈluːbləsnɪsɪz/
noun
寡言少语;沉默寡言
The state or quality of being nonvoluble; reticence or taciturnity.  It refers to a lack of fluency or readiness of speech.
His nonvolubleness made him seem aloof and mysterious. 他的沉默寡言使他显得冷漠而神秘。
synonyms: reticence, taciturnity, reserve, silence, uncommunicativeness
antonyms: volubility, loquacity, talkativeness, garrulity, effusiveness
