asking-prices:/ˈæskɪŋ ˌpraɪsɪz/| n.|要价，索价|The price that a seller is asking for something, especially a house or other property.  It's the initial price they hope to receive, and it can be subject to negotiation.|The asking-prices for houses in this area have skyrocketed.  这个地区的房屋要价飞涨。|Synonyms: asking price, list price, selling price|Antonyms: bid, offer|noun
