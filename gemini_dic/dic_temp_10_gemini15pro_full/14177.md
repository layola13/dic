auto-exposure:/ˌɔːtəʊɪkˈspəʊʒə(r)/ | n. |自动曝光|The automatic adjustment of the aperture and shutter speed of a camera to produce a correctly exposed image. |My camera's auto-exposure feature makes taking pictures so much easier. 我的相机的自动曝光功能使拍照变得更加容易。| The auto-exposure compensated for the bright sunlight. 自动曝光补偿了明亮的阳光。|Synonyms: automatic exposure, program AE |Antonyms: manual exposure| noun
