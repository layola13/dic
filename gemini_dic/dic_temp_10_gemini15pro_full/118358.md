meritedly:/ˈmerɪtɪdli/
adverb
应得地；理所当然地
deservedly, justly; in a way that is earned or deserved.
He was meritedly praised for his bravery. 他因勇敢而受到应得的赞扬。
Synonyms: deservedly, justly, rightfully, appropriately, suitably
Antonyms: undeservedly, unjustly, unfairly, inappropriately
词性: 副词
