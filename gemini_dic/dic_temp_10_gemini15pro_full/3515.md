afterwards:/ˈæftərwərdz/ (US) /ˈɑːftərwədz/ (UK)| adv.|然后，接着|Following something else in time.|He finished his work and afterwards went to the pub. 他完成了工作，然后去了酒吧。|近义词：then, next, subsequently|反义词:before, prior to|副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *afterwardes*, from Old English *æfterweardes*, equivalent to *after* + *-weardes* (a suffix indicating direction).
