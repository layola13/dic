funereal:/fjuːˈnɪəriəl/ /fəˈnɪriəl/| adj.|丧葬的，适合葬礼的；悲哀的，阴郁的|Suggestive of a funeral, especially in being dark, gloomy, or mournful.|The procession moved at a funereal pace.  队伍以丧礼般的步调前进。|The atmosphere in the room was funereal. 房间里的气氛很阴沉。|Synonyms: mournful, somber, gloomy, doleful, lugubrious|Antonyms: joyful, cheerful, celebratory, lively|adjective
