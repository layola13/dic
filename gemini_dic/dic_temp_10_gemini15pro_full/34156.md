clamatores:/klæməˈtɔːreɪz/
n.
shouters, criers
Those who shout or make a loud outcry.  This is the plural form of the Latin word "clamator," meaning "shouter" or "crier."
The clamatores in the marketplace made it difficult to hear the speaker.  市场上的叫喊声让人很难听到演讲者的声音。
近义词:criers, yellers, shriekers
反义词:whisperers, murmurers
词性: Noun (plural)

Etymology:
From Latin "clamātor," from "clamāre" (to shout, cry out).  The "-ores" ending indicates the masculine plural nominative case.  源自拉丁语 "clamātor"，来自 "clamāre"（喊叫，大声疾呼）。 "-ores" 结尾表示阳性复数主格。
