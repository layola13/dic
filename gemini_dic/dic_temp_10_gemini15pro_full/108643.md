leaf-like:/ˈliːfˌlaɪk/| adj.|叶状的；像叶子的|Resembling a leaf in shape or appearance.|The fern has delicate, leaf-like fronds.  这种蕨类植物有纤细的叶状复叶。|Synonyms: foliaceous, foliar, leaf-shaped|Antonyms: non-foliaceous|adjective

leaf-like:/ˈliːfˌlaɪk/| adj.|叶状的；像叶子的|Having the appearance or characteristics of a leaf.|The insect's wings were leaf-like, providing excellent camouflage.  这种昆虫的翅膀像叶子一样，提供了极好的伪装。|Synonyms:  foliate, leafy|Antonyms:  non-leafy|adjective
