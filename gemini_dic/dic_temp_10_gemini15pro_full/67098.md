explorator:/ɪkˈsplɔːreɪtər/| n.|探险家，勘探者|A person who explores or investigates a new or unfamiliar area or subject.|例句：The explorator ventured into the uncharted jungle.  这位探险家冒险进入了未知的丛林。

例句：The explorator documented her findings in a detailed journal.  这位探险家在详细的日记中记录了她的发现。|近义词：explorer, adventurer, pioneer, investigator|反义词:settler, follower|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *explorator, from Latin explorare "to search out, investigate," from ex- "out" + plorare "to cry out, wail, weep, lament; to explore, search out."  The sense evolution in plorare is perhaps from the idea of calling out to find someone.
