chorion: /ˈkɔːriɒn/ (UK), /ˈkɔːriɑːn/ (US) | n. | 绒毛膜 | The outermost membrane surrounding an embryo of a reptile, bird, or mammal. In mammals (including humans), it contributes to the formation of the placenta. |  The chorion develops villi that extend into the uterine wall. 绒毛膜会长出绒毛，延伸到子宫壁。

The chorion and the amnion together form the amniotic sac. 绒毛膜和羊膜共同构成羊膜囊。|  近义词:  extraembryonic membrane,  | 反义词: None | 词性: noun


chorionic: /kɔːriˈɒnɪk/ (UK), /kɔːriˈɑːnɪk/ (US) | adj. | 绒毛膜的 | Relating to or derived from the chorion. | Chorionic villus sampling (CVS) is a prenatal test. 绒毛膜绒毛取样 (CVS) 是一种产前检查。

Chorionic gonadotropin is a hormone produced during pregnancy.  绒毛膜促性腺激素是怀孕期间产生的一种激素。 | 近义词: placental (in some contexts) | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective
