intemperament: /ɪnˈtɛmpərəm(ə)nt/
n.
坏脾气，性情暴躁
Excessive irritability or irascibility; a tendency to become easily angered or upset.
His intemperament made it difficult for him to maintain relationships.  他暴躁的脾气使他难以维持人际关系。

近义词: irascibility, irritability, petulance, choler, excitability
反义词: patience, calmness, composure, equanimity


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin intemperantia "lack of moderation," from in- "not" + temperantia "moderation."  Borrowed into English in the 15th century.
