moderatrix: /ˈmɒdərətrɪks/ (UK), /ˈmɑːdərətrɪks/ (US)
noun
女主持人，女仲裁人，女版主
A woman who presides over a meeting, discussion, or online forum, ensuring that it runs smoothly and orderly.
The moderatrix skillfully guided the debate, ensuring all participants had a chance to speak. 女主持人巧妙地引导了辩论，确保所有参与者都有机会发言。
The online forum's moderatrix quickly deleted the offensive posts.  在线论坛的女版主迅速删除了攻击性帖子。
Synonyms: chairwoman, facilitator, mediator, administrator
Antonyms: participant, attendee
n.
