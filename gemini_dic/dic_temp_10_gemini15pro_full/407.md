abjudge:/əbˈdʒʌdʒ/| vt.|剥夺，拒绝给予|To deprive of a right by judicial decision.  To pronounce judgment against. |The king abjudged the traitor of all his lands and titles. 国王剥夺了叛徒所有的土地和头衔。

The court abjudged the defendant's claim. 法院驳回了被告的主张。|近义词：deprive, divest, deny, refuse|反义词：grant, bestow, award|Verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *abjudicare, from Latin ab- + judicare "to judge," from judex "judge."

USAGE->
