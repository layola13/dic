chronique:/krɔˈnik/| n. |编年史， chronicle, record|A chronicle is a factual written account of important or historical events in the order of their occurrence.|例句：He kept a chronicle of his travels. 他记录了他的旅行。|例句：The book is a chronicle of the town's history. 这本书是该镇历史的编年史。|近义词：record, history, annals, account|反义词:fiction|词性:名词
