hogchoker:/ˈhɑːɡˌtʃoʊkər/| n.|猪吸盘鱼，圆鳍鱼|A small flatfish (Trinectes maculatus) found in brackish and coastal waters of the western Atlantic. It's known for its ability to cling to the bottom.  It's also known as the American sole.|例句：The hogchoker is well camouflaged on the sandy bottom. 猪吸盘鱼在沙质底部伪装得很好。

例句：Hogchokers are often caught as bycatch in shrimp trawls. 猪吸盘鱼经常在捕虾拖网中被误捕。|近义词：flatfish, sole, flounder|反义词:|词性:noun
