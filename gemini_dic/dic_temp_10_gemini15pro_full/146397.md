overfluently: /ˌoʊvərˈfluːəntli/ | adv. | 过于流利地 | Speaking or writing with excessive fluency, often to the point of being unnatural or insincere.  He spoke overfluently, almost as if he had rehearsed it too many times. | 他说话过于流利，几乎就像排练过太多次一样。| She delivered her lines overfluently, making it sound artificial. | 她台词说得过于流利，听起来很做作。 | 近义词: too smoothly, excessively fluently | 反义词: haltingly, hesitantly | 词性: 副词

词源:

over- (prefix): meaning "excessively," "too much"
fluent (adjective): from Latin *fluentem (nominative fluens), present participle of fluere "to flow"
-ly (suffix): forming adverbs from adjectives
