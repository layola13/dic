condurrite:/kənˈdʌraɪt/| n.|砷铜矿|A naturally occurring mineral composed of copper arsenide, typically found as a dark grey to black metallic solid.  It is an ore of copper and arsenic.|The condurrite sample displayed a characteristic metallic luster. 这块砷铜矿样本呈现出典型的金属光泽。|The miners discovered a rich vein of condurrite. 矿工们发现了一条富含砷铜矿的矿脉。|Synonyms: domeykite, algodonite|Antonyms: None|noun
