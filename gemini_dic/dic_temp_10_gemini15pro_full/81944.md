grime:/ɡraɪm/| n.|污垢，尘垢，煤尘|dirt ingrained on the surface of something, especially skin; dirt; filth|例句：The mechanic's hands were covered in grime. 技工的手上沾满了油污。

例句：The walls were thick with grime. 墙上积满了污垢。|近义词：dirt, filth, soot, dust, muck|反义词：cleanliness, purity|词性:名词

grime:/ɡraɪm/| v.|使…变脏，沾污|make something dirty or stained|例句：His face was grimed with dust. 他的脸上沾满了灰尘。

例句：The windows were grimed with soot. 窗户上沾满了煤烟。|近义词：soil, dirty, stain, tarnish|反义词：clean, cleanse|词性:动词

词源:From Middle English *grimen, from Old Norse grima (“mask, helmet; soot”), from Proto-Germanic *grimmaz (“helmet, mask; soot”), from Proto-Indo-European *ghrey- (“to smear, rub”). Cognate with Danish grim (“soot”), Swedish grim (“mask, visor”). Related to grim.
