nonluminescence:/ˌnɒnˌluːmɪˈnɛsəns/
n.
无光，无冷光
absence of light emission, especially the emission of light at low temperatures that does not derive energy from incandescence.
The nonluminescence of the object made it difficult to see in the dark.  这个物体不发光，使得在黑暗中很难看到它。
近义词: darkness, blackness
反义词: luminescence, glow
词性: 名词

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + luminescence (from Latin "lumen" meaning "light")
