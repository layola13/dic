nonsensical:/nɒnˈsɛnsɪkəl/ | /nɑːnˈsɛnsɪkəl/ | adj. | 荒谬的，无意义的 | Lacking good sense; absurd. |  Her story was completely nonsensical; nobody believed it. 她的故事完全是胡说八道，没有人相信。 |  It's nonsensical to think you can learn a language in a week. 认为你可以在一周内学会一门外语是荒谬的。 |  absurd, foolish, illogical, irrational, silly |  sensible, logical, rational, reasonable | adjective
