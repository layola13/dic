lepidosauria:/ˌlɛpɪdoʊˈsɔːriə/| n. |鱗龍形下綱|A subclass of reptiles, distinguished by characteristics such as overlapping scales, a transverse cloacal slit, and the ability to autotomize their tails.  It includes lizards, snakes, and the tuatara.|Lizards and snakes are both members of the Lepidosauria. 蜥蜴和蛇都是鱗龍形下綱的成員。|The tuatara is the only surviving member of an order within Lepidosauria called Rhynchocephalia.  楔齒蜥是鱗龍形下綱喙頭目中唯一現存的成員。|近义词:squamata (somewhat)|反义词:None|词性:noun
