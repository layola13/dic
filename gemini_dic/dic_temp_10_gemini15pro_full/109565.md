let-in-on:/lɛt ɪn ɑːn/ | phrasal verb | 告诉（某人）秘密；让（某人）参与 | To allow someone to share a secret or be involved in something special. |  例句：They let me in on their plans for the surprise party. 他们让我参与了他们的惊喜派对计划。

例句：She let him in on the gossip about their boss. 她把关于他们老板的八卦告诉了他。

近义词：confide in, share with, include, involve | 反义词：exclude, keep out, keep secret | 词性：动词短语
