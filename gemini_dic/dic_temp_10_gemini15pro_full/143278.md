outdoes:/ˌaʊtˈdʌz/
verb
胜过，超过
to be better or more successful than someone or something else
He outdoes all his rivals in eloquence. 他在雄辩方面胜过所有对手。
surpass, excel, exceed, beat, top
fall behind, lose to
