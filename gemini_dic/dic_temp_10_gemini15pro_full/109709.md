leveed:/ˈlɛvid/ | adj. | 筑有堤防的；被堤坝围住的 | protected or surrounded by a levee | The leveed river protected the town from flooding. 筑有堤坝的河流保护了小镇免受洪水侵袭。 | embanked, diked | unprotected | adjective

Etymology: From the past participle of *levee* (v.), from French *lever*, "to raise." Ultimately from Latin *levāre*, "to lift, lighten."
