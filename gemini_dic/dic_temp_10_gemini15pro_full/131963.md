noncontumacious:/ˌnɒnkɒntjuːˈmeɪʃəs/
adjective
不蔑视法庭的，顺从法庭的
Not stubbornly disobedient to authority, especially to a court of law.
The noncontumacious defendant appeared in court as ordered. 顺从法庭的被告按照命令出庭了。
He offered a noncontumacious apology to the judge. 他向法官表示了真诚的道歉，并非蔑视法庭。
近义词: compliant, obedient, respectful, submissive
反义词: contumacious, defiant, rebellious, insubordinate
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *non contumax, from non "not" + contumax "stubborn, insolent," from con- "with" + tumēre "to swell."
