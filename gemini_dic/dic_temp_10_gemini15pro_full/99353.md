interdentil:/ˌɪntərˈdɛntəl/ | adj. n.|齿间的，齿间音；齿间摩擦音|Situated between the teeth; a sound produced by placing the tip of the tongue between the upper and lower teeth. |例句：The "th" sound in "thin" is an interdental fricative. “thin”中的“th”音是一个齿间摩擦音。

例句：He has a gap between his two front teeth, which makes it difficult to pronounce interdental sounds. 他的两颗门牙之间有缝隙，这使得他很难发出齿间音。|近义词：interdental consonant |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps sounds produced with other parts of the mouth like bilabial or alveolar) |词性：adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *interdentālis*, from inter- (“between”) + dentālis (“of or pertaining to teeth”), from dēns (“tooth”).
