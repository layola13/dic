opacular:/oʊˈpækjələr/ | adj. | 不透明的；浑浊的；模糊的 | Resembling an opaque spot or area, especially of the skin; clouded or obscured. |  The fish has opacular markings on its fins. 这条鱼的鳍上有不透明的斑纹。| Synonyms: opaque, cloudy, turbid, filmy, hazy | Antonyms: transparent, clear, translucent | Word Origin: From Latin *opaculus, diminutive of opacus (“shaded, dark”) | adjective
