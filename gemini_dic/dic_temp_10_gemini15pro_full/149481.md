padlock:/ˈpædlɒk/ (British) /ˈpædlɑːk/ (American)| n. v.|挂锁；用挂锁锁上|A lock that can be put on and removed. |
例句：He secured the shed with a sturdy padlock.  他用一把结实的挂锁锁住了棚屋。

例句：She padlocked the gate to keep out intruders. 她用挂锁锁上了大门以防入侵者。

近义词：lock, bolt, clasp, hasp|反义词:unlock|词性:名词, 动词


padlock:/ˈpædlɒk/ (British) /ˈpædlɑːk/ (American)| v.|用挂锁锁上|To fasten something with a padlock. |
例句：He padlocked the bicycle to the railing. 他用挂锁把自行车锁在了栏杆上。

例句：The chest was padlocked and chained. 箱子用挂锁锁着，还用链子拴着。


近义词：lock, secure, fasten|反义词:unlock|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *padlok, likely from Old English *pad "path" + loc "lock," suggesting a lock for a gate along a path.  Another theory suggests a connection to Old Norse padd "toad," perhaps referring to the shape of some early padlocks.
