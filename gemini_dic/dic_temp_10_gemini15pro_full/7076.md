anenterous: /ænˈɛntərəs/
adjective
无肠的，没有肠子的
Lacking an intestine; gutless.
Anenterous flatworms absorb nutrients directly through their skin.  无肠扁虫直接通过皮肤吸收营养。
These anenterous parasites obtain nutrients from their host. 这些无肠寄生虫从宿主身上获取营养。
近义词: gutless
反义词: enterozoic (having an intestine)
词性: adjective
词源: From Greek *anenteros, from an- ‘without’ + enteron ‘intestine’.
