gaffle:/ˈɡæfəl/| n. v.| 鱼叉;用鱼叉叉; |A gaffe is a barbed spear or hook for landing large fish.| He gaffled a large salmon. 他用鱼叉叉住了一条大鲑鱼。|近义词：spear, hook|反义词: release|n.
gaffle:/ˈɡæfəl/| v.| 用鱼叉叉; |To gaffe is to spear or hook with a gaffe.|  The fisherman gaffled the tuna. 渔夫用鱼叉叉住了金枪鱼。|近义词：spear, hook|反义词: release|v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *gafle, from Old North French gafele, of uncertain origin, probably from Frankish *gaffal or *gabal. Cognate with Dutch gaffel “fork,” German Gabel “fork.”
