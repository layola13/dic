actuated:/ˈæktʃueɪtɪd/
adj. |被驱动的；激发的|Driven or impelled to action; motivated.  Influenced by a specified motive. |The pump is actuated by a small electric motor. 这个泵是由一台小型电动机驱动的。

The robot is actuated by a complex system of gears and levers.  这个机器人是由一套复杂的齿轮和杠杆系统驱动的。|近义词：motivated, driven, impelled, prompted, spurred, inspired|反义词：unmotivated, inactive, passive|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin actuatus, past participle of actuare "to put into action," from Latin actus "a doing, a driving, an impulse,"  from agere "to drive, lead, act, do."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine is actuated by compressed air. = 这台机器由压缩空气驱动。
His actions are actuated by greed. = 他的行为是受贪婪驱使的。

一般过去时:
The door was actuated by a sensor. = 这扇门是由传感器启动的。
She was actuated by a desire to help others. = 她受助人之心的驱使。

现在完成时:
The system has been actuated several times today. =  该系统今天已经启动了好几次。
He has been actuated by malice in spreading those rumors. = 他传播那些谣言是出于恶意。

过去完成时:
The alarm had been actuated by a power surge. = 警报是由一次电源浪涌触发的。
She had been actuated by a sense of duty to report the crime. = 她出于责任感举报了这起犯罪事件。


将来时:
The device will be actuated remotely. = 该设备将被远程启动。
He will be actuated by revenge if he finds out the truth. = 如果他发现了真相，他会因复仇而行动。

被动语态:
The valve is actuated by a solenoid. =  该阀由螺线管驱动。
The decision was actuated by political considerations. = 该决定是出于政治考虑。

hydraulically actuated = 液压驱动的
