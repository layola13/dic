mantra:/ˈmæntrə/ (US), /ˈmʌntrə/ (UK)| n. |咒语，真言；准则，信条|A word or sound repeated to aid concentration in meditation.  A statement or slogan repeated frequently.|She repeated her mantra, "I will succeed." 她重复着她的咒语：“我会成功的。”|synonyms: chant, incantation, slogan, motto, creed|antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "doubt," "uncertainty")|noun
