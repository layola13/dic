minimal:/ˈmɪnɪməl/| adj.|最小的；最低限度的；极少的|Of a minimum amount, quantity, or degree; negligible.|The damage was minimal.  损坏很小。|例句：He made a minimal effort. 他只做了最起码的努力。|例句：The house is furnished in a minimal style. 这房子装修风格极简。|近义词：slight, negligible, least, smallest, minimum|反义词：maximal, maximum, extensive, substantial|词性：形容词
词源: Late 19th century: from minimum + -al.
