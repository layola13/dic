anti-choice:/ˌæntiˈtʃɔɪs/| adj.|反堕胎的，反对选择权的|Opposed to the legal right to have an abortion.  This term is often used by those who support abortion rights to describe their opponents, arguing that "pro-life" is a misnomer.|The politician's anti-choice stance alienated many voters.  这位政治家的反堕胎立场疏远了许多选民。|近义词：pro-life (although this term is contested), anti-abortion|反义词: pro-choice, abortion rights|词性:adjective

anti-choice:/ˌæntiˈtʃɔɪs/| n.|反堕胎运动，反堕胎立场|The movement or stance against the legal right to have an abortion.|The anti-choice movement has gained momentum in recent years.  近年来，反堕胎运动势头强劲。|近义词：pro-life movement (although this term is contested), anti-abortion movement|反义词: pro-choice movement, abortion rights movement|词性:noun
