brainlike: /ˈbreɪnlaɪk/
adjective
像大脑的；类似大脑的
Resembling or characteristic of a brain, especially in function or complexity.
The computer's brainlike ability to process information amazed everyone.  这台计算机处理信息如同大脑般的能力使每个人都感到惊讶。
The complex network of tunnels was brainlike in its intricacy.  这个复杂的隧道网络像大脑一样错综复杂。
近义词: cerebral, intelligent, complex, sophisticated
反义词: simple, unintelligent, basic, primitive
词性: adjective
