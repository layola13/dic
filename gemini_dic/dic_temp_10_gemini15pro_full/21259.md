blood-shedding:/ˈblʌdʃɛdɪŋ/
noun
流血；杀戮； кровопролитие
The act of causing death or injury by violence, especially by the use of weapons.
The war resulted in much bloodshed. 这场战争导致了大量的流血事件。
bloodshed: carnage, slaughter, massacre
bloodless, peaceful
