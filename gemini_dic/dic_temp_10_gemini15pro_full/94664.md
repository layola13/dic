imprest:/ɪmˈprɛst/| n. vt.|预付款，贷款；给予深刻印象|A sum of money advanced for a particular purpose, typically expenditure.|The company provided an imprest to the employee for travel expenses. 公司为员工提供了差旅费预付款。|近义词：advance, loan, float|反义词:repayment, refund|noun
imprest:/ɪmˈprɛst/| vt.|给予深刻印象；铭刻|To impress deeply; to fix firmly (something, such as an idea or image) in the mind.|The beauty of the landscape impressed itself upon his memory.  美丽的景色深深地印在了他的记忆中。|近义词：impress, engrave, instill|反义词:erase, forget|verb
