listerism:/ˈlɪstərɪzəm/| n.|李斯特主义；防腐法；消毒法|Listerism refers to the principles and practices of antiseptic surgery developed by Joseph Lister, 1st Baron Lister.  It revolutionized surgery by significantly reducing post-operative infections.|Listerism saved countless lives in the 19th century.  19世纪，李斯特主义挽救了无数人的生命。|近义词：antisepsis, sterilization|反义词:sepsis|noun
