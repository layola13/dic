animgraph:/ˈænɪmˌɡræf/ |n.|动画图|A visual representation of a character's animation logic, typically used in game engines like Unreal Engine.  It allows developers to create complex animation behaviors by connecting nodes that represent different animation states, transitions, and blending.|Example: The animgraph for the character included states for walking, running, and jumping.|Synonym: animation blueprint|Antonym: N/A|Noun
