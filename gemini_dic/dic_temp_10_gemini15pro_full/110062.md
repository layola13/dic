lich:/lɪtʃ/| n.|巫妖，僵尸|A lich is a type of undead creature in fantasy fiction, often depicted as a powerful wizard or sorcerer who has used magic to achieve a form of immortality by transferring their soul into a phylactery.|The lich king commanded his undead army. 巫妖王指挥着他的亡灵大军。|Synonyms: undead, wraith, ghoul|Antonyms: living, alive|Noun

lich:/lɪtʃ/| adj.|像巫妖的，像僵尸的|Resembling or characteristic of a lich. |His lich-like appearance frightened the villagers. 他那僵尸般的外表吓坏了村民们。|Synonyms: ghastly, corpse-like, deathly|Antonyms: lively, vibrant|Adjective
