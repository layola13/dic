half-hearted:/ˌhæfˈhɑːrtɪd/ | adj. | 三心二意的；不热心的；敷衍的 | lacking enthusiasm or wholeheartedness |  She gave a half-hearted wave goodbye. 她心不在焉地挥了挥手告别。 | synonyms: lukewarm, apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic | antonyms: enthusiastic, wholehearted, eager, keen | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From half + hearted.  "Hearted" itself comes from Old English "heorte," meaning "heart."  The prefix "half-" indicates a lack of completeness or full commitment from the heart.
