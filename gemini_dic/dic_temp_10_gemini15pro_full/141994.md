organisational:/ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃənl/ /ˌɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃənl/
adjective
组织的；机构的
Relating to the way in which an organization is structured, managed, and controlled.
The company underwent a major organisational restructuring.  公司进行了重大的组织结构调整。
The report highlighted several organisational failings. 报告强调了几个组织上的缺陷。
Synonyms: structural, administrative, managerial, institutional
Antonyms: disorganized, chaotic
词性: 形容词
