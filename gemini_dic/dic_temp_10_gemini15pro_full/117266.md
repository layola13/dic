mechanises:/ˈmɛkənaɪzɪz/
verb
使机械化; 机械化
equips (a factory, farm, or process) with machinery, especially in order to replace manual labor.
The farm has been completely mechanised.  这个农场已经完全机械化了。
Modern technology has mechanised many farming tasks. 现代科技使许多农业工作机械化了。
automate, motorize
manualize
