admitting:/ədˈmɪtɪŋ/
v.|承认；准许…进入；容纳|The school is admitting more students this year. 这所学校今年招收更多学生。|近义词：acknowledging, confessing, allowing, granting entry|反义词：denying, refusing, barring|词性:动词现在分词/动名词

admitting:/ədˈmɪtɪŋ/
adj.|承认的；准许进入的|Admitting his mistake was the first step to reconciliation. 承认他的错误是和解的第一步。|近义词：concessive, allowing entry|反义词：denying, refusing entry|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *admitten*, from Anglo-Norman *admettre*, from Latin *admittō* (“I admit, I let in”), from *ad* (“to, toward”) + *mittō* (“I send, I let go”).
