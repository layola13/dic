ohed: /oʊhd/
interj. | 哦 | An exclamation expressing surprise, realization, or a sudden understanding.| Oh, I see what you mean now. 哦，我现在明白你的意思了。| 近义词: ah, oh | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for an interjection) | 词性:感叹词

词源:  Likely a variant or misspelling of "oh" or "ohed" (past tense of "ohe"), potentially related to archaic or dialectal forms.  The word "ohe" itself might be connected to Old English interjections.  However, "ohed" as a distinct word is not recognized in standard dictionaries.  It's most likely a non-standard spelling.
