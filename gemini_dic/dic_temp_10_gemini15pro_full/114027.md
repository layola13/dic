magnate:/ˈmæɡneɪt/| n. |大亨，巨头，权贵|A person of great influence, importance, or power, especially in business or industry.  A powerful or influential person, especially in business or industry.|例句：The oil magnates controlled vast fortunes.  石油巨头们控制着巨额财富。

例句：He became a shipping magnate. 他成为了航运巨头。|近义词：tycoon, mogul, baron, industrialist, leader, luminary|反义词:pauper, nobody|词性:noun

magnates:/ˈmæɡneɪts/| n. |大亨们，巨头们，权贵们|Plural form of magnate.  Refers to multiple individuals who are powerful or influential, especially in business or industry.|例句：The steel magnates gathered for their annual meeting. 钢铁巨头们聚集在一起召开年度会议。
