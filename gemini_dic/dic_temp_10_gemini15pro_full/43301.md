craggier:/ˈkræɡiər/ | adj. | 更崎岖的，更陡峭的 | More rugged and steep; having more crags. | The path became craggier as we climbed higher.  这条路随着我们越爬越高变得更加崎岖。| 近义词:rougher, steeper, rockier | 反义词:smoother, flatter | 词性:形容词
Etymology: From crag + -y + -er (comparative suffix).  Crag comes from the Celtic *kraggos, meaning "rock."
