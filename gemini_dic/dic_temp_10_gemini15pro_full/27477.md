campaniles:/ˌkæmpəˈniːliːz/
n.
钟楼（复数）
a bell tower, especially one freestanding from the main church building.
The campaniles of Venice are famous landmarks. 威尼斯的钟楼是著名的地标。
The campaniles of Italy are beautiful. 意大利的钟楼很漂亮。

近义词:bell towers, belfries
反义词: (无明确的反义词)
词性:名词
