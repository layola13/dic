non-metallic:/ˌnɒn məˈtælɪk/ (US) /ˌnɒn mɪˈtælɪk/ (UK)| adj.|非金属的|Not having the character of a metal; not containing or resembling metal.|例句：Sulfur is a non-metallic element. 硫是一种非金属元素。

例句：She prefers non-metallic jewellery. 她更喜欢非金属珠宝。|近义词：nonmetal, dielectric|反义词：metallic|词性：形容词
