draftily:/ˈdrɑːftɪli/| adv.|有穿堂风地，冷飕飕地|In a drafty manner; so as to be exposed to drafts of air.  Characterized by or admitting drafts of air. |The old house was draftily built, and we could feel the cold air seeping through the cracks. 这栋老房子建得有穿堂风，我们能感觉到冷风从缝隙中渗进来。|Synonyms: airily, breezily, coldly|Antonyms: warmly, snugly|词性:副词


drafty:/ˈdrɑːfti/| adj.|有穿堂风的，冷飕飕的|Characterized by or admitting drafts of air. Exposed to drafts of air.  |The drafty room made us shiver.  这个有穿堂风的房间让我们冷得发抖。|Synonyms: airy, breezy, chilly|Antonyms: warm, snug|词性:形容词

词源:
From draft + -y.  Draft in the sense of "current of air" comes from the verb to draw.
