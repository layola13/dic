ingurgitate:/ɪnˈɡɜːrdʒɪteɪt/ |v. |狼吞虎咽；大量吞食；吸收|to swallow greedily or in large quantities; to absorb something, especially information, quickly and in large amounts|He ingurgitated the entire pizza in minutes. 他几分钟内就把整个披萨狼吞虎咽地吃完了。

The student ingurgitated information from the textbook to prepare for the exam.  这个学生为了准备考试，大量吸收课本上的信息。

|近义词：devour, gulp, guzzle, consume|反义词: abstain, fast|词性: 动词
