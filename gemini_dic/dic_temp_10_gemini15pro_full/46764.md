dang:/dæŋ/ | int. |见鬼，该死 | Expressing mild annoyance or frustration.  A euphemism for "damn". | Dang, I forgot my keys!  见鬼，我忘记带钥匙了！| 近义词: darn, shoot, blast | 反义词: (No direct antonym for an interjection expressing annoyance) | interjection
dang:/dæŋ/ | n. | 损坏，损伤 (较少用) | Damage or harm. | There wasn't a single dang on the car.  车上一点损伤都没有。 | 近义词: damage, harm, injury | 反义词: repair, improvement | noun
