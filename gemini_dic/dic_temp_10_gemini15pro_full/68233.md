fairs:/ferz/
n.
集市，展览会，庙会
A gathering of stalls and amusements for public entertainment.
The county fairs are always popular events.  县里的集市总是很受欢迎的活动。

fairs:/ferz/
n.
商品交易会
A large exhibition at which companies in a particular industry promote their products.
Many international companies attended the trade fairs. 许多国际公司参加了商品交易会。


fairs:/ferz/
adj.
公平的，公正的
Just and impartial.
He made a fair decision. 他做了一个公平的决定。

fairs:/ferz/
adj.
(肤色)白皙的，(头发)金色的
Light in color; blond.
She had long, fair hair. 她有一头金色的长发。

fairs:/ferz/
adj.
(天气)晴朗的
(of the weather) fine and sunny.
The weather forecast predicts fair skies tomorrow. 天气预报预测明天天气晴朗。

Etymology: From Old French *feire, from Latin feria "holiday, festival".

近义词:
(n.)  carnival, festival, exhibition, bazaar, market
(adj.) just, equitable, impartial, unbiased, blond, light, clear, sunny

反义词:
(adj.) unjust, unfair, biased, dark, cloudy

词性:
Noun, Adjective
