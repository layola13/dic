antivenom:/ˈæntivɛnəm/ | n. | 抗蛇毒血清 |A medicine containing antibodies that is used to treat poisoning caused by the venom of a snake or other venomous animal. | The doctor administered antivenom to the snakebite victim. 医生给被蛇咬伤的受害者注射了抗蛇毒血清。 | Example:  After being bitten by a rattlesnake, the hiker was quickly given antivenom. 在被响尾蛇咬伤后，徒步旅行者很快被给予了抗蛇毒血清。| Synonyms: antivenin, antitoxin | Antonyms: venom, poison | noun
