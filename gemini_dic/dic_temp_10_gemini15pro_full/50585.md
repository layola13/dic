derailment:/dɪˈreɪlmənt/
n.
脱轨；出轨；偏离正轨
the act of a train coming off the rails
例句：The derailment of the train caused several injuries. 火车脱轨造成数人受伤。

例句：The project's derailment was due to poor planning.  该项目的失败是由于计划不周。
近义词：divergence, deviation, deflection, disruption
反义词：continuation, progression, adherence, focus
词性：名词
