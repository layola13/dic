acquaint-with:/əˈkweɪnt wɪð/| v.|使熟悉，使了解|To make someone aware of or familiar with something.|I acquainted him with the details of the case. 我让他了解了案件的细节。|近义词：familiarize, inform, introduce|反义词:unfamiliarize, estrange|动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "acointer" (to make known, inform), from Latin "adcognitare" (to become thoroughly acquainted with),  from "ad-" (to) + "cognoscere" (to learn, to know).

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I acquaint myself with the rules before playing the game. = 我在玩游戏前先熟悉规则。
He acquaints his new colleagues with the company's procedures. = 他让他的新同事熟悉公司的流程。

一般过去时:
She acquainted me with her family history. = 她让我了解了她的家族史。
The guide acquainted the tourists with the local customs. = 导游让游客了解了当地的风俗习惯。

现在完成时:
I have acquainted myself with the latest research on the topic. = 我已经熟悉了关于这个主题的最新研究。
They have acquainted their clients with the new investment opportunities. = 他们已经让他们的客户了解了新的投资机会。

过去完成时:
He had acquainted himself with the area before moving there. = 在搬到那里之前，他已经熟悉了该地区。
She had acquainted her boss with the problems before the meeting. = 在会议之前，她已经让她的老板了解了这些问题。

将来时:
I will acquaint you with the details later. = 我稍后会告诉你细节。
The manager will acquaint the team with the new project next week. = 经理下周将向团队介绍新项目。

虚拟语气:
If I were to acquaint you with the truth, you might be shocked. = 如果我告诉你真相，你可能会感到震惊。
I wish someone had acquainted me with the risks beforehand. = 我希望有人事先告诉我这些风险。

被动语态:
The students were acquainted with the school rules on the first day. = 学生们在第一天就熟悉了校规。
New employees are acquainted with the company's policies during orientation. = 新员工在入职培训期间熟悉公司的政策。

疑问句:
Are you acquainted with this author's work? = 你熟悉这位作家的作品吗？
Were you acquainted with the situation before you made the decision? = 在你做出决定之前，你是否了解情况？

否定句:
I am not acquainted with his work. = 我不熟悉他的工作。
They were not acquainted with the local language. = 他们不熟悉当地语言。

PHRASE->
become acquainted with = 熟悉
be acquainted with = 熟悉
get acquainted with = 熟悉
acquaint oneself with = 使自己熟悉
acquaint someone with something = 使某人熟悉某事
