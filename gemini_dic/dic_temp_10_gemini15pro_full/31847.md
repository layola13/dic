cheeps:/tʃiːps/ | n. | 唧唧声；（雏鸟的）吱吱声；廉价物 | a short, high-pitched sound made by a young bird, or a cheap item |  The cheeps of the baby chicks were barely audible. 雏鸡的唧唧声几乎听不见。 | synonyms: chirps, peeps, tweets, chirruping| antonyms: roars, bellows | noun

cheeps:/tʃiːps/ | v. | （雏鸟）吱吱叫；低语；廉价出售 | to make a short, high-pitched sound like a young bird; to speak in a low voice; to sell at a low price | The chicks cheeped loudly for food. 小鸡大声唧唧叫着要吃的。 | synonyms: chirps, peeps, whispers | antonyms: shouts, yells | verb
