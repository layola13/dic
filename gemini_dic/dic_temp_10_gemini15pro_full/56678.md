doof:/duːf/
noun
笨蛋，傻瓜
a stupid or foolish person
例句：He's such a doof, he locked his keys in the car. 他真是个笨蛋，把钥匙锁在车里了。

例句：Don't be a doof!  别犯傻！

近义词：idiot, fool, moron, dork, klutz
反义词：genius, smart person
词性：noun
