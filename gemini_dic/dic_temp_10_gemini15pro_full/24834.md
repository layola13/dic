brown-out:/ˈbraʊnˌaʊt/| n.|电压骤降；部分停电；灯光变暗|A brown-out is a drop in voltage in an electrical power supply system. It can cause lights to dim and electrical appliances to malfunction.|The storm caused a brown-out that lasted for several hours. 暴风雨导致了持续数小时的电压骤降。|The brown-out caused the computer to crash. 电压骤降导致电脑死机。|近义词：power outage, voltage drop, dimming|反义词: power surge, blackout|名词
