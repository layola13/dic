inconvenienced:/ˌɪnkənˈviːniənst/
adjective
不便的，感到麻烦的
caused trouble or difficulty to someone.
例句：I was inconvenienced by the delay.  我因为延误感到不便。

例句：We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause.  对于由此可能造成的任何不便，我们深感抱歉。

近义词：troubled, bothered, disrupted, disturbed
反义词：aided, assisted, helped, convenient
词性: adjective


inconvenience:/ˌɪnkənˈviːniəns/
noun
不便，麻烦
trouble or difficulty caused to one's personal requirements or comfort.
例句：The construction work caused a great deal of inconvenience.  施工造成了极大的不便。

例句：It's a bit of an inconvenience, but I can manage. 这有点不方便，但我可以应付。

近义词：trouble, difficulty, bother, disruption
反义词：convenience, ease, comfort
词性: noun


inconvenience:/ˌɪnkənˈviːniəns/
verb
给…带来不便；打扰
to cause trouble or difficulty to someone.
例句：I hope I'm not inconveniencing you. 我希望我没有打扰到你。

例句：Don't inconvenience yourself on my account.  别因为我而麻烦你自己。

近义词：trouble, bother, disrupt, disturb
反义词：aid, assist, help, accommodate
词性: verb
