gateway:/ˈɡeɪtweɪ/| n.|通道，入口，网关|A place, structure, or means of access or entry.|The Golden Gate Bridge is a gateway to San Francisco. 金门大桥是通往旧金山的大门。|近义词：entrance, portal, door, access|反义词:exit, outlet|noun

gateway:/ˈɡeɪtweɪ/| n.|途径，方法|A means of achieving a particular result.|Education is a gateway to a better life. 教育是通往更美好生活的途径。|近义词：means, avenue, path, route|反义词:barrier, obstacle|noun
