come-up-to:/kʌm ˈʌp tuː/ | phrasal verb | 達到；符合；走到…跟前 | To reach a particular standard or to approach someone.  | The hotel didn't come up to our expectations. 这家旅馆没有达到我们的期望。| 近义词：approach, reach, meet, fulfill, satisfy | 反义词：fall short of, fail to meet | 词性: 动词短语

come-up-to:/kʌm ˈʌp tuː/ | phrasal verb | 比得上；及得上 | To be as good as something. |  His latest novel doesn't come up to his previous one. 他最新的小说不及他上一部。| 近义词：equal, match, rival | 反义词：be inferior to | 词性: 动词短语
