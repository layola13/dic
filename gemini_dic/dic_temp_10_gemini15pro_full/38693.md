condenser:/kənˈdensər/ | n. | 冷凝器；电容器；聚光器 |A device that cools a gas or vapor to a liquid state.  An electrical component that stores energy in an electric field. A lens or mirror system used to concentrate light. |The condenser in the air conditioner is broken. 空调里的冷凝器坏了。| synonyms: capacitor, cooler, concentrator | antonyms: evaporator, heater, diffuser | noun

condensers:/kənˈdensərz/ | n. | 冷凝器；电容器；聚光器（复数）| Plural form of "condenser". Refers to multiple devices that cool gas/vapor to liquid, store energy in an electric field, or concentrate light. |The lab needs new condensers for the experiment. 实验室需要新的冷凝器来做实验。| synonyms: capacitors, coolers, concentrators | antonyms: evaporators, heaters, diffusers | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *condensare, from Latin condensare "to make dense," from com- "together" (see com-) + densare "to make thick," from densus "thick, dense."
