copresident:/koʊˈprɛzɪdənt/| n. |共同主席，联合主席|A person who shares the position and responsibilities of president with another individual.|Example: The company has two copresidents who share decision-making power. 这家公司有两名共同主席，共同拥有决策权。
Example: The copresidents worked together to guide the organization through a difficult period. 两位共同主席共同努力带领该组织度过了一个困难时期。|近义词：co-chairman, joint president|反义词:|noun
