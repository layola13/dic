hyperdelicious:/ˌhaɪpər dɪˈlɪʃəs/
adjective
极其美味的；超级好吃的
Extremely delicious; exceptionally pleasing to the taste.
This chocolate cake is hyperdelicious!  这个巧克力蛋糕极其美味！
The chef created a hyperdelicious meal that left everyone speechless.  厨师做了一顿超级好吃的饭，让大家都说不出话来。
近义词:scrumptious, delectable, exquisite, divine
反义词:disgusting, revolting, unappetizing, tasteless
词性:adjective
