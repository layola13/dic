expropriator:/ɪkˌsproʊpriˈeɪtər/ | n. | 征用者，没收者 | A person who expropriates something, especially land or property.  Someone who seizes property, often without compensation. | The expropriator seized the farmer's land without offering fair market value. 征用者夺取了农民的土地，却没有提供公平的市场价值。|  The government acted as the expropriator, claiming the land for public use. 政府充当征用者，声称这块土地用于公共用途。 | synonyms: confiscator, seizer, appropriator | antonyms:  restorer, giver, donor | noun
