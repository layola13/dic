micaceous:/maɪˈkeɪʃəs/
adjective
云母质的；似云母的
containing, consisting of, or resembling mica
The rock is micaceous. 这块岩石是云母质的。
The soil is micaceous.  这土壤含有云母。

近义词: flaky, scaly, laminated
反义词: compact, solid

词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *micaceus, from mica (“crumb, grain, particle”).
