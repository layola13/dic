clutched:/klʌtʃt/| adj.|感到紧张的，焦虑的|He felt clutched before the exam. 他在考试前感到很紧张。|近义词：tense, anxious, nervous|反义词:relaxed, calm|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *clucchen, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Middle Dutch klotsen (“to strike, clap”).
