moratorium:/ˌmɔːrəˈtɔːriəm/ /ˌmɔːrəˈtɔːriəm/| n. |暂停，中止，延期|A temporary suspension of an activity, especially the payment of debts.|The government declared a moratorium on nuclear testing. 政府宣布暂停核试验。|近义词：suspension, postponement, delay, halt, standstill|反义词:continuation, resumption, progression|词性:名词

Etymology: From Latin *morātōrium*, neuter of *morātōrius* (“delaying, causing delay”), from *morātus*, past participle of *morārī* (“to delay, linger, tarry”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *mey- (“to change, go, move”).
