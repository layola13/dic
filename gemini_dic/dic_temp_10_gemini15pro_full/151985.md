pastureless:/ˈpɑːstʃərlɪs/| adj.|无牧场的，没有牧草地的|Lacking pastures; without land for grazing.|The nomadic tribes wandered through the pastureless desert, searching for an oasis.|近义词：barren, desolate, infertile|反义词: fertile, lush, grassy|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From "pasture" + "-less".  "Pasture" comes from Old French "pasture" (noun and verb), from Late Latin "pastura" ("pasture, grazing"), from Latin "pascere" ("to feed, graze").  "-less" is a native English suffix meaning "without".
