boba:/ˈboʊbə/| n.|珍珠奶茶；波霸奶茶|A Taiwanese tea-based drink that typically includes chewy tapioca balls.|I love to drink boba after lunch. 我喜欢午饭后喝珍珠奶茶。|Synonyms: bubble tea, pearl milk tea|Antonyms: None|Noun


boba:/ˈboʊbə/| n.|（木薯粉圆）|Tapioca balls, the chewy pearls added to boba tea.|The boba in my drink were perfectly cooked. 我饮料里的珍珠煮得恰到好处。|Synonyms: tapioca pearls, tapioca balls|Antonyms: None|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Taiwanese Hokkien 波霸 (POJ po̍ah-pà), literally meaning "large breasts," referring to the large tapioca pearls.  The term later became associated with the drink itself.
