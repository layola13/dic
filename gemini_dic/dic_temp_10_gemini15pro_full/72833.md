folio:/ˈfoʊlioʊ/ (US), /ˈfəʊliəʊ/ (UK)| n. |对开纸；页码；文件夹；（律师等的）委托人|A large sheet of paper folded once to make two leaves, or four pages, of a book or manuscript. Also, a book or manuscript made up of such sheets, or one of the leaves; a page number; a collection of documents relating to a particular person or subject; a client or group of clients, especially of a lawyer. |例句：The medieval manuscript was written on large folios. 这份中世纪手稿是用大张对开纸写成的。
