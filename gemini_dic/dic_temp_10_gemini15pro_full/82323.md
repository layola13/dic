grove:/ɡroʊv/| n.|小树林，果园|A small group of trees located close together.|We walked through the shady grove on a hot summer day. 我们在炎热的夏日穿过阴凉的小树林。|近义词：wood, thicket, copse|反义词:field, plain|名词

词源: From Old English *grāf, from Proto-Germanic *grōbō (“grove, thicket, brushwood”), from Proto-Indo-European *ghrebh- (“to dig, scratch, scrape”). Cognate with Dutch groeve (“groove, trench”), German Grube (“pit, mine, dimple”), Old Norse grǫf (“pit, grave”).
