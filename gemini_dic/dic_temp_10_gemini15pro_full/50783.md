descanting:/dɪˈskæntɪŋ/
v.|详述；评论；演唱；演奏|Speaking or writing at length on a subject; making a detailed commentary. Also, singing or playing a descant.|He was descanting on the merits of organic farming. 他正在详细阐述有机农业的优点。|近义词：commenting, elaborating, expounding, discoursing, holding forth|反义词：summarizing, condensing, being concise|词性：动词

descant:/ˈdɛskænt/
n.|详述；评论；演唱；演奏|A detailed commentary or explanation. Also, a melody sung or played above the principal melody.|His descant on the painting's symbolism was fascinating. 他对这幅画象征意义的详述引人入胜。|近义词：commentary, explanation, exposition, discourse|反义词：summary, précis|词性：名词

PHRASE->
descant on the subject  详细阐述这个主题
descant upon the matter  就此事发表评论
descant at length  详细地讲述
descant briefly  简要地评论
descant eloquently  雄辩地阐述

词源：From Old French deschanter, from Latin discantare "to sing apart, sing an antiphon," from dis- "apart" + cantare "to sing."  Originally referred to singing or playing a countermelody or decorative melody above a plainsong; the meaning extended to discoursing at length.
