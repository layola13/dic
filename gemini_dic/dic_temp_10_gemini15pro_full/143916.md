outpriced:/ˌaʊtˈpraɪst/| v. |定价过高，价格超过|To price something higher than (something else) or too high to be affordable.|Many potential homebuyers have been outpriced by the recent surge in property values. 许多潜在购房者由于近期房产价值飙升而被定价过高。|Synonyms: price out, overprice, outbid|Antonyms: underprice, undervalue|verb

outpriced:/ˌaʊtˈpraɪst/| adj. |价格过高的|Priced higher than (something else) or too high to be affordable.|The outpriced apartments remained vacant for months.  价格过高的公寓空置了数月。|Synonyms: overpriced, unaffordable, expensive|Antonyms: affordable, cheap, inexpensive|adjective
