can-doism: /ˈkænˌduːɪzəm/
n.
积极进取的态度;乐观自信的精神;迎难而上的劲头
An attitude or spirit of optimism and confidence in the face of difficulties; a belief that one can overcome any obstacle.
例句：Her can-doism inspired the whole team. 她的积极进取的精神鼓舞了整个团队。

例句：He approached the project with infectious can-doism. 他以极富感染力的乐观自信态度来处理这个项目。

近义词：optimism, positive attitude, initiative, drive, resourcefulness
反义词：pessimism, negativity, defeatism, apathy

词性: 名词


词源：
"can-do" (adjective) emerged in American English in the early 20th century, combining the verb "can" (expressing ability) with "do."  The suffix "-ism" transforms the adjective into a noun denoting a doctrine, belief, or practice.
