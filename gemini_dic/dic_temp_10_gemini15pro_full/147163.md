overflow:/ˌōvərˈflō/ (US) /ˌəʊvəˈfləʊ/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.|溢出，泛滥；超出，超过|An overflow occurs when a container or space cannot hold the amount of something that is put into it.  溢出是指容器或空间无法容纳放入其中的东西的数量时发生的现象。|The river overflowed its banks after the heavy rain.  大雨过后，河水溢出了河岸。|近义词：inundate, flood, spill, exceed, surpass|反义词:underflow|词性:名词,动词

overflow:/ˌōvərˈflō/ (US) /ˌəʊvəˈfləʊ/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.|溢出，泛滥；超出，超过|To overflow means to flow over the brim or top of a container.  溢出指的是流过容器的边缘或顶部。|The bathtub overflowed and flooded the bathroom.  浴缸溢水，淹没了浴室。|近义词：inundate, flood, spill, exceed, surpass|反义词:underflow|词性:名词,动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *oferflowan, from ofer- "over" + flowan "to flow."  源自古英语 *oferflowan，由 ofer- “超过” + flowan “流动” 组成。
