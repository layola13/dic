nietzschean: /ˈniːtʃiən/ /ˈniːtʃən/ | adj. n.| 尼采的；尼采哲学的；尼采的信徒|  Relating to the German philosopher Friedrich Nietzsche, his philosophical ideas, or his followers.  Characteristic of Nietzsche's philosophy, especially its emphasis on individualism, the will to power, and the rejection of traditional morality.|  His nietzschean worldview led him to believe in the supremacy of the individual.  他的尼采式世界观使他相信个体至上。

His arguments sounded distinctly nietzschean. 他的论点听起来明显是尼采式的。| 近义词: individualistic, existentialist, iconoclastic | 反义词: conventional, traditional, conformist | 词性: adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Derived from the surname of Friedrich Nietzsche (1844-1900), a German philosopher.
