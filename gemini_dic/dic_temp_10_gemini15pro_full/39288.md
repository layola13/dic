conhydrine:/kənˈhaɪdriːn/| n.|毒芹碱；羟基毒芹碱|A poisonous alkaloid found in poison hemlock (Conium maculatum), isomeric with pseudoconhydrine.|Conhydrine is less toxic than coniine, another alkaloid present in the same plant. 毒芹碱的毒性比同一种植物中存在的另一种生物碱——毒芹素要小。|Synonym:hydroxyconiine|Antonym: N/A|noun
