oxygenant:/ˈɒksɪdʒənənt/ | n. | 氧化剂 | A substance that oxidizes another substance.  It accepts electrons in a chemical reaction. | Example: Potassium permanganate is a strong oxygenant. 高锰酸钾是一种强氧化剂。

Example: Hydrogen peroxide acts as an oxygenant in many reactions. 过氧化氢在许多反应中充当氧化剂。 | Near synonyms: oxidizer, oxidizing agent | Antonyms: reductant, reducing agent | Noun
