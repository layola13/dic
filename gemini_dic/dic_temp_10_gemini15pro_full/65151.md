ethnarchy: /ˈeθnɑːrki/
n.
种族统治；民族统治
A form of government where power is held by a dominant ethnic group or where ethnicity plays a primary role in governance, often to the detriment of other ethnic groups.
例句：The country's ethnarchy led to the marginalization of minority groups.  这个国家的种族统治导致了少数群体的边缘化。

例句：The rise of ethnarchy in the region has sparked international concern. 该地区种族统治的兴起引起了国际社会的关注。

近义词：ethnocracy, ethno-nationalism
反义词: multiculturalism, pluralism
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From ethno- (Greek ἔθνος 'nation, people') + -archy (Greek ἀρχία 'rule, government').
