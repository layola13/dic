dyspraxia:/dɪsˈpræksiə/
n.
运动障碍，运用障碍
A developmental disorder affecting coordination and movement.
Children with dyspraxia often have difficulty with writing and other fine motor skills.  患有运用障碍的儿童经常在写字和其他精细运动技能方面有困难。
clumsiness, awkwardness
coordination, dexterity
noun
