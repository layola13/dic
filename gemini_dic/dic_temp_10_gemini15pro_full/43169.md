cpm:/ˌsiːpiːˈɛm/ /ˌsiːpiːˈɛm/| n. |每千次展示费用 (Cost Per Mille/Thousand)|Cost Per Mille (sometimes referred to as Cost Per Thousand) is a marketing term used to denote the price of 1,000 advertisement impressions on one webpage. If a website publisher charges $2 CPM, that means an advertiser must pay $2 for every 1,000 impressions of its ad.|The website charges $5 CPM for banner ads.|Synonyms: cost per thousand, CPT|Antonyms: N/A|noun

cpm:/ˌsiːpiːˈɛm/ /ˌsiːpiːˈɛm/| abbreviation |每分钟次数 (Counts Per Minute)|Counts per minute, a unit of radioactivity.|The Geiger counter registered 2000 cpm near the source.|Synonyms: counts per minute|Antonyms: N/A|abbreviation
