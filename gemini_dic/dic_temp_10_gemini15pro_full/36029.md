codirector:/koʊˌdaɪˈrɛktər/ /kəʊˌdaɪˈrɛktə(r)/| n. |共同导演，联合导演|A person who directs something jointly with another or others.|The film had two codirectors, which sometimes led to creative clashes. 这部电影有两个共同导演，这有时会导致创作冲突。|近义词：joint director, collaborator |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "sole director") |词性: noun

codirector:/koʊˌdaɪˈrɛktər/ /kəʊˌdaɪˈrɛktə(r)/| n. |共同领导人，联合负责人|A person who leads or manages something jointly with another or others.|The project's codirectors ensured its successful completion. 该项目的共同负责人确保了其成功完成。|近义词：joint leader, co-leader |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "sole leader") |词性: noun
