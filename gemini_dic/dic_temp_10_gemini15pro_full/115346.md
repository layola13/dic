manoeuvring:/məˈnuːvərɪŋ/ /məˈnuːvərɪŋ/

|synonyms: manipulation, tactics, strategy, maneuvering (美式)|antonyms: inaction, passivity|n.

manoeuvre:/məˈnuːvər/ /məˈnuːvər/

|synonyms: manipulate, navigate, strategize, maneuver (美式)|antonyms: neglect, fail|v.

manoeuvrable:/məˈnuːvərəbl/ /məˈnuːvərəbl/
adjective|易于操纵的； maneuverable (美式)|The small boat was highly maneuverable in the shallow water.|这艘小船在浅水中非常容易操纵。
