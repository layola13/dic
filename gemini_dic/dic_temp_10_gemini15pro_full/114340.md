majestical:/məˈdʒɛstɪkəl/| adj.|雄伟的，庄严的，威严的|Having or showing impressive beauty or dignity, characteristic of a majesty.|The majestical Himalayas captivated the climbers. 雄伟的喜马拉雅山脉吸引了登山者。|近义词：august, stately, grand, magnificent, majestic|反义词：humble, lowly, insignificant, unimpressive|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from Old French majestique, from Latin majesticus, from majestas ‘greatness’, from major ‘greater’.
