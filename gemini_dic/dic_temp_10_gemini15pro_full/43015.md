cowbird:/ˈkaʊbɜːrd/ (US), /ˈkaʊbɜːd/ (UK)| n. |燕八哥|A North American blackbird (Molothrus ater) the female of which lays its eggs in the nests of other birds.|The cowbird chicks often outcompete the host's own young for food.|近义词:blackbird, brood parasite|反义词:host bird|词性:名词
