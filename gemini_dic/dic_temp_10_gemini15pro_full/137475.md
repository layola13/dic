nontolerantly:/ˌnɒnˈtɒlərəntli/ | adv. |不宽容地，不容忍地 | In a manner that does not tolerate dissenting views or behaviors; intolerantly.  Showing a lack of acceptance or patience for different opinions, beliefs, or practices. | He nontolerantly dismissed her suggestion, refusing to even consider it. 他不宽容地驳回了她的建议，甚至拒绝考虑。|  The principal nontolerantly enforced the school's strict dress code. 校长不宽容地执行学校严格的着装规定。| 近义词: intolerantly, bigotedly, narrowly | 反义词: tolerantly, open-mindedly, liberally | 词性: 副词
