deprivable:/dɪˈpraɪvəbl/
adjective
可剥夺的
Able to be deprived of something.
He believed that no one should be deprivable of their basic human rights. 他认为任何人的基本人权都不应被剥夺。
近义词: revocable, withdrawable, forfeitable
反义词:  inalienable, unalienable
词性: adjective

词源: From deprive + -able. Deprive comes from Old French depriver, from Latin deprivare, from de- "away from" + privare "to deprive," from privus "private, one's own."
