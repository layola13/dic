feud:/fjuːd/
verb
不和，长期争斗
to have a long and bitter quarrel or fight with someone
The two families have feuded for generations. 这两个家族世代不和。
近义词:quarrel, fight, clash, dispute
反义词:reconcile, cooperate
词性:verb

feuded:/ˈfjuːdɪd/
verb (past tense)
不和过，长期争斗过
past tense of "feud" -  engaged in a prolonged and bitter quarrel or fight.
The two families feuded over a piece of land. 这两个家族因为一块土地而长期争斗。
近义词:quarreled, fought, clashed, disputed
反义词:reconciled, cooperated
词性:verb
