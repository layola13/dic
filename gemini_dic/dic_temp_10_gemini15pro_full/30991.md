chaliced:/ˈtʃælɪst/| adj.|圣杯形的，杯状的|Resembling or relating to a chalice, a large cup or goblet, often used in religious ceremonies.|The chaliced flowers of the plant were a vibrant shade of purple. 这种植物的杯状花朵呈现出鲜艳的紫色。|近义词：cup-shaped, goblet-shaped|反义词:flat, open|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *chalice*, from Latin *calix* (cup, goblet).
