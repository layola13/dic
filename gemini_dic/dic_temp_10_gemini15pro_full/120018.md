million:/ˈmɪljən/ | n. | 百万 | A number equal to 1,000,000. | There are millions of stars in the sky.  天空中有数百万颗星星。 | synonyms: thousand thousand, 1,000,000 | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a number.  Could consider "few" or "small number" as contrasting concepts) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *million (modern French million), from Old Italian milione (“great thousand”), augmentative of mille (“thousand”), from Latin mille (“thousand”).
