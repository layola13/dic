nonvegetation:/ˌnɒnˌvɛdʒɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. |非植被，非植物覆盖区|Areas of bare ground, rock, or other surfaces not covered by plants.  This includes areas like paved surfaces, exposed rock, water bodies, and buildings.| The researchers studied the effects of erosion on nonvegetation areas. 研究人员研究了侵蚀对非植被区域的影响。| The satellite imagery clearly showed the distinction between vegetation and nonvegetation. 卫星图像清楚地显示了植被和非植被之间的区别。|Synonyms: bare ground, barren land, unvegetated area |Antonyms: vegetation, flora, plant life |noun
