overspenders:/ˌoʊvərˈspendərz/
| n. |挥霍者，过度消费者|People who spend more money than they should or can afford are overspenders.  过度消费的人就是挥霍者。|They were known as overspenders and often found themselves in debt. 他们以挥霍无度而闻名，经常发现自己负债累累。|近义词：spendthrifts, squanderers|反义词：savers, frugal people|词性: 名词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "spender" (noun derived from the verb "spend")
