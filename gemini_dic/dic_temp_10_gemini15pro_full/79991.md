go-getting:/ˈɡəʊˌɡetɪŋ/
adjective|有进取心的，积极进取的|Energetic and ambitious in pursuing one's goals.|She's a real go-getting businesswoman and I admire her drive.|近义词:ambitious, enterprising, driven, resourceful|反义词:lazy, apathetic, passive|词性:形容词
词源:  Compound adjective from "go" (verb, meaning to proceed or strive) and "getting" (present participle of "get," meaning to obtain or achieve).  The term implies actively pursuing and achieving goals.
