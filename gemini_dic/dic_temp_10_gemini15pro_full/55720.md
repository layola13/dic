divani:/dɪˈvɑːni/| n. |长沙发; 议事厅; 枢密院|A low couch or sofa, often without arms or a back, typically placed against a wall.  Also, a council chamber or privy council, especially in Muslim countries. |例句：The guests lounged on the divani, sipping tea. 客人们躺在长沙发上，品着茶。

例句：The divani met to discuss matters of state. 枢密院开会讨论国家大事。|近义词：couch, sofa, settee, ottoman|反义词:chair, stool|词性:noun
Etymology: From Turkish *divan*, from Persian *dīwān*, meaning "register of accounts, royal court, council chamber," ultimately from Middle Persian *dēwān*.
