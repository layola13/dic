godding:/ˈɡɒdɪŋ/ | n. |  装神弄鬼；故弄玄虚 |  The act of pretending to be god-like or divinely inspired.  Often used ironically or dismissively. |  He was accused of godding around, making pronouncements as if he were all-knowing.  他被指责装神弄鬼，发表声明好像他无所不知。

His constant godding irritated everyone. 他不断的故弄玄虚激怒了所有人。 | 近义词：posturing, pretending, feigning | 反义词:  humility, modesty | 词性: 名词
