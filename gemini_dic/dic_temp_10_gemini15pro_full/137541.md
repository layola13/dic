/nɒn.trænˈskrɪp.ʃənz/ | n. | 非转录本；未转录的内容 | Material or information that has not been transcribed, often referring to audio or video recordings. | The nontranscriptions of the interviews are stored securely.  这些采访的未转录内容被安全地存储着。 |  The nontranscriptions of the meeting were kept confidential. 会议的未转录内容被保密。 | recordings, untranscribed material | transcripts, transcriptions | noun

Etymology:
"Non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "transcription" (from Latin "transcribere," meaning "to copy across").
