nondatival:/ˌnɒnˈdeɪtɪvəl/
adjective
非与格的
Relating to or being a grammatical case other than the dative; not in the dative case.  A nondatival verb does not take a dative argument.
This verb takes a nondatival argument.  这个动词采用非与格论元。
