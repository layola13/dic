nonassonance:/nɒnˈæsənəns/
noun
不协调，不和谐
Lack of assonance; absence of vowel rhyme.  The deliberate avoidance of similar vowel sounds in a series of words.
The poet used nonassonance to create a sense of dissonance.  诗人用不谐音来营造一种不和谐的感觉。
