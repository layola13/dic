hubs:/hʌbz/
n.
中心，枢纽；(轮)毂；网络集线器
A central point of activity or interest; a center around which other things revolve.  The central part of a wheel. A device that connects multiple computers or other devices on a network.
The airport is a major hub for international travel.  这个机场是国际旅行的主要枢纽。
The wheel hubs were damaged in the accident.  车轮的毂在事故中损坏了.
We need a new hub for our network. 我们需要一个新的网络集线器。
近义词:center, core, focus, nucleus, hub
反义词:periphery, edge, fringe, margin, rim
词性:名词
