frescoed:/ˈfrɛskoʊd/| adj.|壁画装饰的；用湿壁画技法绘制的|Decorated with frescoes; painted using the fresco technique.|The walls of the chapel were frescoed with scenes from the Bible. 小教堂的墙壁上绘有圣经故事的壁画。|Synonyms: muraled, painted|Antonyms: bare, unadorned|adjective

frescoed:/frɛˈskoʊd/| v.|用湿壁画技法绘制|Painted using the fresco technique (past participle form of "fresco")|Michelangelo frescoed the Sistine Chapel ceiling. 米开朗基罗用湿壁画技法绘制了西斯廷教堂的天花板。|Synonyms: muraled, painted|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for the verb form. Consider "removed the frescoes", "whitewashed")|verb (past participle)
