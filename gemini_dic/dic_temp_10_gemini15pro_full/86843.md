hemerobid:/hɪˈmɛrəbɪd/| adj. n.|褐蛉科的；褐蛉科昆虫|Relating to or denoting insects of the family Hemerobiidae, which are delicate, lacewing-like insects with brownish wings.|例句：The hemerobid larva is a predator of aphids. 褐蛉幼虫是蚜虫的天敌。

例句：He discovered a new species of hemerobid in the rainforest. 他在雨林中发现了一种新的褐蛉科昆虫。|近义词：lacewing, brown lacewing|反义词:N/A|词性:adjective, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *hēmeróbios* "living for a day," from *hēméra* "day" + *bios* "life."  This refers to the perceived short lifespan of the adult insect.
