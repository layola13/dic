drossiest:/ˈdrɒsiɪst/ | adj. | 最瞌睡的，最困倦的 |  Superlative form of "drowsy," meaning extremely sleepy or lethargic.  |  The drossiest student in the class kept nodding off.  课堂上最困倦的学生不停地打瞌睡。 | Synonyms: sleepiest, most lethargic, most tired | Antonyms: most alert, most awake, most energetic | adjective

Etymology:
From drowsy + -est.  Drowsy comes from Old English *drusian, related to drear.
