millionairesses:/ˌmɪljəˈneərɪsɪz/
n.
女百万富翁（millionairess的复数）
A woman whose wealth is estimated at a million or more (dollars, pounds, etc.).
The millionairesses often met for charity lunches.  这些女百万富翁经常聚在一起吃慈善午餐。

近义词: heiresses, wealthy women, businesswomen
反义词: paupers

词性: noun (plural)

词源:
From millionaire + -ess (feminine suffix).  Millionaire comes from French millionnaire, from million. Ultimately from Latin mille "thousand."
