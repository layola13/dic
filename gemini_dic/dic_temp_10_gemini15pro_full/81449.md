gratin:/ˈɡrætɪn, ɡræˈtæ̃/| n. |烤菜，奶汁烤菜; gratinéed dish| A dish with a browned or crusted top.  A topping of breadcrumbs and grated cheese used to form a crust.|例句：The gratin dauphinois was perfectly cooked. 奶汁烤土豆片做得非常完美。

例句：She sprinkled grated cheese over the gratin before baking.  她在烘烤前在烤菜上撒了磨碎的奶酪。|近义词：au gratin, casserole|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "stew" as a contrasting cooking method) |词性: noun
