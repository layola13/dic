mesityl:/ˈmɛsɪtɪl/| adj. n.|均三甲苯基，2,4,6-三甲苯基|A univalent radical derived from mesitylene by removal of one hydrogen atom.|Mesityl oxide is a common solvent.  均三甲苯氧化物是一种常见的溶剂。|Synonym: 2,4,6-trimethylphenyl|Antonym: N/A|adjective, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From mesitylene + -yl.  Mesitylene ultimately derives from Greek *mesos* (middle) + *hyle* (wood, matter), via acetone, which was historically produced by the dry distillation of wood. The term "mesityl" then refers to a group derived from mesitylene.
