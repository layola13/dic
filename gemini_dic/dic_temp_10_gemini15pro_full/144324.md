outspurn: /ˌaʊtˈspɜːrn/ | v. | 超越，胜过 | To surpass or exceed, especially in speed or scope. | The runner outspurned his rivals in the final stretch. 这名跑步者在最后冲刺阶段超越了他的对手。

She outspurned everyone's expectations and achieved great success. 她超出了所有人的预期，取得了巨大的成功。

| 近义词：outdo, surpass, excel, exceed, outstrip | 反义词：lag, trail, fall behind | 词性：动词

词源：From out- + spurn (meaning "to kick or strike").  The original meaning was to kick or strike out further than someone else.  Over time, it evolved to mean surpassing someone in any kind of competition or endeavor.
