nonconditioned:/ˌnɒnkənˈdɪʃənd/ | adj. | 非条件的，无条件的 | Not subject to conditions; absolute.  | Example:  A nonconditioned reflex is an automatic response. 非条件反射是一种自动反应。 | Example: He offered his nonconditioned support. 他提供了无条件的支持。 | Synonym: unconditioned, absolute, unqualified | Antonym: conditioned, qualified, restricted | adjective
