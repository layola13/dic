martyr: /ˈmɑːrtər/ (UK) /ˈmɑːrtər/ (US)| vt.| 杀害(尤指因宗教信仰)；折磨；使受苦 |To kill someone because of their beliefs.|  They were martyred for their faith.  他们因信仰而殉难。| synonyms:  persecute, torture, kill | antonyms: protect, save, rescue | verb
