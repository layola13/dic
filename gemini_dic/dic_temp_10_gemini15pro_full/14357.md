autophagy:/ɔːˈtɒfədʒi (UK), ɑːˈtɑːfədʒi (US)| n. |细胞自噬|The natural destructive mechanism that involves the breakdown and recycling of cellular components.|Intermittent fasting can induce autophagy, which is beneficial for cellular health.  间歇性禁食可以诱导自噬，这对细胞健康有益。|Near synonyms: self-degradation, self-digestion|Antonyms:  biosynthesis, anabolism|Noun
