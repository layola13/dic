elf:/ɛlf/ | n. |精灵，小妖精|A mythical creature of folk tales, typically having magical powers, and described as a small human in form. |例句：The elf lived in the enchanted forest.  精灵住在魔法森林里。

例句：She believed she saw an elf dancing in the moonlight. 她相信她看到一个小精灵在月光下跳舞。

|近义词：fairy, pixie, sprite, gnome |反义词:giant, ogre |词性:noun
