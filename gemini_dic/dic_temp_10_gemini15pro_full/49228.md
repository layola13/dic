delegation:/ˌdɛlɪˈɡeɪʃən/| n.|代表团，授权，委托|a group of people who are chosen to vote or act for someone else; the act of giving someone else the authority to do something|The company sent a delegation to the conference. 公司派了一个代表团参加会议。|synonyms: deputation, mission, representatives|antonyms: retention, withholding|noun

delegation:/ˌdɛlɪˈɡeɪʃən/| n.|授权，委托|the act of giving control, authority, a task, or responsibility to someone else|The manager believes in delegation of responsibility. 经理相信责任授权。|synonyms: assignment, entrustment, commission|antonyms: micromanagement, retention|noun
