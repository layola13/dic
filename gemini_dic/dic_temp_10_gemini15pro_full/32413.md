chillsome:/ˈtʃɪlsəm/| adj.|阴冷的，令人寒颤的|Causing a chill; chilling.  Creating a feeling of fear or foreboding.|The chillsome wind blew through the graveyard.  阴冷的风吹过墓地。|近义词：chilly, cold, eerie, spooky|反义词：warm, cozy, comforting|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "chill" + "-some," a suffix meaning "characterized by" or "producing." "Chill" ultimately comes from Old English "ciele," meaning "cold."
