allopathy:/əˈlɒpəθi/
noun
对抗疗法
A system of medical practice that aims to combat disease by using remedies that produce effects different from those produced by the disease under treatment.
Allopathy is the dominant system of medicine in the Western world. 对抗疗法是西方世界主要的医疗体系。
Allopathic medicine relies heavily on pharmaceuticals.对抗疗法严重依赖药物。
Synonym: conventional medicine, Western medicine, orthodox medicine
Antonym: homeopathy, naturopathy, alternative medicine
