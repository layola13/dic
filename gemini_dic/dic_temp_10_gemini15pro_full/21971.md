bogwood:/ˈbɒɡwʊd/ /ˈbɑːɡwʊd/| n. |沼泽木，泥炭木|Wood that has been preserved in a peat bog. This wood is often dark in color and is prized for its unique character and durability.  It is often used in aquariums and terrariums.|The artist used bogwood to create a striking sculpture. 艺术家用沼泽木创作了一座引人注目的雕塑。|近义词：driftwood, fenwood|反义词:|noun
