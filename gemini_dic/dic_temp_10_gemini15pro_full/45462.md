The curativeness of this treatment has been proven in numerous studies.  这种疗法的疗效已在众多研究中得到证实。|近义词：remedialness, healing power, therapeutic value|反义词:ineffectiveness, incurability|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "curative" + "-ness".  "Curative" comes from Late Middle English, from Old French "curatif", from Latin "curativus", from "curare" (to take care of, cure).  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, meaning "the state or quality of being".
