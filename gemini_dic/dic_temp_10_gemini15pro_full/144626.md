outwished: /ˌaʊtˈwɪʃt/
verb
胜过…的愿望；比…更希望
to have wished for something more intensely or for a longer time than someone else.
She outwished him for the promotion, dedicating every waking moment to achieving it. 她比他更渴望升职，把所有醒着的时间都投入到实现这一目标上。
The young athlete outwished his competitors, training relentlessly for years.  这位年轻的运动员比他的竞争对手更渴望胜利，多年来坚持不懈地训练。
近义词: outdesire, surpass in wishing
反义词: be outdone in wishing (没有更贴切的反义词，这个表达相对接近)
词性: verb
