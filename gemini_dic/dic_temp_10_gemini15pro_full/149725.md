palmate:/ˈpælmeɪt/ | adj. | 掌状的；蹼状的 | Resembling a hand with the fingers spread; having lobes radiating from a common point. | The maple leaf is a classic example of a palmate leaf. 枫叶是掌状叶子的典型例子。 | The duck has palmate feet, which help it swim. 鸭子有蹼状的脚，这有助于它游泳。 | 近义词: lobed, webbed | 反义词: pinnate | 词性: 形容词
