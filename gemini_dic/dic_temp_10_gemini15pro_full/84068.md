half-heard:/ˈhɑːf hɜːrd/ | adj. | 半听到的；听得不清楚的 | Partially heard; not heard completely or clearly. | I caught a half-heard phrase from their conversation, but couldn't make out the context. 我从他们的谈话中听到一句半截的话，但无法理解上下文。|例句：The half-heard whispers added to the mystery of the old house.  断断续续的低语增加了老房子的神秘感。|近义词：faint, indistinct, muffled, overheard |反义词：clearly heard, distinct, audible | 词性:形容词
