hungrily:/ˈhʌŋɡrɪli/| adv.|饥饿地；渴望地|In a hungry manner; with a strong desire for something.|The children ate hungrily after their long journey. 孩子们经过长途旅行后狼吞虎咽地吃了起来。|近义词：ravenously, voraciously, greedily|反义词: listlessly, apathetically|词性:副词

Etymology: From 'hungry' + '-ly'.  'Hungry' comes from Old English 'hungrig', meaning "very eager, famished," from Proto-Germanic *hungraz.
