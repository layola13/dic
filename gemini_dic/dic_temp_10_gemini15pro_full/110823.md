linocutting:/ˈlaɪnəʊkʌtɪŋ/
n.
油印木刻，麻胶版画
The process of cutting a design into a piece of linoleum, then inking and printing it.
She’s taking a linocutting class at the local art center. 她正在当地艺术中心学习油印木刻课程。
Linocutting is a popular printmaking technique. 油印木刻是一种流行的版画制作技术。

近义词:block printing, relief printing
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider etching or engraving which are different printmaking techniques)
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"lino-" + "cutting."  "Lino" is a shortened form of "linoleum," which comes from Latin "linum" (flax) + "oleum" (oil). "Cutting" comes from the Old English "cytting."
