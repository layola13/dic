hackable:/ˈhækəbl/| adj.|可入侵的，可被攻击的|Susceptible to being hacked or compromised, typically referring to computer systems or networks.|This system is highly hackable due to its outdated security protocols. 这个系统由于其过时的安全协议非常容易被入侵。|The company's website was hackable and vulnerable to cyberattacks.  该公司的网站很容易被黑客入侵，容易受到网络攻击。|近义词：vulnerable, exploitable|反义词：secure, impenetrable|词性:形容词
