bookbinder:/ˈbʊkˌbaɪndər/| n.|书籍装订工，装订商|A person who binds books, especially one who does so as a trade.  A person who makes and repairs bookbindings.|The bookbinder carefully stitched the pages together.  这位书籍装订工仔细地把书页缝合在一起。|Synonyms: binder, book finisher|Antonyms: N/A|noun
