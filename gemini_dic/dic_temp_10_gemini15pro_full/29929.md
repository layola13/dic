cavaliero:/ˌkævəˈlɪəroʊ/ (US), /ˌkævəˈlɪəːrəʊ/ (UK)| n.|骑士，绅士，护花使者|A cavalier was historically a horseman, especially a knight.  It later came to refer to a courteous gentleman, particularly one attentive to women.|He behaved like a true cavalier, opening doors and offering his coat. 他表现得像个真正的绅士，开门并主动提供外套。|近义词：knight, gentleman, escort, suitor|反义词：boor, churl, cad|Noun
