decontaminators:/ˌdiːkənˈtæmɪneɪtərz/
|n.|去污员，净化员，消毒员|People or equipment that remove contaminants.  Those trained to handle and neutralize hazardous materials are decontaminators.|The decontaminators worked tirelessly to clean up the chemical spill.|近义词：cleaners, sanitizers, purifiers|反义词：contaminants, polluters|Noun


decontaminate:/ˌdiːkənˈtæmɪneɪt/
|v.|净化，清除污染，消毒|To remove contaminants, making something safe to handle or use.|The crew decontaminated the area after the oil spill.|近义词：cleanse, sanitize, purify|反义词：contaminate, pollute|Verb
