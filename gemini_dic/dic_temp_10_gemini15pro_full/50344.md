deplumation:/ˌdɪˌpluːˈmeɪʃən/| n. |脱毛，脱羽|The act of plucking or removing feathers from a bird.  The deplumation of the chicken before cooking is essential.|例句：The deplumation of the bird left it vulnerable to the cold.  这只鸟的羽毛被拔掉后，它很容易受凉。

例句：The archaeologist found evidence of deplumation on ancient bird bones. 考古学家在古代鸟类骨骼上发现了脱毛的证据。|近义词：molting, shedding, plucking|反义词: feathering|词性:noun
