musically:/ˈmjuːzɪkli/| adv.|音乐地；悦耳地；和谐地|In a musical manner; with regard to music.|She played the piano musically. 她演奏钢琴很有乐感。|近义词：melodiously, harmoniously|反义词：discordantly, unmusically|词性:副词
Etymology: From music + -al + -ly. Ultimately from Greek mousikē (art of the Muses).
