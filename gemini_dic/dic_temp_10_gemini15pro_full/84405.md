halloo:/həˈluː/ /hæˈluː/
interjection|招呼语，引起注意的喊叫声|A shout to attract attention.
|Halloo! Is anyone there? 喂！有人在吗？
|Halloo! Can you hear me? 喂！你能听到我吗？
|近义词：hello, hey, hi, shout, call
|反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑whisper 低语)
|词性: interjection

halloo:/həˈluː/ /hæˈluː/
verb (used with object)|喊叫，招呼|To call out or shout.
|He hallooed to his friend across the field. 他隔着田野向他的朋友喊叫。
|She hallooed the dog to come back. 她喊叫着让狗回来。
|近义词：shout, call, yell
|反义词: whisper
|词性: verb

halloo:/həˈluː/ /hæˈluː/
noun|喊叫声，招呼声|A shout or call.
|We heard a halloo in the distance. 我们听到远处传来一声喊叫。
|The halloo of the hunter echoed through the woods. 猎人的喊叫声在树林中回荡。
|近义词：shout, call, yell, cry
|反义词: whisper
|词性: noun



ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *haller "to chase," of Germanic origin.  Related to "holla".
