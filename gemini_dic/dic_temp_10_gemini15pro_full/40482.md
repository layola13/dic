contributor:/kənˈtrɪbjətər/| n.|贡献者，投稿者|A person or thing that contributes something, especially money or ideas.  A person who writes articles for a magazine or newspaper.|She is a regular contributor to the magazine. 她是一位定期为该杂志撰稿的人。|synonyms: donor, subscriber, supporter, participant, writer, author|antonyms: recipient, beneficiary|noun
