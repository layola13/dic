|  She made a refreshing pitcher of limeade for the picnic. 她为野餐准备了一壶清爽的酸橙汁。| 近义词: lime juice, lime drink | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps something like "hot chocolate" if considering temperature contrast) | 词性: noun
