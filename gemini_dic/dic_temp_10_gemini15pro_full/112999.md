lumber:/ˈlʌmbər/ (US), /ˈlʌmbə(r)/ (UK)| n. vi.|木材，木料；废物，杂物；笨重地行动|Timber sawn into boards, planks, etc. To move clumsily or heavily.|The warehouse was full of lumber.| The bear lumbered through the forest.|近义词：timber, wood, logs|反义词: (For verb) glide, dart|noun, verb
