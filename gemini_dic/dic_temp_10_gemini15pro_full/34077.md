civilises:/ˈsɪvəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使文明;使开化;教化
to bring (a people or place) to a more highly developed or cultured state.
The Romans civilised many of the less developed regions of Europe. 罗马人教化了欧洲许多欠发达地区。

近义词:educates, refines, cultivates, enlightens
反义词:barbarizes, brutalizes

词性:v.
