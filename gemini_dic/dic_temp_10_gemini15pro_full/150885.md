paralbumin:/ˌpærælˈbjuːmɪn/| n. |副蛋白，甲状旁腺蛋白|A low molecular weight, calcium-binding albumin found in muscle tissue, particularly fast-twitch fibers. It aids in muscle relaxation by sequestering calcium ions. |Elevated parvalbumin expression is observed in certain types of interneurons. 在某些类型的中间神经元中观察到副蛋白表达升高。|Synonym: calcium-binding protein|Antonym: -|noun
