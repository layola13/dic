corninesses:/ˈkɔːrnɪnɪsɪz/
noun
陈腐；老套；矫揉造作
The plural form of corniness, referring to multiple instances of something being sentimental, trite, or overly sweet.
The movie was full of corninesses that made it hard to watch. 这部电影充满了老套的情节，让人看不下去。
The comedian's act was riddled with corninesses.  这位喜剧演员的表演充满了矫揉造作。
synonyms: banalities, platitudes, clichés, sentimentalisms
antonyms: originality, freshness, sophistication, subtlety
