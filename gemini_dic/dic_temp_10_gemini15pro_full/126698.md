name:/neɪm/| n. |名字,名称;名誉,名声|A word or set of words by which a person, animal, place, or thing is known or referred to.  A person's reputation.|例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

My name is John. 我的名字是约翰。|近义词：title, appellation, designation, reputation, fame|反义词：anonymity, obscurity|词性:noun

names:/neɪmz/| n. |名字,名称;名誉,名声 (复数)|Plural form of "name."  Refers to more than one name.|例句：We need the names of everyone attending the party. 我们需要所有参加聚会的人的名字。

The names of the winners will be announced soon. 获奖者的名字将很快公布。|近义词：titles, appellations, designations|反义词：N/A|词性:noun
