mesquite:/mɛˈskiːt/ | n. | 牧豆树；牧豆树木材 | A spiny tree or shrub of the pea family, native to arid regions of the Americas.  It produces pods rich in sugar. |  The carpenter used mesquite wood to build the table. 木匠用牧豆树木材制作了这张桌子。 |  Synonym:  algarrobo, honey locust | Antonym: N/A | noun

mesquites:/mɛˈskiːts/ | n. | 牧豆树（复数） | Plural form of mesquite. |  Mesquites are common in the southwestern United States. 牧豆树在美国西南部很常见。 | Synonym: algarrobos, honey locusts | Antonym: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
