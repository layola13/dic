modest:/ˈmɒdɪst/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdɪst/ (US)| adj.|谦虚的；适度的；朴素的|Having or showing a moderate or humble estimate of one's importance, abilities, etc.; not boastful or arrogant.|He's very modest about his achievements. 他对自己的成就非常谦虚。|近义词:humble, unassuming, unpretentious|反义词:boastful, arrogant, ostentatious|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French modeste, from Latin modestus ‘keeping due measure’, from modus ‘measure’.
