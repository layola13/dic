caledonian:/ˌkælɪˈdoʊniən/ /ˌkælɪˈdɒniən/| adj. n.|苏格兰的，苏格兰人的；苏格兰人|Relating to Scotland or its people.  A native or inhabitant of Scotland.|例句：The Caledonian Canal stretches across Scotland.  喀里多尼亚运河横穿苏格兰。

例句：He's a proud Caledonian. 他是一位自豪的苏格兰人。|近义词：Scottish, Scotch|反义词:English, Irish|词性:形容词, 名词

词源: From Latin *Caledōnia*, the Roman name for northern Britain, of uncertain origin.
