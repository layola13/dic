indigestibility:/ˌɪndɪˌdʒɛstɪˈbɪləti/
n.|难消化；消化不良|The indigestibility of the food caused him stomach pain.  这种食物的难消化让他胃痛。|The complex legal jargon made the contract seem like a document of utter indigestibility. 复杂的法律术语让这份合同看起来完全无法理解。|近义词:non-digestibility, |反义词:digestibility|词性:名词

indigestible:/ˌɪndɪˈdʒɛstɪbl/
adj.|难消化的；不能消化的；难以理解的|Some mushrooms are indigestible and can cause illness.  有些蘑菇是不能消化的，会导致疾病。|The professor's lecture was so full of jargon, it was practically indigestible to the undergraduate students.  教授的讲座充满了术语，本科生几乎无法理解。|近义词: non-digestible, unassimilable, incomprehensible|反义词: digestible, understandable|词性:形容词
