parsleied:/ˈpɑːrslid/| adj.|欧芹碎撒的，饰以欧芹的|Sprinkled or garnished with chopped parsley.|The potatoes were boiled and parsleied.|近义词:garnished, decorated|反义词:plain, undecorated|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "parsley" + "-ed" (suffix indicating "having been treated with or possessing").  Parsley itself comes from Old French "persil," ultimately from Latin "petroselinum," meaning "rock parsley."
