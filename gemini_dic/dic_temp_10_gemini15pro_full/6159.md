amiga:/əˈmiːɡə/ | n. | 女性朋友 (尤指西班牙语国家) | A female friend, especially in Spanish-speaking countries. |  例句：She met her amiga at the market. 她在市场遇到了她的女性朋友。

例句：My amigas and I went dancing last night. 我和我的女性朋友们昨晚去跳舞了。| 近义词：friend, pal, mate (more general) | 反义词：enemy, foe | 词性：名词

词源:
From Spanish amiga (“female friend”), feminine of amigo (“friend”). Ultimately from Latin amīcus (“friend, loved one”).
