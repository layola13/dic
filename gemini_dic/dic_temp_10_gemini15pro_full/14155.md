authorizes: /ˈɔːθəˌraɪzɪz/ | v. | 授权，批准 |  To grant power or authority to someone to do something.  | The CEO authorizes the purchase of new equipment. 首席执行官授权购买新设备。 | allows, approves | denies, refuses | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *autorisen*, from Old French *autoriser*, from Latin *auctorizare*, from *auctor* ("author, originator, promoter") + *-izare* (suffix forming verbs).
