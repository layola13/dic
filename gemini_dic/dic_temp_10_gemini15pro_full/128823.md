nightmarey:/ˈnaɪtˌmeri/ (If meant as "nightmarish")| adj.|噩梦般的，可怕的|Resembling or characteristic of a nightmare; terrifying and distressing.|例句：The whole experience was nightmarey. 整个经历都像噩梦一样。

例句：She had a nightmarey vision of being chased by a monster. 她做了一个噩梦般的梦，梦见被一个怪物追赶。|近义词：horrific, terrifying, dreadful, frightening|反义词：pleasant, delightful, comforting|词性: adjective

*Note: "Nightmarey" is not a standard English word. The correct term is "nightmarish."  The following analysis uses "nightmarish."*


nightmarish:/ˈnaɪtˌmeriʃ/ | adj.|噩梦般的，可怕的|Resembling or characteristic of a nightmare; terrifying and distressing.|词源: From "nightmare" + "-ish" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Nightmare" comes from Middle English *nihtmare*, from *niht* (night) + *mare* (a mythological demon or goblin thought to cause nightmares).|
