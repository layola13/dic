berliners:/ˈbɜːrlɪnərz/ (American) /bɜːˈlɪnəz/ (British)
n.
柏林人；柏林居民；柏林式煎饼
People who live in Berlin, Germany, or a type of jelly doughnut popular in Germany, especially Berlin, filled with jam and often topped with icing sugar.  In some parts of the US, particularly the Midwest, these are called Bismarcks.
例句：Many berliners speak English fluently. 很多柏林人英语说得非常流利。

例句：These berliners are filled with raspberry jam. 这些柏林式煎饼的馅是覆盆子酱。

近义词：(For people) Berlin residents, citizens of Berlin; (For pastry)  jelly doughnut, Bismarck (US Midwest), Krapfen (Austria)
反义词：(For people) Non-Berliners; (For pastry, no direct opposite)
词性：Noun
