falsifications:/ˌfɔːlsɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/
|n.|伪造；弄虚作假；篡改|The investigation uncovered numerous falsifications of the company's financial records. 调查发现了该公司财务记录的大量伪造。|
|n.|伪造品；赝品|The museum discovered that several of its paintings were falsifications.  博物馆发现它的几幅画是赝品。|
|近义词:|forgeries, counterfeits, fabrications, misrepresentations|
|反义词:|truths, facts, verifications, authentications|
|词性:|noun (plural)|
|词源:|From Late Latin *falsificatio*, from Latin falsificare (“to falsify”), from falsus (“false”) + -ficare, from facere (“to make, to do”).|
