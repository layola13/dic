outplacement:/ˌaʊtˈpleɪsmənt/
n.
失业人员再就业安置；职业转换安置
The process of helping redundant employees find new jobs, often with the assistance of a specialist consultancy.
The company offered outplacement services to all employees affected by the restructuring.  这家公司向所有受重组影响的员工提供再就业安置服务。
近义词:career transition services, job placement assistance, redeployment
反义词: unemployment, redundancy
