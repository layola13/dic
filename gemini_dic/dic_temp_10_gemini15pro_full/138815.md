nunavut: /ˈnuːnəvʊt/ | n. | 努纳武特（加拿大北部地区）| Nunavut is the largest, northernmost, newest, and least populous territory of Canada.  It was separated officially from the Northwest Territories on April 1, 1999, via the Nunavut Act and the Nunavut Land Claims Agreement Act, though the actual boundaries were established in 1993. The creation of Nunavut resulted in the first major change to Canada's political map since the incorporation of the new province of Newfoundland in 1949. |  Nunavut is a beautiful but harsh land. 努纳武特是一片美丽却荒凉的土地。|  Northwest Territories, Yukon |  None (as it is a proper noun referring to a specific place) | Noun
