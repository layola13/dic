huanaco:/wɑːˈnɑːkoʊ/ /ɡwɑˈnɑːkoʊ/
noun
骆马（南美的一种野生动物，类似于美洲驼）
A wild South American ruminant mammal (Lama guanicoe), related to the llama and alpaca, having long soft woolly fleece.
The huanaco is native to the Andes Mountains. 骆马原产于安第斯山脉。
The huanaco's thick coat helps it survive the harsh mountain climate. 骆马的厚皮毛帮助它在恶劣的山地气候中生存。
近义词:guanaco, llama (loosely)
反义词: (No direct antonym for an animal species)
词性:n.
