gambolled:/ˈɡæmbəld/ | v. | 嬉戏；雀跃；欢跳 | To run or jump about playfully; to frolic. | The lambs gambolled in the spring meadow. 春天里，小羊在草地上嬉戏。| The children gambolled on the beach. 孩子们在沙滩上欢快地跳跃。 | 近义词: frolic, frisk, cavort, play, romp | 反义词: trudge, plod | 词性: 动词
