nonrationalistically:/ˌnɒnˌræʃəˈnæˈlɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 非理性地 |  In a way that is not based on or in accordance with reason or logic.  | He acted nonrationalistically, driven by pure emotion. 他非理性地行动，完全受情绪驱使。|  The decision was made nonrationalistically, ignoring all available evidence.  这个决定是非理性做出的，忽略了所有可用的证据。 | emotionally, instinctively, impulsively | rationally, logically | adverb
