fusted:/ˈfʌstɪd/ | adj. | 发霉的；腐朽的；过时的；古板的 | Having a stale, musty smell; moldy; decayed. Also, old-fashioned or outdated in ideas or style.  | The fusted old books in the attic smelled of damp and decay.  阁楼里发霉的旧书散发着潮湿和腐烂的气味。 | 近义词：musty, stale, moldy, antiquated, outdated | 反义词：fresh, new, modern, contemporary | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Middle English *fusten, from Old French fust "wine cask," from Latin fustis "cudgel, club."  The original sense referred to the musty smell of old wine casks.  The sense of "old-fashioned" developed later, likely by association with things stored away and forgotten in such casks.
