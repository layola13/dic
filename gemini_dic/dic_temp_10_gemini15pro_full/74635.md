free-base:/ˈfriːbeɪs/ | n. | 游离碱 | A purified form of a substance, especially a drug, that is not combined with another substance such as an acid to form a salt. | Cocaine in its free-base form is highly addictive.  游离碱形式的可卡因成瘾性很强。| synonym: pure form, base form | antonym: salt, acid salt | noun


free-base:/ˈfriːbeɪs/ | v. |  (使)成为游离碱; 吸食(游离碱毒品) | To convert (a substance) to its free-base form, especially to prepare cocaine for smoking as crack. | He free-based the cocaine to make crack. 他把可卡因制成了游离碱形式的快克可卡因。 | synonym: convert, purify | antonym: combine, synthesize | verb
