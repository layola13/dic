breeze-through:/briːz θruː/| phrasal verb |轻松完成，快速通过|To complete something easily and quickly, often without much effort.|He breezed through the exam. 他轻松地完成了考试。|sail through, coast through, walk in the park|struggle through, toil through|phrasal verb
breeze-through:|/briːz θruː/| noun |轻松的胜利，轻而易举的事|Something that is easily accomplished.|The test was a breeze-through for her. 这次考试对她来说轻而易举。|walk in the park, cinch, piece of cake|ordeal, challenge|noun
