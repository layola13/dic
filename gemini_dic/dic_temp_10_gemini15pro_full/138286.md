notary:/ˈnəʊtəri/ (UK) /ˈnoʊtəri/ (US)| n.|公证人；公证员|A public official authorized to certify documents, attest signatures, etc., and to perform certain other official acts, such as witnessing oaths.|Example 1: The notary public stamped and signed the document. 公证人在文件上盖章并签字。

Example 2:  You'll need to get this document notarized. 你需要公证这份文件。|Synonym:  notary public, certifier|Antonym: N/A|Noun

notarize:/ˈnəʊtəraɪz/ (UK) /ˈnoʊtəˌraɪz/ (US)| vt.|公证；证明|To certify or attest to (a document, signature, etc.) as a notary.|Example 1: I had to get my passport application notarized. 我必须公证我的护照申请。

Example 2:  She notarized the contract. 她公证了合同。|Synonym: certify, attest, authenticate|Antonym: N/A|Verb
