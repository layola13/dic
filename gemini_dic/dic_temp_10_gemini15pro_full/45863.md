cut-to-the-chase:/kʌt tə ðə tʃeɪs/| idiom |开门见山，直奔主题|To get to the main point without delay; to skip the preliminaries. |例句：Let's cut to the chase—how much will this cost?  我们开门见山吧——这要多少钱？

例句：Instead of all this small talk, let's cut to the chase.  与其闲聊，不如直奔主题。|近义词：get to the point, come to the point, get down to brass tacks|反义词：beat around the bush, talk in circles| idiom
