grumly:/ˈɡrʌmli/ | adj. |脾气暴躁的，坏脾气的|Bad-tempered and irritable; surly.|He gave me a grumly look. 他没好气地瞪了我一眼。|近义词：surly, grumpy, sullen, morose|反义词：cheerful, happy, pleasant|词性:形容词
Etymology: From grum + -ly.  Grum is of uncertain origin, perhaps imitative.
