noxiously:/ˈnɑːkʃəsli/ /ˈnɒkʃəsli/| adv.|有害地，有毒地，讨厌地|In a harmful, poisonous, or offensive manner.  It refers to something that is physically or morally harmful or offensive.|The factory noxiously polluted the air.  这家工厂有害地污染了空气。|The fumes spread noxiously through the building.  这些烟雾有害地弥漫了整栋楼。|Synonym: harmfully, offensively, deleteriously, perniciously|Antonym: harmlessly, beneficially, healthily|词性: 副词
