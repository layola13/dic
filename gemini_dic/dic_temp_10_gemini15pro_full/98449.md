inspirational:/ˌɪnspəˈreɪʃənl/
adjective
鼓舞人心的；激励的
tending or able to inspire; giving inspiration
Her inspirational speech motivated the team to victory.  她的鼓舞人心的演讲激励团队取得了胜利。

The teacher's inspirational words encouraged the students to pursue their dreams. 老师鼓舞人心的话鼓励学生们追求自己的梦想。

近义词:motivating, encouraging, uplifting, inspiring
反义词:demotivating, discouraging, depressing

词性:adjective
