girardian:/ʒɜːrˈɑːrdiən/ | adj. | 吉拉德式的，模仿欲望的 | Of or relating to the theories of René Girard, particularly his theory of mimetic desire, which posits that human desire is not autonomous but rather learned by imitating the desires of others. |  His latest book offers a girardian interpretation of Shakespeare's tragedies.  他最新的著作提供了对莎士比亚悲剧的吉拉德式解读。|  mimetic, imitative | autonomous, original | adjective

girardian:/ʒɜːrˈɑːrdiən/ | n. | 吉拉德主义者，信奉吉拉德理论的人 | A follower or proponent of the theories of René Girard. | She considers herself a girardian and applies his theories to her literary criticism. 她自认为是吉拉德主义者，并将他的理论应用于她的文学批评。 | follower, disciple | critic, opponent | noun
