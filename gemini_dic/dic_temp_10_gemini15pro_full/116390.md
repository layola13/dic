mastodynia:/ˌmæstəˈdɪniə/| n. |乳房痛，乳腺痛|Pain or discomfort in the breast, often cyclical and related to hormonal changes.|例句：She experienced mastodynia during her menstrual cycle. 她经期时会感到乳房痛。

例句：The doctor prescribed medication for her mastodynia. 医生为她的乳房痛开了药。|近义词：mastalgia, breast pain|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider absence of pain)|词性: noun
