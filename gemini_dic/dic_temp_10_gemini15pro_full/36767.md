collusion:/kəˈluːʒən/
n.|勾结，共谋，串通|Secret or illegal cooperation or conspiracy, especially in order to cheat or deceive others.|The investigation revealed collusion between the two companies to fix prices. 调查揭露了这两家公司串通操纵价格。|近义词:conspiracy, connivance, collaboration|反义词:competition, honesty, integrity|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *collusio (“a playing together”), from Latin colludere, present active infinitive of collūdo (“I play together, conspire”), from com- (“together”) + lūdo (“I play”).
