offensible:/əˈfɛnsɪvəl/ /ɒˈfɛnsɪvəl/| adj.|冒犯的；无礼的；讨厌的；攻击性的|Causing someone to feel resentful, upset, or annoyed; unpleasant or disgusting.|例句：His comments were deeply offensive to many people. 他的评论冒犯了很多人。|近义词：insulting, abusive, objectionable, obnoxious|反义词：inoffensive, harmless, pleasant|词性:形容词
offense:/əˈfɛns/ /ɒˈfɛns/| n.|冒犯；进攻；犯罪；违法|Annoyance or resentment brought about by a perceived insult to or disregard for oneself or one's standards or principles.|例句：I didn't mean to cause any offense. 我没有冒犯任何人的意思。 |近义词：insult, affront, transgression|反义词：defense, compliment|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *offensif, -ive*, from Late Latin *offēnsīvus*, from Latin *offēnsus* (past participle of *offendō* “to strike against, offend”).
