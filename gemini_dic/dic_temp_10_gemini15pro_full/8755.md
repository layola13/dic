antidogmatic:/ˌæntiˌdɒɡˈmætɪk/ /ˌæntiˌdɑːɡˈmætɪk/
| adj. |反教条的，非教条主义的| Opposed to or free from dogma; not bound by authoritarian tenets or beliefs.|  Her antidogmatic approach to teaching encouraged open discussion and critical thinking. 她反教条的教学方法鼓励公开讨论和批判性思维。|  He took an antidogmatic stance on the issue, preferring to consider all perspectives. 他在这个问题上采取了反教条的立场，更愿意考虑所有观点。|近义词：undogmatic, open-minded, liberal, tolerant, flexible |反义词：dogmatic, doctrinaire, rigid, inflexible, authoritarian|词性:形容词
