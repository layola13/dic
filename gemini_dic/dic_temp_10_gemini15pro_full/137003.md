nonspontaneously:/ˌnɑːnspɑːnˈteɪniəsli/ /ˌnɒnspɒnˈteɪniəsli/
adverb|非自发地，不自发地|Occurring or done not spontaneously; not by natural impulse or tendency; requiring outside influence or stimulation.|例句：The reaction proceeds nonspontaneously at room temperature.  这个反应在室温下是非自发进行的。|近义词：involuntarily, reluctantly, forcedly, artificially|反义词：spontaneously, naturally, voluntarily|词性:副词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin, meaning "not"
spontaneous (adjective): from Latin *spontaneus, from sponte "of one's own accord, voluntarily"
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives
