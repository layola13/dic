flaunters:/ˈflɔːntərz/| n.|炫耀者，卖弄者|People who flaunt their wealth or possessions often make others feel uncomfortable.  炫耀自己财富或财产的人经常会让别人感到不舒服。|例句：The flaunters of expensive cars are often seen in this affluent neighborhood.  在这个富裕的社区经常可以看到炫耀豪车的人。

flaunters:/ˈflɔːntərz/| n.|卖弄者|She's such a flaunter, always showing off her new clothes. 她真是个爱炫耀的人，总是炫耀她的新衣服。|近义词：show-offs, braggarts, boasters|反义词：modest people, humble people|词性:noun
