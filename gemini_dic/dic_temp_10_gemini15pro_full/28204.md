caprification:/ˌkæprɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/
n.
人工授粉（特指无花果）
The pollination of fig trees by introducing fig wasps.
例句：Caprification is essential for some types of figs to produce fruit.  一些类型的无花果需要人工授粉才能结果。

例句：Ancient cultures practiced caprification long before the biology of fig wasps was understood. 古代文化在了解榕小蜂的生物学特性之前很久就开始进行人工授粉了。
近义词：pollination, fertilization
反义词：(No direct antonym, potentially 'natural pollination')
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *caprificatio, from caprificare (“to ripen figs”), from caprificus (“wild fig, fig-bearing”), from caper (“goat”) + ficus (“fig”).
