overbusily:/ˌoʊvərˈbɪzɪli/
adverb
过度忙碌地；过分忙碌地
Engaging in activity to an excessive degree; too busily.
She overbusily prepared for the party, decorating every single corner. 她为派对过度忙碌地做准备，装饰了每一个角落。
He overbusily checked his emails, neglecting his family. 他过分忙碌地查看邮件，忽略了家人。
近义词: excessively, too busily, hyperactively
反义词: lazily, idly, leisurely
词性: adverb

Etymology:
From "over-" (prefix indicating excess) + "busy" (adjective meaning actively engaged in work) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  The word "busy" ultimately comes from Old English "bisig."
