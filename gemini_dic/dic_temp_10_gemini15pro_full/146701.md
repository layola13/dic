overheavy:/ˌoʊvərˈhɛvi/| adj.|过重的，超重的|Excessively heavy; heavier than is desirable or suitable.|The box was overheavy and difficult to lift. 这个箱子太重了，很难抬起来。|Synonyms: overweight, overburdened, excessively heavy|Antonyms: light, lightweight, underweight|adjective

overheavy:/ˌoʊvərˈhɛvi/| adj.|负担过重的|Loaded with too much weight or burden; overloaded.|He was overheavy with responsibilities. 他肩上的责任太重了。|Synonyms: overburdened, overloaded, weighed down|Antonyms: unburdened, light, free|adjective
