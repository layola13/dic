ankled:/ˈæŋkəld/
adjective
有...踝的；踝关节形状的
Having ankles of a specified kind.
The fine-ankled deer grazed peacefully. 那只踝关节纤细的鹿平静地吃着草。
近义词:  legged
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider "footless")
词性: adjective

ankled:/ˈæŋkəld/
adjective
(of a stocking or sock) reaching to the ankles.
（指长袜或短袜）长度及脚踝的
She wore sheer, ankled stockings. 她穿着透明的及踝长袜。
近义词: ankle-length, ankle-high
反义词: knee-high, thigh-high
词性: adjective
