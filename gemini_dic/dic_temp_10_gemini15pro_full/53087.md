diphtheria:/dɪfˈθɪəriə/ | n. |白喉|An acute infectious disease caused by the Corynebacterium diphtheriae bacterium. It is characterized by the formation of a false membrane in the throat which hinders breathing and swallowing.|  Diphtheria is a serious disease that can be prevented by vaccination. 白喉是一种可以通过接种疫苗来预防的严重疾病。| Diphtheria outbreaks can occur in areas with low vaccination rates.  在疫苗接种率低的地区，可能会爆发白喉疫情。| synonym: none (This is a specific disease name and doesn't have true synonyms) | antonym: none | noun

Refers to multiple instances or types of the disease.|  While grammatically correct, using "diphtherias" is unusual; "cases of diphtheria" or "different strains of diphtheria" would be preferred.  虽然语法上正确，但使用“diphtherias”很不寻常；最好使用“白喉病例”或“不同菌株的白喉”。|  He studied various diphtherias prevalent in the developing world. 他研究了发展中国家流行的各种白喉。 (This usage is uncommon) | synonym: none | antonym: none | noun
