identity:/aɪˈdɛntɪti/ | n. |身份，特性，一致性|The state or fact of being the same one as described. |She struggled to maintain her identity in a new country.  她在一个新的国家努力保持自己的身份。|synonyms: individuality, personality, character, self, ego, essence |antonyms: anonymity, indistinction| noun

identity:/aɪˈdɛntɪti/ | vt. |认出，识别|Establish or indicate who or what (someone or something) is. |The police officer identified the suspect from the photograph. 警察从照片中认出了嫌疑犯。|synonyms: recognize, pinpoint, distinguish, determine| antonyms: confuse, mistake| verb
