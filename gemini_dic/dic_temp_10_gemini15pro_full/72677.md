foaming:/ˈfoʊmɪŋ/| adj.|起泡沫的，泡沫状的|Producing or covered with foam; frothy.|The foaming waves crashed against the rocks. 汹涌的波浪拍打着岩石。|近义词：frothy, bubbly, lathery|反义词:flat, still|词性:形容词

foaming:/ˈfoʊmɪŋ/| n.|泡沫，起泡沫|The mass of bubbles formed on or in a liquid.|The foaming of the beer made it overflow. 啤酒的泡沫使它溢了出来。|近义词：froth, bubbles, lather|反义词:liquid, stillness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *famian, from Proto-Germanic *faimaz, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)poi- "foam, froth."  Cognates include German feimen "to skim," Dutch vijm "scum," and Latin spuma "foam."
