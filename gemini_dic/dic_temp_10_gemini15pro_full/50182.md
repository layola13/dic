deoppilative:/ˌdiːəˈpɪlətɪv/
adjective

Removing obstructions, especially from the pores or vessels of the body; aperient.
This herbal tea is known for its deoppilative properties, helping to clear congestion. 这种草药茶以其通气的特性而闻名，有助于清除充血。

Synonyms: aperient, laxative, purgative, cathartic
