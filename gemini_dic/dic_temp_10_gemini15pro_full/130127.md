non-realism:/ˌnɒnˈriːəlɪzəm/ | n. | 非现实主义 |  A genre or style in art, literature, film, etc., that does not attempt to represent reality accurately, often using fantasy, surrealism, or other imaginative elements. |  Example: The film embraced non-realism, featuring talking animals and magical powers. 这部电影采用了非现实主义，以会说话的动物和魔法力量为特色。


Example: Non-realism in literature often explores themes of dreams and the subconscious.  文学中的非现实主义经常探索梦境和潜意识的主题。

| 近义词：fantasy, surrealism, idealism, abstraction | 反义词：realism, naturalism | 词性：名词
