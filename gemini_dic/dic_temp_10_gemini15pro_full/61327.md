embassies:/ˈembəsiːz/
noun
大使馆（复数）
The official residences or offices of ambassadors in foreign countries.
Many foreign embassies are located in Washington, D.C. 许多外国大使馆都位于华盛顿特区。
missions, consulates, legations
N/A
plural noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *ambassie*, from *ambasse*, from Late Latin *ambasciata* "embassy," from *ambactus* "messenger, servant," from Latin *ambactus* "a going around, circuit," past participle of *ambagire* "to go around," from *ambi-* "around" + *agere* "to drive, lead, do, act."
