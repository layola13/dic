cathedrals:/kəˈθiːdrəlz/| n. |大教堂（复数）|A large and important church, especially one that is the center of a diocese and is presided over by a bishop.|例句：Many European cathedrals are stunning examples of Gothic architecture. 许多欧洲的大教堂都是哥特式建筑的绝佳范例。

例句：The cathedrals of Europe are repositories of art and history. 欧洲的大教堂是艺术和历史的宝库。|近义词：churches, basilicas|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，或许可以考虑 chapel 小教堂)|词性: 名词
