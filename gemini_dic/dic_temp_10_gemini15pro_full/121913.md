missemblance:/mɪsˈsɛmbləns/ | n. |不像，相异，差异|Lack of resemblance; dissimilarity. | The missemblance between the twins was striking, despite their shared genes. 这对双胞胎尽管基因相同，但外貌差异却很明显。| 近义词:dissimilarity, unlikeness, difference, disparity | 反义词: resemblance, likeness, similarity | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From mis- "wrongly, badly" + semblance.  The prefix "mis-" indicates negation or opposition, suggesting a lack of resemblance.  "Semblance" comes from Old French "semblance" meaning "likeness, resemblance," ultimately from Latin "similis" meaning "like."
