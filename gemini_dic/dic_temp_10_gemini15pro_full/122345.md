mixes:/ˈmɪksɪz/ | n. | 混合物；混音 | a combination of different things; a recording created by combining multiple audio tracks | The DJ played a mix of popular songs. DJ播放了一组流行歌曲的混音。|近义词：blend, combination, medley |反义词: separation | noun (plural mixes)
