anchylosis:/ˌæŋkɪˈloʊsɪs/ (US), /ˌæŋkɪˈləʊsɪs/ (UK)| n. |强直，僵硬|The process of becoming stiff or rigid, especially of a joint.|The doctor diagnosed anchylosis of the spine.|近义词:stiffening, rigidity|反义词:mobility, flexibility|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *ankylose*, from Greek *ankylosis* "stiffening of a joint," from *ankyloun* "to bend, crook, make stiff," from *ankylos* "bent, crooked."
