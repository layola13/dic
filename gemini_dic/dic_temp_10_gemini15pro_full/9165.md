antinepotism:/ˌæntiˈnɛpətɪzəm/
n.
反对裙带关系；反裙带主义
The practice of opposing nepotism, especially in politics or business.  It refers to actions taken to prevent or discourage favoritism based on family connections.
The company has a strict policy of antinepotism to ensure fair hiring practices.  这家公司有严格的反裙带关系政策，以确保公平的招聘实践。
The new CEO implemented antinepotism measures to address concerns about favoritism. 新任首席执行官实施了反裙带关系措施，以解决对徇私的担忧。
近义词: impartiality, fairness, meritocracy
反义词: nepotism, cronyism, favoritism
词性: noun
