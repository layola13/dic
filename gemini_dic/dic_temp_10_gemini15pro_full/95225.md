inbreeder:/ˈɪnˌbriːdər/| n.|近亲繁殖者；近亲交配动物|A person or animal that is produced by inbreeding.|The isolated community had become a group of inbreeders.|Synonyms:  endogamist|Antonyms:  exogamist|noun

inbred:/ˈɪnˌbrɛd/| adj.|近亲繁殖的；天生的|Produced by inbreeding; innate.|The royal family suffered from many inbred diseases.|Synonyms:  congenital, inherent, innate|Antonyms:  acquired, learned|adjective
