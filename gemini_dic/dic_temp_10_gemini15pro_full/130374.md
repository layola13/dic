non-use:/ˌnɒnˈjuːs/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈjuːs/ (US)| n.|不使用；闲置|Lack of use; disuse. The non-use of the equipment led to its deterioration.|The non-use of the swimming pool during winter resulted in algae growth.|近义词：disuse, inactivity, idleness|反义词：use, utilization, employment|词性:名词

Etymology:
From non- + use.  "Non-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "not". "Use" comes from the Old French "us" meaning "custom, practice, usage".
