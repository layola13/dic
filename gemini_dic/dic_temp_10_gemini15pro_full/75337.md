frostily:/ˈfrɒstɪli/ (UK), /ˈfrɔːstɪli/ (US)| adv.|冷淡地，冷冰冰地|In a manner resembling frost; coldly, unfriendliness.  Characterized by coldness of manner; lacking warmth or cordiality. |She replied frostily, "I'm busy." 她冷冰冰地回答：“我很忙。”|例句：He stared frostily at the intruder.  他冷冰冰地盯着入侵者。|近义词：coldly, icily, frigidly|反义词：warmly, cordially, kindly|词性:副词

词源: From frost + -ily.  "Frost" comes from Old English "frost."  The suffix "-ily" forms adverbs from adjectives.
