PHRASE->
believe in: 相信...的存在或真实性 (believe in ghosts 相信鬼魂)
believe + that 从句: 相信...(believe that he is innocent 相信他是无辜的)
make believe: 假装 (Children often engage in make-believe play. 孩子们经常进行假装游戏。)
believe it or not: 信不信由你
lead someone to believe:  使某人相信


词源: From Middle English _bileven_, from Old English _belēfan_  ("believe"), from _be-_ + _lēfan_ ("allow, permit, believe").  The prefix _be-_ adds intensity to the meaning.
