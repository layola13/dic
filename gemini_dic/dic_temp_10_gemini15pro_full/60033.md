educate:/ˈɛdʒʊkeɪt/ (US), /ˈɛdʒʊkeɪt/ (UK)| vt.|教育，培养，训练|To give someone intellectual, moral, or social instruction.  To develop the knowledge, skill, or character of someone. |例句：The program aims to educate children about the dangers of drugs.  这个项目旨在教育儿童毒品的危害。|例句：She was educated at private schools in Switzerland.  她在瑞士的私立学校接受教育。|近义词：train, teach, instruct, school, tutor|反义词:misinform, miseducate|词性:动词
Etymology: From Latin *educatus*, past participle of *educare* "to bring up, rear, educate," from *ex* "out" + *ducare* "to lead."
