cross-fertilise: /ˌkrɒs ˈfɜːtɪlaɪz/ (UK) /ˌkrɔːs ˈfɜːrtɪlaɪz/ (US)
verb
交叉施肥，异花授粉; (使)互相影响，(使)相互促进
To fertilize a plant or animal with pollen or sperm from a different variety; to cause (ideas, methods, etc.) to be exchanged between different groups so that each is improved or made more effective.
例句：Researchers are cross-fertilising ideas from different scientific disciplines. 研究人员正在使不同学科的思想相互影响。

例句：The company benefited from cross-fertilising ideas with other businesses in the industry.  该公司受益于与业内其他企业的思想交流。

近义词：crossbreed, hybridize, interbreed, pollinate, influence, inspire
反义词：inbreed

词性：v.
