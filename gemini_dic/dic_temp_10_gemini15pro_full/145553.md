overcommunicative: /ˌoʊvər kəˈmjuːnɪˌkeɪtɪv/ | adj. | 过度沟通的，沟通过度的 |  Inclined to communicate excessively; sharing more information than necessary or desired.  | She's so overcommunicative, I feel bombarded with information. 她太爱沟通了，我觉得信息轰炸。|  Example: He was so overcommunicative about his trip that I felt like I was there myself.  他滔滔不绝地讲述他的旅行，让我感觉自己身临其境。| Synonyms: verbose, loquacious, effusive, garrulous | Antonyms: reticent, taciturn, laconic | adjective
