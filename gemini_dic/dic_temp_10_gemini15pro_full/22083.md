bollockings: /ˈbɒləkɪŋz/ (UK) /ˈbɑːləkɪŋz/ (US) | n. | 斥责；痛骂 | A severe scolding or reprimand.  | He got a real bollocking from his boss for being late. 他因为迟到被老板狠狠地骂了一顿。 | Synonyms: scolding, reprimand, telling-off, dressing-down | Antonyms: praise, commendation | Noun

Etymology: From "bollocks," a vulgar term for testicles, implying a forceful and unpleasant experience.
