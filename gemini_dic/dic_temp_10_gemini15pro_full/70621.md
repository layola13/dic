Filmstrasse: /ˈfɪlmˌʃtraːsə/ (German pronunciation)| n.|电影街|Filmstrasse is a German word meaning "film street". It often refers to a street or area associated with the film industry. |Berlin's Filmstrasse is home to many cinemas and production companies. 柏林的电影街是许多电影院和制片公司的所在地。| The Babelsberg Filmstrasse is a historic studio complex. 巴伯尔斯贝格电影街是一个历史悠久的电影制片厂。|近义词: cinema street, film district|反义词: None|词性: noun

Etymology:
Film: from English "film"
Strasse: from German "Straße" meaning "street"
