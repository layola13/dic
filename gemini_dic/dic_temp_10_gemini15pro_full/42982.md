covetingly:/ˈkʌvɪtɪŋli/
adverb
贪婪地，渴望地
With a strong desire to possess something belonging to someone else.
She looked covetingly at her friend's new car. 她贪婪地看着她朋友的新车。
Near Synonyms: enviously, jealously, greedily, longingly, yearningly
Antonyms: generously, indifferently, selflessly
词性: 副词
