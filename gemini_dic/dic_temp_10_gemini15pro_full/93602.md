imbay:/ɪmˈbeɪ/ | v. | 嵌入，包埋，使沉浸 | To enclose or embed as in a bay; to shut in. | The jewel was imbayed in the golden setting.  宝石被嵌入金质底座中。

The knight was imbayed by his enemies. 骑士被敌人包围了。 | 近义词：embed, enclose, encase, surround | 反义词: expose, release | 词性: 动词

词源: From im- (expressing intensive force) + bay (a recess or compartment).  "Bay," in this context, refers to an enclosure. The prefix "im-" intensifies the meaning, suggesting a firm or complete enclosure.
