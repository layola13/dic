embittering: /ɪmˈbɪtərɪŋ/
adjective
使痛苦的；令人难受的
Causing someone to feel bitter or resentful.
The constant criticism had an embittering effect on him.
不断的批评使他感到痛苦。
His betrayal had an embittering effect on their friendship.
他的背叛使他们的友谊变得痛苦。
近义词:  resentful, acrimonious, rancorous
反义词:  sweetening, soothing, pleasant
词性: adjective
词源: From the verb "embitter," which comes from the Old English "biter" (bitter) + the prefix "em-" (to cause to be).
