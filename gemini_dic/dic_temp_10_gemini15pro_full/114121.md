magus:/ˈmeɪɡəs/| n.|魔术师，法师，星象家|A magus is a sorcerer, magician, or astrologer, especially a wise man of the ancient East.  It often refers to the biblical Magi, the wise men who visited the infant Jesus.|The old magus cast a powerful spell.  这位老法师施了一个强大的咒语。|Synonyms: sorcerer, wizard, magician, warlock|Antonyms: muggle, ordinary person|Noun
