parisienne:/pəˌrɪziˈɛn/ | n. adj. |巴黎女人；巴黎式的|A woman who is from Paris or embodies Parisian style and culture.  A style or characteristic typical of Paris or its inhabitants. |例句：She is a true parisienne, with her effortless chic and love of cafes. 她是一位真正的巴黎女人，举手投足间都流露出毫不费力的时尚感，而且热爱咖啡馆。

例句：The parisienne style is known for its understated elegance. 巴黎风格以其低调的优雅而闻名。|近义词：Parisian (adj.) |反义词:None| noun, adjective
