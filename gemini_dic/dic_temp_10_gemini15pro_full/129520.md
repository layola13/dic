non-attributably:/ˌnɒn əˈtrɪbjʊtəbli/ (US) /ˌnɑːn əˈtrɪbjətəbli/| adv.|非正式地；不具名地；不愿透露姓名地|Without being able to be attributed to a particular person or source.  Used to indicate that information has been provided on the condition that the source is not identified.| "The official said, non-attributably, that the negotiations were going badly."  这位官员不愿透露姓名地表示，谈判进展不顺利。|anonymously, off the record, unofficially|officially, on the record|adverb
