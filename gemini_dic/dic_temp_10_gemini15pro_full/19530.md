bifilar:/baɪˈfaɪlər/ | adj. | 双线的，双丝的 | Having two threads or filaments.  Used especially of a type of suspension or winding. | The bifilar pendulum is used in physics experiments. 双线摆用于物理实验。 |  double-stranded, twin-wire | single-stranded, single-wire | adjective

bifilar:/baɪˈfaɪlər/ | n. | 双线摆 | A pendulum suspended by two threads or wires. | The experiment utilized a bifilar suspension. 这个实验使用了双线悬挂。| double pendulum | single pendulum | noun
