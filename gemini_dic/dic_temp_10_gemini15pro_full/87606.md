heterotrophic:/ˌhɛtərəˈtrɒfɪk/ | adj. |异养的| Obtaining nutrition from organic substances.  An organism that cannot produce its own food and must obtain its nutrients by consuming other organisms or their products.|例句：Most animals are heterotrophic, relying on plants or other animals for food. 大多数动物是异养的，依靠植物或其他动物获取食物。

例句：Fungi are heterotrophic organisms that decompose organic matter. 真菌是分解有机物的异养生物。|近义词：consumer, organotrophic |反义词: autotrophic|词性:adjective
