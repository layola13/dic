anodizations:/ˌænədaɪˈzeɪʃənz/
n.
阳极氧化处理; 阳极氧化(anodization的复数)
The process of coating a metal, particularly aluminum, with a protective oxide layer by electrolytic means. This increases corrosion resistance and can allow for dyeing or other surface treatments.
例句：The anodizations on the aluminum parts gave them a durable and attractive finish.  铝制零件上的阳极氧化处理赋予它们耐用且美观的表面。

例句：Multiple anodizations were performed to achieve the desired color.  进行了多次阳极氧化处理以达到所需的色彩。

近义词：oxidation, surface treatment
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "cathodic protection" as an opposing process)
词性: noun (plural)
