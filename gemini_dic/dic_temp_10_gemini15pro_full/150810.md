paraffined:/ˈpærəfɪnd/| adj.| 浸过石蜡的，涂过石蜡的| coated or impregnated with paraffin wax. | The floorboards were paraffined to protect them from moisture. 地板涂了石蜡以防潮。|近义词：waxed, coated, sealed |反义词: untreated, bare |词性:形容词
词源：From paraffin + -ed. Paraffin from German Paraffin, from parum (“too little; not enough”) + affinis (“related, connected, similar to”).
