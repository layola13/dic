holey:/ˈhoʊli/| adj.|多孔的，有洞的|Full of holes; perforated.|例句：The socks were holey after years of wear.  这双袜子穿了很多年后都是洞。|例句：The cheese we bought was holey.  我们买的奶酪有很多洞。|近义词：perforated, porous, punctured|反义词:solid, intact|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English holi, from hol "hole" + -y.  来自中古英语 holi，由 hol“洞”+ -y 组成。
