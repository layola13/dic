anthropophagical: /ˌænθrəpəˈfædʒɪkəl/
adjective
食人的；吃人的
Relating to anthropophagy, the practice of eating human flesh.
The tribe was accused of anthropophagical rituals.  这个部落被指控进行食人仪式。
Anthropophagical practices were documented in some ancient cultures.  在一些古代文化中，食人习俗被记录在案。
synonyms: cannibalistic, man-eating
antonyms: herbivorous, vegetarian
word type: adjective
