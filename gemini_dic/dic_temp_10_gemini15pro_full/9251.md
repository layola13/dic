antipathous:/ænˈtɪpəθəs/
adj.
怀有敌意的；厌恶的；不相容的
Having a strong aversion or dislike; feeling antipathy.
He felt antipathous towards his rival. 他对他的对手怀有敌意。
近义词: hostile, averse, antagonistic, inimical
反义词: sympathetic, friendly, amicable
词性: 形容词

antipathy:/ænˈtɪpəθi/
n.
反感；厌恶；憎恶
A deep-seated feeling of aversion.
She felt a strong antipathy towards him. 她对他非常反感。
近义词: aversion, dislike, hostility, animosity
反义词:  affinity, liking, sympathy
词性: 名词

词源: From Greek antipathēs "having opposite feelings," from anti- "against" + pathos "feeling."
