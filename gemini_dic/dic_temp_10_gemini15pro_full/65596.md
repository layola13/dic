evaluates:/ɪˈvæljuːeɪts/| v. |评估，评价|To determine or judge the value or worth of something; to assess.|The teacher evaluates student progress based on test scores and classroom participation. 老师根据考试成绩和课堂参与度来评估学生的进步。|The company evaluates employee performance annually. 公司每年评估员工绩效。|近义词：assess, appraise, judge, examine, analyze|反义词:ignore, disregard|词性:动词
词源: From Late Latin *evaluatus*, past participle of *evaluare* "to determine the value of," from *ex-* "out" + *valere* "to be strong, be worth."
