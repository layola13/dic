deliriant:/dɪˈlɪriənt/| adj. n.|谵妄的；引起谵妄的；谵妄患者|Causing or affected by delirium; a drug that induces delirium.|例句：The patient was delirious with fever. 病人因发烧而神志不清。

例句：Some plants have deliriant properties. 一些植物具有致谵妄的特性。|近义词：hallucinogenic, psychotomimetic, intoxicating|反义词：lucid, clearheaded|词性: adjective, noun

deliriant:/dɪˈlɪriənt/|n.|谵妄患者|A person experiencing delirium.|例句：The deliriant was restrained for his own safety.  这位谵妄的病人为了自身安全而被约束。

例句：The deliriants in the ward required constant monitoring.  病房里的谵妄患者需要持续的监控。|近义词：madman, lunatic (dated and often offensive)|反义词：sane person, rational individual|词性: noun
