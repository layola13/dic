incapacitate:/ˌɪnkəˈpæsɪteɪt/| vt.|使无能力，使丧失能力，使不合格|The injury incapacitated him from playing football.  这次受伤使他无法参加足球比赛。|例句：The virus incapacitated many computers in the network.  该病毒使网络中的许多计算机瘫痪。|近义词：disable, cripple, debilitate|反义词:enable, empower|verb
