decision:/dɪˈsɪʒən/ | n. | 决定，决心；决议 | A conclusion or resolution reached after consideration. |  She made the difficult decision to leave her job. 她做出了艰难的决定，离开她的工作。

The judge's decision is final. 法官的判决是最终的。| 近义词：conclusion, resolution, determination, judgment, choice | 反义词：indecision, hesitation | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *decision, from Latin dēcisiō (“a cutting off, decision”), from dēcīsus, past participle of dēcīdere (“to cut off, decide”), from de- (“off, down”) + caedere (“to cut”).
