adjectivally:/ˌædʒɪkˈtɪvəli/| adv.|以形容词的方式|In a way that functions as an adjective; describing something.|He used the noun "love" adjectivally. 他以形容词的方式使用了名词“爱”。|The word "red" is used adjectivally here to describe the car.  这里“红色”一词被用作形容词来描述这辆车。|similarly, descriptively|n/a|adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From adjective + -ly.  Adjective comes from Late Middle English: from Old French adjectif, from Latin adjectivum (nomen) '(noun) that is added', from the verb adjicere, from ad- 'to' + jacere 'throw'.
