backwards:/ˈbæk.wədz/ (US) /ˈbæk.wədz/ (UK)| adv. adj.|向后地，倒退地；相反地|moving or directed towards the back or rear; in reverse; opposite to the usual or expected direction|He walked backwards slowly. 他慢慢地向后走。|synonyms: backward, in reverse, inversely|antonyms: forwards, forward, ahead|adverb, adjective

backwards:/ˈbæk.wədz/ (US) /ˈbæk.wədz/ (UK)| adj.|落后的，发展迟缓的|behind in development or progress|The country's economy is quite backwards.  这个国家的经济相当落后。|synonyms: underdeveloped, undeveloped, retarded|antonyms: advanced, developed, progressive|adjective
