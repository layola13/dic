outstinking:/ˌaʊtˈstɪŋkɪŋ/ | adj. | 臭气熏天的，恶臭难闻的 |  Emitting a very strong and unpleasant smell; surpassing something else in its foul odor.  It's a non-standard and informal word often used hyperbolically. | The outstinking garbage sat in the alley for days. 臭气熏天的垃圾在巷子里放了好几天。 | The locker room was absolutely outstinking after the game.  比赛结束后，更衣室简直臭气熏天。| 近义词: foul-smelling, reeking, fetid, putrid | 反义词: fragrant, sweet-smelling | 词性: 形容词
