agglutinative:/əˈɡluːtɪnətɪv/| adj.|黏着性的，粘着语的|Characterized by combining several morphemes, each with a single meaning, into a single word.|Turkish is an agglutinative language, where a single word can express a complex idea. 土耳其语是一种粘着语，一个单词可以表达一个复杂的概念。|例句：Swahili is an agglutinative language. 斯瓦希里语是一种黏着语。

例句：Agglutinative languages often have very long words. 粘着语通常有很长的单词。|近义词：synthetic, polysynthetic|反义词：isolating, analytic|词性: adjective
