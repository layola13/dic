pasteurised:/ˈpæstəraɪzd/ /ˈpɑːstəraɪzd/| adj.| 巴氏杀菌的，经过巴氏消毒的|  heated to a particular temperature for a period of time in order to kill harmful bacteria but without changing the flavour or nutritional value of the product.| The milk is pasteurised to kill harmful bacteria. 牛奶经过巴氏消毒以杀死有害细菌。|  sterilized, disinfected| contaminated, unpasteurized| adjective
pasteurize:/ˈpæstəraɪz/ /ˈpɑːstəraɪz/| v.| 巴氏杀菌，巴氏消毒| to heat a liquid, especially milk, to a particular temperature for a period of time in order to kill harmful bacteria but without changing the flavour or nutritional value of the liquid.|  The milk is pasteurized to make it safe to drink.  牛奶经过巴氏消毒以确保饮用安全。| sterilize, disinfect | contaminate | verb
