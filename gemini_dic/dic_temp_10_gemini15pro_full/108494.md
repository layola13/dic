layest:/ˈleɪɪst/| v.|你放置（古英语，第二人称单数形式）| This archaic form is the second person singular present tense of "lay" (to put or place). It is rarely used in modern English.  | Thou layest thy hand upon the sick, and they recover. 你把手放在病人身上，他们就痊愈了。|近义词：placest, puttest, settest|反义词: takest, removest |verb
