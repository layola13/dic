dissipationless:/ˌdɪsɪˈpeɪʃənləs/
adjective
无耗散的
Characterized by the absence of dissipation, meaning energy is conserved and not lost to heat, friction, or other irreversible processes.
A dissipationless current flows without resistance. 无耗散电流无电阻流动。
Superconductors exhibit dissipationless flow of electricity. 超导体表现出无损耗的电流。
lossless, non-dissipative, conservative
