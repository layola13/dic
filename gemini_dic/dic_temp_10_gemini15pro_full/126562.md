naenia:/ˈniːniə/ /ˈneɪniə/| n. |挽歌；葬歌；哀歌|A song of lamentation; a dirge or funeral song, especially one sung by hired mourners in ancient Rome.|The naenia echoed through the ancient Roman streets as the procession moved slowly towards the cemetery. 古罗马的街道上回荡着挽歌，送葬队伍缓缓地向墓地走去。

The mourners sang a mournful naenia for the deceased emperor.  送葬者为已故的皇帝唱了一首悲伤的挽歌。|近义词：dirge, lament, elegy, requiem|反义词:paean, hymn|词性:noun
