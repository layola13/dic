impassioned:/ɪmˈpæʃənd/| adj.|充满激情的，热烈的|Filled with or showing great emotion; fervent.|She delivered an impassioned speech about the importance of education. 她发表了一场充满激情的演讲，讲述了教育的重要性。|He made an impassioned plea for help. 他充满激情地恳求帮助。|近义词：fervent, ardent, passionate, fiery, zealous|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, unemotional|词性:形容词
Etymology:
From Late Latin impassionatus, past participle of impassionare (“to arouse passion in”), from in- (“in, into”) + passio (“passion, suffering”).
