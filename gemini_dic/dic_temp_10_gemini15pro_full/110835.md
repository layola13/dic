linsang:/ˈlɪnsæŋ/ | n. | 灵猫 |A slender, cat-like carnivorous mammal of southeast Asia, related to the civets. |  The linsang is a nocturnal hunter. 灵猫是一种夜间活动的猎手。

|  Linsangs are known for their spotted coats. 灵猫以其斑点皮毛而闻名。 | 近义词: civet, genet | 反义词:  (No direct opposite, as it refers to a specific animal) | 词性: noun
