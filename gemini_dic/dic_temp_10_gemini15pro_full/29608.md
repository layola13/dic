categorises:/ˈkætəɡəˌraɪzɪz/
|v.|分类，归类|This system categorises books by subject.  这个系统按主题对书籍进行分类。|例句：The library categorises its books by genre. 图书馆按体裁对书籍进行分类。|近义词：classifies, sorts, groups, arranges|反义词: mixes, combines, jumbles|词性:动词

categorizes:/ˈkætəɡəˌraɪzɪz/
|v.|分类，归类|She categorizes her spending into different areas, like food, transportation, and entertainment. 她将她的支出分成不同的领域，例如食物、交通和娱乐。|例句：He categorizes his music collection by artist. 他按艺术家对他的音乐收藏进行分类。|近义词：classifies, sorts, groups, arranges|反义词: mixes, combines, jumbles|词性:动词
