behoving:/bɪˈhuːvɪŋ/
adjective
合适的，适宜的
Suitable or appropriate; incumbent (on or upon)
It ill behoves you to speak so rudely to your elders.  对你长辈如此粗鲁说话是不合适的。
Synonyms: appropriate, fitting, proper, suitable, seemly, decorous
Antonyms: inappropriate, unfitting, improper, unsuitable, unseemly, indecorous
词性: adjective
