overdebilitated: /ˌoʊvər dəˈbɪlɪˌteɪtɪd/
adj.
过度衰弱的，极度虚弱的
Excessively weakened or enfeebled, beyond simply being debilitated.  This implies a severe state of weakness, often due to prolonged illness or excessive strain.
The overdebilitated patient could barely lift his head.  过度衰弱的病人几乎抬不起头来。
After months of battling the infection, she was left overdebilitated and unable to work.  与感染斗争了几个月后，她变得极度虚弱，无法工作。
近义词: extremely weak, severely weakened, enfeebled, frail, infirm
反义词: strong, robust, vigorous, healthy
词性: adjective
词源: "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "debilitate" (from Latin debilitare, "to weaken")
