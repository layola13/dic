blotter:/ˈblɒtər/ (UK) /ˈblɑːtər/ (US)| n.|吸墨纸，记事簿，（警察局的）罪犯登记簿|A piece of absorbent paper used to dry wet ink. A pad of blotting paper. A police record of arrests.|例句：He blotted the signature with the blotter. 他用吸墨纸吸干了签名。

例句：The police blotter was full of petty crimes. 警察的罪犯登记簿上满是轻微罪行。|近义词：absorbent paper, blotting pad, police record, diary, journal|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

blotter:/ˈblɒtər/ (UK) /ˈblɑːtər/ (US)| v.|用吸墨纸吸干|To dry something using a blotter.|例句：She blotted the spilled ink with a tissue. 她用纸巾吸干了洒出的墨水。

例句：He carefully blotted his signature. 他仔细地用吸墨纸吸干了他的签名。|近义词：dry, soak up, absorb|反义词:wet, soak|词性:verb
