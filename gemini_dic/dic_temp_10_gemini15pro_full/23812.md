brazier:/ˈbreɪʒər/| n.|火盆；炭盆；烤肉架|A metal container that holds burning coals, used to provide heat or to cook food.|The brazier glowed red in the twilight.  暮色中，火盆发出红色的光芒。|The aroma of grilling meat wafted from the brazier. 烤肉的香味从火盆里飘出来。|Synonyms: fire basket, grill, barbecue|Antonyms: freezer, refrigerator|noun


brazier:/ˈbreɪʒər/| n.|胸罩制造者；胸衣商|A person who makes or sells brassieres.|She trained as a brazier and opened her own lingerie shop. 她接受了胸罩制造者的培训，开了自己的内衣店。|He learned the trade of brazier from his father. 他从他父亲那里学到了胸衣商的行业。|Synonyms: corsetiere, lingerie maker|Antonyms: N/A|noun
