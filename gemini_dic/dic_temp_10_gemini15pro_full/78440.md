get-wise:/ɡet waɪz/ | adj. | 变聪明，学乖，精明起来 | To become knowledgeable or aware of something, often something that was previously hidden or unknown.  Implies gaining shrewdness or street smarts, often through experience. |  He finally got wise to their tricks and stopped falling for them. 他终于识破了他们的诡计，不再上当了。 |  savvy, clued-up, street-smart | naive, gullible | adjective
