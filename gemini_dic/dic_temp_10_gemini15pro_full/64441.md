ericolin:/ˌɛrɪˈkoʊlɪn/ | n. |  艾瑞克林; 埃里科林 (一种糖苷)| A crystalline phenolic glycoside, C14H16O6, obtained from several ericaceous plants, especially the common heather (Calluna vulgaris). It is toxic to grazing animals.| The sheep were poisoned by ericolin in the heather. 羊被石楠花中的艾瑞克林毒死了。 | 近义词: arbutin (熊果苷) (similar chemical structure and found in similar plants) | 反义词:  None (no direct antonym for a specific chemical compound) | 词性: noun
