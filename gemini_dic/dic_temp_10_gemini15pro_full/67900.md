facadism: /fəˈsɑːdɪzəm/ /fəˈseɪdɪzəm/
| n. |门面主义；虚假的门面|The practice of preserving only the facade of a building while demolishing and rebuilding the rest.  保留建筑物仅正面外观而拆除和重建其余部分的做法。|The developers were accused of facadism after they tore down a historic building and only kept the front wall.|近义词：preservationism (部分),  facade retention|反义词： demolition, complete rebuilding|词性: 名词

facadism: /fəˈsɑːdɪzəm/ /fəˈseɪdɪzəm/
| n. |表面主义；虚伪|A superficial or deceptive appearance. 表面或欺骗性的外观|His charm was just facadism, hiding a ruthless ambition.| 近义词：superficiality, hypocrisy, pretense|反义词：genuineness, authenticity, sincerity|词性: 名词
