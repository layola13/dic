gump:/ɡʌmp/ | n. | 傻瓜，笨蛋；容易上当的人 | A foolish or naive person; a dupe. |例句：He's such a gump, he believed everything she said. 他真是个傻瓜，他相信她说的每一句话。

例句：Don't be a gump! Think before you act. 别傻了！行动前要三思。|近义词：fool, idiot, dolt, simpleton, chump |反义词：genius, sage, wise person |词性: noun
