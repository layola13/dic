at-the-hands-of:/æt ðə hændz əv/
prepositional phrase
由…；出自…之手；在…手里
experiencing something, usually something unpleasant, as a result of another person's actions
He suffered greatly at the hands of his kidnappers. 他在绑匪手中吃了很多苦头。
近义词: from, by, because of, through
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider using "by one's own hand" for actions self-inflicted)
词性: prepositional phrase

注意事项:  This phrase is generally used to describe negative experiences or suffering.  While it can technically be used to describe positive experiences, it's less common and may sound awkward or unnatural.  It often carries a connotation of being subjected to something rather than actively participating in it.
