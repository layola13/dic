exenteration: /ˌɛksɛntəˈreɪʃən/ | n. |取出；剜除；清除|The surgical removal of the contents of a body cavity, such as the orbit or pelvis.  |例句：Exenteration of the orbit was necessary due to the aggressive nature of the tumor.  由于肿瘤的侵袭性，需要进行眼眶摘除术。

|例句：Pelvic exenteration is a radical surgical procedure performed for some advanced cancers. 盆腔廓清术是一种针对某些晚期癌症的根治性外科手术。 |近义词：evisceration, removal, excision |反义词: implantation, insertion |词性: noun
