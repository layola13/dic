aww:/ɔː/ | interjection | 哦; 啊（表示喜爱或同情） | An exclamation of affection or sympathy, often in response to something cute, sweet, or sad. | Example: Aww, look at that cute puppy! 哦，看那只可爱的小狗！| Example: Aww, that's too bad. 哦，那太糟糕了。| 近义词:ooh, ah | 反义词: (No direct antonym for an interjection expressing emotion) | 词性:interjection
