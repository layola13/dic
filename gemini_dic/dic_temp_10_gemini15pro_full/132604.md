nondiphthongal:/ˌnɒndaɪfˈθɒŋɡəl/ /ˌnɑːndaɪfˈθɑːŋɡəl/| adj.|非双元音的|Not containing a diphthong; (of a vowel) being a pure vowel, not a diphthong.  A nondiphthongal vowel maintains a single, consistent quality throughout its duration, without gliding into another vowel sound.|The word "bet" has a nondiphthongal vowel sound.|近义词:monophthongal|反义词:diphthongal|词性:形容词

词源:
non- (prefix): meaning "not"
diphthong (noun): from Greek "diphthongos," meaning "having two sounds," referring to a vowel sound that changes quality during its pronunciation
-al (suffix): forming adjectives, meaning "related to" or "characterized by"
