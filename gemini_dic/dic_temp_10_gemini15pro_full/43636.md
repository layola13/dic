creationistic:/ˌkriːeɪʃəˈnɪstɪk/
adjective
创造论的，神造论的
Relating to or advocating the theory of creationism.
Creationistic beliefs often clash with scientific evidence. 创造论的信仰经常与科学证据冲突。
The museum rejected the creationistic interpretation of the fossil record.  博物馆拒绝了对化石记录的创造论解释。

近义词: creationist
反义词: evolutionary, Darwinian
词性: adjective
