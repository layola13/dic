come-true:/kʌmˈtruː/| adj.|实现，成真|To become reality; to happen in the way that someone hoped or wished.|Her dream of becoming a doctor came true. 她成为医生的梦想实现了。|Synonyms: materialize, actualize, realize|Antonyms: fail, fall through|adjective

come-true:/kʌmˈtruː/| n.|实现，梦想成真|The realization of a hope or dream.|The trip to Europe was a come-true for her. 去欧洲旅行对她来说是梦想成真。|Synonyms: realization, fulfillment, actualization|Antonyms: failure, disappointment|noun
