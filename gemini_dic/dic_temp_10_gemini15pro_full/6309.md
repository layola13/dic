amortization:/ˌæmərtaɪˈzeɪʃn/ /ˌæmɔːtaɪˈzeɪʃn/
n.
摊销；分期偿还
The gradual reduction of a loan or debt through periodic payments.  This process allocates the cost of an intangible asset over its estimated useful life.
The company's amortization of its new equipment will take five years.  这家公司的新设备摊销将需要五年时间。
amortization of debt 债务的摊销
amortization schedule 摊销计划表

depreciation, write-off
appreciation
noun
