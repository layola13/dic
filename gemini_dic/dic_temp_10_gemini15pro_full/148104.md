overseasoning:/ˌoʊvərˈsiːzənɪŋ/ | n. | 过度调味 | The act of adding too much seasoning to food, resulting in an unbalanced or unpleasant flavor. | The overseasoning ruined the delicate flavor of the fish. 过度调味破坏了鱼的鲜美。| 近义词: overspicing | 反义词: underseasoning | 词性: 名词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "seasoning" (from Old French "saisun," meaning "seasoning")
