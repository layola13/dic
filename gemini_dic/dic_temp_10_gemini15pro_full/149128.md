oxidator:/ˈɒksɪdeɪtər/ (UK), /ˈɑːksɪdeɪtər/ (US)| n.|氧化剂|A substance that oxidizes another substance. It accepts electrons in a chemical reaction.|Potassium permanganate is a strong oxidator.|Synonyms: oxidizing agent, oxidant|Antonyms: reducing agent, reductant|noun

oxidizer:/ˈɒksɪdaɪzər/ (UK), /ˈɑːksɪdaɪzər/ (US)| n.|氧化剂|A substance that oxidizes another substance. It accepts electrons in a chemical reaction.|Liquid oxygen is a common oxidizer in rocket propellants.|Synonyms: oxidizing agent, oxidant|Antonyms: reducing agent, reductant|noun
