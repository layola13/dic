noncontemplative:/ˌnɒnˈkɒntəmpleɪtɪv/
adjective
非冥想的，不沉思的
Not given to or involving contemplation; not meditative.
She led a noncontemplative life, always busy with practical matters. 她过着一种不沉思的生活，总是忙于实际事务。
The CEO preferred noncontemplative employees who could make quick decisions. 首席执行官更喜欢不沉思的员工，他们可以快速做出决定。
近义词:active, practical, pragmatic, unreflective
反义词:contemplative, meditative, reflective, thoughtful
词性:adjective
