livings:/ˈlɪvɪŋz/
n.
生计，生活费用，收入
means of earning a living; livelihood
例句：He earned his livings by writing. 他靠写作谋生。

例句：She makes a comfortable livings as a doctor. 她作为一名医生过着舒适的生活。

近义词：livelihood, subsistence, income, earnings
反义词：expenses, debts

词性：名词

词源：来自live (v.) + -ing (suffix forming nouns denoting action or process).  Ultimately from Old English libban "to live."
