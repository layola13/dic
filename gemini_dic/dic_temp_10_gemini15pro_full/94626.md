impresario:/ɪmˌprɛˈsɑːrioʊ/ /ɪmˌprɛˈsɑːriəʊ/
noun
演出经理；演出承办人；(尤指)歌剧、芭蕾舞演出经理
A person who organizes and often finances concerts, plays, or operas.
The impresario brought the famous opera singer to our city for a performance.  这位演出经理把著名的歌剧演唱家带到我们城市演出。
The impresario was renowned for discovering new talent.  这位演出经理以发掘新秀而闻名。
synonyms:  producer, manager, director, organizer, promoter
antonyms: audience, spectator
