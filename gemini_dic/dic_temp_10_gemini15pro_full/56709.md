doomsayings:/ˈduːmseɪɪŋz/
|n.|预言灾难；凶兆；厄运预言|Predictions of disaster; prophecies of doom.|The constant doomsayings about the economy are depressing.  关于经济的持续不断的厄运预言令人沮丧。|The media is full of doomsayings about the future of the planet.  媒体充斥着关于地球未来的厄运预言。|近义词：forebodings, prophecies, predictions of doom, pessimistic forecasts|反义词：optimism, hope, positive predictions|词性：名词

词源:  From "doom" (fate, destiny, especially a bad one) + "saying" (a statement).  The word emphasizes the act of speaking about impending doom.  来自“doom”（命运，尤指坏的命运）+“saying”（陈述）。这个词强调了谈论即将到来的厄运的行为。
