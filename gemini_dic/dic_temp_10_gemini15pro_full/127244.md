nautical:/ˈnɔːtɪkəl/| adj.|航海的，海上的|Relating to ships, sailors, or navigation.|The ship had a nautical chart of the area. 这艘船有该地区的海图。|近义词：marine, maritime, seafaring|反义词:land, terrestrial|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin nauticus, from Ancient Greek ναυτικός (nautikós, “of a ship, naval”), from ναύτης (naútēs, “sailor”), from ναῦς (naûs, “ship”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *néh₂us (“ship”).
