enmanche:/ɑ̃ˈmɑ̃ʃ/ | n. | 装柄; 套柄; 刀柄;  (乐器等的)颈 | The act of putting a handle on something, or the handle itself. Especially used in the context of swords, knives, or musical instruments. | The luthier carefully fitted the enmanche to the violin's body.  制琴师小心翼翼地将琴颈装到琴身上。| 近义词: handle, haft, grip, neck | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From French, from en- (in, on) + manche (handle, sleeve). Ultimately from Latin manica (sleeve).
