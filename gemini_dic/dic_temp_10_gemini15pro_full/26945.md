calcareous:/kælˈkɛəriəs/
adjective
钙质的；含钙的
Containing calcium carbonate; chalky.
The soil in this area is calcareous and supports a unique range of plants.  这个地区的土壤是钙质的，生长着一种独特的植物。
The cliffs are calcareous and erode easily. 这些悬崖是钙质的，很容易被侵蚀。
synonyms: chalky, limy
antonyms: siliceous


calcariferous:/ˌkælkəˈrɪfərəs/
adjective
含钙的；产生碳酸钙的
Containing or producing calcium carbonate.
Calcariferous glands are found in some earthworms. 一些蚯蚓体内有含钙腺。
The calcariferous deposits formed the basis of the limestone cave. 含钙沉积物形成了石灰岩洞穴的基础。
synonyms: calcareous, chalky
antonyms: siliceous
