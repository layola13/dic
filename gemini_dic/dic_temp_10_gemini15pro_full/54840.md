displaceable:/dɪsˈpleɪsəbl/
adj.
可替代的; 可转移的; 可置换的
Capable of being displaced or moved from one place to another.
The refugees are living in displaceable tents. 这些难民住在可移动的帐篷里。
The indigenous people were displaceable from their ancestral lands.  这些原住民被迫离开他们祖先的土地。
synonyms: movable, portable, transferable, removable
antonyms: fixed, immovable, permanent
词性: 形容词
