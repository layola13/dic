brankursine: /bræŋˈkɜːrsaɪn/ | n. |熊果碱| A crystalline glucoside, C₁₃H₁₆O₇, obtained from bearberry leaves and used as a urinary antiseptic. | Bearberry extract, standardized for brankursine content, is often used in herbal remedies for urinary tract infections. 熊果叶提取物，以熊果碱含量标准化，常用于治疗尿路感染的草药中。 | 近义词: arbutin | 反义词:  (No direct antonym as it's a chemical compound) | 词性: noun

词源: From German *Bärenklau* (bear's claw) referring to the bearberry plant, combined with "-sine," a common suffix for alkaloids and glycosides.
