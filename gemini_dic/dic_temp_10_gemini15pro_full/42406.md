counterclaiming:/ˌkaʊntərˈkleɪmɪŋ/
| n. | 反诉，反要求 | the act of making a counterclaim | The lawyer specialized in counterclaiming in contract disputes.  律师专门处理合同纠纷中的反诉。 | Synonyms: opposing, rebutting, challenging | Antonyms: agreeing, conceding, accepting
| v. | 反诉，反要求 | to make a counterclaim |  The defendant is counterclaiming for damages.  被告反诉要求赔偿损失。 | Synonyms: opposing, rebutting, challenging | Antonyms: agreeing, conceding, accepting


ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- + claiming (present participle of claim).  Counter- means "against" or "opposite." Claim comes from Old French "clamer" meaning "to call out, proclaim."
