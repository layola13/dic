counterbrace:/ˈkaʊntərbreɪs/
noun
支撑; 对撑; 抗衡力
A counterbrace is a brace or support applied in opposition to another to give extra strength or stability.
The bridge was strengthened with counterbraces.  这座桥用对撑加固了。
Synonyms: support, brace, strut, prop, reinforcement
Antonyms: weakness, instability
