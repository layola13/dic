attester:/əˈtɛstər/| n.|证明人，证实者|Someone who attests to something, especially in an official capacity, a witness.|The lawyer called an attester to confirm the signature on the will. 律师叫来一位证人来确认遗嘱上的签名。|近义词：witness, verifier, corroborator|反义词：denier, contradictor|noun

attest:/əˈtɛst/| v.|证明，证实|To declare to be true or genuine, typically in a formal context.|Several witnesses can attest to his presence at the scene. 几位目击者可以证明他在现场。|近义词：confirm, verify, corroborate|反义词：deny, contradict|verb
