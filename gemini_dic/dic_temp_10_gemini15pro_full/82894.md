gult:/ɡʌlt/ | n. | 暴食；贪吃；狼吞虎咽 | An act or instance of eating or drinking greedily or to excess. | He indulged in a gult of chocolate cake. 他尽情地吃巧克力蛋糕。 | 近义词：gluttony, overeating, binge, pigging out, gorging | 反义词：abstinence, moderation | 词性：名词

gult:/ɡʌlt/ | v. | 狼吞虎咽地吃；暴饮暴食 | To eat or drink greedily or to excess. | He gulted down the entire pizza. 他狼吞虎咽地吃掉了整个披萨。 | 近义词：gobble, devour, gorge, stuff oneself, overeat | 反义词：abstain, nibble | 词性：动词

词源:  Likely related to dialectal Norwegian and Swedish *gulka "to swallow," perhaps of imitative origin.
