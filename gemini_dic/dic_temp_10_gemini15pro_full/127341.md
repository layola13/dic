nazifies:/ˈnɑːtsɪˌfaɪz/
verb
纳粹化;使纳粹化;使法西斯化
to cause to resemble Nazi Germany or its policies; to imbue with Nazi principles.
The historian argued that the propaganda campaign effectively nazified public opinion. 这位历史学家认为，宣传运动有效地将公众舆论纳粹化了。
The new regime swiftly nazified the education system, removing any mention of democracy or dissent. 新政权迅速将教育系统纳粹化，删除了任何提及民主或异议的内容。
Synonyms: fascistize, totalitarianize
Antonyms: democratize, liberalize
词性:v.
