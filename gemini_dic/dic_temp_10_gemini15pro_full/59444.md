earthwork: /ˈɜːθwɜːk/ (US) /ˈɜːθwɜːk/ (UK)
n.
土方工程；土方工事
Earthworks are engineering works created through the processing of parts of the earth's surface involving quantities of soil or unrock materials.
The Roman earthworks are still visible today. 罗马的土方工事至今仍然可见。

近义词: excavation, embankment, fortification
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "demolition" in some contexts)
词性: noun


earthwork: /ˈɜːθwɜːk/ (US) /ˈɜːθwɜːk/ (UK)
n.
(常用复数)土木工事; 土垒
Earthworks are walls or other defenses made of earth.
The ancient city was surrounded by massive earthworks. 这座古城周围环绕着巨大的土木工事。

近义词: rampart, bulwark
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun
