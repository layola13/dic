masalas:/məˈsɑːləz/| n. |香料，调味料|A mixture of ground spices used in South Asian cooking, especially in Indian, Pakistani, and Bangladeshi cuisine.  These blends vary greatly depending on the region and the dish they are intended for.|She added a blend of masalas to the curry. 她往咖喱里加了一混合香料。|Synonyms: spices, seasonings, condiments|Antonyms: blandness|noun
