nonsegregative:/nɒnˈsɛɡrɪɡətɪv/
adjective
非隔离的，不分离的
Not segregating or tending to segregate; not separating or causing separation.
The development aimed to create a nonsegregative community where people of all backgrounds could live together.  这个开发项目旨在创建一个非隔离的社区，让不同背景的人们可以共同生活。
New urban design principles promote nonsegregative access to public spaces. 新的城市设计原则提倡公共空间的非隔离准入。
近义词: integrated, inclusive, unified
反义词: segregative, separatist, discriminatory
词性: adjective

词源:
From non- + segregative, from segregate + -ive.  "Segregate" comes from the Latin word "segregatus," the past participle of "segregare," meaning "to separate from the flock."
