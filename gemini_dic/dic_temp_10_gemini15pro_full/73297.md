forecarriage:/ˈfɔːrˌkærɪdʒ/ /ˈfɔːrˌkerɪdʒ/| n.|前车，前转向架，（飞机的）前起落架|The forecarriage of the aircraft collapsed on landing. 飞机的前起落架在着陆时垮掉了。|Synonyms: front landing gear, undercarriage, nose gear|Antonyms: rear landing gear, main landing gear|noun
forecarriage:/ˈfɔːrˌkærɪdʒ/ /ˈfɔːrˌkerɪdʒ/| n.|（车辆的）前桥|The forecarriage of the truck took the brunt of the impact. 卡车的前桥承受了撞击的主要力量。|Synonyms: front axle, front end|Antonyms: rear axle, back end|noun
forecarriage:/ˈfɔːrˌkærɪdʒ/ /ˈfɔːrˌkerɪdʒ/| n.|（机器的）前部支架|The forecarriage of the crane allows for precise movement. 起重机的前部支架可以进行精确的移动。|Synonyms: supporting frame, chassis, framework|Antonyms: rear support|noun
