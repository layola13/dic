bathmism:/ˈbæθmɪzəm/
n.
生长力，萌发力，兴奋性, (尤指)细胞的生长力
The inherent growth force or irritability of living tissues or cells, especially the power of germination in seeds.
The bathmism of the plant allowed it to recover quickly after the frost.  这种植物的生长力使它在霜冻后迅速恢复。
近义词：germination, growth, irritability, excitability
反义词：dormancy, inactivity, quiescence
词性：noun


Etymology:
From Greek *βαθμός* (*bathmos*) "step, degree, threshold" + *-ismos* (suffix forming abstract nouns).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *gʷʰem-* ("to step, to come, to go").
