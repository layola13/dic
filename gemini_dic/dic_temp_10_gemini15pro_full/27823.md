canoe-like:/kəˈnuːˌlaɪk/
adj.
像独木舟的，类似独木舟的
Resembling or shaped like a canoe.  Having a long, narrow, and relatively shallow hull.
The boat was canoe-like in its shape, long and narrow. 这艘船的形状像独木舟，又长又窄。
The new kayak has a canoe-like hull, making it very stable.  这艘新皮划艇的船体像独木舟一样，使它非常稳定。
近义词:canoe-shaped, elongated, narrow
反义词:wide, broad, expansive
词性:形容词


Etymology:
From canoe + -like.  "Canoe" originates from the Carib word "canoa," adopted into Spanish and then English.  "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
