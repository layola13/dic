osage:/ˈoʊseɪdʒ/| n. adj.|奥色治橙(树)，奥色治人(族)|A thorny North American tree (Maclura pomifera) with hard, durable, yellow wood and large, inedible, yellowish-green fruit. Also refers to a Native American tribe of the Dhegiha Siouan language group, originally from the Ohio River valley. They now live primarily in Oklahoma.|例句：The Osage orange tree is known for its tough wood. 奥色治橙树以其坚硬的木材而闻名。

例句：The Osage Nation has a rich cultural heritage. 奥色治民族有着丰富的文化遗产。|近义词：bow-wood (for the wood),  Siouan (for the people)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun, adjective
