lowlanders:/ˈloʊləndərz/
noun
低地人；居住在低地的人
People who live in the lowlands, especially in Scotland, as opposed to highlanders.
The lowlands are fertile, attracting many lowlanders to farm the land. 低地土地肥沃，吸引了许多低地人来耕种。
