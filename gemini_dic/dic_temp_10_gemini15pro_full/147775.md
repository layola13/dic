overpromising: /ˌoʊvərˈprɑːmɪsɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度承诺的 | Making promises that are unlikely to be fulfilled, often to impress or persuade someone. | The company went bankrupt after overpromising and underdelivering on their contracts.  这家公司因过度承诺和交付不足而破产。| 近义词：exaggerating, overselling | 反义词：underpromising, realistic | 词性：形容词

overpromise: /ˌoʊvərˈprɑːmɪs/ | v. | 过度承诺 | To promise more than one can or will deliver. | The salesman overpromised to make the sale, but couldn't deliver.  为了达成交易，销售员做出了过多的承诺，但却无法兑现。 | 近义词：exaggerate, oversell | 反义词：underpromise, understate | 词性：动词
