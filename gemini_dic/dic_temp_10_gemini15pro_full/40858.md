cooingly:/ˈkuːɪŋli/| adv.|柔声地，亲昵地|In a soft, gentle, and loving voice, often used to describe the way someone speaks to a baby or loved one.  Derived from "coo" (make a soft murmuring sound) + "-ing" (present participle) + "-ly" (adverb suffix).|She spoke cooingly to the baby, soothing him back to sleep. 她柔声地对婴儿说话，哄他入睡。|softly, tenderly, gently, lovingly|harshly, roughly|adverb
