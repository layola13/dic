bespatter:/bɪˈspætər/
verb
溅污，玷污
To cover with drops or spots of something.  To soil by splashing or scattering something.
The car was bespattered with mud after driving through the puddle. 汽车驶过水坑后溅满了泥。
The wall was bespattered with paint. 墙上溅满了油漆。
近义词:splash, splatter, daub, smear
反义词:cleanse, purify
词性:v.
