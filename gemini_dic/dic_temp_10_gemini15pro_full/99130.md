intercepted:/ˌɪntərˈseptɪd/ | adj. |被拦截的，被截获的|Stopped or seized while in progress or motion.|The intercepted message revealed the enemy's plans.  被截获的信息揭露了敌人的计划。|Synonyms: caught, seized, blocked, obstructed |Antonyms: allowed, permitted, released, missed|adjective
