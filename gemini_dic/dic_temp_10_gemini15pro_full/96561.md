indoxylic:/ˌɪndɒkˈsɪlɪk/ | adj. | 吲哚氧基的，吲哚羧酸的 | Relating to or derived from indoxyl or indoxylic acid.  | This indoxylic compound shows promising antibacterial activity.  这种吲哚氧基化合物显示出良好的抗菌活性。 |  Indoxyl-related | N/A | adjective

Etymology:

From indoxyl + -ic.  Indoxyl itself comes from  indigo + -yl, reflecting its role as a precursor to indigo dye.  The "-ic" suffix denotes relation or derivation.
