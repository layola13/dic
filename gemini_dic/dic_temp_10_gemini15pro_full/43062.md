cowlstaff:/ˈkaʊlstæf/ | n. | 扛桶棒；扁担 | A pole about two meters long with a fork at one end for carrying a pail, bucket, or other burden across the shoulders. |  He used a cowlstaff to carry two buckets of water from the well. 他用扁担从井里挑了两桶水。 | synonym: carrying pole, yoke | antonym: N/A | noun
