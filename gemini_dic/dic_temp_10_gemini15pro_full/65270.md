eucharistical:/ˌjuːkərˈɪstɪk(ə)l/
adjective
圣餐的；感恩的
Relating to the Eucharist or Holy Communion; expressing gratitude or thanksgiving.
The eucharistical service was held in the cathedral. 圣餐仪式在大教堂举行。
The priest gave a eucharistical prayer. 牧师做了感恩祷告。
近义词:communion, sacramental, thankful, grateful
反义词:ungrateful, thankless
词性:adjective
词源:From Late Latin eucharisticus, from Greek eucharistikos, from eucharistos grateful, from eu- + charizesthai to show favor.
