grassing:/ˈɡrɑːsɪŋ/ /ˈɡræsɪŋ/
| n. |告密，告发 (UK, informal)|The act of informing the police or other authorities about someone's illegal or wrongdoing.|He denied grassing on his friends to the police. 他否认向警方告发他的朋友。|近义词：informing, snitching, ratting, telling on|反义词: protecting, shielding|名词

| v. |告密，告发 (UK, informal, present participle of "grass")|The act of informing the police or other authorities about someone's illegal or wrongdoing.|He was caught grassing to the teacher about who started the fight.  他被发现向老师告密谁挑起了这场打架。|近义词：informing, snitching, ratting, telling on|反义词: protecting, shielding|动词
