delundung: /dəˈlʊndʊŋ/
verb
驱逐，逐出；倾盆而下；倾泻
to drive or cast out; to expel; to pour down heavily
Heavy rain deluged the city.  暴雨倾盆而下，淹没了这座城市。
The refugees were deluged from their homes by the flood.  难民被洪水逐出家园。
Synonyms: expel, oust, evict, eject, banish, exile, flood, inundate, drench, soak
Antonyms: admit, welcome, harbor, shelter, protect, dry
词性: 动词
