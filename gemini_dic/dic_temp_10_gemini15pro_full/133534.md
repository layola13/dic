nonfiguratively:/ˌnɒnˈfɪɡərətɪvli/ /ˌnɑːnˈfɪɡjərətɪvli/
adv.
字面上地；非比喻地
literally, not figuratively; in a way that relies on the literal meaning of words
She described the scene nonfiguratively, sticking to the observable facts. 她如实地描述了现场，坚持可观察到的事实。
literally, directly
figuratively, metaphorically
adverb
