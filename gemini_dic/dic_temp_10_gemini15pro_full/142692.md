other fish to fry:/ˈʌðər fɪʃ tə fraɪ/ | idiom | 其他更重要的事情要做 |  Having other, more important or pressing matters to attend to. | I can't help you with that right now; I have other fish to fry. 我现在不能帮你做那件事；我还有其他更重要的事情要做。 | Example:  "Sorry, I can't come to the party tonight, I have other fish to fry."  对不起，我今晚不能来参加派对了，我还有其他更重要的事情要做。| synonyms: other priorities, bigger fish to fry, more pressing matters | antonyms: nothing to do, free time | idiom
