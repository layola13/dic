cocas:/ˈkoʊkəs/| n. |古柯叶；可卡因|Coca leaves; cocaine.  Derived from the Quechua word "kuka."  Refers to the leaves of the coca plant, native to South America, which are traditionally chewed for their stimulant effects and used in the production of cocaine.|The indigenous people have chewed cocas for centuries.  土著居民咀嚼古柯叶已有几个世纪了。|Synonym: coca, cuca |Antonym: None |Noun
