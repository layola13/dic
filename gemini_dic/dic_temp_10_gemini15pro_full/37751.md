compass:/ˈkʌmpəs/ (US), /ˈkʌmpəs/ (UK)| n.|指南针；圆规；范围；界限|An instrument containing a magnetized pointer that shows the direction of magnetic north and bearings from it.  A tool used for drawing circles and arcs.|例句：The sailor used a compass to navigate. 水手用指南针导航。|近义词：navigator, sextant (for navigation); dividers (for drawing)|反义词: None|Noun

compasses:/ˈkʌmpəsɪz/ (US), /ˈkʌmpəsɪz/ (UK)| n.|圆规 (复数形式)；界限；范围|A tool used for drawing circles and arcs, typically consisting of two arms joined at one end, one carrying a point and the other a pencil or pen. Also can refer to a range or scope.|例句：She used compasses to draw a perfect circle. 她用圆规画了一个完美的圆。|近义词：dividers, calipers|反义词: None|Noun
