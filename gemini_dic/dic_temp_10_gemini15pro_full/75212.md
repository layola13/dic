froggish:/ˈfrɒɡɪʃ/ (US) /ˈfrɒɡɪʃ/ (UK)| adj.|像青蛙的；呆滞的|Resembling a frog in appearance or behavior; sluggish or apathetic.|He sat there with a froggish expression, unmoving and unresponsive. 他坐在那里，表情呆滞，一动不动，毫无反应。|The child's froggish leap across the puddle was amusing. 孩子像青蛙一样跳过水坑，很有趣。|近义词：amphibian, sluggish, inert, apathetic|反义词：energetic, lively, responsive|词性:形容词
Etymology: From frog + -ish, meaning "like a frog".  The suffix "-ish" is used to form adjectives indicating resemblance or characteristic.
