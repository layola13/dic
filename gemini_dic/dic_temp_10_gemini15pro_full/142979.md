outbanter: /aʊtˈbæntər/
verb
胜过(某人)的妙语连珠；在玩笑方面胜过
To surpass someone in witty repartee; to outdo someone in banter.
He tried to outbanter her, but she was too quick-witted. 他试图在妙语连珠方面胜过她，但她太机智了。
Synonyms: outwit, outsmart, surpass, excel
Antonyms: be outdone, lose to


Etymology:
"Out-" (prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing) + "banter" (noun meaning playful teasing or exchange of witty remarks)
