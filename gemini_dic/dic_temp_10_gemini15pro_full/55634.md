ditchless:/ˈdɪtʃləs/
adjective
无沟的；没有沟渠的
Without a ditch or ditches.  Referring to land that doesn't require drainage ditches due to natural drainage or other methods.
The farmer experimented with ditchless farming techniques to reduce erosion. 农民尝试了无沟耕作技术以减少侵蚀。
The ditchless design of the golf course allows for natural water flow.  高尔夫球场的无沟设计允许自然水流。
近义词:unditched
反义词:ditched
词性:adjective
