discrown:/dɪsˈkraʊn/
verb
废黜；剥夺王位
To remove a crown from; to depose from royal dignity.
The king was discrowned and exiled. 国王被废黜并流放了。

近义词:depose, dethrone, unking
反义词:crown, enthrone
词性:verb
