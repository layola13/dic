hidebound:/ˈhaɪdbaʊnd/| adj.|墨守成规的，迂腐的，保守的|Unwilling or unable to change because of tradition or convention.  Restricted by narrow or rigid views.|The hidebound town council refused to consider any new proposals.  这个墨守成规的镇议会拒绝考虑任何新提案。|近义词：conservative, traditional, inflexible, rigid, unprogressive, reactionary|反义词：progressive, open-minded, flexible, adaptable, liberal|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "hide" (animal skin) + "bound" (constrained).  Originally referred to an animal whose skin is so tight it cannot move freely. This sense was then figuratively applied to people who are inflexible in their thinking.
