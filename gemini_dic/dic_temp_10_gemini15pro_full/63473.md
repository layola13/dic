entropion:/ɛnˈtroʊpiən/ | n. |内翻; 睑内翻 |The inward turning of the eyelid, causing the eyelashes to rub against the eye. |  Entropion can cause corneal irritation and damage. 睑内翻会导致角膜刺激和损伤。| synonym: inturned eyelid | antonym: ectropion | noun

entropion:/ɛnˈtroʊpiən/ | n. |内翻; 睑内翻 |The condition in which the eyelid is turned inwards against the eyeball. | The ophthalmologist diagnosed her with entropion and recommended surgery. 眼科医生诊断她患有睑内翻，并建议手术。| synonym:  inversion of the eyelid | antonym:  eversion of the eyelid | noun
