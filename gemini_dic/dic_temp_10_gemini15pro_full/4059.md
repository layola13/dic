AIDS:/eɪdz/ | n. | 艾滋病 | Acquired Immune Deficiency Syndrome. A disease in which there is a severe loss of the body's cellular immunity, greatly lowering the resistance to infection and malignancy. |  AIDS is a serious global health issue. 艾滋病是一个严重的全球健康问题。|  HIV,  immunodeficiency | health, wellness | noun
