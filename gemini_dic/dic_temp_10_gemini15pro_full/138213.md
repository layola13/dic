noshable:/ˈnɒʃəbl/ (UK) /ˈnɑːʃəbl/ (US)| adj.|可吃的，美味的，适合吃的|Something that is noshable is good to eat, especially as a snack.|These crisps are really noshable. 这些薯片真好吃。|近义词：edible, tasty, palatable, snackable|反义词：inedible, unpalatable|词性:形容词
词源:  "nosh" (verb, meaning "to eat") + "-able" (suffix, making an adjective meaning "capable of being"). "Nosh" itself comes from Yiddish "nashn."
