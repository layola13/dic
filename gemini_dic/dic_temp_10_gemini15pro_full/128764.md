nigglings:/ˈnɪɡlɪŋz/| n. |琐事；烦扰；令人不安的小事|Small complaints, worries, or annoyances that are not very serious but can be irritating.|The constant nigglings about the noise were starting to get to him.  不断地抱怨噪音让他开始感到厌烦。|近义词：complaints, worries, annoyances, quibbles, grievances|反义词:comforts, assurances, praises|词性:名词

Etymology: From the verb "niggle," which originated in the late 16th century.  "Niggle" likely comes from a Scandinavian source, possibly related to Norwegian *knyggla "to twist, entangle."
