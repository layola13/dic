nonincidental:/ˌnɑːnɪnsɪˈdɛntl/| adj.|非附带的，非偶然的，重要的，主要的|Not incidental; significant; important.  Something that is a major or essential part of something else, not secondary or trivial. |例句：The findings of the study played a nonincidental role in shaping future policy.  这项研究的发现对未来政策的制定起了重要作用。

例句：Her contribution to the project was nonincidental, and her expertise proved invaluable. 她对这个项目的贡献非常重要，她的专业知识被证明是无价的。|近义词：essential, important, significant, crucial, vital, integral, substantial|反义词：incidental, minor, trivial, unimportant, negligible, insignificant|词性:形容词
