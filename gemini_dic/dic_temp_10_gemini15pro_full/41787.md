corsair:/ˈkɔːrseər/ (US) /ˈkɔːsɛː/ (UK)| n. |海盗，私掠船|A pirate, especially one of the Barbary Coast, or their ship.  Historically, a privateer authorized by a government to attack enemy shipping.|The corsairs plundered merchant ships throughout the Mediterranean.  这些海盗掠夺了整个地中海的商船。|Synonyms: pirate, buccaneer, privateer, raider|Antonyms: merchant, trader|noun
