electronegatively:/ɪˌlektrəʊˈneɡətɪvli/
| adv. |电负性地|In a way that shows electronegativity; with a tendency to attract electrons.|Fluorine reacts electronegatively with hydrogen. 氟与氢发生电负性反应。|
|Synonym: strongly electronegative|Antonym: electropositively|Adverb|


electronegativity:/ɪˌlektrəʊˌneɡəˈtɪvəti/
| n. |电负性|A measure of the tendency of an atom to attract a bonding pair of electrons.|The electronegativity of oxygen is greater than that of hydrogen. 氧的电负性大于氢的电负性。|
|Synonym: electron affinity|Antonym: electropositivity|Noun|
