nitroprusside:/ˌnaɪtroʊˈprʌsaɪd/| n. |硝普钠|A potent vasodilator, a compound of sodium, iron, and cyanide groups. Used in treating hypertensive emergencies.|Sodium nitroprusside is given intravenously in acute hypertensive emergencies.|Synonyms: Nipride, nitropress|Antonyms:  vasoconstrictor|noun
