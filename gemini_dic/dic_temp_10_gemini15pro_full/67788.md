eyepatch:/ˈaɪˌpæʧ/| n. |眼罩|A patch worn over one eye, typically to protect it or conceal an injury or disfigurement. |He wore an eyepatch after his eye surgery. 他在眼科手术后戴着眼罩。|Synonyms: eye shield, eye cover|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider:  uncovered eye, visible eye) |noun
