nonpalliative: /ˌnɒnˈpælɪətɪv/ /ˌnɑːnˈpælɪeɪtɪv/
adjective
非姑息性的，非缓解性的
Not designed to relieve pain or other symptoms, but to cure or control the underlying disease.
The patient opted for nonpalliative treatment, hoping for a cure.  病人选择了非姑息治疗，希望能治愈。
The doctor recommended nonpalliative chemotherapy. 医生建议采用非姑息性化疗。
近义词: curative, remedial, therapeutic
反义词: palliative
词性: adjective
