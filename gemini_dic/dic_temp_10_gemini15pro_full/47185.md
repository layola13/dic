dayart: /ˈdeɪ.ɑːrt/
noun
日间艺术，白天进行的艺术活动
Dayart refers to artistic activities that take place during the daytime.
Daytime concerts are a form of dayart. 白天举行的音乐会是一种日间艺术形式。
The museum offers dayart workshops for children. 博物馆为孩子们提供日间艺术工作坊。
Synonyms: daytime art, diurnal art
Antonyms: nighttime art, nocturnal art
