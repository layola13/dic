dehydrates:/diːˈhaɪdreɪts/
verb
脱水，使脱水
removes water from; loses water
The sun dehydrates the body. 太阳使身体脱水。

近义词:dries, evaporates
反义词:hydrates, moistens
词性:v.

dehydrates:/diːˈhaɪdreɪts/
noun
脱水物
a substance that has lost water
Certain dehydrates are used in food preservation.  某些脱水物用于食品保存。

近义词:dessicant
反义词:hydrate
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "remove, reverse") + hydrate (from Greek hydōr "water").
