maydays:/ˈmeɪdeɪz/ (US), /ˈmeɪdeɪz/ (UK)| n. |求救信号；紧急呼救|A radiotelephone distress signal used by ships and aircraft.  It is the phonetic equivalent of the French *m'aidez* ("help me").|Example 1: The ship sent out maydays after it struck an iceberg. 这艘船撞上冰山后发出了求救信号。

Example 2:  The pilot issued a mayday call before the plane crashed. 飞行员在飞机坠毁前发出了求救信号。|Synonyms: SOS, distress call, emergency signal|Antonyms: all clear, safety signal|Noun
