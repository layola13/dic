ambrite:/ˈæmbrɪt/ | n. | 琥珀，|A yellowish, translucent fossil resin resembling amber, found in New Zealand coal deposits and used in jewelry. It is often mistaken for true amber (succinite) but is of a different geological age and chemical composition.|New Zealand is known for its deposits of ambrite, a unique type of fossil resin. 新西兰以其独特的化石树脂——琥珀而闻名。| 近义词: amber, copal, resin | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

词源:  From Ambr(i), a Maori name for the North Island of New Zealand + -ite (suffix indicating a mineral or fossil).
