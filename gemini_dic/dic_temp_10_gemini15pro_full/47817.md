decard:/diˈkɑːrd/ |v. |丢弃，抛弃| To discard something is to throw it away or get rid of it because you no longer want or need it.| He discarded his old clothes. 他丢弃了他的旧衣服。| She discarded the broken toy. 她丢弃了坏了的玩具。 | synonyms: abandon, reject, dispose of, ditch, scrap, jettison, cast off, throw away, get rid of | antonyms: keep, retain, preserve, save, cherish | verb
