eulogies:/ˈjuːlədʒiz/
n.
悼词；颂词；赞美词
A speech or piece of writing that praises someone or something highly, typically someone who has just died.
He delivered a moving eulogy at his father's funeral.  他在父亲的葬礼上致了感人的悼词。
近义词：tributes, praises, panegyrics, encomiums
反义词：criticism, condemnation, censure
词性：名词(plural)

eulogy:/ˈjuːlədʒi/
n.
悼词；颂词；赞美词
A speech or piece of writing that praises someone or something highly, typically someone who has just died.
The eulogy celebrated her life and achievements.  这篇悼词颂扬了她的一生和成就。
近义词：tribute, praise, panegyric, encomium
反义词：criticism, condemnation, censure
词性：名词(singular)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *eulogia "praise," from Greek eulogia "praise, blessing," from eu- "well" + -logia "speaking," related to logos "word, speech."
