nothingarianism:/ˈnʌθɪŋɛəriənɪzəm/
n.
虚无主义; 万物虚无论
The belief that nothing is real or matters, or that human values are worthless.
He embraced nothingarianism after the tragic loss of his family.  在痛失家人后，他皈依了虚无主义。
近义词:nihilism, pessimism
反义词:optimism, existentialism
词性:noun
