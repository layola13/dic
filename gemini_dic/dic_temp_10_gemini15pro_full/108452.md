laxnesses:/ˈlæksnəsɪz/
n.
懈怠，松懈，懒散
lack of strictness or firmness; the state of being lax.
The laxnesses in security allowed the thief to enter. 安全方面的松懈让小偷有机可乘。
近义词：negligence, carelessness, slackness, remissness
反义词：diligence, carefulness, strictness
词性: 名词(plural)

词源: From laxness + -es (plural suffix). Laxness comes from lax + -ness. Lax originates from the Latin word "laxus" meaning loose or slack.
