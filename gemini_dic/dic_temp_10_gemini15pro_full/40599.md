convallarin:/kənˌvæləˈrɪn/ | n. |铃兰毒苷|A glycoside, C₁₄H₂₂O₆, extracted from the lily of the valley (Convallaria majalis) as a white crystalline powder, and used as a cardiac stimulant.|Convallarin is a potent cardiotonic glycoside found in lily of the valley. 铃兰毒苷是一种强效的心脏兴奋剂苷，存在于铃兰中。|Convallarin has similar properties to digitalis. 铃兰毒苷的特性与洋地黄相似。|Synonyms: convallamarin |Antonyms: None |Noun
