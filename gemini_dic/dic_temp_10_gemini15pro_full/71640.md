flatulent:/ˈflætʃələnt/ (US), /ˈflætjʊlənt/ (UK)| adj.|胀气的；爱吹牛的；浮夸的|Affected with, characterized by, or producing gas in the stomach or intestines; pompous; inflated.|He felt flatulent after eating too many beans. 他吃了太多豆子后觉得胀气。|Synonyms: gassy, bloated, windy, pompous, pretentious|Antonyms: deflated, humble, modest|adjective

flatus:/ˈfleɪtəs/| n.|肠胃胀气；屁|Gas generated in the stomach or intestines and expelled through the anus.|The doctor said the flatus was normal after the surgery. 医生说手术后胀气是正常的。|Synonyms: gas, wind|Antonyms: N/A|noun
