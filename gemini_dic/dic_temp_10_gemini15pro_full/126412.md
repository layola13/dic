mystagogue: /ˈmɪstəɡɒɡ/ (UK) /ˈmɪstəˌɡɑːɡ/ (US)| n.| 神秘宗教的导师；奥秘的阐释者；装腔作势的人|A person who initiates others into mysteries, especially religious ones; an interpreter of mysteries; a pompous or pretentious teacher.|The mystagogue led the initiates through a series of rituals designed to reveal hidden truths.  那位神秘宗教的导师带领新入教者进行了一系列旨在揭示隐藏真相的仪式。

The professor, a self-proclaimed mystagogue, spoke in riddles and obscure pronouncements.  这位教授，自称是一位奥秘的阐释者，说话总是用谜语和晦涩难懂的言辞。|Synonym: guru, initiate, hierophant|Antonym: skeptic, doubter|Noun
