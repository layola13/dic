misdelivery:/ˌmɪsdɪˈlɪvəri/
noun
投递错误，误送
the act of delivering something to the wrong person or address.
The misdelivery of the package caused a lot of inconvenience. 包裹投递错误造成了很多不便。
misdirection, wrong delivery
correct delivery, proper delivery
n.
