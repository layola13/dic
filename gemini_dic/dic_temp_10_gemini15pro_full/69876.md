ferry:/ˈferi/ (US) /ˈferi/ (UK)| n. v.|渡船，渡运|A boat or ship that carries passengers, vehicles, and goods across a body of water, often a river or short stretch of sea.|The ferry took us across the English Channel. 渡轮带我们穿过英吉利海峡。|近义词：boat, ship, vessel|反义词: (n.) None; (v.) None|noun, verb

ferry:/ˈferi/ (US) /ˈferi/ (UK)| v.|渡运，运送|To transport people or goods across a body of water by ferry.|They ferried the troops across the river. 他们用渡船把部队运过了河。|近义词：transport, convey, carry|反义词: None|verb
