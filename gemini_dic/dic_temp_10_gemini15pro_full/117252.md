mechanicalities:/ˌmekəˈnɪkælɪtiz/
noun
机械部件; 技术细节; 繁琐的程序
the details of how something works or is done
例句：We needn't bother with the mechanicalities of how it works. 我们不必为其运作的机械细节而烦恼。

例句：Let's skip the mechanicalities and get straight to the point.  让我们跳过技术细节，直奔主题。

近义词：details, technicalities, procedures, formalities
反义词：essence, gist, substance
词性:n.
