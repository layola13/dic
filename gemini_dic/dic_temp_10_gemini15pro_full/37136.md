come-hithers:/ˈkʌmˌhɪðəz/ | n. | 勾引的眼神；诱惑的姿态 |  A suggestive look or gesture, typically intended to entice someone into a sexual encounter.  Implies a flirtatious or seductive invitation. | She gave him a come-hither look across the bar. 她隔着吧台给了他一个勾引的眼神。 | synonyms: seductive glance, inviting look, flirtatious gesture | antonyms:  repelling glance, cold stare | noun

come-hither:/ˈkʌmˌhɪðə/ | adj. | 诱惑的；勾引人的 | Having a quality that attracts or entices, often in a sexual way.  Describes something that seems to invite or beckon. | She wore a come-hither dress. 她穿了一件性感的连衣裙。 | synonyms: alluring, enticing, seductive, tempting | antonyms:  repulsive, uninviting, modest | adjective
