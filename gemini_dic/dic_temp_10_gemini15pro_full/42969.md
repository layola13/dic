covertly:/ˈkʌvərtli/ /ˈkoʊvərtli/
adverb
秘密地，隐蔽地
done in a hidden or secret way
She covertly slipped him a note. 她偷偷地塞给他一张纸条。
Synonyms: secretly, stealthily, clandestinely, surreptitiously
Antonyms: openly, publicly, overtly
词性: 副词
