fictionalizations:/ˌfɪkʃənəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/
n.
小说化；虚构化；杜撰
The process of making something fictional; a fictional account or version of something.
The movie was a fictionalization of events that actually happened.  这部电影是对真实事件的小说化。
The book is a fictionalization of his life. 这本书是他人生的虚构化。
synonyms: novelization, fabrication, invention, storytelling
antonyms: factual account, documentation, truth, reality
noun
