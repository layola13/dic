hit-the-fan:/hɪt ðə fæn/
idiom
糟糕透顶；乱成一团；事情败露
When the shit hits the fan, everyone will know who's responsible. 当事情败露时，每个人都会知道谁该负责。
When the news hit the fan, there was public outcry. 当消息传开后，公众一片哗然。
近义词:go south, go pear-shaped, blow up, backfire
反义词:go smoothly, go according to plan
词性:idiom
