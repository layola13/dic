altos:/ˈæltoʊz/ (US), /ˈæl.təʊz/ (UK)| n.|女低音；女低音声部；中音声部|The altos in the choir sang beautifully. 合唱团里的女低音唱得很优美。|A group of singers with low-pitched voices, especially women who sing the alto part in a choir, or the part itself.  From Italian alto "high," used in the phrase contralto, literally "against the high (voice),"  from Latin contra "against" + altus "high." The "contra-" was later dropped, and the meaning shifted from "high" to the lower female voice.|The altos harmonized perfectly with the sopranos. 女低音与女高音完美地融合在一起。|近义词：contraltos|反义词：sopranos|词性：名词
