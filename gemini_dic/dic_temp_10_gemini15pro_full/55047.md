disquisition:/ˌdɪskwɪˈzɪʃən/
noun
专题论文；学术讨论；详尽的探讨
A formal and lengthy discourse on a subject, often in writing.
例句：His disquisition on the nature of good and evil was both insightful and tedious.  他对善恶本质的专题论文既有见地又冗长乏味。

例句：The professor delivered a lengthy disquisition on the history of philosophy. 教授就哲学史发表了长篇大论。

近义词：treatise, discourse, essay, exposition, dissertation
反义词：summary, synopsis, abstract
词性：n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *disquisitionem, from Latin disquisitio (“an inquiry, investigation”), from disquirere (“to investigate”), from dis- (“apart, thoroughly”) + quaerere (“to seek, ask”).
