longhorn:/ˈlɔːŋˌhɔːrn/ (US), /ˈlɒŋˌhɔːn/ (UK)| n. |长角牛; 长角的动物|A breed of cattle with long horns, originally from Spain and Mexico, and common in the southwestern US. |例句：The longhorns grazed peacefully on the open range. 长角牛在开阔的牧场上平静地吃草。

|例句：The rancher herded the longhorns across the river. 牧场主把长角牛赶过河。|近义词：steer, ox, bovine|反义词: shorthorn |noun
