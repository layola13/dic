coleopterist:/ˌkoʊliˈɑːptərɪst/ | n. |鞘翅目昆虫学家|A person who studies or collects beetles (Coleoptera).|例句：The coleopterist spent years cataloging his vast collection of beetles.  这位鞘翅目昆虫学家花了数年时间为他收藏的大量甲虫编目。

例句：The renowned coleopterist discovered a new species of beetle in the Amazon rainforest.  这位著名的鞘翅目昆虫学家在亚马逊雨林中发现了一种新的甲虫。|近义词：entomologist|反义词:|词性:noun
