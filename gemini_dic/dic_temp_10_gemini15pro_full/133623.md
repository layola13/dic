nonforeclosing:/ˌnɑːnfɔːrˈkloʊzɪŋ/
adjective
非取消抵押赎回权的
Not involving or leading to the foreclosure of a mortgage.  This describes a situation where a lender chooses not to seize and sell a property even if the borrower is in default.
The bank offered a nonforeclosing loan modification program to struggling homeowners.  银行向处境艰难的房主提供了一个非取消抵押赎回权的贷款修改计划。
The nonforeclosing solution allowed the family to stay in their home. 这个非取消抵押赎回权的解决方案使这家人得以留在自己的家中。
Synonyms: lenient, forgiving, accommodating
Antonyms: foreclosing, repossessing, seizing
