altaic:/ælˈteɪɪk/
adjective|阿尔泰语系的|Relating to or denoting the Altaic language family, a disputed grouping of Turkic, Mongolic, and Tungusic languages, sometimes also including Japanese and Korean.|The altaic theory suggests a common ancestor for these languages.|近义词: Turkic, Mongolic, Tungusic (when referring to specific branches).|反义词:  (No clear antonym, possibly Indo-European if contrasting language families).|词性: adjective
