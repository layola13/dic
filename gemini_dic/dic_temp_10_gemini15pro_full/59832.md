ectodermal:/ˌɛktəˈdɜːrməl/ | adj. | 外胚层的 | Relating to or derived from the ectoderm, the outermost of the three primary germ layers of an embryo. |  The ectodermal cells differentiate to form the nervous system, epidermis, and sensory organs. 外胚层细胞分化形成神经系统、表皮和感觉器官。|  Ectodermal dysplasia is a group of conditions in which there are abnormalities of two or more ectodermal structures such as the hair, teeth, nails, sweat glands, and skin. 外胚层发育不良是一组疾病，其中两个或多个外胚层结构（如头发、牙齿、指甲、汗腺和皮肤）出现异常。| synonymous:  epiblastic | antonymous: endodermal, mesodermal | adjective
