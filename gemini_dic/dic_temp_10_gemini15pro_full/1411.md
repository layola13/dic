accustomary: /əˈkʌstəməri/ | adj. | 习惯的，惯常的 | Usual or traditional; in keeping with the accepted customs. | It is customary to offer a handshake when meeting someone for the first time.  初次见面握手是一种习惯。

It's customary for the bride's father to walk her down the aisle.  新娘的父亲陪她走过红毯是习俗。

| 近义词: usual, habitual, traditional, conventional | 反义词: unusual, unconventional, atypical, rare | 词性: 形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
It is customary to remove your shoes before entering a Japanese home. =  进日本人的家之前习惯上要脱鞋。
He makes his customary morning visit to the coffee shop. = 他像往常一样早上去了咖啡店。

一般过去时:
It was customary for women to wear hats to church. = 以前女性习惯戴帽子去做礼拜。
The restaurant offered its customary Thanksgiving dinner. = 这家餐厅提供了他们惯常的感恩节晚餐。

现在完成时:
It has become customary to tip service staff. = 给服务人员小费已经成为一种习惯。
The company has followed its customary procedures for hiring new employees. = 公司遵循了其惯常的招聘新员工的程序。

过去完成时:
It had been customary to send handwritten thank-you notes. =  以前习惯手写感谢信。
He had followed the customary greetings before beginning the meeting. = 他在会议开始前遵循了惯常的问候方式。

将来时:
It will be customary for guests to bring a small gift to the housewarming party. = 客人习惯上会带小礼物来参加乔迁派对。
The company will continue its customary practice of donating to charity. = 公司将继续其惯常的慈善捐赠做法。


虚拟语气:
If it were customary to wear a suit, I would wear one. = 如果习惯上要穿西装，我会穿的。
I wish it were customary to take a longer lunch break. = 我希望习惯上能有更长的午休时间。

被动语态:
It is customary for gifts to be opened in front of the giver. = 礼物习惯上要在送礼者面前打开。
A toast was given, as is customary at weddings. =  按照婚礼的惯例，举行了祝酒仪式。

疑问句:
Is it customary to tip in this country? = 在这个国家给小费是惯例吗？
What is customary behavior at a funeral? = 在葬礼上，通常的行为举止是什么？

否定句:
It is not customary to arrive late for a business meeting. = 开商务会议迟到是不习惯的。
It's not customary to talk loudly in libraries. = 在图书馆大声说话是不习惯的。
