half-reasoning:/ˌhæfˈriːzənɪŋ/ | adj. | 半推理的；不完全推理的 | Based on incomplete or flawed reasoning; not fully logical or rational. |  He presented a half-reasoning argument that didn't convince anyone. 他提出了一个不完全推理的论点，没能说服任何人。

His half-reasoning explanation left me with more questions than answers. 他半推理的解释让我产生了更多疑问，而不是答案。| 近义词: illogical, irrational, faulty, unsound | 反义词: logical, rational, sound, valid | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: "half-" (Old English *healf) + "reasoning" (from "reason" - Old French raison - Latin ratio).
