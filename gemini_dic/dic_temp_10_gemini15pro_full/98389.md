insolently: /ˈɪnsələntli/ | adv. | 傲慢地，无礼地 |  In a rude and disrespectful manner, showing a lack of respect for someone or something. | He insolently ignored his teacher's instructions. 他傲慢地无视了老师的指示。|  She insolently tossed her hair and walked away. 她傲慢地甩了甩头发，走开了。|  rudely, disrespectfully, contemptuously, arrogantly, impertinently | respectfully, politely, humbly, submissively | adverb

Etymology: From Late Latin *insolenter, from Latin insolens ("unusual, arrogant"), from in- ("not") + solens ("being accustomed"), present participle of solēre ("to be accustomed").
