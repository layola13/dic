misconstruer:/ˌmɪskənˈstruːər/| n.|误解者，曲解者|Someone who misconstrues something; someone who interprets (something) incorrectly.|He was a misconstruer of good intentions, always suspecting ulterior motives. 他总是误解别人的好意，总是怀疑别人别有用心。|近义词：misinterpreter, distorter|反义词: interpreter, elucidator|词性:noun

misconstrue:/ˌmɪskənˈstruː/| vt.|误解，曲解，错误地解释|To interpret (something, such as a word or action) wrongly.|She misconstrued his silence as anger. 她把他的沉默误解为生气。|近义词：misinterpret, misapprehend, distort|反义词: understand, comprehend, interpret correctly|词性:verb
