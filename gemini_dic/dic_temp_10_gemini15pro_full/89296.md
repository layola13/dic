homoeopath:/ˌhəʊmiˈɒpæθ/ (UK), /ˌhoʊmiˈæpæθ/ (US)| n.|顺势疗法医师; 顺势治疗师|A practitioner of homeopathy, a system of alternative medicine.|例句：The homoeopath prescribed her a remedy for her allergies. 顺势疗法医师给她开了治疗过敏的药方。

例句：She consulted a homoeopath for her chronic headaches. 她咨询了一位顺势疗法医师治疗她的慢性头痛。

近义词：homeopathist|反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps) conventional doctor, allopathic physician|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From homoeopathy + -path.  Homoeopathy comes from Greek homoios  "similar" + -pathy  "suffering" or "feeling."  The suffix -path denotes "one who practices."
