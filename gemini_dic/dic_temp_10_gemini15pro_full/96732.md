inelastic:/ˌɪnɪˈlæstɪk/ | adj. | 无弹性的，缺乏弹性的 | Not elastic; lacking flexibility or resilience.  |  The demand for gasoline is relatively inelastic. 汽油的需求相对缺乏弹性。| synonyms: inflexible, rigid, unyielding | antonyms: elastic, flexible, resilient | adjective

inelastically:/ˌɪnɪˈlæstɪkli/ | adv. | 无弹性地，缺乏弹性地 | In an inelastic manner; without flexibility or resilience. | The ball bounced inelastically off the wall.  球毫无弹性地从墙上弹了回来。| synonyms: rigidly, inflexibly | antonyms: elastically, flexibly | adverb
