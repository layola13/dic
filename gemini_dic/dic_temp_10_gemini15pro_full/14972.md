baalite:/ˈbeɪˌlaɪt/| n. |贝岩；硅藻土|A sedimentary rock formed from diatomaceous earth. It is light-colored, porous, and friable.  It is primarily composed of silica.|The filter used diatomaceous earth, also known as baalite, to purify the water.|近义词：diatomite, kieselguhr, diatomaceous earth|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the locality Baia de Todos os Santos (All Saints Bay) in Brazil, where it was first described.
