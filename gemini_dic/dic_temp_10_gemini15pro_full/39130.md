confusedly:/kənˈfjuːzɪdli/| adv.|困惑地，茫然地|In a way that shows someone is confused or unable to think clearly. |She looked at him confusedly, unsure of what he meant. 她困惑地看着他，不确定他是什么意思。|例句：He stammered confusedly, trying to explain the situation. 他结结巴巴地、困惑地试图解释情况。|近义词：bewilderedly, uncertainly, perplexedly|反义词：clearly, coherently, understandably|词性：副词
