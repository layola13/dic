overthoughtful: /ˌoʊvərˈθɔːtfəl/ | adj. | 想太多的，过度思虑的 | Excessively or unnecessarily thoughtful; given to excessive thinking and worrying, often about unimportant matters. | She was so overthoughtful about the interview that she couldn't sleep the night before. 她对面试想得太多，以至于前一天晚上都睡不着。|  He's overthoughtful and tends to analyze every possible outcome. 他想得太多，总是分析每一个可能的结果。 | 近义词: anxious, apprehensive, neurotic | 反义词: carefree, relaxed, untroubled | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "thoughtful" (adjective meaning "showing consideration for the needs of other people").  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning of "thoughtful," implying an excessive degree of thinking.
