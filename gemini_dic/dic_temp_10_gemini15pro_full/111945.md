lolloped:/ˈlɒləpt/ (UK) /ˈlɑːləpt/ (US)| v. |慢跑，笨拙地奔跑|To move in a clumsy, rolling way, typically with a bouncing or swaying motion.|The dog lolloped happily across the field. 狗欢快地在田野里慢跑。

The bear lolloped toward the picnickers, much to their alarm. 那只熊笨拙地朝野餐的人跑去，把他们吓坏了。|近义词：lumber, bound, gallop, canter|反义词：scamper, dart, sprint|词性:动词

lollop:/ˈlɒləp/ (UK) /ˈlɑːləp/ (US)| n. |笨拙的步态|A clumsy, rolling gait or manner of moving.|The puppy's lollop was endearing. 小狗笨拙的步态很可爱。|近义词：lumber, roll, waddle|反义词：sprint, dash|词性:名词
