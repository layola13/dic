hexagynous:/ˌhɛksəˈdʒaɪnəs/
adjective
六雌蕊的；六心皮的
Having six pistils or styles.
Example: The hexagynous flower had six distinct styles emerging from the ovary.  这种六雌蕊的花有六个明显的从子房伸出的花柱。

Example:  Botanists identified the rare plant as hexagynous due to its six-parted gynoecium.  植物学家根据其六裂的雌蕊群，将这种稀有植物鉴定为六雌蕊的。
synonym: six-styled (less common)
antonym: (No direct antonym,  would need to specify a different number of pistils, e.g., "pentamerous" for five-parted)
词性: adjective
