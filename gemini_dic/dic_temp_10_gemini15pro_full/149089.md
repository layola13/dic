oxanilide:/ˌɒksˈænɪlaɪd/ | n. |草酰替苯胺|A white crystalline organic compound,  N,N'-diphenyloxamide, derived from oxalic acid and aniline. It is used as a fungicide and intermediate in chemical synthesis.|Oxanilide is effective against certain fungal diseases in plants.|近义词: fungicide (broad category)|反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially "growth promoter" in a specific context) | noun
