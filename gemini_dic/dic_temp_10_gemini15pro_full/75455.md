fruitlike:/ˈfruːtlaɪk/
adjective
像水果的；果味的
Resembling or characteristic of fruit.
The candy had a fruitlike flavor.  这种糖果有水果味。
The aroma was sweet and fruitlike.  这香味甜美，像水果一样。
近义词:fruity, fruit-shaped
反义词:bland, savory
词性:adjective
