derailer:/diːˈreɪlər/| n.|脱轨器；出轨者；阻碍者|Something that causes a train or other vehicle to leave its tracks; someone or something that obstructs progress or causes a plan to fail.|The train hit a derailleur placed on the tracks by vandals. 火车撞上了破坏者放在轨道上的脱轨器。|Near synonyms: obstacle, impediment, hindrance, saboteur|Antonym: facilitator|noun

derailer:/diːˈreɪlər/| n.|（自行车）变速器|A mechanism on a bicycle that moves the chain between sprockets to change gears.|The derailleur wasn't working properly, making it difficult to climb hills. 变速器工作不正常，爬坡很困难。|Near synonyms: gear shifter, gear changer|Antonym: N/A|noun
