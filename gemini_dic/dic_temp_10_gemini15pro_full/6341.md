amperian:/æmˈpɪəriən/ |adj.| 安培的，与电流有关的；安培环路的|Relating to electric current or an electric circuit; of or relating to an Amperian loop, a closed loop used in Ampère's circuital law to calculate the magnetic field.| The Amperian loop encloses the current-carrying wire. 安培环路包围着载流导线。|近义词: electromagnetic 电磁的|反义词: None|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From André-Marie Ampère (1775-1836), French physicist and mathematician, + -ian.
