diwali:/dɪˈwɑːliː/ |n.|排灯节; 印度灯节; 万灯节; Diwali; Deepawali|A Hindu festival of lights, held in the period October to November. It is particularly associated with Lakshmi, the goddess of prosperity, and marks the beginning of the financial year in India.|We celebrate Diwali every year. 我们每年都庆祝排灯节。| Diwali is a very important festival for Hindus. 排灯节对印度教徒来说是一个非常重要的节日。|synonyms: Festival of Lights, Deepavali|antonyms: N/A|noun
