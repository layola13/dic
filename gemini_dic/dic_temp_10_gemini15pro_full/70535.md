filibusterism:/ˈfɪlɪˌbʌstərɪzəm/ | n. | 阻挠议事，冗长辩论 | The act of obstructing progress in a legislative assembly by means of long speeches or other delaying tactics. |  The senator's filibusterism prevented the bill from coming to a vote.  参议员的冗长辩论阻止了该法案进行投票。| synonyms: obstructionism, delaying tactics, procrastination | antonyms: cooperation, expedition, facilitation | noun

filibusterism:/ˈfɪlɪˌbʌstərɪzəm/ | n. | 故意拖延 | Deliberate delaying or obstruction of legislation. |  The opposition party engaged in filibusterism to stall the passage of the new law. 反对党采取了阻挠策略来拖延新法律的通过。| synonyms: stonewalling, foot-dragging | antonyms:  cooperation, support | noun
