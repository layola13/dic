hamletish:/hæmˈlɛtɪʃ/ | adj. | 哈姆雷特式的；优柔寡断的；忧郁的 | Resembling or suggestive of Hamlet, especially in being indecisive, melancholy, or introspective. | He gave a hamletish sigh and contemplated his options. 他哈姆雷特式地叹了口气，思考着自己的选择。 |  Example:  She had a hamletish moment, unable to decide whether to stay or go. 她犹豫不决，不知道是走还是留。|Synonyms:  melancholy, brooding, introspective, contemplative, indecisive | Antonyms: decisive, resolute, cheerful, extroverted | word type: adjective
