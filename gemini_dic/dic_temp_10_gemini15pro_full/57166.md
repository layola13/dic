dowdyisms:/ˈdaʊdiɪzəmz/
|n.|过时的衣着或行为；古板作风|The dowdyisms of her wardrobe made her look much older than she was. 她的衣着老土，使她看起来比实际年龄老得多。|例句：Her dowdyisms made her stand out in the fashionable crowd. 她的老土打扮让她在时尚人群中显得格格不入。

例句：He criticized the dowdyisms of the older generation. 他批评老一辈的古板作风。
|近义词：old-fashionedness, frumpiness, shabbiness|反义词：stylishness, trendiness, chic|词性：名词
