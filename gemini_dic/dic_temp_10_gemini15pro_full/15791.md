balize:/ˈbælɪz/ /bəˈliːz/ | n. |航标，信标，界标 | A seamark or buoy, or other object, serving to point out the position of a channel, shoal, rock, etc., and to guide vessels in their course. | The balize marked the entrance to the harbor.  航标标记着港口的入口。| Synonym: beacon, buoy, marker | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "obscurity") | noun

balize:/ˈbælɪz/ /bəˈliːz/ | vt. |设置航标，标示 | To mark with a balize.  | They balized the channel to prevent accidents. 他们为航道设置了航标以防止事故发生。| Synonym: mark, signal, indicate | Antonym:  conceal, hide | verb
