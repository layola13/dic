cabinet:/ˈkæbɪnɪt/| n.|橱柜；内阁；展览柜|A cabinet is a piece of furniture with shelves, cupboards, or drawers, used for storing or displaying things.  A cabinet is also a group of ministers who advise the head of state.|例句：The china was displayed in a glass cabinet.  瓷器陈列在一个玻璃柜里。

例句：The Prime Minister and his cabinet have resigned. 首相和他的内阁已经辞职。|近义词：cupboard, closet, council, ministry|反义词: (No direct antonym.  Could consider "open shelves" for the furniture meaning)|noun
