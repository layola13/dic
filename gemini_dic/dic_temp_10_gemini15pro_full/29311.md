caster:/ˈkæstər/ (US), /ˈkɑːstər/ (UK)| n.|脚轮；施法者；投掷者|A person or thing that casts something.  A small wheel attached to the bottom of furniture or equipment so that it can be moved easily.|The caster on the chair is broken. 椅子上的脚轮坏了.|Synonyms: roller, wheel, spinner, thrower, hurler (for throwing), sorcerer, magician, wizard (for magic user)|Antonyms: receiver, catcher (for throwing)|noun

caster:/ˈkæstər/ (US), /ˈkɑːstər/ (UK)| n.|调味瓶; (尤指)糖罐|A small container with a perforated top for sprinkling sugar, pepper, etc., on food.|Could you please pass the sugar caster?  请把糖罐递给我好吗？|Synonyms: shaker, sprinkler, dispenser|Antonyms: N/A|noun
