hemipterous:/ˌhɛmɪpˈtɛrəs/| adj.|半翅目的|Relating to or belonging to the Hemiptera, an order of insects characterized by piercing and sucking mouthparts, including bugs, aphids, and cicadas. Hemipterans are known for their sucking mouthparts.  半翅目昆虫以其吸吮口器而闻名。

词源：hemi- (half) + -ptera (wing)  来自希腊语，hemi- 表示“一半”，ptera 表示“翅膀”，指的是前翅部分革质化的特征。
