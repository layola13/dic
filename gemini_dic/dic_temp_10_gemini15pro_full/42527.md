countermandable:/ˌkaʊntərˈmændəbəl/| adj.|可撤销的，可取消的|Able to be countermanded; revocable.|The general's orders, though initially firm, proved countermandable when new intelligence arrived. 将军的命令虽然最初很坚定，但在新的情报到达后证明是可以撤销的。|Synonyms: revocable, rescindable, repealable, cancellable|Antonyms: irreversible, irrevocable, unalterable|adjective

countermand:/ˌkaʊntərˈmænd/| v. n.|撤销，取消；相反的命令|To revoke or cancel (a command, order, etc.); an order revoking a previous one.|Headquarters countermanded the attack. 总部取消了进攻。|Synonyms: revoke, rescind, repeal, cancel, retract|Antonyms: order, command, authorize, ratify|verb, noun
