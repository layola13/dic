greenflies:/ˈɡriːnflaɪz/| n. |蚜虫；绿蝇|Small, green insects that are pests of plants, feeding on sap. They reproduce rapidly and can cause significant damage to crops.|例句：The roses are covered in greenflies. 玫瑰花上爬满了蚜虫。

例句：Greenflies are a common garden pest. 蚜虫是常见的园林害虫。|近义词：aphids|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 beneficial insects 益虫)|词性:noun
