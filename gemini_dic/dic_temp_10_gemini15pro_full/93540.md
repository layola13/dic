imaginary:/ɪˈmædʒɪˌnɛri/ | adj. | 想象的，虚构的 | existing only in the imagination; not real |例句：Dragons are imaginary creatures. 龙是虚构的生物。

例句：She has an imaginary friend named Lily. 她有一个名叫莉莉的假想朋友。

近义词：fictional, unreal, hypothetical, fanciful | 反义词：real, actual, factual, true | 词性: adjective

imaginary:/ɪˈmædʒɪˌnɛri/ | n. | 虚数 | a complex number that can be written as a real number multiplied by the imaginary unit i | 例句：Mathematicians use imaginary numbers in complex calculations. 数学家在复杂的计算中使用虚数。

例句：The square root of -1 is an imaginary number. -1的平方根是一个虚数。| 近义词：(for n.) complex number | 反义词：(for n.) real number | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *imaginarius (“pertaining to images”), from Latin imaginari (“to imagine, form an image of”), from imago (“image, likeness”).
