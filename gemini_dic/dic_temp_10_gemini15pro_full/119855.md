mileometer:/ˌmaɪˈlɒmɪtər/ /ˌmaɪˈlɑːmɪtər/
noun
里程表
An instrument in a vehicle that measures the distance travelled.
The mileometer showed we had travelled over 500 miles. 里程表显示我们已经行驶了超过500英里。
The mileometer on my car is broken. 我的车上的里程表坏了。
