impair:/ɪmˈpeər/| vt.|损害，削弱|To weaken or damage something, especially a faculty or function.|Alcohol impairs your judgment. 酒精会损害你的判断力。|The accident impaired his vision.  事故损害了他的视力。|近义词：damage, harm, weaken, undermine|反义词:improve, enhance, strengthen|verb

impairment:/ɪmˈpeər.mənt/| n.|损伤，缺陷|The state of being weakened or damaged.|He suffers from a hearing impairment. 他患有听力障碍。|Cognitive impairment can be caused by various factors. 认知障碍可能由多种因素引起。|近义词：damage, harm, weakness, deterioration|反义词:improvement, enhancement, strengthening|noun
