free-up:/friː ˈʌp/| phrasal verb | 释放，腾出，空出 | To make something available by removing something else; to release someone from a commitment. | I need to free up some space on my hard drive. 我需要释放一些硬盘空间。

I need to free up some time this weekend to finish the project.  这周末我需要腾出一些时间来完成这个项目。

| 近义词: release, liberate, clear, empty | 反义词: occupy, fill, clog | verb phrase
