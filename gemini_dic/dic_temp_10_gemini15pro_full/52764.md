dilates:/daɪˈleɪts/ /dɪˈleɪts/
verb
(使)扩大，(使)膨胀
to become wider or larger; to make something wider or larger
The pupils of the eyes dilate in the dark.  瞳孔在黑暗中会放大。
The surgeon dilated the artery to allow more blood to flow.  外科医生扩张了动脉以便让更多的血液流动。
近义词:expand, widen, enlarge
反义词:contract, constrict, shrink
词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dīlātāre, frequentative of Latin dīlātare (“to spread out, extend”), from dī- (“apart, asunder, in different directions”) + lātus (“wide, broad”).
