adaptationally:/ˌædæpˈteɪʃənəli/| adv.| 适应地，顺应地 | In a way that relates to adaptation or adapting; in a way that is adjusted to suit different conditions or purposes. | Organisms evolve adaptationally to their environments. 生物会适应性地进化以适应环境。

The company adaptationally changed its marketing strategy to target a younger demographic.  这家公司适应性地改变了营销策略，以针对更年轻的人群。 | 近义词: adaptively, flexibly, responsively | 反义词: inflexibly, rigidly | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From adaptation + -ally. Adaptation from adapt (v.) + -ation. Adapt from Latin adaptare "to fit, adjust," from ad "to" + aptare "to fit, fasten."

USAGE->
This word is less common than "adaptively."  While both essentially mean "in a way that adapts," "adaptively" is generally preferred.  Here are some examples demonstrating its usage in different contexts:

一般现在时:
He adaptationally modifies his teaching style to suit each student's learning needs. = 他根据每个学生的学习需求适应性地调整教学风格。
The chameleon adaptationally changes its color to blend in with its surroundings. = 变色龙适应性地改变颜色以融入周围环境。


一般过去时:
The company adaptationally restructured its operations during the economic downturn. = 在经济低迷时期，该公司适应性地调整了其运营结构。
They adaptationally altered their plans when the weather took a turn for the worse. = 当天气恶化时，他们适应性地改变了计划。


现在完成时:
Scientists have adaptationally engineered crops to withstand harsh climates. = 科学家们已经适应性地设计了农作物以抵御恶劣气候。
She has adaptationally adjusted her lifestyle to accommodate her new job. = 她已经适应性地调整了她的生活方式以适应她的新工作。


过去完成时:
The animals had adaptationally migrated to warmer regions before the onset of winter. = 在冬季来临之前，这些动物已经适应性地迁移到温暖的地区。
He had adaptationally modified his behavior after receiving feedback from his colleagues. = 在收到同事的反馈后，他已经适应性地改变了他的行为。


将来时:
We will adaptationally adjust our strategy as the situation evolves. = 随着情况的发展，我们将适应性地调整我们的战略。
They will adaptationally implement new technologies to improve efficiency. = 他们将适应性地实施新技术以提高效率。


PHRASE->
While "adaptationally" doesn't have common set phrases, it can be used with adverbs like:

successfully adaptationally = 成功地适应
quickly adaptationally = 快速地适应
effectively adaptationally = 有效地适应
constantly adaptationally = 不断地适应
dynamically adaptationally = 动态地适应
