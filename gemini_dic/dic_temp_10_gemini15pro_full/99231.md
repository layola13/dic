intercombats:/ˌɪntərˈkɒmbæts/ | n. |交战，战斗 | Fights or battles between individuals or groups within a larger conflict. |  The intercombats between the rival factions weakened the kingdom. 王国内部各派系之间的交战削弱了王国的力量。| The constant intercombats depleted their resources.  不断的内部争斗耗尽了他们的资源。| 近义词: infighting, internal strife, internal conflict | 反义词: peace, truce, harmony | 词性: 名词

词源:  "inter-" (prefix: between, among) + "combat" (n. & v.: fight, battle).  "Combat" comes from Old French "combattre," ultimately from Latin "com-" (together) + "battuere" (to beat).
