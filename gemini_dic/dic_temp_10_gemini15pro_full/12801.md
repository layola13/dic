assistant: /əˈsɪstənt/ (US) /əˈsɪstənt/ (UK)| n. adj.|助手，助理；辅助的|A person who ranks below a senior person and helps them in their work, or a device that helps someone do something. |She works as a teaching assistant. 她担任助教。|近义词：helper, aide, deputy, subordinate|反义词：superior, boss, director|noun

assistant: /əˈsɪstənt/ (US) /əˈsɪstənt/ (UK)| adj.|辅助的，助理的|Giving help or support, especially to a more senior person.|He is an assistant manager. 他是助理经理。|近义词：helping, supporting, auxiliary|反义词：chief, main, primary|adjective
