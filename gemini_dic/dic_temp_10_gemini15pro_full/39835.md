conspirer:/kənˈspaɪərər/| n.|同谋者，阴谋家|A person who takes part in a conspiracy; a plotter.|例句：The conspirers were arrested before they could carry out their plan. 阴谋者在实施计划之前就被逮捕了。

例句：The conspirers met in secret to discuss their plot. 阴谋分子秘密会面讨论他们的阴谋。|近义词：plotter, schemer, collaborator|反义词: loyalist, opponent|词性:noun


Etymology:
From Late Middle English conspiratour, from Anglo-Norman conspirour, Old French conspirator, from Latin cōnspirātor (“one who breathes together, plotter”), from cōnspirō (“I conspire”), from con- (“together”) + spīrō (“I breathe”).
