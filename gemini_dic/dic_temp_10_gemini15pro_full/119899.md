militarized:/ˈmɪlɪtəraɪzd/| adj.|军事化的|Equipped with military forces and equipment; adapted for military purposes.  Having a military character or purpose.|例句：The border region has become increasingly militarized. 边境地区日益军事化。

例句：The government militarized the police force. 政府将警察部队军事化。|近义词：armed, fortified, weaponized|反义词：demilitarized, civilianized|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From militarize + -ed.  Militarize comes from military + -ize. "Military" ultimately derives from Latin "militaris" (of a soldier), from "miles" (soldier).
