no-win:/ˌnoʊ ˈwɪn/ | adj. | 无胜算的，注定失败的 | A no-win situation is one in which whatever you do, the outcome will be negative or undesirable. |  This project is a no-win situation; we're damned if we do and damned if we don't.  这个项目注定要失败；我们做也失败，不做也失败。 | Synonyms: hopeless, unwinnable, doomed, impossible | Antonyms: winnable, promising, advantageous | adjective
