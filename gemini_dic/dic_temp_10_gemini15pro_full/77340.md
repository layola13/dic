gaucho:/ˈɡaʊtʃoʊ/ (US), /ˈɡaʊtʃəʊ/ (UK)| n.|南美牛仔，牧人|A skilled horseman, adept with the lasso and boleadoras, typically of mixed Spanish and Indian descent, who traditionally herded cattle on the pampas of Argentina, Uruguay, and southern Brazil.|例句：The gaucho skillfully herded the cattle across the pampas.  南美牛仔熟练地把牛群赶过潘帕斯草原。

例句：Gauchos are known for their horsemanship and distinctive clothing.  南美牛仔以其骑术和独特的服装而闻名。|近义词：cowboy, herdsman, stockman|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *gaucho*, possibly from Quechua *wakcha* meaning "orphan" or "poor person".
