|n.|未恢复，未复职|The nonreinstatements of the fired workers led to a protest. 被解雇的工人未被复职导致了抗议。|
|英文解析: The plural form of nonreinstatement, referring to instances where something or someone is not restored to a previous position or state.  It's formed by combining the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") with "reinstatement" (the act of restoring someone or something to a previous position or state).|
|例句：The nonreinstatements of the teachers after the strike caused further disruption. 罢工后教师们未被复职，造成了进一步的混乱。|
|例句：The insurance company's nonreinstatements of the policies left many customers vulnerable. 保险公司未恢复保单，使许多客户处于危险之中。|
