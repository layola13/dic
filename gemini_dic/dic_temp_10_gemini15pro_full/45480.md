curbstone:/ˈkɜːrbstoʊn/ (US) /ˈkɜːbstəʊn/ (UK)| n. |路缘石，马路牙子|A stone or concrete edging along a street or sidewalk, separating it from the roadway. |例句：She tripped over the curbstone and fell. 她被路缘石绊倒了。

例句：The car parked with two wheels up on the curbstone. 汽车停着，两个轮子压在路缘石上。


近义词：kerbstone (chiefly British), curb, edging|反义词:|词性:noun
