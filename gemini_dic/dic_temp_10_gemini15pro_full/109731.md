leventhal:/ˈlɛvənˌθɔːl/ | adj. n. | 莱文塔尔的，莱文塔尔 |  Referring to Howard Leventhal, a health psychologist known for his work on the Common Sense Model of Self-Regulation (CSM) or Leventhal's Self-Regulation Model. This model describes how individuals understand and manage their health and illness.  It can also be a surname. |  Leventhal's research has significantly impacted health communication.  Leventhal's model emphasizes the individual's interpretation of their illness. | 近义词: (For the model) self-regulatory,  (For the person) N/A | 反义词: N/A | 词性: Primarily adjective, can be used as a proper noun (surname).
