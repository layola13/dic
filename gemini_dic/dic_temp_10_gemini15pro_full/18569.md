benevolent:/bəˈnevələnt/ | adj. | 仁慈的，慈善的，乐善好施的 | Well meaning and kindly. |  She had a benevolent smile on her face. 她脸上露出了慈祥的笑容。

The benevolent king helped his people. 仁慈的国王帮助了他的人民。

| 近义词：kind, compassionate, charitable, humane, altruistic | 反义词：malevolent, cruel, unkind, selfish | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *benevolens*, present participle of *benevolēre* "wish well," from *bene* "well" + *volēre*  "to wish."
