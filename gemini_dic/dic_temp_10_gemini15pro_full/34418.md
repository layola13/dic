claudent:/ˈklɔːdənt/ | adj. |封闭的，幽闭的| Enclosed, confined, shut in.  Characterized by a sense of being closed in or restricted.  Relating to or characterized by claustrophobia. |  例句：The claudent atmosphere of the small room made her feel anxious. 小房间封闭的氛围让她感到焦虑。

例句：He found the claudent environment of the submarine oppressive. 他觉得潜艇里幽闭的环境令人压抑。 | 近义词：confined, restricted, cramped, closed-in, stuffy, suffocating | 反义词：open, spacious, airy, expansive | 词性：形容词
