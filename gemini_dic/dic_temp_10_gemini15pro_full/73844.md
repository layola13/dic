foremost:/ˈfɔːrməʊst/ (US) /ˈfɔːrmoʊst/| adj.| 最重要的；最著名的；首要的；最前面的 | Situated or placed first.  A person who is foremost in a particular field or group is the most important or best person in it. | Marie Curie was one of the foremost scientists of her time.  玛丽·居里是她那个时代最重要的科学家之一。| leading, primary, chief, principal, paramount | secondary, last, hindmost | adjective
