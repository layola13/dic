hypothesizing:/haɪˈpɑːθəsaɪzɪŋ/
|v.|假设，假定|forming a hypothesis; making an educated guess|Scientists are hypothesizing that the new drug may have serious side effects. 科学家们假设这种新药可能有严重的副作用。|Researchers are hypothesizing a link between diet and certain cancers. 研究人员假设饮食和某些癌症之间存在联系。|supposing, theorizing, conjecturing, speculating, positing|proving, demonstrating, confirming
|verb|

hypothesize:/haɪˈpɑːθəsaɪz/
|v.|假设，假定|to put something forward as a hypothesis|They hypothesized that this might be the cause of the problem. 他们假设这可能是问题的原因。|It has been hypothesized that the virus originated in birds. 据推测，该病毒起源于鸟类。|suppose, theorize, conjecture, speculate, posit|prove, demonstrate, confirm
|verb|

hypothesis:/haɪˈpɑːθəsɪs/
|n.|假设|a supposition or proposed explanation made on the basis of limited evidence as a starting point for further investigation.|Several hypotheses for global warming have been suggested. 已经提出了关于全球变暖的几种假设。|One hypothesis is that the universe is constantly expanding. 一种假设是宇宙在不断膨胀。|theory, supposition, conjecture, speculation, assumption|proof, fact, reality
|noun|
