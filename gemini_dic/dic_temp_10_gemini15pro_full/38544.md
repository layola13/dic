concluder:/kənˈkluːdər/
|n.|总结者；结论者；结束语|A person or thing that concludes something; a concluding statement or section.|The concluder summarized the main points of the discussion. 总结者总结了讨论的要点。|Synonyms: finalizer, summarizer|Antonyms: initiator, beginner|noun


concluder:/kənˈkluːdər/
|n.|结束语；结论部分|The concluding part of something, especially a speech or piece of writing.|The concluder of the essay restated the thesis. 文章的结尾重申了论点。|Synonyms: conclusion, ending|Antonyms: introduction, beginning|noun
