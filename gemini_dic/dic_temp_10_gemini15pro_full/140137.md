off-top-of-my-head:/ˌɒf ðə ˈtɒp əv maɪ ˈhed/ |adv.|不假思索地，即兴地|Without careful thought or investigation; spontaneously; from memory. | "I don't know the exact figures off the top of my head, but I can look them up." 我不记得确切的数字，但我可以查一下。|extemporaneously, impromptu, spontaneously|prepared, planned|副词
ETYMOLOGY->
This idiom alludes to pulling information directly from one's head, as if it were readily available there.  It suggests a spontaneous and unpremeditated response, without consulting notes or other resources.
