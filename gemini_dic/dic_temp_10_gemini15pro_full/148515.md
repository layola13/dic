oversufficient:/ˌoʊvər səˈfɪʃənt/| adj.|过多的，过剩的，过分的|More than sufficient; excessive.|We had oversufficient food for the party, and a lot was wasted.  我们为聚会准备了过多的食物，很多都被浪费了。|例句：The charity received an oversufficient amount of donations.  该慈善机构收到了过多的捐款。|近义词：excessive, surplus, redundant, superfluous|反义词：insufficient, inadequate, deficient|词性: adjective

词源:
From over + sufficient.  "Over" meaning "excessively" and "sufficient" meaning "enough".  Therefore, "oversufficient" literally means "more than enough".
