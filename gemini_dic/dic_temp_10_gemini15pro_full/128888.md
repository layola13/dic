NIMBY:/ˈnɪmbi/| n.|邻避症患者；反对在本社区建造任何新设施的人|A person who objects to the siting of something perceived as unpleasant or potentially dangerous in their own neighborhood, such as a landfill or hazardous waste facility, especially while raising no such objections to similar developments elsewhere.|"The NIMBYs protested the construction of the new power plant." 邻避症患者抗议建造新的发电厂。|近义词:localist, parochialist|反义词:yimby|名词

Etymology:  Acronym of "not in my backyard."  First recorded in 1980–85.
