edgeless:/ˈɛdʒləs/
adj.
无边的，无限的；没有棱角的；钝的
lacking a sharp edge; blunt; boundless; unlimited
The artist created an edgeless sculpture, smooth and flowing in form. 艺术家创作了一个无边雕塑，造型流畅光滑。
The vast, edgeless desert stretched before them. 广阔无垠的沙漠展现在他们面前。

近义词:boundless, limitless, infinite, smooth, blunt
反义词:sharp, edged, finite, limited, bounded
词性:形容词
