dvorak: /ˈdvɔːræk/ (US), /ˈdvɔːrɑːk/ (UK)| n. |德沃夏克(键盘布局), 德沃夏克(人名) |A keyboard layout designed to be more efficient than QWERTY. Also refers to the composer Antonín Dvořák. |  I switched to the Dvorak keyboard layout to improve my typing speed. 我换成了德沃夏克键盘布局以提高我的打字速度。|  He is a big fan of Antonín Dvořák's music. 他是安东宁·德沃夏克音乐的忠实粉丝。|Synonyms: (for keyboard layout)  Colemak, Workman|Antonyms: (for keyboard layout) QWERTY|noun


词源:  Named after August Dvorak, one of its inventors.  For the composer, it's his surname. 源于其发明者之一奥古斯特·德沃夏克的名字。对于作曲家来说，这是他的姓氏。
