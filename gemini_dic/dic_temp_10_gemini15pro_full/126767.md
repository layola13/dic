nannying:/ˈnæniɪŋ/
n.
临时照顾（小孩）
The act of taking care of children as a nanny.
She found nannying rewarding but tiring. 她发现当保姆很有意义，但也很累。
childcare, babysitting
---
nannying:/ˈnæniɪŋ/
v.
当保姆; 照顾（小孩）
Working as a nanny; taking care of children.
She's nannying for a family with three young children.  她正在一个有三小孩的家庭做保姆。
babysit, childcare
