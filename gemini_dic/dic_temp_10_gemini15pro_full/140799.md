on-bench:/ɒnˈbɛntʃ/ | adj. | 在替补席上; 未上场的 |  Relating to or characteristic of a substitute player, especially in sports.  A player who is on the bench is not actively participating in the game but is available to be substituted in. | The on-bench players were cheering their teammates. 替补队员们正在为他们的队友加油。 | The coach kept him on-bench for the entire game. 教练让他整场比赛都坐在替补席上。 | 近义词: benched, sidelined, substitute, reserve | 反义词: starting, active, playing | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
"on" (Old English "on," preposition indicating position) + "bench" (Old English "benc," meaning a long seat). The compound "on-bench" describes the state of being positioned on the bench, specifically in a sporting context where it implies being a substitute.
