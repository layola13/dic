additively:/ˈædɪtɪvli/
adverb
加法地；添加地
In a way that involves adding something.
The ingredients are combined additively. = 这些成分是加法混合的。
Additively manufactured parts are becoming increasingly common. = 增材制造的零件正变得越来越普遍。

近义词: cumulatively, by addition
反义词: subtractively
词性: adverb


USAGE->
Additively is primarily used as an adverb to describe actions or processes involving addition. It doesn't have complex grammatical variations like verb tenses.

Here are some example sentences demonstrating its usage in different contexts:

The values were calculated additively, not multiplicatively. = 这些值是加法计算的，而不是乘法计算的。
The colors were mixed additively, resulting in a brighter hue. = 这些颜色是加法混合的，产生了一种更明亮的色调。


PHRASE->
While "additively" itself doesn't form common phrases, it can be used in conjunction with other words to create descriptive phrases:

additively combined = 加法组合的
additively manufactured = 增材制造的
additively colored = 加法着色的
additively processed = 加法处理的
additively blended = 加法混合的
