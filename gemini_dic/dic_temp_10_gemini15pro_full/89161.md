homeling:/ˈhoʊmlɪŋ/| n.|归巢; 归巢本能; 归巢的动物;|A homing instinct or the process of returning home, especially of animals. The act of returning to one's home or place of origin.  Also refers to an animal that exhibits this behavior.|The pigeons showed strong homing abilities. 这些鸽子展现出很强的归巢能力。|The salmon's homing instinct is remarkable. 鲑鱼的归巢本能非常神奇。|synonyms:  return, homecoming|antonyms:  departure, emigration|noun
