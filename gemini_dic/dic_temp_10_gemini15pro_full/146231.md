overexposed:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspoʊzd/ | adj. | 过度曝光的；过度暴露的；过分宣传的 |  Having been exposed to too much light, resulting in a washed-out photograph or film image. Also, figuratively, having been publicized excessively. | The photograph was overexposed and the details were lost. 这张照片过度曝光，细节都丢失了。| synonyms: overblown, oversaturated, over publicized | antonyms: underexposed, understated, obscure | adjective
