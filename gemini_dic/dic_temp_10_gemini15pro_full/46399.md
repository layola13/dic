daewoo:/ˈdeɪ.wuː/| n.|大宇|A South Korean conglomerate (chaebol) that existed from 1967 to 2000, producing automobiles, electronics, and heavy industry products.  It went bankrupt due to the Asian financial crisis. |Example: My first car was a Daewoo Lanos. 我的第一辆车是大宇蓝鸟。|Synonym: conglomerate, corporation, chaebol|Antonym: None (Daewoo is a proper noun referring to a specific company, so it doesn't have a direct opposite.)|noun
