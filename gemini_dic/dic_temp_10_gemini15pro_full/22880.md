botchedly:/ˈbɑːtʃɪdli/ /ˈbɒtʃɪdli/| adv.|笨拙地；拙劣地；粗制滥造地|in a botched manner; badly; clumsily|The surgery was botchedly performed, leading to complications.  手术做得拙劣，导致了并发症。|The painting was botchedly done and looked like a child's work. 这幅画画得很糟糕，看起来像小孩子画的。|近义词：clumsily, awkwardly, ineptly, poorly, badly|反义词：skillfully, expertly, competently, adeptly|词性:副词

词源:  From the verb "botch" + "-ed" (past participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Botch" likely comes from a Middle English word meaning "to patch or repair clumsily."
