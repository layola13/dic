gastroscopic:/ˌɡæstrəˈskɒpɪk/ | adj. | 胃镜的；胃镜检查的 | Relating to or involving gastroscopy (examination of the inside of the stomach using a gastroscope). | 例句：The doctor recommended a gastroscopic examination to investigate the patient's stomach pain. 医生建议进行胃镜检查以调查病人的胃痛。

例句：The gastroscopic procedure revealed an ulcer in the stomach lining. 胃镜检查发现胃壁有溃疡。

近义词：endoscopic | 反义词：N/A | 词性：形容词

gastroscopy:/ɡæˈstrɒskəpi/ | n. | 胃镜检查 | Examination of the inside of the stomach using a gastroscope. | 例句：The patient underwent a gastroscopy to determine the cause of his bleeding. 病人接受了胃镜检查以确定出血原因。

例句：Gastroscopy is a common procedure used to diagnose stomach problems. 胃镜检查是诊断胃病的常用方法。

近义词：endoscopy | 反义词：N/A | 词性：名词
