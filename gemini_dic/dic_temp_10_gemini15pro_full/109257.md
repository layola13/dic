USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? = 你怎么想？
What does she want for her birthday? = 她生日想要什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing right now? = 他现在在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she eaten for breakfast? = 她早餐吃了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading all day? = 他一整天都在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say? = 你说什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing yesterday at this time? = 昨天这个时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? = 我看到她的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I called? = 在我打电话之前你做了什么？
What had she told you about the party? = 关于派对，她之前告诉了你什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the rain started? = 雨开始下之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么那么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing next week? = 下周你会做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们将在会议上讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by the end of the day? = 到今天结束时，你将完成什么？
What will they have achieved by next year? = 到明年他们将取得什么成就？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been studying for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经学习五年什么？
What will she have been working on by the time she retires? = 到她退休时，她将一直在做什么？
