calcarate: /ˈkælkəreɪt/ | v. | 踢，用马刺踢 | To kick with a spur or spurs. | The rider calcarated his horse to make it jump the fence. 骑手用马刺踢马，让它跳过栅栏。| Synonym: spur, goad | Antonym: restrain | Word Type: Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *calcāriātus, past participle of calcāriāre (“to furnish with spurs”), from calcārius (“of the heel”), from calx (“heel”).
