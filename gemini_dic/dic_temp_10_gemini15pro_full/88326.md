hippobosca:/ˌhɪpəˈbɒskə/ | n. | 虱蝇 | A louse fly, belonging to the family Hippoboscidae. These flies are blood-sucking parasites of birds and mammals. | Example 1: The ornithologist found a hippobosca on the wing of the captured bird. 鸟类学家在被捕获的鸟的翅膀上发现了一只虱蝇。

Example 2:  Hippobosca are known for their flattened bodies, which allow them to move easily through feathers or fur. 虱蝇以其扁平的身体而闻名，这使它们能够轻松地在羽毛或皮毛中移动。 | Synonym: louse fly, keds | Antonym: N/A | Noun
