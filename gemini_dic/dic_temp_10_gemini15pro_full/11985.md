artfully:/ˈɑːrtfəli/ /ˈɑːrtfʊli/| adv. |巧妙地，狡猾地，精巧地| In a manner that is clever and skillful, especially in a deceitful or cunning way.  Also, in a way that shows artistic skill.|She artfully dodged the question. 她巧妙地回避了这个问题。|synonyms: skillfully, cleverly, ingeniously, craftily, cunningly, subtly|antonyms: clumsily, awkwardly, artlessly, honestly, openly|adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From art + -ful.  "Art" in this context originally referred to skill or craft, not just fine arts.  The suffix "-ful" means "full of" or "characterized by."
