cataracted:/ˈkætəræktɪd/| adj.|患白内障的；瀑布似的|Affected with cataracts; resembling a cataract.|例句：The cataracted eyes of the old dog made it difficult for him to see.  老狗的白内障眼睛使它难以看清东西。

例句：The river flowed cataracted over the rocks.  河水像瀑布一样流过岩石。|近义词：opaque, cloudy (for eyes), cascading (for resembling a waterfall)|反义词：clear, transparent (for eyes)|词性：形容词
