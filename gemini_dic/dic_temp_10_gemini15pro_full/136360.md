非体面；不值得尊敬；不名譽
The state or quality of not being respectable; lack of respectability.  A behavior or characteristic that is considered not socially acceptable or morally upright.
Her family was concerned about her descent into nonrespectability.  她的家庭很担心她堕落到不体面的地步。
