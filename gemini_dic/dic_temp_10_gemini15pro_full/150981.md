paramilitaries:/ˌpærəˈmɪlətɛriz/ | n. |  准军事部队；辅助军事部队 |  A group of civilians organized and operating in a military fashion, often outside the official military structure, but with similar training, equipment, and objectives. | The paramilitaries were accused of human rights abuses.  这些准军事部队被指控侵犯人权。| synonyms: militia, irregulars, vigilantes | antonyms:  regular army, official forces | noun

paramilitary:/ˌpærəˈmɪlətri/ | adj. | 准军事的 | Relating to or characteristic of paramilitaries. | The group adopted a paramilitary structure and tactics.  该组织采取了准军事的结构和战术。| synonyms:  quasi-military | antonyms: civilian, non-military | adjective
