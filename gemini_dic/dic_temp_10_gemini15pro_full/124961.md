muflon:/ˈmʌflən/ (US), /ˈmʌflɒn/ (UK)| n. |野绵羊，欧洲盘羊|A wild sheep (Ovis musimon) native to Corsica and Sardinia, having large, curved horns in the male.  It is thought to be ancestral to domesticated sheep.|例句：The muflon darted across the rocky terrain. 野绵羊飞奔穿过岩石地带。

例句：The zoo's muflon exhibit was popular with visitors. 动物园的欧洲盘羊展览很受游客欢迎。|近义词：sheep, wild sheep, mouflon |反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially domesticated sheep) |词性:noun
