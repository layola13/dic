confidentiality:/ˌkɒnfɪˌdenʃiˈælɪti/ | n. | 机密性，保密性 | the state of keeping or being kept secret or private. |  Doctors are required to maintain the confidentiality of their patients' records. 医生必须对病人的记录保密。 | secrecy, privacy, discretion |  disclosure, openness | noun

confidential:/ˌkɒnfɪˈdenʃl/ | adj. | 机密的，秘密的，保密的 | intended to be kept secret. | She told me in confidential that she was looking for a new job. 她私下告诉我她在找新工作。| secret, private, classified | public, open | adjective
