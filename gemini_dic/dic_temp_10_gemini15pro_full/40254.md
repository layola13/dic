continentals:/ˌkɒntɪˈnentlz/ | /ˌkɑːntɪˈnentlz/ | n. | 大陆人；欧洲大陆人（尤指法国人）；大陆式纸币；大陆式早餐 | people who live on a continent, especially the continent of Europe (and often specifically the French); paper money issued by the Continental Congress during the American Revolution; a type of breakfast common in continental Europe. | The continentals enjoyed their café au lait and croissants. 欧洲大陆人享用着他们的牛奶咖啡和羊角面包。|  The Continental Congress authorized the printing of continentals to finance the war. 大陆会议授权印制大陆币为战争提供资金。 |  We had a continental breakfast at the hotel. 我们在酒店吃了欧式早餐。| Synonyms: Europeans, mainlanders (for people); currency, notes (for money); buffet (for breakfast) | Antonyms: islanders (for people) | Noun

continental:/ˌkɒntɪˈnentl/ | /ˌkɑːntɪˈnentl/ | adj. | 大陆的；欧洲大陆的；大陆性的| relating to or characteristic of a continent, especially the continent of Europe. | They have a continental climate. 他们属于大陆性气候。 | She prefers continental cuisine. 她更喜欢欧洲大陆菜肴。| Synonyms: European, mainland | Antonyms: insular, island | Adjective
