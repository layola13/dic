outearned:/ˌaʊtˈɜːrnd/
verb
超过…的收入
to earn more than someone else
She outearned her husband last year. 去年她的收入超过了她丈夫。
synonyms: outstrip, surpass, exceed
antonyms: underperform, lag behind
词性: 动词
