outbreathed:/ˌaʊtˈbriːðd/
adjective
呼出气的，气息奄奄的
Having breathed out; exhaled.  Also, figuratively, exhausted, spent.
She felt outbreathed after running the marathon. 她跑完马拉松后感到气喘吁吁。
Near synonyms: breathless, exhausted, spent
Antonyms: invigorated, refreshed
词性: adjective
