mesolithic:/ˌmɛzəˈlɪθɪk/ | adj. n. | 中石器时代的；中石器时代 |  Characterized by the appearance of microlithic tools and, in some areas, the beginnings of agriculture. | Mesolithic hunter-gatherers lived in a variety of environments. 中石器时代的狩猎采集者生活在各种各样的环境中。| 近义词: Middle Stone Age | 反义词: Paleolithic, Neolithic | 词性: adjective, noun
