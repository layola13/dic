lecherousnesses:/ˈlɛtʃərəsnəsɪz/
|n.|好色，淫荡|Excessive or offensive sexual desire; lustfulness.  The state of being lecherous.  It refers to an overindulgence in or preoccupation with sexual matters.|His lecherousnesses were well known throughout the town. 他镇上人尽皆知的好色。|近义词：lust, lewdness, lasciviousness, salaciousness|反义词：chastity, purity|词性: noun

词源: From Middle English *lecherousnes*, from Old French *lecheros*, from *lechier* ("to lick"), from Latin *lingere* ("to lick").  The "-ness" suffix transforms the adjective "lecherous" into the noun form "lecherousness."
