coppola:/ˈkɒpələ/ (UK), /ˈkɑːpələ/ (US)| n.| 男式鸭舌帽；一种男式平顶帽 |A flat cap, typically with a small, stiff brim at the front, worn by men.|He wore a brown coppola pulled low over his eyes. 他戴着一顶棕色的鸭舌帽，帽檐拉得很低，遮住了眼睛。|近义词: flat cap, newsboy cap, Gatsby cap|反义词: N/A|名词
