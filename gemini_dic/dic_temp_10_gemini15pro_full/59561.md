ebbed:/ɛbd/
verb
退潮；衰退；减弱
recede, fall away, decline
The tide ebbed. 潮水退了。
The pain slowly ebbed away. 疼痛慢慢消退了。
His enthusiasm ebbed. 他的热情减退了。
recede,wane,diminish
flow,increase,intensify
v.
