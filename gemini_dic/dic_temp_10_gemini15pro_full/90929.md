hurdy-gurdy:/ˈhɜːrdi ˈɡɜːrdi/ /ˈhɝːdi ˈɡɝːdi/| n. |手摇风琴；街头手摇风琴|A stringed musical instrument, similar to a lute or guitar, played by turning a crank that rubs against the strings. The hurdy-gurdy often also has keys that, when depressed, shorten the strings to change the pitch.|The musician played a lively tune on his hurdy-gurdy.|近义词：barrel organ, street organ|反义词: None|词性:noun

hurdy:/ˈhɜːrdi/ /ˈhɝːdi/| adj. |(古语) 粗鲁的，无礼的| (archaic) Rough, rude, or boisterous.|  "He answered her with a hurdy laugh." (This usage is rare.) |近义词：rude, boisterous, rough|反义词: polite, courteous|词性:adjective (archaic)
