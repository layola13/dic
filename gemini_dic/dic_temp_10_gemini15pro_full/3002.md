aeons: /ˈiːɒnz/ (UK) /ˈiːɑːnz/ (US)| n.|亿万年，极漫长的时间|An indefinitely long period of time; an age.|Over aeons, the continents have drifted apart.  亿万年来，大陆漂移开来。

The dinosaurs roamed the earth for aeons. 恐龙在地球上漫游了亿万年。|近义词：ages, centuries, millennia, eons|反义词:instant, moment|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek αἰών (aiṓn, “lifetime, age, eternity”). Doublet of *eon*.

USAGE->
For aeons:

The Earth has been evolving for aeons. = 地球已经进化了亿万年。
This tradition has been practiced for aeons. = 这个传统已经实行了几万年。

Over aeons:
Over aeons, the river carved a deep canyon. = 亿万年来，河流雕刻出了一个深深的峡谷。
Over aeons, the climate has changed dramatically. = 亿万年来，气候发生了巨大的变化。


PHRASE->
for aeons =  亿万年来
over aeons =  经过亿万年
through the aeons =  跨越亿万年
since aeons ago = 自亿万年前以来
lasting for aeons =  持续亿万年
