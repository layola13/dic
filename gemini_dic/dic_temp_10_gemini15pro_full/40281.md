continue:/kənˈtɪnjuː/ (British) /kənˈtɪnjuː/ (American)| v. |继续，持续|To persist in an activity or process.|例句：The rain continued to fall all afternoon. 雨持续下了整个下午。|近义词：persist, proceed, resume, carry on, go on|反义词:stop, cease, halt, discontinue|词性:动词

continue:/kənˈtɪnjuː/ (British) /kənˈtɪnjuː/ (American)| v. |(在中断后)继续，重新开始|To begin again after an interruption.|例句：After a short break, they continued their discussion.  短暂休息后，他们继续讨论。|近义词：resume, recommence|反义词:pause, interrupt|词性:动词
