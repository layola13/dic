allottee:/əˌlɒˈtiː/ /əˌlɑːˈtiː/
n.
受分配人；接受分配者
A person to whom something is allotted.
The allottee is responsible for paying the annual maintenance fee.  受分配人负责支付每年的维护费。
The allottee was pleased with the plot of land they received.  受分配人对他们收到的土地感到满意。
Synonyms: recipient, beneficiary, grantee
Antonyms: allocator, grantor, giver
Noun
