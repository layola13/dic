camellia: /kəˈmiːliə/ | n. | 茶花，山茶花 | An evergreen shrub or small tree of the tea family, native to eastern and southern Asia, having glossy leaves and showy flowers.  |  The camellia is the state flower of Alabama. 茶花是阿拉巴马州的州花。 |  The camellias in the garden are in full bloom. 花园里的茶花盛开了。 | synonyms: tea plant,  | antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Latinized name *Camellia*, named by Linnaeus after Georg Joseph Kamel, a Jesuit botanist.
