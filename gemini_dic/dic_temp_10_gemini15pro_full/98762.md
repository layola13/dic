intail:/ɪnˈteɪl/ | vt. n.|限定继承；限定继承权|To limit the inheritance of (property) to a specified line of heirs, often the eldest male descendant.|The will entails the property to the eldest son. 遗嘱规定将财产传给长子。|近义词:limit, restrict, confer|反义词:broaden, expand|v.

intail:/ɪnˈteɪl/ | n.|限定继承权；限定继承的财产|The restriction of property to a specified line of heirs.|The entail prevented the family from selling the land. 限定继承权阻止了这家人出售土地。|近义词:inheritance, legacy|反义词: - |n.
