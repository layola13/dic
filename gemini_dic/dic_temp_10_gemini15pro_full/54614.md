dismay:/dɪsˈmeɪ/ | v.  n. | 使沮丧，使惊慌；沮丧，惊慌 | To cause (someone) to feel discouraged or disappointed. A feeling of discouragement or disappointment. | The news of the layoffs dismayed the workers. 裁员的消息使工人们感到沮丧。 | 近义词: discourage, disheartened, appall, alarm | 反义词: encourage, hearten, cheer | 词性: 动词, 名词
dismay:/dɪsˈmeɪ/ | n. | 沮丧，惊慌 | A feeling of discouragement or disappointment. | She watched in dismay as her carefully laid plans fell apart. 她沮丧地看着自己精心制定的计划破灭了。 | 近义词: discouragement, disappointment, consternation | 反义词: encouragement, hope, joy | 词性: 名词
