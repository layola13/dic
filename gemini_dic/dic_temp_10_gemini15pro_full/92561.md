hysterically:/hɪˈstɛrɪkli/
adverb
歇斯底里地；异常兴奋地
In a way that shows uncontrolled emotion, especially extreme amusement or sadness.
She laughed hysterically. 她歇斯底里地大笑。
Synonyms: uncontrollably, wildly, frantically, madly
Antonyms: calmly, composedly, quietly
词性: 副词
