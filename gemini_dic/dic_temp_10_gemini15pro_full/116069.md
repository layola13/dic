martini: /mɑːrˈtiːni/ /mɑːrˈtini/ | n. | 马提尼 | An alcoholic cocktail made with gin or vodka and dry vermouth, typically garnished with an olive or a lemon twist. |  I ordered a dry martini with an olive. 我点了一杯加橄榄的干马提尼。

martini: /mɑːrˈtiːni/ /mɑːrˈtini/ | n. | 马提尼酒 |  A type of vermouth. | He prefers his martini with a dash of orange bitters. 他喜欢在他的马提尼酒里加一点橙子苦味酒。


近义词: cocktail, drink
反义词: None

注意事项:
