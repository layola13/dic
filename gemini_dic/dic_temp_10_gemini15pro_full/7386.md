animallike:/ˈænɪməlˌlaɪk/
adjective
像动物的；动物似的
Resembling or characteristic of an animal.
The creature emitted an animallike growl.  这个生物发出像动物一样的咆哮。
His animallike instincts took over. 他的动物本能占据了上风。
近义词:animalistic, bestial
反义词:humanlike, humane
词性:adjective

词源: animal + like.  "animal" comes from Old French animal, ultimately from Latin animalis, meaning "having breath, living being, animal."  "like" comes from Old English gelic, meaning "similar, resembling."
