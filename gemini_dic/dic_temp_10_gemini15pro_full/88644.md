hoa:/ˈhoʊə/ (US) /ˈhəʊə/ (UK)| n.|业主协会;  (Home Owners' Association)|A homeowners' association (HOA) is an organization in a subdivision, planned community, or condominium that makes and enforces rules for the properties and residents.|例句：The HOA sent a letter about the overgrown bushes. 业主协会寄了一封关于灌木丛过长的信。|例句：Our HOA fees cover landscaping and pool maintenance. 我们的业主协会费用包括园林绿化和泳池维护。|近义词：residents' association, community association|反义词:  (No direct antonym)|noun
