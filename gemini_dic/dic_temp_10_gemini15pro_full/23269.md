box's:/ˈbɒksɪz/| n.|盒子的；箱子的|This is the box's lid. 这是盒子的盖子。|例句：The box's contents were a mystery. 箱子里的东西是个谜。

The box's label was torn. 箱子的标签撕破了。|近义词：container's, crate's, chest's|反义词: None (Box's indicates possession, not a quality with an opposite)|词性:Possessive noun (noun with the possessive 's)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English "box" (a container), derived from Late Latin "buxis" (boxwood, from which boxes were often made), ultimately from Greek "pyxos" (boxwood). The possessive 's' is a genitive marker indicating ownership or association.
