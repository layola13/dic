among us:/əˈmʌŋ ʌs/ | prep. phrase |在我们之中；我们之间 |  Indicates presence or inclusion within a group.  It implies being surrounded by or part of a particular group of people or things. | The imposter is among us.  内鬼就在我们之中。 | There's a traitor among us. 我们之中有一个叛徒。 |近义词: amidst, between, within | 反义词: outside, beyond | 词性: 介词短语
