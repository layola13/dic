burgage:/ˈbɜːrɡɪdʒ/| n.|市民租地；城市自由保有地|A form of tenure in English medieval boroughs, by which property was held of the king or other lord for a certain yearly rent.|The burgage plots were often long and narrow.|近义词:tenement, freehold|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑leasehold(租赁地))|名词

Etymology:
From Old French *burgaige, from burg, "borough."
