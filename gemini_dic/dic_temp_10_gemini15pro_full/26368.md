by-heart:/baɪ ˈhɑːt/| adv.| 默记，背诵| memorized, committed to memory; learned so well that it can be recited without looking at the text| I learned the poem by heart. 我把这首诗背下来了。|近义词：by memory, word for word, verbatim|反义词: none|词性:副词
by-heart:/baɪ ˈhɑːt/| v.| 默记，背诵| to learn something so well that you can remember it perfectly| She by-hearted her lines for the play. 她把她在戏剧中的台词背下来了。|近义词：memorize, learn by rote|反义词: none|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the Middle English phrase "bi herte," meaning "by memory," literally "by heart."  The "heart" was considered the seat of memory and understanding in ancient and medieval times.
