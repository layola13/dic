her-ladyship:/hɜːr ˈleɪdiʃɪp/ |n.|夫人; 女士(尊称)|A respectful or ironic term of address or reference to a woman of title or high social position.  It is formed from the possessive pronoun "her" and the noun "ladyship," which denotes the rank or position of a lady.|例句：Her ladyship will be joining us for dinner. 夫人将和我们一起共进晚餐。

例句：Her ladyship graciously accepted the gift. 夫人优雅地接受了礼物。|近义词：Lady, madam, ma'am|反义词: (No direct antonym, but a less respectful term might be "that woman")|词性:Noun
