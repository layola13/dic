misconstruction:/ˌmɪskənˈstrʌkʃən/ | n. | 误解，曲解；误建 | An incorrect interpretation or understanding of something; a structure that is poorly or incorrectly built. |  The lawyer argued that there had been a misconstruction of the contract. 律师辩称对合同的理解有误。| synonym: misinterpretation, misunderstanding, misreading, distortion, perversion | antonym: correct interpretation, understanding, accurate reading | noun

misconstruction:/ˌmɪskənˈstrʌkʃən/ | n. | 错误的建筑物；盖错的建筑物 | A structure that is built incorrectly. | The leaning tower was a result of misconstruction. 那座倾斜的塔是错误施工的结果。| synonym: faulty construction, bad construction, flawed building | antonym: sound construction, proper building | noun
