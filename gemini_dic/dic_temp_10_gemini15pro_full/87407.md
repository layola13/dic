hersal: /ˈhɜːrsəl/ (British English) /ˈhɜːrsl/ (American English)
| n.| 排练，预演| A rehearsal is a practice or trial performance of a play, piece of music, or other activity in preparation for a public performance.| The orchestra had their final rehearsal before the concert.  管弦乐队在音乐会前进行了最后一次排练。 | rehearsal | practice, run-through | performance | noun


Etymology:
From Old French *rehercier, from re- + hercier "to harrow," from herce "harrow".  The original meaning was "to go over again," as a harrow goes over a field.
