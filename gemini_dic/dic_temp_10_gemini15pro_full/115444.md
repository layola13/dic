mantuamaker:/ˌmæntuˈmeɪkər/ | n. | 女装裁缝；女服制造商 | A person who makes women's clothing, especially dresses and gowns. |  She visited the mantuamaker for a fitting of her wedding gown. 她去拜访女装裁缝试穿她的婚纱。 | dressmaker, seamstress, tailor |  - | noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian "mantello" (cloak) + "-maker" (one who makes).  Originally referred to makers of cloaks or mantles, but later specialized to mean makers of women's gowns and dresses.
