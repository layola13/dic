biliment:/ˈbɪlɪmənt/| n.| 装饰；服饰；装备| An item of clothing or equipment, especially of an ornamental nature; trappings. | The knight's biliments included a plumed helmet and a shining breastplate.  骑士的装备包括一个羽饰头盔和一个闪亮的胸甲。| 近义词：adornment, decoration, ornament, finery, array, garb, attire | 反义词：plainness, simplicity | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle French *biliment*, from bilier ("to adorn"), from Old French bilier, from bille ("tree trunk, log"), from Gaulish *billios*, from Proto-Celtic *biljos*
