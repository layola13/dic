bungee:/ˈbʌndʒi/ | n. | 蹦极，橡皮筋，松紧绳 | A long, thick piece of elastic used for jumping from a high place, with one end fastened to the jumper and the other to the jump point. |  I want to try bungee jumping this summer. 我想今年夏天尝试蹦极跳。| synonyms: elastic cord, elastic rope | antonyms: rigid rope, stiff rope | noun

bungee jumping:/ˈbʌndʒi ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ/ | n. | 蹦极跳 | The sport of jumping from a high place, such as a bridge, while attached to a long elastic rope. | Bungee jumping is an exhilarating experience. 蹦极跳是一项令人振奋的体验。| synonyms: elastic cord jumping | antonyms:  none | noun
