expertly:/ˈɛkspɜːrtli/| adv.|熟练地，专业地|In a skillful and knowledgeable manner, demonstrating expertise.|She expertly navigated the complex legal jargon. 她熟练地解释了复杂的法律术语。|近义词：skillfully, proficiently, adeptly|反义词：clumsily, awkwardly, ineptly|词性:副词
词源: From "expert" + "-ly".  "Expert" comes from the Latin "expertus," past participle of "experiri" (to try, test).
