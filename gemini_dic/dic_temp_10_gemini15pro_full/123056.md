monetaristic: /ˌmʌnɪˈtærɪstɪk/ | adj. | 货币主义的；货币主义者的 | Relating to or characteristic of monetarism; a school of thought that emphasizes the role of the money supply in controlling inflation and economic growth. |  The government adopted a monetaristic approach to control inflation. 政府采取了货币主义的方法来控制通货膨胀。 |  The monetarist policies of the central bank helped stabilize the economy. 中央银行的货币主义政策帮助稳定了经济。| Synonyms: monetary, fiscal | Antonyms: Keynesian | adjective
