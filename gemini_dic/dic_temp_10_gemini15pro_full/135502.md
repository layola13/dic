nonphysicians:/ˌnɑːn fɪˈzɪʃənz/
n.
非医生，非内科医生
People who are not licensed medical doctors.  This includes nurses, physician assistants, therapists, and other healthcare professionals, as well as people with no medical training.
Many nonphysicians play vital roles in healthcare delivery. 许多非医生在医疗保健服务中扮演着至关重要的角色。
synonyms: non-doctors, healthcare professionals (broad), paramedics, nurses, technicians
antonyms: physicians, doctors, medical doctors
noun
