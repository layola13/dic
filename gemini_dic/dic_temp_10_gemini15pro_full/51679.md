devilish:/ˈdevəlɪʃ/ (US), /ˈdɛvəlɪʃ/ (UK)| adj.|恶魔般的；极坏的；恶作剧的|Resembling or befitting a devil, especially in cunning or wickedness; mischievous.|例句：He had a devilish grin on his face. 他脸上露出恶魔般的笑容。|近义词：wicked, mischievous, fiendish, diabolical|反义词：angelic, virtuous, saintly|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *develissh*, *develish*, equivalent to devil +‎ -ish.
