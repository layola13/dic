granilla:/ɡrɑːˈniːjə/ |ɡrɑːˈniːʎɑ/
n.| 小颗粒，小种子，细粒 |A small grain or granule; a small seed.  Specifically, in some Spanish-speaking regions, it refers to small, toasted sesame seeds or similar small seeds used in confectionery or as a topping.| The bakery uses granilla on their rolls.  这家面包店在面包卷上撒了细粒。 | synonyms: granule, seed, speck, particle | antonyms: lump, chunk, mass | noun
