hemstitch:/ˈhemˌstɪtʃ/ | n. v. | 抽纱；边缘缝纫 | A decorative stitch used to finish a raw edge, often on linen.  It involves drawing out several parallel threads and then grouping the remaining threads together in various patterns. |  She hemstitched the edges of the handkerchiefs. 她给手帕的边缘绣了抽纱。| synonyms:  drawn thread work, fagoting, needlework | antonyms:  N/A | noun, verb

hemstitch:/ˈhemˌstɪtʃ/ | n. v. | 抽纱；边缘缝纫 | To sew a hemstitch. | She is hemstitching a new tablecloth. 她正在给一块新的桌布绣抽纱。| synonyms: embroider, stitch, sew | antonyms: unravel, unstitch | noun, verb
