cesion:/seˈsjɔn/ /seˈʃən/ | n. |cession, assignment, transfer| The act of yielding or formally giving up rights, property, or territory, especially by treaty.  |例句：The treaty involved the cession of Hong Kong to Britain.  该条约涉及香港割让给英国。

例句：The company agreed to the cession of its intellectual property rights. 公司同意转让其知识产权。|近义词：surrender, relinquishment, transfer, assignment |反义词:acquisition, retention, gain
词性: 名词
