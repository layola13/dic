concordancy:/kənˈkɔːrdənsi/ /kɑːnˈkɔːrdənsi/| n. |一致，和谐，符合|Agreement or harmony between people or things.|The concordancy between the two studies suggests that the findings are reliable. 这两项研究之间的一致性表明这些发现是可靠的.|近义词：agreement, harmony, conformity, consistency, unity, accordance|反义词：discordance, disagreement, disharmony, conflict, variance, discrepancy|词性:名词

concordant:/kənˈkɔːrdənt/ /kɑːnˈkɔːrdənt/| adj. |一致的，和谐的，符合的|Agreeing or harmonious; consistent.|The results of the two tests were concordant. 两次测试的结果是一致的.|近义词：agreeing, harmonious, consistent, compatible, corresponding, consonant|反义词：discordant, disagreeing, disharmonious, inconsistent, incompatible, conflicting|词性:形容词
