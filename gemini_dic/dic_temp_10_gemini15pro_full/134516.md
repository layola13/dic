nonliberalisms: /ˌnɒnˈlɪbərəlɪzəmz/ | n. | 非自由主义 |  The doctrines or practices of nonliberal political systems or ideologies. These typically emphasize collective or state power over individual rights, often prioritizing tradition, order, or national interests.  |  The rise of nonliberalisms in the 21st century has become a subject of much debate. 21世纪非自由主义的兴起已成为许多争论的主题。 The conference explored various forms of nonliberalisms across the globe. 会议探讨了全球各种形式的非自由主义。 | 近义词: authoritarianism, conservatism, illiberalism | 反义词: liberalism, progressivism | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "liberalism" (from Latin "liberalis," meaning "pertaining to a free man").
