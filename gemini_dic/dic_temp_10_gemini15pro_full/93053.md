idolatrical:/aɪˌdɑːləˈtrɪkəl/ | adj. | 偶像崇拜的；盲目崇拜的 | Characterized by or given to idolatry; excessively worshipping or admiring someone or something. |  His idolatrical devotion to the pop star was bordering on obsession. 他对这位流行歌星的盲目崇拜近乎痴迷。|  The fans' idolatrical behaviour embarrassed the band. 歌迷们偶像崇拜般的行为让乐队感到尴尬。| 近义词: adoring, worshipping, reverent, devoted | 反义词: irreverent, critical, skeptical | 词性: 形容词
Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin *idolatricus, from Greek eidōlatrikos, from eidōlatrēs ‘idol-worshipper’ (see idolater).
