hubbub:/ˈhʌbʌb/ (US), /ˈhʌbʌb/ (UK)| n. |喧闹，嘈杂，混乱；骚动|A loud, confused noise, as of many voices talking at once; a busy, noisy situation.|The hubbub of the marketplace made it difficult to hear.  市场上的喧闹声让人难以听到。|近义词：commotion, uproar, din, racket, clamor|反义词：silence, quiet, peace, tranquility|noun
