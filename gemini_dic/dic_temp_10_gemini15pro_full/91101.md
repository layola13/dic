hyacinths: /ˈhaɪəsɪnθs/ | n. | 风信子（复数） | A fragrant bell-shaped flower, usually purple, pink, white, or blue, that grows from a bulb.  It is native to the eastern Mediterranean region and is now widely cultivated as an ornamental plant. | I love the sweet scent of hyacinths in the spring. 我喜欢春天风信子的甜美香味。

| The hyacinths in the garden are blooming beautifully. 花园里的风信子开得很漂亮。


| 近义词: bluebells（蓝铃花）, grape hyacinths（葡萄风信子） (These are visually similar but different species) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: Noun
