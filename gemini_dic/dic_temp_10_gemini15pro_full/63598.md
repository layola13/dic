envoy-extraordinary:/ˈɛnvɔɪ ɛkˈstrɔːrdəˌnɛri/ | n. | 特命全权公使；特使 | A diplomatic agent of higher rank than an ordinary envoy, but lower than an ambassador.  Specifically appointed for a particular purpose. |  She served as envoy-extraordinary to the United Nations for human rights. 她担任联合国人权事务特使。| synonyms: diplomat, emissary, legate | antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *envoyé extraordinaire*, from *envoyé* (sent) + *extraordinaire* (extraordinary).  *Envoyé* ultimately derives from Latin *inviare* (to send on the way).
