draughtswoman:/ˈdrɑːftswʊmən/ /ˈdræftswʊmən/
noun
女制图员，女绘图员
A woman who makes drawings, plans, or designs, especially technical ones.
例句：The draughtswoman meticulously rendered the blueprints for the new bridge.  女制图员一丝不苟地绘制了新桥的蓝图。

例句：She worked as a draughtswoman for an architectural firm.  她在一家建筑公司担任绘图员。

近义词：draftswoman, draftsperson, designer, illustrator
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps something like client or commissioner)
词性：noun
