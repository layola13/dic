omniferous:/ˌɒmˈnɪfərəs/
adj.
多产的；无所不包的；包含所有的
Producing or containing everything; all-embracing.
The omniferous library contained books on every conceivable subject.  这个无所不包的图书馆包含了所有你能想到的主题的书籍。

The museum boasts an omniferous collection of artifacts from around the world.  这家博物馆拥有来自世界各地的丰富的文物收藏。
all-encompassing, comprehensive, universal, exhaustive
limited, restricted, narrow
adjective
