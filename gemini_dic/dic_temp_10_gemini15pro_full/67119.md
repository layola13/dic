exponential:/ˌɛkspəˈnɛnʃəl/ | adj. n.| 指数的，幂的；越来越快的；指数 | relating to or expressed by a mathematical exponent. Increasing rapidly.| The company experienced exponential growth in the first few years.  这家公司在最初几年经历了指数级增长。 | synonyms: rapid, accelerating, escalating, burgeoning, mushrooming | antonyms: slow, gradual, declining, diminishing | adjective

exponential:/ˌɛkspəˈnɛnʃəl/ | n.| 指数 | a mathematical expression involving exponents. |  Calculating the exponential requires a specialized calculator. 计算指数需要专门的计算器。 | synonyms: power, index | antonyms: N/A | noun
