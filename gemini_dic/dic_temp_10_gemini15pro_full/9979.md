aphorismatic:/ˌæfərɪˈzmætɪk/
adjective
格言式的；警句的；简洁精辟的
Of, relating to, or resembling an aphorism; terse and pithy.
His style is aphorismatic and memorable. 他的风格简洁精辟，令人难忘。

His speech was peppered with aphorismatic wisdom. 他的演讲充满了格言式的智慧。

synonym:pithy, terse, concise, succinct, epigrammatic, sententious, gnomic
antonym:prolix, verbose, rambling, diffuse

Etymology: From Late Latin aphoristicus, from Ancient Greek ἀφοριστικός (aphoristikós, “definitive”), from ἀφορίζειν (aphorízein, “to define, distinguish, divide”).
