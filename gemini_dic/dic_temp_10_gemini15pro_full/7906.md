antenna:/ænˈtɛnə/ (US), /ænˈtɛniː/ (UK)| n. |天线；触角|A metallic device (such as a rod or wire) for radiating or receiving radio waves.|The car's antenna was broken in the storm. 汽车的天线在暴风雨中断了。|Synonyms: aerial (UK), receiver, transmitter|Antonyms: N/A|noun

antenna:/ænˈtɛnə/ (US), /ænˈtɛniː/ (UK)| n. |触角；触须|Either of a pair of long, thin sensory appendages on the heads of insects and some other arthropods.|The butterfly used its antennae to smell the flowers. 蝴蝶用它的触角闻花香。|Synonyms: feeler, tentacle|Antonyms: N/A|noun
