manurable:/məˈnʊərəbl/
adjective
可施肥的; 可耕种的
Able to be cultivated or fertilized.
The soil in the valley is rich and manurable. 山谷里的土壤肥沃，适宜耕种。
近义词: arable, cultivable, tillable
反义词: infertile, barren, unproductive
词性: adjective

manure:/məˈnʊr/
noun, verb
肥料; 施肥
(noun) Animal dung used for fertilizing land. (verb) To apply manure to.
The farmer spread manure on his fields. 农民在地里施肥。
近义词: fertilizer, compost
反义词: N/A
词性: noun, verb
