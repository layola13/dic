manurer:/məˈnʊərər/| n.|施肥者，施肥器|A person or device that applies manure to land.|The manurer spread the compost evenly across the field. 施肥者将堆肥均匀地撒在田野上。|近义词：fertilizer applicator, spreader|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From the verb "manure" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Manure" comes from Old French "manouvrer," meaning "to cultivate by hand," ultimately from Latin "manus" (hand) and "opera" (work).
