embolic:/ɪmˈbɒlɪk/ (US) /ɛmˈbɑːlɪk/| adj.|栓塞的；栓塞性的|Relating to or caused by an embolus (a blood clot, air bubble, or other foreign matter) that travels through the bloodstream and blocks a blood vessel.|The patient suffered an embolic stroke.  病人患有栓塞性中风。|The doctor suspected an embolic event. 医生怀疑是栓塞事件。|近义词：thromboembolic, obstructive|反义词: unobstructed|词性: adjective

词源: From French *embolique*, from Greek *embolikós* "intercalary," from *emballein* "to throw in, insert."
