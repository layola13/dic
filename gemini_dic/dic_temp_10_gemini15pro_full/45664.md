curtain-call: /ˈkɜː(r)tn kɔːl/ | n. |谢幕|The appearance of actors on stage after a performance to acknowledge the audience's applause.|The actors took several curtain-calls after the standing ovation. 演员们在观众起立鼓掌后多次谢幕。|Synonyms: encore, bow, ovation|Antonyms: N/A|noun

curtain-call: /ˈkɜː(r)tn kɔːl/ | n. | (比喻)最后的露面;最后一次公开讲话|A final public appearance or speech before retiring or leaving a position.|The retiring CEO gave one last curtain-call at the annual meeting. 即将退休的CEO在年会上做了最后一次公开讲话。|Synonyms: farewell, valediction|Antonyms: debut, premiere|noun
