interdependence:/ˌɪntər.dɪˈpɛn.dəns/ | n. |相互依存，互相依赖 |A relationship between two or more things where each one is dependent on the other.  |例句：The interdependence of the global economy is now more apparent than ever. 全球经济的相互依赖性现在比以往任何时候都更加明显。

例句：The project highlighted the interdependence of the various departments within the company.  该项目突出了公司内部各个部门之间的相互依赖性。


近义词：interconnection, interrelationship, mutual dependence|反义词:independence, autonomy |词性:名词
