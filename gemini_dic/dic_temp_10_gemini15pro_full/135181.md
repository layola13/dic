nonoral:/nɒnˈɔːrəl/ | adj. | 非口头的 | Not involving or using speech; not oral. |  The teacher used nonoral cues to get the students' attention. 老师使用非口头提示来吸引学生的注意力。|  The exam included a nonoral component, focusing on written responses. 考试包含一个非口头部分，侧重于书面回答。 | 近义词:  written, unspoken, nonverbal, silent | 反义词: oral, spoken, verbal | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (not) + "oral" (relating to the mouth or speech).  "Oral" comes from the Latin "os," meaning "mouth."
