evaporations:/ɪˌvæpəˈreɪʃənz/
n.
蒸发；蒸发量；消失
the process of a liquid changing to a gas, especially by heating; an instance of this
The evaporation of water from the lake cools the air. 湖水蒸发使空气变凉。
vaporization, disappearance, vanishing
condensation, materialization
noun
