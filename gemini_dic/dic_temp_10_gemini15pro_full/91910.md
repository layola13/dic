hyperlustrous:/ˌhaɪpərˈlʌstrəs/| adj.|极度光亮的，极度有光泽的|Having an extremely bright or lustrous appearance; exceptionally shiny.|The newly polished car had a hyperlustrous finish that gleamed in the sunlight. 新抛光的汽车表面极其光亮，在阳光下闪闪发光。|The diamond was hyperlustrous, sparkling with every color of the rainbow. 这颗钻石光彩夺目，闪耀着彩虹的每一种颜色。|近义词：brilliant, radiant, gleaming, dazzling|反义词：dull, matte, lackluster|词性:形容词
