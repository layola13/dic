concentre:/ˈkɒnsəntreɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsənˌtreɪt/ (US)| v. n.|集中，浓缩|To bring or direct (something, such as attention or resources) to a common center or single purpose; to gather into one body, mass, or space.|例句：The troops were concentrated along the border. 部队集结在边境。

|近义词：focus, converge, gather, amass|反义词：disperse, scatter, diffuse|词性：动词

concentre:/ˈkɒnsəntreɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsənˌtreɪt/ (US)| n.|浓缩物|A substance made by concentrating something, especially a food or drink.|例句：The soup was a concentrate of chicken and vegetables. 这汤是鸡肉和蔬菜的浓缩物。

|近义词：extract, essence|反义词：dilution|词性：名词
