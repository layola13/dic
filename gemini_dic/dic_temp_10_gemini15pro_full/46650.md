damningly:/ˈdæmɪŋli/
adverb| 谴责地；极有害地；非常不利地 |in a way that suggests strong disapproval and condemnation, often implying guilt or wrongdoing.  It suggests that something is very harmful or damaging to someone's reputation or chances of success.|The evidence against him spoke damningly.  这些证据对他非常不利。|Synonyms: incriminatingly, condemningly, accusingly|Antonyms: favorably, approvingly, supportively
词性: 副词
