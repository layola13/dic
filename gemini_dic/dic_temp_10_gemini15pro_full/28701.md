carloads:/ˈkɑːrloʊdz/| n.|车厢装载量；许多；大量|a quantity of something that fills one or more railroad cars|例句：They shipped carloads of oranges from Florida. 他们从佛罗里达州运送了几车皮的橙子。

例句：Carloads of tourists arrive every day during the summer. 每天夏天都有大量的游客到来。|近义词：shipments, lots, tons, heaps|反义词:little, few|词性:名词
