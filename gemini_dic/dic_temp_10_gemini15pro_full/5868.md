ambassador: /æmˈbæsədər/ (US), /æmˈbæsədə(r)/ (UK)| n. |大使；使节；代表|A diplomat of the highest rank, sent by one country to another as its permanent representative.|The US ambassador to the UN addressed the assembly. 美国驻联合国大使向大会发表了讲话。|近义词: envoy, diplomat, legate, representative|反义词: None|词性: noun

ambassador: /æmˈbæsədər/ (US), /æmˈbæsədə(r)/ (UK)| n. |(非正式)（某事物的）代表；倡导者|A person who represents or promotes a particular activity or organization.|She is a goodwill ambassador for the charity. 她是该慈善机构的亲善大使。|近义词: representative, advocate, champion|反义词: None|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *ambassadeor*, from Late Latin *ambactiator* "messenger, servant," from *ambactus* "vassal, servant" (especially among the Gauls and Germans).
