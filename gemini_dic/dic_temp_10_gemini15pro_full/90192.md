hottest:/ˈhɒtɪst/ (UK) /ˈhɑːtɪst/ (US)| adj.|最热的，最性感的，最流行的|Superlative form of "hot."  Indicates the highest degree of heat, attractiveness, or popularity.|例句：This summer is the hottest on record.  这个夏天是有记录以来最热的。

例句：She's the hottest actress in Hollywood right now. 她现在是好莱坞最红的女演员。


近义词：warmest, sexiest, trendiest, most popular|反义词:coldest, least popular|词性:形容词
