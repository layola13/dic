affrayer:/əˈfreɪər/| vt.|恐吓，惊吓|To frighten or terrify; to fill with fear or alarm.|The sudden noise affrayed the horses. 突如其来的噪音吓坏了马匹。|近义词：frighten, scare, terrify, alarm|反义词：calm, soothe, reassure|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *esfreer, from es- (variant of ex-) "out" + freor "to freeze, frighten," from Frankish *friusan "to freeze," from Proto-Germanic *friusanan, from PIE *preus- "to freeze." The sense development is from "to make someone freeze with fear" to "to frighten."
