outluster:/ˌaʊtˈlʌstər/
verb
胜过，使黯然失色
to surpass in brilliance or splendor; to outshine
例句：Her performance completely outlustered everyone else's. 她的表演完全盖过了其他所有人。

例句：The new stadium outlustered the old one in size and amenities.  新的体育场在规模和设施上都胜过旧的。
近义词：outshine, eclipse, surpass, exceed
反义词：pale in comparison to, be overshadowed by
词性：v.
