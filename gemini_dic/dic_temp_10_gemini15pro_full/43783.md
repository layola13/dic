cremation:/krɪˈmeɪʃ(ə)n/ /krɪˈmæʃ(ə)n/
n.
火葬，焚化
The process of burning a dead body to ashes.
例句：Cremation is becoming an increasingly popular choice. 火葬正成为越来越受欢迎的选择。

例句：The family chose cremation over burial. 这家人选择了火葬而不是土葬。

近义词：incineration
反义词：burial, interment
词性：名词

cremate:/krɪˈmeɪt/ /ˈkrimˌeɪt/
vt.
火化，焚烧
To reduce (a corpse) to ashes by fire.
例句：They decided to cremate his body. 他们决定火化他的遗体。

例句：The deceased was cremated according to his wishes.  死者按照他的遗愿被火化了。

近义词：incinerate
反义词：bury, inter
词性：动词
