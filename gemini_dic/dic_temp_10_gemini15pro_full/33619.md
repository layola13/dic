cincinnus:/sɪnˈsɪnəs/
noun
卷云
A cirrus cloud; a curl or ringlet of hair.
High in the sky, delicate wisps of cincinnus clouds drifted lazily.  高空中，纤细的卷云缓缓飘动。
The stylist carefully crafted each cincinnus of her elaborate updo.  造型师精心打造了她精致盘发中的每一个卷发。
synonyms: cirrus, tendril, ringlet, curl
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a cloud formation or hair curl; could consider "straight" for hair)
