murkiness:/ˈmɜːkɪnɪs/ | n. | 昏暗，阴暗，模糊不清 | The murkiness of the water made it impossible to see the bottom.  水的浑浊使得无法看到底部。 | The report was criticized for its murkiness and lack of clarity. 这份报告因其模糊不清和缺乏清晰度而受到批评。 | 近义词：darkness, gloom, obscurity, dimness | 反义词：brightness, clarity, light | 词性：名词

词源：From murky + -ness.  Murky 源自古英语 *myrce, 意为“黑暗的”。
