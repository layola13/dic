doleritic:/ˌdɒləˈrɪtɪk/ /ˌdoʊləˈrɪtɪk/| adj.|粗玄岩的；辉绿岩的|Relating to or consisting of dolerite, a dark, medium-grained igneous rock.|The doleritic rocks formed a dramatic cliff face. 粗玄岩形成了壮观的悬崖峭壁。|Synonyms: basaltic, gabbroic|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a rock type, could consider contrasting rock types like granitic or rhyolitic)|adjective
