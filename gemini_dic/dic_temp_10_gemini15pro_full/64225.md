equinal:/ˈiːkwaɪnəl/
adjective
马的；似马的；属于马科动物的
Of, relating to, or resembling a horse or horses.
例句：The equinal features of the creature were striking.  这个生物的马的特征很显著。

例句：She studied equinal anatomy in veterinary school. 她在兽医学院学习了马的解剖学。
近义词：horselike, equine
反义词： (No direct antonym, could consider "bovine" if contrasting with cattle) 
词性：adjective
