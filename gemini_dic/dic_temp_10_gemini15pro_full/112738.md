lpn:/ɛl piː ɛn/| n.|Licensed Practical Nurse|持照实习护士|A licensed practical nurse (LPN) is a nurse who provides basic nursing care.  LPNs work under the direction of registered nurses (RNs) or physicians.|The LPN checked the patient's vital signs.|Synonyms: LVN (Licensed Vocational Nurse), practical nurse|Antonyms: physician, doctor|noun
