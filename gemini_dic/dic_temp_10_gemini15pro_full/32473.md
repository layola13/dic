Example 2:  The chincapin tree provided welcome shade on a hot day.  这棵栗子树在炎热的天气里提供了受欢迎的阴凉。 | Synonym: chestnut, chinkapin | Antonym: (No direct antonym for a type of tree/nut) | noun
