advance: /ədˈvæns/ | adj. | 预先的，事先的 |
Advance means happening or existing before something else, or arranged or done beforehand. |
例句：We received an advance warning of the storm. 我们收到了风暴的预警。
例句：Advance bookings are essential.  提前预订是必要的。

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *avancer (modern French avancer) "to put forward, promote, bring forward, hasten," from a- "to" (from Latin ad-) + *vancer "to put forward," from Late Latin vanciare "to move in front," from Latin abante "from the front," from ab "from, away from" + ante "before, in front."
