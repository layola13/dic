lousy:/ˈlaʊzi/ /ˈlauzi/| adj.|糟糕的，差劲的；讨厌的；生虱子的|Very poor or bad; unpleasant; infested with lice.  This refers to something of poor quality, unpleasant, or infested with lice. While the lice connection is less common now, the negative connotation remains strong.|The food at the restaurant was lousy.  这家餐馆的饭菜很糟糕。

The hotel room was lousy and overpriced.  这个旅馆房间很差劲，而且价格过高。|Synonyms: awful, terrible, dreadful, rotten, crummy, pathetic|Antonyms: excellent, wonderful, great, fantastic, superb|Adjective
