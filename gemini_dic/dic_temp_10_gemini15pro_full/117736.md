melanistic:/ˌmɛləˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 黑色素的，黑化的 | Having an unusually dark or black pigmentation, especially as an inherited characteristic. |  The melanistic jaguar is a rare and beautiful creature. 黑色美洲虎是一种稀有而美丽的生物。 |  The forest was home to a melanistic form of the leopard. 这片森林是黑豹的家园。 | 近义词: dark, black, pigmented | 反义词: albinistic, leucistic | 词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From Greek *melas* (μέλας) "black, dark" + *-istikos*  (forming adjectives).
