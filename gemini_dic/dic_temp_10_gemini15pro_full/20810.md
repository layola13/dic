blasphemous:/ˈblæsfəməs/ | adj. | 亵渎的，渎神的 | Showing disrespect or contempt for something considered sacred. | His blasphemous remarks shocked the devout worshippers. 他亵渎神明的言论震惊了虔诚的信徒。|  The comedian's blasphemous jokes offended many religious viewers.  这位喜剧演员亵渎神明的笑话冒犯了许多有宗教信仰的观众。 | synonyms: sacrilegious, profane, irreverent, impious | antonyms: reverent, pious, devout | adjective

blasphemy:/ˈblæsfəmi/ | n. | 亵渎，渎神 | The act or offense of speaking sacrilegiously about God or sacred things. |  Burning the holy book was seen as an act of blasphemy. 烧毁圣书被视为亵渎神明的行为。 | The protestors were accused of blasphemy for their disrespectful slogans. 抗议者因其不敬的口号而被指控亵渎神明。 | synonyms: sacrilege, profanation, irreverence, impiety | antonyms: reverence, piety, devotion | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French blasfemer, from Late Latin blasphemare, from Greek blasphēmein "to speak evil of, revile, slander," from blasphemos "evil-speaking," from blaptein "to harm, injure" + phēmē "speech, utterance, rumor, report."
