incharity:/ɪnˈtʃærɪti/
n.
lack of charity; unkindness; ill will
缺乏仁慈；不友善；恶意
He spoke of his ex-wife with incharity. 他以恶意谈论他的前妻。
Synonyms: unkindness, malice, animosity, hostility, bitterness, rancor, malevolence, spite, venom, vitriol
Antonyms: charity, kindness, benevolence, goodwill, compassion
noun
