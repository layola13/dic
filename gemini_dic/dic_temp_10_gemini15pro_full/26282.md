buttressed:/ˈbʌtrɪst/ /ˈbʌtrɛst/| adj.|支撑的，加固的|Made stronger or more secure by the addition of supports.|The crumbling walls of the castle were buttressed by huge stone supports. 城堡摇摇欲坠的墙壁由巨大的石头支撑着。|The theory is buttressed by extensive research.  该理论得到了广泛研究的支持。|Synonyms: supported, reinforced, strengthened, fortified|Antonyms: weakened, undermined, unsupported|adjective
