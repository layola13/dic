copepoda:/ˈkoʊpɪˌpoʊdə/ | n. |桡足类|A subclass of small crustaceans, found in fresh and salt water, that constitute the primary zooplankton of the oceans. |例句：Copepoda are a crucial part of the marine food web. 桡足类是海洋食物网的重要组成部分。

例句：Many species of fish rely on copepoda as a primary food source. 许多鱼类依赖桡足类作为主要食物来源。|近义词：zooplankton, crustacean |反义词:  (No direct antonym for a biological classification) |词性:noun
