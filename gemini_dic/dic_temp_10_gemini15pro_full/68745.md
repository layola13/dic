farmings:/ˈfɑːrmɪŋz/
n.
农耕; 农事; 耕作;
The act or process of working the ground, planting crops, and raising livestock.
例句：Farmings in this region are primarily focused on rice cultivation.  这个地区的农耕主要集中在水稻种植。

例句：Modern farmings utilize advanced technologies like drones and GPS. 现代农业利用无人机和GPS等先进技术。

近义词：agriculture, cultivation, husbandry
反义词：N/A (No direct antonym for the process of farming)
词性：noun


farming:/ˈfɑːrmɪŋ/
n.
农业; 耕作;
The activity or business of growing crops and raising livestock.
例句：Organic farming is becoming increasingly popular. 有机农业正变得越来越流行。

例句：He left the city to pursue a career in farming. 他离开城市去从事农业。
近义词：agriculture, cultivation, husbandry
反义词：N/A
词性：noun
