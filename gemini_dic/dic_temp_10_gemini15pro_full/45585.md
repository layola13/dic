curmudgeonly: /kərˈmʌdʒənli/ | adj. |脾气坏的，暴躁的 | bad-tempered, especially an old person | Example: He's become a curmudgeonly old man, shouting at children to get off his lawn.  他变成了一个脾气暴躁的老人，对着孩子们大喊大叫，让他们离开他的草坪。| Example:  Despite her curmudgeonly exterior, she had a heart of gold. 虽然她外表脾气暴躁，但她心地善良。 |  Near synonyms: grumpy, cantankerous, irascible, crotchety, surly | Antonyms: good-natured, amiable, affable, genial | word property: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain. Possibly from 18th-century dialectal *curmudgeon, perhaps related to corn-mudgin, a fictional name meaning "corn hoarder," or from the obsolete word mudgeon "sullen or grumpy person."
