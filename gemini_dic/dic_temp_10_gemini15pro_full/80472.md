gonochorism:/gɒnəˈkɔːrɪzəm/ | n. |雌雄异体；雌雄异株 |The separation of the sexes in different individuals.  The state of having separate sexes. |Most vertebrates exhibit gonochorism. 大多数脊椎动物都表现出雌雄异体。|  Gonochorism is common in many animal species. 雌雄异体在许多动物物种中很常见。| synonym: dioecy, bisexuality (in plants) | antonym: hermaphroditism, monoecy (in plants) | noun
