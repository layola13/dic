exhausts:/ɪɡˈzɔːsts/ (UK), /ɪɡˈzɑːsts/ (US)| n. v.| 排气管; 废气; 使筋疲力尽; 耗尽|

**Noun (n.)**

*  The car's exhausts were emitting black smoke.  汽车的排气管排放着黑烟。
*  The factory exhausts polluted the air. 工厂的废气污染了空气。

**Verb (v.)**

*  The long journey exhausted the travelers.  漫长的旅程使旅行者筋疲力尽。
*  The company exhausted its resources on the failed project.  公司在失败的项目上耗尽了资源。

**Etymology:**

From Late Middle English *exhausten*, from Latin *exhaustus*, past participle of *exhaurire* (“to draw out, drain, empty, consume entirely”), from *ex-* (“out”) + *haurire* (“to draw, drain”).

**Synonyms:**

**(v.)** tire, weary, drain, deplete, sap, consume, expend
**(n.)** fumes, emissions, discharge


**Antonyms:**

**(v.)** energize, invigorate, replenish
**(n.)** intake


**Word Form:**

exhaust (singular noun, verb)
exhausts (plural noun, third-person singular present tense verb)
exhausted (past tense and past participle of the verb)
exhausting (present participle of the verb)
exhaustion (noun)
exhaustive (adjective)
