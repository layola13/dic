furfuration:/ˌfɜːrfjʊˈreɪʃən/| n. |糠秕;鳞屑;细微脱屑|The process of flaking or scaling off of the epidermis in small particles, like dandruff or bran.  It's characterized by the shedding of small, dry scales from the skin.|Example 1: The patient presented with furfuration on his scalp, a classic sign of dandruff. 病人的头皮出现糠秕，这是头屑的典型症状。

Example 2:  The dermatologist noted the furfuration around the patient's nose, suggesting seborrheic dermatitis. 皮肤科医生注意到病人鼻子周围有细微脱屑，这表明可能是脂溢性皮炎。|近义词：scaling, desquamation, exfoliation|反义词:|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *furfuratio, from Latin furfur (bran, dandruff).
