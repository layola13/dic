circumrotary:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈroʊtəri/
adjective
旋转的;环绕旋转的
Rotating around a center or axis; encircling in rotation.
The circumrotary motion of the planets around the sun.  行星绕太阳的环绕旋转运动。
The dancer's circumrotary movements were mesmerizing.  舞者旋转的动作令人着迷。
近义词:rotary, revolving, gyrating
反义词:stationary, immobile, fixed
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From circum- (around) + rotary (rotating).  来自circum-(周围) + rotary(旋转)。
