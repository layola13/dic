integrities: /ɪnˈtɛɡrɪtiz/
n.
正直，诚实，完整性
The quality of being honest and having strong moral principles; moral uprightness.
He is a man of great integrity. 他是一个正直的人。
honesty, uprightness, probity, rectitude, righteousness, morality, principle, ethics, morals, virtue
dishonesty, corruption, deceit, duplicity, hypocrisy
noun

integrity: /ɪnˈtɛɡrɪti/
n.
完整，完全
The state of being whole and undivided.
The integrity of the building was compromised by the earthquake.  地震损害了建筑物的完整性。
completeness, wholeness, entirety, totality, unity, soundness, solidity, strength, robustness
incompleteness, fragmentation, brokenness
noun
