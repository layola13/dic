nutters:/ˈnʌtəz/
n.
疯子；傻瓜；古怪的人
Crazy or eccentric people.
Those nutters are at it again, protesting outside the town hall. 那些疯子又在市政厅外抗议了。
Synonyms: lunatics, madmen, crazies, eccentrics
Antonyms: sane people, rational people
