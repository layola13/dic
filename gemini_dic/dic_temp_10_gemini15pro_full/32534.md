chip-off-the-old-block:/ˌtʃɪp ɔːf ðiː ˌoʊld ˈblɑːk/| n.|酷似父亲（或母亲）的人；与其父（或母）一模一样的人|A person who is very similar in character or appearance to their father or mother.  This expression is often used in a positive or affectionate way.|He's a chip off the old block - he loves football just like his dad.|近义词：spitting image, like father, like son|反义词:polar opposite|词性:noun
