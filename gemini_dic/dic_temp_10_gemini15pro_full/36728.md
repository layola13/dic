collodionize:/kəˈloʊdiəˌnaɪz/
verb
涂火棉胶于; 用火棉胶处理
to treat or coat with collodion.
The photographer collodionized the glass plate before taking the picture. 摄影师在拍照前在玻璃板上涂了一层火棉胶。
The wound was collodionized to protect it from infection.  伤口涂上了火棉胶以防止感染。

近义词: coat, cover
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "remove" in a specific context)
词性: verb

Etymology:
From collodion + -ize. Collodion itself comes from Greek kollōdēs, meaning "glue-like."
