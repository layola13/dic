deviate:/ˈdiːvieɪt/ (US) /ˈdiːviˌeɪt/ (UK)| v. |偏离，背离，违背|To depart from a course, standard, or norm.|The hikers deviated from the trail to explore a cave.  徒步旅行者偏离了小径去探索一个洞穴。|The plane deviated from its flight path due to bad weather. 由于天气恶劣，飞机偏离了航线。|近义词：stray, diverge, digress, depart|反义词：conform, adhere, follow|词性：动词
deviate:/ˈdiːvieɪt/ (US) /ˈdiːviˌeɪt/ (UK)| n. |偏离者，背离者|One who departs from a norm or standard. |He was considered a deviate for his unconventional beliefs.  由于他非传统的信仰，他被认为是一个偏离者。|The study focused on the deviates within the control group.  这项研究的重点是控制组中的偏差者。|近义词：nonconformist, maverick, dissident|反义词：conformist, traditionalist|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin deviatus, past participle of deviare "to turn aside," from de- "away" + via "way."
