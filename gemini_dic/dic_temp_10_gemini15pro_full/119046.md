meticulously:/məˈtɪkjələsli/
adjective
一丝不苟地，细致地
in a way that shows great attention to detail; very thoroughly
She meticulously planned every detail of the wedding. 她一丝不苟地计划了婚礼的每一个细节。
carefully, precisely, thoroughly, diligently
carelessly, negligently, sloppily
adverb
