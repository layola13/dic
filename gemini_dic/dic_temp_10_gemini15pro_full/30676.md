cerotype:/ˈsɪərəˌtaɪp/
noun
蜡版
a method of printing from a wax plate
例句：Cerotype was a less common printing method compared to letterpress.  蜡版印刷与凸版印刷相比是一种不太常见的印刷方法。

例句：The museum displayed a rare book printed using the cerotype process.  博物馆展出了一本用蜡版印刷工艺印刷的珍本书。
近义词：wax engraving, electrotype
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps other printing methods like letterpress, offset printing)
词性: n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek "kēros" (wax) + "-type" (impression)
