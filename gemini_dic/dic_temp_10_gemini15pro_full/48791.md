deflection:/dɪˈflɛkʃən/| n.|偏斜，偏转，挠度|The deflection of the beam under load was minimal.  梁在负载下的挠度很小。|The compass needle showed a slight deflection.  指南针的指针显示出轻微的偏转。|近义词：deviation, divergence, digression|反义词：straightness, directness|词性：名词

deflections:/dɪˈflɛkʃənz/| n.|偏斜，偏转，挠度（复数）|The engineer measured the deflections of the bridge supports. 工程师测量了桥梁支撑的挠度。|Multiple deflections of light create a rainbow.  光的多次偏转形成了彩虹。|近义词：deviations, divergences, digressions|反义词：straightnesses, directnesses|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin dēflectiōn- (stem of dēflectiō), from Latin dēflectere “to bend down or aside,” from de- “down, away from” + flectere “to bend.”
