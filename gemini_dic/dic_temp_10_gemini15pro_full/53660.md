disciferous: /dɪˈsɪfərəs/
| adj. |
| 持盘的；有盘状物的；(植物)有花盘的 |
| Bearing discs, or disc-like structures, particularly referring to botanical structures like flower discs. |
| The disciferous plant attracted numerous pollinators.  这种有花盘的植物吸引了大量的传粉者。 |

| Many species of sunflowers are disciferous. 许多种类的向日葵都有花盘。 |

| 近义词: discoid |
| 反义词:  (无明确的反义词，可根据语境选择如“lacking a disc”等描述性词语) |
| 词性: adjective |
