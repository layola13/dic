madreporiform:/ˌmædrɪˈpɔːrɪfɔːrm/
adjective
 madreporic; madreporitic
筛板状的；多孔的
Resembling a madreporite, the porous plate by which the vascular system of an echinoderm communicates with the exterior.
类似于筛板，棘皮动物的维管系统通过该多孔板与外界相通。
The starfish has a madreporiform plate on its aboral surface.  海星在其反口面有一个筛板状的板。
The brittle star's madreporiform structure is located on its oral surface.  蛇尾的筛板状结构位于其口面。
Synonyms: porous, perforated, sieve-like
Antonyms:  impermeable, solid, nonporous
adjective
