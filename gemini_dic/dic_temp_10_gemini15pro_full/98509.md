instantiating:/ɪnˈstænʃɪeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 实例化 | The process of creating a specific instance or example of a class or object in programming.  Creating a real-world example of an abstract concept. |  The programmer is instantiating a new object in the code.  程序员正在代码中实例化一个新的对象。 | synonyms: creating, generating, materializing, realizing, exemplifying | antonyms: abstracting, conceptualizing, generalizing | verb
