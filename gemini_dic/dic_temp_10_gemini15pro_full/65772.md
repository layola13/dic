everyman:/ˈevriˌmæn/ | n. |普通人，寻常人；（戏剧）《人人》|An ordinary individual; a typical or representative human being.  Also, the title of a 15th-century morality play. |The play Everyman explores themes of mortality and redemption. 《人人》这出戏探讨了死亡和救赎的主题。| Everyman can relate to the struggles of the main character.  每个人都能理解主人公的挣扎。|synonyms: average person, common man, ordinary individual, the man in the street | antonyms:  superman, celebrity, elite, extraordinary individual | noun
