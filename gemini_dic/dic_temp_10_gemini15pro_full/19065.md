betokening:/bɪˈtoʊkənɪŋ/ | adj. | 预示的，表示的 | indicating; being a sign of |  The dark clouds betokening a storm.  乌云预示着暴风雨。 | synonyms: presaging, foreshadowing, portending, auguring, signifying | antonyms: concealing, hiding, masking | adjective

betoken:/bɪˈtoʊkən/ | v. | 预示，表示 | to be a sign of; to indicate |  A red sky at night often betokens good weather.  傍晚的红霞通常预示着好天气。 | synonyms: presage, foreshadow, portend, augur, signify | antonyms: conceal, hide, mask | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bitokenen*, from Old English *betācnian* (“to signify, betoken, portend, show, indicate, predict”), from *be-* (prefix meaning ‘thoroughly’) + *tācnian* (“to mark, indicate, show, betoken”), from Proto-Germanic *taiknijaną* (“to show, designate”), from *taiknaz* (“sign, mark”). Cognate with Old Frisian *biteknja* (“to signify”), Dutch *betekenen* (“to mean, signify”), German *bezeichnen* (“to mark, denote, designate”).
