fumarine:/ˈfjuməˌrin/ | n. |延胡索素|An alkaloid, C21H19NO4, obtained from fumitory (Fumaria officinalis) and other plants of the same genus. It forms colorless, tasteless crystals that are insoluble in water. It is used medicinally. |Fumarine is being studied for its potential anti-cancer properties. 延胡索素正在被研究其潜在的抗癌特性。|Synonyms: protopine, cryptopine |Antonyms: None|noun
