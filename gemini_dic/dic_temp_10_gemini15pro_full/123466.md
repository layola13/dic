monosperm:/ˈmɒnəʊspɜːm/ /ˈmɑːnəspɜːrm/ | adj. n. |单种子的，单精入卵的| Containing or producing only one seed; characterized by monospermy. |The monosperm fruit develops from a single carpel.  这种单种子的果实由单个心皮发育而来。| The team studied the monosperm fertilization process in mice. 该团队研究了小鼠的单精受精过程。|近义词：unispermous, single-seeded |反义词: polyspermous, multi-seeded |词性: adjective, noun

Etymology:
From mono- (one) + sperm (seed)
