derailleur:/dɪˈreɪlər/ /dəˈreɪlər/ | n. |变速器，拨链器|A derailleur is a mechanism for shifting a bicycle chain from one sprocket wheel to another, allowing for different gears. |例句：The derailleur on my bike needs adjusting. 我的自行车的变速器需要调整。

例句：He damaged his derailleur in a crash. 他的变速器在一次碰撞中损坏了。|近义词：gear mechanism, gear shifter |反义词: None |词性:noun
