hayrides:/ˈheɪˌraɪdz/ | n. | 干草车游乐;  乘干草车游玩 | A ride in a wagon filled with hay, often offered as entertainment at farms and festivals. | We went on a hayride at the pumpkin patch. 我们在南瓜地里玩了干草车。| 近义词: wagon ride, hay wagon ride | 反义词:  (No direct opposite) | noun
