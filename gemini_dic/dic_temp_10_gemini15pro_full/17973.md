befriender:/bɪˈfrɛndər/
n.|施以援手的人；交朋友的人|A person who befriends or supports someone, especially someone who is vulnerable or isolated.|She became a befriender to the lonely old woman. 她成了那位孤独老妇人的朋友。|近义词:supporter, advocate, helper, friend|反义词:enemy, opponent|noun

befriending:/bɪˈfrɛndɪŋ/
n.|友好行为；建立友谊的过程|The act of making friends with someone, especially someone who is vulnerable or isolated.|The befriending scheme has helped many elderly people feel less isolated. 这个结对帮扶计划帮助许多老年人减少了孤独感。|近义词:supporting, helping, aiding|反义词:opposing, hindering|noun
