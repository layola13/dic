outdebate:/ˌaʊtdɪˈbeɪt/
verb
胜过辩论，以辩论击败
to defeat someone in a debate
She outdebated her opponent with her sharp wit and logical arguments. 她以敏锐的才智和逻辑严密的论证在辩论中击败了对手。
近义词: outargue, defeat in debate
反义词: lose to in debate
词性：v.


outdebate:/ˌaʊtdɪˈbeɪt/
verb
通过辩论胜过，通过辩论压倒
to surpass or overwhelm in debating
He outdebated everyone in the room, leaving them speechless. 他在辩论中胜过了房间里的所有人，让他们哑口无言。
近义词: surpass in debate, overwhelm in debate
反义词: be defeated in debate
词性：v.
