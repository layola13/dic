carpooler:/ˈkɑːrˌpulər/ (US) /ˈkɑːˌpuːlər/ (UK)| n.|拼车者；合伙用车的人|A person who travels to work or school regularly with others, especially in a car, to reduce costs or traffic congestion.|例句：My neighbor is my carpooler. 我的邻居是我的拼车伙伴。

例句：She became a carpooler to save money on gas. 她为了省油钱成为了拼车一族。|近义词：ride-sharer, commuter|反义词:solo driver|词性:noun
