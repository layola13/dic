baggish:/ˈbæɡɪʃ/| adj.|袋状的；臃肿的；松弛的|Resembling a bag; loose and hanging.  Characterized by looseness or shapelessness, as of clothing.|Her baggish dress made her look older than she was. 她那件袋状的连衣裙使她看起来比实际年龄大。|Synonyms: loose, baggy, slack, droopy|Antonyms: tight, fitted, snug|词性:形容词

Etymology: From bag + -ish.  The suffix "-ish" is used to form adjectives meaning "somewhat like" or "having the qualities of."
