grapple:/ˈɡræpəl/ | n. | 抓斗；格斗；搏斗 | a struggle or contest; an iron hook used to grab and hold | The ship's grapple caught the sinking boat.  船上的抓斗抓住了正在下沉的船。| Synonyms: struggle, contest, hook | Antonyms: release, surrender | noun
