causerie:/ˈkɔːzəri/ (US), /ˈkɔːzəri/ (UK)| n. |非正式谈话；闲聊|An informal talk or discussion, often on a literary or artistic subject.  A light, informal conversation.| We had a pleasant causerie after dinner about our travels.  我们晚饭后愉快地闲聊了我们的旅行。|Synonyms: chat, conversation, discussion, talk|Antonyms: debate, argument, dispute, quarrel|noun
