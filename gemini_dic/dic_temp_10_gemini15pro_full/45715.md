curvedly:/ˈkɜːrvɪdli/
adverb
弯曲地，曲线地
in a curved manner; not straight or angular.
The path wound curvedly through the woods. 小路弯弯曲曲地穿过树林。

Synonyms: sinuously, windingly, tortuously
Antonyms: straightly, directly

ETYMOLOGY->
From curve + -ed + -ly.  "Curve" comes from the Latin "curvus" meaning "bent, crooked."
