grander:/ˈɡrændər/| adj.|更宏伟的，更壮丽的，更伟大的|Comparative form of grand: more grand.  Larger and more impressive than others of the same kind. |例句：The new stadium is grander than the old one. 新体育场比旧的更宏伟。

例句：He had grander ambitions than his peers. 他的抱负比同龄人更远大。|近义词：more magnificent, more imposing, more majestic, loftier|反义词：smaller, less impressive, humbler|词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *grander, compartive of grand, from Old French grand, from Latin grandis (“great, large, tall, full-grown”).
