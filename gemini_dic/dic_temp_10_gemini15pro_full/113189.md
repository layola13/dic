lurgies:/ˈlɜːrdʒiz/
noun
(plural noun)
轻微的疾病; 不舒服
a generally mild illness, especially one whose precise nature is unclear.
例句：
I've got the lurgies and won't be coming into work today.
我得了点小病，今天不能来上班了。

例句：
She's been off work all week with the lurgies.
她因为小病已经请假一周了。

近义词：
bug, illness, sickness, ailment, malady, indisposition

反义词：
wellness, health

词性:
n.
