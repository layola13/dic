balotade:/ˌbæləˈtɑːd/ /ˌbæləˈteɪd/| n.|腾跃，跳跃（马术术语）|A jumping movement in which a horse leaps into the air with hind legs bent and forelegs extended.|The rider guided her horse into a perfect balotade. 骑手引导她的马完成了一次完美的腾跃。|Synonyms: leap, bound, jump|Antonyms: landing, descent|noun
