maneh:/ˈmeɪneɪ/ /ˈmɑːneɪ/ | n. |玛尼（古代西亚的重量和货币单位）| A unit of weight and currency used in ancient Mesopotamia, Persia, and Israel.  It corresponded to a specific weight of silver or gold, varying over time and location. |The merchant accepted payment in manehs of silver.| 近义词: shekel, talent (though not exact equivalents) | 反义词: None | Noun
