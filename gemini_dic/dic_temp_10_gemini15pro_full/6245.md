amoeba:/əˈmiːbə/ | n. | 变形虫 | A single-celled animal that catches food and moves about by extending fingerlike projections of protoplasm.  A microscopic organism belonging to the amoebozoa group, characterized by its shapelessness and ability to change form. |  The amoeba is a simple organism found in pond water.  变形虫是一种在池塘水中发现的简单生物。 | synonyms: protozoan, microorganism | antonyms: multicellular organism | noun
