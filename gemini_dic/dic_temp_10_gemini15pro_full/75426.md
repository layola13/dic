fruitarian:/fruːˈteəriən/
n.|果食者；水果主义者|A person who eats only fruit.|She's a strict fruitarian and doesn't eat any vegetables or grains. 她是一个严格的果食主义者，不吃任何蔬菜或谷物。|
fruitarian:/fruːˈteəriən/
adj.|果食的；水果主义的|Relating to a diet of only fruit.|He follows a fruitarian diet. 他遵循果食饮食。|
fruitarian:/fruːˈteəriən/
词源:|From fruit + -arian (like vegetarian).|
fruitarian:/fruːˈteəriən/
近义词:|vegan, vegetarian (in a broader sense)|
fruitarian:/fruːˈteəriən/
反义词:|omnivore, carnivore, herbivore (though not direct opposites)|
