defaulted: /dɪˈfɔːltɪd/ | v. | 违约，拖欠 | failed to fulfill an obligation, especially to repay a loan or appear in a court of law | The company defaulted on its loan payments.  该公司拖欠了贷款。|  The defendant defaulted and a warrant was issued for his arrest. 被告未出庭，已发出逮捕令。 | synonyms: failed, lapsed, reneged | antonyms: honored, fulfilled | verb

defaulted: /dɪˈfɔːltɪd/ | adj. |  违约的，拖欠的 | having failed to fulfill an obligation, especially to repay a loan or appear in a court of law | The defaulted loan caused significant problems for the bank.  这笔违约贷款给银行带来了严重问题。 | Many of the mortgages were defaulted. 许多抵押贷款都违约了。| synonyms: overdue, unpaid, delinquent | antonyms: paid, current | adjective
