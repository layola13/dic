gunsights: /ˈɡʌnˌsaɪts/ | n. | 瞄准器；枪的瞄准具 |A device on a gun that allows the user to aim accurately.  | The hunter adjusted his gunsights before taking the shot. 猎人射击前调整了枪的瞄准器。 | 近义词: sights, scope, crosshairs | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑"blind firing" – 盲射，指不使用瞄准具射击) | 词性: 名词

词源:  From "gun" + "sights," the plural of "sight" (in the sense of a device for aiming).  "Sight" ultimately comes from Old English *gesihþ, from Proto-Germanic *sekhwīthiz.
