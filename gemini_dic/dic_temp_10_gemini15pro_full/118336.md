merging:/ˈmɜːrdʒɪŋ/ | n. | 合并，融合 | The merging of the two companies created a larger, more powerful entity.  两家公司的合并创造了一个更大、更强大的实体。 | Example: The merging of the two rivers created a wider channel. 两条河流的汇合形成了一条更宽的河道。 | Synonyms: combining, uniting, integrating, blending | Antonyms: separating, dividing, splitting | noun
