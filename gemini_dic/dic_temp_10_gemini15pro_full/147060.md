overlegislate:/ˌoʊvərˈlɛdʒɪsleɪt/
verb
过度立法
to create too many laws, especially laws that are too detailed or specific, or that try to control too many aspects of people's lives
The government has been criticized for overlegislating in areas of personal freedom. 政府因在个人自由领域过度立法而受到批评。
Congress is often accused of overlegislating on minor issues. 国会经常被指责在小问题上过度立法。
overregulate, overgovern
underregulate
v.
