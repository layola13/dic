augmentatives:/ɔːɡˈmɛntətɪvz/ | n. | 增大词，放大词 | Words formed by adding a suffix or other element to a base word to indicate greater size, intensity, or importance. |  Example: "Gigantic" is an augmentative of "giant."  “巨大无比的”是“巨大的”的增大词。 | 近义词:intensifiers, magnifiers | 反义词:diminutives | 词性:noun
