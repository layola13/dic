indivisibility:/ˌɪndɪˌvɪzəˈbɪləti/| n. |不可分割性|The quality or state of being indivisible; incapable of being divided.|The indivisibility of the nation is a core principle.  国家的不可分割性是一项核心原则。|Synonyms: unity, wholeness, inseparability|Antonyms: divisibility, separability|noun

indivisible:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪzəbl/| adj. |不可分割的|Not capable of being divided.|Human rights are indivisible. 人权是不可分割的。|Synonyms: inseparable, unbreakable, whole|Antonyms: divisible, separable|adjective
