dissents:/dɪˈsɛnts/ | n. |异议，不同意|An expression of disagreement with a prevailing view or official decision. |Two justices dissented from the majority opinion. 两位法官对多数意见表示异议。|synonyms: disagreement, objection, opposition, dispute, demurral |antonyms: agreement, acceptance, concord, concurrence |noun

dissents:/dɪˈsɛnts/ | v. |不同意，持异议|(third-person singular simple present dissents, present participle dissenting, simple past and past participle dissented) To disagree with a prevailing view or official decision. |Several members dissented from the proposed course of action.  几位成员不同意提议的行动方案。|synonyms: disagree, object, oppose, dispute, demur |antonyms: agree, accept, concur, consent |verb
