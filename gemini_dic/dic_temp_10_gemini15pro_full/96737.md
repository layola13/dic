inelegantly:/ɪnˈɛlɪɡəntli/
adverb
不雅地，笨拙地
in an inelegant manner; without elegance; gracelessly.
She inelegantly tripped over the rug. 她笨拙地被地毯绊倒了。
近义词:awkwardly, clumsily, gracelessly
反义词:elegantly, gracefully, smoothly
词性:副词
