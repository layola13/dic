hurrays:/hʊˈreɪz/ | n. |  欢呼声；喝彩声 | An exclamation of joy or approval, typically shouted in unison. | The crowd erupted in hurrays when the team scored.  人群在球队得分时爆发出欢呼声。 | 近义词: cheers, applause, acclamations | 反义词: boos, jeers | 词性: 名词

词源: From hurrah, an alteration of huzzah, of uncertain origin.
