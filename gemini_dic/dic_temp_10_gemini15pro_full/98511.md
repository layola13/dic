instantiations:/ɪnˌstænʃiˈeɪʃənz/| n. |实例化；具体化|The process of creating a concrete instance of a class or object.  The act of creating a specific realization of an abstract concept or template. |例句：The software allows for multiple instantiations of the same object.  该软件允许多个相同对象的实例化。

例句：Different instantiations of the same genetic code can result in diverse phenotypes. 相同遗传密码的不同实例化可以导致不同的表型。|近义词：examples, implementations, manifestations, realizations|反义词: abstractions, generalizations |词性: noun
