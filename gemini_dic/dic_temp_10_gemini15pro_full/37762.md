compassionate:/kəmˈpæʃənət/ | adj. | 富有同情心的，表示怜悯的 | Feeling or showing sympathy and concern for others. |  She was a compassionate nurse who always put her patients first. 她是一位富有同情心的护士，总是把病人放在第一位。

|  He felt compassionate towards the homeless man. 他对这个无家可归的人感到同情。 | 近义词: sympathetic, empathetic, kind, caring, humane | 反义词: uncaring, indifferent, callous, cruel, heartless | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin *compassionatus*, past participle of compassionari "to feel pity," from Latin com- "with" + pati "to suffer."
