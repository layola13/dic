nondenominationalism:/ˌnɑːndɪˌnɑːmɪˈneɪʃənəlɪzəm/ | n. |非宗派主义，不分宗派的|The principle or practice of not being restricted to a particular religious denomination.  It emphasizes common ground and shared beliefs rather than specific doctrines that divide different sects. | Many couples opt for a nondenominational wedding ceremony. 许多夫妇选择举行不分宗派的婚礼。| The rise of nondenominationalism has led to greater religious tolerance. 非宗派主义的兴起带来了更大的宗教包容性。 | Synonyms: interdenominationalism, ecumenism | Antonyms: sectarianism, denominationalism | noun
