funerary:/ˈfjuːnərɛri/ (US), /ˈfjuːnərəri/ (UK)| adj.|丧葬的，葬礼的|Relating to or used in a funeral or burial.|例句：The museum has a fascinating collection of funerary artifacts.  博物馆收藏了引人入胜的丧葬文物。

例句：Funerary rites vary greatly from culture to culture. 不同的文化有不同的丧葬仪式。|近义词：mortuary, sepulchral, burial|反义词：festive, celebratory|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *funerarius, from Latin *funer-, *funeris “of a funeral,” from funus “funeral, burial, death.”
