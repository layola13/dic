imminentnesses:/ˈɪmɪnəntnəsɪz/| n. |紧迫性，迫近性|The state of being imminent; the quality of being about to happen.  It refers to multiple instances of something being about to occur.|The imminentnesses of several deadlines caused her considerable stress. 多个截止日期的临近让她压力很大。|近义词：urgency, nearness, proximity|反义词：remoteness, distance, delay|noun
