fledgelings:/ˈflɛdʒlɪŋz/| n. |雏鸟;  刚会飞的幼鸟;  新手;  初出茅庐的人;|A young bird that has just fledged or become capable of flight.  A person or organization that is immature, inexperienced, or underdeveloped.|The fledglings were ready to leave the nest. 这些雏鸟准备好离开鸟巢了。|Synonyms:  newcomer, beginner, novice, trainee, apprentice, rookie,  greenhorn |Antonyms: expert, veteran, professional|noun


fledgling:/ˈflɛdʒlɪŋ/| adj. |羽翼未丰的;  稚嫩的;  缺乏经验的;|New and not yet developed or experienced. |The company is a fledgling enterprise, full of promise. 这家公司是一个羽翼未丰的企业，充满了希望。|Synonyms: nascent, budding, emergent, developing,  incipient|Antonyms: mature, established, experienced|adjective
