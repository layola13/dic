humanitarianisms:/hjuːˌmænɪˈtɛəriənɪzəmz/
|n.|人道主义（复数）|The various philosophies and practices related to humanitarian aid and action, often reflecting different approaches, priorities, and values.|例句：The conference explored different humanitarianisms in the context of the refugee crisis.  这次会议探讨了在难民危机背景下的不同人道主义。|例句：Debates around humanitarianisms often focus on the role of neutrality and impartiality.  围绕人道主义的辩论通常集中在中立性和公正性的作用上。|近义词：humanitarian principles, humanitarian doctrines|反义词：cruelty, inhumanity|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From humanitarian + -ism (suffix forming nouns of action, state, condition, doctrine, etc.).  Ultimately from Latin *humanitas 'human nature, kindness'.
