ineluctability:/ˌɪnɪˌlʌktəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可避免性；必然性 | The quality of being impossible to avoid or prevent. |  The ineluctability of death is a sobering thought.  死亡的不可避免性是一个发人深省的想法。 | inevitability, certainty, unavoidability | escapability, avoidability | noun

ineluctable:/ˌɪnɪˈlʌktəbl/ | adj. | 不可避免的；必然的 | Not to be avoided, changed, or resisted. |  His argument pointed to the ineluctable conclusion that the project was doomed to fail.  他的论点指向了这个项目注定要失败的不可避免的结论。 | inevitable, unavoidable, inescapable | avoidable, preventable, escapable | adjective
