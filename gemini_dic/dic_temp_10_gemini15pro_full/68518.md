fancer:/ˈfænsiər/ (comparative of fancy)| adj.|更花哨的，更奇特的|More elaborate, decorative, or ornamented than something else considered fancy. |例句：Her dress was fancier than mine. 她的裙子比我的更花哨。

例句：The new hotel is fancier than the old one. 新酒店比旧酒店更豪华。


近义词：more elaborate, more ornate, more decorative, more embellished|反义词:plainer, simpler, less ornate|词性:形容词
