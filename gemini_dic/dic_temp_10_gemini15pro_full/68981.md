fatiguable:/fəˈtiːɡəbl/
adjective
易疲劳的
Susceptible to fatigue; easily tired.
The hikers, though young, proved quite fatiguable after the steep climb. 虽然这些徒步旅行者很年轻，但在陡峭的攀登之后，他们很容易疲劳。
fatiguable:/fəˈtiːɡəbl/
adjective
易疲劳的
Prone to experiencing tiredness or exhaustion; readily becoming weary.
He has a fatiguable nature and needs frequent breaks. 他容易疲劳，需要经常休息。
fatiguable:/fəˈtiːɡəbl/
adjective
易疲劳的
Easily made tired or exhausted; susceptible to fatigue.
The demanding schedule made even the most energetic employees fatiguable. 要求苛刻的时间表使精力最充沛的员工也感到疲惫。


Synonyms: tireable, exhaustible, wearying
Antonyms: tireless, indefatigable, energetic
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French fatigable, from fatiguer (to fatigue) + -able (suffix indicating capability or susceptibility).  Ultimately from Latin fatigare (to weary).
