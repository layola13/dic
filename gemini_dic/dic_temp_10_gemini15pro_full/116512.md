matey:/ˈmeɪti/| n.|伙计，朋友，老兄 (informal)|A friendly way of addressing someone, especially a man. Used mainly in British English.|例句：Ahoy there, matey!  你好啊，伙计！

例句：What's up, matey?  怎么了，老兄？

近义词：friend, pal, buddy, chum, fella (informal), chap (British, informal)|反义词:enemy, foe|词性:noun
