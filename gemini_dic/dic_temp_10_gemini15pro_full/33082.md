chrismation:/krɪzˈmeɪʃən/
n.
涂聖油禮; 傅油禮
the act of anointing with chrism, especially as a confirmation of initiation into the Christian Church.
例句：The chrismation ceremony was a deeply moving experience for the young converts.  年轻皈依者们在涂聖油禮上深受感动。

例句：Chrismation is an important sacrament in many Christian denominations. 在许多基督教派中，傅油禮是一项重要的圣礼。

近义词：anointing, confirmation
反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, but one could consider "desecration" as a contrasting concept)
词性: noun
