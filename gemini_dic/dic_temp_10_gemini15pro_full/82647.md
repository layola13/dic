guerdonable:/ɡɜːrdənəbl/ | adj. | 可酬谢的，值得奖赏的 | Worthy of reward; deserving of recompense. |  Her selfless dedication to the cause was truly guerdonable. 她对事业的无私奉献是 truly guerdonable. | Synonyms: rewardable, compensable, worthy | Antonyms: unworthy, undeserving | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *guerredonable, from guerredon (“reward”), from Frankish *widarlōn (“recompense”). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *widar (“against”) + *lōn (“loan”).
