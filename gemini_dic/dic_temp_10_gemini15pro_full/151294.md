pari:/ˈpɑːri/ | n. | 平局，相等；(尤指赌博中的)平分赌注 | A bet in which the stakes of all the players are pooled and divided equally among the winners. | Example 1: The poker game ended in a pari, with each player getting their initial bet back.  扑克游戏以平局结束，每个玩家都拿回了最初的赌注。

Example 2:  They made a pari on the outcome of the election. 他们就选举结果打了个赌，赌注平分。 |Synonyms: tie, draw, dead heat | Antonyms:  win, loss | Noun
