nonparties:/ˌnɑːnˈpɑːrtiz/
n.
非当事人；非签约方；局外人
People or entities not involved in a particular legal agreement, dispute, or situation.
The nonparties to the agreement were not bound by its terms.  协议的非缔约方不受其条款的约束。
The judge ordered the nonparties to leave the courtroom.  法官命令非当事人离开法庭。
synonyms: outsiders, bystanders, third parties
antonyms: parties, participants, litigants
noun

Etymology->
From non- + party.  Non- is a prefix meaning "not." Party, in this legal context, refers to a person or group involved in a legal agreement or dispute.
