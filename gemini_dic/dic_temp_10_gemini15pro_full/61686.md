emo:/ˈiːmoʊ/ (US), /ˈɛːməʊ/ (UK)| adj. n.| 情绪硬核,  情绪化的（人）|A style of rock music characterized by melodic musicianship and expressive, often confessional lyrics.|He's really into the emo scene. 他真的很喜欢情绪硬核.|近义词：emotional, melancholic, goth|反义词: cheerful, optimistic|词性: adjective, noun


Etymology: Shortened from "emotional hardcore," a subgenre of hardcore punk.
