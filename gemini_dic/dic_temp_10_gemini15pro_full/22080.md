bollen:/ˈbɒlən/ (UK) /ˈboʊlən/ (US)| adj.|肿胀的，膨胀的|Swollen, inflated, distended; enlarged beyond normal size.  Often used in a medical context.  Can also be used metaphorically.|His ankle was bollen after the fall. 他的脚踝摔倒后肿了起来。|The river was bollen with floodwater. 河水因洪水而暴涨。|近义词：swollen, inflated, distended, enlarged|反义词：shrunken, deflated, contracted|词性:形容词
词源: Middle English *bollen*, from Old Norse *bolginn*, past participle of *bólga* (“to swell”), from Proto-Germanic *bulganą* (“to swell”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰelǵʰ- (“to swell”). Cognate with German *bäulig* (“pot-bellied”) and English *belly*.
