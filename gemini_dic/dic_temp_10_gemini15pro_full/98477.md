instal:/ɪnˈstɔːl/ (British), /ɪnˈstɑːl/ (American)| vt.|安装，设置|To put a piece of equipment into position so that it is ready to use; to make a new program ready to use on a computer system. |The plumber installed a new washing machine. 水管工安装了一台新的洗衣机。

The IT technician installed the new software on my computer. IT技术员在我的电脑上安装了新软件。|近义词：fit, set up, mount, put in, place|反义词: uninstall, remove|词性: 动词

Etymology: From Old French *estaler, installer "to fix, establish," from Latin stallum "stable, stall," figuratively "position, station."
