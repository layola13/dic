insected:/ɪnˈsɛktɪd/
adjective
充满昆虫的；被昆虫侵扰的
infested with or divided by insects
例句：The old house was insected with termites.  这栋老房子里到处都是白蚁。

例句：The insected wood was crumbling. 被昆虫侵蚀的木头正在腐烂。
近义词：infested, ridden
反义词: clean, uninfested
词性: adjective
