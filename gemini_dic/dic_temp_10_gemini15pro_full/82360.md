growls:/ɡraʊlz/ | v. |咆哮；低吼；（肚子）咕咕叫|A low, guttural sound, typically made by an animal to express anger, aggression, or hunger. | The dog growls at strangers. 狗对着陌生人咆哮。| synonyms: snarls, roars, grumbles | antonyms: whimpers, whispers | verb

growl:/ɡraʊl/ | n. |咆哮；低吼；（肚子）咕咕叫|A low, guttural sound, typically made by an animal to express anger, aggression, or hunger. | The lion's growl echoed through the jungle. 狮子的咆哮声回荡在丛林中。| synonyms: snarl, roar, grumble | antonyms: whimper, whisper | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *growlen, *groulen, from Old Norse *grylla (“to grunt, grumble”), from Proto-Germanic *grulōną (“to growl, grumble”), from Proto-Indo-European *gʰer- (“to roar, shout”).
