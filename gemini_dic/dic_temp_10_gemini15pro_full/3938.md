agro-ecological:/ˌæɡroʊˌiːkəˈlɒdʒɪkl/
adjective
农业生态的
Relating to or concerned with the application of ecological principles to agriculture.  Applying ecological concepts and principles to the design and management of sustainable agricultural systems.
例句：
Agro-ecological farming practices can improve soil health.
农业生态耕作方法可以改善土壤健康。

The agro-ecological approach considers the complex interactions within the farming system.
农业生态方法考虑了农业系统内的复杂相互作用。

近义词：sustainable agriculture, ecological farming, biodynamic
反义词：conventional agriculture, industrial agriculture
词性：adjective


agro-ecology:/ˌæɡroʊɪˈkɒlədʒi/
noun
农业生态学
The study of ecological processes applied to agricultural production systems. The application of ecological science to the design and management of sustainable agroecosystems.
例句：
Agro-ecology promotes biodiversity within farming systems.
农业生态学促进农业系统内的生物多样性。

The principles of agro-ecology can help to reduce the environmental impact of agriculture.
农业生态学原理有助于减少农业对环境的影响。

近义词：ecological agriculture, sustainable farming
反义词：conventional agriculture, industrial agriculture
词性：noun


词源：
"agro-" comes from the Greek "agrós" meaning "field."
"-ecological" comes from "ecology," which combines the Greek "oikos" (house, dwelling) and "-logia" (study of).
