extramarital: /ˌekstrəˈmærɪtəl/ | adj. | 婚外的，婚外情的 | Relating to a sexual relationship between a married person and someone other than their spouse. |例句：He had an extramarital affair. 他有婚外情。|例句：Extramarital relationships are often condemned. 婚外情经常受到谴责。|近义词：adulterous, illicit |反义词：marital, conjugal |词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From extra- (outside of) + marital (relating to marriage).  "Extra-" comes from Latin, meaning "outside" or "beyond." "Marital" comes from Latin "maritus" meaning "husband, married man."
