nullify: /ˈnʌlɪfaɪ/ | v. | 使无效，取消，废除 | To make legally null and void; invalidate.  To deprive of value or effectiveness; make futile. | The court nullified the election results. 法院宣布选举结果无效。The contract was nullified due to a breach of terms.  由于违反条款，合同被废除。 | synonyms: invalidate, annul, void, repeal, abolish, cancel | antonyms: validate, confirm, ratify, uphold | verb
