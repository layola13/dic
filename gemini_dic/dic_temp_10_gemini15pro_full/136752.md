nonsententiously:/ˌnɒnsenˈtenʃəsli/ | adv. | 简洁地，不带说教意味地 | In a manner that avoids expressing opinions or judgments, especially moral ones; concisely and without moralizing. | He explained the situation nonsententiously, simply stating the facts. 他简洁地解释了情况，只是陈述了事实。|  She nonsententiously accepted his apology. 她简洁地接受了他的道歉。 | 近义词: concisely, succinctly, briefly, simply | 反义词: verbosely, tediously, moralistically, sententiously | 词性: 副词
