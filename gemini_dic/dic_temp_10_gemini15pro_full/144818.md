over-idealistic:/ˌoʊvər aɪˌdiːəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
过于理想化的
excessively idealistic; unrealistically optimistic.
例句：His plans were over-idealistic and failed to take into account the practical difficulties involved. 他的计划过于理想化，没有考虑到实际存在的困难。

例句：She has an over-idealistic view of marriage. 她对婚姻抱有过于理想化的看法。

近义词：unrealistic, impractical, utopian, quixotic
反义词：realistic, pragmatic, cynical
词性: adjective
