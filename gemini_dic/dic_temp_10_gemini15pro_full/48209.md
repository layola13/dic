ETYMOLOGY->
de- (prefix): meaning "reverse" or "remove," from Latin
conglomerate (verb): from Latin conglomeratus, past participle of conglomerare "to roll together," from com- "together" + glomerare "to wind into a ball"
-ed (suffix): forming the past participle of verbs
-ate (suffix): used to form verbs, nouns, and adjectives
