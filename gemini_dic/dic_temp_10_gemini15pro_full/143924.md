outpromised:/ˌaʊtˈprɒmɪst/
Verb
许诺过多，承诺超过(别人或自己)的能力
To have made more or greater promises than someone else or than one can deliver.
He outpromised his rivals in the election campaign and now faces a tough time delivering on his pledges. 他在竞选活动中比对手做出了更多的承诺，现在面临着兑现承诺的艰难时期。
The company outpromised itself and couldn't meet the high expectations it had set. 该公司承诺过多，无法达到自己设定的高期望值。
Synonyms: overpromise, overcommit
Antonyms: underpromise, undercommit
词性: 动词
