figurability:/ˌfɪɡjərəˈbɪləti/
n. |可塑性; 可被描绘性; 能被塑造性|The figurability of clay makes it a favorite medium for sculptors.  黏土的可塑性使它成为雕塑家最喜欢的媒介。|例句：The artist explored the figurability of light and shadow in his paintings.  这位艺术家在他的绘画中探索了光影的可塑性。|近义词：malleability, plasticity, ductility|反义词：inflexibility, rigidity|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *figurabilis, from Latin figurare (“to shape, form, fashion”), from figura (“form, shape, figure”).  Suffix -ity forms abstract nouns denoting a state or quality.
