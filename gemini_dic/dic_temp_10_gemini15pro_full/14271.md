autogenic:/ˌɔːtəˈdʒɛnɪk/
adjective
自生的，自发的
self-produced or generated within the body
Autogenic training is a relaxation technique.  自体训练是一种放松技巧。

Autogenic therapies can help manage stress. 自生疗法可以帮助控制压力。
synonym: endogenous, self-generated, spontaneous
antonym: exogenous, external

词性: adjective
