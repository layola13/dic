encasing:/ɪnˈkeɪsɪŋ/
verb
包裹，封装
The delicate instrument was encased in a protective covering.  这台精密仪器被包裹在一个保护罩中。
Present participle: encasing
Past tense: encased
Past participle: encased
The sword was encased in a leather sheath.  这把剑被装在一个皮革剑鞘里。
近义词: enclosing, surrounding, covering, sheathing
反义词: uncovering, exposing, revealing
词性: verb (also used as adjective, e.g., encasing material)
