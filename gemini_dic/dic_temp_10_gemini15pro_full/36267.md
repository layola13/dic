cohabitant:/koʊˈhæbɪtənt/| n. |同居者，同住者|A person who lives with another, especially in a sexual or romantic relationship.|例句：They were cohabitants for five years before they got married. 他们在结婚前同居了五年。

例句：My cohabitant and I share the household chores. 我和我的同居者分担家务。

近义词：housemate, roommate, partner, live-in partner|反义词：spouse, non-resident|词性：noun
