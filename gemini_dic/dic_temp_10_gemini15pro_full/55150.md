dissects:/daɪˈsekts/ /dɪˈsekts/| v. |解剖，仔细分析|To cut apart (an animal body, plant, etc.) to examine the structure, typically for scientific purposes; to examine or analyze something in minute detail.|The biologist dissected the frog to study its anatomy. 生物学家解剖了青蛙来研究它的解剖结构。|The lawyer dissected the witness's testimony, revealing inconsistencies. 律师仔细分析了证人的证词，揭示了其中的矛盾之处。|Synonyms: analyze, examine, scrutinize, investigate|Antonyms: synthesize, combine, assemble|verb
