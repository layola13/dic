nonreactionaries:/ˌnɒnriˈækʃənɛriz/
noun
非反动分子；不反对变革者
People who are not opposed to change or progress, and are generally open to new ideas.
The nonreactionaries in the group embraced the proposed reforms.  小组中不反对变革的人欣然接受了提议的改革。
Synonyms: progressives, liberals, reformers
Antonyms: reactionaries, conservatives, traditionalists
词性:n.
