osmoses:/ɒzˈməʊsiːz/ /ɑːzˈmoʊsiːz/
n.
渗透；渗透作用
The process by which a solvent passes through a semipermeable membrane from a less concentrated solution into a more concentrated one, equalizing the concentrations on both sides of the membrane.
Water moves into the roots of plants by osmosis. 水通过渗透作用进入植物的根部。

近义词:diffusion, infiltration
反义词:(No direct antonym, but consider "reverse osmosis")
词性:noun
