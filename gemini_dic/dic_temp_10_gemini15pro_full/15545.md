baggiest:/ˈbæɡiɪst/
adjective
最宽松的
Superlative form of baggy: most baggy.  Meaning the most loose-fitting or hanging loosely.
His baggiest pants were the most comfortable for lounging around the house. 他最宽松的裤子是他在家闲逛时最舒服的。
The clown wore the baggiest pants I've ever seen.  那个小丑穿着我见过的最宽松的裤子。
近义词: loosest, roomiest
反义词: tightest, snuggest
词性: adjective
