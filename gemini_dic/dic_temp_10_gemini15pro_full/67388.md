exterminate: /ɪkˈstɜːmɪneɪt/ /ɪkˈstɝːmɪneɪt/
verb
消灭，根除
to kill all the members of a group of people or animals
例句：The government is trying to exterminate the rats in the city. 政府正试图消灭城市中的老鼠。

例句：They hired a company to exterminate the termites in their house.  他们雇了一家公司来消灭他们家里的白蚁。


近义词：annihilate, eradicate, destroy, wipe out
反义词：preserve, conserve, protect
词性：verb
