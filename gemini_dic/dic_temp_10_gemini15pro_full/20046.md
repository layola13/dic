biophilia:/ˌbaɪoʊˈfɪliə/ | n. | 亲生命性，热爱生命的天性 |  An innate and genetically determined affinity of human beings with the natural world.  It suggests that humans possess an inherent tendency to seek connections with nature and other forms of life. |  Her work explored the concept of biophilia and its implications for urban design. 她的工作探索了亲生命性的概念及其对城市设计的意义。

| Example:  Studies suggest that exposure to nature can enhance well-being, supporting the biophilia hypothesis. 研究表明，接触大自然可以提升幸福感，这支持了亲生命性假说。 |  Synonym:  nature affinity, love of nature | Antonym:  biophobia | noun
