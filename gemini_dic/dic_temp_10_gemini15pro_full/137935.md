nonvulgarity:/ˌnɑːnˈvʌlˈɡærɪti/ | n. | 非粗俗；高雅；典雅 |  The nonvulgarity of her language impressed everyone. |  She spoke with a certain nonvulgarity that made her stand out. | 近义词: refinement, elegance, decency, propriety | 反义词: vulgarity, coarseness, crudeness | 词性: 名词

词源:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + vulgarity (from Late Latin vulgaritas, from vulgaris "common").  Essentially meaning "the absence of vulgarity."
