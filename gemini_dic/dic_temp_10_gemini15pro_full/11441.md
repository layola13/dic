一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? = 你怎么想？
What does she want? = 她想要什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing right now? = 他现在正在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned? = 你学到了什么？
What has she eaten today? = 她今天吃了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on? = 你一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 pm? = 晚上8点你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? = 她参加聚会时穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I called? = 在我打电话之前你做了什么？
What had they decided before the meeting? = 会议前他们决定了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the interruption? =  被打断之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about? = 他们一直在争论什么？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? = 你下周末要做什么？
What will the price be? = 价格是多少？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be presenting at the conference? = 他们将在会议上展示什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by Friday? = 到星期五你将完成什么？
What will they have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底他们将取得什么成就？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been studying for five years? = 到那时你将已经学习五年什么了？
What will she have been working on by the time she retires? = 到她退休的时候，她将一直致力于什么？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
I wish I knew what to say. = 我希望我知道该说什么。

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being built over there? = 那边正在建什么？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the summer? = 你暑假的计划是什么？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
There's nothing what I want here. = 这里没有我想要的东西。
