blancard:/blɑ̃ˈkaːr/ | n. | 白卡; 空白表格; 空白支票 | A blank document or form, often used figuratively to represent unlimited freedom or authority. | He was given a blancard to redesign the company's image. 他被给予一张空白支票来重新设计公司的形象。 |  Synonyms: blank check, blank slate, carte blanche | Antonyms: restriction, limitation | noun

From French *blancard*, from *blanc* ("white") + *-ard* (masculine suffix).
