maximalism:/ˈmæksɪməlɪzəm/| n.| 极繁主义；最大化主义 |A philosophy or style that emphasizes abundance and excess, often characterized by the use of many different colors, textures, patterns, and objects.|Her apartment is a testament to maximalism, filled with vibrant colors, bold patterns, and an eclectic mix of furniture.| 近义词:excess, extravagance, opulence| 反义词:minimalism, simplicity, austerity| noun

maximalist:/ˈmæksɪməlɪst/| n. adj.| 极繁主义者；最大化主义者 |A person who believes in or practices maximalism, or a characteristic of maximalism.|As a maximalist, she embraced bold colors and patterns in her artwork. |The maximalist decor of the room was overwhelming.|近义词:extremist|反义词:minimalist| noun, adjective

maximally:/ˈmæksɪməli/ | adv. | 最大程度地; 尽最大可能地 |To the greatest possible extent; in the highest degree.|The system is designed to maximally utilize available resources.| He trained maximally for the marathon.| 近义词:fully, completely, utterly| 反义词:minimally, slightly| adverb
