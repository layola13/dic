crazier:/ˈkreɪziər/ (US) /ˈkreɪzɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更疯狂的，更古怪的|Comparative form of "crazy," meaning more insane, more eccentric, or more intensely enthusiastic.|She's even crazier than I thought. 她比我想象的更疯狂。|He's crazier about her now than ever before. 他现在比以往任何时候都更迷恋她。|近义词：madder, wilder, more insane|反义词：saner, more rational|词性:形容词

crazy:/ˈkreɪzi/ (US) /ˈkreɪzi/ (UK)| adj.|疯狂的，古怪的，热衷的|Insane; mentally deranged; eccentric; intensely enthusiastic.|He drove like crazy to get there on time. 他为了准时到达而疯狂开车。|She's crazy about horses. 她对马非常着迷。|词源：From Old French *crase* "cracked," past participle of *cresser* "to break," from Latin *quassare* "to shake, shatter."|
