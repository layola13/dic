cokewold: /ˈkoʊkwɒld/
noun
戴绿帽子的人；妻子有外遇的男人
a man whose wife has committed adultery
He felt like a cokewold after discovering his wife's affair. 他发现妻子有外遇后觉得自己像个戴绿帽子的傻瓜。
Synonyms: cuckold
Antonyms: faithful husband
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *cokewold, from Old French coqu, cucu "cuckoo" (referring to the cuckoo's habit of laying its eggs in other birds' nests) + -wald "husband," of uncertain origin.
