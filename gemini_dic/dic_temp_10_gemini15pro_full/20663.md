blacksnake:/ˈblækˌsneɪk/| n. |黑蛇，黑游蛇|A common nonvenomous snake, typically black or dark gray in color, found in North America. |The blacksnake slithered through the grass. 黑蛇在草丛中滑行。| The farmer killed a blacksnake in his barn. 农民在谷仓里杀死了一条黑蛇。|近义词：racer, rat snake|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
Etymology: From black + snake.  The name derives from the snake's predominantly black coloration.
