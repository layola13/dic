cced: (This word "cced" isn't a standard dictionary word. It's a shortened form of "carbon copied" used in emails and letters.)

/ˌsiːˈsiːd/ | v. | 抄送 | To send a copy of an email or letter to someone.  Derived from "carbon copy," a method of making copies using carbon paper. | Example: I cced the manager on the email so he's aware of the issue. 我把这封邮件抄送给了经理，让他知道这件事。 | Example:  She was cced on all correspondence related to the project. 所有与该项目相关的信件都抄送给了她。 | Synonym: copied, included | Antonym: excluded | verb
