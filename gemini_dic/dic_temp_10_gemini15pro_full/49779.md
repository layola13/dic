demonic:/dɪˈmɒnɪk/ (US) /diːˈmɒnɪk/ (UK)| adj.|恶魔的；魔鬼似的；着魔的|Of or like a demon; influenced or seemingly influenced by a demon. demonic:/dɪˈmɒnɪk/ (US) /diːˈmɒnɪk/ (UK)| adj.|超凡的；非凡的|Exhibiting extraordinarily great power, energy, or skill.|He played with demonic energy. 他以超凡的精力演奏。|Synonyms: exceptional, extraordinary, phenomenal, prodigious, superhuman|Antonyms: ordinary, mediocre, average|adjective
