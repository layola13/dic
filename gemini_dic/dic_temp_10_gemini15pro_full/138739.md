numbless:/ˈnʌmlɪs/
adjective
麻木的；失去感觉的
lacking sensation, especially the ability to feel pain.
After the accident, his arm was numbless. 事故发生后，他的手臂麻木了。

He felt numbless with grief. 他悲痛得麻木了。

synonym: numb, insensible, anesthetized
antonym: sensitive, feeling, aware
词性: 形容词
