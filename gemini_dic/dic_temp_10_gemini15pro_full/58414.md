duboisine: /djuːˈbɔɪziːn/ | n. | 杜波伊辛，东莨菪碱 | An alkaloid drug, derived from the corkwood tree (Duboisia myoporoides), with anticholinergic properties, used to treat motion sickness and Parkinson's disease.  It is a synonym for scopolamine. |  Duboisine can be used to dilate the pupils during eye examinations.  杜波伊辛可用于在眼科检查中扩张瞳孔。

Duboisine patches are sometimes prescribed for motion sickness.  有时会开杜波伊辛贴片来治疗晕动病。 | 近义词: scopolamine, hyoscine | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific chemical compound.  One might consider cholinergic drugs as having opposing effects.) | 词性: noun
