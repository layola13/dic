nonresuscitative: /ˌnɒnriːˈsʌsɪteɪtɪv/ /ˌnɑːnriːˈsʌsɪteɪtɪv/
| adj. | 非复苏的，不进行心肺复苏的 |  Relating to medical treatment that does not involve attempting to revive a patient who has stopped breathing or whose heart has stopped beating. | The patient had a nonresuscitative order in place.  病人已下达了不进行心肺复苏的医嘱。| 近义词: do-not-resuscitate (DNR), comfort care | 反义词: resuscitative | 词性: adjective

nonresuscitative: /ˌnɒnriːˈsʌsɪteɪtɪv/ /ˌnɑːnriːˈsʌsɪteɪtɪv/
| n. | 非复苏措施 | Medical treatment that focuses on comfort rather than resuscitation. |  The family opted for nonresuscitative care for their terminally ill loved one.  这家人为他们病入膏肓的亲人选择了非复苏性护理。| 近义词: comfort care, palliative care | 反义词: resuscitation | 词性: noun
