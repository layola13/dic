gastriloquist:/ɡæˈstrɪləkwɪst/ /ɡæˈstrɪləkwɪst/
noun
腹语术表演者
a person who can speak in such a way that their voice seems to be coming from somewhere else, typically a dummy
The gastriloquist made the dummy appear to be talking. 腹语表演者使木偶看起来像在说话。
synonym:ventriloquist
antonym: N/A
词性: noun

gastriloquism:/ɡæˈstrɪləkwɪzəm/ /ɡæˈstrɪləkwɪzəm/
noun
腹语术
the art of speaking in such a way that the voice seems to be coming from somewhere else, typically a dummy
He learned gastriloquism as a child. 他小时候学习了腹语术。
synonym:ventriloquism
antonym: N/A
词性: noun

gastriloquial:/ˌɡæstrɪˈloʊkwiəl/ /ˌɡæstrɪˈləʊkwiəl/
adjective
腹语的
relating to or characterized by ventriloquism
He gave a gastriloquial performance. 他表演了腹语。
synonym:ventriloquial
antonym: N/A
词性: adjective
