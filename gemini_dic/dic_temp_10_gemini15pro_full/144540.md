outvoiced:/ˌaʊtˈvɔɪst/ | adj. | 胜过…的声音的；被压倒声音的 |  Having one's voice overpowered; drowned out or surpassed in vocal power or volume. |  The quieter student's opinions were often outvoiced by the more assertive members of the class.  这个比较安静的学生的意见经常被班上更自信的成员盖过。 | synonyms: drowned out, muffled, subdued, overpowered | antonyms: amplified, loud, clear, audible | adjective

outvoiced:/ˌaʊtˈvɔɪst/ |  v. |  胜过…的声音；压倒…的声音 | To speak louder than someone else, so their voice cannot be heard; to overpower or drown out someone's voice. | The lead singer outvoiced the backing vocals. 主唱的声音盖过了伴唱。| synonyms: drown out, overpower, muffle, subdue | antonyms: amplify, listen to, yield to | verb
