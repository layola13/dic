feel-like-oneself:/fiːl laɪk wʌnˈsɛlf/ | v. | 感觉像自己；感觉舒适自在；恢复正常 | To feel healthy and happy again after an illness or period of unhappiness. |  After a week of flu, I'm finally starting to feel like myself again.  流感一周后，我终于开始感觉像自己了。 | 近义词: feel well, feel normal, be back to normal, be oneself | 反义词: feel ill, feel unwell, be out of sorts | 词性: 动词短语
