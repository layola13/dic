parousia:/pəˈruːziə/ /pəˈruːʒə/
|n.|降临，显现，(尤指基督的)再临|The Second Coming, the final and glorious return of Christ to earth.|The church awaits the parousia of Christ.|近义词：advent, arrival, appearance, manifestation|反义词：departure, absence|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek παρουσία (parousía, “presence, arrival”), from πάρειμι (páreimi, “I am present”), from παρά (pará, “beside, near”) + εἰμί (eimí, “I am”).
