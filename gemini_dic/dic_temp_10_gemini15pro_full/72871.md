follicle:/ˈfɒlɪk(ə)l/ (UK), /ˈfɑːlɪk(ə)l/ (US)| n. | 囊泡; 卵泡; 毛囊 | A small cavity or sac in a plant or animal body, containing a developing cell or cells, such as a hair follicle or an ovarian follicle. |  The doctor examined the hair follicles on her scalp. 医生检查了她头皮上的毛囊。| synonyms: sac, cavity, vesicle, gland | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider "pore" in some contexts, but not a true opposite) | noun

follicles:/ˈfɒlɪk(ə)lz/ (UK), /ˈfɑːlɪk(ə)lz/ (US)| n. | 囊泡; 卵泡; 毛囊 (follicle 的复数形式)|  Plural form of follicle. Refers to multiple small cavities or sacs. |  The follicles in her ovaries were developing normally. 她卵巢中的卵泡发育正常。| synonyms: sacs, cavities, vesicles, glands | antonyms: (No direct antonym) | noun
