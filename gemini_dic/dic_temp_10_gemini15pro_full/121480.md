mislabelling:/ˌmɪsˈleɪbəlɪŋ/
noun
错误标记，贴错标签
The act of putting an incorrect label on something.
The mislabelling of the product led to a massive recall. 产品的错误标签导致大规模召回。
mislabeling (US spelling)
correct labelling, accurate labelling
noun

mislabel:/ˌmɪsˈleɪbl/
verb
贴错标签，错误标记
To put an incorrect label on something.
The factory mislabelled several boxes of cereal. 工厂给几箱麦片贴错了标签。
mislabel (US spelling)
label correctly, label accurately
