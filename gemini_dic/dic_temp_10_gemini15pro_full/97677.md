inhospitable:/ɪnˈhɒspɪtəbl/ (UK), /ɪnˈhɑːspɪtəbl/ (US)| adj.|不友好的，不好客的；（环境）恶劣的，荒凉的|Not welcoming or generous; not providing shelter or sustenance; (of an environment) harsh and difficult to live in.|The desert is an inhospitable environment for most plants.  沙漠对大多数植物来说都是不友好的环境。| The local people were surprisingly inhospitable to the tourists. 当地人对游客出奇地不友好。|近义词：unwelcoming, unfriendly, harsh, barren, desolate|反义词：hospitable, welcoming, friendly, fertile, lush|词性:形容词

词源:
From Latin *inhospitalis*, from in- "not" + hospitalis "hospitable."
