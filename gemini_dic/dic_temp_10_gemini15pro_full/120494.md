minuet:/ˌmɪnjuˈɛt/ | n. |小步舞；小步舞曲|A slow, graceful dance in triple time, popular in the 17th and 18th centuries.  A piece of music for such a dance, typically forming a movement in a suite.|The orchestra played a beautiful minuet.  管弦乐队演奏了一首优美的小步舞曲。|Synonyms: dance, waltz (loosely) |Antonyms: jig, reel (in terms of tempo and style)|noun
