nonretroactivity:/ˌnɒnˌrɛtrəʊækˈtɪvɪti/ | n. | 不溯及既往；非追溯性 |  The principle that a law or decision will not apply to events that occurred before its enactment or issuance.  | The contract included a clause ensuring the nonretroactivity of any changes in pricing.  合同中包含一项条款，确保任何价格变化都不溯及既往。 |  non-retroactivity, irretroactivity | retroactivity, retrospectivity | noun
