desktop:/ˈdɛskˌtɑːp/ (US), /ˈdɛsktɒp/ (UK)| n. |桌面；台式电脑|The surface of a desk, or the main display screen of a computer, especially one with graphical icons representing files and programs.|I keep a lot of files on my desktop. 我的桌面上有很多文件。|synonyms: computer screen, monitor, tabletop, work surface|antonyms: floor, underside|noun

desktop:/ˈdɛskˌtɑːp/ (US), /ˈdɛsktɒp/ (UK)| adj. |台式的；桌面的|Relating to a desktop computer or the main display screen of a computer.|I prefer a desktop computer to a laptop. 我更喜欢台式电脑而不是笔记本电脑。|synonyms: tabletop|antonyms: portable, laptop, mobile|adjective
