fluorite:/ˈflʊəraɪt/ /ˈflɔːraɪt/| n. |萤石|A common mineral, calcium fluoride (CaF2), occurring in variously colored cubic crystals and used as a flux and in making some kinds of glass and enamel.|Fluorite is often used in the production of lenses. 萤石常用于透镜的生产。|Synonyms: fluorspar|Antonyms: None|Noun
