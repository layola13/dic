interfacer:/ˈɪntərˌfeɪsər/| n.|接口器，接口程序，界面装置|A person or thing that interfaces, especially a piece of hardware or software that connects two systems.|The interfacer allows the two different computer systems to communicate.|近义词：connector, link, adapter, bridge|反义词:separator, isolator|词性:noun

interfacer:/ˈɪntərˌfeɪsər/| n.|接口器，接口程序，界面装置|A person who interacts with others, especially in a coordinating role.|She acted as the interfacer between the development team and the client.|近义词：liaison, coordinator, intermediary|反义词:divider, isolator|词性:noun
