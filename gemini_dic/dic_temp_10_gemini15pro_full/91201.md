hydriad:/ˈhaɪdriəd/| n. |水螅，水螅体|A small freshwater animal related to jellyfish and corals, having a cylindrical body and tentacles. |例句：The hydriad extended its tentacles to capture prey. 水螅伸出触须捕捉猎物。

例句：Hydriads can reproduce both sexually and asexually. 水螅可以进行有性和无性繁殖。|近义词：polyp|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ὕδρα (húdra, “water serpent, Hydra”) + -iad. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *wódr̥ (“water”).
