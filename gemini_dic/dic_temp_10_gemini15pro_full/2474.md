adjutage: /ˈædʒətɑːʒ/ /əˈdʒuːtɪdʒ/ | n. | 喷嘴，管嘴；放水管 | A tube attached to a container of liquid to regulate its flow.  A nozzle or spout. | The adjutage on the fire hose allowed for a concentrated stream of water. 消防水带上的喷嘴可以集中水流。

The gardener adjusted the adjutage on the watering can to create a gentle spray. 园丁调整了洒水壶上的喷嘴，使其喷出柔和的水雾。 | 近义词: nozzle, spout, jet, aperture | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider "inlet" as an opposite concept) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *ajutage*, from Italian *aggiuttatore* "helper," from *aggiutare* "to help," from Latin *adjutare* "to aid."  The original sense referred to a device that aids the flow of liquid.
