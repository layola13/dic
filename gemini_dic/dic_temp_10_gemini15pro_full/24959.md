brushless:/ˈbrʌʃləs/| adj.|无刷的|Without brushes (referring to an electric motor). An electric motor that does not use brushes to transfer current to the rotating armature.|This brushless drill is much more powerful than my old one. 这款无刷电钻比我的旧电钻强劲得多。|The brushless motor is quieter and more efficient.  这款无刷电机更安静，效率也更高。|近义词：brushfree|反义词：brushed|词性:形容词
