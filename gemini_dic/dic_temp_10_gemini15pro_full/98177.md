inscroll:/ɪnˈskroʊl/ | vt. |  题写；雕刻；铭记 | To write, carve, or engrave (something, especially a name or inscription) on a scroll or as if on a scroll. |  He inscrolled his family motto on the lintel of his new house. 他将他的家训题写在新房的门楣上。

The artist inscrolled the names of the fallen soldiers on the memorial.  这位艺术家将阵亡士兵的名字刻在了纪念碑上。 | 近义词: inscribe, engrave | 反义词: erase, delete |  词性: 动词
