mentee:/mɛnˈtiː/
n.
被指导者，受训者
A person who is advised, trained, or counseled by a mentor.
My mentor introduced me to her other mentee. 我的导师把我介绍给了她的另一个受训者。

近义词:protégé, pupil, trainee
反义词:mentor
词性:名词
