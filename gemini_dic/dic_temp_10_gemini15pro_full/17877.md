beekeepers:/ˈbiːkiːpərz/
noun
养蜂人
A person who keeps bees, especially for the production of honey.
The beekeepers harvested the honey in the autumn. 养蜂人在秋天收获蜂蜜。

The beekeepers carefully inspected their hives.  养蜂人仔细检查了他们的蜂箱。


近义词:apiarist, apiculturist
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "bee exterminator" in a specific context)
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bee + keeper.  "Keeper" comes from the Old English "cēpan" meaning "to take care of."
