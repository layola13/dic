lutose:/ˈluːtoʊs/ | adj. | 泥泞的；多泥的 | Covered in or full of mud; muddy. | The road was lutose after the heavy rain. 大雨过后，道路泥泞不堪。|  The farmer's boots were lutose after working in the fields.  农民在田里干活后，靴子上沾满了泥。 | 近义词: muddy, miry, swampy | 反义词: dry, arid, clean | 词性: 形容词


词源: From Latin *lutosus, from lutum (“mud”).
