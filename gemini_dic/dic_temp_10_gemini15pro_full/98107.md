inroad:/ˈɪnˌroʊd/
noun
入侵，侵蚀，进展
an advance or encroachment, often at the expense of something else.
The new supermarket made serious inroads into the business of local shops.  这家新超市严重侵蚀了当地商店的生意。
advance, encroachment, incursion, intrusion
resistance, defense, protection
n.
