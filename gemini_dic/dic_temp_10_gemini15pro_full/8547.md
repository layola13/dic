antichrist:/ˈæntaɪkraɪst/| n.|反基督者，敌基督，基督的敌人|A person who opposes Christ or Christianity, or who is identified as the great opponent of Christ expected to appear before the end of the world.|Many cults believe that the antichrist will come before the end of times. 许多邪教相信反基督者会在世界末日之前到来。|The book describes the coming of the antichrist. 这本书描述了反基督者的到来。|Synonyms: adversary, opponent, enemy|Antonyms: Christ, Messiah|noun
