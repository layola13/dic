croaked:/kroʊkt/| v. |发出低沉沙哑的声音；呱呱叫；死；失败|to make a deep hoarse sound, like a frog; to die; to fail |The old man croaked his last words. 老人发出低沉的声音说出他最后的话。|近义词：died, perished, failed, rasped |反义词: thrived, succeeded
词性:动词

croaked:/kroʊkt/| adj. |(非正式) 死了的；失败的|dead or failed (informal) |The project is croaked.  这个项目失败了。|近义词：dead, failed |反义词: alive, successful
词性:形容词
