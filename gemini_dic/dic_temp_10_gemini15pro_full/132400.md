nondemonstrativenesses:/ˌnɒndɪˈmɒnstrətɪvnɪsnɪsɪz/
n.
缺乏流露情感；不外露感情
the quality or state of being nondemonstrative; a lack of outward display of emotion.
Her nondemonstrativenesses made it difficult to know how she truly felt. 她不外露感情，让人很难知道她的真实感受。
nondemonstrativeness, reserve, reticence, undemonstrativeness
demonstrativeness, expressiveness, effusiveness
noun
