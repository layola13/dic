demilitarised:/diːˈmɪlɪtəraɪzd/
adjective
非军事化的，解除武装的
Removed of military forces, weapons, or fortifications.
The demilitarised zone between North and South Korea is heavily guarded. 朝鲜和韩国之间的非军事区戒备森严。
The treaty called for a demilitarised buffer zone between the two countries. 该条约要求在两国之间建立非军事化的缓冲区。

近义词:neutral, unarmed, disarmed
反义词:militarised, armed, fortified
词性:adjective

词源:de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + military + -ised (suffix forming adjectives)  来自"de-"（表示“移除”或“反转”的前缀）+ military（军事的）+ "-ised"（构成形容词的后缀）。
