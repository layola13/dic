in the event of: /ɪn ði ɪˈvɛnt əv/ | prepositional phrase | 如果；万一；倘若 |  Used to introduce a possible situation, especially an unpleasant one. It means "if something happens." | In the event of fire, break the glass. 万一发生火灾，打破玻璃。|Synonyms: in case of, if, should, supposing | Antonyms:  N/A (prepositional phrases don't typically have antonyms) | phrase
