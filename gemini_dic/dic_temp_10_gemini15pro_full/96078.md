indefatigable:/ˌɪndɪˈfætɪɡəbl/
adjective
不知疲倦的；不屈不挠的
(of a person or their efforts) persisting tirelessly.
He was indefatigable in his pursuit of knowledge. 他对知识的追求孜孜不倦。
Synonyms: tireless, untiring, unwearied, unflagging, inexhaustible, persevering, persistent, tenacious, determined, resolute, steadfast, unwavering, dedicated, committed, single-minded
Antonyms: tired, weary, exhausted
词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *indéfatigable*, from Latin *indēfatīgābilis* (“that cannot be wearied”), from *in-* (“not”) + *dēfatīgābilis* (“that can be wearied”), from *dēfatīgō* (“I tire out, weary”), from *dē-* + *fatīgō* (“I weary”).
