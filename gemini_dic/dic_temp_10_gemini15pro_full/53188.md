dipsomaniacal:/ˌdɪpsəˈmeɪniək(ə)l/
adjective
嗜酒的; 酗酒成癖的
Of, relating to, or characteristic of a dipsomaniac.  Excessively fond of alcoholic beverages.
例句：His dipsomaniacal tendencies led him to lose his job and his family. 他的酗酒倾向导致他失去了工作和家庭。

例句：Her dipsomaniacal behavior became increasingly erratic and unpredictable. 她的酗酒行为变得越来越古怪和难以预测。

近义词：alcoholic, inebriate, addicted to alcohol
反义词：sober, temperate, abstinent
词性：adjective

Etymology: From Greek *dipsa* (thirst) + *mania* (madness).
