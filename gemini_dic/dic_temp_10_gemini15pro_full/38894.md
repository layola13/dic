conferruminated: adj. |反复思考的，仔细讨论过的 |Having been thought about or discussed carefully and at length. |例句：The conferruminated plan was finally put into action.  经过反复思考的计划终于付诸实施了。|例句：They had a conferruminated discussion about the future of the company. 他们对公司的未来进行了详细的讨论。|近义词：considered, deliberated, pondered, ruminated, discussed |反义词： unconsidered, impulsive, rash |词性: 形容词

词源:
From Latin conferruminare (“to cement together, unite”), from con- (“together”) + ferrumen (“cement, rust”), from ferrum (“iron”).  The original sense was to join or solder together, but it evolved to mean "to discuss thoroughly" perhaps from the notion of forging ideas together.
