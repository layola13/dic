coevally:/koʊˈiːvəli/
adverb|同时地；同期地|existing or occurring at the same time|The two species evolved coevally. 这两个物种同时进化。|
Synonyms: concurrently, simultaneously, synchronously
Antonyms: asynchronously, separately, consecutively
词性: 副词

coeval:/koʊˈiːvəl/
adjective|同时代的；同龄的|of the same age, date, or duration; contemporary|The poet was coeval with the dramatist. 这位诗人和剧作家是同时代的人。|
Synonyms: contemporary, synchronous, concurrent
Antonyms: asynchronous, non-contemporary, successive
词性: 形容词

coeval:/ˈkoʊˌiːvəl/
noun|同龄人；同时代的人或物|a person or thing of the same age, date, or duration|The museum houses coevals of the ancient manuscript.  博物馆收藏了与这份古代手稿同时代的文物。|
Synonyms: contemporary, peer
Antonyms: predecessor, successor, antecedent
词性: 名词
