manipulate:/məˈnɪpjuleɪt/
verb
操纵，控制；处理，操作
to control or influence something or someone, often in a dishonest way, or to handle something skillfully
He manipulated the stock market for his own profit. 他操纵股市以牟取暴利。
The artist skillfully manipulated the clay. 艺术家熟练地处理黏土。
synonyms: control, influence, exploit, maneuver, handle, operate
antonyms: relinquish, liberate, free, neglect
verb
