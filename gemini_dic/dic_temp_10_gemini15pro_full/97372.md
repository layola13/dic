infralittoral:/ˌɪnfrəlɪˈtɔːrəl/ | adj. n.|潮下带的，潮下带|The infralittoral zone is constantly submerged and rich in marine life. 潮下带持续被淹没，海洋生物丰富。|The infralittoral zone extends from the lowest low tide to the edge of the continental shelf. 潮下带从最低的低潮线延伸到大陆架边缘。|近义词：sublittoral, neritic|反义词：supralittoral, terrestrial|词性：形容词，名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From infra- (Latin, below) + littoral (Latin litoralis, of the shore).
