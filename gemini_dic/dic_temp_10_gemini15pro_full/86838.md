hemautography:/ˌhiːmɔːˈtɒɡrəfi/ | n. | 血字书写；血书；自传 | The act or practice of writing with one's own blood.  It can also refer to a biography written by the subject themselves. | He wrote his last testament as a hemautography, using his own blood as ink. 他用自己的血作为墨水，将遗嘱写成了血书。 |  autobiography, memoir | N/A | noun
