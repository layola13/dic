at-ease:/ætˈiːz/
adjective
安心的，自在的，稍息
relaxed and comfortable; (of a soldier) standing in a relaxed position.
The soldiers were told to stand at ease. 士兵们被告知稍息。
近义词: relaxed, comfortable, informal, casual
反义词: tense, uneasy, formal, at-attention
词性: adjective

at-ease:/ætˈiːz/
noun
稍息姿势
a relaxed posture, especially that assumed by soldiers when allowed to stand freely but without moving about.
The sergeant called the platoon to attention, then at ease.  中士让排集合立正，然后稍息。
近义词: rest, break
反义词: attention
词性: noun


词源:  From the military command "Stand at ease!", dating back to the 18th century. It combines the preposition "at" with the adjective "ease."
