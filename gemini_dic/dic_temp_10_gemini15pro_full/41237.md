corals:/ˈkɔːrəlz/| n.|珊瑚|A hard stony substance secreted by certain marine coelenterates as an external skeleton, typically forming large reefs in warm seas.|The corals are vibrant and colorful. 珊瑚色彩鲜艳。|Near synonyms: reefs, polyps|Antonym: none|Noun

corals:/ˈkɔːrəlz/| n.|珊瑚虫|Small marine invertebrate animals that secrete calcium carbonate to form a hard skeleton, which can build up into coral reefs.|The corals are tiny animals that build massive structures. 珊瑚虫是建造巨大结构的小动物。|Near synonyms: polyps, reef-builders|Antonym: none|Noun
