一般现在时 (Simple Present):
The train leaves at eight o'clock every morning.  火车每天早上八点出发。
I usually wake up at seven o'clock. 我通常七点起床。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
It's approaching six o'clock, and I'm still working.  快到六点了，我还在工作。
He's leaving at five o'clock sharp. 他将在五点整离开。


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
It's already eight o'clock; have you finished your homework? 已经八点了，你做完作业了吗？
The clock has just struck ten o'clock. 钟刚敲了十点。


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I've been waiting here since five o'clock. 我从五点起就一直在这里等着。
She's been working since eight o'clock this morning. 她从今天早上八点就开始工作了。


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
The meeting started at nine o'clock. 会议九点开始。
I arrived at six o'clock yesterday. 我昨天六点到的。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
At seven o'clock this morning, I was sleeping. 今天早上七点，我正在睡觉。
She was cooking dinner at six o'clock when I called. 我打电话的时候，她六点正在做晚饭。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
By six o'clock, they had already left. 到六点钟，他们已经离开了。
He had finished his work by five o'clock. 他五点前就完成了工作。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
They had been working since seven o'clock.  他们从七点开始就一直在工作。
She had been waiting for him since eight o'clock. 她从八点就开始等他了。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
The movie will start at seven o'clock. 电影七点开始。
I will be there by nine o'clock. 我九点前会到那里。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be working at eight o'clock tomorrow. 明天八点我将在工作。
She will be traveling at this time next week, around six o'clock.  下周这个时候，大约六点左右，她将在旅行。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By ten o'clock, I will have finished my work. 到十点，我就完成工作了。
They will have arrived by six o'clock. 他们会在六点前到达。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By seven o'clock, I will have been working for eight hours. 到七点钟，我就已经工作八个小时了。
By next year, she will have been living here for two years, always waking up at six o'clock. 到明年，她就住在这里两年了，总是六点起床。


PHRASE->
at six o'clock 在六点
by nine o'clock 到九点之前

sharp at seven o'clock 七点整
until five o'clock 直到五点


注意事项:
o'clock 通常只用于整点，不用于表示分钟。例如，不说 "ten o'clock fifteen minutes"，而说 "ten fifteen" 或 "a quarter past ten"。
