mandate:/ˈmændeɪt/ (US) /ˈmændeɪt/ (UK)| n. vt.|授权，命令，委托；强制执行|An official order or commission to do something.  The election victory gave the party a clear mandate to implement its policies.|The government has a mandate to reduce poverty. 政府有责任减少贫困。|Synonyms: authorization, order, command, instruction, commission, directive|Antonyms: prohibition, forbiddance|noun
