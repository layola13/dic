depulsory:/dɪˈpʌlsəri/ | adj. | 义务的；强制的；必修的 |  Required by law or a rule; obligatory.  Derived from the verb "compel" meaning to force or oblige someone to do something. | Attendance at school is compulsory for children of certain ages.  学校对特定年龄的儿童实行强制入学。| mandatory, obligatory, required | optional, voluntary | adjective
