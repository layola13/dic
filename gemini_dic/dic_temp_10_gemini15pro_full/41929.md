cosmological:/ˌkɒzməˈlɒdʒɪkəl/| adj.|宇宙学的，宇宙论的|Relating to the origin and development of the universe.|例句：Cosmological theories attempt to explain the universe's beginning and evolution. 宇宙学理论试图解释宇宙的起源和演化。|例句：His research focuses on cosmological models that incorporate dark matter and dark energy. 他的研究重点是包含暗物质和暗能量的宇宙学模型。|近义词：universal, cosmic|反义词:N/A|词性:adjective
词源: From Greek *kosmos* "order, world, universe" + -logical "pertaining to the study of."
