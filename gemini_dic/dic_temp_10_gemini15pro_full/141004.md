onerousness:/ˈoʊnərəsnəs/ | n. | 繁重性；麻烦；沉重 | The state or quality of being onerous; burdensomeness. | The onerousness of the task made him procrastinate.  这项任务的繁重让他拖延了。 | Synonyms: burdensomeness, difficulty, hardship, laboriousness, tediousness | Antonyms: easiness, facility, simplicity, lightness | noun

onerous:/ˈoʊnərəs/ | adj. | 繁重的；麻烦的；负有义务的 | (of a task, duty, or responsibility) involving an amount of effort and difficulty that is oppressively burdensome. |  Meeting the deadline proved an onerous task.  赶上最后期限证明是一项繁重的任务。 | Synonyms: burdensome, arduous, taxing, demanding, laborious, strenuous | Antonyms: easy, light, simple, effortless | adjective
