inovate:/ˈɪnəveɪt/
verb
创新
To introduce something new; make changes in anything established.
例句：The company needs to innovate to stay competitive.  这家公司需要创新才能保持竞争力。

例句：They are constantly innovating new products and services. 他们不断地创新新的产品和服务。
近义词：invent, create, pioneer, develop, modernize
反义词：imitate, copy, stagnate, traditionalize
词性：v.
