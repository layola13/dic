palatal:/ˈpælətl/| adj. n.|腭音的；硬腭；|Of or relating to the palate; a speech sound produced by placing the tongue against or near the hard palate.|The "y" sound in "yes" is a palatal consonant.|"yes"中的"y"音是一个硬腭辅音。|近义词:alveolo-palatal|反义词:velar|adjective, noun
