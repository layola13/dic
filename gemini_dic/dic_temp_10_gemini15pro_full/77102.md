garrulous:/ˈɡærələs/ | adj. | 喋喋不休的，絮叨的 | Excessively talkative, especially on trivial matters. | She was so garrulous that I could hardly get a word in edgewise. 她太健谈了，我几乎插不上话。|例句：He became quite garrulous after a few drinks. 他喝了几杯酒后变得非常健谈。|近义词：talkative, loquacious, voluble |反义词：reticent, taciturn, laconic |词性:形容词
