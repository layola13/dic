daher:/daˈheːɐ̯/ (German)
| adv. |因此，所以，由此，从那里|Therefore, so, hence, from there|例句：Daher kommt er.  Therefore he comes.|近义词：deshalb, deswegen, somit, folglich|反义词: dennoch, trotzdem|词性:副词
Etymology: From Middle High German *daher, from Old High German *dahar, from Proto-Germanic *dahar, equivalent to da (“there”) + her (“hither, from”). Cognate with Dutch daher, Danish derfra.
