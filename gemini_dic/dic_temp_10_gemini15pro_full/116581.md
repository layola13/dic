matriarchies:/ˈmeɪtriɑːrkiːz/
n.
母系社会; 女族长统治
A social system in which women hold primary power, particularly in families and clans.
例句：Many anthropologists believe that some early human societies were matriarchies. 许多人类学家认为，一些早期人类社会是母系社会。

例句：The novel explored the complexities of a fictional matriarchy. 这部小说探讨了一个虚构母系社会的复杂性。

近义词：matriarchal societies, gynocracies
反义词：patriarchies
词性: 名词 (plural noun)
