nonethical:/nɒnˈɛθɪk(ə)l/
adjective
不道德的
Not conforming to accepted standards of morality.
His nonethical business practices led to his downfall. 他的不道德商业行为导致了他的垮台。
近义词: immoral, unethical, unscrupulous
反义词: ethical, moral, principled
词性: adjective

词源: non- (not) + ethical (pertaining to ethics)
