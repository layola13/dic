epicene:/ˈɛpɪˌsiːn/ /ˈɛpəˌsin/
adjective
雌雄同体的；阴阳性的；适合男女两性的
Having no characteristics of either sex; of indeterminate sex.  Grammatically, designating a word that can be used of either sex.
例句：The singer cultivated an epicene look.  这位歌手培养了一种雌雄莫辨的形象。

例句：In English, "author" is an epicene noun. 在英语中，“author”（作者）是一个阴阳通性的名词。
近义词：androgynous, unisex, asexual
反义词：masculine, feminine
词性：adjective


epicene:/ˈɛpɪˌsiːn/ /ˈɛpəˌsin/
noun
阴阳人；雌雄同体的人
A person who is epicene.
例句：The character in the film is portrayed as an epicene.  电影中的角色被描绘成一个阴阳人。

例句：Historically, epicenes were often viewed with suspicion.  历史上，阴阳人经常被怀疑。
近义词：androgyne, hermaphrodite
反义词：None
词性：noun
