monoicous: /məˈnɔɪkəs/ | adj. | 雌雄同株的 | Having male and female reproductive organs in the same individual; hermaphroditic, especially of a plant. |  Many moss species are monoicous, meaning a single plant has both male and female reproductive structures. 许多苔藓物种是雌雄同株的，这意味着单个植物同时具有雄性和雌性生殖结构。 | synonyms: hermaphrodite, bisexual, monoecious | antonyms: dioecious | adjective

monoicous: /məˈnɔɪkəs/ | adj. | (指苔藓和地衣)雌雄同体的 |  (of a moss or lichen) having the antheridia and archegonia in separate clusters on the same plant. | The liverwort is monoicous with antheridia and archegonia on separate branches.  这种地钱是雌雄同体的，其精子器和颈卵器位于不同的枝条上。 | synonyms: autoicous | antonyms: dioicous | adjective

词源：
From Ancient Greek *μόνοικος (*mónoikos, “living alone”), from μόνος (mónos, “alone, single, solitary”) + οἶκος (oîkos, “house”).  来自古希腊语 *μόνοικος (*mónoikos，“独自生活”)，由 μόνος (mónos，“单独的，单一的，孤独的”) + οἶκος (oîkos，“房子”) 组成。  尽管意思似乎矛盾，但这个词在生物学上的用法指的是雄性和雌性器官在“同一个”（植物）“房子”里。
