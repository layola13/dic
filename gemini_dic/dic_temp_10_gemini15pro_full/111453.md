lmai 不是一个标准的英文单词。 你可能是想问 "lamai" (拉麦) 或其他拼写相似的词。 请确认拼写。 如果是 "lamai"，它通常是一个名字，没有其他的词性或用法。

如果你是想问 "What"，以下是分析：

what: /wɒt/ (UK), /wʌt/ (US) | pron. det. adv. int.| 什么，多么 |  Used to ask for information about something. | Example: What is your name? 你叫什么名字？ | Synonym: which, how | Antonym:  (What has no direct antonym as it is a question word) | pronoun

what: /wɒt/ (UK), /wʌt/ (US) | pron. det. adv. int.| 什么，多么 | Used to ask for information specifying something. | Example: What book are you reading? 你在读什么书？| Synonym: which, how | Antonym:  (What has no direct antonym as it is a question word) | determiner

what: /wɒt/ (UK), /wʌt/ (US) | pron. det. adv. int.| 什么，多么 | Used to express surprise or disbelief. | Example: What a beautiful day! 多么美好的一天！| Synonym: how | Antonym:  (What has no direct antonym as it is a question word) | adverb

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she do for a living? 她做什么工作？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? 他晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? 她用这笔钱做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading this week? 他这周一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? 她对这部电影说了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 pm last night? 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? 我看到她时她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? 她生病前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I called? 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? 她换专业之前一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will she study in college? 她大学要学什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be working on next month? 她下个月会做什么工作？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have accomplished by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will she have learned by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时，她将学到什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2030? 到2030年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she gets her PhD? 到她获得博士学位时，她将已经学习什么多久了？
