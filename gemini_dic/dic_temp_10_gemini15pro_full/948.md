academias: /əˌkæˈdiːmiəz/ | n. |研究院; 学会; 学术界|  A place of study or training, especially in the arts, or a society of scholars, artists, or scientists.  It is the plural form of "academia".| The various academies of art in Europe have trained some of the world's finest artists. 欧洲各个艺术研究院培养了一些世界上最优秀的艺术家。

Many scholars spend their entire careers within the walls of academia. 许多学者在学术界度过了他们的整个职业生涯。| 近义词: institutes, schools, societies, universities | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider) industry, workforce | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin academia, from Greek akadēmeia "Academy," the name of a gymnasium near Athens where Plato taught, named after Akademos, a local hero.

USAGE->
The word "academias" is primarily used in the plural to refer to multiple academic institutions or the academic world in general.  Here are some usage examples in different grammatical contexts:

一般现在时:
Many academies offer specialized training in the arts. = 许多学院提供艺术方面的专业培训。
These academies attract students from all over the world. = 这些学院吸引了来自世界各地的学生。

一般过去时:
The academies played a crucial role in the Renaissance. =  这些学院在文艺复兴时期发挥了至关重要的作用。
Several academies were founded in the 18th century. =  一些学院成立于18世纪。

现在完成时:
The academies have evolved significantly over time. =  随着时间的推移，这些学院发生了巨大的变化。
Several new academies have been established in recent years. = 近年来，一些新的学院已经建立。

过去完成时:
Before the 20th century, academies had been the primary centers of learning. = 在20世纪之前，学院一直是主要的学习中心。
The academies had already achieved international recognition by the time the war broke out. = 在战争爆发之前，这些学院已经获得了国际认可。

将来时:
The academies will continue to play an important role in education. = 这些学院将继续在教育中发挥重要作用。
New academies will be needed to meet the growing demand for specialized training. = 需要新的学院来满足日益增长的专业培训需求。


被动语态:
