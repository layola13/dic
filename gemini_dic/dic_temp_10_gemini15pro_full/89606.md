hoods:/hʊdz/| n.|兜帽，头巾，引擎盖，街区，黑帮|A covering for the head and neck, often attached to a coat or cloak. Also, the metal covering over the engine of a vehicle, a neighborhood, or a group of criminals.|She wore a hooded cloak to protect herself from the rain. 她穿着一件带兜帽的斗篷以防雨。 The hood of the car was dented in the accident. 汽车的引擎盖在事故中被撞凹了。 He grew up in a rough hood. 他在一个混乱的街区长大。 The hood was known for its high crime rate.  这个黑帮以其高犯罪率而闻名。|近义词：bonnet(引擎盖), cowl(兜帽), district(街区), gang(黑帮)|反义词: (无明确的反义词)|词性:名词


hoods:/hʊdz/| v.|给...装上兜帽|To cover with a hood.|He hooded his falcon before the hunt. 他在狩猎前给他的猎鹰戴上兜帽。|近义词：cover, conceal|反义词: uncover, reveal|词性:动词
