nonglobular:/nɒnˈɡlɒbjʊlə(r)/
adjective
非球形的
Not having a globular or spherical shape; not rounded.
The protein has a nonglobular structure, which makes it difficult to crystallize.  这种蛋白质具有非球状结构，因此难以结晶。
The nonglobular cells were more difficult to identify under the microscope.  非球形细胞在显微镜下更难识别。
近义词:aspherical, nonspherical
反义词:globular, spherical
词性:adjective
