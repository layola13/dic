benefactrix:/ˌbɛnɪˈfæktriks/
n.
女施主，女恩人
a woman who confers a benefit; a female benefactor
The benefactrix donated a large sum of money to the hospital.  这位女施主向医院捐赠了一大笔钱。
近义词：patroness, donor
反义词：malefactor, wrongdoer
词性：noun
