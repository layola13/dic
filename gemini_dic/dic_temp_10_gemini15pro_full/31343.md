characteristic:/ˌkærəktəˈrɪstɪk/ | adj. n. | 特有的，典型的；特征，特性 |  A distinguishing feature or quality of something. |  Her characteristic laugh is easily recognizable. 她特有的笑声很容易辨认。 |  typical, distinctive, attribute, trait |  atypical, unusual  | adjective
characteristic:/ˌkærəktəˈrɪstɪk/ |  n. | 特征，特性 | A distinguishing feature or quality of something. | Kindness is one of her many positive characteristics.  善良是她众多优秀品质之一。 |  feature, trait, attribute, quality |  n/a | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *characteristicus, from Greek kharaktēristikos, from kharaktērizein "to engrave, characterize," from kharaktēr "engraving tool, stamp, distinctive mark, character," from kharassein "to scratch, engrave."
