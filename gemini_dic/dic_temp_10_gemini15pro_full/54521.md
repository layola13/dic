disjuncture:/dɪsˈdʒʌŋktʃər/ | n. | 分离，分裂，脱节 | A disjuncture is a break, separation, or discontinuity between things.  A disjuncture can refer to a gap or inconsistency between two or more ideas, things, or parts of something. | There is a disjuncture between the government's promises and its actions. 政府的承诺和行动之间存在脱节。| synonyms: disconnect, disunity, discrepancy, gap, rift | antonyms:  connection, unity, continuity, agreement, harmony | noun

disjuncture:/dɪsˈdʒʌŋktʃər/ | n. | 不和谐，不一致 | A disjuncture can also refer to a lack of harmony or agreement. |  The disjuncture between her personal life and her professional life was causing her stress. 她的个人生活和职业生活之间的不和谐让她感到压力。 | synonyms:  discord, conflict, clash, incompatibility | antonyms:  harmony, agreement, accord, compatibility | noun
