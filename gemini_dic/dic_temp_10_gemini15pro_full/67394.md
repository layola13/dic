exterminator:/ɪkˈstɜːrmɪneɪtər/| n.|灭虫员，除害者|A person whose job is to kill unwanted insects or animals, especially rodents.|例句：We had to call an exterminator to get rid of the rats in the attic. 我们不得不打电话叫灭虫员来清除阁楼上的老鼠。

例句：The exterminator sprayed the house for termites. 灭虫员喷洒房屋以防白蚁。|近义词：pest controller, fumigator|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'conservationist' in a broader sense) |词性:noun
