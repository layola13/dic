elaoptene: /ɪˈleɪɒptiːn/ | n. | 香精油; 液体香精 | The elaoptene is the liquid portion of an essential oil, as distinguished from the stearoptene. 香精油是精油的液体部分，与硬脂酸油不同。| The elaoptene of rose oil is prized for its fragrance. 玫瑰油的香精油因其香味而备受珍视。 | synonyms: essential oil, volatile oil | antonyms: stearoptene | noun
