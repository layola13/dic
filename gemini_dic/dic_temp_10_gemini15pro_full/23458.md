braggadocio:/ˌbræɡəˈdoʊʃioʊ/ (US), /ˌbræɡəˈdəʊʃɪəʊ/ (UK)
noun
自夸；吹嘘；大话
boastful or arrogant behavior.
He was known for his braggadocio and elaborate tales of his adventures. 他以自吹自擂和精心编造的冒险故事而闻名。
近义词: boasting, bragging, swaggering, vainglory, bombast
反义词: modesty, humility, diffidence, understatement
词性: n.

Etymology:
From Italian *bragadocchio*, from *bragare* "to boast" (from French *braguer*), of uncertain origin.
