citing:/ˈsaɪtɪŋ/ |v.|引用；引证；提及；传唤；嘉奖|The article citing several experts. 这篇文章引用了几位专家的观点。|近义词：quoting, mentioning, referencing, summoning|反义词：ignoring, omitting|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *citen, from Anglo-Norman citer, from Latin citāre (“to summon, urge, call, rouse”), frequentative of cieō (“I set in motion, call, put in motion, shake, excite”).
