nonfugitively:/ˌnɒnˈfjuːdʒɪtɪvli/
adverb|非逃亡地，非短暂地|In a way that is not fugitive or fleeting; permanently, enduringly.|The data was stored nonfugitively on the server.|近义词：permanently, enduringly, lastingly|反义词：fugitively, transiently, temporarily|词性:副词
词源:  From non- + fugitively, from fugitive + -ly. Ultimately from Latin *fugitīvus, from fugere "to flee".
