lazinesses:/ˈleɪzinɪsɪz/
n.
懒惰，懈怠
The state of being lazy; inactivity resulting from an unwillingness to exert oneself.
His numerous lazinesses prevented him from finishing the project. 他多次的懒惰使他无法完成这个项目。
Synonyms: indolence, sloth, idleness, inactivity, shiftlessness
Antonyms: diligence, industry, assiduity
noun
