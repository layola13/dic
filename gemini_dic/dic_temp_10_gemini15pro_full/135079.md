nonnutritiousness:/ˌnɑːnnuˈtrɪʃəsnəs/ | n. | 缺乏营养；无营养 |  The state or quality of being nonnutritious; lacking in nutrients. | The nonnutritiousness of junk food is a major concern for health experts.  垃圾食品缺乏营养是健康专家主要关注的问题。 | Synonyms:  innutritiousness, unhealthfulness, unwholesomeness | Antonyms: nutritiousness, healthfulness, wholesomeness | noun
