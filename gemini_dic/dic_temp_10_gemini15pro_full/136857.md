nonsister:/ˌnɒnˈsɪstər/ /ˌnɑːnˈsɪstər/ | n. | 非姐妹染色体；非姐妹染色单体 |  A chromatid from a different homologous chromosome (as opposed to a sister chromatid).  Refers to chromatids of homologous chromosomes, or to a pair of chromosomes. | During meiosis, crossing over occurs between nonsister chromatids. 在减数分裂过程中，非姐妹染色单体之间会发生交叉互换。|  homologous chromosome, non-homologous chromosome | sister chromatid | noun
