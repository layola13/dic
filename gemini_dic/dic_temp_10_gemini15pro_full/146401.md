overflutter:/ˌoʊvərˈflʌtər/
verb
过度飘动，快速颤动
To flutter excessively or rapidly.
The butterfly's wings overfluttered as it tried to escape the spider's web. 蝴蝶试图逃脱蜘蛛网时，翅膀快速地颤动。
The flags overfluttered in the strong wind. 旗帜在强风中过度飘动。
近义词:flutter, quiver, vibrate
反义词:still, motionless
词性:v.
