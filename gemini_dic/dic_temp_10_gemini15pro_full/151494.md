parry:/ˈpæri/| n. v. |招架，躲避，回避|An act of parrying, especially in sword fighting.  The act of skillfully avoiding a question or accusation. |例句：He parried the blow with his sword. 他用剑挡开了那一击。

例句：The politician skillfully parried the journalist's awkward questions.  这位政治家巧妙地回避了记者的尴尬问题。 |近义词：deflect, avoid, evade, block, fend off|反义词:accept, confront, face|词性:名词, 动词
ETYMOLOGY->
From French *parer*, "to ward off, to avoid," from Latin *parāre*, "to prepare, make ready."
