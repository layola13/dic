influence:/ˈɪnfluəns/ (US), /ˈɪnfluəns/ (UK)| n. vt.|影响，影响力；势力；有影响的人（或事物）|The ability to affect someone or something, or the result of such an effect.|Her parents have a great influence on her. 她的父母对她有很大的影响。|近义词：impact, effect, sway, control|反义词：weakness, impotence|词性:名词,动词

influence:/ˈɪnfluəns/ (US), /ˈɪnfluəns/ (UK)| vt.|影响，左右；支配|To affect or change the way someone or something develops, behaves, or thinks.|His writings have influenced generations of readers. 他的著作影响了几代读者。|近义词：affect, impact, shape, sway|反义词：leave alone, ignore|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French influence, from Medieval Latin influentia (“flowing in”), from Latin influere (“to flow into”), from in- (“into”) + fluere (“to flow”).
