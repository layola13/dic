dipterocarpus:/ˌdɪptərəˈkɑːrpəs/ | n. |龙脑香树；龙脑香科植物|A genus of tall trees of the family Dipterocarpaceae, native to southeast Asia and producing valuable timber and resins. | Dipterocarpus trees are a dominant species in many Southeast Asian rainforests. 龙脑香树是许多东南亚雨林中的优势树种。| synonym: hardwood tree (general term), keruing | antonym: softwood tree | noun
