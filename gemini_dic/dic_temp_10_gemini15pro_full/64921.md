establishes:/ɪˈstæblɪʃɪz/
verb
建立，设立；确定；证实
sets up, creates, or brings into existence something that is meant to last; proves or shows something to be true
The company establishes a new branch in Beijing.  这家公司在北京设立了新的分公司。
The lawyer established his client's innocence. 律师证实了他的委托人的清白。
synonyms:  founds, institutes, creates, proves, validates
antonyms: abolishes, dissolves, disproves, refutes
词性: 动词(v.)
