exploitatively:/ɪkˌsplɔɪˈteɪtɪvli/ | adv. |剥削地，利用地| In a way that unfairly uses someone or something for personal gain. |The company was accused of exploitatively using its workers by paying them low wages and making them work long hours.  该公司被指控剥削工人，支付低工资，并让他们长时间工作。|近义词：unfairly, unjustly, oppressively |反义词: fairly, justly, ethically |词性: 副词

exploit:/ɪkˈsplɔɪt/ | v. |利用，剥削|Make full use of and derive benefit from (a resource).|The company exploited its workers by paying them low wages. 公司剥削工人，支付低工资。|近义词：utilize, misuse, abuse|反义词: protect, conserve, respect |词性: 动词

exploit:/ˈeksplɔɪt/ | n. |功绩，功勋|A bold or daring feat.|His daring exploits made him a legend. 他大胆的功勋使他成为传奇人物。|近义词：feat, accomplishment, achievement|反义词: failure, defeat |词性: 名词

词源: From the Late Latin *explicitare, meaning "to unfold, to develop".  源自晚期拉丁语 *explicitare，意为“展开，发展”。
