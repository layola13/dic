hydrogenize:/ˈhaɪdrədʒənaɪz/ /ˈhaɪdroʊdʒəˌnaɪz/| v. |氢化|To treat with hydrogen; to combine or cause to combine with hydrogen.|Vegetable oils are often hydrogenized to make them solid at room temperature.  植物油通常被氢化以使其在室温下呈固态。|The company hydrogenizes vegetable oil to produce margarine.  该公司将植物油氢化以生产人造黄油。|synonyms: reduce, saturate|antonyms: dehydrogenate, oxidize|verb
