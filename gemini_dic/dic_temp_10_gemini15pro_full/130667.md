nonadvocacies:/ˌnɒnˈædvəkəsiːz/
noun
非倡导；不提倡
Lack of support or promotion; absence of advocacy.
The government's nonadvocacies on climate change are concerning. 政府在气候变化问题上的不提倡令人担忧。
Near Synonyms: inaction, apathy, indifference, silence
Antonyms: advocacy, support, promotion
词性: 名词
