cottages: /ˈkɒtɪdʒɪz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːtɪdʒɪz/ (US)
n.
小屋，村舍；（尤指）度假小屋
Small houses, typically one-story, often found in rural areas and sometimes used for vacations.
The family rented a charming cottage by the lake for their summer holiday.  这个家庭在湖边租了一间迷人的小屋作为他们的暑假度假屋。

We drove past rows of pretty cottages with thatched roofs. 我们开车经过一排排漂亮的茅草屋顶小屋。

近义词: cabin, hut, shack, chalet
反义词: mansion, palace
词性: 名词
