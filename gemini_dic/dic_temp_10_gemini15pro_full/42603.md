counterprotesters:/ˈkaʊntərprəˌtɛstərz/ (US), /ˈkaʊntəprəˌtɛstəz/ (UK)
n.
反抗议者，对立抗议者
People who protest against another group of protesters.
例句：Counterprotesters gathered across the street, voicing their opposing views. 反抗议者聚集在街对面，表达他们的反对意见。

例句：The counterprotesters were smaller in number but equally vocal. 反抗议者的数量较少，但同样声音洪亮。

近义词：opposition protesters, opposing demonstrators
反义词：protesters, demonstrators
词性：名词
