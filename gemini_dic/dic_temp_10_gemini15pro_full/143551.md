outhaul: /ˈaʊthɔːl/ | n. | 拉帆索，后拉索 | A rope used to adjust the tension of the sail, particularly the foot of a mainsail or mizzen, pulling it out along the boom. |  The outhaul is essential for controlling the sail's shape in different wind conditions. 后拉索对于在不同风力条件下控制帆的形状至关重要。

 The strong winds required tightening the outhaul for optimal performance.  强风需要收紧后拉索以获得最佳性能。 | 近义词: foot outhaul | 反义词:  No direct antonym, but easing the outhaul has the opposite effect. | 词性: noun
