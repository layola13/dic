overdye:/ˌoʊvərˈdaɪ/ | v. n. | 过染，套染；套染的织物 | To dye (fabric) again, especially a different color over a previously dyed color.  The process also refers to dyeing a multicolored textile to create a more unified or darker color. |  Example 1:  She overdyed the faded blue curtains with a rich burgundy. 她把褪色的蓝色窗帘套染成了浓郁的酒红色。

Example 2: The overdye gave the dress a beautiful vintage look. 套染给了这件连衣裙一种美丽的复古外观。| 近义词: redye, tint | 反义词: bleach, fade | 词性: 动词，名词
