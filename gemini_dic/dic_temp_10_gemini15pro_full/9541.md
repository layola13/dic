antisensuousnesses:/ˌæntiˌsɛnʃuːəsnɪsɪz/
|n.| 反感官性，禁欲主义 |The state or quality of being opposed to or indifferent to sensual pleasures.  A rejection of or aversion to the pleasures of the senses.|  His antisensuousness manifested in his austere lifestyle and rejection of luxury. 他的反感官性体现在他简朴的生活方式和对奢侈品的排斥。|Near Synonyms: asceticism, austerity, puritanism|Antonyms: sensuality, hedonism|Noun
