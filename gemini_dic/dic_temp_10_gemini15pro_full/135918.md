nonpulmonary:/ˌnɒnˈpʌlmənəri/ /ˌnɑːnˈpʌlməˌneri/
adjective
非肺的；非呼吸系统的
Not relating to or affecting the lungs.
Example: Nonpulmonary causes of breathlessness include heart conditions and anemia. 非肺部呼吸困难的原因包括心脏疾病和贫血。
Example: The doctor diagnosed a nonpulmonary source for the patient's coughing fits. 医生诊断出病人阵发性咳嗽的病因并非来自肺部。
Synonym: extrapulmonary
Antonym: pulmonary
词性: adjective
