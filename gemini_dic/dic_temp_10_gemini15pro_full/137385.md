nontemptations:/ˌnɒntempˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 无诱惑之物；缺乏诱惑力的事物 | Absence of temptations; things that do not entice or allure. |  The monastery offered a life of nontemptations, free from the distractions of the city.  这所修道院提供了一种没有诱惑的生活，远离城市的喧嚣。 | 近义词:  lack of allure, absence of enticement | 反义词: temptations, allurements | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "temptations" (plural of "temptation").  "temptation" comes from Old French "temptation," from Latin "temptatio," from "temptare" "to test, try."
