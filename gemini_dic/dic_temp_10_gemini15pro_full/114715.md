mallemock:/ˈmælɪmɒk/ | n. | 丑角；滑稽人物；玩偶 | A grotesque figure, often used in mockery or ridicule; a puppet. |  The children laughed at the mallemock dangling from the puppeteer's strings. 孩子们看着木偶师绳子上晃来晃去的丑角哈哈大笑。| synonym: buffoon, clown, grotesque, puppet | antonym: dignified figure | noun


mallemock:/ˈmælɪmɒk/ | v. | 愚弄；嘲弄 | To treat with derision or scorn; to make a mockery of. | The comedian mallemocked the politician's speech, exaggerating his mannerisms.  喜剧演员模仿政客的演讲，夸大了他的举止，以此来嘲弄他。| synonym: mock, ridicule, deride, lampoon | antonym: praise, respect, admire | verb



ETYMOLOGY->

From Middle French *malemoque*, a grotesque figure used in mockery, possibly from Dutch *mal* (foolish) + mock (imitation).
