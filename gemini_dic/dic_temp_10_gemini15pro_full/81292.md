granular:/ˈɡrænjʊlər/| adj.|颗粒状的；粒状的；细粒的；详细的；精细的|Resembling or consisting of granules; composed of small discrete particles. Also, highly detailed and specific.|The beach had fine, granular sand.  这片海滩的沙子又细又滑。|The report provides a granular analysis of the data. 这份报告对数据进行了细致的分析。|近义词：grainy, particulate, detailed, specific|反义词：smooth, coarse, general, vague|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *granularis*, from granulum (“little grain”) + -aris.
