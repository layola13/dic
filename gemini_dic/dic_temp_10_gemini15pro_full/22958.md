bottom-up:/ˈbɒtəm ʌp/ (US) /ˈbɒtəm ʌp/ (UK)| adj. |自下而上的，由下往上的|Starting with the smallest or least important parts and building to the larger or more important units.  Constructed from the details or specifics to a larger overview.|The company decided to take a bottom-up approach to reorganizing the department, starting with employee feedback.  该公司决定采用自下而上的方法重组部门，从员工反馈开始。|grassroots, data-driven|top-down|adjective
bottom-up:/ˈbɒtəm ʌp/ (US) /ˈbɒtəm ʌp/ (UK)| n. |自下而上的方法|The act or process of building something from the smallest or least important parts to the larger or more important units.|The bottom-up approach to budgeting allows each department to have input.  自下而上的预算方法允许每个部门都有发言权。|grassroots initiative|top-down management|noun
