doggerel:/ˈdɒɡərəl/ (US) /ˈdɑːɡərəl/ (UK)| n.|打油诗，蹩脚的诗|Trivial, poorly written verse, often humorous or irregular in rhythm and rhyme.  It is characterized by its simple, often nonsensical, rhymes and its generally clumsy or awkward construction.|Example:  His attempt at a love poem was pure doggerel. 他试图写一首爱情诗，结果却是一首打油诗。

Example: The greeting card contained a few lines of sentimental doggerel. 贺卡上写着几行伤感拙劣的诗句。|近义词：verse, rhyme, jingle, limerick|反义词：poetry, epic, sonnet|词性:noun
