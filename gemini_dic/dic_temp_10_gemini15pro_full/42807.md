coupstick: /ˈkuːˌstɪk/ | n. | 政变棍；政变工具 | A derogatory term for a tool or instrument used to instigate or carry out a coup d'état. It often refers to military force or political maneuvering. | The general used his army as a coupstick to seize power. 将军利用他的军队作为政变工具夺取了政权。|  Synonyms: tool, instrument, weapon | Antonyms: peacemaker, mediator | noun
