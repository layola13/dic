bhaji:/ˈbɑːdʒiː/| n.|油炸蔬菜小吃; 蔬菜咖喱|A spicy Indian dish of vegetables, often coated in batter and deep-fried or cooked with spices in a sauce.|例句：We ordered some bhajis as a starter. 我们点了一些油炸蔬菜作为开胃菜。

例句：She made a delicious bhaji with potatoes and spinach. 她用土豆和菠菜做了一道美味的蔬菜咖喱。|近义词：pakora, fritter, vegetable curry|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi and Marathi *bhaji*, meaning "vegetable dish," ultimately from Sanskrit *bharjan*, meaning "roasting, frying."
