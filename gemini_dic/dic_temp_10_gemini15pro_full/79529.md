globulet:/ˈɡlɒbjʊlət/ (US) /ˈɡlɑːbjʊlət/ (UK)| n. |小球，小滴，微球|A tiny globe or spherical body; a small drop or particle.|例句：The chemist analyzed the globulets of mercury. 化学家分析了汞珠。

例句：Globulets of sweat formed on his forehead. 他额头上形成了汗珠。|近义词：droplet, bead, particle, spherule|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From Latin *globulus*, diminutive of *globus* ("globe, sphere").
