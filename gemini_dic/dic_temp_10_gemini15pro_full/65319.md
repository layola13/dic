eugenia:/juːˈdʒiːniə/ | n. |尤金妮娅（女子名）; Eugenia植物属（桃金娘科）| A genus of tropical and subtropical trees and shrubs in the myrtle family (Myrtaceae). The name honors Prince Eugene of Savoy (1663–1736), a patron of botany. |  Eugenia is a popular name for girls in some countries.  尤金妮娅在一些国家是女孩的流行名字。

Eugenia plants are known for their fragrant flowers and edible fruits. 桃金娘科植物以其芬芳的花朵和可食用的果实而闻名。 | 近义词: (for the plant genus) Syzygium (some species formerly classified as Eugenia are now in this genus) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
