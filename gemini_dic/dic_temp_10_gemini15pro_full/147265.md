overmilk:/ˈoʊvərˌmɪlk/ | v. | 过度挤奶；过度产奶 | To extract too much milk from (a cow or other animal).  It can also refer to producing more milk than needed or desired. | The farmer overmilked the cows, which caused them discomfort. 农民过度挤奶，导致奶牛不舒服。

The dairy farm overmilked its cows in an attempt to increase profits.  这家奶牛场为了增加利润而过度挤奶。 | 近义词: overproduce | 反义词: undermilk | 词性: 动词
