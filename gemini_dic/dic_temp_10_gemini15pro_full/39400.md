conjuror:/ˈkʌndʒərər/ (US), /ˈkʌndʒərə(r)/ (UK)| n.|魔术师，巫师|A person who performs magic tricks, especially one who appears to make things appear or disappear.|The conjuror pulled a rabbit out of his hat. 魔术师从帽子里拉出一只兔子。|Synonyms: magician, illusionist, prestidigitator|Antonyms: None|Noun

conjure:/ˈkʌndʒər/ (US), /ˈkʌndʒə(r)/ (UK)| v.|变戏法；用魔法召唤|To make something appear or disappear as if by magic; to summon something by magic.|He conjured a dove from thin air. 他凭空变出一只鸽子。|Synonyms: evoke, summon, materialize|Antonyms: banish, dispel|Verb
