mechanise:/ˈmɛkənaɪz/ (UK) /ˈmɛkəˌnaɪz/ (US)| v. |使机械化；机械化操作|To equip with machinery, especially in order to replace human or animal labor.|The factory was mechanised in the 1950s.  这家工厂在20世纪50年代实现了机械化。|
They decided to mechanise the process of sorting the mail. 他们决定将邮件分拣过程机械化。|
Synonyms: automate, motorize|Antonyms: manualise, demechanise|Verb
