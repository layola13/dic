huti: /ˈhuːti/ | n. | 小屋，棚屋 | A small, simple dwelling, often temporary or makeshift.  A hut is a small, basic shelter, usually made of natural materials like wood, mud, or grass. |  The shepherds lived in hutis during the summer months. 牧羊人在夏季住在小屋里。| synonyms: hut, cabin, shack, shanty | antonyms: mansion, palace | noun

huti: /ˈhuːti/ | n. | 茅草屋 | A house with a thatched roof. A huti can specifically refer to a house with a roof made of straw or reeds. | They built a huti on the beach for shade. 他们在海滩上建造了一间茅草屋遮阳。| synonyms: thatch house, thatched cottage | antonyms: modern house, concrete building | noun
