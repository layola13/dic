good-timer:/ˈɡʊdˈtaɪmər/
n.|享乐主义者；喜欢玩乐的人|A person who enjoys having a good time, especially in a social setting and often involving parties or other forms of entertainment.|He's a real good-timer, always up for a party. 他是个真正的享乐主义者，总是乐于参加聚会。|近义词：party animal, reveler, carouser|反义词：killjoy, stick-in-the-mud|词性:名词
