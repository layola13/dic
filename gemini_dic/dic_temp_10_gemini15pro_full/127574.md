nectarian:/nɛkˈtɛəriən/| adj.|甘甜的，美味的；像花蜜的|Resembling nectar; sweet and delicious; extremely pleasing to the senses.|The cake had a rich, nectarian flavor.  这蛋糕有着浓郁的、甘甜的味道。|近义词：delicious, sweet, luscious, ambrosial|反义词：bitter, sour, unpleasant|adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *nectar* + *-ian*.  From Latin *nectar*, from Greek νέκταρ (néktar, “drink of the gods”).
