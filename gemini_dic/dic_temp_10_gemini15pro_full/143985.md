outranking:/ˌaʊtˈræŋkɪŋ/| v. | 超越，级别高于 | Surpassing in rank or importance; ranking higher than. |  Our team is outranking all the others in the league this year. 今年我们队在联赛中排名高于所有其他队伍。|  The new product is outranking the competition in sales.  这款新产品在销售方面超越了竞争对手。| 近义词: surpassing, exceeding, outstripping, bettering | 反义词: trailing, lagging, falling behind | 动词

outrank:/ˌaʊtˈræŋk/| v. | 比…级别高，比…地位高 | To be of a higher rank than. |  A colonel outranks a major. 上校的级别高于少校。| Captains outrank lieutenants in the navy. 海军上尉的级别高于海军中尉。| 近义词: surpass, exceed | 反义词:  be subordinate to | 动词
