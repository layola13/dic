excurrent:/ˈɛksˌkɜːrənt/ /ˈɛksˌkʌrənt/| adj.| 外伸的，向外伸出的；(尤指树干) 直立的，笔直的|Extending beyond the apex or margin, as a nerve in a leaf or a vein in an insect's wing.  (Of a tree trunk) continuing to the top without dividing or forking.|The pine tree has an excurrent form, with a single, straight trunk.|近义词： projecting, protruding, emergent, upright, erect |反义词: decurrent, included|词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *excurrēns*, present participle of *excurrere* ("to run out, project"), from *ex-* ("out of") + *currere* ("to run").
