nonjudicial:/ˌnɑːnʤuːˈdɪʃəl/
adjective
非司法的；非审判的
Not judicial; not done or occurring in court.
The dispute was settled through nonjudicial means.  这个纠纷是通过非司法途径解决的。
The organization provides nonjudicial conflict resolution services.  该组织提供非司法冲突解决服务。

近义词: extrajudicial, informal, nonlegal
反义词: judicial, legal

词性: adjective
词源: non- (not) + judicial (relating to the administration of justice)
