abstinently: /æbˈstɪnəntli/
adverb
禁欲地，节制地
In a manner characterized by abstaining, especially from alcohol; temperately.
He lived abstinently, avoiding all forms of excess.  他过着禁欲的生活，避免各种过度行为。

She ate abstinently during the week to prepare for the feast. 她在一周内饮食节制，为盛宴做准备。

近义词: temperately, moderately, soberly
反义词: excessively, immoderately, intemperately
词性: adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin abstinens (present participle of abstinere "to hold back from"), from abs- "from, away" + tenere "to hold."

USAGE->
She spoke abstinently about her past struggles with addiction. 她节制地谈到了她过去与成瘾的斗争。

He behaved abstinently at the party, declining all alcoholic beverages.  他在聚会上表现得很节制，拒绝了所有酒精饮料。


PHRASE->
live abstinently =  过着禁欲的生活
eat abstinently =  饮食节制
behave abstinently = 表现节制
speak abstinently =  说话谨慎/节制
dress abstinently =  穿着朴素/保守
