nonchallenging:/ˌnɒnˈtʃælɪndʒɪŋ/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˈtʃælɪndʒɪŋ/
adjective
不具挑战性的，容易的
Not demanding; easy.
The hike was nonchallenging and suitable for beginners. 这次徒步旅行没有什么挑战性，适合初学者。
The quiz was nonchallenging, and everyone passed.  这个测验很简单，每个人都通过了。
近义词:easy, simple, undemanding, straightforward
反义词:challenging, difficult, demanding, arduous
词性:adjective
