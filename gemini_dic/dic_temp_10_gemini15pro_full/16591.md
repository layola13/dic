baro:/ˈbɛəroʊ/ (US), /ˈbærəʊ/ (UK)| n. |气压计；大气压力；晴雨表|An instrument that measures atmospheric pressure.|例句：The baro indicated a drop in pressure. 气压计显示气压下降。

例句：The baro was falling rapidly, suggesting a storm was approaching. 晴雨表迅速下降，表明暴风雨即将来临。|近义词：barometer, pressure gauge|反义词: None|词性:noun


Etymology:
From Greek *báros* "weight" + *-meter* "measurer". Shortened form of *barometer*.
