outspeed:/ˌaʊtˈspiːd/ | v. | 超速; 比…速度更快 | To move faster than something or someone else. | The new car can outspeed most other vehicles on the road. 这辆新车比路上大多数其他车辆都快。|  The cheetah can outspeed any other land animal. 猎豹比任何其他陆地动物都快。 | 近义词：outrun, surpass, exceed | 反义词: lag behind, trail | 词性:动词

词源: out- + speed.  "Out-" is a prefix meaning "beyond, surpassing," and "speed" refers to rapidity of movement.
