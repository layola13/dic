attach:/əˈtætʃ/ | v. | 附加，连接，贴上；使依恋 | To fasten or join one thing to another; to connect something with something else; to cause someone to become emotionally close to someone or something. | I attached a file to the email. 我把一个文件附加到邮件里。

The hospital is attached to the medical school. 这家医院附属于医学院。

She is deeply attached to her family. 她对她的家人感情很深。 | 近义词： affix, append, connect, join, bind, link | 反义词： detach, remove, separate | 词性：动词
