free-trade:/ˌfriː ˈtreɪd/
|n.|自由贸易|International trade left to its natural course without tariffs, quotas, or other restrictions.|The benefits of free-trade are often debated.|近义词: open trade, laissez-faire trade|反义词: protectionism|noun

free-trade:/ˌfriː ˈtreɪd/
|adj.|自由贸易的|Relating to, based on, or supporting the principles of free trade.|A free-trade agreement was signed between the two countries.|近义词: open market, deregulated|反义词: protectionist|adjective
