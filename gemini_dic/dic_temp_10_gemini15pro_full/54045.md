discussive:/dɪˈskʌsɪv/| adj.|讨论的，商讨的，爱讨论的|inclined to discuss or debate; characterized by or given to discussion|The meeting was very discussive, with everyone sharing their ideas.  这次会议充满了讨论，每个人都分享了他们的想法。|例句：The class had a discussive atmosphere, encouraging open dialogue.  课堂气氛很活跃，鼓励公开对话。|近义词：talkative, conversational, argumentative, deliberative|反义词:reserved, reticent, taciturn|词性:adjective

Etymology: From discuss + -ive.  Discuss comes from the Latin discutere "to shake apart, scatter, disperse," from dis- "apart" + quatere "to shake."  The meaning shifted to "examine, investigate" and then to "talk over, debate."
