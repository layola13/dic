deludes:/dɪˈluːdz/
verb
欺骗，哄骗
misleads or tricks (someone)
He deluded himself into thinking he could win. 他欺骗自己，以为自己能赢。
Synonyms: deceives, misleads, tricks, fools
Antonyms: enlightens, informs, undeceives
词性: 动词

deluding:/dɪˈluːdɪŋ/
verb
欺骗，哄骗(现在分词)
present participle of delude
He was deluding himself into thinking he was invisible. 他在欺骗自己，以为自己隐形了。
Synonyms: deceiving, misleading, tricking, fooling
Antonyms: enlightening, informing, undeceiving
词性: 动词

deluded:/dɪˈluːdɪd/
adjective
受骗的，被迷惑的
believing something that is not true
The deluded man thought he could fly.  这个受骗的人认为自己能飞。
Synonyms: deceived, misled, tricked, fooled
Antonyms: enlightened, informed, undeceived
词性: 形容词


词源:  From Latin *dēlūdō (“I play false, mock, deceive”), from dē (“down, away, from”) + lūdō (“I play”).
