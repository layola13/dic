frolics:/ˈfrɑːlɪks/ /ˈfrɒlɪks/ | n. vi.|嬉戏，玩耍|Acts of playful behavior, especially jumping and running about happily.|The lambs were frolicking in the meadow. 羊羔在草地上嬉戏。|近义词：gambol, romp, caper, play, sport|反义词：mope, sulk|noun
frolics:/ˈfrɑːlɪks/ /ˈfrɒlɪks/ | vi.|嬉戏，玩耍|To behave playfully, especially by jumping and running about happily.|The children frolicked on the beach. 孩子们在沙滩上嬉戏。|近义词：gambol, romp, caper, play, sport|反义词：mope, sulk|verb

词源:  From Middle English *froliken, from Old French froliquer (“to leap, dance”), of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Frankish *frōh (“joyful”) (whence also Old French freoler (“to dance”)) + *lēk (“play, dance”).
