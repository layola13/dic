epauletted: /ˌɛpəˈlɛtɪd/ | adj. | 有肩章的，佩戴肩章的 | Having epaulets (shoulder ornaments). |  The epauletted officer barked orders at the troops.  这位佩戴肩章的军官向部队大声下令。 | The general, resplendent in his epauletted uniform, addressed the assembled soldiers.  将军身着华丽的肩章制服，向集合的士兵们讲话。 | 近义词: decorated, adorned, ornamented | 反义词: plain, undecorated | 词性: 形容词

词源: From French "épaulette" (small shoulder), diminutive of "épaule" (shoulder), from Latin "spatula" (shoulder blade).
