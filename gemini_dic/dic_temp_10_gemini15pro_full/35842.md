cockneyfied:/ˈkɒkniˌfaɪd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːkniˌfaɪd/ (US)| adj.|伦敦东区化的，带有伦敦东区口音或特色的|Having characteristics of a Cockney, especially in speech.|Her accent was distinctly cockneyfied after living in London for so long.  在她伦敦住了这么久之后，她的口音明显带有伦敦东区特色。|The play was a cockneyfied version of a Shakespearean classic.  这出戏是莎士比亚经典剧作的伦敦东区版。|Near synonyms:  Cockneyish, East End, Londonish|Antonyms:  posh, refined|adjective
