marshbanker:/ˈmɑːrʃˌbæŋkər/ | n. | 沼泽银行家; 沼泽地银行家 | A person who lives near or works in a marsh, especially one involved in banking or finance related to marsh resources or development.  It often carries a connotation of exploitation or questionable practices. |  The marshbanker bought up all the land around the estuary, hoping to develop it into luxury housing.  这个沼泽银行家买下了河口周围所有的土地，希望把它开发成豪宅。| Near synonyms: developer, speculator, financier | Antonyms: conservationist, environmentalist | noun

marshbanker:/ˈmɑːrʃˌbæŋkər/ | n. | 沼泽地居民; 沼泽居民 | A person who lives in or near a marsh. This meaning is less common and more literal than the previous one. | The old marshbanker knew all the secrets of the swamp.  这位老沼泽居民知道沼泽的所有秘密。| Near synonyms: marsh dweller, swamp resident | Antonyms: city dweller, urbanite | noun
