depardieux:/ˌdɛpɑːrˈdjøː/ /ˌdɛpɑːrˈdjuː/
interjection
我的天啊；哎呀
An exclamation of surprise or emphasis, archaic.
Depardieux, I had forgotten all about that! 我的天啊，我完全忘记了那件事！
近义词：good heavens, good gracious, my goodness, by Jove
反义词:  (No direct antonym for an interjection of surprise.  Consider words conveying calmness like "Indeed" or "I see.")
