chiefly:/ˈtʃiːfli/| adv.|主要地，大体上，首先|Mainly, principally, primarily.  It is used to emphasize the most important part of something. |例句：The problem is chiefly one of money.  这个问题主要还是钱的问题。

例句：He works chiefly in the field of education. 他主要在教育领域工作。|近义词：mainly, primarily, principally, mostly, largely|反义词：slightly, marginally, insignificantly|词性:副词
