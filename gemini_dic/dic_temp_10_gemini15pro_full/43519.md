cravingness:/ˈkreɪvɪŋnɪs/ | n. | 渴望，热切的欲望 | The state of having a strong desire for something. | She experienced a cravingness for chocolate after dinner.  她晚饭后很想吃巧克力。 |  近义词: yearning, longing, desire, hankering | 反义词: apathy, indifference, lack of interest | 词性: 名词


Etymology:  From craving + -ness.  "Craving" comes from the Middle English "craven," meaning "to beg, implore," ultimately from Old Norse "krefja" meaning "to demand." The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives and signifies a state or quality.
