microelectronic:/ˌmaɪkroʊɪlɛkˈtrɒnɪk/ | adj. |微电子的|Relating to or involving microelectronics; made using microelectronic technology.  It pertains to the design and fabrication of very small electronic circuits and components. |Microelectronic devices have revolutionized the electronics industry. 微电子设备彻底改变了电子工业。| The microelectronic circuitry in modern smartphones is incredibly complex. 现代智能手机中的微电子电路极其复杂。|近义词:miniature, integrated, microminiature|反义词:macroelectronic|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From micro- + electronic.  "Micro-" comes from Greek "mikros" meaning "small". "Electronic" relates to electrons and their behavior in devices.
