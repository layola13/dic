good-temperednesses:/ˌɡʊdˈtɛmpərdnəsɪz/ | n. |好脾气，温和的性情 | The state or quality of being good-tempered; an even temper. | Her good-temperednesses made her a popular teacher. 她的好脾气使她成为受欢迎的老师。|Synonyms: amiability, gentleness, placidity | Antonyms: irascibility, irritability, peevishness | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From good-tempered + -ness.  "Good-tempered" itself comes from "good" + "tempered" (meaning having a particular temperament).  "Temper" ultimately derives from the Latin "temperare" (to mix, blend, moderate).
