leukemia: /luːˈkiːmiə/ /ljuːˈkiːmiə/
n.
白血病
A malignant progressive disease in which the bone marrow and other blood-forming organs produce increased numbers of immature or abnormal leukocytes. These suppress the production of normal blood cells, leading to anemia and other symptoms.
My uncle was diagnosed with leukemia last year.  我叔叔去年被诊断出患有白血病。

近义词: cancer, blood cancer
反义词:  (Leukemia doesn't have a direct antonym.  The opposite of having leukemia would be being healthy or having normal blood counts.)
词性: noun
