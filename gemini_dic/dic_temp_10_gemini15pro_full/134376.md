noninterpretability:/nɒnɪntɜːprɪtəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可解释性，无法理解性 | The noninterpretability of the model's results made it difficult to apply in practice.  This complex algorithm suffers from noninterpretability, making it hard to understand how it arrives at its predictions. | 例句：The noninterpretability of the black box AI system raised concerns about its reliability.  这个黑盒人工智能系统的不可解释性引发了人们对其可靠性的担忧。

The noninterpretability of the ancient text made it a puzzle for scholars. 这篇古代文献的不可解释性使其成为学者们的一个难题。 | 近义词：incomprehensibility, opacity, inscrutability | 反义词：interpretability, clarity, understandability | 词性: 名词
