catatonic:/ˌkætəˈtɑːnɪk/ | adj. | 紧张性精神分裂的；僵住症的 |  Relating to or characterized by catatonia, a state of immobility and unresponsiveness. |  The patient remained catatonic for several hours. 病人一动不动，毫无反应地持续了几个小时。|  The shock left him in a catatonic state.  这突如其来的打击使他处于紧张性精神分裂状态。|  Synonyms: stuporous, unresponsive, immobile, rigid, frozen | Antonyms: responsive, active, mobile, alert | adjective
