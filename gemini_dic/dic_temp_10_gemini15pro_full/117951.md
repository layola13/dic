membranology:/ˌmɛm.brəˈnɒl.ə.dʒi/
|n.|膜学|The study of membranes, their structure, function, and properties.|Membranology plays a crucial role in understanding cellular processes. 膜学在理解细胞过程中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词: membrane science, membrane biology|反义词: None|词性: noun

Etymology:
membrane + -ology

membrane (n.): From Latin *membrāna* "skin covering a limb or organ," from *membrum* "limb, member"

-ology (suffix): From Greek -λογία *-logía*, from λόγος *lógos* "word, speech, reason, study"
