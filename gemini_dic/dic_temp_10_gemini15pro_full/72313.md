flourishingly:/ˈflʌrɪʃɪŋli/
adverb
繁荣地，兴旺地
In a way that shows successful growth or development.
The business is flourishingly expanding into new markets. 这家公司正在蓬勃发展地拓展新市场。
The plants grew flourishingly in the greenhouse.  这些植物在温室里生长茂盛。
近义词: prosperously, thrivingly, successfully
反义词: poorly, weakly, failingly
词性: adverb

flourish:/ˈflʌrɪʃ/
verb
繁荣，兴旺
To grow or develop successfully.
The arts flourished during the Renaissance. 文艺复兴时期艺术蓬勃发展。
New businesses are flourishing in this area. 新企业在这个地区蓬勃发展。
近义词: thrive, prosper, boom
反义词: decline, wither, fail
词性: verb

flourish:/ˈflʌrɪʃ/
noun
繁荣，兴旺；炫耀，挥舞
A bold or extravagant gesture or action, made especially to attract attention.
He finished his speech with a flourish. 他以一个华丽的举动结束了演讲。
The conductor gave a flourish of his baton. 指挥家挥舞着他的指挥棒。
近义词: gesture, sweep, display
反义词: understatement, simplicity
词性: noun
