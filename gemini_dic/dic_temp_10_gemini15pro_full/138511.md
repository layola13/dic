now-and-then:/ˌnaʊ ən ˈðen/| adv.|偶尔，有时|Happening sometimes; occasionally.|We see each other now and then at the library. 我们偶尔会在图书馆见面。|近义词：occasionally, sometimes, from time to time, every so often, once in a while|反义词:always, constantly, frequently|词性:副词
词源:From Middle English *now and then*, equivalent to *now* + *and* + *then*.
