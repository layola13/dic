gestureless:/ˈdʒɛstʃərlɪs/
adjective
无手势的；不动声色的
Without gestures; not using gestures.
He delivered a gestureless speech, his hands remaining firmly at his sides. 他做了一个没有手势的演讲，双手始终放在身体两侧。
Near Synonyms: unemotional, impassive, stoic, restrained
Antonyms: expressive, animated, demonstrative
词性: 形容词
