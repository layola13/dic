overdeepen:/ˌoʊvərˈdiːpən/
verb
过度深化，过分加深
To make something excessively deep.  To deepen beyond what is necessary or desirable.
The dredging operation overdeepened the harbor, making it unsafe for smaller vessels.  疏浚作业使港口过度加深，对小型船只不安全。
The river channel was overdeepened by the flood, leading to erosion problems.  洪水使河道过度加深，导致了侵蚀问题。
近义词: deepen excessively, dredge too much
反义词: shoal, fill in
词性: verb
