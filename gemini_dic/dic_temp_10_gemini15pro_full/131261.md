noncatarrhal:/ˌnɒnkəˈtɑːrəl/
adjective
非卡他性的
Not pertaining to or of the nature of catarrh; without increased mucous secretions.
The patient presented with a noncatarrhal cough. 病人的咳嗽是非卡他性的。
近义词: nonmucous, dry
反义词: catarrhal

Etymology:
non- (prefix) + catarrhal (adjective)
  * non-: from Latin *nōn* meaning "not"
  * catarrhal: from Greek *katárrhous* meaning "flowing down," from *katá* "down" + *rheîn* "to flow"
