insecable:/ɪnˈsɛkəbl/ | adj. | 不可分的，不能分割的 | Not able to be separated or divided. |  The two concepts are insecable, deeply intertwined with each other. 这两个概念是不可分割的，彼此深深地交织在一起。

The bond between mother and child is often described as insecable. 母子之间的联系常被描述为不可分割的。


| 近义词: indivisible, inseparable, unbreakable | 反义词: separable, divisible, breakable | 词性: 形容词 |

词源:  From Late Latin *insecabilis, from Latin insecare (“to cut into”), from in- (“not”) + secare (“to cut”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *sek- (“to cut”).
