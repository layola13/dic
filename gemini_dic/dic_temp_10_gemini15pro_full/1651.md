acknowledge /əkˈnɒlɪdʒ/ (US) /əkˈnɑːlɪdʒ/
vt.
承认；确认；答谢
To accept or admit the truth or existence of something.

例句：
The government acknowledged the need for change. = 政府承认需要变革。

She acknowledged his presence with a nod. = 她点点头，表示注意到他的存在。


近义词：admit, recognize, accept, concede
反义词：deny, reject, dispute
词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *knowlechen*, from Old French *acognistre*, *acognoistre* (“to recognize, admit, acknowledge”), from Latin *adcognōscō*, *agcognōscō* (“to get to know, recognize”), from *ad* (“to”) + *cognōscō* (“to learn, get to know”), from *co-* (assimilated form of *com*-, “with, together”) + *gnōscō* (“to know”).
