blocklike:/ˈblɒklaɪk/ (UK) /ˈblɑːklaɪk/ (US)| adj.|块状的，像块的|Resembling a block; shaped like a solid, usually rectangular mass.|The building had a blocklike appearance, with few windows. 这栋建筑外观呈块状，窗户很少。|The cake was dense and blocklike.  这块蛋糕很密实，像块砖一样。|近义词：blocky, chunky, solid, massive|反义词: slender, thin, delicate|词性:形容词

Etymology: "block" + "-like"

"block" (n.) -  from Old French "bloc" meaning "a block of wood, lump," probably of Germanic origin.

"-like" (suffix) - Old English "-lic," meaning "having the form or appearance of."
