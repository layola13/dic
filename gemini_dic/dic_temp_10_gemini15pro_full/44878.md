crybaby:/ˈkraɪˌbeɪbi/| n. |爱哭鬼，哭鼻子的人|A person, especially a child, who cries easily or complains a lot.|例句：He's such a crybaby! He cries over everything. 他真是个爱哭鬼！什么事他都哭。

例句：Stop being a crybaby and deal with it! 别再像个爱哭鬼一样了，去处理它！|近义词：whiner, complainer, moaner|反义词：stoic|词性：名词
