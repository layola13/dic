canvasers:/ˈkænvəsərz/
n.
拉票员，游说者，调查员
People who solicit votes, support, or information, typically by going door-to-door or contacting individuals directly.
The political party sent out canvasers to drum up support before the election.  该政党在大选前派出游说者争取支持。
The charity employed canvasers to raise money for their cause.  该慈善机构雇佣了游说者为他们的目标筹款。
Synonyms:  solicitors, campaigners, canvassers
Antonyms:  recipients, respondents
