hyoglossal:/ˌhaɪoʊˈɡlɒsəl/ | adj. | 舌骨舌的；舌骨舌肌的 | Relating to the hyoid bone and the tongue; denoting a muscle which arises from the hyoid bone and is inserted into the tongue. | The hyoglossal muscle depresses the tongue. 舌骨舌肌降低舌头的位置。 | The hyoglossal nerve runs alongside the hyoglossal muscle. 舌下神经沿着舌骨舌肌延伸。 |  glossohyoid | N/A | adjective
