forested:/ˈfɔːrɪstɪd/ | adj. |  森林覆盖的；树木繁茂的 | Covered with forest; wooded. |  The area is densely forested.  这个地区森林茂密。 |  wooded, timbered, sylvan |  bare, deforested, treeless | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From forest + -ed.  "Forest" comes from Old French "forest" (ultimately from Latin "foris," meaning "outside").  The suffix "-ed" is used to form adjectives meaning "having," "characterized by," or "provided with."
