intermaxillary:/ˌɪntərˈmæksɪləri/| adj.|颌间的，上颌间的|Situated between the maxillae; pertaining to the premaxillae.|The intermaxillary suture joins the two halves of the maxilla.|近义词:mid-maxillary, median maxillary|反义词:(No direct antonym, possibly extramaxillary)|词性:adjective

intermaxillary:/ˌɪntərˈmæksɪləri/| n.|颌间骨|A small bone between the two maxillae, especially the premaxilla in most vertebrates.|The intermaxillary bears the incisor teeth.|近义词:premaxilla, incisive bone|反义词:(No direct antonym)|词性:noun
