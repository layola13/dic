gooier:/ˈɡuːiər/| adj.|更粘的，更甜的|More sticky or sweet; more sentimental or excessively emotional.|The caramel sauce was gooier than I expected.  焦糖酱比我预想的更粘稠。|The movie was a bit too gooier for my taste. 这部电影对我来说有点太煽情了。|近义词：stickier, sweeter, mushy, sentimental|反义词：less sticky, less sweet, dry, unsentimental|词性:形容词

gooey:/ˈɡuːi/| adj.|粘的，甜的|Sticky or sweet; sentimental or excessively emotional.|The chocolate cake was gooey and delicious. 巧克力蛋糕很粘稠，很好吃。|He gave her a gooey look. 他给了她一个深情的眼神。|近义词：sticky, sweet, mushy, sentimental|反义词：dry, unsentimental|词性:形容词

词源: From goo + -y/-ier.  "Goo" itself likely imitates the sound of something sticky.
