gustily:/ˈɡʌstɪli/| adv.|狂暴地，阵阵猛烈地|In a gusty manner; with strong, sudden bursts of wind.|The wind blew gustily, rattling the windows.  风狂暴地吹着，窗户嘎嘎作响。|近义词：fiercely, violently, boisterously|反义词：gently, calmly, softly|词性:副词
词源: From gust + -ily.  来自gust（阵风）+ -ily（副词后缀）。
