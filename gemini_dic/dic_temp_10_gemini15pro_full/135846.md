nonprosaically:/ˌnɑːnproʊˈzeɪɪkli/ | adv. | 非散文地；不平淡地；不寻常地 | In a manner that is not dull, ordinary, or commonplace; imaginatively or unusually. | She described the event nonprosaically, using vivid metaphors and evocative imagery. 她用生动的比喻和唤起共鸣的意象非同寻常地描述了这件事。 |  Near Synonyms: imaginatively, poetically, lyrically, colorfully, vividly | Antonyms: prosaically, dully, ordinarily, plainly, simply | 词性: 副词
