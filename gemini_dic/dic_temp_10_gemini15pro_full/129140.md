no-brainer:/ˌnoʊˈbreɪnər/| n. |不费脑筋的事；显而易见的选择|Something that requires little or no thought; an obvious choice or decision.  The decision to renew his contract was a no-brainer.  续签他的合同是不费脑筋的事。|Synonyms: easy decision, obvious choice, piece of cake, cinch, slam dunk|Antonyms: dilemma, difficult decision, quandary|noun
