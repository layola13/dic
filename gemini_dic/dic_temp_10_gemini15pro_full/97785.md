injudiciousness:/ɪnˌdʒuːˈdɪʃəsnəs/| n. |不明智；缺乏判断力|The state of being injudicious; lack of judgment or discretion.|His injudiciousness led to the downfall of the company. 他的不明智导致了公司的垮台。|近义词：folly, imprudence, recklessness, indiscretion|反义词: wisdom, prudence, judiciousness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From in- (not) + judicious (wise, showing good judgment) + -ness (forming nouns denoting a state or quality).  Judicious ultimately comes from Latin *judicium* (judgment).
