commercializers: /kəˈmɜːrʃəlaɪzərz/
noun
商业化者；商品化者
People or organizations that turn something (like an invention or a discovery) into a product or service that can be sold for profit.
The university partnered with commercializers to bring their research to market. 大学与商业化者合作将其研究成果推向市场。

The commercializers saw the potential in the new technology and invested heavily.  商业化者看到了新技术的潜力，并进行了大量投资。


近义词: marketers, promoters, developers, entrepreneurs
反义词: researchers, inventors (sometimes)
词性: noun (plural)
