outstudying: /ˌaʊtˈstʌdiɪŋ/
verb
学习胜过，比...学习更努力
Surpassing someone in studying; studying longer or more effectively than another.
He was outstudying everyone in his class to prepare for the exams. 他为了准备考试，学习比班上任何人都努力。
Synonyms: outworking, surpassing, excelling
Antonyms: slacking, neglecting, underperforming
词性: 动词
