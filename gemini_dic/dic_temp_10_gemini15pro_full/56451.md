dolomize:/ˈdɒləmaɪz/ (US) /ˈdɒləˌmaɪz/ (UK)| v. |白云石化|To convert (limestone or other calcareous rock) into dolomite.|The process has dolomitized the limestone over millions of years.  这个过程使石灰岩在数百万年中白云石化。|synonyms: alter, change, convert, metamorphose |antonyms:  N/A |verb
dolomite:/ˈdɒləmaɪt/ (US) /ˈdɒləˌmaɪt/ (UK)| n. |白云石，白云岩|A carbonate mineral consisting of calcium magnesium carbonate CaMg(CO3)2, occurring in crystals and in masses as a sedimentary rock.|Dolomite is commonly used in construction. 白云石常用于建筑。|synonyms: dolostone, magnesium limestone |antonyms: N/A |noun
