espaliering:/ɪˈspæliəring/
n.
支撑树木（使其沿墙或框架生长）; 整枝; 沿墙（或框架）生长;
the training of a tree or shrub to grow flat against a wall, often in an artistic shape.
The espaliering of fruit trees maximizes sunlight exposure.  将果树沿墙（或框架）生长可以最大限度地提高阳光照射。

espalier:/ɪˈspæliər/
n.
棚架; 支架;
a framework or lattice on which trees or shrubs are trained to grow flat against a wall, often in an artistic shape.
The espalier supported the climbing roses.  棚架支撑着攀缘的玫瑰。

vt.
