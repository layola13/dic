greater: /ˈɡreɪtər/ | adj. | 更大的，更大的量；更强的；更伟大的；（范围或规模）更大的；（数量）更多的；（程度）更大的 |  comparative of great |  He showed greater courage than any of us. 他表现出的勇气比我们任何一个人都要大。 | 近义词: larger, bigger, superior, more significant | 反义词: smaller, lesser, inferior | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *greter, *gretter, from Old English grēatra (“greater, larger, more”), comparative of great (“great”), from Proto-Germanic *grautizōn (“greater”), comparative of *grautaz (“great”). Cognate with West Frisian grutter (“greater”), Dutch groter (“greater”), German größer (“greater”), Danish større (“greater”).
