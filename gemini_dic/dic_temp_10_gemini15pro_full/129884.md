non-intervener:/ˌnɒnˌɪntəˈviːnə(r)/ | n. | 非干预者，不干涉者 | A person or country that does not intervene in a conflict or dispute. |  The country remained a non-intervener in the regional war.  这个国家在地区战争中保持中立，不干预。| Synonyms: neutral, bystander, onlooker | Antonyms: intervener, meddler, interferer | noun

non-intervening:/ˌnɒnˌɪntəˈviːnɪŋ/ | adj. | 不干预的，不干涉的 | Not intervening; not interfering. | The non-intervening policy allowed the situation to escalate. 不干预政策导致局势升级。| Synonyms: neutral, passive, uninvolved | Antonyms: intervening, interfering, meddlering | adjective
