clef:/klɛf/
n.
谱号
A symbol placed at the beginning of a staff in music notation to indicate the pitch of the notes written on it.
The treble clef is used for higher notes. 高音谱号用于较高的音符。

clef:/klɛf/
n.
关键，线索
Something serving as a key to a mystery or problem.  (Figurative, less common usage)
The discovery of the old diary provided the clef to understanding her grandmother's life.  发现这本日记为了解她祖母的生活提供了线索。


Etymology: From Old French clef, clef "key," from Latin clavis "key."

近义词: key, symbol (in musical context), clue (in figurative context)
反义词:  (No direct antonym for musical clef. Figuratively, perhaps "misdirection")
词性: noun
