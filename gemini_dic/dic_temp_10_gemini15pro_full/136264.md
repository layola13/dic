nonrepentances:/ˌnɒnrɪˈpɛntənsɪz/
noun
不悔改，不忏悔
Lack of repentance or remorse for wrongdoing.
His nonrepentance for his crimes shocked the jury.  他对自己罪行的毫无悔意震惊了陪审团。
Near Synonyms: impenitence, unrepentance, remorselessness
Antonyms: repentance, remorse, contrition
词性: 名词
