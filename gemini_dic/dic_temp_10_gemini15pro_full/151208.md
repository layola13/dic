pardo:/ˈpɑːrdoʊ/ /ˈpɑːdəʊ/| adj. n.|浅棕色的，灰褐色的；混血人；豹|A pardo is a person of mixed European and Amerindian or African ancestry, especially in Latin America. Pardo can also refer to a dull grayish-brown color.|He wore a pardo suit. 他穿了一套灰褐色的西装。|The census identified her as parda. 人口普查将她确定为混血人种。|近义词：brown, tawny, beige, mulatto (for the person)|反义词：white, black|词性: adjective, noun

pardo:/ˈpɑːrdoʊ/ /ˈpɑːdəʊ/| n.|豹 (尤指美洲豹)|The pardo stalked its prey through the jungle. 美洲豹在丛林中跟踪猎物。|近义词：jaguar, leopard|反义词:|词性: noun
