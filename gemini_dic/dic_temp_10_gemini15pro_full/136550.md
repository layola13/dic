nonsacerdotal:/ˌnɒnsækərˈdɒtəl/
adjective
非祭司的；非神职人员的
Not relating to priests or the priesthood; secular.
The nonsacerdotal duties of a pastor might include administrative tasks and community outreach.  牧师的非神职职责可能包括行政任务和社区外展。
The organization held both sacerdotal and nonsacerdotal meetings.  该组织同时举行了神职人员和非神职人员的会议。
synonyms: secular, lay, laic, profane
antonyms: sacerdotal, priestly, clerical
词性: adjective
