felsite: /ˈfɛlsaɪt/ | n. |霏细岩|A very fine-grained volcanic rock, light in color, usually grey, pink or white, and often containing crystals of quartz and feldspar.  It is typically extrusive but can also be hypabyssal.|The cliffs were composed primarily of felsite. 这些悬崖主要由霏细岩构成。|Near synonyms: rhyolite, volcanic rock|Antonym: N/A (Rock types don't have direct opposites)|Noun
