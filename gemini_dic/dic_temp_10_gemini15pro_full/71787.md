fleck:/flek/ | n. | 小斑点，斑点，少量 | a small mark or spot, especially one that is a different color from the surrounding surface; a small amount of something |  There were flecks of paint on the floor. 地板上有一些油漆斑点。| synonyms: speck, spot, dot, particle | antonyms: expanse, whole | noun

fleck:/flek/ | v. | 点缀，使有斑点 | to mark with small spots or patches of color or light | The sunlight flecked the forest floor. 阳光洒落，点缀着森林地面。 | synonyms: speckle, dot, spot, dapple | antonyms: clear, cleanse | verb
