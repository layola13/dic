decretist:/dɪˈkriːtɪst/
n.|法令学家；编纂法令者|A legal scholar specialized in decrees, especially those of canon law.|The decretist spent years compiling and analyzing papal decrees.|近义词:canon lawyer, legal expert|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:Noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *decretista*, from Latin *decretum* ("decree") + *-ista* (agent suffix).
