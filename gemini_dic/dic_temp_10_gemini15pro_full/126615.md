naïve:/naɪˈiːv/ /nɑːˈiːf/| adj.|天真的，幼稚的，轻信的|Showing a lack of experience, wisdom, or judgment.|She was a naive young woman who believed everything she was told. 她是一个天真的年轻女子，对她所说的一切都深信不疑。|Synonyms: innocent, ingenuous, guileless, unsophisticated, credulous|Antonyms: sophisticated, worldly, cynical, skeptical, astute|adjective

naif:/naɪˈiːf/ /nɑːˈiːf/| n.|天真的人，幼稚的人|A naive person.|He's such a naif, he believes everything he reads on the internet. 他真是个天真的人，他相信在网上读到的一切。|Synonyms: innocent, ingenue|Antonyms: sophisticate, cynic|noun
