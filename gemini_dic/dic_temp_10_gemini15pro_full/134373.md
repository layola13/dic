noninterpolating:/ˌnɒnɪnˈtɜːpəleɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非插值的
Not using or involving interpolation; specifically (of a function) not passing exactly through a given set of data points.
The graph shows a noninterpolating spline curve.  这张图显示了一条非插值样条曲线。
non-interpolating, non interpolating
