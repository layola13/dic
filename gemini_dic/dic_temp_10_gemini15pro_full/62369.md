endlessly:/ˈɛndləsli/| adv.|无休止地，不断地|Without end; incessantly.|The waves crashed endlessly against the shore. 海浪不停地拍打着海岸。|近义词:incessantly, perpetually, continuously, eternally|反义词:finitely, briefly, shortly|词性:副词
Etymology: From "endless" + "-ly." "Endless" comes from Old English "endeleas," from "ende" (end) + "-leas" (without).
