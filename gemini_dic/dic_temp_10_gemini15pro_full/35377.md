co-occurring:/koʊ əˈkɜːrɪŋ/| adj.|共同发生，同时出现|Happening or existing at the same time or in the same place.|Researchers are studying the co-occurring disorders of depression and anxiety. 研究人员正在研究抑郁症和焦虑症这两种同时发生的疾病。|The study examines the co-occurring use of alcohol and tobacco. 这项研究调查了同时使用酒精和烟草的情况。|concurrent, coincident, simultaneous|separate, independent|adjective
