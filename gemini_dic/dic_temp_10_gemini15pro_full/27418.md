cameronian: /ˌkæməˈroʊniən/ | n. adj.|卡梅伦派信徒，卡梅伦派的|A follower of Richard Cameron, a Scottish religious reformer of the late 17th century, known for their strict Presbyterianism and opposition to the Stuart monarchy.  Also used as an adjective to describe things related to this group.|例句：The Cameronians were a fiercely independent group. 卡梅伦派信徒是一个非常独立的群体。

例句：He was a staunch Cameronian. 他是一个坚定的卡梅伦派信徒。|近义词：Covenanter, Presbyterian|反义词: Episcopalian|词性:名词，形容词
