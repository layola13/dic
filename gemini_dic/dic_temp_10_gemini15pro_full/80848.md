gourd:/ɡɔːrd/ (US) /ɡɔːd/ (UK)| n. |葫芦；瓜；瓠|A fleshy, typically large fruit with a hard rind, some varieties of which are edible.  Gourds also refer to the dried, hollowed-out shells of these fruits, often used as containers or utensils.|We used the gourd as a water bottle. 我们用葫芦当水壶。|Synonyms: calabash, pumpkin, squash|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps "fruit" in a broader sense)|noun

gourd:/ɡɔːrd/ (US) /ɡɔːd/ (UK)| adj. |葫芦形的|Shaped like a gourd.|The lamp had a gourd-shaped base.  这盏灯有一个葫芦形的底座。|Synonyms:  rounded, bulbous|Antonyms:  narrow, slender|adjective
