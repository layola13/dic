leaseholders:/ˈliːshəʊldərz/ (UK) /ˈliːsholdərz/ (US)
n.
承租人；租户
A person who holds the lease of a property; a tenant.
The leaseholders complained about the rising service charges. 承租人抱怨服务费不断上涨。

近义词:tenant, lessee
反义词:landlord, lessor
词性:noun


leaseholders:/ˈliːshəʊldərz/ (UK) /ˈliːsholdərz/ (US)
n.
租赁人
A person who holds a lease.
The leaseholders are responsible for maintaining their own apartments. 租赁人负责维护自己的公寓。


ETYMOLOGY->
From lease + -holder.  "Lease" comes from Old French "laisser" meaning "to let, allow." "-holder" is a suffix denoting someone who holds or possesses something.
