dinero:/dɪˈnɛroʊ/ (US), /dɪˈneɪroʊ/ (UK)| n. |钱，金钱|Money; cash.  It comes from Spanish.|例句：He doesn't have much dinero. 他没有多少钱。|例句：She needs more dinero to start her business. 她需要更多资金来创业。|近义词：cash, money, funds, capital|反义词:debt, liability|词性:noun
Etymology: From Old Spanish *denario < Latin dēnārius (“containing ten”), originally a Roman silver coin.
