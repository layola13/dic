epileptic:/ˌɛpɪˈlɛptɪk/| adj. n.|癫痫的，患癫痫症的；癫痫患者|Relating to or affected by epilepsy, a neurological disorder characterized by seizures.|例句：He has epileptic seizures. 他有癫痫发作。|近义词：convulsive, seizure-prone|反义词: healthy, non-epileptic|词性:形容词

epileptic:/ˌɛpɪˈlɛptɪk/| n.|癫痫患者|A person who has epilepsy.|例句：The epileptic was given medication to control his seizures.  这个癫痫病人被给予药物来控制他的癫痫发作。|近义词：seizure sufferer|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
