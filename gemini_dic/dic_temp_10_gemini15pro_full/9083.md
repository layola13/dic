antimodernisms: /ˌæntiˈmɒdərnɪzəmz/ /ˌæntiˈmɑːdərˌnɪzəmz/ | n. | 反现代主义（复数） |  Opposition to modernism, especially in the arts, culture, and social practices. It often involves a preference for traditional values and forms, and a skepticism towards the benefits of technological and social progress. | The conference explored various antimodernisms in 21st-century literature.  这次会议探讨了21世纪文学中的各种反现代主义。

Antimodernisms can manifest as nostalgia for a pre-industrial past. 反现代主义可以表现为对前工业时代的怀念。 | 近义词: traditionalism, conservatism | 反义词: modernism, progressivism | 词性: 名词(plural)
