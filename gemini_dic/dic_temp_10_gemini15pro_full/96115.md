indelible:/ɪnˈdɛlɪbl/ /ɪnˈdɛləbl/| adj.|不可磨灭的，永久的|not able to be erased or removed; permanent|例句：The experience left an indelible mark on his memory. 这段经历在他的记忆中留下了不可磨灭的印记。|近义词：permanent, lasting, unforgettable, enduring|反义词：temporary, fleeting, ephemeral|词性:形容词

indelible:/ɪnˈdɛlɪbl/ /ɪnˈdɛləbl/| adj.|（墨水、颜料等）洗不掉的| (of ink or a pen) making marks that cannot be removed.|例句：She wrote his name in indelible ink. 她用不褪色的墨水写了他的名字。|近义词：permanent, fast|反义词：washable, removable|词性:形容词
