concessive:/kənˈsesɪv/| adj.|让步的，妥协的|Granting something as a right or privilege; acknowledging or admitting.|Although she was tired, she conceded to help him with his homework. 尽管她很累，但她还是答应帮他做作业。|近义词：yielding, accommodating, granting|反义词：intransigent, unyielding|adjective

concession:/kənˈseʃən/| n.|让步，妥协，优惠|Something granted or yielded, often in response to demands; a preferential allowance or rate.|He made a concession to his employees by giving them a raise. 他对员工做出了让步，给他们加了薪。|近义词：compromise, allowance, privilege|反义词：resistance, refusal|noun
