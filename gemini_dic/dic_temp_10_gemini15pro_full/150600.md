papering:/ˈpeɪpərɪŋ/
verb
用纸糊,用壁纸裱糊
covering walls with wallpaper.
例句：They are papering the living room walls. 他们正在用壁纸裱糊客厅的墙壁。

例句：The landlord is papering over the cracks in the ceiling. 房东正在用纸糊住天花板上的裂缝。

近义词：wallpapering, covering
反义词: stripping (removing wallpaper)
词性: verb (gerund or present participle)
