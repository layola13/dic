chondropterygian: /ˌkɒndrɒptəˈrɪdʒiən/ | adj. n. | 软骨鱼类的；软骨鱼纲的 | Relating to or denoting fishes of the class Chondrichthyes, which comprises the cartilaginous fishes (such as sharks, rays, and chimaeras), having a skeleton composed primarily of cartilage. |  Sharks and rays are chondropterygian fishes.  鲨鱼和鳐鱼是软骨鱼。 |  cartilaginous | bony | adjective, noun
