hemorrhoid:/ˈheməˌrɔɪd/ (US), /ˈhɛmərɔɪd/ (UK)| n. |痔疮|A swollen vein or group of veins in the anus and rectum.|  I've been suffering from hemorrhoids lately. 我最近一直受痔疮困扰。

He went to the doctor because he had a painful hemorrhoid. 他去看医生，因为他长了个疼的痔疮。|近义词：piles|反义词: (无直接反义词)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *haemorrhoīda, from Ancient Greek αἱμορροΐς (haimorrhoís, “piles”), from αἷμα (haîma, “blood”) + ῥοῦς (rhoûs, “flowing, stream”).
