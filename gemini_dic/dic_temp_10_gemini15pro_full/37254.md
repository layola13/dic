comical:/ˈkɒmɪk(ə)l/ (US) /ˈkɑːmɪkl/ (UK)| adj.|滑稽的，好笑的|Funny or amusing, especially in a ludicrous or absurd way.|His comical expression made everyone laugh. 他滑稽的表情让大家都笑了。|The play was a comical farce. 这部戏是一出滑稽的闹剧。|近义词：funny, amusing, humorous, ludicrous, ridiculous|反义词：serious, solemn, tragic|词性:形容词

词源:
From late Middle English *comical, from late Latin *comicus, from Latin comicus (“of or belonging to comedy”), from Greek κωμικός (kōmikós), from κῶμος (kômos, “revel, merrymaking”).
