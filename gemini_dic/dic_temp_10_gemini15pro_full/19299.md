bibacious:/bɪˈbeɪʃəs/
adjective
嗜酒的，好饮的
Given to or marked by the consumption of alcoholic beverages.
He was a bibacious man, always ready for a drink. 他是个嗜酒的人，总是准备着喝酒。
My uncle is quite bibacious and enjoys a good glass of wine with dinner. 我的叔叔很喜欢喝酒，喜欢在晚餐时喝上一杯好酒。
近义词:alcoholic, boozy, inebriate
反义词:temperate, abstemious, sober
词性:adjective

词源:
From Latin bibax, bibac-  stem of bibere "to drink."
