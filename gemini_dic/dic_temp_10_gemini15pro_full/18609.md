beno: /ˈbeɪnoʊ/
noun
祝福; 祝愿; 保佑
a blessing; a benediction.  (Often used in Italian contexts.)
例句：He gave the family his beno before they departed. 他在家人出发前给了他们祝福。
例句：May you receive the beno of the saints.  愿你得到圣徒的祝福。
近义词：blessing, benediction, grace
反义词：curse, malediction
词性：n.


Etymology:
From Italian *beno*, from Latin *benus* ("good").  Related to "benevolent" and "benign."
