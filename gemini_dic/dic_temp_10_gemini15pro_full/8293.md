anti-Mexican:/ˌæntiˈmɛksɪkən/
adjective
反墨西哥的；歧视墨西哥人的
Opposed to or prejudiced against Mexico, Mexican people, or Mexican culture.
His anti-Mexican sentiment was evident in his speeches. 他的反墨西哥情绪在他的演讲中显而易见。
Synonyms: discriminatory, prejudiced, bigoted, xenophobic, racist
Antonyms: pro-Mexican, tolerant, accepting, inclusive
adjective


anti-Mexican:/ˌæntiˈmɛksɪkən/
noun
歧视墨西哥人者
A person who is prejudiced against Mexico, Mexican people, or Mexican culture.
The anti-Mexicans protested the immigration policy. 歧视墨西哥人者抗议移民政策。
Synonyms: bigot, racist, xenophobe
Antonyms:  supporter, advocate
noun
