participating:/pɑːrˈtɪsɪpeɪtɪŋ/ /pɑːrˈtɪsəˌpeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
参与的，参加的
Taking part in an activity or event.
She was a participating member of the committee. 她曾是委员会的参与成员。
The participating countries signed the agreement. 参与国签署了协议。

近义词:involved, engaged, active, contributing
反义词:non-participating, inactive, uninvolved

词性: adjective
词源: From the Latin word "participatus," the past participle of "participare," meaning "to share in."
