ikebana:/iːkəˈbɑːnə/ | n. |插花; 花道 |The Japanese art of flower arrangement, also known as kadō. It is more than simply putting flowers in a container; it is a disciplined art form in which the arrangement is a living thing where nature and humanity are brought together. |例句：She has been studying ikebana for many years. 她学习花道很多年了。

例句：The ikebana arrangement on the table was stunning. 桌上的插花作品令人惊艳。|近义词：flower arrangement, kadō |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
