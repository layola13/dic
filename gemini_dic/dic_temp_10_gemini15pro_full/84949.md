haphazardly:/ˌhæpˈhæzərdli/ | adv. | 偶然地，随意地，杂乱无章地 | In a manner lacking any obvious principle of organization.  Done or chosen at random. |  He haphazardly threw his clothes on the floor. 他随意地把衣服扔在地板上。|  The books were haphazardly arranged on the shelves. 书架上的书摆放得很杂乱。 | randomly, carelessly, arbitrarily | deliberately, systematically, methodically | adverb
