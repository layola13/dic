chenille:/ʃəˈniːl/| n. adj.|雪尼尔纱，雪尼尔线；雪尼尔织物|A soft tufted cord or yarn of silk, cotton, or worsted used in embroidery or for trimming; a fabric made from this yarn, resembling a caterpillar.  It's often used for blankets, robes, and other soft furnishings.|例句：She bought a chenille throw for the sofa. 她买了一条雪尼尔毯子盖沙发。

例句：The chenille robe felt luxuriously soft against her skin. 这件雪尼尔长袍贴在她皮肤上感觉非常柔软。|近义词：yarn, thread, fabric, textile|反义词:None|词性:Noun, Adjective
