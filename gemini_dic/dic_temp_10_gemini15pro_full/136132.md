nonreflectively:/ˌnɒnriˈflektɪvli/
| adv. | 无反思地，不加思考地 |  Without reflection; in a way that does not involve careful thought or consideration. |  She nonreflectively accepted the offer, later regretting her impulsive decision. 她不假思索地接受了这份工作，后来后悔自己冲动的决定。 | He signed the contract nonreflectively, not realizing the implications. 他不加思考地签了合同，没有意识到其中的含义。 | thoughtlessly, unthinkingly, impulsively, automatically | reflectively, thoughtfully, deliberately | adverb
