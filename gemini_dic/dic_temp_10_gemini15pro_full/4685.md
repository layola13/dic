词源:  "alers" is the plural form of "aler," which is a shortened, informal form of "allergic person" or "allergy sufferer."  The root word "allergy" comes from the German word "Allergie," coined in 1906 by Clemens von Pirquet, from the Greek "allos" (other, different) + "ergon" (work).
