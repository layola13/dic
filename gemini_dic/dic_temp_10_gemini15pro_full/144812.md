over-head-shot:  /ˈoʊvərˌhɛd ʃɑːt/ | vt. |从头顶拍摄，头顶投篮| To shoot something from above the head.  |  The director wanted to over-head-shot the entire scene. 导演想从头顶拍摄整个场景。 He over-head-shot the ball for the winning basket. 他头顶投篮，赢得了比赛。 | 近义词：shoot from above, shoot overhead | 反义词：shoot from below, shoot from ground level | 词性: verb


词源:  This word is a compound word formed from "over," "head," and "shot."  "Over" indicates a position above, "head" specifies the reference point, and "shot" can refer to either a photograph or a projectile.
