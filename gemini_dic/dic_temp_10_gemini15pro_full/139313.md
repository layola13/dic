obnoxiety:/ɒbnɒkˈzaɪəti/
n.
Obnoxiety (rarely used)
A portmanteau of obnoxious and anxiety, referring to the anxiety caused by dealing with obnoxious people or situations.
Dealing with her obnoxious neighbor gave her obnoxiety.
近义词: social anxiety, apprehension, dread
反义词: calmness, tranquility
词性: noun
