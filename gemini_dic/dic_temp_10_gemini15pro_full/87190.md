一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What is here? = 这里有什么？
I work here. = 我在这里工作。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am staying here tonight. = 我今晚待在这里。
She is working here temporarily. = 她暂时在这里工作。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have been here before. = 我以前来过这里。
He has lived here for five years. = 他在这里住了五年。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been waiting here for an hour. = 我在这里等了一个小时了。
They have been working here since last year. = 他们从去年开始就在这里工作了。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I was here yesterday. = 我昨天在这里。
She worked here last summer. = 她去年夏天在这里工作。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was standing here when it happened. = 事情发生时我正站在这里。
They were living here at the time. = 他们当时住在这里。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had been here once before. = 我以前来过这里一次。
He had already left when I arrived here. = 我到达这里时他已经离开了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been waiting here for hours before she arrived. = 在她到达之前，我已经在这里等了几个小时。
They had been living here for years before they moved. = 他们在搬家之前已经在这里住了很多年。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will be here tomorrow. = 我明天会在这里。
She will work here next year. = 她明年会在这里工作。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be staying here tomorrow night. = 我明晚会待在这里。
They will be working here next week. = 他们下周会在这里工作。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next year, I will have lived here for ten years. = 到明年，我将在这里住了十年。
She will have finished her work here by Friday. = 到星期五，她将在这里完成她的工作。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next month, I will have been working here for a year. = 到下个月，我将在这里工作一年了。
By the time he retires, he will have been living here for over twenty years. = 到他退休的时候，他将在这里住了二十多年。
