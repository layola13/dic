disgustful:/dɪsˈɡʌstfəl/| adj.|令人厌恶的；令人作呕的|Causing disgust; offensive to the senses, feelings, or morals.|The smell of the rotten food was disgustful. 腐烂食物的气味令人作呕。|近义词：revolting, repulsive, sickening, nauseating, abhorrent, odious|反义词：pleasant, agreeable, delightful, appealing|词性:形容词
disgusting:/dɪsˈɡʌstɪŋ/| adj.|令人厌恶的；令人作呕的|Causing disgust; offensive to the senses, feelings, or morals.|The behavior of some tourists is truly disgusting. 一些游客的行为真是令人厌恶。|近义词：revolting, repulsive, sickening, nauseating, abhorrent, odious|反义词：pleasant, agreeable, delightful, appealing|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *desgouster (meaning "to cause disgust"), from des- (expressing negation or reversal) + gouster (meaning "to taste").  The English word "disgust" (both noun and verb) predates "disgustful."
