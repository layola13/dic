nonurban:/nɒnˈɜːrbən/ | adj. | 非城市的，乡村的 | Not situated in a city or town; rural. |  Many nonurban areas are experiencing population growth. 许多非城市地区正在经历人口增长。|  The study focused on the economic challenges faced by nonurban communities. 这项研究的重点是非城市社区面临的经济挑战。| 近义词: rural, rustic, country, agrarian | 反义词: urban, metropolitan | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (not) + "urban" (of or relating to a city or town).  "Urban" comes from Latin "urbanus" (of the city), from "urbs" (city).
