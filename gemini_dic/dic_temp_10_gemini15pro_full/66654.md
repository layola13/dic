exiting:/ˈɛksɪtɪŋ/ /ˈɛɡzɪtɪŋ/
adjective
令人兴奋的；使人激动的
thrilling, causing excitement
例句：The exiting news thrilled everyone.  这个令人兴奋的消息使每个人都很激动。

例句：Leaving the theater after the exiting performance, the audience buzzed with enthusiasm.  看完激动人心的演出离开剧院后，观众们兴奋地议论纷纷。

近义词：thrilling, stimulating, exhilarating, interesting
反义词：boring, dull, tedious, uninteresting
词性: adjective


Etymology:  From the verb "exit" + "-ing" suffix, indicating a present participle form used as an adjective. "Exit" ultimately derives from the Latin "exitus," meaning "a going out" or "departure."
