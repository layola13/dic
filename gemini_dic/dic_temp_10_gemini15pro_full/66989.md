expertising:/ˌɛkspɜːˈtaɪzɪŋ/
n.|鉴定; 估价; 专家鉴定|The expertising of the painting confirmed it was a forgery. 这幅画的鉴定证实它是赝品。|近义词：appraisal, assessment, evaluation, valuation|反义词: N/A
词性: 名词 (gerund)

expertising:/ˌɛkspɜːˈtaɪzɪŋ/
v.|鉴定; 估价; 提供专家意见|(present participle of expertise)  He is expertising the jewels for the insurance company. 他正在为保险公司鉴定珠宝。|近义词：appraising, assessing, evaluating, valuing|反义词: N/A
词性: 动词 (present participle)


词源: From the verb "expertise," which comes from the French "expertise," meaning "skill, expert opinion." Ultimately derived from the Latin "expertus," past participle of "experiri," meaning "to try, test, experience."
