barde:/bɑːrd/ | n. |吟游诗人|A poet, traditionally one reciting epics and associated with a particular oral tradition.|例句：The barde sang tales of bravery and love. 吟游诗人歌颂着勇敢和爱情的故事。

例句：The traveling barde entertained the villagers with his music and stories.  这位旅行的吟游诗人用他的音乐和故事娱乐村民们。|近义词：poet, minstrel, troubadour |反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *barde*, from Gaulish *bardos*. Related to Irish *bard*, Welsh *bardd*. Ultimately from Proto-Celtic *bardos*.
