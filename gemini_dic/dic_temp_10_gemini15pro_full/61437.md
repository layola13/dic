emborder:/ɪmˈbɔːrdər/ | vt. | 加边，镶边 | To furnish with a border; to enclose within a border. | The tailor embordered the dress with lace.  裁缝给裙子镶上了蕾丝花边。

The queen's robe was embordered with gold thread. 女王的袍子用金线绣了边。 | 近义词：border, edge, trim, fringe | 反义词: strip, denude | 词性: 动词
