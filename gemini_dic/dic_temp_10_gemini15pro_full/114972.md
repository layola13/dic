manchuko:/mænˈtʃuːkoʊ/
n.|满洲国；伪满洲国|A puppet state established by Japan in Manchuria in 1932.|The Japanese invasion led to the establishment of Manchukuo. 日本的侵略导致了伪满洲国的建立。|
Synonyms: puppet state, Manchukuo
Antonyms: legitimate government, independent state
Etymology: From Japanese 満州国 (Manshū-koku), meaning "State of Manchuria".
Word type: Noun
