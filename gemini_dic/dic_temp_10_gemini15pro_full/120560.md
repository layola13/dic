Miramax: /ˈmɪrəˌmæks/ | n. | 米拉麦克斯 |  A film production and distribution company, known for independent and foreign films. It was founded in 1979 by Bob and Harvey Weinstein and named by combining their parents' names, Miriam and Max. | Miramax produced many successful films, such as Pulp Fiction and Shakespeare in Love. | 近义词: studio, production company | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
