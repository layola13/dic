auen:/ˈaʊən/| n.|water meadow, floodplain |A flat area of grassland next to a river that is regularly flooded.  Derived from Old English *ēa* (river) and related to the German word *Aue*.|例句：The auen provided fertile grazing land for the cattle.  河滩为牛群提供了肥沃的牧场。

例句：The wildflowers bloomed profusely in the auen during springtime. 春天，野花在河滩上盛开。|近义词：floodplain, meadow, water meadow, bottomland|反义词:upland, highland|词性:noun
