antiformalists:/ˌæntiˈfɔːrməlɪsts/
noun
反形式主义者
Those who oppose formalism, particularly in art, literature, or philosophy.  They generally believe that strict adherence to rules and conventions hinders creativity and genuine expression.
The antiformalists argued that the traditional sonnet form was too restrictive for modern poets. 反形式主义者认为传统的十四行诗形式对现代诗人来说限制太多。
The rise of antiformalists marked a shift away from rigid aesthetic principles. 反形式主义者的兴起标志着人们开始摆脱僵化的审美原则。
nonconformists, iconoclasts, rebels
formalists, traditionalists
