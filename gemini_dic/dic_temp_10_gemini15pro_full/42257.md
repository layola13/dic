counter-agent:/ˈkaʊntərˌeɪdʒənt/| n.|反作用剂；反代理人；对策|A person or thing that opposes or counteracts another. |The new drug acted as a counter-agent to the poison. 这种新药充当了毒药的解毒剂。|Synonyms: antidote, remedy, neutralizer|Antonyms:  agent, catalyst, stimulant|noun


counter-agent:/ˈkaʊntərˌeɪdʒənt/| n.|对策，反措施|A plan or action designed to counteract or neutralize an undesirable situation or development. |The government implemented several counter-agents to combat inflation. 政府实施了几项应对通货膨胀的对策。|Synonyms: countermeasure, counteractant|Antonyms:  aggravating factor, stimulant|noun
