oversecularized: /ˌoʊvərˈsɛkjələrˌaɪzd/ | adj. | 过度世俗化的 | Excessively focused on worldly or material concerns, at the expense of spiritual or religious values.  It describes a society or individual that has moved too far away from religious influence or observance. |  The oversecularized society has lost its moral compass.  这个过度世俗化的社会已经失去了道德指南针。

The author argued that the country was becoming oversecularized and losing its traditional values. 作者认为这个国家正变得过度世俗化，失去了它的传统价值观。 | 近义词: secular, worldly, materialistic | 反义词: religious, devout, pious | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "secularized" (from "secular," ultimately from Latin "saecularis," meaning "of an age, worldly").
