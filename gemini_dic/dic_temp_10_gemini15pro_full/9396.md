antiquing:/ænˈtiːkɪŋ/
n.|古董收藏；寻觅古董|The act of shopping for antiques.  The search for and purchase of antiques.|例句：She enjoys antiquing on weekends. 她喜欢在周末逛古董店。|近义词：antique collecting, antique hunting|反义词:modernizing|词性:名词

antiquing:/ænˈtiːkɪŋ/
v.|使具有古色古香|To give something an antique appearance, often by artificial means.|例句：They antiqued the furniture to make it look older. 他们把家具做旧，使它看起来更古老。|近义词：aging, distressing|反义词:modernizing|词性:动词
