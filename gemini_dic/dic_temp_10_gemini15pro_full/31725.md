cheapened:/ˈtʃiːpənd/
verb
降低…的价值；贬低；使…显得低俗
to make something seem less valuable or respected
Mass production has cheapened the image of handcrafted furniture. 大规模生产降低了手工家具的形象。
debased, degraded, depreciated
enhance, elevate, improve
v.
