noncreditableness:/ˌnɑːnˈkrɛdɪtəblnəs/
n.
不可信，不值得信任
lack of credibility; the state of being unworthy of belief or trust.
His noncreditableness made it difficult for anyone to believe his story.  他的不可信使得任何人都难以相信他的故事。
近义词:untrustworthiness, incredibility, unreliability
反义词:credibility, trustworthiness, reliability
词性:名词
