booby:/ˈbuːbi/ | n. |笨蛋，傻瓜；（尤指女性的）胸部；鰹鳥|A person, especially a woman, with large breasts. Also, a type of seabird.|She called him a booby after he made a silly mistake. 她在他犯了一个愚蠢的错误后叫他笨蛋。|Synonyms: fool, idiot, dunce, nitwit, simpleton (for person); breast, bust (for body part); gannet (for bird) |Antonyms: genius, intellect (for person) |noun

booby:/ˈbuːbi/ | adj. |愚蠢的，笨拙的|Like a booby; foolish or inept.|It was a booby mistake to leave the keys in the car. 把钥匙留在车里是一个愚蠢的错误。|Synonyms: foolish, silly, stupid, daft, idiotic |Antonyms: intelligent, smart, clever, wise |adjective
