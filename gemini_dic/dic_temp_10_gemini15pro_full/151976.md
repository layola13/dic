pasttime: /ˈpɑːstˌtaɪm/ (US) /ˈpɑːstˌtaɪm/ (UK)| n. |消遣，娱乐|An activity that someone does for pleasure in their spare time. |例句：His favorite pastime is playing the guitar. 他最喜欢的消遣是弹吉他。|近义词：hobby, recreation, leisure, amusement|反义词：work, labor, toil|词性:名词

pasttime:
Etymology:  From "past" + "time," literally meaning "time that has passed," later evolving to mean an activity done to occupy that passed time.
