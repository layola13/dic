garbaged:/ˈɡɑːrbɪdʒd/
adjective
已成垃圾的；被丢弃的；无用的
Covered with or resembling garbage; ruined or useless.
The project was garbaged after the funding was cut.  这个项目在资金被削减后就废弃了。
近义词:trashed, junked, ruined, discarded
反义词:pristine, valuable, useful
词性:adjective

garbaged:/ˈɡɑːrbɪdʒd/
verb (past tense)
把…当成垃圾丢掉
To throw (something) away as garbage.
He garbaged the old computer. 他把旧电脑当垃圾扔了。
近义词:trashed, junked, discarded, dumped
反义词:saved, kept, preserved
词性:verb
