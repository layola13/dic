maharajahs:/ˌmɑːhəˈrɑːdʒəz/
noun
土邦主，大君（maharajah的复数）
A maharajah is a ruler, especially in India.  Maharajahs were often very wealthy and powerful.
The maharajahs of India lived in opulent palaces.  印度的土邦主住在豪华的宫殿里。
synonyms:  rulers, princes, kings, potentates
antonyms: subjects, commoners
