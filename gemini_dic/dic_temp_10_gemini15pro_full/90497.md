huckstered:/ˈhʌkstərd/ | v. |兜售，叫卖；推销（尤指廉价或质量差的商品）；| to sell something by going from place to place or by advertising it a lot, especially something that is cheap or of low quality |  He huckstered his wares on the street corner. 他在街角兜售他的货物。| peddled, hawked, vended | - | verb
huckster:/ˈhʌkstər/ | n. |小贩，叫卖者；推销员（尤指廉价或质量差的商品的）| someone who sells things, especially cheap or low-quality goods, by going from place to place or by advertising them a lot | The huckster was selling fake watches.  这个小贩在卖假表。 | peddler, hawker, vendor | - | noun
