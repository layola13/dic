hypochondrium:/ˌhaɪpəˈkɒndriəm/ (US) /ˌhaɪpəˈkɒndriəm/ (UK)| n. |季肋部，上腹部|The upper region of the abdomen on either side of the epigastrium, beneath the lower ribs.|He complained of a sharp pain in his right hypochondrium.|Synonyms: upper abdomen, subcostal region|Antonyms: None|Noun


hypochondria:/ˌhaɪpəˈkɒndriə/ (US) /ˌhaɪpəˈkɒndriə/ (UK)| n. |疑病症|An abnormal anxiety about one's health, especially with an unwarranted fear that one has a serious disease.|Her hypochondria led her to believe she had a rare and fatal illness.|Synonyms: health anxiety, illness anxiety disorder|Antonyms: None|Noun
