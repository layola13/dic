friday:/ˈfraɪdeɪ/ /ˈfraɪdi/| n.|星期五|The sixth day of the week, following Thursday and preceding Saturday.  Derived from Old English "Frīgedæg," meaning "day of Frigga," the Norse goddess of marriage and motherhood.|例句：I look forward to Friday every week. 我每周都盼望着星期五。

例句：We have a meeting scheduled for Friday. 我们星期五安排了会议。
