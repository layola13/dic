disgorgement:/dɪsˈɡɔːrdʒmənt/
|n.|吐出；呕出；被迫放弃；交出(非法所得)
|The act of disgorging something, especially the emptying of the stomach by vomiting, or the giving up of something (especially ill-gotten gains) reluctantly.|
|The volcano disgorged lava and ash. 火山喷出了熔岩和火山灰。|
|近义词: expulsion, ejection, vomiting, surrender|
|反义词: retention, ingestion, withholding|
|词性: 名词|

disgorge:/dɪsˈɡɔːrdʒ/
|v.|吐出；呕出；被迫放弃；交出(非法所得)
|To discharge violently; to surrender or relinquish something (especially ill-gotten gains) unwillingly.|
|The company was forced to disgorge its ill-gotten profits. 该公司被迫交出非法所得的利润。|
|近义词: spew, vomit, relinquish, yield|
|反义词: retain, consume, hoard|
|词性: 动词|
