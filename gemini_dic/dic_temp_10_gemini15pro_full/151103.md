parasite:/ˈpærəsaɪt/| n.|寄生虫；寄生生物；食客；靠他人为生的人|An organism that lives in or on another organism (its host) and benefits by deriving nutrients at the other's expense.|The mosquito is a parasite that feeds on the blood of humans and animals. 蚊子是一种以人和动物的血液为食的寄生虫。|
|synonyms: freeloader, sponger, leech, hanger-on|
|antonyms: host, symbiont|
|noun|

parasite:/ˈpærəsaɪt/| adj.|寄生的|Living at the expense of another; parasitic.|a parasitic plant  一种寄生植物|
|synonyms: dependent,  exploitative|
|antonyms: independent, symbiotic|
|adjective|
