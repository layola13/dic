paganity:/ˈpeɪɡənɪti/| n.|异教，异教信仰|The resurgence of paganity in some parts of the world is a fascinating phenomenon. 世界某些地区异教的复兴是一个引人入胜的现象。|Paganity involves a wide range of beliefs and practices. 异教包含了广泛的信仰和实践。|近义词: heathenism, idolatry|反义词: Christianity, Islam|词性:名词

词源:
From Late Latin *paganitas*, from Latin paganus (“rustic, civilian”),  originally referring to a country dweller, later extended to those who had not adopted Christianity.  源自晚期拉丁语 *paganitas*，来自拉丁语 paganus（“乡村的，平民的”），最初指乡村居民，后来扩展到那些没有皈依基督教的人。
