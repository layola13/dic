jalopies:/dʒəˈlɒpiz/
n.
破旧的汽车；老爷车
An old, dilapidated car.
He drove up in one of his jalopies. 他开着他的一辆老爷车来了。
近义词: clunker, beater, wreck
反义词:  luxury car, sports car
词性: 名词


Etymology:
Origin uncertain, possibly from jalap, a purgative drug, implying that the cars were so bad they induced purging or vomiting. Another theory connects it to the name "Joe Lop," possibly a generic name for a scrap dealer.  First recorded in the 1920s.
