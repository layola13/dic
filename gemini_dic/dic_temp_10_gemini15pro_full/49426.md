Delors: /dəˈlɔːr/ | n. | 德洛尔 |  Jacques Delors, a French politician who served as the 8th President of the European Commission from 1985 to 1995.  He is known for his role in strengthening European integration and for the "Delors Report," which laid the groundwork for the creation of the Euro. |  Jacques Delors played a crucial role in shaping the modern European Union. 雅克·德洛尔在塑造现代欧盟方面发挥了至关重要的作用。 | synonym: statesman, leader | antonym:  (No direct antonym for a person's name, perhaps opponent or critic in a specific context) | Proper Noun
