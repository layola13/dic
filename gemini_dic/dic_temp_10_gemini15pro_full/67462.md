extollingly:/ɪkˈstəʊlɪŋli/
adv.
赞美地，颂扬地
In a manner that praises highly; with high praise.
He spoke extollingly of her courage and determination. 他高度赞扬了她的勇气和决心。
近义词:laudingly, praisingly, admiringly
反义词:critically, disparagingly
词性:副词
