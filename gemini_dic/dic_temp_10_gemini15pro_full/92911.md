ideating:/aɪˈdiːeɪtɪŋ/
|v.|构思，形成想法|The team is currently ideating on new marketing strategies. 团队目前正在构思新的营销策略。|例句：We were ideating new ways to improve the product. 我们正在构思改进产品的新方法。|近义词：brainstorming, conceptualizing, generating, innovating, devising|反义词：procrastinating, delaying|词性：动词
Etymology: From the noun "idea" + "-ate" (verb-forming suffix) + "-ing" (present participle suffix).  Ultimately from the Greek "idea" meaning "form, pattern."
