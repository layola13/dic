arras:/ˈærəs/ /ˈærɑːz/| n.|挂毯；壁毯；（尤指旧时用作婚礼背景的）绣帷|A large tapestry, typically one depicting a historical or mythological scene, often used as a backdrop at weddings. |The bride and groom stood before a magnificent arras. 新娘和新郎站在华丽的壁毯前。|Synonym: tapestry, wall hanging|Antonym: None|Noun
arras:/ˈærəs/ /ˈærɑːz/| n.|[古语] 定金，保证金 (earnest money)|A pledge; earnest money, especially that paid by a bridegroom to his bride as a token of his good intentions. This usage is archaic. |He paid the arras to secure the marriage contract.  他支付了定金以确保婚姻契约。（古语用法）|Synonym: deposit, pledge, down payment|Antonym: None|Noun
