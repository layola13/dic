hellanodic: /ˌhɛləˈnɒdɪk/
noun
裁判；仲裁人；法官 (尤指古希腊运动会中的)
a judge in the ancient Greek games, especially one who presided over the contests.
The hellanodics oversaw the Olympic Games and ensured fair play.  希腊运动会的裁判监督奥运会并确保公平竞争。
The hellanodic declared the winner of the chariot race.  裁判宣布了战车比赛的获胜者。
Synonyms: judge, umpire, referee, arbiter, adjudicator
Antonyms: contestant, competitor, player
