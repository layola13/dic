fallowing:/ˈfæloʊɪŋ/
|n.|休耕；休闲地|A period of time during which land is left unplanted.|The farmer practiced fallowing to improve soil fertility.|近义词:resting, uncultivated|反义词:cultivating, planting|词性:名词
fallowing:/ˈfæloʊɪŋ/
|adj.|休耕的|Left unplanted.|The fallowing fields were covered in wildflowers.|近义词:idle, resting|反义词:cultivated, planted|词性:形容词

词源：From Middle English *falwyng, *falewyng, present participle of falwen, fallowen (“to make fallow, plow but not sow”), from Old English fealgian (“to harrow, break up land”).
