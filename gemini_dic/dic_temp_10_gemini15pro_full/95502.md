inclination:/ˌɪnklɪˈneɪʃn/| n. |倾向，爱好，倾斜|A feeling that makes you want to do something.  A slope or the angle at which something slopes.|She had an inclination to agree with him. 她倾向于同意他的观点。|近义词：tendency, propensity, leaning, disposition, proclivity, predilection, bias, preference|反义词:aversion, disinclination|noun
