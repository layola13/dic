chaetetes:/kiːˈtiːtiːz/
n.
Chaetetes, a genus of extinct tabulate corals
An extinct genus of tabulate corals characterized by small, closely packed, prismatic corallites.  These corals were common in shallow marine environments during the Paleozoic Era.
Chaetetes fossils are often found in Devonian-aged limestone.  这些Chaetetes化石经常在泥盆纪时代的石灰岩中发现。
synonym:  tabulate coral, fossil coral
antonym: (No direct antonym for a genus. Could consider "modern coral" as a contrasting term, but not a true antonym.)
