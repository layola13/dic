haematachometry:/ˌhiːmətəˈkɒmɪtri/ | n. | 血流速度测定(法) | The measurement of the speed of blood flow. | Haematachometry is used to assess cardiovascular health. 血流速度测定用于评估心血管健康。| Haematachometry is essential for understanding blood circulation. 血流速度测定对于理解血液循环至关重要。 | 近义词: blood flow measurement,  blood velocity measurement | 反义词:  None | noun
