cleavingly:/ˈkliːvɪŋli/
adv.
紧贴地，粘着地，依附地
In a way that cleaves or adheres closely.
The ivy cleavingly embraced the old stone wall.  常春藤紧紧地攀附在古老的石墙上。
Near synonyms:  adheringly, clingingly, tenaciously
Opposite: loosely, detachingly
词性: 副词


cleave (verb 1) /kliːv/
v.
劈开；砍开；裂开
To split or divide by force, especially with a sharp instrument; to crack or fissure.
He cleaved the wood with an axe. 他用斧头劈开了木头。
Near synonyms: split, chop, hack, divide
Opposite: join, unite, combine
词性: 动词

cleave (verb 2) /kliːv/
v.
粘附；坚持；依附
To adhere firmly and closely; to stick fast; to remain faithful to.
She cleaved to her principles despite the pressure. 尽管压力很大，她仍然坚持自己的原则。
Near synonyms: adhere, cling, stick, hold fast
Opposite: detach, separate, abandon
词性: 动词
