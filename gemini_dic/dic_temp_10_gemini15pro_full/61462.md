embracement:/ɪmˈbreɪsmənt/| n.|拥抱，接受，信奉|The embracement of new technologies has transformed the industry. 新技术的应用改变了这个行业。|例句：Her embracement of the challenge was admirable. 她欣然接受挑战的精神令人钦佩。

例句：The company's embracement of sustainable practices has boosted its image.  该公司对可持续实践的采纳提升了其形象。

近义词：acceptance, adoption, approval, hug, clasp|反义词:rejection, refusal, denial|词性:名词

词源：Late Middle English: from embrace + -ment.
