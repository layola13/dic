illuminators:/ɪˈluːmɪneɪtərz/
noun
照明器；照明者；阐明者
A person or thing that illuminates something.
The illuminators of the manuscript used vibrant colors and gold leaf.  手稿的照明者使用了鲜艳的色彩和金箔。
近义词:lighters, brighteners, elucidators
反义词:darkeners, obscurers
