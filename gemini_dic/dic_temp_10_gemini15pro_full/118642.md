messing-around:/ˈmɛsɪŋ əˈraʊnd/ | n. | 胡闹，闲逛，瞎搞 | Spending time doing things with no particular purpose, often in a playful or mischievous way. |  He spent the whole afternoon messing around in the garage. 他整个下午都在车库里瞎搞。|  synonyms: fooling around, playing around, dawdling, loitering | antonyms: working, focusing, concentrating | noun

messing-around:/ˈmɛsɪŋ əˈraʊnd/ | adj. (informal) | 搞砸了的，混乱的 | Characterized by a lack of order or organization. | The project was a messing-around affair from the start. 这个项目从一开始就是一团糟。 | synonyms: disorganized, chaotic, messy | antonyms: organized, tidy, orderly | adjective
