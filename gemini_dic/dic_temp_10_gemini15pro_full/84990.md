haptic:/ˈhæptɪk/| adj.|触觉的，触觉感知的|Relating to the sense of touch.|例句：Haptic feedback is used in some video game controllers to enhance the gaming experience.  一些电子游戏控制器使用触觉反馈来增强游戏体验。

例句：The museum has a haptic exhibit where visitors can touch different textures.  这家博物馆有一个触觉展览，参观者可以触摸不同的纹理。|近义词：tactile, kinesthetic|反义词: non-tactile |词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *hapt(ikós)*  "able to grasp," from *haptesthai* "to fasten oneself, touch, cling to, grasp."
