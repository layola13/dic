chasm: /ˈkæzəm/ | n. | 深渊，裂缝，巨大差距 | A deep, gaping fissure; a marked division or difference of opinion, feeling, etc. |  The earthquake created a chasm in the earth. 地震在地面上造成了一条裂缝。| synonyms: abyss, gorge, rift, gap, divide | antonyms: closure, bridge, connection | noun

chasm: /ˈkæzəm/ | n. |  巨大分歧，意见分歧 | A profound difference of opinion, feeling, interests, etc. | There is a growing chasm between the rich and the poor. 贫富差距越来越大。| synonyms: gulf, breach, schism | antonyms: agreement, accord, harmony | noun
