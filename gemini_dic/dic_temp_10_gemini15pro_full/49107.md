deindustrialization:/diːɪnˌdʌstriəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
去工业化
The process by which an economy is transformed from primarily manufacturing-based to one based on services.
The city experienced a period of rapid deindustrialization in the 1980s.  这座城市在20世纪80年代经历了快速的去工业化时期。
Detroit's decline is often cited as a prime example of deindustrialization.  底特律的衰落经常被认为是去工业化的主要例子。
近义词:industrial decline, economic restructuring
反义词:industrialization
词性:名词
