circumspection:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈspɛkʃən/| n.|谨慎，小心，慎重|The lawyer advised his client to exercise circumspection in all his dealings with the police. 律师建议他的客户在与警方打交道时要谨慎行事。|例句：He showed great circumspection in his choice of words. 他在措辞上表现得非常谨慎。

例句：She proceeded with circumspection, carefully considering each step. 她谨慎地进行，仔细考虑每一步。


|近义词：caution, prudence, care, vigilance, discretion|反义词：recklessness, carelessness, imprudence|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *circumspectiōn- (stem of circumspectiō ), from Latin circumspectus (past participle of circumspicere ‘look around’), from circum- ‘around’ + specere ‘to look’.
