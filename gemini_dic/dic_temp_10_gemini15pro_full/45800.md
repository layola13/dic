custodialisms:/kʌˈstoʊdiəˌlɪzəmz/
|n.|保管主义；监护主义|The practice of emphasizing care, control, and supervision, often in institutional settings, prioritizing safety and security over individual autonomy and rights.  This can manifest in various contexts, including education, healthcare, and criminal justice.|The school's strict rules and regulations reflect its custodialisms, prioritizing order over student freedom.|近义词:paternalism, institutionalism|反义词:autonomy, self-determination|词性:名词
