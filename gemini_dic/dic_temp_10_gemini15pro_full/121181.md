misdisposition:/ˌmɪsdɪspəˈzɪʃən/
n.
倾向；气质；（尤指）不良倾向
An inclination or tendency, especially one that is not desirable.
He showed a misdisposition to argue with everyone. 他表现出与每个人争论的倾向。
inclination, tendency, propensity, proclivity
disposition, inclination

词源:  From mis- + disposition.  "Mis-"  is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "wrong," "bad," or "mistaken." "Disposition" comes from Old French and ultimately Latin, meaning "arrangement" or "inclination."  Therefore, "misdisposition" literally means a "wrong or bad inclination."
