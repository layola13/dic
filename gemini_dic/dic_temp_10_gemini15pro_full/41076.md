coppering:/ˈkɒpərɪŋ/ | n. |镀铜; 铜锈; 铜钱; 铜币|The act or process of coating with copper, or the copper coating itself. Also, the appearance of a reddish-brown discoloration resembling copper, especially on the coat of a horse.|The old statue showed signs of coppering.  这尊古老的雕像显现出铜锈的痕迹。|近义词:plating, coating, bronzing |反义词:(No direct antonym, consider stripping, removing) |词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From copper + -ing.  Copper itself comes from Old English coper, from Latin cuprum, referring to the island of Cyprus, where copper was mined.
