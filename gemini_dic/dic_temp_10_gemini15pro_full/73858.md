formulated:/ˈfɔːrmjəleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 制定的，公式化的 |  Created or devised methodically and systematically.  Expressing something precisely and clearly. | The scientist formulated a new theory.  科学家制定了一个新理论。 | devised, planned, articulated, expressed, specified | improvised, unplanned, extemporaneous | adjective

formulate:/ˈfɔːrmjəleɪt/ | v. | 规划，制定；明确地表达 | Create or devise methodically (a strategy or a proposal). Express (a thought or idea) clearly and precisely. |  The committee formulated a plan to address the issue. 委员会制定了一个解决问题的计划。| devise, plan, articulate, express, specify | improvise, avoid, neglect | verb

Etymology: From Late Latin *formulātus, past participle of formulāre “to reduce to a formula,” from Latin formula “formula, rule, method.”
