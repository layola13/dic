liberticide:/lɪˈbɜːrtɪsaɪd/
n.
毁灭自由,扼杀自由
The destruction of liberty; the act of suppressing or destroying liberty.
The dictator's policies were acts of liberticide, silencing dissent and crushing individual freedoms.  独裁者的政策是扼杀自由的行为，压制异议，摧毁个人自由。
The government's increasing surveillance powers are seen by some as a slow liberticide. 政府日益增长的监控权力被一些人视为缓慢的自由扼杀。
近义词:tyranny, oppression, repression, despotism
反义词:liberation, freedom, emancipation
词性:noun
