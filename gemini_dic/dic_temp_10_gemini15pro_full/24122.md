brews:/bruːz/| v. n.| 酿造，泡（茶），酝酿，混合物|The coffee brews slowly. 咖啡煮得很慢。|近义词：ferments, steeps, concocts, develops, gathers|反义词：dissipates|词性:动词(verb), 名词(noun)

brews:/bruːz/| n.| 混合物，酿造的饮料|This is a strange brew of herbs and spices. 这是一种奇怪的草药和香料的混合物。|近义词：mixture, concoction, blend|反义词：component, element|词性:名词(noun)

词源：From Old English *brēowan "to brew," of Germanic origin; related to Dutch brouwen and German brauen.
