fibrovascular:/ˌfaɪbroʊˈvæskjələr/
adjective
纤维血管的
Composed of both fibrous and vascular tissue.  Relating to or denoting tissue composed of both fibrous and vascular elements.
The fibrovascular bundles in the stem provide support and transport nutrients. 茎中的纤维血管束提供支撑和运输营养物质。
The plant's fibrovascular system is essential for its growth.  这种植物的纤维维管系统对其生长至关重要。
近义词: vascular, connective
反义词:  (No direct antonym,  consider using terms like "non-vascular" or describing the lack of fibrovascular tissue)
词性: adjective


fibrovascular:/ˌfaɪbroʊˈvæskjələr/
noun
纤维血管组织
Tissue composed of both fibrous and vascular elements.
The fibrovascular tissue of the plant is responsible for transporting water and nutrients. 植物的纤维血管组织负责运输水分和养分。
Damage to the fibrovascular tissue can hinder the plant's growth.  纤维血管组织的损伤会阻碍植物的生长。
近义词: vascular tissue, connective tissue
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider describing tissue lacking fibrous and vascular elements)
词性: noun


词源:  "fibro-" (from Latin fibra, meaning "fiber") + "vascular" (from Latin vasculum, meaning "small vessel").
