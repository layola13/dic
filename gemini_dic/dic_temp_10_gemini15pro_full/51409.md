determinists:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪnɪsts/ | n. | 决定论者 | People who believe in determinism, the philosophy that all events are predetermined by existing causes. | Determinists argue that free will is an illusion. 决定论者认为自由意志是一种幻觉。|  Synonyms: fatalists, necessitarians | Antonyms: libertarians, indeterminists | Noun

determinist:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪnɪst/ | n. | 决定论者 | A person who believes in determinism, the philosophy that all events are predetermined by existing causes. | The determinist argued that our choices are not truly our own. 这位决定论者认为，我们的选择并非真正属于我们自己。| Synonyms: fatalist, necessitarian | Antonyms: libertarian, indeterminist | Noun

deterministic:/dɪˌtɜːrmɪˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 决定论的 | Relating to determinism; characterized by predetermined events or outcomes. |  A deterministic universe leaves no room for chance. 决定论的宇宙没有偶然性的空间。| Synonyms: preordained, fated, inevitable | Antonyms: random, stochastic, indeterministic | Adjective
