drizzlier:/ˈdrɪzliər/ | adj. | 更毛毛细雨的，毛毛雨更大的 | comparative form of drizzly: characterized by light rain |  It was a drizzlier day than yesterday. 今天比昨天毛毛雨更大。| 近义词: rainier, wetter | 反义词: drier, sunnier | 词性: 形容词

词源: From drizzle + -ier (comparative suffix). Ultimately from Old English *dreosan "to fall"
