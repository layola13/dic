dullsville:/ˈdʌlzvɪl/| n.|沉闷的地方，无聊的城镇|A place or situation characterized by extreme boredom and lack of excitement.  It's often used hyperbolically.|例句：This town is a total dullsville on a Saturday night.  这个小镇周六晚上真是无聊透顶。

例句：I can't stand another weekend in dullsville. 我受不了再在这个沉闷的地方过一个周末了。|近义词：boring place, dead town, nowhere|反义词: exciting place, lively town, metropolis|词性:名词
