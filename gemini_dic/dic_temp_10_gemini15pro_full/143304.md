outdrove:/ˌaʊtˈdroʊv/
verb
胜过；超越(在驾驶方面)
surpassed in driving; drove better or faster than
He outdrove all the other racers and won the competition. 他胜过所有其他赛车手，赢得了比赛。
Synonyms: outpaced, outran, surpassed
Antonyms: lagged behind, trailed
词性: 动词

outdrove:/ˌaʊtˈdroʊv/
verb
(比喻) 超越；在…方面胜过
surpassed; excelled in a particular activity (figuratively)
She outdrove her colleagues in sales this quarter. 本季度她的销售额超过了她的同事。
Synonyms: outperformed, excelled, outshone
Antonyms: underperformed, lagged behind
词性: 动词
