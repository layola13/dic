paperchase:/ˈpeɪpərˌtʃeɪs/| n.|文件追蹤；繁文缛節；徒勞無功的追求|A pursuit of something that is difficult to obtain or that leads to a series of frustrating or unfulfilling tasks, often involving paperwork or bureaucratic processes.|例句：Applying for a visa can be a real paperchase. 申请签证可能是一场真正的文件追蹤。

例句：He's been on a paperchase all week trying to get the necessary permits. 他整个星期都在忙着办各种许可证。|近义词：bureaucracy, red tape, wild goose chase, rat race|反义词:streamlined process, efficient system|词性:noun
