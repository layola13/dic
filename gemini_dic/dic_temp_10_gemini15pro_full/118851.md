metaphrastical:/ˌmɛtəˈfræstɪkəl/ | adj. |逐字翻译的，直译的|Characterized by metaphrase, which is a literal, word-for-word translation, as opposed to paraphrase.  It focuses on translating each word individually rather than capturing the overall meaning or sense of the original text.| The translation was too metaphrastical and didn't convey the nuances of the original poem.  这个翻译太逐字逐句了，没有传达出原诗的细微之处。|Synonyms: literal, verbatim, word-for-word|Antonyms: paraphrastic, free, loose|Etymology: From Greek "meta" (beyond, over) + "phrasis" (speech, expression)|
