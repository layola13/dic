overlargely:/ˌoʊvərˈlɑːrdʒli/
adv.
过分地；过大地；主要地
To an excessive degree; predominantly.
The study's conclusions were overlargely based on anecdotal evidence.  这项研究的结论主要基于轶事证据。
The success of the project was overlargely attributed to one person. 这个项目的成功过大地归功于一个人。
Synonym: excessively, predominantly, mainly, chiefly
Antonym: moderately, slightly, insignificantly
