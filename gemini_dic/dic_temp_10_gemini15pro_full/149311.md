PAC's: /pæks/ | n. | 政治行动委员会 | Political Action Committees (PACs) are organizations in the US that raise money from individuals and businesses to support or oppose political candidates, legislation, or ballot initiatives. |  Example 1: The PAC's influence on the election was significant.  这个政治行动委员会对选举的影响很大。

Example 2: Several PACs are supporting the candidate.  几个政治行动委员会正在支持这位候选人。 | synonyms: pressure group, lobby, special interest group | antonyms:  (PACs don't have a direct opposite, perhaps "grassroots movement" represents a contrasting approach) | noun
