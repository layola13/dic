elevation:/ˌɛləˈveɪʃən/ | n. | 高度，海拔；提升，晋升；崇高，高尚 |  The act of raising something to a higher position or level, either literally or figuratively. It can refer to altitude, promotion, or a heightened moral or intellectual state. | The elevation of the mountain peak is 2,000 meters. 这座山峰的海拔是2000米。 | synonyms: height, altitude, promotion, advancement, rise, ascent | antonyms: decline, descent, demotion, lowering, depression | noun
