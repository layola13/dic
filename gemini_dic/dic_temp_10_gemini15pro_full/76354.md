gaddingly:/ˈɡædɪŋli/
adverb
游荡地，闲逛地
in a way that involves spending time enjoying yourself and not working
She spent the summer gaddingly about Europe. 她整个夏天都在欧洲游荡。
近义词: restlessly, aimlessly, frivolously
反义词: diligently, purposefully, seriously
词性: adverb

词源:
From gad + -ing + -ly.  "Gad" comes from Middle English  *gadden, of uncertain origin.
