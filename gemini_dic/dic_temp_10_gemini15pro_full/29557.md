catching:/ˈkætʃɪŋ/| adj.|迷人的，有魅力的；传染性的|Attractive, appealing; infectious or contagious.|例句：She has a catching laugh. 她笑声很感染人。

例句：The flu is a catching illness. 流感是一种传染性疾病。|近义词：infectious, contagious, captivating, alluring|反义词：repulsive, off-putting, resistant|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Middle English *cacchen*, from Old North French *cachier*, from Vulgar Latin *captiāre*, frequentative of Latin *capere* "to take, seize".
