buns:/bʌnz/
n.
小圆面包；屁股；圆形发髻
Small, round bread rolls; buttocks; a hairstyle where the hair is tied in a round shape on the head.
She ate two buns for breakfast. 她早餐吃了两个小圆面包。
The baby has cute little buns.  这个宝宝有可爱的小屁股。
She wore her hair in buns. 她把头发扎成圆形发髻。
近义词：rolls (for bread), bottom/buttocks (for rear end), chignon (for hairstyle)
反义词：(No direct opposite for buns)
词性：noun
