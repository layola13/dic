equipondious:/ˌiːkwɪˈpɒndiəs/ /ˌiːkwɪˈpɑːndiəs/
adjective
等重的；相等的
having equal weight or importance.
例句：The two objects were equipodious, balancing each other perfectly on the scale.  这两个物体重量相等，在秤上完美地相互平衡。

例句：The contributions of both team members were considered equipodious in achieving the project's success.  两位团队成员的贡献被认为对项目的成功同等重要。
近义词：equal, equivalent, commensurate
反义词：unequal, disparate, disproportionate
词性: adjective
