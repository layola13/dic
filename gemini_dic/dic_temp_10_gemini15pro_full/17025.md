bate/beɪt/| v. n.| 减少，减弱；抑制；鱼饵 |to lessen the force or intensity of; restrain; material used to attract fish or other animals |He bated his breath as he waited for the news. 他屏住呼吸等待消息。|近义词：moderate, lessen, diminish, restrain, curb, check, control, subdue, repress|反义词：increase, intensify, aggravate, excite, provoke|词性:动词,名词

词源: From Middle English *baten, from Old French *abatre (“to beat down, put down, abate”), from a- (“down, to”) + batre (“to beat”). Doublet of abate.
