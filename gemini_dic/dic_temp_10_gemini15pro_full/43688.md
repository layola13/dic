credit:/ˈkrɛdɪt/ (US) /ˈkredɪt/ (UK)| n. vt.|信用，信誉；贷款；学分；赞扬；荣誉|recognition that something is due to a person or other entity|She received credit for her hard work. 她因努力工作而受到赞扬。|synonyms: commendation, praise, acclaim, recognition, acknowledgment|antonyms: discredit, blame, censure|noun, verb

credit:/ˈkrɛdɪt/ (US) /ˈkredɪt/ (UK)| vt.|相信；把…归于|to believe something is true|I credited his story. 我相信了他的故事。|synonyms: believe, trust, accept|antonyms: disbelieve, distrust, doubt|verb
