babblingly:/ˈbæblɪŋli/ | adv. | 含糊不清地，唠唠叨叨地 | In a babbling manner; characterized by incoherent or meaningless chatter. |  The toddler babbled happily, and then started speaking babblingly about her day at the park.  这个幼儿高兴地呀呀学语，然后开始含糊不清地说着她在公园里的一天。| Synonyms: incoherently, ramblingly, disconnectedly | Antonyms: clearly, articulately, coherently | word

Etymology->

From babble + -ing + -ly.  "Babble" comes from the imitative verb "bablen" (Middle English), suggesting the sounds of baby talk.
