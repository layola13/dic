narrational:/nəˈreɪʃənəl/| adj.|叙述的，讲述的|Relating to the act or process of narrating a story.  It describes something that is related to narration, the way a story is told. |The film's narrational style was unique, jumping between different time periods.  这部电影的叙事风格很独特，在不同的时间段之间跳跃。|synonyms: narrative, storytelling, descriptive|antonyms: showing, enacting, demonstrative|word type: adjective
