geared:/ɡɪrd/| adj.|齿轮传动的；适合的；准备好的|Designed or adapted for a particular purpose or group of people.|The program is geared towards helping small businesses. 这个项目旨在帮助小型企业。|近义词:suited, adapted, equipped, designed, oriented|反义词:unsuited, inappropriate, ill-equipped|adjective


geared:/ɡɪrd/| adj.|对…有兴趣的；热衷于…的|Interested in or enthusiastic about something.|He's really geared towards making money. 他一心想赚钱。|近义词:keen on, enthusiastic about, interested in|反义词:uninterested in, apathetic to|adjective



ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *gørva (“to make ready”).  Related to gear (n.). The adjectival use developed from the past participle of the verb gear.
