loft-bed:/ˈlɒft bed/ (UK) /ˈlɔːft bed/ (US)| n. |阁楼床；高架床|A bed raised on a frame, creating space underneath that can be used for storage, a desk, or other furniture.|My son has a loft-bed in his room, freeing up floor space for him to play.|Synonyms: high-sleeper bed, elevated bed|Antonyms: bunk bed, regular bed|Noun
