paradiso:/ˌpærəˈdiːzoʊ/ /ˌpærəˈdiːsəʊ/| n.|天堂，乐园|A place of extreme beauty, delight, or happiness, often imagined as resembling the Garden of Eden.|例句：Dante's Paradiso is the final part of his Divine Comedy.  但丁的《天堂篇》是他《神曲》的最后一部分。

例句：He described the island as a veritable paradiso. 他把这座岛描述成名副其实的天堂。|近义词：heaven, utopia, elysium, paradise|反义词：hell, inferno|词性:名词
