fosa: /ˈfʊsə/ | n. | fossa | A cat-like, carnivorous mammal endemic to Madagascar.  It is the largest mammalian predator on the island and has been compared to a small cougar. |  The fosa is a remarkable predator found only in Madagascar.  福萨是一种奇特的掠食动物，仅在马达加斯加发现。|  synonyms: Malagasy civet, fanaloka (loosely) | antonyms: prey | noun
