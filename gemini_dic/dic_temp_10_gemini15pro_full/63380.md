entophyte:/ˈɛndəˌfaɪt/ | n. | 内生菌；内生植物 | An organism, especially a fungus or bacterium, that lives inside a plant without causing apparent disease. | Example 1:  The researchers discovered a new endophyte living within the leaves of the rainforest tree. 研究人员在雨林树木的叶子中发现了一种新的内生菌。

Example 2:  Some endophytes can enhance a plant's growth and resistance to pests. 一些内生菌可以促进植物生长并增强其对害虫的抵抗力。 | 近义词: endosymbiont | 反义词: pathogen | 词性: noun
