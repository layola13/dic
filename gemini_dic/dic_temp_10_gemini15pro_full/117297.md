meconidium:/ˌmiːkəˈnɪdiəm/ | n. | 分生孢子 | An asexual fungal spore produced at the tip or side of a specialized hypha.  A type of spore in fungi, produced asexually. | The meconidia of this fungus are particularly large.  这种真菌的分生孢子特别大。| synonym: conidium, mitospore | antonym: None (No direct antonym for a type of spore) | noun
