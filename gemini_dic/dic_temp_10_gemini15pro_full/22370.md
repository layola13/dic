booby-trap: /ˈbuːbi træp/ | n. | 诱杀装置；陷阱 | A device or setup designed to harm or kill someone who triggers it unintentionally. |  The soldiers carefully checked the area for booby traps. 士兵们仔细检查了该地区是否有诱杀装置。

The tomb was rigged with booby traps to deter grave robbers.  这座坟墓设置了诱杀装置以阻止盗墓贼。 | 近义词: trap, snare, mine | 反义词: safeguard, protection | 词性: 名词
