avanguard:/ˈvænɡɑːrd/ | n. | 先锋；前锋；先驱；先头部队 | a group of people leading the way in new developments or ideas |  The artist was part of the avant-garde movement in painting.  这位艺术家是绘画界先锋运动的一员。| synonyms: forefront, leading edge, pioneers, trailblazers | antonyms: rearguard, laggards, followers | noun

avanguard:/ˈvænɡɑːrd/ | adj. | 先锋的；前卫的 |  favoring or introducing experimental or unusual ideas | The museum has an exhibit of avant-garde sculpture.  博物馆展出了前卫雕塑。| synonyms: innovative, cutting-edge, experimental, unconventional | antonyms: traditional, conventional, orthodox | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *avant-garde*, literally "fore-guard" from *avant* "before" + *garde* "guard."
