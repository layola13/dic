bullpout:/ˈbʊlˌpaʊt/ (US), /ˈbʊlpaʊt/ (UK)| n.|牛头鱼，闷葫芦，生闷气的人|A type of fish with a large head and mouth, resembling a tadpole. Also, a person who is sullen and silent.|例句：The bullpout lay motionless on the riverbed. 牛头鱼一动不动地躺在河床上。

例句：He's such a bullpout; he never says a word. 他真是个闷葫芦，从来不说话。|近义词：sculpin (fish), sulker, grump|反义词：chatterbox|词性:noun
