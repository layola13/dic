nondeporteds: /ˌnɒndɪˈpɔːtɪdz/ | n. | 未被驱逐出境者 | People who have not been deported or expelled from a country.  This is a non-standard term, formed by adding the standard English plural suffix "-s" to "nondeported", itself a somewhat unusual formation. More common terms would be "non-deported persons" or "individuals who have not been deported." | The news report focused on the plight of the nondeporteds seeking asylum.  新闻报道关注的是寻求庇护的未被驱逐出境者的困境。|  The government is reviewing the cases of the nondeporteds currently in the country. 政府正在审查目前在该国未被驱逐出境者的案件。 | Synonyms:  undeported,  non-expelled | Antonyms:  deported, expelled | Noun
