deadhearted:/ˈdedˈhɑːr.tɪd/ | adj. | 无情的，冷酷的，铁石心肠的 | Lacking compassion or feeling; unsympathetic. | He gave a deadhearted refusal to help. 他冷酷地拒绝了帮助。 |  She showed a deadhearted indifference to his suffering. 她对他的痛苦表现出无情的冷漠。 | 近义词：heartless, cruel, callous, unfeeling, indifferent, ruthless | 反义词：kind, compassionate, sympathetic, warmhearted, tenderhearted | 词性: 形容词

词源:
dead (adj.) + hearted (suffix)

dead: From Old English *dēad, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch dood and German tot.

-hearted:  suffix forming adjectives meaning ‘having a specified kind of heart’.
