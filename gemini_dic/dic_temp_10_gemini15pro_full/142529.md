ossifiers:/ˈɒsɪfaɪəz/ (US) /ˈɒsɪfaɪəz/ (UK)| n. |骨化器；成骨细胞|Cells or tissues that cause bone formation.|The ossifiers in the growth plate contribute to lengthening the bone.  生长板中的成骨细胞有助于骨骼的延长。|The fracture healed quickly thanks to the active ossifiers.  由于活跃的成骨细胞，骨折愈合得很快。|Synonyms: osteoblasts|Antonyms: osteoclasts|Noun
