mercurification:/mɜːrkjʊrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |汞化；水银处理|The process of combining or treating with mercury.  The act of impregnating or treating with mercury or a mercury compound. |例句：Mercurification of gold is an ancient technique for extracting gold.  汞化金是一种古老的提取金的技术。

例句：Excessive mercurification can lead to environmental pollution. 过量汞化会导致环境污染。|近义词：amalgamation (with mercury) |反义词: detoxification (removal of mercury) |词性: noun

Etymology: From Latin *mercurificatio*, from *mercurificare* "to combine with mercury", from *mercurius* "mercury".
