overdiligentness:/ˌoʊvərˈdɪlɪdʒəntnəs/
|n.|过度勤奋，过分努力|The state of being excessively diligent or hardworking.  Characterized by working too hard or for too long, often to the detriment of other aspects of life.|His overdiligentness led to burnout.  他过度勤奋导致了倦怠。|近义词:overwork, workaholism|反义词:laziness, indolence|词性:名词

overdiligent:/ˌoʊvərˈdɪlɪdʒənt/
|adj.|过度勤奋的，过分努力的|Excessively diligent or hardworking.  Characterized by working too hard or for too long.|He's an overdiligent student, always studying late into the night. 他是一个过度勤奋的学生，总是学习到深夜。|近义词:overzealous, hardworking|反义词:lazy, indolent|词性:形容词
