arouser:/əˈraʊzər/ | n. | 引起兴奋的人或物；刺激物 | Something that arouses or excites, a stimulant. |  The coffee acted as an arouser, waking me up quickly. 咖啡就像兴奋剂一样，让我很快清醒过来。

The bright colors of the flowers are a visual arouser.  鲜艳的花朵颜色是一种视觉刺激。 | 近义词: stimulant, excitant, trigger | 反义词: depressant, sedative | 词性: noun

词源: From the verb "arouse," which comes from Middle English *arousen*, from Anglo-French *aruser*, ultimately from Latin *ad- + rogare* "to ask." The suffix "-er" denotes an agent or instrument.
