horsemanship:/ˈhɔːrsmənʃɪp/| n. |骑术；马术|The art or skill of riding and managing horses.  It encompasses a broad range of disciplines, from basic riding skills to advanced equestrian techniques.|Her horsemanship was impeccable, guiding her horse effortlessly through the complex course. 她的骑术无可挑剔，轻松地引导她的马穿过复杂的路线。|Synonyms: equitation, riding skill|Antonyms: ineptitude, clumsiness|Noun
