counterfeits:/ˈkaʊntərˌfɪts/| n. | 伪造品；赝品 |Something that is made to look like the original of something else, usually for dishonest or illegal purposes. | The market was flooded with counterfeits of the popular handbag.  这个市场上充斥着这款热门手袋的假货。| synonyms: forgeries, fakes, imitations, knockoffs | antonyms: originals, authentics | noun
counterfeits:/ˈkaʊntərˌfɪts/| v. | 伪造；仿造 | To make an illegal copy of something. | They were arrested for counterfeiting money.  他们因伪造货币而被捕。| synonyms: forge, fake, imitate | antonyms: authenticate, verify | verb
