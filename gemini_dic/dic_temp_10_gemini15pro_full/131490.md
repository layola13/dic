noncollapsable:/ˌnɒnkəˈlæpsəbl/ /ˌnɑːnkəˈlæpsəbl/| adj.|不可折叠的，不可拆卸的|Not able to be collapsed or folded.|This tent uses a noncollapsable frame.  这个帐篷使用不可折叠的框架。|The surgeon inserted a noncollapsable stent.  外科医生插入了一个不可拆卸的支架。|近义词：rigid, inflexible, fixed|反义词：collapsible, foldable|词性:形容词


Etymology->
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin *non < Old Latin *noenum < *ne oinom "not one."
collapse (verb): From Latin *collapsus, past participle of collabi "to fall together, fall down," from com- "together" + labi "to slip, slide, fall."
-able (suffix):  Meaning "capable of, fit for, tending to," from Middle English -able, -ible, from Old French -able and directly from Latin -abilis.
