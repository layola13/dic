extravagant: /ɪkˈstrævəɡənt/ | adj. | 奢侈的，浪费的；过度的，放肆的 | Spending more than is reasonable or necessary; exceeding normal bounds. |  She has extravagant tastes in clothes. 她对服装的品味很奢侈。

The party was an extravagant affair with hundreds of guests.  这个派对奢华至极，有数百位宾客参加。 | 近义词：lavish, profuse, luxurious, immoderate | 反义词：economical, frugal, thrifty, modest | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *extravagantem, accusative singular masculine of extravagans, present participle of extravagari "to wander outside or beyond," from Latin extra "outside" + vagari "to wander."
