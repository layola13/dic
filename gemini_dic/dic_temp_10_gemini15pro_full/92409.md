hypogene:/ˈhaɪpədʒiːn/| adj.|深成的，地下生成的|Formed or originating under the earth's surface, especially by igneous or hydrothermal processes. 指在地球表面以下形成或起源的，尤指由火成岩或热液过程形成的。|例句：Hypogene mineral deposits are often rich in valuable metals. 深成矿床通常富含贵重金属。

例句：The study focuses on the hypogene caves formed by rising hydrothermal fluids. 这项研究的重点是由上升的热液形成的深成洞穴。|近义词：subterranean, plutonic|反义词：supergene, epigene|词性：形容词
