counterinterpretation:/ˌkaʊntərɪnˌtɜːrprɪˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 反释；反解释；对抗性解释 | An explanation that opposes or contradicts another interpretation. |  The defense lawyer offered a counterinterpretation of the evidence, suggesting the suspect was innocent.  辩护律师对证据提出了反解释，暗示嫌疑人无辜。

| Example: His counterinterpretation of the historical event challenged the established narrative.  他对这一历史事件的反解释挑战了既定的说法。 | Synonyms: reinterpretation, alternative interpretation, opposing interpretation | Antonyms:  interpretation, agreement, concordance | Noun
