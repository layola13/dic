boomeranger:/ˈbuːməræŋər/ | n. | 回飞棒使用者；投掷回飞棒的人 |  A person who throws or uses a boomerang. | He's a skilled boomeranger, able to make the boomerang return to him every time. 他是一个熟练的回飞棒使用者，每次都能让回飞棒回到他手中。 | 近义词: thrower (投掷者) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

boomerang:/ˈbuːməræŋ/ | n. | 回飞棒 | A curved wooden missile configured to return to the thrower. | The boomerang sailed through the air and returned to the thrower's hand.  回旋镖飞过空中，回到了投掷者的手中。 | 近义词: throwing stick | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

boomerang:/ˌbuːməˈræŋ/ | vi. |  产生事与愿违的结果；得到反效果| (of a plan or action) recoil on the originator, often with harmful effects. | His attempt to discredit his rival boomeranged and ended up ruining his own reputation. 他试图败坏对手名声的举动适得其反，最终毁掉了自己的名声。| 近义词: backfire, rebound | 反义词: succeed, prevail | 词性: verb
