The artist created a sculpture using an omnilucent resin that seemed to glow from within.  艺术家使用一种全光透树脂创作了一件雕塑，它看起来像从内部发光。|近义词：transparent, translucent, diaphanous|反义词：opaque, impenetrable|词性:形容词
词源: omni- (all) + lucent (shining, giving off light)  来自拉丁语 omnis (all) 和 lucens (shining).
