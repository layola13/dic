hamminess:/ˈhæminəs/| n.|做作；矫揉造作；|The hamminess of his performance was unbearable. 他表演的矫揉造作令人难以忍受。|近义词：artificiality, theatricality, affectation|反义词：naturalness, sincerity, genuineness|词性:名词

Etymology:  From *ham* (overacting actor) + *-iness* (suffix forming nouns indicating a state or quality).  The term "ham" for a bad actor originated in the 19th century, possibly referring to the low-quality ham fat used in cheap theatrical makeup that would melt under the hot stage lights, making the actors look greasy and exaggerated.
