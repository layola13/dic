burn the midnight oil:/bɜːrn ðə ˈmɪdnaɪt ɔɪl/| idiom | 开夜车；熬夜学习/工作 | To work or study late into the night. |  Example:  She's been burning the midnight oil to finish her thesis. 她一直在熬夜完成她的论文。| 近义词: work late, stay up late, study all night | 反义词: go to bed early, get a good night's sleep | idiom

Etymology:
This idiom likely originated in the days before electricity, when people used oil lamps for light.  Burning oil late into the night implied working or studying long after most people had gone to bed.
