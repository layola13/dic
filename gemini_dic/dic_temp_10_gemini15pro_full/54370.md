dishrags:/ˈdɪʃˌræɡz/
noun
抹布；洗碗布
Pieces of cloth used for washing dishes.
She tossed the dirty dishrags into the laundry basket. 她把脏抹布扔进了洗衣篮。
The dishrags were stained with grease. 抹布上沾满了油渍。
synonyms: dishcloths, washcloths, tea towels
antonyms: (No direct antonym, but could be considered opposite to "sponges" in some contexts)
