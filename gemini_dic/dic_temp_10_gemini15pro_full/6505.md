amorous:/ˈæmərəs/| adj.|多情的，恋爱的，怀春的|Showing, feeling, or relating to sexual desire.|He gave her an amorous glance. 他向她投以多情的目光。|近义词：loving, affectionate, passionate, ardent, romantic|反义词：frigid, cold, indifferent, platonic|adjective

Etymology:
From Middle English *amorous, *amerous, from Anglo-Norman amorous, Old French amoros, from Late Latin amorōsus (“loving”), from Latin amor (“love”).
