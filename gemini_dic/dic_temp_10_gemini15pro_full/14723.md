awash:/əˈwɒʃ/ (UK) /əˈwɔːʃ/ (US)| adj.|被淹没的，泛滥的，充满的|Covered or flooded with water or another liquid; overflowing with something.|The streets were awash after the heavy rain. 大雨过后，街道都被淹没了。|The city is awash with tourists during the summer months. 在夏季，这座城市到处都是游客。|近义词：flooded, inundated, overflowing|反义词：dry, arid|词性：形容词
