charta:/ˈtʃɑːrtə/| n.|宪章，特许状|A formal written statement of the rights and privileges of a person, class, or group.  A document constituting a fundamental guarantee of rights and privileges.|The Magna Charta is a cornerstone of English law.|Synonym: charter, constitution, statute|Antonym: decree, edict|noun
