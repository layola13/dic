homoeostatically:/ˌhoʊmiːoʊˈstætɪkli/ | adv. |体内平衡地| In a manner that maintains homeostasis, referring to the ability of a system or living organism to adjust its internal environment to maintain a stable, relatively constant condition. | The body temperature is homoeostatically controlled.  体温是通过体内平衡机制控制的。| The kidneys play a vital role in homoeostatically regulating the body's water and salt balance. 肾脏在体内平衡调节身体水盐平衡方面起着至关重要的作用。| 近义词: stably, constantly | 反义词: erratically, unstably | 词性: 副词

词源:  From homeostasis + -ically. Homeostasis comes from Greek homoios (similar) + stasis (standing still).
