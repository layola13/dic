pailsful:/ˈpeɪlzfʊl/| n. |几桶，若干桶|The amount contained in a specified number of pails.  It refers to a quantity of something that would fill a certain number of pails.|She carried three pailsful of water from the well. 她从井里提了三桶水。|Near synonyms: bucketfuls, loads, lots|Antonym: a little, a small amount|noun
