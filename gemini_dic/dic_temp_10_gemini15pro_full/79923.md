gnomae:/ˈnoʊmiː/| n. |格言，箴言，警句| A pithy saying expressing a general truth, often metaphorical in nature. Derived from Greek 'gnōmē' meaning 'thought, opinion, judgment'.  | The philosopher offered several gnomae on the nature of happiness. 哲学家就幸福的本质提出了几句箴言。|synonyms: aphorism, maxim, adage, proverb| antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "fallacy," "misconception," or "lie" for contrasting ideas.)| noun
