constructive:/kənˈstrʌktɪv/
adjective
建设性的，有益的
Serving a useful purpose; tending to build up.
例句：
The meeting was very constructive, and we made a lot of progress.  会议非常有建设性，我们取得了很大进展。

We had a constructive discussion about the future of the project. 我们就项目的未来进行了一次建设性的讨论。

近义词：helpful, useful, productive, positive
反义词：destructive, negative, unhelpful
词性: adjective
