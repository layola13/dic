overpenalization:/ˌoʊvərˌpiːnələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度惩罚，过度处罚 | The act of penalizing someone or something too harshly or excessively.  It implies that the punishment given is disproportionate to the offense committed. | The overpenalization of minor offenses can lead to resentment and distrust of the legal system.  过度惩罚轻微的违法行为会导致人们对法律制度的怨恨和不信任。| overpunishment, excessive penalization | underpenalization, leniency | noun
