calaveras: /ˌkæləˈvɛrəs/ | n. | 骷髅头（尤指墨西哥亡灵节用的糖果或装饰）; 赌注; 冒险 | Skulls, especially those made of sugar or other materials for the Mexican Day of the Dead; Stakes; Ventures |  例句：The children decorated colorful calaveras for Dia de los Muertos. 孩子们为亡灵节装饰了彩色的骷髅头。 | 近义词：skulls, | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词


Etymology: From Spanish *calavera*, meaning "skull," ultimately from Latin *calvaria*, meaning "skull."
