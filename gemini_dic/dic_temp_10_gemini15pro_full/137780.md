nonvegetatively:/ˌnɒnˌvɛdʒɪˈteɪtɪvli/
adverb
非营养地，无性地
Not involving or related to vegetative reproduction; by means other than vegetative means.
Strawberry plants reproduce both vegetatively and nonvegetatively. 草莓既可以进行营养繁殖，也可以进行非营养繁殖。
Near Antonyms: vegetatively
Antonyms: sexually (in the context of reproduction)

Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + vegetatively (adverb form of vegetative, from Latin vegetativus, from vegetare "to enliven, quicken," from vegetus "lively, vigorous")
