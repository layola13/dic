fibrinous:/ˈfaɪbrɪnəs/| adj.|纤维蛋白的；纤维素性的|Composed of or containing fibrin.  Characterized by the presence of fibrin, as in a wound or inflammation.|The doctor noted a fibrinous exudate around the wound. 医生注意到伤口周围有纤维蛋白渗出物。|The pericardium was thickened and fibrinous. 心包增厚且呈纤维素性。|近义词：fibrous, coagulated|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective
