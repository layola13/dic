othering:/ˈʌðərɪŋ/| n.| 他者化，异化|The process of casting a group or individual as intrinsically different from and alien to oneself or one's own group, often involving negative stereotypes and prejudice.|  The colonizers engaged in the othering of indigenous peoples, portraying them as savages to justify their exploitation. 殖民者对他者化土著人民，把他们描绘成野蛮人，为他们的剥削辩护。

The media's othering of immigrants contributes to societal prejudice. 媒体对他者化移民助长了社会偏见。


|近义词：alienation, marginalization, exclusion, dehumanization|反义词: inclusion, integration, acceptance|词性:noun
