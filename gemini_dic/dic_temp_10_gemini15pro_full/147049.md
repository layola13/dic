overlay:/ˈoʊvərleɪ/ (US), /ˈəʊvəleɪ/ (UK)| n. vt.|覆盖物，覆盖，重叠|A layer or covering that is placed over something else; to cover something with a layer of something else.|例句：The map has an overlay showing population density.  地图上有一个显示人口密度的覆盖层。|近义词：covering, layer, coat, film|反义词：underlay, base|词性: 名词, 动词

overlay:/ˈoʊvərleɪ/ (US), /ˈəʊvəleɪ/ (UK)| vt.|覆盖，重叠|To cover something with a layer of something else.|例句：She overlaid the dress with lace. 她用蕾丝覆盖了连衣裙。|近义词：cover, superimpose, coat|反义词：expose, uncover|词性: 动词
