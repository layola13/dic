espressivo:/esprɛˈsiːvoʊ/
adjective
富于表情的；表现的
marked by expression
Play this passage espressivo.  演奏这段时要富于表情。
近义词: expressive, emotional, passionate
反义词: inexpressive, unemotional, impassive
词性: adjective
词源: Italian, from Espresso, past participle of esprimere to express, from Latin exprimere
用法:  Used as a musical direction to indicate that a passage should be played with expression.
