concessional:/kənˈseʃənəl/| adj.|优惠的，让步的|Granted or allowed as a concession; involving or offered at a reduced rate or price.  Relating to or in the nature of a concession.|例句：The government offered concessional loans to small businesses. 政府向小企业提供优惠贷款。

例句：Concessional rates are available for students and seniors. 学生和老年人可享受优惠价格。|近义词：reduced, preferential, discounted, subsidized|反义词：standard, full-price, regular|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From Late Latin *concessiōnālis, from Latin concessio (“a granting, yielding”), from concedere (“to yield, concede”).
