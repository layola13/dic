difformity:/dɪˈfɔːrmɪti/| n. |畸形，残缺，差异，不同|a difference in form or shape from what is usual or expected; a deformity |The child was born with a difformity of the spine.  这个孩子出生时脊柱畸形。|Synonyms: abnormality, defect, malformation, imperfection, irregularity |Antonyms: normality, regularity, symmetry, perfection| noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *difformitās, from Latin difformis (“different in form, dissimilar”), from dis- (“away, apart”) + formis (“form”).
