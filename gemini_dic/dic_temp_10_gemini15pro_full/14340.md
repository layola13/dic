automobility:/ˌɔːtəmoʊˈbɪləti/ | n. | 汽车普及率；汽车拥有量；机动性 | The ability of people to travel easily from place to place, especially using cars. |  The increasing automobility of the population has led to more traffic congestion. 人们日益增长的汽车拥有量导致了更多的交通拥堵。|  The government is trying to improve automobility in rural areas. 政府正在努力提高农村地区的机动性。| synonyms: mobility, transportability, locomotion | antonyms: immobility, confinement | noun
