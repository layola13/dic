figurant:/ˈfɪɡjərənt/ | n. |群众演员，配角，跑龙套的|A person who has a minor, nonspeaking role in a play, film, or opera; an extra.|The film used hundreds of figurants in the battle scenes. 这部电影在战斗场景中使用了数百名群众演员。|Synonyms: extra, walk-on, supernumerary|Antonyms: star, lead, protagonist|noun

figurante:/fɪɡjʊˈrɑːnt/ | n. | (芭蕾舞剧中的) 女配角 | A female ballet dancer who dances in a corps de ballet and does not dance solo parts. |  She started as a figurante but quickly rose through the ranks. 她最初是名女配角，但很快就晋升了。 | Synonyms:  corps de ballet member | Antonyms: ballerina, prima ballerina | noun
