bbses:/ˈbiː biː ɛsɪz/ | n. | 网络论坛（bulletin board systems 的复数形式） |  Plural form of BBS, which stands for Bulletin Board System. These were computer systems that allowed users to connect and exchange messages, files, and play games before the widespread adoption of the internet. |  He used to spend hours on various bbses, downloading software and chatting with other users. 他过去常常在各种BBS上花费数小时下载软件和与其他用户聊天。 | 近义词: forums, online communities | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "offline communities") | 词性: noun
