childproofed:/ˈtʃaɪldˌpruːft/ | adj. | 防儿童开启的；对儿童安全的 | Designed to prevent children from opening or accessing something potentially dangerous. |  The medicine cabinet was childproofed to keep the pills away from the toddlers.  药柜装有防儿童开启的装置，以防止幼儿拿到药片。 | child-resistant, tamper-proof | accessible, unsafe | adjective

childproofed:/ˈtʃaɪldˌpruːft/ | v. |  使防儿童开启；使对儿童安全 | To make something safe for children by preventing them from opening or accessing it. | They childproofed the house before their baby started crawling.  在他们的宝宝开始爬行之前，他们对房子进行了儿童安全防护。 | secure, protect | endanger, expose | verb
