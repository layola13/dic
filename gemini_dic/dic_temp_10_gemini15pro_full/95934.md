incubate:/ˈɪŋkjubeɪt/ (US), /ˈɪŋkjʊbeɪt/ (UK)| v. |孵化，培养，孕育|To maintain (eggs, bacteria, embryos, etc.) at a suitable temperature so as to promote development; to develop slowly; to devise or contrive (something, such as a plan)|The birds incubated their eggs for several weeks. 鸟类孵蛋数周。|近义词：foster, develop, nurture, hatch|反义词: neglect, stifle, suppress|verb

incubate:/ˈɪŋkjubeɪt/ (US), /ˈɪŋkjʊbeɪt/ (UK)| n. | (尤指早产儿)恒温箱|A device used to maintain environmental conditions suitable for the growth of premature babies, microbes, or cell cultures.|The premature baby was placed in an incubator. 早产儿被放置在恒温箱中。|近义词：brooder, warmer|反义词: N/A|noun
