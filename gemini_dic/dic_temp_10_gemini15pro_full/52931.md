din-dins:/ˈdɪn dɪnz/
noun
晚餐；晚饭
Informal term for dinner, often used with children.
例句：Time for your din-dins!  该吃晚饭啦！

例句：The kids are having their din-dins. 孩子们正在吃晚饭。
近义词：dinner, supper, tea
反义词：(None -  no direct opposite for a meal)
词性:noun
