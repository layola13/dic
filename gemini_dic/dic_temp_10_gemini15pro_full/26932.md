calanus:/ˈkeɪlənəs/| n. |桡足类动物（哲水蚤）|A small copepod crustacean of the genus Calanus, abundant in the plankton of northern seas and an important food source for fish.  It is a key part of the marine food web.|例句：Calanus finmarchicus is a dominant species in the North Atlantic.  北大西洋哲水蚤是北大西洋的优势物种。

例句：The abundance of Calanus populations can influence fish stocks. 哲水蚤种群数量的丰富程度会影响鱼类资源。|近义词：copepod, zooplankton|反义词: None (no direct opposite for a specific genus)|词性: Noun
