overhasty:/ˌoʊvərˈheɪsti/| adj.|仓促的，草率的，轻率的|Acting too quickly and without enough thought; rash.|例句：His overhasty decision led to disastrous consequences. 他草率的决定导致了灾难性的后果。

例句：It's overhasty to judge him without knowing all the facts. 在不了解所有事实的情况下就评判他太草率了。|近义词：rash, impetuous, precipitate, reckless|反义词：cautious, deliberate, prudent, considered|词性:形容词
