lifesaving:/ˈlaɪfˌseɪvɪŋ/
adjective
救命的；救生的
Designed to save lives, especially from drowning.
例句：The crew performed a lifesaving rescue in the stormy seas.  船员在暴风雨中进行了救生救援。

例句：He received an award for his lifesaving actions. 他因救生行为获得了奖励。

近义词：life-preserving, vital
反义词：fatal, deadly
词性：adjective


lifesaving:/ˈlaɪfˌseɪvɪŋ/
noun
救生；救命
The action or skill of saving lives, especially from drowning.
例句：Lifesaving is an important skill for anyone who lives near water. 救生对于任何住在水边的人来说都是一项重要的技能。

例句：The coast guard provides lifesaving services. 海岸警卫队提供救生服务。

近义词：rescue, resuscitation
反义词：killing, murder
词性：noun
