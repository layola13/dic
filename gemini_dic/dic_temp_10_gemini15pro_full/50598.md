derbio:/ˈdɜːrbioʊ/ (US), /ˈdɜːrbiəʊ/ (UK)| n. |德比; 同城德比; 重要比赛|A sporting contest between two rival teams, especially teams from the same city or region.  It often carries heightened importance and rivalry.|例句：The Manchester derby is always a fiercely contested match. 曼彻斯特德比永远是一场竞争激烈的比赛。

例句：The Kentucky Derby is one of the most famous horse races in the world. 肯塔基德比是世界上最著名的赛马比赛之一。|近义词：match, contest, competition, rivalry|反义词:cooperation, harmony|词性:noun
