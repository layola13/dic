antisplitting:/ˌæntiˈsplɪtɪŋ/| adj.|防分裂的，反分裂的|Preventing or designed to prevent splitting, particularly in the context of wood, checks, or psychological conditions.|The carpenter used an antisplitting device when driving the nails into the delicate wood. 木匠在将钉子钉入精致的木头时使用了防裂装置。|The therapist specialized in antisplitting techniques for patients with Borderline Personality Disorder.  这位治疗师专门研究针对边缘型人格障碍患者的防分裂技术。|近义词：unifying, cohesive, integrating|反义词:splitting, dividing, fragmenting|词性:形容词

Etymology:
"anti-" (prefix): from Greek "anti" meaning "against," "opposite of"
"splitting": from Old English "spittan," meaning "to split, divide"
