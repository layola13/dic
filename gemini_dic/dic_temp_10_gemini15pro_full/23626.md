例句：The study of Branchiogastropoda is crucial for understanding marine ecosystems. 研究鳃腹足纲对于理解海洋生态系统至关重要。|近义词：gill-breathing gastropods|反义词:pulmonata (lung-breathing gastropods)|词性:noun
