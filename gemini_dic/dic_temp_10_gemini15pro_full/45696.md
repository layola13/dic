cururo:/kʊˈruːroʊ/
noun
南美栗鼠，一种啮齿动物
A large South American rodent (Spalacopus cyanus) of the family Octodontidae, resembling a guinea pig and living in colonies underground.
The cururo digs extensive burrow systems.  南美栗鼠挖掘广泛的洞穴系统。
Cururos are native to the Andes Mountains. 南美栗鼠原产于安第斯山脉。
近义词:  coruro, degú (related species)
反义词:  None (specific animal, no direct opposite)
词性:  n. (noun)
