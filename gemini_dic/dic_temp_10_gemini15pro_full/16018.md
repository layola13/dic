banan:/bəˈnæn/ | n. | 香蕉 | A long curved fruit with a yellow skin that grows in hot countries. | Example 1:  I eat a banana every morning for breakfast. 我每天早上早餐吃一根香蕉。

Example 2: Monkeys love to eat bananas. 猴子喜欢吃香蕉。 | 近义词: plantain (尤其指烹饪用香蕉) | 反义词:  无 | 词性: 名词
