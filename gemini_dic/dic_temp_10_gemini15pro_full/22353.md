bonuses:/ˈboʊnəsɪz/
noun
奖金，红利，额外津贴
Something given or paid in addition to what is usual or expected.
She received a Christmas bonus. 她收到了圣诞奖金。
synonyms:  perks,  incentives,  rewards,  premiums,  gratuities
antonyms: penalties, deductions, forfeitures
词性: 名词
