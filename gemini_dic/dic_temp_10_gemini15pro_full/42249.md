countenancer:/ˈkaʊntənənsər/| n.|支持者，赞助者；容忍者|Someone who supports or approves of something, especially something wrong or immoral.  One who tolerates or overlooks something.|He was a countenancer of the rebellion. 他是这次叛乱的支持者。|Synonyms: supporter, backer, patron, abettor|Antonyms: opponent, critic, adversary|noun

countenancer:/ˈkaʊntənənsər/| v.|支持，赞助；容忍|To support, encourage, or tolerate (something, especially something wrong or immoral).|She refused to countenance his bad behavior. 她拒绝容忍他的不良行为。|Synonyms: support, encourage, condone, tolerate|Antonyms: oppose, condemn, disapprove|verb
