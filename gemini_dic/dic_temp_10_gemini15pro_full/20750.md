blancmanger:/bləˈmɑːnʒ/ | n. | 杏仁豆腐，牛奶冻 | A sweet, cold dessert made with milk or cream, sugar, and a thickening agent such as gelatin, cornstarch, or almond milk. Traditionally made with almonds. |  The blancmanger was wobbly and delicious. 杏仁豆腐摇摇晃晃的，非常美味。

The chef prepared a rosewater-flavored blancmanger for dessert.  厨师准备了一份玫瑰水味的牛奶冻作为甜点。 | 近义词: pudding, custard, jelly | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "savory dish") | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *blanc mangier “white food,” from blanc “white” + mangier “to eat.”  Originally a dish of shredded chicken or fish in almond milk.
