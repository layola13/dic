frugivores:/ˈfruːdʒɪvɔːrz/
n.
食果动物
Animals that primarily eat fruits.
Many primates, like orangutans, are frugivores. 许多灵长类动物，如猩猩，都是食果动物。
Bats in the tropics are often frugivores, dispersing seeds as they fly.  热带地区的蝙蝠通常是食果动物，它们在飞行时传播种子。
