half-madly:/ˌhæfˈmædli/
| adv. | 半疯狂地，近乎疯狂地 | Partly or almost madly; with a degree of madness. | He laughed half-madly, his eyes gleaming. 他近乎疯狂地大笑，眼睛闪闪发光。| Near-madly, almost insanely, partially madly | Sanely, rationally | adverb
