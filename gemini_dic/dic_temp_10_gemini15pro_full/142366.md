orthophoria:/ˌɔːrθəˈfɔːriə/ |n.|正视|The condition in which the visual axes of the two eyes are parallel when the eyes are at rest and not fixated on an object.  The normal condition of the eyes, where both point directly forward when relaxed. |Example 1:  Her orthophoria allowed her to see clearly without any strain. 她正视的双眼让她看得清晰，没有任何压力。

Example 2: The ophthalmologist confirmed the patient's orthophoria after a thorough examination. 眼科医生在彻底检查后确认了病人的正视。|Synonym: normal binocular vision|Antonym: heterophoria|noun
