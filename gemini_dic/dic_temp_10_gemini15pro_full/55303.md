dissolutive:/dɪˈsɒl.jʊ.tɪv/
adjective
溶解的；分解的；导致解散的
Having the power to dissolve or causing dissolution.
The dissolutive effects of acid on metal. 酸对金属的溶解作用。
The dissolutive power of enzymes. 酶的分解能力。
The dissolutive actions of the board led to the company's downfall. 董事会的解散行动导致了公司的垮台。
近义词: dissolving, disintegrative, disruptive
反义词: constructive, unifying, binding
词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *dissolutivus, from Latin dissolvere (to dissolve).
