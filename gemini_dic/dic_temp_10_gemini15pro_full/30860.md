chadors:/tʃəˈdɔːrz/| n.|伊朗女性穿戴的黑色长袍|A full-length garment worn by some Muslim women, especially in Iran, consisting of a large piece of cloth wrapped around the head and body leaving only the face visible.|Many women in Iran wear chadors in public.  许多伊朗女性在公共场合穿着长袍。| The women entered the mosque wearing their chadors.  妇女们穿着长袍进入了清真寺。|近义词：veil, hijab, burqa|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps Western clothing)|名词
