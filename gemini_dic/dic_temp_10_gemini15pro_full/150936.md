paralogy:/pəˈrælədʒi/| n.|旁系同源|The evolutionary relationship between two genes derived from a duplication event within the same genome.|Hemoglobin and myoglobin are examples of paralogy, arising from a gene duplication in an ancestral vertebrate.|近义词：duplication, homology|反义词: orthology|名词
