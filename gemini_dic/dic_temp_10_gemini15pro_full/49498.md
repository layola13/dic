demagoged:/ˈdeməɡɒɡd/ |v. (past participle)|被煽动的，被蛊惑的|Having been subjected to inflammatory rhetoric by a demagogue, often appealing to emotions and prejudices rather than reason.|The crowd, demagoged by the speaker's fiery rhetoric, turned violent.  人群被演说家煽动性的言辞所蛊惑，变得暴力起来。|inflamed, agitated, incited|reasoned, calmed, pacified|verb (past participle)

demagogue:/ˈdeməɡɒɡ/ |n.|煽动者，蛊惑民心的政客|A political leader who seeks support by appealing to popular desires and prejudices rather than by using rational argument.|The demagogue stirred up the crowd with his hateful speech.  这个煽动者用他充满仇恨的演讲煽动人群。|rabble-rouser, agitator, firebrand|statesman, diplomat|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek dēmagōgos (leader of the people), from dēmos (people) + agōgos (leading).
