alto:/ˈæltoʊ/ (US), /ˈæltoʊ/ (UK)| n. adj.|女低音；中音部；男高音（尤指假声男高音）|A singing voice, part, or instrument between soprano and tenor. Also, the highest adult male voice type that uses falsetto.|The alto section sang beautifully. 女低音部唱得优美。|近义词:contralto, mezzo-soprano (for voices); tenor (for instruments)|反义词:soprano, bass|词性:名词，形容词
词源: Italian, from Latin *altus 'high' (used of male voices an octave higher than tenor).
