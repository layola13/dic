butteriness:/ˈbʌtərɪnɪs/
n.|奶油味；油腻感；黄油味|The butteriness of the croissants was delightful. 羊角面包的奶油味令人愉悦。|近义词: richness, creaminess|反义词: dryness, blandness|词性:名词
Etymology: From butter + -i- + -ness.  Butter comes from Old English butere, from Latin butyrum, from Greek boutyron, of Scythian origin.
