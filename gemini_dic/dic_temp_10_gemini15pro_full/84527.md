hammer:/ˈhæmər/| n. vt.|锤子；榔头；敲打；反复强调|A hammer is a tool with a heavy metal head attached to a handle, used for hitting things.  To hammer something means to hit it repeatedly with a hammer or similar object. It can also mean to defeat someone decisively or to emphasize something repeatedly.|He used a hammer to drive the nail into the wood. 他用锤子把钉子钉进木头里。|近义词：mallet, gavel, pound, beat|反义词: (For the verb) caress, stroke|n. v.
