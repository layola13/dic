colourism: /ˈkʌlərɪzəm/ (US) /ˈkʌlərɪzəm/ (UK)| n. |肤色歧视，颜色主义|Prejudice or discrimination against individuals with a dark skin tone, typically among people of the same ethnic or racial group.|Example: Colourism affects many communities of color, where lighter skin is often seen as more desirable. 肤色歧视影响着许多有色人种社区，在这些社区里，较浅的肤色通常被认为更受欢迎。

Example:  She experienced colourism within her own family, being treated differently from her lighter-skinned siblings.  她在自己的家庭中经历了肤色歧视，受到的待遇与她肤色较浅的兄弟姐妹不同。

|近义词：discrimination, prejudice, bias, bigotry|反义词: equality, acceptance, inclusion|词性: noun
