de-automating:/diːˈɔːtəmeɪtɪŋ/
verb
取消自动化，逆自动化
The process of converting a previously automated system or process back to manual operation.
Companies are considering de-automating some customer service functions to provide a more personal touch.  公司正在考虑取消某些客户服务功能的自动化，以提供更个性化的服务。
Manufacturing plants may de-automate certain assembly lines if the cost of maintenance outweighs the benefits of automation. 如果维护成本超过自动化的好处，制造厂可能会取消某些装配线的自动化。
near synonyms: manualizing, reverting, undoing automation
antonyms: automating, mechanizing
词性: 动词
