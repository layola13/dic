meningitis:/ˌmɛnɪnˈdʒaɪtɪs/
n.
脑膜炎
Inflammation of the meninges, the membranes surrounding the brain and spinal cord, typically caused by an infection.
Meningitis can be a very serious illness. 脑膜炎可能是一种非常严重的疾病。
The child was diagnosed with bacterial meningitis.  这个孩子被诊断出患有细菌性脑膜炎。

近义词:encephalitis (脑炎)  (This is a related but distinct condition affecting the brain itself.)
反义词: (Meningitis doesn't have a direct antonym.  The absence of meningitis would be simply "health" or "wellness" in relation to the meninges.)
词性:noun
