artisanal:/ˌɑːrtɪˈzænəl/ | adj. | 手工的；手工艺的 | Made in a traditional or non-mechanized way, typically by skilled craftspeople. |  例句：We tasted a selection of artisanal cheeses. 我们品尝了一系列手工奶酪。

例句：The artisanal bread was delicious.  这种手工面包很美味。| 近义词：handmade, handcrafted, hand-crafted,  | 反义词：mass-produced, factory-made, industrial | 词性: 形容词

Etymology->
From French *artisanal*, from Italian *artigianale*, from *artigiano* "artisan," from Vulgar Latin *artitianus*, alteration of Latin *articianus* "craftsman," of uncertain origin.
