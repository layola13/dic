brothelly: /ˈbrʌðəlli/ | adj. | 妓院的；淫乱的；像妓院的 | Resembling or characteristic of a brothel; lewd. |  The atmosphere in the club was decidedly brothelly.  俱乐部里的气氛明显很淫乱。| 近义词：bawdy, lewd, whorish | 反义词: chaste, pure, virtuous | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From brothel + -ly.  "Brothel" comes from Middle English "brothel," meaning a worthless person, later a prostitute, and eventually a house of prostitution.  This evolved from Old English "broðen," meaning ruined, depraved.
