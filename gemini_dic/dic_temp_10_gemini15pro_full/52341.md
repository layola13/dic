dictate:/ˈdɪkteɪt/ (US) /dɪkˈteɪt/ (UK)| v. n.|口述；命令；支配；规定|To say or read aloud words for someone to write down; to give orders authoritatively or arrogantly.|She dictated a letter to her secretary. 她向她的秘书口述了一封信。|synonyms: command, order, prescribe, direct|antonyms: obey, comply, submit|verb
