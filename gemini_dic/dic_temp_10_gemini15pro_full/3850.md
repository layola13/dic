agorae:/ˈæɡəriː/
n.
集市，广场，市场
A public space or marketplace used for assemblies and commercial activity, especially in ancient Greece.
The philosophers often debated in the agorae of Athens.  雅典的哲学家们经常在集市上辩论。

近义词:marketplace, forum, plaza
反义词:None
词性:Noun
