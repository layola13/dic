antalkaline:/ˌæntælˈkəlaɪn/
adjective
抗碱的; 中和碱性的
Reduces or neutralizes alkalinity.  Reduces the alkaline content of something.
This antalkaline agent helps to balance the pH of the soil.  这种抗碱剂有助于平衡土壤的pH值。
Antalkaline tablets can help with acid indigestion. 抗碱性药片可以帮助治疗胃酸过多。
synonyms: acidifying, neutralizing
antonyms: alkaline, basic
adjective
