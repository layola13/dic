imperil:/ɪmˈpɛrəl/ /ɪmˈpɛrɪl/ | vt. | 危害，使处于危险 |To put something or someone at risk of being harmed, injured, or destroyed. | The rising floodwaters imperiled the lives of the villagers.  上涨的洪水危及村民的生命。|  The scandal imperiled his career.  这桩丑闻危及了他的事业。|Synonyms: endanger, jeopardize, threaten, risk | Antonyms: protect, safeguard, secure | verb
