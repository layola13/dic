formica:/fɔːrˈmaɪkə/| n. |福米卡，胶木，蚁属|A hard, heat-resistant thermosetting plastic used for countertops, furniture, etc. Also, a genus of ants.|例句：The kitchen countertops were made of formica. 厨房的台面是用福米卡做的。

例句：Formica ants are known for their complex social structures.  蚁属蚂蚁以其复杂的社会结构而闻名。|近义词：laminate, plastic,  (for the material) ant (for the insect)|反义词:wood, metal (for the material) |词性:noun
