allhallowtide: /ɔːlˈhæləʊˌtaɪd/ (UK) /ɔːlˈhæloʊˌtaɪd/ (US)
|n.|万圣节节期；诸圣节期|The period of three days from 31 October to 2 November, encompassing All Hallows' Eve (Halloween), All Saints' Day (All Hallows' Day), and All Souls' Day. |During Allhallowtide, many people remember the dead. 在万圣节期间，许多人都会缅怀逝者。|Synonym: Hallowtide|Antonym: None|Noun
