autisms:/ˈɔːtɪzəmz/
n.
自闭症（复数）
A developmental disorder of variable severity that is characterized by difficulty in social interaction and communication and by restricted or repetitive patterns of thought and behavior.  The plural form "autisms" is sometimes used to acknowledge the diversity of the autism spectrum.
People with autisms often have unique strengths and challenges.  患有自闭症的人通常具有独特的优势和挑战。
autism spectrum disorder, autism spectrum conditions
neurotypical
noun
