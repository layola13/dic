compressible:/kəmˈprɛsəbəl/| adj.|可压缩的，可浓缩的|Able to be compressed or made smaller or more compact.|例句：Air is a compressible fluid. 空气是一种可压缩流体。

例句：The data is compressible to save disk space.  数据可以压缩以节省磁盘空间。|近义词：condensable, reducible, contractible|反义词：incompressible, noncompressible|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *compressĭbilis, from Latin compressus (past participle of comprimere, "to compress"), from com- ("together") + premere ("to press").
