outwiggling:/aʊtˈwɪɡlɪŋ/
verb
扭动胜过; 通过扭动超过
Moving with more vigorous wiggling motions than another; surpassing in wiggling.
The baby eel outwiggled its siblings to reach the ocean first.  小鳗鱼扭动着身体超过了它的兄弟姐妹，第一个到达了大海。
近义词:outmaneuvering, outsquirming, outwriggling
反义词:being outwiggled
