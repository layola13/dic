effusions:/ɪˈfjuːʒənz/
n.
流露；泻出；渗出物；(感情的)迸发
an instance of giving off something such as a liquid, light, or smell. An unrestrained expression of words or feelings.
The effusion of blood from the wound was alarming.  伤口流出的血令人担忧。
Her poem was an effusion of grief after her mother's death. 这首诗是她母亲去世后悲痛的流露。
Synonyms: outpouring, discharge, emission, outburst, expression
Antonyms: suppression, concealment, repression
noun

effusion:/ɪˈfjuːʒən/
n.
流露；泻出；渗出物；(感情的)迸发
An act of talking or writing in an unrestrained or heartfelt way.
His recent effusions of anger have alienated his friends. 他最近的愤怒爆发疏远了他的朋友们。
The article was filled with effusions of praise for the new mayor. 这篇文章充满了对新市长的赞美之词。
Synonyms: outpouring, gush, overflow, spate, torrent
Antonyms: restraint, reserve, reticence
noun
