noncharacters: /nɒnˈkærəktərz/ | n. | 非字符 | Characters are generally considered letters, numbers, punctuation marks, and symbols that are part of a standardized writing system.  Noncharacters are anything that doesn't fall into that category, often used in computing to represent control codes or invalid data. |  Control characters are noncharacters that control a device, such as a printer.  The file contained several noncharacters, which caused the program to crash. | Near synonyms: invalid characters, control codes, unprintable characters | Antonyms: printable characters, valid characters | Noun
