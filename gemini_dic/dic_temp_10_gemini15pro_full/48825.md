defogged:/diːˈfɒɡd/ /diːˈfɑːɡd/ | v. | 除雾；使清晰 |  To remove fog or condensation from something, especially a glass surface. Also metaphorically, to clarify or make something easier to understand. | The mechanic defogged the windshield. 技工擦掉了挡风玻璃上的雾。 |Synonyms: cleared, clarified, demystified | Antonyms: fogged, obscured, clouded | verb
