colophon: /ˈkɒləfən/ (UK) /ˈkɑːləfɑːn/ (US) | n. | 出版商标记，版权页 | An inscription placed at the end of a book or manuscript, often containing facts about its production, such as the printer, date, and place of publication.  It can also refer to an emblem or symbol used by a printer or publisher. | Example 1: The colophon at the end of the book indicated that it was printed in Venice in 1523. 这本书末尾的出版商标记表明它于1523年在威尼斯印刷。

Example 2:  The publisher's colophon, a stylized griffin, appeared on the title page. 出版商的标记，一个程式化的狮鹫，出现在标题页上。| Synonym: imprint, device, logo | Antonym: N/A | noun
