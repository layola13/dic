handover:/ˈhændoʊvər/ (US) /ˈhændəʊvə(r)/ (UK)| n.  |移交，交接，交班|The act of passing responsibility or control of something to someone else.|The handover of the presidency will take place in January. 总统的交接仪式将于一月份举行。|synonyms: transfer, transition, delegation|antonyms: retention, takeover|noun

handover:/ˈhændoʊvər/ (US) /ˈhændəʊvə(r)/ (UK)| v.  |移交，交出|To pass responsibility or control of something to someone else.|The CEO will handover his responsibilities to the new CEO next month. 首席执行官下个月将把他的职责移交给新任首席执行官。|synonyms: transfer, relinquish, surrender|antonyms: retain, keep, seize|verb
