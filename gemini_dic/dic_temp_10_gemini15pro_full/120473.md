minster:/ˈmɪnstər/| n.|大教堂；修道院；部长|A large or important church, originally one attached to a monastery. Also, a person who heads a government department in some countries. |York Minster is a magnificent cathedral.  约克大教堂是一座宏伟的大教堂。|The Prime Minister addressed the nation. 首相向全国发表讲话。|Synonyms: cathedral, abbey (for church meaning); secretary, minister (for government meaning)|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|noun
