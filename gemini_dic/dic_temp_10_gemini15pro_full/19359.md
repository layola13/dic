bibliopole:/ˈbɪbliəˌpoʊl/| n.|书商；售书者|A person who sells books, especially rare or used books. |例句：The bibliopole carefully examined the first edition.  这位书商仔细地检查了这本初版书。

例句：I found a signed copy of my favorite novel at the bibliopole's shop. 我在那个书商的店里找到了一本我喜爱的小说的签名版。|近义词：bookseller, book dealer|反义词:book buyer|词性:noun
