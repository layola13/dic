misproposing:/ˌmɪsprəˈpoʊzɪŋ/
verb
错误地提议；提出不恰当的建议
making or expressing an inappropriate or incorrect proposal
He was misproposing solutions that would only worsen the problem. 他错误地提出了一些只会使问题恶化的解决方案。
misstating, misrepresenting
correctly proposing
