loses: /ˈluːzɪz/ /ˈluːziz/ | v. | 失去，输掉 | Third person singular present tense form of "lose". He loses his keys every week.  他每周都丢钥匙。 | 近义词：mislays, forfeits, drops, fails | 反义词：wins, gains, finds, obtains | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From Middle English *lesen*, from Old English *lēosan* (to lose, destroy, perish), from Proto-Germanic *leusaną* (to lose), from Proto-Indo-European *leu- (to loosen, detach, separate).
