centesimal:/ˌsɛntɪˈsɪməl/
adjective
百分之一的；百分制的
Relating to or divided into hundredths.
The centesimal system of measuring angles divides a right angle into 100 grades. 百分度量角系统将直角分成100度。
The report expressed the results as centesimal fractions.  报告用百分数表示结果。
Synonym: hundredth, percentage
Antonym: whole, entire
词性: adjective
