convellent:/kənˈvʌlsənt/| adj.|痉挛的，抽搐的；令人捧腹的|Producing or characterized by convulsions; causing fits of laughter. |例句：The child suffered convellent fits. 这个孩子患有痉挛性抽搐。

例句：His convellent laughter filled the room. 他充满房间的、令人捧腹的笑声。|近义词：spasmodic, jerky, convulsive, hilarious|反义词：smooth, steady, calm, serious|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *convulsus*, past participle of convellere "to tear loose, pull violently," from com- "together" (see com-) + vellere "to pluck, tear" (see velar).
