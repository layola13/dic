overfeminizes:/ˌoʊvərˈfɛmɪnaɪzɪz/
verb
过度女性化
Makes something or someone excessively feminine, often in a way considered stereotypical or artificial.
Some critics argue that the media overfeminizes young girls. 一些评论家认为媒体过度女性化了年轻女孩。
The author was accused of overfeminizing the male characters in his novel.  这位作者被指责在他的小说中过度女性化了男性角色。
overemphasize, exaggerate
understate, downplay
