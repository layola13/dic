neocracy:/niˈɒkrəsi/ | n. | 新统治；新手当政；新贵统治 | Government by the new or inexperienced, especially in business or technology. | The rise of social media influencers has led to a neocracy in marketing. 社交媒体影响者的兴起导致了营销领域的新贵统治。| The company is a neocracy, run by young, tech-savvy entrepreneurs. 这家公司是由年轻、精通技术的企业家经营的新贵统治。 |  近义词: technocracy, meritocracy | 反义词: gerontocracy, aristocracy | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Greek *νέος (neos, “new”) +‎ *-κρατία (-kratía, “rule, power”), from κράτος (kratos, “strength, power”). 源于希腊语νέος (neos, “新的”) +‎ *-κρατία (-kratía, “统治，权力”), 来自κράτος (kratos, “力量，权力”)。
