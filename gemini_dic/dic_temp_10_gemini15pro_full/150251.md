pandiagonal:/ˌpænˌdaɪˈæɡənəl/
adjective
泛对角线的
Extending the concept of a magic square, a pandiagonal magic square is a magic square with the additional property that the broken diagonals, i.e. the diagonals that wrap around the edges of the square, also add up to the magic constant.
A pandiagonal magic square of order 4 is shown below.  Each row, column, and diagonal (including the broken diagonals) sums to 34.
近义词: magic, diabolic (in the context of magic squares)
反义词: None
