countershaft:/ˈkaʊntərʃæft/| n.| 副轴；中间轴|A short rotating shaft that supports a gear wheel, especially one connected to the main drive shaft of a vehicle.|The countershaft transmits power to the rear wheels.|近义词:layshaft, jackshaft|反义词:mainshaft|词性:noun
