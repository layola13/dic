inappealable:/ˌɪnəˈpiːləbəl/
adjective
不可上诉的
Not subject to appeal; final.
The judge's decision is inappealable.  法官的决定是不可上诉的。
The Supreme Court's ruling on the matter is inappealable. 最高法院对此事的裁决是不可上诉的。
Synonyms: final, conclusive, definitive, unchallengeable
Antonyms: appealable, reversible, questionable
词性: adjective
