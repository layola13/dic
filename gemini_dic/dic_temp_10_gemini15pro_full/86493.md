heinous:/ˈheɪnəs/| adj.|可憎的，十恶不赦的| (of a person or wrongful act, especially a crime) utterly odious or wicked.|The judge called the crime heinous and sentenced the perpetrator to life in prison. 法官称这起犯罪令人发指，判处犯罪者终身监禁。|近义词：abominable, atrocious, monstrous, egregious, odious|反义词：commendable, laudable, praiseworthy|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *heinous, borrowed from Old French haïnos (“hateful, odious”), from hair (“to hate”). Doublet of hateful.
