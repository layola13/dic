arthropodous:/ɑːrˈθrɒpədəs/
| adj. |节肢动物的|Relating to or characteristic of arthropods; having jointed limbs and a segmented body.  Derived from "arthropod" + "-ous".|例句：Spiders, insects, and crustaceans are all arthropodous creatures. 蜘蛛、昆虫和甲壳类动物都是节肢动物。|例句:  The arthropodous exoskeleton provides protection and support. 节肢动物的外骨骼提供保护和支撑。|近义词：invertebrate, segmented|反义词: vertebrate|词性: adjective
