causticness:/ˈkɔːstɪknəs/| n.|苛刻；刻薄；腐蚀性|The causticness of her remarks stung him. 她尖刻的言辞刺痛了他。|近义词：acrimony, bitterness, mordancy, sharpness, vitriol|反义词：gentleness, kindness, mildness|词性:名词
Etymology: From Late Latin *causticus* "burning," from Greek *kaustikos*, from *kaiein* "to burn." The suffix "-ness" transforms the adjective "caustic" into the noun "causticness."
