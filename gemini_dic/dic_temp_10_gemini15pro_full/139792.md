octave: /ˈɒktɪv/ (UK) /ˈɑːktɪv/ (US) | n. | 八度音程；八度音阶；八行诗 | A series of eight notes occupying the interval between (and including) two notes, one having twice or half the frequency of vibration of the other.  A group of eight. | The soprano hit a high octave.  女高音唱到了一个高八度。| synonyms: range, scale, gamut | antonyms: N/A | noun
