词源:  "Come-hither" is an adjective meaning "inviting or alluring, especially in a sexually suggestive way".  It originates from the phrase "come hither", meaning "come here". The suffix "-ness" is added to form the noun "come-hitherness", which denotes the quality of being come-hither.
