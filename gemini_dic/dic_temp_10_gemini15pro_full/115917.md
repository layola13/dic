maror:/mɑːˈrɔːr/
n.
bitter herbs
A bitter herb, especially horseradish, eaten at the Passover Seder.
The maror symbolizes the bitterness of slavery in Egypt.
The Seder plate included charoset, maror, and a roasted egg.  逾越节餐盘包括charoset，maror和一个烤鸡蛋。
During Passover, Jews eat maror to remember the bitterness of slavery. 在逾越节期间，犹太人吃maror来纪念奴隶制的苦涩。
近义词:bitter herbs
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly sweet herbs)
词性:noun
