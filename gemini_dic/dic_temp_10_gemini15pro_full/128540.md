NFTs:/ˌɛnˌɛfˈtiz/ | n. | Non-Fungible Tokens |  Non-Fungible Tokens are unique cryptographic tokens that exist on a blockchain and cannot be replicated. They represent ownership of an asset, which can be digital or physical. |  Example:  NFTs are revolutionizing the art world. 数字艺术品正在彻底改变艺术世界。

Example: He sold his digital artwork as an NFT. 他把他的数码艺术作品作为NFT出售了。 | 近义词: digital assets, crypto collectibles | 反义词: fungible tokens | 词性: noun
