disreputabilities: /dɪsˌrepjʊtəˈbɪlɪtiz/ | n. | 声名狼藉，不光彩的行为 | The politician's past disreputabilities came back to haunt him. 这位政治家过去不光彩的行为回来困扰他了。 |  acts of disrepute, discreditable actions, shady dealings | respectability, honor | noun

Etymology:
From dis- (not, opposite of) + reputable (having a good reputation) + -ity (noun suffix forming abstract nouns).  Ultimately from Latin *reputare* (to reckon, think)
