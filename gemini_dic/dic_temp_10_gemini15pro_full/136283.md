nonreprehensibility:/ˌnɑːnˌrɛprɪˌhɛnsɪˈbɪləti/
n.|无可指责；无懈可击|The quality or state of being not reprehensible; blamelessness. |His nonreprehensibility in the matter was clear to everyone.  他在此事上无可指责，这一点大家都很清楚。|Synonyms: blamelessness, irreproachability, unimpeachability|Antonyms: culpability, blameworthiness, censurability|noun
