fluoridization:/ˌflʊərɪdəˈzeɪʃən/
noun
氟化作用
The process of adding fluoride to something, typically water, to reduce tooth decay.
Community water fluoridization is credited with dramatically reducing cavities in children. 社区饮水加氟被认为显著减少了儿童龋齿。
fluoridation, fluoridation of water
defluoridation
n.
