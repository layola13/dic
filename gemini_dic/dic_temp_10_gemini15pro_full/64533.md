errable:/ˈɛrəbəl/
adjective
易错的，可能出错的
capable of erring; liable to make mistakes.
The system is errable, so regular checks are necessary.  这个系统容易出错，所以需要定期检查。
The errable nature of human judgment makes it essential to have checks and balances. 人类判断容易出错的本质使得制衡机制至关重要。
近义词:fallible, prone to error, imperfect
反义词:infallible, perfect, flawless
词性:adjective
词源:From err (to make a mistake) + -able (capable of being).  来自err（犯错误）+ -able（能够…的）。
