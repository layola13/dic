declaratorily:/dɪˌklærəˈtɔːrɪli/| adv.|声明地，宣言地|In a manner that makes a formal statement or declaration.  It implies an explicit and often official assertion.|The government declaratorily stated its commitment to reducing carbon emissions. 政府声明承诺减少碳排放。|近义词：explicitly, overtly, avowedly|反义词: implicitly, tacitly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From the adjective "declaratory" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Declaratory" comes from the Late Latin "declaratorius," from the Latin "declarare" (to make clear, explain), from "de-" (down, completely) + "clarare" (to make clear), from "clarus" (clear, bright).
