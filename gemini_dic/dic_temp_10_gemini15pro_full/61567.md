emenders:/ɪˈmɛndərz/
n.
修改者；校订者；修正者
People who make corrections or improvements, especially to texts.
The emenders of the ancient texts worked carefully to restore the original meaning. 古代文献的校订者仔细地工作以恢复原文的含义。
近义词: correctors, revisers, editors
反义词: corrupters, distorters


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *emender*, from Anglo-Norman *emender*,  from Old French *emender*  "to correct, amend, improve," from Latin *ēmendāre* "to free from faults, correct, improve," from *ex- "out" + *mendum*  "fault, blemish."
