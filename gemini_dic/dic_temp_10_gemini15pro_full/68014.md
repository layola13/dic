factor-into:/ˈfæktər ˈɪntuː/ | phrasal verb | 考虑到，把…作为因素计入 | To include something as an important element when making a decision or judgment.  | Example 1: We need to factor the weather into our travel plans. 我们需要把天气因素考虑进我们的旅行计划。 | Example 2:  The rising cost of materials needs to be factored into the price of the product.  上涨的材料成本需要计入产品价格。 | Synonyms: consider, account for, take into account, include | Antonyms: disregard, ignore, neglect | verb phrase

factor:/ˈfæktər/ | n. | 因素，要素 | Something that contributes to a result. | Example 1:  Cost was a major factor in our decision. 成本是我们决定的一个主要因素。 | Example 2:  Stress is a factor in many illnesses. 压力是许多疾病的一个因素。 | Synonyms: element, component, ingredient, aspect | Antonyms: whole, entirety | noun

into:/ˈɪntuː/ | prep. | 进入，到…里面 | Expressing movement or action with the result that someone or something becomes enclosed or surrounded by something else. | Example 1: He walked into the room. 他走进了房间。 | Example 2: She poured the milk into the glass. 她把牛奶倒进了玻璃杯。 | Synonyms: to, toward, inside | Antonyms: out of, from | preposition
