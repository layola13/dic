acumen: /ˈækjəmən/ | n. | 敏锐, 精明, 聪明 |  keen insight; shrewdness. The ability to make good judgments and quick decisions, typically in a particular domain. | Her business acumen helped her to succeed in a competitive market. 她的商业敏锐帮助她在竞争激烈的市场中取得了成功。

His political acumen allowed him to navigate the complex situation. 他的政治敏锐使他能够应对复杂的局势。 | 近义词: perspicacity, shrewdness, insight, sharpness, wisdom, sagacity | 反义词: stupidity, ignorance, obtuseness | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *acūmen* "sharp point, sharpness,"  from *acuere* "to sharpen," from *acus* "needle."
