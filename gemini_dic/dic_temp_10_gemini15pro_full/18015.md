begild:/bɪˈɡɪld/| vt.|镀金，虚饰，使迷惑|To cover with or as if with a thin layer of gold; to give a deceptively attractive appearance to.|例句：The setting sun begilded the clouds. 落日给云朵镀上了一层金边。

例句：He was beguiled by her beauty. 他被她的美貌所迷惑。|近义词：adorn, embellish, deceive, mislead|反义词：tarnish, expose, reveal|词性：动词
