flexibilities:/ˌflɛksəˈbɪlətiz/
|n.|灵活性；弹性；适应性|The flexibilities of the new system allow for a wider range of applications. 新系统的灵活性允许更广泛的应用。|
|Example 1: The company's flexibilities in scheduling allowed employees to better manage their work-life balance.  这家公司在日程安排上的灵活性使员工能够更好地管理他们的工作与生活平衡。|
|Example 2:  The flexibilities of the material make it ideal for creating complex shapes.  这种材料的灵活性使其成为创造复杂形状的理想选择。|
|Synonym: adaptability, versatility, elasticity, pliability|
|Antonym: rigidity, inflexibility, stiffness|
|词性: 名词|
