huskiest:/ˈhʌskiɪst/| adj.|最沙哑的，声音最粗的|Having the roughest, deepest, and most hoarse voice.|He had the huskiest voice I'd ever heard. 他有着我听过最沙哑的声音。|近义词：hoarsest, gruffest, raspiest|反义词: clearest, smoothest|词性:形容词
词源: From husk + -y + -est.  Relating to "husk" meaning a dry outer covering, implying a rough or dry quality to the voice.
