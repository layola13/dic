lavolt:/ləˈvoʊlt/| n.| 拉伏特（电压单位，等于一百万伏特）| A lavolt is a unit of electromotive force, equal to one million volts.  It is used to measure extremely high voltages.| The power lines carry electricity at several lavolts.  这些输电线输送的电力高达几百万伏特。|近义词:megavolt|反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement)|词性:noun
