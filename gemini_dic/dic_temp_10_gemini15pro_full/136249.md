nonrepaying:/ˌnɒnriˈpeɪɪŋ/
adjective
不偿还的，不付清的
Not repaying a loan or debt.
The bank classified the loan as nonrepaying after several missed payments.  这家银行在几次逾期付款后将该贷款列为不偿还贷款。
Several nonrepaying customers caused significant losses for the small business.  几个不偿还债务的客户给这家小企业造成了重大损失。
近义词: delinquent, defaulting
反义词: paying, solvent
词性: adjective

词源:
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "repaying" (present participle of "repay")
From "re-" (back, again) + "pay" (from Old French "paier")
