codicil: /ˈkɒdɪsɪl/ (British), /ˈkɑːdəsɪl/ (American)| n.|遗嘱附录，补遗|An addition or supplement that explains, modifies, or revokes a will or part of one.|Example: He added a codicil to his will leaving his collection of stamps to his nephew. 他在遗嘱中添加了一个附录，将他的邮票收藏留给了他的侄子。|Example:  The codicil clarified the distribution of her estate after her husband's death.  这份附录澄清了在她丈夫去世后她遗产的分配。|Synonym:  appendix, supplement, addendum, amendment|Antonym:  original, base (document)|noun
