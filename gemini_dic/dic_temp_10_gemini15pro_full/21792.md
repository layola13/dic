bobby:/ˈbɒbi/ (UK) /ˈbɑːbi/ (US)| n.|警察，巡警|A British informal term for a police officer.|例句：The bobby on the beat helped the lost child. 巡逻的警察帮助了迷路的孩子。|例句：Call a bobby if you see anything suspicious. 如果你看到任何可疑的事情，就打电话给警察。|近义词：policeman, officer, cop|反义词:criminal, lawbreaker|noun
