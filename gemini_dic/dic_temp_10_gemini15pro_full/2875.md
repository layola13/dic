adversities:/ədˈvɜːrsətiz/| n.|逆境，不幸，灾难|Difficulties; misfortunes.|She overcame many adversities in her life. 她克服了生活中的许多逆境。|近义词：hardships, misfortunes, troubles, setbacks|反义词:prosperity, success|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *adversite*, from Old French *adversité*, from Latin *adversitās*, from *adversus* ("turned towards, opposite, unfavorable").

USAGE->
Example 1:  The company faced numerous adversities during the economic downturn. =  在经济低迷时期，该公司面临着许多逆境。

Example 2:  Despite the adversities they faced, they remained optimistic. = 尽管他们面临逆境，他们仍然保持乐观。


PHRASE->
in the face of adversity = 面对逆境
overcome adversity = 克服逆境
struggle with adversity = 与逆境斗争
cope with adversity =  应对逆境
triumph over adversity = 战胜逆境
