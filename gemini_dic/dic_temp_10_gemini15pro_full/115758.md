marinaded:/ˈmærɪneɪtɪd/
adjective|腌制的，浸泡过的|Soaked in a marinade. | The marinaded chicken was delicious. 腌制过的鸡肉很美味。|近义词：soaked, marinated|反义词:dry, unmarinated
