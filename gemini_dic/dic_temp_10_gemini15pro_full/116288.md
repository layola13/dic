massif: /mæˈsiːf/ (US), /mæˈsiːf/ (UK)| n.|山块，大型山体|A compact group of mountains, especially one that is separate from other groups.|The climbers attempted to scale the north face of the massif.|Synonyms: mountain range, mountain chain, block mountain|Antonyms: valley, plain|noun

massif: /mæˈsiːf/ (US), /mæˈsiːf/ (UK)| adj.|块状的，庞大的|Forming a compact mass; massive.|The massif structure of the building dominated the skyline.|Synonyms: massive, bulky, huge|Antonyms: small, tiny, miniature|adjective
