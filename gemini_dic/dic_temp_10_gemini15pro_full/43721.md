credulously:/ˈkrɛdʒələsli/ | adv. | 轻信地；易受骗地 | Showing a readiness to believe things easily; too trusting. | He credulously accepted the stranger's story. 他轻信了陌生人的故事。| 近义词: naively, gullibly, trustingly | 反义词: skeptically, cynically, suspiciously | 词性: 副词

credulous:/ˈkrɛdʒələs/ | adj. | 轻信的；易受骗的 | Having or showing too much willingness to believe things easily. |  A credulous person is easily fooled. 轻信的人很容易被愚弄。 | 近义词: naive, gullible, trusting | 反义词: skeptical, cynical, suspicious | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From Latin *credulus, from credere "to believe"
