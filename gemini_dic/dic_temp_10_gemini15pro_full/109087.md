legators:/ˈleɡeɪtərz/
noun
遗赠者；立遗嘱的人
A person who bequeaths or leaves personal property by a will.
The legators stipulated that the funds be used for educational purposes. 遗赠者规定这些资金应用于教育目的。
testators, donors, benefactors
heirs, beneficiaries
n.
