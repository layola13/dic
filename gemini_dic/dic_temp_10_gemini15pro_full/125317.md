multiheads:/ˈmʌltiˌhɛdz/ | n. | 多头 | Multiple attention mechanisms running in parallel.  This is a common component in Transformer models. | The model uses multiheads to attend to different parts of the input sequence. 该模型使用多头来关注输入序列的不同部分。| synonyms: multiple attention heads | antonyms: single head | noun
