antheroid:/ˈænθərɔɪd/| n. |精子器；游动精子|A motile male gamete of algae, ferns, mosses, and some fungi, analogous to the sperm cell of animals.|The antheroids swim through the water to reach the archegonium and fertilize the egg. 精子器游过水到达颈卵器并使卵子受精。|Near synonyms: spermatozoid, sperm|Antonym: egg|noun

antherozoid:/ˌænθərəˈzɔɪd/| n. |游动精子|A motile male gamete of algae, ferns, mosses, and some fungi, analogous to the sperm cell of animals.|The antherozoids are released into the water when the antheridium ruptures. 当精子器破裂时，游动精子被释放到水中。|Near synonyms: spermatozoid, sperm, antheroid|Antonym: egg|noun
