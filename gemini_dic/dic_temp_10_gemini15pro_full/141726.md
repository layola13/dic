oralism: /ˈɔːrəlɪzəm/ (US) /ˈɔːrəlɪzəm/ (UK)
n.
口语主义; 口语教学法
A system of educating deaf people by encouraging them to speak and lip-read rather than using sign language.
Oralism emphasizes spoken language in the education of deaf children. 口语主义强调在聋哑儿童的教育中使用口语。
The school adopted an oralist approach to deaf education. 这所学校采用了口语教学法来教育聋哑儿童。
近义词: lip-reading, speech therapy
反义词: sign language, manualism

词性: noun

词源: From Latin *ōrālis*  "of the mouth,"  from *ōs* (genitive *ōris*) "mouth."  +-ism.  Coined 1880.
