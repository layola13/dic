atonement:/əˈtoʊnment/ | n. |赎罪，弥补，补偿 |  The act of making amends for a wrong or injury.  | He sought atonement for his sins. 他寻求对自己罪孽的救赎。 |  Example:  She volunteered at the homeless shelter as atonement for her past mistakes. 她为了弥补过去的错误，在流浪汉收容所做义工。

| Synonyms:  reparation, amends, expiation, redemption | Antonyms:  vengeance, retribution | Noun
