mambos:/ˈmæmboʊz/ (US), /ˈmæmbəʊz/ (UK)| n.|曼波舞（复数）|A Latin American dance of Cuban origin, characterized by strong rhythms and stylized hip movements. |例句：The mambos were popular in the 1950s. 曼波舞在20世纪50年代很流行。

例句：He's learning how to do the mambos. 他正在学习跳曼波舞。

近义词：dance, rumba, cha-cha-cha|反义词:|n.

mambo:/ˈmæmboʊ/ (US), /ˈmæmbəʊ/ (UK)| n.|曼波舞（单数）|A Latin American dance of Cuban origin, characterized by strong rhythms and stylized hip movements. |例句：The mambo is a lively and energetic dance. 曼波舞是一种活泼而充满活力的舞蹈。

例句：She danced a fantastic mambo. 她跳了一段精彩的曼波舞。

近义词：dance, rumba, cha-cha-cha|反义词:|n.

mambo:/ˈmæmboʊ/ (US), /ˈmæmbəʊ/ (UK)| v.|跳曼波舞|To dance the mambo. |例句：They mamboed the night away. 他们跳曼波舞跳了一整夜。

例句：Let's mambo!  我们来跳曼波舞吧！

近义词：dance|反义词:|v.


词源: From Cuban Spanish *mambo, of uncertain origin. Possibly of Bantu origin.
