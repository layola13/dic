crystallographers:/ˌkrɪstəlˈɒɡrəfərz/
n.
晶体学家们
Scientists who study the structure and properties of crystals.
Crystallographers use X-ray diffraction to determine the arrangement of atoms in a crystal. 晶体学家使用X射线衍射来确定晶体中原子的排列。
The crystallographers determined the structure of the new protein. 晶体学家确定了新蛋白质的结构。
近义词: crystal scientists, crystal analysts
反义词: None
