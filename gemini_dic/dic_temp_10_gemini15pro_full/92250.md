hypertypical:/ˌhaɪpərˈtɪpɪk(ə)l/| adj.|超典型的，极具代表性的|Exhibiting features of a category to an excessive degree, exceeding the typical or expected characteristics.  It goes beyond simply being typical and represents an intensified or exaggerated form of the category's defining traits.|Her behavior was hypertypical of teenage rebellion, slamming doors and refusing to communicate. 她的行为是典型的青少年叛逆，摔门并且拒绝沟通。

The brightly colored, oversized cartoon characters were hypertypical examples of modern advertising. 这些颜色鲜艳、超大的卡通人物是现代广告的典型例子。|近义词：exaggerated, intensified, stereotypical, quintessential, archetypal|反义词：atypical, unusual, aberrant, unconventional|词性: adjective
