boult:/boʊlt/| v. | 筛(面粉); 脱粒; 猛然移动; | To sift or separate coarser from finer particles, often using a sieve or screen.  Also, to move suddenly or abruptly. | The baker boulted the flour before making the bread. 面包师在做面包前筛了面粉。

The startled deer boulted across the road. 受惊的鹿猛然穿过马路。 | 近义词：sift, bolt, dash, dart | 反义词: dawdle, linger | 动词

词源: From Old French *bulter, from Vulgar Latin *buculāre, from Latin buccula (“cheek, mouthful”), from bucca (“cheek”). Related to buckle.
