gramophonist: /ɡræˈmɒfənɪst/ /ɡrəˈmɑːfənɪst/
n.
留声机演奏者；灌音师；唱片收藏家
A person who plays a gramophone or makes gramophone recordings.  Someone who collects or specializes in gramophone records.
The gramophonist carefully placed the needle on the spinning record. 留声机演奏者小心翼翼地将唱针放在旋转的唱片上。
The famous gramophonist has a vast collection of rare 78s.  这位著名的唱片收藏家收藏了大量的稀有78转唱片。
Synonyms:  record collector, DJ, recording artist
Antonyms: listener, audience
