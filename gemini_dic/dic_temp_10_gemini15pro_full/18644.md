benzoate:/ˈben.zə.weɪt/ | n. | 苯甲酸盐; 安息香酸盐 |A salt or ester of benzoic acid.  It is used as a preservative in food and other products. | Sodium benzoate is a common food preservative. 苯甲酸钠是一种常见的食品防腐剂。| synonym: preservative | antonym:  (No direct antonym, consider terms like "decomposition agent" or "spoilage catalyst") | noun
