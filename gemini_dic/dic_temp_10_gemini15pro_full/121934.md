misshipped:/ˌmɪsˈʃɪpt/
verb
错运，误运
shipped to the wrong destination.  Sent to an incorrect address due to an error in the shipping process.
The package was misshipped to California instead of Colorado.  包裹被错运到加利福尼亚而不是科罗拉多。
The online retailer misshipped my order, and I received the wrong items.  在线零售商错运了我的订单，我收到了错误的商品。
misdirected, wrongly shipped, misrouted
correctly shipped, delivered as intended
verb
