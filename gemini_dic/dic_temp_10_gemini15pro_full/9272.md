antipesticide:/ˌæntiˈpɛstɪsaɪd/| n. adj.| 抗农药；杀虫剂的|A substance that counteracts the effects of a pesticide, or opposes the use of pesticides.|Farmers are exploring the use of antipesticides to mitigate the harmful effects of conventional pesticides. 农民正在探索使用抗农药来减轻传统农药的有害影响。|The antipesticide movement is gaining momentum as people become more aware of the potential dangers of these chemicals.  随着人们越来越意识到这些化学品的潜在危险，反农药运动正在 gaining momentum。|近义词：pesticide antagonist, anti-insecticide |反义词:pesticide, insecticide|词性:名词/形容词
