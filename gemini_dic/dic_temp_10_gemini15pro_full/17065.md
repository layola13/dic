bathygraphic:/ˌbæθɪˈɡræfɪk/
adjective
水深测量的；海底地形的
relating to the measurement of the depths of oceans or other large bodies of water; relating to the mapping of the ocean floor.
The bathygraphic survey revealed a previously unknown trench.  水深测量揭示了一个以前不为人知的深沟。
The report included detailed bathygraphic charts of the region. 报告中包含了该地区详细的海底地形图。
近义词:  hydrographic
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "topographic" for land)
词性: adjective
