mohican:/ˈmoʊhɪkən/ (US), /məˈhiːkən/ (UK)| n. adj.|莫希干人，莫希干族的；莫希干式发型|A member of a Native American people formerly inhabiting the upper Hudson River valley. Also, a hairstyle with a shaved head except for a central strip of hair.  The term can also be used as an adjective to describe something related to the Mohican people or their culture.|例句：He wore his hair in a mohican. 他梳着莫西干头。

例句：The Mohican tribe was known for their bravery. 莫希干部落以其勇敢而闻名。|近义词：Mohegan (often used interchangeably, though technically distinct), Iroquois (related tribe)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun, adjective
