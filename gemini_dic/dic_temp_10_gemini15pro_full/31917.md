chefdoms: /ˈʃɛfdəmz/
n.
酋邦；酋长统治的区域
A territory or people ruled by a chief.
The anthropologist studied the social structures of several Pacific Island chefdoms.  这位人类学家研究了几个太平洋岛屿酋邦的社会结构。
Archaeological evidence suggests that complex chefdoms existed in this region long before European contact. 考古证据表明，早在欧洲人接触之前，该地区就存在着复杂的酋长统治。
近义词: chieftainships, tribes
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "kingdoms" or "empires" for contrasting political structures)
词性: noun (plural)


Etymology:
From "chefdom," from "chief" (leader) + "-dom" (suffix denoting domain, condition, or rank). "Chief" ultimately comes from Old French "chief" (head, leader), from Latin "caput" (head).
