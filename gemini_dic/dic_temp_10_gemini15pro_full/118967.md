meteorological:/ˌmiːtiərəˈlɒdʒɪkl/ | adj. |气象的，气象学的| Relating to the branch of science concerned with the processes and phenomena of the atmosphere, especially as a means of forecasting the weather. |例句：The meteorological office issued a warning about the approaching hurricane. 气象局发布了飓风即将来临的警告。

例句：Meteorological data is crucial for accurate weather forecasting.  气象数据对于准确的天气预报至关重要。

近义词：atmospheric, climatic |反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective
