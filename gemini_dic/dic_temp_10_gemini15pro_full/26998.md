calculatable:/ˈkælkjələbəl/| adj.|可计算的，可推算的|Able to be calculated or estimated.|The risks are not easily calculatable.  这些风险不容易计算。|The cost of the project is calculatable.  这个项目的成本是可以计算的。|近义词：computable, estimable, predictable|反义词：incalculable, unpredictable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From 'calculate' + '-able'. 'Calculate' comes from Latin 'calculus' meaning "pebble" (used in counting).
