paradox:/ˈpærədɒks/ (UK) /ˈpærədɑːks/ (US)| n.|悖论，似非而是的隽语|A statement or proposition that seems self-contradictory or absurd but in reality expresses a possible truth.|This statement is a paradox, but it's true: the more you give, the more you receive. 这句话是一个悖论，但它是真的：你付出的越多，你得到的越多。|synonyms: contradiction, enigma, anomaly, absurdity|antonyms:  normality, standard, convention|noun
