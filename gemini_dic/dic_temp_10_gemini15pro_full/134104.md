nonimmersion:/ˌnɒnɪˈmɜːʃən/ | /ˌnɑːnɪˈmɜːrʒən/ | n. | 非沉浸式 | The process or state of not being immersed or fully involved in something. |  The student opted for nonimmersion language learning, preferring traditional classroom instruction. 这名学生选择了非沉浸式语言学习，更喜欢传统的课堂教学。

|  Nonimmersion virtual reality experiences are less impactful than fully immersive ones. 非沉浸式虚拟现实体验不如完全沉浸式体验那么有冲击力。|  近义词: traditional, conventional | 反义词: immersion | 词性: 名词

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin "non" meaning "not"
immersion (noun): from Latin "immersio" meaning "a dipping, plunging, sinking," from "immers-" past participle stem of "immergere" meaning "to plunge in, dip, sink," from "in-" meaning "in" + "mergere" meaning "to dip, plunge."
