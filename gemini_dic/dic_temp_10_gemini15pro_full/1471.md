acerous:/ˈæsərəs/
adjective
针状的;似针的
Resembling a needle; needle-shaped.  Thin and sharp, like a needle.
The plant has acerous leaves, which help it conserve water in the arid environment.  这种植物有针状的叶子，这有助于它在干旱的环境中保存水分。

The pine tree's acerous needles protect it from browsing animals. 松树针状的松针保护它免受动物啃食。

近义词:acicular, needle-like, sharp, pointed
反义词:blunt, rounded
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *acerōsus, from acer ‘sharp, keen’.
来自拉丁语 *acerōsus，源自 acer “锋利的，敏锐的”。


USAGE->
The cactus has acerous spines for protection. 仙人掌有针状的刺以保护自己。

Many conifers have acerous leaves adapted to cold climates. 许多针叶树有适应寒冷气候的针状叶子。
