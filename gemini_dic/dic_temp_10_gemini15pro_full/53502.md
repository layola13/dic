disassemble:/ˌdɪs.əˈsɛm.bəl/ | vt. | 拆卸，分解 |  He disassembled the clock to clean it. 他拆卸了钟表进行清洁。 | 例句：It's easy to disassemble this piece of furniture.  拆卸这件家具很容易。 | 近义词：take apart, dismantle, break down | 反义词：assemble, construct | 词性: 动词
