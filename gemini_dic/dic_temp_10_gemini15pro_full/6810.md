anarchism: /ˈænəkɪzəm/ (US), /ˈænəkɪzəm/ (UK)| n. |无政府主义|A political philosophy that advocates self-governed societies based on voluntary institutions. It often rejects all forms of hierarchy and authority.|例句：Anarchism is often misunderstood as chaos, but many forms advocate peaceful cooperation. 无政府主义常被误解为混乱，但许多形式提倡和平合作。

例句：The rise of anarchism in the late 19th century led to several significant social movements. 19世纪晚期无政府主义的兴起导致了几场重要的社会运动。

近义词：nihilism, lawlessness|反义词: authoritarianism, totalitarianism|词性:noun
