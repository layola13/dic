nonpsychologically:/ˌnɒnˌsaɪkəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ | adv. | 非心理地；不从心理学角度；| Not in a psychological way; without considering psychological factors. | He approached the problem nonpsychologically, focusing solely on the practical aspects. 他不从心理学角度处理这个问题，只关注实际方面。|  She explained the child's behavior nonpsychologically, attributing it to simple boredom. 她没有从心理学角度解释孩子的行为，而是将其归因于单纯的无聊。 | 近义词: objectively, practically, physically | 反义词: psychologically | 词性: 副词
