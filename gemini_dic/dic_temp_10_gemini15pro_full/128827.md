nightshade: /ˈnaɪtʃeɪd/ (US), /ˈnaɪtʃeɪd/ (UK)| n. |茄科植物；颠茄；夜来香|A plant of the nightshade family, such as the potato or tomato, typically having poisonous leaves and berries.|例句：Some nightshades, like tomatoes and potatoes, are edible.  一些茄科植物，比如西红柿和土豆，是可以食用的。

例句：Belladonna is a highly poisonous nightshade. 颠茄是一种剧毒的茄科植物。|近义词：solanaceous plant|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-poisonous plant)|词性: noun
