ichorhaemia:/ˌaɪkɔːˈriːmiə/
noun
脓血症
The presence of ichor or sanies in the blood.
The patient presented with ichorhaemia, indicating a severe infection.  病人出现脓血症，表明感染严重。
近义词:septicaemia, blood poisoning
反义词:(None, as it's a pathological condition)
词性:noun

ichor:/ˈaɪkɔːr/
noun
脓液;（尤指神话中神的）体液
A watery, acrid discharge from a wound or ulcer.  Also, in Greek mythology, the ethereal fluid that flowed in the veins of the gods.
The wound oozed ichor. 伤口渗出脓液。
近义词:pus, sanies
反义词:(None for the medical definition; blood for the mythological definition)
词性:noun
