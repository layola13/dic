nonbibulousnesses: /ˌnɒnbɪˈbjʊləsnəsɪz/ (British) /ˌnɑːnbɪˈbjʊləsnəsɪz/ (American)
n.
non-drinking, abstention from alcohol
The state or quality of being nonbibulous; abstinence from alcoholic drink.
His nonbibulousnesses surprised everyone at the party, as he was usually the life of the party.  他滴酒不沾让派对上的每个人都感到惊讶，因为他通常是派对的活跃分子。
The community was known for its nonbibulousnesses. 这个社区以其不饮酒而闻名。
近义词: abstinence, sobriety, temperance
反义词: drunkenness, alcoholism, bibulousness
词性: noun


Etymology:
From non- + bibulous + -ness.  Non- is a prefix meaning "not". Bibulous comes from the Latin bibulus "given to drinking," from bibere "to drink."  -ness is a suffix forming nouns expressing state or condition.
