entertain:/ˌentərˈteɪn/ | v. | 娱乐；招待；使有兴趣；怀有；考虑 | To provide amusement or enjoyment for (someone). To receive (someone) as a guest, typically providing food and drink.  |  例句：They entertained us with stories of their travels. 他们用他们的旅行故事招待我们。

例句：We were entertained by a magician at the party.  我们在派对上被一位魔术师娱乐了。 | 近义词：amuse, divert, host | 反义词：bore, tire | 词性：动词

entertain:/ˌentərˈteɪn/ | n. | 娱乐 | Amusement or enjoyment. (Rarely used as a noun) | 例句: The entertain was superb. (Rare usage)  这个娱乐很棒。(很少这样用)

例句: There was plenty of entertain at the resort. (Rare usage)  度假村有很多娱乐。(很少这样用) | 近义词：amusement, diversion | 反义词：boredom, tedium | 词性：名词 (罕用)


词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French entretenir, from entre- ‘between, among’ + tenir ‘to hold’.
