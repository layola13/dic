outsinging: /ˌaʊtˈsɪŋɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的歌声，以歌声压倒
outperforming someone in singing.
She outsang all the other contestants in the competition. 她以歌声压倒了比赛中的所有其他选手。
outsinging
verb

synonyms: surpass in singing, outdo in singing
antonyms: be outsang, sing worse than
