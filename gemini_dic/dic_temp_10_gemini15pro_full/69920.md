fervent:/ˈfɜːrvənt/ (US) /ˈfɜːvənt/ (UK)| adj.|热烈的，热情的，强烈的|Having or displaying a passionate intensity.|She has a fervent belief in the power of education. 她对教育的力量有着热烈的信念。|例句：He delivered a fervent speech about the need for change. 他发表了一场关于变革必要性的热情洋溢的演讲。|近义词：passionate, ardent, zealous, enthusiastic|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, lukewarm|词性:形容词
词源:From Latin *ferventem*, present participle of *fervere* "to boil, glow."
