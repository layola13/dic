outworked:/ˌaʊtˈwɜːrkt/
Verb
比...更努力工作, 工作过度
To have worked harder than someone else.  To have worked to the point of exhaustion.
She outworked everyone on the team and still got little recognition. 她比团队里的任何人都更努力工作，但仍然没有得到多少认可。
He was completely outworked and stressed. 他工作过度，压力很大。
Synonyms: outlabor, outperform, surpass
Antonyms: underperform, slack off
词性: 动词
