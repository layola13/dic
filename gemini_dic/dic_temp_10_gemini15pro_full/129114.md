nixes:/ˈnɪksiːz/
n.
水仙女；女精灵 (plural of nixie)
Mythical water spirits, often depicted as beautiful maidens who lure men to watery deaths.
The nixies of the Rhine are said to have beautiful voices.  莱茵河的水仙女据说有着美妙的歌喉。

The old stories warned of nixies who would drown unsuspecting travelers.  古老的故事警告人们小心水仙女，她们会淹死毫无戒心的旅行者。

近义词:sprites, fairies, mermaids, sirens
反义词:(No direct antonym for a mythological creature)
词性:noun (plural)
