carvone:/ˈkɑːrvəʊn/ (UK) /ˈkɑːrvoʊn/ (US)| n. |香芹酮；葛缕酮|A colorless or pale yellow liquid ketone, C₁₀H₁₄O, found in the oils of caraway, dill, and spearmint and used in flavorings.|Carvone is responsible for the characteristic aroma of spearmint. 香芹酮是留兰香特有香味的来源。|Synonyms:  (R)-(-)-carvone (for spearmint aroma), (S)-(+)-carvone (for caraway aroma),  p-mentha-6,8-dien-2-one|Antonyms: None (as it's a specific chemical compound)|noun
