gazumpers:/ɡəˈzʌmpərz/
| n. |抬价者；敲竹杠者|Someone who gazumps another person, typically a prospective buyer of property.|The gazumpers offered £10,000 more than we did, and the seller accepted their offer.|近义词：outbidder, profiteer|反义词:  underbidder|noun


gazumper:/ɡəˈzʌmpər/
| n. |抬价者；敲竹杠者|Someone who gazumps another person, typically a prospective buyer of property.|The gazumper swooped in at the last minute with a higher offer.|近义词：outbidder, profiteer|反义词:  underbidder|noun


gazump:/ɡəˈzʌmp/
| vt. |抬高要价；敲竹杠|To raise the price of something (typically property) after agreeing on a lower price with another buyer.|We were gazumped just days before we were due to exchange contracts.|近义词：outbid, overbid|反义词: underbid|verb
