bakemeat:/ˈbeɪkmiːt/
noun
烤肉；糕点；(尤指)馅饼
Food (especially meat or pies) that is for baking or has been baked.
The delightful aroma of bakemeat wafted from the kitchen.  厨房里飘来烤肉的香味。
