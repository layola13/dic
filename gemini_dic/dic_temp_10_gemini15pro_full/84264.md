half-successful:/ˌhæf səkˈsɛsfəl/
adj.
部分成功的，不太成功的
Partially successful; not completely successful.
The project was half-successful, meeting some but not all of its goals.  这个项目只取得了部分成功，实现了一些但并非全部的目标。
The marketing campaign was half-successful; sales increased, but not as much as projected.  营销活动不太成功；销售额有所增长，但没有达到预期。
近义词:partially successful, moderately successful, somewhat successful
反义词:successful, completely successful, highly successful
词性:形容词
