词源:
From Late Middle English *induen, from Old French enduire (“to coat, cover, smear”), from Latin inducere, from in- (“in, on, upon”) + dūcere (“to lead, bring”).  The meaning shifted from the literal "to put on" to the more figurative "to invest with qualities."
