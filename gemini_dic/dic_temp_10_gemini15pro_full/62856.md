enjoyably:/ɪnˈdʒɔɪəbli/| adv.| 愉快地，令人愉快地|In a pleasing or enjoyable manner.|She spent the afternoon enjoyably reading in the garden. 她愉快地在花园里度过了一个下午，看书。|近义词：pleasantly, delightfully, agreeably|反义词：unpleasantly, disagreeably|词性:副词
Etymology: From enjoyable + -ly.  "Enjoyable" comes from the verb "enjoy," which originates from Old French "enjoir" (to give joy to, rejoice), from "en-" (in) + "joir" (to rejoice), ultimately from Latin "gaudia" (joy).
