civilnesses: /ˈsɪvɪlnəsɪz/
|n.| 礼貌；客气；殷勤|Plural form of civility.  The state or quality of being polite; courteous behavior. |She treated everyone with the utmost civilnesses. 她对每个人都彬彬有礼。|近义词: courtesies, politenesses, civilities|反义词: rudeness, impoliteness, incivility|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *civilete*, borrowed from Old French *civileté*, from Latin *cīvīlitās*, from *cīvīlis* (“civil”), from *cīvis* (“citizen”).
