closeted: /ˈklɒzɪtɪd/ (UK) /ˈklɑːzɪtɪd/ (US)
adj.
隐瞒的；不愿公开的（尤指同性恋）
Concealed, especially in relation to homosexuality; not open or declared.
例句：He was a closeted gay man for many years. 他多年来一直隐瞒自己的同性恋身份。

例句：She lived a closeted life, afraid to reveal her true feelings. 她过着隐秘的生活，不敢表露自己的真实感受。

近义词：secret, hidden, private, concealed
反义词：open, out, public
词性: adjective
