fanatical:/fəˈnætɪkl/ | adj. | 狂热的，入迷的 | Filled with excessive and single-minded zeal. | He's a fanatical supporter of the local football team. 他是当地足球队的狂热支持者。

fanatical:/fəˈnætɪkl/ | adj. | 狂热的，入迷的 | Marked by excessive enthusiasm and often intense uncritical devotion. | She's fanatical about keeping her house clean. 她对保持房子清洁非常狂热。

近义词:obsessive, zealous, fervent, extremist | 反义词:moderate, temperate, indifferent, apathetic | 词性:adjective


词源: Late 16th century: from fanatic + -al.
