anoints:/əˈnɔɪnts/| vt.|涂抹圣油；施以涂油礼；选定；指定|to put oil on (someone) as part of a religious ceremony; to choose someone for a special job or position|The priest anointed the new king.  牧师为新国王涂抹圣油。

The board anointed her as the new CEO. 董事会指定她为新的首席执行官。|近义词：bless, consecrate, ordain, designate, appoint|反义词:curse, condemn|词性:动词
词源:From Old French enoint "smear with oil, anoint," from Vulgar Latin *inointare, from Latin in- "in" + ungere "to anoint."
