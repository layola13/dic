engagements:/ɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənts/ /ɛnˈɡeɪdʒmənts/
n.
约定, 责任, 婚约
An agreement to do something or go somewhere at a fixed time.  A formal agreement to get married.  The act of engaging or being engaged.

例句：
I have several engagements this week.  我这周有几个约会。

She announced her engagement to her family. 她向家人宣布了订婚的消息。

近义词：appointments, commitments, promises, betrothal (for marriage)
反义词： cancellations, freedom (from obligations)
词性：名词
