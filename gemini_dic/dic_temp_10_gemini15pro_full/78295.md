germinations:/ˌdʒɜːrmɪˈneɪʃənz/
|n.|发芽，萌芽；发生，发展|The process of a seed or spore beginning to grow and put out shoots after a period of dormancy.  Also, the beginning or development of something.|The warm spring rains encouraged the germinations of many seeds.|Synonyms: sprouting, beginnings, origins, inception, development|Antonyms: decay, death, dormancy|noun
