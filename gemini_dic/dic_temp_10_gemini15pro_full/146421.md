overfoolishness: /ˌoʊvərˈfuːlɪʃnəs/
noun
过度愚蠢，极端的愚蠢
The state or quality of being excessively foolish.
His overfoolishness led him to make a series of bad decisions. 他的过度愚蠢导致他做出了一系列糟糕的决定。
Overfoolishness caused him to lose all his savings. 过度的愚蠢让他失去了所有的积蓄。

Synonyms: stupidity, folly, idiocy, absurdity, silliness
Antonyms: wisdom, prudence, sagacity, intelligence, sense
词性: 名词
