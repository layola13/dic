iceblocks:/ˈaɪs blɒks/ (UK) /ˈaɪs blɑːks/ (US)| n.|冰块，冰棍|Frozen blocks of flavored water, typically eaten as a treat, especially in Australia and New Zealand.  Essentially a popsicle.|例句：The children cooled down with iceblocks on the hot summer day. 孩子们在炎热的夏日吃着冰棍解暑。

例句：We always have a box of iceblocks in the freezer during summer.  夏天我们冰箱里总有一盒冰棍。|近义词：popsicles, ice lollies (UK), freezer pops (US)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps hot drinks like hot chocolate)|词性:noun
