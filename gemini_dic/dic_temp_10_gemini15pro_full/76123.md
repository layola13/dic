furuncle:/ˈfjʊrʌŋkəl/ (US), /ˈfjʊərʌŋkəl/ (UK)| n. |疖子，疖|A painful, infected swelling of a hair follicle, typically caused by a staphylococcal infection.  A boil.|  He had a painful furuncle on his neck. 他脖子上长了一个疼的疖子。|Synonyms: boil, abscess|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, consider healthy skin)|noun
