nonvigilantness:/nɒnˈvɪdʒɪləntnəs/
noun
缺乏警觉性,疏忽
lack of vigilance; the state of not being watchful or alert.
The nonvigilantness of the security guard allowed the thief to slip past unnoticed. 保安的疏忽让小偷溜了过去。
The accident was caused by the driver's nonvigilantness. 这起事故是由驾驶员的疏忽造成的。
synonyms: inattentiveness, carelessness, negligence, heedlessness, disregard
antonyms: vigilance, alertness, watchfulness, carefulness, attentiveness
