mispackaging:/ˌmɪsˈpækɪdʒɪŋ/
n.
错误包装，包装不当
The act or process of packaging something incorrectly.
The mispackaging of the product led to customer complaints. 产品的包装错误导致了客户投诉。

mispackage:/ˌmɪsˈpækɪdʒ/
v.
错误地包装
To package something incorrectly.
The factory mispackaged several items, leading to returns. 工厂错误地包装了几个物品，导致了退货。
