dearnesses:/ˈdɪərnɪsɪz/
n.
高物价；昂贵
high prices; expensiveness
The dearnesses of basic commodities made life difficult for many.  基本商品的高物价让许多人的生活变得困难。
The war caused terrible dearnesses. 战争导致了可怕的物价飞涨。
近义词: expensiveness, high cost, high prices
反义词: cheapness, affordability, low cost
词性: 名词
