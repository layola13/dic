oversalt:/ˌoʊvərˈsɔːlt/ /ˌəʊvəˈsɔːlt/ | vt. n. | 加太多盐；过咸 | To add too much salt to (food).  As a noun, it refers to the act of adding too much salt, or the state of being over-salted. | I oversalted the soup and it was inedible. 我把汤加了太多盐，没法吃了。

The oversalt ruined the dish.  过多的盐毁了这道菜。 | 近义词：season excessively, overseason | 反义词：underseason | 词性: verb, noun

oversalted:/ˌoʊvərˈsɔːltɪd/ /ˌəʊvəˈsɔːltɪd/ | adj. | 太咸的 | Containing too much salt. | The steak was oversalted. 牛排太咸了。

The oversalted fries were disappointing. 太咸的薯条令人失望。 | 近义词：too salty, excessively seasoned | 反义词：underseasoned, bland | 词性: adjective
