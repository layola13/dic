anglophile:/ˈæŋɡləfaɪl/ | n. | 亲英派；崇英者 | A person who greatly admires or favors England and its people and customs. |  She's a confirmed anglophile who vacations in Britain every year. 她是一位坚定的亲英派，每年都会去英国度假。

| 近义词: admirer of England, devotee of Britain | 反义词: Anglophobe | 词性: noun


anglophile:/ˈæŋɡləfaɪl/ | adj. | 亲英的；崇英的 | Having or showing a strong affinity for England and its culture. | Her anglophile tendencies were evident in her love of British literature.  她对英国文学的热爱显现出她亲英的倾向。 | 近义词: pro-English, Britophilic | 反义词: Anglophobic | 词性: adjective
