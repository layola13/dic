athletically:/æθˈlɛtɪkli/
adverb
运动地，强健地
In a manner characteristic of athletes; with skill, strength, and agility.
She moved athletically across the field. 她轻盈矫健地穿过田野。
近义词:strongly, powerfully, vigorously, nimbly, agilely
反义词:clumsily, awkwardly, weakly
词性:adverb

athletically:/æθˈlɛtɪkli/
adverb
体育竞技般地
In a way that relates to athletic activities or sports.
He was athletically gifted from a young age. 他从小就展现出运动天赋。
近义词:sportingly, competitively
反义词:unathletic, nonathletic
词性:adverb
