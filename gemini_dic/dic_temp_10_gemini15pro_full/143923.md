outpromise:/ˌaʊtˈprɑːmɪs/
v.
胜过承诺；做出比…更慷慨的承诺
To exceed in promising; to make a more generous promise than.
He outpromised his rival in the election campaign, pledging lower taxes and better healthcare.  他在竞选活动中做出了比对手更慷慨的承诺，保证降低税收和改善医疗保健。
The company outpromised its competitors by offering a lifetime warranty. 该公司通过提供终身保修，做出了比竞争对手更慷慨的承诺。
近义词: outbid, surpass, exceed
反义词: underpromise
词性: 动词
