exhaustive:/ɪɡˈzɔːstɪv/ | adj. | 彻底的，详尽的，全面的 |  Leaving no possibilities unexplored; thorough.  |  The police conducted an exhaustive search of the area. 警方对该地区进行了彻底的搜查。 |  After an exhaustive study, they finally found the solution.  经过详尽的研究，他们终于找到了解决方案。 | thorough, comprehensive, complete, in-depth | superficial, cursory, incomplete | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *exhaustīvus, from Latin exhaustus (past participle of exhaurire “to drain out, exhaust”), from ex- “out” + haurire “to draw, drain”.
