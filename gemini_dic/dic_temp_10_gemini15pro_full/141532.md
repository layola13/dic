opponent:/əˈpəʊnənt/ (British) /əˈpoʊnənt/ (American)| n.|对手，反对者|Someone who competes against another person in a contest, game, or argument; someone who disagrees with or resists a proposal or plan.|例句：He easily defeated his opponent in the chess match. 他在象棋比赛中轻松击败了对手。|近义词：rival, adversary, competitor, antagonist|反义词：ally, supporter, proponent|词性：名词

opponent:| n.|对手，反对者|Someone who competes against another person in a contest, game, or argument; someone who disagrees with or resists a proposal or plan.|例句：She respects her opponents even though they have differing viewpoints. 即使观点不同，她也尊重她的对手。|近义词：rival, adversary, competitor, antagonist|反义词：ally, supporter, proponent|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *oponant*, present participle of oponer "to oppose," from Latin oppōnens, present participle of oppōnere "to set against, oppose," from ob "against" + pōnere "to put, place."
