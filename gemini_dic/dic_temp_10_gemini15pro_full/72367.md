flown-out:/ˈfloʊn aʊt/
adjective
已飞出的，已撤离的
Having been transported by aircraft, especially after an emergency or for a specific purpose.  Relates to people or goods.
The flown-out refugees were housed in temporary shelters.  已飞出的难民被安置在临时住所。
The medical team was flown out to the disaster zone.  医疗队被空运到灾区。
近义词:evacuated, airlifted
反义词:grounded, stranded
词性:adjective

fly:/flaɪ/
verb
飞，飞行
From Old English *flēogan, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch vliegen and German fliegen.
The birds fly south for the winter. 鸟儿们冬天飞往南方。
He flew to London for a business meeting. 他飞往伦敦参加商务会议。
近义词:soar, wing
反义词:land, descend
词性:verb
