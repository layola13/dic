fictionalising:/ˈfɪkʃənəlaɪzɪŋ/ |v. |使小说化；虚构化|The author fictionalized his own experiences to create a compelling narrative.  作者将自己的经历小说化，创作了一个引人入胜的故事。|例句：He was accused of fictionalising his war experiences.  他被指控虚构了他的战争经历。

例句：The movie fictionalises the events that led to the war.  这部电影将导致战争的事件虚构化了。

近义词：inventing, fabricating, fictionalizing, novelizing|反义词：factualizing, documenting |词性：动词(present participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From fictionalize + -ing.  Fictionalize comes from fiction + -ize. Fiction ultimately derives from the Latin word "fictio," meaning "a shaping, a feigning."
