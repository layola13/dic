existentialisms: /ˌɛɡzɪˈstɛnʃəlɪzəmz/
n.
存在主义（哲学流派）
a philosophical theory or approach that emphasizes the existence of the individual person as a free and responsible agent determining their own development through acts of the will.
Example: Existentialisms emphasizes individual freedom and responsibility. 存在主义强调个体自由和责任。

Example:  Many 20th-century writers explored themes of existentialisms in their works. 许多20世纪的作家在他们的作品中探讨了存在主义的主题。

近义词:  existentialism,  absurdism
反义词:  essentialism, determinism
词性: noun
