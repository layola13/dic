nonadjudicated:/ˌnɒnəˈdʒuːdɪkeɪtɪd/
adjective
未经裁决的；未被判决的
Not having been formally judged or decided upon in a court of law.
The case remained nonadjudicated for several months due to a backlog in the court system.  由于法院系统积压，该案件几个月来一直未得到判决。
The nonadjudicated claim was finally settled out of court. 这项未经裁决的索赔最终在庭外和解了。
Synonyms: unsettled, unresolved, pending, undecided, undetermined
Antonyms: adjudicated, decided, settled, resolved, determined
词性: 形容词
