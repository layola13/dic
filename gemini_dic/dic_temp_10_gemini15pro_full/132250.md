nondeclarations:/ˌnɒnˌdɛkləˈreɪʃənz/| n.|未申报，未声明|Absence or lack of declarations; items or information that have not been formally declared.  This often pertains to customs declarations, tax declarations, or other official statements of information.|The customs officer seized the smuggled goods due to the nondeclarations on the traveler's forms. 海关官员因旅客表格上未申报而查获了走私货物。|The company was fined for nondeclarations of certain income streams.  该公司因未申报某些收入来源而被罚款。|近义词：nondisclosure, omission|反义词：declaration, disclosure|词性:noun
