menage:/ˈmeɪnɑːʒ/  /məˈnɑːʒ/| n.|家庭，一家人；家务；(尤指) ménage à trois 三角家庭|A menage is a household.  More specifically, it can refer to a ménage à trois, a domestic arrangement in which three people live together in a sexual or romantic relationship.|She ran a busy menage with four children and a dog.|近义词：household, family, domestic arrangement|反义词:|noun
