iambic:/aɪˈæmbɪk/ | adj. n.|抑扬格的，抑扬格|Of or using iambs.  A metrical foot consisting of one short (or unstressed) syllable followed by one long (or stressed) syllable.|Shakespeare’s sonnets are written in iambic pentameter.  莎士比亚的十四行诗是用五步抑扬格写的。|The poem’s rhythm is iambic. 这首诗的韵律是抑扬格。|近义词:metrical, rhythmic|反义词:trochaic|词性:形容词，名词

Etymology:  From Late Latin *iambicus, from Latin iambus, from Greek iambos.
