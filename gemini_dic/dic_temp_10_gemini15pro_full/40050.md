The word "consumptioner" is not a standard English word.  It appears to be a misspelling of "consumer."  Therefore, the following analysis is for the word "consumer."


consumer: /kənˈsuːmər/ (US) /kənˈsjuːmə(r)/ (UK)| n. |消费者；用户；消耗者|A person who purchases goods and services for personal use.| Example: The consumer is always right. 消费者永远是对的。

Example: Consumers are demanding more fuel-efficient cars. 消费者要求更高效的汽车。


|近义词: customer, buyer, purchaser, shopper, user, end-user|反义词: producer, manufacturer, supplier, provider|词性: noun|
