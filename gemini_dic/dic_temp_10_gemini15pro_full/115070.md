manes:/meɪnz/ | n. | 鬼魂；亡灵；（尤指马的）鬃毛 | The spirits of the dead, especially those of Roman household gods. Also, the long hair growing on the neck of a horse or lion. | The ancient Romans believed that the manes of their ancestors could influence their lives. 古罗马人相信他们祖先的亡灵会影响他们的生活。|  The horse tossed its manes proudly. 这匹马骄傲地甩动着它的鬃毛。 | synonyms: spirits, ghosts, shades (for spirits); mane, crest (for hair) | antonyms:  (no direct antonym for spirits);  (no direct antonym for hair) | noun
