fend:/fɛnd/
verb
抵挡，抵御，照顾自己
to defend oneself from difficulty or danger
He had to fend for himself from an early age. 他从小就得自己照顾自己。
近义词: defend, protect, guard, look after, take care of
反义词: succumb, surrender
词性: verb

fend off:/fɛnd ɔːf/
verb
挡开，避开
to repel or ward off
She managed to fend off the attacker with a stick. 她用一根棍子成功地挡开了攻击者。
近义词: repel, ward off, parry, deflect
反义词: attract, invite
词性: verb
