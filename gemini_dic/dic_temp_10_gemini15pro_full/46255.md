cytology: /saɪˈtɑːlədʒi/ /saɪˈtɒlədʒi/
noun
细胞学
The study of cells, their structure, function, and pathology.
Cytology plays a crucial role in diagnosing various diseases. 细胞学在诊断各种疾病中起着至关重要的作用。
Cytological examination of the tissue sample revealed cancerous cells.  组织样本的细胞学检查发现了癌细胞。

近义词: cell biology
反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider "histology" which focuses on tissues)
词性: noun
