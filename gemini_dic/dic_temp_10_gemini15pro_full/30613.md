cerebritis: /ˌsɛrɪˈbraɪtɪs/
n.
脑炎
Inflammation of the cerebrum, the largest part of the brain.
Cerebritis can be caused by bacterial or viral infections. 脑炎可以由细菌或病毒感染引起。
The patient presented with symptoms suggestive of cerebritis.  这位病人表现出的症状提示脑炎。
