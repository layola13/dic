beeline:/ˈbiːlaɪn/| n.|直线，捷径；直奔|A straight line or direct route to something. Also used figuratively to mean going directly and quickly to something or someone.|He made a beeline for the refrigerator after his workout.  他锻炼完后直奔冰箱。|synonyms: shortcut, direct route, bee line|antonyms: detour, indirect route|noun

beeline:/ˈbiːlaɪn/| v.|直奔，径直前往|To go directly and quickly towards something or someone.|She beelined it across the room to grab the last cookie. 她径直穿过房间去拿最后一块饼干。|synonyms: rush, dash, hurry, bolt|antonyms: meander, wander, stroll|verb
