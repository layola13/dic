hyperintellectual:/ˌhaɪpərˌɪntəˈlɛktʃʊəl/ | adj. | 过度理智的，过分沉迷于智力活动的 | Excessively intellectual; overly concerned with intellectual matters. | He was seen as a hyperintellectual, more interested in abstract theories than real-world problems. 他被认为是一个过度理智的人，对抽象理论比对现实世界的问题更感兴趣。 |  She buried herself in books, becoming increasingly hyperintellectual and detached from her friends. 她埋首于书本之中，变得越来越过度理智，与朋友们疏远了。| 近义词: cerebral, intellectual, highbrow, egghead | 反义词: unintellectual, simple, philistine | 词性: adjective
