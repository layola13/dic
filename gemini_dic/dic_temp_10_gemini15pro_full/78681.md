gifting:/ˈɡɪftɪŋ/| n.|馈赠，送礼|The act of giving gifts, especially as a profession or business.  It also refers to the talent or skill in choosing and giving appropriate gifts.|例句：Corporate gifting is a common practice during the holidays.  企业馈赠是节假日期间的常见做法。|例句：She has a real knack for gifting; she always finds the perfect present. 她真的很有送礼的诀窍；她总能找到最完美的礼物。|近义词：presenting, giving, bestowing|反义词:receiving, accepting|词性:noun
