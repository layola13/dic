dispassioned:/dɪsˈpæʃənd/ | adj. | 不带感情的；冷静的；客观的 | Not influenced by strong emotion, and therefore able to think clearly and make good decisions. |  The judge delivered a dispassioned verdict. 法官作出了冷静的判决。

The historian gave a dispassioned account of the war. 历史学家对这场战争作出了客观的描述。

| 近义词: unemotional, objective, impartial, calm, cool, detached, impersonal | 反义词: passionate, emotional, biased, prejudiced | 词性: adjective


词源:

Late 16th century: from dis- (expressing negation) + passionate.
