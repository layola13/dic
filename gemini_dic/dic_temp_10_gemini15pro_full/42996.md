cow:/kaʊ/| n.|奶牛，母牛|A fully grown female animal of a domesticated breed of ox, kept to produce milk or beef.|The farmer milked the cow every morning. 农民每天早上给奶牛挤奶。|近义词:heifer (小母牛), bovine (牛科动物)|反义词:bull (公牛)|词性:名词

cow:/kaʊ/| vt.|恐吓，吓唬|To frighten or subdue by threats or a show of force.|He tried to cow his opponents into submission. 他试图恐吓对手屈服。|近义词:intimidate, bully, frighten|反义词:embolden, encourage|词性:动词
