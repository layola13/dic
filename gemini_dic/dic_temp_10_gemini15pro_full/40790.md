convocate: /ˈkɒnvəkeɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnvəkeɪt/ (US)
|v.|召集，召开|To call together, summon, especially for a formal meeting.|The president convocated Congress for a special session. 总统召开国会特别会议。|近义词: assemble, convene, summon|反义词: adjourn, dismiss|verb

convocation: /ˌkɒnvəˈkeɪʃn/ (UK) /ˌkɑːnvəˈkeɪʃn/ (US)
|n.|召集，会议|The act of convoking or calling together, or the assembly so convened.|The university convocation was held in the grand hall. 大学的毕业典礼在大厅举行。|近义词: assembly, gathering, meeting|反义词: dispersal, adjournment|noun
