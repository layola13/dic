nitrobenzol:/ˌnaɪtroʊˈbɛnzɒl/ |n.|硝基苯|A pale yellow liquid with an almond-like odor, made by nitrating benzene and used in the manufacture of aniline and other organic chemicals.|Nitrobenzol is used in the production of aniline. 硝基苯用于苯胺的生产。|Synonyms: nitrobenzene, oil of mirbane|Antonyms: None|noun
