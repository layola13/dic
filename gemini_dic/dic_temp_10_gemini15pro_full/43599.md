creaminess:/ˈkriːminəs/| n.| 奶油状; 浓厚; 滑腻; |The creaminess of the sauce was perfect. 酱汁的浓稠度恰到好处。|近义词：smoothness, richness, thickness|反义词:thinness, wateriness|noun

Etymology->
From creamy + -ness.  Creamy itself comes from cream, which ultimately derives from the Old French "cresme," meaning cream. The suffix "-ness" is a common English suffix used to form nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
