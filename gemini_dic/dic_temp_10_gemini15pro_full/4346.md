akinetic:/ˌeɪkaɪˈnɛtɪk/ /ˌeɪkɪˈnɛtɪk/
adjective
运动不能的，运动迟缓的
Relating to or characterized by akinesia; lacking voluntary movement.
例句：The patient presented with akinetic mutism, unable to speak or move.  病人表现为运动不能性缄默症，无法说话或移动。

例句：Akinetic seizures can cause a sudden, brief loss of muscle tone. 运动不能性癫痫发作会导致肌肉张力突然短暂丧失。
近义词：motionless, immobile, still, static
反义词：kinetic, mobile, active
词性：adjective
