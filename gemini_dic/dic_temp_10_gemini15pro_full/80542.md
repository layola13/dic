goodly:/ˈɡʊdli/| adj.|美好的，漂亮的；相当大的，数量可观的|Pleasing; of a considerable size, extent, or amount.|She inherited a goodly sum of money. 她继承了一大笔钱。|近义词：substantial, considerable, sizable, handsome, pleasant, agreeable|反义词：meager, scanty, paltry, insignificant, unpleasant|词性:形容词
词源：From Middle English *goodly*, from Old English *gōdlīc* (“goodly, beautiful, excellent, virtuous, kind, noble, gentle”), equivalent to *good* +‎ *-ly*. Cognate with Scots *guidlie* (“goodly”), West Frisian *goedlik* (“goodly, benevolent, kind”), Dutch *goedelijk* (“charitable”), German *gütig* (“kind, benevolent”). More at good, -ly.
