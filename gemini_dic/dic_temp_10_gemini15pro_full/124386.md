词源:  From Middle English *motteley, from Old French mattelé (“curdled”), from mattel (“clot of milk”), from Vulgar Latin *mattea, from Latin materia (“matter, timber”).  The sense of "parti-colored cloth" arose from the appearance of curdled milk.
