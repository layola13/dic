noncharacteristic:/ˌnɒnkærɪktəˈrɪstɪk/
adjective
非典型的，不具特色的，异常的
Not typical or representative of a particular person or thing; unusual or atypical.
His outburst was completely noncharacteristic; he's usually very calm. 他的爆发完全不典型；他通常很平静。
近义词: atypical, unusual, uncharacteristic, abnormal, irregular
反义词: characteristic, typical, normal, usual, regular
词性: adjective

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + characteristic
