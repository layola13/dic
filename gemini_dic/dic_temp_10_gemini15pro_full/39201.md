congeries:/ˈkɑːndʒəriːz/ /ˈkɒndʒəriːz/
n.
堆积，聚集，混合物
a collection of things or parts in one mass; a disorderly heap; an aggregation
The flea market was a congeries of secondhand goods, from antique furniture to vintage clothing.  这个跳蚤市场是二手商品的聚集地，从古董家具到复古服装，应有尽有。
The report was a congeries of unrelated facts and figures. 这份报告是互不相关的零散事实和数字的堆积。
近义词: aggregation, collection, accumulation, assemblage, mass, heap, pile
反义词: scattering, dispersion, separation
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *congeries*, from congerō ("heap together, collect, compile"), from com- ("together") + gerō ("carry, bear").
