auripigment:/ɔːˈrɪpɪɡmənt/
n.
雌黄；鸡冠石
A bright yellow mineral, arsenic trisulfide (As₂S₃), used as a pigment.
The artist used auripigment to create a vibrant yellow in the painting.  这位艺术家在画中使用了雌黄来创造鲜艳的黄色。
Synonyms: orpiment, arsenic yellow, King's yellow
