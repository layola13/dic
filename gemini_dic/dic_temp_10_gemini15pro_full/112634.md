low-ranking:/ˌloʊˈræŋkɪŋ/
adjective
低级别的，地位低的
holding a position of low importance or status within an organization or society
He was just a low-ranking officer in the army. 他只是军队里一个低级别的军官。
Near synonyms: subordinate, junior, inferior
Antonyms: high-ranking, senior, superior
词性: 形容词
