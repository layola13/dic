finchlike:/ˈfɪntʃlaɪk/
adjective

The small, finchlike bird flitted among the branches.  这只小小的、像雀一样的鸟在树枝间飞来飞去。
The bird's finchlike song was pleasant to hear.  这只鸟像雀一样的歌声悦耳动听。
