charlatanic:/ˌʃɑːrləˈtænɪk/ | adj. | 江湖骗子的；装神弄鬼的；招摇撞骗的 | Relating to or characteristic of a charlatan; falsely claiming to have special knowledge or skill. |  He made charlatanic claims about his ability to cure cancer.  他吹嘘自己能够治愈癌症，纯属江湖骗子。 | synonyms: fraudulent, deceptive, quack, bogus, phony, sham | antonyms: genuine, authentic, real, honest | adjective

charlatan:/ˈʃɑːrlətən/ | n. | 江湖郎中；骗子 | A person falsely claiming to have a special knowledge or skill; a fraud. | The charlatan sold fake cures to desperate people.  这个骗子把假药卖给绝望的人们。 | synonyms: fraud, imposter, quack, fake | antonyms: expert, professional, authority | noun
