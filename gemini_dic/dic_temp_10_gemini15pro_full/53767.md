discomforting:/dɪsˈkʌmfərtɪŋ/
adjective
令人不安的；使人难受的
Causing anxiety, uneasiness, or embarrassment.
The silence after his joke was disconcerting. 他讲完笑话后的沉默令人尴尬。
近义词: unsettling, disturbing, unnerving, troubling
反义词: comforting, reassuring, soothing
词性: adjective

词源:
From discomfort + -ing.  Discomfort itself comes from the Old French desconfort, meaning "lack of ease or solace," from des- "not" + confort "comfort."
