adducer: /əˈdʌktər/
noun
内收肌
A muscle that draws a part of the body toward the median line.
The adductor muscles in the thigh bring the legs together. 大腿内收肌使双腿并拢。

The adductor muscles of the hip are responsible for moving the leg inwards. 髋部的内收肌负责向内移动腿部。

近义词: adductor muscle, abductor muscle
反义词: abductor
词性: n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adducere*, from ad- (“to, towards”) + ducere (“to lead, draw”).

USAGE->
The adductor longus is one of the adductor muscles of the hip. 内收长肌是髋部内收肌之一。

Physical therapy can help strengthen weak adductor muscles. 物理治疗可以帮助加强虚弱的内收肌。

PHRASE->
adductor muscles  内收肌
adductor longus 内收长肌
adductor brevis 内收短肌
adductor magnus 内收大肌
hip adductors 髋内收肌
