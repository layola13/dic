fossilizable:/ˈfɒsəlaɪzəbl/ /ˈfɑːsəlaɪzəbl/| adj.|可化石化的，能变成化石的|Capable of being fossilized; able to be preserved as a fossil. |The hard parts of the organism are fossilizable.  该生物体的坚硬部分是可以化石化的。|  Trilobites are highly fossilizable.  三叶虫很容易形成化石。|近义词：preservable|反义词:unfossilizable|词性:adjective

fossilize:/ˈfɒsəlaɪz/ /ˈfɑːsəlaɪz/| v. |使成化石，石化；使…僵化|To convert into a fossil; to preserve an organism as a fossil.  To make or become rigid or fixed.|The dinosaur bones were fossilized over millions of years. 恐龙骨骼经过数百万年变成了化石。|  His thinking has fossilized. 他的思想僵化了。|近义词：petrify, harden|反义词:decay, decompose|词性:verb
