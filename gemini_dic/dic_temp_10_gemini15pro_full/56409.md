dolichocephalous:/ˌdɒlɪkəˈsɛfələs/ /ˌdɑːlɪkoʊˈsɛfələs/| adj.|长头型的|Having a long skull, characterized by a cephalic index of less than 75.  This means the skull's breadth is less than 75% of its length.|The archaeologist discovered a dolichocephalous skull dating back to the Bronze Age. 考古学家发现了一个可以追溯到青铜时代的长头骨。|The study examined the prevalence of dolichocephalous head shapes in ancient Egyptian populations. 这项研究调查了古埃及人口中长头型的流行程度。|long-headed, elongated|brachycephalous|adjective
