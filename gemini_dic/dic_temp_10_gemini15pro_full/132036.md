noncorrosively:/ˌnɒnkəˈrəʊsɪvli/
adv.
无腐蚀性地
in a way that does not cause corrosion
The metal was treated noncorrosively to prevent rust.  这种金属经过了无腐蚀性处理以防止生锈。
The lubricant is applied noncorrosively.  润滑剂以无腐蚀性的方式涂抹。
近义词: protectively, safely
反义词: corrosively
词性: adverb

noncorrosive:/ˌnɒnkəˈrəʊsɪv/
adj.
无腐蚀性的；不腐蚀的
not causing corrosion
This cleaner is noncorrosive and safe for most surfaces.  这种清洁剂无腐蚀性，对大多数表面安全。
A noncorrosive material is essential for this application. 对于这个应用来说，无腐蚀性材料是必不可少的。
近义词: non-corroding, inert
反义词: corrosive
词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix): not
corrosive (adjective): tending to cause corrosion
-ly (suffix): forming adverbs from adjectives
