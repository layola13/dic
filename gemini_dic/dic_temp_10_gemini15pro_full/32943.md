choppering:/ˈtʃɒpərɪŋ/ | n. | 切碎，砍，直升机飞行 | The act of chopping something into small pieces, or the sound of a helicopter's rotors. |  The choppering of the vegetables was rhythmic and soothing.  菜刀切碎蔬菜的声音很有节奏，令人舒缓。| The sound of choppering blades filled the air. 直升机桨叶的轰鸣声充满了空中。 | synonyms: chopping, mincing, dicing, whirring,  | antonyms:  assembling, combining, constructing | noun

choppering:/ˈtʃɒpərɪŋ/ | adj. |  (非正式)寒冷刺骨的 | (Informal)  Bitterly cold. | It was a choppering January day.  一月的天气寒冷刺骨。| The wind was choppering, and I was glad to be inside.  风刮得刺骨的冷，我很庆幸待在室内。 | synonyms: freezing, frigid, icy, biting | antonyms: warm, balmy, mild | adjective
