deism:/ˈdiːɪzəm/ /ˈdeɪɪzəm/| n. |自然神论；理神论|The belief in a God who created the universe but has since remained uninvolved in it.  相信上帝创造了宇宙，但此后不再介入其中。|例句：Deism was a popular belief among intellectuals in the 18th century. 18世纪，自然神论在知识分子中很流行。

|例句：He subscribed to the principles of deism, rejecting organized religion. 他赞同自然神论的原则，反对有组织的宗教。|近义词：rationalism, freethinking|反义词:theism, fideism|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *déisme*, from Latin *deus* ("god").  源自法语 *déisme*，来自拉丁语 *deus*（“神”）。
