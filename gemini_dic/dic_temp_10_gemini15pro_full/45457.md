curating:/ˈkjʊəreɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 策展，管理；精选 | The act of selecting, organizing, and presenting content, often with the goal of creating a meaningful experience or narrative. |  She's curating an exhibition of modern art. 她正在策划一个现代艺术展。 |  He's curating a playlist for the party. 他正在为派对准备一个播放列表。 |  organizing, compiling, assembling, selecting | neglecting, ignoring | noun

curating:/ˈkjʊəreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 策展，管理；精选 |  Present participle of curate.  |  She is curating a collection of vintage photographs. 她正在整理一系列老式照片。 | He is curating a series of online lectures. 他正在策划一系列在线讲座。 | organizing, compiling, assembling, selecting | neglecting, ignoring | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From curate (v.) + -ing. Curate (v.) comes from Late Latin curatus, past participle of curare "to take care of," from cura "care."
