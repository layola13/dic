multigyrate: /ˌmʌltiˈdʒaɪreɪt/
adjective
多旋的，多轮生的
Having multiple whorls or spirals.
例句：The multigyrate shell of the ammonite was a beautiful specimen. 这只菊石的多旋壳是一个美丽的标本。

例句：Multigyrate arrangements of leaves are common in certain plant families.  某些植物科中，叶子的多轮生排列很常见。

近义词：spiral, whorled, coiled
反义词: straight


词性: adjective


词源:
From Latin *multigyratus, from multi- 'many' + gyratus 'turned, rotated', past participle of gyrare 'to turn, revolve'.  Ultimately from Greek gyros 'circle, ring'.
