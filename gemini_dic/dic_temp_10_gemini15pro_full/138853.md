nurselike: /ˈnɜːrsˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 像护士的；有护士气质的；体贴的 | Resembling or characteristic of a nurse, especially in being caring and attentive. | She had a nurselike bedside manner, always calm and reassuring. 她有一种像护士一样的 bedside manner，总是很冷静，让人安心。 | The volunteer showed a nurselike compassion for the injured animals.  这位志愿者对受伤的动物表现出了像护士一样的同情心。 | 近义词: caring, compassionate, attentive, solicitous | 反义词: uncaring, inattentive, neglectful | 词性: 形容词

词源:  nurse (n.) + -like (suffix).  "Nurse" comes from Old French "nurice" meaning "wet nurse, foster-mother," ultimately from Latin "nutrire" meaning "to nourish, suckle." "-like" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling," "characteristic of," from Old English "-lic."
