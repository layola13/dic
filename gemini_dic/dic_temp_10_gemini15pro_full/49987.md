denitrify:/diːˈnaɪtrɪfaɪ/
verb
反硝化，脱氮
To remove nitrogen from (a compound), especially from nitrates in the soil, by chemical reduction.
The bacteria in the soil denitrify the nitrates. 土壤中的细菌使硝酸盐脱氮。

近义词:reduce, break down
反义词:nitrify
词性:v.

denitrification:/diːˌnaɪtrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
noun
反硝化作用，脱氮作用
The process of denitrifying something, especially the conversion by bacteria of nitrates in the soil to free nitrogen.
Denitrification is a crucial process in the nitrogen cycle. 反硝化作用是氮循环中的一个关键过程。

近义词:nitrogen reduction
反义词:nitrification
词性:n.
