americanise:/əˈmɛrɪkənaɪz/ /əˈmɛrɪkəˌnaɪz/ |v.|使美国化|To cause something or someone to become American in character or to adopt American customs and culture.|Many immigrants have been americanised over time. 许多移民随着时间的推移已经美国化了。|The company americanised its marketing strategy to appeal to a wider audience.  这家公司将其营销策略美国化，以吸引更广泛的受众。|americanize, westernize|localize, nationalize|verb
americanisation:/əˌmɛrɪkənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /əˌmɛrɪkəˌnaɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.|美国化|The process of making something or someone American in character or causing them to adopt American customs and culture.|The americanisation of global culture is a complex phenomenon. 全球文化的美国化是一个复杂的现象。|The rapid americanisation of the food industry has led to the proliferation of fast food chains. 食品工业的迅速美国化导致了快餐连锁店的激增。|americanization, westernization|localization, nationalization|noun
