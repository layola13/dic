boffins:/ˈbɒfɪnz/ (UK) /ˈbɑːfɪnz/ (US)| n. |科学家；科研人员 (informal, often humorous) |A boffin is a scientist, especially one engaged in technological research. The word is often used humorously or slightly dismissively.| Example 1: The boffins in the lab are working on a new type of battery. 实验室里的科学家们正在研制一种新型电池。

Example 2:  Leave it to the boffins to come up with a solution.  让科学家们想出一个解决办法吧。| 近义词: scientist, researcher, technician, engineer | 反义词: layman, amateur | 词性: noun
