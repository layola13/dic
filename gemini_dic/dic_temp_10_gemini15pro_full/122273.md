mitigative:/ˈmɪtɪˌɡeɪtɪv/
adjective
减轻的，缓和的
Having the effect of making something bad less severe, serious, or painful.
The government is taking mitigative measures to address the economic crisis. 政府正在采取缓解措施来应对经济危机。
mitigating, palliative, alleviating
exacerbating, aggravating
adjective
