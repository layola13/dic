greyed:/ɡreɪd/| adj.|灰色的，灰白的；（头发）变灰的；（照片）灰暗的|Turned grayish; became gray.  Also used metaphorically to describe something that has become less vibrant or lively.|Her hair has greyed over the years. 她的头发这些年来变白了。 The old photograph had greyed with age.  这张老照片因为年代久远而褪色了。|Synonyms:  gray, grayish, grizzled, silvered, faded, dull, washed-out|Antonyms:  bright, vibrant, colorful, black, dark|adjective
