overinterests:/ˌoʊvərˈɪntrɪsts/ | n. | 过度利益，过分关注 |  Excessive or disproportionate interests, often to the detriment of other considerations.  This word isn't common and may be interpreted as simply "interests that are too strong" or as a more specific financial term relating to excessive stakes in a venture. |  His overinterests in the company led to accusations of insider trading. 他对公司的过度利益导致了对其内幕交易的指控。 |  Excessive interests, preoccupations | Lack of interest, apathy | noun
