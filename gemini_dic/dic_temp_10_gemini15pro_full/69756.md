ferial:/ˈfɪəriəl/| adj.|工作日的，节日的，世俗的|Relating to holidays or festivals, or pertaining to weekdays (as opposed to the Sabbath or Sunday).|The ferial days are marked on the calendar. 日历上标注了工作日。|The museum is open during ferial hours.  博物馆在工作日开放。|近义词：weekday, workday, holiday, festive|反义词：Sabbath, Sunday, non-working|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *feriālis*, from *feriae* "holidays".
