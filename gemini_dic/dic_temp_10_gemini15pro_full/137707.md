nonutilitarians:/ˌnɒnjuːˌtɪlɪˈteəriənz/ | n. |非功利主义者| People who do not subscribe to the philosophy of utilitarianism, which holds that the best action is the one that maximizes overall happiness or well-being. |  These nonutilitarians argue that some actions are inherently right or wrong, regardless of their consequences. 这些非功利主义者认为，有些行为本身是对的或错的，无论其后果如何。|  Non-Utilitarians prioritize individual rights and duties over maximizing overall happiness. 非功利主义者优先考虑个人权利和义务，而不是最大化整体幸福。 |  deontologists, Kantians | utilitarians | noun
