averseness: /əˈvɜːsnəs/
n.
厌恶，反感
A strong feeling of dislike or opposition.
He showed a marked averseness to hard work. 他对辛苦工作表现出明显的厌恶。
Near Synonyms: dislike, distaste, antipathy, repugnance, aversion
Antonyms: liking, fondness, inclination, preference, proclivity
noun
