effective:/ɪˈfektɪv/| adj.|有效的，生效的，起作用的|producing a decided, decisive, or desired effect|The medicine is effective in relieving pain. 这种药对缓解疼痛有效。|近义词：efficient, successful, potent, powerful|反义词：ineffective, useless, futile|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin *effectivus, from Latin effectus (“accomplishment, execution”), past participle of efficere (“to work out, accomplish, complete”), from ex- (“out”) + facere (“to make, do”).
