exhilaration:/ɪɡˌzɪləˈreɪʃn/ | n. | 兴奋， exhilaration | A feeling of great happiness and excitement. |  The roller coaster ride filled her with exhilaration. 过山车让她感到兴奋不已。

She felt a sense of exhilaration as she crossed the finish line.  她冲过终点线时感到一阵兴奋。| 近义词: elation, euphoria, joy, excitement, thrill | 反义词: boredom, apathy, depression, gloom | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin exhilaratio, from Latin exhilaratus, past participle of exhilarare (“to gladden, cheer”), from ex- (“out, upward”) + hilarare (“to make cheerful”), from hilaris (“cheerful”).
