cochairwoman: /ˌkoʊˈtʃɛrwʊmən/ | n. | 女共同主席；女联合主席 | A woman who shares the chairmanship of a committee or organization with another person. |  Ms. Smith and Ms. Jones are cochairwomen of the fundraising committee. 史密斯女士和琼斯女士是筹款委员会的联合主席。| chairperson, chair, president | vice-chair | noun

Etymology: co- (together) + chair + woman.  "Co-" comes from the Latin word "com-" meaning "together with." "Chair" refers to the position of authority, and "woman" specifies the gender.
