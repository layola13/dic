gruff:/ɡrʌf/| adj.|粗暴的；生硬的；（声音）粗哑的|Rough or surly in manner, voice, or appearance.  Often implies an abrupt and unfriendly demeanor, but not necessarily hostile.|He answered in a gruff voice. 他用粗哑的声音回答。|Synonyms:  surly, brusque, curt, abrupt, hoarse, rough|Antonyms:  gentle, polite, courteous, smooth, soft|adjective

gruffness:/ˈɡrʌfnəs/| n.|粗暴；生硬；（声音）粗哑|The quality of being gruff in manner or voice.|His gruffness hid a kind heart. 他的粗暴掩盖了一颗善良的心。|Synonyms:  surliness, brusqueness, curtness, abruptness, hoarseness, roughness|Antonyms:  gentleness, politeness, courtesy, smoothness, softness|noun
