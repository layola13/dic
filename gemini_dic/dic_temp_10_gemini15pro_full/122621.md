modesty:/ˈmɑːdəsti/ (US) /ˈmɒdɪsti/ (UK)| n.|谦虚，谦逊；端庄；朴素|The quality or state of being unassuming or moderate in the estimation of one's abilities or achievements; freedom from vanity, boastfulness, or ostentation.  Also: behavior, appearance, or clothing intended to avoid impropriety or indecency.|She accepted the award with modesty and grace. 她谦虚而优雅地接受了奖项。|synonyms: humility, unpretentiousness, diffidence, reserve, meekness|antonyms: arrogance, conceit, vanity, boastfulness, ostentation|noun
