entomostracous:/ˌɛntəˈmɒstrəkəs/ | adj. |  介形亚纲的；小型甲壳动物的 | Relating to or denoting small crustaceans, such as shrimps, crabs, barnacles, etc |  Entomostracous crustaceans are abundant in freshwater. 介形亚纲甲壳类动物在淡水中很丰富。 | 近义词: crustacean | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider large marine animals) | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Greek *entoma* "insects" + *ostrakon* "shell".
