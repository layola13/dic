impulsive:/ɪmˈpʌlsɪv/| adj.|冲动的，易冲动的|acting or done without forethought.|She made an impulsive decision to quit her job. 她一时冲动决定辞掉工作。|近义词：rash, impetuous, spontaneous|反义词：cautious, deliberate, thoughtful|词性:形容词
impulsive:/ɪmˈpʌlsɪv/| n.|冲动的人|a person who is impulsive|He's an impulsive guy and often regrets his decisions. 他是个冲动的人，经常后悔自己的决定。|近义词：hothead, firebrand|反义词：plodder|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *impulsivus, from Latin impulsus (past participle of impellere “to push, drive on”), from in- “into, in, on, upon” + pellere “to push, drive”.
