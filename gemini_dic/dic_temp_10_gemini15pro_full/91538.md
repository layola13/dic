hypatia:/haɪˈpeɪʃə/ /haɪˈpeɪtiə/
noun
希帕蒂亚(女子名)
Hypatia was a Hellenistic Neoplatonist philosopher, astronomer, and mathematician, who lived in Roman Egypt.
Hypatia lived in Alexandria, Egypt, where she taught philosophy and astronomy. 希帕蒂亚住在埃及的亚历山大，在那里她教授哲学和天文学。
Hypatia's death is often seen as marking the end of classical antiquity. 希帕蒂亚之死常被视为古典时代的终结。
Synonym: philosopher, scholar, thinker
Antonym: (No direct antonym for a person, but perhaps) ignoramus, philistine
词性: 名词
