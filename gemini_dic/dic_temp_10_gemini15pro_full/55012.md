disputative: /dɪˈspjuːtətɪv/ /dɪˈspjutətɪv/
adjective
爱争论的，好辩的
inclined to dispute or argue; argumentative
例句：He has a disputative personality and always enjoys a good debate. 他性格好辩，总是喜欢好好辩论一番。

例句：The disputative nature of the meeting made it difficult to reach a consensus.  会议上争论不休，难以达成共识。

近义词：argumentative, contentious, quarrelsome
反义词：agreeable, conciliatory, amenable
词性：adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *disputativus, from Latin disputare "to discuss, examine, argue," from dis- "apart" + putare "to think, reckon, believe."
