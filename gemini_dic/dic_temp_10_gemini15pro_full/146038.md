overdrink:/ˌoʊvərˈdrɪŋk/| v. |过度饮酒；喝过量|To consume an excessive amount of alcoholic beverages.|He overdrank at the party and felt sick the next morning. 他在派对上喝多了，第二天早上感觉不舒服。|She had overdrunk the night before and couldn't remember how she got home. 她前一天晚上喝多了，不记得自己是怎么回家的。|近义词：imbibe too much, drink excessively|反义词：drink moderately, abstain|verb
