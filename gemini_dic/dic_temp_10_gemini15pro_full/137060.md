nonstories:/ˈnɑːnˌstɔːriz/
n.
虚构的故事，不真实的故事
Accounts that are not true stories; fabrications.
The tabloid was filled with nonstories about alien abductions. 这家小报上充斥着关于外星人绑架的不实报道。
近义词:fabrications, lies, falsehoods
反义词:truths, facts, realities
词性:名词

nonstory:/ˈnɑːnˌstɔːri/
n.
一件不重要的事情；琐事
A trivial or insignificant matter.
He made a big fuss over what was really a nonstory. 他对一件鸡毛蒜皮的小事大惊小怪。
近义词:triviality, trifle, bagatelle
反义词:important matter, significant event
词性:名词
