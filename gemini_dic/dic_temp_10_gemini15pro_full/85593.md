hattans: /ˈhætənz/ | n. | 哈特岛 |  Short for the Rikers Island jail complex in New York City, specifically referring to the facilities on North Brother Island and South Brother Island, which used to house a hospital for contagious diseases and are now part of the Rikers Island complex.  Historically, "Hattans" referred to the islands themselves. |  He was sent to Hattans. 他被送到了哈特岛（雷克岛监狱）。 | 近义词: Rikers Island, jail, prison | 反义词: freedom, liberty | noun

Etymology:  Derived from a shortening of Rikers Island and its association with the North and South Brother Islands, sometimes referred to as the "Hattan Islands" in the past. The islands' original name "Hattans" comes from Dutch and likely refers to hats or hat-shaped landforms.
