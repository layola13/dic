doubters:/ˈdaʊtərz/
n.
怀疑者，持怀疑态度的人
People who question or hold reservations about something or someone.
Many doubters questioned the feasibility of the project. 许多怀疑者质疑该项目的可行性。
synonyms: skeptics, cynics, questioners
antonyms: believers, supporters, adherents
noun

doubt:/daʊt/
n.
怀疑；疑惑；疑问
A feeling of uncertainty or lack of conviction.
There is no doubt about his guilt. 他的罪行无可置疑。
v.
怀疑；不相信；不能肯定
To feel uncertain or unconvinced about something.
I doubt he will come. 我怀疑他会不会来。
synonyms: uncertainty, suspicion, mistrust (n.); question, distrust, disbelieve (v.)
antonyms: certainty, confidence, trust (n.); believe, trust, accept (v.)
noun, verb

Etymology: From Old French *douter, from Latin dubitare "to hesitate, waver in opinion, be in doubt, question," frequentative of dubare "to be in doubt," related to duo "two."  The sense evolution is from "hesitate" to "be uncertain."
