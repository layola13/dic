leftward:/ˈleftwərd/| adj. adv.|向左的，向左地|Moving or facing toward the left.|The car drifted leftward. 汽车向左漂移。|近义词：left, leftwards|反义词:rightward, rightwards|词性:形容词,副词

词源: From "left" + "-ward" (a suffix indicating direction). "Left" comes from Old English "lyft," meaning "weak" or "useless," as the left hand was traditionally considered less dominant.
