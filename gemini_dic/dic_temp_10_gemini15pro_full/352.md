abide-by:/əˈbaɪd baɪ/| phrasal verb |遵守，遵照|To accept and follow a decision, rule, agreement, etc.|例句：Both parties agreed to abide by the judge's decision. 双方都同意遵守法官的裁决。

例句：We must abide by the rules of the game. 我们必须遵守游戏规则。

近义词：comply with, adhere to, observe, follow, respect|反义词：disobey, break, violate, ignore|词性：动词短语


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ābīdan "remain, continue, wait, endure," from ā- "on" + bīdan "to wait." The "by" element originally meant "near, at, in, on, upon."


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company abides by all local regulations. = 这家公司遵守所有当地法规。
He always abides by his promises. = 他总是信守诺言。

一般过去时:
They abided by the terms of the contract. = 他们遵守了合同条款。
She abided by her parents' wishes. = 她遵从了父母的意愿。

现在完成时:
We have always abided by the law. = 我们一直遵守法律。
He has abided by the agreement since it was signed. = 自从协议签署以来，他一直遵守该协议。


过去完成时:
They had abided by the rules until that incident. = 在那次事件之前，他们一直遵守规则。
She had abided by her principles her entire life. = 她一生都坚持自己的原则。


将来时:
We will abide by the decision of the court. = 我们将遵守法院的判决。
I will abide by your request. = 我会遵从你的请求。


虚拟语气:
If they had abided by the treaty, the war might have been avoided. = 如果他们遵守了条约，战争或许可以避免。
I wish he would abide by the agreement. = 我希望他能够遵守协议。


被动语态:
The rules must be abided by. = 规则必须遵守。
The agreement was abided by both parties. =  双方都遵守了协议。

疑问句:
Do you always abide by your principles? = 你总是坚持你的原则吗？
Did they abide by the terms of the contract? = 他们遵守了合同条款吗？

否定句:
They did not abide by the agreement. = 他们没有遵守协议。
He doesn't always abide by the rules. = 他并不总是遵守规则。
