cabesa:/kɑˈbesɑ/| n.| 头，脑袋 (尤指动物); 源头，根源 (比喻); 首领，领导人|This word is primarily used in Spanish and Portuguese and means "head." It can also refer to the leader or the source of something.|La cabesa del caballo es grande. (The horse's head is large.)|近义词：(Spanish) cabeza, cráneo; (English) head, chief, leader, source, origin|反义词: (figurative) tail end, bottom|词性:noun
