out-jumps:/ˌaʊtˈdʒʌmps/
verb
超越，跳得比…远/高
To jump further or higher than someone or something else.
He out-jumps everyone on the team. 他比队里的任何人都跳得高。
The kangaroo easily out-jumped the fence. 袋鼠轻松地跳过了篱笆。
surpass, exceed, outleap
lag behind, fall short of
词性:动词
