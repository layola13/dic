administration:/ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən/ | n. |管理；行政；实施；行政部门|The process of managing, directing, or controlling something, especially a business, organization, or government. Also refers to the group of people who manage an organization. |The university administration announced new policies. 大学行政部门宣布了新的政策。

The administration of the new law has been difficult. 新法律的实施很困难。|近义词：management, governance, execution |反义词：mismanagement, neglect |词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *administrationem, accusative singular of administratio, from Latin administrare (“to manage, direct, serve”), from ad- + ministrare (“to serve, attend”), from minister (“servant, attendant”).
