flute-like:/ˈfluːtˌlaɪk/| adj.|像长笛的，笛声般的|Resembling the sound or shape of a flute.|Her voice was high and flute-like. 她的声音又高又像笛声。|The bird's song was flute-like. 这只鸟的歌声像笛声。|近义词：melodious, musical, high-pitched|反义词：raucous, harsh, grating|词性:形容词

詞源:
"flute" + "-like".  "Flute" comes from Old French "flaute," ultimately of Germanic origin.  "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
