devaporation:/diːˌvæpəˈreɪʃən/
n.
去蒸发，逆蒸发
The process of removing vapor or moisture, especially from a gas or liquid.  This word isn't commonly used; "condensation" or "dehumidification" are more frequently used.
Due to the devaporation process, the humidity in the room significantly decreased. 由于去蒸发过程，房间内的湿度显著降低。
The devaporation system successfully extracted excess moisture from the air. 去蒸发系统成功地从空气中提取了多余的水分。
近义词: condensation, dehumidification, drying
反义词: evaporation, vaporization
