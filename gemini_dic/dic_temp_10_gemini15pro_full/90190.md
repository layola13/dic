hottentotism:/ˈhɒtəntətɪzəm/ | n. |霍屯督人文化；（带有贬义）原始文化；落后文化|A term, now considered offensive, that was formerly used to refer to the customs and language of the Khoekhoe peoples of southern Africa, often used to imply primitiveness or inferiority.  It is now considered a derogatory and racist term. |  This book promotes outdated and offensive notions of hottentotism. 这本书宣扬过时且冒犯人的霍屯督人文化观念。|Synonym: primitivism (often used with negative connotations), barbarism (highly offensive) | Antonym: civilization, sophistication | noun
