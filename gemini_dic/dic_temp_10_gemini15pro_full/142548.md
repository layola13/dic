显示；表明；展览

The ostentation of his wealth was evident in his expensive car and designer clothes. 他的财富炫耀体现在他昂贵的汽车和名牌服装上。

The museum's ostentation of ancient artifacts drew large crowds.  博物馆展出的古代文物吸引了大批观众。

近义词: display, exhibition, demonstration, show, manifestation
反义词: concealment, hiding, suppression
