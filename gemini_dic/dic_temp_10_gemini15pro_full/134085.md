nonimaginarily:/ˌnɒnɪˈmædʒɪnərɪli/
adverb|非想象地，无想象力地|Not in a way that uses imagination; realistically; literally.  Existing in reality, not just in the imagination. |She described the events nonimaginarily, sticking to the facts. 她如实地描述了这些事件，坚持事实。| The artist, surprisingly, portrayed the fantastical creatures nonimaginarily, as if they were ordinary animals.  这位艺术家令人惊讶地以非想象的方式描绘了这些奇幻生物，就好像它们是普通的动物一样。| synonym: realistically, literally, factually, practically| antonym: imaginatively, fancifully, creatively, fictionally
