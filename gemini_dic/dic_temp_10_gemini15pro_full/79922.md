gnof:/nɒf/ | n. | 笨蛋，傻瓜 | A foolish or stupid person. |  Example: He acted like a complete gnof. 他表现得像个十足的笨蛋。| Synonyms: fool, idiot, dolt, nincompoop | Antonyms: genius, sage, savant | noun

Etymology:  "Gnof" is likely a humorous or slang term with uncertain origins.  It may be a playful reversal of "fong," itself a slang term for a fool or simpleton, which in turn might be related to "fungus," implying something useless or decaying.  It could also be a completely invented word.
