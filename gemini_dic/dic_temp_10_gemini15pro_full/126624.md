nailer:/ˈneɪlər/| n.|钉枪；钉子工|A tool used to drive nails, or a person who drives nails (especially as a profession).|He used a pneumatic nailer to quickly attach the boards. 他用气动钉枪快速地把木板钉在一起。|The nailer was very skilled and finished the job quickly.  这位钉子工非常熟练，很快就完成了工作。|Synonyms: nail gun, nail driver|Antonyms: (For tool) hammer (somewhat), (For person) none applicable|noun
