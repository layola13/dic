farms:/fɑːrmz/
n.
农场；农田；养殖场
A tract of land devoted to the raising of crops or livestock.
例句：The farms in this area are mostly used for growing corn.  这个地区的农场主要用来种植玉米。

例句：Many farms have been abandoned due to drought. 许多农场由于干旱而被废弃。

近义词：ranches, plantations
反义词：city, urban areas
词性：名词


词源：Old English *feorm, *firma "fixed payment, provision, food, feast, sustenance, goods, property," from Proto-Germanic *fermaz (source also of Old Frisian ferm "living, livelihood," Old Saxon firmo, Middle Dutch and Dutch veerm "cattle, food," Old High German firmu "provision," German Ferme "lease, rent," Gothic faurms "a meal").  The original sense was "a provision of food rendered as rent." The current sense of "tract of land leased or held for agricultural purposes" is from late 14c.
