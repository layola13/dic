commands:/kəˈmændz/ | n. | 命令，指令；掌握，控制| An authoritative order to do something.  | The general gave the command to attack. 将军下达了进攻的命令。 | synonyms: order, instruction, directive, dictate, mandate | antonyms: request, plea, entreaty | noun

commands:/kəˈmændz/ | v. | 命令，指挥；控制；博得，赢得| To give an authoritative order.  | He commanded the troops to retreat. 他命令部队撤退。 | synonyms: order, direct, instruct, control, manage | antonyms: obey, comply, follow, submit | verb
