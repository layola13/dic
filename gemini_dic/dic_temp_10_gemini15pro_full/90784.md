humorsomely:/ˈhjuːmərsəmli/ | adv. | 幽默地；滑稽地 | In a way that is humorous or amusing.  | He humorsomely recounted the story of his misadventure. 他滑稽地讲述了他这次不幸的遭遇。 |  She humorsomely mimicked her teacher's accent. 她幽默地模仿了她老师的口音。| 近义词: humorously, wittily, amusingly, comically | 反义词: seriously, solemnly, gravely | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From humor + -some + -ly.  "Humor" comes from Middle English humour, from Old French humour, ultimately from Latin humor "fluid, moisture." "-some" is a suffix meaning "characterized by" or "tending to." "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs.
