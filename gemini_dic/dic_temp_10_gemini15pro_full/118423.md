merrymeeting:/ˈmeriˌmiːtɪŋ/ | n. |欢乐聚会；庆祝集会|A festive gathering; a convivial meeting or celebration. |例句：The annual merrymeeting was a time for the whole village to come together and celebrate.  一年一度的欢乐聚会是全村人聚在一起庆祝的时刻。

例句：The merrymeeting included feasting, dancing, and storytelling.  欢乐聚会包括宴饮、跳舞和讲故事。|近义词：celebration, festival, feast, gathering, party|反义词：wake, funeral|词性:名词
