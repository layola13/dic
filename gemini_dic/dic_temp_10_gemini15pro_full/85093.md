hardbitten:/ˈhɑːrdbɪtn/ | adj. |顽强的，坚毅的，饱经风霜的|Toughened and cynical from hardship or experience.  A hardbitten detective. | The hardbitten journalist had seen it all before.  这位饱经风霜的记者以前见过这一切。 |近义词: tough, cynical, gritty, hardened, world-weary |反义词: naive, tender, soft, sentimental |词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From hard + bitten.  Originally referred to horses whose bits had made their mouths tough and insensitive.  This sense extended metaphorically to people who had become toughened and unemotional due to difficult experiences.
