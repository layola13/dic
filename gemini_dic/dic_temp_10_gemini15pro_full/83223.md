gybe: /dʒaɪb/ | v. n. | 转帆；转向 | To change the course of a sailing vessel by swinging the stern through the wind, causing the boom to shift sides.  The act of gybing. |  The sailor gybed the boat to avoid the rocks.  水手转帆以避开岩石。| The sudden gybe caught the crew off guard. 突如其来的转向让船员措手不及。 | synonyms: jibe | antonyms: tack | verb/noun

gybe: /dʒaɪb/ | v. n. | (非正式)嘲讽；讥讽 | To make a taunting or sarcastic remark. A taunting or sarcastic remark. | "Nice outfit," he gybed. “好衣服，”他讥讽道。 |  He made a gybe about her new hairstyle.  他嘲讽了她的新发型。 | synonyms: sneer, jeer, taunt, mock | antonyms: compliment, praise | verb/noun
