opetide: /oʊˈpɛpˌtaɪd/
noun
寡肽
A peptide containing a small number of amino acids.
Short chains of amino acid monomers linked by peptide (amide) bonds.
Oligopeptides are classified by the number of amino acids present in the chain.
例句：Oligopeptides play a crucial role in various biological processes. 寡肽在各种生物过程中起着至关重要的作用。

Oligopeptides can be synthesized in the laboratory. 寡肽可以在实验室合成。

近义词：peptide, polypeptide
反义词: protein (in a broader sense)
词性：n.
