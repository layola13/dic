corrosive:/kəˈroʊsɪv/| adj.|腐蚀性的；侵蚀性的；有害的|Having the power to gradually wear away or destroy something, especially by chemical action; harmful or destructive.|The battery acid is highly corrosive.  电池酸腐蚀性很强。|Synonyms: caustic, erosive, abrasive, mordant|Antonyms: benign, harmless, innocuous|adjective

corrosive:/kəˈroʊsɪv/| n.|腐蚀物；腐蚀剂|A corrosive substance.|The spill released a powerful corrosive that damaged the road surface.  泄漏物释放出一种强腐蚀剂，损坏了路面。|Synonyms: caustic, acid|Antonyms: neutralizer|noun
