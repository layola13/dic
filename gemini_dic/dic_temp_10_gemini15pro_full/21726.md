boarish:/ˈbɔːrɪʃ/ | adj. | 粗鲁的；粗野的；像野猪的 | Resembling a boar in coarseness or rudeness; swinish, ill-mannered, insensitive. | He behaved in a boarish manner, stuffing his face with food and belching loudly. 他举止粗鲁，狼吞虎咽地吃东西，还大声打嗝。 |  The customer's boarish behavior offended the waiter.  这位顾客粗鲁的行为冒犯了服务员。 | Synonyms: rude, crude, uncouth, ill-mannered, churlish, loutish, oafish, swinish | Antonyms: polite, courteous, refined, civilized, sophisticated | adjective
