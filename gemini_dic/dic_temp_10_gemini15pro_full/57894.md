drifters:/ˈdrɪftərz/| n.|漂流者；流浪汉；随波逐流的人；(尤指) 漂流物|People who drift from place to place without any clear purpose or direction.  Those who are driven by circumstances rather than by their own will.|The beach was littered with drifters – seaweed, driftwood, and plastic bottles.  海滩上到处都是漂流物——海藻、浮木和塑料瓶。|
The drifters moved from town to town, looking for work. 这些流浪汉为了找工作，从一个镇子流浪到另一个镇子。|synonyms: nomads, wanderers, vagrants, roamers|antonyms: settlers, residents, stay-at-homes|noun
