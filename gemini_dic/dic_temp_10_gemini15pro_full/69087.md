faus:/faʊs/ | adj. | false的古体拼写，假的，错误的 | An archaic spelling of "false," meaning not true or correct. |  He told a faus tale about his adventures.  他讲述了一个关于他冒险经历的虚假故事。 | 近义词: false, untrue, erroneous, incorrect | 反义词: true, correct, accurate | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fals, *faus, from Old French fals, faus, from Latin falsus, past participle of fallere “to deceive, trick, lead astray, disappoint, fail.”
