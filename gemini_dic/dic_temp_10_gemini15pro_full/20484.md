bituminizations: /bɪˌtjuːmɪnaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. |沥青化，沥青处理| The process of converting organic matter into bitumen or becoming impregnated with bitumen. |  The bituminizations of the roads significantly improved their durability. 道路的沥青化显著提高了它们的耐久性。| The bituminizations of the ancient forest floor created a unique geological formation. 古代森林地面的沥青化形成了独特的地质构造。 | 近义词: asphaltation, tarring | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From bituminize + -ation.  Bituminize comes from bitumen + -ize. Bitumen traces back to Latin *bitūmen, of uncertain origin.
