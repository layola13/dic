nonscholastically:/ˌnɒnˌskəˈlæstɪkli/
adverb
非学术地；非学校地；课外地
in a way that is not connected with school or education
例句：She spends her time nonscholastically, pursuing hobbies like painting and hiking. 她把时间花在课外活动上，比如绘画和徒步旅行。

例句：The children were encouraged to develop their talents nonscholastically.  孩子们被鼓励在课外发展他们的才能。

近义词：extracurricularly, informally
反义词：academically, scholastically
词性：adverb
