forced:/fɔːrst/ (UK) /fɔːrst/ (US)| adj. v.|被迫的，强迫的；勉强的|Done against someone's will or against the natural order of things.  Origin: From the past participle of force (v.).|He made a forced smile. 他勉强笑了笑。|Synonyms: compelled, obliged, involuntary, unnatural|Antonyms: voluntary, natural, willing|adjective

forced:/fɔːrst/ (UK) /fɔːrst/ (US)| v. |强迫，迫使|To make someone do something against their will. Origin: From the verb force.|They forced him to confess. 他们强迫他认罪。|Synonyms: compel, coerce, oblige|Antonyms: allow, permit|verb
