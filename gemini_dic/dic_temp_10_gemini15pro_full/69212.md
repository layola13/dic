fearful:/ˈfɪərfəl/| adj.|害怕的，担心的，可怕的|Feeling or showing fear or anxiety; causing fear or anxiety.|She was fearful of walking home alone at night. 她害怕晚上一个人走回家。|The dark, empty house was a fearful sight.  黑暗空旷的房子是一个可怕的景象。|近义词：afraid, frightened, anxious, apprehensive, terrified|反义词：brave, courageous, fearless, bold|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *ferful, from Old English *fǣrful, from Proto-Germanic *fairafullaz, equivalent to *fair (fear) + *-ful. Cognate with Dutch vaarvol (“perilous”), German furchtbar (“terrible”).
