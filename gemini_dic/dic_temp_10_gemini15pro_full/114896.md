man-hour:/ˈmænˌaʊər/| n.|工时；人时|A unit of work representing one worker working for one hour.  The amount of work that can be done by one person in one hour.|It took 100 man-hours to complete the project. 完成这个项目需要100个工时。|Synonyms: labor-hour, work-hour|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but consider concepts like idleness, inactivity)|Noun
