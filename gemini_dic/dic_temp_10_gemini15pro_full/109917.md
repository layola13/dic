libelant:/ˈlaɪbələnt/| n.|原告（尤指在诽谤案中）|A person who brings a lawsuit, especially one who files a libel suit. |The libelant claimed that the newspaper article had damaged his reputation. 原告声称报纸上的文章损害了他的名誉。|Synonym: plaintiff, complainant|Antonym: defendant|noun

libelant:/ˈlaɪbələnt/| n.|控告诽谤者|A person who sues another for libel. |The libelant presented strong evidence to support his claim. 控告诽谤者提供了强有力的证据来支持他的主张。|Synonym: accuser, prosecutor|Antonym: defendant|noun
