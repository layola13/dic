anti-leprosy:/ˌæntiˈlɛprəsi/
| adj. |麻风病的；抗麻风病的；治疗麻风病的|Preventing or curing leprosy; relating to the treatment of leprosy.|The doctor prescribed anti-leprosy drugs. 医生开了抗麻风病的药。|Synonyms: antileprotic|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider "pro-leprosy" but this is not a standard term) |adjective


anti-leprosy:/ˌæntiˈlɛprəsi/
| n. |抗麻风病药物|Medication used to treat leprosy.|The development of new anti-leprosy drugs has greatly improved the prognosis for patients. 新型抗麻风病药物的开发极大地改善了患者的预后。|Synonyms: antileprotic medication|Antonyms: (No direct antonym) |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + leprosy.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to," derived from Greek. "Leprosy" comes from Old French "lepre," ultimately from Greek "lepra" meaning "scaly disease."
