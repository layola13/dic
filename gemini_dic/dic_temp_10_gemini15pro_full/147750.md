overprogram:/ˌoʊvərˈproʊɡræm/ | v. | 过度安排；安排过多的节目；为…编制过多的程序 | to schedule too many activities or to create a computer program that is too complex | The summer camp was overprogrammed, leaving the children exhausted. 夏令营的活动安排得过于紧凑，孩子们都累坏了。 | The software developer overprogrammed the application, making it prone to crashes.  软件开发人员为应用程序编写了过多的程序，使其容易崩溃。 | 近义词: oversaturate, overload | 反义词: underprogram, simplify | 词性: 动词
