cursores:/kɜːrˈsɔːrez/ | n. | 光标; 游标 |  Pointers or indicators on a computer screen that show the position of the mouse or other input device.  They can also refer to database cursors, which are control structures that enable traversal over the records in a database. | The mouse cursor moved across the screen. 鼠标光标在屏幕上移动。 |  The database cursor fetched the next row of data. 数据库游标提取了下一行数据。 | synonyms: pointers, indicators, arrows | antonyms: N/A | noun
