craspedote:/kræˈspiːdoʊt/| adj.|有缘毛的，边缘有流苏的|Having a craspedon; fringed.  Referring specifically to the wing margin of certain insects, especially butterflies and moths, bearing a fringe of hair-like scales.|The craspedote wings of the butterfly shimmered in the sunlight. 蝴蝶有缘毛的翅膀在阳光下闪闪发光。|Synonyms: fringed, fimbriate, ciliate|Antonyms: smooth, unfringed|adjective
