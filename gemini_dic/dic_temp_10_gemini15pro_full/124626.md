moustacheless:/məˈstɑːʃləs/ |adj.| 没有胡子的，无胡须的 | lacking a moustache; having no moustache | He looked surprisingly young and almost boyish moustacheless. 他看起来出奇的年轻，几乎像个没有胡子的男孩。| The actor played a moustacheless villain, a departure from his usual roles.  这位演员扮演了一个没有胡子的反派，这与他通常的角色不同。| 近义词：clean-shaven, beardless | 反义词：moustached, bearded | 词性: 形容词

词源: moustache (n.) + -less (suffix)

moustache: from French moustache, from Italian mostaccio, mosto upper lip, moustache, from Medieval Greek μουστάκιον (moustakion), diminutive of μύσταξ (mustax) upper lip, moustache.

-less: Old English -lēas "without," cognate with Old Frisian -lēs, Old Saxon -lōs, Old High German -lōs, Gothic -laus, German -los.
