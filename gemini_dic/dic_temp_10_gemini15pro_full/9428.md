antireactionaries:/ˌæntiˌriːˈækʃəˌnɛriz/
n.|反动派，反对反动的人|People who oppose reactionaries or reactionary policies.  Those who fight against political or social conservatism, especially those who oppose a return to a previous, less progressive state.|The antireactionaries protested the government's new policies.|近义词：progressives, liberals, radicals|反义词:reactionaries, conservatives|词性:名词

antireactionary:/ˌæntiˌriːˈækʃəˌnɛri/
adj.|反反动的，反对反动的|Opposing or resisting reaction or reactionaries; progressive.|The antireactionary movement gained momentum after the war.|近义词：progressive, liberal, radical|反义词:reactionary, conservative|词性:形容词
