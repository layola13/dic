freeloading:/ˈfriːloʊdɪŋ/| n. adj.|吃白食，揩油；免费使用|The act of using something or taking advantage of someone without paying or contributing anything.|He was accused of freeloading off his friends. 他被指控占朋友的便宜。|近义词：sponging, leeching, mooching|反义词：paying, contributing|noun, adjective

freeload:/ˈfriːloʊd/| v.|吃白食，揩油；免费使用|To use something or take advantage of someone without paying or contributing anything.|He freeloaded off his friends for months, never offering to pay for anything.  他几个月来一直占朋友的便宜，从来不主动付钱。|近义词：sponge, leech, mooch|反义词：pay, contribute|verb
