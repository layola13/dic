carvol: /ˈkɑːrvɒl/ (British) /ˈkɑːrvɑːl/ (American)| n. |拼车；合伙用车|The act of sharing a car journey, especially to and from work, with other people.|Example: I found a carvol to London. 我找到了去伦敦的拼车。 Example:  Carvoling helps reduce traffic congestion. 拼车有助于减少交通拥堵。|近义词：ride-sharing, carpooling, lift-sharing|反义词: driving alone, solo trip|词性:noun

词源: Blend of *car* and *convolve*, possibly influenced by *car pool*.  While "car pool" refers to a group of people regularly sharing transportation, "carvol" often implies a more ad-hoc arrangement.
