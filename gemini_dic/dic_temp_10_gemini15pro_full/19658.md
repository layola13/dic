bilaminar:/baɪˈlæmɪnər/| adj.|双层的，两层的|Composed of two layers; having two laminae.|The bilaminar disc is a crucial stage in early embryonic development.  双层胚盘是早期胚胎发育的关键阶段。|The bilaminar germ disc gives rise to the amniotic cavity and yolk sac. 双层胚芽盘形成羊膜腔和卵黄囊。|double-layered, two-layered|monolayer, single-layered|adjective
