hysteretic:/ˌhɪstəˈrɛtɪk/ | adj. | 磁滞的，迟滞的，滞后的 | Exhibiting hysteresis; lagging or slow to respond. | The material displayed hysteretic behavior, meaning its response depended on its past state.  这种材料表现出磁滞行为，这意味着它的响应取决于它过去的状态。| Example: The hysteretic properties of the material made it ideal for use in shock absorbers.  这种材料的迟滞特性使其成为减震器的理想材料。| Synonyms: lagging, slow, delayed, retarding | Antonyms:  immediate, instantaneous, prompt | adjective

hysteresis:/ˌhɪstəˈriːsɪs/ | n. | 磁滞现象，滞后现象 | The phenomenon in which the value of a physical property lags behind changes in the effect causing it, as for instance when magnetic induction lags behind the magnetizing force. |  The hysteresis of the thermostat caused the temperature to fluctuate.  恒温器的滞后现象导致温度波动。 | Example:  The system exhibited hysteresis, taking time to adjust to changes in input. 系统表现出滞后现象，需要时间来适应输入的变化。| Synonyms: lag, retardation, delay | Antonyms:  immediacy, instantaneousness | noun
