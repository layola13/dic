chant:/tʃænt/ | n. v. | 圣歌，吟唱；反复地唱或说 | A rhythmic song or phrase repeated many times. | The monks chanted their prayers.  僧侣们吟诵着他们的祈祷。|  The crowd chanted the team's name. 人群反复呼喊着队伍的名字。 | 近义词：sing, chorus, intone, recite | 反义词: whisper, murmur | 词性: 名词, 动词
Etymology: From Old French chanter "to sing," from Latin cantare "to sing," frequentative of canere "to sing."
