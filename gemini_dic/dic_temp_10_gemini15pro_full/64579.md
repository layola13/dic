erstwhile:/ˈɜːrstˌwaɪl/| adj.|以前的，昔日的|Formerly; in the past.|例句：The erstwhile friends had a falling out. 昔日的朋友闹翻了。

例句：The company is now led by the erstwhile assistant, who has taken over after the former CEO's retirement.  这家公司现在由以前的助理领导，他在前任首席执行官退休后接管了公司。|近义词：former, previous, onetime, past, ex-|反义词：current, present, future|词性：形容词
词源：From Middle English *erstwhile, equivalent to erst (“formerly”) + while (“time”).  源自中古英语 *erstwhile，等同于 erst（“以前”）+ while（“时间”）。
