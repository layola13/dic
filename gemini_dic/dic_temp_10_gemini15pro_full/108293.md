laureates:/ˈlɔːriəts/ | n. | 获奖者；桂冠诗人 |  A person who has been honored with an award, especially the Nobel Prize.  A poet officially appointed by a government or conferring organization, typically for an indefinite period, to compose poems for state occasions and other ceremonial events. | The Nobel laureates gathered in Stockholm. 诺贝尔奖获得者聚集在斯德哥尔摩。 | synonyms: prizewinner, winner, victor, champion, recipient, honoree | antonyms: loser, runner-up | noun
