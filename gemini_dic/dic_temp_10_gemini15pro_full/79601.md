glorifier:/ˈɡlɔːrɪfaɪər/| n.|颂扬者，赞美者|A person or thing that glorifies someone or something.  Giving praise or honor.|例句：He was a glorifier of all things traditional. 他是所有传统事物的颂扬者。|例句：The poem is a glorifier of nature's beauty. 这首诗歌颂了自然之美。|近义词：praiser, exalter, eulogist|反义词：critic, detractor|词性:noun

glorify:/ˈɡlɔːrɪfaɪ/ | vt.|赞美，颂扬；美化，吹捧|To praise or honor someone or something highly; to make something seem better or more important than it really is.|例句：They glorified their leader as a hero. 他们把他们的领袖誉为英雄。|例句：The movie glorifies violence. 这部电影美化了暴力。|近义词：extol, exalt, laud, celebrate|反义词：denigrate, belittle, disparage|词性:verb
