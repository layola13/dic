flatbread:/ˈflætˌbred/ | n. | 薄饼，扁面包 | A thin, unleavened bread.  A general term for various breads made without yeast or other leavening agents, resulting in a flat, often chewy texture. |  We had hummus and flatbread for lunch. 我们午餐吃了鹰嘴豆泥和扁面包。 | synonyms: pita, tortilla, naan, lavash | antonyms: loaf (not a direct antonym, but represents a leavened, raised bread) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *flætbrēad  (unattested), from  flæt "flat" + brēad "bread".  The word reflects the simple combination of the bread's shape and its basic ingredient.
