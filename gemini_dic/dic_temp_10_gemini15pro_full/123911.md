moreen:/məˈriːn/| n. |摩尔缎，毛利呢|A heavy, ribbed, usually cotton fabric used for curtains, upholstery, etc.|例句：The curtains were made of a heavy moreen. 窗帘是用厚重的摩尔缎做的。

例句：She chose a dark green moreen for the sofa upholstery. 她选择了深绿色的摩尔缎来做沙发套。|近义词：rep, damask, poplin |反义词: None|词性:noun
