legalizers:/ˈliːɡəˌlaɪzərz/
noun
合法化者，支持合法化的人
People who advocate for making something legal, especially drugs or activities previously considered illegal.
The legalizers of marijuana argue that it has medicinal benefits.  大麻合法化的支持者认为它具有药用价值。
The group of legalizers held a rally in support of their cause.  这群合法化的支持者举行集会以支持他们的主张。
synonyms:  proponents, advocates, supporters
antonyms:  opponents, prohibitionists
n.
