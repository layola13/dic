echidna:/ɪˈkɪdnə/ | n. | 针鼹，刺食蚁兽 | A spiny insectivorous mammal native to Australia and New Guinea, characterized by its pointed snout and covering of spines and coarse hair. It lays eggs and has a pouch for carrying its young. |  例句：The echidna uses its long sticky tongue to catch ants and termites.  针鼹用它长长的粘性舌头捕捉蚂蚁和白蚁。

例句：Echidnas are one of the few mammals that lay eggs. 针鼹是少数几种产卵的哺乳动物之一。| 近义词：spiny anteater | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific animal) | 词性: noun
