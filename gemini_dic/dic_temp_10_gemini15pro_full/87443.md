hesitatingly:/ˈhezɪteɪtɪŋli/| adv.|犹豫地，迟疑地|In a way that shows hesitation or uncertainty.|She hesitatingly agreed to go with him. 她犹豫地同意和他一起去。|Synonyms: tentatively, cautiously, reluctantly|Antonyms: decisively, confidently, readily|adverb

hesitate:/ˈhezɪteɪt/| v.|犹豫，迟疑|To pause before saying or doing something, especially because you are nervous or uncertain.|He hesitated before answering the question. 他在回答问题之前犹豫了一下。|Synonyms: pause, waver, falter|Antonyms: decide, resolve, proceed|verb
