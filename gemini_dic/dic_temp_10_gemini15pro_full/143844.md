outpitying:/ˌaʊtˈpɪtiɪŋ/| v. | 比...更同情；胜过...的同情| Showing more pity than someone else; surpassing someone in pity. | She was outpitying everyone else at the funeral, wailing loudly and dramatically. 她胜过葬礼上的所有人，大声而夸张地哭泣。| He was outpitying his friend, whose pet had just died, with tales of his own childhood losses. 他对朋友的宠物刚去世表示同情，并讲述了自己童年失去宠物的故事。| 近义词：outsympathizing, outdoing in compassion | 反义词：unsympathetic, callous | 词性：动词 (present participle)
