blastostyle: /ˈblæstəˌstaɪl/ | n. | 芽茎；生殖体 | A stalk-like individual in certain colonial hydrozoans that bears medusae or gonophores.  It is a modified polyp specialized for asexual reproduction. |  The blastostyle of the Obelia is covered in medusa buds.  奥贝利亚水螅的芽茎上覆盖着水母芽。|  The researchers studied the development of the blastostyle in different hydrozoan species. 研究人员研究了不同种类水螅芽茎的发育。 | synonym: gonophore,  gonangium | antonym: N/A | noun
