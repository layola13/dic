beginningless:/bɪˈɡɪnɪŋlɪs/
adj.
无始的，没有开端的
Having no beginning; eternal.
The universe, in some cosmological models, is considered beginningless.  宇宙，在一些宇宙学模型中，被认为是无始的。
The cycle of reincarnation is often described as beginningless. 轮回的循环常被描述为无始的。
eternal, infinite, primordial
finite, temporal
