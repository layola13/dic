misinforming: /ˌmɪsɪnˈfɔːrmɪŋ/ | v. | 误导，提供错误信息 | Giving someone incorrect or misleading information. |  He was accused of misinforming the public about the dangers of the virus. 他被指控向公众提供了关于病毒危险的错误信息。

The company was misinforming customers about the product's features.  这家公司在产品功能方面误导了顾客。

| 近义词：deceiving, misleading, lying | 反义词：informing, enlightening, educating | 词性: 动词

Etymology:
From mis- + inform + -ing.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "wrong" or "badly." "Inform" comes from the Latin "informare," meaning "to shape, form, train, instruct, educate."  The "-ing" suffix turns the verb "misinform" into its present participle form.
