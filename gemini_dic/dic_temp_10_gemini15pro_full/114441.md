makeover's:/ˈmeɪkoʊvərz/
noun
改造，翻新，改头换面
A makeover is a complete transformation or renovation of someone's appearance, a room, or a building.
She gave her bedroom a complete makeover. 她把她的卧室彻底改造了一下。
renewal, renovation, revamp
decline, deterioration
n.
