nondamnation:/ˌnɒndæmˈneɪʃən/
n.
non-condemnation; absence of damnation; forgiveness
The state of not being condemned or damned.
He preached a gospel of love and nondamnation. 他宣扬爱与不谴责的福音。
近义词: forgiveness, absolution, redemption, pardon
反义词: damnation, condemnation
