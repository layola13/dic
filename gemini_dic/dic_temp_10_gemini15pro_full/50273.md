dephlegmation:/ˌdiːflɛɡˈmeɪʃən/| n. |去痰；冷凝；净化|The process of removing water or other liquids from a gaseous mixture by cooling it, causing the liquid to condense.|Dephlegmation is essential in the distillation of spirits to remove unwanted impurities.  去痰在烈酒蒸馏中是必不可少的，可以去除不需要的杂质。

The dephlegmation process improved the purity of the final product. 冷凝过程提高了最终产品的纯度。|Synonyms: condensation, rectification, purification|Antonyms: vaporization, evaporation|noun
