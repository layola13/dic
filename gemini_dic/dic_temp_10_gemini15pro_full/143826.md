outpeer:/ˌaʊtˈpɪr/
verb
超越，胜过
to surpass someone in ability or achievement
Example: She outpeered all her rivals in the competition.  她在比赛中胜过所有对手。

Example: He consistently outpeered his colleagues in sales. 他在销售方面一直胜过他的同事。

近义词: surpass, excel, outdo, outstrip, outperform
反义词: lag behind, fall short of
词性: verb
