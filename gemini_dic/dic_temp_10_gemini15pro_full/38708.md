condescend:/ˌkɒndɪˈsend/ (UK), /ˌkɑːndɪˈsend/ (US)| v.|屈尊，俯就，摆架子|To behave as if one is conscious of descending from a superior position, rank, or dignity.|例句：The CEO condescended to eat lunch with the factory workers. 首席执行官屈尊与工厂工人一起吃午饭。

例句：He condescended to explain the situation to us. 他屈尊向我们解释情况。

近义词：patronize, deign, stoop|反义词：respect, admire|词性：动词
