alteration:/ˌɔːltəˈreɪʃn/ | n. |改变，修改，变更|A change or modification made to something. |例句：The dress needed some alterations to fit perfectly. 这件衣服需要一些修改才能完全合身。

例句：They made some alterations to the original plan. 他们对原计划做了一些修改。|近义词：change, modification, adjustment, amendment, revision |反义词：preservation, stagnation, consistency |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *alteracioun*, from Anglo-Norman *alteracion*, and Old French *alteracion*, from Latin *alterātiō*, *alterātiōnem*, from *alterātus*, past participle of *alterāre* (“to alter”), from *alter* (“other”).
