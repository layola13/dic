gunarchy:/ˈɡʌnɑːrki/
n.
枪支统治;枪权至上
A social system or political philosophy in which gun ownership is paramount and unrestricted, often associated with a belief that individual liberty is best protected through the widespread possession of firearms.
The senator's opponents accused him of promoting gunarchy and undermining public safety.  参议员的反对者指责他宣扬枪权至上，破坏公共安全。
The documentary explored the concept of gunarchy and its potential consequences.  这部纪录片探讨了枪支统治的概念及其潜在后果。
Synonyms: gun culture, gun fanaticism, gun worship
Antonyms: gun control, gun restriction, disarmament
