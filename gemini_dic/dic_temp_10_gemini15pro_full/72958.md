foodie: /ˈfuːdi/ | n. | 美食家；吃货 | A person who has a keen interest in food, especially in eating and cooking a wide variety of foods. |  例句：He's a real foodie and loves trying new restaurants. 他是个真正的美食家，喜欢尝试新的餐馆。

例句：She's become quite the foodie since she started watching cooking shows. 自从她开始看烹饪节目以来，她已经变成了一个十足的美食家。| 近义词：gourmet, gourmand, epicure | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可用indifferent to food来表示对食物不感兴趣的人) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "food" + "-ie" (a suffix forming nouns denoting a person interested in or associated with something).  The term gained popularity in the 1980s.
