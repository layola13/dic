frailly:/ˈfreɪlli/| adv.|脆弱地；虚弱地|In a frail or weak manner.  Characterized by or appearing to be weak and delicate. |Her frailly hand reached out for the glass. 她那只瘦弱的手伸向玻璃杯。|近义词：weakly, feebly, delicately|反义词：strongly, robustly|词性:副词

frail:/freɪl/| adj.|脆弱的；虚弱的|Weak and delicate; easily broken or damaged. |Her frail health was a constant worry. 她虚弱的健康状况一直令人担忧。|近义词：weak, delicate, fragile|反义词：strong, robust, sturdy|词性:形容词

词源: From Old French *fraile, *frele, from Latin fragilis "breakable," from frangere "to break."
