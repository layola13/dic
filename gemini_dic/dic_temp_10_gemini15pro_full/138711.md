nullifying:/ˈnʌlɪfaɪɪŋ/
adjective
使无效的，取消的
Making something legally or officially invalid; depriving something of value or effectiveness.
The treaty was nullifying by the subsequent agreement.  该条约被后来的协议废止。
The evidence was nullifying due to police misconduct.  由于警方的不当行为，证据失效了。
synonyms: invalidating, negating, voiding, annulling
antonyms: validating, confirming, ratifying
词性: adjective
