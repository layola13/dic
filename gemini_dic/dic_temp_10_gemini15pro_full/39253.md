congregational:/ˌkɒŋɡrɪˈɡeɪʃənəl/ (US) /ˌkɑːŋɡrɪˈɡeɪʃənəl/| adj.|会众的；公理教会的|Relating to a congregation or its members; of or pertaining to Congregationalism (a system of church governance in which each local church is independent).|The congregational singing was uplifting. 会众的歌声令人振奋。|The church follows a congregational polity.  这个教会遵循公理教会的管理体制。|近义词：communal, collective|反义词：individual, private|词性:形容词

congregation:/ˌkɒŋɡrɪˈɡeɪʃən/ (US) /ˌkɑːŋɡrɪˈɡeɪʃənəl/| n.|会众；人群|A group of people assembled for religious worship; a gathering or assembly of people.|The congregation stood to sing the hymn. 会众起立唱赞美诗。|A large congregation gathered for the outdoor service.  很多人聚集在一起参加户外礼拜。|近义词：assembly, gathering|反义词：dispersal, scattering|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *congregationalis, from Latin congregatio (“a gathering together”), from congregare (“to gather together”), from com- (“together”) + gregare (“to flock”), from grex (“flock”).
