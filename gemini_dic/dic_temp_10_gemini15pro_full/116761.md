mauritians:/mɔːˈrɪʃənz/
noun
毛里求斯人
People from Mauritius, an island nation in the Indian Ocean.
Many Mauritians speak both English and French. 许多毛里求斯人既说英语也说法语。
synonyms: Mauritian people, islanders (in the context of Mauritius)
antonyms: N/A (no direct antonym for a nationality)
