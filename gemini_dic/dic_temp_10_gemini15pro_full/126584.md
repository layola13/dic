naganas:/nəˈɡɑːnəs/| n. |非洲锥虫病，昏睡病|A disease of livestock and wild animals, especially horses, cattle, and camels, caused by trypanosome parasites and transmitted by the tsetse fly.  It is characterized by fever, lethargy, and emaciation, often leading to death.|The cattle were infected with naganas.|近义词：sleeping sickness, trypanosomiasis|反义词:|noun
