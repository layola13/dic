(of a bad, difficult, or unwelcome situation or phenomenon) present or experienced to a severe or intense degree.|He has an acuter sense of smell than a dog. 他的嗅觉比狗还灵敏。

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: comparative of *acute, from Latin acutus ‘sharpened’ (past participle of acuere ), from acus ‘needle’.
