decolorant:/diːˈkʌlərənt/
n.|脱色剂，漂白剂|A substance that removes color from something.|The decolorant quickly bleached the stain from the fabric.  这种脱色剂迅速漂白了织物上的污渍。|synonyms: bleach, whitener, fading agent|antonyms: dye, colorant, pigment|noun

decolorant:/diːˈkʌlərənt/
adj.|脱色的，漂白的|Having the property of removing color.|The decolorant solution effectively removed the dye.  这种脱色溶液有效地去除了染料。|synonyms: bleaching, whitening|antonyms: dyeing, coloring|adjective
