helicopter:/ˈhɛlɪkɒptər/ (UK), /ˈhɛlɪkɑːptər/ (US)| n.|直升飞机|A type of aircraft that derives both lift and propulsion from one or more sets of horizontally revolving overhead rotors. It can take off and land vertically, hover, and fly forwards, backwards, and sideways.|The helicopter landed on the hospital roof.|近义词：chopper, rotorcraft, whirlybird|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps airplane/fixed-wing aircraft)|noun
