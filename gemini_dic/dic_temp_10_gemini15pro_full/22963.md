bottomless:/ˈbɒtəmləs (UK), ˈbɑːtəmləs (US)/
adj.
无底的，深不可测的; 极其大量的，无限的
Having no bottom; very deep.  Seemingly limitless in amount or depth.
例句：She stared into the bottomless pit. 她凝视着无底的深渊。
例句：He has a bottomless appetite for knowledge. 他对知识有着无限的渴望。

近义词：infinite, immeasurable, boundless, inexhaustible
反义词：shallow, finite, limited
词性：adjective


bottomless:/ˈbɒtəmləs (UK), ˈbɑːtəmləs (US)/
adj.
(非正式)大量的，无限供应的
(informal) Very large or in plentiful supply.
例句：They serve bottomless coffee at the diner. 那家小餐馆提供无限续杯的咖啡。
例句：She seemed to have a bottomless supply of money. 她似乎有用不完的钱。
近义词：abundant, copious, plentiful
反义词：limited, scarce, meager
词性：adjective
