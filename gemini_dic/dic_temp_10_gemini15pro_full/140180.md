offering: /ˈɒfərɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈɔːfərɪŋ/ (US)| n.| 产品，供应物 |Something that is offered for sale or use. |The company's latest offering is a new smartphone. 这家公司最新的产品是一款新的智能手机。| synonyms: product, service, commodity | antonyms: demand | noun
