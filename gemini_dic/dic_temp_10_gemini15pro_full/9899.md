apatite:/ˈæpətaɪt/ (US), /ˈæpətʌɪt/ (UK)| n. |磷灰石|A common complex mineral consisting of calcium phosphate together with fluorine, chlorine, or hydroxyl, occurring in various colours and in hexagonal crystalline form. It is used in the manufacture of fertilizers and is the main source of phosphorus.|Apatite is a primary component of phosphate rock. 磷灰石是磷酸盐岩的主要成分。| Apatite crystals can be found in a variety of colors, including green, blue, and yellow. 磷灰石晶体可以找到各种颜色，包括绿色、蓝色和黄色。 |近义词:phosphate mineral |反义词: None |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *apatite*, from German *Apatit*, from Ancient Greek *ἀπάτη* (*apátē*, “deceit”) + *-ίτης* (*-ítēs*, adjectival suffix), because it was often mistaken for other minerals.  因为它经常被误认为是其他矿物。
