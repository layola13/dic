euphonic:/juːˈfɒnɪk/ /juːˈfɑːnɪk/
adjective
悦耳的；和谐的
pleasing to the ear.
Her voice was soft and euphonic. 她的声音柔和悦耳。

近义词:harmonious, melodious, sweet-sounding, pleasant, agreeable
反义词:cacophonous, dissonant, harsh, jarring, unpleasant


词性:adjective
