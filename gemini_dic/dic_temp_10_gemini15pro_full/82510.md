gryphons:/ˈɡrɪfənz/ | n. |狮鹫，格里芬|A mythical creature with the body, tail, and back legs of a lion; the head and wings of an eagle; and an eagle's talons as its front feet.|例句：Gryphons are often depicted as guardians of treasure. 狮鹫通常被描绘成宝藏的守护者。

例句：The gryphons soared through the air, their powerful wings beating rhythmically. 狮鹫翱翔在空中，它们强有力的翅膀有节奏地拍打着。|近义词：griffin|反义词:  (No direct antonym for a mythical creature) |词性:noun
