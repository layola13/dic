dominoes:/ˈdɒmɪnəʊz/ (UK) /ˈdɑːmɪnoʊz/ (US)| n.|多米诺骨牌|A set of small rectangular blocks, typically 28, marked with spots and used for playing a game.  The game itself is also referred to as dominoes.|We played dominoes all afternoon. 我们整个下午都在玩多米诺骨牌。|Synonyms: tiles, pieces|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

domino:/ˈdɒmɪnəʊ/ (UK) /ˈdɑːmɪnoʊ/ (US)| n.|多米诺骨牌（单数）;  面具; 斗篷; 披风|A single piece from a set of dominoes; a loose cloak worn with a mask for the upper part of the face.|She picked up a domino and added it to the chain. 她拿起一块多米诺骨牌，把它加到链条上。  He wore a domino and mask to the masquerade ball. 他戴着面具和披风去参加化妆舞会。|Synonyms: tile, piece, cloak, cape|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
