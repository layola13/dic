hispano:/ɪˈspænoʊ/ /hɪˈspænoʊ/
noun, adjective
西班牙裔的；西班牙语的
A person of Spanish or Spanish-speaking origin or descent.  Relating to Spanish-speaking people or their culture.
例句：Many Hispanics live in California. 许多西班牙裔人住在加利福尼亚。

例句：Hispanic culture is rich and diverse. 西班牙文化丰富多样。

近义词：Latino/Latina, Spanish-speaking, Spanish-American
反义词：Anglo-American (in a US context)
词性：noun, adjective
