what:/wɒt/(UK) /wɑːt/(US)| pron. det. adv.|什么，多么|Used to ask for information specifying something.|例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？|近义词：which|反义词: (No direct antonym)|pronoun, determiner, adverb

names:/neɪmz/| n.|名字，名称|A word or set of words by which a person, animal, place, or thing is known or referred to.|例句：What are their names? 他们的名字是什么？|近义词：titles, appellations|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun


common-names:/ˈkɒmən neɪmz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmən neɪmz/ (US)| n.|俗名，通称|The name by which something is known to the general public, as distinct from its scientific name.|例句：The common-name for *Felis catus* is "cat".  *Felis catus*的俗名是“猫”。|近义词：vernacular names, trivial names|反义词：scientific names, technical names|noun

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing right now? = 他现在正在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she eaten for lunch? = 她午饭吃了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all week? = 你整个星期都在做什么？
What has she been reading lately? = 她最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say? = 你说了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么事？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 pm yesterday? = 昨天晚上8点你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? = 她参加聚会时穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had they eaten before dinner? = 他们在晚餐前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the phone rang? = 电话响之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? =  在她换专业之前，她一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们将在下次会议上讨论什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by Friday? = 你到星期五会完成什么？
What will they have accomplished by the end of the year? = 到年底他们会完成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):

虚拟语气:
If I knew what to say, I would say it. = 如果我知道该说什么，我就会说。
I wish I knew what she was thinking. = 我希望我知道她在想什么。

被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being built next to the library? = 图书馆旁边正在建什么？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what he's saying. = 她不明白他在说什么。
