mnemosyne:/nɪˈmɒsɪniː/| n. |记忆女神，记忆力|Mnemosyne is the Greek goddess of memory and the mother of the Muses.  Mnemosyne是希腊神话中的记忆女神，也是缪斯的母亲。|例句：Artists often invoke Mnemosyne for inspiration. 艺术家经常祈求记忆女神的灵感。

例句：Her memory was like that of Mnemosyne, seemingly infinite. 她的记忆力就像记忆女神一样，似乎无限。|近义词：memory, remembrance|反义词：forgetfulness, amnesia|词性:noun
