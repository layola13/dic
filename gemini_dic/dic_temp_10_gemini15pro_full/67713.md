exuberance:/ɪɡˈzjuːbərəns/ /ɪɡˈzuːbərəns/
n.
繁茂，茂盛;  活力，热情洋溢;  丰富，充沛;
The quality of being full of energy, excitement, and cheerfulness.  A state of overflowing abundance.
Her exuberance and zest for life were infectious. 她充沛的活力和对生活的热情很有感染力。
The garden was bursting with color and exuberance. 花园里充满了色彩和生机。
synonyms: ebullience, buoyancy, elation, vivacity, enthusiasm, energy, zeal
antonyms: apathy, dullness, listlessness, lifelessness
