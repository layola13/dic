enflower:/ɪnˈflaʊər/ | v. | 使繁荣，使兴旺；鼓励，激励 | To cause to flourish or prosper; to encourage or inspire. | The new arts program enfowered the community and brought a sense of pride to the residents. 新的艺术项目使社区繁荣起来，并给居民带来了一种自豪感。

The coach's words enfowered the team and gave them the confidence to win. 教练的话激励了球队，给了他们赢得比赛的信心。 | 近义词: encourage, inspire, uplift, empower, invigorate | 反义词: discourage, suppress, hinder, inhibit | 词性: 动词

词源:
From en- + flower (v.).  The verb "flower" meaning "to blossom" dates back to Middle English. The prefix "en-" adds the sense of "to cause to be" or "to put into a certain condition".
