bunker:/ˈbʌŋkər/| n. v.|掩体，燃料舱；加燃料|A reinforced underground shelter, typically for use in wartime.  To fill with fuel.|The soldiers took shelter in the bunker during the bombardment. 士兵们在轰炸期间躲进了掩体。|The ship bunkered in port before its transatlantic voyage. 这艘船在横渡大西洋之前在港口加了燃料。|近义词：shelter, dugout, hideout, fuel tank|反义词:exposure, open ground|词性:名词, 动词
bunkered:/ˈbʌŋkərd/| adj.|有掩体的，位于掩体中的|Protected by a bunker. |The bunkered troops were safe from the artillery fire.  位于掩体中的部队躲过了炮火。|近义词：protected, sheltered, safe|反义词:exposed, vulnerable|词性:形容词
