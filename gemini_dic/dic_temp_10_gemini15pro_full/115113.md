mangers:/ˈmænɪdʒərz/ | n. |经理，管理者|A person responsible for controlling or administering an organization or group of staff.|The company hired new mangers to oversee the expansion project. 公司聘请了新的经理来监督扩建项目。|近义词：supervisors, administrators, executives, directors|反义词：subordinates, employees|noun

mangers:/ˈmænɪdʒərz/ | n. |马槽，饲料槽|A long open box or trough from which horses or cattle feed.|The mangers were filled with hay for the horses. 马槽里装满了给马吃的干草。|近义词：troughs, cribs|反义词： (No direct antonym)|noun
