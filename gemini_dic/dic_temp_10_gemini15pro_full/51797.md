devoutest:/dɪˈvaʊtɪst/| adj.|最虔诚的，最衷心的|Superlative form of devout: having or showing deep religious feeling or commitment.|She was the devoutest member of the congregation, attending every service and prayer meeting.|近义词：pious, religious, dedicated, committed|反义词：irreverent, impious, faithless|词性:形容词
词源: From Old French *devot, from Latin dēvōtus (“devoted”), past participle of dēvovēre (“to vow, dedicate, devote”), from dē (“down, from”) + vovēre (“to vow”).
