contagionist:/kənˈteɪdʒənɪst/
n.
传染病学家；接触传染论者
A person who believes that diseases are spread by contact.
The contagionist argued that the disease was spread through physical touch.  传染病学家认为这种疾病是通过身体接触传播的。
The rise of contagionist theories led to improvements in sanitation.  接触传染论的兴起促进了卫生条件的改善。
Synonyms:  infectionist, epidemiologist
Antonyms:  miasma theorist
noun
