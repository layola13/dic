officiators:/əˈfɪʃieɪtərz/
noun
主祭人；主持仪式的人；官员
A person who performs a ceremony, especially a religious one; an official.
The officiators of the wedding ceremony were very professional. 婚礼仪式的主持人非常专业。
synonyms:officials, administrators, judges, referees, umpires, moderators, conductors, masters of ceremonies
antonyms:spectators, audience, participants, guests
n.
