fetuses:/ˈfiːtəsiz/| n. |胎儿（复数）|A fetus is an unborn offspring of a mammal, especially a human, from the end of the eighth week after conception until birth.  The stage of development before this is called an embryo.|The ultrasound showed two healthy fetuses. 超声波显示有两个健康的胎儿。|The fetuses are developing normally. 胎儿正在正常发育。|Synonyms: unborn baby, embryo (in earlier stages)|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, possibly infant, newborn, baby)|Noun
