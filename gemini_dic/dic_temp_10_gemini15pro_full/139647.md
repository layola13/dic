occultism:/əˈkʌltɪzəm/
n.
神秘学，玄学
The study or practice of occult arts, such as magic, alchemy, and astrology.
例句：He has a keen interest in occultism and the paranormal. 他对神秘学和超自然现象有浓厚的兴趣。

例句：The rise of occultism in the 19th century reflected a growing dissatisfaction with traditional religion. 19 世纪神秘学的兴起反映了人们对传统宗教日益增长的不满。

近义词：mysticism, esotericism, magic
反义词：science, rationalism
词性：noun
