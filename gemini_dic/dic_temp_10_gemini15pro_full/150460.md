pantheisms:/ˈpænθiːɪzəmz/
n.
泛神论，泛神信仰
The belief that God is everything and everywhere, or that God is the universe itself.
Emerson's transcendentalist views bordered on pantheisms. 爱默生的超验主义观点近乎泛神论。
近义词:pantheism, universalism
反义词:atheism, polytheism
词性:noun
