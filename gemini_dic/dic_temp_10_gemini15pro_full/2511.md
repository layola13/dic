administrational:/ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃənəl/
adjective
行政的，管理的
Relating to the process of managing or running an organization, business, or government.
The report highlighted several administrational problems within the company.  这份报告强调了公司内部的一些行政问题。

The administrational changes improved efficiency. 行政上的变革提高了效率。

近义词: administrative, managerial, executive
反义词: operational, non-managerial
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From administration + -al.  Ultimately from Latin administratio.
