concerting:/kənˈsɜːrtɪŋ/ | adj. | 协调的，一致的，共同策划的 |  Arranging something together; acting in cooperation or harmony.  | Example 1: They were concerting their efforts to raise money for the charity. 他们正在齐心协力为慈善机构筹款。

Example 2: The concerting of their plans took several weeks of meetings.  他们协调计划花了好几个星期的会议时间。 | Near synonyms: coordinating, collaborating, cooperating, harmonizing | Antonyms: conflicting, clashing, disagreeing | word property: adjective
