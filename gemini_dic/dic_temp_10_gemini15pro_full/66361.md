excusation:/ˌɛkskjuˈseɪʃən/ |n.|辩解；借口；饶恕|The act of excusing or being excused; an apology or explanation offered to justify or obtain forgiveness for a fault or offense.|He offered a weak excusation for his lateness. 他为他的迟到提出了一个站不住脚的借口。|近义词：excuse, apology, justification, plea, pretext|反义词：accusation, blame|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin excusatio(n-), from excusare ‘excuse’.
