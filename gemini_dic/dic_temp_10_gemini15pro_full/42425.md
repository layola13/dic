counterculturalism:/ˌkaʊntərˈkʌltʃərəlɪzəm/ | n. | 反文化主义 |  A social movement that opposes mainstream culture, often challenging traditional values and norms. | The hippie movement of the 1960s was a prime example of counterculturalism.  嬉皮士运动是反文化主义的典型例子。| 近义词: antiestablishmentarianism, nonconformity | 反义词:  conformism, traditionalism | noun

counterculture:/ˈkaʊntərˌkʌltʃər/ | n. | 反文化 | A culture with values and customs that differ substantially from or oppose those accepted by most of a society. | The punk rock scene is a vibrant counterculture. 朋克摇滚圈是一个充满活力的反文化。 | 近义词: subculture, underground | 反义词: mainstream culture, dominant culture | noun
