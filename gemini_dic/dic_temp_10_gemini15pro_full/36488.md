coleoptera:/ˌkoʊliˈɑːptərə/ | n. |鞘翅目（昆虫）| An order of insects having biting mouthparts and forewings modified to form hard protective coverings (elytra) for the hindwings when at rest.  This order includes beetles and weevils.| Beetles and weevils belong to the order Coleoptera. 甲虫和象鼻虫属于鞘翅目。| synonyms: beetle order | antonyms: None | noun

coleopteran:/ˌkoʊliˈɑːptərən/ | adj. |鞘翅目的| Relating to or belonging to the order Coleoptera.|  Coleopteran larvae are often called grubs. 鞘翅目幼虫通常被称为蛴螬。 | synonyms: belonging to Coleoptera | antonyms: None | adjective

coleopterist:/ˌkoʊliˈɑːptərɪst/ | n. |鞘翅目昆虫学家| A person who studies or specializes in coleoptera (beetles).| The coleopterist discovered a new species of beetle in the rainforest. 鞘翅目昆虫学家在雨林中发现了一种新的甲虫。 | synonyms: beetle specialist | antonyms: None | noun
