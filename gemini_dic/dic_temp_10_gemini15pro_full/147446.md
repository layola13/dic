overobsequious: /ˌoʊvərˌɒbsɪˈkwiːəs/ /ˌoʊvərˌɑːbsɪˈkwiːəs/ | adj. | 过分殷勤的，谄媚的 | Excessively eager to please or fawning.  Characterized by excessive deference and flattery. |  The overobsequious waiter kept hovering around the table, constantly refilling our water glasses and asking if we needed anything.  过分殷勤的服务员一直在桌子周围徘徊，不断地给我们倒水，问我们是否需要什么。|  He bowed overobsequiously to the queen. 他对女王卑躬屈膝地鞠躬。 | 近义词: fawning, servile, sycophantic, obsequious, unctuous | 反义词: aloof, distant, independent, supercilious | 词性: 形容词

词源:

From over- + obsequious.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," and "obsequious" comes from the Latin word "obsequiosus," meaning "compliant, obedient, dutiful."
