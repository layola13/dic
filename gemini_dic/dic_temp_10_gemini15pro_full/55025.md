disqualifies:/dɪsˈkwɑːlɪfaɪz/
verb
使…没有资格，取消…的资格
To make someone ineligible for something.
例句：
His poor eyesight disqualified him from military service.
他糟糕的视力使他无法服兵役。

The judge disqualified the runner for a false start.
裁判因抢跑取消了这名选手的资格。

近义词：bar, preclude, prohibit, debar, rule out
反义词：qualify, entitle, enable
词性：v.
