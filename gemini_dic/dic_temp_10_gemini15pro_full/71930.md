flicked:/flɪkt/| v. |轻弹，轻拍，快速地移动|A quick, sharp movement, often with a finger.  The act of hitting something lightly with a whip-like motion. To move or propel (something) with a sudden, light stroke or jerk. |He flicked the ash off his cigarette. 他弹掉了香烟上的灰烬。|Synonyms: flip, flick, twitch, jerk, snap|Antonyms: press, hold, caress|verb

flicked:/flɪkt/| adj. |(非正式)被轻弹的，被轻拍的 |Having been flicked; moved with a quick, light stroke. |(Rarely used as an adjective)  The flicked cigarette butt landed in the dry grass. 被弹掉的烟蒂落在了干草上。|Synonyms: flipped, twitched, jerked |Antonyms: pressed, held |adjective
