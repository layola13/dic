nonrevolutions:/ˌnɒnˌrɛvəˈluːʃənz/
noun
非革命，不发生革命
the absence of revolutions; periods of stability or gradual change rather than abrupt upheaval.
The historian specialized in the nonrevolutions of the 19th century, exploring the subtle shifts in power rather than the dramatic overthrows.
non-events, stability, gradualism
revolutions, upheavals
