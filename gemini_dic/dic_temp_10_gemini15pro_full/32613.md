chirpiness:/ˈtʃɜːrpɪnɪs/
n.
快乐；活泼；唧唧喳喳
the quality of being cheerful and lively, like the sound of a bird chirping.
Her chirpiness was infectious, brightening everyone's mood. 她的快乐很有感染力，照亮了每个人的心情。
liveliness, cheerfulness, lightheartedness
gloominess, sullenness, grumpiness
