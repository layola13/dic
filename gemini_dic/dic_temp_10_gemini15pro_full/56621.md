dongan:/ˈdoʊŋɡɑːn/ | n. | 东干人；东干语 |  A member of a group of Hui Muslims who migrated from northwestern China to Central Asia (primarily Kyrgyzstan and Kazakhstan) in the 19th century.  Also refers to their language, which is a dialect of Mandarin Chinese written in Cyrillic. | Example: The Dongans maintain a strong cultural identity, even after generations in Central Asia. 东干人即使在中亚生活了几代，仍然保持着强烈的文化认同。

Example:  He is studying the Dongan language, which is surprisingly similar to some Mandarin dialects. 他正在学习东干语，令人惊讶的是它与一些官话方言非常相似。 | 近义词:  Hui (sometimes), Muslim (broadly) | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: Noun
