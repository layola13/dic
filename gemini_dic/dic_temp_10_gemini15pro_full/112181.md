一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing right now? = 他现在在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you been up to lately? = 你最近在忙什么？
What has she learned in this class? = 她在这门课上学到了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all week? = 你整个星期都在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What time did she arrive? = 她几点到的？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 pm last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? = 她参加聚会穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I called? = 在我打电话之前你做了什么？
What had she eaten for breakfast? = 她早餐吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the power went out? = 停电之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 她转专业之前一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? = 你下周末要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be studying next semester? = 她下学期会学什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by the end of the day? = 到今天结束时你会完成什么？
What will she have learned by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时她会学到什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做了什么五年了？
What will she have been studying for by the time she gets her PhD? = 到她获得博士学位时她将已经学习什么多久了？
