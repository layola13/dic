cracker:/ˈkrækər/ (US), /ˈkrækə(r)/ (UK)| n.|薄脆饼干，咸饼干；鞭炮；古怪的人|A thin, crisp biscuit, often eaten with cheese or other savory toppings; a firecracker; an eccentric person.|He ate a cracker with some cheese. 他吃了一块饼干加了些奶酪。

The children were excited to light the crackers on Bonfire Night. 孩子们很兴奋要点燃篝火之夜的鞭炮。
