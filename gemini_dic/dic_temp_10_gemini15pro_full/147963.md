overresoluteness:/ˌoʊvərˌrɛzəˈluːtnəs/| n.|过度坚决; 过于果断|The general's overresoluteness led him to make a hasty decision that cost many lives. 将军的过度坚决导致他做出了一个草率的决定，使许多人丧生。|His overresoluteness in pursuing the deal alienated his partners. 他在追求这笔交易时的过度坚决疏远了他的合作伙伴。|近义词:doggedness, inflexibility, rigidity|反义词:hesitancy, indecision, vacillation|词性:名词

Etymology: over- + resoluteness
over-: prefix meaning "excessively," "too much"
resoluteness: noun form of resolute, from Latin resolutus "resolved," past participle of resolvere "to loosen, untie, dissolve; explain, solve; decide, determine," from re- "back, again" + solvere "to loosen, dissolve"
