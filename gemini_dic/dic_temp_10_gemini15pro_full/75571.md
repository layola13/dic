fud:/fʌd/| n.| 恐惧、不确定性和怀疑 (Fear, Uncertainty, and Doubt)| An acronym for "Fear, Uncertainty, and Doubt," FUD is often used to describe disinformation or negative information spread about a product, company, or idea, usually with the intent to discourage its adoption or support. |The company's competitors spread FUD about their new product to scare away potential customers. 该公司的竞争对手散布关于他们新产品的负面消息，以吓跑潜在客户。

There was a lot of FUD surrounding the new cryptocurrency, making investors hesitant.  围绕着这种新的加密货币有很多的恐惧、不确定性和怀疑，使得投资者犹豫不决。|近义词：misinformation, disinformation, propaganda|反义词: clarity, certainty, truth|词性: noun
