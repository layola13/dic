grower:/ˈɡroʊər/ (US), /ˈɡrəʊər/ (UK)| n. |种植者；栽培者；生长物|Someone who grows something, especially crops or plants.  A grower can also refer to something that grows well in a particular place or condition.|例句：He is a successful grape grower in the Napa Valley. 他是纳帕谷一位成功的葡萄种植者。

例句：These tomatoes are from a local grower. 这些西红柿来自当地的一位种植者。|近义词：cultivator, farmer, producer|反义词:consumer, buyer|词性:noun

grower:/ˈɡroʊər/ (US), /ˈɡrəʊər/ (UK)| n. |容易生长之物|Something that grows well in a particular place or condition.|例句：This area is a grower of fine wines. 这个地区盛产优质葡萄酒。

例句：Strawberries are a fast grower in warm climates. 草莓在温暖的气候下生长迅速。|近义词：flourisher, thrivers|反义词:non-grower, struggler|词性:noun
