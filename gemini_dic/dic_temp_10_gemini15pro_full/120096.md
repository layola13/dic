mimer:/ˈmaɪmər/ | n. | 模仿者; 哑剧演员; | A person who acts or performs using mime. |  He's a talented mimer, conveying complex emotions without a single word. 他是一位才华横溢的哑剧演员，不用一句话就能表达复杂的情感。 | Example:  The street mimer captivated the audience with his silent storytelling.  这位街头哑剧演员以其无声的故事讲述吸引了观众。| Synonyms: mime artist, pantomime, impersonator | Antonyms: speaker, orator | noun
