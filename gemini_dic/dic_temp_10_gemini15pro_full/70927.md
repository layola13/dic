firebrand:/ˈfaɪərbrænd/ (US), /ˈfaɪəbrænd/ (UK)| n.|煽动者；燃烧的木块；导火线|a person who stirs up trouble or incites others to rebel or fight; a piece of wood that is burning or on fire; something that causes trouble or violence|例句：She was a firebrand, always stirring up trouble. 她是个煽动者，总是挑起事端。

例句：The rebels threw firebrands into the houses. 叛乱分子把燃烧的木块扔进房子里。|近义词：agitator, incendiary, troublemaker, rabble-rouser, hothead, extremist|反义词：peacemaker, mediator, conciliator|词性:名词

firebrand:/ˈfaɪərbrænd/ (US), /ˈfaɪəbrænd/ (UK)| adj.|煽动性的|tending to stir up conflict|例句：His firebrand speeches roused the crowd to action. 他煽动性的演讲激起了群众的行动。

例句：The firebrand journalist was known for his controversial articles.  这位煽动性的记者以其有争议的文章而闻名。|近义词：inflammatory, provocative, incendiary|反义词：conciliatory, appeasing, calming|词性:形容词
