heathendoms:/ˈhiːðən.dəmz/
n.
异教，异教徒的信仰；异教世界
The state or condition of being a heathen; heathen lands or peoples collectively.
The missionaries worked tirelessly to convert the heathendoms of distant lands.  传教士们不知疲倦地工作，以改变遥远国度的异教信仰。
The explorer encountered many different heathendoms on his travels. 探险家在旅行中遇到了许多不同的异教世界。
近义词: paganism, heathenism, idolatry
反义词: Christianity, Islam, Judaism
词性: noun (plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hæðendōm, from hæðen "heathen" (see heathen) + -dōm "-dom."
