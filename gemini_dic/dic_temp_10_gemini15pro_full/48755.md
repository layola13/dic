definitive:/dɪˈfɪnətɪv/| adj.|最终的；决定性的；权威性的；明确的|Conclusive or authoritative; serving as a final settlement or decision.|This is the definitive biography of Abraham Lincoln. 这是亚伯拉罕·林肯的权威传记。|近义词：conclusive, final, ultimate, decisive|反义词：tentative, provisional, inconclusive|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *definitivus*, from Latin *definire* "to limit, define," from *de-* "completely" + *finire* "to end, limit."
