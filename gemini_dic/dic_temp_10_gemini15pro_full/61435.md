emboly:/ˈɛmbəli/| n. |栓塞; 栓子|The blockage of a blood vessel by an embolus (a detached clot or other foreign material).|A pulmonary emboly can be life-threatening. 肺栓塞可能危及生命。|Synonyms: embolism, blockage, obstruction|Antonyms: patency, opening|Noun

emboly:/ˈɛmbəli/| n. |插层; 夹层|The insertion or intercalation of something.|The emboly of this new genetic material altered the plant's characteristics.  这种新遗传物质的插入改变了植物的特性。|Synonyms: insertion, intercalation, interposition|Antonyms: removal, extraction|Noun
