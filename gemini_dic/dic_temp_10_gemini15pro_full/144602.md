outweed:/ˌaʊtˈwiːd/
verb
超过…的生长；长得比…快，长得比…茂盛
to grow faster and more vigorously than (another plant) and so prevent its development
The weeds have outweeded the flowers in the garden. 花园里的杂草长得比花还快，把花都盖住了。
近义词: outgrow, surpass
反义词: be stunted, wither
词性:v.
