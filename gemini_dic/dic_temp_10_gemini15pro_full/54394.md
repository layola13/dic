disimbitter:/ˌdɪsɪmˈbɪtər/
verb
消除苦味; 使不再怨恨
To free from bitterness; to sweeten; to make pleasant.
She tried to disimbitter the coffee with sugar. 她试图用糖来消除咖啡的苦味。
sweeten, alleviate, ameliorate
embitter, aggravate, worsen
