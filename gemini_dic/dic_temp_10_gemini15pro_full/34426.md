clause:/klɔːz/ (US) /klɔːz/ (UK)| n.|从句，条款|A group of words containing a subject and predicate and forming part of a compound or complex sentence.  A distinct article, stipulation, or provision in a document.|例句：The contract contains a clause that allows either party to terminate the agreement.  合同中包含一项允许任何一方终止协议的条款。|近义词：provision, stipulation, article, section|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "whole" in the context of referring to the entire sentence/document)|noun
