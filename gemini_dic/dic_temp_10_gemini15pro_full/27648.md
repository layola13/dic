candid:/ˈkændɪd/| adj.|坦率的，直言不讳的；真诚的；率直的；偷拍的|Characterized by openness and sincerity of expression; free from bias, prejudice, or malice; informal and unposed (especially of a photograph).|例句：He gave a candid interview about his experiences. 他坦率地讲述了自己的经历。

例句：The photographer captured a candid moment between the bride and groom. 摄影师捕捉到了新娘和新郎之间一个自然的瞬间。|近义词：frank, honest, straightforward, open, forthright, unbiased, impartial, unposed|反义词：dishonest, evasive, guarded, secretive, posed|词性:形容词
