factoring-in:/ˈfæktərɪŋ ɪn/ | phrasal verb | 考虑到，把…因素包括进去 | To include or consider something when making a decision or plan. |  The company is factoring in the rising cost of raw materials when setting prices.  公司在制定价格时考虑了原材料成本上涨的因素。

factoring-in:/ˈfæktərɪŋ ɪn/ | phrasal verb | 计入，算入 | To include something as part of a calculation or estimate. |  Have you factored in the cost of travel? 你把旅行费用算进去了吗？


factoring-in:/ˈfæktərɪŋ ɪn/ | phrasal verb | 将…纳入考量 | To take something into account or consideration. |  We need to factor in the potential risks before proceeding. 在继续进行之前，我们需要将潜在风险纳入考量。


ETYMOLOGY->
From "factor" (meaning an element contributing to a result) + "in" (adverb indicating inclusion).


SYNONYMS->
consider, account for, include, take into account, allow for


ANTONYMS->
disregard, ignore, exclude, omit
