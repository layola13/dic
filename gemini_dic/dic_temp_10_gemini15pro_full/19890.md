binoculars:/bɪˈnɒkjələz/ (UK) /bɪˈnɑːkjələrz/ (US)| n. |双筒望远镜|An optical instrument with two eyepieces, used for viewing distant objects.|He used binoculars to watch the birds. 他用双筒望远镜观察鸟类。|
近义词:field glasses, opera glasses|反义词:|名词


binocular:/bɪˈnɒkjələ(r)/ (UK) /bɪˈnɑːkjələr/ (US)| adj. |双目的，双筒的|Relating to or using both eyes.|Binocular vision is essential for depth perception. 双眼视觉对于深度感知至关重要。|近义词:stereoscopic|反义词:monocular|形容词
