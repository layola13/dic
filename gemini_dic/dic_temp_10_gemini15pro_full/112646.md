lowbell:/ˈloʊˌbɛl/| n.|牛铃；低沉的铃声|A bell, often hung from a cow's neck, that makes a low-pitched sound.|The lowbell clanged as the cow ambled through the pasture. 牛在牧场上漫步时，牛铃叮当作响。|Synonyms: cowbell, cattle bell|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps a high-pitched bell or chime)|noun
