frenetic:/frəˈnɛtɪk/| adj.|疯狂的，狂乱的，发狂似的|Characterized by fast and energetic activity, often in a wild or uncontrolled way.|The atmosphere in the trading room was frenetic.|近义词：frantic, frenzied, hectic, wild|反义词：calm, peaceful, relaxed, tranquil|词性:形容词
词源: From French *frénétique*, from Late Latin *phreneticus*, from Greek *phrenitikos*, from *phren* "mind, diaphragm (as the seat of emotions)."
