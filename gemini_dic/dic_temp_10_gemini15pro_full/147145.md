overloosely:/ˌoʊvərˈluːsli/
adverb
过于宽松地; 过于松散地
in a way that is too loose
The rope was tied overloosely and came undone.  绳子绑得太松散而解开了。
近义词: too loosely, excessively loosely
反义词: tightly, firmly, securely
词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + loosely.  Over- is a prefix meaning "excessively," and loosely is an adverb meaning "in a loose manner."
