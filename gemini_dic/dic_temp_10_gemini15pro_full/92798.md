icky:/ˈɪki/| adj.|令人作呕的；不舒服的；黏糊糊的|Something that is icky is unpleasant, often because it is sticky or slimy.  It can also refer to something that is slightly unpleasant or disgusting in a vague, nonspecific way.|The oatmeal had gone cold and icky. 燕麦粥变凉了，黏糊糊的，令人作呕。|近义词：gross, nasty, disgusting, repulsive, yucky|反义词:pleasant, agreeable, appealing|词性:形容词

Etymology:  Likely a playful alteration of "sticky," influenced by words like "yucky."  Its informal and expressive nature suggests a relatively recent origin, likely in the 20th century.
