oxidizer:/ˈɒksɪdaɪzər/ (US) /ˈɒksɪdaɪzə(r)/ (UK)| n.|氧化剂|A substance that oxidizes another substance; a substance that accepts electrons in an oxidation-reduction reaction.|Potassium nitrate is a strong oxidizer. 硝酸钾是一种强氧化剂。|Synonyms: oxidizing agent, oxidant|Antonyms: reducing agent, reductant|noun


oxidize:/ˈɒksɪdaɪz/ (US) /ˈɒksɪdaɪz/ (UK)| v.|氧化|Combine or become combined chemically with oxygen.|Iron oxidizes to form rust. 铁氧化形成锈。|Synonyms: rust, corrode, tarnish|Antonyms: reduce|verb
