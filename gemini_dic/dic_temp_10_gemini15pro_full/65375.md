eunuchize:/ˈjuːnəkʌɪz/ | vt. | 阉割；使失去男子气概 | To castrate or emasculate someone; to deprive of strength or vigor. |  The emperor eunuchized the young boys to serve in his palace.  皇帝阉割了小男孩们，让他们在宫里服侍。

The constant criticism from his boss eunuchized him, destroying his confidence.  老板不断的批评使他失去了男子气概，摧毁了他的自信心。| 近义词: castrate, emasculate, geld, neuter | 反义词: empower, strengthen | 词性: verb

Etymology: From Late Latin *eunuchizare, from Greek eunoukhizein, from eunoukhos (eunuch).
