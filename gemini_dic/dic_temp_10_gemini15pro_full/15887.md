balloonist:/bəˈluːnɪst/
n.
气球驾驶员；热气球运动员
A person who operates or travels in a balloon, especially a hot-air balloon.
The balloonist skillfully navigated the aircraft through the air currents.  气球驾驶员熟练地驾驶飞行器穿过气流。

The balloonist waved to the crowd below as he ascended into the sky.  气球驾驶员升上天空时向下面的人群挥手致意。

近义词：aeronaut, airman
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
