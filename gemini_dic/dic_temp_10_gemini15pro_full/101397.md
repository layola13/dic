what:/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US) |pron. det. adv.|什么，多么|Used to ask for information about something.|例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

What are you doing? 你在做什么？|近义词：which|反义词: N/A|pronoun

what:/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US) |det.|什么，哪个|Used to ask for information specifying something.|例句：What book are you reading? 你在读哪本书？

What color is your car?  你的车是什么颜色的？|近义词：which|反义词: N/A|determiner

what:/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US) |adv.|多么|Used to express surprise or other strong emotions.|例句：What a beautiful day! 多么美好的一天！

What a shame!  真可惜！|近义词：how|反义词: N/A|adverb

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she like to do in her free time? 她空闲时间喜欢做什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? 他晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned recently? 你最近学了什么？
What has she done to help? 她做了些什么来帮忙？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all week? 你整个星期都在忙什么？
What has he been reading lately? 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? 她对这部电影说了些什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you eaten before you came here? 你来这之前吃了什么？
What had she finished before the deadline?  她在截止日期前完成了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I called? 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been discussing for so long? 他们一直在讨论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 毕业后你会做什么？
What will they eat for lunch tomorrow? 他们明天午餐吃什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be studying next semester? 她下学期会学什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will they have done by the time we arrive?  在我们到达之前他们将完成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？
