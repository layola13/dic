bitangent:/baɪˈtændʒənt/ | n. adj.|双切线; 切于两点的|A line that is tangent to a curve at two distinct points. |The bitangent line touched the curve at two separate points. 这条双切线在两个不同的点与曲线相切。| A bitangent divides the area of the oval into two parts. 一条双切线将椭圆形的面积分成两部分。|近义词:double tangent|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps secant) |词性: noun, adjective
