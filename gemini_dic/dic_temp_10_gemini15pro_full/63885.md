epiloguing:/ˈɛpɪˌlɔːɡɪŋ/
verb
作结语；写结语
delivering or composing an epilogue.
The author was epiloguing his latest novel.  作者正在为他最新的小说写结语。
She is epiloguing the play with a moving speech about forgiveness. 她正在用一段关于宽恕的动人演讲为戏剧作结语。
近义词: concluding, summarizing, finishing
反义词: beginning, commencing, starting
词性: v.
