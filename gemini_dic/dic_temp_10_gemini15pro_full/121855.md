misrepresentative:/ˌmɪsrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪv/| adj.|失实的，歪曲的，不能代表的|Giving a false or misleading representation of something or someone.|The advertisement was misrepresentative, showing the car as much larger than it actually was. 这广告失实，把车显示得比实际大得多。|Synonyms: misleading, deceptive, distorted, inaccurate, false, fallacious|Antonyms: accurate, truthful, representative, faithful, veridical|adjective
