/bəˈθɛtɪk/ | adj. | 陈腐的，老套的，矫揉造作的 | Characterized by bathos; excessively sentimental or emotional, often in an insincere or exaggerated way.  Evoking pity, sadness, or compassion, especially through exaggerated or artificial means.  |  The movie's bathetic ending, complete with slow-motion doves and swelling music, left the audience unimpressed.  这部电影的结尾陈腐老套，配上慢动作的鸽子和膨胀的音乐，让观众毫无印象。

Her bathetic pleas for forgiveness were met with stony silence. 她矫揉造作的求饶声只换来了死一般的寂静。 | 近义词: mawkish, sentimental, trite, clichéd, hackneyed | 反义词:  genuine, sincere, understated | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek "bathos" (depth) + "-ic" (suffix forming adjectives).  Originally referred to a descent from the sublime to the ridiculous, but now commonly used to describe excessive sentimentality.
