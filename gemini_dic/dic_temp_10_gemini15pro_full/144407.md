outstrove:/ˌaʊtˈstrəʊv/
verb
胜过，超越
surpassed in effort; excelled.
He outstrove all his competitors in the race and won the gold medal. 他在比赛中胜过所有对手，赢得了金牌。
Synonyms: outdid, surpassed, excelled, exceeded, triumphed over
Antonyms:  failed, lost, succumbed, underperformed
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + strive.  "Out-" acts as a prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing. "Strive" comes from Middle English striven, from Old French estriver, of Germanic origin.
