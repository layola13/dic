fluavil:/ˈfluːəvɪl/| n. |黄沥青|A yellow, amorphous resinous constituent of gutta-percha, soluble in alcohol but not in ether.|Fluavil is a component separated from gutta-percha through specific chemical processes.  黄沥青是通过特定的化学工艺从古塔胶中分离出来的成分。|Fluavil is less dense than the other components found in gutta-percha. 黄沥青的密度低于古塔胶中的其他成分。|近义词:resin, gutta-percha component|反义词: None|词性:noun
