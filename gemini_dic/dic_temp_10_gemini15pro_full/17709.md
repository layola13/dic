bedazzlingly:/bɪˈdæzəlɪŋli/
adverb
令人眼花缭乱地；耀眼地
In a way that is brilliantly and dazzlingly impressive.
The fireworks display was bedazzlingly beautiful.  烟花表演美得令人眼花缭乱。
The actress wore a bedazzlingly glamorous gown.  这位女演员穿着一件耀眼迷人的礼服。
Synonyms: dazzlingly, brilliantly, resplendently, glitteringly
Antonyms: dully, plainly, simply
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From bedazzle + -ingly.  Bedazzle itself comes from be- + dazzle.  Dazzle ultimately originates from Old English *dasen, of uncertain origin.
