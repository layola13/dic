ballsier:/ˈbɔːlziər/ (US), /ˈbɔːlsiə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更有胆量的，更大胆的，更敢于冒险的|Comparative adjective of "ballsy," meaning showing courage, guts, or audacity. It implies a willingness to take risks or to be unconventional.|He made a ballsier move than anyone expected. 他做了一个比任何人预料的都更大胆的举动。|Synonyms: gutsier, bolder, more daring, more audacious|Antonyms: timid, cautious, cowardly|adjective

ballsy:/ˈbɔːlzi/ (US), /ˈbɔːlsi/ (UK)| adj.|有胆量的，大胆的，敢于冒险的|Informal adjective used to describe someone who is courageous, determined, and takes risks.|She's a ballsy woman who isn't afraid to speak her mind. 她是一个勇敢的女人，敢于表达自己的想法。|Synonyms: gutsy, bold, daring, audacious|Antonyms: timid, cautious, cowardly|adjective
