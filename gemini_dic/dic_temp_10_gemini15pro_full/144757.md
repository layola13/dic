over-communication:/ˌoʊvər kəmˌjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən/
|n.|过度沟通|The act of communicating excessively, often to the point of redundancy or inefficiency.|Example: Over-communication can sometimes lead to confusion rather than clarity. 过度沟通有时会导致混乱而不是清晰。 Example:  He bombarded her with emails, a classic case of over-communication. 他不停地给她发邮件，这是一个典型的过度沟通的例子。|Synonyms: oversharing, excessive communication|Antonyms: under-communication, lack of communication|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"over-" (prefix): from Old English "ofer," meaning "above," "across," "beyond"
"communication": from Late Latin "communicationem,"  meaning "sharing," "participation," derived from "communicare," meaning "to share," "to impart," related to "communis," meaning "common"
