megera:/məˈɡɛərə/ | n. |泼妇，悍妇|A shrewish or сварливая woman; a termagant.|She's a real megera, always shouting and complaining. 她真是个泼妇，总是大喊大叫，抱怨个不停。|例句：The landlord's wife was a megera, making life miserable for all the tenants. 房东太太是个泼妇，让所有房客的生活都很痛苦。 |近义词：shrew, virago, harpy, termagant|反义词:angel, saint|词性:名词

megera:/məˈɡɛərə/ | n. |悍妇，凶婆|A quarrelsome, bad-tempered woman.|He married a megera who made his life hell. 他娶了一个悍妇，让他生活在地狱里。|例句：Don't be such a megera!  别这么凶！|近义词：harridan, vixen|反义词:sweetheart, darling|词性:名词


词源: From Latin *Megara*, from Greek *Μέγαιρα* (Mégaira), one of the Erinyes (Furies).
