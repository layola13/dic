hylopathism:/ˈhaɪləˌpæθɪzəm/ | n. |万物有灵论；泛灵论|The belief that matter, especially wood, has consciousness and can feel pain.|Indigenous cultures often incorporate hylopathism into their understanding of the natural world. 土著文化通常将万物有灵论融入他们对自然世界的理解。|Near synonyms: animism, panpsychism|Antonyms: materialism, mechanism|noun
