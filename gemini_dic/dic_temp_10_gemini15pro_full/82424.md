gruesomer:/ˈɡruːsəmər/ | adj. | 更可怕的，更令人毛骨悚然的 | More gruesome; more causing repulsion or horror. | The sequel was even gruesomer than the original film.  续集比原电影更可怕。|  The details of the crime were gruesomer than the newspapers reported.  犯罪细节比报纸报道的更令人毛骨悚然。 | 近义词:ghastlier, more horrific, more grisly | 反义词: less gruesome, less horrific, pleasant | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From gruesome + -er (comparative suffix).  Gruesome itself comes from Middle English grusome, probably related to Old Norse grysja "to shudder."
