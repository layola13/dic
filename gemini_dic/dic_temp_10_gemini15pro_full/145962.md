overdistorted:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈstɔːrtɪd/ | adj. |过度扭曲的，过度失真的| Excessively distorted; distorted to an excessive degree.  The word combines the prefix "over-" (meaning "excessively") with the past participle of the verb "distort" (meaning "to twist out of shape"). |The image was so overdistorted it was barely recognizable.  这张图片过度失真，几乎无法辨认。| The guitar solo was overdistorted and unpleasant to listen to. 吉他独奏过度失真，听起来很不舒服。 | synonyms: exaggerated, warped, misshapen | antonyms: accurate, undistorted, clear | adjective

Etymology:
"Over-" (Old English *ofer) + "distort" (from Latin distortus, past participle of distorquēre, from dis- "apart, asunder" + torquēre "to twist")
