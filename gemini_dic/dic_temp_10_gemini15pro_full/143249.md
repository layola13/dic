outdesigned:/ˌaʊtdɪˈzaɪnd/
verb
在设计上胜过
To have designed something in a superior or more effective way than another.
Tesla outdesigned its competitors with its sleek and innovative electric vehicles.  特斯拉凭借其时尚和创新的电动汽车在设计上胜过了竞争对手。
The new bridge was outdesigned by the older, more robust structure. 新桥在设计上不如更古老、更坚固的结构。
synonym:surpassed, outmatched, eclipsed
antonym:outmoded, outdated
词性:动词
