barnyards:/ˈbɑːrnjɑːrdz/
n.
农家场院；谷仓前的空地
An area surrounding a barn, typically used to house livestock.
The chickens pecked at the grains scattered across the barnyard.  鸡在谷仓前的空地上啄食散落的谷粒。
