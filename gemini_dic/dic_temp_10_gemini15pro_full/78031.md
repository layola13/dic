gents:/dʒɛnts/| n.|先生们；各位|A contraction of "gentlemen," used as a polite way to address a group of men.|例句：Good evening, gents. 晚上好，先生们。|例句：Right this way, gents, the show is about to begin. 先生们，这边请，演出马上就要开始了。|近义词：gentlemen, sirs, fellows|反义词:ladies, women|名词
