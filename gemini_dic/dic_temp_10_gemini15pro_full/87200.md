hereafterward:/ˌhɪərˈæftərwərd/ | adv. | 此后，以后 | From now on; subsequently. |  Hereafterward, he resolved to be more careful with his spending. 此后，他决定更加谨慎地花钱。

Hereafterward, the two families lived in peace. 此后，这两个家族和平相处。 | 近义词: subsequently, afterward, thereafter, henceforth | 反义词: previously, beforehand, heretofore | 词性: 副词

词源:  "hereafterward" is a combination of "hereafter" and "ward."  "Hereafter" comes from Old English "hēræfter," meaning "after this," with "hēr" meaning "here" and "æfter" meaning "after." "Ward" is a suffix indicating direction, derived from Old English "weard."
