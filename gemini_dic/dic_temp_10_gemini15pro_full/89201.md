homesickness: /ˈhoʊmsɪknɪs/ (US) /ˈhəʊmsɪknɪs/ (UK)| n.|想家，思乡病|A feeling of longing for one's home.|例句：She suffered from terrible homesickness during her first semester at college.  她大学第一学期非常想家。

例句：He tried to combat his homesickness by calling his family every day. 他每天都给家人打电话以克服想家之情。


近义词：nostalgia, yearning|反义词: contentment|词性:noun
