bucking:/ˈbʌkɪŋ/ | adj. | 猛然震荡的；赚钱的 | resisting; earning money | The bucking bronco threw the rider. 猛然弓背跳跃的野马把骑手摔了下来。| 近义词：resisting, opposing, lucrative | 反义词：yielding, submitting, unprofitable | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From Middle English *bukken, from Old English buccing (“male deer, buck”), equivalent to buck +‎ -ing. Cognate with Scots buck (“male deer, goat”). More at buck.
