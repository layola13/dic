furled:/fɜːrld/ | adj. | 卷起的，收拢的 | Rolled or gathered up closely or compactly. | The sailor furled the sails. 水手卷起了帆。| The flag was furled after the ceremony.  仪式结束后，旗帜被卷了起来。|  Synonyms: rolled, folded, gathered, tucked | Antonyms: unfurled, spread, open | adjective

Etymology->
From Middle English *furlen, *furlen, from Old French ferler (“to furl”), from Frankish *furljan, from Proto-Germanic *furþjaną (“to support, further, promote”).
