engine:/ˈendʒɪn/ | n. |引擎，发动机；机车，火车头；工具 |A machine with moving parts that converts power into motion. |The car's engine sputtered and died. 汽车的发动机嘎嘎作响，然后熄火了。|Synonyms: motor, machine, apparatus|Antonyms:  (Engine doesn't have a direct opposite.  You could consider things that stop motion like "brake" or "impediment") | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French engin (meaning skill, wit, ingenuity, machine, device), from Latin ingenium (meaning inborn quality, talent, intelligence, clever invention).  The meaning shifted from mental ability to the product of that ability – machines and devices.
