microfilm: /ˈmaɪkrəʊfɪlm/ (US) /ˈmaɪkrəʊfɪlm/ (UK)| n. |缩微胶卷；缩微胶片|A film on which materials are photographed at greatly reduced size. |例句：The library has a large collection of newspapers on microfilm. 图书馆收藏了大量的缩微胶卷报纸。

例句：The original documents were stored and microfilm copies were made available to the public. 原始文件被保存起来，并向公众提供缩微胶卷副本。


近义词：microfiche |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "original document") |词性: noun
