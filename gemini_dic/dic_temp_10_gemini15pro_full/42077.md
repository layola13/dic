coticular:/kəˈtɪkjʊlə(r)/
adjective
表皮的；角质层的
relating to or like a cuticle
The coticular layer of a plant protects it from water loss.  植物的表皮层保护它免于水分流失。

近义词: epidermal, cutaneous
反义词: None

词性: adjective
