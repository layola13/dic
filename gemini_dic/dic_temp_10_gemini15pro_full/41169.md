It refers to the tendency to mimic or copy the actions, behaviors, or ideas of others. |  Her designs are full of copycatisms, lacking any originality. 她的设计充满了抄袭，缺乏任何 originality. |  The industry is rife with copycatisms, with many companies simply replicating successful products.  这个行业充斥着抄袭行为，许多公司只是简单地复制成功的产品。 | Synonyms: imitation, mimicry, emulation,  aping | Antonyms: originality, innovation, creativity | noun
