decarbonised: /diːˈkɑːrbənaɪzd/ | adj. | 脱碳的 |  Having had carbon removed, or relating to the process of removing carbon.  Specifically, reducing carbon dioxide emissions. | The UK aims to have a fully decarbonised electricity system by 2035.  英国的目标是在2035年之前实现电力系统完全脱碳。| The company invested in decarbonised technologies to reduce its environmental impact. 该公司投资脱碳技术以减少其对环境的影响。|  Synonyms: carbon-neutral, low-carbon, green | Antonyms: carbon-intensive, polluting | adjective
