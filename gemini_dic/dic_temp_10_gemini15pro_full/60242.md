effront: /ɪˈfrʌnt/ | n. |  厚颜无耻，放肆 | A bold and disrespectful act or remark. | His effrontery shocked everyone in the room. 他的厚颜无耻震惊了房间里的所有人。 | Synonyms: impudence, audacity, boldness | Antonyms: respect, humility, modesty | 词性: 名词

词源: From French *effronterie*, from *effronté* "shameless, insolent," past participle of *effronter* "to make shameless, make brazen," from *ex-* "out" (see ex-) + *front* "forehead" (see front (n.)).  The notion is of having a hardened, shameless forehead.
