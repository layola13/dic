gauntree:/ˈɡɔːntri/ | n. |  绞刑架;  | A wooden frame, typically consisting of two upright posts and a crossbeam, used for hanging criminals. | The condemned man was led to the gauntree. 那个被判刑的人被带上了绞刑架。|  gallows, gibbet | scaffold, platform | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *galowtre*, from Old Norse *galgtr* ("gallows").  Related to Old English *galga* ("gallows").
