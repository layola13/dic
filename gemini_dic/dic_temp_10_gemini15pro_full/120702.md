misagree:/ˌdɪsəˈɡriː/ /ˌdɪsəˈɡriː/ | vi.| 不同意，有分歧 |To have a different opinion or view from someone else.|  I disagree with his assessment of the situation. 我不同意他对形势的评估。

The two experts disagreed about the cause of the problem.  两位专家对问题的起因意见不一。

|近义词：differ, dissent, object|反义词：agree, concur, consent|verb|

misagreement:/ˌdɪsəˈɡriːmənt/ /ˌdɪsəˈɡriːmənt/ | n.| 意见不合，分歧 |A difference of opinion or view.| There was considerable disagreement about the best course of action.  对于最佳行动方案存在相当大的分歧。

The disagreement between the two parties led to a stalemate. 双方之间的分歧导致了僵局。

|近义词：conflict, difference, dispute|反义词：agreement, accord, harmony|noun|
