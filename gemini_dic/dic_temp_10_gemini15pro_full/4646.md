alebench: /ˈeɪlˌbɛntʃ/ | n. | 酒馆长凳，酒吧长凳 | A long bench or table in an alehouse or pub. |  He sat on the alebench, nursing a pint. 他坐在酒吧长凳上，慢慢地喝着一品脱啤酒。|  The alebench was crowded with patrons. 酒吧长凳上挤满了顾客。 | synonyms: bench, settle, pew | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, possibly chair, stool if emphasizing individual seating) | noun
