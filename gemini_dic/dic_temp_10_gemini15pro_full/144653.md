outwrenched:/ˌaʊtˈrɛntʃt/
verb
扭伤，扳伤，猛扭
To wrench more violently or effectively than another.  To surpass in wrenching; to overpower by wrenching.
He outwrenched his opponent and gained possession of the ball. 他猛扭对手，夺得了球。
近义词: wrest, wring
反义词:  (No clear single antonym, perhaps "yield")
词性: verb
