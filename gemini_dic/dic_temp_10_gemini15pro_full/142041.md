organological:/ˌɔːrɡənəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
器官学的；乐器学的
Relating to the scientific study of musical instruments, their classification, development, and construction.
The museum boasts an impressive organological collection, showcasing instruments from around the world.  博物馆拥有令人印象深刻的乐器学藏品，展示了来自世界各地的乐器。
The organological study of ancient flutes reveals much about the musical practices of past civilizations. 对古代长笛的乐器学研究揭示了许多关于过去文明音乐实践的信息。
