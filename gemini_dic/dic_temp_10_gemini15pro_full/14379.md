注意事项:

词源： "auto-" (self) + "optical" (relating to sight or light).  "Auto-" comes from Greek "autos" (self). "Optical" comes from Late Latin "opticus" and Greek "optikos" (of or for sight).

这个词本身并不常见，更多的是用其组成部分"auto"和"optical"来描述具有自动光学功能的系统或设备。
