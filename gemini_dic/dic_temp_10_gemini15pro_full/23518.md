brain-damaged: /ˈbreɪn ˌdæmɪdʒd/
adjective
脑损伤的
Having lasting damage to the brain, which can cause problems with thinking, movement, or behavior.
The brain-damaged patient struggled with basic motor skills.  这个脑损伤的病人连基本的运动技能都有困难。
He suffered brain damage in the accident and now has difficulty speaking. 他在事故中脑部受损，现在说话有困难。

近义词: brain-injured, impaired
反义词: healthy, unimpaired
词性: adjective
