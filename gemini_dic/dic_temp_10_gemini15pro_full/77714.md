gemmed:/dʒemd/ | adj. |镶嵌宝石的；饰以宝石的 | Adorned or decorated with gems. | The queen wore a gemmed crown. 女王戴着镶嵌宝石的王冠。 |  The actress wore a gemmed necklace.  这位女演员戴着一条宝石项链。 | 近义词： jeweled, bejeweled | 反义词: plain, unadorned | 词性:形容词
词源: From the verb "gem" (meaning to adorn with gems) + "-ed" (past participle suffix).  Ultimately from Latin "gemma" (bud, gem).
