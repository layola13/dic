i've:/aɪv/| contraction |我已，我曾经|Contraction of "I have".  It combines the pronoun "I" and the auxiliary verb "have".|I've finished my homework. 我已经完成了我的作业。|I've been to Paris twice. 我去过巴黎两次。|近义词: (No direct synonym for a contraction, but synonyms for "I have" would be depending on context: I possess, I own, I've experienced)|反义词:(No direct antonym, but antonyms for "have" might be lack, need)|contraction


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I've a cat. = 我有一只猫。
I've many friends. = 我有很多朋友。(Less common in modern English, but still grammatically correct.)

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
(I've is not used in present continuous. The full form "I have been..." is used.)
I have been working on this project all day. = 我一整天都在做这个项目。
I have been thinking about you. = 我一直在想你。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I've seen that movie before. = 我以前看过那部电影。
I've just finished my dinner. = 我刚吃完晚饭。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I've been studying English for five years. = 我学习英语已经五年了。
I've been waiting for you for an hour. = 我已经等了你一个小时了。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
(I've is not used in past tenses. The past form is "I had".)
I had a great time at the party. = 我在派对上玩得很开心。
I had already finished my work when you called. = 你打电话的时候我已经完成工作了。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
(I've is not used in past continuous. The past form is "I had been...")
I had been working on the project for months. = 我已经在这个项目上工作了好几个月了。
I had been waiting for her arrival. = 我一直在等她到来。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
(I've is not used in past perfect. The past perfect form is "I had had".)
I had had breakfast before I left. = 我离开之前吃过早餐了。
I had had that car for ten years before I sold it. = 我卖掉那辆车之前已经开了十年了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
(I've is not used in past perfect continuous. The form is "I had been...")
I had been studying all night. = 我整夜都在学习。
I had been living in London for five years before I moved to Paris. = 在我搬到巴黎之前，我在伦敦住了五年。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
(I've is not used in simple future. The future form is "I will have".)
I will have finished the project by next week. = 我下周之前会完成这个项目。
I will have enough money to buy a car soon. = 我很快就会有足够的钱买车了。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
(I've is not used in future continuous. The future continuous form is "I will have been...")
I will have been working here for ten years next year. = 明年我在这里工作就满十年了。
I will have been travelling for a month by the time I get home. = 我到家的时候，我已经旅行了一个月了。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
(I've is not used in future perfect. The future perfect form is "I will have...")
I will have finished the book by the end of the week. = 我将在周末前读完这本书。
I will have visited ten countries by the time I'm thirty. = 到我三十岁的时候，我将已经访问过十个国家了。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
(I've is not used in future perfect continuous.  The form is "I will have been...")
I will have been living here for twenty years by 2043. = 到 2043 年，我将已经在这里住了二十年了。
I will have been studying French for two years by next summer. = 到明年夏天，我学习法语就两年了。


虚拟语气:
If I've offended you, I apologize. = 如果我冒犯了你，我道歉。(Less common but grammatically acceptable)
I wish I've more time. = 我希望我有更多时间。（Less common and often considered incorrect.  "I wish I had more time" is preferred.)

被动语态:
(I've is not used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
(I've is rarely used in formal questions, especially at the beginning.  "Have I..." is generally preferred)
Have I met you before? = 我以前见过你吗？
Have I done something wrong? = 我做错什么了吗？

否定句:
I've not finished yet (more common in British English). = 我还没有完成.
I haven't finished yet (more common in American English). = 我还没有完成。
