coulure:/kuːˈljʊər/| n.|落果；流胶|The excessive rain caused coulure in the vineyards, resulting in a smaller harvest this year. 过多的雨水导致葡萄园落果，导致今年的收成减少。|Coulure is a physiological disorder that can affect grapevines. 流胶是一种可能影响葡萄藤的生理障碍。|例句：The vineyard experienced significant coulure due to the unseasonable frost.  由于不合时宜的霜冻，葡萄园出现了严重的落果。

The heavy rains contributed to the coulure, reducing the grape yield.  暴雨导致落果，减少了葡萄产量。|近义词：shatter, abscission|反义词:|noun
