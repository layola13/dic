cozinesses:/ˈkoʊzinɪsɪz/
n.
舒适；温馨；惬意

The room exuded a sense of cozinesses with its soft lighting and plush furnishings. 房间里柔和的灯光和豪华的家具营造出一种舒适感。
The multiple fireplaces added to the cozinesses of the ski lodge.  多个壁炉增加了滑雪小屋的舒适感。
comfort, snugness, warmth
discomfort, coldness, bleakness
