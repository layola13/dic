antiepileptic:/ˌæntiˌɛpɪˈlɛptɪk/
antiepileptic| adj. |抗癫痫的|Preventing or controlling epileptic seizures.|The doctor prescribed an antiepileptic medication. 医生开了抗癫痫药。|Synonym: anticonvulsant|Antonym: convulsant|adjective
antiepileptic| n. |抗癫痫药|A medication used to prevent or control epileptic seizures.|She takes antiepileptics to manage her epilepsy. 她服用抗癫痫药来控制癫痫。|Synonym: anticonvulsant|Antonym: convulsant|noun

Etymology:
anti- (Greek) "against" + epileptic (from epilepsy, derived from Greek epilepsia "seizure")
