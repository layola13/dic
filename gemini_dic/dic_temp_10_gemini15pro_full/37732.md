compartmentalized:/kəmˌpɑːrtˈmentəlaɪzd/
| adj. | 分隔的，划分成各自独立部分的 | Divided into separate sections or categories. |  The human brain is highly compartmentalized, with different areas responsible for different functions. 人脑高度分隔化，不同的区域负责不同的功能。 |  She compartmentalized her life, keeping her work and personal life strictly separate. 她将自己的生活划分开来，将工作和个人生活严格分开。 |  segmented, categorized, partitioned, sectioned, divided | integrated, unified, holistic | adjective


compartmentalise:/kəmˌpɑːrtˈmentəlaɪz/
| v. | 分隔，划分 | To divide something into separate sections or categories. |  He compartmentalizes his emotions, never letting his feelings interfere with his work. 他将自己的情绪分隔开来，从不让自己的感情干扰工作。 | The company compartmentalized its operations to improve efficiency.  公司将其运营划分开来以提高效率。 | categorize, classify, divide, separate, section | integrate, unify, combine | verb



ETYMOLOGY->
From compartment + -alize/-ise. Compartment ultimately comes from the Latin word "compartiri," meaning "to share with."
