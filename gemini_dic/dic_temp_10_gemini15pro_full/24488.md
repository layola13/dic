brittling:/ˈbrɪtlɪŋ/| n. |脆化，变脆|The process of becoming brittle.  The brittling of the metal made it unsafe to use.|例句：The brittling of the plastic over time made it crack.  随着时间的推移，塑料的脆化导致其破裂。

例句：Exposure to sunlight caused the brittling of the rubber seals.  暴露在阳光下导致橡胶密封圈变脆。|近义词：embrittlement, hardening|反义词: softening, toughening|词性:名词

brittling:/ˈbrɪtlɪŋ/| adj. |使变脆的|Causing something to become brittle.|例句：The brittling effect of the cold weather was evident on the tree branches. 寒冷天气造成的脆化效应在树枝上很明显。


例句：The brittling agent weakened the material's structure.  脆化剂削弱了材料的结构。|近义词：embrittling|反义词: softening, toughening|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "brittle" + "-ing" (present participle suffix).  "Brittle" comes from the Old English "brytel" meaning "fragile, easily broken."
