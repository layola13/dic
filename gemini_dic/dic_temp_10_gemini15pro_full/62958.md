ennoble:/ɪˈnoʊbəl/ /ɛnˈnəʊbəl/| vt. |使高贵，使崇高|To give someone a noble rank or title. To make something or someone seem better or more worthy of respect. |The king ennobled him for his bravery in battle. 国王因他在战斗中的英勇而授予他贵族爵位。

He felt ennobled by their trust in him.  他们的信任让他感到高尚。

|近义词：elevate, uplift, exalt, dignify|反义词：degrade, demean, abase|词性：动词
