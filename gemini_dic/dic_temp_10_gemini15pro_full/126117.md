muteness:/ˈmjuːtnəs/| n.|沉默，缄默，哑|The muteness of the witness was unsettling.  证人的沉默令人不安。|例句：The sudden muteness of the child worried his parents.  孩子的突然沉默让他的父母很担心。

Her muteness was interpreted as agreement. 她的沉默被理解为同意。|近义词：silence, taciturnity, dumbness|反义词：loquacity, talkativeness|词性：名词

词源: Late Middle English: from mute + -ness.
