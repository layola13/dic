myotomes: /ˈmaɪəˌtoʊmz/ | n. | 肌节 |  One of a series of segments of skeletal muscle innervated by the pair of spinal nerves associated with a single spinal segment. |  Damage to specific myotomes can result in weakness in predictable muscle groups. 特定肌节的损伤会导致可预测的肌肉群无力。|  The physician tested the patient's myotomes to determine the location of the spinal cord injury. 医生检查了病人的肌节，以确定脊髓损伤的位置。 | synonyms: muscle segments, muscle groups | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "whole muscle" or "undifferentiated mesoderm" in a developmental context) | noun

myotome: /ˈmaɪəˌtoʊm/ | n. | 肌节 |  A group of muscles innervated by a single spinal nerve root. |  The doctor tested the patient's C5 myotome by asking him to abduct his arm. 医生通过让病人外展手臂来测试他的C5肌节。|  Weakness in the L4 myotome can affect ankle dorsiflexion. L4肌节的无力会影响踝关节的背屈。 | synonyms: muscle group, muscle segment | antonyms: (No direct antonym) | noun
