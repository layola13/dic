overseed:/ˌoʊvərˈsiːd/| v. | 补播;  在…上过度播种; 超量播种 | To sow seed over (an area) that has already been seeded, especially with a different kind of grass seed. | The golf course is overseeded with ryegrass in the winter.  高尔夫球场在冬天补播黑麦草。|  The lawn was overseeded with a fast-growing variety of grass. 草坪上补播了一种生长迅速的草。 | resow, replant | - | verb
