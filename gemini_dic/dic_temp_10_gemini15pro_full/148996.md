ovoviviparity:/ˌoʊvoʊˌvaɪvɪˈpærɪti/ | n. | 卵胎生 | A mode of reproduction in which eggs are retained within the mother's body until they hatch, but the developing embryos derive nourishment solely from the yolk within the egg, not from the mother directly. |  The shark exhibits ovoviviparity, with the eggs hatching inside the female's body.  鲨鱼表现出卵胎生，卵在雌性体内孵化。|  近义词: aplacental viviparity | 反义词: oviparity, viviparity | 词性: noun

ovoviviparous:/ˌoʊvoʊˌvaɪvɪˈpærəs/ | adj. | 卵胎生的 | Producing young by means of eggs which are hatched within the body of the parent. | The ovoviviparous lizard gives birth to live young.  这种卵胎生的蜥蜴产下活的幼崽。| 近义词: aplacental viviparous | 反义词: oviparous, viviparous | 词性: adjective
