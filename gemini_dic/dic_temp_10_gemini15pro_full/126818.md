naped:/neɪpt/
adjective
有...颈背的，有...后颈的
Having a nape of a specified kind.  The nape is the back of the neck.
The yellow-naped amazon is a popular pet parrot.  黄颈亚马逊鹦鹉是一种很受欢迎的宠物鹦鹉。

近义词:  none (This word is quite specific, it's hard to find a true synonym)
反义词:  none

词性: adjective
词源: From *nape* + *-ed*.  *Nape* comes from Old English *hnaepp*, and ultimately from Proto-Germanic *knappaz*.
