pair-off:/ˈper ɒf/ /ˈper ɑːf/| vi.|配对，成对离开，(使)成对结合|To form pairs or become partners, often for a specific purpose, or to leave a larger group in pairs.|The teacher paired off the students for the project. 老师将学生们配对完成这个项目。|The guests paired off and started dancing. 客人们成双成对地开始跳舞。|近义词：partner up, couple up|反义词:separate, split|词性:动词

pair-off:/ˈper ɒf/ /ˈper ɑːf/| n.|配对，一对|A pairing or a couple.|The pair-off for the tennis match was decided by a draw. 网球比赛的配对是由抽签决定的。|They made a cute pair-off. 他们真是可爱的一对。|近义词：couple, pair|反义词:individual, single|词性:名词

词源： "pair" 源自古法语 "paire," 最终源自拉丁语 "paria," 指的是一对相等或相似的东西。“off”表示分离或离开。因此，"pair off" 字面意思是“成对分离”。
