cyanogen:/saɪˈænədʒən/ | n. | 氰，氰基|A colorless, poisonous, flammable gas, (CN)₂ , with a pungent almond-like odor.  It is used in organic synthesis and as a fumigant.|Cyanogen is a highly toxic gas.| 近义词: dicyan | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

cyanogen:/saɪˈænədʒən/ | adj. | 产生氰的；含氰的 |Producing or containing cyanide. | Cyanogen compounds are highly toxic. | 近义词: cyanide | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective

词源:  From French cyanogène, from cyano- + -gène (“-gen”).  Ultimately from Ancient Greek κύανος (kuanos, “dark blue”) (because of the color of Prussian blue, a cyanide compound) and γεννάω (gennáō, “I generate; I produce”).
