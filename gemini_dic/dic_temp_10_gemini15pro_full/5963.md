ambrotype: /ˈæmbrətaɪp/ | n. | 银版照相法；银版照片 | A photograph made by a process in which a negative image is produced on a glass plate coated with collodion. |  Example: The museum displayed a collection of ambrotypes from the 19th century.  博物馆展出了一批19世纪的银版照片。 | Example:  She found an ambrotype of her great-grandmother in the attic. 她在阁楼里找到了一张她曾祖母的银版照片。| 近义词: tintype, ferrotype | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "digital photograph" in a modern context) | 词性: noun
