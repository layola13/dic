overemotionally:/ˌoʊvərɪˈmoʊʃənəli/
adverb
过于情绪化地; 情绪激动地
in a way that shows too much emotion
She reacted overemotionally to the news. 她对这个消息反应过度情绪化。
Synonyms: excessively emotionally, too emotionally, hysterically, dramatically
Antonyms: calmly, composedly, impassively
词性: 副词


Etymology:
From over- + emotionally.  "Over-" acts as a prefix indicating excess or too much. "Emotionally" derives from "emotion" + "-ally." "Emotion" traces back to Old French "emouvoir" (to stir up, excite), ultimately from Latin "emovere" (to move out, remove, agitate).
