greisen:/ˈɡraɪzən/ | n. | 伟晶岩 | A coarse-grained igneous rock typically composed of quartz, mica, and feldspar, formed by alteration of granite by hydrothermal fluids. | The geologist identified the sample as greisen.  地质学家将该样品鉴定为伟晶岩。| The miners discovered a vein of greisen rich in tin ore. 矿工们发现了一条富含锡矿石的伟晶岩脉。 | Synonyms: altered granite, granite pneumatolyte | Antonyms: N/A | noun
