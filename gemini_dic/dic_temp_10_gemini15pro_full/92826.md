iconologies:/ˌaɪkəˈnɒlədʒiz/
noun
图像学；图像志；图像解释学
The study of the interpretation of images, symbols, and other forms of visual representation, especially in art.  It investigates the cultural and historical context of these representations and their significance.
Her latest book delves into the iconologies of medieval manuscripts. 她最新的书深入研究了中世纪手稿的图像学。
iconography, symbology, semiotics
