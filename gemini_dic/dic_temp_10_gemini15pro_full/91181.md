hydras:/ˈhaɪdrəz/| n.|九头蛇（Hydra的复数）；水螅（hydra的复数）|A many-headed serpent in Greek mythology that if one head is cut off, two grow in its place.  Also, a freshwater polyp with a tubular body and a ring of tentacles around the mouth. |The myth of the Lernaean Hydra is one of the most famous in Greek mythology.  勒拿九头蛇的神话是希腊神话中最著名的神话之一。

The pond was teeming with hydras and other microscopic life.  池塘里充满了水螅和其他微生物。|近义词：monster, serpent (for mythological Hydra), polyp (for biological hydra)|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "hero" in the mythological context, as Hercules defeated the Hydra)|Noun
