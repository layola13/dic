mittyesque: /mɪtiˈɛsk/
adjective
米蒂式的；白日梦似的；爱幻想的
Having characteristics of or resembling Walter Mitty, a fictional character known for his elaborate daydreams.  Characterized by unrealistic and impractical daydreaming.
He lives a rather mittyesque existence, imagining himself a hero in all sorts of daring scenarios. 他过着一种相当米蒂式的生活，想象着自己在各种大胆的场景中扮演英雄。
Mittyesque fantasies filled his head as he sat at his boring desk job. 当他坐在无聊的办公桌前时，米蒂式幻想充满了他的头脑。

近义词: fanciful, dreamy, imaginative, quixotic, unrealistic
反义词: realistic, practical, down-to-earth, pragmatic

词性: adjective
