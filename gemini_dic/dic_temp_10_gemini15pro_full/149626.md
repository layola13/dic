paint-by-numbers:/peɪnt baɪ ˈnʌmbərz/ | n. | 数字油画; 按数字填色的画;  | A painting kit containing a canvas pre-printed with outlines and numbers, and paints with corresponding numbers, allowing anyone to create a finished painting by filling in the numbered areas. |  My grandmother loves paint-by-numbers kits.  我的祖母喜欢数字油画套装。 |  coloring book, craft kit |  original artwork, masterpiece | noun

paint-by-numbers:/peɪnt baɪ ˈnʌmbərz/ | adj. |  公式化的; 刻板的; 缺乏创意的; |  Following a simple, prescribed formula, lacking originality or creativity. | The movie plot was paint-by-numbers and predictable.  这部电影的情节公式化，可以预见。 |  formulaic, unoriginal, predictable, routine |  original, creative, innovative, imaginative | adjective
