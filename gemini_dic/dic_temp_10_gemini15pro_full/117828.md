mellifluent:/məˈlɪfluənt/| adj.|流畅的，甜美的，悦耳的|Having a smooth rich flow; smooth and sweet.  This word often describes speech or music that is pleasing to the ear.|Her mellifluent voice soothed the crying baby. 她悦耳的声音安抚了哭闹的婴儿。|近义词：euphonious, harmonious, dulcet, smooth, sweet|反义词：cacophonous, harsh, discordant, grating|词性:adjective
词源:From Latin melli- 'honey' + fluent- 'flowing'.
