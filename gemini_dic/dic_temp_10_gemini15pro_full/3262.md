affirmably:/əˈfɜːməbli/
adverb|肯定地，断然地|In a manner that can be stated positively; assertively.|He affirmably stated his innocence. 他肯定地声明自己是无辜的。|例句：She affirmably agreed to the proposal. 她肯定地同意了这个提议。|近义词：definitely, positively, assertively, categorically|反义词：negatively, uncertainly, doubtfully|词性:副词
词源: From affirm + -ably.  Affirm comes from Late Latin affirmare "to strengthen, confirm," from Latin ad- "to" + firmare "to make firm, strengthen," from firmus "firm, strong."
