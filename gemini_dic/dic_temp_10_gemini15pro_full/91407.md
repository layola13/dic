hydrozoan:/haɪdrəˈzoʊən/
n.
水螅纲动物
An invertebrate animal belonging to the class Hydrozoa, which includes hydras, jellyfish, and corals.
Hydras and some jellyfish are examples of hydrozoans.  水螅和一些水母是水螅纲动物的例子。
The Portuguese man-of-war is a colonial hydrozoan. 僧帽水母是一种群体性水螅纲动物。
synonyms:  cnidarian, coelenterate
antonyms: N/A
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Hydrozoa, from hydro- + -zoa ("animal").  From Greek *ὕδωρ (hýdōr, “water”) + ζῷον (zôion, “animal”).
