grateful: /ˈɡreɪtfəl/ (US) /ˈɡreɪtfʊl/ (UK)| adj.|感激的，感谢的|Feeling or showing an appreciation of kindness; thankful.|例句：I am very grateful for your help. 我非常感谢你的帮助。

例句：She was grateful to him for being so kind. 她很感激他如此友善。

近义词：thankful, appreciative, indebted|反义词：ungrateful, unappreciative|词性:形容词

词源: From Late Middle English: from grate + -ful.  The original sense was ‘pleasing, agreeable’, later ‘thankful’.
