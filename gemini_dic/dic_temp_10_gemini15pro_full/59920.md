edentation:/ˌiːdɛnˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 缺牙；牙缺失；齿状缺失 | The process of indenting or the state of being indented; a notch, recess, or depression.  A loss of tooth substance from any cause other than caries |  The edentation of the coastline provides many sheltered harbors. 海岸线的锯齿状提供了许多避风港。 | 近义词:  indentation, notch, recess, depression, tooth loss | 反义词: protrusion, projection | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *edentatio, from edentare “to make toothless,” from Latin e- + dens (genitive dentis) “tooth.”
