cystoplast: /ˈsɪstəˌplæst/
n.
膀胱成形术；膀胱修补
A piece of tissue used for cystoplasty, specifically a graft of tissue used to repair a defect in the urinary bladder.
The surgeon used a cystoplast taken from the patient's intestine to repair the bladder wall.  外科医生用取自病人肠道的一块膀胱成形材料来修补膀胱壁。
Synonym: bladder graft
Antonym: N/A (No direct antonym)
noun
