cabernet:/ˌkæbərˈneɪ/ /ˌkæbərˈnet/| n.|赤霞珠（一种红葡萄品种及其酿造的葡萄酒）|A red grape variety originally from Bordeaux, France, widely grown elsewhere and used to produce full-bodied, dark-colored wines; a wine made from this grape.|例句：This Cabernet has a rich, complex aroma.  这款赤霞珠葡萄酒香气浓郁复杂。

例句：We enjoyed a bottle of Cabernet with our steak. 我们享用了一瓶赤霞珠葡萄酒配牛排。|近义词：red wine, Bordeaux|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific grape variety)|词性:noun
