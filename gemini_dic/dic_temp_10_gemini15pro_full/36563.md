词源:  "collapse" + "-ibility".  "Collapse" comes from the Latin "collapsus," the past participle of "collabi," meaning "to fall together."  "-ibility" is a suffix forming nouns meaning "capable of being." Thus, "collapsibility" refers to the capability of something to collapse.
