overwhelmer:/ˌəʊvəˈwelmə(r)/| n. |压倒性的事物/人; 征服者|Something that overwhelms; a conqueror. |The sheer amount of information was an overwhelmer for the new employee.  新员工被大量的信息压得喘不过气。| The enemy army was an overwhelmer, crushing all resistance. 敌军势不可挡，粉碎了所有抵抗。|近义词:conqueror, victor, subduer|反义词:loser, failure, underdog|词性:名词

overwhelm:/ˌəʊvəˈwelm/| vt. |征服，压倒；淹没；使不知所措|To overfill; to submerge; to overcome completely.|The flood overwhelmed the village. 洪水淹没了村庄。|  She was overwhelmed by grief. 她悲痛欲绝。|近义词:overpower, crush, inundate|反义词:resist, withstand, cope with|词性:动词

overwhelmed:/ˌəʊvəˈwelmd/| adj. |被压倒的，不知所措的|Feeling overcome with emotion or information.|I was overwhelmed by the kindness of strangers. 我被陌生人的善意深深感动。| He felt overwhelmed by the amount of work he had to do.  他被堆积如山的工作压得喘不过气来。|近义词:overpowered, inundated, swamped|反义词:calm, composed, coping|词性:形容词
