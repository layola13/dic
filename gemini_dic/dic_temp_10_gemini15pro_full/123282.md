monogamy:/məˈnɒɡəmi/ /məˈnɑːɡəmi/ | n. |一夫一妻制；单配偶制 | The practice or state of having only one spouse at a time.  |  Monogamy is the most common form of marriage in Western cultures. 西方文化中最常见的婚姻形式是一夫一妻制。 | synonyms: single marriage,  monogamousness | antonyms: polygamy, polygyny, polyandry | noun
