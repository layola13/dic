diverberation:/dɪˌvɜːrbəˈreɪʃən/
n.|消混响，去混响|The reduction of reverberation in a room or hall.|The diverberation in the new concert hall is excellent, making the music crisp and clear.  新音乐厅的消混响效果极佳，使音乐清晰悦耳。|近义词：dereverberation, sound absorption|反义词: reverberation|词性:名词

Etymology:
From de- + reverberation.  "De-" is a prefix meaning "remove" or "reduce", and "reverberation" refers to the persistence of sound after the source has stopped.
