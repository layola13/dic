huffinesses:/ˈhʌfinɪsɪz/
n.
生气，恼怒，发脾气
The state of being huffy; a display of peevishness or irritable displeasure.
Her huffinesses were frequent and easily provoked. 她经常生气，很容易被激怒。
The child's huffinesses subsided after he got a cookie.  孩子拿到饼干后就不生气了。
Synonyms: petulance, pique, irritability, sullenness, resentment, indignation
Antonyms: good humor, cheerfulness, amiability, affability, placidity
