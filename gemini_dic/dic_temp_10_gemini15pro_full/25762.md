burbot:/ˈbɜːrbɒt/ (US) /ˈbɜːrbət/ (UK)| n. | 淡水鳕鱼；江鳕 |A freshwater fish of the cod family, found in northern Europe and North America. It is also known as eelpout, ling, or freshwater ling. |例句：The burbot is a nocturnal fish, preferring to hunt in the dark. 淡水鳕鱼是一种夜行性鱼类，喜欢在黑暗中捕猎。

例句：Burbot are known for their delicate, flaky white flesh. 淡水鳕鱼以其鲜嫩的白色鱼肉而闻名。|近义词：eelpout, ling, freshwater ling, cusk|反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑其他鱼类，例如salmon 鲑鱼，trout 鳟鱼, 作为对比)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
