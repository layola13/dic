dermopteran:/dɜːrˈmɒptərən/
noun
皮翼目动物，鼯猴
A gliding mammal of the order Dermoptera, also known as a colugo or flying lemur, found in Southeast Asia.  These animals are not true lemurs and do not fly, but glide using a membrane of skin stretched between their limbs.
The Philippine dermopteran is critically endangered.  菲律宾鼯猴极度濒危。
colugo, flying lemur
