maximizers:/ˈmæksɪmaɪzərz/
|n.|最大化者|Individuals who seek the optimal solution, often at the expense of time and energy. 指那些寻求最佳解决方案的人，通常以时间和精力为代价。 |Example: Maximizers often compare their choices to those of others, leading to dissatisfaction. 极致追求者经常将自己的选择与他人进行比较，从而导致不满意。 |Synonym: optimizers, perfectionists|Antonym: satisficers|noun
