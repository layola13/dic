inked:/ɪŋkt/| adj.| 墨水的；有墨迹的；用墨水写的；(非正式)纹身的；（俚语）签了合同的|Covered or marked with ink; tattooed; (slang) having signed a contract. |例句：My fingers were inked from writing all afternoon. 我的手指因为写了一下午而沾满了墨水。

例句：He showed off his newly inked arm. 他炫耀着他新纹的手臂。|近义词：tattooed, written, signed|反义词: blank, clean|词性:形容词
