overtorturing:/ˌoʊvərˈtɔːrtʃərɪŋ/
|v.|过度折磨；过分拷打|The constant questioning and sleep deprivation were amounting to overtorturing.  不断的审问和睡眠剥夺相当于过度折磨。| The regime was accused of overtorturing political prisoners.  该政权被指控过度拷打政治犯。|近义词：torturing excessively, tormenting severely|反义词: comforting, soothing|词性:动词(present participle)
Etymology:  "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above, beyond") + "torture" (from Old French torture, from Latin tortura "twisting, torment," from tortus, past participle of torquēre "to twist").  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning of "torture," indicating an excessive or extreme degree of torment.
