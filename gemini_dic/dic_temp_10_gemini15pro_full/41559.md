corporality:/kɔːrpəˈrælɪti/ /kɔːrpɔːˈrælɪti/
noun
物质性；肉体性；有形性
The state of being or having a body; material existence; physical nature.
The painting explores themes of corporality and spirituality. 这幅画探索了物质性和精神性的主题。

corporality:/kɔːrpəˈrælɪti/ /kɔːrpɔːˈrælɪti/
noun
团体；法人团体
A corporate body; a corporation.  (This meaning is less common.)
The corporality voted to increase membership fees.  法人团体投票决定提高会员费.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *corporalitas, from Latin corporalis (“bodily”), from corpus (“body”).

SYNONYMS->
physicality, materiality, embodiment, tangibility

ANTONYMS->
immateriality, spirituality, incorporeality, abstractness
