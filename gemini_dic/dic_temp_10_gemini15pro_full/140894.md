onagga:/ˈɒnəɡə/ | n. | 斑驴（已灭绝的一种斑马） | An extinct subspecies of the plains zebra, characterized by stripes on the front half of the body only. | Example: The onagga once roamed the plains of South Africa. 斑驴曾经在南非的平原上漫游。| Example:  Museum displays sometimes feature reconstructions of the onagga. 博物馆的展品有时会展出斑驴的复原模型。 | Synonym: zebra, quagga | Antonym: N/A | noun
