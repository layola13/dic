ecclesiastical: /ɪˌkliːziˈæstɪkəl/
adjective
教会的；宗教的
Of or relating to the Christian Church or its clergy.
The archbishop wore elaborate ecclesiastical robes.  大主教穿着精致的教会长袍。
The college offers courses in ecclesiastical history.  这所学院提供教会历史课程。
Synonyms:  religious, clerical, churchly, spiritual
Antonyms: secular, lay, worldly
词性: adjective
