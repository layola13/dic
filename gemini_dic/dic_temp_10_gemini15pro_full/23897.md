breakages:/ˈbreɪkɪdʒɪz/
noun
破损，损坏；断裂；损耗
the act or process of breaking; the state of being broken.  The loss of something broken.
The company suffered heavy breakages during the earthquake.  公司在地震中遭受了严重的损失。
synonyms: damage, loss, destruction, ruin, impairment
antonyms: repair, restoration, construction, improvement
