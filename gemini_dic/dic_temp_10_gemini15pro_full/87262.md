heritor:/ɪnˈhɛrɪtər/ | n. |继承人；后嗣|A person who inherits something, especially property, a title, or a tradition.|例句：She is the sole heritor of her father's vast estate. 她是她父亲庞大遗产的唯一继承人。

例句：The museum is a heritor of a rich artistic tradition. 这家博物馆继承了丰富的艺术传统。

近义词：inheritor, successor, heir |反义词: predecessor |词性: noun
