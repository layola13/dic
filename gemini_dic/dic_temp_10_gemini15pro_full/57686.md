drawling:/ˈdrɔːlɪŋ/ | adj. | 慢吞吞的，拖长的 | Speaking in a slow, lengthened way, often in a monotonous tone.  |  He had a slow, drawling Southern accent. 他操着慢吞吞的南方口音。 | synonyms: slow, prolonged, lengthened,  monotonous | antonyms: rapid, fast, quick, brisk | adjective

drawl:/drɔːl/ | v. |  慢吞吞地说，拖长地说 | To speak in a slow, lengthened way, often in a monotonous tone. | "Well, now," he drawled, "I reckon that's about right."  “嗯，现在，”他慢吞吞地说，“我想这就差不多了。” | synonyms: drone, intone | antonyms:  rush, hurry,  gabble | verb
