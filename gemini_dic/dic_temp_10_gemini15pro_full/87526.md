heterogeneous:/ˌhɛtərəˈdʒiːniəs/ | adj. |异质的，由不同成分组成的|Consisting of different elements or parts; diverse in character or content.|The city is a heterogeneous mix of cultures and ethnicities.  这座城市是文化和种族的异质混合体。|例句：The classroom was a heterogeneous group of students, ranging in age from 18 to 45.  这个教室里有一群不同年龄的学生，从18岁到45岁不等。|近义词：diverse, varied, mixed, dissimilar|反义词：homogeneous, uniform, similar|词性:形容词
词源:From Ancient Greek  ἕτερος (héteros, “other, different”) + γένος (génos, “kind, race, stock”).
