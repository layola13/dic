dump:/dʌmp/| n. vt.|垃圾场，倾倒，丢弃|A place where rubbish or waste material is deposited. To deposit or get rid of (something unwanted) in a careless or hurried way.|例句：They dumped the old furniture at the side of the road. 他们在路边丢弃了旧家具。|近义词：discard, dispose of, jettison, unload, tip|反义词:keep, retain|noun, verb

dump:/dʌmp/| n. vt.| (informal) 肮脏或不舒适的地方|An unpleasant or dreary place.|例句：The hotel was a real dump. 这家旅馆真是个脏地方。|近义词：dive, hovel|反义词:palace, mansion|noun

dump:/dʌmp/| vt.| (computing) 转储 (数据)|Copy (stored data) to a different location, especially to a larger storage device.|例句：He dumped the contents of his hard drive onto a flash drive. 他把硬盘里的内容复制到U盘里。|近义词：copy, transfer|反义词:load|verb
