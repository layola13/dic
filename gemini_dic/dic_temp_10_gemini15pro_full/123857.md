moralizingly:/ˈmɔːrəlaɪzɪŋli/ /ˈmɔːrələɪzɪŋli/
adverb
以道德化的方式；教训地
in a moralizing manner; preachily
He spoke moralizingly about the dangers of drinking. 他以教训的口吻谈论了饮酒的危害。
近义词: preachily, sanctimoniously, sententiously, didactically
反义词: nonjudgmentally, objectively
