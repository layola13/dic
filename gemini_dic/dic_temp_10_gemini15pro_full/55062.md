disrecommendation:/dɪsˌrɛkəmɛnˈdeɪʃən/
n.
不推荐，反对，劝阻
The act of advising against something; an expression of disapproval or opposition.
His disrecommendation of the project surprised everyone. 他对该项目的不推荐令所有人感到惊讶。
Synonyms: deprecation, disapproval, condemnation, objection
Antonyms: recommendation, endorsement, approval, support
noun
