contemptuousness:/kənˈtɛmptʃuːsnəs/
n.
轻蔑；鄙视；傲慢
The state of feeling or showing contempt; disdain.
He spoke with utter contemptuousness in his voice.  他说话的语气充满了轻蔑。
contempt, disdain, scorn, arrogance, haughtiness
respect, admiration, reverence, deference
noun
