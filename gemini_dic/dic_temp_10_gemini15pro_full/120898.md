miscalculation:/ˌmɪskælkjuˈleɪʃn/
n.
错误估计；估算错误
An act of calculating something wrongly.
The project's failure was due to a miscalculation of the costs involved.  这个项目的失败是由于对相关成本的错误估计。
misjudgment, misestimate, error, blunder
correct calculation, accurate estimation

词性: 名词
