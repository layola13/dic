nontraitorously:/ˌnɒnˈtreɪtərəsli/
adverb
非叛逆地，忠诚地
In a manner that is not traitorous; loyally.
He served his country nontraitorously. 他忠诚地为国家服务。
Synonyms:loyally, faithfully, patriotically
Antonyms: traitorously, disloyally, treacherously
词性: 副词
