counselor:/ˈkaʊnsələr/ (US), /ˈkaʊnslər/ (UK)| n. | 顾问，辅导员 |  A person trained to give guidance on personal, social, or psychological problems. | I spoke to a guidance counselor about my career options. 我和一位指导顾问谈了我的职业选择。| synonyms: advisor, guide, mentor, consultant | antonyms: advisee, mentee | noun

counsel:/ˈkaʊnsəl/ (US), /ˈkaʊnsl/ (UK) | n. vt. |  建议，忠告；法律顾问；商议；劝告 | Advice, especially that given formally. To give advice to (someone).  |  The lawyer gave me some good counsel. 律师给了我一些好建议。  He counseled the president on foreign policy. 他就外交政策向总统提供咨询。| synonyms: advice, guidance, recommendation, advocate (v.) | antonyms: dissuade, discourage (v.) | noun, verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "conseil"  (advice, counsel),  from Latin "consilium" (consultation, deliberation, advice), from "consulere" (to consult).
