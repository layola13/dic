fashion:/ˈfæʃən/ (US), /ˈfæʃn/ (UK)| n. v.|时尚，潮流；方式，方法|A popular trend, especially in styles of dress and ornament or manners of behavior; a manner or way of doing something.|She always dresses in the latest fashion. 她总是穿着最新的时尚服装。|Synonyms: trend, style, vogue, mode, craze|Antonyms: old-fashioned, outdated, unfashionable|Noun

fashion:/ˈfæʃən/ (US), /ˈfæʃn/ (UK)| v.|制作，塑造|To make or shape something, usually with careful attention.|He fashioned a small boat out of wood. 他用木头做了一艘小船。|Synonyms: create, make, form, construct, mold|Antonyms: destroy, dismantle, ruin|Verb
