mungrel:/ˈmʌŋɡrəl/ | n. | 杂种狗，混血儿，来历不明的人 | A dog of mixed breed, especially one of uncertain parentage.  Figuratively, it can refer to anything of mixed origin or character. |  Example: The stray dog was a friendly mungrel, a mix of several breeds. 那只流浪狗是一只友善的杂种狗，是几个品种的混血。

Example:  He dismissed their proposal as a mungrel of ill-conceived ideas. 他认为他们的建议是各种拙劣想法的混杂，不予理会。 | 近义词: mutt, cur, hybrid, crossbreed | 反义词: purebred, pedigree | 词性: noun
