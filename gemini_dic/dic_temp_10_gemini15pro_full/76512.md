galen: /ˈɡeɪlən/ | n. | 盖伦（古希腊医生及医学家）|Aelius Galenus or Claudius Galenus (AD 129 – c. 200/c. 216), often Anglicized as Galen and better known as Galen of Pergamon, was a prominent Greek physician, surgeon and philosopher in the Roman Empire. Arguably the most accomplished of all medical researchers of antiquity, Galen influenced the development of various scientific disciplines, including anatomy, physiology, pathology, pharmacology, and neurology, as well as philosophy and logic.| Galen's theories dominated Western medical science for well over a millennium. | 近义词: physician, doctor, healer | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词
