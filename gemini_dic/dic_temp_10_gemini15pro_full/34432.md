claustrophobias:/ˌklɔːstrəˈfoʊbiəz/ | n. | 幽闭恐惧症 | An abnormal fear of being in enclosed or narrow spaces. |  Example 1:  Her claustrophobia made it difficult for her to ride in elevators. 她的幽闭恐惧症使她很难乘坐电梯。

Example 2: His claustrophobias was triggered by being stuck in a traffic jam. 他的幽闭恐惧症是由交通堵塞引起的。 | Near synonyms:  claustrophobia, fear of enclosed spaces | Antonym: agoraphobia (fear of open spaces) | Noun
