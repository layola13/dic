curative:/ˈkjʊərətɪv/ | adj. n.|治疗的，治病的；治疗药|able to cure disease or illness.  Having healing powers or properties.  Also, a medicine or remedy that cures. |例句：The doctor prescribed a curative herb for her cough. 医生给她开了一种治疗咳嗽的草药。

例句：The spa boasted of the curative properties of its mineral springs.  这家水疗中心吹嘘其矿泉水具有疗效。|近义词：healing, remedial, therapeutic|反义词: harmful, damaging|词性:adjective, noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *cūrātīvus, from Latin cūrō (“I care for, attend, heal”), from cūra (“care, concern”).
