greenlet:/ˈɡriːnlɪt/| n.|微线程，小协程|A small independent pseudo-thread within a process. It allows for concurrent execution without the overhead of full-fledged threads.|Example: Greenlets are useful for asynchronous I/O operations. 微线程对于异步 I/O 操作很有用.|Synonym: microthread, coroutine, fiber|Antonym: thread, process|noun

Etymology->

From "green" + "let" (as in "applet"), suggesting a small, lightweight execution unit.
