compellability:/kəmˌpɛləˈbɪləti/| n.|可强制性，可强迫性|The quality of being subject to compulsion, especially of being legally required to give evidence in a court of law.|The witness challenged his compellability to testify.  证人对要求他作证的可强制性提出了质疑。|Synonyms:  obligatoriness, enforceability|Antonyms:  non-compellability, optionality|noun
