parky:/ˈpɑːki/ (UK) /ˈpɑːrki/ (US)| adj.|寒冷的，冷飕飕的|Cold, chilly, especially outdoors.  Referring to weather that is cold enough to make one want to wear a parka.|It's a bit parky out there today, so wrap up warm. 今天外面有点冷，穿暖和点。|近义词:chilly, cold, crisp, nippy|反义词:warm, balmy, mild|词性:adjective
Etymology:  Derived from "parka," a type of heavy winter jacket, with the suffix "-y" added to form an adjective. The word "parka" itself comes from the Nenets language of northern Russia.
