hyperpathetic:/ˌhaɪpərˈpæθɛtɪk/
adjective
过度敏感的；过度反应的
Characterized by excessive sensibility or emotional response.  Showing exaggerated feelings or reactions.
She had a hyperpathetic response to the criticism, bursting into tears immediately. 她对批评反应过度，立刻泪流满面。
The hyperpathetic dog would cower at the slightest loud noise. 这只过度敏感的狗会在听到轻微的响声时畏缩。
近义词:oversensitive, hypersensitive, overreactive
反义词:insensitive, apathetic, stoic
词性:adjective

Etymology: From Greek *hyper* (over, excessive) + *pathētikos* (sensitive, capable of feeling)
