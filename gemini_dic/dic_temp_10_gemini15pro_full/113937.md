mafioso:/ˌmɑːfiˈoʊsoʊ/ /ˌmæfiˈoʊsoʊ/
|n.|黑手党成员|A member of the Mafia, especially one in a position of authority.|The mafioso ordered a hit on the informant.|近义词：gangster, mobster, racketeer|反义词：law enforcement officer|Noun

mafiosos:/ˌmɑːfiˈoʊsoʊz/ /ˌmæfiˈoʊsoʊz/
|n.|黑手党成员（复数）|Members of the Mafia, especially those in positions of authority.|Several mafiosos were arrested in the raid.|近义词：gangsters, mobsters, racketeers|反义词：law enforcement officers|Noun

词源:
From Italian *mafioso*, from Sicilian *mafiusu*.  Ultimately of uncertain origin, but possibly related to Arabic *mahya* (meaning "boasting, bragging").
