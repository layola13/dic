immortalizes: /ɪˈmɔːrtəˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
使不朽，使永垂不朽
to make someone or something famous or respected for a long time, especially by writing about them, painting them, etc.
The poem immortalizes the beauty of the natural world. 这首诗使自然世界的美丽永垂不朽。
His bravery was immortalized in song. 他的勇敢事迹被歌颂而永垂不朽。
synonyms: commemorate, celebrate, eternalize, memorialize
antonyms: forget, ignore, disregard
词性: 动词
