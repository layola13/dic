outleaped:/ˌaʊtˈliːpt/ | v. | 超越，跳得比...远 | Surpassed, jumped farther than. |  He outleaped all his competitors in the long jump. 他在跳远比赛中超越了所有对手。|  The basketball player outleaped his defender to grab the rebound.  这名篮球运动员跳得比防守队员高，抓住了篮板球。| 近义词: outjumped, surpassed, exceeded | 反义词: lagged behind, trailed | 词性: 动词

词源:  From "out-" (meaning exceeding or surpassing) + "leap" (to jump).  由“out-”（表示超过或超越）+“leap”（跳跃）组成。
