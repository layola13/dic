nonpreventible:/ˌnɒnprɪˈvɛntəbl/ /ˌnɑːnprɪˈvɛntəbl/| adj.|不可预防的|Not able to be prevented; unavoidable.|The spread of the virus was rapid and, at that stage, nonpreventible.|近义词：unavoidable, inescapable, inevitable|反义词：preventable, avoidable|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From non- + preventable.  "Preventable" comes from the Old French "prevenable" and ultimately from the Latin "praevenire" meaning "to come before, anticipate."
