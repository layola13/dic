glaciers:/ˈɡleɪʃərz/ /ˈɡlæsiərz/ | n. | 冰川 |  A large mass of ice formed from compacted snow that moves slowly over land. |  The glaciers are melting at an alarming rate. 冰川正在以惊人的速度融化。

The hikers were awestruck by the majestic glaciers.  徒步旅行者对雄伟的冰川感到敬畏。 | 近义词: ice fields, ice caps | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用描述融化后状态的词) meltwater, rivers | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *glacier*, from Franco-Provençal *glaciér*, from Vulgar Latin *glaciārium*, from Latin *glacies* ("ice"), from *glaciēs* ("ice").
