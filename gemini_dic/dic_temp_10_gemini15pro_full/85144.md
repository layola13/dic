hardpan:/ˈhɑːrdpæn/ (US) /ˈhɑːdpæn/ (UK)| n.| 硬磐，硬土层 |A dense, compacted layer of soil, usually found below the topsoil, that is largely impervious to water and roots. |The hardpan prevented the tree roots from growing deep.  硬磐阻止了树根深入生长。

The plow couldn't penetrate the hardpan.  犁无法穿透硬土层。|近义词:claypan, caliche, duricrust|反义词:topsoil|词性:名词

hardpan:/ˈhɑːrdpæn/ (US) /ˈhɑːdpæn/ (UK)| adj. (informal)|  顽固的，不妥协的|Stubbornly resistant to change or influence. |He has a hardpan attitude about politics. 他对政治的态度很顽固。


She was known for her hardpan negotiating style. 她以强硬的谈判风格而闻名。|近义词:inflexible, unyielding, obstinate|反义词:flexible, yielding, amenable|词性:形容词
