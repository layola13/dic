oversimplify:/ˌōvərˈsɪmplɪfaɪ/| v. |过度简化|To describe or explain something in a way that makes it seem less complex or complicated than it really is.|He oversimplified the issue, ignoring many important details. 他过度简化了这个问题，忽略了许多重要的细节。|The explanation oversimplified the complex process of photosynthesis.  这个解释过度简化了复杂的光合作用过程。|近义词：trivialize, minimize, dumb down|反义词: complicate, elaborate, nuance |词性: 动词

oversimplification:/ˌōvərˌsɪmplɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |过度简化|The act of oversimplifying something.|The oversimplification of the issue led to misunderstanding. 对这个问题的过度简化导致了误解。|His oversimplification of the historical event was misleading. 他对这一历史事件的过度简化具有误导性。|近义词：distortion, misrepresentation|反义词: elaboration, nuance |词性: 名词
