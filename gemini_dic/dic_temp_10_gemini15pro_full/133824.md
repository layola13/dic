nonglucoses:/nɒnˈɡluːkoʊsiz/
noun
非葡萄糖
Substances that are not glucose, a simple sugar.
Many sugars, like fructose and galactose, are nonglucoses. 许多糖，例如果糖和半乳糖，都是非葡萄糖。
Synonyms: non-glucose substances
Antonyms: glucoses
n.
