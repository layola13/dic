concentratedly:/ˌkɒnsənˈtreɪtɪdli/ /ˌkɑːnsənˈtreɪtɪdli/| adv. |集中地，专心地|With focused attention; intently.  It describes the manner in which an action is performed when all one's attention and effort are directed towards a particular task or goal.|She worked concentratedly on the problem for hours. 她几个小时集中精力研究这个问题。|近义词：intently, fixedly, attentively, earnestly|反义词：distractedly, carelessly, inattentively|词性: 副词

Etymology:
From concentrate + -ed + -ly.  "Concentrate" comes from the Latin "concentrare," meaning "to draw to a center."
