guerrilla:/ɡəˈrɪlə/ /ɡəˈrɪjə/ | n. | 游击队员，游击队 | A member of a small independent group taking part in irregular fighting, typically against larger regular forces. |  The guerrillas ambushed the army convoy. 游击队员伏击了军队的车队。

The guerrilla fighters hid in the mountains. 游击队员藏在山里。


| 近义词：partisan, insurgent, rebel, freedom fighter | 反义词：soldier, regular | 词性：名词
