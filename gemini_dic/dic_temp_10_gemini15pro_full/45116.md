cucujo:/kuːˈkuːhoʊ/ /kuːˈkuːdʒoʊ/
noun
萤火虫（主要指热带美洲的叩甲科昆虫）
A type of beetle, especially a firefly, found primarily in tropical America, belonging to the family Elateridae.
例句：The cucujo flashed its light in the dark forest.  萤火虫在黑暗的森林里闪着光。

例句：Researchers are studying the bioluminescence of the cucujo. 研究人员正在研究萤火虫的生物发光。

近义词：firefly, glow-worm
反义词:  None (No direct opposite for a type of insect)
词性: n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *cucuyo*, probably of imitative origin.
