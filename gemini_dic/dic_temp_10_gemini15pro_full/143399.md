outfitter:/ˈaʊtfɪtər/| n.|装备商；户外用品店；旅行用品店|A person or company that sells equipment and clothing for a particular activity, especially outdoor activities.|We bought our camping gear from a local outfitter. 我们从当地一家户外用品店买了露营装备。|Synonyms: supplier, provider, vendor|Antonyms: customer, buyer|noun

outfitter:/ˈaʊtfɪtər/| vt.|装备；为…提供服装|To provide someone with equipment or clothing for a particular activity.|The company outfits expeditions to Mount Everest. 这家公司为珠峰探险队提供装备。|Synonyms: equip, furnish, supply|Antonyms: strip, divest|verb
