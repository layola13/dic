enhalo:/ɪnˈheɪloʊ/ /ɛnˈheɪloʊ/
verb
环绕以光环;使笼罩在光环中;使像有光环一样
surround with or as if with a halo.
The setting sun enhaloed the mountains. 落日给群山镶上光环。
近义词:aureole, halo, glorify, exalt
反义词:demean, disgrace, dishonor
词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From en- + halo.  "Halo" comes from the Greek "halos," meaning "disk of the sun or moon."
