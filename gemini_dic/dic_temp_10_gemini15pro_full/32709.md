chlorinate:/ˈklɔːrɪneɪt/ /ˈklɔːrəˌneɪt/| vt. |氯化，使与氯化合|To treat or combine with chlorine.  |例句：They chlorinate the swimming pool regularly. 他们定期给游泳池加氯消毒。

例句：The water is chlorinated to kill bacteria.  水经过氯化消毒以杀死细菌。

近义词：disinfect, sterilize, sanitize|反义词: |v.
