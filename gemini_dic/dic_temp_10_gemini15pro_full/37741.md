ETYMOLOGY->
From compartment + -ize + -ing.  "Compartment" comes from the French "compartiment," meaning a division or separate part. "-ize" is a suffix used to form verbs meaning "to make or cause to be." "-ing" is a suffix used to form present participles and gerunds.
