gossaniferous:/ɡɒsˈænɪfərəs/ | adj. | 含褐铁矿的，多孔状铁帽的 | Containing or resembling gossan; rich in gossan.  |  The gossaniferous outcrop suggested the presence of sulfide mineralization below. 这片含褐铁矿的露头表明下面存在硫化物矿化。 | Synonyms: ferruginous, iron-rich | Antonyms: barren, unmineralized | Adjective

gossan:/ˈɡɒsən/ | n. | 褐铁矿，铁帽 |  The oxidized and weathered cap of a sulfide ore deposit, often rich in iron oxides and hydroxides. | The miners discovered a large gossan, indicating a potential copper deposit. 矿工们发现了一大片褐铁矿，这预示着可能存在铜矿床。 | Synonyms: iron hat, caprock | Antonyms: orebody, sulfide deposit | Noun
