autarchies: /ɔːˈtɑːrkiːz/
noun
专制统治；自给自足的国家（autarchy的复数）
A system of government in which supreme power is concentrated in the hands of one person, whose decisions are subject to neither external legal restraints nor regularized mechanisms of popular control.  Also refers to a country or state that is economically self-sufficient.
Many small island nations strive for autarchies, aiming to produce all essential goods within their own borders. 许多小岛国都力求自给自足，目标是在自己的境内生产所有必需品。
The dictator established a series of autarchies to solidify his control over the region.  独裁者建立了一系列专制统治以巩固他对该地区的控制。
Synonyms: dictatorships, absolutisms, self-sufficiency, independence
Antonyms: democracies, interdependence, reliance
