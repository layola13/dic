denigrating:/dɪˈnɪɡreɪtɪŋ/| adj.| 诋毁的，贬低的 |criticizing someone or something unfairly; speaking damagingly of; belittling. |例句：He was accused of denigrating the achievements of his colleagues. 他被指责诋毁同事的成就。

例句：Her denigrating remarks about his family upset him greatly. 她对他家人的贬低性言论让他非常难过。

近义词：disparaging, belittling, deprecating, defaming, slandering, vilifying|反义词：praising, commending, lauding, extolling, applauding|词性: adjective
