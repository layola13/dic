deep-six:/ˌdiːp ˈsɪks/ | v. | 丢弃，抛弃；彻底放弃；取消；杀死 | To discard or dispose of something completely; to get rid of something permanently.  It can also mean to kill someone. |  例句：The project was deep-sixed due to lack of funding.  这个项目因缺乏资金而被取消了。

例句：He deep-sixed the evidence by throwing it in the river. 他把证据扔进河里销毁了。 | 近义词：discard, ditch, scrap, abandon, kill | 反义词：keep, retain, resurrect, revive | 词性: 动词
