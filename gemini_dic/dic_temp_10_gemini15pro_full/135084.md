nonobjectification:/ˌnɒnˌɒbdʒɛktɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 非对象化，非物化 | The act of treating a person as a human being with agency and inherent worth, rather than as a mere object or commodity.  It emphasizes respecting individuals for who they are, not just for what they can do or provide. |  Feminist theory promotes the nonobjectification of women in media.  女权主义理论提倡在媒体中不要物化女性。

The company's new policy encourages the nonobjectification of employees and values their individual contributions. 公司的新政策鼓励员工的非物化，并重视他们的个人贡献。 | 近义词: humanization, personhood, subjectivity | 反义词: objectification, commodification | 词性: noun


词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + objectification (from object + -ify + -cation)  The word "object" ultimately comes from the Latin "objectum," meaning "something placed before" or "presented."
