confessary:/kənˈfɛsəri/ | n. |忏悔室，告解室|A small enclosure in a Roman Catholic church, screened to allow the priest and penitent to speak privately during confession. |The priest waited in the confessary for the next penitent. 神父在忏悔室里等待下一位忏悔者。|近义词：confessional|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

confessory:/kənˈfɛsəri/ | adj. |忏悔的，告解的 |Relating to confession or penance. | He made a confessory statement to the police. 他向警方做了忏悔声明。|近义词:penitential, contrite|反义词:unrepentant|词性:形容词
