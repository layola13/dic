drubber:/ˈdrʌbər/| n.|擦拭器；擦鞋垫；（板球）擦球器|A drubber is a tool or material used for rubbing, typically to clean or polish something.  It can also refer to a cricket ball polisher.|He used a drubber to clean the mud off his boots. 他用擦拭器擦掉了靴子上的泥巴。|近义词：wiper, polisher, cloth|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "drub," meaning "to rub hard,"  + "-er," a suffix denoting an agent or instrument.  "Drub" originates from the Middle Dutch "drobbe," meaning "dregs, mud."
