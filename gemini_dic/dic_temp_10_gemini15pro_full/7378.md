animalian:/ˌænɪˈmeɪliən/ | adj. | 動物的；動物界的 | Relating to or characteristic of animals.  |  例句：Animalian behavior is a fascinating field of study. 动物行为是一个引人入胜的研究领域。 | 近义词：animal, beastly,  zoological| 反义词：vegetal, plant | 词性: 形容词
词源: From Latin *animalis*, meaning "of or belonging to an animal,"  from *animal*, meaning "a living being, animal," ultimately from *anima*, meaning "breath, soul, life."
