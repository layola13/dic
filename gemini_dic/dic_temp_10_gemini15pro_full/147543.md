overpedal:/ˌəʊvəˈpɛdəl/ /ˌoʊvərˈpɛdəl/| v. |过度使用踏板；过分渲染；夸张|To use a pedal (especially in a car or on a musical instrument) excessively or inappropriately. To exaggerate or overemphasize something.|He overpedaled the brakes and the car skidded. 他过度踩刹车，导致车子打滑了。

The politician overpedaled his accomplishments during the debate.  这位政治家在辩论中夸大了自己的成就。|近义词：exaggerate, overstate, overemphasize, overdo|反义词：understate, downplay|词性:动词
