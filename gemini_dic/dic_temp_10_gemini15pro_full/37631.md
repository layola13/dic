community:/kəˈmjuːnəti/ | n. | 社区，团体，社会，共同体 | A group of people living in the same place or having a particular characteristic in common. |例句：The local community came together to support the victims of the fire. 当地社区团结起来支持火灾受害者。|例句：The online gaming community is vast and diverse.  网络游戏社区庞大而多样。| 近义词：society, group, public, neighborhood | 反义词：individuality, isolation | noun

community:/kəˈmjuːnəti/ | n. | 共有，共享，共性 |  A feeling of fellowship with others, as a result of sharing common attitudes, interests, and goals. | 例句：There is a strong sense of community among the members of the club.  俱乐部成员之间有很强的社区意识。 | 例句：They share a community of interests. 他们有共同的利益。| 近义词：commonality, shared, collective | 反义词：division, discord | noun
