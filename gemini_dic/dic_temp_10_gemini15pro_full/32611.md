chirpiest:/ˈtʃɜːrpiɪst/| adj.|最欢快的，最活泼的|Superlative form of chirpy: most chirpy.  It means extremely cheerful and lively.|例句：She was the chirpiest person in the room, always smiling and chatting. 她房间里最活泼的人，总是微笑着聊天。

例句：The chirpiest bird in the forest woke everyone up with its song. 森林里最欢快的鸟儿用它的歌声唤醒了所有人。|近义词：merriest, most cheerful, most lively, most upbeat|反义词：gloomiest, saddest, most depressed, most morose|词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->

From chirpy + -est.  Chirpy itself likely imitates the sound of cheerful bird songs.
