gregal:/ˈɡriːɡəl/| adj.|东北风；格雷戈尔风的|Of or relating to the northeast wind, especially in the Mediterranean.|The gregal wind brought cooler temperatures to the island. 东北风给岛上带来了凉爽的气温。|近义词:northeasterly, boreal|反义词:southwesterly|词性:adjective
词源:From Italian *grecale* "Greek wind," from Medieval Latin *Graecalis* (ventus) "(wind) from Greece."
