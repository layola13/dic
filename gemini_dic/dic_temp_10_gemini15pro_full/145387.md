overcast:/ˈoʊvərˌkæst/ (US) /ˌəʊvəˈkɑːst/ (UK)| adj. n.|阴天的，多云的；阴暗的；（天空）布满云的；（光线）昏暗的；（情绪）忧郁的|Covered with clouds; dull.  As a noun, it refers to a covering of clouds or the state of being overcast.|例句：The sky was overcast and threatened rain. 天空阴沉，预示着要下雨。|近义词：cloudy, dull, gloomy, gray|反义词：sunny, bright, clear|词性:形容词,名词

overcasts:/ˌoʊvərˈkæsts/ (US) /ˌəʊvəˈkɑːsts/ (UK)| n. v. (third-person singular simple present)| 阴天，多云天气；遮蔽，覆盖；(尤指缝纫时)锁边；使忧郁；使（颜色等）变灰暗|(n.) cloudy weather; the state of being overcast. (v.) covers, darkens, clouds over, overspreads. Primarily used in third-person singular simple present tense.|例句：The heavy overcasts made the day seem gloomy.  厚厚的云层使白天显得阴沉。|近义词：cloud cover, dullness, gloom|反义词：sunshine, brightness, clarity|词性:名词,动词
