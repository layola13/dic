coalgoose:/ˈkoʊlˌɡuːs/| n.|白额雁，黑额树鸭|A type of bird, specifically the Brent Goose (Branta bernicla) in some dialects, particularly in Lincolnshire and Norfolk, England.  It is also sometimes used to refer to the White-fronted Goose (Anser albifrons).|例句：The coalgoose arrived in large numbers on the marshes. 大群的白额雁到达了沼泽地。|例句： We saw a flock of coalgooses flying overhead. 我们看到一群白额雁从头顶飞过。 |近义词：Brent Goose, White-fronted Goose, barnacle goose|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
