narwhal:/ˈnɑːrˌhwɔːl/ (US), /ˈnɑːwəl/ (UK)| n.|独角鲸|A medium-sized toothed whale (Monodon monoceros) that lives year-round in Arctic waters.  The male narwhal's most conspicuous characteristic is a long, straight, spiraled tusk, which is an enlarged canine tooth.|The narwhal is an elusive creature of the Arctic Ocean. 独角鲸是一种难以捉摸的北冰洋生物。

The documentary showed footage of a narwhal swimming beneath the ice.  这部纪录片展示了一只独角鲸在冰下游泳的画面。
