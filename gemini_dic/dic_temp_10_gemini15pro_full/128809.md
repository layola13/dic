nightjars:/ˈnaɪtˌdʒɑːrz/ (US) /ˈnaɪtˌdʒɑːz/ (UK)| n. |夜鹰|Nocturnal birds with mottled plumage, large eyes, short beaks, and long wings, known for their distinctive churring or jarring calls.|例句：Nightjars are elusive birds, rarely seen during the day.  夜鹰是难以捉摸的鸟类，白天很少见到。

例句：The nightjar's call echoed through the forest. 夜鹰的叫声在森林中回荡。|近义词：goatsucker, nighthawk|反义词: N/A (No opposite for a type of bird)|词性: Noun
