highlighted:/ˈhaɪlaɪtɪd/ | adj. | 突出显示的；强调的；醒目的 |  Brought to special attention; emphasized.  Something that is highlighted is made more noticeable or prominent. | The report highlighted the need for improved safety measures. 这份报告强调了加强安全措施的必要性。

The key findings of the study were highlighted in the executive summary.  这项研究的主要发现都被重点总结在了执行摘要中。| synonyms: emphasized, stressed, underscored, featured | antonyms: obscured, downplayed, minimized | adjective
