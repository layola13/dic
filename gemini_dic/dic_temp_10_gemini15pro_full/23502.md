braided:/ˈbreɪdɪd/| adj.| 编织的，辫状的| interwoven or plaited strands of hair or other material. | Her braided hair was adorned with flowers. 她的编织的头发上装饰着花朵。| 近义词：plaited, woven, intertwined | 反义词: unbraided, loose | 词性:形容词
braid:/breɪd/| n. v.| 辫子，编织 | a length of hair or other material made up of three or more interlaced strands; the act of weaving or interlacing strands together. | She wore her hair in a braid. 她把头发扎成辫子。 She braided her daughter's hair. 她给她女儿编头发。| 近义词：plait, weave, intertwine | 反义词: unbraid, unravel | 词性:名词,动词

词源: Old English *bregdan 'to move quickly, brandish, weave,' of Germanic origin.
