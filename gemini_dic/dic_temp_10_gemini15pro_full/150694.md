parasailing: /ˈpærəˌseɪlɪŋ/
n.
滑翔伞运动,空中拖曳伞运动
the activity of being towed through the air wearing a parachute while being pulled by a motorboat
We went parasailing on our vacation. 我们在度假时玩了滑翔伞。
近义词: paragliding
反义词: (No direct opposite, perhaps consider activities like scuba diving or hiking as contrasting activities)
词性: noun
