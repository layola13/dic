chromo-/ˈkroʊmoʊ-/| prefix |表示“颜色”| Chromo- is a combining form meaning "color" or "pigment". |Example 1: Chromotherapy uses colors to heal. 色彩疗法使用颜色来治疗。

Example 2:  Chromosomes get their name from their ability to be stained with dyes. 染色体因其能够被染料染色而得名。|近义词：color-, chromato-|反义词: N/A|词性:prefix
