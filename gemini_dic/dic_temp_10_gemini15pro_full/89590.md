hoodie:/ˈhʊdi/ (US), /ˈhʊdiː/ (UK)| n.|连帽衫，帽衫|A hooded sweatshirt, a sweatshirt with a hood.|例句：I wear my hoodie when it's cold. 天冷的时候我穿我的连帽衫。

例句：He pulled his hoodie up to hide his face. 他拉起帽衫来遮住脸。

近义词：sweatshirt, hooded top, jumper (UK)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
