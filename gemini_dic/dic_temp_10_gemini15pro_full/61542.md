embryotomy:/ˌembriˈɒtəmi/ /ˌembriˈɑːtəmi/| n.|胚胎切碎术|The surgical destruction of a fetus in utero when natural delivery is impossible.|Embryotomy was performed due to obstructed labor.  由于难产进行了胚胎切碎术。|Synonyms: fetotomy, craniotomy (in specific contexts)|Antonyms: cesarean section (a method to avoid embryotomy), natural childbirth|noun
