giveaway:/ˈɡɪvəweɪ/ (US) /ˈɡɪvəweɪ/ (UK)
n.|赠品；泄露；暴露|Something that is given away free, especially for promotional purposes; something that unintentionally reveals a secret or true feeling.|例句：The radio station was having a giveaway.  这家广播电台正在进行赠品派送。|近义词：prize, freebie, premium, giveaway, promotion|反义词: purchase, sale|词性:名词

giveaway:/ˈɡɪvəweɪ/ (US) /ˈɡɪvəweɪ/ (UK)
adj.|泄露秘密的；暴露的|Revealing unintentionally.|例句：Her nervous laugh was a dead giveaway. 她紧张的笑声完全暴露了她。|近义词：revealing, telltale|反义词: concealing, hiding|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb phrase "give away," meaning to give something freely or to reveal something unintentionally.  The noun and adjective forms developed from this verb phrase.
