inframarginal:/ˌɪnfrəˈmɑːrdʒɪnl/
adjective
次边际的，边际内的
Existing or operating below the margin; not marginal.  Specifically relating to units of consumption or production that are unaffected by a particular change in price or other economic variable.
例句：Inframarginal consumers are those who would buy the product even at a higher price.  次边际消费者是指即使价格更高也会购买该产品的消费者。

例句：Inframarginal firms are those that are profitable enough to withstand a small increase in costs.  次边际企业是指那些盈利足够高，足以承受成本小幅上涨的企业。
近义词：submarginal, non-marginal
反义词：marginal, extramarginal
词性：adjective
