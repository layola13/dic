affixing:/əˈfɪksɪŋ/
verb
附加；贴上；固定
present participle of affix
例句：He was affixing a stamp to the envelope.  他正在把邮票贴到信封上。

例句：She is affixing the notice to the board. 她正在把通知贴到布告板上。

近义词：attaching, sticking, fastening
反义词：detaching, removing, unfastening
词性：v. (动词)
