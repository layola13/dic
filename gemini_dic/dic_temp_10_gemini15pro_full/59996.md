editorialises:/ˌedɪˈtɔːriəlaɪzɪz/
verb
发表社论；在报道中加入个人意见
expresses opinions rather than just reporting facts
The news anchor was criticized for editorialising during the broadcast.  这位新闻主播因在播报新闻时发表个人意见而受到批评。
He tends to editorialise even in his supposedly objective reports.  即使在他所谓的客观报道中，他也倾向于发表个人意见。
synonyms: comment, opine, offer an opinion, pontificate
antonyms: report, state facts, be objective
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From editorial + -ize.  源自 editorial（社论） + -ize（动词后缀）。
