lymphocytotoxic: /ˌlɪmfəˌsaɪtəˈtɒksɪk/ (UK) /ˌlɪmfəˌsaɪtəˈtɑːksɪk/ (US)| adj.| 淋巴细胞毒性的 |Destructive to lymphocytes.  Capable of killing or damaging lymphocytes. |例句：The lymphocytotoxic antibody test is used to detect antibodies that can kill lymphocytes. 淋巴细胞毒性抗体测试用于检测可以杀死淋巴细胞的抗体。

例句：Certain drugs can be lymphocytotoxic, suppressing the immune system. 某些药物可能具有淋巴细胞毒性，会抑制免疫系统。|近义词：cytotoxic, lymphotoxic |反义词:immunostimulatory |词性: adjective
