lifehold:/ˈlaɪfˌhəʊld/| n.|生命维持; 生命线; 救命稻草|A lifehold is something that you hold onto very tightly in order to avoid falling or being swept away. It can also refer to something that someone depends on completely.|The rope was his only lifehold as he dangled from the cliff.  这根绳子是他悬挂在悬崖上的唯一救命稻草。| The community center is a lifehold for many elderly people.  社区中心是许多老年人的生命线。| lifeline, support, anchor | opposite: (No direct opposite, but consider) threat, danger | noun
