embitterer:/ɪmˈbɪtərər/ | n. | 使人痛苦的人或事 |  Something or someone that causes someone to become bitter or resentful. | The constant criticism from his boss was the embitterer of his life. 他老板不断的批评是他生活中痛苦的根源。| 近义词: spoiler, bane, nemesis | 反义词: comforter, solace | 词性: noun

embitterer:/ɪmˈbɪtərər/ | n. | 使情况恶化的事物 | Something that makes a situation worse. | The new tax law was an embitterer for small businesses. 新的税法使小企业的情况雪上加霜。| 近义词: aggravator, exacerbator | 反义词: ameliorator, mitigator | 词性: noun
