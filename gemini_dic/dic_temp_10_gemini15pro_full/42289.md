counter-incentive:/ˌkaʊntərɪnˈsɛntɪv/ | n. | 反激励；抑制因素 |Something that discourages a particular activity or behavior. |  The high taxes on cigarettes are a counter-incentive to smoking.  香烟的高税收是对吸烟的一种反激励。

The complexity of the application process acts as a counter-incentive for potential applicants. 复杂的申请流程对潜在申请者来说是一种抑制因素。 | 近义词: deterrent, discouragement, impediment | 反义词: incentive, encouragement, motivation | 词性: 名词
