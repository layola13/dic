nonconscientiously: /ˌnɒnkɒnʃiˈɛnʃəsli/
词性: adverb
中文翻译:  不认真地，不尽责地，马虎地
英文解析: In a manner that lacks conscientiousness; without due care, attention, or diligence.  It describes actions performed without a strong sense of moral obligation or concern for details.
例句: He nonconscientiously completed the assignment, resulting in a poor grade.  他马虎地完成了作业，结果成绩很差。

例句:  The worker nonconscientiously swept the floor, leaving dirt in the corners.  工人不认真地扫了地，角落里还留着灰尘。

近义词: carelessly, negligently, irresponsibly, perfunctorily
反义词: conscientiously, diligently, meticulously, carefully
词性: 副词 (adverb)
