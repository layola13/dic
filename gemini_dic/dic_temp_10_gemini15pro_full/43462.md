crashers:/ˈkræʃərz/| n. |不速之客，闯入者|People who attend a party or other event without being invited. |例句：The party was overrun with crashers.  派对上挤满了不速之客。

例句：Security tried to keep out the crashers.  保安试图阻止不速之客进入。|近义词：gatecrashers, intruders, uninvited guests|反义词:invitees, guests|词性:名词

Etymology: From the verb "crash" (meaning to enter without invitation) + "-ers" (suffix forming nouns denoting persons who perform an action).  "Crash" in this sense originated in the early 20th century.
