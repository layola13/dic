overcritically: /ˌoʊvərˈkrɪtɪkli/ | adv. | 过度批判地，吹毛求疵地 |  In an excessively critical manner; finding fault with everything.  | He reviewed the report overcritically, pointing out every minor flaw. 他过度批判地审查了报告，指出了每一个小瑕疵。 |  hypercritically, excessively critically, captiously | uncritically, leniently | adverb

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "above, over") + "critical" (from Late Latin criticus, from Greek kritikos "able to judge," from krinein "to separate, decide") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix)
