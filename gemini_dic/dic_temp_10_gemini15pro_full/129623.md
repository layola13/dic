non-constant:/ˌnɒnˈkɒnstənt/ | adj. |非常数的，非恒定的| Not constant; varying or changing. | The velocity of the car was non-constant, accelerating and decelerating throughout the journey. 汽车的速度非常数，在整个旅程中都在加速和减速。

The non-constant temperature made it difficult to conduct the experiment.  不恒定的温度使得实验难以进行。 | synonyms: variable, fluctuating, inconstant, unstable | antonyms: constant, steady, fixed, stable | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + constant. "Constant" comes from Latin "constans" (present participle of "constare" - "to stand firm").
