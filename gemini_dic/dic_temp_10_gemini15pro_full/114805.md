mam:/mæm/ | n. | 妈妈；女士；夫人 | A term of respectful address to a woman, especially one older than the speaker.  | Example: Excuse me, mam, could you tell me where the restroom is?  对不起，女士，请问洗手间在哪里？ | Example: My mam always told me to be polite. 我妈妈总是告诉我要有礼貌。 | Synonym: mother, madam, ma'am | Antonym: sir (when used as a term of address) | noun
