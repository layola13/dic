migrating:/ˈmaɪɡreɪtɪŋ/| v. adj.|迁移，移居；（鸟类）迁徙|The process of moving from one place to another, often seasonally or in search of resources. Birds migrating south for the winter.|例句：The geese are migrating south for the winter. 这些鹅正向南迁徙过冬。|近义词：moving, relocating, traveling, journeying|反义词:staying, remaining|词性:Verb (present participle), Adjective

migrating:/ˈmaɪɡreɪtɪŋ/| adj.|迁移的，迁徙的|Describing something that is in the process of migrating.|例句：Migrating birds often fly in large flocks. 迁徙的鸟类通常成群结队地飞行。|近义词：mobile, nomadic|反义词:settled, stationary|词性:Adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *migratus*, past participle of migrare ("to move from one place to another, depart, migrate"), from migro ("I wander, flit, migrate").
