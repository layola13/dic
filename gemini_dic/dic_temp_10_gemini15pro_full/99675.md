interlingual:/ˌɪntərˈlɪŋɡwəl/ | adj. |跨语言的，不同语言间的| Existing between or involving different languages.  |Example: Interlingual communication can be challenging. 跨语言交流可能具有挑战性。|Example:  Machine translation is an example of interlingual technology. 机器翻译是跨语言技术的一个例子。|近义词: multilingual, cross-linguistic |反义词: monolingual, intralingual |词性: adjective

interlingual:/ˌɪntərˈlɪŋɡwəl/ | n. |跨语言体，居间语|A language intended to facilitate communication between speakers of different languages. |Example: Esperanto is sometimes considered an interlingual language. 世界语有时被认为是一种跨语言体。|Example: The development of a true interlingual remains a complex undertaking.  开发一个真正的跨语言体仍然是一项复杂的任务。|近义词:auxiliary language, lingua franca|反义词:vernacular, native language|词性:noun
