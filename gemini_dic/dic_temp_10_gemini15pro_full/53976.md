discrete:/dɪˈskriːt/| adj.|离散的，不连续的，分离的，独特的|Individually separate and distinct.|例句：The process involved a series of discrete steps.  这个过程包括一系列独立的步骤。|例句：Speech sounds are produced as a continuous flow of sound, not as discrete units. 语音是作为连续的声音流产生的，而不是作为离散的单元。|近义词：separate, distinct, individual, discontinuous|反义词：continuous, connected, attached|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin discretus, past participle of discernere "to separate, distinguish," from dis- "apart" + cernere "to sift, separate, distinguish."
