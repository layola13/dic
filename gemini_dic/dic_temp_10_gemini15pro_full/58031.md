drogues:/drəʊɡz/ /drʌɡz/
|n.| 毒品；劣质商品|Drugs are illegal and harmful substances. 毒品是非法且有害的物质。|Drugs can ruin people's lives. 毒品会毁掉人的一生。|近义词：narcotics, dope|反义词: medicine|词性:名词

词源:
From French *drogue*, from Middle Dutch *droge vate* (“dried barrels”), from *droge* (“dry”) + *vate* (“vat, barrel”). Originally referred to dried herbs, spices, and other medicinal substances kept in barrels.
