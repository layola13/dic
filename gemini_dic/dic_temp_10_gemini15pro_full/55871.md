divisibilities:/dɪˌvɪzɪˈbɪlɪtiz/| n. |可除性，整除性|The property of being divisible by a number.  The divisibilities of a number are the different ways it can be divided evenly by other numbers.|The teacher explained the divisibilities of 12. 老师讲解了12的整除性。|Synonym: divisibility (singular form)|Antonym: indivisibility|noun
