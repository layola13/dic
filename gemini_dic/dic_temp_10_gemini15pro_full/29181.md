cashew:/ˈkæʃuː/ (US), /ˈkæʃuː/ (UK)| n.|腰果|A kidney-shaped nut that is edible and rich in oil. It grows on a tropical American tree related to the mango.|例句：I love to snack on cashews. 我喜欢吃腰果零食。

例句：Cashew nuts are a good source of protein. 腰果是蛋白质的良好来源。|近义词：nut, cashew nut|反义词: None (Cashews don't have a direct opposite)|词性:noun
