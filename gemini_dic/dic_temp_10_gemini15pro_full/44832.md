crustaceans:/krʌˈsteɪʃənz/
|n.|甲壳类动物
|Aquatic arthropods with hard, external shells, typically including crabs, lobsters, shrimps, and barnacles.|
|Lobsters and crabs are crustaceans. 龙虾和螃蟹是甲壳类动物。|
|Crabs are my favorite crustaceans. 螃蟹是我最喜欢的甲壳类动物。|
|shellfish, seafood|
