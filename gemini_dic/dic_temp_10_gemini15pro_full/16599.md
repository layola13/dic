baroque: /bəˈroʊk/ /bəˈrɒk/ | adj. n. | 巴洛克式的；巴洛克风格；奇形怪状的；巴洛克式艺术（或建筑）|  A style of European architecture, music, and art of the 17th and 18th centuries characterized by ornate detail.  Derived from the Portuguese word "barroco" meaning "irregular pearl". | The palace is a magnificent example of baroque architecture. 这座宫殿是巴洛克式建筑的宏伟典范。|  ornate, elaborate, flamboyant | simple, plain | adjective, noun
