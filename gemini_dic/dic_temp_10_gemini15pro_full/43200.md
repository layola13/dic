crabbled:/ˈkræbəld/
verb
抓螃蟹; 捕蟹; 横行; 抱怨
past tense of crab
He crabbled along the beach. 他在沙滩上横行。
近义词: caught, trapped, snared, grumbled, complained
反义词: released, freed, praised, complimented
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From crab + -le (frequentative suffix).  The original sense was "to fish for crabs."  The sense of "to move sideways like a crab" and "to grumble" are later developments.
