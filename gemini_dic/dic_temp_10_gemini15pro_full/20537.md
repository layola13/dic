blabbed:/blæbd/
verb
泄露（秘密），胡说八道
revealed secrets or told something indiscreetly.
He blabbed everything to the police. 他把一切都告诉了警察。
synonyms: divulged, revealed, tattled, gossiped
antonyms: concealed, hid, kept secret
词性:动词

blab:/blæb/
verb
泄露（秘密），胡说八道
reveal secrets or tell something indiscreetly.
Don't blab about our plans. 不要泄露我们的计划。
synonyms: divulge, reveal, tattle, gossip
antonyms: conceal, hide, keep secret
词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English blabben, probably of imitative origin.  Relates to the sound of indiscreet or foolish talk.
