bisectionally:/ˌbaɪsɛkʃənəli/
adv.
二等分地，对半分地
in a manner that bisects something; by cutting or dividing into two equal parts.
The line bisects the circle bisectionally. 这条线二等分了这个圆。
近义词: equally, evenly
反义词: unequally, unevenly
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From bisection + -ally.  Bisection comes from Latin *bisectio, from bisecare "to cut in two," from bi- "two" + secare "to cut."
