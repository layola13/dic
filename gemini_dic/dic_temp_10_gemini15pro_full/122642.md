modifiers:/ˈmɒdɪfaɪəz/ | n. | 修饰语 |  A word, phrase, or clause that qualifies or describes another word or phrase.  A modifier adds information about another word. | Example: In "the red car," "red" is a modifier describing the noun "car."  在“红色的车”中，“红色”是修饰名词“车”的修饰语。 |  Example:  In "He runs quickly", "quickly" modifies the verb "runs". 在“他跑得很快”中，“quickly”修饰动词“runs”。| Synonyms: qualifiers, descriptors | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
