conducent:/kənˈdʌs(ə)nt/| adj.|传导的，导电的|Having the property of conducting something (especially heat or electricity).|Copper is a highly conducent material. 铜是高度导电的材料。|The conducent properties of this metal make it ideal for wiring. 这种金属的导电性使其成为理想的布线材料。|近义词：conductive, transmitting|反义词：nonconductive, insulating|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *conducēns*, present participle of conducere "to lead together," from com- "together" + ducere "to lead."
