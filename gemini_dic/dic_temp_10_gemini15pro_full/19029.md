betake:/bɪˈteɪk/
verb
前往；致力于
to go to a place, especially for rest or shelter; to apply oneself to something
He betook himself to his studies. 他专心致志地学习。
近义词：go, repair, resort, proceed
反义词：leave, depart
词性：v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bitaken*, from Old English *betæcan* "to entrust, commit, deliver," from *be-* "about, around" + *tæcan* "to teach, show."  The sense shifted from "commit oneself to" to "go to, resort to."
