paraschematic:/ˌpærəskiːˈmætɪk/
adjective
准图式的，近乎图式的
partially or almost schematic; resembling a schema but not completely conforming to it.
This theory offers a paraschematic understanding of the complex social dynamics at play.  这个理论提供了一种对复杂社会动态的准图式理解。
The artist's paraschematic drawings hinted at the underlying structure of the natural world.  这位艺术家的准图式绘画暗示了自然世界的底层结构。
Near synonyms:  quasi-schematic, partially schematic,  sketchy, rudimentary
Antonyms: detailed, comprehensive, thorough
词性: adjective
