directoire:/dəˌrɛktˈwɑːr/
n.
督政府时期（风格）
A style in furniture and women's clothing, popular in France during the Directoire period (1795-99).
The directoire style was a reaction against the excesses of the previous regime.  督政府时期风格是对前政权过度行为的一种反弹。
The museum has an impressive collection of directoire furniture.  这家博物馆收藏了令人印象深刻的督政府时期家具。
近义词:French neoclassical, Empire style
反义词:Rococo, Baroque
词性:noun


Etymology:
From French *Directoire*, "Directory", referring to the Directory, the five-member committee which governed France from 1795 to 1799.
