connect:/kəˈnɛkt/ (US) /kəˈnɛkt/ (UK)| v. n. adj.|连接，联系，接通；连接物；有关系的|To join or link two or more things together; to establish communication or a relationship.|例句：The two cities are connected by a highway. 这两座城市由一条高速公路连接起来。|近义词：join, link, unite, associate, relate|反义词:disconnect, separate, detach|verb

connect:/kəˈnɛkt/ (US) /kəˈnɛkt/ (UK)| n.|连接，联系；连接物；熟人|A point or means of connection; a person one knows, especially professionally or socially.|例句：He has many useful connects in the industry. 他在业内有很多有用的熟人。|近义词：link, contact, association|反义词:separation, detachment|noun

connect:/kəˈnɛkt/ (US) /kəˈnɛkt/ (UK)| adj.|连接的，关联的 (主要用于构成复合词，例如 connect-the-dots)|Joined or linked together.|例句：This is a connect-the-dots puzzle. 这是一个连点成线的谜题。|近义词：joined, linked, related|反义词:disconnected, separated, unrelated|adjective
