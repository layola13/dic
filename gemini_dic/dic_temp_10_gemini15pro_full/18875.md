beshrew:/bɪˈʃruː/
verb
诅咒，咒骂
To curse or invoke evil upon.
Beshrew me if I know what he means. 我要是知道他的意思，我就天打雷劈。
Synonyms: curse, damn, execrate
Antonyms: bless, praise
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *beshreuen*, from *be-* (prefix meaning 'thoroughly') + *shreven* (to curse), from Old English *scrēawian* (to curse).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *skrēwwaną*.
