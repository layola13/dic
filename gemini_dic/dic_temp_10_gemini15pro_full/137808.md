nonverminously:/nɒnˈvɜːmɪnəsli/
adjective
无害虫地;非寄生虫地
in a way that does not involve vermin or parasites.  Free from vermin; not infested with vermin.  Figuratively, in a way that is not harmful or destructive.
The food was stored nonverminously, ensuring it was safe to eat. 食物被无害虫地储存，确保可以安全食用。
The house was kept surprisingly nonverminously considering its age and location. 考虑到房屋的年龄和位置，它被保持得异常干净，没有害虫。
Synonyms: cleanly, hygienically, sanitarily
Antonyms: verminously, infested, polluted
词性: adjective
