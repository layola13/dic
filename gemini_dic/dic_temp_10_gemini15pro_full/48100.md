declinate:/ˈdɛklɪneɪt/ /dɪˈklaɪneɪt/
verb
拒绝; 衰落; 倾斜
to refuse politely; to bend or turn downwards; to deviate or move away from a particular direction.
The minister declined to comment on the scandal. 部长拒绝评论这起丑闻。
The sun declinated towards the west. 太阳向西倾斜。
refuse, reject, turn down, avoid
accept, agree, assent
v.

declinate:/ˈdɛklɪneɪt/ /dɪˈklaɪneɪt/
adjective
倾斜的
bent or turned downwards.
The declinate branches of the weeping willow trailed in the water. 垂柳倾斜的树枝垂在水中。
sloping, descending, inclined
upright, vertical, erect
adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin declinatus, past participle of declinare ("to bend down, turn aside"), from de- ("down, away") + clinare ("to lean, bend").
