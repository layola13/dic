grouser:/ˈɡraʊzər/| n.|履带板，防滑钉，抱怨者|A heavy cleated chain or metal plate which is attached to the track of a vehicle such as a tractor or tank to increase its grip on soft ground. Also, a person who complains a lot.|The tractor got stuck in the mud because a grouser was broken.|近义词：cleat, tread, grip, complainer, grumbler|反义词:praiser|noun
