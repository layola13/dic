flood:/flʌd/ | n. vt. vi.|洪水，泛滥，大量涌入|A large amount of water covering an area that is usually dry.  Also, to cause such a situation or to be overwhelmed by a large number of things.|The river overflowed its banks and caused a flood. 河水漫过河岸，造成了洪水。|近义词：deluge, inundation, torrent|反义词:drought, ebb|noun, verb

flood:/flʌd/ | vt. vi.|淹没，充斥，涌入|To fill or cover something completely with water or a large number of things.|The stadium was flooded with fans.  体育场挤满了球迷。|近义词：inundate, overwhelm, swamp|反义词:drain, empty|verb
