pasteurize:/ˈpæstʃəˌraɪz/ | v. | 巴氏消毒，巴氏杀菌 | to heat (a food, especially milk) to a specific temperature to destroy harmful bacteria but without changing the flavor or nutritional value | The company pasteurizes the juice before bottling it.  这家公司在装瓶前对果汁进行巴氏消毒。| sterilize, disinfect | contaminate | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *pasteuriser*,  from Louis Pasteur, French chemist and microbiologist (1822–1895) + -iser.
