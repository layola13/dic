culprit: /ˈkʌlprɪt/ | n. | 罪犯，肇事者，问题的根源 | A person who has committed a crime or done something wrong; the cause of a problem or difficulty. | The police finally caught the culprit who had been robbing banks. 警方终于抓住了那个一直在抢劫银行的罪犯。

The culprit behind the computer crash was a faulty power supply. 导致电脑崩溃的罪魁祸首是电源故障。 | synonyms: offender, perpetrator, criminal, guilty party, villain, miscreant | antonyms: victim, innocent | noun
