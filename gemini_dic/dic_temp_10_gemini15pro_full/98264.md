inserting:/ɪnˈsɜːtɪŋ/ |adj. |插入的，嵌入的 |Present participle of "insert," meaning the act of putting something into something else. |
He is inserting the key into the lock. 他正在把钥匙插入锁中。

She is inserting a picture into the document. 她正在把一张图片插入文档中。

|近义词：placing, putting, introducing, incorporating|反义词：removing, extracting, withdrawing|v.
