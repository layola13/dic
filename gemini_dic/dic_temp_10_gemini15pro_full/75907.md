funds:/fʌndz/
|n.|资金，基金；现款|A sum of money saved or made available for a particular purpose.|The company has secured funds for the new project.  公司已为新项目获得资金。|synonyms: capital, resources, assets, money, cash|antonyms: debt, liabilities|noun


funds:/fʌndz/
|v.|为…提供资金|To provide money for (something)|The government is funding a new education program. 政府正在资助一项新的教育计划。|synonyms: finance, back, support, subsidize|antonyms: defund|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *fons* (plural *fons*), from Latin *fundus* ("bottom, foundation, piece of land, farm"). Doublet of *fond* and *found* (verb).
