fifteenth:/fɪfˈtiːnθ/ | adj. | 第十五的；十五分之一的 |  The fifteenth of March is my birthday. | Example: This is the fifteenth time I've told you this.  这是我第十五次告诉你这件事了。| Synonym: ordinal | Antonym: N/A | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *fīftēoða (attested only as a variant of fīftēna "fifteen"), from fīf "five" + -tēoða, ordinal suffix related to -tēne.
