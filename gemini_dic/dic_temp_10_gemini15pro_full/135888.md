nonprovocations:/ˌnɒnˌprɒvəˈkeɪʃənz/
noun
非挑衅行为，无挑衅行为
Absence of actions or behaviors that are likely to provoke a reaction, especially a hostile or aggressive one.
The ceasefire held despite minor nonprovocations from both sides.  尽管双方都有一些小的非挑衅行为，停火协议仍然维持。
The diplomat emphasized the importance of nonprovocations to maintain peace.  这位外交官强调了非挑衅行为对于维护和平的重要性。
Synonyms:  pacifism, peacefulness, non-aggression, non-interference
Antonyms:  provocations, aggression, hostility, incitement
