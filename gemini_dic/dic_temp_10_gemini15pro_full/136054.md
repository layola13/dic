ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + recalcitrant.  "Recalcitrant" comes from Latin "recalcitrare," meaning "to kick back," from "re-" (back) + "calcitrare" (to kick), ultimately from "calx" (heel).  Therefore, "nonrecalcitrancy" signifies the absence of kicking back or resisting.
