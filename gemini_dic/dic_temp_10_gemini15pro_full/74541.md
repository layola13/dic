fratricide:/ˈfrætrɪsaɪd/
n.
兄弟相残；杀害兄弟的人
The act of killing one's brother or sister.
Cain committed fratricide when he killed Abel.  该隐杀了亚伯，犯了杀害兄弟的罪。
近义词：sibling murder, sororicide (specifically killing one's sister)
反义词：(No direct antonym, potentially "brotherly love", "sibling harmony")
词性：noun

From Late Latin *fratricidium, from Latin frāter (“brother”) + -cīdium, from caedere (“to cut, kill”).
