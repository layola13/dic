assaying:/əˈseɪɪŋ/| n. |化验，分析; 鉴定; 尝试; 试验|The process of determining the content or quality of a metal or ore, or the potency of a drug.|例句：The assaying of the gold ore revealed a high concentration of the precious metal. 对金矿石的化验表明其中含有高浓度的贵金属。

例句：The chemist was assaying the purity of the new compound.  化学家正在分析新化合物的纯度。|近义词：analyzing, testing, evaluating, examining|反义词: synthesizing, compounding|词性:Noun (Gerund)


assaying:/əˈseɪɪŋ/| v. |化验，分析; 鉴定; 尝试; 试验|To examine and determine the content, quality, or potency of something.|例句：They assayed the soil for traces of pollutants. 他们化验了土壤中污染物的痕迹。

例句：The company is assaying new methods of production.  这家公司正在尝试新的生产方法。|近义词：analyze, test, evaluate, examine|反义词: synthesize, compound|词性:Verb (Present Participle)
