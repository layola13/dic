nonundergraduate:/ˌnɒnˌʌndəˈɡrædʒuɪt/ | adj. | 非本科的；非大学生的 | Not relating to or characteristic of an undergraduate student or their studies.  |  This library section is for nonundergraduate students.  这个图书馆区域是为非本科生准备的。 | The journal publishes articles geared towards a nonundergraduate audience. 这份期刊发表的文章面向非本科生读者。 | 近义词：graduate, postgraduate | 反义词：undergraduate | 词性: 形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non.
undergraduate (noun/adjective):  From under + graduate.
