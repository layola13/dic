lentoid:/ˈlɛntɔɪd/| adj. n.|透镜状的，扁豆状的；透镜体|Resembling a lens in shape; biconvex.  A lenticular body. |The fish has small, lentoid scales. 这条鱼有小的、透镜状的鳞片。|The doctor discovered a small, lentoid opacity in the patient's eye. 医生在病人的眼睛里发现了一个小的、透镜状的混浊物。|lens-shaped, lenticular|spherical, globular|adjective, noun
Etymology->
From Latin *lentoidem, from lent-, lens (“lentil”) + -oidem, -oid (“-oid”).
