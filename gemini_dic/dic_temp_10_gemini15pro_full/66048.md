exasperate:/ɪɡˈzæspəreɪt/ /ɪɡˈzɑːspəreɪt/| vt.|激怒，使恼怒|To irritate intensely; infuriate.|Her constant complaining exasperated me. 她不断的抱怨激怒了我。|Synonyms: annoy, irritate, infuriate, provoke, anger |Antonyms: soothe, calm, placate|verb
exasperating:/ɪɡˈzæspəreɪtɪŋ/ /ɪɡˈzɑːspəreɪtɪŋ/| adj.|令人恼火的|Intensely irritating; infuriating.|The exasperating delay made me miss my flight. 恼人的延误使我错过了航班。|Synonyms: annoying, irritating, frustrating |Antonyms: soothing, calming, pleasing|adjective
exasperation:/ɪɡˌzæspəˈreɪʃn/ /ɪɡˌzɑːspəˈreɪʃn/| n.|恼怒，激怒|A feeling of intense irritation or annoyance.|She sighed in exasperation. 她恼怒地叹了口气。|Synonyms: annoyance, irritation, frustration, anger |Antonyms: calmness, patience, composure|noun
