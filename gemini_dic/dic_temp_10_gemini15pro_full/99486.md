interfiber:/ˌɪntərˈfaɪbər/
adjective
纤维间的；存在于纤维之间的
Existing or occurring between fibers.
The interfiber bonding strength is crucial for the material's durability.  纤维间的结合强度对材料的耐久性至关重要。
