alertness:/əˈlɜːrtnəs/| n.|警觉性，警惕性|The state of being watchful and prompt to meet danger or emergency.  A state of readiness to respond. |The security guard maintained a high level of alertness throughout the night. 保安整夜保持高度警觉。|近义词：vigilance, watchfulness, awareness|反义词：inattentiveness, drowsiness, apathy|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From alert + -ness.  Alert (adjective) comes from Italian all'erta "on the watch," from erta "watch," from Latin erigere "to erect, raise."
