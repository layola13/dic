materializer:/məˈtɪəriəˌlaɪzər/ | n. | 物质化器；实现者；具体化的人 |  A person or thing that materializes, that is, causes something to appear in physical form or to become actual fact. | The inventor claimed his device was a materializer that could create food from thin air.  发明家声称他的装置是一个可以凭空造出食物的物质化器。 | synonyms: realizer, actualizer, concretizer | antonyms: destroyer, dematerializer | noun

materializer:/məˈtɪəriəˌlaɪzər/ | n. | 物质化器；实现者；具体化的人 |  Someone who brings ideas or plans into reality. | He was a true materializer, turning his vision for the company into a multi-million dollar business. 他是一个真正的实现者，将他对公司的愿景变成了一个价值数百万美元的企业。 | synonyms: implementer, executor, developer | antonyms: dreamer, idealist | noun
