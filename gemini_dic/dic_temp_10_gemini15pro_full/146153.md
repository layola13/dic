overentertained:/ˌoʊvərˌɛntərˈteɪnd/
adjective
过度娱乐的
Having been entertained to an excessive degree; having had too much entertainment.
The children were overentertained during the holidays and became restless and bored when they returned to school.  孩子们假期里玩得过度了，回到学校后变得焦躁不安。
The overentertained audience seemed jaded and unresponsive to the comedian's jokes.  过度娱乐的观众似乎已经麻木了，对喜剧演员的笑话反应冷淡。
近义词: oversaturated, overstimulated
反义词: bored, understimulated
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively, too much") + "entertain" (verb meaning "to provide amusement or enjoyment for")
