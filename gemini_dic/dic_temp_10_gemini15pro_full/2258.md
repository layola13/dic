adeli:/əˈdeɪli/| n.|阿德利企鹅|A species of penguin (Pygoscelis adeliae) that breeds on the Antarctic coast and adjacent islands.|We saw a large colony of Adelie penguins on the ice floe. 我们在浮冰上看到了一大群阿德利企鹅。|近义词:penguin|反义词:|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French Adélie Land, named after Adèle Dumont d'Urville, wife of French explorer Jules Dumont d'Urville. 源于法语 Adélie Land，以法国探险家 Jules Dumont d'Urville 的妻子 Adèle Dumont d'Urville 的名字命名。

USAGE->
The Adelie penguin is a common sight in Antarctica.  阿德利企鹅在南极洲很常见。

The Adelie penguins were busy building their nests.  阿德利企鹅正忙着筑巢。
