herded:/ˈhɜːrdɪd/ /ˈhɝːdɪd/
verb
放牧；驱赶；聚集
moved or controlled a large group of animals or people
The shepherd herded the sheep into the pen. 牧羊人把羊群赶进了羊圈。


近义词:corralled, rounded up, gathered, shepherded
反义词:scattered, dispersed
词性:Verb (Past tense and past participle of "herd")
