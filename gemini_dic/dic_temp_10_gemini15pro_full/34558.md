clear-sightedness:/ˌklɪərˈsaɪtɪdnəs/| n.|远见卓识；洞察力；清晰的视野|The ability to understand situations or people clearly and make good judgments.  The quality of having clear vision.|She demonstrated remarkable clear-sightedness in her assessment of the political situation. 她对政治局势的评估展现出非凡的远见。|近义词：perspicacity, acumen, discernment, insight, vision|反义词：short-sightedness, myopia, obtuseness, blindness|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From clear + -sighted + -ness.  Clear ultimately from Latin *clarus. -sighted from sight + -ed. -ness from Old English -nes(s).
