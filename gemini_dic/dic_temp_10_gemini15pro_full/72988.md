foolisher:/ˈfuːlɪʃər/| adj.|更愚蠢的|Comparative form of foolish: more foolish.  Means exhibiting a greater lack of good sense or judgment.|例句：That was an even foolisher decision than the last one. 那是一个比上一个更愚蠢的决定。|近义词：sillier, more stupid, more senseless|反义词:wiser, smarter, more intelligent|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *folischer*, comparative of *folish* (“foolish”), from Old English *fōl* (“foolish”) + *-isc* (“-ish”).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *fōlaz* (“foolish”).
