governability:/ˌɡʌvərnəˈbɪləti/| n.|可 управляемость; 可治理性|The governability of a country depends on many factors, including its political stability and economic strength. 一个国家的可治理性取决于许多因素，包括其政治稳定性和经济实力。|例句：The recent unrest has raised serious questions about the country's governability. 最近的动乱让人们对该国的可治理性产生了严重的疑问。

The new prime minister’s main challenge will be restoring governability to the nation. 新总理的主要挑战将是恢复国家的可治理性。


|近义词：controllability, manageability|反义词: ungovernability, chaos|词性:noun
