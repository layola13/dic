immiscibly:/ɪˈmɪsɪbli/
adverb|不能混合地，不互溶地|Not capable of being mixed; incapable of forming a homogeneous mixture.|Oil and water blend immiscibly. 油和水不能互溶。|
近义词:insolubly, unmixably
反义词:miscibly, solubly
词性:副词

Etymology:
From immiscible + -ly.  Immiscible comes from in- "not" + miscible "mixable," from Latin miscere "to mix."
