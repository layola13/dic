earthlier:/ˈɜːrθliər/
adjective
更早的;更世俗的
Belonging to an earlier stage of the earth's history; more worldly.
The earthlier rocks are found below the more recent strata. 更早期的岩石位于较新的地层之下。
She had earthlier concerns, like paying the rent. 她有更世俗的担忧，比如付房租。
近义词:earlier, more mundane
反义词:later, more spiritual
词性:adjective

词源:
From earth + -ly + -er.  Relating to earth, in the sense of being more connected to the material world or occurring earlier in the Earth's history.
