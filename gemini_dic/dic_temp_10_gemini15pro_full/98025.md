inordinate:/ɪnˈɔːrdɪnət/ /ɪnˈɔːrdənət/| adj.|过度的，过分的，无节制的|Exceeding reasonable limits; excessive.|例句：He spent an inordinate amount of time playing video games. 他花了过多的时间玩电子游戏。|近义词：excessive, immoderate, exorbitant, undue|反义词：moderate, reasonable, temperate|词性:形容词
Etymology: Late Middle English: from Latin inordinatus, from in- ‘not’ + ordinatus ‘ordered, regulated’ (from ordo ‘order’).
