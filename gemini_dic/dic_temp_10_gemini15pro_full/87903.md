hieratic:/ˌhaɪəˈrætɪk/
adjective
僧侣的，神职的；(尤指古埃及绘画、雕塑) hieratic style 僧侣体的，祭司体的
Of or relating to a cursive form of hieroglyphic writing used by priests in ancient Egypt.  与古埃及祭司使用的象形文字草书形式有关的。
The tomb was covered in hieratic inscriptions.  墓穴上覆盖着僧侣体的铭文。
Synonyms: priestly, sacred, ceremonial, ritualistic
Antonyms: secular, lay, profane
adjective

hieratic:/ˌhaɪəˈrætɪk/
noun
祭司体
A cursive form of ancient Egyptian writing.  古埃及文字的一种草书形式。
The hieratic script was used for religious texts.  僧侣体文字用于宗教文本。
Synonyms: cursive script, priestly script
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but could consider "demotic script" as a contrasting term, representing a more popular, less formal script)
noun
