Auschwitz: /ˈaʊʃvɪts/ | n. | 奥斯维辛 | A town in southern Poland, the site of a Nazi concentration camp during World War II. |  Millions of Jews and other minorities were systematically murdered at Auschwitz.  数百万犹太人和其他少数民族在奥斯维辛被系统地杀害。 | synonyms: concentration camp, death camp | antonyms: sanctuary, haven | noun

Etymology: From German _Auschwitz_, derived from Polish _Oświęcim_.
