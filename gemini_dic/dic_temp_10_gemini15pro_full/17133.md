battiest:/ˈbætiɪst/| adj.|最疯狂的，最古怪的|Most eccentric or crazy.|例句：He has some of the battiest ideas I've ever heard. 他有一些我听过的最疯狂的想法。|例句：She's the battiest artist in the whole gallery. 她是整个画廊里最古怪的艺术家。|近义词：craziest, maddest, wackiest|反义词：sanest, most rational|词性:形容词
詞源: From batty (crazy) + -est (superlative suffix). Ultimately from bat (n.), referring to bats being perceived as erratic and unpredictable.
