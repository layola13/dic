deculassment:/deɪˈkʌləsmɑːnt/ |n.|  去文化化，文化剥夺|The deculassment of indigenous populations through residential schools is a tragic chapter in history. 通过寄宿学校对土著居民进行去文化化是历史上悲惨的一章。|  The forced assimilation policies led to the deculassment of many minority groups. 强制同化政策导致许多少数群体被剥夺了文化。| 近义词: cultural alienation, cultural deprivation, acculturation | 反义词: enculturation, cultural preservation | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From French *déculasser*, from *dé-* (reversal, removal) + *culasse* (breech, buttstock).  Originally a military term referring to the removal of the breech of a firearm.  Figuratively extended to mean the stripping away of someone's cultural identity.
