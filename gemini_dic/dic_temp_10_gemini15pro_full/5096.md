allayment:/əˈleɪmənt/| n. | 减轻，缓和，平静 |The allayment of fears/concerns. 对恐惧/担忧的缓和。|例句：The medicine provided some allayment of his pain.  这种药减轻了他的疼痛。

例句：Her calming words brought an allayment to the tension in the room. 她抚慰人心的话缓和了房间里的紧张气氛。

|近义词：relief, alleviation, mitigation, assuagement |反义词: aggravation, intensification|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *alayment, from Anglo-Norman aleiement, aleiment, from aleier, aleer (“to alleviate, allay”), from Late Latin *alleviare, from Latin ad (“to”) + levis (“light”).
