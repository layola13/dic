froglike:/ˈfrɒɡˌlaɪk/ (US) /ˈfrɑːɡˌlaɪk/| adj.|像青蛙的，蛙形的|Resembling a frog in appearance or behavior.|The creature had large, froglike eyes.  这个生物有着大大的、像青蛙一样的眼睛。|
The toad's skin was bumpy and froglike.  这只蟾蜍的皮肤疙疙瘩瘩的，像青蛙一样。|近义词：amphibian, batrachian|反义词: (No direct opposite, consider "unfroglike", "mammalian", "avian" etc. depending on context)|词性: adjective
