archlet: /ˈɑːrklet/ | n. | 小拱, 小拱券 | A small arch, often used as a decorative element. | The archlets in the cloister added a touch of elegance.  修道院中的小拱券增添了一丝优雅。 | 近义词:  arch, arcuation | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "straight line" in a design context) | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From the Old French "arcelet," a diminutive of "arc" (arch), ultimately from Latin "arcus" (bow, arch).
