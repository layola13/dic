mesentery:/ˈmezənˌteri/ | n. | 肠系膜 | A fold of the peritoneum that attaches the stomach, small intestine, pancreas, spleen, and other organs to the posterior wall of the abdomen. | The surgeon carefully examined the patient's mesentery. 外科医生仔细检查了病人的肠系膜。 |  The mesentery connects the intestines to the abdominal wall. 肠系膜将肠连接到腹壁。 | Synonyms: peritoneum (部分同义),  abdominal lining | Antonyms: None | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *mesenterium, from Ancient Greek μεσέντερον (mesénteron, “mid-gut”), from μέσος (mésos, “middle”) + ἔντερον (énteron, “intestine”).
