disannulment:/ˌdɪsəˈnʌlmənt/
n.
废除，取消，撤销
The act of disannulling something, typically a marriage or law.
The disannulment of their marriage was granted by the court. 法庭批准了他们婚姻的废除。
近义词: annulment, cancellation, revocation, invalidation
反义词: ratification, validation, confirmation
词性: 名词
