bagh: /bɑːɡ/ | n. | garden, park | From Persian باغ (bāgh) meaning "garden," "park," or "orchard." Refers to an enclosed area, often with cultivated plants and trees. In some Indian languages, it can also refer to a tiger's lair. |  The Mughal emperors built magnificent baghs throughout India.  莫卧儿皇帝在整个印度建造了宏伟的巴格花园。| synonyms: garden, park, orchard | antonyms: wilderness, desert | noun
