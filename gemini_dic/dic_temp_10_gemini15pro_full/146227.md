overexploited: /ˌoʊvərɪkˈsplɔɪtɪd/
adjective
过度开发的；过度利用的
Having been exploited excessively.  Resources that are used more quickly than they can regenerate.
例句：The overexploited fishing grounds are now barren.  过度捕捞的渔场现在已经荒芜了。

例句：The Amazon rainforest is being overexploited for timber. 亚马逊雨林正因木材而被过度开发。

近义词：overused, overtaxed, depleted
反义词：underutilized, conserved, protected
词性：adjective


词源:
From over- + exploited, the past participle of exploit.  "Over-" indicates an excess or surpassing of a limit. "Exploit" comes from Old French "exploitier" meaning "to make use of, to accomplish."  Ultimately from Latin "explicitare" meaning "to unfold, to explain."
