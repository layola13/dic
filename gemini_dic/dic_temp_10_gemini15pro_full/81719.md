greenlier:/ˈɡriːnliər/ | adj. | 更环保的，更绿色的 | More environmentally friendly; exhibiting more qualities associated with environmentalism or ecological awareness. | The company is striving for greenlier practices to reduce its carbon footprint.  该公司正在努力推行更环保的做法以减少碳足迹。|  近义词：more eco-friendly, more sustainable | 反义词：less eco-friendly, less sustainable | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From green + -lier (comparative adjective suffix).  The suffix -lier is a variant of -ly used with some adjectives ending in -n.
