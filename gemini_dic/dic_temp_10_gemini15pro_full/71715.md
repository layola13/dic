flavourless:/ˈfleɪvələs/| adj.|无味的；淡而无味的|Lacking flavour; tasteless.|The soup was disappointingly flavourless. 这汤淡而无味，真令人失望。|Synonyms: bland, tasteless, insipid, unappetizing|Antonyms: flavourful, tasty, delicious, savoury|adjective
