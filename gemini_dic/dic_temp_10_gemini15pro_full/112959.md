luigi:/luˈiːdʒi/ | n. | 路易吉 |  A male given name, of Italian origin, a short form of Luigi.  It is most famously associated with the fictional character Luigi, the younger brother of Mario in the Nintendo *Mario* franchise.  | Luigi is a skilled plumber just like his brother. 路易吉和他哥哥一样，是个熟练的水管工。 | Example: Luigi is often portrayed as being timid but loyal.  路易吉通常被描绘成胆小但忠诚的。| Synonym: (As a name, no direct synonym, perhaps "brother" in the context of the *Mario* franchise) | Antonym: (As a name, no antonym) | Noun
