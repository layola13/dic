candock:/ˈkændɑːk/ | n. | 干船坞；浮船坞 | A structure where ships are built or repaired.  | The ship was towed into the candock for repairs. 这艘船被拖进干船坞修理。 |  近义词: dry dock, floating dock | 反义词:  (无直接反义词) | 词性: 名词

词源:  From the Dutch word "kantdok",  meaning "side dock".  "Kant" means side or edge, and "dok" means dock.
