offcuts:/ˈɒfkʌts/ (UK), /ˈɔːfkʌts/ (US)
noun
残料，边角料，零头布
pieces of material that are left over after the main part has been cut
The carpenter used the offcuts of wood to make a small shelf. 木匠用木头的边角料做了一个小架子。
remnants, scraps, leftovers
