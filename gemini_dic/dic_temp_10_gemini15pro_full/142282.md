orsedew:/ˈɔːrsˌdjuː/ /ˈɔːrsˌduː/
n.
horsedew, orsedew
A non-standard spelling of "hoarsedue," which is itself an archaic variant of "hoarfrost."  It refers to the white, frozen dew that forms on cold, clear nights.
The orsedew glistened on the grass.  白霜在草地上闪闪发光。
近义词: hoarfrost, frost
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) dew, warmth
