auxiliary:/ɔːɡˈzɪliəri/ /ɑːɡˈzɪləri/| adj. n.|辅助的，附属的；助动词；辅助者|Giving support or help, but not the most important; a person or thing that gives help.|The hospital ship will serve as an auxiliary medical center. 这艘医院船将充当一个辅助医疗中心。|近义词：supplementary, additional, supporting, reserve, backup|反义词：primary, main, principal|词性：adjective, noun

auxiliary:/ɔːɡˈzɪliəri/ /ɑːɡˈzɪləri/| n.|助动词|A verb (such as have, be, or do) that is used with another verb to show tense, person, etc.|The auxiliary verb "will" indicates future tense. 助动词“will”表示将来时。|近义词：helping verb|反义词：main verb|词性：noun
