institutio: /ɪnstɪˈtjuːʃioʊ/ /ɪnstɪˈtjuːʃiːoʊ/
noun
建立，设立；制度；教导，指导
the act of establishing something; a custom, practice, or law that is established and accepted; education or training
The institutio of new laws is the responsibility of the government. 新法律的设立是政府的责任。
The institutio of marriage is a fundamental social structure. 婚姻制度是一个基本的社会结构。
His institutio in the classics began at a young age. 他对古典文学的学习始于年幼时期。
Synonyms: establishment, foundation, instruction, training
Antonyms: abolition, destruction, termination
