oompahing:/ˈuːmpɑːɪŋ/ | n. | 低音喇叭声；(尤指)伴奏奥姆帕舞的音乐 | The sound of a oompah band, especially one playing for an oompah dance. |  The oompahing of the tuba filled the beer hall. 低音号的嗡嗡声充满了啤酒馆。 | 近义词：brass band music, tuba sound | 反义词：silence, quiet | 词性：名词

oompahing:/ˈuːmpɑːɪŋ/ | adj. |  （指音乐）低沉单调的，嗡嗡作响的 | (of music) having a monotonous, repetitive rhythm. | The oompahing music made me sleepy. 低沉单调的音乐让我昏昏欲睡。 | 近义词：monotonous, repetitive | 反义词：varied, complex | 词性：形容词

词源:  From German *Umpapa*, imitative of the sound.  源自德语 *Umpapa*，模仿声音。
