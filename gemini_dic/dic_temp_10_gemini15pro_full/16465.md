baresthesiometer:/ˌbæriːsˌθiːziˈɒmɪtər/ /ˌbæriːsˌθiːziˈɑːmɪtər/
n.
触觉计，压觉计
An instrument for measuring sensitivity to touch or pressure.
The doctor used a baresthesiometer to assess the patient's nerve damage. 医生使用触觉计来评估病人的神经损伤。
esthesiometer, pressure aesthesiometer
(No common antonyms)
