body-blow:/ˈbɒdi bləʊ/ (UK) /ˈbɑːdi bloʊ/ (US)| n.|打击；沉重打击|A damaging setback; a severe blow to one's hopes, plans, or morale.  It often refers to something that has a significant emotional or psychological impact, as well as a physical one (in its original boxing sense).|The election result was a real body-blow to the party. 选举结果对该党来说是一个真正的打击。

The company's bankruptcy was a body-blow to the local economy. 该公司的破产对当地经济是一个沉重打击。|近义词：setback, shock, jolt, wallop, knockout blow|反义词:boost, encouragement, triumph|词性:noun
