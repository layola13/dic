bromize:/ˈbroʊmaɪz/| v. |溴化|To treat or combine with bromine.  To introduce bromine into a compound.|例句：They bromized the hydrocarbon to create a new compound. 他们将烃溴化以产生新的化合物。

例句：Bromizing the solution changed its color. 溴化溶液改变了它的颜色。|近义词：halogenate|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "debrominate" for removal of bromine)|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From *bromine* + *-ize*.  Bromine derives from the Greek word *brōmos*, meaning "stench," due to its strong and unpleasant odor.
