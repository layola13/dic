organic:/ɔːrˈɡænɪk/ /ɔːrˈɡænɪk/
adjective|有机的，器官的，自然的|Relating to or derived from living matter.|例句：Organic farming avoids the use of synthetic fertilizers. 有机农业避免使用合成肥料。|近义词：natural, biological|反义词：synthetic, artificial
noun|有机物，有机体|A substance that contains carbon and is found in living things.|例句：Soil is rich in organic matter. 土壤富含有机物质。|近义词：organism|反义词：inorganic
