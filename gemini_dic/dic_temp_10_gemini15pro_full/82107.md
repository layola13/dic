grogged:/ˈɡrɒɡd/ (UK) /ˈɡrɑːɡd/ (US)| adj.|喝醉的，陶醉的，脚步不稳的|Intoxicated, drunk, often to the point of staggering or unsteadiness.|He was so grogged he could barely stand. 他醉得几乎站不住了。|近义词：drunk, intoxicated, inebriated, tipsy|反义词:sober, clear-headed|词性:形容词

词源: From "grog," a diluted rum ration given to British sailors, named after Admiral Vernon, nicknamed "Old Grog" because of his grogram cloak.  The term later expanded to mean any alcoholic drink, and "grogged" came to mean intoxicated by alcohol.
