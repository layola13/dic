misship: /ˌmɪsˈʃɪp/ | v. | 装错船；运错货物 | to ship (goods) to the wrong destination. | Example: The factory misshipped the order, sending it to California instead of Florida. 工厂把订单装错了船，把它送到了加利福尼亚州，而不是佛罗里达州。

Example:  Due to a clerical error, the consignment was misshipped to the wrong warehouse. 由于文书错误，这批货物被误运到了错误的仓库。| 近义词: misdirect, misroute | 反义词: ship correctly | 词性: 动词
