limpidities:/lɪmˈpɪdɪtiz/
n.
清澈度，透明度
The limpidities of the water allowed us to see the bottom of the lake.
The limpidities of her prose style make her writing a pleasure to read.
clarity, transparency, pellucidity
murkiness, opacity, cloudiness
noun

Etymology:
From limpid + -ity.  Limpid comes from the Latin limpidus, meaning "clear, transparent," related to lympha, meaning "water."
