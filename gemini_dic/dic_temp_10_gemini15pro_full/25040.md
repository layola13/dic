bryozoan: /ˌbraɪəˈzoʊən/ | n. |苔藓虫；苔藓动物|A small invertebrate animal, usually marine, that forms colonies.|Bryozoans are often found encrusting rocks and seaweed. 苔藓虫经常被发现包裹在岩石和海藻上。|Synonyms: moss animal|Antonyms: N/A|noun

bryozoan: /ˌbraɪəˈzoʊən/ | adj. |苔藓虫的；苔藓动物的|Relating to or characteristic of bryozoans.|The bryozoan colony was a vibrant orange color.  这个苔藓虫群体呈现鲜艳的橙色。|Synonyms: N/A|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
