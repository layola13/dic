blabbered:/ˈblæbərd/| v.|喋喋不休地说，胡言乱语|talked foolishly or too much, divulged secrets indiscreetly|He blabbered about the surprise party, ruining it for everyone. 他把惊喜派对的事情说漏了嘴，把大家的兴致都破坏了。|近义词：chattered, babbled, prattled|反义词:kept quiet, clammed up|词性:动词
词源: From Middle English *blaberen, of imitative origin.
