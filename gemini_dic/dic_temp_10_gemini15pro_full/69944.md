fessing:/ˈfɛsɪŋ/
verb
坦白; 供认; 招供
confessing something bad, especially a crime, to someone in authority
Example: He ended up fessing to the robbery.  他最终承认了抢劫。
Example: She fessed up to eating all the cookies. 她承认自己吃光了所有的饼干。
Synonym: confessing, admitting, owning up
Antonym: denying, concealing, hiding
词性: 动词 (Verb)
