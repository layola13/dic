downtrodden:/ˌdaʊnˈtrɒdn/ /ˌdaʊnˈtrɑːdn/| adj.|被压迫的，被蹂躏的|Oppressed or treated badly by people in power.|The downtrodden people of the country finally revolted against the dictator. 这个国家被压迫的人民最终反抗了独裁者。|Synonyms: oppressed, subjugated, suppressed, tyrannized|Antonyms: empowered, liberated, free, privileged|adjective


downtrodden:/ˌdaʊnˈtrɒdn/ /ˌdaʊnˈtrɑːdn/| n.|被压迫的人，受欺凌的人|(plural) People who are oppressed or treated badly by those in power.|The downtrodden rose up against their oppressors. 被压迫者起来反抗压迫他们的人。|Synonyms: the oppressed, the underprivileged, the disadvantaged|Antonyms: the elite, the privileged, the ruling class|noun
