overfeeding: /ˌoʊvərˈfiːdɪŋ/ | n. | 过度喂食 | The act of giving someone or something too much food. | Overfeeding can lead to obesity in pets. 过度喂食会导致宠物肥胖。| Overfeeding can cause digestive problems in infants.  过度喂食会导致婴儿消化问题。 | overeating, stuffing | underfeeding | noun

overfeed: /ˌoʊvərˈfiːd/ | vt. | 过度喂食 | To give someone or something too much food. |  Don't overfeed your dog, it will get fat. 不要过度喂你的狗，它会变胖的。|  They overfed the fish in the pond, and now the water is polluted. 他们过度喂食池塘里的鱼，现在水被污染了。 | overnourish, stuff | starve, underfeed | verb
