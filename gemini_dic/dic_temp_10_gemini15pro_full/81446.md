gratify: /ˈɡrætɪfaɪ/ (US) /ˈɡrætɪfaɪ/ (UK)
| vt. | 使满足；使高兴；| To please or satisfy. |  She was gratified to hear that her work was appreciated. 她听到自己的工作受到赞赏感到很高兴。

| | | |He was gratified by their invitation.  他们的邀请让他感到很高兴。


近义词: please, satisfy, delight, fulfill
反义词: disappoint, frustrate, displease
词性: verb (动词)

词源:
From Latin gratificari, from gratus (“pleasing, agreeable, thankful”) + -ficari, passive infinitive of facere (“to do, to make”).
