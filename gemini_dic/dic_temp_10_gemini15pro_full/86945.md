hemstitched:/ˈhɛmˌstɪtʃt/
adj.
抽纱边的；缝有抽纱边的
A decorative stitch made by pulling out several parallel threads from a fabric and tying the remaining threads together in groups.  It is often used on hems, hence the name.
The tablecloth was hemstitched with a delicate floral pattern.  这条桌布缝有精致的花卉图案抽纱边。
hemstitched sheets 抽纱边的床单

hemstitched napkins 抽纱边的餐巾


近义词:embroidered, drawn-thread
反义词:(None -  lacks a direct opposite. The absence of hemstitching could be considered the opposite, but there isn't a single word for that.)
词性:adjective
