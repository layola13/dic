bushnells:/ˈbʊʃnəlz/| n. |布什内尔（Bushnell）|A surname, derived from a place name meaning "bushy knoll" or "small hill covered with bushes".  It's also a unit of measurement used in rifle shooting, specifically for bullet drop compensation.|例句：The Bushnells family has lived in this town for generations. 布什内尔家族世世代代都住在这个镇上。

例句：He adjusted his scope by five bushnells to account for the distance. 他将瞄准镜调整了五个布什内尔，以考虑距离因素。|近义词：(For the surname) No direct synonyms.  (For the unit) clicks, MOA (minute of angle)|反义词:None|词性:Noun
