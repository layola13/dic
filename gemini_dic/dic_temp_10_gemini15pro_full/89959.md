horses:/ˈhɔːrsɪz/ (US) /ˈhɔːsɪz/ (UK)| n. |马匹|Plural form of "horse," referring to more than one horse.  A horse is a large, hoofed mammal, domesticated for riding, racing, and working.|The horses galloped across the field. 马儿在田野里飞奔。|The farmer used two horses to plow his field. 农夫用两匹马犁地。|Near synonyms: steeds, mounts, equines|Antonym: (Singular) None for the plural form.  The antonym of "horse" might be considered "rider" in certain contexts.|noun
