anticonformity:/ˌæntiˈkənˈfɔːrməti/
n.|反从众，逆从,故意不顺从|The act of opposing or resisting social norms and expectations, often by doing the opposite of what is considered acceptable or popular.  It's a deliberate rebellion against conformity.|He showed his anticonformity by wearing mismatched socks to the formal event. 他在正式场合穿不搭的袜子以示他对从众的反抗。|nonconformity, dissent,  rebellion,  resistance|conformity, obedience, compliance|noun
