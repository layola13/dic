chosens:/ˈtʃoʊzənz/
|n.|被选中的人或物|People or things that have been selected.|The chosens were given special privileges. 被选中的人被赋予了特权。|近义词: selected, elect, favored|反义词: rejected, unchosen|词性:名词

词源:
From the past participle of *choose*, from Old English *cēosan* "to choose, seek out, select, test," from Proto-Germanic *keusan* (source also of Old Saxon *kiosan*, Old Frisian *kiasa*, Middle Dutch *kiesen*, Dutch *kiezen*, Old High German *kiosan*, German *kiesen*, Old Norse *kjósa* "to choose"), from PIE root *ǵews-* "to taste, choose."  The "-n" ending signifies the plural form of the past participle used as a noun.
