disassociate:/dɪsəˈsoʊʃieɪt/ |v. |分离；脱离；撇清关系|To separate oneself from something; to deny any connection with something.|She disassociated herself from the group after the scandal.  她在那起丑闻之后脱离了该组织。|The company disassociated itself from the controversial advertisement. 公司撇清了与那则争议性广告的关系。|近义词：detach, separate, disavow, renounce|反义词：associate, connect, join|verb
