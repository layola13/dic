imbrangle:/ɪmˈbræŋɡl/
verb
使纠缠；使混乱
to involve in a tangle or confused mass; to mix up or confuse things
The lawyer imbrangled the case with irrelevant details. 律师用不相干的细节把案子弄得一团糟。
Synonyms: entangle, complicate, confuse, muddle
Antonyms: disentangle, simplify, clarify, organize
词性: 动词
