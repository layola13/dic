examined:/ɪɡˈzæmɪnd/ /ɪɡˈzæmɪnd/
verb
检查；审查；测验
carefully inspected or scrutinized.
The doctor examined the patient's wound. 医生检查了病人的伤口。
近义词: inspected, scrutinized, investigated, analyzed
反义词: ignored, overlooked, neglected
词性: verb

examined:/ɪɡˈzæmɪnd/ /ɪɡˈzæmɪnd/
adjective
经过仔细检查的
having been carefully inspected or scrutinized.
The examined documents revealed no new information.  经过仔细检查的文件没有透露任何新信息。
近义词: inspected, scrutinized, investigated
反义词: unchecked, uninspected, unexamined
词性: adjective
