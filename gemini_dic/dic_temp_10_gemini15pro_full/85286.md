harmonization:/ˌhɑːrmənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.|协调，一致，和谐|The harmonization of international trade laws is a complex process. 国际贸易法的协调是一个复杂的过程。|The company is working towards the harmonization of its different departments. 公司正在努力协调其不同部门的工作。| 近义词:coordination, unification, standardization, alignment|反义词:discord, conflict, disharmony|词性:名词

Etymology: From Late Latin *harmonizatio, from harmonizare "to harmonize," from harmonia "harmony," from Greek harmonia "agreement, concord of sounds," from harmos "a fitting, joint."
