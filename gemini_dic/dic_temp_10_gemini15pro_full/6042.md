amendatory:/əˈmɛndətɔːri/
adjective
修正的，补充的
serving to amend; of the nature of an amendment.
The legislature passed an amendatory bill to correct an error in the previous law. 立法机关通过了一项修正案，以纠正先前法律中的错误。
近义词: amending, corrective, revising, modifying
反义词: original, unamended, unchanged
词性: adjective
