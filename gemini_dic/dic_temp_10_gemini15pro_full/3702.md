aggregator's:/ˈæɡrɪˌɡeɪtərz/| n.|聚合器的，聚合商的|This refers to something belonging to an aggregator, a person or company that collects and combines information from multiple sources.|Example: The aggregator's website provides a comprehensive list of hotels. 聚合商的网站提供了全面的酒店列表。|Example:  The aggregator's fees are quite reasonable. 聚合商的费用相当合理。|近义词:collector's, compiler's|反义词:distributor's, disseminator's|词性: possessive noun (名词所有格)
aggregate:/ˈæɡrɪɡeɪt/| v. adj. n.|聚集，合计；总计的；集合体|From Latin *aggregatus*, past participle of *aggregare* "to add to a flock, attach, join to," from *ad-* "to" + *gregare* "to herd," from *grex* "flock."|
aggregator:/ˈæɡrɪˌɡeɪtər/| n.|聚合器，聚合商|One who or that which aggregates.  Derived from the verb "aggregate."|
