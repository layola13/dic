daunting:/ˈdɔːntɪŋ/ (US) /ˈdɔːntɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. | 使人畏惧的；令人气馁的 | Seeming difficult to deal with in a way that discourages you. | Climbing Mount Everest is a daunting task. 攀登珠穆朗玛峰是一项艰巨的任务。| The sheer size of the project was daunting. 项目的规模令人望而生畏。 | intimidating, challenging, formidable, discouraging | encouraging, easy, simple | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "daunt," which comes from the Old French word "danter," meaning "to tame" or "subdue."  Interestingly, the original meaning has shifted from causing fear to experiencing fear.
