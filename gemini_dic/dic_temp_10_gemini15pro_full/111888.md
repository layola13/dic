logon:/ˈlɒɡɒn/ (UK) /ˈlɔːɡɑːn/ (US)| n. vi. vt.|登录，注册，登录名|The act of accessing a computer system or network by entering a username and password.  Also refers to the username itself.|Please enter your logon and password.  请输入你的登录名和密码。|Synonyms: login, sign-in, sign-on|Antonyms: logoff, logout, sign-out|noun, verb

logon:/ˈlɒɡɒn/ (UK) /ˈlɔːɡɑːn/ (US)| vi. |登录，注册|To access a computer system or network by entering a username and password.|I can't logon to the network. 我无法登录到网络。|Synonyms: login, sign-in, sign-on|Antonyms: logoff, logout, sign-out|verb

logon:/ˈlɒɡɒn/ (UK) /ˈlɔːɡɑːn/ (US)| vt. |注册（用户）|To register a user on a computer system or network.|The administrator logoned the new employee. 管理员注册了新员工。|Synonyms: register, enroll|Antonyms: unregister, remove|verb
