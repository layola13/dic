inscattering:/ɪnˈskætərɪŋ/
noun
内散射
The process of radiation being scattered into a particular direction or region, especially within a medium.
Light undergoing inscattering appears brighter. 光线经过内散射后显得更亮。
近义词: scattering, diffusion
反义词: outscattering
