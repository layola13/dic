buckled:/ˈbʌkəld/| v. adj.|扣住，弯曲，屈服|fasten with a buckle; bend and give way under pressure; collapse physically or mentally|例句：He buckled his belt. 他扣上了皮带。

例句：The roof buckled under the weight of the snow. 屋顶在雪的重压下弯曲了。|近义词：fasten, clasp, bend, collapse, crumple, give way|反义词:unbuckle, straighten, resist|词性:动词/形容词

buckled:/ˈbʌkəld/| adj.|扣好的，弯曲的|fastened with a buckle; bent or warped|例句：She wore buckled shoes. 她穿着带扣的鞋子。

例句：The buckled wheel made the car difficult to drive. 弯曲的轮子使车子难以驾驶。|近义词：fastened, clasped, bent, warped, crooked|反义词:unbuckled, straight|词性:形容词
