observable:/əbˈzɜːrvəbl/ | adj. n. |可观察的；可观测的；可观测量；可观测量|Something that is observable can be noticed or seen.  In quantum mechanics, an observable is a physical quantity that can be measured. |The comet will be observable with the naked eye. 这颗彗星用肉眼就可以观察到。|synonyms: perceptible, noticeable, visible, detectable, apparent |antonyms: imperceptible, invisible, undetectable, hidden|adjective

observable:/əbˈzɜːrvəbl/ | n. |可观察量；可观测量|In quantum mechanics, an observable is a physical quantity that can be measured, such as position or momentum.|The position of the electron is an observable. 电子的位置是一个可观测量。|synonyms:  measurable quantity, parameter |antonyms: N/A|noun
