auctions:/ˈɔːkʃənz/
noun
拍卖；竞卖
a public sale in which goods or property are sold to the highest bidder
例句：The painting was sold at auction for $1 million. 这幅画在拍卖会上以100万美元的价格售出。

例句：They're holding an auction of antique furniture. 他们正在举行一场古董家具拍卖会。

近义词：sales, bidding
反义词：purchase, buy
词性:n.


auction:/ˈɔːkʃən/
verb
拍卖
to sell something at an auction
例句：The house will be auctioned off next week. 这栋房子将于下周拍卖。

例句：They auctioned off their possessions to raise money for charity. 他们拍卖了自己的财产为慈善机构筹款。

近义词：sell, vend
反义词：buy, purchase
词性:v.
