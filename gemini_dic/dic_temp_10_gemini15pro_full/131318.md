nonchalky:/ˌnɑːnˈtʃɔːki/
adjective
非白垩的；不似白垩的
Not chalky; having a smooth, firm texture, not crumbly or powdery like chalk.
The apples were crisp and nonchalky, perfect for eating. 这些苹果脆而没有白垩感，非常适合食用。
The paint dried to a nonchalky, matte finish.  这油漆干后呈现出一种非白垩的哑光效果。
Synonyms: smooth, firm, solid, dense
Antonyms: chalky, powdery, crumbly, friable
