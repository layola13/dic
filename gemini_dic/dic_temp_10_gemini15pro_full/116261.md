massacres:/ˈmæsəkərz/ (US), /ˈmæsəkəz/ (UK)| n.|大屠杀，残杀|The intentional and brutal killing of a large number of people.|The massacres of civilians during the war were horrific.  战争期间对平民的屠杀骇人听闻。|近义词：slaughter, carnage, genocide, butchery|反义词:rescue, salvation|Noun


massacre:/ˈmæsəkər/ (US), /ˈmæsəkə(r)/ (UK)| v.|屠杀，残杀|To kill a large number of people indiscriminately and brutally.|The army massacred the villagers. 军队屠杀了村民。|近义词：slaughter, butcher|反义词:protect, save|Verb
