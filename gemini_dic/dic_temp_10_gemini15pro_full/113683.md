mackereling:/ˈmækərəlɪŋ/ | n. |鲭鱼捕捞; 鲭鱼群 |The act of fishing for mackerel, or a catch of mackerel.  |The mackereling season is upon us. 鲭鱼捕捞季节到了。| They brought in a huge mackereling. 他们带回了大量的鲭鱼。| 近义词: mackerel fishing | 反义词: 无 | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From mackerel + -ing.  "Mackerel" comes from Middle English *makerel, from Old North French maquerel, of uncertain origin.  The "-ing" suffix forms nouns denoting an action or its result.
