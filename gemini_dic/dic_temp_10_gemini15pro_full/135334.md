nonpenalized: /ˌnɒnˈpiːnəlaɪzd/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈpiːnəlaɪzd/ (UK)| adj.|未受处罚的，免除处罚的|Not subject to a penalty or punishment.  Formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to "penalized" (meaning "subject to a penalty").  "Penalized" comes from "penalty" which has roots in the Latin word "poena" meaning "punishment."|This is a nonpenalized parking area. 这是一个不罚款的停车区。|The nonpenalized withdrawal period for this investment is one year.  这项投资的免罚提取期为一年。|近义词：exempt, excused, unpunished|反义词：penalized, punished|词性:形容词
