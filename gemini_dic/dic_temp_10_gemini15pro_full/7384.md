animalization:/ˌænɪməlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.|动物化；兽性化|The process of making something or someone seem like an animal, or of giving something or someone animal qualities.|The novel explores the animalization of humans in a post-apocalyptic world.  这部小说探讨了后世界末日时代人类的动物化。|近义词: brutalization, dehumanization |反义词: humanization |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *animalisation*, from *animaliser* "to animalize," from *animal* "animal," from Latin *animal, animale* "living being, animal,"  from *anima* "breath, soul."
