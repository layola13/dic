likings:/ˈlaɪkɪŋz/
n.
喜好，嗜好
A person's likings are the things they like.
例句：Her likings are simple: good food, good company, and good books. 她喜欢的东西很简单：美食、良伴和好书。

例句：His likings include classical music and vintage cars.  他的爱好包括古典音乐和老爷车。

近义词：preferences, tastes, inclinations
反义词：dislikes, aversions
词性：noun (plural)

词源：From the verb "like" + "-ing" suffix forming a gerund or present participle, which in this case functions as a noun.  The "-s" ending indicates plural.  "Like" comes from Old English *līcian "be pleasing, agreeable," from Proto-Germanic *likōjanan (source also of Old Saxon likon, Old Frisian likia, Old High German lihhan "to please," German leuchten "to shine," Dutch lijken "to seem").
