branchiura:/bræŋˈkjʊərə/
n.
鳃尾亚纲；鱼虱
A subclass of crustaceans, commonly known as fish lice, that are parasitic on fish.
Fish lice, belonging to the subclass Branchiura, are common parasites found on various fish species.
近义词:fish lice (common name)
反义词: (No direct antonym for a taxonomic classification)
词性: noun


branchiura:/bræŋˈkjʊərə/
n.
鱼虱
A small crustacean parasite of fish.
Branchiura are ectoparasites that attach to the skin and gills of fish.
近义词: Arguloida (alternative scientific name)
反义词: (No direct antonym for a taxonomic classification)
词性: noun



Etymology: From the Greek words "branchia" (gills) and "oura" (tail), referring to the position of the respiratory structures on the animal.
