carburetor:/ˈkɑːrbəreɪtər/ (US) /ˈkɑːbərɛtər/ (UK)| n. |化油器|A device in an internal combustion engine for mixing air with a fine spray of liquid fuel.|The carburetor is responsible for delivering the correct air-fuel mixture to the engine. 化油器负责将正确的空燃混合物输送到发动机。|The mechanic cleaned the carburetor. 机械师清洗了化油器。|synonyms:  fuel mixer, atomiser|antonyms: fuel injector|noun
