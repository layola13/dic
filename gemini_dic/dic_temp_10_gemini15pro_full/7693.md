anom:/ˈænəm/ /əˈnɒm/ | n. |异常，反常，不规则|An anomaly is something that deviates from what is standard, normal, or expected.|例句：Scientists are studying the climate anomalies that have occurred in recent years. 科学家们正在研究近年来出现的气候异常现象。

例句：There was an anomaly in the data that couldn't be explained. 数据中出现了一个无法解释的异常。|近义词：aberration, irregularity, deviation, exception|反义词：norm, standard, regularity|词性：名词
