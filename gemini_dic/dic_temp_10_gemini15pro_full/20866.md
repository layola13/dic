blatterer:/ˈblætərər/ (US), /ˈblætərə(r)/ (UK)| n.|饶舌者，吹牛大王|A person who talks a lot, especially in a foolish or boastful way.  Someone who blathers.|He's such a blatterer, always going on about himself. 他真是个饶舌的家伙，总是喋喋不休地谈论自己。

He was known as a blatterer, never missing a chance to exaggerate his achievements.  他以饶舌而闻名，从不放过任何一个夸大自己成就的机会。|近义词：windbag, braggart, blowhard|反义词：quiet person, reticent person|noun
