encheson: /ɛnˈtʃiːzən/ /ɛnˈtʃɛzən/
n.
原因，起因
a cause or reason for something
The encheson of the war was a territorial dispute. 这场战争的起因是领土争端。
近义词: cause, reason, origin, source
反义词: result, consequence, effect
词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English enchesoun, from Old French enchaison, from Late Latin incāsio (“occasion, cause”), from incāsus, past participle of incidere, from in- + cadere (“to fall”).
