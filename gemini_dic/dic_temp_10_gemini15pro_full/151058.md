paraphrasing:/ˈpærəˌfreɪzɪŋ/ | n. |  释义; 改述; 意译; | Expressing the meaning of (the writer or speaker or something written or spoken) using different words, especially to achieve greater clarity. | He paraphrased the poem in modern English. 他用现代英语改述了这首诗。| synonyms: rewording, rephrasing, restating, summarizing, recapitulating | antonyms: quoting, copying, plagiarizing | noun

paraphrase:/ˈpærəˌfreɪz/ | v. |  释义; 改述; 意译; | Express the meaning of (the writer or speaker or something written or spoken) using different words, especially to achieve greater clarity. |  She paraphrased the paragraph for me. 她为我改述了这段。 | synonyms: reword, rephrase, restate, summarize, recapitulate | antonyms: quote, copy, plagiarize | verb
