dreamlike:/ˈdriːmlaɪk/| adj.|梦境般的，梦幻的|Resembling a dream; surreal, ethereal, or enchanting.|The forest had a dreamlike quality, with mist swirling between the trees. 森林有一种梦幻般的感觉，薄雾在树木间缭绕。|近义词：dreamy, surreal, ethereal, fantastical, otherworldly|反义词：real, realistic, mundane, ordinary|词性:形容词
词源: From dream + -like.  The suffix "-like" forms adjectives meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of".
