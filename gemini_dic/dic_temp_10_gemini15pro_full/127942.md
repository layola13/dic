neopallium:/ˌnɪoʊˈpæliəm/ | n. | 新大脑皮层; 新皮质 | The neopallium is the newest part of the cerebral cortex, responsible for higher-level cognitive functions. 新大脑皮层是大脑皮层最新的部分，负责高级认知功能。|  The human neopallium is significantly larger than that of other mammals. 人类的新大脑皮层比其他哺乳动物的要大得多。 | synonyms: neocortex, isocortex | antonyms: allocortex, paleocortex | noun
