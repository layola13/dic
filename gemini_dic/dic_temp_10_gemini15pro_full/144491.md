outthunder:/ˌaʊtˈθʌndər/
verb
胜过雷鸣般的声音；发出比…更大的雷声
to surpass in loudness of thunder; to produce louder thunder than
The rock concert outthundered the nearby storm. 这场摇滚音乐会的声响盖过了附近的暴风雨。
Synonyms: drown out, overpower
Antonyms: whisper, murmur
词性：动词
