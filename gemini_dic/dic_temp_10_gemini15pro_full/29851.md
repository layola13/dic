causativeness:/ˈkɔːzətɪvnəs/| n.| 使役性，致使性|The property of a verb or construction that indicates that a subject causes someone or something else to do something.  It describes the relationship where one entity causes another to perform an action.|The causativeness of the verb "make" is clear in the sentence "She made him clean the room." 她让他打扫房间。|近义词：causality, agency|反义词:inertness, passivity|词性:noun
