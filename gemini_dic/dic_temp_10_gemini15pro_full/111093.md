litany:/ˈlɪtəni/ | n. | 连祷；冗长而枯燥的陈述 | a series of petitions for use in church services or processions; a tedious recital or repetitive series | The litany of complaints continued for hours.  抱怨声持续了几个小时。| synonyms: recital, enumeration, repetition, catalog, rigmarole | antonyms: brevity, conciseness | noun
