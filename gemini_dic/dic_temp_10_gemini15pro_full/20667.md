blacktail:/ˈblækˌteɪl/| n. |黑尾鹿|A deer of western North America, having a black-tipped tail. |例句：The blacktail deer grazed peacefully in the meadow. 黑尾鹿在草地上平静地吃草。

例句：Hunters prize the blacktail for its venison. 猎人珍视黑尾鹿的鹿肉。|近义词：mule deer (specifically the Columbian black-tailed deer is a subspecies of mule deer)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


blacktail:/ˈblækˌteɪl/| adj. |黑尾的|Having a black tail. |例句：The blacktail doe cautiously approached the stream. 那只黑尾母鹿小心翼翼地靠近小溪。

例句：We saw a blacktail buck with impressive antlers. 我们看到了一只长着令人印象深刻的鹿角的黑尾公鹿。|近义词：dark-tailed|反义词:white-tailed|词性:adjective
