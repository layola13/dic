moyle:/mɔɪl/| n. |杂交动物，骡子，（尤指苏格兰）强壮的劳动妇女|A hybrid animal, especially a mule. Also (especially Scottish) a strong workwoman.|The old farmer relied on his moyle for plowing the fields. 老农依靠他的骡子耕地。|近义词：mule, hybrid, crossbreed|反义词:purebred|词性:noun
moyle:/mɔɪl/| v. |劳累，苦干，拖拉|To work hard, toil, or drag.|She moyled all day in the garden. 她在花园里辛勤劳作了一整天。|近义词：toil, labor, drudge|反义词:rest, relax|词性:verb
