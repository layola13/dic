incessant:/ɪnˈsesənt/| adj.|不停的，不断的|Continuing without pause or interruption.|The incessant noise of the traffic kept me awake all night. 交通不停的噪音让我整夜无法入睡。|近义词：constant, continuous, perpetual, unremitting|反义词：intermittent, occasional, sporadic|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from Old French, or from late Latin incessant- ‘not ceasing’, from the verb incessare, from in- ‘not’ + cessare ‘cease’.
