draughtsboards:/ˈdrɑːftsbɔːrdz/ /ˈdræftsbɔːrdz/
n.
西洋跳棋棋盘
a board marked with squares of alternating colors, used for playing draughts or checkers.
We spent hours playing draughts on the draughtsboards. 我们花了几个小时在跳棋棋盘上玩跳棋。
近义词：checkerboards
