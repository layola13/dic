heptameters:/hɛpˈtæmɪtərz/
| n. |七步格; 七音步诗行; |A line of verse consisting of seven metrical feet.|  Edgar Allan Poe's "The Raven" is written primarily in trochaic octameter, but some lines are in trochaic heptameter. | 近义词:  septenary (less common) | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *hepta* (seven) + *metron* (measure)
