lowballer:/ˈloʊˌbɔːlər/| n.|低价竞标者；压价者|Someone who makes an offer that is significantly lower than the asking price, often considered unfair or insulting.|He's a notorious lowballer, always trying to get things for next to nothing. 他是个臭名昭著的压价者，总是想以极低的价格买东西。|Synonyms: cheapskate, bargain hunter, tightwad|Antonyms: high roller, big spender|noun
