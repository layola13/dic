nunciature: /ˈnʌnʃiətʃər/ (US), /ˈnʌnsiətʃə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 教廷大使馆；教廷大使的职位 |The official residence or office of a papal nuncio.  A nunciature is the equivalent of an embassy. | The Vatican established a nunciature in the newly independent country.  梵蒂冈在新独立的国家设立了教廷大使馆。| embassy, legation | consulate | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *nuntiatura, from nuntiare "to announce,"  from nuntius "messenger."
