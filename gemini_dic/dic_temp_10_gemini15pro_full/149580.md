paid-for:/ˌpeɪd ˈfɔːr/| adj.|付费的，已付清的|Something that is paid-for has already been paid for.  It implies that the item is now owned outright and there are no further payments required.|例句：The company car is paid-for. 公司的汽车已经付清了。

例句：We stayed in a paid-for hotel room. 我们住在一个已经付清的酒店房间。|近义词：financed, purchased, settled, cleared|反义词:unpaid, outstanding, due
