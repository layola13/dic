baktuns:/ˌbɑːkˈtuːnz/
n.
巴克盾（玛雅历法中的时间单位）
A baktun is a period of 144,000 days (approximately 394 years) in the ancient Maya Long Count calendar.
The end of the 13th baktun was interpreted by some as the end of the world in 2012.  玛雅长纪年法的第13个巴克盾的结束被一些人解释为2012年的世界末日。
