nonseparability:/ˌnɒnˌsɛpərəˈbɪləti/ |n.| 不可分性；不可分离性 |The nonseparability of mind and body is a central tenet of some philosophies.  身心不可分是某些哲学的核心原则。|  The quantum entanglement demonstrates the nonseparability of two particles. 量子纠缠证明了两个粒子的不可分离性。 | inseparability, indivisibility | separability, divisibility | noun

Etymology->

From non- + separability.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "separability" comes from the verb "separate," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "separare," meaning "to sever, divide."
