nebula:/ˈnɛbjələ/| n.|星云|a cloud of gas and dust in outer space, visible in the night sky either as an indistinct bright patch or as a dark silhouette against other luminous matter.|The Orion Nebula is a stunning sight through a telescope.  猎户座星云透过望远镜看起来令人叹为观止。|Synonyms: cloud, haze|Antonyms: clarity, clearness|词性:名词
