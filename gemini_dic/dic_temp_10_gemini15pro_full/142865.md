out-lot:/ˈaʊtˌlɒt/ (US), /ˈaʊtˌlɒt/ (UK)
n.
外围地块，独立地块
A parcel of land that is part of a subdivision but is separated from other lots, often located on the perimeter or in a less desirable area.
The developer decided to build a convenience store on the out-lot.  开发商决定在外围地块上建一家便利店。
The out-lot provided more privacy than the other lots in the subdivision. 这块独立的地块比小区里的其他地块更具私密性。
synonym: outlying lot, peripheral lot
antonym: inner lot, central lot
