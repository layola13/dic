anti-creationist:/ˌænti krɪˈeɪʃənɪst/
n. |反创造论者|A person who opposes creationism, the belief that the universe and living organisms originate from specific acts of divine creation, as in the biblical account, rather than by natural processes such as evolution.|Richard Dawkins is a well-known anti-creationist. 理查德·道金斯是一位著名的反创造论者。|近义词：evolutionist, Darwinist|反义词:creationist|词性:名词

anti-creationist:/ˌænti krɪˈeɪʃənɪst/
adj. |反创造论的|Opposing or disagreeing with creationism.|He holds an anti-creationist viewpoint. 他持有反创造论的观点。|近义词：pro-evolution, Darwinian|反义词:creationist|词性:形容词
