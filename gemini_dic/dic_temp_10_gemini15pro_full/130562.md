nonacting:/nɒnˈæktɪŋ/
adjective
非表演的；非演技的；不表演的
Not relating to or involving acting; not performing in a play or film.
例句：He has a nonacting role in the theater company, focusing on set design. 他在剧团里担任非表演的角色，专注于舞台设计。

例句：She pursued a nonacting career in the film industry, working as a producer.  她在电影行业追求非表演的职业，担任制片人。

近义词：non-performing, non-theatrical
反义词：acting, performing
词性：adjective
