locomotive: /ˌloʊkəˈmoʊtɪv/ (US), /ˌləʊkəˈməʊtɪv/ (UK)| n. adj.| 火车头；机车；运动的|A locomotive is a powerful engine that pulls trains along railroad tracks.  It can also be used figuratively to describe something that drives or motivates progress.|The old steam locomotive chugged along the tracks.  老式蒸汽机车沿着轨道缓慢行驶。|Synonyms: engine, train, motor, driving force|Antonyms: stationary, immobile|noun
