dropsy:/ˈdrɑːpsi/| n.|水肿，积水|An outdated term for edema, which is the abnormal accumulation of fluid in the body's tissues, resulting in swelling.|He was suffering from dropsy and could barely walk. 他患有水肿，几乎不能走路。|近义词:edema, swelling, fluid retention|反义词:dehydration|词性:noun
