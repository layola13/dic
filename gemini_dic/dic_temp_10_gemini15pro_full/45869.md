cutaways:/ˈkʌtəˌweɪz/
n.
剪辑镜头; 切换镜头;  剖面图; 剖视图;
A cutaway is a short scene in a film or television programme that shows something which is connected with the main action but not part of it.  A cutaway is also a drawing or model of something such as a building or a machine that has been cut in half to show what it looks like inside.
The film used several cutaways to show the character's backstory.  这部电影用了几段剪辑镜头来展现角色的背景故事。
The architect presented a cutaway of the proposed building.  建筑师展示了拟建建筑的剖面图。
近义词: inserts, crosscuts, sections
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "continuous shot" for film context)
词性: noun
