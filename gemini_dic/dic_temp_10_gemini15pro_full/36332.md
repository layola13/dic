coiffeur:/kwɑːˈfɜːr/ (US), /kwɑːˈfɜː/ (UK)| n. |理发师，发型师|A hairdresser, especially a fashionable one.|例句：I went to a famous coiffeur in Paris. 我去了巴黎一位著名的发型师那里。

例句：The coiffeur styled her hair beautifully for the wedding.  这位发型师为她的婚礼做了漂亮的发型。

近义词：hairdresser, stylist, barber|反义词: None|词性: Noun
