circuital:/sɜːrˈkjuːɪtəl/ | adj. |电路的；迂回的|Of or relating to an electric circuit; roundabout, indirect.| The circuital route took us through several small towns. 电路迂回的路线带我们穿过几个小镇。|Example: The circuital flow of electricity powers our homes.  电流的回路流动为我们的家庭供电。

Example: He took a circuital route to avoid the traffic jam. 他绕道而行以避开交通堵塞。| 近义词:circuitous, indirect|反义词:direct, straight|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *circuitalis, from Latin circuitus (“a going around”), past participle of circuire (“to go around”), from circum (“around”) + ire (“to go”).
