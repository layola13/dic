forejudge:/fɔːrˈdʒʌdʒ/| v. |预先判断；预先断定；未审先判|To judge beforehand or before having all the facts.|The jury was instructed not to forejudge the defendant's guilt. 陪审团被指示不要预先判断被告是否有罪。|He was careful not to forejudge the outcome of the election. 他小心翼翼地不去预先判断选举结果。|近义词：prejudge, presuppose, anticipate|反义词：reserve judgment, withhold judgment|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *forejuggen*, from Old French *forejugier*, from *fore-* (before) + *jugier* (to judge), from Latin *judicare* (to judge).
