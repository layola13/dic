bargainer:/ˈbɑːrɡənər/ | n. | 谈判者；讨价还价的人 |  Someone who bargains; a person who negotiates the terms of a transaction. |  He's a shrewd bargainer and always gets a good deal. 他是一个精明的谈判者，总是能得到很好的交易。| synonyms: negotiator, haggler, trader | antonyms: conciliator, mediator | noun

bargainer:/ˈbɑːrɡənər/ | n. | 讲价者；爱讲价的人 | Someone who habitually tries to get things at a lower price than is asked. | She's a real bargainer; she never pays full price for anything. 她真是个爱讲价的人，从来不付全价买任何东西。| synonyms: cheapskate, tightwad | antonyms: spendthrift, big spender | noun
