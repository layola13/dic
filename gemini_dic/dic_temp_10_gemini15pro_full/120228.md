mineworker:/ˈmaɪnˌwɜːrkər/ (US) /ˈmaɪnˌwɜːkə(r)/ (UK)| n. |矿工|A person who works in a mine, extracting minerals such as coal, metals, or precious stones.|例句：The mineworkers went on strike for better pay and working conditions. 矿工们为了争取更高的工资和更好的工作条件而举行了罢工。|近义词：miner, collier (specifically for coal miners)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
