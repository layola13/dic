non-revival:/ˌnɒn rɪˈvaɪvəl/ |adj.| 未复兴的 |Not revived; lacking a revival.  The non-revival status of the old factory left the town economically depressed.  这座旧工厂没有复兴，使该镇经济萧条。| Near Synonyms: stagnant, dormant, inactive | Antonyms: revived, resurgent, flourishing | adjective
