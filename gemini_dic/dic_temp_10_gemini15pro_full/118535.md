mesohippus:/ˌmeɪsoʊˈhɪpəs/ | n. | 中新马 | An extinct three-toed horse of the Oligocene epoch, intermediate in form between the *Orohippus* and the *Miohippus*. | Mesohippus was a browsing herbivore that lived in North America. 中新马是一种生活在北美的食草动物。 | synonyms: prehistoric horse, ancestor of the horse | antonyms: modern horse | noun
