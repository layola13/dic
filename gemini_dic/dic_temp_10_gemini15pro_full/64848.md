espionage:/ˈɛspiənɑːʒ/ /ˈɛspiənɑːdʒ/ | n. | 间谍活动，刺探 | The act of spying or using spies to obtain secret information, especially about another government or a company. |  The government was accused of engaging in industrial espionage. 政府被指控从事工业间谍活动。

He was charged with espionage. 他被指控犯有间谍罪。

| 近义词：spying, intelligence gathering, surveillance, reconnaissance | 反义词：counterespionage, countersurveillance | 词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French espionnage, from espionner "to spy," from espion "spy," from Italian spione. Ultimately from Old High German spehon "to look out, spy."
