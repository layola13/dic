ahistoricism:/ˌeɪhɪˈstɔːrɪsɪzəm/
n.
非历史性；非史学方法；无视历史背景
A lack of historical perspective or context, often involving a disregard for the influence of the past on the present.
The politician's ahistoricism led him to make inaccurate comparisons between past and present events.  这位政治家的非历史性导致他对过去和现在发生的事件进行了不准确的比较。
Her ahistoricism is evident in her analysis, which completely ignores the social context of the time. 她的分析明显地非历史化，完全忽略了当时的社会背景。
近义词：presentism, anachronism
反义词：historicism
词性：名词
