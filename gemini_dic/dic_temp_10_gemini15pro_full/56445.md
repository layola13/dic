dolmen:/ˈdɒlmən/ (UK), /ˈdoʊlmən/ (US)| n. |支石墓; 巨石墓|A prehistoric monument typically consisting of two or more large upright stones supporting a large, flat horizontal capstone, often used as a tomb or burial chamber.|例句：The archaeologists discovered a well-preserved dolmen on the hillside. 考古学家在山坡上发现了一座保存完好的支石墓。

例句：The dolmen's capstone was estimated to weigh several tons.  这支石墓的顶石估计重达数吨。|近义词：megalith, tomb, burial chamber|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
