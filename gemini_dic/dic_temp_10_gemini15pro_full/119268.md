mick:/mɪk/
n.
爱尔兰人，(尤指)体力劳动者
An Irishman, especially a manual laborer.
Mick is a common nickname for Michael, and has sometimes been used as a derogatory term for Irish people.  米克是迈克尔的常见昵称，有时也被用作对爱尔兰人的贬义词。
He's a mick from County Cork. 他是科克郡的爱尔兰人。
近义词: Irishman, Paddy (often derogatory)
反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider terms for other nationalities)
