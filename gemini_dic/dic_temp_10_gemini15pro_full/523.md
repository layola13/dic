abomasus:/ˌæbəˈmeɪsəs/| n. |皱胃|The fourth and true stomach of ruminants, such as cows and sheep, in which gastric digestion takes place.|The abomasus is the final compartment of a cow's stomach.  牛的皱胃是它胃的最后一个隔间。

The veterinarian found an ulcer in the abomasus.  兽医在皱胃里发现了一个溃疡。|近义词：maw, stomach (in general sense)|反义词: None (Specific anatomical term)|词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *abomasum*, perhaps from a Gaulish source or perhaps related to Latin *omasum* (“tripe, rumen”).
