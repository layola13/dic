casketlike:/ˈkɑːskɪtlaɪk/
adjective
像棺材的；像小盒的
Resembling a casket, especially in being small and boxlike.
The room was small and dark, almost casketlike.  这个房间又小又暗，几乎像个棺材。
The narrow, windowless space felt casketlike.  狭窄且没有窗户的空间感觉像个棺材。
近义词: coffinlike, boxlike
反义词: spacious, roomy
词性: adjective
