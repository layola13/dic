expedite:/ˈekspədaɪt/| v. | 加快；促进；迅速办理 |To make a process happen more quickly.|We need to expedite the delivery of these goods. 我们需要加快这些货物的交付。|The new software is expected to expedite the entire production process. 预计新软件将加快整个生产过程。|近义词：accelerate, hasten, speed up|反义词：delay, hinder, obstruct|词性:动词

expedition:/ˌekspəˈdɪʃn/| n. | 远征；探险；考察；迅速 |A journey organized for a particular purpose.|The expedition to the North Pole was a dangerous undertaking.  北极探险是一项危险的任务。|They embarked on a scientific expedition to study the rainforest. 他们开始了一次科学考察，研究雨林。|近义词：journey, voyage, quest|反义词：delay|词性:名词

expeditious:/ˌekspəˈdɪʃəs/| adj. |迅速的；敏捷的|Done with speed and efficiency.|The company is known for its expeditious handling of customer complaints. 这家公司以快速处理客户投诉而闻名。|We need an expeditious solution to this problem. 我们需要一个快速的解决方案来解决这个问题。|近义词：quick, swift, prompt|反义词：slow, tardy|词性:形容词
