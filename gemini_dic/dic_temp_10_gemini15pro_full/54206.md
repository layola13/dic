disfavour:/dɪsˈfeɪvər/ | vt. |  不喜欢；冷待 | To regard or treat with disfavor; to dislike. | The boss disfavoured his suggestion. 老板不赞成他的建议。| 近义词: dislike, oppose, reject | 反义词: favor, approve, support | verb
