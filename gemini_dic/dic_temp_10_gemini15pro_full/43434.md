crapple:/ˈkræpəl/| n.| 劣质苹果产品; 蹩脚的苹果产品;  苹果公司的劣质产品 |A derogatory term for Apple products, especially those perceived as poorly designed, overpriced, or unnecessarily complex.  It blends "crap" (meaning something of poor quality) and "Apple" (the company name).|例句：He called the new iPhone a crapple because of its high price and lack of innovation. 他称新款iPhone是垃圾苹果，因为它价格高昂却缺乏创新。

例句：Some tech reviewers dismissed the latest Apple Watch as a crapple due to its short battery life. 一些科技评论员认为最新的Apple Watch是垃圾苹果，因为它的电池续航时间很短。|近义词：dud, lemon, rip-off|反义词：masterpiece, gem|词性: noun
