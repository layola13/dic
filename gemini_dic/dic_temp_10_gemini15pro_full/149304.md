paas:/pæs/ | n. | Platform as a Service (平台即服务) |  A cloud computing model where a third-party provider delivers hardware and software tools -- usually over the internet -- to users so they can develop, run, and manage applications without the complexity of building and maintaining the infrastructure typically associated with developing and launching an application.|  Our company decided to use a PaaS solution to streamline our development process. 我们的公司决定使用PaaS解决方案来简化我们的开发流程。 | 近义词: cloud platform, application platform | 反义词: IaaS (Infrastructure as a Service), on-premise | 词性: noun
