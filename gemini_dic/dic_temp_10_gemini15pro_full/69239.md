feasiblenesses:/ˌfiːzəˈbɪlɪtɪz/
|n.|可行性，可行的方法|The feasiblenesses of the various proposals were carefully considered.  对各种提案的可行性进行了仔细的考虑。|
|英文解析:Plural form of feasibility, referring to the state or degree of being easily or conveniently done. It implies the practicality and possibility of achieving something.  可行性的复数形式，指的是容易或方便地完成的状态或程度。它暗示了实现某事的实用性和可能性。|
|例句：We are exploring the feasiblenesses of different approaches. 我们正在探索不同方法的可行性。|
|例句：The report analyzed the feasiblenesses of three potential solutions.  报告分析了三种潜在解决方案的可行性。|
|近义词：possibilities, practicabilities, viabilities|
|反义词：impossibilities, impracticalities|
|词性:noun|
