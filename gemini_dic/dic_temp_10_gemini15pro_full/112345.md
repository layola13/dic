loricata:/lɔːrɪˈkeɪtə/ | n. |  loricates,  有甲类动物;  被甲动物 |  A group of animals characterized by having a lorica, a protective outer covering.  Loricata encompasses several groups, including chitons (Polyplacophora) and some rotifers (Rotifera). | The chiton, a member of the Loricata, has a shell made of eight overlapping plates.  石鳖，一种有甲类动物，有一个由八块重叠的板组成的壳。 | synonyms:  shell-bearing animals, armored animals | antonyms:  soft-bodied animals | noun
