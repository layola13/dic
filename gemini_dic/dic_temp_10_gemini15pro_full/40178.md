contentional: /kənˈtɛnʃənl/
adjective
有争议的；争论的；爱争论的
Relating to or involving contention; argumentative.  Characterized by disputes or disagreements.  Inclined to argue or dispute.
The judge's decision was highly contentional, sparking protests from both sides.  法官的判决极具争议性，引发了双方的抗议。
The meeting became quite contentional when the topic of budget cuts was raised. 当提出预算削减的话题时，会议变得相当有争议。
近义词: controversial, disputed, debatable, argumentative
反义词: uncontroversial, undisputed, agreeable, amicable
词性: adjective
