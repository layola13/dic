antidogmatically:/ˌæntiˌdɔːɡˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 反教条地，不教条地 | In a manner that opposes or avoids dogma, asserting opinions or doctrines as undeniably true.  | He approached the issue antidogmatically, considering all perspectives before forming an opinion. 他反教条地处理这个问题，在形成意见之前考虑了所有观点。 | 近义词: open-mindedly, liberally, flexibly | 反义词: dogmatically, rigidly, doctrinally | 词性: 副词
