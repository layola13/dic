outro:/ˈaʊtrəʊ/ (US also /ˈaʊtroʊ/) | n. | 结尾，片尾，结束曲 | A concluding section of a piece of music or a radio or television programme. | The outro of the song was a beautiful piano melody. 这首歌的结尾是一段优美的钢琴旋律。| The radio DJ played a short outro before the news bulletin.  电台DJ在新闻简报前播放了一段简短的结束曲。| synonyms: ending, coda, finale, postlude | antonyms: intro, beginning, prelude | noun
