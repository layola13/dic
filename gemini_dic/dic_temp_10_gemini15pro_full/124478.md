mouldable:/ˈməʊldəbl/ (US) /ˈmoʊldəbl/| adj.|可塑的，易成型的|Able to be shaped or formed, as by hammering or pressing.|The clay is very mouldable when wet. 湿的粘土很容易塑形。|Synonyms: pliable, ductile, malleable, plastic, adaptable|Antonyms: rigid, inflexible, stiff, unyielding|adjective

mouldable:/ˈməʊldəbl/ (US) /ˈmoʊldəbl/| adj.|易受影响的|Easily influenced or changed.|Young children's minds are very mouldable.  小孩子的心智很容易受影响。|Synonyms: impressionable, susceptible, receptive|Antonyms: resistant, impervious, unyielding|adjective
