antihierarchical:/ˌæntiˌhaɪəˈrɑːrkɪkəl/
|adj.|反等级制度的，反对等级制度的|Characterized by opposition to or rejection of a hierarchical system, especially in social or political structures.|The antihierarchical nature of the internet allows anyone with a connection to publish their thoughts.|Synonyms: egalitarian, democratic, nonhierarchical|Antonyms: hierarchical, authoritarian, stratified
|adjective|
