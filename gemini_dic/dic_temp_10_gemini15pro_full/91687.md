hyperconcentration:/ˌhaɪpərˌkɑːnsənˈtreɪʃən/ | n. | 超集中，过度集中 |  The state of being excessively focused on a single task or activity, often to the exclusion of everything else. |  His hyperconcentration on the video game made him oblivious to his surroundings. 他对电子游戏的过度集中让他对周围的环境浑然不觉。|  intense focus, absorption, preoccupation | distraction, inattention | noun
