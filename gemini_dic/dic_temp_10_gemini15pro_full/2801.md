advancingly:/ədˈvænsɪŋli/
adverb
前进地，逐步地
in a manner that progresses or moves forward
The company is advancingly investing in new technologies.  这家公司正在逐步投资新技术。
He spoke advancingly about the future of the industry. 他对该行业的未来进行了前瞻性的发言。
近义词: progressively, increasingly, gradually
反义词: regressively, decreasingly, statically
词性: adverb

词源:
From advance (v.) + -ing + -ly.  Advance itself comes from Old French avancer "to move forward," from a- "to" + van "front."


USAGE->
修饰动词:
The project is advancingly moving towards completion. = 该项目正在逐步走向完成。
The negotiations are advancingly progressing. = 谈判正在逐步取得进展。

修饰形容词:
The situation is becoming advancingly complex. = 局势正变得越来越复杂。(此处用法略有不自然, 更推荐用increasingly complex)
His health is advancingly deteriorating. = 他的健康状况正在逐步恶化。(此处用法略有不自然, 更推荐用progressively deteriorating)


PHRASE->
(由于advancingly本身并非常用词，因此很难找到常见的词组搭配。以下列出一些可能的用法，但并不常见)
advancingly towards =  逐步走向
advancingly into = 逐步进入
advancingly through = 逐步通过
advancingly over = 逐步克服
advancingly beyond = 逐步超越


注意事项:
advancingly 并非一个常用的副词，在很多情况下使用 progressively, increasingly 或 gradually 会更自然流畅。  尽管语法上正确，但它在现代英语中相对少见。  在使用时需要注意语境，并考虑是否有更合适的替代词。
