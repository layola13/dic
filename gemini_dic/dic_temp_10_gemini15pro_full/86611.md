词源:
From French *héliotrope*, from Latin *heliotropium*, from Ancient Greek *ἡλιοτρόπιον* (hēliotrópion, “heliotrope (plant)”), from *ἥλιος* (hḗlios, “sun”) + *τρόπος* (trópos, “turn, way”).  So called because its flowers were thought to turn towards the sun.
