argent's:/ˈɑːrdʒənts/
noun
银币，银，钱财
Possession form of "argent," meaning silver or money.  Refers to something belonging to or associated with silver or money.
The knight's argent's gleamed in the sunlight. 骑士的银币在阳光下闪闪发光。
Near synonyms: money, funds, cash, wealth, riches.
Near antonyms: debt, poverty, lack.
