pamphletary: /ˌpæmfləˈtɛri/ | adj. | 小册子式的；像小册子一样的 | Relating to or resembling a pamphlet, especially in being short, polemical, or cheaply produced. | The candidate's pamphletary pronouncements offered little in the way of concrete policy.  候选人小册子式的声明几乎没有提供任何具体的政策。|  The organization distributed pamphletary materials to raise awareness about the issue. 该组织分发了小册子式的材料来提高人们对这个问题的认识。| synonyms: polemical, propagandistic,  booklet-like | antonyms: comprehensive, in-depth, extensive | adjective
