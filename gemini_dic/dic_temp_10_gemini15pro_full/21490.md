blubberlike:/ˈblʌbərlaɪk/| adj.|像鲸脂的，肥胖的|Resembling blubber; fat and flabby. |The man had a blubberlike stomach.  这个男人有一个像鲸脂一样的肚子。|The walrus had thick, blubberlike skin. 海象有着厚厚的、像鲸脂一样的皮肤。|近义词：fatty, flabby, obese, plump, chubby|反义词：thin, lean, slender|词性:形容词
blubber:/ˈblʌbər/| n.|鲸脂|The fat of sea mammals, especially whales and seals.|Whales use blubber for insulation. 鲸鱼用鲸脂保暖。|近义词：fat, adipose tissue|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑muscle, lean meat)|词性:名词
-like:| suffix.|像…的；似…的；如同…的 |A suffix used to form adjectives meaning “resembling,” “characteristic of,” or “suitable for.”| childlike  像孩子一样的|近义词:-ish, -esque, -y|反义词:unlike|词性:后缀

词源: "blubber" comes from Middle English "blober," likely of imitative origin, suggesting the sound of something splashing or bubbling.  "-like" is a common Old English suffix.
