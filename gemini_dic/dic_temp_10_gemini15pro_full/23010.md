bouillabaisse:/ˌbuːjəˈbeɪs/| n.|法式海鲜浓汤|A rich, spicy stew or soup made with various kinds of fish, shellfish, and vegetables, typically flavored with saffron and herbs, originating in Provence, France.|We had a delicious bouillabaisse at the restaurant by the sea. 我们在海边的餐厅享用了一顿美味的法式海鲜浓汤。|例句：The bouillabaisse was rich with the flavors of the sea. 这道法式海鲜浓汤充满了浓郁的海洋风味。|近义词：fish stew, seafood stew|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps something like "plain broth")|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Provençal *bolhabaissa*, from *bolhir* ("to boil") + *abaissar* ("to lower the heat").  The cooking process involves boiling the ingredients and then reducing the heat to simmer.
