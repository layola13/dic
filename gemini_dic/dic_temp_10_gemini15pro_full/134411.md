noninvidiously:/ˌnɒnɪnˈvɪdiəsli/
adverb
没有恶意地；不带偏见地；公平地
Without envy or ill will; impartially; fairly.
He noninvidiously distributed the prizes among the winners. 他公平地将奖品分发给获奖者。
Synonyms: impartially, fairly, equitably, justly
Antonyms: invidiously, unfairly, biasedly, prejudicially
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + invidious + -ly.  "Invidious" comes from Latin invidiosus "envied, hateful," from invidia "envy, odium," from invidere "to look askance at, envy, grudge," from in- "upon, at" + videre "to see."  Thus, "noninvidious" literally means "not envious" or "not causing envy."
