barker:/ˈbɑːrkər/ | n. |叫卖者；狂吠的狗；手枪；树皮剥取者|A person who shouts to attract customers to a show, stall, etc.  A dog that barks frequently. A type of pistol. A person who removes bark from trees. |The barker outside the circus tent promised amazing feats.  马戏团帐篷外的叫卖者承诺会有惊人的表演。

The dog was a real barker, disturbing the neighbors constantly. 这只狗真是个爱叫的家伙，不断打扰邻居。

He carried a .22 barker in his pocket. 他的口袋里带着一把.22口径的手枪。

The barker skillfully stripped the bark from the oak tree.  剥皮工熟练地剥下橡树的树皮。 |近义词：shouter, hawker, crier (for a person), yapper (for a dog) |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


bark:/bɑːrk/ | v. |吠叫；厉声说出；剥皮| (of a dog) to make a loud, rough noise. To say something quickly and sharply. To remove the bark from a tree. |The dog barked at the mailman. 狗对着邮递员吠叫。

"Get out!" he barked angrily. “滚出去！”他愤怒地吼道。

They barked the logs before using them to build the cabin. 他们在用原木建造小屋之前先剥了皮。 |近义词：yelp, howl, yap (for dog barking), shout, yell (for human speech), peel, strip (for removing bark) |反义词: whisper, murmur (for human speech) |词性: verb
