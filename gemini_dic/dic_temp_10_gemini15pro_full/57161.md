dowdinesses:/ˈdaʊdinɪsɪz/
noun
邋遢，寒酸（dowdiness的复数形式）
The state of being dowdy; shabbiness or unattractiveness in dress or appearance.  The dowdinesses of her wardrobe made her feel self-conscious.  她衣橱里的邋遢衣服让她感到局促不安。
近义词:shabbiness, frumpiness, drabness
反义词:smartness, stylishness, elegance
词性:noun (plural)

dowdiness:/ˈdaʊdinɪs/
noun
邋遢，寒酸
The state of being dowdy; shabbiness or unattractiveness in dress or appearance. Her dowdiness made her stand out in the fashionable crowd. 她的邋遢让她在时尚人群中显得格格不入。
近义词:shabbiness, frumpiness, drabness
反义词:smartness, stylishness, elegance
词性:noun

dowdy:/ˈdaʊdi/
adjective
邋遢的，寒酸的
(of a person or their clothes) unfashionable and dull in appearance.  She felt dowdy in her old dress. 她穿着旧裙子感觉很邋遢。
近义词:frumpy, drab, shabby
反义词:stylish, smart, fashionable
词性:adjective

词源: From the noun "dowd," meaning "an unfashionable or dowdy woman," of uncertain origin.
