cryogenic:/kraɪəˈdʒɛnɪk/ | adj. |低温的;低温学的|Relating to or involving the branch of physics that deals with the production and effects of very low temperatures.|例句：Cryogenic freezing is used to preserve biological samples.  低温冷冻用于保存生物样本。

例句：Cryogenic fuels are used in rocket propulsion. 低温燃料用于火箭推进。|近义词：low-temperature, freezing, frigid|反义词：high-temperature, hot, boiling|词性:形容词

cryogenics:/kraɪəˈdʒɛnɪks/ | n. |低温学|The branch of physics that deals with the production and effects of very low temperatures.|例句：Cryogenics plays a vital role in space exploration. 低温学在太空探索中起着至关重要的作用。

例句：He specializes in cryogenics and its applications in medicine. 他专攻低温学及其在医学上的应用。|近义词：low-temperature physics|反义词：high-temperature physics|词性:名词
