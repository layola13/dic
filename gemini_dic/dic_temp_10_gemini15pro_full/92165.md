hypersomnolently:/ˌhaɪpərˈsɒmnələntli/ /ˌhaɪpərˈsɑːmnələntli/
| adv. | 过度嗜睡地 | In a manner characterized by excessive sleepiness. |  He moved hypersomnolently, as if still half-asleep. 他行动迟缓，仿佛还没睡醒。|Synonyms: sleepily, drowsily, lethargically | Antonyms: alertly, energetically, vigilantly | Word Type: Adverb

Etymology:
From hyper- (meaning "excessive") + somnolent (meaning "sleepy") + -ly (adverbial suffix).  The word "somnolent" comes from Latin "somnolentus," from "somnus" meaning "sleep."
