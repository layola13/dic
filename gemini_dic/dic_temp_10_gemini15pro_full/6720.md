analogousness:/əˈnæləɡəsnəs/
n.
相似性；类比性
The state or quality of being analogous; resemblance or similarity in certain respects between things otherwise unlike.
The analogousness between the human brain and a computer is often discussed.  人脑和计算机之间的相似性经常被讨论。
近义词: similarity, resemblance, likeness, parallel, comparability
反义词: difference, dissimilarity, unlikeness, contrast
