churnability:/ˌtʃɜːrnəˈbɪləti/
n.
客户流失率，客户流失倾向
The rate at which customers stop using a service or buying a product.
The company is trying to reduce churn by offering better customer service. 该公司正试图通过提供更好的客户服务来降低客户流失率。
近义词：attrition, turnover
反义词：retention, loyalty
词性: 名词

Etymology:  From "churn" (meaning to agitate or stir violently) + "-ability" (suffix indicating capability or possibility).  The original meaning of "churn" referred to the process of making butter, where milk or cream is agitated to separate the butterfat.  In a business context, "churn" refers to the "stirring up" or loss of customers.
