enopla:/ɪˈnəʊplə/
noun
刺胞动物门下的一个纲
A class within the phylum Cnidaria, characterized by the presence of nematocysts (stinging cells) in the epidermis and gastrodermis.  They typically have a complex life cycle with both polyp and medusa stages.
Enopla contains sea anemones and corals. 刺胞动物包含海葵和珊瑚。
近义词:Anthozoa (sometimes used interchangeably, but not taxonomically equivalent)
反义词:None (as it's a taxonomic classification)
词性:n.
