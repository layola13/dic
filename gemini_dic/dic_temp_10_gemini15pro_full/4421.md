alarmist:/əˈlɑːmɪst/ | n. adj. | 危言耸听者；危言耸听的 | A person who exaggerates danger and creates unnecessary fear; causing unnecessary fear or anxiety. | The news report was criticized for being alarmist and creating panic. 这篇新闻报道因危言耸听制造恐慌而受到批评。 |  n. scaremonger, doomsayer; adj. panicky, hysterical | n. realist; adj. calm, reassuring | noun, adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From alarm + -ist.  "Alarm" comes from the Italian "all'arme" meaning "to arms!".
