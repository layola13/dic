amphigamous:/æmˈfɪɡəməs/
adjective
两性繁殖的
Reproducing sexually; having the sexes united in the same individual or hermaphroditic.
Amphigamous plants rely on pollination for reproduction. 两性繁殖的植物依靠授粉进行繁殖。
Most flowering plants are amphigamous. 大多数开花植物是两性繁殖的。
synonym: sexual, hermaphroditic, monoecious
antonym: asexual, agamous, dioecious
