impartations:/ˌɪmpɑːrˈteɪʃənz/| n.|传授，赋予，给予|The impartations of knowledge and wisdom are essential for personal growth.  知识和智慧的传授对个人成长至关重要。|例句：The teacher's impartations of knowledge inspired her students. 老师传授的知识鼓舞了她的学生。

例句：His impartations of spiritual wisdom were profound. 他所传授的精神智慧意义深远。

近义词：bestowal, conferral, communication, transmission|反义词: withholding, deprivation|词性:名词
