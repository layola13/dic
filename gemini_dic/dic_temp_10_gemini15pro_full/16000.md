bamboozle:/bæmˈbuːzl/ | v. | 欺骗，迷惑，哄骗 | to trick or deceive someone, often in a playful or mischievous way |  The magician bamboozled the audience with his clever tricks. 魔术师用他巧妙的戏法迷惑了观众。

He bamboozled me into lending him money. 他骗我借钱给他。| 近义词：deceive, trick, hoodwink, mislead, dupe, confound, bewilder, perplex | 反义词：enlighten, clarify, explain | 词性: 动词
