nonatrophic:/ˌnɒnəˈtrɒfɪk/ | adj. | 非萎缩的 | Not atrophic; without atrophy.  Relating to or characterized by the absence of atrophy. | The biopsy revealed nonatrophic tissue, suggesting the condition was recently developed.  活检显示组织没有萎缩，表明病情是最近才发展的。| The doctor noted the patient's nonatrophic muscles despite prolonged immobility. 尽管长期不动，医生注意到病人的肌肉没有萎缩。| 近义词: healthy, normal, undeveloped | 反义词: atrophic, wasted, shrunken | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
"non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "atrophic" (from Greek "a-" (without) + "trophikós" (nourishing)).
