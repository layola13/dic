mismeasure:/mɪsˈmɛʒər/ | v. n. | 误判，错误估计；错误的衡量 | To measure incorrectly; to misjudge or underestimate. |  The tailor mismeasured the fabric and the suit was too small.  裁缝量错了布料，导致西装太小了。 | synonyms: misjudge, underestimate, miscalculate, err | antonyms: assess accurately, evaluate correctly, judge properly | verb

mismeasure:/mɪsˈmɛʒər/ | n. | 错误的衡量；误判 | An incorrect measurement; a misjudgment or underestimation. | The mismeasure of the ingredients ruined the cake.  配料的错误计量毁了蛋糕。 | synonyms: misjudgment, miscalculation, error, blunder | antonyms: accurate assessment, correct evaluation, proper judgment | noun
