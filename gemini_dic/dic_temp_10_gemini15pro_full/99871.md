intermuscularity:/ˌɪntərˈmʌskjʊˈlærɪti/
n.
肌肉间性
The state or quality of being intermuscular; the arrangement or distribution of muscles within a particular region or structure.
The intermuscularity of the thigh affects its flexibility. 大腿肌肉间性影响其灵活性。
近义词:  None (This is a highly specific term with no direct synonyms.)
反义词: None
词性: noun


intermuscular:/ˌɪntərˈmʌskjʊlər/
adj.
肌肉间的；位于肌肉之间的
Situated between muscles.
Intermuscular injections are given deep into the muscle tissue. 肌内注射是将药物注射到肌肉组织深处。
近义词: intramuscular
反义词: None
词性: adjective
