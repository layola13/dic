cronish:/ˈkrɔːnɪʃ/ | adj. | 康沃尔郡的；康沃尔郡人的；康沃尔语的 | Relating to Cornwall, its people, or their language. |  Cornish pasties are a popular regional dish. 康沃尔馅饼是一种很受欢迎的地区菜肴。

|  He speaks Cornish fluently. 他能流利地说康沃尔语。 | synonyms:  Cornwall, Devonian (related geographically) | antonyms:  None | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *Cornyshe, from Old English *Cornwilisc (attested as Cornwālisc), from Proto-West Germanic *Kurnwalhisk, from Proto-Germanic *Kurnwalhaz (“Cornish person”), from *kurn- + *walhaz (“foreigner, Celt”). Cognate with Dutch *Kornels, German *Kornis.  The -ish suffix is a common Germanic adjective-forming suffix.
