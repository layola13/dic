antirecession:/ˌænti rɪˈsɛʃən/
|adj.|
反衰退的，防止经济衰退的|
Designed or intended to prevent or counteract a recession.  Aimed at mitigating the effects of an economic downturn.|
例句：
The government introduced an antirecessionary budget. 政府推出了一个反衰退预算。

The central bank implemented antirecession measures. 中央银行实施了反衰退措施。

|近义词：countercyclical, stimulative|
|反义词：procyclical|
|词性：adjective|
