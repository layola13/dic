overbrightnesses:/ˈoʊvərˈbraɪtnəsɪz/
noun
过度亮度；过亮
The state of being excessively bright.
The overbrightnesses of the headlights made it difficult to see.  前灯的过亮使得很难看清。
The overbrightnesses on the screen strained my eyes. 屏幕的过亮使我的眼睛很疲劳。
synonyms: excessive brightness, glare, overexposure
antonyms: dimness, darkness, faintness
n.
