一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I do my homework every day.  我每天都做作业。
She does her exercises in the morning.  她早上做运动。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am doing my homework right now. 我现在正在做作业。
He is doing the dishes at the moment. 他现在正在洗碗。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have done all my chores. 我已经完成了所有家务。
She has done a great job. 她做得很好。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been doing yoga for an hour. 我已经做瑜伽一个小时了。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I did my homework last night. 我昨晚做了我的家庭作业。
She did her best in the competition. 她在比赛中尽力了。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was doing my homework when you called. 你打电话时我正在做作业。
He was doing the dishes when the phone rang.  电话响的时候他正在洗碗。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had done my homework before dinner. 我在晚饭前已经完成了作业。
She had done all the preparations before the guests arrived.  客人们到达之前，她已经完成了所有准备工作。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been doing homework for two hours before I took a break. 我做了两个小时的作业才休息。
They had been working on that project for months before they finally finished it.  他们在那个项目上工作了好几个月才最终完成。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will do my homework tomorrow. 我明天会做作业。
She will do her best to help you. 她会尽力帮助你。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be doing my homework this evening.  今晚我将做作业。
He will be doing his presentation at 3 pm tomorrow. 明天下午3点他将做报告。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I will have done my homework by the time you get home.  到你回家的时候，我就已经完成作业了。
She will have finished her degree by next summer.  到明年夏天，她将完成她的学位。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next year, I will have been doing yoga for five years.  到明年，我将已经练习瑜伽五年了。
By the time he retires, he will have been working at this company for 40 years.  到他退休的时候，他将在这家公司工作了40年。

注意事项:

"doed" 虽然在某些方言中使用，但在标准英语中应避免使用。  它的标准过去式和过去分词是 "did"。
