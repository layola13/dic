indentors:/ɪnˈdɛntərz/| n.|缩进器；压头；压痕器|A tool or device used to make indentations, especially in metal or other hard materials.  Also, in software, it refers to spaces or tabs used to create indentation in code.|The machinist used an indentor to mark the metal plate.  机械师用压头在金属板上做标记。|Synonyms:  indenter, stamp, die, marker|Antonyms: smoother, leveler|noun
