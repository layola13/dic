nontolerant:/nɒnˈtɒlərənt/ /nɑːnˈtɑːlərənt/| adj.|不宽容的，不能容忍的|Not tolerant; unwilling or unable to tolerate differences in opinion, behavior, etc.|例句：She is nontolerant of any dissent. 她不能容忍任何异议.

例句：The community is known for its nontolerant attitude towards outsiders. 这个社区以对外来人不宽容的态度而闻名.

近义词：intolerant, bigoted, prejudiced, narrow-minded|反义词:tolerant, accepting, open-minded|词性:形容词
