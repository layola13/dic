carburises:/ˈkɑːrbjʊˌraɪzɪz/
verb
渗碳; 使增碳
To introduce carbon into (iron or steel) in order to harden it.
The gears are carburised to increase their surface hardness. 这些齿轮经过渗碳处理以提高其表面硬度。
synonyms:carbonize, carburize
antonyms:decarburize, decarburize
词性:v.
