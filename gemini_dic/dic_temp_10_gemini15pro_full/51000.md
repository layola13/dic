desist:/dɪˈzɪst/ /dɪˈsɪst/| vi.|停止，中止|To cease to proceed or act; stop.|The company was ordered to desist from using the misleading advertisement.  该公司被勒令停止使用误导性广告。|例句：They were ordered to desist from any further attempts to enter the building.  他们被命令停止任何进一步进入大楼的企图。|近义词：stop, cease, discontinue, abstain, refrain|反义词：continue, persist, persevere|词性：动词
Etymology: From Old French desister "to leave off, cease," from Latin desistere "to leave off, stand aside," from de- "off, away" + sistere "to cause to stand, place, set, stand still."
