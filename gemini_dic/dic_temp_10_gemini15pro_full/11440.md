argot:/ˈɑːrɡoʊ/ (US), /ˈɑːɡɒt/ (UK)| n.|暗语，行话，黑话|The special vocabulary of a particular group, such as criminals or a profession.|例句：Thieves’ argot is hard for most people to understand. 小偷的黑话大多数人很难理解。

例句：The computer industry has its own argot, full of acronyms and technical terms. 计算机行业有自己的行话，充满了首字母缩写词和技术术语。|近义词：jargon, slang, cant, vernacular|反义词:standard language, formal language|词性:noun
