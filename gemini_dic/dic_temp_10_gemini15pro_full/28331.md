caramelised: /ˈkærəməlaɪzd/ | adj. | 焦糖化的，糖煮的 | Heated with sugar until brown and sweet. | Example 1: The caramelised onions added a rich sweetness to the burger. 焦糖化的洋葱给汉堡增添了浓郁的甜味。

Example 2:  She served a delicious caramelised apple tart for dessert. 她端上了一份美味的焦糖苹果馅饼作为甜点。 | 近义词: candied, glazed | 反义词: bland, unsweetened | 词性: 形容词


caramelize: /ˈkærəməlaɪz/ | v. |  焦糖化，糖煮 | To cook (something) with sugar until it becomes brown and sweet. | Example 1:  I caramelized the bananas for the topping. 我把香蕉做成了焦糖色的配料。

Example 2: The chef caramelized the sugar for the crème brûlée. 厨师将糖焦糖化，用于制作焦糖布丁。| 近义词: candy, glaze | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 动词
