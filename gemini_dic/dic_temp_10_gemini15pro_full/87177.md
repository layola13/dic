herderite:/ˈhɜːrdəˌraɪt/ | n. | Herderite（羟基磷灰石） | A rare mineral, a beryllium phosphate hydroxide, often found in granitic pegmatites. It can be colorless, white, yellowish, or greenish. | Herderite is a sought-after mineral by collectors.  羟基磷灰石是收藏家们梦寐以求的矿物。 | Herderite crystals can be quite beautiful. 羟基磷灰石晶体可以非常漂亮。 |Synonyms: none (as it is a specific mineral name) | Antonyms: none | noun
