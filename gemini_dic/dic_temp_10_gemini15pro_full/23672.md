branks:/bræŋks/| n. |嚼子，口衔，责骂，苛评|A kind of bridle for scolding women. A metal framework that enclosed the head and had a bit or gag that pressed against the tongue. Used as a punishment for gossiping or nagging. |The branks were used to punish gossiping women in the 17th century.|近义词：scold's bridle, gag, muzzle|反义词:|名词
