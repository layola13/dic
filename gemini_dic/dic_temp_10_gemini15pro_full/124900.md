muddler:/ˈmʌdlər/| n.| 搅棒; 糊涂的人; 笨手笨脚的人 |A person or tool used for muddling, especially one used to crush ingredients for cocktails.  A person who muddles things or acts in a confused or clumsy way. |  He used a muddler to crush the mint leaves for the mojito. 他用搅棒捣碎了莫吉托用的薄荷叶。

She's such a muddler, she always forgets her keys. 她真是个糊涂虫，总是忘记带钥匙。| 近义词：pestle, stirrer, blunderer, bungler | 反义词：organizer, expert | 词性: 名词
