mummifying:/ˈmʌmɪfaɪɪŋ/
verb
木乃伊化;使成木乃伊;使干瘪
The process of preserving a body by embalming and wrapping it in cloth.
The ancient Egyptians were experts in mummifying their pharaohs.  古埃及人精通将他们的法老木乃伊化。
The hot desert air mummified the body. 炎热的沙漠空气使尸体干瘪。
embalming, preserving
desiccating, drying
verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From mummy + -fying.  Ultimately from Persian *mūmiya (“bitumen, wax”).
