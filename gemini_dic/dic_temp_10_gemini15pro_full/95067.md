inadmissibly:/ˌɪnədˈmɪsəbli/ | adv. | 不可接受地，不能容许地 | Not admissible; in a way that cannot be allowed or accepted. |  His evidence was ruled inadmissibly obtained. 他的证据被裁定为非法获取。|  The lawyer argued that the evidence was inadmissibly prejudicial. 律师辩称，该证据具有不可接受的偏见性。| 近义词: unacceptably, improperly, wrongly | 反义词: acceptably, properly, legitimately | 词性: 副词

词源:
From in- (not) + admissible + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Admissible" comes from the Latin "admittere" (to admit).
