cosy:/ˈkoʊzi/ (US) /ˈkəʊzi/ (UK)| adj.|舒适的，温暖的，惬意的|Giving a feeling of comfort, warmth, and relaxation.|例句：We spent a cosy evening by the fire. 我们在火炉旁度过了一个舒适的夜晚。

例句：The room was small and cosy. 房间小而舒适。

近义词：comfortable, snug, warm, homely|反义词：uncomfortable, cold, bleak|词性:形容词

cosey:/ˈkoʊzi/ (US) /ˈkəʊzi/ (UK)| n.|保暖罩，茶壶套|A covering for a teapot or other vessel to keep its contents warm.|例句：She put a cosy on the teapot. 她给茶壶套上一个保暖罩。

例句：The tea cosy kept the tea warm.  茶壶套使茶保持温暖。


近义词：tea cosy (specifically for teapots)|反义词：N/A|词性:名词
