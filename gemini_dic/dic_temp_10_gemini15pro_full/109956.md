liberalness:/ˈlɪbərəlnəs/
n.
慷慨；大方；自由主义；开明
The quality of being liberal; generosity; broad-mindedness.
His liberalness with his money was well known. 他花钱的大方是众所周知的。
Synonyms: generosity, magnanimity, open-handedness, munificence, bounty, largesse, liberality
Antonyms: stinginess, meanness, parsimony, miserliness, illiberality
noun


Etymology:
From liberal + -ness.  "liberal" comes from Late Middle English: from Old French, or from late Latin liberalis ‘of freemen, noble, generous’, from liber ‘free’.
