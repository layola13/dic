lossful:/ˈlɔːsfʊl/
adjective
导致损失的；徒劳的
incurring loss; unprofitable
例句：
The company's lossful investment in the new technology ultimately led to its bankruptcy.
这家公司在该新技术上的亏损投资最终导致其破产。

This proved to be a lossful venture.
这证明是一次亏损的冒险。

近义词：unprofitable, losing, disadvantageous, detrimental, costly
反义词：profitable, lucrative, beneficial, advantageous
