grimacingly:/ɡrɪˈmeɪsɪŋli/ | adv. |  做鬼脸地，痛苦地 |  In a way that shows a grimace, a facial expression of pain, disgust, or disapproval. | He grimacingly accepted the bitter medicine. 他痛苦地接受了苦药。| 近义词: wryly, painfully, reluctantly | 反义词: cheerfully, gladly, willingly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From grimace (n.) + -ing + -ly.  "Grimace" comes from Old French "grimace" (a wry face), of uncertain origin.
