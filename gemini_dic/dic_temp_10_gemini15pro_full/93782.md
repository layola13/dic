immensible:/ɪˈmɛnsəbl/ | adj. | 不可浸没的，不能浸入的 | Not capable of being immersed or dipped; not immersible.  |  The special coating makes the fabric immensible, preventing it from absorbing water.  这种特殊的涂层使织物不可浸没，防止其吸水。 | 近义词: waterproof, nonabsorbent, impermeable | 反义词: permeable, absorbent, immersible (rarely used in this sense) | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From in- "not" + immensible, from Latin immers- past participle stem of immergere "to plunge in," from in- "in" + mergere "to dip, plunge."
