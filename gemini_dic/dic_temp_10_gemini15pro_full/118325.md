meretriciousness:/ˌmɛrɪˈtrɪʃəsnɪs/| n. |俗艳；华而不实；虚有其表|The meretriciousness of the decorations was overwhelming. 装饰的俗艳令人难以忍受。| 近义词：gaudy, tawdry, flashy, ostentatious, superficiality, showiness | 反义词：simplicity, genuineness, authenticity, understatement, substance |词性：名词

meretricious:/ˌmɛrɪˈtrɪʃəs/| adj. |俗艳的；华而不实的；虚有其表的|Her meretricious style of dress attracted attention, but not admiration. 她俗艳的穿着吸引了人们的目光，但并没有赢得赞赏。| 近义词：gaudy, tawdry, flashy, ostentatious | 反义词：simple, genuine, authentic, understated |词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *meretricius* "of or pertaining to prostitutes," from *meretrix* "prostitute," from *merere, merit-* "to earn, deserve, gain, acquire."  The original sense was "like a prostitute, alluring by false attractions," hence "showily but falsely attractive."
