hydrologists:/haɪˈdrɒlədʒɪsts/ | n. | 水文学家 | Scientists who study the distribution, circulation, and properties of water on the earth and in the atmosphere. | The hydrologists are studying the effects of the drought on the river. 水文学家正在研究干旱对河流的影响。|  Hydrologists predicted that the rainfall would increase this year. 水文学家预测今年降雨量将会增加. | synonyms: water scientists, hydrographers | antonyms: N/A | noun
