blight:/blaɪt/| n. vt.|枯萎病，祸害，破坏，使…枯萎|A disease that causes plants to wither and die. / Something that spoils or damages something.|例句：The potato blight caused widespread famine. 马铃薯枯萎病造成了 widespread famine.

The abandoned factories are a blight on the landscape. 废弃的工厂是这片土地上的一个祸害。|近义词：disease, affliction, curse, ruin, spoil, damage|反义词：blessing, boon, improvement|n.

blight:/blaɪt/| vt.|使…枯萎，破坏，摧毁|To cause something to wither and die. / To spoil or damage something.|例句：The frost blighted the crops. 霜冻使农作物枯萎了。

His career was blighted by scandal. 他的职业生涯被丑闻毁了。|近义词：wither, shrivel, ruin, spoil, damage|反义词：nourish, improve|vt.
