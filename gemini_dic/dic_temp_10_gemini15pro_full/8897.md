antigay:/ˌæntiˈɡeɪ/ | adj. | 反同性恋的；恐同的 | Characterized by prejudice, discrimination, or antagonism directed against gay people. |  He made some antigay remarks. 他发表了一些反同性恋的言论。| homophobic, bigoted |  pro-gay, supportive | adjective


词源:

anti- (prefix): against, opposed to.  From Greek "anti".
gay: homosexual.  Of uncertain origin, but likely from Old French "gai" meaning joyful or merry.  Its association with homosexuality began in the 20th century.
