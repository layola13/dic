diazo:/ˌdaɪˈæzoʊ/ /ˌdaɪˈeɪzoʊ/ | adj. n. | 重氮基，重氮化合物 |  Relating to or containing two linked nitrogen atoms (N₂), especially as a functional group in an organic compound.  As a noun, it refers to a compound containing the diazo group. |  Diazo compounds are used in the production of dyes. 重氮化合物用于染料生产。| 近义词:  (For the functional group) dinitrogen | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective, noun

diazotize:/daɪˈæzəˌtaɪz/ /daɪˈeɪzəˌtaɪz/ | vt. | 使重氮化 | To introduce a diazo group into (a compound), typically by reacting an aromatic amine with nitrous acid. | The aniline was diazotized and then coupled with a phenol to form the dye.  苯胺被重氮化，然后与苯酚偶联形成染料。| 近义词: N/A | 反义词: N/A | 词性: verb
