gesticulate:/dʒɛˈstɪkjʊleɪt/| v. |做手势；用手势表达|To make gestures, especially when speaking, to emphasize one's words or express an idea.|She was gesticulating wildly, trying to explain what had happened. 她疯狂地做着手势，试图解释发生了什么。|The coach gesticulated to the referee, complaining about the foul. 教练向裁判做着手势，抱怨犯规。|Synonym: gesture, signal, motion, wave|Antonym: be still, stay put|verb
