catonian: /kəˈtoʊniən/
adjective|卡托式的，卡托学派的|Relating to Cato the Younger (95-46 BC), a Roman statesman known for his conservatism, stern morality, and opposition to Julius Caesar.  Characterized by austerity, frugality, and moral strictness. |His catonian speeches emphasized traditional Roman values. 他的卡托式演讲强调了传统的罗马价值观。|近义词: austere, spartan, stoic, unyielding |反义词: luxurious, extravagant, hedonistic|词性:形容词
