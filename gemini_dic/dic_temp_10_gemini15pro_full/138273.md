notaeum:/nəʊˈtiːəm/ | n. | 背板，中胸背板 | The notaeum is the dorsal sclerite of the thoracic segment of an insect.  | The beetle's bright green notaeum was clearly visible.  甲虫亮绿色的背板清晰可见。 | synonyms:  pronotum (when referring to the first thoracic segment),  scutum (a part of the notaeum) | antonyms:  venter (the underside of the insect) | noun
