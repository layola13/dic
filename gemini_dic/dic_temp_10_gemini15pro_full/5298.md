allotropical:/ˌæləˈtrɒpɪkəl/ (US) /ˌæləˈtrɑːpɪkəl/ (UK)| adj.|同素异形的|Relating to allotropy; existing in two or more different physical forms (as an element).|Diamond and graphite are allotropical forms of carbon. 钻石和石墨是碳的同素异形体。|Synonyms: polymorphic, allotropic|Antonyms: monomorphic|adjective
