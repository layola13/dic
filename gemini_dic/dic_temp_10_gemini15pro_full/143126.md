outcarol:/ˌaʊtkærəl/ |v.|以更高的热情唱颂歌；胜过…的颂歌|To sing carols more loudly or enthusiastically than someone else.  To surpass in carolling.|例句：The choir outcaroled all the other groups at the festival.  唱诗班在节日上胜过了所有其他团体。|例句：She outcaroled even the most seasoned singers in the church. 她甚至比教堂里最有经验的歌手唱得更热情。|近义词：outsing, outdo|反义词：be outsung, be outdone|词性：动词
