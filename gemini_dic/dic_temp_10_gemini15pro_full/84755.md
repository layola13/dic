handmade:/ˌhændˈmeɪd/| adj.|手工制的，手工的|Made by hand, not by machine.  Made by a person, not by a factory. |例句：This sweater is handmade. 这件毛衣是手工制作的。|例句：She sells handmade jewelry at the local market. 她在当地市场出售手工珠宝。|近义词：handcrafted, artisanal|反义词: machine-made, factory-produced|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From hand + made, past participle of make. "Hand" meaning "by the hand" and "made" meaning "created or constructed." The combination literally translates to "created by hand."
