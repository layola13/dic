exceed:/ɪkˈsiːd/
verb
超过，超出
to be greater than a number or amount, or to go past an allowed limit
The final cost should not exceed $5,000.  最终费用不应超过 5,000 美元。
surpass, outstrip, transcend
fall short of, be less than
