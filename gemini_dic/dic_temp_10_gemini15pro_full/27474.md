Campania: /kæmˈpeɪnjə/ | n. |坎帕尼亚|A region of southwestern Italy on the Tyrrhenian Sea. Its capital is Naples. | Naples is the largest city in Campania. 那不勒斯是坎帕尼亚最大的城市。| Campania is known for its beautiful coastline. 坎帕尼亚以其美丽的海岸线而闻名。 | 近义词: region, province | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a geographical location) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Latin Campānia, from campus (“plain, field”).
