distream:/daɪˈstriːm/ | v. | 使痛苦，使悲伤 | To cause someone to feel distress or sorrow. | The news of her death distreamed her family. 她去世的消息使她的家人悲痛欲绝。| synonyms: upset, grieve, sadden | antonyms: comfort, soothe, cheer | verb
