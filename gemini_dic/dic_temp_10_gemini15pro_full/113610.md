mach:/mɑːk/ /mæk/ | n. | 马赫; 马赫数 | A unit of speed equal to the speed of sound in a given medium, such as air.  |  例句：The plane flew at Mach 2.  飞机以两倍音速飞行。

例句：The Concorde could travel at speeds of over Mach 2.  协和式飞机可以以超过两倍音速的速度飞行。


近义词：speed of sound | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement) | 词性：noun
