enisled:/ˈɛnˌaɪld/
adjective
孤立的，被隔离的
isolated, cut off, as if on an island
例句：The small village felt enisled after the heavy snowstorm blocked the only road.  大雪封住了唯一的道路后，这个小村庄感觉与世隔绝了。

例句：He felt enisled in his grief after the loss of his wife.  妻子去世后，他沉浸在悲痛中，感觉与世隔绝。
近义词：isolated, marooned, stranded, secluded
反义词：connected, integrated, accessible
词性：adjective


词源: enisle (v.) + -ed.  "Enisle" itself comes from the noun "isle" (island) and the prefix "en-", meaning "to put into or onto."  Therefore, "enisled" literally means "put onto an island," figuratively implying isolation.
