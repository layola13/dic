maucaco:/məˈkɑːkoʊ/ /mɔːˈkɑːkəʊ/
|n.|
|卷尾猴；僧帽猴|
|A type of monkey found in Central and South America, known for its prehensile tail and intelligence.  It is sometimes kept as a pet.|
|The maucaco used its tail to swing from branch to branch. 卷尾猴用尾巴在树枝间荡来荡去。|

|The researchers studied the social behavior of the maucaco in its natural habitat. 研究人员在其自然栖息地研究了僧帽猴的社会行为。|


|近义词: capuchin, monkey|
|反义词: None (No direct opposite for an animal name)|
|词性: noun|
