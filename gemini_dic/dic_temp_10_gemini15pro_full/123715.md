moonlighted:/ˈmuːnˌlaɪtɪd/
verb (past tense)|兼职;  做夜工|Worked a second job, typically secretly, in addition to one's regular employment. |He moonlighted as a taxi driver to earn extra money.  他兼职开出租车赚外快。|Synonyms: freelanced, worked on the side, held a second job|Antonyms: unemployed, jobless|词性: 动词

moonlighting:/ˈmuːnˌlaɪtɪŋ/
noun|兼职; 夜间工作|The act of working a second job, typically secretly, in addition to one's regular employment.|His moonlighting was discovered by his boss. 他的兼职被老板发现了。|Synonyms: freelancing, side hustle, second job|Antonyms: unemployment, main job|词性: 名词
