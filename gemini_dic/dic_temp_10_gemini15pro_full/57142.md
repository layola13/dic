dovi:/ˈdoʊvi/| proper n.|多维，杜比视界（Dolby Vision的缩写）|Dolby Vision (DoVi) is a high dynamic range (HDR) video format developed by Dolby Laboratories. It offers enhanced brightness, contrast, and color compared to standard dynamic range (SDR) video, resulting in a more realistic and immersive viewing experience.  It is often used in streaming services and high-end televisions.|Example 1: My new TV supports DoVi, and the picture quality is amazing. 我的新电视支持杜比视界，画质惊人。

Example 2: The movie is available in DoVi on Netflix. 这部电影在Netflix上有杜比视界版本。|近义词：HDR, HDR10, HLG|反义词:SDR|词性:名词
