biddery:/ˈbɪdəri/| n.|旧货，廉价货，小玩意儿|A miscellaneous collection of small, cheap items, often of little value; trinkets.|She filled her shelves with biddery from the flea market. 她把从跳蚤市场淘来的小玩意儿摆满了架子。|近义词：trinkets, knick-knacks, baubles, gewgaws|反义词: valuables, treasures|词性:名词

biddery:/ˈbɪdəri/| n.|低劣的艺术品，粗制滥造的作品|Artwork or other creative work of poor quality, often mass-produced and gaudy.|The tourist shops were full of cheap biddery.  旅游商店里满是廉价粗糙的工艺品。|近义词：trash, junk, kitsch|反义词:masterpiece, fine art|词性:名词
