bemangle:/biˈmæŋɡəl/
verb
损坏；弄糟；毁坏
to spoil something, especially by making mistakes or by being careless
The text was so bemangled in translation as to be incomprehensible.  译文错误太多，令人费解。
近义词: damage, ruin, spoil, mar, mutilate, distort
反义词: repair, improve, fix
词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + mangle, from Middle English manglen, from Old French mangonier (“to handle roughly, maim, mangle”),  probably of Germanic origin; akin to Old High German mangōn (“to trade, barter”).
