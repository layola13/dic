broken:/ˈbroʊkən/ (US) /ˈbrəʊkən/ (UK)| adj.|破碎的，坏掉的；被打败的；不完整的；（语言）不流利的|Damaged or in pieces; defeated or subdued; incomplete or interrupted; (of a language) not fluent|例句：The vase is broken. 花瓶碎了。

例句：He was a broken man after the loss of his wife. 妻子去世后，他成了一个颓废的人。

近义词：fractured, shattered, damaged, ruined, defeated, beaten|反义词：whole, intact, repaired, fixed, undefeated|词性：形容词

broken:/ˈbroʊkən/ (US) /ˈbrəʊkən/ (UK)| v.|break的过去分词|Past participle of the verb "break"|例句：He has broken his leg. 他摔断了腿。

例句：She has broken the record. 她打破了纪录。

近义词：fractured, shattered, damaged, ruined|反义词：fixed, repaired|词性：动词(过去分词)
