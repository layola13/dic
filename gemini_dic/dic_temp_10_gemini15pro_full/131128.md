nonblasphemies:/nɒnˈblæsfəmiz/
|n.|非亵渎的言论；不亵渎神明的话|The preacher encouraged nonblasphemies and respectful language towards the divine.|牧师鼓励使用非亵渎的言论，并对神明保持尊敬。|Synonyms: reverent words, pious expressions |Antonyms: blasphemies, profanities|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + blasphemy.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of". "Blasphemy" comes from Late Latin blasphemia, from Greek blasphēmia, from blasphemein "to speak evil of, revile, defame," from blaptein "to injure, harm" + phēmē "speech, utterance, rumor, report."
