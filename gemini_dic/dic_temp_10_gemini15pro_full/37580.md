communalizing:/kəˈmjuːnəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使共有化；使公有化；使集体化
The act of making something communal; sharing something among a group.
The government is communalizing healthcare, making it accessible to all citizens. 政府正在将医疗保健公有化，使其惠及所有公民。

The community is communalizing the local garden, allowing everyone to contribute and share the produce. 社区正在将当地花园集体化，允许每个人都出力并分享农产品。

Synonyms: sharing, collectivizing, pooling
Antonyms: privatizing, individualizing
