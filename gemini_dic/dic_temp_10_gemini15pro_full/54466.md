disintegration:/dɪsˌɪntɪˈɡreɪʃn/
|n.| 瓦解，解体，崩溃|The disintegration of the Roman Empire took centuries.  罗马帝国的瓦解历经数个世纪。|
| |分解，碎裂|The disintegration of the rock was caused by weathering. 岩石的碎裂是由风化作用引起的。|
| |衰变|The disintegration of radioactive materials releases energy. 放射性物质的衰变会释放能量。|
| |衰败，堕落|The disintegration of moral values is a serious problem. 道德价值观的衰败是一个严重的问题。|
近义词：decay, decomposition, breakdown, collapse, crumbling, fragmentation
反义词：integration, unification, consolidation, fusion, synthesis
词性：noun

Etymology: From dis- "apart, asunder" + integrate + -ion.  "Dis-" comes from Latin, "integrate" from Latin "integratus," past participle of "integrare" meaning "to make whole," and "-ion" is a suffix forming nouns of action or condition.
