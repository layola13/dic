nonexercise:/ˌnɒnˈɛksəsaɪz/ | adj. | 非运动的；非锻炼性的 | Not related to or involving physical exercise. |  Many factors, including nonexercise activity thermogenesis (NEAT), influence weight. 许多因素，包括非运动性活动产热，都会影响体重。 |  Nonexercise physical activity can contribute significantly to overall health. 非运动性身体活动可以对整体健康做出重大贡献。 | 近义词: sedentary, inactive | 反义词: exercise, active | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "exercise" (from Old French "exercice," ultimately from Latin "exercere" meaning "to keep busy, train").
