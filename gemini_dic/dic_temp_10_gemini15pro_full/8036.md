anthropocentric:/ˌænθrəpəˈsɛntrɪk/ | adj. | 以人类为中心的，人类中心主义的 | Considering human beings as the most significant entity of the universe; interpreting or regarding the world in terms of human values and experiences. |  例句：An anthropocentric worldview often leads to environmental degradation. 以人类为中心的 worldview 经常导致环境恶化。

例句：Many religions have anthropocentric beliefs. 许多宗教都抱持以人类为中心的信仰。| 近义词：human-centered, human-oriented | 反义词：biocentric, ecocentric | 词性: adjective
