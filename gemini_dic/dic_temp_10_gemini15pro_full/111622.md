localising:/ˈləʊkəlaɪzɪŋ/ /ˈloʊkəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
本地化；局限；定位
adapting something to a particular place or market.  Making something relevant to a specific local area or culture.
The company is localising its products for the Asian market.  这家公司正在将其产品本地化为亚洲市场。
The pain was localised in his lower back. 疼痛局限于他的下背部。
localize, adapting, restricting, confining, pinpointing
globalizing, spreading, dispersing
v.
