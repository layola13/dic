overchange:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃeɪndʒ/ /ˌəʊvəˈtʃeɪndʒ/ | vt. n.|多找(钱)；过度收费|To give too much change back to a customer; to charge someone too much for something.|The cashier overcharged me by $5. 收银员多收了我5美元。|Synonyms: overprice, gouge|Antonyms: undercharge, discount|Verb

overchange:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃeɪndʒ/ /ˌəʊvəˈtʃeɪndʒ/ | n.|多找的零钱；过多的收费|The amount of money that is given back in excess of the correct change; an excessive charge.|I received an overchange of $10. 我多收了10美元的零钱。|Synonyms: overpayment|Antonyms: underpayment|Noun
