empanel:/ɪmˈpænəl/| vt.|组成陪审团；选入名单|To enroll a list of jurors; to list or enter in a panel. |The judge empaneled the jury for the murder trial. 法官为这起谋杀案审判挑选了陪审团。|Synonyms: enlist, enroll, register, appoint |Antonyms: discharge, dismiss| verb

empanel:/ɪmˈpænəl/| n.|陪审团名单|A list of jurors. |The empanel for the trial was finalized.  该审判的陪审团名单已最终确定。|Synonyms: jury list, panel, roster |Antonyms: N/A| noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Anglo-French *empaneler, from en- + panel.
