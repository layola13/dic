magisteriality:/ˌmædʒɪˈstɪəriælɪti/ | n. |权威性；威严；专横 |The quality of being magisterial; authoritative, commanding, and dignified. |  His magisteriality was evident in the way he addressed the court. 他在法庭上的发言展现了他的权威性。 |Synonyms: authority, dominance, ascendancy, command, power, imperiousness, pompousness | Antonyms: humility, modesty, meekness, subservience | noun

magisterial:/ˌmædʒɪˈstɪəriəl/ | adj. |权威的；威严的；专横的 |Having or showing great authority; domineering; dictatorial. | He delivered a magisterial performance. 他的表演非常有气势。| Synonyms: authoritative, commanding, imperious, lordly, masterful, domineering | Antonyms: humble, modest, meek, subservient | adjective
