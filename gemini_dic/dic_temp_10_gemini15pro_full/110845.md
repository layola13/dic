lintiest:/ˈlɪntiɪst/
adjective
最满是棉絮的；最挑剔的
most covered in lint; most pedantic or critical
This is the lintiest sweater I own.  这是我拥有的最满是棉絮的毛衣。
He's the lintiest editor I've ever worked with; he finds fault with everything. 他是我合作过最挑剔的编辑；他对每件事都吹毛求疵。
近义词:fussiest, pickiest
反义词:least fussy, least critical
词性:adjective
词源:From lint (fine fluff) + -y (suffix forming adjectives) + -est (superlative suffix).
