confluxibility:/ˌkɒnfluːksɪˈbɪləti/ | n. | 汇流性，融合性 | The property or capability of flowing together or merging.  The confluxibility of the two rivers creates a fertile delta.  两条河流的汇流性创造了一个肥沃的三角洲。 | 近义词:mergeability, combinability | 反义词:divergence, separation | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Latin "confluxus" (flowing together) + "-ibility" (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality).
