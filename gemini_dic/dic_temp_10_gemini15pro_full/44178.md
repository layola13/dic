critiquing:/krɪˈtiːkɪŋ/
verb
评论；批判；鉴定
the act of giving your opinion or judgment about the good or bad qualities of something
She is critiquing the latest exhibition at the museum. 她正在评论博物馆的最新展览。
evaluating, reviewing, assessing, analyzing
praising, commending, lauding
v.
