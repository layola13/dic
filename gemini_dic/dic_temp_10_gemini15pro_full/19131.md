bevelers:/ˈbɛvələrz/
noun
斜切工，磨边工
A person or a machine that bevels something (creates a sloping edge).
The bevelers carefully shaped the edges of the glass.  斜切工仔细地 shaping 玻璃的边缘。
synonyms: chamferers,  edge cutters
antonyms: (No direct antonym for a person, but for the action) straighteners, squarers
词性: noun


beveler:/ˈbɛvələ(r)/
noun
斜切工具，磨边工具
A tool used for beveling (creating a sloping edge).
He used a specialized beveler to create the perfect angle on the wood. 他用一个专门的斜切工具在木头上 creating 出完美的角度。
synonyms: chamfering tool,  beveling plane
antonyms: (No direct antonym for a tool, but for the action) squaring tool
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "bevel" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Bevel" comes from Old French "bivel" meaning "oblique."
