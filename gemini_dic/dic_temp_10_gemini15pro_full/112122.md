longueurs:/lɔ̃ˈɡœʁ/ (French, no direct English equivalent)| n.|冗长，沉闷，乏味|A state of tediousness or boredom, often referring to extended periods of inactivity or dullness.  It's typically used in the plural.  While there's no single perfect English equivalent, it captures the feeling of time stretching out unpleasantly due to lack of stimulation or interest.|The longueurs of the afternoon were unbearable.  下午的冗长乏味令人难以忍受。|Synonyms: tedium, boredom, monotony, dullness|Antonyms: excitement, interest, stimulation|Noun
