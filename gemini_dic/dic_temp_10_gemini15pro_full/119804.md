miio:/ˈmiːoʊ/ | n. | 米家 | Mi Home is a smart home platform developed by Xiaomi. |  Miio is the core communication protocol used in the Xiaomi smart home ecosystem.  It allows different devices, such as smart bulbs, air purifiers, and robots, to communicate with each other and with the Mi Home app. | Example: My Mi Robot Vacuum connects to my phone through miio. 我的米家扫地机器人通过miio连接到我的手机。 | Synonym: Xiaomi IoT platform, Mi Home platform | Antonym: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
"Mi" is short for Xiaomi, the Chinese electronics company that developed the platform. "io" stands for "Internet of Things".  "miio" therefore represents Xiaomi's Internet of Things platform.
