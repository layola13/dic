ingratiates:/ɪnˈɡreɪʃieɪts/
verb
使…讨好；使…迎合
to behave in a way intended to gain someone's favor; to bring oneself into someone's favor
He ingratiated himself with his boss by offering to do extra work. 他主动加班来讨好老板。
近义词: charm, flatter, win over, curry favor with
反义词: alienate, offend, repulse
词性:v.


ingratiating:/ɪnˈɡreɪʃieɪtɪŋ/
adjective
讨好的；迎合的
intended to gain someone's favor
She had an ingratiating smile. 她满脸堆着奉承的笑容。
近义词: charming, flattering, pleasing
反义词: repellent, off-putting
词性:adj.
