noncosmopolitans:/ˌnɒnkɒzməˈpɒlɪtənz/ | n. | 非世界主义者；非世界性的人；地方主义者 | People who are not cosmopolitan; those who are not interested in or familiar with other cultures or countries; those who hold local or regional rather than global perspectives. |  The noncosmopolitans in the village were suspicious of the foreign visitors. 村里的非世界主义者对外国游客抱有怀疑态度。

The candidate's noncosmopolitan views alienated many voters in the diverse city. 这位候选人狭隘的观点疏远了这座多元化城市里的许多选民。 | 近义词: parochialists, localists, insular, provincial | 反义词: cosmopolitans, internationalists, globalists | 词性: 名词


词源:

non- (prefix):  meaning "not" or "opposite of"
cosmopolitan (adjective/noun): from Greek "kosmopolitēs" meaning "citizen of the world"
