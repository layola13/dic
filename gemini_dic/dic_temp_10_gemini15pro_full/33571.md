cilia: /ˈsɪliə/ (plural) /ˈsɪliəm/ (singular)| n. |纤毛|Minute hairlike organelles, identical in structure to flagella, that line the surfaces of certain cells and beat in rhythmic waves, providing locomotion to ciliate protozoans and moving liquids along internal epithelial tissues in higher animals.|The cilia in our respiratory system help to trap and remove dust and other foreign particles.| 近义词: flagella (in some contexts)| 反义词: None|词性: Noun
