madwort:/ˈmædwɜːrt/| n. |疯草 (欧洲产的一种开黄花的草本植物，曾被认为可以治疗狂犬病和精神错乱)|A European plant with yellow flowers, formerly believed to cure rabies and mental illness.|The herbalist recommended madwort as a treatment, but its efficacy is unproven.|近义词:ragwort, groundsel (因植物种类相似)|反义词: N/A (植物名称通常没有直接的反义词)|词性:noun

Etymology->
From Middle English *madwort*, from Old English *madwyrt*, from *mad* (“mad, insane”) + *wyrt* (“plant, herb”).  So literally, "mad plant".  The belief that it could cure madness led to its name.
