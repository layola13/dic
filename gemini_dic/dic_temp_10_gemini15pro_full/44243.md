crocose: /krɒkoʊs/ | n. |番红花，秋水仙|A plant with colorful, cup-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring or fall. It is a member of the iris family and is native to the Mediterranean region. |例句：The crocuses are blooming in the garden.  花园里的番红花正在盛开。

|例句：She planted crocuses in the fall for a spring display. 她秋天种了番红花，以便春天观赏。|近义词：colchicum (sometimes called autumn crocus, though not a true crocus), spring flower |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
