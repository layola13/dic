notifier:/ˈnəʊtɪfaɪər/ (UK) /ˈnoʊtɪfaɪər/ (US)| n. |通知者；通知器|Someone or something that notifies.  A person or device that sends out notifications.|The system sends an email to the notifier when a change is made. 当发生更改时，系统会向通知者发送电子邮件。|synonyms: informant, advisor, announcer|antonyms:  recipient, receiver|noun
