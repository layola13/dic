词源：insect + 's (possessive suffix)
insect: From Latin *insectum, a calque of Greek ἔντομον (éntomon, “cut into sections”), neuter of ἔντομος (éntomos), from ἐν (en, “in”) + τέμνω (témnō, “I cut”).
's:  From Middle English -es, -is, from Old English -es.
