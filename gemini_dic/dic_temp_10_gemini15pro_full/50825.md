descriptive:/dɪˈskrɪptɪv/| adj.|描述性的，描写的|Serving or tending to describe; containing or consisting of descriptions.  Relating to, or characterized by description. |The novel contained some wonderfully descriptive passages about the countryside. 这部小说中有一些关于乡村的精彩描写。|近义词：explanatory, illustrative, detailed, vivid, graphic|反义词：vague, unclear, ambiguous|词性: adjective
