outdodging:/ˌaʊtˈdɒdʒɪŋ/
verb
胜过；比...更机智地躲避
surpassing in dodging; evading more cleverly than
The spy was skilled at outdodging his pursuers.  这个间谍擅长躲避追捕者。
近义词: outmaneuvering, outwitting, evading, eluding
反义词: being caught, being outwitted
词性:v.
