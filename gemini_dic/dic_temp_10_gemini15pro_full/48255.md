decoratively:/ˈdɛkərətɪvli/ | adv. |装饰地，装潢地 |In a way that makes something look more attractive. |She had decoratively arranged flowers on the table. 她把花装饰性地摆放在桌子上。|例句：The cake was decoratively iced with intricate patterns. 蛋糕用复杂的图案装饰性地覆上了糖霜。|近义词：ornately, elaborately, artistically |反义词：plainly, simply, austerely |词性:副词

词源: From the adjective "decorative" + "-ly".  "Decorative" comes from the Late Latin "decorativus," from "decorare" (to adorn), related to "decus" (ornament, grace).
