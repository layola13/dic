adheres:/ədˈhɪrz/| v.|粘附，坚持，遵守|To stick firmly to a surface or substance; to remain attached; to be devoted to a cause, belief, or rule.|The sticker adheres to the wall.  这贴纸粘在墙上。

The glue adheres the two pieces of wood together. 这胶水把两块木头粘在一起。|近义词：sticks, clings, abides by, complies with|反义词:detaches, separates, violates|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "adhaerere," from "ad-" (to) + "haerere" (to stick).

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The tape adheres firmly to the surface. = 胶带牢牢地粘在表面上。
He adheres to a strict diet. = 他坚持严格的饮食。

一般过去时:
The mud adhered to my shoes. = 泥巴粘在我的鞋子上。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:

I wish she would adhere to the plan. = 我希望她能遵守计划。
