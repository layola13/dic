diagometer:/ˌdaɪəˈɡɒmɪtər/ | n. |电阻计，绝缘测试仪|An instrument for measuring electrical resistance, especially the insulation resistance of electrical cables and equipment. |例句：The diagometer showed a significant drop in resistance, indicating a fault in the cable. 电阻计显示电阻显著下降，表明电缆存在故障。

例句：The electrician used a diagometer to test the integrity of the wiring. 电工使用电阻计来测试线路的完整性。


|近义词：ohmmeter, resistance meter, insulation tester|反义词: N/A | noun
