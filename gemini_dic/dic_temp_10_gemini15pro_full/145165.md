overassured:/ˌoʊvərəˈʃʊrd/ | adj. | 过于自信的，自以为是的 | Excessively confident; presumptuous. Having too much assurance or confidence, often to the point of arrogance. | He was so overassured that he didn't bother to prepare for the interview and, unsurprisingly, he didn't get the job. 他过于自信，面试前根本懒得准备，不出所料，他没有得到这份工作。 |  She walked into the room with an overassured air, as if she already owned the place. 她带着一种过于自信的神态走进房间，仿佛她已经是这里的主人了。 | 近义词: cocky, arrogant, presumptuous, overconfident | 反义词: insecure, diffident, humble, modest | 词性: adjective

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "assured" (adjective meaning "confident").  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning of "assured," indicating an excessive or inappropriate level of confidence.
