counterinstitution:/ˈkaʊntərˌɪnstɪˈtjuːʃən/ |n.| 对抗机构，反建制机构 |An institution that is created or developed in opposition to an existing institution, often with the aim of challenging or undermining it.  It provides alternative services or promotes different values from the established institution. |  Example 1: The free clinic served as a counterinstitution to the expensive private hospitals in the area. 这个免费诊所充当了该地区昂贵私立医院的对抗机构。

Example 2:  The online educational platform emerged as a counterinstitution to traditional universities. 这个在线教育平台作为对抗传统大学的机构而出现。 |Synonyms: alternative institution, opposing institution, rival organization | Antonyms: established institution, mainstream institution | noun
