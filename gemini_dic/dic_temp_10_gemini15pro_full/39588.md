consecrater:/ˈkɒnsɪkreɪtə(r)/| n.|祝圣者，授职者|A person who consecrates something or someone, typically for religious purposes.  Someone who performs the act of consecrating, dedicating something to a sacred use.|The bishop was the consecrater of the new church. 主教是这座新教堂的祝圣者。|近义词：ordainer, dedicator|反义词：desecrater|noun

consecrate:/ˈkɒnsɪkreɪt/| vt.|祝圣，奉献|To make or declare something, typically a church or a bishop, sacred; to dedicate formally to a religious or divine purpose.|The church was consecrated in 1875. 这座教堂于1875年祝圣。|近义词：hallow, sanctify, bless, dedicate|反义词：desecrate, profane|verb
