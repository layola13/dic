laurer:/ˈlɔːrər/| n.|劳雷尔码, 劳雷尔编码|A type of barcode symbology used primarily in the United States for marking shipping containers. It is often used to encode the Universal Product Code (UPC) on corrugated cardboard boxes.|The warehouse uses a laurer scanner to track inventory.|近义词:barcode, UPC code|反义词:None|词性:noun
