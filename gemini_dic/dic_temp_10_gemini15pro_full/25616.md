bumpkinish:/ˈbʌmpkɪnɪʃ/| adj.|土气的，粗俗的，乡巴佬的|Having the characteristics of a bumpkin; unsophisticated, awkward, and often unintelligent.  Relating to or characteristic of a bumpkin; rustic and unsophisticated.|He had a bumpkinish way of speaking that made him seem out of place in the city. 他说话的方式很土气，让他在城市里显得格格不入。|Synonyms: rustic, unsophisticated, awkward, clumsy, uncouth, boorish, hick, yokel |Antonyms: sophisticated, urbane, polished, refined, cultured, elegant|adjective

Etymology->
From bumpkin + -ish.  Bumpkin's etymology is uncertain, possibly from Middle Dutch *bommekijn "little barrel, small cask" or Low German *boomken "small tree, shrub." The original sense may have been mockingly referring to someone as a "little barrel" or "block of wood," implying clumsiness or lack of intelligence.
