hyperbole:/haɪˈpɜːrbəli/ (US) /haɪˈpɜːbəli/ (UK)| n. |夸张；夸张法|A figure of speech that uses extreme exaggeration to create emphasis or humor. It is not meant to be taken literally.|例句：He said he had a million things to do, which was a hyperbole. 他说他有一百万件事要做，这是一种夸张的说法。

例句：She told me her shoes were killing her, a common hyperbole for uncomfortable footwear. 她告诉我她的鞋子要把她折磨死了，这是形容鞋子不舒服的一种常见夸张说法。|近义词：exaggeration, overstatement|反义词:understatement, litotes|词性:noun
