incommode:/ˌɪnkəˈmoʊd/| v. |打扰，不便，使不舒服|To cause inconvenience or discomfort to someone or something.|例句：The construction noise outside incommoded me while I was trying to work. 外面的施工噪音打扰了我工作。|例句：The lack of air conditioning incommoded the guests during the summer heat. 夏天没有空调给客人们带来了不便。|近义词：disturb, bother, trouble, inconvenience|反义词:accommodate, assist, help|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *incommodāre, from Latin in- (“not”) + commodus (“convenient, suitable”).
