dialyze:/ˈdaɪəˌlaɪz/ | v. | 透析，用透析法净化 | To purify (a colloid) by dialysis, separating crystalloids from colloids by this method. | The patient's blood was dialyzed to remove toxins. 病人的血液被透析以去除毒素。 | synonyms: purify, cleanse | antonyms: contaminate, pollute | verb


dialyzed:/ˈdaɪəˌlaɪzd/ | adj. | 透析过的 | Having been subjected to dialysis. | The dialyzed solution was then used for further experiments. 透析后的溶液随后用于进一步的实验。 | synonyms: purified, cleansed | antonyms: contaminated, polluted | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From dia- (through) + -lyze (to loosen, dissolve), from Greek lyein (to loosen).
