hereto:/ˌhɪəˈtuː/| adv.|迄今，至今，在此之前|Up to this time; until now.|The documents referred to hereto are attached.  (在此提及的文件已附上。)|近义词：hitherto, so far, thus far|反义词:henceforth, hereafter|副词

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
The agreement hereto applies to all parties. (本协议适用于所有各方。)
The provisions hereto are binding. (本协议的条款具有约束力。)

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
We are referring to the documents attached hereto. (我们指的是附于此处的文件。)
They are discussing the clauses hereto. (他们正在讨论此处的条款。)


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
The parties have agreed to the terms hereto. (各方已同意本协议的条款。)
We have attached the exhibits hereto. (我们已将附件附于此处。)

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
We have been referring to the documents hereto for some time. (我们一直参考此处的文件已有一段时间了。)
They have been discussing the implications hereto. (他们一直在讨论此处的含义。)

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
The contract hereto referred to all previous agreements. (该合同提及了所有先前的协议。)
The parties hereto agreed on the terms. (本协议的各方就条款达成了一致。)

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
They were reviewing the documents attached hereto. (他们正在审查附于此处的文件。)
We were discussing the clauses hereto before the meeting. (我们在会议前一直在讨论此处的条款。)

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
The parties had agreed to the terms hereto before the signing. (签约前各方已同意本协议的条款。)
We had attached the necessary documents hereto. (我们已经附上了必要的文档。)

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
They had been discussing the implications hereto for several weeks. (他们已经讨论了这里的影响好几个星期了。)
We had been reviewing the documents hereto prior to the decision. (在做决定之前，我们一直在审查此处的文件。)

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
The agreement hereto will apply to all future transactions. (本协议将适用于所有未来的交易。)
The parties hereto will sign the contract tomorrow. (本协议的各方将于明天签署合同。)

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
We will be referring to the documents hereto during the meeting. (我们将在会议期间参考此处的文件。)
They will be discussing the clauses hereto next week. (他们下周将讨论此处的条款。)

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next month, we will have reviewed all the documents hereto. (到下个月，我们将审查完所有此处的文件。)
The parties will have agreed to the terms hereto by the deadline. (各方将在截止日期前同意本协议的条款。)

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next year, they will have been working on the project hereto for five years. (到明年，他们将已经为这个项目工作五年了。)
We will have been referring to the documents hereto for several months by the time the decision is made. (到做出决定的时候，我们将已经参考此处的文件好几个月了。)


PHRASE->
the parties hereto (本协议的双方)
the documents attached hereto (附于此处的文件)
the terms and conditions hereto (本协议的条款和条件)
the provisions hereto (本协议的规定)
referring hereto (指的是这里)

注意事项:
hereto是一个比较正式的词语，常见于法律或商业文件中。  在日常口语中很少使用。
