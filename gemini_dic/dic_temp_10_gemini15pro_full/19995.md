biography:/baɪˈɒɡrəfi/ (British) /baɪˈɑːɡrəfi/ (American)| n. |传记，生平|An account of someone's life written by another person.|例句：I'm reading a biography of Abraham Lincoln. 我正在读一本亚伯拉罕·林肯的传记。|例句：She's writing a biography of her mother. 她正在写她母亲的传记。|近义词：life history, memoir, profile|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From the Greek words *bios* (life) and *graphein* (to write).
