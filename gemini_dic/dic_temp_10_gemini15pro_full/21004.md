blessedly:/ˈblesɪdli/ | adv. | 幸福地，幸运地；谢天谢地 | In a blessed manner; luckily, fortunately.  Expressing relief. | The rain finally stopped, blessedly. 雨终于停了，谢天谢地。|  Thankfully, fortunately, luckily, mercifully | cursedly, unfortunately, wretchedly | adverb

词源:
From "blessed" + "-ly." "Blessed" comes from Old English "blēdsian" (to consecrate with blood, bless), from "blōd" (blood).  The meaning shifted from the literal use of blood in religious rituals to a more general sense of divine favor and happiness.
