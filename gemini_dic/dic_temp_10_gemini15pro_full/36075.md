coehorn:/ˈkəʊhɔːrn/| n.|科霍恩迫击炮; 小型迫击炮|A small, light mortar, named after its inventor, Baron Menno van Coehoorn (1641-1704), a Dutch military engineer.  This type of mortar was particularly useful in siege warfare.|The army used coehorns to bombard the enemy fortifications.|Synonyms: mortar, bombard|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific weapon.  One could consider "shield" or "defense" in a broad sense.)|noun
