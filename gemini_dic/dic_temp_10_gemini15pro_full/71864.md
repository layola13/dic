fleshings:/ˈflɛʃɪŋz/
n.
肉色紧身衣，肉色戏服
Tight-fitting garments, typically worn by dancers or actors, that are designed to resemble the appearance of bare skin.
The dancers wore fleshings for the contemporary piece. 舞者们穿着肉色紧身衣表演现代舞。
近义词:tights, leotards
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "costume" in a broader sense)
词性:noun


fleshings:/ˈflɛʃɪŋz/
n.
(马的)皮革边角料
Scraps of leather, especially those trimmed from hides in the process of tanning.
The tannery sold the fleshings to be used in glue production. 制革厂把皮革边角料卖掉用来生产胶水。
近义词:scraps, offcuts, trimmings
反义词:(No direct antonym)
词性:noun
