nonmigration:/ˌnɒnmaɪˈɡreɪʃən/ |n.| 不迁徙，不移居 |Absence of migration; lack of movement from one place to another.| The nonmigration of the birds this year puzzled the scientists. 今年鸟类的不迁徙令科学家们感到困惑。| 近义词:sedentariness, immobility | 反义词: migration, emigration, immigration | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non "not"
migration: from Late Latin migrationem (nominative migratio) "a moving from one place to another," noun of action from past-participle stem of migrare "to move from one place to another," perhaps from PIE root *mei- (1) "to change, go, move."
