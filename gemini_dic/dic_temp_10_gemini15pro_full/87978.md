high-hatting:/ˈhaɪˌhætɪŋ/| n.|摆架子；傲慢；轻蔑|The act of behaving in a condescending or supercilious manner, often suggesting a sense of superiority and disdain.  It can involve snubbing or ignoring others, acting aloof, or making condescending remarks.|He lost some friends due to his constant high-hatting. 他因为总是摆架子而失去了一些朋友。|Synonyms: snobbishness, arrogance, disdain, haughtiness, superciliousness|Antonyms: humility, modesty, respectfulness|noun


high-hat:/ˈhaɪˌhæt/| v.|摆架子；轻蔑地对待|To treat someone with disdain or condescension, often by ignoring or snubbing them.|She high-hatted her former colleagues after her promotion.  她升职后就轻蔑地对待以前的同事了。|Synonyms: snub, condescend, disdain, patronize|Antonyms: respect, admire, appreciate|verb
