assertion:/əˈsɜːʃən/ | n. |断言，声明，主张| a confident and forceful statement of fact or belief |  He made the assertion that he was innocent. 他断言自己是无辜的。| synonyms: declaration, affirmation, claim, contention | antonyms: denial, retraction, disavowal | noun

assertion:/əˈsɜːʃən/ | n. |维护，坚持| the act of stating something confidently and forcefully |  The assertion of his rights led to a conflict. 他对自身权利的维护导致了一场冲突。| synonyms: upholding, maintenance, defense | antonyms: surrender, relinquishment, abandonment | noun
