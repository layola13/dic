bono:/ˈboʊnoʊ/ (US) /ˈbəʊnəʊ/ (UK)| n.| 奖金；额外津贴；证券溢价|A bonus is something good that is given to you in addition to what you expect or what you have earned. |Example 1:  The company offered a Christmas bonus to all employees. 公司给所有员工发了圣诞奖金。

Example 2:  She received a bonus for completing the project ahead of schedule. 她因提前完成项目而获得了奖金。|Synonym:  premium, perk, incentive, reward|Antonym:  penalty, deduction|noun
