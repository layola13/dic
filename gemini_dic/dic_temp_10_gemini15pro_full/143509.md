outgnaws:/ˌaʊtˈnɔːz/
verb
啮穿；咬穿；超过
to gnaw something from the outside or to surpass in gnawing.  It can also be used figuratively to mean to erode or destroy something gradually.
Jealousy outgnawed his heart.  嫉妒蚕食着他的心。
gnaw, erode, corrode, consume
build, construct, create
词性: 动词
