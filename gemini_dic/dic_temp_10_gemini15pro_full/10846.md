arabesque:/ˌærəˈbɛsk/ | n. |蔓状花纹，阿拉伯式花纹，（芭蕾舞中）单腿直立，另一条腿向后弯曲的舞姿 |An ornamental design consisting of intertwined flowing lines, typically based on floral or geometric patterns.  Also, a position in ballet in which one leg is extended horizontally backward and the arms are held in various harmonious positions.| The ceiling was decorated with intricate arabesques.  天花板上装饰着复杂的蔓状花纹。 | The ballerina held a graceful arabesque for several seconds. 芭蕾舞演员优雅地保持了几秒钟的阿拉伯式舞姿。 | synonyms: flourish, ornamentation, embellishment, adornment, decoration, design (for visual arabesques); pose, attitude, posture (for ballet arabesques) | antonyms: plainness, simplicity (for visual arabesques) | noun
