offings:/ˈɔːfɪŋz/
noun
产品；商品；祭品；供品
products, goods, or services that are offered for sale; something offered as a religious sacrifice or gift
The company's latest offerings include a new range of smartphones. 这家公司最新的产品包括一系列新的智能手机。
They made an offering of fruits to the gods.  他们向神灵供奉水果。
synonyms: products, goods, merchandise, wares, services, donations, gifts, sacrifices, oblations
antonyms: demands, requests, needs

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English offringe, from Old English offrung (“offering, sacrifice”), from offeren (“to offer”).
