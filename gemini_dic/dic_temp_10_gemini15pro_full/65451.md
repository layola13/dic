eureka:/juˈrɪːkə/ /jʊˈriːkə/ | int. | 我发现了！；找到了！ | An exclamation of triumph upon a discovery.  Derived from the Greek heureka, meaning "I have found (it)."  Archimedes supposedly shouted "Eureka!" when he discovered the principle of buoyancy. |  Eureka! I've finally found the solution to the problem!  我发现了！我终于找到了问题的解决方案！| Synonym:  I found it!,  Aha!, Discovery! | Antonym: Lost,  Confused | interjection
