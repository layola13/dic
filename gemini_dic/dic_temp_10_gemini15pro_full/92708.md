icelantic:/ˌaɪsəlˈæntɪk/ | adj. n. |冰岛的；Icelantic (滑雪板品牌) | Relating to Iceland; a brand of skis known for their vibrant graphics and performance. |  He bought a pair of Icelantic skis for his trip to the Alps. 他买了一副Icelantic滑雪板去阿尔卑斯山旅行。| The Icelantic landscape is breathtaking. 冰岛的风景令人叹为观止。 | 近义词:Icelandic (for the adjective) | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology:  A portmanteau of "Ice" (referring to Iceland) and "Atlantic" (referring to the Atlantic Ocean, which surrounds Iceland).  The brand name Icelantic was coined to evoke the spirit of Icelandic adventure and exploration.
