demeaned:/dɪˈmiːnd/

demeaned:/dɪˈmiːnd/
|adj.| 被贬低的|Having been lowered in status, dignity, or self-respect. |She felt demeaned by his comments. 她觉得他的评论贬低了她。|humiliated, degraded|exalted, honored|adjective
