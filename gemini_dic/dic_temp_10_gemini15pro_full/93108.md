if-you-please:/ɪf juː pliːz/ | adv. | 如果你愿意；请 | A polite addition to a request.  Used to make a request or command sound more polite. | Pass the salt, if you please. 请把盐递给我。| 近义词: please, kindly, if you would, if you will | 反义词: (No direct antonym, implies politeness; an antonym would imply rudeness) | 词性: 副词

USAGE->

Bring me a glass of water, if you please. = 请给我一杯水。
Hand me that book, if you please. =  请把那本书递给我。
