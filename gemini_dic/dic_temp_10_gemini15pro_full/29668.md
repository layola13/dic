cathartic:/kəˈθɑːrtɪk/
adjective
宣泄的; 净化心灵的
Providing psychological relief through the open expression of strong emotions; causing catharsis.
After a good cry, I felt cathartic. 痛哭一场后，我感觉心情舒畅。
synonyms: cleansing, purifying, purging, relieving
antonyms: repressive, suppressive
词性: 形容词

cathartic:/kəˈθɑːrtɪk/
noun
宣泄物; 净化心灵的事物
Something that provides psychological relief through the open expression of strong emotions.
Journaling can be a cathartic for many people. 写日记对很多人来说是一种情感宣泄的方式。
synonyms: release, outlet, vent
antonyms: suppression, repression
词性: 名词
