calico:/ˈkælɪkoʊ/ (US), /ˈkælɪkəʊ/ (UK)| n. adj.|印花棉布；花斑猫|A type of cotton cloth printed with a bright-colored pattern, typically with flowers. Also used to describe a cat with a tri-colored coat (typically white, black, and orange).|例句：She wore a calico dress to the picnic. 她穿着印花棉布连衣裙去野餐了。|近义词：chintz, gingham|反义词:|noun, adjective

calicos:/ˈkælɪkoʊz/ (US), /ˈkælɪkəʊz/ (UK)| n.|印花棉布（复数）；花斑猫（复数）|Plural form of "calico."  Refers to multiple pieces of calico fabric or multiple calico cats.|例句：The market stall was overflowing with brightly colored calicos.  市场摊位上摆满了色彩鲜艳的印花棉布。|近义词：chintzes, ginghams|反义词:|noun
