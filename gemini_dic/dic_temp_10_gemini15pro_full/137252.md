nonsustenance:/ˌnɒnˈsʌstənəns/
n.
缺乏营养; 无营养的食物; 无益的东西
Lack of nourishment; food that does not nourish; something that does not provide benefit or support.
The diet of primarily processed foods offered little nonsustenance and contributed to his declining health.  这种主要由加工食品组成的饮食几乎没有营养，导致他的健康状况下降。
His constant complaining offered no nonsustenance to the team’s morale. 他不断的抱怨对团队士气没有任何益处。
近义词:innutrition, starvation, barrenness, emptiness
反义词:nourishment, sustenance, nutriment
词性:noun

词源:non- (prefix meaning "not") + sustenance (from Old French sustenance, from sustenir "to sustain," from Latin sustinere "to hold up, support")
