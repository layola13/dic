mandrill:/ˈmænˌdrɪl/ (US), /ˈmæn.drɪl/ (UK)| n.|山魈|A large, brightly colored monkey (Mandrillus sphinx) of western Africa, having a long muzzle with prominent ridges and a bright red and blue face.|例句：The mandrill is known for its vibrant facial colors. 山魈以其鲜艳的面部颜色而闻名。

例句：We saw a mandrill at the zoo. 我们在动物园看到了一只山魈。

近义词：baboon, primate|反义词: None (No direct opposite for an animal species)|词性:noun


Etymology:
From French *mandrille*, from earlier *mantille*, perhaps from a native African language.
