graspingnesses:/ˈɡræspɪŋnɪsɪz/ | n. | 贪婪，贪得无厌 |  The state of being excessively desirous of acquiring or possessing, especially wealth or material possessions.  It refers to the quality of being grasping or avaricious.  |  His graspingnesses led him to exploit his workers. 他的贪婪导致他剥削工人。 |Synonyms: avarice, greed, rapacity, covetousness | Antonyms: generosity, altruism, charity | Noun
