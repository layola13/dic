overconsume:/ˌoʊvər kənˈsuːm/ /ˌəʊvə kənˈsjuːm/| v. |过度消费；过量消耗|To consume something in excess; to use up too much of something.|例句：Many Americans overconsume sugary drinks. 许多美国人过度饮用含糖饮料。|近义词：overspend, overuse, overindulge|反义词:underconsume, conserve|词性:动词

词源:
over- (prefix) + consume (verb). "Over-" signifies "in excess" or "too much". "Consume" comes from the Latin "consumere" meaning "to take up completely, devour, destroy".
