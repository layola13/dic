discourager:/dɪˈskʌrɪdʒər/
n.|阻止者，劝退者|Someone or something that discourages. |The high cost of tuition can be a discourager for prospective students.  高昂的学费可能会让未来的学生望而却步。|Synonym: deterrent, obstacle, impediment|Antonym: encourager, motivator|noun
discourage:/dɪˈskʌrɪdʒ/
vt.|阻止，劝退，使气馁|To cause (someone) to lose confidence or enthusiasm; to deter. |The rain discouraged us from going on a picnic.  下雨打消了我们去野餐的念头。|Synonym: deter, dissuade, hinder|Antonym: encourage, motivate, inspire|verb
