mazm:/ˈmæzəm/ | n. | 迷宫，曲径，错综复杂的事物 | A complex network of paths or passages; a labyrinth. |  He found himself lost in a mazm of bureaucratic regulations. 他发现自己迷失在官僚规章的迷宫中。

|  The old city's streets were a mazm of narrow alleys and winding passages.  这座老城的街道是由狭窄的小巷和蜿蜒的通道组成的迷宫。 | 近义词: labyrinth, maze, network, web | 反义词: simplicity, clarity, straightforwardness | 词性: 名词


词源:  From Arabic *mazam*, "place of entering, place of going in," verbal noun of *mazama*, "to enter."
