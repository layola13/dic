financiers:/fɪˈnænsiərz/
| n. |金融家，财务人员|A person who works in finance, especially one who deals with large sums of money or investments.  |The financiers were impressed by the company's strong performance.|Synonyms: investors, bankers, brokers, capitalists|Antonyms: debtors, borrowers|Noun

financiers:/fɪˈnænsiərz/
| n. |金主，出资人|A person who provides financial support for a project or enterprise. |The film was funded by a group of wealthy financiers.|Synonyms: backers, patrons, sponsors|Antonyms: recipients, beneficiaries|Noun
