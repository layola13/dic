glissette:/ɡlɪˈset/| n.|滑行; 滑行轨迹; 滑翔; |A curve traced by a point, or the envelope of a curve, as it moves according to a given rule.|The aircraft made a smooth glissette onto the runway. 飞机平稳地滑行到跑道上。|近义词：glide, slide, slip|反义词:ascent, climb|noun
