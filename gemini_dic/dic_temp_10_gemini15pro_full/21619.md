blunts:/blʌnts/| n.|钝器; (俚语)大麻烟卷|These scissors are blunts; they won't cut anything.  这些剪刀是钝的，什么也剪不了。|He offered me one of his blunts.  他给了我一根他的大麻烟。|近义词：(for marijuana cigarette) joint, spliff|反义词: (for object) sharp object |词性:名词

词源:
From Old English *blunt, of uncertain origin, perhaps from or related to Old Norse blundra (“to doze, slumber”).  More at blunder.  Possibly related to blend.
