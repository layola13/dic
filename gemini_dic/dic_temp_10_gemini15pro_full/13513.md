attainable:/əˈteɪnəbl/| adj.|可达到的，可获得的|able to be attained; achievable.  Something that is attainable is possible to achieve or reach.|Her goal of running a marathon seemed attainable with enough training. 她跑马拉松的目标似乎通过足够的训练是可以实现的.|近义词：achievable, reachable, accessible, obtainable|反义词：unattainable, impossible, unreachable|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from attain + -able.
