cryptocurrencies:/ˌkrɪptoʊˈkɜːrənsiz/| n. |加密货币|Digital or virtual currencies that use cryptography for security and operate independently of a central bank.|Bitcoin and Ethereum are popular cryptocurrencies. 比特币和以太坊是流行的加密货币。|Synonyms: digital currencies, virtual currencies, crypto, coins, tokens|Antonyms: fiat currency, traditional currency|Noun
