dilatedly:/daɪˈleɪtɪdli/
| adv. |膨胀地；扩张地|In a dilated manner; with dilation.  Characterized by expansion or widening.|Her pupils were dilatedly wide with fear. 她的瞳孔因恐惧而极度放大。|Synonyms: expansively, widely, broadly|Antonyms: narrowly, contractedly|词性:副词

dilated:/daɪˈleɪtɪd/
| adj. |膨胀的；扩张的|Made wider or larger; expanded. |Her eyes were dilated with fear. 她的眼睛因恐惧而放大。|Synonyms: expanded, widened, enlarged|Antonyms: contracted, narrowed, constricted|词性:形容词

dilate:/daɪˈleɪt/
| v. |（使）膨胀；（使）扩张|To make or become wider or larger; expand.|The drug dilates the blood vessels. 这种药能扩张血管。|Synonyms: expand, widen, enlarge|Antonyms: contract, narrow, constrict|词性:动词

词源: From Latin *dīlātāre*, frequentative of *dīlātare*, from *dī-*, intensive prefix + *lātus* "wide, broad."
