brittlenesses:/ˈbrɪtlnəsɪz/
n.
脆性；脆弱性；易碎性
The quality of being brittle; easily broken or cracked.
The brittlenesses of the old bones made them vulnerable to fractures.  老骨头的脆性使它们容易骨折。
fragility, frangibility, delicacy, weakness
toughness, strength, resilience, flexibility
noun


brittle:/ˈbrɪtl/
adj.
易碎的；脆弱的；易怒的
Hard but liable to break or shatter easily.
The brittle glass shattered into a thousand pieces. 脆玻璃碎成一千块。
fragile, frail, delicate, frangible
tough, strong, resilient, flexible
adjective
