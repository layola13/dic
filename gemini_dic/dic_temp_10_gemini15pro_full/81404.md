grassiest:/ˈɡræsɪɪst/| adj.|最多草的，草最茂盛的|Covered with grass to the greatest extent; having the most grass. |The grassiest field in the valley was chosen for the picnic. 山谷里草最多的田地被选为野餐地点。|The park boasts the grassiest lawns in the city. 这个公园拥有全市草最茂盛的草坪。|近义词：lush, verdant, leafy|反义词：barren, arid, desolate|词性:形容词
词源: From grass + -y + -est.  "Grass" comes from Old English "græs,"  meaning "grass, herb, plant."  The suffix "-y" forms adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "full of," and "-est" is the superlative suffix.
