carnivorous: /kɑːrˈnɪvərəs/ | adj. | 肉食性的；食肉的 | Feeding on other animals. | Lions are carnivorous animals. 狮子是肉食性动物。|  The Venus flytrap is a carnivorous plant.捕蝇草是一种肉食性植物。 | synonyms: predatory, meat-eating | antonyms: herbivorous, vegetarian | adjective


Etymology:

From Late Latin *carnivorus*, from Latin carn- (stem of caro “flesh”) + -vorus*-eater*, from vorare “to devour.”
