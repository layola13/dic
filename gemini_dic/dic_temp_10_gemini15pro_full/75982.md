funnelling:/ˈfʌnəlɪŋ/ | v. |  (使)成漏斗状; 汇集;  | To move or cause to move through a narrow opening or into a restricted space, often converging at the end. |  They are funnelling all their resources into the new project. 他们正把所有资源都投入到这个新项目中。| channeling, directing, guiding | dispersing, spreading | verb
