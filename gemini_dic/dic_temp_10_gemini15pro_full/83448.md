habited:/ˈhæbɪtɪd/
adj.
有人居住的；有动物栖息的
Inhabited means lived in or occupied by people or animals.
The island is inhabited by a small community of fishermen.  这个岛屿上居住着一小群渔民。
The dense forest is inhabited by various species of birds. 这片茂密的森林里栖息着各种鸟类。
近义词:populated, occupied, settled
反义词:uninhabited, deserted, vacant
词性:形容词


Etymology:
From Middle English *habited*, from Old French *habite*, past participle of *habiter* (“to inhabit”), from Latin *habitāre* (“to dwell, inhabit”), frequentative of *habēre* (“to have, hold”).
