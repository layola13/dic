beakier:/ˈbiːkiər/| adj.|更像喙的，喙更大的|Having a more prominent or larger beak than something else of the same kind.  This word is the comparative form of "beaky".|The robin seemed beakier than the other birds at the feeder. 这只知更鸟似乎比喂食器上的其他鸟的喙更大。|近义词：more beaky, more pointed, sharper|反义词:less beaky, rounder, blunter|词性:形容词
词源:From "beak" + "-y" (forming adjectives) + "-er" (comparative suffix). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *bekkaz "beak".
