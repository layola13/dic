needfulnesses:/ˈniːdfʊlnəsɪz/
n.
必需品；必要性；贫困
The state or quality of being needful; necessity. The condition of being in need; poverty.
The needfulnesses of life, such as food and shelter, should be provided for everyone.  生活必需品，例如食物和住所，应该提供给每个人。
Near Synonyms: necessities, essentials, requirements, exigencies, needs, poverty, indigence, want
Antonyms:  abundance, affluence, luxury, superfluity, wealth
词性: 名词 (Plural Noun)
