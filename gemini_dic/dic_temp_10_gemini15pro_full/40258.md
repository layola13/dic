contingencies:/kənˈtɪndʒənsiz/| n.| 意外事件，偶发事件，可能性| a future event or circumstance that is possible but cannot be predicted with certainty|例句：We must be prepared for all possible contingencies. 我们必须为所有可能发生的意外事件做好准备。|近义词：possibilities, eventualities, uncertainties|反义词:certainties|noun

contingency:/kənˈtɪndʒənsi/| n.| 意外事件，偶发事件，可能性| a future event or circumstance that is possible but cannot be predicted with certainty|例句：The success of the project is contingent upon the weather.  项目的成功取决于天气。|近义词：possibility, eventuality, uncertainty|反义词:certainty|noun
