micro-machining:/ˈmaɪkroʊ məˈʃiːnɪŋ/| n. |微加工|The process of fabricating miniature structures or devices using techniques such as etching, laser ablation, and electroplating.|Micro-machining is crucial for creating microfluidic devices. 微加工对于制造微流体装置至关重要。|Synonyms: micromanufacturing, microfabrication|Antonyms: macro-machining|noun
micro-machining:/ˈmaɪkroʊ məˈʃiːnɪŋ/| adj. |微加工的|Relating to or involving micro-machining.|Micro-machining techniques are used in the production of MEMS devices. 微加工技术用于MEMS器件的生产。|Synonyms: micromanufacturing, microfabrication|Antonyms: macro-machining|adjective
