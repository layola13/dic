crankbird:/ˈkræŋkbɜːrd/
noun
古怪的人；脾气坏的人；爱抱怨的人
A crankbird is a grumpy, eccentric, or complaining person.
He's a bit of a crankbird, always complaining about the weather. 他有点古怪，总是抱怨天气。
synonyms: grouch, curmudgeon, complainer
antonyms: optimist, cheerful person
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
"crank" (meaning an eccentric person) + "bird" (informal term for a person)
