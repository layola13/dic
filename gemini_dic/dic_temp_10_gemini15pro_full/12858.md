assonantal:/əˈsɑːnəntl/
adjective
谐音的，类韵的
Relating to or marked by assonance; containing assonance.  Characterized by the resemblance of sounds between syllables or words, especially the resemblance of vowel sounds.
The poet used assonantal sounds to create a sense of flow and rhythm in his verse. 这位诗人用谐音来营造他诗歌中流畅和韵律的感觉。
