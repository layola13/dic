boyism: /ˈbɔɪɪzəm/
n.
男孩气概，男孩行为，男孩的举止
Behavior or traits considered typical of boys, often in a negative or exaggerated way.
He exhibited classic boyism, including loud talking and roughhousing. 他表现出典型的男孩气概，包括大声说话和打闹。
The headmistress frowned upon the boyism prevalent in the school. 女校长对学校里盛行的男孩气概皱起了眉头。
synonyms: boyishness, tomboyishness, boisterousness
antonyms: girlishness, femininity
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From boy + -ism.  "Boy" comes from Middle English boi, probably of uncertain Germanic origin. "-ism" is a suffix forming nouns of action, state, condition, doctrine, etc.
