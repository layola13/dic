de-numbers:/diːˈnʌmbərz/| v. |去除编号，匿名化|To remove identifying numbers from something, typically to protect privacy or anonymity. |例句：The researchers de-numbered the patient data before analyzing it. 研究人员在分析病人数据之前先去除了编号。

例句：The survey responses were de-numbered to ensure confidentiality.  调查回复被匿名化以确保机密性。|近义词：anonymize, redact|反义词: identify, number|词性:动词
