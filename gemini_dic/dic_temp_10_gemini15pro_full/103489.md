jock's:/ˈdʒɒksɪz/ (US) /ˈdʒɒksɪz/ (UK)
noun
运动员的，健壮男子的（东西）
belonging to or associated with a jock (an athlete, typically one who is muscular or good-looking and popular)
That's a jock's jacket. 那是运动员的夹克。
近义词: athlete's, sportsman's
反义词: nerd's, geek's
词性: 名词所有格
