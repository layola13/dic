clitellum:/klaɪˈtɛləm/| n. |环带，生殖带|A thickened glandular and non-segmented section of the body wall in earthworms and leeches, that secretes a viscous sac in which the eggs are deposited.|例句：The clitellum of the earthworm is easily visible. 蚯蚓的环带很容易看到。

例句：The clitellum plays a crucial role in earthworm reproduction.  环带在蚯蚓的繁殖过程中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：cingulum|反义词: None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *clitellum* "pack-saddle," diminutive of *clitellae* "pack-saddle," related to *clinare* "to lean, bend."  The resemblance of the earthworm's clitellum to a saddle likely inspired the name.
