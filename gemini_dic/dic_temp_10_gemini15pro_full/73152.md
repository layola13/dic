foray:/ˈfɒreɪ/ (UK), /ˈfɔːreɪ/ (US)| n.  vi.|突袭，袭击；涉足，初次尝试|A sudden or brief attempt to become involved in a new activity or sphere.|The company made a foray into the computer market. 这家公司进军了计算机市场。|例句：
Her foray into politics was short-lived. 她短暂涉足政坛。
The troops made a foray into enemy territory. 部队突袭了敌方领土。

|近义词：raid, incursion, venture, expedition|反义词：retreat, withdrawal|词性：名词，动词
