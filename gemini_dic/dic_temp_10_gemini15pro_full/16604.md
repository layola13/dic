barometer:/bəˈrɒmɪtər/ (US) /bəˈrɑːmɪtər/ (UK)| n.|气压计；晴雨表；指标；标志|An instrument measuring atmospheric pressure, used especially in forecasting the weather and determining altitude.|The barometer is falling, which indicates that rain is coming. 气压计正在下降，这表明要下雨了。|Synonyms: indicator, gauge, measure, index|Antonyms: N/A|noun

barometric:/ˌbærəˈmɛtrɪk/ (US) /ˌbærəˈmɛtrɪk/ (UK)| adj.|气压的；晴雨表的|Relating to or measured by a barometer.|Barometric pressure is a good indicator of weather changes. 气压是天气变化的良好指标。|Synonyms: atmospheric|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
