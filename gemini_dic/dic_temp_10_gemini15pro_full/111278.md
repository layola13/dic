litterateur:/ˌlɪtəˈrəˈtɜːr/ /ˌlɪtəˈrəˈtʊr/
n.
文学家；文人；作家
A person who is interested in and knowledgeable about literature.
例句：He was a renowned litterateur, respected for his insightful critiques and elegant prose. 他是一位著名的文学家，以其深刻的评论和优雅的散文而备受尊敬。

例句：The litterateur frequented literary salons, engaging in lively discussions with fellow writers and critics. 这位文学家经常出入文学沙龙，与其他作家和评论家进行热烈的讨论。

近义词：writer, author, man of letters, intellectual, scholar
反义词：philistine, ignoramus
词性：noun
