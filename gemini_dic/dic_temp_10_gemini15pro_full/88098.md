hightail:/ˈhaɪteɪl/| v. |迅速离开，逃之夭夭|To leave somewhere quickly.|Example: When they saw the police coming, they hightailed it out of there. 当他们看到警察来时，他们迅速逃离了那里。|Example:  The cat hightailed it up a tree when the dog barked.  当狗吠叫时，猫迅速爬上了树。|近义词：flee, run away, escape, bolt, dash|反义词：stay, remain, linger|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the image of a horse or other animal raising its tail high when running away.  源自马或其他动物逃跑时高举尾巴的形象。
