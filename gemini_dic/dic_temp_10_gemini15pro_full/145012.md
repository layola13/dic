overadorn:/ˌoʊvərəˈdɔːrn/
verb
过度装饰
To adorn excessively; to decorate to an excessive degree.
Be careful not to overadorn the cake, or it will look messy.  小心不要过度装饰蛋糕，否则会看起来很乱。
She overadorned her apartment with knick-knacks and souvenirs. 她用小摆设和纪念品过度装饰了她的公寓。
近义词: overdecorate, overembellish
反义词: simplify, understate
词性: verb

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "adorn" (verb meaning "to make more beautiful or attractive")
