beaconing:/ˈbiːkənɪŋ/| n. |信标；信标传输；导航|The act of transmitting a beacon signal, often for identification or location purposes.  A beacon can be a light, radio signal, or other form of transmission. |The beaconing from the lighthouse warned ships of the dangerous rocks. 灯塔发出的信号警告船只注意危险的岩石。|Synonyms: signaling, transmitting, broadcasting|Antonyms: concealing, hiding|noun

beaconing:/ˈbiːkənɪŋ/| v. |发出信标信号；指引；引导|To transmit a beacon signal. To guide or attract with a beacon.|The airport was beaconing its location to incoming aircraft. 机场正在向进港飞机发送其位置信号。|Synonyms: guiding, directing, signaling|Antonyms: misleading, confusing|verb
