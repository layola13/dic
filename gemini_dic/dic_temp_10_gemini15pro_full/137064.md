nonstrategically:/ˌnɑːnstrəˈtiːdʒɪkli/| adv. | 非战略性地，不从战略角度 |  Without any strategic plan or consideration; in a way that is not based on strategy. | They approached the problem nonstrategically, simply reacting to each issue as it arose. 他们没有战略性地处理这个问题，只是对出现的每个问题做出反应。 | The company nonstrategically invested in a failing market. 该公司非战略性地投资于一个失败的市场。| 近义词: aimlessly, haphazardly, randomly, thoughtlessly | 反义词: strategically, deliberately, purposefully | 词性: 副词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "strategically" (adverb form of "strategic"). "Strategic" comes from the Greek "stratēgikos" meaning "of or belonging to a general," from "stratēgos" meaning "general."
