murkest:/ˈmɜːrkɪst/| adj.|最黑暗的，最阴暗的，最模糊的|Superlative form of murky: most murky.  Meaning characterized by darkness, gloom, or obscurity.|The murkest depths of the ocean remain largely unexplored. 海洋最黑暗的深处很大程度上仍未探索。|The scandal took place during the murkest days of the regime.  这桩丑闻发生在该政权最黑暗的时期。|近义词：darkest, gloomiest, dimmest, obscurest|反义词：brightest, clearest, lightest|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *mirke, from Old English mirce (“dark, murky”), from Proto-Germanic *murkaz (“dark”), from Proto-Indo-European *merk- (“to grow dark, twinkle”). Cognate with West Frisian mirk (“dark”), Dutch merk (“mark, sign”), German merklich (“perceptible, noticeable”). Related to mizzle.
