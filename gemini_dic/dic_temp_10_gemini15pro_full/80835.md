gougeshell:/ˈɡaʊdʒʃɛl/
noun
凿贝壳；用凿子刻出的贝壳；(比喻)被欺骗的人
a shell that has been gouged or carved; (figuratively) someone who has been cheated or swindled
The archaeologist discovered a gougeshell used as a tool in the ancient settlement. 考古学家在古代定居点发现了一个用作工具的凿贝壳。
He felt like a gougeshell after losing all his money in the scam. 在骗局中损失了所有钱后，他觉得自己像个被欺骗的人。
synonyms:carved shell, dupe, victim
antonyms: perpetrator, swindler
