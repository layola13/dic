homeliest:/ˈhəʊmliɪst/| adj.|最朴素的；最不好看的；最平凡的|Plain or unattractive, suggesting home life in a simple or unsophisticated way.  Superlative form of "homely".|例句：She wore the homeliest dress to the party, but she had the kindest smile. 她穿着最朴素的裙子参加了聚会，但她拥有最亲切的笑容。

例句：Of all the houses on the street, theirs was the homeliest. 在街上所有的房子中，他们家的房子是最不好看的。|近义词：plain, simple, unattractive, unpretentious, modest|反义词：beautiful, attractive, glamorous, gorgeous, sophisticated|词性:形容词
