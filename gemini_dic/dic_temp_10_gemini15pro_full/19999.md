biohazardous:/ˌbaɪoʊˈhæzərdəs/
adjective
生物危害的
Presenting a risk to human health or the environment arising from biological work, especially with microorganisms.
The biohazardous waste was carefully disposed of.  生物危害废物被小心地处理掉了。
The lab is equipped to handle biohazardous materials.  这个实验室配备了处理生物危害材料的设备。
hazardous, dangerous, risky, unsafe
safe, harmless
