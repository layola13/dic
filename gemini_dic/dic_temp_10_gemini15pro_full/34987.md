cloggy:/ˈklɒɡi/ (UK) /ˈklɑːɡi/ (US)| adj.|阻塞的，堵塞的；笨重的，沉重的|Thick, sticky, and heavy; impeding movement.  Can also refer to something that causes clogging.|The heavy rain made the field cloggy and difficult to walk across. 大雨使田野变得泥泞难行。|Synonyms: miry, muddy, sticky, heavy, cumbersome|Antonyms: clear, free, unobstructed|adjective

clog:/klɑːɡ/ (US) /klɒɡ/ (UK)| n. v.|木底鞋；障碍物；阻塞；堵塞|A shoe with a thick wooden sole; something that obstructs or hinders passage; to obstruct or hinder passage.|The drain was clogged with hair. 排水管被头发堵塞了。|Synonyms: obstruct, block, hinder, impede|Antonyms: clear, unblock, free|noun, verb
