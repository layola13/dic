mundane:/mʌnˈdeɪn/ /mʌnˈdeɪn/| adj.|世俗的；平凡的；单调的|Lacking interest or excitement; dull.  Of this earthly world rather than a heavenly or spiritual one.|例句：He led a remarkably mundane life. 他过着非常平凡的生活。|近义词：ordinary, commonplace, routine, humdrum, boring, tedious|反义词：extraordinary, remarkable, exciting, thrilling|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Old French *mondain, from Late Latin mundānus "belonging to the world," from Latin mundus "world."
