outshown:/ˌaʊtˈʃoʊn/
verb
胜过；使相形见绌
surpassed in performance or achievement, especially in a competitive context.
例句：He was completely outshown by his younger rival. 他完全被年轻的对手比下去了。
例句：Her performance outshone all the others. 她的表演胜过所有其他人。
近义词：outperformed, eclipsed, surpassed, exceeded
反义词：outshone, overshadowed
词性：verb
