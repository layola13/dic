companiable:/kəmˈpeɪniəbl/ /kəmˈpæn.jə.bəl/
adjective
友善的；好交际的；适于做伴的
Enjoyable to be with; sociable.
例句：She's a very companiable person. 她是一个非常友善的人。

例句：He was a quiet, companiable man. 他是一个安静、好相处的人。

近义词：sociable, friendly, amiable, congenial, gregarious
反义词：unsociable, unfriendly, aloof, reserved
词性：adjective

词源：
Late Middle English: from Old French compaignable, from compaigne ‘companion’, from late Latin companio (see companion).
