hypoptilum:/ˌhaɪpɒˈtɪləm/ | n. |下羽，绒羽|The soft, fluffy feathers that form the insulating layer beneath the contour feathers of a bird.  |The hypoptilum of the eider duck is highly prized for its insulating properties.  绒鸭的下羽因其绝缘性能而备受推崇。|Synonyms: down, underplumage |Antonyms: contour feather, flight feather | noun
