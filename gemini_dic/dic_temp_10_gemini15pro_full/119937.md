milksop:/ˈmɪlksɒp/ (UK) /ˈmɪlksɑːp/ (US)| n.|懦夫，胆小鬼，没有男子气概的人|A person who is indecisive, timid or unassertive. |He's such a milksop, he wouldn't say boo to a goose.|近义词: coward, wimp, sissy|反义词: hero, tough guy|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From milk + sop.  "Sop" originally referred to bread soaked in milk, considered a soft food for invalids or children.  The term evolved to describe someone weak or effeminate.
