heavyweight:/ˈhɛviˌweɪt/| n. adj.|重量级选手，重量级人物，举足轻重的人或物|a person or thing of great influence or importance; in boxing, a weight division for boxers over a specified weight (traditionally over 175 pounds)|例句：He is a heavyweight in the political arena. 他是政坛上的重量级人物。|近义词：influential, powerful, leading, prominent|反义词：lightweight, unimportant, insignificant|词性:名词，形容词
