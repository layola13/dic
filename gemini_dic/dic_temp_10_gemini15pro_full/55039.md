disquieting:/dɪsˈkwaɪətɪŋ/| adj.|令人不安的，令人忧虑的|Causing anxiety or uneasiness; disturbing.|The disquieting news of the approaching storm made everyone nervous. 接近风暴的令人不安的消息让每个人都感到紧张。|Synonyms: disturbing, unsettling, troubling, worrying, alarming|Antonyms: calming, soothing, reassuring, comforting|adjective

disquiet:/dɪsˈkwaɪət/| n. vt.|不安，忧虑；使不安，使忧虑|A feeling of anxiety or worry; to make someone feel anxious or worried.|The constant rumors of layoffs caused a great deal of disquiet among the employees.  不断裁员的传言在员工中引起了极大的不安。  The strange noises in the night disquieted the children. 夜晚奇怪的噪音让孩子们感到不安。|Synonyms: unease, anxiety, worry, concern; disturb, upset, unsettle|Antonyms: calm, peace, tranquility; soothe, comfort, reassure|noun, verb
