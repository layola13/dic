effuse:/ɪˈfjuːz/ | v. | 散发，流出；倾诉，流露 | To flow out freely; to express (feelings, etc.) in an unrestrained way. | The flowers effused a sweet fragrance.  花朵散发出甜甜的香味。 |  She effused her happiness to everyone she met. 她向遇到的每个人倾诉她的快乐。 | emanate, pour out, exude | contain, suppress | verb

effusive:/ɪˈfjuːsɪv/ | adj. |  热情洋溢的，感情奔放的 | Expressing feelings of gratitude, pleasure, or approval in an unrestrained or heartfelt manner. |  He gave me an effusive welcome. 他热情地欢迎我。| She was effusive in her praise of the project. 她对这个项目赞不绝口。| enthusiastic, gushing, exuberant | reserved, restrained | adjective
