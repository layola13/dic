cilantro:/sɪˈlæntroʊ/ (US) /sɪˈlɑːntroʊ/ (UK)| n. |香菜，芫荽|Referring to the leaves of the coriander plant, Coriandrum sativum, used as a culinary herb, especially in Mexican and Asian dishes.  The seeds of the same plant are referred to as coriander.|例句：The cilantro added a fresh, citrusy flavor to the dish. 香菜给这道菜增添了新鲜的柑橘味。

例句：Many people either love or hate the taste of cilantro. 很多人要么喜欢香菜的味道，要么讨厌它。 |近义词:Chinese parsley, Mexican parsley, coriander leaves|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
