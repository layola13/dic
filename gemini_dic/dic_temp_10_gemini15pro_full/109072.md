legatary: /ˈleɡətɛri/ | n. | 受遗赠人;遗产继承人 | A person to whom a legacy is bequeathed. |  My aunt named me as her legatary in her will. 我的姑姑在遗嘱中指定我为她的遗产继承人。|  The legatary received a substantial sum of money.  这位遗产继承人得到了一大笔钱。 | synonym: inheritor, beneficiary, heir | antonym: testator, bequeather | noun

legatary: /ˈleɡətɛri/ | adj. |  遗赠的;遗产的 | Of, relating to, or given as a legacy.  (This usage is less common.) | The legatary funds were used to establish a scholarship.  遗赠的资金被用来设立奖学金。 | The legatary property included a house and a car. 遗产财产包括一栋房子和一辆汽车。| synonym: bequeathed, inherited | antonym:  N/A (adjective form less common) | adjective
