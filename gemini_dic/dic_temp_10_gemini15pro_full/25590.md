bummed:/bʌmd/
adjective
沮丧的，失望的
Feeling disappointed, upset, or depressed.
I was really bummed when I failed the test. 我考试不及格，真的很难过。
近义词:disappointed, down, sad, depressed, upset
反义词:happy, cheerful, elated, delighted
词性:adjective

bummed:/bʌmd/
verb
使沮丧
To make someone feel disappointed, upset, or depressed.  (Often used in the passive voice: "I got bummed out.")
The bad weather bummed me out. 坏天气让我心情低落。
近义词:disappoint, sadden, depress, upset
反义词:cheer up, delight, gladden
词性:verb
