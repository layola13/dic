antifatigue:/ˌæntiˈfəˈtiːɡ/| adj.|抗疲劳的|Designed to reduce or prevent fatigue.  Relating to or characterized by the reduction or prevention of fatigue.|This chair has anti-fatigue properties that make it comfortable for long hours of sitting. 这把椅子具有抗疲劳特性，可以长时间坐着舒适。|The anti-fatigue mat really helps my back when I'm standing for long periods.  这个抗疲劳垫在我长时间站立时对我的背部很有帮助。| 近义词:ergonomic, energy-saving | 反义词:fatiguing, exhausting | 词性:adjective
