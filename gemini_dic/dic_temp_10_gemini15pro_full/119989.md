millibar:/ˈmɪlɪˌbɑːr/| n.|毫巴|A unit of atmospheric pressure equal to one thousandth of a bar, equivalent to 100 pascals.  It is commonly used in meteorology.|The atmospheric pressure is 1013 millibars.|近义词：hectopascal|反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement)|noun
