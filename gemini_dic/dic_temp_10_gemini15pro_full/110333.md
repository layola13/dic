lightfastness:/ˈlaɪtˌfæstnəs/
noun
耐光性，不褪色性
The ability of a pigment or dye to resist fading or discoloration when exposed to light.
This paint boasts superior lightfastness, ensuring your artwork remains vibrant for years.  这种颜料拥有卓越的耐光性，确保您的作品多年保持鲜艳。
The artist used paints known for their lightfastness.  这位艺术家使用了以耐光性著称的颜料。
permanence, durability, stability
fugitiveness, instability
n.
