inhumanely:/ˌɪnhjuːˈmeɪnli/
| adv. | 残忍地；不人道地 | In a cruel and brutal manner, lacking compassion and empathy, as if not human. | The prisoners were inhumanely treated. 囚犯们受到了非人的待遇。 | cruelly, barbarously, savagely, brutally | humanely, kindly, compassionately | adverb

inhumane:/ˌɪnhjuːˈmeɪn/
| adj. | 残忍的；不人道的 | Lacking compassion, empathy, or kindness; cruel and brutal. | Keeping animals in such small cages is inhumane.  把动物关在这么小的笼子里是不人道的。| cruel, brutal, barbaric, savage | humane, kind, compassionate, merciful | adjective
