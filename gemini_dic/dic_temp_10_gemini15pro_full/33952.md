cirrostratus:/ˌsɪroʊˈstrætəs/ /ˌsɪrəˈstreɪtəs/| n. |卷层云|A high-altitude, thin, sheetlike cloud made of ice crystals, often producing a halo around the sun or moon and sometimes covering the whole sky. |例句：The cirrostratus clouds created a beautiful halo around the sun. 卷层云在太阳周围形成了一个美丽的光晕。

例句：The appearance of cirrostratus clouds often indicates an approaching warm front. 卷层云的出现通常预示着暖锋的来临。|近义词：high cloud, ice cloud|反义词:(No specific antonym for a cloud type)|词性:noun
