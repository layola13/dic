oozoa:/ˌoʊ.əˈzoʊ.ə/ | n. | 卵生动物 | Animals that reproduce by laying eggs.  These animals can range from insects to birds to reptiles.  | Birds are part of the oozoa group as they lay eggs. 鸟类属于卵生动物，因为它们产卵。 |  oviparous | viviparous | noun

Etymology:
From Greek  *ō(ó)on* (egg) + *zôion* (animal).

PHRASE->
Many oozoa, such as chickens, lay eggs for consumption. 许多卵生动物，例如鸡，产蛋供人们食用。

Some oozoa, like snakes, lay eggs that hatch into miniature versions of the adults. 一些卵生动物，例如蛇，产下的卵会孵化成成体的微型版本。

Not all oozoa lay their eggs in nests; some, like certain fish, release them into the water. 并非所有的卵生动物都把卵产在巢里；有些，比如某些鱼类，会把卵释放到水中。

The study of oozoa is crucial for understanding the diversity of reproductive strategies in the animal kingdom. 研究卵生动物对于理解动物王国中繁殖策略的多样性至关重要。

The eggs of different oozoa species vary greatly in size, shape, and color. 不同卵生动物的卵大小、形状和颜色差异很大。
