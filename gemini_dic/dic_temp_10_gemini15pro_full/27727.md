cangue: /kæŋ/ | n. | 枷, 颈枷 | A heavy wooden yoke borne on the shoulders and enclosing the neck, formerly used in China for punishing petty criminals. | The cangue was a cruel and unusual punishment.  枷是一种残酷而不寻常的惩罚。| The prisoner was forced to wear the cangue for a week. 囚犯被迫戴了一星期的枷。 | synonyms: yoke, pillory, stocks | antonyms: freedom, liberation | noun

cangue: /kæŋ/ | v. | 给…上枷 | To place in a cangue. | They cangued the thief and paraded him through the streets. 他们给小偷上了枷，并让他游街示众。|  The magistrate ordered the man to be cangued.  法官下令将该男子上枷。| synonyms: imprison, confine, restrain | antonyms: release, free, liberate | verb
