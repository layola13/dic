cross-sectioned:/ˈkrɒsˌsɛkʃənd/ /ˈkrɔːsˌsɛkʃənd/| adj.| 横截面的；剖面的|Showing the internal structure as if cut through vertically or transversely.|The museum displayed a cross-sectioned tree trunk, revealing its growth rings. 博物馆展出了一个横截面的树干，展示了它的年轮。|The architect presented a cross-sectioned drawing of the building. 建筑师展示了建筑物的剖面图。|近义词：sectioned, dissected|反义词: whole, complete|词性:形容词

cross-section:/ˈkrɒsˌsɛkʃən/ /ˈkrɔːsˌsɛkʃən/| n.| 横截面；剖面；代表性样本|A surface or shape that is or would be exposed by making a straight cut through something, especially at right angles to an axis; a group of people or things that are typical of a larger group.|The cross-section of the pipe was circular. 管道的横截面是圆形的。|A cross-section of society was interviewed for the survey.  为了这项调查，采访了社会各阶层的人。|近义词：section, sample|反义词: whole, entirety|词性:名词
