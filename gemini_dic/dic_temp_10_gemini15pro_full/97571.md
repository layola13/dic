USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃什么？
What does he do for a living? = 他以什么为生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching? = 你在看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? =  她晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned recently? = 你最近学了什么？
What has she bought at the store? = 她在商店买了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on? = 你一直在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say? = 你说什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing yesterday at noon? = 你昨天中午在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间她在想什么？


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had they eaten before the movie? = 看电影前他们吃了什么？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the phone rang? = 电话响之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been discussing for hours? = 他们已经讨论了几个小时什么？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎样？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们下次会议会讨论什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by the end of the day? = 你会在今天结束前完成什么？
What will she have accomplished by next year? = 到明年她将完成什么？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for the past ten years by 2030? = 到2030年，你将已经做了十年什么？
What will they have been studying by the time they graduate? = 他们毕业时将已经学习什么多久了？
