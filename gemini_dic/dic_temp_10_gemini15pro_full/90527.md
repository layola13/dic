hues:/hjuːz/
noun
色彩，色调
a gradation or variety of a color; tint; shade.
The sunset painted the sky in vibrant hues of orange and pink.  日落将天空染上了鲜艳的橙色和粉色。
synonyms: colors, shades, tints, tones
antonyms: monochrome, achromatic
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *huis, plural of *hui (“manner, way, appearance”), from Latin modus (“manner, way”). Doublet of mode.
