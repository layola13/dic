misoccupying:/ˌmɪsˈɒkjupaɪɪŋ/
Verb
误用，占用错误
Using or occupying wrongly or improperly.
The company was misoccupying the land, as it was designated for residential use only. 这家公司错误地占用了这块土地，因为这块地被指定只能用于住宅用途。
The squatters were misoccupying the abandoned building. 占据者错误地占用了这栋废弃的建筑。
misusing, misapplying
