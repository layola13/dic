countermove:/ˈkaʊntərmuːv/| n.|反击，应对措施|An action taken in opposition to another action.|例句：His countermove was unexpected and effective. 他的反击出人意料且有效。|例句：The company launched a countermove against its competitor's new product.  这家公司针对其竞争对手的新产品采取了应对措施。|近义词：response, reaction, retaliation, maneuver|反义词:initiative, attack|noun
