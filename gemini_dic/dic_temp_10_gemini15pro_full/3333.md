afforest:/əˈfɔːrɪst/ /əˈfɑːrɪst/| vt.|造林，绿化|To establish a forest on land that has not been forested for a long time.|The government has plans to afforest large areas of wasteland. 政府计划在荒地上大面积造林。|The council decided to afforest the bare hills surrounding the town.  市议会决定绿化城镇周围的秃山。|近义词：reforest, plant trees|反义词:deforest|词性:动词
