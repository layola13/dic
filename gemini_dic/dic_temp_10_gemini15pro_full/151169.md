parcel:/ˈpɑːrsl/ (US) /ˈpɑːsəl/ (UK)| n. vt.|包裹，小包；(土地的)一块，一片|A thing or collection of things wrapped in paper or cloth in order to be carried or sent by mail.  To make (something) into a parcel or parcels. |The postman delivered a parcel this morning. 邮递员今天早上送来一个包裹。|Synonyms: package, bundle, pack|Antonyms: whole, entirety|noun, verb

parcel:/ˈpɑːrsl/ (US) /ˈpɑːsəl/ (UK)| vt.|分配，分给|Divide (something) into portions and distribute it. |The land was parceled out among the settlers.  这块土地被分配给了定居者。|Synonyms: distribute, allocate, apportion|Antonyms: collect, gather, combine|verb
