heartier:/ˈhɑːrtiər/ (US), /ˈhɑːtɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更热情的；更健壮的；更丰盛的|More warm-hearted, robust, or substantial.|例句：They gave us a heartier welcome this time. 这次他们给了我们更热烈的欢迎。|近义词：warmer, stronger, more generous|反义词：less enthusiastic, weaker, smaller|词性:形容词
