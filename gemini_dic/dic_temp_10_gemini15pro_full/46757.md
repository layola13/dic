dandling:/ˈdændlɪŋ/
adjective
悬挂的，摇晃的
hanging loosely and swinging lightly
The monkey was dandling from a tree branch. 猴子在树枝上荡来荡去。
近义词:swinging, dangling, hanging
反义词:fixed, stationary
词性:adjective


dandling:/ˈdændlɪŋ/
verb
悬摆，使摇摆
to move something or someone gently back and forth or up and down
She dandled the baby on her knee. 她把婴儿放在膝盖上摇晃。
近义词:swing, sway, rock
反义词:still, steady
词性:verb (present participle)
