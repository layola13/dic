eddaic: /ˈɛdeɪɪk/
adjective
埃达的；与埃达相关的
Relating to or characteristic of the Eddas, two Icelandic collections of Norse mythology and heroic legends.
The eddaic poems offer a glimpse into the pre-Christian beliefs of the Norse people. 埃达诗歌让我们得以一窥北欧民族的基督教之前的信仰。
Near synonyms: Norse, Icelandic, mythological, legendary
Antonyms: modern, contemporary, factual, historical
词性: adjective
