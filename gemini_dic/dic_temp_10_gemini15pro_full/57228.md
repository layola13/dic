downgrade from (从...降级): She downgraded from the premium plan to the basic one. 她从高级计划降级到基本计划。

downgrade someone's position (降低某人的职位): They downgraded his position from manager to assistant manager. 他们把他的职位从经理降级为助理经理。
downgrade the importance of something (降低某事的重要性):  They downgraded the importance of employee feedback. 他们降低了员工反馈的重要性。
