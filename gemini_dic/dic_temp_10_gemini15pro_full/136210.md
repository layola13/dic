nonreligiously:/ˌnɑːnrɪˈlɪdʒəsli/ | adv. | 非宗教地，世俗地 |  In a way that is not influenced by or connected with religion. |  She decided to raise her children nonreligiously, focusing on ethical principles instead of religious doctrines. 她决定用非宗教的方式抚养她的孩子，注重伦理原则而不是宗教教义。| Synonyms: secularly, worldly, irreligiously | Antonyms: religiously, piously | adverb
