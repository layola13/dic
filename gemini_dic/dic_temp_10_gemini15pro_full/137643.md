nonubiquitousnesses:/ˌnɒnjuːˈbɪkwɪtəsnəsɪz/
noun
非普遍性；非无所不在性
The state or quality of not being ubiquitous; the lack of ubiquity.  The fact that something is not present everywhere.
The nonubiquitousnesses of certain species of birds makes them highly prized by birdwatchers.  某些鸟类物种的非普遍性使它们受到观鸟者的高度重视。
Near synonyms: rarity, scarcity, infrequency
Antonyms: ubiquity, omnipresence, universality
词性: noun
