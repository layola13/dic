leden: /ˈleɪdən/ | adj. | 负担沉重的；充满的；装满的 | Laden means heavily loaded or weighed down. It can also mean filled with a large quantity of something. | The ship was laden with cargo. 这艘船装满了货物。

The table was laden with food. 桌子上摆满了食物。| 近义词: loaded, burdened, fraught, filled | 反义词: empty, unburdened, light | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From Middle English *laden, from Old English hladan (“to load, lade, heap up, draw water”), from Proto-Germanic *hlaþaną (“to load, lade, invite”), from Proto-Indo-European *kleh₂- (“to lay, put”). Cognate with Dutch laden (“to load, lade”), German laden (“to load, invite”), Old Norse hlaða (“to load, pile up”). Related to load.
