accompany: /əˈkʌmpəni/
verb
陪伴，伴随；伴奏
to go somewhere with someone or to exist at the same time as something else.
She accompanied me to the doctor. 她陪我去看了医生。
The text is accompanied by illustrations. 正文附有插图。

近义词: escort, attend, follow
反义词: leave, abandon
词性: verb

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I often accompany my grandmother to the park. = 我经常陪祖母去公园。
He accompanies his singing with guitar playing. = 他用吉他为自己的歌声伴奏。


一般过去时:
She accompanied him on his trip to Europe. = 她陪他去了欧洲旅行。
Thunder accompanied the heavy rain. = 雷声伴随着大雨。


现在完成时:
He has accompanied her on many business trips. = 他陪她参加过很多商务旅行。
The singer has accompanied herself on the piano for years. = 这位歌手多年来一直用钢琴为自己伴奏。


过去完成时:
He had accompanied her to the airport before he went home. = 他送她去机场后才回家。
The pianist had accompanied many famous singers before he retired. = 这位钢琴家在退休前曾为许多著名歌手伴奏过。


将来时:
I will accompany you to the party tomorrow. = 我明天会陪你去参加聚会。
She will accompany the children on their school trip. = 她将陪同孩子们进行学校旅行。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would accompany you to the concert. = 如果我有时间，我会陪你去音乐会。
I wish I could accompany you on your journey. = 我希望我能陪你一起去旅行。


被动语态:
The singer was accompanied by the orchestra. = 歌手由管弦乐队伴奏。
The president was accompanied by his security team. = 总统由他的安全团队陪同。


疑问句:
Will you accompany me to the store? = 你能陪我去商店吗？
Who accompanied her to the party? = 谁陪她去参加聚会了？


否定句:
I cannot accompany you to the movie tonight. = 我今晚不能陪你看电影了。
She did not accompany him on the business trip. = 她没有陪他去出差。



PHRASE->
accompany someone on a trip = 陪某人旅行
accompany someone to a place = 陪某人去某个地方
accompany something with something = 与…搭配，伴…发生
accompany a singer on the piano = 用钢琴为歌手伴奏
be accompanied by = 由…陪同/伴奏
