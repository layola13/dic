angst-ridden:/ˈæŋstˌrɪdən/ | adj. |充满焦虑的，焦虑不安的 | Experiencing or expressing a feeling of persistent worry or anxiety about something.  Characterized by or showing angst. |  The angst-ridden teenager paced her room, worried about her exams.  焦虑不安的青少年在房间里踱来踱去，担心着她的考试。 | synonyms: anxious, worried, apprehensive, stressed, uneasy, troubled, tormented | antonyms: calm, peaceful, serene, tranquil, relaxed, carefree | adjective
