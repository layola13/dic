chesteyn:/ˈtʃɛstn/| n.|栗子; 栗树; 栗色; 栗色马|A chestnut is a smooth, shiny nut that grows on a sweet chestnut tree.  Chestnut can also refer to the tree itself, the reddish-brown color of the nut, or a horse with a coat of that color. |例句：The horse was a deep chestnut color. 这匹马是深栗色的。

例句：We roasted chestnuts over the open fire. 我们在篝火上烤栗子。|近义词：marron (for a specific type of large chestnut), auburn (for the color)|反义词: (No direct antonym, but contrasting colors could be used, e.g., white, black)|词性:noun
