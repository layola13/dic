misaligned:/ˌmɪsəˈlaɪnd/| adj.|未对准的，错位的|Not properly aligned; out of alignment.|The wheels of the car were misaligned after the accident. 汽车的轮子在事故后错位了。|The text was misaligned on the page. 页面上的文字没有对齐。|近义词：crooked, askew, out of line, awry|反义词：aligned, straight, true|词性:形容词
Etymology: mis- (prefix meaning "wrong" or "bad") + aligned (past participle of align, from Old French aligner "arrange in a line," from a- "to" + ligne "line")
