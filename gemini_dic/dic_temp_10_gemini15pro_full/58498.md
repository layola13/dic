dueling:/ˈduːəlɪŋ/ /ˈdjuːəlɪŋ/ | n. |决斗; 抗衡; 竞争 |A prearranged combat between two persons to settle a private quarrel. |  The dueling between the two companies for market share intensified.  两家公司对市场份额的竞争加剧了。 | Synonyms: combat, fight, contest | Antonyms: cooperation, peace, agreement | noun

dueling:/ˈduːəlɪŋ/ /ˈdjuːəlɪŋ/ | adj. |决斗的 |Engaged in or used for a duel. | The dueling pianists entertained the crowd.  两位相互竞争的钢琴家娱乐了观众。 | Synonyms: contesting, competing, opposing | Antonyms: cooperating, collaborating, uniting | adjective
