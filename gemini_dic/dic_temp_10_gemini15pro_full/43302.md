craggiest: /ˈkræɡiɪst/ | adj. | 最崎岖的，最陡峭的 |  Superlative form of craggy: having many crags; rugged and rocky. | The craggiest peaks of the Himalayas are a challenge for even the most experienced climbers. 喜马拉雅山最崎岖的山峰即使对最有经验的登山者来说也是一个挑战。| Synonyms: roughest, steepest, most rugged | Antonyms: smoothest, flattest | adjective

Etymology: From crag + -y + -est.  "Crag" comes from the Middle English "cragge," from a Celtic origin, possibly related to the Welsh "craig" (rock).
