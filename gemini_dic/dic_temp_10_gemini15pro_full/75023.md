frier:/ˈfraɪər/| n.|油炸食品；油炸锅；油炸工人|A person or thing that fries something, especially a deep fryer or a person who works in a fast-food restaurant frying food. |The frier expertly dipped the potatoes into the hot oil. 油炸工人熟练地把土豆浸入热油中。|The fish came out of the frier golden brown.  炸鱼从油炸锅里出来，呈金黄色。|近义词：fry cook, deep fryer|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 baker 烤面包师)|词性: noun

词源: From Middle English *friere, from Old French freire, frire (“to fry”), from Latin frīgere (“to roast, fry, parch, burn”).
