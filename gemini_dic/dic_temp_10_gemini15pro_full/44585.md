croupiest:/ˈkruːpiɪst/ | adj. | 最像赌场荷官的;最阴郁的 |  (Informal) Most resembling or suggestive of a croupier, especially in terms of manner or appearance; most gloomy or depressing. It can also refer to a place or atmosphere. |  "He had the croupiest voice I've ever heard, like a bored casino dealer." 他说话的声音是我听过最像赌场荷官的，就像一个无聊的赌场发牌员。 |  gloomy, sullen, morose, dour, downcast | cheerful, upbeat, jovial, bright | adjective
