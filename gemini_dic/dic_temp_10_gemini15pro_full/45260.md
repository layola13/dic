cultivable:/ˈkʌltɪvəbl/ | adj. |可耕种的，可栽培的 | Suitable for cultivation; arable. | The land is rich and cultivable. 这片土地肥沃，可以耕种。 |  The farmer invested in new equipment to make more of his land cultivable.  这位农民投资了新设备，使他的更多土地可耕种。| 近义词: arable, tillable, fertile | 反义词: barren, infertile, uncultivable | 词性: adjective

cultivate: /ˈkʌltɪveɪt/ | vt. |耕种；培养；陶冶；建立（关系）|  1. To prepare and use (land) for crops or gardening. 2. To acquire or develop (a quality, sentiment, or skill). 3. To try to acquire or develop (a relationship or friendship). | 词源: From Latin *cultivare*, from *cultus* "care, cultivation," originally past participle of *colere* "to till, cultivate, inhabit." | 词性: verb
