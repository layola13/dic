barding:/ˈbɑːrdɪŋ/| n.|包油烤肉，(尤指禽类)；(旧时)马的披甲|The act of covering meat, especially poultry, with strips of fat before roasting to keep it moist; (historical) protective armor for a horse.|The chef prepared the turkey with a flavorful **barding** of bacon.  厨师用美味的培根包裹火鸡进行烤制。|Near synonyms: larding, covering, wrapping|Antonyms: uncovering, exposing|noun

barding:/ˈbɑːrdɪŋ/| v.|用(尤指培根)包油烤(肉)|To cover (meat, especially poultry) with strips of fat before roasting.|He **barded** the chicken with pancetta before putting it in the oven. 他在把鸡肉放进烤箱前，用意大利培根包裹起来。|Near synonyms: larding, basting|Antonyms: uncovering, exposing|verb
