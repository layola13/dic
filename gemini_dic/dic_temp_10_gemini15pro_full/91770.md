hyperendemic:/ˌhaɪpərɛnˈdɛmɪk/ | adj. | 超地方性的 | Constantly present at a high rate of incidence in a population.  A disease that is hyperendemic is constantly present at a high rate and affects a large percentage of the population.  It is a more severe state than endemic. | Malaria is hyperendemic in some parts of Africa. 疟疾在非洲某些地区是超地方性的。 | Synonym: highly endemic | Antonym: sporadic | adjective
