focusses:/ˈfəʊkəsɪz/ | v. | 集中；聚焦 |  To concentrate attention or effort on a particular thing or activity. | The photographer focussed the camera on the flower. 摄影师把相机对准了花。| synonyms: concentrates, centers, hones in | antonyms: distracts, scatters | verb

focusses:/ˈfəʊkəsɪz/ | n. | 焦点；中心 | The center of interest or activity. | This issue has become the main focus of the debate.  这个问题已成为辩论的焦点。| synonyms: center, focal point, target | antonyms: periphery, edge | noun


Etymology: From the Latin word "focus" meaning "hearth, fireplace".  The meaning later shifted to "point where rays of light or heat converge" and then to the more abstract sense of "center of activity or attention."
