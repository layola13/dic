monopolist:/məˈnɒpəlɪst/ (US) /məˈnɑːpəlɪst/ (UK)| n. | 垄断者，独占者| A person or company that has a monopoly, meaning they are the sole provider of a particular good or service and can therefore control prices. |  The tech giant was accused of being a monopolist and stifling competition.  这家科技巨头被指控垄断并扼杀竞争。|
近义词:monopolizer, trust, cartel, oligarch|
反义词:competitor, small business owner|
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *monopolista, from Greek monos "alone" + polein "to sell".
