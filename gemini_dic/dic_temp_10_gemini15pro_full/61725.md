emotionlessness:/ɪˈmoʊʃənləsnəs/| n.|无感情；冷漠；无动于衷|The emotionlessness of his voice sent a chill down her spine. 他毫无感情的声音让她背脊发凉。|近义词：apathy, indifference, coldness, impassivity|反义词：emotionality, passion, fervor|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From emotionless + -ness.  Emotionless is formed from emotion + -less.  Emotion ultimately comes from Latin *ēmovēre "to move out, remove, agitate," from ex "out" + movēre "to move."
