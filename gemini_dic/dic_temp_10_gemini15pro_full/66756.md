exoskeletal:/ˌeksoʊˈskelɪtl/
adjective
外骨骼的
Relating to or having an exoskeleton.  An exoskeleton is a rigid external covering for the body in some invertebrate animals, especially arthropods, providing both support and protection.
The exoskeletal structure of the beetle protects it from predators. 甲虫的外骨骼结构保护它免受捕食者的侵害。

近义词:external
反义词:endoskeletal
词性:adjective
