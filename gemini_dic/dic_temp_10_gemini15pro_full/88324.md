hippish:/ˈhɪpɪʃ/| adj.|嬉皮士的，嬉皮风格的|Resembling or characteristic of a hippie, especially in dress and lifestyle; unconventional and countercultural.|She had a hippish style with long flowing skirts and flowers in her hair. 她留着长长的飘逸的裙子和头发上的花朵，有一种嬉皮士的风格。|Example: The festival had a very hippish vibe, with lots of tie-dye and folk music.  这个节日有一种非常嬉皮的氛围，有很多扎染和民间音乐。|Synonyms: bohemian, unconventional, countercultural, alternative|Antonyms: conventional, traditional, mainstream|adjective
