marv:/mɑːrv/| v. |感到惊奇；感到惊叹；感到高兴|To be filled with wonder or astonishment; to marvel.  From the Middle English *marven*, from Old French *merveiller*, ultimately from Latin *mirari* "to wonder at".|例句：I marv at your skill. 我对你的技巧感到惊叹。

例句：He marved at the beauty of the sunset.  他对日落的美丽感到惊叹。|近义词：marvel, wonder, admire, be amazed|反义词:disregard, ignore|词性:动词
