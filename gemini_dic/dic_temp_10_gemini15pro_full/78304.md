germule:/ˈdʒɜːrmjuːl/
n.
胚芽；微生物；幼芽
A small bud, or a newly developing part of an organism, especially a plant or sponge, capable of developing into a complete individual or a new part.
The gemmules of sponges are resistant to freezing and drying. 海绵的胚芽能够抵抗冰冻和干燥。
synonyms: bud, sprout, seed, spore, germ
antonyms: adult, mature, developed
词性: 名词
