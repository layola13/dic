misbegotten:/ˌmɪsbɪˈɡɒtn/ (US) /ˌmɪsbɪˈɡɒtən/ (UK)| adj.|私生的；不幸的；拙劣的；违法的；|ill-conceived; poorly planned or executed; illegitimate (of a child).  Born out of wedlock; having an unfortunate origin; badly conceived or designed. |The movie was a misbegotten attempt at a romantic comedy. 这部电影是一次拙劣的浪漫喜剧尝试。|Synonyms: ill-conceived, ill-fated, unfortunate, illegitimate, bastard, spurious|Antonyms: legitimate, well-conceived, fortunate, blessed, well-planned|adjective
