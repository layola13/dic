hunadi: /huːˈnɑːdi/ | n. | 胡纳迪 |  Hunadi refers to the members of a noble family in medieval Transylvania and Wallachia, particularly associated with John Hunyadi (Hungarian: Hunyadi János), a renowned military leader. |  The Hunadi family played a significant role in the defense of Hungary and Wallachia against the Ottoman Empire. | 近义词: Wallachian nobility, Transylvanian nobility | 反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider Ottoman aristocracy) | 词性: noun
