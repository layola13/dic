glee:/ɡliː/
n.
欢乐，高兴，欣喜
A feeling of great happiness, especially from satisfaction or triumph.
She couldn't hide her glee at the news. 她听到这个消息后掩饰不住内心的喜悦。
joy, delight, elation
sadness, sorrow
noun
