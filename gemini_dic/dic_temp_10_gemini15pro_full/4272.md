airtight:/ˈɛːrtaɪt/| adj.|密封的，不透气的|Impermeable to air or nearly so; sealed so that no air can enter or escape.|例句：The container is airtight to keep the food fresh.  这个容器是密封的，以保持食物新鲜。

例句：We need an airtight alibi. 我们需要一个无懈可击的不在场证明。|近义词：sealed, hermetic, impermeable, watertight|反义词:leaky, permeable, porous|词性:adjective
Etymology: air + tight.  "air" from Old English *ær (akin to Old High German ar "air"), from Proto-Germanic *aira- "air" (source also of Old Norse, Gothic air, Old Frisian er, Dutch aar "ear of grain"). "tight" from Old English þiht "dense, close, compact, watertight, airtight," from Proto-Germanic *thinghta- "thick, dense."
