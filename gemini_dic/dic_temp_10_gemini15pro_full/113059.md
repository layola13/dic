lumpier:/ˈlʌmpiər/| adj.|更块状的，更粗糙的|More lumpy; having more or larger lumps.|The batter was too thick and lumpier than usual. 面糊太稠了，比平时更块状。|The road was lumpier after the heavy rain. 大雨过后，道路变得更加坑洼不平。|近义词：bumpy, uneven, knobby, lumpy|反义词：smooth, even, flat|词性:形容词

词源:
From lump + -y + -er (comparative suffix).  "Lump" comes from Middle English *lumpe*, of uncertain origin.
