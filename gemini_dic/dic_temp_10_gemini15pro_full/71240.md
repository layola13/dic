fittingly:/ˈfɪtɪŋli/| adv.|适当地，恰当地|In a way that is suitable or appropriate to the particular circumstances.|She was fittingly dressed for the occasion. 她穿着得体，适合这个场合。|appropriately, suitably, properly, aptly|inappropriately, unsuitably, improperly|adverb

Etymology->
From fitting + -ly.  Fitting comes from the Old English "fitt" meaning a division or section of a song or poem. This evolved to mean "suited" or "appropriate," and the adverbial form "fittingly" followed.
