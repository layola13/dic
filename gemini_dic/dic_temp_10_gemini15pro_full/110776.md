It's rare to see "linguistics" used in the plural form.  It generally refers to the field of study as a whole. | Example:  His linguisticses are impressive (unusual usage).  他的语言学知识令人印象深刻（不常见的用法）。| Synonym: linguistics | Antonym: (No direct antonym for a field of study) | Noun
