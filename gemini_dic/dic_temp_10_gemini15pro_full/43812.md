crenulated:/ˈkrɛnjʊleɪtɪd/| adj.|  圆齿形的，锯齿状的| having a notched or scalloped edge, like a battlement| The crenulated edge of the leaf was a distinguishing feature.  叶子的锯齿状边缘是一个显著特征。| Example 2: The ancient fortress walls were crenulated, providing defensive positions for archers. 古代堡垒的城墙呈锯齿状，为弓箭手提供了防御阵地。| synonyms: notched, scalloped, indented, serrated | antonyms: smooth, even, flat | adjective

crenulate:/ˈkrɛnjʊleɪt/ | v. | 使成圆齿形，使成锯齿状 | to make notches or scallops in something |  He crenulated the edge of the paper with pinking shears. 他用锯齿剪刀把纸的边缘剪成锯齿状。 | Example 2: The artist crenulated the clay pot to give it a decorative texture.  艺术家在陶罐上刻出锯齿状，使其具有装饰性的纹理。 | synonyms: notch, scallop, indent, serrate | antonyms: smooth, flatten | verb

词源: From Latin *crenulatus*, past participle of *crenulare*  "to make notched," from *crena* "notch."
