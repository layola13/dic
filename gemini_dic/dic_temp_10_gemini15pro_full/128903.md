nimby:/ˈnɪmbi/ (US) /ˈnɪmbi/ (UK)| n. |邻避症候群者；反对在自己家附近建设公共设施的人|A person who objects to the siting of something perceived as unpleasant or potentially dangerous in their own neighborhood, such as a landfill or hazardous waste facility, especially while raising no such objections to similar developments elsewhere.  It's an acronym for "Not In My Back Yard".|The residents are acting like total NIMBYs, protesting the new homeless shelter.|Synonyms: localist, parochialist, self-preservationist|Antonyms: altruist, communitarian|noun

nimbyism:/ˈnɪmbɪɪzəm/ (US) /ˈnɪmbɪɪzəm/ (UK)| n. |邻避症候群；反对在自己家附近建设公共设施的现象|The attitude of opposing the siting of something considered undesirable in one's neighborhood.|NIMBYism is preventing the construction of much-needed affordable housing.|Synonyms: localism, parochialism|Antonyms: altruism, civic-mindedness|noun
