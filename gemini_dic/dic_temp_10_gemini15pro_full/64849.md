espionages:/ˈɛspɪənɑːʒɪz/
n.
间谍活动, 刺探
The act or practice of spying or using spies to obtain secret information.
He was found guilty of several counts of espionage.  他被判犯有数项间谍罪。
Espionage played a key role in the Cold War.  间谍活动在冷战中扮演了关键角色。
Synonyms: spying, surveillance, intelligence gathering, undercover work
Antonyms: counterintelligence, security
词性: 名词
