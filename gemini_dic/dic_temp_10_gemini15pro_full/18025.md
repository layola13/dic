一般现在时 (Simple Present):
He begins his work at 8 am every day.  他每天早上8点开始工作。
She begins her day with a healthy breakfast. 她以一顿健康的早餐开始新的一天。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
The concert is beginning now.  音乐会现在开始了。
He is beginning to understand the concept. 他开始理解这个概念了。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
The play has already begun.  戏剧已经开始了。
The rain has begun to fall. 雨开始下了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
They have been beginning new projects all year. 他们一整年都在开始新的项目。
She has been beginning to learn Spanish. 她开始学习西班牙语了。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
