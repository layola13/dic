congressist:/ˈkɑːŋɡrəsɪst/ /ˈkɒŋɡrəsɪst/| n. |国会议员|A member of a congress, especially of the U.S. Congress.|例句：The congressist voted against the bill.  这位国会议员投票反对该法案。|例句：Several congressists expressed concerns about the new policy. 几位国会议员对新政策表示担忧。|近义词：representative, senator, legislator, lawmaker|反义词:citizen, constituent|词性:noun
Etymology: From Congress + -ist.  "Congress" ultimately derives from the Latin "congressus," meaning "a coming together."
