defected:/dɪˈfɛktɪd/
adjective
已脱党的；已叛逃的；有缺陷的
Having abandoned allegiance or duty (to a country, cause, or leader); deserted.  Also, imperfect or faulty.
Several soldiers defected to the enemy.  几个士兵叛逃到了敌方。
The product was defected and had to be returned.  这个产品有缺陷，必须退货。
synonyms:rebellious, disloyal, traitorous, flawed, imperfect, faulty
antonyms:loyal, faithful, perfect, flawless
词性:adjective
