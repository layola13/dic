hallmark:/ˈhɔːlmɑːrk/| n.| 标志，特征，特点，纯度印记|A distinctive feature, especially one of excellence.  The hallmark of this artist's work is the use of vibrant colors.  这位艺术家的作品的特点是使用鲜艳的色彩。|近义词：characteristic, trademark, indication, sign, feature, attribute|反义词: anomaly, exception|词性: noun
词源: From Middle English *halmark*, from Old French *halemarke*, from *hale* (“hall”) + *marque* (“mark”).  Originally referred to the official mark stamped on gold and silver articles at Goldsmiths' Hall in London to guarantee their purity.
