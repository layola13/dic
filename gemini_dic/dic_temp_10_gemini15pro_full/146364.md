overflattens:/ˌoʊvərˈflætənz/
verb
过度扁平化; 过度压平
to flatten something excessively, beyond what is desirable or necessary.
The pastry chef overflattened the dough, making it too thin for the pie crust.  糕点师把面团擀得过薄，做馅饼皮太薄了。
The constant ironing overflattened the collar of his shirt.  不断的熨烫把他的衬衫领子熨得太扁了。
Synonym: flatten excessively, compress too much
Antonym: puff up, inflate
词性: 动词
