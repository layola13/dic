nondegenerative:/ˌnɒndɪˈdʒɛnərətɪv/ (US) /ˌnɑːndɪˈdʒɛnərətɪv/ (UK)
adjective
非退行性的，非变性的
Not characterized by degeneration or deterioration.  A nondegenerative disease does not worsen over time.
The doctor assured her that the condition was nondegenerative and wouldn't get worse. 医生向她保证病情是非退行性的，不会恶化。
Her eyesight problems are thankfully nondegenerative. 谢天谢地，她的视力问题不是退行性的。
近义词: stable, nonprogressive
反义词: degenerative, progressive
词性: adjective
词源: non- (not) + degenerative (from degenerate, meaning to decline in quality)
