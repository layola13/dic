checkerboard:/ˈtʃɛkərbɔːrd/| n. |棋盘; 方格图案|A board with squares of alternating colors, typically black and white, used for playing checkers or other games. Also refers to a pattern resembling such a board.|The checkerboard pattern on the floor was mesmerizing. 地板上的棋盘图案令人着迷。|Synonyms: checkered pattern, grid, lattice|Antonyms: solid color, plain|noun

checkerboard:/ˈtʃɛkərˌbɔrd/| adj. |方格图案的|Having a pattern of alternating squares, typically black and white, resembling a checkerboard.|She wore a checkerboard dress to the party. 她穿着一件方格连衣裙去参加聚会。|Synonyms: checkered, tessellated|Antonyms: plain, solid|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "checker" (a piece used in the game of checkers) + "board" (a flat piece of wood or other material). "Checker" comes from Old French "eschequier" meaning "chessboard".
