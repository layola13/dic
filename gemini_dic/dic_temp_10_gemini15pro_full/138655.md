nudger:/ˈnʌdʒər/| n.|推动者；轻推者|Someone or something that nudges. A person who encourages or gently persuades someone to do something.|The app acts as a nudger, reminding users to drink water throughout the day. 这个应用程序充当一个推动者，提醒用户全天喝水。|Synonyms: prompter, persuader, influencer|Antonyms: deterrent, obstacle|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "nudge" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Nudge" comes from Middle English "noggen," meaning "to push or strike gently."
