alienee:/ˌeɪliˈiːniː/
noun
受让人，接受让与者
the person to whom property is transferred
The alienee is responsible for paying property taxes from the date of transfer.  受让人从转让之日起负责缴纳房产税。
The alienee in this transaction will assume all liabilities associated with the property.  本次交易中的受让人将承担与该房产相关的所有责任。
grantee, transferee
alienor, grantor
n.
