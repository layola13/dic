angerless:/ˈæŋɡər.ləs/
adjective
无怒的；平静的；不生气的
Free from anger; not easily angered; calm.
She met his accusations with an angerless gaze. 她用平静的目光回应他的指责。
Synonyms: calm, peaceful, tranquil, serene, placid, composed, unruffled, imperturbable
Antonyms: angry, furious, enraged, wrathful, irate, indignant, cross, irritable
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From anger + -less (suffix forming adjectives meaning 'without', 'free from').
