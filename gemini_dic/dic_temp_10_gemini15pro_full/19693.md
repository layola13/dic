bilgy:/ˈbɪldʒi/| adj.| 胀气的，充满液体的|Swelling or bulging with liquid; distended.  The ship's bilge was bilgy after the storm.  暴风雨过后，船的舱底充满了液体。|近义词：swollen, distended, bloated|反义词:empty, deflated|词性:形容词
词源:From bilge (the lowest compartment of a ship's hull) + -y (suffix forming adjectives).
