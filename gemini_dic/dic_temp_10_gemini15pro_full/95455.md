incisivenesses:/ɪnˈsaɪsɪvnəsɪz/
n.
深刻性；敏锐性；尖锐性
The quality of being incisive; keenness and forcefulness of thought or expression.
Her incisivenesses in debate were truly remarkable. 她在辩论中的深刻见解令人印象深刻。
The lawyer's incisivenesses quickly exposed the inconsistencies in the witness's testimony.  律师敏锐的洞察力迅速揭露了证人证词中的矛盾之处。
近义词: acuity, sharpness, penetration, perspicacity
反义词: vagueness, obtuseness, dullness
词性: Noun (plural)

词源: From incisive + -ness.  Incisive comes from Late Latin incisivus, from incidere "to cut into," from in- "into" + caedere "to cut."
