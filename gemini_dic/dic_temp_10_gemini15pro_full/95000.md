in-trust:/ɪn ˈtrʌst/ | adj. | 受托的，信托的 | Held or administered by someone for the benefit of another. |  The money was held in-trust for his children.  这笔钱由他人代为保管，以供他的孩子们使用。| 近义词: fiduciary, entrusted | 反义词: outright, absolute | 词性: 形容词

in trust:/ɪn ˈtrʌst/ | n. | 信托，委托 | The state of being held or administered by someone for the benefit of another. | The property was placed in trust until the heir came of age.  这处房产被置于信托管理，直到继承人成年。 | 近义词: trusteeship, guardianship | 反义词: ownership, possession | 词性: 名词
