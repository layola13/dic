disconformity:/ˌdɪskənˈfɔːrməti/ | n. |不整合（地质学）| A break in a sedimentary sequence in which the older and younger strata are parallel.  A type of unconformity in which the beds above and below the unconformity are parallel, but there is a significant time gap represented by the erosion surface separating them.|  The geologist discovered a disconformity between the two layers of rock. 地质学家发现了两层岩石之间的不整合。| synonym: unconformity | antonym: conformity | noun

disconformity:/ˌdɪskənˈfɔːrməti/ | n. |不一致，不符合| Lack of agreement or harmony; incongruity. | There is a disconformity between his words and his actions. 他的言行不一致。 | synonym: discrepancy, inconsistency, discordance | antonym: agreement, conformity, harmony | noun
