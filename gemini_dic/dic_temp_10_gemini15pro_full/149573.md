pahi:/ˈpɑːhiː/| n.|帕希，毛利独木舟|A large, double-hulled Polynesian sailing canoe (especially in New Zealand). It was used for voyaging and fishing.|The pahi was skillfully crafted from native timber. 这艘帕希独木舟是由本地木材精巧地制作而成。|The pahi sailed gracefully across the ocean. 这艘帕希独木舟优雅地航行在大海上。|近义词：waka, canoe|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "small boat")|Noun
