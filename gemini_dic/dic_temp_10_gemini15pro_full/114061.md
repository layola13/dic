magnetizable:/ˌmæɡnɪˈtaɪzəbl/
adjective
可磁化的
Capable of being magnetized; susceptible to magnetism.
Iron is a magnetizable material. 铁是一种可磁化的材料。
The metal alloy proved to be highly magnetizable.  这种金属合金被证明具有很高的磁化性。

近义词:magnetic, ferrimagnetic, ferromagnetic
反义词:nonmagnetic, non-magnetizable
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From magnetize + -able.  Magnetize comes from magnet + -ize.  Magnet ultimately derives from the Greek "Magnes lithos" meaning "Magnesian stone," referring to lodestone found in Magnesia, a region in ancient Greece.
