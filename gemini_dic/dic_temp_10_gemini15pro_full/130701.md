nonagonal:/nɒˈnæɡənəl/ /nɑːˈnæɡənəl/
adjective
九边形的; 九角形的
Relating to or having the properties of a nonagon; nine-sided.
A nonagonal prism has two nonagonal bases and nine rectangular sides.  一个九棱柱有两个九边形的底面和九个长方形的侧面。

The architect designed a building with a nonagonal courtyard.  这位建筑师设计了一座带有九边形庭院的建筑。

synonym:  nine-sided
antonym: (No direct antonym, but could consider shapes with different numbers of sides, e.g., octagonal, decagonal)
