agenesis:/eɪˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
n.
先天性缺失，发育不全
The absence of an organ or part of the body from birth due to a developmental failure.
Renal agenesis is a serious condition.  肾脏发育不全是一种严重的疾病。
Congenital agenesis of the corpus callosum can cause a range of developmental problems.  先天性胼胝体发育不全会导致一系列发育问题。

近义词:absence, lack, nondevelopment, aplasia
反义词:development, growth, presence
词性:noun
