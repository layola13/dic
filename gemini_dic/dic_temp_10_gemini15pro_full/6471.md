ampullary:/æmˈpʌləri/
|adj.|壶腹的；安瓿的|Relating to an ampulla, a dilated sac or cavity.|The ampullary portion of the fallopian tube is where fertilization typically occurs. 输卵管的壶腹部是通常发生受精的地方。|近义词:dilated, sac-like|反义词:constricted, narrow|词性:形容词

ampulla:/æmˈpʌlə/
|n.|壶腹；安瓿|A dilated sac or cavity, such as the dilations in semicircular canals or a small sealed glass vial containing a solution for injection.|The ampulla of Vater is where the pancreatic duct and common bile duct enter the duodenum. 壶腹是胰管和胆总管进入十二指肠的地方。|近义词:sac, cavity, vial|反义词:duct, tube|词性:名词
