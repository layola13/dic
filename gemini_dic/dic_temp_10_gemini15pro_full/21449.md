blown:/bloʊn/| adj.|吹制的，吹出的，喘不过气的，开花的，被炸毁的|Past participle of "blow".  Meaning carried by the wind, inflated, exploded, or ruined.|The wind had blown the leaves all over the yard. 风把树叶吹得满院子都是。|近义词：inflated, exploded, ruined, exhausted|反义词:deflated, intact, unexhausted|词性:形容词

PHRASE->
blow out:  The candles were blown out.  蜡烛被吹灭了。
blow up: The building was blown up by terrorists.  这栋建筑被恐怖分子炸毁了。
blow over:  The storm will blow over soon.  暴风雨很快就会过去。
blow away: The wind blew away my hat. 风吹走了我的帽子。
blow one's mind: The concert blew my mind.  这场音乐会让我大为震撼。


注意事项:
blown 主要用作形容词，是 blow 的过去分词形式。  尽管 blow 也有名词形式（指打击），但 blown 没有名词用法。
