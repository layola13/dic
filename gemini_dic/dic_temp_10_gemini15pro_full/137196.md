nonsuggestiveness:/ˌnɒnsəˈdʒestɪvnəs/ | n. | 无暗示性; 不含蓄性 |  The nonsuggestiveness of the advertisement was appreciated by parents.  The lack of any suggestive content, meaning it did not hint at or imply anything inappropriate or sexual. | 例句：The movie's nonsuggestiveness made it suitable for family viewing. 这部电影没有暗示性内容，适合全家观看。

例句：The nonsuggestiveness of the children's book was praised by reviewers.  评论员称赞这本儿童读物不含暗示性内容。| 近义词：innocence, purity, chasteness | 反义词：suggestiveness, impropriety, indecency | 词性: noun


词源:

From non- + suggestiveness.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of."  "Suggestiveness" comes from the verb "suggest," ultimately from Latin "suggerere," meaning "to carry, bring forth, prompt."
