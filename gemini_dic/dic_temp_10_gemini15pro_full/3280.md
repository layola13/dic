affix:/əˈfɪks/ | n. | 附加物，词缀 | something attached to something else; a prefix or suffix |  The "-ness" in "happiness" is an affix.  幸福中的“-ness”是一个词缀。 | attachment, addition | detachment, removal | noun

affix:/əˈfɪks/ | vt. | 粘贴，贴上；把...固定 | to attach, stick, or fasten something to something else |  He affixed a stamp to the envelope. 他在信封上贴了一张邮票。 | attach, stick, fasten | detach, remove | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *affixāre, from Latin affixus, past participle of affigere (“to fasten to”), from ad- (“to”) + figere (“to fix, fasten”).
