bagasse:/bəˈɡæs/ | n. | 甘蔗渣; 蔗渣;| The dry pulpy fibrous residue remaining after the extraction of juice from sugar cane, used as fuel, in the manufacture of building materials, and as a source of cellulose. | Sugarcane bagasse is used as biofuel for electricity generation. 甘蔗渣被用作生物燃料发电。|  Bagasse is often used in the production of paper and building materials. 甘蔗渣常用于造纸和建筑材料的生产。 | synonyms: residue, pulp, fiber, remains | antonyms:  N/A (No direct antonym for a physical byproduct) | noun
