darkly:/ˈdɑːrkli/ | adv. |昏暗地，阴沉地，秘密地| in a dark manner; gloomily; mysteriously| The room was darkly lit, creating a sense of mystery. 房间里光线昏暗，营造出一种神秘感。| The future looked darkly for the struggling company.  这家苦苦挣扎的公司前途暗淡。 | 近义词：gloomily, dimly, obscurely, secretly, ominously | 反义词：brightly, cheerfully, openly | 词性: 副词

词源: From Middle English *derkliche, equivalent to dark +‎ -ly.
