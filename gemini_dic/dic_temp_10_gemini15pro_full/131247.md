noncarbonates:/nɒnˈkɑːrbənɪts/ |n.| 非碳酸盐 | Minerals or rocks that do not contain carbonate ions (CO3²⁻).  These are typically silicate minerals, oxides, sulfides, or other mineral groups. | The geologist studied the noncarbonates in the region.  这位地质学家研究了该地区的非碳酸盐。 | Noncarbonate minerals are often resistant to weathering. 非碳酸盐矿物通常耐风化。 | 近义词: non-carbonate minerals | 反义词: carbonates | 词性: noun
