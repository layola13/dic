hit-the-books:/hɪt ðə bʊks/|idiom|开始认真学习；用功读书|To begin studying hard; to concentrate on one's studies.|Example 1: Finals are next week, so I need to hit the books.|期末考试下周就到了，所以我得开始认真学习了。

Example 2:  I've been slacking off, so I'm going to hit the books tonight.|我一直在偷懒，所以今晚我要好好学习。|近义词：study, cram, bone up (on), get down to work, buckle down|反义词: procrastinate, goof off, slack off|词性: idiom (idiomatic phrase)
