clam-up:/ˈklæm ʌp/| phrasal verb | 闭嘴，沉默不语 | To suddenly become silent, usually because you are embarrassed or nervous. |  He clammed up when we asked him about the money. 他当我们问他钱的事时就闭口不言了。|  fall silent, shut up, refuse to speak | speak up, open up | verb

clam-up:/ˈklæm ʌp/| n. | 沉默，闭嘴 | The act of suddenly becoming silent. | There was a clam-up when the teacher entered the room. 老师进教室时，大家都静了下来。 | silence, reticence | outspokenness, loquacity | noun
