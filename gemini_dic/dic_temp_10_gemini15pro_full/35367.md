co-instantaneously:/ˌkoʊˌɪnstənˈteɪniəsli/
adverb|同时地|occurring at the same instant|The two events happened co-instantaneously.|Synonyms: simultaneously, concurrently, at the same time|Antonyms: asynchronously, sequentially
Etymology: co- (together) + instantaneous (happening in an instant) + -ly (adverb suffix)
