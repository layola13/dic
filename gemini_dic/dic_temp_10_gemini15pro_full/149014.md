owch:/aʊtʃ/
interjection
哎哟; 痛
An exclamation expressing sudden pain.
Owch! I stubbed my toe! 哎哟！我的脚趾撞到了！
近义词:ouch, yikes,哎哟
反义词:(None - interjections generally don't have antonyms)
词性:interjection
