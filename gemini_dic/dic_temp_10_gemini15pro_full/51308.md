detailing:/ˈdiːteɪlɪŋ/ | n. | 详细说明；详述 | The detailing in the painting is incredible. 这幅画的细节令人难以置信。|  The report lacked sufficient detailing about the incident.  这份报告缺乏关于这起事件的足够细节。| 近义词：particulars, specifics, elaboration | 反义词: overview, summary | 词性: 名词

detailing:/ˈdiːteɪlɪŋ/ | v. | 详细说明；详述 | He detailed his plans for the project. 他详细说明了他的项目计划。|  She detailed the events leading up to the accident. 她详细描述了事故发生前的一系列事件。| 近义词：describe, explain, elaborate | 反义词: summarize, outline | 词性: 动词 (现在分词)

词源: From the verb "detail" (from Old French "detaillier," meaning "to cut into pieces," from "de-" meaning "down, from" + "tailler" meaning "to cut"), the suffix "-ing" forms the noun or present participle, indicating the act of detailing or the details themselves.
