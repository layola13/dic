conversionist:/kənˈvɜːrʒənɪst/
n.
皈依者；改变信仰者；改变主意者
A person who has been converted to a different religious belief or political opinion.
The conversionist joined the church after a profound spiritual experience.  这个皈依者在一次深刻的灵性体验后加入了教会。
He was a staunch Republican before becoming a conversionist to the Democratic Party. 他曾是坚定的共和党人，后来变成了民主党的皈依者。
Synonyms: convert, proselyte, neophyte
Antonyms: apostate, heretic
