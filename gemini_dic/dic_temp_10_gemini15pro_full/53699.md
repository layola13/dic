disclaimer:/dɪsˈkleɪmər/
|n.|免责声明，否认，放弃权利|A statement that denies something, especially responsibility.|The website included a disclaimer about the accuracy of the information provided.  该网站包含一项关于所提供信息准确性的免责声明。|近义词：denial, waiver, repudiation|反义词：acceptance, acknowledgment, admission|词性:名词
disclaimers:/dɪsˈkleɪmərz/
|n.|免责声明（复数）|Statements that deny something, especially responsibility.|Many websites now include disclaimers about the content they host. 许多网站现在都包含关于其所托管内容的免责声明。|近义词：denials, waivers, repudiations|反义词：acceptances, acknowledgments, admissions|词性:名词
