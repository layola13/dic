gill-less:/ˈɡɪllɪs/
adjective
无鳃的，没有鳃的
Lacking gills; having no gills.
The gill-less tadpole struggled to breathe in the polluted water. 这只没有鳃的蝌蚪在污染的水中挣扎着呼吸。
The scientist discovered a new species of gill-less salamander.  科学家发现了一种新的无鳃蝾螈。
Near Antonyms: gilled
