inappropriately:/ˌɪnəˈproʊpriɪtli/ | adv. | 不适当地，不得体地 | Not suitable or proper in the circumstances. | She was dressed inappropriately for the occasion. 她穿着不适合这个场合。| He laughed inappropriately during the serious discussion. 在严肃的讨论中，他很不适当地笑了。 |近义词：unsuitably, improperly, wrongly | 反义词：appropriately, suitably, properly | 词性: 副词

inappropriate:/ˌɪnəˈproʊpriɪt/ | adj. | 不适当的，不合适的 | Not suitable or proper in the circumstances. | Her inappropriate remarks offended many people. 她不恰当的言论冒犯了很多人。| It's inappropriate to wear jeans to a formal wedding. 穿牛仔裤参加正式婚礼是不合适的。 | 近义词：unsuitable, improper, wrong | 反义词：appropriate, suitable, proper | 词性: 形容词

词源:  in- (not) + appropriate (suitable)  来自拉丁语 appropriatus, 过去分词 appropriare "to make one's own," from ad- "to" + proprius "one's own, special."
