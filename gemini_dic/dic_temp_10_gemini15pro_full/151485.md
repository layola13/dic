parred:/pɑːrd/
verb
达到标准杆; 与对手打成平手;
achieve a par score on (a hole); tie with an opponent.
例句：He parred the first hole. 他在第一个洞打出了标准杆。
例句：She parred her opponent in the first round. 她在第一轮与对手打成平手。
近义词：tied, equaled, matched
反义词：bogeyed, lost, defeated
词性:v.

Etymology:
From par (n.), ultimately from Latin pār ("equal").
