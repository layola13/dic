blameworthiest:/ˈbleɪmˌwɜːrðiɪst/| adj.|最应受责备的，最该谴责的|Most deserving of blame; most culpable.|例句：He was judged the blameworthiest of all the conspirators. 他被认为是所有阴谋者中最应受谴责的。|近义词：most culpable, most guilty, most reprehensible|反义词:blameless, innocent, faultless|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From blameworthy + -est (superlative suffix).  Blameworthy is from blame + worthy. Blame comes from Old French "blasmer" meaning "to blame, censure." Worthy comes from Old English "wyrðe" meaning "worthy, noble, honorable."
