bistro:/ˈbiːstroʊ/ (US), /ˈbɪːstrəʊ/ (UK)| n. |小酒馆，小餐馆|A small, informal restaurant, typically one serving moderately priced simple meals. |例句：We ate at a charming little bistro in Paris. 我们在巴黎一家迷人的小酒馆用餐。

例句：The bistro offers a cozy atmosphere and delicious food.  这家小酒馆提供舒适的氛围和美味的食物。

近义词：cafe, restaurant, eatery|反义词: (No direct opposite, perhaps) formal restaurant, fine dining establishment |词性: noun
