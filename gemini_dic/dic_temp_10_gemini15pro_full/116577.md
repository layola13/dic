matriarchal:/ˌmeɪtriˈɑːrkl/
adj.
母系的，女族长的
Of or relating to a matriarchy; headed by a woman.
The anthropologist studied the matriarchal society of the Minangkabau people.  这位人类学家研究了米南佳保人的母系社会。
The tribe has a matriarchal social structure where women hold the primary power.  这个部落拥有母系社会结构，女性掌握主要的权力。
近义词:matriarchic, female-led, female-dominated
反义词:patriarchal
词性:adjective
