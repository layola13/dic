morsel:/ˈmɔːrsl/ (US) /ˈmɔːsəl/ (UK)| n.|一口，少量，一小块|A small piece of food; a small amount of something.|She savored each morsel of the delicious cake. 她细细品尝着这块美味蛋糕的每一小口。| The book contained a few interesting morsels of information. 这本书包含了一些有趣的信息片段。|近义词：bit, piece, mouthful, fragment, snippet|反义词: lot, abundance|noun
