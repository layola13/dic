homotypy: /hɒˈmɒtɪpi/ | n. |同型性，同型现象 | Similarity of structure, especially in different organisms as a result of parallel evolution; correspondence of an organ in one form of animal with the fundamentally different but functionally analogous organ in another.|The homotypy between the wings of birds and bats is a classic example of convergent evolution. 鸟类和蝙蝠翅膀之间的同型性是趋同进化的典型例子。|近义词: analogy, similarity, resemblance |反义词: heterotypy, dissimilarity | 词性: noun
