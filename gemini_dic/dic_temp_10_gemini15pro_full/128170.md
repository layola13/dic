network:/ˈnɛtwɜːrk/ (US) /ˈnɛtwɜːk/ (UK)| n. v.|网络，系统，关系网|A group or system of interconnected people or things.|例句：The company has a large network of suppliers.  这家公司拥有庞大的供应商网络。|近义词：system, web, grid|反义词:isolation, disconnection|词性:名词

network:/ˈnɛtwɜːrk/ (US) /ˈnɛtwɜːk/ (UK)| v.|建立联系，联网|To connect with other people, especially professionally.|例句：She networked extensively at the conference. 她在会议上广泛地建立了联系。|近义词：connect, liaise|反义词:disconnect, isolate|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English *netwark*, from *net* + *wark* "work".  The verb sense developed later.
