leonidas:/liːˈɒnɪdəs/| n.|列奥尼达|A male given name, most famously associated with Leonidas I, king of Sparta who led the Spartans at the Battle of Thermopylae.|Leonidas led the 300 Spartans against the Persian army. 列奥尼达率领三百斯巴达勇士对抗波斯军队。|近义词: (For the historical figure) Spartan king, warrior, hero|反义词: (No direct antonym for a name, perhaps) coward, traitor|词性:noun
