availment:/əˈveɪlmənt/
noun
利用，使用
The act of availing oneself of something; the act of using or taking advantage of something.
Employees should confirm their availment of the company's health insurance program.  员工应该确认他们是否使用了公司的健康保险计划。
The program encourages the availment of government services. 该计划鼓励利用政府服务。
近义词: use, utilization, exploitation
反义词: non-use, neglect
