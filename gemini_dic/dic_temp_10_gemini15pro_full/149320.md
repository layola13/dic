pace-making:/ˈpeɪsˌmeɪkɪŋ/ | n. |  起搏；领跑；定步调 |The act or process of setting the pace, especially in a race or other competitive activity.  Also refers to the regulation of the heart's rhythm, either naturally or artificially. |  The pacemaker in the lead group set a blistering pace. 领跑小组的起搏员设定了一个非常快的速度。 | synonyms: pacing, leading, front-running | antonyms: following, trailing | noun

pace-maker:/ˈpeɪsˌmeɪkər/ | n. | 起搏器；领跑者；标兵 | A person or thing that sets the pace, especially in a race. Also, a device that regulates the heartbeat. |  The experienced marathon runner acted as a pacemaker for the less experienced runners.  这位经验丰富的马拉松运动员为经验不足的运动员担任起搏员。 | synonyms:  pacesetter, leader, frontrunner | antonyms: follower, laggard | noun
