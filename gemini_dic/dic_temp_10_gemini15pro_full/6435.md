amplectant:/æmˈplɛktənt/
adjective
抱合的，紧抱的
Clasping or embracing tightly.
The amplectant vines covered the old wall.  抱合的藤蔓覆盖了旧墙。
amplectant roots held the soil firmly. 抱合的根系牢牢地抓住了土壤。
近义词: clinging, embracing, entwining
反义词: loose, detached, separate
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From Latin *amplectans*, present participle of amplecti ("to embrace"), from ambi- ("around") + plectere ("to weave, braid, plait").
