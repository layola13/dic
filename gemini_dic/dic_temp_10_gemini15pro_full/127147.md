nattiest:/ˈnætiɪst/
adjective
最整洁的；最时髦的
(superlative of)  Neat and trim in dress and appearance; stylish.  Implies a certain smartness and attention to detail in one's attire.
He was always the nattiest dresser in the room, sporting perfectly tailored suits and polished shoes. 他总是房间里穿着最整洁的人，穿着剪裁完美的西装和擦得锃亮的鞋子。
She wore her nattiest outfit to the awards ceremony.  她穿着她最漂亮的衣服去参加颁奖典礼。
近义词:smartest, sharpest, most stylish, dapper, chic
反义词:scruffiest, shabbiest, most unkempt, sloppiest
