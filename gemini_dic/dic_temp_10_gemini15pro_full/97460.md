infusory:/ɪnˈfjuːsəri/ | adj. | 灌输的；用于浸泡的 | Of or relating to infusion or steeping; used in or for infusing. |  Herbal teas are made using an infusory process. 草药茶是通过浸泡方法制成的。 | The apothecary used an infusory apparatus to extract the essence of the herbs. 药剂师用浸泡仪器提取草药的精华。 | 近义词: infusive | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

infusory:/ɪnˈfjuːsəri/ | n. | 浸泡器；注入器 | A vessel or apparatus used for infusing.  |  She used an infusory to make her morning tea. 她用浸泡器泡早茶。 | The infusory allowed for a slow and even extraction of flavor. 这个浸泡器可以缓慢而均匀地提取味道。 | 近义词: infuser, percolator | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *infusorius, from Latin infusus (past participle of infundere "to pour in"), from in- "in" + fundere "to pour".
