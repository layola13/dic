decillion:/dɪˈsɪljən/ | n. |百万兆；十亿的六次方；[英]千的三十三次方；[美]千的三十次方|A decillion is a very large number.  |Example 1: A decillion is 1 followed by 33 zeros in British English. (百万兆在英式英语中是1后面跟着33个零。)

Example 2: In American English, a decillion is represented as 1 followed by 30 zeros. (在美式英语中，百万兆表示为1后面跟着30个零。) |Synonym: enormous number, vast number |Antonym: small number |noun
