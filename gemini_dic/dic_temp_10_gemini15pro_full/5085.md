allantois:/əˈlæntɔɪs/
|n.| 尿囊|A membranous sac that develops from the posterior part of the alimentary canal in the embryos of mammals, birds, and reptiles. It forms part of the placenta in mammals and plays a role in respiration and excretion in birds and reptiles.|The allantois plays a crucial role in the development of the embryo.| 近义词:chorion (in mammals)| 反义词:None|noun


allantoidea:/ˌælənˈtɔɪdiə/
|n.| 尿囊类|A subclass of vertebrates comprising the reptiles, birds, and mammals, characterized by the presence of an allantois during embryonic development.|The allantoidea are a diverse group of animals, including snakes, eagles, and humans.| 近义词:Amniota| 反义词: Anamniota|noun


allantoic:/ˌælənˈtɔɪk/
|adj.| 尿囊的|Relating to or characterized by the allantois.|The allantoic membrane helps in gas exchange.| 近义词:chorionic (in mammals)| 反义词:None|adjective
