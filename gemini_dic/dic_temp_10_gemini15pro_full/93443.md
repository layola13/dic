illuminator:/ɪˈluːmɪneɪtər/ | n. | 照明器，照明者，阐明者 | A device or substance that illuminates something, or a person who makes something clearer or easier to understand. | The photographer used an illuminator to brighten the subject's face. 摄影师用照明器照亮了拍摄对象的脸。| synonyms: light, lamp, brightener, elucidator | antonyms: darkener, obscurer | noun

illuminator:/ɪˈluːmɪneɪtər/ | n. | 照明器，照明者，阐明者 |  She acted as an illuminator, explaining the complex theory in simple terms. 她充当了一个阐明者，用简单的术语解释了这个复杂的理论。 | synonyms: light, lamp, brightener, elucidator | antonyms: darkener, obscurer | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin illuminator,  from illuminare "to enlighten," from in- "in" + luminare "to light," from lumen "light."
