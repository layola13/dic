chlorinators:/ˈklɔːrɪneɪtərz/
noun
氯化器，加氯装置
A device or substance used to add chlorine to something, typically water, for the purposes of disinfection or sanitation.
The pool maintenance crew regularly checks the chlorinators to ensure proper sanitation. 泳池维护人员定期检查加氯装置，以确保卫生。
Public water systems use chlorinators to kill harmful bacteria. 公共供水系统使用加氯装置来杀死有害细菌。
chlorinator, chlorine dispenser, chlorination system
dechlorinator
n.
