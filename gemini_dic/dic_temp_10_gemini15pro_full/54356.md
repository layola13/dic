dishonorable:/dɪsˈɑːnərəbl/ | adj. |不光彩的，不名誉的，卑鄙的 | Lacking honor; shameful or disgraceful. |  He was discharged from the army for dishonorable conduct. 他因不光彩的行为被军队开除。

| 近义词: shameful, disgraceful, ignoble, base, contemptible | 反义词: honorable, respectable, noble, virtuous | 词性: adjective

dishonor:/dɪsˈɑːnər/ | n. |耻辱，不光彩 | Lack of honor or respect; shame or disgrace. | The scandal brought dishonor upon the entire family.  这桩丑闻给整个家族带来了耻辱。 |近义词: shame, disgrace, ignominy | 反义词: honor, respect, glory |词性: noun


dishonor:/dɪsˈɑːnər/ | vt. |使丢脸，玷辱 | To deprive of honor or respect; to disgrace. | His actions dishonored his family name. 他的行为玷污了他的姓氏。 | 近义词: shame, disgrace, humiliate | 反义词: honor, respect, glorify |词性: verb
