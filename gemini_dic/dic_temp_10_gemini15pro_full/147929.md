overreligiosity:/ˌoʊvərˌrɪˌlɪdʒiˈɑːsəti/ | n. | 过度虔诚，宗教狂热 | Excessive or undue emphasis on religion or religious practices; extreme piety. |  Her overreligiosity made her family uncomfortable. 她的过度虔诚让她的家人感到不舒服。|  He displayed overreligiosity by praying loudly in public places. 他在公共场所大声祈祷，表现出宗教狂热。 | 近义词: fanaticism, zealotry, pietism | 反义词: irreligion, secularism | 词性: noun

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "religiosity" (from "religious" + "-ity" (suffix forming nouns of quality or state)).  The prefix "over-" indicates an excess or extreme degree of religiosity.
