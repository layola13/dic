divinization:/ˌdɪvɪnaɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.|神化；神化作用|The divinization of Roman emperors was a common practice. 罗马皇帝的神化是一种常见的做法。| The artist's work explored the divinization of nature. 这位艺术家的作品探索了对自然的崇拜。|近义词：deification, apotheosis, exaltation|反义词：humanization, degradation|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *divinizatio*, from Latin divinizare (“to make divine”), from divinus (“divine”), from divus (“god”).
