literati:/ˌlɪtəˈrɑːti/ | n. | 文人; 学者; (总称)知识界; | Learned people; intellectuals. |  The literati of the city gathered for the book launch.  该市的文人学者聚集在一起参加新书发布会。| synonym: intellectuals, scholars, men of letters | antonym: philistines, ignoramuses | noun

Etymology:
From Latin *lītterātus*, past participle of *lītterāre* ("to instruct in letters, educate"), from *lītterae* ("letters, literature")
