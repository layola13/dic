frosted:/ˈfrɒstɪd/ (UK) /ˈfrɔːstɪd/ (US)| adj.|磨砂的，结霜的，(头发)灰白的|Covered with frost; having a matte, opaque, or textured surface resembling frost; (of hair) streaked with grey or white.|The windows were frosted over. 窗户上结满了霜。|近义词：icy, frosty, glazed, frosted, rimed|反义词：clear, un-frosted|词性:形容词

frosted:/ˈfrɒstɪd/ (UK) /ˈfrɔːstɪd/ (US)| v.|结霜，使…模糊 (frosted, frosting)|To cover with or as if with frost; to give a matte, opaque, or textured surface resembling frost.|The cold air frosted the windows. 冷空气使窗户结霜了。|近义词：ice over, freeze, chill|反义词：thaw, melt|词性:动词
