overdilated: /ˌōvərˈdaɪˌleɪtɪd/
adjective
过度扩张的
Excessively dilated; widened beyond normal limits.
The patient's pupils were overdilated due to the medication.  病人的瞳孔由于药物作用而过度扩张。
The ophthalmologist noted that the patient's eyes were overdilated. 眼科医生注意到病人的眼睛过度扩张。
近义词:  expanded, enlarged, widened
反义词: constricted, contracted, narrowed
词性: adjective

overdilate: /ˌōvərˈdaɪˌleɪt/
verb
过度扩张
To dilate excessively; to widen beyond normal limits.
The drug can overdilate the pupils. 这种药物会使瞳孔过度扩张。
The doctor was careful not to overdilate the blood vessels. 医生小心谨慎，避免血管过度扩张。
近义词: expand, enlarge, widen
反义词: constrict, contract, narrow
词性: verb


词源：over- (prefix meaning "excessively") + dilate (from Latin dilatare, "to spread wide")
