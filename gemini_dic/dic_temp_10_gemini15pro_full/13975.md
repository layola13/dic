aureolae:/ɔːˈriːəliː/ |n.| 光环，晕轮，圣像上的光环 |A bright light or colored ring appearing to surround a luminous body, especially the sun or moon.  It can also refer to the halo depicted around the head or body of a sacred personage, especially in religious art.| The painter depicted the saint with a golden aureolae around her head. 画家描绘了这位圣人，在她头部周围画了一个金色的光环。| synonyms: halo, nimbus, corona, glory | antonyms: none | noun
