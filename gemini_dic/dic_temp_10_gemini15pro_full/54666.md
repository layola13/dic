disobligement:/ˌdɪsəˈblaɪdʒmənt/
n.
失礼;冒犯;不帮忙
The act of refusing a request or failing to do something that someone has asked you to do, especially when this causes problems for them.
Her disobligement caused him to miss an important meeting. 她的失礼让他错过了一个重要的会议。

近义词:offense, slight, disrespect, incivility
反义词:courtesy, kindness, favor, accommodation
词性:名词
