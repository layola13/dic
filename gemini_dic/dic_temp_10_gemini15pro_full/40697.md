conversionism:/kənˈvɜːrʒənɪzəm/
n.
皈依主义，转变主义
The belief or practice of religious conversion.  A focus on converting people to a particular religion or belief system.
The preacher's sermon focused on conversionism and the importance of accepting his faith.  这位传教士的布道重点是皈依主义以及接受他的信仰的重要性。
synonyms: proselytism, evangelism
antonyms: secularism, atheism, agnosticism
