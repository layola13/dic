odorisi: /oʊdəˈriːsi/ | n. | 踊る踊り子 (Odoru Odoriko) | A Japanese term referring to "The Dancing Girl," a novel by Mori Ōgai published in 1890.  It tells the story of a young Japanese student in Germany who falls in love with a German dancer. The term can also generally refer to a dancing girl. |  The novel "Odorisi" is considered a classic of Japanese literature.  踊る踊り子（おどるおどりこ）は、日本の文学の古典と考えられています。 | Synonyms: dancer, dancing girl | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
