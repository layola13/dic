graffito:/ɡrəˈfiːtoʊ/ (US), /ɡræˈfiːtəʊ/ (UK)| n.|涂鸦，（尤指）乱涂乱画|An inscription or drawing made on a wall or other surface, usually without permission.  A piece of graffiti was sprayed on the wall of the museum.  博物馆的墙上被喷上了涂鸦。|近义词：doodle, scribble, inscription|反义词: mural (when referring to authorized art)|词性:noun

graffiti:/ɡrəˈfiːtiː/ (US), /ɡræˈfiːtiː/ (UK)| n.|涂鸦（复数）|Inscriptions or drawings made on walls or other surfaces, usually without permission. The walls were covered in graffiti. 墙上满是涂鸦。|近义词：doodles, scribbles, inscriptions|反义词: murals (when referring to authorized art)|词性:noun
