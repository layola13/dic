anticlotting:/ˌæntiˈklɒtɪŋ/ /ˌæntiˈklɑːtɪŋ/| adj.|抗凝的，抗凝血的|Preventing the formation of blood clots.  It describes something that reduces the ability of blood to clot.|The patient was given anticlotting medication after the surgery. 手术后，病人服用了抗凝药物。|Synonyms: anticoagulant, antithrombotic|Antonyms: coagulant, procoagulant|adjective
