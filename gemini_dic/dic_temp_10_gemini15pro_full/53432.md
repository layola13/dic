disapplication:/ˌdɪsˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.|不适用，失效，撤销|The disapplication of a rule means it no longer applies in a specific situation.  一条规则的不适用意味着它在特定情况下不再适用。|
The judge ordered the disapplication of certain evidence. 法官下令某些证据失效。|
近义词：invalidation, revocation, annulment
反义词：application, enforcement
词性: noun

词源: dis- (not) + application (act of putting to use)
