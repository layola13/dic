marcionite:/ˈmɑːrʃəˌnaɪt/| n. adj.|马西昂派信徒，马西昂派的|A follower of Marcion, a second-century Christian theologian who rejected the Hebrew Bible and much of the New Testament.|例句：The Marcionites believed in two gods, one of the Old Testament and one of the New Testament. 马西昂派信徒相信有两个神，一个是旧约中的神，一个是新约中的神。

例句：  His beliefs were deemed heretical, and he was labeled a Marcionite.  他的信仰被认为是异端的，他被贴上了马西昂派的标签。|近义词：heretic, gnostic|反义词：orthodox|词性:名词，形容词
