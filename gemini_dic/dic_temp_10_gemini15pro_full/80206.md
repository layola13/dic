godmother:/ˈɡɒdmʌðə(r)/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːdmʌðər/ (US)| n.|教母；名义上的母亲|A woman who sponsors a person at baptism.  A female mentor or protector.|例句：My godmother gave me a beautiful necklace for my confirmation. 我的教母在我行坚振礼时送了我一条漂亮的项链。

例句：She's always been like a godmother to me, offering guidance and support. 她一直像个教母一样待我，给予我指导和支持。|近义词：sponsor, mentor, protector|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
