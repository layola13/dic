crawfish: /ˈkrɔːfɪʃ/ (US) /ˈkrɔːfɪʃ/ (UK)| n. |小龙虾；螯虾|A freshwater crustacean resembling a small lobster, with pincers on its front legs. It is also known as crayfish.|例句：We caught crawfish in the creek. 我们在小溪里抓小龙虾。

例句：Crawfish are a popular dish in Louisiana. 小龙虾是路易斯安那州的热门菜肴。|近义词：crayfish, crawdad, mudbug|反义词:  (无明确反义词)|词性: 名词
