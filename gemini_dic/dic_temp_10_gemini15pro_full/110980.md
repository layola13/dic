liquidator:/ˈlɪkwɪdeɪtər/| n. |清算人，清理者，破产管理人|A person appointed to wind up the affairs of a company or other organization, especially one that is insolvent. |The liquidators were called in to sell off the company's assets. 清算人被召来出售公司的资产。

The liquidator is responsible for distributing the remaining funds to creditors. 清算人负责将剩余资金分配给债权人。

|近义词：receiver, administrator, trustee|反义词:founder, creator|noun
