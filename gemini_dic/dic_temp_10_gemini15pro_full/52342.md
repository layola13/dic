dictate:/ˈdɪkteɪt/ (US), /dɪkˈteɪt/ (UK)| v.  n.|口述，命令，规定|to say words aloud for someone to write down or to type, or to tell someone exactly what they must do|例句：He dictated a letter to his secretary. 他向他的秘书口述了一封信。|近义词：command, order, prescribe|反义词：obey, follow|verb

dictate:/ˈdɪkteɪt/ (US), /dɪkˈteɪt/ (UK)| n.|命令，指示，规定|an order or principle that must be obeyed|例句：The dictates of fashion. 时尚的规则。|近义词：command, order, decree|反义词：request, suggestion|noun
