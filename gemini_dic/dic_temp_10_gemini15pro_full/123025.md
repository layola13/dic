monazite:/ˈmɒnəzaɪt/ (US) /ˈmɒnəzaɪt/ (UK)| n. |独居石|A reddish-brown phosphate mineral containing cerium, lanthanum, and other rare-earth elements.  It is an important ore of thorium. |Monazite is a valuable source of rare earth elements. 独居石是稀土元素的宝贵来源。| Monazite sands are often found on beaches. 独居石砂通常在海滩上发现。 | synonyms:  rare-earth mineral, phosphate mineral | antonyms: N/A | noun
