orologist:/ɔːˈrɒlədʒɪst/ /ɔːˈrɑːlədʒɪst/
n.
气象学家, 山地学家
A scientist who studies mountains, or weather and climate.
The orologist predicted heavy snowfall in the mountain regions.  气象学家预测山区会有大雪。
The orologist studied the geological formations of the Himalayas. 山地学家研究了喜马拉雅山的地质构造。
近义词：meteorologist (for weather), geomorphologist (for mountains)
反义词: N/A
词性: noun

词源: From Greek óros (“mountain”) + -logist
