infighters:/ˈɪnˌfaɪtərz/
noun
内斗者，内讧者
People who engage in fighting or disputes within a group or organization.
The infighters in the political party were causing a lot of disruption.  党内的内斗者造成了很大的混乱。
The company was weakened by infighters who cared more about personal gain than the company's success. 公司被那些更关心个人利益而不是公司成功的内斗者削弱了。
synonyms:  dissidents, rivals, adversaries, opponents
antonyms:  allies, collaborators, teammates, partners
n.
