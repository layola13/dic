macerative:/ˈmæsərətɪv/| adj.|浸软的，软化的|Causing softening or breaking down by soaking; relating to maceration.  Making something soft by soaking it in a liquid.|The chef used a macerative process to tenderize the fruit for the dessert.  厨师用浸渍法使水果变软，用于制作甜点。|Synonyms: softening, tenderizing, soaking, steeping|Antonyms: hardening, drying, dehydrating|adjective

macerate:/ˈmæsəreɪt/| v.|浸软，软化|To soften or break down by soaking; to become softened by soaking.|The strawberries macerated in sugar and balsamic vinegar overnight. 草莓在糖和香醋中浸泡了一夜。|Synonyms: soak, steep, soften, tenderize|Antonyms: dry, dehydrate, harden|verb
