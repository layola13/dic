defamer:/ˈdefəmə(r)/
n.|诽谤者，中伤者|Someone who harms another person's reputation by making false statements about them.  A person who defames. |The defamer was sued for libel. 这个诽谤者被控诽谤罪。|Synonyms: slanderer, libeler, calumniator, traducer|Antonyms: admirer, praiser, eulogizer|noun

defame:/dɪˈfeɪm/
vt.|诽谤，中伤|To harm someone's reputation by making false statements about them.|The article defamed the politician's character. 这篇文章诽谤了这位政治家的品格。|Synonyms: slander, libel, calumniate, traduce|Antonyms: praise, commend, laud|verb
