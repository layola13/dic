nonpunitive:/ˌnɒnˈpʌnɪtɪv/ /ˌnɑːnˈpjuːnɪtɪv/| adj.|非惩罚性的|Not intended as punishment; designed to encourage or correct rather than to penalize.|例句：The school adopted a nonpunitive approach to discipline, focusing on restorative justice. 学校采取了非惩罚性的纪律方法，侧重于恢复性司法。

例句：Nonpunitive measures, such as warnings and counseling, are often more effective than harsh punishments. 非惩罚性措施，如警告和咨询，通常比严厉的惩罚更有效。|近义词：lenient, merciful, forgiving, restorative|反义词：punitive, disciplinary, retributive|词性: adjective

词源:
From non- + punitive.  "Punitive" comes from the Latin word "pūnītīvus" meaning "serving for punishment,"  derived from "poena" meaning "punishment."
