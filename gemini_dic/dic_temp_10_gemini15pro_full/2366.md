adidas: /əˈdɪdəs/ | n. | 阿迪达斯 | A German multinational corporation that designs and manufactures shoes, clothing, and accessories. It is the largest sportswear manufacturer in Europe and the second largest in the world.  The name is a portmanteau of the founder's nickname, Adi (short for Adolf), and the first three letters of his last name, Dassler. | He's wearing adidas shoes. 他穿着阿迪达斯的鞋子。 | 近义词: Nike, Puma, Reebok | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
