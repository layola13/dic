alleviate:/əˈliːvieɪt/
verb
减轻，缓和
to make something bad such as pain or problems less severe
The painkillers helped to alleviate the pain. 止痛药帮助减轻了疼痛。
近义词: ease, lessen, relieve, mitigate, soothe
反义词: aggravate, worsen, exacerbate
词性:v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *alleviāre, from Latin ad- (“to, toward”) + levis (“light”).
