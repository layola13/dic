handsaw:/ˈhændˌsɔː/| n.|手锯|A saw operated by hand, used for cutting wood.|He used a handsaw to cut the plank in half. 他用一把手锯把木板锯成两半。|近义词：saw, hand saw|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps power saw) |noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hand* (hand) + *sagu* (saw), from Proto-Germanic *sagō* (saw), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)eǵʰ-* (to cut).
