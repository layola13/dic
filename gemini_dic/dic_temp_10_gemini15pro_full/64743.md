ETYMOLOGY->
From the Old French "escorter," from Italian "scortare," ultimately from Latin "excorticare," meaning "to flay, to strip off bark."  The original meaning referred to stripping an enemy of their armor or weapons, and later evolved to mean accompanying someone for protection.
