bawling:/ˈbɔːlɪŋ/
verb
大声哭泣；嚎啕大哭
Crying loudly and uncontrollably.
The baby was bawling because it was hungry.  婴儿因为饿了而大声哭闹。
The little girl was bawling her eyes out after she lost her teddy bear. 小女孩在丢失了泰迪熊后哭得稀里哗啦。
近义词: crying, sobbing, wailing, weeping
反义词: laughing, giggling, chuckling
词性: verb

bawling:/ˈbɔːlɪŋ/
noun
大声哭泣；嚎啕大哭
The act of crying loudly and uncontrollably.
The constant bawling of the child kept everyone awake.  孩子不停的哭闹让每个人都无法入睡。
We could hear the bawling of the mourners from across the street. 我们可以听到街对面送葬者的哭号声。
近义词: crying, sobbing, wailing, weeping
反义词: laughter, giggling, chuckling
词性: noun

词源:  From Middle English *bauelen, *bawlen, from Old Norse baula (“to bellow”), from Proto-Germanic *baulōną (“to bellow”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰawl- (“to bark, shout”). Cognate with Danish baule (“to bellow”), Swedish böla (“to bellow, low”), Icelandic baula (“to low”).
