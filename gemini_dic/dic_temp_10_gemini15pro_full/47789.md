decapitates:/dɪˈkæpɪteɪts/
verb
斩首
cuts off the head of (someone or something).
The executioner decapitates the condemned prisoner. 刽子手将被判死刑的囚犯斩首。

The king decapitates his enemies. 国王将他的敌人斩首。

近义词:beheads, executes, guillotines
反义词:spares

词性:verb
