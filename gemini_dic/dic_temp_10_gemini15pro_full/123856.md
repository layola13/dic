moralizing: /ˈmɔːrəlaɪzɪŋ/ | adj.  n.| 说教的，道德化的；说教 | The act of expressing moral judgments, especially in a self-righteous or tedious way. | He couldn't stand his father's constant moralizing about honesty.  他无法忍受他父亲不断地就诚实问题进行说教。 |  moralistic, preachy, didactic | amoral, non-judgmental | adjective, noun


moralize: /ˈmɔːrəlaɪz/ | v. | 说教，进行道德说教 | To comment on issues of right and wrong, typically in a way that is intended to instruct someone. | She tends to moralize about everything, even the smallest things. 她倾向于对所有事情都进行道德说教，即使是很小的事情。 | preach, sermonize, lecture |  - | verb
