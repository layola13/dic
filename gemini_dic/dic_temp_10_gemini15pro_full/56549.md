domine:/ˈdɒmɪniː/ /ˈdɑːmɪneɪ/| v.|统治，支配，控制|To rule over or control something/someone completely or authoritatively.|He seems determined to domine the conversation. 他似乎决心要主导这场谈话。|近义词：control, rule, govern, dominate|反义词:submit, obey|词性:verb
