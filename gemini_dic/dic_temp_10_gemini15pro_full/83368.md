gyromancy:/ˈdʒaɪrəʊˌmænsi/ /ˈdʒaɪroʊˌmænsi/ | n. | 陀螺占卜 | Divination by means of a spinning top or similar device. The top is spun on a circle marked with letters or numbers, and the message is deciphered based on where the top stops. |  The ancient Greeks practiced gyromancy to predict the future. 古代希腊人用陀螺占卜来预测未来。

|  Gyromancy, though less common today, was once a popular form of divination. 虽然现在不太常见，但陀螺占卜曾经是一种流行的占卜形式。 | 近义词: divination, augury | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially "scientific prediction") | 词性: noun
