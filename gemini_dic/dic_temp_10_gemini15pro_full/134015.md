nonhouseholder:/ˌnɑnˈhaʊshəʊldər/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈhaʊshəʊldə(r)/ (UK)| n.|非户主；非家庭户成员|A person who is not the head of a household or family unit.  This often refers to someone living in a shared housing situation, like a roommate, or a dependent living with their family but not considered the head of the household.|Example: The survey collected data on both householders and nonhouseholders.  该调查收集了户主和非户主的数据。

Example:  Many nonhouseholders are students living in dormitories. 许多非户主是住在宿舍的学生。|近义词：lodger, tenant, roommate, dependent|反义词：householder, head of household|词性：名词
