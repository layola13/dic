acridness:/ˈækrɪdnəs/| n.|辛辣；苦味；尖刻|The acridness of the smoke made me cough.  烟的辛辣味让我咳嗽。|The chemical spill left an acridness in the air. 化学品泄漏使空气中弥漫着一股刺鼻的气味。|近义词：bitterness, harshness, pungency, sharpness|反义词：sweetness, mildness|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *acreté* (modern French *âcreté*), from Latin *ācritūdō*, from *ācer* ("sharp, pungent, bitter").

USAGE->
The acridness of the politician's remarks offended many. 这位政客尖刻的言辞冒犯了许多人。

The acridness of the burnt toast ruined my breakfast. 烤焦的吐司的苦味毁了我的早餐。

The wine had an unpleasant acridness. 这酒有一种令人不快的苦味。

The acridness of the factory fumes made it difficult to breathe. 工厂排放的刺鼻烟雾使人难以呼吸。

The acridness of his criticism stung. 他尖刻的批评很伤人。
