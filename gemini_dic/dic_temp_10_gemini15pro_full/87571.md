heterophony:/ˌhɛtərˈɒfəni/ /ˌhɛt̬əˈrɑːfəni/ | n. |异音（同音异奏）|The simultaneous performance of slightly different versions of the same melody by two or more performers.|The traditional folk music featured beautiful heterophony, with each instrument adding its own subtle variations to the main theme. 这首传统的民间音乐以美丽的异音为特色，每件乐器都在主旋律上添加了自己微妙的变化。|近义词:polyphony, multiphony |反义词:monophony, homophony |词性:noun

heterophonic:/ˌhɛtərəˈfɒnɪk/ /ˌhɛt̬əroʊˈfɑːnɪk/ | adj. |异音的|Characterized by heterophony; consisting of simultaneous variations of a single melody.|The heterophonic texture of the music created a rich and layered sound.  这首音乐的异音织体创造了丰富而有层次的声音。|近义词:polyphonic, multiphonic |反义词:monophonic, homophonic |词性:adjective
