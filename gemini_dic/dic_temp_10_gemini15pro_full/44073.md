crinkly:/ˈkrɪŋkli/| adj.|皱巴巴的；易皱的|Having many small folds or wrinkles; wrinkled.  Characterized by a crinkling sound.|例句：The old woman's face was crinkly with age. 老妇人的脸因年老而布满皱纹。

例句：The crinkly cellophane wrapping made a satisfying sound as I tore it open.  当我撕开皱巴巴的玻璃纸包装时，发出了一种令人满足的声音。|近义词：wrinkled, creased, crumpled|反义词：smooth, flat|词性:adjective
词源:From crinkle + -y.  Ultimately from Dutch krinkel.
