non-conformist: /ˌnɒn kənˈfɔːmɪst/ (UK), /ˌnɑːn kənˈfɔːrmɪst/ (US) | n. adj. |不墨守成规的人；不遵奉英国国教的人| A person who does not conform to prevailing ideas or established practice, especially in religion.  A Protestant who did not conform to the Church of England. |  Example: He was considered a non-conformist in his time because of his unconventional beliefs. 他因为不落俗套的信仰，在他那个时代被认为是一个不墨守成规的人。 | Example:  She dressed as a non-conformist, shunning the latest fashions.  她穿着不落俗套，避开最新的时尚。 | 近义词: maverick, individualist, rebel, dissenter, iconoclast | 反义词: conformist, traditionalist | 词性: 名词, 形容词

non-conformity: /ˌnɒn kənˈfɔːmɪti/ (UK), /ˌnɑːn kənˈfɔːrmɪti/ (US) | n. |不墨守成规；不遵奉英国国教| Failure or refusal to conform to a prevailing rule or practice. | Example:  Her non-conformity made her stand out from the crowd. 她的不墨守成规使她从人群中脱颖而出。 | Example:  He was celebrated for his non-conformity in an age of conformity.  在一个墨守成规的时代，他因其不墨守成规而备受赞誉。 | 近义词: dissent, individuality, rebellion, unconventionality | 反义词: conformity, conventionality | 词性: 名词
