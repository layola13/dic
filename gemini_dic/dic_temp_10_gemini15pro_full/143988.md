outranted:/ˌaʊtˈræntɪd/
verb
胜过…的咆哮；以更大的声音压倒
outdo or defeat in ranting.
He outranted the other speakers at the rally. 他在集会上以更大的声音压倒了其他发言者。
Synonyms: outshouted, drowned out
Antonyms:  be quiet, listen


Etymology:
"outrant" comes from "out-" (a prefix meaning "to surpass or exceed") + "rant" (meaning to speak or shout at length in a wild, impassioned way).
