alkalinization:/ˌælkələnaɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.|碱化；碱化作用|The alkalinization of the soil is important for certain crops. 土壤的碱化对某些作物很重要。|The process resulted in the alkalinization of the solution. 这个过程导致溶液碱化。|近义词:neutralization, basification|反义词:acidification|noun

alkalinize:/ˈælkəlaɪnaɪz/ |v.|使碱化|Farmers sometimes alkalinize the soil to improve crop growth.  农民有时会碱化土壤以促进作物生长。|Adding lime can alkalinize the water. 添加石灰可以碱化水。|近义词:neutralize, basify|反义词:acidify|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From alkali + -ize + -ation.  Alkali ultimately comes from Arabic *al-qalīy*, "ashes of the saltwort plant".
