outfangthief:/ˈaʊtˌfæŋˌθiːf/
n.
Outlaw; a thief who is banished or outlawed.
A thief caught outside the jurisdiction of his or her lord.  The word implies a particularly egregious thief, one deserving of exile.
"The outfangthief was captured and brought to justice."  被放逐的窃贼被抓获并绳之以法。
Synonyms: bandit, brigand, robber, outlaw
Antonyms: law-abider, citizen

词性: noun
