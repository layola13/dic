machinist:/məˈʃiːnɪst/| n.|机械师，机工|A person who operates or repairs machinery, especially metal-working machinery.|The machinist carefully calibrated the lathe before starting the job. 机械师在开始工作前仔细校准了车床。|Synonyms: mechanic, operator, craftsman, artisan|Antonyms: amateur, novice|noun

machinist:/məˈʃiːnɪst/| n.|机械师，机工|A skilled worker who uses machine tools to make or repair metal parts.|The machinist created a complex part for the aircraft engine.  机械师为飞机引擎制造了一个复杂的零件。|Synonyms: toolmaker, metalworker, fitter|Antonyms: designer, engineer|noun
