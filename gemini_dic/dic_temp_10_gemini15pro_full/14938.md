Aztlán: /ɑːtsˈlɑːn/ | n. |  阿兹特兰 | The mythical ancestral home of the Aztec peoples.  It is often described as an island located in a lake or lagoon.  Its location is debated and often associated with the southwestern United States or northwestern Mexico. |  The legend of Aztlán plays a significant role in Chicano identity and culture.  |  mythical homeland, place of origin | N/A | Noun
