contraindicant:/ˌkɒntrəˈɪndɪkənt/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈɪndɪkənt/ | n. |禁忌症；禁忌征候|A contraindication is a specific situation in which a drug, procedure, or surgery should not be used because it may be harmful to the person.|This medication is contraindicated in patients with kidney disease.  这种药物禁忌用于肾病患者。|Synonym:  prohibition,  restriction|Antonym: indication |noun

contraindication:/ˌkɒntrəˌɪndɪˈkeɪʃn/ /ˌkɑːntrəˌɪndɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. |禁忌症候；禁忌证|A contraindication is a specific situation in which a drug, procedure, or surgery should not be used because it may be harmful to the person.|Pregnancy is a contraindication for this medication. 怀孕是这种药物的禁忌症。|Synonym:  prohibition,  restriction|Antonym: indication |noun
