epitaphian:/ˌepɪˈtæfiən/
adjective
墓志铭的;适合做墓志铭的;（尤指）像墓志铭一样简短而深刻的
of, relating to, or resembling an epitaph; especially :brief and pithy like an epitaph
His final words were suitably epitaphian. 他的临终遗言非常适合作为墓志铭。
The inscription on the tombstone was brief and epitaphian. 墓碑上的铭文简短而深刻。
近义词:inscriptional, commemorative, monumental
反义词:prosaic, mundane, verbose
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *epitaphianus, from Ancient Greek ἐπιτάφιος (epitáphios, “funerary, relating to a funeral”)
