micrometrical: /ˌmaɪkrəˈmɛtrɪk(ə)l/
| adj. | 微米的，精密的 | Relating to small measurements, typically on the scale of a micrometer (one millionth of a meter); extremely precise or meticulous. |  The micrometrical adjustments on the telescope allowed for incredibly accurate observations. 这台望远镜上的微米级调整允许进行极其精确的观测。| synonyms: precise, exact, accurate, meticulous, fine, minute | antonyms: imprecise, inaccurate, rough, approximate | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From micro- (small) + -metrical (relating to measurement). Ultimately from Greek mikros (small) and metron (measure).
