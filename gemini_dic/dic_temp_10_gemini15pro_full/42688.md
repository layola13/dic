countersway:/ˈkaʊntərsweɪ/| n. v.|平衡力，抵消；抵消，抗衡|An opposing influence or force that balances or checks another.|The dancer used a countersway to maintain balance during the spin. 舞蹈演员用平衡力在旋转过程中保持平衡。|近义词：counterbalance, offset, counteract|反义词：reinforce, augment|词性:名词，动词


Etymology:
From counter- + sway.  "Counter-" indicates opposition or contrast, and "sway" refers to movement or influence.
