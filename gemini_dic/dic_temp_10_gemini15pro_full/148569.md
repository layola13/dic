overtakers:/ˌoʊvərˈteɪkərz/| n. | 超车者；赶超者 |  People who overtake other vehicles or individuals.  Those who pass others, often in a race or competition, but also in other contexts like business or personal development. | The overtakers sped past the slower cars on the highway.  高速公路上，超车者飞速超过了速度较慢的车辆。

The overtakers in the technology industry are often innovative startups.  科技行业的赶超者通常是具有创新精神的初创企业。 | 近义词：passers, leaders | 反义词：laggards, followers | 词性: noun

词源:  From "overtake" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Overtake" itself comes from "over-" (meaning "above" or "beyond") + "take" (meaning "to seize" or "to capture").  This suggests the idea of going beyond or passing someone.
