firebricks:/ˈfaɪərbrɪks/| n.|耐火砖|A brick capable of withstanding high temperatures, typically used in lining furnaces, fireplaces, and other high-heat applications.|The kiln was lined with firebricks to withstand the intense heat.  窑炉内衬耐火砖以承受高温。|Synonyms: refractory brick, fireclay brick|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps regular brick in a non-high-heat context)|Noun
