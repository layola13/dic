ossiculated: /ˈɒsɪkjʊleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 骨化的；变成骨的 | Having become bone or bony; hardened into bone. |  The ossiculated skeleton of the ancient fish was remarkably well-preserved.  这具古代鱼类的骨化骨骼保存得非常好。 | Example 2: The ligament had ossiculated over time due to the injury. 由于受伤，韧带随着时间的推移骨化了。 | 近义词: ossified, hardened, calcified | 反义词: cartilaginous, soft | 词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *ossiculatus, past participle of ossiculare (“to form bone”), from ossiculum (“small bone”), diminutive of os (“bone”).
