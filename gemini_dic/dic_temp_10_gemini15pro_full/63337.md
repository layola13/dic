ento-/ˈɛntoʊ-/
prefix
内部；在内；进入
within; inner; into
The prefix "ento-" signifies "within," "inner," or "into."  It's often used in scientific terminology.
Entomology is the study of insects. 昆虫学是研究昆虫的学科。
An endoparasite lives inside its host.  体内寄生虫生活在其宿主体内。
近义词: endo-, intra-
反义词: ecto-, extra-
词性: prefix
