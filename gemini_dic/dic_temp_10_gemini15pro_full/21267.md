bloodhound:/ˈblʌdhaʊnd/| n. |猎犬；侦探，警犬|A breed of dog with an exceptional sense of smell, used for tracking. Also used figuratively to describe someone who is persistent in pursuing a goal or investigation.|例句：The police used a bloodhound to track the escaped convict. 警察用猎犬追踪逃犯。

例句：He's a real bloodhound when it comes to researching his family history.  他在研究家族史方面真是个侦探。|近义词：sleuth, detective, tracker dog|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English 'blodhund', literally 'blood-hound', not in the sense of a dog which hunts blood, but a dog of pure or unmixed breed.
