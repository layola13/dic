converge:/kənˈvɜːrdʒ/ (US), /kənˈvɜːdʒ/ (UK)| v. |汇聚，集中，趋同|To come together from different directions and meet at a single point; to become similar or identical.|The two rivers converge at this point. 这两条河流在此处汇合。|Synonyms: meet, join, unite, intersect, merge, coalesce|Antonyms: diverge, separate, deviate|verb

converge:/kənˈvɜːrdʒ/ (US), /kənˈvɜːdʒ/ (UK)| v. |(意见、政策等)趋于一致|To become more similar or to agree about something.|Our views on this issue are beginning to converge. 我们在这个问题上的观点开始趋于一致。|Synonyms: coincide, agree, correspond, conform|Antonyms: differ, disagree, diverge|verb
