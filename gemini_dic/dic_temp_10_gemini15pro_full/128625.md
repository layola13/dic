nickelic:/nɪˈkelɪk/
adjective
含镍的；三价镍的
Containing or relating to nickel, especially with valence 3.
Nickelic oxide is a black powder.  氧化镍是一种黑色粉末。
Nickelic hydroxide is insoluble in water. 氢氧化镍不溶于水。
Near Synonyms: nickel, nickelous
Antonyms: N/A (Lack of a direct chemical antonym)
词性: adjective
