cerographist: /sɪˈrɒɡrəfɪst/ | n. |蜡版画家，蜡刻师 | A person who makes engravings on wax; an artist who specializes in cerography (a method of engraving on wax). |  Cerographists were highly skilled in creating intricate designs on wax tablets. 蜡版画家非常擅长在蜡板上创作复杂的图案。|  The cerographist carefully etched the portrait onto the wax surface.  这位蜡刻师小心翼翼地在蜡面上刻画肖像。 | engraver, etcher | sculptor, painter | noun
