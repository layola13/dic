flavoring:/ˈfleɪvərɪŋ/ /ˈfleɪvərɪŋ/ | n. |调味品；调料；香料|A substance added to food or drink to give it a particular flavor. |例句：This soup needs a bit more flavoring. 这汤需要加一点调味料。

例句：Artificial flavorings are often used in processed foods. 加工食品中经常使用人工香料。|近义词：seasoning, spice, condiment, additive|反义词: blandness |词性:noun
