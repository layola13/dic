enunciatory:/ɪˈnʌnʃiətɔːri/ /ɪˈnʌnsieɪtɔːri/| adj.|阐述的；声明的；宣言的|Relating to or characterized by enunciation; declarative.|His enunciatory style of speaking was clear and precise. 他清晰而精确的阐述风格令人印象深刻。|The president delivered an enunciatory address outlining his plans for the next four years. 总统发表了阐述未来四年计划的宣言。|近义词：declarative, assertive, pronounce, articulate, expressive|反义词：mumbling, inarticulate, unclear|词性:adjective
Etymology->
From Late Latin ēnūntiātōrius, from ēnūntiāre  "to disclose, reveal, pronounce, utter,"  from ex- "out" + nūntiāre "to announce, report," from nūntius "messenger."
