adragant:/ˈædrəɡænt/
n.
黄蓍胶，黄蓍树胶
A gum obtained from several species of Astragalus, a low, spiny, branching shrub of the Middle East.  It is used as a thickening agent in textile printing, in the preparation of candies and other foods, and in pharmaceuticals.
The adragant is used as a stabilizer in ice cream.  这种黄蓍胶用作冰淇淋中的稳定剂。

The pharmacist used adragant to compound the medicine. 药剂师用黄蓍树胶配制药物。
