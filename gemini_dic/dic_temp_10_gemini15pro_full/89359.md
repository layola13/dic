homomorphy:/ˌhəʊməˈmɔːfiː/ /ˌhoʊməˈmɔːrfi/
homomorphy|n.|同形异义; 同形性; 同态|A homomorphy is a similarity in form or structure, often referring to words that look the same but have different meanings or origins.|The words "lead" (the metal) and "lead" (to guide) are examples of homomorphy.|近义词:homomorphism, analogy|反义词:heteromorphy|词性:名词

homomorphy:/ˌhəʊməˈmɔːfiː/ /ˌhoʊməˈmɔːrfi/
homomorphy|n.|同形; 同形性|In biology, homomorphy refers to similarity in form or structure.|The wings of bats and birds show homomorphy, despite evolving independently.|近义词:similarity, resemblance|反义词:heteromorphy, dissimilarity|词性:名词
