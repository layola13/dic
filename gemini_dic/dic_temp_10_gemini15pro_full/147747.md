overproduction:/ˌoʊvərprəˈdʌkʃən/ | /ˌəʊvəprəˈdʌkʃən/ | n. | 过度生产；生产过剩 | The overproduction of goods led to a drop in prices. 商品的过度生产导致价格下跌。 |  Excessive production beyond demand or beyond the capacity to sell or consume.  The factory's overproduction resulted in a large surplus of inventory. 工厂的过度生产导致了大量库存过剩。 | oversupply, surplus | undersupply, shortage | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + production.  "Over-" signifies "excessive" or "too much". "Production" comes from the Latin "prōdūctiō" meaning "a leading forth" or "a bringing out".
