chivalrously:/ˈʃɪvəlrəsli/
adverb
骑士般地; 勇敢地; 豪侠地;
In a manner befitting a knight; gallantly and courteously.
He behaved chivalrously, opening doors and offering her his coat. 他表现得很有骑士风度，为她开门，并把外套给她披上。
She chivalrously defended her friend's honor. 她侠义地捍卫了朋友的荣誉。
近义词:gallantly, courteously, bravely, honorably
反义词:rudely, cowardly, dishonorably
词性:adverb
词源:From chivalry + -ous + -ly. Ultimately from French chevalier.
