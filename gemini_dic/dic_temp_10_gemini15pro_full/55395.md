distendible:/dɪˈstɛndɪbl/
adjective
可扩张的，可膨胀的
Capable of being stretched or expanded.
The stomach is a distendible organ. 胃是一个可扩张的器官。
A balloon is distendible.  气球是可膨胀的。
近义词: expandable, stretchable, inflatable
反义词: inextensible, nondistensible
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *distendibilis, from Latin distendere (“to stretch out, expand”), from dis- (“apart, asunder”) + tendere (“to stretch”).
