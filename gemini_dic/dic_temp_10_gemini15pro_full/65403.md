euphonicon:/juːˈfɒnɪkɒn/| n. |优音词；悦耳的词|A word that is pleasant to the ear, due to its harmonious combination of sounds.|Her name, "Melliflua," was a true euphonicon, flowing smoothly off the tongue.|近义词：mellifluous word, harmonious word|反义词:cacophonous word, dissonant word|词性:noun
Etymology: From Greek *euphōnos* "well-sounding" (from *eu-* "well" + *phōnē* "sound, voice") + *-ikon*, neuter singular of the Greek adjective suffix *-ikos*.
