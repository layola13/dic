backlash:/ˈbæklæʃ/| n.|强烈抵制；强烈反对；反冲；后坐力|A strong negative reaction by a large number of people, especially to a social or political change.|The government is facing a backlash from voters over its economic policies. 政府正面临选民对其经济政策的强烈反对。|synonyms: reaction, resistance, opposition, recoil, kickback|antonyms: support, acceptance|noun

backlash:/ˈbæklæʃ/| n.|反冲；后坐力（机械装置的）|A sudden violent backward movement or reaction.|There was a backlash from the engine as it started. 引擎启动时发生了一次反冲。|synonyms: recoil, kickback|antonyms: forward motion|noun
