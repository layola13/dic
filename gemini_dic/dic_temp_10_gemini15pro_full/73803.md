formativeness:/ˈfɔːrmətɪvnəs/
n.|形成性，形成力|The quality of being formative; the power to form or mold.|The teacher emphasized the formativeness of early childhood experiences. 老师强调了幼儿时期经历的形成性作用。|Synonyms: influence, shaping, molding|Antonyms: unimportance, insignificance|noun

formative:/ˈfɔːrmətɪv/
adj.|形成的，塑造的|Relating to development or growth; influential in shaping character.|The formative years of a child are crucial for their future development.  一个孩子的形成时期对他们未来的发展至关重要。|Synonyms: developmental, influential, crucial|Antonyms: uninfluential, insignificant|adjective
