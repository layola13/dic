conker:/ˈkɒŋkə(r)/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋkər/ (US)| n.| 七叶树果实（玩斗果实游戏用），斗果实游戏|A hard, shiny, brown nut that grows on horse chestnut trees, used by children in a game.|例句：We collected conkers in the autumn and played conkers. 我们在秋天收集七叶树果实，玩斗果实游戏。

例句：He won the conker championship. 他赢得了斗果实比赛冠军。|近义词：horse chestnut, buckeye (US)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
