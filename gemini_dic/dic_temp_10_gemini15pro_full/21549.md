bluejay:/ˈbluːdʒeɪ/| n.|蓝鸟|A North American bird known for its bright blue plumage, noisy calls, and intelligent behavior.  It is a member of the corvid family, which also includes crows and ravens.|例句：A bluejay swooped down and snatched a peanut from the feeder.  一只蓝鸟俯冲下来，从喂食器里叼走了一颗花生。

例句：The bluejay's distinctive crest makes it easily recognizable.  蓝鸟独特的羽冠使它很容易辨认。|近义词：jaybird|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "blue" + "jay," with "jay" ultimately from Old French "jai."  The name reflects the bird's vibrant blue color.
