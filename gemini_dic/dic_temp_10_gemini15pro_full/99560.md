interhybridized:/ˌɪntərˈhaɪbrɪdaɪzd/ | adj. | 杂交的，相互杂交的 |  Formed by hybridization between two or more already hybrid forms. Created by crossing different hybrids.  | The interhybridized roses exhibited a stunning array of colors. 这些杂交的玫瑰展现出一系列令人惊艳的色彩。 | The interhybridized orchids produced unusual flower shapes. 这些杂交的兰花开出了奇特的花形。 | 近义词: crossbred, hybrid, intercrossed | 反义词: purebred, inbred | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
inter- (prefix): between, among (Latin)
hybrid (noun/adjective): offspring of two different species or varieties (Latin hybrida "mongrel offspring")
-ize (suffix): to make or become; to subject to a process (from Greek -izein)
