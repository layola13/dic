concameration:/kɒnˌkæməˈreɪʃn/
n.
拱形结构，穹顶；(尤指)穹顶状结构
An arched structure, a vault; especially, a vaulted structure.
The concameration of the church's ceiling was breathtaking. 教堂天花板的拱形结构令人叹为观止。
近义词: vault, dome, arch
反义词: flatness


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *concameratio*, from concamerare "to vault, arch over," from com- "together" + camera "vault, arched chamber."
