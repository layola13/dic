cariopsis:/kɛrɪˈɒpsɪs/ /kærɪˈɑːpsɪs/
noun
颖果
A dry one-seeded fruit in which the ovary wall is united with the seed coat, characteristic of grasses and cereals.
The wheat cariopsis is the source of flour for bread making. 小麦颖果是制作面包面粉的来源。

caryopsis:/kɛrɪˈɒpsɪs/ /kærɪˈɑːpsɪs/
n.
颖果
A dry, indehiscent, one-seeded fruit in which the pericarp is fused to the thin seed coat.  This type of fruit is characteristic of the grass family (Poaceae or Gramineae).
Barley, rice, and corn all produce cariopsises. 大麦、水稻和玉米都产生颖果。
