correlation:/ˌkɔːrəˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 相关性，关联 | a mutual relationship or connection between two or more things | There is a strong correlation between smoking and lung cancer. 吸烟和肺癌之间有很强的相关性。 | 近义词: relationship, association, connection, link | 反义词: independence, disassociation | noun

correlation:/ˌkɔːrəˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 相互关系 | the degree to which two or more things tend to vary together |  The study found no correlation between income and happiness.  这项研究发现收入和幸福之间没有关联。 | 近义词: interdependence, interaction | 反义词:  lack of relationship | noun
