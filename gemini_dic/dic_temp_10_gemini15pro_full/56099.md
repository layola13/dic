doctorer:/ˈdɑːktərər/ /ˈdɒktərə(r)/| n.|修补者；窜改者；庸医|A person who alters something improperly or deceptively; a person who treats something superficially or incompetently, especially someone who claims medical skill without proper qualifications.|例句：The art doctorer tried to pass off the forgery as a genuine masterpiece.  这个艺术品修补者试图把赝品冒充真品杰作。

例句：He was branded a doctorer for his questionable medical practices. 他因其可疑的医疗行为而被冠以庸医之名。|近义词：faker, charlatan, quack, tamperer, manipulator|反义词:expert, professional, specialist|词性:名词
