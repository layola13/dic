crowding:/ˈkraʊdɪŋ/| n.|拥挤，挤满；群集|The crowding in the subway made it difficult to breathe. 地铁里的拥挤让人难以呼吸。|例句：The crowding at the concert was intense.  演唱会上人山人海。

The market was filled with the crowding of shoppers during the holiday season. 假日期间，市场里挤满了购物者。|近义词：congestion, overcrowding, thronging|反义词:emptiness, sparseness|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From crowd (v.) + -ing (suffix forming present participles and gerunds).  The verb "crowd" originates from Old English "crudan" meaning "to press, urge, hasten".
