dropworm:/ˈdrɑːpwɜːrm/| n.|尺蠖，吊丝虫|A larva of a geometer moth, which moves by looping its body.|The dropworm inched its way along the branch. 尺蠖沿着树枝慢慢移动。|近义词：inchworm, looper, measuring worm|反义词: None|词性:noun

Etymology:  "drop" + "worm". "Drop" refers to the way the caterpillar seems to drop from branches when disturbed, hanging suspended by a silken thread. "Worm" is a general term for a crawling creature.
