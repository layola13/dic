edenite:/ˈɛdənˌaɪt/ | n. | 伊甸石 | A complex silicate mineral of the amphibole group, typically dark green or black in color.  It is named after Edenville, New York, where it was first discovered. |  Edenite is a common mineral found in metamorphic rocks. 伊甸石是一种常见的变质岩矿物。|  The geologist identified the sample as edenite. 地质学家将样本鉴定为伊甸石。 | Synonyms: amphibole, hornblende | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
