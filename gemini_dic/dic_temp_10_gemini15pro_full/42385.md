counterblockades:/ˌkaʊntərblɑːˈkeɪdz/ | n. |反封锁，对抗封锁| An action taken to prevent or obstruct a blockade. It involves setting up a blockade against the party that initiated the first blockade.| The navy launched counterblockades against the enemy's ports. 海军对敌人的港口实施了反封锁。|Synonyms: countermeasures, retaliation |Antonyms: blockade, siege| noun
