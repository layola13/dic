mouldiness:/ˈməʊldɪnəs/ | n. | 发霉；霉味；霉烂 | The mouldiness in the basement was overwhelming.  地下室的霉味很重。 | mouldiness is the state of being covered with or smelling of mould. | The old books had a distinct mouldiness to them. 这些旧书有一股明显的霉味。 | 近义词：mustiness, mildew, dampness | 反义词：freshness, cleanness | 词性：名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *moldeynes, equivalent to mouldy +‎ -ness.
