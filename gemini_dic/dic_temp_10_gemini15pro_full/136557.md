nonsacrifices:/ˌnɒnˈsækrɪfaɪsɪz/ | n. | 非牺牲，不牺牲的事物 |  Things that do not involve sacrifice; actions or objects that are not given up or lost for a greater purpose.  It is formed by the prefix "non-" (meaning not) and the noun "sacrifices". |  Keeping the business afloat required some nonsacrifices, like reducing marketing spend but not employee salaries. 保持业务运转需要一些非牺牲，比如减少营销支出，但不减少员工工资。

The company’s success was due in part to its focus on nonsacrifices, like improving efficiency rather than cutting jobs. 公司的成功部分归功于它对非牺牲的关注，比如提高效率而不是裁员。 | 近义词: concessions, compromises, adjustments | 反义词: sacrifices, offerings, immolations | 词性: noun
