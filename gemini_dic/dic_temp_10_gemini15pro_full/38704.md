condescending:/ˌkɒndɪˈsendɪŋ/ /ˌkɑːndɪˈsendɪŋ/| adj.|居高临下的，屈尊俯就的|Showing or characterized by a patronizing or superior attitude.|例句：He spoke in a condescending tone, as if explaining something to a child. 他说话的语气很 condescending，好像在给小孩解释什么。

例句：She gave me a condescending smile, which made me feel even smaller. 她居高临下地对我笑了笑，这让我感觉更渺小了。|近义词：patronizing, supercilious, haughty|反义词：humble, respectful, egalitarian|词性:adjective

condescend:/ˌkɒndɪˈsend/ /ˌkɑːndɪˈsend/| v.|屈尊，俯就|To behave in a way that shows that you consider yourself to be superior to other people.|例句：He condescended to explain the rules to us. 他屈尊给我们解释规则。

例句：Would you condescend to join us for dinner? 你愿意屈尊和我们一起共进晚餐吗？|近义词：deign, stoop, patronize|反义词：respect, honor|词性:verb
