calamitous:/kəˈlæmɪtəs/
adjective
灾难性的；悲惨的
involving calamity; disastrous or catastrophic.
The calamitous earthquake destroyed the entire city.  灾难性的地震摧毁了整个城市。
The ship met a calamitous end, sinking in a storm. 这艘船遭遇了悲惨的结局，在暴风雨中沉没了。
近义词: disastrous, catastrophic, tragic, devastating, ruinous
反义词: fortunate, lucky, beneficial, advantageous, auspicious
词性: adjective
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin calamitosus, from Latin calamitas ‘disaster,’ from calamus (see haulm).
