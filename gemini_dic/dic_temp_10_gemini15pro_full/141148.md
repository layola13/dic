oogenesis: /ˌoʊəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
n.
卵子发生；卵子生成
The formation and maturation of an ovum.
Oogenesis is the process by which female gametes, or ova, are created. 卵子发生是产生雌性配子或卵子的过程。
The hormones play a crucial role in oogenesis. 激素在卵子发生中起着至关重要的作用。
近义词: ovogenesis
反义词: spermatogenesis
词性: noun
