hacqueton:/ˈhækɪtən/| n. |短上衣，一种中世纪的厚衬衣|A hacqueton was a padded defensive jacket worn under armour in the Middle Ages.  它是一种在中世纪穿在盔甲下面的厚衬垫防御性夹克。|The knight donned his hacqueton before his chainmail.  骑士在穿上锁子甲之前先穿上了他的短上衣。|Synonyms:  padded jack, doublet, jerkin|Antonyms:  N/A|noun
