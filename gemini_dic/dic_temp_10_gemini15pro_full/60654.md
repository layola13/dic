elections:/ɪˈlɛkʃənz/| n.|选举|A formal and organized process of choosing a person for a political office or other position by voting.|The upcoming elections are crucial for the country's future. 即将举行的选举对国家的未来至关重要.|synonyms: polls, balloting, voting|antonyms: appointment, nomination|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *election, from Latin ēlectiō (“a choosing out, choice, selection”), from ēlegō (“choose out, select”), from ē- (“out”) + legō (“choose, gather, select”).
