ballastage: /ˈbæləstɪdʒ/ | n. |压载，压舱物；（尤指船舶的）稳定；安定（因素）| Material, such as gravel, sand, or iron, placed low in a vessel to improve its stability.  Also, figuratively, something that gives stability or steadiness. | The ship took on ballastage in the harbor before setting sail. 这艘船在启航前在港口装载了压舱物。

The ballastage of his temperament was his quiet confidence. 他性情的稳定因素是他那份沉静的自信。 | synonyms: ballast, counterweight, stabilizer | antonyms: instability, volatility | noun


ballastage: /ˈbæləstɪdʒ/ | v. | 给…加压载，装压舱物 | To provide or load with ballast. | They ballasted the ship with rocks and sand. 他们用岩石和沙子给船加压载。

The hot air balloon was ballasted with sandbags. 热气球用沙袋加压载。 | synonyms: stabilize, steady | antonyms: destabilize | verb
