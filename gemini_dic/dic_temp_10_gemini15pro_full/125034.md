mula:/ˈmuːlə/| n.|基础；根基；本源；起源；资金；资本|The mula for the project came from a private investor.  这个项目的资金来自一位私人投资者。|Example:  We need more mula to get this business off the ground. 我们需要更多资金来启动这项业务。|Synonyms: foundation, basis, origin, source, capital, funds|Antonyms: result, outcome|Noun


Etymology: From Sanskrit मूल (mūla) meaning "root," "basis," or "foundation."  Borrowed into Pali and Prakrit, eventually making its way into American slang via Romani.
