outgabbles:/ˌaʊtˈɡæbəlz/
verb
胜过(某人)的饶舌；夸夸其谈地胜过
outdo someone in garrulous or boastful talk.
He outgabbled his opponent in the debate, leaving them speechless. 他在辩论中滔滔不绝地胜过了对手，让他们哑口无言。
Synonyms: outtalk, outbluster
Antonyms: be quiet, listen
词性: 动词
