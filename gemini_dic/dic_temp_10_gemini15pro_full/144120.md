outscorn:/ˌaʊtˈskɔːrn/
verb
胜过，比…得分更高
To surpass or defeat someone in scoring or achieving a higher score.
The rookie outscored the veteran in the final game.  这个新秀在决赛中得分超过了老将。
outshine, outdo, surpass, beat
be outscored by, lose to
