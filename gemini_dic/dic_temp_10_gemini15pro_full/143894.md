outpourings:/ˈaʊtpɔːrɪŋz/
noun
流露；倾泻；迸发
an unrestrained expression of feelings or opinions.
例句：There was an outpouring of grief after the death of the beloved leader.  在敬爱的领袖去世后，人们悲痛万分。

例句：The charity received an outpouring of donations after the disaster.  灾难发生后，慈善机构收到了大量的捐款。
synonym:effusions, expressions, outbursts, discharges, releases
antonym:suppressions, withholdings
词性:n.
