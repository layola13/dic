mindednesses:/ˈmaɪndɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
心态，思想倾向
A general attitude or disposition of mind.
The various mindsets of the students were apparent in their responses. 学生们不同的心态在他们的回答中显而易见。
近义词:mentalities, attitudes, outlooks, dispositions
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) apathy, indifference


ETYMOLOGY->
From "minded" (having a specified kind of mind or disposition) + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition).  "Minded" comes from Old English "gemyndig," meaning "mindful," related to "mind."
