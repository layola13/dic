imploringness:/ɪmˈplɔːrɪŋnɪs/ | n. |恳求， imploring 的名词形式| The imploringness in her eyes made it difficult to refuse her request. 她眼神里的恳求让人难以拒绝她的请求。|The child's imploringness for a candy bar was hard to resist. 孩子恳求一块糖果的样子难以抗拒。| 近义词: supplication, entreaty, pleading | 反义词: demand, command | 词性: 名词

词源:  From implore + -ing + -ness. Implore comes from Old French *implorer, from Latin implōrāre “to invoke with tears, beseech,” from in- “in, on” + plōrāre “to weep, cry out.”
