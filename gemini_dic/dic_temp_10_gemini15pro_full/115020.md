mandible:/ˈmændɪbl/ (US), /ˈmændɪbəl/ (UK)| n.|下颌骨；(昆虫等的)上颚|The mandible is the lower jawbone in vertebrates and the upper jaw of insects. 下颌骨是脊椎动物的下颚骨，也是昆虫的上颚骨。|The boxer broke his opponent's mandible with a powerful right hook. 那位拳击手用一记强有力的右勾拳打断了对手的下颌骨。|Synonyms: jawbone, jaw|Antonyms: N/A|noun

mandible:/ˈmændɪbl/ (US), /ˈmændɪbəl/ (UK)| adj. (rare)|下颌的；上颚的|Mandibular movements are essential for chewing. 下颌运动对咀嚼至关重要。|The mandibular nerve controls the muscles of the jaw. 下颌神经控制着下颌肌肉。|Synonyms: jaw, gnathic|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
