multivalence:/ˌmʌltɪˈveɪləns/ | n. |多价; 多重意义; 多价性|The state or quality of having multiple meanings, interpretations, or values.|The poem’s multivalence makes it a source of endless fascination for scholars.|近义词：polysemy, ambiguity, pluralism|反义词：univocality, singularity|noun
