ETYMOLOGY->
From French *fontanelle*, diminutive of *fontaine* ("fountain, spring"), from Old French *fontaine*, from Late Latin *fontāna* ("spring, source"), from Latin *fōns* ("fountain, spring").  The anatomical term likely arose from the perceived pulsating of the fontanel, reminiscent of a spring.
