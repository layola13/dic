daffily:/ˈdæfɪli/| adv.|愚蠢地，傻傻地|In a silly, frivolous, or foolish manner.  Characterized by lighthearted silliness.|She danced daffily around the room, singing at the top of her lungs. 她傻傻地绕着房间跳舞，大声唱歌。|近义词：foolishly, giddily, ridiculously, sillily|反义词：sensibly, seriously, soberly|词性:副词

Etymology:  From *daffy* (adjective) + *-ly* (adverbial suffix). *Daffy* is of uncertain origin, but is likely related to *daft*, meaning “mildly insane; foolish.”  Both words likely ultimately derive from a Scandinavian source.
