numbskulled:/ˈnʌmskʌld/| adj.|愚蠢的，笨的|Lacking intelligence or common sense; stupid.|例句：He made a numbskulled decision to invest all his money in a single stock. 他愚蠢地决定把所有的钱都投资在一支股票上。

例句：Don't be so numbskulled! Think before you act. 别那么笨！三思而后行。|近义词：stupid, foolish, dense, dimwitted, doltish|反义词：intelligent, bright, clever, smart, astute|词性:形容词
