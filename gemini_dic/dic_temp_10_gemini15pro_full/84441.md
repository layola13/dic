hallways:/ˈhɔːlweɪz/ (US) /ˈhɔːlweɪz/ (UK)
n.
走廊，过道
A long, narrow passage inside a building, with doors leading off it into rooms.
The students were chatting in the hallways between classes.  学生们在课间在走廊里聊天。
The hotel hallways were dimly lit.  酒店的走廊灯光昏暗。
corridors, passages
rooms, chambers
noun
