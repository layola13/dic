Celsius: /ˈselsiəs/ | adj. n. |摄氏的；摄氏度| Relating to the Celsius temperature scale, in which 0° represents the freezing point of water and 100° represents the boiling point of water. | Water boils at 100 degrees Celsius. 水在100摄氏度沸腾。|  Synonym: centigrade | Antonym: Fahrenheit | adjective, noun
