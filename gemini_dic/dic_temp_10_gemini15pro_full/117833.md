mellifluousness:/ˌmɛlɪˈfluːəsnəs/| n. |流畅；甜美；悦耳|The mellifluousness of her voice charmed the audience. |例句：The mellifluousness of his voice soothed the crying baby.  他的声音甜美流畅，安抚了哭闹的婴儿。

例句：The mellifluousness of the poetry made it easy to memorize.  这首诗流畅悦耳，很容易记住。|近义词：euphony, sweetness, smoothness, harmony|反义词：cacophony, harshness, dissonance|词性:名词

词源: From Latin mellifluus (“flowing with honey”), from mel (“honey”) + fluere (“to flow”).
