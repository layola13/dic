half-days:/ˌhɑːf ˈdeɪz/
| n.|半天；半天上课/上班|A period of time lasting half a day, typically used in the context of work or school.|We have half-days on Fridays during the summer.  我们夏天星期五是半天上课/上班。|Synonym: half-day (singular)|Antonym: full days|Noun

half-days:/ˌhɑːf ˈdeɪz/
| n.|半天假|A period of time off work or school lasting half a day.|I took a half-days vacation on Friday. 我星期五休了半天假。|Synonym: half-day leave|Antonym: full-day leave|Noun
