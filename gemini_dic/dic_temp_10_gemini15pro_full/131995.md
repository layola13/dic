noncoordinating:/ˌnɒnkoʊˈɔːrdɪneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非协调的，非对等的
Not coordinating; not of equal rank, importance, or function.
Noncoordinating conjunctions like "although" and "because" connect two clauses of unequal importance.  虽然和因为这样的非协调连词连接两个重要性不相等的从句。
The noncoordinating committee members felt their opinions weren't valued. 非协调委员会的成员觉得他们的意见不被重视。
近义词:subordinate, dependent
反义词:coordinating, independent
词性:adjective
