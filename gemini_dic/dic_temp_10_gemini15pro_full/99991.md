interparochialnesses:/ˌɪntərˌpærˈoʊkiəlˌnɛsɪz/
noun
堂区间性；跨堂区的性质
The state or quality of being interparochial.  Relating to or involving more than one parish.
The interparochialnesses of the dispute made it difficult to resolve. 这个争端的跨教区性质使其难以解决。
The council addressed the interparochialnesses of the shared resources. 理事会讨论了共享资源的跨教区问题。
Synonyms: inter-parish, cross-parochial
Antonyms: intraparochial
词性: noun
