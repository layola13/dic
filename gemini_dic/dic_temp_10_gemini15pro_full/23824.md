breached:/briːtʃt/| v. adj.|违反，突破；被攻破的|To break or violate a law, agreement, or code of conduct; to make a hole or gap in something, especially a wall or defense.|例句：The company breached the contract by failing to deliver the goods on time.  这家公司因未能按时交货而违反了合同。

例句：The hackers breached the company's security system.  黑客入侵了公司的安全系统。|近义词：violated, broken, infringed, penetrated|反义词：obeyed, upheld, honored, defended|词性:动词

breached:/briːtʃt/| adj.|被攻破的，有缺口的|Having been broken or violated; having a hole or gap.|例句：The breached dam caused widespread flooding.  决堤的大坝造成了大范围的洪水。

例句：The breached contract led to a lengthy legal battle.  违约合同导致了漫长的法律诉讼。|近义词：broken, damaged, ruptured, violated|反义词：intact, undamaged, secure, defended|词性:形容词
