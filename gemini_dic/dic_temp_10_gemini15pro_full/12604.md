assailers:/əˈseɪlərz/| n. |攻击者，袭击者|People who attack someone or something physically or verbally. |The castle was overrun by the assailants. 城堡被攻击者占领了。|
The journalist was harassed by online assailants. 这名记者受到网络攻击者的骚扰。|
Synonyms: attackers, aggressors, assaulters, raiders|
Antonyms: defenders, protectors, guardians|
