fastigiate:/fæˈstɪdʒiˌeɪt/| adj.| 尖顶的，束状的| Having branches that are erect and close to the stem, so that the crown has a narrow or columnar shape. | The Lombardy poplar is known for its fastigiate form.  伦巴第杨树以其尖顶形状而闻名。

The fastigiate branches of the tree reached towards the sky.  这棵树的束状树枝伸向天空。|近义词：columnar, erect, upright|反义词：spreading, horizontal|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fastigiatus*, past participle of *fastigiare* "to bring to a point, to slope up to a point," from *fastigium* "roof, gable, top, summit, highest point, slope, elevation," from *fastus* "haughtiness, arrogance, pride," of uncertain origin.
