carson:/ˈkɑːrsən/ | n. |卡森（人名；地名）|A surname of Scottish origin, also used as a given name. It can also refer to places named Carson, like Carson City in Nevada. |Carson went to the store. 卡森去了商店。| Carson City is the capital of Nevada. 卡森市是内华达州的首府。 | 近义词: (作为名字时无近义词，作为地名时，近义词取决于具体地点) | 反义词: (无) | 名词
