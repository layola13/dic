epiloguize: /ɪˈpɪləˌɡaɪz/ | v. |作结语；写结语；以结语结束|To add or write an epilogue to something; to conclude with an epilogue. | He epiloguized his novel with a poignant scene set ten years later. 他用十年后一个辛酸的场景为他的小说作了结语。|  The author epiloguized the story by summarizing the characters' fates. 作者总结了人物的命运，以此为故事作结。 | 近义词: conclude, summarize, finish | 反义词: begin, commence, start | 词性: verb

Etymology: From epilogue + -ize.  Epilogue comes from the Greek epilogos, from epi- "upon, after" + logos "speech, word."
