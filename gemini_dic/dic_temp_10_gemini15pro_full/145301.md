overbreed:/ˌoʊvərˈbriːd/
verb
过度繁殖；过多育种
To breed animals or plants in excessive numbers, typically leading to undesirable consequences.
The farmer overbred his sheep, resulting in a weakened and sickly flock.  农民过度繁殖他的羊，导致羊群虚弱多病。
Overbreeding can lead to genetic defects. 过度繁殖会导致基因缺陷。
synonyms: overproduce, propagate excessively
antonyms: underbreed, control breeding
词性: 动词
