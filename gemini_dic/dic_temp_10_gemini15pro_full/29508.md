catastasis:/kəˈtæstəsɪs/
noun
灾难的转折点; 剧情发展的转折点; (病情)急剧变化
The point in a drama, especially a tragedy, where there is a distinct change of fortune for the protagonist, often marking the beginning of the decline to disaster.  A sudden change, as in a disease.
The catastasis of the play occurred when the hero discovered his best friend's betrayal.  这出戏的转折点发生在主人公发现他最好的朋友背叛了他。
The doctor said the catastasis in her condition was unexpected. 医生说她病情的急剧变化是出乎意料的。
synonym: turning point, climax, crisis
antonym: stability, equilibrium
