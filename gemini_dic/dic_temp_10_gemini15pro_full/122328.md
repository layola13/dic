mixabilities:/ˌmɪksəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |可混合性，互溶性|The mixabilities of different types of paint can vary greatly. 不同类型油漆的可混合性差异很大。|例句：The mixabilities of the two chemicals are being tested.  这两种化学品的互溶性正在被测试。

例句：Understanding the mixabilities of different polymers is crucial for creating new materials. 了解不同聚合物的可混合性对于创造新材料至关重要。

近义词：compatibility, miscibility|反义词: immiscibility, incompatibility|词性:noun
