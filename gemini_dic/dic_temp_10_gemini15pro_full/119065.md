metopic:/mɪˈtɒpɪk/
adjective
額縫的；額骨的
Relating to the metopic suture, the fibrous joint that divides the two halves of the frontal bone of the skull in a fetus or infant.
The metopic suture usually fuses during infancy. 額縫通常在嬰兒期閉合。
Synonyms: frontal, forehead
Antonyms: None (lack of a direct anatomical opposite)
词性: adjective
