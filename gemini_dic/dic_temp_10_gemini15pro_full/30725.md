cerussite:/ˈsɛrəsaɪt/ /ˈsɪərəsaɪt/
n.
白铅矿
A mineral consisting of lead carbonate (PbCO3), usually white or colorless, and an important ore of lead.
Cerussite is a common secondary mineral of lead. 白铅矿是一种常见的铅的次生矿物。
Large cerussite crystals are prized by collectors.  大型白铅矿晶体受到收藏家的珍视。
Synonym: lead carbonate, white lead ore
