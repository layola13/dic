breadthless:/ˈbrɛθləs/| adj.|气喘吁吁的；屏息的；惊叹的|Out of breath; breathless from exertion or excitement; also, filled with awe or wonder.|例句：She ran up the stairs, arriving at the top breathless. 她跑上楼梯，到顶时气喘吁吁。|近义词：winded, gasping, awestruck, amazed|反义词:calm, composed, relaxed|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Old English *bræþlēas "without breath," from bræþ "breath" + -lēas "without."
