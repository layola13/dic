foundering:/ˈfaʊndərɪŋ/ | n. vi. | 沉没，失败，崩溃 | The ship is foundering in the storm. 这艘船在暴风雨中沉没了。 | 近义词：failing, sinking, collapsing | 反义词: succeeding, thriving, flourishing | 词性: 名词，动词

foundering:/ˈfaʊndərɪŋ/ | adj. | 失败的，崩溃的 |  The foundering economy caused widespread hardship. 崩溃的经济造成了广泛的困难。 | 近义词：failing, collapsing, struggling | 反义词: successful, thriving, prosperous | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *foundren, from Anglo-Norman *fundrer, *fondrer, from Old French fondrer (“to sink, submerge, go to the bottom, collapse, perish”), from Vulgar Latin *fundrāre, from Latin fundus (“bottom, foundation”).
