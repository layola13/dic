laurel: /ˈlɔːrəl/ (US) /ˈlɒrəl/ (UK)| n. adj.|月桂树；桂冠；荣誉；胜利|A small evergreen tree with shiny dark green leaves.  A wreath of laurel leaves, given in ancient times as a symbol of victory or honour.|The poet received laurels for his work. 这位诗人因其作品而获得殊荣。|近义词:accolade, award, honor, triumph, victory|反义词:defeat, failure, loss|noun

laurel: /ˈlɔːrəl/ (US) /ˈlɒrəl/ (UK)| adj.|桂冠的；荣誉的|Relating to, or resembling laurel.|He rested on his laurel after winning the championship. 赢得冠军后，他安于现状。|近义词:honored, acclaimed|反义词:dishonored, disgraced|adjective
