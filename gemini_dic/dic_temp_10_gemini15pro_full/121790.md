misrecollects:/ˌmɪsrɪˈkəˈlɛkts/
verb
错误地回忆；记错
To remember something incorrectly.
She misrecollects the details of the accident. 她对事故的细节记错了。
misremember, misrecall
correctly recall, remember accurately
词性: 动词
