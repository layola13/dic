gutterpup:/ˈɡʌtər pʌp/| n. |流浪狗，卑鄙的人|A dog of mixed or unknown breed, often found wandering in the streets; a person considered to be despicable or contemptible.|The poor gutterpup was shivering in the cold. 可怜的流浪狗在寒冷中瑟瑟发抖。 He acted like a real gutterpup, spreading rumors and trying to sabotage everyone. 他的行为像个卑鄙小人，散布谣言并试图破坏每个人。|近义词：mongrel, cur, scoundrel, villain|反义词:pedigree dog, gentleman|词性:名词
