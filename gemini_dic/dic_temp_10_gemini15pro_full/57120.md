dousing:/ˈdaʊsɪŋ/
noun
浇灭；浸泡；痛打
the act of pouring a liquid over someone or something; a thorough wetting.
例句：The dousing of the bonfire with water finally put out the flames. 消防员用水浇灭了篝火，最终扑灭了火焰。

例句：The protesters received a dousing from the police water cannons. 抗议者被警方的水炮浇透了。
近义词：soaking, immersing, submerging, quenching
反义词：drying, igniting
词性：noun

douse:/daʊs/
verb
浇灭；浸泡；痛打
to pour a liquid over someone or something; to extinguish a fire with water or other liquid
例句：He doused the flames with a bucket of water. 他用一桶水浇灭了火焰。

例句：She doused herself in perfume before going out. 她出门前往身上喷了很多香水。
近义词：extinguish, soak, drench, submerge
反义词：ignite, dry
词性：verb
