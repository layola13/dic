diastasis:/daɪˈæstəsɪs/
noun
分离，裂开，腹直肌分离
Separation of normally joined parts, especially the abdominal muscles.
The doctor diagnosed her with diastasis recti after her pregnancy.  医生诊断她在怀孕后患有腹直肌分离。
separation, split, rift
union, joining, closure
