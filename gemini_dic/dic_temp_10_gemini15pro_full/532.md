abominating:/əˈbɒmɪneɪtɪŋ/| adj. |憎恶的，厌恶的|Feeling or expressing great hatred or disgust.|He gave an abominating look at the rotten food. 他厌恶地看着腐烂的食物。|She found the smell of the garbage utterly abominating. 她觉得垃圾的味道令人作呕。|近义词：detesting, loathing, abhorring, execrating|反义词：adoring, loving, cherishing|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin abominatus, past participle of abominari (“to deprecate as an ill omen, to shudder at, to detest, to abominate”), from ab (“away from”) + ominari (“to forebode, to prophesy”), from omen (“omen, portent”).
