chapatti: /tʃəˈpɑːtiː/ (UK), /tʃəˈpɑːti/ (US) | n. | 印度薄饼，恰巴提 | A thin, flat unleavened bread originating from the Indian subcontinent, made from whole-wheat flour and water. |  Example 1:  We had chapatti with our curry.  我们咖喱配了印度薄饼。

Example 2: She skillfully rolled out the dough for the chapatti. 她熟练地擀出做恰巴提的面团。| Synonym: roti, flatbread | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps leavened bread) | noun
