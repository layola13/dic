half-petrified:/ˌhæf ˈpɛtrɪfaɪd/
adj.
半石化的；吓呆的
partly petrified; extremely frightened and unable to move or react
例句：The explorers found a half-petrified tree in the ancient forest. 探险家们在古老的森林里发现了一棵半石化的树。

例句：She stood half-petrified, staring at the ghostly figure in the doorway. 她吓呆了，站在那里盯着门口的鬼影。
近义词：half-frozen, terrified, stunned, petrified
反义词：calm, relaxed, unafraid
