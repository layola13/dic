malians:/ˈmeɪliənz/ | n. | 马里人 | People from Mali, a country in West Africa. |  Many Malians speak French, as it is the official language of the country. 许多马里人说法语，因为它是马里的官方语言。 |  Nigeriens, Senegalese | None | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Mali + -ian (suffix denoting belonging to or relating to a place or people).  Mali itself derives from the name of the Mali Empire, a powerful medieval West African state.
