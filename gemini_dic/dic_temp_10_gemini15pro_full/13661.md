attractant:/əˈtræktənt/ | n. | 吸引物；引诱物 |Something that attracts or is intended to attract people or animals. |The flowers' bright colors are an attractant to bees. 这些花朵鲜艳的颜色对蜜蜂很有吸引力。| synonyms: lure, bait, enticement | antonyms: repellent, deterrent | noun

attractant:/əˈtræktənt/ | adj. | 吸引人的 |Having the quality of attracting. |The company offered an attractant salary and benefits package. 这家公司提供了一个诱人的薪水和福利待遇。| synonyms: appealing, alluring, enticing | antonyms: repulsive, repellent | adjective
