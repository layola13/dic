chrysalis: /ˈkrɪsəlɪs/ | n. |蛹，茧；(比喻)过渡期|The butterfly emerged from its chrysalis. 蝴蝶从蛹中羽化而出。| A chrysalis is the hard, protective outer case of a butterfly or moth pupa. 蛹是蝴蝶或蛾的蛹的坚硬的保护性外壳。|例句：The company is in a chrysalis stage, about to emerge as a major player in the market.  这家公司正处于过渡期，即将成为市场上的主要参与者。

例句：The artist saw her studio as a chrysalis where her creativity could develop.  这位艺术家把她的工作室看作是一个可以发展她创造力的茧。|近义词：cocoon, pupa|反义词:imago (成虫)|词性:noun
