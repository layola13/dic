accustom:/əˈkʌstəm/| vt.|使习惯于，使适应|To make someone or something familiar with something through experience.|She quickly accustomed herself to the new surroundings. 她很快适应了新环境。

It took him a while to accustom himself to the spicy food. 他花了一段时间才适应辛辣的食物。

|近义词：habituate, adapt, inure|反义词: unaccustom|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *accoustumer*, from Vulgar Latin *accostumare*, from Latin *consuetudo* "custom, habit".

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I accustom myself to waking up early. = 我习惯早起。
He accustoms his dog to walking on a leash. = 他让他的狗习惯用皮带牵着走。


一般过去时:
She accustomed herself to the cold weather quickly. = 她很快就适应了寒冷的天气。
They accustomed their children to eating a variety of vegetables. = 他们让他们的孩子习惯吃各种蔬菜。


现在完成时:
He has accustomed himself to living in a small apartment. = 他已经习惯了住在一个小公寓里。
We have accustomed ourselves to the noise of the city. = 我们已经习惯了城市的噪音。


过去完成时:
By the time she moved to the city, she had accustomed herself to driving in heavy traffic. = 当她搬到这个城市的时候，她已经习惯了在拥挤的交通中开车。
They had accustomed their horses to the sound of gunfire before the battle. = 他们在战斗前让他们的马习惯了枪声。


将来时:
You will soon accustom yourself to the new routine. = 你很快就会适应新的日常安排。
She will accustom her cat to the new house slowly. = 她会慢慢地让她的猫适应新房子。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would accustom myself to the new environment as soon as possible. = 如果我是你，我会尽快适应新环境。
I wish I had accustomed myself to public speaking earlier in my career. = 我希望我在职业生涯早期就习惯了公开演讲。


被动语态:
The children were accustomed to eating dinner early. = 孩子们习惯早点吃晚饭。
The soldiers are being accustomed to the harsh conditions of the desert. = 士兵们正在适应沙漠的恶劣条件。


疑问句:
Are you accustomed to the spicy food here? = 你习惯这里的辛辣食物吗？
How long did it take you to accustom yourself to the new job? = 你花了多长时间才适应新工作？


否定句:
I am not accustomed to such formalities. = 我不习惯这样的礼节。
She wasn't accustomed to being the center of attention. = 她不习惯成为关注的焦点。
