palinode:/ˈpælɪˌnoʊd/ | n. | 撤回声明；道歉；翻案诗 | A poem in which the author retracts something said in an earlier poem, or a formal retraction of a previous statement. |  He wrote a palinode retracting his earlier criticism of the king. 他写了一首翻案诗，收回了他早先对国王的批评。

The politician issued a palinode for his offensive remarks.  这位政治家为他冒犯性的言论发表了道歉声明。 | 近义词：recantation, retraction, apology | 反义词：affirmation, confirmation | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *palinodia, from Greek palinōidia, from palin "back, again" + ōidē "song, ode."  Literally, a "song again," implying a reversal of the original song's sentiment.
