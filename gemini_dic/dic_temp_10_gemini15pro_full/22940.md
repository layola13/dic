bottlefuls:/ˈbɒtlfʊlz/ (US) /ˈbɒtlfʊlz/ (UK)
noun
几瓶的量；满瓶的量
The quantity contained in several bottles; the amount that fills several bottles.
She drank three bottlefuls of water during the marathon.  她在马拉松比赛中喝了三瓶水。
The chemist prepared several bottlefuls of the solution. 药剂师配制了几瓶溶液。
synonyms: bottles, containers
antonyms: drops, sips
词性: noun
