overtimorousness:/ˌoʊvərˈtɪmərəsnəs/| n. |过度胆怯，过分小心谨慎|Excessive timidity or caution.|She displayed overtimorousness in her reluctance to speak up. 她因不愿大声说话而表现出过度胆怯。|His overtimorousness prevented him from taking risks. 他的过分小心谨慎使他不敢冒险。|近义词：timidity, diffidence, apprehension, fearfulness|反义词：boldness, courage, audacity, confidence|词性:noun

Etymology:
From over- + timorous + -ness.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much." "Timorous" comes from the Latin word "timor" meaning "fear." "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives.
