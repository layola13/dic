analyze:/ˈænəlaɪz/ (US) /ˈænəlaɪz/ (UK)| vt. |分析，解析|To examine something methodically and in detail, typically in order to explain and interpret it.|例句：We need to analyze the data before making a decision. 我们需要在做决定之前分析数据。

例句：The scientist analyzed the chemical composition of the substance.  科学家分析了该物质的化学成分。

|近义词：examine, study, investigate, scrutinize, dissect|反义词: synthesize, combine|词性:动词
