nonlustrousnesses:/ˌnɒnˈlʌstrəsnəsɪz/
noun
无光泽；暗淡
lack of luster; dullness
The nonlustrousnesses of the old painting suggested it needed restoration. 这幅旧画的暗淡表明它需要修复。
The rock's nonlustrousnesses made it difficult to identify.  这块岩石的无光泽使它难以识别。
近义词:dullness, opacity, matteness
反义词:luster, shine, brilliance
词性:n.
