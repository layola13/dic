crew-necked:/ˈkruːnekt/
adjective
圆领的；套头衫式的
Having a close-fitting round neckline, like that of a crew neck sweater.
She wore a crew-necked sweater and jeans. 她穿着一件圆领毛衣和牛仔裤。
close-fitting, round-necked
v-necked, open-necked
adjective
