crescendoed: /krəˈʃendoʊd/ | v. |  渐强；声音逐渐增强 |The music crescendoed to a dramatic climax.  音乐逐渐增强到戏剧性的高潮。| The applause crescendoed as the performer took a bow. 掌声随着表演者鞠躬而逐渐增强。 | 近义词: intensified, swelled, amplified | 反义词: diminuendoed, subsided, faded | 词性: 动词

词源:  From Italian crescendo, present participle of crescere "to grow," from Latin crescere "to arise, grow," related to crescere "create."
