overplenties:/ˈoʊvərˌplɛntiːz/
n.
过剩，过多
excess; an amount that is more than enough
The farm had overplenties of apples this year, more than they could sell. 今年这个农场苹果产量过剩，比他们能卖的还要多。
近义词：surplus, abundance, excess, superfluity
反义词：scarcity, lack, shortage, deficiency
词性: 名词

词源:  "Over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *ubar, "above, beyond") + "plenty" (from Old French plenté, from Latin plenitatem, accusative of plenitas "fullness," from plenus "full").
