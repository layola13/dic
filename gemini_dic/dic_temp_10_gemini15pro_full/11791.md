arraign:/əˈreɪn/| v. |传讯；控告；责难|to call or bring (someone) before a court to answer to an indictment|The suspect was arraigned on charges of murder. 嫌疑犯被传讯，罪名是谋杀。|synonyms: indict, prosecute, accuse|antonyms: acquit, exonerate|verb

arraign:/əˈreɪn/| v. |责备；谴责|to criticize severely; find fault with publicly|The newspaper arraigned the government for its handling of the crisis. 报纸谴责政府对这场危机的处理方式。|synonyms: censure, denounce, reprimand|antonyms: praise, commend|verb
