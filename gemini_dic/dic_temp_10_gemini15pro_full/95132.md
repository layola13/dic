inapplicable:/ˌɪnəˈplɪkəbl/ | adj. |不适用的，不相关的|Not able to be applied or relevant; not appropriate.|This rule is inapplicable in this case.  这条规则在这种情况下不适用。|The theory is inapplicable to this particular situation.  这个理论不适用于这种特殊情况。|近义词：irrelevant, inappropriate, unsuitable|反义词：applicable, relevant, appropriate|词性:形容词
Etymology: Late 16th century: from in- ‘not’ + applicable.
