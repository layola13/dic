caging:/ˈkeɪdʒɪŋ/
n.
关在笼子里；囚禁
The act of confining something or someone in a cage.
The caging of the wild animals was controversial.  野生动物被关在笼子里引起了争议。

The prolonged caging of the bird led to its depression.  长时间将鸟关在笼子里导致它抑郁。

confinement, imprisonment, incarceration
liberation, release, freedom
noun
