amiable:/ˈeɪmiəbl/| adj.|和蔼可亲的；友善的|Having or displaying a friendly and pleasant manner.|She had an amiable personality, always ready with a smile and a kind word. 她性格和蔼可亲，总是面带微笑，说着友善的话。|例句：He was an amiable host, making everyone feel welcome. 他是一位和蔼可亲的主人，让每个人都感到宾至如归。

例句：The amiable shopkeeper chatted with each customer.  这位和蔼可亲的店主与每位顾客聊天。|近义词：friendly, affable, agreeable, pleasant, cordial|反义词：unfriendly, hostile, disagreeable, unpleasant|词性:adjective
