glare:/ɡlɛər/| n. v.| 怒视，瞪眼；强光；炫耀；显眼|A glare is a long, angry stare.  A glare can also refer to a bright, dazzling light.|She gave him a fierce glare. 她狠狠地瞪了他一眼。

The sun glared on the water. 阳光在水面上闪耀。|近义词：stare, scowl, blaze, dazzle|反义词：glimpse, glance|词性：名词，动词

glare:/ɡlɛər/| v.| 怒视，瞪眼；发出强光|To glare is to stare angrily and intensely, or to shine with a strong, dazzling light.|He glared at the intruder. 他怒视着入侵者。

The sun glared down from the sky. 太阳从天空照耀下来。|近义词：stare, scowl, blaze, dazzle|反义词：glimpse, glance|词性：动词
