murkiest:/ˈmɜːrkɪɪst/ | adj. | 最黑暗的；最阴暗的；最模糊的 | Superlative form of murky: most murky.  Characterized by a lack of clarity or distinctness; obscure and difficult to understand. Often used figuratively to describe situations, motives, or concepts that are difficult to discern or comprehend. | The investigation delved into the murkiest depths of corporate corruption.  调查深入到公司腐败最黑暗的深处。

The murkiest waters of the lake concealed unseen dangers.  湖中最黑暗的水域隐藏着看不见的危险。 | 近义词: darkest, gloomiest, obscurest, vaguest | 反义词: clearest, brightest, transparent, lucid | 词性: 形容词
