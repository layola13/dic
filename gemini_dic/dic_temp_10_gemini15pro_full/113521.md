maas:/mɑːs/|/mæs/| n. |Metal-as-a-Service| 金属即服务，指通过订阅模式提供按需访问金属资源（如服务器、存储、网络和软件）的服务。|MaaS allows companies to access and manage their IT infrastructure without having to own the underlying hardware.  金属即服务允许公司在无需拥有底层硬件的情况下访问和管理其IT基础设施。|MaaS providers offer a range of services, from basic server hosting to complex cloud-based solutions.  金属即服务提供商提供一系列服务，从基本的服务器托管到复杂的基于云的解决方案。|近义词：IaaS (Infrastructure as a Service), HaaS (Hardware as a Service)|反义词：Traditional IT infrastructure, On-premises hardware|词性: noun
