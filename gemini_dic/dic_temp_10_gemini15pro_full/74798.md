fremitus:/ˈfrɛmɪtəs/| n. |震颤，震动|A palpable vibration, as felt by the hand placed on the chest during coughing or speaking.|例句：The doctor detected a fremitus in the patient's chest, suggesting fluid buildup. 医生在病人的胸部检测到震颤，提示有液体积聚。

例句：Increased vocal fremitus can be a sign of pneumonia.  语音震颤增强可能是肺炎的征兆。|近义词：vibration, tremor, thrill|反义词: stillness, quiescence|词性:noun
