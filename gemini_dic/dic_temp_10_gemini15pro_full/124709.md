moxie: /ˈmɑːksi/ (US) /ˈmɒksi/ (UK)| n.| 勇气，胆量，魄力，进取心|Force of character, determination, or nerve.|She showed a lot of moxie standing up to the bully. 她勇敢地对抗恶霸，展现了十足的勇气。| He had the moxie to start his own business at a young age. 他很有魄力，年纪轻轻就开始了自己的事业。|近义词: nerve, guts, courage, grit, determination, spirit, pluck|反义词: timidity, cowardice, fearfulness|词性: 名词

词源:  Likely originated in the late 19th century as a brand name for a non-alcoholic beverage called "Moxie Nerve Food." The drink was marketed as having energy-boosting properties, and the name "Moxie" eventually came to be associated with courage and determination.
