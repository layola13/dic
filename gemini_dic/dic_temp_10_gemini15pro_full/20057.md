biopics:/ˈbaɪˌɔːpɪks/ | n. |传记片|A biographical movie; a film that dramatizes the life of a non-fictional or historical person.|Example:  The biopic about the famous singer was a box office hit.  关于那位著名歌手的传记片获得了票房成功。

Example:  She prefers watching biopics to action movies. 她比起动作片更喜欢看传记片。|近义词：biographical film, biographical picture|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
