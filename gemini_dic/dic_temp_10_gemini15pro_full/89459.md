honestness:/ˈɑːnɪstnɪs/ | n. |诚实，正直|The quality of being honest; uprightness and fairness.|Her honesty is one of her most admirable qualities. 她的诚实是她最令人钦佩的品质之一。|integrity, probity, sincerity, trustworthiness|dishonesty, deceit, duplicity|noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *honestnes*, equivalent to honest +‎ -ness.  Honest traces back to Old French honeste, from Latin honestus (“honorable, respectable”), from honos (“honor”).
