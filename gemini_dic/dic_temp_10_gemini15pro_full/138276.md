notarial:/noʊˈtɛəriəl/| adj.|公证的；公证人的|Relating to or done by a notary public.  Pertaining to, or performed by, a notary.|例句：The document required notarial certification. 这份文件需要公证认证。|例句：He obtained notarial services to verify his signature. 他获得公证服务来验证他的签名。|近义词：certified, authenticated, legalized|反义词：uncertified, unauthenticated|词性: adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *notariālis, from Latin notārius (“shorthand writer, secretary”), from nota (“mark, character, letter”).
