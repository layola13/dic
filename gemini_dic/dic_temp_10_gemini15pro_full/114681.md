maligners:/məˈlaɪnərz/ | n. | 诽谤者，恶意中伤者 | People who malign others; those who speak ill of someone or something, often with the intent to harm their reputation. |  The maligners spread false rumors about the company. 诽谤者散布关于公司的谣言。 | detractors, slanderers, backbiters |  supporters, admirers | noun

malign:/məˈlaɪn/ | v. | 诽谤，污蔑，中伤 | To speak about someone or something in a critical and often untrue way; to defame. |  The politician was maligned by the press.  这位政治家被媒体诽谤了。 |  defame, slander, vilify | praise, commend | verb
