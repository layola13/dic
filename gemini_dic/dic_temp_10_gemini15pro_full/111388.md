livias:/ˈlɪviəs/| n. |莉薇娅斯 (女子名)|A female given name, likely a variant of Livia, which is of Latin origin and related to the word "lividus," originally meaning "bluish" or "lead-colored," but later associated with envy.  It was the name of the wife of the Roman emperor Augustus.| Livia is a popular name in several European countries. 莉薇娅在几个欧洲国家很受欢迎。| Livia's grandmother also had the same name. 莉薇娅的祖母也叫同样的名字。| 近义词:Livia, Liv, |反义词:N/A| Noun
