overangry: /ˌoʊvərˈæŋɡri/ | adj. | 过于愤怒的，暴怒的 | Excessively angry; enraged beyond reason.  This word is not standard English and is considered informal or nonstandard. It emphasizes a degree of anger beyond what is considered normal or appropriate. | He was overangry about the spilled milk, reacting disproportionately. 他对洒出的牛奶反应过度，过于愤怒了。|  She was overangry with her child for breaking the vase. 她因为孩子打破花瓶而对他大发雷霆。 | 近义词: furious, enraged, livid, infuriated | 反义词: calm, peaceful, tranquil | 词性: adjective

词源: "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "angry" (adjective)
