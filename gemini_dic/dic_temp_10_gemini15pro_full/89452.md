honest-to-goodness:/ˌɑːnɪst tə ˈɡʊdnɪs/ | adj. | 真正的，货真价实的 | Authentic, genuine, real, not fake or artificial. | That's an honest-to-goodness antique. 那是一件货真价实的古董。| It was an honest-to-goodness miracle. 那真是一个货真价实的奇迹。 | 近义词: genuine, real, authentic, bona fide | 反义词: fake, counterfeit, sham, phony | 词性: 形容词

词源: This phrase originated in American English in the late 19th or early 20th century. It's an emphatic form of "honest," using "to goodness" as an intensifier, similar to expressions like "for goodness' sake."  The "goodness" here likely refers to God or a general sense of moral rightness, adding weight to the assertion of honesty.
