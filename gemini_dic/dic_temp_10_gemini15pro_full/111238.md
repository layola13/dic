lithotypy: /lɪˈθɒtɪpi/ | n. | 岩相学，煤岩类型学 | The study of the macroscopic characteristics of coal, including its appearance, texture, and structure, used to classify coal into different lithotypes.  |  The geologist used lithotypy to analyze the coal samples. 地质学家使用岩相学来分析煤样。 |  Synonyms: coal petrography | Antonyms: N/A | noun

lithotypy: /lɪˈθɒtɪpi/ | n. | 岩石类型学|  The study and classification of rocks based on their physical characteristics and composition. | Lithotypy played a crucial role in understanding the geological history of the region. 岩石类型学在理解该地区的地质历史中发挥了至关重要的作用。 | Synonyms: petrography, petrology | Antonyms: N/A | noun
