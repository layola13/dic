arsenopyrite:/ˈɑːrsənəʊpaɪraɪt/ | n. | 毒砂 | A common iron arsenic sulfide mineral, FeAsS, typically found in hydrothermal veins and metamorphic rocks. It is a major source of arsenic. | Arsenopyrite is a common source of arsenic contamination in groundwater. 毒砂是地下水中砷污染的常见来源。|  Arsenopyrite is often associated with gold deposits. 毒砂经常与金矿伴生。 | synonyms: mispickel | antonyms: N/A | noun
