dipyridine:/daɪˈpɪrɪdiːn/| n.|联吡啶，二吡啶|A chemical compound consisting of two pyridine rings linked together.  It is used as a ligand in coordination chemistry.|Dipyridine is commonly used in transition metal complexes.|近义词:bipyridine (This is essentially the same compound and the preferred name)|反义词:None (It's a specific chemical compound, not a concept with an opposite.)|词性:noun
