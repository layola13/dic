agalaxy:/ˈæɡələksi/ | n. |星系；银河系；一群杰出或著名的人物|A system of millions or billions of stars, together with gas and dust, held together by gravitational attraction.  A large group of impressive people or things. |例句：Our solar system is located in the Milky Way galaxy. 我们的太阳系位于银河系。

例句：The event was attended by a galaxy of movie stars.  众多影星出席了这次活动。|近义词：cosmos, universe, star system, constellation, a host of|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
