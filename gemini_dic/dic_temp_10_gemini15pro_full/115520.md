Also used as an adjective to describe things related to or characteristic of Maoism. |  Example 1: The maoist rebels seized control of the radio station. 毛泽东主义叛军占领了广播电台。

Example 2:  The government accused the group of being maoist sympathizers. 政府指责该组织是毛泽东主义的同情者。 | 近义词: communist, Marxist-Leninist | 反义词: capitalist, anti-communist | 词性: noun, adjective
