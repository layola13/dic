arboriform:/ɑːrˈbɔːrɪfɔːrm/
adjective
树形的，树状的
Resembling a tree in growth or appearance.  Having the form or shape of a tree.
The arboriform structure of the coral provided shelter for small fish.  这种珊瑚的树状结构为小鱼提供了庇护所。
近义词:treelike, dendritic, arboreal, arborescent
反义词:shrubby, bushy
词性:adjective
