parsee:/pɑːrˈsiː/ /pɑːrˈseɪ/
noun|帕西人，拜火教徒|A descendant of Persian Zoroastrians who emigrated to India, especially to the region of Bombay, to avoid religious persecution.|Many Parsees settled in Mumbai and became successful merchants. 许多帕西人定居在孟买，成为了成功的商人。|Synonyms: Zoroastrian|Antonyms: None|n.
