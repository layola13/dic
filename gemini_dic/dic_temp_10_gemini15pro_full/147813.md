overprovision:/ˌoʊvərprəˈvɪʒən/| n. |过度供应，超额配置|The act of supplying more resources than are estimated to be necessary.  This is often done as a safeguard against unexpected demand or failure.| We decided on overprovisioning of server capacity to handle unexpected traffic spikes. 我们决定过度配置服务器容量以应对意外的流量高峰。|Near Synonyms: oversupply, excess capacity|Near Antonyms: underprovision, undersupply|noun


overprovision:/ˌoʊvərprəˈvɪʒən/| v. |过度供应，超额配置|To supply more resources than are estimated to be necessary.| Cloud providers often overprovision storage to ensure high availability. 云提供商通常会过度配置存储以确保高可用性。|Near Synonyms: oversupply, glut|Near Antonyms: underprovision, skimp|verb
