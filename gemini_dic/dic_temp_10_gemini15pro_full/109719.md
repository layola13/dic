levelest:/ˈlɛvəlɪst/ | adj. | 最水平的，最平坦的 |  superlative form of level; most level. Having the least inclination from a horizontal plane. | The levelest ground we could find was still slightly sloped. 我们能找到的最平坦的地面仍然略微倾斜。|  The carpenter used a spirit level to ensure the shelf was the levelest it could be. 木匠用水平仪确保架子尽可能平。 |  flattest, smoothest, evenest | steepest, most uneven, most inclined | adjective
