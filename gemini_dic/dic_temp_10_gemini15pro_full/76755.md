gamelans:/ˈɡæməlænz/ /ɡɑːməˈlɑːnz/
n.
甘美兰
A traditional Indonesian orchestra, typically including many bronze percussion instruments.
The haunting sounds of the gamelans drifted through the night air.  甘美兰萦绕的声音在夜空中飘荡。
Synonyms: orchestra, ensemble
Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific type of orchestra)
