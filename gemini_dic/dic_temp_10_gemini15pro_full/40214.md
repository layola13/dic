Simple Present:
What do you do for a living? = 你靠什么谋生？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

Present Continuous:
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing right now? = 他现在在做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned so far? = 到目前为止你学到了什么？
What has she been up to lately? = 她最近在忙些什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading all afternoon? = 他整个下午都在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you say? = 你说了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么事？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing at 8 pm yesterday? = 昨天晚上8点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you came here? = 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had they discussed prior to the announcement? = 宣布之前他们讨论了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be presenting at the conference? = 他们将在会议上展示什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the age of 30? = 到30岁时你将取得什么成就？
What will have changed by the end of the century? = 到本世纪末会有什么变化？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for the past five years? = 过去五年你一直在做什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？

词源：From Middle English *what, *hwat, from Old English hwæt (“what”), from Proto-Germanic *hwat (“what”), from Proto-Indo-European *kʷod (“what”). Cognate with Scots what (“what”), West Frisian wat (“what”), Dutch wat (“what”), German was (“what”), Danish hvad (“what”), Swedish vad (“what”), Icelandic hvað (“what”), Latin quod (“what, which”), Sanskrit कत् (kat, “who, which, what”).
