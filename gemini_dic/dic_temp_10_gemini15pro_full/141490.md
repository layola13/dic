opisometer:/ˌōpɪˈsɒmɪtər/ (US) /ˌəʊpɪˈsɒmɪtər/ (UK)| n. |里程计，曲线计|An instrument for measuring the lengths of curved lines, especially on maps.|He used an opisometer to measure the distance of the winding road on the map. 他用曲线计测量了地图上蜿蜒道路的距离。|Synonyms: curvimeter, map measurer|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
