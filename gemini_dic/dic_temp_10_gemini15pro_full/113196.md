luringly:/ˈlʊərɪŋli/ | adv. | 诱惑地，诱人地 | In a way that attracts and tempts someone to do something, often something wrong or unwise. | The aroma of freshly baked bread wafted luringly from the bakery.  新鲜出炉的面包的香味从面包店里飘出来，十分诱人。|  The salesman described the benefits of the product luringly.  销售员诱人地描述了该产品的好处。 |  temptingly, seductively, enticingly |  repellingly, off-puttingly | adverb

词源:
From lure (v.) + -ing + -ly.  The verb "lure" comes from Old French "luerre" (a decoy for hawks), ultimately from Germanic origin.
