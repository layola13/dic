anthracosis:/ˌænθrəˈkoʊsɪs/
n.
煤工尘肺，炭末沉着病
A lung disease caused by inhaling coal dust.
Miners are prone to anthracosis. 矿工容易患煤工尘肺。
Synonyms: black lung, coal-worker's pneumoconiosis
Antonyms: healthy lungs
