off-air:/ˌɒf ˈeər/ /ˌɔːf ˈer/ | adj. | 停止播送的，私下的，非正式的 | Not being broadcast; private or informal. | The interview was off-air, so it wasn't recorded.  这次采访没有播出，所以没有被录制。|The president made some off-air comments that were later leaked to the press. 总统私下发表了一些评论，后来被泄露给了媒体。 | 近义词：unbroadcast, private, informal | 反义词: on-air, broadcast, public, formal | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "off" (meaning not operating or active) + "air" (referring to broadcasting).  "Off" 源于古英语 "of,"  "air" 源于古法语 "air" 最终源于拉丁语 "aer."
