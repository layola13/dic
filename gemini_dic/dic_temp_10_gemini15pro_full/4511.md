albumenize:/ælˈbjuːmənaɪz/
verb
使蛋白化，使成蛋白状
To convert or transform into albumen or a substance resembling albumen; to coat or impregnate with albumen.
Egg whites are used to albumenize photographic paper.  蛋清用来使相纸蛋白化。
The heat albumenized the egg white.  热量使蛋清蛋白化。
近义词: coagulate, denature

词性: verb
