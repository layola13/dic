disappointing:/ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪntɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人失望的 | Causing disappointment; failing to fulfill expectations. | The movie was disappointing; it wasn't as good as the reviews said.  这部电影令人失望；它不像评论说的那么好。| Synonyms: unsatisfactory, unsatisfying, underwhelming, disheartening | Antonyms: satisfying, gratifying, pleasing, encouraging | adjective


disappoint:/ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪnt/ | v. | 使失望 | Fail to fulfill the hopes or expectations of. |  I hate to disappoint you, but I can't make it to the party. 我很不想让你失望，但我不能去参加派对了。 | Synonyms: let down, dissatisfy, frustrate | Antonyms: please, satisfy, gratify | verb
