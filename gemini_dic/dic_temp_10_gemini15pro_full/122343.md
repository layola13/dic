mixer: /ˈmɪksər/ (US), /ˈmɪksə(r)/ (UK)| n.|搅拌器; 混合器; 调音师; (尤指鸡尾酒)调酒师|a machine used for mixing things, especially food; a person who operates the sound controls, especially for a recording; a person who mixes alcoholic drinks, especially cocktails|She used a mixer to blend the ingredients for the cake. 她用搅拌机混合蛋糕的配料。

The sound mixer adjusted the levels for the band. 音响师调整了乐队的音量。

He's a skilled mixer and can make any cocktail you want. 他是一位技艺高超的调酒师，可以调制任何你想要的鸡尾酒。|近义词：blender, amalgamator, sound engineer, bartender|反义词: separator|noun
