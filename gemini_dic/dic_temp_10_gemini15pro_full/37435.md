commingling:/ˌkɒˈmɪŋɡlɪŋ/ /kəˈmɪŋɡlɪŋ/
noun
混合，混杂
The act of mixing or blending together.
The commingling of funds from different investors is a common practice.  不同投资者的资金混合是一种常见的做法。
The artist's work represents a commingling of traditional and modern styles.  这位艺术家的作品代表了传统和现代风格的融合。
mixing, blending, combining, merging
separation, division, segregation
n.
