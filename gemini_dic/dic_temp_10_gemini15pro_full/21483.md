blubbed:/blʌbd/ | v. |  啜泣，呜咽 | to cry noisily, making sounds like bubbles |  The little boy blubbed uncontrollably when he lost his toy. 小男孩丢了玩具后嚎啕大哭。| 近义词: sobbed, cried, wept | 反义词: laughed, rejoiced | 词性: 动词

词源:  Imitative origin, echoing the sound of crying.  源于模仿哭泣的声音。
