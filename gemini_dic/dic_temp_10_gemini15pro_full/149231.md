oxyquinoline: /ˌɒksiˈkwɪnəliːn/ | n. | 羟基喹啉 | An organic compound with the formula C9H7NO. It is a heterocyclic aromatic compound derived from quinoline by the addition of a hydroxyl group. | Oxyquinoline is used as a chelating agent. 羟基喹啉用作螯合剂。 |  Oxyquinoline derivatives are used in some medications. 一些药物中使用了羟基喹啉衍生物。 | Synonyms: 8-hydroxyquinoline, oxine | Antonyms: None | noun
