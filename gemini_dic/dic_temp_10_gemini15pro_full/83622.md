haemochromogen:/ˌhiːməˈkroʊmədʒən/ | n. |血色原|A coloured compound formed when haemoglobin is treated with alkali and a reducing agent. It contains iron in the ferrous state. | Haemochromogen is an important compound in forensic science. 血色原是法医学中的重要化合物。| The presence of haemochromogen can confirm the presence of blood. 血色原的存在可以证实血液的存在。 | synonyms: reduced haematin | antonyms: N/A | noun


haemochromogen:/ˌhiːməˈkroʊmədʒən/ | n. |血色素原|A coloured compound formed by the reduction of haematin, containing iron in the ferrous state.  It's used in forensic tests for the presence of blood.|  Forensic scientists use haemochromogen tests to detect even trace amounts of blood. 法医科学家使用血色素原测试来检测即使是微量的血液。| Haemochromogen formation is a key step in confirming the presence of bloodstains. 血色素原的形成是确认血迹存在的关键步骤。 | synonyms: haem, heme | antonyms: N/A | noun
