antiperistatic:/ˌæntiˌperɪˈstætɪk/ | adj. | 逆蠕动的，反常的；[哲]（指冷等）通过压缩周围介质而增强的 | Increasing or intensifying by resisting or opposing a surrounding force, especially of cold by compressing surrounding air or heat by compressing surrounding cold.  | The antiperistatic effect of the cold air intensified the heat from the fire. 冷空气的反常效应增强了火产生的热量。 | 近义词: intensified, enhanced, augmented | 反义词: diminished, weakened, reduced | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Ancient Greek ἀντιπερίστασις (antiperistasis, “counter-circumstance”), from ἀντί (anti, “against, opposite”) + περί (peri, “around, about”) + στάσις (stasis, “standing”).
