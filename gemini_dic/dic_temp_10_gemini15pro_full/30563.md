ceramic:/səˈræmɪk/| adj. n.|陶瓷的；陶瓷制品|Made of clay and permanently hardened by heat. A product made of such material.|例句：The museum has a fine collection of ancient ceramics.  这家博物馆收藏了精美的古代陶瓷。

例句：Ceramic tiles are often used in kitchens and bathrooms.  瓷砖常用于厨房和浴室。|近义词：pottery, earthenware, porcelain|反义词:(no direct opposite, perhaps plastic, metal)|词性:adjective, noun
