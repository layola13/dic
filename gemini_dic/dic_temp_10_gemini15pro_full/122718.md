mohawk:/ˈmoʊhɔːk/| n.|莫霍克族人，莫霍克语，莫霍克发型|A member of an Iroquois people formerly inhabiting the Mohawk River valley in central New York. Also: the Iroquoian language of this people.  A hairstyle in which the hair is shaved on both sides of the head, leaving a strip of longer hair in the middle.|He wore a bright red mohawk. 他梳了一个亮红色的莫霍克发型。|近义词：Iroquois (for the people), mohican (for the hairstyle)|反义词: None|词性: Noun


mohawk:/ˈmoʊhɔːk/| adj.|莫霍克族的，莫霍克语的|Relating to the Mohawk people or their language.|Mohawk traditions are still practiced today. 莫霍克族的传统今天仍在沿袭。|近义词：Iroquoian|反义词: None|词性: Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Dutch Moohaak, from a Narragansett word meaning "man-eaters."
