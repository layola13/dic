outsteaming:/ˌaʊtˈstiːmɪŋ/
adjective
向外喷涌的，冒出蒸汽的
emitting steam outwards; surpassing in steaming
The outsteaming vents released a cloud of hot, sulfurous gas. 向外喷涌的喷口释放出一团热的含硫气体。
The train’s engine was outsteaming furiously as it pulled away from the station. 火车离开车站时，引擎猛烈地向外喷着蒸汽。
近义词: steaming, venting, emitting, spewing
反义词: inhaling, absorbing
