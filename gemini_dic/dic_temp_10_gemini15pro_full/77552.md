GDP:/ˌdʒiː diː ˈpiː/ | n. | 国内生产总值 (Gross Domestic Product) | The total value of goods produced and services provided in a country during one year. |  The country's GDP grew by 2% last year.  这个国家的国内生产总值去年增长了2%。 | synonyms: output, production | antonyms:  (GDP itself doesn't have a direct antonym; a decrease might be represented by terms like "decline," "contraction," or "recession.") | noun
