non-valuable:/ˌnɒnˈvæljuəbl/ | adj. | 无价值的，不值钱的 | Not valuable; having little or no worth. |  The old newspapers were non-valuable and were thrown away. 旧报纸没有什么价值，被扔掉了。| The trinkets she found in the attic were non-valuable but sentimentally precious. 她在阁楼里找到的小饰品不值钱，但在情感上很珍贵。 | 近义词: worthless, valueless, cheap | 反义词: valuable, precious, costly | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "valuable" (from Old French "valuabel," ultimately from Latin "valere" - to be strong, be worth).
