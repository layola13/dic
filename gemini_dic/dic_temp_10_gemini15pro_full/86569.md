heliconia:/ˌhɛlɪˈkoʊniə/| n. |鹤望兰;蝎尾蕉|A tropical American plant with large, brightly colored, banana-like leaves and flowers.|例句：The heliconia flowers added a vibrant splash of color to the garden. 鹤望兰的花朵为花园增添了鲜艳的色彩。

例句：Heliconias are popular ornamental plants in tropical regions. 鹤望兰是热带地区流行的观赏植物。|近义词：bird of paradise, lobster claw|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *Ἑλικώνιος (Helikṓnios)*  "of Helicon", from *Ἑλικών (Helikṓn)*, a mountain in Boeotia sacred to the Muses.  Linnaeus chose this name because of the supposed resemblance of the plant to Musa, the banana plant, itself believed to be named after Antonius Musa, physician to Augustus.
