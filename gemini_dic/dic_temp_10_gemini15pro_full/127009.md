NASCAR: /ˈnæskɑːr/ (US) /ˈnæskɑː(r)/ (UK)
n.
National Association for Stock Car Auto Racing (纳斯卡赛车)
An American auto racing sanctioning and operating company that is best known for stock car racing.
例句：NASCAR races are incredibly popular in the Southern United States.  纳斯卡赛车在美国南部非常受欢迎。

例句：He dreams of becoming a NASCAR driver. 他梦想成为一名纳斯卡赛车手。

近义词：stock car racing, auto racing
反义词:  None (No direct antonym for an organization)
词性: noun
