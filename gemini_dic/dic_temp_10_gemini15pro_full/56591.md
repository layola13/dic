donate:/ˈdoʊneɪt/ (US) /dəʊˈneɪt/ (UK)| v. |捐赠，捐献|To give money or goods to a charity or other organization.|He donated $100 to the Red Cross. 他向红十字会捐赠了100美元。|近义词：contribute, give, bestow|反义词:receive, take|词性:动词

donation:/doʊˈneɪʃən/ (US) /dəʊˈneɪʃən/ (UK)| n. |捐赠，捐款|Something that is given to a charity or other organization.|The library received a large donation of books. 图书馆收到了一大笔书籍捐赠。|近义词：contribution, gift, offering|反义词:receipt|词性:名词

donator:/ˈdoʊneɪtər/ (US) /dəʊˈneɪtə(r)/ (UK) | n. |捐赠者，捐献者|A person who donates something.|The donator wished to remain anonymous.  捐赠者希望保持匿名。|近义词：contributor, giver, benefactor|反义词:recipient|词性:名词
