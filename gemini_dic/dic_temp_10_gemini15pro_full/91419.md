hyenic:/haɪˈiːnɪk/
adjective
鬣狗似的；食腐动物似的；残忍的
Resembling or characteristic of a hyena; scavenging; rapacious.
The hyenic laughter echoed through the halls.  鬣狗般的笑声回荡在大厅里。
The hyenic behavior of the vultures was unsettling. 秃鹫食腐动物似的行为令人不安。
近义词:rapacious, predatory, scavenging, cruel
反义词:generous, kind, compassionate
词性:adjective
