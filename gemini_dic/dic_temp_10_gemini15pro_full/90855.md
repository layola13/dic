hungering:/ˈhʌŋɡərɪŋ/ | adj. |渴望的，极度需要的| Feeling a strong desire or craving for something, especially food.|  She had a hungering look in her eyes as she stared at the cake. 她盯着蛋糕，眼神里充满了渴望。| synonyms: craving, yearning, longing, desiring | antonyms: satisfied, sated, full, content | adjective

Etymology: From the Old English *hungrian*, from *hungor* "hunger".  The "-ing" suffix transforms the noun into a present participle, which can function as an adjective.
