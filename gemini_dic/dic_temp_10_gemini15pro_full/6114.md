ametabolian:/ˌeɪmɛtəˈbɒlɪən/
adjective
无变态的，不变态发育的
(of an insect) not undergoing metamorphosis.  Developing directly into the adult form without a pupal stage.
Silverfish are ametabolous insects.  衣鱼是无变态昆虫。
近义词:ametabolic
反义词:metabolic, holometabolous
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek  *ἀ- (a-, 'not') + *μεταβολ- (metabol-, 'change') + *-ικός (-ikós, '-ic').
