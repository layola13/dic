ltd:/ˌɛl tiː ˈdiː/
abbreviation
limited
有限的；股份有限公司
A common abbreviation used after a company's name, indicating that it is a private limited company.
Example: Acme Widgets Ltd. is a leading manufacturer.  艾克米小部件有限公司是一家领先的制造商。
近义词:Inc., Corp.
反义词:unlimited
noun
