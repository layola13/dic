creepiness:/ˈkriːpinəs/| n.|令人毛骨悚然; 阴森森的感觉|The creepiness of the abandoned house made me shiver.  那栋废弃的房子的阴森感让我不寒而栗。|例句：The creepiness of the dark alley was palpable. 黑暗小巷的阴森感非常明显。

例句：The movie's creepiness was enhanced by the unsettling soundtrack.  这部电影的恐怖感因令人不安的配乐而增强。


近义词：eeriness, spookiness, uneasiness|反义词:comfort, pleasantness|词性:名词
