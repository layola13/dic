burrow:/ˈbɜːroʊ/ (US) /ˈbʌrəʊ/ (UK)| n. v.|洞穴，挖洞|A hole or tunnel dug by an animal as a dwelling, refuge, or passageway.  To dig such a hole or tunnel.|The rabbit disappeared down its burrow. 兔子消失在它的洞穴里。  The moles burrowed deep into the ground. 鼹鼠在地下挖得很深。|近义词：hole, tunnel, den, lair|反义词: mound, hill|noun, verb
