disclaims:/dɪsˈkleɪmz/
|v.|否认; 放弃; 拒绝承认|
|The company disclaims all responsibility for the accident.  该公司否认对这起事故负有任何责任。|
|He disclaimed any knowledge of the stolen goods. 他声称对被盗的货物一无所知。|
|近义词:deny, renounce, repudiate|
|反义词:acknowledge, admit, claim, profess|
|词性:动词|


disclaim:/dɪsˈkleɪm/
|n.|否认; 放弃; 拒绝承认; 免责声明|
|The advertisement included a disclaimer about the product's effectiveness.  该广告包含一项关于产品有效性的免责声明。|
|The website has a disclaimer about the accuracy of its information.  该网站有一份关于其信息准确性的免责声明。|
|近义词:denial, renunciation, repudiation|
|反义词:acknowledgment, admission, claim|
|词性:名词|
