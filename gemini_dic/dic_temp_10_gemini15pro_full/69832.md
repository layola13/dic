ferreters:/ˈfɛrɪtərz/
noun
雪貂饲养者；搜寻者
People who keep ferrets as pets or those who use ferrets for hunting (typically rabbits).
例句：The ferreters were preparing their animals for the upcoming rabbit hunt. 雪貂饲养者正在为即将到来的兔子狩猎准备他们的动物。

例句：Experienced ferreters know how to handle their animals safely and effectively.  有经验的雪貂饲养者知道如何安全有效地处理他们的动物。
近义词：hunters, searchers
反义词: N/A
词性: noun

Etymology:  From "ferret" (noun, meaning a small, domesticated, weasel-like mammal used for hunting rabbits) + "-er" (suffix forming agent nouns, indicating a person who performs an action or works with something).  "Ferret" itself comes from Old French "furet" meaning "little thief," ultimately from Latin "fur," meaning "thief."
