commensal:/kəˈmɛnsəl/| adj. n.|共生的，共生生物|An organism that lives in close association with another organism, benefiting from the relationship without harming or helping the host.|Barnacles are commensal on whales. 藤壶与鲸鱼共生。|近义词：symbiotic, mutualistic|反义词：parasitic|词性:形容词, 名词

commensal:/kəˈmɛnsəl/| n.|共生生物|An organism that lives in close association with another organism, benefiting from the relationship without harming or helping the host.|The remora fish is a commensal of the shark. 鮣鱼是鲨鱼的共生生物。|近义词：symbiont|反义词：parasite|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *commensalis*, from *com-* (together) + *mensa* (table).  Literally "eating at the same table".
