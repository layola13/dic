alligate: /ˈælɪɡeɪt/ | v. |  指派；委托；归于 | To assign, attribute, or connect something to a particular cause, person, or thing. |  He alligated his failure to bad luck. 他把自己的失败归咎于运气不好。 | 近义词: ascribe, attribute, impute | 反义词: disavow, deny | 词性: 动词
Etymology: From Late Latin *alligatus, past participle of alligare ("to bind to, tie to"). From Latin ad ("to") + ligare ("to bind").
