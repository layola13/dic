diorthotic:/ˌdaɪɔːrˈθɒtɪk/ | adj. |矫正的，矫形的 |Relating to or serving to correct or improve posture or skeletal alignment.  It refers to devices or treatments designed to correct deformities or improve musculoskeletal function.| Example:  She was prescribed diorthotic insoles to correct her flat feet. 她被开了矫正扁平足的矫形鞋垫。| Example: The diorthotic brace helped to stabilize his injured knee.  这个矫形支架帮助稳定了他受伤的膝盖。|Synonym:  orthopedic, corrective, remedial |Antonym:  deforming, worsening |adjective
