boofhead:/ˈbuːfhed/| n. |傻瓜，笨蛋|A derogatory term for a stupid or foolish person.  It implies a lack of intelligence or common sense. |Those boofheads locked their keys in the car again. 那些笨蛋又把钥匙锁在车里了。|Synonyms: idiot, fool, moron, dimwit, airhead, nitwit|Antonyms: genius, intellectual, bright spark|noun
