enlivener:/ɪnˈlaɪvənər/| n.| 使活跃的人或物; 增添生气的人或物|Something or someone that enlivens something else. |The comedian was the enlivener of the party.  这位喜剧演员是聚会的活跃气氛者。|Synonyms: stimulant, invigorator, animater|Antonyms: depressant, dampener|noun
