impermeably:/ɪmˈpɜːrmiəbli/
adv.
不能渗透地；不透水地； непроницаемо
in a way that does not allow fluids to pass through.
Raincoats are designed to be impermeably layered to keep you dry.  雨衣设计成不透水层以保持干燥。
The virus was sealed impermeably within the container.  病毒被密封在容器内，无法渗透。
近义词: imperviously, impenetrably
反义词: permeably, porous
词性: 副词
