nonscientific:/ˌnɒnˌsaɪənˈtɪfɪk/ | adj. | 非科学的 | Not based on or conforming to the principles of science; not learned by or based on scientific methods. |  His approach to the problem was completely nonscientific.  他解决问题的方法完全不科学。 | Examples of nonscientific methods include relying on anecdotes, intuition or traditional beliefs.  非科学方法的例子包括依赖轶事、直觉或传统信仰。 | 近义词: unscientific, subjective, empirical | 反义词: scientific, objective, methodical | 词性: adjective

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + scientific (adjective).  From Latin *non* (not) and *scientificus* (scientific).
