embale:/ɪmˈbeɪl/ /ɛmˈbeɪl/| vt.|打包，装入包内|to pack or wrap tightly, especially in bales|The workers embaled the hay for transport. 工人们把干草打包以便运输。|近义词：pack, bundle, wrap|反义词:unpack, unwrap|词性:动词
embale:/ɪmˈbeɪl/ /ɛmˈbeɪl/| n.|（打包的）大包，大捆|a large wrapped or bound bundle of material|The embales of cotton were stacked high in the warehouse.  仓库里堆放着高高的棉花包。|近义词：bale, package, bundle|反义词:None|词性:名词
