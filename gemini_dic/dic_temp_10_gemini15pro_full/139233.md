oblatrate:/ˈɒblətreɪt/ /ˈɑːblətreɪt/
verb
吠叫；大声斥责
To bark or clamor vehemently; to rail at vociferously.
The dog oblatrated furiously at the mailman. 狗对着邮递员狂吠不止。
近义词：bark, yell, clamor, rant
反义词：whisper, murmur
词性：v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin oblatrare, from ob- (against) + latrare (to bark).
