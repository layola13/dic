lectures:/ˈlektʃərz/ (US), /ˈlektʃəz/ (UK)
n.|讲座，讲课；教训，训斥|A series of talks to a group of people on a particular subject, especially as part of a university or college course; a long serious talk that someone gives you in order to criticize your behaviour|例句：The professor's lectures on history were fascinating.  这位教授的历史讲座引人入胜。|近义词：talks, speeches, addresses, sermons, presentations|反义词:discussions, conversations
词性:名词
