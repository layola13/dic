parapsoriasis:/ˌpærəpsəˈraɪəsɪs/| n. |副牛皮癣|A group of skin conditions that resemble psoriasis but are distinct and generally less severe.|He was diagnosed with parapsoriasis, a relief as it wasn't full-blown psoriasis.|Synonyms: Premycotic eruption, parapsoriasis en plaques|Antonyms:  None (as it's a medical condition, a direct opposite doesn't exist)|noun
