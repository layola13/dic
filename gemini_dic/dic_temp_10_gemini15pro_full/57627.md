draughting: /ˈdrɑːftɪŋ/ /ˈdræftɪŋ/
n.
起草；制图；绘图；通风
The process of making detailed technical drawings or plans, typically of buildings or machinery.  Also refers to the movement of air through a space.
The architect spent weeks on the draughting of the new stadium.  建筑师花了几个星期的时间绘制新体育场的设计图。
The chimney provided excellent draughting for the fire.  烟囱为火提供了极好的通风。
drafting, drawing, planning, designing
n/a
noun

draughting: /ˈdrɑːftɪŋ/ /ˈdræftɪŋ/
adj.
用于绘图的
Relating to or used for draughting.
