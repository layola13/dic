medicares:/ˈmɛdɪˌkɛərz/
n.
医疗保险（Medicare的复数形式，通常用单数）
the plural form of Medicare, which refers to the U.S. federal health insurance program for people who are 65 or older, certain younger people with disabilities, and people with End-Stage Renal Disease (permanent kidney failure requiring dialysis or a transplant).  It's rarely used as "medicares," as the program is singular.
Medicare helps pay for hospital stays, doctor visits, and other medical services.  (This sentence refers to the singular "Medicare.")
近义词: health insurance, government healthcare
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) private insurance
