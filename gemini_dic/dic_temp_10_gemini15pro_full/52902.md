dimmer:/ˈdɪmər/| n. |调光器，光线调节器|A device for varying the brightness of an electric light.|The dimmer switch allows you to adjust the lighting to suit the mood. 调光开关可以让你调节灯光以适应气氛。|近义词：rheostat, control|反义词:brightener|noun

dimmer:/ˈdɪmər/| adj. |(光线)较暗的，昏暗的|Not so bright or clear.|The room was dimmer than usual because one of the light bulbs had blown. 房间比平时暗，因为一个灯泡坏了。|近义词：darker, fainter|反义词:brighter|adjective
