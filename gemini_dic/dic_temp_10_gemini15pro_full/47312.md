de-licenses:/diːˈlaɪsənz/
verb
取消许可，撤销执照
To remove or revoke a license from someone or something, allowing them to operate or function without official permission.
The government de-licensed several businesses that failed to meet safety regulations. 政府取消了几个不符合安全规定的企业的许可证。
The new regulations de-licensed certain types of financial transactions. 新规定取消了某些类型的金融交易的许可。
synonyms: deregulate, liberalize
antonyms: license, regulate, authorize
词性:v.
