jugsful:/ˈdʒʌɡsfʊl/
n.
满满几罐; 几罐的量
The quantity contained in a specified number of jugs.
She brought three jugsful of water from the well. 她从井里提了满满三罐水。
近义词:  jugfuls, several jugs of
反义词:  a little, a drop
词性: 名词
