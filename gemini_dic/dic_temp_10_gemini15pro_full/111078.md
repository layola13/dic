listful:/ˈlɪstfəl/ | adj. | 注意倾听的；乐于助人的；专心的 | Attentive; willing to help; mindful. | She was always listful of her students' needs. 她总是很关注学生的需要。|  He was a listful and compassionate doctor. 他是一位细心而富有同情心的医生。 | 近义词: attentive, observant, mindful, heedful, considerate | 反义词: inattentive, unobservant, heedless, inconsiderate | 词性:形容词

词源:  From "list" (to listen) + "-ful" (suffix indicating abundance).  源自“list”（倾听）+“-ful”（表示丰富的后缀）。
