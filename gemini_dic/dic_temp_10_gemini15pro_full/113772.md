macropyramid:/ˈmækroʊˈpɪrəmɪd/ | n. |巨型金字塔; 巨锥体 | A very large pyramid.  A pyramid-shaped structure, especially one that is unusually large. |  The macropyramid dominated the city's skyline. 巨型金字塔俯瞰着城市的天际线。| synonym: mega-pyramid, colossal pyramid | antonym: micropryamid | noun

Etymology:
macro- (Greek): large, long
pyramid (Greek):  a monumental structure with a square or triangular base and sloping sides that meet in a point at the top.
