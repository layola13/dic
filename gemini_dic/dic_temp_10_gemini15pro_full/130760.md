nonamphibiously:/ˌnɒnæmˈfɪbiəsli/
adverb
非两栖地; 不像两栖动物那样
Not in an amphibious manner; not able to live both on land and in water.
The troops landed nonamphibiously, using a standard port facility.  部队没有进行两栖登陆，而是使用了标准的港口设施。
The marine iguana, unlike most lizards, can live amphibiously, while the chuckwalla is strictly nonamphibious. 海鬣蜥不像大多数蜥蜴那样可以两栖生活，而石鬣蜥则完全是非两栖动物。
Near Antonyms: amphibiously
Antonyms: terrestrially, aquatically
Word Family: amphibious, amphibian
