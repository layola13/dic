bell-ringers:/ˈbel rɪŋərz/| n.|敲钟人；（尤指）教堂司事；（集合称）一组短而有趣的活动（尤指用于课堂开始时）；预示即将发生之事的信号|People employed to ring bells, especially church bells. Also, a set of short, engaging activities, especially used to start a class.  Can also refer to something that signals an upcoming event.|The bell-ringers called the parishioners to Sunday service.|近义词：ringers, chime-ringers, sextons (for church bell-ringers), warm-up activities, icebreakers (for classroom activities), harbingers, precursors (for signals)|反义词: (No direct antonym, depends on the context)
