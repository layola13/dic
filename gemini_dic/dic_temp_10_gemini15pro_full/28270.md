captivates:/ˈkæptɪveɪts/
verb
迷住，使着迷
holds the attention of someone by being extremely interesting, exciting, pleasant, or attractive
Her singing captivated the audience. 她的歌声迷住了观众。
近义词:enchants, fascinates, charms, enthralls
反义词:bores, repels, disgusts
词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin captivatus, past participle of captivare "to take captive," from Latin captivus "captive," from captus, past participle of capere "to take, seize."
