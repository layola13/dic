deescalation:/ˌdiːɛskəˈleɪʃən/
n.
缓和；降级；减轻
the reduction of the intensity or extent of something, especially violence or hostility.
The de-escalation of the conflict was welcomed by all parties.  冲突的缓和受到了各方的欢迎。
Negotiations led to the de-escalation of tensions between the two countries.  谈判导致两国紧张局势的缓和。
近义词:reduction, lessening, mitigation, alleviation
反义词:escalation, intensification, aggravation
词性:名词


词源:  From de- + escalation, from escalate, from scala "ladder"
