nonaccountable:/ˌnɑːnəˈkaʊntəbl/| adj.|不需负责的，不负责任的，无法解释的|Not required to account for one's actions; not responsible.  Also, unable to be explained.|The CEO's actions were deemed nonaccountable, leading to his dismissal. 首席执行官的行为被认为是不负责任的，导致他被解雇。|Synonyms: irresponsible, unanswerable, inexplicable|Antonyms: responsible, accountable, explainable|adjective
