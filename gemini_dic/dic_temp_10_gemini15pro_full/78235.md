gerbus: /ˈdʒɜːrbəs/ | n. | 跳鼠; 沙鼠 | A rodent of the Dipodidae family, typically having long hind legs for jumping and a long tail. |  Gerbils are popular pets because they are relatively low-maintenance. 跳鼠是很受欢迎的宠物，因为它们相对容易照顾。

Gerbils are native to the deserts of Africa and Asia. 跳鼠原产于非洲和亚洲的沙漠。| 近义词：jerboa, kangaroo rat | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词
