glittered:/ˈɡlɪtərd/| v.|闪耀，闪烁，闪光|shone with many small, bright flashes of light|The diamonds glittered in the sunlight. 钻石在阳光下闪闪发光。|近义词：sparkled, shone, shimmered, glistened|反义词:dulled, faded
词性:动词

glittered:/ˈɡlɪtərd/| adj.|(literary) 闪耀的，闪光的|shining with many small, bright flashes of light|The glittered snow covered the mountains. 闪闪发光的雪覆盖着群山。|近义词：sparkling, shining, shimmering, glistening|反义词:dull, faded
词性:形容词


词源: From Middle English *gliteren, from Old Norse glitra (“to glitter, sparkle”), from Proto-Germanic *glitrōną (“to glitter”). Cognate with Swedish glittra (“to glitter”), Danish glitre (“to glitter”), Icelandic glitra (“to glitter”), German glitzern (“to glitter, sparkle”).
