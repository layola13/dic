foreseers:/fɔːrˈsiːərz/
| n. |预言者，预见者|People who can foresee future events or trends. |The ancient foreseers predicted the fall of the empire. 古代的预言者预言了帝国的衰落。|近义词：prophets, visionaries, prognosticators|反义词：followers, reactors|词性:Noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *foreseer*, from *foreseen* + *-er*, from Old English *foresēon* (“to foresee”), from *fore-* (“before”) + *sēon* (“to see”).
