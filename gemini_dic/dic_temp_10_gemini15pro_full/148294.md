oversoftened: /ˌoʊvərˈsɔːfənd/ | adj. | 过度软化的 | Excessively softened; made too soft.  This term often applies to fabrics, water, or other materials that have been treated to reduce hardness, but have been treated for too long or with too much softening agent. | The oversoftened towels felt limp and unabsorbent.  这些过度软化的毛巾摸起来软塌塌的，不吸水。 | The oversoftened water left a slippery residue on the dishes.  过度软化的水在盘子上留下了滑腻的残留物。 | 近义词：overly soft, excessively softened | 反义词：hard, firm, stiff | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (prefix indicating excess) + "softened" (past participle of "soften," from "soft" + "-en" (suffix forming verbs)).  "Soft" has Proto-Germanic roots.
