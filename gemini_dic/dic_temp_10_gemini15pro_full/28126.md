capitation:/ˌkæpɪˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 人头付费，按人收费 | A system of payment for health care services in which a physician or hospital is paid a fixed amount per patient for a specified period, regardless of the actual services provided. |  The clinic operates on a capitation basis, receiving a set fee for each enrolled patient.  这家诊所按人头收费，每位注册病人收取固定费用。

The doctor receives a capitation payment for each patient registered with his practice. 医生为每位在他诊所注册的病人收取人头费。 |  per capita payment, per patient payment | fee-for-service | noun
