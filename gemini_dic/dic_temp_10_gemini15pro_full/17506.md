beastings:/ˈbiːstɪŋz/
noun
初乳，兽乳
The first milk produced by a mammal after giving birth, rich in antibodies and nutrients.
The farmer collected the beastings from the cow to feed the newborn calf.  农场主收集了母牛的初乳来喂养新生的小牛。
近义词:colostrum, first milk
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性:n.
