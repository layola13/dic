he-man:/ˈhiːmæn/| n.| 男子汉;猛男;大力士|A strong, muscular man, often a hero or action figure.|例句：He's a real he-man. 他是个真汉子。|例句：The toy store sold a variety of he-men action figures. 玩具店出售各种各样的猛男动作玩偶。|近义词：strongman, muscleman, hunk|反义词: weakling, wimp|词性:名词

词源:  A compound formed from *he* and *man*. Popularized by the *Masters of the Universe* franchise starting in the 1980s.
