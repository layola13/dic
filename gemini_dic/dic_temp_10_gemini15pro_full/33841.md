circumnavigate: /ˌsɜːkəmˈnævɪɡeɪt/ | v. | 环航世界；绕...航行 | To sail or travel completely around (something, especially the world). | Magellan's expedition was the first to circumnavigate the globe. 麦哲伦的探险队是第一个环球航行的。

The satellite circumnavigates the Earth every 90 minutes. 这颗卫星每90分钟绕地球一圈。


近义词: sail around, travel around, circle, globe-trot | 反义词: stay put | 词性: 动词
