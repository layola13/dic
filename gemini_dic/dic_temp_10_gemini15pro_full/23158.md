bovate:/ˈbəʊveɪt/ /ˈboʊveɪt/
n.
一份耕地; 牛轭地
A unit of land area, originally as much as an ox could plough in a year, varying from about eight to eighteen acres.
The farmer worked his small bovate diligently.  农民辛勤地耕作着他那一小块牛轭地。

The family held a bovate of land in the village. 这个家庭在村里拥有一份牛轭地。

近义词:oxgang, hide (of land)
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "smallholding" as a contrasting term for a smaller piece of land)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Medieval Latin *bovata, from Latin bos (“ox”) + -ata.
