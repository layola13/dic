mikados:/mɪˈkɑːdoʊz/ | n. | 御所, 日本天皇, 一种捡木棒的游戏 |  A game of skill in which a bundle of thin sticks are allowed to fall in a heap, and each player in turn tries to remove one stick at a time without disturbing the others, the winner being the one who removes the most sticks. Also, any of the sticks used in this game. Formerly, a term used to refer to the Emperor of Japan. | The children were playing mikado in the living room. 孩子们在客厅玩捡木棒游戏。| The emperor, once known as the Mikado, now holds a largely symbolic role.  曾经被称为天皇的皇帝，现在主要扮演着象征性的角色。 | synonyms: emperor, sovereign, ruler, monarch, pick-up-sticks | antonyms: subject, commoner | noun
