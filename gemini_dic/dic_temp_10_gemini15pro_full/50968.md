desilts:/diːˈsɪlt/
verb
去除淤泥;清除淤泥
to remove silt from (a river, harbor, etc.)
例句：The dredging machine desilts the harbor regularly.  挖泥船定期清除港口的淤泥。

例句：Heavy rains desilted the river, making it clearer and deeper.  暴雨冲走了河里的淤泥，使河水更清澈更深。
近义词：dredge, clear, clean
反义词：silt up, clog
词性：v.
