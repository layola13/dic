bursaries:/ˈbɜːrsəriz/
n.
奖学金，助学金
A grant, especially one provided to enable a student to attend school or university.
She was awarded a bursary to study abroad. 她获得了出国留学的奖学金。
The university offers several bursaries for low-income students.  这所大学为低收入学生提供几项助学金。
scholarships, grants, awards, fellowships
loans, debts
noun
