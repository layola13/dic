feldspar:/ˈfɛldˌspɑːr/ /ˈfɛlˌspɑːr/| n.|长石|Any of a group of crystalline minerals that consist of aluminum silicates of potassium, sodium, calcium, or barium and that are essential constituents of nearly all crystalline rocks.|Feldspar is the most abundant mineral in the Earth's crust. 长石是地壳中最丰富的矿物。|The granite countertop was speckled with feldspar. 花岗岩台面上散布着长石。|Synonyms: albite, anorthite, orthoclase|Antonyms: None|Noun

feldspathic:/ˌfɛldˈspæθɪk/| adj.|长石的|Of, relating to, or containing feldspar.|The feldspathic rock glittered in the sunlight. 长石质的岩石在阳光下闪闪发光。|The geologist identified the sample as a feldspathic sandstone. 地质学家将该样品鉴定为长石砂岩。|Synonyms: None|Antonyms: None|Adjective
