essaylet: /ˈɛseɪlɛt/ | n. | 小短文，短论 | A short essay; a brief literary composition. |  She wrote an essaylet on the importance of recycling for the school newspaper. 她为校报写了一篇关于回收重要性的小短文。| Example: He submitted an essaylet for the writing competition.  他提交了一篇短文参加写作比赛。 | synonym: short essay, vignette, sketch, piece | antonym: novel, dissertation, thesis | noun
