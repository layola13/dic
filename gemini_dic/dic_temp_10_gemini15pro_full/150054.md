palpitations:/ˌpælpɪˈteɪʃənz/| n. |心悸，心跳加快|A noticeably rapid, strong, or irregular heartbeat due to agitation, exertion, or illness.|例句：She experienced heart palpitations after drinking too much coffee. 她喝太多咖啡后感到心悸。

例句：He felt palpitations in his chest when he realized he was late for the meeting.  当他意识到自己开会迟到时，感到胸口一阵心悸。|近义词：fluttering, throbbing, pounding, racing heart|反义词:regular heartbeat|词性:noun
