entitle:/ɪnˈtaɪtl/ /ɛnˈtaɪtl/| vt.|赋予…权利；给…命名|To give someone the right to have or do something; to give a title to a book, film, etc.|例句：The ticket entitles you to a free drink. 这张票可以让你免费获得一杯饮料。

例句：He entitled the painting "Sunset Over the Lake". 他给这幅画取名为“湖上日落”。|近义词：authorize, empower, name, designate|反义词: disqualify, prohibit|词性:动词
