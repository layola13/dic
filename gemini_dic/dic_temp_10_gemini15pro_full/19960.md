bioenergy:/ˈbaɪoʊˌɛnərdʒi/| n. |生物能源|Energy derived from biomass (organic matter). It includes wood, wood waste, black liquor from the paper manufacturing process, landfill gas, biogas, ethanol, biodiesel, and other biofuels.|Bioenergy is a renewable source of energy. 生物能源是一种可再生能源。|Bioenergy offers a sustainable alternative to fossil fuels. 生物能源提供了一种可持续的化石燃料替代品。|近义词：renewable energy, biomass energy|反义词：fossil fuel|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
bio- + energy
bio-:  from Greek bios "life"
energy: from Late Latin energia, from Greek energeia "activity, operation," from energos "active, working," from en- "in, at" + ergon "work"
