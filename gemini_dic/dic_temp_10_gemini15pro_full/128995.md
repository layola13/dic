nipple-like:/ˈnɪpəlˌlaɪk/
adjective
乳头状的，像乳头的
Resembling a nipple in shape or form.
The cactus had nipple-like protrusions all over its surface.  仙人掌的表面布满了乳头状的突起。
The barnacles formed nipple-like clusters on the rocks. 藤壶在岩石上形成了乳头状的簇群。
