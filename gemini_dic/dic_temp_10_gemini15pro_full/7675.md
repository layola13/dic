anodizes:/ˈænəˌdaɪzɪz/
verb
阳极氧化处理
coats (a metal, especially aluminum) with a protective oxide layer by an electrolytic process.
The company anodizes aluminum parts to make them more resistant to corrosion.  这家公司对铝制零件进行阳极氧化处理，以提高其耐腐蚀性。
oxidizes, plates
reduces, strips
v.
