bundly:/ˈbʌndli/| adj.| 捆扎的，成捆的；像捆的|  Resembling or characteristic of a bundle; bundled.  This word is not commonly used and may be considered archaic or dialectal.  It's likely formed by adding the suffix "-ly" to the noun "bundle."| The hay was stacked in bundly heaps.  干草堆成捆状。| The thick, bundly wool made the sweater very warm. 厚厚的、捆状的羊毛使这件毛衣非常暖和。 |近义词：bundled, compact, clustered |反义词: loose, scattered |词性: adjective

词源: From "bundle" + "-ly". "Bundle" comes from Middle English *bundel, from Low German or Dutch *bundel, *bondel, of uncertain origin.
