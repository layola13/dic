bicaudal:/baɪˈkɔːdəl/
adjective
双尾的，两尾的
Having two tails, or relating to the possession of two tails.
A bicaudal mutation in Drosophila can result in flies with two tails. 果蝇的双尾突变会导致果蝇长出两条尾巴。
The bicaudal embryo displayed a duplicated posterior region. 双尾胚胎显示出重复的后部区域。
synonym: two-tailed
antonym: single-tailed
词性: adjective
