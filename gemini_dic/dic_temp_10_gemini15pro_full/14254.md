autodynamic:/ˌɔːtəʊdaɪˈnæmɪk/ | adj. |自动力学的；自动的| Relating to or characterized by self-generated motion or force.  It describes something that is capable of moving or changing by its own power. |  The autodynamic properties of the robot allowed it to navigate complex terrain.  这个机器人的自动力学特性使它能够在复杂的地形中航行。

The autodynamic system adjusted its output based on real-time feedback.  这个自动系统根据实时反馈调整其输出。 | 近义词：automatic, self-propelled, autonomous | 反义词：manual, inert | 词性：形容词

Etymology:  "auto-" (Greek origin) meaning "self" + "dynamic" (Greek origin) meaning "power, force".
