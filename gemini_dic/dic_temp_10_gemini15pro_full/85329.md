harpooner:/hɑːrˈpuːnər/ | n. | 捕鲸者；鱼叉手 | A person who uses a harpoon to hunt whales or large fish. | The harpooner stood ready to strike. 鱼叉手准备好了要出击。|  The experienced harpooner aimed carefully before throwing his weapon.  经验丰富的鱼叉手在投掷武器之前仔细瞄准。 | synonyms: whaler, fisherman | antonyms: None | noun

harpoon:/hɑːrˈpuːn/ | n. | 鱼叉 | A barbed spear-like weapon used for hunting whales and other large fish. | The whale was struck by a harpoon.  鲸鱼被鱼叉击中。| They used a harpoon to catch the giant tuna. 他们用鱼叉捕获了巨大的金枪鱼。 | synonyms: spear, lance | antonyms: None | noun
