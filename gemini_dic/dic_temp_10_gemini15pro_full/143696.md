outlearn:/ˌaʊtˈlɜːrn/
verb
超越学习；在学习上胜过
To surpass someone in learning; to learn faster or more effectively than someone else.
The young apprentice quickly outlearned his master.  年轻的学徒很快在学习上超过了他的师傅。
The AI system can outlearn human doctors in diagnosing certain diseases. 这个AI系统在诊断某些疾病方面可以超越人类医生。
近义词: surpass, excel, outstrip
反义词: lag behind, fall behind
词性: verb
