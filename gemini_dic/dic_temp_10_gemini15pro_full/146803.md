overimitates:/ˌoʊvərˈɪmɪteɪts/
verb
过度模仿
To copy or emulate someone or something excessively, often to a point where it is inappropriate or counterproductive.
The child overimitates his older brother, even copying his mistakes.  这个孩子过度模仿他的哥哥，甚至连错误也照搬。
He overimitates the comedian's mannerisms, making his performance seem forced. 他过度模仿那个喜剧演员的举止，使他的表演显得做作。
近义词:  mimics excessively, copies too much, apes, parrots
反义词:  innovates, originates, creates
词性: verb
