overcommunication:/ˌoʊvərˌkəmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.|过度沟通；过分沟通|The act of communicating excessively, often to the point of redundancy or inefficiency.|例句：Overcommunication can sometimes lead to confusion. 过度沟通有时会导致混乱。

例句：The team suffered from overcommunication, with everyone sending multiple emails about the same issue.  团队因过度沟通而受阻，每个人都就同一问题发送多封电子邮件。
近义词：overexplaining, excessive communication
反义词: undercommunication, lack of communication
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "communication" (from Late Latin communicatio, from communicare "to share, impart," from communis "common").
