celebrity: /səˈlebrəti/ | n. | 名人；名声；著名；|A famous person, especially in entertainment or sport. |例句：The celebrity arrived surrounded by bodyguards.  这位名人到达时周围簇拥着保镖。

例句：He achieved celebrity status after starring in the hit movie.  他主演了这部热门电影后获得了名人地位。| 近义词：star, VIP, icon, luminary | 反义词：nobody, unknown | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin celebritas (“fame, renown”), from celeber (“frequented, populous, famous, renowned”), from celebro (“I frequent, celebrate”).
