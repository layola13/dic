haywire:/ˈheɪwaɪər/| adj. |混乱的，故障的| out of control; malfunctioning |例句：The computer system went haywire and deleted all the files. 电脑系统失控，删除了所有文件。|例句：My plans went haywire when my flight was cancelled. 我的航班取消后，我的计划全乱了。|近义词：amok, awry, chaotic, disordered, messed up|反义词：orderly, organized, functional|词性:形容词

词源: Originally referring to wire used for baling hay, which could become tangled and difficult to manage. This sense extended to machinery and other things that stopped working properly.
