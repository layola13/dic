casualist:/ˈkæʒʊəlɪst/
n.
不经意的人；随便的人；漠不关心的人
a person who shows a casual lack of concern; a careless person.
He was a casualist about his studies and rarely attended classes.  他对学习漫不经心，很少上课。
近义词：idler, slacker, dawdler
反义词：perfectionist, stickler, enthusiast
词性: 名词

casualist:/ˈkæʒʊəlɪst/
adj.
随便的；漫不经心的
showing a casual lack of concern; careless.
His casualist attitude towards his work eventually led to his dismissal. 他对工作的漫不经心的态度最终导致了他的解雇。
近义词：casual, nonchalant, indifferent
反义词：concerned, meticulous, diligent
词性: 形容词 (较少使用)
