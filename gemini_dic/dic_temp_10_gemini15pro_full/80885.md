governante:/ˈɡʌvənɑːnt/ | n. | 女家庭教师；女管家 | A woman employed to teach and train children in a private household; a woman responsible for the management of a household. |  She was employed as a governante to the young prince. 她受雇为年轻的王子担任家庭教师。

The governante ran the household efficiently.  这位女管家把家管理得井井有条。 | 近义词：governess, housekeeper, nanny | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 pupil, student  对于家庭教师身份，或 employer 对于管家身份) | 词性: noun


词源: From French gouvernante, present participle of gouverner "to govern," from Latin gubernare "to steer, guide, direct, govern."
