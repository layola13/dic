colorized: /ˈkʌləraɪzd/ | adj. | 着色的，彩色的 |  Converted from black and white to color.  A black and white film or photograph that has been digitally or otherwise altered to appear in color. | The documentary used colorized footage of World War II.  这部纪录片使用了二战的彩色化镜头。| colorized photos brought history to life. 这些彩色照片使历史鲜活起来。 | synonyms: colored, tinted, painted, dyed | antonyms: black and white, monochrome, grayscale | adjective

colorize: /ˈkʌləraɪz/ | v. | 给...着色，彩色化 | To add color to something that was previously black and white. | They colorized the old black and white movie. 他们把那部黑白老电影彩色化了。 | The photos were colorized using advanced software.  这些照片是用先进的软件进行彩色化的。 | synonyms: color, tint, paint, dye | antonyms:  desaturate, bleach, make monochrome | verb

词源: color (颜色) + -ize (动词后缀，表示“使成为…;使…化”)
