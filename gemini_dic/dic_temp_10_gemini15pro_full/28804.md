caromel: /ˈkærəməl/ | n. | 焦糖，焦糖色 | A brown, brittle, and sweet substance made by heating sugar, or a food containing this.  It can also refer to the brown color of burnt sugar. |  The chef drizzled caramel over the dessert.  厨师在甜点上淋了焦糖。

The cake had a rich caramel flavor.  蛋糕有浓郁的焦糖味。 | synonyms: toffee, butterscotch | antonyms: bland, tasteless | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French "caramel,"  from Spanish "caramelo" (sugar candy), probably of Arabic origin.
