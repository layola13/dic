overgarrisoning:/ˌoʊvərˈɡærɪsənɪŋ/
n.
过度驻军，驻军过多
The act of stationing too many troops in a particular location, resulting in logistical strain and potential negative impact on the local population.
The overgarrisoning of the small island strained its limited resources.  过度驻军给这个小岛有限的资源带来了压力。
The general realized the overgarrisoning was a tactical error. 将军意识到过度驻军是一个战术错误。
近义词:overdeployment, excessive garrisoning
反义词:undergarrisoning, insufficient garrisoning
词性:名词
