contraindication:/ˌkɒntrəˌɪndɪˈkeɪʃn/ /ˌkɑːntrəˌɪndɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. |禁忌症；禁忌 |A specific situation in which a drug, procedure, or surgery should not be used because it may be harmful to the person.| This medication is contraindicated in patients with kidney disease. 这种药物禁用于肾病患者。| 近义词:prohibition, restriction | 反义词: indication | 词性:名词

contraindications:/ˌkɒntrəˌɪndɪˈkeɪʃnz/ /ˌkɑːntrəˌɪndɪˈkeɪʃnz/ | n. |禁忌症（复数）|The plural form of contraindication, referring to multiple factors that advise against a particular treatment or action.|  There are several contraindications for this type of surgery. 这类手术有几个禁忌症。| 近义词: prohibitions, restrictions | 反义词: indications | 词性:名词
