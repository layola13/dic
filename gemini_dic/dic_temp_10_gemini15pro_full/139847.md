octosepalous: /ˌɒktəˈsɛpələs/ | adj. | 八萼片的 | Having eight sepals.  A sepal is a part of the calyx of a flower, enclosing the petals and typically green and leaflike. |  例句：The newly discovered flower is octosepalous, a rare characteristic in this family.  这种新发现的花是八萼片的，这在这个科中是一个罕见的特征。

例句：Octosepalous flowers are less common than pentasepalous ones. 八萼片的花比五萼片的花少见。 | 近义词： eight-sepaled | 反义词： N/A | 词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "octo-" (Latin, meaning "eight") + "sepalous" (from "sepal" + "-ous," a suffix forming adjectives).  "Sepal" comes from New Latin "sepalum," coined by Necker in 1789, possibly from Greek "skepe," meaning "covering."
