antienthusiast:/ˌæntiɪnˈθuːziæst/| n.| 反热情者；缺乏热情的人|A person who is opposed to or lacking in enthusiasm.| He's such an antienthusiast; nothing seems to excite him. 他真是个缺乏热情的人；似乎没有什么能让他兴奋起来。|近义词：apathist, cynic, skeptic|反义词: enthusiast, zealot|noun
