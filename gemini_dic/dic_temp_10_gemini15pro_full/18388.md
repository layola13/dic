bellylike:/ˈbɛliˌlaɪk/| adj.|像肚子的，鼓起的|Resembling a belly; protuberant. |The pregnant woman had a bellylike bulge. 孕妇的肚子鼓起来了。|近义词：protuberant, bulging, swollen|反义词：flat, concave|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From 'belly' + '-like'.  'Belly' comes from Old English 'belg', meaning "bag, pouch, belly," related to Old Norse 'belgr' "bag, bellows." '-like' is a common suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling, similar to," from Old English '-lic'.
