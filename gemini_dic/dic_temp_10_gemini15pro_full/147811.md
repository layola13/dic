overproviding: /ˌoʊvərprəˈvaɪdɪŋ/ | v. | 过度提供，供应过多 | The government was criticized for overproviding free services, leading to inefficiency. 政府因过度提供免费服务而受到批评，这导致了效率低下。 |  Overproviding resources can sometimes stifle innovation.  过度提供资源有时会扼杀创新。 | 近义词: oversaturating, oversupplying | 反义词: underproviding, undersupplying | 词性: 动名词 (gerund) or present participle


Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *ubar "over, above") + "provide" (from Latin providēre "to foresee, take care of," from pro- "before" + vidēre "to see").
