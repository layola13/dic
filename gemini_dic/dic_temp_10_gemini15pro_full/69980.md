festschrift:/ˈfɛstʃrɪft/| n. |纪念文集； Festschrift |A collection of essays or articles written by colleagues and former students of a scholar, presented to that person as a mark of honor, usually on a special anniversary such as retirement.|A festschrift was published in honor of Professor Smith's 70th birthday.  史密斯教授70岁生日时，出版了一本纪念文集。|
|近义词：testimonial, commemoration, tribute|反义词: N/A|词性:noun|
