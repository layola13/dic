archegony:/ˌɑːrkiˈɡɒni/ | n. | 原始生殖，卵式生殖 | The development of an egg within an archegonium, as in mosses, ferns, and most gymnosperms.  It refers to the formation and development of the female reproductive organs and the subsequent development of the egg within them.  |  Archegony is a key feature of the reproductive cycle in many plant groups. 原始生殖是许多植物群繁殖周期的关键特征。|  Ferns reproduce by archegony. 蕨类植物通过卵式生殖繁殖。|  Synonyms:  oogenesis, gametogenesis | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
