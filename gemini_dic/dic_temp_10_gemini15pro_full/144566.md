outwander:/ˌaʊtˈwɑːndər/ | v. |漫游，漂泊，远游|To wander forth; to roam about; to travel far.|He outwandered his companions and got lost in the forest. 他比同伴走得更远，在森林里迷路了。|
例句：
The explorers outwandered into uncharted territory. 探险家们漫游到未知的领域。
She outwandered the boundaries of the kingdom. 她漫游到王国疆界之外。

近义词：roam, wander, stray, ramble, rove|
反义词：stay, remain|
词性：动词
