imperturbable:/ˌɪmpərˈtɜːrbəbəl/| adj.|沉着的，冷静的，泰然自若的|Not capable of being upset or agitated; calm.|例句：He remained imperturbable even under intense pressure. 即使在巨大的压力下，他仍然保持冷静。|近义词：calm, composed, serene, unflappable|反义词：excitable, nervous, agitated, anxious|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin imperturbabilis, from in- 'not' + perturbare 'to disturb' (from per- 'thoroughly' + turbare 'to disturb, confuse').
