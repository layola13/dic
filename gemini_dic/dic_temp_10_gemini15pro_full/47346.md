de-stratifies:/diːˈstrætɪfaɪz/
verb
消除分层，使均匀
to remove the layers or strata from something, often a liquid, so that it becomes uniform throughout
The mixing process de-stratifies the lake water, distributing oxygen and nutrients more evenly.  混合过程消除了湖水分层，使氧气和营养物质分布更均匀。
近义词: homogenize, blend, mix
反义词: stratify, layer
