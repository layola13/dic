frank:/fræŋk/| adj.|坦率的，直率的；真诚的；明显的，不加掩饰的|Open, honest, and direct in speech or writing; sincere.  Marked by free, forthright, and sincere expression.|He was frank about his limitations. 他坦率地谈到了自己的不足之处。|近义词：honest, candid, direct, forthright, open, sincere, straightforward|反义词：dishonest, deceitful, evasive, indirect, secretive, insincere|词性:形容词

frank:/fræŋk/| n.|邮资已付戳；免费邮寄特权|A mark, stamp, or signature on a letter or package indicating that postage has been paid or does not need to be paid.|Members of Congress have the frank. 国会议员享有免费邮寄特权。|近义词：stamp, postmark|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

frank:/fræŋk/| vt.|免费邮寄|To send (mail) free of postage.|The senator franked the letter. 参议员免费邮寄了这封信。|近义词：post, mail|反义词: N/A|词性:动词
