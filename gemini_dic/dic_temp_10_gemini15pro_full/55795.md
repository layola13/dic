diverticulum: /ˌdaɪvərˈtɪkjʊləm/ (US), /ˌdaɪvəˈtɪkjʊləm/ (UK) | n. | 憩室 | A small pouch, or sac, that bulges outward through a weak spot in the wall of a hollow organ, such as the intestine. |  The doctor found a diverticulum in her colon during the colonoscopy. 医生在结肠镜检查中发现了她结肠中的一个憩室。| synonym: pouch, sac | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "cavity" in certain contexts which implies an inward space rather than an outward bulge) | noun
