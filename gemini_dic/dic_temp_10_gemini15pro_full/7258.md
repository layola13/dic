anglomaniacal: /ˌæŋɡloʊˈmeɪniækəl/ | adj. | 醉心英国文化的；亲英的 | Excessively and unreasonably devoted to English customs, institutions, or culture. | She has an anglomaniacal obsession with British period dramas. 她对英国时代剧有着狂热的痴迷。 |  He decorated his entire house in an anglomaniacal style, filled with Union Jack flags and portraits of the Queen. 他用亲英的风格装饰了他的整个房子，里面挂满了英国国旗和女王的画像。 | 近义词: pro-British, Anglophile | 反义词: anti-British, Anglophobe | 词性: 形容词
Etymology: From Anglo- (referring to England) + maniac (obsessive enthusiast) + -al (adjective suffix).
