nemathelminthes:/ˌnɛməˈθɛlmɪnˌθiːz/
n.
线虫动物门; roundworms
A phylum of unsegmented, cylindrical worms, including parasitic forms such as hookworms and pinworms, as well as free-living forms.
例句：Nemathelminthes are a diverse group of animals found in a wide range of habitats. 线虫动物门是一类多样化的动物，分布在各种各样的栖息地。

例句：Some Nemathelminthes are beneficial to the soil, while others are parasites of plants and animals.  一些线虫对土壤有益，而另一些则是动植物的寄生虫。

近义词：roundworms, nematodes
反义词:  (None, as it is a taxonomic classification)
词性: noun
