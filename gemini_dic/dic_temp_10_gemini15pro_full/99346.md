interdefinability:/ˌɪntərˌdɪˌfaɪnəˈbɪləti/| n.|相互定义性，互定义性|The state or quality of being definable in terms of each other.  Two concepts are interdefinable if one can be defined using the other, and vice-versa.|The interdefinability of mass and energy is a cornerstone of modern physics.|近义词：interdependence, correlation|反义词: independence|noun
