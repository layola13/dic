divaricate: /daɪˈvæɹɪkeɪt/ /dɪˈvæɹɪkeɪt/
verb
分叉；岔开；分歧
to spread apart, diverge, or branch out.
The branches divaricate from the main trunk.  树枝从主干上分叉。
The paths divaricated at the fork in the road.  小路在岔路口分叉。
近义词: diverge, branch, fork, separate, spread
反义词: converge, unite, join, merge
词性: verb

divaricate: /daɪˈvæɹɪkeɪt/ /dɪˈvæɹɪkeɪt/
adjective
分叉的；岔开的
spreading apart, diverging, or branching out.
The divaricate branches of the tree provided ample shade.  这棵树分叉的树枝提供了充足的阴凉。
The divaricate paths led to different parts of the forest.  分叉的小路通向森林的不同地方。
近义词: divergent, branching, forked, spread, separated
反义词: convergent, united, joined, merged
词性: adjective


词源: From Latin dīvāricātus, past participle of dīvāricāre (“to spread apart, straddle”), from dī- (“apart, asunder”) + vāricus (“straddling, with feet apart”).
