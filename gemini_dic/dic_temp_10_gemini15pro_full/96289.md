indifferently:/ɪnˈdɪfrəntli/ | adv. | 冷漠地，不关心地，平庸地 | without interest or concern; in a mediocre way | She shrugged indifferently and turned away. 她漠然地耸了耸肩，转过身去。| He played the piece indifferently, making several mistakes. 他演奏这首曲子很平庸，犯了好几个错误。 | 近义词：apathetically, listlessly, unconcernedly | 反义词：eagerly, enthusiastically, passionately | 词性: 副词

词源:
From Late Latin *indifferenter, from Latin indifferens (adjective) + -ter (adverbial suffix).  The Latin indifferens means "not different, impartial," from in- "not" + differens "differing," present participle of differre "to differ."
