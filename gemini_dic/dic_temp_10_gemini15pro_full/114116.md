magpie:/ˈmæɡpaɪ/| n.|喜鹊；饶舌的人；收藏癖的人|A magpie is a bird with black and white feathers and a long tail, known for its chattering call and habit of collecting shiny objects.  A magpie can also refer to a person who chatters a lot or someone who collects things, often of little value.|例句：I saw a magpie stealing a shiny coin. 我看到一只喜鹊偷了一枚闪亮的硬币。

例句：She's a real magpie, always collecting trinkets. 她真是个收藏癖，总是收集一些小玩意儿。|近义词：collector, chatterbox (for people), crow (for bird)|反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps minimalist for the collecting sense)|词性:noun
