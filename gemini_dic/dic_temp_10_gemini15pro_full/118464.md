mesethmoid:/ˌmɛsˈɛθmɔɪd/
n.
中筛骨
The central part of the ethmoid bone.  It forms part of the nasal septum.
The mesethmoid separates the two nasal cavities.  中筛骨将两个鼻腔分开。
近义词: median ethmoid
词性: noun
