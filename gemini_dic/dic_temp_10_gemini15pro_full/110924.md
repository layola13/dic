liposomal:/ˌlaɪpəˈsoʊməl/ | adj. | 脂质体的 |  Relating to or consisting of liposomes, which are spherical sacs made of a lipid bilayer that can encapsulate drugs or other substances. | Liposomal drug delivery can improve the efficacy and reduce the toxicity of certain medications. 脂质体药物递送可以提高某些药物的疗效并降低其毒性。 |  lipid-based, liposome-encapsulated | N/A | adjective
