obstructedly:/əbˈstrʌktɪdli/
adv.
阻碍地，妨碍地
in a way that obstructs or hinders something.
The road was obstructedly blocked by a fallen tree. 道路被一棵倒下的树阻碍了。
Her view was obstructedly obscured by the tall building.  她的视野被高楼阻碍了。
近义词: hinderingly, obstructively
反义词: helpfully, supportively
词性: adverb

词源:
From obstruct + -ed + -ly.  "Obstruct" comes from the Latin "obstruere," meaning "to build against" or "to block up."
