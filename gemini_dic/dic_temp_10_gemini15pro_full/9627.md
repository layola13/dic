antistimulant:/ˌæntiˈstɪmjʊlənt/
noun| 抗兴奋剂；抑制剂 |A substance that reduces or counteracts the effects of a stimulant.|Doctors prescribed him an antistimulant to help him sleep.|Synonyms: depressant, sedative, tranquilizer|Antonyms: stimulant, excitant|n.


antistimulant:/ˌæntiˈstɪmjʊlənt/
adjective| 抗兴奋的；抑制的 |Having the effect of reducing or counteracting stimulation.|The doctor recommended an antistimulant medication for her hyperactivity.|Synonyms: calming, soothing, relaxing|Antonyms: stimulating, exciting|adj.
