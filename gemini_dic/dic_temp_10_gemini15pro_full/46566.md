dalmatic: /dælˈmætɪk/
n.
达尔马提卡长袍, 宽袖长袍
A wide-sleeved tunic worn by deacons in the Roman Catholic and some other churches, and formerly by bishops.
The deacon wore a white dalmatic.  执事穿着一件白色的达尔马提卡长袍。
近义词: tunic, robe, vestment
反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific garment)
词性: noun


dalmatic: /dælˈmætɪk/
adj.
达尔马提亚的
Relating to Dalmatia, a historical region on the eastern coast of the Adriatic Sea.
The Dalmatic coast is known for its beautiful scenery. 达尔马提亚海岸以其美丽的景色而闻名。
近义词: Dalmatian
反义词: (No direct antonym for a geographical adjective)
词性: adjective
