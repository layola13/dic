outglittered:/ˌaʊtˈɡlɪtərd/
verb
胜过…的光辉，比…更闪耀
surpassed in glitter or brilliance; outshone.
Her dress outglittered all the others at the gala. 她的礼服在晚会上比其他所有人的都闪耀。
Synonyms: outshone, eclipsed, surpassed
Antonyms: dimmed, overshadowed
词性: 动词
