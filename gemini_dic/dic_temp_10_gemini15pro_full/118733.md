metagraphy:/mɛˈtæɡrəfi/ | n. | 后设写作；元写作 |  A form of writing that deals with the process of writing itself, often exploring the nature of authorship, the relationship between writer and reader, and the conventions of literature.  It can also refer to writing about writing or a description of a writing system. | Example 1: The novelist's new book is a work of metagraphy, exploring the challenges and rewards of the writing life.  这位小说家的新书是一部元写作作品，探讨了写作生活的挑战和回报。

Example 2: Her metagraphy included a detailed analysis of the symbolism used in her previous novels. 她的元写作包括对她以前小说中使用的象征主义的详细分析。 | 近义词: metafiction, self-reflexive writing | 反义词:  N/A (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
