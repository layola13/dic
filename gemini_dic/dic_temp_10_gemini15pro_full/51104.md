desperations: /ˌdɛspəˈreɪʃənz/
| n. |绝望，拼命，铤而走险的行为|The act of being desperate; a desperate measure or measures. | Driven to desperation by hunger, they stole food.  由于饥饿的驱使，他们偷了食物。|近义词：acts of despair, last resorts, frantic efforts |反义词:hope, calmness, composure|词性:名词
词源: From Late Middle English *desperacioun, from Old French desperacion, from Late Latin dēsperātiō (“despair”), from Latin dēspērō (“I despair, give up hope”). Equivalent to desperate +‎ -ion.
