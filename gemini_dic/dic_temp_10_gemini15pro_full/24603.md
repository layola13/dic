broiderer:/brɔɪdərər/ /broʊˈɪdərər/
noun
刺绣者，绣花工
A person who embroiders cloth.
The skilled broiderer created intricate designs on the fabric.  熟练的刺绣工在织物上创作了复杂的图案。
embroiderer, needleworker, stitcher
n/a
