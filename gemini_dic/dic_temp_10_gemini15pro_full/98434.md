inspectoral:/ɪnˌspɛkˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
检查的；视察的
of or relating to an inspector or inspection.
The inspectoral visit revealed several safety violations. 视察访问揭露了几起安全违规行为。
The board conducted an inspectoral review of the company's finances. 董事会对公司的财务状况进行了检查审查。
近义词: supervisory, examining, scrutinizing
反义词:  unobserved, unchecked, unexamined
词性: adjective
