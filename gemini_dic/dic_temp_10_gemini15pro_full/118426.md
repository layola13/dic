mersion:/ˈmɜːʃən/| n. |浸没，沉浸|The act of immersing or the state of being immersed.  The submersion of the submarine took only a few minutes.|例句：The mersion of the baptismal candidate symbolized a new beginning. 受洗者浸入水中象征着一个新的开始。

例句：The priest performed the mersion during the baptism ceremony.  牧师在洗礼仪式上进行了浸礼。|近义词：immersion, submersion, dipping, plunge|反义词: emersion, surfacing|词性:noun

词源: From Late Latin *mersiō, from Latin mersus, past participle of mergō (“to dip, plunge, sink, immerse”).
