fricative:/ˈfrɪkətɪv/ (US), /ˈfrɪkətɪv/ (UK)| adj. n.|摩擦的；摩擦音|A consonant sound, such as /f/ or /θ/, produced by forcing the breath through a narrow channel formed by the tongue and other parts of the mouth.|The /f/ sound in "fan" is a fricative.|"fan"中的/f/音是一个摩擦音。|近义词:spirant|反义词:plosive, stop|词性:adjective, noun


fricative:/ˈfrɪkətɪv/ (US), /ˈfrɪkətɪv/ (UK)| n.|摩擦音|A consonant sound produced by friction of the breath in a narrow opening in the vocal tract.|The sounds /s/, /z/, /f/, and /v/ are fricatives. |/s/, /z/, /f/ 和 /v/ 都是摩擦音。|近义词:spirant|反义词:plosive, stop|词性:noun
