nightlight:/ˈnaɪtˌlaɪt/ | n. |夜灯；小夜灯 |A small light kept on at night, especially in a child's bedroom, to provide comfort and reassurance. |例句：The child was afraid of the dark, so he always slept with a nightlight. 孩子怕黑，所以他总是开着夜灯睡觉。

例句：She left the nightlight on in the hallway. 她在走廊里留了一盏夜灯。

近义词：bedside lamp, night lamp |反义词: daylight, sunlight | 词性: noun
