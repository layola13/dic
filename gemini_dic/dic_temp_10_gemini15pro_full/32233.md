chickadee: /ˈtʃɪkədiː/ (US), /ˈtʃɪkədiː/ (UK)| n. |山雀，美洲黑顶山雀|A small North American songbird with a black cap and throat.|例句：The chickadee flitted from branch to branch. 山雀在树枝间飞来飞去。

例句：We heard the cheerful chirp of a chickadee. 我们听到了山雀欢快的鸣叫声。|近义词：titmouse, tit|反义词: N/A |词性: noun


Etymology: Imitative of the bird's call.
