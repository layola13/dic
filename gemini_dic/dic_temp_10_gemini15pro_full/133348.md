nonextensiveness:/ˌnɒnɪkˈstɛnsɪvnəs/| n. |非广延性，非可加性|The state or quality of not being extensive; specifically, in physics, the property of a system where the total value of a property is not simply the sum of its parts.|The nonextensiveness of entropy in black holes poses a challenge to traditional thermodynamics. 黑洞中熵的非广延性对传统热力学提出了挑战。|Near-synonyms: nonadditivity, non-extensiveness|Antonyms: extensiveness, additivity|noun
