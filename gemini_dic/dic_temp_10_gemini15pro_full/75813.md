fumid:/ˈfjuːmɪd/ | adj. | 烟熏的，充满烟的 | Filled with smoke or fumes; smoky. | The air in the bar was thick and fumid. 酒吧里的空气又浓又充满烟味。 | The fumid atmosphere made it difficult to breathe.  烟雾弥漫的空气使人难以呼吸。 | 近义词: smoky, hazy, foggy | 反义词: clear, airy | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *fumidus, from fumus ("smoke") + -idus (adjective suffix).
