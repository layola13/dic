broadeners:/ˈbrɔːdənərz/
noun
扩展者；拓宽者
Things that broaden one's experience, knowledge, or perspective.
Travel can be one of the best broadeners of the mind.  旅行可以是拓宽思路的最佳方式之一。
Synonyms: expanders, wideners, enrichers
Antonyms: limiters, restrictors
词性: 名词(plural)
