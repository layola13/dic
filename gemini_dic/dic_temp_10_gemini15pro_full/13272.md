atheisms:/ˈeɪθiɪzəmz/
n.
无神论，不信神
Disbelief or lack of belief in the existence of God or gods.
Many different atheisms exist, as there are various reasons for not believing in deities.  许多不同的无神论存在，因为不相信神灵的原因有很多。
Her atheism was a point of contention with her religious family. 她的无神论是她与宗教家庭争论的焦点。
近义词:nonbelief, unbelief, disbelief
反义词:theism, faith
词性:noun
