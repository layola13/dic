loudlier:/ˈlaʊdliər/ (US), /ˈlaʊdɪə(r)/ (UK)| adv.|更大声地，更喧闹地|Comparative adverb of "loudly."  Indicates a greater degree of loudness or noise.|She sang even loudlier than before. 她比以前唱得更大声了。|He spoke loudlier to be heard over the noise. 为了盖过噪音，他说话的声音更大了。|Synonyms: more loudly|Antonyms: more softly, more quietly|adverb
