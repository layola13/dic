imputing:/ɪmˈpjuːtɪŋ/ | v. | 归罪；归咎；推断；估算 |  Ascribing a quality or characteristic (especially a fault or bad action) to a person or thing;  inferring a value for a missing piece of data based on existing data. |  The company was imputing the losses to the new manager.  该公司将损失归咎于新经理。|Synonyms: ascribe, attribute, assign, blame, charge, credit, infer, estimate| Antonyms: exculpate, absolve, exonerate | verb

imputation:/ˌɪmpjuˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 归罪；归咎；推断；估算 | The act of imputing; something that is imputed. | There was an imputation of malice in his words. 他的话中暗含恶意。 | Synonyms: accusation, attribution, charge, reproach | Antonyms: exoneration, absolution | noun
