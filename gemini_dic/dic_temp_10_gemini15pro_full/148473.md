overstrengthens:/ˌoʊvərˈstrɛŋθənz/
verb
过度加强；过分增强
Makes something excessively strong, often beyond what is necessary or desirable.
The new regulations overstrengthen the banking sector, stifling innovation. 新规定过度加强了银行业，扼杀了创新。
The alloy is overstrengthened, making it brittle and prone to cracking.  这种合金被过度强化，使其变脆并且容易开裂。
近义词: reinforce, fortify, overpower
反义词: weaken, undermine, impair
词性: verb
