emulsion:/ɪˈmʌlʃən/ | n. | 乳状液; 乳浊液; 感光乳剂 | A mixture of two or more liquids that are normally immiscible (unmixable), such as oil and water, where one liquid is dispersed as small droplets in the other. |  Photographic film is coated with a light-sensitive emulsion.  照相胶片上涂有一层感光乳剂。

The lotion is an emulsion of oil and water. 这乳液是油和水的乳状液。 | synonyms: suspension, colloid, mixture | antonyms: solution, compound | noun
