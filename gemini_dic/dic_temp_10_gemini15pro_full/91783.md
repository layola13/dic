hyperexaltation:/ˌhaɪpərˌɛksɔːlˈteɪʃən/
|n.|过度兴奋，极度亢奋|A state of excessive exaltation; extreme elation or enthusiasm.|His hyperexaltation after winning the lottery was understandable. 他中彩票后极度兴奋，这是可以理解的。|The hyperexaltation of the crowd during the concert was palpable.  音乐会期间观众的极度亢奋是显而易见的。| 近义词: elation, euphoria, exhilaration, rapture|反义词: depression, dejection, melancholy|词性: 名词
词源:  From hyper- "over, excessive" + exaltation.  "hyper-" comes from Greek "hyper" (ὑπέρ) meaning "over, above, beyond". "Exaltation" comes from Late Latin "exaltationem" meaning "a raising up," from "exaltare" meaning "to raise high,"  from "ex-" (out of, from) + "altus" (high).
