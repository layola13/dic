lay-away:/ˈleɪ.əˌweɪ/
n.|预留，分期付款|A system of paying for goods in installments, where the goods are kept by the store until the final payment is made.|She put the dress on lay-away until she could afford it. 她把那件连衣裙预留了，等她买得起的时候再付款。|layby, installment plan| - |noun
lay-away:/ˈleɪ.əˌweɪ/
v.|预留，分期付款|To purchase goods on a lay-away plan.|They lay-awayed a new television for their living room. 他们为客厅预留了一台新电视机，采用分期付款的方式。|layby, purchase on installment| - |verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From the phrasal verb "lay away," meaning to put something aside for later use.  The term gained its commercial sense in the early 20th century.
