overabstracted:/ˌoʊvərəbˈstræktɪd/ | adj. | 过于抽象的 |  Removed too far from concrete experience or specific details; excessively abstract.  Characterized by excessive abstraction. | The theory was so overabstracted that it had little practical application.  这个理论过于抽象，以至于几乎没有实际应用。 |  The philosopher's overabstracted arguments left the audience confused. 哲学家过于抽象的论证让听众感到困惑。 | abstract, theoretical, conceptual, esoteric | concrete, specific, practical, tangible | adjective
