counterinflationary:/ˌkaʊntərɪnˈfleɪʃəneri/
adjective
反通货膨胀的
Intended to counteract inflation.
The government implemented counterinflationary measures to stabilize the economy. 政府实施了反通货膨胀措施以稳定经济。
Counterinflationary policies are designed to slow down the rate of inflation. 反通货膨胀政策旨在减缓通货膨胀率。
近义词:anti-inflationary, deflationary
反义词:inflationary
词性:adjective
