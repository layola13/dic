neophyte:/ˈniːəˌfaɪt/| n. |新手，初学者，新皈依者|A person who is new to a subject, skill, or belief.|例句：The neophytes were eager to learn the ropes. 这些新手渴望学习窍门。|例句：As a neophyte to coding, I found the jargon confusing.  作为一名编程新手，我发现这些术语令人困惑。|近义词：beginner, novice, tyro, rookie|反义词：expert, veteran, professional|词性:名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *neophytus*, from Greek *neóphytos* "newly planted," from *néos* "new" + *phytón* "plant."
