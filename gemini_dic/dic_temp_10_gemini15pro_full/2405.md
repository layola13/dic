adjectitious:/ˌædʒɪˈtɪʃəs/
adjective
附加的，外来的
Coming from outside; added or acquired.  Not inherent or essential.
The book had several adjectitious illustrations that were not part of the original manuscript. 这本书有几幅附加的插图，不是原稿的一部分。

The museum's collection includes some adjectitious pieces acquired through donations. 博物馆的藏品中有一些通过捐赠获得的外来作品。

近义词:extrinsic, incidental, adventitious, supplementary, accessory
反义词:intrinsic, inherent, essential, innate
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *adjectitius, from Latin adjectus (past participle of adicere ‘to add’), from ad- ‘to’ + jacere ‘to throw’.
来自晚期拉丁语 *adjectitius，源自拉丁语 adjectus（adicere “添加”的过去分词），来自 ad- “到” + jacere “投掷”。
