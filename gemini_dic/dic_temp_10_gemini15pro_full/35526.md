coagulated:/koʊˈæɡjəleɪtɪd/| adj. | 凝结的，凝固的 |having changed from a liquid to a thick, almost solid state|The chef removed the coagulated egg whites from the pan.  厨师从锅里移走了凝固的蛋白。|近义词：clotted, thickened, congealed|反义词:liquid, fluid|词性:形容词
