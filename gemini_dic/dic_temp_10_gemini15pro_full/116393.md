mastoiditis:/ˌmæstɔɪˈdaɪtɪs/
n.|乳突炎|Inflammation of the mastoid process, a bony prominence located behind the ear.|He was diagnosed with mastoiditis and prescribed antibiotics.|近义词:inflammation of the mastoid, mastoid infection|反义词: (No direct antonym for a disease)|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Modern Latin *mastoiditis, from mastoid + -itis (“inflammation”).  Mastoid comes from Greek mastos ("breast") + -eidēs ("resembling"), because of the breast-like shape of the mastoid process.
