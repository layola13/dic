ducable:/ˈdjuːkəbl/ |adj.| 可延展的，可拉丝的，可塑的 |Capable of being drawn out into wire or a thread.  Able to be molded or shaped. | This metal is highly ducable and can be easily formed into different shapes. 这种金属延展性很好，可以很容易地塑造成不同的形状。| The clay was soft and ducable, perfect for sculpting.  这黏土柔软可塑，非常适合雕刻。 | 近义词: malleable, pliable, ductile, tractable | 反义词: brittle, inflexible, rigid | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *ducābilis*, from ducō (“I lead, draw”).  Related to ductile.
