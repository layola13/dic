overthrongs:/ˌoʊvərˈθrɒŋz/ | v. | 挤满；过度拥挤 | Fills or crowds excessively; overcrowds. | The market square was overthronged with holiday shoppers. 节日购物者把市场广场挤得水泄不通。| The narrow streets were overthronged with tourists. 狭窄的街道上挤满了游客。 | 近义词：overcrowd, overcrowd, jam, pack, swarm | 反义词：deserted, empty | 词性：动词

overthrong:/ˌoʊvərˈθrɒŋ/ | n. |  人群；拥挤 | A crowd; an excessive number of people. |  The overthrong in the stadium made it difficult to move. 体育场里人山人海，难以移动。|  The overthrong at the concert was overwhelming.  演唱会上的人群拥挤不堪。 | 近义词：crowd, throng, multitude | 反义词：scarcity, few | 词性：名词
