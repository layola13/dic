bloviate: /ˈbloʊviˌeɪt/ | v. | 夸夸其谈，高谈阔论 | to speak or write verbosely and pompously | He bloviated for hours about his latest business venture. 他就他最新的商业冒险夸夸其谈了几个小时。 | synonyms: rant, rave, spout, declaim | antonyms: be concise, be brief | verb
bloviation: /ˌbloʊviˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 夸夸其谈，空洞的言辞 | pompous and empty talk or writing | His speech was filled with bloviation and lacked substance. 他的演讲充满了夸夸其谈，缺乏实质内容。| synonyms: bombast, rhetoric, verbosity | antonyms: conciseness, brevity | noun
