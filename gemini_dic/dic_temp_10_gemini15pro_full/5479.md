along: /əˈlɒŋ/ (UK), /əˈlɔːŋ/ (US) | adv. prep. | 沿着，顺着；一起；向前；进展 | Moving in a constant direction on (or beside) a road, path, or any more or less horizontal surface; in company; forward; into a more advanced stage. | We walked along the beach. 我们沿着海滩散步。 | 近义词: down, alongside, together, forward | 反义词: back, backward | 词性: 副词 (adv.), 介词 (prep.)
