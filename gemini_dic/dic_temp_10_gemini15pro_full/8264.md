anti-Irish:/ˌæntiˈaɪrɪʃ/
adj.
反爱尔兰的；歧视爱尔兰人的
Demonstrating prejudice or hostility against Irish people or their culture.
There was a lot of anti-Irish sentiment in 19th century Britain.
19世纪的英国有很多反爱尔兰情绪。
The politician's anti-Irish remarks caused outrage.
这位政治家的反爱尔兰言论引起了众怒。

近义词: discriminatory, prejudiced, bigoted, biased
反义词: pro-Irish, phil-Irish
