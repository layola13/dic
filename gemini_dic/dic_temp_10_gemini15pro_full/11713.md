arnaut:/ɑːrˈnɔːt/ | n. | 阿尔瑙特人，阿尔巴尼亚人 | An Albanian, especially one living abroad.  Derived from the Italian word "Arnauta," ultimately from the medieval name of Albania, Arbëria. |  例句：The arnaut community in Italy maintained strong ties to their homeland.  意大利的阿尔瑙特社区与其家乡保持着紧密的联系。

例句：Many arnauts emigrated to Greece in the 20th century. 许多阿尔瑙特人在20世纪移民到希腊。 | 近义词：Albanian | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词
