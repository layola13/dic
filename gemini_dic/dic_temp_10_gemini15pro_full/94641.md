impression:/ɪmˈprɛʃən/ | n. | 印象；感想；印记；压痕 | An idea, feeling, or opinion about something or someone, especially one formed without conscious thought or on the basis of little evidence. |  I got the impression that she wasn't happy. 我感觉她不开心。 | synonym: feeling, sense, notion | antonym: reality, fact | noun

impression:/ɪmˈprɛʃən/ | n. | 模仿；滑稽模仿 | An imitation of a person or thing, done to entertain. | He did a hilarious impression of the president. 他对总统进行了滑稽的模仿。 | synonym: imitation, impersonation, mimicry | antonym: original | noun
