aggrandizer:/əˈɡrændaɪzər/
n.|夸大者; 吹嘘者|A person who exaggerates or makes something appear greater than it actually is.|He was an aggrandizer, always inflating his accomplishments. 他是一个爱吹嘘的人，总是夸大自己的成就。|Synonyms: exaggerator, boaster, embellisher|Antonyms: minimizer, downplayer|noun

aggrandize:/əˈɡrændaɪz/
v.|夸大; 吹捧; 提高…的地位|To increase the power, status, or wealth of.|He sought to aggrandize himself by claiming a royal lineage. 他试图通过声称自己有皇室血统来抬高自己的身价。|Synonyms: exalt, elevate, enhance|Antonyms: diminish, belittle, reduce|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *agrandiser*, from *a-* (to) + *grand* (large) + *-iser* (to make). Ultimately from Latin *grandis* (large).
