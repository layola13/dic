corroders:/kəˈroʊdərz/
n.|腐蚀物，腐蚀剂|Substances that cause corrosion; agents that eat away or destroy gradually.|Acid rain is one of the most common corroders of stone buildings. 酸雨是石材建筑物最常见的腐蚀剂之一。|The salty air acted as a corroder on the metal parts of the ship. 咸湿的空气腐蚀了船的金属部件。|synonyms: corrosives, erosives, oxidants|antonyms: preservatives, inhibitors|noun
