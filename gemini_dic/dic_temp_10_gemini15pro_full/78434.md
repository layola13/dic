get-togethers: /ˈɡet təˌɡɛðərz/ | n. | 聚会；联欢 | Informal gatherings of people, often for social purposes. | We have family get-togethers every Christmas. 我们每年圣诞节都有家庭聚会。 |  We're having a small get-together at my place on Saturday.  我们周六要在我家举行一个小聚会。 | 近义词：gatherings, meetings, parties, reunions | 反义词：separation, isolation | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  Compound of "get" + "together." "Get" meaning "to come into a particular position or condition" and "together" meaning "with each other or in one place."
