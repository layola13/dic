hypotaxes:/ˈhaɪpəˌtæksiːz/
n.
下位结构，从属结构
A syntactic construction containing subordinate clauses or phrases.  It is characterized by the use of subordinating conjunctions to link clauses in a hierarchical manner, creating a complex sentence structure where one clause is dependent on another.
The author's elaborate hypotaxes made the text dense and difficult to follow.  作者精细的下位结构使得文本密集且难以理解。

hypotaxes are common in academic writing. 下位结构在学术写作中很常见。
近义词：subordination, complex sentence structure
反义词：parataxis
