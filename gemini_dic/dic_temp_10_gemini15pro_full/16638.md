baroscopic: /ˌbærəˈskɒpɪk/ | adj. | 受气压影响的 | Affected by changes in atmospheric pressure. | Baroscopic materials expand and contract with changes in air pressure. 受气压影响的材料会随着气压的变化而膨胀和收缩。| synonyms: pressure-sensitive | antonyms: pressure-insensitive | adjective
