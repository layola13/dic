coster:/ˈkɒstə(r)/ (UK) /ˈkɔːstər/ (US)| n. |叫卖小贩； costermonger |A person who sells goods, especially fruit and vegetables, from a barrow or stall in the street.| He bought a bag of apples from the coster at the market. 他在市场上从一个小贩那里买了一袋苹果。|近义词：vendor, hawker, peddler, street vendor|反义词: buyer, customer|noun
