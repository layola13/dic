counterrally: /ˈkaʊntərˌræli/ | n. | 反集会，对抗性集会 | A public meeting or demonstration in opposition to another one. |  The group organized a counterrally to protest the proposed cuts to education funding.  该组织举行了一次反集会，抗议削减教育经费的提议。| synonyms: counterdemonstration, counterprotest | antonyms: rally, demonstration, protest | noun

counterrally: /ˈkaʊntərˌræli/ | v. | 举行反集会，参与反集会 | To hold or participate in a counterrally. |  Students counterrallied against the speaker's controversial views. 学生们举行反集会，反对演讲者有争议的观点。 | synonyms: counterdemonstrate, counterprotest | antonyms: rally, demonstrate, protest | verb
