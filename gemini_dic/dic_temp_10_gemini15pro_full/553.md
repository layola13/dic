verb|中止，夭折 (过去分词)|Stopped prematurely; not completed successfully.  (past participle form of the verb "abort")|The program aborted unexpectedly.  程序意外中止了。

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *abortus*, past participle of *aborīrī* ("to miscarry, fail to be born, perish"), from *ab-* ("away, from") + *orīrī* ("to arise, be born").
