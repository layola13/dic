folwe 不是一个标准的英文单词。 你可能是想拼写 **follow**。如果是这样，以下是 **follow** 的解析：

follow: /ˈfɒləʊ/ (UK) /ˈfɑːloʊ/ (US) | v.  n.|跟随，遵循，理解，密切关注|  To come or go after; to proceed along the course of; to act according to; to understand| He followed her into the room. 他跟着她进了房间。|近义词：pursue, trail, obey, heed, comprehend|反义词:lead, precede, disobey, ignore|词性:动词, 名词

follow: /ˈfɒləʊ/ (UK) /ˈfɑːloʊ/ (US) | n.|跟随，追随者|The act of following; a group of followers|He has a large follow on social media. 他在社交媒体上有很多粉丝。 |近义词：following, supporters|反义词:leaders, predecessors|词性:名词
