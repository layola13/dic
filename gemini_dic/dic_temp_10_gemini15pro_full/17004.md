bat-around:/ˌbæt əˈraʊnd/| n.| 讨论，随意讨论；试探性讨论|An informal discussion in which ideas or suggestions are exchanged.|We had a good bat-around about the project before submitting the proposal. 我们在提交提案之前对这个项目进行了充分的讨论。|Example: The team had a bat-around to brainstorm new marketing ideas.  团队进行了一场头脑风暴，讨论新的营销理念。|Synonyms: discussion, brainstorming, exchange of ideas|Antonyms: debate, argument, dispute|noun

bat-around:/ˌbæt əˈraʊnd/| v.| 讨论，随意讨论；试探性讨论|To discuss informally, exchanging ideas or suggestions.|They batted around a few ideas before making a decision. 他们在做决定之前讨论了一些想法。|Example:  We batted the idea around for a while, but couldn't reach a consensus.  我们讨论了一下这个想法，但没有达成共识。|Synonyms: discuss, brainstorm, consider|Antonyms: decide, conclude, finalize|verb
