honeycomb:/ˈhʌnikəʊm/ (UK) /ˈhʌnikom/ (US)| n. adj.|蜂窝，蜂巢; 蜂窝状的|A structure of hexagonal wax cells built by honey bees to store honey and eggs.  Also used to describe things with a similar structure.|The beekeeper carefully removed the honeycomb from the hive. 养蜂人小心地从蜂箱中取出蜂窝。|Synonyms: beehive, network, lattice|Antonyms: solid, mass|noun

honeycomb:/ˈhʌnikəʊm/ (UK) /ˈhʌnikom/ (US)| adj.|蜂窝状的|Resembling the structure of a honeycomb.|The chocolate bar had a honeycomb texture. 这块巧克力棒有着蜂窝状的纹理。|Synonyms: cellular, porous|Antonyms: solid, compact|adjective
