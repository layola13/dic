bloodbath:/ˈblʌdbɑːθ/ | n. |大屠杀，血腥的杀戮|A massacre; an event involving a lot of bloodshed. |The battle was a bloodbath.  这场战斗是一场大屠杀。| 近义词：massacre, slaughter, carnage | 反义词: peace, truce | noun

bloodbath:/ˈblʌdbɑːθ/ | n. |（股票市场）大跌，暴跌|A period of severe price falls in a stock market or other market. (Figurative) |The stock market experienced a bloodbath yesterday. 昨天股市经历了一场大跌。| 近义词：slump, crash, collapse | 反义词: boom, surge | noun

词源:  A compound of *blood* and *bath*.  First recorded in the 1930s.
