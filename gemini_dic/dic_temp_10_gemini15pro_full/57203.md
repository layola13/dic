down-tubes:/ˈdaʊnˌtuːbz/
n.
下管；下水管
The down-tubes carry rainwater from the roof to the ground.  下水管将雨水从屋顶引到地面。
Bicycle frames are often made with two down-tubes for extra strength. 自行车车架通常由两根下管制成，以增加强度。
Synonym: downpipes, drainpipes
Antonym: up-tubes (less common, context-dependent)
词性: 名词


Etymology: 'down' (Old English *dūne) + 'tubes' (Latin tubus)
