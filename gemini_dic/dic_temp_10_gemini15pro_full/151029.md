parapack: /ˈpærəpæk/ | n. | 防水背包；伞兵背包 | A backpack designed to be waterproof, often used by paratroopers or for outdoor activities. |  He carried all his essential gear in his parapack. 他把所有必需的装备都放在防水背包里。

The parapack kept his supplies dry during the heavy rain.  在大雨中，防水背包使他的补给保持干燥。 | 近义词: waterproof backpack, rucksack, dry bag | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "permeable bag") | 词性: 名词
