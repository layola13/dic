disembark:/ˌdɪsɪmˈbɑːrk/ | v. |下船，登陆|To leave a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle. |Passengers are requested to disembark the plane.  请乘客下飞机。| Synonyms: debark, land, get off | Antonyms: embark, board, get on | verb
