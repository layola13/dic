noncensoriousness:/ˌnɒnsɛnˈsɔːriəsnəs/
noun
不加评判；宽容；不吹毛求疵
The state of being noncensorious; refraining from judging or criticizing others.
Her noncensoriousness made her a popular confidante. 她不加评判的态度使她成为受欢迎的知己。
Synonyms: tolerance, acceptance, open-mindedness, forbearance, leniency
Antonyms: judgmentalness, criticalness, censoriousness, intolerance, bigotry
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + censorious + -ness.  "Censorious" comes from the Latin "censor" meaning "judge" or "critic."  The suffix "-ness" turns the adjective "censorious" into the noun "noncensoriousness."
