eat-your-words:/ˌiːt jɔːr ˈwɜːrdz/ /ˌiːt jʊər ˈwɜːrdz/
verb phrase
收回前言；承认说错话
To admit that something you said was wrong.
例句：He was forced to eat his words when the project proved to be a success.  当项目被证明成功时，他被迫承认自己说错了。

例句：I made him eat his words after he said I couldn't finish the marathon.  在他说我无法完成马拉松比赛后，我让他收回前言。

近义词：retract, apologize, backtrack, take back
反义词：insist, persist, maintain
词性：动词短语
