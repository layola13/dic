esterification:/ɪˌstɛrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ |n.|酯化(作用)|The process of forming an ester.  The reaction between an alcohol and a carboxylic acid (or its derivative) to produce an ester and water.|例句：The esterification of acetic acid with ethanol produces ethyl acetate. 乙酸与乙醇的酯化反应生成乙酸乙酯。

例句：The rate of esterification can be increased by adding a catalyst. 添加催化剂可以提高酯化反应速率。|近义词：ester formation|反义词：hydrolysis (of esters)|词性:名词
