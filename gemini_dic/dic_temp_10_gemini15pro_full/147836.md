overpunishment:/ˌoʊvərˈpʌnɪʃmənt/| n. |过度惩罚，过分惩罚|The act of punishing someone too harshly or excessively for a particular offense.|The judge decided the overpunishment of the minor was unjust. 法官认为对未成年人的过度惩罚是不公正的。|The overpunishment led to resentment and further behavioral problems. 过度惩罚导致了怨恨和进一步的行为问题。|近义词：excessive punishment, disproportionate punishment, overkill|反义词:leniency, underpunishment|noun
