bituminate:/bɪˈtjuːmɪneɪt/ /baɪˈtuːmɪneɪt/
verb
涂沥青；使柏油化
to cover or treat with bitumen or asphalt.
Example: The road was bituminated last year.  这条路去年铺上了沥青。

Example: They plan to bituminate the driveway to make it more durable.  他们计划给车道铺上沥青以使其更耐用。
近义词: asphalt, pave, surface
反义词: (None readily available - consider the opposite action might be "excavate" or "remove paving")
词性: verb


bituminous:/bɪˈtjuːmɪnəs/ /baɪˈtuːmɪnəs/
adjective
含沥青的；沥青质的
containing or treated with bitumen.
Example: Bituminous coal is a type of coal that contains a high percentage of volatile matter. 烟煤是一种含有高挥发性物质的煤。

Example:  The bituminous surface of the road glistened in the rain.  雨水淋湿的路面闪闪发光。
近义词: asphaltic, tarry
反义词: (None readily available)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *bituminatus, past participle of bituminare "to treat with bitumen," from Latin bitumen "bitumen, asphalt" (see bitumen).
