neatens:/ˈniːtnz/
verb
使整洁；整理
makes something tidy or more orderly.
She neatens her desk before leaving the office. 她离开办公室前整理了桌子。
He neatens his hair with a comb. 他用梳子整理头发。
tidy, organize, straighten, arrange
mess up, disorganize, clutter
v.
