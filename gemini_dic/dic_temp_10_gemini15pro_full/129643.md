non-corporation:/ˌnɑːn kɔːrpəˈreɪʃn/ | n. | 非公司实体，非法人团体 | An organization that is not incorporated and thus not recognized as a separate legal entity from its owners.  It does not offer the limited liability protection of a corporation. | Example 1: Many small businesses operate as non-corporations, such as partnerships or sole proprietorships. 许多小型企业以非公司实体的形式运营，例如合伙企业或独资企业。

Example 2: The non-corporation was held liable for the debts incurred by its members.  这个非法人团体对其成员产生的债务负有责任。 | Synonyms: unincorporated business, unincorporated association | Antonyms: corporation, incorporated entity | Noun
