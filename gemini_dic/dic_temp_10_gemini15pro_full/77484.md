gazeful:/ˈɡeɪzfʊl/| adj.|凝视的，充满注视的|Tending to gaze; given to gazing; steadily gazing.  Characterized by a steady gaze. |She gave him a long, gazeful look. 她久久地凝视着他。|近义词：observant, watchful, attentive|反义词：inattentive, unobservant|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From gaze + -ful.  Gaze originates from the Old Norse "gaesa" meaning "to stare."
