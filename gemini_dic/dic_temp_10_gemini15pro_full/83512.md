hacky:/ˈhæki/| adj.|蹩脚的；不优雅的；凑合的；临时性的|A solution or implementation that is clumsy, inelegant, or uses a workaround rather than a proper solution.  It often implies a quick fix that is not ideal but works for the time being.|例句：The code is a bit hacky, but it gets the job done. 代码有点蹩脚，但是能完成任务。

例句：We had to use a hacky workaround to get the feature working by the deadline.  为了在截止日期前完成这个功能，我们不得不使用一个凑合的权宜之计。|近义词：clumsy, inelegant, makeshift, quick and dirty, jury-rigged, crude|反义词：elegant, refined, sophisticated, well-designed|词性:形容词
