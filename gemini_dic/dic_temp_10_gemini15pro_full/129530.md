non-believer:/ˌnɒn bɪˈliːvər/ (US) /ˌnɒn bəˈliːvər/ (UK)| n. |无信仰者，不信教者|A person who does not believe in a particular religion, god, or set of religious beliefs.|例句：He's a non-believer, but he respects other people's faiths. 他是一个无信仰者，但他尊重其他人的信仰。|近义词：atheist, agnostic, skeptic, unbeliever|反义词：believer, devotee, faithful|词性:名词
