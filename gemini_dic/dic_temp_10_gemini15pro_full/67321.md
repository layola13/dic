extemporary:/ɪkˈstɛmpəˌrɛri/ /ɛkˈstɛmpəˌrɛri/
adjective
即兴的；临时的；无准备的
Done, spoken, performed, etc., without preparation or practice.
He delivered an extemporary speech. 他发表了即兴演讲。
近义词:impromptu, improvised, off-the-cuff, ad-lib, unrehearsed
反义词:prepared, rehearsed, planned
词性:adjective

extempore:/ɪkˈstɛmpəri/ /ɛkˈstɛmpəri/
adjective, adverb
即席的，即兴的；无准备地
Spoken or done without preparation.
He gave an extempore performance on the piano. 他即兴演奏了钢琴曲。
近义词:impromptu, improvised, off-the-cuff, ad-lib, unrehearsed
反义词:prepared, rehearsed, planned
词性:adjective, adverb
