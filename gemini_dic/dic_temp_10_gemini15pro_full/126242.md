mycology:/maɪˈkɒlədʒi/ /maɪˈkɑːlədʒi/
n.
真菌学
The scientific study of fungi, including their taxonomy, distribution, and effects on humans and other organisms.
Mycology is a fascinating field of study.  真菌学是一个引人入胜的研究领域。
The professor's research in mycology led to the discovery of a new species of fungus.  这位教授在真菌学方面的研究导致发现了一种新的真菌。
近义词:fungi study
反义词:None
词性:noun
