obtrusive: /əbˈtruːsɪv/ /əbˈtruːsɪv/
| adj. | 扎眼的；突兀的；冒昧的 | Noticeable or prominent in an unwelcome or intrusive way. |  The waiter was overly obtrusive, constantly interrupting our conversation. 服务员过于冒昧，不断打断我们的谈话。|近义词: intrusive, conspicuous, prominent | 反义词: discreet, subtle, unobtrusive | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *obtrūsus, past participle of obtrūdere (“to thrust against, push against, oppose”), from ob- (“against, toward”) + trūdere (“to thrust, push”).
