fallowist:/ˈfæloʊɪst/| n.|追随者；盲从者|A person who imitates or follows the ideas or opinions of another without independent thought or judgment.|He's just a fallowist, never coming up with his own original ideas. 他只是一个追随者，从不提出自己的原创想法。|Synonyms: imitator, follower, copycat, disciple|Antonyms: leader, innovator, pioneer|Noun


fallow:/ˈfæloʊ/| adj.  n.  v.|休耕的；休闲的； fallow land 休耕地； lie fallow 休耕|Left unplanted or uncultivated for a period in order to restore its fertility; inactive; dormant.|The farmer left the field fallow for a year. 农民让这块地休耕一年。|Synonyms: uncultivated, unplanted, dormant, inactive|Antonyms: cultivated, planted, active|Adjective, Noun, Verb
