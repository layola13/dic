overconstructing: /ˌoʊvərˈkənˈstrʌktɪŋ/
|v.|过度解读; 过度阐释; 过度分析|The act of interpreting something in an excessively complex or elaborate way, often attributing more meaning or significance than is actually present.|He was overconstructing her silence, assuming she was angry when she was just tired. 他过度解读了她的沉默，以为她生气了，而实际上她只是累了。|overanalyzing, overthinking, overinterpreting|underestimating, simplifying|verb
overconstruct: /ˌoʊvərˈkənˈstrʌkt/
|v.|过度构建; 过度建造|To construct something in an excessively complex or elaborate way.|The architect overconstructed the bridge, making it far stronger than necessary.  这位建筑师过度建造了这座桥，使其比需要的坚固得多。|overbuild, overengineer|underbuild, simplify|verb
overconstruction: /ˌoʊvərˈkənˈstrʌkʃən/
|n.|过度构建; 过度建造|The act of overconstructing something.|The overconstruction of the building led to unnecessary expenses.  建筑物的过度建造导致了不必要的开支。|overbuilding, overengineering|underbuilding, simplification|noun
