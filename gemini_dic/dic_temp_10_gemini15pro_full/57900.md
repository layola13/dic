driftlessness:/ˈdrɪftləsnəs/
n.
无漂移; 未受冰川作用
The state of being unaffected by glacial drift.  This refers specifically to areas, particularly in the American Midwest, that were not covered by glaciers during the last ice age.
The Driftless Area is known for its unique topography. 无漂移区以其独特的地形而闻名。
The driftlessness of the region resulted in the preservation of older landscapes. 该地区未受冰川作用，使得古老的景观得以保存。
unglaciated, uneroded
glaciated, eroded
noun
