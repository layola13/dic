guzzle: /ˈɡʌzəl/ | v. | 大口地喝；狂饮；狼吞虎咽；（车辆或机器）耗油；大量消耗 | to drink or eat something quickly and in large amounts |  He guzzled down his beer. 他一口气喝光了他的啤酒。| synonyms: gulp, chug, devour, consume | antonyms: sip, nibble | verb

guzzle: /ˈɡʌzəl/ | n. | 狂饮；豪饮 | an act of guzzling something | He took a long guzzle of water. 他猛灌了一大口水。| synonyms: draft, swig | antonyms: sip | noun
