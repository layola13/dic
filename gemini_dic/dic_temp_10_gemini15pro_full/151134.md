parataxis:/ˌpærəˈtæksɪs/| n.|并列，连接|The juxtaposition of clauses or phrases without the use of coordinating or subordinating conjunctions.|Example: "I came, I saw, I conquered."  我来了，我看见了，我征服了。|Example: "The dog barked, the cat meowed, the bird sang." 狗叫了，猫喵喵叫，鸟儿歌唱。|Synonym: juxtaposition, asyndeton|Antonym: hypotaxis|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek παράταξις (parátaxis, “a placing side by side, arrangement”), from παρά (pará, “beside, alongside”) + τάξις (táxis, “arrangement, order”).
