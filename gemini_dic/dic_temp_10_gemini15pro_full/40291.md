continuities:/ˌkɒntɪˈnjuːɪtiz/ | n. | 连续性，延续性 | the state of continuing over time, especially without interruption or change | The film explores the continuities and discontinuities of French history. 这部电影探讨了法国历史的连续性和非连续性。 | synonyms:  continuation, succession, sequence, progression | antonyms: discontinuity, interruption, break, cessation | noun

continuity:/ˌkɒntɪˈnjuːɪti/ | n. | 连续性，连贯性 | the unbroken and consistent existence or operation of something over a period of time |  The editor is responsible for maintaining continuity between scenes.  剪辑师负责保持场景之间的连贯性。| synonyms:  uninterruptedness, coherence, consistency, flow | antonyms: discontinuity, disruption, inconsistency, break | noun
