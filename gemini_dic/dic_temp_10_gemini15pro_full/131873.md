noncontagion:/ˌnɒnkənˈteɪdʒən/ | n. | 非传染性，非接触传染 | The doctor assured her that the rash was a noncontagion. |  Doctors confirmed the noncontagion of the illness. 医生确认了这种疾病的非传染性。| 近义词：noncommunicability, noninfectiousness | 反义词：contagion, infectiousness | 词性：名词

Etymology:
From non- + contagion.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "contagion" refers to the communication of disease from one person to another by close contact.
