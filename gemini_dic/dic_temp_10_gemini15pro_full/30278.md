cementer:/sɪˈmentər/ | n. | 水泥工；固井工 | A worker who cements things, especially a worker who lines oil wells with cement. |  The cementer carefully sealed the well. 水泥工仔细地封好了油井。|  Synonyms: concrete worker, well liner | Antonyms: N/A | noun

cementer:/sɪˈmentər/ | n. | 粘合剂 | Something that unites or binds together. | Shared experiences acted as a cementer for their friendship. 共同的经历成为了他们友谊的粘合剂。 | Synonyms: binder, adhesive, glue | Antonyms: solvent, divider | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "cement" + "-er" (suffix denoting a person who performs an action).  "Cement" ultimately comes from the Latin "caementum" meaning "rough stone, rubble."
