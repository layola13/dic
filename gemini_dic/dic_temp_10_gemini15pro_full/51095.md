desperadoism:/ˌdɛspəˈrɑːdoʊɪzəm/
n.
亡命之徒的行为；不顾一切的冒险主义
The reckless behavior characteristic of a desperado; desperate or reckless action.
He showed a streak of desperadoism when he robbed the bank. 他抢劫银行时表现出亡命之徒的行径。
recklessness, desperation, audacity
caution, prudence
noun
