inchoatively:/ɪnˈkoʊətɪvli/
adv.
开始地，起初地
in a way that is just beginning to happen or develop.
The project is inchoatively planned.  这个项目刚刚开始计划。
The feeling of love was inchoatively forming. 爱的感觉刚刚开始萌生。
Synonym: incipiently, initially, beginningly
Antonym: completely, fully, maturely
词性: 副词


inchoate:/ɪnˈkoʊeɪt/
adj.
刚开始的，未发展的，不完整的
just begun and so not fully formed or developed; rudimentary.
She had a few inchoate ideas about what she wanted to do. 她对想做的事情有一些初步的想法。
The plan is still inchoate and needs further development.  这个计划仍处于初期阶段，需要进一步发展。
Synonym: rudimentary, nascent, undeveloped
Antonym: developed, complete, mature
词性: 形容词
