brightnesses:/ˈbraɪtnəsɪz/
n.
亮度；光明；鲜艳；聪明
the quality of being bright
例句：The brightnesses of the stars amazed the children. 星星的亮度让孩子们惊叹不已。

例句：He adjusted the screen's brightnesses to reduce eye strain. 他调整了屏幕的亮度以减少眼睛疲劳。

近义词：luminosity, radiance, brilliance, intensity, vividness
反义词：dullness, darkness, dimness
词性: 名词(plural of brightness)

Etymology:
From Middle English *bryghtnesse, from Old English *beorhtnes (“brightness, clearness”), equivalent to bright +‎ -ness.
