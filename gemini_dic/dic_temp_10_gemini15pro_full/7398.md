animater:/ˈænɪˌmeɪtər/| n.|动画师|A person who creates animated movies, cartoons, or computer graphics.|例句：The animater spent months working on the character's movements. 动画师花了几个月的时间研究角色的动作。

例句：The animater used sophisticated software to create realistic special effects.  动画师使用先进的软件来创造逼真的特效。|近义词：cartoonist, animator, designer|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

animate:/ˈænɪmeɪt/| vt. |使有生气；赋予生命；激励；使活泼|To give life or motion to; to make lively or spirited.|例句：The director's passion animated the entire cast. 导演的热情激励了全体演员。

例句：Her smile animated her face. 她的笑容使她的脸生动起来。|近义词：enliven, invigorate, inspire, stimulate|反义词: deaden, depress, discourage|词性: verb

animated:/ˈænɪmeɪtɪd/| adj. |活泼的；有生气的；栩栩如生的|Full of life and energy; lively.|例句：They had an animated discussion about the upcoming election. 他们就即将举行的选举进行了热烈的讨论。

例句：The animated film delighted children of all ages.  这部动画片让所有年龄段的孩子都感到高兴。|近义词：lively, spirited, energetic, vibrant|反义词: dull, lifeless, inanimate|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *animatus, past participle of animare (“to give life to”), from Latin anima (“breath, soul”).
