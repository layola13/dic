hyperidealistic:/ˌhaɪpərˌaɪdiəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
过度理想化的；极端理想主义的
Excessively idealistic; unrealistically aiming for perfection or believing in ideals. Characterized by unrealistic expectations or beliefs, especially about social or political issues.
He has a hyperidealistic view of marriage, expecting a fairytale romance. 他对婚姻抱有过度理想化的看法，期待童话般的浪漫。

Her hyperidealistic plans for reforming the education system were met with skepticism. 她改革教育体系的极端理想主义计划遭到了质疑。

Synonyms: utopian, quixotic, impractical, starry-eyed, romantic
Antonyms: realistic, pragmatic, cynical, disillusioned
词性: 形容词
