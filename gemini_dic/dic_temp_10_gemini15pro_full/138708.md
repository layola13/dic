nullifier:/ˈnʌlɪfaɪər/
n.|作废者；取消者；使无效的人或物|a person or thing that nullifies something|The treaty acted as a nullifier of the previous agreement.|近义词:invalidator, negator, counteractor|反义词:validator, confirmer, ratifier|词性:名词

词源: From the verb "nullify" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Nullify" comes from the Late Latin "nullificare," from "nullus" (none, no) + "-ficare" (to make).
