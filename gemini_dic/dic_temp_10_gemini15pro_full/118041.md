menace:/ˈmɛnɪs/ | n. | 威胁，恐吓；危险的人（或物） | something that is likely to cause harm; a dangerous or threatening quality | The approaching hurricane is a menace to the entire coastal region.  逼近的飓风对整个沿海地区都是一种威胁。 | synonyms: threat, danger, peril, hazard | antonyms: safety, security | noun

menace:/ˈmɛnɪs/ | vt. | 威胁，恐吓 | to threaten, especially in a subtle or indirect way |  Dark clouds menaced the clear blue sky.  乌云威胁着晴朗的蓝天。 | synonyms: threaten, intimidate, endanger, jeopardize | antonyms: protect, safeguard | verb
