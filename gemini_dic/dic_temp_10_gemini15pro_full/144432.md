outswaggered:/ˌaʊtˈswæɡərd/
verb
以更嚣张的态度胜过；在气势上压倒
To surpass or outdo someone in swaggering; to behave in a more arrogant or boastful manner than another.
He outswaggered the other cowboys in the saloon. 他在酒吧里以更嚣张的气势压倒了其他牛仔。
Synonyms: outbragged, outboasted, outfaced
Antonyms: cowered, submitted, deferred
词性: 动词
