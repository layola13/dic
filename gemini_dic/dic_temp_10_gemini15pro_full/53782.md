discompany:/dɪsˈkʌmpəni/
v.
使失去同伴,使孤单
To deprive of companionship; to leave alone.
He was discompanied by his friends when he needed them most.  在他最需要他们的时候，他的朋友们离开了他，让他感到孤单。
The traveler was discompanied on his journey through the mountains.  旅行者在穿越山脉的旅程中孤身一人。
近义词: isolate, abandon, desert
反义词: accompany, join, attend
词性: 动词
