adored: /əˈdɔːrd/ /əˈdɔːrt/ | v. | 敬爱；爱慕；非常喜欢 |  Loved deeply and intensely; cherished. | She adored her grandfather. 她非常爱她的祖父。

He absolutely adored his new car. 他对他的新车爱不释手。

近义词: loved, cherished, worshipped, idolized | 反义词: despised, hated, loathed | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *adorer*, from Latin *adorare* "to worship, pray to," from *ad-* "to" + *orare* "to speak, pray."  The word's roots relate to speaking words of praise or worship.

USAGE->
一般现在时:
She adores her children. = 她爱她的孩子们。
He adores playing the guitar. = 他喜欢弹吉他。

一般过去时:
She adored her grandmother who passed away last year. = 她爱她去年去世的祖母。
He adored that old movie theater. = 他喜欢那个老电影院。

现在完成时:
I have always adored her paintings. = 我一直很喜欢她的画。
He has adored his wife since the day they met. = 从他们相遇的那天起，他就一直深爱着他的妻子。

过去完成时:
She had adored him for years before they finally got together. = 在他们最终在一起之前，她已经爱慕了他很多年。
He had adored that car until it broke down. = 他一直很喜欢那辆车，直到它抛锚。

将来时:
I will always adore my family. = 我将永远爱我的家人。
She will adore the gift you got her. = 她会喜欢你送给她的礼物的。

虚拟语气:
If I adored him, I would tell him. = 如果我喜欢他，我会告诉他。
I wish I adored my job more. = 我希望我能更喜欢我的工作。

被动语态:
The singer was adored by her fans. = 这位歌手受到了歌迷们的喜爱。
The old house was adored by its inhabitants. = 这栋老房子深受其居民的喜爱。

疑问句:
Do you adore your pet? = 你喜欢你的宠物吗？
What do you adore most about living here? = 你最喜欢住在这里的什么？

否定句:
I don't adore spicy food. = 我不喜欢辛辣的食物。
She doesn't adore him as much as she used to. = 她不像以前那样喜欢他了。
