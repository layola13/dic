dauntlessness:/ˈdɔːntləsnəs/| n.|无畏；勇敢；大胆|The quality of being fearless and resolute.|Her dauntlessness in the face of danger was truly inspiring. 她面对危险时的无畏精神着实令人鼓舞。|近义词: courage, bravery, fearlessness, boldness, intrepidity|反义词: fear, cowardice, timidity|词性:名词

Etymology:
From dauntless + -ness.  Dauntless comes from the Old French *danter "to tame, subdue," from Vulgar Latin *domitare, frequentative of Latin domare "to tame."  The -less suffix indicates a lack of something.  Therefore, dauntlessness literally means "a lack of being able to be tamed or subdued."
