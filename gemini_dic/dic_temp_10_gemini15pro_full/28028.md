capacitate:/kəˈpæsɪteɪt/
verb
使能够；使有能力
to make someone or something able to do something
例句：
The training course capacitates students to work in the healthcare industry.
这个培训课程使学生能够在医疗保健行业工作。

例句：
The new law capacitates the government to regulate the industry more effectively.
新法律使政府能够更有效地监管该行业。

近义词：enable, empower, qualify
反义词：disable, incapacitate
词性：v.
