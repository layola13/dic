fogeyish:/ˈfoʊɡiɪʃ/| adj.|守旧的，古板的|Old-fashioned and resistant to change; having old-fashioned ideas or tastes. Characterized by outdated attitudes, beliefs, or behaviors.|He has some rather fogeyish ideas about women's role in society. 他对女性的社会角色有一些相当守旧的看法。|近义词：old-fashioned, antiquated, conservative, stuffy, fuddy-duddy|反义词：modern, progressive, contemporary, liberal|词性:形容词
