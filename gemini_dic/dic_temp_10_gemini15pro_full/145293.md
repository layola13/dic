overbrags: /ˌoʊvərˈbræɡz/
verb
过度吹嘘，夸大其词
Boasts excessively about one's accomplishments or qualities.
He constantly overbrags about his golf game, even though he's a terrible player. 他总是吹嘘他的高尔夫球技，即使他打得很糟糕。
She overbrags about her children's achievements to the point of being annoying. 她夸大她孩子们的成就，到了令人讨厌的地步。
近义词: boasts, brags, shows off
反义词: downplays, understates
词性: verb
