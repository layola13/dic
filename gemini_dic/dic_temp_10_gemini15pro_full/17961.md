befool:/bɪˈfuːl/
verb
愚弄，欺骗
to trick someone into believing something that is not true
The salesman tried to befool me into buying a damaged car.  推销员试图骗我买一辆破损的汽车。
deceive, trick, dupe
enlighten, inform
