formalisms:/ˈfɔːrməlɪzəmz/| n.|形式主义；形式体系；规范|A set of rules and procedures used to represent or describe something, often in a mathematical or logical way.  These rules can be applied to various fields, like computer science, linguistics, and logic.|The formalisms of symbolic logic can be used to represent arguments. 符号逻辑的形式体系可以用来表示论证。|近义词：formalities, conventions, methods, systems, frameworks|反义词: informalities,  improvisations|noun

formalism:/ˈfɔːrməlɪzəm/| n.|形式主义；拘泥形式|Excessive adherence to prescribed forms, rules, or ceremonies, often at the expense of substance or meaning.|His formalism alienated many of his colleagues. 他的形式主义疏远了他的许多同事。|近义词：rigidity, ritualism, conventionalism|反义词: flexibility, informality|noun
