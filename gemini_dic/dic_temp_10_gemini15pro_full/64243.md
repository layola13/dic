equiparable:/ɪˈkwɪpərəbl/ | adj. | 可比较的，相似的 | Similar or equivalent; capable of being compared. | The two cars are equiparable in terms of performance. 这两辆汽车在性能方面是可比的。|  The resources available to the two teams were equiparable.  两个团队可用的资源是相似的。 | 近义词: comparable, similar, analogous, equivalent | 反义词: incomparable, dissimilar, different, unequal | 词性: adjective | 词源: From Late Latin *aequiparabilis, from Latin aequus (“equal”) + parabilis (“comparable”).
