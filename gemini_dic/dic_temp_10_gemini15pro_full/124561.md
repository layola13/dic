mountlet: /ˈmaʊntlət/ | n. |小山, 小丘| A small mountain; a hillock. |  The hikers climbed the mountlet for a panoramic view.  登山者爬上了小山，欣赏全景。| Synonyms: hillock, knoll, mound | Antonyms: mountain, peak | noun

Etymology:  From mount + -let (diminutive suffix).  "Mount" comes from Old French "mont" and ultimately from Latin "mons".  "-let" is a diminutive suffix indicating smallness.
