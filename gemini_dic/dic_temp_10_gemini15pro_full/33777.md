circumambage:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈæmbɪdʒ/ | n. | 迂回，绕圈子 | An indirect and lengthy way of expressing something; a roundabout way of speaking or writing. |  He told us the story without any  circumambage. 他没有绕圈子，直接给我们讲了故事。

circumambage:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈæmbɪdʒ/ | n. | 迂回，绕圈子 | The lawyer's circumambage made it difficult to understand the contract. 律师的迂回措辞使得合同难以理解。

circumambage:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈæmbɪdʒ/ | n. | 迂回，绕圈子 | 近义词: circumlocution, periphrasis, indirectness | 反义词: directness, straightforwardness | 词性: 名词
