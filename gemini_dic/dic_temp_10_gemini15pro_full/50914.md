deserving:/dɪˈzɜːrvɪŋ/| adj.|值得的，应得的|Worthy of reward, praise, or attention.  Having good qualities that merit a particular reward or benefit.|She's a deserving winner. 她是一位实至名归的赢家。|近义词：worthy, meritorious, entitled|反义词：undeserving, unworthy|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *deserven*, from Old French *deservir*, from Latin *dēservīre* (“to serve well, deserve”), from *dē* (“fully, completely”) + *servīre* (“to serve”).
