chomper:/ˈtʃɒmpər/ (UK) /ˈtʃɑːmpər/ (US)| n.|大吃大喝的人；牙齿；爱咬东西的人（尤指小孩）|Someone who chomps or bites things, especially a child or animal.  It can also refer to the teeth themselves, often humorously or figuratively.|例句：That kid's a real chomper – he's always eating something.  那个孩子真是个小吃货，他总是在吃东西。

例句：Look at those chompers! He could bite through a tree trunk!  看看那些牙齿！他能咬穿树干！|近义词：eater, muncher, biter, nibbler, teeth (informal)|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly a "finicky eater")|词性:noun
