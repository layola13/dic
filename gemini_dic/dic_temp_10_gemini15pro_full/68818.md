fascinated:/ˈfæsɪneɪtɪd/| adj.| 着迷的，入迷的|Extremely interested and attracted.|例句：I was fascinated by the documentary about ancient Egypt. 我被关于古埃及的纪录片深深吸引了。

例句：She was absolutely fascinated by his stories of adventure. 她完全被他讲的冒险故事迷住了。|近义词：captivated, enthralled, intrigued, spellbound|反义词：bored, uninterested, apathetic|词性:形容词

Etymology: From the Latin fascinatus, past participle of fascinare "to bewitch, enchant," from fascinum "spell, witchcraft."
