disambiguations:/dɪsˌæmbɪɡjuˈeɪʃənz/ | n. | 消歧，消除歧义 | The process of identifying the intended meaning of a word or phrase that has multiple possible meanings. |  The software uses complex algorithms for the disambiguations of search queries.  该软件使用复杂的算法来消除搜索查询的歧义。 | synonyms: clarifications, resolutions, explanations | antonyms: confusions, ambiguities | noun
