faunally:/ˈfɔːnəli/
adjective
动物区系地；动物群的
Relating to the animal life of a particular region or period.
The faunal diversity of the Amazon rainforest is astonishing. 亚马逊雨林的动物多样性令人惊叹。
Near Antonyms: floral
Antonyms: abiotic
