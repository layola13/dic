agileness:/ˈædʒɪlnəs/
n.|敏捷性，灵活性|The ability to move quickly and easily; nimbleness.|The gymnast displayed incredible agileness on the balance beam. 体操运动员在平衡木上展现出惊人的敏捷性。|nimbleness, flexibility, quickness, dexterity|clumsiness, stiffness, rigidity|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From agile + -ness.  Agile comes from the French word *agile*, from the Latin *agilis*, meaning "quick, nimble, active."  The suffix *-ness* transforms an adjective into a noun denoting a state or quality.
