antiresistants:/ˌæntiɹɪˈzɪstənts/ | n. | 抗药性抑制剂；抗药性对抗剂 | Substances that combat or counteract the development of resistance, especially to drugs or pesticides. |  The researchers are developing new antiresistants to combat the growing problem of antibiotic resistance. 研究人员正在开发新的抗药性抑制剂，以应对日益严重的抗生素耐药性问题。| Synonyms: resistance inhibitors, resistance blockers | Antonyms: resistance promoters, resistance enhancers | noun
