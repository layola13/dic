astrometeorology:/ˌæstroʊˌmiːtiərˈɑːlədʒi/ | n. | 天文气象学 | The study of the supposed influence of celestial bodies on weather.  It attempts to correlate weather patterns with astronomical phenomena. |  Astrometeorology was popular in ancient times, but is now considered a pseudoscience.  天文气象学在古代很流行，但现在被认为是伪科学。| Synonym: astroclimatology | Antonym: meteorology | Noun
