coproducer:/koʊprəˈduːsər/ /kəʊprəˈdjuːsə(r)/| n. |共同制片人；联合制作者|A person who produces something jointly with another or others, especially a film, play, or broadcast.|例句：The film's coproducer was also the screenwriter.  这部电影的联合制片人也是编剧。|例句：She served as coproducer and lead actress in the independent film.  她在这部独立电影中担任联合制片人和女主角。|近义词：joint producer, associate producer|反义词:|词性:noun
