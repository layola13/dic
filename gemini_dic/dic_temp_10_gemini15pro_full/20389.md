bitch-up:/ˈbɪtʃ ʌp/
|phrasal verb|搞砸; 把事情弄糟;抱怨,发牢骚|To spoil or ruin something, often through incompetence or carelessness. Can also mean to complain excessively.|Example 1: He really bitched up the presentation with his unpreparedness. 他真的把演讲搞砸了，因为他没有做好准备。

Example 2: Stop bitching up about the weather, we can't control it.  别再抱怨天气了，我们控制不了它。|Synonyms: mess up, screw up, foul up, complain, whine, gripe|Antonyms: succeed, improve, fix, praise, compliment|verb
