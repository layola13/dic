equivalency:/ɪˈkwɪvələnsɪ/
n.
等价；等效；同等学历
the condition of being equal in value, amount, function, meaning, etc.
Her years of work experience gave her an equivalency to a college degree. 她多年的工作经验使她获得了相当于大学学位的资格。
equivalence, parity, equality
inequality, disparity
noun
