acronyc:/əˈkrɒnɪk/ (UK) /əˈkrɑːnɪk/ (US)| adj.|偕日落西的，与日落同时的|Happening at sunset.  Relating to the rising or setting of a star in conjunction with the sun.|The acronyc rising of Sirius marked the beginning of the ancient Egyptian calendar. 天狼星偕日升标志着古埃及历法的开始。

The acronyc setting of the star coincided with the winter solstice. 这颗星的偕日落与冬至重合。|近义词：cosmical, occidental|反义词：heliacal|词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀκρόνυχος (akrónukhos, “happening at nightfall”), from ἄκρος (ákros, “at the tip, extreme”) + νύξ (núx, “night”).

USAGE->
The acronyc rising of a star is when it rises at sunset.  一颗恒星的偕日升是指它在日落时升起。
The acronyc setting of a star is when it sets at sunrise.一颗恒星的偕日落是指它在日出时落下。


PHRASE->
acronyc rising = 偕日升
acronyc setting = 偕日落
