microphytal: /ˌmaɪkroʊˈfaɪtəl/
adjective|微植物的; 与微植物有关的|Relating to or consisting of microphytes, which are microscopic plants, especially algae, growing submerged in or on the bottom of a body of water.|The microphytal community plays a crucial role in the aquatic ecosystem. 微植物群落在水生生态系统中起着至关重要的作用。|例句：The study focused on the microphytal algae in the lake.  这项研究的重点是湖中的微植物藻类。

|近义词: algal, microscopic plant|反义词: macrophytal|词性: adjective
