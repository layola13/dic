likelinesses:/ˈlaɪklɪnɪsɪz/
n.
可能性，相似性
The statistical analysis increased the likelinesses of finding a cure.  统计分析增加了找到治愈方法的可能性。
There are striking likelinesses between the two paintings.  这两幅画之间有着惊人的相似之处。
Synonym: probabilities, chances, possibilities, resemblances, similarities
Antonym: unlikelihoods, improbabilities, differences, dissimilarities
noun
