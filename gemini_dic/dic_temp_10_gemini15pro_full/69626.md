feminism:/ˈfɛmɪˌnɪzəm/| n.|女权主义|the advocacy of women's rights on the grounds of the equality of the sexes.|例句：She is a strong advocate for feminism. 她是一位坚定的女权主义倡导者。

例句：Feminism is not about hating men, but about achieving equality. 女权主义不是憎恨男性，而是关于实现平等。

近义词：women's liberation, gender equality|反义词: sexism, misogyny|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *féminisme*, from *féminin* (“feminine”) + *-isme* (“-ism”).  Ultimately from Latin *fēmina* (“woman”).
