orblet:/ˈɔːrblɪt/
n.
小球体；小装饰球；（尤指）玻璃装饰球
A small, decorative globe or ball, especially one made of glass.
She placed a delicate glass orblet on the Christmas tree. 她把一个精致的玻璃小球挂在了圣诞树上。

orblet:/ˈɔːrblɪt/
n.
(in fantasy or science fiction) a magical globe or sphere
（奇幻或科幻小说中的）魔法球体
The wizard summoned an orblet of pure energy.  巫师召唤出一个纯能量球。


近义词:globe, sphere, orb, bauble, trinket
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "fragment" if referring to wholeness)


词性: noun (名词)


词源:  Likely a diminutive of "orb,"  from Latin "orbis" meaning "circle" or "disk."  The "-let" suffix is a diminutive, indicating smallness.
