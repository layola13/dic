modernizer:/ˈmɑːdərˌnaɪzər/ | n. | 现代化者，革新者 | A person or thing that modernizes something. |  The company hired a modernizer to update their outdated systems.  这家公司聘请了一位革新者来更新他们过时的系统。 | Synonyms: innovator, reformer, updater | Antonyms: traditionalist, conservative | noun

modernize:/ˈmɑːdərˌnaɪz/ | v. | 使现代化，更新 | To make something more modern. | They decided to modernize the factory with new equipment. 他们决定用新设备使工厂现代化。| Synonyms: update, renovate, innovate | Antonyms: preserve, maintain, keep | verb

modern:/ˈmɑːdərn/ | adj. | 现代的，近代的 | Relating to the present or recent times as opposed to the remote past. | The building has a very modern design. 这栋建筑的设计非常现代。| Synonyms: contemporary, current, latest | Antonyms: ancient, old-fashioned, outdated | adjective
