overmodesties:/ˌoʊvərˈmɒdɪstiz/ | n. | 过分谦虚，过度的谦逊 | Excessive or exaggerated modesty. |  Her overmodesties were beginning to irritate him.  她过分的谦虚开始让他恼火。 | Near Synonyms:  humility, self-effacement, diffidence, unpretentiousness | Antonyms:  arrogance, boastfulness, conceit, egotism | Noun

overmodest:/ˌoʊvərˈmɒdɪst/ | adj. | 过分谦虚的，过分谦逊的 | Excessively or unnecessarily modest. | She was overmodest about her achievements. 她对自己的成就过分谦虚。 | Near Synonyms: humble, self-effacing, diffident, unpretentious | Antonyms:  arrogant, boastful, conceited, egotistical | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
