drearinesses:/ˈdrɪəriːnɪsɪz/
n.
沉闷，单调，乏味
The state of being dreary; dullness and monotony.
The drearinesses of small-town life drove her to seek excitement in the city. 小镇生活的沉闷驱使她去城市寻找刺激。
The long winter brought with it a sense of drearinesses. 漫长的冬天带来了一种沉闷的感觉。
Synonyms: monotony, dullness, tedium, boredom, drabness
Antonyms: excitement, vibrancy, liveliness, interest, stimulation
