myrtiform:/ˈmɜːrtɪfɔːrm/
adjective
桃金娘形的；桃金娘状的
Shaped like a myrtle berry or leaf.  Resembling the leaves of a myrtle.
The surgeon pointed out the small, myrtiform carcarunculae at the entrance of the vagina.  外科医生指出了阴道入口处小的、桃金娘状的肉阜。
The scar tissue was myrtiform in shape.  疤痕组织呈桃金娘状。
近义词: myrtle-shaped
反义词: (No common direct antonym; describe the opposite shape, e.g., elongated, circular)
词性: adjective
