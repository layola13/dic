diversity:/daɪˈvɜːrsəti/ /dɪˈvɜːrsəti/| n.|多样性，差异性，多样化|The diversity of plant and animal life in the rainforest is amazing.  雨林中动植物的多样性令人惊叹。|synonyms: variety, difference, multiplicity|antonyms: uniformity, homogeneity, sameness|noun

diversity:/daɪˈvɜːrsəti/ /dɪˈvɜːrsəti/| n.|分歧，不同|There is a wide diversity of opinion on this issue. 在这个问题上，意见分歧很大。|synonyms: disagreement, discord, variance|antonyms: agreement, concord, harmony|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin diversitas, from Latin diversus (“different, diverse”), past participle of divertere (“to turn aside, separate”), from dis- (“apart”) + vertere (“to turn”).
