harsh:/hɑːrʃ/ /hɑːrʃ/| adj.|刺耳的，严厉的，粗糙的，严酷的|unpleasantly rough or jarring to the senses; cruel or severe.|The harsh winter left many animals dead. 严酷的冬天导致许多动物死亡。|近义词：severe, austere, rough, rigorous, stringent|反义词：gentle, mild, lenient, smooth, pleasant|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *harsch, from Old Norse harþr (“rough, sharp”), from Proto-Germanic *harþuz (“rough, sharp, hairy”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱerh₂- (“to scratch, comb, card”). Cognate with Dutch harsch (“sharp, harsh”), German harsch (“harsh”), Swedish harsk (“rancid”).
