inharmony:/ɪnˈhɑːrməni/ | adj. | 不和谐，不协调 | Lacking harmony; discordant. |  The bright pink walls were inharmony with the antique furniture.  亮粉色的墙壁与古董家具不协调。 | 近义词: discordant, dissonant, incongruous, clashing, conflicting, incompatible | 反义词: harmonious, concordant, agreeable, compatible, consistent | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From in- (not) + harmony (agreement, concord).  "Harmony" comes from the Greek "harmonia" meaning "joint, agreement, concord of sounds."
