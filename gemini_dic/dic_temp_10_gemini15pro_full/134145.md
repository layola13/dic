nonimposition:/ˌnɒnɪmpəˈzɪʃən/
n.
不强加;不强制
the act of not imposing something, especially taxes or restrictions
The nonimposition of tariffs helped boost international trade.  不强加关税有助于促进国际贸易。

近义词:tolerance, forbearance, latitude, leniency, permissiveness
反义词:imposition, enforcement, compulsion, coercion
