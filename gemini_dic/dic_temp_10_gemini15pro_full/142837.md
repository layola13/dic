out-design:/ˌaʊt dɪˈzaɪn/
verb
胜过…的设计; 设计比…更好
To design something that is superior to something else.
Her new dress completely out-designed all the others at the fashion show. 她的新裙子在时装秀上彻底胜过了所有其他裙子的设计。
The new stadium out-designed all the previous ones in terms of functionality and aesthetics.  这座新体育场在功能性和美学方面都比以往的体育场设计得更好。
近义词: surpass, outdo, excel
反义词:  be inferior to, be worse than
词性: verb
