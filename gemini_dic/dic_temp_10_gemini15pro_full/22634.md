borable:/ˈbɔːrəbl/ | adj. | 无聊的，令人厌烦的 | Capable of being bored; susceptible to boredom.  | That movie was so borable, I almost fell asleep. 那部电影太无聊了，我差点睡着了。| The lecture was incredibly borable.  这讲座真是无聊透顶。| 近义词: tedious, dull, monotonous, tiresome | 反义词: interesting, exciting, engaging, stimulating | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From bore (verb) + -able (suffix).  The suffix "-able" forms adjectives meaning "capable of being" or "worthy of being".  "Bore" itself comes from Old English "borian," meaning "to pierce, perforate," later developing the sense of "to weary by tedious repetition."
