cauldrons:/ˈkɔːldrənz/ (US), /ˈkɔːldrɒnz/ (UK)| n. |大锅， cauldron的复数形式 |A large metal pot with a lid and handle, used for cooking over an open fire.  A cauldron is often associated with magic and witchcraft.|The witches brewed a potent potion in their cauldrons. 女巫们在大锅里煮了一种强效的药水。|Synonyms: pots, kettles, vats|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "small pot" or "bowl")|Noun
