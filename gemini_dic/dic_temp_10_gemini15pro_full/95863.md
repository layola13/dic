incorruptibly:/ˌɪnkəˈrʌptɪbli/
adverb
廉洁地，不可腐蚀地
in a way that is not and cannot be corrupted; in a way that is honest and just.
The judge ruled incorruptibly on the case, refusing to be swayed by bribes or threats.  法官廉洁地对案件作出裁决，拒绝受到贿赂或威胁的影响。
He served the public incorruptibly for many years. 他廉洁地为公众服务了很多年。
Synonym: honestly, uprightly, ethically, virtuously
Antonym: corruptly, dishonestly, unethically, venally
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + corrupt + -ible (capable of being) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *corrumpere* (to destroy).
