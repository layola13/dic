emotional:/ɪˈmoʊʃənəl/ (US), /ɪˈməʊʃənəl/ (UK)| adj.| 情绪化的，感性的| Relating to or characterized by emotion; readily affected by or expressing emotion.| She became very emotional when talking about her childhood. 她谈到童年时变得非常情绪化。| He gave an emotional speech about the importance of family. 他发表了一场关于家庭重要性的感人演讲。|近义词：sentimental, passionate, sensitive|反义词：unemotional, apathetic, indifferent|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *emotif*, from Old French *emotif*, from Late Latin *ēmōtīvus*, from Latin *ēmōtus* (“moved, roused”), past participle of *ēmoveō* (“I move out, remove, stir up, excite”), from *ē* (“out”) + *moveō* (“I move”).
