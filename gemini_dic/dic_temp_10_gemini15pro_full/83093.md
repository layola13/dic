gushingness:/ˈɡʌʃɪŋnɪs/
n.
热情奔放；滔滔不绝；过分热情
The quality of being effusive or excessively enthusiastic.
Her gushingness about the new movie star was a bit much. 她对这位新电影明星的热情有点过头了。
effusiveness, exuberance, enthusiasm
restraint, reserve
noun
