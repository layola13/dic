binges:/ˈbɪndʒɪz/| n.| 放纵；狂欢；狂热行为| periods of excessive indulgence in an activity, especially eating, drinking, or taking drugs|  She went on a shopping binge and spent all her money. 她疯狂购物，花光了所有的钱。|  He's been on a Netflix binge all weekend. 他整个周末都在狂看Netflix。|近义词：sprees, excesses, indulgences|反义词: moderation, abstinence|词性:名词


词源:
From the verb "binge," which originated in the early 19th century. Its exact etymology is uncertain, but it's thought to be related to the dialectal word "bing," meaning "to soak" or "to steep." This might refer to the idea of being saturated or overwhelmed by an activity.
