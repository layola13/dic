hemihedral:/ˌhɛmiˈhiːdrəl/
adjective
半面象的;半面晶形的
Having half the usual number of plane surfaces, or only half the faces developed, said of crystals.
Crystals of pyrite are sometimes hemihedral. 黄铁矿晶体有时是半面象的。
hemihedric
anhedral, holohedral
adjective
