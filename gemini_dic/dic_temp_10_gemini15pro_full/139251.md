obligato:/ˌɒblɪˈɡɑːtəʊ/ (British), /ˌɑːblɪˈɡɑːtoʊ/ (American)| n.| obbligatos (plural)|义不容辞的事；强制性的事物；（尤指乐曲中的）强制性乐段|An obligato is a musical part that must be played exactly as written and cannot be omitted.  It is often a technically challenging and prominent part of a piece.|The cello has a beautiful obligato in the second movement.|近义词：requirement, necessity, must|反义词：optional|词性:noun

obligato:/ˌɒblɪˈɡɑːtəʊ/ (British), /ˌɑːblɪˈɡɑːtoʊ/ (American)| adj.| obbligato (also spelled obbligati)|强制性的；（尤指乐曲中的）强制性的|An obligato part in a musical composition is one that must be played; it is essential and indispensable.|The clarinet has an obligato passage in this sonata.|近义词：required, compulsory, mandatory|反义词：optional, discretionary|词性:adjective
