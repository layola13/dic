de-stratified:/diːˈstrætɪfaɪd/
adjective
去分层的，消除阶层的
Removed the stratification from; made less stratified.  The process of mixing the layers of a stratified fluid.
The researchers de-stratified the lake by introducing oxygen to the lower levels. 研究人员通过向较低层引入氧气来消除湖泊的分层。
近义词:homogenized, mixed, blended
反义词:stratified, layered, tiered
词性:adjective

de-stratify:/diːˈstrætɪfaɪ/
verb
去分层，消除阶层
To remove the stratification from; to make less stratified.
Regular mixing de-stratifies the lake, preventing oxygen depletion. 定期混合可以消除湖泊的分层，防止氧气耗尽。
近义词:homogenize, mix, blend
反义词:stratify, layer, tier
词性:verb
