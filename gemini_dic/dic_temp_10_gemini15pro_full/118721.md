metagastric:/ˌmɛtəˈɡæstrɪk/| adj.|胃后的，位于胃后的|Situated behind or posterior to the stomach.  Relating to the posterior part of the stomach.|The metagastric pouch in crustaceans aids in digestion.|近义词：postgastric|反义词：pregastric|词性:adjective
Etymology: meta- (Greek, "after, beyond, with, among") + gastric (from Greek gaster, "stomach")
