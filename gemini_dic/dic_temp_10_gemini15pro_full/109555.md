lest:/lɛst/
conjunction
唯恐，以免
for fear that; to avoid the risk of
例句：She spoke softly lest she should wake the baby. 她轻声说话，以免吵醒婴儿。

例句：He gripped his brother's arm lest he be trampled by the surging crowd. 他紧紧抓住他哥哥的胳膊，以免被人群冲撞倒地。

近义词：in case, for fear that, so that...not
反义词: so that
词性: conjunction
