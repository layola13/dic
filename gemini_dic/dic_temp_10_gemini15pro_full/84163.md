half-pleased:/ˌhɑːfˈpliːzd/
adjective
半高兴的，不太满意的
Partly pleased; not fully satisfied.  Showing a mixture of pleasure and displeasure or dissatisfaction.
She gave a half-pleased, half-doubtful smile. 她露出了一个半高兴半怀疑的微笑。
Near synonyms: partially pleased, mildly pleased, lukewarm, ambivalent
Antonyms: delighted, thrilled, overjoyed, displeased, dissatisfied
词性: 形容词
