merrily:/ˈmerəli/| adv.|快乐地，高兴地|In a cheerful and happy way. |They sang merrily as they walked through the forest. 他们一路穿过森林，快乐地唱着歌。|例句：The children laughed merrily as they played in the park. 孩子们在公园里玩耍，高兴地笑着。|近义词：happily, joyfully, cheerfully, gaily|反义词：sadly, gloomily, morosely|词性:副词

词源: From Middle English *merili, from Old English *mergelice (“merrily, pleasantly, sweetly, gently”), equivalent to merry +‎ -ly.
