menthol:/ˈmenθɒl/ (UK), /ˈmenθɔːl/ (US)| n.|薄荷醇，薄荷脑|A crystalline alcohol with a minty taste and odor, found in peppermint and other mint oils. It is used in flavorings, medicines, and cosmetics.|例句：The cough drops have a strong menthol flavor. 这些止咳糖有很强的薄荷味。

例句：Menthol is often used in balms to relieve muscle pain. 薄荷醇常用于药膏中缓解肌肉疼痛。|近义词：mint, peppermint oil|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
Etymology: From German *Menthol*, from *Minze* (“mint”) + *-ol* (chemical suffix denoting an alcohol). Ultimately from Latin *menta* and Greek *μίνθη* (mínthē).
