hams:/hæmz/ | n. | 火腿肉; (無線電)業餘愛好者 |  The cured meat from the hind leg of a pig, often smoked or salted. Also, (informal) amateur radio operators. |  We had ham and eggs for breakfast. 我们早餐吃了火腿和鸡蛋。  The hams were chatting on the radio all night. 那些火腿族整晚都在无线电上聊天。 |  Near synonyms: gammon, bacon, prosciutto (for specific types), radio amateurs  | Antonyms: (for the food sense) none; (for the radio sense) professionals | noun
