apologetic:/əˌpɒləˈdʒetɪk/ /əˌpɑːləˈdʒetɪk/| adj.|道歉的，愧疚的，抱歉的|Showing or expressing regret, especially for having caused trouble or offense.|例句：He gave her an apologetic smile. 他给了她一个歉意的微笑。|近义词：sorry, remorseful, repentant, contrite|反义词：unapologetic, unrepentant|词性:形容词
apologetics:/əˌpɒləˈdʒetɪks/ /əˌpɑːləˈdʒetɪks/| n.|辩护学，护教学|Reasoned arguments or writings in justification of something, typically a theory or religious doctrine.|例句：Christian apologetics defends the beliefs of Christianity. 基督教护教学捍卫基督教的信仰。|近义词：defense, justification, vindication|反义词：criticism, attack, condemnation|词性:名词
