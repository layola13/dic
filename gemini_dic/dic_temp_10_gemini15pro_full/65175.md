ethnolinguisticses:/ˌɛθnoʊlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪksɪz/
|n.|民族语言学|The study of the relationships between language and culture, and how they mutually influence each other.  It explores how language reflects and shapes cultural values, beliefs, and practices.|Ethnolinguisticses examines the intricate connections between language and culture.|近义词: anthropological linguistics, linguistic anthropology|反义词: N/A|词性: Noun
