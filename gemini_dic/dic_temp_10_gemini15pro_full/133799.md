nongeometrical:/ˌnɒnˌdʒiːəˈmetrɪkəl/
adjective
非几何的
Not pertaining to or according to the rules or principles of geometry; not geometrical.
例句：The artist's work explored nongeometrical shapes and forms.  这位艺术家的作品探索了非几何的形状和形式。

例句：The software can generate both geometrical and nongeometrical patterns.  这款软件可以生成几何和非几何图案。

近义词：amorphous, irregular, shapeless
反义词：geometrical, geometric
