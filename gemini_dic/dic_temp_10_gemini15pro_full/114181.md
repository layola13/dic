mahrati:/məˈrɑːti/| n. adj.|马拉地语；马拉地人的|Marathi is an Indo-Aryan language spoken predominantly by the Marathi people of Maharashtra, India. It is the official language and co-official language in the Maharashtra and Goa states of Western India, respectively, and is one of the 22 scheduled languages of India.|She speaks fluent Marathi.|近义词:Marathi language|反义词: (No direct antonym for a language)|noun, adjective
