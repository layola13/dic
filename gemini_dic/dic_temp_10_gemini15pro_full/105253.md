khanga:/ˈkɑːŋɡə/| n. |坎加|A brightly colored piece of cloth, about 1.5 x 1 meters (5 x 3 feet), printed with sayings (usually in Swahili) and worn by women and men throughout eastern Africa.|She wore a beautiful khanga with a proverb printed on it. 她穿着一件印有谚语的漂亮坎加。|Synonym: kanga, leso|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "Western clothing")|Noun
khanga:/ˈkɑːŋɡə/| n. |坎加(复数)|Multiple khangas.|She brought back a collection of khangas from her trip to Tanzania. 她从坦桑尼亚之旅带回了一系列坎加。|Synonym: kangas, lesos|Antonym: (No direct antonym)|Noun
