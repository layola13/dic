outeaten:/ˌaʊtˈiːtn/
verb
吃得比…多，胜过
to have eaten more than someone else
The caterpillar outeaten all its siblings. 这条毛毛虫比它所有的兄弟姐妹都吃得多。
近义词: outconsume, surpass in eating
反义词: be outeaten by

词性: verb
