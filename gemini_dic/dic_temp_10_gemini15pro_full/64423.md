ergonomists:/ˌɜːrɡəˈnɒmɪsts/
noun
人体工程学家
Experts in ergonomics, a discipline focused on designing and arranging things people use so that the people and things interact most efficiently and safely.
The ergonomists redesigned the office workstations to reduce strain on employees.  人体工程学家重新设计了办公室工作站，以减少员工的压力。
Ergonomists play a vital role in improving workplace safety.  人体工程学家在改善工作场所安全方面发挥着至关重要的作用。
Synonyms: human factors specialists, human factors engineers
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps) designers ignoring human factors, inefficient designers
