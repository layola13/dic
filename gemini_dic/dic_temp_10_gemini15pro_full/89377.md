homophobias:/ˌhəʊməˈfəʊbiəz/ /ˌhoʊməˈfoʊbiəz/| n. |同性恋恐惧症（复数）|An irrational fear of, aversion to, or discrimination against homosexuality or homosexuals. It can manifest in various forms, including prejudice, discrimination, and violence.|

例句：Homophobias are still prevalent in many societies. 同性恋恐惧症在许多社会仍然盛行。

例句：He faced many challenges due to deeply ingrained homophobias. 由于根深蒂固的同性恋恐惧症，他面临许多挑战。


近义词：anti-gay prejudice, homonegativity|
反义词:  acceptance, tolerance|
词性: noun|

词源：homo- (from Greek homós, meaning "same") + -phobia (from Greek phobos, meaning "fear") + -s (plural suffix)
