carbines:/ˈkɑːrˌbaɪnz/ /ˈkɑːrˌbiːnz/| n. |卡宾枪|A lightweight rifle, shorter than a standard rifle but longer than a submachine gun, often used by cavalry or other mounted troops historically, and now frequently used by special forces and law enforcement.|The soldiers were armed with carbines and pistols.|近义词：rifle, short-barreled rifle|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "long gun")|noun
