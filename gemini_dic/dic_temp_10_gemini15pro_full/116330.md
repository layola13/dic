masterliness:/ˈmɑːstərlɪnɪs/
n.
精通；熟练；大师风范
The quality of being masterly; skill and expertise.
Her masterliness of the piano was evident in her flawless performance.  她精湛的钢琴技艺在她完美的演奏中显而易见。
skill, expertise, proficiency, mastery, competence
ineptness, clumsiness, awkwardness, incompetence
词性: 名词
