euhemerize:/juːˈhiːməraɪz/ | v. | 赋予神话以世俗解释；将神话历史化；理性解释神话 | To interpret myths as traditional accounts of historical persons and events. |  Historians tried to euhemerize the ancient myths, suggesting that gods were originally just powerful kings. 历史学家试图将古代神话历史化，认为神最初只是强大的国王。

 | Examples:
Some scholars euhemerize the story of Atlantis, claiming it was based on a real island. 一些学者将亚特兰蒂斯的故事历史化，声称它基于一个真实的岛屿。

Mythologists often euhemerize ancient legends to find historical kernels within them. 神话学家经常将古代传说历史化，以从中找到历史的内核。

 | Synonyms: rationalize, historicize, demythologize, secularize | Antonyms: mythologize, spiritualize | verb
