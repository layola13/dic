nonmeetings:/nɒnˈmiːtɪŋz/
noun
非会议，无会议
A period of time scheduled for focused, individual work, free from interruptions like meetings.  It's a dedicated time for deep work, allowing individuals to concentrate on tasks requiring sustained attention.
Many companies are now implementing nonmeeting days to improve productivity. 许多公司现在正在实施无会议日以提高生产力。
synonyms:  focus time, deep work time, uninterrupted work time
antonyms:  meeting time, scheduled meetings
