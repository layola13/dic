dreamier:/ˈdriːmiər/ | adj. | 更梦幻的，更爱幻想的 | More dreamy; given to or characterized by daydreaming.  | The music created a dreamier atmosphere.  这音乐营造了一种更梦幻的氛围。 |She had a dreamier look in her eyes after the trip. 旅行结束后，她的眼神里充满了梦幻般的神采。 | 近义词: more fanciful, more imaginative, more whimsical | 反义词: more realistic, more practical, more down-to-earth | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From dreamy + -er (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from dream (n.) + -y (adjective suffix) + -er.  "Dream" comes from Old English *drēam, of Germanic origin.
