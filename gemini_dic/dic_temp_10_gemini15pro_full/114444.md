makers:/ˈmeɪkərz/| n. |制造者，创造者|People or companies that create or produce something. |例句：The makers of this software are based in California.  这款软件的制造商位于加利福尼亚州。

例句：The makers of the new electric car promise it will revolutionize the industry.  这款新型电动汽车的制造商承诺它将彻底改变该行业。|近义词：creators, producers, manufacturers, builders, authors, designers|反义词：destroyers, demolishers|词性:名词(plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *makeres*, plural of *makere*, from Old English *macera*, agent noun from *macian* ("to make"). Cognate with Dutch *maker*, German *Macher*.
