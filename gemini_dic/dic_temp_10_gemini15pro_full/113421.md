lymphocytes:/ˈlɪmfəsaɪts/ | n. | 淋巴细胞 | A type of white blood cell that is part of the immune system.  Lymphocytes develop from lymphoblasts (immature lymphocytes) within lymphoid tissue in organs such as the thymus, lymph nodes, and spleen. |  The doctor said my lymphocyte count was low. 医生说我的淋巴细胞计数低。|  leukocytes, white blood cells | erythrocytes, red blood cells | noun
