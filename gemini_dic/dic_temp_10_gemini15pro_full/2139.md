adaptor:/əˈdæptər/ (US) /əˈdæptə(r)/ (UK)| n.|适配器; 转接器|A device used to connect two incompatible pieces of equipment.|I needed an adaptor to use my European hairdryer in the US. 我需要一个适配器才能在美国使用我的欧洲吹风机。|近义词：converter, connector, adapter|反义词:|noun

adaptor:/əˈdæptər/ (US) /əˈdæptə(r)/ (UK)| n.|改编者; 改写者|A person who adapts a literary work, such as a novel or play, for another medium, such as film or television.|He is a well-known adaptor of classic novels for the stage. 他是一位著名的经典小说舞台剧改编者。|近义词：rewriter|反义词:|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From adapt + -or.  Ultimately from Latin *ad- "to" + aptare "to fit."

USAGE->
With electrical devices:
I bought a new adaptor for my laptop charger. = 我买了一个新的笔记本电脑充电器适配器。
The travel adaptor allows me to use my phone in different countries. = 旅行适配器可以让我在不同的国家使用我的手机。

Referring to a person:
The adaptor did a great job of translating the novel into a screenplay. =  改编者出色地将小说改编成了剧本。
She is a talented adaptor of Shakespeare's plays for a modern audience. = 她是一位才华横溢的莎士比亚戏剧现代改编者。
