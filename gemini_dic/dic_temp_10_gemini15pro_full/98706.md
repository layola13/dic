insupportably:/ˌɪnsəˈpɔːrtəbli/ | adv. | 无法忍受地，难以支撑地 |  Not able to be supported or borne; intolerably. | The heat was insupportably intense.  酷热难耐。| Synonyms: intolerably, unbearably, unendurably | Antonyms: bearably, tolerably, supportably | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From in- (not) + supportable + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Supportable" comes from the verb "support," from Old French "supporter," ultimately from Latin "supportare" (to carry from below).
