bernicle: /ˈbɑːrnɪkəl/ (US) /ˈbɑːnɪkəl/ (UK)| n. |藤壶，茗荷|A marine crustacean with a hard shell, that attaches itself firmly to rocks, ships' bottoms, etc.|例句：The hull of the ship was covered in barnacles. 船体上覆盖着藤壶。|例句：Barnacles are filter feeders, straining plankton from the water. 藤壶是滤食动物，从水中过滤浮游生物。|近义词：limpet |反义词: None |词性:noun

bernicle: /ˈbɑːrnɪkəl/ (US) /ˈbɑːnɪkəl/ (UK)| v. |依附，缠着（尤指像藤壶一样）|To attach oneself or something else firmly to something.|例句：He barnacled himself to the project and wouldn't let go.  他紧紧地抓住这个项目不放。|例句：The children barnacled themselves to their mother. 孩子们紧紧地依偎着他们的母亲。|近义词：cling, adhere, attach|反义词: detach, separate |词性:verb
