hyperarousal:/ˌhaɪpərəˈraʊzəl/ | n. |过度 возбуждение|A state of increased physiological and psychological alertness and activity, often characterized by anxiety, insomnia, and difficulty concentrating.|After the accident, she experienced hyperarousal and couldn't sleep for days. 事故发生后，她经历了过度兴奋，好几天都睡不着觉。|近义词:hyperstimulation, overstimulation, agitation |反义词:calmness, tranquility, relaxation |词性:名词
