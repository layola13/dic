oversour:/ˌoʊvərˈsaʊər/ | v. | 过度酸化；使过酸 | To make something too sour or acidic.  |  The cook oversoured the soup with too much lemon juice.  厨师在汤里放了太多的柠檬汁，使汤变得过酸了。| synonyms: overacidify | antonyms: sweeten, neutralize | verb

oversour:/ˌoʊvərˈsaʊər/ | adj. | 过酸的 | Excessively sour. | The oversour lemonade was undrinkable.  这杯过酸的柠檬水没法喝。 | synonyms: too sour, excessively acidic | antonyms: sweet, mild | adjective
