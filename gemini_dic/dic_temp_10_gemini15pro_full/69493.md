felicitous:/fəˈlɪsɪtəs/ | adj. | 贴切的；恰当的；幸福的 | Well-chosen or suited to the circumstances. Marked by happiness or good fortune. | Her felicitous choice of words impressed everyone. 她恰当的措辞给每个人都留下了深刻的印象。 | synonyms: apt, appropriate, suitable, fitting, fortunate, lucky | antonyms: inappropriate, unsuitable, unfortunate, unlucky | adjective

felicitous:/fəˈlɪsɪtəs/ | adj. | 幸福的；愉快的 |  Experiencing or expressing great happiness. |  It was a felicitous occasion for the whole family.  这对全家来说都是一件喜事。 | synonyms: joyful, happy, blissful, merry | antonyms: unhappy, sad, miserable, sorrowful | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *felicitosus*, from felix (“lucky, fortunate, happy, fruitful, fertile”) +‎ -osus (suffix forming adjectives meaning ‘full of, prone to, resembling’).
