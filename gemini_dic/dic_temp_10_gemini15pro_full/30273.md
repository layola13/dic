cemental:/sɪˈmɛntəl/
adjective
水泥的；胶结的
Relating to or resembling cement; used for cementing.
The cemental surface of the tooth is prone to decay.  牙齿的牙骨质表面容易腐烂。
近义词:adhesive, binding
反义词:separating, detaching
词性:adjective

cemental:/sɪˈmɛntəl/
noun
牙骨质
A specialized calcified substance covering the root of a tooth.
Cemental resorption can occur due to various factors. 牙骨质吸收可能由于多种因素而发生。
近义词:cementum
反义词:None (This is a specialized anatomical term)
词性:noun
