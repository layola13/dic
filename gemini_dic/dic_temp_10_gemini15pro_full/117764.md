melatonins: /ˌmeləˈtoʊnɪnz/ | n. |褪黑素，褪黑激素（复数）| A hormone secreted by the pineal gland that regulates sleep-wake cycles. It is often used as a supplement to treat insomnia and jet lag. |  Melatonins are naturally produced in the body.  褪黑素是人体自然产生的。

Melatonin supplements can help with jet lag.  补充褪黑素可以帮助缓解时差。 | Synonym: N-acetyl-5-methoxytryptamine | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but light suppresses melatonin production) | Noun
