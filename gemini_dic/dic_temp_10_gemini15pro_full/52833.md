diluvium:/dɪˈluːviəm/
noun
洪积层，冲积层
A deposit of clay, sand, gravel, and boulders left by flowing water, especially a flood.
The archaeologist found ancient tools embedded in the diluvium. 考古学家在洪积层中发现了古代工具。
近义词: alluvium, drift
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps bedrock)
词性: n.

diluvium:/dɪˈluːviəm/
Etymology: From Latin *diluvium, from diluere "to wash away," from di- "away" + -luere, related to lavare "to wash."
词源：源自拉丁语 *diluvium，来自 diluere “冲走”，来自 di- “离开” + -luere，与 lavare “洗”相关。
