excommunication: /ˌɛks.kəˌmjuː.nɪˈkeɪ.ʃən/ | n. | 开除教籍; 逐出教会 | The act of officially excluding someone from participation in the sacraments and services of the Christian Church. |  He was threatened with excommunication for his heretical views. 他因为异端观点而被威胁要逐出教会。 |  The church imposed excommunication on the blasphemer. 教会对亵渎神明者施以开除教籍的处罚。 |  banishment, expulsion, ostracism | inclusion, acceptance | noun
