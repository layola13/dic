calumniatory:/kəˈləmniətɔːri/ |adj.| 诽谤的，中伤的 |Making false and defamatory statements about someone. | The newspaper published a calumniatory article about the politician. 这家报纸刊登了一篇关于这位政治家的诽谤性文章。|Synonyms: defamatory, libelous, slanderous, malicious, false, untrue,  |Antonyms:  laudatory, complimentary, praising, flattering | adjective

calumnious:/kəˈləmniəs/ |adj.| 诽谤的，中伤的 |Containing or constituting calumny. | He made calumnious accusations against his rival. 他对他的竞争对手提出了诽谤性的指控。 | Synonyms: defamatory, libelous, slanderous, malicious, false, untrue, | Antonyms: laudatory, complimentary, praising, flattering | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *calumniatorius, from Latin calumniari "to slander," from calumnia "slander, false accusation," perhaps from calvi "to deceive"
