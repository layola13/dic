fault:/fɔːlt/ (British) /fɑːlt/ (American)| n. vi.|错误，缺点，故障；断层|A defect or imperfection; responsibility for a mistake or wrongdoing. |The fault lies with the manufacturer.  这个错误在于制造商。|Synonyms: defect, flaw, imperfection, blemish, error, mistake |Antonyms:  perfection, strength, virtue|noun
fault:/fɔːlt/ (British) /fɑːlt/ (American)| vi.|找出缺点；弄错|To find a defect or imperfection in something. |He faulted her argument for lack of evidence. 他指责她的论点缺乏证据。|Synonyms: criticize, censure, blame |Antonyms:  praise, commend|verb
