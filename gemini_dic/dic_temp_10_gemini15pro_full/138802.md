nummulites: /ˌnʌmjʊˈlaɪtiːz/
| n. |货币虫，货贝|A large, lens-shaped foraminifer of the genus Nummulites, common as fossils in Tertiary rocks, especially the Eocene.|The pyramids of Egypt are built largely of nummulitic limestone. 埃及的金字塔主要由货币虫灰岩建造而成。|Synonyms: foraminifer, fossil|Antonyms: N/A|noun
