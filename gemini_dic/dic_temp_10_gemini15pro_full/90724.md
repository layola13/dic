humiliant:/hjuːˈmɪliənt/ | adj. | 羞辱性的，丢脸的 | Causing humiliation; humbling. |  He found the experience utterly humiliant. 他觉得这次经历非常丢脸。|  The teacher's harsh criticism was humiliant for the student. 老师严厉的批评让学生感到羞辱。 | 近义词:humiliating, degrading, shameful, mortifying | 反义词:exalting, uplifting, honoring | 词性:形容词

词源:  From Late Latin *humiliant- (nominative *humilians), present participle of humiliare "to humble," from Latin humilis "low, humble" (from humus "ground").
