gnatworm:/ˈnætˌwɜːrm/| n. |蚊幼虫，摇蚊幼虫|A small, transparent, aquatic larva of a gnat, especially a midge, often used as fish food. | The fish tank was teeming with gnatworms.  鱼缸里到处都是摇蚊幼虫。|Near synonyms: bloodworm, midge larva| Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps adult gnat/midge)| noun
