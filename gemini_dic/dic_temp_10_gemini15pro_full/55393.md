distender:/dɪˈstɛndər/| n.|扩张器，膨胀器|Something that distends or causes distension.  A device or substance that expands or stretches something, such as a surgical instrument used to dilate a body cavity.|The surgeon used a distender to examine the patient's esophagus.|近义词：dilator, expander, stretcher|反义词:constrictor, compressor|noun
