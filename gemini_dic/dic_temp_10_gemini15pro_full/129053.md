nitrifies:/ˈnaɪtrɪfaɪz/
verb
硝化；使硝化
converts (ammonia) into nitrates, especially by the action of bacteria.
Certain bacteria in the soil nitrify ammonia, converting it into nitrates that plants can use. 土壤中的某些细菌会硝化氨，将其转化为植物可以利用的硝酸盐。
近义词:oxidizes
反义词:denitrifies
词性:v.
