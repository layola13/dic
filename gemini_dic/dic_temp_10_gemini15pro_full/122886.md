mollusk:/ˈmɒləsk/ (UK) /ˈmɑːləsk/ (US)| n. |软体动物|Any invertebrate of the phylum Mollusca, typically having a calcareous shell of one, two, or more pieces that wholly or partly enclose the soft, unsegmented body. This phylum includes snails, slugs, mussels, and octopuses.|例句：The octopus is a highly intelligent mollusk. 章鱼是一种非常聪明的软体动物。

例句：Seashells are the exoskeletons of marine mollusks.  贝壳是海洋软体动物的外骨骼。

近义词：invertebrate, shellfish (for some mollusks)|反义词: vertebrate|词性:noun
