carjacking:/ˈkɑːrdʒæˌkɪŋ/
n.
劫车
The act of taking control of a motor vehicle by force or threat of force from the driver or other occupant.
The police are investigating a series of carjackings in the city. 警方正在调查该市发生的一系列劫车案。

carjacking:/ˈkɑːrdʒæˌkɪŋ/
n.
劫持汽车
The act of stealing a car by force.
He was the victim of a violent carjacking. 他是一起暴力劫车案的受害者。


近义词:auto theft, robbery, hijacking
反义词:(None -  lack of a direct opposite action)
词性:noun
