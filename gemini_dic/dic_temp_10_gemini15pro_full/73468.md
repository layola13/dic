foreplan:/fɔːrˈplæn/| v.|预先计划，提前规划|To plan or arrange something in advance.|We need to foreplan our strategy for the next quarter. 我们需要提前规划下个季度的战略。|例句：They foreplanned the entire project, anticipating potential problems. 他们提前规划了整个项目，预见到了潜在的问题。

例句：Foreplanning your meals can save you time and money. 提前计划你的膳食可以节省时间和金钱。|近义词：preplan, plan ahead, anticipate|反义词:improvise|词性:动词
