n.|棚屋，披屋；单坡屋顶|A simple shelter with a sloping roof, typically built against a wall or other support.|We built a lean-to against the side of the house to store firewood.|shed, outhouse|house, building|noun

lean-to: /ˈliːn tuː/ (US), /ˈliːn tuː/ (UK)
adj.|单坡的；披屋式的|Having a single sloping roof.|The lean-to roof provided some protection from the rain.|sloping, pitched|flat, level|adjective
