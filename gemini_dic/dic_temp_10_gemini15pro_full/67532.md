extradition:/ˌɛkstrəˈdɪʃən/ | n. |引渡|The act of delivering by one state or nation to another of a person accused or convicted of a crime in the latter state or nation.|The government is seeking the extradition of the suspected terrorist. 政府正在寻求引渡这名恐怖嫌疑犯。| 近义词：deportation, rendition | 反义词: asylum | 词性: 名词

etymology:
From French *extradition*, from Late Latin *extraditio*, from Latin *ex-*, "out" + *traditio*, "handing over".
