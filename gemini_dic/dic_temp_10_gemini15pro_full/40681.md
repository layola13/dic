conservationism:/ˌkɑːnsərˈveɪʃənɪzəm/
n.
保护主义，保守主义
The belief in the wise use and protection of natural resources.
例句：Conservationism is crucial for protecting endangered species.  保护主义对于保护濒危物种至关重要。

例句：Her conservationism led her to a career in environmental law. 她的保护主义使她从事了环境法律方面的工作。

近义词：environmentalism, preservationism
反义词：exploitation, wastefulness
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From conserve (v.) + -ation + -ism.  Conserve ultimately from Latin conservare "to keep, preserve," from com- "together" + servare "to keep, guard."
