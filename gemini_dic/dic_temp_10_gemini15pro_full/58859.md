dura:/ˈdjʊərə/| n.|硬脑膜|The outermost, toughest, and most fibrous of the three membranes (meninges) covering the brain and spinal cord. |The surgeon carefully repaired the tear in the dura. 外科医生仔细地修补了硬脑膜上的裂口。|Synonyms: meninx, pachymeninx||Noun
