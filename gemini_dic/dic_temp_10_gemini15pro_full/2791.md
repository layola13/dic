advance: /ədˈvæns/ (US), /ədˈvɑːns/ (UK)| n. v. adj.|前进，进步；预付，提前；进展|An advance is a forward movement or improvement. As a verb, it means to move forward or to make progress. As an adjective, it describes something happening or existing before a particular time.|The army advanced on the enemy. 部队向敌人进发。|近义词：progress, development, improvement, proceed, further, promote|反义词：retreat, decline, hinder, withdraw|词性: noun, verb, adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *avancer* (to put forward, promote), ultimately from Latin *abante* (from before, ahead).

USAGE->
Noun:
The advance of technology has changed our lives. = 科技的进步改变了我们的生活。
The company made an advance of $10,000 to the employee. = 公司预付给员工10,000美元。

Verb:
The soldiers advanced slowly through the forest. = 士兵们缓慢地穿过森林。
He advanced his career by taking on new challenges. = 他通过接受新的挑战来提升自己的职业生涯。

Adjective:
We received an advance warning of the storm. = 我们收到了风暴的预警。
The advance team arrived a week before the event. = 先遣队在活动前一周到达。

Present Simple:
The company advances its employees based on merit. = 公司根据员工的功绩晋升他们。
She advances her argument with clear evidence. = 她用清晰的证据来推进她的论点。

Past Simple:
They advanced towards the city walls. = 他们向城墙推进。
He advanced a new theory about the origin of the universe. = 他提出了一个关于宇宙起源的新理论。

Present Perfect:
Medical science has advanced significantly in recent years. = 近年来医学取得了显著进步。
The project has advanced to the next stage. = 该项目已进入下一阶段。

Past Perfect:
The army had advanced several miles before encountering resistance. =  军队在遇到抵抗之前已经前进了几英里。
She had advanced her career considerably before retiring. =  她在退休前已经大大提升了她的职业生涯。

Future Simple:
We will advance the date of the meeting. = 我们将提前开会日期。
Technology will continue to advance at a rapid pace. = 科技将继续快速发展。

Passive Voice:
The date of the meeting was advanced by a week. = 会议日期提前了一周。
Funds were advanced to the company by the investors. = 投资者向公司预付了资金。


PHRASE->
in advance = 提前
advance on = 向...前进
advance payment = 预付款
advance warning = 预先警告
advance to = 晋升到
