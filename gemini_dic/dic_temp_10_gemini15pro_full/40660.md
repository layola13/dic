convergence:/kənˈvɜːrdʒəns/| n. |趋同，汇聚，收敛|The convergence of technologies is blurring the lines between traditional industries. 技术的融合模糊了传统产业之间的界限。|例句：The convergence of mobile and internet technologies has created new opportunities. 移动和互联网技术的融合创造了新的机遇。

例句：The report examined the convergence of economic and political factors. 该报告考察了经济和政治因素的趋同。|近义词：confluence, meeting, junction, union, merging, coalescence|反义词：divergence, separation, division|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin convergentia, from Latin convergere "to incline together," from com- "together" + vergere "to bend, turn, incline."
