outtricks: /ˌaʊtˈtrɪks/
verb|以计谋胜过，智胜|to defeat or surpass someone by using tricks or cleverness|He outtricked his opponents and won the game. 他智胜对手，赢得了比赛。|Example: The magician outtricked the audience with his illusions. 魔术师用他的幻觉欺骗了观众。|Synonyms: outsmart, outwit, outmaneuver|Antonyms: be outwitted, be outsmarted|词性:动词
