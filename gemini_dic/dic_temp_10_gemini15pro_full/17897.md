beeper:/ˈbiːpər/| n.|寻呼机，传呼机，蜂鸣器|A small electronic device that makes a beeping sound, often used as a pager or to signal an event.|例句：He clipped the beeper to his belt. 他把寻呼机别在腰带上。

例句：The beeper went off, signaling the end of the break. 蜂鸣器响了，标志着休息结束。|近义词：pager, buzzer, alarm|反义词:|noun
