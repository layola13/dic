green-manured:/ˌɡriːn məˈnjʊərd/
adjective
绿肥的
(of land) fertilized with green manure.
例句：The green-manured field showed improved soil health. 绿肥田显示出土壤健康状况的改善。

例句：Green-manured crops can help fix nitrogen in the soil. 绿肥作物可以帮助固定土壤中的氮。

近义词：fertilized, enriched
反义词：unfertilized, depleted
词性: adjective

词源：
"green" (referring to the use of living plants) + "manure" (fertilizer)
