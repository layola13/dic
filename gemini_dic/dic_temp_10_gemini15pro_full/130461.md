nonabstainer:/ˌnɒnəbˈsteɪnər/ /ˌnɑːnəbˈsteɪnər/| n.|不节制者；放纵者|A person who does not abstain from something, especially alcohol.|He's a nonabstainer and enjoys a good glass of wine with dinner. 他不戒酒，喜欢晚餐时喝杯好酒。|近义词：indulger, libertine|反义词：abstainer, teetotaler|词性: noun

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not" (from Latin)
abstainer (noun): from abstain (verb), from Latin "abstinere" (to hold back from)
