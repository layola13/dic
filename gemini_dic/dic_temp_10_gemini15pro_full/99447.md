interesting:/ˈɪntrəstɪŋ/| adj.|有趣的，引起兴趣的|Arousing or holding the attention.|例句：The book is very interesting. 这本书很有趣。|近义词：fascinating, engaging, captivating, intriguing, compelling|反义词：boring, dull, tedious, monotonous|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *interessen*, from Anglo-Norman *interesser*, from Old French *interesser* (“to concern, involve”), from Latin *inter* (“between, among”) + *esse* (“to be”).  The meaning shifted from "of importance, significance" to "exciting attention, curiosity" in the late 18th century.
