bacilluses:/bəˈsɪləsɪz/
n.
杆菌; 细菌（bacillus的复数）
Rod-shaped bacteria; plural form of bacillus.
Bacilluses were found in the soil sample. 土壤样本中发现了杆菌。
Many types of bacilluses are harmless. 许多类型的杆菌是无害的。
bacteria, germs, microbes
viruses
noun
