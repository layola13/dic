nondisastrous:/ˌnɒndɪˈzɑːstrəs/
adjective
非灾难性的；未造成灾难的
Not disastrous; without disastrous consequences.
The mission was nondisastrous, although it wasn't entirely successful.  这次任务没有造成灾难，虽然它并不完全成功。
The economic downturn was nondisastrous for our company. 经济衰退对我们公司来说并非灾难性的。
Synonyms: fortunate, lucky, uneventful, successful, harmless
Antonyms: disastrous, catastrophic, calamitous, devastating, ruinous
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + disastrous (causing great damage or harm).  Disastrous ultimately comes from the Latin dis- (badly) and astrum (star), reflecting the ancient belief that disasters were caused by unfavorable astrological alignments.
