delirant:/dɪˈlɪərənt/| adj.|谵妄的，神志不清的，疯狂的|Experiencing or characterized by delirium; raving.  Affected with or characteristic of delirium.|The patient became delirant from the high fever.|近义词：raving, frenzied, mad, insane, unhinged|反义词：lucid, sane, rational|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin dēlīrāns, dēlīrant- (present participle of dēlīrāre ‘be crazy’), from dē- ‘away from’ + līra ‘furrow, line’.
