nisi:/ˈnaɪsaɪ/| conj.|除非，如果不|unless; if not.  It introduces a condition.  It is often used in formal or legal contexts.|The contract will be void nisi you sign it by Friday.  除非你在星期五之前签署，否则合同无效。|例句：Nisi prius.  除非先前。|近义词:unless, except, if not|反义词:if|词性:连词
