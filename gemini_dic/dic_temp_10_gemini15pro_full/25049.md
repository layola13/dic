bse:/biː ɛs iː/ | n. | 牛海绵状脑病 (Bovine Spongiform Encephalopathy) |  A fatal neurodegenerative disease in cattle that causes a spongy degeneration of the brain. It is also known as "mad cow disease". | The outbreak of BSE in the UK led to a ban on beef exports. 英国的疯牛病爆发导致了牛肉出口的禁令。| synonym: Mad Cow Disease | antonym:  (No direct antonym for a specific disease. Could consider "health" in a broad sense) | noun
