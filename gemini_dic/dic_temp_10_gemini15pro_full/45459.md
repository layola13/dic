curations:/kjʊˈreɪʃənz/| n.|策展，管理，精选集|The museum's curations have been praised for their diversity and depth.  博物馆的策展因其多样性和深度而受到称赞。|例句：The online platform offers curations of articles and videos on various topics. 该在线平台提供关于各种主题的文章和视频的精选集。

例句：The gallery's latest curations showcase emerging artists from around the world.  画廊最新的策展展示了来自世界各地的新兴艺术家。|近义词：collections, selections, exhibitions|反义词: accumulations,  disorder|词性:名词

词源: From the Latin word "cura" meaning "care," referring to the careful selection and organization of items in a collection. 源于拉丁语单词 "cura"，意思是 "关心"，指的是对集合中物品的仔细选择和组织。
