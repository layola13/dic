dolorousness:/ˈdoʊlərnəsnəs/ /ˈdɒlərəsnəs/
n.
悲痛，忧伤
The state of being dolorous; grief, sorrow.
Her dolorousness after the loss of her pet was palpable. 她失去宠物后的悲痛显而易见。
The poem conveyed a deep sense of dolorousness. 这首诗表达了深深的悲伤。
近义词：sorrow, grief, sadness, misery, anguish
反义词：joy, happiness, elation, cheerfulness
词性: 名词

dolorous:/ˈdoʊlərəs/ /ˈdɒlərəs/
adj.
悲伤的，痛苦的
Feeling or expressing sorrow; mournful.
He let out a dolorous sigh. 他发出一声悲叹。
The funeral was a dolorous occasion. 葬礼是一个悲伤的场合。
近义词：sorrowful, mournful, grieving, sad, heartbroken
反义词：joyful, happy, cheerful, elated
词性: 形容词

词源:
From Late Latin *dolorosus, from Latin dolor (“pain, grief”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *dólh₁es (“wound, sore”).
