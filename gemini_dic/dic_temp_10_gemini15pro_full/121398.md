mishna:/ˈmɪʃnə/| n.|密西拿，米示拿|The Mishnah is the first major written collection of the Jewish oral traditions known as the Oral Torah.|例句：He is studying the Mishnah. 他正在学习密西拿。

例句：The Mishnah is a foundational text of Rabbinic Judaism. 密西拿是拉比犹太教的 foundational  文本。|近义词：Talmud, Gemara|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Hebrew מִשְׁנָה (mišnā, “repetition, study, teaching”), from the verb שָׁנָה (šanā, “to repeat, to learn, to teach”).  Ultimately from the Proto-Semitic *šny (“two, second”). Related to Arabic مِثْنَىٰ (miṯnā, “dual”).
