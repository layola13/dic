lubricate:/ˈluːbrɪkeɪt/ /ˈljuːbrɪkeɪt/| v. |润滑，涂油；使…顺利进行|To apply a substance such as oil or grease to (a machine or engine) to reduce friction and allow smooth movement; to make (something) operate or run more smoothly.|例句：The mechanic lubricated the engine. 机械师给发动机上了油。|近义词：oil, grease, smooth, facilitate|反义词: jam, stick, obstruct|词性:动词

lubricate:/ˈluːbrɪkeɪt/ /ˈljuːbrɪkeɪt/| adj. |润滑的|Having been lubricated; smooth and slippery.| 例句：The lubricated surface reduced friction. 润滑的表面减少了摩擦。|近义词：oiled, greased, slippery, smooth|反义词: rough, dry|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin lubricus "slippery," related to labi "to glide," from PIE root *sleub- "to slip, slide."
