freestyle:/ˈfriːstaɪl/| n. adj. adv. v.|自由式，即兴的，自由泳，自由式地|A style of performance, usually artistic, that is improvised or unrehearsed.|He did a freestyle rap during the concert. 他在音乐会上表演了一段即兴说唱。|近义词：improvised, ad-lib, extemporaneous|反义词：rehearsed, planned, prepared|词性:名词, 形容词, 副词, 动词
词源: free (自由的) + style (风格)
