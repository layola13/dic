monocracy:/məˈnɒkrəsi/ | n. | 独裁统治; 独裁政体 | A government ruled by a single person. |  The small island nation was under a strict monocracy.  这个小岛国家处于严格的独裁统治之下。 | The king's monocracy lasted for decades. 国王的独裁统治持续了几十年。 | 近义词：dictatorship, autocracy, despotism | 反义词：democracy, republic | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Greek *μόνος (mónos, “alone, single”) +‎ *-κρατία (-kratía, “-cracy”).
