hypernaturally:/ˌhaɪpərˈnætʃrəli/
adverb|超自然地，极其自然地|In a way that is beyond what is considered natural; with extreme naturalness.|She behaved hypernaturally calm during the crisis.  她在危机中表现得异常冷静。|例句：He spoke hypernaturally, as if reading from a script. 他说话极其自然，就好像照着稿子念一样。|近义词：unnaturally, artificially, preternaturally, extraordinarily|反义词：naturally, normally|词性: 副词


Etymology:
"hyper-" (Greek): over, beyond, excessive
"natural" (Latin "naturalis"): related to nature
