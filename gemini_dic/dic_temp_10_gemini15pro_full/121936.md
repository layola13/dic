misships: /mɪsˈʃɪps/
n.
错误装运; 装错船
the act of shipping goods to the wrong destination or on the wrong vessel.
The misships caused a significant delay in the project.  错误装运导致项目严重延误。
The company had to pay a hefty fine for the misships. 公司不得不为装错船支付巨额罚款。
近义词: misdirection, misrouting, misexpedition
反义词: correct shipment


词性: 名词

词源:  A compound word formed from "mis-" (meaning "wrong" or "bad") and "ships" (the plural of "ship," referring to vessels or the act of transporting goods by sea).  来源于"mis-"（意为“错误的”或“坏的”）和“ships”（“ship”的复数形式，指船只或通过海运货物）的复合词。
