atavism:/ˈætəvɪzəm/| n.|返祖现象；隔代遗传|The reappearance of a characteristic in an organism after several generations of absence, especially one that belonged to a remote ancestor.  The sudden appearance of a primitive trait or characteristic in an individual, typically one that has not appeared in recent generations. |例句：The child's hairy back was considered an atavism, a throwback to our primate ancestors.  孩子的毛茸茸的背被认为是一种返祖现象，是对我们灵长类祖先的回归。

例句：The artist's fascination with ancient mythology could be seen as a kind of cultural atavism.  这位艺术家对古代神话的迷恋可以被看作是一种文化返祖现象。|近义词：reversion, throwback, regression|反义词: evolution, progression|词性:noun
