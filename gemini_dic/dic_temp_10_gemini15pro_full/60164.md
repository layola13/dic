effeminations:/ɪˌfemɪˈneɪʃənz/
noun
女人气的行为；柔弱的姿态
The act of making someone or something appear more feminine, typically in a way considered unfitting or undesirable.
His effeminations, such as constantly adjusting his hair and speaking in a high-pitched voice, were often mocked by his peers.  他女性化的行为，例如不断地整理头发和用尖细的声音说话，经常受到同龄人的嘲笑。
