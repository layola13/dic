organics:/ɔːrˈɡænɪks/ | n. |有机物，有机食品|Substances or products that are produced or grown naturally, without the use of synthetic pesticides, herbicides, or fertilizers.|  例句：She only buys organics from the local farmers market. 她只从当地农贸市场购买有机食品。

例句：Organics are becoming increasingly popular. 有机食品正变得越来越受欢迎。


|近义词：organic produce, organic food, bio-products|反义词：inorganics, conventional (foods)|词性：名词

organics:/ɔːrˈɡænɪks/ | adj. |有机的|Relating to or derived from living matter.| 例句：Organic farming methods are better for the environment. 有机耕作方法对环境更有利。

例句：The store sells organic fruits and vegetables. 这家商店出售有机水果和蔬菜。


|近义词：biological, natural|反义词：inorganic, synthetic, artificial|词性：形容词
