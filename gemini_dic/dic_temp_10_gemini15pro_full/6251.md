amoebas:/əˈmiːbəz/ (US) /əˈmiːbiːz/ (UK)| n. |变形虫，阿米巴|A single-celled animal that catches food and moves about by extending fingerlike projections of protoplasm.  A microscopic single-celled organism that has no fixed shape and moves by extending pseudopodia.|Amoebas are simple organisms found in pond water. 变形虫是在池塘水中发现的简单生物。|  Amoebas can reproduce by splitting in two. 变形虫可以通过分裂成两个进行繁殖。|Synonyms: protozoan, microorganism|Antonyms: multicellular organism|Noun
