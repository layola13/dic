diesinking:/ˈdaɪˌsɪŋkɪŋ/
noun
落料加工，型腔加工，模具沉降加工
The shaping of a metal die or mold by forcing a hardened steel master die into it under pressure.
Diesinking is a crucial process in the manufacturing of molds. 落料加工是模具制造中的一个关键工序。
The company specializes in high-precision diesinking. 这家公司专门从事高精度模具沉降加工。
近义词: die making, mold making, cavity sinking
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "mold removal" as a contrasting process)
词性: n.
