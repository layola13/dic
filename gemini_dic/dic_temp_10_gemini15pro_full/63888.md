epimer:/ˈɛpɪmər/| n.|差向异构体|Diastereomers that differ in the absolute configuration at only one chiral center.  Two sugars that differ only in the configuration around one carbon atom are called epimers.|Glucose and galactose are epimers. 葡萄糖和半乳糖是差向异构体。|Near Synonyms:diastereomer, isomer|Antonyms:enantiomer|noun
