heptarchist:/ˈhɛptɑːkɪst/
n.
七国时代的君主;七雄之一;拥护七国统治的人
a ruler or supporter of a heptarchy (a government of seven rulers)
The historian described the ambitions and rivalries of the various heptarchists. 这位历史学家描述了七位君主的野心和竞争。
King Æthelberht of Kent is considered one of the most powerful heptarchists. 肯特郡的埃塞尔伯特国王被认为是七位君主中最有权势的一位。
Synonym:ruler, monarch, sovereign
Antonym:subject, citizen
