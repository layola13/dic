nutlet:/ˈnʌtlɪt/ | n. | 小坚果; 小核果 | A small nut, especially a hard, one-seeded fruit that does not dehisce at maturity. | The mint family produces tiny nutlets.  薄荷科植物产生微小的坚果。|  The nutlets are dispersed by wind. 这些小坚果靠风传播。| synonyms: nut, achene, seed | antonyms: (No direct antonym for a botanical term like nutlet) | noun
