epicedial: /ˌɛpɪˈsaɪdiəl/ | adj. | 插曲式的，片段式的; 附带的，次要的 |  Relating to or resembling an epicedium; a short musical or literary composition commemorating a deceased person.  Often used to describe something that is incidental or secondary to the main narrative or event. |  The epicedial poem was a touching tribute to the fallen hero.  这首插曲式的诗是对这位 fallen hero 感人的致敬。 | synonyms: incidental, digressive, tangential, subsidiary, ancillary | antonyms: central, main, principal, primary | adjective
