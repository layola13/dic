draw-line:/ˈdrɔː laɪn/ |n.|划线；界限|The act of drawing a line, often to create a boundary or separation.  It can also refer to the line itself or a limit beyond which one will not go.|He drew a line in the sand. 他在沙滩上画了一条线。|近义词：boundary, limit, demarcation|反义词：connect, unite|noun

draw-line:/ˈdrɔː laɪn/ |v.|划线；设定界限|To create a line by drawing.  Figuratively, it means to set a limit or boundary.|She drew a line under the total. 她在总数下划了一条线。|近义词：demarcate, delimit, separate|反义词：merge, blend|verb
