antiexpressionist:/ˌæntiɪkˈspreʃənɪst/
| adj. | 反表现主义的 | Opposed to or rejecting expressionism, an artistic style that emphasizes the artist's subjective emotional experience rather than objective representation. |  His antiexpressionist paintings focused on precise geometric forms. 他的反表现主义绘画侧重于精确的几何形式。|  The architect's antiexpressionist design favored clean lines and functionalism.  这位建筑师的反表现主义设计倾向于简洁的线条和功能主义。| near antonyms: expressionist, emotional, subjective | antonyms: objective, representational | adjective
