magnesic:/mæɡˈniːzɪk/
adjective
镁的；含镁的
Containing or consisting of magnesium.
例句：Magnesic soils are rich in magnesium. 含镁的土壤富含镁。

例句：Magnesic limestone is a type of rock containing magnesium carbonate. 白云石是一种含有碳酸镁的岩石。

近义词：magnesium-bearing, magnesium-rich
反义词: (No common direct antonym, perhaps magnesium-deficient or magnesium-poor)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From magnesium + -ic.  Magnesium ultimately derives from Magnesia, a district in Thessaly, Greece.
