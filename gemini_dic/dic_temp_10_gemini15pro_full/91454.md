hygroscopic:/ˌhaɪɡrəˈskɑːpɪk/ | adj. |吸湿的；吸水的 | Readily absorbing moisture; tending to absorb moisture from the air. |  Table salt is hygroscopic and will clump together if left uncovered in humid weather.  食盐具有吸湿性，如果在潮湿的天气下不加盖就会结块。

| Example: Silica gel is a hygroscopic substance often used as a desiccant. 硅胶是一种吸湿性物质，常用作干燥剂。


|Synonyms:  absorbent, moisture-absorbing, water-attracting | Antonyms:  water-repellent, hydrophobic | Word type: Adjective
