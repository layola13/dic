outhustled:/ˌaʊtˈhʌsəld/
verb
以更积极的方式胜过，比…更努力
To defeat or surpass someone by working harder or being more determined.
Our team was outhustled by the opponents in the final quarter.  我们的团队在最后一节被对手以更积极的方式击败了。
They outhustled their competitors and won the contract. 他们比竞争对手更努力，赢得了合同。
近义词: outwork, outmaneuver, outperform
反义词: underperform, be outdone

词性: verb
