crash: /kræʃ/ | n. v. | 撞车，崩溃，坠毁，破产，(金融)崩盘 | A sudden, violent, and destructive collision, or a sudden failure or collapse. | The car crash was caused by icy roads. 汽车撞车是由结冰的路面造成的。| Synonyms: collision, smash, wreck, collapse, failure | Antonyms:  success, thrive, flourish | noun

crash: /kræʃ/ | v. | 撞车，崩溃，坠毁，破产，(金融)崩盘，(尤指未经允许)突然闯入 | To collide violently and destructively, to suddenly fail or collapse, or to enter suddenly and without permission.  | The stock market crashed in 1929. 股市在1929年崩盘了。 | Synonyms: collide, smash, wreck, collapse, fail, intrude | Antonyms: succeed, thrive, flourish, exit | verb

Etymology: From imitative origin.
