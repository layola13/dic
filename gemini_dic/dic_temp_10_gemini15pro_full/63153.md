ensues:/ɪnˈsuːz/
verb
接着发生；因而产生
follows as a consequence or result
Heavy rains fell, and flooding ensued.  暴雨倾盆，接着发生了洪水。
The police officer pulled the driver over, and a lengthy discussion ensued.  警察让司机靠边停车，随后进行了 lengthy 的讨论。
近义词:follows, results, develops, arises, stems
反义词:precedes, causes, originates
词性:v.
