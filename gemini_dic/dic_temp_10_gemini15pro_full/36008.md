codicil: /ˈkɒdɪsɪl/ (US) /ˈkəʊdɪsɪl/ (UK)| n. |遗嘱附录，附加条款|An addition or supplement that explains, modifies, or revokes a will or part of one.|Example: He added a codicil to his will leaving his watch to his grandson. 他在遗嘱中添加了一个附录，将他的手表留给了他的孙子。

Example:  The codicil clarified the distribution of her estate.  这个附录澄清了她的遗产分配。|近义词：addendum, appendix, supplement|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

codicils: /ˈkɒdɪsɪlz/ (US) /ˈkəʊdɪsɪlz/ (UK)| n. |遗嘱附录（复数）|Plural form of codicil, referring to multiple additions or supplements to a will.|Example: Several codicils were added to the will over the years. 多年来，遗嘱中添加了几个附录。

Example: The lawyer reviewed all the codicils to ensure they were legally sound. 律师审查了所有附录，以确保它们在法律上是健全的。|近义词：addenda, appendices, supplements|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
