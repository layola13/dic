gunsmiths:/ˈɡʌnsmɪθs/| n.|枪匠，修枪匠|A gunsmith is a person who repairs, modifies, designs, or builds firearms.|The local gunsmiths kept the town's hunting rifles in good condition. 当地的枪匠们使镇上的猎枪保持良好状态。|The gunsmiths meticulously crafted each component of the rifle. 枪匠们精心制作了步枪的每一个部件。|Synonyms: armorer, firearms specialist|Antonyms: N/A|noun
