corrugation:/ˌkɔːrəˈɡeɪʃn/ | n. | 波纹；褶皱；起皱 |  The process of forming wrinkles or folds in a material, or the resulting wrinkled or folded state.  | The roof was made of corrugated iron. 屋顶是用波纹铁皮做的。 | The corrugations in the cardboard add strength.  纸板上的波纹增加了强度。 | synonym: furrow, wrinkle, crimp, crease | antonym: smoothness, flatness | noun

corrugated:/ˈkɔːrəɡeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 波纹的；褶皱的；起皱的 |  Shaped into alternating ridges and grooves. |  Corrugated cardboard is commonly used for packaging. 瓦楞纸板通常用于包装。 | The old barn had a corrugated iron roof.  旧谷仓有一个波纹铁皮屋顶。 | synonym: wrinkled, ridged, furrowed, crimped | antonym: smooth, flat | adjective
