antitechnologies:/ˌænti-tekˈnɒlədʒiz/ | n. | 反技术；抵制技术 | Technologies that are perceived as harmful, undesirable, or working against human interests.  These can include technologies that are specifically designed to disrupt or disable other technologies, or technologies whose unintended consequences are deemed negative. |  Example:  The development of antitechnologies, like EMP devices, poses a significant threat to modern infrastructure.  开发像电磁脉冲装置这样的反技术，对现代基础设施构成了重大威胁。

Example: Some argue that social media, despite its benefits, is an antitechnology due to its addictive nature and potential for spreading misinformation.  一些人认为，尽管社交媒体有其好处，但由于其令人上瘾的性质和传播错误信息的可能性，它是一种反技术。 | 近义词: counter-technologies, disruptive technologies | 反义词:  technologies, pro-technology | 词性: 名词
