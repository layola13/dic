noninteractive:/ˌnɒnɪntərˈæktɪv/ | adj. |非交互式的|Not designed for or allowing direct communication or feedback between a user and a computer system.|The process was entirely noninteractive; the user simply input the data and waited for the output.|近义词:automatic, passive, one-way|反义词:interactive, dynamic, two-way|词性:形容词
Etymology: non- + interactive.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "interactive" describes a process where two or more things affect each other.
