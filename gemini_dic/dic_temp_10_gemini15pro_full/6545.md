amylogen:/ˈæmɪləʊdʒən/ | n. |淀粉生成素；淀粉形成体|A substance present in some plant cells that initiates the formation of starch.|Amylogen is essential for the plant to store energy. 淀粉生成素对植物储存能量至关重要。|Synonyms: starch synthase, starch-forming enzyme |Antonyms: amylase (an enzyme that breaks down starch)|noun
