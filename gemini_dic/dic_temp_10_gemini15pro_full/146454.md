overfrequent:/ˌoʊvərˈfriːkwənt/ | adj. | 过于频繁的，过多的 | Happening too often; excessively frequent. |例句：Her overfrequent visits became a burden. 她过于频繁的拜访成了一个负担。

例句：The overfrequent use of antibiotics can lead to resistance. 过于频繁地使用抗生素会导致耐药性。 | 近义词：excessive, too frequent, inordinate | 反义词：infrequent, rare, occasional | 词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "above, beyond") + "frequent" (from Latin frequens, frequent-, meaning "crowded, numerous, often").  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning of "frequent" to indicate an excessive degree.
