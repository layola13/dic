mistitling: /ˌmɪsˈtaɪtlɪŋ/
noun
错误命名；贴错标签
the act of giving something a wrong title or label.
The mistitling of the painting led to confusion among art historians. 这幅画的错误命名导致了艺术史学家之间的困惑。

The report was a case of deliberate mistitling. 这份报告是故意贴错标签的。

mislabeling, misnaming
correct titling, accurate labeling
n.
