ego-trip:/ˈiːɡoʊ trɪp/| n.|自我陶醉之旅；自私的炫耀行为；|An act or course of action that boosts one's self-esteem, often in a way that seems excessive or self-centered to others.|He went on an ego trip after winning the award, bragging about it to everyone. 他获奖后开始自我膨胀，逢人就吹嘘。|近义词：self-indulgence, vanity trip, boasting, showing off|反义词：humility, modesty|noun
