huck:/hʌk/| v. |兜售，叫卖；用力扔|To sell something, often small items, in a loud and enthusiastic way, especially on the streets; to throw something with force.| He was hucking vegetables on the street corner. 他在街角叫卖蔬菜。|  He hucked the ball over the fence. 他用力把球扔过篱笆。|近义词：peddle, hawk, pitch, toss, fling|反义词:buy, purchase|词性:动词
