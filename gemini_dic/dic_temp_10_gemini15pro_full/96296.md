indigene:/ˈɪndɪdʒiːn/ /ɪnˈdɪdʒən/| n.|土著居民，原住民|A person who is originally from a particular place; a native.|例句：The government is working with indigenous peoples to protect their land rights. 政府正在与原住民合作，以保护他们的土地权利。

例句：The indigenes of this island have a unique culture.  这个岛上的土著居民有着独特的文化。|近义词：native, aboriginal, original inhabitant|反义词：foreigner, immigrant, alien|词性:n.
