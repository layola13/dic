humanizing:/ˈhjuːmənaɪzɪŋ/| adj.|使人性化；赋予人性的；使变得更人道的|Making something more humane or giving it human qualities.|例句：The company is trying to humanize its image by focusing on the stories of its employees. 公司正试图通过关注员工的故事来使其形象更人性化。

例句：Humanizing the workplace can lead to increased productivity and employee satisfaction.  使工作场所更人性化可以提高生产力和员工满意度。


|近义词：personalizing, individualizing, warming, softening|反义词：dehumanizing, objectifying|词性:形容词
