bumped:/bʌmpt/
verb|碰撞，撞到；提高；取消预定|To come into contact with something suddenly and forcefully.  To increase something. To remove someone from a reserved place, especially on a flight.|I bumped into an old friend at the supermarket. 我在超市撞见了一位老朋友。|近义词：hit, knock, collide, raise, increase|反义词:decrease, lower
adjective|凸起的，有肿块的|Having a bump or bumps; raised or swollen.|The car had a bumped fender after the accident.  事故后，汽车的挡泥板凹凸不平。|近义词：lumpy, uneven, swollen|反义词:smooth, flat
noun|碰撞，撞击|An instance of bumping into something.|The car suffered a few bumps and scratches. 这辆车有一些碰撞和刮痕。|近义词：knock, collision, impact|反义词: N/A
