mournfulnesses:/ˈmɔːrnfəlnəsɪz/
n.
悲哀，忧伤
The state of being mournful; sadness or sorrow.
Her mournfulnesses were evident in her downcast eyes and quiet demeanor. 她的悲伤从她低垂的眼睛和安静的举止中显露无疑。
近义词：sadnesses, griefs, sorrows
反义词：joys, happinesses
词性：名词


mournfulness:/ˈmɔːrnfəlnəs/
n.
悲哀，忧伤
The state of being mournful; sadness or sorrow.
The mournfulness of the occasion was palpable. 这个场合的悲伤气氛是显而易见的。
近义词：sadness, grief, sorrow
反义词：joy, happiness
词性：名词


mournful:/ˈmɔːrnfəl/
adj.
悲伤的，忧伤的
Expressing or feeling sorrow or grief.
The mournful sound of the bagpipes filled the air.  风笛悲伤的声音弥漫在空气中。
近义词：sad, sorrowful, grieving
反义词：joyful, happy, cheerful
词性：形容词


mourn:/mɔːrn/
v.
哀悼，悲伤
Feel or express sorrow or grief.
They mourned the loss of their beloved pet. 他们哀悼他们心爱宠物的去世。
近义词：grieve, sorrow, lament
反义词：rejoice, celebrate
词性：动词

词源：From Old English *murnan, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch moeren and German murren ‘to grumble’.
