colera:/ˈkɒlərə/ (UK) /ˈkɑːlərə/ (US)| n. |霍乱|An acute intestinal infection caused by ingestion of contaminated water or food, characterized by profuse watery diarrhea, vomiting, and muscle cramps.|Cholera is a serious health concern in many developing countries. 霍乱在许多发展中国家是一个严重的健康问题。|The epidemic of cholera spread rapidly through the refugee camp. 霍乱疫情在难民营迅速蔓延。|近义词:dysentery, gastroenteritis|反义词: (No direct antonym for a disease) |词性:noun
