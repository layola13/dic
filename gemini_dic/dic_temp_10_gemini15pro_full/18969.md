bestializes:/ˈbɛstiəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使兽性化；使野蛮化；使堕落
To make someone or something behave like a beast; to deprive someone of human qualities and reduce them to a savage level.
The war bestialized the soldiers, stripping them of their humanity. 战争使士兵们兽性大发，剥夺了他们的人性。
His constant exposure to violence has bestialized him. 他不断接触暴力使他变得野蛮。
