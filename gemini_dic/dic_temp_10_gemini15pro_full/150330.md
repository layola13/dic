panhellenist:/ˌpænˈhɛlɪnɪst/| n. adj.|泛希腊主义者，泛希腊主义的|A supporter or advocate of Pan-Hellenism, a movement in the 19th century advocating the unification of all Greeks.|The panhellenist movement gained momentum in the lead-up to the Greek War of Independence.|近义词：nationalist, patriot|反义词:separatist|词性:名词, 形容词

Etymology:
From pan- + Hellenist, from Hellen, ultimately from Greek Héllēn (Ἕλλην).
