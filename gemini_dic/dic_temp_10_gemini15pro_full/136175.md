nonreinforcement:/ˌnɒnriːɪnˈfɔːsmənt/ /ˌnɑːnriːɪnˈfɔːrsmənt/| n. |不强化，无强化|The absence of reinforcement, which can lead to the extinction of a learned behavior.  It means not providing any consequence (positive or negative) following a behavior. | The nonreinforcement of the child's tantrums eventually led to their cessation.  孩子发脾气没有得到强化，最终停止了这种行为。|Synonyms: extinction, nonreward|Antonyms: reinforcement, reward|noun
