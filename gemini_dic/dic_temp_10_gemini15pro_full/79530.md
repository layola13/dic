globuliferous:/ˌɡlɒbjʊˈlɪfərəs/
adj.
球状的；多球体的；由小球组成的
Bearing globules; composed of globules.  Resembling small globes or spheres.
例句：The scientist examined the globuliferous bacteria under the microscope.  科学家在显微镜下检查了球状细菌。

例句：The plant produced a globuliferous fruit, filled with tiny seeds. 这种植物结出球状的果实，里面充满了细小的种子。

近义词：globular, spherical, round, orbicular
反义词：amorphous, shapeless
词性：adjective


词源：From Latin globulus (“small globe”) + -ferous (“bearing”).
