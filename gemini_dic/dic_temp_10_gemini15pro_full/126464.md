mythicalnesses:/ˈmɪθɪkəlnəsɪz/
noun
神话性；神秘性；虚构性
The state or quality of being mythical.  The accumulated legendary qualities.
The mythicalnesses of ancient Greece captivated her.  她被古希腊的神话性所吸引。
Near Synonyms: mythicality, legend, fable, lore, mythology
Antonyms: reality, actuality, factuality
