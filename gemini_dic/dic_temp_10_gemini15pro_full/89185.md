homeownerships:/ˌhoʊmˈoʊnərʃɪps/
n.
房屋所有权，房产所有权
The state of owning a home or homes.  The collective ownership of homes by individuals.
Homeownerships in the area have increased in recent years. 近年来该地区的房屋所有权有所增加。
Homeownerships are often seen as a sign of financial stability. 房屋所有权通常被视为财务稳定的标志。
Synonyms: homeownership (singular often used), property ownership
Antonyms: tenancy, renting
