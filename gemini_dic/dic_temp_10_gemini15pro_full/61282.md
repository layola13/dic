embalmed:/ɪmˈbɑːmd/ |v. |防腐处理过的；经过防腐处理的|preserved a dead body from decay, usually by treating it with chemicals|The pharaoh was embalmed and placed in a sarcophagus. 法老被进行了防腐处理，并被安置在石棺中。|例句：His body was embalmed and laid to rest in the family tomb. 他的遗体经过防腐处理后安葬在家族墓地。

例句：The embalmed body of Lenin is still on display in Moscow. 列宁经过防腐处理的遗体仍在莫斯科展出。|近义词：preserved, mummified|反义词:decomposed, decayed|词性:Verb (past participle)
