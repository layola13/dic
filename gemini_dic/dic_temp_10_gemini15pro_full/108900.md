ledden:/ˈlɛdn/ | adj. | 沉重的，阴沉的，像铅的 |  (of the sky or clouds) dark grey, as if full of rain; (of a person's eyes or expression) dull and heavy, especially with sadness or exhaustion | The leaded sky hinted at impending rain.  铅灰色的天空预示着即将到来的雨。| synonyms: heavy, dull, gloomy, leaden, gray, overcast | antonyms: bright, cheerful, light | adjective

ledden:/ˈlɛdn/ | v. | 使沉重，使阴沉 | To make or become heavy, dull, or sluggish. |  Grief leaded her steps. 悲痛使她的脚步沉重。| synonyms: weigh down, burden, oppress | antonyms: lighten, enliven | verb
