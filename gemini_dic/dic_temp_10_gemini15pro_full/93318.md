ill-manneredly: /ˌɪlˈmænərdli/ | adv. | 粗鲁地，无礼地 | Showing a lack of good manners; impolitely. |  She ill-manneredly scoffed at his attempt to help. 她粗鲁地嘲笑他试图帮忙的举动。 | Synonyms:  impolitely, disrespectfully, rudely | Antonyms: politely, respectfully, graciously | 词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From ill- + mannered + -ly.  "Ill-" is a prefix meaning "bad" or "poorly." "Mannered" is an adjective meaning "having or showing a particular type of manners." "-ly" is a suffix that forms adverbs.
