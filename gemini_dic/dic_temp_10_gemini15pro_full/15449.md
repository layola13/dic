bad-temperedly: /ˌbædˈtɛmpədli/ | adv. | 脾气暴躁地，坏脾气地 | In a bad-tempered manner; angrily and irritably. | He slammed the door bad-temperedly.  他生气地摔门。| 近义词：angrily, irritably, grumpily, crossly | 反义词：good-naturedly, pleasantly, cheerfully | 词性: 副词

词源:  From "bad-tempered" + "-ly."  "Bad-tempered" comes from "bad" + "tempered."  "Temper" in this sense refers to a person's state of mind regarding being angry or calm.
