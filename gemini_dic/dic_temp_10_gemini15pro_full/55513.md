distressedly:/dɪˈstrɛsɪdli/
adv.
悲伤地，痛苦地
In a way that shows great worry and unhappiness.
She looked at him distressedly. 她悲伤地看着他。
Synonyms: anxiously, worriedly, nervously, apprehensively, despairingly, sorrowfully
Antonyms: calmly, peacefully, serenely, tranquilly
