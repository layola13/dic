nontemporarily:/ˌnɑːnˌtɛmpəˈrɛrəli/
adverb
非临时地；永久地
not temporarily; permanently.
The position was made nontemporarily to address the ongoing need.  这个职位被设为永久职位，以解决持续的需求。
The refugees were housed nontemporarily in the abandoned school.  难民被永久安置在废弃的学校里。
近义词: permanently, indefinitely
反义词: temporarily, briefly
词性: adverb

Etymology:
From non- + temporarily.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "temporarily" means "for a limited time."
