envelope:/ˈɛnvəˌloʊp/ (US), /ˈɒnvələʊp/ (UK)| n.|信封；封皮；包膜；外壳|A flat, usually paper container used for sending letters or documents through the mail, or for holding small items. |She sealed the letter in the envelope. 她把信封封好。|synonyms: wrapper, covering, sheath, case|antonyms: contents, interior|noun

envelope:/ɪnˈvɛləp/ (US), /ɪnˈvɛləp/ (UK)| vt.|包住；包围；遮盖|To wrap or cover something completely.|Fog enveloped the city. 雾笼罩着城市。|synonyms: surround, encase, engulf, shroud|antonyms: uncover, reveal, expose|verb
