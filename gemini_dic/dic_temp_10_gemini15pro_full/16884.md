basin:/ˈbeɪsn/| n.|盆；盆地；流域|A wide, round container used for holding liquids, especially water, for washing.|She filled the basin with warm water. 她往盆里倒满了温水。|近义词：bowl, sink, tub|反义词:|noun


basin:/ˈbeɪsn/| n.|盆地；流域|A large area of land where water from rivers flows into a lake or sea.|The Amazon basin is the largest rainforest in the world. 亚马逊盆地是世界上最大的雨林。|近义词：valley, depression|反义词:|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *bassin* (“basin”), from Vulgar Latin *baccinus*, diminutive of Latin *bacca* ("water vessel").
