haughtier:/ˈhɔːtiər/ | adj. | 更傲慢的，更自大的 |  The comparative form of haughty, meaning  arrogantly superior and disdainful.  It describes someone who acts as if they are more important or better than other people. | She became haughtier after receiving the promotion.  她升职后变得更加傲慢了。

He gave her a haughtier look than usual. 他给了她一个比平常更傲慢的眼神。 | 近义词: arroganter, prouder, more supercilious, more disdainful | 反义词: humbler, more modest, more unassuming | 词性: adjective
