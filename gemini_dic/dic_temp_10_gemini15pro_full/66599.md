exsiccate:/ˈɛksɪkeɪt/
verb
使干燥；脱水；烘干
To remove moisture from; dry up thoroughly.
The botanist exsiccated the plant specimens for preservation.  植物学家将植物标本烘干以保存。
近义词：dehydrate, desiccate, dry
反义词：hydrate, moisten
词性：v.

exsiccate:/ˈɛksɪkeɪt/
adjective
干燥的；脱水的
Dried up; desiccated.
The exsiccate landscape offered little sustenance for the animals. 干燥的景观几乎没有为动物提供食物。
近义词：arid, dry, parched
反义词：hydrated, moist, wet
词性：adj.


词源：From Latin exsiccātus, past participle of exsiccāre (“to dry up, drain”), from ex- (“out”) + siccāre (“to dry”).
