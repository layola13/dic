hagfish:/ˈhæɡfɪʃ/| n. |盲鰻|A primitive, eel-shaped marine fish without jaws, eyes, or fins, producing copious slime.|例句：The hagfish is a scavenger that feeds on dead or dying sea creatures. 盲鰻是一種食腐動物，以死亡或垂死的海洋生物為食。

例句：Hagfish are known for their ability to tie themselves in knots. 盲鰻以能將自己打結而聞名。|近义詞：slime eel|反义詞: (No direct antonym, could consider fish with developed features like tuna)|詞性:noun
