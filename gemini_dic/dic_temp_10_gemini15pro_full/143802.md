outorganizes:/ˌaʊtˈɔːrɡəˌnaɪzɪz/
verb
组织得比...更好
To organize something more effectively than someone or something else.
Our team outorganized the competition and won the championship.  我们的团队比对手组织得更好，赢得了冠军。
The new company quickly outorganized its older rivals. 这家新公司很快就比它那些老对手组织得更好了。
近义词: outmaneuver, surpass, outperform
反义词:  be outorganized, be disorganized
词性: verb
