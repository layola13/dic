diphthongizes:/dɪfˈθɒŋɡaɪzɪz/ /daɪfˈθɔŋɡaɪzɪz/
verb
使成双元音；变成双元音
changes a single vowel sound into a diphthong (a sound formed by the combination of two vowels in a single syllable, in which the sound begins as one vowel and moves toward another).
The Great Vowel Shift diphthongized many Middle English long vowels. 大元音推移使许多中古英语的长元音变成了双元音。
近义词: combines, blends
反义词: monophthongizes, separates
