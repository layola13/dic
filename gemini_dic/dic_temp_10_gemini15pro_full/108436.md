lax:/læks/
adjective
松的，松懈的，不严格的
not sufficiently strict, severe, or careful
The security at the airport was surprisingly lax. 机场安检出奇地松懈。

lax:/læks/
adjective
松弛的，不紧的
(of muscles or a part of the body) not tense or rigid; relaxed
His body went lax as he realized what had happened. 当他意识到发生了什么时，他的身体变得松弛了。


近义词:slack, loose, negligent, careless
反义词:strict, tight, firm, rigorous
词性:adjective
