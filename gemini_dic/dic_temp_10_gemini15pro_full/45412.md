cuppas:/ˈkʌpəz/| n.|几杯茶 (尤指英式英语非正式说法)|Cuppas is an informal, primarily British English, plural form of "cuppa," which itself is a shortened form of "cup of tea."|Fancy a few cuppas later?  待会儿想喝几杯茶吗？|Let's have some cuppas and a chat. 我们喝几杯茶，聊聊天吧。|近义词：cups of tea, teas|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 coffee 咖啡)|noun
