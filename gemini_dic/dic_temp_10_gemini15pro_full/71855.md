flesher:/ˈflɛʃər/ | n. |屠夫；剔骨工；去皮工人| A person who removes the skin or flesh from an animal carcass.  A person who prepares animal carcasses for market by removing skin, separating cuts, etc.|  The flesher skillfully removed the hide from the deer.  剔骨工熟练地剥下了鹿皮。| synonyms: butcher, meat cutter, slaughterer | antonyms: vegetarian, vegan | noun
