outmouthing: /ˌaʊtˈmaʊðɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的言辞;说得比…更大声;说得比…更流利
to surpass someone in loudness or fluency of speech; to outdo someone in a verbal contest.
He outmouthed his opponent in the debate, leaving them speechless. 他在辩论中以雄辩的言辞压倒了对手，让他们哑口无言。
The salesman outmouthed all the other competitors and secured the deal.  这位销售员说得比所有其他竞争对手都更流利，从而 secured the deal.
outspeak, outtalk, overpower
