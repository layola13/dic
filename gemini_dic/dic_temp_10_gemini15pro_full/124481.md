moulded:/ˈməʊldɪd/ (UK) /ˈmoʊldɪd/ (US)| adj.|模压的，塑造的|Shaped or formed in a mould; influenced or developed.|The toy soldiers were moulded from plastic. 这些玩具士兵是用塑料模压的。|Synonyms: shaped, formed, cast, fashioned|Antonyms: unformed, natural, shapeless|adjective

mould:/məʊld/ (UK) /moʊld/ (US)| n. v.|模具，霉菌；塑造|A hollow container used to give shape to molten or hot liquid material when it cools and hardens; a furry growth of minute fungal hyphae forming on the surface of organic matter, typically with a stale odour and flavour; to shape or form something.|She poured the jelly into a mould. 她把果冻倒入模具。  He was moulded by his experiences. 他被他的经历塑造。|Synonyms: cast, form, shape, matrix (n.); shape, form, fashion (v.)|Antonyms: deform, distort (v.)|noun, verb
