faust:/faʊst/| n.|浮士德，魔鬼，(Faust)歌剧名|A scholar who sells his soul to the devil in exchange for knowledge and power, based on the legendary figure of Johann Georg Faust.  Refers to the protagonist of Goethe's famous tragedy *Faust*.|He was fascinated by the story of Faust. 他被浮士德的故事迷住了。|近义词:mephistopheles, fiend, devil|反义词:saint, angel|noun
