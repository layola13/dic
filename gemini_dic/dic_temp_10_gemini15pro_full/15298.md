backpacked:/ˈbækˌpækt/ | v. | 背包旅行 |  Travelled or went camping with a backpack. | Example 1: We backpacked through Europe last summer. 我们去年夏天背包游览了欧洲。

Example 2:  They backpacked in the Himalayas for a month. 他们在喜马拉雅山背包旅行了一个月。 | 近义词: hiked, trekked, camped | 反义词: stayed (in a hotel), toured (with luggage) | 词性: 动词 (past tense of "backpack")


backpacking:/ˈbækˌpækɪŋ/ | n. | 背包旅行 | The activity of travelling or camping with a backpack. | Example 1: Backpacking is a great way to see the world. 背包旅行是游览世界的好方式。

Example 2: She enjoys backpacking in remote areas. 她喜欢在偏远地区背包旅行。| 近义词: hiking, trekking, camping | 反义词: staying (in a hotel), touring (with luggage) | 词性: 名词


词源:  "Backpack" comes from the German word "Rucksack" (literally "back sack"). The English term "backpack" gained popularity in the mid-20th century.
