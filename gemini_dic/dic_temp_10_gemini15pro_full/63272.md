enthetic:/ɛnˈθɛtɪk/
adjective
附着性的；起于附着点炎症的
Relating to or denoting the site of attachment of a muscle, tendon, or ligament to bone or cartilage.  Inflammation at such a site.
Example: Enthetic inflammation is common in athletes who overuse certain joints.  附着点炎症在过度使用某些关节的运动员中很常见。
Example:  The doctor diagnosed her pain as enthetic-related, stemming from the attachment of her Achilles tendon. 医生诊断她的疼痛与附着点有关，源于她的跟腱附着处。
Synonym:  insertional
Antonym: N/A (No direct antonym exists)
词性: adjective
