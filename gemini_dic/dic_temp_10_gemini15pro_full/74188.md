fourdrinier:/ˌfɔːrdrɪˈnɪər/ | n. | 长网造纸机;  | A machine with a continuously moving wire mesh belt on which a web of paper is formed from a pulp suspension. | The fourdrinier machine revolutionized paper production. 长网造纸机彻底改变了造纸生产。| The paper mill uses a modern fourdrinier for high-speed production. 这家造纸厂使用现代化的长网造纸机进行高速生产。 | synonyms: paper machine | antonyms: N/A | noun
