grubbing:/ˈɡrʌbɪŋ/| n. v. |挖，翻找，觅食|The act of digging or searching, especially for food.|例句：The pigs were grubbing for roots in the forest. 猪在森林里拱土觅食。

例句：He was grubbing in the dirt for his lost key. 他在泥土里翻找丢失的钥匙。|近义词：digging, foraging, rummaging, searching|反义词:planting, sowing|词性:名词, 动词

grubbing:/ˈɡrʌbɪŋ/| adj. |卑微的，辛苦的|Characterized by digging or searching, often implying tedious or menial work.|例句：Grubbing for a living can be exhausting.  为了生计而辛苦劳作令人筋疲力尽。

例句：She found the grubbing work of cleaning the stables unpleasant. 她觉得打扫马厩的辛苦工作令人不愉快。|近义词：laborious, tedious, menial|反义词:easy, pleasant|词性:形容词
