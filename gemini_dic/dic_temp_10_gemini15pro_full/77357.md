gaudygreen:/ˈɡɔːdi ɡriːn/
adjective
俗丽的绿色; 花哨的绿色
A shade of green that is considered excessively bright, showy, or tasteless.  It implies a negative connotation, suggesting a lack of sophistication or elegance.
The clown wore a gaudygreen suit.  那个小丑穿着一套俗丽的绿色西装。
近义词:garish green, loud green, flashy green
反义词:subtle green, muted green, understated green
词性:adjective

gaudy:/ˈɡɔːdi/
adjective
俗丽的; 花哨的
Extravagantly bright or showy, typically so as to be tasteless.
She wore a gaudy dress to the party. 她穿着俗丽的衣服去参加了聚会。
近义词:garish, loud, flashy
反义词:subtle, understated, elegant
词性:adjective

green:/ɡriːn/
adjective, noun
绿色的; 绿色;  
Of the color between blue and yellow in the spectrum; colored like grass or emeralds. Also, can refer to the color itself, or things that are green (e.g., vegetation).
The grass is green. 草是绿色的。
近义词:emerald, verdant, olive (depending on shade)
反义词: (no single direct antonym for color, depends on specific shade)
