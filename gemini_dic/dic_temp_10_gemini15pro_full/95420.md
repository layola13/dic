incidentals:/ˌɪnsɪˈdentlz/ | n. |杂费，杂项费用| Minor expenses that are incidental to a larger cost, such as travel or accommodation. |例句：Remember to budget for incidentals like tips and snacks. 记住把小费和零食等杂费编入预算。

例句：The company will reimburse you for all travel incidentals. 公司会报销你所有的差旅杂费。|近义词：miscellaneous expenses, sundry expenses, extras |反义词: main expenses, major costs |词性:noun
