oreographic: /ˌɔːrɪəˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. | 山岳学的，地形学的 | Relating to orographic rainfall, which is rain caused by the forced ascent of air over hills or mountains. |  The oreographic effect played a significant role in the region's high precipitation levels.  该地区的高降水量与地形效应密切相关。

The oreographic lift caused the clouds to release their moisture as rain. 地形抬升导致云层以降雨的形式释放水分。

| 近义词: mountainous, upland | 反义词: lowland, flat | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Greek *oros* "mountain" + *-graphikos* "-graphic."
