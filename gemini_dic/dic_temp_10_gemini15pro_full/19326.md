biblicism:/ˈbɪblɪsɪzəm/ | n. |圣经用语；圣经主义|A phrase, expression, or style of writing that is characteristic of the Bible or imitates it. |例句： "A land flowing with milk and honey" is a common biblicism.  “流淌着牛奶和蜂蜜的土地”是一个常见的圣经用语。

例句：His speech was full of biblicisms. 他的演讲充满了圣经用语。|近义词：archaism, biblical expression |反义词:modernism, neologism |词性:noun
