fatuously:/ˈfætʃuəsli/ | adv. | 愚蠢地，傻乎乎地 | In a foolish or silly manner; complacently or foolishly stupid. | He fatuously believed he could fly. 他愚蠢地相信自己能飞。 | synonym: foolishly, stupidly, idiotically, absurdly, senselessly | antonym: wisely, intelligently, sensibly, judiciously | 词性: 副词

fatuous:/ˈfætʃuəs/ | adj. | 愚蠢的，傻的 | Silly and pointless. |  He made a fatuous remark about the weather. 他对天气做了一个愚蠢的评论。 | synonym: silly, foolish, stupid, inane, asinine | antonym: intelligent, sensible, wise, smart | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *fatuus* "foolish, silly, inspired by a deity," originally "prophetic," from *fari* "to speak"
