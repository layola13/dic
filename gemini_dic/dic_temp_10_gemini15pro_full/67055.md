explicates:/ˈɛksplɪkeɪts/| v. |解释；说明；阐明| to explain something in detail, especially a piece of writing or an idea | The professor explicates the poem line by line. 教授逐行解释这首诗。| synonyms: explain, clarify, interpret, elucidate | antonyms: confuse, obscure | verb

explicate:/ˈɛksplɪkət/| adj. |详尽的；清楚的；明白的| clearly and fully expressed; leaving nothing implied |  His instructions were explicit; there was no room for misunderstanding. 他的指示很明确，没有误解的余地。| synonyms: clear, precise, definite, unambiguous | antonyms: implicit, vague, ambiguous | adjective
