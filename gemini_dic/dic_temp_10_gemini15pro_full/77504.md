gazillions:/ɡəˈzɪljənz/| n.|无数，极多，数不尽的|An informal term indicating a very large, indeterminate number.|There were gazillions of mosquitoes at the lake last night. 昨晚湖边有数不清的蚊子。|Synonyms: countless, innumerable, myriad, a lot of, tons of|Antonyms: few, little|Noun
