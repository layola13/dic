nonmusical:/ˌnɑːnˈmjuːzɪkl/
adjective
非音乐的；不懂音乐的
Not related to or skilled in music.
She has a nonmusical ear and can't tell if someone is singing off-key. 她没有音乐方面的才能，听不出有人唱歌跑调。
