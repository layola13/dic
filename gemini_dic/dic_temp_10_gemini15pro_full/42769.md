countryship: /ˈkʌntriʃɪp/
n.
乡村风情；乡村生活；热爱乡村
A love or appreciation for rural life and its customs.
She felt a deep countryship, preferring the quiet of the fields to the bustle of the city. 她深爱乡村生活，喜欢田野的宁静胜过城市的喧嚣。
近义词:rurality, rusticity
反义词:urbanity
