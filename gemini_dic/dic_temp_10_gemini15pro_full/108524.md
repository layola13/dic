laywomen:/ˈleɪˌwɪmɪn/
noun
外行女性；俗女；非专业人士（尤指女性）
A woman who is not trained or ordained as a member of the clergy, or who does not have expert knowledge of a particular subject.
例句：The medical jargon was incomprehensible to the laywomen.  这些医学术语对外行女性来说难以理解。

例句：The laywomen on the jury struggled to understand the complex legal arguments. 陪审团中的外行女性难以理解复杂的法律论证。

近义词：nonprofessional, amateur, non-expert
反义词：professional, expert, specialist
词性：n.
