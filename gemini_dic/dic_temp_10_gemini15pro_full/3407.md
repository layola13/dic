afoot:/əˈfʊt/| adj.|进行中的；徒步的；策划中的| Happening or in progress; on foot; planned or underway. |A plan is afoot to build a new shopping mall. 一个建造新购物中心的计划正在进行中。|近义词：underway, in progress, ongoing, developing, brewing, hatching, planned, contemplated|反义词：dormant, inactive, stopped, completed, finished|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From Middle English *on fote*, from Old English *on fōte* (“on foot”), from *on* (“on”) + *fōt* (“foot”). Cognate with Dutch *te voet* (“on foot”), German *zu Fuß* (“on foot”). The single word *afoot* arose from the unstressed pronunciation of *on* in the phrase *on foot*.
