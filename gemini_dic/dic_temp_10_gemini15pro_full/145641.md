overcontentedness:/ˌoʊvərˌkənˈtɛntɪdnəs/ | n. | 过度满足，过分知足 | A state of being excessively content or satisfied, often to the point of complacency. | He exhibited an overcontentedness that bordered on laziness. 他表现出一种近乎懒惰的过度满足感。 |  The cat, basking in the sun, displayed a perfect picture of overcontentedness.  晒着太阳的猫，展现出一幅完美满足的画面。 | 近义词: complacency, satisfaction, smugness | 反义词: dissatisfaction, discontent, ambition | 词性: 名词

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "contentedness" (noun form of "content," from Latin "contentus," past participle of "continere" meaning "to contain, hold together")
