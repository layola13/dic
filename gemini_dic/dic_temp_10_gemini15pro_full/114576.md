malaysia:/məˈleɪʒə/ | n. |马来西亚|A country in Southeast Asia, occupying part of the Malay Peninsula and the northern part of the island of Borneo.|Malaysia is a popular tourist destination. 马来西亚是一个受欢迎的旅游目的地。|近义词: (地理位置上) None, as it's a proper noun |反义词: None, as it's a proper noun |词性: 名词


Etymology: The name "Malaysia" is a combination of "Malay" and the Sanskrit suffix "-sia" or "-siya," meaning "land" or "country."  The term "Malay" itself is of uncertain origin, with various theories proposed.
