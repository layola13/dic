clogging:/ˈklɑːɡɪŋ/ /ˈklɒɡɪŋ/| n. v.|阻塞，堵塞；障碍物|The clogging of the arteries can lead to heart disease. 动脉堵塞会导致心脏病。|近义词：blocking, obstructing, jamming|反义词:clearing, opening|noun

clogging:/ˈklɑːɡɪŋ/ /ˈklɒɡɪŋ/| v.|(使)阻塞，堵塞|The drain is clogged with hair. 排水管被头发堵塞了。|近义词：block, obstruct, jam|反义词:clear, open|verb

词源: From clog + -ing.  Clog itself comes from Middle English clogge, probably of Scandinavian origin.
