boring:/ˈbɔːrɪŋ/ (UK), /ˈbɔːrɪŋ/ (US)| adj.|无聊的，乏味的|Causing weariness and restlessness through lack of interest; tedious.|The lecture was so boring that I almost fell asleep.  这讲座太无聊了，我差点睡着了。|Synonyms: tedious, dull, monotonous, uninteresting|Antonyms: interesting, exciting, stimulating, engaging|Adjective
