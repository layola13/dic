what:/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US)|pron. det. adv.|什么，多么|Used to ask for information about something or someone.|例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

例句：What a beautiful day! 多么美好的一天！|近义词：which|反义词: (无直接反义词，根据语境可考虑 none, nothing 等)|pronoun

what:/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US)|det.|什么|Used to ask for information specifying something.|例句：What book are you reading? 你在读什么书？

例句：What color is your car? 你的车是什么颜色的？|近义词：which|反义词: (无直接反义词)|determiner

what:/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US)|adv.|多么|Used to emphasize the degree of something.|例句：What a lovely surprise! 多么可爱的惊喜！

例句：What a shame! 多么遗憾！|近义词：how|反义词: (无直接反义词)|adverb


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you do for a living? = 你靠什么谋生？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? = 他在花园里做什么？


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? = 她用这笔钱做了什么？


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you eat for lunch? = 你午饭吃了什么？
What did she say about the movie? = 她对这部电影说了些什么？


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? = 我看到她时她在想什么？


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? = 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? = 她生病前吃了什么？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I called? = 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 她转专业前一直在学什么？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will she bring to the party? = 她会带什么来参加聚会？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be studying next semester? = 她下学期会学什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the age of 30? = 到30岁时你会取得什么成就？
What will she have finished by the end of the day? = 到今天结束时她会完成什么？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？
