darkest:/ˈdɑːrkɪst/| adj.|最黑暗的，最阴暗的|Superlative form of dark: most dark.  It refers to the highest degree of darkness, either literally or figuratively.|The darkest hour is just before the dawn.  黎明前的时刻是最黑暗的。|Near Synonyms: blackest, dimmest, gloomiest|Antonyms: brightest, lightest, most luminous|adjective
