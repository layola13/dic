muddiest:/ˈmʌdiɪst/| adj.|最泥泞的|Covered with the most mud; dirtiest.|The trail was muddiest after the rainstorm.  这条小路在暴雨后变得最泥泞。|例句：This is the muddiest path I've ever seen. 这是我见过的最泥泞的小路。|近义词：dirtiest, swampiest, muckiest|反义词:cleanest, driest|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From mud + -y + -est.  "Mud" comes from Middle Dutch "modde".  "-y" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "full of".  "-est" is a suffix forming the superlative degree of adjectives.
