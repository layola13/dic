harsh:/hɑːrʃ/ (US) /hɑːʃ/ (UK)| adj.|刺耳的，严厉的，残酷的，粗糙的，恶劣的|Unpleasantly rough or jarring to the senses; severe, cruel, or demanding; unpleasantly coarse and rough to the touch; difficult and unpleasant to live in.|例句：The harsh winter left many animals dead. 严冬导致许多动物死亡。

例句：The judge gave him a harsh sentence. 法官对他处以严厉的判决。|近义词：severe, strict, rigorous, austere, cruel, unkind, ruthless, brutal, callous, unfeeling, rough, jarring, grating, strident, discordant, unpleasant, disagreeable, austere, grim, bleak, desolate|反义词：gentle, kind, lenient, mild, soft, smooth, pleasant, agreeable|词性:形容词
