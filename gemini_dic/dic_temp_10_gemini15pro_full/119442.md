microlinguistic:/ˌmaɪkroʊlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk/| adj.|微观语言学的|Relating to the study of language as a system of rules and patterns, focusing on individual elements like sounds, words, and grammar, rather than broader social or cultural contexts.|Microlinguistic analysis focuses on the specific grammatical structures used in a text. 微观语言学分析侧重于文本中使用的特定语法结构。|Microlinguistic variation can be observed in the different dialects of a language.  微观语言学的变化可以在一种语言的不同方言中观察到。|近义词：grammatical, structural|反义词：macrolinguistic|词性: adjective
