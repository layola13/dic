magnetify:/ˈmæɡnɪtaɪ/| v. |磁化，使具有磁性|To make something magnetic; to attract strongly, as if with a magnet.|例句：The iron filings were magnetized by the bar magnet. 铁屑被条形磁铁磁化了。

例句：His charisma magnetized everyone in the room. 他的魅力吸引了房间里的每一个人。|近义词：attract, mesmerize, influence|反义词：demagnetize|词性：动词

magnetize:/ˈmæɡnɪtaɪz/| v. |磁化，使具有磁性|To make something magnetic; to attract strongly, as if with a magnet.|例句：The process magnetizes the steel bar. 这个过程使钢条磁化。


例句：Her beauty magnetized all the men. 她的美貌吸引了所有的男人。|近义词：attract, mesmerize, influence|反义词：demagnetize|词性：动词
