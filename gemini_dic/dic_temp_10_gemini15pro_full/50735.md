derogatory:/dɪˈrɒɡət(ə)ri/ /dɪˈrɑːɡətɔːri/
adj.
贬低的，贬损的
showing a critical or disrespectful attitude.
例句：He made some derogatory remarks about her appearance. 他对她的外貌做了一些贬低的评论。

例句：Using derogatory language towards others is unacceptable. 对他人使用贬损的语言是不可接受的。

近义词：disparaging, belittling, insulting, offensive, pejorative
反义词：complimentary, flattering, praising, laudatory
词性：adjective

词源：Late Middle English: from late Latin *derogatorius, from Latin derogare ‘repeal (part of a law),’ from de- ‘away, down’ + rogare ‘ask.’
