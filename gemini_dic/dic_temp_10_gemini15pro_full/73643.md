forfended:/fɔːrˈfɛndɪd/
| adj. |被禁止的，严禁的|Prohibited or forbidden, especially by law or other authority.|Entry to the sacred site is forfended to all but the priests.  进入圣地除祭司外，所有人均被禁止。|The sale of alcohol is forfended to minors. 向未成年人出售酒类是被禁止的。|近义词：forbidden, prohibited, proscribed, banned|反义词：permitted, allowed, authorized|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *forfenden, from Old English *forfēndan (unattested), from for- + fēndan (“to find, provide, support”), ultimately from Proto-Germanic *fanþijaną (“to find, provide”).  The meaning shifted from "to ward off" to "to prohibit."
