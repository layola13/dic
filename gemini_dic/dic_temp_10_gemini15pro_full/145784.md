overdazzling: /ˌoʊvərˈdæzəlɪŋ/
adjective
令人眼花缭乱的；耀眼过度的
Too bright or showy, so as to cause someone to be unable to see clearly or to think clearly.
The overdazzling headlights of the oncoming car made it difficult to see.
迎面而来的汽车耀眼的前灯使人难以看清道路。
The overdazzling display of wealth made some guests uncomfortable.
过于炫耀的财富展示让一些客人感到不舒服。
synonyms: blinding, glaring, dazzling, overwhelming
antonyms: subdued, muted, understated
词性: 形容词
