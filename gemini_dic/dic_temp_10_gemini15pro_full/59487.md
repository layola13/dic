east: /iːst/ | n. adj. adv.|东方，东方的，向东方|The sun rises in the east. 太阳从东方升起。|近义词：oriental, eastern|反义词: west|词性:名词,形容词,副词
Etymology: From Old English *ēast, from Proto-Germanic *austrą, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ews- (“to shine,” “dawn”). Cognate with Dutch oost, German Ost, Gothic austeins.  The original meaning referred to the direction of the rising sun.
