begums:/ˈbeɪɡəmz/ | n. | 女王，穆斯林贵族妇女 | A term of respect for a Muslim woman of high rank, especially a princess or a wife of a high official. |  The begums lived in opulent palaces. 女王们住在富丽堂皇的宫殿里。|  The begums were known for their generosity. 这些女王以慷慨大方而闻名。 | synonyms: princesses, ladies, noblewomen | antonyms: commoners | noun
