fidgetingly:/ˈfɪdʒɪtɪŋli/| adv.|不安地，烦躁地|In a restless or nervous manner, characterized by fidgeting.|She tapped her foot fidgetingly throughout the meeting. 她整个会议期间都烦躁地敲着脚。|近义词：restlessly, nervously, impatiently|反义词：calmly, patiently|词性: 副词

fidget:/ˈfɪdʒɪt/| n. vi.|烦躁不安，坐立不安|A state of restlessness or unease.|The children began to fidget after sitting still for so long. 孩子们静静地坐了这么久后开始坐立不安了。|近义词：restlessness, unease|反义词：calm, stillness|词性: 名词，动词

词源：From the earlier *fike*, of unknown origin. Possibly related to *fit*, in the sense of a sudden, brief spell of activity.
