kopek:/ˈkoʊpɛk/| n.|戈比（俄罗斯货币单位）|A kopek is a unit of currency in Russia, equal to one hundredth of a ruble. |例句：The price was 50 kopeks. 价格是50戈比。|近义词:cent, penny|反义词:ruble (in a relative sense, as the kopek is a subdivision of the ruble)|noun
