anticommercially:/ˌænti kəˈmɜːrʃəli/
adverb|反商业地，非商业性地|In a way that opposes or avoids commercial principles or practices.|He lived a simple, almost anticommercially life, rejecting materialism and consumerism.|Synonyms: uncommercially, noncommercially|Antonyms: commercially|词性:副词
