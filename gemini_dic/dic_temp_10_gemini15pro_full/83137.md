gutlessnesses:/ˈɡʌtləsnəsəz/
n.
缺乏勇气；怯懦；胆怯
The state or quality of lacking courage; cowardice.
His gutlessnesses prevented him from speaking up. 他的怯懦使他不敢大声说话。

His repeated gutlessnesses in the face of injustice angered his friends.  他在面对不公时的再三胆怯激怒了他的朋友。

近义词:cowardice, timidity, pusillanimity, spinelessness, faintheartedness
反义词:courage, bravery, boldness, valor, daring
词性:名词
