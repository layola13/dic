bakkie:/ˈbæki/| n.|小货车，皮卡|A small truck with an open back, often used for transporting goods or people. Primarily used in South Africa. |例句：He loaded the furniture into the bakkie.  他把家具装进了小货车。

例句：We took the bakkie to the beach for a picnic. 我们开着小货车去海滩野餐。|近义词：pickup truck, ute (Australia), truck|反义词: sedan, car|词性: noun
