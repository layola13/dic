muffle up:/ˈmʌfəl ʌp/
verb
裹得严严实实；捂得严严实实
to wrap or cover someone or something warmly, especially in order to protect them from cold weather
Example:  Muffle up well - it's freezing outside.  裹得严严实实的——外面冷极了。

Example:  She muffled the baby up in a blanket. 她用毯子把婴儿裹得严严实实的。
近义词:wrap up, bundle up, cover up
反义词:uncover, expose
词性:verb (phrasal verb)
