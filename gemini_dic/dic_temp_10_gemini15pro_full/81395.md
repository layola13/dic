grasscutter:/ˈɡræsˌkʌtər/ | n. | 草食动物; 割草机 | An animal that feeds on grass; a machine used for cutting grass. | The grasscutter quickly devoured the fresh blades of grass. 这只草食动物迅速地吃掉了新鲜的草叶。

The farmer used a grasscutter to trim his lawn. 农民用割草机修剪他的草坪。 | 近义词：(animal) herbivore, grazer; (machine) lawnmower | 反义词: (animal) carnivore; (machine) N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "grass" + "cutter".  "Grass" comes from Old English "græs".  "Cutter" comes from "cut" (Old English "cytan").  The combined term simply describes either an animal that cuts (eats) grass, or a machine designed to cut grass.
