consonant:/ˈkɑːnsənənt/ | n. adj.|辅音；辅音的|A speech sound that is not a vowel.  A letter representing a consonant.|The word "strength" begins with a consonant blend.  这个单词“strength”以辅音连缀开头。|The final consonant is doubled before adding -ing.  在添加-ing之前，最后的辅音要双写。|近义词:non-vowel|反义词:vowel|词性:noun, adjective
