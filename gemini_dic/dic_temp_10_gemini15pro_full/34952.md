clocking-out:/ˈklɒkɪŋ aʊt/ (US) /ˈklɒkɪŋ aʊt/ (UK)| n./gerund |打卡下班|The act of recording the time one finishes work, typically by using a time clock or electronic system.  It signifies the end of a work period. |I'm clocking-out early today. 我今天早点下班。|Synonyms: checking out, signing out, punching out|Antonyms: clocking-in, checking in, signing in|noun/gerund

clocking-out:/ˈklɒkɪŋ aʊt/ (US) /ˈklɒkɪŋ aʊt/ (UK)|  | 打卡下班 | Derived from "clock in" + "out". "Clock" referring to a time clock and "out" indicating departure from work.  Originally referred to the physical act of punching a timecard. Now often used even with electronic systems. |例句：She was clocking-out when the fire alarm went off. 她正在打卡下班时，火警响了。|近义词：checking out, signing out|反义词: clocking-in|gerund
