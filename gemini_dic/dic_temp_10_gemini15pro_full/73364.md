foregoer:/fɔːrˈɡoʊər/
n.
先行者，先驱
Someone or something that precedes another in time; a predecessor.
The Wright brothers were the foregoers of modern aviation.  莱特兄弟是现代航空的先驱。
predecessor, precursor, ancestor, forerunner
successor, descendant
noun
