hypobranchial:/ˌhaɪpəˈbræŋkiəl/
adjective|舌下的，舌骨下的|Situated below the gills or branchiae; pertaining to the lower elements of the branchial arch. |The hypobranchial muscles play a crucial role in the feeding mechanism of fish.  鱼类的舌骨下肌在其摄食机制中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词: subbranchial|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "epibranchial" - above the gills)|词性:adjective


hypobranchial:/ˌhaɪpəˈbræŋkiəl/
noun|舌下骨，舌骨下部|A bone or cartilage element lying below the gills or branchiae, often part of the hyoid apparatus. |The hypobranchial is a key component of the fish's jaw structure. 舌下骨是鱼类颌骨结构的关键组成部分。|近义词: subbranchial element|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "epibranchial element")|词性:noun
