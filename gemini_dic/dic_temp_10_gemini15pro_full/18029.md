beglerbeg:/ˈbɛɡlərˌbɛɡ/
n.
Provincial governor; a high-ranking official in the Ottoman Empire.
A beglerbeg was a governor of a province (beylerbeylik) and commander of its military forces in the Ottoman Empire.
The beglerbeg of Rumelia held considerable power.  鲁米利亚的贝格勒贝格掌握着相当大的权力。
synonyms: governor, pasha, viceroy
antonyms: subject, commoner
noun
