crees:/kriːz/| n.|克里人（加拿大原住民）|A group of indigenous people who live in central Canada. They are one of the largest First Nations groups in North America.|The crees have a rich history and culture. 克里人有着丰富的历史和文化。|近义词：Cree Nation, First Nations people|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

crees:/kriːz/| v. (third-person singular simple present *crees*, present participle *creeing*, simple past and past participle *creed*)| 爬行，缓慢移动（通常指动物或昆虫）|To move slowly and quietly, close to the ground.|The snake crees through the grass. 蛇在草丛中爬行。|近义词：crawl, slither|反义词: run, sprint|词性:动词
