americanised:/əˈmɛrɪkənaɪzd/
adjective
美式化的
Made or changed to be like something from the United States, or to suit the taste of people in the United States.
Many British TV shows are Americanised before they are shown in the US. 许多英国电视节目在进入美国市场前都进行了美式化改造。

americanised:/əˈmɛrɪkənaɪzd/
adjective
美国化的
Influenced by American culture or values.
The restaurant offered an Americanised version of Italian food. 这家餐厅提供美式意大利菜。


近义词:westernized, Americanized
反义词:traditional, indigenous
词性:adjective
