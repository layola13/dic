murram:/ˈmʌrəm/ | n. |红土，风化土，砖红壤|A hard reddish soil, often used for road surfacing in East Africa. | Example: The road was made of murram and became very dusty in the dry season. 这条路是用红土铺成的，旱季尘土飞扬。

Example:  They used murram to build the foundation of the house. 他们用红土建造房屋的地基。|Synonym: laterite, gravel, hardpan|Antonym: topsoil, clay|noun
