darkrooms:/ˈdɑːkruːmz/
n.
暗室，暗房
A room where photographic film can be developed and printed, kept dark to prevent exposure to light.
Photographers used to spend hours in darkrooms, developing their pictures. 摄影师过去常常在暗室里待上几个小时冲洗照片。

darkrooms:/ˈdɑːkruːmz/
n.
(比喻)秘密进行的场所或活动
The deal was negotiated in the darkrooms of political intrigue. 这笔交易是在政治阴谋的暗室里谈判达成的。

darkrooms:/ˈdɑːkruːmz/
n.
(旧)用于医学治疗的光线昏暗的房间
In the past, darkrooms were sometimes used to treat certain skin conditions. 过去，暗室有时被用来治疗某些皮肤病。
