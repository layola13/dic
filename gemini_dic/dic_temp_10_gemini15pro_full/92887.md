idealizations:/ˌaɪdiːələˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. |理想化；理想化的东西|The process of regarding or representing something as perfect or better than in reality.  The product of such a process.|His account of their relationship was an idealization, omitting all the arguments they had. 他对他们关系的描述是理想化的，省略了他们所有的争论。|Synonyms: romanticization, glorification, exaltation, idolization|Antonyms:  vilification, denigration, disparagement|noun
