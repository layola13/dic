martyrish:/ˈmɑːrtərɪʃ/| adj.|殉道似的，装作殉道者的|Having or showing qualities of a martyr, especially an exaggerated or affected piety or resignation.|She gave him a martyrish look when he asked her to do the dishes. 当他让她洗碗时，她给了他一个殉道者的眼神。|近义词：self-sacrificing, long-suffering, resigned|反义词：selfish, complaining, rebellious|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From martyr + -ish.  "Martyr" comes from the Greek word "μάρτυς" (mártys), meaning "witness".  The suffix "-ish" indicates resembling or characteristic of.
