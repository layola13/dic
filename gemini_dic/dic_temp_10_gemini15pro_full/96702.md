ineffably:/ɪˈnɛfəbli/ | adv. | 不可言喻地，难以形容地 | Too great or extreme to be expressed or described in words. | The beauty of the sunset was ineffably sublime. 日落的美丽难以言喻地壮丽。 |  The music was ineffably beautiful.  这音乐美得难以言喻。 | 近义词：indescribably, unspeakably, unutterably | 反义词：describably, expressibly | 词性: 副词

ineffable:/ɪˈnɛfəbəl/ | adj. | 不可言喻的，难以形容的 | Too great or extreme to be expressed or described in words. |  She felt an ineffable sense of peace. 她感到一种难以言喻的平静。 | The Grand Canyon is an ineffable natural wonder. 大峡谷是一个难以形容的自然奇观。 | 近义词：indescribable, unspeakable, unutterably | 反义词：describable, expressible | 词性: 形容词


词源: Late Middle English: from Old French ineffable, or from late Latin ineffabilis, from in- ‘not’ + effabilis (from effari ‘utter’).
