cockswain:/ˈkɒksən/ (UK), /ˈkɑːksən/ (US)| n.|舵手，艇长|A person who steers a boat, especially the helmsman of a racing crew.|The cockswain steered the boat expertly through the rough waters.  舵手熟练地驾驶着船只穿过汹涌的海面。|Synonyms: helmsman, steersman|Antonyms: crew, passenger|noun
