luller:/ˈlʌlər/| n.| 催眠曲；使人平静的事物；间歇期 |something that lulls or soothes; a period of calm or inactivity|The gentle rocking of the boat was a luller. 船的轻柔摇晃令人昏昏欲睡。|Synonyms:  sedative, tranquilizer, soother, pacifier, respite, break, pause |Antonyms:  stimulant, irritant, agitator|noun
lull:/lʌl/| vt. |使平静；使安静；哄骗；缓和 |to calm or soothe; to deceive into a false sense of security; to cause to become less active|The sound of the waves lulled me to sleep. 海浪声使我昏昏欲睡。|Synonyms: soothe, calm, tranquilize, pacify, quiet, subdue, hush, lull somebody into something|Antonyms:  rouse, agitate, excite, stimulate|verb
lull:/lʌl/| n. |平静时期；间歇 |a temporary period of quiet or inactivity|There was a lull in the storm. 暴风雨中出现了短暂的平静。|Synonyms: pause, break, respite, intermission, abeyance|Antonyms:  upsurge, increase, intensification|noun
