conditionals:/kənˈdɪʃənəlz/ | n. | 条件句；条件式 |Clauses in grammar that express a condition and its consequences.  These often involve "if" or "unless". | If it rains tomorrow, we will stay home. 如果明天下雨，我们将待在家里。| synonyms: provisions, stipulations | antonyms: absolutes, certainties | noun
