night-clubbed:/ˈnaɪtˌklʌbd/
adj.
夜总会泡的;常去夜总会的
Frequenting nightclubs; dissipated.
He had a rather night-clubbed look about him. 他看上去一副常泡夜总会的样子。
Synonyms: dissipated, decadent, dissolute
Antonyms: wholesome, upright, virtuous
词性:形容词


night-clubbed:/ˈnaɪtˌklʌbd/
v.
去夜总会;常去夜总会(night-club的过去式和过去分词)
Went to nightclubs, especially habitually.
They night-clubbed every weekend. 他们每个周末都去夜总会。
Synonyms: frequented (nightclubs), caroused
Antonyms: stayed home, abstained
词性:动词
