acrotarsium: /ˌækrəˈtɑːrsiəm/
noun
顶端分生组织; 顶端组织
The acrotarsium is the growing point at the apex of a moss sporophyte.
顶端分生组织是苔藓孢子体顶端的生长点。
This study examines the development of the acrotarsium in various moss species. 这项研究 بررسی了各种苔藓物种中顶端分生组织的发育。
近义词: apical meristem, apex
反义词: base
词性: n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ákros* (topmost, outermost) + *tarsós* (flat of the foot, instep).  The term likely refers to the flattened, foot-like shape of the growing tip in some mosses.
