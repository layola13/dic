emigrating:/ˈemɪɡreɪtɪŋ/
verb
移居国外
leaving one's own country to settle permanently in another
Many people are emigrating from the country due to the economic crisis. 由于经济危机，许多人正移居国外。
近义词: migrating, relocating, departing
反义词: immigrating, returning
词性: verb
