concusses:/kənˈkʌsɪz/
verb
使脑震荡，使震动
To injure the brain by a violent blow, causing temporary loss of consciousness, etc.
The boxer concussed his opponent with a powerful uppercut.  拳击手一记强劲的上勾拳把对手打得脑震荡。
近义词：dazes, stuns, shocks
反义词：heals, cures
词性：v.

concussion:/kənˈkʌʃən/
noun
脑震荡，震动
A violent shaking or jarring; an injury to the brain caused by a blow, causing temporary loss of consciousness, etc.
He suffered a concussion after falling off his bike. 他从自行车上摔下来后脑震荡。
近义词：impact, shock, trauma
反义词：recovery, healing
词性：n.
