functionless:/ˈfʌŋkʃənləs/
adjective
无功能的；无效的
Having no function; useless.
The machine became functionless after the power outage.  这台机器在停电后就无法使用了。
The decorative buttons on the coat are completely functionless.  外套上的装饰性纽扣完全没有用处。
useless, ineffective, nonfunctional
functional, useful, operative
词性: 形容词
