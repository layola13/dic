nonreprisals:/ˌnɒn rɪˈpraɪzəlz/ | n. | 不报复，不采取报复行动 |  The agreement included a clause on nonreprisals, ensuring that neither side would retaliate against the other. | 例句：The treaty guaranteed nonreprisals against civilians.  该条约保证不报复平民。

例句：The government promised nonreprisals for those who surrendered. 政府承诺对投降者不采取报复行动。| 近义词：forbearance, restraint, leniency, clemency | 反义词：reprisal, retaliation, revenge | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From non- (not) + reprisal (from Old French *reprisaille, from repris-, past participle stem of reprendre "to take back," from re- "back" + prendre "to take").
