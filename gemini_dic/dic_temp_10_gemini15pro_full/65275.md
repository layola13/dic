euchloric: /juˈklɔːrɪk/ | adj. | 常绿的；冬绿的 | Having normal green pigmentation; not chlorotic.  Relating to or denoting plants that retain their green leaves throughout the year. | The garden boasted a variety of euchloric plants, vibrant even in the winter months.  这个花园里生长着各种各样的常绿植物，即使在冬天也充满生机。 | Synonyms: evergreen, verdant,  | Antonyms: deciduous, chlorotic | adjective
