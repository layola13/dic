nextgen:/ˌnɛkst ˈdʒɛn/| adj. n.|下一代，次世代|Relating to or characteristic of the next generation.  Often used in a technological context to refer to products or services that are more advanced than the current generation.  Can also refer to the next generation of people.|例句：This nextgen console has amazing graphics.  这款下一代游戏机拥有惊人的图像。|近义词：cutting-edge, advanced, state-of-the-art|反义词:obsolete, outdated|形容词,名词
