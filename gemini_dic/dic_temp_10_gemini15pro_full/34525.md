cleanlily:/ˈkliːnlɪli/| adv.|干净地，整洁地|Cleanlily is an adverb meaning "in a clean and neat manner". It describes how something is done. It's less common than "cleanly".|She folded the clothes cleanlily and placed them in the drawer. 她干净利落地叠好衣服，放进抽屉里。|He wiped the counter cleanlily with a disinfectant wipe. 他用消毒湿巾干净地擦拭了柜台。|近义词：cleanly, neatly, tidily|反义词:messily, sloppily|词性:副词
