bubby:/ˈbʌbi/| n.|奶奶；祖母（尤用于美国南部和犹太人）；老妇人|A term of endearment for grandmother, especially used in the Southern US and by Jewish people. It can also refer to an elderly woman in general.|My bubby used to bake the best cookies. 我的奶奶以前烤的饼干最好吃。|Grandmother, Grandma, Nana, Granny| (No direct opposite for a term of endearment) |noun
