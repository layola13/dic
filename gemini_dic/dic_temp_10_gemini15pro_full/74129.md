foumart:/ˈfuːmɑːrt/| n.|鼬，臭猫，欧洲臭鼬|A European polecat, a small, dark brown carnivorous mammal with a foul smell. It is also known as a fitch, foul marten, or simply polecat.|例句：The foumart, a nocturnal hunter, preys on small rodents.  臭猫，一种夜行动物猎人，捕食小型啮齿动物。

例句：In medieval times, the fur of the foumart was highly valued. 在中世纪，臭猫的皮毛非常珍贵。|近义词：polecat, fitch, foul marten|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑prey猎物)|词性:noun
