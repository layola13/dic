lobsterish:/ˈlɒbstərɪʃ/ (US) /ˈlɒbstərɪʃ/ (UK)| adj.|像龙虾的；龙虾似的|Resembling or characteristic of a lobster, especially in color, shape, or movement.|例句：The child's hands, red and claw-like from the cold, looked lobsterish.  孩子的手冻得通红，像爪子一样，看起来像龙虾的钳子。

例句：The robot's jerky movements were strangely lobsterish.  机器人的抽搐动作奇怪地像龙虾。|近义词：crustacean-like, crab-like|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider something like "smooth," "graceful," or the opposite of a lobster's typical characteristics depending on the context.)|词性:形容词
