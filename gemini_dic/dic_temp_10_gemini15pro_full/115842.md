marking:/ˈmɑːrkɪŋ/ (US), /ˈmɑːkɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |标记，评分，打分|A mark or a series of marks made on a surface.  The process of awarding a grade or score.| The teacher spent hours marking the exams. 老师花了几个小时批改试卷。|synonyms: score, grade, rating, assessment|antonyms: unmarked, blank|noun


marking:/ˈmɑːrkɪŋ/ (US), /ˈmɑːkɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. |显著的，有记号的|Serving to identify or distinguish something.|The distinctive markings on the butterfly's wings helped identify its species. 蝴蝶翅膀上独特的标记帮助确定了它的种类。|synonyms: distinctive, characteristic, noticeable|antonyms: indistinct, unremarkable|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English markinge, derived from marken ("to mark"), from Old English mearcian, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *markōną.
