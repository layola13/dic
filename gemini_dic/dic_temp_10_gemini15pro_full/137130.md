nonsubsidized:/ˌnɒnˈsʌbsɪdaɪzd/ | /ˌnɑːnˈsʌbsɪdaɪzd/ | adj. | 非补贴的，无津贴的 | Not receiving financial aid or support from a government or other organization.  |  Example:  Nonsubsidized student loans accrue interest while the student is still in school.  非补贴的学生贷款在学生还在上学期间就开始累积利息。 | Example:  She opted for a nonsubsidized mortgage with a lower interest rate. 她选择了利率较低的非补贴抵押贷款。 | Synonyms: unsubsidized, private, independent | Antonyms: subsidized, supported, funded | Adjective
