ignitions:/ɪɡˈnɪʃənz/
noun
点火；点燃；着火
the process of something starting to burn or explode, or a device that starts this process
The car's ignition is faulty. 这辆车的点火装置有问题。
The ignition of the fuel caused a massive explosion. 燃料的点燃引起了一场巨大的爆炸。

Synonyms: firing, kindling, combustion, starting
Antonyms: extinguishing, quenching
