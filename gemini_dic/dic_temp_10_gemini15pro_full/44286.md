croodle:/ˈkruːdl/| v.|哼唱；低声吟唱|To sing softly and quietly, often in a relaxed or informal way. |She croodled a lullaby to her baby. 她低声哼唱着摇篮曲哄她的宝宝.|近义词：hum, murmur, sing softly|反义词:shout, yell|词性:动词
词源:Blend of 'croon' and 'noodle'.  A relatively recent coinage, likely originating in the mid-20th century.  Combines the sense of gentle singing ("croon") with a sense of improvisation or casual playing ("noodle").
