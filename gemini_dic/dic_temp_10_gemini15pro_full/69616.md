feminine-rhymes:/ˈfɛmɪnɪn raɪmz/ |n.| 阴性押韵 | Rhymes that use the final two unstressed syllables, creating a feminine ending.  This is common in languages like English and French. |  Example: "motion" and "ocean" are feminine rhymes.  “Running” and “sunning” are feminine rhymes. | Near Synonyms: double rhyme, disyllabic rhyme | Antonyms: masculine rhyme (single rhyme) | Noun
