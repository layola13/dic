loculous:/ˈlɒkjʊləs/ (US) /ˈlɒkjʊləs/ (UK)| adj.|多室的，有小腔的|Divided into small cavities or compartments; having loculi.|The loculous fruit of the plant contained numerous seeds. 这种植物的多室果实包含许多种子。|Example 2: The archaeologist discovered a loculous artifact, suggesting it was used for storage. 考古学家发现了一个多室的人工制品，表明它曾被用于储存。|近义词: compartmentalized, cellular, multilocular|反义词: unilocular, non-compartmentalized|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *loculus* (small place, compartment), diminutive of *locus* (place).
