aversely:/əˈvɜːrsli/
adverb
不利地，负面地
In a manner that is opposed or contrary to something.
High taxes aversely affect trade. 高税收对贸易产生不利影响。

The humid weather aversely affected her health. 潮湿的天气对她的健康产生了不利影响。

negatively, unfavorably, badly, poorly
favorably, positively, beneficially
adverb
