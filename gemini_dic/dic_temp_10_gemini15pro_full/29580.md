catechetical:/ˌkætɪˈkɛtɪk(ə)l/
adjective
教义问答式的；教理问答的
Relating to or characterized by questions and answers, especially in religious instruction.
The priest led a catechetical session with the children preparing for their first communion.  神父为准备初领圣餐的孩子们主持了一场教义问答会。
The textbook provided a catechetical approach to learning the basic doctrines of the faith. 这本教科书提供了一种通过问答学习基本教义的方法。
synonyms:  didactic, instructive, pedagogical
antonyms:  uninformative, unenlightening
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *catecheticus, from Greek katēkhētikos "instructive," from katēkhein "to teach orally," from kata- "down" + ēkhein "to sound, resound."
