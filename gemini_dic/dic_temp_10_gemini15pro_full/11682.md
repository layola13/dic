armorist:/ˈɑːrmərɪst/| n.|盔甲师，军械师|A person who makes, repairs, or deals in armor.|The armorist carefully polished the knight's breastplate.|Synonyms:  armourer,  metalworker|Antonyms: None|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *armurer*, from Old French *armeürier*, from *armeüre* (armor), from Latin *armatura* (equipment, armor). Ultimately from Latin *arma* (arms, weapons).
