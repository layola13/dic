antetemple:/ˈæntiˌtɛmpl/| n.|前殿，前庭|A space or building in front of a temple, often serving as an entrance or porch.|The antetemple of the Parthenon was adorned with intricate carvings.|近义词:vestibule, porch, forecourt|反义词:naos, cella|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Latin *antetemplum*, from *ante* ("before") + *templum* ("temple").
