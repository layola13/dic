deinternationalizing:/diːɪntərˌnæʃənəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
去国际化
The process of reducing the involvement of international elements or influences in something, such as an organization, economy, or policy.
Many companies are deinternationalizing their operations due to the current global economic uncertainty. 由于当前全球经济的不确定性，许多公司正在将其业务去国际化。
Governments are considering deinternationalizing certain sectors to protect domestic industries. 政府正在考虑将某些行业去国际化，以保护国内产业。
Synonyms: localizing, regionalizing, domesticating
Antonyms: internationalizing, globalizing
词性: 动词
