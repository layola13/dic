despairingness:/dɪˈspɛərɪŋnɪs/
n.
绝望，感到无望
the state of being without hope
He sank into a state of utter despairingness after losing his job.  失去工作后，他陷入了彻底的绝望。

The despairingness of the situation was overwhelming.  局势的绝望令人难以承受。

hopelessness, desperation, despondency
hopefulness, optimism

Etymology: From despairing + -ness.  Despairing comes from Old French desperer, meaning "to deprive of hope," from des- "without" + esperer "to hope."
