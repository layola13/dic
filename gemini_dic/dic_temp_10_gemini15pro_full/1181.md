acclimatising: /əˈklaɪmətaɪzɪŋ/
verb
适应环境; 习惯气候
The process of becoming accustomed to a new climate or environment.
It took him a few weeks to acclimatising to the high altitude.  他花了几个星期才适应了高海拔。
The team is acclimatising to the hot and humid conditions.  该团队正在适应炎热潮湿的环境。
近义词: adapting, adjusting, getting used to
反义词: unaccustomed, unfamiliar
词性: Verb (present participle)
词源: From French *acclimatiser*, from *à* "to" + *climat* "climate" + *-iser* "-ize."


acclimatise: /əˈklaɪmətaɪz/
verb
适应环境; 习惯气候
To become accustomed to a new climate or environment.
She found it difficult to acclimatise to the cold weather. 她发现很难适应寒冷的天气。
The climbers will spend a few days acclimatising to the altitude before attempting the summit.  登山者将在尝试登顶之前花几天时间适应海拔高度。
近义词: adapt, adjust, get used to
反义词: unaccustomed, unfamiliar
词性: Verb
