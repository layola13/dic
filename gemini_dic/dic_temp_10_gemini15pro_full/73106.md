for-all:/fɔːr ɔːl/| quantifier| 对于所有；尽管；虽然 |  Used to indicate that something is true or applies despite appearances or expectations.  Despite seeming otherwise, it applies to everyone or everything. | For all his wealth, he was not happy. 尽管他很富有，但他并不快乐。 | Near synonyms: despite, in spite of, notwithstanding, regardless of | Antonyms: none (the concept doesn't lend itself to direct opposites) |  Other

注意事项:

for-all 并非一个独立的单词，而是由 for 和 all 两个词组成的短语，通常用作连接词或介词短语。
