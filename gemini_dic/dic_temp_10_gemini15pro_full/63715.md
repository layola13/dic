ephemeral: /ɪˈfɛmərəl/ | adj. | 短暂的，朝生暮死的 | Lasting for a very short time. |  The beauty of the cherry blossoms is ephemeral, lasting only a few weeks. 樱花的美丽是短暂的，只持续几个星期。

The joy he felt was ephemeral, quickly replaced by a sense of dread. 他感受到的快乐转瞬即逝，很快被恐惧感所取代。

fleeting, transient, short-lived, momentary | enduring, lasting, permanent, perpetual | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ephēmeros* (lasting for a day), from *epi* (on, upon) + *hēmera* (day).
