palliate: /ˈpæliˌeɪt/ /ˈpælɪeɪt/ | v. | 减轻，缓和（痛苦等）; 掩饰，辩解（罪行等） | to make a disease or its symptoms less severe or unpleasant without removing the cause; to try to make something bad seem less serious |  The doctor palliated the patient's pain with morphine. 医生用吗啡减轻了病人的疼痛。

The government tried to palliate the effects of the economic crisis. 政府试图减轻经济危机的影响。| 近义词: alleviate, ease, mitigate, relieve, soothe | 反义词: aggravate, exacerbate, worsen | 词性: 动词

palliated: /ˈpæliˌeɪtɪd/ /ˈpælɪeɪtɪd/ | adj. |  减轻的，缓和的 | lessened, relieved, mitigated | His palliated cough still bothered him at night. 他缓和的咳嗽仍然在晚上困扰着他。 | 近义词: alleviated, eased, mitigated, relieved, soothed | 反义词: aggravated, exacerbated, worsened | 词性: 形容词
