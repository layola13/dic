avicularia:/əˌvɪkjʊˈlɛəriə/ | n. | 鸟蛛属；捕鸟蛛 |A genus of spiders belonging to the family Theraphosidae, commonly known as pinktoe tarantulas. They are arboreal spiders known for their vibrant colors and relatively docile nature. | Example 1: The avicularia versicolor is a popular pet tarantula due to its striking blue and green coloration.  这只多米尼加蓝宝石华丽雨林是受欢迎的宠物捕鸟蛛，因为它引人注目的蓝色和绿色。
Example 2: Avicularia species are native to the tropical forests of South America and the Caribbean islands. 鸟蛛属物种原产于南美洲和加勒比群岛的热带森林。| 近义词: pinktoe tarantula, Theraphosidae (family) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Latin "avicula" (small bird) + "-aria" (relating to).  This refers to Maria Sibylla Merian's 1705 illustration of an avicularia species eating a bird, which popularized the term "bird-eating spider."
