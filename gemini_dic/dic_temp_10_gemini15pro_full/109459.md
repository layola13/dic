lepidomelane:/ˌlɛpɪˈdɒməleɪn/ | n. | 黑云母 |A black variety of biotite, a common rock-forming mineral.  It is a phyllosilicate mineral of iron, magnesium, potassium, aluminium, and silicon. | Lepidomelane is a common constituent of igneous and metamorphic rocks. 黑云母是火成岩和变质岩的常见成分。| Lepidomelane can be found in granites and pegmatites. 黑云母可以在花岗岩和伟晶岩中找到。| Synonyms: biotite, annite | Antonyms: N/A | noun
