bunny-hop:/ˈbʌni hɒp/ (UK) /ˈbʌni hɑːp/ (US)| n. vi.|兔子跳，双足跳，(自行车)前轮离地跳跃|A jump made with both feet leaving the ground at the same time, like a rabbit.  Also refers to a bicycle trick where the front wheel is lifted off the ground.|The children bunny-hopped across the lawn. 孩子们像兔子一样双足跳跃穿过草坪。|The cyclist bunny-hopped over the log.  骑自行车的人跳过了原木。|synonyms: hop, skip, jump, leap|antonyms: walk, crawl|noun, verb
