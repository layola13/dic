equidistant:/ˌiːkwɪˈdɪstənt/ | adj. | 等距的 |  At an equal distance from two or more points. | The town is equidistant from both London and Birmingham.  这个小镇距离伦敦和伯明翰的距离相等。| equidistant points are equally far from a center point.| equidistant from three vertices of a triangle; the center of the circumscribed circle of a triangle. 等距于三角形的三个顶点；三角形外接圆的中心 | synonym:  equally distant | antonym: unequally distant | adjective
