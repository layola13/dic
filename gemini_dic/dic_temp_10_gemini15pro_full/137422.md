nontexturally:/ˌnɑːnˈtɛkstʃərəli/
adjective
非结构地，非质地地
Not related to or based on texture; lacking textural qualities.
The artist presented a nontexturally smooth sculpture.  艺术家展示了一个非质地光滑的雕塑。
The music was described as nontexturally complex, focusing on melody rather than layers of sound. 这段音乐被描述为非质地复杂，专注于旋律而不是声音的层次。
near synonyms: smoothly, flatly, plain, untextured
opposite: texturally, textured, rough, uneven
word type: adjective
