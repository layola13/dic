chavviest:/ˈtʃæviɪst/| adj.|最俗气的，最粗俗的|Superlative form of chavvy: most chavvy.  Describes someone or something considered to exhibit the stereotypical characteristics of a "chav" to the greatest degree, often including ostentatious displays of designer brands, a particular style of dress, and certain behaviours.|That's the chavviest outfit I've ever seen. 那是我见过的最俗气的衣服。|Synonyms:tacky, gaudy, vulgar, ostentatious|Antonyms:classy, sophisticated, refined|adjective
