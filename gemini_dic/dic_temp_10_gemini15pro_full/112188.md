loon:/luːn/
noun
潜鸟；疯子；笨蛋
A large, black-and-white diving bird with a haunting cry. / A crazy or foolish person.
The loon's eerie call echoed across the lake.  这潜鸟怪异的叫声在湖面上回荡。
That guy's a complete loon!  那家伙是个十足的疯子！
近义词:crazy person, lunatic, simpleton, idiot
反义词:genius, sane person
词性:n.

loon:/luːn/
adjective
疯狂的；愚蠢的
Crazy or foolish.
That's a loon idea! 那真是个疯狂的主意！
He had a loon look in his eyes. 他眼神看起来很疯狂。
近义词:crazy, foolish, insane
反义词:sane, sensible, wise
词性:adj.
