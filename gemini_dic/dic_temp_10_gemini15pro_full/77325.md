gato:/ˈɡɑːtoʊ/ (US), /ˈɡætəʊ/ (UK)| n.|猫|A small domesticated carnivorous mammal with soft fur, a short snout, and retractile claws. It is widely kept as a pet or for catching mice.|The gato sat on the mat. 猫坐在垫子上。|Synonyms: cat, feline, kitty|Antonyms: dog, canine|noun
