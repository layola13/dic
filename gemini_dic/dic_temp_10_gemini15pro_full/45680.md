curtilage: /ˈkɜːrtɪlɪdʒ/ | n. | 宅地，庭院 | The area immediately surrounding a house, including any outbuildings and yards, considered legally part of the house. |  The police searched the curtilage of the suspect's house. 警方搜查了嫌疑人住宅的庭院。

The fire spread to the curtilage of the neighboring property. 火势蔓延到了邻近房产的庭院。 | 近义词: yard, grounds, property | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Old French *cortillage, from Vulgar Latin *cortiliāgium, from Late Latin cortile “courtyard,” from Latin cohors “enclosed yard, cohort.”
