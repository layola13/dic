mechanographist:/məˈkænəɡrɑːfɪst/| n.|机械打字员|A person who operates a keyboard perforator or other keyboard-operated machine, especially for punching holes representing data, or for setting type.|The mechanographist quickly input the data into the machine. 机械打字员快速地将数据输入机器。|Synonyms: typist, keyboard operator, data entry clerk|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "scribe" for a handwritten equivalent)|Noun
