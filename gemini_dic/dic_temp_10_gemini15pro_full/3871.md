agrammatist:/əˈɡræmətɪst/
n.
不懂语法的人; 写作语法错误的人
A person who writes ungrammatically or ignores the rules of grammar.
He was known as an aggrammatist, frequently making errors in his writing. 他因经常在写作中犯语法错误而被称为语法盲。
The aggrammatist submitted a paper full of errors. 这个语法盲提交了一篇充满错误的论文。
近义词:bad grammarian, poor writer
反义词:grammarian, good writer
词性:noun

Etymology:
From Greek *a- 'not' + grammatistēs 'one who knows grammar', from grámma 'letter'.
