babus:/ˈbɑːbuːs/
n.
官员，公务员，官僚 (Often derogatory)
A bureaucrat, especially in India, Pakistan, and other South Asian countries.  Often used with a negative connotation, suggesting inefficiency or corruption.
He's just another babu pushing papers. 他只是另一个推纸的官僚。
近义词: bureaucrat, official, clerk, mandarin
反义词: revolutionary, rebel
