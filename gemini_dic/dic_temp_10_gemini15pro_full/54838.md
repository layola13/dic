displace:/dɪˈspleɪs/ /dɪsˈpleɪs/| vt.|取代，转移，迫使…离开家园|To force something or someone out of its usual or original position.|The flood displaced thousands of people.  洪水迫使数千人离开家园。|The new technology has displaced the old one. 新技术取代了旧技术。|近义词：replace, substitute, move, relocate, oust|反义词：place, settle, reinstate|词性:动词


displace:/dɪˈspleɪs/ /dɪsˈpleɪs/| n.|排量|The volume or weight of a fluid (such as water) displaced by a floating body.|The ship has a displacement of 10,000 tons. 这艘船的排水量为1万吨。|The engine's displacement is 2 liters.  这台发动机的排量是2升。|近义词：volume, capacity|反义词：N/A|词性:名词
