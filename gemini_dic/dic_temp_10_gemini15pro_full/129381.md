nomarchy: /ˈnɒmɑːki/ (US) /ˈnɒmɑːki/ (UK)| n. |省级行政区域；地方行政区域|The area ruled by a nomarch, a form of local government in ancient Egypt and modern Greece. |Egypt was divided into several nomarchies, each governed by a nomarch. 埃及被划分为几个省级行政区域，每个区域由一个省长管理。| The nomarchy struggled with maintaining its autonomy. 这个省级行政区域努力维持其自治权。|Synonyms: province, prefecture, district, region|Antonyms: central government, national government|Noun
