myriopoda:/ˌmɪriˈɒpədə/ | n. |多足类，多足纲动物|A large class of arthropods having the body composed of numerous similar segments nearly all of which bear true jointed legs. |例句：Myriopoda includes centipedes and millipedes. 多足纲包括蜈蚣和千足虫。

例句：The myriopoda are a fascinating group of invertebrates. 多足纲是一组迷人的无脊椎动物。|近义词：myriapod |反义词: (无明确反义词，可考虑其他动物类别，如 insects 昆虫) |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek μυριόπους (muriópous, “having ten thousand feet”), from μύριοι (múrioi, “ten thousand”) + πούς (poús, “foot”).
