inter-strain:/ˌɪntərˈstreɪn/
adjective
菌株间的，品系间的
Occurring between or involving different strains (of a species, virus, etc.)
The study compared the inter-strain variability of the virus.  这项研究比较了该病毒不同株系间的变异性。
Inter-strain competition can lead to the dominance of one strain over others. 菌株间竞争会导致一种菌株对其他菌株的支配。
近义词:intervarietal
反义词:intra-strain
词性:adjective
