nunciate:/ˈnʌnʃieɪt/ /ˈnʌnsiˌeɪt/| v. |宣布；宣告；清晰地说|To announce or declare something formally or solemnly. To pronounce words or parts of words clearly and distinctly.|The ambassador was chosen to nunciate the president's message. 大使被选中宣布总统的讯息。  He enunciated each syllable with precision. 他清晰地发出每一个音节。|近义词：announce, proclaim, pronounce, articulate|反义词：mumble, mutter|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *nuntiatus, past participle of nuntiare "to announce," from Latin nuntius "messenger, message."
