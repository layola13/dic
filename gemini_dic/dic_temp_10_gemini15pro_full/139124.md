obeisance:/oʊˈbeɪsəns/ /əˈbiːsəns/ | n. | 敬礼，鞠躬，尊崇 | A gesture expressing deference or homage, such as a bow or curtsy. |  The knight made a deep obeisance to the king. 骑士向国王深深鞠了一躬。 |  She made a slight obeisance as she entered the room. 她进房间时微微鞠了一躬。 | synonyms: bow, curtsy, deference, homage, respect, reverence | antonyms: disrespect, irreverence, contempt | noun
