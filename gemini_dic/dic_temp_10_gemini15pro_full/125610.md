multiview:/ˈmʌltiˌvjuː/
adjective|多视角的，多视图的|Relating to or involving multiple views or perspectives.|The researchers used a multiview approach to study the problem, incorporating data from various sources.|近义词:multifaceted, comprehensive, holistic|反义词:limited, narrow, restricted|词性:adjective
noun|多视图，多视角|A perspective or collection of perspectives from multiple viewpoints.|The multiview provided a more complete understanding of the situation.|近义词:panorama, overview, composite|反义词:single view, limited perspective|词性:noun
