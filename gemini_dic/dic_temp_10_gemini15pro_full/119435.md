microinstructions:/ˌmaɪkroʊɪnˈstrʌkʃənz/ | n. | 微指令 | A low-level instruction that controls the operation of a computer's central processing unit (CPU). These instructions are typically very simple operations, like moving data between registers or performing basic arithmetic. | Microinstructions are used to implement complex instructions in the CPU's instruction set. 微指令用于在 CPU 的指令集中实现复杂指令。| micro-operations, microcode | macroinstructions | noun
