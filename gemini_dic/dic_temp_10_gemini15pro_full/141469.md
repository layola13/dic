opinion: /əˈpɪnjən/ | n. | 意见，看法，主张 | A belief or judgment that rests on grounds insufficient to produce complete certainty. |  Example 1: In my opinion, this is the best course of action.  我的意见是，这是最好的行动方案。

Example 2:  Public opinion is divided on this issue.  公众对此事的意见不一。 | Synonyms: belief, view, perspective, standpoint, judgment | Antonyms: fact, proof, certainty | noun
