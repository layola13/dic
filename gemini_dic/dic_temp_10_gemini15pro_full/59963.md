edificatory:/ˈɛdɪfɪkətɔːri/
adjective
有教益的，启发的
Intended to instruct or improve morally or intellectually.
His speech was intended to be edificatory, but the audience found it dull. 他的演讲旨在具有启发性，但观众觉得很无聊。
His edificatory sermons often focused on the importance of community service. 他具有启发性的布道经常强调社区服务的重要性。

近义词: instructive, enlightening, educational, improving, didactic
反义词: unedifying, demoralizing, corrupting
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin aedificatorius (“building”), from Latin aedificator (“builder”), from aedificō (“I build”), from aedēs (“house, temple”) + faciō (“I do, make”).  Influenced by French édificatoire.
