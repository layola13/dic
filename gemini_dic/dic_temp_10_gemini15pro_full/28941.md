carrotiest:/ˈkærətiɪst/| adj.|最胡萝卜色的，最像胡萝卜的|Most resembling a carrot in color; most intensely orange.  It's a superlative form of "carroty."| That's the carrotiest orange I've ever seen! 那是我见过的颜色最像胡萝卜的橙子！|  The sunset painted the sky in the carrotiest hues imaginable. 日落把天空染成了可以想象到的最胡萝卜的颜色。|近义词：orange, reddish-orange|反义词:palest, least orange|词性:adjective
