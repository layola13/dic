heteromorphic:/ˌhɛtərəˈmɔːrfɪk/| adj.|异形的，不同形的|Occurring in two or more different forms, especially at different stages in the life cycle.  Having different forms at different periods of the life cycle.|Example 1: The butterfly is a heteromorphic insect, its larval stage being a caterpillar. 蝴蝶是一种异形昆虫，它的幼虫阶段是毛虫。

Example 2:  Many plants exhibit heteromorphic alternation of generations, with a distinct sporophyte and gametophyte. 许多植物表现出异形世代交替，具有明显的孢子体和配子体。|近义词：polymorphic, allomorphic|反义词：isomorphic, monomorphic|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From hetero- (different, other) + -morphic (form, shape).  Hetero- comes from the Greek "heteros" (other, different), and -morphic comes from the Greek "morphē" (form, shape).
