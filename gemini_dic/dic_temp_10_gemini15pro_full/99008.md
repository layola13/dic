interactive:/ˌɪntərˈæktɪv/ | adj. | 交互的，互动的 | Allowing a two-way flow of information between a computer and a user; responding to a user's input. |  Example 1: This museum has many interactive exhibits that children will love.  这个博物馆有许多孩子们会喜欢的互动展品。

Example 2:  The online course included interactive quizzes and exercises. 这个在线课程包含互动测验和练习。 | Synonyms: reciprocal, two-way, responsive | Antonyms: passive, unresponsive, one-way | adjective

词源:
From inter- + active.  Inter- means "between" or "among," and active means "engaged in action."  So, interactive means "acting between" or "acting among."
