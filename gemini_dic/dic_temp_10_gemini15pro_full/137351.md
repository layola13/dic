nontaxes:/nɒnˈtæksɪz/
noun
非税收入
government revenue derived from sources other than taxes.
The government relies on nontaxes such as fees and fines to supplement tax revenue. 政府依靠罚款和收费等非税收入来补充税收。
近义词:non-tax revenue, other revenue
反义词:tax revenue, taxes
词性:n.
