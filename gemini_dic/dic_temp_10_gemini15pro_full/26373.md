by and large:/baɪ ənd lɑːrdʒ/
|adv.|大体上，总的来说|Generally speaking; on the whole.  It refers to something that is mostly, but not completely, true.|By and large, the public approves of the government's policies. 大体上，公众赞成政府的政策。|Synonyms: generally, mostly, on the whole, overall|Antonyms: specifically, particularly, exceptionally|Adverb
