fatherlessness:/ˈfɑːðərləsnəs/| n.|无父状态，缺乏父爱|The state of being without a father, either physically or emotionally. This can refer to the absence of a father due to death, abandonment, or estrangement, or to a father's emotional unavailability or neglect.|His fatherlessness affected him deeply throughout his childhood.|近义词：paternal deprivation, absence of a father|反义词:paternal presence|词性:noun
