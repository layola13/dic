fencer:/ˈfɛnsər/| n. |击剑运动员；剑客|A person who practices fencing.  A person who fences, especially one who competes in the sport of fencing.|例句：The fencer lunged forward, scoring a touch. 击剑运动员向前猛冲，得分了。

例句：She is a skilled fencer with years of experience. 她是一位经验丰富的熟练击剑运动员。|近义词：swordsman, swordswoman, duelist |反义词: N/A |词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fensere, from Anglo-Norman fencere, Old French fensier, from Vulgar Latin *fensārius, from Latin fēnsum (“fence, defense”), past participle of fendere (“to strike, defend, ward off”), from Proto-Italic *fendō, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)pʰen- (“to draw, stretch, spin”). Cognate with Spanish esgrimir (“to fence”), Italian schermire (“to fence”).
