incoagulable:/ˌɪnkoʊˈæɡjələbəl/ | adj. | 不可凝固的 | Not capable of being coagulated; not clotting. | The patient's blood was found to be incoagulable due to a rare genetic disorder.  病人的血液由于一种罕见的遗传疾病而无法凝固。 |  Synonyms: noncoagulable, uncoagulable | Antonyms: coagulable, clottable | adjective

Etymology:
From in- (not) + coagulable (capable of being coagulated). Coagulable comes from Latin coagulāre, from coagulum (curd, rennet), from cogere (to drive or bring together).
