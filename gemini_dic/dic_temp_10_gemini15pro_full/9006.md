antilogous:/ænˈtɪləɡəs/
adjective
对立的；对抗性的；矛盾的
Relating to or involving αντιλογία, meaning contradiction or opposition in speech.  Characterized by contrasting or opposing principles, statements, or arguments.
The antilogous views presented by the two debaters made it difficult to reach a consensus.  两位辩论者提出的对立观点使得很难达成共识。
antithetical, contradictory, opposing
consistent, harmonious, agreeing
