churner:/ˈtʃɜːrnər/| n.|搅拌器；搅乳器；制造大量低劣作品的人|A device used for churning milk or cream into butter.  A person who produces large quantities of work, especially of low quality. | The old farmer used a hand-cranked churner to make butter. 老农夫用一个手摇搅拌器来制作黄油。

The publisher dismissed him as a churner of pulp fiction. 出版商认为他是一个粗制滥造的低俗小说作家而解雇了他。|近义词：mixer, agitator, producer (for a person)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


churn:/tʃɜːrn/| v.|搅拌；翻腾；大量生产（尤指低劣作品）|To agitate or stir (milk or cream) in order to produce butter. To produce something, especially something of low quality, in large quantities. |The cream is churned to make butter. 奶油被搅拌做成黄油。

He churns out several novels a year. 他每年粗制滥造出好几部小说。|近义词：stir, agitate, produce|反义词: N/A|词性:verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ċyornan, from Proto-Germanic *kurnaną, from Proto-Indo-European *kʷer- (“to turn, twist”). Related to quern.  The noun "churner" is derived from the verb "churn" with the addition of the agentive suffix "-er."
