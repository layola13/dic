columns:/ˈkɒləmz/ (British) /ˈkɑːləmz/ (American)| n. | 列；圆柱；专栏 | Vertical sections of a printed page; supporting pillars; regular articles in a newspaper or magazine. |  The newspaper has reduced the number of columns on its front page. 这家报纸减少了头版上的专栏数量。

The ancient temple was supported by massive marble columns. 这座古老的寺庙由巨大的大理石柱支撑。

She writes a weekly column for the local newspaper. 她为当地报纸撰写每周专栏。 | synonyms: pillars, supports, posts (for structural columns), articles, features (for newspaper columns), fields (in databases) | antonyms: rows (in databases, tables) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "columpne," from Latin "columna" meaning "pillar, column." Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European root "*kel-" meaning "to project, to bend."
