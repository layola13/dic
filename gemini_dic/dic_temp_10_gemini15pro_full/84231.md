half-seriousness:/ˌhæfˈsɪəriəsnəs/
|n.|
|半认真; 半开玩笑|
|A state between seriousness and playfulness, not entirely serious but not entirely joking either.|
|He delivered the news with a tone of half-seriousness, leaving us unsure whether to laugh or be concerned. 他用半开玩笑的语气宣布了这个消息，让我们不知道该笑还是该担心。|

|She spoke with half-seriousness about quitting her job, but we all knew she wouldn't. 她半开玩笑地说要辞职，但我们都知道她不会。|
|Near synonyms:  playfulness, semi-seriousness, mock seriousness|
|Near antonyms: seriousness, earnestness, solemnity|
