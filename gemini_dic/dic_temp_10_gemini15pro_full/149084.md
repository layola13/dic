oxamide:/ˈɒksəmaɪd/ | /ˈɑːksəmaɪd/ | n. | 乙二酰胺 |A white crystalline powder, (CONH₂)₂, formed by the dry distillation of oxalates, or by heating ethyl oxalate with aqueous ammonia. It is used in the manufacture of fertilizers, pharmaceuticals, and other organic compounds. | Oxamide is a relatively insoluble compound. 乙二酰胺是一种相对不溶的化合物。 | Oxamide is used as a slow-release nitrogen fertilizer. 乙二酰胺用作缓释氮肥。 | synonyms:  ethanediamide | antonyms: N/A | noun
