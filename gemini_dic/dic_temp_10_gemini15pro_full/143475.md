outgallops:/ˌaʊtˈɡæləps/
verb
超越，跑得比…快
To run faster than another horse, especially in a horse race.
The young horse easily outgalloped the older, more experienced one.  这匹年轻的马轻松地超过了那匹年纪更大、经验更丰富的马。
Synonyms: outrun, outstrip, overtake
Antonyms: lag behind, trail
词性: 动词

outgallops:/ˌaʊtˈɡæləps/
noun
超越，胜过
The act of outgalloping.
The outgallop in the final stretch secured his victory.  在最后冲刺阶段的超越确保了他的胜利。
Synonyms: outrunning, outstripping
Antonyms: falling behind
词性: 名词
