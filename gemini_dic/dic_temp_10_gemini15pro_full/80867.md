goutily:/ˈɡaʊtɪli/| adv.|痛风地，患痛风地|In a manner characteristic of gout; affected with gout.  Like someone suffering from gout. |He walked goutily, his foot throbbing with pain. 他一瘸一拐地走着，脚痛得厉害。|近义词:arthritically|反义词:healthily|词性:副词

词源: From *gouty* + *-ly*.  *Gouty* ultimately derives from the Latin *gutta* (drop), reflecting the ancient belief that gout was caused by drops of morbid humor falling into the joints.
