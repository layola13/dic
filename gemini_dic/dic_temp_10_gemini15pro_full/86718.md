helminthes:/hɛlˈmɪnθiːz/
noun
蠕虫类；寄生虫类
A group of invertebrate animals, including parasitic worms such as roundworms, flatworms, and thorny-headed worms.
Helminthes are a major cause of disease in developing countries. 蠕虫病是发展中国家的主要疾病原因。
Parasitic helminthes can infect humans and animals. 寄生蠕虫可以感染人类和动物。
synonyms: worms, parasites
antonyms: None (no direct antonym for a biological classification)
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἕλμινς (hélmins, “worm”).
