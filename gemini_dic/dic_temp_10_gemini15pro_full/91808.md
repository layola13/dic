hyperflexibility:/ˌhaɪpərˌflɛksɪˈbɪləti/
noun
过度灵活；超柔韧性
The ability of a joint to move beyond its normal range of motion.  This can be a natural variation in anatomy, or a result of training or a medical condition.
She has hyperflexibility in her joints, which allows her to do contortions. 她的关节过度灵活，可以做各种扭曲动作。

近义词:  laxity, suppleness, flexibility
反义词:  stiffness, rigidity, inflexibility
词性: noun
