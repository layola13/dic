antiphoner:/ænˈtɪfənər/ |n.|对经轮唱圣歌集；轮唱圣歌集|A book containing antiphons, verses, or responses sung or chanted during divine service, especially in the Roman Catholic Church.  It's a liturgical book containing the music and texts for antiphonal singing.|The choir sang beautifully from the antiphoner.|Synonym: antiphonary|Antonym: N/A|noun
