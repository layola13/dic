explode:/ɪkˈsploʊd/| v.|爆炸，爆发，激增|To burst or shatter violently and noisily as a result of rapid combustion, decomposition, or overpressure; to suddenly increase greatly in number, amount, or intensity.|The bomb exploded in the city center. 炸弹在市中心爆炸了。|近义词：burst, detonate, erupt, blow up|反义词:implode, collapse|verb

explode:/ɪkˈsploʊd/| v.|勃然大怒；突然发作|To suddenly express a strong emotion, such as anger, laughter, or grief.|He exploded with rage when he heard the news. 他听到这个消息后勃然大怒。|近义词：erupt, outburst|反义词:calm down, subside|verb
