bearing:/ˈbeərɪŋ/ (US), /ˈbeərɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|方位；轴承；关系；举止；忍受|A device that allows a rotating shaft to rest on a support, reducing friction and allowing smooth movement.  Also refers to the direction or position of something, or a person's posture and behavior. |The ship lost its bearings in the fog. (这艘船在雾中失去了方向。)  The bearings in the engine need to be replaced. (发动机里的轴承需要更换。) |Synonyms: direction, position, posture, demeanor, tolerance, endurance|Antonyms:  (For direction)  misdirection,  (For tolerance) intolerance|noun


bearing:/ˈbeərɪŋ/ (US), /ˈbeərɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|关系；意义|Relevance or significance to something else.|His comments had no bearing on the case. (他的评论与本案无关。)|Synonyms: relevance, significance, pertinence|Antonyms: irrelevance, insignificance|noun
