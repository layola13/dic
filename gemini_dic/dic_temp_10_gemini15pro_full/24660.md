bromeliaceous: /ˌbroʊməliˈeɪʃəs/
| adj. |凤梨科的；属于凤梨科的 |Relating to or characteristic of the Bromeliaceae family of plants, which includes pineapples, Spanish moss, and many other tropical and subtropical species.| The bromeliaceous plants in her garden were thriving in the humid climate. 她花园里的凤梨科植物在潮湿的气候中茁壮成长。 | 近义词:  pineapple family (非正式) | 反义词: 无 | 词性: 形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin *Bromeliaceae*, named after the Swedish botanist Olaf Bromelius (1639-1705).
