gatehouses:/ˈɡeɪthaʊzɪz/
noun
大门口的守卫室；门房；（尤指城堡或围墙的）门楼
A small building by the gate of a large building such as a castle, college, or prison, where the gatekeeper lives or works.
The gatehouses of Hampton Court Palace are magnificent. 汉普顿宫的门楼非常壮观。

gatehouses:/ˈɡeɪthaʊzɪz/
noun
（收费公路的）收费站
A building at the entrance to a toll road where drivers pay to use the road.
The gatehouses on the motorway were very busy. 高速公路上的收费站非常繁忙。


近义词: lodge, gatehouse (singular), portal, entrance, gateway
反义词: exit, egress

词性: n. (noun)
