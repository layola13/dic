idioms:/ˈɪdiəmz/| n. |习语，成语|A group of words established by usage as having a meaning not deducible from those of the individual words (e.g., over the moon, see the light).|She uses a lot of idioms in her writing, which makes it more colorful. 她写作中用了很多习语，使文章更生动。|近义词：expressions, phrases, sayings|反义词:literal language|noun
