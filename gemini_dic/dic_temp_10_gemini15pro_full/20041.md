bionically:/baɪˈɒnɪkli/
adverb.
仿生地
In a way that resembles or is suggested by natural biological processes.
例句：The prosthetic limb allowed him to move bionically.  这个假肢让他能够仿生运动。

例句：She was fitted with a bionically engineered arm. 她安装了一个仿生工程手臂。


近义词：synthetically, artificially
反义词：naturally, organically
词性：副词

Etymology:
From bionic + -ally.  Bionic from bio- + electronic.  Bio- from Greek bios "life".  Electronic from electron + -ic.
