pacifics: /pəˈsɪfɪks/
n.
太平洋(尤指与大西洋相对而言);太平洋沿岸地区
the Pacific Ocean, especially as contrasted with the Atlantic.
例句：The Pacifics are vast and full of diverse marine life. 太平洋广阔无垠，充满了各种各样的海洋生物。

例句：Trade routes across the Pacifics have connected nations for centuries.  跨越太平洋的贸易路线几个世纪以来一直连接着各国。

近义词：Pacific Ocean
反义词: Atlantic

词性: 名词
