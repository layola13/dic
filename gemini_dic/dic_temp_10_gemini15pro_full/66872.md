expectorant:/ɪkˈspɛktərənt/| n. adj.|祛痰药，祛痰的|A medicine that helps to bring up mucus from the airways.|The doctor prescribed an expectorant to help clear his chest. 医生给他开了祛痰药以帮助清除胸腔里的痰。|近义词：mucolytic, antitussive (when used to suppress coughs related to mucus)|反义词: None (no direct opposite, but decongestants address different respiratory issues)|词性: Noun, Adjective
Etymology: From Late Latin expectorant-, present participle stem of expectorare "to clear the breast," from ex- "out" + pector-, stem of pectus "breast."
