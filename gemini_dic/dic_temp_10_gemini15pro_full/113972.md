maggoty:/ˈmæɡəti/| adj.|蛆虫的；腐烂的；令人作呕的|Infested with maggots; decaying and foul-smelling.  Relating to or resembling maggots, especially in being pale, soft, and wriggling.|The dumpster behind the restaurant was maggoty and smelled terrible.  餐厅后面的垃圾箱里都是蛆，而且气味难闻。|Synonyms: wormy, putrid, festering, rotten,foul, disgusting|Antonyms: clean, fresh, pure, wholesome|adjective


maggoty:/ˈmæɡəti/| adj.|令人厌恶的；堕落的|Morally or aesthetically repugnant; degenerate.|He led a maggoty existence, indulging in every vice imaginable. 他过着堕落的生活，沉溺于各种恶习。|Synonyms: depraved, degenerate, corrupt, vile|Antonyms: virtuous, moral, upright, noble|adjective
