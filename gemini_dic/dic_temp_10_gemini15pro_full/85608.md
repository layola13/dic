haughty:/ˈhɔːti/ | adj. | 傲慢的，高傲的 | Arrogantly superior and disdainful.  | Example: She gave him a haughty look. 她傲慢地看了他一眼。 | Example: His haughty demeanor alienated his colleagues. 他高傲的态度疏远了他的同事。 | 近义词: arrogant, disdainful, supercilious, proud, snobbish | 反义词: humble, modest, unassuming | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *hautif*, *altif*, from Latin *altus* ("high").
