newsreader: /ˈnjuːzˌriːdər/ (British English), /ˈnuːzˌriːdər/ (American English)
|n.|新闻播报员；新闻主播|A person who reads the news on television or radio.|The newsreader announced the breaking news with a serious tone.  新闻播报员以严肃的语气宣布了突发新闻。|
|例句：The newsreader delivered the news calmly and clearly.  这位新闻播报员平静而清晰地播报了新闻。|
|例句：She hopes to become a newsreader one day. 她希望有一天成为一名新闻播报员。|
|近义词：anchor, announcer, broadcaster, presenter|
|反义词: listener, viewer (in a relative sense)|
|词性: noun|
