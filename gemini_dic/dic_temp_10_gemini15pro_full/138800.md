nummulation:/ˌnʌmjʊˈleɪʃən/| n.|麻木；失去感觉|The nummulation in his fingers made it difficult to button his shirt. 手指麻木让他难以系上衬衫纽扣。|Numbness, insensibility|Sensitivity|noun
Etymology: From Latin *nummulatio*, from nummus (coin) + -atio (suffix forming nouns of action).  The original sense related to the tingling sensation resembling coins pressed against the skin.
