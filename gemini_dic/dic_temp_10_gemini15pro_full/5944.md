amblyopic:/ˌæmbliˈɒpɪk/ | adj. |弱视的|Having amblyopia; relating to or characterized by amblyopia, a condition in which the vision in one eye is reduced because the eye and brain are not working together properly.|例句：The child's amblyopic eye was treated with an eye patch.  这个孩子的弱视眼用眼罩治疗。

例句：Early detection is crucial for treating amblyopic children. 早期发现对于治疗弱视儿童至关重要。|近义词：lazy eye, strabismic|反义词：N/A (No direct antonym for a medical condition)|词性: adjective
