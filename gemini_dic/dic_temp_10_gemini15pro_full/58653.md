dumbo:/ˈdʌmboʊ/| n. |笨蛋，傻瓜|A name, often used as an insult, referring to someone considered stupid or foolish, derived from the Disney character of a flying elephant with large ears.|例句：Don't be such a dumbo! 别这么傻！

例句：He felt like a dumbo for forgetting his keys. 他觉得自己忘了钥匙像个笨蛋。|近义词：idiot, fool, moron, nitwit, simpleton|反义词：genius, intellectual, smart, bright|词性:noun
