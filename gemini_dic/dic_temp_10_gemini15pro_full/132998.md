nonengagement:/ˌnɑːnɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt/ | n. |不参与，不介入，脱离| Lack of engagement or involvement; the state of not being engaged or involved. |  The policy of nonengagement in foreign wars has been debated for decades.  几十年来，不卷入外国战争的政策一直备受争议。

The artist's nonengagement with the art world led to obscurity.  这位艺术家不参与艺术圈导致默默无闻。| 近义词: detachment, aloofness, apathy, inaction | 反义词: engagement, involvement, participation, intervention | 词性: 名词
