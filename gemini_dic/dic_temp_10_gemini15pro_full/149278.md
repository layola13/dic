ozoned:/ˈoʊˌzoʊnd/
adjective
臭氧化的；充满臭氧的
treated with or containing ozone.
例句：The ozoned air smelled fresh and clean. 臭氧化的空气闻起来清新干净。

例句：They used ozoned water to sterilize the equipment. 他们用臭氧水对设备进行消毒。
近义词：sterilized, disinfected
反义词：contaminated, polluted
词性: adjective
