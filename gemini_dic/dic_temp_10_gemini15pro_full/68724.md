farl:/fɑːrl/ |n.|烤饼，燕麦饼|A quarter section of a thin, round cake made from oatmeal, flour, fat, and milk, baked on a griddle.  A type of scone, especially common in Northern Ireland and Scotland. |I had a farl with butter and jam for breakfast. 我早餐吃了块烤饼，涂了黄油和果酱。|She offered me a freshly baked farl. 她给了我一块刚烤好的燕麦饼。|近义词：scone, bannock, oatcake|反义词: None|词性: noun
