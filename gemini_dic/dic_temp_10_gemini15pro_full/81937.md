grimacier:/ɡrɪˈmeɪsiə(r)/ | n. |  扮鬼脸的人；丑角演员 | A performer, often a clown or mime, who makes exaggerated facial expressions. |  The grimacier amused the children with his silly faces.  这个扮鬼脸的人用他滑稽的表情逗乐了孩子们。 | synonyms: clown, mime, comedian, buffoon | antonyms:  serious person, stoic | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *grimacier*, from *grimace* ("grimace"), from Old Provençal *grimaissa*, of uncertain origin. Possibly from a Vulgar Latin *grima* (“mask”).
