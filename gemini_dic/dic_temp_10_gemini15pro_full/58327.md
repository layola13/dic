dry-nurse:/ˈdraɪˌnɜːrs/ | v. |  试运行，预演；排演；（婴儿）由非亲生母亲抚养 | To rehearse (a play, for example) before public performance; to practice or test something in advance | The director decided to dry-nurse the play before opening night. 导演决定在首演之夜前预演这出戏。 |  The team dry-nursed the new software before its official launch. 团队在新软件正式发布前进行了试运行。 | synonyms: rehearse, practice, trial run | antonyms: perform, implement | verb

dry-nurse:/ˈdraɪˌnɜːrs/ | n. | （婴儿）非亲生母亲；奶妈 | A woman who breastfeeds another's baby |  In the past, wealthy families often employed dry-nurses to care for their infants. 过去，富裕的家庭经常雇佣奶妈来照顾他们的婴儿。 | The dry-nurse rocked the baby gently to sleep. 奶妈轻轻地摇着婴儿入睡。 | synonyms: wet nurse (sometimes), nanny, nursemaid | antonyms: biological mother | noun
