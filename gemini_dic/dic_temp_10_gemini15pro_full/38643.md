concupy: /kənˈkjuːpi/ | v. | 贪求，渴望 | To have a strong, usually sexual, desire for someone or something. | He was consumed by concupy for his neighbor's wife. 他被对邻居妻子的贪欲所吞噬。|  She struggled with feelings of concupy. 她与心中的欲望作斗争。 | 近义词: lust, desire, covet | 反义词: abstinence, chastity | 词性: 动词

词源:  From Late Latin *concupia, from Latin concupīscere (“to long for”), present active infinitive of concupīscō (“I desire”), from con- + cupīscō (“I desire”).
