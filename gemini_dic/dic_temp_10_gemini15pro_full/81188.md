grandiloquent:/ˌɡrændɪˈləʊkwənt/| adj.|夸大的，浮夸的，辞藻华丽的|Characterized by pompous or bombastic language; inflated in style.|The politician delivered a grandiloquent speech, full of lofty rhetoric but lacking substance. 这位政客发表了一篇辞藻华丽的演讲，充满了崇高的言辞，但缺乏实质内容。|近义词：pompous, bombastic, ostentatious, florid|反义词：simple, plain, unpretentious, concise|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "grandiloquus," from "grandis" (grand, large) + "loqui" (to speak).
