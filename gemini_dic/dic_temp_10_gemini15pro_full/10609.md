approach:/əˈproʊtʃ/ (US) /əˈprəʊtʃ/ (UK)| n. v.|接近，方法，途径|A way of dealing with something; the act of coming nearer.|例句：The new approach to teaching is very effective.  这种新的教学方法很有效。|近义词：method, way, tactic, strategy|反义词:avoidance, retreat|noun
approach:/əˈproʊtʃ/ (US) /əˈprəʊtʃ/ (UK)| v.|接近，着手处理|Come near or nearer to (someone or something) in space, time, quality, or amount.|例句：Winter is approaching. 冬天快到了。|近义词：near, advance, come close to|反义词:recede, retreat|verb
