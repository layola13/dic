messiah:/məˈsaɪə/| n.|救世主，弥赛亚|A savior or liberator of a group or nation.  A divinely appointed king.  Often capitalized when referring to Jesus Christ.|例句：He was hailed as the messiah of the movement. 他被誉为这场运动的救世主。

例句：Many believe Jesus Christ is the Messiah. 许多人相信耶稣基督是救世主。|近义词：savior, redeemer, deliverer|反义词: oppressor, tyrant|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin Messias, from Greek Messīas, from Aramaic mšīḥā "anointed," from mšḥ "to anoint."  Ultimately from Hebrew māšīaḥ.
