blote:/bloʊt/| v. | 祭祀，供奉（古语）;  显露，暴露(古语/罕用);  玷污, 弄脏(古语/罕用) | To offer or sacrifice something, especially to a god; to display or reveal something publicly; to stain or sully something. |  He bloted the altar with the blood of the sacrifice. 他用祭品的鲜血涂抹祭坛。 | 近义词：sacrifice, offer, immolate, expose, reveal, stain, sully | 反义词：conceal, hide, cleanse, purify | 词性: 动词


词源:
From Middle English *bloten, from Old English *blōtan, from Proto-Germanic *blōtaną (“to sacrifice, worship”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰlew- (“to blow, swell, bloom”).  Cognate with Old Norse blóta (“to sacrifice”), Old High German bluotan (“to sacrifice, worship”), Gothic 𐌱𐌻𐍉𐍄𐌰𐌽 (blōtan, “to worship”).
