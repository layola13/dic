lunchers:/ˈlʌntʃərz/
n.
吃午餐的人;午餐者
People who eat lunch.
The restaurant was crowded with lunchers.  这家餐馆挤满了吃午餐的人。
The lunchers enjoyed the sunny patio. 用餐者很喜欢阳光明媚的露台。

近义词: diners, patrons, customers
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑非用餐时间的人，例如：breakfasters, dinner-goers)
词性: 名词(plural noun)


词源:  From "lunch" + "-er" (a suffix forming nouns denoting a person or thing that performs a specified action).  "Lunch" itself comes from the shortened form of "luncheon," which has roots in the Spanish word "lonja" (slice of ham) and ultimately the Latin "lumbus" (loin).
