Overlaxness itself refers to a state of being excessively lax or negligent. | The overlaxness's consequences were dire for the company.  公司的过度松懈导致了可怕的后果。 | Synonyms: negligence's, carelessness's, laxity's | Antonyms: diligence's, strictness's, meticulousness's | noun


Etymology:

"Overlaxness" is formed by combining the prefix "over-" (meaning excessive) with the adjective "lax" (meaning loose, slack, or negligent) and the suffix "-ness" (used to form nouns from adjectives).  The apostrophe and "s" indicate possession.  "Lax" ultimately derives from the Latin word "laxus."
