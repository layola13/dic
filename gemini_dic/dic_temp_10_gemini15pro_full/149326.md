pacemaking:/ˈpeɪsmeɪkɪŋ/
noun
起搏；领跑；标兵作用
The act of setting the pace, especially in a race or other competition, or the act of regulating the heart's rhythm.
The marathon runner's pacemaking was crucial for his team's success.  这位马拉松运动员的领跑对他的团队的成功至关重要。
The doctor adjusted the settings on the patient's pacemaker to improve their pacemaking. 医生调整了病人心脏起搏器的设置，以改善他们的起搏。
Synonyms: pacing, leading, setting the pace
Antonyms: following, trailing
n.

pacemakings:/ˈpeɪsmeɪkɪŋz/
noun
(通常指复数)起搏活动；领跑策略
Multiple instances of setting the pace, or regulating heart rhythms.  This usually refers to multiple occurrences or strategies.
The coach analyzed the pacemakings of the different runners to devise a winning strategy. 教练分析了不同跑步者的起搏策略，以制定获胜策略。
The researchers are studying different pacemakings to optimize the performance of artificial hearts. 研究人员正在研究不同的起搏活动，以优化人工心脏的性能。
Synonyms: pacings, leadings
Antonyms: followings, trailings
n.
