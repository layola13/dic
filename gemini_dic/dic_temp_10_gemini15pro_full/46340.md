dacoit:/dəˈkɔɪt/| n.|土匪，强盗|A member of a band of armed robbers, especially in India or Burma (Myanmar).|The dacoits terrorized the villages, stealing livestock and valuables. 土匪恐吓村庄，偷窃牲畜和贵重物品。|Synonyms: bandit, robber, brigand, outlaw, highwayman|Antonyms: lawman, police officer|noun
