pacemaker: /ˈpeɪsmeɪkər/ (US) /ˈpeɪsmeɪkə/ (UK)| n.| 起搏器；领跑者；标兵|A device that uses electrical impulses to regulate the heart rhythm or a person or thing that sets the pace in something.| The doctor implanted a pacemaker in his chest.  医生在他的胸腔里植入了一个起搏器。|  He was a pacemaker in the marathon. 他是马拉松比赛的领跑者。| synonyms: pacesetter, leader, regulator | antonyms: follower, laggard | noun
