muriform:/ˈmjʊərɪfɔːrm/
adjective
多室的，隔成多室的，砖墙状的
Divided into chambers by intersecting walls, resembling the bricks of a wall.
The fungus has muriform spores.  这种真菌有砖墙状的孢子。
