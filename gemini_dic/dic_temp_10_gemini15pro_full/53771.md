discommendable:/ˌdɪskəˈmɛndəbl/
adjective
不值得推荐的；应受谴责的
Unworthy of recommendation; deserving of blame.
His discommendable behavior led to his dismissal. 他的不当行为导致他被解雇。
Synonyms: reprehensible, objectionable, blameworthy, censurable
Antonyms: commendable, laudable, praiseworthy
词性: adjective
