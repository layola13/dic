circumnavigators:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈnævɪɡeɪtərz/ /ˌsɝkəmˈnævɪɡeɪtərz/ | n. |环球航行者，环球航海家| People who travel completely around something, especially the world, by ship or aircraft. | Ferdinand Magellan and his crew were the first circumnavigators of the globe. 费尔南多·麦哲伦和他的船员是世界上第一批完成环球航行的人。

The circumnavigators faced many challenges during their long journey. 这些环球航海家在漫长的旅程中面临许多挑战。

| 近义词：globetrotters, world travelers | 反义词: landlubbers | 词性: noun
