gravitate-toward:/ˈɡrævɪteɪt twɔːrd/
verb
被吸引；倾向于；受吸引而转向
to be drawn toward something; to tend toward something; to move toward something as if by an invisible force
Young people tend to gravitate toward the cities in search of work. 年轻人往往会为了找工作而涌向城市。
The conversation gravitated toward politics.  谈话转向了政治。
synonyms: be drawn to, be attracted to, incline towards, tend towards, head for, drift towards, be pulled towards
antonyms: avoid, shun, repel, repulse
verb
