hypodicrotic:/ˌhaɪpəʊdaɪˈkrɒtɪk/
adjective
低切迹脉的
Relating to or characterized by a hypodicrotic pulse, where a small secondary wave appears on the descending part of the main pulse wave.
The patient's hypodicrotic pulse suggested a problem with their aortic valve.  病人低切迹脉搏提示主动脉瓣有问题。
Near synonyms:  dicrotic (though not precisely the same, as dicrotic implies two distinct peaks)
Antonyms:  monocrotic (having a single pulse wave)
词性: adjective
