dreichly:/ˈdriːxli/| adv.|沉闷地，阴郁地，凄凉地|Drearily; gloomily; dismally.  Characterized by dreary, overcast, and typically wet weather.|The rain fell dreichly all day.  雨沉闷地下了一整天。|近义词：drearily, gloomily, dismally, miserably|反义词：brightly, cheerfully, sunnily|词性:副词

Etymology:
From Scots *dreich* ("dreary, dull, tedious"), from Middle English *dreri* ("sad, sorrowful"), from Old English *drēorig* ("sad, sorrowful, mournful, bloody").  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *drēuzijaną* ("to become troubled").
