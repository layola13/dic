arapaima:/ˌærəˈpaɪmə/ | n. | 巨骨舌鱼 | A large South American freshwater fish (Arapaima gigas) with bony scales and a primitive lung, able to breathe air. It is one of the largest freshwater fish in the world. |  The arapaima is a prized game fish and is also farmed for food. 巨骨舌鱼是一种珍贵的游钓鱼，也有人工养殖以供食用。| synonyms: pirarucu | antonyms: N/A | noun
