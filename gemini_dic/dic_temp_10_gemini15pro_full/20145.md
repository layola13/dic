bipetalous:/baɪˈpɛtəlʌs/
adjective
二瓣的；有两花瓣的
Having two petals.
The rare orchid was bipetalous, a characteristic that made it highly sought after by collectors.  这种稀有兰花是二瓣的，这一特征使其成为收藏家们梦寐以求的珍品。
The botanist identified the flower as bipetalous due to its two distinct petals.  植物学家根据其两个不同的花瓣将这朵花鉴定为二瓣的。
Synonym: dipetalous
Antonym: polypetalous
词性: adjective
