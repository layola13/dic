nonperformer:/ˌnɒnpərˈfɔːmə(r)/ /ˌnɑːnpərˈfɔːrmər/| n. |不履行义务者；不称职的人；表现不佳者|A person, especially an employee, who does not perform their duties or meet expectations.| The company had to let go of several nonperformers due to budget cuts.  这家公司由于预算削减不得不解雇几名不称职的员工。|Synonyms: underachiever, failure, slacker, deadbeat |Antonyms: achiever, high-performer, star, success|Noun

nonperformer:/ˌnɒnpərˈfɔːmə(r)/ /ˌnɑːnpərˈfɔːrmər/| adj.(less common) |表现不佳的；不成功的|Not performing well or successfully.|  The nonperformer investment was quickly liquidated. 表现不佳的投资很快就被清算了。|Synonyms: unsuccessful, unproductive, ineffective|Antonyms: successful, productive, effective|Adjective
