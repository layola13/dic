impenetrably:/ɪmˈpɛnɪtrəbli/
adverb
无法穿透地；难以理解地
in a way that cannot be penetrated, either physically or mentally.
The jungle was impenetrably dense.  这片丛林密不透风。
His motives were impenetrably obscure. 他的动机令人费解。
近义词:inaccessibly, imperviously, unfathomably
反义词:permeably, understandably, clearly
词性:adverb
