cynanthropy:/saɪˈnænθrəpi/ | n. |  犬狂症; 变狼妄想症 | The belief that one is a dog or wolf, or that one can transform into such an animal. | He suffered from cynanthropy and believed he could change into a wolf at night. 他患有犬狂症，相信自己可以在晚上变成狼。| Synonym: lycanthropy | Antonym: N/A | Noun
