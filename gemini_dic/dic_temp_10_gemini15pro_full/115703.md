marginals:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪnəlz/ | n. | 边缘事物；边缘人；次要的东西；很少的利润；（议会选举中）边缘席位|Things, people, or activities that are not considered important or mainstream; a small profit; a constituency held by a narrow margin |The library has a section for marginal books that are rarely borrowed. 图书馆有一个专门放很少被借阅的边缘书籍的区域。|近义词：peripheral, unimportant, insignificant, minor, borderline|反义词：central, important, significant, major|词性:noun

marginals:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪnəlz/ | adj. | 边缘的；很少的；微不足道的|Relating to or situated at the edge or margin; small in amount or effect; insignificant|The company made only marginal profits last year. 公司去年只赚取了微薄的利润。|近义词：peripheral, unimportant, insignificant, minor, borderline|反义词：central, important, significant, major|词性:adjective
