educating:/ˈɛdʒʊkeɪtɪŋ/
v.|教育; 教导; 培养; 训练; 使(某人)明了(某事);|The government wants to educate young people about the dangers of drugs. 政府想教育年轻人吸毒的危害。|近义词:teaching, training, instructing, schooling|反义词:misinforming, misleading|词性:动词(present participle of educate)
Etymology: From Latin educatus, past participle of educare (“to bring up, rear, educate”), from ex- (“out”) + ducere (“to lead”).
