audubon:/ˈɔːdəbɑːn/ |n.|奥杜邦，(尤指)美国鸟类学家约翰·詹姆斯·奥杜邦; 奥杜邦协会(美国的一个鸟类保护组织)|John James Audubon was a famous ornithologist. 约翰·詹姆斯·奥杜邦是一位著名的鸟类学家。| The Audubon Society protects birds and their habitats. 奥杜邦协会保护鸟类及其栖息地。|近义词: ornithologist, naturalist |反义词: N/A |词性: 名词

词源: Named after John James Audubon (1785-1851), a French-American ornithologist, naturalist, and painter noted for his studies and detailed illustrations of North American birds.
