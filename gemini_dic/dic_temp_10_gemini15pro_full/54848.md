displaces:/dɪˈspleɪsɪz/
verb
取代，移置，迫使…离开家园
forces something or someone out of its usual or original position
The rising floodwaters displaced thousands of people.  上涨的洪水迫使数千人离开家园。
dislodge, replace, remove, supplant
retain, keep, hold
v.
