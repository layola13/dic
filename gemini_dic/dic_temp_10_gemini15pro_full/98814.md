intellectual property: /ˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəl ˈprɒpəti/ (British) /ˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəl ˈprɑːpərti/ (American) | n. | 知识产权 |  A legal right granted to the creators of original works, inventions, and other intellectual creations, allowing them to control how their work is used and distributed. |  The company is aggressively protecting its intellectual property.  这家公司正在积极保护其知识产权。| synonyms: copyright, patent, trademark | antonyms:  public domain | noun

intellectual properties: /ˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəl ˈprɒpətiz/ (British) /ˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəl ˈprɑːpərtiz/ (American)| n. | 知识产权（复数） |  The plural form of "intellectual property," referring to multiple instances of legally protected creations.  |  The university's intellectual properties are a valuable asset. 这所大学的知识产权是一笔宝贵的资产。 | synonyms: copyrights, patents, trademarks | antonyms: public domain (singular doesn't truly apply to multiple entities) | noun
