词源：
Named after Edmond Hoyle (1672-1769), an English writer who became an authority on card games and other social games. He published a book of rules for whist in 1742, which became highly influential and led to his name becoming synonymous with rules and proper conduct in games.
以埃德蒙·霍伊尔（1672-1769）的名字命名，他是一位英国作家，是纸牌游戏和其他社交游戏的权威。 他在 1742 年出版了一本惠斯特规则书，这本书影响很大，并导致他的名字成为游戏中规则和正确行为的代名词。
