additives:/ˈædɪtɪvz/| n.|添加剂|Substances added to something in small quantities, typically to improve or preserve it.|Many processed foods contain additives. 许多加工食品都含有添加剂。

Food additives can enhance flavor and extend shelf life. 食品添加剂可以增强口味并延长保质期。

|近义词：adjuncts, supplements, ingredients|反义词:|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *additīvus*, from Latin additus (past participle of addere ‘to add’), from ad- ‘to’ + dare ‘to give’.  Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European root *dō- "to give".
