nonremittably:/ˌnɑːnrɪˈmɪtəbli/
adverb
不可饶恕地，不间断地
in a way that cannot be remitted or forgiven; constantly, incessantly
The pain continued nonremittably throughout the night.  这疼痛整夜不停地持续着。
近义词:incessantly, constantly, unremittingly, perpetually
反义词:intermittently, occasionally, sporadically
词性:adverb

Etymology:
From non- + remit + -able + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Remit" comes from the Latin "remittere," meaning "to send back, relax, slacken."  "-able" forms an adjective meaning "capable of being." "-ly" forms an adverb.
