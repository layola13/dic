melburnians:/melˈbɜːrniənz/
noun
墨尔本人
People who live in or are from Melbourne, Australia.
Melburnians are known for their love of coffee. 墨尔本人以热爱咖啡而闻名。
Many Melburnians commute to work by tram. 许多墨尔本人乘坐电车上班。
synonyms: Melbourne residents, Melbourne people
antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider residents of other cities, e.g., Sydneysiders)
