nodules:/ˈnɒdjuːlz/ (US) /ˈnɒdʒuːlz/ (UK)| n. |结节; 小瘤; 小块|A small rounded lump or swelling, especially an abnormal one in a bodily tissue or organ. |The doctor found nodules on her thyroid gland. 医生在她的甲状腺上发现了结节。

The plant's roots had nodules containing nitrogen-fixing bacteria.  这种植物的根部有含有固氮细菌的结节。

近义词: lumps, bumps, swellings, growths
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) smoothness, evenness
词性: Noun
