antienthusiasts:/ˌæntiɪnˈθuːziæsts/
|n.|反热情者；缺乏热情的人|People who are against or lacking enthusiasm; those who oppose or dampen the enthusiasm of others.|The antienthusiasts in the meeting quickly shot down every new proposal.  会议上的反热情者迅速否决了每一项新提议。|The project was met with resistance from the antienthusiasts within the company.  这个项目遭到了公司内部反热情者的抵制。|近义词：skeptics, cynics, pessimists|反义词：enthusiasts, supporters, zealots|词性:noun

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek "anti-")
enthusiast (noun): a person filled with enthusiasm (from Greek "enthousiasmos" - divine inspiration)
