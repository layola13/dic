carolinian:/ˌkærəˈlɪniən/ | adj. n.|卡罗来纳的；卡罗来纳州居民|Relating to or characteristic of North or South Carolina, or its people.  A native or inhabitant of North or South Carolina.|例句：The Carolinian landscape is beautiful. 卡罗来纳州的风景很美。|近义词：Southern, Southeastern (for the region)|反义词: Northern (generally, not specific)|词性:形容词,名词
