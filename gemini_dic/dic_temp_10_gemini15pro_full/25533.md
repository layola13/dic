bullshitted:/ˈbʊlʃɪtɪd/
|v.|胡说，瞎扯，欺骗| to talk nonsense; to lie or deceive someone.|He bullshitted his way through the interview. 他在面试中胡诌一通。|Synonyms: lied, deceived, fabricated, prevaricated|Antonyms: told the truth, was honest|verb (past tense)

bullshits:/ˈbʊlʃɪts/

bullshit:/ˈbʊlʃɪt/
|n. v.|废话，胡说；胡说八道|nonsense, lies; to talk nonsense|That's a load of bullshit. 那是一派胡言。 He's just bullshitting you. 他只是在胡扯。|Synonyms: nonsense, rubbish, lies, claptrap|Antonyms: truth, facts, reality|noun, verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "bull" (possibly referring to the pompous pronouncements of a bull) + "shit" (excrement), implying worthless or deceptive talk.  The term gained popularity in the 20th century.
