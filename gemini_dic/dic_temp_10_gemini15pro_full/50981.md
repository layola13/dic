desirably:/dɪˈzaɪərəbli/
adv.
理想地，合意地
in a desirable manner; preferably
It is desirably located near the city center. 它理想地坐落在市中心附近。
近义词: preferably, ideally, favorably
反义词: undesirably, unfavorably, disadvantageously
词性: 副词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *desirabli, from Old French desirable, from Late Latin dēsirābilis (“desirable”), from Latin dēsīderāre (“to long for, desire”), from de- (“down, from”) + sīderāre (“to observe the stars, consider, expect”), from sīdus, sīderis (“star, constellation”).
