bevelings:/ˈbɛvəlɪŋz/
n.
斜角，斜面，倒角
The bevelings on the edges of the table prevent splintering.
桌子的边缘的斜角防止了碎片的产生。
The precise bevelings on the crystal caught the light beautifully.
水晶上精确的斜面漂亮地捕捉到了光线。
近义词: chamfers, bevels, slopes
反义词: rounds, curves
