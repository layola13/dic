gibbously:/ˈɡɪbəsli/| adv.|凸状地，隆起地|More than half but less than fully illuminated, as the moon between first quarter and full moon or between full moon and last quarter.  (指月亮)凸圆的，盈凸的|The moon shone gibbously in the night sky.  月亮在夜空中凸圆地照耀着。|近义词：convexly, roundedly|反义词：concavely|词性:副词

gibbous:/ˈɡɪbəs/| adj.|凸出的，隆起的|Swelling out on one side; convex. |The gibbous moon hung like a silver coin in the sky. 凸圆的月亮像一枚银币一样挂在天空。|近义词：convex, protuberant|反义词：concave|词性:形容词

词源:
From Late Latin gibbosus (“humpbacked”), from Latin gibbus (“hump, hunch”).
