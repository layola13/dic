apanage:/ˈæpənɪdʒ/ /ˈæpəneɪdʒ/| n.|俸禄，津贴，特权|Something, such as the prerogatives or income, that belongs to a specific position or office by right or tradition; provision for the support of younger members of a ruling family.|The prince's apanage included a castle and a sizable estate. 王子的俸禄包括一座城堡和一大片地产。|Synonyms: allowance, stipend, endowment, prerogative, privilege|Antonyms: debt, liability|noun
