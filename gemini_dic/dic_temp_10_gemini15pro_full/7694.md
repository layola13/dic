anomaly:/əˈnɒməli/ /əˈnɑːməli/| n. |异常，反常；不规则；异常的人或物|Something that deviates from what is standard, normal, or expected.|The student's poor performance on the test was an anomaly, as he usually scored very high.|近义词：abnormality, irregularity, deviation, exception, oddity|反义词：normality, regularity, conformity|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *anōmalia*, from Ancient Greek ἀνωμαλία (anōmalía, “unevenness, irregularity”), from ἀνώμαλος (anṓmalos, “uneven, irregular”), from ἀν- (an-, “not, without”) + ὁμαλός (homalós, “even, level”).
