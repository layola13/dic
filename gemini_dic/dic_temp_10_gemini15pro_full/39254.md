congregationalism:/ˌkɑːŋɡrɪˈɡeɪʃənəlɪzəm/
n.
公理会制，会众制
A system of church governance in which each local church congregation is independent and self-governing.
The church follows the principles of congregationalism.  这个教会遵循公理会制原则。
Congregationalism is a key feature of many Protestant churches.  公理会制是许多新教教会的一个关键特征。
autonomy, independence, self-governance
hierarchy, episcopacy
noun
