in-bad:/ɪnˈbæd/ | adj. | 处于困境；关系不好；失宠；拖欠；| In a difficult or unfavorable situation; having a poor relationship; out of favor; owing money. | He's in bad with his boss after missing the deadline. 他因为错过了截止日期而与老板关系不好。| 近义词:troubled, strained, indebted | 反义词:favorable, good, solvent | 词性:形容词

词源:  "in" (in or into) + "bad" (unfavorable). This phrase functions as a compound adjective.
