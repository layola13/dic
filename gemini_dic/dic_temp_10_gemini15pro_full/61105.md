elocutionist:/ˌɛləˈkjuːʃənɪst/
n.
演说家；雄辩家；朗诵家
A person who is skilled in the art of public speaking, especially one who delivers speeches with expressive delivery and clear articulation.
She was a renowned elocutionist, captivating audiences with her powerful voice and elegant gestures. 她是一位著名的演说家，以其有力的声音和优雅的姿态吸引着观众。
The elocutionist delivered a moving speech about the importance of education.  这位演说家发表了一场关于教育重要性的动人演讲。

近义词:speaker, orator, rhetorician
反义词:mumbler, stammerer
词性:noun
