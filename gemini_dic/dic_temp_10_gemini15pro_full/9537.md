antisensitizing:/ˌæntiˈsɛnsɪtaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
抗敏的，脱敏的
Reducing sensitivity or preventing sensitization.  This term describes something that works against the process of becoming sensitive to something, whether it's a physical substance, an emotion, or an idea.
This cream contains antisensitizing ingredients to soothe irritated skin.  这款面霜含有抗敏成分，可以舒缓受刺激的皮肤。
The therapist used antisensitizing techniques to help the patient cope with their trauma.  治疗师使用脱敏技术来帮助患者应对他们的创伤。
近义词: desensitizing, hyposensitizing
反义词: sensitizing
词性: adjective
