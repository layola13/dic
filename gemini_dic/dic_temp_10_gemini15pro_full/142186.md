ornamented: /ˈɔːrnəmɛntɪd/ | adj. | 装饰的，华丽的 | Decorated, adorned, embellished, typically with elaborate or complex designs. | The queen wore an ornamented gown. 女王穿着一件华丽的礼服。 | The ornamented Christmas tree sparkled with lights. 装饰的圣诞树闪闪发光。| 近义词: decorated, adorned, embellished, elaborate, ornate | 反义词: plain, simple, unadorned, undecorated | 词性: 形容词
词源: From the verb "ornament" + "-ed" (past participle suffix).  "Ornament" comes from Old French "ornement," from Latin "ornamentum,"  meaning "equipment, adornment,"  derived from "ornare" meaning "to equip, adorn, furnish."
