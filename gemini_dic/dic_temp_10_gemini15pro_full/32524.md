Chinua: /ˈtʃɪn.wɑː/ | n. |奇努阿 (人名)| A masculine given name of Igbo origin, meaning "God's blessing" or "may God fight for us".  It is most famously associated with Chinua Achebe, the Nigerian novelist. |例句：Chinua Achebe wrote Things Fall Apart. 奇努阿·阿切贝写了《瓦解》。

例句：My friend's name is Chinua. 我的朋友名叫奇努阿。|近义词: (As a name, it doesn't have direct synonyms, but similar Igbo names exist) |反义词: (Names generally don't have antonyms) |词性: Noun (proper noun)
