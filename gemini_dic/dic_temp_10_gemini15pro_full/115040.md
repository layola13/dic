mandoline:/ˈmændəlɪn/ /ˈmændəlˌiːn/| n.|曼陀林（一种乐器）; 曼陀林式切片机（一种厨房工具）|A musical instrument of the lute family with a pear-shaped body and a fretted neck.  A kitchen utensil consisting of a flat frame with adjustable blades for slicing vegetables. |She played a beautiful melody on her mandoline. 她用她的曼陀林演奏了一曲优美的旋律。

|The chef used a mandoline to thinly slice the potatoes. 厨师用曼陀林把土豆切成薄片。|近义词：(for instrument) lute, (for utensil) slicer, vegetable slicer|反义词: None|词性: Noun
