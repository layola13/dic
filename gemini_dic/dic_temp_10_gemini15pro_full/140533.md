oleography: /ˌəʊliˈɒɡrəfi/ /ˌoʊliˈɑːɡrəfi/
noun
油画复制品，彩色石印画
A print resembling an oil painting, produced by chromolithography.
The oleography of the Madonna hung above the fireplace.  油画复制品的圣母像挂在壁炉上方。

Oleography was a popular way to reproduce famous paintings in the 19th century.  在19世纪，彩色石印画是复制名画的流行方式。
