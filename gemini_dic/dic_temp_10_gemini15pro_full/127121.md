natively:/ˈneɪtɪvli/| adv.|本地地，天然地，天生|In a way that is natural or instinctive. She speaks English natively. 她以英语为母语。|Synonym: naturally, innately, inherently|Antonym: unnaturally, artificially|adverb
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *nativus, from Latin natīvus (“native, born in a place, natural”), from natus, perfect passive participle of nāscor (“be born”).
