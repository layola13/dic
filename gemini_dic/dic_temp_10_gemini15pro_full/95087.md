inalienably:/ɪnˈeɪliənəbli/
adv.
不可剥夺地
Not able to be taken away or given away by the possessor.
The right to vote is inalienably yours. 你拥有不可剥夺的投票权。
Synonyms:untransferable, nontransferable, intransferable, unassignable
Antonyms:alienable, transferable
词性:副词
