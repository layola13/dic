mislactation:/ˌmɪslækˈteɪʃən/
n.
分泌异常的乳汁；不正常的泌乳
The secretion of milk of abnormal composition or quality.
The doctor diagnosed her with mislactation due to hormonal imbalance.  医生诊断她由于荷尔蒙失调导致泌乳异常。
She experienced mislactation after giving birth prematurely. 她早产后经历了泌乳异常。
近义词:galactorrhea, abnormal lactation
反义词:normal lactation
词性:noun
