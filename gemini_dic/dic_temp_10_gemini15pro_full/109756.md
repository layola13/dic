lever:/ˈliːvər/ /ˈlevər/| n. |杠杆；控制杆；手段，途径|A rigid bar resting on a pivot, used to help move a heavy or firmly fixed load with one end when pressure is applied to the other.|She used a lever to pry open the crate. 她用杠杆撬开了板条箱。|Near synonyms: bar, crowbar, handle, grip|Antonyms:  (For the physical object, no direct antonym. For the figurative meaning, perhaps "obstacle" or "impediment")|Noun


levier:/ˈliːvər/ /ˈlevər/| n. |（法语）杠杆；控制杆；手段，途径|(French) Same meaning as the English word lever.|Il a utilisé un levier pour ouvrir la caisse. (He used a lever to open the crate.)|Near synonyms: (French) barre, levier, manche|Antonyms: (For the physical object, no direct antonym. For the figurative meaning, perhaps "obstacle" or "impediment")|Noun


(Note: "leviers" is simply the plural form of "lever" or "levier".)
