degum:/diːˈɡʌm/ | v. | 脱胶，去除树胶 | To remove gum or gummy substances from something, especially silk or other natural fibers. | The silk is degummed before it is dyed.  丝绸在染色前要先脱胶。

The raw silk needs to be degummed to improve its luster. 生丝需要脱胶以提高光泽。 | 近义词: scour, clean | 反义词: gum | 词性: 动词
