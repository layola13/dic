atomized:/ˈætəˌmaɪzd/ | adj. | 原子化的；雾化的；分散的 | Reduced to atoms or particles; sprayed as a fine mist; divided into many small units or parts.  |  例句：The perfume was atomized into the air. 香水被喷成雾状散布在空气中。 | 近义词：fragmented, dispersed, scattered, sprayed, vaporized | 反义词：unified, consolidated, combined, concentrated | 词性：形容词

atomized:/ˈætəˌmaɪzd/ | v. | 原子化；雾化；分散 | To reduce to atoms or particles; to spray as a fine mist; to divide into many small units or parts. | 例句：The paint was atomized for an even coat.  油漆被雾化以获得均匀的涂层。| 近义词：fragment, disperse, scatter, spray, vaporize | 反义词：unify, consolidate, combine, concentrate | 词性：动词
