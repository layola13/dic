maturest:/məˈtʊərɪst/ /məˈtʃʊrɪst/
adj.|最成熟的|Superlative form of mature: most mature.  Having completed natural growth and development.|The maturest of the apples were picked for the pie.  最成熟的苹果被摘下来做馅饼。|近义词:ripened, developed, grown, complete|反义词:immature, undeveloped, green, raw|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *matur*, from Old French *matur, meür*, from Latin *maturus* (“ripe, timely, early”), from *Proto-Italic *maturos*, from *Proto-Indo-European *h₂eméh₂-tru-* (“at the right time”). Cognate with Spanish and Portuguese *maduro*.
