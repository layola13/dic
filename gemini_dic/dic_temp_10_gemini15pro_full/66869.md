expectingly:/ɪkˈspektɪŋli/
adverb|期待地，期望地|In a manner that shows anticipation or expectation.|She looked at him expectingly, waiting for his response. 她期待地看着他，等待他的回应。|例句：The children waited expectingly for the arrival of Santa Claus. 孩子们期待着圣诞老人的到来。

例句：He stared expectingly at the closed door, hoping she would appear. 他期待地盯着紧闭的门，希望她会出现。

近义词：hopefully, anticipatively, eagerly
反义词：apathetically, indifferently
词性：副词

词源：From "expecting," the present participle of the verb "expect," ultimately from the Latin "expectare" (to look out for, await).
