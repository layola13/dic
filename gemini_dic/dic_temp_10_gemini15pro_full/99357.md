interdependability:/ˌɪntərˌdɪˌpɛndəˈbɪlɪti/| n. |相互依存性，互相依赖性|The quality or condition of being interdependent; mutual dependence.|The global economy is characterized by the increasing interdependability of nations. 全球经济的特点是各国之间日益增长的相互依存性。|Synonyms: interdependence, interreliance, reciprocity, symbiosis|Antonyms: independence, self-sufficiency, autonomy|noun

interdependent:/ˌɪntərˌdɪˈpɛndənt/| adj. |相互依存的，互相依赖的|Mutually dependent; reliant on each other.|The success of the project depends on the interdependent efforts of all team members.  项目的成功取决于所有团队成员相互依存的努力。|Synonyms: interconnected, interrelated, interactive, symbiotic|Antonyms: independent, separate, autonomous|adjective
