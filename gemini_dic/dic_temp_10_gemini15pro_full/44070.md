crinkliest:/ˈkrɪŋkliɪst/
adjective
最皱的，最起皱的
superlative adjective of crinkly: having many folds or wrinkles
例句：The crinkliest leaf I've ever seen fell from the oldest tree in the park. 我见过的最皱的叶子从公园里最古老的树上掉了下来。

例句：Her grandmother's face was the crinkliest, etched with a lifetime of smiles and worries. 她祖母的脸是最皱的，上面刻满了毕生的笑容和忧虑。
近义词：most wrinkled, most creased
反义词：smoothest, flattest
词性: adjective
词源: From crinkle + -y + -est.  Crinkle likely imitative of the sound of something crinkling.
