nulling:/ˈnʌlɪŋ/
n.
消除，无效化，调零
The act of reducing something to zero or making it ineffective.
The astronomer detected a planet through the nulling of starlight. 天文学家通过消除星光探测到一颗行星。

v.
使无效，消除
Making something ineffective or reducing it to zero.
The software update nulled the previous version's features. 软件更新使先前版本的功能无效。

近义词: canceling, negating, voiding
反义词: activating, enabling, validating
词性: noun, verb (present participle)
