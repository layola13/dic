biferous:/ˈbɪfərəs/
adjective
一年开两次花的；一年结果两次的
Bearing fruit twice a year; flowering twice a year.
例句：Some citrus trees are biferous, producing two crops annually. 一些柑橘树一年开两次花，结两次果。

例句：Biferous plants are highly valued in agriculture for their increased yield.  一年两次结果的植物在农业中因其产量高而备受重视。
