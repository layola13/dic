misrepeat:/ˌmɪsrɪˈpiːt/ | v. | 错误地重复；说错；背错 | To repeat something incorrectly, often a word or phrase. |  The actor misrepeated his line and had to start the scene again. 演员背错了台词，不得不重新开始这场戏。

The child misrepeated the teacher's instructions and got the answer wrong. 孩子复述老师的指示时说错了，所以答案错了。 | 近义词: misspeak, mispronounce, stumble, fluff | 反义词: repeat correctly, articulate clearly | 词性: 动词
