opposed: /əˈpoʊzd/ (US) /əˈpəʊzd/ (UK)|  adj. | 对立的，相反的 | Situated or placed opposite or facing each other; contrasting or conflicting. | 例句：They sat on opposed benches in the courtroom. 他们在法庭上坐在相对的长凳上。 | 例句：The two candidates hold opposed views on the issue. 两位候选人对这个问题持相反的观点。 | 近义词：opposite, contrasting, conflicting | 反义词：similar, alike, congruent | 词性:形容词



ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *oppositus*, past participle of *opponere* ("to oppose"), from *ob-* ("against") + *ponere* ("to place").
