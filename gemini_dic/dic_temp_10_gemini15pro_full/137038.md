nonstereotypic:/ˌnɒnˌstɛrɪəˈtɪpɪk/
adjective
非刻板印象的
Not conforming to a fixed or general pattern; not stereotypical.
The advertisement presented a nonstereotypic image of working mothers. 这则广告展现了职业母亲的非刻板印象。
She gave a nonstereotypic portrayal of a teenage girl. 她对一个十几岁女孩的刻画是非刻板印象的。
近义词: atypical, unconventional, unorthodox, individualistic
反义词: stereotypical, conventional, typical, traditional
词性: adjective
