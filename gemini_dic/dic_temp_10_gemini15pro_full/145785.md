overdeal:/ˌoʊvərˈdiːl/ /ˌəʊvəˈdiːl/| v. |过度发牌；发牌过多|To deal more cards than are required in a card game.|He overdealt in the poker game, giving one player an extra card. 他在扑克游戏中发了太多的牌，给一个玩家多发了一张。|Synonyms: misdeal, overhand|Antonyms: underdeal|verb
