come-forward:/kʌm ˈfɔːrwərd/| phrasal verb |站出来，挺身而出，自告奋勇|To present oneself or step forward, especially to offer help or information. |例句：Several witnesses have come forward with new information.  几位目击者站出来提供了新的信息。|例句：The police are appealing for anyone with information to come forward.  警方呼吁任何知情人士站出来提供信息。|近义词：volunteer, step forward, speak up|反义词：hold back, stay silent|词性：动词短语

come-forward:/kʌm ˈfɔːrwərd/| phrasal verb |被提出，被提交|To be presented or submitted, often for consideration. |例句：A new proposal has come forward for improving public transport. 一项改进公共交通的新提案已被提出。|例句：Several interesting ideas came forward during the brainstorming session.  在头脑风暴会议期间，提出了一些有趣的想法。|近义词：be proposed, be submitted|反义词：be withdrawn, be retracted|词性：动词短语
