hypnology:/hɪpˈnɒlədʒi/ /hɪpˈnɑːlədʒi/
n.|催眠术；催眠学|The study of sleep and hypnosis.  It encompasses the induction of hypnosis, its phenomena, its therapeutic applications, and its relationship to other psychological processes.|He's been studying hypnology for years and is considered an expert in the field. 他研究催眠术多年，被认为是该领域的专家。|Synonyms: hypnotism, mesmerism|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider concepts like  wakefulness, alertness)|Noun
