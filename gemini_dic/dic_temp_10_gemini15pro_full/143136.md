outcatch:/ˌaʊtˈkætʃ/ | v. | 胜过，超过（在捕鱼、狩猎等方面）| To surpass or exceed someone in catching fish, game, etc. |  He outcaught his brother by three fish. 他比他弟弟多钓了三条鱼。| 近义词：outdo, surpass, excel | 反义词: lose to, be surpassed | 词性:动词

outcatch:/ˌaʊtˈkætʃ/ | n. | 超过的量，胜过的量（尤指捕鱼、狩猎）| The amount by which one person surpasses another in catching fish, game, etc. | His outcatch was impressive, a full basket more than anyone else. 他的收获令人印象深刻，比任何人都多满满一篮子。 | 近义词：excess, surplus | 反义词: deficit, shortfall | 词性:名词
