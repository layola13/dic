osteocranium: /ˌɒstiəʊˈkreɪniəm/ (US) /ˌɒstiəʊˈkreɪnɪəm/ (UK)| n. | 骨颅，脑颅 |The part of the skull enclosing the brain, composed of bony plates.  The osteocranium forms the protective outer casing for the brain. | The developing osteocranium of the fetus is crucial for proper brain development.  胎儿的骨颅发育对大脑的正常发育至关重要。| synonym: neurocranium, braincase | antonym:  (No direct antonym, potentially viscerocranium - refers to the facial skeleton) | noun
