counter-attraction:/ˈkaʊntər əˈtrækʃn/ | n. | 对抗性吸引；反吸引力；竞争性吸引力 | Something that diverts attention or interest from something else; a competing attraction. | The new shopping mall proved a powerful counter-attraction to the traditional downtown stores.  这个新的购物中心证明了对传统市中心商店的强大对抗性吸引力。

The circus offered a counter-attraction to the dull routine of daily life. 马戏团为单调的日常生活提供了一种对抗性吸引力。| synonyms: alternative, rival, competition, diversion | antonyms:  main attraction, primary draw, focus | noun
