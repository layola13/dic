amaryllidaceous:/ˌæmərɪlɪˈdeɪʃəs/
adjective
石蒜科的;像石蒜科植物的
Relating to or belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family of plants, which includes daffodils, snowdrops, and amaryllis.
Example: The amaryllidaceous plants are known for their beautiful flowers.  石蒜科植物以其美丽的花朵而闻名。

Example:  Many amaryllidaceous species are popular garden plants. 许多石蒜科物种是受欢迎的园林植物。

Synonym:  liliaceous (somewhat outdated synonym, as classification has changed)
Antonym: (No direct antonym, as it refers to a specific plant family.)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin *Amaryllidaceae*, named after the type genus *Amaryllis*,  ultimately from the Greek name Ἀμαρυλλίς (Amaryllis), a country girl mentioned in Theocritus' Idylls, perhaps from ἀμάρυγμα (amárygma, "sparkle," "gleam").
