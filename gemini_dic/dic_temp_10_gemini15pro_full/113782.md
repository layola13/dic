macrospore:/ˈmækrəˌspɔːr/| n. |大孢子|A larger spore of a heterosporous plant, that develops into a female gametophyte.|The macrospore germinates to form the female gametophyte.|近义词:megaspore|反义词:microspore|词性:noun

词源:
From macro- + spore, from Greek *makros* "large" + *sporā* "seed, a sowing"
