chatterbox: /ˈtʃætərˌbɒks/ (UK) /ˈtʃætərˌbɑːks/ (US) | n. | 喋喋不休的人，话匣子 | A person who talks a lot. |  She's a real chatterbox and can talk for hours. 她真是个话匣子，能说上好几个小时。

My nephew is a little chatterbox, always full of stories. 我的侄子是个小话匣子，总是有说不完的故事。

近义词: talker, conversationalist, gabber

反义词: quiet person, introvert


词性: noun

词源: chatter (v.) + box (n.)  "Chatter" comes from the imitative word "chatter," referring to rapid, indistinct sounds, and "box" is used metaphorically to suggest a container for the chatter.
