monsters:/ˈmɑːnstərz/ (US) /ˈmɒnstəz/ (UK)| n.|怪物，妖怪|A creature that is typically large, ugly, and frightening. Also used figuratively to describe someone cruel or wicked. |The movie featured terrifying monsters.  这部电影里有可怕的怪物。|Synonyms: beast, creature, fiend, ogre, demon|Antonyms: hero, angel|Noun
