paisano:/paɪˈzɑːnoʊ/ (US), /paɪˈsɑːnəʊ/ (UK)| n.|同胞，老乡，同乡；(尤指)意大利裔美国人|A person from one's own country or region, especially used for Italian Americans.|例句：He greeted his paisano warmly.  他热情地问候了他的老乡。

例句：Many paisanos gathered for the annual festival. 许多同胞聚集在一起参加一年一度的节日。|近义词：compatriot, fellow countryman, landsman|反义词:foreigner, alien|词性:noun
