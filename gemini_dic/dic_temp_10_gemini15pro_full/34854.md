clinkery: /ˈklɪŋkəri/ | adj. | 似熔渣的；含熔渣的 | Resembling or containing clinker, which is a hard, stony residue formed during the burning of coal or other substances. | The clinkery remains of the fire were difficult to remove. 火留下的熔渣状残留物很难清除。| The path was covered in clinkery debris from the nearby factory. 小路被附近工厂排出的熔渣状残渣覆盖。 | 近义词：slaggy, cinderlike | 反义词：smooth, fine | 词性：形容词

词源: From "clinker" + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives). "Clinker" comes from Middle Dutch "klinke," meaning "brick," "tile," or "clinker."
