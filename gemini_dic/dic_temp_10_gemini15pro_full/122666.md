modularly:/ˈmɒdʒʊləli/ (US) /ˈmɒdjʊləli/ (UK)| adv.|模块化地，以模块方式|In a way that involves separate units or modules that can be combined or arranged in different ways. |The software is designed modularly, allowing users to customize their experience.  该软件采用模块化设计，允许用户自定义体验。|Example 2:  The curriculum is structured modularly, so students can choose the courses that best fit their needs.  课程结构是模块化的，因此学生可以选择最适合他们需要的课程。|Synonyms: component-wise, sectionally, piecemeal|Antonyms: integrally, monolithically|词性: 副词
