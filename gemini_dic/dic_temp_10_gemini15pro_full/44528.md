crossruff: /ˈkrɒsˌrʌf/ (British), /ˈkrɔːsˌrʌf/ (American) | n. v. |交叉将牌,交叉出牌 | In card games, especially whist and bridge, to trump a card in one hand with a trump from the other hand when the first hand has no cards of the suit led. |  He crossruffed the queen of hearts with the three of spades. 他用黑桃三交叉将了红桃Q。 |  trump, ruff | discard | noun, verb
