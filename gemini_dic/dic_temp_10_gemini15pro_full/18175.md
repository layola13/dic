belabour:/bɪˈleɪbər/ /biˈlābər/| vt.|抨击；痛打；反复讨论；赘述|To explain, criticize, or argue something more than is necessary.|He belaboured the point about the importance of hard work. 他反复强调努力工作的重要性。|近义词：overemphasize, harp on, dwell on, overwork,  labor, beat|反义词: understate, skim over, gloss over|词性:动词

belabour:/bɪˈleɪbər/ /biˈlābər/| vt.|痛打；|To attack someone physically.|The mugger belaboured the victim with a lead pipe. 抢劫犯用铅管猛击受害者。|近义词：beat, batter, thrash, pummel|反义词: caress, soothe|词性:动词
