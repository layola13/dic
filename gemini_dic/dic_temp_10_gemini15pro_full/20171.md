bipyramidal:/ˌbaɪpɪˈræmɪdəl/| adj.|双锥体的，双棱锥体的|Having the form of two pyramids placed base to base.|Phosphorus pentachloride has a bipyramidal molecular geometry. 五氯化磷具有双锥体分子几何形状。|Sulfur hexafluoride adopts a bipyramidal structure. 六氟化硫采用双锥体结构。|pyramidal, dipyramidal| - |adjective
