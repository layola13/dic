dioptric:/daɪˈɒptrɪk/ /daɪˈɑːptrɪk/| adj.|屈光的，折射的|Relating to the refraction of light, especially by the eye.|The dioptric strength of the lens is +2.00.  镜片的屈光度是+2.00。|Near synonyms: refractive, lenticular|Near antonyms: non-refractive|adjective

dioptre:/daɪˈɒptə(r)/ /daɪˈɑːptər/| n.|屈光度|A unit of measurement of the refractive power of a lens, equal to the reciprocal of the focal length in meters.|The ophthalmologist prescribed glasses with a dioptre of -1.5. 眼科医生开了-1.5屈光度的眼镜。|Near synonyms: diopter (American spelling)|No common antonym|noun
