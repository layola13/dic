oiliness:/ˈɔɪlinəs/| n.|油腻；油性|The oiliness of the food made me feel sick.  食物的油腻让我感到恶心。|例句：The oiliness of the skin can be controlled with proper skincare.  适当的护肤可以控制皮肤的油性。

The oiliness of the engine indicated a leak. 发动机的油腻表明有泄漏。|近义词：greasiness, slickness|反义词：dryness, aridity|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From oily + -ness.  Oily comes from Old English *ælig (attested only in ælige "oil").  The suffix -ness forms nouns meaning "state or quality of being."
