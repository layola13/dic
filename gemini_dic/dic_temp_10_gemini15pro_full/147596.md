overplant: /ˌoʊvərˈplænt/ | vt. | 过度种植；种植过多；超种 | To plant too densely or excessively. |  The farmer overplanted his field with corn, and the overcrowding stunted the growth.  农民在田里过度种植了玉米，过度拥挤阻碍了玉米的生长。| Overplanted tomatoes produced smaller fruit.  过度种植的西红柿果实较小。 | overcrowd, over sow | underplant | verb

overplant: /ˌoʊvərˈplænt/ | n. |  过度种植；过剩的植物 | An area that has been planted too densely. | The overplant of seedlings resulted in a weak crop. 幼苗的过度种植导致了作物产量低。 |  The overplant in the garden made it difficult to weed.  花园里过度种植使得除草很困难。| overcrowding, excess vegetation | underplanting | noun
