noncontributive:/ˌnɑːnkənˈtrɪbjətɪv/ | adj. | 非贡献的，无贡献的 | Not contributing to something, especially not contributing financially. |  Example:  A noncontributive pension plan is one where the employee does not make regular payments into the plan.  非缴费型养老金计划是指雇员不定期向该计划缴款的计划。

Example: He was deemed a noncontributive member of the team due to his lack of effort.  由于缺乏努力，他被认为是团队中没有贡献的成员。 | Synonyms: non-paying, unproductive, idle, inert | Antonyms: contributory, productive, active, contributing | Word Type: Adjective
