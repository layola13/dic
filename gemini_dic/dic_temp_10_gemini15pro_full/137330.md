nontannins:/ˌnɒnˈtænɪnz/ | n. | 非单宁 | Substances in plant materials other than tannins, often contributing to color and flavor but not astringency. |  The nontannins in tea contribute to its smoothness. 茶叶中的非单宁物质使其口感顺滑。 | Synonyms: non-tannic substances | Antonyms: tannins | noun

nontannin:/ˌnɒnˈtænɪn/ | adj. | 非单宁的 | Not containing or relating to tannins. |  The nontannin components of the bark were analyzed.  树皮中的非单宁成分被分析了。| Synonyms: non-tannic | Antonyms: tannic | adjective
