overdrainages:/ˌoʊvərˈdreɪnɪdʒɪz/
|n.|
|过度排水；排水过度|
|The act of draining excessively, resulting in the removal of too much water from an area, often leading to negative environmental consequences.|
|The overdrainages of the peat bogs have led to significant soil subsidence.|泥炭沼泽的过度排水导致了严重的土壤沉降。
