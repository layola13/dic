levulosan: /ˌlɛvjuˈloʊsæn/ | n. | 左旋糖酐；左旋葡聚糖 | An anhydrohexose formed by pyrolysis of starch or cellulose.  It is a white or yellowish powder soluble in water. |  Levulosan is a common product of biomass pyrolysis.  左旋糖酐是生物质热解的常见产物。

Levulosan can be converted into valuable chemicals.  左旋糖酐可以转化成有价值的化学品。 |  Near synonyms:  fructosan, anhydrosugar | Antonyms: None commonly used | noun
