anti-bolsheviki:/ˌæntiˌbɒlʃɪˈviːki/ | n. | 反布尔什维克主义者；反布尔什维克分子 |  A person opposed to Bolshevism, the political ideology and practices of the Bolshevik party.  |  The White Army during the Russian Civil War was largely composed of anti-bolsheviki.  俄国内战期间的白军主要由反布尔什维克分子组成。 |  counter-revolutionary, anti-communist | Bolshevik, communist | noun

anti-bolshevik:/ˌæntiˌbɒlʃɪˈviːk/ | adj. | 反布尔什维克的 | Opposed to Bolshevism. | The anti-bolshevik forces were ultimately defeated. 反布尔什维克势力最终被打败了。| counter-revolutionary, anti-communist | Bolshevik, communist | adjective


Etymology:
"anti-" (prefix) from Greek "anti" meaning "against, opposite of" + "Bolshevik" from Russian "большевик" (bol'shevik) meaning "member of the majority," referring to the Bolshevik faction of the Russian Social Democratic Labour Party.
