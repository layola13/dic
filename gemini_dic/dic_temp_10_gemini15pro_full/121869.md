misrewarding:/ˌmɪsrɪˈwɔːrdɪŋ/
adjective
给予错误回报的；不值得的；徒劳的
Providing inadequate or inappropriate rewards; not worth the effort; unproductive.
The task was misrewarding, offering little satisfaction for the immense effort involved.  这项任务得不偿失，巨大的努力只换来了微薄的回报。
The misrewarding nature of the job led to high employee turnover. 这份工作回报不足，导致员工流动率很高。
Synonyms: unrewarding, fruitless, thankless, unproductive, futile
Antonyms: rewarding, fulfilling, satisfying, fruitful, productive
词性: adjective
