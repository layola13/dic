anti-British:/ˌæntiˈbrɪtɪʃ/
adjective
反英的；仇视英国的
Opposed to or hostile towards Britain or the British.
Many anti-British sentiments arose in India during colonial rule. 在殖民统治时期，印度出现了许多反英情绪。
The rebels held strong anti-British views. 叛军持有强烈的反英观点。

近义词: anti-English
反义词: pro-British, Anglophile

词性: adjective

词源:  "anti-" (prefix meaning "against, opposed to") + "British" (adjective relating to Great Britain).  The prefix "anti-" comes from Greek "anti" meaning "against, opposite of." "British" derives from the Old English "Bryttisc," referring to the Britons.
