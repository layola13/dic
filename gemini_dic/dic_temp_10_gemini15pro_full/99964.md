interoperculum:/ˌɪntərəˈpɜːrkjʊləm/ | n. |鳃盖间骨 |A small, median bone located between the opercula (gill covers) in some fishes. | The interoperculum is an important bone for studying fish evolution. 鳃盖间骨是研究鱼类进化的一块重要骨骼。| The position of the interoperculum can help identify different fish species. 鳃盖间骨的位置有助于识别不同的鱼类。 | synonyms: suboperculum (sometimes)| antonyms:  (none specific - could be argued that the absence of an interoperculum is an antonym) | noun
