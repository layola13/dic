moused:/maʊst/ | v. |  用鼠标操作；悄悄地移动；搜索 | to use a computer mouse; to move like a mouse; to search for something diligently |  She moused over the icon to see what would happen. 她把鼠标移到图标上，看看会发生什么。 | 近义词：searched, hunted, rummaged, crept, sneaked | 反义词：charged, strode | 词性：动词

moused:/maʊst/ | adj. |  (罕见) 胆小的；像老鼠一样的 | timid; resembling a mouse |  He had a moused expression on his face. 他脸上带着胆怯的表情。(这个用法比较罕见) | 近义词：timid, mousy, mousey | 反义词：bold, brave | 词性：形容词

词源：From *mūs* (Proto-Germanic *mūs-), from Proto-Indo-European *muHs-. Cognate with Old English *mūs*, Old Frisian *mūs*, Old Saxon *mūs*, Old High German *mūs*, Old Norse *mús*, Gothic **.  Originally referring to the rodent, the verb sense related to computer mice is a relatively recent development.
