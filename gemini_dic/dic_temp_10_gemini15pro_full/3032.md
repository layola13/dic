aerobically:/ɛəˈrəʊbɪkli/ /ɛrˈoʊbɪkli/
adverb
有氧地；需氧地
in a way that uses oxygen
例句：
The bacteria grow aerobically. = 这些细菌进行有氧生长。

Most living things respire aerobically. = 大多数生物进行有氧呼吸。

近义词: with oxygen, in the presence of oxygen
反义词: anaerobically
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From aerobic + -ally. Aerobic from aero- + -bic. Aero- from Greek aēr, air. -bic from Greek bios, life. -ally from -ly.

USAGE->
She exercises aerobically for 30 minutes every day. = 她每天进行30分钟的有氧运动。
He prefers to train aerobically rather than anaerobically. = 他更喜欢有氧训练而不是无氧训练。

The process occurs aerobically, meaning it requires oxygen. =  这个过程是有氧进行的，意味着它需要氧气。
These organisms thrive in environments where they can respire aerobically. = 这些生物在可以进行有氧呼吸的环境中茁壮成长。

If the bacteria are grown aerobically, they produce a different byproduct. = 如果这些细菌在有氧环境下生长，它们会产生不同的副产品。
The cells were cultured aerobically to observe their growth patterns. = 这些细胞在有氧环境下培养以观察它们的生长模式。


PHRASE->
aerobically grown = 有氧生长的

aerobically active = 有氧活跃的
exercising aerobically = 进行有氧运动
metabolizing aerobically = 有氧代谢
