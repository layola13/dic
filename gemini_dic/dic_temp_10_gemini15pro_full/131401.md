nonclarified:/ˌnɒnˈklærɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 未澄清的，未阐明的 | Not made clear or explained. | The instructions were nonclarified and confusing. 指示不明确，令人困惑。| The situation remained nonclarified, leading to further misunderstandings.  情况仍然不明朗，导致进一步的误解。| synonyms: unclear, vague, ambiguous, obscure | antonyms: clear, clarified, explicit, precise | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + clarified.  "Clarified" comes from the Latin "clarificare," meaning "to make clear," from "clarus" (clear) + "-ficare" (to make).  The prefix "non-" simply negates the meaning of "clarified."
