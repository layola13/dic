devitalize:/diːˈvaɪtəlaɪz/
verb
使失去活力;使失去生命力
To deprive of life or vitality; to make lifeless or inactive.
The long illness devitalized him.  这场久病使他失去了活力。
Synonyms: weaken, enfeeble, sap, drain
Antonyms: energize, invigorate, revitalize
词性: 动词
