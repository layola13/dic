gyroidal:/ˈdʒaɪrɔɪdəl/
adjective
螺旋状的，环形的
Resembling a gyroid, a surface that has a saddle point everywhere.
The gyroidal structure of the diatom frustule provides strength and lightness. 硅藻壳的螺旋状结构既坚固又轻巧。
Near Synonyms: helicoidal, spiral, helical, convoluted, twisted
Antonyms: straight, linear
词性: adjective
