blissfulnesses: /ˈblɪsfʊlnəsɪz/
n.
极乐状态; 幸福; 天赐之福 (plural form of blissfulness)
The state of being extremely happy or peaceful.

例句：
He experienced moments of profound blissfulnesses during his meditation retreat.
他在冥想静修期间经历了极度幸福的时刻。

She described the blissfulnesses of motherhood with radiant joy.
她带着灿烂的喜悦描述了为人母的幸福。

近义词：
joys, ecstasies, raptures, blessings, felicities

反义词：
miseries, agonies, torments, sufferings, unhappinesses

词性:
Noun (plural)

词源:
From bliss + -ful + -ness + -es.  Bliss comes from Old English *bliþs, from Proto-Germanic *bliþiz (meaning "joy, kindness, friendliness, mercy").
