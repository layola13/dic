laughing-stock: /ˈlɑːfɪŋ stɒk/ (British) /ˈlæfɪŋ stɑːk/ (American)
noun
笑柄；嘲笑对象
a person who is regarded as foolish or incompetent, and so is much ridiculed
After his disastrous presentation, he became the laughing-stock of the whole company.  他糟糕的演讲之后，成了全公司的笑柄。
synonyms: butt of jokes, object of ridicule, figure of fun
antonyms: respected figure, role model
词性: 名词
