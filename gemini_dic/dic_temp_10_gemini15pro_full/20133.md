bipas:/ˈbaɪpɑːs/| n. vt.|旁路，绕过|A bypass is a road or route that goes around a town or other busy area, so that traffic does not have to go through it.  A bypass can also refer to a medical procedure that creates a new path for blood to flow around a blocked artery or vein.|例句：The new bypass will reduce traffic congestion in the city center. 新的旁路将减少市中心的交通拥堵。|近义词：detour, alternative route, shunt|反义词:direct route, main route|词性 名词, 动词

bypass:/ˈbaɪpɑːs/| vt.| 绕过，避开|To bypass something means to avoid it or go around it.|例句：They bypassed the congested city center by taking the ring road. 他们绕过拥堵的市中心，走了环城路。|近义词：circumvent, avoid, evade|反义词:confront, face|词性 动词
