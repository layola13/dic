nonglare:/ˌnɒnˈɡleə(r)/ /ˌnɑːnˈɡler/| adj.|无眩光的，不反光的|Not causing or reflecting glare; matte.  Designed to reduce or eliminate glare.|The computer screen has a nonglare surface, making it easier on the eyes. 电脑屏幕具有无眩光表面，使眼睛更舒适。|Synonyms: matte, anti-glare, non-reflective|Antonyms: glossy, reflective|Adjective
