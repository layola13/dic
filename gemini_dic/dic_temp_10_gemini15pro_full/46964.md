darting:/ˈdɑːrtɪŋ/ | adj. | 迅速移动的，飞奔的 | Moving swiftly and suddenly like a dart. | The lizard's tongue made a darting movement to catch the fly.  蜥蜴的舌头飞快地动了一下，抓住了苍蝇。 | The hummingbird made a darting flight to the flower.  蜂鸟飞快地飞向花朵。| synonyms: flashing, dashing, quick, rapid | antonyms: slow, sluggish | adjective

darting:/ˈdɑːrtɪŋ/ | n. | 猛冲，飞奔 | A sudden, rapid movement. | The cat made a darting run across the room. 猫飞奔穿过房间。| With a darting motion, she snatched the book from the shelf.  她猛地一下从书架上抓起那本书。| synonyms: dash, sprint, bolt | antonyms: crawl, creep | noun
