inharmoniousness:/ˌɪnhɑːrˈmoʊniəsnəs/ | n. |不和谐，不协调|Lack of harmony; discord.  A state of being disagreeable in sound, action, or feeling.|The inharmoniousness of their singing made the performance unpleasant.|近义词：discord, disharmony, conflict, clash, dissonance, friction|反义词：harmony, concord, agreement, unity, consonance|词性:名词

Etymology:
From in- (not) + harmonious + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  "Harmonious" ultimately derives from Greek "harmonia" (fitting together, agreement).
