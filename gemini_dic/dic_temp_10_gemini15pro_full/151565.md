part-work:/ˈpɑːrtwɜːrk/ | n. | 分期出版物; 分册; | A publication, typically a magazine or encyclopedia, issued in installments which are collected over time to form a complete work. |  He's collecting a part-work on the Second World War. 他正在收集一套关于第二次世界大战的分期出版物。| synonyms: serial publication, installment publication, fascicle | antonyms: monograph, single volume | noun

part-work:/ˈpɑːrtwɜːrk/ | adj. | 分期出版的 | Published in installments or parts. | The part-work edition of the dictionary was cheaper than buying the whole thing at once.  这本词典的分册版比一次性购买整套要便宜。| synonyms: serialized, installment-based | antonyms: complete, unabridged | adjective
