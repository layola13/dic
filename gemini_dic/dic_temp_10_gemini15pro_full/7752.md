anorthic:/ˌeɪnˈɔːrθɪk/
adjective
三斜的；非直角的
Relating to or denoting a crystal system characterized by three unequal axes intersecting at oblique angles.
The mineral plagioclase commonly occurs in anorthic crystals. 斜长石矿物通常以三斜晶体形式出现。
Feldspar is an example of an anorthic mineral. 长石是三斜晶体矿物的一个例子。
