matter-of-fact:/ˌmætər əv ˈfækt/
matter-of-fact| adj.|实事求是的；平静的；不带感情的|Accepting something without showing emotion, especially in a difficult situation.  Not showing feelings or emotion, especially in a situation when emotion would be expected.|He spoke in a matter-of-fact way about the accident. 他以实事求是的口吻讲述了这起事故。|Synonyms: unemotional, objective, practical, pragmatic|Antonyms: emotional, subjective, sentimental|adjective


matter-of-fact:/ˌmætər əv ˈfækt/
matter-of-fact| n.|实事求是；不带感情色彩的陈述|A statement made without feeling or emotion.|Her matter-of-fact delivery of the bad news was unsettling.  她平淡地宣布了这个坏消息，令人不安。|Synonyms: statement, report, account|Antonyms: outburst, emotional display|noun
