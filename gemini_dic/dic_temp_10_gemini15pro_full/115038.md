mandola:/mænˈdoʊlə/ | n. | 曼陀林 |A musical instrument of the lute family, similar to the mandolin but larger and with a deeper tone.  It is tuned a fifth lower than the mandolin. |  He played a beautiful melody on his mandola. 他用他的曼陀林演奏了一段优美的旋律。|  Synonyms: mandore,tenor mandolin | Antonyms: N/A | Noun

mandola:/mænˈdoʊlə/ | n. | 曼陀罗 |(Rare, archaic) A representation of the universe in Buddhist and Hindu symbolism; a mandala. | The ancient text described the creation of a complex mandola. 这本古籍描述了一个复杂曼陀罗的创造过程。| Synonyms: mandala | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
