etiological:/ˌiːtiəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ /ˌiːtioʊˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/
adjective
病因学的；病原学的
Relating to the study of the causes or origins of diseases or abnormal conditions.
The etiological agent of the disease is a bacterium.  这种疾病的病原体是一种细菌。
The researchers are investigating the etiological factors contributing to the rise in autism. 研究人员正在调查导致自闭症发病率上升的病因。
synonyms: causal, causative
antonyms: resultant, consequential
词性: adjective
