blowziness:/ˈblaʊzinəs/| n. |邋遢，不整洁，蓬头垢面|The state of being blowzy; disorderliness or untidiness, especially in personal appearance.|The room had a distinct air of blowziness about it. 房间里弥漫着一股邋遢的气息。|近义词：untidiness, dishevelment, messiness, slovenliness|反义词：tidiness, neatness, orderliness|词性:名词

blowzy:/ˈblaʊzi/| adj. |蓬头垢面的，邋遢的，(脸色)红润的|Disheveled and untidy in appearance; having a flushed, ruddy complexion.|She looked rather blowzy after her long journey.  长途旅行后，她看起来很邋遢。|近义词：disheveled, untidy, rumpled, flushed|反义词：tidy, neat, groomed|词性:形容词
