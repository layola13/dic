inimitable:/ɪˈnɪmɪtəbl/ | adj. | 不可模仿的；独特的；无双的 | Not able to be imitated; so good or unusual as to be impossible to copy. | Her inimitable style made her a fashion icon. 她的独特风格使她成为时尚偶像。 |  His inimitable wit charmed everyone. 他那无人能及的智慧迷住了所有人。 | 近义词: unique, matchless, peerless, unparalleled | 反义词: imitable, common, ordinary | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin inimitabilis, from in- ‘not’ + imitabilis ‘imitable’.
