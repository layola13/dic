euphemistic:/juːfəˈmɪstɪk/| adj.|委婉的|Substituting a mild, indirect, or vague term for one considered offensive, harsh, or blunt.|He used a euphemism to describe the difficult situation, saying it was a "challenging opportunity." 他用委婉的说法来描述困难的局面，称之为“充满挑战的机会”。|近义词：indirect, mild, polite, delicate|反义词：blunt, direct, frank, harsh|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Greek *euphēmistos* "auspicious, sounding good," from *eu- "good" + *phēmē* "speech, voice, utterance" + *-istos* adjectival suffix.
