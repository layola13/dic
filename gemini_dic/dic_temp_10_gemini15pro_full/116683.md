maturational:/ˌmætʃəˈreɪʃənəl/
adjective
成熟的
relating to the process of becoming mature
例句：Maturational changes in the brain occur throughout adolescence. 大脑的成熟变化贯穿整个青春期。

例句：The study examined the maturational stages of child development. 这项研究考察了儿童发展的成熟阶段。

近义词：developmental, evolving, ripening
反义词：immature, undeveloped

词性：adjective
词源：From maturation + -al.  Maturation itself comes from Late Latin maturationem, from Latin maturus "ripe."
