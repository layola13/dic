cockiness's:/ˈkɒkɪnɪsɪz/| n. |自负，傲慢|The possessive form of "cockiness," referring to the quality of being excessively self-confident or arrogant.  It indicates that the cockiness belongs to someone or something.|His cockiness's downfall was his underestimation of the opponent. 他的自负导致了他的失败，因为他低估了对手。|Near synonyms: arrogance, swagger, conceitedness, presumption|Antonyms: humility, modesty, diffidence|noun
