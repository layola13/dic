breloque:/brəˈloʊk/| n.|小饰物，挂件，坠子|A small ornament, trinket, or charm, often worn dangling from a bracelet or necklace.|She attached a delicate silver breloque to her bracelet.|Synonyms: charm, pendant, trinket, ornament|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From French *breloque*, ultimately from Provençal *berloque* ("pendant, trinket"). Possibly related to *berlòca* ("a little bell").
