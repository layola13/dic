abridge: /əˈbrɪdʒ/
verb
缩短，节略
To shorten or condense (a book, movie, speech, or other text) without losing the sense.
The author abridges the original novel for a younger audience.  作者为年轻读者缩短了原版小说。

They abridged the documentary from three hours to 90 minutes. 他们将纪录片从三个小时缩短到90分钟。

反义词: expand, lengthen, extend, amplify
