nonfalteringly:/nɒnˈfɔːltərɪŋli/ /ˌnɑːnˈfɔːltərɪŋli/| adv.|毫不犹豫地，坚定地|Without faltering; steadily and resolutely.|She walked nonfalteringly towards her goal. 她坚定地朝着目标走去。|He spoke nonfalteringly, despite the pressure. 尽管压力很大，他还是毫不犹豫地说了出来。|近义词：resolutely, firmly, steadily, unwaveringly|反义词：hesitantly, uncertainly, waveringly|词性:副词

nonfalteringly (词源):
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"
falter (verb): to hesitate or waver
-ing (suffix): forming a present participle, often used as an adjective
-ly (suffix): forming an adverb
