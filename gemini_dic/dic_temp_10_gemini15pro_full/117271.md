mechanist:/ˈmɛkənɪst/| n.|机械论者，机械师|A person who believes in or advocates the theory of mechanism, especially in philosophy or biology; someone skilled in the design, construction, or operation of machinery.|He argued against the vitalists, asserting his belief as a mechanist. 他反对活力论者，声称自己是一个机械论者。|近义词：determinist, materialist|反义词：vitalist|noun
