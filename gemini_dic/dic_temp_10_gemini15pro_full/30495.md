centry:/ˈsɛntri/ | n. |岗哨；哨兵；值班人|A soldier stationed to keep guard or to control access to a place.|The centry stood guard at the gate. 哨兵在门口站岗。|近义词：guard, sentinel, watchman|反义词:None|词性:名词
Etymology: From Middle French *centrie*, from Old French *centree*, from Vulgar Latin *centuria*, from Latin *centum* (“hundred”). Originally referred to a company of one hundred soldiers.
