incommoding:/ˌɪnkəˈmoʊdɪŋ/
verb
打扰；使不便；妨碍
causing inconvenience or trouble to someone.
I hope I'm not incommoding you by asking you to move.  我希望请你挪一下不会打扰到你。
近义词: inconveniencing, disturbing, bothering, troubling
反义词: accommodating, assisting, helping
词性: verb (present participle)

incommode:/ˌɪnkəˈmoʊd/
verb
打扰；使不便；妨碍
to cause inconvenience or trouble to someone.
The construction work outside my window has been incommoding me all week. 我窗外施工扰了我一整周。
近义词: inconvenience, disturb, bother, trouble
反义词: accommodate, assist, help
词性: verb

词源:  From French *incommoder*, from Latin *incommodare* "to be unsuitable, inconvenient," from *in-* "not" + *commodus* "suitable, convenient."
