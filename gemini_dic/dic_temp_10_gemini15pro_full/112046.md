longboards:/ˈlɔːŋbɔːrdz/ | n. | 长板 | A type of skateboard that is longer than a typical skateboard, often used for cruising, downhill racing, and freestyle riding.  | He bought a new set of longboards for cruising along the beach. 他买了一套新的长板，用来在海滩上滑行。 | 近义词: skateboards, cruisers | 反义词: N/A | noun

longboard:/ˈlɔːŋbɔːrd/ | v. | 滑长板 | To ride a longboard. | She longboards to work every day. 她每天滑长板去上班。 | 近义词: skateboard, cruise | 反义词: N/A | verb

词源: longboard is a compound word formed from "long" and "board".  "Long" traces back to Old English "lang",  meaning having considerable linear extent. "Board" comes from Old English "bord", referring to a flat piece of wood.
