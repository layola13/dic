moronically: /məˈrɒnɪk(ə)li/
| adv. |
| 愚蠢地；呆头呆脑地 |
| In a foolish or stupid manner; like a moron. |
| He moronically walked into the pole. 他愚蠢地撞到了电线杆上。|
|  She moronically agreed to the ridiculous terms of the contract. 她愚蠢地同意了合同中荒谬的条款。|
| stupidly, foolishly, idiotically, absurdly |
| intelligently, wisely, sensibly, prudently |
| 副词 |

Etymology:
From moron + -ic + -ally.  "Moron" comes from the Greek word "moros" meaning "dull" or "foolish."
