engulfed:/ɪnˈɡʌlft/ | adj. | 吞没的，席卷的 | Completely surrounded or covered by something, especially something large or powerful. | The small boat was engulfed by the monstrous wave. 小船被巨浪吞没了。

The city was engulfed in flames. 城市被火焰吞没。 | 近义词：submerged, overwhelmed, enveloped, consumed | 反义词：exposed, uncovered | 词性:形容词

engulf:/ɪnˈɡʌlf/ | vt. | 吞没，席卷 | To completely surround or cover something, especially something large or powerful. | The flames engulfed the building. 火焰吞没了这栋楼。

The rising floodwaters engulfed the town.  上涨的洪水吞没了城镇。 | 近义词：submerge, overwhelm, envelop, consume | 反义词：expose, uncover | 词性:动词
