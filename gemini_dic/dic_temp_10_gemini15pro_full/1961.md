activ:/ˈæktɪv/| adj.|活跃的，积极的，主动的|Being in a state of action; characterized by energetic activity; brisk.|例句：She leads a very active life. 她过着非常积极的生活。|近义词：energetic, dynamic, vigorous, brisk, lively|反义词：passive, inactive, inert, dormant|adjective

activ:/ˈæktɪv/| n.|积极分子，活跃分子|A person who is actively involved in something, especially in political or social activities.|例句：The activists campaigned for environmental protection. 这些积极分子为环境保护而奔走。|近义词：campaigner, advocate, supporter|反义词：opponent|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *activus, from Latin actus (“act, impulse, activity”), past participle of agere (“to do, drive, act”).

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is an active member of the club. = 他是俱乐部的活跃会员。
The volcano is active. = 这座火山是活火山。

一般过去时:
She was very active in student politics. = 她积极参与学生政治活动。
The market was active yesterday. = 昨天市场很活跃。

现在完成时:
He has been an active participant in the community. = 他一直是社区的积极参与者。
The company has been active in promoting its new product. = 这家公司一直积极推广其新产品。

过去完成时:
They had been active supporters of the cause for many years. = 多年来，他们一直是这项事业的积极支持者。
She had been active in the theater group before moving away. = 在搬走之前，她一直活跃在剧团里。

将来时:
He will be active in the campaign. = 他将在竞选活动中发挥积极作用。
The organization will be active in raising awareness about the issue. = 该组织将积极提高人们对这个问题的认识。

虚拟语气:
If I were more active, I would be healthier. = 如果我更活跃一些，我会更健康。
I wish I were more active in the community. = 我希望我在社区里更活跃一些。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
