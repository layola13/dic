auxiliary:/ɔːɡˈzɪliəri/ /ɑːɡˈzɪləri/| adj. n.|辅助的，附属的；助手，辅助设备|Providing supplementary or additional help and support.  A person or thing that gives help.|例句：The hospital has an auxiliary power supply in case of a blackout.  这家医院有一个备用电源以防停电。|近义词：supplementary, additional, supporting, reserve, backup|反义词：primary, main, principal|词性:形容词,名词

auxiliary:/ɔːɡˈzɪliəri/ /ɑːɡˈzɪləri/| n.|助手，辅助人员|A person or thing that gives help.|例句：She works as an auxiliary nurse at the local hospital. 她在当地医院担任助理护士。|近义词：assistant, aide, helper|反义词：primary, main|词性:名词
