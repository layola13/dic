anemically:/əˈniːmɪkli/ | adv. | 贫血地，无力地，没有生气地 | In a manner lacking vigor, vitality, or spirit; weakly. | The economy has been performing anemically for the past few years.  过去几年经济一直表现疲软。| Synonyms: weakly, feebly, listlessly, languidly | Antonyms: robustly, strongly, vigorously | Adverb

Etymology: From anemic + -ally.  "Anemic" comes from the French anémique, from Greek anaimia "lack of blood," from an- "without" + haima "blood."
