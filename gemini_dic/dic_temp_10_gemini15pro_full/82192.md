grosses:/ˈɡroʊsɪz/ (US), /ˈɡrəʊsɪz/ (UK)| n. |总收入，总额| The grosses for the film were impressive, exceeding all expectations. 这部电影的总收入令人印象深刻，超过了所有预期。| The Broadway show's grosses declined sharply after the lead actor left.  这部百老汇演出的总收入在主角离开后急剧下降。|  earnings, revenue, proceeds | net income, net profit | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French "grosse," meaning "large, thick, coarse," ultimately from Late Latin "grossus."  The use of "grosses" in the entertainment industry to refer to total revenue developed in the 19th century.
