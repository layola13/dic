barometric: /ˌbærəˈmɛtrɪk/ | adj. | 气压的，气压计的 | Relating to or measured by a barometer; of or relating to atmospheric pressure. |  The barometric pressure dropped significantly before the storm.  风暴前气压显著下降。 | Synonym: atmospheric | Antonym: N/A | Word Type: Adjective

barometer: /bəˈrɒmɪtə(r)/ | n. | 气压计，晴雨表 | An instrument measuring atmospheric pressure, used especially in forecasting the weather and determining altitude. | The barometer indicated a coming storm. 气压计显示暴风雨即将来临。 | Synonym: gauge, indicator | Antonym: N/A | Word Type: Noun
