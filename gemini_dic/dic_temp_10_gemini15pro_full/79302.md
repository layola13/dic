glazieries: /ˈɡleɪʒəriːz/
noun
玻璃厂；玻璃作坊；玻璃制品
places where glass is made or worked with, or the products made there.
The old glazieries in Murano, Venice are famous for their intricate glasswork. 威尼斯的穆拉诺岛上的古老玻璃厂以其复杂的玻璃制品而闻名。

The town was once known for its bustling glazieries.  这个小镇曾经以其繁忙的玻璃厂而闻名。

Synonym: glassworks, glass factories
Antonym: N/A (No direct antonym for a place of manufacturing)
词性: noun
