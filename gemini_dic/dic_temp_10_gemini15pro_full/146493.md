overgarnish: /ˌoʊvərˈɡɑːrnɪʃ/
verb
过度装饰；装饰过分
To garnish excessively, adding too many decorative elements to a dish, making it look cluttered or overwhelming rather than appealing.
The chef overgarnished the plate, making it look messy.  厨师过度装饰了盘子，使它看起来很乱。
He tends to overgarnish his desserts, piling on whipped cream, sprinkles, and fruit until they're almost unrecognizable. 他喜欢过度装饰他的甜点，堆上鲜奶油、糖屑和水果，直到它们几乎认不出来。
近义词: overdecorate
反义词: undergarnish (less common)
词性: verb
