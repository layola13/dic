grilse:/ɡrɪls/| n. |幼鲑；回溯性大西洋鲑|A young salmon returning to fresh water from the sea for the first time, especially one of the smaller size. |例句：The grilse were plentiful in the river this year. 今年河里的幼鲑很多。

例句：The fisherman caught a beautiful grilse.  渔夫捕到一条漂亮的幼鲑。|近义词：salmon, parr, smolt|反义词:|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Scots *grels, from Old Norse *grísleifr, diminutive of gríss "piglet".  The connection is thought to be due to the greyish colour of young salmon.
