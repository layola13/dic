coupe: /kuːp/ (UK), /kup/ (US)| n.|双门轿跑车; 划艇; 大奖杯|A car with a fixed roof, two doors, and a sloping back.  A closed, usually two-seated, horse-drawn carriage. A large cup or trophy.|例句：He bought a sleek new coupe. 他买了一辆时髦的新轿跑车。

例句：The winning team received a silver coupe. 获胜的队伍收到了一个银制大奖杯。|近义词：car, automobile, sedan (for the car meaning), trophy, cup (for the trophy meaning)|反义词: (No direct antonym.  Could consider "convertible" for the car meaning, as it's the opposite of a fixed roof.)|词性:noun
