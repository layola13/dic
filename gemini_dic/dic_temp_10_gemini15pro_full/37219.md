comeuppance:/kʌmˈʌpəns/
n.
应得的惩罚；报应
A punishment or fate that someone deserves.
例句：After years of bullying his classmates, he finally got his comeuppance.  多年来欺负同学之后，他终于得到了报应。

例句：She cheated on the exam, but she got her comeuppance when she was caught and failed the course. 她在考试中作弊，但当她被抓到并挂科时，她得到了应有的惩罚。

近义词：retribution, deserts, just deserts, punishment, payback
反义词：reward,  impunity
词性：名词
