equitum:/ˈɛkʷɪtʊm/| n.|骑兵，骑士 (plural of eques)|Of the Roman cavalry, or knights.  Referring specifically to the order of *equites*, a wealthy class in Roman society who originally served as cavalry. |The strength of the Roman legions was often supplemented by the swift maneuvers of the *equitum*.  罗马军团的力量通常由快速机动的骑兵来补充。| 近义词: cavalrymen, horsemen, knights| 反义词: infantry, foot soldiers| Noun
