overhumanities:/ˌoʊvərhjʊˈmænɪtiz/ | n. | 超人文学；后人类主义|  A philosophical and cultural movement that explores the implications of emerging technologies and scientific advancements on the nature of humanity, often considering the potential for transcending current human limitations.  It goes beyond traditional humanities by incorporating perspectives from fields like biotechnology, nanotechnology, and artificial intelligence.|  The conference on overhumanities explored the ethical implications of genetic engineering.  这次关于超人文学的会议探讨了基因工程的伦理意义。|  The speaker discussed the potential for overhumanities to reshape our understanding of consciousness. 演讲者讨论了超人文学重塑我们对意识理解的潜力。| 近义词：transhumanism, posthumanism | 反义词: humanism | 词性: 名词
