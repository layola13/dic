geometric mean:/ˌdʒiːəˈmɛtrɪk ˈmiːn/ | n. | 几何平均数 | The average of a set of numbers calculated by multiplying them all together and then taking the nth root, where n is the total number of numbers. | The geometric mean of 2, 4, and 8 is 4 (∛(2*4*8) = 4).  几何平均数为4。| Example 2: Investors often use the geometric mean to calculate the average return of an investment over a period of time. 投资者经常使用几何平均数来计算一段时间内投资的平均回报。| synonym: geometric average | antonym: arithmetic mean | noun


geometric means:/ˌdʒiːəˈmɛtrɪk ˈmiːnz/ | n. | 几何平均数（复数） | The plural form of "geometric mean," referring to multiple such averages. |  We calculated the geometric means for each set of data. 我们计算了每组数据的几何平均数。| Example 2:  The geometric means of the different portfolios showed varying performance. 不同投资组合的几何平均数显示出不同的表现。 | synonym: geometric averages | antonym: arithmetic means | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"geometric" comes from the Greek "geometria" meaning "measurement of earth" combining "geo" (earth) and "metria" (measurement).  "Mean" in this context comes from the Old French "meien" meaning "middle" or "average."
