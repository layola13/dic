owed:/əʊd/ | v. | 欠(债)；应给予；感激；归功于 |  past tense and past participle of *owe* meaning to be under obligation to pay or repay someone (money or gratitude) | I owed him fifty dollars. 我欠他五十美元。|  Synonym: due, payable | Antonym: paid, collected | verb

owe:/oʊ/ | v. | 欠(债)；应给予；感激；归功于 | to be under obligation to pay or repay (something, especially money) to (someone) | I owe you a drink for helping me move.  我欠你一杯酒，谢谢你帮我搬家。| Synonym: be indebted to, be obliged to | Antonym: be entitled to,  be credited with | verb
