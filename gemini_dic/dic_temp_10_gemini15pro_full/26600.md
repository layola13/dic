cache:/kæʃ/| n. v.|缓存，隐藏处|A hidden store of things, or a place for hiding things;  a special part of a computer's memory that stores copies of frequently used information so that the computer can access it more quickly.|例句：The squirrels have a cache of nuts buried in the garden. 松鼠在花园里藏了一堆坚果。|近义词：hoard, store, reserve|反义词:expenditure, waste|词性:noun, verb

cache:/kæʃ/| v.|缓存，隐藏|To store something in a hidden or secure place.|例句：The pirates cached their treasure on a desert island. 海盗把他们的宝藏藏在一个荒岛上。|近义词：conceal, hide, stash|反义词:reveal, expose|词性:verb
