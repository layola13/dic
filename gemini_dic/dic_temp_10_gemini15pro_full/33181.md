chromogenic:/ˌkroʊməˈdʒɛnɪk/ | adj. | 发色的，显色的，生色的 | Producing color; relating to the production of color.  A chromogenic substance is one that can be converted into a dye or other colored compound. | Chromogenic bacteria are used in some types of water testing.  显色细菌用于某些类型的水测试。|  Chromogenic substrates are used in ELISA tests. 显色底物用于 ELISA 测试。 | color-producing, colorimetric | achromatic | adjective
