laumontite:/ˈlɔːmɒntaɪt/| n.|浊沸石|A hydrous calcium-aluminum silicate mineral that occurs in fibrous or granular masses, typically white or pink. It is a member of the zeolite group and is characterized by its tendency to dehydrate and crumble on exposure to air.|Laumontite is often found in volcanic rocks. 浊沸石常发现于火山岩中。|近义词：leonhardite (partially dehydrated laumontite)|反义词:|名词

laumontite:/ˈlɔːmɒntaɪt/| n.|浊沸石|A zeolite mineral that loses water and becomes powdery on exposure to dry air.|The laumontite crystals were unstable and quickly disintegrated. 浊沸石晶体不稳定，迅速分解。|近义词：zeolite|反义词:|名词
