effectless:/ɪˈfɛktləs/
adjective
无效的；无用的；不起作用的
Without any noticeable or desired effect; futile.
His protests were effectless against the decision. 他对这个决定的抗议无效。
近义词:futile, ineffective, useless, vain, unavailing
反义词:effective, potent, powerful
词性:adjective

词源:
From effect + -less.  "Effect" comes from the Latin "effectus," meaning "accomplishment, result."  "-less" is a suffix meaning "without."
