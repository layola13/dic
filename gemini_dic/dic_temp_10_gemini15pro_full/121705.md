misprescribe:/ˌmɪsprɪˈskraɪb/
verb
错误地开处方；开错药
to prescribe (a medicine or treatment) incorrectly.
The doctor misprescribed the medication, which caused further health problems. 医生开错了药，导致了进一步的健康问题。
misprescribe medication:  The overworked doctor misprescribed medication to the patient. 过度劳累的医生给病人开错了药。
The medication was misprescribed, leading to an adverse reaction.  药物被错误地处方，导致了不良反应。
近义词: prescribe incorrectly, overprescribe, underprescribe
反义词: prescribe correctly, prescribe appropriately
词性: verb

misprescription: /ˌmɪsprɪˈskrɪpʃən/
noun
错误处方；开错药
an instance of misprescribing a medicine or treatment.
The misprescription of antibiotics can contribute to antibiotic resistance.  滥用抗生素会导致抗生素耐药性。
Due to a misprescription, the patient received the wrong dosage. 由于处方错误，病人服用了错误的剂量。
The pharmacist caught the misprescription before the patient took the medication. 药剂师在病人服药前发现了处方错误。
Due to the misprescription, the patient's condition worsened. 由于处方错误，病人的病情恶化了。

近义词: incorrect prescription, wrong prescription
反义词: correct prescription, appropriate prescription
词性: noun
