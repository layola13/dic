conimene: /ˈkɒnɪmiːn/ | n. |科尼米烯| A bicyclic monoterpene found in various plants. It is a colorless liquid with a pleasant odor. | Conimene is a constituent of the essential oil of Calamus oil. 科尼米烯是菖蒲油精油的一种成分。|  Conimene has been studied for its potential insecticidal properties. 科尼米烯的潜在杀虫特性已被研究。| Synonyms:  δ-selinene, (+)-δ-selinene | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
