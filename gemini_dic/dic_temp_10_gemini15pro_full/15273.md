backlashers:/ˈbæklæʃərz/
n.
强烈反对者，反对者
People who react negatively and intensely to a social or political change.
The backlashers protested the new law outside the government building.  强烈反对者在政府大楼外抗议新法律。
The company faced a wave of backlashers after announcing their decision to close the factory.  公司在宣布关闭工厂的决定后，面临一波强烈反对者的浪潮。
opponents, critics, dissenters
supporters, advocates, proponents
noun
