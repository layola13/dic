anticonsumerism:/ˌæntiˈkənsjuːmərɪzəm/| n.|反消费主义|Opposition to consumerism, the continual buying and consuming of material goods.|Example: Anticonsumerism is a growing movement that encourages people to buy less and live more sustainably. 反消费主义是一项日益壮大的运动，鼓励人们少买东西，更可持续地生活。

Example:  She embraced anticonsumerism and started a community garden. 她接受了反消费主义，并开办了一个社区花园。|近义词：anti-materialism, simple living, minimalism|反义词: consumerism, materialism|词性:noun
