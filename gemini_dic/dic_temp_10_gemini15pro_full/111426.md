lizards:/ˈlɪzərdz/
n.
蜥蜴
reptiles that typically have four legs, a long body and tail, and movable eyelids.
Lizards bask in the sun to regulate their body temperature. 蜥蜴晒太阳来调节体温。
Lizards are cold-blooded animals. 蜥蜴是冷血动物。

近义词: reptiles, geckos (某些种类)
反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑哺乳动物 mammals 或鸟类 birds)
词性: noun
