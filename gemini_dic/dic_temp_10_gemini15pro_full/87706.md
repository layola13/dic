hexapoda:/hɛkˈsæpədə/ | n. | 六足亚门 |  A subphylum of arthropods, characterized by having six legs, which includes insects. | Insects, belonging to the Hexapoda subphylum, are incredibly diverse.  六足亚门中的昆虫种类繁多。| The class Insecta is synonymous with Hexapoda. 昆虫纲与六足亚门同义。| synonym: Insecta | antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps other arthropod subphyla like Chelicerata or Crustacea could be considered contrasting groups) | noun
