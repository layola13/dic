ETYMOLOGY->
From Yiddish בלינצע (blintse), from Belarusian блінцы (blintsy), plural of блін (blin, “pancake”), from Proto-Slavic *blinъ. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰlei- (“to shine, gleam”).  The shiny surface of the cooked pancake is the likely connection to the root.
