corvorant: /ˈkɔːrvərənt/ (US) /ˈkɔːvərənt/ (UK)| n.|鸬鹚；贪婪的人|A large, dark-colored waterbird with a long neck and a hooked bill, known for its diving ability and often used in Asia for fishing.  Figuratively, it refers to a greedy or rapacious person.|例句：The cormorant plunged into the water to catch a fish. 鸬鹚跳入水中捕鱼。

例句：He's a real cormorant when it comes to money.  说到钱，他真是个贪婪的人。|近义词：sea raven, shag, glutton|反义词: generous person|词性: noun
