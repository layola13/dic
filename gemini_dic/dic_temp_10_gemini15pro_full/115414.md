mantelpiece: /ˈmæntlˌpiːs/ (US), /ˈmæntlˌpiːs/ (UK)| n. |壁炉架，壁炉台|A shelf above a fireplace, usually part of a decorative frame surrounding it. |例句：She placed the family photo on the mantelpiece. 她把全家福放在壁炉台上。

例句：The mantelpiece was adorned with Christmas stockings. 壁炉台上装饰着圣诞袜。

近义词：fireplace shelf, chimneypiece|反义词: (No direct antonym exists) |词性: noun
