bellbird:/ˈbelbɜːrd/ (UK) /ˈbelbɜːrd/ (US)| n. |铃鸟|A bird known for its bell-like song.|例句：We woke to the sound of a bellbird in the rainforest. 我们在雨林中醒来，听到了铃鸟的叫声。|例句：The bellbird's song is a distinctive sound of the New Zealand bush. 铃鸟的歌声是新西兰灌木丛中独特的声音。|近义词：honeyeater (some species), tui (New Zealand bellbird)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From bell + bird.  The name refers to the clear, resonant song of these birds, which resembles the ringing of a bell.
