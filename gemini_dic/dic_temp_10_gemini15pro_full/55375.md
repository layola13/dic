distasting:/dɪsˈteɪstɪŋ/
adjective
令人厌恶的；使人反感的
causing or showing dislike or disgust.
The idea of eating insects is distasting to many people.  很多人觉得吃昆虫很令人反感。
He found the smell of the cheese distasting. 他觉得奶酪的味道很恶心。
近义词:repulsive, repugnant, offensive, unpalatable, disagreeable
反义词:appealing, attractive, pleasant, agreeable
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From dis- (expressing negation) + tasting (present participle of taste).  The word "taste" ultimately comes from Latin "taxare" meaning "to touch, handle, estimate."
