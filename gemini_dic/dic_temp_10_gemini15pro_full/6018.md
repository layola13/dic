|反义词:incurability, irreparability, unimprovability|
|词性: noun|


ETYMOLOGY->
From ameliorate (verb) + -able (suffix) + -ness (suffix).
"Ameliorate" comes from the Late Latin "amelioratus," past participle of "ameliorare," meaning "to make better," from Latin "melior," meaning "better."
"-able" forms adjectives meaning "capable of being," "worthy of being," or "tending to."
"-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
