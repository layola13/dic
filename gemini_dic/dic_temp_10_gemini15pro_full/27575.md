canals:/kəˈnælz/
n.
运河，水道
Artificial waterways constructed to allow the passage of boats or ships inland or to convey water for irrigation.
The Panama Canal connects the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans. 巴拿马运河连接大西洋和太平洋。
The canals of Venice are famous tourist attractions. 威尼斯的运河是著名的旅游景点。
近义词：waterways, channels
反义词：(没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 natural rivers 自然河流)
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *chanel*, from Latin canalis (“pipe, groove, channel”).
