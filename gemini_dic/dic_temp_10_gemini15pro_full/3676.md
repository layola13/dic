aggrandize: /əˈɡrændaɪz/ | vt. | 增加，夸大，提高 | To increase the power, status, or wealth of; to enhance or exaggerate the importance, power, or reputation of someone or something. |  He aggrandized his achievements to impress his boss. 他夸大了自己的成就来给老板留下深刻印象。

The king sought to aggrandize himself by conquering neighboring territories. 国王试图通过征服邻近的领土来扩大自己的权力。 | 近义词: enhance, exalt, augment, amplify, magnify | 反义词: diminish, belittle, reduce, minimize, detract | 词性: 动词
