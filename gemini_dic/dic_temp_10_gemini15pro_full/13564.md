attendant:/əˈtɛndənt/ | n. adj.| 服务员，侍者；出席者；伴随的 | a person whose job is to provide a service to the public in a particular place | The flight attendant offered us drinks.  空乘人员给我们提供了饮料。 | synonyms: assistant, helper, server, aide | antonyms: boss, manager | noun

attendant:/əˈtɛndənt/ | adj. | 伴随的，随之而来的 | accompanying or following as a consequence | There are attendant risks with any investment. 任何投资都有随之而来的风险。 | synonyms: accompanying, associated, related, connected | antonyms: separate, detached, unrelated | adjective
