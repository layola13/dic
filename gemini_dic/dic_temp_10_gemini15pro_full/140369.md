oilclothed:/ˈɔɪlkloθd/
adj.
涂油布的，覆盖着油布的
Covered or protected with oilcloth.
The table was oilclothed to protect it from spills. 桌子上铺了油布以防止泼溅。
近义词:waterproof, water-resistant
反义词:unprotected, exposed
词性:形容词

oilclothed:/ˈɔɪlkloθd/
n.
油布（通常指覆盖家具或地板的）
A piece of oilcloth, especially one used to cover furniture or floors.
The oilclothed kept the floor dry. 油布保持了地板干燥。
近义词:tarpaulin, oilskin
反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps something like "bare surface")
词性:名词

词源: oil + cloth + -ed.  "Oil" refers to the linseed oil or other similar oils used to treat the cloth. "Cloth" refers to the fabric base. "-ed" is a suffix indicating the presence of something, in this case being covered with oilcloth.
