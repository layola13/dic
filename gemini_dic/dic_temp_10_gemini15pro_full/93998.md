impalatable:/ɪmˈpælətəbl/| adj.|令人不快的；不好吃的；难接受的|Not pleasant to taste; unacceptable or disagreeable.|The medicine had an impalatable, bitter taste.  这种药有一种令人不快的苦味。|Example: The political candidate's views on immigration were impalatable to many voters. 这位政治候选人关于移民的观点让很多选民难以接受。|Synonyms: unappetizing, distasteful, objectionable, offensive, unacceptable|Antonyms: palatable, tasty, agreeable, pleasant, acceptable|adjective
