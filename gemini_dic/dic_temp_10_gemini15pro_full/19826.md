bimonthlies:/baɪˈmʌnθliz/
n.
双月刊；一月两次的出版物；一月两次的事件
Publications or events that occur every two months.
The library subscribes to several bimonthlies on gardening.  图书馆订阅了几种园艺方面的双月刊。
The company's bimonthlies meetings are held in January, March, May, July, September, and November.  该公司的双月会议在1月、3月、5月、7月、9月和11月举行。

近义词:bi-monthly publications, periodicals
反义词:weeklies, monthlies, quarterlies, annuals
