opiate:/ˈoʊpiət/ (US) /ˈəʊpiət/ (UK)| n. adj.| 鸦片制剂；麻醉剂；令人麻醉的东西 |A drug containing or derived from opium, used to relieve pain and induce sleep; something that has a soothing or dulling effect.|  She was given an opiate to help her sleep after the surgery. 她手术后服用了鸦片制剂来帮助睡眠。|Synonyms: narcotic, analgesic, painkiller, sedative|Antonyms: stimulant, invigorant| Noun, Adjective

opiate:/ˈoʊpiət/ (US) /ˈəʊpiət/ (UK)| adj.| 令人麻醉的；像鸦片的 |Resembling opium in its effects; inducing drowsiness or inaction.| The music had an opiate effect on the audience.  这音乐对观众有麻醉作用。|Synonyms: soporific, narcotic, numbing|Antonyms: stimulating, invigorating| Adjective
