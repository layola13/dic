french-kissed:/ˈfrɛntʃ kɪst/
adjective
法式轻吻的，轻微碰触的
lightly or briefly touched, as if by a French kiss; delicately brushed
Her lips were french-kissed by the morning sun. 她的嘴唇被晨曦轻吻。
近义词:grazed, brushed, kissed
反义词:soaked, drenched
