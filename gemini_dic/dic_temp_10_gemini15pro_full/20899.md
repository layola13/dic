bleachability:/ˌbliːtʃəˈbɪləti/
n.
可漂白性
The capacity of a material to be bleached or whitened by a bleaching agent.
This fabric has excellent bleachability.  这种织物具有优异的可漂白性。

近义词:whitening ability, decolorizing ability
反义词:non-bleachability, colorfastness
词性:noun

词源: bleach + -ability

bleach:/bliːtʃ/
v.  漂白
n.  漂白剂
From Middle English *blechen, from Old English *blǣċan (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *blaikijaną (“to bleach, whiten”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰleg- (“to shine, gleam, burn”). Cognate with Dutch bleken (“to bleach”), German bleichen (“to bleach”), Swedish bleka (“to bleach”).

-ability:/əˈbɪləti/
suffix.  表示“…的能力”或“…的可能性”
From Middle English -abilite, -ablete, from Old French -abilité, from Latin -abilitās, from habilis (“able”). Equivalent to -able +‎ -ity.
