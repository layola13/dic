overtell:/ˌoʊvərˈtɛl/| v. |讲述过分详细地；夸大；过分渲染|To tell a story in too much detail, often exaggerating or embellishing it.|He overtold the story, adding unnecessary details and dramatic flourishes. 他把故事讲得过于详细，添加了不必要的细节和夸张的修饰。|近义词：exaggerate, embellish, overstate|反义词：understate, downplay|词性:动词

Etymology: From Old English *ofertellan, from ofer- (over) + tellan (to tell).
