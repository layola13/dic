overfocussing:/ˌoʊvərˈfoʊkəsɪŋ/| n.|过度聚焦|The act of focusing excessively on one aspect of something, often to the detriment of others.  This can manifest as an inability to shift attention away from a particular thought, feeling, or sensation.|He was so overfocussing on the negative comments that he missed all the praise. 他过于关注负面评论，以至于错过了所有的赞扬。|近义词：obsessing, fixating|反义词: neglecting, ignoring|词性:名词 (derived from verb)


overfocus:/ˌoʊvərˈfoʊkəs/| v.|过度聚焦|To focus excessively on one aspect of something, often to the detriment of others.|She tends to overfocus on details and misses the bigger picture. 她往往过度关注细节而忽略了大局。|近义词：obsess, fixate|反义词: neglect, ignore|词性:动词

词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "focus" (from Latin "focus" meaning "hearth, fireplace," later used metaphorically for a center of activity or attention).
