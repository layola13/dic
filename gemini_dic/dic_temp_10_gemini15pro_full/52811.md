dilogy:/ˈdɪlədʒi/| n. |二部曲，两部曲|A dilogy is a literary or musical work consisting of two parts, often designed to be a complete work in itself, but also able to stand alone.|The author's fantasy dilogy captivated readers with its intricate world-building.  这位作者的奇幻二部曲以其错综复杂的世界构建吸引了读者。|Synonym: duology|Antonym: N/A|Noun
