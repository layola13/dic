orthographic:/ˌɔːθəˈɡræfɪk/ (US) /ˌɔːrθəˈɡræfɪk/| adj. |正字法的；拼写的|Orthographic errors can make your writing difficult to understand. 拼写错误会使你的文章难以理解。|The teacher marked all the orthographic mistakes in the essay. 老师在文章中标出了所有的拼写错误。|近义词：spelling, written|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective
