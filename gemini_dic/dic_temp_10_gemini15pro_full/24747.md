broodingly:/ˈbruːdɪŋli/| adv.|沉思地，忧郁地|In a way that shows deep unhappiness or thoughtfulness.|He stared broodingly out the window at the rain. 他忧郁地凝视着窗外的大雨。|Synonyms: thoughtfully, pensively, moodily, sullenly|Antonyms: cheerfully, brightly, happily|词性:副词

broodingly:/ˈbruːdɪŋli/| adv.|沉思地，（令人）抑鬱地|In a manner characterized by deep, often troubled thought; moodily.|She sat broodingly by the fire, lost in her thoughts. 她坐在火炉旁沉思，陷入了自己的思绪。|Synonyms: contemplatively, meditatively, morosely|Antonyms: blithely, lightheartedly, joyfully|词性:副词
