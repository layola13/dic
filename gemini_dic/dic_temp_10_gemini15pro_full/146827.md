overimpressionabilities:/ˌoʊvərɪmˌprɛʃənəˈbɪlətiz/
|n.| 过度敏感性，过度影响性 |The state of being excessively easily influenced or impressed.|  His overimpressionabilities made him susceptible to various scams. 他过度敏感的性格使他容易上当受骗。| 近义词: sensitivity, suggestibility, impressionability | 反义词:  resistance, imperviousness| 词性: noun


overimpressionable:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈprɛʃənəbəl/
|adj.| 过度敏感的，易受影响的 | Excessively easily influenced or impressed.| Young children are often overimpressionable.  小孩子通常很容易受影响。| 近义词: sensitive, suggestible, impressionable | 反义词: resistant, impervious | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
over- (prefix): from Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above, beyond".
impressionable (adjective): from impression (noun) + -able (suffix). Impression from Late Middle English: from Old French impression, from late Latin impressio(n-), from imprimere ‘imprint’, from in- ‘into’ + premere ‘press’.
-able (suffix): from Old French -able, from Latin -abilis.
