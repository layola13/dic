curioso:/kuˈrjoʊsoʊ/ (US), /kjʊəˈrɪəʊsəʊ/ (UK)| adj.|好奇的，古怪的|Inquisitive, eager to learn or know; strange or unusual.|He was a curioso, always collecting odd and interesting objects. 他是个好奇心重的人，总是收集一些稀奇古怪的东西。|The shop sold curious antiques and artifacts. 这家商店出售稀奇古怪的古董和文物。|近义词：inquisitive, nosy, peculiar, odd, strange, unusual|反义词：indifferent, apathetic, ordinary, common|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Spanish *curioso*, from Latin *cūrĭōsus* "careful, inquisitive, meticulous," derived from *cūra* "care, concern."
