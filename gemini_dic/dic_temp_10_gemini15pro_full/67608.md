extravascular:/ˌekstrəˈvæskjələr/
adjective
血管外的
Located or occurring outside a blood vessel.
The drug was administered extravascularly.  该药物被血管外给药。
Extravascular fluid accumulates in the tissues. 组织中积聚了血管外液体。
extracellular, interstitial
intravascular
