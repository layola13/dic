flattening:/ˈflætənɪŋ/ | n. | 变平; 扁平化; 夷平; (增长率、价格等的)下降, 减少 |The process of making something flat or flatter. ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *flatr, meaning "flat."  Related to Old English flett "floor, dwelling," and German Fläche "surface, area."
