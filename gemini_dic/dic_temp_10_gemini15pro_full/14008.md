aurist:/ˈɔːrɪst/
n.
耳科医生，耳科专家
A physician specializing in diseases of the ear.
An aurist can diagnose and treat ear infections.  耳科医生可以诊断和治疗耳部感染。
The aurist prescribed ear drops for my infection. 耳科医生给我开了耳药水治疗我的感染。
近义词:otologist, ear doctor, ear specialist
反义词:None
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin auris (“ear”) + -ist.
