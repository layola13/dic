croc:/krɒk/ (UK) /krɑːk/ (US)| n.|鳄鱼；（尤指）鳄鱼皮革|A large reptile with a long body, thick skin, and sharp teeth, that lives in rivers and lakes in tropical areas. Also refers to leather made from crocodile skin. |例句：
I saw a huge croc basking in the sun. 我看到一条巨大的鳄鱼在晒太阳。


She was carrying a handbag made of croc. 她提着一个鳄鱼皮手提包。

|近义词：crocodile, alligator|反义词:|noun
