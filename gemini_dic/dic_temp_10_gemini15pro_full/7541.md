annexation:/ˌænɛkˈseɪʃən/
n.|吞并，并吞；附加；附加物|The annexation of Austria by Germany in 1938. 1938年德国吞并奥地利。|近义词：acquisition, appropriation, seizure, takeover|反义词:cession, surrender|词性:名词

annex:/əˈnɛks/
vt.|吞并；附加；获得|The United States annexed Texas in 1845. 美国于1845年吞并了德克萨斯州。|近义词：acquire, appropriate, seize, conquer|反义词:cede, relinquish|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *annexionem, accusative of annexio (“act of joining”), from Latin annectere (“to bind to, fasten, connect”), from ad- (“to”) + nectere (“to bind”).
