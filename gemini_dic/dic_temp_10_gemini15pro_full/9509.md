antischolastic:/ˌænti-skəˈlæstɪk/
adjective
反对学究的；非学院派的；反学校教育的
opposed to or hostile to the principles or methods of scholasticism; not of or characteristic of a scholastic; not of or relating to schools or education.
His antischolastic attitude made him unpopular with the teachers. 他反对学究的态度使他不受老师欢迎。
Synonyms: anti-intellectual, unscholarly, philistine
Antonyms: scholarly, academic, intellectual
词性: adjective

antischolastic:/ˌænti-skəˈlæstɪk/
noun
反对学究者；非学院派人士
a person who is opposed to or hostile to the principles or methods of scholasticism.
He was considered an antischolastic by his peers.  他的同龄人认为他是一个反对学究的人。
Synonyms: anti-intellectual, philistine
Antonyms: scholar, academic
词性: noun
