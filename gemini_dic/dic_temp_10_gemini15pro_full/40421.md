contrarian:/kənˈtreəriən/ | n. adj. | 反向投资者；逆向思维者；持相反意见者 | a person who opposes or rejects popular opinion or current practice.  Someone who goes against the prevailing sentiment, especially in financial markets. | He's a contrarian investor, always betting against the market. 他是一位反向投资者，总是与市场反着来。| synonyms: nonconformist, dissenter, maverick, iconoclast | antonyms: conformist, follower, traditionalist | noun

contrarian:/kənˈtreəriən/ | adj. | 反向的；逆向的；持相反意见的 | opposing or rejecting popular opinion or current practice. | He took a contrarian view on the issue. 他在这个问题上采取了相反的观点。| synonyms: opposing, dissenting, unorthodox, unconventional | antonyms: conforming, conventional, orthodox, traditional | adjective
