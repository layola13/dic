lazaretto:/ˌlæzəˈrɛtoʊ/ (US), /ˌlæzəˈrɛtəʊ/ (UK)| n.|检疫所，隔离医院|A building or ship used for isolating people with infectious diseases, especially plague.|The ship was used as a lazaretto during the cholera outbreak. 这艘船在霍乱爆发期间被用作检疫所。|Synonyms: quarantine station, isolation hospital, pesthouse|Antonyms: hospital (general), clinic|noun

lazaretto:/ˌlæzəˈrɛtoʊ/ (US), /ˌlæzəˈrɛtəʊ/ (UK)| n.|隔离区|An area, especially within a larger hospital or medical facility, designated for isolating patients with infectious diseases.|The hospital set up a lazaretto for patients with tuberculosis. 医院为肺结核病人设立了隔离区。|Synonyms: quarantine zone, isolation ward|Antonyms: general ward|noun
