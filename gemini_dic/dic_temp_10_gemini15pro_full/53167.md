dipper:/ˈdɪpər/ (US), /ˈdɪpə/ (UK)| n.|北斗七星；浸渍器；汲取的人；河鸟|A person or thing that dips something into a liquid.  A ladle or scoop for dipping liquids.  A name for various birds that characteristically dip or dive in the water, such as the American dipper.  Also, a colloquial name for the constellation Ursa Major (the Big Dipper).|例句：He used a dipper to get some water from the bucket. 他用汲水器从桶里取了一些水。

例句：The dipper flew low over the stream, searching for food. 河鸟低飞掠过小溪，寻找食物。|近义词：ladle, scoop, water ouzel (for the bird)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
