dissentment:/dɪˈsɛntmənt/| n. |不满，不平|A feeling of resentment or disapproval regarding something regarded as unjust, offensive, or wrong.|  She harbored a deep dissentment towards her unfair treatment. 她对不公平的待遇深怀不满。|  He expressed his dissentment over the new policy. 他对新政策表示不满。|近义词：resentment, displeasure, dissatisfaction, grievance|反义词：contentment, satisfaction, approval|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *dessentement* (modern French *dissention*) and directly from Late Latin *dissentimentum*, from Latin *dissentire* "to disagree," from *dis-* "apart, away" + *sentire* "to feel."
