nursling: /ˈnɜːslɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈnɜːrslɪŋ/ (US)| n. | 婴儿，乳儿；幼儿 | A baby or young child still being nursed or breastfed.  An infant or child still needing care and attention. |  例句：The mother cradled her nursling close. 母亲紧紧地抱着她的婴儿。

例句：The nursling cried out in hunger. 婴儿饿得哭了起来。 | 近义词：infant, baby, toddler, suckling | 反义词: adult, grown-up | 词性: noun

Etymology: From nurse + -ling, a diminutive suffix.  Ultimately from Latin *nutricia, "wet-nurse," from nutrire, "to nourish."
