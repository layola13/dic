drape:/dreɪp/
n.|帷幔，窗帘；褶皱|A piece of cloth hung to decorate or cover something.|The drapes were drawn to block out the sunlight. 窗帘拉上了，以遮挡阳光。|curtain, drapery|blind|noun
drape:/dreɪp/
v.|披上，覆盖；(使)优雅地悬挂|To hang or arrange cloth loosely or casually over something.|She draped a shawl over her shoulders. 她把披肩披在肩上。|cover, adorn|uncover, expose|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "draper" meaning "to make cloth," from the Frankish "*drappan" meaning "to pull, drag," related to Old English "drepan" meaning "to strike, reap."
