multipartite:/ˌmʌltɪˈpɑːrtaɪt/ | adj. | 多部分的；多方的；多重的 | Divided into many parts; having many parts or divisions.  Involving several parties or groups. |  例句：The treaty was a multipartite agreement involving seven nations.  该条约是一个涉及七个国家的复式协定。

例句：The artist created a multipartite sculpture consisting of various materials.  这位艺术家创作了一个由多种材料组成的多部分雕塑。 | 近义词：multipart, many-sided, multifaceted | 反义词：simple, unified, single | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *multipartitus*, from *multus* "many" + *partitus* "divided," past participle of *partiri* "to divide."
