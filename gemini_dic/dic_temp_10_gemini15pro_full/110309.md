light-headed:/laɪtˈhɛdɪd/ | adj. | 头晕目眩的，轻飘飘的 | Feeling dizzy and slightly faint. |  I felt light-headed after standing up too quickly.  我站起来太快了，感觉头晕。|  dizzy, faint, giddy | clear-headed, steady | adjective

light-headed:/laɪtˈhɛdɪd/ | adj. |  轻率的，不谨慎的 |  Frivolous and not sensible. | He made some light-headed decisions after winning the lottery. 他中彩票后做了一些轻率的决定。|  frivolous, giddy, thoughtless | serious, cautious, prudent | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From light (adj.) + -headed (suffix).  The literal meaning is "having a light head," implying a feeling of dizziness or a lack of seriousness.
