heels:/hiːlz/
n.
鞋跟；高跟鞋；脚后跟
the back part of a shoe that is raised
She wore high heels to the party. 她穿着高跟鞋去参加了派对。
synonyms:stilettos, pumps, wedges
antonyms:flats


heels:/hiːlz/
n.
(速度比赛中)最后几步；冲刺阶段
the last few steps in a race
He won the race in a photo finish, just ahead by a heel. 他以微弱优势赢得了比赛，最后几步才领先。
synonyms:final stage, home stretch
antonyms:start, beginning


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hēla (attested only in the plural hēlan) "heel," from Proto-Germanic *hanhilon. Cognate with Old Norse hæll, Old Frisian hel, Middle Dutch hiele, Old High German hala, German Hacke "hock."
