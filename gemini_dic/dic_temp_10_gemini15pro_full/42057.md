cotangent:/koʊˈtæn.dʒənt/ | n. | 余切 |A trigonometric function that is equal to the ratio of the adjacent side to the opposite side of an angle in a right triangle. In other words, cot(θ) = 1/tan(θ) = adjacent/opposite. |The cotangent of 45 degrees is 1. | 近义词: None (as it's a specific trigonometric function) | 反义词: None (as it's a specific trigonometric function)| noun
