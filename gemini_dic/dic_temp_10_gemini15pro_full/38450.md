concessionaire:/kənˈseʃəˈneər/ | n. | 特许经营者， concession holder, concession operator, franchisee| A person or company to whom a concession has been granted.  The concessionaire operates the restaurant at the airport.  这家餐馆由特许经营者在机场经营。| synonym: licensee, operator | antonym: grantor, licensor | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *concessionnaire*, from *concession* (concession), from Late Latin *concessio* (granting), from Latin *concedere* (to yield, grant), from *com-* (together) + *cedere* (to go, yield).
