crimsoning:/ˈkrɪmzənɪŋ/ | v. | 变红，脸红 |  The sky was crimsoning as the sun began to set. |  As she realized her mistake, her cheeks began crimsoning.  天空随着太阳开始落山而变红。| 近义词: reddening, blushing, flushing | 反义词: paling, blanching | 词性: 动词

词源: From crimson (深红色) + -ing (动名词/现在分词后缀).  Crimson ultimately derives from the Arabic word "qirmiz," referring to a kermes insect used to produce a red dye.
