parascend:/ˌpærəˈsɛnd/ | v. |  上升，攀登（尤指借助降落伞） | To ascend using a parachute, typically a ram-air parachute designed for controlled descent and maneuvering. |  The paratroopers parascended from the aircraft and landed precisely on the target zone.  伞兵们从飞机上伞降，精确地降落在目标区域。 | Example:  He parascended down the cliff face, using the wind currents to his advantage. 他利用风力，顺着悬崖面伞降而下。 | Synonyms: descend, glide, parachute | Antonyms: ascend, climb | verb

parascend:/ˌpærəˈsɛnd/ | n. | 伞降, 空降 |  The act of descending using a parachute, particularly a ram-air parachute that allows for controlled maneuvering. | The parascend was successful, and the team landed safely near the summit. 伞降很成功，团队安全降落在峰顶附近。 | Example: The parascend allowed them to access a remote area otherwise inaccessible. 伞降让他们得以进入一个原本无法到达的偏远地区。 | Synonyms: descent, landing, drop | Antonyms: ascent, climb | noun
