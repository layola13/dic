bravado:/brəˈvɑːdoʊ/ (US), /brəˈvɑːdəʊ/ (UK)| n.| 虚张声势，逞能|A show of boldness intended to impress or intimidate. |He displayed a false bravado that hid his insecurity. 他表现出一种虚假的 bravado 以掩盖他的不安全感。|例句：Despite his bravado, I could tell he was nervous. 尽管他装作勇敢，但我看得出他很紧张。

例句：Her bravado impressed her friends but not her teacher. 她的虚张声势给她的朋友们留下了深刻印象，但她的老师却没有。

近义词：swagger, bluster, boastfulness, ostentation|反义词: timidity, humility, modesty|词性: noun
