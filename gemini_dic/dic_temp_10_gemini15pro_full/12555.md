aspirationally:/ˌæspəˈreɪʃənəli/
adv. |充满抱负地；积极进取地| In a way that expresses hopes and ambitions, often in a way that is not realistic. | She spoke aspirationally about becoming a CEO one day. 她充满抱负地谈到有一天要成为首席执行官。|  例句：He aspirationally talked about buying a yacht. 他充满希望地谈论着买一艘游艇。| 近义词：ambitiously, hopefully, optimistically | 反义词：pessimistically, realistically, apathetically| 词性: 副词
