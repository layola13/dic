complicity:/kəmˈplɪsɪti/ | n. | 共谋，同谋，串通 | involvement in an illegal activity or wrongdoing. |  He denied any complicity in the robbery. 他否认参与了抢劫。

The investigation revealed the complicity of several government officials. 调查揭露了几位政府官员的同谋关系。

| 近义词：collusion, connivance, collaboration | 反义词：innocence, non-involvement | 词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->

From Late Latin *complicitatem, from Latin complicit-, past participle stem of complicare (“to fold together, embrace, involve”), from com- (“together”) + plicare (“to fold”).
