don:/dɒn/ (British), /dɑːn/ (American)| vt.|穿上，戴上；披上；装出，扮作|To put on (an item of clothing).|He donned his coat and hat and went out. 他穿上了外套，戴上帽子，出去了。

don:/dɒn/ (British), /dɑːn/ (American)| vt.|穿上，戴上；披上；装出，扮作|To assume or put on (a particular expression or quality).|She donned a look of surprise. 她装出一副惊讶的样子。

don:/dɒn/ (British), /dɑːn/ (American)| n.|大学教师，指导教师；西班牙贵族|A university teacher, especially a senior member of a college at Oxford or Cambridge.|He was a don at Oxford University. 他是牛津大学的一位指导教师。
