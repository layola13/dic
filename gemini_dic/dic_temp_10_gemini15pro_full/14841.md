axisymmetry:/ˌæksiˈsɪmətri/ | n. | 轴对称 |The property of being symmetric about an axis.  A body is axisymmetric if it can be generated by rotating a plane curve about an axis.  It is characterized by having rotational symmetry about a central axis. | The cylindrical pipe exhibits axisymmetry.  圆柱形管道呈现轴对称。| synonyms: rotational symmetry, axial symmetry | antonyms: asymmetry, non-axisymmetry | noun

axisymmetric:/ˌæksiˈsɪmɛtrɪk/ | adj. | 轴对称的 | Having symmetry around an axis.  Describing an object or shape that is identical on either side of a central axis. | The design of the vase is axisymmetric. 花瓶的设计是轴对称的。| synonyms: rotationally symmetric, axially symmetric | antonyms: asymmetric, non-axisymmetric | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "axi-" (combining form of "axis") + "-symmetry" (from Greek "symmetria," meaning "agreement in dimensions, due proportion, arrangement").
