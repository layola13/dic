echauguette:/eɪʃəˈɡɛt/ | n. |瞭望台，观察哨|A watchtower or observation post, often used in a military context.|例句：The soldier kept watch from the echauguette. 士兵在瞭望台上守望。

例句：From the echauguette, they could see the enemy approaching. 从瞭望台上，他们可以看到敌人正在靠近。|近义词：watchtower, lookout, observation post|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "hiding place", "concealment")|词性:noun
