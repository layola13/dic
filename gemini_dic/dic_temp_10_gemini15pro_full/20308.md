biseriate:/baɪˈsɪəriət/
adjective
双列的；二系列的
Arranged in two rows or series.
The stomata are biseriate, arranged in two longitudinal rows, one on each side of the midrib. 气孔是双列的，排列成两纵行，在中脉的两侧各一行。

biseriate:/baɪˈsɪəriət/
adjective
(of a vascular bundle) having two groups of phloem, on either side of the xylem.
（指维管束）具有两组韧皮部，位于木质部的两侧。
The vascular bundles in the stem are biseriate. 茎中的维管束是双列的。


近义词:double-rowed, two-seriate
反义词:uniseriate (单列的)
词性:adjective
