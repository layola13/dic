linseed:/ˈlɪnˌsiːd/| n. |亚麻籽|The seeds of the flax plant, used as a source of oil and fiber, and also medicinally.|Linseed oil is used in paints and varnishes. 亚麻籽油用于油漆和清漆。|Synonyms: flaxseed|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

linseed oil:/ˈlɪnˌsiːd ɔɪl/| n. |亚麻籽油|Oil extracted from linseeds.|Linseed oil is a drying oil, meaning it hardens when exposed to air. 亚麻籽油是一种干性油，这意味着它暴露在空气中会变硬。|Synonyms: flaxseed oil|Antonyms: N/A|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *līnsæd, from līn (“flax”) + sæd (“seed”). Cognate with Dutch lijnzaad, German Leinsaat, Swedish linfrö.
