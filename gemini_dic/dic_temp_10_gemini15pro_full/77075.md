garotters:/ˈɡærətərz/
n.
扼杀者；勒索者；抢劫犯
A person who garottes someone; a robber who strangles victims.
The garotters attacked the old man in the dark alley.  勒索者在黑暗的小巷里袭击了那个老人。

近义词: stranglers, muggers, robbers, thieves, thugs
反义词: protector, rescuer

词性: noun (plural)

词源: From Spanish *garrote*, an iron collar formerly used in Spain for capital punishment, from Old Occitan *garrot* "stick, cudgel."
