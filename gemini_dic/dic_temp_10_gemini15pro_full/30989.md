chalet:/ˈʃæleɪ/ (US) /ˈʃæleɪ/ (UK)| n. |小木屋；度假屋|A wooden house or cottage with overhanging eaves, typically found in the Swiss Alps. |例句：We rented a chalet in the mountains for our ski trip. 我们在山上租了一间小木屋滑雪度假。

例句：The chalet offered stunning views of the valley. 这座小木屋可以欣赏到山谷的壮丽景色。

|近义词：cabin, cottage, lodge|反义词:apartment, skyscraper|词性:noun
