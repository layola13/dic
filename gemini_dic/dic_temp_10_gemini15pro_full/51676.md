devilhead:/ˈdɛvəlˌhɛd/| n. |恶魔头，恶棍，坏蛋|A cruel or wicked person; a rogue or scoundrel.  It can also literally refer to a representation of a devil's head.|He acted like a real devilhead, tormenting the smaller children.|近义词：villain, scoundrel, rogue, miscreant|反义词: saint, angel|词性:名词

devilhead (Alternative spelling: devil-head): /ˈdɛvəlˌhɛd/ | n. |恶魔头| Sometimes used to describe a particularly troublesome or mischievous person, or a representation of a devil's head. |The kids called him devilhead because he was always pulling pranks. | 近义词: rascal, scamp, troublemaker | 反义词: angel | 词性: 名词
