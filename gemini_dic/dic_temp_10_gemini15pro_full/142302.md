orthodiagonal:/ˌɔːrθoʊdaɪˈæɡənəl/| adj.|正交对角线的，正交对角的|Relating to or denoting a diagonal that is orthogonal to another diagonal or to a specific set of lines or planes.  A line or vector that is perpendicular to another line or vector is said to be orthogonal to it.|The orthodiagonal axes of the ellipse are perpendicular. 椭圆的正交对角轴是垂直的.|orthogonal, perpendicular|oblique, askew|adjective
