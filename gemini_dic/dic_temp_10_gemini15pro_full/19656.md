bilamellate:/baɪˈlæməleɪt/ | adj. | 双层板的；由两个薄片组成的 | Composed of two lamellae or thin plates or layers. |  The bilamellate structure of the fish's gills allows for efficient oxygen uptake.  这种鱼的鳃的双层板结构使其能够有效地吸收氧气。| 近义词: laminated, layered | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "unilamellar" if referring to a single layer) | 词性: 形容词

词源: bi- (two) + lamella (thin plate) + -ate (having the form of)
