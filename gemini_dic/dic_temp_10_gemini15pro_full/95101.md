inane:/ɪˈneɪn/| adj.|空洞的；愚蠢的；无意义的|Lacking sense, substance, or significance; silly.|Her comments were completely inane and added nothing to the conversation. 她的评论完全是空洞无物，对谈话没有任何贡献。|The politician's inane remarks were met with laughter.  这位政客愚蠢的言论引来了笑声。|近义词：silly, foolish, absurd, pointless, vapid|反义词：sensible, intelligent, meaningful, profound|词性:形容词
词源:Late 16th century: from Latin *inanis ‘empty, vain’, from in- ‘not’ + root related to annus ‘year’ (with the sense ‘unproductive, barren’).
