genettes:/dʒɪˈnɛts/| n.| Genet, 也可指香貓|Small, slender carnivorous mammals of the genus Genetta, native to Africa but also found in parts of Europe and the Middle East. They are known for their spotted coats, long tails, and nocturnal habits.|Genettes are known for their excellent climbing abilities. 香貓以其出色的攀爬能力而闻名。|synonyms: civet, civet cat|antonyms: (No direct antonym for an animal.  Could use "domesticated animal" for contrast)|noun
