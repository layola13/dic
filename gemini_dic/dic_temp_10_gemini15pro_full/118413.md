merriness:/ˈmerɪnɪs/
noun
快乐，高兴，愉快
The state of being merry; cheerful or joyful activity.
The room was filled with merriness and laughter. 房间里充满了欢声笑语。
近义词: gaiety, mirth, cheerfulness, joviality
反义词: sadness, gloom, unhappiness, dejection
词性:n.

Etymology:
From Middle English *merinesse*, from Old English *myrignes*, from *myrige* ("pleasant, merry") + *-nes* (suffix forming abstract nouns). Cognate with Old High German *murignessa* ("merriness").
