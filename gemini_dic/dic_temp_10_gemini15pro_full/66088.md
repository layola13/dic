excavational:/ˌekskəˈveɪʃənəl/
adjective
挖掘的; 开凿的
Relating to or involved in excavation.
The excavational work unearthed a Roman villa.  挖掘工作出土了一座罗马别墅。
The team used excavational techniques to uncover the ancient city. 团队使用挖掘技术发现了这座古城。
近义词: unearthing, digging, excavating
反义词: burying, filling, covering
词性: adjective

excavation:/ˌekskəˈveɪʃən/
noun
挖掘; 开凿; 挖掘现场; 出土文物
The act of digging or removing earth, especially to find buried objects or to create a cavity or hole; the site of an excavation; something unearthed.
The excavation revealed ancient artifacts. 挖掘工作发现了古代文物。
The excavation of the site took several months.  该遗址的挖掘工作花了几个月时间。
近义词: digging, unearthing, disinterment
反义词: burial, interment, filling
词性: noun

词源:
From Late Latin excavationem, noun of action from past participle stem of excavare "to hollow out," from ex- "out" + cavare "to hollow," related to cavus "hollow."
