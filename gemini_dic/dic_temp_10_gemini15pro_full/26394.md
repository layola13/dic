by-the-numbers:/ˌbaɪ ðə ˈnʌmbərz/ | adj. | 按部就班地；机械地；系统地 |  Methodically and systematically, following a set procedure or plan.  It implies a lack of creativity or spontaneity. | The investigation was conducted by-the-numbers, following established protocols.  调查是按部就班地进行的，遵循了既定的协议。|  Example: The artist felt stifled by the by-the-numbers approach to painting taught at the academy.  这位艺术家觉得学院里按部就班的绘画教学方法让他感到窒息。 | Synonyms: methodical, systematic, routine, formulaic, conventional | Antonyms: creative, imaginative, spontaneous, improvisational | adjective
