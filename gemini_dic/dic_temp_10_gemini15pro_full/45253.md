cultishnesses:/ˈkʌltɪʃnɪsɪz/
n.
狂热；对某事物或某人的过度迷恋；膜拜
excessive or exaggerated devotion to something or someone; the quality of resembling a cult
The cultishnesses surrounding the new band surprised even the music critics.  这个新乐队周围的狂热气氛甚至让音乐评论家都感到惊讶。
近义词:fanaticism, zealotry, obsession
反义词:apathy, indifference
词性:名词

cultish:/ˈkʌltɪʃ/
adj.
狂热的；像邪教的
having characteristics of a cult; excessively devoted
The band had a small but cultish following.  这支乐队拥有一小群但狂热的追随者。
近义词:cultlike, faddish
反义词:mainstream, conventional
词性:形容词

cult:/kʌlt/
n.
狂热的追随者；邪教；膜拜
a system of religious veneration and devotion directed toward a particular figure or object; a relatively small group of people having religious beliefs or practices regarded by others as strange or sinister
The movie has achieved cult status.  这部电影已获得了狂热的地位。
近义词:sect, clique, following
反义词:mainstream
词性:名词
