half-alike:/ˌhæf əˈlaɪk/ | adj. | 半相似的，略微相似的 | Resembling each other to some degree but not exactly. | The twins were half-alike, sharing some features but with distinct differences. 这对双胞胎半相像，有一些共同特征，但也有一些明显的区别。 | 近义词: similar, resembling, akin | 反义词: dissimilar, different, unlike | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "half-" (一半) + "alike" (相似的).  A straightforward compound word combining the concepts of partial and similar.
