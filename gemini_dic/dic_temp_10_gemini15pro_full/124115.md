mortar:/ˈmɔːrtər/ (UK) /ˈmɔːr.t̬ɚ/ (US)| n. v. |灰浆；迫击炮；研钵；用灰浆砌| A mixture of lime or cement, sand, and water, used in building to bond bricks or stones. Also, a short, high-angle-firing cannon used to lob shells.  Can also refer to a bowl used for grinding substances.|

例句：
The builder mixed the mortar and applied it to the bricks.  建筑工人混合了灰浆并将其涂抹在砖块上。

The mortar fire rained down on the enemy positions. 迫击炮火力倾泻在敌人的阵地上。

近义词：(n.) cement, plaster, grout, (v.) bombard, shell
反义词:  (No direct antonym for mortar)
词性: noun, verb


mortar:/ˈmɔːrtər/ (UK) /ˈmɔːr.t̬ɚ/ (US)| n. |研钵|A bowl, typically made of hard material, in which ingredients are ground or crushed with a pestle.|

例句：
She used a mortar and pestle to grind the spices. 她用研钵和杵研磨香料。

The pharmacist used a mortar to prepare the medicine. 药剂师用研钵来配制药物。

近义词: pestle and mortar, bowl
反义词: (No direct antonym for mortar in this sense)
词性: noun
