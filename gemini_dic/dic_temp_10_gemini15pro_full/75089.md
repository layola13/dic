frillies:/ˈfrɪliz/
noun
女式内衣；褶边，饰边
Women's underwear or lingerie, often trimmed with lace or ruffles.
She bought some new frillies for her honeymoon. 她为蜜月买了一些新的内衣。
Synonyms: lingerie, underwear, undergarments, intimate apparel
Antonyms: outerwear
词性: 名词(plural noun)


frilly:/ˈfrɪli/
adjective
有褶边的；饰边的
Decorated with frills or ruffles.
She wore a frilly pink dress. 她穿着一件粉红色的褶边连衣裙。
Synonyms: ruffled, lacy, ornate, fussy
Antonyms: plain, simple, unadorned
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From frill + -y/-ies. "Frill" originates from the late 16th century, likely from a Low German or Dutch source, related to words for "curl" or "ripple".
