improbabilities:/ɪmˌprɑːbəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 不可能性；极不可能发生的事情；没有希望 |The improbabilities of the situation made it all the more exciting.  情况的不可能使得它更加令人兴奋。|例句：The sheer improbabilities of her story made it hard to believe. 她故事的极不可能使人难以置信。

例句：Despite the improbabilities, they managed to succeed. 尽管不太可能，他们还是设法成功了。 | 近义词：unlikelihoods, impossibilities | 反义词：likelihoods, possibilities | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From *improbable* + *-ities*. *Improbable* comes from French *improbable*, from Latin *improbabilis*, from *in-* (not) + *probabilis* (probable).  *-ities* is a suffix used to form plural nouns indicating a quality or state.
