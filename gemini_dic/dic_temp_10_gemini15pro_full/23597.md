bramble:/ˈbræmbəl/ | n. | 荆棘，黑莓灌木 | A prickly shrub, especially a blackberry bush. |  The path was overgrown with brambles. 小路被荆棘覆盖了。| Synonyms: thicket, thornbush | Antonyms: meadow, clearing | noun

bramble:/ˈbræmbəl/ | v. |  在荆棘中穿行，缠绕 | To scramble or clamber through brambles. | We had to bramble through the undergrowth to reach the river. 我们不得不穿过灌木丛才能到达河边。| Synonyms: scramble, clamber | Antonyms: glide, sail | verb
