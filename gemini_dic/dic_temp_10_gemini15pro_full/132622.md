nondisarmament: /ˌnɒndɪsˈɑːməmənt/ | n. |不裁军，不解除武装|The state of not disarming; refusal or failure to disarm.  The opposite of disarmament, meaning the reduction or elimination of military forces and weapons.|  The country's policy of nondisarmament has led to increased tensions in the region.  该国的不裁军政策导致该地区紧张局势加剧。 |Near synonyms:  non-reduction of arms,  maintenance of military strength, armament| Antonyms: disarmament, demilitarization| Noun
