criticizable:/ˈkrɪtɪsaɪzəbl/
adjective
可批评的; 可非难的
Open to or deserving of criticism.
His conduct is highly criticizable. 他的行为非常值得批评。
Synonyms: censurable, reproachable, blameworthy, reprehensible, culpable
Antonyms: commendable, laudable, praiseworthy
词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From criticize + -able.  Criticize comes from the Greek kritikos "able to judge," from krinein "to separate, decide."
