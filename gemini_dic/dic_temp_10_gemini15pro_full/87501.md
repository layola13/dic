heterochronic:/ˌhɛtərəˈkrɒnɪk/ | adj. | 异时性的，异时发生的 | Relating to a change in the timing of developmental events in an organism compared to its ancestors. | Heterochronic mutations can cause significant changes in the morphology of an organism. 异时性突变会导致生物体形态的显著变化。

The heterochronic shift in the development of the feathers led to the evolution of flight. 羽毛发育的异时性转变导致了飞行的进化。


|近义词：allochronic, heterochronous |反义词: isochronic, synchronous |词性: adjective
