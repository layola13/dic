demisability:/ˌdɛmiəˈbɪləti/ | n. |轻微残疾，部分残疾|A condition of having a mild or partial disability, not severe enough to prevent someone from performing most daily activities but still presenting some challenges. |Example 1:  His demisability made it difficult for him to climb stairs, but he could still walk short distances.  他的轻微残疾使他难以爬楼梯，但他仍然可以走一小段路。

Example 2:  Despite her demisability, she excelled in her academic studies. 尽管她有轻微残疾，她在学业上仍然表现出色。 |Near synonyms: mild disability, partial disability, slight impairment |Antonyms: full disability, severe disability, able-bodiedness| noun
