epigynous:/ˌepɪˈdʒaɪnəs/| adj.|上位花的，子房上位的|Having the ovary enclosed in the receptacle, with the other floral parts appearing to arise from its summit.|The apple blossom is epigynous, meaning the ovary is enclosed within the receptacle.| 近义词: inferior ovary | 反义词: hypogynous, perigynous |词性: adjective
