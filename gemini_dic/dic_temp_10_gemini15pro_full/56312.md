doghouse:/ˈdɒɡˌhaʊs/ (UK) /ˈdɑːɡˌhaʊs/ (US)| n. |狗窝；困境；失宠|A small shelter for a dog, typically placed outdoors. Also refers to being in trouble or out of favor. |The dog was banished to the doghouse after chewing up the sofa.  这只狗在咬坏沙发后被赶到狗窝里去了。|Synonyms: kennel,  hut,  in the dog house, in disfavor |Antonyms: favor, good graces |noun
