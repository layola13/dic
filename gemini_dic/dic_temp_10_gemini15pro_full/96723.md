inefficacy:/ˌɪnˈɛfɪkəsi/ | n. | 无效；无能；无效力 | Lack of power to produce a desired effect; ineffectiveness. | The inefficacy of the drug was apparent after several trials. 这种药物的无效性在几次试验后变得显而易见。| Synonyms: impotence, uselessness, futility, failure | Antonyms: efficacy, effectiveness, potency, success | noun
