fuca:/ˈfjuːkə/| n. |褐藻，墨角藻|A genus of brown algae, commonly known as rockweed or wrack. These algae are found in intertidal zones of rocky seashores.|Example: Fuca is a common sight along the coast.  褐藻在海岸线上很常见。|Example:  Scientists are studying the properties of Fuca for potential medicinal uses. 科学家正在研究墨角藻的特性，以寻找潜在的药用价值。|Synonym: rockweed, wrack|Antonym: N/A|noun
