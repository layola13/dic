barterer:/ˈbɑːrtərər/ | n. | 物物交换者；以货易货的人 | A person who exchanges goods or services for other goods or services without using money. |  Example:  The barterer traded his surplus grain for tools.  这个物物交换者用他多余的谷物换取工具。

Example: In the absence of a common currency, the villagers became adept barterers.  由于缺乏通用货币，村民们成了熟练的物物交换者。| 近义词: trader, exchanger, swapper | 反义词: buyer, seller, purchaser | 词性: noun | 词源: From the verb "barter," which comes from Old French "barater" (to cheat, haggle).
