eily: /ˈaɪli/ | n. | 艾丽（女子名） | Eily is a diminutive of Eileen, which is the Anglicized form of the Irish name Ailín, meaning "light."  It is also sometimes considered a variant of Emily. | Eily is a beautiful name for a girl. 艾丽是一个女孩的漂亮名字。| My friend Eily is a talented musician. 我的朋友艾丽是一位才华横溢的音乐家。 | 近义词: Eileen, Emily | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词
