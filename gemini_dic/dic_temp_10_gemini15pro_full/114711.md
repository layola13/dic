malleate:/ˈmælieɪt/ /ˈmɑːlieɪt/
|v.|锤打，延展|To hammer into thin sheets; to shape or form by beating with a hammer.  To extend or spread thinly.|The blacksmith malleated the iron into a horseshoe. 铁匠把铁锤打成马蹄铁。|近义词：hammer, forge, beat, shape, flatten, extend, spread|反义词:compress, thicken, contract|verb


malleable:/ˈmæliəbl/ /ˈmɑːliəbl/
|adj.|可锻的，可塑的，易受影响的|Capable of being hammered or rolled into thin sheets without breaking. Adaptable or tractable.|Gold is a highly malleable metal. 金是一种高度延展性的金属。His young mind was still malleable. 他年轻的心灵仍然可塑。|近义词：pliable, ductile, adaptable, flexible, tractable|反义词:inflexible, rigid, unyielding, brittle|adjective
