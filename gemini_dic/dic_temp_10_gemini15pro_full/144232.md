outsliding:/ˈaʊtslaɪdɪŋ/
verb
向外滑动; 向外滑行
The action of sliding outwards.
The drawer was outsliding smoothly. 抽屉平稳地向外滑出。
近义词: extending, protruding, sliding out
反义词: retracting, sliding in
词性: verb (动词)

outsliding:/ˈaʊtslaɪdɪŋ/
adjective
向外滑动的
Sliding outwards.
The outsliding drawer caught on something. 向外滑动的抽屉被什么东西卡住了。
近义词: extending, protruding
反义词: retracting
词性: adjective (形容词)
