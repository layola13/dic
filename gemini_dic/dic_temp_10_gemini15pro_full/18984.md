bestirring:/bɪˈstɜːrɪŋ/
verb
激勵；喚起；鼓動；使奮起
To rouse to action; stir up.
He finally bestirred himself to finish the project.  他終於奮起精神完成了這個項目。
The coach bestirred his team to play harder. 教練激勵他的隊員們更加努力地比賽。
synonyms: rouse, stir, activate, galvanize, energize
antonyms: calm, soothe, pacify, quiet
词性: 动词
