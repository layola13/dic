nonfissility:/ˌnɑːnfɪsɪləti/
n.
不可裂变性
The inability of a substance to undergo nuclear fission.
Uranium-238 exhibits nonfissility under normal conditions.  铀-238在正常情况下表现出不可裂变性。
The nonfissility of the material makes it safe for handling.  这种材料的不可裂变性使其可以安全处理。
nonfissionable, non-fissile
fissility, fissionable
noun
