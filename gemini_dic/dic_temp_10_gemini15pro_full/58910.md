durometer:/duːˈrɒmɪtər (UK), duˈrɑːmɪtər (US)| n. |硬度计|An instrument for measuring the hardness of materials, especially rubber and plastics.  It measures the resistance of a material to indentation.|The technician used a durometer to test the hardness of the rubber.|Synonyms: hardness tester, sclerometer|Antonyms: N/A|noun

durometer:/duːˈrɒmɪtər (UK), duˈrɑːmɪtər (US)| n. |硬度|A measure of hardness obtained using a durometer.|The durometer of the tire was 60 Shore A.|Synonyms: hardness reading, hardness value|Antonyms: softness|noun
