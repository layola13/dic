hermaphroditism:/hɜːrˈmæfrədaɪtɪzəm/ | n. |雌雄同体;两性状态 |The condition of having both male and female reproductive organs. | Hermaphroditism is rare in mammals. 哺乳动物的雌雄同体现象很罕见。|  Hermaphroditism can occur in some fish species.  有些鱼类会出现雌雄同体现象。 | synonyms: intersexuality, bisexuality (in biology), androgyny (in botany) | antonyms: unisexuality, gonochorism | noun

hermaphrodite:/hɜːrˈmæfrədaɪt/ | n. adj. |雌雄同体者;两性体 | An organism that has both male and female reproductive organs. | The earthworm is a hermaphrodite. 蚯蚓是雌雄同体动物。 | Some flowers are hermaphrodites, containing both stamens and pistils.  有些花是雌雄同体的，既有雄蕊也有雌蕊。| synonyms: intersex, bisexual (in biology), androgynous (in botany) | antonyms: unisexual, gonochoristic | noun, adjective
