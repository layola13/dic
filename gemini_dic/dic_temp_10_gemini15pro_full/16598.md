baroka:/bəˈroʊkə/
adjective
巴洛克式的；巴洛克风格的
Relating to or characteristic of a style of European architecture, music, and art of the 17th and 18th centuries that followed mannerism and is characterized by ornate detail, curves, and counterpoint.
The palace is a magnificent example of baroka architecture. 这座宫殿是巴洛克式建筑的宏伟典范。
Near synonyms: baroque, ornate, elaborate, flamboyant
Opposite: simple, plain
word class: adjective
