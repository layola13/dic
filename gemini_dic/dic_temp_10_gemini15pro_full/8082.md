anthroponomics:/ˌænθrəpəˈnɒmɪks/
n.|人类经济学；人类经济活动研究|The study of human economic activity, especially in relation to environmental and social factors. |Anthroponomics considers the impact of human activities on the environment.  人类经济学考虑了人类活动对环境的影响。|Anthroponomics explores how economic systems can be designed to be more sustainable. 人类经济学探索如何设计更可持续的经济系统。| 近义词: human economics, ecological economics| 反义词: N/A| 词性: noun
