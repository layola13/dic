canalizing:/ˈkænəlaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
引导的，限制的，槽化的，渠化的
restricting or limiting development to a particular path or outcome.
例句：Canalizing selection tends to maintain phenotypic uniformity within populations. 引导选择倾向于维持群体内的表型一致性。

例句：The canalizing genes buffer the development of the organism against environmental or genetic disturbances.  引导基因缓冲生物体发育免受环境或遗传干扰。
近义词：restricting, limiting, directing, channeling
反义词：divergent, varied, flexible
词性 adjective
