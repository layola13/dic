eudemonistical:/ˌjuːdɪˈmɒnɪstɪkəl/ | adj. | 功利主义的; 快乐主义的; 幸福主义的 | Of or relating to eudemonism; based on the pursuit of happiness. |  He held a eudemonistical view of morality, believing that actions are right in proportion as they tend to promote happiness. 他持有快乐主义的道德观，认为行为的正确性与它们促进幸福的程度成正比。| synonyms: hedonistic, utilitarian, Epicurean | antonyms: ascetic, self-denying | adjective

eudemonism:/ˌjuːdɪˈmɒnɪzəm/ | n. | 功利主义; 快乐主义; 幸福主义 | A system of ethics that bases moral obligation on the pursuit of happiness. | Eudemonism prioritizes happiness and well-being as the ultimate goals of human life.  幸福主义将幸福和福祉作为人类生活的终极目标。 | synonyms: hedonism, utilitarianism | antonyms: asceticism | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *eudaimōnia* "happiness, prosperity,"  from *eu* "good" + *daimōn* "divine power, fate, fortune."
