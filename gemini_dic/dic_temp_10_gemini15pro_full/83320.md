gypping:/ˈdʒɪpɪŋ/| v. n.|欺骗，诈骗，敲竹杠|Cheating or defrauding someone, especially by charging them too much money for something.|例句：I felt like I was gypped when I saw the low quality of the product. 我看到产品质量如此低劣，感觉自己被骗了。

例句：The tourist was gypped out of $100 by the street vendor.  这个游客被街头小贩骗走了100美元。|近义词：cheating, swindling, defrauding, conning|反义词：honest dealing, fair trade|词性：动词/名词

gypped:/ˈdʒɪpt/| adj.|被骗的，被敲竹杠的|Feeling cheated or defrauded.|例句：I felt gypped after paying so much for a broken product.  我为一件坏掉的产品付了这么多钱，感觉被骗了。

例句：He felt completely gypped when he realized the car he bought was faulty. 他意识到自己买的车有问题时，感觉完全被骗了。|近义词：cheated, swindled, defrauded, conned|反义词：satisfied, content|词性：形容词
