lumenal:/ˈluːmɪnəl/| adj.|管腔的，腔内的|Relating to or located within the lumen of a tubular structure, such as a blood vessel or the intestine.|The lumenal surface of the intestine is lined with villi. 肠道的腔内表面衬有绒毛。|近义词：intraluminal|反义词：extraluminal|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin "lumen" (light, opening) + "-al" (suffix forming adjectives).
