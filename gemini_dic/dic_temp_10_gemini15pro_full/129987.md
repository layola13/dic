non-mutable:/ˌnɒn ˈmjuːtəbl/ | adj. | 不可变的 | Not able to be changed after creation.  |  Example: Strings in Python are non-mutable, meaning you cannot change individual characters after the string is created. Python中的字符串是不可变的，这意味着字符串创建后您无法更改单个字符。

Example: The constant value of pi is non-mutable.  圆周率的常数值是不可变的。 | Synonyms: immutable, unchangeable, fixed, constant | Antonyms: mutable, changeable, variable | adjective
