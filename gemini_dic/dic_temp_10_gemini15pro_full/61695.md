emollient:/ɪˈmɒliənt/ /ɪˈmɑːliənt/| n. adj.|润肤剂，软化剂；润肤的，软化性的|A substance that softens or soothes the skin.|例句：This cream contains natural emollients to moisturize dry skin.  这款面霜含有天然润肤剂，可以滋润干燥的皮肤。|近义词：moisturizer, softener, balm|反义词:irritant, astringent|词性:名词, 形容词
emollients:/ɪˈmɒliənts/ /ɪˈmɑːliənts/| n.|润肤剂，软化剂|Substances that soften or soothe the skin.|例句：Many emollients are available over the counter. 许多润肤剂是非处方药。|近义词：moisturizers, softeners, balms|反义词:irritants, astringents|词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin *emollient-, from Latin emollire ‘to soften,’ from e- (variant of ex-) ‘out’ + mollire ‘to soften.’
