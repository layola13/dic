evander:/ɪˈvændər/| n. |埃万德（男子名）|A male given name, of Greek origin, meaning "good man" or "well-meaning man". It's derived from the Greek elements "eu" (good) and "aner" (man).  It is also associated with Evander, the Arcadian king in Virgil's Aeneid who welcomed Aeneas to Italy.|例句：Evander is a popular name in some parts of Europe. 埃万德在欧洲某些地区是一个流行的名字。

例句：My grandfather's name was Evander. 我祖父的名字叫埃万德。|近义词： Alexander, Andrew |反义词: N/A |词性:Noun
