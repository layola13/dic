earthy:/ˈɜːrθi/ /ˈɝːθi/| adj.|世俗的，尘世的|Worldly; not spiritual. |He has very earthy tastes. 他有很世俗的品味。|Mundane, worldly, material, secular|Spiritual, ethereal, otherworldly|adjective
