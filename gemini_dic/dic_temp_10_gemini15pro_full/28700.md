carload:/ˈkɑːrloʊd/ (US) /ˈkɑːləʊd/ (UK)| n.|一车（货物）；满载一车的量|A quantity of something that fills a railroad freight car or a large truck.|The farmer brought a carload of watermelons to market.  农民把一车西瓜运到市场。|Synonyms: truckload, shipment, consignment|Antonyms: handful,少量|noun
