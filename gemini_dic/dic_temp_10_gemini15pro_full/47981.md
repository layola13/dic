decimalises:/ˈdɛsɪməlaɪzɪz/
verb
十进制化；改用十进制
To convert (a system of weights, measures, or currency) to the decimal system.
Many countries decimalized their currency in the 20th century. 许多国家在20世纪将货币十进制化。
The UK decimalised its currency in 1971. 英国于1971年将其货币十进制化。
Synonyms: metrify, convert
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "revert to a non-decimal system")
词性: verb
