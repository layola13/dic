multiplexor:/ˈmʌltɪˌpleksər/ (US), /ˈmʌltɪˌpleksə(r)/ (UK)| n.|多路复用器，多工器|A device that selects one of several analog or digital input signals and forwards the selected input into a single line.|The multiplexor routes the data from several computers onto the network cable.|近义词:multiplexer, mux|反义词:demultiplexer, demux|noun
