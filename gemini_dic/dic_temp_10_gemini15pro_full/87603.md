heterotopy:/ˈhɛtərəˌtəʊpi/ /ˈhɛtərəˌtoʊpi/ | n. |异位，异位性|The presence of a body tissue or organ in a place other than where it is normally found. |Example 1: Heterotopy of the brain is a rare congenital anomaly. 脑异位是一种罕见的先天性异常。

Example 2:  Gastric heterotopia is the presence of gastric tissue in an abnormal location.  胃异位是指胃组织出现在异常位置。|Near synonyms: displacement, malposition, ectopia |Antonyms: orthotopy | noun
