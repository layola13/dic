fallency: /ˈfælənsi/ | n. |谬误；谬论 | A mistaken belief, especially one based on unsound argument. |  Example 1: The fallency of his argument was quickly exposed. 他的论证的谬误很快就被揭露了。

Example 2:  It's a common fallency to assume that wealth brings happiness.  认为财富带来幸福是一种常见的谬误。| 近义词: fallacy, error, misconception, delusion | 反义词: truth, fact, reality | 词性: noun
