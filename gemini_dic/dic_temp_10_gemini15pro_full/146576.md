overgratified: /ˌoʊvərˈɡrætɪfaɪd/
adjective
过度满足的
Excessively gratified; having had one's desires or appetites indulged to excess.  Being in a state where one's needs or wants have been fulfilled beyond a healthy or reasonable point.
He felt overgratified after the three-course meal and dessert buffet. 他在吃完三道菜和自助甜点后感到过度满足。
The children were overgratified with presents and had lost appreciation for the value of things. 孩子们被礼物过度满足，已经失去了对事物价值的欣赏。
近义词: oversatiated, overindulged, spoiled, pampered
反义词: deprived, unsatisfied, unfulfilled
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "gratified" (past participle of "gratify," from Latin "gratificari," meaning "to make agreeable").  The root "gratus" means "pleasing" or "thankful."
