deviancies: /ˈdiːviənsiz/
n.
偏差，背离，越轨行为
Actions or behaviors that violate social norms or expectations.
His minor deviancies from the norm went unnoticed. 他轻微的越轨行为没有被注意到。

近义词: abnormalities, irregularities, eccentricities, perversions, aberrations
反义词: normalities, conformities

词性: 名词 (plural of deviancy)
词源:  Late 16th century: from late Latin deviantia, from deviare ‘go astray’ (see deviate).
