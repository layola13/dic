enormous:/ɪˈnɔːrməs/| adj.|巨大的，庞大的|extremely large in size or amount|例句：The elephant was enormous. 这头大象体型庞大。|例句：They inherited an enormous fortune. 他们继承了一笔巨额财富。|近义词：huge, immense, gigantic, massive|反义词：tiny, small, minute|词性:形容词
Etymology: From French énorme, from Latin enormis “out of rule, irregular, immense,” from e- “out of” + norma “rule, pattern.”
