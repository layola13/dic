liar: /ˈlaɪər/ (US), /ˈlaɪə(r)/ (UK) | n. | 说谎者，骗子 | A person who tells lies. | He's a liar and a cheat. 他是个骗子，而且还作弊。

She called him a liar to his face. 她当面骂他是骗子。

近义词: deceiver, fibber, prevaricator | 反义词: truth-teller, honest person | 词性: noun
