multicostate:/ˌmʌltiˈkɒsteɪt/ | adj. | 多肋的；多主脉的 | Having multiple ribs or costa (midribs).  This term is primarily used in botany to describe leaves or other plant structures. | The multicostate leaf of the plant was easily identifiable.  这株植物的多肋叶子很容易辨认。 |  The fern had delicate, multicostate fronds.  这株蕨类植物有着精致的多主脉叶。 |  costate, ribbed |  – | adjective
