certifying:/ˈsɜːrtɪfaɪɪŋ/ |adj. |证明的，验证的 |Officially confirming or guaranteeing something.  | The document was certifying the authenticity of the artifact.  这文件证明了文物的真实性。 |  certifying  | verifying, validating, attesting, endorsing |  invalidating, disproving, denying | adjective


certify:/ˈsɜːrtɪfaɪ/ |vt.|证明，保证；发证书 |To formally attest or confirm something; to issue a certificate. |The doctor certified the patient's death. 医生证明了病人的死亡。 | attest, confirm, validate, endorse | deny, disprove, refute | verb

Etymology: From Late Latin *certificāre, from Latin certus (“certain”) + -ficāre (from facere, “to make”).
