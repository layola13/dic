autumns:/ˈɔːtəmz/| n. |秋天，秋季|The season after summer and before winter, characterized by falling leaves and cooler temperatures.  The time of year when crops are gathered and leaves change color.|例句：The autumns in New England are famous for their vibrant colors. 新英格兰的秋天以其鲜艳的色彩而闻名。

例句：We enjoy long walks in the autumns. 我们喜欢在秋天长时间散步。


|近义词：fall (American English)|反义词:spring|词性:名词
