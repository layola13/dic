elam:/ˈiːləm/ | n. | 伊兰（古代西亚国家）| An ancient civilization located in what is now southwest Iran. |  The Elamites were known for their advanced metalworking skills. 伊兰人以其先进的金属加工技能而闻名。

The kingdom of Elam frequently clashed with its Mesopotamian neighbors. 伊兰王国经常与其美索不达米亚的邻国发生冲突。 | 近义词: ancient civilization, kingdom | 反义词: modern country | 词性: noun
