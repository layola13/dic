heteropelmous:/ˌhɛtərəˈpɛlməs/
adjective
异基的，异掌形的
Having the bases of the stamens unequal, as in some flowers.  Referring to stamens of unequal length, or inserted at different heights on the receptacle.
Example:  The heteropelmous flowers of the plant are a distinguishing feature.  这种植物的异基花是一个显著特征。
Example: Botanists studied the heteropelmous arrangement of the stamens.  植物学家研究了雄蕊的异基排列。
Near Synonyms: anisodynamous (unequal stamens), unequal
Antonym: isodynamous (equal stamens)
word type: adjective
