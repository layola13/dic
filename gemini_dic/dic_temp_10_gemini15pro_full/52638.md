digit:/ˈdɪdʒɪt/| n.|数字，手指或脚趾|A digit is any of the numerals from 0 to 9, especially when forming part of a number.|The number 123 has three digits.|近义词: numeral, figure|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: from Latin digitus ‘finger, toe’.  The numerical sense arose from counting on the fingers.
