febrifacient:/ˌfebrɪˈfeɪʃənt/| adj. n.| 发热的，致热的；致热剂 | Causing fever; a substance that causes fever. |  This medicine is a febrifacient and can help reduce your fever. 这种药是退烧药，可以帮你退烧。 | The doctor prescribed a febrifacient to induce a fever and help fight the infection. 医生开了一种致热剂来诱发发烧以帮助对抗感染。 | synonyms: pyrogenic, fever-producing | antonyms: antipyretic, febrifuge | adjective, noun
