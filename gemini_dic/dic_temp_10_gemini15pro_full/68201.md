fair-trade:/ˌfer ˈtreɪd/ | adj. n. |公平贸易的; 公平贸易|  Relating to trade in which fair prices are paid to producers in developing countries.| I always buy fair-trade coffee. 我总是买公平贸易的咖啡。| 近义词: ethical, responsible | 反义词: exploitative, unethical | 词性: 形容词, 名词

fair-trade:/ˌfer ˈtreɪd/ | n. |公平贸易|The activity of buying and selling goods produced under fair-trade conditions.|  The growth of fair trade has helped many farmers. 公平贸易的发展帮助了许多农民。| 近义词: ethical trade, responsible trade| 反义词: free trade (in some contexts) | 词性: 名词

词源:  Compound of "fair" and "trade".  "Fair" ultimately derives from Old English "fæger" (beautiful, pleasing). "Trade" from Middle Low German "trade" (track, course).
