overhold:/ˌoʊvərˈhoʊld/| vt.|遵守，奉行|To adhere to or comply with (a law, rule, or agreement).|例句：They failed to overhold the terms of the contract. 他们未能遵守合同条款。|例句：The company overholds strict safety regulations.  这家公司遵守严格的安全规定。|近义词：observe, comply with, adhere to, follow, uphold|反义词：violate, breach, disobey, ignore|词性:动词
Etymology: From Middle English *overholden, from Old English *oferhealdan (unattested), from ofer- (over) + healdan (to hold, keep, guard).  The modern form is a back-formation from the past participle overheld.
