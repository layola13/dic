defensibility:/dɪˌfɛnsɪˈbɪlɪti/| n.|可防御性，可辩护性|The quality of being defensible; capable of being defended, justified, or protected.|The defensibility of their position was questionable. 他们的立场的可辩护性值得怀疑。|近义词：justifiability, defendableness|反义词：vulnerability, indefensibility|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From defendable + -ity.  "Defendable" comes from the Old French "defendre" (to defend), ultimately from the Latin "defendere" (to ward off, protect).  The suffix "-ity" is used to form nouns expressing state or quality.
