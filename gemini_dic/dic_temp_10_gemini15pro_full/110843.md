lintie:/ˈlɪnti/| n.|亚麻雀，红额金翅雀|A lintie is a small brown and red finch, commonly found in Scotland.  It's an affectionate term for the bird.|I saw a lintie perched on the branch. 我看到一只亚麻雀栖息在树枝上。|近义词：twite, linnet|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

词源:
From Scots, a diminutive of *lintwhite* (from *lint* (flax) + *white* (referring to the white markings on the wing)), a variant of *linnet*.
