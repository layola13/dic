celluloses:/ˈsɛljʊˌloʊsiz/ | n. |纤维素|A complex carbohydrate that is the main constituent of the cell walls of plants.|例句：Celluloses are important for the structure of plants. 纤维素对植物的结构很重要。

例句：Cotton is almost pure cellulose. 棉花几乎是纯纤维素。|近义词：fiber, roughage |反义词: None |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cellulose*, from *cellule* (“small cell”) + *-ose* (suffix forming names of carbohydrates).  Ultimately from Latin *cellula* ("small room, storeroom").
