gratified:/ˈɡrætɪfaɪd/| adj.|满足的，高兴的|Feeling pleasure or satisfaction, especially because of something you have achieved or been given.|例句：I was gratified to hear that you passed the exam. 我很高兴听到你通过了考试。|例句：She was gratified by the positive feedback she received. 她对收到的积极反馈感到满意。|近义词：pleased, satisfied, content, fulfilled|反义词：disappointed, dissatisfied, discontented|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from Latin gratificat- ‘made agreeable’, from the verb gratificari, from gratus ‘pleasing’ + -ficare (from facere ‘make’).
