laa: /lɑː/ | interj. | 啦 | An informal exclamation expressing surprise, mild annoyance, or acknowledgement. Often used in Singaporean and Malaysian English. | "Laa, I forgot my keys!" 啦，我忘记带钥匙了！ | Synonyms: oh, oops, ah | Antonyms: N/A | Interjection

laa: /lɑː/ | n. | (Singaporean/Malaysian informal) a scolding; a telling-off | "My mom gave me a good laa for coming home late." 我妈妈因为我回家晚了狠狠地骂了我一顿。 | Synonyms: scolding, reprimand | Antonyms: praise, compliment | Noun
