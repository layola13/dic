nonimpressionability:/ˌnɒnɪmˌprɛʃənəˈbɪləti/
n.
不易受影响；无动于衷
The state or quality of being nonimpressionable; resistance to being impressed or influenced.
His nonimpressionability made him a difficult person to persuade. 他不易受影响，很难说服他。
Near Synonyms: impassivity, imperturbability, insensitivity, aloofness
Antonyms: impressionability, sensitivity, responsiveness
词性: 名词
