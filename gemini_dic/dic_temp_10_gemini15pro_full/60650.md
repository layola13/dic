electional:/ɪˈlɛkʃənəl/
adjective
选举的；与选举有关的
Relating to an election or elections.
The electional process was marred by irregularities.  选举过程因违规行为而受损。
The candidate focused on electional reform. 候选人专注于选举改革。
近义词:elective, electoral
反义词:none
词性:adjective

词源: From Late Latin *electionalis, from Latin electio (“choice, election”).
