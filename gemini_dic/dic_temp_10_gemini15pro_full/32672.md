chivalrousness:/ˈʃɪvəlrəsnəs/
n.
骑士精神；侠义；殷勤
The quality of being chivalrous, characterized by honor, courtesy, and valor, especially toward women.
He showed his chivalrousness by opening the door for her. 他为她开门展现了他的骑士精神。

近义词:gallantry, courtesy, nobility, politeness
反义词:rudeness, discourtesy, boorishness
词性:noun

chivalrous:/ˈʃɪvəlrəs/
adj.
骑士精神的；侠义的；殷勤的
Marked by honor, courtesy, and valor, especially toward women.
He was known for his chivalrous behavior. 他以其骑士风范而闻名。

近义词:gallant, courteous, noble, polite
反义词:rude, discourteous, boorish
词性:adjective
