hyperexcitements:/ˌhaɪpərɪkˈsaɪtmənts/
|n.|过度兴奋，过度激动|A state of being excessively excited.  A plural form of hyperexcitement.|The hyperexcitements of the crowd before the concert were palpable.  演唱会前人群的过度兴奋 palpable.|近义词: overexcitements, frenzies, exhilarations|反义词: calmness, tranquility|词性: 名词

词源:
From hyper- (prefix meaning "over, excessive") + excitement.  "Hyper-" comes from Greek "hyper" (ὑπέρ), meaning "over, above, beyond." "Excitement" comes from the Latin "excitare," meaning "to rouse, stir up."
