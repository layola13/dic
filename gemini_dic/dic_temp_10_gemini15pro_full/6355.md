amphibians:/æmˈfɪbiənz/| n.|两栖动物|Cold-blooded vertebrate animals of a class that comprises the frogs, toads, newts, salamanders, and caecilians. They are distinguished by having an aquatic gill-breathing larval stage followed (typically) by a terrestrial lung-breathing adult stage.|例句：Amphibians are sensitive to environmental changes. 两栖动物对环境变化很敏感。

例句：Many amphibians return to the water to breed. 许多两栖动物回到水中繁殖。|近义词：herptiles, amphibia|反义词:|n.
