abdications: /ˌæbdɪˈkeɪʃənz/
n.
退位，放弃，辞职
The act of formally giving up a high office or responsibility.
The king's abdication sent shockwaves through the country. 国王的退位在全国引起了轩然大波。

Recent abdications of European monarchs have changed the political landscape.  最近欧洲君主的退位改变了政治格局。

近义词: resignations, relinquishments, renunciations
反义词: accessions, assumptions, usurpations
词性: 名词(plural)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin abdicatio, from Latin abdicare "to disown, renounce, reject," from ab- "away, from" + dicare "to proclaim, dedicate, devote" (from PIE root *deik- "to show," also "to pronounce solemnly").
