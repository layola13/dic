accessory: /əkˈsesəri/ | n. | 配件，附件；装饰品；从犯 |  A thing that can be added to something else in order to make it more useful, versatile, or attractive. |  She wore a simple black dress with a few striking accessories. 她穿着一件简单的黑色连衣裙，搭配了一些引人注目的配饰。

The car comes with a wide range of accessories. 这辆车配备了各种各样的配件。| 近义词：attachment, supplement, addition,  adornment, embellishment, accomplice (for a person) | 反义词: essential, necessity | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *accessorius, from Latin accedere "to approach," from ad- "to" + cedere "to go."  The original sense was "something added," later evolving to its current meanings.
