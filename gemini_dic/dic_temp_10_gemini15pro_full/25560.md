bulwarking:/ˈbʊlwərkɪŋ/
n.|防御工事，壁垒|The act of fortifying or protecting something, like building a bulwark.  加固或保护某物的行为，如建造壁垒。|例句：The bulwarking of the city walls was essential to its defense.  加固城墙对其防御至关重要。|近义词：fortifying, strengthening, reinforcing, securing|反义词:weakening, undermining|词性:名词 (derived from verb)

bulwark:/ˈbʊlwərk/
n.|壁垒，防御工事|A defensive wall or rampart. 防御墙或壁垒。|例句：The castle's bulwarks protected it from invaders. 城堡的壁垒保护它免受入侵者的侵害。|近义词：rampart, fortification, defense, barrier|反义词: weakness, vulnerability|词性:名词

bulwark:/ˈbʊlwərk/
vt.|保护，防御|To fortify or protect something. 加固或保护某物。|例句：They bulwarked the town against the flood. 他们筑起防御工事保护城镇免受洪水侵袭。|近义词：fortify, strengthen, reinforce, protect, defend|反义词: weaken, expose|词性:动词

词源:
From Middle Dutch *bolwerc, from bol "plank, tree trunk" + werc "work".  源自中古荷兰语 *bolwerc，由 bol “木板，树干” + werc “工作” 组成。
