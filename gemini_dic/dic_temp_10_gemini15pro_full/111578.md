lobotomy: /ləˈbɒtəmi/ (UK) /ləˈbɑːtəmi/ (US)| n.| 脑叶白质切除术| A surgical operation involving incision into the prefrontal lobe of the brain, formerly used to treat mental illness.|  Lobotomies were a common practice in the mid-20th century. 脑白质切除术在20世纪中期是一种常见的做法。| The lobotomy left him with permanent brain damage. 脑白质切除术给他留下了永久性脑损伤。| Synonyms: leucotomy, prefrontal lobotomy | Antonyms: therapy, medication | Noun
