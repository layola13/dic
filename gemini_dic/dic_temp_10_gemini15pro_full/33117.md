christianly:/ˈkrɪstʃənli/ | adv. | 基督徒般地；像基督徒一样地 | In a manner befitting a Christian; piously or charitably. |  She bore her suffering christianly, never complaining or losing faith. 她基督徒般地忍受着痛苦，从不抱怨或丧失信心。|  She always acted christianly, even towards those who were unkind to her. 她总是像基督徒一样行事，即使是对待那些对她不友好的人。 | 近义词: piously, devoutly, charitably | 反义词: impiously, wickedly | 词性: 副词

词源: From Christian + -ly.  The suffix "-ly" is used to form adverbs from adjectives.
