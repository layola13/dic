amalgamation:/əˌmælɡəˈmeɪʃn/ | n. | 合并，混合，融合 | The act of combining or uniting multiple entities into a single, unified whole. It can refer to the merging of companies, the blending of different substances, or the integration of diverse cultures. | The amalgamation of the two companies created a powerful new force in the market. 这两家公司的合并创造了市场上的一股强大的新力量。

The chef's latest creation was an amalgamation of French and Italian cuisine.  这位厨师的最新作品是法国和意大利菜肴的融合。 | synonyms: merger, union, blend, fusion, synthesis, combination | antonyms: separation, division, fission, splitting | noun


amalgamations:/əˌmælɡəˈmeɪʃnz/ | n. | 合并，混合，融合（复数）| Plural form of "amalgamation," referring to multiple instances of combining or uniting entities. | The recent amalgamations in the banking sector have reduced the number of independent institutions. 最近银行业的多起合并减少了独立机构的数量。

Several amalgamations of different metal alloys were tested for their strength and durability.  几种不同金属合金的混合物进行了强度和耐久性测试。 | synonyms: mergers, unions, blends, fusions, syntheses, combinations | antonyms: separations, divisions, fissions, splittings | noun

From Late Latin *amalgamatio, from Latin  amalgamare “to alloy with mercury”.
