overplentifully:/ˌoʊvərˈplɛntɪfəli/| adv.| 过于丰富地，过剩地 |  Meaning "in an overplentiful manner," from overplentiful + -ly.  It describes something that exists or is provided in excessive abundance. | The table was overplentifully laden with food. 桌子上摆满了食物。| The garden produced vegetables overplentifully this year. 今年花园里的蔬菜产量过剩。| 近义词: excessively, abundantly, copiously | 反义词: sparsely, meagerly, insufficiently | 词性: 副词
