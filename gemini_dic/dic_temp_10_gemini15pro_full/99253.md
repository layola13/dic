intercomparing:/ˌɪntərkəmˈpɛərɪŋ/
v.
相互比较
Comparing different things or sets of data against each other.
The researchers are intercomparing results from different laboratories. 研究人员正在比较不同实验室的结果。
Synonyms: comparing, contrasting, juxtaposing
Antonyms: separating, isolating
