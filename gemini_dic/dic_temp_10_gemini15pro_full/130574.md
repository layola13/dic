nonactivities:/ˌnɒnækˈtɪvətiz/ | n. | 非活动；不活动的事项 |Lack of exercise and excessive nonactivities can contribute to health problems. 缺乏锻炼和过多的不活动会导致健康问题。|  A schedule filled with nonactivities can lead to boredom.  充满不活动的日程安排会导致无聊。| 近义词: inactivity, idleness, passiveness | 反义词: activities, actions, endeavors | 词性: 名词

词源:  Formed by the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the noun "activities."  "Non-" negates the meaning of "activities," creating a word that refers to the absence of activity.
