innocuously:/ɪˈnɒkjuəsli/
adv.
无害地，无恶意地
Not harmful or offensive.
The comment seemed innocuous at first, but later I realized it was a subtle insult. 这句话起初看起来并无恶意，但我后来意识到这是种微妙的侮辱。
innocuously:/ɪˈnɑːkjuəsli/
adv.
无害地，无恶意地
Not harmful or offensive.
He smiled innocuously, hiding his true intentions. 他无害地笑了笑，掩饰了他真正的意图。
近义词:harmlessly, safely, inoffensively
反义词:harmfully, dangerously, offensively
词性:adverb

词源:
From Latin innocuus ("harmless, innocent") + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Innocuus is formed from in- ("not") + nocuus ("harmful").  Nocuus is related to nocere ("to harm").
