influxes:/ˈɪnflʌksɪz/
| n. |涌入；汇集|A sudden arrival or entry of large numbers of people or things.|
|例句：The city experienced an influx of tourists during the summer months.  这座城市在夏季经历了游客的涌入。

|
|例句：There has been a large influx of refugees into the country.  大量难民涌入该国。

|
|近义词：inflow, inrush, surge, flood|
|反义词：outflow, exodus, decline|
|词性: noun (plural)|
