footless:/ˈfʊtləs/| adj.|无足的，无基础的，笨拙的，无效的|Lacking skill or effectiveness; clumsy; incompetent.  Also literally lacking feet.|He made a footless attempt to climb the wall. 他笨手笨脚地试图爬墙。|Synonyms: clumsy, awkward, inept, incompetent, ineffective, futile|Antonyms: skillful, adept, competent, effective, graceful|adjective
