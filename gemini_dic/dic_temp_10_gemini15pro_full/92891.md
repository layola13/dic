idealizers:/aɪˈdiːəlaɪzərz/
n.
理想化者，完美化者
People who idealize others often overlook their flaws.  理想化别人的人常常忽视他们的缺点。
Those who idealize historical figures often forget they were also human.  那些将历史人物理想化的人常常忘记他们也是人。
People who tend to idealize their romantic partners may be setting themselves up for disappointment.  倾向于理想化浪漫伴侣的人可能会让自己失望。
admirers, romanticizers,  worshippers
critics, realists, cynics
noun
