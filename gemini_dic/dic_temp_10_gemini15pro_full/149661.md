pairings:/ˈpɛərɪŋz/
noun
配对；组合；搭配
the act of putting two people or things together, especially in order to achieve a particular result.
The chef carefully considered the wine pairings for each dish. 厨师仔细考虑了每道菜的葡萄酒搭配。
combinations, matches, couplings
separations, divisions
