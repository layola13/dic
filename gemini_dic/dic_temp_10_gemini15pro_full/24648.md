brollies: /ˈbrɒliz/ (British English) | n. | 雨伞（复数） | Plural of "brolly," an informal British term for "umbrella."  It refers to a portable device used for protection from rain. |  例句：I left my brollies at home. 我把我的伞落在家里了。

例句：Don't forget your brollies, it's supposed to rain. 别忘了带伞，好像要下雨。

近义词：umbrellas, parasols | 反义词: (No direct antonym.  One might consider "sunshade" as a contrasting term for protection from sun instead of rain.) | 词性: noun
