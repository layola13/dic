over-sufficient:/ˌoʊvər səˈfɪʃənt/
adj.
过剩的，过多的
more than sufficient; excessive
The over-sufficient supply of food led to waste.  过剩的食物供应导致了浪费。
The charity received an over-sufficient amount of donations.  慈善机构收到了过多的捐款。
近义词: excessive, surplus, redundant, superfluous
反义词: insufficient, inadequate, deficient, lacking
词性: adjective
词源: From over- + sufficient, from Middle English *sufficiaunt, from Old French sufficient, from Latin sufficiēns, present participle of sufficere (“to suffice”).
