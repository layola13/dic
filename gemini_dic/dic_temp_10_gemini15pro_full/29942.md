caveated:/ˈkæviˌeɪtɪd/
| adj.| 附带警告的，有保留的 | qualified by reservations or exceptions; made less absolute |  The offer was caveated with several conditions.  这个提议附带了几个条件。 | qualified, conditional, restricted, limited | unconditional, absolute | adjective

caveated:/ˈkæviˌeɪtɪd/
| v. | 附带警告，加限制条件（caveat的过去分词和过去式）| To qualify by specifying exceptions or conditions. | He caveated his agreement by insisting on certain changes. 他对他的同意附加了警告，坚持要进行某些修改。 | qualify, restrict, limit, stipulate |  unqualify, generalize | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Latin word "caveat" meaning "let him beware".  The verb "to caveat" originated later, derived from the noun.
