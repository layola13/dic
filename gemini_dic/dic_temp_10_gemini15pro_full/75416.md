frugality:/fruːˈɡæləti/| n. |节俭，俭省|The quality of being economical with money or food; thriftiness.|  She lived a life of frugality, rarely spending money on anything but necessities. 她过着节俭的生活，很少花钱买生活必需品以外的东西。|synonyms: thrift, economy, prudence, austerity| antonyms: extravagance, lavishness, prodigality | noun
