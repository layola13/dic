dioramic:/ˌdaɪəˈræmɪk/ | adj. | 全景的；透视画法的；逼真的 | Relating to or having the form of a diorama; vividly descriptive or pictorial. | The museum featured a dioramic exhibit of a prehistoric forest.  博物馆展出了一个史前森林的透视模型展览。| The novel's dioramic descriptions brought the historical setting to life. 这部小说的逼真描写使历史背景栩栩如生。 |  近义词: panoramic, scenic, picturesque, vivid | 反义词:  dull, uninteresting, bland | 词性: adjective

词源: From French *dioramique*, from *diorama* + *-ique*.  *Diorama* itself comes from Greek *dia* (“through”) + *orama* (“that which is seen, a sight”).
