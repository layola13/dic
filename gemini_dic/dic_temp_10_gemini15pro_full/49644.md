demilitarize:/diːˈmɪlɪtəraɪz/
verb
使非军事化；解除武装
To remove all military forces, weapons, and fortifications from a place.
The treaty required both countries to demilitarize the border zone.  该条约要求两国将边境地区非军事化。
The government decided to demilitarize the island. 政府决定将该岛非军事化。
synonyms: disarm, neutralize
antonyms: militarize, arm, fortify
词性: 动词
