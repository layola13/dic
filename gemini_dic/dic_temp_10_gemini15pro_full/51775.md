devotionally:/dɪˈvoʊʃənəli/ | adv. | 虔诚地，忠实地 | In a manner characterized by or expressing devotion; with piety and reverence. | She prayed devotionally every morning. 她每天早上虔诚地祈祷。|  He served his country devotionally for many years. 他忠诚地为国家服务多年。 | 近义词: piously, religiously, reverently, faithfully | 反义词: irreverently, impiously, faithlessly | 词性: 副词

词源: From the adjective "devotional" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Devotional" comes from the Late Latin "devotionalis," from "devotio" (devotion), ultimately from the Latin "devovere" (to dedicate, vow).
