aroynt:/əˈrɔɪnt/
interjection
走开；滚开；退散
Begone!  A command to depart; an imprecation.
Aroynt thee, witch!  走开，女巫！
Synonyms:  begone, away, avaunt, get out
Antonyms:  come, welcome
词性:  感叹词


aroynt:/əˈrɔɪnt/
verb (archaic)
驱逐；赶走
To drive away; to banish.
He aroynt thee, foul fiend! 他把你赶走了，你这恶鬼！
Synonyms:  expel, banish, drive away
Antonyms:  welcome, invite
词性:  动词(古语)
