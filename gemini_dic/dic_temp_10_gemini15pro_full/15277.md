backlight:/ˈbækˌlaɪt/ | n. v. | 背光；逆光 |Light source placed behind or at the back of a subject, especially to improve visibility or create a special effect. | The photographer used a backlight to create a silhouette. 摄影师用逆光创造了一个剪影。 | Synonyms: illumination, lighting | Antonyms: frontlight | noun
