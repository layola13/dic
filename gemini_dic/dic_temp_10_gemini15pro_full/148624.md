overthick:/ˌoʊvərˈθɪk/
adjective
过厚的，太厚的
Excessively thick; thicker than desirable or necessary.
The walls were overthick, making the rooms feel small.  墙太厚了，让房间感觉很小。
The batter was overthick, resulting in heavy pancakes. 面糊太稠了，导致煎饼很厚。
近义词: too thick, excessively thick
反义词: thin, flimsy
词性: adjective
