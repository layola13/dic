anticounterfeiting:/ˌæntiˈkaʊntərˌfɪtɪŋ/
adjective
防伪的
Relating to measures taken to prevent the illegal copying and production of goods, documents, etc.
The company invests heavily in anticounterfeiting technology.  这家公司在防伪技术上投入巨资。
synonyms: anti-forgery, anti-piracy
antonyms:  N/A (lack of direct antonym; concepts like "pro-counterfeiting" are not standard English)
词性: adjective
