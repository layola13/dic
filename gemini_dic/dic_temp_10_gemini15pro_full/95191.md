inaugurators:/ɪˈnɔːɡjəreɪtərz/
n.
就职典礼的主持人；开创者；开幕者
Those who formally induct someone into office or begin something new.
The inaugurators of the new museum welcomed the first visitors.  新博物馆的开幕者欢迎第一批参观者。
The president and other inaugurators were seated on the platform. 总统和其他就职典礼的主持人坐在台上。
近义词: initiators, founders, pioneers
反义词: successors, followers
