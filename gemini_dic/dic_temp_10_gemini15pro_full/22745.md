boroughhead: /ˈbʌrəˌhɛd/
noun
自治市镇首领；区长
The chief officer of a borough.  This term is largely historical and rarely used in modern contexts.
The boroughhead presided over the annual fair.  自治市镇首领主持了年度集市。
synonyms: mayor, council leader, borough president
antonyms: citizen, resident, constituent
