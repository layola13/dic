chastise:/ˈtʃæstɪz/ (US) /tʃæˈstaɪz/ (UK)| vt.| 惩罚；责备；严厉批评| to rebuke or reprimand severely. To inflict punishment on. | The teacher chastised the student for disrupting class. 老师因为学生扰乱课堂秩序而责备他。|Synonyms: rebuke, reprimand, scold, admonish, censure, upbraid, castigate |Antonyms: praise, commend, laud, compliment|verb
