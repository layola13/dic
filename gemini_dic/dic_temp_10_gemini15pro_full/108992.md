left-out-in-the-cold:/ˌleft aʊt ɪn ðə ˈkoʊld/
adjective
被冷落的，被排斥的，被忽视的
Excluded, ignored, or rejected, especially by close friends or associates.
例句：After the merger, many loyal employees felt left out in the cold.  合并之后，许多忠诚的员工感到被冷落了。

例句：She was left out in the cold when her friends went to the party without her. 当她的朋友们没有带她去参加聚会时，她被冷落了。
近义词：ignored, neglected, ostracized, excluded, abandoned
反义词：included, welcomed, embraced
