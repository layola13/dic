assizor:/əˈsaɪzər/| n.|协助者；陪审员；估价员|A person who assists or helps another, particularly a judge or other official in legal proceedings.  An assizor could also be a person appointed to help assess taxes or damages.|The assizor aided the judge in determining the value of the disputed property.  陪审员协助法官确定争议财产的价值。|Synonyms: assistant, helper, aide, collaborator, associate|Antonyms: opponent, adversary, enemy|noun

assizor:/əˈsaɪzər/| n.|陪审员|A person who serves on a jury, especially one summoned to advise a judge on technical matters.|The assizor was called upon to explain the complex engineering details to the court.  陪审员被要求向法庭解释复杂的工程细节。|Synonyms: juror, juryman, jurywoman|Antonyms: N/A|noun

assizor:/əˈsaɪzər/| n.|估价员|A person appointed to assess the value of something, such as property or damages.|The assizor determined the amount of compensation owed to the plaintiff. 估价员确定了应支付给原告的赔偿金额。|Synonyms: appraiser, assessor, valuer, evaluator|Antonyms: N/A|noun
