amaze:/əˈmeɪz/
|vt.|使惊奇，使惊叹|To surprise or impress someone very much.|The magician's tricks amazed the children. 魔术师的戏法使孩子们惊叹不已。|astonish, surprise, astound|bore, disappoint|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *amaisier, from Vulgar Latin *exmagiscare, from Latin magis ("more").  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *megʰ- ("great, big").
