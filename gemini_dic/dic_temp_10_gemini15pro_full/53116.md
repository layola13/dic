dipleidoscope:/daɪˈpliːɪdəˌskoʊp/| n. |双像测日镜|An instrument for observing the sun, using two prisms to produce two images, one slightly displaced, allowing precise measurement of the sun's position.|The astronomer used a dipleidoscope to determine the exact time of the solar transit.|Synonym: helioscope|Antonym: N/A|noun
