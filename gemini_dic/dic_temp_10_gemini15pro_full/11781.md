arquebus:/ˈɑːrkɪbʌs/ | n. | 火绳枪 | An early type of long gun, used in the 15th-17th centuries, that fired a single projectile. It was a precursor to the musket. |  The arquebus was a significant advancement in military technology. 火绳枪是军事技术上的一个重大进步。

The soldiers were armed with arquebuses and pikes. 士兵们配备了火绳枪和长矛。 | 近义词: firearm, gun, musket | 反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "bow and arrow" as a contrasting older weapon) | 词性: noun
