exuberate:/ɪɡˈzjuːbəreɪt/| v. |显示出丰富，洋溢; 变得繁茂，茂盛|To show or feel great happiness, excitement, or abundance.  To grow luxuriantly.|The flowers exuberated in the warm spring sunshine. 春天温暖的阳光下，花儿们繁茂地生长。|近义词: flourish, thrive, prosper, abound, overflow |反义词: wither, decline, languish|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin exuberare "to be fruitful, abound,"  from ex- "out" + uber "udder, fertile, abundant."  The original sense referred to the overflowing of milk from a cow's udder, hence abundance.
