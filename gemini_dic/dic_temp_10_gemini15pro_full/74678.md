freeborn:/ˈfriːbɔːrn/
adjective
自由出生的；生而自由的
Born free, not a slave.  A person who is freeborn is not a slave by birth.
He was proud of his freeborn status. 他为自己的自由出身感到自豪。
Synonyms: independent, free, liberated
Antonyms: enslaved, captive, subservient
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *frēoġeboren (attested as frēoġeborennes "freeborn state"), from frēo "free" + ġeboren "born."  The element ġe- was lost in Middle English.
