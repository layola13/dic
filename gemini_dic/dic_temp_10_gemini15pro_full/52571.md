diffuses:/dɪˈfjuːzɪz/| v.|扩散，散布，传播|spreads out over a large area; becomes less concentrated|The scent of flowers diffused through the garden. 花香弥漫在整个花园里。|近义词：spreads, disperses, dissipates|反义词：concentrates, gathers, collects|词性:动词

diffuse:/dɪˈfjuːs/| adj.|弥漫的，散开的，冗长的|spread out over a large area; not concentrated|The report was long and diffuse, making it hard to understand.  这份报告冗长而散漫，很难理解。|近义词：widespread, scattered, rambling|反义词：concentrated, concise, focused|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin diffusus, past participle of diffundere "to pour out, spread, scatter," from dis- "out, apart" + fundere "to pour."
