impermeabilities:/ɪmˌpɜːrmiəˈbɪlətiz/
|n.|不渗透性；不透水性； непроницаемость (Russian)|The state or quality of not allowing a substance, such as water or gas, to pass through.|The impermeabilities of certain rocks make them ideal for containing underground waste.  某些岩石的不渗透性使它们成为储存地下废物的理想选择。|imperviousness, resistance|permeability, porosity|noun
