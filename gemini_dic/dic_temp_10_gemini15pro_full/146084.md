overeducated:/ˌoʊvərˈɛdʒʊkeɪtɪd/
adjective
受教育过度的；学历过高的
having more education than is necessary for a particular job
例句：Many graduates are overeducated for the jobs they find. 许多毕业生学历过高，与其找到的工作不相称。

例句：He felt overeducated and underemployed. 他觉得自己学历过高，大材小用。

近义词：overqualified
反义词：undereducated, underqualified
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + educated, past participle of educate.  Over- is a prefix meaning "excessively," "too much."  Educate comes from the Latin educatus, past participle of educare "to bring up, rear, educate," from ex- "out" + ducere "to lead."
