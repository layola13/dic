oystered:/ˈɔɪstərd/
adjective
牡蛎般的；像牡蛎的；呆滞的；沉默寡言的
Resembling or characteristic of an oyster, especially in being rough, gray, or lumpy; also:  dull, unresponsive, or taciturn.
The man had an oystered complexion, pale and slightly rough.  这个男人的脸色像牡蛎一样，苍白且略显粗糙。
He sat oystered in his chair, refusing to speak. 他呆滞地坐在椅子上，拒绝说话。
近义词:shellfish-like, clam-like, taciturn, unresponsive, dull
反义词:responsive, lively, talkative, bright
词性:adjective

词源:From oyster + -ed.  源自 oyster（牡蛎）+ -ed（形容词后缀）。
