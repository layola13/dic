intermediaries:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdiɛriz/ | n. | 中间人，媒介，代理人 |  A person who acts as a link between people or things, especially in business or politics.  A mediator. | The real estate agent acted as an intermediary between the buyer and the seller. 房地产经纪人充当了买方和卖方之间的中间人。 | synonyms: mediator, negotiator, agent, broker, go-between, middleman | antonyms: principal, direct | noun
