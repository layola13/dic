mackintoshed:/ˈmækɪntɒʃt/ (UK) /ˈmækɪnˌtɑːʃt/ (US)| adj.|穿着雨衣的；受麦金托什式防水布保护的|Wearing a mackintosh; protected by mackintosh material.  Derived from Charles Macintosh, the inventor of the waterproof fabric.|例句：He arrived mackintoshed and umbrellaed, ready for the downpour. 他穿着雨衣，带着雨伞，准备迎接倾盆大雨。|例句：The mackintoshed figure disappeared into the fog.  那个穿着雨衣的身影消失在雾中。|近义词：raincoated, waterproofed|反义词:exposed, unprotected|词性:形容词
