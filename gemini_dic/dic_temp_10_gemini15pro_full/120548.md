miraculize: /mɪˈrækjʊlaɪz/
verb
使...发生奇迹；使...变得神奇
To transform something ordinary into something extraordinary or seemingly miraculous.
The cosmetics company claims its new cream can miraculize aging skin. 这家化妆品公司声称它的新面霜可以让老化的皮肤变得神奇。
The author miraculized the mundane details of everyday life in his novel.  作者在他的小说中将日常生活的平凡细节变得神奇。
近义词: transform, transmute, elevate, enhance
反义词: ruin, spoil, degrade
词性: verb

词源:  From "miracle" (from Latin *miraculum "a wonder, marvel") + "-ize" (a verb-forming suffix meaning "to make or cause to be").
