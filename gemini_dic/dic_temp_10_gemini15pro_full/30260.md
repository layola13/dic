celtiberian:/ˌsɛltɪˈbɪəriən/| adj. n.|凯尔特伊比利亚的；凯尔特伊比利亚人|Relating to or denoting the Celts of the Iberian Peninsula, or their language or culture.  A member of an ancient people inhabiting the Iberian Peninsula, considered to be of mixed Celtic and Iberian origin.|The Celtiberian language is poorly attested. 凯尔特伊比利亚语的文献记载很少。| The Celtiberians were skilled warriors. 凯尔特伊比利亚人都是熟练的战士。|近义词：Iberian, Celtic|反义词:|adjective, noun
