Its intuitive interface makes it easy to learn and use.  |  This material has excellent handleabilities, making it ideal for various applications.  | 近义词: manageability, controllability, usability | 反义词: unmanageability, intractability | 词性: 名词

词源:  From "handle" (v.) + "-able" (suffix making adjectives) + "-ity" (suffix making nouns).  "Handle" comes from Old English *handlian, meaning "to touch, feel, or manage with the hands."
