desktops:/ˈdɛskˌtɒps/ (UK) /ˈdɛskˌtɑːps/ (US)| n. |台式电脑|Multiple computers used at a fixed location, typically on a desk.  Refers to the computer itself, not just the screen.|I have two desktops at home, one for work and one for gaming. 我家里有两台台式电脑，一台用于工作，一台用于游戏。|synonyms: desktop computers, personal computers (PCs), workstations|antonyms: laptops, notebooks, tablets|noun

desktops:/ˈdɛskˌtɒps/ (UK) /ˈdɛskˌtɑːps/ (US)| adj. |台式的|Relating to or designed for use on a desktop computer.|I prefer desktop publishing software to online options. 我更喜欢台式出版软件而不是在线选项。|synonyms: stationary, non-portable|antonyms: mobile, portable, laptop|adjective
