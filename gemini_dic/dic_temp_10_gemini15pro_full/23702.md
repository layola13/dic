brassart:/ˈbræsɑːrt/| n.|臂甲，腕甲|A piece of armour protecting the arm, especially the upper arm between the elbow and the shoulder.|The knight polished his brassart until it gleamed. 骑士把他的臂甲擦得闪闪发光。|例句：The museum displayed a beautifully crafted 15th-century brassart.  博物馆展出了一件制作精美的15世纪臂甲。

例句：His brassart was dented from a heavy blow during the tournament.  他的臂甲在比赛中受到重击而凹陷了。|近义词：armour, vambrace|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "unprotected arm")|词性:noun
