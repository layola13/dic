imitational:/ˌɪmɪˈteɪʃənl/
adjective
模仿的; 仿制的
Copying or based on something else:  designed to simulate or copy something.
Her painting style is highly imitational. 她的绘画风格非常模仿化。

The bag is made of imitational leather.  这个包是仿皮做的。

Near Synonyms:  imitative, copied, simulated, ersatz, artificial, mock, replica, pseudo
Antonyms: original, genuine, authentic, real
词性: 形容词
