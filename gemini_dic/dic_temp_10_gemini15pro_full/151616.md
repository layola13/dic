participates:/pɑːrˈtɪsɪpeɪts/| v. |参与，参加|takes part in something; becomes involved in something.|She actively participates in class discussions. 她积极参与课堂讨论。|The company participates in several community projects.  这家公司参与了几个社区项目。|近义词：joins, engages in, takes part in, contributes to|反义词:abstains, withdraws, avoids|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: from Latin *participat- ‘shared in’, from the verb participare, from pars, part- ‘part’ + capere ‘take’.
