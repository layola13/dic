nonburnable:/ˌnɒnˈbɜːrnəbl/ |adj.|不可燃烧的；不燃的|Not capable of being burned; incombustible.|Example: Asbestos is a nonburnable material. 石棉是一种不可燃材料。|Example:  They stored the important documents in a nonburnable safe. 他们将重要文件存放在一个不燃保险箱里。|近义词:incombustible, fireproof, fire-resistant|反义词:burnable, combustible, flammable|词性:形容词
