nonsugars:/ˌnɑːnˈʃʊɡərz/ | n. |非糖类| Substances other than sugars, typically including dietary fiber, starches, and sugar alcohols.  They contribute to the overall carbohydrate content of food but are metabolized differently than simple sugars. |  The food label lists both sugars and nonsugars.  食品标签上列出了糖和非糖类。|  The nonsugars in this cereal provide a good source of fiber.  这种麦片中的非糖类物质是很好的纤维来源。 | fiber, starches, carbohydrates | sugars | noun
