frontage:/ˈfrʌntɪdʒ/| n.|正面；临街地段；房屋正面|The house has a frontage of 20 meters. 这座房子有20米的正面。|例句：The hotel has a beautiful frontage overlooking the sea. 这家酒店有一个美丽的正面，可以俯瞰大海。

例句：The shop has a prime frontage on the main street. 这家商店在主要街道上有一个黄金地段的正面。|近义词：facade, front, face|反义词:rear, back|词性:noun

词源: From Old French *frontage, from front (“front”) + -age. Ultimately from Latin frons (“forehead, front”).
