externalistic:/ˌɛkstɜːrnəˈlɪstɪk/
| adj. | 外在主义的，外在论的 | Relating to or characteristic of externalism, a philosophical theory that emphasizes the role of external factors in determining mental states and content. |  Externalistic theories of mental content hold that what we think and believe is shaped by our environment.  外在主义的精神内容理论认为，我们的思想和信仰是由我们的环境塑造的。 |  His philosophical stance was decidedly externalistic, emphasizing the causal role of the environment. 他的哲学立场明显是外在主义的，强调环境的因果作用。 | external, extrinsic | internal, intrinsic |词性: 形容词
