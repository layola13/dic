noctilucine:/ˌnɒktɪˈluːsɪn/| n. |夜光素|A substance that emits light, especially at night; found in some organisms like fireflies and certain fungi.|The noctilucine in the fireflies illuminated the forest path. 森林小径上的萤火虫的夜光素照亮了道路。|Synonyms: luciferin, photoprotein|Antonyms: N/A|noun

noctiluca:/ˌnɒktɪˈluːkə/| n. |夜光虫|A marine organism that exhibits bioluminescence, often causing the sea to glow at night.|The shimmering waves were caused by a bloom of noctiluca. 波光粼粼的海浪是由夜光虫的大量繁殖引起的。|Synonyms: sea sparkle, firefly of the sea|Antonyms: N/A|noun
