microstate: /ˈmaɪkroʊsteɪt/ | n. | 微观状态; 小国 | A specific microscopic configuration of a thermodynamic system, characterized by the positions and momenta of all its constituent particles.  A very small sovereign state. |  The microstate of the gas can change rapidly.  气体的微观状态可以快速变化。|  Andorra is a microstate located in the Pyrenees mountains. 安道尔是位于比利牛斯山脉的一个小国。 | synonyms: small state, ministate,  microscopic configuration | antonyms: macrostate, large state | noun
