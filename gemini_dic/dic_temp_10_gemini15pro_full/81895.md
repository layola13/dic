griffiness:/ˈɡrɪfɪnɪs/
n.
狮鹫性；狮鹫的特征
The griffiness of the creature was evident in its sharp talons and eagle-like head.  这个生物的狮鹫特征在其锋利的爪子和鹰一样的头部表现得很明显。
The artist captured the griffiness of the mythical beast perfectly.  这位艺术家完美地捕捉到了这只神话野兽的狮鹫特征。

Etymology: From "griffin" (a mythical creature with the body of a lion and the head and wings of an eagle) + "-ness" (a suffix forming nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition).  源于“griffin”（狮鹫，一种神话生物，身体像狮子，头部和翅膀像鹰）+“-ness”（一个构成名词的后缀，表示状态、性质或条件）。
