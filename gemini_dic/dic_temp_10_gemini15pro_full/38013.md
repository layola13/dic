complimentingly:/ˌkɒmplɪˈmentɪŋli/ | adv. | 赞美地，恭维地 | In a way that expresses praise or admiration. | She spoke complimentingly about his work. 她赞美了他的工作。| He smiled complimentingly at her. 他赞许地对她笑了笑。 | 近义词: approvingly, admiringly, flatteringly | 反义词: critically, disapprovingly | 词性: 副词

词源: From the adjective "complimentary" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Complimentary" ultimately derives from the Latin word "complementum," meaning "completion."
