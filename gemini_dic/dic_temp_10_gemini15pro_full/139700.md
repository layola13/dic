oceanlike:/ˈoʊʃənˌlaɪk/| adj.|像海洋的；广阔的|Resembling the ocean, especially in vastness or depth.  Having qualities similar to an ocean. |The lake was so vast and deep, it seemed oceanlike. 这个湖如此广阔而深邃，看起来像海洋一样。|近义词：vast, immense, boundless, expansive |反义词:shallow, narrow, limited|adjective
