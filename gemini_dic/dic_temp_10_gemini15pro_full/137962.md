nonwool:/ˌnɑːnˈwʊl/ | adj. | 非羊毛的，非毛织的 | Made of a fiber other than wool; not woolen. |  Example: She prefers nonwool sweaters because they are easier to care for. 她更喜欢非羊毛毛衣，因为它们更容易打理。 | Example: The nonwool blanket was perfect for the summer months.  非羊毛毯子非常适合夏季使用。 | 近义词: synthetic, acrylic, cotton | 反义词: wool, woolen | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "wool" (noun).  The prefix "non-" is derived from Latin.
