bunions:/ˈbʌnjənz/ (US), /ˈbʌnjənz/ (UK)| n. |拇囊炎；拇趾外翻|A bunion is a bony bump that forms on the joint at the base of your big toe. It occurs when your big toe pushes against your next toe, forcing the joint of your big toe to stick out.|My grandmother has bunions on both feet. 我祖母双脚都有拇囊炎。|She had surgery to correct her bunions. 她做了手术矫正拇囊炎。|Synonyms: hallux valgus|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|Noun
