ETYMOLOGY->
From cumbersome + -ness.  Cumbersome comes from Middle English  *combersom, from cumber + -some. Cumber comes from Old French  encombrer, from Late Latin  incombrare  "to block up," from in-  "in" + combrus  "barrier."
