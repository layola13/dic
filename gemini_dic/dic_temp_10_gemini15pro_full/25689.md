bunk's:/bʌŋks/| n.|床铺（通常指双层床的下铺或单人床）; (非正式)住处，铺位|A possessive form of bunk, referring to something belonging to or associated with a bunk.  It usually implies the space or belongings associated with a particular bunk, especially in a shared sleeping arrangement.|例句：He kept his belongings under his bunk's mattress. 他把他的东西放在他的床铺的床垫下面。

例句：She tripped over her bunk's ladder. 她被她床铺的梯子绊倒了。|近义词：bed, berth, cot|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
