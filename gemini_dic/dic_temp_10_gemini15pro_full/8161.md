anti-creative:/ˌænti-kriˈeɪtɪv/
adjective
反创造力的；扼杀创造力的
Suppressing or discouraging creativity.
The strict rules and regulations at the school had an anti-creative effect on the students.  学校严格的规章制度对学生产生了反创造力的影响。
The company's rigid hierarchy had an anti-creative impact on new product development.  公司僵化的等级制度对新产品开发产生了反创造力的影响。

近义词:unimaginative, uninspired, stifling, restrictive
反义词:creative, imaginative, inspiring, innovative
词性:adjective
