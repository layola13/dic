assimilationist: /əˌsɪməˈleɪʃənɪst/ | n. adj. | 同化主义者，同化主义的 | Someone who advocates or practices assimilation, especially cultural or racial assimilation.  A policy that encourages assimilation. |  He was accused of being an assimilationist for suggesting immigrants should abandon their native languages.  他因为建议移民放弃他们的母语而被指责为同化主义者。|  The government's assimilationist policies were met with resistance from minority groups. 政府的同化政策遭到了少数民族的抵制。 | 近义词：integrationist, conformist | 反义词：separatist, pluralist | 词性: 名词，形容词

Etymology:  From assimilate + -ist.  Assimilate comes from Latin assimulare "to make similar," from ad- "to" + simulare "to copy, represent, feign," from similis "like."
