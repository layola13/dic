overnutrition:/ˌoʊvərnuˈtrɪʃən/| n. |营养过剩|A state of excessive intake of nutrients, leading to adverse health effects.|  Overnutrition can lead to obesity and other health problems. 营养过剩会导致肥胖和其他健康问题。|  Excessive nutrition can result in overnutrition, particularly in developed countries.  过多的营养会导致营养过剩，尤其是在发达国家。|近义词：hyperalimentation, excessive nutrition|反义词: malnutrition, undernutrition|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From 'over-' (meaning excessive) + 'nutrition' (the process of providing or obtaining the food necessary for health and growth).  'Nutrition' derives from Latin 'nutritio', from 'nutrire' (to nourish).
