miszealous: /ˌmɪsˈzɛləs/ | adj. | 过分热心的；狂热的；不理智的 | Excessively zealous; fanatical; unreasonably enthusiastic.  Characterized by excessive or misguided zeal. |  His miszealous attempts to convert everyone he met became tiresome. 他过分热心地试图让他遇到的每个人都皈依，这变得令人厌烦。| Synonyms: fanatical, overzealous, rabid, immoderate, extremist | Antonyms: apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic, moderate | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "mis-" (meaning "wrong" or "bad") + "zealous" (from Greek "zelos" meaning "zeal, ardor").
