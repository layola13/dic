heavenly-minded:/ˈhɛvənliˈmaɪndɪd/
|adj.|虔诚的；一心向神的；精神高尚的；超脱尘世的|Having thoughts fixed on heaven or spiritual things; not concerned with earthly matters.|She was a quiet, heavenly-minded woman. 她是一位安静虔诚的女人。|He was so heavenly-minded that he seemed oblivious to the problems around him. 他如此超凡脱俗，似乎对周围的问题浑然不觉。|
近义词:spiritual, pious, devout, otherworldly
|反义词:worldly, materialistic, earthly, carnal
|词性:adjective
