heatlike:/ˈhiːtlaɪk/
adj.
像热的;类似热的; 热似的
Resembling or characteristic of heat.
The engine emitted a heatlike shimmer in the desert air.  发动机在沙漠的空气中发出类似热的闪烁。
The sensation was heatlike, but not quite burning. 这种感觉像热，但又不完全是灼烧。
similar, thermal, burning
cool, cold
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
heat (n.) + like (suffix)

From Old English *hætu (unattested) meaning "heat," related to Old High German heiz "hot," and from the Proto-Germanic *hait-, from the Proto-Indo-European *kaid- "to glow, be hot."

The suffix "-like" is from Old English -lic, meaning "having the form or qualities of," from Proto-Germanic *-likan.
