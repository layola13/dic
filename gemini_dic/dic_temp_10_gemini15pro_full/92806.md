iconism: /ˈaɪkənɪzəm/ | n. |图像主义，肖像崇拜|The use of icons or figures in worship or art, especially representing religious figures or scenes.  A devotion to or reverence for icons. |The Byzantine church was known for its iconism. 拜占庭教堂以其肖像崇拜而闻名。| The iconism in this painting is reminiscent of early Renaissance art. 这幅画中的图像主义让人想起早期文艺复兴时期的艺术。|  近义词: idolatry, iconography, symbolism | 反义词: aniconism | 词性: noun
