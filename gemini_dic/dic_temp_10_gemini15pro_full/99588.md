interinstitutionally:/ˌɪntərˌɪnstɪˈtuːʃənəli/
adverb|机构间地|In a manner involving or occurring between different institutions.|The project was managed interinstitutionally, with collaboration from several universities.|近义词: collaboratively, jointly, cooperatively|反义词: independently, separately|词性:副词
词源: inter- (between) + institution + -al + -ly
