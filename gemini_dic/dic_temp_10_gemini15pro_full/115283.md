manlessness:/ˈmæn.ləs.nəs/
noun
缺乏男子气概，缺乏男人，没有男子汉
The state or condition of lacking men, masculine qualities, or male leadership.
The film portrays the manlessness of modern society. 这部电影描绘了现代社会的缺乏男子气概。
近义词: effeminacy, unmanliness
反义词: masculinity, manliness
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From man + -less + -ness.  "Man" comes from Old English "mann," meaning "human being, person, male."  "-less" is a suffix indicating absence or lack of something. "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, denoting a state or quality.
