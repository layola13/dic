capsule:/ˈkæpsjuːl/ (US), /ˈkæpsjuːl/ (UK)| n. | 胶囊；太空舱；荚膜；简要概括 |A small case or container, typically cylindrical and made of gelatin, for holding medicine that is to be swallowed.  A sealed cabin in a spacecraft for astronauts or animals. A concise summary.  |The doctor prescribed antibiotics in capsule form. 医生开了胶囊状的抗生素。|Synonyms: tablet, pill, spacecraft, pod, summary, synopsis|Antonyms: expansion, enlargement|noun

capsule:/ˈkæpsjuːl/ (US), /ˈkæpsjuːl/ (UK)| adj. | 微型的；精简的；压缩的 | Extremely concise or compact. |The report provided a capsule summary of the event. 报告对事件作了简要的概述。|Synonyms: concise, compact, miniature|Antonyms: lengthy, expansive|adjective
