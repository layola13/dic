metaphonic:/ˌmɛtəˈfɒnɪk/ | /ˌmɛtəˈfɑːnɪk/ | adj. | 变音的，元音变化的 | Relating to or characterized by metaphony, a type of sound change in which a vowel is influenced by a following vowel or consonant. | The metaphonic changes in this dialect are quite noticeable.  这个方言中的变音变化非常明显。| near synonyms: vowel mutation, umlaut (specifically for Germanic languages), vowel harmony | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, as it describes a linguistic process) | Adjective
