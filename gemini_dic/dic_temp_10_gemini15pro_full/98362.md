insisting:/ɪnˈsɪstɪŋ/ | adj. | 坚持的，坚决要求的 | Present participle of "insist," meaning continuing to assert or demand something firmly. |  He was insistent on paying for the meal. 他坚持要付饭钱。|  persistent, adamant, pertinacious | yielding, flexible | verb (present participle)


insist:/ɪnˈsɪst/ | v. | 坚持，坚决要求 | To demand something firmly and not give up. | She insisted that she was innocent. 她坚持说她是无辜的。 | persist, persevere, maintain | yield, concede | verb
