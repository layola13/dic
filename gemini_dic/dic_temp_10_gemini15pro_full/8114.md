anti-aliasing:/ˈæntiˈeɪliəsɪŋ/ | n. | 抗锯齿，反走样 |  A technique used in computer graphics to smooth out the jagged edges of lines and curves, making them appear smoother and less pixelated. |  Anti-aliasing is important for making images look more realistic. 抗锯齿对于使图像看起来更逼真是很重要的。 |  Anti-aliasing is essential for high-resolution displays.  抗锯齿对于高分辨率显示器至关重要。 | synonyms: smoothing, de-jagging | antonyms: aliasing | noun
