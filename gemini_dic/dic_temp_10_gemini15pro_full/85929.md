headliners:/ˈhedˌlaɪnərz/
noun
标题人物；主要演员；头条新闻人物
A person or group of people who are the main performers or stars in a show, concert, or other event, or whose names are featured in the headlines of newspapers or other media.
The headliners of the festival included some of the biggest names in music.  这个音乐节的领衔人物包括一些乐坛巨星。
synonyms: stars, leading actors, top performers, main attraction
antonyms: supporting cast, minor characters, opening act
n.
