foolify: /ˈfuːlɪfaɪ/ | v. | 愚弄，欺骗 | To make someone look or feel foolish; to trick or deceive. |  He foolified me with his ridiculous story. 他用他荒谬的故事愚弄了我。

He tried to foolify the police with a fake alibi. 他试图用一个假的不在场证明来愚弄警察。

| 近义词: trick, deceive, dupe, hoodwink | 反义词: enlighten, educate, inform | 词性: 动词
