nonvehicular:/ˌnɒnviˈhɪkjʊlə(r)/ | adj. | 非车辆的；非机动车的 | Not relating to or involving vehicles; not carried in or by a vehicle. |  The city promotes nonvehicular transportation like biking and walking.  这座城市提倡自行车和步行等非机动车交通方式。 | The park offers numerous nonvehicular activities such as hiking and picnicking.  这个公园提供许多非车辆活动，例如徒步旅行和野餐。 | 近义词: pedestrian, non-motorized | 反义词: vehicular, motorized | 词性: adjective

词源:
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "vehicular" (from "vehicle," ultimately from Latin "vehiculum").
