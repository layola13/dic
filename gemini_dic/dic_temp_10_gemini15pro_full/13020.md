astrachan:/ˈæstrəkən/| n.|阿斯特拉罕羔羊皮，阿斯特拉罕(俄罗斯西南部城市)|A type of curly lambskin with a tight, lustrous curl, used for coats, hats, etc.; also, a fabric with a similar appearance.  It's named after Astrakhan, a city in southwestern Russia.|She wore an astrachan coat. 她穿着一件阿斯特拉罕羔羊皮大衣。|近义词:persian lamb, karakul|反义词:None|词性:noun
