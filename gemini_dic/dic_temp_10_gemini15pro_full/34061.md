civically: /ˈsɪvɪkli/ | adv. |公民地，市民地，在公民方面|In a way that relates to the duties or activities of people in relation to their town, city, or local area.|  She has always been civically engaged, volunteering for local charities and attending town hall meetings. 她一直积极参与公民活动，为当地慈善机构做义工，并参加市政厅会议。|近义词: publicly, socially, responsibly|反义词: privately, irresponsibly|词性: 副词

civically: /ˈsɪvɪkli/ | adv. |文明地，有礼貌地|In a polite and courteous manner.|They debated the issue civically and respectfully. 他们文明而尊重地辩论了这个问题。|近义词: politely, courteously, respectfully|反义词: rudely, disrespectfully|词性: 副词


词源:
From "civic" + "-ly."  "Civic" comes from the Latin "civicus," meaning "relating to a citizen," from "civis," meaning "citizen."
