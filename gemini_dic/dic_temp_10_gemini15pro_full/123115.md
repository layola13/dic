mongrels:/ˈmʌŋɡrəlz/ | n. | 杂种狗，杂种动物，混血儿（贬义）| A dog of mixed breed; a cross between different breeds.  Can also be used (offensively) to refer to people of mixed descent. | The shelter was full of adorable mongrels waiting for homes.  收容所里满是可爱的杂种狗，等待着被收养。|  The racist referred to immigrants as "mongrels".  那个种族主义者把移民称为“杂种”。 |Synonyms:  mutt, crossbreed, hybrid (for animals), mixed-race (for people - more neutral) | Antonyms: purebred, pedigree | noun
