insularly:/ˈɪnsjələrli/ | adv. | 狭隘地；孤立地 | In a narrow-minded or isolated way; characteristic of an island or islanders. |  He viewed the world insularly, unaware of the larger global context. 他狭隘地看待这个世界，没有意识到更大的全球背景。| Example: She insularly clung to the traditions of her small town. 她固执地坚守着她的小镇的传统。 | Synonyms: narrowly, provincially, parochially | Antonyms: broadly, cosmopolitanly, open-mindedly |词性:副词
