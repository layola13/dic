noncongealing:/nɒnkənˈdʒiːlɪŋ/
adjective
不凝固的，不冻结的
Not solidifying or thickening, especially not changing from a liquid to a solid or semisolid state.
The scientist was studying a noncongealing liquid for use in cold climates.  科学家正在研究一种用于寒冷气候的不凝固液体。
The noncongealing oil remained fluid even at subzero temperatures. 即使在零度以下，不凝固的油仍然保持流动状态。
non-freezing, non-solidifying
