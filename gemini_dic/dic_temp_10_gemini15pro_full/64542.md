errantry: /ˈerəntri/ | n. | 骑士的冒险，游侠生涯；冒险行为；错误的行为 | The act of wandering in search of adventure, typically as a knight-errant; adventurous wandering; a wandering from the right course. |  He set off on a life of errantry, seeking to prove his bravery. 他开始了骑士冒险生涯，寻求证明自己的勇气。

|  Her youthful errantry led her to many strange and wonderful places.  她年轻时的冒险经历带她去了许多奇异而美好的地方。 | Synonyms: adventure, quest, roaming, wandering | Antonyms:  staying, settling, inactivity | noun
