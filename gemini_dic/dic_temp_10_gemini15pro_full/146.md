abasedly: /əˈbeɪsɪdli/
adverb
卑躬屈膝地，低声下气地
In a manner showing shame and humility.  Showing humiliation or submissiveness.
He abasedly apologized for his mistake. 他低声下气地为自己的错误道歉。

She abasedly begged for forgiveness. 她卑躬屈膝地乞求原谅。

近义词: humbly, subserviently, abjectly, shamefacedly
反义词: proudly, arrogantly, haughtily
词性: adverb

词源: From the verb "abase" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Abase" comes from Old French "abaissier," meaning "to lower," from "a-" (down) + "baissier" (to lower), ultimately from Latin "bassus" (low).
