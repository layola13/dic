dishallucination:/dɪsˌhəˌluːsɪˈneɪʃən/
n.
消除幻觉，停止幻觉
The process of ceasing to hallucinate.  The reduction or cessation of hallucinations.
After taking the medication, he experienced a gradual dishallucination. 服用药物后，他的幻觉逐渐消失了。
The new therapy aims to promote dishallucination in patients with schizophrenia.  这种新疗法旨在促进精神分裂症患者停止幻觉。
近义词:  recovery, remission, abatement
反义词: hallucination, delusion
词性: noun
