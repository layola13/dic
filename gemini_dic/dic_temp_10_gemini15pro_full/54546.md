dislimb:/dɪsˈlɪm/
verb
肢解; 切断...的四肢
To deprive of limbs; cut the limbs off.
The executioner dislimbed the traitor's body after he was hanged. 刽子手将叛徒绞死后肢解了他的尸体。

The savage beast dislimbed its prey. 这头野兽肢解了它的猎物。

近义词: mutilate, dismember
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 heal, mend)
词性: verb
