hypersensitizing:/ˌhaɪpərˈsɛnsɪˌtaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使过敏；使过度敏感
Increasing the sensitivity of something, typically photographic film, to light or other stimuli.
The photographer hypersensitized the film to capture images in the low light conditions. 摄影师使胶片过度感光以便在弱光条件下拍摄图像。
hypersensitizing:/ˌhaɪpərˈsɛnsɪˌtaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
过敏的；过度敏感的
Having or causing increased sensitivity.
The hypersensitized film allowed for shooting in near darkness. 过度感光的胶片可以在近乎黑暗的环境下拍摄。


近义词:sensitizing, heightening
反义词:desensitizing, lessening
词性:verb, adjective
