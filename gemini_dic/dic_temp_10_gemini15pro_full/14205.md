autocatalysis:/ˌɔːtəʊkəˈtælɪsɪs/ | n. | 自催化 |The process by which a reaction product or by-product acts as a catalyst, accelerating the reaction.  A single reaction is autocatalytic if one of the reaction products is also a catalyst for the same or a coupled reaction.| The oxidation of oxalic acid by permanganate is an example of autocatalysis. 草酸被高锰酸盐氧化是自催化的一个例子。|  catalysis, positive feedback | inhibition | noun
