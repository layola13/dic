evesdropper:/ˈiːvzˌdrɒpər/ (US) /ˈiːvzˌdrɒpə(r)/ (UK)| n. |窃听者；偷听者|A person who secretly listens to a conversation.|例句：The eavesdropper was caught listening in on a private conversation.  窃听者在偷听私人谈话时被抓住了。

例句：She suspected an eavesdropper was outside her window. 她怀疑窗外有人在偷听。

近义词：listener, snooper, wiretapper|反义词: None|词性: noun
