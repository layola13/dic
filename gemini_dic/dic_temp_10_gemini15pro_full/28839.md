carpellary: /kɑːrˈpɛləri/ | adj. | 心皮的，由心皮构成的 | Relating to or consisting of a carpel or carpels, the female reproductive organs of a flower. | The carpellary leaves form the pistil. 心皮叶形成了雌蕊。| The flower has five carpellary segments. 这朵花有五个心皮部分。|  synonymous: gynoecial, pistillate | antonymous: staminate | adjective
