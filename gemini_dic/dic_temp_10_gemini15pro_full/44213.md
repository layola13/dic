crochet:/kroʊˈʃeɪ/ (US) /ˈkrəʊʃeɪ/ (UK)| n. v. |钩针编织；钩针编织品|A method of creating fabric from yarn or thread using a crochet hook.  Also refers to the fabric created by this method. |She crocheted a beautiful blanket for her granddaughter. 她为孙女钩织了一条漂亮的毯子。|knitting, tatting|sewing, weaving|noun, verb


crochet:/kroʊˈʃeɪ/ (US) /ˈkrəʊʃeɪ/ (UK)| n. v. |钩住；用钩钩住|To catch or pull with a hook. |He crocheted the fish out of the water. 他用钩子把鱼从水中钩了出来。|hook, snag, catch|release, free|noun, verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *crochet* "small hook," diminutive of *croc* "hook," from Old Norse *krókr*.
