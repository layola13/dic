hurrahed:/həˈrɑːd/ |v. |欢呼，喝彩|To cheer or acclaim enthusiastically.|The crowd hurrahed as the winning team took the field.  人群为获胜的队伍欢呼雀跃。|Synonyms: cheered, acclaimed, applauded|Antonyms: booed, jeered|verb

hurrahed:/həˈrɑːd/ |adj. |受到欢呼的| Greeted with cheers; acclaimed. |The hurrahed hero returned home to a ticker-tape parade. 受到欢呼的英雄回到家乡，接受了彩带游行。|Synonyms: acclaimed, celebrated, lauded|Antonyms: condemned, criticized|adjective
