deletors:/dɪˈliːtərz/
n.
删除者，删除程序
People or programs that delete things.  Specifically referring to something or someone that removes data or files.
The deletors erased all the sensitive data from the server. 删除程序从服务器上删除了所有敏感数据。
The company hired deletors to purge the old files. 公司雇佣了删除者来清除旧文件。
Synonyms: erasers, removers, cleaners
Antonyms: creators, savers, preservers
noun
