intermeningeal:/ˌɪntərˌmɛnɪnˈdʒiːəl/
adjective
脑膜间的；硬脑膜与蛛网膜之间的
Located or occurring between the meninges (the membranes surrounding the brain and spinal cord).
An intermeningeal hemorrhage can be life-threatening. 脑膜间出血可能是致命的。
Synonyms: intradural (more general)
Antonyms: extradural, epidural
词性: 形容词
