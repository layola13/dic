nondyspeptical:/ˌnɒndɪsˈpɛptɪkəl/ | adj. |  无消化不良的；促进消化的 | Not suffering from or causing dyspepsia (indigestion). |  This herbal tea is considered nondyspeptical and can be enjoyed after a meal. 这种凉茶被认为是促进消化的，可以在饭后饮用。|  Synonyms: digestible, easy to digest | Antonyms: dyspeptic, indigestible | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + dyspeptical (relating to dyspepsia).  Dyspepsia comes from Greek dys- (bad, difficult) + pepsis (digestion).
