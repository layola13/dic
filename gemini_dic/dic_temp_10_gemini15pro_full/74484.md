frankl: /ˈfræŋkl/ | n. | 弗兰克尔 (专有名词，指Viktor Frankl)| Refers to Viktor Frankl, an Austrian neurologist, psychiatrist, philosopher, author, and Holocaust survivor. He is the founder of logotherapy, a school of psychotherapy which focuses on finding meaning in life. | Viktor Frankl's book, "Man's Search for Meaning," details his experiences in Auschwitz and other concentration camps. 维克多·弗兰克尔的著作《活出生命的意义》详细描述了他在奥斯维辛和其他集中营的经历。 |  近义词: (无直接近义词，可根据语境使用 synonyms for "psychotherapist," "philosopher," or "author") | 反义词: (无直接反义词) | 词性: noun
