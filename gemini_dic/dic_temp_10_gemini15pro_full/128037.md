nerd:/nɜːrd/ (US) /nɜːd/ (UK)| n.|书呆子，讨厌鬼；电脑迷|A person who is extremely interested in one subject, especially computers, and knows a lot of facts about it, but who might not be good at communicating with other people.| He's a real computer nerd. 他是个十足的电脑迷。|Synonyms: geek, dweeb, wonk|Antonyms: jock, social butterfly|Noun
