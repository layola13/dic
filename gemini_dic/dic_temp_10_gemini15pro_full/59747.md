ecologist:/iˈkɒlədʒɪst/ (UK) /iˈkɑːlədʒɪst/ (US)| n.|生态学家|A scientist who studies the relationships between organisms and their environment.|例句：The ecologist studied the impact of pollution on the local bird population.  这位生态学家研究了污染对当地鸟类种群的影响。

例句：The ecologist warned about the dangers of deforestation.  这位生态学家警告了森林砍伐的危险。|近义词：environmental scientist, conservationist|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
