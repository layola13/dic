accessible:/əkˈsesəbl/| adj.|可进入的，可接近的；可理解的，易懂的|Easy to approach, reach, enter, speak with, or use.  Able to be understood or appreciated.|例句：The beach is easily accessible by car.  这个海滩开车很容易到达。

The information is readily accessible online.  这些信息在网上很容易查到。|近义词：approachable, available, attainable, reachable, understandable|反义词：inaccessible, unapproachable, unavailable, unreachable, obscure|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *accesseble*, from Late Latin *accessibilis* (“approachable”), from Latin *accessus* (“approach”) + *-ibilis* (suffix forming adjectives meaning “able to be”). *Accessus* is from *accedere* (“to approach”), from *ad* (“to, towards”) + *cedere* (“to go, move”).
