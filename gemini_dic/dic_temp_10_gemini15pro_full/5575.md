altair: /ælˈtɛər/ | n. | 牛郎星; 牵牛星 | A bright star in the constellation Aquila.  It is one of the vertices of the Summer Triangle. |  例句：Altair is easily visible in the summer sky. 牛郎星在夏季夜空中很容易看到。

例句：The myth of Altair and Vega is a popular love story in East Asia. 牛郎星和织女星的神话在东亚是一个流行的爱情故事。| 近义词: Alpha Aquilae (formal name) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
