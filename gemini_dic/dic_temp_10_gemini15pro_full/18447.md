bemeet: /biˈmiːt/
verb |相遇，相逢| To encounter or come into contact with someone, often unexpectedly. |例句：I bemet my old friend at the airport. 我在机场遇见了我的老朋友。|近义词：encounter, meet, run into|反义词: avoid, miss|词性: verb
词源： "be-" (prefix meaning "to be") + "meet" (verb meaning "to come into contact with"). This construction, though grammatically possible, is archaic and rarely used in modern English.  The preferred form is simply "meet."
