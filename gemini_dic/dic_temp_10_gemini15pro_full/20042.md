bionicist: /baɪˈɑːnɪsɪst/
n.
仿生学家；仿生专家
A specialist in bionics; a person who designs, builds, or maintains bionic devices or systems.
The bionicist explained how the prosthetic limb interacted with the nervous system. 仿生学家解释了假肢如何与神经系统相互作用。
The bionicist was awarded a prize for her groundbreaking work in developing artificial organs.  这位仿生学家因其在开发人造器官方面的突破性工作而获奖。
近义词: bioengineer, biomedical engineer
反义词:  None (This is a specialized field; a direct antonym doesn't exist.)
词性: noun


Etymology:
From bionic + -ist.  "Bionic" combines bio- (life) + electronic.
