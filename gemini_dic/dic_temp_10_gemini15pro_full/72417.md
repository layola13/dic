fluffiest:/ˈflʌfiɪst/| adj.|最毛茸茸的|Superlative form of fluffy; having the most fluff or soft, downy material.|The fluffiest kitten I've ever seen curled up in my lap. 我见过最毛茸茸的小猫蜷缩在我的腿上。|近义词：softest, downiest, plushiest|反义词:smoothest, flattest, hardest|词性:形容词

词源:
From *fluff* + *-y* (adjective suffix) + *-est* (superlative suffix). *Fluff* likely originates from imitative or expressive words mimicking the sound of light, airy material.
