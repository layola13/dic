continuant:/kənˈtɪnjʊənt/ |adj. n.|连续的，持续的；延续音|A continuant is a sound produced by allowing air to flow continuously through the mouth, without complete closure at any point.|The sound /f/ is a continuant. /f/音是一个延续音。|  The river flowed in a continuant stream. 河水持续不断地流淌。|Synonyms: continuous, ongoing, sustained, prolonged|Antonyms: intermittent, interrupted, sporadic|adjective

continuant:/kənˈtɪnjʊənt/ |n.|延续音|A continuant is a speech sound, such as /f/, /v/, /s/, /z/, /θ/, /ð/, /ʃ/, /ʒ/, /m/, /n/, /ŋ/, /l/, /r/, /w/, and /j/, produced by allowing air to flow continuously through the mouth, without complete closure at any point.|Nasals like /m/ and /n/ are classified as continuants. 像/m/和/n/这样的鼻音被归类为延续音。|The speech therapist explained the difference between stops and continuants.  言语治疗师解释了塞音和延续音之间的区别。|Synonyms: fricative, approximant, nasal|Antonyms: stop, plosive|noun
