glyphographic:/ˌɡlɪfəˈɡræfɪk/
adjective
雕刻的，字形的
relating to or consisting of glyphs
The glyphographic inscriptions on the ancient tomb were difficult to decipher. 古墓上的雕刻文字难以辨认。
近义词:inscribed, carved, engraved
反义词:plain, smooth
