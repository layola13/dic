oedipal:/ˈiːdɪp(ə)l/ /iːˈdɪpəl/
|adj.|
俄狄浦斯的；恋母情结的；
|Relating to or characteristic of the Oedipus complex.|
His oedipal feelings made him resent his father. 他恋母情结的感情使他怨恨他的父亲。

|
The play explored the protagonist's oedipal conflicts. 这部戏剧探讨了主人公的俄狄浦斯冲突。

|
Synonyms: incestuous, libidinal
|
Antonyms: non-sexual, platonic
|
