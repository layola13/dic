enharmonical: /ɛnˌhɑːrˈmɒnɪkəl/ /ɛnˌhɑːrˈmɑːnɪkəl/
enharmonical: adj.
enharmonical: 等音的，调和的
enharmonical:  Relating to two notes or intervals having the same pitch in some systems of tuning, but written differently and representing different functions within a key or scale.  In Western music, enharmonic notes sound the same on a piano, but they are conceptually distinct.
enharmonical:  C sharp and D flat are enharmonic equivalents. 升C和降D是等音.
enharmonical: synonymous, equivalent, identical
enharmonical: different, distinct, unrelated
enharmonical: adjective
