nonreductive:/ˌnɒnrɪˈdʌktɪv/ | adj. | 非还原性的 | Not involving or causing reduction, especially in chemistry (not involving a decrease in oxidation number).  In a broader sense, it can also mean preserving or maintaining the complexity or integrity of something. | This artist uses a nonreductive approach, layering images and ideas rather than simplifying them. 这位艺术家使用非还原性方法，将图像和想法分层而不是简化它们。| The chef's nonreductive sauce preserved the distinct flavors of each ingredient.  这位厨师的非还原性酱汁保留了每种成分的独特风味。 | 近义词: holistic, integrative, comprehensive | 反义词: reductive, simplistic | 词性: adjective

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + reductive (from reduce, ultimately from Latin reducere "to lead back")
