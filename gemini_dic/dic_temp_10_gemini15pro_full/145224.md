overbeating: /ˌoʊvərˈbiːtɪŋ/
verb
过度搅拌，过度敲打
Mixing ingredients, especially egg whites or cream, too vigorously, resulting in a loss of volume and a tough texture.
The cake was dense and dry because I overbeat the batter.  蛋糕又硬又干，因为我过度搅拌了面糊。
She overbeat the egg whites until they were stiff and dry. 她把蛋清打得又硬又干。
synonyms: overmix, overwhip
antonyms: undermix, underwhip
verb

overbeating: /ˌoʊvərˈbiːtɪŋ/
noun
过度搅拌，过度敲打
The act of mixing ingredients too vigorously.
The overbeating of the egg whites caused the soufflé to collapse. 蛋白的过度搅拌导致蛋奶酥塌陷。
The recipe cautioned against overbeating. 食谱警告不要过度搅拌。
synonyms: overmixing, overwhipping
antonyms: undermixing, underwhipping
noun
