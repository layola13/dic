demisuit:/ˈdɛmiˌsut/| n.|半正式西装|A lounge suit or business suit of intermediate formality. It is less formal than a full suit and more formal than casual wear.|He wore a demisuit to the business casual event.|Near synonyms: business casual attire, lounge suit|Opposite: formal suit|noun
