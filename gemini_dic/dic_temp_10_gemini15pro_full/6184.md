amitotic: /ˌeɪmaɪˈtɒtɪk/ /ˌeɪmaɪˈtɑːtɪk/
adjective
无丝分裂的
Relating to or denoting cell division by simple cleavage of the nucleus and division of the cytoplasm without spindle formation or the appearance of chromosomes.
Amitotic cell division is a form of cell division that occurs without the involvement of mitosis.
近义词: direct, simple
反义词: mitotic
词性: adjective

例句：
Some cells undergo amitotic division.
一些细胞进行无丝分裂。

Amitotic division is less common than mitotic division.
无丝分裂比有丝分裂少见。
