non-regardance:/ˌnɒn rɪˈɡɑːrdəns/ | n. |漠视，忽视，不理会|The state of not regarding or paying attention to something. |His non-regardance of the rules resulted in his dismissal. 他对规则的漠视导致了他的解雇。|The company showed a non-regardance for environmental regulations.  这家公司对环境法规漠不关心。|Synonyms: disregard, neglect, indifference, inattention|Antonyms: regard, attention, heed, notice|Noun
