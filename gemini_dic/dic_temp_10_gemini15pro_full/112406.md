lote:/ˈloʊteɪ/ (US), /ˈlɒteɪ/ (UK)| n.| 批次，一批，地段| A lote is a batch or group of items, often used in commercial contexts, or a plot of land.| The company received a new lote of computer chips.  这家公司收到了一批新的电脑芯片。| The builder purchased a lote of land to develop new homes.  建筑商购买了一块地来开发新住宅。|synonyms: batch, consignment, lot, parcel, plot, tract|antonyms: individual, single|noun
