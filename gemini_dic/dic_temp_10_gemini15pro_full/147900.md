overreductions:/ˌoʊvərɪˈdʌkʃənz/ | n. | 过度还原; 过度简化 |  The act of reducing something too much, often simplifying it to the point of inaccuracy or distortion.  It can refer to simplifying a complex issue to the point where crucial details are lost, or reducing a chemical compound beyond its intended state in a chemical reaction. |  The overreductions in the model made it useless for predicting real-world outcomes.  模型的过度简化使其无法预测现实世界的结果。

The overreductions in his explanation of the conflict left out important historical context. 他对冲突的解释过于简化，忽略了重要的历史背景。 | 近义词: oversimplifications, distortions | 反义词: elaborations, expansions | 词性: 名词
