infraction: /ɪnˈfrækʃən/ | n. | 违规，违反；侵害 | A violation of a rule, law, or agreement; a breach. |  The driver received a ticket for a traffic infraction. 司机因交通违规收到了一张罚单。

The student's infraction of the school's dress code resulted in detention.  这个学生违反了学校的着装规定，被留校了。

| 近义词: violation, breach, offense, transgression, infringement | 反义词: obedience, compliance, observance | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *infraccion*, from Middle French *infraction*, and directly from Latin *infractiō*, from *infringere* (“to break, shatter”), from *in-* + *frangere*.  Equivalent to *in-* +‎ *fraction*.
