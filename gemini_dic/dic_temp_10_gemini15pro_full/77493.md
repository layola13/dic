gazers:/ˈɡeɪzərz/
noun
凝视者；注视者
People who gaze; those who look intently at something.
The gazers watched the fireworks light up the night sky. 凝视者们看着烟火照亮夜空。
The gazers were captivated by the acrobat's performance.  注视者们被杂技演员的表演迷住了。
observers, onlookers, viewers
ignorers
