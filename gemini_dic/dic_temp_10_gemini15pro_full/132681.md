nondispersive:/ˌnɒndɪˈspɜːsɪv/ | adj. | 非弥散的，非分散的 | Not dispersing or tending to disperse; specifically (of a medium) not separating light of different wavelengths, or (of light) not separated into its constituent wavelengths. |  Nondispersive infrared sensors are commonly used in gas detection. 非弥散红外传感器常用于气体检测。 | non-dispersive, non-scattering | dispersive, scattering | adjective
