grocer:/ˈɡroʊsər/ (US) /ˈɡrəʊsə(r)/ (UK)| n.|食品杂货商；杂货店|A grocer is a person who sells food and other household supplies. A grocer can also refer to the store itself, especially a small, independent one.| I bought some milk and bread from the local grocer. 我从当地的杂货店买了些牛奶和面包。|近义词：shopkeeper, retailer, store owner|反义词: customer, buyer | noun

grocer's:/ˈɡroʊsərz/ (US) /ˈɡrəʊsəz/ (UK)| n.|杂货店|A grocer's is a grocery store. It's a shortened form of "grocer's shop".|I'm going to the grocer's to pick up some vegetables. 我要去杂货店买些蔬菜。|近义词：grocery store, supermarket, market|反义词: N/A | noun
