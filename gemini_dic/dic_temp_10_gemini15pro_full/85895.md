headfish:/ˈhɛdfɪʃ/| n. | 翻车鱼, 头鱼 |A large, pelagic, bony fish (Mola mola) with a compressed, disk-shaped body and very short tail. It is the heaviest bony fish in the world. |例句：The headfish, also known as the ocean sunfish, can grow to enormous sizes.  翻车鱼，也被称为海洋太阳鱼，可以长到巨大的尺寸。

例句：We spotted a headfish basking near the surface of the water. 我们发现一条翻车鱼在水面附近晒太阳。|近义词：ocean sunfish, mola mola |反义词: N/A |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From 'head' + 'fish', referring to the seemingly large head in proportion to the body.
