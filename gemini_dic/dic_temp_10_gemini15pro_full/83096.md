gusseting:/ˈɡʌsɪtɪŋ/
n.
三角形衬布；衬料；加固片
The act of adding gussets (triangular pieces of fabric) to a garment or other item to provide shaping, extra room, or reinforcement.
The gusseting under the arms of the shirt allows for greater freedom of movement.  衬衫腋下的三角形衬布可以提供更大的活动自由。
The backpack features robust gusseting for increased durability.  这款背包的特点是结实的加固片，增加了耐用性。
reinforcement, strengthening, bracing
reduction, weakening
noun

gusset:/ˈɡʌsɪt/
n.
三角形衬布；衬料；加固片
A triangular piece of fabric inserted into a garment or other item to provide shaping, extra room, or reinforcement.
The gusset in the crotch of the trousers provides extra comfort. 裤裆处的三角形衬布提供了额外的舒适感。
The tent has gussets at the corners for added stability.  帐篷的角落有加固片，增加了稳定性。
insert, piece, patch
gap, hole
noun
