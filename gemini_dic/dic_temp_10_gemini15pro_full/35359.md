co-infinite:/ˌkoʊˈɪnfɪnɪt/ | adj. | 共无限的；余无限的 | Having the same complement.  In set theory, two subsets of a given set are co-infinite if their complements are both infinite. |  The sets of even numbers and odd numbers are co-infinite within the set of integers. 整数集中的偶数集和奇数集是共无限的。 | The two co-infinite sets had an interesting intersection.  这两个共无限集合有一个有趣的交集。 |  Near-synonyms: complementary (in some contexts) | Antonym: finite (in a relative sense, as the complement would be finite) | adjective
