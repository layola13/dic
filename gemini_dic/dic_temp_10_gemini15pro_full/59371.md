earnings-related:/ˈɜːrnɪŋz rɪˈleɪtɪd/
adjective
与收入相关的; 按收入比例计算的
Relating to the amount of money that someone earns.
Your earnings-related state pension depends on how much you have paid in National Insurance contributions. 你的与收入相关的国家养老金取决于你缴纳的国民保险费的多少。
Synonyms: income-related, salary-related, wage-related
Antonyms: fixed, flat (in the context of payments)
词性: adjective
