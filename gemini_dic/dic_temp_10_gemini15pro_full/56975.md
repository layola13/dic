double-checking:/ˌdʌbəlˈtʃɛkɪŋ/ | n./v./adj. | 双重检查；再次确认 | the act of checking something for a second time to make sure that it is correct or safe | I'm double-checking all the figures before I submit the report. 我在提交报告前正在再次核对所有数字。| synonyms: verifying, confirming, rechecking | antonyms: overlooking, neglecting | noun/verb/adjective

double-checking:/ˌdʌbəlˈtʃɛkɪŋ/ | adj. | 双重检查的 |  done with a second check to ensure accuracy or safety |  We use a double-checking system to minimize errors. 我们使用双重检查系统来减少错误。| synonyms: redundant, backup | antonyms: single, unchecked | adjective
