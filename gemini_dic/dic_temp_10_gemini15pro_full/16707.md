barrio:/ˈbɑːrioʊ/ (US), /ˈbærioʊ/ (UK)| n. |街区，社区（尤指西班牙裔聚居区）|A district of a town or city, especially one with a strong ethnic identity, typically Hispanic. |例句：
I grew up in a vibrant barrio in Los Angeles.
我在洛杉矶一个充满活力的街区长大。

Many of the city's Puerto Rican residents live in this barrio.
这个城市的许多波多黎各居民都住在这个街区。

|近义词：neighborhood, district, community, quarter|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
