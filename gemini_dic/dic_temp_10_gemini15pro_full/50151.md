denunciative:/dɪˈnʌnsiˌeɪtɪv/
adjective
谴责的；告发的；抨击的
Expressing strong disapproval; condemning.
His denunciative speech criticized the government's policies. 他谴责性的演讲批评了政府的政策。
The article was denunciative of the corruption within the organization. 这篇文章谴责了该组织内部的腐败现象。
Synonyms: condemnatory, critical, accusatory, incriminatory
Antonyms: complimentary, laudatory, approving, supportive
词性: adjective
