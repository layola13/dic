adjective

Not able to be explored or investigated; not able to be discovered or understood.
例句：The dense jungle was inexploreable.  这片茂密的丛林无法探险。

例句：His motives remain inexploreable. 他的动机仍然难以理解。

近义词：uncharted, impenetrable, inscrutable, mysterious
反义词：exploreable, accessible, understandable, known
词性: adjective
