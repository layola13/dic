globals:/ˈɡloʊbəlz/| n.|全局变量|Variables that are defined outside of any function and are accessible from anywhere within the program.|例句：The globals dictionary stores all global variables. 全局变量字典存储所有全局变量。

例句：Modifying globals directly is generally discouraged. 直接修改全局变量通常是不鼓励的。|近义词：global variables|反义词：local variables|词性:noun
