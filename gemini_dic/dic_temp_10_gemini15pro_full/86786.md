hemachrome:/ˈhiːməkroʊm/| n.|血色素，血色原|A colored substance containing iron and derived from hemoglobin.  It is responsible for the red color of blood and muscle.|The hemachrome in the sample indicated a high iron content.|近义词：hemoglobin, myoglobin|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider substances that inhibit hemachrome formation)|noun
