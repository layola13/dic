helmeting:/ˈhɛlmɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 戴头盔；装安全帽 | The act of putting a helmet on someone or something. |  The construction workers began helmeting before entering the site. 建筑工人进入现场前开始戴上安全帽。|  The company enforced a strict policy of helmeting for all cyclists.  公司对所有骑自行车的人强制执行严格的戴头盔政策。 | synonyms: equipping with helmets, protecting with helmets | antonyms:  unhelmeting, removing helmets | noun
