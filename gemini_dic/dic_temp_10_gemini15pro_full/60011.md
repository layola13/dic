editress:/ˈedɪtrəs/
n.
女编辑；女性编辑
A woman who edits written material.
例句：The editress reviewed the manuscript carefully before publication. 女编辑在出版前仔细审阅了稿件。

例句：The editress worked late into the night to meet the deadline.  这位女编辑工作到深夜以赶上截止日期。

近义词：editor, copyeditor
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps author or writer)
词性：noun
