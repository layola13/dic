barringer:/ˈbærɪŋər/| n. |巴林杰|Relating to Daniel Barringer, an American geologist and mining engineer known for his impact crater studies, particularly the Barringer Crater (also known as Meteor Crater) in Arizona.  It can be used attributively to describe things related to him or the crater.|The Barringer Crater is a popular tourist destination. 巴林杰陨石坑是一个热门的旅游目的地。|近义词: (For the crater itself) meteor crater, impact crater|反义词: N/A|词性: Noun (proper adjective)
