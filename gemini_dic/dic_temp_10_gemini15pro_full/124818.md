mucinogen:/mjuːˈsɪnədʒən/ | n. | 粘蛋白原 | A substance that stimulates the formation of mucin. |  The goblet cells secrete mucinogen, which hydrates to form mucin. 杯状细胞分泌粘蛋白原，水合后形成粘蛋白。|  The researchers identified the specific mucinogen responsible for the increased viscosity of the mucus. 研究人员确定了导致粘液粘度增加的特定粘蛋白原。 | synonym: mucin precursor | antonym:  (No direct antonym, potentially mucinolytic agent - a substance that breaks down mucin) | noun
