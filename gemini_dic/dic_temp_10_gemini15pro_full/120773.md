misappraising:/ˌmɪsəˈpreɪzɪŋ/
verb
错误评估；估错；误判
Incorrectly estimating or judging the value or worth of something.
He was misappraising the situation and underestimated the risks involved. 他错误地评估了形势，低估了所涉及的风险。
The company misappraised the market demand for their new product.  这家公司错误估计了他们新产品的市场需求。

synonyms: undervaluing, underestimating, misjudging, miscalculating
antonyms: overestimating, overvaluing, correctly assessing, accurately judging
