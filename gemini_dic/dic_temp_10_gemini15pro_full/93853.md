immobilizations:/ɪˌmoʊbəlɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n.|固定；不动产；固定资产|The act of immobilizing something, or the state of being immobilized.  The conversion of liquid assets into fixed assets.|The company's immobilizations in new equipment significantly increased its production capacity. 公司对新设备的固定资产投资大大提高了其生产能力。|Near Synonyms: fixed assets, capital assets, long-term assets|Near Antonyms: liquid assets, current assets|noun
