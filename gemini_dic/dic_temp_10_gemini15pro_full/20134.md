bipectinate:/baɪˈpektɪneɪt/
adj.
双栉形的，两侧具栉齿状的
Having two sets of comb-like teeth or projections, one on each side, like the teeth of a double comb.
The bipectinate antenna of the moth is quite distinctive.  这只蛾子的双栉形触角非常独特。

近义词:comb-like, pectinate
反义词:None
词性:adjective

词源:From Latin *bipectinatus, from bi- "two" + pectinatus "comb-shaped", from pecten "comb".
