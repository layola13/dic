gilovich:/ˈɡɪləvɪtʃ/| n. |吉洛维奇 (通常指社会心理学家 Thomas Gilovich)|Refers to Thomas Gilovich, a social psychologist known for his research on cognitive biases, heuristics, and the psychology of happiness.  Specifically, he's known for his work on the "spotlight effect" and the "experiential advantage."|例句：Gilovich's research has significantly impacted our understanding of social cognition. 吉洛维奇的研究极大地影响了我们对社会认知的理解。

例句：Have you read Gilovich's book on happiness? 你读过吉洛维奇关于幸福的书吗？|近义词： (As a proper noun, it doesn't have direct synonyms.  One might list other social psychologists like Daniel Kahneman or Amos Tversky if referring to the field of study.)|反义词: N/A|词性:Proper Noun
