armadillo:/ˌɑːrməˈdɪloʊ/ (US), /ˌɑːməˈdɪləʊ/ (UK)| n. |犰狳|A small mammal found in Central and South America, covered in bony plates and able to roll into a ball for protection.|The armadillo quickly curled into a ball when it sensed danger. 犰狳感觉到危险时迅速蜷缩成一团。|Synonyms: none (unique animal)|Antonyms: none (unique animal)|noun
