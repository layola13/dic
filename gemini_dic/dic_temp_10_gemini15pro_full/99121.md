interceding:/ˌɪntərˈsiːdɪŋ/
verb
调解，说情，斡旋
pleading on behalf of another; intervening
例句：The lawyer is interceding for his client. 律师正在为他的客户求情。

例句：She interceded with the authorities on behalf of the refugees. 她向当局为难民说情。
近义词：mediating, intervening, advocating, pleading
反义词：ignoring, neglecting

词性: Verb (动词)
