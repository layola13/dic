infinitesimal:/ˌɪnfɪnɪˈtesɪməl/| adj. n.| 极小的，无限小的；无穷小量 |An infinitesimal quantity or difference is so small that it is impossible to measure or calculate.|The effect on sales was infinitesimal. 对销售额的影响微乎其微。|近义词:tiny, minute, microscopic, negligible|反义词:huge, enormous, immense|词性:形容词, 名词

infinitesimal:/ˌɪnfɪnɪˈtesɪməl/| n.| 无穷小; 极小量 |An indefinitely small quantity; a value approaching zero.|Calculus deals with infinitesimals. 微积分处理无穷小量。|近义词:atom, iota, jot|反义词:infinity|词性:名词
