augustanism:/ɔːˈɡʌstənɪzəm/ | n. |奥古斯都主义;奥古斯都时代风格|A style of literature and art characterized by clarity, elegance, and balance, reminiscent of the Roman Empire under Augustus.  It emphasizes order, reason, and restraint, often drawing on classical models.|The architecture of the new library reflected a clear augustanism in its design.  这座新图书馆的建筑在其设计中体现了明显的奥古斯都主义。|Synonyms: classicism, neoclassicism |Antonyms: romanticism, baroque |noun
