heightened:/ˈhaɪtənd/| adj.|加剧的，增强的，提高的|Increased in intensity, degree, or amount.  Derived from the verb "heighten," meaning to make higher or increase.|例句：The heightened security measures after the attack made everyone feel safer.  袭击发生后加强的安保措施让每个人都感觉更安全。|近义词：increased, intensified, elevated, amplified|反义词:reduced, diminished, lowered, decreased|词性:形容词

heighten:/ˈhaɪtn/| v.|提高，增加，加强|To make higher or increase.  From Middle English heighte, ultimately from Old English hēah.|例句：The new policy will heighten awareness of environmental issues.  新政策将提高人们对环境问题的认识。|近义词：increase, intensify, elevate, amplify|反义词:reduce, diminish, lower, decrease|词性:动词
