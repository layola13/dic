fundamentalist:/ˌfʌndəˈmɛntəlɪst/| n. adj.|原教旨主义者，基要主义的|A person who believes in the strict, literal interpretation of scripture in a religion.|例句：He is a religious fundamentalist. 他是一个宗教原教旨主义者。|近义词：dogmatist, traditionalist, orthodox|反义词：liberal, modernist, reformist|词性: noun, adjective

fundamentalist:/ˌfʌndəˈmɛntəlɪst/| adj.|基要主义的|Based on a strict, literal interpretation of scripture in a religion.|例句：The party holds a fundamentalist view of the constitution.  该党对宪法持原教旨主义的观点。|近义词：dogmatic, traditional, orthodox|反义词：liberal, modern, reformist|词性: adjective
