combater:/kəmˈbætər/ /kəmˈbeɪtər/| n.|战士，战斗员|A person who fights, especially in a war or battle. |例句：The brave combaters defended their city.  勇敢的战士们保卫了他们的城市。

例句：The skilled combaters quickly gained the upper hand.  熟练的战士们迅速占据了上风。|近义词：fighter, soldier, warrior|反义词：pacifist, civilian|词性:noun
combater:/kəmˈbætər/ /kəmˈbeɪtər/| v.|与…战斗，搏斗|To fight or contend against; oppose vigorously.|例句：The firefighters combated the blaze for hours. 消防队员与大火搏斗了几个小时。

例句：They combated the disease with a new vaccine.  他们用一种新疫苗对抗这种疾病。|近义词：fight, battle, oppose, resist|反义词：surrender, yield|词性:verb
