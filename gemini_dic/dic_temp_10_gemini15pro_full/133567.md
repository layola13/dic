nonflagellated:/ˌnɒnˈflædʒɪleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 无鞭毛的 | Lacking flagella, whip-like appendages used for locomotion in some cells and microorganisms. |  Nonflagellated bacteria are less mobile than flagellated ones. 无鞭毛细菌的移动性比有鞭毛细菌差。|  Nonflagellated sperm are unable to swim effectively.  无鞭毛精子无法有效游动。| 近义词: immotile, nonmotile | 反义词: flagellated | 词性: 形容词
