guiro:/ˈɡwiːroʊ/ | n. | 刮瓜; 刮葫 | A Latin American percussion instrument consisting of a hollow gourd with parallel notches across which a stick is rubbed to produce a rasping sound. |  He played a rhythmic pattern on the guiro. 他用刮瓜演奏了一段节奏型的音乐。| The band's percussionist used a guiro and maracas. 乐队的打击乐手使用了刮瓜和沙槌。 |  近义词: scraper, rasp | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a musical instrument) | 词性: noun
