overtrusts:/ˌoʊvərˈtrʌsts/
verb
过分信任，过度信赖
places excessive trust or confidence in someone or something.
He overtrusts his employees and gives them too much responsibility. 他过分信任他的员工，赋予他们过多的责任。
She overtrusted the online reviews and bought a faulty product. 她过分相信网上的评论，买了一个有缺陷的产品。
synonyms: relies too heavily on, has too much faith in, puts too much stock in
antonyms: distrusts, doubts, suspects
