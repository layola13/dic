innumerable:/ɪˈnjuːmərəbl/ /ɪˈnuːmərəbl/| adj.|无数的，数不清的|Too numerous to be counted; countless.  So many that they cannot be counted.|The stars in the sky are innumerable. 天上的星星数不胜数。|近义词：countless, myriad, numerous, infinite|反义词:few, countable, limited|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *innumerable*, from Late Latin *innumerabilis*, from Latin *in-* (not) + *numerabilis* (countable), from *numerus* (number). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *nem-* (to take, distribute, allot).
