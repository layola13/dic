decidable:/dɪˈsaɪdəbl/| adj.|可决定的；可判定的|Able to be decided or determined; capable of being resolved.  Relating to a problem that can be solved by a computer algorithm.|The question of whether a given number is prime is decidable.  这个问题，即一个给定的数是否是质数，是可以判定的。|synonyms: determinable, resolvable, solvable|antonyms: undecidable, indeterminable|adjective

decidability:/dɪˌsaɪdəˈbɪləti/| n.|可判定性|The quality of being decidable; the property of a problem for which an algorithm can determine a correct yes/no answer.|The decidability of the halting problem is a fundamental concept in computer science.  停机问题的可判定性是计算机科学中的一个基本概念。|synonyms: determinability|antonyms: undecidability|noun
