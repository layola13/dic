belittle:/bɪˈlɪtl/| vt.|贬低，轻视|To make someone or something seem unimportant.  To cause someone or something to seem less important.|He belittled her achievements, making her feel insignificant. 他贬低了她的成就，让她觉得自己微不足道。|synonyms: disparage, denigrate, minimize, underestimate, depreciate|antonyms: praise, commend, extol, magnify, overestimate|verb
