Her clothes were draped blowsily over the chair. 她的衣服凌乱地搭在椅子上。 | 近义词: untidily, sloppily, messily, dishevelledly, carelessly | 反义词: neatly, tidily, smartly, elegantly | 词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From blowsy + -ly. Blowsy itself comes from a combination of 'blowze' (a ruddy-faced woman, especially a peasant woman) and 'blow' (referring to wind dishevelling hair and clothing).
