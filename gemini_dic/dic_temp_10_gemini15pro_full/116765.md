mausolea:/ˌmɔːsəˈliːə/
n.
陵墓（mausoleum 的复数形式）
A large stately tomb or building housing tombs.  These are the grand mausolea of Roman emperors. 这些是罗马皇帝的宏伟陵墓。
The family's mausoleum is in the old cemetery.  这个家族的陵墓在古老的墓地里。
tombs, sepulchers, crypts

词源：From Latin *mausōlēum, from Ancient Greek Μαυσωλεῖον (Mausōleîon), from Μαύσωλος (Maúsōlos), a satrap in the Persian Empire.  The term originally referred specifically to the tomb of Mausolus, which was considered one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World.
