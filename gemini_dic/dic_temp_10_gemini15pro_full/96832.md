inestimableness:/ɪnˈɛstɪməblənəs/| n.| 无价, 极大的价值, 无法估量 |The inestimableness of her contribution to the project cannot be overstated. 她对这个项目的贡献无法估量。|The painting's inestimableness made it a prime target for thieves. 这幅画的巨大价值使它成为窃贼的主要目标。|近义词:pricelessness, invaluable, immeasurable worth|反义词:worthlessness, insignificance|词性:名词

词源:  From inestimable + -ness.  Inestimable itself comes from in- (not) + estimable (able to be estimated), ultimately from Latin aestimare (to estimate).
