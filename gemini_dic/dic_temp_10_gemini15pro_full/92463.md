hypostatical:/ˌhaɪpəˈstætɪkl/
adjective
本体的；位格的；神性位格的
relating to a hypostasis (an underlying substance or essential nature) or the union of the divine and human natures in Christ.
The hypostatical union of Christ's divine and human natures is a core doctrine of Christian theology.  基督神性和人性的位格合一是基督教神学的一个核心教义。
The Council of Chalcedon affirmed the hypostatical union of Christ.  迦克墩会议肯定了基督的位格合一。
synonyms: essential, substantial, constitutive
antonyms: insubstantial, immaterial, accidental
adjective
