denounces:/dɪˈnaʊnsɪz/
verb
谴责，公开指责
To publicly declare that something is wrong or evil.
The newspaper denounced the government's new policy. 这家报纸谴责了政府的新政策。
condemns, criticizes, castigates
supports, praises, commends
v.
