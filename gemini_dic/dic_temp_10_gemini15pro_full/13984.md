auricle: /ˈɔːrɪk(ə)l/ (US) /ˈɔːrɪkəl/ (UK)
n.
外耳; 心耳
The outer ear, also called the pinna or auricle, collects sound waves.  外耳，也称为耳廓或耳廓，收集声波。
The left auricle is a small, muscular pouch-like structure in the heart. 左心耳是心脏中一个小的、肌肉发达的袋状结构。
The surgeon repaired the damaged auricle.  外科医生修复了受损的耳廓。
Synonym: pinna, atrium (for heart auricle)
Antonym:  (No direct antonym for anatomical structures)
