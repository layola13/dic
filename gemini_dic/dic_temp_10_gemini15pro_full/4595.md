例句：The government increased taxes on alcopops to discourage underage drinking. 政府提高了酒精汽水的税收以阻止未成年人饮酒。|近义词：cooler, pre-mixed drink, ready-to-drink (RTD)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "hard liquor")|词性:noun
