emaculate:/ɪˈmækjʊlət/ | adj. | 完美的；洁净的；无瑕疵的 | Perfectly clean, neat, or tidy; free from flaws or errors. |  Her apartment was always immaculate. 她的公寓总是 spotless. | 近义词：spotless, pristine, flawless, impeccable | 反义词：dirty, messy, flawed, imperfect | 词性: 形容词

emaculate:/ɪˈmækjʊleɪt/ | v. |  使完美；使洁净；去除…的污点 | To remove flaws or errors from; to make perfect or spotless. | He spent hours trying to immaculate his work. 他花了数小时试图完善他的作品。 | 近义词：cleanse, purify, refine | 反义词：soil, stain, corrupt | 词性: 动词
