buckskin:/ˈbʌkskɪn/| n. adj.|鹿皮，鹿皮制的|A strong, soft leather made from the skin of a deer or similar animal, typically light yellowish-brown in color.  Also used as an adjective to describe something made of this leather.|He wore a buckskin jacket. 他穿着一件鹿皮夹克。|近义词：deerskin, suede|反义词: -(not applicable for materials)|noun, adjective
