conchifer:/ˈkɒŋkɪfər/| n.|贝壳动物，有壳动物|An animal that has a shell, especially a mollusk.|例句：Conchifers like clams and oysters are popular seafood.  蛤蜊和牡蛎等贝壳类动物是受欢迎的海鲜。

例句：The beach was littered with the shells of various conchifers. 海滩上散落着各种贝壳类动物的壳。|近义词：mollusk, shellfish|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider 'vertebrate' as a contrasting group)|词性:noun
conchiferous:/kɒŋˈkɪfərəs/| adj.|有壳的，具贝壳的|Bearing a shell, especially a shell of one piece as in gastropods and cephalopods.|例句：Conchiferous mollusks are a diverse group of animals. 有壳软体动物是动物的一个多样化群体。

例句：The scientist studied the conchiferous remains found in the ancient seabed.  科学家研究了在古代海床上发现的贝壳残骸。|近义词：shelled|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:adjective
