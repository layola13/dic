overfeed:/ˌoʊvərˈfiːd/ | vt.  n. | 过度喂养；喂得过多；过量喂食 | To give too much food to someone or something. |  The farmer overfed the chickens, and they became fat and lazy.  农民过度喂养鸡，它们变得又肥又懒。

The cat's overfeeding led to health problems. 这只猫的过度喂食导致了健康问题。 | 近义词：stuff, gorge, overeat | 反义词：starve, underfeed |词性:动词，名词
