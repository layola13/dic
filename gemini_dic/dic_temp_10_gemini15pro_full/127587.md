nectosack: /ˈnektəˌsæk/ | n. |  蜜囊，蜜胃 | A pouch in the foregut of some insects, such as bees, used for carrying nectar. |  The bee filled its nectosack with nectar from the flower. 蜜蜂用花蜜装满了它的蜜囊。 | synonym: honey sac, crop | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "digestive tract" in a broader sense) | noun
