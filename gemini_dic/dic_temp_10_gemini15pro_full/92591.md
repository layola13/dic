image:/ˈɪmɪdʒ/ (US), /ˈɪmɪdʒ/ (UK)| n. vt.|图像，形象，映像|A picture or representation of someone or something.|例句：The image on the screen was blurry. 屏幕上的图像很模糊。|近义词：picture, photograph, likeness, representation|反义词: N/A |词性:noun, verb


image:/ˈɪmɪdʒ/ (US), /ˈɪmɪdʒ/ (UK)| vt.|想象，反映|To form a mental picture of something or someone; to reflect.|例句：I can't image life without you. 我无法想象没有你的生活。|近义词：picture, visualize, represent, reflect|反义词: N/A |词性:verb
