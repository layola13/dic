alone: /əˈloʊn/ (US) /əˈləʊn/ (UK)| adj. adv.|单独的，独自的；只有，仅仅|Being without anyone else; only.|She lives alone. 她独自一人生活。|He alone knows the truth. 只有他知道真相。|近义词：solitary, lonely, by oneself|反义词: together, accompanied|词性:形容词, 副词|词源: From Old English *allāna (unattested), from all + āna (“one”). Cognate with Old Frisian allāne, Old Saxon alōno, Old High German al(l)eina (German allein), Old Norse all einn.
