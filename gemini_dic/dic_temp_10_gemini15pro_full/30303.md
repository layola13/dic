cenotaph: /ˈsenəˌtæf/ | n. |纪念碑；衣冠冢| A monument erected in honor of a person or group of people whose remains are elsewhere. |  A cenotaph was erected in the town square to commemorate the fallen soldiers.  在城镇广场上竖起了一座纪念碑，以纪念阵亡的士兵。 |  monument, memorial, shrine | tomb, grave | noun
