geminal:/ˈdʒɛmɪnəl/| adj.|孪生的；成对的；双键的|Identical atoms or groups attached to the same atom in a molecule.  Attached to the same atom or part.|例句：Geminal protons are protons attached to the same carbon atom.  孪生质子是连接到同一个碳原子上的质子。

例句：The geminal substitution pattern in this molecule is unique.  该分子中的孪生取代模式是独一无二的。|近义词：twin, paired, double|反义词：single, separate|词性:adjective
词源: From Latin *geminus* "twin, double," related to *gignere* "to beget."
