layabouts:/ˈleɪəbaʊts/
n.
懒汉，游手好闲的人
A person who habitually avoids work or exertion.
例句：The town was full of layabouts who spent their days drinking in the pub.  这个镇上到处都是懒汉，他们整天在酒吧里喝酒。

例句：He's such a layabout; he never lifts a finger to help around the house. 他真是个懒汉，从来不帮忙做家务。
近义词：idler, loafer, slacker, good-for-nothing
反义词：workaholic, busybody
词性：名词 (Noun)
