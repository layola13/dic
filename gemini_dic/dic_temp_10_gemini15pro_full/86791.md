hemadromometry:/ˌhiːmədrɒˈmɒmɪtri/ | n. |血流速度测定法|The measurement of the velocity of blood flow.  It involves techniques and instruments for measuring the speed of blood circulation in different parts of the circulatory system.|Hemadromometry is crucial for assessing cardiovascular health. 血流速度测定法对于评估心血管健康至关重要。

|Hemadromometry techniques are constantly evolving. 血流速度测定技术在不断发展。|Synonyms: blood flow measurement, blood velocity measurement|Antonyms: N/A|noun
