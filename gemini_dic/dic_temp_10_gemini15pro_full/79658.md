glossology:/ɡlɒˈsɒlədʒi/ | /ɡlɑːˈsɑːlədʒi/ | n. | 词汇学；语言学 | The study of language; the science of the origin, history, and development of words. | Glossology is a fascinating field for those interested in the evolution of language. 词汇学对于那些对语言演变感兴趣的人来说是一个迷人的领域。| synonyms: linguistics, philology | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "illiteracy" in a broad sense) | noun
