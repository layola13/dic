entrapping: /ɪnˈtræpɪŋ/
Verb
诱捕；使陷入圈套
Catching someone in a trap, either literally or figuratively.
The police set up a sting operation, entrapping the drug dealers. 警方设下圈套，诱捕了毒贩。
Synonyms: trapping, ensnaring, catching
Antonyms: freeing, releasing, liberating
v.
