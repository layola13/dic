noncoerciveness:/ˌnɒnkəʊˈɜːsɪvnəs/
n.
非强制性；非胁迫性
The quality or state of being noncoercive; the absence of coercion or force.
The agreement emphasized the noncoerciveness of the process.  该协议强调了这一过程的非强制性。
The survey highlighted the noncoerciveness of the voting procedures.  这项调查强调了投票程序的非强制性。
近义词： voluntariness, freedom, autonomy
反义词： coercion, compulsion, force
词性：名词

词源：
non- (prefix):  not, absence of  (来自拉丁语 non)
coercive (adjective): relating to or using force or threats. (来自拉丁语 coercere,  意为 "to restrain, confine")
-ness (suffix):  forms nouns indicating a state, quality, or condition.
