modernising:/ˈmɒdənaɪzɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdərnaɪzɪŋ/ (US)| v. | 使现代化 | The act of making something more modern; updating or improving something to meet contemporary standards. | The company is modernising its factory with new equipment.  这家公司正在用新设备使其工厂现代化。| synonyms: updating, upgrading, innovating, renovating, improving, reforming, overhauling | antonyms:  outdating,  antiquating, regressing | verb (present participle)

modernize:/ˈmɒdənaɪz/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdərnaɪz/ (US)| v. | 使现代化 | To make something more modern; to update or improve something to meet contemporary standards. | They plan to modernize the kitchen by installing new appliances.  他们计划通过安装新电器来使厨房现代化。| synonyms: update, upgrade, innovate, renovate, improve, reform, overhaul | antonyms: outdate, antiquate, regress | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From modern + -ize/-ise.  "Modern" comes from Late Latin "modernus,"  meaning "of the present time."
