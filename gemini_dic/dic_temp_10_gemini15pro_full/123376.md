monometallic:/ˌmɒnəməˈtælɪk/ | adj. |单金属的|Made of only one metal.| The catalyst used in the experiment was monometallic, composed solely of platinum.  该实验中使用的催化剂是单金属的，仅由铂组成。|Synonyms: single-metal, homometallic|Antonyms: bimetallic, polymetallic|adjective
