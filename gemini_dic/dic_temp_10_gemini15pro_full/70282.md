fiddly:/ˈfɪdli/| adj.|繁琐的，难弄的|Awkward or complicated to do or use because of many small parts or details.|例句：It was a fiddly job, separating the tiny seeds from the pulp. 这是一项繁琐的工作，要把小种子从果肉中分离出来。

例句：I hate these fiddly little clasps on my necklace. 我讨厌我项链上这些难弄的小扣子。|近义词：fussy, finicky, tricky, awkward, cumbersome|反义词：simple, easy, straightforward|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From fiddle (v.) + -y.  Originally meaning "playing the fiddle," it later evolved to mean "trifling, frivolous," and finally settled on its current meaning of "awkward to handle due to small parts."
