outflying:/ˌaʊtˈflaɪɪŋ/
verb
胜过飞行; 比...飞得远; 超越;
Surpassing in flight; flying farther or faster than.
The new plane outflew all its competitors.  这架新飞机的飞行速度超过了所有竞争对手。
The bird outflew the storm. 这只鸟飞过了风暴。
近义词: outstrip, surpass, exceed
反义词: lag behind, trail
词性: v.
