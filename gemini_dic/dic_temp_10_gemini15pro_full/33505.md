cicadas:/sɪˈkeɪdəz/ /sɪˈkɑːdəz/| n. |蝉|Large insects known for their loud, buzzing songs, especially during the summer.  They spend most of their lives underground as nymphs before emerging as adults to reproduce.| The cicadas were buzzing loudly in the trees. 树上的蝉鸣声很大。|  Cicadas emerged from the ground after 17 years.  十七年后，蝉从地下钻了出来。 |synonyms:  locusts (sometimes confused, but not the same) |antonyms: N/A |noun
