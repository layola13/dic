hydromagnesite:/ˌhaɪdrəʊˈmæɡnɪsaɪt/ | n. | 水菱镁矿 | A hydrated magnesium carbonate mineral, typically occurring as white, chalky masses. It is often found in association with other magnesium minerals such as brucite and serpentine. | Hydromagnesite deposits were discovered in the caves. 在洞穴里发现了水菱镁矿矿床。|Synonym:  hydrated magnesium carbonate| Antonym: None (Minerals typically don't have direct opposites.) | noun
