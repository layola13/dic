nonincrease:/ˌnɒnɪnˈkriːs/| n.|不增加，无增长|Absence of increase; the state of not increasing.|The graph showed a period of nonincrease in sales.|近义词：stagnation, plateau, standstill|反义词:increase, rise, growth|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + increase.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "lack of," derived from Latin. "Increase" comes from the Old French "encreiss-" and ultimately the Latin "increscere," meaning "to grow."
