overexpansively: /ˌoʊvərɪkˈspænsɪvli/
adverb
过度扩张地，过分扩展地
in a way that expands or spreads too much
The company overexpansively opened too many new branches in a short period and ultimately faced financial difficulties.  这家公司在短时间内过度扩张，开设了太多新分店，最终面临财务困难。
The author overexpansively detailed the character's backstory, bogging down the narrative. 作者过度扩展地描述了角色的背景故事，拖累了叙事节奏。
近义词: excessively, immoderately, too much
反义词: moderately, cautiously, restrainedly
词性: adverb

词源:
From "over-" (meaning "excessively") + "expansive" (meaning "tending to expand") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Expansive" ultimately derives from the Latin "expandere" (to spread out).
