affable:/ˈæfəbl/| adj.|和蔼可亲的；友善的；容易交谈的|Friendly and easy to talk to.|He struck me as an affable sort of a man. 他给我的印象是一个和蔼可亲的人。|近义词：amicable, genial, cordial, approachable|反义词：unfriendly, aloof, hostile, disagreeable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin affābilis (“easy to speak to”), from ad- + fārī (“to speak”).
