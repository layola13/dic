lichenist:/ˈlaɪkənɪst/ | n. | 地衣学家 | A person who studies lichens. | Example 1: The lichenist discovered a new species of lichen in the rainforest.  这位地衣学家在雨林中发现了一种新的地衣。

Example 2:  The lichenist carefully examined the lichen samples under the microscope. 这位地衣学家在显微镜下仔细检查了地衣样本。 | Synonym:  lichenologist | Antonym: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From lichen + -ist.  "lichen" comes from Greek  "leikhēn"  meaning "to lick," referring to the appearance of some lichens as if they are licking rocks. "-ist" is a suffix denoting a person who studies or practices something.
