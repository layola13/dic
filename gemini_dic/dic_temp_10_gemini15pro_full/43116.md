The coxswaining of the eight-man crew was crucial to their victory.  八人赛艇的舵手工作对他们的胜利至关重要。 | 近义词：steering, piloting, guiding, directing | 反义词: None | 词性：名词

coxswaining:/ˈkɒksweɪnɪŋ/ /ˈkɑːksweɪnɪŋ/| v. | 担任舵手；指导；指挥 | To act as a coxswain; to guide or direct a group. |  She coxswained the university rowing team to victory. 她担任大学赛艇队的舵手，带领队伍取得了胜利。
