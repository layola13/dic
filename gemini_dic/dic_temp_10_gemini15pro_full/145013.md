overadorns:/ˌoʊvərəˈdɔːrnz/| v. |过度装饰，过分修饰|To adorn excessively; to decorate too much. | She overadorned the Christmas tree with so many ornaments that the branches were bending. 她用太多的装饰品过度装饰了圣诞树，树枝都弯了。| The wedding cake was so overadorned it looked gaudy. 婚礼蛋糕装饰得太过华丽，看起来很俗气。|近义词：overdecorate, embellish excessively|反义词: underdecorate, simplify|词性:动词

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *ubar "over, above") + "adorn" (from Old French adorner, from a- "to" + torner "to turn," from Latin tornare "to turn in a lathe," ultimately from PIE *ter- "to rub, turn.")
