disassemblers:/ˌdɪsəˈsɛmblərz/ | n. | 反汇编器 | A program that translates machine language into assembly language.  It allows programmers to analyze the inner workings of a program. |  The disassembler helped me understand how the malware functioned.  反汇编器帮助我理解了恶意软件是如何工作的。 |  The disassembler revealed the hidden code within the executable. 反汇编器揭示了可执行文件中的隐藏代码。 | synonyms: decompiler, reverse assembler | antonyms: assembler, compiler | noun

disassembler:/ˌdɪsəˈsɛmblər/ | n. | 反汇编程序 | A person who disassembles something, particularly mechanical or electronic devices. | The disassembler carefully took apart the engine. 反汇编程序小心地拆开了引擎。 |  The disassembler meticulously documented each step of the process. 反汇编程序一丝不苟地记录了过程的每一步。 | synonyms: deconstructor, dismantler | antonyms: assembler, constructor | noun
