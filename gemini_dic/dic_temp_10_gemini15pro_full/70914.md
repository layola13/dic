fireback:/ˈfaɪərbæk/| n.|后挡板，壁炉挡板|A fireback is a plate of metal, often cast iron, placed at the back of a fireplace to protect the masonry and reflect heat into the room.|The antique fireback depicted a hunting scene.|Synonym: backplate, fireplate|Antonym: (No direct antonym)|Noun
