europeanises:/ˌjʊərəˈpiːənaɪzɪz/
verb
使欧洲化
To cause something to become more European in character or style.
The new regulations are gradually europeanising the industry. 新规定正在逐渐使该行业欧洲化。
synonyms: westernize, modernize
antonyms: localize, nationalize
词性: 动词


europeanises:/ˌjʊərəˈpiːənaɪzɪz/
verb
欧洲化
To adopt European customs, practices, or styles.
Many countries in Eastern Europe have europeanized since the fall of the Soviet Union. 自苏联解体以来，许多东欧国家已经欧洲化了。
synonyms: occidentalize, globalize
antonyms: isolate, segregate
词性: 动词
