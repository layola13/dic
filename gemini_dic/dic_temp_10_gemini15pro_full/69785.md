fermer:/fɛʁ.me/ | v. | to close, to shut, to farm | to close a door, window, etc.; to enclose land; to operate a farm | Ex:  Il faut fermer la fenêtre. (It's necessary to close the window.) | Synonym:  fermer à clé (to lock), boucher (to plug), clôturer (to enclose) | Antonym: ouvrir (to open) | Verb

fermier:/fɛʁ.mje/ | n. | farmer | a person who cultivates land | Ex: Le fermier cultive du blé. (The farmer cultivates wheat.) | Synonym: agriculteur (agriculturalist), cultivateur (cultivator) | Antonym: - | Noun

fermière:/fɛʁ.mjɛʁ/ | n. | farmer (female) | a woman who cultivates land | Ex: La fermière élève des poules. (The farmer raises chickens.) | Synonym: agricultrice | Antonym: - | Noun

fermière:/fɛʁ.mjɛʁ/ | adj. | farming, agricultural | related to farming | Ex: Une coopérative fermière (A farming cooperative) | Synonym: agricole | Antonym: - | Adjective


*Note:  The word "fermerere" is not a standard French word.  It likely represents a misspelling of either "fermer," "fermier," or "fermière."*
