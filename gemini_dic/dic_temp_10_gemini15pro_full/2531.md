admiralissimo:/ˌædmərəˈlɪsɪmoʊ/
noun
最高统帅，海军元帅
The supreme commander of a combined fleet or of all naval forces of a country.  A rank higher than admiral.
Historically, the Duke of Medina Sidonia was appointed admiralissimo of the Spanish Armada. 西班牙无敌舰队的总司令历史上由梅迪纳西多尼亚公爵担任。

The admiralissimo reviewed the fleet before the decisive battle. 最高统帅在决战前检阅了舰队。

近义词:commander-in-chief, supreme commander
反义词:subordinate, inferior
词性:n.
