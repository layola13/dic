nontoxicities:/ˌnɒntɒkˈsɪsɪtiz/
n.
无毒性；无毒状态
The absence of toxic properties or harmful effects.
The researchers are exploring the nontoxicities of various natural compounds. 研究人员正在探索各种天然化合物的无毒性。
近义词:harmlessness, safety, innocuousness
反义词:toxicity, poisonousness
词性:名词

nontoxic:/ˌnɒnˈtɒksɪk/
adj.
无毒的
Not poisonous or toxic; harmless.
This cleaning product is nontoxic and safe for use around children. 这种清洁产品是无毒的，可以安全地用于儿童周围。
近义词:harmless, safe, innocuous
反义词:toxic, poisonous
词性:形容词

toxic:/ˈtɒksɪk/
adj.
有毒的
Poisonous; harmful.
Exposure to these toxic chemicals can cause serious health problems. 接触这些有毒化学物质会导致严重的健康问题。
近义词:poisonous, harmful, venomous
反义词:nontoxic, harmless, safe
词性:形容词
