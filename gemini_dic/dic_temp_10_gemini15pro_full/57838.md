dressiest:/ˈdrɛsiɪst/
adjective
最衣着光鲜的；最正式的
(superlative of 'dressy') Most suitable for formal occasions; most elegant or elaborate in style.
She wore her dressiest gown to the gala. 她穿着她最华丽的礼服去参加晚会。
Near Synonyms:  most formal, most elegant, fanciest, smartest
Antonyms:  most casual, scruffiest, plainest
Word Origin: From 'dressy', ultimately from 'dress' + '-y' (suffix forming adjectives).
