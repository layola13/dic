cowpoke:/ˈkaʊpoʊk/| n.|牛仔；牧童|A cowpoke is a person, typically a hired hand, who tends cattle, especially in the western United States.|The old cowpoke reminisced about his days on the open range.  这位老牛仔回忆起他在开阔牧场上的日子。|Synonyms: cowboy, cowhand, rancher, herdsman|Antonyms: city-dweller|noun
