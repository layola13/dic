durability:/ˌdjʊərəˈbɪləti/ /ˌdʊrəˈbɪləti/| n. |耐久性，耐用性|The ability of something to withstand wear, pressure, or damage over a long period; lasting quality.|This furniture is known for its durability. 这款家具以其耐用性而闻名。|近义词：sturdiness, strength, resilience, toughness|反义词:fragility, weakness, impermanence|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dūrābilitās, from Latin dūrābilis (“lasting”), from dūrō (“harden, endure”), from dūrus (“hard”).
