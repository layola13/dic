dubb:/dʌb/| vt.| 授予称号；配音；轻点；涂抹；打击 | to give a name or nickname to someone or something; to provide a film or broadcast with a soundtrack in a different language from the original; to touch lightly; to spread a thin layer of something on a surface; to strike lightly | He was dubbed "The King" by his fans.  他的粉丝称他为“国王”。 | synonyms: name, nickname, entitle, call, label, christen, designate, style, term, denominate; voice, redub, translate, subtitle, interpret; tap, pat, dab; smear, spread, coat; hit, strike, knock | antonyms: strip (of a title), mute (for sound) | verb
