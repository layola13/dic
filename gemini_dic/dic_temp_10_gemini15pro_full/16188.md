bankrupting:/ˈbæŋkrʌptɪŋ/
adjective
使人破产的；导致破产的
Causing someone to become bankrupt; financially ruinous.
The bankrupting medical bills forced them to sell their house.  巨额医疗费用使他们不得不卖掉房子。
近义词:ruinous, crippling, devastating
反义词: enriching, profitable
词性: adjective


bankrupting:/ˈbæŋkrʌptɪŋ/
verb
使破产
To make someone bankrupt.
The lawsuit threatened to bankrupting the small business. 这场官司有可能使这家小企业破产。
近义词: ruining, impoverishing
反义词: enriching, prospering
词性: verb (present participle)
