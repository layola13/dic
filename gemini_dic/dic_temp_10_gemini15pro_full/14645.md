avoucher:/əˈvaʊtʃər/ | n. | 代金券；凭证；收据 | A document that serves as proof of a financial transaction, entitlement, or agreement.  It can be used to redeem goods or services or as evidence of a payment. |  I have a gift avoucher for a free meal at the restaurant. 我有一张这家餐厅的免费餐券。

She presented an avoucher for her travel expenses. 她提交了旅行费用的收据。|  Synonym: coupon, voucher, receipt, certificate, warrant | Antonym:  debt, bill | noun
