madhouses:/ˈmædˌhaʊzɪz/| n. |精神病院，疯人院；混乱的场所|A place for the care of people affected by mental illness (often formerly). Also, a place or scene of uproar and confusion.|例句：The old hospital was once considered a madhouse. 这家老医院曾被认为是疯人院。

例句：The stock exchange floor was a madhouse during the market crash. 股市崩盘期间，交易大厅一片混乱。|近义词：asylum, bedlam, chaos, pandemonium|反义词：order, sanity, peace|noun
