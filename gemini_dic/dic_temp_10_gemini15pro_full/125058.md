muli:/ˈmuːli/| n.|骡子|A hybrid offspring of a male donkey (jack) and a female horse (mare).|例句：The farmer used a muli to carry heavy loads. 农民用骡子驮重物。

例句：Mulis are known for their strength and endurance. 骡子以力气大和耐力强而闻名。|近义词：hybrid, crossbreed|反义词: None (Mules are a specific hybrid, not a category with an opposite)|词性:noun
