offsider:/ˈɒfˌsaɪdə(r)/ /ˈɔːfˌsaɪdər/
noun
局外人；旁观者；助手；帮手
A person who is not a member of a particular group or team, especially in politics or business; an assistant or helper.
The prime minister relies on a small team of offsiders for political advice. 首相依靠一小群局外人的政治建议。
He works as an offsider to the head chef. 他是主厨的助手。
Synonyms: assistant, helper, aide, deputy, subordinate, right-hand man/woman, second in command
Antonyms: leader, boss, chief, superior, insider
词性: 名词
