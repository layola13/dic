financiered:/fɪˈnænsɪərd/
verb
提供资金
provided financial support for
The museum's new wing was financed by a wealthy benefactor.  博物馆的新侧楼是由一位富有的捐助者资助的。

The project was financed through a combination of private and public funds. 这个项目是由私人和公共资金共同资助的。

近义词:funded, bankrolled, capitalized, subsidized
反义词:defunded

词性:v.
