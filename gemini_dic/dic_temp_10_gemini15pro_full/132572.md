nondidactically:/ˌnɒndaɪˈdæktɪkli/
adverb
非教条式地，非说教式地
in a way that is not intended to teach a lesson or moral
She explained the complex theory nondidactically, using simple language and relatable examples. 她用简单的语言和相关的例子非教条式地解释了这个复杂的理论。
Synonyms:informally, casually, conversationally
Antonyms:didactically, pedagogically
