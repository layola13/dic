habeas:/ˈheɪbiəs/| n.|人身保护令|A writ requiring a person under arrest to be brought before a judge or into court, especially to secure the person's release unless lawful grounds are shown for their detention.  A procedure that allows a prisoner to challenge the legality of their detention.|例句：The lawyer filed a writ of habeas corpus to challenge his client's imprisonment. 律师提交了人身保护令状，对委托人的监禁提出质疑。

例句：She sought habeas corpus, claiming her detention was unlawful. 她申请人身保护令，声称对她的拘留是非法的。|近义词：writ,  mandamus (in some cases)|反义词: detention, imprisonment|词性:noun
