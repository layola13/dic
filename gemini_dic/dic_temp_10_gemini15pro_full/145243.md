overblamed:/ˌoʊvərˈbleɪmd/
adjective
过度责备的
Having been blamed excessively or unfairly.
He felt overblamed for the project's failure, as he wasn't the only one responsible. 他觉得对这个项目的失败负有过度责备，因为他不是唯一负责任的人。
The child was overblamed for the broken vase, as it turned out the dog had knocked it over.  这个孩子因为打破花瓶而被过度责备，结果发现是狗把它撞倒了。
近义词:overcriticized, unfairly blamed
反义词:underblamed, excused, exonerated
词性:adjective

词源:
"Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "blamed" (past participle of "blame")
