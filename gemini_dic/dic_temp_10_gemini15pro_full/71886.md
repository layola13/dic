fleuron:/ˈflʊərɒn/ (US) /ˈflɜːrɒn/ (UK)| n.|花饰；装饰；精华；精英|An ornament resembling a stylized flower or leaf, used in architectural decoration or on book covers, etc.  A distinguishing characteristic or achievement.|The fleurons adorning the gates were intricately carved.  大门上的花饰雕刻精美。|Synonyms: ornament, decoration, embellishment, adornment, motif|Antonyms: plainness, simplicity|noun
