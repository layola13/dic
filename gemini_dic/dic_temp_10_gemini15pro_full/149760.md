palatially:/pəˈleɪʃəli/ | adv. | 宫殿般地，华丽地，奢华地 | In a manner resembling a palace; magnificently, luxuriously. |  He lived palatially in a huge mansion with sprawling grounds.  他住在一座带有广阔庭院的巨大宅邸里，过着宫殿般的生活。

| The banquet was prepared palatially, with an abundance of delicacies.  宴会准备得非常奢华，有丰富的美味佳肴。 | 近义词：luxuriously, grandly, splendidly, opulently | 反义词：humbly, modestly, plainly, simply | 词性：副词

palatial:/pəˈleɪʃl/ | adj. | 宫殿般的，宏伟的，富丽堂皇的 | Resembling a palace in magnificence or spaciousness; grand, luxurious. | The palatial estate boasted numerous fountains and meticulously manicured gardens.  这座宫殿般的庄园拥有众多喷泉和精心修剪的花园。

|  They stayed in a palatial hotel suite overlooking the ocean.  他们住在一个可以俯瞰大海的富丽堂皇的酒店套房里。 | 近义词：grand, magnificent, splendid, luxurious | 反义词：humble, modest, simple, plain | 词性：形容词


词源:
From Late Latin *palatialis, from Latin palatium (“palace”) + -alis. Ultimately from the Palatine Hill in Rome, where emperors had their residences.
