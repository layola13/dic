durabilities:/ˌdjʊərəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |耐久性，耐用性|The plural form of durability, referring to the ability of multiple items or systems to withstand wear, pressure, or damage over time.  It emphasizes the lasting quality or long-lasting nature of several things.|The car manufacturer boasted about the durabilities of its new line of trucks, highlighting their reinforced frames and robust engines. 汽车制造商吹嘘其新卡车系列的耐用性，强调其加固的车架和强劲的发动机。

The durabilities of different building materials are crucial factors to consider when designing a skyscraper. 不同建筑材料的耐久性是设计摩天大楼时需要考虑的关键因素。|近义词：strength, resilience, toughness, longevity|反义词:fragility, weakness, vulnerability|词性:名词
