outwave:/ˈaʊtweɪv/
n.
外行波，输出波
A wave that travels outward from a source.
The antenna emitted an outwave that interfered with nearby electronics. 天线发出一个外行波，干扰了附近的电子设备。

The earthquake generated an outwave that caused significant damage. 地震产生的外行波造成了严重的破坏。

近义词:outgoing wave
反义词:inwave

词性:名词
