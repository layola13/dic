nonlineal:/ˌnɑːnˈlɪniər/ | adj. | 非线性的 | Not linear; not arranged in a straight line.  Relating to or denoting a system whose output is not directly proportional to its input. | The graph shows a nonlineal relationship between the two variables.  这张图显示了两个变量之间的非线性关系。|  The growth of the bacteria was nonlineal.  细菌的生长是非线性的。|  nonlinear, curvilinear | linear, straight | adjective
