inconsolable:/ˌɪnkənˈsoʊləbl/ | adj. | 无法安慰的，悲痛欲绝的 | Not able to be comforted.  Unable to be soothed or alleviated. |例句：She was inconsolable after the death of her son. 她儿子去世后悲痛欲绝。

例句：He was inconsolable after his team lost the championship. 他的球队输掉了冠军后，他伤心欲绝。

近义词：heartbroken, devastated, grief-stricken, disconsolate | 反义词：comforted, cheerful, happy | 词性: adjective
