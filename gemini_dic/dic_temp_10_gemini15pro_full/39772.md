consolingly:/kənˈsōlɪŋli/| adv.|安慰地，抚慰地|In a way that offers comfort or solace.  |She spoke to the grieving child consolingly. 她安慰地对悲伤的孩子讲话。|例句：He patted her hand consolingly. 他安慰地拍了拍她的手。|近义词：comfortingly, soothingly, reassuringly|反义词：harshly, cruelly, unsympathetically|词性:副词
