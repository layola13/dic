mellifluence:/məˈlɪfluəns/| n.|流畅；甜美；娓娓动听|A smooth, sweet flow of language or sound.|Her mellifluence captivated the audience. 她的娓娓动听迷住了观众。|近义词：eloquence, fluency, smoothness, sweetness|反义词：harshness, dissonance, cacophony|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *mellifluus* ("flowing with honey"), from *melli-* ("of honey") + *fluus* ("flowing").  The Latin *melli-* is related to *mel* ("honey"), from Proto-Indo-European *\*mélit\* ("honey"). The Latin *fluus* is related to *fluere* ("to flow"), from Proto-Indo-European *\*bʰlew-/* ("to blow, swell").
