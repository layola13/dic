cobbler:/ˈkɑːblər/ (US) /ˈkɒblər/ (UK)| n.|补鞋匠；水果馅饼；含酒精混合饮料|A person who mends shoes; a deep-dish fruit dessert covered with a thick crust; a cocktail made with fruit, sugar, and a spirit such as whiskey or brandy.|例句：The cobbler repaired my worn-out boots. 补鞋匠修好了我破旧的靴子。

例句：My grandmother makes a delicious peach cobbler. 我祖母做了一个美味的桃子馅饼。|近义词：(for person) shoemaker, shoe repairer; (for dessert) crisp, crumble, pie|反义词: (No direct antonym for either meaning)|词性:noun


cobbler:/ˈkɑːblər/ (US) /ˈkɒblər/ (UK)| n.|修补匠|Someone who repairs things, especially shoes. |例句：The cobbler skillfully stitched the torn leather. 这位修补匠熟练地缝合了撕裂的皮革。

例句：He took his worn shoes to the cobbler. 他把破鞋拿到补鞋匠那里去。|近义词：mender, repairer, tinker|反义词:None|词性:noun
