diagraphic:/ˌdaɪəˈɡræfɪk/| adj.|双字母的，由双字母构成的|Relating to or consisting of a digraph (a combination of two letters representing one sound).|The "sh" in "ship" is a digraphic combination.|近义词:digraphical|反义词:monographic|词性:形容词

digraph:/ˈdaɪɡræf/| n.|双字母，二合字母|A combination of two letters representing one sound, as in "ph" or "ea".|The word "head" contains the digraph "ea".|近义词:digram, ligature|反义词:monograph|词性:名词
