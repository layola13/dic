macrocosmically:/ˌmækroʊˈkɒzmɪkli/| adv.|宏观地，从宇宙角度|In a macrocosmic view, individual human lives are but fleeting moments. 从宇宙的角度来看，个人的生命不过是转瞬即逝的时刻。|例句：Considering the issue macrocosmically, the impact of this event is negligible. 从宏观角度来看，这次事件的影响微不足道。

例句：Macrocosmically speaking, Earth is a tiny speck in the vastness of space. 从宇宙角度来说，地球在浩瀚的宇宙中只是一个微小的斑点。

近义词：universally, cosmically, globally|反义词:microscopically, locally|词性:副词
