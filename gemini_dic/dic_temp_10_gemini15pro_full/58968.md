dutcher:/ˈdʌtʃər/ | n. | 捕野鸭的人；诱捕野鸭的装置 | A person who hunts or traps wild ducks; a device used for trapping wild ducks. |  The old dutcher set his decoys in the marsh. 老捕鸭者在沼泽地里设置了他的诱饵。 | 近义词: duck hunter, fowler | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps game warden - someone who protects ducks) | 词性: noun

dutcher:/ˈdʌtʃər/ | n. | 荷兰人（尤指来自宾夕法尼亚州的荷兰裔美国人） (chiefly US dialect, Pennsylvania Dutch) | A person of Dutch descent, especially a Pennsylvania Dutch person. |  His family were dutchers who settled in Pennsylvania generations ago. 他的家族是几代以前定居在宾夕法尼亚州的荷兰人。| 近义词: Pennsylvanian Dutchman/Dutchwoman | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
