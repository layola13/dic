hedges:/ˈhedʒɪz/| n.|树篱，障碍物，防备措施，限制|A row of bushes or small trees planted close together, especially along a border or to form a fence or screen.  Figuratively, it refers to strategies or actions designed to reduce risk or loss.|The garden is surrounded by tall hedges. 花园被高高的树篱包围着。|近义词：fence, barrier, safeguard, precaution|反义词:opening, vulnerability|词性:noun
hedges:/ˈhedʒɪz/| v.|用树篱围住，限制，避免做出明确的承诺|To enclose with a hedge. Figuratively, it means to avoid giving a direct answer or committing oneself.|He hedged his bets by investing in a variety of stocks. 他通过投资各种股票来规避风险。|近义词：evade, avoid, qualify, limit|反义词:commit, confront|词性:verb
