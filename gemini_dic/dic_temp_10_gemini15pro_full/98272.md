inservient:/ɪnˈsɜːrviənt/
adjective
屈从的; 奴性的; 有用的; 有助于…的
submissive; subservient; useful; conducive to
例句：Her work was entirely inservient to his. 她的工作完全从属于他的工作。

例句：Technology should be inservient to human needs. 科技应该服务于人类的需求。

近义词：submissive, subservient, subordinate, compliant, obedient, docile, yielding, accommodating, helpful, useful, conducive, contributory, instrumental
反义词：dominant, controlling, assertive, independent

词性：adjective
