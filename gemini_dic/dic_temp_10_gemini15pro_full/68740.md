farmhand:/ˈfɑːrmhænd/ | n. |农场工人，农业工人 | A person hired to do manual labor on a farm. |  He worked as a farmhand for many years before saving enough money to buy his own land. 他做了多年的农场工人，才攒够钱买自己的地。

| Synonyms: agricultural worker, farm laborer, ranch hand | Antonyms:  owner, manager, supervisor | noun
