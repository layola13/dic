durian:/ˈdʊəriən/ /ˈdjʊəriən/| n. |榴莲|A large, oval, spiny tropical fruit containing a creamy, pungent pulp.|例句：The durian is known for its strong smell. 榴莲以其强烈的气味而闻名。
