concussion: /kənˈkʌʃən/ | n. |脑震荡；震动；强烈冲击|A concussion is a temporary injury to the brain caused by a blow to the head or a violent shaking of the head and body. |例句：He suffered a concussion after falling off his bike. 他从自行车上摔下来后脑震荡。

例句：The explosion caused a concussion that shattered nearby windows. 爆炸引起的震动震碎了附近的窗户。|近义词：injury, trauma, shock|反义词: healing, recovery |词性: noun
