multi-national:/ˌmʌltiˈnæʃənəl/ | adj. n. | 跨国的，多国的；跨国公司 | Operating in several countries. |  Example:  She works for a multi-national corporation. 她为一家跨国公司工作。

multi-national:/ˌmʌltiˈnæʃənəl/ | adj. n. | 跨国的，多国的；跨国公司 | Composed of many nationalities. | Example: The conference attracted a multi-national audience.  这次会议吸引了来自多个国家的观众。

multi-national:/ˌmʌltiˈnæʃənəl/ | n. | 跨国公司 | A company operating in several countries. | Example: Many multi-nationals have their headquarters in this city. 许多跨国公司总部设在这个城市。

multi-national:/ˌmʌltiˈnæʃənəl/ | n. | 跨国公司 | A person of more than one nationality. | Example:  He considers himself a multi-national, having lived in several different countries. 他认为自己是一个跨国人士，在几个不同的国家生活过。


Etymology->
From multi- + national. "Multi" comes from Latin "multus" meaning "much, many". "National" comes from Late Middle English "nacional", from Old French, and ultimately from Latin "natio" meaning "birth, race, nation."

Synonyms->
international, global, transnational, cosmopolitan

Antonyms->
domestic, local, national

Word Type->
Adjective, Noun
