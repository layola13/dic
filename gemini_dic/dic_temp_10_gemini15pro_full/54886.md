displosive:/dɪˈspləʊsɪv/ /dɪˈsploʊzɪv/
adjective|爆破的，爆发的|Relating to or characterized by an explosion; produced by an explosion.  A consonant sound produced by stopping the flow of air from the lungs and then suddenly releasing it.|The displosive sound of the fireworks startled the dog.|近义词：explosive, eruptive|反义词：implosive|词性:adjective
displosive:/dɪˈspləʊsɪv/ /dɪˈsploʊzɪv/
noun|爆破音|A consonant sound produced by stopping the flow of air from the lungs and then suddenly releasing it, such as 'p', 't', 'k', 'b', 'd', and 'g'.|The letter 't' is a displosive.|近义词：plosive, stop|反义词：fricative|词性:noun
