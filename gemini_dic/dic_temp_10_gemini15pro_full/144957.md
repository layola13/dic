overabundant:/ˌoʊvərəˈbʌndənt/| adj.|过剩的，过多的|Existing or available in much greater quantities than is needed or wanted; excessively abundant. |The farmers had an overabundant harvest this year. 今年农民的收成过多。|Synonyms: excessive, superfluous, profuse, plethora|Antonyms: scarce, deficient, meager, insufficient|adjective
