antitypical: /ˌæntiˈtɪpɪkəl/
adjective
非典型的，反典型的
Deviating from or opposite to a type; not typical.  Something that is antitypical does not conform to the expected characteristics of its type.
Her quiet demeanor was antitypical of her normally boisterous personality. 她安静的举止和她通常喧闹的性格截然不同。
The warm winter was antitypical of the region's usual frigid temperatures.  温暖的冬天与该地区通常的寒冷气温不同。
近义词: atypical, unusual, irregular, abnormal, deviant
反义词: typical, usual, normal, regular, standard
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + typical.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposite of," and "typical" refers to conforming to a type.
