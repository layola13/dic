escribed:/ɪˈskraɪbd/ | adj. | 外接的，旁切的 |  Relating to a circle tangent to one side of a triangle and to the extensions of the other two sides.  A triangle has three escribed circles. |  The escribed circle of the triangle touched the extended sides.  这个三角形的外接圆与延长边相切。| Example 2: The radius of the escribed circle opposite to angle A is denoted by ra.  与角A相对的外接圆的半径用ra表示。 | synonyms:  excircle,  | antonyms:  inscribed | adjective
