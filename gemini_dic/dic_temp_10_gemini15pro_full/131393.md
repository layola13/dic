noncivilians:/ˌnɒn.sɪˈvɪl.i.ənz/
noun
平民以外的人; 軍人;
people who are not civilians, especially members of the armed forces
例句：The attack killed several noncivilians. 这次袭击杀害了几名军人。

例句：The government restricted the movement of noncivilians in the area. 政府限制了该地区非平民的行动。

近义词：military personnel, soldiers, troops, armed forces
反义词：civilians
词性：noun
