counterprotests:/ˈkaʊntərˌprəʊtests/ (US) /ˈkaʊntərˌproʊtests/ (UK)| n. |抗议，反示威|A demonstration in opposition to another protest.  It expresses disagreement with the message or demands of the initial protest.|Thousands of people joined the counterprotests, voicing their support for the government's policies. 数千人加入了反示威，表达他们对政府政策的支持。

The counterprotests grew larger than the original demonstration. 反示威的规模超过了最初的示威活动。|近义词：demonstrations, rallies, protests|反义词: support, agreement |词性: 名词
