masoret:/mɑːˈsɔːrɛt/ /mæˈsɔːrɛt/| n.|马索拉文本，马索拉学|The Masoret refers to the system of vowel points and accent marks added to the Hebrew consonantal text of the Bible by Jewish scholars (Masoretes) in the Middle Ages.|The Leningrad Codex is a prominent example of the Masoret.|近义词: Masoretic Text, Masorah|反义词: (No direct antonym.  One could potentially consider pre-Masoretic texts as a contrasting concept, but not a true opposite.)|词性:noun
