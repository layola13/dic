insubstantiality:/ˌɪnsəbˌstænʃiˈælɪti/
n.|无实体; 非物质性; 虚无;|Lack of substance; immateriality.  The state of lacking firm basis in reality; unsubstantial nature.|The insubstantiality of the evidence made it difficult to prove the case.  证据的虚无缥缈使得很难证明这个案子。|intangibility, immateriality, ethereality, flimsiness|substantiality, concreteness, solidity|noun
