ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *osculatus*, past participle of *osculari*  "to kiss," from *osculum*  "little mouth," diminutive of *os* "mouth."

osculating circle (密切圆): The osculating circle is the circle that best approximates a curve at a given point. 密切圆是在给定点最接近曲线的圆。
osculating plane (密切平面): The osculating plane is the plane that contains the osculating circle. 密切平面是包含密切圆的平面。
osculating orbit (密切轨道): An osculating orbit is an instantaneous Keplerian orbit used to describe the motion of a perturbed object. 密切轨道是用来描述受扰动物体运动的瞬时开普勒轨道。
