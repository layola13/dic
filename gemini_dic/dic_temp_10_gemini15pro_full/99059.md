例句：Strong interassociations within the community fostered a sense of belonging. 社区内部紧密的联系促进了归属感。|近义词：interconnections, interrelationships, links|反义词: divisions, separations|词性:名词

Etymology:
From inter- + association.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," derived from Latin. "Association" comes from the Late Latin "associatio," meaning "union," from the verb "associare," meaning "to join with."
