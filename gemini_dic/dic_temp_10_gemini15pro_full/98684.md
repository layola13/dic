insultable:/ɪnˈsʌltəbl/| adj.|可侮辱的，容易被冒犯的|Able to be insulted; vulnerable to insult.  Someone who is easily offended is insultable.|He was so sensitive about his weight that he was practically insultable.| 近义词:sensitive, touchy, vulnerable, susceptible|反义词:thick-skinned, insensitive, imperturbable|词性:形容词
词源: From insult + -able.  "Insult" comes from Latin "insultare" meaning "to jump or leap upon, to assail, to insult."
