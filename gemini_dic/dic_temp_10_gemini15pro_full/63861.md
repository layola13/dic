epigraphical:/ˌɛpɪˈɡræfɪkəl/
adj.
题词的，碑文的
Of, relating to, or consisting of an epigraph; inscribed on a monument.
The book began with an epigraphical quotation from Shakespeare. 这本书以莎士比亚的一句题词作为开头。
Synonyms:  inscribed, monumental, commemorative
Antonyms:  unmarked, blank
词性: 形容词
