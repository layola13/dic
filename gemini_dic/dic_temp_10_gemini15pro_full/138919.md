nutrition:/njuˈtrɪʃn/ | n. | 营养，营养学 | The process by which living organisms obtain and utilize the nutrients required for growth, maintenance, and repair of tissues. |  Good nutrition is essential for good health. 良好的营养对健康至关重要。

A balanced diet provides all the necessary nutrition. 均衡的饮食提供所有必要的营养。 | synonyms: nourishment, sustenance, aliment | antonyms: malnutrition, starvation | noun
