assuaging:/əˈsweɪdʒɪŋ/
|v.|减轻，缓和|making (an unpleasant feeling) less intense.|The government has tried to assuage public fears about the virus. 政府试图减轻公众对病毒的恐惧。|Synonyms: relieve, ease, alleviate, mitigate, soothe, mollify, allay|Antonyms: aggravate, exacerbate, intensify|Verb

assuage:/əˈsweɪdʒ/
