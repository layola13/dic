draperied:/ˈdreɪpərid/
adjective
垂褶的，披着布的
Covered with or decorated with drapery; arranged in folds like drapery.
The windows were draperied in heavy velvet curtains. 窗户上挂着厚重的丝绒窗帘。
draped, covered
bare, uncovered
adjective
