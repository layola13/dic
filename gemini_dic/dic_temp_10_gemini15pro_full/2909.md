advisablenesses: /ədˌvaɪzəblnəsɪz/
n.
明智之举；适当；可取性
The quality or state of being advisable; propriety; expedience.
The advisablenesses of such a course of action are questionable.  采取这种行动的明智性值得怀疑。
The advisablenesses of investing in the stock market are dependent on various factors.  投资股票市场的可取性取决于多种因素。
近义词: wisdom, prudence, suitability, appropriateness, expedience
反义词: inadvisability, imprudence, unsuitability, inappropriateness
词性: 名词

词源: advisable + -ness
ad- (to) + vis- (to see) + -able (suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being," "fit to be," "worthy of being") + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives, meaning "state or quality of being")
