nonreciprocals:/ˌnɒnˌrɪˈsɪprəkəlz/ | n. |非互惠物，非互易物|Things or relationships that are not reciprocal; lacking reciprocity.  This refers to situations where two parties do not exchange things or feelings equally or in a similar manner.|The research focused on nonreciprocals in international trade. 这项研究的重点是国际贸易中的非互惠物。| 近义词:asymmetrical, unbalanced, one-sided, unilateral| 反义词:reciprocal, mutual, two-sided, bilateral| Noun

nonreciprocal:/ˌnɒnˌrɪˈsɪprəkəl/ | adj. |非互惠的，非互易的|Not reciprocal; not given or felt by both sides equally.  Describes a relationship or interaction where one party gives or feels something that is not returned in kind by the other.|Their relationship was nonreciprocal; she gave him a lot of support, but he offered little in return. 他们的关系是非互惠的；她给了他很多支持，但他几乎没有回报。|近义词:asymmetrical, unbalanced, one-sided, unilateral| 反义词:reciprocal, mutual, two-sided, bilateral| Adjective
