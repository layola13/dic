counterraiding:/ˈkaʊntərˌreɪdɪŋ/ | n. | 反突袭，反劫掠 | The act of raiding in retaliation for a previous raid.  It involves launching an attack against an enemy force that has just conducted a raid, often to disrupt their retreat, recover stolen goods, or inflict punishment. |  The army launched a counterraiding operation against the rebel base.  军队对叛军基地发动了反突袭行动。

The villagers organized a counterraiding party to retrieve their stolen cattle. 村民们组织了一支反突袭队伍去追回被盗的牛。 | Synonyms: counterattack, reprisal, retaliation | Antonyms: raid, attack, aggression | noun
