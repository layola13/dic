outsuck:/ˌaʊtˈsʌk/| v.| 比...更糟糕，胜过（通常用于负面语境）| To be worse than, to surpass in a negative way.  This word is considered informal and somewhat vulgar.  It's often used in competitive contexts, especially gaming.|例句：He outsucked me at Call of Duty. 他在使命召唤里把我打得落花流水。|近义词：outdo, surpass, beat, defeat (in a negative context)|反义词: lose to, be worse than|词性:动词
