debris:/dəˈbriː/ (US), /ˈdeɪbriː/ (UK)| n. |碎片，残骸|scattered fragments, typically of something wrecked or destroyed|The hurricane left streets covered in debris.  飓风过后，街道上到处都是残骸。|rubble, wreckage, remains|intactness, wholeness|noun

debrises: This word is not standard English. The word "debris" is already plural and does not take an -s ending. Use "debris" for both singular and plural contexts.  "Debrises" is grammatically incorrect.||||noun
