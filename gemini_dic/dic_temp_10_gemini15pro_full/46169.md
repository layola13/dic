ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *cynosūra, from Ancient Greek κυνόσουρα (kunósoura, “dog's tail, Ursa Minor”), from κύων (kúōn, “dog”) + οὐρά (ourá, “tail”).  Originally referring to the constellation Ursa Minor, which contains the North Star (Polaris), used for navigation.  The meaning extended metaphorically to anything that serves as a focal point or guide.
