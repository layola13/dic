erythematic: /ˌerɪθəˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 红斑的，充血的 | Relating to or characterized by erythema (redness of the skin). | The patient presented with an erythematic rash on her arms. 病人的手臂上出现了红斑性皮疹。| Synonyms: reddened, inflamed, flushed | Antonyms: pale, blanched | adjective

erythema: /ˌerɪˈθiːmə/ | n. | 红斑 | Redness of the skin, either localized or generalized, caused by capillary congestion. | Sunburn is a common cause of erythema. 晒伤是红斑的常见原因。| Synonyms: redness, rash, inflammation | Antonyms: pallor | noun
