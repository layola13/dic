criticaster:/ˈkrɪtɪkˌæstər/| n. |批评家（贬义）|A minor or insignificant critic; one who habitually criticizes, often unfairly or with little understanding.  A faultfinder. |He's nothing but a criticaster, always finding fault with everyone else's work. 他不过是个爱挑剔的人，总是对别人的工作吹毛求疵。| 近义词：faultfinder, nitpicker, carper | 反义词：supporter, admirer |词性: noun

criticaster:/ˈkrɪtɪkˌæstər/| n. |爱挑剔的人|Someone who criticizes, especially someone who does so unfairly or with little understanding.|The play was panned by the criticasters, but the audience loved it.  这部戏被评论家们批评得很厉害，但观众们很喜欢。|近义词：detractor, caviler |反义词：proponent, advocate|词性: noun
