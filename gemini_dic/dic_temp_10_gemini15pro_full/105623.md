kinetic:/kɪˈnɛtɪk/ /kəˈnɛtɪk/
adjective
运动的;动力的
Relating to or resulting from motion.
Kinetic energy is the energy an object has because of its motion.  动能是物体由于其运动而具有的能量。
The sculpture relies on kinetic energy to create a mesmerizing effect. 这雕塑依靠动能来创造迷人的效果。
synonyms:active, dynamic, mobile
antonyms:static, stationary, inert
词性:形容词
