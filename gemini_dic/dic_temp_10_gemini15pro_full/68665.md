farandams: /ˌfærənˈdæmz/ | n. |  狂欢，庆祝活动 | A lively and boisterous celebration or merrymaking, often with dancing and drinking. | The town held farandams to celebrate the harvest.  小镇举行狂欢庆祝丰收。 | 近义词： revelry, festivity, merrymaking, celebration | 反义词： mourning, grieving | 词性: 名词

词源： From Middle English *farendames, from Old French farendames, plural of farendame (“entertainment, amusement”). Ultimately from Latin *farandam.
