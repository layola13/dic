foray:/ˈfɒreɪ/ (US) /ˈfɔːreɪ/ (UK)| n. vi.|突袭，袭击；涉足，尝试|A sudden attack or incursion into enemy territory, especially to obtain something; a brief but spirited attempt to become involved in a new activity or sphere.|The troops made a foray into enemy territory. 部队对敌方领土进行了突袭。|近义词：raid, incursion, venture, sally|反义词：retreat, withdrawal|noun
foray:/ˈfɒreɪ/ (US) /ˈfɔːreɪ/ (UK)| vi.|进行突袭；冒险尝试|To make a foray; to venture into something new or different.|She made a foray into acting but soon returned to her first love, music. 她曾涉足表演，但很快又回到了她最初的挚爱——音乐。|近义词：venture, dabble, try|反义词：refrain, abstain|verb
