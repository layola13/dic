detailed:/ˈdiːteɪld/| adj.|详细的，详尽的|Marked by abundant detail or thoroughness; comprehensive and precise.|The report provided a detailed account of the incident. 这份报告提供了事件的详细描述。|Synonyms: comprehensive, thorough, exhaustive, in-depth, meticulous, precise|Antonyms: brief, concise, cursory, sketchy, superficial|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *detaillen*, from Anglo-Norman *detaillier*, from Old French *detaillier* ("to cut in pieces"), from *de-* + *tailler* ("to cut"), from Late Latin *taliare*, of Gaulish origin.
