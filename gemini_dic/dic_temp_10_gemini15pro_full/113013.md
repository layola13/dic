lumberyard: /ˈlʌmbərjɑːrd/ | n. | 木材场，木材堆置场 | A place where lumber is stored and sold. |  We bought the wood for the deck at the local lumberyard. 我们在当地的木材场买了做平台的木材。

| Example: The lumberyard was filled with the scent of freshly cut pine.  木材场里充满了新鲜砍伐的松木的香味。

| Synonyms: timber yard, lumber mill, sawmill | Antonyms: N/A | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From lumber (n.) + yard (n.).  "Lumber" originally meant "discarded household items," evolving to mean "timber" in American English.  "Yard" refers to an enclosed space.
