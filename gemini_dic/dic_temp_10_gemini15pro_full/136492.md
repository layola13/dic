nonriparians:/ˌnɒnraɪˈpɛəriənz/ | n. | 非河岸居民，非沿岸居民 | People who do not own land adjacent to a river or other body of water and therefore do not have riparian rights. |  Nonriparians are often subject to different water usage regulations. 非河岸居民通常会受到不同的用水规定约束。

The new law sparked protests from nonriparians who felt their access to water was being unfairly restricted.  这项新法律引发了非河岸居民的抗议，他们认为自己获得水的权利受到了不公平的限制。 | 近义词: uplanders | 反义词: riparians | 词性: 名词
