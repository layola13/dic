insiccation:/ˌɪnsɪˈkeɪʃən/ |n.|干燥，脱水|The insiccation of the land led to desertification.  土地的干燥导致了荒漠化。|例句：The prolonged drought led to the insiccation of the crops. 长期干旱导致农作物枯萎。|近义词：dehydration, drying, desiccation|反义词：hydration, moistening|词性：名词

Etymology: From Latin insiccatus, past participle of insiccare “to dry up,” from in- “in, on” + siccare “to dry,” from siccus “dry.”
