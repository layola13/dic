adventurer:/ədˈventʃərər/ (US), /ədˈventʃərə/ (UK)| n.|冒险家；冒险者|A person who enjoys or seeks adventure, often involving travel or exploration to unusual, exciting, or dangerous places.|He was a seasoned adventurer who had travelled the world. 他是一位游历世界的经验丰富的冒险家。

The young adventurer set off to explore the uncharted territory.  这位年轻的冒险家出发去探索未知的领域。

|近义词：explorer, traveler, voyager, globetrotter, daredevil|反义词：homebody, stay-at-home, cautious person|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French aventure "adventure," from Latin adventura "that which happens, chance," from adventurus "about to happen," future participle of advenire "to arrive, come to," from ad- "to" + venire "to come."  The suffix "-er" denotes a person who performs a particular action.
