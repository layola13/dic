crib:/krɪb/| n.|婴儿床；剽窃；小抄；马槽；小屋；简陋的住处|A child's bed with barred or latticed sides; plagiarism; cheat sheet; manger; small dwelling; a mean, small apartment. |The baby slept soundly in her crib. 婴儿在婴儿床里睡得很香。|Synonyms: cot, cradle, plagiarize, copy, cheat, manger, hut, shack|Antonyms:  (For dwelling) mansion, palace|noun


crib:/krɪb/| v.|抄袭；剽窃；关入小屋；将…关入婴儿床|To copy or plagiarize; to confine in a small space; to put a baby in a crib.|He cribbed his answers from his classmate. 他抄袭了同学的答案。|Synonyms: copy, plagiarize, cheat, confine|Antonyms: originate, create|verb
