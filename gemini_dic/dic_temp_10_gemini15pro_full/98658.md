insufflate:/ɪnˈsʌfleɪt/ /ɪnˈsʌfˌleɪt/ | vt.|吹入；注入；灌输|To blow or breathe (something, such as air, gas, or powder) into a body cavity. To inspire or introduce as if by blowing. |The doctor insufflated the patient's lungs with air. 医生给病人的肺里吹入空气。

The coach tried to insufflate some confidence into his team. 教练试图给他的团队注入一些信心。|近义词：inject, introduce, inspire, instill|反义词：extract, remove, deflate|词性：动词
