friendsourcing:/ˈfrɛndˌsɔːrsɪŋ/
n.
朋友资源外包；众包朋友
The act of soliciting ideas, services, or content from one's online social network, rather than from traditional employees or suppliers.
例句：
The company used friendsourcing to generate new marketing ideas.
这家公司利用朋友资源外包来产生新的营销理念。

例句：
Friendsourcing proved to be a cost-effective way to gather feedback on the new product.
事实证明，朋友资源外包是一种收集新产品反馈的经济有效的方式。

近义词：crowdsourcing, social networking
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps traditional outsourcing)
词性: noun
