expandable:/ɪkˈspændəbl/| adj.|可扩展的，可扩充的|Able to be expanded or made larger. |The suitcase is expandable, so you can pack more clothes.  这个行李箱是可扩展的，所以你可以装更多的衣服。|近义词：adjustable, extendable, flexible, stretchable|反义词:fixed, rigid, inflexible, non-expandable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From expand + -able.  From Latin expandere "to spread out."
