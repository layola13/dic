macrofauna:/ˈmækroʊfɔːnə/ | n. |大型动物群|Animals large enough to be seen with the naked eye, generally greater than 0.5mm. |例句：The macrofauna of the coral reef is incredibly diverse. 珊瑚礁的大型动物群非常多样化。

例句：Scientists are studying the macrofauna of the deep sea to better understand its ecosystem. 科学家们正在研究深海的大型动物群，以便更好地了解其生态系统。|近义词：megafauna|反义词：microfauna|词性：名词

Etymology:  From macro- + fauna.  "macro-" comes from Greek "makrós" meaning "long", "large". "fauna" comes from Latin Fauna, the Roman goddess of animals, and the sister of Faunus, a nature spirit.
