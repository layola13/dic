brothy:/ˈbrɔːθi/ | adj. | 肉汤味的；像肉汤的；清淡的 | Resembling broth in flavor, thinness, or consistency. | The soup was light and brothy, perfect for a cold day.  这汤清淡鲜美，非常适合在寒冷的日子里享用。 |  Example: The aroma wafting from the kitchen was rich and brothy. 厨房飘来的香味浓郁而鲜美。 | Synonyms: thin, watery, soupy | Antonyms: thick, creamy, rich | word type: adjective
