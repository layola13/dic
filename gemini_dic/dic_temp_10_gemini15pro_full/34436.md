claustrum:/ˈklɔːstrəm/| n.|屏状核;  隔膜;  |A thin sheet of gray matter located between the external capsule and the extreme capsule of the brain. |The claustrum's function is still largely a mystery to neuroscientists. 屏状核的功能对神经科学家来说仍然很大程度上是个谜。|The claustrum is involved in integrating various sensory inputs. 屏状核参与整合各种感觉输入。|synonyms:  insula (somewhat),  pallidum (loosely)|antonyms: N/A|noun
