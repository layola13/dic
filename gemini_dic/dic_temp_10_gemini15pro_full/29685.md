catheterism:/ˈkæθɪtərɪzəm/ | n. |导管插入术；插管术|The act of inserting a catheter into a body cavity or vessel, especially into the bladder to withdraw urine.|The patient underwent catheterism to drain the urine.|Synonyms: catheterization, cannulation|Antonyms: N/A|noun

catheterize:/ˈkæθɪtəraɪz/ | vt. |插管；用导管插入|To insert a catheter into (a body cavity or vessel).|The nurse catheterized the patient to empty his bladder.|Synonyms: cannulate, intubate|Antonyms: decannulate, extubate|verb
