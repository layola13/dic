nebbishly:/ˈnɛbɪʃli/| adv.|怯懦地，窝囊地|In a timid, nervous, or ineffectual manner; like a nebbish.|He nebbishly asked her to dance, his voice barely above a whisper. 他怯生生地邀请她跳舞，声音细若蚊蝇。|近义词：timidly, nervously, sheepishly, awkwardly|反义词：confidently, assertively, boldly|词性:副词

nebbish:/ˈnɛbɪʃ/| n.|懦弱的人，窝囊废|A timid, meek, or ineffectual person.|He was a nebbish in high school, always getting picked on by the bullies. 他在高中是个窝囊废，总是被恶霸欺负。|近义词：wimp, milksop, weakling|反义词：alpha male, leader, extrovert|词性:名词
