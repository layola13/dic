crl:/ˈsiːɑːrˈɛl/ |abbr.| Carriage Return Line Feed (回车换行)| A control character or sequence that moves the cursor to the beginning of the next line.  It is commonly used to denote the end of a line of text. | Example: The crl character is essential for formatting text files correctly. 回车换行符对于正确格式化文本文件至关重要。 | Synonym: newline, EOL (end of line) | Antonym: N/A | abbreviation
