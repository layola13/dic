misadjudicated:/ˌmɪsəˈdʒuːdɪkeɪtɪd/
|adj.|错误判决的，误判的|Incorrectly judged or decided, especially in a legal context.  The outcome reflects a flawed judicial process where the judge made a mistake in applying the law or evaluating the evidence.|The case was misadjudicated, leading to an innocent man's imprisonment.  这起案件被误判，导致一名无辜男子入狱。|misjudged, wrongly decided|correctly adjudicated, justly decided|adjective
