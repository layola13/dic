pacifyingly:/ˈpæsɪfaɪɪŋli/| adv.|平静地；安抚地；抚慰地|In a manner that soothes or calms someone or something.  It describes actions or words intended to bring peace or quiet. |She spoke pacifyingly to the frightened child. 她平静地对受惊的孩子说话。|Synonyms: soothingly, calmingly, appeasingly, tranquilly, placidly|Antonyms: agitatingly, disturbingly, provokingly, antagonistically|词性:副词
