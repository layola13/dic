misruled:/ˌmɪsˈruːld/
adjective
统治不善的，管理不当的
governed badly or unjustly.
The country was misruled for decades by a corrupt dictator.  这个国家被一个腐败的独裁者统治不善了几十年。
The misruled kingdom was rife with poverty and unrest.  这个统治不善的王国充满了贫困和动荡。
misgoverned, ill-governed
well-governed, well-ruled
