overmobilize: /ˌoʊvərˈmoʊbəˌlaɪz/ | v. | 过度动员，过分动员 | To mobilize (troops, resources, etc.) in excessive numbers or to an excessive degree.  | The country overmobilized its army, leaving its economy vulnerable.  这个国家过度动员了军队，使其经济变得脆弱。 | The general was criticized for overmobilizing troops for the minor conflict. 将军因在小规模冲突中过度动员军队而受到批评。 | 近义词: overdeploy, overextend | 反义词: undermobilize, underdeploy | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + mobilize.  "Mobilize" comes from French "mobiliser," from "mobile" (movable), ultimately from Latin "mobilis."
