id'ing:/ˈaɪdiɪŋ/ | v. | 识别；鉴定；确认 | Identifying.  A shortened form of the word "identifying." It describes the act of establishing the identity of someone or something. |  Example:  They were id'ing all the plants in the garden. 他们在辨认花园里所有的植物。| Example:  The police are id'ing the suspect from the security footage. 警方正在通过监控录像确认嫌疑人的身份。 | Synonyms: identifying, recognizing, verifying, confirming, pinpointing | Antonyms: obscuring, concealing, hiding | Verb (present participle)
