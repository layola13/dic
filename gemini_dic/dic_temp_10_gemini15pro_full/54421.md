disinfect:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfekt/| v. |消毒，杀菌|To cleanse of infection, usually by chemical means; to destroy disease germs in. |例句：We disinfected the wound with iodine. 我们用碘酒给伤口消毒。|近义词：sterilize, sanitize, cleanse|反义词:infect, contaminate|词性:动词
disinfectant:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfektənt/| n. |消毒剂|A chemical liquid used to destroy bacteria. |例句：Bleach is a common household disinfectant. 漂白剂是一种常见的家用消毒剂。|近义词：antiseptic, germicide|反义词:contaminant|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *disinfecta, from dis- + infect.
