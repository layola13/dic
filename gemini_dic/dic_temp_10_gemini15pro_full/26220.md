butterflies:/ˈbʌtərˌflaɪz/ | n. | 蝴蝶（复数） |  The plural form of "butterfly," referring to multiple of these colorful insects.  | I saw many colorful butterflies in the garden. 我在花园里看到许多色彩斑斓的蝴蝶。 | synonyms: Lepidoptera (technical), flutterbys (informal) | antonyms: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English buterfleoge, possibly because butterflies were attracted to dairy products like butter, or because their excrement resembles butter.  Another theory suggests it's from the pale yellow color of some species.
