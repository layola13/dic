impoverished: /ɪmˈpɒvərɪʃt/ /ɪmˈpɑːvərɪʃt/
| adj. | 贫困的，赤贫的；贫瘠的，枯竭的 |  Reduced to poverty; deprived of strength, vitality, or richness. |  The impoverished family struggled to afford basic necessities.  这个贫困的家庭难以负担基本生活必需品。 | synonyms: poor, destitute, indigent, needy, deprived, penniless | antonyms: rich, wealthy, affluent, prosperous, opulent | adjective
