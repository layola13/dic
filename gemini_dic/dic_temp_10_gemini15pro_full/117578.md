megalocephalia:/ˌmeɡəloʊsəˈfeɪliə/ | n. |巨头畸形|Abnormal enlargement of the head, a condition in which the head is abnormally large, usually due to an underlying medical condition. |Example 1: The doctor diagnosed the child with megalocephalia after observing the unusual head size.  医生在观察到异常的头部尺寸后，诊断孩子患有巨头畸形。

Example 2:  Megalocephalia can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetic mutations. 巨头畸形可能由多种因素引起，包括基因突变。|近义词: macrocephaly |反义词: microcephaly |词性: noun
