palped:/pælp/ /pælpt/| v. |触诊；触摸；抚摸|To examine (something) by touch, especially for medical purposes.|The doctor palped the patient's abdomen. 医生触诊了病人的腹部。|
The biologist palped the insect's antennae. 生物学家触摸了昆虫的触角。|近义词：touch, feel, examine, probe|反义词: ignore, neglect|词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *palpāre*, frequentative of *palpare* "to stroke, touch softly".
