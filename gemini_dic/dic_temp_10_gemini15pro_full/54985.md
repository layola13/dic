disprove:/dɪsˈpruːv/
verb
反驳，证明…是虚假的
to prove that something is not true
The lawyer disproved the witness's testimony. 律师反驳了证人的证词。
近义词:refute, debunk, invalidate
反义词:prove, confirm, verify
词性:verb
