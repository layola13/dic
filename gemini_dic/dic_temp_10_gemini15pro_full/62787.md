engross:/ɪnˈɡroʊs/ /ɛnˈɡroʊs/| vt.|全神贯注，吸引；正式誊写|to occupy someone's attention completely|The new video game completely engrossed him for hours.  这款新的电子游戏让他着迷了好几个小时。|近义词：absorb, engage, immerse, preoccupy|反义词：bore, distract, tire|verb

engross:/ɪnˈɡroʊs/ /ɛnˈɡroʊs/| vt.|正式誊写（法律文件等）|to write or copy in a clear, large script|The lawyer engrossed the final version of the contract. 律师正式誊写了合同的最终版本。|近义词：transcribe, copy|反义词：-|verb
