enhanced:/ɪnˈhɑːnst/ | /ɪnˈhænst/ | adj. | 增强的，提高的 |  Increased or improved in quality, value, or extent. |  例句：The enhanced security measures made the building safer.  增强的安全措施使建筑物更安全。

例句： This software has enhanced features for photo editing. 这个软件有增强的照片编辑功能。


近义词：improved, better, superior, augmented, strengthened | 反义词：diminished, reduced, weakened, impaired | 词性：形容词

词源: From the Old French "enhaucer" meaning "to raise," from "en-" (in) + "haucer" (to raise).  Ultimately from the Latin "altus" (high).
