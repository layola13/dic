impeccableness:/ɪmˈpekəbəl.nəs/| n.|完美无瑕，无可挑剔|The impeccableness of her performance stunned the audience. 她的表演完美无瑕，令观众惊叹。|近义词：perfection, flawlessness, faultlessness|反义词：imperfection, faultiness, defectiveness|词性:名词

Etymology: From impeccable + -ness. Impeccable comes from Late Latin impeccabilis "not liable to sin," from in- "not" + peccare "to sin," from Latin peccare "to stumble, sin, make a mistake, transgress."
