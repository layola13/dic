centralizer:/ˈsɛntrəlaɪzər/| n.|中心化器，扶正器；中心化装置；【数学】中心化子|A device that centers something. In mathematics, the centralizer of a subset of a group consists of all elements of the group that commute with every element of the subset.|The centralizer of the wheel keeps it aligned. 轮子的中心化装置使其保持对齐。  The centralizer of a subgroup H is a subgroup. 子群 H 的中心化子是一个子群。|近义词: stabilizer|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

centralizer:/ˈsɛntrəlaɪzər/| n.|集中化者，中央集权者|A person or thing that centralizes something, particularly power or control.|The company president acted as the centralizer, making all the major decisions. 公司总裁充当了集权者，做出了所有重大决策。|近义词: unifier, consolidator|反义词: decentralizer|词性:noun
