dilution:/daɪˈluːʃn/ /dɪˈluːʃn/| n.|稀释；冲淡；稀释液|The act of making a liquid weaker by adding water or another solvent to it.  The resulting liquid that is weaker.|The chemist prepared a dilution of the acid. 化学家配制了稀释的酸。|synonyms: weakening, thinning, attenuation|antonyms: concentration, strengthening|noun
