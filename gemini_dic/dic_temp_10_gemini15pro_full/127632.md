needles:/ˈniːdlz/| v.|用针刺，刺激，激怒|To prick or pierce with a needle; to provoke or goad.|He needled her about her messy room. 他拿她凌乱的房间刺激她。|synonyms: provoke, irritate, annoy|antonyms: soothe, calm, placate|verb
