medals:/ˈmedlz/
n.
奖牌；勋章
A metal disc or other small object bearing an inscription or design, given as an award or commemoration of some outstanding action, achievement, etc.
She won three gold medals at the Olympics. 她在奥运会上赢得了三枚金牌。

近义词: awards, decorations, honors, prizes
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用dishonor, disgrace表示失去奖牌的荣誉)
词性: 名词
