geopolitist:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈpɑːlɪtɪst/ | n. |地缘政治学家；地缘政治论者| A person who studies or specializes in geopolitics, the study of the influence of geography on politics and international relations. |  He's a renowned geopolitist, often consulted by government officials. 他是一位著名的地缘政治学家，经常受到政府官员的咨询。

| 近义词: political geographer, international relations expert | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

geopolitist:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈpɑːlɪtɪst/ | n. |地缘政治论者|Someone who applies geopolitical principles to analyze and interpret international events and power dynamics.|The geopolitist argued that control of the region's resources was key to global dominance.  这位地缘政治论者认为，控制该地区的资源是全球主导地位的关键。 | 近义词: strategist, analyst | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
