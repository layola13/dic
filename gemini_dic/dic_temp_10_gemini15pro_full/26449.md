byssaceous:/bɪˈseɪʃəs/
adjective
丝状的；麻布似的；由细丝组成的
Consisting of or resembling fine filaments or threads; like cotton or flax.
The byssaceous material felt soft and delicate against her skin.  这种丝状的材料贴着她的皮肤感觉柔软细腻。
The spider spun a byssaceous web to trap its prey.  蜘蛛吐出丝状的网来捕捉猎物。
近义词:filamentous, threadlike, cottony, flossy
反义词:coarse, rough, thick
词性:adjective

词源:
From Latin *byssaceus, from byssus (“fine linen, cotton”) + -aceus (suffix forming adjectives denoting resemblance or material).  来自拉丁语 *byssaceus，源自 byssus（“细麻布，棉花”）+ -aceus（构成表示相似或材料的形容词的后缀）。
