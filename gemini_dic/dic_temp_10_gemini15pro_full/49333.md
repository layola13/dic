delimitative:/dɪˈlɪmɪtətɪv/ | adj. |限定的，划界的 | Serving to delimit; delimiting.  Marking or defining the limits of something. | The delimitative powers of the treaty were clearly defined.  该条约的划界权被明确界定。 | 近义词: defining, restrictive, limiting | 反义词: expansive, unlimited, boundless | 词性: adjective
Etymology: From Late Latin *dēlimitātīvus, from dēlimitātus (past participle of dēlimitāre, "to define the limits of"), from dē- ("from, down from, away") + līmitāre ("to limit"), from līmes ("boundary, limit").
