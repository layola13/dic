hurricane-lamps:/ˈhɜːrɪkeɪn læmps/ |n.|飓风灯；防风灯|A lamp designed to be shielded from strong winds, typically with a glass chimney and a base designed to hold oil or other fuel. |The hurricane-lamps flickered in the wind. 飓风灯在风中闪烁。|They lit the hurricane-lamps as the storm approached. 随着暴风雨的临近，他们点亮了飓风灯。|synonyms: storm lanterns, oil lamps|antonyms: electric lamps, flashlights|noun
