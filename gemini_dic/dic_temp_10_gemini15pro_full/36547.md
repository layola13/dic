collagenous:/kəˈlædʒənəs/
adjective
胶原的；产生胶原的
Relating to or containing collagen.
Collagenous fibers provide structural support to tissues. 胶原纤维为组织提供结构支撑。
The dermis is rich in collagenous and elastic fibers. 真皮富含胶原纤维和弹性纤维。
近义词: fibrous, connective
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "non-collagenous")
词性: adjective
