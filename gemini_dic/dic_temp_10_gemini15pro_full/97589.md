inhabitate: /ɪnˈhæbɪteɪt/ | v. |居住于，栖息于 |To live or dwell in (a place). While technically correct, "inhabit" is generally preferred in modern usage. "Inhabitate" is considered archaic or redundant by many. |例句：Birds inhabitate the trees. 鸟类栖息在树上。|近义词：inhabit, live in, reside in, occupy, dwell in, populate|反义词:desert, vacate|词性:动词
