no-goods:/ˈnoʊɡʊdz/
n.|废物；无用的人；次品|A person or thing of no value or use. | Those no-goods just hang around the street corner all day.  那些废物整天就在街角闲逛。|Near synonyms:  bums, deadbeats, good-for-nothings|Antonyms:  assets, valuables|Noun

no-goods:/ˈnoʊɡʊdz/
adj.|无用的；不值钱的|Having no value or use. |He called the broken toys a pile of no-goods. 他把那些坏掉的玩具称为一堆废物。|Near synonyms: worthless, useless, valueless|Antonyms: valuable, useful, worthwhile|Adjective
