outdueled:/ˌaʊtˈduːəld/
verb
胜过，击败（尤指用技巧）
to defeat someone by being more skillful or clever than they are, especially in a duel or fight.
He outdueled his opponent in the final round of the competition. 他在比赛的最后一轮击败了对手。
Synonyms: outmaneuver, outwit, defeat, conquer
Antonyms: lose to, be defeated by
词性：动词
