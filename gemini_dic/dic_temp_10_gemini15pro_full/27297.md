calvinist: /ˈkælvɪnɪst/ | n. | 加尔文主义者；加尔文教徒 | A follower of the theological doctrines of John Calvin, emphasizing predestination, the sovereignty of God, and the authority of the Scriptures. |  Many early American settlers were Calvinists. 许多早期的美国定居者都是加尔文主义者。|  synonyms:  Reformed Protestant, Presbyterian (often), Puritan (historically) | antonyms: Arminian, free will advocate | noun

calvinistic: /ˌkælvɪˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 加尔文主义的；加尔文派的 | Relating to or characteristic of the theological doctrines of John Calvin or his followers. | The community had a strong Calvinistic tradition. 这个社区有着浓厚的加尔文主义传统。| synonyms: Reformed, Presbyterian (often), Puritanical (sometimes) | antonyms: Arminian | adjective
