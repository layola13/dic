bulbosity:/bʌlˈbɒsɪti/
n.|球茎状，膨胀，|The bulbousness of the onion made it difficult to chop. 洋葱的球茎状使它很难切碎。|English Explanation: The state or quality of being bulbous; a rounded swelling or protuberance.|例句：The artist exaggerated the bulbousness of the figure's nose. 艺术家夸大了人物鼻子膨胀的形状。

例句：The bulbousness of the plant's stem indicated it was storing water.  植物茎干的膨胀表明它正在储存水分。

近义词：roundness, swelling, protuberance
反义词：slimness, thinness
词性: noun
