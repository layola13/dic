bubale:/ˈbjuːbəleɪ/ /ˈbuːbɑːleɪ/| n.|狷羚|A large African antelope with backward-curving horns, of the genus *Alcelaphus*.|The bubale grazed peacefully on the savanna. 狷羚在热带草原上平静地吃草。|The bubale stared intently at the approaching lion. 狷羚目不转睛地盯着逼近的狮子。| 近义词:hartebeest, alcelaphus|反义词:|noun
