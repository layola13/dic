incantational:/ˌɪnkænteɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 咒语的，魔咒的 | Relating to or characterized by incantation; using spells or charms. | The witch muttered incantational words over the cauldron. 女巫对着大锅低声念着咒语。|  近义词: magical, enchanting, spellbinding | 反义词: mundane, ordinary | 词性: 形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin incantatio(n-), from incantare ‘enchant’, from in- ‘into’ + cantare ‘sing’.
