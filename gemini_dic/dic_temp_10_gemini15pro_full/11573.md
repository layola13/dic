aristotle:/ˈærɪˌstɒtl/ (US) /ˈærɪstɒtl/ (UK)| n.|亚里士多德|A Greek philosopher and scientist, a student of Plato and teacher of Alexander the Great. He is considered one of the most important figures in Western philosophy.|Aristotle's writings cover a vast range of subjects. 亚里士多德的著作涵盖了广泛的主题。|近义词:philosopher, thinker|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

aristotelian:/ˌærɪstəˈtiːliən/ (US) /ˌærɪstəˈtiːlɪən/ (UK)| adj.|亚里士多德的；亚里士多德学派的|Relating to Aristotle, his philosophy, or his followers.|Aristotelian logic is based on syllogisms. 亚里士多德逻辑学是基于三段论的。 | Aristotelian ethics emphasizes virtue. 亚里士多德伦理学强调美德。|近义词:Peripatetic|反义词:Platonic|词性:adjective
