anamorphoscope:/ˌænəˈmɔːrfəskoʊp/| n. |变形镜，变形显示器|An optical device that restores an image distorted by anamorphosis to its normal proportions.|The artist used an anamorphoscope to view his distorted painting.|Synonym:  None (highly specific term)|Antonym: None (highly specific term)|noun
anamorphosis:/ˌænəˈmɔːrfəsɪs/| n. |变形，变形图像|A distorted projection or drawing that appears normal when viewed from a particular point or with a special instrument (such as a cylindrical mirror).|The anamorphosis on the wall looked like a jumbled mess until viewed through the mirror.|Synonym: distortion, deformation|Antonym:  normal form, undistorted image|noun
