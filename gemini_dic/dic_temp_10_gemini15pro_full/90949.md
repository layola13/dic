hurly-burly:/ˈhɜːrli ˈbɜːrli/ /ˈhɝːli ˈbɝːli/
n.|喧闹；骚动；混乱|A commotion; a turbulent situation. |The marketplace was a hurly-burly of sights and sounds.|市场上人声鼎沸，景象纷乱。
hurly-burly:|近义词：commotion, tumult, uproar, chaos, bedlam, pandemonium|反义词：peace, quiet, tranquility, calm, order|名词
hurly-burly:|词源：From Middle English *hurlyburly*, probably imitative of commotion.|
hurly-burly:|例句：The hurly-burly of city life can be overwhelming. 城市生活的喧嚣有时会让人难以承受。
hurly-burly:|例句：He escaped the hurly-burly of the office for a quiet lunch. 他从办公室的喧嚣中逃离出来，去吃了一顿安静的午餐。
