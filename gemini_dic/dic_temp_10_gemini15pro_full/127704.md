neglectedness:/nɪˈɡlɛktɪdnəs/| n.|被忽视; 疏忽; 荒废|The state of being neglected; lack of attention or care.|The neglectedness of the garden was apparent from the overgrown weeds and dead flowers. 花园里杂草丛生，花朵枯萎，显然是被忽视了。|Synonyms: disregard, abandonment, dereliction, inattention|Antonyms: care, attention, maintenance, diligence|noun

neglect:/nɪˈɡlɛkt/| vt. n.| 忽视，忽略；疏忽，疏漏|fail to care for properly.|He neglected his studies and failed the exam. 他忽视了学习，考试不及格。|Synonyms: ignore, disregard, overlook|Antonyms: attend to, heed, cherish|verb & noun
