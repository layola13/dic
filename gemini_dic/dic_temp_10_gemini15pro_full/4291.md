airworthy:/ˈɛərwɜːrði/ (US) /ˈɛːwɜːði/ (UK)| adj.|适航的；飞行安全的|Fit or safe to fly.|The aircraft was declared airworthy after a thorough inspection.  这架飞机经过彻底检查后被宣布适航。|The pilot confirmed the plane was airworthy before takeoff.  飞行员在起飞前确认飞机适航。|近义词：safe, flyable|反义词:unairworthy, unsafe|词性:形容词
Etymology: air + worthy.  From air (n.) + worthy (adj.).  Air meaning "of or relating to aircraft" and worthy meaning "having merit or value; deserving."
