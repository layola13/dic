hindrances:/ˈhɪndrənsɪz/
hindrance:/ˈhɪndrəns/| n.|障碍，阻碍|Something that provides resistance, delay, or obstruction to something or someone.|The lack of funding was a major hindrance to the project. 缺乏资金是该项目的主要障碍。|近义词：obstacle, impediment, barrier, obstruction|反义词：aid, help, assistance|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *hindraunce*, from Anglo-Norman *hindrance, hindreance*, and Old French *hindrance*, from *hindrer* ("to hinder"), from Frankish *hindran* ("to hold back, restrain"), from Proto-Germanic *hindar* ("behind, back"), from Proto-Indo-European *h₁éndʰer* ("behind, at the back, after").
