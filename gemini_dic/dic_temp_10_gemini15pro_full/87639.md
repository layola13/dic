hewn:/hjuːn/| adj.|凿成的；雕琢的|shaped or cut from a large piece of stone or wood|The doorway was hewn from a single block of granite.  门口是由一整块花岗岩凿成的。

The statue was hewn out of marble.  这座雕像是用大理石雕刻而成的。|近义词：carved, sculpted, shaped, fashioned|反义词:cast, molded|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hēawan (past participle hēawen), meaning "to cut, chop, hew."  Related to German hauen.
