epitomizing:/ɪˈpɪtəˌmaɪzɪŋ/
verb
是…的典型；体现；概括
being a perfect example of something
Her work epitomizes the highest standards of journalism. 她的工作体现了新闻界的最高标准。
synonyms: embody, exemplify, typify, represent
antonyms: misrepresent, belie, contradict
verb
