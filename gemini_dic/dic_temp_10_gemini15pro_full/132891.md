nonelector:/ˌnɒnɪˈlɛktə(r)/
noun
非选举人
A person who is not entitled or allowed to vote in an election.
例句：Nonelectors are not allowed to participate in the voting process.  非选举人不得参与投票过程。

例句：The nonelectors felt their voices were not being heard. 非选举人觉得他们的声音没有被听到。

近义词：disenfranchised, ineligible voter
反义词：voter, elector
词性：n.
