anamniotic:/ˌænæmniˈɒtɪk/ | adj. | 无羊膜的 | Lacking an amnion (the innermost membrane of an amniotic egg or embryo).  Relating to or characteristic of Anamniota, a group of vertebrates comprising fishes and amphibians that lack an amnion during embryonic development. |  Fish and amphibians are anamniotic vertebrates. 鱼类和两栖类是无羊膜脊椎动物。|  The anamniotic egg is laid in water. 无羊膜卵产在水中。 | synonyms:  anamniote | antonyms: amniotic | adjective
