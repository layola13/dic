awakes:/əˈweɪks/| v.| 醒来，唤醒 |The sound of birdsong awakes me every morning. 鸟儿的歌声每天早晨把我唤醒。|  He awakes to the smell of coffee. 他闻到咖啡的味道就醒了。 |近义词：wakens, rouses, awakens |反义词：sleeps, slumbers |verb

awakes:/əˈweɪks/| adj. (古语，罕用)| 醒着的 |He lay awakes, staring at the ceiling. 他醒着躺在那儿，盯着天花板。|  (This usage is archaic and rarely seen) |近义词：awake, wakeful |反义词：asleep |adjective
