in-the-way:/ɪn ðə weɪ/
adjective
碍事的，挡道的
obstructing or hindering someone's movement or progress.
例句：Your suitcase is in the way. 你的行李箱挡道了。
例句：The toys were in the way, so I picked them up. 玩具挡路了，所以我把它们捡起来了。
近义词：obstructing, hindering, blocking, impeding
反义词：helpful, assisting, out-of-the-way
