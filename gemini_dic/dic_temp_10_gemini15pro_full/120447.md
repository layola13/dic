minny:/ˈmɪni/| n.|小可爱，小家伙 (尤指女孩或女人)|A term of endearment, often used for a girl or woman, suggesting smallness and affection. It can be seen as a diminutive of "minnie."|例句：She's such a minny, always smiling and cheerful. 她真是个小可爱，总是笑容满面，兴高采烈的。

例句：He called his daughter "minny" as a term of endearment. 他亲昵地叫他的女儿“小可爱”。|近义词：sweetie, darling, dear, honey, cutie|反义词: (No direct antonym; perhaps brute, lout, or other words implying the opposite of endearing qualities) |词性:noun
