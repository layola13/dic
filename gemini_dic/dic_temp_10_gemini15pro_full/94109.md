impeachments:/ɪmˈpiːtʃmənts/| n.|弹劾；控告；检举|The act of formally accusing a public official of misconduct in office.  The process of calling into question the integrity or validity of something.|The president faced two impeachments during his time in office. 总统在任期内面临两次弹劾。|synonyms: accusations, charges, indictments|antonyms: defenses, acquittals|noun
