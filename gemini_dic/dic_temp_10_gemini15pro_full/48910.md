defreezing:/diːˈfriːzɪŋ/ | n. | 解冻 | The process of thawing something that has been frozen. | The defreezing of the meat took several hours. 肉的解冻花了几个小时。| 近义词: thawing | 反义词: freezing | 词性: 名词

词源:  "de-" (prefix meaning "removal" or "reversal") + "freezing" (present participle of "freeze").  The word signifies the reversal of the freezing process.
