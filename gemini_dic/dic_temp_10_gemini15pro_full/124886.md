muddish:/ˈmʌdɪʃ/| adj.|浑浊的，混乱的，糊涂的|Like muddy water, not clear; confused and not thinking clearly.|The river was muddish after the storm.  暴风雨过后，河水浑浊不堪。|The explanation was muddish and unclear.  这个解释含糊不清，令人费解。|近义词:murky, cloudy, obscure, confused|反义词:clear, lucid, transparent|词性:形容词
Etymology: From mud + -ish.  源自 mud（泥）+ -ish（形容词后缀，表示“像…的”）。
