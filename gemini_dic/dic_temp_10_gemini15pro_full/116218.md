masonically:/məˈsɒnɪkli/
adverb
以共济会的方式，秘密地
In a manner resembling or characteristic of Freemasons, often implying secrecy or ritual.
The group met masonically, exchanging secret handshakes and whispers.  这个团体以共济会的方式聚会，交换秘密握手和耳语。
The business deal was conducted masonically, with details kept from the public. 这笔交易是以共济会的方式进行的，细节没有向公众公开。
近义词:secretly, privately, covertly
反义词:openly, publicly, transparently
词性:adverb

词源:
From "masonic" + "-ly". "Masonic" derives from "Mason," referring to a member of the Freemasons, from Old French "maçon" meaning "mason," ultimately from Latin "machio" meaning "builder." The suffix "-ly" transforms the adjective "masonic" into an adverb.
