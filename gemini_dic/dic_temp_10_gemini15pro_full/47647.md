debonair:/ˌdɛbəˈnɛər/| adj.|温文尔雅的，潇洒的|Confident, stylish, and charming.|He strolled into the room with a debonair smile. 他带着潇洒的微笑走进房间。|近义词：suave, urbane, sophisticated, charming, elegant|反义词：awkward, clumsy, gauche, uncouth|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *de bon aire* "of good air/manner," from *de* "of" + *bon* "good" + *aire* "air, manner." Originally meant "courteous, of good demeanor."  The meaning shifted to encompass a sense of stylishness and charm.
