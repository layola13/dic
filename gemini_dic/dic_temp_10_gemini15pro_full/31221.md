channelly:/ˈtʃænəli/ | adj. | 沟渠状的；像沟渠的 | Resembling a channel or canal; having channels or grooves. | The surface of the rock was channelly, worn smooth by the flowing water. 岩石表面呈沟渠状，被流水磨得很光滑。 | The dry riverbed was channelly and cracked. 干涸的河床呈沟渠状，并且布满了裂缝。 | 近义词: grooved, furrowed, channeled | 反义词: smooth, flat | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From "channel" + "-ly" (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Old North French "chanel".
