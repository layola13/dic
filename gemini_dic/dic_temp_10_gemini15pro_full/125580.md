multitracks:/ˈmʌltiˌtræks/ | n. |多轨录音，多音轨|A recording of a song or piece of music in which the separate instrumental or vocal parts are recorded on individual tracks. | The band recorded the song using multitracks, allowing them to adjust each instrument's volume later. 这支乐队使用多轨录音技术录制了这首歌，以便之后可以调整每种乐器的音量。| 近义词: multitrack recording | 反义词: monaural recording |词性:名词

multitrack:/ˈmʌltiˌtræk/ | adj. | 多轨的 | Relating to or involving multitrack recording. |  They used a multitrack recorder to capture all the nuances of the performance. 他们使用多轨录音机捕捉了表演的所有细微之处。 |近义词: multichannel | 反义词: single-track |词性:形容词
