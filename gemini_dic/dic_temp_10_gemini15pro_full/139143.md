obeyable:/əˈbeɪəbl/| adj.|服从的，顺从的|Capable of being obeyed; submissive.  Suitable or amenable to being obeyed. |The dog was well-trained and very obeyable. 这只狗训练有素，非常听话。| The rules are strict but obeyable. 规则很严格，但是可以遵守。|近义词：compliant, docile, submissive, tractable|反义词:disobedient, rebellious, defiant, intractable|词性:adjective
Etymology:  From obey + -able.  Ultimately from Latin oboedire.
