bonninesses:/ˈbɒnɪnɪsɪz/
noun
骨瘦如柴；极瘦
the state of being very thin, especially because of illness or lack of food
The bonninesses of the refugees were shocking.  难民们骨瘦如柴的样子令人震惊。

The bonninesses of her wrists were accentuated by the bracelets she wore. 她佩戴的手镯更加突显了她手腕的骨感。

近义词:  thinness, emaciation, gauntness, skinniness
反义词:  plumpness, stoutness, chubbiness, obesity
词性: noun (plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From bonny (attractive, healthy-looking, especially in a slightly plump way) + -ness (suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality) + -es (plural suffix).  Ironically, while "bonny" originally implied a healthy plumpness, "bonniness" in modern usage refers to extreme thinness.
