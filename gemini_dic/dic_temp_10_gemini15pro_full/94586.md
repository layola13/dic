impracticablenesses:/ɪmˌpræktɪkəˈbɪlnəsɪz/| n. |不可行性；不切实际之处|The impracticablenesses of the proposal were immediately apparent.  这项提议的不可行性显而易见。|例句：The numerous impracticablenesses of the plan led to its abandonment.  该计划的诸多不可行之处导致其被放弃。|近义词：infeasibility, impossibility, unworkability|反义词:feasibility, practicality, possibility|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From impracticable + -ness.  Impracticable from in- (not) + practicable (able to be put into practice). Practicable from practic (practice) + -able (able to be).  Ultimately from Latin *practicare (to practice).
