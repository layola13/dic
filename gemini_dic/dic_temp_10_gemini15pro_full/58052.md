drolly:/ˈdroʊlli/| adv.|滑稽地，古怪地，幽默地|In a droll, amusing manner; whimsically.  Characterized by dry, subtle humor.|He drolly remarked that the weather was unusually clement for a nuclear winter.|近义词：humorously, wittily, amusingly, whimsically, comically|反义词：seriously, solemnly, gravely|词性:副词
Etymology: From French *drolle* ("funny, comical"), from Middle Dutch *drol* ("imp, goblin, buffoon").  Ultimately of uncertain origin.
