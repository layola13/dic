nondenial:/ˌnɒndɪˈnaɪəl/ | adj. | 不可否认的 |  Not able to be denied; undeniable.  Relating to the inability to deny something. | The evidence of his guilt was nondenial.  他的罪证确凿，不可否认。| The nondenial fact is that climate change is happening. 不可否认的事实是气候变化正在发生。 |Synonym: undeniable, irrefutable, indisputable | Antonym: deniable, refutable, disputable |词性: adjective
