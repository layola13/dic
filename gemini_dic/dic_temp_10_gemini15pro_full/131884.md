noncontemplativenesses: /nɒnkənˈtɛmplətɪvnəsɪz/ | n. | 非沉思性，不沉思 |  The state or quality of not being contemplative; absence of contemplation or reflection. |  His noncontemplativenesses often led him to make impulsive decisions. 他的不沉思常常导致他做出冲动的决定。| 近义词: impulsiveness, rashness, thoughtlessness | 反义词: contemplativeness, thoughtfulness, reflection | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From non- (not) + contemplative + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality) + -es (plural suffix)
