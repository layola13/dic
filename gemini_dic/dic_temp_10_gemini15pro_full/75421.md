frugivora:/fruːˈdʒɪvərə/ | n. |食果动物；食果者|An animal that feeds primarily on fruit. | Bats are sometimes frugivora, depending on the species.  蝙蝠有时是食果动物，取决于种类。|Fruit bats are frugivora and play a vital role in seed dispersal. 果蝠是食果动物，在种子传播中起着至关重要的作用。| synonyms: fruit-eater, fructivore | antonyms: carnivore, herbivore | noun
