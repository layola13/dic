overremissly:/ˌoʊvərɪˈmɪsli/ | adv. | 过于疏忽地，极度懈怠地 | In an extremely neglectful or careless manner; excessively remiss. | He overremissly handled the important documents, resulting in a significant loss for the company. 他过于疏忽地处理了重要文件，导致公司重大损失。 | Examples:
The security guard overremissly left his post, allowing the thieves to enter. 保安人员玩忽职守，让小偷进来了。

She overremissly forgot to pay her bills, resulting in late fees. 她太疏忽了，忘记付账单，导致滞纳金。

| 近义词: negligently, carelessly, irresponsibly | 反义词: diligently, carefully, responsibly | 词性: 副词


词源:

"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "remissly" (adverb form of "remiss," from Old French "remis," past participle of "remettre" meaning "to put back, place again," from Latin "remittere" meaning "to send back, slacken").  "Remiss" itself carries the sense of being negligent or careless in one's duty.
