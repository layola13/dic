funhouse:/ˈfʌnˌhaʊs/ | n. |游乐场, 鬼屋 |A building at a fairground or amusement park containing amusements and games.  A place designed to evoke amusement, often with distorting mirrors, slides, and other playful features. |例句：The children loved the funhouse at the carnival. 孩子们喜欢嘉年华的游乐场。

例句：The distorting mirrors in the funhouse made us all laugh.  鬼屋里的哈哈镜让我们都笑了。|近义词：amusement park, carnival, fairground |反义词: library, school |词性:noun
