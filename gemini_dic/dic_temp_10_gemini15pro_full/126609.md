naia:/ˈnaɪə/| n.|水仙女; (希腊神话中)居于江河湖海的仙女|A Naia is a type of water nymph in Greek mythology, associated with freshwater springs, rivers, and lakes.|The ancient Greeks believed that naiads, like Naia, protected the purity of the water sources they inhabited. 古希腊人相信水仙女，比如纳伊阿得斯，保护着她们居住的水源的纯净。|nymph, sprite| |noun
