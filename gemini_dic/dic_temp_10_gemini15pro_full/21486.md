blubberer:/ˈblʌbərər/| n.|爱哭的人，哭哭啼啼的人|A person who cries easily and excessively.|例句：He's such a blubberer; he cries at the drop of a hat. 他真是个爱哭鬼，动不动就哭。

例句：The little blubberer finally stopped crying when his mother gave him a candy.  这个小哭包在他妈妈给他一颗糖后终于不哭了。|近义词：crybaby, whiner, sniveller|反义词:stoic|词性:noun
