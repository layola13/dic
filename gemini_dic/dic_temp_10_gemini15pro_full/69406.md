feel-goodism:/ˈfiːlˌɡʊdɪzəm/| n.| 令人感觉良好的主义；乐观主义；|A focus on positive news and happy endings, sometimes at the expense of accuracy or realism.|The movie was pure feel-goodism, offering little in the way of plot or character development. 这部电影纯粹是为了让人感觉良好，在情节和人物发展方面几乎没有什么内容。|Synonyms: optimism, positivism, cheerfulness|Antonyms: pessimism, cynicism, negativity|Noun


feel-goodism:/ˈfiːlˌɡʊdɪzəm/| n.| 令人感觉良好的事物；|Something designed to make people feel good, often excessively sentimental or simplistic.|The charity's annual gala was a feel-goodism event, raising millions for a worthy cause.  该慈善机构的年度庆典是一个令人感觉良好的活动，为一项有价值的事业筹集了数百万美元。|Synonyms:  uplifting experience, heartwarming story, positive message|Antonyms: downer, depressing news, negative message|Noun
