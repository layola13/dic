elapse: /ɪˈlæps/ | v. | 流逝，过去 | Time elapses quickly when you're having fun.  当你玩得开心的时候，时间过得很快。| A few seconds elapsed before anyone spoke.  几秒钟过去了，才有人说话。| 近义词: pass, go by, slip away | 反义词: stand still |词性:动词

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Time elapses quickly. 时间过得很快。
A year elapses before they meet again. 一年过去了，他们才再次见面。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Time is elapsing as we speak.  我们说话间时间正在流逝。
More time is elapsing than we realized.  比我们意识到的时间流逝得更多。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Five years have elapsed since the incident.  事件已经过去五年了。
A considerable amount of time has elapsed.  相当长的时间已经过去了。


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
Time has been elapsing slowly while we wait. 我们等待的时候，时间一直在缓慢地流逝。
Years have been elapsing without any significant progress. 多年来一直没有取得重大进展。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
Several weeks elapsed before he received a reply.  几个星期过去了，他才收到回复。
A full year elapsed before she returned home.  整整一年过去了，她才回家。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
Time was elapsing quickly as they worked on the project.  他们忙于项目时，时间过得飞快。
Precious minutes were elapsing while they argued.  他们在争论的时候，宝贵的时间在流逝。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
Two years had elapsed before they met again. 两年过去了，他们才再次见面。
Much time had elapsed since their last conversation. 自从他们上次谈话以来，已经过去了很长时间。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
Time had been elapsing uneventfully before the news broke.  新闻爆出之前，时间一直平静地流逝着。
Decades had been elapsing without a solution to the problem.  几十年来，这个问题一直没有得到解决。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
Many years will elapse before this project is completed.  这个项目要完成还需要很多年。
Little time will elapse before the next meeting.  下次会议很快就要召开了。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
Time will be elapsing quickly as we work towards the deadline.  随着我们努力赶上截止日期，时间将过得很快。
Years will be elapsing before they see each other again.  他们要过几年才能再次见面。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next year, five years will have elapsed since the accident.  到明年，事故发生就过去五年了。
By the time we finish, hours will have elapsed.  到我们完成的时候，几个小时就过去了。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next month, I will have been working here for two years. 到下个月，我就已经在这里工作两年了。 (这个例句不太适合elapse，因为elapse通常不用于进行时态，这里提供一个其他动词的例句以满足格式要求)
By 2030, ten years will have elapsed since the new policy was implemented. 到2030年，新政策实施就将过去十年了。


虚拟语气:
If more time elapsed, we might have a better solution. 如果有更多的时间，我们可能会找到更好的解决方案。
I wish more time had elapsed before the deadline. 我希望截止日期前有更多的时间。


被动语态: (elapse没有被动语态)



疑问句:
How much time elapsed before they responded?  他们过了多久才回应？
Has much time elapsed since our last meeting?  我们上次见面以来过了很久吗？

否定句:
Not much time elapsed before the storm hit.  暴风雨来临之前没过多久。
Little time elapsed before they realized their mistake.  他们很快就意识到了自己的错误。




PHRASE->
as time elapses 随着时间的推移
before time elapses 在时间流逝之前
after time has elapsed 时间流逝之后
since time elapsed 自从时间流逝
while time elapses 当时间流逝


注意事项:

elapse主要用于正式场合，指时间段的流逝，而不是时间点的流逝。

elapse通常不用于进行时态。
