halo:/ˈheɪloʊ/ (US) /ˈheɪləʊ/ (UK)| n. |光环，晕轮；荣光|A circle of light appearing to surround the sun, moon, or other luminous body. A ring of light around the head of a holy person in a religious painting, etc., representing holiness.|The sun had a halo around it. 太阳周围有一个光环。|近义词：aura, nimbus, ring, corona|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "darkness" or "shadow" in specific contexts)|noun
