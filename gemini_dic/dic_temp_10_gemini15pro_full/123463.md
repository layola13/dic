monosaccharide:/ˌmɒnəˈsækəraɪd/ /ˌmɑːnəˈsækəraɪd/| n. |单糖|A simple sugar that constitutes the basic unit of carbohydrates; it cannot be hydrolyzed to simpler sugars.|Glucose is a common monosaccharide found in fruits. 葡萄糖是一种常见的单糖，存在于水果中。|Synonyms: simple sugar, single sugar, |Antonyms: polysaccharide|noun
