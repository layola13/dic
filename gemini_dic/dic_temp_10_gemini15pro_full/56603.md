donator:/doʊˈneɪtər/ /ˈdɒneɪtə(r)/ |n.|捐赠者，赠与人|A person who donates something, especially money to a fund or charity.|例句：The donator wished to remain anonymous.  这位捐赠者希望保持匿名。

例句：The hospital received a large sum of money from a generous donator.  这家医院收到了一位慷慨捐赠者的大笔捐款。|近义词：donor, contributor, benefactor|反义词:recipient, beneficiary|词性:noun

donator (noun): from Latin *dōnātōr*, from *dōnāre* (to give, present).  The word shares a root with "donate."
