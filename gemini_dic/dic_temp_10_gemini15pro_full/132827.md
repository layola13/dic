nonecumenical:/ˌnɒnˌɛkjuːˈmɛnɪk(ə)l/
adjective
非普世教会的；不涉及所有基督教派的
Not promoting or relating to Christian unity or ecumenism; not representative of all Christian churches or denominations.
The conference focused on nonecumenical theological debates within a specific Protestant denomination.  这次会议的重点是某个特定新教教派内部的非普世神学辩论。
The priest's nonecumenical views caused controversy in the diocese.  这位神父的非普世教会观点在教区引起了争议。

synonym:sectarian, denominational, parochial
antonym:ecumenical, universal, interdenominational
词性:adjective
