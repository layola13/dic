hyperclimax:/ˌhaɪpərˈklaɪmæks/ | n. | 超级高潮，层进式高潮 | A figure of speech in which a series of clauses or phrases is arranged in ascending order of importance, with the most important one last, often with an unexpected twist or humorous effect at the end.  A series of climaxes, each one exceeding the previous in force or intensity. |  The comedian’s joke built to a hyperclimax, leaving the audience in stitches.  这位喜剧演员的笑话达到了一个超级高潮，让观众笑得前仰后合。

The argument escalated to a hyperclimax, ending in a shouting match. 这场争论升级到了一个超级高潮，以一场叫喊比赛告终。 | 近义词: climax, escalation | 反义词: anticlimax | 词性: noun
