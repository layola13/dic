eclamptic:/ɪˈklæmp.tɪk/ | adj. |子痫的；惊厥的 |Relating to or affected with eclampsia, a condition in pregnancy characterized by convulsions. |例句：The eclamptic seizures can be life-threatening for both the mother and the baby. 子痫发作可能危及母亲和婴儿的生命。

例句：Her blood pressure was dangerously high, suggesting a possible eclamptic episode. 她的血压高得危险，表明可能出现子痫。|近义词：convulsive, seizing |反义词: stable, healthy |词性:adjective


Etymology: From French *éclamptique*, from Greek *eklampsis* "a shining forth," from *eklampein*  "to flash forth," from *ek-* "out" + *lampein* "to shine."
