abandon:/əˈbændən/| vt. n.|放弃，抛弃; 放纵|To leave completely and finally; desert.  As a noun, a state of unrestrained freedom or emotion.|He abandoned his family and ran away. 他抛弃了家人，逃跑了。

The city was abandoned after the earthquake. 地震后，这座城市被遗弃了。

She danced with wild abandon. 她狂放地跳舞。|近义词：desert, forsake, relinquish, surrender|反义词：retain, keep, maintain, cherish|词性: verb, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *abandoner*, from *a bandon* "at one's disposal, at will," from *a* "at, to" + *bandon* "power, jurisdiction." Ultimately from Frankish *band* "proclamation, ban."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They abandon their old car. = 他们丢弃了他们的旧车。
He abandons his dream of becoming a doctor. = 他放弃了成为医生的梦想。

一般过去时:
The captain abandoned the sinking ship. = 船长弃船了。
She abandoned her attempt to climb the mountain. = 她放弃了攀登这座山的尝试。

现在完成时:
He has abandoned all hope. = 他已经放弃了所有希望。
They have abandoned their original plan. = 他们已经放弃了最初的计划。


过去完成时:
The house had been abandoned for years. = 这所房子已经被废弃多年了。
He had abandoned his studies before he joined the army. = 他在参军前就放弃了学业。


将来时:
They will abandon the project if it's too expensive. = 如果太贵，他们将放弃这个项目。
She will abandon her car if it breaks down again. = 如果她的车再次抛锚，她就会把它丢弃。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would abandon this crazy idea. = 如果我是你，我会放弃这个疯狂的想法。
I wish he would abandon his bad habits. = 我希望他能改掉他的坏习惯。



被动语态:
The building was abandoned after the fire. = 火灾后，这栋楼被废弃了。
The project has been abandoned due to lack of funding. = 由于缺乏资金，该项目已被放弃。


疑问句:
Why did they abandon the ship? = 他们为什么弃船？
When was the house abandoned? = 这房子是什么时候被废弃的？


否定句:
They didn't abandon their friends in need. = 他们没有抛弃需要帮助的朋友。
She will not abandon her principles. = 她不会放弃她的原则。
