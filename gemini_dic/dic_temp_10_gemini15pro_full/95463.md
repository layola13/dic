incitants:/ɪnˈsaɪtənts/
noun
煽动者; 刺激物
Something that incites or provokes action, feeling, or thought.
The inflammatory speech acted as an incitant to the riot. 这场煽动性的演讲成了引发骚乱的导火索。
Synonyms: stimulus, provocation, impetus, instigation, incitement
Antonyms: deterrent, inhibitor, suppressant
词性: 名词
