ginniest:/ˈɡɪniɪst/
adjective
最像杜松子酒的; 最有杜松子酒味道的
Most resembling or characteristic of gin.
The ginniest drink I've ever had. 我喝过最有杜松子酒味道的饮料。
近义词: gin-like
反义词: (No clear antonym exists, perhaps "least gin-like")
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
Derived from "gin" (the alcoholic beverage) + "-iest" (superlative suffix). "Gin" ultimately comes from the Dutch word "jenever," referring to juniper, the primary flavoring of gin.
