lightstruck:/ˈlaɪtˌstrʌk/| adj.|曝光过度的，光照损坏的|Damaged by excessive exposure to light, especially of wine or beer, resulting in an unpleasant taste or smell.|The wine was lightstruck and undrinkable. 这酒曝光过度，不能喝了。|近义词:skunked, spoiled, tainted|反义词:fresh, unspoiled|adjective

lightstruck:/ˈlaɪtˌstrʌk/| adj.|神魂颠倒的，茫然的|Dazed or confused as if by a blow.|He wandered around, lightstruck, after the accident. 事故发生后，他茫然地四处游荡。|近义词:dazed, bewildered, stunned|反义词:alert, aware, conscious|adjective
