fractionate:/ˈfrækʃəneɪt/ (US), /ˈfrækʃəneɪt/ (UK)| v. |分馏，分级，分离|To separate something into different parts or fractions, especially by distillation. |The chemist fractionated the crude oil into gasoline, kerosene, and other products. 化学家将原油分馏成汽油、煤油和其他产品。

The mixture was fractionated by column chromatography. 混合物用柱层析法分馏。|近义词：separate, divide, split, refine, distill|反义词：combine, mix, blend|词性：动词

fractionation:/ˌfrækʃəˈneɪʃən/ (US), /ˌfrækʃəˈneɪʃən/ (UK)| n. |分馏，分级，分离|The act or process of fractionating. |The fractionation of crude oil is an essential process in the petroleum industry. 原油的分馏是石油工业中的一个重要过程。

Protein fractionation is commonly used in biochemistry. 蛋白质分馏在生物化学中很常用。|近义词：separation, division, splitting, refinement, distillation|反义词：combination, mixture, blending|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *fractionatus*, past participle of *fractionare* "to break," from Latin *fractio* "a breaking," from *frangere* "to break."
