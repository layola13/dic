beadily:/ˈbiːdɪli/
adverb
锐利地；炯炯有神地；警惕地
In a sharp, watchful, and alert manner, often suggesting intense scrutiny or suspicion.
He watched her beadily, his eyes narrowed. 他目不转睛地盯着她，眼睛眯成了一条缝。
近义词: watchfully, sharply, intently, piercingly, hawk-like
反义词: unobservantly, vaguely, distractedly
词性: 副词

beadily:/ˈbiːdɪli/
adjective
锐利的；炯炯有神的；警惕的
Having or showing sharp, watchful eyes; observant.  This usage is less common than the adverbial form.
His beadily eyes missed nothing. 他锐利的眼睛什么也没错过。
近义词: sharp, watchful, alert, keen, observant
反义词: unobservant, inattentive, distracted
词性: 形容词
