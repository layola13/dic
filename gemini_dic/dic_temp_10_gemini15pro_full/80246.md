gof:/ɡɒf/ /ɡɑːf/| n.|高尔夫球|A game played on a large outdoor course with clubs and a small hard ball. The object is to hit the ball into a series of small holes in the ground in as few strokes as possible.|例句：He plays gof every Sunday. 他每个星期天都打高尔夫球。

例句：She's a keen gof player. 她是一位热衷于高尔夫球的球员。|近义词：golfing|反义词: (无直接反义词)
