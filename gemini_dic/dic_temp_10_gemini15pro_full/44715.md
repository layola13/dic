cruelnesses:/ˈkruːəltiːz/
n.
残忍行为；残酷；暴行
acts of being cruel; instances of inflicting pain or suffering
The history books are filled with the cruelnesses of war. 历史书中充满了战争的残酷。
The cruelnesses inflicted upon the prisoners were appalling. 对囚犯施加的暴行令人震惊。
近义词:atrocities, barbarities, brutalities
反义词:kindnesses, mercies, compassions
词性:名词

cruelty:/ˈkruːəlti/
n.
残忍；残酷行为
the quality of being cruel; behavior that causes pain or suffering
He was known for his cruelty to animals. 他以虐待动物而闻名。
The cruelty of the situation was unbearable.  这种情况的残酷令人难以忍受。
近义词:brutality, inhumanity, savagery
反义词:kindness, compassion, mercy
词性:名词

cruel:/ˈkruːəl/
adj.
残忍的；残酷的
willfully causing pain or suffering to others, or feeling no concern about it
It was a cruel joke to play on her.  对她来说，这是一个残酷的玩笑。
He was a cruel and heartless man. 他是一个残忍无情的人。
近义词:brutal, harsh, callous
反义词:kind, compassionate, merciful
词性:形容词
