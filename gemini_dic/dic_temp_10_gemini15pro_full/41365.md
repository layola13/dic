corkiness:/ˈkɔːrkɪnɪs/
n.
软木塞味；木塞味；呆滞
A dry, astringent, woody taste in a wine, often caused by a contaminated cork.  Also, a general lack of liveliness or expressiveness.
The wine had a distinct corkiness that ruined the flavor. 这酒明显的软木塞味破坏了味道。
The presentation lacked energy and had a certain corkiness.  这个演讲缺乏活力，显得有些呆板。
近义词:mustiness, staleness, flatness, lifelessness
反义词:liveliness, vibrancy, freshness
词性:noun

corkiness:/ˈkɔːrkɪnɪs/
n.
软木塞性质; 似软木塞的性质
The quality of resembling cork, especially in texture or lightness.
The material had a corkiness that made it ideal for insulation. 这种材料具有类似软木的性质，使其成为理想的绝缘材料。
He noticed the corkiness of the soil as he dug in the garden.  当他在花园里挖土时，他注意到土壤像软木一样。
近义词:sponginess, porousness
反义词:density, compactness
词性:noun
