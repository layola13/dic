behead:/bɪˈhed/| vt.|斩首，砍头|To cut off the head of; decapitate. |The king ordered the traitor to be beheaded. 国王下令将叛徒斩首。

The executioner beheaded the prisoner. 死刑执行人将囚犯斩首。|近义词：decapitate, execute, guillotine|反义词: spare, pardon|动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + head.  "Be-" is a prefix meaning "to make" or "to cause to be."  So, "behead" literally means "to cause to be without a head." The word is first attested in the late 14th century.
