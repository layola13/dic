bursitises:/bɜːrˈsaɪtiːz/
n.
滑囊炎（复数）
Inflammation of a bursa, a small fluid-filled sac that cushions joints.
She suffers from bursitises in both her knees. 她双膝都患有滑囊炎。
近义词:inflammations
反义词:(None -  lack of inflammation isn't a direct opposite)
词性:noun


bursitis:/bɜːrˈsaɪtɪs/
n.
滑囊炎
Inflammation of a bursa, a small fluid-filled sac that cushions joints.
He developed bursitis in his shoulder from overuse.  他因为过度使用而患上了肩部滑囊炎。
近义词:inflammation
反义词:(None - lack of inflammation isn't a direct opposite)
词性:noun


词源: From Modern Latin *bursītis, from bursa (“bursa”) +‎ -itis.  "Bursa" itself comes from Late Latin, meaning "purse, bag," and ultimately from Greek βύρσα (búrsa), "hide, skin."
