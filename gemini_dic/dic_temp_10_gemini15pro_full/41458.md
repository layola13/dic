cornier:/ˈkɔːrniər/| adj.|更老套的，更陈腐的|Comparative form of corny: more corny.  Meaning excessively sentimental, banal, or mawkish.|The jokes in that movie were even cornier than the first one. 那部电影里的笑话比第一部更老套。|Synonyms: cheesier, mushier, sappier, schmaltzier|Antonyms: sophisticated, tasteful, refined|adjective

corny:/ˈkɔːrni/| adj.|老套的，陈腐的|Excessively sentimental, banal, or mawkish.|His pickup lines were so corny. 他的搭讪词太老套了。|Synonyms: cheesy, mushy, sappy, schmaltzy|Antonyms: sophisticated, tasteful, refined|adjective
