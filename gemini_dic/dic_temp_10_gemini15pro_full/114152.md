mahdi: /ˈmɑːdi/ (US), /ˈmædi/ (UK) | n. | 马赫迪，救世主 | A spiritual and temporal leader who, according to Islamic belief, will appear at the end of time to rid the world of evil and injustice. |  Example 1: Muslims believe in the coming of the Mahdi.  穆斯林相信马赫迪的到来。

Example 2: The Mahdi is a central figure in Islamic eschatology. 马赫迪是伊斯兰末世论中的核心人物。 | Near synonyms: messiah, savior, redeemer | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider terms like "destroyer," "tyrant" in a contrasting context) | Noun
