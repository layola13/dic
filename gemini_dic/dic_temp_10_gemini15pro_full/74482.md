franking:/ˈfræŋkɪŋ/
n.
特许邮寄; 免贴邮票权; 盖销邮票
The act of franking a letter or other postal matter.  The privilege of sending mail free of charge.
The CEO enjoys the franking privilege for company business. 首席执行官享有公司业务的免贴邮票特权。

近义词: free postage, prepayment of postage
反义词: postage due

词性: noun

词源: From Frank (free), referring to the free postage privilege historically granted to certain officials.
