entailable:/ɪnˈteɪləbl/
adjective
可推导的，必然伴随的
Something that is entailable is necessarily true as a consequence of something else being true.
If a statement is true, then its logical consequence is entailable. 如果一个陈述是真的，那么它的逻辑结果是可推导的。
The truth of the premise entails the truth of the conclusion. 前提的真实性必然导致结论的真实性。
近义词: inferable, deducible, consequent
反义词:  None readily available.  The opposite concept might be expressed with phrases like "not necessarily true" or "independent."
词性: adjective
