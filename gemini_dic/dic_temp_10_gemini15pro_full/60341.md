egomanias: /ˌiːɡoʊˈmeɪniəz/ | n. | 极度自负，极端利己主义 | An obsessive preoccupation with oneself; excessive vanity and self-importance.  It's the plural form of "egomania." | His egomanias alienated even his closest friends. 他的极端自负甚至疏远了他最亲密的朋友。 | Synonyms: narcissism, vanity, self-centeredness, egotism | Antonyms: humility, modesty, selflessness | Noun
