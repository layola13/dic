nannies:/ˈnæniz/
noun
保姆（nanny的复数）
A person, typically a woman, employed to care for a child or children in their own home.
The nannies took the children to the park. 保姆们带孩子们去了公园。
The family employs two nannies to care for their children. 这家人雇佣了两个保姆来照顾他们的孩子。
近义词:childminders, babysitters, au pairs
反义词:parents
词性:noun
