infertile:/ɪnˈfɜːrtaɪl/ /ɪnˈfɝːtəl/ | adj. |不育的，不肥沃的|Not fertile; incapable of producing offspring (of a person, animal, or plant); not capable of sustaining crops (of land).|

例句：
The couple were infertile and sought medical advice.
这对夫妇不育，寻求医疗建议。

The land was infertile and unsuitable for farming.
这片土地贫瘠，不适合耕种。


近义词：sterile, barren, unproductive|
反义词：fertile, productive, fecund|
词性：adjective|

词源：
From in- (not) + fertile (capable of producing offspring or vegetation).  "Fertile" comes from Latin fertilis, from ferre "to bear, carry."
