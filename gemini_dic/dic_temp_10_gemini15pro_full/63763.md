epiclinal:/ˌɛpɪˈklaɪnəl/
adjective
向地表倾斜的；顺地层走向的
Relating to or denoting a geological structure in which the strata dip in the same direction as the slope of the land surface.
The epiclinal nature of the rock formations made it easy to identify the direction of ancient water flow.  向地表倾斜的岩层结构使得识别古代水流方向变得容易。
The geologist identified the valley as having an epiclinal structure. 地质学家确定该山谷具有顺地层走向的结构。
Near-synonym: homoclinal
Antonym: anticlinal
