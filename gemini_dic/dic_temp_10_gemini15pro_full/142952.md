outact:/ˌaʊtˈækt/
verb
胜过；比...演得好
to perform better than
例句：She outacted everyone else in the play. 她在剧中比其他所有人都演得好。
例句：He was completely outacted by his co-star. 他的演技完全被他的搭档盖过了。
近义词：outperform, surpass, excel
反义词：underperform
词性：verb
