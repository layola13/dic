The word "entryng" is likely a misspelling of "entering."  Therefore, the following analysis is for "entering."

entering:/ˈɛntərɪŋ/ | adj. | 进入的，正要开始的 | Present participle of "enter," signifying the act of going into a place or beginning something. |  例句：He is entering the building. 他正在进入大楼。| 近义词：incoming, arriving, commencing | 反义词: leaving, exiting, departing | verb (present participle)


entering:/ˈɛntərɪŋ/ | n. | 进入，入口 | The act of going in.  This usage is less common than the adjectival form. | 例句：The entering of the stadium was chaotic.  进入体育场一片混乱。| 近义词：entrance, entry, ingress | 反义词: exit, egress | noun
