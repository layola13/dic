noncomplaisantly:/ˌnɒnkəmˈplaɪzəntli/
adverb|不服从地，不顺从地|In a manner that is not compliant; stubbornly or rebelliously. |She noncomplaisantly refused to sign the document. 她不服从地拒绝签署文件。|He noncomplaisantly ignored the direct order. 他不服从地无视了直接的命令。|Synonyms: defiantly, rebelliously, disobediently, contumaciously|Antonyms: obediently, submissively, compliantly
词性: 副词
