doze off:/doʊz ˈɔːf/ |vi.| 打瞌睡，睡着 |To fall into a light sleep, especially unintentionally. | Example 1: I dozed off during the movie. 我在看电影的时候打瞌睡了。

Example 2:  The lecturer was so boring that several students dozed off. 演讲者太无聊了，几个学生都打瞌睡了。 |Synonyms: nod off, drift off, drop off, fall asleep |Antonyms: wake up, stay awake |Verb
