nonarchitecturally:/ˌnɒnˌɑːrkɪˈtektʃərəli/
adjective
非建筑地；非建筑学地
Not in an architectural manner; without regard to architectural principles.
The building was nonarchitecturally modified to fit the new equipment. 这栋建筑被非建筑式地修改以适应新设备。
The space was divided nonarchitecturally, resulting in an awkward layout.  这个空间被非建筑式地划分，导致布局很别扭。
Synonym: inartistically, unskillfully
Antonym: architecturally, aesthetically
词性: adjective
