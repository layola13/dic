fermillet:/ˈfɜːrmɪlɪt/
noun
珍珠粟，御谷
A cereal grass widely cultivated in warm regions of the Old World. Its grain is used as food for humans and livestock.
Fermillet is a drought-tolerant crop. 珍珠粟是一种耐旱作物。
