foaminesses:/ˈfoʊminɪsɪz/
noun
泡沫性；起泡沫的性质
The quality of being foamy or producing foam.
The foaminesses of the detergent made it difficult to rinse off.  这种洗涤剂的泡沫太多，很难冲洗干净。
The various foaminesses of the different beers were compared.  比较了不同啤酒的各种泡沫程度。
近义词: frothiness, spumescence
反义词: flatness
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From foamy + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *faimaz.
