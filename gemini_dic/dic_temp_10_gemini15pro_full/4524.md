albuminuria:/ˌælbjʊmɪˈnjʊəriə/| n. |蛋白尿|The presence of albumin in the urine, typically a sign of kidney disease.|Example: The doctor detected albuminuria in the patient's urine sample, suggesting a potential kidney problem. 医生在病人的尿液样本中检测到蛋白尿，提示可能存在肾脏问题。|Example:  Persistent albuminuria can be an indicator of diabetic nephropathy.  持续性蛋白尿可能是糖尿病肾病的一个指标。|近义词:proteinuria|反义词: (No direct antonym, absence of albuminuria implies normal kidney function)|词性:noun
