mischarging:/ˌmɪsˈtʃɑːrdʒɪŋ/
|n.|错误收费，过度收费；充电不足|The act of charging incorrectly, either by overcharging or undercharging.  This can refer to financial transactions or the charging of batteries.|
|v.|错误地收费，过度收费；充电不足|(present participle of mischarge) Charging incorrectly, either by overcharging or undercharging. This can refer to financial transactions or the charging of batteries.|


例句：
The customer complained about the mischarging on their bill.
顾客抱怨账单上的收费错误。

The mischarging of the battery led to its premature failure.
电池充电不足导致其过早失效。


近义词：overcharging, undercharging, incorrect billing, faulty charging
反义词：correct charging, accurate billing


词性: noun, verb (present participle)
词源: mis- (prefix meaning "wrong" or "bad") + charge (verb meaning "to demand payment")
