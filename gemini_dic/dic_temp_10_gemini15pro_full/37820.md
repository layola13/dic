compensative:/kəmˈpɛnsətɪv/| adj.|补偿性的，赔偿的|Serving to compensate; making up for a loss or deficiency. | The government offered compensative measures for the flood victims. 政府为洪灾受害者提供了补偿措施。|Example:  He received a compensative payment for his injuries. 他因伤获得了赔偿金。|Synonyms: compensatory, remedial, restorative|Antonyms: punitive, penalizing|adjective


compensative:/kəmˈpɛnsətɪv/| n.|补偿物，赔偿物 (较少用)|Something that compensates. (This usage is less common than the adjective form.)| The company offered a compensative package to the laid-off workers.  公司向被解雇的工人提供了一揽子补偿方案。|Example:  The insurance company offered a compensative for the damages. 保险公司为损失提供了赔偿。|Synonyms: compensation, recompense, restitution|Antonyms: penalty, forfeiture|noun
