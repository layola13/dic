nectarial:/nɛkˈtɛəriəl/
adjective
蜜腺的;分泌花蜜的
Relating to, producing, or resembling nectar; secreting nectar.
The nectarial glands of the flower attract pollinators. 花的蜜腺吸引传粉者。
Near Antonyms: nectarless
