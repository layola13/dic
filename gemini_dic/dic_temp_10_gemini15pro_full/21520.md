blue-sky:/ˈbluː skaɪ/| adj.|蓝天的，理想主义的，不切实际的|Relating to or involving visionary or impractical ideas or schemes.  Often used to describe thinking that is not grounded in reality.|例句：They had a blue-sky meeting to brainstorm new product ideas. 他们开了一个蓝天会议，集思广益，构想新产品创意。

例句：The blue-sky proposal was ultimately rejected as too expensive. 这个不切实际的提案最终因为过于昂贵而被拒绝了。|近义词：idealistic, utopian, visionary, impractical|反义词：realistic, pragmatic, practical|词性:adjective

Etymology:  A compound of "blue" and "sky," referring to the open and limitless nature of the sky, thus metaphorically extended to describe thinking that is similarly unbound by practical constraints.
