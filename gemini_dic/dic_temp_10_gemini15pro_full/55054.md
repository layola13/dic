disquisitory:/dɪˈskwɪzɪtɔːri/ /dɪˈskwɪzətɔːri/
adjective
探讨的；论述的；爱议论的
inclined to discussion or debate; argumentative.  Characterized by formal discourse or investigation.
例句：He adopted a disquisitory tone in his lecture.  他讲课时采用了一种探讨的语气。
例句：The book takes a disquisitory approach to the topic of artificial intelligence. 这本书对人工智能主题采取了论述的方式。
近义词：analytical, argumentative, discursive, investigative, inquisitive
反义词：conclusive, definitive, dogmatic, unquestioning
词性: adjective
