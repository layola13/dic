agouti:/əˈɡuːti/ | adj. | 栗鼠色的 | Of a brownish grey speckled colour. |  Her hair was an agouti shade of brown. 她的头发是栗鼠色的棕色。 | 近义词: grizzled, speckled | 反义词: solid, uniform | 词性: adjective
