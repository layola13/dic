noncereal:/ˌnɑːnˈsɪriəl/
adj.|非谷物(制)的；非谷类食物的|Made from something other than grain; not a cereal.|Many people choose noncereal breakfast options like yogurt or eggs. 很多人选择酸奶或鸡蛋等非谷物早餐。|non-grain|cereal
词性：形容词

noncereal:/ˌnɑːnˈsɪriəl/
n.|非谷物食物|Food made from something other than grain.|Yogurt and fruit is a popular noncereal breakfast. 酸奶和水果是一种受欢迎的非谷类早餐。|non-grain food|cereal
词性：名词
