hussite:/ˈhʌsaɪt/| n. adj.|胡斯派教徒；胡斯派的|A follower of Jan Hus, a Czech religious reformer of the 15th century who advocated for church reforms and whose teachings influenced the Protestant Reformation.  Can also be used as an adjective to describe things related to Huss or his followers.|例句：The Hussites fought several wars against the Catholic Church. 胡斯派教徒与天主教会进行了数次战争。|近义词：reformer, Protestant, iconoclast|反义词:Catholic, traditionalist|词性:noun, adjective

hussite:/ˈhʌsaɪt/| adj.|胡斯派的|Relating to Jan Hus or his followers.|例句：The Hussite Wars had a profound impact on the religious landscape of Europe. 胡斯战争对欧洲的宗教格局产生了深远的影响。|近义词：Hussitic|反义词:Catholic|词性:adjective
