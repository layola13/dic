hydrencephalocele:/ˌhaɪdrənˈsɛfəloʊˌsiːl/
n.
脑积水膨出
A congenital neural tube defect characterized by herniation of brain tissue containing cerebrospinal fluid through a defect in the cranium.
The infant was born with a large hydrencephalocele at the back of its head.  这个婴儿出生时后脑勺有一个很大的脑积水膨出。
近义词: encephalocele, craniocele
反义词: None (This is a medical condition; it doesn't have a direct opposite.)
词性: noun

hydrencephaloid:/ˌhaɪdrənˈsɛfəˌlɔɪd/
adj.
类脑积水的
Resembling or characteristic of hydrencephalus (hydrocephalus).  Marked by sunken eyes and fontanelles, and symptoms like dehydration, though not related to true hydrocephalus.
The baby's sunken fontanelle gave it a hydrencephaloid appearance. 婴儿凹陷的囟门使其看起来像脑积水。
近义词: hydrocephaloid
反义词: None
词性: adjective

hydrencephalus:/ˌhaɪdrənˈsɛfələs/
n.
脑积水
An abnormal buildup of cerebrospinal fluid (CSF) in the ventricles of the brain.
The child was diagnosed with hydrencephalus and required surgery to drain the excess fluid.  这个孩子被诊断出患有脑积水，需要手术来排出多余的液体。
近义词: hydrocephalus
反义词: None
词性: noun


词源:
Hydro- (Greek: water) + enkephalos (Greek: brain)  + -oid (Greek: resembling) / -cele (Greek: hernia, swelling) / -us (noun suffix)
