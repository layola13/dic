ghostwrote:/ˈɡəʊstˌrəʊt/ | v. |代笔，为人捉刀 |To write a book, speech, etc. for someone else, who then claims to be the author. |Example 1: He ghostwrote the autobiography of the famous athlete. 他为那位著名的运动员代笔写了自传。

Example 2:  She ghostwrote several speeches for the politician. 她为这位政客代笔写了几篇演讲稿。 | 近义词: authored for, penned for | 反义词: (No direct antonym, consider) wrote oneself, authored |词性: 动词
