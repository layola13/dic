infuser:/ɪnˈfjuːzər/ | n. | 注入器，浸泡器 | A device used to steep herbs, tea leaves, or fruit in hot water to extract their flavor. | I use an infuser to make loose leaf tea. 我用浸泡器泡散叶茶。 |  She added sliced lemons to her water infuser for a refreshing drink.  她在她的水注入器里加入了柠檬片，以获得清爽的饮料。| synonym: strainer, filter, diffuser | antonym: N/A | noun
