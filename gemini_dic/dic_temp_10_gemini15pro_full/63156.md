ensulphur:/ɪnˈsʌlfər/ /ɛnˈsʌlfər/
verb
使硫化；用硫磺处理；使充满硫磺味
to treat or impregnate with sulphur.
例句：The workers ensulphured the vines to protect them from mildew. 工人们用硫磺处理葡萄藤以防止霉变。

例句：The fumes from the volcano ensulphured the air. 火山喷出的烟雾使空气中充满了硫磺味。
近义词：sulphurize, sulfurate
反义词: N/A
词性: verb
