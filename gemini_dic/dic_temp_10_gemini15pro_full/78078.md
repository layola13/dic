geodetical:/ˌdʒiːəˈdɛtɪk(ə)l/
adjective
大地测量的
Relating to geodesy, the science of accurately measuring and understanding the Earth's shape, size, and gravitational field.
Geodetical surveys are essential for mapping and navigation.  大地测量对于测绘和导航至关重要。
Modern GPS systems rely on geodetical data. 现代GPS系统依赖于大地测量数据。
近义词:geodetic
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "inaccurate" in context)
词性: adjective
