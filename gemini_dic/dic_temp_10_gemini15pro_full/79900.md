gnaw:/nɔː/ (British), /nɑː/ (American)| n. | 啃咬；痛苦，焦虑 |The act of gnawing or the feeling of anxiety or worry.|The gnaw of hunger kept him awake. 饥饿的折磨让他无法入睡。|Synonyms: bite, pang, worry |Antonyms: comfort, peace|noun
