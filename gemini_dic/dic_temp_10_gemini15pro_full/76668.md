galvanizations:/ˌɡælvənaɪˈzeɪʃənz/
|n.|镀锌，激励，鼓舞|The process of applying a protective zinc coating to iron or steel to prevent rust. Also, the act of stimulating or exciting someone or something into action.|
例句：The galvanizations on the bridge protected it from corrosion. 桥上的镀锌层保护它免受腐蚀。

例句：The coach's speech provided the galvanizations the team needed to win. 教练的讲话给了球队获胜所需的激励。
|近义词：coatings, stimulation, inspiration|
|反义词：corrosion, discouragement, apathy|
|词性：名词|
