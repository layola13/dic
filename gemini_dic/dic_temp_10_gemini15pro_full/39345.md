conjointly:/kənˈdʒɔɪntli/
adverb
共同地；联合地
Together; in conjunction with; in combination with.
The two companies worked conjointly on the project.  两家公司在这个项目上共同合作。
The report was prepared conjointly by the two departments. 这份报告是由两个部门联合准备的。
近义词: together, jointly, collectively, combinedly
反义词: separately, individually, independently
词性: adverb

词源:
From Late Latin *conjunctē, from Latin conjunctus ("joined together"), past participle of conjungere ("to join together"). Equivalent to con- +‎ jointly.
