cagily:/ˈkeɪdʒɪli/| adv.|谨慎地，狡猾地|In a cautious and secretive manner, often with the intent to deceive or manipulate.  It suggests a sly and cunning approach.|He cagily avoided answering the direct question. 他谨慎地回避了直接回答这个问题。|近义词：slyly, cunningly, craftily, surreptitiously, stealthily|反义词：openly, honestly, frankly, forthrightly|词性:副词
Etymology: From "cagy" + "-ly." "Cagy" is of uncertain origin, possibly from Scottish or northern English dialect.
