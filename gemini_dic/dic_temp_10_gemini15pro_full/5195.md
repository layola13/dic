alliance: /əˈlaɪəns/ | n. |关联，联系|A connection or relationship between people or groups. |  There is a close alliance between the two families. 这两个家族之间关系密切。 | synonyms: connection, relationship, association, link | antonyms: disconnection, separation | noun
