anticlinorium:/ˌæntiklaɪˈnɔːriəm/ | n. | 背斜群，复背斜 | A large anticlinal structure composed of many smaller folds. A regional uplift or dome formed by a group of smaller anticlines. | The Appalachian Mountains are a prime example of an anticlinorium. 阿巴拉契亚山脉是背斜群的一个主要例子。| The geologists identified the structure as an anticlinorium rather than a simple anticline. 地质学家将该结构确定为背斜群而不是简单的背斜。| synonym: arch, uplift | antonym: synclinorium | noun
