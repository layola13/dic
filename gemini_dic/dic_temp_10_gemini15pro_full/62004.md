enamelists:/ɪˈnæməlɪsts/
n.
珐琅匠；搪瓷匠；瓷釉工人
A person who applies enamel to metal, glass, or pottery.
The enamelists carefully applied the vibrant colors to the copper surface. 珐琅匠小心地将鲜艳的色彩涂在铜的表面上。
近义词：enameller
反义词：（无明显的反义词）
词性：名词

enamel:/ɪˈnæml/
n.
珐琅；瓷釉；搪瓷
A glassy substance, usually opaque, applied by fusion to the surface of metal, glass, or pottery. Also, the hard, glossy surface of a tooth.
The enamel on the antique vase was intricately detailed. 古董花瓶上的珐琅图案复杂精细。
近义词：glaze, lacquer
反义词：（无明显的反义词）
词性：名词

enamel:/ɪˈnæml/
vt.
涂以瓷釉；上珐琅
To coat or decorate with enamel.
They enameled the jewelry with a delicate floral design. 他们在珠宝上涂上了精致的花卉图案。
近义词：glaze, lacquer
反义词：（无明显的反义词）
词性：动词


词源:
From Old French *esmaillier (Modern French émailler), from esmail "enamel," from Frankish *smalt, from Proto-Germanic *smeltaną "to smelt, melt."
