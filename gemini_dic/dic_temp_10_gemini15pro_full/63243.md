enterprise: /ˈentərpraɪz/ (US) /ˈentəpraɪz/ (UK)| n.| 企业; 事业; 创业; 进取心; |A project or undertaking, typically one that is difficult or requires effort.  A company or business. |例句：She embarked on a new business enterprise. 她开始了新的商业企业。|例句：The enterprise quickly expanded into new markets. 该企业迅速扩展到新的市场。|近义词：venture, undertaking, business, company, firm, initiative, ambition, drive |反义词: apathy, laziness, idleness |词性: noun

etymology-> From Old French enterprise "undertaking," from entreprendre "undertake," from entre- "between" + prendre "to take."
