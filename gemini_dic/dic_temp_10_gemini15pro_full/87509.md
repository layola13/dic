heterocyst:/ˈhɛtərəˌsɪst/| n. |异形胞|A specialized cell in certain filamentous cyanobacteria that is the site of nitrogen fixation.|Example 1: Heterocysts are essential for nitrogen fixation in some cyanobacteria. 异形胞在某些蓝细菌的固氮过程中至关重要。

Example 2: The formation of heterocysts is triggered by nitrogen starvation. 氮饥饿会触发异形胞的形成。|近义词: specialized cell, nitrogen-fixing cell|反义词: vegetative cell|词性: noun
