engravings: /ɪnˈɡreɪvɪŋz/
n.
雕刻品；版画；铭文
Pictures or words cut into a hard surface, especially as decoration or to make a print.
The museum has a collection of 18th-century engravings.  这家博物馆收藏了18世纪的版画。
The engravings on the tombstones were barely legible. 墓碑上的铭文几乎难以辨认。
近义词: carvings, etchings, inscriptions, prints
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "plain surface")
词性: noun
