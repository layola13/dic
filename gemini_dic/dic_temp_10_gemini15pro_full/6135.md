amiablest:/ˈeɪmiəblɪst/| adj.|最和蔼可亲的|most amiable; having or showing pleasant, good-natured personal qualities; friendly.|He had the amiablest of smiles. 他有着最和蔼可亲的笑容。|近义词：friendliest, most agreeable, most affable, most cordial, most genial|反义词：unfriendliest, most disagreeable, most unpleasant|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle French amiable, from Latin amicabilis (“friendly”), from amicus (“friend”).  The -est suffix is the superlative form.
