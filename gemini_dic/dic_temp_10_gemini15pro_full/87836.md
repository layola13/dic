hidalgo:/iˈðælɡoʊ/ (US), /ɪˈdælɡəʊ/ (UK)| n.| 贵族，绅士（尤指西班牙的）| A man of noble birth or social position, especially in Spain.  Originally, a hidalgo was a member of the Spanish lower nobility, ranking below a noble but above a commoner. The term derives from the phrase "hijo de algo" (son of something), indicating a person of some substance or standing.| He was a hidalgo from a distinguished family. 他是一位出身名门的西班牙贵族。|近义词：nobleman, gentleman, aristocrat|反义词: commoner, peasant|词性:noun
