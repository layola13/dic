gyte: /ɡaɪt/
n.
spawning ground; the place where fish deposit their eggs
The salmon return to their gyte to spawn.  鲑鱼回到它们的产卵地产卵。
The gyte was teeming with fish. 产卵地充满了鱼。
近义词: spawning ground, breeding ground, redd (specifically for salmonids)
反义词:  None (A gyte is a specific location, it doesn't have a direct opposite.)
词性: noun

Etymology: From Old Norse *gyta (“to pour”), from Proto-Germanic *geutaną (“to pour”). Cognate with Old English gēotan (“to pour”).
