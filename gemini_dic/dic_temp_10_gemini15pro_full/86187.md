heated:/ˈhiːtɪd/| adj.|激烈的，热烈的，加热的|Characterized by anger, excitement, or passion; made hot.|The debate became heated as the candidates argued over the key issues. 辩论随着候选人就关键问题进行争论而变得激烈起来.|近义词：intense, fervent, passionate, hot|反义词：calm, cool, peaceful, cold|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From heat (v.) + -ed.  The verb "heat" comes from Old English *hætan "to make hot," from Proto-Germanic *haitan, from Proto-Indo-European *kait- "to glow, be hot."
