coastline:/ˈkoʊstlaɪn/| n. |海岸线|The outline of a coast, especially with regard to its shape and appearance.|The rugged coastline of the Pacific Northwest is breathtaking. 太平洋西北部的崎岖海岸线令人叹为观止。|The coastline stretched for miles. 海岸线绵延数英里。|近义词：shore, seashore, coast|反义词:|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From coast + line.  "Coast" comes from Old French "coste" (rib, side, flank), ultimately from Latin "costa" (rib). "Line" comes from Old French "ligne," from Latin "linea" (linen thread, string, line).
