institutionally:/ˌɪnstɪˈtuːʃənəli/
adverb|制度上地；机构上地|In a way that relates to or is part of a large and important organization, such as a government, university, or bank.|例句：The problem is being addressed institutionally. 这个问题正在从制度上得到解决。|例句：Institutionally, the company is committed to diversity.  这家公司在制度上致力于多元化。|近义词：organizationally, formally, officially, systematically|反义词：informally, individually, personally|词性: 副词

词源:
From institution + -al + -ly.  "Institution" comes from the Latin "institutio," meaning "establishment" or "instruction."
