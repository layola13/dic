channellers:/ˈtʃænələrz/| n. |通灵者，灵媒|A person who claims to be able to communicate with spirits or other entities from a non-physical realm.|She consulted a group of channellers hoping to contact her deceased husband. 她咨询了一群灵媒，希望能联系上她已故的丈夫。|Synonyms: mediums, psychics, spiritualists|Antonyms: skeptics, doubters|noun
