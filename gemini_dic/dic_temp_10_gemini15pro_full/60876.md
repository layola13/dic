eleidin:/ɪˈlɛɪɪdɪn/ | n. | 角蛋白|A semi-fluid, intracellular protein found in the stratum lucidum of the epidermis, thought to be a precursor to keratin.|Eleidin is found in the clear layer of the skin and helps to form the protective barrier.|近义词:keratohyalin|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially proteins involved in earlier stages of skin cell differentiation)|词性:noun
