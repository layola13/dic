pastorate:/ˈpɑːstərət/ | n. | 牧师的职位；牧师任期；教区 | The period of time that a pastor serves a church or the area or position a pastor serves. |  He began his pastorate in a small rural community. 他在一个小的农村社区开始了他的牧师生涯。

The pastorate requires a deep commitment to the congregation. 牧师的职位需要对会众的深深承诺。

| 近义词：ministry, priesthood, clergy | 反义词：laity | 词性：名词
