PHRASE->
Gloria in excelsis Deo = Glory to God in the highest (a common phrase in Christian liturgy)  愿荣耀归于至高之上的神（基督教礼拜仪式中的常用语）
In Gloria Patri = In glory to the Father (another liturgical phrase)  荣耀归于圣父 (另一个礼拜用语)
Soli Deo gloria = Glory to God alone (a theological term)  唯独荣耀归于上帝（神学用语）
Gloria Dei = The glory of God (a theological concept)  上帝的荣耀（神学概念）
Gloria mundi = The glory of the world (often used ironically to refer to fleeting worldly fame)  世间的荣耀（常被讽刺地用来指短暂的世俗名声）
