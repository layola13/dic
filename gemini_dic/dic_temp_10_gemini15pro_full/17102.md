batsmanship:/ˈbætsmənʃɪp/| n.|击球技巧；击球技术|The skill of a batsman in cricket, especially with regard to technique and the ability to score runs.  A batsman's skill in hitting the ball.|His batsmanship was superb in the last match. 他在上一场比赛中击球技术非常出色。|近义词: batting, hitting |反义词: bowling |词性: noun
