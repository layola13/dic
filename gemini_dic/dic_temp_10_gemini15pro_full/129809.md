non-governmental:/ˌnɒnɡʌvənˈmentl/ | adj. | 非政府的；民间性质的 | Not run or controlled by a government.  Relating to or involving organizations that are not part of a government. |  例句：Many non-governmental organizations are working to improve human rights around the world. 许多非政府组织正在努力改善世界各地的人权。| 近义词：private, independent, civil, voluntary | 反义词：governmental, public, state | 词性: adjective
non-governmental:/ˌnɑːnɡʌvərˈmentl/ | adj. | 非政府的；民间性质的 | Not run or controlled by a government.  Relating to or involving organizations that are not part of a government. |  例句：The project was funded by a non-governmental organization. 该项目由一个非政府组织资助。| 近义词：private, independent, civil, voluntary | 反义词：governmental, public, state | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + governmental.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Governmental" relates to government, which comes from the Old French word "governement," ultimately derived from the Latin "gubernare" (to steer, govern).
