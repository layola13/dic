expurgate:/ˈɛkspərˌɡeɪt/
verb
删除（不当内容）；净化；删节
to remove objectionable material from (a book, for example).
The editor expurgated the offensive passages from the novel before its publication. 编辑在小说出版前删除了其中的冒犯性段落。
近义词: censor, bowdlerize, sanitize, purify
反义词: include, insert, add, retain
词性:v.

Etymology:  From Latin *expurgatus*, past participle of *expurgare* "to cleanse, purify," from *ex-* "out" + *purgare* "to purge, cleanse."
