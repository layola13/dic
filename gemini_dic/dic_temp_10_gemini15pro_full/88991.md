holla:/ˈhɒlə/ (UK) /ˈhɑːlə/ (US)| int. n. v.| 嘿，你好；打招呼；呼喊 |A shout or call to attract attention.  It can also be used as a noun meaning the act of shouting or calling, or as a greeting.|例句：Holla at me when you're ready to go. 你准备好走的时候叫我一声。

例句：He gave a loud holla to his friends across the street. 他向街对面的朋友大声喊叫。|近义词：shout, yell, call, hello, hey |反义词: whisper|词性: interjection, noun, verb
