cysticercus:/sɪstɪˈsɜːrkəs/ | n. | 囊尾蚴，囊虫 | The larval stage of certain tapeworms (Taenia), typically found encysted in the muscles of an intermediate host. | Cysticerci can cause serious health problems if they lodge in the brain. 如果囊尾蚴寄居在大脑中，会导致严重的健康问题。 | synonym:  bladder worm | antonym: N/A | noun
