counterargues:/ˌkaʊntərˈɑːrɡjuːz/
|v.|反驳，提出相反的论点|
presents a reason or argument against something|
The defense attorney counterargues that the evidence is circumstantial.|辩护律师反驳说证据是间接的。|
Synonym: rebut, refute, dispute, challenge|
Antonym: support, agree, confirm, endorse|
verb
