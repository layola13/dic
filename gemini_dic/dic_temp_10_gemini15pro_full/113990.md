magisterial:/ˌmædʒɪˈstɪəriəl/| adj.|权威的；威严的；专横的|Having or showing great authority; befitting a master; domineering.|例句：The judge delivered his verdict in a magisterial tone. 法官以威严的语气宣布了他的判决。|例句：She swept into the room with a magisterial air. 她以一种权威的姿态走进房间。|近义词：authoritative, commanding, imperious, domineering, dictatorial|反义词：humble, meek, subservient|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *magisterialis*, from Latin magister ("master, chief, director, teacher, superintendent").
