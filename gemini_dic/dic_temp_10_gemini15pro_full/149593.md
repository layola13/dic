paillon:/pæˈjɒn/ (UK), /pæˈjɔːn/ (US)| n.|薄片,装饰物 (尤指食物)|A thin, often decorative, piece, usually of food.  Specifically, a thin, crisp slice of food, often used as a garnish. It is typically made of potato, pastry, or metal (especially gold or silver leaf).|例句：The chef garnished the dish with a delicate paillon of potato.  厨师用一片精致的土豆薄片装饰了这道菜。

例句：The paillon of gold leaf added a touch of luxury to the dessert. 金箔薄片为甜点增添了一丝奢华。|近义词：garnish, decoration, trimming, sliver, flake|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "chunk" or "slab" to indicate the opposite of thinness)|noun
