counterbalancing:/ˌkaʊntərˈbælənsɪŋ/
n.|抗衡，抵消；平衡|The study used counterbalancing to control for order effects. 这项研究使用抗衡来控制顺序效应。|Synonyms: offsetting, compensating, neutralizing|Antonyms: exacerbating, amplifying|noun
counterbalance:/ˌkaʊntərˈbæləns/
