montezuma:/ˌmɒntəˈzuːmə/ /ˌmɑːntəˈzuːmə/ | n. | 蒙特祖玛 |  The name of two Aztec emperors, most famously Montezuma II (Moctezuma Xocoyotzin), who ruled the Aztec Empire during the Spanish conquest in the early 16th century.  | Example: Montezuma II was the last Aztec emperor. 蒙特祖玛二世是最后一位阿兹特克皇帝。

Example: The reign of Montezuma was a period of great upheaval. 蒙特祖玛的统治时期是一个动荡的时期。| 近义词: Moctezuma (alternative spelling) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
