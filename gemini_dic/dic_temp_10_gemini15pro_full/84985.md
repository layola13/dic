happy-go-luckiness:/ˌhæpi ɡoʊ ˈlʌkinəs/ | n. | 无忧无虑；随遇而安 | The state of being happy-go-lucky; carefree unconcern. |  Her happy-go-luckiness is infectious. 她的无忧无虑很有感染力。|  carefreeness, lightheartedness, nonchalance | anxiety, worry | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From happy-go-lucky + -ness.  Happy-go-lucky itself likely originated in the 18th century, possibly from a dialectal phrase.  The "go-lucky" part might be related to "luck" or perhaps a now-obsolete sense of "lucky."
