canary:/kəˈnɛəri/
n.
金丝雀；淡黄色；告密者
a small, bright yellow bird, often kept as a pet, known for its singing; a bright yellow color; an informer.
The canary in the coal mine warned the miners of danger. 煤矿里的金丝雀警告矿工有危险。
近义词: songbird, finch (for the bird); yellow (for the color); informer, snitch (for the informer)
反义词: (None - depends on the meaning)
词性:Noun

词源：From Old French *canarie, from Late Latin canaria (insula) “(island) of dogs”, from Latin canis “dog”. So called from the large dogs found there. The bird is so named from its origin in the Canary Islands.
