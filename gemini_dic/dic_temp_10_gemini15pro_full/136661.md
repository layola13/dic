nonseasonably:/nɒnˈsiːzənəbli/ /nɑːnˈsiːzənəbli/
adjective
不合时宜地; 反季节地
Not typical of the time of year; unseasonable.
例句：The weather was nonseasonably warm for February. 二月份的天气异常暖和。

例句：We had a nonseasonably cold summer this year. 今年夏天异常寒冷。

近义词：unseasonable, untimely, inappropriate
反义词：seasonable, timely, appropriate
词性：adjective
