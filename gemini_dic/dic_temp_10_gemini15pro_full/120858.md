misbehaving:/ˌmɪsbɪˈheɪvɪŋ/
|adj.| 行为不端的，调皮的 | behaving badly or improperly. | The misbehaving child was sent to his room. 这个调皮的孩子被送回房间。| 近义词: naughty, disobedient, unruly | 反义词: well-behaved, obedient | adjective

misbehave:/ˌmɪsbɪˈheɪv/
|v.| 行为不端，调皮 | to act badly or improperly. | The children misbehaved during the assembly. 孩子们在集会上表现不佳。| 近义词: act up, misact | 反义词: behave | verb
