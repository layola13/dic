housework: /ˈhaʊswɜːrk/ (US), /ˈhaʊswɜːk/ (UK)| n. |家务劳动，家务|Regular housework includes cleaning, laundry, and cooking.  定期的家务包括清洁、洗衣和做饭。|例句：I spent all morning doing housework. 我整个上午都在做家务。|近义词：chores, domestic work|反义词: leisure, relaxation|词性:名词

词源：From house + work.  由“house”（房子）和“work”（工作）组合而成。
