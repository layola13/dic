feudalist:/ˈfjuːdəlɪst/ | n. | 封建主义者；封建制度拥护者 | A person who supports or advocates feudalism, a social system in which land ownership and political power are linked. |  The feudalists resisted the rise of a centralized government.  封建主义者抵制中央集权政府的崛起。

| 近义词:lord,noble | 反义词:commoner, peasant | 词性:noun

feudalistic:/ˌfjuːdəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 封建的；封建主义的 | Relating to or characteristic of feudalism. | The feudalistic system was based on land ownership. 封建制度是建立在土地所有制之上的。

| 近义词:medieval, hierarchical | 反义词:democratic, egalitarian | 词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *feodaliste*, derived from *feodal* (feudal) + *-iste* (-ist).  Ultimately from Medieval Latin *feudalis*,  from *feudum* (fief).
