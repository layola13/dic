caesura: /siˈzjʊərə/ (US), /siːˈzjʊərə/ (UK)| n. |休止；停顿；诗行中的停顿|A pause or break in a line of verse, or in a musical phrase.|The poem uses caesuras to create a sense of drama. 这首诗运用休止来营造戏剧性的感觉。|Synonyms: pause, break, interruption, rest|Antonyms: continuation, flow|noun

caesurae: /siˈzjʊəriː/ (US), /siːˈzjʊəriː/ (UK)| n. |休止；停顿；诗行中的停顿（caesura的复数形式）|The plural form of caesura, referring to multiple pauses or breaks in lines of verse or musical phrases.|The poet's skillful use of caesurae enhances the rhythm of the poem. 诗人巧妙地运用停顿增强了诗歌的节奏。|Synonyms: pauses, breaks, interruptions, rests|Antonyms: continuations, flows|noun
