agronomist:/əˈɡrɑːnəmɪst/
n.
农学家；农艺师
A specialist in the science of soil management and crop production.
例句：The agronomist advised the farmers on the best time to plant their crops.  农学家建议农民最佳的播种时间。

例句：An agronomist studies the soil to determine the best crops to grow.  农艺师研究土壤以确定最适合种植的作物。

近义词：agricultural scientist, crop scientist, soil scientist
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
