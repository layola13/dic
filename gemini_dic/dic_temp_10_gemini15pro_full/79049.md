giveback:/ˈɡɪvbæk/| n.|回报；回馈；捐赠|Something that is returned or given back, often as a gesture of gratitude or reciprocity.  It can also refer to a portion of profits donated to a charitable cause.|The company's annual giveback to the community includes scholarships and park cleanup days.  这家公司每年对社区的回馈包括奖学金和公园清洁日。|近义词：contribution, donation, return, feedback|反义词:take, withhold|词性:名词

词源:  Compound of "give" and "back."  "Give" comes from Old English "giefan," and "back" comes from Old English "bæc."
