ectomorphy:/ˈɛktəmɔːfi/ | n. |外胚型体质；瘦长型体质 |  The predominance of linearity and fragility in body build.  A tendency toward a lean, slightly muscular body build in which tissues derived from the embryonic ectoderm predominate. |  He had the classic ectomorph build: tall, thin, and wiry. 他有着典型的外胚型体格：高、瘦、结实。| synonym: leanness, thinness | antonym: endomorphy, mesomorphy | noun
