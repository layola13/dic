calendographer:/ˌkælɪnˈdɒɡrəfər/
n.
日历学家，日历编制者
A person who constructs or writes calendars.
The calendographer meticulously calculated the dates for the next lunar cycle.  日历学家仔细地计算了下一个阴历周期的日期。
The calendographer noted the equinoxes and solstices in his new calendar. 日历学家在他的新日历中标注了春分、秋分、夏至和冬至。
近义词:chronographer
反义词: None
