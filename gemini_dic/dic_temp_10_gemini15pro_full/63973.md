epispastic:/ˌɛpɪˈspæstɪk/| adj. n.| 发疱剂，起泡的|Causing blisters; an agent that causes blisters.  A medicine or agent which produces blisters.|Example 1: The doctor applied an epispastic to draw out the infection. 医生涂了一种发疱剂来抽出感染。

Example 2:  Epispastic agents were historically used to treat a variety of ailments.  历史上，发疱剂被用于治疗各种疾病。|近义词：blistering, vesicant, vesicatory|反义词: demulcent, emollient|词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From Greek *epispastikos*, from *epispaō* "I draw upon," from *epi* "upon" + *spaō* "I draw."
