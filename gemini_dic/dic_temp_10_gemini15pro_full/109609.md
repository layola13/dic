lette:/ˈlɛtə/ | n. | 字母；信，函件 | A character representing one or more of the sounds used in speech; a written, typed, or printed communication, especially one sent in an envelope by mail or messenger. |  She wrote him a long letter. 她给他写了一封长信。| synonyms: character, message, note, communication | antonyms: number, symbol (in some contexts) | noun

lette (obsolete):/ˈlɛtə/ | v. | 阻碍；妨碍 | To hinder or obstruct.  This word is obsolete and rarely used in modern English. |  The heavy rain letted our progress.  大雨阻碍了我们的前进。（古语用法，现在很少使用） | synonyms: hinder, obstruct, impede | antonyms: aid, assist, help | verb
