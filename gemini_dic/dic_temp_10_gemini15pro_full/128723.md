niftily:/ˈnɪftɪli/| adv.|灵巧地，巧妙地，整洁地|In a nifty manner; cleverly, skillfully, or neatly.|She niftily solved the puzzle. 她巧妙地解决了这个难题。|近义词：cleverly, skillfully, adeptly, neatly, smartly|反义词:clumsily, awkwardly, ineptly|词性:副词

词源:
From nifty + -ly.  "Nifty" originated in American slang in the late 19th century, possibly a variation of "magnificent."
