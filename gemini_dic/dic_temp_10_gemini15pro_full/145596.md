overcondenses:/ˌoʊvərˈkənˌdɛnsɪz/ | v. | 过度压缩，过分简缩 | To condense something excessively, making it too dense or compact, often losing important details or nuance in the process. | The textbook overcondenses the complex history of the Roman Empire into a single chapter. 这本教科书把罗马帝国的复杂历史过度压缩成了一章。

The editor overcondenses the author's manuscript, losing some of its original flavor. 编辑过度压缩了作者的手稿，丢失了一些原有的韵味。

| 近义词：abbreviate, compress, shorten, summarize | 反义词：expand, elaborate, lengthen, detail | 词性：动词
