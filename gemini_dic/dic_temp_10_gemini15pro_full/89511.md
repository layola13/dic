honied:/ˈhʌnid/ /ˈhʌnɪd/| adj.|甜的，涂蜜的，甜蜜的，奉承的|Covered or sweetened with honey; flattering and persuasive.|Her honied words concealed a venomous intent. 她甜言蜜语的背后隐藏着恶毒的意图。|近义词：sweet, sugary, mellifluous, flattering|反义词：bitter, harsh, acrid|词性:形容词
词源: From honey + -ed.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *hunang-.
