clammily:/ˈklæmɪli/ | adv. | 湿冷地；黏糊糊地 | In a clammy manner; damp and sticky. |  His hands felt clammy with sweat. 他的手汗津津的，黏糊糊的。

The air was thick and clammy. 空气又闷又湿冷。| 近义词: damply, stickily | 反义词: dryly | 词性: 副词

词源: From clammy + -ly. Clammy comes from Old English *clæmig, related to clam (meaning "to stick, adhere").
