grovel:/ˈɡrɒvəl/ (UK), /ˈɡrɑːvəl/ (US)| vi.| 卑躬屈膝，匍匐，奴颜婢膝 |To behave in a way that shows you are very sorry for something, especially by being too polite and respectful towards someone.| He groveled to his boss to keep his job. 他为了保住工作向老板卑躬屈膝。|近义词：crawl, cringe, fawn, kowtow|反义词：stand up to, defy| verb

grovel:/ˈɡrɒvəl/ (UK), /ˈɡrɑːvəl/ (US)| n.| 卑躬屈膝，匍匐|The act of groveling.| His grovel was pathetic. 他的卑躬屈膝真是可悲。|近义词：fawning, subservience|反义词：defiance, resistance| noun
