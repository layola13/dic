overabstract:/ˌōvərˈæbˌstrækt/| adj.|过度抽象的|Thinking or talking in a way that is too theoretical and removed from concrete reality, making it difficult to understand or apply.|His philosophical arguments were so overabstract that even other philosophers struggled to grasp them. 他的哲学论证过于抽象，即使其他哲学家也很难理解。|近义词：abstruse, recondite, esoteric, overtheoretical|反义词：concrete, specific, tangible|词性:形容词

overabstract:| /ˌōvərˈæbˌstrækt/ | adj. | 过度抽象的 |  Formed by adding the prefix "over-" (meaning "excessively" or "too much") to "abstract".  "Abstract" itself comes from the Latin "abstractus," past participle of "abstrahere," meaning "to draw away" or "to separate."| The artist's later work became increasingly overabstract, losing the emotional connection with the audience.  这位艺术家的后期作品变得越来越抽象，失去了与观众的情感联系。 | 近义词：overtheoretical, excessively abstract | 反义词：concrete, specific, down-to-earth | 词性: 形容词
