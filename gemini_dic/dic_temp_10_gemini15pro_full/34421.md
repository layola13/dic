claudicant:/ˈklɔːdɪkənt/| adj.|跛行的，蹒跚的|Limping; walking with difficulty.|The old man walked with a claudicant gait due to his arthritis. 这位老人由于关节炎，步态蹒跚。|近义词：limping, halting, hobbled, faltering|反义词：steady, firm, sound, agile|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *claudicāns*, present participle of *claudicāre* (“to limp”), from *claudus* (“lame”).
