overinclined: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈklaɪnd/
adjective
过度倾向的；过分偏爱的
Having a tendency to do something to excess; excessively inclined.
He was overinclined to exaggerate his achievements. 他过分倾向于夸大自己的成就。
Synonyms: predisposed, prone, susceptible, liable
Antonyms: averse, disinclined, resistant
词性: adjective

overinclined: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈklaɪnd/
verb (past participle)
过度倾向；过分偏爱(这是overincline的过去分词形式，单独使用较少)
Having been given an excessive tendency towards something.
She felt overinclined to trust him after his initial charm.  在他最初的魅力之后，她感觉自己过分倾向于信任他。
Synonyms:  (作为过去分词，近义词取决于具体语境，例如：influenced, swayed)
Antonyms: (作为过去分词，反义词取决于具体语境，例如：deterred, dissuaded)
词性: verb (past participle)

词源：over- + inclined.  "Over-"  prefix meaning "excessively." "Inclined" from Latin "inclinare" meaning "to bend, lean, slope."  Therefore, "overinclined" literally means "excessively leaned or bent towards something."
