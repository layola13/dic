heptandrous:/hepˈtændrəs/
adjective
七雄蕊的
Having seven stamens.
A heptandrous flower has seven stamens.  七雄蕊的花有七个雄蕊。
近义词:  (No direct synonym, can use descriptive phrases like "having seven stamens")
反义词: (No direct antonym, could use terms like "isostemonous" if referring to a specific number other than seven)
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From Greek ἑπτά (heptá, “seven”) + ἀνήρ (anḗr, “man”) (genitive ἀνδρός (andrós)), used botanically to refer to the stamen.
