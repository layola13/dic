chines:/tʃaɪnz/ | n. |裂缝，裂口|A long narrow opening in a surface, especially in rock or earth. |例句：There was a deep chine in the cliff face. 悬崖面上有一道深深的裂缝。|近义词：crack, fissure, crevice, chasm|反义词: closure, seal|名词


chines:/tʃaɪnz/ | n. |山谷，峡谷(尤指在南英格兰)|A deep, narrow ravine or valley, especially one formed by a stream or river, typically on the coast of southern England. |例句：The path led down a steep chine to the beach. 小路沿着陡峭的峡谷通向海滩。|近义词：ravine, gorge, canyon, gully|反义词:  peak, summit, ridge|名词
