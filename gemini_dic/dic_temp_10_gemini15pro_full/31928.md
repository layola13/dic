cheiroptera:/kaɪˈrɒptərə/
n.
蝙蝠类；翼手目动物
An order of mammals comprising the bats.  These mammals are characterized by their forelimbs, which are modified as wings, enabling flight.
Bats, belonging to the order Chiroptera, are the only mammals capable of true and sustained flight. 蝙蝠，属于翼手目，是唯一能够真正持续飞行的哺乳动物。

Many species of cheiroptera use echolocation to navigate and find food. 许多种类的蝙蝠使用回声定位来导航和寻找食物。

近义词:bats
反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑其他动物类别，例如birds)
词性:noun
