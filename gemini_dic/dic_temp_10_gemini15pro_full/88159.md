hilliest:/ˈhɪliɪst/
adj.
最丘陵的，最多山丘的
Having the most hills; most hilly.
The hilliest part of the region is in the north.  这个地区最丘陵的部分在北部。
The race was run through the hilliest terrain imaginable. 这场比赛是在可以想象到的最丘陵的地形上进行的。
近义词:mountainous, undulating, rolling
反义词:flat, level, plain
词性:形容词

Etymology:
From hill + -y + -est.  "Hill" comes from Old English *hyll (akin to Old Frisian hil, Middle Dutch hille, Old High German hul).  "-y" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "full of". "-est" is a suffix used to form the superlative degree of adjectives.
