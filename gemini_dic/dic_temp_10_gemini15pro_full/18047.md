begrime:/bɪˈɡraɪm/
verb
弄脏，玷污
To make dirty; soil with grime.
The mechanic's hands were begrimed with oil. 技工的双手沾满了油污。
The children begrimed their clothes playing in the mud. 孩子们在泥里玩耍，弄脏了衣服。
synonyms: soil, dirty, stain, besmirch
antonyms: clean, cleanse, purify
词性:v.
