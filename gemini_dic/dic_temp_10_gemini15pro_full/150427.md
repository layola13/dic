panspermic:/pænˈspɜːrmɪk/
adjective
泛种论的
Relating to or supporting the theory of panspermia, which holds that life exists throughout the universe and can be distributed by space dust, meteoroids, asteroids, comets, planetoids, and also by spacecraft carrying unintended contamination by microorganisms.
The panspermic theory suggests that life on Earth may have originated from microorganisms carried by comets. 泛种论认为地球上的生命可能起源于彗星携带的微生物。
Synonyms:  cosmozoic
Antonyms:  abiogenetic
词性: adjective
