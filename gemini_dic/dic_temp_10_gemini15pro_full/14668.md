avoyer:/əˈvɔɪ.ər/ | n. | 行政长官；税务官；市政官 | A magistrate or municipal officer in some French-speaking areas.  A historical term referring to a chief magistrate in certain French and Swiss towns. |  The avoyer of Bern held considerable power in the canton.  伯尔尼的行政长官在州里拥有相当大的权力。| synonym: magistrate, official, administrator | antonym: citizen, resident | noun

avoyer:/əˈvɔɪ.ər/ | n. | 征税官；税务员 | An officer responsible for collecting taxes or duties.  A historical term for a tax collector in some French-speaking regions. | The avoyer collected taxes on goods entering the city.  税务官对进入城市的货物征税。| synonym: tax collector, revenue officer | antonym: taxpayer | noun
