mini-condo:/ˈmɪni ˈkɑːndoʊ/ (US) /ˈmɪni ˈkɒndəʊ/ (UK)| n. |小型公寓|A small condominium, typically less spacious than a standard condo unit.  It often emphasizes affordability and efficient use of space.|She bought a mini-condo near the university, perfect for a student's budget.|Synonyms: studio apartment, small apartment, efficiency apartment|Antonyms: mansion, large house|noun
