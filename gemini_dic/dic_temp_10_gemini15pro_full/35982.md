coder's:/ˈkoʊdərz/| n.|程序员的，编码者的|This is a coder's paradise. 这是程序员的天堂。|近义词：programmer's, developer's|反义词:user's|词性:名词所有格

Etymology:
From "coder" + "'s" (possessive suffix). "Coder" comes from the verb "to code," which originated in the mid-20th century, derived from "code," ultimately from Latin "codex," meaning "tree trunk, book."
