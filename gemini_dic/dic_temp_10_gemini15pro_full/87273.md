hermaphrodites:/hɜːˈmæfrəˌdaɪts/ |n.|雌雄同体；阴阳人|An organism that has both male and female reproductive organs.|Many snails are hermaphrodites. 许多蜗牛是雌雄同体的。|synonyms: androgyne, intersex|antonyms: male, female|noun

hermaphrodite:/hɜːˈmæfrəˌdaɪt/ |adj.|雌雄同体的|Having both male and female reproductive organs.|The earthworm is a hermaphrodite animal. 蚯蚓是雌雄同体的动物。|synonyms: bisexual, androgynous|antonyms: unisexual, male, female|adjective
