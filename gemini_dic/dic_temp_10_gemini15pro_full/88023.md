high-visibility:/ˌhaɪ vɪzəˈbɪləti/ | adj. | 高能见度的；显眼的 | Easily seen; conspicuous.  Designed to be easily seen, especially in darkness or fog. |  Construction workers are required to wear high-visibility clothing.  建筑工人被要求穿高能见度的衣服。

The police officer wore a high-visibility vest. 警察穿着一件高能见度的背心。 | 近义词：conspicuous, bright, noticeable, fluorescent | 反义词：inconspicuous, dull, hidden, camouflaged | 词性: 形容词
