panentheistic:/ˌpænɛnˈθiːɪstɪk/| adj.|泛神论的|Believing that God is both immanent in and transcendent to the universe; encompassing all things but not limited to them.  God is both within and beyond the universe.|例句：Her beliefs are panentheistic, seeing the divine in all aspects of the natural world. 她持有泛神论的信仰，认为自然界的一切都体现了神性。

例句：Panentheistic philosophies often emphasize the interconnectedness of all things. 泛神论哲学通常强调万物之间的相互联系。|近义词：pantheistic, cosmic, universal|反义词：atheistic, secular, materialistic|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek πᾶν (pan, "all") + ἐν (en, "in") + θεός (theos, "god") + -istic.  Literally, "all-in-God."
