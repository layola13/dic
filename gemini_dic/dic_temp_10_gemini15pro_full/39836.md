conspirers:/kənˈspaɪrərz/
noun
阴谋者，共谋者
People who secretly plan together to do something illegal or harmful.
The conspirers were arrested before they could carry out their plan.  阴谋者在他们实施计划之前就被逮捕了。
synonyms: plotters, schemers, collaborators
antonyms: allies, supporters
词性: 名词
