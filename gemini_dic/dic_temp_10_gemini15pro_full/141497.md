opisthodome: /ˈɒpɪsθəˌdəʊm/ | n. |后体腔，后室|The opisthodome is the posterior part of the body in certain mollusks, such as snails and chitons. |Opisthodomes in some snails house gills and other respiratory structures.  某些蜗牛的后体腔容纳鳃和其他呼吸结构。|Synonym: posterior body chamber |Antonym: prothodome |noun
