overaffirmed:/ˌōvərəˈfɜːrmd/ | adj. | 过分肯定的，过度强调的 |  Affirmed to an excessive degree; excessively emphasized.  Overstating the truth or validity of something. |  The lawyer overaffirmed his client's innocence, which made the jury suspicious.  律师过分强调了他的当事人的清白，这使得陪审团产生了怀疑。 | Near Synonyms: overstated, overemphasized, exaggerated | Antonyms: understated, downplayed | adjective
