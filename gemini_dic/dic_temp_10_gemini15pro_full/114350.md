major-domo:/ˌmeɪdʒər ˈdoʊmoʊ/ | n. | 大管家；总管 | A chief steward of a large household.  A person who speaks, makes arrangements, or takes charge for another. |  The major-domo announced the guests' arrival. 大管家宣布了客人们的到来。
