harkening:/ˈhɑːrkənɪŋ/ (US), /ˈhɑːkənɪŋ/ (UK)| v. (present participle)|倾听，关注; 回忆，追溯|Evoking or suggesting something from the past; listening attentively.|The movie's soundtrack, harkening back to the 80s, was a hit with older audiences.  这部电影的原声带让人回想起80年代，在老观众中很受欢迎。

|近义词：listening, attending, recalling, reminiscing|反义词:ignoring, forgetting|词性:动词 (present participle)
