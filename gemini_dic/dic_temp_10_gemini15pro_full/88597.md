hitting:/ˈhɪtɪŋ/| n. v. adj.|击中，撞击，打击;  令人印象深刻的;  成功的|The act of striking something with a blow.  Experiencing something intensely. Very successful or effective.|He practiced his hitting every day. 他每天练习击球。|近义词：striking, impacting, bashing, impressive, successful|反义词：missing, failing|词性: 主要为动名词(n.)，也可作现在分词(v.)，形容词(adj.)

词源: From Middle English *hitten*, from Old Norse *hitta* (to meet, find, light upon), from Proto-Germanic *hitjaną*
