flanking:/ˈflæŋkɪŋ/ | n. | 包抄，侧翼攻击 | The act of attacking from the side. | The flanking maneuver was successful. 包抄战术成功了。 | The flanking of the enemy position led to their defeat. 对敌人阵地的包抄导致了他们的失败。 | attack from the side, outflanking, surrounding | frontal attack, direct assault | noun
