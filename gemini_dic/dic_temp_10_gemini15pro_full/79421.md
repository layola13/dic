glissando:/ɡlɪˈsændəʊ/ (UK), /ɡlɪˈsɑːndoʊ/ (US)| n. |滑音，滑奏|A rapid slide up or down a scale on a musical instrument, especially a stringed instrument or the human voice.|The pianist executed a brilliant glissando up the keyboard.|Synonyms: slide, glide, swoop|Antonyms: staccato|noun

glissandos:/ɡlɪˈsændəʊz/ (UK), /ɡlɪˈsɑːndoʊz/ (US)| n. |滑音，滑奏（复数）|Plural form of glissando, referring to multiple rapid slides up or down a scale on a musical instrument.|The piece was full of dramatic glissandos on the violins.|Synonyms: slides, glides, swoops|Antonyms: staccatos|noun
