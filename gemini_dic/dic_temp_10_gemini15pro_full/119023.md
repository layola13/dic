methodologically:/ˌmɛθədəˈlɒdʒɪkli/| adv.|方法论地，在方法论上|Relating to the system of methods used in a particular area of study or activity. |The research was conducted methodologically, ensuring reliable results. 这项研究是按方法论进行的，确保了结果的可靠性。|例句：The experiment was methodologically sound. 实验在方法论上是合理的。|近义词：systematically, rigorously, scientifically|反义词: haphazardly, randomly|词性:副词
