nonfortifying:/ˌnɒnˈfɔːrtɪfaɪɪŋ/
adjective
不加强的；不增进健康的；无营养的
Lacking the quality of fortifying or strengthening, especially in regards to physical health or morale.
The doctor advised him to avoid nonfortifying foods like sugary drinks and processed snacks. 医生建议他避免食用无营养的食品，例如含糖饮料和加工零食。
Near Antonyms: fortifying, nourishing, strengthening, enriching
Antonyms: weakening, debilitating
词性: 形容词
