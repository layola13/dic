kitch:/kɪtʃ/ | adj. | 俗气的；庸俗的；媚俗的 |  Characterized by pretentious bad taste or sentimentality, usually in an attempt to appear sophisticated or cultured.  Often applied to art, literature, music, or decor that is overly ornate, sentimental, or clichéd. |  The movie was so kitsch, with its over-the-top romance and predictable plot.  这部电影太俗气了，充满了夸张的浪漫和老套的情节。 |  The velvet Elvis painting was the epitome of kitsch.  这幅天鹅绒的猫王画像是媚俗的缩影。 | synonyms: tacky, gaudy, cheesy, corny, camp | antonyms: tasteful, sophisticated, refined, elegant, understated | adjective
