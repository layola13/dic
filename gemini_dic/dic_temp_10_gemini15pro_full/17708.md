bedazzling:/bɪˈdæzlɪŋ/
adjective
令人眼花缭乱的；炫目的
Impressively splendid or striking in appearance or other qualities.
Her bedazzling smile captivated everyone in the room. 她 dazzling 的笑容迷住了房间里的每一个人。

The fireworks display was bedazzling. 烟花表演令人眼花缭乱。

近义词: dazzling, brilliant, stunning, glamorous, glittering, sparkling
反义词: dull, drab, boring, underwhelming
词性: adjective
