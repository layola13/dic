downlike:/ˈdaʊnˌlaɪk/| adj.|像绒毛的，柔软的|Resembling down; soft and fluffy.|The blanket had a downlike texture. 这条毯子有着像绒毛一样的质感。|The chick was covered in downlike fluff. 这只小鸡身上覆盖着像绒毛一样的绒毛。|近义词：downy, fluffy, soft, feathery|反义词：coarse, rough, hard|词性:形容词

词源:  From "down" (n.) referring to the soft underfeathers of birds + "-like" (suffix) meaning resembling or similar to.
