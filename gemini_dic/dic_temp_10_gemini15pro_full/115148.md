manhandle:/mænˈhændl/ | vt. | 粗暴地对待；推搡；用手搬运 | To handle roughly or physically; to move something heavy using the hands. |  The police manhandled the suspect during the arrest. 警察在逮捕过程中粗暴地对待了嫌疑人。

The movers manhandled the piano up the stairs. 搬运工用手把钢琴搬上楼。 | 近义词：mistreat, abuse, rough up, shove, push | 反义词：treat gently, handle with care | 词性：动词
