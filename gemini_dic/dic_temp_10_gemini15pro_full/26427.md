bynames:/baɪˈneɪmz/| n.|别名，化名|A name that a person or thing is known by in addition to their real or usual name; an alias.|He was known by several bynames, including "The Professor." 他有几个别名，包括“教授”。|Synonyms: alias, pseudonym, sobriquet, nickname|Antonyms: real name, true name|noun
