minimalist: /ˈmɪnɪməlɪst/ | n. adj. | 极简主义者；极简主义的 | A person who adopts a minimalist lifestyle, characterized by owning only essential possessions and living simply; something characterized by extreme spareness and simplicity. | She's a minimalist and her apartment is sparsely furnished. 她是一位极简主义者，她的公寓家具很少。 |  近义词：ascetic, simple, essentialist | 反义词：maximalist, extravagant | 词性: noun, adjective

minimalist: /ˈmɪnɪməlɪst/ | n. adj. | 极简主义者；极简主义的 |  He prefers a minimalist design for his website. 他喜欢为他网站设计极简的风格。 | 近义词：spartan, austere | 反义词：ornate, elaborate | 词性: noun, adjective

词源:  From minimal + -ist.  "Minimal" comes from the Latin "minimus," meaning "smallest."
