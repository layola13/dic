orangist:/ˈɔːrɪndʒɪst/| n. adj.|奥兰治主义者，橙色爱好者|A supporter of the House of Orange, especially historically in the Netherlands or Ireland; a Protestant loyal to William of Orange.  (As an adjective) Characterized by or relating to the political principles of Orangism.|例句：The orangist army marched on Dublin. 奥兰治军队向都柏林进军。

例句：He was known for his strong orangist sympathies. 他以其强烈的奥兰治主义倾向而闻名。|近义词：loyalist, supporter, adherent|反义词：republican, nationalist|词性: noun, adjective

词源: From the name of the House of Orange, the Dutch royal family.  The name "Orange" itself originally referred to the principality of Orange in southern France.
