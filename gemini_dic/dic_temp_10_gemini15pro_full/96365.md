indiscernible:/ˌɪndɪˈsɜːrnɪbl/ /ˌɪndɪˈsɝːnɪbl/| adj.|难以辨认的，无法区分的|Not clearly recognizable or distinguishable.|The twins were so alike they were virtually indiscernible.|近义词: imperceptible, indistinguishable, unnoticeable, invisible|反义词: discernible, perceptible, visible, noticeable|词性:形容词

Etymology:  Late Middle English: from in- ‘not’ + discernible, from Old French discernable (see discernible).
