fletiferous:/flɛˈtɪfərəs/
adjective
多泪的；爱哭的；催人泪下的
Causing tears; tearful; given to weeping.  Also, causing others to weep, especially through pathos.
Her fletiferous performance in the play moved the audience to tears. 她在那部戏剧中催人泪下的表演让观众感动得流泪。
Synonyms: tearful, lachrymose, weepy, dolorous
Antonyms: cheerful, joyous, happy
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From Latin *flētifer, from flētus (“a weeping, wailing, lamentation”) + -fer (“bearing, carrying, producing”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰléw- (“to blow, swell”).
