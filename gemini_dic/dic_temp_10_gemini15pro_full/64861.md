espouser:/ɪˈspaʊzər/ | n. |拥护者，支持者；信奉者|A person who supports or advocates a particular cause, belief, or way of life. |  She is a strong espouser of animal rights. 她是一位坚定的动物权利拥护者。

|近义词：advocate, supporter, proponent, champion, defender|反义词：opponent, critic, detractor|词性:noun

espouse:/ɪˈspaʊz/ | vt. |支持，拥护；信奉|To adopt or support a cause, belief, or way of life. | He espoused the principles of non-violent resistance. 他拥护非暴力抵抗的原则。

|近义词：adopt, embrace, support, advocate, champion|反义词：reject, oppose, renounce|词性:verb

词源：From Old French *espouser "to marry," from Latin sponsare "to betroth, promise solemnly," frequentative of spondere "to pledge oneself, promise."  The meaning shifted from "marry" to "take up, embrace a cause" in the 16th century.
