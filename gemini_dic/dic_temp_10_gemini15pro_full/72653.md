fm:/ɛf ɛm/ | n. | 调频 | Frequency Modulation, a method of radio broadcasting in which the frequency of the carrier wave is varied in accordance with the sound being transmitted. |  The radio station broadcasts on 98.5 fm.  这个广播电台在98.5兆赫的调频波段广播。 |  synonyms: FM radio, FM broadcast | antonyms: AM | noun


Etymology->
Abbreviation of *frequency modulation*.
