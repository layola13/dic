clarigate:/ˈklærɪˌɡeɪt/| v. |澄清；净化；阐明|To make clear or intelligible; to purify.|The committee was formed to clarigate the issues surrounding the new policy. 委员会的成立是为了澄清围绕新政策的问题。|例句：The spokesperson attempted to clarigate the company's position on the environmental issue. 发言人试图澄清公司在环境问题上的立场。

例句：The professor's lecture helped to clarigate the complex scientific concepts for the students. 教授的讲座帮助学生们理解了复杂的科学概念。

近义词：clarify, elucidate, explicate, illuminate|反义词：obfuscate, confuse, complicate|词性：动词


词源：From Latin clarigatus, past participle of clarigare ("to make clear"), from clarus ("clear").
