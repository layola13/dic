nontheocratic:/ˌnɒnθiːəˈkrætɪk/
adjective
非神权政治的
Not governed by a theocracy; secular.  A system where religious leaders do not have governing authority.
例句：The country transitioned to a nontheocratic government after the revolution.  这个国家在革命后过渡到非神权政府。

例句：Many people believe a nontheocratic state is essential for true freedom of religion. 许多人认为非神权国家对于真正的宗教自由至关重要。
synonym:secular, laic, lay
antonym:theocratic, religious
词性:adjective
