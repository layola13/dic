nonstudied:/nɒnˈstʌdid/ /nɑːnˈstʌdid/| adj. |未被研究的，未经学习的|Not having been studied or learned.  Something that hasn't been the subject of research or academic work, or hasn't been learned or acquired through study.|The effects of the new drug on nonstudied populations remain unknown. 这种新药对未被研究人群的影响仍然未知。|Synonyms: unexamined, unexplored, uninvestigated|Antonyms: studied, researched, investigated|adjective
