discourtesy's:/dɪsˈkɜːrtəsiːz/
n.
无礼，失礼
Lack of courtesy; rudeness.
His discourtesy's offended many. 他的无礼冒犯了很多人。
近义词:rudeness, impoliteness, incivility
反义词:courtesy, politeness, civility
词性:名词 (Noun),  所有格形式

词源:
From dis- (expressing negation) + courtesy.  Courtesy comes from Old French curteisie, from curteis "courteous," from court.
