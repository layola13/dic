disavows:/dɪsəˈvaʊz/
| v. |否认，拒绝承认， repudiate|
|The government disavowed any knowledge of the secret negotiations. 政府否认对秘密谈判有任何了解。|
|He disavowed his earlier statements. 他否认了他早先的声明。|
|近义词：deny, repudiate, reject, renounce|
|反义词：acknowledge, admit, confess, confirm|
|词性: 动词|
|词源:  From dis- "expressing reversal" + avow. Ultimately from Latin advocare "to summon."|
