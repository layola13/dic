enneagon:/ˈɛniəɡɒn/ /ˈɛniəɡɑːn/
n.
九边形; 九角形
A plane figure with nine straight sides and nine angles.
A regular enneagon has nine equal sides and nine equal angles. 一个正九边形有九条相等的边和九个相等的角。
The architect incorporated enneagons into the building's design.  建筑师将九边形融入建筑设计中。
近义词:nonagon
反义词:(没有确切的反义词，可以考虑triangle, square等具体形状)
词性:noun
