mesocarp: /ˈmɛzəˌkɑːrp/ | n. | 中果皮 | The fleshy middle layer of a fruit's pericarp, between the exocarp (skin) and endocarp (pit). | The mesocarp of a peach is the juicy part that we eat.  桃子的中果皮就是我们吃的多汁的部分。| Synonyms: sarcocarp | Antonyms: exocarp, endocarp | noun

mesocarp: /ˈmɛzəˌkɑːrp/ | n. | 中果皮 | The middle layer of the pericarp of a fruit, between the epicarp and the endocarp. | The mesocarp of a mango is soft and delicious. 芒果的中果皮柔软可口。| Synonyms: sarcocarp | Antonyms: exocarp, endocarp | noun
