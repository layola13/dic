cautionry:/ˈkɔːʃənri/| n.|保证，担保；保证金；保证人|The act of giving or the thing given as security; something deposited or pledged as a guarantee of good faith or the performance of an engagement; bail, security, pledge.|He offered his house as cautionry for the loan. 他把自己的房子作为贷款的担保。|近义词：security, guarantee, pledge, bail, surety|反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可用表示风险或不安全状态的词) risk, jeopardy|词性: noun
