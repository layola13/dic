mistutored:/ˌmɪsˈtjuːtərd/
adjective
中文翻译：被错误教导的；接受了不良教育的
英文解析：Taught badly or incorrectly; having received poor or inadequate instruction.  Implies that the teaching has had a negative effect.
例句：The mistutored youth struggled to adapt to the demands of college life.  被错误教导的年轻人难以适应大学生活的要求。

例句：Feeling mistutored in the basics of math, she enrolled in a remedial course.  感觉自己在数学基础知识方面受到了错误的教育，她报名参加了补习课程。

近义词：miseducated, ill-educated, poorly taught
反义词：well-educated, well-taught, properly educated
词性：adjective
