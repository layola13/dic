cloudberry:/ˈklaʊdbɛri/| n.|云莓，莓苔越橘|A type of edible golden-yellow berry that grows in arctic and subarctic regions and is related to the raspberry and blackberry.  It is a rich source of vitamin C.|We found some cloudberries growing in the bog. 我们在沼泽地里发现了一些云莓。|Synonyms: bakeapple, knotberry, salmonberry (sometimes)|Antonyms: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *klátr-ber, from klátr (“rock”) + ber (“berry”).
