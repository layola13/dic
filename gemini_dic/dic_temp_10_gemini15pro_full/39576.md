conscript:/kənˈskrɪpt/ | v. | 征兵，强制征募 | To force someone to serve in the army. |  The government conscripted men into the army during the war. 政府在战争期间强征男子入伍。

conscript:/ˈkɑːnskrɪpt/ | n. | 被征召的士兵 | A person who has been forced to serve in the army. | Many young conscripts were sent to the front lines. 许多年轻的被征召士兵被派往了前线。

conscript:/kənˈskrɪpt/ | adj. | 被征召的 |  Relating to conscription. | The conscript army was poorly trained. 被征召的军队训练很差。


近义词:draft, enlist, recruit | 反义词:volunteer, discharge | 词性:verb, noun, adjective
