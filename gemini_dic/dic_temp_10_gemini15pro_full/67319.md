extemporaneous:/ɪkˌstɛmpəˈreɪniəs/ /ɛkˌstɛmpəˈreɪniəs/ | adj. | 即兴的，临时的，无准备的 | Done, spoken, performed, etc., without special or advance preparation; impromptu. |  He gave an extemporaneous speech at the wedding. 他在婚礼上发表了即兴演讲。| The musician played an extemporaneous solo. 音乐家即兴演奏了一段独奏。 |  impromptu, ad-lib, off-the-cuff, improvised | prepared, rehearsed | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ex tempore*  "out of the time," from *ex* "out of" + *tempore* "time, season."
