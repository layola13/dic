despot's:/ˈdɛspɒts/| n.|暴君的，专制者的|Of or belonging to a despot; characteristic of a despot. |The despot's cruelty was well known throughout the land. 暴君的残暴在全国都很有名。|Synonyms: tyrannical, autocratic, dictatorial |Antonyms: democratic, egalitarian| possessive noun

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *despote*, from Old French *despote*, from Late Latin *despotes*, from Greek *despótēs* (master, lord), from *despozein* (to be master of).
