ekes:/iːks/
n.
额外的东西；剩余物；少量；（尤指从较大数量中剩下的）少量
small additions; something extra
He managed to scrape together a few ekes of food from the bottom of the cupboard. 他设法从橱柜底部刮出了一些残羹剩饭。
近义词:leftovers, remnants, scraps, odds and ends
反义词:abundance, surplus
词性:名词
