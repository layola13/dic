inductiveness:/ɪnˈdʌktɪvnəs/| n.|归纳性，感应性|The quality of being inductive, characterized by the use of inductive reasoning, proceeding from particular facts to a general conclusion.|His theory is known for its inductiveness, building upon numerous observations. 他的理论以其归纳性而闻名，建立在大量的观察之上。|Synonyms: inductive character, inductive nature, inferential capacity|Antonyms: deductiveness, deductive nature|noun

inductive:/ɪnˈdʌktɪv/| adj.|归纳的，感应的|Characterized by the use of inductive reasoning, proceeding from particular facts to a general conclusion; relating to or caused by electromagnetic induction.|The scientist used an inductive approach to form his hypothesis.  这位科学家用归纳法形成了他的假设。|Synonyms: inferential, generalizing, reasoned|Antonyms: deductive|adjective
