bettors:/ˈbetərz/
n.
下注者，投注者
People who place bets, especially on horse races or other sporting events.
The bettors cheered as their horse crossed the finish line first. 下注者们在他们的马第一个冲过终点线时欢呼起来。
近义词: gamblers, punters, wagerers
反义词: bookmakers
词性: 名词
