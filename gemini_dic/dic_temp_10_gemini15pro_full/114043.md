magnetic-inclination:/mæɡˈnɛtɪk ˌɪŋklɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 磁倾角 |The angle between the direction of the Earth's magnetic field lines and the horizontal plane at a given location.  It's also known as dip angle. | The magnetic inclination at the magnetic poles is 90 degrees. 在磁极，磁倾角是90度。

The magnetic inclination varies with latitude. 磁倾角随纬度而变化。 | Synonyms: dip angle, magnetic dip | Antonyms: N/A | noun
