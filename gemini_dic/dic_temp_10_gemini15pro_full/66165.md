excerptors:/ɪkˈsɜːrptərz/
noun
摘录者，选录者
People who select and extract excerpts from a larger work, such as a book, article, or film.
The excerptors chose the most impactful scenes for the trailer.  摘录者选择了最具影响力的场景用于预告片。
The excerptors carefully selected passages that represented the author's main arguments. 摘录者仔细挑选了代表作者主要论点的段落。
Synonyms: abstractors, compilers, selectors
Antonyms:  --- (No direct antonym.  One could consider "fabricators," or "inventors," but these aren't perfect.)
