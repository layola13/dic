egophony:/ɪˈɡɒfəni/ (US) /iːˈɡɒfəni/ (UK)| n. |羊鸣音，支气管语音|An increased resonance of voice heard when auscultating the lungs, often indicating consolidation of lung tissue, such as in pneumonia.  It sounds like the patient is saying "A" when they are actually saying "E".|

例句：
Egophony was detected in the right lower lobe, suggesting pneumonia.
在右下叶检测到羊鸣音，提示肺炎。

Egophony can be a valuable sign in diagnosing pleural effusion.
羊鸣音在诊断胸腔积液方面可能是一个有价值的体征。


近义词：bronchophony|
反义词: N/A|
词性: Noun|
