homepage:/ˈhoʊmˌpeɪdʒ/ | n. | 主页，首页 | The main page of a website, typically the first page seen by visitors. |  Example: The company's homepage provides links to all its products and services. 公司的首页提供了其所有产品和服务的链接。 | Example:  I updated the homepage with the latest news and announcements. 我用最新的新闻和公告更新了主页。 | Synonyms:  main page, start page, front page, index page | Antonyms:  subpage, internal page | noun
