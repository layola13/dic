engravers:/ɪnˈɡreɪvərz/
noun
雕刻师，雕刻匠
A person who carves designs, letters, etc., onto a hard surface, such as metal, wood, or stone.
The engravers meticulously carved the intricate designs onto the silver platter.  雕刻师一丝不苟地在银盘上雕刻复杂的图案。
The museum showcased the works of renowned engravers from past centuries.  博物馆展出了过去几个世纪著名雕刻师的作品。
synonyms: carvers, etchers, sculptors
antonyms: N/A (lack of a direct opposite action)
