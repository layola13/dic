hexone:/ˈhɛksoʊn/| n.|甲基异丁基酮|A colorless liquid ketone, CH3COCH2CH(CH3)2, used as a solvent.|Hexone is an excellent solvent for many types of resins and lacquers.|近义词：methyl isobutyl ketone, MIBK|反义词: (No direct antonym for a chemical compound)|noun
Etymology->From *hexa-* (six) + *-one* (ketone), reflecting its six-carbon structure.
