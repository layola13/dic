amine:/əˈmiːn/, /ˈæmiːn/ | n. | 胺; 氨基化合物 | An organic compound derived from ammonia by replacement of one or more hydrogen atoms by organic groups.  Amines are basic in nature due to the unshared electron pair on the nitrogen atom. |  Many amines have a fishy odor. 许多胺类化合物都有一种鱼腥味。|  Putrescine and cadaverine are amines responsible for the smell of decaying flesh. 腐胺和尸胺是造成腐肉气味的胺类化合物。 |  amino, imine |  acid | noun
