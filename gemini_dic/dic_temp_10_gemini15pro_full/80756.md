gossamered:/ˈɡɒsəməd/ (US) /ˈɡɒsəməd/ (UK)| adj.|轻薄的，薄如蝉翼的|Covered with or resembling gossamer; extremely light, delicate, or tenuous.|例句：The gossamered wings of the butterfly caught the sunlight. 蝴蝶薄如蝉翼的翅膀捕捉到了阳光。|例句：Her dress was a gossamered creation of silk and lace. 她的裙子是用丝绸和蕾丝制成的轻薄作品。|近义词：diaphanous, ethereal, filmy, gauzy|反义词：thick, heavy, dense|词性:形容词

词源:
From gossamer + -ed.  Gossamer itself comes from Middle English *gosomer, from gos (goose) + somer (summer).  It originally referred to the fine cobwebs seen floating in the air on calm autumn days, sometimes called "goose summer."
