groundwater:/ˈɡraʊndˌwɔːtər/ (US) /ˈɡraʊndˌwɔːtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |地下水|Water held underground in the soil or in pores and crevices in rock.|The drought has severely depleted groundwater levels.  干旱严重消耗了地下水位。|The well provides groundwater for the entire community. 这口井为整个社区提供地下水。|近义词：subsurface water, underground water|反义词: surface water|noun
