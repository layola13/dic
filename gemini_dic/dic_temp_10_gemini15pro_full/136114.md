nonreducible:/ˌnɒnrɪˈdjuːsəbl/ | /ˌnɑːnrɪˈduːsəbl/ | adj. | 不可约的，不能简化的 | Not able to be reduced or simplified further. |  The fraction 7/11 is nonreducible.  7/11这个分数是不可约的。 | The equation is nonreducible to a simpler form. 这个方程式无法简化成更简单的形式。 | 近义词: irreducible, indecomposable | 反义词: reducible, decomposable | 词性: 形容词

词源:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + reducible (from Latin reducere, "to lead back," meaning "capable of being made smaller or simpler")
