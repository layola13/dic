mannikin:/ˈmænɪkɪn/ | n. | 人体模型；侏儒；时装模特儿 | A jointed model of the human body, used especially by artists; a dwarf; a fashion model. | The artist used a mannikin to study human anatomy. 艺术家用人体模型来研究人体解剖。| synonyms: dummy, model, figure, mannequin | antonyms: giant | noun


mannikin:/ˈmænɪkɪn/ | n. | 矮小的人；侏儒 | A person of abnormally small stature; a dwarf. |  The circus employed a troupe of mannikins. 马戏团雇佣了一群侏儒。| synonyms: dwarf, midget, pygmy | antonyms: giant | noun
