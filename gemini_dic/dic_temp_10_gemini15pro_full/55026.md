disqualify:/dɪsˈkwɒlɪfaɪ/ | v. | 使…没有资格，取消…的资格 | To make someone or something not eligible or suitable for something. | He was disqualified from the competition for cheating. 他因作弊被取消了比赛资格。 | The injury disqualified him from playing in the final.  他的伤势使他无法参加决赛。 | 近义词：bar, preclude, prohibit, debar | 反义词：qualify, enable, authorize | 词性：动词

词源:  From dis- (expressing negation) + qualify (from Latin qualificare, "to give a quality to").
