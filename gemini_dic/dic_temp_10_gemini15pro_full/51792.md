devour:/dɪˈvaʊər/
|vt.|
|吞食; 吞噬; 狼吞虎咽; (尤指因感情激动) 使着迷, 充满|
|1. Eat (food or prey) hungrily or quickly.
2. (of fire or a similar destructive force) destroy completely.
3. Read quickly and eagerly.|
|1. He devoured the entire pizza in one sitting. 他一口气吃完了整个披萨。
2. The flames devoured the building. 大火吞噬了整栋建筑。
3. She devoured every book she could find on the subject. 她如饥似渴地阅读所有能找到的关于这个主题的书籍。|
|consume, gobble, gulp|
|nibble, pick at|
