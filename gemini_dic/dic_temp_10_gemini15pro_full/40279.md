continuative:/kənˈtɪnjuətɪv/
adjective
延续的；持续的
Continuing without interruption; expressing continuation.
The lecture was a continuative discussion of the previous week's topic.  这节课是延续上周主题的讨论。
The medication has a continuative effect, lasting for several hours. 这种药物有持续的效果，能持续几个小时。
近义词:continuous, ongoing, uninterrupted, sustained, persistent
反义词:discontinuous, intermittent, sporadic, interrupted

词性: adjective
