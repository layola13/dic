hipsterish:/ˈhɪpstərɪʃ/| adj.|有点时髦的，略带嬉皮士风格的|Resembling or characteristic of a hipster, but not fully embodying the subculture.  Often used to describe someone who adopts certain hipster trends or aesthetics without fully committing to the lifestyle.|Her apartment had a hipsterish vibe, with vintage furniture and Edison bulbs.|Near synonyms: trendy, fashionable, stylish, bohemian, alternative|Near antonyms: mainstream, conventional, traditional|adjective
