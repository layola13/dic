enthused:/ɪnˈθjuːzd/ | adj. | 热情的；充满激情的 | Filled with enthusiasm; eager. |  She was enthused by the prospect of traveling to Europe. 她对去欧洲旅行的前景充满热情。|  excited, passionate, eager, zealous | apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic | adjective


enthuse:/ɪnˈθjuːz/ | v. | 使充满热情；使热衷 | To fill with enthusiasm; make eager. | The coach enthused the team with his pep talk. 教练用他的激励讲话使队员们充满热情。 |  inspire, excite, motivate, stimulate | discourage, dampen, bore | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From en- (cause to be) + Greek theos (god) + -ed (past participle suffix).  Originally meant "inspired by a god," later broadened to mean "filled with enthusiasm."
