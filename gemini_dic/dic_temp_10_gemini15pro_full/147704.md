overpreoccupations: /ˌoʊvərpriːˌɑːkjuˈpeɪʃənz/
| n. | 过度关注；过分担忧；念念不忘的事情 |  An excessive preoccupation with something; an obsession.  It signifies a state of being overly focused on particular thoughts or concerns, often to the detriment of other aspects of life. | His overpreoccupations with work led to him neglecting his family.  他过度专注于工作导致他忽略了家人。|  fixations, obsessions, preoccupations | indifference, detachment | noun
