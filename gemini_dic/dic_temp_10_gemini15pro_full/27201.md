callosity:/kəˈlɑːsəti/
n.
老茧；硬结；无情
an area of thick hard skin on a hand or foot, usually caused by rubbing;  The state of being hardened or thickened;  Insensitivity or lack of feeling, especially from long exposure to suffering.
The constant friction of the oar handle had created a painful callosity on his palm.  不断的摩擦桨柄在他的手掌上形成了一个疼痛的老茧。

His years in the war had led to a callosity of spirit. 他多年的战争经历导致了他的精神麻木。

近义词:callus, hardness, induration, insensitivity
反义词:sensitivity, softness, tenderness
词性:noun
