onerously:/ˈəʊnərəsli/ | adv. | 繁重地；艰难地；累赘地 | in a burdensome or oppressive manner |  The regulations were applied onerously, stifling innovation.  这些规章制度执行得过于繁琐，扼杀了创新。 | The debt weighed onerously upon him. 债务沉重地压在他身上。 | 近义词：burdensomely, oppressively, heavily, taxing, arduously | 反义词：lightly, easily, effortlessly | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From Late Latin *onerosus, from Latin onus ("burden") + -osus.
