aspires:/əˈspaɪərz/ | v. |渴望，追求，有志于| To have a strong desire to achieve something high or great. | He aspires to be a great writer. 他渴望成为一名伟大的作家。|  She aspires to a leadership position in the company. 她渴望在公司担任领导职位。 |近义词：desire, yearn, aim, strive |反义词：resign oneself, give up, despair |词性: 动词

Etymology:
From Middle English *aspyren*, from Old French *aspirer* (French *aspirer* “to breathe, inhale; to aspire to”), from Latin *aspirare* “to breathe upon, blow upon, breathe on; to strive for, long for, aim at,” from *ad-* (“to, toward”) + *spirare* (“to breathe”).
