froggy:/ˈfrɒɡi/ (UK) /ˈfrɔːɡi/ (US)| adj.|像青蛙的；潮湿的；声音沙哑的|Resembling a frog in appearance, sound, or behavior; damp and chilly; having a hoarse voice.|例句：The ground was froggy after the rain. 雨后地面很潮湿。|例句：He had a froggy voice after shouting all day. 他喊了一整天后嗓子沙哑了。|近义词：damp, chilly, hoarse, croaky|反义词：dry, warm, clear|词性:形容词
