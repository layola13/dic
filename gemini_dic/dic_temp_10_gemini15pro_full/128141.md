netizens:/ˈnetɪzənz/ /ˈne.tɪ.zənz/| n. |网民，网络公民|A person who actively participates in online communities or the internet in general.  A blend of "net" (from "internet") and "citizens".|例句：Netizens quickly shared the news on social media. 网民们很快在社交媒体上分享了这个消息。


|例句：The opinions of netizens can significantly impact a company's reputation. 网民的意见会显著影响公司的声誉。|近义词：internet users, online users, cybercitizens|反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly "offline individuals")|词性:Noun
