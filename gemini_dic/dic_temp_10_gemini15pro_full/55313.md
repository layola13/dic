dissolvents:/dɪˈzɒlvənts/ | n. | 溶剂 | Substances that dissolve other substances, forming a solution. | Example:  Acids are powerful dissolvents. 酸是强力溶剂。

Example: Many dissolvents are harmful if inhaled. 许多溶剂吸入有害。 | 近义词: solvents, diluents | 反义词: None (Dissolvents describe a function, not an inherent property with a direct opposite.) | 词性: Noun
