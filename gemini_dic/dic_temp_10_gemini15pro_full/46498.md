dairy:/ˈdeəri/| n. adj.|乳制品；牛奶场；乳品店；日记|A place where milk and cream are stored and processed into butter, cheese, etc., or a business that makes or sells these products. Also, relating to milk or products made from it.  A dairy cow is a cow raised to produce milk. A dairy can also refer to a personal journal or diary.|例句：We buy milk from the local dairy. 我们从当地的乳品店买牛奶。

例句：She works at a dairy farm. 她在一家奶牛场工作。

近义词：creamery, milk plant|反义词:|词性:名词, 形容词
