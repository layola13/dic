booster:/ˈbuːstər/ (US), /ˈbuːstə/ (UK)| n.|助推器；增强剂；追加剂；支持者；(非正式)偷窃犯|Something that increases strength, power, or effectiveness; a dose of a vaccine given after an earlier dose to strengthen the effect. |The government announced a booster program for the economy. 政府宣布了一项促进经济发展的计划。|synonyms: amplifier, enhancer, stimulant, supporter, advocate |antonyms: inhibitor, suppressant, deterrent|noun

booster:/ˈbuːstər/ (US), /ˈbuːstə/ (UK)| adj.|加强的；推进的|Increasing strength, power, or effectiveness. |He got a booster shot of the vaccine. 他接种了加强针疫苗。|synonyms: strengthening, enhancing, boosting |antonyms: weakening, diminishing, reducing|adjective
