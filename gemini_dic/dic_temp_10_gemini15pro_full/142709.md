othman:/ˈɒθmən/ | n. |奥斯曼|A male given name, of Arabic origin, meaning "young bustard" (a type of bird). It is also a common surname and the name of the founder of the Ottoman Empire.|Example: Othman was a wise and just ruler. 奥斯曼是一位英明公正的统治者.|Example: Many people named Othman live in Turkey. 许多名叫奥斯曼的人住在土耳其。|Synonym: Ottoman (as in the empire), Osman|Antonym: N/A|Noun
