chassepot:/ˈʃæspoʊ/| n.|夏塞波步枪|A type of breech-loading rifle adopted by the French army in 1866. It was named after its inventor, Antoine Alphonse Chassepot.|The Chassepot rifle was a significant improvement over previous muzzle-loading rifles. 夏塞波步枪是对以前的前膛装填步枪的重大改进。

The French army used Chassepot rifles during the Franco-Prussian War. 法国军队在普法战争中使用了夏塞波步枪。|近义词:rifle, firearm|反义词:None|词性:noun

Etymology: Named after its inventor, Antoine Alphonse Chassepot.
