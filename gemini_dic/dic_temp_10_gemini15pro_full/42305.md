counter-opening:/ˈkaʊntərˌoʊpənɪŋ/ | n. |反建议，反提案，还价 |An offer or proposal made in response to a previous offer or proposal, typically in a negotiation or bargaining situation. |  The union presented a counter-opening to the company's initial offer. 工会对公司的最初提议提出了反建议。

The seller made a counter-opening after I submitted my initial offer on the house.  在我提交了对房子的最初报价后，卖方提出了还价。 | synonyms: counteroffer, counterproposal,  riposte | antonyms:  acceptance, agreement, concession | noun
