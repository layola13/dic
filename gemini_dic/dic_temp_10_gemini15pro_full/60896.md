what: /wɒt/ (British), /wʌt/ (American) | pron. det. adv.| 什么，多么 | Used to ask for information about something. | 例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？| 近义词: which (in some cases)| 反义词:  (What doesn't have a direct antonym) | 词性: pronoun, determiner, adverb

What does she do for a living? 她以什么为生？

What are you doing right now? 你现在正在做什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? 他正在做什么晚餐？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned so far? 你到目前为止学到了什么？
What has she done to help? 她做了什么来帮忙？
