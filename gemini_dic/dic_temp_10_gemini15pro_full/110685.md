linea:/ˈlɪniə/ /ˈlɪneə/ | n. | 线，线条，谱系，血统 | A long, narrow mark or band.  A sequence of ancestors or descendants. |  The artist drew a fine linea with charcoal. 艺术家用木炭画了一条细线。  He could trace his linea back to the 17th century. 他可以追溯他的血统到17世纪。 | synonyms: line, streak, stripe, lineage, ancestry, descent | antonyms: blur, smudge | noun
