euthanatizing:/ˌjuːθəˈnaɪzɪŋ/
|v.| 对...实施安乐死|The act of inducing a painless death, especially in an animal or person suffering from an incurable and painful disease. |The vet euthanized the dog to end its suffering.| 近义词: putting down, putting to sleep | 反义词: resuscitating, reviving
|Verb|
