hyperadiposity:/ˌhaɪpərˌædɪˈpɒsɪti/
noun
过度肥胖，肥胖症
Excessive fatness; obesity.  The condition of having excessive body fat.
Hyperadiposity is a major health concern in developed countries. 过度肥胖是发达国家的一个主要健康问题。
Childhood hyperadiposity can lead to serious health problems later in life. 儿童期过度肥胖会导致以后生活中出现严重的健康问题。
Synonyms: obesity, corpulence, adiposity
Antonyms: thinness, leanness
