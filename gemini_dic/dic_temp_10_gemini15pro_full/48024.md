decisions:/dɪˈsɪʒənz/
n.
决定，决心；决策
The process of making choices or reaching conclusions.
She made the difficult decision to leave her job. 她做出了离开工作的艰难决定。

近义词:choices, resolutions, determinations, conclusions
反义词:indecision, hesitation

词源: From Old French decision, from Latin dēcīsiō (“a cutting off, settlement”), from dēcīdō (“I decide”).  The root word, -cid-, comes from the Latin caedere, meaning "to cut, kill."  Therefore, a decision implies a cutting off of other possibilities.
