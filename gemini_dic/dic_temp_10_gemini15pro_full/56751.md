doorstone:/ˈdɔːrstəʊn/ (UK) /ˈdɔːrˌstoʊn/ (US)| n. |门槛，门石|A stone forming the sill of a doorway.|She tripped on the worn doorstone as she left the cottage. 她离开小屋时被磨损的门槛绊倒了。|The old house had a high doorstone. 这座老房子有一个很高的门槛。|近义词：threshold, sill, doorstep|反义词: (No direct antonym exists.  One could consider something like "lintel" which is the top part of a doorway, but it isn't truly opposite.)|词性:noun
