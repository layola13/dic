creatively:/kriˈeɪtɪvli/
adv. | 创造性地，有创意地 | In a creative or imaginative way. | She solved the problem creatively. 她创造性地解决了这个问题。| imaginatively, innovatively, ingeniously | routinely, conventionally, dully
词性: 副词

词源:
From the adjective "creative" + the adverbial suffix "-ly."  "Creative" comes from the Latin "creare" (to create).
