paracaseinate:/ˌpærəˈkeɪsɪneɪt/ | n. | 副酪蛋白酸盐; 副酪蛋白酸酯 | A salt or ester of paracasein, formed by the action of rennin on casein.  It's a less soluble form of casein produced in milk during cheese making. |  The paracaseinate formed a curd, separating from the whey. 副酪蛋白酸盐形成了凝乳，与乳清分离。| The scientist analyzed the properties of the paracaseinate. 科学家分析了副酪蛋白酸盐的特性。 | synonyms: calcium paracaseinate | antonyms: casein (in its original, soluble form) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From para- (beside, altered) + casein + -ate (suffix forming nouns, often salts or esters).  Casein itself comes from Latin *caseus* (cheese).
