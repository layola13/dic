microphonic:/ˌmaɪkrəˈfɒnɪk/ (US) /ˌmaɪkrəˈfɒnɪk/ (UK)| adj.|麦克风的；受话筒干扰的；对振动敏感的|Relating to or resembling a microphone; sensitive to vibrations, especially in such a way as to produce unwanted noise in electronic equipment.|The guitar pickup was microphonic, causing feedback during the performance.  吉他拾音器受话筒干扰，在演出期间引起了反馈。|近义词：sensitive, responsive, resonant|反义词：insensitive, unresponsive, damped|词性:形容词

microphonic:/ˌmaɪkrəˈfɒnɪk/ (US) /ˌmaɪkrəˈfɒnɪk/ (UK)| n.|麦克风效应|The unwanted noise produced in electronic equipment due to sensitivity to vibrations.|The engineer noticed a slight microphonic in the amplifier. 工程师注意到放大器中轻微的麦克风效应。|近义词：feedback, noise|反义词：(none specific)|词性:名词
