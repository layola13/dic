cost-benefit:/ˈkɒst ˈbɛnɪfɪt/ /ˈkɑːst ˈbɛnɪfɪt/| adj. n.|成本效益；利弊|A cost-benefit analysis is a process used to measure the benefits of a decision or taking action minus the costs associated with taking that action.|Example:  A cost-benefit analysis showed that the project was not worthwhile. 成本效益分析表明该项目不值得进行。|Synonym: cost-effectiveness,  pros and cons|Antonym: N/A|adjective/noun

cost-benefit:/ˈkɒst ˈbɛnɪfɪt/ /ˈkɑːst ˈbɛnɪfɪt/| n.|成本效益分析|A systematic approach to estimating the strengths and weaknesses of alternatives.|Example:  We need to do a cost-benefit analysis before making a decision.  我们需要在做决定之前进行成本效益分析。|Synonym: cost-effectiveness analysis|Antonym: N/A|noun
