nibblers:/ˈnɪblərz/| n. |小口吃东西的人；零食；啃咬者|People or animals that nibble; small snacks often eaten between meals.  Those who take small bites of food.|The squirrels are nibblers, constantly eating nuts and seeds. 松鼠是小口吃东西的动物，经常吃坚果和种子。|近义词：grazers, snackers|反义词：gobbler, gorger|词性:noun
