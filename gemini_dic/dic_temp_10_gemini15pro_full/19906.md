This word is considered extremely disrespectful and sexist. | Example: Using "bint" in any context is offensive and unacceptable. 词源:
From Arabic بنت (bint), meaning "daughter," "girl."  Entered English through British colonial contact with Arabic-speaking populations. Its usage in English has always been pejorative.
