incisory:/ɪnˈsaɪsəri/| adj.|深刻的，尖锐的，敏锐的| penetrating, clear, and sharp, typically of comment or style.|Her incisory remarks cut to the heart of the matter. 她一针见血的评论直指问题的核心。|近义词：penetrating, acute, sharp, keen, astute, insightful, piercing, trenchant|反义词:dull, vague, obtuse, rambling|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin incisorius (“cutting, apt to cut”), from Latin incidere (“to cut into”), from in- (“into, in, on, upon”) + caedere (“to cut, kill”).
