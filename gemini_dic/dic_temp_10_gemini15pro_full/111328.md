live-it-up: /lɪv ɪt ˈʌp/
verb phrase
尽情享受；过着奢华的生活
to enjoy oneself in an extravagant way, especially by spending a lot of money
They decided to live it up in Las Vegas for a week. 他们决定在拉斯维加斯尽情享受一周。
live it up while you can. 在你还能的时候尽情享受吧。
近义词: celebrate, indulge, splurge, carouse
反义词: scrimp, save, economize
词性: verb phrase
