embrasure: /ɪmˈbreɪʒər/ | n. | 枪眼，炮眼；(墙上的)凹进处 | An opening in a wall or parapet through which a cannon can be fired.  A recess in a wall, especially one containing a window. |  The soldiers peered through the embrasures, searching for the enemy. 士兵们透过枪眼寻找敌人。

The castle walls were punctuated by narrow embrasures. 城墙上点缀着狭窄的炮眼。 | 近义词: loophole, opening, aperture, crenel, battlement, niche, recess | 反义词: protrusion, projection | 词性: noun
