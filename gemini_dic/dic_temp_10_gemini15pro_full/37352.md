commender:/kəˈmɛndər/| n.|推荐人；赞扬者；委托者|A person who recommends or praises something or someone; someone who entrusts something to the care of another.|The professor was a strong commender of her student's research.  这位教授大力推荐了她学生的研究。|近义词：advocate, supporter, proponent, recommender|反义词：critic, detractor, opponent|noun
