outflatter: /ˌaʊtˈflætər/
verb
奉承，胜过…的奉承; 以更谄媚的方式奉承
To flatter someone more than someone else does, or to flatter someone excessively.
He outflattered all the other suitors vying for her attention. 他比所有其他追求她注意力的追求者都更会奉承。
She outflattered even the most seasoned diplomats. 她甚至比最有经验的外交官还要会奉承。
近义词: outdo, surpass, eclipse
反义词: understate, downplay
词性: verb

outflatter: /ˌaʊtˈflætər/
Etymology:  "out-" + "flatter".  "Out-" is a prefix meaning "to surpass or exceed," and "flatter" comes from the Old French word "flater," meaning "to stroke, caress, or blandish."
