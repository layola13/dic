disbar:/dɪsˈbɑːr/
verb
剥夺律师资格
To expel a lawyer from the bar; to deprive (a lawyer) of the right to practice law.
The lawyer was disbarred for misconduct.  这位律师因行为不端被取消了律师资格。
synonyms:  expel, debar, suspend
antonyms:  admit, reinstate
词性: 动词

disbar:/dɪsˈbɑːr/
vt.
剥夺…的资格
To exclude or prohibit someone from doing something.
He was disbarred from holding public office. 他被禁止担任公职。
synonyms:  prohibit, forbid, exclude
antonyms:  permit, allow, authorize
词性: 动词
