antireflectiveness:/ˌæntiˌrɪˈflɛktɪvnəs/
n.
抗反射性，防反光性
The quality of being antireflective; the extent to which a surface does not reflect light.
The antireflectiveness of the coating on the camera lens improved image quality.  相机镜头上的涂层的抗反射性提高了图像质量。

Near synonyms: nonreflectivity, nonreflectance
Antonyms: reflectivity, reflectance
noun
