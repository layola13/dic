mounted:/ˈmaʊntɪd/| adj. |安装的，骑马的，裱好的|Fixed or placed on a support; riding an animal, especially a horse; framed or displayed.|例句：The museum displayed a mounted skeleton of a dinosaur. 博物馆展出了一具安装好的恐龙骨架。|近义词：attached, fixed, riding, displayed|反义词:dismounted, unmounted|词性:形容词

mounted:/ˈmaʊntɪd/| v. |登上，骑上，增加，发起|To get up on something, especially a horse or other animal; to increase gradually; to organize and begin something.|例句：She mounted her horse and rode off. 她骑上马，骑走了。|近义词：climbed, ascended, increased, launched|反义词:dismounted, decreased|词性:动词
