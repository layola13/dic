handset:/ˈhændsɛt/ (US), /ˈhændsɛt/ (UK)| n.|手机，电话听筒|A mobile phone.  Specifically, the part of a telephone that you hold to your ear and mouth.|例句：I need to buy a new handset. 我需要买一部新手机。

例句：The handset slipped from my grasp and fell to the floor.  手机从我的手中滑落到地板上。

近义词：mobile phone, cell phone, telephone, receiver|反义词: (No direct antonym.  Could consider "landline" in some contexts)|词性: noun
