e-float:/ˈiːfloʊt/ | n. | 电子浮动, 电子浮筒 | An electronic system or device designed to float or maintain buoyancy, often used in marine or aquatic applications.  This can refer to a variety of things, from a sensor that floats on the water surface to a platform designed for electronic equipment in a marine environment. | The company developed an e-float to monitor ocean currents.  该公司开发了一种电子浮标来监测洋流。 |  The e-float transmitted data wirelessly to the research vessel. 电子浮标将数据无线传输到研究船。 | synonyms: floating platform, buoy, sensor float | antonyms:  sink, submerge | noun
