calamist:/ˈkæləmɪst/| n.| 诽谤者；灾难预言者|A person who predicts disaster; a prophet of doom.|The calamist predicted the end of the world was near. 这个灾难预言者预言世界末日即将来临。|近义词: pessimist, doomsayer, alarmist|反义词: optimist|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *calamiste*, from Latin *calamitas* ("disaster, damage, loss"), from *calamus* ("reed, stalk").  Originally referred to someone afflicted by disaster, later evolving to mean someone who predicts or exaggerates disasters.
