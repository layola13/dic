dissepiment:/dɪˈsɛpɪmənt/ | n. | 隔膜，隔板 | A partition or dividing wall, especially in a plant ovary or coral. | The dissepiment separates the chambers of the fruit.  隔膜将果实的腔室隔开。| synonym: partition, septum, divider | antonym: connection, union | noun

dissepiment:/dɪˈsɛpɪmənt/ | n. | (解剖学)隔膜 | A thin membrane or partition, as in the human body. | The dissepiment between the nostrils was deviated. 鼻孔之间的隔膜偏斜了。| synonym: membrane, septum, diaphragm | antonym: opening, aperture | noun
