gutturo:/ɡʊˈtʊəroʊ/ /ɡʌˈtʊəroʊ/
adjective
喉音的；粗声粗气的
produced in the throat; harsh-sounding.
His voice was a low gutturo growl. 他的声音是低沉的喉音咆哮。
近义词: guttural, throaty, husky
反义词: clear, melodious, smooth
词性: adjective
