grayish:/ˈɡreɪɪʃ/| adj.|浅灰色的，略灰的|Somewhat gray; having a tinge of gray.|例句：The sky was a grayish white. 天空是灰白色的。|近义词：greyish, grizzled, silver, ashen|反义词:black, jet-black, dark|词性:形容词
词源:From gray + -ish.
