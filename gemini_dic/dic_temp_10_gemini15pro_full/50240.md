dependants:/dɪˈpɛndənts/| n.|受抚养者；依赖他人生活者|People with children or other dependants often find it difficult to work long hours. 有孩子或其他受抚养人的人通常很难长时间工作。|例句：He has three dependants: his wife and two young children. 他有三个受抚养人：他的妻子和两个年幼的孩子。

例句：The company's health insurance plan covers employees and their dependants. 公司的健康保险计划涵盖员工及其受抚养人。|近义词：dependents, wards|反义词: providers, supporters|词性:名词

dependant:/dɪˈpɛndənt/| adj.|依赖的；依靠的|The project is dependant on government funding. 这个项目依赖于政府资金。|例句：Her income is dependant on her husband's salary. 她的收入依赖于她丈夫的工资。

例句：The success of the project is dependant on everyone working together.  项目的成功取决于每个人的共同努力。|近义词：reliant, contingent|反义词: independent, autonomous|词性:形容词
