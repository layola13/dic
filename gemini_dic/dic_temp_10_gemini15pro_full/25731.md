buo: /ˈbuːoʊ/ | n. | 浮标，浮筒 | A float, typically a sealed, air-filled container used to support something in water. | The buo marked the channel for the boats.  浮标为船只标记了航道。| synonym: float, buoy | antonym: sinker, anchor | noun

buo: /ˈbuːoʊ/ | v. | 使浮起，支撑 | To keep afloat or support with a buoy. | The life jacket buoed him up until help arrived.  救生衣让他一直浮着直到救援到来。| synonym: float, support, uplift | antonym: sink, submerge | verb
