antonyms:/ˈæntənɪmz/ | n. |反义词|Words that have opposite meanings.|例句：Hot and cold are antonyms. 热和冷是反义词。

例句：'Big' and 'small' are antonyms.  大和小是反义词。|近义词：opposites|反义词：synonyms|词性：noun
