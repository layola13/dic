double-park: /ˈdʌbəl pɑːrk/ (US) /ˈdʌbl pɑːk/ (UK)| v. | 并排停车; 双排停车 | To park a vehicle alongside another vehicle that is already parked at the curb, thus obstructing traffic. | Example: He double-parked his car outside the store while he ran in to grab a few things. 他把车并排停在商店外面，然后跑进去拿了几样东西。

Example: Double-parking is illegal in most cities and can result in a hefty fine. 在大多数城市，双排停车是违法的，可能会导致巨额罚款。 | 近义词: park illegally | 反义词: park legally | 词性: 动词
