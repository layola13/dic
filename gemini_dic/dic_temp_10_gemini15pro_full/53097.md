diphthonging:/dɪfˈθɒŋɪŋ/ |n.|双元音化|The process by which a single vowel sound changes into a diphthong (a sound formed by the combination of two vowels in a single syllable).|The Great Vowel Shift involved a significant amount of diphthonging in the English language.|近义词: vowel shift, coalescence|反义词: monophthongization|词性: noun

diphthongize:/dɪfˈθɒŋaɪz/ |v.|使成双元音|To change a single vowel sound into a diphthong.|The vowel /iː/ diphthongized to /aɪ/ in certain dialects.|近义词: combine vowels|反义词: monophthongize|词性: verb
