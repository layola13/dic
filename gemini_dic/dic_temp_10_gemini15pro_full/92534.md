hypsiloid:/ˈhɪpsɪlɔɪd/| adj. n.| 舌骨的，舌骨形的；舌骨|Relating to or resembling the hyoid bone; the hyoid bone itself. |The hypsiloid apparatus is a cartilaginous and bony structure that supports the tongue.  舌骨器是一种支撑舌头的软骨和骨结构。

The doctor palpated the patient's hypsiloid. 医生触摸了病人的舌骨。|近义词：hyoid|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From Greek *hypsiloeidēs* "shaped like the letter upsilon (υ),"  from *hypsilon* (υ) + *-oeidēs* "-oid."  The hyoid bone is shaped somewhat like the Greek letter upsilon.
