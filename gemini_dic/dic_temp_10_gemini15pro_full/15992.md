bamboos:/ˌbæmˈbuz/ | n. |竹子；竹竿 |A tall, treelike grass having a hollow, jointed woody stem.  The stems are used for building, furniture, and many other purposes. | Pandas eat bamboos. 熊猫吃竹子。| synonym:cane, reed | antonym: none | noun
