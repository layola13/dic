half-light:/ˈhɑːfˌlaɪt/ /ˈhæfˌlaɪt/| n.|半明半暗；微光；朦胧的光线|A subdued or dim light; twilight. |The room was bathed in half-light. 房间里沐浴在朦胧的光线中。

half-light:/ˈhɑːfˌlaɪt/ /ˈhæfˌlaɪt/| n.|黎明或黄昏|The time of twilight. |The birds were singing in the half-light of dawn. 鸟儿在黎明的微光中歌唱。

half-light:/ˈhɑːfˌlaɪt/ /ˈhæfˌlaɪt/| n.|模糊不清的状态|A state of obscurity or vagueness. |The truth remained hidden in the half-light of speculation. 真相隐藏在模糊不清的猜测之中。


近义词:twilight, dusk, dawn, dim light, gloom|
反义词:bright light, full light, daylight|
词性:noun|

词源：half + light.  "half" 来自古英语 healf,  "light" 来自古英语 leoht.  组合在一起表示光线的一半，引申为微光、朦胧的光线。
