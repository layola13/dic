archimagus:/ˌɑːrkɪˈmeɪɡəs/ | n. | 大法师；首席魔法师 | A chief magician or wizard; the highest-ranking magician. |  The archimagus summoned a powerful storm. 大法师召唤了一场强大的风暴。|  The archimagus cast a spell to protect the kingdom. 大法师施放了一个咒语来保护王国。 | synonyms: sorcerer, wizard, magician | antonyms: muggle, layman | noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *archimagus, from Greek arkhimagos, from arkhi- ‘chief’ + magos ‘magician, priest.’
