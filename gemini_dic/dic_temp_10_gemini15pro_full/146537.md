overglamourized: /ˌoʊvərˈɡlæmərˌaɪzd/ (US) /ˌəʊvəˈɡlæmərˌaɪzd/ (UK) | adj. | 过分美化的，过分渲染的 |  Presented as more glamorous than it actually is.  Often implies a superficial or unrealistic portrayal.  Derived from "over-" (excessively) + "glamorize" (to make something seem glamorous) + "-ed" (past participle suffix). | The movie overglamourized the life of a rock star, ignoring the struggles and hardships. 这部电影过分美化了摇滚明星的生活，忽略了其中的挣扎和艰辛。

The advertising campaign overglamourized the product, making it seem far more effective than it actually was.  这个广告宣传活动过分美化了该产品，使其看起来比实际效果要好得多。| 近义词: romanticized, idealized, exaggerated | 反义词: realistic, understated, downplayed | 词性: 形容词
