cerulein:/ˌsɪˈruːliɪn/| n.|青蛙皮素；雨蛙肽|A peptide hormone, similar to gastrin and cholecystokinin, originally isolated from the skin of certain Australian frogs.|Cerulein stimulates gastric, pancreatic, and biliary secretion, and smooth muscle contraction in the gastrointestinal tract.  青蛙皮素刺激胃、胰腺和胆汁分泌，以及胃肠道平滑肌收缩。|Synonym: caerulein|Antonym: None|noun
