familial:/fəˈmɪliəl/
adjective
家族的；家庭的；亲属的
Relating to or occurring within a family or its members.
Familial hypercholesterolemia is a genetic disorder. 家族性高胆固醇血症是一种遗传性疾病。
Her cancer was not familial. 她的癌症不是家族遗传的。
近义词: genetic, hereditary, inherited
反义词: nonfamilial, acquired
