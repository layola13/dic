hermitress:/ˈhɜːrmɪtrɪs/ | n. | 女隐士 | A woman who lives in seclusion, often for religious reasons. |  She lived as a hermitress in the mountains, dedicating her life to prayer. 她像个女隐士一样住在山上，毕生致力于祈祷。

The hermitress rarely ventured into town, preferring the solitude of her forest dwelling.  这位女隐士很少冒险进城，更喜欢她森林住所的宁静。 | 近义词: recluse, anchorite, nun | 反义词: socialite, extrovert | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *ermitece, from ermite "hermit" (from Late Latin eremita, from Greek erēmitēs "desert dweller," from erēmos "desert, solitary") + -esse (feminine suffix).
