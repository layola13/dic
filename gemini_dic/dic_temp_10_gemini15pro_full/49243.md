deleterious: /ˌdɛlɪˈtɪəriəs/| adj.|有害的，不利的|Causing harm or damage.|例句：The chemical has deleterious effects on the environment. 这种化学物质对环境有不利影响。

例句：Smoking is deleterious to your health. 吸烟对你的健康有害。

近义词：harmful, damaging, injurious, detrimental, adverse|反义词：beneficial, advantageous, helpful, salutary|词性:形容词
