dehumanization:/diːˌhjuːmənaɪˈzeɪʃn/ /diːˌhjuːmənəˈzeɪʃn/
n.
非人化，去人性化
The process of depriving a person or group of positive human qualities.
The propaganda campaign engaged in the systematic dehumanization of the enemy.  宣传运动对敌人进行了系统的非人化。

The dehumanization of refugees is often used to justify denying them basic rights.  难民的非人化经常被用来作为剥夺他们基本权利的理由。

dehumanizing:/diːˌhjuːmənaɪzɪŋ/ /diːˌhjuːmənəˌzeɪzɪŋ/
adj.
使失去人性的，非人化的
Causing someone to be regarded as less than human.
Working in the factory was a dehumanizing experience. 在工厂工作是一种非人化的经历。

The prisoners were subjected to dehumanizing treatment. 囚犯们受到了非人化的待遇。

dehumanize:/diːˌhjuːmənaɪz/ /diːˌhjuːmənəˌzeɪz/
vt.
使失去人性，使非人化
To deprive of human qualities or attributes.
War can dehumanize people. 战争可以使人失去人性。

Slavery dehumanizes both the slave and the slave owner. 奴隶制使奴隶和奴隶主都失去了人性。

近义词: degradation, brutalization, depersonalization
反义词: humanization, personalization
词性:  noun (dehumanization), adjective (dehumanizing), verb (dehumanize)
