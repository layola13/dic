discutient:/dɪˈskjuːʃənt/ | adj. n. | 讨论的；消散的；讨论者；消散剂 |  (adj.) Dissolving or dispersing morbid matter; promoting dispersion of tumors, inflammation, etc. (n.) A discutient medicine or application. | (adj.) The doctor prescribed a discutient ointment for the swelling. 医生给肿胀处开了消散软膏。 (n.) From Late Latin *discutiēns, present participle of discutere "to disperse," from dis- "apart" + quatere "to shake."
