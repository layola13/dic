misentreat:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtriːt/| vt.|虐待，苛待|To treat badly or cruelly.|He misentreats his animals, never giving them proper food or shelter. 他虐待他的动物，从不给他们提供适当的食物或住所。|例句：The wicked stepmother misentreated her stepdaughter. 恶毒的继母虐待她的继女。|近义词：abuse, maltreat, mistreat|反义词: cherish, protect|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (wrongly) + entreat (to treat).  The "entreat" part ultimately comes from the Old French "entraiter"  (to treat, handle), and further back from the Latin "tractare" (to handle, manage).
