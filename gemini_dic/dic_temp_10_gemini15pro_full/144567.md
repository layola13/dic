outwandered:/ˌaʊtˈwɑːndərd/
verb
漫游过；游遍；漂泊过
having wandered extensively or beyond usual limits.
He had outwandered his companions and was now alone in the vast wilderness. 他游荡得比同伴们远，现在独自一人在广阔的荒野中。
Synonyms: roamed, rambled, traversed, explored
Antonyms: stayed, remained, settled
词性：动词(past participle)
