anilinism:/ˈænɪlɪnɪzəm/
n.
苯胺中毒
Poisoning by aniline, marked by cyanosis, headache, weakness, and dizziness.
Exposure to aniline can cause anilinism, a serious health condition. 暴露于苯胺会导致苯胺中毒，这是一种严重的健康状况。
Synonyms: aniline poisoning
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "health" or "wellness" in a broad sense)
