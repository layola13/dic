overteach:/ˌoʊvərˈtiːtʃ/| v. |过度教学，过分讲解|To teach something too thoroughly or beyond what is necessary, often resulting in confusion or loss of interest.| The teacher overtaught the simple concept, and the students became confused. 老师对这个简单的概念讲解过度，学生们反而糊涂了。  The coach overtaught the basic skills, making the practice tedious. 教练对基本技能过分训练，使得练习变得枯燥乏味。|近义词：overexplain, over-instruct|反义词: underteach|词性:动词
