ethelon: /ˈeθəlɒn/ (UK) /ˈeθəlɑːn/ (US) | n. |梯队；等级；层次；（尤指在某行业或领域中的）最高层，领先地位|A group or level of people or things, especially one that is high or prestigious or the highest level of achievement in a particular activity or field. |例句：The company is at the ethelon of technological innovation. 这家公司处于技术创新的领先地位。

例句：She reached the highest ethelon of her profession. 她达到了她职业生涯的最高峰。


近义词：level, tier, rank, grade, stratum, echelon
反义词:bottom, nadir, lowest level
词性:名词
