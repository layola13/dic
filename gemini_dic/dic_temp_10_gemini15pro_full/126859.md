napoleonic:/nəˌpoʊliˈɑːnɪk/| adj.|拿破仑的；拿破仑时代的；像拿破仑的；专横的|Of or relating to Napoleon Bonaparte or his reign; characteristic of or resembling Napoleon, especially in his ambition, energy, or authoritarianism.|例句：The Napoleonic Wars reshaped the map of Europe.  拿破仑战争重塑了欧洲版图。

例句：He had a Napoleonic ambition to conquer the business world. 他有着拿破仑式的征服商界的野心。|近义词：imperial, authoritarian, dictatorial|反义词：democratic, egalitarian|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Napoleon + -ic.  Napoleon itself comes from the Italian name Napoleone, ultimately from a Greek name meaning "lion of the new city."
