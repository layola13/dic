outexecuted:/ˌaʊtˈɛksɪˌkjuːtɪd/ | v. |  超越执行；在执行方面胜过 |  Outperform in execution; to execute something better than someone else. | Our team outexecuted theirs in the final quarter, securing the win. 我们的团队在最后一节的执行力超过了他们，从而确保了胜利。 | The company outexecuted its competitors by streamlining its production process.  这家公司通过精简生产流程，在执行方面胜过了竞争对手。 | 近义词: outperformed, surpassed, excelled | 反义词: underperformed | 词性: 动词

词源:
"out-" (prefix) + "execute" (verb)

"out-" 表示超过，胜过。
"execute" 表示执行，完成。
