corypheus:/kɔːˈriːfiəs/ /ˌkɔːrɪˈfiːəs/ | n. | 领袖；巨擘；合唱指挥 |  A leader, especially of a movement, rebellion, or political party; a prominent figure who acts as a leader or chief.  Derived from Greek "koryphaios" meaning "leader of the chorus." Originally referred to the leader of the chorus in ancient Greek drama, who also trained the chorus and sometimes spoke the prologue. | The politician emerged as the corypheus of the new reform movement.  这位政治家成为新改革运动的领袖。| The tech entrepreneur is considered a corypheus in the world of artificial intelligence.  这位科技企业家被认为是人工智能领域的巨擘。 | synonyms: leader, chief, head, principal, mastermind, conductor | antonyms: follower, subordinate | noun
