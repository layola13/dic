apostrophizing: /əˈpɒstrəfaɪzɪŋ/
verb
以撇号表示省略; 省略;  用呼语称呼;对…作呼语
Addressing something inanimate or absent as if it were present and able to understand.
He stood there, apostrophizing the empty sky. 他站在那里，对着空旷的天空说话。
addressing, invoking, appealing to
ignoring, neglecting
