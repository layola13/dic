backboned:/ˈbækˌboʊnd/| adj.|有脊骨的，有骨气的，坚定的|Having a backbone; vertebrate.  Figuratively, having strength of character; resolute.|The museum displayed the skeletons of several backboned animals. 博物馆展出了几种脊椎动物的骨骼。|近义词：vertebrate, resolute, staunch, determined|反义词：spineless, invertebrate, weak|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From back + bone + -ed.  The figurative sense of "having strength of character" developed from the literal meaning.
