megatheroid: /ˈmɛɡəˌθɪrɔɪd/ | n. adj. |大地懒；大地懒类的 | An extinct group of large, sloth-like mammals that inhabited the Americas during the Pleistocene epoch.  They were related to modern sloths but much larger, some reaching the size of elephants.  Can also be used as an adjective to describe something related to or resembling these animals. |  Example 1: The Megatherium was a giant ground sloth, a megatheroid that roamed South America.  大地懒是一种巨大的地懒，一种漫游在南美洲的大地懒类动物。

Example 2:  Scientists discovered a new megatheroid fossil in a cave. 科学家在一个山洞里发现了一种新的大地懒类动物化石。 | 近义词: ground sloth, giant sloth | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "small mammal") | 词性: noun, adjective
