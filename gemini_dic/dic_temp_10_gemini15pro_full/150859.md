paragraphed:/ˈpærəɡræftd/
adjective|分段的，分成段落的|Divided into paragraphs.
The essay was carefully paragraphed and easy to read.|这篇文章仔细地分成了段落，很容易阅读。
近义词:sectioned, divided
反义词:unparagraphed, continuous


ETYMOLOGY->
From paragraph + -ed.  Ultimately from the Greek *paragraphos*, meaning "written beside," from *para-* "beside" + *graphein* "to write."
