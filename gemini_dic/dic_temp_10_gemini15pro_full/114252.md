mailshot:/ˈmeɪlʃɒt/ (UK) /ˈmeɪlʃɑːt/ (US)| n.|邮件群发，邮寄广告|A bulk mailing of advertising or promotional material, sent to a large number of people at the same time.|The company sent out a mailshot advertising their new product. 这家公司群发邮件宣传他们的新产品。|Synonyms: circular, flyer, mailer, advertising campaign, direct mail, junk mail|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider "personal communication" or "targeted advertising" as contrasting concepts)|noun
