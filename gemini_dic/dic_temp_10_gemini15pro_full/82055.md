grists:/ɡrɪsts/
n.
谷物;可供利用的东西
grain to be ground; material for a specified process
The diaries provided rich grists for her biographer's mill.  这些日记为她的传记作者提供了丰富的素材。

近义词:material, resources, stuff
反义词:lack, dearth, scarcity
词性:名词


词源:
From Middle English *gristes, plural of grist (“grinding”), from Old English grist (“grinding, gnashing, grinding of teeth”), from Proto-Germanic *gristaną (“to gnash, grind”), from Proto-Indo-European *ghrísd- (“to rub, scratch, scrape”). Cognate with Old Norse grist (“gnashing of teeth”). Related to grind.
