clamp:/klæmp/| n. vt.|夹具，钳子；夹紧，固定|A device used to hold or secure objects tightly together. To fasten or hold something firmly with a clamp.|The carpenter used a clamp to hold the pieces of wood together. 木匠用夹具把木块夹在一起。 He clamped the two pieces of metal together. 他把两块金属夹在一起。|近义词：fasten, secure, grip, vice|反义词:release, loosen|词性:名词, 动词

clamp:/klæmp/| v.|夹紧，固定|To fasten or hold something firmly with a clamp.|He clamped the papers together so they wouldn't blow away. 他把文件夹在一起，这样它们就不会被风吹走了。|近义词：fasten, secure, grip|反义词:release, loosen|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch or Middle Low German *klampe, related to Dutch klamp and German Klammer.  Ultimately from a Proto-Germanic root meaning "to press, squeeze."
