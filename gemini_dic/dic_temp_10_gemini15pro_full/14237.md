autocratic:/ˌɔːtəˈkrætɪk/ | adj. | 独裁的，专制的 | Relating to a ruler who has absolute power.  Exercising power in a domineering and peremptory manner. |  The autocratic leader made all the decisions without consulting his team.  独裁的领导者在没有咨询他的团队的情况下做出了所有决定。

The company's autocratic management style stifled creativity. 公司的专制管理风格扼杀了创造力。 |  dictatorial, tyrannical, despotic, authoritarian | democratic, egalitarian | adjective
