hemitrope:/ˈhɛmɪˌtroʊp/| n.|半晶；孪晶；偏方面体|A crystal twinned in such a manner that one half has been rotated 180 degrees about an axis perpendicular to a plane of symmetry and composition.|The hemitrope showed a distinct line of demarcation where the two halves met.|Synonym: twin crystal|Antonym: single crystal|Noun
