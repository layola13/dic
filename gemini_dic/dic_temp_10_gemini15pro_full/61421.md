emboldener:/ɪmˈboʊldənər/| n.|使大胆的人/物；激励者|Something that emboldens someone.  It can be a person, an event, or a substance.|The victory emboldened the troops.  这场胜利鼓舞了军队。|近义词:encourager, inspirer, stimulant|反义词:deterrent, inhibitor|noun
