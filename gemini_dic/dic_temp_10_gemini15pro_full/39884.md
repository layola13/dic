constituents:/kənˈstɪtʃuənts/ | n. | 成分; 要素; 选民; |  People or things that combine to form something.  A member of a constituency, especially one who is entitled to elect a representative to a legislative or other body. | The constituents of the mixture were carefully measured. 这种混合物的成分经过仔细测量。 | components, elements, ingredients | opposites, wholes | noun

constituent:/kənˈstɪtʃuənt/ | adj. | 构成的; 组成的; |  Being a part of a whole. Having power to elect or appoint.  |  Hydrogen and oxygen are constituent elements of water. 氢和氧是水的组成元素。 | component, elemental, integral | non-constituent, separate | adjective
