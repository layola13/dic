flavoury:/ˈfleɪvəri/ (British) /ˈfleɪvərri/ (American)| adj.|美味的，有香味的，有味道的|Having a pleasant and distinct taste or smell.  Relates to the taste and smell of food, suggesting it is tasty and well-seasoned.|The stew was rich and flavoury.  这道炖菜味道浓郁可口。|Synonyms: tasty, delicious, savory, palatable, aromatic|Antonyms: bland, tasteless, insipid|adjective

flavourful:/ˈfleɪvərfəl/ (American)| adj.|美味的，有香味的，有味道的|Having a pleasant and distinct taste or smell. Primarily used in American English.|The chef created a flavourful dish with a blend of spices.  厨师用混合香料调制出一道美味佳肴。|Synonyms: tasty, delicious, savory, palatable, aromatic|Antonyms: bland, tasteless, insipid|adjective

Etymology:
