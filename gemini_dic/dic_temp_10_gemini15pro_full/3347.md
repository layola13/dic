affrayment:/əˈfreɪmənt/
n.|恐吓，惊恐|The state of being affrayed; fright, terror.|The affrayment of the villagers was palpable after the dragon sighting. 村民们在目睹巨龙之后，恐惧之情显而易见。|近义词：fright, terror, fear, alarm, dismay|反义词：calm, composure, peace, tranquility|词性:名词

Etymology: From Middle English *affraiement, from Old French affraiement, from affraier "to frighten," from Vulgar Latin *exfridare, from Latin frigidus "cold."  The sense development likely arose from the physical sensation of coldness associated with fear.
