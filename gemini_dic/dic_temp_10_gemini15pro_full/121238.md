miserables:/ˈmɪzərəbəlz/| n.|悲惨的人，可怜的人；<Les Misérables>《悲惨世界》|People living in poverty often lead miserable lives. 生活在贫困中的人们往往过着悲惨的生活。|The miserables huddled together for warmth. 这些可怜的人挤在一起取暖。|近义词：wretches, unfortunates, sufferers|反义词：fortunates, privileged|Noun

miserable:/ˈmɪzərəbəl/| adj.|悲惨的；痛苦的；可怜的；糟糕的|The weather was miserable - cold and rainy. 天气很糟糕——又冷又下雨。|I felt miserable after failing the exam. 考试不及格后，我感到很痛苦。|近义词：unhappy, wretched, pathetic, awful|反义词：happy, joyful, cheerful, excellent|Adjective
