greenhill:/ˈɡriːnˌhɪl/| n.|绿色的山丘；绿山|A hill covered with green vegetation.|We climbed the greenhill to enjoy the panoramic view. 我们爬上绿色的山丘欣赏全景。|近义词：grassy hill, verdant hill|反义词:barren hill, brown hill|名词

词源:
From Middle English *grene hulle*, from Old English *grēne hyll* (green hill).  The word is a compound of *grēne* (green) and *hyll* (hill).

用法说明:
"Greenhill" is a descriptive compound noun referring to a hill covered with green vegetation. It is generally used in a literal sense.
