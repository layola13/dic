borrowable:/ˈbɒrəʊəb(ə)l/ /ˈbɑːroʊəbl/| adj.|可借的，可借用的|Something that is borrowable can be borrowed.  It means that it is permissible and possible to borrow the item.|例句：Are your notes borrowable? 你的笔记可以借吗？|近义词：loanable, lendable|反义词:unborrowable, non-lendable|词性:adjective
词源: From "borrow" + "-able".  "Borrow" comes from Old English *borgian, from Proto-Germanic *borganą, meaning "to borrow, lend, pledge."  "-able" is a suffix of Latin origin, meaning "capable of, worthy of, tending to."
