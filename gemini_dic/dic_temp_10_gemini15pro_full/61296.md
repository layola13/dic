embargo:/ɪmˈbɑːrɡoʊ/ (US) /ɪmˈbɑːɡəʊ/ (UK)| n. vt.|禁运，禁止贸易；禁止发表或公开|An official ban on trade or other commercial activity with a particular country.|The US imposed an embargo on trade with Cuba.|近义词：ban, prohibition, restriction, sanction|反义词:allowance, permission|noun, verb

embargo:/ɪmˈbɑːrɡoʊ/ (US) /ɪmˈbɑːɡəʊ/ (UK)| vt.|禁止发表或公开|To officially ban the release or publication of something.|The government embargoed the report until after the election.|近义词：suppress, withhold, censor|反义词:release, publish|verb
