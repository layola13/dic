greenhead:/ˈɡriːnˌhed/| n.|绿头苍蝇; 绿头马蝇; 一种咬人的苍蝇 (尤指雌性);|A greenhead is a type of biting fly, especially a female, known for its metallic green head and painful bite. These flies are common in coastal areas and wetlands.|例句：The greenheads were so bad at the beach that we had to leave.  海滩上的绿头苍蝇太多了，我们不得不离开。

例句：She got bitten by a greenhead while kayaking.  她在划皮艇时被绿头苍蝇咬了。|近义词：horsefly, stable fly, deer fly|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
