extrorsal:/ɛkˈstrɔːrsl/ | adj. | 外生的，向外弯曲的 | Turned or directed outwards; specifically (Botany) designating anthers that dehisce towards the periphery of the flower. | The extrorsal anthers released pollen towards the petals.  外生的花药向花瓣方向释放花粉。| The fern's sporangia were extrorsal, facing away from the stem. 蕨类植物的孢子囊是外生的，朝向茎干的反方向。|  近义词: outward, exterior | 反义词: introrse, inward | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  From Latin *extrorsus* "outward," from *extra* "outside" + *versus* "turned," past participle of *vertere* "to turn."
