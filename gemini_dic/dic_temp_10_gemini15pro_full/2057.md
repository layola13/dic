acupuncturing: /ˈækjʊpʌŋktʃərɪŋ/
noun
针灸；针刺疗法
The practice of inserting thin needles into specific points on the body to treat illness or relieve pain.
She tried acupuncture to relieve her back pain. 她尝试针灸来缓解背痛。
Acupuncturing can be used to treat a variety of ailments. 针灸可以用来治疗各种疾病。
synonyms: acupuncture treatment, needle therapy
antonyms: conventional medicine, Western medicine
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *acupunctura, from acus "needle" + punctura "pricking, puncture".  The English word is a back-formation from the earlier acupuncturist.
