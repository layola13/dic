docent:/ˈdoʊsənt/ | n. | 讲解员，博物馆讲解员 | A person who acts as a guide, typically on a voluntary basis, in a museum, art gallery, or zoo, and provides information to visitors on the exhibits. |  The docent led the tour group through the ancient Egyptian exhibit.  讲解员带领旅游团参观了古埃及展览。| synonyms: guide, lecturer, instructor, teacher | antonyms: student, pupil | noun

docent:/ˈdəʊs(ə)nt/ | n. |  大学讲师 | A university lecturer or teacher of the lowest academic rank. (chiefly US) | The docent gave a fascinating lecture on the history of art.  这位讲师做了一个关于艺术史的精彩讲座。| synonyms: lecturer, instructor, professor, teacher | antonyms: student, pupil | noun
