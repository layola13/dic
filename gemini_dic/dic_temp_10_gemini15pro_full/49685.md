demitone:/ˈdɛmɪˌtəʊn/ (UK), /ˈdɛmiˌtoʊn/ (US)| n.|半音|A musical interval of a half step, the smallest interval used in Western tonal music.  It is the difference in pitch between two adjacent notes in a 12-tone scale.|The piece is full of demitones, giving it a melancholic feel. 这首曲子充满了半音，使其带有忧郁的感觉。|Synonyms: half step, half tone|Antonyms: whole tone, whole step|noun
