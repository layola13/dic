adjective

The company is committed to a decarbonized future.  该公司致力于一个脱碳的未来。

decarbonize:/diːˈkɑːrbənaɪz/
verb
脱碳
To remove carbon from something, or to reduce carbon emissions.
The government plans to decarbonize the power sector by 2050. 政府计划到2050年实现电力行业的脱碳。
They are exploring ways to decarbonize the transportation system. 他们正在探索交通系统脱碳的方法。
Synonyms: neutralize carbon emissions, reduce carbon footprint
Antonyms: increase carbon emissions, pollute
词性: 动词
