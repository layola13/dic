overpay:/ˌoʊvərˈpeɪ/ | v. | 多付，付得过多 | to pay someone too much money for something | Example: I think I overpaid for that car. 我觉得我那辆车买贵了。 | Example:  Don't overpay your employees, but don't underpay them either. 不要多付员工工资，但也不要少付。 | Synonyms: overspend, compensate excessively | Antonyms: underpay, shortchange | verb

overpayment:/ˌoʊvərˈpeɪmənt/ | n. | 多付的款项，超额付款 | an amount of money that is more than the amount that should have been paid | Example: I received an overpayment of $50 on my last paycheck. 我的上一次工资单多付了50美元。 | Example: The company issued an apology for the overpayment and promised to rectify the situation. 公司就多付款项道歉，并承诺纠正这种情况。 | Synonyms: excess payment, overspend | Antonyms: underpayment, shortfall | noun
