non-resisting:/ˌnɒn rɪˈzɪstɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːn rɪˈzɪstɪŋ/| adj.|不抵抗的，无抵抗的，顺从的|Not offering resistance; submissive.|The non-resisting crowd was easily dispersed by the police.  警察很容易就驱散了不抵抗的群众。|Example: The lamb was non-resisting as the farmer carried it away.  小羊羔被农场主抱走时没有反抗。|Synonyms: passive, yielding, submissive, compliant|Antonyms: resisting, defiant, opposing|adjective

non-resisting:/ˌnɒn rɪˈzɪstɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːn rɪˈzɪstɪŋ/| n.|不抵抗者|A person who does not resist.|The non-resistings were often persecuted for their beliefs. 不抵抗者经常因为他们的信仰而受到迫害。|Example:  Historically, non-resistings often faced social ostracism. 从历史上看，不抵抗者经常面临社会排斥。|Synonyms: pacifist, passivist|Antonyms: rebel, fighter|noun
