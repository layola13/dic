instigant:/ˈɪnstɪɡənt/| n.|煽动者；刺激物|Something that instigates or incites an action or feeling. |The instigant of the riot was never identified.  这场骚乱的煽动者从未被确认。|近义词：agitator, provoker, inciter, stimulator|反义词：pacifier, deterrent, inhibitor|noun

instigant:/ˈɪnstɪɡənt/| adj.|煽动的；刺激的|Serving to instigate; provocative. |His instigant words sparked a heated debate. 他煽动性的话引发了激烈的辩论。|近义词：provocative, inflammatory, inciting|反义词：soothing, calming, pacifying|adjective
