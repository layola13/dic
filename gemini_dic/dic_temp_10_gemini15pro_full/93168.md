igniting:/ɪɡˈnaɪtɪŋ/
verb
点燃，引发
the process of setting something on fire or starting to burn; also, arousing strong feelings or excitement
The chef was igniting the brandy for the flambé. 厨师正在点燃白兰地准备做火焰菜。
kindle, spark, inflame
extinguish, quench
v.
