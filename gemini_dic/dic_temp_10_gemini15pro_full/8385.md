anti-romans:/ˌæntiˈroʊmənz/ | n. |反浪漫小说；非传统小说| Novels that subvert or reject the conventions of traditional romance, often focusing on the darker aspects of relationships or challenging idealized notions of love and heroism. |  Example 1:  His new book is considered an anti-romance, exploring the complexities and disappointments of marriage. 他的新书被认为是一部反浪漫小说，探索了婚姻的复杂性和失望。

Example 2: The film is an anti-romance, portraying love as a messy and often painful experience.  这部电影是一部反浪漫小说，将爱情描绘成一种混乱且经常痛苦的经历。 | Near Synonyms:  dark romance, unconventional romance, subversive romance | Antonyms:  romance novel, romantic comedy | Noun
