emulators:/ˈɛmjʊleɪtərz/
n.|仿真器，模拟器|A device or program that imitates the behavior of another system, especially a computer system.|I use an emulator to play old video games on my PC. 我用模拟器在我的电脑上玩老游戏。|synonyms: simulator, imitator|antonyms: original, authentic|noun

emulator:/ˈɛmjʊleɪtər/
n.|仿真器，模拟器|A person who tries to equal or excel the qualities or actions of another.|He is an emulator of Michael Jordan. 他是迈克尔·乔丹的模仿者。|synonyms: imitator, copier|antonyms: original, innovator|noun
