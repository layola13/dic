beatnik: /ˈbiːtnɪk/| n.|垮掉的一代(成员)|A member of the Beat Generation, a group of people, especially young people, in the 1950s and early 1960s who rejected conventional society and its values, often expressing themselves through art, literature, and music.|Jack Kerouac's novel "On the Road" is a classic of beatnik literature.  杰克·凯鲁亚克的小说《在路上》是垮掉的一代文学的经典之作。

|Synonym: hipster, nonconformist|Antonym: conformist|Noun|
