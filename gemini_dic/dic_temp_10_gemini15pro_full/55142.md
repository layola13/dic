dissectible:/dɪˈsɛktəbl/ /daɪˈsɛktəbl/
adjective
可解剖的; 可剖析的
Capable of being dissected; that may be divided into parts for examination and study.
The frog is a commonly used dissectible animal in biology classes.  青蛙是生物课上常用的解剖动物。
The complex issue is dissectible into smaller, more manageable parts.  这个复杂的问题可以分解成更小、更容易处理的部分。
近义词: analyzable, divisible
反义词: indivisible, inseparable
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *dissectibilis, from Latin dissectus (past participle of dissecare "to cut apart"), from dis- "apart" + secare "to cut".
