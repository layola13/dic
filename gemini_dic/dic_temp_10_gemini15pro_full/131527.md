noncomicalness:/ˌnɑːnˈkɑːmɪkəlnəs/
n.
缺乏幽默; 不滑稽; 严肃性
lack of humor or amusement; seriousness
His noncomicalness made him seem aloof. 他的不苟言笑使他显得冷漠。
近义词：seriousness, solemnity, gravity
反义词：humor, comicality, levity
词性：名词
