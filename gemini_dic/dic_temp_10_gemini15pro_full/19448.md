bicrescentic: /ˌbaɪkrɪˈsɛntɪk/
adjective
新月形的，双月牙形的
Having the shape of two crescents, often with their concave sides facing each other.  Resembling two joined or overlapping crescents.
The bicrescentic shape of the amulet was believed to ward off evil spirits.  这个护身符的新月形被认为可以驱除邪灵。
The artist used a bicrescentic motif throughout the painting.  艺术家在整幅画中都使用了双月牙形的图案。
近义词: crescent-shaped, lunate
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "straight" or "linear" depending on context)
词性: adjective
