inculpate:/ɪnˈkʌlpeɪt/ | vt. | 控告；归罪于；连累 | To accuse or blame someone for something, especially a crime. | The evidence was not enough to inculpate him in the robbery.  证据不足以证明他参与了抢劫。| Synonyms: incriminate, accuse, blame, charge | Antonyms: exonerate, absolve, acquit | verb

inculpate:/ɪnˈkʌlpeɪt/ | vt. | 使负罪；牵连 |  To involve someone in guilt or wrongdoing. |  His careless words inculpated his friend in the scandal. 他粗心的话把他朋友牵扯进了丑闻。 | Synonyms: implicate, involve, entangle | Antonyms: extricate, disengage, clear | verb
