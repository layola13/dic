daywear:/ˈdeɪˌwer/ | n. | 日装，日常服装 | Clothing suitable for daytime wear, as opposed to evening wear or active wear.  | She prefers comfortable daywear to formal attire. 她喜欢舒适的日装而不是正式的服装。 |  She selected a simple, chic outfit from her daywear collection for the luncheon. 她从她的日装系列中选择了一套简洁别致的服装去参加午宴。 | 近义词：casual clothes, everyday clothes | 反义词：evening wear, formal wear | 词性: 名词

词源:  Compound of "day" and "wear".  "Day" from Old English "dæg," and "wear" from Old English "werian" (to wear).
