feng:/fʌŋ/ | n. | 风水 |  A Chinese philosophical system of harmonizing everyone with the surrounding environment.  Based on the belief that the land is alive and filled with Chi, or energy. |  The house has good feng shui. 这房子风水好。 | synonyms: geomancy | antonyms: N/A | noun

feng:/fʌŋ/ | n. | 风 | Wind; air in motion. This pronunciation is a less common transliteration of the Chinese word for wind. | A cool feng blew through the open window. 一阵凉风从敞开的窗户吹进来。 | synonyms: breeze, gale, wind | antonyms: stillness, calm | noun
