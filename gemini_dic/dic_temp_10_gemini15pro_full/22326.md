bonneting:/ˈbɒnɪtɪŋ/ /ˈbɑːnɪtɪŋ/
n.
引擎盖，女帽，戴引擎盖的行为，(旧) 殴打
The act of putting on or wearing a bonnet.  The act of covering with a bonnet, as a hawk's head. A scolding; a reprimand.
She received a bonneting from her mother for staying out late. 她因为在外面待到很晚而被母亲责骂。
The falconer placed a bonneting over the hawk's head.  驯鹰人把一个帽子盖在鹰头上。
synonyms: scolding, reprimand, rebuke, chastisement, dressing-down
antonyms: praise, commendation, compliment
