liquidation:/ˌlɪkwɪˈdeɪʃən/| n.|清算；偿付；清除，消灭|The process of selling assets to pay debts, or the process of closing down a company.  The act of getting rid of something.|例句：The company went into liquidation after failing to secure new funding.  这家公司在未能获得新的资金后进入了清算程序。

例句：The liquidation of the company's assets took several months.  该公司资产的清算花了几个月的时间。|近义词：dissolution, winding-up, clearance, disposal|反义词:establishment, founding|词性:noun
