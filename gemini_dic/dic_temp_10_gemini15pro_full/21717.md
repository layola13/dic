boardsailing:/ˈbɔːrdˌseɪlɪŋ/
n.
帆板运动
The sport of riding on a board with a sail attached.
He enjoys boardsailing on the weekends.  他周末喜欢玩帆板运动。

近义词：windsurfing
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps other water sports like waterskiing or kayaking could be considered conceptually opposite)
词性：noun
