abstractitious: /ˌæbstrækˈtɪʃəs/
adjective
抽象的，空洞的
Given to abstract reasoning; tending to separate ideas from practice; over-theoretical.  Existing only in theory; not concrete.
His abstractitious arguments had no bearing on the practical matters at hand. 他抽象的论点与手头的实际问题毫无关联。

The philosopher's abstractitious concepts were difficult for the average person to grasp. 这位哲学家抽象的概念让普通人难以理解。

近义词: abstract, theoretical, notional, conceptual, speculative
反义词: concrete, practical, pragmatic, realistic
词性: adjective
