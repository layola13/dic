basileo:/bæˈsɪlioʊ/
noun
国王，君主
A king or sovereign.
例句：The basileo addressed his subjects. 国王向他的臣民讲话。

例句：The young basileo inherited the throne at just eighteen.  这位年轻的君主在十八岁时继承了王位。

近义词：king, monarch, sovereign, ruler
反义词：subject, commoner
词性：noun
