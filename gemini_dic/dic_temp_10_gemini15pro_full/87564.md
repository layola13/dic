heteropathic:/ˌhɛtərəˈpæθɪk/
adjective
异源性的，由外来原因引起的
Caused by a different agent or disease; relating to a disease originating outside the body.
Heteropathic diseases are caused by external factors, unlike idiopathic diseases. 异源性疾病是由外部因素引起的，不像特发性疾病。
Exposure to certain toxins can lead to heteropathic conditions. 接触某些毒素会导致异源性疾病。
近义词: exogenous, external
反义词: endogenous, internal
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From hetero- (different, other) + -pathic (suffering, disease), from Greek heteros (other) + pathos (suffering).
