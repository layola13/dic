hemadromometer:/ˌhiːmədrəˈmɒmɪtər/ | n. |血流速度计，血流计|An instrument for measuring the velocity of the blood flow.|The doctor used a hemadromometer to check the patient's blood circulation. 医生用血流计检查病人的血液循环。|Synonyms: blood flow meter, rheometer|Antonyms: N/A|noun


Etymology:  From Greek *haima* (blood) + *dromos* (running, course) + *-meter* (instrument for measuring).
