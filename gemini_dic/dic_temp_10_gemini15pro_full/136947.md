nonspecializing:/nɒnˈspɛʃəlaɪzɪŋ/
adjective|非专业的，不专门化的|Not specializing in any particular area or activity; general. |例句：Nonspecializing doctors are often the first point of contact for patients. 非专科医生通常是病人的第一接触点。|近义词：general, generalized, versatile|反义词：specialized, specialist, expert|词性:adjective
Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "specializing" (present participle of "specialize," from Late Latin *specialisare, from Latin specialis "individual, particular," related to species "kind, sort").
