caaba:/ˈkɑːbə/ /ˈkæbə/| n.|克尔白|A small, cubical building in Mecca, the holiest shrine of Islam, toward which Muslims turn five times each day in prayer. It is believed to have been built by Abraham and Ishmael.|Pilgrims circle the Kaaba during the Hajj. 朝圣者在朝觐期间绕克尔白行走。|近义词:shrine, sanctuary|反义词:None|noun
