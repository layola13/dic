behoove:/bɪˈhuːv/| v.|有必要，应该，理应|It behooves us to be careful. 我们有必要小心谨慎。|It behooves the government to take action. 政府有必要采取行动。|近义词：befit, become, suit|反义词:  (No direct antonym, but one could use phrases like "be unnecessary for," or "be inappropriate for")|verb

It behooves us to be prepared.  我们有必要做好准备。

It behooves everyone to follow the rules.  每个人都有必要遵守规则。


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):

It is behoving us to consider all options.  我们有必要考虑所有选择。（较少使用）

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):

PHRASE->

It behooves someone to do something. 某人有必要做某事。

It would behoove someone to do something. 某人应该做某事。


词源：From Old English *behōfian "to be necessary, be fitting, be useful," from be- "by" + *hōfian "to be fit, be suitable."  Related to "behoof," now mostly used in the phrase "on/in someone's behalf."
