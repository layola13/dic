overdistantly:/ˌoʊvərˈdɪstəntli/| adv.|过于疏远地，冷漠地|From "over" (excessively) + "distant" (far away) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  Meaning excessively distant, either physically or emotionally.|She spoke to him overdistantly, as if he were a stranger. 她对他说话很冷淡，就好像他是陌生人一样。|He observed the scene overdistantly, showing no emotion. 他冷漠地观察着这一幕，没有表现出任何情绪。|近义词：coldly, aloofly, remotely, reservedly|反义词：warmly, affectionately, intimately|词性:副词
