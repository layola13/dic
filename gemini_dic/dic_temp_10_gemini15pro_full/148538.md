oversuspicious: /ˌoʊvər səˈspɪʃəs/ | adj. | 过分怀疑的，过于多疑的 | Excessively suspicious; inclined to suspect without sufficient reason.  Having or showing a tendency to suspect something wrong, evil, or harmful without sufficient evidence. |  She was oversuspicious of everyone after the break-in.  入室盗窃案发生后，她对每个人都过分怀疑。|  He gave me an oversuspicious look when I asked about the money.  当我问起钱的事情时，他给了我一个充满怀疑的眼神。 | synonyms: distrustful, wary, skeptical, leery, paranoid | antonyms: trusting, naive, gullible | adjective
