listenability:/ˌlɪsənəˈbɪləti/| n.|可听性；易懂性|The quality of being easy to listen to and understand.  It refers to how easy it is to understand spoken or recorded audio. It's influenced by factors like clarity, pace, and complexity of language.|The podcast has high listenability due to the host's clear voice and engaging stories.  这个播客由于主持人清晰的声音和引人入胜的故事而具有很高的可听性。|Synonyms: clarity, comprehensibility, intelligibility|Antonyms: incomprehensibility, unintelligibility|noun
