debatingly:/dɪˈbeɪtɪŋli/
adv.
争论地，辩论地
in a debating manner; argumentatively.  Engaging in or characterized by debate.
They discussed the matter debatingly for hours. 他们就此事争论了好几个小时。
近义词: argumentatively, contentiously, disputatiously
反义词: agreeably, harmoniously, concordantly
词性: 副词

Etymology:  From "debate" + "-ing" + "-ly".  "Debate" comes from Old French "debatre"  meaning "to fight, contend," from "de-" (down, completely) + "batre" (to beat).
