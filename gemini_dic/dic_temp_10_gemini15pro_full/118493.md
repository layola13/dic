mesmerizable:/ˈmezməˌraɪzəbl/| adj.|令人着迷的，迷人的|Able to be mesmerized; captivating; fascinating.  It describes something or someone that has the power to hold your attention completely, often in a way that feels almost magical.|例句：Her mesmerizing performance captivated the entire audience. 她令人着迷的表演吸引了全场观众。

例句：The mesmerizing sunset over the ocean was a breathtaking sight.  迷人的海上日落景色令人叹为观止。|近义词：captivating, enchanting, fascinating, hypnotic, spellbinding|反义词：uninteresting, boring, dull, repulsive|词性:形容词

词源: From mesmerize + -able.  Mesmerize itself comes from Franz Mesmer, an 18th-century Austrian physician who practiced hypnosis, then called mesmerism.
