creamed:/kriːmd/ | adj. | 奶油状的，涂满奶油的，（俚语）精疲力竭的 | Covered with or containing cream; (informal) utterly exhausted. |  The cake was creamed with butter and sugar.  蛋糕上涂满了奶油和糖。 | 近义词: buttery, rich, exhausted, wiped out | 反义词: plain, energized | 词性:形容词

creamed:/kriːmd/ | v. |  (cream的过去式和过去分词) 将…打成奶油状，在…上涂抹奶油，彻底击败 | Past tense and past participle of "cream". To beat (ingredients) to a creamy consistency; to cover with cream; to defeat soundly. |  She creamed the butter and sugar together. 她把黄油和糖一起打成奶油状。 | 近义词: whip, blend, thrash, rout | 反义词: lose to, be defeated by | 词性:动词


词源: From cream + -ed.  Ultimately from Latin "crama" (skimmings, froth).
