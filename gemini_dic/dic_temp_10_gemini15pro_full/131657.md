nonconceiving:/ˌnɒnkənˈsiːvɪŋ/
adjective
未怀孕的; 不孕的
Not conceiving; unable to conceive.
She was diagnosed as nonconceiving after years of trying to start a family.  她多年来一直试图组建家庭，但被诊断为不孕。
The couple sought medical advice for their nonconceiving status. 这对夫妇因不孕状况寻求医疗建议。

近义词: infertile, barren, sterile
反义词: fertile, fecund
词性: adjective
