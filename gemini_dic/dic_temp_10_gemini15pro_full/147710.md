overprescribed: /ˌoʊvərprɪˈskraɪbd/
adjective
过度处方的；开药过多的
prescribed too often or in too large amounts.
Antibiotics are often overprescribed for minor infections.  抗生素经常被过度用于轻微感染。
Doctors are being urged to avoid overprescribing antibiotics. 医生们被敦促避免过度开抗生素。
近义词: overused, excessively prescribed
反义词: underprescribed
词性: adjective

overprescribe: /ˌoʊvərprɪˈskraɪb/
verb
过度处方；开药过多
to prescribe (medication) too often or in too large amounts.
Some doctors tend to overprescribe antibiotics. 一些医生倾向于过度开抗生素。
Painkillers are often overprescribed for back pain. 止痛药经常被过度用于背痛。
近义词: overuse
反义词: underprescribe
词性: verb

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "prescribe" (verb meaning "to advise and authorize the use of (a medicine or treatment)")
