notoriously:/nəˈtɔːriəsli/ /noʊˈtɔːriəsli/| adv.|臭名昭著地；众所周知地 (通常指不好的方面)|This area is notoriously difficult to access.  这个地区众所周知难以进入。| notoriously difficult to access.  这个地区众所周知难以进入。|Synonyms: infamously, widely known (for something bad), disreputably|Antonyms: famously, favorably, well-regarded|adverb
Etymology->
From Latin *nōtōrius* (more known, more remarkable), comparative of *nōtus* (known), past participle of *nōscō* (I come to know, I get acquainted with).
