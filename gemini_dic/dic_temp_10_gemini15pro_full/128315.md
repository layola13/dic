neurotome:/ˈnjʊərəˌtoʊm/ | n. |神经节段; 神经刀|A segment of the neural tube and its associated mesoderm that gives rise to a spinal nerve and the structures it innervates.  A surgical instrument used to divide a nerve.|例句：The development of each neurotome is precisely controlled by Hox genes. 每个神经节段的发育都受到Hox基因的精确控制。

例句：The surgeon used a neurotome to sever the damaged nerve. 外科医生使用神经刀切断了受损的神经。|近义词：somite (in a broader sense) |反义词:  (No direct antonym exists) |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From neuro- + -tome  (Greek *neuro-* nerve + *-tomos* cutting)
