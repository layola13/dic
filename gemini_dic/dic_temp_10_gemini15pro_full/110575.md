limitation:/ˌlɪmɪˈteɪʃn/| n.|限制；局限性；缺陷；不足之处|A limitation is a restriction or a defect.  It's something that limits or restricts something else.|The main limitation of this study is its small sample size. 这项研究的主要局限性是样本量小。|Synonyms: restriction, constraint, curb, handicap, shortcoming, defect, flaw|Antonyms: advantage, strength, benefit|noun
