clear-cutness:/ˌklɪrˈkʌtnəs/ | n. | 清晰度，明确性 | The state or quality of being clear-cut; definiteness. | The clear-cutness of the evidence led to a swift verdict.  证据的清晰明确导致了快速判决。| Synonyms: clarity, precision, distinctness, sharpness | Antonyms: ambiguity, vagueness, obscurity, fuzziness | noun


clear-cut:/ˌklɪrˈkʌt/ | adj. | 清晰的，明确的 | Sharply defined; unambiguous. | The lawyer presented a clear-cut case. 律师提出了一个清晰的案例。| Synonyms: definite, precise, distinct, obvious | Antonyms: ambiguous, vague, obscure, fuzzy | adjective
