managey:/ˈmænɪdʒi/ | adj. | 疥癣的；患疥癣的；肮脏的 |Infested with mange; shabby, dirty, or run-down.  It suggests a state of neglect and poor condition, often associated with animals but also applicable to things.  Derived from "mange" + "-y." |  The managey dog needed veterinary care. 那只患疥癣的狗需要兽医护理。|  The old house looked managey and abandoned. 那栋老房子看起来破旧不堪，像是被遗弃了。| 近义词: scabby, scruffy, seedy, dilapidated | 反义词: clean, pristine, well-kept | 词性: adjective
