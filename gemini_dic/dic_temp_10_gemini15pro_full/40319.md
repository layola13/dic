contour:/ˈkɒntʊə(r) (UK), ˈkɑːntʊr (US)| n. v.|轮廓，外形；等高线；概要；(v.)画轮廓，画等高线|The contour of the hills was clearly visible against the setting sun.  山丘的轮廓在落日余晖的映衬下清晰可见。|近义词：outline, shape, profile|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "formlessness")|n. v.

contour:/ˈkɒntʊə(r) (UK), ˈkɑːntʊr (US)| v.|画轮廓，画等高线|She expertly contoured her face with makeup. 她熟练地用化妆品修饰脸部轮廓。|近义词：outline, delineate|反义词: (No direct antonym)|v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *contour* "outline, circuit," from Italian *contorno*, from *con* "with" + *torno* "a turn," from Latin *tornus* "lathe, turning."
