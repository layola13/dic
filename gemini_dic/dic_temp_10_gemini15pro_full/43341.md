crampit:/ˈkræmpɪt/ | n. | 羊胃肚; 填馅羊肚; (苏格兰) 填馅的牛肚 | A traditional Scottish dish made of sheep's stomach (or sometimes cow's stomach) stuffed with a mixture of seasoned offal, suet, oatmeal, and onions. |例句：Haggis is sometimes served with crampit.  羊杂碎有时与填馅羊肚一起食用。

例句：My grandmother used to make a delicious crampit. 我祖母过去常常做美味的填馅羊肚。| 近义词：haggis (though not always identical in ingredients), stuffed tripe | 反义词: None (specific dish) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From Scots *crampit,* probably related to *cramp,* meaning to stuff tightly, due to the way the ingredients are packed into the stomach.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *krampōną.*
