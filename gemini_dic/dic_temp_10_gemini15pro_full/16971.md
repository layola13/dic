bastardism:/ˈbæstərdɪzəm/
n.
私生子身份，非婚生子女状态，庶子身份
The state of being a bastard; illegitimate birth.
His bastardism prevented him from inheriting the title. 他的私生子身份使他无法继承爵位。

近义词: illegitimacy, bastardy
反义词: legitimacy

词性: noun

词源: From bastard + -ism.  "bastard" comes from Old French *bastard,  meaning "pack saddle," implying a child conceived on a makeshift bed, hence illegitimate.  "-ism" is a suffix forming nouns of action, state, condition, doctrine, etc.
