labials:/ˈleɪbiəlz/
n.
唇音；唇音字母
sounds articulated using the lips; letters representing such sounds
The sounds /p/, /b/, and /m/ are labials. /p/、/b/ 和 /m/ 都是唇音。
近义词:bilabial consonants
反义词:None
词性:noun
