chicaned:/ʃɪˈkeɪnd/
Verb
欺骗; 诡辩; 耍花招
deliberately caused delays or difficulties for someone or something, especially using complicated legal or bureaucratic processes.
The lawyer chicaned to delay the trial.  律师耍花招拖延审判。
近义词:deceived, tricked, outwitted, manipulated, defrauded
反义词:aided, assisted, helped, supported
词性:v.

chicanery:/ʃɪˈkeɪnəri/
Noun
欺骗; 诡辩; 耍花招
the use of trickery to achieve a political, financial, or legal purpose.
The investigation revealed widespread financial chicanery. 调查揭露了普遍存在的金融欺诈行为。
近义词:trickery, deception, subterfuge, fraud, guile
反义词:honesty, integrity, forthrightness, openness
词性:n.

词源:
From Spanish *chicane* "petty quarrel, legal quibble," perhaps related to Old French *chicaner* "to cavil, wrangle, haggle," and Middle French *chicane* "dispute, legal trick."
