huckleberry:/ˈhʌkəlˌbɛri/| n.|越橘，黑果，无价值的人|A small, round, dark blue or purplish berry that grows on bushes in North America.  It can also refer to a person of little importance or worth.|例句：We went huckleberry picking in the mountains. 我们去山上采越橘。

例句：He was just a huckleberry in their grand scheme. 在他们的宏伟计划中，他只是一个无足轻重的小人物。|近义词：bilberry, blueberry|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
