half-evaporated:/ˌhæf ɪˈvæpəreɪtɪd/
adj.
半蒸发的
partially evaporated; having lost some of its liquid content through evaporation
The half-evaporated puddle shimmered in the sunlight.  阳光下，半蒸发的泥潭闪闪发光。
近义词:partially evaporated, semi-evaporated
反义词:fully evaporated, condensed
词性:形容词
