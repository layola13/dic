bedazzlement:/bɪˈdæzlmənt/| n. |眼花缭乱，眩晕，迷惑 |The state of being bedazzled; overwhelmed with wonder or admiration; a state of confusion.| The sheer bedazzlement of the Las Vegas strip can be overwhelming. 拉斯维加斯大道的绚丽繁华让人眼花缭乱。|近义词：amazement, astonishment, dazzlement, fascination |反义词: clarity, disillusionment |词性: noun

Etymology:
From *bedazzle*, from *be-* (making a verb transitive) + *dazzle*.  *Dazzle* comes from Middle English *dasen*, probably from Old Norse *dasa*, meaning "to become exhausted, stupefied".
