dependences:/dɪˈpɛndənsiz/
|n.|依赖性，依存性；依赖关系；相关性|The project's success has many dependences. 这个项目的成功有很多依赖关系。|例句：The dependences between different parts of the ecosystem are complex. 生态系统不同部分之间的依赖关系很复杂。|近义词：dependencies, reliance, interdependency|反义词：independence, autonomy|词性:名词

dependence:/dɪˈpɛndəns/
|n.|依赖，依靠；依存(关系)；信赖|His dependence on his parents is understandable. 他对父母的依赖是可以理解的。|例句：The country's dependence on foreign oil is a major concern.  这个国家对外国石油的依赖是一个主要问题。|近义词：reliance, addiction (for unhealthy dependence)|反义词：independence, self-reliance|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *dependens*, from Late Latin *dependentia*, from Latin *dependēns* (present participle of *dependēre* “to hang down from, to depend on”), from *de-* (“down from, away from”) + *pendēre* (“to hang, to weigh”).
