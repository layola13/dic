mannitol:/ˈmænɪtɒl/ (US) /ˈmænɪtɔːl/ (UK)| n. |甘露醇|A white crystalline sugar alcohol; a hexahydric alcohol derived from mannose or fructose, used medicinally as a diuretic and in the manufacture of resins and plasticizers.|Mannitol is used to reduce swelling in the brain.|Synonyms: hexahydric alcohol|Antonyms: N/A|noun
