demonym:/ˈdemənɪm/ | n. |  居民名称，地名形容词 | A name for a resident or native of a particular place. | The demonym for Canada is Canadian. 加拿大的居民名称是加拿大人。| synonym:  inhabitant, resident, native | antonym: foreigner, alien | noun

demonym:/ˈdemənɪm/ | n. | 地名居民的名称 | A word used to describe the people who live in a specific place. |  A person from Paris is a Parisian, which is the demonym for that city. 巴黎人的名称是巴黎人，这是那个城市的居民名称。| synonym: appellation, name | antonym: N/A | noun
