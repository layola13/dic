ETYMOLOGY->
From livable + -ness.  "Livable" comes from the verb "live" (from Old English libban, meaning "to live") + the suffix "-able" (meaning "capable of being").  The suffix "-ness" transforms the adjective "livable" into the noun "livableness," indicating the state or quality of being livable.
