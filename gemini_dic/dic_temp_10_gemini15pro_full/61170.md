elucidation:/ɪˌluːsɪˈdeɪʃn/ | n. | 说明，阐明，解释 |  The professor's elucidations helped the students understand the complex theory. 教授的讲解帮助学生理解了这个复杂的理论。 | Example: His further elucidations confused me even more.  他进一步的解释让我更加困惑了。 | Synonyms: explanation, clarification, exposition, illustration | Antonyms: obfuscation, confusion, mystification | noun

elucidations:/ɪˌluːsɪˈdeɪʃnz/ | n. | 说明，阐明，解释（复数） |  The scientist offered several elucidations of the experimental results. 科学家对实验结果提供了几种解释。 | Example:  Her elucidations of the poem were insightful and thought-provoking. 她对这首诗的阐释富有洞察力，引人深思。 | Synonyms: explanations, clarifications, expositions, illustrations | Antonyms: obfuscations, confusions, mystifications | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin elucidatio, from elucidare "to make clear," from the Latin prefix e- "out" + lucidus "clear, bright," from lucere "to shine."
