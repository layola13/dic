come-unglued:/kʌm-ʌnˈɡluːd/
|v.|失去控制；崩溃；瓦解|To become emotionally unstable; to lose control of oneself.|例句：He almost came unglued when he heard the bad news.  他听到坏消息后几乎崩溃了。|例句：The project came unglued when the funding was cut.  项目在资金被削减后就瓦解了。|近义词：fall apart, lose it, crack up, freak out|反义词：calm down, compose oneself, keep it together|verb phrase
