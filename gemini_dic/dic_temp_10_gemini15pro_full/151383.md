parliamentary:/ˌpɑːrləˈmentəri/ | adj. |议会的；国会的；由议会决定的|Relating to, enacted by, or suitable for a parliament. |例句：The UK has a parliamentary system of government. 英国实行议会制政府。|例句：She raised a parliamentary question about the new law. 她就新法律提出了一个议会问题。|近义词：legislative, congressional |反义词：executive, judicial |词性：形容词
Etymology: From Old French *parlementarie (Modern French parlementaire), from parlement 'parliament'. Ultimately from the verb parler 'to speak'.
