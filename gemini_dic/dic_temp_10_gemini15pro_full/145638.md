overconsumption:/ˌoʊvər kənˈsʌmpʃən/ | n. | 过度消费；过量消耗 | The act of consuming something to excess.  It refers to a situation where resource use has outpaced the sustainable capacity of the ecosystem. |  Overconsumption of fast food can lead to health problems. 过度消费快餐会导致健康问题。

|  The documentary highlighted the environmental impact of overconsumption in developed countries.  这部纪录片强调了发达国家过度消费对环境的影响。| 近义词：overuse, excess consumption | 反义词: underconsumption, moderation | 词性: noun

Etymology: From "over-" (meaning "excessive") + "consumption" (from Latin "consumere," meaning "to consume, use up").
