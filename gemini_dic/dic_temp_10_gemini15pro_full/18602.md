benjamin:/ˈbendʒəmɪn/| n.|本杰明（男子名）|A masculine given name of Hebrew origin, meaning "son of the right hand" or "son of the south." It is a biblical name, referring to the youngest son of Jacob.|My youngest son's name is Benjamin. 我的小儿子的名字是本杰明。|近义词: (As a name, no direct synonyms, but other biblical names like Joseph, David, Samuel) |反义词: N/A|名词
