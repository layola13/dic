nonhemolytic:/ˌnɒnˌhiːməˈlɪtɪk/
adjective
非溶血性的
Not causing hemolysis; not pertaining to or characterized by hemolysis.  Hemolysis is the rupture or destruction of red blood cells.
The patient presented with nonhemolytic jaundice.  病人表现出非溶血性黄疸。
The lab results confirmed a nonhemolytic reaction. 实验室结果证实了非溶血性反应。
Synonyms: nonhaemolytic (British spelling), ahemolytic
Antonyms: hemolytic, haemolytic (British spelling)
词性: adjective
