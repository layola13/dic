paridigitata:/ˌpærɪˌdɪdʒɪˈtɑːtə/ | adj. | 指状复叶的，掌状分裂的 | Having digits or finger-like lobes radiating from a common point. |  The leaves of the horse chestnut tree are paridigitata. 七叶树的叶子是指状复叶的。| Synonym: palmate, digitate | Antonym: pinnate | Adjective
