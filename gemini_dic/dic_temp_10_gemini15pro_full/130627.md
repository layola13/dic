nonadministrant:/ˌnɒnədˈmɪnɪstrənt/
noun
非管理人员；非行政人员
A person who is not an administrator.  Not having administrative duties or power.
The nonadministrant staff members were not involved in the decision-making process. 非管理人员没有参与决策过程。
The project requires input from both administrants and nonadministrants. 这个项目需要管理人员和非管理人员的共同参与。
近义词: non-manager, non-executive, staff member
反义词: administrator, manager, executive
词性: noun
