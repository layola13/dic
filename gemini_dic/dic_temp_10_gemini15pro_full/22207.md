bona fide:/ˌboʊnə ˈfaɪdiː/ /ˌbɒnə ˈfaɪdiː/
adjective
真诚的；真实的；合法的
genuine; real; undertaken in good faith.
例句：He's a bona fide expert in his field. 他是该领域的真正专家。

例句：The offer was a bona fide attempt to resolve the issue.  这个提议是真心实意地尝试解决问题。

近义词：genuine, authentic, real, legitimate, valid
反义词：fake, counterfeit, spurious, fraudulent, sham
词性：adjective

bona fide:/ˌboʊnə ˈfaɪdiː/ /ˌbɒnə ˈfaɪdiː/
adverb
真诚地；真实地；合法地
genuinely; really; legitimately.
例句：He acted bona fide in all his dealings with the company. 他在与公司的所有交易中都表现得真诚。

例句：The documents were bona fide and legally binding. 这些文件是真实的，具有法律约束力。

近义词：genuinely, truly, actually, really, legitimately
反义词：dishonestly, falsely, fraudulently, deceptively
词性：adverb

bona fide:/ˌboʊnə ˈfaɪdiː/ /ˌbɒnə ˈfaɪdiː/
noun
真诚；善意
good faith; sincerity.
例句：The court questioned the bona fides of the witness. 法院质疑证人的诚信。

例句：There is no question about his bona fides; he’s a truly honest man. 他的真诚毋庸置疑；他是一个真正诚实的人。

近义词：good faith, sincerity, honesty, integrity
反义词：bad faith, dishonesty, deceit, fraud
词性：noun
