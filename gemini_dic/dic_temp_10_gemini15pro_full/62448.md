endorse:/ɪnˈdɔːrs/ /ɛnˈdɔːrs/| vt.|背书，赞同，支持；签署（姓名），认可|To express formal support or approval for someone or something.|The newspaper endorsed the candidate for mayor. 这家报纸支持这位市长候选人。|近义词：support, approve, back, champion, ratify|反义词：oppose, reject, condemn|verb

endorse:/ɪnˈdɔːrs/ /ɛnˈdɔːrs/| n.|背书，赞同|An act of giving public approval or support.|The candidate received many endorsements from prominent figures. 这位候选人获得了许多知名人士的背书。|近义词：support, approval, backing|反义词：opposition, rejection, condemnation|noun
