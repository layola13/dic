heralds:/ˈherəldz/| v. |预示；预告；宣布|Be a sign that (something) is about to happen.|The speech heralded a change in policy.  这篇演讲预示着政策的改变。|synonyms: announce, proclaim, signal, portend|antonyms: conceal, suppress|verb
