filiality:/fɪˈliːəlti/ /fɪˈliːælɪti/
n.|孝顺，孝心|The quality of being respectful and obedient to one's parents.|Her filiality was evident in the way she cared for her aging mother. 她对年迈母亲的照顾充分体现了她的孝心。|
近义词：obedience, respect, devotion, piety
反义词：disrespect, disobedience, impiety
词性：noun
词源：Late Middle English: from Old French filialté, from late Latin filialitas, from filius ‘son’.
