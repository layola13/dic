epistoler:/ɪˈpɪstələr/ | n. | 书信体小说；书信作者 | A novel written in the form of letters; a writer of epistles. |  Example: Samuel Richardson's "Pamela" is a classic epistoler. 塞缪尔·理查森的《帕梅拉》是一部经典的书信体小说。 | Example: The epistoler crafted a moving narrative through a series of letters.  这位书信作者通过一系列的信件创作了一个感人的故事。 | Synonym: letter writer, novelist (if writing novels), correspondent | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps) oral storyteller, speaker | noun
