disprovers:/dɪsˈpruːvərz/
|n.| 反驳者，证伪者|People who disprove something; those who demonstrate something to be false.|The scientist's theory was challenged by several disprovers who presented conflicting evidence.  这位科学家的理论受到几位提出相反证据的反驳者的挑战。|Critics, challengers, refuters|Supporters, believers|noun
