micro-evolution:/ˌmaɪkroʊˌɛvəˈluːʃən/
noun
微进化
Evolutionary change within a species or small group of organisms, especially over a short period.
Micro-evolution can be observed in the change of beak sizes in finches on the Galapagos Islands.  加拉帕戈斯群岛上的雀类的喙大小的变化可以观察到微进化。
Micro-evolution led to the development of antibiotic resistance in some bacteria. 微进化导致某些细菌对抗生素产生耐药性。
Synonyms: microevolutionary change, small-scale evolution
Antonyms: macro-evolution
