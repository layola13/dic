hyperresponsive:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈspɑːnsɪv/| adj.|过度反应的，反应过度的|Characterized by excessive or exaggerated responsiveness to stimuli.|The child was hyperresponsive to loud noises, covering his ears and crying.  这个孩子对巨大的噪音反应过度，捂着耳朵哭了起来。|例句：The patient is hyperresponsive to certain medications.  这个病人对某些药物反应过度。

例句：Her hyperresponsive immune system caused her to have allergic reactions frequently. 她的过度反应的免疫系统导致她经常过敏。|近义词：oversensitive, overreactive, touchy|反义词：hyporeactive, unresponsive|词性:形容词
