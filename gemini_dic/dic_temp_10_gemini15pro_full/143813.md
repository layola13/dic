outparish:/ˈaʊtˌpærɪʃ/| n.|外教区；郊区教区|An outparish is a part of a parish that is geographically separated from the main part, often in a rural or remote area. |The small chapel served the outparish community.  这个小教堂服务于郊区教区的社区。|Synonym: outlying district, suburb |Antonym: city center|noun
