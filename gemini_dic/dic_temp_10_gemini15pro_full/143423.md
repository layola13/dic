outfleeing: /ˌaʊtˈfliːɪŋ/
|Verb (present participle)|逃离，比…逃得快|The act of fleeing faster or further than someone or something else.  Escaping something by running faster or further.|例句：The gazelle was outfleeing the cheetah.  瞪羚羊逃得比猎豹快。

例句：They were outfleeing the rising floodwaters. 他们逃离了不断上涨的洪水。|近义词：outrunning, escaping, evading|反义词: surrendering, succumbing|词性: Verb (present participle)
Etymology:  "out-" (meaning "exceeding, surpassing") + "flee" (from Old English *flēon, meaning "to flee, escape")
