benediction:/ˌbenɪˈdɪkʃən/
n.
祝福;祈祷
an utterance of good wishes, typically at the end of a religious service.
The priest delivered a benediction at the end of the mass.  神父在弥撒结束时做了祝福祈祷。

The service ended with a benediction.  仪式以祝福结束。
近义词:blessing, invocation
反义词:curse, malediction
词性:名词

benediction:/ˌbenɪˈdɪkʃən/
n.
恩惠;幸事
something that promotes goodness or well-being.
The rain was a benediction for the drought-stricken farmers. 这场雨对饱受干旱之苦的农民来说是一场恩惠。

Her kind words were a benediction.  她友善的话语是一种恩惠。
近义词:boon, gift
反义词:bane, misfortune
词性:名词
