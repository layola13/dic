modest:/ˈmɑːdɪst/ (US) /ˈmɒdɪst/ (UK)| adj.|谦虚的，谦逊的；适度的，不太大的；朴素的，不奢华的|Not excessive or extreme; moderate. Having or showing a moderate or humble estimate of one's merits, importance, etc.|She's very modest about her achievements. 她对自己的成就非常谦虚。

He inherited a modest fortune. 他继承了一笔不大的财产。

They live in a modest house. 他们住在一所朴素的房子里。|近义词: humble, unassuming, moderate, simple|反义词: arrogant, boastful, extravagant, immodest|词性: adjective
