acclive: /əˈklaɪv/
adjective
向上倾斜的, 陡峭的
sloping upwards; rising.  A surface that is acclive inclines upwards.
The road became increasingly acclive as we neared the summit. =  当我们接近山顶时，道路变得越来越陡峭。

The acclive path made the climb challenging. = 向上倾斜的小路使攀登变得很有挑战性。

近义词: ascending, uphill, rising, sloping upward
反义词: declive, descending, downhill

词性: adjective
词源: From Latin acclivis (“sloping upwards”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + clivus (“slope”).
