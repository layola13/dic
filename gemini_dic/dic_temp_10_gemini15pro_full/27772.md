cannibalise:/ˈkænɪbəlaɪz/| v. |拆用，挪用，拼凑|To reuse parts from one machine or system in order to build or repair another.|The mechanic cannibalised an old engine to repair the customer's car. 技工拆用了一台旧引擎来修理顾客的汽车。|Synonyms: strip, dismantle, scavenge|Antonyms: assemble, construct, build|verb

cannibalization:/ˌkænɪbəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |拆用，挪用，拼凑|The act of reusing parts from one machine or system in order to build or repair another.|The cannibalization of old equipment saved the company a lot of money.  拆用旧设备为公司节省了很多钱。|Synonyms: reuse, repurposing, salvaging|Antonyms: construction, assembly|noun
