dismissions:/dɪsˈmɪʃənz/
n.
解雇；免职；解散；驳回
The act of dismissing someone from their job, a group of people from a meeting, or a legal case.
The company announced the dismissions of several employees. 公司宣布解雇几名员工。
layoffs, redundancies, discharges
appointments, hirings, retentions
noun


dismiss:/dɪsˈmɪs/
v.
解雇；免职；解散；驳回
To allow or order someone to leave; to send someone away; to reject something, such as a case or idea.
The judge dismissed the case due to lack of evidence. 法官因缺乏证据驳回了此案。
discharge, fire, sack, release
retain, hire, appoint, accept
verb
