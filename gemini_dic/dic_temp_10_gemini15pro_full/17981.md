befuddlement:/bɪˈfʌdəlmənt/| n. |困惑，迷惑，糊涂|A state of being confused and unable to think clearly. |The complex instructions caused complete befuddlement among the users. 复杂的说明书让用户们一头雾水。|She stared at the puzzle in befuddlement. 她困惑地盯着拼图。|近义词：confusion, bewilderment, perplexity, disorientation|反义词：clarity, understanding, lucidity|词性: noun

Etymology: From the verb "befuddle," which is likely a blend of "be-" (making a verb transitive) and "fuddle" (to make drunk or confused). "Fuddle" itself likely comes from Middle English "fudlen," of unknown origin.
