papillote:/ˌpæpiˈjoʊt/ /ˌpæpɪˈjɒt/| n.|纸包菜；用纸包着烘烤的食物|A piece of food, usually fish or meat, that is baked in a folded piece of greaseproof paper or foil.|The salmon cooked en papillote was delicious. 纸包烤鲑鱼很美味。|近义词:parcel, packet|反义词:|noun
