pancetta:/pænˈtʃetə/ /pɑːnˈtʃetə/
n.
意大利熏猪肉
Italian cured meat from the belly of a pig, similar to bacon but not smoked.
She added pancetta to the pasta sauce for a richer flavor. 她为了更浓郁的口味在意大利面酱里加了意大利熏猪肉。
近义词:bacon, prosciutto
反义词:None.
词性:noun
