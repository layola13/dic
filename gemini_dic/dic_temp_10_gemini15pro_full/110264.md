lift:/lɪft/| n. v.|电梯；举起；提升；鼓舞；顺风车|An upward movement; the act of lifting something. To raise to a higher position or level.|
The elevator took us to the tenth floor. 电梯把我们带到了十楼。
He lifted the heavy box with ease. 他轻松地举起了沉重的箱子。|
近义词：raise, elevate, boost, hoist, ride|
反义词：lower, drop, depress|
词性:名词,动词

lift:/lɪft/| n. v.|电梯；举起；提升；鼓舞；顺风车|Something that is lifted or raises spirits.  A free ride in someone's vehicle.|
The good news gave her spirits a lift.  这个好消息让她精神振奋。
I got a lift to work from a colleague. 我搭同事的顺风车去上班。|
近义词：encouragement, inspiration, ride|
反义词：discouragement, burden|
词性:名词,动词


词源: From Old Norse *lypta, from Proto-Germanic *luftijaną (“to lift”), from Proto-Indo-European *lewp- (“to peel, break off, flay”). Cognate with Danish løfte (“to lift”), Swedish lyfta (“to lift”), Icelandic lyfta (“to lift”).
