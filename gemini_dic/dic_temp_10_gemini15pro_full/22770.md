词源： Borscht comes from the Ukrainian word "борщ" (borshch), which ultimately derives from the Proto-Slavic word "*bŭrščǐ", referring to the hogweed plant, whose leaves were originally used in the soup.  罗宋汤来自乌克兰语 "борщ" (borshch)，最终源自原始斯拉夫语 "*bŭrščǐ"，指的是猪草植物，其叶子最初用于汤中。
