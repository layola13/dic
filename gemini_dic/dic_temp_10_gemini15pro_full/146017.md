overdrain:/ˌoʊvərˈdreɪn/| v.|过度排水，排水过度|To drain excessively, removing too much water or moisture.|The farmer overdrained the field, causing the soil to become dry and cracked. 农民过度排干了田地，导致土壤变得干燥和开裂。|Example:  Overdraining the soil can lead to nutrient loss. 过度排水会导致养分流失。|Synonyms: overdry, deplete|Antonyms: irrigate, waterlog|verb

overdrain:/ˌoʊvərˈdreɪn/| n.|过度排水|Excessive draining, resulting in the removal of too much water or moisture.|The overdrain of the marsh led to a decline in the local wildlife population. 沼泽的过度排水导致当地野生动物数量下降。|Example: The overdrain caused the plants to wilt. 过度排水导致植物枯萎。|Synonyms:  depletion, exhaustion|Antonyms: saturation, irrigation|noun
