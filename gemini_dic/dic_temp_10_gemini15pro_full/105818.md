kistful: /ˈkɪstfʊl/
noun
一满盒子（尤指食物）; 一盒子的量
a box full of something, especially food.
The baker gave us a kistful of pastries.  面包师给了我们满满一盒糕点。
近义词: boxful, chestful
反义词: handful
词性: noun
