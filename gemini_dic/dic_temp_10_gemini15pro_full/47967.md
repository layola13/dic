deciduousness:/dɪˈsɪdʒuːəsnəs/
|n.|落叶性；脱落性|The deciduousness of the trees in autumn creates a beautiful display of color. 秋天树木的落叶性造就了美丽的色彩.|例句：The deciduousness of the maple trees is evident in the fall. 枫树的落叶性在秋天很明显。|近义词：deciduity|反义词：evergreenness|词性: 名词
Etymology: From Late Latin *deciduusness*, from deciduus (falling off) + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality).  The Latin "deciduus" comes from "decidere," meaning "to fall off," from "de-" (down, off) + "cadere" (to fall).
