narrated:/nəˈreɪtɪd/ /ˈnæˌreɪtɪd/
verb
讲述的，叙述的
told a story, gave an account of events
The documentary was narrated by David Attenborough. 这部纪录片由大卫·爱登堡解说。
synonyms: recounted, described, related, told
antonyms: silent, unvoiced

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *narrātus, past participle of narrō (“I tell, relate, recount”) < Old Latin gnārō, from Proto-Indo-European *ǵneh₃- (“to know”).
