nonexpectant:/ˌnɒnɪkˈspektənt/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnɪkˈspektənt/ (US)| adj.|非妊娠的；没有怀孕的；未怀孕的|Not expecting a baby; not pregnant.|She was nonexpectant and could therefore drink alcohol freely. 她没有怀孕，因此可以随意喝酒。|The doctor confirmed she was nonexpectant. 医生确认她没有怀孕。|近义词：not pregnant|反义词：pregnant, expectant|词性:形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
expectant (adjective): from Latin exspectant- (stem of exspectans), present participle of exspectare "to look out for, await, expect," from ex- "out" + spectare "to look," frequentative of specere "to look at"
