outmanipulating:/ˌaʊtməˈnɪpjuleɪtɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的操纵; 技胜于…的操纵
To manipulate or control someone or something more effectively than another.
He outmanipulated his opponents in the business deal. 他在商业交易中技胜了他的对手。
Synonyms: outmaneuver, outsmart, outwit
Antonyms: be outmaneuvered, be outsmarted, be outwitted
词性: 动词
