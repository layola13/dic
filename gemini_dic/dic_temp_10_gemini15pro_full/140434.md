okey-doke:/ˌoʊkiˈdoʊki/ | interj. | 好；行；没问题 | An informal way of expressing agreement or understanding. | Example: "Okey-doke, I'll meet you there at noon."  好的，中午在那里见。| Example: "Okey-doke, sounds like a plan." 行，听起来像个计划。 | Synonym: okay, all right, sure | Antonym: no, nope | interjection
