intermomentary:/ˌɪntərˈmoʊmənteri/| adj.|瞬间的，片刻的|Existing or occurring between moments; very brief; momentary.|The power flickered intermomentarily, causing the computer screens to go dark.|近义词：momentary, fleeting, transient, ephemeral|反义词：prolonged, lasting, enduring|词性:形容词
Etymology: From inter- + momentary. "Inter-" is a Latin prefix meaning "between," and "momentary" derives from the Latin "momentum," meaning "movement" or "instant."
