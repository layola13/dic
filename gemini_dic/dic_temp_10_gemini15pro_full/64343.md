erasable:/ɪˈreɪsəbl/ /ɪˈreɪzəbl/
adjective
可擦除的; 可抹去的
Able to be erased.
例句：
The whiteboard uses erasable markers.
白板使用可擦除的马克笔。

The notes were written in erasable ink.
笔记是用可擦除的墨水写的。

近义词：deletable, removable, effaceable
反义词：permanent, indelible
词性: adjective
