distinguishing:/dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃɪŋ/| adj.|区别的，有特色的，显著的|Serving to distinguish; characteristic; differentiating.  Marking as different. |Her distinguishing feature is her bright red hair. 她显著的特征是她亮红色的头发。|近义词：characteristic, distinctive, differentiating, individual, peculiar, special, typical |反义词：common, ordinary, typical, undistinguished|adjective

distinguishing:/dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃɪŋ/| n.|区别，辨别|The act of recognizing or noting a difference. |The distinguishing of subtle differences in wine takes practice.  区分葡萄酒的细微差别需要练习。|近义词：differentiation, discrimination, discernment|反义词：confusion,  indistinction|noun
