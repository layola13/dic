pangrammatic:/ˌpænɡrəˈmætɪk/| adj.| 涵盖字母表的，全字母句的| Containing all the letters of the alphabet.  A pangrammatic sentence uses every letter of the alphabet at least once.|  The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog is a pangrammatic sentence.| 近义词: holoalphabetic| 反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "incomplete" or "partial" in certain contexts)| 词性: adjective
