dairies:/ˈdɛəriːz/
n.
牛奶场，乳品店，日记
places where milk and milk products are produced or sold; a book in which one keeps a daily record of events and experiences
We get our milk from local dairies. 我们从当地的奶牛场获得牛奶。
She kept diaries throughout her adolescence. 她整个青春期都坚持写日记。
synonyms: creameries, milk plants, journals, logs
antonyms: N/A
