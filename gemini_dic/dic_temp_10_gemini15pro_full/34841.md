clinically:/ˈklɪnɪkli/| adv.|临床地；冷静地；客观地|Relating to the observation and treatment of actual patients rather than theoretical or laboratory findings.  It can also mean in a detached and unemotional way.|例句：The drug has been clinically tested and proven effective.  这种药物已经过临床测试，并被证明有效。|近义词：medically, objectively, dispassionately, analytically|反义词:subjectively, emotionally|词性:adverb
词源: From clinic + -al + -ly.  Ultimately from the Greek klinikē (meaning "bedside").
