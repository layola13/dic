PHRASE->
(Since "eles" is a pronoun, it doesn't form phrases in the same way nouns do.  Here are examples of phrases involving masculine plural pronouns in Portuguese, keeping in mind these may not directly translate with "eles" in all contexts.)

para eles - for them
com eles - with them
sem eles - without them
sobre eles - about them
de eles - of them (less common)


注意事项:

"Eles" is specifically used in Portuguese and Spanish. It does not exist in English.  The closest equivalent is the pronoun "they."  The examples given reflect usage in Portuguese, as it wasn't specified which language was intended.  Spanish usage would be similar, but with different verb conjugations.
