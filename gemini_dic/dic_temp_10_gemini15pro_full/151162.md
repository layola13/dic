parboiled:/pɑːrˈbɔɪld/ | adj. | 部分煮熟的，预煮的 | Partially cooked by boiling, usually to prepare for further cooking. |  The parboiled rice cooks more evenly. 预煮的米饭煮得更均匀。|  The potatoes were parboiled before being roasted. 土豆在烤之前先煮了一部分。|Synonyms: precooked, partially cooked | Antonyms: raw, uncooked | adjective

parboil:/pɑːrˈbɔɪl/ | v. | 部分煮熟，预煮 | To boil (something, especially food) partially, usually to prepare it for further cooking. | I parboiled the potatoes before roasting them. 我在烤土豆之前先把它们煮了一部分。 | She parboiled the rice to reduce the cooking time later. 她预煮了米饭以减少之后的烹饪时间。 | Synonyms: precook, partially cook | Antonyms: undercook | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "par-" (partly) + "boil".  "Par-" comes from the Old French "par" meaning "by, through," from Latin "per" meaning "through, across, during, by means of."  "Boil" comes from the Old French "boillir," from Latin "bullire," meaning "to bubble, boil."
