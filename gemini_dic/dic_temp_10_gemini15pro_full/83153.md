gutted:/ˈɡʌtɪd/| adj.| 非常失望的，极度沮丧的;  被摧毁的，严重受损的|Feeling bitterly disappointed or upset.| I was absolutely gutted when I heard the news. 我听到这个消息时非常难过。|Synonyms: devastated, heartbroken, crushed|Antonyms: elated, overjoyed, thrilled|adjective

gutted:/ˈɡʌtɪd/| v.|取出…的内脏;  损毁…的内部;  (非正式) 彻底摧毁 (建筑物)| remove the intestines of; destroy the essential part of; (informal) destroy the inside of (a building)| The fish was gutted and cleaned before being cooked. 这条鱼在烹饪前被取出内脏并清洗干净了。|Synonyms: eviscerate, disembowel (for removing intestines), demolish, destroy (for buildings)|Antonyms: repair, renovate, restore|verb
