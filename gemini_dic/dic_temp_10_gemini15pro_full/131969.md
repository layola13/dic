nonconverging:/ˌnɒnkənˈvɜːrdʒɪŋ/
adjective
不收敛的，不汇聚的
Not coming closer together; not tending towards a limit or agreed point.
The two lines are nonconverging, meaning they will never meet.  这两条线是不收敛的，意味着它们永远不会相交。
The nonconverging paths of the two companies led to very different outcomes.  两家公司的不收敛路径导致了截然不同的结果。
Near Antonyms: converging, convergent
Antonyms: diverging, divergent
词性: 形容词
