nonhyperbolical:/ˌnɒnˌhaɪpəˈbɒlɪkəl/ | adj. | 非双曲线的；不夸张的 | Not hyperbolic; not exaggerated; moderate. | The witness gave a nonhyperbolical account of the accident. 目击者对事故作了不夸张的描述。 | The professor offered a nonhyperbolical explanation of the complex theory.  教授对这个复杂的理论进行了一种不夸张的解释。 | 近义词: moderate, understated, realistic, temperate | 反义词: hyperbolic, exaggerated, overstated, inflated | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "hyperbolical" (from Greek "hyperbole" meaning "excess, exaggeration").
