deactivator:/diːˈæktɪveɪtər/
noun
去活化剂; 失活剂; 钝化剂
a substance that renders something inactive.
The enzyme deactivator prevented the reaction from occurring. 这种酶去活化剂阻止了反应的发生。
近义词: inhibitor, neutralizer
反义词: activator
词性: 名词
