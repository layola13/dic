biliation: /ˌbɪlɪˈeɪʃən/
Noun
胆汁分泌；胆汁生成
The process of bile production and secretion.
Biliation is essential for fat digestion. 胆汁分泌对脂肪消化至关重要。
Increased biliation can be a sign of liver problems. 胆汁分泌增加可能是肝脏问题的征兆。
Synonyms: bile production, bile secretion
Antonyms: N/A
