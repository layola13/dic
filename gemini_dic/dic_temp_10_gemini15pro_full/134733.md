nonmechanical:/ˌnɒnməˈkænɪkəl/ /ˌnɑːnməˈkænɪkəl/
| adj. |非机械的，非力学的|Not involving or relating to machines or mechanics; not operated or produced by a machine.|The growth of crystals is a nonmechanical process. 晶体的生长是一个非机械过程。|近义词：non-mechanical, manual, electronic|反义词：mechanical, automated|词性:形容词
