cross-platform:/ˌkrɒsˈplæt.fɔːm/ /ˌkrɑːsˈplæt.fɔːrm/ | adj. | 跨平台的 |  Able to be used on different types of computers or other electronic devices. | This new software is cross-platform, so you can use it on your Windows PC, Mac, and even your phone.  这款新软件是跨平台的，所以你可以在你的Windows电脑、Mac，甚至你的手机上使用它。| 近义词: multi-platform, platform-independent | 反义词: platform-specific | 词性: 形容词

cross-platform:/ˌkrɒsˈplæt.fɔːm/ /ˌkrɑːsˈplæt.fɔːrm/ | n. | 跨平台 |  A technology or system that works across multiple platforms. | The development team is working on a new cross-platform for their next game. 开发团队正在为他们的下一个游戏开发一个新的跨平台。 | 近义词: multi-platform | 反义词: platform-specific | 词性: 名词
