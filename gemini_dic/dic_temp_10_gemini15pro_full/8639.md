anticonfederationism:/ˌæntiˌkənˌfɛdəˈreɪʃənɪzəm/ | n. | 反联邦主义 | Opposition to the creation or existence of a confederation, specifically referring to the opposition in some of the British North American colonies (now Canada) to Confederation in 1867. |  Many in Nova Scotia held strong anticonfederationist views, fearing economic and political domination by the larger provinces.  新斯科舍省的许多人持有强烈的反联邦主义观点，担心更大的省份会在经济和政治上占据主导地位。 | 近义词:  separatism, disunionism | 反义词:  federalism, unionism | 词性: 名词
