diathermous:/ˌdaɪəˈθɜːrməs/| adj.|透热的，热的能穿透的|Allowing heat to pass through readily.  A diathermous wall allows heat to radiate through it.|Infrared radiation is diathermous to dry air, but not to water vapor.|transparent (to heat), permeable (to heat)|adiathermic|adjective
diathermanous:/ˌdaɪəˈθɜːmənəs/| adj.|透热的，热的能穿透的|Allowing heat to pass through readily. Synonym of diathermous.|Infrared radiation is diathermanous to dry air, but not to water vapor.|transparent (to heat), permeable (to heat)|adiathermic, adiathermanous|adjective
