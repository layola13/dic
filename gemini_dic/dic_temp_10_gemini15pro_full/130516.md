nonaccommodations:/ˌnɒnəkɒməˈdeɪʃənz/
| n. |无住宿;不提供住宿;缺乏便利设施|Lack of accommodations or facilities, especially for people with disabilities.|The nonaccommodations for wheelchair users made the building inaccessible.  这座建筑因没有为轮椅使用者提供便利设施而无法进入。|例句：The hotel's nonaccommodations for families with young children led them to seek other lodging. 这家旅馆没有为有小孩的家庭提供住宿，导致他们不得不另寻住处。

例句：The lack of nonaccommodations for dietary restrictions made it difficult for him to find suitable meals. 缺乏对饮食限制的照顾让他很难找到合适的饭菜。|近义词：lack of facilities, inaccessibility, inconvenience|反义词: accommodations, facilities, accessibility |词性:名词


词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + accommodation (noun meaning "a place to stay or live")
