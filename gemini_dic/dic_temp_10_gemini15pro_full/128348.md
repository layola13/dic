neutralize:/ˈnjuːtrəlaɪz/ | v. | 中和，抵消，使无效 | To make something neutral, or to counteract or cancel the effect of something. |  The acid was neutralized by the alkali. 酸被碱中和了。

The vaccine neutralizes the virus. 疫苗使病毒失效。 | synonyms: counteract, counterbalance, offset, negate, annul | antonyms: activate, enhance, intensify | verb

neutralized:/ˈnjuːtrəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 被中和的，失效的 | Having been made neutral, or having had its effect counteracted or cancelled. | The neutralized solution was safe to handle. 中和后的溶液可以安全处理。

The neutralized threat no longer posed a danger. 被消除的威胁不再构成危险。 | synonyms: counteracted, counterbalanced, offset, negated, annulled | antonyms: activated, enhanced, intensified | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *neutraliser*, from *neutre* (neutral) + *-iser* (suffix forming verbs from adjectives).  The word *neutre* ultimately comes from Latin *neuter* (neither).
