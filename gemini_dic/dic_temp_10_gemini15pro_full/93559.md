imagines:/ɪˈmædʒɪnz/
verb
想象，设想
forms the third-person singular present tense of imagine.  It means to form a mental image or concept of something.
She imagines a life of travel and adventure. 她想象着一种旅行和冒险的生活。
He imagines himself winning the lottery. 他想象自己中了彩票。
近义词: visualizes, conceives, pictures, thinks, supposes
反义词: disbelieves, forgets

词性
v.
