obstructer:/əbˈstrʌktər/
noun
阻碍者,妨碍者
Someone or something that obstructs.
The protestors were obstructers of traffic. 抗议者阻碍了交通。
Heavy snow can be a major obstructer to travel.  大雪会严重阻碍出行。
Synonyms:impediment, obstacle, barrier, hindrance
Antonyms:facilitator, aid, helper
n.


obstructing:/əbˈstrʌktɪŋ/
adjective
阻碍的,妨碍的
Acting to obstruct something; hindering or preventing movement or progress.
The obstructing branches made it difficult to walk through the forest. 阻碍的树枝使人们难以穿过森林。
His obstructing presence prevented me from leaving the room. 他挡在那儿，使我无法离开房间。
Synonyms: hindering, impeding, blocking, barring
Antonyms: aiding, assisting, facilitating, helping
adj.
