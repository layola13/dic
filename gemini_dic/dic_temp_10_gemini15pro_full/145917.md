overdiligently:/ˌoʊvərˈdɪlɪdʒəntli/ | adv. | 过于勤奋地，过度努力地 | Excessively diligently; with too much diligence.  Working or studying with more effort than is necessary or healthy. | He studied overdiligently for the exam and ended up burning himself out. 他为了考试学习得过于勤奋，结果把自己累垮了。|  She cleaned the house overdiligently, scrubbing every surface until it gleamed. 她过度勤奋地打扫房子，把每个表面都擦得闪闪发光。 | 近义词: excessively, too much, overzealously | 反义词: lazily, carelessly, negligently | 词性: 副词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "diligently" (adverb form of "diligent," from Latin *diligens*, present participle of *diligere* "to love, esteem, single out, select, value").
