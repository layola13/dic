cyanobacteria: /saɪˌænoʊbækˈtɪəriə/ | n. | 蓝细菌，蓝绿藻 | Photosynthetic bacteria that contain chlorophyll and other pigments and are found in various aquatic and terrestrial habitats. | Cyanobacteria are sometimes called "blue-green algae," though they are not truly algae. 蓝细菌有时被称为“蓝绿藻”，尽管它们并非真正的藻类。

Cyanobacteria blooms can be toxic to humans and animals. 蓝细菌大量繁殖会对人类和动物造成毒害。| 近义词: blue-green algae | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it is a specific type of organism) | 词性: noun
