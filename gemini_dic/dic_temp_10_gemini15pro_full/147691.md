overpracticing:/ˌoʊvərˈpræktɪsɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度练习
Practicing something excessively, to the point where it becomes detrimental.
She was overpracticing her piano piece and ended up making more mistakes. 她过度练习钢琴曲，结果反而犯了更多错误。
He was overpracticing his free throws and injured his shoulder. 他过度练习罚球，伤到了肩膀。
Synonym: overtraining, overdoing
Antonym: underpracticing
