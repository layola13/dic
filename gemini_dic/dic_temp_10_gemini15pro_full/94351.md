impious:/ˈɪmpiəs/| adj. |不敬的，不虔诚的|Not showing respect or reverence, especially for God; wicked.|It was considered impious to question the priest. 质疑神父被认为是不敬的。|Near Synonyms: irreverent, profane, blasphemous, sacrilegious, ungodly|Antonyms: pious, reverent, devout, holy|Adjective
