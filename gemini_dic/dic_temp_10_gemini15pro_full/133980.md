nonhierarchic:/ˌnɒnˌhaɪəˈrɑːkɪk/ | adj. | 非等级的，非层级的 | Not organized in a hierarchy; lacking a hierarchical structure.  Meaning that there are no levels of power or authority, and everyone is considered equal. |  The team adopted a nonhierarchic structure, with everyone contributing equally.  团队采用了非等级结构，每个人都贡献平等。|  The online forum encourages nonhierarchic discussion, allowing all members to voice their opinions freely.  在线论坛鼓励非等级讨论，允许所有成员自由表达意见。| synonyms: egalitarian, democratic, flat, non-stratified | antonyms: hierarchical, stratified, graded, ranked | adjective
