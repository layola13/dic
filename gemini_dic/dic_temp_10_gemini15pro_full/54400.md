disimprison:/dɪsɪmˈprɪzn/
verb
释放，使从狱中释放
To release from prison; to free from confinement.
The king disimprisoned the political prisoners. 国王释放了政治犯。
近义词:liberate, release, free, discharge
反义词:imprison, jail, incarcerate
词性:v.

Etymology:
From dis- (expressing negation, reversal) + imprison.
