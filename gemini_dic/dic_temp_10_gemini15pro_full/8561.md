anticipated: /ænˈtɪsɪpeɪtɪd/
verb (past participle)
预期的，预料的
Expected or foreseen.
The anticipated arrival time is 3 PM. 预计到达时间是下午3点。
The movie's release was highly anticipated. 这部电影的发行备受期待。
Synonyms: expected, foreseen, predicted
Antonyms: unexpected, unforeseen, unpredicted
verb

anticipate: /ænˈtɪsɪpeɪt/
verb
预料，预期，期望
Regard as probable; expect or predict.
We anticipate a large crowd at the concert. 我们预计音乐会会有很多人。
I didn't anticipate having so much trouble. 我没想到会有这么多麻烦。
Synonyms: expect, foresee, predict, await
Antonyms: doubt, dread


Etymology:
From Latin *anticipatus*, past participle of *anticipare* (“to take beforehand, to anticipate”), from *ante* (“before”) + *capere* (“to take”).
