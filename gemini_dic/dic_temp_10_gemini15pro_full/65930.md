exacerbatingly:/ɪɡˈzæsərbeɪtɪŋli/
adv.
加剧地，恶化地
making a problem, bad situation, or negative feeling worse
The situation was exacerbatingly complex due to the language barrier. 由于语言障碍，情况变得更加复杂。
近义词:worseningly, aggravatingly
反义词:soothingly, alleviatingly
词性:副词

exacerbate:/ɪɡˈzæsərbeɪt/
v.
使恶化；使加剧
make (a problem, bad situation, or negative feeling) worse
Stress can exacerbate health problems. 压力会加剧健康问题。
近义词:worsen, aggravate
反义词:alleviate, improve
词性:动词

词源: From Late Latin exacerbatus, past participle of exacerbare "to irritate," from ex- "thoroughly" + acerbare "to make bitter," from acerbus "bitter, harsh."
