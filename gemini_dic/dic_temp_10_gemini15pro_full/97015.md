infeasibility:/ˌɪnˌfiːzəˈbɪləti/
n.|不可行性，不可实现性|The infeasibility of the project became apparent after a thorough analysis.  该项目在经过彻底分析后显示出不可行性。|
The government acknowledged the infeasibility of implementing the policy in the current economic climate. 政府承认在当前经济环境下实施该政策是不可行的。|
近义词: impracticability, impossibility, unworkability|
反义词: feasibility, possibility, practicality|
词性: noun
