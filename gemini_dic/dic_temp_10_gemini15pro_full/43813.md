crenulation:/ˌkrenjʊˈleɪʃn/
n.
城垛；锯齿状；【生理学】环状收缩
Small, regular, rectangular projections on a battlement. Also refers to any such notched or indented formation.
The crenulations of the castle walls provided excellent defensive positions for archers. 城墙的垛口为弓箭手提供了绝佳的防御阵地。

The leaf margins showed a distinct crenulation.  叶子的边缘呈现出明显的锯齿状。

Intestinal crenulation was observed under the microscope. 在显微镜下观察到了肠道的环状收缩。

近义词: indentation, notching, serration, battlement
反义词: smoothness, flatness
词性: noun

crenulate:/ˈkrenjʊleɪt/
v.
使成锯齿状；使成城垛状
To furnish with crenulations; to make notched or indented.
The constant erosion crenulated the coastline.  不断的侵蚀使海岸线呈锯齿状。

He crenulated the edge of the paper with pinking shears. 他用锯齿状剪刀将纸的边缘剪成锯齿状。

近义词: notch, indent, serrate
反义词: smooth, flatten
词性: verb

crenulated:/ˈkrenjʊleɪtɪd/
adj.
锯齿状的；有城垛的
Having crenulations; notched or indented.
The crenulated edge of the cookie looked appealing.  饼干的锯齿状边缘看起来很诱人。

The ancient fortress boasted high, crenulated walls. 古老的堡垒拥有高耸的城垛墙。

近义词: notched, indented, serrated, battlemented
反义词: smooth, flat
词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *crenulatio, derived from Latin crena (“notch, indentation”).
