dieted:/ˈdaɪətɪd/
v.
节食过；控制饮食
past participle of diet:  having restricted oneself to small amounts or special kinds of food in order to lose weight.
She dieted for months to fit into her wedding dress. 她为了穿上婚纱节食了好几个月。
近义词: slimmed, reduced, restricted (one's diet)
反义词: gorged, overate, indulged

词性: verb (past participle)
