annexed:/əˈnekst/| v. adj.|吞并，附加，附带|To incorporate (territory) into the domain of a city, country, or state; added or appended.|例句：Texas was annexed by the United States in 1845.  德克萨斯于1845年被美国吞并。

例句：The annexed document provides further details. 附加的文件提供了更多细节。|近义词：attached, appended, incorporated, added, seized, appropriated|反义词:detached, separated, released|词性:动词/形容词
annexed:/əˈnekst/| adj.|附加的，附带的|Added or appended, especially to a larger or more important thing.|例句：The annexed territories were quickly integrated into the empire. 被吞并的领土很快被并入帝国。

例句：Please see the annexed schedule for further information.  请参阅附件的时间表以获取更多信息。|近义词：attached, appended, enclosed|反义词:detached, separate|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *annexus*, past participle of *annectere* "to bind, tie, or fasten to," from *ad-* "to" + *nectere* "to bind."
