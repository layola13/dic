bulgier:/ˈbʌldʒiər/ | adj. | 更加凸起的，更加鼓胀的 | More bulging; having a more pronounced bulge or swelling. |  Her pockets were bulgier than usual, filled with candy. 她的口袋比平时鼓鼓的，装满了糖果。

My backpack is bulgier today because I'm carrying extra books. 我的背包今天更鼓了，因为我带了额外的书。| 近义词：rounder, fuller, more prominent | 反义词：flatter, smoother, less prominent | 词性：形容词

词源:  From bulge + -ier (comparative adjective suffix).  Ultimately from Old French *bouge "leather bag," of uncertain origin.
