oversecurely:/ˌoʊvər səˈkjʊərli/
adjective
过于安全地，过分谨慎地
protected or guarded too much; excessively cautious
He oversecurely locked all the doors and windows before leaving on vacation.  他出发度假前把所有的门窗都锁得过于严实。
The data was oversecurely encrypted, making it inaccessible even to authorized personnel. 数据被加密得过于严密，即使是授权人员也无法访问。
近义词:excessively cautiously, too carefully, overprotectively
反义词:carelessly, negligently, insecurely
词性:adjective
