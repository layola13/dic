effeminize:/ɪˈfɛmɪˌnaɪz/| v. |使女性化，使柔弱 |To make someone or something appear or behave in ways traditionally associated with women; to make something less masculine. |例句：The boy's parents worried that his interest in poetry would effeminize him.  这个男孩的父母担心他对诗歌的兴趣会让他变得女性化。

例句：Critics argued that the new policy would effeminize the military.  批评家认为，新政策会使军队女性化。|近义词：emasculate, weaken, soften |反义词: masculinize, strengthen, harden |词性:动词
