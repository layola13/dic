nonilluminatingly:/ˌnɒnɪˈluːmɪneɪtɪŋli/
adverb
没有启发性地，无益地
failing to provide insight or clarity; not helpful in understanding something.
The spokesperson answered the question nonilluminatingly, leaving the audience more confused than before.  发言人对这个问题的回答没有启发性，让听众比以前更困惑了。
The report, while comprehensive, discussed the topic nonilluminatingly and provided little new information.  这份报告虽然全面，但对该主题的讨论没有启发性，几乎没有提供新的信息。
Synonyms: unhelpfully, uselessly, pointlessly, vaguely, obscurely
Antonyms: helpfully, usefully, informatively, clearly, insightfully
词性: 副词
