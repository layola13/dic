bipolarities: /baɪˌpoʊˈlærɪtiz/ | n. | 双极性，两极性 | The state of having two opposite poles or extremes.  This can refer to literal poles like those of a magnet, or figurative poles like opposing viewpoints or emotional extremes. | The bipolarities of political discourse make compromise difficult. 政治话语的两极分化使得妥协变得困难。| Synonyms: dualities, dichotomies, polarities | Antonyms: uniformities, similarities | noun

bipolarities: /baɪˌpoʊˈlærɪtiz/ | n. | （尤指躁狂抑郁症的）两极性状态，躁郁症 |  Specifically referring to the presence of two opposing emotional states, most commonly associated with bipolar disorder, characterized by periods of mania and depression. | The patient's bipolarities made it challenging to manage his medication. 病人的两极性状态使得管理他的药物变得很有挑战性。 | Synonyms: manic-depressive illness, bipolar disorder | Antonyms: stability, emotional balance | noun
