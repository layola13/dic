encircles:/ɪnˈsɜːrk(ə)lz/
verb
环绕，包围
surrounds or forms a circle around something.
The fence encircles the entire property.  篱笆环绕着整个房产。
A ring of trees encircles the clearing. 一圈树木环绕着空地。

surround, enclose, encompass, ring
confine, restrict
