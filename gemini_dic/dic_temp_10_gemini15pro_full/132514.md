nondeterminable:/ˌnɒndɪˈtɜːmɪnəbl/ |adj.|不可确定的，无法判定的|Not able to be determined or established; undecidable.|The halting problem is nondeterminable for Turing machines. 图灵机停机问题是不可判定的。|The outcome of the experiment is nondeterminable.  实验结果是不可确定的。|近义词: undecidable, indeterminable, unascertainable|反义词: determinable, decidable, ascertainable|词性: 形容词

nondeterminability:/ˌnɒndɪˌtɜːmɪnəˈbɪləti/ |n.|不可确定性，无法判定性|The quality or state of being nondeterminable.|The nondeterminability of the problem makes it a challenge for researchers. 这个问题的不可确定性使其成为研究人员的挑战。|The theorem proves the nondeterminability of certain mathematical statements. 该定理证明了某些数学陈述的不可确定性。|近义词: undecidability, indeterminacy|反义词: determinability, decidability|词性: 名词

词源:  non- (not) + determinable (capable of being determined)  来自拉丁语 determinare,  意思是 "to limit, to fix, to determine".
