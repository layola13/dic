byline: /ˈbaɪlaɪn/ | n. | 作者署名；报刊文章首行 | A line at the beginning of a newspaper or magazine article that gives the writer's name. | The byline read "By Jane Doe."  作者署名是“简·多伊”。 | synonym: attribution, credit line | antonym: anonymous | noun

byline: /ˈbaɪlaɪn/ | n. | (广播或电视节目)制作人员名单 | A list of the people involved in making a broadcast or television program. | The byline for the documentary included the director and producer.  这部纪录片的制作人员名单包括导演和制片人。 | synonym: credits | antonym: N/A | noun
