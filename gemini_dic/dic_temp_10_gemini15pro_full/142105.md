orientative:/ɔːrˈiɛntətɪv/ | adj. | 指导性的，指示性的，初步的，大致的 | Serving to orient; providing guidance or direction; approximate or provisional. |  This is just an orientative price; the final cost may vary. 这只是一个参考价格，最终费用可能会有所不同。|  The meeting provided an orientative framework for future collaborations.  这次会议为未来的合作提供了一个指导性框架。 | 近义词: approximate, preliminary, provisional, indicative | 反义词: definitive, conclusive, final | 词性: adjective

词源: From the French word "orientatif," ultimately derived from the Latin "oriens" (east) and "orientare" (to set towards the east).  The original meaning related to finding one's bearings, which evolved into the more general sense of providing guidance.
