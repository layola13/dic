noun
联合，结合
The process of uniting or being united.
The adunation of the two companies created a powerful conglomerate.  两家公司的合并创造了一个强大的企业集团。

词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *adunatio*, from Latin adunare ("to unite"), from ad- ("to") + unus ("one").

USAGE->
