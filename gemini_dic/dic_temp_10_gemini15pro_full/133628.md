nonforeignnesses:/ˌnɒnˈfɒrɪnɪsnəsɪz/ /ˌnɑːnˈfɔːrɪnɪsnəsɪz/
noun
非外国性; 非异国性
The lack of foreign characteristics or influences.  A state of being not foreign. This word is extremely rare and likely coined. It describes the state or quality of not being foreign.
Due to globalization, many products exhibit a certain nonforeignness, blending elements from various cultures. 由于全球化，许多产品都展现出一定的非外国性，融合了不同文化的元素。
The nonforeignness of the cuisine disappointed the tourists hoping for an authentic experience.  这道菜的非外国性让希望体验地道风味的游客感到失望。
Near Synonyms: domesticity, nativeness, indigenousness
Antonyms: foreignness, exoticism
