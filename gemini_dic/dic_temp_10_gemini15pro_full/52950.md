dinful:/ˈdɪnfʊl/| adj.|喧闹的，嘈杂的|Characterized by a loud, unpleasant, and prolonged noise.|The dinful construction site made it impossible to concentrate. 喧闹的建筑工地让人无法集中注意力。|例句：The dinful bar made it hard to hear our conversation. 嘈杂的酒吧让我们很难听到彼此的谈话。

例句：The dinful city traffic kept me awake all night. 喧闹的城市交通让我整夜无法入睡。|近义词：noisy, clamorous, boisterous, raucous|反义词：quiet, peaceful, silent|词性:形容词


词源: From "din" (noise) + "-ful" (full of).  "Din" comes from Old English "dyne" (noise, uproar), ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*duniz" (noise, roar).
