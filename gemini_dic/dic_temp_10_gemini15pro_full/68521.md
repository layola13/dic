fancier:/ˈfænsiər/ | adj. | 更喜欢的，更昂贵的，更精致的 | More elaborate, expensive, or refined than is usual or necessary. | She has become much fancier since she got promoted.  她升职后变得讲究多了。 |  Synonyms: more elaborate, more expensive, more refined, upscale, luxurious | Antonyms: simpler, plainer, cheaper, basic, ordinary | adjective

fancier:/ˈfænsiər/ | n. | 爱好者，饲养者(尤指动物) | A person who has a strong interest in or breeds a particular animal. | He is a fancier of exotic birds. 他是一位珍奇鸟类的爱好者。 | Synonyms: enthusiast, devotee, admirer, breeder | Antonyms: critic, detractor | noun
