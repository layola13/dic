dents:/dɛnts/
n.
凹痕；齿；缺口
hollows or small depressions in a hard surface.
The car had a few dents from the hail storm.
这辆车在冰雹暴风雨中留下了一些凹痕。

近义词: depressions, hollows, indentations, notches
反义词: bulges, protrusions

词性: 名词

词源:  From Old French *denter "to tooth, indent," from dent "tooth," from Latin dentem (nominative dens) "tooth."
