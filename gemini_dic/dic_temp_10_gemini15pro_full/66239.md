exclamation:/ˌɛkskləˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 惊叹，感叹，惊呼 | A sudden, sharp cry or remark, especially one expressing surprise, anger, or pain. | She gave an exclamation of surprise. 她惊呼了一声。 |  synonyms: cry, shout, yell, shriek, whoop, roar, interjection, ejaculation | antonyms: whisper, murmur, undertone | noun

exclamation:/ˌɛkskləˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 感叹号 | A punctuation mark (!) indicating an exclamation. | The sentence ended with an exclamation point. 这个句子以感叹号结尾。| synonyms: exclamation point, exclamation mark | antonyms:  N/A | noun
