nontraceable:/ˌnɒnˈtreɪsəbl/ /ˌnɑːnˈtreɪsəbl/| adj.|无法追踪的，难以追查的|Not able to be traced or followed.  Something that is nontraceable cannot be found or identified as belonging to a particular person or source.|The police were baffled by the nontraceable gun used in the robbery. 警方对抢劫案中使用的无法追踪的枪支感到困惑。|The money was transferred through a series of nontraceable accounts. 这笔钱是通过一系列无法追踪的账户转移的。|untraceable, undetectable|traceable, identifiable|adjective
