artistlike:/ˈɑːrtɪstˌlaɪk/
adjective
像艺术家的；有艺术气质的
Resembling or characteristic of an artist; artistic.
Her apartment was decorated in an artistlike fashion. 她的公寓以艺术家的方式装饰。
近义词:artistic, creative, imaginative
反义词:uncreative, unimaginative
词性:形容词

词源: artist + -like.  "artist" comes from Old French "artiste" and ultimately from Latin "ars" (art). "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
