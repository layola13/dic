anachronously:/əˈnækrənəsli/
adv. |不同时地，时代错误地|Existing or occurring at a time other than the one in which events or ideas from another time are chronologically appropriate.|例句：The film anachronously places a cell phone in the hands of a medieval knight. 这部电影时代错误地让一个中世纪骑士拿着手机。|例句：He anachronously used modern slang in his historical novel. 他在他的历史小说中时代错误地使用了现代俚语。|近义词：out of time, chronologically misplaced, unseasonably |反义词:chronologically, contemporaneously |词性:副词
