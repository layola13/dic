nonfeasibly:/nɒnˈfiːzɪbli/
adv. |不可行地，办不到地|In a way that is not capable of being done or carried out. |The project was nonfeasibly expensive.  这个项目贵得让人无法承受。|Synonym: impracticably, impossibly|Antonym: feasibly, practically|词性: 副词

nonfeasible:/nɒnˈfiːzɪb(ə)l/
adj. |不可行的，办不到的|Not capable of being done or carried out. |The plan was deemed nonfeasible due to lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，该计划被认为不可行。|Synonym: impracticable, impossible|Antonym: feasible, practical|词性: 形容词

PHRASE->
not nonfeasibly = feasibly  并非不可行地 = 可行地
deemed nonfeasibly = considered impractically  被认为不可行地 = 被认为不切实际地
prove nonfeasibly = turn out impossibly  证明不可行 = 结果证明不可能
inherently nonfeasibly = fundamentally impractically  内在地不可行 = 根本上不切实际
considered nonfeasibly = judged impractically  被认为不可行 = 被判断为不切实际
