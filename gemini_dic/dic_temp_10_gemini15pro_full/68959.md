fatherliness:/ˈfɑːðərlɪnɪs/
noun
父爱，慈父般
The quality of being kind and protective, like a good father.
He radiated fatherliness and warmth. 他散发着父爱和温暖。
Synonyms: paternalism, protectiveness, benevolence, kindness, affection
Antonyms: coldness, harshness, cruelty, indifference, neglect
词性: 名词
