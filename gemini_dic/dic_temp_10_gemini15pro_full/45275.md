cultrate:/ˈkʌltreɪt/
adjective
刀形的；有刀的

The cultrate leaves of the iris plant are a striking feature.  鸢尾植物刀形的叶子是一个显著的特征。

cultrate:/ˈkʌltreɪt/
noun
刀状器
A knife-like instrument or weapon.
The archaeologist unearthed a bronze cultrate from the burial site. 考古学家从墓地挖掘出一把青铜刀状器。
