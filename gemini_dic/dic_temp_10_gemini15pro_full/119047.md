meticulousness:/məˈtɪkjələsnəs/| n.|一丝不苟，细致，严谨|The meticulousness of her work is impressive. 她的工作一丝不苟，令人印象深刻。|例句：The scientist conducted the experiment with meticulousness.  科学家一丝不苟地进行了实验。

例句：She approached the task with extreme meticulousness. 她以极度的细致处理这项任务。|近义词：precision, accuracy, carefulness, thoroughness, exactness|反义词：carelessness, negligence, sloppiness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From meticulous + -ness.  Meticulous comes from the Latin meticulosus, meaning "fearful, timid," from metus "fear." Over time, the meaning shifted to "overly cautious," and eventually to "showing extreme care about minute details."
