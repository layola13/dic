diuretic: /ˌdaɪjʊˈrɛtɪk/ | adj. n. | 利尿的；利尿剂 |  A diuretic is a substance that promotes increased production of urine.  A diuretic increases the excretion of water from the body. |  The doctor prescribed a diuretic to help reduce the swelling in her legs. 医生给她开了利尿剂来帮助减轻腿部肿胀。

The patient was given a diuretic to control fluid retention.  病人服用利尿剂来控制体液潴留。 | 近义词: water pill | 反义词: antidiuretic | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From French *diurétique*, from Greek *διουρητικός (diourētikos)*, from *διά (dia, “through”) + οὖρον (ouron, “urine”) +‎ *-ikos* (-ic).
