anthologies:/ænˈθɑːlədʒiz/
n.
选集，文集
A published collection of writings by different authors.
例句：I'm reading an anthology of American short stories. 我正在读一本美国短篇小说集。

例句：The poetry anthology includes works from both established and emerging poets. 这本诗歌选集收录了知名诗人和新兴诗人的作品。

近义词：collection, compilation, compendium
反义词：(没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用individual work, single piece)
词性：noun
