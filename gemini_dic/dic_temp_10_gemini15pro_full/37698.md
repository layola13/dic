companionry:/kəmˈpæn.jən.ri/
n.
陪伴；友谊；交谊
The state of being with a companion or companions; fellowship.
例句：She enjoyed the companionry of her fellow travelers. 她享受着和旅伴们在一起的时光。

例句：He missed the companionry of his old friends. 他想念老朋友们的陪伴。

近义词：fellowship, companionship, camaraderie, friendship, sociability
反义词：isolation, loneliness, solitude
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From companion + -ry.  Companion comes from Late Latin *compāniōn- (stem of compāniō ), from com- 'with' + pānis 'bread'.  Literally "one who shares bread with another." The suffix -ry denotes a state, condition, or practice.
