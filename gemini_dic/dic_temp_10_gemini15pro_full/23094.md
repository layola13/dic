bountiful: /ˈbaʊntɪfəl/ | adj. | 丰富的，大量的，慷慨的 | Giving freely and generously; plentiful.  | The land produced a bountiful harvest. 这片土地带来了丰收。 | abundant, plentiful, copious, ample, generous | meager, scarce, scanty, sparse | adjective

bountifully: /ˈbaʊntɪfəli/ | adv. | 丰富地，大量地，慷慨地 | In a bountiful manner; generously. |  The table was bountifully laden with food. 桌子上摆满了食物。 | abundantly, plentifully, copiously, amply, generously | meagerly, scarcely, scantily, sparsely | adverb

bounty: /ˈbaʊnti/ | n. | 慷慨，大方；赏金；赠款；丰饶 |  Generosity; something given liberally; a reward or premium. | The bounty of the sea provided food for the villagers.  大海的慷慨为村民提供了食物。 | generosity, liberality, munificence, abundance, plenty | scarcity, lack, dearth, deficiency | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bountevous, from Anglo-Norman bountevus, from bountee (“goodness, kindness, generosity”) + -ous, from Latin -ōsus.  Ultimately from Latin bonitas (“goodness”).
