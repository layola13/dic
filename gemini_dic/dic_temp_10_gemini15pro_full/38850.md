confectioning:/kənˈfɛkʃənɪŋ/
n.|糖果制造，糕点制作|The confectioning of the wedding cake took three days. 婚礼蛋糕的制作花了三天时间。|Example: The confectioning of fine chocolates requires skill and precision. 精致巧克力的制作需要技巧和精度。|Synonyms: making, producing, creating, preparing|Antonyms: destroying, dismantling|noun

confectioning:/kənˈfɛkʃənɪŋ/
v.|制作糖果，制作糕点|She was confectioning a batch of truffles for the holiday. 她正在为假期制作一批松露巧克力。|Example:  He spent the afternoon confectioning a beautiful dessert. 他用了一下午的时间制作了一道美味的甜点。|Synonyms: making, producing, creating, preparing|Antonyms: destroying, dismantling|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *confectio, from Latin conficere "to make together, accomplish, complete," from com- "together" (see com-) + facere "to do, make" (see fac-).
