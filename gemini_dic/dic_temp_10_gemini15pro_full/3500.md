afterpains:/ˈæftərpeɪnz/| n.|产后痛, 分娩后阵痛|Cramping abdominal pain after childbirth caused by uterine contractions as the uterus returns to its normal size.|She experienced severe afterpains after the birth of her twins. 她生完双胞胎后经历了剧烈的产后痛。|近义词：uterine contractions, postpartum cramps|反义词: (No direct antonym)|名词
