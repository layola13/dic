overinsures:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈʃʊrz/
verb
过度投保
To insure something for more than its value.
He overinsured his car in hopes of profiting from an accident. 他给自己的车过度投保，希望从事故中获利。
overprotect, overestimate
underinsure
