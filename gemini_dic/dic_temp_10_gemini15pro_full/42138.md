cottony:/ˈkɒtəni/ (UK), /ˈkɑːtəni/ (US)| adj.|棉的；像棉花的；柔软的|Resembling cotton in texture, softness, or appearance.  Made of cotton.|例句：The plant has soft, cottony leaves. 这种植物有柔软的、像棉花一样的叶子。

例句：She wrapped the baby in a cottony blanket. 她用一条棉质的毯子把婴儿裹了起来。|近义词：fluffy, downy, soft, fleecy|反义词：coarse, rough, harsh|词性:形容词
