bombora:/bɒmˈbɔːrə/ /bɑːmˈbɔːrə/
bombora|n.|暗礁，暗流，巨浪|A bombora, or bommie, is a shallow reef or submerged rock. It can also refer to the waves breaking over such a reef.  A bombora can be a dangerous hazard to ships and boats.|The boat was wrecked on the bombora. 这艘船在暗礁上失事了。|Synonyms: reef, shoal, submerged rock|Antonyms: deep water|Noun


bombora:/bɒmˈbɔːrə/ /bɑːmˈbɔːrə/
bombora|n.| (Australian slang) 汹涌的海浪，巨浪|In Australian slang, a bombora can also refer to a large wave, especially one that breaks heavily.|Surfers love riding the bombora.  冲浪者喜欢驾驭巨浪。|Synonyms: wave, breaker, surge|Antonyms: calm, still water|Noun
