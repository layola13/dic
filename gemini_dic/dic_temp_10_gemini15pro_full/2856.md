adversarial:/ˌædvərˈsɛriəl/| adj.|对抗的，敌对的|Characterized by or expressing hostility; relating to or characteristic of an adversary. |The two countries have an adversarial relationship. 这两个国家的关系是敌对的。

The lawyer adopted an adversarial approach during the cross-examination.  律师在盘问时采取了对抗性的方式。|近义词：antagonistic, hostile, opposed, conflicting|反义词：cooperative, friendly, supportive, collaborative|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *adversarius* "opponent," from Latin *adversus* "against, toward," past participle of *advertere* "to turn toward," from *ad-* "to" + *vertere* "to turn."

USAGE->
Attributive Use:
Adversarial proceedings are common in legal systems. 对抗性程序在法律体系中很常见。
The company faced adversarial market conditions. 该公司面临着不利的市场环境。


Predicative Use (less common):
Their relationship has become increasingly adversarial. 他们的关系变得越来越敌对。
The debate was highly adversarial.  这场辩论非常激烈。


USAGE->
In legal contexts:
The adversarial system ensures both sides are represented. 对抗制确保双方都有代表。
Adversarial legalism can sometimes be inefficient. 对抗性法制有时效率低下。

In business/competition:
Adversarial advertising can damage a company's reputation.  恶意广告会损害公司的声誉。
They took an adversarial stance in the negotiations. 他们在谈判中采取了对抗的立场。


PHRASE->
adversarial relationship = 敌对关系
adversarial system = 对抗制（尤指法律制度）
adversarial proceedings = 对抗性程序
adversarial approach = 对抗性方法
adversarial environment =  敌对环境
