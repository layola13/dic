hypodicrotous:/ˌhaɪpəˈdɪkrətəs/
adj.
次泛音的；次泛音性强的
Having or characterized by a secondary or subsidiary pulse or beat, especially in the arterial pulse.  Relating to a dicrotic pulse with a secondary wave so marked as to give the impression of a double beat.
The doctor noted a hypodicrotous pulse in the patient, indicating a possible circulatory issue.  医生注意到病人的脉搏是次泛音的，这表明可能存在循环系统问题。
Near Synonyms: dicrotic, double-beat
Antonyms: monocrotic
