mavournin:/məˈvɔːrnɪn/ |n.|爱人，亲爱的|A term of endearment, often used in Irish English, meaning "my darling" or "my love". It's derived from the Irish phrase "mo mhúirnín," meaning "my little darling."| Mavournin, will you dance with me?  亲爱的，你愿意和我跳舞吗？|Synonyms: darling, sweetheart, love, dear | Antonyms: enemy, foe | Noun
