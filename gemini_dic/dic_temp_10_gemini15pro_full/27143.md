call-a-day:/ˈkɔːl ə deɪ/| n.|一天一次的电话；每天一次的电话；|A sales strategy involving making one sales call per day to each potential client.|例句：The company implemented a call-a-day policy to reach out to more customers.  这家公司实施了每天一次的电话政策，以联系更多客户。

例句：The call-a-day approach can be effective for building relationships with potential clients. 每天一次的电话方式可以有效地与潜在客户建立关系。|近义词：daily call, regular contact|反义词: infrequent contact|noun
