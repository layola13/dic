overbashfulness:/ˌoʊvərˈbæʃfʊlnəs/| n. |过度害羞，过分羞怯|Excessive shyness or timidity.|例句：His overbashfulness prevented him from speaking up. 他的过度害羞使他不敢开口说话。

例句：Her overbashfulness made it difficult for her to make new friends.  她的过分羞怯让她很难结交新朋友。

|近义词：shyness, timidity, diffidence, modesty|反义词: boldness, confidence, extroversion|词性:noun
