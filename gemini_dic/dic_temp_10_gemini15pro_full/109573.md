let-slide:/lɛt slaɪd/ | v. | 疏忽; 放过; 听之任之 | to allow something to go unchecked or to deteriorate; to neglect or ignore something | I let the housework slide while I was on vacation. 我在度假时忽略了家务。 |  neglect, ignore, overlook | address, attend to | verb


let-slide:/lɛt slaɪd/ | n. |  疏忽; 放任 | an instance of letting something go unchecked or deteriorate |  Giving him a let-slide this time will only encourage him to do it again. 这次放过他只会鼓励他再犯。 | negligence, oversight | attention, care | noun
