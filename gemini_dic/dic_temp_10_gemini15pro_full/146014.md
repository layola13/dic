overdraft: /ˈəʊvədrɑːft/ (UK) /ˈoʊvərdræft/ (US)
|n.|透支；透支额度|An overdraft is an agreement with your bank that allows you to spend more money than you have in your account, up to a certain limit.  透支是指与银行达成的一项协议，允许你在账户余额不足的情况下支出一定限额的款项。|I had to pay a lot of interest on my overdraft. 我的透支利息很高。|Synonyms: overdrawn balance, deficit, shortfall|Antonyms: credit, surplus|noun
overdraft: /ˈəʊvədrɑːft/ (UK) /ˈoʊvərdræft/ (US)
|v.|透支|To overdraft an account means to draw more money than is available in the account.  透支一个账户意味着提取超过账户可用余额的款项。|I accidentally overdrafted my account when I bought those plane tickets. 我买机票的时候不小心透支了账户。|Synonyms: overdraw|Antonyms: deposit, credit|verb
