pamela:/ˈpæmələ/ | n. |帕梅拉（女子名）|A female given name, ultimately derived from Greek. It was popularized by Sir Philip Sidney's pastoral romance *Arcadia* (1590).|例句：Pamela is my best friend. 帕梅拉是我最好的朋友。

例句：He named his daughter Pamela. 他给他女儿取名叫帕梅拉。

近义词：Pam, Mel |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
