incorrigibleness:/ɪnˈkɔːrɪdʒɪblənəs/
n.
不可救药, 无可救药, 顽固不化
The state of being incorrigible; incapable of being corrected or reformed.
His incorrigibleness made it difficult for him to hold down a job.  他的无可救药使他很难保住一份工作。
Synonyms: inveteracy, obstinacy, perverseness, depravity
Antonyms: corrigibility, amendability, docility, tractability
noun


incorrigible:/ɪnˈkɔːrɪdʒɪbl/
adj.
不可救药的, 无可救药的, 顽固不化的
Not capable of being corrected or reformed.
He's an incorrigible flirt. 他是个无可救药的花花公子。
Synonyms: incurable, irredeemable, hopeless, inveterate
Antonyms: corrigible, reformable, redeemable, tractable
adjective
