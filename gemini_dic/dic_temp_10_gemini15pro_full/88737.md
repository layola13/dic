hobos:/ˈhoʊboʊz/| n. |流浪汉，游民|A homeless person, especially one who travels around, usually by riding freight trains illegally.|例句：The hobos gathered around the fire to warm themselves. 流浪汉们聚集在火堆旁取暖。

例句：During the Great Depression, many people became hobos. 大萧条时期，许多人变成了流浪汉。|近义词：vagrant, tramp, drifter, bum|反义词:settler, resident|词性:noun
