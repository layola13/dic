instate:/ˈɪnˈsteɪt/| adj.|州内的；州际的|Existing or occurring within a state.  Relating to education at a reduced tuition rate, available to students who are residents of the state where the college or university is located.|例句：Instate tuition is significantly cheaper than out-of-state tuition. 州内学费比州外学费便宜得多。

例句：She qualified for instate tuition because she had lived in the state for over a year. 因为她在该州居住了一年多，所以有资格获得州内学费。|近义词：domestic, local|反义词：out-of-state, interstate|词性:adjective
