instratified:/ɪnˈstrætɪfaɪd/ | adj. |未分层的，非成层的 | Not stratified; not arranged in strata or layers. | The soil sample was instratified, making it difficult to determine its age. 土壤样本未分层，因此很难确定其年代。|  The mixture was instratified, resulting in uneven baking. 混合物未分层，导致烘烤不均匀。 | 近义词：homogeneous, unlayered, uniform | 反义词: stratified, layered | 词性: 形容词

词源: in- (not) + stratified (arranged in layers)  来自拉丁语 stratum，意思是“铺开的，覆盖物”，后来指地质层。
