cross-examine:/ˌkrɒsɪɡˈzæmɪn/ | v. | 盘问，反复询问 | To question a witness called by the other side in a court of law to check or challenge their evidence. | The lawyer cross-examined the witness about his alibi. 律师盘问了证人关于他不在场证明的事情。

The prosecutor cross-examined the defendant rigorously.  检察官严厉地盘问了被告。| 近义词：question, interrogate, grill | 反义词：corroborate, support | 词性：动词
