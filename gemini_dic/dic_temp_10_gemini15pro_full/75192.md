frizzles:/ˈfrɪzəlz/ /ˈfrɪzlz/| v. n.|卷曲，使卷曲，油炸至卷曲；油炸食物发出的嘶嘶声（frizzle的第三人称单数形式）|The sun frizzles the grass. 太阳晒得草都卷起来了。|近义词：curls, crimps, sizzles, fries|反义词:straightens|词性:动词/名词

词源:  From Middle English *frislen, frequentative of  Middle English  frisen (“to fry”), from Old French friser (“to curl, crisp, shrink”), from Frankish *frisōn (“to curl, crisp”), from Proto-Germanic *frisōną (“to curl, crisp”). Cognate with Middle Dutch vrisen (“to fry”), Old High German frisōn (“to curl, crisp”). Related to freeze.
