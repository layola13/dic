absorptional:/əbˈzɔːrpʃənl/ /əbˈsɔːrpʃənl/
adjective
吸收性的; 与吸收有关的
Relating to or characterized by absorption.
The absorptional properties of the sponge are excellent.  海绵的吸收性能非常好。

The scientist studied the absorptional capacity of the new material.  科学家研究了这种新材料的吸收能力。

近义词: absorbent, absorptive
反义词: nonabsorbent, impermeable
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From absorption + -al.  Absorption comes from Late Latin absorptionem, from absorbere "to swallow up," from ab- "away" + sorbere "to suck in."

USAGE->
This word is less common than "absorbent" or "absorptive."  It is typically used in more technical or scientific contexts.
