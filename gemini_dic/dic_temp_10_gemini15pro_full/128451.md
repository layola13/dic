newscasters:/ˈnjuːzkæstərz/
n.
新闻播报员
A person who presents news stories on television or radio.
The newscasters reported on the developing situation.  新闻播报员报道了正在发展的情况。
The newscasters announced the election results.  新闻播报员宣布了选举结果。
synonyms: anchor, reporter, broadcaster, journalist, announcer
antonyms: audience, viewer, listener
