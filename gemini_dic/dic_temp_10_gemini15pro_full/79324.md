gleaning:/ˈɡliːnɪŋ/ | n. | 收集，拾落， gleaning(s)复数形式| The act of collecting leftover crops from farmers' fields after they have been harvested. | After the harvest, the poor were permitted to glean the remaining grain from the fields. 收获之后，穷人们被允许从田里拾取剩余的谷物。 | synonyms: gathering, collecting, harvesting, scavenging | antonyms: discarding, wasting, sowing, planting | noun

glean:/ɡliːn/ | v. | 收集，拾取 | To gather (leftover grain or other produce) after a harvest. To obtain (information) bit by bit. | She gleaned information from various sources. 她从各种来源收集信息。| synonyms: gather, collect, obtain, extract | antonyms: scatter, disperse, lose | verb
