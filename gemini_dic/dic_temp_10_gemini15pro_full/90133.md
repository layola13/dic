hotbedding:/ˈhɒtˌbɛdɪŋ/ (US) /ˈhɒtˌbedɪŋ/ (UK)
|n.|轮用工作位；床位轮用；(喻)温床|The practice of having employees share desks or workstations, typically on different shifts.  Also used figuratively to describe an environment that fosters rapid growth or development of something, often something negative.|Many tech companies use hotbedding to maximize office space. 许多科技公司采用轮用工作位来最大化利用办公空间。|synonyms: desk-sharing, workstation sharing,  (figuratively) breeding ground, incubator|antonyms: dedicated workspace, private office|noun
