ambushments:/ˈæmbʊʃmənts/| n. |埋伏；伏击；埋伏地点|The act of lying in wait to attack by surprise; or, the place of concealment for such a purpose.|The soldiers lay in ambushments, waiting for the enemy to pass. 士兵们埋伏起来，等待敌人经过。|The general planned a series of ambushments along the enemy's supply route. 将军沿着敌人的补给路线计划了一系列伏击。|近义词：ambushes, traps, snares|反义词: open confrontation, direct attack|noun
