overtechnicality:/ˌoʊvərˌtɛknɪˈkælɪti/| n.| 过于技术性，过分拘泥于技术细节 | An overtechnicality in the contract caused confusion among the signatories.  合同中过于技术性的条款让签署方感到困惑。 | The lawyer's explanation was bogged down in overtechnicality. 律师的解释陷入了过多的技术细节。 |近义词：technicality, jargon, complexity |反义词:simplicity, clarity| noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (excessive) + "technicality" (a detail related to technique).  "Technicality" comes from "technical" + "-ity." "Technical" derives from the Greek "tekhnikos" (artistic, skillful), from "tekhnē" (art, skill, craft).
