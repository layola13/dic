oversoak: /ˌoʊvərˈsoʊk/
verb
过度浸泡；浸泡过久
to soak something for too long
I oversoaked the beans and they turned to mush. 我把豆子泡得太久了，它们都变成糊状了。
The dress was oversoaked and the color ran.  这件衣服浸泡过久，颜色都褪色了。
近义词: oversaturate, waterlog
反义词: under-soak, dehydrate
词性: verb
