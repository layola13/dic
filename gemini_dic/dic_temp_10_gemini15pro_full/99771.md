intermediates:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdiəts/ | n. | 中间体，中间物；媒介物；中间人 |A substance formed during a chemical process before the desired product is obtained.  Something that is between two other things in size, amount, quality, etc. A person who acts as a link between people or things. | The reaction proceeds via a series of intermediates.  这个反应通过一系列中间体进行。| synonyms: intermediary, mediator, go-between, agent, middleman | antonyms: extremes, endpoints | noun

intermediate:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdiət/ | adj. | 中间的，中级的；媒介的 |Lying or occurring between two extremes or in a middle position or state.  Being at a level of development or skill between basic and advanced. | He enrolled in an intermediate English course. 他报名参加了一个中级英语课程。| synonyms: middle, medium, middling, average, moderate | antonyms: beginning, elementary, advanced, extreme | adjective
