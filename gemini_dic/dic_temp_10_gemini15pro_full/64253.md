equipoising:/ˌikwɪˈpɔɪzɪŋ/
n.|平衡，均衡|The equipoising of different interests in society is a complex task. 社会上不同利益的均衡是一项复杂的任务。|
v.|使平衡，使均衡|The government tried to equipoise the budget. 政府试图平衡预算。|
英文解析: The act of bringing into or keeping in equilibrium; balancing.  Can be used as a noun (the act of balancing) or as a verb (to balance).  Often used in a more abstract sense, referring to balancing forces, ideas, or interests.|
例句：The tightrope walker's equipoising act was breathtaking. 走钢丝者的平衡表演令人叹为观止。
例句：The judge equipoised the rights of the individual against the needs of society. 法官权衡了个人权利与社会需求之间的关系。
近义词：balancing, equalizing, counterbalancing, stabilizing
反义词：destabilizing, upsetting, unbalancing
词性:  n., v.


词源: From equi- (equal) + poise (balance).  Ultimately from Latin aequus (equal) and pendere (to weigh).
