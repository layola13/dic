parthenogenic:/ˌpɑːrθɪnəˈdʒɛnɪk/ | adj. | 单性生殖的，孤雌生殖的 |  Relating to or characterized by parthenogenesis, a type of asexual reproduction in which the offspring develops from an unfertilized egg. |  Example: Some species of lizards are parthenogenic.  一些种类的蜥蜴是单性生殖的。| Synonym: asexual, agamic | Antonym: sexual |  adjective
