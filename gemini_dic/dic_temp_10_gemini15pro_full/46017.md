ETYMOLOGY->
"Cyberpunk" is a portmanteau of "cybernetics" and "punk."  "Cybernetics" refers to the science of communication and control systems in both machines and living things.  "Punk" suggests a rebellious and counter-cultural attitude.  The term was coined by Bruce Bethke as the title of a short story in 1980 and popularized by science fiction writer William Gibson in the 1980s.
