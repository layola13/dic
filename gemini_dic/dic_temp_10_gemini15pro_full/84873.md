hangingly:/ˈhæŋɪŋli/
adverb
悬挂地，垂悬地
in a hanging manner; so as to hang down
The vines draped hangingly over the entrance. 藤蔓悬挂在入口上方。
近义词:pendulously, droopingly
反义词:upright, erect
词性:副词


词源: From "hanging" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Hanging" comes from the Old English "hangian," meaning "to hang."

PHRASE->
This is rarely used in phrases.  It is primarily a descriptive adverb. However, you could construct something like:
The leaves hung hangingly, almost sadly, from the branches. 树叶垂悬在树枝上，几乎带着悲伤。
