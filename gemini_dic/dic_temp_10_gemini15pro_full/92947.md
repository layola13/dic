ideogram:/ˈaɪdiəɡræm/ | n. | 表意文字; 表意符号 | A written symbol that represents an idea or object directly rather than a particular word or speech sound, as in Chinese script. |  Chinese characters are ideograms, representing concepts rather than sounds. 汉字是表意文字，代表概念而不是声音。| synonym: logogram, glyph | antonym: phonogram | noun
