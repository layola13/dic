furthermore:/ˌfɜːrðərˈmɔːr/| adv.|此外，而且|Used to introduce a new point or fact that supports or adds to what has already been said.  It indicates a continuation and strengthening of the argument or point being made.|The house is too small for our family; furthermore, it's too far from the city.|近义词：moreover, in addition, besides, also|反义词:however, nevertheless, nonetheless|词性:副词
