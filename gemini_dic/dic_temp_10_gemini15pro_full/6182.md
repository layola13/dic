amities: /ˈæmɪtiz/
n.
友好关系，友谊
peaceful relations, as between nations; friendship.
The two countries maintained cordial amities despite their political differences. 这两个国家尽管政治分歧，但仍保持着友好的关系。
近义词: friendships, rapports, goodwill, concord
反义词: hostilities, enmities, antagonism

词性: 名词 (Noun)

词源:  From Old French *amité, from Latin amicitas (“friendship”), from amicus (“friend”).
