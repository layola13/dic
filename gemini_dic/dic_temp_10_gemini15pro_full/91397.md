hydroxanthane:/haɪˌdrɒkˈsænθeɪn/ | n. | 羟基黄原胶 | A polysaccharide produced by certain bacteria, similar to xanthan gum but with hydroxyl groups.  It is used as a thickening and stabilizing agent in food and other products. |  Hydroxanthane is used as a stabilizer in some ice creams. 羟基黄原胶在一些冰淇淋中用作稳定剂。| Synonym:  (no direct synonym, but similar to) xanthan gum, guar gum | Antonym:  (no direct antonym) | noun
