helicity:/hɪˈlɪsɪti/ |n.|螺旋性，螺旋度|A measure of the degree to which a particle spins along the direction of its motion.|The helicity of a photon can be either +1 or -1. 光子的螺旋度可以是+1或-1。|spirality, twist, torsion| |noun
