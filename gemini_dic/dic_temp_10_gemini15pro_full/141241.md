op-artists:/ˈɒp ɑːtɪsts/ | n. | 欧普艺术家；光效应艺术家 | Artists who create optical illusions through abstract patterns and colors.  These artists are associated with the Op Art movement, short for Optical Art. |  The exhibition showcased several prominent op-artists of the 1960s.  这次展览展示了几位1960年代杰出的欧普艺术家。 | synonyms: optical artists, Op artists | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "representational artists") | noun

Etymology:  "Op" is a shortened form of "Optical," derived from the Greek word "optikos," meaning "relating to sight."  "Art" comes from the Latin "ars," meaning "skill" or "craft."
