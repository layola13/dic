bhai:/ˈbaɪ/| n.|兄弟，哥们（主要用于南亚地区）|A term of endearment and respect for a male, similar to "brother" or "mate" but often with deeper cultural connotations in South Asian communities.|例句：Hey bhai, how are you doing? 嘿，兄弟，你好吗？

例句：Bhai, can you help me with this? 兄弟，你能帮我一下吗？

近义词：brother, mate, dude, pal|反义词:enemy, foe|词性:名词
