flamboyer:/ˌflæmˈbwɑːjeɪ/ /ˌflɒmˈbwɑːjeɪ/
n.
凤凰木, 火焰树
A flamboyant tree, noted for its scarlet flowers.  A tree of the genus Delonix, especially D. regia, native to Madagascar and widely cultivated in the tropics.
The flamboyant tree was in full bloom, its scarlet flowers a vibrant splash of color against the blue sky. 凤凰木盛开着，猩红色的花朵在蓝天下构成了一幅鲜艳的色彩。
The hotel grounds were shaded by flamboyant trees.  酒店的庭院里种满了凤凰木。

近义词:royal poinciana, flame tree
反义词:N/A
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French flamboyant "flaming," from Old French flamboiant, present participle of flamboier "to blaze," from flambe "flame."
