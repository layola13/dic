considering:/kənˈsɪdərɪŋ/ | prep. conj.| 考虑到，鉴于 | Taking into account; in view of. | Example: Considering the weather, we decided to stay indoors. 考虑到天气，我们决定待在室内。| Synonyms: given, in view of, taking into account | Antonyms: disregarding, ignoring | 词性: 介词/连词

considering:/kənˈsɪdərɪŋ/ | adj.|  值得考虑的；相当大的 | Worth thinking about; quite large. | Example: That's a considering amount of money. 那是一笔相当大的钱。| Synonyms: significant, substantial | Antonyms: insignificant, negligible | 词性: 形容词

词源: From the present participle of consider, from Old French considerer, from Latin considerare "to look at closely, contemplate, observe, examine," from com- "together" + sidus (genitive sideris) "constellation, star," thus literally "to observe the stars together, consult the stars."
