heliographers:/ˌhiːliˈɒɡrəfəz/
n.
日照记录员，日光摄影师
A person who operates a heliograph, especially for sending signals.
The heliographers signaled the approaching ships. 日照记录员向正在靠近的船只发出信号。
近义词:signalman, signaller
反义词:None
词性:Noun


heliograph:/ˈhiːliəɡrɑːf/
n.
日光反射信号机，日光摄影机
An apparatus for signaling by flashes of sunlight reflected from a mirror. Also, a photograph taken using sunlight.
The army used a heliograph to communicate across long distances. 军队使用日光反射信号机进行远距离通信。
近义词:semaphore, signal lamp
反义词:None
词性:Noun


词源:
From Greek hēlios "sun" + -graph "writing instrument."
