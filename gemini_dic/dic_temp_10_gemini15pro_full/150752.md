paracrystalline:/ˌpærəˈkrɪstəlɪn/| adj.|准晶的，近晶的|Having a partially ordered structure, like that of a liquid crystal; intermediate between crystalline and amorphous.|例句：The polymer exhibited a paracrystalline structure.  这种聚合物呈现出准晶结构。

例句：Paracrystalline materials have unique optical properties. 准晶材料具有独特的光学特性。|近义词：mesomorphic, semicrystalline|反义词：amorphous, crystalline|词性：形容词

Etymology: para- (beside, alongside, resembling) + crystalline (having the structure of a crystal)
