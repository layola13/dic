intercontradiction:/ˌɪntərˌkɒntrəˈdɪkʃən/ | n. |相互矛盾，内在矛盾|A logical incompatibility between two or more propositions.|His argument was full of intercontradictions, making it difficult to follow his reasoning. 他的论点充满了内在矛盾，很难理解他的推理。|Example: The witness's testimony was filled with intercontradictions, leading the jury to question his credibility.  证人的证词充满了相互矛盾之处，导致陪审团质疑他的可信度。|Synonyms: inconsistency, discrepancy, conflict, clash|Antonyms: consistency, agreement, harmony|noun
