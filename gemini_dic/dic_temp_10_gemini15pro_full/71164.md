fissipalmate:/ˌfɪsɪˈpælmɪt/ | adj. | 半蹼足的；趾间半蹼的 | Having the toes or fingers webbed about halfway to the tips. |  The red-breasted merganser has fissipalmate feet, which help it swim and dive efficiently. | 红胸秋沙鸭有半蹼足，这有助于它高效地游泳和潜水。| semi-palmate |  -- | adjective
Etymology-> From Latin *fissus* (cleft, divided) + *palmatus* (palmate, having the shape of a hand).
