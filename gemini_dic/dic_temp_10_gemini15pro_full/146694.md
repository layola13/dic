overheat:/ˌoʊvərˈhiːt/ (US) /ˌəʊvəˈhiːt/ (UK)| v. n.|过热；过热；|To become excessively hot; to cause something to become excessively hot.  When an engine or a machine overheats, it becomes too hot. When a person overheats, their body temperature becomes dangerously high.|The engine overheated because the radiator was empty. (发动机因为水箱空了而过热了。)  He overheated during the marathon and had to drop out. (他在马拉松比赛中体温过高，不得不退出。)|近义词：overcook, burn out, scorch|反义词: cool, chill|词性:动词, 名词

overheat:/ˌoʊvərˈhiːt/ (US) /ˌəʊvəˈhiːt/ (UK)| n.|过热|The state of being excessively hot, especially of a machine.|The mechanic said the overheat was caused by a faulty thermostat. (机械师说过热是由一个有缺陷的恒温器引起的。)|近义词： overheating|反义词: cooling|词性:名词
