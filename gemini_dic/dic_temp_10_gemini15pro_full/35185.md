clubhouse:/ˈklʌbˌhaʊs/ | n. | 会所；俱乐部；俱乐部会所 | A building occupied by a club or used for its activities. |  He's a member of the golf clubhouse. 他是高尔夫俱乐部会所的会员。

The clubhouse is available for private parties.  这个会所可用于举办私人派对。

| 近义词：club, association, society | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 non-member area 非会员区) | 词性: 名词
