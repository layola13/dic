opuscule: /ˈɒpəskjuːl/ (US) /ˈɒpʌskjuːl/ (UK)| n. |小作品；短篇作品；小册子|A small or minor literary or musical work. | He published several opuscules on local history. 他出版了几本关于当地历史的小册子。| The composer's early opuscules showed promise of his later greatness. 这位作曲家早期的短篇作品展现了他日后伟大的潜力。 |近义词: pamphlet, booklet, tract, brochure |反义词: tome, magnum opus |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *opuscule*, diminutive of Latin *opusculum*, diminutive of *opus* ("work").
