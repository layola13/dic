aeromancy:/ˈɛəroʊˌmænsi/ | n. |占卜, 气象占卜| Divination by observing the air or atmospheric conditions.  It's a form of divination that attempts to interpret atmospheric conditions to predict the future. |Ancient cultures practiced aeromancy to forecast weather patterns and divine the will of the gods. 古代文化运用气象占卜来预测天气模式和神明的旨意。

Aeromancy was often used in conjunction with other forms of divination, such as astrology. 气象占卜经常与其他形式的占卜一起使用，例如占星术。| 近义词: divination, augury, soothsaying | 反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially "scientific forecasting") | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀήρ (aḗr, “air”) + μαντεία (manteía, “divination”).
