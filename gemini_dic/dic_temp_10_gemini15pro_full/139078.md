oats:/əʊts/ (UK), /oʊts/ (US)| n.|燕麦|A cereal grain (Avena sativa) and its edible starchy seeds.  Oats are a common breakfast food, often served as oatmeal or rolled oats.|I eat oats every morning for breakfast. 我每天早上都吃燕麦作为早餐。|近义词：oatmeal, porridge|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially other grains like rice or wheat as alternatives) |noun
