leprosy:/ˈlɛprəsi/| n. |麻风病|A chronic infectious disease that mainly affects the skin, nerves, and mucous membranes.  It is caused by the bacterium Mycobacterium leprae. |例句：Leprosy is a curable disease if detected early.  如果及早发现，麻风病是可以治愈的。

例句：In the past, people with leprosy were often ostracized. 过去，麻风病人经常被排斥。|近义词：Hansen's disease|反义词:health|词性:noun
