backbone:/ˈbækˌboʊn/ (US), /ˈbækˌbəʊn/ (UK)| n.| 支柱；骨干；脊柱；毅力；决心|The central or most important part of something; strength of character.|例句：The steel industry forms the backbone of the local economy. 钢铁工业是当地经济的支柱。

例句：She showed real backbone when she stood up to the bullies. 她勇敢地对抗恶霸，展现了真正的毅力。

近义词：spine, foundation, core, support, mainstay, fortitude, resolution|反义词：weakness, indecision|词性：名词
