cos:/kɒz/ (UK), /kɑːz/ (US) | conj. | 因为 | Short for "because". Used informally. | It's cold cos it's winter. 因为是冬天，所以很冷。|  I didn't go, cos I was tired. 我没去，因为我累了。 | 近义词: because, as, since | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: conjunction


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I eat breakfast every day. = 我每天都吃早餐。
She goes to school by bus. = 她乘公共汽车上学。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am eating lunch now. = 我现在正在吃午饭。
They are playing football in the park. = 他们在公园里踢足球。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have finished my homework. = 我已经完成了我的家庭作业。
She has been to Paris twice. = 她去过巴黎两次。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been studying English for three years. = 我一直在学习英语三年了。
They have been working on this project for months. = 他们几个月来一直在做这个项目。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I went to the cinema yesterday. = 我昨天去看电影了。
She ate a sandwich for lunch. = 她午餐吃了一个三明治。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was watching TV when you called. = 你打电话时我正在看电视。
They were playing cards when the power went out. = 停电时他们正在打牌。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had finished my work before he arrived. = 在他到达之前我已经完成了我的工作。
She had already left when I got there. = 当我到达那里时，她已经离开了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been waiting for an hour when the bus finally arrived. = 公共汽车终于到达时，我已经等了一个小时了。
They had been working on the project for weeks before they finished it. = 他们在完成项目之前已经工作了几个星期。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will go to the beach tomorrow. = 我明天要去海滩。
She will visit her grandparents next week. = 她下周要去看望她的祖父母。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be watching the game at 8 pm tomorrow. = 明天晚上8点我会在看比赛。
They will be traveling to Europe next summer. = 明年夏天他们将去欧洲旅行。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I will have finished the book by the end of the week. = 我将在本周末之前读完这本书。
She will have graduated from university by next year. = 她明年将大学毕业。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
I will have been living here for five years next month. = 下个月我将在这里住了五年。
They will have been working on this project for a year by the time it's finished. = 到项目完成时，他们将已经做了一年了。

注意事项:  "cos" 本身没有反义词，因为它是一个连接词，表示因果关系。 而且，它是一个非正式的缩写，主要用于口语或非正式的书面语中。  虽然"because"可以被认为是近义词，但"cos" 更口语化和非正式。
