palaeography: /ˌpeɪliˈɒɡrəfi/ /ˌpɑːliˈɑːɡrəfi/
noun
古文字学；古文书学
The study of ancient writing systems and the deciphering and dating of historical manuscripts.
The professor of palaeography lectured on the evolution of the Latin alphabet.  古文字学教授讲授了拉丁字母的演变。
