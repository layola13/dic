civicses:/ˈsɪvɪksɪz/
noun
公民学；公民教育（civics的复数形式）
The study of the rights and duties of citizenship.  This word is rarely used in the plural form.
He's taking a civics class this semester. 他这学期在学习公民课程。
(It's more common to say "civics class" rather than "civicses class.") 他这学期在上公民课。（比"civicses class"更常见的说法）
Near Synonyms: citizenship education, government studies
Antonyms: (Difficult to provide direct antonyms, perhaps) apathy, disengagement
词性: noun
