obelus: /ˈɒbələs/ (UK), /ˈoʊbələs/ (US) | n. | 分隔号; 除号 | A mark (÷ or †) used in ancient manuscripts to mark a spurious or doubtful passage, or to indicate a corruption.  It is also used as a division sign. |  The editor used an obelus to indicate a questionable passage in the manuscript. 编辑用一个分隔号来表示手稿中一段有疑问的文字。 |  The obelus is sometimes used in place of the division sign. 分隔号有时用来代替除号。 | synonyms: division sign, dagger, cross | antonyms: N/A | noun

obelus: /ˈɒbələs/ (UK), /ˈoʊbələs/ (US) | n. | 剑号，短剑号 | A mark resembling a small dagger or cross ( † ) used as a reference mark or to indicate a death date. | The footnote was marked with an obelus. 脚注标有剑号。 | His death date was marked with an obelus in the family Bible.  他的死亡日期在家族圣经中用剑号标出。 | synonyms: dagger, cross, reference mark | antonyms: N/A | noun
