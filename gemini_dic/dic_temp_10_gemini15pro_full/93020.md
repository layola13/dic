idioticalness:/ˌɪdiˈɒtɪkəlnəs/| n. |愚蠢，白痴|The idioticalness of his decision shocked everyone. |His plan was remarkable for its sheer idioticalness. | 近义词：stupidity, foolishness, absurdity |反义词: wisdom, intelligence, brilliance |词性:noun

Etymology:
From idiotical + -ness.  Idiotical from idiot + -ical. Idiot from Middle French idiote "unlearned, ignorant person," from Latin idiota "ordinary person, layman; ignorant person," from Greek idiotes "private person, layman, ignorant person," from idios "one's own."  -ness from Old English -nes(s), forming abstract nouns from adjectives.
