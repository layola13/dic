non-taxonomic:/ˌnɒnˌtæksəˈnɒmɪk/
adjective
非分类学的
Not relating to or involving taxonomy; not based on taxonomic categories.
The museum uses a non-taxonomic approach to display its collection, focusing on cultural significance rather than scientific classification.  博物馆采用非分类学的方法来展示其藏品，侧重于文化意义而不是科学分类。
The study employed a non-taxonomic approach, grouping the organisms by habitat instead of phylogeny.  这项研究采用了一种非分类学的方法，根据栖息地而不是系统发育对生物进行分组。
Synonyms: non-classificatory
Antonyms: taxonomic
