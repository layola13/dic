overencouraging: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈkɜːrɪdʒɪŋ/
adjective
过度鼓励的；鼓励过度的
Giving someone too much encouragement, which can sometimes have a negative effect.
He was overencouraging to the point where it became counterproductive. 他过度鼓励以至于适得其反。
The overencouraging coach created a team of players who couldn't handle criticism.  这位过度鼓励的教练造就了一支无法承受批评的队伍。

近义词: oversupportive, excessively encouraging
反义词: discouraging, under-supportive
词性: adjective
