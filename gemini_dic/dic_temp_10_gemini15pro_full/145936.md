overdiscounts:/ˌoʊvərˈdɪskaʊnts/ | n. |过度折扣，过低折扣|A discount that is excessively large, potentially resulting in a loss or reduced profit margin.| The company's overdiscounts on its products led to a significant decrease in profits. 该公司对其产品的过度折扣导致利润大幅下降。|Synonyms: excessive discounts, steep discounts, deep discounts|Antonyms: small discounts, minimal discounts|noun


overdiscount:/ˌoʊvərˈdɪskaʊnt/ | v. |过度打折，过低打折|To apply a discount that is excessively large.|The retailer overdiscounted the merchandise in an attempt to clear out inventory.  零售商对商品进行了过度打折，试图清理库存。|Synonyms: discount excessively, discount heavily, discount steeply|Antonyms: discount lightly, discount minimally|verb
