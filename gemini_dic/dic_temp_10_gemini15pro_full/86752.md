helpful:/ˈhɛlpfəl/| adj.|有帮助的；有用的|Giving or ready to give help.|例句：She offered some helpful advice. 她提供了一些有用的建议。|近义词：useful, beneficial, supportive, assisting|反义词:unhelpful, useless|词性:形容词
Etymology: From help + -ful.  "help" comes from Old English "helpan," meaning "to aid, support, succor."
