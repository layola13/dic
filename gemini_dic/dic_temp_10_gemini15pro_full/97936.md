innovates: /ˈɪnəveɪts/
verb
创新，改革
introduces something new; makes changes in anything established.
The company constantly innovates to stay ahead of the competition.  这家公司不断创新以保持竞争优势。
Tesla innovates in the field of electric vehicles. 特斯拉在电动汽车领域进行创新。
Synonym: creates, pioneers, develops, invents, modernizes
Antonym: stagnates, imitates, copies, regresses
词性: v.
