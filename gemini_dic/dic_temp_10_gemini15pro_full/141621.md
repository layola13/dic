optically:/ˈɒptɪkli/ (US) /ˈɑːptɪkli/ (UK)| adj.|光学地，在光学上|Relating to sight or the properties of light.  It describes something that is related to vision, the behavior of light, or the use of optical instruments.|The telescope is optically superior to older models. 这台望远镜在光学性能上优于旧型号。|Synonyms: visually, by sight|Antonyms: acoustically, auditorily|adjective
