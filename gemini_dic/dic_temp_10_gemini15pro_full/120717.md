misalleging:/ˌmɪsəˈledʒɪŋ/
verb
错误地断言；歪曲；诬告
present participle of misallege.  Making a false statement about something.
The lawsuit claims the defendant is misalleging the facts of the case.  该诉讼声称被告歪曲了案件的事实。
He was accused of misalleging the company's financial situation. 他被指控谎报公司的财务状况。
synonyms: misrepresenting, distorting, falsifying, misconstruing, misstating
antonyms: representing accurately, stating correctly, portraying truthfully
