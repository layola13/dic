ligulated:/ˈlɪɡjʊleɪtɪd/
adjective
有小舌的；舌状的
Having a ligule or ligules; strap-shaped.
The ligulated ray flowers of a daisy are white.  雏菊舌状的射线状花是白色的。

The petals of the dandelion are ligulated. 蒲公英的花瓣是舌状的。

synonym: strap-shaped, ligulate
antonym:  (No direct antonym, could consider terms like "tubular" or "non-ligulate" depending on the specific context)
词性: adjective
