clavicornes:/ˌklævɪˈkɔːrniːz/
| n. | 棒角甲虫 |Clavicornes are a group of beetles characterized by their clubbed antennae.  They are a now-obsolete taxonomic group, formerly considered a family (Clavicornia) but now recognized as a polyphyletic assemblage.|  Many museum collections still use the term Clavicornes for organization. | 近义词: club-horned beetles (more descriptive), various specific beetle families (e.g., Staphylinidae, Silphidae, Histeridae depending on the specific beetle) | 反义词: N/A | noun
