overcontentment: /ˌoʊvərˌkənˈtɛntmənt/
| n. |过度满足，过分知足|A state of being excessively content or satisfied, often to the point of complacency or lack of motivation.|He seemed to have drifted into a state of overcontentment and no longer strived for improvement. 他似乎陷入了过度满足的状态，不再努力改进自己了。| 近义词: complacency, satisfaction, smugness |反义词: discontent, dissatisfaction, ambition |词性:名词


overcontentment: /ˌoʊvərˌkənˈtɛntmənt/
| adj. (rarely used) |过度满足的，过分知足的 |Excessively content or satisfied.| While the overcontentment citizens enjoyed their current prosperity, they failed to anticipate the coming economic downturn. 虽然过度满足的公民享受着他们当前的繁荣，但他们未能预料到即将到来的经济衰退。 |近义词: complacent, satisfied, smug |反义词: discontent, dissatisfied, ambitious |词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "contentment" (from "content," ultimately from Latin "contentus," past participle of "continere" meaning "to contain, hold together").
