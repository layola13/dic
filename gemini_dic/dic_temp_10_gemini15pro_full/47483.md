deambulation:/diːˌæmbjəˈleɪʃn/
n.
行走，漫步
the act of walking or moving about
例句：The patient's deambulation was slow and unsteady after the surgery. 病人手术后行走缓慢且不稳。

例句：Deambulation is encouraged after surgery to prevent blood clots.  手术后鼓励下床走动以预防血栓。

近义词：walking, ambulation, strolling, perambulation
反义词：inactivity, immobility, sitting, resting
词性：名词
