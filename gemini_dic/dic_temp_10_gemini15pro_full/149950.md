palliobranchiata: /ˌpælioʊbræŋˈkiːətə/
| n. | 外套鳃类 |A class of brachiopods characterized by having the mantle lobes folded into a spiral to form a pair of brachial supports. |  Palliobranchiata are a group of marine animals with a shell and a lophophore. |  The study focused on the diversity of Palliobranchiata in the fossil record. 这项研究集中于化石记录中外套鳃类的多样性。| synonym: Articulata (in older classifications) | antonym:  N/A | noun
