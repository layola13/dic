musicologists:/ˌmjuːzɪˈkɒlədʒɪsts/ | n. | 音乐学家 |  Experts in the history, theory, and cultural context of music.  |  例句：The musicologists debated the composer's influences. 音乐学家们就这位作曲家的影响进行了辩论。

例句：Many musicologists specialize in a particular period or genre of music. 许多音乐学家专攻某个特定时期或类型的音乐。| 近义词：music scholars, music historians | 反义词: N/A | 词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "musicology" + "-ist".  "Musicology" comes from the Greek "mousikē" (music) + "-logia" (study of).  "-ist" is a suffix denoting a person who practices or is an expert in something.
