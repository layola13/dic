nonelectors:/ˌnɒnɪˈlɛktəz/
|n.| 非选举人；未投票者；无选举权者|People who are eligible to vote but choose not to are called nonelectors. | Those who are not eligible to vote, such as non-citizens or felons (in some jurisdictions), are also considered nonelectors. |例句：A high percentage of nonelectors among young people is a cause for concern. 年轻人中高比例的非选举人数令人担忧。

|例句：The nonelectors often feel their voices are not heard. 非选举人常常觉得他们的声音没有被听到。|近义词：nonvoters, abstainers|反义词：voters, electors|词性: 名词
