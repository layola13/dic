infiltrative:/ɪnˈfɪltrətɪv/| adj.|渗透的，浸润的|Relating to or characterized by infiltration, the process of permeating or spreading throughout something.  Specifically used in medicine to describe the spread of disease or cells into surrounding tissue.|The doctor discovered an infiltrative tumor. 医生发现了一个浸润性肿瘤。|Synonyms: invasive, pervasive, permeating|Antonyms: localized, contained|Adjective
