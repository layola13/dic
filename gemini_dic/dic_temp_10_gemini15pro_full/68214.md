fairey:/ˈfeɪri/ /ˈfɛːri/| n. |仙女，小精灵|A mythical being of folk tales, typically having magical powers and appearing in the form of a small person with wings.|She believed she saw a fairey flitting among the flowers. 她相信她看到一个仙女在花丛中飞舞。|Synonyms: sprite, pixie, elf, nymph|Antonyms: giant, ogre|noun


fairey:/ˈfeɪri/ /ˈfɛːri/| adj. |仙女的，似仙女的|Resembling or characteristic of a fairy; delicate and ethereal.|The dress had a fairey, otherworldly quality. 这件衣服有一种仙女般、超凡脱俗的气质。|Synonyms: magical, enchanting, whimsical|Antonyms: mundane, ordinary|adjective
