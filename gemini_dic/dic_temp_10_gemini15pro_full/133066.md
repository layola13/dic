nonequalization: /ˌnɒnˌiːkwəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 不均衡；非均衡化 | The lack of nonequalization resulted in significant disparities in funding between schools. |  The report highlighted the nonequalization of resources across the country. | 近义词: disparity, imbalance, inequality | 反义词: equalization, parity, balance | 词性: 名词

词源:  Formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to "equalization," which comes from "equalize" + "-ation." "Equalize" comes from "equal" (from Latin "aequalis") + "-ize."
