oleate:/ˈoʊliːeɪt/| n. |油酸盐；油酸酯|A salt or ester of oleic acid.  Oleic acid is a common unsaturated fatty acid, and its salts and esters are often found in soaps, lubricants, and other products. | Sodium oleate is a common component of soap.  油酸钠是肥皂的常见成分。| Potassium oleate is also used in some soaps. 油酸钾也用于一些肥皂中。|近义词:  None (as a specific chemical compound) |反义词: None (as a specific chemical compound)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From oleic + -ate.  Oleic comes from the Latin 'oleum' meaning "oil." The suffix '-ate' is used to form nouns denoting salts or esters of acids.
