helicoidal:/ˌhɛlɪˈkɔɪdəl/| adj.|螺旋形的，螺旋状的|Having the form or shape of a helix; spiral.|The helicoidal staircase wound gracefully to the top floor. 螺旋形的楼梯优雅地盘旋到顶层。|近义词：spiral, helical, corkscrew, winding, twisting|反义词：straight, linear|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *hélicoïdal*, from *hélicoïde* (“helicoid”) + *-al*.  *Hélicoïde* from Ancient Greek *ἕλιξ* (hélix, “spiral, coil, helix”) + *-oïde* (from *-oid*).
