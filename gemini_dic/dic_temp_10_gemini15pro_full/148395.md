overstaffing: /ˌoʊvərˈstæfɪŋ/
noun
人员过剩，人浮于事
The act of providing an organization with more staff than is necessary.
The company is suffering from overstaffing and needs to make some redundancies.  这家公司人浮于事，需要裁员。
