overdrains:/ˈoʊvərˌdreɪnz/ /ˈəʊvəˌdreɪnz/
n.|排水暗管，超额排水|Pipes installed beneath the ground surface to intercept and remove excess groundwater or surface water.  These are often used to improve stability in soil, particularly in road construction or landscaping.|The overdrains prevented waterlogging of the playing field.|近义词：drains, French drains, subdrains|反义词:|名词

overdrain:/ˈoʊvərˌdreɪn/ /ˈəʊvəˌdreɪnz/
v.|过量排水，过度消耗|To drain excessively, to deplete beyond a safe or sustainable limit.  This can refer to physical resources like water or financial resources.|Excessive irrigation can overdrain the soil of essential nutrients. |近义词：deplete, exhaust, overdraw|反义词:replenish, refill|动词
