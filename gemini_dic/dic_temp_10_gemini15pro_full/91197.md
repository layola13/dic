hydraulics: /haɪˈdrɔːlɪks/ | n. | 液压学，水力学 | The study of fluids, especially liquids, and their mechanical properties and applications, including the transmission of power. | Hydraulics is essential for understanding how dams work.  液压学对于理解大坝如何工作至关重要。 |  Hydraulics plays a crucial role in modern machinery. 液压技术在现代机械中起着至关重要的作用。 | synonyms: fluid mechanics, fluid power | antonyms: pneumatics | noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *hydraulica,  from Greek hydrauliká órgana, neuter plural of hydraulikós (“of water pipes, hydraulic”), from hýdōr (“water”) + aulós (“pipe”).
