dishonestly:/dɪsˈɑːnɪstli/ | adv. | 不诚实地 | In a manner that is not honest; deceitfully. | He acted dishonestly in the business deal. 他在商业交易中表现得不诚实。|  She dishonestly claimed she hadn't seen the report. 她不诚实地声称她没有看过这份报告。 | 近义词: deceitfully, fraudulently, falsely | 反义词: honestly, truthfully, honorably | 词性: 副词

词源:  From dishonest + -ly.  Dishonest comes from the Old French *deshonest (modern French déshonnête), from des- (dis-) + honest.
