humanizes:/ˈhjuːmənaɪzɪz/
verb
使人性化;使通情达理;赋予人性
To make something more humane or to make something seem more human.
The company tried to humanize its image by featuring employees in its advertising.  这家公司试图通过在广告中突出员工来使其形象更加人性化。
He humanizes the characters in his novels by giving them relatable flaws. 他通过赋予小说人物容易引起共鸣的缺点来使他们更人性化。
humanize, personalize, civilize
dehumanize, brutalize
