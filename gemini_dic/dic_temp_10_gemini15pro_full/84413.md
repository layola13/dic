hallowedly:/ˈhæləʊɪdli/| adv.|神圣地，庄严地|In a hallowed manner; sacredly.  Referencing something holy or highly respected with reverence.|He hallowedly spoke of his deceased grandfather. 他虔诚地谈起了他已故的祖父。|reverently, piously, sacredly|profanely, irreverently|adverb

词源: From "hallowed" + "-ly." "Hallowed" comes from Old English "hālgod," past participle of "hālgan" (to hallow, consecrate), related to "hālig" (holy).
