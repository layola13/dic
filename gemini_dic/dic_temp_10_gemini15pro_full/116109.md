marvel:/ˈmɑːrvl/ (US), /ˈmɑːvəl/ (UK)| n. v.|奇迹，惊叹；感到惊奇|Something that causes wonder; be filled with wonder or astonishment.|例句：The Grand Canyon is a natural marvel. 大峡谷是一个自然奇观。|近义词：wonder, miracle, phenomenon|反义词：ordinariness, commonplace|词性:名词

marvel:/ˈmɑːrvl/ (US), /ˈmɑːvəl/ (UK)| v.|感到惊奇|Be filled with wonder or astonishment.|例句：I marveled at her talent. 我对她的才华感到惊叹。|近义词：wonder, gape, stare|反义词：disregard, ignore|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *merveile*, from Latin *mirabilia*, neuter plural of *mirabilis* "wonderful," from *mirari* "to wonder at."
