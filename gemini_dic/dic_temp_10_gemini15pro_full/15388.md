backwardnesses:/ˈbækwərdnəsɪz/
n.
落后，迟钝，发展缓慢
The state of being backward or underdeveloped; lack of progress or advancement.
The country's economic backwardnesses hindered its development. 这个国家的经济落后阻碍了它的发展。
backwardness, underdevelopment, retardation
advancement, progress, development
