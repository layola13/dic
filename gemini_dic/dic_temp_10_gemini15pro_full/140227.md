officiator: /əˈfɪʃieɪtər/
Noun
主祭人；主持者；司仪
A person who performs a ceremony, especially a religious one.
The officiator at the wedding was a close friend of the bride. 婚礼的主持人是新娘的密友。
synonyms: celebrant, conductor, master of ceremonies, presider
antonyms: attendee, guest, participant
n.
