insurer:/ɪnˈʃʊrər/ | n. |保险人，承保人 |A person or company that provides insurance. | The insurer will cover the cost of repairs. 保险公司将承担维修费用。| synonyms: underwriter, guarantor, assurer | antonyms: insured, policyholder | noun

insurer:/ɪnˈʃʊrər/ | n. |保险公司，保险商|A company that provides insurance. |  The insurer refused to pay the claim.  保险公司拒绝赔偿。 | synonyms: insurance company, insurance provider, insurance carrier | antonyms: insured, policyholder | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "insure" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Insure" comes from Old French "enseurer" (to make certain, guarantee), from "en-" (in) + "seur" (sure, secure). Ultimately from Latin "securus" (safe, secure).
