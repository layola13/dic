half-orphan:/ˈhæf ˈɔːrfən/ (US) /ˈhɑːf ˈɔːfən/ (UK)| n. |半孤儿|A child who has lost one parent.|例句：The half-orphan received support from his remaining parent and extended family.  这个半孤儿得到了他尚在的父母和大家庭的支持。

例句：Being a half-orphan can be a difficult experience for a young child.  对一个年幼的孩子来说，成为半孤儿可能是一段艰难的经历。|近义词：semi-orphan|反义词: (None -  A direct opposite doesn't exist.  One could argue "child with both parents" but that isn't a single word.) |词性:noun
