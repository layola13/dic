herders:/ˈhɜːrdərz/| n.|牧民|People who take care of a herd of animals, such as sheep, cattle, or goats.|The herders moved their flocks to higher ground for the summer. 牧民们夏季把羊群赶到更高的地方放牧。|
The herders led their cattle across the plains. 牧民们带领他们的牛群穿过平原。|
Synonyms: shepherds, herdsmen, stockmen, pastoralists|Antonyms: farmers (not always a direct opposite, as some farmers also raise livestock)|Noun
