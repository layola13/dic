multiplexer:/ˈmʌltɪˌpleksər/ (US) /ˈmʌltɪˌpleksə(r)/ (UK)| n. |多路复用器，多工器|A device that selects one of several input signals and forwards the selected input into a single line.|The network uses a multiplexer to combine multiple data streams onto a single fiber optic cable.|Synonyms: multiplexor, mux|Antonyms: demultiplexer, demux|noun


multiplexers:/ˈmʌltɪˌpleksərz/ (US) /ˈmʌltɪˌpleksəz/ (UK)| n. |多路复用器，多工器（复数）|Plural form of multiplexer, referring to more than one such device.|The system requires several multiplexers to handle the high volume of data.|Synonyms: multiplexors, muxes|Antonyms: demultiplexers, demuxes|noun
