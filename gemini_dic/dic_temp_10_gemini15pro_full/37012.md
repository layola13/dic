columelliform:/kɒləˈmɛlɪfɔːm/ | adj. | 小柱状的，形似小柱的 | Shaped like a small pillar or columella.  Columella refers to a small column or central axis, particularly in botany and anatomy. | The columelliform teeth of the ancient fish were fascinating.  这些古代鱼类的小柱状牙齿令人着迷。| Near Synonyms: columnar, pillar-shaped, cylindrical | Antonyms: amorphous, shapeless | Adjective
