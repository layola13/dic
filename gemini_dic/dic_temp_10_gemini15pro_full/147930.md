overreligious:/ˌoʊvər rɪˈlɪdʒəs/
adjective
过度虔诚的；过分宗教的
Excessively religious; too concerned with religion.
例句：His overreligious aunt disapproved of dancing. 他那过分虔诚的姑妈不赞成跳舞。

例句：Some people find her overreligious zeal a bit off-putting.  有些人觉得她过分的宗教热情有点令人反感。

近义词：fanatical, bigoted, devout, pious
反义词：irreligious, secular, agnostic, atheistic
词性: adjective
