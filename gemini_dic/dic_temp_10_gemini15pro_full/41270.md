corded:/ˈkɔːrdɪd/| adj.|有绳的，带线的|Having a cord or cords; operated by means of a cord.|例句：She prefers a corded phone because she doesn't have to worry about charging it. 她更喜欢有线电话，因为不用担心充电问题。

例句：The corded drill was more powerful than the cordless one.  这把有线电钻比无线的更有力。|近义词：wired, cable-connected|反义词:cordless, wireless|词性:形容词

词源: From the noun "cord" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives).  来自名词 "cord"（绳索） + "-ed"（构成形容词的后缀）。
