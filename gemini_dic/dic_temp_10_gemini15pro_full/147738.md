overprocrastination:/ˌoʊvərproʊˌkræstɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. |过度拖延|The act of delaying tasks or decisions to the point of causing significant negative consequences, often involving excessive planning, preparation, or perfectionism that prevents actual progress.|  She was paralyzed by overprocrastination, spending weeks researching the "perfect" planner instead of actually starting her project. 她因过度拖延而陷入瘫痪，花了数周时间研究“完美”的计划，而不是真正开始她的项目。

He suffered from overprocrastination, constantly refining his business plan but never actually launching the company.  他患有过度拖延症，不断完善他的商业计划，但从未真正创办公司。


近义词:procrastination, analysis paralysis|反义词:action, decisiveness|词性:noun
