papilio:/pəˈpɪlioʊ/| n. |蝴蝶；凤蝶|A butterfly, specifically of the genus *Papilio*, known for their large size and often brightly colored wings.|例句：The papilio soared through the air, its vibrant wings catching the sunlight.  这只凤蝶在空中翱翔，它鲜艳的翅膀捕捉着阳光。

例句：The entomologist identified the butterfly as a papilio demoleus. 昆虫学家将这只蝴蝶鉴定为柑橘凤蝶。|近义词：butterfly, swallowtail|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
