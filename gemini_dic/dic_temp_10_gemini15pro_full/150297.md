panentheist:/ˌpænˈɛnθiːɪst/| n. |泛神论者|A person who believes in panentheism, the belief that God is both immanent in and transcendent to the universe; that God is everything and everywhere, but also distinct from the created universe.|例句：The panentheist saw the beauty of the divine in every aspect of nature.  泛神论者在自然的各个方面都看到了神圣之美。

例句：She identified as a panentheist, believing God to be both within and beyond the world. 她自认为是泛神论者，相信上帝既在世界之内也在世界之外。|近义词：mystic, transcendentalist|反义词：atheist, materialist|词性:noun
