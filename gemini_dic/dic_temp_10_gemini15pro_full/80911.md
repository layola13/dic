gowdnook: /ˈɡaʊdnʊk/ | n. | 傻瓜，笨蛋 | A foolish or simple person. | He's such a gowdnook, he locked his keys in the car again. 他真是个傻瓜，又把钥匙锁在车里了。 | 近义词：fool, idiot, nitwit, simpleton | 反义词: genius, intellectual | 词性: 名词

词源:  Scottish and Northern English dialect, possibly from "gowd," meaning gold (referring to a fool's gold, something seemingly valuable but worthless), and "nook," meaning corner (suggesting someone hiding in a corner out of foolishness or fear).  The exact etymology is uncertain.
