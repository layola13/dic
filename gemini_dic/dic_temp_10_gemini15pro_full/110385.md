lignone:/ˈlɪɡnoʊn/| n.|木质素，木素|A complex polymer found in the cell walls of many plants, making them rigid and woody.|Lignone is a major component of wood. 木质素是木材的主要成分。|Lignin, wood fiber| Cellulose|noun
lignone:/ˈlɪɡnoʊn/| n.|木质酮|Any of various ketones that are obtained from or related to lignin.|Lignone derivatives can be used in the production of adhesives. 木质酮衍生物可用于生产粘合剂。|Ketone derived from lignin|||noun
