cingle:/ˈsɪŋɡl/| n.| 腰带，带状物，围长|A girth, girdle, or band, especially one used to secure a pack or saddle.|The horse's cingle was too tight.  马的肚带太紧了。|近义词：girth, strap, band|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
cingle:/ˈsɪŋɡl/| v.|  用带子系住，围住|To encircle or bind with a cingle.|He cinkled the saddle onto the horse. 他用肚带把马鞍固定在马上。|近义词：girth, fasten, secure|反义词: unfasten, loosen|词性:verb
