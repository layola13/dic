brandish:/ˈbrændɪʃ/| v. |挥舞，炫耀|To wave or flourish (something, especially a weapon) as a threat or in anger or excitement.  To display something ostentatiously.|The knight brandished his sword. 骑士挥舞着他的剑。|He brandished his new phone in front of his friends. 他在朋友面前炫耀他的新手机。|近义词：wave, flourish, wield, flaunt|反义词：conceal, hide|词性：动词
