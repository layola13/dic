despisements:/dɪˈspaɪzmənts/
|n.|轻蔑，鄙视，蔑视|The act of despising; disdain; scorn.|His despisements of his colleagues were well known. 他对同事的蔑视是众所周知的。|近义词:contempt, disdain, scorn, disregard|反义词:admiration, respect, esteem|词性:名词

despise:/dɪˈspaɪz/
|vt.|鄙视，蔑视，看不起|To feel contempt or a deep repugnance for.|He despised liars and cheats. 他鄙视说谎者和骗子。|近义词:loathe, detest, abhor, hate|反义词:admire, respect, love|词性:动词
