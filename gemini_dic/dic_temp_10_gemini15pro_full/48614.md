defector:/dɪˈfɛktər/ | n. |叛逃者，变节者| A person who leaves their country or cause to join an opposing one. |  例句：The defector was granted asylum in the United States.  这名叛逃者在美国获得了政治庇护。

例句：Several high-ranking officers defected to the rebel army. 几名高级军官叛逃到叛军。

近义词：traitor, renegade, deserter | 反义词：loyalist, patriot | 词性：noun
