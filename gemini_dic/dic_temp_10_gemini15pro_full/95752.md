inconsonance:/ɪnkənˈsɒnəns/ /ɪnkənˈsɑːnəns/ | n. |不和谐，不一致，不协调|lack of harmony or agreement; discord|Her aggressive attitude was in inconsonance with her usual gentle nature. 她咄咄逼人的态度与她平常温柔的性格不符。|Synonyms: discord, disharmony, disagreement, conflict, clash, friction, dissonance|Antonyms: consonance, harmony, agreement, concord, accord|noun

inconsonant:/ɪnkənˈsɒnənt/ /ɪnkənˈsɑːnənt/ | adj. |不和谐的，不一致的，不协调的|not in agreement or harmony; discordant|His actions were inconsonant with his words. 他的行为与他的言辞不一致。|Synonyms: discordant, disharmonious, disagreeing, conflicting, clashing, jarring, incompatible|Antonyms: consonant, harmonious, agreeing, concordant, compatible|adjective
