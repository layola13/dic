foci's:/ˈfoʊsaɪz/| n.|焦点（复数）|The possessive form of "foci," the plural of "focus." It refers to the belonging of multiple focal points.  It indicates ownership or association with multiple points of convergence or concentration. |The foci's locations were carefully marked on the map. 焦点的位置在地图上被仔细地标记出来。|近义词:centers', focal points'|反义词:peripheries'|词性:名词
