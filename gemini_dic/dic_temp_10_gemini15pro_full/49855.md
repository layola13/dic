demote:/dɪˈmoʊt/ (US) /dɪˈməʊt/ (UK)| v. |降级，降职|To lower in rank or position; to relegate to a lower grade or status.|He was demoted from manager to assistant manager. 他被从经理降职为助理经理。| The soldier was demoted for disobeying orders.  这名士兵因不服从命令而被降级。|synonyms: downgrade, relegate, lower, reduce|antonyms: promote, advance, elevate, upgrade|verb
