dehydrate:/ˌdiːˈhaɪdreɪt/
verb
脱水，使脱水
To remove water from something.
The sun can dehydrate the body very quickly. 太阳会很快使身体脱水。
desiccate, dry
hydrate, moisten
v.

dehydrate:/ˌdiːˈhaɪdreɪt/
verb
(使)失去活力；(使)变得枯燥无味
To make something less interesting or less exciting.
Long meetings can really dehydrate you.  冗长的会议真的会让你失去活力。
bore, exhaust
invigorate, energize
v.


dehydrate:/ˌdiːˈhaɪdreɪt/
adjective
脱水的
Having lost water.
Dehydrated hikers need to drink plenty of water. 脱水的徒步旅行者需要喝大量的水。
dried, parched
hydrated, moist
adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (Latin, "away from, down, completely") + hydrate (from Greek hydōr, "water").
