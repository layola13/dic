nonentomological:/ˌnɒnˌɛntəməˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
非昆虫学的
Not related to the study of insects.
Her research interests are diverse and decidedly nonentomological, ranging from astrophysics to medieval literature. 她的研究兴趣广泛，而且明显与昆虫学无关，从天体物理学到中世纪文学都有涉猎。
The museum's latest exhibit covers a wide range of scientific topics, both entomological and nonentomological.  博物馆最新的展览涵盖了广泛的科学主题，包括昆虫学和非昆虫学。
Synonyms: non-insectological
Antonyms: entomological
词性: adjective
