multiform:/ˈmʌltɪfɔːrm/
adjective
多种形式的；多样的
having many forms or appearances.
The artist's multiform sculptures were a testament to her creativity.  这位艺术家的多形态雕塑证明了她的创造力。
The protein has multiform functions in the body.  这种蛋白质在体内有多种功能。

近义词:versatile, varied, diverse, multifaceted, polymorphic
反义词:uniform, homogeneous, consistent, single, unvaried
词性:adjective


词源:
From Latin *multiformis, from multus (“much, many”) + forma (“form, shape”).
