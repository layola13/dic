beguiler:/bɪˈɡaɪlər/ | n. | 欺骗者；诱惑者 | someone who deceives or tricks someone | The beguiler tricked the old woman out of her savings. 这个骗子骗走了老太太的积蓄。| synonyms: deceiver, trickster, charlatan, fraud, swindler | antonyms: honest person, truth-teller | noun

beguile:/bɪˈɡaɪl/ | v. | 欺骗；诱惑；使着迷 | to charm or enchant (someone), sometimes in a deceptive way | Her beauty beguiled him. 她的美貌使他着迷。 | synonyms: charm, enchant, captivate, allure, entice, deceive, trick | antonyms: repel, disgust, disenchant | verb
