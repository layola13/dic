meandered:/miˈændərd/ | v. | 漫步，蜿蜒而行 | followed a winding or indirect course. | The river meandered through the valley.  这条河蜿蜒流过山谷。| 近义词: wandered, rambled, strayed, roamed | 反义词: rushed, hurried, darted | verb

词源: From the Greek "Maiandros," the name of a winding river in Phrygia (now Turkey).
