forgivably:/fərˈɡɪvəbli/
adv.
可原谅地
in a way that can be forgiven
He was forgivably upset after losing his job. 他失业后感到沮丧，这是可以理解的。
近义词：understandably, excusably
反义词：unforgivably, inexcusably
词性：副词
