hoven:/ˈhoʊvən/| n. | 悬停，盘旋 |The hawk was in a hoven above the field.  老鹰在田野上空盘旋。|例句：The helicopter hovered over the landing pad. 直升机在着陆坪上方盘旋。 The drone hovered in the air, capturing breathtaking footage. 无人机悬停在空中，捕捉着令人叹为观止的画面。|近义词：hover, float, drift |反义词: descend, drop, fall |词性:名词

词源: "Hoven" is derived from the past participle of the verb "heave," which means to lift or raise something heavy. Over time, "hoven" developed the specific meaning of remaining suspended in the air.
