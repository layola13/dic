deciphering:/dɪˈsaɪfərɪŋ/
verb
解密，破译
the act of figuring out the meaning of something difficult to understand, like a code or puzzle.
Historians are deciphering ancient scrolls. 历史学家们正在破译古代卷轴。
synonyms: decoding, interpreting, unraveling
antonyms: encoding, encrypting, concealing
verb

deciphering:/dɪˈsaɪfərɪŋ/
noun
解密，破译
the act of figuring out the meaning of something difficult to understand, like a code or puzzle.
The deciphering of the enemy's code was crucial to the war effort.  对敌人密码的破译对战争的努力至关重要。
synonyms: decoding, interpretation, unraveling
antonyms: encoding, encryption, concealment
