interabsorption:/ˌɪntərəbˈsɔːrpʃən/ | n. |相互吸收; 互相吸收 |The interabsorption of two liquids can create a new substance. 两种液体的相互吸收可以创造一种新物质。| The interabsorption of gases in the atmosphere affects weather patterns. 大气中气体的相互吸收会影响天气模式。| 近义词:  absorption, assimilation | 反义词: desorption, release | 词性: 名词

词源: inter- (prefix meaning "between, among, mutually") + absorption (noun from Latin absorbere "to swallow up")
