nonconsignment:/ˌnɒnkənˈsaɪnmənt/ |n.|非寄售，非委托销售|A system of selling goods where the seller retains ownership until the goods are sold.  The retailer does not pay for the goods until they are sold to the final customer. |The boutique operates on a nonconsignment basis, meaning they purchase all inventory outright.| 近义词: outright purchase, direct purchase |反义词: consignment |词性:名词
