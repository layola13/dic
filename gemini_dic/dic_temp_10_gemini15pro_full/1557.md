achievements: /əˈtʃiːvmənts/
n.
成就；成绩；功绩
Something that has been accomplished, especially by hard work, skill, or courage.
Her greatest achievement was climbing Mount Everest. 她最大的成就是攀登珠穆朗玛峰。

He felt a sense of achievement after finishing the marathon. 完成马拉松后，他有一种成就感。

近义词: accomplishments, successes, triumphs, feats
反义词: failures, setbacks, defeats
词性: 名词(plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "achever" (to finish, complete)  < Vulgar Latin *accapāre (to come to a head) < Latin ad (to) + caput (head). The noun form "achievement" arose later in English.
