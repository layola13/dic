heraldry: /ˈherəldri/ (US); /ˈhɛrəldri/ (UK) | n. | 纹章学，纹章制度 | The system or practice of devising, granting, displaying, describing, and recording coats of arms and other heraldic bearings, as well as the study of this practice as a historical, genealogical, and artistic pursuit.  | The college of heraldry is responsible for granting new coats of arms. 纹章院负责授予新的纹章。

The museum has a fascinating exhibit on medieval heraldry.  博物馆有一个关于中世纪纹章学的引人入胜的展览。| 近义词: blazonry, armory | 反义词:  (No direct antonym;  perhaps "anonymity" in a metaphorical sense) | 词性: Noun
