creux:/krø/| adjective|空的，凹陷的，中空的|A hollow space, especially in a wall; an indentation or cavity. creux:/krø/| noun|凹陷处，空洞|A hollow space or cavity.|The archaeologist discovered a creux in the ancient wall, possibly used for storage. 考古学家在古墙上发现了一个凹陷处，可能是用来储存东西的。|Synonyms: hollow, cavity, indentation|Antonyms:  protuberance, bulge|noun
