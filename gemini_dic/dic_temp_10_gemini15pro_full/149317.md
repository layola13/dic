pacated:/ˈpeɪkeɪtɪd/
adjective
平静的，安抚的
brought to a state of peace or quiet; tranquil.
例句：The pacated crowd listened attentively to the speaker. 平静下来的人群专心听着演讲者讲话。

例句：The baby, finally pacated with a bottle, drifted off to sleep.  婴儿终于被奶瓶安抚平静，渐渐睡着了。
近义词：calmed, soothed, appeased, tranquilized
反义词：agitated, disturbed, restless, turbulent
词性: adjective
