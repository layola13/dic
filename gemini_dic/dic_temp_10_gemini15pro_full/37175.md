come to your senses:/kʌm tə jɔːr ˈsɛnsɪz/ |phrase|  清醒过来；恢复理智；|To begin thinking clearly and rationally again after a period of confusion or irrationality.| He finally came to his senses and realized he was making a huge mistake. 他终于醒悟过来，意识到自己犯了一个巨大的错误。|例句：She needs to come to her senses and stop spending so much money. 她需要清醒过来，别再花这么多钱了。

例句：It took him a while, but he eventually came to his senses and apologized.  他花了一段时间，但最终还是醒悟过来并道歉了。

近义词： regain consciousness, become aware, wise up, see the light|反义词：lose one's mind, go crazy, become irrational|词性：phrase
