agreeable-to:/əˈɡriːəbl tuː/
adjective
可以接受的；同意的；适合的
willing to agree or consent to something; acceptable to
例句：The terms of the contract were agreeable to both parties.  合同条款双方都能接受。

例句：Find a time agreeable to everyone for the meeting. 找个大家都能接受的开会时间。

近义词：acceptable, amenable, consenting, compliant
反义词：unacceptable, objectionable, disagreeable
词性：adjective

词源：From Middle English *agreable, from Old French agreable, from agreer (“to please, agree”), from Late Latin aggrātiāre, from Latin ad (“to”) + grātia (“grace, favor”).

注意事项:
Agreeable-to is less common than "agreeable with" when referring to people.  For example, "The plan is agreeable with me" is more common than "The plan is agreeable to me." However, "agreeable to" is used when referring to things like terms, conditions, or proposals.
