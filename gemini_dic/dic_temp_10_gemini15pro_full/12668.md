assembly: /əˈsɛmbli/ /əˈsɛmbli/ | n. | 集会，会议；装配，组装 | A group of people gathered together in one place for a common purpose. The process of putting parts together to make a complete product. | The school assembly is held every Monday morning. 学校集会每周一早上举行。

The assembly of the new furniture took several hours.  新家具的组装花了几个小时。 | synonyms: gathering, meeting, congregation, construction, fitting | antonyms: dispersal, separation, dismantling, disassembly | noun

assembly: /əˈsɛmbli/ /əˈsɛmbli/ | n. | (尤指作为立法机构的)议会，会议 | A group of people, especially one that meets regularly for a particular purpose, such as making laws. | The National Assembly voted in favor of the new law.  国民议会投票赞成新法律。

The student assembly discussed the new school rules. 学生议会讨论了新的校规。 | synonyms: parliament, congress, legislature, council | antonyms: N/A | noun
