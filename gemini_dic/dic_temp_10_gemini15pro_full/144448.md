outswindled:/ˌaʊtˈswɪnd(ə)ld/
verb
被骗得更多，被彻底诈骗
cheated or defrauded more thoroughly than someone else or in a more extensive way than usual.  It implies being tricked out of something, often money or possessions, through deceit or cunning.
例句：The con artist outswindled his victims, leaving them penniless. 骗子彻底骗了他的受害者，让他们身无分文。

例句：Not only did they lose their savings, but they were also outswindled of their family heirlooms. 他们不仅失去了积蓄，还被骗走了祖传的传家宝。
近义词：defrauded, cheated, conned, scammed, ripped off
反义词：rewarded, compensated, enriched
