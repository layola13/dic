decentralise: /diːˈsɛntrəlaɪz/ (UK) /ˌdiːˈsɛntrəˌlaɪz/ (US) | v. | 分散，权力下放 | To distribute the administrative powers or functions of (a central authority) over a less concentrated area; to reorganize (a government, industry, etc.) so as to transfer power from central to local authorities. | The company decided to decentralise its operations and give more autonomy to regional branches.  这家公司决定分散运营，给予地区分支机构更多的自主权。 | The government is planning to decentralise the education system. 政府正计划将教育系统权力下放。 | synonyms: distribute, disperse, devolve, delegate | antonyms: centralise, consolidate | verb

decentralization: /diːˌsɛntrəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ (UK) /ˌdiːˌsɛntrəˌlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ (US)| n. | 分散化，权力下放 | The act or process of decentralising; the state of being decentralised. | The decentralization of power led to greater regional autonomy. 权力下放导致了更大的区域自治。 | The company is undergoing a period of decentralization. 公司正在经历一个权力下放的时期。 | synonyms: distribution, dispersal, devolution, delegation | antonyms: centralization, consolidation | noun
