overelaborating: /ˌoʊvərɪˈlæbəreɪtɪŋ/
verb
过度阐述；过于详细地说明
present participle of overelaborate: elaborating something too much; adding more detail than is necessary.
He tends to overelaborate his points, making his presentations tedious. 他倾向于过度阐述自己的观点，使他的演讲冗长乏味。
Synonyms: overexplaining, belaboring, dwelling on,  overdoing
Antonyms: simplifying, summarizing, condensing
