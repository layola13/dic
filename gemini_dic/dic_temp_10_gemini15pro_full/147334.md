overmournfully: /ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrnfəli/ | adv. | 过度悲伤地，悲痛欲绝地 |  In a way that expresses excessive or exaggerated grief.  It combines "over-" (meaning excessively) with "mournfully" (sadly, with grief). | She wept overmournfully at the funeral, drawing attention to herself. 她在葬礼上哭得过度悲伤，引起了人们的注意。|  He sighed overmournfully, as if the weight of the world were on his shoulders.  他悲痛欲绝地叹了口气，仿佛肩负着整个世界的重担。 | 近义词: excessively sadly, very sorrowfully, too mournfully | 反义词: joyfully, happily | 词性: 副词
词源: From "over-" (Old English *ofer) + "mournful" (from "mourn," from Old English murnan "to grieve, mourn") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).
