paraconic:/ˌpærəˈkɒnɪk/ | adj. | 简洁的，寡言少语的 |  Reluctant to speak, especially about one's feelings. Characterized by few words; terse or concise. | He gave a paraconic reply, simply nodding his head. 他只是点头，简短地回答。| 近义词: laconic, taciturn, reticent, concise, brief | 反义词: loquacious, garrulous, verbose, prolix | 词性: 形容词

paraconic:/ˌpærəˈkɒnɪk/ | n. | 寡言少语的人 | A person who is reluctant to speak, especially about their feelings. |  He was a paraconic by nature, rarely revealing his inner thoughts. 他天生寡言少语，很少表露自己的内心想法。 | 近义词: stoic | 反义词: chatterbox | 词性: 名词
