attracting:/əˈtræktɪŋ/
|v.|吸引；引起…的兴趣；引起；招来|present participle of attract|The colorful flowers are attracting bees. 这些色彩鲜艳的花朵正在吸引蜜蜂。|Synonyms: charming, alluring, captivating, enticing, drawing, fascinating|Antonyms: repelling, deterring, disgusting|Verb

attract:/əˈtrækt/
|v.|吸引；引起…的兴趣；引起；招来|to cause to come to a place or participate in a particular activity by offering something of interest, favorable conditions, or opportunities|The museum attracts thousands of visitors each year.  这个博物馆每年吸引成千上万的游客。|Synonyms:  allure,  captivate,  draw,  entice,  fascinate|Antonyms: repel, deter|Verb


Etymology:
From Latin attractus, past participle of attrahere "to draw to, attract," from ad- "to" + trahere "to draw, pull."
