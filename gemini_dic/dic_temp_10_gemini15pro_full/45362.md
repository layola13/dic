cuneated:/ˈkjuːnieɪtɪd/
adjective
楔形的；楔状的
Having the shape of a wedge; tapering to a thin edge.
The cuneated script of ancient Sumeria is one of the earliest known forms of writing.  苏美尔楔形文字是最早的文字形式之一。
The artist used a cuneated tool to carve the intricate design into the wood.  艺术家用楔形工具在木头上雕刻复杂的图案。
Synonyms: wedge-shaped, tapered, triangular
Antonyms: rounded, blunt, square
词性:adjective
