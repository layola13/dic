newsrooms:/ˈnjuːzruːmz/ /ˈnuːzruːmz/
n.
新闻编辑室；新闻中心
The place where journalists and editors work to produce news.  新闻工作者和编辑制作新闻的地方。
Reporters were frantically typing in the newsrooms. 记者们在新闻编辑室里疯狂地打字。
Newsrooms around the world are adapting to the digital age. 全世界的新闻编辑室都在适应数字时代。
近义词：news desks, editorial offices
反义词: None
词性: Noun
