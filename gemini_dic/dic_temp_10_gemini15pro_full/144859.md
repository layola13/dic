over-positive:/ˌoʊvərˈpɒzɪtɪv/ | adj. | 过分积极的；过于乐观的 | Excessively positive; unrealistically optimistic.  Characterized by excessive optimism and confidence, often to the point of being unrealistic or impractical. | He has an over-positive attitude towards the project, ignoring the potential risks. 他对这个项目的态度过于乐观，忽略了潜在的风险。|  She's over-positive about her chances of winning the lottery. 她过于乐观地估计自己中彩票的机会。 | 近义词: over-optimistic, excessively positive, unrealistic, sanguine | 反义词: pessimistic, negative, realistic, cautious | 词性: adjective
