duodecimfid: /ˌduːoʊˈdɛsɪmfɪd/ | adj. | 十二裂的，十二深裂的 | Divided into twelve segments or lobes, cleft to about two thirds of the way to the midrib. | The duodecimfid leaves of the fig tree are distinctive. 无花果树的十二裂叶子很独特。 |  近义词: multifid, dissected | 反义词: entire, undivided | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *duodecimfidus, from duodecim (“twelve”) + -fidus (from findō (“to split, cleave”)).
