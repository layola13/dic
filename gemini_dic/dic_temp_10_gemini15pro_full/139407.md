observed: /əbˈzɜːrvd/ (UK) /əbˈzɜːrvd/ (US) | v. | 观察；注意到；遵守；评论 |  The scientist observed the behavior of the animals. 科学家观察了动物的行为。|  例句：She observed that he was wearing a new suit. 她注意到他穿了一套新西装。| 近义词：watched, noticed, monitored, followed, complied with, remarked | 反义词：ignored, disregarded, overlooked | 词性: 动词 (past participle)

observed: /əbˈzɜːrvd/ (UK) /əbˈzɜːrvd/ (US) | adj. | 观察到的；遵守的 | The observed data was analyzed thoroughly.  观察到的数据被彻底分析了。| 例句：The observed increase in temperature is concerning.  观察到的温度升高令人担忧。 | 近义词：seen, witnessed, noted, recorded | 反义词：unseen, unnoticed, unrecorded | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From Late Middle English *observiden, from Latin observāre (“to watch, pay attention to, heed, comply with”), from ob- (“toward”) + servāre (“to keep, save, preserve, watch”).
