collaborated: /kəˈlæbəreɪtɪd/
verb
合作，协作
worked together on a project
例句：We collaborated on a research project. 我们在一个研究项目上合作。

例句：The two companies collaborated to develop the new software.  两家公司合作开发这款新软件。

近义词：cooperated, partnered, teamed up, worked together
反义词：acted alone, worked independently
词性: verb (past tense of collaborate)


collaborate: /kəˈlæbəreɪt/
verb
合作，协作
to work jointly with others or together especially in an intellectual endeavor
例句：The two scientists collaborated on a groundbreaking study. 两位科学家合作进行了一项开创性的研究。

例句：She collaborated with him on several successful projects. 她和他合作完成了好几个成功的项目。


近义词：cooperate, partner, team up, work together
反义词：act alone, work independently
词性: verb

词源:
From Late Latin collaboratus, past participle of collaborare “to work together,” from Latin com- “together” + laborare “to work.”
