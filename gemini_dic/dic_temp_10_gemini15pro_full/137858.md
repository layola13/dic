nonviolent:/ˌnɒnˈvaɪələnt/ /ˌnɑːnˈvaɪələnt/| adj.|非暴力的，不使用暴力的|Not involving or using violence; peaceful.|例句：They staged a nonviolent protest outside the government building. 他们在政府大楼外举行了一场非暴力抗议。|近义词：peaceful, pacifistic, non-aggressive|反义词：violent, aggressive, forceful|词性:形容词
Etymology: non- (prefix meaning "not") + violent (from Latin violentus, meaning "characterized by great force")
