dumping:/ˈdʌmpɪŋ/ | n. | 倾销；抛售；倾倒（垃圾等） | The act of selling goods in a foreign market at a price lower than the price charged in the home market, or below the cost of production, usually considered unfair competition.  |  The government accused the company of dumping steel on the international market. 政府指责该公司在国际市场上倾销钢铁。 | synonyms: unloading, discarding, jettisoning | antonyms: hoarding, accumulating | noun

dumping:/ˈdʌmpɪŋ/ | n. |  倾倒；丢弃 | The act of discarding something in a careless or inappropriate manner.  |  The illegal dumping of toxic waste is a serious environmental problem.  非法倾倒有毒废物是一个严重的环境问题。 | synonyms: discarding, disposing, littering | antonyms: collecting, gathering, hoarding | noun
