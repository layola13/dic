ensembles:/ɑːnˈsɑːmblz/ (US), /ɒnˈsɒmblz/ (UK)
|n.|乐团，合唱团，全体，剧团|A group of musicians, actors, or dancers who perform together.|The string ensemble played beautifully.  弦乐团演奏得非常优美。|synonyms: group, band, troupe, company, collection, aggregation|antonyms: individual, soloist|noun

|n.|全體，整體|The whole or complete set of something.|The data was analyzed for the entire ensemble of measurements.  对整个测量集合的数据进行了分析。|synonyms: whole, totality, entirety, aggregate, sum total|antonyms: part, portion, fragment|noun
