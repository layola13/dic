crocoite:/ˈkroʊkoʊaɪt/| n.|铬铅矿|A bright red mineral consisting of lead chromate (PbCrO4).  It is a secondary mineral formed by the oxidation of lead-bearing minerals in the presence of chromium-bearing solutions.|The museum's collection includes a stunning specimen of crocoite.|近义词:lead chromate, red lead ore|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific mineral)|词性:noun
