corvine:/ˈkɔːrvaɪn/ | adj. | 乌鸦的；乌鸦般的；黑色的 | Relating to or resembling a crow or raven, especially in color or voice. | The old house had a distinctly corvine atmosphere, dark and foreboding.  那栋老房子有一种明显的乌鸦般的氛围，黑暗而充满预兆。|Synonyms: ravenlike, crowlike, black, dark, ominous | Antonyms: bright, colorful, cheerful | Adjective

corvine:/ˈkɔːrvaɪn/ | n. | 鸦科鸟类 | A bird of the crow family (Corvidae). | The corvine perched on the branch, surveying its surroundings. 这只鸦科鸟类栖息在树枝上，观察着周围的环境。| Synonyms: crow, raven, rook, jackdaw | Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a biological family) | Noun
