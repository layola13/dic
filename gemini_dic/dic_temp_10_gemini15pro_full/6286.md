amoroso:/ˌæmɔːˈroʊsoʊ/ /ˌaməˈrəʊsəʊ/
adjective
充满爱意的；柔情的
Showing or expressing love or affection.
例句：He played the piece with an amoroso touch. 他以充满爱意的触感演奏了这首曲子。

例句：She gave him an amoroso glance. 她给了他一个充满爱意的眼神。

近义词：loving, affectionate, tender, passionate
反义词：cold, unfeeling, indifferent
词性: adjective

词源: Italian, from *amoroso, from Vulgar Latin *amorōsus, from Latin amor love.
