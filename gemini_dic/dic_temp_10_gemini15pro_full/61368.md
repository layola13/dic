embiotocoid:/ɛmˌbaɪəˈtoʊkɔɪd/
noun
胎生鲈鱼；胎生鲈鱼科的鱼
A fish belonging to the family Embiotocidae, characterized by internal fertilization and the bearing of live young.
The embiotocoid surfperch is a fascinating example of a fish that gives birth to live young.  胎生鲈鱼是一种迷人的鱼类，它们会产下幼鱼。
The scientist studied the reproductive strategies of various embiotocoid species.  这位科学家研究了各种胎生鲈鱼的繁殖策略。
近义词: surfperch (more general term), viviparous fish
反义词: oviparous fish
