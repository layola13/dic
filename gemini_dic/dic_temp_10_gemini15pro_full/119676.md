midgard: /ˈmɪdɡɑːrd/ | n. | 中庭，人间 | In Norse mythology, Midgard is the realm of humans, located between Asgard, the realm of the gods, and Niflheim, the realm of the dead.  It is often depicted as a flat disc surrounded by a vast ocean. |  Midgard is protected from the giants of Jotunheim by a great wall built from the eyebrows of Ymir. 中庭由用尤弥尔眉毛建造的巨大城墙保护，免受约顿海姆巨人的侵害。 |  The Vikings believed they lived in Midgard. 维京人相信他们生活在中庭。 | synonyms: Earth, world, human realm | antonyms: Asgard, Niflheim, Hel | noun
