doctrinaire:/ˌdɑːktrɪˈnɛər/ | adj. n. |教条主义的；空谈理论的；教条主义者；空谈家| rigidly adhering to theories or doctrines, especially without regard for practical considerations; a person who adheres rigidly to theories or doctrines |  He has a doctrinaire approach to politics. 他对政治持教条主义的态度。| synonyms: dogmatic, rigid, inflexible, unyielding, uncompromising, opinionated | antonyms: pragmatic, practical, flexible, open-minded | adjective, noun
