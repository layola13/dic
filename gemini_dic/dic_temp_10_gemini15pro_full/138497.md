noviciates:/noʊˈvɪʃiɪts/ /nəˈvɪʃiɪts/
n.
见习期;见习僧;见习修女
The period during which a novice of a religious order lives in a monastery or convent before taking vows.  A person who is new to a religious order and is preparing to take vows; a novice.
The young doctor is still in her noviciate.  这位年轻的医生仍在见习期。
They spent a year in the noviciate. 他们花了一年时间在见习期。
synonyms: apprenticeship, probation, traineeship, internship, novitiate, initiation
antonyms: experience, expertise, mastery
noun
