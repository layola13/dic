daysman:/ˈdeɪzmən/| n.|仲裁人，调解人|An impartial person chosen to settle a dispute between parties; an arbitrator or mediator.|Job 9:33: "Neither is there any daysman betwixt us, that might lay his hand upon us both."  约伯记 9:33：“在我们中间也没有仲裁人，可以按手在我们两人身上。”|Synonyms: arbitrator, mediator, umpire, referee|Antonyms: disputant, litigant|noun
