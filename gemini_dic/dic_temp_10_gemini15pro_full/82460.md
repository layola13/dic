grumpier:/ˈɡrʌmpɪər/ (US), /ˈɡrʌmpiə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|脾气更坏的，更暴躁的|The comparative form of "grumpy," meaning more grumpy. It describes someone who is in a bad mood or easily annoyed, to a greater degree than they usually are. |He was much grumpier than usual this morning. 他今天早上比平时脾气暴躁得多。|She gets grumpier the less she sleeps. 她睡得越少，脾气就越坏。|Synonyms: more irritable, more bad-tempered, crankier, more churlish|Antonyms: happier, cheerier, more pleasant, more agreeable|adjective
