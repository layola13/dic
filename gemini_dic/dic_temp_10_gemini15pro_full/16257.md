bantling:/ˈbæntlɪŋ/
n.
child, youngster, offspring
A young child; an infant.  Often used humorously or deprecatingly.
The farmer looked fondly at his newest bantling, a wobbly calf.  农夫深情地望着他最新的孩子，一头摇摇晃晃的小牛犊。
synonyms: baby, infant, toddler, child, kid, youngster, offspring, progeny
antonyms: adult, elder, senior
noun
