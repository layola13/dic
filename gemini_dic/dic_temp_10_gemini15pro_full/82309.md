grouselike:/ˈɡraʊslaɪk/| adj.|类似松鸡的，松鸡似的|Resembling or characteristic of a grouse (a type of game bird).|The bird had a grouselike appearance with its mottled brown feathers.  这只鸟有着类似松鸡的外观，羽毛呈斑驳的棕色。|The bird's plumage and behavior were distinctly grouselike. 这只鸟的羽毛和行为明显像松鸡。|Synonyms:  grouse-like, like a grouse|Antonyms: unlike a grouse|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From grouse +‎ -like.
