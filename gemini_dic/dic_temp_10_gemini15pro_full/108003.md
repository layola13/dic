lassoed:/ˈlæsuːd/ /læˈsud/
verb
套住（用套索）; 
past tense and past participle of lasso.  Meaning "to catch with a lasso."
The cowboy lassoed the calf. 牛仔用套索套住了小牛。
近义词:roped, caught, snared
反义词:released, freed
词性:verb
