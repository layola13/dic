half-republican:/ˌhæf rɪˈpʌblɪkən/
|adj.|
|半共和主义的；部分共和主义的|
|Describing something or someone that only partially adheres to the principles or policies of a republican form of government or a Republican party.|
|The governor's policies were seen as half-republican, incorporating some democratic ideals.|州长的政策被视为半共和主义的，融合了一些民主理念。

|His half-republican stance alienated both staunch Republicans and Democrats.|他半共和主义的立场疏远了坚定的共和党人和民主党人。
|partly republican|
|fully republican, completely democratic|
|adjective|
