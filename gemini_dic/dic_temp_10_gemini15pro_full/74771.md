freeze-frames:/ˈfriːz freɪmz/ | n. | 静止帧; 冻结画面 |A freeze-frame is a single frame of a film or video recording that is displayed for a period of time, appearing as a still image. |The director used freeze-frames to emphasize the character's emotions. 导演使用静止帧来强调角色的情感。| synonyms: still image, still frame, stopped image | antonyms: motion picture, moving image | noun

freeze-frame:/ˈfriːz freɪm/ | v. |  (使)定格；(使)冻结画面 | To freeze-frame is to pause a film or video recording on a single frame, creating a still image. |He freeze-framed the video to get a better look at the suspect's face. 他暂停了视频，以便更清楚地看到嫌疑人的脸。| synonyms: pause, stop, hold | antonyms: play, continue, resume | verb
