charm's:/tʃɑːrmz/
n.|魅力，吸引力（charm的名词所有格）|The necklace held a special charm's power. 这条项链拥有特殊的魅力。
English Explanation: This is the possessive form of the noun "charm," indicating that something belongs to or is associated with a charm.  It signifies the power, influence, or allure emanating from the charm itself.
Example: The charm's effect was immediate. 护身符的效果立竿见影。
Synonyms: magic, allure, appeal, attractiveness
Antonyms: repulsiveness, unpleasantness, unattractiveness
Part of Speech: Noun (possessive)
