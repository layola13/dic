dictatorialness:/ˌdɪktəˈtɔːriəlnəs/ | n. |独裁;专制;专横 |The state or quality of being dictatorial; imperiousness or overbearingness. |His dictatorialness alienated his colleagues. 他的专横疏远了他的同事们。| 近义词:authoritarianism, tyranny, despotism, autocracy | 反义词:democracy, egalitarianism | 词性:名词

dictatorial:/ˌdɪktəˈtɔːriəl/ | adj. |独裁的;专制的;专横的 |Of, relating to, or characteristic of a dictator or a dictatorship; imperious or overbearing. |He has a dictatorial management style. 他的管理风格很专横。| 近义词:autocratic, tyrannical, despotic, imperious | 反义词:democratic, egalitarian | 词性:形容词

dictator:/ˈdɪkteɪtər/ | n. |独裁者;专制者 |A ruler with total power over a country, typically one who has obtained power by force. |He ruled the country as a dictator for over 20 years. 他以独裁者的身份统治了这个国家20多年。| 近义词:autocrat, tyrant, despot | 反义词:democrat | 词性:名词
