lithocarp:/ˈlɪθəˌkɑːrp/ | n. | 核果；硬壳果 |A fruit with a stony pericarp, like a peach or cherry. | Example 1: The peach is a classic example of a lithocarp, with its hard pit surrounding the seed. 桃子是核果的典型例子，它坚硬的果核包裹着种子。

Example 2:  The ancient Greeks cultivated various lithocarps, including olives and almonds. 古希腊人种植各种核果，包括橄榄和杏仁。 |Synonym: drupe, stone fruit | Antonym:  berry, pome | noun
