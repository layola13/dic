marveled:/ˈmɑːrvəld/ | v. |感到惊奇，大为赞叹| To feel or express great surprise or admiration.| She marveled at the beauty of the sunset. 她对日落的美丽感到惊叹。|  He marveled at her quick thinking. 他对她的思维敏捷感到惊叹。| synonyms: wondered, admired,  was amazed | antonyms:  disregarded, ignored | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *marvelen*, from Old French *merveiller*, from Late Latin *mirābiliāre* (“to admire”), from Latin *mīrābilis* (“wonderful, admirable”), from *mīror* (“to wonder at”).
