deynte:/deɪnt/| adj.|美味的；可口的；挑剔的|Something "deynte" is considered delicate and pleasing, especially in taste or appearance. It can also refer to someone who is fastidious or picky about their food.|The deynte pastries were beautifully decorated and tasted divine.  这些美味的糕点装饰精美，味道神圣。|近义词：delicious, exquisite, dainty, fastidious|反义词：bland, unappetizing, coarse|词性:形容词
词源: Middle English: from Old French *daintié, from daint ‘delight, pleasure’, based on Latin dignitas ‘worthiness’.
