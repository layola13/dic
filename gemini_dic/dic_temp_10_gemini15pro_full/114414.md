make one's hair stand on end:/meɪk wʌnz heər stænd ɑn ɛnd/ /meɪk wʌnz heə stænd ɒn end/ | idiom | 令人毛骨悚然；使人恐惧 | To cause someone to feel extreme fear or terror.  This idiom describes a physical sensation often associated with fear. | The horror movie made my hair stand on end.  这部恐怖电影让我毛骨悚然。| synonyms: terrify, frighten, horrify, scare, petrify | antonyms: calm, soothe, comfort, reassure | idiom
