dedesignated:/diːˈdɛzɪɡneɪtɪd/
|adj.|不再指定的，被取消指定的|Removed from a designated status or assignment.  No longer officially assigned a specific role or purpose.|The area was dedesignated as a national park.  该区域不再被指定为国家公园。|Former, ex-|Designated, assigned|adjective

dedesignate:/diːˈdɛzɪɡneɪt/
|v.|取消指定，撤销指定|To remove from a designated status or assignment. To officially remove a specific role or purpose.|The government dedesignated the building as a historical landmark. 政府取消了该建筑物作为历史地标的指定。|Remove, revoke, rescind|Designate, appoint|verb
