capnomancy:/ˈkæpnəmænsi/| n. |占卜术（通过观察烟雾）|Divination by interpreting the smoke of a sacrifice.|The ancient Greeks practiced capnomancy to predict the outcome of battles. 古希腊人通过观察祭祀烟雾来预测战争结果。|例句：The shaman performed capnomancy to determine the will of the gods.  萨满通过烟雾占卜来确定神明的旨意。

例句：Capnomancy was a popular form of divination in ancient times. 在古代，烟雾占卜是一种流行的占卜形式。|近义词：divination, soothsaying, augury|反义词: None|词性: noun
