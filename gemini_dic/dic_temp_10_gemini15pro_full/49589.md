demeriting: /diːˈmɛrɪtɪŋ/
verb
给予缺点；记过；贬低
The act of assigning a demerit or fault; the act of detracting from merit or worth.
He is demeriting the employee for being late. 他因为员工迟到而对其进行处罚。
Synonyms: penalizing, faulting, criticizing, censuring
Antonyms: praising, commending, rewarding, extolling
词性: 动词 (verb)


demerit: /diːˈmɛrɪt/
noun
缺点；过失；记过
A mark awarded against someone for a fault or offense.
His driving record has several demerits. 他的驾驶记录上有好几项记过。
Synonyms: fault, flaw, defect, shortcoming
Antonyms: merit, virtue, strength, advantage
词性: 名词 (noun)


词源:  From the Latin "demereri" meaning "to deserve ill of," from "de-" (down, away from) + "mereri" (to deserve).
