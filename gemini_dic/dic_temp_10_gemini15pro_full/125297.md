multifunctionality:/ˌmʌltiˌfʌŋkʃəˈnæləti/ | n. |多功能性，多用途性 | The ability of something to perform multiple functions or serve multiple purposes. |  This new smartphone boasts multifunctionality, acting as a phone, camera, and GPS device.  这款新的智能手机以多功能性著称，可以作为电话、相机和GPS设备。 | versatility, adaptability |  lack of specialization, single-purpose | noun
