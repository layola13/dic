palpifer:/ˈpælpɪfər/| n. |下颚须|A small, often bristly or hairy, lobe at the base of the maxillae in some insects, especially moths and butterflies, that supports the maxillary palps.  It's part of the insect's mouthparts and aids in sensory functions and food manipulation.|The palpifer of this moth species is unusually large.  这只蛾子的下颚须异常大。|Synonym: maxillary palp base|Antonym: N/A|Noun
