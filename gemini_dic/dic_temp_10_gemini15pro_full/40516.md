controllable:/kənˈtroʊləbl/ (US) /kənˈtrəʊləbl/ (UK)| adj.|可控制的；可支配的|Able to be controlled or managed.|例句：The temperature in the room is controllable by thermostat. 房间里的温度可以用恒温器控制。|例句：Her anger was barely controllable. 她的愤怒几乎无法控制。|近义词：manageable, governable, regulable|反义词：uncontrollable, unmanageable, intractable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From control + -able, ultimately from Latin contrarotulus "counter-roll".
