nationally:/ˈnæʃənəli/ | adv. | 全国地，在全国范围内 | In a way that involves all parts of a nation. | The competition is broadcast nationally. 这场比赛在全国范围内播出。| synonym: countrywide, nationwide | antonym: locally, regionally | adverb

nationally:/ˈnæʃənəli/ | adv. | 举国地，全国性地 | Throughout the whole nation; on a national scale. | The issue was debated nationally.  这个问题在全国范围内进行了辩论。 | synonym: across the country, throughout the nation | antonym: locally, regionally | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From nation + -al + -ly.  "Nation" comes from Old French "nacion" and ultimately from Latin "natio" (birth, race, nation), from "nat-" (born), the past participle stem of "nasci" (to be born).
