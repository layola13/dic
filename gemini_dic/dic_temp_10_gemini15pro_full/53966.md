discreeter:/dɪˈskriːtər/
adjective
更谨慎的，更小心的
more careful and showing good judgment about what one says or does, so as not to cause embarrassment or difficulty
She was always very discreet about her personal life. 她对自己的私生活总是非常谨慎。
近义词: careful, cautious, prudent, tactful
反义词: indiscreet, careless, imprudent
词性: adjective

discreet:/dɪˈskriːt/
adjective
谨慎的，小心的
careful and showing good judgment about what one says or does, so as not to cause embarrassment or difficulty
He is very discreet in his business dealings. 他在生意上很谨慎。
近义词: careful, cautious, prudent, tactful
反义词: indiscreet, careless, imprudent
词性: adjective


词源: From Middle French *discret, from Latin discretus (“separated, distinct”), past participle of discernere (“to separate, distinguish”).
