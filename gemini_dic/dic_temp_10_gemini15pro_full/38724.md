conditionalist:/kənˈdɪʃənəlɪst/ | n. | 条件主义者；约定论者 | A person who believes that salvation is conditional upon faith and obedience. |  Example: The conditionalist argued that salvation was not guaranteed.  条件主义者认为救恩不是必然的。

Example: Some theologians believe that conditionalism offers a more just understanding of God's nature. 一些神学家认为，条件主义对上帝的本质提供了更公正的理解。 | 近义词: proponent of conditional salvation, believer in conditional immortality | 反义词: universalist, eternalist | 词性: noun
