befriend:/bɪˈfrɛnd/
v.
交朋友；与…为友
To act as a friend to; to form a friendship with.
She befriended the new student. 她和新来的学生交了朋友。

befriend:/bɪˈfrɛnd/
verb
与…交朋友
To act as a friend to (someone), especially when they are in need of help or support.
He was befriended by a kind old lady. 他得到了一位善良的老太太的帮助。

近义词:pal up with, make friends with
反义词:alienate, shun
