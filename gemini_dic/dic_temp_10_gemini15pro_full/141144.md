oocyte:/ˈoʊ.ə.saɪt/ | n. | 卵母细胞 | An immature female reproductive cell that can develop into an ovum (egg cell) after undergoing meiosis. |  The oocyte matures in the ovary before being released during ovulation. 卵母细胞在卵巢中成熟，然后在排卵期释放。|  The primary oocyte undergoes meiosis I to produce a secondary oocyte. 初级卵母细胞经过第一次减数分裂产生次级卵母细胞。 | Synonyms: egg cell, ovum (mature oocyte) | Antonyms: spermatozoon, sperm cell | Noun
