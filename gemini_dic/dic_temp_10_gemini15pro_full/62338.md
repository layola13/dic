endemicities:/ˌɛndɛˈmɪsɪtiz/| n. |地方病，特有现象|The state of being endemic; restriction to a particular locality or region.  The prevalence of diseases within a specific area or population.|例句：The island nation has high endemicities of several tropical diseases.  这个岛国几种热带疾病的地方病发病率很高。

例句：Researchers are studying the endemicities of certain genetic traits in isolated communities. 研究人员正在研究孤立社区中某些遗传特征的地方性。|近义词：prevalence, incidence, occurrence|反义词：absence, rarity|词性:noun
