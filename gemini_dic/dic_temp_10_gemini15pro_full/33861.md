circumscribed:/sərkəmˈskraɪbd/ | adj. | 受限制的，被约束的 |  Restricted within limits; confined.  Drawing a figure around another, touching at points but not cutting it. |  Her social life was circumscribed by her husband's career. 她的社交生活受到了她丈夫事业的限制。 | The inscribed circle is inside the triangle; the circumscribed circle is outside. 内切圆在三角形内；外接圆在三角形外。 |  limited, restricted, confined, bound, constrained | unlimited, unrestricted, unbound, free | adjective

circumscribe:/ˈsɜːrkəmˌskraɪb/ | vt. | 限制，约束 | To draw a line around; to encircle; to restrict something within limits. |  Poverty circumscribed his choices. 贫困限制了他的选择。 | The government's powers are circumscribed by the constitution. 政府的权力受宪法限制。| restrict, limit, confine, constrain, bound |  expand, enlarge, broaden, extend | verb
