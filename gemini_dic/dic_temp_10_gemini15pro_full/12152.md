aruspicy:/əˈrʌspɪsi/ | n. | 占卜; 预测; 预言 | The art or practice of divination by inspecting the entrails of sacrificial animals, especially the livers of slain victims. |  The aruspice examined the liver of the sacrificed ox to determine the will of the gods.  占卜师检查了祭祀公牛的肝脏以确定神灵的旨意。 |  divination, augury, prophecy, soothsaying | N/A | noun
