passionately:/ˈpæʃənətli/| adv.|热情地，充满激情地|With great emotion and enthusiasm; fervently.|She argued passionately for her beliefs. 她热情地为自己的信仰辩护。|Near Synonyms: fervently, ardently, intensely, zealously|Near Antonyms: apathetically, indifferently, coldly|adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *passionatus*, from Latin passio (“suffering, enduring”), from patior (“I suffer, endure”).  The sense evolution is from "suffering" to "strong emotion" to "enthusiasm."
