nonluminescence:/ˌnɑːnlʊmɪˈnɛsəns/
n.
无光，无冷光，不发光
The property of not emitting light.
The material exhibited nonluminescence even when exposed to UV light. 即使暴露在紫外线下，这种材料也表现出无光特性。
Near synonyms: non-emission, lack of luminescence
Antonyms: luminescence, fluorescence, phosphorescence
noun
