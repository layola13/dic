man-eater:/ˈmænˌiːtər/ | n. |食人者；吃人野兽；危险人物(尤指女人) | A person or animal that kills and eats people.  Figuratively, a person, especially a woman, who is perceived as ruthless or destructive, particularly in romantic relationships. | The villagers feared the man-eater that lurked in the jungle. 村民们害怕潜伏在丛林中的食人者。

She's a real man-eater; she's already been through three husbands. 她真是个“危险女人”；她已经结过三次婚了(意指她离过两次婚)。 | 近义词：cannibal, predator, femme fatale (for a woman) | 反义词: prey | 词性: 名词
