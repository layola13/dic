nonbanishments:/nɒnbænɪʃmənts/
noun
非放逐；未被驱逐的状态
The state of not being banished or exiled.
The king declared a general amnesty, resulting in numerous nonbanishments. 国王宣布大赦，导致许多人未被流放。
His nonbanishment was a surprise to everyone. 他没有被流放，这让每个人都感到惊讶。
Synonyms: nonexpulsions, nondeportations
Antonyms: banishments, exiles
词性: 名词
