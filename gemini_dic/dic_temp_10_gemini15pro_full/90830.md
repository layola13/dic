hunches:/ˈhʌntʃɪz/ | n. | 预感，直觉 | An idea based on feeling and not on evidence; a guess. |  I had a hunch that he was lying. 我预感到他在撒谎。 | synonyms: intuitions, feelings, suspicions, premonitions | antonyms: certainties, facts, knowledge | noun

hunches:/ˈhʌntʃɪz/ | n. | 驼背，弓背 | A humped or rounded position of the back. (Less common usage) | He sat with his hunches raised, looking dejected. 他弓着背坐着，看起来很沮丧。 | synonyms: humps, curves | antonyms: straight posture | noun
