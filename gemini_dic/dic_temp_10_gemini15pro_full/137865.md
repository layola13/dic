nonvirtue:/ˌnɒnˈvɜːrtʃuː/
n.
缺乏美德；非美德；缺点
Lack of virtue; a fault or imperfection.
His nonvirtue of impatience often got him into trouble. 他缺乏耐心的缺点经常给他带来麻烦。
Synonyms: vice, fault, flaw, defect, imperfection
Antonyms: virtue, merit, strength, goodness
词性: 名词
