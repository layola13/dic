confab:/ˈkɒnfæb/ (UK) /kənˈfæb/ (US)| n. vi.|闲谈，交谈；(尤指非正式的)会议，讨论|an informal private conversation or discussion|例句：We had a confab about the upcoming project. 我们就即将进行的项目进行了交谈。

例句：They confabbed for hours about their travel plans. 他们就旅行计划聊了好几个小时。|近义词：chat, discussion, meeting, conversation|反义词: silence, argument|词性:名词,动词
