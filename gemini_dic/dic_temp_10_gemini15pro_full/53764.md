The silence in the room made everyone feel discomfortable. 房间里的沉默让每个人都感到不自在。 | 近义词: uncomfortable, uneasy, awkward | 反义词: comfortable, relaxed, at ease | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Formed from the prefix "dis-" (meaning "not" or "opposite of") + "comfortable."  The prefix "dis-" is from Latin, while "comfortable" comes from Old French "confortable."
