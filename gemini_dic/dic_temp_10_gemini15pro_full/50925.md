desiccate:/ˈdɛsɪkeɪt/| v.  adj.|使干燥，使脱水；干燥的，脱水的|To remove the moisture from something, typically in order to preserve it.  To become thoroughly dried out.|The hot desert sun desiccated the plants.  炎热的沙漠阳光使植物干枯。|Synonyms: dry, dehydrate, parch, sear|Antonyms: moisten, hydrate, saturate|verb, adjective


desiccated:/ˈdɛsɪkeɪtɪd/| adj.|干燥的，脱水的|Thoroughly dried out; lacking moisture.|The desiccated landscape offered little sign of life. 干涸的土地几乎没有生命迹象。|Synonyms: dried, dehydrated, parched|Antonyms: moist, hydrated, saturated|adjective
