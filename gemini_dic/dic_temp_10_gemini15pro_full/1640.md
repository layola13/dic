aciniform:/əˈsɪnɪfɔːrm/
adjective
葡萄状的，像浆果的
Resembling a cluster of grapes or berries; shaped like a grape.
The aciniform glands secrete a viscous fluid. = 这些葡萄状腺体分泌粘稠液体。
The botanist identified the plant by its aciniform fruit. = 植物学家通过其葡萄状果实识别了该植物。
近义词:grapelike, berrylike, botryoidal
反义词:N/A
词性:adjective
词源:From Latin *aciniformis, from acinus (“grape, berry”) + -formis.

USAGE->
The aciniform structure of the gland was clearly visible under the microscope. = 在显微镜下，腺体的葡萄状结构清晰可见。
The berries on the bush were aciniform and juicy. = 灌木上的浆果呈葡萄状，多汁。
