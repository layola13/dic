determinatenesses:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪnɪtnɪsɪz/
|n.|确定性；明确性；坚定性|The state or quality of being determinate; definiteness; resoluteness.|The determinatenesses of his plans ensured their success.  他计划的坚定性确保了它们的成功。|近义词:definiteness, precision, resoluteness, decisiveness|反义词:vagueness, ambiguity, indecision, uncertainty|词性:名词


Etymology:
From determinate + -ness.  Determinate itself comes from Late Latin determinatus, past participle of determinare "to limit, determine," from Latin de- "completely" + terminare "to bound, limit," from terminus "boundary, limit."
