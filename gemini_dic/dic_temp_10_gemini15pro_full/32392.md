chiller:/ˈtʃɪlər/| n.|冷却器；冷藏库；恐怖小说/电影；令人恐惧的事物|A machine that cools something, especially a liquid or a room.  Something that causes a feeling of fear or excitement, especially a film or book.|例句：The chiller kept the milk cold.  冷却器使牛奶保持低温。

例句：That horror movie was a real chiller! 那部恐怖电影真是令人毛骨悚然！|近义词：refrigerator, cooler, freezer (for machine); thriller, shocker (for film/book)|反义词:heater (for machine)|词性:noun


chilly:/ˈtʃɪli/ | adj.|寒冷的；冷漠的|Unpleasantly cold; unfriendly or unwelcoming.|例句：It was a chilly autumn evening.  那是一个寒冷的秋夜。

例句：She gave me a chilly reception. 她对我的接待很冷淡。|近义词：cold, cool, frosty (for temperature); distant, unfriendly (for demeanor)|反义词:warm, friendly|词性:adjective
