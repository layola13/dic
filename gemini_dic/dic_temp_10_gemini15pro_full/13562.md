attendances:/əˈtɛndənsɪz/| n. |出席人数；出席率；出席记录|The number of people present at an event or meeting; the fact of being present at an event or meeting; a record of how many people attend something.|例句：The attendances at the football games have been declining this season.  这个赛季足球比赛的上座率一直在下降。|近义词：presence, turnout, participation|反义词：absence, nonattendance|词性:noun
