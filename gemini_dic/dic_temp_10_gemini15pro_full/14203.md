autocarpous:/ˌɔːtəˈkɑːrpəs/
adjective
自花授粉的；自体结实的
Producing fruit by self-fertilization; self-fertile.
Autocarpous plants do not require cross-pollination. 自花授粉植物不需要异花授粉。
Some varieties of tomatoes are autocarpous, making them ideal for greenhouse cultivation. 一些西红柿品种是自花授粉的，这使得它们非常适合温室种植。
synonyms: self-fertile, self-pollinating
antonyms: cross-fertile, cross-pollinating
词性: adjective
