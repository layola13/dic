bulgiest:/ˈbʌldʒiɪst/
adjective
最鼓的，最膨胀的
superlative adjective of bulgy: most bulgy.  Having a protruding shape; swollen.
His pockets were bulgiest with candy. 他的口袋里鼓鼓囊囊地装着糖果。

The clown's bulgiest pants were comical.  小丑最鼓的裤子很滑稽。

近义词: swelling, protruding, distended
反义词: flat, concave, sunken
词性: adjective
