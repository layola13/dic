accepts:/əkˈsɛpts/| v.|接受，承认|To receive or agree to take something offered; to regard as true, proper, or acceptable.|The university accepts applications until the end of May. 这所大学接受申请直到五月底。

The company accepts credit cards.  这家公司接受信用卡。|近义词：receives, admits, approves, acknowledges|反义词：rejects, declines, refuses|词性:动词(第三人称单数形式)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *accepten*, from Old French *accepter*, from Latin *acceptāre*, frequentative of *accipere* (“to receive”), from *ad* (“to, towards”) + *capere* (“to take, seize”).

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He accepts the gift gratefully. = 他感激地接受了礼物。
She accepts the job offer. = 她接受了这份工作。

一般过去时:
They accepted our invitation. = 他们接受了我们的邀请。
He accepted the blame for the mistake. = 他承担了这个错误的责任。

现在完成时:
We have accepted their proposal. = 我们已经接受了他们的提议。
She has accepted the consequences of her actions. = 她已经接受了她行为的后果。

过去完成时:
They had accepted the terms before I arrived. = 在我到达之前，他们已经接受了这些条款。
She had accepted the offer before she heard about the other job. = 在她听说另一份工作之前，她已经接受了这份工作。

将来时:
I will accept the challenge. = 我将接受挑战。
He will accept the position if it is offered. = 如果提供这个职位，他会接受的。

虚拟语气:
If I were offered the job, I would accept it. = 如果给我这份工作，我会接受的。
I wish he would accept my apology. = 我希望他能接受我的道歉。

被动语态:
The offer was accepted. = 提议被接受了。
His application was accepted by the university. = 他的申请被大学录取了。

疑问句:
Do you accept credit cards? = 你们接受信用卡吗？
Will she accept the offer? = 她会接受这个提议吗？

否定句:
I do not accept your explanation. = 我不接受你的解释。
He did not accept the blame. = 他没有承担责任。

PHRASE->
