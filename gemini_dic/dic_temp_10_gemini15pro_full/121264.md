misevaluations:/ˌmɪsɪˌvæljuˈeɪʃənz/| n. |错误评估，误判|An act of misevaluating something; an incorrect or faulty evaluation.|The company's misevaluations of the market led to significant financial losses. 公司对市场的错误评估导致了巨大的经济损失。|Examples:
1.  His misevaluations of his employees' skills resulted in a poorly executed project.  他对员工技能的错误评估导致了项目执行不力。

2.  The coach's misevaluations of the opposing team's strengths cost them the game.  教练对对方球队实力的误判让他们输掉了比赛。|Synonyms: misjudgments, miscalculations, misinterpretations|Antonyms: accurate assessments, correct evaluations|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- + evaluation.  "Mis-" is a prefix of French or Latin origin meaning "wrong," "badly," or "wrongly." "Evaluation" comes from the French "évaluation,"  ultimately from the Latin "valere" meaning "to be strong" or "to be worth."
