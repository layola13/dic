entertainer:/ˌɛntərˈteɪnər/ | n. | 演艺人员，表演者 | A person who entertains people, such as a singer, dancer, or comedian. |  例句：The entertainer captivated the audience with his magic tricks.  这位魔术师用他的魔术吸引了观众。| 近义词：performer, artist, musician, comedian, actor | 反义词: audience, spectator | noun


entertain:/ˌɛntərˈteɪn/ | v. |  娱乐，招待 | To provide amusement or enjoyment for someone. | 例句：They entertained their guests with stories and jokes. 他们用故事和笑话招待客人。| 近义词：amuse, divert, please | 反义词: bore, tire | verb
