nonclearance:/ˌnɒnˈklɪərəns/
n.
未清除；未清算；未获准
The state of not being cleared, approved, or authorized.
The shipment was delayed due to nonclearance by customs.  这批货物由于海关未放行而延误了。
non-approval, non-authorization
clearance, approval
noun
