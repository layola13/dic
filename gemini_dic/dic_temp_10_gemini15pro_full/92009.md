hyperorthodoxies:/ˌhaɪpərˈɔːrθədɑːksiz/
|n.|极端正统观念，极端正统派|Extreme or excessive adherence to orthodox beliefs or practices, often characterized by rigid interpretations and strict observance.|The hyperorthodoxies of certain religious sects can lead to isolation and intolerance.|近义词: fundamentalism, dogmatism, rigidity|反义词: liberalism, heterodoxy, flexibility|词性: Noun

Etymology:
hyper- (prefix): from Greek, meaning "over, above, beyond"
orthodox (adjective/noun): from Greek "orthos" (right, correct) + "doxa" (opinion, belief)
-ies (suffix): plural form
