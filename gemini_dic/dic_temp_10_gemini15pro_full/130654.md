nonadvantageous:/ˌnɒnˌædvənˈteɪdʒəs/
adjective
不利的，无益的
Not advantageous; unfavorable or unhelpful.
This nonadvantageous position made it difficult for the team to win. 这个不利的位置使团队难以获胜。
The current economic climate is nonadvantageous to small businesses.  当前的经济环境对小型企业不利。
近义词: disadvantageous, unfavorable, detrimental, unhelpful, adverse
反义词: advantageous, favorable, beneficial, helpful, positive
词性: adjective

词源:
Prefix "non-" (not) + "advantageous" (giving an advantage).  "Advantageous" comes from "advantage" (from Old French avantage), ultimately derived from Latin "abante" (from before, in front).
