antimonarchic:/ˌæntiˈmɒnəkɪk/ /ˌæntiˈmɑːnərkɪk/| adj.| 反君主的，反对君主制的 | Opposed to monarchy; against the principle of having a monarch. |  The antimonarchic sentiment in the country was growing.  这个国家反君主的情绪日益高涨。| synonyms: republican, anti-royalist, democratic | antonyms: monarchist, royalist, pro-monarchy | adjective

antimonarchist:/ˌæntiˈmɒnəkɪst/ /ˌæntiˈmɑːnərkɪst/| n.| 反君主主义者 | A person who opposes monarchy. | The antimonarchists protested outside the palace. 反君主主义者在王宫外抗议。| synonyms: republican, anti-royalist | antonyms: monarchist, royalist | noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From anti- (against) + monarchic (relating to monarchy).  "Monarchic" derives from the Greek "monarkhos" (sole ruler).
