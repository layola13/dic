intermigration:/ˌɪntərmaɪˈɡreɪʃən/
n.
相互迁移，互相移居
The intermigration between rural and urban areas has increased significantly in recent years.
近年来，城乡之间的相互迁移显著增加。
The intermigration of different ethnic groups led to a rich cultural blend.
不同族群的相互迁移导致了丰富的文化融合。
近义词:migration, movement, exchange
反义词:sedentariness, immobility
词性:noun
词源:From inter- + migration
