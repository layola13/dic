glancingly: /ˈɡlænsɪŋli/ | adv. | 瞥一眼地，匆匆地 | In a way that involves looking at something quickly and briefly. | She glanced at him glancingly and then looked away. 她匆匆瞥了他一眼，然后看向别处。| She smiled glancingly at him as she passed. 她走过时匆匆地对他笑了笑。| 近义词：briefly, cursorily, hastily | 反义词：intently, fixedly | 词性：副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From glance (v.) + -ing + -ly.  "Glance" comes from Old French "glacier" meaning "to slip, slide, glance." The suffix "-ly" forms adverbs.
