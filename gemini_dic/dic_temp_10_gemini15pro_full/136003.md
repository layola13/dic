nonrationalistic:/ˌnɒnˌræʃəˈnəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
非理性主义的
Not based on or appealing to reason; not rationalistic.  Rejecting the principle that reason is the primary source of knowledge and guide to action.
His nonrationalistic approach to decision-making often led to unpredictable outcomes. 他非理性主义的决策方式经常导致不可预测的结果。
The artist's nonrationalistic paintings evoked strong emotions.  这位艺术家的非理性主义绘画引发了强烈的情感。
Synonyms: irrational, emotional, intuitive, instinctive, subjective
Antonyms: rational, logical, reasoned, objective, empirical
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non.
rationalistic (adjective): From rational + -istic. Rational from Latin rationalis "of or belonging to reason," from ratio "reason, calculation, account, explanation."  -istic is a suffix forming adjectives from other adjectives or nouns, meaning "relating to," "characterized by."
