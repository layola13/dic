multiperils:/ˌmʌltiˈpɛrəlz/
noun
多重风险，多重危险
Relating to or involving insurance against more than one specified peril.  It covers a range of risks, such as fire, theft, flood, and windstorm damage.
The farmer purchased a multiperils insurance policy to protect his crops. 农民购买了一份多重风险保险单来保护他的作物。
synonyms: comprehensive insurance, all-risks insurance
antonyms: single-peril insurance, specific-peril insurance
