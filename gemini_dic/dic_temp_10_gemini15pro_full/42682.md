countersuing:/ˌkaʊntərˈsuːɪŋ/
n.
反诉，反起诉
the act of suing someone who has sued you
例句：The company is countersuing for damages.  这家公司正在反诉要求赔偿损失。

例句：She decided on countersuing her former employer for wrongful dismissal. 她决定反诉她的前雇主非法解雇。

近义词：countersuit
反义词：suing, prosecuting
