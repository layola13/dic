bovid:/ˈboʊvɪd/| n. adj.|牛科动物；牛科的|A ruminant mammal of the family Bovidae, such as cattle, sheep, goats, or antelopes.|例句：The cow is a bovid.  牛是一种牛科动物。|例句：Bovid horns are never branched. 牛科动物的角从不分叉。|近义词：ox, cow, ruminant|反义词:|noun, adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin bōs (“ox, cow”) + -id.
