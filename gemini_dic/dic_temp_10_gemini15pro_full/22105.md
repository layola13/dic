bolshevik:/ˈbɒlʃəvɪk/ (UK), /ˈboʊlʃəvɪk/ (US)| adj. | 布尔什维克的 | Relating to the Bolsheviks or their doctrines. |  The Bolshevik ideology emphasized a vanguard party. 布尔什维克的意识形态强调先锋党的作用。 | synonyms: communist, revolutionary, Marxist, socialist | antonyms: Tsarist, Menshevik | adjective
