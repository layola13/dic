go-round:/ˈɡoʊˌraʊnd/ | n. |旋转木马；一轮；一圈 | A carousel, typically found at amusement parks or fairs, on which people, usually children, ride on wooden or fiberglass horses or other animals that go up and down as the platform rotates. Also refers to a single cycle or turn of something. | The children loved riding the go-round at the fair. 孩子们喜欢在集市上玩旋转木马。 |  The peace talks went through another go-round before an agreement was reached. 和平谈判又进行了一轮才达成协议。| synonyms: carousel, merry-go-round, rotation, cycle, round | antonyms: halt, stop | noun
