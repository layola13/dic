anti-turkish:/ˌæntiˈtɜːrkɪʃ/ | adj. | 反土耳其的；敌视土耳其的 | Opposed to or hostile towards Turkey, its people, or its culture.  |  The anti-Turkish sentiment in the region has been growing.  该地区反土耳其的情绪一直在增长。 | Synonyms: Turkophobic | Antonyms: Pro-Turkish, Turkophile | adjective


anti-turkish:/ˌæntiˈtɜːrkɪʃ/ | n. | 反土耳其主义者；敌视土耳其的人 | A person who is opposed to or hostile towards Turkey, its people, or its culture. |  He was known as an anti-turkish for his strong views. 他因其强烈的观点而被称为反土耳其主义者。 | Synonyms: Turkophobe | Antonyms: Turkophile | noun
