nonbelief:/ˌnɒnbɪˈliːf/ /ˌnɑːnbɪˈliːf/| n. |不信；无信仰；怀疑|Lack of belief, especially in a religious doctrine.|例句：His nonbelief in God caused conflict with his family. 他对上帝的 disbelief 导致了与家人的冲突。

例句：Her nonbelief in traditional medicine led her to explore alternative therapies. 她对传统医学的 disbelief 让她去探索替代疗法。|近义词：disbelief, skepticism, doubt, unbelief|反义词：belief, faith, conviction|词性:名词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + belief (from Old English gelēafa "belief, trust")
