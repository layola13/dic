carbohydrate:/ˌkɑːrboʊˈhaɪdreɪt/ /ˌkɑːbəˈhaɪdreɪt/| n. |碳水化合物；糖类|A large group of organic compounds occurring in foods and living tissues and including sugars, starch, and cellulose. They contain hydrogen and oxygen in the same ratio as water (2:1) and typically can be broken down to release energy in the animal body.|例句：Bread, rice, and potatoes are good sources of carbohydrates.  面包、米饭和土豆都是碳水化合物很好的来源。

例句：Athletes often "carbo-load" before a race.  运动员在比赛前通常会“加碳水”。|近义词：saccharide|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider terms like protein, fat)|词性:noun
