lull:/lʌl/| n. v. |平静；间歇；暂停；使平静；使安静|A lull is a temporary period of quiet or inactivity.|The lull in the conversation allowed me to slip away unnoticed.  谈话的间歇让我得以不被注意地溜走。|Synonyms: pause, respite, break, calm, soothe|Antonyms:  agitation, commotion, disturbance, excitement|noun

lull:/lʌl/| v. |使平静；使安静|To lull someone means to cause them to feel calm or sleepy.|The gentle rocking of the boat lulled the baby to sleep.  船的轻柔摇晃使婴儿睡着了。|Synonyms: calm, soothe, pacify, quiet|Antonyms:  agitate, excite, stimulate, rouse|verb
