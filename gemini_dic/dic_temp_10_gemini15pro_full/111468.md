load-down:/ˈloʊd daʊn/| adj.|负载过重的，负担过多的|Overburdened; heavily laden.  Carrying a heavy or excessive load.|The hikers were load-down with supplies for their week-long trek.  这些徒步旅行者背负着为期一周的徒步旅行用品，负重不堪。|例句：The truck was load-down with bricks. 卡车装满了砖块。|近义词：overburdened, overloaded, weighed down|反义词：unburdened, light|词性：形容词

load-down:/ˈloʊd daʊn/| v.|使负载过重，使负担过重|To burden heavily; to overload.|The responsibilities of the job began to load him down. 这份工作的责任开始让他不堪重负。|例句：Don't load yourself down with too much baggage. 不要带太多行李，以免负重过多。|近义词：overload, overburden, weigh down|反义词：unload, lighten|词性：动词
