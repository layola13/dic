forceable:/ˈfɔːrsəbl/ (US) /ˈfɔːsəbl/ (UK)| adj.|可强迫的，强行的；强有力的|Achieved by force; done by compulsion.  Also, powerful; impressive.|例句：The police made a forcible entry into the building. 警察强行进入了大楼。|例句：He made a forcible argument for his proposal. 他强有力地论证了他的提议。|近义词：compulsory, coercive, powerful, vigorous|反义词：gentle, peaceful, voluntary, weak|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *forcible*, from Old French *forcible*, from Late Latin *forcibilis* (“strong”), from Latin *forcis* (“force”), from *fortis* (“strong”).
