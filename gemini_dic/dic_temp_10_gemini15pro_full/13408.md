atomizations:/ˌætəmaɪˈzeɪʃənz/
|n.|雾化，原子化|The process of reducing a liquid to a fine spray or mist.  The act of dividing something into very small parts. |The atomization of the perfume created a pleasant scent in the room. 香水雾化后，房间里充满了宜人的香味。|Synonyms: spraying, nebulization, dispersal, dissemination|Antonyms: condensation, solidification, unification|noun


atomization:/ˌætəmaɪˈzeɪʃən/
|n.|原子化|The act or process of reducing something to atoms or atom-like particles.  The act of breaking something down into its smallest components. |The atomization of the fuel is crucial for efficient combustion in a car engine.  燃料的雾化对于汽车发动机的有效燃烧至关重要。|Synonyms: disintegration, fragmentation, pulverization|Antonyms: fusion, combination, synthesis|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From atomize + -ation.  Atomize comes from atom + -ize.  Atom ultimately derives from the Greek word atomos, meaning "indivisible."
