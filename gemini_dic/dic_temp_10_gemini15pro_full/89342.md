homolateral:/ˌhɒməˈlætərəl/ /ˌhoʊməˈlætərəl/ | adj. |同侧的，同侧发生的| Situated on or affecting the same side of the body.  Relating to or affecting the same side of the body.| Example:  Homolateral limb movements are essential for walking. 同侧肢体运动对行走至关重要。

Example: The homolateral reflex causes the stimulated muscle to contract. 同侧反射引起受刺激的肌肉收缩。| 近义词: ipsilateral | 反义词: contralateral | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *homolateralis*, from homo- (same) + lateralis (of the side).
