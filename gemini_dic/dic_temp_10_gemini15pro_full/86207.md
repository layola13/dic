heathenisms: /ˈhiːðənɪzəmz/
noun
异教信仰;异教习俗
The practices or principles of heathens; paganism.
例句：The missionaries worked tirelessly to convert the local population from their heathenisms.  传教士们不知疲倦地工作，以使当地居民从他们的异教信仰中皈依。

例句：He viewed their rituals as bizarre heathenisms. 他把他们的仪式看作是古怪的异教习俗。
synonym: paganism, idolatry, barbarism
antonym: Christianity, Islam, Judaism
词性: 名词
