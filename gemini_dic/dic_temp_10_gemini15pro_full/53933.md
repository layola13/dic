discourteousnesses:/dɪsˈkɜːrtiəsnɪsɪz/
noun
粗鲁，失礼，无礼貌
The plural form of discourteousness, referring to multiple instances of rude or impolite behavior.
His repeated discourteousnesses to the staff eventually led to his dismissal. 他对员工的反复无礼最终导致他被解雇。
discourteousnesses towards the elderly 对待老年人的无礼行为
Synonyms: rudenesses, incivilities, impolitenesses
Antonyms: politenesses, courtesies, civilities
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From discourteous + -ness.  Discourteous from dis- (not) + courteous (polite, respectful). Courteous ultimately from Old French corteis (courteous).
