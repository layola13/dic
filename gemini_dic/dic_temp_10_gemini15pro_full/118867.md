metapterygium:/ˌmɛtəptəˈrɪdʒiəm/| n. |后鳍基骨|The basal cartilage or bone of the hind fin of fishes, or the homologous element of other vertebrates.|The metapterygium is a crucial element in the fish's locomotion.|Synonym: basal fin support|Antonym: (No direct antonym, potentially a fin ray which is a distal element)|noun
