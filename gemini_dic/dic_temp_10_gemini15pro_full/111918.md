loiter:/ˈlɔɪtər/ (US), /ˈlɔɪtə(r)/ (UK)| v. |闲逛，徘徊|To stand or wait around idly or aimlessly with no apparent purpose.|He loitered on the street corner. 他在街角闲逛。|Synonyms: linger, hang around, dawdle, tarry|Antonyms: hurry, hasten, rush|verb

loiters:/ˈlɔɪtərz/ (US), /ˈlɔɪtəz/ (UK)| v. |(第三人称单数) 闲逛，徘徊|Third-person singular present tense form of "loiter".|He loiters near the school after classes. 他下课后在学校附近闲逛。|Synonyms: lingers, hangs around, dawdles, tarries|Antonyms: hurries, hastens, rushes|verb
