copings:/ˈkoʊpɪŋz/
n.
应对机制; 顶盖; 墙帽
Ways of dealing with difficult or unpleasant situations. Also, the protective covering on top of a wall.
She developed healthy copings to deal with the stress of her job. 她形成了健康的应对机制来应对工作压力。
The copings on the garden wall prevented water damage. 花园墙上的墙帽防止了水损坏。
近义词: coping mechanisms, strategies, methods, coverings, caps
反义词:  (For coping mechanisms) succumbing, avoidance, denial.  (For wall coverings) base, foundation
词性: 名词 (plural)
