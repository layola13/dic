fogged:/fɒɡd/ (British), /fɑːɡd/ (American)| adj.| 模糊的，起雾的，困惑的 | Covered with fog; made unclear or indistinct; confused mentally. | The windows were fogged with condensation. 窗户上凝结了雾气。| Near synonyms: misty, blurry, hazy, clouded, bewildered, confused | Near antonyms: clear, distinct, lucid, transparent | adjective

fogged:/fɒɡd/ (British), /fɑːɡd/ (American)| v. (past tense and past participle of *fog*) | (使)起雾，(使)模糊，(使)困惑| To cover or become covered with fog; to make or become unclear or indistinct; to confuse mentally. |  The bathroom mirror fogged up after my shower. 洗完澡后，浴室的镜子起雾了。 | Near synonyms: mist, cloud, obscure, bewilder, confuse | Near antonyms: clarify, clear | verb
