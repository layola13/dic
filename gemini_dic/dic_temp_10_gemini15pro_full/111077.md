Etymology:
'Listest' is the superlative form of the adjective 'list', which means "to incline or lean."  The word 'list' itself has a complex etymology, ultimately deriving from Proto-Germanic *līstianą, meaning "to incline," and related to Old English *lystan "to desire."  The '-est' suffix is a common superlative ending in English.
