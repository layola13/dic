equalitarian: /ɪˌɡælɪˈtɛəriən/ | adj. n. | 平等主义的；平等主义者 | Believing in the principle that all people are equal and deserve equal rights and opportunities. |  Example:  The equalitarian policies of the government aimed to create a fairer society. 政府的平等主义政策旨在创造一个更公平的社会。| Example: He is a staunch equalitarian and believes in equal opportunities for all. 他是一个坚定的平等主义者，相信人人机会均等。 | Synonyms: egalitarian, democratic, equitable | Antonyms: elitist, hierarchical, authoritarian | adjective
equalitarian: /ɪˌɡælɪˈtɛəriən/ | n. | 平等主义者 | A person who believes in the principle that all people are equal and deserve equal rights and opportunities. | Example:  The equalitarians advocated for universal healthcare. 平等主义者倡导全民医保。 | Example:  She's a well-known equalitarian in her community, constantly fighting for social justice. 她在她的社区是一位著名的平等主义者，不断地为社会正义而奋斗。 | Synonyms: egalitarian | Antonyms: elitist, snob | noun
