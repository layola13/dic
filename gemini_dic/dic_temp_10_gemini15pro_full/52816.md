dilutable:/daɪˈluːtəbl/ /dɪˈluːtəbl/
adjective
可稀释的
Capable of being diluted; that can be made thinner or weaker by the addition of water or another solvent.
The paint is dilutable with water.  这种油漆可以用水稀释。
近义词:thinnable, weakenable
反义词:concentrated, undilutable
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dilūtabilis, from Latin dilūtus (past participle of diluere "to wash away, dissolve") + -abilis "-able".  Ultimately from Latin dis- "apart" + luere "to wash".
