insociable:/ɪnˈsoʊʃəbl/ | adj. | 不合群的，不爱交际的 | Not enjoying the company of or associating with others; unsociable. | He was a quiet, insociable man who preferred to spend his evenings alone. 他是一个安静、不合群的人，喜欢独自度过夜晚。|  She found him rather insociable and difficult to talk to. 她觉得他相当不合群，很难与他交谈。 | antisocial, unsociable, unfriendly, reserved, withdrawn | sociable, gregarious, outgoing, friendly | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + sociable (sociable), from French sociable, from Latin sociabilis, from sociare (to associate), from socius (companion).
