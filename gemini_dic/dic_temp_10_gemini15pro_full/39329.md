conjecturalist:/kənˈdʒɛktʃərəlɪst/| n.|推断者，猜测者|A person who makes conjectures; someone who bases their opinions or theories on guesswork rather than firm evidence.|His theories were interesting, but he was more of a conjecturalist than a scientist.|近义词：speculator, theorist, hypothesizer|反义词：empiricist, pragmatist|词性:名词
conjectural:/kənˈdʒɛktʃərəl/| adj.|推断的，猜测的|Based on or involving conjecture; speculative.|The evidence is purely conjectural.|近义词：speculative, hypothetical, theoretical|反义词：factual, empirical, proven|词性:形容词
