oversupply:/ˌoʊvər səˈplaɪ/| vt. |过度供应，供给过多|The market was oversupplied with cheap imports. 市场充斥着廉价的进口商品。|  Manufacturers oversupplied the market, leading to a drop in prices. 制造商过度供应市场，导致价格下跌。|近义词：glut, saturate, flood|反义词: undersupply, starve| verb
