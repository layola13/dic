debtless:/ˈdetləs/
adjective
无债的；没有负债的
Free from debt; not owing any money.
He was finally debtless after years of hard work. 他经过多年的努力终于摆脱了债务。
Synonyms: unencumbered, solvent, clear
Antonyms: indebted, in debt, owing
