buntings:/ˈbʌntɪŋz/
buntings|n.|彩旗; (某些种类的)燕雀|Small flags or decorations, often in bright colors, hung on strings; also refers to a family of small, seed-eating songbirds.|The street was decorated with colorful buntings for the festival. 节日里，街道上装饰着五颜六色的彩旗。|Synonyms: flags, decorations, streamers, pennants|Antonyms: (For the decorative sense) plainness, bareness|noun
buntings|n.|燕雀|A family of small, seed-eating songbirds.|We saw several buntings at the bird feeder. 我们在喂鸟器上看到几只燕雀。|Synonyms: finches, sparrows|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a type of bird)|noun
