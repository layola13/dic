noncomplaisant:/ˌnɒnkəmˈpleɪzənt/ | /ˌnɑːnkəmˈpleɪzənt/ | adj. | 不顺从的，不殷勤的，不讨好的 | Unwilling to comply; disobliging; not ready to please.  |  Example: The noncomplaisant student refused to follow the teacher's instructions.  这个不顺从的学生拒绝听从老师的指示。 | Example: His noncomplaisant attitude made him unpopular. 他不讨好的态度让他不受欢迎。 | Synonyms: intractable, recalcitrant, uncooperative, resistant, defiant, obstinate, stubborn | Antonyms: compliant, obliging, amenable, agreeable, cooperative, docile, submissive | Adjective
