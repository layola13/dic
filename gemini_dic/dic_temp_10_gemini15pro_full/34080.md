civilities:/səˈvɪlətiz/
n.
礼貌；客气话；礼节；客套
polite acts or expressions
She greeted us with the usual civilities. 她以惯常的客套话向我们问好。

近义词:courtesies, manners, politeness, decorum
反义词:rudeness, impoliteness, discourtesy

词性:名词(plural)

词源:Late Middle English: from Old French civilite, from Latin civilitas, from civilis ‘relating to citizens’ (see civil).
