berets:/bəˈreɪz/
n.
贝雷帽（复数）
A soft, round, flat-crowned cap, usually made of felt or wool.
The soldiers wore berets as part of their uniform.  士兵们戴着贝雷帽作为他们制服的一部分。
The artist often wore a black beret. 这位艺术家经常戴一顶黑色贝雷帽。
近义词:caps, hats
反义词:(无明确反义词)
词性:名词
