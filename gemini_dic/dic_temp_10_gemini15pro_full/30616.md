cerebrology:/ˌsɛrɪˈbrɒlədʒi/ /ˌsɛrɪˈbrɑːlədʒi/
n.
脑科学，大脑学
The study of the brain, its functions, and its disorders.
Cerebrology is a complex and fascinating field of study. 脑科学是一个复杂而迷人的研究领域。
Modern cerebrology has made significant advancements in understanding how the brain works. 现代脑科学在理解大脑运作机制方面取得了重大进展。
近义词: neuroscience, brain science
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider concepts like "ignorance of the brain" or "misunderstanding of the brain")
词性: noun
