lurk's:/lɜːrks/| n.| 潜伏者的，潜伏的东西| This refers to something or someone that lurks.  It is the possessive form of "lurk".  Because "lurk" is a verb, "lurk's" is unusual and would typically only be used in poetic or informal contexts.| The lurk's shadow stretched across the wall. (这潜伏者的影子投射在墙上). |近义词: lurker's, hiding person's|反义词: overt person's| 词性：名词 (possessive form of a verb, used attributively)


lurk:/lɜːrk/| vi.| 潜伏，埋伏|To wait in a hidden place, typically with malicious intent.  To be or remain hidden so as to wait in ambush for someone or something.|A crocodile was lurking in the swamp. (一条鳄鱼潜伏在沼泽里). He lurked in the shadows. (他潜伏在阴影中).|近义词: skulk, prowl, slink|反义词: appear, emerge| 词性: 动词
