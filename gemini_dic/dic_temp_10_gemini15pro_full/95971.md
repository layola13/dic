incultivation: /ɪnkʌltɪˈveɪʃən/
n.
耕作；开垦；培养；陶冶
The incultivation of the land brought forth bountiful harvests.  土地的耕作带来了丰收。
The incultivation of virtues is essential for personal growth.  培养美德对个人成长至关重要。
Etymology: From Late Latin *incultivatio, from Latin in- + cultivatio "cultivation."
近义词: cultivation, tillage, fostering, nurturing
反义词: neglect, abandonment
