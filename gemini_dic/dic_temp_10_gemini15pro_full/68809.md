fascicular:/fəˈsɪkjələr/ | adj. | 束状的，成束的；簇生的 | Arranged in or growing in bundles or clusters; resembling a fascicle. |  The fascicular roots of the plant helped it anchor firmly in the sandy soil.  这种植物的簇生根系帮助它牢固地 anchored 在沙土中。 |  bundled, clustered, fasciculate | scattered, diffuse | adjective

fascicular:/fəˈsɪkjələr/ | adj. | 小束的，小捆的 | Relating to or forming a small bundle or fascicle (a close cluster or bundle, as of flowers, leaves, or fibers). | The doctor pointed out the fascicular arrangement of the muscle fibers. 医生指出了肌纤维的束状排列。 | clustered, bundled, grouped | dispersed, separate | adjective
