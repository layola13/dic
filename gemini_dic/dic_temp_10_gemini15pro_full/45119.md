cucullus:/kʊˈkʌləs/
n.
hood, cowl
A hooded garment or covering, especially one attached to a cloak or monastic habit.
The monk pulled his cucullus over his head to shield himself from the rain.  和尚拉起他的风帽以遮挡雨水。

The cucullus of the cloak provided warmth and anonymity.  斗篷的风帽提供了温暖和匿名性。
