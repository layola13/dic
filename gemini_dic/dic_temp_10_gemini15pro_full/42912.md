couture:/kuːˈtʊr/ (US), /kʊˈtjʊər/ (UK)| n.|高级定制服装；高级时装业|The design and manufacture of high-quality, fashionable clothes to a client's specific requirements and measurements.|She wore a stunning couture gown to the gala. 她穿着一件令人惊艳的高级定制礼服出席了晚会。|Synonyms: high fashion, designer clothing|Antonyms: ready-to-wear, off-the-rack|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *couture*, from Old French *costure* "sewing, stitching," from Latin *consutura* "a sewing together," from *consuere* "to sew together," from *con-* "together" + *suere* "to sew."
