nonfraudulent:/ˌnɑːnˈfrɔːdʒələnt/ | adj. | 非欺诈性的 | Not fraudulent; genuine, honest, or authentic. |  This transaction appears to be nonfraudulent. 这笔交易看起来是非欺诈性的。|  The company certified the nonfraudulent nature of the transaction.  该公司证明了该交易的非欺诈性。| 近义词: genuine, authentic, legitimate, honest, bona fide | 反义词: fraudulent, deceptive, dishonest, counterfeit | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "fraudulent" (from Latin "fraudulentus," meaning "deceitful, cheating").
