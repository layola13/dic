hemeralopia:/ˌhɛmərəˈloʊpiə/| n.|昼盲症；白昼视力减退|Day blindness; abnormally low visual acuity in bright light. |She suffers from hemeralopia and can't see well in daylight. 她患有昼盲症，在白天看不清楚。|Near synonyms: day blindness|Antonyms: nyctalopia (night blindness)|noun
