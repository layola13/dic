overcharged:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃɑːrdʒd/
verb (past tense)|过高收费，索价过高|To charge someone more than the proper or fair price.|例句：I think I was overcharged for the groceries. 我觉得我的杂货被多收钱了。|近义词：overprice, gouge|反义词：undercharge|词性:v.
overcharged:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃɑːrdʒd/
adjective|过高的，过分的|Charged with too strong an electric current.|例句：The battery is overcharged. 电池过度充电了。|近义词：excessive, exorbitant|反义词：moderate|词性:adj.
