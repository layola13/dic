dawes:/dɔːz/| n.|道斯, 道斯计划 (尤指一战后美国贷款给德国的计划)| Dawes refers to the Dawes Plan, an attempt in 1924 to solve the World War I reparations problem, which had bedeviled international politics following the Treaty of Versailles and the occupation of the Ruhr.  It provided for an end to the Allied occupation, and a staggered payment plan for Germany's payment of war reparations.|The Dawes Plan was implemented in 1924 to help stabilize the German economy. 道斯计划于1924年实施，旨在帮助稳定德国经济。

The Dawes Plan is often studied as a case study in international finance. 道斯计划经常作为国际金融的案例研究。|近义词:plan, agreement|反义词:disagreement, conflict|词性:名词
