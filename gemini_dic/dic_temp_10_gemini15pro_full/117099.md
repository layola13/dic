meal-ticket:/ˈmiːlˌtɪkɪt/ (US) /ˈmiːlˌtɪkɪt/ (UK)| n.|饭票；提供经济支持的人（或事物）；摇钱树|A person or thing that is a source of income or support.|He married her because she was a meal ticket. 他和她结婚是因为她是个摇钱树。|Synonyms: breadwinner, cash cow, gravy train, moneybags|Antonyms: dependent, freeloader|noun

meal-ticket:/ˈmiːlˌtɪkɪt/ (US) /ˈmiːlˌtɪkɪt/ (UK)| n.|生计；生活来源|A means of livelihood or survival.|His job as a software developer was his meal ticket. 他作为软件开发员的工作是他的生计。|Synonyms: livelihood, living, sustenance|Antonyms: unemployment, destitution|noun
