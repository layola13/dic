biparous:/ˈbɪpərəs/ | adj. | 双生的，一次产两子的 | Producing two offspring at a birth. |例句：The biparous sheep gave birth to two healthy lambs. 这只双产的母羊生了两只健康的小羊羔。

例句：Biparous reproduction is common in some species of primates.  双胎繁殖在某些灵长类物种中很常见。 | 近义词：twin-bearing, dichorionic | 反义词: uniparous, multiparous | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *biparus*, from bi- 'two' + parere 'to bear, bring forth'.
