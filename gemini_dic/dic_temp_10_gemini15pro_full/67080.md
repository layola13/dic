exploitationally:/ˌɛksplɔɪˈteɪʃənəli/ | adv. | 剥削地，利用地 | In a way that involves exploitation; taking unfair advantage of someone or something for personal gain. | The company was accused of treating its workers exploitationally.  该公司被指控剥削其工人。| 近义词: abusively, unfairly, selfishly | 反义词: fairly, ethically, responsibly | 词性: 副词

词源:  From "exploitation" + "-al" + "-ly". "Exploitation" comes from the French "exploitation" and ultimately from the Latin "explicare" (to unfold, explain).
