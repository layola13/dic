palisades:/ˌpæliˈseɪdz/ | n. | 悬崖，峭壁 | A line of high steep cliffs, usually along a river. |  The house overlooked the palisades along the Hudson River. 这座房子俯瞰着哈德逊河沿岸的悬崖峭壁。

The hikers carefully navigated the treacherous palisades.  徒步旅行者小心翼翼地穿过险峻的悬崖峭壁。


| 近义词：cliffs, bluffs, precipices | 反义词: plains, lowlands | 词性: 名词
