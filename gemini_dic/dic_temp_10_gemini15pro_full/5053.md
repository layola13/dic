all-pervading:/ɔːl-pərˈveɪdɪŋ/
| adj. |普遍存在的，弥漫的，无所不在的| Spreading throughout every part of something.| The all-pervading smell of woodsmoke filled the air. 空气中弥漫着木烟的味道。 | Example 2: An all-pervading sense of doom hung over the town. 一种无所不在的厄运感笼罩着整个小镇。 |  Synonyms: ubiquitous, omnipresent, pervasive, widespread, rife | Antonyms: localized, limited, contained, restricted | adjective
