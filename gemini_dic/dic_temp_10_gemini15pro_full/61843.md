employable:/ɪmˈplɔɪəbl/ | adj. | 可雇佣的；适宜就业的 | Suitable for being employed; having the necessary skills and qualifications to get a job. |  She's intelligent and employable, but lacks experience. 她很聪明，也具备就业能力，但缺乏经验。

|  The program aims to make young people more employable.  这个项目旨在使年轻人更具就业能力。 | 近义词：qualified, skilled, suitable, hirable | 反义词：unemployable, ineligible | 词性: adjective
