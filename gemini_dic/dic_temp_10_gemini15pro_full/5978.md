ambulatory:/ˈæmbjʊləˌtɔːri/ (US) /ˈæmbjʊlətri/ (UK)| adj. n.|可走动的，流动的；门诊病人；散步场所|able to walk; of or relating to walking; a place for walking, especially in a hospital or monastery|The patient is now ambulatory and can move around the hospital freely.  这位病人现在可以走动了，可以在医院里自由活动。|The hospital has a beautiful ambulatory where patients can get fresh air. 这家医院有一个漂亮的散步场所，病人可以在那里呼吸新鲜空气。|近义词: mobile, walking, peripatetic|反义词: bedridden, immobile, sedentary|词性: adjective, noun

ambulatories:/ˈæmbjʊləˌtɔːriz/ (US) /ˈæmbjʊlətriz/ (UK)| n.|可走动的病人；门诊部；散步场所|plural form of ambulatory|The ambulatories were filled with patients recovering from surgery.  门诊部里挤满了正在术后恢复的病人。|The hospital's ambulatories offer various therapeutic services. 这家医院的门诊部提供各种治疗服务。|近义词: outpatients, clinics, walking areas|反义词: wards, inpatient facilities, bedridden patients|词性: noun
