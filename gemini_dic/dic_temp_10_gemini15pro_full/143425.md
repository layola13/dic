outflew:/ˌaʊtˈfluː/
verb
胜过飞行，飞得比…远/快
to fly faster or farther than something or someone else
The small plane outflew the larger one. 这架小飞机比那架大飞机飞得快。
surpassed in flight, flew faster than
outspeed, outrun
lag behind
v.
