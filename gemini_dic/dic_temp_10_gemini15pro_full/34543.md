clear:/klɪr/ | adj. | 清晰的，清楚的；晴朗的；畅通的；明显的 | Free from obscurity or ambiguity; easy to perceive, understand, or interpret. | The instructions were clear and easy to follow. 说明书清晰易懂。|  synonyms: obvious, evident, transparent, lucid, distinct | antonyms: unclear, obscure, vague, ambiguous | adjective

clear:/klɪr/ | adv. | 清楚地；完全地；远离地 | In a clear or distinct manner; completely; at a distance. | He spoke clearly and concisely. 他讲话清晰简洁。| synonyms: distinctly, plainly, evidently, completely, entirely | antonyms: unclearly, indistinctly, vaguely | adverb

clear:/klɪr/ | v. | 清除；放晴；变清澈；跳过 | To remove an obstruction or unwanted item; to become free from clouds or mist; to become transparent; to jump over. |  Please clear the table after dinner. 晚饭后请清理桌子。| synonyms: remove, clean, tidy, clarify, brighten | antonyms: clutter, obstruct, obscure, cloud | verb
