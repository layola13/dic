bulldogs: /ˈbʊlˌdɔːɡz/ (US), /ˈbʊlˌdɒɡz/ (UK) | n. |斗牛犬（复数）|A breed of powerful, compact dog with a wrinkled face and a protruding lower jaw, originally bred for bull-baiting. |Example: The bulldogs were playing in the park. 斗牛犬们正在公园里玩耍。

Example:  He breeds French bulldogs. 他饲养法国斗牛犬。

近义词:  None (as it refers to a specific breed) 没有（因为它指的是一个特定品种）
反义词: None (as it refers to a specific breed) 没有（因为它指的是一个特定品种）
词性: Noun 名词


词源:
From "bull" (referring to the breed's original use in bull-baiting) + "dog". 来自“bull”（指该品种最初用于斗牛）+“dog”。
