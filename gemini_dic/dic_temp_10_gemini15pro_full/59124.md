dynamics:/daɪˈnæmɪks/ | n. | 动态; 动力学; 力学; | forces that produce movement | The dynamics of the market are constantly changing. 市场动态在不断变化。 |  近义词: forces, energy, motion, processes | 反义词: statics, inertia | noun

dynamics:/daɪˈnæmɪks/ | n. | 活力; | the way in which people or things behave and react to each other in a particular situation | Group dynamics can have a powerful effect on decision-making. 群体动力会对决策产生强大的影响。 | 近义词: interactions, relationships, interplay | 反义词: apathy, stagnation | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dynamicus, from Ancient Greek δυναμικός (dunamikós, “powerful”), from δύναμις (dúnamis, “power, strength”).
