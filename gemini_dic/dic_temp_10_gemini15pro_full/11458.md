argumental:/ˌɑːrɡjəˈmɛntəl/
adj.
爱争论的；好辩的
Given to argument; disputatious.
He's very argumental and always wants to have the last word. 他很爱争论，总是想说最后一句话。
近义词：contentious, disputatious, quarrelsome
反义词：agreeable, amenable, conciliatory
词性：adjective

Etymology:
From argument + -al.  "argument" comes from Old French from Latin *argumentum from arguere "to make clear, prove, accuse."
