hexing:/ˈhɛksɪŋ/| n.|施魔法，施咒语|The act of putting a hex (a curse) on someone or something.|She believed she was suffering from a hexing. 她相信自己正遭受着咒语的折磨。|近义词：cursing, bewitching, enchanting (negative connotation)|反义词:blessing|noun


hexing:/ˈhɛksɪŋ/| adj.|施咒的，令人着魔的|Having the quality of a hex; enchanting in a negative way. |She gave him a hexing stare. 她给了他一个令人着魔的凝视。|近义词：cursed, bewitched, enchanted (negative connotation)|反义词:blessed|adjective


Etymology: From the Pennsylvania German word "hex" meaning "witch," ultimately from German "Hexe."
