aconddylose: /eɪˌkɒnˈdaɪloʊs/
adjective
无髁的，缺乏髁的
Lacking condyles, the rounded protuberances at the ends of some bones.
The aconddylose femur made it difficult to articulate with the tibia.  这根无髁的股骨使其难以与胫骨连接。
The fossil remains showed an aconddylose humerus, unlike modern mammals.  这些化石残骸显示出一个无髁的肱骨，与现代哺乳动物不同。

近义词: acondylar
反义词: condylar
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From a- (without) + condyle + -ose (forming adjectives).  Condyle comes from the Greek kondylos, meaning "knuckle."

This word is primarily used in a scientific/anatomical context.
