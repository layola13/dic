breathings:/ˈbriːðɪŋz/
noun
呼吸; 呼吸法; 微弱的声音; 气息
the process of taking air into and expelling it from the lungs.
Example: Deep breathings can help calm you down.  深呼吸可以帮助你平静下来。
Example:  His breathings were shallow and rapid. 他的呼吸又浅又快。
Synonyms: respiration, inhalation, exhalation, ventilation
Antonyms: suffocation, asphyxiation
