antidiphtheritic:/ˌæntiˌdɪfθəˈrɪtɪk/
adjective
抗白喉的
Preventing or curing diphtheria.
例句：
The doctor administered an antidiphtheritic serum.
医生注射了抗白喉血清。

The antidiphtheritic medication proved effective.
抗白喉药物证明有效。

近义词：antitoxin
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but conceptually:  pro-diphtheria - though this isn't a real word)
词性: adjective


Etymology:
anti- (Greek: against) + diphtheria (disease name) + -itic (adjective suffix)
