flapped:/flæpt/
verb
拍打，摆动，激动，慌张
moved (wings, arms, etc.) up and down quickly and with force. Also, acted with agitation or panic.
The bird flapped its wings and flew away. 鸟儿拍打着翅膀飞走了。
The flags flapped in the wind. 旗帜在风中飘扬。
He started flapping when he realized he was late.  他意识到自己迟到时开始慌张。
近义词:fluttered, waved, agitated, panicked
反义词:calmed, stilled, composed
