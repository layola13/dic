cottier:/ˈkɒtiə(r)/ (UK), /ˈkɑːtiər/ (US)| n.|佃农，小户农|A cottier is a peasant farmer who occupies a small holding of land, often rented, and who typically lives in a cottage.|The cottiers lived in simple dwellings, cultivating small plots of land.|Synonyms: peasant, smallholder, tenant farmer, crofter|Antonyms: landowner, landlord|Noun


cottier:/ˈkɒtiə(r)/ (UK), /ˈkɑːtiər/ (US)| n.|散工，短工|A cottier is also a laborer who does odd jobs for hire.|The cottiers were often employed by the larger farms during harvest time.|Synonyms: laborer, worker, odd-jobber|Antonyms: employer, boss|Noun
