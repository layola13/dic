antireligiously:/ˌænti rɪˈlɪdʒəsli/
adv.
反宗教地，反对宗教地
In an irreligious manner; against religion.
He spoke antireligiously about the church's practices. 他用反宗教的言辞谈论教会的惯例。
近义词: irreligiously, profanely, impiously
反义词: religiously, piously, devoutly
词性: 副词


Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek "anti-")
religious (adjective): relating to religion (from Latin "religiosus")
-ly (suffix): forming adverbs from adjectives
