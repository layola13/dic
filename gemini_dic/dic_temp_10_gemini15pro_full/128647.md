nickel:/ˈnɪk(ə)l/| n.|镍; (美国和加拿大的)五分镍币|A hard, silvery-white metallic element, resistant to corrosion and used in alloys.  A five-cent coin in the US and Canada. |I found a nickel on the sidewalk. 我在人行道上发现了一枚五分镍币。|The alloy contains a high percentage of nickel.  这种合金含有很高比例的镍。|近义词: five-cent piece (for the coin)|反义词: (No direct antonym for the element or the coin)|词性:noun
