consensual:/kənˈsenʃuəl/| adj.|双方同意的，一致的|Existing or made by mutual consent : agreed to by the people involved; involving the willing participation of all the people involved|例句：The consensual nature of the agreement ensured its smooth implementation.  协议的双方同意性质确保了其顺利实施。

例句：Consensual sex is distinguished from sexual assault.  双方同意的性行为与性侵犯是有区别的。|近义词：agreed, mutual, joint, collaborative, cooperative|反义词：nonconsensual, unilateral, forced, imposed|词性:形容词
Etymology->
From Latin *consentualis*, from consentire "to agree, consent," from com- "together" (see com-) + sentire "to feel" (see sense (n.)).
