grouse:/ɡraʊs/| n. |松鸡；抱怨，牢骚|A type of game bird found in upland areas. / A complaint.|例句：The hunter bagged two grouse. 猎人捕获了两只松鸡。

例句：He had a grouse about the food. 他抱怨食物不好。|近义词：grumble, complain, beef, gripe|反义词：compliment, praise|n.

grouse:/ɡraʊs/| vi. |抱怨，发牢骚|To complain or grumble.|例句：He's always grousing about something. 他总是抱怨个不停。

例句：She groused about the long hours. 她抱怨工作时间太长。|近义词：grumble, complain, whine, moan|反义词：compliment, praise|v.
