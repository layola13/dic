held-with:/held wɪð/ | adj. | 同意的，赞同的；被接受的 |  Generally used in the negative, meaning "not agreed upon" or "not accepted". It implies a lack of agreement or consensus. It can also suggest that something is not popular or widely supported. | The proposal was not held-with by the committee.  这个提议没有得到委员会的同意。| synonyms: accepted, agreed upon, approved | antonyms: rejected, opposed, disapproved | adjective
