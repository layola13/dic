bioconversion:/ˌbaɪoʊkənˈvɜːrʒən/ | n. | 生物转化；生物转换 | The process by which organic materials are converted into other substances by the action of living organisms, especially microorganisms.  |  Example 1: Bioconversion of agricultural waste into biofuel is a promising area of research.  农业废弃物转化为生物燃料是很有前景的研究领域。

Example 2: The bioconversion of cellulose into glucose is a complex process. 纤维素转化为葡萄糖是一个复杂的过程。 | synonyms: biotransformation, biocatalysis | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider "chemical synthesis" as a contrasting process) | noun
