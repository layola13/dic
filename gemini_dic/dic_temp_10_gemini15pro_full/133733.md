nonfulminating:/ˌnɒnˈfʌlmɪneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非暴发性的；非爆发的；非雷鸣般的
Not exploding or detonating violently; not fulminating.
The patient presented with a nonfulminating form of the disease.  病人表现出非暴发性疾病的症状。
The doctor assured us that the infection was nonfulminating.  医生向我们保证感染是非暴发性的。
近义词:mild, moderate, gradual
反义词:fulminating, explosive, severe
词性:adjective

词源:
From non- + fulminating (present participle of fulminate).  Fulminate comes from Latin fulminare "to flash with lightning," from fulmen "lightning flash, thunderbolt."
