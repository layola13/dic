pardon:/ˈpɑːrdn/ (US) /ˈpɑːdn/ (UK)| n. vt. int. |原谅，宽恕，对不起|A request to someone to repeat something because one did not hear or understand it.  Also used to express polite apology. |例句：Pardon me, I didn't quite catch that. 对不起，我没有听清楚。

Pardon my French, but this is ridiculous!  对不起，我说句粗话，但这太荒谬了！|近义词：excuse, forgive, apologize|反义词:accuse, blame|noun, verb, interjection
