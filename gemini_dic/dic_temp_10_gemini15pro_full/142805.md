ours:/ɑːrz/ |pron.|我们的|Something belonging to us.  It is the possessive form of "we". |This house is ours. 这房子是我们的。|That car over there is ours. 那边那辆车是我们的。|近义词: belonging to us|反义词: theirs, yours, his, hers, its|词性:代词
