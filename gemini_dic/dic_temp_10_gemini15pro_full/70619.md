filmography:/ˌfɪlˈmɒɡrəfi/
noun
电影作品集；影片目录
A list of the films made by a particular director, actor, or production company, or those dealing with a particular theme.
His filmography includes some of the best-loved movies of all time. 他的电影作品集中包含一些有史以来最受欢迎的电影。
synonym:oeuvre, canon
antonym: N/A
词性: 名词
