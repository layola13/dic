dry-cleaned:/ˌdraɪˈkliːnd/
adjective
干洗的
Cleaned with chemical solvents rather than with water.
例句：
My suit is dry-cleaned. 我的西装是干洗的。

Her dry-cleaned dress looked brand new. 她干洗过的裙子看起来像全新的。


近义词:cleaned, laundered
反义词:uncleaned, dirty
词性:adjective
