blankness:/ˈblæŋknəs/| n. |空白, 空虚, 茫然|A state of being empty, void, or without expression; a lack of understanding or memory.|Her face was a mask of blankness after she heard the news.  她听到这个消息后，脸上毫无表情。|Synonyms: emptiness, void, vacancy, hollowness, oblivion|Antonyms: fullness, awareness, understanding|noun
