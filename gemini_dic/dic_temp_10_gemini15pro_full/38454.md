concessionary: /kənˈseʃənəri/ (UK), /kənˈseʃəneri/ (US) | adj. n. |优惠的，特许的；受惠者|Relating to or offered at a reduced price, especially to certain groups of people; a person who receives a concession. |The museum offers concessionary rates for students.  博物馆为学生提供优惠价格。|Synonyms: reduced, discounted, preferential |Antonyms: full-price, standard, regular |adjective

concessionary: /kənˈseʃənəri/ (UK), /kənˈseʃəneri/ (US) | n. |受惠者，持优惠票者|A person who receives a concession. |Senior citizens are concessionaries on public transport. 老年人是公共交通的优惠对象。|Synonyms: beneficiary, recipient |Antonyms: non-beneficiary |noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *concessionarius, from Latin concessio (“a granting, yielding”), from concedere (“to yield, concede”).
