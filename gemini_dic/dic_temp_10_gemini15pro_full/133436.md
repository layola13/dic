nonfashionable:/ˌnɒnˈfæʃənəbl/ | /ˌnɑːnˈfæʃənəbl/ | adj. | 不时髦的，过时的 | Not conforming to the current fashion; out of style. |  Her nonfashionable clothes made her stand out in the crowd. 她的过时的衣服让她在人群中显得格格不入。 | The antique furniture, while nonfashionable, held a certain charm.  虽然这件古董家具不时髦，但它有一种独特的魅力。 | 近义词: outdated, unstylish, old-fashioned | 反义词: fashionable, stylish, trendy | 词性: 形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
fashionable (adjective): from fashion (noun) + -able (suffix). Fashion ultimately comes from Old French "façon" meaning "make, manner."
