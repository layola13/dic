dyad:/ˈdaɪæd/| n.|二元体，二人组，二分体|A group of two people, a pair; something that consists of two elements or parts.|The mother-daughter dyad is central to the story. 母女二元关系是这个故事的核心。|synonyms: pair, duo, couple, twosome|antonyms:  monad, individual, group (of more than two)|noun

dyad:/ˈdaɪæd/| adj.|二元的，二价的 (chemistry)|Relating to or consisting of two elements or parts.|A dyad molecule contains two atoms. 二元分子包含两个原子。|synonyms: binary, dual, twofold|antonyms: unary, singular, monadic|adjective
