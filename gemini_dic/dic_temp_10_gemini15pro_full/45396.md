cupellation:/ˌkʌpəˈleɪʃən/ | n. |灰吹法；试金法|The process of assaying gold or silver by heating the metal in a porous cup with lead, which oxidizes and carries away impurities into the cup, leaving the precious metal behind. | The cupellation process revealed the true gold content of the ore. 灰吹法揭示了矿石中真正的黄金含量。|Synonyms: assaying, refining |Antonyms: contamination, adulteration |noun
