intercouple:/ˌɪntərˈkʌpəl/
verb
互相连接，相互结合
to connect or join together
The two systems are intercoupled to ensure smooth operation. 这两个系统相互连接以确保平稳运行。
The gears are intercoupled to transfer power. 齿轮相互连接以传递动力。
Synonyms: interconnect, link, join
Antonyms: disconnect, separate
词性: 动词
