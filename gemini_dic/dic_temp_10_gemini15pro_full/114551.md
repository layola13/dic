malarkey:/məˈlɑːrki/
noun
胡说八道；废话；空话
Meaningless talk; nonsense.
例句：That's a load of malarkey!  那是一派胡言！

例句：Don't give me that malarkey.  别跟我胡扯。


近义词：nonsense, rubbish, baloney, hogwash, bunk
反义词：truth, fact
词性：n.
