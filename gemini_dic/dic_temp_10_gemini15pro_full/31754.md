cheaters:/ˈtʃiːtərz/
n.
骗子；作弊者
People who act dishonestly to gain an advantage are cheaters.
那些为了获得优势而不诚实行事的人是骗子。
Those students were caught cheating on the exam; they are cheaters.
那些学生考试作弊被抓；他们是骗子。
近义词：frauds, deceivers, swindlers
反义词：honest people, rule-followers
