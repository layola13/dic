infrared:/ˈɪnfrərɛd/ (US) /ˌɪnfrəˈred/ (UK)| adj. n.|红外线的；红外线|Electromagnetic radiation with wavelengths longer than visible light but shorter than microwaves.|Infrared cameras can detect heat signatures. 红外摄像机可以探测到热信号。|Synonyms: heat radiation, thermal radiation|Antonyms: ultraviolet|adjective

infrared:/ˈɪnfrərɛd/ (US) /ˌɪnfrəˈred/ (UK)| n.|红外线|The portion of the electromagnetic spectrum with wavelengths longer than visible light but shorter than microwaves.|The remote control uses infrared to communicate with the television. 遥控器使用红外线与电视通信。|Synonyms: infrared radiation, infrared light|Antonyms: ultraviolet light|noun
