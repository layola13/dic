inobedience:/ˌɪnəˈbiːdiəns/
n.|不服从，违抗|The child's disobedience angered his parents.  孩子的忤逆激怒了他的父母。|
例句：His disobedience led to his expulsion from school. 他的不服从导致他被学校开除。
例句：Acts of disobedience can have serious consequences.  违抗行为可能会造成严重后果。
近义词：defiance, rebellion, noncompliance
反义词：obedience, compliance, submission
词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *inobedience*, from Latin *inoboedientia*, from *inoboedient-* (stem of *inoboediens*) "disobedient," from *in-* "not" + *oboediens* "obedient."
