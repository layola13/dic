antifeminine:/ˌæntiˈfɛmɪnɪn/
adjective
反对女性的；不具女性气质的
Opposed to or lacking traditionally feminine qualities.
He made some antifeminine remarks about women's role in society. 他对女性的社会角色发表了一些反女性的言论。
近义词:anti-woman, misogynistic
反义词:feminine, womanly
词性:adjective
