mistressly:/ˈmɪstrɪsli/| adj.|情妇般的，专横的，傲慢的|Having the qualities or behaving in the manner of a mistress; domineering; haughty.|She gave him a mistressly look, daring him to disobey. 她给了他一个情妇般的神色，挑战他敢不敢不服从。|近义词：domineering, haughty, imperious, overbearing|反义词：submissive, humble, meek|词性:adjective
词源: From mistress + -ly.
