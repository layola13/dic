observe:/əbˈzɜːrv/(US) /əbˈzɜːv/(UK)| vt. vi.| 观察；遵守；评论；庆祝 |To watch carefully the way something happens or the way someone does something, especially in order to learn more about it.  To obey a law, rule, or custom. To make a remark about something.|例句：Scientists observed the behavior of the animals. 科学家们观察了这些动物的行为。|近义词：watch, monitor, comply with, follow, comment, remark, celebrate|反义词：disregard, ignore, violate, break|词性:动词
