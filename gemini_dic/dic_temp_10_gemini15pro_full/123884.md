morbidness:/ˈmɔːrbɪdnəs/ | n. | 病态，不健全，着迷于死亡 | An unhealthy and excessive interest in disturbing and unpleasant subjects, especially death and disease. |  Her morbidness was evident in her fascination with true crime documentaries. 她对真实犯罪纪录片的痴迷显露出了她的病态。

| Example 1: His morbidness led him to collect newspaper clippings about gruesome accidents.  他的病态让他收集关于可怕事故的剪报。

| Example 2:  There's a certain morbidness in wanting to visit the site of the disaster.  想去参观灾难现场有一种病态心理。

| 近义词: morbidity, ghoulishness, macabre | 反义词: cheerfulness, wholesomeness | 词性: 名词
