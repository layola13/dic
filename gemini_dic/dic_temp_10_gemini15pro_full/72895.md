PHRASE->
(Following's doesn't typically appear in phrases.  It's more common to use "the following" as a standalone introductory phrase.)
Following up on the following's... =  跟进以下内容...
In regards to the following's implications... = 关于以下内容的含义...
Please note the following's importance... = 请注意以下内容的重要性...
Concerning the following's relevance... =  关于以下内容的相关性...
Regarding the following's significance... = 关于以下内容的重要性...


注意事项:

Although "following's" is grammatically correct, it sounds slightly awkward and is less commonly used than "the following."  It is generally recommended to use "the following" instead.
