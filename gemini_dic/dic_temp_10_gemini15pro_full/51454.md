detonatable:/ˈdɛtənˌeɪtəbl/
adjective
可引爆的
Capable of being detonated; explosive.
The bomb squad carefully handled the detonatable materials.  炸弹小组小心翼翼地处理可引爆的材料。
Highly volatile chemicals are often detonatable. 高度挥发性的化学物质通常是可引爆的。
近义词:explosive, flammable, inflammable
反义词:non-explosive, inert
词性:adjective
词源:From detonate + -able. Detonate comes from Latin detonare "to thunder down," from de- "down" + tonare "to thunder."
