meaninglessly:/ˈmiːnɪŋləsli/| adv.|毫无意义地；无目的地|Without meaning or purpose; in a way that does not achieve anything.|He spent his days meaninglessly wandering the streets. 他整天毫无目的地在街上游荡。|Synonyms: pointlessly, aimlessly, senselessly, futilely, uselessly|Antonyms: meaningfully, purposefully, significantly|Adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From meaning + -less + -ly. Meaning comes from Old English *mænan "to mean, intend," from Proto-Germanic *mainijan "to think, have in mind."  The suffix -less is a native English suffix meaning "without."  The suffix -ly is a common adverbial suffix.
