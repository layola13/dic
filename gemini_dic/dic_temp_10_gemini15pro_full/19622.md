bigwiggednesses:/ˌbɪɡˈwɪɡɪdnɪsɪz/ | n. |  大人物作风；要人习气 | The state of being characterized by the behavior or mannerisms of important or influential people. |  The meeting was filled with bigwiggednesses, everyone trying to outdo each other with their importance.  这次会议充满了大人物作风，每个人都试图以自己的重要性胜过别人。 | 近义词：pomposity, arrogance, haughtiness | 反义词：humility, modesty | 词性: 名词

词源:  From bigwig + -ed + -ness. "Bigwig" itself originates from the large wigs formerly worn by people of importance, especially in British courts.
