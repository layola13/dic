guerrilla:/ɡəˈrɪlə/ | n. | 游击队员；游击战 | A member of a small independent group taking part in irregular fighting, typically against larger regular forces. | The guerrillas ambushed the army convoy. 游击队员伏击了军队的车队。|  The guerrillas were hiding in the mountains. 游击队员藏在山里。| synonyms: partisan, freedom fighter, insurgent, rebel | antonyms: soldier, regular, loyalist | noun

guerrilla warfare:/ɡəˈrɪlə ˈwɔːrfeər/ | n. | 游击战 | A form of irregular warfare in which a small group of combatants, such as paramilitary personnel, civilians, or irregulars, use military tactics including ambushes, sabotage, raids, petty warfare, hit-and-run tactics, and mobility to fight a larger and more conventional regular army. | The guerrillas waged guerrilla warfare against the occupying forces. 游击队员对占领军发动了游击战。| The country was embroiled in a long and bloody guerrilla warfare.  这个国家卷入了一场漫长而血腥的游击战。| synonyms: unconventional warfare, irregular warfare, partisan warfare | antonyms: conventional warfare, regular warfare | noun
