brilliantnesses:/ˈbrɪljəntnɪsɪz/
n.
才华横溢；光辉；辉煌
the state or quality of being brilliant.
Her writing sparkles with brilliance and wit. 她的作品闪耀着才华和智慧。
Synonyms: radiance, luminosity, splendor, magnificence
Antonyms: dullness, dimness
