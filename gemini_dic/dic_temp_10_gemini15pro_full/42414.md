counterconvention:/ˈkaʊntər kənˈvɛnʃən/ |adj. n.| 反传统的，反常规的；反传统的事物 |Going against established norms or conventions; something that opposes established conventions.|

Her counterconvention style made her stand out from the crowd.
她反传统的风格使她从人群中脱颖而出。


The artist's counterconvention work challenged traditional artistic norms.
这位艺术家的反传统作品挑战了传统的艺术规范。

counterconvention:/ˌkaʊntər kənˈvɛnʃ(ə)n/| n. | 反传统的事物；与传统相反的做法 |An act or instance of going against established norms or conventions.|

例句：
Holding the meeting outdoors was a welcome counterconvention.
在户外举行会议是一种受欢迎的反传统做法。

Their embrace of technology was a counterconvention in their traditionally conservative industry.
他们对技术的拥抱在他们传统保守的行业中是一种反传统做法。


近义词：unconventionality, nonconformity, heterodoxy|
反义词：conventionality, tradition, orthodoxy|
词性：noun|
