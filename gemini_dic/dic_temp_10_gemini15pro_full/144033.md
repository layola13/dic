outrhyming:/ˌaʊtˈraɪmɪŋ/
verb
超越韵律; 比…韵律更优美
surpassing in rhyming; excelling in the use of rhyme.
Shakespeare's sonnets often feature complex and outrhyming schemes.  莎士比亚的十四行诗通常以复杂而超越韵律的结构为特色。
His skill in outrhyming all his contemporaries made him a legend. 他在韵律上超越所有同时代人的技巧使他成为一个传奇。
近义词: outdoing, surpassing, excelling
反义词: failing, lacking
