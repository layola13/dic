backward:/ˈbæk.wəd/ (US), /ˈbæk.wədz/ (UK)| adj. adv.|向后的，落后的，迟钝的|Moving or directed towards the back or rear; behind in development or progress; shy or reluctant.|The car moved backward slowly. 汽车缓慢地向后移动。|Synonyms: reverse, rearward, behind, retarded|Antonyms: forward, progressive, advanced|词性: 形容词/副词

backward:/ˈbæk.wəd/ (US), /ˈbæk.wədz/ (UK)| n.|后退，落后|The state of being backward or underdeveloped.|There has been a backward slide in the economy. 经济出现了倒退。|Synonyms: regression, relapse, setback|Antonyms: progress, advance, development|词性: 名词
