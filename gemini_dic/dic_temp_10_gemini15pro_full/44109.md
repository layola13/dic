crisper:/ˈkrɪspər/ (US), /ˈkrɪspə/ (UK)| n. adj.|保鲜盒，保鲜的|A compartment in a refrigerator designed to keep fruit and vegetables fresh;  Something that is crisp, especially food.|例句：I put the lettuce in the crisper to keep it fresh. 我把生菜放在保鲜盒里保鲜。|近义词：refrigerator drawer, produce drawer, crisp (adj.) |反义词: stale, limp, soggy|词性: noun, adjective
crisp:/krɪsp/| adj. n. v.|脆的，薯片，使变脆|Firm and brittle;  A thin slice of potato fried until crisp; To make something crisp.|例句：The air was crisp and cool. 空气清新凉爽。 |近义词： crunchy, brittle, firm |反义词: soft, soggy, flexible|词性: adjective, noun, verb
