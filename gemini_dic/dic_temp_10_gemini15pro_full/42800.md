couplings:/ˈkʌplɪŋz/
n.
联轴器，连接，耦合
A device used to connect two shafts end to end.
The couplings between the train cars failed, causing a derailment. 火车车厢之间的联轴器失效，导致脱轨。
connections, links, joints
separations, disconnections
noun

couplings:/ˈkʌplɪŋz/
n.
连接物，接头
Something that connects two things.
The plumber tightened the couplings on the pipes. 水管工拧紧了管道上的接头。
connectors, fasteners, links
disconnectors, separators
noun

couplings:/ˈkʌplɪŋz/
n.
耦合
The act of joining or connecting two things.
The coupling of the two companies created a powerful conglomerate.  两家公司的合并造就了一个强大的企业集团。
joining, linking, uniting
separation, disconnection
noun
