aana:/ˈɑːnə/| n.|母象 (尤指印度象)|A female elephant, especially an Indian elephant.|We saw a mother aana with her calf.|近义词:female elephant, cow elephant|反义词:bull elephant|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi आना (ānā), ultimately from Sanskrit आनय (ānaya).

USAGE->
The aana protectively stood over her young. = 母象保护性地站在它的幼崽身上。

The aana trumpeted loudly, warning off the approaching tiger. = 母象大声鸣叫，警告靠近的老虎。


PHRASE->
aana and her calf = 母象和她的幼崽
a wild aana = 野生母象
