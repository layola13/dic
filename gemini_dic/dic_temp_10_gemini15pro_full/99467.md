interfascicular:/ˌɪntərˈfæsɪkjʊlər/ | adj. |束间的，束内的| Situated between fascicles or fasciculi, as of nerve or muscle fibers.| The interfascicular cambium is a type of meristematic tissue found in plants.  束间形成层是一种在植物中发现的分生组织。| interfasciculi, intrafascicular | extrafascicular | adjective
词源->
From inter- + fascicular, from fasciculus (“small bundle”) + -ar.
