closer:/ˈkləʊzə(r) (UK), /ˈkloʊzər (US)| adj. n.|更近的，更接近的；关闭者，结束者|Something that is closer is nearer in space or time.  A closer is a person or thing that closes something. |The closer we got to the city, the more traffic there was. 我们离城市越近，交通就越拥堵。|The closer finalized the deal.  结束者完成了交易。|近义词：nearer, adjacent, finisher, concluder|反义词:farther, more distant, opener|词性:形容词,名词

closer:/ˈkləʊzə(r) (UK), /ˈkloʊzər (US)| n.|关闭者，结束者|(baseball) A relief pitcher who specializes in finishing games.|The closer came in during the ninth inning.  终结者在第九局上场。|近义词：finisher, concluder|反义词:opener|词性:名词
