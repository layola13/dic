consequent-to:/ˈkɒnsɪkwənt tuː/
adj.
作为…的结果；由于
resulting from something; as a result of
The flooding was consequent to the heavy rain. 洪水是暴雨造成的。
Synonyms: resulting from, as a result of, due to, because of, owing to, on account of, in consequence of
Antonyms: prior to, preceding, before
Etymology: From Latin consequentem (nominative consequens) "following, ensuing," present participle of consequi "to follow after, overtake, reach, attain, result," from com- "with, together" (see com-) + sequi "to follow" (see sequel).
词性: 形容词
