dragoonage:/drəˈɡuːnɪdʒ/
n.
强制征兵；强迫服役；压制
The act of coercing someone into military service or other forms of forced labor.  It also refers to oppressive actions or policies.
Many young men were subjected to dragoonage during the war.  战争期间，许多年轻人被迫服役。
Conscription, impressment, coercion, forced enlistment
Voluntary enlistment, freedom of choice
