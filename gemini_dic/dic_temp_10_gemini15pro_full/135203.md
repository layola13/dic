nonornamentally:/ˌnɒnɔːrnəˈmentəli/
adverb
非装饰地；朴素地
without ornament; plainly.
She dressed nonornamentally for the funeral. 她穿着朴素地参加了葬礼。
The room was nonornamentally furnished. 房间里的家具朴素无华。
近义词: plainly, simply, unadorned, unembellished
反义词: ornately, elaborately, decoratively
词性: adverb

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + ornamental + -ly (adverb suffix)
"non-"（前缀，表示“不”） + ornamental（装饰性的）+ "-ly"（副词后缀）
