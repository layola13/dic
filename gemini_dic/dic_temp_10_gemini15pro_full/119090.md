metricities:/mɛˈtrɪsɪtiz/
n.
韵律学；诗体学；格律
The study or analysis of verse meter, rhythm, and stanzaic structure.
His lectures on the metricities of Milton were fascinating. 他关于弥尔顿诗歌韵律学的讲座引人入胜。

近义词: prosody, metrics
反义词: (无明显的反义词)
词性: noun
