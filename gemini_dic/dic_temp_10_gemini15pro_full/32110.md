chestnut:/ˈtʃɛstnʌt/ (US) /ˈtʃɛsnʌt/ (UK)| n. adj.|栗子；栗色；栗树；陈词滥调|A glossy brown nut that is edible when roasted. Also, the tree that bears these nuts, and the reddish-brown color of the nuts.|例句：We roasted chestnuts over the open fire. 我们在篝火上烤栗子。|近义词：maroon, auburn (for color)|反义词: N/A (for nut and tree);  (no direct opposite for color)|词性: noun, adjective

chestnut:/ˈtʃɛstnʌt/ (US) /ˈtʃɛsnʌt/ (UK)| adj.|栗色的|Having a reddish-brown color.|例句：She has chestnut hair. 她有一头栗色的头发。|近义词：maroon, auburn|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective
