florae's:/ˈflɔːriːz/
noun
植物群的
Genitive case of "flora," meaning "plants of a particular region or period."  Indicates possession or association.
The florae's diversity was astonishing.  植物群的多样性令人震惊。
Near Antonyms: fauna's (动物群的)
