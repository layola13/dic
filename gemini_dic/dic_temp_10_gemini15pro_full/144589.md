outwaves:/ˈaʊtweɪvz/
| v. | 超越，胜过，发出比...更强的波 | To emit waves that are stronger or more impactful than those of something else. | The new antenna outwaves the old one, providing a much stronger signal. 新天线比旧天线发出更强的波，信号更强。| outperforms, surpasses, exceeds |  falls short of, is weaker than | verb


outwaves:/ˈaʊtweɪvz/
| n. | (比喻)影响力，辐射力，扩散 | The radiating influence or impact of something. | The outwaves of the scandal reached far beyond the initial participants.  丑闻的影响远超最初的参与者。|  ripples, repercussions, impact, influence | containment, limitation | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"out-" (prefix): from Old English "ūt," meaning "out, outside, outwards."
"-wave" (suffix): from Old English "wæġ," meaning "wave, billow, flood."
