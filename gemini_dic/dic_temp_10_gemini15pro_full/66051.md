exasperatedness:/ɪɡˈzæspəreɪtɪdnəs/ | n. |恼怒，愤怒，激怒 |The state of being exasperated; extreme annoyance or irritation. |Her exasperatedness at his constant interruptions was evident. 她对他不间断的打断感到非常恼火。 | 近义词: irritation, annoyance, frustration, anger | 反义词: calmness, patience, composure | 词性: 名词

exasperate:/ɪɡˈzæspəreɪt/ | v. | 使恼怒，激怒 | To irritate or annoy greatly. |His condescending tone exasperated her. 他那居高临下的语气激怒了她。 | 近义词: annoy, irritate, infuriate, provoke | 反义词: soothe, calm, placate | 词性: 动词
