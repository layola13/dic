out-grossed:/ˌaʊtˈɡroʊst/
verb
超过...的票房收入
To surpass in gross revenue, especially in film or entertainment.
The new superhero movie out-grossed all previous films in the franchise.  这部新的超级英雄电影票房超过了该系列之前的所有电影。
The sequel out-grossed the original film by a significant margin. 续集的票房收入大大超过了原版电影。
近义词: out-earned, surpassed, exceeded
反义词: underperformed
