limsy:/ˈlɪmzi/| adj.|脆弱的，薄弱的，站不住脚的|Thin and easily damaged or broken; flimsy cloth, paper, etc. Not strong or solid; a flimsy excuse, argument, etc.|The box was made of flimsy cardboard and fell apart easily. 这个盒子是用薄纸板做的，很容易散架。| synonyms: fragile, weak, thin, slight, insubstantial | antonyms: strong, sturdy, robust, solid|adjective
