discloses:/dɪˈskloʊziz/
verb
揭露；透露；公开
makes (secret or new information) known.
The company disclosed its profits for the last quarter.  该公司公布了上个季度的利润。
reveals, divulges, unveils
conceals, hides, suppresses
词性:动词(第三人称单数)


disclose:/dɪˈskloʊz/
verb
揭露；透露；公开
makes (secret or new information) known.
He refused to disclose the source of his information. 他拒绝透露信息来源。
reveal, divulge, unveil
conceal, hide, suppress
词性:动词

词源: From Old French *desclos, past participle of desclore (“to uncover, reveal”), from des- (“dis-”) + clore (“to close”). Compare French déclore.
