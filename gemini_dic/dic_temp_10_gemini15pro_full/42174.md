cough:/kɒf/ (British) /kɔːf/ (American)| v. n. |咳嗽; 咳嗽声|1.(v.) To expel air from the lungs with a sudden, sharp sound. 2.(n.) The act or sound of coughing.|1.(v.) He coughed loudly to get our attention. 他大声咳嗽以引起我们的注意。

2.(n.)  Her cough sounds terrible. 她的咳嗽听起来很糟糕。|近义词：hack, splutter (v.); cough, hack (n.)|反义词:  (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑inhale 吸气)|词性:动词，名词

PHRASE->
coughing fit: 一阵剧烈的咳嗽  He had a terrible coughing fit. 他一阵剧烈地咳嗽。
cough up: 咳出 (尤指痰或血)  She coughed up some blood. 她咳出了一些血。
cough drop: 润喉糖  He took a cough drop to soothe his throat. 他吃了一颗润喉糖来舒缓喉咙。
whooping cough: 百日咳 Whooping cough is a highly contagious respiratory tract infection. 百日咳是一种高度传染的呼吸道感染。
dry cough: 干咳 He has a persistent dry cough. 他一直干咳。
