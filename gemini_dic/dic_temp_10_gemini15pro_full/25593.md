bummery:/ˈbʌməri/| n.|令人失望的事；扫兴的事|A disappointing or unpleasant situation or experience. |例句：It's a real bummery that the concert was cancelled.  音乐会被取消真是太令人失望了。

例句：Losing my wallet was a total bummery. 丢了钱包真是倒霉透了。|近义词：disappointment, drag, downer, letdown|反义词:joy, blessing, boon|词性:noun
