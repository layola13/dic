例句：He keeps the handest little gadget in his pocket for fixing things. 他口袋里装着一个非常方便的小工具，用来修理东西。|近义词：convenient, useful, practical, handy|反义词:inconvenient, awkward, cumbersome|词性:形容词

词源: "handest" is the superlative form of "handy," derived from the Old English "hand" and later influenced by the Old Norse "hendr."  The suffix "-y" transforms the noun into an adjective, and "-est" creates the superlative.
