allopathically:/ˌæləˈpæθɪkli/
adverb|对抗疗法地，以对抗疗法|Treated by means of remedies that produce effects differing from those produced by the disease treated.|The infection was treated allopathically with antibiotics.|Synonym: conventionally, traditionally|Antonym: homeopathically|词性:副词

Etymology:
allo- (Greek: different, other) + -pathy (Greek: suffering, disease) + -ically (adverbial suffix)
