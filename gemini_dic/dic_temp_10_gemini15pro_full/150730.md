parachutage:/ˈpærəʃuːtɑːʒ/| n.|空投，空降，伞降|The parachutage of supplies to the refugees was a success.  向难民空投物资很成功。|例句：

The parachutage of troops behind enemy lines was a risky operation.  向敌后空降部队是一项危险的行动。

空投物资对被困人员至关重要。 The parachutage of supplies was crucial for the stranded people.

|近义词：airdrop, airlift|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用land transport 陆路运输)|词性:noun
