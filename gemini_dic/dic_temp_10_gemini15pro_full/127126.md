nativism: /ˈneɪtɪvɪzəm/ | n. | 本土主义；地方主义 | A policy of favoring native inhabitants as opposed to immigrants. | The rise of nativism in the 20th century led to stricter immigration laws. 20世纪本土主义的兴起导致了更严格的移民法。| synonyms: xenophobia, isolationism, jingoism | antonyms: multiculturalism, internationalism, cosmopolitanism | noun

Etymology:  From Latin *nativus* (“native, born”), from *natus* (“born”), perfect passive participle of *nascor* (“I am born”).
