defenestration:/ˌdiːˌfɛnɪˈstreɪʃən/ |n.| 扔出窗外;去窗;去窗作用|The defenestration of Prague sparked the Thirty Years' War. 布拉格抛窗事件引发了三十年战争。|Synonyms: ejection, expulsion, ouster |Antonyms: admittance, inclusion, welcoming|noun

Etymology->
From Late Latin *dēfenestrātiō, from dē- + fenestra (“window”).  Ultimately from Latin de- ("from, down from") + fenestra ("window"). Coined in the 17th century to describe the throwing of Catholic officials from a window in Prague, an event that precipitated the Thirty Years War.
