humectant:/hjuːˈmɛktənt/
noun
保湿剂
A substance that absorbs or helps another substance retain moisture.
Humectants are used in many skincare products to keep skin hydrated.  保湿剂被用于许多护肤产品中以保持皮肤水分。
Humectants draw moisture from the air. 保湿剂从空气中吸收水分。
synonym: moisturizer, emollient
antonym: desiccant
