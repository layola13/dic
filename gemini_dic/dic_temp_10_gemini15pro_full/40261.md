contingential: /ˌkɒntɪnˈdʒɛnʃəl/ | adj. | 附带的，偶然的，依赖于…的 | Depending on something else that might or might not happen; subject to chance. |例句：The bonus payment is contingent on meeting sales targets. 奖金的发放取决于是否达到销售目标。

例句：Our success is contingent upon everyone working together. 我们的成功取决于每个人的共同努力。

近义词：dependent, conditional, subject to, provisional | 反义词：independent, unconditional, absolute, certain | 词性: adjective
