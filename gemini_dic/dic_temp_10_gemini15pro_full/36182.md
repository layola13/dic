coffre:/kɔfr/ (UK), /kɔːfr/ (US)| n. |箱子，保险箱；(汽车的)行李箱|A strongbox or chest, especially one for valuables. Also, the trunk of a car (chiefly British).|例句：The pirates buried the coffre filled with gold on the deserted island. 海盗们把装满黄金的箱子埋在了荒岛上。

例句：She put her suitcases in the coffre of the car. 她把行李箱放进了汽车的后备箱。|近义词：chest, trunk, box, safe|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French coffre, from Latin cophinus ("basket, chest"), from Greek κόφινος (kóphinos, "basket").
