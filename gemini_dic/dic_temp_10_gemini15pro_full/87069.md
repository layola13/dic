heptandrian:/hɛpˈtændriən/
adjective
七雄的；七雄统治的
Relating to a heptarchy, a government of seven rulers.
The heptandrian system of ancient Greece consisted of seven powerful city-states. 古希腊的七雄体制由七个强大的城邦组成。
近义词:  septuple, sevenfold
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider using terms like "unified", "monarchic" depending on the context)
词性: adjective
