outloving:/ˌaʊtˈlʌvɪŋ/ | v. | 比...活得更久；比...更爱 |  To live longer than someone else; To love someone more deeply or for a longer duration than someone else. |  She outloved her husband by ten years. 她比她丈夫多活了十年。 | 近义词: outlive, surpass in love | 反义词: predeceased | 词性: 动词

outloving:/ˌaʊtˈlʌvɪŋ/ | adj. |  (比...活得更久)的； (比...更爱)的|  Characterized by living longer than or loving more deeply than another. | Her outloving devotion to her children was inspiring. 她对孩子们持久而深沉的爱令人鼓舞。 | 近义词: enduring, devoted | 反义词: short-lived, unloving | 词性: 形容词
