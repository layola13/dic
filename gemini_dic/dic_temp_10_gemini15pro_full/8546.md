anticholesterol:/ˌæntiˌkəˈlɛstəˌrɔl/
adjective
抗胆固醇的,降低胆固醇的
Relating to or denoting a substance or process that reduces levels of cholesterol in the body.
Eating an anticholesterol diet is important for heart health.  吃抗胆固醇的饮食对心脏健康很重要。
Anticholesterol medication can help lower your LDL levels. 抗胆固醇药物可以帮助降低你的低密度脂蛋白水平。
synonyms: cholesterol-lowering, lipid-lowering
antonyms: cholesterol-raising, hypercholesterolemic
词性: adjective
