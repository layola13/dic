nonpsychoanalytical:/ˌnɒnˌsaɪkoʊˌænəˈlɪtɪkəl/
adjective
非精神分析的
Not pertaining to or involving psychoanalysis; not interpreted or explained in terms of psychoanalysis.
His approach to therapy is nonpsychoanalytical, focusing on behavioral modification instead.  他采用非精神分析疗法，专注于行为矫正。
The study offered a nonpsychoanalytical explanation for the observed behavior.  这项研究对观察到的行为提供了一种非精神分析的解释。
Synonyms: non-Freudian
Antonyms: psychoanalytical, Freudian
词性: adjective
