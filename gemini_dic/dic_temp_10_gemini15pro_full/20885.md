blazon:/ˈbleɪzən/ | v. |  纹章；炫耀；宣布；公开宣扬 | To display or proclaim conspicuously; to describe or depict heraldically. |  His name was blazoned across the headlines. 他的名字赫然出现在报纸头条上。 | synonyms:  proclaim, publicize, display, emblazon, trumpet, broadcast  | antonyms: conceal, hide, suppress, obscure  | verb
