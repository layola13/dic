non-sympathetic:/ˌnɒnˌsɪmpəˈθetɪk/ | /ˌnɑːnˌsɪmpəˈθetɪk/ | adj. | 不 sympathetic 的，不同情的，不体谅的 | Not feeling, showing, or expressing sympathy. |  He remained non-sympathetic to her plight, offering no help. 他对她的困境漠不关心，没有提供任何帮助。 | The judge gave a non-sympathetic response to the defendant's plea. 法官对被告的请求作出了冷淡的回应。 | 近义词：unsympathetic, callous, indifferent, uncaring | 反义词：sympathetic, compassionate, caring, understanding | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From non- + sympathetic. "Non-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "not". "Sympathetic" comes from the Greek "sympathetikos", meaning "affected by like feelings".
