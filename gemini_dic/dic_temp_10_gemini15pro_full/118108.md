meniver: /ˈmɛnɪvər/ | n. | 灰鼠皮；用灰鼠皮做的衣服或饰物 | The fur of a small, grey squirrel, typically used in the past for trimming or lining clothes. Also refers to a garment or accessory made of this fur. | Her cloak was lined with meniver. 她的斗篷衬着灰鼠皮。 | Near synonyms: ermine, miniver | Antonym: None (lack of a specific antonym for a type of fur) | Noun
