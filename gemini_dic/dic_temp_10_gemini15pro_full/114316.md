maintainor:/meɪnˈteɪnər/ | n. | 维护者，维护人 | A person or organization responsible for maintaining something, typically software or equipment. | Example: The maintainor of this software is a small team based in California.  这个软件的维护者是一个位于加利福尼亚的小团队。| Example:  The city is looking for a maintainor for the park's playground equipment.  市政府正在为公园的游乐场设备寻找维护人员。 | Synonyms: custodian, keeper, upholder, administrator | Antonyms: destroyer, saboteur | Noun
