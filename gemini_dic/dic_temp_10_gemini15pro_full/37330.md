commemoratory: /kəˈmɛməˌreɪtɔːri/ /kəˈmɛmərətɔːri/
adjective
纪念的，庆祝的
Serving to commemorate; intended to preserve the memory of a person or event.
例句：The commemorative plaque was placed on the wall.  这块纪念牌被安放在墙上。

例句：A commemorative stamp was issued to mark the centenary. 为纪念一百周年发行了一枚纪念邮票。

近义词：memorial, celebratory, remembrance
反义词：unremarkable, ordinary
词性: adjective


commemoration: /kəˌmɛməˈreɪʃən/ /kəˌmɛməˈreɪʃn/
noun
纪念，庆祝
The act of remembering or marking a significant event or person.
例句：The ceremony was a commemoration of the war dead.  这个仪式是为了纪念战争死难者。

例句：The statue was erected in commemoration of the town's founder. 这座雕像是为了纪念该镇的创始人而建立的。

近义词：remembrance, celebration, memorial
反义词：ignorance, disregard
词性: noun
