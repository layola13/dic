draftsmanship:/ˈdræftsmənʃɪp/
n.
制图术; 绘图技巧; 草拟(法案、信件等)的技巧
The skill of making drawings, plans, etc., or the quality of a drawing, plan, etc.
His draftsmanship is impeccable, capturing every detail with stunning accuracy. 他的制图技巧无可挑剔，以惊人的准确性捕捉每一个细节。
The architect's draftsmanship was evident in the precise lines and elegant curves of the building's design.  这位建筑师的绘图技巧体现在建筑设计精确的线条和优雅的曲线中。
skill, artistry, technique
clumsiness, awkwardness
noun
