ichthyophagy:/ɪkθiˈɒfədʒi/ | n. |食鱼; 鱼食性 |The practice of eating fish.  The diet of a creature that primarily consumes fish. |例句：The traditional diet of some island cultures is based on ichthyophagy. 一些岛屿文化的传统饮食以食鱼为主。

例句：The otter's ichthyophagy is evident in its streamlined body and webbed feet. 水獭的鱼食性在其流线型的身体和蹼足上表现得很明显。|近义词：piscivory |反义词:herbivory|词性:noun
