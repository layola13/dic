bioethicses:/ˌbaɪoʊˈɛθɪksɪz/
|n.| 生物伦理学 |The study of ethical issues raised by advances in biology and medicine, especially in areas such as genetic engineering, cloning, and organ transplantation.|  Bioethicses are becoming increasingly important in the 21st century.  21 世纪，生物伦理学变得越来越重要。|Synonyms:  biomedical ethics, medical ethics|Antonyms:  None (lack of ethical consideration is not a direct antonym)|Noun
