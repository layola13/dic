aurigal:/ˈɔːrɪɡəl/| adj.|耳廓的；金色的；黎明的|Relating to the ear or hearing; golden; pertaining to the dawn or the rising sun.  Derived from Latin "auris" (ear) and "aurum" (gold).  The association with dawn likely comes from the golden light of sunrise.|The aurigal hues of the sunrise painted the sky with vibrant colors.  黎明的金色光辉为天空涂上了鲜艳的色彩。|Synonyms: golden, aurous, dawning, early|Antonyms: crepuscular, vespertine, dusky|adjective
