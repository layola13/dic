eupatrid:/juːˈpeɪtrɪd/ | adj. | 本土的，土生的；原产地的 | Of, relating to, or exhibiting eupatry.  Existing or occurring in the same geographical area.  |  The eupatrid subspecies of the butterfly are found throughout the valley.  这个蝴蝶的本土亚种遍布整个山谷。| Synonym: native, indigenous, endemic | Antonym: alien, foreign, exotic | Adjective

eupatry:/juːˈpeɪtri/ | n. | 同域性 | The existence or occurrence of two or more kinds of organisms in the same geographical area. | The eupatry of the two species led to competition for resources. 这两个物种的同域性导致了对资源的竞争。 | Synonym: sympatry | Antonym: allopatry | Noun
