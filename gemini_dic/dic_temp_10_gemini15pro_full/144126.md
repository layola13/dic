outscream:/ˌaʊtˈskriːm/
verb
大声喊叫，盖过…的喊叫声
to shout louder than someone or something else
The fans outscreamed the band.  粉丝们的喊叫声盖过了乐队。
近义词: outshout, yell, shriek
反义词: whisper, murmur
词性: verb

outscream:/ˌaʊtˈskriːm/
noun
震耳的喊叫声
a loud shout
An outscream of rage echoed through the hall. 一声愤怒的喊叫声回荡在大厅里。
近义词: yell, shriek, shout
反义词: whisper, murmur
词性: noun
