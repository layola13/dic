affordabilities:/əˌfɔːrdəˈbɪlətiz/
noun
可负担性；负担能力
The plural form of affordability, referring to the quality of being affordable or the degree to which something is affordable.
The government is working to improve housing affordabilities for low-income families. 政府正在努力提高低收入家庭的住房负担能力。
Synonyms: affordability levels, means, resources
Antonyms: unaffordability,  expensiveness
n.
