nephritic:/nɪˈfrɪtɪk/
adjective
肾炎的；患肾炎的
Relating to or affected by nephritis (inflammation of the kidneys).
The patient exhibited nephritic syndrome, characterized by proteinuria and edema.  这位病人表现出肾病综合征，其特征是蛋白尿和水肿。
The nephritic symptoms included high blood pressure and reduced urine output. 肾炎症状包括高血压和尿量减少。

近义词:renal, kidney-related
反义词:healthy (kidneys)
词性:adjective

nephritis:/nɪˈfraɪtɪs/
noun
肾炎
Inflammation of the kidneys.
He was diagnosed with acute nephritis. 他被诊断为急性肾炎。
Chronic nephritis can lead to kidney failure. 慢性肾炎会导致肾衰竭。

近义词:kidney inflammation
反义词:(none - denotes a disease state)
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek nephros (kidney) + -itis (inflammation) for "nephritis". "Nephritic" is derived from "nephritis" with the suffix -ic (pertaining to).
