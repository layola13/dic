divertimenti:/ˌdɪvɜːrtɪˈmɛnti/ /ˌdaɪvɜːrtɪˈmɛnti/| n. |嬉游曲；娱乐性作品；消遣|A composition, usually for chamber ensemble, in several movements, often light and entertaining in character.  A series of pieces, often for chamber orchestra, similar to a serenade or a suite.|Mozart composed numerous divertimenti for various instrumental combinations.|近义词：serenades, cassations, suites|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "serious compositions")|Noun
