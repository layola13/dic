autocatalytic:/ˌɔːtəʊˌkætəˈlɪtɪk/
adjective
自动催化的
Relating to or involving a process or reaction that is catalyzed by one of its products.  A reaction is autocatalytic if a product of the reaction acts as a catalyst for the reaction. 
The oxidation of oxalic acid by potassium permanganate is an autocatalytic reaction. 草酸与高锰酸钾的氧化反应是一个自动催化反应。

autocatalytic:/ˌɔːtəʊˌkætəˈlɪtɪk/
adjective
自催化的
(of a process or reaction) catalyzed by one of its products.
The growth of the firm became autocatalytic, as the more successful they were, the more customers they attracted. 该公司的发展变得自催化，因为他们越成功，吸引的客户就越多。

autocatalytic:/ˌɔːtəʊˌkætəˈlɪtɪk/
noun
自动催化作用
The process or action of being autocatalytic.
The autocatalytic nature of the reaction made it difficult to control. 反应的自催化性质使其难以控制。


近义词: self-catalyzing, self-accelerating
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "inhibited")


词源:  auto- (self) + catalytic (relating to catalysis)  From Greek "autos" (self) +  "katalysis" (dissolution).
