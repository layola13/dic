blancmange:/bləˈmɑːnʒ/ | n. |牛奶杏仁冻，果子冻|A sweet, molded dessert made with milk or cream thickened with starch, gelatin, or similar ingredients, often flavored with almonds. |例句：The blancmange wobbled precariously as she carried it to the table. 果子冻在她把它端到桌子上时摇摇晃晃的。

例句：For dessert, we had a delicious raspberry blancmange. 甜点我们吃了美味的覆盆子果冻。|近义词：pudding, jelly, custard |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps something savory like "steak") |词性:noun
