egotism: /ˈiːɡəˌtɪzəm/ (US) /ˈiːɡətɪzəm/ (UK)| n. |自我主义; 自负; 自我中心|Excessive self-importance; conceit; the practice of talking about oneself too frequently.|Example: His egotism alienated his colleagues. 他的自负疏远了他的同事。|Example: Her constant bragging is a clear sign of her egotism. 她不断的吹嘘是她自负的明显标志。|Synonyms: self-centeredness, narcissism, vanity, conceit, self-importance|Antonyms: humility, modesty, selflessness|noun
