abyss:/əˈbɪs/| n.|深渊，无底洞；地狱；极大的痛苦|A deep, immeasurable space, gulf, or cavity; anything profound, immeasurable, or infinite.|She stared into the abyss below. 她凝视着下面的深渊。|近义词：chasm, gorge, void, depth|反义词：summit, peak|名词

abyssal:/əˈbɪsəl/| adj.|深渊的；深不可测的|Relating to or inhabiting the abyssal zone of the ocean.|Abyssal creatures are adapted to extreme pressure. 深海生物适应极端的压力。|近义词：deep, profound, unfathomable|反义词：shallow, superficial|形容词
