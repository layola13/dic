bestrode:/bɪˈstrəʊd/ | v. | 跨越，跨坐，支配 | to sit or stand with one leg on either side of something; to dominate or control | He bestrode the world like a colossus. 他像巨人一样雄踞世界。 |  straddled, spanned, dominated | N/A | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *bestrīdan, from be- + strīdan (“to stride”). Cognate with Dutch berijden (“to ride, bestride”), German besreiten (“to mount, bestride, contest”), Old Norse bestreiða (“to bestride”).
