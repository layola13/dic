deference:/ˈdefərəns/ /ˈdɛfərəns/| n. |尊重，顺从，敬重|respectful submission or yielding to the judgment, opinion, will, etc. of another.|例句：He showed deference to his elders. 他对长辈表示尊重。

例句：She treated the judge with deference. 她对法官毕恭毕敬。

近义词：respect, reverence, courtesy, honor, esteem, regard|反义词：disrespect, irreverence, contempt, defiance|词性:名词

deferences:/ˈdefərənsɪz/ /ˈdɛfərənsɪz/| n. |尊重，顺从，敬重（复数）|plural form of deference|例句：He paid his deferencess to the queen. 他向女王表示敬意。(较少用复数形式)

例句：She expressed her deferencess to the senior members of the team. 她对团队中的资深成员表示了敬意。(较少用复数形式)

近义词：respects, courtesies|反义词：disrespects, contempts|词性:名词
