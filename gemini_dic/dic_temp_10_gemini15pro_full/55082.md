disreputably:/ˌdɪsˈrɛpjətəbli/
adverb|不名誉地，声名狼藉地|in a way that damages someone's reputation; in a way that is not respectable|He acted disreputably throughout the entire affair. 他在整个事件中的行为声名狼藉。|Synonyms: dishonorably, shamefully, disgracefully|Antonyms: honorably, respectably, creditably|词性:副词

disreputably:/ˌdɪsˈrɛpjətəbli/
adjective|(古旧) 声名狼藉的，不名誉的|Not considered respectable in character or appearance.|He frequented disreputable bars and gambling dens. 他经常出入声名狼藉的酒吧和赌场。|Synonyms: shady, notorious, infamous|Antonyms: reputable, respectable, esteemed|词性:形容词
