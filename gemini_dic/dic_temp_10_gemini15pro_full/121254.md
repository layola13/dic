misestimated:/ˌmɪsˈɛstɪmeɪtɪd/
verb
错误估计；估错
having estimated something incorrectly.
The company misevaluated the cost of the project.  该公司错误估计了项目的成本。
The government misevaluated the impact of the new policy. 政府错误估计了新政策的影响。
miscalculated, underestimated, overestimated
correctly estimated, accurately estimated
