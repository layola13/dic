gnam:/nɑːm/ | n. | 天空, 天堂 (藏语) |  "Gnam" is the Tibetan word for "sky" or "heaven." It is often used in religious contexts and can also refer to the atmosphere or the celestial realm. |  The prayer flags flutter in the gnam.  经幡在天空飘扬。| Synonym: heaven, sky (in Tibetan context) | Antonym: earth, ground | noun
