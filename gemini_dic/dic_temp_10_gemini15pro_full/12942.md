aster:/ˈæstər/| n. |紫菀；翠菊|A plant of the daisy family that has bright rayed flowers, typically of purple, pink, or white.|The asters bloomed in vibrant colors in the fall. 秋天，紫菀开出了鲜艳的花朵。|近义词：daisy, chrysanthemum|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

aster:/ˈæstər/| n. |星号|A star-shaped symbol (*), used in printing or writing to indicate an omission, a doubtful matter, etc., or as a reference mark.|He added an aster to the footnote. 他在脚注处加了个星号。|近义词:star symbol|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
