dulip: /ˈdʒuːlɪp/ | n. | 杜丽普（一种以波旁威士忌、薄荷和糖为原料的鸡尾酒） | A sweet alcoholic drink made with bourbon whiskey, mint, and sugar, traditionally served in a silver or pewter cup. |  例句：He sipped his mint julep slowly, enjoying the cool drink on a hot day. 他慢慢地啜饮着他的薄荷朱利酒，在炎热的天气里享受着清凉的饮料。

例句：The Kentucky Derby is famous for its mint juleps. 肯塔基赛马会以其薄荷朱利酒而闻名。

近义词：cocktail, drink | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
