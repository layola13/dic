captive:/ˈkæptɪv/| adj. n.|被俘虏的，囚禁的；俘虏|A person or animal that is held prisoner. |The soldiers were held captive by the enemy. 士兵们被敌人俘虏了。|近义词：prisoner, hostage, confined|反义词：free, liberated|词性:形容词,名词
captive:/ˈkæptɪv/| adj. n.|着迷的，被吸引的|strongly attracted to someone or something, and unable to think about anything else|She was held captive by his intense gaze. 她被他热切的目光所吸引。|近义词：enthralled, fascinated|反义词：uninterested, indifferent|词性:形容词,名词
