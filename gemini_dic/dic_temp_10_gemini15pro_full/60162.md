effeminating:/ɪˈfɛmɪneɪtɪŋ/
|adj.|使女性化的；使柔弱的|Making someone or something appear or behave more feminine, often in a way considered weak or undesirable.|His effeminating mannerisms made him the target of bullies. 他的女性化举止使他成为欺凌的目标。|近义词:emasculating, weakening, unmanly|反义词: masculinizing, strengthening, manly|verb (present participle)

effeminate:/ɪˈfɛmɪneɪt/
|adj.|女人气的；柔弱的|(of a man) having or showing characteristics regarded as typical of a woman; unmanly.|He had a high, somewhat effeminate voice. 他的嗓音很高，有点女性化。|近义词:womanish, unmanly|反义词:masculine, manly|adjective

词源:
From Late Latin effeminatus, past participle of effeminare (“to make womanish, effeminate”), from ex (“out”) + femina (“woman”).
