coccobacterium:/ˌkɒkəʊbəˈsɪliəm/ (US) /ˌkɑːkoʊbəˈsɪliəm/| n. |球杆菌|A bacterium that is intermediate in morphology between a coccus and a bacillus. It is typically oval or slightly elongated in shape.| Coccobacilli can cause various infections. 球杆菌可以引起各种感染。|近义词：ovoid bacterium, coccobacillary bacteria|反义词: N/A| noun
