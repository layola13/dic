misinformative: /ˌmɪsɪnˈfɔːrmətɪv/ | adj. | 误导性的，提供错误信息的 | Providing incorrect information; misleading. |例句：The article was misinformative and caused unnecessary panic. 这篇文章具有误导性，造成了不必要的恐慌。

|例句：The misinformative report led to a public outcry.  这份误导性的报告引起了公众的强烈抗议。

|近义词： misleading, deceptive, inaccurate, fallacious |反义词： informative, accurate, truthful, factual |词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + informative (meaning "providing information"). The prefix "mis-" alters the meaning of "informative" to indicate the provision of *incorrect* information.
