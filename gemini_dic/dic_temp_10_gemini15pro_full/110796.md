linkages:/ˈlɪŋkɪdʒɪz/
n.
连接，联系，联动
connections, links, ties between two or more things or systems.
The report explored the linkages between poverty and crime.  这份报告探讨了贫困与犯罪之间的联系。
The linkages between diet and health are complex. 饮食和健康之间的联系很复杂。
connections, links, ties
separations, divisions
noun
