excitingest:/ɪkˈsaɪtɪŋɪst/ | adj. | 最令人兴奋的 |  This is a non-standard superlative form of "exciting." While grammatically incorrect, it is sometimes used informally for emphasis.  The standard superlative is "most exciting." | That was the excitingest rollercoaster ride ever! 那是有史以来最刺激的过山车！| Near Synonyms: most exciting, thrillingest (also non-standard), most thrilling | Antonyms: least exciting, most boring, dullest | Word Type: Adjective
