contextualization:/kənˌtɛkstʃuələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 语境化；置于语境中 | The act of placing something, such as a word, statement, or event, within its relevant context for better understanding. |  Proper contextualization of the poem is crucial for interpreting its meaning. 正确地将这首诗置于语境中对于理解其含义至关重要。| contextualization, placing in context, situating | decontextualization | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From contextualize + -ation. Contextualize comes from context + -ualize. Context ultimately derives from Latin contexere "to weave together."
