outjumps:/ˌaʊtˈdʒʌmps/
verb
超越，跳得比…远/高
jumps further or higher than
The athlete outjumps all his competitors.  这位运动员跳得比所有竞争对手都远/高。
近义词:surpasses, exceeds, betters, outleaps
反义词:loses to, trails, falls short of
词性:v.
