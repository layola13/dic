anorthoclase:/əˈnɔːrθəˌkleɪs/
n.
歪长石
A triclinic alkali feldspar, (Na,K)AlSi3O8, that occurs typically as grey or white crystals in igneous rocks.
Anorthoclase is a common constituent of many volcanic rocks. 歪长石是许多火山岩的常见成分。
Anorthoclase crystals were identified in the sample. 在样本中发现了歪长石晶体。
Synonym:feldspar
Antonym:None
