discolorment:/dɪsˈkʌlər mənt/
n.
变色，褪色
The act or process of becoming discolored; a change in color, usually to something less attractive.
The antique chair showed signs of discoloration from years of sun exposure.  这把古董椅子因多年的阳光照射而出现褪色的迹象。
staining, fading, discoloration, bleaching
brightening, coloring
noun
