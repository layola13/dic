craver:/ˈkreɪvər/| n.|渴望者；热衷者；瘾君子|A person who strongly desires or yearns for something.|He's a craver of knowledge and always eager to learn new things. 他是一个求知若渴的人，总是渴望学习新事物。|Synonyms: addict, devotee, enthusiast, fiend, fan, aficionado|Antonyms: hater, detractor|noun
