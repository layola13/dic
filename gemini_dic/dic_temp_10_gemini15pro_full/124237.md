mosses:/ˈmɒsɪz/ (UK), /ˈmɑːsɪz/ (US)| n. |苔藓; 苔|Small flowerless plants that typically grow in dense green clumps or mats, often in damp or shady locations.|The rocks were covered in mosses.|Synonyms: bryophytes, liverworts|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a plant type.  Could consider "flowering plants" as a broad contrast.)|Noun
