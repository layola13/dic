discriminator:/dɪˈskrɪmɪneɪtər/ | n. | 鉴别器，判别器，歧视者 | A person or thing that discriminates, especially a device that separates signals or substances. |  The discriminator in the radio receiver separates the audio signal from the carrier wave. 收音机里的鉴频器把音频信号从载波中分离出来。 | synonyms: distinguisher, differentiator, separator | antonyms: integrator, combiner | noun

discriminator:/dɪˈskrɪmɪneɪtər/ | n. | 鉴别器，判别器，歧视者 | In machine learning, a discriminator is a neural network that is trained to differentiate between real and generated data. | The discriminator in the GAN is crucial for generating realistic images.  生成对抗网络中的鉴别器对于生成逼真的图像至关重要。 | synonyms: classifier, evaluator | antonyms: generator, creator | noun
