caterwaulers:/ˈkætərˌwɔːlərz/
n.
喧闹的歌手；发出刺耳叫声的人或动物；
A person or animal that makes a loud, harsh, and unpleasant cry, especially at night.
The caterwaulers next door kept us awake all night. 隔壁那些喧闹的人让我们整夜没睡。
Synonyms: shriekers, screechers, wailers
Antonyms: singers, melodists
noun
