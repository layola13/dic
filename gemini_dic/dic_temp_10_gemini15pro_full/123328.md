monographous:/məˈnɒɡrəfəs/
adjective
单配的，一雄一雌的
Confined to a single host species;  (of a parasite or symbiont) living on only one kind of host.
This monogamous wasp species lays its eggs in the larvae of only one species of beetle.  这种单配的黄蜂只在一种甲虫的幼虫中产卵。
近义词:monospecific, uniparous
反义词:polygamous, promiscuous
词性:adjective

词源:
From mono- (one) + -graph (writing, description) + -ous (adjective ending).  Ultimately from Greek monos (single, alone) and graphē (writing, description).
