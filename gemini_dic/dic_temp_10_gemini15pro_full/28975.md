carse:/kɑːrs/| n.|低洼肥沃的冲积地，泛滥平原|A fertile, low-lying area of land, typically alluvial, next to a river.|The farmers cultivated the rich carse land along the river. 农民们在河边的肥沃的冲积平原上耕作。|Synonyms:  lowland, floodplain, valley, bottomland, meadow|Antonyms: upland, highland|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Scottish Gaelic *cars*, from Old Norse *kjarr*, meaning "marsh" or "brushwood."
