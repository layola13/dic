amendable:/əˈmendəbl/ | adj. | 可修改的，可修正的；顺从的，服从的 | Open to or accepting of correction or improvement; willing to comply or yield. | The contract is amendable, so we can still make changes.  合同是可以修改的，所以我们仍然可以做出更改。|  The government proved amendable to the people's demands. 政府证明愿意服从人民的要求。 |  近义词: adaptable, flexible, compliant, receptive, susceptible | 反义词: inflexible, rigid, unyielding, intractable, obstinate | 词性: adjective

词源:  Late Middle English (in the sense ‘liable to amendment’): from late Latin *amendabilis, from emendare (see emend).
