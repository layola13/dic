facette:/fæˈsɛt/ | n. |(事物的)面，方面；(宝石的)刻面，小平面|A particular aspect or feature of something.  A small, flat surface on a cut gem. |The diamond had many facets, each catching the light.  这颗钻石有很多刻面，每一个都闪闪发光。|synonym: aspect, side, phase, surface |antonym: whole, entirety |noun

facet:/fæˈsɛt/ | v. | 把…刻成多面 |To cut facets on.  |He carefully faceted the gemstone. 他小心翼翼地为宝石刻面。|synonym: cut, carve, shape |antonym: smooth, round |verb
