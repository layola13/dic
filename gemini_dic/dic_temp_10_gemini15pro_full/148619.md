overtheorizations: /ˌoʊvərˌθiːərɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 过度理论化，过度理论化的情况 |  The act of theorizing excessively, often to the point of impracticality or detachment from reality. It involves developing complex theories that may not be supported by evidence or that are unnecessarily complicated. |  His overtheorizations about the economy ultimately proved inaccurate. 他对经济的过度理论化最终被证明是不准确的。

The book suffered from overtheorizations and lacked practical examples. 这本书充斥着过度理论化，缺乏实际例子。 | 近义词：over-intellectualization, speculation, abstraction | 反义词: pragmatism, practicality | 词性: noun
