advises:/ədˈvaɪzɪz/
verb
建议，劝告
Third-person singular present tense form of "advise".  It means to offer suggestions about the best course of action to someone.
He advises her to invest in real estate. = 他建议她投资房地产。

My doctor advised me to quit smoking. = 我的医生建议我戒烟。

近义词:recommends, suggests, counsels
反义词:dissuades, discourages
词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *aviser, from Latin ad- + visum (past participle of videre "to see").  Originally, "to look at," hence "to consider, reflect." The sense of "to give counsel" developed from the notion of considering a matter and offering an opinion.

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He advises his clients on financial matters. = 他就财务事宜向客户提供建议。
She advises against eating too much sugar. = 她建议不要吃太多糖。


一般过去时:
The lawyer advised his client to plead guilty. = 律师建议他的客户认罪。
My friend advised me against buying that car. = 我的朋友建议我不要买那辆车。


现在完成时:
He has advised me on several occasions. = 他已经多次给我提建议了。
She has advised the company on its marketing strategy. = 她为公司的营销策略提供了建议。


过去完成时:
He had advised her to leave before the storm hit. = 他曾建议她在暴风雨来临之前离开。
They had advised the president against signing the treaty. = 他们曾建议总统不要签署该条约。


将来时:
I will advise him to see a doctor. = 我会建议他去看医生。
She will advise the board on the best course of action. = 她将就最佳行动方案向董事会提供建议。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would advise him to apologize. = 如果我是你，我会建议他道歉。
I wish he had advised me against making that investment. = 我希望他当时建议我不要进行那项投资。


被动语态:
I was advised to rest. = 我被建议休息。
He was advised against travelling alone. = 他被建议不要单独旅行。


疑问句:
Who advises the president on foreign policy? = 谁就外交政策向总统提供建议？
What do you advise me to do? = 你建议我做什么？


否定句:
He does not advise investing in risky stocks. = 他不建议投资高风险股票。
She did not advise him to take the job. = 她没有建议他接受那份工作。



PHRASE->
advise against =  建议不要
advise on =  就…提供建议
seek advice = 寻求建议
take someone's advice = 采纳某人的建议
give advice =  给予建议
