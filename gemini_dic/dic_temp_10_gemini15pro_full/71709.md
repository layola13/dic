flavour:/ˈfleɪvər/ (US) /ˈfleɪvə/ (UK)| n. v.|味道，滋味；特色，特点|The dish has a distinctive smoky flavour.  这道菜有一种独特的烟熏味。|The chef flavoured the soup with herbs and spices.  厨师用香草和香料给汤调味。|近义词：taste, aroma, savor, relish, characteristic|反义词：blandness, insipidness|词性:名词,动词
Etymology: From Old French *flaur, from Latin *flātor (“a blowing, blast”), related to flāre (“to blow”).  Doublet of flatus.
