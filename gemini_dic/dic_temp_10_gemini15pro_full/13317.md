Athos: /ˈæθɒs/ (UK), /ˈeɪθɒs/ (US)| n. |阿托斯山，阿托斯|One of the three musketeers in Alexandre Dumas's novel "The Three Musketeers".  It also refers to Mount Athos in Greece, an autonomous monastic state within the borders of Greece. |Athos was the most serious of the three musketeers. 阿托斯是三个火枪手中最严肃的一个。

The monasteries of Mount Athos are a UNESCO World Heritage site. 阿托斯山的修道院是联合国教科文组织世界遗产。|近义词: (for the character) musketeer, swordsman|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
