hypnotizer:/ˈhɪpnəˌtaɪzər/ | n. |催眠师，施催眠术的人|A person who practices hypnotism.  A hypnotizer uses techniques to induce a state of hypnosis in others.|The hypnotizer swung a pocket watch back and forth. 催眠师来回摆动一块怀表。|Synonyms: mesmerist, hypnotist|Antonyms: None|Noun

hypnotize:/ˈhɪpnəˌtaɪz/ | vt. |催眠，施催眠术|To induce a state of hypnosis in (someone). To influence, control, or direct by or as if by hypnosis.|The magician hypnotized his assistant on stage. 魔术师在舞台上催眠了他的助手。|Synonyms: mesmerize, entrance, spellbind|Antonyms: awaken, arouse|Verb
