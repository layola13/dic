florification: /ˌflɔːrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
开花，繁荣，兴盛
The act or process of flowering; the state of being in flower. Also, a flourishing or thriving, especially of art, literature, or learning.
The florification of the cherry blossoms is a beautiful sight. 樱花盛开是一道美丽的风景。
The Renaissance saw a florification of art and literature. 文艺复兴时期见证了艺术和文学的繁荣。

近义词: blooming, blossoming, flowering, thriving, flourishing
反义词: decline, decay, withering
