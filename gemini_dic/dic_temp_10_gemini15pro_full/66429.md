exegetic:/ˌɛksɪˈdʒɛtɪk/| adj.|解释的，诠释的，注释的|Relating to or consisting of exegesis; interpretative.|例句：The professor's exegetic commentary on the ancient text illuminated its meaning. 教授对古代文本的解释性评论阐明了它的意义。

例句：His exegetic skills allowed him to unpack the complex symbolism of the poem. 他高超的诠释技巧使他能够解读诗歌中复杂的象征意义。|近义词：interpretative, explanatory, hermeneutical, expository|反义词：uninterpretive, obscure, ambiguous|词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin exegeticus, from Greek exēgētikos, from exēgeisthai 'interpret', from ex- 'out' + hēgeisthai 'to lead'.
