anticivism:/ænˈtɪsɪvɪzəm/
n.
反公民主义，反社会行为
Opposition to civic duty or social order.
He showed his anticivism by refusing to vote or pay taxes. 他以拒绝投票和纳税的方式表达了他的反公民主义。
近义词:anarchy, nihilism, rebellion
反义词:patriotism, civic duty, social responsibility
词性:名词
