booed:/buːd/
| v. |嘘；发出嘘声|to express disapproval of someone or something by shouting "boo"|The crowd booed the politician off the stage.  观众把政客嘘下了台。|近义词：jeered, hissed, heckled|反义词：applauded, cheered|verb

booed:/buːd/
| adj. |被嘘的|(past participle of boo) having been booed|The booed performer left the stage in tears. 被嘘的表演者哭着离开了舞台。|近义词：jeered at, hissed at, heckled|反义词：applauded, cheered|adjective
