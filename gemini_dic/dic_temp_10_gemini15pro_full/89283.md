homodont:/ˈhɒməʊdɒnt/ (UK) /ˈhoʊməˌdɑːnt/ (US)| adj. n.|同齿型的，同齿型动物|Having teeth that are all of the same type, or (of an animal) possessing such teeth.  Relating to or denoting the homodont dentition of most non-mammalian vertebrates, characterized by teeth of similar shape and function.|

例句：
The dolphin is a homodont animal.
海豚是一种同齿型动物。

Many reptiles are homodont, with all their teeth being conical.
许多爬行动物是同齿型的，它们的牙齿都是圆锥形的。

|近义词：isodont|反义词：heterodont|词性：adjective, noun
