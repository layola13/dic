gluttons:/ˈɡlʌtnz/| n. | 贪吃的人；暴食者 |  A person who eats and drinks excessively; one who indulges in something to excess. | The gluttons devoured the entire buffet in record time. 这些贪吃的人以创纪录的速度吃光了整个自助餐。|  The gluttons cleared their plates and asked for seconds. 这些贪吃的人清空了盘子，又要了第二份。| synonyms: gourmands, overeaters, pig, hog | antonyms: ascetics, moderates | noun
