barnstormers:/ˈbɑːrnˌstɔːrmərz/
n.
巡回演出的演员（尤指早期飞行员）; 政治演说家;
A group of performers, especially early aviators, who traveled around the country giving shows or speeches. Also refers to politicians who make whirlwind campaign tours.
The barnstormers thrilled the crowds with their daring aerial acrobatics. 这些巡回演出的飞行员以他们大胆的空中特技表演使观众兴奋不已。
The senator's barnstorming tour of the state rallied support for his re-election. 参议员在该州的旋风式竞选之旅为他争取连任赢得了支持。
近义词:stump speakers, campaigners, itinerant performers
反义词:homebodies, stay-at-homes

词性:名词(plural)
