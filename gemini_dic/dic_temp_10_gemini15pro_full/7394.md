animatedly:/ˈænɪmeɪtɪdli/
adverb
活泼地，生气勃勃地
in a lively or excited manner
She spoke animatedly about her trip to Italy. 她兴致勃勃地谈论着她的意大利之旅。
energetically, excitedly, vividly, spiritedly
apathetically, dully, listlessly
词性: 副词

Etymology:
From animate + -ed + -ly.  Animate comes from Latin animatus, past participle of animare "to give life to," from anima "breath, soul."
