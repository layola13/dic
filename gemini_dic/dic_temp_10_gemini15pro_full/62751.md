engorged:/ɪnˈɡɔːrdʒd/
adjective
充血的，肿胀的
(of a part of the body) swollen with blood or other fluid.
The tick was engorged with blood after feeding on the dog.  这只蜱虫吸了狗的血后变得鼓胀起来。
Her breasts were engorged with milk. 她的乳房胀满了奶水。
近义词: swollen, distended, inflated, turgid
反义词: shrunken, deflated, empty
词性: adjective

engorge:/ɪnˈɡɔːrdʒ/
verb
使充血，使肿胀
cause to swell with blood, water, or another fluid.
The heavy rains engorged the river.  暴雨使河水暴涨。
近义词: swell, distend, inflate
反义词: shrink, deflate
词性: verb
