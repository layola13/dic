diffracts:/dɪˈfrækts/ | v. |  衍射，绕射 |  spreads waves of light or other energy as they pass through an opening or around an obstacle | The light diffracts as it passes through the narrow slit. 光线穿过狭缝时发生衍射。| synonyms: scatter, disperse, spread | antonyms: focus, concentrate | verb
