partitive:/pɑːˈtɪtɪv/ | adj. n.|部分的，表示部分的；部分|A partitive word or construction denotes part of a whole.  Specifically in grammar, it refers to a word or phrase that indicates a part or quantity taken from a larger whole.|She drank some of the milk. ("some of" is a partitive construction)|近义词：fractional, segmental |反义词: whole, entire|词性:形容词, 名词
