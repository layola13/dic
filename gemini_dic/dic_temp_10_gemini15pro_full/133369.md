nonextraction:/nɒnɪkˈstrækʃən/ | n. | 非提取，非萃取 | The process of not extracting something, especially a natural resource.  This often refers to leaving resources in the ground or not harvesting them. |  The environmental group advocated for nonextraction of fossil fuels.  该环保组织提倡不提取化石燃料。 | synonyms: conservation, preservation, non-harvesting | antonyms: extraction, mining, harvesting | noun
