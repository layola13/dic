disclose:/dɪˈskloʊz/ /dɪsˈkləʊz/
verb
公开；透露；揭露
to make something known publicly, or to show something that was hidden
The company disclosed that it had lost millions of dollars.  该公司透露其损失了数百万美元。

The report discloses a disturbing trend in teen drug use. 这份报告揭示了青少年吸毒的一个令人不安的趋势。

近义词: reveal, divulge, unveil, expose
反义词: conceal, hide, suppress
词性: verb
