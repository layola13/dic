hedge-about:/ˈhɛdʒ əˈbaʊt/
adjective
被树篱包围的; 受限制的; 躲躲闪闪的
Surrounded by hedges; restricted; evasive.
The small cottage was hedge-about, giving it a secluded feel.  这间小农舍被树篱包围，给人一种隐蔽的感觉。
He gave a hedge-about answer, clearly avoiding the real issue. 他给了一个躲躲闪闪的答案，显然是在回避真正的问题。
近义词: hedged in, enclosed, restricted, evasive, noncommittal
反义词: open, exposed, unrestricted, direct, forthright
词性: adjective

词源:  From "hedge" (noun, meaning a barrier of bushes or shrubs) + "about" (adverb, meaning on all sides).  The combination emphasizes the sense of being enclosed or surrounded, and figuratively, of being restricted or evasive.
