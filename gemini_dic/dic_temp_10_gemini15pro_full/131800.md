nonconscription:/ˌnɑːnkənˈskrɪpʃən/
n.
非征兵; 免役; 反对征兵
The absence of conscription, or opposition to it.
The group advocated for nonconscription during the war.  这个团体在战争期间倡导免役。
Nonconscription became a popular political stance.  非征兵成为一种受欢迎的政治立场。
近义词: exemption from military service, draft resistance
反义词: conscription, draft, enlistment
词性: 名词
