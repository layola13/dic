outstreet:/ˈaʊtˌstriːt/
n.
郊区街道；偏僻街道
A street in the outskirts or less frequented part of a town or city.
We drove down a quiet outstreet, away from the bustle of the city center. 我们沿着一条安静的郊区街道行驶，远离市中心的喧嚣。
Synonyms: backstreet, side street, byway
Antonyms: main street, high street
词性: 名词
