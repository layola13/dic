comedic:/kəˈmɪdɪk/ | adj. | 喜剧的，滑稽的 | Relating to or characteristic of comedy or comedians. | Her comedic timing was impeccable. 她的喜剧时机把握得恰到好处。 |  The movie had some comedic moments. 这部电影有一些喜剧桥段。 | 近义词：funny, humorous, comical | 反义词：serious, tragic | 词性：形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *comicus, from Latin comicus (“of or belonging to comedy”), from Greek κωμικός (kōmikós, “of or for comedy, comic”), from κωμῳδία (kōmōidía, “comedy”).
