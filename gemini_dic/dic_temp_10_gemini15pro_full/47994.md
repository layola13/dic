decimator:/ˈdesɪmeɪtər/| n.| 大量毁灭者，屠杀者 |Someone or something that decimates or destroys a large proportion of a group or population. | The disease acted as a decimator of the local population.  这种疾病是当地人口的大量杀手。| synonyms: destroyer, killer, exterminator | antonyms: preserver, protector, savior | noun

decimate:/ˈdesɪmeɪt/ | v. | 大量毁灭，屠杀  |To kill, destroy, or remove a large percentage or part of. | The rabbit population was decimated by the disease. 兔子的数量因疾病而大量减少。 | synonyms: destroy, devastate, ravage | antonyms: build, create, protect | verb
