hydrolysis:/haɪˈdrɒlɪsɪs/ (US) /haɪˈdrɑːləsɪs/ (UK)| n. |水解|The chemical breakdown of a compound due to reaction with water.|The hydrolysis of starch produces glucose. 淀粉的水解产生葡萄糖。| 近义词: decomposition, degradation | 反义词: dehydration, synthesis | noun
