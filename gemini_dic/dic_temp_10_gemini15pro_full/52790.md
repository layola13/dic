dilettantish:/ˌdɪləˈtæn.tɪʃ/ | adj. | 业余爱好的；浅尝辄止的；不专业的 | Showing superficial interest in an art or a branch of knowledge; amateurish. | He has a dilettantish interest in painting. 他对绘画只有三分钟热度。 | 近义词: amateur, superficial, dabbling | 反义词: professional, expert, accomplished | 词性: 形容词

dilettante:/ˌdɪləˈtæn.ti/ | n. | 业余爱好者；浅尝辄止的人 | A person who cultivates an area of interest, such as the arts, without real commitment or knowledge. | He's a bit of a dilettante as far as music is concerned. 就音乐而言，他不过是个浅尝辄止的爱好者。 | 近义词: amateur, hobbyist, dabbler | 反义词: professional, expert, connoisseur | 词性: 名词

词源: From Italian *dilettante*, present participle of *dilettare* "to delight," from Latin *delectare*.
