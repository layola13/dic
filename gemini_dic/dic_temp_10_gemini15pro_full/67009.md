expirable:/ɪkˈspaɪərəbl/
adjective
可过期的，会失效的
Subject to expiry; able to expire or lapse.
The coupon is expirable at the end of the month. 这优惠券月底到期。

近义词:perishable, temporary
反义词:permanent, everlasting
词性:adjective
