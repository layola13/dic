overcivilizations: /ˌoʊvərˌsɪvɪləˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 过度文明（现象或状态）| The state of being excessively civilized, often implying a loss of vitality, spontaneity, or connection with nature.  |  Some critics argue that modern society suffers from overcivilizations, leading to widespread anxiety and alienation.  一些评论家认为现代社会过度文明化，导致普遍的焦虑和疏离感。 | 近义词: overrefinement, decadence | 反义词: primitiveness, barbarity | 词性: 名词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "civilization" (from Latin "civilis," meaning "citizen," "relating to citizens")
