disclosing:/dɪˈskloʊzɪŋ/
verb, adjective
揭露，透露；公开
revealing something previously unknown; making something known
例句：
He is disclosing confidential information. 他正在泄露机密信息。

The company is disclosing its financial results. 该公司正在公布其财务业绩。

近义词：revealing, divulging, unveiling, exposing
反义词：concealing, hiding, suppressing
词性：Verb (动词), Adjective (形容词)
