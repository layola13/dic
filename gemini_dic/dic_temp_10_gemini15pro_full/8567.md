anticipative:/ænˈtɪsɪpeɪtɪv/
adjective
预期的；预料的；抢先的
Characterized by or showing anticipation; expecting something.
She had an anticipative smile as she waited for her birthday present. 她等待生日礼物时露出了期待的微笑。
The company took anticipative measures to prevent a potential crisis.  公司采取了预期措施来防止潜在的危机。
expectant, predictive, proactive
reactive, unprepared
adjective


anticipate: /ænˈtɪsɪpeɪt/
verb
预期；预料；期望
Regard as probable; expect or predict.
We anticipate a large crowd at the concert. 我们预计音乐会会有很多人。
I anticipate finishing the project by next week. 我预计下周完成这个项目。
foresee, predict, expect
doubt, disregard
