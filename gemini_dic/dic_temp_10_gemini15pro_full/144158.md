outshoot: /ˌaʊtˈʃuːt/
verb
胜过，比...射得远，产量超过
To shoot better or farther than; to surpass in shooting or production.
The Warriors outshot the Cavaliers in the final quarter. 勇士队在最后一节比骑士队投篮得分更多。
outshot: /ˌaʊtˈʃuːt/
verb
产量超过
To exceed in output or production.
This year, our factory outshot its production target by 10%. 今年，我们工厂的产量超过了目标10%。
outshoot: /ˌaʊtˈʃuːt/
verb
比...射得远
To shoot a projectile farther than.
Robin Hood could outshoot all the other archers. 罗宾汉能比其他所有弓箭手射得更远。
近义词: surpass, exceed, outdo, outperform
反义词: underperform, lose to, fall behind
