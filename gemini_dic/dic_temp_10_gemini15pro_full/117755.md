ETYMOLOGY->
From French *mélanure*, from Greek *melas* (black) + *oura* (tail), referring to the black tail feathers of some birds believed to be melancholic.  This word is now obsolete or rare.  Many dictionaries do not list it.  It likely fell out of use in favor of more common synonyms like melancholy.
