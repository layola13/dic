duking:/ˈdjuːkɪŋ/
noun
决斗；争斗
engaging in a fight or contest, especially between rivals of equal status.
The two rivals were duking it out for the championship title.  这两个对手正在为冠军头衔而战。
近义词: fighting, battling, contesting, struggling, competing
反义词: cooperating, yielding, surrendering
词性: n.

duking:/ˈdjuːkɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
决斗；争斗
the present participle form of the verb "duke," meaning to engage in a fight or contest.
They were seen duking it out in the alley.  有人看到他们在小巷里打架。
近义词: fighting, battling, contesting, struggling, competing
反义词: cooperating, yielding, surrendering
词性: v.
