enchain:/ɪnˈtʃeɪn/
verb
束缚，囚禁
To bind or restrain with or as if with chains; fetter.
The prisoners were enchained and led away. 囚犯们被锁链束缚并带走了。
enchain:/ɪnˈtʃeɪn/
verb
吸引住；使着迷
To hold fast as if by chains; captivate.
The speaker's eloquence enchained the audience. 演讲者的口才吸引住了观众。
近义词:fetter, shackle, bind, restrain, captivate, enthrall
反义词:free, liberate, release
词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French enchainer, from en- (“in, on”) + chainer (“to chain”), from Latin catēna (“chain”).
