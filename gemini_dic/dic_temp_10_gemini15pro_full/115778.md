marinorama: /ˌmærɪnəˈrɑːmə/ | n. | 海景画; 海景模型; 海景电影| A large painting or a three-dimensional model representing a seascape, or a film of a seascape. |  Example: The museum's marinorama transported visitors to the shores of the Mediterranean.  博物馆里的海景画将参观者带到了地中海沿岸。

Example: The marinorama depicted a bustling harbor scene with ships coming and going. 这幅海景画描绘了一个繁忙的港口景象，船只来来往往。 | 近义词: diorama, panorama, seascape | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From marine + -orama (a suffix denoting a panoramic view or representation). "Marine" ultimately derives from Latin "marinus," meaning "of the sea." "-orama" comes from Greek "horama," meaning "sight," "spectacle."
