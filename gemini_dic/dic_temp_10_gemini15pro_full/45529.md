noun
教廷官员，罗马教廷的人，（尤指）红衣主教
An official of the papal court, especially a cardinal.
Curialists wielded considerable power in the Vatican.  教廷官员在梵蒂冈掌握着相当大的权力。
The curialist was known for his shrewd political maneuvering.  这位教廷官员以其精明的政治手腕而闻名。
Synonyms: cardinal, prelate, churchman, ecclesiastic
Antonyms: layman, secularist
