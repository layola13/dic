matukas: /mɑːˈtuːkɑːs/
noun
马图卡斯（肯尼亚货币单位，先令的复数形式）
Matukas is the plural form of matuka, a subunit of the Kenyan shilling.  One shilling is equal to 100 matukas (cents).  Though rarely used due to inflation, it technically still exists.
Matukas are no longer commonly used in Kenya due to inflation.  肯尼亚由于通货膨胀，马图卡斯已经不再普遍使用了。
The price was 50 shillings and 50 matukas. 价格是50先令和50马图卡斯。
近义词: cents, senti (Swahili)
反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider larger denominations like shillings or pounds)
