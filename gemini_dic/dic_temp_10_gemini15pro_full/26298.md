butyrin:/ˈbjuːtɪrɪn/ | n. |丁酸甘油酯|A triglyceride of butyric acid. It is found naturally in butter.  It is being investigated for various potential health benefits, including as a source of energy for colon cells.|Butyrin is added to some animal feeds to promote gut health. 丁酸甘油酯被添加到一些动物饲料中以促进肠道健康。|Butyrin supplements are available commercially. 丁酸甘油酯补充剂可在市场上买到。|Synonyms: Tributyrin, Glyceryl tributyrate|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
