disaffiliating:/dɪs əˈfɪlɪˌeɪtɪŋ/
|v.|脱离关系，断绝联系|The act of officially ending a relationship or connection with a larger group or organization.  The union is disaffiliating from the national federation. 工会正在脱离国家联合会。|
|verb|脱离关系，断绝联系|The process of ending an official connection with a group or organization. The local chapter is considering disaffiliating from the national organization due to disagreements over policy.  当地分会由于在政策上的分歧正在考虑脱离国家组织。|
|Synonym:|severing, detaching, withdrawing, disconnecting, breaking away|
|Antonym:|affiliating, joining, uniting, associating, connecting|
|词性:|Verb (present participle)|
