carrying: /ˈkæriɪŋ/ (US) /ˈkæriɪŋ/ (UK)
|v. |搬运；携带；输送；传播；怀着；支撑；持有；意味着|The porter was carrying our luggage.  搬运工正在搬运行李。|
|adj. |承载的；运输的；怀孕的|She has a carrying voice that projects well. 她嗓音洪亮，穿透力强。|
|n. |运送；搬运；运输；负载|The carrying capacity of the bridge is 10 tons. 这座桥的承载能力是10吨。|
Etymology: From Middle English *carien*, from Old North French *carier*, from Vulgar Latin *carricāre*, from Latin *carrus* ("wagon").
Synonyms: transporting, conveying, bearing, holding, supporting
Antonyms: dropping, leaving, abandoning
