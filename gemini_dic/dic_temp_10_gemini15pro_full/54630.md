dismissed: /dɪsˈmɪst/ | v. | 解雇；解散；驳回；不予考虑 | To send someone away or allow them to leave; to reject something, such as an idea or suggestion. | The case was dismissed due to lack of evidence.  案件因缺乏证据被驳回。|  The employee was dismissed for misconduct.  该员工因行为不端被解雇。 | synonyms: discharged, fired, rejected, refused | antonyms: hired, employed, accepted, approved | verb

dismissed: /dɪsˈmɪst/ | adj. |  被解雇的；被解散的；被驳回的 | Having been sent away or allowed to leave; having been rejected. | The dismissed employee filed a lawsuit. 被解雇的员工提起了诉讼。 |  The dismissed case will not be reopened. 被驳回的案件将不会重新审理。 | synonyms: discharged, fired, rejected, refused | antonyms: hired, employed, accepted, approved | adjective
