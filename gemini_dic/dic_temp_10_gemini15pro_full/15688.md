bakshish: /ˈbækʃiːʃ/ (US), /ˈbækʃɪʃ/ (UK)| n.|小费，赏钱，贿赂|A tip or gratuity, especially in some Middle Eastern and Asian countries.  It can also refer to a bribe.|例句：He gave the waiter a generous bakshish. 他给了服务员丰厚的小费。

例句：The official hinted that a little bakshish would expedite the process.  这位官员暗示，一点贿赂可以加快办理进程。|近义词：tip, gratuity, bribe, kickback|反义词:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider penalties for bribery as an opposite concept) |词性: noun
