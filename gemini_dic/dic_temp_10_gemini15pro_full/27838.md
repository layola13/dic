canonic:/kəˈnɒnɪk/ /kəˈnɑːnɪk/| adj.|规范的，经典的，权威的|Following the accepted rules or standards; recognized as genuine or authoritative.|The canonic gospels are Matthew, Mark, Luke, and John. |规范的福音书是马太福音、马可福音、路加福音和约翰福音。|
近义词：canonical, standard, orthodox, authorized, accepted, traditional|反义词：unorthodox, noncanonical, apocryphal, heretical|词性:形容词

canonic:/kəˈnɒnɪk/ /kəˈnɑːnɪk/| n.|经典作品，真作|A work recognized as genuine or authoritative.|Shakespeare's plays are considered canonics of English literature. |莎士比亚的戏剧被认为是英国文学的经典之作。|近义词：classic, masterpiece|反义词：apocrypha|词性:名词
