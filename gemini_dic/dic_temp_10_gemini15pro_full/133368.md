nonextractible:/ˌnɒnɪkˈstræktəbəl/
adjective
不可提取的; 不能被提取的
Not capable of being extracted.
The nonextractible component of the plant makes it unsuitable for certain uses.  这种植物的不可提取成分使其不适合某些用途。
The dye proved to be nonextractible, staining the fabric permanently.  这种染料被证明是不可提取的，永久地染上了织物。
近义词: unrecoverable, irretrievable, nonremovable
反义词: extractible, removable, recoverable
词性: adjective


词源:
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "extractible" (from Latin "extractus," past participle of "extrahere," meaning "to draw out")
