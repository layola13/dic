lust:/lʌst/
n.|欲望，贪欲，渴望|An intense longing or craving, especially for sexual gratification; uncontrolled or illicit sexual desire; a passionate desire for something.|She was consumed by lust for power. 她被对权力的渴望所吞噬。
v.|强烈渴望|To have an intense sexual desire for someone; to have a passionate desire for something.|He lusted after her. 他对她充满了渴望。
近义词:desire, craving, yearning, passion, avarice, greed
反义词:indifference, apathy, aversion, contentment
