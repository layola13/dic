curse:/kɜːrs/ (UK), /kɝːs/ (US)| n. vt. vi.|诅咒，咒骂；祸根，灾星|A solemn utterance intended to invoke a supernatural power to inflict harm or punishment on someone or something.  To use offensive language, especially as an expression of anger.|例句：He muttered a curse under his breath. 他低声咕哝了一句咒骂。|近义词：malediction, imprecation, anathema, hex, jinx, swear, cuss|反义词：blessing, benediction|词性:名词,动词
