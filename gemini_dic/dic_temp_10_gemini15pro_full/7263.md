anglophilisms:/ˈæŋɡləʊfɪlɪzəmz/
n.
亲英主义；对英国文化的喜爱；英语中的英式表达
Words or expressions borrowed from English into another language.  The adoption or imitation of English customs, institutions, or fashions.
Many French chefs sprinkle anglophilisms throughout their menus. 许多法国厨师在他们的菜单中穿插使用英语表达。
