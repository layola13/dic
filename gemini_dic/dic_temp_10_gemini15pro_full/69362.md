federative:/ˈfɛdərətɪv/ | adj. | 联邦的；联盟的；联合的 | Relating to or forming a federation; based on a union of states or organizations. |  The United States has a federative system of government. 美国有一个联邦政府体系。| The federative structure allows for regional autonomy. 联邦结构允许区域自治。 |Synonyms: federal, allied, confederate, united | Antonyms: centralized, unitary | adjective
