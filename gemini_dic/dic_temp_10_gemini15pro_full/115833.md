marketing:/ˈmɑːrkɪtɪŋ/ (US), /ˈmɑːkɪtɪŋ/ (UK)| n.| 市场营销，营销| The process of planning and executing the conception, pricing, promotion, and distribution of ideas, goods, and services to create exchanges that satisfy individual and organizational objectives. |  They're launching a new marketing campaign next month.  他们下个月将要发起新的营销活动。| synonyms: advertising, promotion, branding| antonyms:  None (Marketing doesn't have a direct antonym.  Concepts like "anti-marketing" or "boycott" represent opposition to specific marketing efforts, not the concept itself).| noun
