fashioner:/ˈfæʃənər/| n.|时装设计师；塑造者|Someone who designs or creates fashion, or someone who shapes or molds something.|She was a renowned fashioner of hats, known for her unique and elegant designs. 她是一位著名的帽子设计师，以其独特而优雅的设计而闻名。|近义词：designer, creator, maker, stylist|反义词:destroyer|noun


Etymology: From the verb "fashion" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Fashion" comes from Old French "façon" (manner, shape, form), ultimately from Latin "factio" (a making, doing), from "facere" (to do, make).
