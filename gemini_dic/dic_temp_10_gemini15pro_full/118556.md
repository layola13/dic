mesophyllum:/ˌmɛzəˈfɪləm/| n. |中叶层; 中胶层|A layer of tissue in certain red algae and fungi, especially lichens, lying between the upper and lower cortices.|The mesophyllum of this lichen species is particularly thick, aiding in water retention.|近义词:medulla|反义词:cortex|词性:noun
Etymology:  From meso- (middle) + -phyllum (leaf).  Ultimately from Greek *mésos* (middle) and *phúllon* (leaf).
