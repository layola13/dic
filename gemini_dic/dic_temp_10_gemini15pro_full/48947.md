defy:/dɪˈfaɪ/| n.| 挑战；对抗；|  His act of defiance landed him in jail. 他的反抗行为使他入狱。| challenge, resistance| obedience, submission| noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *desfier, from des- (“dis-”) + fier (“to trust, confide”).  Ultimately from Latin diffīdō (“distrust”).
