clouder:/ˈklaʊdər/| n.| 云的形成者，聚集者；加剧阴沉的人或物|Something that makes something cloudier, or someone who makes a situation more gloomy.| The pollution acted as a clouder, obscuring the view of the mountains. 污染就像云的形成者，遮蔽了山景。|近义词：obscurer, darkener|反义词：brightener, clearer|Noun

clouder:/ˈklaʊdər/| v.|(使)阴沉，(使)混浊，(使)模糊|To make something cloudy or less clear.|  The sky clouded over, and rain began to fall. 天空变得阴沉，开始下雨了。|近义词：obscure, darken, obfuscate|反义词：brighten, clarify|Verb
