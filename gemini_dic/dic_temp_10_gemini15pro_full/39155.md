confuter:/kənˈfjuːtər/
n.|驳斥者；反驳者|Someone who confutes an argument or claim. |The confuter presented strong evidence to disprove the opponent's theory. 驳斥者提出了强有力的证据来反驳对方的理论。|
近义词:opponent, refuter, contradicter, gainsayer|反义词:proponent, supporter, advocate|词性:名词

confute:/kənˈfjuːt/
v.|驳斥；反驳|To prove (a person or an assertion) to be wrong.|The lawyer skillfully confuted the witness's testimony. 律师巧妙地驳斥了证人的证词。|
近义词:refute, disprove, debunk, invalidate|反义词:prove, confirm, validate, corroborate|词性:动词
