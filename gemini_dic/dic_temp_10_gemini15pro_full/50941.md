desight:/dɪˈsaɪt/ |n.| 愚蠢; 缺乏远见; 鲁莽 |A lack of foresight or wisdom; folly. | He showed great desight in investing all his money in one venture. 他把所有的钱都投资在一个项目上，表现出极大的愚蠢。| 近义词: folly, foolishness, imprudence, shortsightedness | 反义词: foresight, wisdom, prudence | 词性: 名词

词源: From Old French *desoit, from des- (expressing negation) + soit (from Latin sit, third person singular present subjunctive of esse "to be").  The original sense was "something that should not be," hence "something inappropriate or foolish."
