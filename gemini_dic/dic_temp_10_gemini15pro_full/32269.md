chide:/tʃaɪd/| v. |斥责，责备|To scold or rebuke someone gently or mildly.|例句：She chided him for his carelessness. 她责备他粗心大意。|例句：The teacher chided the student for being late. 老师斥责学生迟到。|近义词：rebuke, scold, reprimand, reproach|反义词:praise, commend|词性:动词

chide:/tʃaɪd/| n. |斥责，责备 (较少用作名词)|A rebuke or scolding.|例句：His gentle chide made her realize her mistake.  他温和的责备让她意识到了自己的错误。|例句：He received a chide for his inappropriate behavior. 他因行为不当受到了责备。|近义词：rebuke, scolding, reprimand, reproach|反义词:praise, commendation|词性:名词

词源:
From Old English *cīdan, from Proto-Germanic *kīdaną (“to complain, accuse, express sorrow”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰeyd- (“to call, shout”). Cognate with Old Frisian chīda (“to chide”), Middle Dutch kijden (“to quarrel”), German klagen (“to complain”).
