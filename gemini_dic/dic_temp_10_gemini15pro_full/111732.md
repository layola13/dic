loculicidal: /ˌlɒkjʊˈlɪsɪdəl/ | adj. | 沿室背缝线开裂的；室间开裂的 | Splitting along the dorsal suture of the locule (a small cavity or compartment within an organ or part of an organism, especially in an ovary or fruit).  It describes the way a fruit or seed capsule opens. | The ripe seed pods of the lily are loculicidal.  成熟的百合种荚是沿室背缝线开裂的。| The plant's loculicidal capsules release their seeds upon maturity.  这种植物的室间开裂蒴果在成熟时释放种子。 | Near synonyms: septicidal (splitting along the septa), valvular (opening by valves) | Antonym: indehiscent (not opening at maturity) | adjective
