orthodoxies: /ˈɔːθədɒksiz/ (UK) /ˈɔːrθədɑːksiz/ (US) | n. | 正统观念，正统做法 |  Generally accepted beliefs or practices within a group or culture.  |  The scientific community challenged the long-held orthodoxies about the nature of the universe. 科学界对宇宙本质的长期正统观念提出了挑战。 |  The company's rigid orthodoxies stifled innovation.  这家公司僵化的正统观念扼杀了创新。|  dogmas, doctrines, tenets |  heterodoxies, unorthodoxies | noun

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: from Old French orthodoxie, via ecclesiastical Latin from late Greek orthodoxia, from orthos ‘right’ + doxa ‘opinion’.
