nitrifiable:/ˌnaɪtrɪˈfaɪəbl/
| adj. |可硝化的|Capable of being nitrified; convertible into nitrates, especially by the action of bacteria.|The nitrifiable nitrogen content of the soil was tested. 土壤中可硝化氮的含量进行了测试。|The ammonia in the wastewater is nitrifiable. 废水中的氨是可硝化的。|近义词:oxidizable|反义词:non-nitrifiable|词性:形容词
词源:From nitrify + -able.  Nitrify comes from  nitr-  "nitrogen" + -ify.
