captcha:/ˈkæptʃə/| n.|验证码，人机验证|A type of challenge-response test used in computing to determine whether or not the user is human.|Please enter the captcha to verify your identity.  请输入验证码以验证您的身份。|synonyms:  human verification, Turing test|antonyms: None|noun
