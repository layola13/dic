laureations:/ˌlɔːriˈeɪʃnz/
n.
授予桂冠；桂冠仪式；授学位仪式
Ceremonies or the act of conferring an academic degree, especially a doctorate, or of crowning with laurel.
The laureations were held in the university's grand hall.  授学位仪式在大学的大礼堂举行。
The annual laureations are a time of great celebration.  一年一度的授学位仪式是一个盛大的庆祝时刻。
近义词:graduation ceremonies, commencement, conferring of degrees
反义词:(No direct antonym, could consider something like "revocation of degrees")
词性:noun
