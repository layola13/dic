launders:/ˈlɔːndərz/ (US) /ˈlɔːndəz/ (UK)| v. (third-person singular simple present) | 洗涤，清洗（尤指衣物）；洗黑钱 | cleanses, especially clothes; conceals the source of illegally obtained money | He launders his shirts every week. 他每周都洗他的衬衫。| cleans, washes | soils, dirties | verb
launders:/ˈlɔːndərz/ (US) /ˈlɔːndəz/ (UK)| n. (plural) | 洗衣店；洗涤设备 | laundry facilities; equipment for washing clothes | The launders were busy on Saturday. 周六洗衣店很忙。| laundry, laundromat | - | noun


Etymology: From Old French *laver, from Latin lavāre "to wash."
