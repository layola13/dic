cartoonery:/kɑːrˈtuːnəri/
noun
卡通画；卡通风格；卡通创作
the art or style of creating cartoons; exaggerated representation, often for satirical or humorous effect
The political cartoonery in the newspaper was particularly biting this week. 这周报纸上的政治漫画特别辛辣。
synonyms: cartoons, caricatures, lampoons, satires, parodies
antonyms: realism, verisimilitude
