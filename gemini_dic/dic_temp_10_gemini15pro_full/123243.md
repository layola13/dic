monocultures:/ˈmɒnəˌkʌltʃəz/ | n. |单作，单一栽培，单一文化|The practice of growing only one type of crop on a large area of land.  The dominance of a single idea, perspective, or way of life. |Example 1: The reliance on monocultures in agriculture can lead to soil depletion.  农业依赖单作会导致土壤贫瘠。

Example 2: The internet can sometimes create monocultures of thought, limiting exposure to diverse perspectives. 互联网有时会造成思想的单一化，限制人们接触不同观点。|Synonym: uniformity, homogeneity |Antonym: diversity, polyculture, variety | noun
