giveables:/ˈɡɪvəblz/
n.
可赠送物；赠品
Things that are suitable or intended to be given as gifts.
例句：The company handed out giveables at the conference.  这家公司在会议上分发了赠品。

例句：She prepared a basket of giveables for her friends. 她为朋友们准备了一篮子礼物。

近义词：gifts, presents, donations
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用"receipts" 或 "kept items" 表示收到或保留的物品)
词性: 名词
