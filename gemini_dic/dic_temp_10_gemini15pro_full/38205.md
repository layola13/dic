compulsory:/kəmˈpʌlsəri/ | adj. | 必须的，强制的，义务的 | Required by law or a rule; obligatory. |Attendance at school is compulsory for children of certain ages.  某些年龄段的儿童必须上学。|It is compulsory for all motorcyclists to wear helmets. 所有摩托车骑手都必须戴头盔。|近义词：mandatory, obligatory, required |反义词：optional, voluntary|词性:形容词

词源: Late Middle English (in the sense ‘compulsive’): from late Latin compulsorius, from Latin compellere ‘compel.’
