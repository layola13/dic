loneful: /ˈloʊnli/ (US) /ˈləʊnli/ (UK)| adj. |孤独的，寂寞的| Experiencing or characterized by loneliness; sad because one has no friends or company. | She felt loneful in the big city, missing her family and friends. 她在大城市感到孤独，想念她的家人和朋友。|  He spent a loneful evening reading by the fire. 他独自一人在火炉旁读书，度过了一个寂寞的夜晚。| 近义词: lonely, solitary, isolated, lonesome | 反义词: sociable, gregarious, accompanied | 词性: 形容词

词源: From lone + -ful.  "Lone" itself comes from Old English "ān," meaning "one."  The suffix "-ful" means "full of" or "characterized by."
