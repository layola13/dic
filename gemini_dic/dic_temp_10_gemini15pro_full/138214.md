noshed:/nɒʃt/ (UK) /nɑːʃt/ (US)| v. |吃零食，少量地吃|To eat food, especially between meals, in small amounts.|We noshed on some popcorn during the movie. 我们在看电影时吃了一些爆米花。|Synonyms: nibbled, snacked, grazed|Antonyms: gorged, feasted|verb
