marcescent:/mɑːrˈsɛsənt/| adj.| 凋萎的，枯而不落的|Withering but not falling off, as of leaves; persistent.|例句：The oak tree held its marcescent brown leaves throughout the winter.  橡树整个冬天都保留着枯而不落的褐色叶子。

例句：Marcescent leaves provide some protection for the buds beneath. 枯而不落的叶子为下面的芽提供了一些保护。|近义词：withered, faded, desiccated, sere|反义词：fresh, vibrant, verdant|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *marcescens, present participle of marcescere (“to wither, fade, droop”), from marcere (“to be faint, weak, feeble, languid, drooping”).
