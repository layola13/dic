non-latin:/ˌnɑːnˈlætɪn/ | adj. | 非拉丁的，非拉丁语系的 | Not belonging to the group of languages descended from Latin (such as French, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, and Romanian) or written in the Latin alphabet. |  Many non-Latin scripts are used around the world, such as Cyrillic, Greek, and Arabic.  世界上许多地方使用非拉丁文字，例如西里尔字母、希腊字母和阿拉伯字母。| Synonyms: non-Roman, non-Latin-based | Antonyms: Latin, Roman, Latin-based | adjective
