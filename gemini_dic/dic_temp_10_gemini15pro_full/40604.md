convector:/kənˈvɛktər/ | n. | 对流式加热器; 对流器 | A device that heats a room by circulating warm air. |  The convector quickly warmed up the small office. 对流式加热器很快使小办公室暖和起来。

|  Our new house has convectors in every room. 我们的新房子每个房间都有对流式加热器。 | Synonyms: heater, radiator | Antonyms: cooler, air conditioner | noun

convection:/kənˈvɛkʃən/ | n. | 对流 | The transfer of heat by the circulation or movement of the heated parts of a liquid or gas. | Convection currents in the atmosphere cause wind. 大气中的对流气流引起风。

| The oven uses convection to cook food evenly.  烤箱利用对流来均匀地烹饪食物。| Synonyms: circulation, flow | Antonyms: conduction, radiation | noun
