blare: /blɛər/ | v. n. | 响亮地发出; 喧闹声 | to make a loud, harsh sound | The trumpets blared as the king entered. 国王进场时，喇叭大声响起。 | The car horn blared impatiently. 汽车喇叭不耐烦地响着。 | 近义词: blast, roar, bellow, resound | 反义词: whisper, murmur | 词性: 动词, 名词

blare: /blɛər/ | n. | 喧闹声; 刺耳的声音 | a loud, harsh sound | The blare of the foghorn warned ships of the danger. 雾号的刺耳声警告船只注意危险。| The sudden blare of music startled me. 突然响起的音乐声吓了我一跳。 | 近义词: blast, roar, din | 反义词: silence, quiet | 词性: 名词


词源:  From Middle English *blaren, of imitative origin.
