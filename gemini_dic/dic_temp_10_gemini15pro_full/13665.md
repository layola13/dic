attracter:/əˈtræktər/| n. |吸引子，吸引物|Something that attracts someone or something else. |The colorful flowers are attracters for bees. 这些色彩鲜艳的花朵吸引着蜜蜂。|Synonyms: magnet, lure, draw, appeal|Antonyms: repellent, deterrent|Noun

attracters:/əˈtræktərz/| n. |吸引子们，吸引物们|Plural form of "attractor".  Multiple things that attract someone or something else. |Bright lights and loud music are common attracters for young people. 明亮的灯光和喧闹的音乐是吸引年轻人的常见因素。|Synonyms: magnets, lures, draws, appeals|Antonyms: repellents, deterrents|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "attract," which comes from the Latin "attrahere" (to draw to oneself), from "ad" (to) + "trahere" (to draw).
