eco-geographical:/ˈiːkəʊˌdʒiːəˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
生态地理的
Relating to the geographical distribution of organisms in relation to their environment.  It considers both the ecological and geographical factors influencing the distribution and abundance of species.
例句：Eco-geographical factors play a crucial role in shaping biodiversity patterns. 生态地理因素在塑造生物多样性模式中起着至关重要的作用。

例句：The study explored the eco-geographical variations in the species' morphology. 这项研究探讨了该物种形态的生态地理变异。
近义词：biogeographical, ecological
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective


eco-:/ˈiːkəʊ/

geographical:/ˌdʒiːəˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
地理的
Relating to geography or the features of a particular area.
例句：Geographical factors such as altitude and rainfall influence the distribution of plant species.  地理因素，如海拔和降雨量，会影响植物物种的分布。

例句：The geographical location of the city makes it an important trading hub.  这座城市的地理位置使其成为重要的贸易中心。
近义词：spatial, locational
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective
