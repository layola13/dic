govern:/ˈɡʌvərn/ (US) /ˈɡʌvən/ (UK)| v. |统治；管理；支配；控制；影响|To exercise control or authority over; to rule; to administer; to direct; to influence.|The prime minister governs the country. 首相治理国家。|The laws of physics govern the universe. 物理定律支配着宇宙。|近义词：rule, administer, manage, control, direct|反义词：follow, obey, comply|词性:动词

词源: From Old French *governer (Modern French gouverner), from Latin gubernare "to steer, guide, direct, govern," from Greek kybernan "to steer, guide."
