consonantly:/kənˈsɒnəntli/| adv.|协调地，一致地，和谐地|In a manner that is consistent or harmonious; in agreement.|The siblings, despite their differences, worked consonantly towards their shared goal.  兄弟姐妹尽管存在分歧，但仍朝着共同的目标协调一致地努力。|例句：The gears turned consonantly, ensuring a smooth operation. 齿轮协调转动，确保运转顺畅。|近义词：harmoniously, consistently, agreeably, accordingly|反义词：discordantly, inconsistently, disharmoniously|词性:副词
词源: From consonant + -ly. Consonant from Latin consonantem (nominative consonans) present participle of consonare "to sound together," from con- "together" + sonare "to sound," from sonus "sound."
