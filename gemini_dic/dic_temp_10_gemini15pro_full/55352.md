distal:/ˈdɪstəl/| adj.|远端的，末梢的|Situated away from the center of the body or from the point of attachment.|The distal end of the femur connects to the knee.  股骨远端与膝盖相连。|Example:  The pain was located in the distal part of her arm. 疼痛位于她手臂的远端。|Synonyms: peripheral, outermost, furthest, terminal|Antonyms: proximal, central, medial|adjective

distal:/ˈdɪstl/| adj.|末梢的，远离中心的|Located farthest from the origin or point of attachment; peripheral.|Distal nerves are those farthest from the central nervous system.  末梢神经是距离中枢神经系统最远的神经。|Example:  He suffered a distal radius fracture. 他桡骨远端骨折。|Synonyms: outermost, extreme, endmost, terminal|Antonyms: proximal, central, near|adjective
