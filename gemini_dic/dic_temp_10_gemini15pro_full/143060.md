outborn:/ˈaʊtbɔːrn/| adj.|天生的，生来的|Existing from birth; innate.  Derived from "out" (external) and "born" (brought into existence).  It emphasizes a quality present at the time of birth.|例句：Her outborn talent for music was evident from a young age. 她与生俱来的音乐天赋从小就显而易见。

例句：He had an outborn aversion to dishonesty. 他天生厌恶不诚实。|近义词：innate, congenital, inherent, natural|反义词：acquired, learned|词性:adjective
