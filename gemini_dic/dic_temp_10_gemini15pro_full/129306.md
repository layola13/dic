nog:/nɒɡ/| n.| 蛋酒（尤指圣诞节喝的加白兰地或朗姆酒的甜蛋奶酒）|A sweet alcoholic drink made with eggs, typically brandy or rum, and spices, traditionally drunk at Christmas. |例句：He warmed himself with a glass of nog. 他喝了一杯蛋酒暖暖身子。|例句：The traditional Christmas nog was rich and creamy.  传统的圣诞蛋酒浓郁而顺滑。 |近义词：eggnog, Christmas drink|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-alcoholic beverage) |词性:noun
