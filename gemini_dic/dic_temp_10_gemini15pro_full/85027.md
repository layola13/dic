harassment:/ˈhærəsmənt/
| n. |骚扰，困扰|She faced harassment at work due to her gender. 她因为性别在工作中面临骚扰。|
| 英文解析:Aggressive pressure or intimidation.|
| 例句：He was accused of harassment by a former employee. 他被一名前雇员指控骚扰。|
| 近义词：abuse, intimidation, persecution|
| 反义词: encouragement, support |
| 词性: noun |


词源:
From the Old French word "harasser," meaning "to set a dog on," which in turn comes from "harer," meaning "to urge on."  The word evolved to encompass any kind of aggressive or persistent annoyance.
