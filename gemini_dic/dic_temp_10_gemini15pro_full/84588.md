hamperedness:/ˈhæmpərdnɪs/
n.
阻碍，束缚，受限制的状态
The state of being hampered; the condition of being hindered or restricted.
Her hamperedness by the injury prevented her from competing in the race. 她因伤受阻，无法参加比赛。
近义词: hindrance, obstruction, impediment, restriction
反义词: freedom, liberty, ease, facility
词性: 名词


Etymology:
From hampered + -ness.  Hampered itself comes from the verb hamper,  which has uncertain origins, possibly related to a Middle Dutch word meaning "to entangle in a net."
