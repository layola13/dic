assemblies:/əˈsɛmbliz/| n.|集会，会议；装配，组装|A group of people gathered together in one place for a common purpose. Also, the process of putting parts together to make a whole.|例句：The school assemblies are held every Monday morning. 学校集会每周一早上举行。

例句：The car assemblies are done by robots. 汽车组装由机器人完成。|近义词：gatherings, meetings, congregations, constructions|反义词:dispersals, separations, dismantlings|词性:noun
