methodic:/məˈθɒdɪk/ (US) /məˈθɑːdɪk/ (UK)| adj.|有条理的，有条不紊的，井然有序的|Characterized by method and orderliness; systematic.|She has a methodic approach to problem-solving. 她解决问题的方法很有条理。|例句：He is a methodic worker, always planning his tasks carefully. 他是一个有条理的工作者，总是仔细地计划他的任务。|近义词：systematic, organized, orderly|反义词：disorganized, chaotic, haphazard|词性:形容词
词源: Late 16th century: from French méthodique or modern Latin methodicus, from Greek methodos ‘pursuit of knowledge, method’.
