genealogist:/ˌdʒiːniˈælədʒɪst/| n. |系谱学家，家谱学家|A person who studies family history and ancestry.|例句：The genealogist traced her family back to the 17th century.  这位系谱学家追溯了她的家族史到17世纪。

例句：He hired a professional genealogist to research his family tree. 他聘请了一位专业的系谱学家来研究他的家谱。|近义词：family historian, ancestry researcher|反义词:|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *généalogiste*, from Greek *genealogia* "tracing of descent," from *genealogos* "reciting one's descent," from *genos* "race, kind" + *-logos* "one who speaks (in a certain way)," related to *legein* "to speak."
