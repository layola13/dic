hot-desking:/hɒtˈdɛskɪŋ/ | n. |  办公桌轮用；流动办公；共享办公 | The practice of having employees work at any available desk, rather than having a permanently assigned workspace. |  Our company has adopted a hot-desking policy to save space.  我们公司采用了办公桌轮用政策以节省空间。|  Example: Hot-desking allows employees to choose a workspace that best suits their needs for the day.  流动办公允许员工选择最适合他们当天需要的办公空间。 | Synonyms:  flexible working, agile working, shared workspace | Antonyms: fixed desks, assigned seating | noun
