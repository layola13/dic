grouted:/ˈɡraʊtɪd/ |adj.|灌浆的，填缝的|Filled or reinforced with grout (a thin mortar).|The tiles were grouted with a waterproof sealant.  瓷砖用防水密封胶灌浆。|例句：The newly grouted bathroom floor looked pristine.  新灌浆的浴室地板看起来很干净。|例句：The brick wall was grouted with cement. 砖墙用水泥灌浆。|近义词：sealed, filled, cemented|反义词:ungrouted, leaky

词性:
adjective

词源:
From grout + -ed.  From Old French *grote, from Frankish *grūta (“groats, coarse meal”), from Proto-Germanic *grutō (“groats, crushed grain”). Ultimately from the same root as grind.
