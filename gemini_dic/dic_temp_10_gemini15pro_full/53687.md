discipline:/ˈdɪsəplɪn/| n. vt.|纪律；学科；训练；惩罚|A controlled behaviour or way of working; A particular area of study; Training to improve strength or self-control; Punishment intended to correct or train someone|
例句：The teacher maintained discipline in the classroom. 老师在课堂上维持纪律。

例句：He studied the discipline of history. 他学习了历史这门学科。

近义词：order, control, training, punishment, subject, field|反义词：disorder, chaos, freedom, reward|词性:名词,动词


discipline:/ˈdɪsəplɪn/| vt.|训练；惩罚|To train someone to follow rules or to behave in a controlled way; To punish someone in order to correct or train them|
例句：She disciplined her children for their bad behavior. 她因为孩子们的不良行为而惩罚他们。

例句：Athletes must discipline themselves to train effectively. 运动员必须训练自己有效地训练。

近义词：train, punish, correct, chastise|反义词：reward, praise, indulge|词性:动词
