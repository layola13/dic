incurved:/ɪnˈkɜːrvd/
adjective
弯曲的，向内弯曲的
Curved inwards; bent.
The petals of the flower are incurved. 这朵花的花瓣向内弯曲。
近义词:bent, curved, hooked, bowed
反义词:straight, outcurved
词性:adjective
词源:From in- + curved.
