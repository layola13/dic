commiserate:/kəˈmɪzəreɪt/| v. |同情，怜悯，慰问|To express or feel sympathy or pity for someone.|She called to commiserate with him after his dog died. 她打电话慰问他痛失爱犬。|Synonyms: sympathize, empathize, console|Antonyms: disregard, ignore|verb

commiseration:/kəˌmɪzəˈreɪʃn/| n. |同情，怜悯，慰问|An expression of sympathy or pity.|She offered her commiserations on his loss. 她对他的损失表示慰问。|Synonyms: sympathy, condolence, empathy|Antonyms: indifference, apathy|noun
