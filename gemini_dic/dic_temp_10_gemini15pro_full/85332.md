harpooning:/hɑːrˈpuːnɪŋ/
n.|用鱼叉捕鱼，叉住|The act of hunting with a harpoon.  The whale was killed by harpooning.|例句：The harpooning of whales is a controversial practice.  捕鲸是一项有争议的做法。

例句：The documentary showed the brutal harpooning of a large whale.  这部纪录片展示了对一头大鲸鱼残忍的捕鲸过程。


|近义词：spearing, lancing|反义词:releasing|词性:noun (名词),  can also act as a gerund/present participle (动名词/现在分词)
