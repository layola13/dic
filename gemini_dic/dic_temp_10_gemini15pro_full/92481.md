hypotenuses:/haɪˈpɒtənjuːzɪz/ /haɪˈpɑːtənˌjuːsɪz/
|n.|斜边（复数）|The longest side of a right-angled triangle, opposite the right angle.|The hypotenuses of these triangles are all the same length. 这些三角形的斜边长度都相同。|
|近义词:longest side (in a right triangle)|反义词:leg, cathetus|词性:名词|

hypotenuse:/haɪˈpɒtənjuːz/ /haɪˈpɑːtənˌjuːs/
|n.|斜边|The longest side of a right-angled triangle, opposite the right angle.|The Pythagorean theorem states that the square of the hypotenuse is equal to the sum of the squares of the other two sides. 勾股定理指出，斜边的平方等于其他两边的平方和。|
|近义词:longest side (in a right triangle)|反义词:leg, cathetus|词性:名词|
