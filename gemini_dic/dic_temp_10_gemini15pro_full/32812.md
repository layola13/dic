choke-back: /ˈtʃoʊk bæk/ (美式) /ˈtʃəʊk bæk/ (英式)
|phrasal verb|抑制，强忍住(尤指感情)|To suppress (a strong emotion, such as tears or anger) and prevent it from being expressed openly.|He choked back tears as he spoke about his lost dog. 他强忍着泪水，讲述着他丢失的狗。|Synonyms: suppress, stifle, repress, contain, hold back|Antonyms: express, release, vent, unleash|verb

choke-back: /ˈtʃoʊk bæk/ (美式) /ˈtʃəʊk bæk/ (英式)
|noun|抑制，强忍|The act of suppressing a strong emotion.|Her choke-back was evident in the trembling of her voice. 她强忍着(情绪)的举动从她颤抖的声音中显而易见。|Synonyms: suppression, repression|Antonyms: expression, release|noun
