neele:/ˈniːlə/ | n. |靛青，深蓝色|A deep blue color, similar to indigo. |The artist mixed a vibrant neele for the sky in his painting. 艺术家在他的画作中调了一种充满活力的靛蓝色来描绘天空。 |近义词:indigo, sapphire, azure |反义词:pale blue, light blue |词性:名词

词源: From the Middle English *nele*, from Old English *neolu*, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *nīlaz* (meaning "dark blue"), related to Old High German *nila*, Old Norse *nǫl*, and Latin *niger* ("black").  The modern spelling "neele" is a less common variant, with "indigo" being more prevalent.
