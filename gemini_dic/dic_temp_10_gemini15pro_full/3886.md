agreeable:/əˈɡriːəbl/| adj.|令人愉快的，惬意的；同意的，乐意的|Pleasing, giving pleasure or contentment; willing to agree or consent.|例句：We spent an agreeable evening together. 我们一起度过了一个愉快的夜晚。|近义词：pleasant, enjoyable, amiable, affable, willing|反义词：disagreeable, unpleasant, hostile, unwilling|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin *agreabilis, from Latin agere (“to do, act, drive”) + -abilis.
