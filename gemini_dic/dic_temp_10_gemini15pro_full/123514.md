monotoned:/ˈmɒnətəʊnd/ /ˈmɑːnətəʊnd/| adj.|单调的，无变化的|Having a single, unchanging tone or pitch; lacking in variation or interest.|The professor's monotoned lectures made it difficult for the students to stay awake. 教授单调的讲课让学生很难保持清醒。|The background music was monotoned and repetitive. 背景音乐单调且重复。|近义词：dull, tedious, monotonous, boring|反义词：varied, interesting, lively, dynamic|词性:形容词
monotone:/ˈmɒnətəʊn/ /ˈmɑːnətəʊnd/| n.|单调，单音调|A single, unchanging tone or pitch; a lack of variation or interest.|The speaker's monotone made the presentation incredibly boring. 演讲者的单调语气让演讲非常无聊。|The constant monotone of the engine lulled me to sleep.  引擎持续不断的单调声音让我昏昏欲睡。|近义词：monotony, sameness|反义词：variety, diversity|词性:名词
