doggie:/ˈdɒɡi/ (UK), /ˈdɔːɡi/ (US)| n.|小狗，狗狗 (Informal)|A childish or affectionate way of saying "dog".  It's often used when talking to children or expressing fondness for a dog.|例句：Look at the cute doggie!  看看这只可爱的小狗！

例句：My little doggie loves to play fetch. 我的小狗狗喜欢玩捡东西的游戏。


近义词：puppy, dog, pooch|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "cat" in a playful context)|词性:noun
