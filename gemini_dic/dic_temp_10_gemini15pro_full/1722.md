acquaintance: /əˈkweɪntəns/ | n. | 熟人；认识；了解；知道 | A person one knows slightly, but who is not a close friend.  A state of being acquainted; familiarity. | I have a wide circle of acquaintances through work. (我通过工作认识了很多人。)
She has some acquaintance with Spanish. (她懂一点西班牙语。) | 近义词: associate, contact, familiar | 反义词: stranger, enemy | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French acointance, from acointier (“to make known”), from Vulgar Latin *accognitare, from Latin adcognitare (“to get to know”), frequentative of adcognoscere (“to recognize, acknowledge”), from ad- (“to”) + cognoscere (“to know, learn”).

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is an acquaintance of mine from college. = 他是我大学时的熟人。
My acquaintance with him is limited to a few brief meetings. = 我和他只是见过几次面而已。

一般过去时:
I made the acquaintance of several interesting people at the party. = 在聚会上我结识了几位有趣的人。
She renewed her acquaintance with an old friend. = 她和一位老朋友重新取得了联系。

现在完成时:
I have had a nodding acquaintance with her for years. = 我和她点头之交已经好几年了。
We have made the acquaintance of our new neighbors. = 我们已经认识了我们的新邻居。

过去完成时:
I had made his acquaintance several years before. = 我几年前就认识他了。
She had already made the acquaintance of the director before the meeting. = 在会议之前她就已经认识了主管。

将来时:
I will make her acquaintance at the conference next week. = 我将在下周的会议上认识她。
I hope to renew my acquaintance with him soon. = 我希望很快能和他重新联系。

虚拟语气:
If I had made his acquaintance earlier, things might have been different. = 如果我早点认识他，事情可能会有所不同。
I wish I had a wider circle of acquaintances. = 我希望我有更广阔的交际圈。

被动语态:
His acquaintance was made at a business conference. = 他是在一次商务会议上认识的。
Their acquaintance has been renewed after many years. = 他们在多年后重新取得了联系。

疑问句:
Is he an acquaintance of yours? = 他是你认识的人吗？
How did you make his acquaintance? = 你是怎么认识他的？

否定句:
He is not a close friend, just an acquaintance. = 他不是亲密的朋友，只是一个熟人。
I don't have much acquaintance with that subject. = 我对那个主题不太了解。


PHRASE->
make someone's acquaintance = 结识某人
have a nodding acquaintance with = 与...点头之交
renew one's acquaintance with = 与...重新取得联系
a wide circle of acquaintances = 交际圈很广
have some acquaintance with = 对...有所了解
