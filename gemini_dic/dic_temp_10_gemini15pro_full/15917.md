ballsiest: /ˈbɔːlsiɪst/
adjective
最有胆量的；最大胆的；最有种的
Extremely brave and determined; showing a lot of courage and guts.  It's considered vulgar slang.
That was the ballsiest move I've ever seen on a skateboard. 那是我见过在滑板上最大胆的动作。
She made the ballsiest decision of her career. 她做出了她职业生涯中最有胆识的决定。


近义词: gutsy, courageous, daring, audacious, fearless
反义词: cowardly, timid, fearful, apprehensive

词性: adjective
