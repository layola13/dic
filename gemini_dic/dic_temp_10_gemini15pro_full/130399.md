non-virtuous:/ˌnɒnˈvɜːrtʃuəs/| adj.|不道德的，邪恶的|Lacking virtue; not morally good or righteous.  It describes someone or something that doesn't adhere to moral principles.|His non-virtuous behavior led to his downfall. 他的不道德行为导致了他的垮台。|Synonyms: immoral, unethical, wicked, sinful, depraved|Antonyms: virtuous, moral, ethical, righteous, upright|adjective
