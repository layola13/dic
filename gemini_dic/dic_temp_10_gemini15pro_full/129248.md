nocturne:/ˈnɑːktɜːrn/ (US) /ˈnɒktɜːn/ (UK)| n. |夜曲，夜景画|A musical composition, typically for piano, of a pensive, dreamy mood, suggestive of night; a painting of a night scene.| Chopin's nocturnes are particularly famous. 肖邦的夜曲尤其著名。| synonym: serenade, lullaby | antonym:  march, sonata | noun
