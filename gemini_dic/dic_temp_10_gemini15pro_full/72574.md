fluxed:/flʌkst/ | adj. | 熔化的；焊接的；用焊剂处理的 | Treated with a flux; melted or fused. | The metal was fluxed before being molded. 金属在模塑前先熔化了。 | The circuit board components were fluxed and ready for soldering. 电路板元件已涂上焊剂，准备焊接。 | Near synonyms: melted, fused, molten, soldered | Near antonyms: solid, solidified, hardened | adjective

fluxed:/flʌkst/ | v. | 熔化；焊接；用焊剂处理 (past tense of *flux*) | Treated with a flux; melted or fused; fused together.  |  The jeweler fluxed the gold before shaping it. 珠宝商在塑形前熔化了黄金。| The wires were fluxed together. 电线被焊接在一起。| Near synonyms: melted, fused, molten, soldered, joined | Near antonyms: solidified, separated | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fluxus*, past participle of *fluere* "to flow".
