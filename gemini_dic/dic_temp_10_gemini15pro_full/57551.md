dral: /dræl/
n.
空想; 虚构; 想象之物
A figment of the imagination; a chimera.
例句：The monster in the closet was a dral, born of childhood fears.  衣橱里的怪物是一个空想，源于童年的恐惧。

例句：Her perfect world was a dral, unlikely to ever exist. 她的完美世界是一个虚构，不太可能存在。

近义词：chimera, fantasy, illusion, figment
反义词：reality, actuality, fact
词性：名词
