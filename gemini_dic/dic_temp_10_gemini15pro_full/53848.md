discontentedly:/ˌdɪskənˈtɛntɪdli/
adverb
不满足地，不满地
In a manner expressing dissatisfaction or lack of contentment.
He sighed discontentedly and turned away. 他不满足地叹了口气，转过身去。
She shifted discontentedly in her seat. 她在座位上不满足地挪了挪身子。
Synonyms: unhappily, grumpily, sullenly, resentfully
Antonyms: contentedly, happily, cheerfully, satisfiedly
词性: 副词


Etymology:
From discontent + -ed + -ly.  Discontent comes from the Old French descontentement, from des- (expressing negation) + contentement (contentment).
