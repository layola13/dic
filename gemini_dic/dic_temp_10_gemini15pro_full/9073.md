antimilitarists:/ˌæntiˈmɪlɪtərɪsts/
|n.|反军国主义者；反对军事主义的人|People who oppose war and militarism. |The antimilitarists protested the government's increased military spending. 反军国主义者抗议政府增加军费开支。| 近义词:pacifists, peace activists|反义词:militarists, warmongers|Noun

antimilitarist:/ˌæntiˈmɪlɪtərɪst/
|adj.|反军国主义的|Opposed to war and militarism. |She holds strong antimilitarist views. 她持有强烈的反军国主义观点。|近义词:pacifistic, anti-war|反义词:militaristic, pro-war|Adjective

词源:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to  (源自希腊语 anti-)
militarist (noun): a supporter of militarism  (源自拉丁语 militaris, meaning "of soldiers")
-ist (suffix): forming nouns denoting persons who practice or hold certain beliefs
