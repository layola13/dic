geospheres:/ˈdʒiːoʊsfɪrz/
n.
地球圈层
The interacting spheres of the Earth system, including the lithosphere, hydrosphere, atmosphere, and biosphere.
The geospheres are constantly interacting and influencing each other. 地球圈层不断相互作用和影响。
Scientists study the complex interactions within the Earth's geospheres. 科学家研究地球圈层内的复杂相互作用。
近义词: Earth systems, Earth's spheres
反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly "outer space" in a contrasting context)
词性: noun
