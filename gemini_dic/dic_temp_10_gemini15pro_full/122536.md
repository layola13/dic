modding:/ˈmɒdɪŋ/ (US) /ˈmɒdɪŋ/ (UK)| n.| 模组修改，改装 | The act of modifying something, typically a video game or piece of software, to alter its content or functionality. | Example: He spends hours modding his favorite game, adding new characters and levels.  他花了好几个小时修改他最喜欢的游戏，添加了新的角色和关卡。 | Example:  The modding community for that game is incredibly active and creative.  那个游戏的模组社区非常活跃，而且很有创造力。 | Synonym:  modifying, tweaking, customizing, hacking (informal) | Antonym:  leaving as is, keeping original,  preserving | noun
