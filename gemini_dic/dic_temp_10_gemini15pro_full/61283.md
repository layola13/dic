embalmer:/ɪmˈbɑːmər/ | n. |  embalmer | A person who prepares dead bodies for burial or cremation by injecting them with preservatives. | The embalmer prepared the body for the viewing.  殡仪员为瞻仰遗容准备了遗体。 |  mortician, undertaker | N/A | noun
Etymology:  From the French "embaumer" (to embalm), from "en-" (in) + "baume" (balm). Ultimately from the Greek "balsamon" (balsam).
