finance:/ˈfaɪnæns/ /fɪˈnæns/| n. vt.| 财政，金融，资金；为…提供资金|The finance minister announced new tax cuts. 财政部长宣布了新的减税政策。|近义词：funding, capital, investment|反义词:debt, expenditure|noun
finance:/ˈfaɪnæns/ /fɪˈnæns/| vt.| 为…提供资金|The project is financed by the government. 该项目由政府资助。|近义词：fund, capitalize, back|反义词:defund|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *finer ("to end, settle a debt, pay"), from fin ("end"), from Latin finis ("end, boundary, limit"). The meaning shifted from "ransom," to "payment," to "revenue," to "management of money."
