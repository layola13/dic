homochlamydeous:/ˌhɒməʊkləˈmɪdiəs/| adj.|同被的，同花被的|Having a perianth, especially a calyx and corolla, that are similar in appearance;  not differentiated into distinct sepals and petals.  The perianth segments are often referred to as tepals.|The tulip flower is homochlamydeous, with brightly colored tepals.| 近义词:monochlamydeous (sometimes), undifferentiated perianth| 反义词:heterochlamydeous, dichlamydeous|词性:adjective
