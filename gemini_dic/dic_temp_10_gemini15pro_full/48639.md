defending:/dɪˈfɛndɪŋ/| v. | 保卫，防护，辩护 |  Protecting or supporting someone or something from attack, criticism, or other harm. | The soldiers were defending the city from the enemy. 士兵们正在保卫城市免受敌人攻击。|近义词：protecting, guarding, shielding, advocating |反义词:attacking, assaulting |词性:动词
defending:/dɪˈfɛndɪŋ/| adj. | 防御的，保护的 | Used to describe something that is designed or intended to defend or protect. |  A defending champion is the current holder of a title. 卫冕冠军是当前的头衔持有者。|近义词：protective, guarding |反义词:offensive, attacking |词性:形容词


词源: From Old French *defendre, from Latin dēfendere (to ward off, protect), from de- (down, away) + fendere (to strike, push).
