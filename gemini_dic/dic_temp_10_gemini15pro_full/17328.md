be-sought-after: /biː ˈsɔːt ˌæftər/ | adj. | 受欢迎的，抢手的 |  Highly desired or in demand.  It describes something that many people want or are trying to obtain. | This antique vase is highly be-sought-after by collectors.  这个古董花瓶深受收藏家的追捧。|  The tickets to the concert were be-sought-after.  这场音乐会的门票一票难求。|近义词: desirable, coveted, popular, in demand |反义词: undesirable, unpopular, unwanted |词性:形容词

词源:  "Be-" is a prefix indicating a state or condition. "Sought" is the past participle of the verb "seek," meaning "to try to find or get something." "After" is a preposition indicating pursuit or desire.  Therefore, "be-sought-after" literally means "being sought after" or "being pursued."
