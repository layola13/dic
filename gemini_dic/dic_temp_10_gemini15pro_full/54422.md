disinfectant:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfektənt/| n. adj.|消毒剂，消毒的|A chemical liquid that destroys bacteria.|例句：The disinfectant used in hospitals must be very strong. 医院里使用的消毒剂必须非常强效。|近义词：antiseptic, germicide, sterilizer|反义词：contaminant|词性:名词, 形容词

disinfectant:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfektənt/| adj.|消毒的|Having the property of destroying or inhibiting the growth of microorganisms, especially pathogenic bacteria.|例句：Disinfectant wipes are useful for cleaning surfaces. 消毒湿巾对清洁表面很有用。|近义词：antiseptic, germicide, sterilizing|反义词：contaminating|词性:形容词
