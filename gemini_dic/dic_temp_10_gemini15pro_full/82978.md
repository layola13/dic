gunmetal:/ˈɡʌnˌmetl/| n. adj.|炮铜色，青铜色|A dark grey colour, like that of gunmetal.|The car was painted a sleek gunmetal grey.|近义词：charcoal, slate, pewter|反义词:bright, vibrant|词性:名词，形容词
Etymology: From gun + metal, referring to the alloy traditionally used in making cannons.
