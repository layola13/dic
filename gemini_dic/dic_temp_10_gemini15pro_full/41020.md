copastor:/ˈkoʊˌpæstər/| n.|共同牧师，联合牧师|A copastor is a pastor who shares the responsibility of leading a church with another pastor or pastors. |例句：Pastor John and Pastor Mary serve as copastor of the church.  约翰牧师和玛丽牧师担任教会的共同牧师。

例句：The copastor team worked together to create a welcoming environment. 共同牧师团队一起努力创造一个友好的环境。|近义词：co-pastor, associate pastor, joint pastor|反义词: None|词性:noun
