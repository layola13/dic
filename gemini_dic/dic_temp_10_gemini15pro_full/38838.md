confabulatory:/kənˈfæbjʊlətɔːri/ /kənˈfæbjəˌlɛtɔri/
adjective
虚构的，编造的，错乱记忆的
Relating to or characterized by confabulation, the act of filling in gaps in memory with fabricated stories and details, often without conscious awareness of doing so.
The patient's confabulatory narratives were often quite elaborate and detailed. 病人的虚构故事通常非常详尽和细致。
confabulatory symptoms began to emerge after the accident. 事故发生后，开始出现错乱记忆的症状。
Synonyms: fabricated, invented, delusional, illusory, fictitious, made-up, untrue
Antonyms: factual, true, real, genuine, authentic
词性: adjective
