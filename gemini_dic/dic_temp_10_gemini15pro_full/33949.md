cirrostomi:/sɪˈrɒstəmaɪ/ /sɪˈrɑːstəˌmaɪ/
n.
圆口纲动物
Jawless vertebrates characterized by a circular mouth, including lampreys and hagfish.
Lampreys and hagfish are classified under Cirrostomi.  七鳃鳗和盲鳗被归类为圆口纲。
The cirrostomi are an ancient group of fish. 圆口纲是一种古老的鱼类。
近义词:Cyclostomata
反义词:Gnathostomata
词性:noun
