haum:/hɔːm/ | n. | home; dwelling; residence | A place of residence; the place where one lives permanently, especially as a member of a family or household.  It can also refer to a person's native land or town. From Old Norse *heimr, meaning "world, dwelling." Related to German *heim, Gothic *haims.  The word evolved from a broader sense of "world" to the more specific "dwelling" over time. |  She returned to her childhood haum after years abroad. 她多年后回到了她童年的家。| synonyms: home, abode, dwelling, residence, domicile | antonyms:  exile, diaspora| noun
