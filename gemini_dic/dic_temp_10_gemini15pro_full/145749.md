overculling:/ˌoʊvərˈkʌlɪŋ/
n.
过度捕杀，过度剔除
The act of culling too many animals or things, resulting in a depletion of the population or resource.
Overculling of deer has led to a decline in their numbers. 过度捕杀鹿导致它们的数量下降。
近义词:overhunting, overfishing, excessive culling
反义词:underculling, insufficient culling
