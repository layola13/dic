greenwash:/ˈɡriːnwɒʃ/ (UK) /ˈɡriːnwɑːʃ/ (US)| n. v. |漂绿；洗绿|Disinformation disseminated by an organization so as to present an environmentally responsible public image.|The company was accused of greenwashing after it was revealed that its "eco-friendly" products were actually harmful to the environment.  该公司被揭露其“环保”产品实际上对环境有害后，被指控为漂绿。|近义词：eco-fraud, green sheen|反义词：environmental responsibility, sustainability|词性: noun, verb
