deintegrate:/diːˈɪntɪɡreɪt/
|v.|取消种族隔离；使分解；使瓦解|To reverse the process of integration; to break up or decompose something that was previously integrated.|The government decided to deintegrate the school system. 政府决定取消学校系统的种族隔离。|The complex molecule began to deintegrate under high heat. 在高温下，复杂分子开始分解。|近义词：disintegrate, segregate, separate, dismantle|反义词：integrate, combine, unite|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
de- (prefix): meaning "reverse" or "remove," from Latin
integrate (verb): from Latin integratus, past participle of integrare "to make whole," from integer "whole, complete"
