besmearer:/bɪˈsmɪrər/
noun
涂抹者；诽谤者
someone who smears something or someone; someone who spreads false and damaging information about someone
The besmearer was caught on camera defacing the monument.  涂抹者在破坏纪念碑时被摄像头拍到。
The politician denounced his opponent as a besmearer of reputations.  这位政治家谴责他的对手是一个诽谤者。
smearer, dauber, slanderer, defamer
