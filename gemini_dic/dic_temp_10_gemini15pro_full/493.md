abo:/ˈeɪboʊ/| n.|原住民，土著居民|A person who is Aboriginal; an indigenous inhabitant of Australia.|例句：The abos have lived on this land for thousands of years. 土著居民在这片土地上生活了数千年。|近义词：Aboriginal, indigenous person|反义词:settler, colonist|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
Shortened from "Aborigine," from Latin *ab origine*, meaning "from the beginning."

USAGE->
This term is now widely considered offensive.  It is best to use "Aboriginal people" or "Indigenous Australians" instead.  Providing usage examples would perpetuate the use of this offensive term.
