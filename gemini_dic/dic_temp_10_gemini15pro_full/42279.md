counter-embattled:/ˌkaʊntər ɪmˈbætld/| adj.|反设垛口的，相对设防的|Having battlements facing or opposing each other, as in two fortifications or castles. This signifies a state of mutual defense or opposition.|The two counter-embattled castles glared at each other across the valley.|Synonyms: opposed, confronting, facing|Antonyms: allied, united|Adjective
