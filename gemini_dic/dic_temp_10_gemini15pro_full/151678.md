Relating to or involving the division of something into parts, especially a country or territory.
The partitionary agreement led to the creation of two independent states.  该分区协议导致了两个独立国家的建立。
The partitionary wall separated the two rooms. 分区墙隔开了两个房间。

反义词: unifying, joining, merging
