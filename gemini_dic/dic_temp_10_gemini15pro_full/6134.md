amiabler:/ˈeɪmiəblər/ | adj. | 更和蔼的，更友善的 |  Comparative form of amiable: more amiable. amiably:/ˈeɪmiəbli/ | adv. | 和蔼地，友善地 | In a friendly and pleasant manner. | She smiled amiably at the guests. 她对客人和蔼地笑了笑。| synonyms: agreeably, affably, genially, pleasantly | antonyms: disagreeably, unaffably, ungenially, unpleasantly | adverb
