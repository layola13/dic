disertitude:/ˌdɪsəˈtjuːd/| n.|论文；专题学术论文；尤指博士论文|A long piece of writing on a particular subject, especially one that is done in order to receive a doctor's degree from a university.|Example: She is writing her dissertation on the history of feminist thought. 她正在写关于女权主义思想史的博士论文。

Example: He spent years researching his dissertation on the effects of climate change on coastal communities. 他花了好几年时间研究关于气候变化对沿海社区影响的博士论文。|近义词：thesis, treatise, monograph, essay, paper|反义词: (No direct antonym for a formal piece of writing)|词性:noun
