galactophagous:/ˌɡæləktˈɒfəɡəs/
adjective
食乳的，以乳汁为食的
Feeding on milk.
例句：The newborn kitten is galactophagous, relying entirely on its mother's milk.  新生小猫咪是食乳的，完全依赖母乳。

例句：Certain marsupials have a galactophagous stage in their development.  某些有袋动物在其发育过程中有一个食乳阶段。

近义词：lactating (for the mother), milk-fed,  nursing
反义词：N/A (Difficult to have a direct opposite for a dietary habit)
词性：adjective

Etymology:
From Greek *gala*, *galaktos* "milk" + *-phagos* "eater," from *phagein* "to eat."
