mechanisation:/ˌmɛkənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |机械化|The process of introducing machines or automatic devices into a process, activity, or place. |The mechanisation of agriculture has greatly increased crop yields. 农业机械化大大提高了农作物产量。|Synonyms: automation, industrialization|Antonyms: manual labor, hand labor|noun
