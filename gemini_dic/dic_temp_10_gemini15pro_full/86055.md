heaped:/hiːpt/| adj.|堆满的，堆积的|piled up; loaded high; filled above the brim|例句：A heaped tablespoon of sugar. 一满勺糖。|近义词：piled, stacked, laden, overflowing|反义词:level, flat, scant, sparse|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From heap + -ed.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *haupōną (“to cut, hew”).
