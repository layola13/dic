balling:/ˈbɔːlɪŋ/ | adj. | 极好的，非常棒的；赚大钱的；哭得很伤心的 | Extremely good, excellent; making a lot of money; crying intensely |  例句：He's balling out there on the court! 他在球场上表现得非常出色！

例句：They've been balling ever since they started their own business. 自从他们开始创业以来，他们就一直在赚大钱。

近义词：excellent, great, fantastic, amazing, superb, thriving, prosperous, weeping, sobbing | 反义词：terrible, awful, poor, failing, struggling | 词性：形容词

balling:/ˈbɔːlɪŋ/ | v. |  哭得很伤心；玩得很开心；取得巨大成功 | Crying intensely; having a great time; achieving great success | 例句：She was balling her eyes out after the breakup. 分手后，她哭得死去活来。

例句：They were balling at the club last night. 他们昨晚在俱乐部玩得很开心。

近义词：crying, weeping, sobbing, partying, celebrating, succeeding, thriving | 反义词：laughing, rejoicing, failing, struggling | 词性：动词


词源：balling 源于动词 ball，原意指“滚成球状”。在俚语中，它逐渐演变出多种含义，例如哭泣时眼泪滚落，像球一样；玩得开心，像球一样充满活力；以及取得巨大成功，像滚雪球一样越滚越大。
