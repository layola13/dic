contrastiveness:/kənˈtræstɪvnəs/| n.|对比性，对照性|The contrastiveness of the two approaches is evident. 这两种方法的对比性很明显。|例句：The contrastiveness of the two paintings highlights the different styles of the artists.  这两幅画的对比性突出了艺术家们不同的风格。

例句：The study explored the contrastiveness of various language learning methods. 这项研究探讨了各种语言学习方法的对比性。|近义词：difference, distinction, contrast, dissimilarity|反义词：similarity, likeness, resemblance|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From contrast + -ive + -ness.  Contrast ultimately comes from the Latin prefix *contra-* (against) and *stare* (to stand).
