hokkaido:/hɒˈkaɪdoʊ/ (US), /hɒˈkaɪdəʊ/ (UK)| n. |北海道|A large island, the northernmost and second largest of Japan's main islands, known for its natural beauty, volcanoes, ski resorts, and seafood.|I'm going to Hokkaido for a ski trip next winter. 我明年冬天要去北海道滑雪旅行。|Synonym:  North Island (less precise), Ezo (historical)|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, could consider Kyushu, the southernmost main island)|Noun
