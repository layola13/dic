gasped:/ɡæspt/ | v. | 喘息，倒吸气 |  To inhale suddenly and sharply, as in shock or surprise. | She gasped when she saw the blood. 她看到血时倒吸了一口气。| synonyms: inhaled sharply, breathed quickly, choked,  | antonyms: exhaled, breathed slowly | verb
