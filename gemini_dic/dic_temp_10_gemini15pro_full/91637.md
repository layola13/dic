hyperbolic:/ˌhaɪpərˈbɒlɪk/ (US) /ˌhaɪpəˈbɒlɪk/ (UK)| adj.|夸张的；双曲线的|Relating to hyperbole; exaggerated. |例句：He made hyperbolic claims about his product's effectiveness. 他对他的产品功效夸大其词。|近义词：exaggerated, inflated, overstated|反义词：understated, moderate, realistic|词性:形容词

hyperbola:/haɪˈpɜːrbələ/ (US) /haɪˈpɜːrbələ/ (UK)| n.|双曲线|A symmetrical open curve formed by the intersection of a circular cone with a plane at a smaller angle with its axis than the side of the cone.|例句：The graph of the equation is a hyperbola. 这个方程的图象是一条双曲线。|近义词：(In a figurative sense) exaggeration|反义词: (No direct antonym for the mathematical concept)|词性:名词

hyperbole:/haɪˈpɜːrbəli/ (US) /haɪˈpɜːrbəli/ (UK)| n.|夸张法|Exaggerated statements or claims not meant to be taken literally.|例句：The salesman's claim that the car could fly was pure hyperbole.  销售员说这辆车能飞，纯属夸张。|近义词：exaggeration, overstatement|反义词：understatement|词性:名词
