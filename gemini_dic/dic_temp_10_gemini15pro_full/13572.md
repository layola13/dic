attenders:/əˈtɛndərz/
n.
出席者，参加者
People who are present at a meeting, event, etc.
The attenders at the conference were mainly from the IT industry.
会议的出席者主要来自IT行业。

The wedding attenders wished the happy couple well.
婚礼的参加者祝愿这对幸福的夫妇一切顺利。

近义词:participants, attendees, audience, guests
反义词:absentees

词性:noun
词源:From the verb "attend" (from Old French atendre, from Latin attendere, from ad- "to" + tendere "to stretch"), + -er (suffix forming nouns denoting persons who perform an action).
