hyposplenic:/ˌhaɪpoʊˈsplɛnɪk/ /ˌhaɪpəʊˈspliːnɪk/
adjective
脾功能减退的; 脾功能不全的
Having reduced or absent spleen function.  Relating to or affected by hyposplenism.
The patient's hyposplenic state made him more susceptible to infections.  这位病人脾功能减退的状态使他更容易感染。
The doctor considered the hyposplenic patient at high risk for certain bacterial infections. 医生认为脾功能低下的病人患某些细菌感染的风险很高。
Near Synonyms: asplenic, hyposplenistic
Antonyms: hypersplenic, hypersplenism
word type: adjective
