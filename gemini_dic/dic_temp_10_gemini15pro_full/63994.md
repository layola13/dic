epistolary: /ɪˈpɪstəˌleri/ /ɪˈpɪstəˌlɛri/
noun, adjective
书信体的；书信集
Relating to or denoting the writing of letters; (as noun) a novel told through the medium of letters written by one or more of the characters.
Example:  "an epistolary novel" 她写了一部书信体小说。 She wrote an epistolary novel.
Example: "The Perks of Being a Wallflower" is a coming-of-age epistolary novel.  《壁花少年》是一部书信体的成长小说。
Synonym: letter-based, correspondence-based
Antonym: non-epistolary
word type: noun, adjective
