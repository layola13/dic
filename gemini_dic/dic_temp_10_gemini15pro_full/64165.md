equanimity:/ˌɛkwəˈnɪməti/ /ˌiːkwəˈnɪməti/
n.
平静；镇定；沉着
Mental calmness, composure, and evenness of temper, especially in a difficult situation.
She accepted both the good and the bad with equanimity. 她平静地接受了好与坏。
近义词：composure, calmness, tranquility, serenity
反义词：anxiety, agitation, distress, panic
词性: 名词
