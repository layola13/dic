billiard:/ˈbɪljərd/ | n. adj. | 台球，撞球;  |A game played on a rectangular table covered with green felt, in which two or more players use cues to strike balls into pockets along the rails of the table. Also used as an adjective to describe things related to the game. | He enjoys playing billiard. 他喜欢打台球。 |  近义词: pool, snooker | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑其他类型的游戏，例如 chess 国际象棋) | 词性: 名词, 形容词
