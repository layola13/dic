bluebelled:/ˈbluːbɛld/| adj.|开着蓝铃花的，点缀着蓝铃花的|Covered or adorned with bluebells (typically used to describe a wood or landscape).|The bluebelled wood was a magical place in spring. 春天，开满蓝铃花的树林是一个神奇的地方。|The path wound through a bluebelled meadow. 小路蜿蜒穿过开满蓝铃花的草地。|近义词：flowery, blooming|反义词:barren, desolate|词性:形容词

词源:  "bluebell" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives). "Bluebell" itself combines "blue" with "bell," referring to the flower's color and shape.
