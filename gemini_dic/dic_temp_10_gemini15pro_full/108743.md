leaps:/liːps/| v. n.| 跳跃，飞跃；激增|A sudden, energetic jump or quick movement.  A sudden large change or increase.|The cat leaps onto the table. 猫跳到桌子上。|近义词：jumps, bounds, springs, soars|反义词:falls, plummets, declines|词性:动词(第三人称单数), 名词(复数)
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hlēapan (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *hlaupaną (“to leap, run”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)lew- (“to slip, slide, flow”). Cognate with Old Frisian hlēpa (“to leap”), Middle Dutch lopen (“to run”), Old High German loufan (“to run”), German laufen (“to run”), Old Norse hlaupa (“to leap, run”).
