headscarf:/ˈhedˌskɑːrf/ (US), /ˈhedˌskɑːf/ (UK)| n. |头巾，头纱|A piece of cloth worn over the head, often for religious or cultural reasons, or simply as a fashion accessory. |She wore a colorful headscarf to protect her hair from the sun. 她戴了一条色彩鲜艳的头巾来保护头发免受阳光照射。

She tied a headscarf around her head before going out in the cold.  她在出门受寒之前，用一条头巾裹住了头。

|近义词：scarf, head covering, veil, bandana|反义词: None|词性: Noun
