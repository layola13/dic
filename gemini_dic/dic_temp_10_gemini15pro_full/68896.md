fastidiousness:/fæˈstɪdiəsnəs/| n.|一丝不苟；挑剔；讲究|Excessive attention to detail and a tendency to be overly critical and demanding.|Her fastidiousness about cleanliness was well-known. 她对清洁的挑剔是众所周知的。|近义词：meticulousness, scrupulousness, choosiness|反义词：carelessness, sloppiness|noun

fastidious:/fæˈstɪdiəs/| adj.|一丝不苟的；挑剔的；讲究的|Very attentive to and concerned about accuracy and detail.|He is fastidious about his clothes. 他对衣着很讲究。|近义词：meticulous, scrupulous, choosy|反义词：careless, sloppy|adjective
