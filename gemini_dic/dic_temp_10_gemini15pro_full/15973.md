baltica:/ˈbɔːltɪkə/ | adj. n.| 波罗的海的；波罗的海地区 | Relating to the Baltic Sea or the Baltic States; the Baltic region itself. | The Baltica craton is one of Earth's oldest continental crust segments.  波罗的海克拉通是地球上最古老的大陆地壳部分之一。

The Baltica region is known for its amber. 波罗的海地区以其琥珀而闻名。 | 近义词：Baltic | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology:  From Latin *Balticus, derived from the name of a large island in the region mentioned by Pliny the Elder as *Baltia.  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bhel- (white, shining).
