botch:/bɑːtʃ/ /bɒtʃ/| n. vt.|拙劣的工作；笨拙地修补；弄糟|A botch is a piece of work that is done badly, or a mistake that spoils something. |例句：The builder made a real botch of the roof. = 建筑工人把屋顶弄得一团糟。

The operation was a botch. = 手术搞砸了。|近义词：mess, bungle, ruin, spoil|反义词：mend, repair, fix|词性：名词，动词

botch:/bɑːtʃ/ /bɒtʃ/| vt.|拙劣地修补；弄糟|To botch something means to spoil it by doing it badly. |例句：He botched the repair job. = 他把修理工作搞砸了。
