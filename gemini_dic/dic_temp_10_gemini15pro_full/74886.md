freshens:/ˈfrɛʃənz/
v.
使…清新；使…精神焕发；更新；使…恢复生气
Makes something cleaner, cooler, or newer; revives.
The cool breeze freshens the air. 凉爽的微风使空气清新。
Adding some mint freshens the drink. 加一些薄荷可以让饮料更清爽。
refresh, revive, renew, revitalize
stale, tire, exhaust
verb

PHRASE->
freshen up (v.): to wash and make yourself look clean and tidy  梳洗打扮，使显得干净整洁
freshen up a room (v.): to make a room look more attractive by cleaning it, adding flowers, etc.  通过清洁、添加鲜花等方式使房间看起来更具吸引力
