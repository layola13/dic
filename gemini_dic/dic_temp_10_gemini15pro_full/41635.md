correctingly:/kəˈrektɪŋli/
adv.
正确地，纠正地
in a way that corrects something that is wrong.
He correctingly pointed out the error in my calculations. 他正确地指出了我计算中的错误。
近义词: accurately, properly, rightly
反义词: incorrectly, wrongly, inaccurately
词性: 副词

词源:
From the adverb "correct" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Correct" comes from the Latin "correctus," past participle of "corrigere" (to make straight, to set right), from "com-" (together, thoroughly) + "regere" (to guide, rule).
