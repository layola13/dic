bonduc:/ˈbɒndʌk/ | n. |灰毛豆，尼克尔豆|A tropical climbing shrub (Caesalpinia bonduc) having prickly stems and branches and bearing two-seeded pods with gray seeds.  These seeds are also called bonduc nuts and are used in traditional medicine and for making jewelry. |The bonduc nuts were collected and strung into a necklace. 灰毛豆的果实被收集起来串成一条项链。|Synonyms: nicker nut, knicker nut|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
