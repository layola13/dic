anorexic: /ˌænəˈreksɪk/ | adj. n. | 厌食的；厌食症患者 |  Relating to or suffering from anorexia nervosa, an eating disorder characterized by an abnormally low body weight, an intense fear of gaining weight, and a distorted perception of weight or shape. |  She was diagnosed as anorexic. 她被诊断为厌食症。|  synonyms: emaciated, gaunt, skeletal, thin, underweight | antonyms: obese, overweight | adjective

anorexic: /ˌænəˈreksɪk/ | n. | 厌食症患者 | A person who suffers from anorexia nervosa. | The anorexic was hospitalized for treatment.  这位厌食症患者住院接受治疗。| synonyms:  anorexia nervosa sufferer | antonyms: compulsive eater | noun
