cobalt:/ˈkoʊbɔːlt/| n. adj.|钴, 钴蓝色|A hard, ferromagnetic, silver-white, metallic element, resembling iron and nickel in chemical and physical properties.  It is used in alloys for permanent magnets and high-speed steels and in cobalt blue pigment.  As an adjective, it refers to the deep blue color.|The artist used cobalt paint for the sky. (adj.)|近义词:azure, sapphire (for the color)|反义词: (No direct antonym for the element itself. For the color, perhaps drab, muted)|词性:noun, adjective
