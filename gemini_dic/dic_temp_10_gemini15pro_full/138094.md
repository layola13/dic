normatively:/ˈnɔːrmətɪvli/
adv.
规范地；从规范角度
in a way that relates to or is based on norms or standards, especially of behavior.
例句：Normatively, stealing is wrong. 从规范上讲，偷窃是错误的。

例句：The behavior was judged normatively unacceptable. 这种行为被规范地判定为不可接受。

近义词：prescriptively, ideally, typically, standardly
反义词：descriptively, atypically, abnormally
词性：副词
