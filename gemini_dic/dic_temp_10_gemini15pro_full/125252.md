multidiscipline:/ˌmʌltiˈdɪsəplɪn/| adj. n.|多学科的，多学科|Involving or drawing on several disciplines.|例句：The project requires a multidiscipline approach, incorporating expertise from engineering, design, and marketing. 这个项目需要多学科方法，结合工程、设计和营销方面的专业知识。

例句：Multidiscipline research is becoming increasingly important in addressing complex global challenges. 多学科研究在应对复杂的全球挑战中变得越来越重要。|近义词：interdisciplinary, cross-disciplinary|反义词：monodisciplinary, single-discipline|词性:形容词/名词


Etymology:
From multi- (meaning "many") + discipline.  "Multi-" comes from Latin "multus" meaning "much, many."  "Discipline" comes from Latin "disciplina" meaning "instruction, knowledge, learning, military discipline."
