hyperflexible:/ˌhaɪpərˈflɛksəbəl/
adjective
过度灵活的；极度柔韧的
Excessively or extremely flexible; able to bend or stretch beyond the normal range.
She's a hyperflexible gymnast, able to contort her body into incredible positions. 她是一位极度柔韧的体操运动员，能够将她的身体扭曲成令人难以置信的姿势。

Her hyperflexible joints allow her to do yoga poses most people can only dream of. 她极其灵活的关节使她能够做出大多数人只能梦想的瑜伽姿势。

近义词: double-jointed, supple, pliable, elastic
反义词: stiff, rigid, inflexible

词性: adjective
