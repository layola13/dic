passerine:/ˈpæsəraɪn/ |adj. n.| 雀形目的；雀形目鸟类 |Relating to or characteristic of the largest order of birds, Passeriformes, which includes perching birds and songbirds, distinguished by having feet adapted for perching.  Also, a bird of this order.| The robin is a common passerine. 知更鸟是一种常见的雀形目鸟类。| synonyms: perching bird, songbird | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps flightless bird as a contrast) | adjective, noun
