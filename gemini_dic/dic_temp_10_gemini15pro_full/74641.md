free-falling:/ˈfriːˌfɔːlɪŋ/
adjective
自由落体的
Falling freely under the influence of gravity, typically without a parachute.
The free-falling object gained speed rapidly.  自由落体的物体速度迅速增加。
The skydiver was free-falling for several seconds before deploying his parachute. 跳伞运动员在打开降落伞之前自由落体了几秒钟。
Synonyms: plummeting, plunging, dropping
Antonyms: rising, ascending
adjective


free-falling:/ˈfriːˌfɔːlɪŋ/
verb
自由落体
The act of falling freely under the influence of gravity.
The stuntman free-falled from the building. 特技演员从大楼上自由落体下来。
The BASE jumper free-falled from the cliff. 定点跳伞者从悬崖上自由落体下来。
Synonyms:  plummeting, plunging, dropping
Antonyms: rising, ascending
verb


free-fall:/ˈfriːfɔːl/
noun
自由落体
The state of falling freely under the influence of gravity.
The skydiver was in free-fall before opening his parachute. 跳伞运动员在打开降落伞之前处于自由落体状态。
The object experienced a brief period of free-fall. 物体经历了短暂的自由落体。
Synonyms: plunge, drop
Antonyms: ascent, rise
noun

Etymology:  "free" + "fall".  "Free" in this sense means unrestricted. "Fall" refers to the downward movement caused by gravity.
