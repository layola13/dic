amnesty: /ˈæmnɪsti/ | n. | 大赦，特赦 | An official pardon for people who have been convicted of political offenses. |  The government granted amnesty to all political prisoners. 政府给予所有政治犯特赦。

The rebels were granted amnesty after they surrendered their weapons. 叛乱分子交出武器后获得了特赦。 | 近义词: pardon, reprieve, forgiveness | 反义词: conviction, punishment, prosecution | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *amnistie* (modern French *amnistie*), from Latin *amnestia*, from Greek *amnēstía* "forgetfulness (especially of wrongdoings), amnesty," from *amnēstos* "forgotten, not remembered; pardoned," from *a-* "not" + *mnēstos* "remembered," verbal adjective of *mnasthai* "to remember."
