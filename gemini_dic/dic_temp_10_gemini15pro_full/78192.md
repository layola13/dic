geosystems:/ˈdʒiːoʊˌsɪstəmz/
noun
地球系统
Earth systems; the interconnected components of the Earth, including the geosphere, hydrosphere, atmosphere, and biosphere, and the processes that link them.
The class is studying the complex interactions within geosystems. 这门课正在研究地球系统内的复杂相互作用。
Geosystems are crucial for understanding climate change. 地球系统对于理解气候变化至关重要。
Synonyms: Earth systems, environmental systems
Antonyms: N/A
