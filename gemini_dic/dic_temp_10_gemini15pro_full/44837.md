crustaceousness:/krʌˈsteɪʃəsnəs/
|n.| 甲壳性; 甲壳类动物的特征 |The crustaceousness of the lobster was evident in its hard shell.| 龙虾的甲壳性在其坚硬的外壳中显而易见。|
|近义词: shell-like, hard-shelled|
|反义词: soft-bodied|


ETYMOLOGY->
From crustaceous + -ness.  Crustaceous comes from Late Latin crustaceus "having a shell, crust," from Latin crusta "crust, shell."
