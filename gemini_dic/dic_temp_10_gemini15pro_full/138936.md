nutshell:/ˈnʌtʃɛl/| n.| 坚果壳，简而言之，概要|a summary of something said or written using few words|The problem, in a nutshell, is money.  简而言之，问题就是钱。|synonym: summary, gist, essence, core, epitome|antonym: elaboration, detail, expansion|noun

nutshell:/ˈnʌtʃɛl/| n.| 坚果壳，简而言之，概要|a hard outer covering of a nut|The squirrel cracked open the nutshell. 松鼠咬开了坚果壳。|synonym: shell, casing, husk|antonym: kernel, meat (of a nut)|noun
