finch:/fɪntʃ/| n. |雀科鸣鸟|A small songbird with a short, stout beak, belonging to the family Fringillidae.|例句：A finch perched on the bird feeder. 一只雀鸟栖息在喂鸟器上。

例句：The male finch has brightly colored plumage. 雄性雀鸟有鲜艳的羽毛。|近义词：bird, songbird|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑predator 捕食者) |词性:noun
