gymnosomata:/ˌdʒɪmnoʊˈsoʊmətə/ | n. | 裸体翼足类 |  A taxonomic order of pelagic sea slugs, also known as sea angels, characterized by their wing-like parapodia used for swimming and the absence of a shell in the adult stage.  They are carnivorous, feeding primarily on other pteropods. |  Gymnosomata are found in all oceans, from the tropics to the poles. 裸体翼足类分布于所有海洋，从热带到两极。 |  近义词: sea angels, pteropods | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps shelled mollusks) | 词性: Noun

Etymology: From Greek *gymnos* (naked) + *soma* (body), referring to their lack of a shell.
