nondismemberments:/ˌnɒndɪsˈmɛmbəmənts/ | n. | 未肢解，未分割 | The treaty ensured the nondismemberment of the smaller nations. |  The museum's policy emphasizes the nondismemberment of ancient artifacts, preserving them whole. | 近义词: preservation, wholeness, integrity | 反义词: dismemberment, division, fragmentation | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (not) + "dismemberment" (the act of cutting or tearing something into pieces). "Dismemberment" comes from the Old French "desmembrement," from "desmembrer" (to dismember), ultimately from Latin "membrum" (limb).
