anti-degradation:/ˌæntiˌdeɡrəˈdeɪʃən/| adj.|反退化，防降级|Preventing something from becoming worse in quality.  It often refers to environmental regulations that prevent the decline of existing environmental resources.|The Clean Water Act has anti-degradation provisions to protect existing high-quality waters.|近义词:preservation, conservation, protection|反义词:degradation, deterioration|词性:adjective

anti-degradation:/ˌæntiˌdeɡrəˈdeɪʃən/| n.|反退化，防降级|The act or process of preventing degradation.|The state's commitment to anti-degradation is evident in its strict environmental policies.|近义词:preservation, conservation, protection|反义词:degradation, deterioration|词性:noun
