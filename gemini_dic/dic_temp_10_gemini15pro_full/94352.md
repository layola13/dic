impiousnesses:/ɪmˈpaɪəsnɪsɪz/| n. |不虔诚，不敬，亵渎|The state of being impious; lack of reverence or respect for God or sacred things. |His impiousnesses shocked the devout community. 他的不敬行为震惊了虔诚的社区。|The king was enraged by the impiousnesses of his subjects. 国王被臣民的不敬激怒了。| 近义词：irreverence, blasphemy, sacrilege, profaneness, ungodliness | 反义词：piety, reverence, devotion, holiness | 词性: 名词

impious:/ɪmˈpaɪəs/| adj. |不虔诚的，不敬的，亵渎的|Not showing respect or reverence, especially for God; wicked. |It was considered impious to question the priest. 质疑神父被认为是不敬的。|Her impious remarks offended many believers. 她不敬的言论冒犯了许多信徒。| 近义词：irreverent, blasphemous, sacrilegious, profane, ungodly | 反义词：pious, reverent, devout, holy | 词性: 形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from Latin impius, from in- ‘not’ + pius ‘dutiful’.
