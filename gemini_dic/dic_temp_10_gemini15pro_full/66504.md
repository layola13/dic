exfoliate: /ɛksˈfoʊliˌeɪt/ (American) /ɛksˈfəʊliˌeɪt/ (British)
verb
脱落；去角质
To remove dead skin cells from the surface of the skin.
She exfoliates her skin twice a week. 她每周去角质两次。
The outer layers of the rock naturally exfoliate due to weathering. 由于风化作用，岩石的外层自然脱落。
近义词: peel, shed, slough
反义词: accumulate


词性: v.

词源:  From Latin exfoliātus, past participle of exfoliāre (“to strip off leaves”), from ex (“out, off”) + folium (“leaf”).
