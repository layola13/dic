marionettist:/ˌmærɪəˈnɛtɪst/ | n. | 木偶表演者，操纵木偶的人 | A person who operates marionettes (puppets manipulated by strings). | The marionettist skillfully brought the wooden figures to life. 木偶表演者巧妙地将木偶赋予了生命。 |  The marionettist manipulated the strings with precision, making the puppet dance gracefully across the stage. 木偶表演者精确地操纵着绳子，使木偶在舞台上优雅地舞动。 | synonyms: puppeteer | antonyms: audience, spectator | noun
