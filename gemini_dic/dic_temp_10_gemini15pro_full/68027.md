factoring:/ˈfæktərɪŋ/| n.|因式分解；保理|The process of finding the factors of a mathematical expression or a number.  It also refers to a financial transaction and a type of debtor financing in which a business sells its accounts receivable (invoices) to a third party called a factor at a discount.|He learned about factoring in algebra class. 他在代数课上学了因式分解。|synonyms: factorization, decomposition|antonyms: multiplication, expansion|noun

factoring:/ˈfæktərɪŋ/| n.|保理|A financial transaction and a type of debtor financing in which a business sells its accounts receivable (invoices) to a third party called a factor at a discount.|The company used factoring to improve its cash flow. 这家公司利用保理业务来改善现金流。|synonyms: accounts receivable financing, invoice discounting|antonyms: lending, borrowing|noun
