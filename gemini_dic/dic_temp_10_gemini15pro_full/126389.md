myrosin:/ˈmaɪrəsɪn/ | n. | 黑芥子酶 | An enzyme found in plants of the Brassicaceae family (e.g., mustard, horseradish, cabbage) that catalyzes the hydrolysis of glucosinolates to produce isothiocyanates, which are responsible for the characteristic pungent flavor and odor of these plants. |  Myrosinase plays a key role in the plant's defense mechanism. 黑芥子酶在植物的防御机制中起着关键作用。

Myrosin is released when the plant tissue is damaged. 当植物组织受损时，黑芥子酶就会被释放出来。 | 近义词: thioglucosidase | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
