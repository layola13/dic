ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English abregen, abreggen, from Old French abregier, abreger (“to shorten, abridge”), from Latin abbreviō (“I shorten”), from ad (“to”) + brevis (“short”).

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He often abridges long articles for the newspaper. = 他经常为报纸删节长篇文章。
She abridges classic novels for younger readers. = 她为年轻读者删节经典小说。

一般过去时:
The editor abridged the manuscript before publication. = 编辑在出版前删节了手稿。
They abridged the documentary for television broadcast. = 他们为电视播出删节了纪录片。

现在完成时:
The author has abridged his own novel for a new edition. = 作者已经为他自己的小说新版本做了删节。

过去完成时:
