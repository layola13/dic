deviants:/ˈdiːviənts/| n.|离经叛道者；不正常的人；异常值|People who deviate significantly from societal norms are often labeled as deviants.  那些严重偏离社会规范的人通常被贴上离经叛道者的标签。|例句：The deviants were ostracized by the community. 这些离经叛道者被社区排斥了。

deviants:/ˈdiːviənts/| n.|偏差值；异常值|In statistics, deviants are data points that are significantly different from the mean. 在统计学中，异常值是与平均值显著不同的数据点。|例句：The researchers identified several deviants in the data set. 研究人员在数据集中发现了几个异常值。


近义词：nonconformists, mavericks, outcasts, eccentrics, anomalies|
反义词：conformists, traditionalists|
词性: noun (plural)|

词源：From Late Latin devians, present participle of deviare "to turn aside," from de- "from" + via "way."|
