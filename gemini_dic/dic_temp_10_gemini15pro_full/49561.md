demedicate:/diːˈmɛdɪkeɪt/
verb
使脱离药物治疗；停止用药
To discontinue medication for someone; to wean someone off medication.
The doctor decided to demedicate the patient after seeing positive improvements in their condition. 医生看到病人病情好转后决定停止用药。
After years of being on various medications, she decided to slowly demedicate herself under the supervision of a naturopath.  在服用各种药物多年后，她决定在自然疗法医生的监督下慢慢停止用药。
近义词: wean (off), discontinue, cease, stop
反义词: medicate, treat (with medication)
词性: verb

词源: de- (prefix meaning "removal" or "reversal") + medicate (verb meaning "to treat with medicine")
