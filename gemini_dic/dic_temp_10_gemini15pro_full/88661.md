hoariness:/ˈhɔːrɪnəs/ /ˈhorɪnəs/
noun
灰白；白霜；古老
the state of being covered with white hairs or a white, powdery substance, or something that looks like this.  Also used to describe something very old.
The hoariness of his beard gave him a venerable appearance. 他的胡须灰白，使他显得德高望重。

Synonyms: whiteness, grayness, age, antiquity
Antonyms: youth, newness, blackness
