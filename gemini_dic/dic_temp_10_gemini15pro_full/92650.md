-ible:/ɪbl/ | adj. | 可...的，能...的 |  A suffix meaning "capable of, worthy of, tending to, fit for," used in forming adjectives.  It corresponds to Latin adjectives ending in -ibilis. |  This metal is malleable, which means it's flexible and fusible. 这种金属具有延展性，这意味着它是柔韧的且易熔的。| The solution is divisible by two.  这个方案可以被二整除。 | 近义词: -able, capable, possible | 反义词: impossible, incapable | 词性: 后缀
