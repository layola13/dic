defoliations:/ˌdiːfoʊliˈeɪʃənz/| n. |落叶; 落叶(现象); 除叶; |The act of removing leaves from a plant or tree, or the state of having leaves removed. |The defoliations caused by the insects weakened the trees, making them more susceptible to disease. 昆虫造成的落叶削弱了树木，使它们更容易生病。|近义词：defoliation, leaf fall, leaf drop|反义词:foliation|noun
