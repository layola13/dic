fumaric:/fjuːˈmærɪk/| adj.|富马酸的；延胡索酸的|Relating to or derived from fumaric acid.|Fumaric acid is used in the food industry. 富马酸用于食品工业。|近义词: butenedioic (technical)|反义词: maleic (isomer)|词性: adjective

fumarate:/ˈfjuːməreɪt/| n.|富马酸盐；延胡索酸盐；富马酸酯；延胡索酸酯|A salt or ester of fumaric acid.|Iron fumarate is a common iron supplement. 富马酸铁是一种常见的铁补充剂。|近义词: butenedioate (technical)|反义词: maleate (isomer salt/ester)|词性: noun
