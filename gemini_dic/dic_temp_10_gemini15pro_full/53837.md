ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *disconsolat*, from Latin *disconsolatus*, from *dis-* (“not”) + *consolatus* (“comforted”), past participle of *consolari* (“to comfort, console”), from *con-* (“with, together”) + *solari* (“to comfort, soothe”).
