fattener:/ˈfætnər/ | n. |使（某事物）更有趣、更令人印象深刻或更成功的事物|Something that makes something more interesting, impressive, or successful.|The exciting climax was a real fattener for the otherwise dull movie.  激动人心的高潮真正让这部原本沉闷的电影增色不少。|Synonyms: enhancer, booster, improvement |Antonyms:  spoiler, detriment | noun
