decollectivizes: /ˌdiːkəˈlɛktɪvaɪzɪz/
verb
取消集体化; 私有化
To reverse the process of collectivization, typically by returning land or businesses to private ownership.
The government decollectivized agriculture in the 1990s. 政府在20世纪90年代取消了农业集体化。
The reforms decollectivized many formerly state-owned farms.  这些改革将许多以前的国有农场私有化了。
近义词: privatize, individualize
反义词: collectivize, nationalize
词性: verb

词源:  "de-" (prefix meaning "reverse" or "remove") + "collectivize" (from "collective" + "-ize").  The root "collective" ultimately comes from the Latin "colligere," meaning "to gather together."
