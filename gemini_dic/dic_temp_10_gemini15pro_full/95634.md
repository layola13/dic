incompetent: /ɪnˈkɑːmpɪtənt/ | adj. | 无能的，不胜任的 | Not having the necessary skills or ability to do something successfully. |  He was fired for being incompetent. 他因不称职而被解雇了。

The incompetent worker caused a lot of problems.  这个无能的工人造成了很多问题。

近义词: incapable, unskilled, inept, unqualified | 反义词: competent, capable, skilled, proficient | 词性: 形容词
