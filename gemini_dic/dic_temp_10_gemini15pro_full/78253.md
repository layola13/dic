germaine:/dʒɜːrˈmeɪn/| adj.|有关的，相关的|Relevant, pertinent, relating to the subject at hand. |例句：Her remarks were germane to the discussion. 她的发言与讨论密切相关。

例句：The evidence was not germane to the case.  这个证据与案件无关。


近义词：relevant, pertinent, applicable, appropriate, apposite|反义词:irrelevant, extraneous, inappropriate|词性:形容词
