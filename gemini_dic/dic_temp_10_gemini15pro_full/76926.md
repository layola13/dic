gaoling: /ˈɡaʊlɪŋ/ | n. | 高岭; 高岭土; 陶土 | Kaolin, a fine white clay used in ceramics and medicine. It's named after Gaoling (高岭), a mountain in Jingdezhen, China, where it was originally mined. | The finest porcelain is made from gaoling.  最好的瓷器是用高岭土制成的。 | Synonym: kaolin, china clay | Antonym: N/A | Noun
