falsie:/ˈfɔːlsi/ (US), /ˈfɔːlsi/ (UK)| n.|假睫毛; 垫子|An artificial device, especially a pad of some material, used to create a fuller or more shapely appearance.|She wore falsies to make her eyelashes look longer. 她戴着假睫毛让睫毛看起来更长。|Synonyms: false eyelashes, eyelash extensions|Antonyms: natural eyelashes|noun
