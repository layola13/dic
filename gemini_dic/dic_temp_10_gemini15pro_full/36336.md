coiffuring: /kwɑːˈfjʊərɪŋ/ | n. |  理发，做头发 | The act of styling or arranging hair. | She spent hours on her coiffuring before the gala.  她在晚会前花了几个小时做头发。 | 近义词: hairstyling, hairdressing | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

词源: From French "coiffure," from Old French "coife," meaning a cap or headdress.
