noninhibitive:/ˌnɒnɪnˈhɪbɪtɪv/
adjective
非抑制性的
Not inhibiting or restraining; allowing or encouraging free expression or activity.
This creates a noninhibitive atmosphere for learning. 这创造了一个轻松自由的学习氛围。
The drug has a noninhibitive effect on the central nervous system. 这种药物对中枢神经系统没有抑制作用。
近义词:  permissive, unrestrained, liberating, freeing
反义词: inhibitory, restrictive, suppressive
词性: adjective
词源:  non- (not) + inhibitive (tending to inhibit)
