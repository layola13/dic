chronogrammatist:/ˌkrɒnəˈɡræmətɪst/ | n. | 年份诗作者，纪年铭文作者 | A person who composes or studies chronograms.  A chronogram is a phrase or sentence in which certain letters, when interpreted as Roman numerals, express a date or epoch. |  The chronogrammatist skillfully embedded the date of the building's completion into the inscription above the doorway.  这位年份诗作者巧妙地将建筑竣工日期嵌入门口上方的铭文中。|  Synonym: chronographer | Antonym: N/A | noun
