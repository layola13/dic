cantalever:/ˈkæntilɪvər/ (US), /ˈkæntilɪːvər/ (UK)| n. adj. v.|悬臂，悬臂梁；|A projecting beam or structure supported at only one end.|例句：The balcony is a cantilever.  阳台是一个悬臂结构。|例句：The bridge was built with a cantilever design. 这座桥采用了悬臂式设计。|近义词：projection, overhang, bracket|反义词:support, pier|词性:名词, 形容词, 动词

cantalever:/ˈkæntilɪvər/ (US), /ˈkæntilɪːvər/ (UK)| v.|使成悬臂；以悬臂梁支撑|To support by a cantilever; to project like a cantilever.|例句：They cantilevered the balcony out over the garden. 他们把阳台悬挑到花园上方。|例句：The architect cantilevered the roof to create a covered walkway. 建筑师将屋顶悬挑出来，形成一个有顶棚的走道。|近义词：project, extend, overhang|反义词:support|词性:动词
