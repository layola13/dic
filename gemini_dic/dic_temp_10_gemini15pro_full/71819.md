fleer:/flɪr/| vi. n.|嘲笑，讥讽；逃走；嘲笑，轻蔑的言语|To laugh or grimace in a way that shows scorn or contempt; to run away.|He fleered at their attempts to improve the system. 他嘲笑他们试图改进系统的做法。|近义词：mock, sneer, scoff, jeer, gibe, deride, scorn, contempt, disdain|反义词：respect, admire, praise, commend|词性:动词,名词

fleer:/flɪr/| n.|嘲笑，轻蔑的言语|A mocking or contemptuous look or remark.|His face twisted into a fleer. 他的脸扭曲成一副嘲笑的表情。|近义词：mockery, sneer, scoff, jeer, gibe|反义词：compliment, praise|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *flerien, from Old Norse *flīra (“to grin, make grimaces”).
