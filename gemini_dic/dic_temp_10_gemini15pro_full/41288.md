cordwainer:/ˈkɔːrdweɪnər/| n.|鞋匠|A shoemaker, especially one who makes new shoes and boots from leather.|例句：The cordwainer carefully stitched the leather upper to the sole. 鞋匠仔细地把鞋面缝到鞋底上。

例句：My grandfather was a skilled cordwainer, crafting bespoke footwear for the town's elite. 我的祖父是一位技艺精湛的鞋匠，为镇上的精英阶层制作定制鞋履。|近义词：shoemaker, cobbler|反义词: None|词性:noun
