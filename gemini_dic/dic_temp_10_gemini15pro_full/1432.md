ace-out:/eɪs ˈaʊt/
verb
淘汰，险胜
To narrowly defeat or eliminate someone, often in a competitive situation.
He aced out his rival in the final round of the tournament. 他在锦标赛的最后一轮险胜了他的对手。

She aced out the other candidates for the job. 她险胜其他候选人，得到了这份工作。

近义词: edge out, narrowly defeat, outperform
反义词: lose to, be defeated by
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "ace," meaning a playing card with a single spot, signifying high value or excellence, combined with "out," meaning to remove or surpass.  The original meaning referred to winning a trick in card games with an ace, thus eliminating opponents.
