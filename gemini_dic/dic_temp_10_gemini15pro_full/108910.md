ledging:/ˈlɛdʒɪŋ/
n.
壁架; 岩架; 窗台;
A narrow horizontal surface projecting from a wall, cliff, or other surface.
The climber found a precarious ledging on the cliff face. 攀岩者在悬崖面上找到了一个危险的岩架。
shelf, ledge, sill, projection
cavity, recess
