drg:/drʌɡ/| v. (dragged, dragging)|拖，拉，拽|To pull something along the ground, often with difficulty.|He dragged the heavy suitcase across the floor.|She dragged herself out of bed, feeling exhausted.|近义词:pull, haul, tow|反义词:push, carry|动词
