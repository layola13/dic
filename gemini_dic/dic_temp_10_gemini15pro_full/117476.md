medjidie: /məˈdʒiːdiə/ | n. | 麦吉迪勋章; 麦吉迪币(一种旧时的土耳其金币) | A Turkish order instituted in 1852 by the sultan Abdülmecid I, awarded in five classes for distinguished military or other service. Also, a former Turkish gold coin. |  He was awarded the Medjidie for his bravery in battle. 他因在战斗中的英勇表现而被授予麦吉迪勋章。 |  Synonym: decoration, medal, award | Antonym: (For the medal) dishonor, disgrace (For the coin) debt | noun
