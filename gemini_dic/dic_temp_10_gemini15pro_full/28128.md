capitative:/ˈkæpɪteɪtɪv/
adjective
按人头计算的,人头费的
Relating to or involving a payment or fee based on the number of individuals, especially in healthcare, where a fixed amount is paid per patient.
Capitative payments encourage doctors to see more patients.  按人头付费鼓励医生诊治更多病人。

Capitation payments are common in managed care organizations.  按人头付费在管理式医疗机构中很常见。

近义词:per capita, per head
反义词:fee-for-service
词性:adjective
