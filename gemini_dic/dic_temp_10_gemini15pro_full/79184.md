glamourising:/ˈɡlæməraɪzɪŋ/
v.
美化；使更有魅力；给…增添魅力
The advertisement was glamourising smoking, making it seem like a sophisticated and desirable habit.  这则广告美化了吸烟，使其看起来像是一种成熟而令人向往的习惯。
verb, present participle
美化；使更有魅力；给…增添魅力
Making something seem more attractive or exciting than it really is.
The movie glamorises the life of a rock star.  这部电影美化了摇滚明星的生活。
Synonyms: romanticizing, idealizing, glorifying
Antonyms: deglamorising, demythologizing, exposing
