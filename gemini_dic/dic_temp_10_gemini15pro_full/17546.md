beatified:/biˈætɪfaɪd/
adjective
被宣福的；荣获至福的
Declared by the Catholic Church to be among the blessed in heaven, and thus entitled to public veneration in a specified region or religious community.
Mother Teresa was beatified in 2003.  特蕾莎修女于2003年被宣福。
近义词:blessed, consecrated, hallowed
反义词:condemned, damned
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin beatificatus, past participle of beatificare "to make supremely happy," from Latin beatus "happy" + -ficare, combinining form of facere "to make."
