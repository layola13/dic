nonheme:/ˌnɑːnˈhiːm/ | adj. | 非血红素的 | Not containing heme, the iron-containing component of hemoglobin. |  Nonheme iron is found in plant-based foods. 非血红素铁存在于植物性食物中。

Nonheme iron is less readily absorbed than heme iron. 非血红素铁不如血红素铁容易吸收。 | 近义词: non-heme | 反义词: heme | 词性: adjective
