nonrationalizations:/ˌnɒnˌræʃənəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ /ˌnɑːnˌræʃənəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/
|n.|非理性化，不合理化的解释|The absence of justifications or explanations that aren't based on logic or reason.  The act of avoiding or rejecting rationalizations.|
例句：His nonrationalizations for his behavior were baffling. 他对其行为的非理性解释令人费解。

例句：The therapist helped him confront his nonrationalizations and address the underlying issues.  治疗师帮助他正视自己不合理的解释并解决根本问题。
|近义词：illogical explanations, unjustified actions, unreasoned behavior|
反义词：rationalizations, justifications, logical explanations|
词性:名词
