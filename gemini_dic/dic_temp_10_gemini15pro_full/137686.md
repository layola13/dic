nonuprightnesses:/ˌnɒnʌpˈraɪtnɪsɪz/
|n.|不正直，不正当行为|Lack of uprightness; dishonesty or immoral behavior.  A state of being not upright, either literally or figuratively.|His nonuprightnesses in business dealings eventually led to his downfall. 他在商业交易中的不正当行为最终导致了他的垮台。|Synonyms: dishonesty, corruption, immorality, wickedness, wrongdoing|Antonyms: honesty, integrity, righteousness, morality, virtue|noun
