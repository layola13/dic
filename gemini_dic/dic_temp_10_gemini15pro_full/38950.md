confiders:/kənˈfaɪdərz/ | n. | 信任者，吐露心事者 | People who confide in others; those entrusted with secrets. |  She told her closest confiders about her plans. 她把她的计划告诉了她最亲密的知己。| 近义词: confidant, intimate, friend | 反义词: stranger, enemy | 词性: 名词

词源:  From the verb "confide," which comes from the Latin "confidere," meaning "to trust fully."  The "-er" suffix denotes the person who performs the action of the verb.
