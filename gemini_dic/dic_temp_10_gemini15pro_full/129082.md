nitrophenol:/ˌnaɪtroʊˈfiːnɒl/ /ˌnaɪtroʊˈfiːnɔːl/| n.|硝基苯酚|An organic compound with the formula HOC6H4NO2.  It is an aromatic compound containing a hydroxyl group and a nitro group attached to a benzene ring. There are three isomers of nitrophenol: ortho-nitrophenol, meta-nitrophenol, and para-nitrophenol.|Para-nitrophenol is used as an indicator in titrations. 对硝基苯酚在滴定中用作指示剂。|Nitrophenols are intermediate products in the production of many dyes. 硝基苯酚是许多染料生产的中间产物。|synonyms:  hydroxy-nitrobenzene|antonyms: N/A|noun
