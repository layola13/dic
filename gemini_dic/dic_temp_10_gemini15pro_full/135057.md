nonnormative:/ˌnɒnˈnɔːmətɪv/ | adj. | 非规范的，非标准的 | Not conforming to a norm or standard, especially of behavior or social interaction.  Describing something that deviates from or challenges established norms or standards. Often used in sociology, psychology, and other social sciences. |  Her nonnormative behavior caused a stir at the formal event. 她的非规范行为在正式场合引起了轰动。 |  The study explored nonnormative family structures. 这项研究探讨了非规范的家庭结构。 | Synonyms: atypical, deviant, unconventional, irregular, unorthodox | Antonyms: normative, standard, conventional, typical, orthodox | adjective
