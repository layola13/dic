bewilderedly: /bɪˈwɪldərdli/ | adv. | 困惑地，茫然地 | In a bewildered manner; showing confusion and uncertainty. | She looked at him bewilderedly, trying to understand what he meant. 她困惑地看着他，试图理解他的意思。|  He stumbled out of the building bewilderedly, unsure of where to go. 他茫然地跌跌撞撞地走出大楼，不知道该去哪里。| 近义词: confusedly, perplexedly, dazedly | 反义词: clearly, knowingly, confidently | 词性: 副词

词源: From the adjective "bewildered" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Bewildered" comes from the verb "bewilder," which originated in the late 16th century. It's formed from "be-" (a prefix meaning "thoroughly") and "wilder" (to lead astray, confuse), which is related to "wild."
