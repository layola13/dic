ensnarl:/ɪnˈsnɑːrl/ /ɛnˈsnɑːrl/
verb
纠缠；使...陷入困境；使...缠绕
to cause to become entangled in or as in a snare. To involve in a perplexing or troublesome situation.
The fishing net ensnarled the dolphin.  渔网缠住了海豚。
Bureaucracy ensnarled the project for months.  官僚主义使这个项目拖延了数月。
近义词: entangle, trap, snare, tangle
反义词: disentangle, free, release
词性: verb

ensnarl:/ɪnˈsnɑːrl/ /ɛnˈsnɑːrl/
noun
纠缠；缠绕；困境
A tangled or confused state or condition.
The vines created an ensnarl at the edge of the forest.  藤蔓在森林边缘形成了一片缠绕。
The legal ensnarl delayed the sale of the property.  法律纠纷推迟了房产的出售。
近义词: tangle, entanglement, snare, trap
反义词: order, freedom
词性: noun
