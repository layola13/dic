electrolyze: /ɪˈlɛktrəˌlaɪz/ | vt. | 电解 | To decompose a chemical substance by passing an electric current through it. |  The water was electrolyzed to produce hydrogen and oxygen.  水被电解生成氢气和氧气。

Copper sulfate solution can be electrolyzed using copper electrodes.  可以使用铜电极电解硫酸铜溶液。| 近义词: decompose, separate | 反义词: synthesize, combine | 词性: 动词


词源: electro- (electricity) + -lyze (to loosen, dissolve)
