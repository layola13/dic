oiled:/ɔɪld/
adjective
涂了油的；油滑的
covered or lubricated with oil; operating smoothly and efficiently.
The oiled machine ran smoothly.  涂了油的机器运转顺畅。
The politician gave an oiled speech. 这位政客发表了一场油滑的演说。
近义词:greased, lubricated, smooth
反义词:dry, unlubricated, rough
词性:adjective

词源：From oil + -ed.  来自 oil + -ed（过去分词后缀，表示“被…的”）。
