mandate:/ˈmænˌdeɪt/ | n. vt.|授权，命令；委托管理；强制执行|An official order or commission to do something.|The government has a mandate to reduce poverty. 政府有责任减少贫困。|Synonyms: order, command, instruction, directive|Antonyms: prohibition, forbiddance|noun, verb
