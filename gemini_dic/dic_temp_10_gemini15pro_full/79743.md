glume:/ɡluːm/ | n. |颖，谷壳；外稃 |The glume is the tough outer covering of a grain of cereal such as wheat or barley. | The glumes protect the developing seed.  颖片保护正在发育的种子。| synonyms: husk, chaff, hull | antonyms: kernel, grain | noun
