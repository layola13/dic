parsism:/ˈpɑːrzɪzəm/ | n. | 拜火教;琐罗亚斯德教 |  The belief system and practices of Zoroastrianism, an ancient monotheistic religion originating in Persia (modern-day Iran).  |  Parsism is one of the world's oldest monotheistic religions. 拜火教是世界上最古老的一神教之一。 |  Zoroastrianism | None | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Parsi + -ism.  Parsi refers to the Zoroastrians of India, while Zoroastrianism is the broader term for the religion itself.  The word Parsi derives from Pars, the historical name of a region in Persia.
