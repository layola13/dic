alarmable:/əˈlɑːməbl/| adj.|易惊恐的，容易担心的|Capable of being alarmed; easily frightened or worried.|The news was so alarming that she became quite alarmable.|近义词：apprehensive, anxious, nervous, uneasy, worried, skittish|反义词：calm, composed, relaxed, tranquil|词性:形容词
词源: From alarm + -able. Ultimately from the Italian all'arme "to arms!"
