liberation:/ˌlɪbəˈreɪʃn/| n.|解放，释放|The act of setting someone free from imprisonment, slavery, or oppression; release.|The liberation of France from Nazi occupation was a momentous event. 法国从纳粹占领下的解放是一个重大事件。|近义词：freedom, release, emancipation, deliverance|反义词:captivity, confinement, oppression, subjugation|词性:noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *līberātiōn-*, from *līberāre* "to set free," from Latin *līber* "free."
