diamantiferous:/ˌdaɪəˈmænˈtɪfərəs/
adjective
含钻石的；产钻石的
Containing or yielding diamonds.
The diamantiferous soil of the region made it a popular destination for prospectors.  这个地区的含钻土壤使其成为探矿者的热门目的地。
The mine is known for its diamantiferous kimberlite pipes.  该矿以其含钻石的金伯利岩管而闻名。
近义词: diamond-bearing, diamond-rich
反义词: barren, unproductive
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *diamantem (accusative of diamas), from Greek adamas, adamantos "unconquerable, hardest metal, diamond" + -ferous "bearing."
