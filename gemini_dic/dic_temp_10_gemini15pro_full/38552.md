conclusionally: /kənˈkluːʒənəli/
adverb
结论性地; 最终地
in a way that relates to or expresses a conclusion.
Conclusionally, the study found that regular exercise is beneficial for health.  最终，研究发现规律运动有益于健康。
The report conclusionally demonstrated the link between smoking and lung cancer.  报告最终证明了吸烟和肺癌之间的联系。
Synonyms: finally, ultimately, lastly, in conclusion, to conclude, in the end
Antonyms: initially, firstly, at first, in the beginning
词性: 副词
