dramaturgs:/ˈdræmətɜːrɡz/ /ˈdrɑːmətɜːrɡz/
n.
剧作家；戏剧构作家；戏剧学者
A dramaturg is a literary editor on the staff of a theater who consults with authors and edits texts.
The dramaturg helped the playwright refine the script.  这位戏剧构作家帮助剧作家润色了剧本。
Synonyms: playwright, script editor, literary advisor
Antonyms: N/A
词性: noun
