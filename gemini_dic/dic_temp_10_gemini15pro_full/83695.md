haggle:/ˈhæɡl/| v. |讨价还价，争论不休 |To dispute or bargain persistently, especially over the price of something.| We haggled with the street vendor over the price of the souvenir. 我们与街头小贩就纪念品的價格讨价还价。|  I spent hours haggling with the dealer before we agreed on a price for the car. 我和经销商花了几个小时讨价还价才就汽车价格达成一致。 | synonyms: bargain, barter, negotiate, dicker | antonyms: agree, acquiesce | verb
