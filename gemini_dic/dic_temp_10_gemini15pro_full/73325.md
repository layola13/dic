foredeem:/fɔːrˈdiːm/
verb
预先赎回；提前偿还
to redeem or pay off (something, such as a mortgage or loan) before it is due.
He foredeemed the mortgage on his house. 他提前偿还了房屋抵押贷款。
prepay, buy back, redeem early
delay, postpone
v.
