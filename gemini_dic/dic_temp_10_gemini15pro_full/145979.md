overdocumented:/ˌoʊvərˈdɑːkjəˌmɛntɪd/
adjective
过度记录的
Having too much documentation; excessively documented.  Provided with an overabundance of supporting documents.
The project was so overdocumented that it became difficult to find the essential information.  这个项目过度记录，以至于很难找到重要的信息。
The process was overdocumented, making it cumbersome and inefficient.  这个过程过度记录，使其繁琐且低效。
Synonyms: excessively documented, overly documented
Antonyms: underdocumented, sparsely documented
词性: adjective
