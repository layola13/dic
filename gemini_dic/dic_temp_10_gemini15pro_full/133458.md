nonfealty:/nɒnˈfiːəlti/ | n. | 不忠; 背信; 违背誓言 |  The act of failing to fulfill one's oath of loyalty or allegiance.  Absence or violation of allegiance owed by a vassal to a feudal lord. | His nonfealty to the king resulted in his imprisonment. 他对国王的不忠导致了他的监禁。 | Near synonyms: disloyalty, treachery, perfidy, infidelity | Antonyms: loyalty, fidelity, allegiance | noun
