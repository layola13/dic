over-attached:/ˌoʊvərəˈtætʃt/ | adj. | 过度依恋的，过度依赖的 | Excessively or inappropriately attached to someone or something, often to the point of being clingy or obsessive.  Characterized by an unhealthy level of emotional dependence. | She's over-attached to her phone; she can't go anywhere without it. 她过度依赖她的手机，没有它哪都去不了。

| Example: He was so over-attached to his mother that he couldn't make any decisions on his own. 他太依赖他的母亲了，以至于自己做不了任何决定。 | Synonyms: clingy, needy, dependent, possessive | Antonyms: independent, detached, self-sufficient | word type: adjective
