geometer:/dʒiˈɒmɪtər/ | n. | 几何学家；几何学者 | A person skilled in geometry or who uses it in their work. |  Euclid is considered the father of geometry, so he was a great geometer. 欧几里得被认为是几何之父，所以他是一位伟大的几何学家。|  mathematician, geometrician |  N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *geometra, from Ancient Greek γεωμέτρης (geōmétrēs, “geometer, land-surveyor”), from γῆ (gê, “earth, land”) + μέτρον (métron, “measure”).
