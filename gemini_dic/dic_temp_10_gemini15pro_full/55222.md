disshadow:/dɪˈʃædəʊ/ |v.| 使黯然失色；使蒙上阴影；使失去光泽 |To deprive of luster or brilliance; to obscure or overshadow.| The scandal disshadowed his brilliant career.  这桩丑闻使他辉煌的职业生涯蒙上阴影。 |例句：


His recent failures have disshadowed his previous accomplishments. 他最近的失败使他以前的成就黯然失色。


The setting sun disshadowed the valley.  落日使山谷笼罩在阴影中。 | 近义词: eclipse, obscure, overshadow, dim, tarnish | 反义词: brighten, illuminate, enhance |词性: 动词
