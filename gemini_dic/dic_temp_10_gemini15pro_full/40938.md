cooncan:/ˈkuːnkæn/| n.|康肯牌戏（一种四人纸牌游戏）|A four-player card game popular in the southwestern United States, related to rummy.|We played cooncan all night. 我们整晚都在玩康肯牌戏。|近义词:rummy (一种类似的纸牌游戏)|反义词: None|noun


词源:
The etymology of "cooncan" is uncertain. It's believed to be derived from a Mexican Spanish word, possibly "con quién" (with whom), reflecting the social nature of the game.
