metazoic:/ˌmɛtəˈzoʊɪk/ | adj. n.|后生动物的，后生动物|Relating to or denoting an animal of the Metazoa division.  A metazoic animal. |Example 1: Metazoic organisms are multicellular and exhibit cellular differentiation. 后生动物是有多细胞的并且展现出细胞分化。

Example 2:  Sponges are among the simplest metazoic animals. 海绵是最简单的后生动物之一。|Synonym: multicellular|Antonym: protozoan|adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From meta- (beyond, after) + zo- (animal) + -ic (adjective suffix).  Ultimately from Greek *meta (after, beyond) and *zōion (animal).
