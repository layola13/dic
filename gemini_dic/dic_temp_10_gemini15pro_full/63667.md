epanorthosis:/ˌɛpənɔːrˈθoʊsɪs/
|n.|修辞上的自我更正，反复，修正|A figure of speech in which a speaker corrects or qualifies something they have just said, often to amplify or soften the previous statement. | "I'm angry — no, furious — at his behavior!" 我很生气——不，是暴怒——因为他这样的行为！|"The cat sat on the mat — no, sprawled elegantly across the luxurious rug."  猫坐在垫子上——不，是优雅地躺在豪华的地毯上。|correction, amendment, revision|N/A|noun
