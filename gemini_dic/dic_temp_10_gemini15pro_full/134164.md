nonimputative:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpjuːtətɪv/
adjective
非归罪的，不归咎的
Not involving imputation; not ascribing or attributing (especially fault or blame).
His nonimputative remark diffused the tension in the room. 他不带责备的言论缓和了房间里的紧张气氛。
近义词: nonaccusatory, nonjudgmental, forgiving
反义词: accusatory, blaming, incriminating
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From non- + imputative, from Late Latin imputativus, from imputare "to reckon, count, attribute," from in- "in" + putare "to think, consider, reckon."
