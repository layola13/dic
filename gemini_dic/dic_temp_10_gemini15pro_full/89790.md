horizontalize:/ˌhɔrɪˈzɒntəlaɪz/ | v. |使水平，水平放置|To make something horizontal or to spread something out over a wider area. |例句：The workers had to horizontalize the large pipe before they could transport it. 工人们不得不先把大管子放平才能运输。

例句：The company tried to horizontalize its market by offering a wider range of products.  这家公司试图通过提供更广泛的产品来横向拓展市场。|近义词：level, flatten, broaden, expand |反义词：verticalize, tilt, steepen |词性:动词
