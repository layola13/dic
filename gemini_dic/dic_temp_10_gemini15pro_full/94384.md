impleader:/ɪmˈpliːdər/| n.|追加被告; 第三方诉讼|The impleader of the manufacturer was necessary to determine liability.  对制造商的追加诉讼对于确定责任是必要的。|例句：The defendant filed a third-party complaint, seeking impleader of the subcontractor.  被告提交了第三方诉讼，要求追加分包商为被告。

例句：The impleader action allowed the original defendant to bring another potentially liable party into the lawsuit. 追加诉讼允许原始被告将另一个可能承担责任的方带入诉讼。|近义词：joinder, third-party action|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
