animatic: /ˌænɪˈmætɪk/ | n. | 动画脚本，动态故事板 | A preliminary version of a film, produced by shooting successive sections of a storyboard and adding a soundtrack.  It's essentially an animated storyboard, used to visualize the film before full animation begins. | The director showed the animatic to the producers to get approval for the film's pacing. 导演向制片人展示了动画脚本以获得对影片节奏的批准。| The animatic helped the team visualize the action sequences and make necessary changes before starting the expensive animation process.  动画脚本帮助团队将动作序列可视化，并在开始昂贵的动画制作过程之前进行必要的更改。 | synonyms: storyboard, previsualization, mockup | antonyms: final cut, finished film | noun
