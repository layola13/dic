distoma:/daɪˈstəʊmə/ (US) /daɪˈstəʊmə/ (UK)| n. |吸虫|A parasitic flatworm of the class Trematoda, including flukes.|The distoma can cause serious health problems in livestock.  这种吸虫会导致家畜严重的健康问题。|Example:  Infection with liver distomas is a common problem in sheep. 感染肝吸虫是绵羊中的常见问题。|Synonyms: fluke, trematode|Antonyms: None|noun

distomae:/daɪˈstəʊmiː/ (US) /daɪˈstəʊmiː/ (UK)| n. |吸虫（复数）|Plural of distoma.|Several species of distomae were identified in the sample.  在样本中鉴定出几种吸虫。|Example: The researchers studied the life cycle of various distomae. 研究人员研究了各种吸虫的生命周期。|Synonyms: flukes, trematodes|Antonyms: None|noun
