indunas:/ɪnˈduːnəz/ | n. |酋长，首领，顾问| A chief, headman, or advisor, especially in Southern Africa. |  The indunas met with the king to discuss tribal matters. 酋长们会见了国王，讨论部落事务。|  The king consulted his indunas before making any important decisions. 国王在做出任何重要决定之前都会咨询他的顾问。| 近义词: chiefs, headmen, leaders, advisors | 反义词: followers, subordinates | 词性: noun

词源: From Zulu *induna*, meaning "officer," "leader," or "captain."  Ultimately from the verb *ukunduna*, "to lead."
