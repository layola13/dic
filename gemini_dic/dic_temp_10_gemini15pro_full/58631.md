dumb-bell: /ˈdʌmˌbel/ (US), /ˈdʌmbɛl/ (UK)| n.|哑铃|A short bar with weights at each end, used for exercising.|He lifts dumbbells to build muscle. 他举哑铃来锻炼肌肉。|synonyms: weight, free weight|antonyms: N/A|noun

dumb-bell: /ˈdʌmˌbel/ (US), /ˈdʌmbɛl/ (UK)| adj. (informal, often offensive)|愚蠢的|Stupid or silly.|He made a dumb-bell decision. 他做了一个愚蠢的决定。|synonyms: stupid, foolish, silly, idiotic|antonyms: intelligent, smart, clever|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "dumb" (mute) + "bell" (originally referring to a large bell that could not be rung).  Early dumbbells were made by removing the clapper from a bell, rendering it silent or "dumb."  Later, the term was applied to the exercise equipment we know today.
