drywall: /ˈdraɪˌwɔːl/ (US) /ˈdraɪwɔːl/ (UK)| n.| 石膏板，干式墙| A panel made of gypsum plaster pressed between two thick sheets of paper, used for interior walls and ceilings.|  They used drywall to build the partition. 他们用石膏板建造隔墙。|  The drywall was damaged by the leak. 石膏板被漏水损坏了。|synonyms: plasterboard, gypsum board, sheetrock (US trademark)|antonyms: plaster, lath and plaster|noun
