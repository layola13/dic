londons:/ˈlʌndənz/
| n. |伦敦（通常情况下不加s）| Refers to London, the capital city of England. While grammatically possible as a plural, it's uncommon and usually implies a metaphorical or poetic usage, perhaps referring to different facets or districts of the city.  "The Londons of my youth are gone." implies different experiences or perceptions of London over time.|例句：I visited many Londons on my trip, from the bustling financial district to the quiet residential areas.  我在旅途中游览了伦敦的许多地方，从繁华的金融区到安静的住宅区。

例句：He spoke of two Londons - the London of the rich and the London of the poor. 他谈到了两个伦敦——富人的伦敦和穷人的伦敦。
