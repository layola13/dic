affricated:/ˈæfrɪkeɪtɪd/| adj.| 擦化的；塞擦音化的 |Consonant sounds that begin as stops (plosives) and are released as fricatives are called affricates. This means the airflow is initially stopped, then released with friction, creating a characteristic sound.| The sound /tʃ/ in "church" is an affricated consonant. 教堂里的/tʃ/音是一个塞擦音。| The "j" sound in "judge" is also affricated. “judge”中的“j”音也是塞擦音。 |近义词:fricative, sibilant |反义词:plosive, stop |词性:adjective
