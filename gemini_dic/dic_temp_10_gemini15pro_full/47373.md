deactivators:/diːˈæktɪveɪtərz/| n.|去激活剂；使无效者；灭活剂|Substances or factors that make something inactive or ineffective.  They neutralize or suppress activity. |The deactivators in the solution prevented the catalyst from working.  溶液中的去活剂阻止了催化剂工作。

The security system has built-in deactivators to prevent accidental triggering.  该安全系统内置了去活装置，以防止意外触发。|近义词:inhibitors, suppressants, neutralizers|反义词:activators, catalysts, stimulants|Noun
