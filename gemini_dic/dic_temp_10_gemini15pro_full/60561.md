elasmobranchii:/ɪˌlæzməˈbræŋkiaɪ/| n. |板鳃亚纲|A subclass of Chondrichthyes (cartilaginous fishes) that includes sharks, rays, skates, and sawfish, characterized by having five to seven pairs of gill clefts opening individually to the exterior, a cartilaginous skeleton, placoid scales, and the absence of a swim bladder.|Sharks and rays belong to the subclass Elasmobranchii.  鲨鱼和鳐鱼属于板鳃亚纲。|近义词:chondrichthyan (软骨鱼纲)|反义词: None (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑硬骨鱼类 Osteichthyes)|词性: noun

Etymology:
From Greek *elasmos* (plate) + *brankhia* (gills)
