backcast: /ˈbækˌkæst/ (US), /ˈbækˌkɑːst/ (UK) | n. v. | 回溯推算，追溯预测 |  The act of making a projection about the past from present data.  It involves estimating the value of a variable at a specified date in the past, based on current values and knowledge of past trends. |  The researchers backcast the population size of the endangered species to 1950. 研究人员回溯推算了濒危物种在1950年的种群规模。 |  retrodict, hindcast | forecast | verb, noun
