hatbands: /ˈhætˌbændz/
n.
帽带，帽箍
A band of ribbon, cloth, or leather encircling the crown of a hat.
The gentleman wore a fedora with a stylish feather hatband.  这位绅士戴着一顶饰有时尚羽毛帽带的软呢帽。
synonyms: hat band, headband
antonyms: (No direct antonym, potentially "brim" as a contrasting part of a hat)
