pagodite:/ˈpæɡəˌdaɪt/| n.|叶蜡石，寿山石，雕刻用滑石|A soft, easily carved stone, often used for ornamental carvings, seals, and small sculptures.  It's a variety of pyrophyllite.|The artist skillfully carved a delicate flower from a piece of pagodite. 艺术家巧妙地用一块叶蜡石雕刻出一朵精致的花。|Synonyms: agalmatolite, pyrophyllite, steatite|Antonyms: granite, marble|noun
