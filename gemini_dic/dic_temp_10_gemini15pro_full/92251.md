hyperurban:/ˌhaɪpərˈɜːrbən/
adjective
超级都市的
Characterized by excessive urban development, often with negative connotations such as overcrowding, pollution, and social problems.  Relating to or designating a city or cities exhibiting extreme characteristics of urbanization, especially in size, density, and environmental impact.
The study examined the hyperurban landscape of Tokyo and its impact on residents' mental health. 这项研究考察了东京超级都市景观及其对居民心理健康的影响。
The hyperurban environment often leads to increased stress and a decline in the quality of life. 超级都市环境通常会导致压力增加和生活质量下降。
Synonyms:  megacity, megalopolis, densely populated
Antonyms: rural, suburban
词性: adjective
