fabulize:/ˈfæbjʊlaɪz/| v. |编造；虚构；杜撰|To invent or create a story or a narrative, often based on slight or no foundation.|He fabulized a thrilling tale of adventure on a desert island. 他虚构了一个在荒岛上惊险刺激的冒险故事。|Synonyms: invent, fabricate, concoct, make up|Antonyms: report, document, verify|verb
