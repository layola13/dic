paragraph:/ˈpærəɡræf/ | n. | 段落，节 | A distinct section of a piece of writing, usually dealing with a single theme and indicated by a new line, indentation, or numbering. | This paragraph explains the meaning of the word.  这一段解释了这个词的意思。| section, passage | chapter, book | noun

/ˈpærəɡræf/ | vt. | 将…分成段落 | To divide (text) into paragraphs. |  He paragraphed the article carefully. 他仔细地将文章分成了段落。| divide into paragraphs, format | combine, merge | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *paragraphe*, from Late Latin *paragraphus* "horizontal line marking a break in sense", from Greek *paragraphos* "line drawn in the margin to mark a division in a text, a marginal note", from *paragraphein* "to write beside, write in the margin", from *para-* "beside" + *graphein* "to write".


近义词: section, passage
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider "sentence" or "word" as smaller units.)
词性: noun, verb
