diclinic:/daɪˈklɪnɪk/| adj.|雌雄异株的，雌雄异花的|Having separate male and female flowers on different plants.  Relating to a plant species that has unisexual flowers (male and female flowers) borne on separate individual plants. |例句：The willow tree is a diclinic species. 柳树是雌雄异株的物种。

例句：Diclinic pollination is common in many tree species. 雌雄异株的授粉在许多树种中很常见。|近义词：dioecious|反义词：monoecious|词性:形容词

词源:  From Greek *di- (“two”) + *kline (“bed”)  referring to the separate "beds" or plants for the male and female reproductive structures.
