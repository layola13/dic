bacchus:/ˈbækəs/| n. |巴克斯，酒神|Bacchus is the Roman god of wine, equivalent to the Greek god Dionysus. He is associated with winemaking, intoxication, fertility, and revelry.|例句：The festival honored Bacchus with wine and song.  这个节日用美酒和歌曲来纪念巴克斯神。

例句：The statue depicted Bacchus holding a bunch of grapes.  这座雕像描绘了巴克斯手持一串葡萄。|近义词：Dionysus|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
