panic-button:/ˈpænɪk ˌbʌtn/ | n. | 恐慌按钮，紧急按钮 | A button that, when pressed, alerts others to an emergency. |  She hit the panic button when she saw the intruder. 她看到入侵者时按下了紧急按钮。| synonyms: alarm button, emergency button, distress button | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "off switch" in a specific context) | noun

panic-buttons:/ˈpænɪk ˌbʌtnz/ | n. | 恐慌按钮（复数） | Multiple buttons designed for emergencies. |  The building is equipped with several panic buttons. 这栋楼安装了几个紧急按钮。| synonyms: alarm buttons, emergency buttons, distress buttons | antonyms:  (No direct antonym) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"panic" from Greek "panikos" (influenced by Pan, the god of woods and fields, whose sudden appearances were said to cause groundless fear) + "button" from Old French "boton" (bud, button)
