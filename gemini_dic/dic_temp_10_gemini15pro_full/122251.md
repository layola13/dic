miter:/ˈmaɪtər/ (US), /ˈmaɪtə/ (UK)| n. v.|斜接；斜接角；主教冠；|A joint made between two pieces of wood or other material at an angle, typically 45°, to form a corner.| The carpenter used a miter saw to create a perfect miter joint. 木匠用斜切锯做了一个完美的斜接接头。  He carefully mitered the two pieces of molding. 他仔细地将两块造型线斜切对接。|近义词：bevel, joint|反义词: (没有明确的反义词)|noun, verb
