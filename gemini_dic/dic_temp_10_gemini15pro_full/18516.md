benedictionary:/ˌbɛnɪˈdɪkʃənɛri/| n.|祝福; 祝祷; 恩赐; 福泽;|A benedictionary is a prayer that asks for God's blessing, especially a prayer that concludes a religious service.  It can also refer to something that brings good fortune or benefit.|The priest delivered a benedictionary at the end of the mass.  神父在弥撒结束时做了祝祷。|近义词：blessing, invocation, grace|反义词：curse, malediction|noun
