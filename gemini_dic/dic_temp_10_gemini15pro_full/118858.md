metaplasia:/ˌmɛtəˈpleɪʒə/ /ˌmɛtəˈpleɪʒɪə/
|n.|化生|The reversible replacement of one differentiated cell type with another mature differentiated cell type.  It's an adaptive response to stress, often seen in epithelial tissues.|Barrett's esophagus is an example of metaplasia where the normal squamous epithelium of the esophagus is replaced by columnar epithelium.|近义词:transformation, conversion|反义词:dysplasia|词性:noun
