expelling:/ɪkˈspɛlɪŋ/
verb
驱逐，排出，开除
forcing something or someone to leave a place or organization.
The school is expelling him for bad behavior. 学校因为他行为不良将他开除。
Synonyms: ejecting, ousting, dismissing, removing, discharging
Antonyms: admitting, accepting, welcoming, retaining
词性: 动词

expelling:/ɪkˈspɛlɪŋ/
gerund/present participle
驱逐，排出，开除
the act of forcing something or someone to leave a place or organization
The expelling of the troublemakers brought peace back to the classroom.  把捣乱分子驱逐出去后，教室里恢复了平静。
Synonyms: ejection, ouster, dismissal, removal
Antonyms: admission, acceptance, welcome
词性: 动名词/现在分词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin expellere, from ex- "out" + pellere "to drive, push"
