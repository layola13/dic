choking:/ˈtʃoʊkɪŋ/ (US), /ˈtʃəʊkɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. n. |窒息的；令人窒息的；噎住；窒息|The act of being unable to breathe because something is blocking the throat, or the feeling that you are unable to breathe.  A choking gas or smoke makes you unable to breathe easily.|例句：The child was choking on a piece of candy.  孩子被一块糖卡住了。|近义词：suffocating, asphyxiating, gagging|反义词:breathing, inhaling|词性:adjective, noun

choking:/ˈtʃoʊkɪŋ/ (US), /ˈtʃəʊkɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |窒息；噎|The act of being unable to breathe because something is blocking the throat.|例句：He died from choking. 他死于窒息。|近义词：suffocation, asphyxiation|反义词:breathing, respiration|词性:noun


词源: From the Middle English *choken*, from Old English *cēocian* "to choke," related to *ceaca* "jaw, cheek."
