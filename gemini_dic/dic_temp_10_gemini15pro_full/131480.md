noncoincidental:/ˌnɒnkəʊˌɪnsɪˈdɛntəl/ | adj. | 非偶然的，非巧合的 | Not coincidental; not occurring by chance; deliberate or planned. | The noncoincidental timing of the two events suggests a connection. 这两个事件非巧合的时间安排表明它们之间存在联系。|  The CEO's resignation was noncoincidental with the company's recent financial troubles. 首席执行官的辞职与公司最近的财务困境并非巧合。 | Synonyms: deliberate, intentional, purposeful, planned | Antonyms: coincidental, accidental, chance, fortuitous | adjective
