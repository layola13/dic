nonscented:/ˌnɒnˈsɛntɪd/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈsɛntɪd/ (US)| adj.|无香味的，无气味的|Having no scent or fragrance; not perfumed.|例句：She prefers nonscented lotions. 她更喜欢无香味的乳液。|近义词：unscented, odorless, fragrance-free|反义词：scented, fragrant, perfumed|词性:形容词
词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + scented (past participle of scent, meaning "to perfume")
