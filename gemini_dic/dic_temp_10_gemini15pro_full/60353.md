egranulose:/iːˈɡræn.ju.loʊs/
adjective
无颗粒的; 脱粒的
lacking granules; having lost granules.
The bone marrow biopsy revealed egranulose cells, indicating a serious condition. 骨髓活检显示无颗粒细胞，表明病情严重。

近义词: agranular, nongranular
反义词: granular, granulated
