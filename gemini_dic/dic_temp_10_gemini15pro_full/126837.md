naphthas:/ˈnæfθəz/ | n. | 石脑油；粗汽油 | A volatile, flammable liquid hydrocarbon mixture, intermediate between gasoline and kerosene, obtained from petroleum distillation and used as a solvent, fuel, and in the manufacture of various chemicals. |  Naphthas are used as solvents in various industrial processes. 石脑油在各种工业过程中用作溶剂。

Naphthas are a significant component of gasoline. 石脑油是汽油的重要组成部分。| 近义词: petroleum distillate, gasoline, solvent | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "residue" in the context of distillation) | 词性: noun
