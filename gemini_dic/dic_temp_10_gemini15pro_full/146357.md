overfix:/ˌoʊvərˈfɪks/ | v. | 过度修理；过度固定 | To repair or fix something excessively, often to the point of making it worse. |  Example 1:  He overfixed the engine, and now it won't start at all. 他过度修理了引擎，现在它完全无法启动了。

Example 2:  Be careful not to overfix the software; sometimes simpler is better.  小心不要过度修复软件；有时简单反而更好。| 近义词: overrepair, overdo  | 反义词: underfix, neglect | 词性: 动词
