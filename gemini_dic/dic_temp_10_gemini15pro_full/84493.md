halul:/ˈhɑːluːl/| n.|亵渎，玷污，废墟|A state of desecration, profanation, or ruin.  Derived from the Arabic word "ḥalāl," meaning permissible or lawful, "halul" denotes its opposite – that which is rendered unlawful or desecrated.|The abandoned temple fell into halul after years of neglect. 这座废弃的寺庙在多年的忽视后变成了废墟。|近义词：desecration, profanation, ruin, defilement|反义词:sanctification, consecration|词性:名词
