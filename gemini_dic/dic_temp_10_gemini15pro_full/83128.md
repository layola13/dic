gut:/ɡʌt/| n.  v.  adj.|肠子，内脏；胆量；直觉；精华|The intestines or stomach, especially of an animal; courage and determination; instinctive feeling; the essential part of something.|He had a gut feeling that something was wrong. 他有一种直觉，觉得事情不对劲。|Synonyms: intestines, bowels, stomach, belly, courage, nerve, bravery, instinct, intuition|Antonyms: cowardice, fear, exterior, surface|noun, verb, adjective

gut:/ɡʌt/| v. |取出内脏；摧毁（建筑物）内部|To remove the internal organs of; to destroy the inside of a building.|The fire gutted the building. 大火烧毁了大楼的内部。|Synonyms: disembowel, eviscerate, devastate, destroy|Antonyms: repair, renovate, restore|verb
