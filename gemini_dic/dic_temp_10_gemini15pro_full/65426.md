euphotide:/ˈjuːfətaɪd/
noun
辉长岩
A rock consisting of saussurite (altered plagioclase) and diallage (a variety of pyroxene).
The geologist identified the sample as euphotide. 地质学家将样品鉴定为辉长岩。
Synonyms: gabbro, diabase
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a specific rock type)
