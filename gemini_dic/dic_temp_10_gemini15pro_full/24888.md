bruh:/brʌ/| interjection|哥们；老兄；哎哟| An informal greeting or exclamation, expressing surprise, disbelief, or resignation.  Derived from "bro," a shortened form of "brother."|Bruh, you won the lottery?! 哥们，你中彩票了？！|近义词：bro, dude, man|反义词: (No direct antonym for an interjection like this)|词性:interjection
