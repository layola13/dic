allod:/ˈælɒd/ (UK), /ˈælɑːd/ (US)| n.|份地，保有地，世袭地|An allod is land owned absolutely, without any feudal dues or other obligations owed to a superior.|In medieval times, owning an allod was a sign of great power and independence. 在中世纪，拥有份地是权力和独立的象征。

The king granted him an allod in the newly conquered territory. 国王在新征服的领土上授予他一块份地。|近义词：freehold, estate|反义词：fief|词性：名词
