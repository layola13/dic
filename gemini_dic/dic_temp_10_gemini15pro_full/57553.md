drama:/ˈdrɑːmə/ (US), /ˈdrɑːmə/ (UK)| n.| 戏剧，戏剧艺术；剧本；戏剧性事件，激动人心的事件|A play for theater, radio, or television; exciting, emotional, or unexpected series of events or set of circumstances.|例句：The drama unfolded before our very eyes.  这出戏在我们眼前展开。

例句：Her life was full of drama. 她的人生充满了戏剧性事件。|近义词：play, theatrical, spectacle, performance, excitement, tension|反义词：calm, peace, tranquility|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *drama, from Ancient Greek δρᾶμα (drâma, “action, deed, play”), from δράω (dráō, “I do, act”).
