inevitable:/ɪˈnevitəbl/| adj.|不可避免的，必然发生的|Something that is inevitable cannot be avoided or prevented. |例句：Their defeat was inevitable. 他们的失败是不可避免的。|例句：Death and taxes are inevitable. 死亡和税收是不可避免的。|近义词：unavoidable, certain, inescapable|反义词：avoidable, preventable|词性：形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin inevitabilis, from in- "not" + evitabilis "avoidable," from evitare "to avoid."
