airdrop:/ˈeərdrɒp/ (UK) /ˈeərdrɑːp/ (US)| n. vt.|空投;空投物资;空投补给;通过空投发送; 通过空投散播|An airdrop is the delivery of items by parachute, or the items themselves. In the context of cryptocurrency, it refers to the distribution of tokens or coins to a large number of people, usually for free or in exchange for completing certain tasks.|The military performed an airdrop of supplies to the stranded refugees. 軍方向受困的難民空投了物資。|Synonyms: airlift, paradrop, delivery, distribution|Antonyms: pickup, collection|noun
