mass-markets:/ˈmæs ˌmɑːrkɪts/ (US) /ˈmæs ˌmɑːkɪts/ (UK)| n. |大众市场|Markets for goods that are produced and sold in large quantities and at low prices, targeting a broad consumer base.|The company decided to mass-market its new line of budget laptops. 该公司决定将其新的廉价笔记本电脑系列推向大众市场。|synonyms: consumer markets, popular markets|antonyms: niche markets, specialized markets|noun

mass-market:/ˌmæs ˈmɑːrkɪt/ (US) /ˌmæs ˈmɑːkɪt/ (UK)| v. |向大众市场销售|To market a product or service to a large number of people.|The automaker mass-marketed its new electric car. 这家汽车制造商将其新的电动汽车推向了大众市场。|synonyms: popularize, distribute widely|antonyms: specialize, target a niche|verb

mass-marketed:/ˌmæs ˈmɑːrkɪtɪd/ (US) /ˌmæs ˈmɑːkɪtɪd/ (UK)| adj. |大众市场的|Produced and sold in large quantities and at low prices, targeting a broad consumer base.|The mass-marketed clothing line was a huge success.  面向大众市场的服装系列获得了巨大成功。|synonyms: widely available, commercially produced|antonyms: exclusive, limited edition|adjective
