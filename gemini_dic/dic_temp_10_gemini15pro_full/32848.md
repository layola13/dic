词源:
From Late Latin *cholericus, from Latin cholera, from Greek kholera "bile," from khole "bile, gall."  Related to choler (bile, anger), and choleric (easily angered).  The "-ness" suffix transforms the adjective "choleric" into a noun, indicating the state or quality of being choleric.
