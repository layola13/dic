grinte: /ɡrɪnt/ | v. | 露齿而笑，咧嘴笑 | To smile broadly, often baring the teeth, especially in a way that suggests satisfaction or amusement. |  He grinte at the thought of his upcoming vacation. 他想着即将到来的假期，咧嘴一笑。

She grinte mischievously as she revealed the surprise. 她淘气地咧嘴一笑，揭晓了惊喜。 | 近义词: grin, beam, smirk | 反义词: frown, scowl | 词性: 动词

词源:  Likely a variant or alteration of "grin," which itself has uncertain origins. Possibly related to Old Norse "grina" (to grimace).
