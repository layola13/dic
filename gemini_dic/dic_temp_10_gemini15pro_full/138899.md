nuthatch:/ˈnʌthætʃ/ (US), /ˈnʌthætʃ/ (UK)| n. |鳾|A small songbird with a short tail, a pointed bill, and the ability to climb down trees head first.|We saw a nuthatch clinging to the tree trunk. 我们看到一只鳾 clinging 在树干上。|The nuthatch spiralled down the oak tree looking for insects.  鳾沿着橡树螺旋向下寻找昆虫。|Synonyms: treecreeper, woodcreeper|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific bird species)|noun
