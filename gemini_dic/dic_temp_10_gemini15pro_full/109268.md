lemon:/ˈlɛmən/|n.|柠檬；柠檬黄；傻瓜|a yellow, oval citrus fruit with thick skin and sour juice; something that is defective and worthless|例句：She added a slice of lemon to her tea. 她往茶里加了一片柠檬。

例句：He bought a used car that turned out to be a lemon. 他买了一辆二手车，结果发现是个次品。|近义词：citrus, sour fruit|反义词:sweet fruit|词性:noun
