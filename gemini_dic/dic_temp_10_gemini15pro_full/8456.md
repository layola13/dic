antiar:/ˈæn.tɪ.ɑːr/| n.|箭毒木，安提亚毒|A tree of the mulberry family (Moraceae) native to Southeast Asia, yielding a milky sap used as an arrow poison.|The antiar tree is known for its highly toxic sap. 箭毒木以其剧毒的汁液而闻名。|The natives used antiar sap to poison their arrows for hunting. 土著人用箭毒木的汁液涂在箭上用于狩猎。|近义词:upas, ipoh|反义词:antidote (解毒剂 -  概念上的反义词)|noun
