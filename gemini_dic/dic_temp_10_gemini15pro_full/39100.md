confrontation:/ˌkɑːnfrənˈteɪʃn/ /ˌkɒnfrənˈteɪʃn/| n. |对抗，冲突，对峙|a situation in which people or groups are in opposition to each other and ready to fight or argue.|The two countries are on the brink of confrontation. 这两个国家处于对抗的边缘。|
confrontation:| n. |面对，正视|the act of facing a difficult situation or person.|She decided on a policy of confrontation with her boss. 她决定采取与老板对抗的策略。|
confrontation:| n. |(证据或信息的) 出示，提交|the presenting of evidence or information to someone.|The police avoided a confrontation with the protesters. 警方避免了与抗议者的冲突。|
近义词:conflict, clash, encounter, showdown|反义词:agreement, harmony, compromise, avoidance|词性:noun
