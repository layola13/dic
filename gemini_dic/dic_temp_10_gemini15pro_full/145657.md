overcontributed:/ˌoʊvərˌkənˈtrɪbjuːtɪd/
verb
过度捐赠，过度贡献
Having given or contributed more than necessary or expected.
He overcontributed to his retirement account and had to withdraw some of the funds. 他向他的退休账户过度缴款，不得不取出部分资金。
The charity was overcontributed to after the natural disaster. 自然灾害过后，慈善机构收到了过多的捐款。
