outcharged:/ˌaʊtˈtʃɑːrdʒd/
verb
收取比...更高的费用；过度收费
to charge someone more than another person or company charges
例句：They outcharged their competitors by 10%. 他们比竞争对手多收了10%的费用。

例句：The hotel outcharged us for the room service.  酒店的客房服务对我们收费过高。
近义词：overcharge, overprice
反义词：undercharge
词性：v.
