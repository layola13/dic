autopoiesis:/ˌɔːtəʊpɔɪˈiːsɪs/ /ˌɑːtəpoʊˈiːsɪs/
n.|自我生成，自创生|The concept of autopoiesis refers to a system capable of reproducing and maintaining itself.  自我生成的理念指的是一个能够自我复制和维持的系统。|Autopoiesis is a key concept in understanding living organisms. 自我生成是理解生物体的一个关键概念。|近义词：self-organization, self-production|反义词：allopoiesis|词性：noun
