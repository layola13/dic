blindling:/ˈblaɪndlɪŋ/| adv.|盲目地；轻率地|Acting or done without careful thought or consideration; recklessly. |He rushed into the venture blindling, without any research. 他盲目地冲进了这项风险投资，没有做任何调查。|近义词:recklessly, rashly, impulsively, heedlessly|反义词:carefully, cautiously, thoughtfully, deliberately|词性:副词

Etymology: From "blind" + "-ling" (a suffix forming adverbs, meaning "in a ... manner").  The word "blind" itself comes from Old English "blind" and ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*blindaz."
