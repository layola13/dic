moulds:/məʊldz/ (US: /moʊldz/)
| n. | 模具；霉菌；类型，气质 | a hollow container used to give shape to molten or hot liquid material when it cools and hardens; a furry growth of minute fungal hyphae occurring typically on organic matter undergoing decomposition; a distinctive and typical style, form, or character | The jelly was set in a heart-shaped mould.  果冻被放在心形模具里凝固。| synonyms: cast, form, matrix, die, fungus, mildew | antonyms:  (For fungus/mildew) cleanliness, sterility | noun

moulds:/məʊldz/ (US: /moʊldz/)
| v. | 塑造；使发霉 | to shape (something) in a mould; to become mouldy; to influence the formation or development of | He moulded the clay into a small figure. 他用黏土塑成一个小雕像。| synonyms: shape, form, fashion, influence | antonyms: deform, distort | verb
