bloodlike:/ˈblʌdlaɪk/
adjective
像血的; 血红色的
Resembling blood in appearance, consistency, or smell.
The liquid oozing from the wound was bloodlike in color.  从伤口渗出的液体颜色像血。
The paint had a thick, bloodlike consistency.  这油漆又浓又稠，像血一样。
Near synonyms: bloody, blood-red, gory, sanguineous
Opposite: bloodless
词性: 形容词
