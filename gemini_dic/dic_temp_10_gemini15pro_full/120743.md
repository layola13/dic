misanthrope: /ˈmɪsənˌθrōp/ (US), /mɪˈzænθrəʊp/ (UK) | n. | 厌世者；愤世嫉俗者 | A person who dislikes, distrusts, or hates humankind. |  He was a bitter misanthrope who lived alone in a cabin in the woods. 他是一个愤世嫉俗的苦命人，独自一人住在森林里的小屋中。 |  Example: The old hermit was considered a misanthrope because he avoided all human contact.  这位老隐士被认为是一个厌世者，因为他避免与任何人接触。 | Synonym: cynic, pessimist, recluse | Antonym: philanthropist, humanitarian | noun
