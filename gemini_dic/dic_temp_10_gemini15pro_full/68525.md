fanciest:/ˈfænsiɪst/| adj.|最高级的；最花哨的；最奇特的|superlative adjective of fancy; most elaborate, ornamental, or extravagant.|She wore her fanciest dress to the gala. 她穿着她最华丽的礼服去参加晚会。|The restaurant had the fanciest desserts I've ever seen.  这家餐厅有我见过的最精致的甜点。|近义词：most elaborate, most ornate, most extravagant|反义词：plainest, simplest, most basic|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *fancien, from Anglo-Norman fancier, from Old French fancier (“to be vain, to take delight in”), of uncertain origin, perhaps from Vulgar Latin *phantasiare, from Late Latin phantasia (“fantasy”).
