drek:/drɛk/| n.|糟粕，垃圾，废物|Something regarded as worthless; rubbish.  It is often used to describe something of poor quality, particularly writing or entertainment.|That movie was pure drek. 那部电影纯粹是垃圾。|近义词：rubbish, garbage, trash, junk, crap|反义词：treasure, gem, masterpiece|词性:noun
Etymology: From Yiddish דרעק (drek), from Middle High German trec, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *þrekkaz (“excrement, dung”).
