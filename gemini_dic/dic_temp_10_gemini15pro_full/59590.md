ebullience:/ɪˈbʊliəns/ /ɪˈbʌljəns/| n.|热情洋溢；兴高采烈；沸腾|The ebullience of the crowd was infectious. 人群的热情很有感染力。|Synonyms: exuberance, buoyancy, elation, enthusiasm, zest|Antonyms: apathy, dullness, gloom, listlessness|noun

Etymology->
From Latin *ēbullientia* "a bubbling up," from ēbullīre "to boil over," from ex- "out" + bullīre "to bubble, boil."
