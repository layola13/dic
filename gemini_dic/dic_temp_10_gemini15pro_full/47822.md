decasyllabic: /ˌdɛkəsɪˈlæbɪk/
adjective
十音节的
Having ten syllables.
Example:  "The professor challenged the students to write a decasyllabic poem." 教授要求学生写一首十音节的诗。

Example: "Decasyllabic lines are common in heroic verse." 十音节诗行在英雄诗中很常见。

Synonym: ten-syllable, ten-syllabled
Antonym: (No direct antonym, but consider terms like monosyllabic, disyllabic for contrasting syllable counts)
词性: adjective
