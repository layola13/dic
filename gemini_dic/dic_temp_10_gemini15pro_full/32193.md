chibouque:/tʃɪˈbuːk/ | n. | 长柄烟斗，土耳其烟斗 | A long-stemmed Turkish tobacco pipe. | He smoked his chibouque contemplatively. 他若有所思地抽着他的长柄烟斗。| Synonym: hookah, narghile | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps cigarette as a modern contrast) | Noun

Etymology:
From French *chibouque*, from Turkish *çubuk* (stick, rod, pipe).
