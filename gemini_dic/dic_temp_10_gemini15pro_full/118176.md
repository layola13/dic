menthols: /ˈmɛnθɒlz/ (British) /ˈmɛnθɔːlz/ (American)| n.|薄荷醇（复数）|A crystalline alcohol with a peppermint taste and odor, found in peppermint oil and used in perfumes, cigarettes, and medicines.|Menthols are often added to cigarettes. 薄荷醇经常被添加到香烟中。|The cough drops contain menthols to soothe sore throats. 这些止咳糖含有薄荷醇，可以舒缓喉咙痛。|Synonyms: peppermint, mint|Antonyms: None|Noun
