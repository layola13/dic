go-fers:/ˈɡoʊfərz/
noun|地鼠；跑腿的人；杂役|A burrowing rodent with large cheek pouches; someone who performs menial tasks, especially fetching things.|例句：The gophers had dug a network of tunnels under the lawn. 地鼠在草坪下挖了一张隧道网。

例句：He was treated like a gopher, fetching coffee and making copies all day. 他被当作跑腿的，整天端茶倒水，复印文件。|近义词：groundhog, errand boy/girl, lackey|反义词:boss, manager|词性:n
