excogitate: /ɪkˈskɒdʒɪteɪt/ /ɛkˈskɑːdʒɪteɪt/
verb
深思熟虑；细想；构想
To think out; devise; invent.  To consider deeply and thoroughly; ponder.
例句：The scientist excogitated a new theory.  科学家构思了一个新的理论。

例句：She spent weeks excogitating her next novel. 她花了数周时间构思她的小说。

近义词：devise, contrive, conceive, formulate, ponder, ruminate, contemplate
反义词：ignore, neglect, overlook


词性: verb
