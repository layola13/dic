nonnational:/nɒnˈnæʃənəl/ /nɑːnˈnæʃənəl/
adjective
非国民的；非本国的
Not belonging to or associated with a particular nation.
Foreign workers are classified as nonnational residents. 外籍劳工被归类为非国民居民。

noun
外国人；非本国人
A person who is not a citizen of a particular country.
Nonnationals are not eligible to vote. 外国人没有投票资格。

近义词:foreign, alien, immigrant
反义词:national, citizen
词性:adjective, noun
