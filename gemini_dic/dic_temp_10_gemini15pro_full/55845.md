divinator:/ˈdɪvɪneɪtər/| n. |预言家，占卜者|A person who practices divination; a soothsayer.|The divinator foretold a great victory. 预言家预言了一场伟大的胜利。|近义词：soothsayer, prophet, seer, oracle|反义词: skeptic|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *divinator, from Latin divinatus, past participle of divinare (“to foretell, predict, prophesy”), from divinus (“divine”), from divus (“god”).
