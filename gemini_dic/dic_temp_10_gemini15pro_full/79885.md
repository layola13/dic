gnathite: /ˈnæθaɪt/
n.
颚基; 口器附肢
A mouthpart appendage of an arthropod.
Gnathite complexity is often used to distinguish between arthropod groups.  颚基的复杂性通常用来区分节肢动物群。
The gnathite of the insect helps it manipulate food.  昆虫的颚基帮助它处理食物。

近义词: mouthpart, appendage
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a body part)
词性: noun
