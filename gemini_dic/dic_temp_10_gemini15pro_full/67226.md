expropriable:/ɛksˈproʊpriəbəl/
adjective
可被征用的，可被没收的
Susceptible to expropriation; that may be expropriated.
例句：The government declared the land expropriable for the construction of a new highway. 政府宣布这块土地可被征用以建造一条新的高速公路。

例句：Many foreign investors are concerned about their assets being expropriable in certain countries. 许多外国投资者担心他们的资产在某些国家会被征用。
近义词：seizable, confiscatable, appropriable
反义词：protected, secure, inalienable
词性：adjective
