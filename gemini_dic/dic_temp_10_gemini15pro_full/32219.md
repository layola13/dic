chicha:/ˈtʃiːtʃə/ | n. |奇恰酒；玉米啤酒|A fermented (or non-fermented) beverage usually derived from maize.  It is a traditional drink of the Andes.|She offered me a glass of chicha. 她给我一杯奇恰酒。|近义词: corn beer, maize beer |反义词: None | Noun
