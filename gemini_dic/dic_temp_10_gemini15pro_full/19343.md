bibliomania:/ˌbɪbliəˈmeɪniə/ | n. | 藏书癖； bibliomania | An excessive desire to acquire and possess books, often regardless of their content or value.  | He suffered from bibliomania, his house overflowing with books he never read. 他患有藏书癖，他的房子里堆满了从未读过的书。 |  His bibliomania led him to spend his life savings on first editions. 他的藏书癖导致他把毕生的积蓄都花在了初版书上。 |  book collecting, bibliophilism | bibliophobia (fear of books) | noun
