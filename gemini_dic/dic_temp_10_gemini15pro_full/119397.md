microexpression:/ˌmaɪkroʊɪkˈspreʃən/ | n. | 微表情 | A brief, involuntary facial expression that reveals a person's true emotions, often contradicting what they are verbally expressing. |  She gave a microexpression of disgust when he mentioned his ex-girlfriend. 她在他提到前女友时露出了一个厌恶的微表情。|  Synonym: tell, subtle cue, fleeting expression | Antonym:  blank expression, poker face | noun
