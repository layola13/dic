multitasker:/ˌmʌltiˈtæskər/ | n. | 多任务处理者 | A person who is able to do many things at the same time. |  She's a great multitasker, juggling work, family, and hobbies. 她是一位出色的多任务处理者，能够兼顾工作、家庭和爱好。 | synonym: polymath, all-rounder | antonym: specialist, one-track mind | noun
