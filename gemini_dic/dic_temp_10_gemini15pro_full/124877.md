mudded:/ˈmʌdid/ | adj. | 泥泞的；沾满泥的 | Covered or smeared with mud. | The car was mudded after driving through the dirt road. 汽车在泥泞的路上行驶后沾满了泥。| 近义词：dirty, muddy, soiled | 反义词：clean, spotless | 词性：形容词

词源：From mud (n.) + -ed (suffix forming past participles and adjectives).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *mudam.
