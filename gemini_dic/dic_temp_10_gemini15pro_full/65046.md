etches:/ˈɛtʃɪz/| v.|蚀刻；铭记；留下深刻印象|The acid etches the metal. 酸腐蚀金属。|例句：The artist etched the design onto the plate. 艺术家把图案蚀刻在板上。

例句：His face was etched with worry. 他的脸上刻满了忧虑。|近义词：engrave, carve, inscribe|反义词:erase, obliterate|词性:Verb (third-person singular simple present etches, present participle etching, simple past and past participle etched)

Etymology: From Old French "eschier" (to cut), from Frankish *skīran (to shear, cut), from Proto-Germanic *skeraną (to cut, shear). Related to English "shear" and "score."
