horoscopy: /həˈrɒskəpi/ /həˈrɑːskəpi/
noun
占星术；星象
The art or practice of predicting the future or describing the character of a person from the positions of the stars and planets at the time of their birth.
She consulted a horoscopy expert about her future. 她向一位占星专家咨询了她的未来。
synonyms: astrology, stargazing, astromancy
antonyms:  (Horoscopy lacks a direct antonym.  Consider terms like "scientific method" or "empiricism" as contrasting concepts.)
