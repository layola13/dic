exactest:/ɪɡˈzæktɪst/ | adj. | 最精确的，最确切的 |  Superlative form of "exact," meaning most precise or accurate.  This form is less common than "most exact." | He demanded the exactest measurements for the new building. 他要求新建筑的测量必须最精确。 |  most exact, most precise, most accurate | least exact, least precise, least accurate | adjective

Etymology:
From Middle English *exacte*, from Latin *exactus*, past participle of *exigō* (“I demand, require, drive out, weigh, measure”), from *ex* (“out of, from”) + *agō* (“I drive, lead, act”).
