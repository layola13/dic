observerships:/əbˈzɜːrvərʃɪps/
n.
见习;实习;观察期
A period of time spent observing a skilled worker or another professional in order to learn about their job.
Dr. Smith completed his observership in cardiology at a prestigious hospital. 史密斯医生在一所著名的医院完成了他的心脏病学见习。
The program offers observerships for international medical graduates. 该项目为国际医学毕业生提供见习机会。
