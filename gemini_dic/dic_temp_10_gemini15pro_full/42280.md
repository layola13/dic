counter-enamel:/ˈkaʊntər ɪˈnæməl/
noun
反面釉质，对釉
The opaque, usually white, enamel applied to the back of a normally transparent enamel, often to conceal the metal base.
The counter-enamel on the back of the brooch was slightly chipped.  胸针背面的反面釉质略有缺损。
Synonyms: backing enamel
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider "face enamel" or "surface enamel" as conceptually opposite)
