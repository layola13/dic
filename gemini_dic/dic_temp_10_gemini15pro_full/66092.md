excavatory: /ˈɛkskəveɪtəri/ | adj. | 挖掘的；挖土的 | Relating to or used for excavation. | The excavatory work unearthed ancient artifacts. 挖掘工作出土了古代文物。 |  近义词: digging, unearthing | 反义词: filling, burying | 词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *excavatorius*, from excavat- ‘dug out’, from the verb excavare, from ex- ‘out’ + cavare ‘to hollow’.
