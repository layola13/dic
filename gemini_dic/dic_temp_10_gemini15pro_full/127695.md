negativities:/ˌneɡəˈtɪvɪtiz/
n.
消极性，消极情绪，负面因素
The state or quality of being negative; a pessimistic or cynical attitude.  A tendency to  express or focus on unpleasant aspects, conditions, or consequences.
Her constant negativities were draining for everyone around her. 她持续的消极情绪让周围的人都感到精疲力竭。
The report focused on the negativities of the current economic situation. 报告集中讨论了当前经济形势的负面因素。
近义词: pessimism, cynicism, negativity, downbeatness
反义词: optimism, positivity, hopefulness
词性: 名词 (Noun)

negativity:/ˌneɡəˈtɪvɪti/
n.
消极性，消极态度
The expression of or tendency to express criticism, doubt, or a lack of hope.
He exuded an air of negativity. 他浑身散发着消极的气息。
Try to overcome the negativity and focus on the positive aspects. 尝试克服消极情绪，关注积极方面。
近义词: pessimism, cynicism, negativities, downbeatness
反义词: optimism, positivity, hopefulness
词性: 名词 (Noun)
