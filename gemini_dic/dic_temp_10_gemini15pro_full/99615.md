interject:/ˌɪntərˈdʒekt/ | v. | 插话；打断；突然插入 | To insert between other things; interrupt. |  "He interjected a question during the lecture." 他在讲座中插话提问。| synonyms: interrupt, interpose, insert, inject | antonyms: continue, proceed | verb

interject:/ˌɪntərˈdʒekt/ | n. | 插话；感叹词 | An exclamation or remark abruptly inserted in a conversation. | "Her speech was punctuated by frequent interjects of 'Oh dear!'" 她的讲话中不时穿插着“哦，天哪！”的感叹。| synonyms: exclamation, ejaculation, interruption | antonyms: silence, pause | noun
