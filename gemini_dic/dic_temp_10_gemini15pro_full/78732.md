giggliness:/ˈɡɪɡlɪnəs/| n. | 傻笑，咯咯笑 | The state of being prone to giggling; a tendency to giggle.  A fit of giggling. |Her giggliness was infectious, and soon everyone was laughing. 她的傻笑很有感染力，很快大家都笑了。|  The children's giggliness filled the room. 孩子们的咯咯笑声充满了整个房间。 | synonyms:  merriment, mirth, levity, lightheartedness | antonyms: seriousness, solemnity, gravity | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From giggle + -ness. Giggle itself likely comes from a Scandinavian source, akin to Old Norse *gikkla.
