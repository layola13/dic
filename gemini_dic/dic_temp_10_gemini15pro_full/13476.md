atrophous:/ˈætrəfəs/| adj.|萎缩的，发育不全的|Wasted; withered; shrunken; having lost its substance, or having failed to attain its proper development.|The doctor noted the patient's atrophous limbs, a sign of the progressing disease. 医生注意到病人萎缩的四肢，这是疾病恶化的迹象。|Synonyms: shriveled, withered, wasted, stunted, underdeveloped|Antonyms: developed, robust, healthy, strong, vigorous|adjective
