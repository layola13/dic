deteriority:/dɪˌtɪriəˈreɪʃn/ | n. | 恶化，退化，变质 | The deterioration of the building was evident in the crumbling facade.  这栋建筑的恶化在摇摇欲坠的正面很明显。 | Example: The patient's condition showed signs of deterioration. 病人的情况显示出恶化的迹象。 | Synonyms: decline, decay, worsening, degeneration | Antonyms: improvement, amelioration, enhancement | noun

deteriorate:/dɪˈtɪriəreɪt/ | v. | 恶化，退化，变质 |  The weather conditions deteriorated rapidly. 天气状况迅速恶化。 | Example:  His health deteriorated after the surgery.  他的健康在手术后恶化了。| Synonyms: worsen, decline, decay, degenerate | Antonyms: improve, ameliorate, enhance | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dēteriorātus, past participle of dēteriorāre (“to make worse”), from Latin dēterior (“worse”), from de (“from”) + *terior (“further, inner”).
