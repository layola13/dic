melanochroic:/ˌmeləˈnɒkrɔɪk/ | adj. |黑发的，黑皮肤的|Having dark hair and a dark complexion.|例句：Melanochroic populations are often found in regions with high UV radiation.  黑发黑皮肤的人群通常出现在紫外线辐射高的地区。

例句：The study focused on the genetic predispositions of melanochroic individuals. 这项研究的重点是黑发黑皮肤个体的遗传倾向。|近义词：dark-haired, dark-complexioned, swarthy |反义词：blond, fair, light-complexioned |词性:adjective

词源:
From Greek *melanochroos* "having black skin," from *melano-* "black" + *chroa* "skin, complexion".
