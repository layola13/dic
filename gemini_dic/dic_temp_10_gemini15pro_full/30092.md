ceaselessly:/ˈsiːsləsli/
adverb
不停地，不断地
Without stopping or pausing; continuously.
The river flows ceaselessly towards the sea.  这条河不停地流向大海。
The machine worked ceaselessly for three days.  这台机器连续工作了三天。
近义词:continuously, incessantly, constantly, perpetually
反义词:intermittently, sporadically, occasionally
词性:副词

Etymology:
From cease + -less + -ly.  Cease comes from Middle English cesen, from Anglo-French cesser, from Latin cessare "to stop, cease," frequentative of cedere "to go, yield."
