oestrual:/ˈɛstrual/ /ˈɛstrʊəl/ | adj. | 发情的，动情期的 | Relating to or occurring during oestrus.  | The oestrual cycle of the cow is approximately 21 days. 母牛的发情周期大约是21天。| 近义词：in heat, in estrus | 反义词：anoestrus | 词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *oestrus "gadfly, frenzy, passion" (from Greek oistros "gadfly, sting, frenzy"), + -al.
