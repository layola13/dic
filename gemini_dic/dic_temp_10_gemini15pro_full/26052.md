bushido:/ˌbuːˈʃiːdoʊ/ /ˌbʊˈʃiːdoʊ/
noun
武士道
The code of the samurai, stressing honor, loyalty, and martial skill.
例句：Bushido was the guiding principle for many samurai. 武士道是许多武士的指导原则。

例句：He lived his life by the tenets of bushido. 他按照武士道的信条生活。

近义词：chivalry, code, ethos
反义词: cowardice, dishonor
词性:n.
