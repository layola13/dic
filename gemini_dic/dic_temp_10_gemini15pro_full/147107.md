overlingering:/ˌoʊvərˈlɪŋɡərɪŋ/| adj.|逗留过久的，迟迟不去的|Lingering for longer than expected or desired.  Staying past a welcome point. Continuing to exist or remain longer than usual.|The overlingering smell of smoke made her cough.|近义词：prolonged, lingering, protracted|反义词:brief, fleeting, short|词性:形容词
词源:From 'over-' (meaning excessively) + 'linger' (from Old English 'langian', to grow long, to yearn).
