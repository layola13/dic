boardsailing:/ˈbɔːrdseɪlɪŋ/
n.|帆板运动|The act of riding on a board with a sail attached, powered by the wind. |例句：Boardsailing is a popular watersport. 帆板运动是一项很受欢迎的水上运动。|近义词：windsurfing|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps kayaking, swimming)|词性:noun

Etymology:  Compound of "board" and "sailing," reflecting the activity's nature. "Board" from Old English "bord" (plank). "Sailing" from Old English "seglung" (act of sailing).
