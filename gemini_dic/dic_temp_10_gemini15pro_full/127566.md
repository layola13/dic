nectar:/ˈnektər/| n.| 花蜜；甘露；美味的饮料|A sweet liquid secreted by flowers, collected by bees and other insects.|The bees buzzed around the flower, collecting nectar. 蜜蜂绕着花朵嗡嗡叫，采集花蜜。|近义词：honeydew, ambrosia|反义词:poison|名词

nectarous:/ˈnektərəs/| adj.| 花蜜的；甘美的|Producing or containing nectar; sweet and delicious.|The nectarine drink was refreshing. 这甘美的饮料令人神清气爽。|近义词：sweet, delicious|反义词:bitter, sour|形容词
