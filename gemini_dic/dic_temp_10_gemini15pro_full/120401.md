ministers:/ˈmɪnɪstərz/
noun
部长；大臣；牧师
A head of a government department.  A person authorized to conduct religious worship.
The Prime Minister addressed the nation. 首相向全国发表讲话。
The minister visited the sick parishioner. 牧师探望了生病的教区居民。
synonyms:secretary, clergyman, pastor, preacher, vicar
antonyms:congregant, parishioner
