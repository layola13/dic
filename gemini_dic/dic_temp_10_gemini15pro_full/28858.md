carpetbag:/ˈkɑːrpɪtˌbæɡ/| n.|旅行包；投机分子；|A traveling bag made of carpet fabric.  A person from the northern United States who went to the South after the Civil War to profit from the Reconstruction.|He carried all his possessions in a battered carpetbag. 他所有的财产都装在一个破旧的旅行包里。|近义词：suitcase, luggage, bag, opportunist (for the figurative sense)|反义词: native, resident (for the figurative sense)|noun

carpetbagger:/ˈkɑːrpɪtˌbæɡər/| n.|投机分子；提包客|A person from the northern United States who went to the South after the Civil War to profit from the Reconstruction.|Many carpetbaggers were seen as opportunists exploiting the South's misfortune. 许多提包客被视为利用南方不幸的投机分子。|近义词：opportunist, speculator|反义词: native, resident|noun
