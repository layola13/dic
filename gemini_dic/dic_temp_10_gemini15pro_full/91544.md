hyper-aggressive:/ˌhaɪpərəˈɡrɛsɪv/
adjective
过度侵略性的；极富攻击性的
Excessively aggressive; exhibiting extreme or exaggerated aggression.
His hyper-aggressive driving style made other drivers nervous. 他的过度侵略性的驾驶风格让其他司机感到紧张。

The company's hyper-aggressive marketing campaign alienated many potential customers.  该公司极富攻击性的营销活动疏远了许多潜在客户。


synonym
aggressive, belligerent, hostile, pugnacious, combative
antonym
passive, peaceful, nonviolent, docile, submissive
Etymology
From hyper- + aggressive. Hyper- comes from Greek *hyper* "over, beyond."
