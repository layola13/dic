insures:/ɪnˈʃʊərz/
verb
投保，确保，保证
makes certain that (something) will occur or be the case.
The policy insures his house against fire. 这份保险单为他家的房子投了火险。
近义词: guarantee, ensure, secure, protect
反义词: endanger, jeopardize, risk
词性: verb

词源:
From Old French *enseurer, from en- (“in, into”) + seur (“sure, secure”), from Latin securus.
