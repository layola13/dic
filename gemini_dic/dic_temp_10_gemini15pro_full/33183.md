chromoleucite:/ˈkrəʊməˌluːsaɪt/ | n. | 色白榴石 | A rare colorless to white feldspathoid mineral, KAlSi2O6, occurring in low-silica igneous rocks.  It is colorless in thin section but white in hand specimen.  | Chromoleucite was found in the volcanic rocks.  色白榴石在火山岩中被发现。 |  leucite, analcime |  N/A | noun
