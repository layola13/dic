It relies on context, shared knowledge, or inference to convey meaning. | Her agreement was implicit in her silence. 她的沉默暗示了她同意了。|synonyms: implication, suggestedness, allusiveness, indirectness| antonyms: explicitness, directness, clarity, openness| noun
