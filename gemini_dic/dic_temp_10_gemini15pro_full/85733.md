hawkbit:/ˈhɔːkbɪt/ (US) /ˈhɔːkbɪt/ (UK)| n. |鬼针草，山柳菊|A plant of the daisy family, with yellow flowers and toothed leaves.  The dried flower heads form a globe of hooked scales which cling to clothing and fur.  The name comes from the belief that hawks used the plant to improve their eyesight.|I saw a field full of hawkbits on my walk. 我在散步时看到一片长满鬼针草的田野。|Synonym: dandelion, chicory|Antonym: N/A|noun
