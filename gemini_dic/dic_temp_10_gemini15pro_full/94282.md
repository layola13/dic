impersonators:/ɪmˈpɜːrsəneɪtərz/| n. |模仿者；假扮者|A person who impersonates someone else, often for entertainment or fraudulent purposes.  A person who pretends to be another person, typically by adopting their mannerisms, voice, or appearance. |例句：The impersonators did a hilarious take on several famous politicians.  这些模仿者对几位著名政治人物进行了滑稽的模仿。

例句：The police arrested the impersonator who was pretending to be a police officer. 警察逮捕了那个假扮警察的冒名顶替者。 |近义词：mimic, imitator, copycat, actor, pretender|反义词:original, genuine|词性:noun
