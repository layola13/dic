overpowered:/ˌoʊvərˈpaʊərd/| adj.|压倒性的，过于强大的，无法抗拒的|Having too much power; excessively strong or influential.  Defeated by superior strength or force.|例句：The enemy was completely overpowered. 敌人被彻底击败了。|例句：The smell of the perfume was overpowered and gave me a headache. 香水的味道太浓了，让我头疼。|近义词：dominant, overwhelming, invincible, superior|反义词：weak, powerless, inferior|词性：形容词

overpower:/ˌoʊvərˈpaʊər/ | vt.|压倒，制服，使无法抗拒|To defeat or subdue by superior force. To overwhelm or overcome, especially with emotion.|例句：The police overpowered the suspect and arrested him. 警察制服了嫌疑犯并逮捕了他。|例句：Grief overpowered her after the loss of her child.  失去孩子后，悲痛压倒了她。|近义词：conquer, subdue, overwhelm, defeat|反义词：surrender, yield, succumb|词性：动词

词源:  From over- + power.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much".
