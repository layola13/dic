inspective:/ɪnˈspɛktɪv/ | adj. | 检查的；审视的；探究的 | Given to examining one's own thoughts and feelings; introspective.  Tending to examine things closely; observant. | Example 1: Her inspective gaze swept over the room, taking in every detail. 她审视的目光扫过房间，注意到每一个细节。

Example 2: He had an inspective nature, always questioning and analyzing. 他有一种探究的本性，总是质疑和分析。 | 近义词: analytical, observant, inquiring, scrutinizing, examining | 反义词: unobservant, careless, inattentive | 词性: adjective


词源: From Latin *inspectīvus*, from inspectus, past participle of inspicere (“to look into, inspect”).
