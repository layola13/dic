around-the-bend:/əˈraʊnd ðə bend/
adjective
疯狂的，精神错乱的
crazy, insane, mentally unstable.  The stress of the job drove him around the bend.  工作的压力把他逼疯了。
例句：
He went around the bend after losing his job and his wife in the same week. 他在同一周失去工作和妻子后精神崩溃了。

He acted like he was around the bend, shouting at everyone in the street. 他的行为像疯了一样，对着街上所有人大喊大叫。


近义词：crazy, insane, mad, unhinged, demented
反义词：sane, rational, lucid
词性：adjective


around-the-bend:/əˈraʊnd ðə bend/
adjective

例句：

近义词：remote, isolated, secluded, out-of-the-way
反义词：central, nearby, accessible
词性：adjective
