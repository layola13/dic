at-hand:/ætˈhænd/ | adj. | 在手边的，即将发生的 | readily available; close by; about to happen | I always keep a dictionary at hand when I'm writing. 我写作时总把字典放在手边。|  例句：The opportunity at hand is too good to miss.  眼前的机会太好了，不容错过。 | 近义词：available, accessible, imminent, approaching | 反义词：distant, remote, unavailable | 词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *at hand*, equivalent to *at* + *hand*.  The word "hand" here refers to nearness or proximity.
