abound-in:/əˈbaʊnd ɪn/| phrasal verb | 富于，盛产 |  to be rich in; to have a lot of something |  The forests abound in wildlife. 这些森林里有很多野生动物。| 近义词: be full of, be rich in, teem with | 反义词: lack, be deficient in | 词性:  phrasal verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French abonder, from Latin abundare "to overflow, abound," from ab- "from, away" + undare "to rise in waves," from unda "wave."  The "-in" specifies the area or thing in which something abounds.

USAGE->
一般现在时:
This lake abounds in fish. = 这个湖里有很多鱼。
The area abounds in natural resources. = 这个地区自然资源丰富。


一般过去时:
The garden abounded in colorful flowers last spring. = 去年春天，花园里开满了五颜六色的花。
The region abounded in coal during the Industrial Revolution. = 工业革命时期，该地区煤炭资源丰富。


现在完成时:
The country has abounded in opportunities for young people. = 这个国家一直为年轻人提供大量机会。
This forest has always abounded in deer. = 这片森林一直有很多鹿。


过去完成时:
The area had abounded in wildlife before the construction began. = 在开始建设之前，该地区野生动物丰富。
The river had abounded in salmon before the dam was built. = 在修建大坝之前，这条河里有很多鲑鱼。


将来时:
With careful planning, the city will abound in green spaces. = 只要精心规划，这座城市就会拥有大量的绿地。
The future will abound in technological advancements. = 未来科技将会非常发达。


虚拟语气:
If this land abounded in water, it would be a perfect place to live. = 如果这片土地水资源丰富，那将是一个理想的居住地。
I wish this area abounded in job opportunities. = 我希望这个地区有大量的就业机会。


被动语态 (abound is intransitive and generally not used in the passive voice)
N/A


疑问句:
Does this region abound in mineral resources? = 这个地区矿产资源丰富吗？
Did the forest abound in deer in the past? = 过去这片森林里有很多鹿吗？


否定句:
The desert does not abound in vegetation. = 沙漠里植被稀少。
The city didn't abound in cultural attractions in the past. = 这座城市过去文化景点不多。



PHRASE->
(Phrasal verbs don't typically form set phrases in the same way single words do.  Here are some usage examples within larger phrases)
areas that abound in wildlife =  野生动物丰富的地区
a land abounding in opportunities = 一个充满机会的土地
a region abounding in natural resources = 一个自然资源丰富的地区
countries abounding in sunshine = 阳光充足的国家
a market abounding in cheap goods = 一个充满廉价商品的市场


注意事项:
“abound in” 通常用于正式或文学性写作中。
