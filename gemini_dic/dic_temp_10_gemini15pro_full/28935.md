carromata:/ˌkærəˈmɑːtə/| n. |轻便马车，轻型双轮马车|From the Spanish *carromata*, diminutive of *carro* "cart," from Latin *carrus*.  Ultimately from Gaulish.  Primarily used in the Philippines.|例句：The carromata driver expertly navigated the crowded streets.  轻便马车夫熟练地在拥挤的街道上穿行。|例句：Riding in a carromata is a traditional way to experience the countryside.  乘坐轻便马车是体验乡村的传统方式。|近义词：kalesa (Philippines), calesa (Philippines),  carriage, cart, buggy|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
