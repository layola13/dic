outtinkling:/ˌaʊtˈtɪŋklɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的叮当声;发出比…更响亮的叮当声
surpassing in tinkling; making a louder tinkling sound than
The wind chimes were outtinkling the bells of the nearby church. 风铃的声音盖过了附近教堂的钟声。
近义词: outsounding, overriding
反义词:  being drowned out by, being quieter than
词性:v.
