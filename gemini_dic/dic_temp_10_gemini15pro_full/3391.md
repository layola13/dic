aficionado:/əˌfɪʃjəˈnɑːdoʊ/ (US) /əˌfɪʃəˈnɑːdəʊ/ (UK)| n.|爱好者；狂热者|A person who is very knowledgeable and enthusiastic about an activity, subject, or pastime.|He's a wine aficionado and has a cellar full of vintage bottles. 他是一位葡萄酒爱好者，地窖里收藏了大量的年份葡萄酒。|Synonyms: enthusiast, devotee, fan, buff, connoisseur|Antonyms: novice, beginner|Noun
