absent-mindedly: /ˌæbsəntˈmaɪndɪdli/
adverb
心不在焉地
In a manner showing that someone is not paying attention to what is happening around them because they are thinking about something else.
He absent-mindedly left his keys on the table. 他心不在焉地把钥匙忘在了桌子上。

She absent-mindedly stirred her coffee, lost in thought. 她心不在焉地搅着咖啡，陷入了沉思。

近义词: distractedly, inattentively, thoughtlessly
反义词: attentively, carefully, mindfully
词性: adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From absent-minded + -ly.  Absent-minded is from absent + minded. Absent comes from the Latin "absens," present participle of "abesse," meaning "to be away." Minded comes from the Old English "gemyndig," meaning "mindful."


USAGE->
She absent-mindedly walked into the wrong room. 她心不在焉地走错了房间。
He absent-mindedly put salt in his coffee instead of sugar. 他心不在焉地把盐放进了咖啡里，而不是糖。


PHRASE->
absent-mindedly doing something (心不在焉地做某事)
He was absent-mindedly tapping his pen on the desk. 他心不在焉地用笔敲着桌子。

speaking absent-mindedly (心不在焉地说着)
She answered absent-mindedly, her thoughts elsewhere.  她心不在焉地回答，思绪飘到了别处。

walking absent-mindedly (心不在焉地走着)
He was walking absent-mindedly down the street when he bumped into a lamppost. 他心不在焉地走在街上，撞到了路灯杆。

doing something absent-mindedly (心不在焉地做某事)
She absent-mindedly chewed on her pencil while trying to solve the problem. 她心不在焉地咬着铅笔，试图解决这个问题。

an absent-minded gesture/action (心不在焉的动作/姿态)
He made an absent-minded gesture with his hand as he spoke. 他说话时心不在焉地做了个手势。
