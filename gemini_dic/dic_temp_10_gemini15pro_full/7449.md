anisodactylous: /ˌænɪsoʊˈdæktɪləs/
adjective
不等趾的，异趾的
Having three toes pointing forward and one pointing backward, characteristic of most perching birds.
例句：The robin is an anisodactylous bird, allowing it to perch securely on branches. 知更鸟是一种不等趾的鸟，这使得它能够稳稳地栖息在树枝上。

例句：The anisodactylous foot structure is a key adaptation for arboreal life.  不等趾的足部结构是树栖生活的一个关键适应性特征。

近义词：zygodactyl (although technically different, refers to a similar arrangement with two toes forward and two back)
反义词：pamprodactyl (all four toes pointing forward)
词性：adjective

Etymology:
From Greek *ánisos* "unequal" + *dáktulos* "finger, toe"
