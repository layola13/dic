dilly-dally:/ˈdɪli ˌdæli/| v.|磨蹭，闲荡，浪费时间|To waste time or delay doing something. |例句：Stop dilly-dallying and get ready! 别磨蹭了，赶紧准备！

例句：They dilly-dallied over breakfast and missed the bus. 他们在吃早餐时磨磨蹭蹭，错过了公共汽车。|近义词：dawdle, procrastinate, linger, loiter|反义词:hurry, hasten, rush|词性:动词
