avalanching:/ˈævəlɑːntʃɪŋ/
verb|雪崩；涌现；迅速增加|increasing rapidly in quantity or intensity|例句：The number of complaints is avalanching. 投诉的数量正在激增。|例句：Support for the candidate avalanched after his televised speech.  候选人在电视讲话后获得了大量的支持。|近义词：surging, escalating, mushrooming, proliferating|反义词：dwindling, decreasing, diminishing|词性：v.
Etymology: From avalanche (n.), from French *avalance, from avaler "to descend," from Vulgar Latin *ad vallem "toward the valley," from Latin ad "to" + vallem (accusative of vallis) "valley."  The "-ing" suffix transforms the noun into a present participle, used here as a verb.
