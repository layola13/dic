boilerless:/ˈbɔɪlərles/| adj.|无锅炉的，免煮的|Lacking a boiler; designed to operate without a boiler.  This typically refers to systems that heat water or generate steam using methods other than a traditional boiler.|This new boilerless heating system is much more efficient.  这种新型的无锅炉供暖系统效率更高。|The campers enjoyed the convenience of boilerless instant noodles.  露营者们享受了免煮方便面的便利。|tankless, instant|boilered|adjective
