collapsar:/kəˈlæpsɑːr/
n.
坍缩星；塌缩星
A massive star that undergoes gravitational collapse, especially one that forms a black hole.  A hypothetical end stage in the evolution of very massive stars, resulting in the formation of a black hole.
The formation of a collapsar is thought to be associated with long-duration gamma-ray bursts. 坍缩星的形成被认为与长时间的伽马射线暴有关。
Scientists believe some gamma-ray bursts are signals of collapsars forming. 科学家们相信一些伽马射线暴是坍缩星形成的信号。
近义词: hypernova, supernova
反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially "stable star")
