exhilarated:/ɪɡˈzɪləreɪtɪd/| adj.|兴高采烈的，非常兴奋的|Feeling very happy and excited.|例句：I felt exhilarated after winning the race. 赢了比赛后我感到非常兴奋。|近义词：thrilled, elated, joyous, excited|反义词：depressed, down, sad, discouraged|词性:形容词

exhilarate:/ɪɡˈzɪləreɪt/| vt.|使兴高采烈，使非常兴奋|To make someone feel very happy and excited.|例句：The news of their engagement exhilarated the whole family. 他们订婚的消息令全家人都非常兴奋。|近义词：thrill, elate, excite, stimulate|反义词：depress, discourage, sadden, disappoint|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin exhilaratus, past participle of exhilarare (“to gladden, cheer”), from Latin ex- (“out, from”) + hilarare (“to cheer”), from hilaris (“cheerful”).
