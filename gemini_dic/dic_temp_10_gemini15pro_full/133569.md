nonflagrances:/ˌnɑːnˈfleɪɡrənsɪz/ | n. | 非现行犯状态；非公然犯罪状态 | The absence of a crime being committed openly or immediately. It refers to situations where an offense is not discovered or witnessed as it is happening. |  The police cannot arrest someone for a misdemeanor based on nonflagrances unless they have a warrant. 警方不能在非现行犯的情况下逮捕轻罪嫌疑人，除非他们有逮捕令。

The suspect was apprehended hours after the robbery, a clear case of nonflagrances.  嫌疑人在抢劫发生几小时后被捕，这是一个明显的非现行犯案例。 | 近义词: non-observance, unobserved commission | 反义词: flagrancy, in flagrante delicto | 词性: 名词
