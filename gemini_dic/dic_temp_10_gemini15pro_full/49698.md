demobbing:/diːˈmɒbɪŋ/
n.
复员，遣散（军人）
The process of discharging people from military service.
After the war, demobbing of the troops took several months. 战后，部队复员花了几个月的时间。
discharge,  muster out
enlistment, recruitment
noun
