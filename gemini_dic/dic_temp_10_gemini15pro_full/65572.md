evacuate: /ɪˈvækjueɪt/ (US), /ɪˈvækjʊeɪt/ (UK)| v. |撤离，疏散|To remove (someone) from a dangerous place.|The government evacuated the citizens before the hurricane hit. 政府在飓风来临前疏散了市民。|The building was evacuated due to a fire alarm. 因为火警，大楼里的人被疏散了。|近义词：remove, clear, withdraw|反义词: occupy, inhabit|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin evacuare ("to empty out"), from ex- ("out") + vacuus ("empty").
