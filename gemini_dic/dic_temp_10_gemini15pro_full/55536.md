distributions:/ˌdɪstrɪˈbjuːʃənz/ | n. | 分布；分配；分发 | The act of distributing or the state of being distributed.  The way in which something is spread or shared. |  The company optimized its product distributions to reach a wider market.  该公司优化了其产品分销，以覆盖更广阔的市场。 | synonyms: allocation, apportionment, dispersion, dissemination | antonyms: collection, concentration, gathering | noun

distributions:/ˌdɪstrɪˈbjuːʃənz/ | n. | (统计) 分布；概率分布 | A listing or function showing all the possible values (or intervals) of the data and how often they occur. | The normal distribution is commonly used in statistics. 正态分布在统计学中很常用。 | synonyms: frequency distribution, probability distribution | antonyms: N/A | noun
