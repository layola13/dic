normanist:/ˈnɔːrmənɪst/| n.|诺曼主义者，诺曼学派学者|A scholar specializing in Norman language, literature, history, or culture.  Someone who adheres to the Norman Conquest theory of the origin of the English language, which emphasizes the influence of Norman French.|例句：The normanist argued that many English words have French origins.  这位诺曼主义学者认为许多英语单词源自法语。

例句：The conference attracted normanists from all over the world.  这次会议吸引了来自世界各地的诺曼学派学者。|近义词：medievalist, historian, philologist|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps Anglo-Saxonist as someone focusing on the pre-Norman period) |词性:noun
