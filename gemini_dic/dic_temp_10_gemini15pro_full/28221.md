capsaicin:/kæpˈseɪɪsɪn/ | n. |辣椒素|A pungent crystalline compound present in chili peppers that causes a burning sensation.|例句：Capsaicin is the active component of chili peppers. 辣椒素是辣椒的活性成分。

例句：Researchers are exploring the medicinal uses of capsaicin. 研究人员正在探索辣椒素的药用价值。|近义词：capsicum oleoresin|反义词: None|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From New Latin *capsicum + -in, from Latin capsa (“box, case”) (because the fruit is hollow like a case).
