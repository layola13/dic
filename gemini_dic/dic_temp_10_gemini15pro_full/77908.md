genipap: /ˈdʒɛnɪpæp/ | n. | 染料木; 染料木果实; | A tropical American tree (Genipa americana) of the madder family, cultivated for its fruit and for a dye obtained from the fruit.  Also, the fruit of this tree; a dark purple berry.  Also, the dye itself. | The indigenous people used genipap to create intricate body paintings.  土著人用染料木果实创作复杂的彩绘。

The genipap fruit makes a delicious juice. 染料木果实可以做成果汁，味道鲜美。| 近义词: jagua | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific fruit/dye.  Could consider "bleach" if referring to the dyeing process) | 词性: noun
