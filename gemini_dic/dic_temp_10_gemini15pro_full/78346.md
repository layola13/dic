gesling:/ˈɡɛzlɪŋ/| n.|小鹅|A young goose, especially one that is not yet fully fledged.|例句：The gosling followed its mother closely. 小鹅紧紧地跟着它的妈妈。|例句：The farmer protected the goslings from predators. 农夫保护小鹅免受捕食者的侵害。|近义词：chick, young bird|反义词:adult goose|词性:noun

词源: From Middle English *goslyng, *gosling, equivalent to gos (from Old English gōs (“goose”)) + -ling (diminutive suffix). Cognate with Scots gosling (“gosling”), West Frisian goesling (“gosling”), Dutch gosseling (“gosling”), German Gänslein (“gosling”).
