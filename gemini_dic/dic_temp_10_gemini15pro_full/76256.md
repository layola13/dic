fuzing:/ˈfjuːzɪŋ/ | n. |引信，熔接，熔合|The act or process of joining by melting together, especially metal parts; also, the state of being fused. fuzing:/ˈfjuːzɪŋ/ | adj. |熔化的，熔合的|Being fused or melted together.  Related to or involving a fuse. |The fuzing temperature of the metal is very high.  这种金属的熔化温度非常高。|近义词：melting, fusing, blending |反义词：solidifying, hardening | adjective
