cambrian:/ˈkæmbriən/| adj. n.|寒武纪的，寒武纪|Relating to the Cambrian period or the system of rocks formed during it.  The Cambrian period is the earliest period of the Paleozoic era, marked by the first appearance of abundant fossil evidence.|例句：The Cambrian explosion saw a rapid diversification of life. 寒武纪大爆发见证了生命的快速多样化。

|例句：Cambrian rocks contain fossils of the earliest known complex life forms. 寒武纪岩石包含了已知最早的复杂生命形式的化石。|近义词：Early Paleozoic|反义词:Precambrian|词性:adjective, noun
