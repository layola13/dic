globule:/ˈɡlɒbjuːl/ (US) /ˈɡlɑːbjuːl/| n. |小球，小滴，微球|A small spherical body; a drop of liquid.|例句：A tiny globule of mercury rolled across the table. 一颗微小的汞珠滚过桌子。|例句：Globules of fat floated on the surface of the soup.  汤的表面漂浮着油脂小球。|近义词：drop, droplet, bead, sphere|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "film" or "sheet" for a spread-out state) |noun
