iguanas:/ɪˈɡwɑːnəz/
n.
鬣蜥；大蜥蜴
Large lizards, native to tropical parts of Central and South America and the Caribbean.
The iguanas basked in the sun on the rocks.  鬣蜥在岩石上晒太阳。
