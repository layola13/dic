fire-retardant: /ˌfaɪər rɪˈtɑːrdənt/ | adj. n. | 防火的；阻燃的；阻燃剂 |  A substance applied to materials to make them resistant to catching fire or to slow down the spread of fire. |  This sofa is covered in a fire-retardant fabric. 这款沙发覆盖着阻燃织物。 | Synonyms: flame-retardant, fireproof, nonflammable | Antonyms: flammable, inflammable | adjective

fire-retardant: /ˌfaɪər rɪˈtɑːrdənt/ | n. | 阻燃剂 | A substance used to make something fire-retardant. | They used a fire-retardant on the curtains. 他们在窗帘上使用了阻燃剂。 | Synonyms: flame retardant, fireproofing agent | Antonyms: accelerant | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From fire + retardant (from the verb retard, meaning "to slow down or delay," from Latin retardare, from re- "back" + tardare "to make slow").
