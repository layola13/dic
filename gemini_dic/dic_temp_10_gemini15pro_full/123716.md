moonlighter:/ˈmuːnˌlaɪtər/| n.|  兼职者；夜间工作者|Someone who holds a second job in addition to their regular employment, often working at night.| He's a moonlighter, working as a bartender after his day job as a teacher. 他白天当老师，晚上在酒吧兼职调酒师。|Synonyms: part-timer, second jobber|Antonyms: full-timer|noun

moonlight:/ˈmuːnlaɪt/ |v. |兼职；从事第二职业|To work at a second job, typically in addition to one's regular employment, often at night.|  She moonlights as a waitress to make ends meet. 她兼职做服务员来维持生计。|Synonyms: hold a second job, work part-time|Antonyms: work full-time|verb
