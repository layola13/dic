paedogenesis:/ˌpiːdəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
n.
幼体生殖，幼期生殖
Reproduction by immature or larval forms.
Certain salamanders exhibit paedogenesis, reproducing while still in the larval stage.  某些蝾螈表现出幼体生殖，在幼虫阶段就可以繁殖。
Paedogenesis allows some species to reproduce rapidly in favorable conditions. 幼期生殖使一些物种能够在有利条件下快速繁殖。
neoteny, progenesis
