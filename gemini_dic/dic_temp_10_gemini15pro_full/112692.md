lowlights:/ˈloʊlaɪts/| n.|低光部分；（尤指头发的）挑染的较深部分；不精彩的部分；败笔|The lowlights in her hair really brought out the color of her eyes. 她头发上挑染的深色部分衬得她的眼睛更漂亮了。|例句：The lowlights of the trip included the delayed flight and the lost luggage.  这次旅行的不愉快之处包括航班延误和行李丢失。

例句：The stylist added some lowlights to give her hair more dimension.  发型师给她挑染了一些深色，让她的头发更有层次感。|近义词：dark streaks, darker shades|反义词：highlights, brighter shades|词性:名词

lowlight:/ˈloʊlaɪt/| vt.|使（照片或影片的某一部分）显得较暗；淡化；不突出|He lowlighted the background to make the subject stand out more. 他调暗了背景，让主体更加突出。|例句：The report lowlighted the company's failures while glossing over its successes. 报告淡化了公司的成就，却着重强调了它的失败。

例句：She deliberately lowlighted her role in the project. 她故意淡化了自己在这个项目中的作用。|近义词：downplay, de-emphasize|反义词：highlight, emphasize|词性:动词
