hockherb:/ˈhɑːkˌɜːrb/ | /ˈhɒkˌhɜːb/ | n. |蜀葵，药蜀葵| A mallow plant (Althaea officinalis) whose roots and leaves were formerly used medicinally. |The hockherb was once a staple in apothecary gardens.  药蜀葵曾经是药剂师花园里的主要植物。| The hockherb has soothing properties. 药蜀葵具有舒缓的特性。|  近义词:marshmallow, althaea | 反义词: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *hokherbe*, from Old English *hoc* (“mallow”) + *wyrt* (“herb, plant, root”). Cognate with German *Hockkraut*. The "hock" part likely refers to the plant's use in treating ailments of the hocks (ankles) of horses.
