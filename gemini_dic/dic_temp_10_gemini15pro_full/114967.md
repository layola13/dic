manchesterize:/ˈmæntʃɪstəraɪz/
verb
曼彻斯特化
To transform a place (especially a city) in the style or manner of Manchester, often implying industrialization, urban development, or a particular cultural influence associated with Manchester.
The rapid growth of the textile industry manchesterized the small rural town.  这个小乡村城镇由于纺织工业的迅速发展而曼彻斯特化了。

The council's plans will further manchesterize the city centre.  委员会的计划将进一步使市中心曼彻斯特化。


Synonym: industrialize, urbanize, modernize
Antonym: ruralize, pastoralize
词性:v.
