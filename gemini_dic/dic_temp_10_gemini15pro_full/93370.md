illegitimation:/ɪˌlɪˌdʒɪtɪˈmeɪʃən/
n.
非法化；剥夺合法地位；宣布…为非法；污名化
the act of making something or someone illegitimate
例句：The illegitimation of his birth caused him much pain. 他的出身被宣布为非法，这给他带来了很大的痛苦。

例句：The government's actions led to the illegitimation of the opposition party. 政府的行为导致了反对党的非法化。


近义词：bastardization, delegitimization, invalidation
反义词：legitimation, validation
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French illégitimation, from illégitime (“illegitimate”) + -ation.
