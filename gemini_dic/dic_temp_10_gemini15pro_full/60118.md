effaces:/ɪˈfeɪsɪz/
|v.|消除，抹去，使不明显|To remove (a mark, for example) from a surface. To erase or obliterate.|Time gradually effaces the memory of childhood. 时间逐渐抹去了童年的记忆。|synonyms: erase, obliterate, expunge, delete|antonyms: enhance, emphasize, highlight|verb
effaces:/ɪˈfeɪsɪz/
|v.|使…谦逊；使…不突出|To make (oneself) inconspicuous; withdraw modestly.|He effaced himself so that others might take the spotlight. 他让自己变得不显眼，以便其他人可以成为焦点。|synonyms: withdraw, retire, recede|antonyms: assert, impose, intrude|verb
