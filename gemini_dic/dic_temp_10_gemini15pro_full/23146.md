bousy:/ˈbaʊzi/| adj.| 酗酒的，爱喝酒的；微醉的；喧闹的；爱吵闹的|Given to or marked by excessive drinking; slightly intoxicated; boisterous; rowdy.|He became rather bousy after several pints. 他喝了几品脱啤酒后变得相当醉醺醺的。|近义词：drunk, intoxicated, tipsy, boisterous, rowdy|反义词：sober, quiet, calm|词性:形容词

词源:  Likely a variant of "boozy," which comes from "booze," a word of uncertain origin, possibly related to Middle Dutch *būsen "to drink heavily."
