mockado:/ˌmɒkəˈdeɪdoʊ/ | n. | 摩卡多，一种模仿天鹅绒的厚织物 | A thick, rich fabric with a deep pile, often used for curtains and upholstery, designed to imitate velvet. |  The curtains were made of a luxurious mockado. 窗帘是用奢华的摩卡多制成的。|  The antique chair was upholstered in a vibrant crimson mockado.  这把古董椅子用鲜艳的深红色摩卡多装饰。 | 近义词: velvet, plush, velour | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑一些质地粗糙的布料) burlap, canvas | 词性: 名词
