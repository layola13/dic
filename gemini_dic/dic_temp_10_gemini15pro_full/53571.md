disbelieving:/ˌdɪsbɪˈliːvɪŋ/| adj.|不相信的，怀疑的|Showing or expressing lack of belief; skeptical.|He gave me a disbelieving look when I told him I'd won the lottery.  他听我说中了彩票后，给了我一个怀疑的眼神。|近义词：skeptical, incredulous, doubtful|反义词：believing, trusting, credulous|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From dis- (expressing negation) + believing (present participle of believe, from Old English belīefan "to believe," from be- (intensifying prefix) + līefan "to believe, allow, permit," from Proto-Germanic *laubjanan "to love, hold dear").
