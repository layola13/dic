candy-floss:/ˈkændi flɒs/ (UK) /ˈkændi flɔːs/ (US)| n.| 棉花糖 |A spun confection of sugar, often tinted with food coloring and served on a stick or cone.  It is made by spinning melted sugar into thin threads which solidify into a fluffy mass.| Example: The children clamored for candy-floss at the fair. 孩子们在 ярмарке吵着要棉花糖。

Example:  He bought a huge pink candy-floss. 他买了一个巨大的粉红色棉花糖。|近义词：cotton candy (US), spun sugar|反义词: None|词性: noun
