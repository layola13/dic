overidentifies:/ˌoʊvər aɪˈdɛntɪˌfaɪz/
|v.|过度认同，过度同一|To associate oneself too closely with someone or something else, often to the point of losing one's own identity or objectivity.|He overidentifies with his favorite sports team, to the point where their losses feel like personal failures.|Synonyms: over-empathize, over-relate|Antonyms: detach, disassociate|verb
