eighteenmo:/ˌeɪˈtiːnmoʊ/
n.
十八开本
A size of book resulting from folding a sheet of paper into eighteen leaves (thirty-six pages).
This rare first edition is an eighteenmo, bound in leather. 这本罕见的初版是十八开本，皮革装订。
近义词:octodecimo
反义词:(无)
词性:名词
