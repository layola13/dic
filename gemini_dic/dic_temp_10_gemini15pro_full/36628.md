collectivism: /kəˈlɛktɪvɪzəm/
n.
集体主义
A political or economic theory advocating collective control especially over production and distribution;  emphasis on collective rather than individual action or identity.
Collectivism is a core tenet of communism. 集体主义是共产主义的核心原则。
The kibbutz system in Israel is a form of collectivism.  以色列的基布兹制度是集体主义的一种形式。
近义词:communalism, socialism, communism
反义词:individualism, libertarianism
词性:noun

Etymology: From French *collectivisme*, from *collectif* (“collective”) + *-isme* (“-ism”).
