palleting:/ˈpælətɪŋ/| n.| 装货盘, 托盘化|The palleting of goods for shipment is an important part of the logistics process. 将货物装上货盘以便运输是物流过程中的重要环节。|例句：The efficient palleting of the products minimized shipping costs.  产品的高效托盘化最大限度地降低了运输成本。

例句：The company invested in automated palleting machinery. 公司投资了自动化装货盘机械。|近义词：palletization|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 unloading 卸货)|noun
