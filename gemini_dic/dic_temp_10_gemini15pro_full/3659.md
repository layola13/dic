agglomerate:/əˈɡlɒməreɪt/ (UK), /əˈɡlɑːməreɪt/ (US)| n. v. adj.|聚集体，凝聚；聚集，凝聚；聚集的|A mass of things clustered together.|例句：The rock was an agglomerate of various minerals. 这块岩石是由各种矿物聚集而成的。|近义词：aggregate, cluster, amass, accumulate|反义词:separate, disperse, scatter|词性:n.
