devotions:/dɪˈvoʊʃənz/| n.|虔诚；祈祷；宗教仪式；热爱；挚爱|acts of religious worship or private prayer; feelings of love or fondness|例句：She attended her daily devotions. 她参加了她的日常祈祷。

例句：He had a deep devotion to his family. 他对他的家人有着深厚的爱。|近义词：prayers, worship, piety, dedication, commitment, love, affection|反义词：indifference, apathy, neglect, hatred|词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin *devotionem (nominative devotio) "a dedicating, consecration," noun of action from past-participle stem of devovere "dedicate by vow, devote," from de "from" (see de-) + vovere "to vow" (see vow).
