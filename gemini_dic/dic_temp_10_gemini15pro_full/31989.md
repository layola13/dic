chemotherapies:/ˌkiːmoʊˈθɛrəpiz/| n.|化疗（复数）|The use of chemical agents in the treatment or control of disease, especially cancer, or the agents themselves.  It involves administering drugs that kill or inhibit the growth of rapidly dividing cells, including cancer cells.|She underwent several rounds of chemotherapies to combat the aggressive cancer. 她接受了几轮化疗来对抗恶性癌症。|Synonyms: chemo (informal), drug therapy,  antineoplastic therapy|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider) alternative medicine, holistic therapy|Noun
