non-abelian:/ˌnɒn əˈbiːliən/ | adj. | 非阿贝尔的 |  Relating to a group in which the order in which operations are performed affects the result.  In mathematics, specifically abstract algebra, a non-abelian group is a group for which the group operation is not commutative. | The quaternion group is a classic example of a non-abelian group. 四元数群是非阿贝尔群的经典例子。| Non-abelian gauge theories play a crucial role in the Standard Model of particle physics. 非阿贝尔规范理论在粒子物理标准模型中起着至关重要的作用。 | noncommutative | abelian, commutative | adjective
