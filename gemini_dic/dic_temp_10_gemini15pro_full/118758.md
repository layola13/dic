metallical: /məˈtælɪkəl/
adjective|金属的，含金属的|Relating to or resembling metal; containing metal.|The ore had a metallical luster. 矿石具有金属光泽。|近义词: metallic, metalline |反义词: nonmetallic|词性:形容词

词源: From Late Latin *metallicus, from Latin metallicum ("metal mine, mineral") + -al. Equivalent to metal + -ic + -al.
