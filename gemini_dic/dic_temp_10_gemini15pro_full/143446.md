outfoot:/ˌaʊtˈfʊt/ | v. | 超越，胜过；（尤指在速度或耐力方面）比……走得远，比……跑得快| to walk or run faster or farther than someone else |  He easily outfooted the other runners in the marathon. 他在马拉松比赛中轻松地超越了其他选手。 | synonyms: outrun, outpace, outstrip, surpass | antonyms: lag behind, fall behind | verb

outfoot:/ˌaʊtˈfʊt/ | n. | (古语，罕用) 出发，远行；最外面的脚 | An act of going out or away; the outer foot in walking or running |  The outfoot of the journey began at dawn. (古语用法) 旅程的出发在黎明时分。 | synonyms: departure, exit (for the first meaning, the second meaning has no direct synonym) | antonyms: arrival, entrance (for the first meaning, the second meaning has no direct synonym) | noun
