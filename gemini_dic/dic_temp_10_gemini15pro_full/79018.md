gittern: /ˈɡɪtərn/ | n. | 吉特琴 | A medieval stringed instrument, similar to a guitar, popular in Europe from the 13th to the 16th centuries. It typically had four or five courses of strings and a pear-shaped body. | The minstrel played a lively tune on his gittern.  吟游诗人用他的吉特琴演奏了一曲轻快的曲子。 | Synonym: cittern, guitar | Antonym: (No direct antonym for a musical instrument) | noun
