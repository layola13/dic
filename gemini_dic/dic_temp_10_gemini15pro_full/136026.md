nonrealizable:/ˌnɒnriːəˈlaɪzəbl/ /ˌnɑːnriːəˈlaɪzəbl/
adjective
不可实现的，无法实现的
Not capable of being realized or made real.
例句：The inventor's plans for a perpetual motion machine were ultimately nonrealizable.  发明家关于永动机的计划最终无法实现。

例句：Her dreams of becoming a famous actress seemed nonrealizable given her lack of experience.  鉴于她缺乏经验，她想成为著名女演员的梦想似乎无法实现。

近义词：unachievable, unattainable, impracticable, infeasible
反义词：realizable, achievable, attainable, feasible
词性: adjective

词源:  non- + realizable.  "Realizable" comes from the verb "realize," which in turn comes from the Old French "realiser" and ultimately from the Late Latin "realis," meaning "real."
