actions:/ˈækʃənz/ (US) /ˈækʃnz/ (UK)| n.| 行为，行动；作用；举动|The actions of the government have been criticized. 政府的行动受到了批评。|Her quick actions saved the child's life. 她的迅速行动救了孩子的命。|近义词：deeds, activities, movements, behaviors|反义词：inaction, passivity|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *accioun*, from Old French *accion*, from Latin *actiō*, from *agō* (“I do, I act”).

USAGE->
