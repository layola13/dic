hookiest:/ˈhʊkiɪst/| adj.|最喜欢逃学的，最喜欢偷懒的|Most inclined to play hooky; most given to shirking duties.  Characterized by or involving a high degree of truancy or shirking. |That kid is the hookiest one in the class; he's always finding an excuse to skip school. 那个孩子是班里最爱逃学的，他总能找到理由不去上学。|Synonyms: truant, shirking, indolent, lazy|Antonyms: diligent, hardworking, conscientious, attentive|adjective
