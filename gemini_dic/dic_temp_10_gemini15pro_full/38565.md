concoction:/kənˈkɒkʃn/ (US) /kənˈkɒkʃən/ (UK)| n. |混合物；调合物；调制品|A mixture of various ingredients or elements, often unusual or intriguing.  A concoction can refer to a drink, a dish, a story, or even a plan.|She whipped up a strange concoction of herbs and spices. 她调制了一种奇怪的香草和香料混合物。|mixture, blend, brew, combination|separation, division|noun
