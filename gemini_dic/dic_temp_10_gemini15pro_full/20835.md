blastodermatic:/ˌblæstədərˈmætɪk/
adjective
胚盘的, 囊胚层的
Relating to or derived from the blastoderm, the layer of cells forming the embryo of most animals.
The blastodermatic vesicle is a crucial stage in early embryonic development.  胚盘泡是早期胚胎发育的关键阶段。
The researchers studied the blastodermatic cells to understand their differentiation potential.  研究人员研究了胚盘细胞以了解它们的分化潜能。
Synonyms: blastodermic
Antonyms: None
