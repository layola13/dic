delict:/ˈdiːlɪkt/ /dəˈlɪkt/ | n. | 违法行为；民事侵权行为 | An act, especially a minor one, that is regarded as an offense against the law.  A wrongful act, especially a breach of duty in the law of torts or a minor offense. |例句：The delict of driving under the influence resulted in a hefty fine.  酒后驾驶的违法行为导致了巨额罚款。

例句：In some legal systems, a delict is distinguished from a crime. 在某些法律体系中，民事侵权行为与犯罪是有区别的。|近义词：offense, misdemeanor, tort, wrong, transgression |反义词:  nonfeasance (不作为), compliance (遵守) |词性: 名词
