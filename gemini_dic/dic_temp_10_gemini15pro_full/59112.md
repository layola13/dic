Dijkstra: /ˈdaɪkstrə/ | n. | 迪杰斯特拉 |  Edsger Wybe Dijkstra (1930-2002) was a Dutch computer scientist.  The term "Dijkstra" most commonly refers to Dijkstra's algorithm, a graph search algorithm that solves the single-source shortest path problem for a graph with non-negative edge path costs, producing a shortest path tree. | Dijkstra's algorithm is widely used in navigation systems. 迪杰斯特拉算法广泛用于导航系统中。 |  Synonym: (referring to the algorithm) shortest path algorithm | Antonym: N/A | Noun
