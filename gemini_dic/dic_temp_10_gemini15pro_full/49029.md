degreasers:/diːˈɡriːzərz/
|n.|脱脂剂，除油剂
|A substance used to remove grease and oil, typically a solvent or detergent.|
|Example 1:  Use a degreaser to clean the oven.  用除油剂清洁烤箱。

|Example 2: The mechanic used a powerful degreaser to clean the engine parts.  机械师用强力除油剂清洗发动机零件。
|Synonyms: cleaner, solvent, detergent|
|Antonyms: lubricant, oil|
|noun|
