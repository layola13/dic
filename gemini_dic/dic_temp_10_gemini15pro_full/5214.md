allin:/ˌɔːl ˈɪn/ | adj. adv.| 全押，孤注一掷 |  Putting all of one's resources or efforts into a single venture or undertaking; committed to a course of action without reservation. | He went all in with his poker hand. 他把所有的筹码都押上了。| 近义词：committed, invested, dedicated | 反义词: hesitant, cautious | 词性:形容词, 副词

allin:/ˌɔːl ˈɪn/ | n.| 全押，全部投入 | The act of putting all of one's resources or efforts into a single venture. | His all-in bet paid off handsomely. 他的孤注一掷获得了丰厚的回报。| 近义词：gamble, risk, venture | 反义词: hedge, safety, caution | 词性:名词

词源:  A shortening of the phrase "all in," originating from poker where it signifies betting all of one's chips.
