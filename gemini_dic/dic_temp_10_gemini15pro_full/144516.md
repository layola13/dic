outtricking: /ˌaʊtˈtrɪkɪŋ/
Verb
中文翻译：以计胜过，智胜，用计谋战胜
英文解析：defeating someone by being more clever or cunning than they are
例句：The spy succeeded in outtricking the enemy agents.  这个间谍成功地智胜了敌方特工。

例句：She outtricked him in negotiations and got a better deal. 她在谈判中用计谋胜过他，得到了更好的交易。
近义词：outmaneuvering, outsmarting, deceiving, tricking
反义词：being outwitted, being fooled
词性：v.
