funnel:/ˈfʌn(ə)l/| n. v.|漏斗，漏斗状物；汇集，集中 |A cone-shaped utensil with a tube at the apex for conducting liquid or other substances through a small opening, or something shaped like a funnel.  As a verb, it means to guide or channel something into a particular place or direction. |例句：The bartender used a funnel to pour the liquor into the bottle.  调酒师用漏斗把酒倒进瓶子里。


|近义词：channel, conduit, direct, filter, concentrate|反义词: disperse, scatter|词性: noun, verb

funnels:/ˈfʌn(ə)lz/| n. v.|漏斗，漏斗状物；汇集，集中（复数）|Plural form of "funnel."|例句：The factory uses several funnels to sort different sizes of nuts. 工厂使用多个漏斗来筛选不同大小的螺母。


|近义词：channels, conduits, filters|反义词: none|词性: noun
