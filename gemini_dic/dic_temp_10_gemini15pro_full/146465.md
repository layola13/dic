overfruitfully:/ˌoʊvərˈfruːtfəli/
adv. | 过于丰盛地，过度多产地 | Excessively fruitfully; producing an overabundance of fruit or results. | The orchard overfruitfully produced apples this year, leaving many to rot on the ground. 今年果园苹果产量过剩，许多苹果烂在地上。|Synonym: excessively, prolifically, abundantly | Antonym: sparsely, meagerly, barrenly | 词性: 副词

overfruitfully:/ˌoʊvərˈfruːtfəli/
