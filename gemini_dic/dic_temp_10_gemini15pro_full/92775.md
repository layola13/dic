ichus: /ˈaɪkəs/ | n. | 茅草；白茅 | A tall, perennial grass of the genus *Imperata*, especially *I. cylindrica*, with extensive rhizomes, native to warm temperate and tropical regions. It is often a troublesome weed but is also used as a thatching material and for making paper. |  The roof of the hut was thatched with ichus. 小屋的屋顶是用茅草盖的。 | synonyms: cogon grass, spear grass, blady grass | antonyms: N/A | noun
