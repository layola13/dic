bursten:/ˈbɜːrstən/ /ˈbɝːstən/| v.|爆裂，胀破；突然发作；充满|The dam burst and flooded the valley.  大坝决堤，淹没了山谷。|近义词：explode, rupture, break, crack, burst|反义词: mend, repair, contract|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bersten, from Old English *berstan, from Proto-Germanic *brestana, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰregs-. Cognate with Old High German brestan (“to burst, break”), Old Norse bresta (“to burst, crack”).
