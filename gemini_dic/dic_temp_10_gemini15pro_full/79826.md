glycosuria: /ˌɡlaɪkəˈsjʊəriə/ /ˌɡlaɪkoʊˈsjʊəriə/| n. | 糖尿 | The presence of glucose in the urine, typically a symptom of diabetes. |  Example 1: The doctor diagnosed glycosuria after finding glucose in the patient's urine sample. 医生在病人的尿样中发现葡萄糖后诊断出糖尿。

Example 2:  Glycosuria can be a sign of kidney problems as well. 糖尿也可能是肾脏问题的征兆。 | Synonym: glucosuria | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, absence of glucose in urine is normal) | noun
