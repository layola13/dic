at-risk:/æt ˈrɪsk/ | adj. | 处于危险中的，有风险的 |  In a dangerous position; vulnerable to negative outcomes. |  The at-risk students received extra support from the school.  处于危险中的学生得到了学校的额外支持。 | synonyms: endangered, vulnerable, threatened, imperiled, jeopardized | antonyms: safe, secure, protected | adjective


at risk:/æt ˈrɪsk/ | adj. | 处于危险中的，有风险的 | In a dangerous position; vulnerable to negative outcomes. |  Children at risk of abuse need special protection. 受到虐待风险的儿童需要特殊保护。| synonyms: endangered, vulnerable, threatened, imperiled, jeopardized | antonyms: safe, secure, protected | adjective

Etymology:  "at" (preposition indicating location) + "risk" (noun from Old French "risque," meaning "danger"). The phrase became used as a compound adjective in the mid-20th century.
