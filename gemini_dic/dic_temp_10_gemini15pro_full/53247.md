directory's:/dəˈrɛktəriːz/ /daɪˈrektəriːz/
noun
目录的；电话簿的
Possessive form of "directory," indicating ownership or association with a directory.  It signifies that something belongs to or is related to a particular directory.
This is the directory's main page. 这是该目录的主页。
The directory's contents are outdated.  这个目录的内容已经过时了。
Synonym: index, catalog, guide, register, listing
Antonym: (No direct antonym for a possessive form, but antonyms for "directory" could include) disorder, chaos, randomness
