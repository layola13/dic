outtyrannizes:/ˌaʊtˈtɪrəˌnaɪzɪz/
verb
压制; 比...更专横
to be a worse tyrant than; to surpass in tyranny; to tyrannize over more severely.
The new dictator outtyrannizes his predecessor, imposing even stricter curfews and limitations on free speech.  这位新的独裁者比他的前任更加专横，实施了更严格的宵禁和言论限制。
He outtyrannizes all who came before him. 他比所有前任都更加专横。
synonyms: dominates, oppresses, subjugates, overpowers
antonyms: liberates, empowers, frees, supports
词性: 动词
