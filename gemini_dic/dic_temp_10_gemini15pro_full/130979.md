nonastronomically:/ˌnɒnˌæstrəˈnɒmɪkəli/
adverb
非天文数字地；数额不大的；价格适中的
Not astronomically large; of a moderate amount or price.
The price of the house was, thankfully, nonastronomically high.  谢天谢地，这房子的价格并不高。
The damages were assessed at a nonastronomically high figure. 损失评估的数字并不高。
Synonym: reasonable, moderate, affordable, modest
Antonym: astronomical, exorbitant, excessive
词性: 副词
