butterier:/ˈbʌtəriər/| adj.|更黄油味的，更像黄油的|Containing or tasting of more butter than something else.|The cookies were butterier than usual.  这些饼干比平时更黄油味。|The bread was deliciously butterier. 这面包的黄油味真好吃。|近义词：richer, creamier|反义词：less buttery, drier|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From butter + -y + -er (comparative suffix). The word "butter" ultimately comes from the Latin "butyrum" and Greek "boutyron," both meaning "butter."
