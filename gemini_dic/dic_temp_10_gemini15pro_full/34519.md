cleaning: /ˈkliːnɪŋ/ (US), /ˈkliːnɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |清洁，打扫|The act of making something clean.  The removal of dirt, marks, or unwanted substances.|例句：The spring cleaning took all weekend. 春季大扫除花了整个周末。

例句：The cleaning of the river is a priority. 清理河流是首要任务。|近义词：tidying, washing, purifying|反义词:dirtying, soiling|词性:noun

Etymology: From Middle English *clensinge*, from Old English *clǣnsung* (“a cleansing, purification”),  verbal noun of *clǣnsian* (“to cleanse”), from *clǣne* ("clean").
