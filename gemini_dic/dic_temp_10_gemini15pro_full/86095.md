heart-searchers:/ˈhɑːrtˌsɜːrtʃərz/
|n.|探索内心的人；追求真爱的人；寻找精神寄托的人|People who are searching for deep meaning, emotional fulfillment, or spiritual truth in their lives.  Those who are on a quest for self-discovery and understanding of their own hearts.|例句：The retreat attracted heart-searchers from all walks of life.  这次静修吸引了各行各业探索内心的人。

例句：Many heart-searchers turn to philosophy and religion for answers. 许多追求精神寄托的人转向哲学和宗教寻求答案。
|近义词：seekers, soul-searchers, truth-seekers|反义词：conformists, followers|词性: 名词 (Noun)
