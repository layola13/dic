endear:/ɪnˈdɪr/ /ɛnˈdɪr/| vt.|使喜爱；使受钟爱|To cause to be loved or admired.|Her kindness endeared her to everyone. 她的善良使她受到大家的喜爱。|近义词：charm, captivate, attract|反义词：alienate, repel|verb

endearing:/ɪnˈdɪrɪŋ/ /ɛnˈdɪrɪŋ/| adj.|可爱的；讨人喜欢的|Inspiring love or affection.|He has an endearing smile. 他有一个迷人的微笑。|近义词：charming, lovable, appealing|反义词：irritating, annoying|adjective
