administer:/ədˈmɪnɪstər/
verb
管理，执行，给予
to manage, control, or execute; to give or apply (something, such as a drug or oath)
The nurse administered the medication to the patient.  护士给病人服用了药物。
The principal administers the school. 校长管理学校。

近义词:manage, direct, govern, execute, apply, dispense
反义词:neglect, ignore, withhold
词性:verb

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The manager administers the company's finances. = 经理管理公司的财务。
She administers first aid to the injured. = 她对伤者进行急救。

一般过去时:
The doctor administered a painkiller. = 医生给予了止痛药。
They administered the test to all the students. = 他们对所有学生进行了测试。

现在完成时:
The government has administered a new policy. = 政府已经实施了一项新政策。
He has administered the funds fairly. = 他公平地管理了资金。

过去完成时:

They had administered the survey before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前完成了调查。

将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
