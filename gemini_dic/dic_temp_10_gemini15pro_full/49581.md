demerge:/diːˈmɜːrdʒ/
verb
分拆，拆分
To separate (a part of a company) from the main business.
The company demerged its retail division last year.  该公司去年将其零售部门分拆了出去。
demerged:/diːˈmɜːrdʒ/
verb
分拆，拆分
To separate (a part of a company) from the main business.
They plan to demerge the telecommunications business from the parent company.  他们计划将电信业务从母公司中分离出来。
demerged:/diːˈmɜːrdʒ/
adjective
已分拆的，被拆分的
Relating to or denoting a company or part of a company that has been separated from a larger organization.
The demerged subsidiary is now listed on the stock exchange.  这家被拆分的子公司现在已在证券交易所上市。
近义词: separate, divest, spin off
反义词: merge, combine, consolidate
词性: verb, adjective
