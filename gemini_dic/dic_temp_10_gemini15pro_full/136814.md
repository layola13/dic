nonshrink:/ˌnɑːnˈʃrɪŋk/ | adj. | 不缩水的，防缩的 |  Made of a material that does not shrink when washed or dried.  | This shirt is nonshrink, so you don't have to worry about it getting smaller after washing.  这件衬衫是不缩水的，所以你不用担心洗后会变小。 | synonyms: preshrunk, washable, permanent press | antonyms: shrinkable | adjective
