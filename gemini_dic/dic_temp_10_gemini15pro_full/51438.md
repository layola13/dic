detesters:/dɪˈtɛstərz/
noun
憎恶者；厌恶者
People who strongly dislike or hate something or someone.
The singer's detractors and detractors were equally vocal.  这位歌手的诋毁者和憎恶者都同样直言不讳。
synonyms: haters, loathers, abominators, despisers
antonyms: admirers, lovers, supporters
n.

detest:/dɪˈtɛst/
verb
憎恶；厌恶
To dislike intensely; to hate.
I detest hypocrisy. 我憎恶虚伪。
synonyms: hate, loathe, abhor, despise
antonyms: love, adore, admire
v.
