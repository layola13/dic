idealess:/aɪˈdiːəˌlɛs/
adjective
无主意的；缺乏想法的；没创意的
Lacking ideas; unimaginative.
He was completely idealess about how to fix the problem. 他对于如何解决这个问题毫无头绪。
Synonyms: unimaginative, uninspired, uncreative, uninventive
Antonyms: imaginative, creative, inventive, inspired
词性: 形容词
