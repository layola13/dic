overamplify: /ˌoʊvərˈæmplɪfaɪ/ | v. | 过度放大；过度增强 | To amplify excessively; to make something seem larger, more important, or more intense than it actually is. | The media tends to overamplify minor incidents, turning them into national crises. 媒体倾向于过度放大一些小事件，把它们变成全国性的危机。

The politician's fiery rhetoric overamplified the public's fears.  这位政客激烈的言辞过度放大了公众的恐惧。 | 近义词: exaggerate, overstate, overemphasize, magnify | 反义词: downplay, minimize, understate | 词性: 动词
