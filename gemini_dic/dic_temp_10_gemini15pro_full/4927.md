aliphatic:/ˌælɪˈfætɪk/| adj.|脂肪族的|Relating to or denoting organic compounds in which carbon atoms form open chains (as in the alkanes), not aromatic rings.|例句：Aliphatic hydrocarbons are less stable than aromatic hydrocarbons. 脂肪烃不如芳香烃稳定。|例句：The aliphatic chain of the molecule is hydrophobic. 分子的脂肪族链是疏水的。|近义词：non-aromatic, acyclic|反义词:aromatic|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *aleiphar, *aleiphatos (“fat, oil”) + -ic.
