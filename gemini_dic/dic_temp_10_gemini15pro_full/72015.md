flinty:/ˈflɪnti/ /ˈflɪn.t̬i/| adj.|坚硬的；冷酷无情的；坚定的；燧石似的|Resembling flint; unyielding; showing an unfeeling determination; very hard.|His flinty stare made her uncomfortable. 他那冷酷无情的凝视让她感到不舒服。|近义词：hard, unyielding, inflexible, stern, impassive, steely|反义词：soft, yielding, compassionate, sympathetic|词性:形容词

flint:/flɪnt/ /flɪnt/| n.|燧石；打火石；极硬的东西|A very hard grey or black stone that produces sparks when struck against steel.|Early humans used flint to make tools. 早期人类用燧石制作工具。|近义词：stone, rock, quartz|反义词： (No direct antonym for the material itself)|词性:名词
