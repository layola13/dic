liposuction:/ˈlɪpoʊˌsʌkʃən/ /ˈlaɪpəʊˌsʌkʃən/
|n.|抽脂; 吸脂术|The surgical removal of fat beneath the skin with the aid of suction.  通过抽吸辅助手术去除皮下脂肪。|She's considering liposuction to remove excess fat from her thighs. 她正在考虑抽脂来去除大腿上多余的脂肪。|Synonyms: liposculpture, body contouring|Antonyms: fat grafting, fat injection|noun
