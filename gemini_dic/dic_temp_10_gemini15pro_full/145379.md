overcareless:/ˌoʊvərˈkɛrlɪs/| adj.| 过于粗心的，极不小心的 | Excessively careless; lacking in due care or attention.  Derived from "over-" (excessively) + "careless" (lacking care). | He made an overcareless mistake and lost the game. 他犯了一个极其粗心的错误，输掉了比赛。|  She was overcareless with her belongings and often lost things. 她对自己的物品太粗心了，经常丢东西。 | negligent, reckless, heedless | careful, cautious, meticulous | adjective
