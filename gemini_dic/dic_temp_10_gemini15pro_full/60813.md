electrophotographic:/ɪˌlɛktrəʊˌfəʊtəˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. | 电子照相的 | Relating to or produced by electrophotography, a process of making copies using the effects of light on an electrically charged surface. | Electrophotographic printers are commonly used in offices. 电子照相打印机在办公室里很常用。| 近义词: xerographic | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 形容词

electrophotography:/ɪˌlɛktrəʊfəˈtɒɡrəfi/ | n. | 电子照相术 | A photocopying process that uses electrostatic charges to create an image on a light-sensitive surface, which is then transferred to paper. | Electrophotography is the technology behind laser printers and copiers. 电子照相术是激光打印机和复印机背后的技术。| 近义词: xerography | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词
