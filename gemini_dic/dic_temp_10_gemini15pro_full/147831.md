overpunch:/ˈoʊvərˌpʌntʃ/| n. vt.|重复打孔|The act of punching a hole in a punched card on top of an existing hole to represent a sign (+ or -) or a specific character. This was common in older data processing systems.|The program detected an overpunch in column 12, indicating a negative value. 程序在第12列检测到一个重复打孔，表示负值。|Near synonyms: zone punch, multi-punch|Antonym: N/A (The opposite would be the absence of an overpunch, which is simply a standard punch.) |noun, verb
