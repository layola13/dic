overdispersion:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈspɜːrʒən/| n. |过度离散|The tendency of data to be more variable than would be expected based on a given statistical model, usually the Poisson distribution.  It indicates that the variance is greater than the mean. |The researchers found evidence of overdispersion in the data, suggesting the Poisson model was not appropriate. 研究人员发现数据中存在过度离散的证据，表明泊松模型不合适。

The overdispersion in the number of accidents per month suggests that there may be unobserved factors influencing the accident rate. 每月事故数量的过度离散表明可能存在未观察到的因素影响事故率。|近义词:extra-Poisson variation, heterogeneity|反义词:underdispersion|词性:noun
