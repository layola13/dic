leafcutter:/ˈliːfˌkʌtər/| n.|切叶蚁|An ant of the genera Atta and Acromyrmex, native to South and Central America and parts of the southern US, which cuts and carries pieces of leaves back to its nest to cultivate the fungi on which it feeds.|Leafcutter ants can carry objects many times their own weight. 切叶蚁可以搬运比自身重许多倍的物体。|
Leafcutter ants are fascinating creatures to observe.  观察切叶蚁是件令人着迷的事情。|synonyms:  ant,  Atta, Acromyrmex|antonyms:  N/A|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From leaf + cutter.  "Cutter" ultimately derives from the Proto-Germanic *kuttan, meaning "to cut."
