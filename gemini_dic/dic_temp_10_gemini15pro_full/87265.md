herling:/ˈhɜːrlɪŋ/ | n. | 投掷；投石；男子投石球运动 (爱尔兰)| An Irish game resembling field hockey, played with curved sticks and a small ball.| He is a skilled hurler in herling. 他是投石球运动中的技术娴熟的球员。 |近义词：hurling, throwing, casting |反义词: catching |词性: noun

Etymology: From Irish *iorling, from *ior "to throw, cast".
