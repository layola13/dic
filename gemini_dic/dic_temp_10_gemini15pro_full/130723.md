nonalliances:/ˌnɒnəˈlaɪənsɪz/
n.
非联盟，不结盟
The state of not being allied with any other country or group.
Many small nations choose a path of nonalliances in international conflicts. 许多小国在国际冲突中选择不结盟的道路。
The country's policy of nonalliances kept it out of the war. 该国的不结盟政策使其免于战争。
neutrality, nonalignment
alliances, coalition
noun
