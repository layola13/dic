nondivinities:/ˌnɒndɪˈvɪnɪtiz/
n.
非神，凡人
people or things that are not divine or godlike
The ancient Greeks believed that nondivinities were subject to the whims of the gods.  古希腊人认为，凡人受神灵的摆布。
近义词:mortals, humans
反义词:divinities, gods
词性:名词
