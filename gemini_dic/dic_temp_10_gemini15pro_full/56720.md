doorbelled:/dɔːrˈbɛld/ | v. | 按门铃 | To ring or press a doorbell.  The action of ringing someone's doorbell. |  I doorbelled three times, but no one answered. 我按了三次门铃，但没有人应门。| ring, chime, buzz | knock | verb
ETYMOLOGY->
From *doorbell* + *-ed*. *Doorbell* is a compound of *door* and *bell*. *Door* comes from Old English *dor*. *Bell* comes from Old English *belle*. The *-ed* suffix indicates the past tense or past participle form of a verb.
