multisport:/ˈmʌltiˌspɔːrt/ /ˈmʌltaɪˌspɔːrt/| n.|多项运动|A competition or event involving several different sports.|The multisport race included kayaking, running, and cycling. 这项多项运动比赛包括皮划艇、跑步和自行车。|近义词：multi-sport event, combined sports competition|反义词：single-sport event, individual sport competition|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From multi- (prefix meaning "many, multiple") + sport.  The prefix "multi-" comes from Latin "multus" meaning "much, many."
