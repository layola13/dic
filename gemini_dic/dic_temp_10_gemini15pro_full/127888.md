neodamode:/ˈniːoʊdəˌmoʊd/| n.|新式女装; 新女性时尚; 新女性风尚|A style of dress and behavior popular among fashionable young women in the early 20th century, characterized by a rejection of traditional feminine styles and an embrace of more masculine or androgynous looks.|Flappers were the epitome of neodamode in the 1920s.  20世纪20年代，轻佻女郎是新女性时尚的缩影。|Synonyms: flapper style, modern style, garçonne look|Antonyms: traditional style, Victorian style|noun
