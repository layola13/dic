boldnesses:/ˈboʊldnəsɪz/
n.
大胆；勇敢；冒昧；鲁莽
The quality or state of being bold; audacity.
He showed great boldness in confronting the bully. 他在面对恶霸时表现出极大的勇气。
近义词: audacity, courage, bravery, nerve, daring
反义词: timidity, cowardice, fearfulness
词性: 名词(plural)
