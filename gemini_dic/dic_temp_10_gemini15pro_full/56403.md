doles:/doʊlz/| n. |施舍物；失业救济金|Something given as charity, especially a small portion of money or food; government payments made to unemployed people.|The government provided doles to the unemployed during the recession. 政府在经济衰退期间向失业者提供救济金。|Synonyms: handout, allowance, benefit, charity|Antonyms: earnings, income|noun
doles:/doʊlz/| v. (doles out) |少量地发放；谨慎地给予|To distribute something in small portions, often reluctantly or sparingly.|The cook doled out the meager rations to the hungry refugees.  厨师把少量的口粮分发给饥饿的难民。|Synonyms: distribute, dispense, allot, apportion|Antonyms: hoard, withhold|verb
