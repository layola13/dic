caressed:/kəˈrɛst/| vt.|爱抚，抚摸|To touch someone or something gently and affectionately.|She caressed the soft fur of the kitten. 她轻抚着小猫柔软的皮毛。|近义词：stroked, touched, fondled|反义词：struck, hit|verb

词源: From Old French caresser "to caress," from Vulgar Latin *caritiare "to make dear," from Latin carus "dear."
