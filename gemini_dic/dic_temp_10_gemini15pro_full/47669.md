debris:/dəˈbriː/ (US), /ˈdeɪbriː/ (UK)| n. |碎片，残骸；废墟|scattered fragments, typically of something wrecked or destroyed|例句：The debris from the explosion was scattered over a wide area. 爆炸的碎片散落在很大一片区域。

例句：After the storm, the beach was covered in debris.  暴风雨过后，海滩上满是残骸。

近义词：rubble, wreckage, remains, fragments|反义词:  whole, intact|词性: 名词
