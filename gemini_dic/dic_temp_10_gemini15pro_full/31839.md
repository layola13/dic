cheeking:/ˈtʃiːkɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
厚着脸皮做; 嬉皮笑脸地做;  (尤指)无耻地做
acting in a bold or disrespectful way, especially in a way that shows no shame
He had the nerve to come cheeking me after what he'd done. 他竟然有脸在他做了那件事之后还嬉皮笑脸地对我说话。

cheeking:/ˈtʃiːkɪŋ/
noun (uncountable)
厚脸皮的行为; 嬉皮笑脸的行为; (尤指)无耻的行为
the act of behaving in a bold or disrespectful way, especially in a way that shows no shame
I've had enough of his cheeking. 我受够了厚脸皮的行为。

近义词: impudence, audacity, nerve, boldness
反义词: respect, deference, humility

词性:
verb (present participle), noun (uncountable)
