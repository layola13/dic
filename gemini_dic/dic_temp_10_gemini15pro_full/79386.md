glided:/ɡlaɪdɪd/| v. |滑行，滑翔，飘动|moved smoothly and effortlessly, as if floating|The swan glided gracefully across the lake. 天鹅优雅地在湖面上滑行。|The skater glided effortlessly across the ice.  滑冰者毫不费力地在冰面上滑行。|近义词：soared, drifted, floated|反义词: stumbled, lurched|verb

Etymology->
From Old English *glīdan, from Proto-Germanic *glīdaną (“to glide, slide”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰleydʰ- (“to slip, slide”). Cognate with Old Norse glīða (“to glide”), Dutch glijden (“to glide, slide”), German gleiten (“to glide, slide”).
