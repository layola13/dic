demagogue: /ˈdeməɡɒɡ/ (UK) /ˈdeməɡɑːɡ/ (US)| n. |煽动者，蛊惑民心的政客|A political leader who seeks support by appealing to popular desires and prejudices rather than by using rational argument.| Hitler was a demagogue who exploited people's fears and prejudices. 希特勒是一个利用人们的恐惧和偏见煽动民心的政客。|近义词：rabble-rouser, agitator, firebrand|反义词：statesman, diplomat|词性: noun

demagogues: /ˈdeməɡɒɡz/ (UK) /ˈdeməɡɑːɡz/ (US)| n. |煽动者们，蛊惑民心的政客们|Plural form of demagogue.|  Many demagogues throughout history have used fear-mongering to gain power. 历史上许多煽动者都利用散布恐惧来获得权力。|近义词：rabble-rousers, agitators, firebrands|反义词：statesmen, diplomats|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek dēmagōgos (δημαγωγός, “leader of the people”), from dēmos (δῆμος, “people”) + agōgos (ἀγωγός, “leader”).
