imprecisenesses:/ɪmˌprɪˈsaɪsnəsɪz/
|n. (plural)|不精确性；不准确性；模糊性|The imprecisenesses in the report made it difficult to draw any firm conclusions. 报告中的不精确性使得难以得出任何确凿的结论。|Example: The imprecisenesses of the language used in the contract led to a dispute. 合同中使用的语言不精确导致了争议。|Synonyms: inaccuracies, vagueness, ambiguities, uncertainties|Antonyms: precision, accuracy, clarity, exactness|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From imprecision + -ness.  Imprecision comes from im- (not) + precision. Precision ultimately derives from Latin *praecīsiō, from praecīsus (cut off in front).
