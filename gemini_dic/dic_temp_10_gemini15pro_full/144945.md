overabstaining:/ˌoʊvərəbˈsteɪnɪŋ/ | adj. |过度节制; 过度禁欲|Practicing excessive abstinence, typically from alcohol or other indulgences.| He was overabstaining from all forms of pleasure, making him seem joyless. 他过度节制所有形式的享乐，让他看起来毫无乐趣。|She became so obsessed with healthy eating that some worried she was overabstaining. 她对健康饮食如此痴迷，以至于有些人担心她过度节制。|近义词: over-restricting, excessively abstaining |反义词: indulging, overindulging |词性:形容词

词源:
"over-" (prefix) + "abstain" (verb)
"over-" 表示“过度”，“超过”。
"abstain" 源自拉丁语 "abstinere"，由 "abs-" (from) + "tenere" (to hold) 组成，意思是“远离，克制”。
