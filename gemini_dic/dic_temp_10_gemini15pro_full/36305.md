cohesionless:/koʊˈhiːʒən.ləs/
adjective
无凝聚力的；不粘结的
Lacking cohesion; unable to stick together.
The cohesionless soil is prone to erosion.  无凝聚力的土壤容易被侵蚀。
The dry sand was cohesionless and flowed easily. 干燥的沙子没有凝聚力，很容易流动。
Synonyms: loose, friable, unbound, unconsolidated
Antonyms: cohesive, bound, consolidated, compact
word class: adjective
