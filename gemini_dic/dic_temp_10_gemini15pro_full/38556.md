conclusiveness:/kənˈkluːsɪvnəs/
noun
结论性；决定性；确凿性
The quality of being conclusive; putting an end to doubt or question.
The conclusiveness of the evidence led to a guilty verdict.  证据的确凿性导致了有罪判决。
The DNA evidence provided the conclusiveness needed to close the case. DNA证据提供了结案所需的结论性证据。
synonyms: decisiveness, definitiveness, finality, certainty
antonyms: inconclusiveness, ambiguity, uncertainty, vagueness
n.
