bivalves:/ˈbaɪˌvælvz/
n.
双壳贝类
Aquatic mollusks having a shell consisting of two hinged valves.
Clams, oysters, and mussels are bivalves. 蛤蜊、牡蛎和贻贝都是双壳贝类。
shellfish, mollusks
univalves
noun

bivalve:/ˈbaɪˌvælv/
n.
双壳贝
A single shellfish of the bivalve group.
The bivalve was found washed up on the shore.  这个双壳贝被发现冲上了岸。
clam, oyster, mussel
univalve
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *bivalvis (“having two valves”), from bi- (“two”) + valva (“valve, leaf of a folding door”).
