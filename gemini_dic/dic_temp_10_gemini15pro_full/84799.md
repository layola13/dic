handsprang:/ˈhændspræŋ/ | v. |手工编织（一种不用针或钩针的编织技法）| to create a type of lace by twisting and looping threads with the hands, without using needles or hooks.  A technique for making lace without using needles, by manipulating threads wrapped around the fingers. | She handsprang a beautiful lace shawl. 她手工编织了一条漂亮的蕾丝披肩。|  She painstakingly handsprang the delicate trim for the wedding dress. 她煞费苦心地为婚纱手工编织了精致的饰边。| 近义词: knot, braid, plait | 反义词: untie, unravel | 词性: 动词
