heliotropic:/ˌhiːliəˈtrɒpɪk/ /ˌhiːlioʊˈtrɑːpɪk/| adj.|向日性的，趋日性的|Turning or growing in the direction of the sun.|Sunflowers are heliotropic, always facing the sun. 向日葵是向日性的，总是面向太阳。|The heliotropic plant followed the sun across the sky.  这种向日性植物跟随太阳划过天空。|近义词：phototropic|反义词: aphototropic|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Greek *hēlios* (sun) + *trope* (a turning)
