bilestone:/ˈbɪlˌstoʊn/ | n. | 胆结石 | A hard, crystalline mass formed abnormally in the gallbladder or bile ducts, consisting chiefly of cholesterol, calcium salts, and bile pigments. |  My grandmother had to have her gallbladder removed because of bilestones. 我祖母因为胆结石不得不切除胆囊。|  Gallstone, cholelith | 反义词: None | noun
