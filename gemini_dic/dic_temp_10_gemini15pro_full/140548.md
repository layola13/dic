olfactories:/ɒlˈfæktəriz/ /oʊlˈfæktəriz/| n.|嗅觉器官|The parts of the body related to the sense of smell, specifically the nasal passages and olfactory bulbs in the brain.  |Dogs have highly developed olfactories. 狗的嗅觉器官非常发达。|The mice used their olfactories to locate the cheese.  老鼠用它们的嗅觉器官找到了奶酪。|synonyms: noses, olfactory systems, olfactory organs|antonyms: N/A|noun

olfactory:/ɒlˈfæktəri/ /oʊlˈfæktəri/| adj.|嗅觉的|Relating to the sense of smell. |The olfactory nerve transmits signals from the nose to the brain. 嗅觉神经将信号从鼻子传递到大脑。|The olfactory system is crucial for many animals' survival. 嗅觉系统对许多动物的生存至关重要。|synonyms: aromatic, scented, odoriferous|antonyms: anosmic|adjective
