machs:/mɑːks/ /mæks/| n. |马赫数|A unit of speed equal to the speed of sound in a given medium, used especially of aircraft.  It is named after Austrian physicist Ernst Mach.|The jet flew at Mach 2.  这架喷气式飞机以2马赫的速度飞行。|近义词: supersonic speed, hypersonic speed |反义词: subsonic speed |词性:noun

Etymology:  From the surname of Ernst Mach (1838–1916), Austrian physicist and philosopher.
