metamictization:/ˌmɛtəˌmɪktɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.|蜕晶质化；非晶化|The process by which a crystalline material, especially a mineral, becomes amorphous (non-crystalline) due to radioactive decay within its structure.|The zircon crystals underwent metamictization due to prolonged exposure to uranium.  锆石晶体由于长时间暴露于铀而发生蜕晶质化。|amorphization, disordering|crystallization, ordering|noun
