countereducational:/ˌkaʊntərˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃənəl/
adjective
反教育的，与教育目标相悖的
Going against the aims of education; not conducive to learning.
The professor argued that the new policy was countereducational and would only hinder student progress. 教授认为这项新政策与教育目标相悖，只会阻碍学生的进步。
counterproductive, detrimental, unhelpful
beneficial, constructive, helpful
