operant:/ˈɒpərənt/ (UK) /ˈɑːpərənt/ (US)| adj. n.|操作性的，操作性的反应; 操作者|Influencing or controlled by the consequences of behavior.  A response that is emitted and controlled by its consequences. |The child's operant behavior of crying was reinforced by the mother's attention.  这孩子的哭闹这种操作性行为因母亲的关注而得到强化。|The operant was rewarded with a treat. 操作者得到了奖励。|synonyms:  behavioral, learned, conditioned|antonyms:  unconditioned, reflexive, innate|adjective, noun

operant:/ˈɒpərənt/ (UK) /ˈɑːpərənt/ (US)| n.|操作者|A person or animal that performs an operant behavior.|The operant in the experiment learned to press the lever for food.  实验中的操作者学会了按压杠杆获取食物。|synonyms:  subject, participant, actor|antonyms: experimenter, observer|noun
