emcee:/ˌemˈsiː/| n. vt.|司仪，主持人；主持|A person who acts as host at a formal occasion, especially an entertainment.|The emcee introduced the band with a flourish. 司仪隆重地介绍了乐队。|近义词：host, presenter, moderator|反义词:audience, guest|词性:名词,动词
emcee:/ˌemˈsiː/| v. |主持|To act as master of ceremonies at (an event).|He emceed the awards ceremony last night. 他昨晚主持了颁奖典礼。|近义词：host, present, moderate|反义词:attend, participate|词性:动词
