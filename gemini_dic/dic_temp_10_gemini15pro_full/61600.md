emery: /ˈeməri/ (US), /ˈɛməri/ (UK)| n.|金刚砂，刚玉粉|A very hard mineral substance used for grinding and polishing.|例句：He used emery paper to smooth the surface of the wood. 他用砂纸打磨木头的表面。

例句：Emery boards are essential for manicures.  金刚砂板对于美甲来说是必不可少的。|近义词：corundum, abrasive|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *esmeril*, from Old French *esmeril*, from Medieval Latin *smerillus*, alteration of Latin *smaragdus* (“emerald”), from Ancient Greek σμάραγδος (*smáragdos*) (“emerald, emery”).  Doublet of emerald.
