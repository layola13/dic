anthropometrical:/ˌænθrəpəˈmɛtrɪk(ə)l/
adjective
人体测量的
Relating to anthropometry; the scientific study of the measurements and proportions of the human body.
Anthropometrical data is used in designing furniture and clothing.  人体测量数据用于设计家具和服装。
Anthropometrical studies helped researchers understand the physical changes during puberty. 人体测量研究帮助研究人员了解青春期期间的身体变化。
Synonyms:  biometric, morphometric
