flutier:/ˈfluːtiər/ (US), /ˈfluːtɪə/ (UK)| adj.|更像长笛的，笛声更清脆的|More flute-like; having a clearer, purer flute-like tone.|Her voice became flutier as she sang the high notes. 她的声音在唱高音时变得更像长笛声了。|近义词：melodious, sweet, clear|反义词：raspy, hoarse, harsh|词性:形容词

Etymology:  Derived from "flute" (ultimately from Old French "flaute" and Old Provençal "flauta") with the comparative suffix "-ier."
