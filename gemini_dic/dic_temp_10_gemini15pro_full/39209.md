conglaciate:/kənˈɡleɪʃieɪt/ /kənˈɡlæsieɪt/
verb
凝结，冻结
to change from a soft or fluid state to a solid or rigid state, as by freezing or gelling.
The water conglaciated into ice overnight. 水一夜之间冻成了冰。
freeze, solidify, harden
melt, liquefy
