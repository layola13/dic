co-existence:/ˌkoʊɪɡˈzɪstəns/
n.|共存，共处|The peaceful co-existence of different cultures is a goal worth striving for.  不同文化间的和平共处是一个值得努力的目标。|例句：The co-existence of predators and prey is a delicate balance. 捕食者和猎物的共存是一种微妙的平衡。|近义词：cohabitation, living together, symbiosis|反义词：conflict, antagonism|词性:名词

co-exist:/ˌkoʊɪɡˈzɪst/
v.|共存，共处|Different species of animals can co-exist in the same habitat. 不同种类的动物可以在同一栖息地共存。|例句：Can different political ideologies co-exist peacefully? 不同的政治意识形态能否和平共存？|近义词：cohabit, live together|反义词：clash, conflict|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From co- + existence.  Co- is a prefix meaning "together," "jointly," or "mutually." Existence comes from the Latin "existentia," meaning "being," "presence," or "reality."
