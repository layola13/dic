furrower:/ˈfʌrəʊər (UK), ˈfɜːroʊər (US)| n.|犁沟器，开沟器|A device or implement used to make furrows or grooves, especially in the soil for planting seeds or irrigation.|The farmer used a furrower to create neat rows for planting his corn. 农民用开沟器犁出整齐的沟来播种玉米。|Synonyms: groover, trench digger, furrow maker|Antonyms: leveler, smoother|noun
