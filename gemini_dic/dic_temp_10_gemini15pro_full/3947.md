agroforester:/ˌæɡrəʊˈfɒrɪstər/ | n. |农林复合经营者，农林学家|A person who practices or studies agroforestry, a land use management system in which trees or shrubs are grown around or among crops or pastureland. |例句：The agroforester explained the benefits of integrating trees into farming systems.  农林复合经营者解释了将树木融入农业系统的好处。

|例句：She trained as an agroforester to help communities develop sustainable agricultural practices. 她接受了农林复合经营者的培训，以帮助社区发展可持续的农业实践。|近义词：forester, agriculturalist, silviculturist |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
