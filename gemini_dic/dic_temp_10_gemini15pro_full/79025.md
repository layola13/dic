receivables:/rɪˈsiːvəblz/
|n.|应收款项|Amounts owed to a company by its customers for goods or services delivered or used but not yet paid for.  These are assets for the company.|The company's receivables totaled $1 million at the end of the quarter. 该公司在本季度末的应收款总额为100万美元。|Synonyms: debts owed, outstanding balances|Antonyms: payables|Noun
