overimaginativeness:/ˌoʊvərɪˌmædʒɪˈnətɪvnəs/ | n. | 过度想象力；想象力过剩 |  The quality of having an overly active imagination, often leading to unrealistic or fantastical thoughts. |  His overimaginativeness often got him into trouble as a child. 他小时候想象力太丰富，经常给他惹麻烦。| synonyms:  fantasy, fancifulness, fantasticalness | antonyms:  realism, practicality | noun
