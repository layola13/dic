biotechnology:/ˌbaɪoʊtekˈnɒlədʒi/ /ˌbaɪoʊtekˈnɑːlədʒi/| n. |生物科技|The use of living organisms or other biological systems in the manufacture of drugs or other products or for environmental management, etc. |The company is investing heavily in biotechnology research. 这家公司正在大力投资生物技术研究。|synonyms: bioengineering, genetic engineering|antonyms: None|noun
