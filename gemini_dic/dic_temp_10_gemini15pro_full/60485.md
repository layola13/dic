ejaculate: /ɪˈdʒækjʊleɪt/| v. |射精，突然说出|To eject semen from the penis. Also, to utter suddenly and briefly.|He ejaculated a curse. 他突然咒骂了一句。|近义词：exclaim, blurt|反义词: N/A|词性: verb

词源: From Late Latin *eiaculātiōn-, *eiaculātiō, from Latin ēiaculārī ‘to hurl out’, from ē- (variant of ex-) ‘out’ + iaculum ‘javelin, dart’.
