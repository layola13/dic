ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *Florida* "flowery," feminine form of *florido*, from Latin *floridus* "blooming, flowery," from *flos* "flower."  Named by Juan Ponce de León in 1513, possibly because of the Easter season (Pascua Florida, "Feast of Flowers") or the land's abundant flora.
