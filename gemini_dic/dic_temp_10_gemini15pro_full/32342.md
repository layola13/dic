childermas: /ˈtʃɪldərməs/ | n. | 圣婴节 | A liturgical feast celebrated on December 28, commemorating the massacre of the innocents by Herod the Great. It is also known as Holy Innocents' Day. |  Childermas is a somber reminder of the cost of discipleship. 圣婴节是对门徒代价的沉痛提醒。| synonyms: Holy Innocents' Day, Feast of the Holy Innocents | antonyms: N/A | noun
