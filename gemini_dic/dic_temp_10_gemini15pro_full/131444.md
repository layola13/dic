noncodified:/ˌnɒnˈkəʊdɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 未编纂的，非成文的 | Not arranged into a systematic code; unwritten. |  The constitution of the United Kingdom is largely noncodified, relying on precedent and convention. 英国的宪法很大程度上是非成文的，依赖于先例和惯例。 |  Customary law is often noncodified and passed down through generations. 习惯法通常是非成文的，代代相传。 | Synonyms: unwritten, informal, unformulated | Antonyms: codified, written, formal | adjective
