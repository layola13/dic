fortifying:/ˈfɔːrtɪfaɪɪŋ/
adjective
使人强壮的；增强防御的
making someone physically or mentally stronger; strengthening something, especially in order to protect it
例句：A fortifying breakfast of oatmeal and fruit. 一顿燕麦和水果的营养早餐。
例句：The general ordered the fortifying of the city walls. 将军下令加固城墙。
近义词：strengthening, invigorating, bolstering, reinforcing
反义词：weakening, debilitating
词性：adjective

fortify:/ˈfɔːrtɪfaɪ/
verb
增强；加强；设防于；（尤指用酒精）强化（饮料）
to strengthen something, especially in order to protect it; to make someone physically or mentally stronger; to add alcohol to a drink
例句：They fortified the town with walls and a moat. 他们用城墙和护城河加固了城镇。
例句：He fortified himself with a large breakfast. 他用一顿丰盛的早餐来补充体力。
近义词：strengthen, reinforce, bolster, invigorate
反义词：weaken, undermine
词性：verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *fortifier*, from Old French fortifier, from Late Latin fortificare, from Latin fortis (“strong”) + facere (“to make”).
