expatiate:/ɪkˈspeɪʃieɪt/ /ɛkˈspeɪʃɪˌeɪt/| vi.|详述，细说；漫游|To speak or write at length or in detail. |She expatiated on the benefits of vegetarianism. 她详细阐述了素食主义的好处。|He expatiated upon his travels in Europe. 他详细地讲述了他在欧洲的旅行。|近义词:elaborate, dilate, enlarge, descant|反义词:summarize, condense, abbreviate|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *expatiatus, past participle of expatiari ("to wander, digress"), from ex- ("out of, from") + spatiari ("to walk"), from spatium ("space").
