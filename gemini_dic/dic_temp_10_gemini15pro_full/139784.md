octanoyl:/ˈɒktənɔɪl/ | adj. | 辛酰的；辛酰基的 |  Relating to or containing the octanoyl group (C7H15CO-), a univalent radical derived from octanoic acid. | Octanoyl chloride is a corrosive chemical. 辛酰氯是一种腐蚀性化学物质。 | Octanoyl ghrelin is an important hormone. 辛酰基生长素释放肽是一种重要的激素。 |  Synonyms: caprylyl (less common) | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a chemical group) | adjective
