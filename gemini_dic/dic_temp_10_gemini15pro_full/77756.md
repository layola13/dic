generation's:/ˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃənz/
n.|一代人的；世代的|belonging to or associated with a particular generation. |This is a problem for today's generation's parents. 这是当今一代父母的问题。|The generation's future is at stake. 这一代人的未来岌岌可危。|synonyms: contemporaries, age group|antonyms: ancestors, descendants
word type: possessive noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French generacion, from Latin generatio(n-), from generare ‘beget’, from genus ‘stock, kind, race’.
