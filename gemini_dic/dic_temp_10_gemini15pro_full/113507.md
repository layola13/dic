m-commerces:/ˈɛmˌkɑːmərsɪz/
|n.|移动商务|Mobile commerce, commonly referred to as m-commerce, involves the buying and selling of goods and services through wireless handheld devices such as smartphones and tablets. It encompasses a wide range of activities, including online shopping, mobile banking, and bill payments.|She runs a successful m-commerce business selling handmade jewelry through a dedicated app.|Synonyms: mobile commerce, wireless commerce|Antonyms: e-commerce (in a broader sense, as e-commerce can include m-commerce), traditional commerce|noun
