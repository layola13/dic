overexpanding:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspændɪŋ/
verb (present participle) |过度扩张|Expanding beyond a safe or sustainable limit. |The company overexpanded during the boom years and went bankrupt when the market crashed.  这家公司在繁荣时期过度扩张，市场崩溃时破产了。|overextending, overreaching |downsizing, contracting |v.

overexpanding:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspændɪŋ/
adjective |过度扩张的|Characterized by excessive expansion or growth. |The overexpanding economy eventually led to a recession. 过度扩张的经济最终导致了衰退。|overextended, inflated |contracted, reduced|adj.
