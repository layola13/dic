maltiest:/ˈmɔːltiɪst/| adj.|最麦芽味的，麦芽味最浓的|Having the most malt flavor; superlative of 'malty'.|This is the maltiest beer I've ever tasted. 这是我喝过麦芽味最浓的啤酒。|The aroma of the maltiest loaf filled the bakery.  麦芽味最浓的面包的香味弥漫了整个面包店。|近义词：mustiest, richest (in malt flavor)|反义词：least malty, blandest|词性:形容词

词源:
From malt + -y + -est.  "Malt" comes from Old English *mealt, from Proto-Germanic *malt-/*malta-.
