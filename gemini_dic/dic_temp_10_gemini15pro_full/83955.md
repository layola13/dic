half-consciousnesses:/ˌhɑːfˈkɒnʃəsnɪsɪz/ |n.| 半意识；朦胧状态 |A state of being only partly conscious; a reduced level of awareness.|  He drifted in and out of half-consciousness. 他在半昏迷状态中时而清醒时而昏迷。|Near synonyms: semiconsciousness, daze, stupor | Antonyms: alertness, awareness, consciousness | Noun

half-conscious:/ˌhɑːfˈkɒnʃəs/ |adj.| 半清醒的；半昏迷的 |Only partly conscious; not fully aware of one's surroundings. |  She was half-conscious after the fall. 她摔倒后处于半昏迷状态。| Near synonyms: semiconscious, dazed, stupor | Antonyms: alert, aware, conscious | Adjective
