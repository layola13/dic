atrial: /ˈeɪtriəl/ | adj. | 心房的 | Relating to the atrium (a chamber of the heart). |  Atrial fibrillation is a common heart rhythm disorder. 心房颤动是一种常见的心律失常。|  The patient's atrial pressure was elevated. 病人的心房压力升高了。 | cardiac, auricular | ventricular | adjective

atria: /ˈeɪtriə/ | n. | 心房 (复数) | The upper chambers of the heart, which receive blood from the veins. | The atria contract to push blood into the ventricles. 心房收缩将血液推入心室。 | The left and right atria receive oxygenated and deoxygenated blood, respectively. 左心房和右心房分别接受含氧血液和缺氧血液。| auricles | ventricles | noun
