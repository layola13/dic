consumed:/kənˈsuːmd/ | v. | 消耗，吃光，吞噬，充满（某种情感）| The fire consumed the entire building.  大火吞噬了整栋建筑。|Synonyms: devour, expend, use up, absorb, engross | Antonyms: preserve, save, conserve | verb

Etymology: From Late Latin consumere, from Latin con- (thoroughly) + sumere (to take).
