immanent:/ˈɪmənənt/| adj.|内在的，固有的|Existing or operating within; inherent.|God's grace is immanent in the world. 上帝的恩典存在于世。|例句：The divine is often considered immanent in nature. 神性通常被认为是大自然固有的。|近义词：inherent, intrinsic, ingrained|反义词：external, extrinsic, transcendent|词性:形容词
immanent:/ˈɪmənənt/| adj.|即将发生的，逼近的|(especially of bad qualities) contained within; about to happen.|Danger was immanent during the flood. 洪水期间危险迫在眉睫。|例句：A sense of immanent doom hung over the town. 一种厄运即将来临的感觉笼罩着整个城镇。|近义词：impending, imminent|反义词：distant, remote|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin immanens, present participle of immanēre (“to remain in, dwell in”), from in- (“in, within”) + manēre (“to stay, remain”).
