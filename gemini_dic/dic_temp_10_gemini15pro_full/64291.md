equity:/ˈekwɪti/| n.|公平，公正；资产净值；股票；普通股|The value of a company's shares minus its debts.|例句：He sold his equity in the company. 他出售了他在公司的股份。|例句：They are campaigning for equity in the workplace. 他们在为工作场所的公平而奔走。|近义词：fairness, justice, impartiality, assets, stock, share|反义词：injustice, unfairness, bias, liabilities|noun
