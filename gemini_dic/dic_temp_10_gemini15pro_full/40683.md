conversations:/ˌkɒnvəˈseɪʃənz/ (British), /ˌkɑːnvərˈseɪʃənz/ (American)| n. |对话，交谈|The act of talking with someone else, typically involving an exchange of ideas, information, or feelings.|We had a long conversation about our future plans. 我们就未来的计划进行了长时间的交谈。|近义词：talks, discussions, dialogues|反义词: silence|noun
