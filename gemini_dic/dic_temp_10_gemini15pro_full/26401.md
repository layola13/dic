bycatch: /ˈbaɪkætʃ/ | n. | 兼捕，误捕 |  The unwanted fish and other marine creatures that are caught during commercial fishing for a different species. |  例句：Bycatch is a serious problem for marine ecosystems. 兼捕是海洋生态系统的一个严重问题。

例句：The fishermen tried to minimize bycatch by using specialized nets. 渔民们尝试使用专门的网来减少兼捕。| 近义词：incidental catch, non-target catch | 反义词: target catch | 词性: 名词
