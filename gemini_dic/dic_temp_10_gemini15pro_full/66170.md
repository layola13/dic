excessing:/ɪkˈsesɪŋ/
verb
过量; 超量; 超过
going beyond the usual, necessary, or proper limit
例句：They were excessing their authority. 他们滥用职权。

例句：The company was excessing its budget. 公司超出了预算。

近义词：exceeding, surpassing, overdoing
反义词：limiting, restricting, moderating
词性：动词 (verb)
