lithotriptor:/ˈlɪθəˌtrɪptər/ | n. | 碎石机 | A medical device that uses shock waves to break up kidney stones or other calculi in the body, allowing them to be passed naturally. | The lithotriptor successfully shattered the kidney stones without the need for invasive surgery.  碎石机成功地粉碎了肾结石，无需进行侵入性手术。 | Synonyms: lithotripter, stone crusher | Antonyms: N/A | noun

lithotripsy:/ˈlɪθəˌtrɪpsi/ | n. | 碎石术 | The medical procedure of using a lithotriptor to break up kidney stones or other calculi. | The patient underwent lithotripsy to treat his kidney stones.  病人接受了碎石术来治疗他的肾结石。| Synonyms: lithotripsy, extracorporeal shock wave lithotripsy (ESWL) | Antonyms: N/A | noun
