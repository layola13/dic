inexact:/ˌɪnɪɡˈzækt/ | adj. | 不精确的，不准确的 | not precisely correct or accurate; vague. | The measurements were inexact, leading to errors in the calculations. 测量不精确，导致计算出错。|  The witness's recollection of events was inexact. 目击者对事件的回忆不准确。| 近义词: inaccurate, imprecise, approximate, vague | 反义词: exact, precise, accurate | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Late 16th century: from in- ‘not’ + exact.
