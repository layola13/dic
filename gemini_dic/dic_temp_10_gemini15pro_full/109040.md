legal-pads: /ˈliːɡəl pædz/ | n. | 便签本；法律备忘录 |  Yellow lined pads of paper, typically 8.5 x 11 inches, often used by lawyers and for note-taking. These pads usually have a cardboard backing and are bound at the top. | I always keep a legal pad and pen next to my phone for taking notes. 我总是在电话旁边放一个便签本和一支笔，以便做笔记。 | synonyms: notepad, writing pad, memo pad | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps digital note-taking app) | noun
