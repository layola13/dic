cleat:/kliːt/| n. |防滑钉；系绳扣；夹板|A metal or plastic projection on the sole of a shoe, designed to prevent slipping, or a similar projection on a bicycle pedal.  A piece of wood or metal used to fasten ropes or cables. |例句：The soccer player's cleats dug into the muddy field.  足球运动员的防滑钉扎进了泥泞的场地。

例句：He secured the rope to the cleat on the dock. 他把绳子系在码头的系绳扣上。|近义词：spike, stud, grip, fastener|反义词: (no direct antonym) |词性:noun


cleat:/kliːt/| v. |用夹板固定；系住|To fasten with a cleat. |例句：He cleated the rope to the mast. 他把绳子系在桅杆上。

例句：The boards were cleated together.  这些木板用夹板固定在一起。|近义词：fasten, secure, attach|反义词: unfasten, detach |词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *clete, from Old English *clēat (“patch, clout, wedge”), from Proto-Germanic *klautiz (“lump, clod”). Cognate with Middle Low German klūt (“piece of cloth, patch, lump”), Middle Dutch clute (“lump, clod, piece”), Dutch kluit (“clod”), German Klotz (“block, log”), Old Norse klútr (“lump, ball of thread”), Gothic 𝑘𝑙𝑢𝑡𝑠 (“rock”).
