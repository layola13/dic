locomotor:/ˌloʊkəˈmoʊtər/ | adj. n. | 运动的；运动器官 | relating to movement or locomotion; a locomotor organ or apparatus |  The locomotor system is responsible for movement. 运动系统负责运动。|  The snail's locomotor  appendage is its foot. 蜗牛的运动器官是它的腹足。|  mobile, motile | immobile, sessile | adjective, noun

locomotion: /ˌloʊkəˈmoʊʃən/ | n. | 运动；移动 | the act or power of moving from place to place |  The locomotion of the amoeba is by means of pseudopods. 变形虫通过伪足进行运动。|  Birds use wings for locomotion. 鸟类用翅膀移动。| movement, travel, motion | stillness, immobility | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *locomōtōrius*, from *locus* (place) + *mōtiō* (motion).
