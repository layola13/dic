hiveless:/ˈhaɪvlɪs/| adj.|无蜂巢的，无蜂群的|Lacking a beehive; without bees or a bee colony.  Referring to a situation where bees are absent from their hive, or a colony lacks a hive structure.|The abandoned orchard felt lifeless and hiveless.  废弃的果园感觉毫无生气，也没有蜜蜂。 The hiveless swarm struggled to survive the harsh winter.  没有蜂巢的蜂群难以熬过严冬。|近义词：colony-less, deserted, empty|反义词:inhabited, colonized|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From hive + -less.  "Hive" comes from Old English *hīfe, related to Old High German hūba "hut."  "-less" is a suffix meaning "without" from Old English -lēas.
