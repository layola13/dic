mourningly:/ˈmɔːrnɪŋli/ | adv. | 悲哀地，哀悼地 |  Derived from "mourning" (n.) + "-ly" (adv. suffix).  "Mourning" ultimately comes from the Old English word "murnan," meaning "to grieve, lament."|
mourningly:/ˈmɔːrnɪŋli/ | adv. | 悲哀地，哀悼地 |  Used to describe actions, speech, or expressions that convey sorrow and grief, typically associated with death or loss.  |
mourningly:/ˈmɔːrnɪŋli/ | adv. | 悲哀地，哀悼地 | 近义词: sadly, sorrowfully, dolefully | 反义词: joyfully, happily |
