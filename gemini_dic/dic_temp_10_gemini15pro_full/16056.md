bandied:/ˈbændɪd/ | v. |  轻率地传播；散布；争论 |  To pass back and forth; to exchange; to discuss lightly or carelessly. | The name of the suspect was bandied about by the media. 嫌疑犯的名字被媒体轻率地传播。| circulate, toss, exchange | conceal, suppress | verb

bandied:/ˈbændɪd/ | adj. | （通常不用于名词前）被议论纷纷的 |  (usually postpositive) Frequently mentioned or discussed, often in a casual or frivolous way. |  His name was bandied about as a possible successor. 他的名字作为可能的继任者被人们议论纷纷。| circulated, discussed, rumored |  unmentioned, undisclosed | adjective
