anthracite-coal:/ˈænθrəˌsaɪt koʊl/| n.|无烟煤|A hard, compact variety of coal that has a high luster and burns with very little flame or smoke. It is the highest rank of coal.|The blacksmith used anthracite-coal to heat the forge.|Synonyms: hard coal, black diamond|Antonyms: lignite, peat|noun
