fatteners:/ˈfæt(ə)nərz/
noun
增肥物；使发胖的东西
Substances or feeds that promote weight gain, especially in livestock.
Farmers use various fatteners to prepare their livestock for market.  农民们使用各种增肥剂来让牲畜上市。
The bakery uses high-calorie fatteners in their pastries. 面包店在糕点中使用高热量的增肥剂。
synonyms:  enrichers, supplements, additives
antonyms:  diet aids, slimming products
