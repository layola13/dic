neurosciences:/ˌnjʊərəʊˈsaɪənsɪz/ /ˌnʊroʊˈsaɪənsɪz/| n. |神经科学|The study of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and nerves.  It encompasses various disciplines, including neuroanatomy, neurophysiology, and neurochemistry. |Advances in the neurosciences have led to a better understanding of how the brain works. 神经科学的进步使人们对大脑的工作方式有了更好的理解。

The neurosciences are crucial for developing new treatments for neurological disorders. 神经科学对于开发神经系统疾病的新疗法至关重要。|近义词：neurobiology, neurology |反义词: N/A |词性: Noun (plural)


Etymology:
neuro- (from Greek neuron 'nerve') + science
