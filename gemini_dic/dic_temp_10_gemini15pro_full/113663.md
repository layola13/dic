machismo:/məˈtʃɪzmoʊ/| n.| 大男子主义；男子气概|A strong sense of masculine pride; an exaggerated masculinity.  An attitude of male dominance.|He displayed a swaggering machismo that intimidated some people. 他表现出一种虚张声势的大男子主义，吓倒了一些人。|Synonyms: masculinity, virility, manliness|Antonyms: femininity, effeminacy|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *machismo*, from *macho* ("male"), ultimately from Latin *masculus* ("masculine," "male").
