incorporated: /ɪnˈkɔːrpəreɪtɪd/ /ɪnˈkɔːrpərˌeɪtɪd/
adjective
已注册成立的，包含的
Combined into a single body; formed into a legal corporation.  Included as part of something else.
Example: The newly incorporated company is ready for business. 这家新成立的公司已经准备好开始营业了。

Example:  The principles of fairness and equality are incorporated into the company's mission statement. 公平和平等的原则被纳入了公司的使命宣言中。
Synonym: integrated, included, combined, merged, consolidated
Antonym: separate, independent, unincorporated, excluded
词性: adjective


incorporate: /ɪnˈkɔːrpəreɪt/ /ɪnˈkɔːrpərˌeɪt/
verb
包含，合并，组成公司
To combine (one thing) with another so that they become a whole. To form into a legal corporation.
Example:  We will incorporate your suggestions into the new design. 我们会将你的建议纳入新设计中。

Example: They decided to incorporate their business in Delaware. 他们决定在特拉华州注册成立公司。
Synonym: integrate, include, combine, merge, absorb
Antonym: separate, divide, exclude, remove
词性: verb
