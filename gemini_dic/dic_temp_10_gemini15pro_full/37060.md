combatant: /ˈkɒmbətənt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmbətənt/ (US)| n. adj.|战斗员，交战的|A person who fights in a war or battle; engaged in fighting.|例句：The two combatants faced each other across the battlefield.  两个战斗员在战场上互相面对面。|近义词：fighter, soldier, warrior, belligerent|反义词：civilian, noncombatant|词性:名词

combatant: /ˈkɒmbətənt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmbətənt/ (US)| adj.|交战的，战斗的|Engaged in fighting.|例句：The combatant nations signed a truce.  交战各国签署了停战协议。|近义词：fighting, warring, belligerent|反义词：peaceful, non-combatant|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: combataunt, from Old French combatant, present participle of combatre ‘to fight’, from com- (expressing intensive force) + batre ‘to beat’.
