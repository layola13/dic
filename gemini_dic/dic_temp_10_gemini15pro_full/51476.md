detoxes:/ˈdiːtɒksɪz/ /ˈdiːtɑːksɪz/| n. | 解毒; 排毒;  |A process or period of time in which one abstains from or rids the body of toxic or unhealthy substances.  |She went on a juice detox to cleanse her system. 她进行了果汁排毒来清洁身体系统。

detox:/ˈdiːtɒks/ /ˈdiːtɑːks/| n. v. | 解毒; 排毒; |A process or period of time in which one abstains from or rids the body of toxic or unhealthy substances (noun); to undergo such a process (verb). |She did a detox to get rid of toxins. 她进行了排毒以去除毒素。

|She is detoxing from sugar. 她正在戒糖排毒。|近义词：cleanse, purify|反义词: contaminate, pollute|词性:noun, verb

词源:  "Detox" is a shortened form of "detoxification," ultimately derived from the Greek words "apo" (away from) and "toxikon" (poison).  The modern usage focusing on diet and lifestyle originated in the 20th century.
