gasconade:/ˌɡæskəˈneɪd/ | n. | 吹牛，夸口 | A boastful or exaggerated statement. | He was known for his gasconades about his military exploits. 他以吹嘘自己的军事功绩而闻名。 |  He regaled everyone with gasconades about his adventures. 他向每个人夸耀自己的冒险经历。 | synonyms: boasting, bragging, bluster, braggadocio | antonyms: modesty, humility | noun

gasconade:/ˌɡæskəˈneɪd/ | vi. | 吹牛，夸口 | To boast or brag. | He gasconaded about his wealth and connections. 他吹嘘自己的财富和人脉。 | She gasconaded endlessly about her children's accomplishments. 她不停地吹嘘她孩子的成就。 | synonyms: boast, brag, bluster | antonyms: downplay, understate | verb
