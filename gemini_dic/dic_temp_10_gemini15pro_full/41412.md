cornemuse:/kɔːr.nəˈmjuːz/| n.|风笛|A wind instrument of the bagpipe family, especially popular in France and Belgium. It consists of a bag inflated by the player's breath or bellows, a chanter with finger holes, and one or more drones that produce a continuous note.|The band played a lively tune on the cornemuse.|Synonyms: bagpipe, musette|Antonyms: None|Noun
