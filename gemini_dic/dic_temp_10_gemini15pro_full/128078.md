nervinesses:/ˈnɜːrvɪnɪsɪz/
n.
紧张不安，神经质
The state of being nervous; nervousness.
Her nervinesses before the performance were obvious.  演出前她的紧张很明显。
近义词：anxiety, apprehension, jumpiness, jitters
反义词：calmness, composure, tranquility
词性：名词(plural)
