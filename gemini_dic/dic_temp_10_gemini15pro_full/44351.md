cross-bearings: /krɒsˈbɛərɪŋz/ (British) /krɔːsˈbɛrɪŋz/ (American) | n. |交叉方位, 交叉方位测定法 |The method of determining a position by taking bearings to two or more known points. |We used cross-bearings from two landmarks to pinpoint our location on the map. 我们利用两个地标的交叉方位确定了我们在地图上的位置。| The ship's position was fixed by cross-bearings. 船的位置是通过交叉方位测定的。 | 近义词: triangulation, cross fix | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "single bearing") | noun
