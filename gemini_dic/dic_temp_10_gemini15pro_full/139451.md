obsolete:/ˈɒbsəliːt/ | adj. | 过时的，废弃的 | No longer produced or used; out of date. | The old computer system is now obsolete. 旧的计算机系统现在已经过时了。 | synonyms: outdated, outmoded, archaic | antonyms: modern, current, contemporary | adjective
