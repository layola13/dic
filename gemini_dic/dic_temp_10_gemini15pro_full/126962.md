narrowband:/ˈnæroʊbænd/ (US) /ˈnærəʊbænd/ (UK)| adj. n.|窄带（的）|A narrowband signal occupies a small range of frequencies in the electromagnetic spectrum.  A narrowband connection has a low bandwidth.|例句1：Narrowband audio is often used for voice communication. 窄带音频通常用于语音通信。

例句2：The narrowband signal was easily disrupted by interference. 窄带信号很容易受到干扰的干扰。|近义词：limited bandwidth, low bandwidth|反义词：wideband, broadband|词性：adjective, noun

narrowband:/ˈnæroʊbænd/ (US) /ˈnærəʊbænd/ (UK)| n.|窄带|A range of frequencies within a given band, characterized by a relatively small bandwidth.|例句1：The radio operated on a narrowband to minimize interference. 这台收音机在窄带上运行以最大限度地减少干扰。

例句2：They used a narrowband for the secure transmission. 他们使用窄带进行安全传输。|近义词：narrow band|反义词：wideband, broadband|词性：noun
