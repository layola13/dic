dialectic: /ˌdaɪəˈlɛktɪk/ | n. adj. | 辩证法，辩证的 | a method of argument in which two opposing ideas are discussed and a solution is reached; relating to the logical discussion of ideas and opinions |  Hegel's dialectic proposes a thesis, antithesis, and synthesis. 黑格尔的辩证法提出了正题、反题和合题。 | synonyms: argumentation, reasoning, logic | antonyms: dogma, irrationality | noun

dialectic: /ˌdaɪəˈlɛktɪk/ | adj. | 辩证的 | relating to the logical discussion of ideas and opinions | The dialectic process helps us understand complex issues. 辩证过程帮助我们理解复杂的问题。 | synonyms: logical, analytical, rational | antonyms: illogical, irrational, emotional | adjective
