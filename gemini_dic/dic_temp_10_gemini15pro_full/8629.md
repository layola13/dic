anticommercialnesses:/ˌæntiˌkəˈmɜːʃəlˌnəsɪz/
|n.| 反商业主义，不重商业性 |The state of being opposed to or indifferent to commercial principles or practices.  The quality or state of not being primarily concerned with profit or commerce.|  Their anticommercialnesses led them to reject the lucrative offer. 他们的不重商业性导致他们拒绝了那份利润丰厚的提议。|Synonyms: noncommercialism, uncommercialism| Antonyms: commercialism, mercantilism | noun
