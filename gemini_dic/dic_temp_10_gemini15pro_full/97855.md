inleaguer:/ˈɪnˌliːɡər/
n.|同伙，盟友，同党|A person who belongs to the same league, group, or team as another; an ally or confederate.|He found an inleaguer in his fight against corruption. 他在反腐败斗争中找到了一个盟友。|
近义词:ally, confederate, partner, teammate, associate
反义词:opponent, adversary, enemy, foe
词性:名词

inleaguer:/ˈɪnˌliːɡər/
vt.|使成为同盟; 加入同盟|To bring into a league or alliance.|They inleaguered themselves with other groups to increase their power. 他们与其他团体结盟以增强实力。|
近义词:ally, unite, join forces, combine, collaborate
反义词:separate, divide, disunite, isolate
词性:动词
