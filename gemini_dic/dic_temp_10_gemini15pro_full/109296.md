lemuroidea:/ˌlɛmjʊˈrɔɪdiə/ | n. |狐猴总科; 狐猴超科|A superfamily of primates, the Lemuroidea, comprising the lemurs, lorises, and bushbabies, now usually restricted to the lemurs of Madagascar. |Lemuroidea are endemic to Madagascar. 狐猴总科是马达加斯加特有的。|Synonym: Lemuriformes| Antonym: (No direct antonym, but could consider other primate groups like Anthropoidea)| Noun
