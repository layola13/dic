defraying:/dɪˈfreɪɪŋ/
|v.|支付，承担费用|The company offered to defray the costs of travel and accommodation. 公司提出支付旅行和住宿费用。|
|v.|支付，承担费用| The scholarship defrays the cost of tuition. 奖学金支付了学费。|
|近义词: pay, cover, finance, subsidize|
|反义词: charge, bill, invoice|
|词性: 动词 (present participle of defray)|
|词源: From Old French *desfraier, from des- (expressing removal) + fraier (“to rub, break”), from Latin fricāre. Compare defrayment, affray.|
