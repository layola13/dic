disgracefully: /dɪsˈɡreɪsfəli/ | adv. | 可耻地，不光彩地 | in a way that causes shame or loss of respect | He behaved disgracefully at the party, embarrassing his family. 他在聚会上表现得可耻，让他的家人很尴尬。| She treated her employees disgracefully, often yelling and belittling them. 她对待员工的方式很可耻，经常对他们大吼大叫和贬低他们。| 近义词: shamefully, dishonorably, ignominiously | 反义词: honorably, respectably, creditably | 词性: 副词
词源: From disgrace + -fully. Disgrace from Old French desgracier, from des- expressing reversal + grace.
