hydric:/ˈhaɪdrɪk/ | adj. |水合的，含水的，潮湿的 | Characterized by, relating to, or requiring conditions of abundant moisture.  Relating to a wetland environment. | The hydric soils in the marsh support a unique ecosystem.  沼泽中的含水土壤支持着独特的生态系统。| The botanist specialized in hydric plants.  这位植物学家专门研究水生植物。 | synonyms: waterlogged, saturated, boggy, marshy | antonyms: dry, arid, xeric | adjective
