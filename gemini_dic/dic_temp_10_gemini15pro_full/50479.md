depressingness:/dɪˈprɛsɪŋnɪs/
noun
沮丧，抑郁，令人沮丧的性质
The depressingness of the situation was overwhelming.  这种情况的压抑感让人难以承受。
Etymology: From depressing + -ness.  Depressing from depress (v.) + -ing. Depress from Latin depressus, past participle of deprimere "to press down," from de- "down" + premere "to press."
Synonyms: gloom, misery, despondency
Antonyms: cheerfulness, happiness, joy
词性: 名词
