nonabsorbents:/ˌnɒnəbˈzɔːbənts/
| n. |非吸收性物质；非吸收体|Materials that do not absorb liquids or gases. |These nonabsorbents are perfect for wiping up spills. 这些非吸收性材料非常适合擦拭溢出物。|The table was made of nonabsorbents, so the spilled water beaded up. 这张桌子是用非吸收性材料做的，所以洒出来的水珠凝结起来了。|近义词：impermeable materials, water-resistant materials, nonporous materials|反义词: absorbents, porous materials|词性:名词

nonabsorbent:/ˌnɒnəbˈzɔːbənt/
| adj. |非吸收性的|Not able to absorb liquids or gases. |Apply a nonabsorbent dressing to the wound. 在伤口上敷上非吸收性敷料。|This fabric is nonabsorbent, making it ideal for raincoats. 这种织物不吸水，非常适合做雨衣。|近义词：impermeable, water-resistant, nonporous|反义词: absorbent, porous|词性:形容词


词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + absorbent (from Latin absorbēre, "to suck up")
