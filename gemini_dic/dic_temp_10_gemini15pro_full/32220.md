chichan:/ˈtʃiːtʃɑːn/ | n. | 小鸡; 小孩 (尤指女孩); (昵称)宝贝; |A term of endearment, often used for a young girl or child, similar to "sweetie" or "honey." It can also refer to a baby chick.  Derived from Japanese slang, likely influenced by "chii" (small) and "chan" (an affectionate suffix).| My niece is such a cute chichan. 我的侄女真是个可爱的小宝贝。 | synonyms: sweetie, darling, honey, babe, chick (informal), little one | antonyms: (Generally no direct antonyms for terms of endearment) | noun
