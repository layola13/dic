exquisitely:/ɪkˈskwɪzɪtli/ /ɛkˈskwɪzɪtli/
adverb
精致地；精巧地；优美地
In a delicate and refined manner; with great beauty and skill.
The cake was exquisitely decorated with delicate sugar flowers.  蛋糕用精致的糖花装饰得非常漂亮。
She sang exquisitely, moving the audience to tears. 她唱得优美动听，感动得观众流下了眼泪。
近义词: beautifully, delicately, elegantly, perfectly, superbly
反义词: crudely, roughly, clumsily, poorly
词性: adverb
词源: From Late Latin *exquisitus, past participle of *exquirere "to search out," from Latin ex- "out" + quaerere "to seek, ask."  The adverb form is derived by adding the suffix "-ly."
