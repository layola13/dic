frowner:/ˈfraʊnər/| n. |皱眉者，不悦者|A person who frowns; someone who has a disapproving or displeased expression.|She's a real frowner; nothing seems to make her happy.|近义词：grumbler, complainer, pessimist|反义词: smiler, optimist|词性:noun

Etymology: From the verb "frown" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Frown" comes from Old North French *frogner "to wrinkle," of uncertain origin.
