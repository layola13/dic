parlaying:/ˈpɑːrleɪɪŋ/ /pɑːrˈleɪɪŋ/
v.
善用；使增值；成功利用
To use (something, such as an asset or victory) to achieve something else. To transform something into something of greater value.
He parlayed his small inheritance into a fortune. 他把他继承的一小笔遗产变成了巨额财富。
近义词: capitalize on, leverage, exploit, boost
反义词: squander, waste, lose, forfeit
词性: 动词

Etymology: From the French "parler" (to speak), via the noun "parle" (speech, conversation), originally referring to a conference between enemies.  The meaning shifted to negotiating terms and then to using something gained in negotiation to gain even more.
