cigarless:/sɪˈɡɑːrlɪs/
adjective
无雪茄的；不抽雪茄的
without a cigar; not smoking a cigar
He felt strangely vulnerable and cigarless. 他感到异常脆弱，而且没有雪茄。
近义词:non-smoking
反义词:cigar-smoking
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From cigar + -less.  "Cigar" comes from Spanish "cigarro," ultimately from Mayan "siyar."  "-less" is a suffix of Germanic origin meaning "without."
