dime-store:/ˈdaɪm stɔːr/ (US) /ˈdaɪm stɔː(r)/ (UK)| adj. n.|廉价的，十美分商店；廉价商店|A store originally selling a variety of low-priced merchandise, typically for ten cents or less.
