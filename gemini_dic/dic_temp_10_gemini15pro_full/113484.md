lyricists:/ˈlɪrɪsɪsts/
n.
作詞家；歌詞作者
A person who writes words for songs.
例句：The lyricists of this musical are very talented.  这部音乐剧的作词家非常有才华。

例句：Famous lyricists often collaborate with renowned composers.  著名的作詞家经常与著名的作曲家合作。

近义词：songwriter, wordsmith
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑composer 作曲家)
词性: noun
