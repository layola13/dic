abolition:/ˌæbəˈlɪʃən/| n.|废除，废止|The abolition of slavery marked a turning point in history. 奴隶制的废除标志着历史的转折点。|The group campaigned for the abolition of the death penalty.  该团体致力于废除死刑运动。|近义词：abolishment, eradication, elimination|反义词：establishment, institution, enactment|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French abolition, from Latin abolitio (“a destroying”), from abolere (“to destroy, abolish”), from ab- (“from, away”) + olere (“to grow”).
