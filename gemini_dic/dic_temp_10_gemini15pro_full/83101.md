ETYMOLOGY->
From the earlier term "gussy up," which likely originated in the early 20th century. The exact etymology is uncertain, but it may be related to the word "gusset," referring to a piece of fabric inserted into a garment for decoration or shaping. This connection suggests the idea of adding extra details or embellishments.
