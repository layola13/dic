choke:/tʃoʊk/ (US) /tʃəʊk/ (UK)| v. n.|窒息，哽咽；阻塞；（使）勒死；抑制；失败|to be unable to breathe because something is blocking your throat; to block a passage or opening; to suppress feelings|
The smoke was choking me.  烟把我呛得透不过气。
He choked to death on a piece of meat. 他被一块肉噎死了。


choke:/tʃoʊk/ (US) /tʃəʊk/ (UK)| v. n.|抑制；失败|to suppress or fail to achieve something, especially due to pressure|
He choked back his tears. 他强忍住眼泪。
She choked under pressure in the final. 她在决赛中因压力过大而失利。


近义词:suffocate, strangle, stifle, gag, obstruct, inhibit|
反义词:breathe, express, succeed|
词性:verb, noun
