intercede:/ˌɪntərˈsiːd/| v. |求情，调解，斡旋|To act on behalf of someone in difficulty or trouble, especially by pleading or negotiating with someone in authority.|例句：The lawyer interceded with the judge on behalf of his client. 律师为他的客户向法官求情。

例句：I interceded in the dispute between the two brothers. 我调解了兄弟俩之间的争执。|近义词：mediate, intervene, negotiate, plead, arbitrate|反义词:ignore, disregard, exacerbate|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *intercedere*, from Latin intercedō ("I intervene, come between, am between, pass, elapse"), from inter ("between") + cēdō ("I go, move, withdraw").
