fountainlike:/ˈfaʊntɪnˌlaɪk/| adj.|像喷泉的，喷泉似的|Resembling a fountain in appearance or manner; spraying upward like the jets of a fountain.|The water shot up from the geyser in a fountainlike spray. 间歇泉的水像喷泉一样喷涌而出。|Near synonyms: fountainesque, jetting, spraying, spouting|Antonyms: trickling, dripping, oozing|Etymology: fountain (from Old French *fontane, from Latin fontāna, feminine of fontānus “of a spring,” from fons “spring”) + -like (Old English -līc)|
