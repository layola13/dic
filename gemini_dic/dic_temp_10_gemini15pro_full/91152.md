hydatiform:/ˌhaɪdəˈtɪfɔːrm/ | adj. | 葡萄胎的；水泡状的 | Resembling a hydatid cyst; cystic; vesicular.  Relating to or characterized by a hydatidiform mole. |  The doctor suspected a hydatiform mole based on the ultrasound. 医生根据超声波检查怀疑是葡萄胎。 |  cystic, vesicular |  solid | adjective
