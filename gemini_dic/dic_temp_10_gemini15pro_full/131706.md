nonconduciveness:/ˌnɒnkənˈdjuːsɪvnəs/ |n.| 不利; 不利条件; 不利环境 |The nonconduciveness of the environment to sleep made it difficult to rest.  这种环境不利于睡眠，让人难以休息。| English Explanation: The state or quality of not being conducive; unfavorable or unhelpful to a particular purpose or outcome. | Example: The nonconduciveness of the noisy street to concentration made it hard to study. 嘈杂的街道不利于集中注意力，使学习变得困难。| Synonyms: unfavorableness, disadvantageousness, inauspiciousness | Antonyms: conduciveness, favorableness, advantageousness | noun
