chorda:/ˈkɔːrdə/| n. |腱，弦，索|A cord or string-like structure, especially in anatomy or botany.  It refers to a primitive skeletal structure, a tendon, or a plant part resembling a cord. |The spinal chorda is a flexible rod that runs along the back of the embryo.|近义词：cord, tendon, filament|反义词: (No direct antonym, depends on context)|noun
