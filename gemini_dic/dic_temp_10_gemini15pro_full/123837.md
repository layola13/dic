moraless: /ˈmɔːrəllɪs/
adjective
不道德的，无道德的
lacking or indifferent to moral standards
He acted in a completely moraless way. 他表现得完全不道德。
近义词: unethical, immoral, unscrupulous, unprincipled
反义词: moral, ethical, principled
词性: adjective

Etymology:  "moral" + "-less"

"moral" comes from Old French *moral*, from Latin *moralis* "concerning character, proper behavior," from *mos* (plural *mores*) "custom, habit, way of life, conduct, morals, character."

"-less" is a native English suffix meaning "without," from Old English *-leas*.
