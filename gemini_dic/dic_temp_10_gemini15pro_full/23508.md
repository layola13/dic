braille:/breɪl/| n. |盲文|A system of writing and printing for blind or visually impaired people, in which raised dots represent the letters of the alphabet. |She learned to read braille when she was five. 她五岁时就学会了读盲文。|近义词:raised dots, tactile writing|反义词:print|词性:noun
