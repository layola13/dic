pang:/pæŋ/
noun
一阵剧痛；一阵苦闷
a sudden sharp pain or painful emotion.
She felt a sudden pang of guilt. 她突然感到一阵内疚。
近义词:twinge, spasm, stab
反义词:comfort, relief
词性:n.

pang:/pæŋ/
noun
一阵强烈的情感
a sudden strong feeling of an emotion
a pang of nostalgia 怀旧之情
近义词:burst, surge, wave
反义词:dullness, apathy
词性:n.
