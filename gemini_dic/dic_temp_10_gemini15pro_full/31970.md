chemist:/ˈkɛmɪst/ (US), /ˈkɛmɪst/ (UK)| n.|化学家；药剂师|A scientist trained in chemistry. A person who prepares and sells medicines and drugs.|例句：The chemist analyzed the substance to determine its composition.  化学家分析了这种物质以确定其成分。

例句：I went to the chemist to get my prescription filled. 我去药店配药。|近义词：pharmacist, druggist (for one who prepares and sells medicines), scientist, researcher (for one trained in chemistry)|反义词: None (no direct opposite)|词性: Noun
