nonorthodoxly: /ˌnɒnˈɔːθədɒksli/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈɔːθədɒksli/ (UK) | adv. | 非正统地，异端地 | In a manner that departs from established or traditional beliefs or practices. | He approached the problem nonorthodoxly, using a method no one had tried before. 他用一种前所未有的方法，非正统地解决了这个问题。|  She dressed nonorthodoxly, preferring vibrant colors and unusual combinations. 她穿着非正统，喜欢鲜艳的颜色和不寻常的搭配。 | 近义词: unconventionally, atypically, unusually, heterodoxly | 反义词: orthodoxly, conventionally, traditionally | 词性: 副词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + orthodox + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Orthodox" comes from Greek "orthos" (right, straight) + "doxa" (opinion, belief).
