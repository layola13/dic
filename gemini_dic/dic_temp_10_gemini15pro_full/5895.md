ambidexterity:/ˌæmbɪdɛkˈstɛrɪti/| n.| 双手灵巧，左右开弓， ambidextrousness|The surgeon demonstrated ambidexterity during the complex procedure.  这位外科医生在复杂的手术过程中展现了双手的灵巧。| Ambidexterity is a valuable asset for a musician.  对于音乐家来说，双手灵巧是一项宝贵的资产。|近义词：versatility, adaptability, two-handedness|反义词：one-handedness, clumsiness|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ambidexter* ("having two right hands," figuratively "skillful," literally "right-handed on both sides"), from *ambi-* ("both") + *dexter* ("right, right-handed").
