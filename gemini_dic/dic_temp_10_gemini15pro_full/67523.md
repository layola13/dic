extracts:/ˈekstrækts/ (US), /ˈekstrækts/ (UK)| n. | 提取物，摘录，选段 |  A substance that has been extracted from something else. A passage selected from a book, film, or piece of music. |  The chemist prepared extracts from various medicinal plants.  化学家从各种药用植物中制备提取物。

She read several extracts from her new novel. 她朗读了她的新小说的几个选段。| synonyms: excerpts, selections, essences, concentrates | antonyms: wholes, originals, entirety | noun

extract:/ɪkˈstrækt/ (US), /ɪkˈstrækt/ (UK)| v. | 提取，取出，摘录 | To remove or take out something. To select a passage from a book, film, or piece of music. |  The dentist extracted my wisdom tooth. 牙医拔掉了我的智齿。

He extracted several key points from the report. 他从报告中提取了几个要点。| synonyms: remove, withdraw, derive, excerpt | antonyms: insert, inject, implant, add | verb
