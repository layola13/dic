parchmentize:/ˈpɑːrtʃmɛntaɪz/
verb
使像羊皮纸；使变干
To make something resemble parchment, often by drying it out or making it brittle.
The hot sun parchmentized the leaves, making them crisp and brown.  炙热的阳光使叶子变得像羊皮纸一样，又脆又黄。
The old documents had been parchmentized by time.  古老的文件因年代久远而变得像羊皮纸一样。
近义词: desiccate, dry, wither
反义词: moisten, hydrate
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From parchment + -ize.  源自 parchment（羊皮纸） + -ize（使...化）。
