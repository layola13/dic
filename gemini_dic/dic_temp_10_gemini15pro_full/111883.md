logography:/ləˈɡɒɡrəfi/ /loʊˈɡɑːɡrəfi/
noun
表意文字; 语标书写
A system of writing in which each word or morpheme is represented by a single symbol, such as Chinese characters.
Chinese is the best-known example of a logographic writing system.  汉语是表意文字书写系统的最著名例子。
The ancient Egyptians used a logographic script. 古埃及人使用一种表意文字。
近义词:ideography
反义词:alphabetic writing
词性:n.
