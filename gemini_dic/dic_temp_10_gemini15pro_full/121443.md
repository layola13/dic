ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (wrongly) + interpret (to explain the meaning of). The prefix "mis-" comes from Old English "mis-", meaning "wrongly, badly, wrongly."  "Interpret" comes from the Latin "interpretārī," meaning "to explain, expound, translate."
