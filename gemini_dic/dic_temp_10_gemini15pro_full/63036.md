enrapturer:/ɪnˈræptʃərər/| n.|使人狂喜的人或物|Someone or something that causes someone to feel great pleasure or delight.|Her beauty was such that she became an enrapturer of all who saw her. 她的美貌使所有见到她的人都为之倾倒。|近义词：charmer, captivator, fascinator|反义词：bore, repellent|noun

enrapture:/ɪnˈræptʃər/| vt.|使狂喜，使入迷|To fill with delight; to enchant.|The music enraptured the audience.  音乐使观众陶醉。|近义词：captivate, enthrall, enchant|反义词：bore, disgust, repel|verb
