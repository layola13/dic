oxeye:/ˈɒksaɪ/ (UK) /ˈɑːksaɪ/ (US)| n. | 牛眼菊；牛眼雏菊; 一种拥有大眼睛的人或动物 |A type of daisy with a large, yellow center resembling an ox's eye. Also, someone or something with large eyes.| The field was full of oxeyes. 田野里开满了牛眼菊。|Synonyms: ox-eye daisy, Leucanthemum vulgare, white daisy| Antonyms: N/A | noun

oxeye:/ˈɒksaɪ/ (UK) /ˈɑːksaɪ/ (US)| adj. | 牛眼状的 |Resembling an ox's eye, typically large and round.|  She had big, oxeye eyes. 她有一双大大的，牛眼般的眼睛。|Synonyms: large-eyed, round-eyed| Antonyms: small-eyed, narrow-eyed | adjective
