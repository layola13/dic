moderating:/ˈmɒdəreɪtɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdəreɪtɪŋ/ (US)| v. adj.|缓和，调节；主持；适度的|Present participle of moderate, meaning to make or become less extreme, intense, or severe; to preside over a discussion or meeting; not extreme, excessive, or intense.|例句：The chairman is moderating the debate. 主席正在主持辩论。|近义词：tempering, lessening, mitigating, chairing, mediating|反义词: intensifying, exacerbating, aggravating|词性: verb (present participle), adjective


moderating:/ˈmɒdəreɪtɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdəreɪtɪŋ/ (US)| adj.|适度的，温和的|Meaning not extreme, excessive, or intense.|例句：The weather was moderating. 天气变得温和了。|近义词：mild, temperate, reasonable|反义词: extreme, immoderate, excessive|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin moderatus, past participle of moderari "to restrain, regulate," from Latin modus "measure, limit, manner."
