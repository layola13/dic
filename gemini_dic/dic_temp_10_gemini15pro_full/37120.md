combusting:/kəmˈbʌstɪŋ/
verb
燃烧；燃烧的
The process of burning, especially rapidly and completely.
The rocket fuel is combusting as it ascends. 火箭燃料在上升时燃烧。
The wood was combusting slowly in the fireplace. 木头在壁炉里慢慢燃烧。
synonyms: burning, igniting, inflaming
antonyms: extinguishing, quenching
v.
