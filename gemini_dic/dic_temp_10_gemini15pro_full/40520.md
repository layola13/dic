controllables:/kənˈtroʊləbəlz/| n.|可控因素，可控变量|Factors or variables that can be influenced or manipulated. |The project manager identified the key controllables in the project plan. 项目经理确定了项目计划中的关键可控因素。|Synonyms: manageable factors, adjustable parameters|Antonyms: uncontrollables, unmanageable factors|noun


controllable:/kənˈtroʊləbəl/| adj.|可控制的，可操纵的|Able to be controlled or influenced. |The temperature in the room is controllable with the thermostat.  房间的温度可以用恒温器控制。|Synonyms: manageable, adjustable, governable|Antonyms: uncontrollable, unmanageable, ungovernable|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From "control" (from Old French "controle," ultimately from Medieval Latin "contrarotulus" - a counter-roll used to verify accounts) + "-able" (suffix meaning "capable of being").
