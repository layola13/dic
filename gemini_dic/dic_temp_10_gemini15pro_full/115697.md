marginalizations:/ˌmɑːrdʒɪnəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. |边缘化，边缘化的情况|The process of making a group or class of people less important or influential.|The marginalization of indigenous communities is a serious issue.  原住民社区的边缘化是一个严重的问题。| Synonyms: exclusion, sidelining, disempowerment, oppression | Antonyms: inclusion, integration, empowerment | noun

marginalization:/ˌmɑːrdʒɪnəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |边缘化，边缘化的情况|The act of treating someone or something as if they are not important.| The report highlights the marginalization of women in the tech industry.  这份报告强调了科技行业中女性的边缘化。| Synonyms:  alienation, disenfranchisement | Antonyms: centralisation, mainstreaming | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From marginalize + -ation.  Marginalize comes from marginal + -ize. Marginal comes from French marginal, from Latin marginalis, from margin-, margo "edge, border."
