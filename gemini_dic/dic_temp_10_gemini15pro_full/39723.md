consilience:/kənˈsɪliəns/| n. |一致;符合;整合|The linking together of principles from different disciplines, especially when forming a comprehensive theory.|Wilson’s book aimed to create a consilience of knowledge from biology, the social sciences, and the humanities.  威尔逊的书旨在整合来自生物学、社会科学和人文学科的知识。|近义词：convergence, agreement, coherence, unity, synthesis|反义词：discordance, disagreement, divergence|noun

consilience:/kənˈsɪliəns/| n. |整合;统一|The act of coming together or agreeing.|The consilience of their views on the project was surprising. 他们对这个项目的看法出奇地一致。|近义词：accord, concurrence, harmony|反义词：conflict, discord, disunity|noun
