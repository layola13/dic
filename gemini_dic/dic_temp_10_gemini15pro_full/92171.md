hyperspace:/ˈhaɪpərˌspeɪs/| n.|超空间|A fictional space of more than three dimensions, through which spacecraft in science fiction stories can travel faster than light.|The spaceship jumped into hyperspace to escape the enemy. 飞船跳入超空间以躲避敌人。|Near synonyms: subspace, another dimension|Antonyms:  normal space (though not a perfect antonym)|noun
