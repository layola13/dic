anatocism: /əˈnætəsɪzəm/
n.
anatocism
The practice of charging compound interest; interest on interest.
The bank was accused of anatocism for charging interest on the unpaid interest.  这家银行因对未付利息收取利息而被指控重复计息。
近义词: compound interest, usury
反义词: simple interest
词性: noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *anatocismus, from Ancient Greek ἀνατοκισμός (anatokismós, “a producing interest on interest”), from ἀνα- (ana-, “up, again”) + τόκος (tókos, “interest”).
