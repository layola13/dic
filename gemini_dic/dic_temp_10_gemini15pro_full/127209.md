naughtest:/ˈnɔːtɪɪst/| adj.|最顽皮的，最淘气的|Superlative form of naughty; most disobedient, mischievous, or badly behaved.|Example: He was the naughtiest boy in the class, always getting into trouble. 他是班上最淘气的男孩，总是惹麻烦。
Example:  Of all the kittens, she was the naughtest, constantly batting at the dangling string.  在所有的小猫中，她是最淘气的，不停地拍打着悬挂的绳子。|近义词：wickedest, worst, most mischievous|反义词: best-behaved, most well-behaved|词性: adjective
词源: From Old English *nawiht (nothing, worthless) > Middle English naught, nawt (wicked, bad, worthless) > naughti (naughty) > naughtiest.
