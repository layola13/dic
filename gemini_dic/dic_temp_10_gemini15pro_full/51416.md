deterrently:/dɪˈtɜːrəntlɪ/
adv.
威慑地，制止地
in a manner that discourages or prevents a particular action
The high security fence was built deterrently to prevent trespassers.  高安全栅栏的建造是为了威慑入侵者。
The government imposed heavy fines deterrently to curb illegal parking. 政府课以重罚以遏制非法停车。
近义词: dissuasively, preventively
反义词: encouragingly, invitingly
词性: adverb

词源: From "deterrent" + "-ly". "Deterrent" comes from the Latin "deterrēre", meaning "to frighten from, discourage".
