clayes:/kleɪz/ | n. | 粘土; 泥土 | Clay is a type of fine-grained natural rock or soil material that combines one or more clay minerals with traces of metal oxides and organic matter. | The potter shaped the clayes into a beautiful vase. 陶艺家用粘土塑造成一个漂亮的花瓶。| 近义词: mud, earth, dirt | 反义词: rock | 词性: 名词


词源:  From Middle English *clayes, plural of *claye, from Old English clæg (“clay”), from Proto-Germanic *klaijō (“clay”). Cognate with West Frisian klaai (“clay”), Dutch klei (“clay”), German Klei (“clay”), Danish ler (“clay”), Swedish lera (“clay”).
