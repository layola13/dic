chymist:/ˈkɪmɪst/| n. |炼金术士; 化学家 (古语)|An archaic term for a chemist or alchemist, someone who practices or studies chemistry, especially as it relates to alchemy. |例句：The chymist worked tirelessly in his laboratory, attempting to transmute base metals into gold.  炼金术士孜孜不倦地在实验室里工作，试图将贱金属转化为黄金。

例句：The old books spoke of chymists who possessed secret knowledge of the elements.  古书中提到炼金术士拥有关于元素的秘密知识。|近义词：alchemist, chemist (modern usage)|反义词: None|词性:Noun
