paleozoology:/ˌpeɪliəʊzoʊˈɒlədʒi/ /ˌpælioʊzoʊˈɑːlədʒi/
n.|古动物学|The study of prehistoric animal life, including the study of fossils to determine the evolution and relationships of extinct animals.|Paleozoology helps us understand the biodiversity of past ecosystems. 古动物学帮助我们了解过去生态系统的生物多样性。|Paleontology, paleobiology| - |noun
