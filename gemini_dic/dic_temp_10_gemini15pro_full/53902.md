discountable:/dɪsˈkaʊntəbl/ | adj. |可贴现的，可打折的 | Something that is discountable can have its price reduced. | Example: The store offered discountable merchandise during the holiday sale.  这家商店在假日促销期间提供可打折的商品。| Example:  These bonds are discountable at most banks. 这些债券在大多数银行都可以贴现。 | Synonyms: reducible, negotiable | Antonyms: non-discountable, fixed-price |词性:形容词

词源:
From "discount" + "-able".  "Discount" comes from Old French "desconter," meaning "to count off, deduct." "-able" is a suffix that forms adjectives meaning "capable of being" or "worthy of being."
