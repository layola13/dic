nonrendition:/ˌnɒnˈrɛnˈdɪʃən/ | n. | 不引渡，不交出，不演奏，不表演 | The nonrendition of the fugitive angered the authorities.  当局对逃犯的不引渡感到愤怒。 |  The nonrendition of the famous aria disappointed the audience.  没有演奏那首著名的咏叹调让观众很失望。 | 近义词：non-performance, failure to deliver, refusal to surrender | 反义词：extradition, rendition, performance | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (not) + "rendition" (the act of rendering or performing something).  "Rendition" comes from the Old French "rendicion"  and ultimately from the Latin "reddere" (to give back).
