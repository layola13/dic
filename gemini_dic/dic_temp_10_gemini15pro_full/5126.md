allegoric:/ˌæləˈɡɔːrɪk/ | adj. | 寓言的，讽喻的 | Constituting or containing allegory; figurative.  | Example: The novel is highly allegoric, with each character representing an aspect of society.  这部小说充满了寓言，每个人物都代表着社会的某个方面。

Example:  His paintings are often allegoric, exploring themes of life and death. 他的画作通常是寓言式的，探讨着生与死。 | 近义词: symbolic, metaphorical, figurative | 反义词: literal, factual | 词性: adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French allegorique, or via late Latin from Greek allēgorikos, from allēgorein ‘speak figuratively’, from allos ‘other’ + -agoreuein (as in agoreuein ‘speak publicly’).
