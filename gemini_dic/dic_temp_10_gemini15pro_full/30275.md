cementations: /ˌsɛmɛnˈteɪʃənz/
| n. |胶结；胶结物|The process of cementing or becoming cemented, or the material that binds particles together. The cementations in this rock are primarily calcite.|例句：The cementations within the sandstone made it a strong building material. 砂岩中的胶结物使其成为坚固的建筑材料。

例句：The study of ancient cementations can reveal much about past environments. 对古代胶结物的研究可以揭示很多关于过去环境的信息。|近义词：consolidation, binding, adhesion|反义词: disintegration, erosion|词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin *cēmentātiōn- (stem of *cēmentātiō ), from Latin *cēmentāre (see cementation).
