edging:/ˈɛdʒɪŋ/
n.|边缘，边饰，镶边|A border or trimming on something.|The tablecloth had delicate lace edging. 桌布有精致的蕾丝边.
|border, trimming, fringe|center, middle
n.

edging:/ˈɛdʒɪŋ/
v.|缓慢移动，徐徐前进|Moving slowly and carefully.|The car was edging forward in the heavy traffic. 汽车在拥堵的交通中缓慢向前移动.
|inch, creep, sidle|rush, sprint
v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *eggyng*, from Old English *ecg*, meaning "edge."  The "-ing" suffix denotes a noun formed from a verb, implying the act of making an edge, or the material used to create an edge.
