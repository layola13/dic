chatoyment:/ʃætˈɔɪmənt/| n. | 变彩效应，猫眼效应，闪光，光彩 | A changeable luster or silky sheen, especially in some minerals such as tiger's eye.  The play of light across a surface, like that of a cat's eye. | The chatoyment of the opal was mesmerizing. 蛋白石的变彩效应令人着迷。| The tiger's eye gemstone is known for its distinctive chatoyment. 虎眼石以其独特的猫眼效应而闻名。 | 近义词：sheen, luster, shimmer, iridescence | 反义词：dullness, flatness | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From French *chatoyer*, "to shine like a cat's eyes," from *chat*, "cat" + *oeil*, "eye".
