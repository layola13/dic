misperceive:/ˌmɪspərˈsiːv/| v. |误解，错误地理解|To understand or interpret something incorrectly; to have a mistaken idea or impression of something.|She misperceived his silence as anger. 她把他的沉默误解为愤怒。|Example: He misperceived her intentions and thought she was being rude. 他误解了她的意图，认为她很粗鲁。|Synonyms: misunderstand, misinterpret, misconstrue|Antonyms: understand, comprehend, perceive correctly|verb
