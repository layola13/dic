gashing:/ˈɡæʃɪŋ/| adj.| 划伤的；切开的；割破的；|Making or having gashes.  Deeply or severely cut.|The knife left a gashing wound.|近义词：cutting, slashing, lacerating, tearing, ripping|反义词:healing, mending|词性:形容词
gash:/ɡæʃ/| n. vt.| 深长的伤口，切口；划破；砍伤|A long, deep cut.|He suffered a deep gash to his leg. 他的腿上有一道很深的伤口。|The falling tree gash the side of the car. 倒下的树划破了车身。|近义词：cut, laceration, wound, slash|反义词: - |词性:名词，动词
词源: From Old French *gasche, from *gaschier, from Frankish *gaskijan, from Proto-Germanic *gaskijaną (“to gash, pierce, sting”). Cognate with Old High German gasken (“to pierce, sting”).
