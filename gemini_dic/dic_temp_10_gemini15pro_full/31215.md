channeled:/ˈtʃænəld/ | v. |引导，集中，传导|To direct or guide along a particular route or path; to convey or transmit something as if through a channel. |She channeled her anger into her work. 她把愤怒转化为工作的动力。|The medium claimed to be channeling the spirit of a dead king.  灵媒声称在传达一位已故国王的灵魂。|近义词：directed, guided, conveyed, transmitted|反义词：dispersed, scattered|词性: 动词

channeled:/ˈtʃænəld/ | adj. |有沟渠的，有槽的|Having a channel or channels. |The river flowed through a channeled course. 河水流经一条有沟渠的河道。| The surface of the rock was channeled by erosion. 岩石表面被侵蚀形成了沟壑。| 近义词：grooved, furrowed | 反义词：smooth, flat |词性: 形容词
