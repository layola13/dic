harvestless: /ˈhɑːrvɪstlɪs/ | adj. | 无收获的，歉收的 | Without a harvest; unproductive. | The farmer experienced a harvestless season due to the drought. 由于干旱，农民经历了一个歉收的季节。| The harvestless fields lay barren under the scorching sun.  在烈日下，没有收获的田野一片荒芜。 | 近义词: barren, unproductive, unfruitful | 反义词: fruitful, productive, bountiful | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "harvest" (n.) + "-less" (suffix). "Harvest" comes from Old English "hærfest," meaning "autumn." "-less" is a suffix that means "without" or "lacking."
