dwarfnesses:/ˈdwɔːrfnɪsɪz/
noun
矮小，侏儒状态
The state or quality of being a dwarf; unusually small size.
The dwarfnesses of the trees in the bonsai garden were remarkable. 盆景园里树木的矮小令人惊叹。
Near Synonyms: shortness, smallness, diminutiveness
Antonyms: tallness, height, largeness
