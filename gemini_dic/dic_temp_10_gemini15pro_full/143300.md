outdrinks:/ˌaʊtˈdrɪŋks/
verb
胜过…的酒量，比…喝得更多
consumes more alcoholic beverages than
He outdrinks everyone at the party. 他在派对上比任何人都喝得多。
近义词: out-drink, drink more than
反义词: be outdrunk by, drink less than
词性: verb

Etymology:
"out-" (Old English *ūt, from Proto-Germanic *ūt) + "drink" (Old English drincan)
