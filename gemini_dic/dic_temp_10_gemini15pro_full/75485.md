frustratory:/ˈfrʌstreɪtəri/ /frʌˈstreɪtɔːri/
adjective
令人沮丧的；使人泄气的
Causing frustration; tending to frustrate.
It's frustrating to have to deal with so much bureaucracy.  处理这么多官僚程序真让人沮丧。
近义词:disappointing, discouraging, vexing, annoying
反义词:satisfying, gratifying, rewarding
词性:adjective

词源:
From frustrate + -ory. Ultimately from Latin frustra.
