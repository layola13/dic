nigrification:/ˌnaɪtrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |硝化作用| The process by which ammonia is converted to nitrites and then to nitrates by bacteria in the soil. |  The nigrification process is essential for plant growth. 硝化作用对植物生长至关重要。| synonyms: nitritation, nitratation | antonyms: denitrification | noun
