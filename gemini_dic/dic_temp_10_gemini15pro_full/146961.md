overirrigates:/ˌoʊvərˈɪrɪɡeɪts/
verb

to irrigate (land or crops) excessively.  The farmer overirrigates his fields, leading to waterlogging.
The farmer overirrigates his fields, leading to waterlogging.  农民过度灌溉了他的田地，导致土壤涝渍。
