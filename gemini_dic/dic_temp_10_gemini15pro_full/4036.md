aic:/eɪ aɪ siː/ |abbr.| Akaike information criterion| 赤池信息量准则 | A metric used in statistical model selection to assess the relative quality of different models for a given set of data, balancing goodness of fit with model complexity. | The model with the lowest AIC is preferred.  这个AIC值最低的模型是首选的。 |近义词:BIC (Bayesian information criterion)|反义词: N/A | abbreviation
