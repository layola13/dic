outsonnet:/ˌaʊtˈsɒnɪt/
n.
超越十四行诗；非十四行诗
A poem that deliberately avoids the conventions of the sonnet form, especially in terms of length or rhyme scheme.
The poet experimented with form, writing several outsonnets that challenged traditional structure.  这位诗人尝试了不同形式，写了几首挑战传统结构的非十四行诗。
Near synonyms:  anti-sonnet, non-sonnet, unsonnet
Antonyms: sonnet
Noun
