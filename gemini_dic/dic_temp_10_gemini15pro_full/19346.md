bibliopegic:/ˌbɪblɪəˈpɛdʒɪk/|adj.|装订精美的；关于书籍装订的|Used to describe books bound elaborately, often with luxurious materials. The biblipegic binding, featuring inlaid jewels and hand-tooled leather, made the book a work of art. 这本装订精美的书籍，镶嵌着宝石和手工雕刻的皮革，使它成了一件艺术品。|近义词: ornately bound, elaborately bound|反义词: plainly bound, simply bound


词源: From Greek *biblio- (book) + *pēg- (to fasten, fix).
