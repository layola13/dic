intercircle:/ˌɪntərˈsɜːrkl/ | v. |互相环绕，相互围绕|To form a circle around one another; to encircle each other.|The dancers intercircled, their arms intertwined. 舞蹈演员们互相环绕，手臂交缠在一起。|近义词：intertwine, interweave, encircle|反义词: separate, disentangle|词性:动词

Etymology:
From inter- (meaning "between, among, mutually, reciprocally") + circle (meaning "a round plane figure whose boundary consists of points equidistant from a fixed point").
