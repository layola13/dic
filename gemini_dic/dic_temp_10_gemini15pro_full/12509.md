aspersive:/əˈspɜːsɪv/
adjective
诽谤的，中伤的
Tending to asperse; defamatory.
例句：His aspersive remarks damaged her reputation. 他的诽谤性言论损害了她的名誉。

例句：The article was filled with aspersive accusations against the company. 这篇文章充满了对公司的诽谤指控。

近义词：defamatory, slanderous, libelous, calumnious, denigratory, disparaging
反义词：laudatory, complimentary, praising
词性：adjective
