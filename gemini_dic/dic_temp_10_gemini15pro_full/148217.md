overshroud:/ˌoʊvərˈʃraʊd/| v.|覆盖，遮蔽，笼罩|To cover or envelop completely, especially so as to conceal or protect.|The mountains were overshrouded in mist. 群山被薄雾笼罩。|Synonyms: envelop, veil, cloak, shroud, conceal, obscure|Antonyms: reveal, uncover, expose|verb

overshroud:/ˌoʊvərˈʃraʊd/| n.|覆盖物，遮蔽物|Something that covers or conceals.|The overshroud of secrecy surrounding the project made it difficult to learn any details.  围绕该项目的保密氛围使其难以了解任何细节。|Synonyms: covering, veil, cloak, shroud|Antonyms:  exposure, revelation|noun
