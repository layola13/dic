overindustrializes: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌstriəˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
过度工业化
to industrialize excessively; to develop industry in a region or country to an excessive degree.  This often implies negative consequences, such as pollution, resource depletion, or social disruption.
The small island nation overindustrialized in the 20th century, leading to severe environmental problems.  这个小岛国家在20世纪过度工业化，导致了严重的环境问题。
The government's policies have overindustrialized certain areas, neglecting the development of others. 政府的政策导致某些地区过度工业化，而忽略了其他地区的发展。
