nonnomad:/ˌnɒnˈnəʊmæd/ | n. | 非游牧民；定居者 |  A person who lives in a settled community, rather than a nomadic lifestyle.  A person who doesn't travel from place to place. |  The majority of the population are nonnomads, living in established towns and cities.  大多数人口是非游牧民族，生活在已建立的城镇中。

My grandparents were nonnomads, deeply rooted in their small village. 我的祖父母是非游牧民族，深深扎根于他们的小村庄。 | 近义词: settler, resident, townsman | 反义词: nomad, wanderer, migrant | 词性: noun
