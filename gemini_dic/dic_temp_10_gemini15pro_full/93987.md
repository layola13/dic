impairable:/ɪmˈpeərəbl/
adjective
可损害的，可削弱的
capable of being impaired or weakened.
Her hearing was impairable due to the loud noise. 她的听力由于噪音过大而可能受损。
The virus can cause impairable lung function. 这种病毒会导致肺功能受损。
近义词: vulnerable, susceptible, damageable
反义词: invulnerable, resistant, impervious
词性: adjective
词源: From impair + -able.  Impair comes from Old French empeirier "make worse, damage," from Vulgar Latin *impeiorare, from Latin in- "not, opposite of" + pejorare "make worse," from pejor "worse."
