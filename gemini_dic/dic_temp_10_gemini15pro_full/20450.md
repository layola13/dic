bittern:/ˈbɪtərn/ | n. |麻鳽;卤水|A type of wading bird with mottled brown plumage, found in marshes and reedbeds. Also, a bitter-tasting liquid remaining after salt has been extracted from seawater. |The bittern is a master of camouflage in its marshy habitat.  麻鳽是沼泽地带的伪装大师。

The bittern's booming call can be heard across the marshes. 麻鳽的低沉叫声在沼泽上回荡。|近义词：(bird) heron, egret (liquid) brine |反义词: (liquid) freshwater|词性:noun
