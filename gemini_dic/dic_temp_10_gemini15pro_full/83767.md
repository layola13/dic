haircloth:/ˈhɛəklɒθ/ (US) /ˈheəklɒθ/ (UK)| n. |毛布，粗麻布；用以苦修的粗麻衣|A coarse, rough fabric typically made from horsehair or goat hair, often worn as a garment for penance or mourning.|The hermit wore a haircloth shirt as a sign of his devotion.|近义词:sackcloth, burlap|反义词:silk, satin|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Old English *hǣrclaþ, from hǣr (hair) + claþ (cloth).
