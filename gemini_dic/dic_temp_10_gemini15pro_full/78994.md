gishes:/ˈɡɪʃɪz/| n.| 古怪的把戏；古怪的行为 (通常指复数形式) |  A series of strange or unusual acts, often performed as a game or challenge, typically involving creativity, absurdity, and public interaction.  "Gishes" are tasks undertaken as part of the annual "GISHWHES" competition (Greatest International Scavenger Hunt the World Has Ever Seen). | Example:  The gishes this year included creating a portrait of your pet using only vegetables. 今年的古怪任务包括只用蔬菜创作你的宠物肖像。

Example: Completing all the gishes in time was a real challenge.  按时完成所有古怪的任务是一个真正的挑战。|近义词：pranks, stunts, antics, escapades|反义词: normal behavior, conventional activities|词性:noun (plural)
