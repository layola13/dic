middle-aged:/ˌmɪdlˈeɪdʒd/ | adj. | 中年的 | Being in the period of life between youth and old age, typically between 45 and 65. | He's a middle-aged man with a receding hairline. 他是一个中年男子，头发开始后退。 | She found herself relating more to the middle-aged characters in the novel. 她发现自己更能与小说中的中年角色产生共鸣。 | 近义词: elderly | 反义词: young, youthful | 词性: 形容词
