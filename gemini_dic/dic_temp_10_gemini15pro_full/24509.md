broad-spectrum:/ˌbrɔːd ˈspɛktrəm/| adj.|广谱的，范围广泛的|Effective against a wide range of organisms. |This broad-spectrum antibiotic is effective against various types of bacteria. 这种广谱抗生素对多种细菌有效。|
broad-spectrum:/ˌbrɔːd ˈspɛktrəm/| adj.|广谱的，范围广泛的|Covering a wide range of frequencies or wavelengths. |A broad-spectrum light source emits light across a wide range of the visible spectrum.  广谱光源在可见光谱的宽范围内发射光。|近义词：wide-ranging, comprehensive|反义词：narrow-spectrum, limited|词性: adjective
