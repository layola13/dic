balls-out:/ˌbɔːlz ˈaʊt/| adj. |全力以赴的，竭尽全力的；鲁莽的，不计后果的|Characterized by reckless abandon and maximum effort, often with disregard for consequences.  It implies going all in, holding nothing back, and taking risks.  Can be used both positively (to describe courageous action) and negatively (to describe reckless behavior).|He went balls-out in the final race and won. 他在最后一场比赛中全力以赴，赢得了胜利。

The CEO's balls-out strategy ultimately led to the company's downfall.  这位首席执行官孤注一掷的策略最终导致了公司的垮台。|近义词：all-out, full-throttle, hell-bent, reckless, unrestrained |反义词：cautious, restrained, careful, hesitant|adjective
