madras:/məˈdræs/, /ˈmædrəs/ | n. | 马德拉斯布；马德拉斯格子呢；马德拉斯衬衫 | A lightweight cotton fabric, typically patterned with colorful plaid or checks, originally from Madras, India. Also refers to clothing made from this fabric, especially shirts. |  She wore a madras shirt to the picnic. 她穿着一件马德拉斯衬衫去野餐了。 |  The madras curtains added a touch of color to the room. 马德拉斯窗帘给房间增添了一抹色彩。 | 近义词: gingham, plaid | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "plain fabric") | 词性: noun
