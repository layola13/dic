listenable:/ˈlɪsənəbl/| adj.|可倾听的；可以一听的；值得一听的|Easy to listen to; agreeable to the ear.  Relatively easy or enjoyable to listen to.|The podcast is quite listenable, even for people who aren't usually interested in the topic.  这个播客很容易听，即使是对这个话题通常不感兴趣的人来说也是如此。|近义词：audible, agreeable, pleasant, enjoyable|反义词：unlistenable, tedious, boring|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
