monandric:/məˈnændrɪk/
| adj. |
单雄蕊的；一雄蕊的|
Having only one stamen.  Relating to or characterized by monandry (the condition of having only one stamen).|
Example 1:  The flower is monandric, possessing a single stamen.  这朵花是单雄蕊的，只有一根雄蕊。

Example 2:  Monandric flowers are characteristic of certain orchid species.  单雄蕊花是某些兰花品种的特征。
|
Synonyms: monandrous|
Antonyms: polyandrous (having many stamens)|
adjective
