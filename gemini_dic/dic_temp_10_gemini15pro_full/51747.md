devolvements: /ˌdiːˈvɒlvmənts/ | n. | 移交，下放，转让（权力、责任等） | The act of devolving something, especially power or responsibility, to a lower level. |  The government plans further devolvements of power to regional assemblies. 政府计划进一步将权力下放给地方议会。|  The recent devolution of responsibilities has led to greater efficiency. 最近的责任下放提高了效率。 | synonyms: delegation, transfer, decentralization | antonyms: centralization, consolidation | noun
