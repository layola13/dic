draw-string:/ˈdrɔːstrɪŋ/| adj.|抽绳的，用抽绳束紧的|Closed or tightened with a drawstring.|This bag has a convenient draw-string closure. 这个包有一个方便的抽绳封口。|近义词：adjustable, tie, laced|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective
