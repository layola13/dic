duels:/ˈduːəlz/ (US), /ˈdjuːəlz/ (UK)| n. v.|决斗（名词），决斗（动词）|A prearranged combat between two persons to settle a private quarrel or point of honor, fought with deadly weapons according to an accepted code of procedure.|The two knights engaged in a duel to the death. 两位骑士进行了一场生死决斗。|近义词： combats, fights, contests|反义词： truces, peace, agreements|n.

duels:/ˈduːəlz/ (US), /ˈdjuːəlz/ (UK)| v.|决斗|To fight a duel; to engage in a formal combat with someone.|They dueled at dawn. 他们在黎明决斗。|近义词： fight, combat, contend|反义词： negotiate, compromise, concede|v.
