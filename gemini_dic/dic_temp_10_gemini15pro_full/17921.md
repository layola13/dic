词源:
From Middle English *beeswax, *beiswax, from Old English bēoweax (“beeswax”), from bēo (“bee”) + weax (“wax”). Cognate with Scots beis-wax (“beeswax”), Saterland Frisian Bjuunwoaks (“beeswax”), West Frisian bijewaaks (“beeswax”), Dutch bijenwas (“beeswax”), German Bienenwachs (“beeswax”).
