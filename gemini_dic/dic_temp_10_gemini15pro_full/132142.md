noncultivation:/ˌnɒnkʌltɪˈveɪʃən/
|n.| 非耕作，未开垦 |The noncultivation of the land led to soil erosion.|这片土地的未开垦导致了水土流失。|
|例句：The noncultivation of the fields resulted in a decrease in crop yields.|田地未耕作导致作物产量下降。|
|例句：Long-term noncultivation can improve soil health.|长期休耕可以改善土壤健康。|
|近义词：fallowness, idleness, abandonment|
|反义词：cultivation, tillage, farming|
|词性：名词|


noncultivation:/ˌnɒnkʌltɪˈveɪʃən/
|n.|  (精神、能力等)未开发，未培养 |His noncultivation of his musical talent is a shame.|他没有培养自己的音乐才能，真是可惜。|
|例句：Her noncultivation of social skills made it difficult for her to make friends.|她没有培养社交技能，这使她很难交到朋友。|
|例句：The noncultivation of critical thinking skills can lead to poor decision-making.|批判性思维能力的缺乏会导致糟糕的决策。|
|近义词：neglect, underdevelopment, lack of development|
|反义词：cultivation, development, nurture|
