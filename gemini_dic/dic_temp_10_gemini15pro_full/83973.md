half-counted:/ˌhɑːfˈkaʊntɪd/
| adj. |
| 半计算的；未完全计算的；被低估的 |
| Not fully or accurately counted; underestimated or undervalued.  It implies that something hasn't been given its full due or consideration. |
| The refugees were half-counted in the initial census, leading to a shortage of resources. 初步人口普查中难民人数被低估了，导致资源短缺。|
|  The contributions of women in science are often half-counted in history books.  科学界女性的贡献在历史书中经常被低估。|
| underestimated, undervalued, shortchanged, slighted, overlooked |
| fully counted, overestimated, overvalued |
| adjective |
