overfat:/ˌoʊvərˈfæt/| adj.| 过胖的，脂肪过多的| Having too much body fat, especially compared to lean muscle mass.  It goes beyond simply being overweight, focusing on body composition. | Example:  Studies show that people can be overfat even if they have a "normal" BMI. 研究表明，即使BMI“正常”，人们也可能脂肪过剩。

Example: He was diagnosed as overfat despite not being significantly overweight. 尽管体重没有明显超重，他还是被诊断为脂肪过剩。 | 近义词: obese, overweight, fat | 反义词: lean, fit, healthy | 词性: adjective
