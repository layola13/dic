outsprinting:/ˌaʊtˈsprɪntɪŋ/
verb
超越，冲刺得比…快
The act of running faster than someone else in a sprint.
Usain Bolt outsprinted his rivals to win the gold medal. 尤塞恩·博尔特在短跑比赛中超越了他的对手，赢得了金牌。
