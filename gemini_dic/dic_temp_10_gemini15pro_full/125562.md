multitasking:/ˌmʌltiˈtɑːskɪŋ/ | n. | 多任务处理 | The simultaneous execution of more than one program or task by a computer or operating system.  The ability of a person to do several things at the same time. |  She's good at multitasking and can handle several projects at once. 她擅长多任务处理，可以同时处理多个项目。 | synonyms: polytasking, concurrent processing | antonyms: single-tasking, serial processing | noun


multitask:/ˌmʌltiˈtɑːsk/ | v. | 多任务处理 | To perform more than one task at the same time. |  He tried to multitask, but ended up making mistakes on both projects. 他试图同时处理多项任务，但最终两个项目都出错了。 | synonyms: juggle, manage multiple tasks | antonyms: focus on one task, single-task | verb
