caruncular: /kəˈrʌŋkjʊlə(r)/ | adj. | 肉阜的，似肉阜的 | Relating to or resembling a caruncle. | The caruncular membrane at the inner corner of the eye can sometimes become inflamed.  眼睛内角的肉阜有时会发炎。| Synonym: fleshy, wattled | Antonym: smooth | adjective

caruncle: /ˈkærʌŋkl/ | n. | 肉阜，肉垂 | A small, fleshy outgrowth on an animal or plant.  | The turkey's caruncle wobbled as it gobbled. 火鸡的肉垂随着它的咯咯叫声而摆动。| Synonym: wattle, papilla | Antonym: N/A | noun
