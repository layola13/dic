deadbeats:/ˈdɛdˌbiːts/| n.|赖账的人，游手好闲的人|A person who deliberately avoids paying debts or fulfilling responsibilities.  Someone who is lazy and avoids work.|He's a real deadbeat; he never pays his rent on time. 他真是个赖账的，从不按时付房租。|Synonyms: loafer, slacker, freeloader, sponger|Antonyms: hard worker, responsible person|noun

deadbeat:/ˈdɛdˌbiːt/| adj.|赖账的，游手好闲的|Describing someone who habitually avoids paying debts or fulfilling responsibilities. Also, describing something dull or lifeless.|She called him a deadbeat dad for not paying child support. 她骂他不付抚养费，是个赖账的父亲。|Synonyms: delinquent, irresponsible, lazy|Antonyms: responsible, diligent, hardworking|adjective
