lenses:/ˈlɛnzɪz/
n.
镜片；透镜；镜头；隐形眼镜
a piece of glass or other transparent substance with curved sides that is used for making things look clearer, smaller, or bigger
例句：She wears contact lenses. 她戴隐形眼镜。

例句：The camera lens was scratched. 相机镜头被刮花了。

近义词：glasses, spectacles, contacts (for eyeglasses/contact lenses)
近义词：lens (singular)

反义词：(No direct antonym, depends on the specific meaning)

词性: Noun (Plural)
