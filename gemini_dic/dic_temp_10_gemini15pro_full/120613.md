mirza:/ˈmɪərzə/ | n. | 王子；贵族；先生（用于穆斯林男子名字前）| A title of respect for a Muslim nobleman, especially in India and Pakistan.  It is placed before a given name. | Example 1: Mirza Ghalib was a renowned Urdu poet. 米尔扎·加利卜是一位著名的乌尔都语诗人。

Example 2: He addressed him as Mirza Asad. 他称呼他为米尔扎·阿萨德。 | Near Synonyms:  prince, nobleman, lord, sir | Antonyms: commoner, peasant | Noun
