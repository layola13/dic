enlister:/ɪnˈlɪstər/| n.|应募者；入伍者；征募者|A person who enlists or causes another to enlist, especially in the armed forces.|Many young enlisters were eager to serve their country. 许多年轻的应募者渴望为国效力。|Example: The army enlister was successful in recruiting many new soldiers.  陆军征兵员成功招募了许多新兵。|Synonyms: recruit, volunteer, enrollee|Antonyms: draftee, conscript|noun
