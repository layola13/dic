over-ripeness:/ˈoʊvərˈraɪpnəs/
n.
过度成熟
the state of being too ripe
The over-ripeness of the bananas made them mushy.  这些香蕉因过熟而变得软烂。

overripe:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪp/
adj.
过熟的
too ripe
The overripe fruit attracted swarms of flies.  过熟的水果引来了成群的苍蝇。


ETYMOLOGY->
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *ubari) + "ripe" (Old English rīpe, of Germanic origin) + "-ness" (Old English -nes(s), suffix forming nouns of state or quality)
