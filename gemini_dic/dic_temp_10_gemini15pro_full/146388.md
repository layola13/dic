overflowed:/ˌoʊvərˈfloʊd/ | v. | 溢出，泛滥 | to flow over the brim or top of a container; to be filled beyond capacity | The river overflowed its banks after the heavy rain. 河水在大雨后漫过了河岸。| 近义词：inundated, flooded, overwhelmed | 反义词：drained, emptied | 词性：动词

overflowed:/ˌoʊvərˈfloʊd/ | adj. | 溢出的，泛滥的 | filled beyond capacity | The sink was overflowed with dirty dishes. 水槽里堆满了脏盘子。| 近义词：full, brimming, overflowing | 反义词：empty, drained | 词性：形容词
