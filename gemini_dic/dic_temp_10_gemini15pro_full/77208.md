gasteropoda:/ˌɡæstəˈrɒpədə/ (US) /ˌɡæstrəˈpɒdə/ (UK)| n. |腹足纲|A large class of mollusks, typically having a single, often spirally coiled shell and a muscular foot for locomotion.  Includes snails, slugs, and limpets.|Snails and slugs belong to the class Gasteropoda.  蜗牛和蛞蝓属于腹足纲。|Synonyms: univalves, gastropod mollusks|Antonyms: None|noun


gasteropod:/ˈɡæstərəpɒd/ (US) /ˈɡæstrəpɒd/ (UK)| n. adj. |腹足动物|A mollusk of the class Gasteropoda.|The garden snail is a common gasteropod. 花园蜗牛是一种常见的腹足动物。|Synonyms: univalve, snail, slug|Antonyms: None|noun, adjective
