hard-faced:/ˌhɑːrdˈfeɪst/ /ˌhärdˈfāst/ | adj. | 冷酷的，铁石心肠的 | Having a face that looks severe, strict, or unfeeling.  This often suggests a lack of compassion or empathy. |  He was a hard-faced man, not easily moved by sentiment. 他是一个铁石心肠的人，不容易被感情所打动。 | The hard-faced judge showed no mercy.  这位冷酷的法官没有表现出任何怜悯。 | stern, austere, grim, flinty, ruthless | kind, compassionate, sympathetic | adjective
