nondeflation:/ˌnɒndɪˈfleɪʃən/ | n. | 非通货紧缩 | The state of not being in deflation; the absence of a sustained decrease in the general price level of goods and services in an economy. |  The government's policies aimed at achieving nondeflation. 政府的政策旨在实现非通货紧缩。| Synonyms: price stability, reflation | Antonyms: deflation, disinflation | noun
