osteotomist:/ˌɒstiˈɒtəmɪst/ | n. | 骨科手术医生；截骨术医师 | A surgeon who performs osteotomies. | The osteotomist skillfully reshaped the patient's tibia.  骨科医生巧妙地重塑了病人的胫骨。|  Synonyms: bone surgeon, orthopedic surgeon | Antonyms: None | Noun

Etymology:
From osteo- (Greek 'osteon', bone) + -tomist (from Greek 'tome', cutting)
