acclaimworthy: /əˈkleɪmˌwɜːrði/
adjective
值得称赞的
Deserving of acclaim; praiseworthy.  Something that is so good it deserves praise and recognition.
Her performance in the play was acclaimworthy and received a standing ovation. 她在这部剧中的表演值得称赞，并获得了全场起立鼓掌。

Her latest novel is an acclaimworthy achievement. 她最新的小说是值得称赞的成就。

近义词: laudable, commendable, praiseworthy, meritorious
反义词: unremarkable, mediocre, unimpressive
词性: adjective

词源:  From acclaim + -worthy.  Acclaim ultimately comes from the Latin acclamare "to shout at, applaud," from ad- "to" + clamare "to cry out."  -worthy is a suffix meaning "deserving of."
