dogfishes:/ˈdɒɡfɪʃɪz/ /ˈdɔːɡfɪʃɪz/
n.
角鲨，狗鲨，棘鲛
Small sharks with spines in front of their dorsal fins.  They are found in many parts of the world's oceans.
Dofishes are bottom-dwelling sharks found in temperate and tropical seas worldwide. 角鲨是生活在世界各地温带和热带海洋中的底栖鲨鱼。
Fishermen often catch dogfishes as bycatch. 渔民经常捕到角鲨作为副产品。
近义词：spiny dogfish, shark,  
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用其他类型的鱼类，例如：trout 鳟鱼)
词性:noun
