gassier:/ˈɡæsɪər/ | adj. | 更充满气的；更多气体的 | Containing or resembling gas; more gaseous. | The air in the cave was gassier than usual. 洞穴里的空气比平时更多气体。 | The balloon became gassier as it was inflated. 气球充气时变得更充满气。 | 近义词: airier, more gaseous | 反义词: less gaseous, denser | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From gas + -ier (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from Dutch gas.
