burnishable:/ˈbɜːrnɪʃəbl/| adj.|可抛光的，可擦亮的|Able to be polished by rubbing, especially with a burnisher.|The old silver tray was burnishable and could be made to gleam.  这旧的银盘可以抛光，可以擦得很亮。|The copper pot was burnishable to a high shine.  这个铜壶可以擦得非常亮。|近义词：polishable, buffable|反义词:dull, matte|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English *burnisshable, from burnish + -able.  Burnish comes from Old French burnir, ultimately from Frankish *brunnon "to make brown, polish."
