attachments:/əˈtætʃmənts/| n. |附件，附属物，依恋|Something attached to something else; supplementary material added to a letter, email, etc. |例句：Please find the attachments for your reference.  请查看附件供您参考。

例句：She felt a strong attachment to her hometown. 她对家乡有着强烈的依恋。|近义词：appendix, supplement, enclosure, affection, bond|反义词:detachment, aversion|词性:noun
