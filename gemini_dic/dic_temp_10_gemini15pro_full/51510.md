detrimental:/ˌdetrɪˈmentl/
adjective
有害的，不利的
tending to cause harm
Smoking is detrimental to your health. 吸烟有害健康。

近义词: harmful, damaging, injurious, adverse, prejudicial
反义词: beneficial, advantageous, helpful, favorable

词性: adjective

词源:  From Late Latin *dētrimentālis, from Latin dētrīmentum (“loss, damage”), from dēterere (“to rub away, wear off”), from dē- (“away, off”) + terere (“to rub”).
