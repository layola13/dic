nonconviviality:/ˌnɒnkənˌvɪviˈælɪti/
n.
不 convivial; 不歡樂; 不友好
Lack of conviviality; the state or quality of being unconvivial.
The nonconviviality of the atmosphere made it difficult to relax. 气氛不友好，让人难以放松。
nonconviviality|noun
Lack of cheerfulness or friendliness.
The meeting was marked by a distinct nonconviviality. 会议的特点是明显的不友好。

近义词:unfriendliness, inhospitability, coldness, aloofness
反义词:conviviality, friendliness, warmth, sociability
