noncorrection:/ˌnɒnkəˈrɛkʃən/ |n.| 不改正，不纠正|The noncorrection of errors can lead to bigger problems down the line. 不纠正错误会导致以后出现更大的问题。|The policy of noncorrection was controversial.  不纠正的政策是有争议的。|近义词: non-intervention, inaction|反义词: correction, amendment|词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (not) + "correction" (the act of correcting).  From Latin "non" (not) and "corrigere" (to correct).
