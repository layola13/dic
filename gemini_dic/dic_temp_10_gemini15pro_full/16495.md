bargepole:/ˈbɑːrdʒˌpoʊl/ (US) /ˈbɑːdʒˌpəʊl/ (UK)| n. |驳船撑杆；（用于强调拒绝）绝不；想都别想|A long pole used to propel or guide a barge.  Figuratively used to express strong aversion or refusal.|I wouldn't touch that with a bargepole. 我绝对不会碰那个东西。|Near synonyms: pole, staff|Antonyms:  (Figurative) willingly, gladly|noun
