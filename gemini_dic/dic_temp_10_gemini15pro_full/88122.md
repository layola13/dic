hijra:/ˈhɪdʒrə/ | n. | 海吉拉，阉人，变性人 |  A term used in South Asia, particularly in India, Pakistan, and Bangladesh, to refer to a transgender person, typically assigned male at birth, who identifies with a feminine gender expression.  Hijras often live in communities and have a long history and distinct cultural identity.  While often considered a third gender, their experiences and identities are diverse. |  She met a hijra at the market who told her fortune.  她在市场上遇到一个海吉拉，给她算命。 | Synonyms: transgender, transsexual (these terms are not perfectly equivalent and should be used with caution), kinnar (regional synonym) | Antonyms: cisgender (this is a broad antonym relating to gender identity, not specifically to hijras) | noun
