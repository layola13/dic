abide:/əˈbaɪd/| v.|遵守，忍受，居留|To remain; continue; stay; endure.  To put up with.|例句：She couldn't abide the loud music. 她无法忍受大声的音乐。

He abided by the terms of the contract. 他遵守了合同条款。|近义词：tolerate, endure, bear, stay, remain, comply with, obey|反义词:disobey, break, leave|词性:动词

abided:/əˈbaɪdɪd/| v.|遵守(abide的过去式和过去分词)，忍受(abide的过去式和过去分词)，居留(abide的过去式和过去分词)|Past tense and past participle of abide. To have remained; continued; stayed; endured. To have put up with.|例句：They abided by the rules. 他们遵守了规则。

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He abides by the rules. = 他遵守规则。
She abides in a small cottage. = 她住在一个小村舍里。

一般过去时:
They abided by their promise. = 他们遵守了诺言。
He abided the pain without complaint. = 他毫无怨言地忍受了痛苦。

现在完成时:
We have abided here for many years. = 我们在这里住了很多年。
She has always abided by her principles. = 她一直坚持自己的原则。

过去完成时:
They had abided by the agreement until it was broken. = 他们在协议被破坏之前一直遵守协议。
She had abided in that house since childhood. = 她从孩提时代起就住在那所房子里。

将来时:
I will abide by the court's decision. = 我将遵守法院的判决。
He will abide with us for a while. = 他将和我们待一段时间。

虚拟语气:
If I were to abide by their rules, I would be miserable. = 如果我要遵守他们的规则，我会很痛苦。
I wish I could abide the noise, but it's too loud. = 我希望我能忍受噪音，但它太大了。

被动语态:(abide 通常不用于被动语态)

疑问句:
Do you abide by the law? = 你遵守法律吗？
Where does she abide? = 她住在哪里？

否定句:
I cannot abide such behavior. = 我不能容忍这种行为。
He does not abide in this city anymore. = 他不再住在这个城市了。


PHRASE->
