centerless:/ˈsɛntərlɪs/
adj.
无中心的，无轴的
lacking a center, especially lacking a central axis for support or rotation.
Centerless grinding is a machining process that uses abrasive grinding wheels to remove material from a workpiece. 无心磨削是一种机械加工工艺，使用砂轮从工件上去除材料。
centerless grinding wheels are commonly used for high volume production. 无心磨床砂轮通常用于大批量生产。
centralized, centered
acentric, eccentric
adjective
