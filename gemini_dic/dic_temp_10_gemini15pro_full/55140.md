dissect:/daɪˈsekt/ /dɪˈsekt/| vt. n.|解剖，仔细分析|To cut apart (an animal body, plant, etc.) to examine the structure; to analyze (something) closely and critically. |The biology students dissected a frog in class. 生物系的学生在课堂上解剖了一只青蛙。

The lawyer dissected the witness's testimony. 律师仔细分析了证人的证词。|近义词：analyze, examine, scrutinize, anatomize|反义词：synthesize, combine, construct|词性:动词/名词

dissect:/daɪˈsekt/ /dɪˈsekt/| n.|解剖|The act of dissecting something. |The dissect of the specimen revealed the cause of death.  对标本的解剖揭示了死因。|近义词：anatomy, dissection|反义词：synthesis, construction|词性:名词
