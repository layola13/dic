intermutual:/ˌɪntərˈmjutʃuəl/ | adj. | 相互的，彼此的 |  Existing or occurring between two or more people or groups. It emphasizes a reciprocal relationship where the feeling or action is shared equally. | Example: Intermutual respect is essential for a successful partnership.  相互尊重对于成功的合作至关重要。 | Example:  Their intermutual distrust made collaboration impossible. 他们彼此的不信任使得合作不可能。 | 近义词: mutual, reciprocal, shared | 反义词: one-sided, unilateral | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "Inter-" (between) + "mutual" (shared).  "Mutual" comes from Old French "mutuel," ultimately from Latin "mutuus" (lent, borrowed, reciprocal).
