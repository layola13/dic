magistratically: /ˌmædʒɪˈstreɪtɪkli/ | adv. | 专横地；威严地；有权威地 | In a manner befitting a magistrate; authoritatively and importantly. | He dismissed the objection magistratically. 他专横地驳回了反对意见。|  Synonyms: authoritatively, imperiously, dictatorially, domineeringly, pompously, peremptorily | Antonyms: humbly, submissively, meekly | 词性: 副词

magistrate: /ˈmædʒɪstreɪt/ | n. | 地方法官；治安官 | A civil officer who administers the law, especially one who conducts a court dealing with minor offenses and holds preliminary hearings for more serious ones. | The magistrate sentenced him to community service. 地方法官判他进行社区服务。 | Synonyms: judge, justice, official | Antonyms: criminal, defendant | 词性: 名词
