abandoning: /əˈbændənɪŋ/
verb
放弃，抛弃
Ceasing to support or look after (someone or something); deserting.
They were accused of abandoning their child. = 他们被指控遗弃了他们的孩子。

He was abandoning his car and fleeing on foot. = 他弃车而逃。

近义词: deserting, forsaking, leaving, relinquishing
反义词: keeping, maintaining, preserving, retaining
词性: 动名词 (gerund) or 现在分词 (present participle)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French abandoner, from a bandon "at one's disposal," from a "to" + bandon "power, jurisdiction."  Originally meaning "to put (something) under someone else's control." The modern sense of leaving completely developed later.

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is abandoning his old habits. = 他正在放弃他的旧习惯。
She is abandoning the project due to lack of funding. = 由于缺乏资金，她正在放弃这个项目。

一般过去时:
They abandoned the ship when it began to sink. = 当船开始下沉时，他们弃船了。
The soldiers abandoned their post. = 士兵们放弃了他们的岗位。

现在完成时:
He has abandoned all hope of finding his lost dog. = 他已经放弃了找到他丢失的狗的所有希望。
She has abandoned her career to raise her children. = 她为了抚养孩子而放弃了她的事业。

过去完成时:
They had abandoned the car by the side of the road before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，他们已经把车丢弃在路边了。
He had abandoned his dreams of becoming a doctor. = 他已经放弃了成为一名医生的梦想。

将来时:
They will be abandoning the building soon. = 他们很快就要放弃这栋楼了。
She will be abandoning her current strategy. = 她将放弃她目前的策略。


虚拟语气:
If I were abandoning the project, I would tell you. = 如果我要放弃这个项目，我会告诉你的。
I wish he weren't abandoning his family. = 我希望他不要抛弃他的家人。

被动语态 (abandoning本身不是被动语态，但可以构成被动语态的一部分):
The project is being abandoned due to lack of funds. = 由于缺乏资金，该项目正在被放弃。
The ship was abandoned by the crew. = 船被船员遗弃了。

疑问句:
Why are they abandoning the ship? = 他们为什么要弃船？
Is he abandoning his studies? = 他要放弃学业吗？

否定句:
He is not abandoning his post. = 他没有放弃他的岗位。
They are not abandoning the project. = 他们没有放弃这个项目。


PHRASE->
abandoning ship = 弃船，比喻放弃一个快要失败的事业或计划
abandoning hope = 放弃希望
abandoning all pretense = 放弃所有伪装
abandoning oneself to =  沉溺于；放纵自己
abandoning a child = 遗弃孩子
