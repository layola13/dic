fossilizations:/ˌfɒsɪlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. |  化石化（作用），僵化 | The process of becoming a fossil; the state of being fossilized.  The process by which organic remains are transformed into fossils.  Also, a state of being stagnant or unchanging, like a fossilized idea. |  Example 1: The fossilizations found in the La Brea Tar Pits offer a glimpse into prehistoric Los Angeles. 拉布雷亚沥青坑中发现的化石让我们得以一窥史前洛杉矶的面貌。

Example 2:  The museum exhibits showcased various stages of fossilization.  博物馆的展品展示了化石化的不同阶段。 | Near synonyms: petrification, lithification | Antonyms:  decay, decomposition | Noun
