crash-landed:/kræʃˈlændɪd/ | v. | 紧急迫降，坠毁着陆 | To land an aircraft in an emergency, usually roughly and causing damage. | The pilot crash-landed the plane in a field after the engine failed. 飞行员在引擎失灵后将飞机迫降在一块田野里。| The plane crash-landed on a deserted island.  飞机迫降在一个荒岛上。 | Synonyms: crash, ditch, force-land | Antonyms: take off, land smoothly | verb

crash-landed:/kræʃˈlændɪd/ | adj. |  (指飞机)紧急迫降的，坠毁着陆的|  Having landed in an emergency, usually roughly and causing damage. | The crash-landed plane lay in the field, a mangled wreck.  迫降的飞机躺在田野里，成了一堆扭曲的残骸。| The survivors of the crash-landed aircraft were rescued by helicopter. 迫降飞机的幸存者被直升机救起。 | Synonyms: wrecked, downed | Antonyms: intact, airworthy | adjective
