discommon:/dɪsˈkɒmən/
adjective
不寻常的，罕见的
unusual, infrequent, not common
例句：Discommon occurrences like these are rarely seen.  像这样不寻常的事情很少见。

例句：It's discommon to see snow in July. 七月份下雪很不寻常。
近义词：rare, unusual, atypical, uncommon, infrequent, scarce
反义词：common, usual, typical, frequent, ordinary, prevalent
词性：adjective
词源：dis- (not) + common
