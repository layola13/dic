castability:/ˌkæstəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可铸性; 流动性 | The ease with which a metal or other material can be cast into a desired shape. | The castability of the alloy is excellent.  这种合金的可铸性极佳。| Synonyms: fluidity, pourability | Antonyms: uncastability, intractability | noun
