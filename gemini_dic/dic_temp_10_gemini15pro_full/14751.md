awfuller:/ˈɔːfələr/ | adj. | 更糟糕的，更可怕的 |  A comparative form of "awful," meaning more awful. Although grammatically correct, "more awful" is generally preferred in formal writing. | The traffic was awfuller today than yesterday.  今天交通比昨天更糟糕。|  The movie was awfuller than I expected. 这部电影比我想象的更糟糕。| 近义词: worse, more terrible, more dreadful | 反义词: better, nicer, more pleasant | 词性: 形容词

词源: From "awful" + "-er" (comparative suffix). "Awful" originally meant "inspiring awe" but evolved to mean "extremely bad" or "unpleasant."
