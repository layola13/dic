cameoed:/ˈkæmioʊd/
| v. |客串；扮演小角色；|To make a brief appearance, especially as a celebrity, in a film, play, or other work.|例句：The famous actor cameoed in the latest Marvel movie.  这位著名演员在最新的漫威电影中客串了一下。|例句：She cameoed as a waitress in the play. 她在剧中客串了一个服务员的角色。|近义词：appeared, guested, featured|反义词: starred (as a main role)
|词性: 动词
