inferobranchiate:/ˌɪnfəroʊˈbræŋkiət/
adjective
下鳃的；鳃位低下的
having gills located low on the body, specifically beneath the mantle skirt, as in certain snails.
The inferobranchiate snail clung to the rock.  这种下鳃的蜗牛紧紧地贴在岩石上。
近义词:hypobranchial
