filthying:/ˈfɪlθiɪŋ/
|v.|使污秽，弄脏|The act of making something filthy or disgusting.  It implies making something dirty with particularly unpleasant substances.|He was accused of filthying the public park with litter. 他被指控用垃圾弄脏了公园。|近义词:soiling, dirtying, polluting, contaminating|反义词:cleaning, purifying|词性:动词

Etymology: From "filthy" + "-ing" suffix. "Filthy" comes from Old English "fylthig," meaning "foul, dirty," related to "ful" (foul).
