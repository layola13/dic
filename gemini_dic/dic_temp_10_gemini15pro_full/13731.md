auberge:/oʊˈbɛrʒ/ | n. | 小旅馆，客栈 | A small inn or hotel, typically in a rural area. |  We stayed at a charming auberge in the French countryside. 我们在法国乡村一个迷人的小旅馆里住了下来。

例句：
The auberge offered a cozy atmosphere and delicious regional cuisine. 这家小旅馆提供舒适的氛围和美味的区域菜肴。

例句：
They found an auberge nestled in the valley. 他们在山谷里找到了一家小旅馆。

近义词：inn, hotel, lodge, guesthouse | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) motel, chain hotel | 词性: noun

词源: From Old French *alberge, from Vulgar Latin *albergium, from Frankish *haribergon (“army shelter, lodging”), from *harja (“army”) + *bergon (“protection”). Ultimately from the Proto-Germanic *harjaz (“army”) and *berganą (“to protect, save”). Cognate with Old High German heriberga (“army shelter, lodging”).
