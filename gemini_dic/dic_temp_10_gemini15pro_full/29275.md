cassus:/ˈkæsəs/| adj.|空的，无效的，无用的|Empty, void, invalid, of no effect.  Derived from Latin "cassus" meaning "empty, hollow, vain."  Often used in legal contexts.|The contract was declared cassus because of a technicality.  合同由于技术性原因被宣布无效。| 近义词: void, null, invalid, futile, ineffective| 反义词: valid, effective, binding|词性:形容词
