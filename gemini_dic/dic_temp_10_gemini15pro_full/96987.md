infantility:/ˌɪnfənˈtɪləti/| n.|幼稚，幼稚的行为，幼儿期|The state of being infantile; childishness or babyishness.  It can refer to behavior, mental state, or physical development.|His constant tantrums are a sign of his infantility. 他不停地发脾气是幼稚的表现。|Synonyms: childishness, immaturity, puerility, babyishness|Antonyms: maturity, adulthood, sophistication|noun

infantilism:/ˌɪnfæntɪlɪzəm/| n.|幼稚病，婴儿期持续状态|A condition in which adult development is retarded and infantile characteristics are retained.|The doctor diagnosed him with infantilism due to a hormonal imbalance. 医生诊断他患有由于荷尔蒙失调引起的幼稚病。|Synonyms: immaturity, retardation, underdevelopment|Antonyms: maturity, development|noun
