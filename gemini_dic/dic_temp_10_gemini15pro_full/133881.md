nongregariously:/ˌnɒnɡrɪˈɡɛəriəsli/ | adv. | 非群居地，不爱交际地 |  Not in a gregarious manner; unsociably.  Describes behavior that is not sociable or enjoyed in groups. | He worked nongregariously, preferring the solitude of his office. 他不喜欢群居，喜欢在办公室里独处。| The artist nongregariously pursued his craft, rarely interacting with other painters. 这位艺术家不爱交际地追求他的艺术，很少与其他画家互动。 | 近义词：unsociably, solitarily, aloofly | 反义词: gregariously, sociably | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): Latin, meaning "not"
gregarious (adjective): from Latin "gregarius" meaning "belonging to a flock or herd"
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs
