horrifications:/ˌhɒrɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/ |/ˌhɔːrɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n. |恐怖事件；骇人听闻的事情| something that causes horror; a shocking or terrifying act or event |The horrifications of war are etched in the memories of survivors. 战争的恐怖深深地印在幸存者的记忆中。|  The museum's exhibit on medieval torture devices detailed various horrifications.  博物馆关于中世纪刑具的展览详细描述了各种恐怖事件。|近义词：atrocities, horrors, terrors|反义词: delights, pleasures|词性:名词

词源:  From horrific + -ation.  Ultimately from Latin *horrificāre, from horrificus (“causing horror”), from horrēre (“to bristle, shudder, tremble”) + -ficus.
