homeward:/ˈhoʊmwərd/ (US) /ˈhəʊmwəd/ (UK)| adj. adv.| homeward(adj): 向家的，回家的; homeward(adv): 向家地，回家地|Going in the direction of home.  Relating to a return home.|例句：We started our homeward journey. 我们开始了回家的旅程。|近义词：homebound, returning|反义词：outward|词性：adjective, adverb

homeward:/ˈhoʊmwərd/ (US) /ˈhəʊmwəd/ (UK)| adv.| 向家地，回家地|Towards home.|例句：They walked homeward through the fields. 他们穿过田野回家。|近义词：home, back|反义词：away|词性：adverb
