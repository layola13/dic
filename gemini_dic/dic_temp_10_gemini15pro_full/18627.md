bentwood:/ˈbɛntˌwʊd/| n.|曲木；曲木家具|Wood that has been steamed and bent into a curved shape, typically used for furniture.| The cafe had elegant bentwood chairs. 这家咖啡馆有优雅的曲木椅子。|近义词: laminated wood, molded wood|反义词: solid wood|noun


bentwood:/ˈbɛntˌwʊd/| adj.|曲木制的|Made of bentwood.|She inherited a beautiful bentwood rocking chair. 她继承了一把漂亮的曲木摇椅。|近义词: curved wood, molded wood|反义词: straight wood|adjective
