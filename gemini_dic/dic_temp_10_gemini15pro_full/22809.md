bosomed:/ˈbʊzəmd/ | adj. |  胸怀的，藏于胸间的，被...环抱的 |  Having a bosom (chest) of a specified kind, or embraced as if by a bosom. Often used poetically or figuratively to describe something nestled or enclosed.  Derived from "bosom" + "-ed." | The village is bosomed amid green hills.  这村庄坐落在青山之中。|  The ship lay bosomed in the quiet harbor.  船停泊在宁静的港湾里。 | nestled, embraced, enclosed, sheltered | exposed, bare | adjective
