ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *finishen, *finischen, from Anglo-Norman fenir, finir, from Old French fenir, finir (“to finish, end, conclude, die”), from Latin fīnīre (“to limit, bound, put an end to, complete”), from fīnis (“boundary, limit, end”).
