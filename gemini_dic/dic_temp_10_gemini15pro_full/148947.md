overwrought: /ˌoʊvərˈrɔːt/ | adj. | 过度劳累的，过度紧张的，（作品风格）过分雕琢的 | In a state of nervous excitement or anxiety; deeply agitated especially from emotion |  She was overwrought after the long and difficult journey.  她经过漫长而艰难的旅程后疲惫不堪。 |  The novel's overwrought plot left me confused. 这部小说的情节过于雕琢，让我感到困惑。 | 近义词：tense, stressed, anxious, agitated, hysterical, overdone | 反义词：calm, relaxed, composed, simple | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  From *over-+ *wrought*, past participle of *work*.  Originally meant "worked all over," then "elaborately decorated," and finally "agitated, excited."
