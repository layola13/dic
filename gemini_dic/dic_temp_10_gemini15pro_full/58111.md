dropout: /ˈdrɑːpaʊt/ (US) /ˈdrɒpaʊt/ (UK)| n.|辍学者；退学；中途退出|A person who has abandoned a course of study or activity before completion; the act of abandoning a course of study or activity.|The high dropout rate is a serious concern for educators. 高辍学率是教育工作者严重关心的问题。|synonyms: withdrawal, attrition|antonyms:  continuation, persistence|noun

dropout: /ˈdrɑːpaʊt/ (US) /ˈdrɒpaʊt/ (UK)| n.| (在神经网络中) 随机失活|A technique used in machine learning, especially in neural networks, to prevent overfitting by randomly ignoring some nodes during training.|Dropout helps the network to generalize better.  随机失活帮助网络更好地泛化。|synonyms: regularization (in machine learning context)|antonyms: N/A|noun
