hickish:/ˈhɪkɪʃ/| adj.|土气的，乡巴佬的|Characteristic of a hick; unsophisticated, rustic, or countrified.|例句：His hickish accent made it clear he wasn't from the city. 他土气的口音表明他不是城里人。|例句：She found his hickish manners charming. 她觉得他土里土气的举止很迷人。|近义词：rustic, unsophisticated, countrified, provincial, backwoods|反义词：sophisticated, urbane, cosmopolitan|词性:形容词
词源: From hick + -ish. Hick (meaning "an unsophisticated country person") is of uncertain origin, possibly a shortened form of Richard.
