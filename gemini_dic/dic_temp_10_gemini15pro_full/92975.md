idiocracy:/ˌɪdiˈɒkrəsi/ | n. | 愚蠢社会；反智社会 | A society in which intelligence and critical thinking are devalued, leading to a decline in culture and civilization. |  The movie "Idiocracy" portrays a future where society has become dumbed down due to anti-intellectualism.  这部电影《蠢蛋进化论》描绘了一个未来社会，由于反智主义，社会变得愚蠢。| Idiocy, stupidity, foolishness | Intelligence, wisdom, enlightenment | noun

Etymology:
From idiocy + -cracy, modeled after words like aristocracy and democracy.  "Idiocy" comes from the Greek "idiotes," meaning a private person, layman, ignorant person.
