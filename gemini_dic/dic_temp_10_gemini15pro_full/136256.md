nonrepellence:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈpɛləns/ | n. | 非驱避性，无驱避性 | The nonrepellence of the insecticide allows it to be effective against a wider range of pests, as they do not avoid contact with it.  这种杀虫剂的非驱避性使其能够有效地对抗更广泛的害虫，因为它们不会避免与之接触。 | 例句：The nonrepellence of the new formula is its key selling point. 新配方的非驱避性是其主要卖点。 | 近义词: nondeterrence | 反义词: repellence | 词性: 名词
