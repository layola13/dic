mesocuneiform:/ˌmezoʊˈkjuːnɪfɔːrm/ | adj. n.| 中楔形的；中楔形文字 |Relating to or denoting a simplified form of cuneiform writing, intermediate between the early complex forms and later simpler forms, used in Assyria and Babylonia (especially for writing Akkadian). It is characterized by fewer wedges and more linear strokes than the earlier forms.  Also, a writing made in this form. | The inscription was written in mesocuneiform script. 这段铭文是用中楔形文字书写的。| The clay tablet contained a mesocuneiform legal document. 这块泥板上有一份用中楔形文字书写的法律文件。 | 近义词: cuneiform | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "alphabetic" in a broad sense) | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology:
meso- + cuneiform

* meso-: from Greek *mesos* "middle"
* cuneiform: from Latin *cuneus* "wedge" + *-formis* "having the form of"
