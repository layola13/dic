boohooing:/ˌbuːˈhuːɪŋ/
| n. | 哭诉，假哭 |The constant boohooing from the child was starting to annoy the other passengers. 孩子不停的哭诉开始惹恼了其他乘客。|  The act of crying loudly and dramatically, often in a way that is not sincere. |  She was boohooing about how unfair her life was. 她在大声哭诉生活多么不公平。 |  whining, wailing, sobbing, crying, lamenting |  laughing, rejoicing | noun

boohoo:/ˌbuːˈhuː/
| int. | 呜呜（哭泣声）; vi. | 哭诉，假哭 |"Boohoo," she cried, pretending to be upset. “呜呜，”她假装很伤心哭了起来。| Used to represent the sound of someone crying, or to express mock sympathy.  | He boohooed about losing his phone, but I knew he didn't really care. 他哭诉着丢了手机，但我知道他并不真的在意。|  whine, wail, sob, cry, lament |  laugh, rejoice | interjection, verb
