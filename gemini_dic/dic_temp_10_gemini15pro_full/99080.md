interbasin:/ˌɪntərˈbeɪsɪn/| adj.|流域间的，盆地间的|Existing or occurring between basins, especially drainage basins.|例句：Interbasin water transfer projects can have significant environmental impacts.  流域间调水工程会对环境造成重大影响。

例句：Interbasin cooperation is essential for managing shared water resources.  流域间合作对于管理共享水资源至关重要。|近义词：transbasin|反义词:intrabasin|词性:形容词

词源:
inter- (prefix): between, among (from Latin)
basin (noun): a natural depression in the earth's surface, typically containing water (from Old French *bacin)
