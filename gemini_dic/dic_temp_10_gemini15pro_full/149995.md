palmipedes:/ˌpælˈmɪpɪˌdiːz/
| n. |蹼足鸟；游禽|Web-footed birds; an outdated taxonomic group that encompassed birds with webbed feet, such as ducks, geese, and swans. This term is no longer scientifically valid.|例句：Palmipedes were once classified together, but modern taxonomy has refined this grouping.|近义词：waterfowl, aquatic birds|反义词:land birds|词性:noun

palmiped:/ˈpæl.mɪ.ped/
| n. |蹼足鸟；游禽|A web-footed bird; a single bird belonging to the outdated group of palmipedes.|例句：The duck, a palmiped, paddled effortlessly across the pond.|近义词：waterfowl, aquatic bird|反义词:land bird|词性:noun
