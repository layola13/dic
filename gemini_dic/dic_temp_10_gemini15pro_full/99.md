abacinate: /ˈæbəsɪneɪt/
verb
使失明；使眼花缭乱
to blind or dazzle by exposing to a bright light
The interrogators attempted to abacinate the prisoner with a powerful spotlight. 审讯者试图用强烈的聚光灯使囚犯失明。
The sudden flash of lightning abacinated me momentarily. 突然的闪电使我暂时眼花缭乱。
synonym: blind, dazzle, bedazzle
antonym: enlighten, illuminate
词性: 动词(verb)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *abacinare, from Latin bacīnus (“basin, bowl”).  Originally referred to blinding someone by holding a red-hot metal basin close to their eyes.
