footboy:/ˈfʊtbɔɪ/| n.|侍童，男仆|A young male servant, especially one who attends a person of rank or runs errands.|The footboy opened the carriage door for the lady.  侍童为女士打开了马车门。|Synonyms: page, servant, lackey|Antonyms: master, lord|noun
