contempt:/kənˈtɛmpt/ /kənˈtɛmpt/ | n. |轻蔑，蔑视；耻辱；[律]藐视法庭（或国会） |The feeling that someone or something is worthless or beneath consideration. |She looked at him with contempt. 她轻蔑地看着他。| synonyms: disdain, scorn, disrespect, disregard | antonyms: respect, admiration, reverence | noun

contemptuous: /kənˈtɛmptjʊəs/ /kənˈtɛmptjʊəs/ | adj. |轻蔑的，鄙视的 | Showing contempt; scornful. |He gave a contemptuous laugh. 他轻蔑地笑了笑。| synonyms: disdainful, scornful, disrespectful, insulting | antonyms: respectful, admiring, reverent | adjective
