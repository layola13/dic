hygeists: /ˈhaɪdʒiɪsts/
noun
卫生学家；保健专家
Experts in hygiene and public health.
Hygeists play a crucial role in preventing the spread of diseases. 卫生学家在预防疾病传播方面发挥着至关重要的作用。
The hygeists inspected the restaurant's kitchen for cleanliness. 卫生学家检查了餐厅厨房的清洁度。
Synonyms: sanitarians, public health officials
Antonyms: N/A
