bore-out:/ˈbɔːr aʊt/ | n. | 职业倦怠；无聊透顶 |A state of emotional, physical, and mental exhaustion caused by prolonged and excessive boredom at work.  It's often characterized by a lack of stimulation, challenge, and meaning in one's job. |  Many employees experience bore-out when their jobs become repetitive and unchallenging. 许多员工在工作变得重复且缺乏挑战性时会经历职业倦怠。

She suffered from bore-out and eventually quit her job. 她饱受职业倦怠之苦，最终辞掉了工作。 | 近义词：ennui, tedium, burnout, under-stimulation | 反义词: engagement, excitement, fulfillment | 词性: noun


bore-out:/ˈbɔːr aʊt/ | v. | 使…极其厌烦 | To cause someone to become extremely bored, especially through work that is repetitive and unchallenging. | The monotonous tasks bored him out. 单调的任务使他极其厌烦。

The repetitive nature of the assembly line work bored the employees out. 装配线工作的重复性使员工感到极其厌烦。 | 近义词：tire, exhaust, wear out | 反义词: excite, stimulate, engage | 词性: verb
