comfortlessly:/ˈkʌmfətləsli/
adv.
不舒服地，不自在的
Without comfort; in a way that provides no comfort.
She sat comfortlessly on the hard wooden chair. 她不舒服地坐在硬木椅子上。
近义词:uncomfortably, miserably, wretchedly
反义词:comfortably, cozily, snugly
词性:副词

Etymology:
From comfortless + -ly.  Comfortless from comfort + -less. Comfort from Old French confort, from Latin confortāre "to strengthen much," from com- "together" + fort- "strong."
