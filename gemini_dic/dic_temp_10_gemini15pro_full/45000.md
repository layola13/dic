crystallographically:/ˌkrɪstələˈɡræfɪkli/| adv.| 晶体学地，从晶体学的角度|Relating to the study of crystals and their structure. The atoms are arranged crystallographically in a repeating pattern. 这些原子以重复的模式晶体学排列。|Synonym: crystal-wise|Antonym: amorphously|词性:副词
