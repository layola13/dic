filigreed:/ˈfɪlɪɡrid/ | adj. | 镂空的，金银丝细工的 | Decorated with delicate and intricate ornamentation, like filigree work. |  The antique locket was filigreed with tiny gold flowers. 这枚古董小盒子上用细小的金花做成了金银丝细工。 | 近义词: ornate, delicate, intricate | 反义词: plain, simple | 词性: 形容词

filigree:/ˈfɪlɪɡriː/ | n. | 金银丝细工 | Delicate and intricate ornamental work made of fine gold or silver wire. | The craftsman specialized in creating intricate filigree jewelry.  这位工匠专门制作复杂的金银丝细工珠宝。| 近义词: ornamentation, tracery | 反义词: plainness, simplicity | 词性: 名词

词源: From Italian *filigrana* "filigree," from *filo* "thread" (from Latin *filum*) + *grano* "grain" (from Latin *granum*).
