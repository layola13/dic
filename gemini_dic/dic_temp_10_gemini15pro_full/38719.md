condite:/ˈkɒndaɪt/ /kənˈdaɪt/ | v. |腌制，保存；调味 |To preserve or season food, typically by pickling, salting, or spicing. |  The chef condited the meat with a mixture of herbs and spices.  厨师用香草和香料的混合物腌制了肉。

 The olives were condited in brine for several months. 橄榄在盐水中腌制了几个月。 | 近义词：preserve, pickle, cure, season | 反义词: spoil, decay | 词性: verb

词源: From Latin *conditus*, past participle of *condīre*  "to put together, preserve, pickle, season," from *com-* "together" + *-dīre*, related to *dō* "I give."
