cycas: /ˈsaɪkæs/ | n. |苏铁|A genus of palmlike gymnospermous plants (family Cycadaceae), including the sago palm and other species.  Cycads are woody, seed-bearing plants with fern-like or palm-like leaves. They are considered living fossils, having existed since prehistoric times.|The cycas in the botanical garden was particularly impressive. 植物园里的苏铁尤其引人注目。|Synonyms: sago palm, fern palm|Antonyms: N/A|noun
