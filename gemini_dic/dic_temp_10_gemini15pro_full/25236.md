buds:/bʌdz/
n.
花蕾，芽；萌芽；蓓蕾；未成熟的事物
A small swelling on a plant stem from which a shoot, leaf, or flower develops.  A person or thing that is not yet fully developed.
The rose buds are starting to open. 玫瑰花蕾开始绽放。
He’s one of the best buds in the industry. 他是这个行业里最有前途的新人之一。
synonyms: sprout, shoot, germen,  embryo
antonyms: flower, fruit, decay, decline
noun

buds:/bʌdz/
n.
朋友，伙伴（非正式）
(informal) a friend
He's one of my best buds. 他是我最好的朋友之一。
We've been buds since high school. 我们从高中起就是好朋友了。
synonyms: pal, mate, chum, friend, buddy
antonyms: enemy, foe, rival, opponent
noun
