immailed:/ɪmˈeɪld/ | v. | 已通过电子邮件发送 | sent by email.  This verb is formed by adding the prefix "im-" (meaning "in" or "into") to "mailed" (past participle of "mail"). While "immailed" is grammatically correct, it is not commonly used. "Emailed" is the preferred and more widely accepted term. | I immailed the documents yesterday. (我昨天通过电子邮件发送了文件。) |  I immailed him the contract. (我通过电子邮件把合同发给他了。) | Synonyms: emailed, sent electronically | Antonyms: received by mail, sent physically | verb
