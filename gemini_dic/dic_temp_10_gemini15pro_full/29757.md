catrina:/kɑːˈtriːnə/ | n. | 卡特里娜，墨西哥亡灵节的女性骷髅形象 | A Catrina is a skeletal figure, often depicted as a female, which is commonly associated with the Mexican holiday Día de Muertos (Day of the Dead). |  Example: The Catrina she painted was adorned with colorful flowers and elegant lace. 她画的卡特里娜骷髅装饰着五彩缤纷的花朵和优雅的蕾丝。

Example: Catrinas are a popular symbol of Dia de los Muertos. 卡特里娜骷髅是亡灵节的流行象征。 | 近义词: skeleton (in a broader sense), calaca | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From the Mexican Spanish diminutive of *catrín* "dandy," "elegant man." Originally, the Catrina was a satirical portrayal of upper-class Mexicans during the Porfiriato era.
