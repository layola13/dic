bacterium:/bækˈtɪrɪəm/ | n. | 细菌 | A single-celled microscopic organism, some of which are pathogenic. |  Example 1: The bacterium E. coli can cause food poisoning.  大肠杆菌会导致食物中毒。

Example 2:  Beneficial bacteria are essential for healthy digestion. 有益细菌对健康消化至关重要。 |  Synonyms: microbe, germ | Antonym: None (not applicable for this biological term) | Noun
