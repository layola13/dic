obtruder:/əbˈtruːdər/| n. |侵入者，打扰者|Someone who obtrudes, or forces themselves, on others; an intruder.|She felt like an obtruder, walking into the party uninvited. 她觉得自己像个不速之客，未经邀请就闯入了派对。|Synonyms: intruder, trespasser, encroacher, interferer|Antonyms: guest, invitee|Noun
