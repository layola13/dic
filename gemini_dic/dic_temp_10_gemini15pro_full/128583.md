nicaraguas: /ˌnɪkəˈrɑːɡwəz/ | n. | 尼加拉瓜（指尼加拉瓜共和国，可用于单数或复数形式，但复数形式更常见于指尼加拉瓜人民或某些特定语境） | Nicaragua, officially the Republic of Nicaragua, is the largest country in Central America. It is bordered by Honduras to the northwest, the Caribbean to the east, Costa Rica to the south, and the Pacific Ocean to the west. The multi-ethnic population of six million includes indigenous peoples, Europeans, Africans, and Asians.  |  Nicaragua is known for its beautiful lakes and volcanoes. 尼加拉瓜以其美丽的湖泊和火山而闻名。| Nicaraguans are known for their hospitality. 尼加拉瓜人以热情好客而闻名。 | synonym: Republic of Nicaragua | antonym: N/A | noun
