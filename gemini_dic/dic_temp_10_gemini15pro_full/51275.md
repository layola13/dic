desynchronize:/diːˈsɪŋkrənaɪz/| v. |使不同步，去同步|To cause to lose synchronization; to become out of step or phase.|The server desynchronized from the network, causing data loss. 服务器与网络不同步，导致数据丢失。|近义词：mismatch, disalign|反义词：synchronize, align|词性:动词


词源:  From de- + synchronize + -ation.  "De-" is a prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse," "synchronize" means "to occur at the same time," and "-ation" is a suffix forming nouns of action or process.
