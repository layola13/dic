insightfully:/ˈɪnsaɪtfʊli/| adv.|深刻地；富有洞察力地|With deep understanding and perception. Showing clear and deep understanding of a complex situation or problem.|She analyzed the situation insightfully and offered a brilliant solution. 她深刻地分析了局势，并提出了一个绝妙的解决方案。|近义词：perceptively, astutely, discerningly, wisely|反义词：superficially, shallowly, obtusely, thoughtlessly|词性:副词
词源: From "insightful" + "-ly."  "Insightful" comes from "insight" (from "in-" + "sight"), meaning the ability to see and understand clearly, plus "-ful" meaning "full of."
