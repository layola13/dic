begonias:/bɪˈɡoʊnjəz/
n.
秋海棠（复数）
A popular garden and houseplant with colorful flowers and often asymmetrical leaves.  The genus Begonia contains over 2,000 species.
Begonias thrive in shady areas. 秋海棠在阴凉处生长茂盛。
Begonias are popular for their vibrant colors. 秋海棠因其鲜艳的色彩而受欢迎。
近义词: wax plant, fibrous begonia, tuberous begonia
反义词: (No direct antonym for a plant. Perhaps contrasting plant based on sunlight needs) sun-loving plants
词性: noun (plural)


begonia:/bɪˈɡoʊnjə/
n.
秋海棠（单数）
A plant of the genus Begonia, popular as a garden or houseplant.
This begonia has beautiful pink flowers. 这株秋海棠开着美丽的粉红色花朵。
The begonia is a relatively easy plant to care for. 秋海棠是一种相对容易照顾的植物。
近义词: wax plant, fibrous begonia, tuberous begonia
反义词: (No direct antonym for a plant. Perhaps contrasting plant based on sunlight needs) sun-loving plants
词性: noun (singular)


词源:
Named after Michel Bégon (1638-1710), a French governor of Santo Domingo and patron of botany.
