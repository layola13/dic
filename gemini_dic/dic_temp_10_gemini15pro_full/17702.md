bedaub:/bɪˈdɔːb/| vt.|涂抹，玷污|to smear or cover with something sticky or messy|The child bedaubed the wall with paint.  孩子把墙上涂满了油漆。|近义词：smear, daub, smudge, besmirch|反义词:clean, cleanse|verb

bedaub:/bɪˈdɔːb/| vt.|玷污（名声）|to damage the reputation of|His reputation was bedaubed by the scandal. 他的名声被丑闻玷污了。|近义词：tarnish, sully, besmirch|反义词:exalt, enhance|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + daub.  "Be-" is a prefix meaning "thoroughly," and "daub" means to smear or coat with a soft adhesive substance.
