obrok:/ˈɔbrɔk/| n.|餐食，定量口粮|A fixed amount of food, especially for soldiers or sailors, or for animals.|The soldiers received their daily obrok of bread and water. 士兵们领到了每日的面包和水口粮。|Synonyms: ration, allowance, portion|Antonyms: feast, abundance|noun
