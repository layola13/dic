dubber:/ˈdʌbər/| n.|配音员；译制片配音演员；复制者|A person who dubs films or other visual media; someone who makes copies.|The dubber's voice didn't quite match the actor's lip movements. 配音演员的声音与演员的唇部动作不太匹配。

The dubber was praised for his excellent performance. 这位配音演员因其出色的表演而受到称赞。|近义词：voice actor, voice artist, voice-over artist|反义词:|n.
