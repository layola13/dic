hyperprolactinaemia:/ˌhaɪpərˌproʊlæktɪˈniːmiə/| n.|高泌乳素血症|A condition characterized by abnormally high levels of prolactin in the blood.|Hyperprolactinaemia can cause infertility in both men and women. 高泌乳素血症会导致男性和女性不育。|Synonyms: hyperprolactinemia (American spelling)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
