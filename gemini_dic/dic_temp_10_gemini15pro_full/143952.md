outquench: /aʊtˈkwɛntʃ/
verb
熄灭，压制，彻底扑灭
To extinguish completely; to quench more thoroughly than quench alone.
The firefighters worked tirelessly to outquench the raging inferno. 消防队员不知疲倦地工作，彻底扑灭了熊熊大火。
His deep sorrow seemed to outquench all other emotions. 他深深的悲伤似乎压倒了所有其他情绪。
近义词: extinguish, quench, stifle, suppress
反义词: ignite, kindle, inflame
词性: verb

词源: out- + quench.  "Out-" acts as an intensifier, meaning completely or thoroughly.  "Quench" comes from Old English *cwencan, from Proto-Germanic *kwenkjanan, meaning "to extinguish."
