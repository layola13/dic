entrench:/ɪnˈtrɛntʃ/ /ɛnˈtrɛntʃ/| v. | 巩固；使牢固；确立；根深蒂固；挖壕沟；用壕沟围住 | To establish something firmly so that it is very difficult to change.  To dig trenches for defense. | The government's policies are now deeply entrenched. 政府的政策现在根深蒂固。 The soldiers entrenched themselves behind the hill. 士兵们在山后挖壕沟防守。 | 近义词：establish, solidify, fortify, reinforce, embed | 反义词：weaken, undermine, dislodge | 词性: 动词
