comparative:/kəmˈpærətɪv/ (US), /kəmˈpærətɪv/ (UK)| adj. n.|比较的；相对的；比较级|Relatively, by comparison;  the form of an adjective or adverb that expresses a higher degree of a quality, but not the highest possible.|Her house is comparative to a palace. 她的房子跟宫殿比起来差不多。|synonyms: relative, comparable, analogous|antonyms: absolute, incomparable|adjective

comparative:/kəmˈpærətɪv/ (US), /kəmˈpærətɪv/ (UK)| n.|比较级|The comparative form of an adjective or adverb.|The comparative of "good" is "better".  “good”的比较级是“better”。|synonyms: comparative degree|antonyms: positive degree, superlative degree|noun
