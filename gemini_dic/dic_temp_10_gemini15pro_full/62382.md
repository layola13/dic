endocardial:/ˌɛndəˈkɑːrdiəl/| adj.|心内膜的|Relating to the endocardium, the thin, smooth membrane that lines the inside of the heart chambers.|Endocardial inflammation can be a serious condition. 心内膜炎可能是一种严重的疾病。|The doctor diagnosed endocardial fibrosis. 医生诊断出心内膜纤维化。|近义词:intracardiac|反义词:epicardial|词性:形容词
词源: endo- (within) + -cardi- (heart) + -al (adjectival suffix)
