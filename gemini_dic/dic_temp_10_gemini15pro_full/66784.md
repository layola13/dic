exoticist:/ɪɡˈzɑːtɪsɪst/ | n. |异国情调主义者；崇尚异国事物者|A person who is fascinated by foreign cultures, especially those perceived as unusual or different from their own, often romanticizing or stereotyping them. | She was criticized for her exoticist portrayal of the local tribes. 她因对当地部落的异国情调的描绘而受到批评。| Near Synonyms: orientalist, romanticist | Antonyms: nativist, localist | noun

exoticist:/ɪɡˈzɑːtɪsɪst/ | adj. |异国情调的；崇尚异国事物的 | Characterized by the fascination with foreign cultures, often romanticizing or stereotyping them. | Her exoticist tastes were reflected in her home decor. 她对异国情调的品味体现在她的家居装饰中。| Near Synonyms: exotic, foreign, unusual | Antonyms: native, local, indigenous | adjective
