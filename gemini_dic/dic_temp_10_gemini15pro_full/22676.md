borderings:/ˈbɔːrdərɪŋz/
n.
边界地区；毗邻地区；边缘地带
Areas that are along or adjacent to a border.
The borderings of the country are heavily guarded.  这个国家的边境地区戒备森严。
synonyms:  borders, edges, fringes, peripheries, margins
antonyms: center, interior, core
noun
