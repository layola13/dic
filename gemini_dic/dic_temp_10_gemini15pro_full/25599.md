ETYMOLOGY->
"Bump off" likely originated in American slang in the early 20th century. "Bump" itself can mean to knock or jolt, and "off" often implies removal or cessation.  The combination suggests a sudden and violent removal from life.  The exact origin is uncertain, but it's believed to be connected to underworld slang.
