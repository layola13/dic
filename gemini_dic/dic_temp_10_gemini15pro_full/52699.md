digraph:/ˈdaɪɡræf/| n.|双字母; 二合字母|A digraph is a combination of two letters representing one sound, such as 'sh' in 'ship' or 'ea' in 'head'.|The digraph 'ch' can be pronounced in two different ways.|近义词: ligature, diphthong (in phonetics)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|n.
