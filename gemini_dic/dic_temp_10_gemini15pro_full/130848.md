nonapproachabilities:/ˌnɒnəˌproʊtʃəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 不可接近性；难以接近；|The nonapproachabilities of the remote mountain peaks made them a challenge for even the most experienced climbers.  遥远山峰的难以接近性，即使对最有经验的登山者来说也是一种挑战。| The queen's nonapproachabilities made her seem distant and aloof. 女王的难以接近让她显得疏远和冷漠。 | 近义词：inaccessibility, unapproachability, remoteness | 反义词：accessibility, approachability, nearness | 词性: 名词

词源:  From non- + approachable + -ity + -ies.  "Non-" 表示否定，"approachable" 表示可接近的，"-ity" 名词后缀，"-ies" 复数形式。
