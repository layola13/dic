detainment:/dɪˈteɪnmənt/
n.
拘留；扣留；阻止；延误
the act of officially stopping someone from leaving a place
例句：
His detainment was unlawful. 他的拘留是非法的。

The police subjected him to hours of detainment without charge. 警方未经指控就将他拘留了数小时。

近义词：
detention, custody, confinement, imprisonment, arrest
反义词：
release, liberation, freedom

词性: 名词

词源: From detain + -ment, from Old French detenir, from Latin detinere "to hold back, detain."
