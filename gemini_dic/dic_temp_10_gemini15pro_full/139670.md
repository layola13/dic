occupy: /ˈɒkjupaɪ/ (UK) /ˈɑːkjupaɪ/ (US)| v. | 占据，占用；居住；使忙碌 | to fill or use a space, an area, or a position; to live in a building; to fill or use a period of time | The building is occupied by a large company. 这栋大楼被一家大公司占据。|近义词：inhabit, reside, fill, use, engage |反义词: vacate, abandon |词性:动词

occupy: /ˈɒkjupaɪ/ (UK) /ˈɑːkjupaɪ/ (US)| v. | 使忙碌 | to keep someone busy or interested |  Her time was fully occupied with work. 她的时间完全被工作占据了。|近义词：engage, absorb, preoccupy |反义词: idle |词性:动词
