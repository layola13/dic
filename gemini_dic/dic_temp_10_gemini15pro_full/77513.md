gazpacho:/ɡəˈspɑːtʃoʊ/ /ɡæˈspætʃoʊ/| n. |西班牙冷汤|A cold Spanish soup made with raw blended vegetables, especially tomatoes.|例句：We had a refreshing gazpacho for lunch on the patio. 我们在露台上享用了清爽的西班牙冷汤作为午餐。

例句：The gazpacho was a perfect starter on a hot summer day.  在炎热的夏日，西班牙冷汤是一道完美的开胃菜。|近义词：cold soup, chilled soup|反义词:hot soup|词性:noun
