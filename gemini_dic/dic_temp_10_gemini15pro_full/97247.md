inflexible:/ɪnˈflɛksəbl/| adj.|不灵活的，僵硬的，固执的|Not easily bent; rigid. Unwilling to change or compromise.|The company's inflexible policy regarding vacation time frustrated many employees.  这家公司关于休假时间的僵硬政策让很多员工感到沮丧。|近义词：rigid, stiff, unyielding, obstinate, stubborn|反义词：flexible, adaptable, pliable, compliant|词性:形容词
inflexibility:/ɪnˌflɛksəˈbɪləti/| n.|不灵活，僵硬，固执|The quality or state of being inflexible.|His inflexibility made it difficult to negotiate a compromise. 他的固执使得很难达成妥协。|近义词：rigidity, stiffness, obstinacy|反义词：flexibility, adaptability, pliability|词性:名词
