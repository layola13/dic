bundesrat:/ˈbʊndəsˌrɑːt/| n.|联邦参议院，联邦委员会|The Bundesrat represents the sixteen Länder (states) of Germany at the federal level.  联邦参议院在联邦一级代表德国的十六个州。|The Bundesrat approved the new legislation. 联邦参议院批准了新立法。|近义词: Senate, upper house|反义词: Bundestag (in the German context, representing the lower house)|noun
