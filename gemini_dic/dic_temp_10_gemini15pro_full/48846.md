deforestation:/ˌdiːˌfɔːrɪˈsteɪʃən/| n. |森林砍伐；滥伐森林|The act or process of clearing forests; the conversion of forested land to non-forest uses.  |例句：Deforestation is a major contributor to climate change. 森林砍伐是气候变化的主要原因。|近义词：logging, clear-cutting, forest clearance|反义词:afforestation, reforestation| Noun
