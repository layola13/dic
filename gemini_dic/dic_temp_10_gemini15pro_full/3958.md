agrope:/əˈɡroʊp/ | adj. | 惊恐的，惊呆的 |  Feeling stunned or bewildered; struck with terror and astonishment.  Derived from a combination of "aghast" and "grope" suggesting a state of shocked confusion. | He stood agrope, unable to comprehend the devastation before him. 他惊呆了，无法理解眼前的惨状。| The sudden darkness left her agrope and disoriented. 突如其来的黑暗让她惊恐万分，不知所措。 | Near synonyms: dumbfounded, bewildered, stunned, aghast, shocked. | Antonyms: composed, calm, unfazed, collected.
