electees:/ɪˌlɛkˈtiːz/
n.
当选者；被选中的人
People who have been elected to a position or office but have not yet officially begun serving their term.
The electees will be sworn in next month. 当选者将于下个月宣誓就职。
synonyms: winners, successful candidates
antonyms: losers, unsuccessful candidates
noun
