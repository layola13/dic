nonruling:/ˌnɒnˈruːlɪŋ/
adjective
非统治的，非执政的
Not ruling or governing; not in power.
The nonruling party criticized the government's handling of the economy.  非执政党批评了政府对经济的处理方式。
The nonruling members of the committee had little influence on the final decision.  委员会的非执政成员对最终决定几乎没有影响力。
Synonyms: non-governing, non-dominant
Antonyms: ruling, governing
词性: 形容词
