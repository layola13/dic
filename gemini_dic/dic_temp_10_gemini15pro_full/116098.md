martyrly:/ˈmɑːrtərlɪ/ | adv. |  殉道般地; 像烈士一样地 | In a manner resembling a martyr; with an air of suffering or sacrifice. | He bore his illness martyrly, never complaining. 他以殉道者的姿态忍受着病痛，从未抱怨过。|  She martyrly sacrificed her career for her family. 她像烈士般为了家庭牺牲了自己的事业。 | 近义词: resignedly, stoically, patiently | 反义词: rebelliously, defiantly | 词性: 副词

martyr:/ˈmɑːrtər/ | n. | 烈士；殉道者 |  A person who is killed because of their religious or other beliefs. |  He was a martyr for his faith. 他是为信仰而死的烈士。 | She became a martyr to the cause of women's rights. 她成为了争取妇女权利事业的殉道者。 | 近义词: victim, sufferer | 反义词: persecutor, oppressor | 词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English *martir, from Old French martir, from Late Latin martyr, from Ecclesiastical Greek μάρτυρ (mártyr), from Ancient Greek μάρτυς (mártys, “witness”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂mr̥-tús (“witness, one who remembers”).
