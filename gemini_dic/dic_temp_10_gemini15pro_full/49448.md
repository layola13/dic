delsarte:/dɛlˈsɑːrt/ | adj. n. | 德尔萨特式的；德尔萨特体系 | Relating to or characteristic of the system of dramatic expression developed by François Delsarte.  Also, the system itself. |  She took delsarte classes to improve her stage presence. 她学习德尔萨特课程来提升她的舞台表现力。 | The delsarte method emphasizes graceful movement and emotional expression. 德尔萨特体系强调优雅的动作和情感表达。 | 近义词: expressionistic, stylized | 反义词: natural, unstudied | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology:
Named after François Delsarte (1811-1871), a French musician and teacher who developed a system of dramatic expression.
