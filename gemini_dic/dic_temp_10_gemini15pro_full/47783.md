decanter:/dɪˈkæntər/ | n. |醒酒器;  倾析器;  雕花玻璃瓶|A decorative glass bottle, typically with a stopper, into which wine or other alcoholic drinks are decanted. |She poured the wine into a crystal decanter. 她把酒倒进了一个水晶醒酒器。| The port was decanted two hours before dinner.  晚餐前两小时就把波特酒倒进了醒酒器。 | synonyms: carafe, flagon, bottle | antonyms: none | noun

decanters:/dɪˈkæntərz/ | n. |醒酒器(复数) |Plural form of decanter. |  The decanters were gleaming on the sideboard.  醒酒器在餐具柜上闪闪发光。 | We need more decanters for the party.  派对需要更多醒酒器。 | synonyms: carafes, flagons, bottles | antonyms: none | noun
