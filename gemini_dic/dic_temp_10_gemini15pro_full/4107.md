aimlessly:/ˈeɪmlɪsli/| adv.|漫无目的地，无目的地|Without purpose or direction.  Wandering around without any specific goal in mind. |He wandered aimlessly through the streets. 他在街上漫无目的地游荡。|  She scrolled aimlessly through social media. 她在社交媒体上漫无目的地浏览。|Synonyms: purposelessly, randomly, haphazardly, |Antonyms: purposefully, deliberately, intentionally|adverb
