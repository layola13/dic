crosspatch:/ˈkrɒspætʃ/ (UK), /ˈkrɔːspætʃ/ (US)| n.|脾气暴躁的人，爱抱怨的人|A grumpy or irritable person, especially a child.|例句：He's a real crosspatch in the mornings before he's had his coffee. 他早上没喝咖啡之前脾气很暴躁。

例句：The little girl was being a crosspatch because she didn't get the toy she wanted. 小女孩因为没拿到想要的玩具而耍脾气。|近义词：grump, grouch, crank, curmudgeon|反义词：good-natured person, cheerful person|词性:名词
