outlearned:/ˌaʊtˈlɜːrnd/
verb
超越，通过学习胜过
To surpass someone in knowledge or skill through learning.
The student outlearned his teacher in computer programming.  这个学生在计算机编程方面超过了他的老师。
Synonyms: surpassed, exceeded, outstripped
Antonyms: lagged behind, fell short of
词性: 动词
