dozzled:/ˈdɒzəld/ (UK) /ˈdɑːzəld/ (US)| adj.| 眼花缭乱的，昏眩的|Confused and disoriented, especially by bright light or a sudden change.| The bright flash of the camera left me dozzled. 相机闪光灯的强光让我眼花缭乱。|Synonyms: dazed, bewildered, stunned, confused|Antonyms: clear-headed, alert, focused|adjective
