overharden:/ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrdn/ | v. | 过度硬化，硬化过度 | To harden excessively; to make too hard. | The blacksmith overhardened the steel, making it brittle. 铁匠把钢过度硬化了，使它变得很脆。 | The concrete was overhardened, causing it to crack. 混凝土过度硬化，导致其开裂。 | 近义词: overtemper | 反义词: soften | 词性: 动词
