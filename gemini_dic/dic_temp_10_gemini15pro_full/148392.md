oversqueamishnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈskwiːmɪʃnɪsɪz/
|n.| 过分拘谨, 过分洁癖, 过分神经质|Excessive fastidiousness or sensitivity, especially in matters of propriety or morality.|Her oversqueamishnesses about cleanliness made it difficult to share a living space with her. 她对清洁的过分挑剔使得很难和她共享一个生活空间。|Synonyms: prudishness, fastidiousness, squeamishness, daintiness|Antonyms: boldness, brashness, insensitivity|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + squeamish + -ness.  "Over-" intensifies "squeamish," meaning easily nauseated or disgusted.  "-ness" turns the adjective into a noun, indicating the state or quality of being oversqueamish.
