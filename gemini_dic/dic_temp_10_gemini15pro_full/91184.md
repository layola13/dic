hydrated:/ˈhaɪdreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 水合的，含水的，水化的 | combined with water, especially chemically. | The athletes were advised to stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water. 运动员被建议多喝水以保持水分。 |  The soil is well hydrated after the rain. 雨后土壤水分充足。 | 近义词：moist, damp, hydrous | 反义词：dehydrated, dry, anhydrous | 词性：形容词

词源: From hydrate + -ed.  Ultimately from Greek *hydor* "water".
