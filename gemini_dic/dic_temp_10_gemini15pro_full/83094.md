gusset:/ˈɡʌsɪt/| n. |三角形衬布；角撑板；腋下三角形插布|A piece of fabric inserted into a garment to strengthen or enlarge it, often at the underarm, crotch, or elbow.|The gusset in the armpit of the shirt allows for greater range of motion. 衬衫腋下的三角形衬布可以增大活动范围。

The gusset in these trousers provides extra comfort.  这条裤子的角撑板提供了额外的舒适感。|近义词：insert, reinforcement, patch|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, perhaps "seam" in some contexts, but it's not a true opposite)|noun
