noneligibility:/ˌnɒnˌɛlɪdʒəˈbɪləti/ | n. |不合格，无资格|The state of not being eligible for something.  The applicant's noneligibility for the loan was due to his poor credit history.|例句：His noneligibility for the scholarship was a major disappointment. 他没有资格获得奖学金，这让他非常失望。

例句：The committee confirmed the noneligibility of the candidate due to a missed deadline. 委员会确认该候选人因错过截止日期而不符合资格。|近义词：ineligibility, disqualification|反义词: eligibility, qualification|词性:名词
