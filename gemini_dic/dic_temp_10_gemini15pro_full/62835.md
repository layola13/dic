enigmatist: /ˌɛnɪɡˈmætɪst/ | n. |谜语制造者；出谜人；神秘莫测的人|A person who makes or poses riddles; a person of puzzling or contradictory character. |例句：The enigmatist challenged the audience to solve his latest creation. 出谜人挑战观众解开他最新的谜语。

例句：He was an enigmatist, his motives always shrouded in secrecy. 他是一个神秘莫测的人，他的动机总是笼罩在秘密之中。|近义词：riddler, puzzler, sphinx |反义词:straightforward person, open book |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French énigmatiste, from énigme ("enigma") + -iste, from Latin aenigma + -ista.
