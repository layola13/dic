accountantship: /əˈkaʊntənʃɪp/
n.
会计学；会计职业
The theory and practice of accounting.  The profession of an accountant.
She studied accountancy at university. 她在大学学习会计。
He has a degree in accountantship. 他拥有会计学学位。
近义词: accounting, book-keeping
反义词: (无直接反义词)
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From accountant + -ship.  Accountant comes from Old French acomptant, present participle of acompter "to count, render an account," from a- "to" + compter "to count."  The suffix -ship denotes "state, condition, office, skill."
