cross-multiplication:/ˌkrɒs mʌltɪplɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 交叉相乘 | The process of multiplying the numerator of one fraction by the denominator of another fraction, and vice versa, often used to solve proportions or compare fractions. | Example: We used cross-multiplication to solve the proportion. 我们用交叉相乘来解比例。| Example: Cross-multiplication is a useful technique in algebra. 交叉相乘是代数中一个有用的技巧。| Synonyms: cross-product | Antonyms: N/A | noun
