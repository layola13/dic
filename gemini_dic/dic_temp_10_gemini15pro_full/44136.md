cristate:/ˈkrɪsteɪt/| adj.|鸡冠状的，羽冠的|Having a crest or tuft, especially on the head.|The bird has a bright red cristate head. 这只鸟有一个鲜红色的羽冠头。|近义词：crested, tufted|反义词:smooth, unadorned|形容词

词源:
From Latin *cristātus*, from crista (“crest, plume”).
