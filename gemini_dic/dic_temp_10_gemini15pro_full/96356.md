indirection:/ˌɪndɪˈrɛkʃən/ | n. | 间接，迂回 | The act of accomplishing something in a roundabout or indirect manner; lack of straightforwardness.  |He achieved his goal through indirection, using a third party to negotiate the deal. 他通过间接的方式实现了目标，用第三方来协商这笔交易。 | synonyms: circumvention, roundabout way, subterfuge | antonyms: directness, straightforwardness | noun

indirection:/ˌɪndɪˈrɛkʃən/ | n. | 间接寻址 | In computer science, a method of accessing data or a resource through a pointer or reference, rather than directly. |  Indirection allows for more flexible memory management. 间接寻址允许更灵活的内存管理。 | synonyms: referencing, pointing | antonyms: direct addressing | noun
