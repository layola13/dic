narcotine:/ˈnɑːrkətiːn/ | n. | 麻黄碱；那可汀 | An alkaloid obtained from opium, formerly used to treat coughs. It is not as strong as morphine and is not addictive. | 例句：Narcotine is an antitussive alkaloid found in opium. 麻黄碱是在鸦片中发现的一种镇咳生物碱。

例句：Narcotine was once used in cough syrups. 麻黄碱曾经被用于止咳糖浆中。 | 近义词：noscapine | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From French *narcotine*, from Greek *narkōtikos* "benumbing," from *narkoun* "to benumb".
