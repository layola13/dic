lignous:/ˈlɪɡnəs/| adj.|木质的，木本的|Resembling wood; woody.  Made of or containing wood. |例句：The lignous tissue of the tree provided strong support.  这棵树的木质组织提供了强有力的支撑。

例句：Lignous fossils can reveal much about past climates. 木质化石可以揭示很多关于过去气候的信息。|近义词：woody, ligneous, xyloid|反义词：herbaceous, soft|词性: 形容词

词源:
From Latin *lignosus*, from lignum (“wood”).
