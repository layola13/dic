airbrain:/ˈɛrbreɪn/| n.| 空想家；不切实际的人 |A person who has impractical or unrealistic ideas.|He's such an airbrain, always coming up with these crazy schemes that never work. 他真是个空想家，总是想出这些永远行不通的疯狂计划。|近义词：dreamer, idealist, visionary, fantasist|反义词：realist, pragmatist|词性:名词

词源:  A combination of "air" (suggesting something unsubstantial or insubstantial) and "brain" (referring to the mind or intellect).  The word implies that the person's ideas are "airy" or lacking in substance.
