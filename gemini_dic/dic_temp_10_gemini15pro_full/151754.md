paschals: /ˈpæskəlz/ | adj. n.| 逾越节的；逾越节糕点 | Relating to Passover or Easter; Paschal candles, Paschal lamb, Paschal eggs. |  The paschal lamb is a traditional symbol of Passover. 逾越节羔羊是逾越节的传统象征。 |  The bakery makes special paschal breads for Easter. 这家面包店为复活节制作特别的逾越节面包。| Synonyms: Easter, Passover | Antonyms: N/A | adjective, noun


Etymology:
From Late Latin *paschalis, from Latin pascha ("Passover"), from Greek πάσχα (páskha), from Aramaic פַּסְחָא (pasḥā), from Hebrew פֶּסַח (pessaḥ). Ultimately from a verb meaning "to hop over, to pass over."
