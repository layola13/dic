festoons:/ˈfestuːnz/
n.
花彩；花环；垂花饰
A chain or garland of flowers, leaves, ribbons, etc., hung in a curve as a decoration.
The hall was festooned with balloons and streamers.  大厅里装饰着气球和彩带。
近义词：garlands, decorations, ornaments
反义词:bareness, plainness
词性:名词

festoons:/ˈfestuːnz/
vt.
用花彩装饰；结彩于
To decorate with festoons.
They festooned the room with Christmas lights. 他们用圣诞彩灯装饰房间。
近义词：decorate, adorn, ornament
反义词:strip, denude
词性:动词
