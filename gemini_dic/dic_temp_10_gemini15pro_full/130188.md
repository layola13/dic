non-sabbatical:/ˌnɒn səˈbætɪkl/ | adj. | 非休假的，不休假的 |  Relating to a period of time when someone is not taking a sabbatical, or relating to work or activities that are not part of a sabbatical.  A sabbatical is a period of paid leave granted to a university teacher or other worker for study or travel, traditionally one year for every seven years worked. |  She took a non-sabbatical leave of absence to care for her sick mother. 她请了非休假假来照顾她生病的母亲。|  Her non-sabbatical research focuses on climate change.  她非休假期间的研究重点是气候变化。 |  working, regular, ordinary | sabbatical | adjective
