nonchurchgoing:/ˌnɑːnˈtʃɜːrtʃˌɡoʊɪŋ/
adjective
不常去教堂的；非经常去教堂的
Not regularly attending church services.
例句：Many nonchurchgoing people still hold religious beliefs. 许多不常去教堂的人仍然持有宗教信仰。

例句：The survey targeted both churchgoing and nonchurchgoing individuals.  这项调查针对经常去教堂和不常去教堂的个人。

近义词：unreligious, secular, irreligious
反义词：churchgoing, devout, religious
词性：adjective


词源：
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "churchgoing" (adjective describing someone who attends church regularly).  "Churchgoing" itself comes from "church" + "going," the present participle of "go."
