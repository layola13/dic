nonemanating:/nɒnɪˈmeɪneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非散发性的；非发射的
Not emanating; not emitting anything.
The cave was dark and nonemanating, giving off no light or heat. 这个洞穴黑暗且非散发性的，没有发出任何光或热。
The device is designed to be nonemanating, minimizing any detectable radiation. 这个装置被设计成非散发性的，最大限度地减少任何可探测到的辐射。
近义词: non-emitting, non-radiating
反义词: emanating, emitting, radiating
词性: adjective
