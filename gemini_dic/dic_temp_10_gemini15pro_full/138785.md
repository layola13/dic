numinousness:/ˌnuːmɪˈnɒsnəs/ |/ˌnuːmɪˈnɑːsnəs/| n. | 神圣感；超自然感；神秘感 | The quality of being filled with a sense of awe, mystery, and the presence of something greater than oneself.  It often describes experiences that are deeply moving and spiritually significant.|  The numinousness of the ancient cathedral filled her with a sense of peace. 古老教堂的神圣感让她内心充满了平静。 | synonyms: holiness, sacredness, divinity, spirituality, transcendence | antonyms: profanity, secularity, worldliness | noun
