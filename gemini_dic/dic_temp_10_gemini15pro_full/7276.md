angoras:/æŋˈɡɔːrəz/
noun
安哥拉猫; 安哥拉山羊; 安哥拉兔; 安哥拉羊毛
Long-haired cats, goats, rabbits, or the yarn made from the hair of angora goats or rabbits.  Specifically refers to breeds known for their long, silky fur.
The sweater was made of soft angoras.  这件毛衣是用柔软的安哥拉山羊毛制成的。
近义词: mohair, cashmere
反义词: (无明确反义词，可考虑短毛动物或其毛制品，例如: shorthair)
词性:n.
