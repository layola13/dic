indulgence:/ɪnˈdʌldʒəns/ | n. | 放纵；沉溺；嗜好；宽容；恩惠 | The act of indulging; gratification of desire.  | She allows herself the occasional indulgence of a glass of wine. 她偶尔会放纵自己喝一杯葡萄酒。 |  He has an indulgence for expensive cars. 他对昂贵的汽车有一种嗜好。 | synonyms: gratification, pleasure, luxury, treat, pampering, excess, self-indulgence | antonyms: abstinence, self-denial, moderation, restraint | noun


indulge:/ɪnˈdʌldʒ/ | v. | 放纵；沉溺；满足（欲望）；纵容 | Allow oneself to enjoy something desired. | I indulged in a long hot bath. 我尽情享受了一个长时间的热水澡。|  She indulged her passion for travel. 她沉溺于她对旅行的热情。 | synonyms:  gratify, satisfy, humor, spoil, pamper, coddle  | antonyms: deny, deprive, abstain | verb
