mugginesses:/ˈmʌɡɪnɪsɪz/
n.
愁苦的表情；苦相；闷闷不乐
Expressions of sullenness or displeasure.
He surveyed the room full of mugginesses. 他环顾了整个房间，里面满是愁眉苦脸的人。

近义词：scowls, frowns, glumness, sullenness
反义词：smiles, cheerfulness, happiness


词性: 名词(plural)
英文解析:  A plural form of "mugginess," which refers to a sullen and unpleasant facial expression. Often used to describe a group of people looking unhappy or dissatisfied.  Derived from "mug," meaning face, with the suffix "-iness" denoting a state or quality.
中文翻译: 愁眉苦脸；苦相（复数）
例句：The mugginesses of the children suggested they were not enjoying the trip. 孩子们愁眉苦脸的样子表明他们不喜欢这次旅行。

例句：Despite the mugginesses on their faces, I knew they were secretly pleased. 尽管他们脸上愁眉苦脸，我知道他们暗自高兴。


词源: From "mug" (face) + "-in-" + "-ness"
