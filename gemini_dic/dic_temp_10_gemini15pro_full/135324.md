nonpecuniary:/ˌnɒnpɪˈkjuːniəri/
adjective
非金钱的；非经济的
Not consisting of or measured in money; not related to money.
例句：
She received nonpecuniary damages for the emotional distress caused by the accident.
她因事故造成的情绪困扰获得了非金钱赔偿。

例句：
The job offers many nonpecuniary benefits, such as flexible working hours and opportunities for professional development.
这份工作提供了许多非金钱福利，例如灵活的工作时间和专业发展机会。

近义词:non-monetary, nonfinancial
反义词:pecuniary, monetary, financial
词性:adjective


词源:
From non- + pecuniary.  "Pecuniary" comes from the Latin word "pecunia," meaning "money" or "property," ultimately derived from "pecus," meaning "cattle" (as cattle were an early form of wealth).
