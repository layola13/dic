anemometric:/ˌænɪməˈmɛtrɪk/ | adj. | 风速测量的；风力计的 | Relating to or measured by an anemometer (an instrument for measuring the speed of the wind). | The anemometric data showed a significant increase in wind speed. 风速测量数据显示风速显著增加。| The anemometric readings were consistent throughout the experiment. 风力计读数在整个实验过程中保持一致。 |  Synonyms: wind-related, wind-measuring | Antonyms: None | adjective
