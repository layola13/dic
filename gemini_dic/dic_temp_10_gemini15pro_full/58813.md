duopolies: /djuːˈɒpəliz/ /duˈɑːpəliz/ | n. | 双头垄断，双寡头垄断 | A market structure dominated by two sellers. | The telecommunications industry in this country is essentially a duopoly. 这个国家的电信行业本质上是双寡头垄断。 |  The duopoly enjoyed huge profits due to limited competition.  由于竞争有限，这两家公司获得了巨额利润。 | near synonyms: oligopoly, two-firm market | antonym: perfect competition | noun
