nonreservable:/ˌnɑːn.rɪˈzɜːr.və.bəl/
adjective
不可预订的
Not able to be reserved or booked in advance.
例句：
The restaurant is nonreservable, so we'll just have to try our luck. 这家餐厅不可预订，所以我们只能碰碰运气了。

This particular room type is nonreservable online.  这种特定房型无法在线预订。

近义词：unbookable, unavailable
反义词：reservable, bookable
词性: adjective
