abdominal:/æbˈdɒmɪnəl/ /æbˈdɑːmɪnl/| adj.|腹部的；肚子的|Relating to the abdomen (belly).|She had abdominal pain after eating the spoiled food. 她吃了变质的食物后腹部疼痛。

He underwent abdominal surgery last year. 他去年接受了腹部手术。

|近义词：ventral, stomachic|反义词：dorsal|词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *abdominalis*, from *abdomen* (belly).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ab* (away, off) and *domos* (house).

USAGE->
Attributive usage (before a noun):

The abdominal muscles are important for core strength.  腹肌对核心力量很重要。
He suffered an abdominal injury in the car accident.  他在车祸中腹部受伤。

Predicative usage (after a verb):

The pain is abdominal.  疼痛在腹部。
The swelling seemed abdominal.  肿胀似乎在腹部。

PHRASE->
abdominal pain = 腹痛
abdominal muscles = 腹肌
abdominal cavity = 腹腔
abdominal surgery = 腹部手术
abdominal aorta = 腹主动脉
