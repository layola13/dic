diffidently:/ˈdɪfɪdəntli/| adv.|胆怯地，羞怯地，缺乏自信地|In a manner showing a lack of self-confidence.|She diffidently approached the podium, her hands trembling slightly. 她胆怯地走近讲台，双手微微颤抖。|Near Synonyms: timidly, shyly, hesitantly, reservedly|Antonyms: confidently, boldly, assertively|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *diffīdens*, present participle of *diffīdere* "to distrust, lack confidence," from *dis-* "not, opposite of" + *fīdere* "to trust."
