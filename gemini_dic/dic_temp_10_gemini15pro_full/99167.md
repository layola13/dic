interchoked:/ˌɪntərˈtʃoʊkt/
| adj. |互相扼住的，互相阻塞的|Mutually choked or obstructed; strangled.  Describes a situation where two or more things are blocking or hindering each other.|The interchoked roots of the two trees made it impossible to separate them. 这两棵树互相缠绕的树根使得它们无法分开。|近义词：intertwined, entangled, interlocked|反义词:separated, disentangled|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From *inter-* (between, among) + *choke* (to obstruct the passage of air).
