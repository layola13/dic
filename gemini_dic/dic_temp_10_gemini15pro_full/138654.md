nudger:/ˈnʌdʒər/| n.|轻推者；推动者；用肘轻推的人|Someone who nudges (prods gently) another person, often to get their attention or to encourage them to do something. |例句：He's a bit of a nudger when it comes to getting people to donate to charity.  他有点喜欢劝别人捐款给慈善机构。

例句：My friend is a nudger; she always pushes me to try new things. 我的朋友是一个推动者；她总是鼓励我尝试新事物。|近义词：prompter, encourager, persuader|反义词:deterrent, obstacle|词性:noun
