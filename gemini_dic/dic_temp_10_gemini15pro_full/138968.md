例句：The old sailor, a seasoned nyctalops, could see the approaching ships long before anyone else.  这位老水手，一个经验丰富的夜视者，比任何人都更早地看到了驶来的船只。|近义词：(for someone who sees well at night) night owl |反义词：(for someone afflicted with night blindness) day-blind person |词性:noun
