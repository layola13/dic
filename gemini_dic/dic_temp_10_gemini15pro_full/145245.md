overblaming:/ˌoʊvərˈbleɪmɪŋ/ | n. | 过度责备；过分归咎 | The act of blaming someone excessively or unfairly for something. | She engaged in a lot of overblaming after the accident, even though it wasn't entirely her fault. 事故发生后，她进行了大量的过度责备，即使这并非完全是她的错。 |  He felt his parents' overblaming was damaging his self-esteem. 他觉得父母的过度责备正在损害他的自尊。 | 近义词: excessive blaming, undue blaming | 反义词: underblaming, excusing | 词性: 名词

overblame:/ˌoʊvərˈbleɪm/ | v. | 过度责备；过分归咎 | To blame someone excessively or unfairly for something. |  She tended to overblame herself for things that were out of her control. 她倾向于过度责备自己无法控制的事情。 |  Don't overblame the child for the broken vase; it was an accident. 不要过度责备孩子打碎花瓶；那是个意外。| 近义词: excessively blame, unduly blame | 反义词: underblame, excuse | 词性: 动词
