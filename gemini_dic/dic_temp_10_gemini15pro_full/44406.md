cross-legged:/ˈkrɒsˌleɡɪd/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsˌleɡɪd/ (US)| adj.|盘腿的，交叉着腿的|Sitting with legs crossed, typically at the ankles.|She sat cross-legged on the floor. 她盘腿坐在地板上。|He listened intently, cross-legged on the cushion. 他盘腿坐在垫子上，聚精会神地听着。|近义词: cross-legged(本身), with legs crossed|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑) legs uncrossed, with legs extended|词性: adjective
