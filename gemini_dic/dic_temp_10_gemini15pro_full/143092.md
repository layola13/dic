outbreathe:/ˌaʊtˈbriːð/
verb
呼气;  吐气
to breathe out; to exhale.
She outbreathed slowly, trying to calm her racing heart. 她慢慢地呼气，试图让狂跳的心平静下来。
The diver outbreathed a stream of bubbles.  潜水员吐出一串气泡。
近义词:exhale, breathe out
反义词:inhale, breathe in
词性:v.
