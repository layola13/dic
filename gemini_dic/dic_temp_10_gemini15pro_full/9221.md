antipacifists:/ˌæntiˈpæsɪfɪsts/ | n. | 反和平主义者；主战者 | People who oppose pacifism and believe that war or violence can sometimes be necessary or justified. |  The antipacifists argued that appeasement would only embolden the aggressor.  反和平主义者认为，绥靖只会助长侵略者的气焰。| synonyms: warmongers, hawks, jingoists | antonyms: pacifists, doves, peacemakers | noun

antipacifist:/ˌæntiˈpæsɪfɪst/ | adj. | 反和平主义的；主战的 | Holding or expressing opinions opposed to pacifism. |  He took an antipacifist stance during the debate. 在辩论中，他采取了反和平主义的立场。 | synonyms: pro-war, militaristic, hawkish | antonyms: pacifist, dovish, peace-loving | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + pacifist. "anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to."  "Pacifist" comes from the Latin "pacificus," meaning "peacemaking," from "pax" (peace) and "-ficus" (making).
