drum-up:/drʌm ˈʌp/| phrasal verb |招揽，鼓动，激起|To try to increase business activity or support for something. |例句：The company is trying to drum up support for its new product.  公司正在努力为其新产品争取支持。

例句：They were trying to drum up enthusiasm for the project. 他们试图激起人们对这个项目的热情。


近义词：solicit, generate, rally, canvass, promote|反义词：suppress, discourage, stifle|词性:动词短语
