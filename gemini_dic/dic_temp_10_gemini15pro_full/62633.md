enforcingly:/ɪnˈfɔːrsɪŋli/
adverb
强制地，强有力地
in a manner that compels observance of or compliance with a law, rule, or obligation.
The rules were enforcingly applied to everyone. 规则被强制应用于每个人。
The government is enforcingly implementing the new policy. 政府正在强制执行新政策。
近义词:compulsorily, mandatorily, obligatorily, peremptorily
反义词:leniently, permissively, optionally
词性:adverb
词源:From enforce + -ingly.  Enforce comes from Old French enforcier "to strengthen, reinforce," from en- "in" + force "force, strength."
