bummer:/ˈbʌmər/| n. |令人失望的事；扫兴的事|A disappointing or unpleasant situation or experience.|例句：Losing the game in the last minute was a real bummer. 在最后一分钟输掉比赛真是太令人失望了。

例句：It's a bummer that you can't come to the party. 你不能来参加派对，真遗憾。|近义词：disappointment, drag, downer|反义词：joy, pleasure|词性：名词

词源:  Likely a shortening of "bum rap" (an unfair accusation or punishment), influenced by German "Bummler" (loafer, idler). First recorded in the 1960s, gaining popularity in the counter-culture movement.
