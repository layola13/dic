hindrance:/ˈhɪndrəns/| n.|障碍，阻碍，妨碍|Something that makes it more difficult for you to do something or for something to develop.|例句：The lack of funding was a major hindrance to the project. 资金的缺乏是该项目的主要障碍。|例句：Her long illness was a hindrance to her career. 她长期的疾病是她事业的障碍。|近义词：obstacle, impediment, barrier, obstruction|反义词：aid, help, assistance|词性:名词
Etymology: From Middle English *hinderaunce*, from Old French *hindrance*, from *hindrer* "to hinder," from Frankish *hindrōn* "behind, back," related to Old High German *hintar* "behind." Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *hindar* "behind," from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ent-/*h₂end- "in front of, before; over."
