overcentralize: /ˌoʊvərˈsɛntrəˌlaɪz/ | v. | 过度集中，过度中央集权 | To concentrate excessive power or control in a central authority or location. |  The company overcentralized its decision-making process, stifling innovation in its branch offices.  这家公司过度集中了决策过程，扼杀了分支机构的创新。| Synonyms: centralize excessively, concentrate excessively | Antonyms: decentralize, distribute | verb

overcentralization: /ˌoʊvərˌsɛntrəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度集中，过度中央集权 | The act of overcentralizing power or control. | The overcentralization of power led to inefficiencies and resentment. 权力过度集中导致效率低下和怨恨。| Synonyms: excessive centralization, excessive concentration | Antonyms: decentralization, distribution | noun
