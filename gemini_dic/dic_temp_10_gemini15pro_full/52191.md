diarrhoetic:/ˌdaɪəˈrɛtɪk/ (US) /ˌdaɪəˈriːtɪk/ (UK)| adj. n.|腹泻的；引起腹泻的；泻药|Causing diarrhea; relating to or characterized by diarrhea; a medicine that causes diarrhea.|This medication is known to be diarrhoetic. 这种药物已知会引起腹泻。|The patient experienced diarrhoetic symptoms after eating contaminated food. 病人在吃了受污染的食物后出现了腹泻症状。|近义词：laxative, purgative, cathartic|反义词：constipating, antidiarrheal|词性: adjective, noun|词源: From Greek *diarrhoia* "diarrhea," from *diarreein* "to flow through," from *dia-* "through" + *rhein* "to flow."


diarrhoea (chiefly British English):/ˌdaɪəˈrɪə/| n.|腹泻|An illness in which the body's solid waste is more liquid than usual and is discharged from the bowels more often than usual.|He suffered from severe diarrhoea and vomiting. 他患有严重的腹泻和呕吐。|The contaminated water caused diarrhoea among the villagers. 受污染的水导致村民腹泻。|近义词：the runs, the trots|反义词：constipation|词性: noun|词源: From Greek *diarrhoia* "diarrhea," from *diarreein* "to flow through," from *dia-* "through" + *rhein* "to flow."
