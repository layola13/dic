limelighting: /ˈlaɪmˌlaɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 聚光灯照明；公众瞩目；引人注目 | The act of focusing attention on someone or something, especially in a theatrical performance or in the public eye. | The media's limelighting of her personal life made her uncomfortable. 媒体对她个人生活的关注让她感到不舒服。 |  近义词: spotlighting, highlighting, publicizing | 反义词: obscuring, hiding, concealing | 词性: 名词
