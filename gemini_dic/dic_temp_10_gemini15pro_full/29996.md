cavities:/ˈkævɪtiz/| n.|腔，洞，龋齿|A cavity is a hollow space within a solid object, or a hole in a surface, especially a tooth.|The dentist filled the cavities in my teeth. 牙医填补了我牙齿上的龋齿。|Synonyms: holes, hollows, gaps|Antonyms: solids, fullness|noun

cavities:/ˈkævɪtiz/| n.|空腔|A cavity can also refer to a hollow space within the body.|The heart is located in the thoracic cavity. 心脏位于胸腔内。|Synonyms: chambers, compartments|Antonyms: solids, fullness|noun
