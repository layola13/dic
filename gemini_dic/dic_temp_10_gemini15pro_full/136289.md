nonrepresentation:/nɒnˌreprizenˈteɪʃn/ | /nɑːnˌreprizenˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 不代表，未被代表，缺乏代表 | lack of representation; failure to represent.  The state of not being represented, especially in a legislative body or other official capacity. |  Example 1: The nonrepresentation of women in senior management roles is a serious issue.  高级管理职位中女性缺乏代表性是一个严重问题。

Example 2:  The community protested the nonrepresentation of their interests in the city council. 社区抗议市议会中没有代表他们的利益。 | 近义词: underrepresentation, exclusion | 反义词: representation, inclusion | 词性: noun


Etymology: non- (prefix meaning "not") + representation (from Latin repraesentare, "to present again, exhibit")
