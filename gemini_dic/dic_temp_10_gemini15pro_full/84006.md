half-discriminated:/ˌhæf dɪˈskrɪmɪneɪtɪd/
| adj. | 半歧视的；部分歧视的 | Partially discriminated; discriminated against in some ways but not others.  This term suggests an incomplete or inconsistent application of discriminatory practices.  |  The study revealed that women were half-discriminated against in the hiring process, receiving interviews but ultimately being passed over for male candidates.  这项研究表明，女性在招聘过程中受到半歧视，她们获得了面试机会，但最终还是被男性候选人取代。 | Near Synonyms: partially discriminated, subtly discriminated, quasi-discriminated | Antonyms: fully discriminated, undiscriminated, equally treated | adjective
