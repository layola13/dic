hydrofoil:/ˈhaɪdrəfɔɪl/ | n. | 水翼船；水翼 | A boat that rises out of the water on hydrofoils when traveling at speed. | Example: The hydrofoil skimmed across the waves. 水翼船掠过波浪。

Example:  We took a hydrofoil to the island. 我们乘坐水翼船去了岛上。


|近义词：hovercraft, surface-effect ship|反义词:submarine|词性:noun|


hydrofoil:/ˈhaɪdrəfɔɪl/ | n. | 水翼 | A winglike structure, or pair of structures, attached to the hull of a boat that lifts it out of the water to increase speed. | Example: The hydrofoils lifted the boat clear of the water. 水翼将船抬离水面。

Example: The damage to the hydrofoil reduced the boat's speed.  水翼的损坏降低了船的速度。



|近义词：fin, wing|反义词:hull|词性:noun|


ETYMOLOGY->
From hydro- (water) + -foil (leaf, wing).  "Hydro-" comes from the Greek "hydōr" (water). "-foil" ultimately comes from the Latin "folium" (leaf).
