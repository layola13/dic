alienate: /ˈeɪliəneɪt/ (US) /ˈeɪliəneɪt/ (UK)| vt.|疏远；离间；使格格不入|To make someone feel that they are different and not part of a group or community. |His comments alienated his colleagues. 他的言论疏远了他的同事们。|近义词: estrange, isolate, distance|反义词: reconcile, unite|词性: 动词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin alienatus, past participle of alienare "to estrange," from Latin alienus "of or belonging to another, foreign," from alius "other."
