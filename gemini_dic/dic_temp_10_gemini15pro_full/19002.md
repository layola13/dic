bestrew:/bɪˈstruː/ /bɪˈstrɔː/
verb
散布，撒满
To scatter or cover (a surface) with things:
例句：Fallen leaves bestrewed the path. 落叶撒满了小路。

例句：The floor was bestrewed with toys. 地板上撒满了玩具。

近义词：scatter, sprinkle, strew
反义词：gather, collect
词性：verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bestrewen, from Old English *bestreowian (unattested), from be- + strewian (“to strew”).  Cognate with Dutch bestrooien, German bestreuen.
