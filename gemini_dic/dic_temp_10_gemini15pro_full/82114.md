grogginess:/ˈɡrɒɡɪnəs/ /ˈɡrɑːɡɪnəs/
noun
昏昏沉沉；迷糊；不清醒
The state of being groggy; drowsiness or mental confusion, typically as a result of intoxication or inadequate sleep.
After the long flight, I felt a deep grogginess. 长途飞行后，我感到非常昏沉。
Near synonyms: drowsiness, sleepiness, lethargy, sluggishness, stupor, torpor
Antonyms: alertness, wakefulness, vigilance
词性: 名词
