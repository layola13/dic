fillies: /ˈfɪliz/
noun
小雌马（复数）
Young female horses, typically under four years old.
The fillies frolicked in the meadow. 小雌马在草地上嬉戏。
近义词: female horses, mares (mature female horses)
反义词: colts (young male horses), stallions (mature male horses)
词性: noun (plural)

词源:
From Middle English *filli*, from Old English *filih*, from Proto-Germanic *fōlijǭ* (“female foal”), from Proto-Indo-European *pel- (“pale, grey”). Cognate with Old Frisian *fol*, Old Saxon *foli*, Old High German *fole*, Old Norse *foli*, Gothic *fula*, Latin *pullus* (“young animal, chick”).
