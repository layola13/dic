leukaemia /ljuːˈkiːmiə/ (also spelled leukemia)
| n. |白血病|A malignant progressive disease in which the bone marrow and other blood-forming organs produce increased numbers of immature or abnormal leucocytes. These suppress the production of normal blood cells, leading to anaemia and other symptoms.| He was diagnosed with acute leukaemia. 他被诊断出患有急性白血病。|Synonyms: cancer of the blood, blood cancer|Antonyms: health, wellness|Noun


leucocythaemia /ˌljuːkəʊsaɪˈθiːmiə/ (British English, less common spelling)
| n. |白血病|An older term for leukaemia, a malignant progressive disease characterized by the abnormal production of white blood cells.|  Leucocythaemia was once a widely used term.  白细胞血症曾经是一个广泛使用的术语。|Synonyms: leukaemia, cancer of the blood, blood cancer|Antonyms: health, wellness|Noun
