chalk:/tʃɔːk/ (UK) /tʃɑːk/ (US)| n. v.|粉笔；用粉笔写|A soft, white, powdery limestone used for writing or drawing. |She used chalk to draw a hopscotch grid on the sidewalk. 她用粉笔在人行道上画了个跳房子的格子。|Synonyms: crayon, pastel|Antonyms: eraser, marker|Noun

chalk:/tʃɔːk/ (UK) /tʃɑːk/ (US)| v.|用粉笔写|To write or draw with chalk. |He chalked the specials on the blackboard. 他用粉笔把今日特价菜写在黑板上。|Synonyms: scribble, scrawl|Antonyms: erase, wipe|Verb
