nosebleed:/ˈnoʊzˌbliːd/| n.|鼻出血；流鼻血|A nosebleed is the loss of blood from the tissue that lines the inside of your nose.|I got a nosebleed after being hit in the face. 我被打到脸后流鼻血了。|Synonyms: epistaxis, hemorrhage (more general)|Antonyms: N/A|noun

nosebleed:/ˈnoʊzˌbliːd/| n.|麻烦，困境 (informal)|A nosebleed can also informally refer to a difficult or unpleasant situation, particularly one involving a lot of stress or pressure.|Negotiating that deal was a real nosebleed.  谈判那笔交易真是让人头疼。|Synonyms: headache, hassle, ordeal|Antonyms: N/A|noun
