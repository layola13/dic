non-hellenic:/nɒn-hɛˈlɛnɪk/
adjective
非希腊的；非希腊文化的
Not Hellenic; not of or pertaining to ancient Greece, its language, its people, or its culture.
The museum's exhibit showcased non-Hellenic artifacts from the surrounding regions.  博物馆的展览展示了来自周边地区的非希腊文物。
The study compared Hellenic and non-Hellenic influences on Roman architecture.  这项研究比较了希腊和非希腊对罗马建筑的影响。
Synonyms: non-Greek, foreign (to Greece)
Antonyms: Hellenic, Greek
word type: adjective
