outfitting:/ˈaʊtfɪtɪŋ/
n.
装备; 配备; 服装; 全套装备
the process of providing someone or something with equipment or clothes.
The company specializes in the outfitting of expeditions to remote areas.  这家公司专门为前往偏远地区的探险队提供装备。
They spent a fortune on outfitting their new apartment.  他们在布置新公寓上花了一大笔钱。
equipment, gear, supply, provision
lack
noun

outfitting:/ˈaʊtfɪtɪŋ/
n.
一套服装; 全套装备
a set of clothes worn together, especially for a particular occasion or activity.
She bought a new outfitting for the wedding. 她为婚礼买了一套新衣服。
He wore a special outfitting for the hiking trip.  他为徒步旅行穿了一套特殊的服装。
outfit, ensemble, suit, costume
disarray
noun
