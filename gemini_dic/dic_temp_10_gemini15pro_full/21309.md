bloodstreams:/ˈblʌdstriːmz/
n.
血流；血液循环
the flowing blood in a circulatory system
The drug enters the bloodstream quickly.  这种药物很快进入血液循环。

bloodstreams:/ˈblʌdstriːmz/
n.
思潮；潮流
the prevailing opinion or feeling in a group or society
A new wave of optimism is flowing through the nation's bloodstream.一股新的乐观情绪正在全国蔓延。
