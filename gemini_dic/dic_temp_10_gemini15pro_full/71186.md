fistful:/ˈfɪstfʊl/| n.|少量；一小撮|A small quantity or amount.|She added just a fistful of rice to the pot.  她只往锅里加了一小撮米饭。|Synonyms: smattering, pinch, dash|Antonyms: abundance, plenty|noun
