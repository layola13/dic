annulled:/əˈnʌld/
verb
宣告无效；取消；废除
to declare officially that something is no longer legally valid
His second marriage was annulled because he never divorced his first wife. 他的第二次婚姻被宣告无效，因为他从未与第一任妻子离婚。

The election results were annulled because of voter fraud. 由于选举舞弊，选举结果被宣布无效。

synonym: invalidate, nullify, revoke, cancel, abolish
antonym: validate, confirm, ratify, uphold
