governs:/ˈɡʌvərnz/| v.|统治，管理，支配，控制，影响|The prime minister governs the country. 首相治理国家。|近义词：rules, manages, controls, directs, influences|反义词：obeys, follows, submits|verb

Etymology->
From Old French governer, from Latin gubernare "to steer, guide, direct, govern,"  from Greek kybernan "to steer, guide."
