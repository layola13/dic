blowtorched:/ˈbloʊˌtɔːrtʃt/| adj.|喷灯烧过的，用喷灯处理过的|Having been heated or treated with a blowtorch.|The chef blowtorched the top of the crème brûlée to caramelize the sugar.  厨师用喷灯烧焦了焦糖布丁的顶部以使糖焦糖化。|
The metal was blowtorched to create a unique texture.  金属被喷灯烧过以创造独特的纹理。|近义词:seared, scorched, singed|反义词:frozen, chilled|词性:形容词
Etymology:  blow + torch + -ed.  From "blowtorch" (a tool that produces a very hot flame) and the past participle suffix "-ed," indicating a state resulting from the action of the verb.
