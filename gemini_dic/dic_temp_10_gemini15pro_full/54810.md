dispersability:/dɪˌspɜːrsəˈbɪləti/ /dɪˌspərsəˈbɪlədi/
n.
分散性，分散能力
The ability of a substance to be dispersed or spread out, typically in a liquid or gas.
The dispersability of the powder in water is excellent.  这种粉末在水中的分散性非常好。
dispersion, spreadability
cohesion, agglomeration
noun
