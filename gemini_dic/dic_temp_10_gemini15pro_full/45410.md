cupolated:/ˈkʌpəleɪtɪd/
adjective
成杯状的；圆顶状的
Shaped like a dome or cupola.
The cupolated roof of the church was a striking feature of the skyline. 教堂的圆顶屋顶是天际线的一个显著特征。
The mineral formed cupolated crystals. 这种矿物形成了杯状晶体。

近义词: domed, vaulted, arched
反义词: flat, planar

词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *cupulatus, from Latin cupula (“small cask, cupola”), diminutive of cupa (“cask, tub”).
