compatriotisms: /kəmˌpeɪtriˈɒtɪzəmz/ | n. | 同胞情誼，愛國主義 | The feeling of love and loyalty that people have for their country, especially when it is shared by a group of people who live in a foreign country. |  Expatriates often feel a surge of compatriotism when they meet fellow countrymen abroad.  海外侨民在国外遇到同胞时，往往会感到一阵爱国热情。| 近义词: patriotism, nationalism, fellow feeling | 反义词: xenophobia, animosity, disloyalty | 词性: 名词

Etymology:

From French *compatriotisme*, from compatriote (“compatriot”) + -isme.  Ultimately from Latin *compatriōta* (“fellow countryman”).
