inamorata:/ɪˌnæməˈrɑːtə/ /ɪˌnæməˈrɑːdə/| n. |情人；女爱人；女友|A woman with whom someone is in love or has a romantic relationship. |例句：The poet wrote many sonnets to his inamorata. 这位诗人给他的爱人写了很多十四行诗。

例句：He showered his inamorata with gifts. 他给他的女友送了很多礼物。


近义词：girlfriend, sweetheart, lover, beloved|反义词:enemy, foe|词性:noun

inamorato:/ɪˌnæməˈrɑːtoʊ/ /ɪˌnæməˈrɑːtəʊ/| n. |情人；男爱人；男友|A man with whom someone is in love or has a romantic relationship.  (This form is less commonly used than "inamorata")|例句：The young inamorato serenaded his beloved under her balcony.  这位年轻的恋人在他爱人的阳台下唱起了小夜曲。

例句：She accepted a gift from her inamorato. 她接受了男友的礼物。


近义词：boyfriend, sweetheart, lover, beloved|反义词:enemy, foe|词性:noun
