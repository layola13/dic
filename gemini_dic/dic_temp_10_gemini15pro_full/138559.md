what: /wɒt/ (British) /wʌt/ (American) | pron. det. adv. int.| 什么，多么 |

英文解析:  "What" is a versatile word used as a pronoun, determiner, adverb, and interjection. It's primarily used to ask questions about things, people, actions, or qualities. It can also express surprise or emphasis.

中文翻译: 什么，多么
