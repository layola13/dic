alerts:/əˈlɜːrts/ | n. | 警报；警告；提醒 | something that tells or warns people about a danger, problem, or emergency | The system sends email alerts to users.  该系统向用户发送电子邮件警报。| synonyms: warnings, alarms, notifications | antonyms: silences | noun

alerts:/əˈlɜːts/ | v. | 使警觉；使意识到；提醒 | to warn someone of a danger, problem, or emergency |  The security guard alerted the police. 保安提醒了警察。| synonyms: warns, notifies, informs | antonyms: ignores, neglects | verb
