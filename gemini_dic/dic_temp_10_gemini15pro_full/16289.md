baptizement:/bæpˈtaɪzmənt/| n. |洗礼，浸礼|The act of baptizing someone, usually with water, as a religious rite or sacrament.|His baptizement took place in a small church. 他的洗礼仪式在一个小教堂举行。| The baptizement marked the beginning of his spiritual journey.  洗礼标志着他精神旅程的开始。 |近义词：baptism, christening|反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially "desecration" in a figurative sense if the act is considered sacred)|词性:noun
