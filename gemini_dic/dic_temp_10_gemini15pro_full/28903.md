carrels:/ˈkærəlz/ |n. |小单间，卡座|A small cubicle or enclosed desk in a library or other public space, providing privacy for individual study or work.|例句：The library has several carrels where students can study in peace. 图书馆里有几个小单间，学生可以在那里安静地学习。

例句：She prefers to work in a carrel because she finds it less distracting. 她更喜欢在卡座里工作，因为她觉得这样不容易分心。|近义词：cubicle, booth, study desk |反义词: open space |词性: 名词
