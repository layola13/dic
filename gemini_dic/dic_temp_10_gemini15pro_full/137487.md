nontortuously:/ˌnɒntɔːrˈtʃuəsli/
adjective
非曲折地，直接地
in a nontortuous manner; straightforwardly.  The path led nontortuously to the summit. 这条路径笔直地通向山顶。
The surgeon made a nontortuous incision.  外科医生做了一个直切口。
Synonym: directly, straightforwardly, simply
Antonym: tortuously, indirectly, circuitously
词性: adjective
