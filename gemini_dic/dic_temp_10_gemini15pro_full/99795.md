intermeshing:/ˌɪntərˈmɛʃɪŋ/| adj. |互相啮合的，互相交错的|The process or state of becoming intermeshed, like gears fitting together.|The intermeshing gears of the clockwork mechanism kept perfect time.|近义词：interlocking, interwoven, intertwined|反义词：separate, disconnected|词性:形容词
intermesh:/ˌɪntərˈmɛʃ/| v. |互相啮合，互相交错|To become locked together like gears; to engage or connect closely.|The cogs intermesh smoothly.|近义词：interlock, intertwine, interweave|反义词：disengage, separate|词性:动词
