grudgingly:/ˈɡrʌdʒɪŋli/ | adv. | 勉强地，不情愿地 | Done or said unwillingly; reluctantly. | He grudgingly admitted he was wrong. 他勉强承认自己错了。 | She grudgingly agreed to help. 她很不情愿地同意帮忙。 | 近义词: reluctantly, unwillingly, resentfully | 反义词: willingly, readily, eagerly | 词性: 副词

词源: From the adjective "grudging" + "-ly". "Grudging" comes from the Old English "grutcian" meaning "to murmur, grumble."
