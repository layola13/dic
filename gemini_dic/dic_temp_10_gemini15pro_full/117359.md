medias:/ˈmiːdiəz/| n.| 媒体（medium 的复数形式）| Medium refers to a means of communication or expression, such as newspapers, television, or radio. Medias is the plural form, though "media" is more commonly used as a plural. |Many medias reported on the election. 许多媒体报道了这次选举。|近义词：media, press, channels|反义词: None|词性:Noun
