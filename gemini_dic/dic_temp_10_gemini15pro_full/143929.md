outpunching:/ˌaʊtˈpʌntʃɪŋ/
|v.|超过…的拳击力; (比喻) 超越，胜过|Defeating someone by punching harder or more effectively, often used figuratively to describe outperforming someone in a competitive situation.|The smaller fighter surprised everyone by outpunching his larger opponent.  这位较小的拳击手以更强的拳击力击败了他的对手，令所有人惊讶。|The new company is outpunching its rivals in the market. 这家新公司在市场上胜过其竞争对手。|近义词: outperforming, surpassing, exceeding, beating|反义词: underperforming, losing to|动词
