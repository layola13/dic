expenditures:/ɪkˈspendɪtʃərz/
|n.|支出，花费|The government's expenditures on education have increased this year. 政府今年的教育支出有所增加。|
|Etymology: From Late Middle English expenditure, from Late Latin expenditūra, from Latin expendere "to weigh out, pay out," from ex- "out" + pendere "to weigh, pay."|
|Synonyms: spending, costs, outlays, expenses|
|Antonyms: income, revenue, earnings, profits|
|Noun|
