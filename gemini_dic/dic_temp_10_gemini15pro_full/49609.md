demi-hake:/ˈdemi heɪk/ | n. | 半熟熏鳕鱼 | A lightly smoked haddock that is not fully cured.  | Example:  The demi-hake was flaky and moist, with a subtle smoky flavor. 半熟熏鳕鱼片片分明，湿润多汁，带有淡淡的烟熏味。

Example: For a quick and easy meal, try grilling some demi-hake with lemon and herbs.  想吃一顿快捷简便的餐点，可以试试烤一些半熟熏鳕鱼，配上柠檬和香草。 | 近义词: lightly smoked haddock,  kipper (though kippers are typically split and gutted) | 反义词: fully cured haddock, unsmoked haddock | 词性: noun

词源:
"demi-" comes from French, meaning "half"
"hake" is of Germanic origin, referring to the fish species.
