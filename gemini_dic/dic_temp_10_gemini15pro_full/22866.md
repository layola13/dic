botanicals:/bəˈtænɪkəlz/
noun
植物药；植物性物质
Substances obtained from plants.
This gin features a unique blend of botanicals.  这款金酒的特点是混合了独特的植物成分。
The skincare product contains natural botanicals.  这款护肤品含有天然植物成分。
synonyms: herbs, plant extracts, plant-based ingredients
antonyms: synthetics, chemicals
n.
