hyperpnoea:/ˌhaɪpərˈniːə/ /ˌhaɪpərˈpniːə/
noun
呼吸过度，过度换气
Increased depth and rate of breathing.
After strenuous exercise, hyperpnoea is normal. 剧烈运动后，呼吸过度是正常的。
hyperventilation, overbreathing
hypopnea, bradypnea
