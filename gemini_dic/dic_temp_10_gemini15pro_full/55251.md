dissimulation:/dɪˌsɪmjʊˈleɪʃn/
n.
掩饰，假装，伪装
The act of concealing one's true feelings or intentions.
He practiced dissimulation to hide his true motives. 他 practiced dissimulation来掩盖他真实的动机。
Her dissimulation was so perfect that no one suspected her true feelings. 她的伪装如此完美，以至于没有人怀疑她的真实感受。

近义词: deception, pretense, feigning, masking, camouflage
反义词: honesty, candor, frankness, openness, sincerity
词性: noun
