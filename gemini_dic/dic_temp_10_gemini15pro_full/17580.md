beauteous:/ˈbjuːtiəs/| adj.|美丽的，美好的|Very beautiful; pleasing to the senses.|例句：She possessed a beauteous smile that could light up a room. 她拥有一个能照亮整个房间的美丽笑容。

例句：The beauteous landscape captivated all who visited.  美丽的景色迷住了所有到访的人。|近义词：beautiful, pretty, lovely, attractive, gorgeous, stunning|反义词：ugly, hideous, unattractive|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *beautevous, from Old French beauteus, from beauté (“beauty”) + -ous (suffix forming adjectives). Ultimately from Latin bellus (“beautiful”).
