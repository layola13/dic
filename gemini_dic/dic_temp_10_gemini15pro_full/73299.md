forecastable:/ˈfɔːrkæstəbl/| adj.|可预测的|Able to be predicted or estimated.|The weather is forecastable for the next few days. 未来几天的天气是可以预测的。|近义词：predictable, foreseeable, anticipated|反义词：unpredictable, unforeseeable, unexpected|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From forecast + -able. Forecast comes from Middle English forcasten, from for- + casten "to throw".  The original sense was "to scheme beforehand," hence "to predict."
