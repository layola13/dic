elevational:/ˌɛləˈveɪʃənəl/
adjective
高度的；海拔的；仰角的
Relating to elevation, especially above sea level or the horizon.
The elevational gradient of species richness is a common pattern in ecology.  物种丰富度的海拔梯度是生态学中的一个常见模式。
The study examined the elevational distribution of bird species in the Himalayas.  这项研究调查了喜马拉雅山脉鸟类的海拔分布。
近义词: altitudinal, hypsometric
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "lowland")
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From elevation + -al.  Elevation itself comes from the Late Latin ēlevātiōn- (stem of ēlevātiō), from the past participle stem of ēlevāre "to lift up," from e- "out, up" + levāre "to raise, lighten."
