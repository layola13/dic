lumberjack: /ˈlʌmbərˌdʒæk/ (US) /ˈlʌmbədʒæk/ (UK)| n.|伐木工人；伐木工|A person who fells trees, especially as an occupation.|The lumberjack swung his axe and brought down the giant redwood. 伐木工人挥动斧头，砍倒了巨大的红杉树。|近义词：logger, woodsman|反义词: None (No direct opposite for an occupation)|Noun

词源：lumber (timber) + jack (man)  “lumber”指木材，“jack”是对男性的称呼，因此“lumberjack”指处理木材的男人，即伐木工人。
