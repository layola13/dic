gallicize:/ˈɡælɪsaɪz/ /ˈɡæləˌsaɪz/| v. |使法国化；使具有法国风格；法语化|To make French in form or character; to adopt French customs or ways.|The English language was Gallicized after the Norman Conquest. 诺曼征服后，英语被法语化了。| The restaurant's menu was Gallicized to appeal to a wider audience.  这家餐厅的菜单被法语化以吸引更广泛的顾客。|近义词：frenchify|反义词：anglicize|词性:动词
