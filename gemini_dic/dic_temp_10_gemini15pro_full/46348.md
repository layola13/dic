dactyl: /ˈdæktɪl/
dactyl: | n.| 长短短格；抑扬格 | A metrical foot consisting of one stressed syllable followed by two unstressed syllables. |  The word "poetry" is a dactyl. “poetry”这个词是一个抑扬格。 | anapest | iamb | noun
