hibernators:/ˈhaɪbərˌneɪtərz/| n. |冬眠动物|Animals that hibernate, spending the winter in a dormant state.|Bears and groundhogs are hibernators. 熊和土拨鼠都是冬眠动物。|Squirrels and chipmunks are also hibernators, although their hibernation is less deep than that of bears. 松鼠和花栗鼠也是冬眠动物，尽管它们的冬眠不如熊那么深。|synonyms: dormant animals, winter sleepers|antonyms: non-hibernators, active animals|noun
