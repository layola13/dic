nondiverging:/ˌnɒndaɪˈvɜːrdʒɪŋ/
adjective
非发散的，不发散的
Not diverging; remaining parallel or converging.  Staying on the same path or coming closer together, not spreading apart.
The nondiverging lines of the railway track stretched towards the horizon.  铁路轨道不发散的线条延伸至地平线。
The laser emitted a nondiverging beam of light.  激光发射出一束不发散的光束。
Synonyms: parallel, converging, collimated
Antonyms: diverging, spreading, branching
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + diverging (present participle of diverge, from Latin dis- "apart" + vergere "to incline").
