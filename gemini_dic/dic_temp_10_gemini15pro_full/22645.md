boragineous:/ˌbɔːrəˈdʒɪniəs/
adjective
紫草科的; 似紫草的
Relating to or resembling the borage family (Boraginaceae), typically having hairy leaves and small, blue, star-shaped flowers.
Borage is a boragineous plant with vibrant blue, edible flowers. 紫草是一种开着鲜艳的蓝色可食用花朵的紫草科植物。
