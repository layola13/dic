editorships:/ˈedɪtərʃɪps/
n.
编辑职位；编辑工作
The position and duties of an editor.
She held several editorships during her career.  她在职业生涯中担任过多个编辑职位。
synonyms: editorship, editorial position, editorial post
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, consider perhaps "authorship" for a contrasting concept)
