overcorrupts:/ˌoʊvərˈkərʌpts/
verb
过度腐蚀；极度败坏
To corrupt excessively or to an extreme degree.  The influence of unchecked power overcorrupts even the most well-intentioned leaders.
The constant stream of bribes and kickbacks overcorrupted the officials.  持续不断的贿赂和回扣极度败坏了官员们。
debase, pervert, subvert, undermine
elevate, purify, uplift
v.
