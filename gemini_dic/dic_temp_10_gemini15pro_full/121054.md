misconjunction:/ˌmɪskənˈdʒʌŋkʃən/ | n. | 错误连接；误用连词 | An incorrect or illogical joining of phrases or clauses by a conjunction. |  The sentence "She went to the store, and she bought milk, but she forgot her wallet" is an example of misconjunction because the "but" doesn't logically follow the previous clauses. 她去了商店，买了牛奶，但她忘了带钱包（这是一个连词误用的例子，因为“但是”在逻辑上不符合前面的分句）。 |  misuse of conjunctions, faulty conjunction | correct conjunction, proper conjunction | noun
