bilateral:/baɪˈlætərəl/| adj.|双边的，双方的|Involving two sides, parties, or countries.|例句：The two countries signed a bilateral trade agreement. 两国签署了一项双边贸易协定。

例句：Bilateral talks between the two leaders are scheduled for next week.  两位领导人之间的双边会谈定于下周举行。

近义词：mutual, reciprocal, two-sided|反义词:unilateral, multilateral|词性:adjective
