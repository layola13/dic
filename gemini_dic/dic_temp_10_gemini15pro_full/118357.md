merited: /ˈmɛrɪtɪd/ | adj. | 应得的，理所当然的 | Deserved, earned, or justified.  Something that is merited is appropriate or suitable because of the qualities or actions of someone or something. |  His long years of service merited a generous retirement package.  他多年的服务应得丰厚的退休金。| Synonyms: deserved, earned, justified, warranted, appropriate | Antonyms: undeserved, unearned, unjustified, unwarranted, inappropriate | adjective

merited: /ˈmɛrɪtɪd/ | v. | 值得，应得 |  Past participle of the verb "merit," meaning to deserve or be worthy of something.  | His bravery merited the highest honor. 他的勇敢值得最高的荣誉。 | Synonyms: deserved, earned, warranted | Antonyms: forfeit | verb
