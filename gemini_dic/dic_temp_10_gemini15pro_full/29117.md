cascading:/kæˈskeɪdɪŋ/ | adj. | 倾泻的，层叠的 | arranged in a series of overlapping stages so that each stage derives from or acts upon the product of the preceding. | The cascading waterfall was a breathtaking sight. 倾泻而下的瀑布景色令人叹为观止。 | synonyms: flowing, tumbling, descending, overflowing | antonyms: ascending, rising, climbing | adjective

cascading:/kæˈskeɪdɪŋ/ | n. | 倾泻，层叠 | the action of cascading. | The cascading of information through the organization was slow. 信息在组织内的层叠传递很慢。| synonyms: flow, tumble, descent, overflow | antonyms: ascent, rise, climb | noun
