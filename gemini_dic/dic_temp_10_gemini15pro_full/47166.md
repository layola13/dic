dawn:/dɔːn/ (US) /dɔːn/ (UK)| n. vi.|黎明，拂晓；开端，萌芽|The period of time each day when light first appears, marking the beginning of the day; the beginning of something.|例句：The dawn broke over the mountains. 黎明破晓，照亮了群山。|近义词：sunrise, daybreak, beginning, outset|反义词：dusk, sunset, end|noun

dawn:/dɔːn/ (US) /dɔːn/ (UK)| vi.|破晓；开始出现；变得明朗|To begin to grow light as the sun rises; to begin to appear or develop.|例句：A new era is dawning. 一个新时代正在来临。|近义词：emerge, appear, begin|反义词：disappear, end, vanish|verb
