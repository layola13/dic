exploit:/ɪkˈsplɔɪt/ | v. | 利用，剥削 | To make full use of a resource or situation, sometimes in a way considered unfair or unethical. |  The company exploited its workers by making them work long hours for low pay.  这家公司剥削工人，让他们长时间工作，却只支付低工资。| synonyms: utilize, capitalize on, take advantage of | antonyms: preserve, protect, neglect | verb

exploit:/ˈeksplɔɪt/ | n. | 功绩，功勋，壮举 | A bold or daring feat. | His daring exploits in the war made him a hero. 他在战争中的英勇壮举使他成为英雄。 | synonyms: feat, achievement, accomplishment | antonyms: failure, defeat, setback | noun
