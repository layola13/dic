divisible:/dɪˈvɪzəbl/| adj.|可分的，可除尽的|Capable of being divided, usually with no remainder.|The number 12 is divisible by 2, 3, 4, and 6.  12可以被2、3、4和6整除。|近义词：factorable|反义词：indivisible|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English divisible, from Late Latin dīvīsibilis, from Latin dīvīsus (past participle of dīvidere ‘to divide’)+ -ibilis (suffix forming adjectives).
