misdeclaring:/ˌmɪsdɪˈkleərɪŋ/
verb
错误地声明；虚报
The act of declaring something incorrectly, either intentionally or unintentionally.
He was accused of misdeclaring his income to the tax authorities. 他被指控向税务机关虚报收入。
misrepresent, falsify
declare correctly, report accurately
词性: 动词
