faren:/ˈfɛərən/ | n. |华氏温度|A temperature scale where 32° is the freezing point and 212° is the boiling point of water.|The temperature today is 75 degrees Farenheit. | 今天气温是75华氏度。|Synonym: Fahrenheit|Antonym: Celsius, Centigrade|noun
