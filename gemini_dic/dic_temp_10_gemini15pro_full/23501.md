braid:/breɪd/| n. v.| 辫子；编织；编织物 | A length of hair made up of three or more interlaced strands. 词源:
From Old English *bregdan, from Proto-Germanic *bregdaną (“to braid, weave, pull, draw”). Cognate with Old Norse bregða (“to move quickly, braid”), Old High German brettan (“to weave”), German flechten (“to braid”).
