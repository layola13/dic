cytologically:/ˌsaɪtəˈlɑːdʒɪkli/
adv.
细胞学地
In a way that relates to the study of cells.
The tissue sample was analyzed cytologically.  组织样本进行了细胞学分析。
近义词:microscopically, cellularly
反义词:macroscopically

词性:adv. (副词)

词源:
From cytology + -ically.  Cytology comes from cyto- + -logy.  Cyto- means "cell," and -logy means "study of." -ically forms adverbs from adjectives.
