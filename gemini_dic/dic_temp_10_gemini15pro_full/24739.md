broodest:/ˈbruːdɪst/| adj.|最爱沉思的；最忧郁的|Superlative form of broody: most broody.  Meaning most inclined to sit on eggs (of a hen), or most preoccupied with depressing, morbid, or painful thoughts. |She was the broodest of the hens, rarely leaving her nest.|近义词：gloomiest, melancholiest, morosest|反义词：cheeriest, happiest, most cheerful|词性:adjective

Etymology: From Old English *brōd, from Proto-Germanic *brōdīaną "to warm, cherish." The original sense referred to a hen sitting on eggs.  The figurative sense of being preoccupied with gloomy thoughts developed later.
