dissightly:/dɪsˈsaɪtli/
adjective
难看的，不雅观的
unpleasant to look at; ugly
例句：The dissightly garbage dump spoiled the view.  难看的垃圾场破坏了景色。

例句：The abandoned building was a dissightly mess. 这栋废弃的建筑物是一片难看的景象。

近义词：unattractive, ugly, unsightly, hideous, repulsive
反义词：attractive, beautiful, pleasing, lovely, appealing
词性：adjective
