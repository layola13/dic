non-prohibition:/ˌnɒn prəʊɪˈbɪʃən/ | n. | 非禁止，不禁止 | The non-prohibition of certain activities does not imply their endorsement.  某些活动的非禁止并不意味着对其的认可。 |  The policy of non-prohibition on street vendors led to a surge in their numbers.  对街头摊贩的非禁止政策导致他们的数量激增。 | 近义词: allowance, permission, authorization | 反义词: prohibition, ban, forbiddance | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "prohibition" (from Latin "prohibere" - to prevent, forbid).
