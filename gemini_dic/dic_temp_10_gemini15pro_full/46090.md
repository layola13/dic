cyclostomi: /saɪˈklɒstəmaɪ/ /saɪˈkloʊstəˌmaɪ/
noun
圆口纲动物
Jawless fish characterized by a sucking, circular mouth.  A group of agnathan vertebrates that includes the lampreys and hagfishes.
Lampreys and hagfishes belong to the Cyclostomi. 七鳃鳗和盲鳗属于圆口纲。
synonyms: Agnatha (more inclusive), jawless fish
antonyms: Gnathostomata (jawed vertebrates)
n.
