attemptless:/əˈtemptləs/| adj. |未尝试的；没有尝试的|Without any attempt; not tried.  Characterized by a lack of effort or initiative.|He made an attemptless shot at the basket. 他投篮时根本没有尝试瞄准。|Near Synonyms: effortless, half-hearted, unmotivated, lackadaisical, perfunctory|Antonyms: determined, persistent, ambitious, striving, industrious|Etymology:  A combination of "attempt" (from Old French "attempter," from Latin "attentare" - to try) and the suffix "-less" (meaning without).|Word Type: Adjective
