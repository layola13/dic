bend-up:/ˈbɛnd ʌp/ | adj. n.| 向上弯曲的，上翘的; 上弯部分 |A bend-up is a part of a reinforcing bar that is bent upwards at a specific angle, typically used in reinforced concrete construction to resist shear forces and improve structural integrity.| The bend-up bars in the foundation help to distribute the load.  地基中的上弯钢筋有助于分散荷载。| synonym: upturned,  curved upwards | antonym: bent-down, downturned | adjective, noun

bend-up:/ˈbɛnd ʌp/ | n.| 上弯部分 |The bend-up section of the rebar provides extra support. 钢筋的上弯部分提供了额外的支撑。| synonym: upturn, upward bend | antonym: downturn, downward bend | noun
