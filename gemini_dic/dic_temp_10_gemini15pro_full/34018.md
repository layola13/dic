citizenly:/ˈsɪtɪzənli/
adjective
公民的；市民的；像公民的；有公民责任感的
characteristic of a citizen; behaving like a good citizen.
例句：He displayed citizenly behavior by reporting the crime. 他表现出公民的责任感，举报了犯罪行为。

例句：Her citizenly duty led her to vote in every election. 她的公民责任感促使她在每次选举中都投票。

近义词：civic, public-spirited, responsible
反义词：uncivil, irresponsible, antisocial
词性：adjective
