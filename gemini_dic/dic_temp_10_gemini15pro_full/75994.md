funny:/ˈfʌni/| adj.|有趣的，好笑的；奇怪的，古怪的|Causing laughter or amusement; strange or unusual.|例句：That's a funny joke! 那真是个好笑的笑话！|例句：He had a funny feeling that something was wrong. 他有一种奇怪的感觉，觉得有什么不对劲。|近义词：amusing, humorous, comical, odd, peculiar, strange|反义词：serious, sad, normal, ordinary|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *funnien (“to be glad, rejoice”), from *funne (“fun, enjoyment, pleasure”), of uncertain origin. Possibly related to fonnen (“to be foolish, act like a fool”).
