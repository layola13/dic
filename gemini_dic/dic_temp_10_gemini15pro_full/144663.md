outwriggles:/ˌaʊtˈrɪɡəlz/
|v.|胜过，扭动胜过|To surpass or defeat someone by wriggling or twisting more effectively.|The snake outwriggled its prey and escaped the grasp of the eagle. 蛇扭动身体胜过了猎物，逃脱了老鹰的抓捕。|Synonyms: outmaneuver, outwit, surpass|Antonyms:  be defeated, lose to|Verb

outwriggles:/ˌaʊtˈrɪɡəlz/
|v.|扭动得比...厉害|To wriggle or twist more vigorously than someone or something else.|The worm outwriggled the other worms in the compost bin.  那条蠕虫在堆肥箱里比其他蠕虫扭动得更厉害。|Synonyms: squirm more, wiggle more|Antonyms: be less agile, be less flexible|Verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From "out-" (meaning exceeding or surpassing) + "wriggle" (to twist and turn the body with quick writhing movements).
