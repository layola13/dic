ignominy:/ˈɪɡnəmɪni/ /ˈɪɡnɑːməni/ | n. |耻辱，羞辱，不名誉| Public disgrace or shame. |  He suffered the ignominy of being expelled from school. 他遭受了被学校开除的耻辱。

|Synonym: shame, disgrace, humiliation, dishonor, degradation, abasement|Antonym: honor, glory, dignity, respect, esteem|Noun|

ignominies:/ˈɪɡnəmɪniz/ /ˈɪɡnɑːməniz/ | n. |耻辱，羞辱，不名誉（ignominy的复数形式）| Plural form of ignominy, referring to multiple instances of public disgrace or shame. | She endured many ignominies throughout her life. 她一生中忍受了许多耻辱。

|Synonym: shames, disgraces, humiliations, dishonors |Antonym: honors, glories, dignities, respects|Noun|
