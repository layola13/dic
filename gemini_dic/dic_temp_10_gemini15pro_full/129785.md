non-figurative: /nɒnˈfɪɡərətɪv/ (UK) /nɑːnˈfɪɡjərətɪv/ (US) | adj. | 非比喻的，字面的 | Not using figures of speech; literal. Representing actual things or events rather than abstract ideas or emotions. |  The witness gave a non-figurative account of what she saw, sticking to the plain facts.  目击者对所见所闻进行了非比喻性的描述，坚持简单的事实。

The instructions were clear and non-figurative, leaving no room for misinterpretation. 说明书清晰明了，没有比喻，没有误解的余地。 | 近义词: literal, factual, concrete, objective | 反义词: figurative, metaphorical, symbolic, abstract | 词性: adjective
