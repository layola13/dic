berserker:/bərˈsɜːrkər/ | n. | 狂战士；暴怒的人 |  A fierce, wild warrior of the ancient Scandinavian Vikings, legendary for fighting in an uncontrollable frenzy.  A person behaving in a wildly aggressive or unrestrained manner. | The berserkers charged into battle, heedless of danger. 狂战士冲入战场，不顾危险。 He became a berserker when he found his car had been vandalized. 当他发现他的车被破坏了时，他变成了一个暴怒的人。 | 近义词: maniac, wildman, madman, fiend | 反义词: pacifist, calm person | 词性: noun
