dicers:/ˈdaɪsərz/| n.|骰子手；切丁者|People who dice, or cut into small cubes.  Those who play with dice.|The dicers gathered around the table, their eyes glued to the rolling dice. 骰子手们围在桌子旁，眼睛紧盯着滚动的骰子。

The chef instructed the kitchen staff to be meticulous dicers, ensuring uniformity in the vegetable cuts.  厨师指示厨房工作人员要细致地切丁，确保蔬菜切块均匀。|近义词：gamblers, players (for those who play dice); cutters, choppers (for those who cut into cubes)|反义词: (No direct antonym, depends on context.  Could be "collectors" for gamblers, or "leavers whole" for cutters) |词性:noun
