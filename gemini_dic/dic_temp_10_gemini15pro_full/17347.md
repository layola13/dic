beachheads:/ˈbiːtʃhɛdz/
n.
滩头阵地，立足点，根据地
A defended position on a beach taken from enemy forces by an attacking party landing from the sea, from which an advance can be made.  A secure initial position that has been gained and can be used for further advancement.
The troops established a beachhead and began to move inland. 部队建立了滩头阵地，并开始向内陆推进。
The company gained a beachhead in the European market. 该公司在欧洲市场获得了立足点。
synonyms: foothold, bridgehead, base, starting point, lodgement
antonyms: retreat, withdrawal
noun
