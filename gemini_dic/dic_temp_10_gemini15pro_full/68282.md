falafel:/fəˈlɑːfəl/ | n. | 炸鹰嘴豆丸子，沙拉三明治 | Deep-fried balls or patties made from ground chickpeas or fava beans. | Example: I had a delicious falafel sandwich for lunch. 我午餐吃了一个美味的炸鹰嘴豆丸子三明治。 | Example:  She prefers falafels with hummus and tahini sauce. 她更喜欢配鹰嘴豆泥和芝麻酱的炸鹰嘴豆丸子。 | Synonym:  fava bean fritters | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "meatball" as a contrasting food) | Noun
