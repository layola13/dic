outland:/ˈaʊtlænd/
n.
外国; 异域; 边远地区
A foreign land; a strange or remote place.
The book tells of their adventures in an outland. 这本书讲述了他们在异域的冒险经历。
近义词：foreign land, strange land, remote area, wilderness
反义词：homeland, native land


outlandish:/aʊtˈlændɪʃ/
adj.
古怪的; 奇异的; 异国情调的;
Looking or sounding bizarre or unfamiliar.
She wore an outlandish costume. 她穿着一套古怪的服装。
近义词：bizarre, strange, peculiar, eccentric, exotic, unusual
反义词：ordinary, conventional, normal, common


词源：
From Middle English *outeland, from Old English *ūtlend, equivalent to out +‎ land. Cognate with Dutch buitenland (“foreign country”), German Ausland (“foreign country”).
