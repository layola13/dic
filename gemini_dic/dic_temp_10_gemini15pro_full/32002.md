chenin: /ʃɛˈnɛ̃/ (US), /ˈʃɛnɪn/ (UK) | n. | 白诗南（一种白葡萄品种） | A white grape variety originating in the Loire Valley of France, known for its versatility and ability to produce wines ranging from dry to sweet. |  This Chenin Blanc has a lovely aroma of honey and quince.  这白诗南带有蜂蜜和榅桲的迷人香气。

Chenin blanc is a popular varietal in South Africa.  白诗南是南非的热门品种。| 近义词:  Pineau de la Loire (another name for Chenin Blanc in some regions) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
