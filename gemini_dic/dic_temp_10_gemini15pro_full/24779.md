bros:/broʊz/ | n. | 兄弟们；哥们儿 | Informal plural of "bro," short for "brother." Used to address male friends or a group of men in a casual and friendly way. | Example: Hey bros, what are you doing tonight? 嘿，兄弟们，今晚你们要做什么？| Example:  The bros went out for a beer after work.  这帮兄弟下班后出去喝啤酒了。| Synonym: dudes, guys, pals, mates | Antonym: enemies, foes | Noun

bro:/broʊ/ | n. | 兄弟；哥们儿 | Short for "brother." Used to address a male friend in a casual and friendly way. | Example: Hey bro, what's up? 嘿，兄弟，怎么了？| Example: That's my bro from college. 那是我大学的兄弟。 | Synonym: dude, guy, pal, mate | Antonym: enemy, foe | Noun
