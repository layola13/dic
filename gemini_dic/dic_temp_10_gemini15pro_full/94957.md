in-service:/ˈɪnˈsɜːrvɪs/ | adj. | 在职的，在役的 |  Relating to or provided for employees currently working in a particular profession, especially in order to develop their skills. |  The teachers attended an in-service training course on new teaching methods.  老师们参加了一个关于新教学方法的在职培训课程。| Synonyms: on-the-job, in-house | Antonyms: pre-service, out-of-service | adjective

in-service:/ˈɪnˈsɜːrvɪs/ | n. | 在职培训 | A period of training or education provided to employees while they are working. | The in-service was helpful for updating our skills.  这次在职培训对更新我们的技能很有帮助。| Synonyms: professional development, continuing education | Antonyms: N/A | noun
