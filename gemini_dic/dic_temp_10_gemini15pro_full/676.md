abscess:/ˈæbses/ (US), /ˈæbsɪs/ (UK)| n.|脓肿|A localized collection of pus surrounded by inflamed tissue.|The doctor drained the abscess on his back. 他背上的脓肿被医生排掉了。

The dentist said the abscess on my gum needed immediate attention. 牙医说我牙龈上的脓肿需要立即处理。

近义词:boil, ulcer, sore, infection|反义词:|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin abscessus, past participle of abscedere "to go away, withdraw," from ab- "away" + -cedere "to go."  The original meaning referred to the formation of pus as a "withdrawal" of bad humors from the body.
