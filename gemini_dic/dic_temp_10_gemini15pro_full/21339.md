bloomery:/ˈbluːməri/| n.|锻铁炉，冶铁炉|A bloomery is a type of furnace historically used for smelting iron from its oxides.  The bloomery was the earliest form of iron smelting.|The archaeologist discovered the remains of an ancient bloomery.|Synonyms: forge, furnace, smelter|Antonyms: N/A|noun
