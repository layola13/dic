overcanopy: /ˈoʊvərˌkænəpi/ | n. | 上层树冠，林冠上层 | The overcanopy of the rainforest teems with life. 雨林的上层树冠充满了生命。| The researchers studied the biodiversity of the overcanopy in the Amazon. 研究人员研究了亚马逊地区林冠上层的生物多样性。 | 近义词: emergent layer, canopy top | 反义词: understory, forest floor | 词性: 名词

词源:  "over-" (above) + "canopy" (the uppermost layer of branches in a forest).  "Over" comes from Old English "ofer," and "canopy" comes from Middle English "canope," ultimately from Greek "kōnōpeion" (mosquito net), referring to a cloth covering.
