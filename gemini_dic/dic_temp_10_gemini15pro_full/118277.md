merchet:/ˈmɜːrtʃɪt/
n.
婚姻税，赎身金
A fine paid by a tenant to his lord for permission to give his daughter in marriage.
In medieval times, a lord could demand merchet from his serfs when their daughters married.  在中世纪，领主可以在他们的女儿结婚时向他们的农奴索取婚姻税。
This archaic custom of merchet was a significant source of income for some feudal lords.  这种古老的婚姻税习俗是某些封建领主的重要收入来源。
Synonyms: fine, levy, tax, fee
Antonyms:  recompense, reward
