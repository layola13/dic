megafauna:/ˈmɛɡəˌfɔːnə/| n. |巨型动物群|Large or giant animals of a particular region or time period.  Specifically, animals with an adult body weight of over 45 kg (roughly 100 pounds).|例句：The Pleistocene megafauna included mammoths and saber-toothed cats. 更新世巨型动物群包括猛犸象和剑齿虎。

|例句：Australia's megafauna suffered greatly after the arrival of humans.  人类到来之后，澳大利亚的巨型动物群遭受了巨大的打击。|近义词：large animals, giant animals|反义词:microfauna|词性:noun
