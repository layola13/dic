deductibility:/dɪˌdʌktɪˈbɪləti/
n.|可扣除性；扣除额|The quality of being deductible, especially from taxable income.  This refers to expenses that can be subtracted from gross income to reduce the amount of income subject to taxation.|例句：The deductibility of charitable donations is a significant tax advantage.  慈善捐款的可抵扣性是一项重要的税收优惠。|例句：He questioned the deductibility of these expenses. 他对这些费用的可扣除性提出了质疑。|近义词：allowance, exemption, write-off|反义词：non-deductibility, inclusion|词性：noun
