desertrice: /ˈdɛzərt raɪs/ | n. | 沙漠稻 | A type of rice that is grown in arid or semi-arid regions, often requiring less water than traditional rice varieties. It is also known as drought-resistant rice or dryland rice.  |  Example: Desertrice is a promising crop for food security in drought-prone areas. 沙漠稻是在干旱易发地区保障粮食安全的有希望的作物。 | Example:  Researchers are developing new strains of desertrice to improve yields. 研究人员正在开发新的沙漠稻品种以提高产量。 | Synonym: drought-resistant rice, dryland rice | Antonym:  water-intensive rice | Noun
