outgreens:/ˌaʊtˈɡriːnz/
|v.|胜过，在环保方面超过|To surpass someone in terms of environmentally friendly practices or beliefs.|She completely outgreens me with her zero-waste lifestyle. 她零浪费的生活方式让我自愧不如。|近义词：outdo, surpass, excel|反义词：lag behind, fall short|词性:动词

Etymology:
"out-" (prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing) + "green" (adjective related to environmentalism)
