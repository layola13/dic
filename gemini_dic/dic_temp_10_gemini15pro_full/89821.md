hornednesses:/ˈhɔːrnɪdnɪsɪz/
noun
有角的状态；角的數量
The state or quality of having horns; the number or quantity of horns.
The hornednesses of the various species of antelope differed significantly. 不同种类的羚羊的角的数量差异很大。
near synonyms: hornedness, cornification
antonym: hornlessness
n.
