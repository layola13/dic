half-fascinating:/ˌhæfˈfæsɪneɪtɪŋ/
adj.
一半着迷的；略感有趣的；有点意思的
Partially fascinating; interesting to some degree, but not completely captivating.
The documentary was half-fascinating, but ultimately too long and repetitive. 这部纪录片有点意思，但最终太长太重复了。
Near Synonyms: mildly interesting, somewhat engaging, partly captivating
Antonyms: boring, tedious, uninteresting, dull
Word Origin:  A combination of "half" and "fascinating." "Half" acts as an adverb modifying "fascinating."
