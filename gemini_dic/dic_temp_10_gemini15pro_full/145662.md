overcontrite:/ˌoʊvərˈkɒnˌtraɪt/ | adj. | 过分忏悔的，悔恨过度的 | Excessively contrite; too apologetic.  Showing remorse that is excessive or exaggerated. |  He was overcontrite for such a minor offense. 他对这样一个小错误感到过分内疚。| 近义词: excessively apologetic, overly remorseful, too penitent | 反义词: unrepentant, remorseless, unapologetic | 词性: adjective

词源:
From "over-" (excessive) + "contrite" (feeling or expressing remorse). "Contrite" comes from the Latin "contritus," past participle of "conterere" (to grind, bruise), figuratively meaning "worn down by grief or remorse."
