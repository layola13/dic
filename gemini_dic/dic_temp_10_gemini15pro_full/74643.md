free-fell:/friːˈfɛl/
verb
自由落体，自由下降
past tense of free-fall: descended rapidly under the force of gravity.
The skydiver free-fell for several seconds before deploying his parachute. 跳伞员在打开降落伞之前自由落体了几秒钟。
Near synonyms:  plummet, plunge, dive
Antonyms: ascend, rise, climb
