contentiousness:/kənˈtɛnʃəsnəs/
n.
好争论，爱争吵
a tendency to argue or dispute
His contentiousness often led to arguments with his colleagues. 他的好争辩经常导致与同事发生争执。

近义词:argumentativeness, quarrelsomeness, disputatiousness
反义词:agreeableness, amiability, peacefulness
词性:名词


contentious:/kənˈtɛnʃəs/
adj.
有争议的；引起争论的
likely to cause disagreement or argument
The contentious issue of immigration reform has divided the country.  移民改革这个有争议的问题分裂了国家。
好争论的；爱争吵的
given to arguing or disputing
He was a contentious man, always ready for a fight. 他是一个好争论的人，随时准备吵架。

近义词:controversial, disputed, debatable, quarrelsome, argumentative
反义词:uncontroversial, undisputed, agreeable, amiable, peaceful
词性:形容词

词源:
From Late Latin contentiosus "quarrelsome," from Latin contentus, past participle of contendere "to strive, struggle, contend," from com- "together" + tendere "to stretch"
