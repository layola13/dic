meetups:/ˈmiːtʌps/| n. |聚会，见面会|Informal gatherings, especially of people with shared interests, typically organized online or through social media. |例句：I regularly attend tech meetups to network with other professionals. 我定期参加科技聚会，与其他专业人士建立联系。

例句：There are a lot of photography meetups in the city where you can learn new skills. 城市里有很多摄影聚会，你可以在那里学习新技能。|近义词：gatherings, get-togethers, assemblies, meetings|反义词:separations, dispersals|词性:noun
