omnilingual:/ˌɒmniˈlɪŋɡwəl/
adjective
通晓所有语言的; 全语言的
able to speak and understand all languages.
She created an omnilingual translation device that could interpret any language. 她创造了一个可以翻译任何语言的全语言翻译设备。
Synonyms: polyglot, multilingual
Antonyms: monolingual
