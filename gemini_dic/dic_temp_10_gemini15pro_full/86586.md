heliochromic:/ˌhiːliəˈkrəʊmɪk/
adjective
日光变色的，光致变色的
Relating to or denoting a substance that changes colour when exposed to sunlight.
The heliochromic ink changes from clear to blue in sunlight.  日光变色墨水在阳光下由透明变为蓝色。
The company developed a new heliochromic material for sunglasses.  该公司开发了一种新的用于太阳镜的光致变色材料。
Synonyms: photochromic, light-sensitive
Antonyms: colorfast, non-photochromic
词性: adjective
