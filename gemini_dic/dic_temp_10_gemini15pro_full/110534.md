limeades:/ˌlaɪˈmeɪdz/ | n. | 酸橙汁（复数） | A carbonated drink similar to lemonade but flavored with lime juice.  A refreshing beverage made with lime juice, sugar, and water, often carbonated. |  例句：
We ordered two limeades and a plate of nachos.  我们点了两杯酸橙汁和一盘烤干酪辣味玉米片。

The limeades were perfectly tart and sweet. 这些酸橙汁酸甜可口，恰到好处。

| 近义词：lime rickeys, lime sodas | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps something like "hot drinks" in certain contexts) | 词性: noun
