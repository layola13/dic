hexamerous:/hɛkˈsæmərəs/
adjective
六数的，六基数的
Having six parts, especially six parts in each whorl of a flower.
The hexamerous flowers are characteristic of this family of plants. 这种植物的科的特点就是有六基数的花。
Near Synonyms: six-part, six-membered
Antonyms:  pentamerous, tetramerous
词性: adjective
