gigabit:/ˈɡɪɡəbɪt/| n. |千兆位|A unit of information equal to one thousand million (10^9) bits or 125 megabytes.|The internet connection has a speed of one gigabit per second.  互联网连接速度为每秒一千兆位。|近义词：Gb, Gbit|反义词:kilobit, megabit|noun
