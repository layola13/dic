mercantilely:/mɜːrˈkæntaɪlli/ /mərˈkænˌtaɪl·li/| adv.| 商业地，重商主义地 |In a mercantile manner; from a business perspective, especially with regard to profit.  Focusing on trade and profit.| The company acted mercantilely, prioritizing profit over ethical considerations.  该公司以商业化的方式行事，将利润置于道德考虑之上。|近义词：commercially, tradingly|反义词：charitably, philanthropically|词性:adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From mercantile + -ly.  Mercantile comes from Late Latin *mercantilis*, from *mercans*, present participle of *mercari* "to trade," from *merx* "merchandise."
