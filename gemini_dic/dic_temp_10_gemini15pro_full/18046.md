begrease: /bɪˈɡriːz/
verb
涂满油脂;  使沾满油污
To cover or smear with grease.
The mechanic begreased the engine. 机械师给引擎涂满了油脂。
His hands were begreased after working on the car.  修完车后，他的手上沾满了油污。
近义词: grease, lubricate, oil
反义词: clean, degrease
词性: verb

词源:  be- (prefix meaning "thoroughly" or "all over") + grease (noun and verb)
