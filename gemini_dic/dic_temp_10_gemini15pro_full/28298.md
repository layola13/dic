capulin:/kəˈpuːlɪn/ | n. |墨西哥櫻桃，卡普林櫻桃|A type of cherry (Prunus salicifolia) native to Mexico and Central America, also the name of the tree itself. The fruit is dark red to almost black when ripe and is often used to make jams, jellies, and liqueurs.  The word is derived from Nahuatl, an indigenous language of Mexico. |例句：The capulin trees were laden with ripe fruit. 卡普林櫻桃树上果实累累。

例句：She made a delicious capulin jam. 她做了一罐美味的卡普林櫻桃酱。|近义词：cherry, Mexican cherry|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
