minium:/ˈmɪniəm/| n.|朱红色，铅丹|A bright reddish orange pigment originally made by roasting lead, and consisting chiefly of lead tetroxide (Pb3O4). It is used as a pigment, especially in anti-corrosive paints.|The minium on the gate had faded over the years. 大门上的铅丹已经褪色多年了。|近义词：red lead, lead tetroxide|反义词: None|词性:noun

minium:/ˈmɪniəm/| adj.|朱红色的|Of a bright reddish orange color.|The minium-painted walls of the temple were striking. 寺庙朱红色的墙壁引人注目。|近义词：vermilion, scarlet|反义词: None|词性:adjective
