garnishment:/ˈɡɑːrnɪʃmənt/| n.|扣押令，扣押（工资）|A legal process that allows part of a person's wages to be withheld for payment of a debt.|例句：His wages are under garnishment for child support. 他的工资因子女抚养费而被扣押。|例句：The creditor obtained a garnishment order against the debtor's bank account. 债权人获得了针对债务人银行账户的扣押令。|近义词：attachment, seizure, levy|反义词:release|noun
