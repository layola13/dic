devourment:/dɪˈvaʊəmənt/ | n. |吞噬，吞食；狼吞虎咽 | The act of devouring something completely.  The rapid or violent consumption of something. | The devourment of the cake by the children was astonishing. 孩子们狼吞虎咽地吃掉蛋糕的样子令人惊讶。| synonyms: consumption, ingestion, gorging | antonyms: abstinence, fasting | noun
