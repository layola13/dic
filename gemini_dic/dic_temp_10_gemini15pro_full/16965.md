basta:/ˈbɑːstə/ /ˈbæstə/| int. |够了，停止，住手|Used to express that one has had enough of something and wants it to stop. |"Basta! I'm tired of listening to your complaints." 够了！我厌倦了听你的抱怨。|"Basta! I don't want to argue anymore." 住手！我不想再争论了。|近义词:enough, stop, quit|反义词:continue, go on|词性:interjection
词源: From Italian *basta* "enough," from Vulgar Latin *bastat*, third-person singular present indicative of Latin *bastare* "to suffice, be enough."

PHRASE->
(While "basta" doesn't typically form phrases in the same way English words do, it can be used in conjunction with other words for emphasis.)

"Basta cosi!" = Enough like this!/Enough of this!
"Basta ya!" = Enough already!
"Oh, basta!" = Oh, enough!
"Basta, per favore." = Enough, please.
"Ma basta!" = But enough!/Come on, enough!


注意事项:

"Basta" is primarily used in informal contexts.  While understood in many Spanish-speaking regions, it's most commonly associated with Italian.  It conveys a sense of finality and impatience.
