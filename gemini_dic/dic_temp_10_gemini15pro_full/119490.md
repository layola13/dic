microp:/ˈmaɪkrɒp/ (UK) /ˈmaɪkrɑːp/ (US)| n. |微操作; 微指令|A microoperation is an elementary operation performed by the processor. A microinstruction is a sequence of microoperations. |The CPU executes a series of microps to carry out an instruction. 中央处理器执行一系列微操作来执行一条指令。| Microinstructions control the flow of data within the CPU. 微指令控制CPU内部的数据流。 |Synonyms: microinstruction (sometimes), micro command| Antonyms: macrooperation, macroinstruction|noun
