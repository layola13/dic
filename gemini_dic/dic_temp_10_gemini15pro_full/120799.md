misassembled:/ˌmɪsəˈsɛmbld/
adjective
错误组装的，装配错误的
incorrectly assembled; put together wrongly.
The product was faulty because it was misassembled in the factory.  这个产品有缺陷，因为它在工厂装配错误。
The misassembled furniture wobbled precariously.  装配错误的家具摇摇晃晃，很不稳固。
synonyms:  incorrectly assembled, wrongly put together, improperly constructed, badly built, faulty assembled
antonyms: correctly assembled, properly constructed, well-built
词性: adjective
