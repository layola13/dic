impressing:/ɪmˈprɛsɪŋ/| v. | 给…留下深刻印象；使铭记 |  present participle of impress.  Making someone feel admiration or respect. | Her knowledge of the subject impressed me. 她对这门学科的了解给我留下了深刻的印象。| synonyms: affecting, influencing, swaying, moving | antonyms:  disappointing, discouraging, dissuading| verb

impressing:/ɪmˈprɛsɪŋ/| adj. |  令人印象深刻的| Striking; making a strong impact. |  The view from the mountaintop was truly impressing.  从山顶望下去的景色真是令人印象深刻。| synonyms:  impressive, remarkable, striking, memorable | antonyms: unimpressive, forgettable, ordinary | adjective
