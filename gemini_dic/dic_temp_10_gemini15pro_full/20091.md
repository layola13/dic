biosignature:/ˌbaɪoʊˈsɪɡnətʃə(r)/| n.|生物签名，生命印记|A measurable indicator of the presence of life, past or present.|The discovery of methane in the atmosphere could be a biosignature. 在大气中发现甲烷可能是一种生物签名。|Synonyms: life marker, biological indicator|Antonyms: None|noun
