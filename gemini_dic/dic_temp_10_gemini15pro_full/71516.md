flannelling:/ˈflænəlɪŋ/| n. |闲聊，胡扯，敷衍|The politician was accused of flannelling during the debate, avoiding any direct answers to the questions. 这位政客在辩论中被指责胡扯，回避了所有问题的直接回答。|例句：He was just flannelling to avoid answering the difficult question. 他只是在胡扯以避免回答难题。|近义词：waffling, rambling, prevaricating|反义词:being direct, being forthright|noun

flannelling:/ˈflænəlɪŋ/| v. |闲聊，胡扯，敷衍|He flanneled for a few minutes, hoping the interviewer wouldn't notice he didn't know the answer. 他胡扯了几分钟，希望面试官不会注意到他不知道答案。 |例句：Stop flannelling and get to the point! 别胡扯了，说重点！|近义词：waffling, rambling, prevaricating|反义词:being direct, being forthright|verb
