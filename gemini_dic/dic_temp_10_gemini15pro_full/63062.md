enroll:/ɪnˈroʊl/ (US) /ɪnˈrəʊl/ (UK)| v. n. |注册，登记；入学；参军| To officially register as a member of an institution or a student in a course. |例句：I enrolled in the online course. 我注册了在线课程。


例句：She enrolled her children in a summer camp. 她给她的孩子们报名参加了夏令营。

近义词：register, sign up, join, enlist|反义词: withdraw, unenroll, drop out |词性:动词, 名词

enroll:/ɪnˈroʊl/ (US) /ɪnˈrəʊl/ (UK)| n. |注册，登记；入学；参军|The act of enrolling or being enrolled. |例句：Enrollment in the class is limited to 20 students. 这个班级的注册人数限制在20名学生。


例句：The school saw a significant increase in enrollment this year.  今年学校的入学人数显著增加。

近义词：registration, admission|反义词: withdrawal |词性:名词
