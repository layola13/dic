inconsequential:/ˌɪnkɒn.sɪˈkwen.ʃəl/ /ˌɪnkɑːn.səˈkwen.ʃəl/
adjective
无关紧要的；不重要的
Not important or significant.

例句：
The cost was inconsequential compared to the potential benefits.
与潜在的收益相比，成本微不足道。

Her opinion on the matter was inconsequential.
她对此事的意见无关紧要。

近义词：trivial, insignificant, negligible, unimportant, petty
反义词：important, significant, consequential, crucial, vital
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + consequential.  "Consequential" itself comes from consequence, ultimately from Latin consequi "to follow after."
