nonstainable:/ˌnɒnˈsteɪnəbl/ | adj. | 不可染色的，不沾污的 | Not easily stained; resistant to staining. | This tablecloth is nonstainable, so spills are easy to clean up.  这块桌布是不沾污的，所以很容易清理洒出来的东西。| The new carpet is nonstainable and perfect for families with young children.  新的地毯是不易染色的，非常适合有小孩的家庭。 | 近义词: stain-resistant, spot-resistant, stainproof | 反义词: stainable, staining | 词性: 形容词

词源:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + stainable (adjective from "stain" + "-able" suffix indicating capability)
