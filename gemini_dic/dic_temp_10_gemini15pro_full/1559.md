achievers:/əˈtʃiːvərz/
n.
成功者，获得成就的人
People who have accomplished something significant; successful people.
The awards ceremony celebrated the achievers in various fields.  颁奖典礼表彰了各个领域的成功人士。

The company is looking to recruit high achievers. 该公司正在寻找招聘高成就者。

近义词: winners, victors, champions, high-fliers
反义词: failures, losers, underachievers
词性: 名词(plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "achieve" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting a person who performs an action). "Achieve" comes from Old French "achever" meaning "to finish, complete, bring to a head," from Vulgar Latin "*accapitare,"  from Latin "ad" (to) + "caput" (head).
