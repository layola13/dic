coitally:/koʊˈɪtəli/
adverb
性交地，通过性交
Relating to or involving sexual intercourse.
The virus can be transmitted coitally.  该病毒可以通过性交传播。

近义词: sexually
反义词: nonsexually
词性: adverb

词源: From Late Latin *coitālis, from coitāre (“to copulate”), frequentative of coeō (“to come together”).
