dosshouse:/ˈdɒshaʊs/ (UK) /ˈdɑːshaʊs/ (US)| n. |廉价客栈；收容所|A cheap, often dirty lodging house for homeless people. |例句：He spent a few nights in a dosshouse before finding a more permanent place to stay. 他在找到一个更固定的住所之前，在廉价客栈里住了几个晚上。

例句：The dosshouse was crowded and smelled of stale beer.  这间廉价客栈拥挤不堪，散发着陈啤酒的味道。

近义词：hostel, flophouse, shelter|反义词:hotel, luxury accommodation|词性:名词
