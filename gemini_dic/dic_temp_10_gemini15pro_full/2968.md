adzukis: /əˈdzuːkiːz/
n.
红豆（复数）
Small, round, dark red beans used in Japanese cooking.
I added adzukis to the rice for extra flavor. 我在米饭里加了红豆来增加风味。
She made a delicious dessert with sweet adzukis. 她用甜红豆做了一道美味的甜点。

近义词: red beans, red mung beans
反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific type of bean)
词性: noun (plural)
