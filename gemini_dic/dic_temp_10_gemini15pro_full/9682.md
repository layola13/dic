antitheism:/ˈæntiˌθiːɪzəm/ /ˈæntiˌθiːɪzəm/
n.|反神论；对抗神论|Active opposition to theism; the belief that theism is harmful.  It goes beyond mere atheism (the absence of belief in a god) by actively opposing it.|Antitheism is a growing movement in some parts of the world.  反神论在世界某些地区正在发展壮大。|Antitheism expresses not just disbelief in gods, but opposition to the concept itself. 反神论不仅表达了对上帝的不相信，而且反对上帝这个概念本身。|近义词:militant atheism, strong atheism|反义词:theism, deism|词性:noun
