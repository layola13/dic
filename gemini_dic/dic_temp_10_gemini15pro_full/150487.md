pantisocratic:/ˌpænˌtɪsəˈkrætɪk/ | adj. | 平等主义的；乌托邦式的；泛爱主义的 | Relating to or advocating pantisocracy, a system of government where all rule equally.  It often carries connotations of idealism and utopianism. |  Samuel Taylor Coleridge and Robert Southey envisioned a pantisocratic community in America.  柯勒律治和骚塞设想在美国建立一个泛爱主义的社区。| The group’s pantisocratic ideals eventually proved impractical. 这个团体的泛爱主义理想最终被证明是不切实际的。 | Synonym: egalitarian, utopian, idealistic | Antonym: authoritarian, hierarchical, elitist | adjective
