alibiing:/ˈælɪbaɪɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
提供不在场证明；为...辩解
The act of providing an alibi, or an excuse, especially to avoid blame.
He was alibiing for his friend who was suspected of the robbery. 他在为涉嫌抢劫的朋友提供不在场证明。
Synonym: excusing, justifying
Antonym: accusing, incriminating
词性: 动词(现在分词)
