atokous:/ˈætəkəs/ | adj. | 无性生殖的，非胎生的 | Lacking a placenta; not viviparous.  Developing without metamorphosis. | Example:  Some species of insects are atokous, laying eggs that hatch into nymphs. 一些昆虫物种是无性生殖的，产下的卵孵化成若虫。| Example: Atokous parthenogenesis is a form of asexual reproduction. 无性生殖孤雌生殖是一种无性繁殖的形式。 | Synonym: oviparous, nonplacental | Antonym: viviparous, placental | word type: adjective
