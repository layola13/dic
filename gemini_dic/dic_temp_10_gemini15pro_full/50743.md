derring-do:/ˈdɛrɪŋ ˈduː/| n.|勇敢的行为；大胆的举动|Audacious or adventurous action; feats of bravery or daring.|例句：Tales of derring-do are always exciting.  勇敢的故事总是令人兴奋的。

例句：He was known for his derring-do in battle. 他因在战斗中的英勇行为而闻名。|近义词：bravery, courage, audacity, boldness, valor, heroism|反义词:cowardice, timidity, fearfulness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *derring do*, from derring (present participle of *durren*, to dare) + do (noun, deed).  So literally, "daring (to) do".
