meningitis:/ˌmɛnɪnˈdʒaɪtɪs/
|n.|脑膜炎|Inflammation of the meninges, the membranes surrounding the brain and spinal cord.  It is usually caused by a viral or bacterial infection.|Bacterial meningitis is a serious illness that can be fatal.|近义词:encephalitis (though this refers to brain inflammation, not just the meninges)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin *meningitis*, from Ancient Greek *μῆνιγξ* (*mēninx*, “membrane”) + *-ῖτις* (*-ītis*, medical suffix indicating inflammation).
