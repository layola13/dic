nonstudiously:/nɒnˈstjuːdiəsli/ | adv. | 非勤奋地，不认真地 | Not in a studious manner; without serious attention or application to learning. | He nonstudiously flipped through the textbook, more interested in the pictures than the words. 他漫不经心地翻阅课本，对图片比文字更感兴趣。 |  She nonstudiously glanced at the notes before the exam, knowing she hadn't prepared enough.  她在考试前漫不经心地看了一眼笔记，知道自己准备不足。 | 近义词: carelessly, idly, inattentively, casually | 反义词: studiously, diligently, attentively, carefully | 词性: 副词

词源:
non- (prefix): not, opposite of  (Latin origin)
studious (adjective): spending a lot of time studying or reading (Latin origin: studiosus)
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs
