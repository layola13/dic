hindered:/ˈhɪndərd/| adj.|受阻的，受妨碍的|Slowed down or prevented from making progress.  This describes something or someone whose progress is being negatively affected by something else.|例句：The heavy rain hindered our progress.  暴雨阻碍了我们的前进。

例句：Her career was hindered by her lack of education.  她缺乏教育阻碍了她的职业发展。|近义词：impeded, obstructed, hampered, restricted|反义词：aided, assisted, facilitated, helped|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *hindren, from Old English hindrian (“to hinder, prevent, restrain, withhold”), from Proto-Germanic *hindrōną (“to hinder, prevent, hold back”), from Proto-Indo-European *kent- (“behind”). Cognate with Dutch hinderen (“to hinder”), German hindern (“to hinder”), Gothic 𐌷𐌹𐌽𐌳𐍂𐌰𐌽 (hindran, “to hinder”).
