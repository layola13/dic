archencephalon:/ɑːrkɛnˈsɛfəlɒn/ | n. | 前脑 | The anteriormost of the three primary divisions of the developing vertebrate brain or the corresponding part of the adult brain that includes especially the cerebral hemispheres. | The archencephalon develops into the cerebrum, which is responsible for higher-level cognitive functions.  前脑发展成大脑，负责高级认知功能。| synonym: forebrain | antonym:  None (It's an anatomical structure, not a concept with an opposite.) | noun


archencephala:/ɑːrkɛnˈsɛfələ/ | n. | 前脑（复数）|  Plural form of archencephalon, referring to multiple instances of the anteriormost part of the brain.  | Studies on archencephala across various species reveal evolutionary trends in brain development. 对不同物种前脑的研究揭示了大脑发育的进化趋势。 | synonym: forebrains | antonym: None | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
