nonrecipients:/ˌnɑːn.rɪˈsɪ.pi.ənts/
n.
未接受者，非接受者
People who do not receive something, such as a benefit, payment, or service.
The study compared the health outcomes of recipients and nonrecipients of the vaccine. 这项研究比较了接种疫苗者和未接种疫苗者的健康结果。
non-beneficiaries, excluded
recipients, beneficiaries
