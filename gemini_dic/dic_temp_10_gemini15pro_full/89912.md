horrors:/ˈhɔːrərz/ | n. | 恐怖，惊骇，令人恐惧的事物 |  A feeling of great fear and shock, or something that causes this feeling.  It is often used in the plural to express a strong emotional reaction to something unpleasant or shocking. |  "She recoiled in horrors at the sight of the mutilated body." 她看到被肢解的尸体吓得魂飞魄散。 | synonyms: terrors, fright, dread, alarm, dismay, panic, fear, trepidation | antonyms: delight, joy, pleasure, calmness, serenity | noun
