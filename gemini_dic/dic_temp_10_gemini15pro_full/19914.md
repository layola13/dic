bio-gasses:/ˈbaɪoʊˌɡæsɪz/
noun
沼气，生物气体
A mixture of gases, primarily methane and carbon dioxide, produced by the breakdown of organic matter in the absence of oxygen.
Bio-gasses can be used as a renewable energy source. 沼气可以用作可再生能源。
Biogas is produced from anaerobic digestion. 沼气是由厌氧消化产生的。
Synonyms: biogas, methane
Antonyms: natural gas (sometimes considered as a contrasting conventional fuel)
