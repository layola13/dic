limey:/ˈlaɪmi/| n.|英国佬（贬义）|A derogatory slang term for a British person, originally referring to British sailors because of the lime juice they drank to prevent scurvy.|He called the Englishman a "limey." 他叫那个英国人“英国佬”。|近义词：Brit, Englishman (if male), Englishwoman (if female)|反义词:None (lacks a direct antonym, as it's a nationality-based slur)|noun
