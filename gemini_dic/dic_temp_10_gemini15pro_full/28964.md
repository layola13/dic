carryall:/ˈkæriɔːl/ (US) /ˈkæriɔːl/ (UK)| n. | 手提包，旅行包；能装很多东西的袋子 | A large bag or case used for carrying a variety of items, especially while traveling. |例句：She packed her clothes and toiletries in a spacious carryall. 她把衣服和洗漱用品装在一个宽敞的旅行包里。

例句：The carryall was overflowing with groceries. 旅行包里装满了食品杂货。|近义词：tote bag, duffel bag, holdall |反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用small bag/purse)|词性:noun
