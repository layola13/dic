overpolitic: /ˌoʊvərˈpɑːlətɪk/ | adj. | 过于政治化的，过分讲究策略的 | Excessively or inappropriately political; too concerned with political maneuvering or strategy.  Characterized by excessive or shrewd political maneuvering. | The decision was seen as overpolitic, prioritizing party interests over public good.  这个决定被认为是过于政治化的，将党派利益置于公共利益之上。| The campaign became overpolitic, focusing more on attacking opponents than on discussing real issues. 这场竞选变得过于政治化，更多地关注攻击对手，而不是讨论真正的问题。 | 近义词: calculating, scheming, maneuvering, strategic, tactical | 反义词: candid, straightforward, artless, ingenuous | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "politic" (adjective meaning "shrewd or prudent in practical matters; diplomatic").
