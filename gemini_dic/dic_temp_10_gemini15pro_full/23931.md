breakups: /ˈbreɪkʌps/ (US), /ˈbreɪkʌps/ (UK)| n. | 分手，破裂 |The act of ending a relationship or partnership.  |例句：The band announced their breakup after 20 years together.  乐队在合作20年后宣布解散。

例句：She's been going through a tough breakup. 她正在经历一段艰难的分手。

近义词：splits, separations, dissolutions|反义词：reconciliations, unions|词性:名词
