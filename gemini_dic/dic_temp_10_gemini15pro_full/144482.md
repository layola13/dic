outthrobbing:/ˌaʊtˈθrɒbɪŋ/
verb
强烈跳动，剧烈搏动
pulsating or beating strongly, especially outwards
Her heart was outthrobbing with excitement. 她的心脏因兴奋而剧烈跳动。
近义词: throbbing, pulsating, pounding
反义词: still, quiet, calm
词性: Verb (present participle)
词源:  A combination of "out-" (meaning "outwardly, exceeding") and "throbbing" (from Old English "throbban," meaning "to beat, pulsate").
