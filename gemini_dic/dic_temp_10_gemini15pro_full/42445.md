counterdeployment:/ˈkaʊntər dɪˈplɔɪmənt/ | n. |反部署，对抗部署| The act of deploying forces or resources to counter or oppose an enemy's deployment.  It involves positioning military units or assets to neutralize or offset the effectiveness of an adversary's forces. | Example 1: The counterdeployment of troops to the border region aimed to deter further aggression.  边境地区的反部署部队旨在阻止进一步的侵略。

Example 2:  The navy initiated a counterdeployment of its fleet in response to the enemy's naval movements. 海军启动了舰队的反部署，以应对敌人的海军行动。 |Synonyms: countermeasure, countermove, counteraction | Antonyms: deployment, advance | noun
