genteelest:/ˌdʒɛnˈtiːlɪst/
adjective
最文雅的，最高贵的
Of or relating to the gentry; aristocratic.  Refined in manner; polite, elegant, and stylish, especially in a way that is affected or superficial.
例句：She spoke in her genteelest voice, careful not to offend anyone. 她用她最文雅的声音说话，小心翼翼地不冒犯任何人。

例句：His genteelest manners were on display at the formal dinner. 他在正式晚宴上展现了他最高贵的举止。

近义词：refined, polished, courteous, aristocratic, elegant, sophisticated
反义词：vulgar, coarse, rude, unrefined, boorish, uncouth
词性: adjective

词源：From French gentil, from Latin gentilis "of the same clan or family," from gens "clan, family, nation, people." The -est suffix denotes the superlative form.
