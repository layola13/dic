corrosivity:/kəˈroʊsɪvɪti/
noun
腐蚀性
The quality of being corrosive; the degree to which something is corrosive.
The corrosivity of the acid damaged the metal.  这种酸的腐蚀性损坏了金属。
Near synonyms:  corrosiveness, causticity
Antonyms:  inertness, harmlessness
