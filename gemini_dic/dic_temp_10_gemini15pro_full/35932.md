cocurator:/koʊˈkjʊəreɪtər/
|n.| 共同策展人 |A person who curates something together with another person or people, such as an exhibition or a collection. They share the responsibility and work collaboratively in the selection, organization, and presentation of the materials.|The museum appointed two cocurators to oversee the new exhibition.| 近义词: joint curator, co-organizer| 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "sole curator")| 词性: noun
