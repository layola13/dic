contrition:/kənˈtrɪʃən/ | n. | 悔恨，痛悔 | The feeling of remorse or guilt for having done something wrong. | She expressed deep contrition for her actions. 她对自己的行为表示深深的忏悔。

He showed no contrition for the crime he committed. 他对犯下的罪行毫无悔意。

| 近义词: remorse, repentance, penitence | 反义词: impenitence, indifference, apathy | 词性: noun
