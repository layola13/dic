oncohematology: /ˌɒŋkoʊˌhiːməˈtɒlədʒi/ /ˌɑːŋkoʊˌhiːməˈtɑːlədʒi/
noun
肿瘤血液学
The study of blood cancers and blood disorders related to cancer.  It combines oncology (the study of cancer) and hematology (the study of blood).
The oncohematology department treats patients with leukemia, lymphoma, and multiple myeloma. 肿瘤血液科治疗白血病、淋巴瘤和多发性骨髓瘤患者。
The advancements in oncohematology have significantly improved survival rates for many blood cancers. 肿瘤血液学的进步显著提高了许多血癌患者的存活率。
Near Synonyms:  hematooncology, cancer hematology
Antonyms:  None (It's a field of study, not a concept with an opposite.)
