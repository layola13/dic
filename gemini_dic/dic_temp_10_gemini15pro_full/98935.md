inter-African:/ˌɪntərˈæfrɪkən/
adjective
非洲之间的；泛非的
Relating to or involving relations between the countries of Africa.
The inter-African trade has seen significant growth in recent years. 近年来，非洲国家间的贸易增长显著。
The organization promotes inter-African cooperation on various issues. 该组织促进非洲国家在各种问题上的合作。
Synonyms:  Pan-African, Afro-centric
Antonyms:  Intra-African (arguably - focuses on within a single African country),  Extra-African
Word type: Adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From "inter-" (prefix meaning "between, among") + "African".  "Inter-" comes from Latin, and "African" ultimately derives from the name of a Berber tribe, the Afri.
