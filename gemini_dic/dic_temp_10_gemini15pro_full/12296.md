ascites: /əˈsaɪtiːz/
noun
腹水
The accumulation of fluid in the peritoneal cavity, causing abdominal swelling.
The patient presented with ascites and jaundice.  病人出现腹水和黄疸。

近义词: hydroperitoneum
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a medical condition like ascites)
词性: noun
