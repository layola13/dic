burschenschaft:/ˈbʊərʃənʃaft/| n.|学生联谊会，兄弟会|A traditional German student fraternity, characterized by its emphasis on dueling, drinking, and nationalist sentiment.|Many 19th-century German nationalists were members of a Burschenschaft. 许多19世纪的德国民族主义者都是学生联谊会的成员。|Synonyms: fraternity, brotherhood|Antonyms: sorority|Noun

Etymology: From German, from Bursche (fellow, lad) + -schaft (suffix forming abstract nouns).
