clientele:/ˌklaɪənˈtɛl/ | n. |顾客，客户；主顾，客户群 | A body of clients; the customers of a professional person or business. | The restaurant has a loyal clientele.  这家餐厅有一批忠实的顾客。| synonyms: customers, patrons, clientele, following | antonyms:  None (It's difficult to have a direct antonym for a group of customers) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French clientèle, from client "client," ultimately from Latin cliens "dependent, protected person, client."
