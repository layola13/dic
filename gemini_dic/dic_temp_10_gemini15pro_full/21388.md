blottier:/ˈblɒtiər/ (UK) /ˈblɑːtiər/ (US)| adj.|更多墨迹的，污迹更多的|Covered with more blots or stains than something else.  Having more blotches.|The first draft was blottier than the final copy.  第一稿比最终稿有更多墨迹。|The painter's newer works are blottier and more abstract.  这位画家的新作品墨迹更多，也更抽象。|近义词：stained, spotted, smeared, smudged|反义词：clean, pristine, immaculate|词性:形容词

词源: From blot + -ier (comparative adjective suffix).  Ultimately from Old Norse *blatta "to strike."
