dactylitis:/ˌdæktɪˈlaɪtɪs/
n.
指炎，趾炎
Inflammation of a finger or toe, typically resulting in sausage-shaped swelling.
Dactylitis is a common symptom of psoriatic arthritis. 指炎是银屑性关节炎的常见症状。
The child presented with dactylitis and fever.  这个孩子表现出指炎和发烧的症状。
近义词:  none (This is a specific medical term)
反义词:  none
词性: noun

Etymology:  From Greek *daktylos* (finger) + *-itis* (inflammation).
