deportees:/ˌdiːpɔːrˈtiːz/
noun
被驱逐出境者；被遣返者
People who are deported; exiles.
The deportees were sent back to their country of origin. 这些被驱逐出境者被遣返回了他们的原籍国。
exiles, refugees
immigrants, citizens
