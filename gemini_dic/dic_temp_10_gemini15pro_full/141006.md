ones:/wʌnz/ |pron.| 那些；这一些；(用以代替前面提到过的复数名词) |  Used to refer to people or things that have already been mentioned. |  I've got some new shoes. The old ones are worn out.  我买了几双新鞋。旧的都穿破了。 | synonyms: these, those (in some contexts) | antonyms: none | pronoun

ones:/wʌnz/ |n.| 个体；(one 的复数) | Plural form of 'one,' referring to multiple unspecified people or things.  | The little ones are asleep. 小家伙们都睡着了。 | synonyms: individuals, units | antonyms: none | noun
