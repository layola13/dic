monotonously:/məˈnɒtənəsli/ | adv. |单调地，无聊地 | In a manner that lacks variation and is tedious. |  The lecturer spoke monotonously, and many students fell asleep. 演讲者单调地说着，许多学生都睡着了。

| Synonyms: tediously, dully, boringly, repetitively, uniformly  | Antonyms: interestingly, excitingly, variedly, dynamically | 词性: 副词

monotone:/ˈmɒnətəʊn/ | n. | 单调的声音，单调的讲话 | A sound or utterance that does not vary in pitch. | The monotone of the chanting lulled me to sleep.  吟诵的单调声音使我昏昏欲睡。 | Synonyms: drone, chant | Antonyms: melody, harmony | 词性: 名词

monotonic:/ˌmɒnəˈtɒnɪk/ | adj. | 单调的，无变化的 | (of a sequence or function) consistently increasing or decreasing. | The function is monotonic increasing.  这个函数是单调递增的。 | Synonyms: unchanging, unvarying | Antonyms: varying, fluctuating | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *monotonement*, from *monotone* ("monotone"), from Ancient Greek *μονότονος* (monótonos, “monotonous”), from *μόνος* (mónos, “alone, single”) + *τόνος* (tónos, “tone, pitch”).
