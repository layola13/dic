hamperer:/ˈhæmpərər/| n.|阻碍者，妨碍者|A person or thing that hampers someone or something.|The heavy rain was a real hamperer to our picnic plans.  大雨真的阻碍了我们的野餐计划。|Synonyms: obstacle, impediment, hindrance, obstruction|Antonyms: facilitator, helper, aid, assistant|noun

Etymology->
From the verb "hamper," which comes from the Old North French "hanapier" meaning "to entangle the feet of horses with ropes," ultimately from the Frankish "hamp" meaning "fetter."
