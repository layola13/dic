ineradicablenesses:/ɪˌrædɪˈkeɪblənəsɪz/ | n. | 不可根除性；无法消除性 | The quality of being ineradicable; impossible to eradicate or destroy. | The ineradicablenesses of certain prejudices are a persistent social problem.  某些偏见的不可根除性是一个持续存在的社会问题。| Synonyms: permanence, fixity, ingrainedness | Antonyms: eradicability, removability | noun

ineradicable:/ˌɪrædɪˈkeɪbl/ | adj. | 不可根除的；无法消除的 | Not capable of being eradicated; impossible to destroy or remove. |  His ineradicable optimism helped him through difficult times. 他那不可磨灭的乐观精神帮助他度过了难关。 | Synonyms: permanent, ingrained, fixed, indelible | Antonyms: eradicable, removable, temporary, fleeting | adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin ineradicabilis, from in- "not" + eradicabilis "that can be rooted out," from eradicare "to root out," from ex- "out" + radix "root."
