filled:/fɪld/| adj.|充满的，填满的|Containing or holding as much as possible; full.|The room was filled with smoke. 房间里充满了烟雾。|近义词：full, crammed, packed, replete|反义词：empty, vacant|adjective

filled:/fɪld/| v.|填充，填满|(past tense and past participle of fill) To make or become full; to occupy available space.|She filled the vase with water. 她把花瓶装满了水。|近义词：load, pack, stuff|反义词：empty, drain|verb
