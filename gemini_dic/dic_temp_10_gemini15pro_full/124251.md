mostahiba:/mʊsˈtɑːhɪbə/ | adj. | commendable, desirable, preferable, recommended, advisable |  Arabic origin, meaning something recommended or desirable, often in a religious context, relating to actions that are encouraged but not obligatory. | It is mostahiba to give charity to the poor.  给予穷人施舍是值得赞扬的。| Synonyms: recommended, advisable, desirable, praiseworthy, commendable | Antonyms: undesirable, reprehensible, objectionable, discouraged, forbidden | Adjective
