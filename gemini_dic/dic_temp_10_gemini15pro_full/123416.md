monoplanists:/ˈmɒnəˌpleɪnɪsts/ | n. | 单翼机飞行员；单翼飞机设计师 | A person who designs, builds, or flies monoplanes. |  The early monoplanists faced many challenges in aircraft stability. 早期的单翼飞机设计师在飞机稳定性方面面临许多挑战。

The Wright brothers, while initially successful with biplanes, later experimented with monoplane designs. 莱特兄弟虽然最初在双翼飞机上取得了成功，但后来也尝试了单翼飞机的设计。 | 近义词: aviator, pilot, aircraft designer | 反义词: biplanist | 词性: 名词
