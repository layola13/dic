demiurge:/ˈdɛmiˌɜːrdʒ/| n.|造物主，创世神|A being responsible for the creation of the physical world, in particular in the Platonic, Gnostic, and other philosophical traditions.  A powerful creative force or influence.|The Gnostics believed in a demiurge, a lesser god who created the imperfect material world.| 近义词:creator, maker, architect, author|反义词:destroyer, annihilator|noun

demiurge:/ˈdɛmiˌɜːrdʒ/| n.|推动者，主导者|A powerfully influential person or thing.|The software CEO was the demiurge of the personal computer revolution.|近义词: driving force, prime mover, catalyst|反义词:inhibitor, obstacle|noun
