lithoidal:/lɪˈθɔɪdəl/
adjective
石质的，像石头的
Resembling stone; stony.  Having the texture or appearance of stone.
The lithoidal fragments were embedded in the clay matrix.  这些石质碎片嵌在粘土基质中。
The statue had a lithoidal appearance, cold and unyielding. 这座雕像有一种石头般的外观，冰冷而坚硬。
近义词: stony, rocky, petrous
反义词: soft, yielding, malleable
词性: adjective

词源: From Greek *lithos* (stone) + *-oidal* (like, resembling).
