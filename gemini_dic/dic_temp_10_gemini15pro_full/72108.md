flockless:/ˈflɑːklɪs/
adjective
无群的，没有羊群的，孤独的
Without a flock; solitary.
The flockless sheep wandered aimlessly.  那只离群的羊漫无目的地游荡。
adjective
无毛的
featherless
The baby bird was small and flockless. 这只雏鸟又小又没长毛。
近义词:solitary, lone, isolated, alone, individual
反义词:gregarious, social, flocked
