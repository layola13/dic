disparateness:/dɪˈspærɪtnəs/
n.
不同，差异，迥异
The state of being disparate or distinct; difference; unlikeness.
The disparateness of their backgrounds made communication difficult.  他们背景的差异使得沟通变得困难。
近义词:difference, dissimilarity, diversity, variation, heterogeneity
反义词:similarity, likeness, homogeneity, uniformity
词性:noun

disparate:/ˈdɪspərət/
adj.
不同的，根本不同的，异类的
Fundamentally different; entirely unlike.
The company's disparate divisions were difficult to manage. 这家公司差异巨大的部门很难管理。
近义词:different, dissimilar, unlike, distinct, diverse, various, varied
反义词:similar, alike, homogeneous, uniform
词性:adjective
