outscold:/ˌaʊtˈskoʊld/
verb
责骂，胜过…的责骂
To scold vehemently or rebuke severely; surpass in scolding.
She outscolded her neighbor for letting his dog run loose. 她严厉斥责她的邻居让他家的狗到处乱跑。

近义词:berate, rebuke, reprimand, chide, upbraid, castigate
反义词:praise, commend, laud

词性:v.
