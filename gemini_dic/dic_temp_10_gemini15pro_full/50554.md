deputing:/dɪˈpjuːtɪŋ/
v.
代理；委派；指派
acting or serving in place of another person
He is deputing for the manager while she is on maternity leave. 他在经理休产假期间代理经理职务。
近义词:substituting, representing, acting for
反义词: N/A
词性: Verb (present participle)

deputy:/ˈdepjuti/
n.
副手；代理人；代表
a person who is appointed to undertake the duties of a superior in the superior's absence
She's the deputy head of the school. 她是学校的副校长。
近义词:assistant, substitute, representative
反义词:superior, chief, principal
词性: Noun
