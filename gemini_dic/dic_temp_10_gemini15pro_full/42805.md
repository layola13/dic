coupons:/ˈkuːpɒnz/ (UK), /ˈkuːpɑːnz/ (US)| n.|优惠券，礼券|A small piece of paper that gives you the right to buy something at a reduced price or get something free.|I have a coupon for 10% off my next purchase. 我有一张下次购买可享受九折的优惠券。|synonyms: voucher, discount, token|antonyms: surcharge, extra charge|noun


coupons:/ˈkuːpɒnz/ (UK), /ˈkuːpɑːnz/ (US)| n.|配给券|A ticket or other document that can be exchanged for a particular amount of rationed goods.|During the war, families relied on coupons for basic necessities. 战争期间，家庭依靠配给券获得基本必需品。|synonyms: ration card, ticket, chit|antonyms: abundance, surplus|noun
