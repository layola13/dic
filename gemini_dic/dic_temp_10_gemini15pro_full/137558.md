nontransitive:/ˌnɒnˈtrænsɪtɪv/ /ˌnɑːnˈtrænsɪtɪv/| adj.|不及物的|Not taking a direct object; designating a verb or verb construction that does not require or cannot take a direct object to complete its meaning.|The verb "sleep" is intransitive.  睡觉这个动词是不及物的。|近义词:incompletive|反义词:transitive|词性:形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
The sun rises in the east.  太阳从东方升起。
He sleeps for eight hours every night. 他每晚睡八个小时。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
The baby is sleeping soundly. 婴儿睡得很香。
She is walking to school. 她正步行去学校。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
The flowers have bloomed. 花已经开了。
He has arrived at the airport. 他已经到达机场了。


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
She has been sleeping for hours. 她已经睡了几个小时了。
They have been walking all day. 他们一整天都在走路。


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
The bird flew away. 鸟飞走了。
He slept peacefully last night. 他昨晚睡得很安稳。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
The children were playing in the park. 孩子们正在公园里玩耍。
She was sleeping when the phone rang. 电话响的时候她正在睡觉。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
The train had left before we arrived. 火车在我们到达之前已经离开了。
He had slept for ten hours before waking up. 他睡了十个小时才醒来。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
They had been sleeping for hours when the alarm went off. 警报响起时，他们已经睡了几个小时了。
She had been walking for miles before she found a place to rest.  她走了好几英里才找到一个休息的地方。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
The plane will arrive at 8 pm. 飞机将于晚上8点到达。
He will sleep later. 他晚些时候会睡觉。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
This time tomorrow, I will be flying to Paris. 明天这个时候，我将飞往巴黎。
She will be sleeping when we arrive. 我们到达时她会在睡觉。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next week, I will have finished the project. 到下周，我将完成这个项目。
He will have slept by the time you get there. 你到那里的时候他应该已经睡着了。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next year, they will have been living here for five years. 到明年，他们将在这里住了五年。
She will have been sleeping for 12 hours by then. 到那时她将已经睡了12个小时。



虚拟语气:
If I were a bird, I would fly. 如果我是一只鸟，我会飞。
I wish I could fly. 我希望我能飞。


被动语态:
Intransitive verbs cannot be used in the passive voice. 不及物动词不能用于被动语态。


疑问句:
Did he arrive on time? 他准时到达了吗？
Is she sleeping? 她睡着了吗？


否定句:
The train did not arrive on time. 火车没有准时到达。
She is not sleeping. 她没睡着。
