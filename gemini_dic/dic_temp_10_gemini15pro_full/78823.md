gimmes:/ˈɡɪmiz/| n.|免费赠品，好处，不劳而获的东西|Something that is freely given or granted, often with little effort on the part of the receiver; a handout.  It's often used informally and can have a slightly negative connotation, suggesting a sense of entitlement. |"The kids are always asking for gimmes."  孩子们总是要些免费的东西。|近义词：freebies, handouts|反义词: earnings,  payment|名词
