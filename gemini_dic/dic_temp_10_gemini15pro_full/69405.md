feel-good:/ˈfiːlˌɡʊd/| adj.|令人愉悦的；使人感觉良好的|Evoking pleasant feelings; uplifting.|Watching a feel-good movie always cheers me up. 看一部令人愉悦的电影总是能让我振作起来。|Synonyms: heartwarming, uplifting, cheerful, positive|Antonyms: depressing, disheartening, saddening|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From the phrase "feel good," combining the verb "feel" (from Old English *fēlan) and the adjective "good" (from Old English gōd). The compound adjective emerged in the mid-20th century.
