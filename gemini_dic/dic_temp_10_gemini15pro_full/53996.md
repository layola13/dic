discriminately:/dɪˈskrɪmɪnətli/
adverb|有区别地，有识别力地|In a way that shows careful judgment and an ability to distinguish between good and bad; selectively.|She chooses her friends discriminately. 她慎择朋友。|近义词:selectively, judiciously, discerningly|反义词:indiscriminately, randomly, blindly|词性:副词
词源: From discriminate + -ly.  Discriminate comes from Latin discriminare "to divide, separate, distinguish," from discrimen "separation, distinction," related to discernere "to separate, distinguish."
