adopts:/əˈdɒpts/ (UK) /əˈdɑːpts/ (US)| vt.|采用，采纳；收养|To take and use as one's own; to formally accept or approve.|The company adopts a flexible working policy.  这家公司采取弹性工作制。

The couple adopted a child from overseas. 这对夫妇从海外收养了一个孩子。|近义词：accept, embrace, foster, choose, approve|反义词:reject, abandon, discard|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English adopten, from Old French adopter, from Latin adoptare (“to choose for oneself, adopt”), from ad- + optare (“to choose, wish”).

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The school adopts a new teaching method. = 这所学校采用一种新的教学方法。
He adopts a positive attitude towards life. = 他对生活采取积极的态度。

一般过去时:
They adopted the proposal without any changes. = 他们未作任何修改就采纳了这项提议。
She adopted a stray dog last year. = 她去年收养了一只流浪狗。


现在完成时:
The government has adopted a series of measures to control inflation. = 政府采取了一系列措施来控制通货膨胀。
He has adopted a healthier lifestyle. = 他采用了一种更健康的生活方式。


过去完成时:
They had adopted the new technology before their competitors. = 他们在竞争对手之前就采用了这项新技术。
She had adopted two children before she met her husband. = 在遇到她丈夫之前，她已经收养了两个孩子。


将来时:
The company will adopt a new marketing strategy next year. = 公司明年将采用新的营销策略。
They will adopt a dog once they move into their new house. = 一旦他们搬进新家，他们就会收养一只狗。


虚拟语气:
If they adopted my suggestion, they would save a lot of money. = 如果他们采纳我的建议，他们会节省很多钱。
I wish they would adopt a more environmentally friendly policy. = 我希望他们能采取更环保的政策。


被动语态:
A new policy was adopted by the committee. = 委员会通过了一项新政策。
The abandoned puppy was adopted by a loving family. = 这只被遗弃的小狗被一个充满爱的家庭收养了。


疑问句:

Have they adopted a child yet? = 他们收养孩子了吗？


否定句:
The company didn't adopt my proposal. = 公司没有采纳我的建议。
They haven't adopted any new policies this year. = 他们今年没有采用任何新政策。
