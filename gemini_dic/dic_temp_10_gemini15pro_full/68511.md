fanaticism:/fəˈnætɪsɪzəm/
n.
狂热; 盲信;
Excessive enthusiasm or zeal; extreme devotion to a belief or cause.
He was known for his fanaticism about cleanliness. 他以对清洁的狂热而闻名。

fanaticism:/fəˈnætɪsɪzəm/
n.
狂热主义
The quality or state of being fanatical.
Religious fanaticism can lead to violence. 宗教狂热会导致暴力。


近义词:zealotry, extremism, bigotry
反义词:moderation, tolerance, apathy
词性:noun
