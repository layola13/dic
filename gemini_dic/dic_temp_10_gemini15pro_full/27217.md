callow:/ˈkæloʊ/ (US), /ˈkæloʊ/ (UK)| adj.|幼稚的，不成熟的，没有经验的|Lacking adult sophistication; immature; inexperienced. |The callow youth made a series of foolish mistakes.  这个幼稚的年轻人犯了一系列愚蠢的错误。|Example:  He was just a callow youth when he left home. 他离家时还是个乳臭未干的小伙子。|Synonyms: inexperienced, immature, naive, green, unsophisticated|Antonyms: experienced, mature, sophisticated, worldly, wise|adjective
