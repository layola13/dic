go-bag:/ˈɡoʊ bæɡ/| n.|逃生包；应急包|A bag packed with essential supplies, kept ready for use in an emergency, especially in case of evacuation.|I keep a go-bag in my car in case of earthquakes. 我在车里放了一个逃生包，以防地震。|After the hurricane warning, we grabbed our go-bags and headed for the shelter.  飓风警报发布后，我们抓起逃生包前往避难所。|Synonyms: emergency bag, bug-out bag, survival kit|Antonyms: N/A|noun
