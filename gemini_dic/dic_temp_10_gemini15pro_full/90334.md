housling:/ˈhaʊzlɪŋ/
n.
communion
The act of receiving the Eucharist, especially in the context of a specific service or ritual.
After the wedding ceremony, the couple received housling. 婚礼结束后，这对夫妇领了圣餐。
近义词: communion, Eucharist
反义词: None.
词性: noun


词源:  From Middle English *houseling, from Old English *hūslian (“to administer the Eucharist”), from hūsel (“Eucharist”), from Proto-Germanic *hunslą (“sacrifice”).  Related to Gothic 𐌷𐌿𐌽𐍃𐌻 (hunsl, “sacrifice”). Cognate with Old Norse húsl (“sacrifice, Eucharist”), Old High German huosal, husel (“sacrifice, Eucharist”), Old Frisian hūsle (“Eucharist”).
