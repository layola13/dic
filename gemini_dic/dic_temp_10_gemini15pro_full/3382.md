affusion:/əˈfjuːʒən/ | n. |浇灌，倾注，灌输|The act of pouring a liquid on something, especially as part of a religious ceremony.|The priest performed the affusion during the baptism.  牧师在洗礼仪式中进行了浇灌仪式。|近义词:pouring, sprinkling, shower, douche|反义词:dehydration, drainage|noun


affusion:/əˈfjuːʒən/ | n. |（强烈感情的）迸发，流露|An outpouring of strong emotion.|There was an affusion of grief after the tragedy.  悲剧发生后，人们悲痛欲绝。|近义词:outpouring, outburst, effusion, surge|反义词:suppression, repression|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *affusio*, from Latin affusus, past participle of affundere (“to pour upon”), from ad- (“to, toward”) + fundere (“to pour”).
