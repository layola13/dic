hypogyn:/haɪˈpɒdʒɪn/ /haɪˈpɑːdʒɪn/
adjective
下位花的，子房上位花的
(of a flower) having the other floral parts inserted below the gynoecium, as in most flowers.
The buttercup has a hypogynous flower. 毛茛科植物有下位子房的花。
近义词: inferior
反义词: epigynous, perigynous
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek ὑπό (hupó, “under”) + γυνή (gunḗ, “woman, female”).  The term refers to the position of the ovary relative to other floral parts, imagining the gynoecium as the "female" part of the flower.
