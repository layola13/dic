almanac: /ˈɔːlmənæk/ (US) /ˈɔːlmənæk/ (UK) | n. | 年鉴；历书 | A publication containing astronomical data, weather forecasts, tide tables, and other information often arranged according to the calendar. |  I checked the almanac to see when the next full moon would be. 我查看了年鉴，看看下一次满月是什么时候。

| Example: The farmer consulted the almanac to determine the best time to plant his crops. 农民查阅年鉴以确定种植作物的最佳时间。
| Synonym: calendar, yearbook, annual, register | Antonym: N/A | Noun
