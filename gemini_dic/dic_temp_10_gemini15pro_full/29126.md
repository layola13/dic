case-sensitive:/keɪsˈsɛnsɪtɪv/| adj.|区分大小写的；大小写敏感的|Describes something, typically a computer program or system, that treats uppercase and lowercase letters as distinct characters.|Passwords are case-sensitive, so "Password" is different from "password".|密码区分大小写，所以“Password”与“password”不同。|case-dependent|case-insensitive|adjective
