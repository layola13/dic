misfare:/ˌmɪsˈfeər/ | n. | 误价，票价错误 | A mistake in the price of a fare, especially for transportation. |  Example 1: The airline apologized for the misfare and refunded the difference. 航空公司为票价错误道歉并退还了差价。

Example 2: Due to a system misfare, I was able to book a first-class ticket at the price of an economy ticket. 由于系统票价错误，我能够以经济舱的价格预订头等舱机票。 | Near Synonyms: pricing error, fare error, incorrect fare, inaccurate fare | Antonyms: correct fare, accurate fare | Noun
