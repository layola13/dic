aeroponics:/ˌeɪrəˈpɒnɪks/ /ˌɛrəˈpɑːnɪks/| n.|气栽法，雾培法|A method of growing plants in which the roots hang suspended in the air and are sprayed regularly with a nutrient solution.|The farm uses aeroponics to grow lettuce and herbs. 这个农场使用气栽法种植生菜和香草。|近义词：hydroponics, soilless culture|反义词: geoponics, traditional soil-based agriculture|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From aero- (air) + -ponics (cultivation, from Greek ponos "labor"). Coined in the 1950s.
