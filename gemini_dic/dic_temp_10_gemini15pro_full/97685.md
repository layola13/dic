inhumanities:/ˌɪnhjuːˈmænɪtiz/
|n.|非人道行为，残暴行为|Extreme cruelty or lack of humanity.|The inhumanities of war are appalling. 战争的残酷令人震惊。|
|近义词：atrocities, barbarities, cruelties|反义词：humanities, kindness, compassion|词性：名词|
|词源：Late Middle English: from Old French inhumanite, from Latin inhumanitas, from inhumanus (see inhuman).|
