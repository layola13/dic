interlotting:/ˌɪntərˈlɒtɪŋ/ | n. | 交错分配，穿插分配 | The interlotting of different crops helps maintain soil fertility. 不同作物的交错分配有助于保持土壤肥力。|  The interlotting of tasks allowed for efficient use of resources. 任务的穿插分配使得资源得以有效利用。 |  interlacing, interweaving, alternation | segregation, separation | noun

interlot:/ˌɪntərˈlɒt/ | v. | 交错分配，穿插分配  |  They interlotted the vegetables and flowers in the garden. 他们在花园里交错种植蔬菜和鲜花。 | The farmers interlotted their crops to maximize yield. 农民们交错分配他们的作物以最大化产量。 | interlace, interweave, alternate | segregate, separate | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- (between) + lot (a portion of land).  The verb form "interlot" is less common than the noun "interlotting."
