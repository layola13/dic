Mail To Order

The company started as a small mto business. 这家公司最初是一家小型邮购企业。
近义词: mail order, direct mail
反义词: in-store purchase, brick-and-mortar store

mto:/ˈɛm tiː oʊ/
abbreviation
More Than One
多个
Please indicate if you require MTO. 请说明您是否需要多个。
近义词: multiple, several, numerous
反义词: single, one, individual
词性: abbreviation


mto:/ˈɛm tiː oʊ/
abbreviation
Make To Order
按订单生产
This furniture is MTO, so it takes a few weeks to deliver. 这件家具是按订单生产的，所以需要几周时间才能交付。
近义词: custom-made, bespoke, built-to-order
反义词: ready-made, off-the-shelf, in-stock
词性: abbreviation


词源:  MTO is an abbreviation, so it doesn't have a traditional etymology. It simply represents the initial letters of the phrases it stands for: Mail To Order, More Than One, and Make To Order.
