nonpolarity:/ˌnɑːnpəˈlærɪti/ | n. | 非极性 | The property of a substance or molecule having no dipole moment, meaning there is no separation of electrical charge. | Oil and water don't mix because of the nonpolarity of oil molecules.  油和水不相溶，是因为油分子的非极性。| synonyms: hydrophobicity, lipophilicity | antonyms: polarity | noun
