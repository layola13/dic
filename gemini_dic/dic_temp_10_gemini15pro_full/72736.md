foetid:/ˈfiːtɪd/ /ˈfetɪd/ | adj. |恶臭的，腐臭的 | Having a strong, unpleasant smell; stinking. | The foetid smell of the garbage made me gag. 垃圾的恶臭让我作呕。|  The stagnant water in the ditch emitted a foetid odor.  沟里 stagnant 的水发出恶臭。| synonyms: stinking, foul, rank, putrid, noisome, fetid | antonyms: fragrant, sweet-smelling, aromatic, perfumed | adjective

From Latin *foetidus*, from *foetēre* "to stink," related to *fimus* "dung."
