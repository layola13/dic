donning:/ˈdɑːnɪŋ/ /ˈdɒnɪŋ/| v. |穿上，戴上|The act of putting on an item of clothing or attire.  The process of assuming or taking on something, such as a role or responsibility.|He was donning his coat when the phone rang. 他正在穿外套时电话响了. |Synonyms: putting on, wearing, assuming, adopting|Antonyms: doffing, removing, taking off|verb
