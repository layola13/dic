adriatic:/ˌeɪdriˈætɪk/| adj. n.|亚得里亚海的；亚得里亚海|Relating to the Adriatic Sea, a body of water separating the Italian Peninsula from the Balkan peninsula.  As a noun, it refers to the Adriatic Sea itself.|We sailed across the Adriatic. 我们横渡了亚得里亚海。|近义词：Mediterranean (as in, relating to a sea in Southern Europe)|反义词:None|adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *Adriaticus*, from *Adria*, a town on the Adriatic coast. Ultimately from Illyrian *adur* meaning "water" or "sea".

USAGE->
The Adriatic Sea is a popular tourist destination. = 亚得里亚海是一个受欢迎的旅游目的地。

The Adriatic coast is known for its beautiful beaches. = 亚得里亚海岸以其美丽的海滩而闻名。
