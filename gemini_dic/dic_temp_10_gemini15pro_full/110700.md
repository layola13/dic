linearize:/ˈlɪniəraɪz/ | v. |线性化，使线性化 |To make something linear; to represent something in a linear form, or to make something behave in a linear way.  |  We need to linearize this equation to solve it. 我们需要将这个方程式线性化来求解。| synonyms: straighten, align | antonyms: curve, bend | verb

linearize:/ˈlɪniəraɪz/ | n. |线性化 |The process of making something linear. | The linearization of the model simplified the calculations. 模型的线性化简化了计算。 | synonyms: linearization, alignment | antonyms: curving, bending | noun
