miscounts:/ˌmɪsˈkaʊnts/ | n. |错误计数，错误统计| An act of counting something incorrectly. |  The cashier's miscounts resulted in a shortage at the end of the day. 收银员的错误计数导致一天结束时出现短缺。| miscalculations, errors, inaccuracies | correct counts, accurate calculations | noun

miscounts:/ˌmɪsˈkaʊnts/ | v. |数错，算错 | To count something incorrectly. | She miscounted the number of guests and didn't have enough chairs. 她数错了客人的数量，没有足够的椅子。 | miscalculate, misjudge, err | count correctly, calculate accurately | verb
