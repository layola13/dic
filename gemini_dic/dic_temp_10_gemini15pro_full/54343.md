disheveled: /dɪˈʃevəld/ | adj. |  凌乱的，蓬乱的 |  (of a person's hair, clothes, or appearance) untidy; disordered. | Her hair was disheveled and her clothes were torn. 她的头发蓬乱，衣服也破了。 | The disheveled man stumbled down the street.  那个衣衫不整的男人在街上蹒跚而行。 |  synonyms: untidy, messy, scruffy, rumpled, unkempt, disordered | antonyms: tidy, neat, orderly, groomed | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *descheveler, from des- (expressing reversal) + chevel (hair), from Latin capillus.
