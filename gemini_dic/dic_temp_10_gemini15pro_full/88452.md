histogenesis:/ˌhɪstoʊˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/| n.|组织发生；组织形成|The study of histogenesis helps us understand how tissues develop and differentiate.  组织发生的研究帮助我们理解组织如何发育和分化。| Histogenesis of the lung is a complex process involving multiple cell types.  肺的组织发生是一个涉及多种细胞类型的复杂过程。|近义词：tissue development, tissue formation|反义词: tissue degradation, tissue degeneration|词性:noun


Etymology: histo- (tissue) + -genesis (origin, development)  来自希腊语 histos（组织）和 genesis（起源，发展）
