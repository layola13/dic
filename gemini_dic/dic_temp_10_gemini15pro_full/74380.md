frame:/freɪm/| n. vt.|框架，边框；体格；画面；构架；陷害|A rigid structure that surrounds or encloses something.|The painting's frame was ornately carved. 这幅画的画框雕刻精美。|synonyms: structure, framework, chassis, skeleton, scaffolding|antonyms: disorganize, dismantle|noun
frame:/freɪm/| vt.|给…装框；陷害；表达；制定；使…适合|To construct by fitting parts together; to formulate or devise.|He was framed for a crime he didn't commit. 他被诬陷犯了他没有犯的罪。|synonyms: formulate, devise, compose, construct, build|antonyms: destroy, ruin|verb
