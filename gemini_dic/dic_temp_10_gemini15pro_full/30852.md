chaddars:/ˈtʃɑːdərz/ | n. | 印度大披巾;  薄床单(尤指印度用的) | A large piece of thin cotton or silk cloth, especially one used in India as a bed covering. | She wrapped herself in a warm chaddar. 她裹着一条温暖的印度大披巾。| The chaddars were spread out on the floor for the guests to sit on. 薄床单铺在地上供客人坐。| synonyms: sheet, blanket, shawl, wrap | antonyms: (No direct antonym, possibly "coverlet" as a more substantial bed covering) | noun
