highbinder:/ˈhaɪˌbaɪndər/| n.|敲诈者；勒索者；恶棍；(尤指旧时美国的)秘密社团勒索者|A person, especially a member of a secret society, who extorts money by threats and violence, especially in the past in the US.|The highbinders threatened local businesses with arson if they didn't pay protection money. 那些敲诈勒索者威胁当地企业，如果不交保护费就放火。|Synonyms: extortionist, racketeer, blackmailer|Antonyms: philanthropist, benefactor|Noun
