deghost:/diːˈɡoʊst/
v.|去鬼影，解除鬼影状态|To re-establish communication with someone after a period of ghosting, often with an explanation for the absence.|He deghosted her after a year, explaining that he'd been dealing with a family emergency.|近义词:reconnect, reach out, get back in touch|反义词:ghost|词性:动词
