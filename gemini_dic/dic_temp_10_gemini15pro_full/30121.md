cedars:/ˈsiːdərz/| n. |雪松，西洋杉|Cedar trees, often prized for their aromatic wood and imposing stature, are evergreen conifers found in various parts of the world. 雪松树，因其芳香的木材和雄伟的身材而备受珍视，是在世界各地发现的常绿针叶树。|The cedars of Lebanon are famous for their size and longevity.黎巴嫩的雪松以其规模和寿命而闻名。|The carpenter used cedar to build the chest. 木匠用雪松木制作了箱子。|近义词:cedarwood, conifer|反义词:|名词
