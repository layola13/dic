overroll:/ˌoʊvərˈroʊl/| v. |碾过，压过; 制服，压倒; 推翻，颠覆|To roll over or across something; to defeat or overwhelm someone or something. |The tank overrolled the enemy position. 坦克碾过了敌人的阵地。

The waves overrolled the small boat. 海浪吞没了小船。

The government was overrolled by the rebels. 政府被叛军推翻了。|近义词：overwhelm, overrun, crush, defeat, conquer|反义词：surrender, yield|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over + roll.  "Over" implies an action happening above and beyond or completely covering something. "Roll" refers to the action of turning over and over or moving along a surface by rotation.  Therefore, "overroll" literally means to roll completely over something.
