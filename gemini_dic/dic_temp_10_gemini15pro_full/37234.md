comfortables:/ˈkʌmfərtəblz/
|n.|舒适的衣服（或鞋子）; 保暖内衣裤|Comfortable clothing or shoes, especially warm underwear or pajamas. |She packed her comfortables for the ski trip. 她为滑雪旅行打包了保暖内衣。|Near synonyms: loungewear, pajamas, sweats|Antonyms: formals, dress clothes|Noun

comfort:/ˈkʌmfərt/
|n.|安慰; 慰藉; 安逸; 舒适|A state of physical ease and freedom from pain or constraint.|She found comfort in her friend's words. 她从朋友的话语中得到了安慰。|Near synonyms: solace, consolation, relief, ease|Antonyms: discomfort, distress, pain|Noun
comfort:/ˈkʌmfərt/
|vt.|安慰; 慰藉|To give strength and hope to someone who is in distress.|He comforted her after her loss.  他在她失去亲人后安慰她。|Near synonyms: console, soothe, reassure|Antonyms: distress, upset, worry|Verb
