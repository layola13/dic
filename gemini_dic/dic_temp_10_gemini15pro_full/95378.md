inch: /ɪntʃ/ | n. | 英寸 | A unit of linear measure equal to one twelfth of a foot (2.54 centimeters). | The snake was twelve inches long. 这条蛇有十二英寸长。 | synonyms:  cm, millimeter | antonyms: foot, yard | noun

inch: /ɪntʃ/ | v. | 缓慢移动 | To move slowly and carefully in a specified direction. |  The car inched forward in the traffic jam. 汽车在交通堵塞中缓慢前行。 | synonyms: creep, edge, move | antonyms:  rush, dart | verb
