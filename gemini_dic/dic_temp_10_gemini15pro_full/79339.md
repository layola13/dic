gleeful:/ˈɡliːfəl/| adj.|兴高采烈的；欣喜若狂的|Full of glee; merry; joyous. Showing or expressing joy, especially in a playful or mischievous way.|She gave a gleeful laugh when she heard the news. 她听到这个消息后高兴地笑了。|Synonyms: joyous, merry, cheerful, elated, jubilant|Antonyms: sad, miserable, gloomy, dejected, sorrowful|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From glee + -ful.  Glee (Old English *glēow, glēo "mirth, joy, entertainment")  + -ful (suffix forming adjectives meaning "full of, characterized by").
