elysium:/ɪˈlɪziəm/ /iˈlɪʒiəm/ | n. | 极乐世界；天堂；至福之境 | A place or state of perfect happiness; paradise. |  The hero finally reached Elysium after his long and arduous journey. 英雄在漫长而艰辛的旅程后终于到达了极乐世界。

Elysium is often depicted as a land of idyllic beauty and tranquility. 极乐世界常被描绘成一个田园诗般美丽宁静的地方。| 近义词：paradise, heaven, utopia | 反义词：hell, inferno, purgatory | 词性: 名词

词源: From Latin *Ēlysium*, from Ancient Greek *Ἠλύσιον πεδίον (Ēlýsion pedíon)* (Elysian field), the abode of the blessed after death.
