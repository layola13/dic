fertilises:/ˈfɜːtɪlaɪzɪz/
verb
施肥，使肥沃，使受精
To make something fertile or more fertile; to supply with fertilizer or nutrients; to cause fertilization.
The farmer fertilises his fields every spring. 农民每年春天都给田地施肥。
synonyms: enrich, nourish, feed, impregnate
antonyms: sterilize, deplete, impoverish
词性: 动词


fertilizes:/ˈfɜːrtɪlaɪzɪz/
verb
施肥，使肥沃，使受精
To make something fertile or more fertile; to supply with fertilizer or nutrients; to cause fertilization.
Bees fertilize flowers by carrying pollen from one to another. 蜜蜂通过携带花粉从一朵花到另一朵花来授粉。
synonyms: pollinate, inseminate
antonyms: sterilize, castrate
词性: 动词
