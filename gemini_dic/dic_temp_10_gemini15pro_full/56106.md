doctorly:/ˈdɑːktərli/| adj.|医生般的；有学问的；博学的|Like a doctor; learned; scholarly.  It suggests a manner or behavior that is reminiscent of a doctor, often implying seriousness, carefulness, or deep knowledge.|He had a doctorly air about him, calm and reassuring. 他身上有一种医生般的气质，沉着而令人安心。|近义词：learned, scholarly, erudite, professorial|反义词：unlearned, ignorant, uneducated|词性:形容词

词源: From doctor + -ly.  The suffix "-ly" is used to form adjectives from nouns, meaning "like" or "characteristic of."  Thus, "doctorly" means "like a doctor."
