damned's:/dæmdz/ |  n. (possessive) |  该死的…的；被诅咒的…的；非常…的 (强调语气) |  Contraction of "damned's," itself a less common alternative to "damned one's."  Expresses strong emphasis, often negative, similar to "damn" plus possessive. Primarily colloquial and informal. | The damned's luck ran out.  这该死的家伙的运气用完了。 |  Synonyms: accursed's, cursed's (more formal), blasted's (informal) | Antonyms: blessed's, fortunate's | possessive noun (contraction)

注意事项:
This word is highly informal and often considered vulgar.  Avoid using it in formal settings. It emphasizes negative emotion, so be mindful of the context and audience.  It is more common in spoken than written English.
