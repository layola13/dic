difficult:/ˈdɪfɪkəlt/| adj.|困难的；艰难的；难对付的|Something that is difficult requires a lot of effort or skill.|Learning a new language can be difficult. 学习一门新的语言可能会很困难.|近义词：hard, challenging, tough, arduous, laborious|反义词：easy, simple, straightforward|词性:形容词|词源:From Old French *difficulte (Modern French difficulté), from Latin difficultas (“difficulty”), from difficilis (“difficult”), from dis- (“not”) + facilis (“easy”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *dʰeh₁- (“to put, place, set”).
