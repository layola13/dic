epigrammatists: /ɪˈpɪɡrəməˌtɪsts/
noun
警句家；善于写警句的人
Writers of epigrams, short witty poems or sayings.
Oscar Wilde and Mark Twain were considered master epigrammatists. 奥斯卡·王尔德和马克·吐温被认为是警句大师。
Synonyms:  wits, pundits,  aphorists
Antonyms:  dullards, bores
