clearish:/ˈklɪərɪʃ/| adj.|较为清晰的，有点透明的|Somewhat clear; not completely clear. |The liquid was clearish, like weak tea.  这种液体有点透明，像淡茶。|The sky was clearish, with a few wispy clouds. 天空略显晴朗，飘着几缕稀疏的云彩。|近义词：lucid, transparent, limpid|反义词:opaque, murky, cloudy|词性:形容词
词源: clear + -ish.  From "clear" (meaning transparent or easily understood) + the suffix "-ish" (which indicates a degree or quality that is less than absolute).  "Clear" ultimately comes from the Old French "cler" and the Latin "clarus".
