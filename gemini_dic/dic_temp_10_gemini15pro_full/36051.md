codriver:/ˈkoʊˌdraɪvər/ (US) /ˈkəʊˌdraɪvər/ (UK)| n.|副驾驶，领航员|A person who sits next to the driver in a car, especially to give directions or help with navigation, often in rallies or other motorsports.|例句：The codriver read the map and gave directions to the driver. 领航员看着地图并给驾驶员指路。

例句：The driver and codriver worked together seamlessly during the rally. 在拉力赛中，驾驶员和领航员配合得天衣无缝。|近义词：navigator, co-pilot|反义词:driver|词性:noun
