mistranslated:/ˌmɪsˌtrænsˈleɪtɪd/
adjective
翻译错误的
Incorrectly translated from one language to another.
The document was poorly mistranslated, leading to confusion.  这份文件翻译得很差，导致了混乱。
The instructions were mistranslated, causing the user to make a mistake. 说明书翻译错了，导致用户犯了错误。

synonym:misinterpreted, wrongly translated
antonym:correctly translated, accurately translated
word type:adjective
