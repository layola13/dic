USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃什么？
What does she do for a living? = 她靠什么谋生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? = 他晚饭在做什么菜？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned so far? = 到目前为止你学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? = 她用这笔钱做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么书？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? = 她参加聚会穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you eaten before you got sick? = 你生病之前吃了什么？
What had they discussed before the meeting started? = 会议开始前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I arrived? = 我到达之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 她换专业之前一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? = 你下周末要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们下次会议将讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by the end of the day? = 到今天结束时你将完成什么？
What will they have achieved by next year? = 到明年他们将取得什么成就？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been studying for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经学习了五年什么？
What will she have been working on for so long? = 到那时她将已经做了那么长时间的什么工作？
