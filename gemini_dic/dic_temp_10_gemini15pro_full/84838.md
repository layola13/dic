hang-one-on:/hæŋ wʌn ɑːn/ | idiom |痛饮; 狂饮; 豪饮|To drink heavily, especially alcoholic beverages.  It implies a period of excessive drinking, often to the point of intoxication. | He went out last night and hung one on with his friends. 他昨晚出去和朋友们痛饮了一番。| 近义词: tie one on, go on a bender, get drunk, paint the town red | 反义词: abstain, stay sober | 词性: idiom

hang-one-on:/hæŋ wʌn ɑːn/ | idiom |猛烈抨击; 严厉批评|To criticize someone harshly or severely. This usage is less common than the drinking-related meaning. | The coach hung one on the team after their poor performance. 教练在球队表现糟糕后严厉批评了他们。| 近义词: berate, scold, reprimand, castigate | 反义词: praise, commend | 词性: idiom
