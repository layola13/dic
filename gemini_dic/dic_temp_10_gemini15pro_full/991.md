acapsular: /eɪˈkæpsjʊlər/
adjective
无荚膜的
Lacking a capsule, especially referring to bacteria or other microorganisms that do not have a protective outer capsule.
Certain acapsular bacteria are less virulent than their capsulated counterparts.  某些无荚膜细菌的毒性低于其有荚膜的对应物。
Acapsular strains of the bacteria were used in the experiment.  实验中使用了该细菌的无荚膜菌株。
近义词: noncapsulated
反义词: capsulated
词性: adjective
