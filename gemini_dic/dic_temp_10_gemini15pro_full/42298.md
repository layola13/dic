counter-letter:/ˈkaʊntər ˌlɛtər/
|n.|回信；反驳信；抵消文件|A letter written in reply to another, especially one that opposes or contradicts the first.|The company issued a counter-letter to refute the allegations made against them.  该公司发出了一封反驳信来驳斥对他们的指控。|Synonym: reply, response, rebuttal|Antonym:  original letter, initial letter|noun

counter-letter:/ˈkaʊntər ˌlɛtər/
|n.|对照信；副本|A duplicate or copy of a letter, kept as a record or for security.|He kept a counter-letter of the agreement in a safe place. 他把协议的副本保存在安全的地方。|Synonym: duplicate, copy, replica|Antonym: original|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- + letter.  "Counter-"  acts as a prefix indicating opposition or response, originating from the Old French "contre-" meaning "against." "Letter" comes from Old French "lettre" and ultimately from Latin "littera."
