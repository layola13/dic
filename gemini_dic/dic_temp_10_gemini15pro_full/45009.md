一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? = 你怎么想？
What does she want for her birthday? = 她生日想要什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing over there? = 他在那里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she been up to lately? = 她最近在忙什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all week? = 你整个星期都在做什么？
What has he been reading recently? = 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the wedding? = 她婚礼上穿了什么？


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? = 我到达之前你做了什么？
What had they eaten for dinner? = 他们晚饭吃了什么？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been studying before you changed your major? = 你转专业前一直在学什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be presenting at the conference? = 他们在会议上将要展示什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the age of 30? = 到30岁时你将取得什么成就？
What will have changed in the next ten years? = 未来十年会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经做了五年什么？
What will they have been researching by the end of the grant period? = 到拨款期结束时，他们将一直在研究什么？



虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
I wish I knew what to tell her. = 我希望我知道该怎么告诉她。

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
There's nothing what I want here. = 这里没有我想要的东西。
