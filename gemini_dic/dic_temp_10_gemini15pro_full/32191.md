chibo:/ˈtʃiːboʊ/| n.|小餐馆，小饭馆; (尤指)供应廉价食品的小餐馆|A small restaurant or café, especially one serving inexpensive food.| We grabbed a quick bite at a local chibo. 我们在当地一家小餐馆随便吃了点东西。|近义词：bistro, café, diner, eatery|反义词: restaurant (especially a formal or expensive one)|词性: 名词

词源:  "Chibo" is a Japanese word (チボ)  meaning "small restaurant" or "café". It's often used to describe a small, casual eatery, particularly one that offers affordable meals. While it originated in Japan, it has gained some usage in English, particularly in contexts related to Japanese food or culture.
