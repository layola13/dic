lugubrious:/luːˈɡuːbriəs/ /ləˈɡuːbriəs/
adjective
悲哀的；忧郁的；阴森的
mournful, dismal, or gloomy, especially in an affected, exaggerated, or unrelieved manner.
The funeral was a lugubrious affair, with everyone dressed in black and weeping openly. 葬礼是一场悲哀的事件，每个人都穿着黑衣，公开哭泣。
The dog gave me a lugubrious look, as if begging for a treat. 小狗给了我一个忧郁的眼神，好像在乞求零食。

synonym
mournful, gloomy, sad, somber, melancholy, doleful, depressing, funereal, dismal, dark, dreary, cheerless, joyless, sorrowful, plaintive, heartbroken, despondent, despairing, dejected, crestfallen

antonym
cheerful, happy, joyful, merry, bright, lighthearted, jovial, buoyant, optimistic, positive, upbeat, cheery, blithe, sanguine, light, airy, sunny, radiant, vibrant

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *lūgubris, from lūgēre (“to mourn”).
