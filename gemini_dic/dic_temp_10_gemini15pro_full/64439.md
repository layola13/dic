ericinol:/ˌɛrɪsɪˈnɒl/ | n. |刺猬醇|A crystalline substance found in certain fungi, notably in some species of the genus *Hydnum*.  It is known for its antibiotic properties.|The researchers isolated ericinol from the fruiting bodies of the fungus.|近义词:  (Based on its antibiotic properties) antibiotic, antifungal |反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps) growth promoter|词性:noun
