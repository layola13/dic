hydrosorbic:/ˌhaɪdrəˈsɔːrbɪk/| adj.|氢山梨酸的|Relating to or containing hydrosorbic acid (a saturated fatty acid).  Derived from "hydro-" (water or hydrogen) and "sorbic" (relating to sorbic acid, a natural organic compound).|Hydrosorbic acid is used as a preservative in some food products. 氢山梨酸在一些食品中用作防腐剂。|The hydrosorbic properties of this compound make it useful in preventing spoilage.  该化合物的氢山梨酸特性使其可用于防止腐败。|近义词:hexenoic (related, but not a true synonym as it refers specifically to hexenoic acid),  |反义词: (No direct antonym exists, as it's a specific chemical term).|词性:adjective
