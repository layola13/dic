cabaret:/ˈkæbəreɪ/ (US), /ˈkæbəreɪ/ (UK)| n.|卡巴莱，歌舞表演|A restaurant or nightclub providing entertainment, typically including music, song, and dance.|例句：We went to a cabaret show in Paris. 我们去巴黎看了场歌舞表演。

例句：The cabaret featured a variety of acts, from singers to comedians.  这家卡巴莱剧场有各种各样的表演，从歌手到喜剧演员。|近义词：nightclub, club, entertainment, show|反义词:None|词性:noun
