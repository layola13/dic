muleteer:/ˌmjuːlɪˈtɪə(r)/
n.|赶骡人；骡夫|A person who drives mules.|The muleteer led his animals along the narrow mountain path. 赶骡人沿着狭窄的山路赶着他的骡子。|Synonyms: mule driver, teamster|Antonyms: N/A
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *muletier, from mule + -ier (agent suffix).  Ultimately from Latin mulus (mule).
