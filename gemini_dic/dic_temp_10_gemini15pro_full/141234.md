oozed:/uːzd/
verb
渗出；慢慢流出
(of a thick liquid) pass or flow slowly; (of a quality) be gradually revealed
The wound oozed blood.  伤口渗出了血。
His confidence oozed away as the interview went on.  随着面试的进行，他的自信逐渐消失了。
synonyms: seep, leak, discharge, exude, emanate
antonyms: gush, spurt, rush, absorb, solidify
词性: 动词
