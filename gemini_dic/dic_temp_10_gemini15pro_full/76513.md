galena:/ɡəˈliːnə/ | n. | 方铅矿 | A common lead sulfide mineral (PbS) that is the chief ore of lead. | Galena is a heavy, metallic mineral with a cubic crystal structure. 方铅矿是一种重的金属矿物，具有立方晶体结构。

Galena is often found in association with other sulfide minerals. 方铅矿经常与其他硫化物矿物伴生。 | 近义词: lead sulfide, lead glance | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *galena, from Latin galēna (“lead ore”), of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Ancient Greek γαλήνη (galḗnē, “calm, stillness, peace of mind”), referring to its supposed medicinal use in soothing ailments.
