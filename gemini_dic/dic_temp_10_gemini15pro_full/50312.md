depletory:/dɪˈpliːtəri/
adjective
消耗性的，使枯竭的
Tending to deplete or exhaust.  Reducing the fullness or richness of something.
The depletory effects of the drought on the reservoir were evident.  干旱对水库的消耗性影响是显而易见的。
近义词: exhausting, draining, depleting, impoverishing
反义词: replenishing, enriching, restorative, augmenting
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *depletorius, from deplere "to empty out," from de- "down, completely" + plere "to fill."
