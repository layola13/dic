actiniform:/ækˈtɪnɪfɔːrm/
adjective
放射形的，星形的
Resembling a ray; radiating from a centre; arranged like the rays of a star.  Having a radial structure.
The coral exhibited an actiniform pattern.  这珊瑚呈现出放射状的图案。
The starfish has an actiniform body.  海星有一个星形的身体。
近义词:radial, star-shaped, stellate
反义词:bilateral
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *actiniformis, from actino- (from Greek aktinos "ray") + -formis "-shaped."
