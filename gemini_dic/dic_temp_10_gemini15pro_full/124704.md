mown:/moʊn/
adjective
已割的；修剪过的
cut down (grass, grain, etc.) with a blade; harvested.
The freshly mown lawn smelled wonderful. 新修剪的草坪散发着美妙的香味。
近义词: cut, trimmed, reaped
反义词: overgrown, unkempt
词性: adjective

词源:  Past participle of *mow*.  From Old English *māwan*, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch *maaien* and German *mähen*.
