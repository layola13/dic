hyperbolically:/ˌhaɪpərˈbɒlɪkli/ /ˌhaɪpərˈbɑːlɪkli/| adv.|夸张地；言过其实地|In a way that uses hyperbole, or exaggeration.|He hyperbolically described the small scratch as a gaping wound. 他夸张地把小伤口说成是一个巨大的伤口。|Synonyms: exaggeratedly, extravagantly, overstatedly|Antonyms: realistically, accurately, moderately|adverb

hyperbole:/haɪˈpɜːrbəli/ /haɪˈpɜːrbəli/| n.|夸张法；夸张|Exaggerated statements or claims not meant to be taken literally.|Her claim to be the best singer in the world was pure hyperbole. 她自称是世界上最好的歌手，这纯粹是夸张。|Synonyms: exaggeration, overstatement, magnification|Antonyms: understatement, litotes|noun
