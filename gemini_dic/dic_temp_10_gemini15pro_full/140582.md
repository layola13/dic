oligosiderite:/ˌɒlɪɡoʊˈsaɪdəraɪt/
noun
低铁陨石，橄榄陨铁
A stony-iron meteorite consisting of approximately equal parts of nickel-iron and olivine.
Oligosiderites are a rare type of meteorite. 低铁陨石是一种罕见的陨石类型。
The museum acquired a new oligosiderite for its collection.  博物馆为其收藏品购置了一块新的低铁陨铁。
Synonyms: stony-iron meteorite, pallasite (sometimes)
Antonyms: N/A (lack of a direct opposite for a specific meteorite type)
