anchor:/ˈæŋkər/ (US), /ˈæŋkə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|锚，主播，依靠，固定；抛锚，担任主播，使固定|An anchor is a heavy object dropped from a boat into the water to prevent the boat from drifting.|The ship dropped anchor in the bay. 船在海湾抛锚了。|近义词：mooring, fastener, mainstay, support, host (v.)|反义词：drift, detach|noun, verb

anchor:/ˈæŋkər/ (US), /ˈæŋkə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|主持人，主播，新闻主持人；担任主持人，主持节目|A news anchor is a person who presents news on television or radio.|The news anchor reported on the latest developments. 新闻主播报道了最新进展。|近义词：presenter, host, commentator|反义词: guest, interviewee|noun, verb
