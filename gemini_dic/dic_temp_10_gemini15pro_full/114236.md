mailbags:/ˈmeɪlbæɡz/
n.
邮袋；邮包
Large bags used for carrying mail.
The postman struggled with the heavy mailbags. 邮递员吃力地搬着沉重的邮袋。
近义词：mailsacks, pouches
反义词：（无明显的反义词）
词性：名词

词源:
From "mail" (meaning "bag," later specialized to "bag for letters") + "bag."  "Mail" in this sense comes from Old French "male," ultimately from Frankish "malha" (meaning "bag, sack").
