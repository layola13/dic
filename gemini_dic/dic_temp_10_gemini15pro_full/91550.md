hyper-genetically:/ˌhaɪpər-dʒəˈnɛtɪkli/ | adv. | 超遗传地 |  Relating to or involving hypergenetics, which refers to the manipulation or alteration of genetic material beyond the natural scope of inheritance. This term is not widely used in established scientific literature and often appears in science fiction or speculative contexts.  |  "The hyper-genetically modified organisms exhibited unprecedented growth rates."  超遗传修饰的生物体表现出前所未有的生长速度。 | Near Synonyms: genetically engineered, genetically modified | Antonyms: naturally occurring, wild-type | Word Type: Adverb
