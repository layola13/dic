hoister:/ˈhɔɪstər/| n.|起重机，升降机；举起的人或物|A person or thing that hoists something.  A machine used for hoisting. |The hoister lifted the heavy cargo onto the ship. 起重机将沉重的货物吊上了船。|Synonyms: lifter, elevator, crane, winch|Antonyms: lowerer|noun
