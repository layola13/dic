ageisms:/ˈeɪdʒɪzəmz/
n.
年龄歧视（现象）, 对老年人的歧视
Prejudice or discrimination on the grounds of a person's age, typically against older people.
Ageisms in the workplace can prevent older people from getting hired or promoted.  职场上的年龄歧视会阻止老年人获得聘用或晋升。
The new policy aims to combat ageisms in hiring practices. 这项新政策旨在打击招聘中的年龄歧视。
近义词:age discrimination, prejudice against the elderly
反义词:respect for elders, age equality
