humoristic:/ˌhjuːməˈrɪstɪk/| adj.|幽默的，诙谐的|Intended to be funny; humorous.  Characterized by or expressive of humor.|His humoristic stories always lighten the mood. 他幽默的故事总是能让人心情愉悦。|近义词：humorous, comical, witty, droll|反义词：serious, solemn, grave|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *humoriste* (now *humoristique*), from *humour* + *-iste*. Ultimately from Latin *humor* ("moisture, fluid").
