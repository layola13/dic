masqueraded:/ˌmæskəˈreɪdɪd/ | v. | 伪装的，乔装的 | appeared or behaved in a way that was intended to deceive people about their true identity or intentions |  He masqueraded as a police officer to gain entry to the building. 他伪装成警察进入大楼。 | synonyms: posed, impersonated, pretended, feigned | antonyms: revealed, exposed | verb (past tense)


masquerade:/ˌmæskəˈreɪd/ | n. | 伪装，化装舞会 | an attempt to hide something, or to appear to be something that you are not; a ball at which guests wear masks and costumes | The charity ball was a masquerade, with everyone in elaborate costumes.  慈善舞会是一个化装舞会，每个人都穿着精致的服装。 | synonyms: pretense, facade, charade, disguise, masked ball | antonyms: reality, truth | noun


词源: From French *mascarade*, from Italian *mascarata*, from Arabic *maskharah* "buffoonery," from *sakhira* "to ridicule."
