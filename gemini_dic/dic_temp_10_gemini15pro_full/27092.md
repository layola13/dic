calicoes:/ˈkælɪˌkoʊz/ /ˈkælɪkəʊz/
noun
印花棉布；印花棉布制品
Plain woven cotton cloth, typically with a bright floral pattern printed on one side.
She wore a dress made of brightly colored calicoes. 她穿着一件鲜艳的印花棉布连衣裙。
Synonyms: chintz, printed cotton
Antonyms: plain cloth
n.


calico:/ˈkælɪkoʊ/ /ˈkælɪkəʊ/
noun
印花棉布；印花棉布制品；印花棉布衣服；(毛色斑驳的)三色猫
Plain woven cotton cloth, typically with a bright floral pattern printed on one side. A garment made from this cloth. A domestic cat with fur of three colours, typically black, white, and ginger.
She wore a calico dress. 她穿了一件印花棉布连衣裙。
The calico cat sat on the windowsill. 那只三色猫坐在窗台上。
Synonyms: chintz, printed cotton (for the fabric); calico cat (for the animal)
Antonyms: plain cloth (for the fabric)
n.
