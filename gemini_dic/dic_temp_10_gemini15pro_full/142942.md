out-waved:/ˌaʊtˈweɪvd/
verb
超过挥手致意
surpassed in waving; waved more vigorously or for a longer period than
The enthusiastic fans out-waved the departing celebrities. 热情的粉丝们向离开的名人们挥手致意，超过了他们的挥手。
Synonyms: outdo, surpass, exceed
Antonyms: lose to, be inferior to
词性: 动词
