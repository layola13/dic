freelancers:/ˈfriːlænsərz/| n. |自由职业者|People who work independently for various clients, rather than being employed by a single company. |Many companies hire freelancers for specialized tasks. 许多公司聘请自由职业者来完成专门的任务。|Freelancers often work from home. 自由职业者经常在家工作。|近义词：independent contractors, self-employed, gig workers|反义词：employees, staff|词性:Noun, plural
