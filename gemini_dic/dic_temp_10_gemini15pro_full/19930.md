biocompatibility:/ˌbaɪoʊkəmˌpætəˈbɪləti/ | n. |生物相容性 | The ability of a material to perform with an appropriate host response in a specific application.  |  This new implant material has excellent biocompatibility.  这种新的植入材料具有优异的生物相容性。 | synonyms: biotolerability, histocompatibility | antonyms: bioincompatibility | noun
