From Latin *nitēns*, present participle of *nitēre* "to shine," from Proto-Italic *niteō*, from Proto-Indo-European *kʷni-t-eie-*, from *kʷni-t-* "to shine," *kʷni-* "bright, shining."  Cognate with Sanskrit knītáḥ "shining."
