menschy:/ˈmɛnʃi/| adj.|正直的；有人情味的；高尚的|Having qualities of character that are admirable and humane; decent, honorable.|Example: It was a menschy thing to do, helping the elderly woman across the street.  帮助老太太过马路是一件高尚的事。
Example: He's a menschy guy, always willing to lend a hand. 他是个正直的人，总是乐于助人。|近义词：decent, honorable, kind, compassionate, upright|反义词：dishonorable, mean, cruel, selfish, unscrupulous|词性:形容词

词源:
From Yiddish מענטש (mentsh), from Middle High German mensch (“human being”), from Old High German mennisco (“human being”), from Proto-Germanic *manniskō (“human being”), diminutive of *mann- (“man”). Cognate with German Mensch (“human being”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *men- (“to think”).
