cultivatability:/ˌkʌltɪveɪtəˈbɪləti/| n. |可耕性，可培养性|The cultivatability of the land is crucial for successful farming.  这块土地的可耕性对于成功的农业至关重要。|例句：The soil's cultivatability was assessed before planting.  在种植之前对土壤的可耕性进行了评估。

例句：The cultivatability of new strains of wheat is being researched. 新的小麦品种的可培养性正在研究中。|近义词：fertility, arability, cultivableness|反义词：infertility, barrenness|词性:noun
