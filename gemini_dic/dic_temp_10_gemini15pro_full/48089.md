declassify:/diːˈklæsɪfaɪ/
verb
解密；撤销机密等级
To remove the security classification from (information, a document, etc.); to make (something) no longer secret.
The government declassified the documents relating to the Cold War. 政府解密了与冷战相关的文件。
The CIA declassified a trove of documents about the Kennedy assassination. 中央情报局解密了一批关于肯尼迪遇刺的文件。
synonyms: disclose, reveal, release, unveil, publish
antonyms: classify, conceal, hide, protect, secure
词性: 动词
