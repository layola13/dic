eschara:/ˈɛskərə/| n. |痂; (尤指苔藓虫或珊瑚的)骨骼; |A hard calcareous or chitinous supporting structure, especially of a bryozoan or coral.|The diver pointed to the eschara, a delicate, lace-like structure formed by the coral. 潜水员指着珊瑚形成的痂，一种精致的蕾丝状结构。|Synonyms: skeleton, framework, shell|Antonyms: flesh, tissue|noun
