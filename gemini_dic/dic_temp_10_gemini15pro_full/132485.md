nondescriptiveness:/ˌnɒndɪˈskrɪptɪvnəs/ | n. | 无特色；难以描述；平淡无奇 | Lack of distinguishing features or qualities; the state of being difficult to describe. |  The nondescriptiveness of the building made it easy to overlook. 这栋楼平淡无奇，很容易被忽略。| synonyms: anonymity, ordinariness, unremarkableness, featurelessness, blandness | antonyms: distinctiveness, uniqueness, remarkableness, memorability | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + descriptive + -ness.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Descriptive" comes from the Latin "describere" meaning "to write down" or "to copy." "-ness" is a suffix that forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
