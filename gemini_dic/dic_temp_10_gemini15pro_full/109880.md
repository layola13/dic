liable:/ˈlaɪəbl/| adj.|有责任的，有义务的；易于…的，倾向于…的|Subject to legal obligation; likely to do or experience something|例句：The area is liable to flooding.  这个地区容易发生洪水。|近义词：responsible, accountable, prone, susceptible|反义词:exempt, immune|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *liable (Modern French lier "to bind"), from Latin ligare "to bind".  The legal sense developed from the idea of being bound by law.
