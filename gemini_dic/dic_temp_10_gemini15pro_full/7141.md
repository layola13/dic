angelical:/ænˈdʒɛlɪkəl/ | adj. | 天使般的；似天使的；天国的 | Resembling or characteristic of an angel; exceptionally beautiful, innocent, or kind. | Her angelical voice filled the church. 她的天使般的声音充满了教堂。| The sunset painted the sky in angelical hues. 日落将天空染成了天使般的色彩。 | 近义词:cherubic, seraphic, heavenly, divine, ethereal | 反义词:devilish, demonic, fiendish, wicked, unholy | 词性:形容词
词源:Late Middle English: from late Latin angelicalis, from angelus ‘angel’.
