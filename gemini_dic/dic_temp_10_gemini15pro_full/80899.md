ETYMOLOGY->
From "governor-general" + "-ship". "Governor" comes from Old French "governeur," ultimately from Latin "gubernator" (helmsman, governor), from "gubernare" (to steer, govern). "General" comes from Old French "general," from Latin "generalis" (belonging to a kind or genus, general).  "-ship" is an Old English suffix denoting state, condition, or office.
