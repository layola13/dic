micro-environmental:/ˌmaɪkroʊɪnˌvaɪrənˈmɛntl/
adjective|微环境的|Relating to the immediate local environment surrounding something, especially a living organism or a specific factor such as a business.|The micro-environmental factors affecting the plant's growth include temperature, humidity, and light levels.  植物生长的微环境因素包括温度、湿度和光照水平。|local, immediate, surrounding|global, general, overall|adjective

micro-environment:/ˌmaɪkroʊɪnˈvaɪrənˌmɛnt/
noun|微环境|The immediate local environment surrounding something, especially a living organism or a specific factor such as a business.|The company's micro-environment includes its customers, suppliers, and competitors.  公司的微环境包括其客户、供应商和竞争对手。|locale, setting, surroundings|macro-environment, global environment|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"micro-" + "environment"
"micro-" : from Greek "mikrós" meaning "small"
"environment": from Old French "environer" (to surround)
