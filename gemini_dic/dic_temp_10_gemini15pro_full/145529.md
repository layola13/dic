overcomers:/ˌoʊvərˈkʌmərz/ | n. |克服者，战胜者|Those who overcome obstacles or adversity.  People who triumph over difficulties or challenges.|
例句：The overcomers of this challenging race will be rewarded.  这场充满挑战的比赛的胜出者将会获得奖励。

例句：The book tells the stories of overcomers who battled against all odds. 这本书讲述了克服重重困难的人们的故事。

近义词：victors, conquerors, champions, winners, survivors|
反义词：losers, defeated, vanquished|
词性：名词
