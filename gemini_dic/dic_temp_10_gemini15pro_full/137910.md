nonvocalization:/ˌnɒnvoʊkələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 不发声；非发声化 | The nonvocalization of a vowel can make a word difficult to understand. 元音的不发声会使一个单词难以理解。| Example: His nonvocalization of his disagreement made it difficult to address the issue. 他没有将他的反对意见说出来，这使得问题难以解决。| Synonyms: silence, muteness, voicelessness | Antonyms: vocalization, utterance, expression | noun

nonvocalization:/ˌnɒnvoʊkələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 非发声；不发声化 | The nonvocalization of certain consonants is common in some dialects.某些辅音的不发声在某些方言中很常见。| Example: The nonvocalization of the 'h' sound is a characteristic of this regional accent. 'h' 音的不发声是这种地方口音的一个特征。 | Synonyms: suppression of sound, unvocalized expression | Antonyms: vocalization, articulation | noun
