overbarren:/ˌoʊvərˈbærən/ | adj. | 过于贫瘠的，荒芜的 | Excessively barren; too barren to produce much or anything at all.  Characterized by extreme infertility or unproductive. | The overbarren landscape offered little hope for the settlers.  这片过于贫瘠的土地给定居者带来渺茫的希望。 | The overbarren fields yielded no crops this year. 今年这些过于贫瘠的田地颗粒无收。 | 近义词：infertile, sterile, unproductive, desolate | 反义词：fertile, productive, fruitful | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "above, over") + "barren" (Middle English barain, from Old French baraigne, of uncertain origin, possibly related to Late Latin *barranus "having a bare head").
