overcaustic:/ˌoʊvərˈkɔːstɪk/
adjective
过于刻薄的，过分尖酸的
Excessively sarcastic or critical; too harsh or biting.
Her overcaustic remarks alienated her friends. 她过于刻薄的言辞疏远了她的朋友。
The critic's overcaustic review of the play angered the actors. 剧评人对这部戏过于尖酸刻薄的评论激怒了演员们。
近义词:abrasive, acrimonious, biting, harsh, sardonic, scathing, vitriolic
反义词:gentle, kind, mild, pleasant, polite, supportive
词性:adjective

词源:
From "over-" (meaning "excessively") + "caustic" (from Latin "causticus," from Greek "kaustikos," meaning "burning").
