debunker:/diːˈbʌŋkər/
n.
揭穿真相的人；戳穿谎言的人
a person who exposes the falseness of a myth, idea, or belief.
The journalist worked as a debunker of popular myths surrounding the health benefits of certain foods.  这位记者揭穿了围绕某些食物健康益处的流行误区。

He's a debunker of conspiracy theories. 他是一个阴谋论的揭穿者。


近义词:exposer, unmasker, refuter
反义词:propagandist, promoter, advocate
词性:noun
