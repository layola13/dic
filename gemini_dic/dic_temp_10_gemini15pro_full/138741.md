numbness:/ˈnʌmnəs/| n.|麻木，麻痹|A loss of sensation or feeling in a part of your body.|My foot went to sleep and I felt a strange numbness. 我的脚麻了，感觉很奇怪的麻木。|近义词：insensibility, deadness, paralysis|反义词: feeling, sensation|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *nome, num, from Old English numen (“taken, seized, captured, plundered”), past participle of niman (“to take, seize, capture, receive, obtain”), from Proto-Germanic *numaną (“to take”), from Proto-Indo-European *nem- (“to take, distribute, allot”). Cognate with German nehmen (“to take”), Dutch nemen (“to take”), Swedish nymma (“to seize”), Latin emō (“I buy”).  The sense development is from "taken, seized (by illness)" to "benumbed."
