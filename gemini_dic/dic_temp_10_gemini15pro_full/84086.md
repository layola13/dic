half-ingenious:/ˌhɑːf ɪnˈdʒiːniəs/
adjective
半巧妙的；略显机灵的
Partially or somewhat ingenious; clever or inventive to a limited degree.
His solution to the problem was half-ingenious, it worked, but there was a much simpler way. 他解决问题的方法略显机灵，虽然有效，但还有更简单的方法。
It was a half-ingenious plan, showing some creativity but also some flaws.  这计划有点小聪明，展现了一些创造力，但也有一些缺陷。
near synonyms:  partially clever, somewhat inventive, slightly resourceful
near antonyms:  unimaginative, uninspired, dull, clumsy
