gadolinia:/ˌɡædɵˈlɪniə/ | n. |氧化钆|A white or slightly pink powder, Gd₂O₃, used in making phosphors for color television tubes, as a control material in nuclear reactors, and in certain glasses.|Gadolinia is used as a burnable poison in nuclear reactors.|近义词: (As a neutron absorber) boron carbide, hafnium oxide|反义词:  (Generally no direct opposite, but in the context of nuclear reactors) fuel, moderator|词性:noun
