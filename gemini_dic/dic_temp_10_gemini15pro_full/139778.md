octandrous: /ɒkˈtændrəs/
adjective
八雄蕊的；具八个雄蕊的
Having eight stamens.
例句：The octandrous flower has eight stamens.  这朵八雄蕊的花有八个雄蕊。

例句：Octandrous plants are common in certain families.  八雄蕊植物在某些科中很常见。

近义词: eight-stamened
反义词:  (No single direct antonym; would need to specify a different number of stamens, e.g., tetrandrous, pentandrous, etc.)
词性: adjective
