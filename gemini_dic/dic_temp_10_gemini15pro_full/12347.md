ashamednesses:/əˈʃeɪmdnɪsɪz/
n.
羞愧，惭愧（复数）
The state of being ashamed; feelings of shame.
He confessed his sins with many ashamednesses. 他羞愧地忏悔了他的罪过。
近义词:shame, humiliation, embarrassment, mortification
反义词:pride, honor, confidence
词性:名词
