overforces:/ˌoʊvərˈfɔːrsɪz/ | v. |过度用力；过分强调；强迫| To exert excessive force on something; to emphasize or enforce something too strongly. | The coach overforces the importance of winning, putting immense pressure on the young athletes. 教练过分强调获胜的重要性，给年轻运动员带来了巨大的压力。| The government overforces the new law, leading to public resentment. 政府过度执行新法律，导致公众不满。 | 近义词: overemphasize, overdo, overstress | 反义词: understate, downplay | 词性: 动词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "force" (verb meaning "to compel by physical, moral, or intellectual means").
