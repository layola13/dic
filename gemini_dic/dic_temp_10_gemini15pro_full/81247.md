grangerism: /ˈɡreɪndʒərɪzəm/ | n. |剽窃，抄袭（尤指从多处来源拼凑）| The act of compiling a work derived from other unacknowledged sources, especially by piecing together sections from various sources. |  His history essay was flagged for grangerism because he had copied sections from multiple websites without citation. 他的历史论文被标记为剽窃，因为他从多个网站复制了部分内容而没有注明出处。| Example 2: The professor warned the students against grangerism, emphasizing the importance of original thought. 教授警告学生不要剽窃，强调了原创思想的重要性。 | Synonym: plagiarism, copying, appropriation | Antonym: originality, attribution | noun
