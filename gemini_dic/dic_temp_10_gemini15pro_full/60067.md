edwardianism:/ˌɛdwɔːrˈdiːənɪzəm/
noun
爱德华时代风格；爱德华七世时代思潮
The characteristics of the Edwardian era, especially in Britain, typically regarded as a period of peace and prosperity.  This includes the fashions, social norms, and artistic styles prevalent during the reign of King Edward VII (1901-1910).
The house was a perfect example of Edwardianism, with its ornate details and spacious rooms. 这座房子是爱德华时代风格的完美典范，拥有华丽的细节和宽敞的房间。
Synonyms: Edwardian era, Edwardian period
Antonyms: Victorianism, Georgianism
