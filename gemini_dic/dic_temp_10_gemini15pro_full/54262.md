disgracing:/dɪsˈɡreɪsɪŋ/
verb
使丢脸，使蒙羞
bringing shame or dishonor to
例句：He is disgracing his family by his behavior.  他因自己的行为使家人蒙羞。

例句：She felt she was disgracing herself by accepting charity. 她觉得自己接受施舍很丢脸。

近义词：shaming, humiliating, dishonoring
反义词：honoring, glorifying
词性：v.
