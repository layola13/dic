demissive:/dɪˈmɪsɪv/
adjective
轻蔑的，不屑一顾的
Treating something as unimportant, unworthy of serious consideration, or showing little regard.
She gave a demissive wave of her hand. 她轻蔑地挥了挥手。
近义词:contemptuous, disdainful, scornful
反义词:respectful, attentive, considerate
词性:adjective

demissive:/dɪˈmɪsɪv/
adjective
表示轻蔑的
Showing a lack of respect or interest.
He responded to my question with a demissive shrug. 他对我的问题不屑一顾地耸了耸肩。
近义词:disdainful, contemptuous
反义词:respectful, interested
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin dismissivus, from Latin dimittere "to send away," from dis- "away" + mittere "to send."
