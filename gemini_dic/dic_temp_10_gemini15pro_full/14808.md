axel:/ˈæksəl/| n.|阿克塞尔跳，轴，车轴|A figure-skating jump executed by taking off from the forward outside edge of one skate, making one and a half turns in the air, and landing on the backward outside edge of the other skate.  Also a general term for an axle or shaft.|She landed the triple axel perfectly. 她完美地完成了三周半跳。|近义词：spin, jump, shaft, axle|反义词:|n.
