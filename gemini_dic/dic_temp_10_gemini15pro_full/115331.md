mannitol:/ˈmænɪtɒl/ (UK) /ˈmænɪtɔːl/ (US)| n. |甘露醇|A white crystalline alcohol found in many plants and used as a diuretic (increases urination) and a sweetener, especially in foods for diabetics.|Mannitol is commonly used to reduce swelling in the brain. 甘露醇常用于减轻脑肿胀。|The bakery uses mannitol as a sweetener in its sugar-free cookies.  这家面包店在其无糖饼干中使用甘露醇作为甜味剂。|Synonyms: sugar alcohol, hexitol|Antonyms: None (Not applicable for this type of word)|Noun
