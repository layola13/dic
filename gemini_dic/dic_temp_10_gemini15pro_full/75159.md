fritter:/ˈfrɪtər/ (US), /ˈfrɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |油炸馅饼； fritter away (v.) 浪费，挥霍|A small quantity of batter, often containing fruit or meat, which is fried.  / To waste time, money, or opportunity.|例句：She made apple fritters for dessert. 她做了苹果油炸饼当甜点。


例句：He frittered away his inheritance on gambling. 他把遗产都浪费在了赌博上。|近义词：(n.) pancake, doughnut, beignet; (v.) squander, waste, dissipate |反义词：(v.) save, conserve|词性: 名词；动词
