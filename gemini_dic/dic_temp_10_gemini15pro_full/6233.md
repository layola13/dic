amniocenteses:/ˌæmniəʊsɛnˈtiːsiːz/| n.|羊膜穿刺术（复数）|The sampling of amniotic fluid using a hollow needle inserted into the uterus, to screen for developmental abnormalities in a fetus.|例句：Amniocenteses are typically performed between 15 and 20 weeks of pregnancy. 羊膜穿刺术通常在怀孕15到20周之间进行。

例句：Multiple amniocenteses were performed to monitor the fetus's lung development.  多次进行羊膜穿刺术以监测胎儿的肺部发育。


近义词：amniotic fluid test|反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑相关概念如：non-invasive prenatal testing 无创产前检测) |词性:noun
