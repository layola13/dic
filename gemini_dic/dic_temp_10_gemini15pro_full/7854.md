ante-chamber:/ˈæntiˌtʃeɪmbər/ /ˈæntiˌtʃeɪmbər/
n.
前厅，接待室
A smaller room leading to a main room; a waiting room.
The guests waited in the ante-chamber before being admitted to the main hall. 客人们在被允许进入大厅之前在前厅等候。

The important client was kept waiting in the ante-chamber. 重要的客户被留在前厅等候。

近义词: waiting room, lobby, foyer, vestibule
反义词: main room, inner chamber

词性: noun

词源: From Old French *antechambre*, from ante- + chambre (“room”).
