factfinder:/ˈfæktˌfaɪndər/| n.|事实调查员，真相调查者|A person who investigates and determines the facts of a case or situation. | The judge appointed a factfinder to determine the cause of the accident. 法官指派了一名事实调查员来确定事故原因。| The factfinder interviewed witnesses and reviewed evidence to establish what happened. 事实调查员询问了证人并审查了证据以确定发生了什么。| 近义词: investigator, researcher, inquirer | 反义词: fabricator, liar | 词性: 名词

词源: fact (事实) + finder (发现者)  From the noun "fact" and the agentive suffix "-finder," denoting someone who finds or establishes something.
