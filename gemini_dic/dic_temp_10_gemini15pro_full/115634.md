marchman:/ˈmɑːrtʃmən/ | n. |士兵；行军者|A soldier or someone who marches regularly. |例句：The weary marchmen finally reached their destination.  疲惫的行军者终于到达了目的地。

例句：The marchmen sang songs to keep their spirits up during the long journey. 行军者们在漫长的旅途中唱歌以保持士气。|近义词：soldier, infantryman, trooper|反义词:civilian|词性:名词
