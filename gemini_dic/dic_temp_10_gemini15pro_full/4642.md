ale:/eɪl/ | n. |麦芽酒；啤酒 | A type of beer, usually bitter, made without hops originally but now usually hopped. |  例句：He ordered a pint of ale. 他点了一品脱麦芽酒。

例句：The pub served a variety of local ales. 这家酒吧供应各种当地酿造的麦芽酒。

近义词：beer, brew | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "lager" as a contrasting type of beer) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *alu (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *aluth- (compare Old Norse ǫl, Old High German alo), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂elut- (“bitter”). Cognate with Lithuanian alùs, Latin alumen (“alum”).
