partnerships:/ˈpɑːrtnərʃɪps/| n. |合作关系，伙伴关系|The state of being a partner; participation with another or others in some action or endeavor.  A relationship resembling that of partners; an association.| We have built strong partnerships with local businesses. 我们与当地企业建立了牢固的合作关系。|Synonyms: alliances, collaborations, associations, unions|Antonyms: rifts, divisions, separations|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From partner + -ship.  Partner comes from the Old French parçonier meaning "partner, sharer, associate," ultimately from the Latin partiri "to divide, share."
