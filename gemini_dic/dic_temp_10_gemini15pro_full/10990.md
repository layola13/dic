arborator:/ˈɑːrbərˌeɪtər/ | n. | 树木栽培家；树艺师；树木专家 | A specialist in the cultivation and care of trees, especially one who is trained and licensed to do pruning, spraying, and other work on trees. |  The arborator recommended removing the diseased branches to save the tree.  树艺师建议移除病枝以拯救这棵树。 | Synonyms: tree surgeon, arboriculturist | Antonyms: N/A | Noun

arborator:/ˈɑːrbərˌeɪtər/ | n. | 仲裁人，裁决人 | A person chosen to settle a dispute or difference, especially one formally appointed to do so. (Less common usage) | The two companies chose an arborator to resolve their contract dispute. 两家公司选择了一名仲裁人来解决他们的合同纠纷。 | Synonyms: arbitrator, mediator, judge | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
