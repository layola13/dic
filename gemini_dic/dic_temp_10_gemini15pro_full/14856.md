axolotl:/ˈæksəlɒtl/ (US) /ˈæksəlɒtəl/ (UK)| n.|美西螈；墨西哥钝口螈|A type of salamander that lives underwater and is native to Mexico. It is known for its ability to regenerate lost limbs.|The axolotl is a fascinating creature. 美西螈是一种迷人的生物。

The axolotl is critically endangered in the wild. 野生美西螈极度濒危。

|近义词：salamander, Mexican salamander|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
