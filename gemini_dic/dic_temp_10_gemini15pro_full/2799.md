advances:/ədˈvænsɪz/
n.|前进，进展；预付款；求爱|progress or development in a particular activity or area of understanding.  A sum of money paid or credited before it is due or earned. An approach made to someone, often with romantic or sexual intent.|Recent advances in medical technology have saved countless lives. 近年来医学技术的进步拯救了无数生命。|近义词：progress, development, improvement, headway, gain, breakthrough, leap, step, progression, rise|反义词：decline, setback, retreat, regression, loss|词性:名词(复数)

advances:/ədˈvænsɪz/
v.|前进，推进；预付；提出；提升|move forward, typically in a purposeful way. Pay (someone) money before it is due. Put forward (a theory or suggestion). Further the progress of (something); promote or develop.|The army advanced towards the city. 军队向城市推进。|近义词：proceed, progress, move forward, go forward, forge ahead, push on, press on, make headway|反义词：retreat, withdraw, recede, retire|词性:动词(第三人称单数)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *avancer (Modern French avancer), from Late Latin *abanteare "to move forward," from Latin ab "from, away from" + ante "before."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company advances its research in new technologies. = 这家公司推进其在新技术方面的研究。
He advances a new theory about the origin of the universe. = 他提出了一个关于宇宙起源的新理论。

一般过去时:
The soldiers advanced bravely into enemy territory. = 士兵们勇敢地向敌方领土推进。
She advanced him some money for the trip. = 她预付给他一些旅行的费用。

现在完成时:
Medical science has advanced significantly in recent years. = 近年来医学取得了显著的进步。
They have advanced their understanding of the disease. = 他们对这种疾病的理解有所加深。

过去完成时:
The team had advanced to the finals before they were disqualified. = 该队在被取消资格之前已经晋级决赛。
He had advanced a considerable sum of money to the project. = 他已经为这个项目预付了相当多的资金。

将来时:
We will advance our plans for the new product launch. = 我们将推进新产品发布的计划。
The company will advance him to a higher position. = 公司将提拔他到更高的职位。

虚拟语气:
If he advanced a more convincing argument, I might agree. = 如果他提出更有说服力的论点，我可能会同意。
I wish they would advance their research more quickly. = 我希望他们能更快地推进他们的研究。

被动语态:
The project was advanced by the generous donation. = 这项计划因慷慨的捐赠而得以推进。
A new theory is being advanced by the researchers. = 研究人员正在提出一个新的理论。

疑问句:
How quickly is the technology advancing? = 这项技术发展有多快？
Why did they advance him so much money? = 他们为什么预付给他这么多钱？

否定句:
The army did not advance any further that day. = 那天军队没有再往前推进。
He didn't advance any new ideas in the meeting. = 他在会议上没有提出任何新想法。


PHRASE->

advance payment = 预付款
