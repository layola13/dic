overpicture:/ˈoʊvərˌpɪktʃər/ | n. |  叠加图像；覆盖画面；附加意义 | An image superimposed on another, often conveying additional meaning or symbolism. | The director used an overpicture of a dove to symbolize peace. 导演用鸽子的叠加图像来象征和平。| synonym:  superimposition, overlay | antonym: background, underlay | noun

overpicture:/ˈoʊvərˌpɪktʃər/ | vt. |  在…上叠加图像；赋予…附加意义 | To superimpose an image on another; to add a further meaning or interpretation to something. | The news channel overpictured the president's speech with images of the protesting crowds.  新闻频道在总统演讲的画面上叠加了抗议人群的图像。| synonym:  superimpose, overlay | antonym:  underlay | verb
