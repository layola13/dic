barons: /ˈbærənz/ | n. | 男爵（复数）|  A nobleman ranking below a viscount but above a knight; a powerful person in a particular business or industry. | The barons controlled vast estates and held considerable power. 男爵们控制着广阔的土地，拥有相当大的权力。| The media barons own most of the newspapers.  媒体大亨拥有大部分报纸。| 近义词：noblemen, lords, magnates, tycoons | 反义词: commoners, peasants | 词性: noun

词源: From Old French *baron*, itself of uncertain origin, possibly from a Frankish word *baro* meaning "free man, warrior."
