no-nonsense:/ˌnoʊˈnɒnsəns/| adj.|务实的；严肃的；直截了当的|Practical and serious, and only interested in doing things efficiently; straightforward.|She has a no-nonsense approach to management. 她有一套务实的管理方法。|The teacher took a no-nonsense approach to discipline. 这位老师对纪律采取了严肃的态度。|近义词：practical, serious, straightforward, businesslike, down-to-earth|反义词：frivolous, impractical, unrealistic, whimsical|词性:形容词

词源:  The term "no-nonsense" originated in the mid-19th century.  It's a simple combination of "no" and "nonsense," literally meaning "no frivolous or foolish behavior."
