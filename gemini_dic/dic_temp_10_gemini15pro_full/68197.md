fair-mindedness:/ˌfeərˈmaɪndɪdnəs/ | n. | 公正;公平;不偏不倚 | The state of being fair and unbiased in one's judgment and consideration of others' views.  |  She demonstrated fair-mindedness in considering both sides of the argument. 她考虑了论证的双方，展现了公正的态度。 | impartiality, objectivity, neutrality, justice | bias, prejudice, partiality | noun

fair-minded:/ˌfeərˈmaɪndɪd/ | adj. | 公正的;公平的;不偏不倚的 | Having or showing a disposition to deal with people and situations in a just, unbiased way. |  He is a fair-minded judge who listens carefully to all the evidence. 他是一位公正的法官，认真听取所有证据。 | impartial, objective, unbiased, just, equitable | biased, prejudiced, partial, unfair | adjective
