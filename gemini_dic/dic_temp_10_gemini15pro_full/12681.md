assentatory:/əˈsɛntətɔːri/ /əˈsɛntətɔːri/
adjective
谄媚的；奉承的
Given to or marked by flattery or exaggerated praise.
例句：His assentatory remarks did not impress the CEO.  他谄媚的话并没有打动CEO。

例句：She delivered an assentatory speech, praising the king's wisdom and benevolence. 她发表了一篇阿谀奉承的演讲，赞扬国王的智慧和仁慈。

近义词：flattering, adulatory, fawning, sycophantic
反义词：critical, disapproving, censorious
词性：adjective
