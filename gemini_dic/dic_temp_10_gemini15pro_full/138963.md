ny:/niː/ /naɪ/
pron.|什么,哪一个,哪一些|Something or someone that is not known or specified. |What's your name? 你叫什么名字？|近义词:which, whatever|反义词: (无明确的反义词，取决于具体语境)|pron.


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What does he do for a living? = 他以什么为生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? = 她正在做什么晚餐？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done to deserve this award? = 她做了什么才配得上这个奖？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been studying at university? = 他一直在大学里学什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃了什么？
What happened at the party last night? = 昨晚的派对发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? = 我看到她的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you came here? = 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had they discussed before the meeting started? = 会议开始前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been working on before she changed jobs? = 她换工作之前一直在做什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后打算做什么？
What will happen if we don't finish this project on time? = 如果我们不按时完成这个项目会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们将在下次会议上讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到今年年底你将取得什么成就？
What will have changed by the time we get back? = 等我们回来的时候会有什么变化？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? = 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业的时候，她将已经学习了什么多久了？


疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what you're saying. = 她不明白你在说什么。

被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
I wish I knew what to say. = 我希望我知道该说什么。
