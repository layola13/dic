paretic: /pəˈrɛtɪk/ | adj. | 部分麻痹的；瘫痪的 |  Relating to or affected by paresis, a condition characterized by partial paralysis. |  The paretic patient struggled to lift his arm.  这位部分瘫痪的病人努力抬起他的手臂。 | synonyms: paralyzed, palsied, crippled | antonyms: healthy, mobile, sound, robust | adjective

Etymology:  From Greek *parētikós*, from pariemi "I let go, relax," related to *paresis*.
