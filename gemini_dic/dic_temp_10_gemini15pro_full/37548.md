commons:/ˈkɒmənz/ (UK), /ˈkɑːmənz/ (US)| n. |公共用地；平民；下议院|Land or resources belonging to or affecting the whole of a community.  The elected representatives of the ordinary people in the UK Parliament. |The sheep grazed on the village commons. 羊群在村庄的公共牧场上吃草。|Synonyms: common land, communal land, public land, grassland, pasture, green, open space|Antonyms: private land|Noun
