mounting:/ˈmaʊntɪŋ/| adj. n.|上升的，增长的；安装，装配|increasing, growing (adj.); the act of fixing something in place (n.)|例句：Mounting pressure forced the government to act.  越来越大的压力迫使政府采取行动。|近义词：increasing, rising, escalating, assembling, installing|反义词:decreasing, declining, dismantling, removing|词性:adjective, noun
mounting:/ˈmaʊntɪŋ/| n.|底座，支架|a supporting structure or base|例句：The diamond was held in a gold mounting.  钻石被固定在一个金底座上。|近义词：base, support, frame, setting|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mounten*, from Old French *monter* "to go up, climb, mount," from Vulgar Latin *montare*, from Latin *mons*, *montem* "mountain." The noun form developed later.
