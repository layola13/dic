loaves:/loʊvz/
n.
面包(loaf 的复数形式)
Plural form of loaf, referring to more than one unit of bread.
The baker sold five loaves of bread this morning.  今天早上面包师卖了五个面包。
近义词:breads (informal, less common)
反义词:None (Loaves is a plural noun; there's no direct opposite)
词性:Noun


loaf:/loʊf/
n.
一条面包;一个面包
A shaped mass of baked bread.
I bought a loaf of sourdough bread from the bakery. 我从面包店买了一条酸面包。
近义词:bread (general term)
反义词:None (Loaf refers to a unit; there's no direct opposite)
词性:Noun


Etymology:
From Old English *hlāf, from Proto-Germanic *hlaibaz, from Proto-Indo-European *kloybos. Cognate with Old Frisian hlâf, Old Saxon hlâf, Old High German leib, Old Norse hleifr, Gothic hlaifs.
