elaolite:/ˈɛləˌlaɪt/| n. |霞石|A feldspathoid mineral, Na(AlSi3O8), occurring in intrusive igneous rocks. It is usually massive or granular and ranges in color from colorless to gray, green, or reddish.|Elaeolite syenite is a rare rock type. 霞石正长岩是一种稀有岩石。|Synonyms: nepheline, nephelite|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a specific mineral)|Noun
