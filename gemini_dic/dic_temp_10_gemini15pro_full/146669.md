overhauler:/ˌoʊvərˈhɔːlər/| n.|检修员，大修者，彻底革新者|A person who overhauls something, especially a machine or a system.|The aircraft overhauler found a critical fault in the engine.  飞机检修员在引擎中发现了一个严重故障。|近义词：renovator, repairer, restorer|反义词：saboteur, damager|noun

overhaul:/ˌoʊvərˈhɔːl/| vt.  n.|彻底检修，大修，全面改革|To examine and repair something thoroughly, especially a machine or system; a thorough examination and repair.|The engine needs a complete overhaul.  这台发动机需要彻底检修。|近义词：renovate, repair, restore|反义词：damage, neglect|verb, noun
