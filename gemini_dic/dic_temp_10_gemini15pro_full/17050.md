bathometer:/bæˈθɒmɪtər/| n. |测深仪，水深测量器|An instrument used for measuring the depth of water, especially in the sea.|The oceanographers used a bathometer to determine the depth of the trench.  海洋学家使用测深仪来确定海沟的深度。|The researchers lowered the bathometer into the lake to measure its deepest point. 研究人员将测深仪放入湖中以测量其最深处。|近义词：depth gauge,  fathometer,  sounding line|反义词: None|词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek bathys "deep" + -meter "measure."
