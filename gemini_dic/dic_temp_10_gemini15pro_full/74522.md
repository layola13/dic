fraternals:/frəˈtɜːrnəlz/
noun
兄弟会成员; 兄弟会组织
Organizations, especially men's clubs, societies, or orders, often with secret rituals and a social or charitable purpose.  Often used in the plural.
The local fraternals sponsored a charity golf tournament. 当地的兄弟会组织赞助了一场慈善高尔夫球赛。
Synonyms: brotherhoods, societies, lodges, orders
Antonyms: sororities (specifically for women)
词性: noun
