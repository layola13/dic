dieticians:/ˌdaɪəˈtɪʃənz/
n.
营养师；饮食学家
A dietician is an expert on diet and nutrition.
例句：The dietician advised her to eat more fruits and vegetables.  营养师建议她多吃水果和蔬菜。

例句：Many athletes consult with dieticians to optimize their performance. 许多运动员咨询营养师以优化他们的表现。


近义词：nutritionists, dietitians
反义词: (没有直接的反义词)
词性: 名词 (plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From "diet" + "-ician" (suffix forming nouns denoting a specialist in a specified field).  "Diet" ultimately comes from the Greek "diaita" meaning "manner of living."
