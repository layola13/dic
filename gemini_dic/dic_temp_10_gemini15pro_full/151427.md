parodies: /ˈpærədiz/ (US), /ˈpærədiːz/ (UK)| n. |滑稽模仿；拙劣的模仿；恶搞|An imitation of a particular writer, artist, or genre, exaggerating it deliberately to produce a comic effect.|The show featured several hilarious parodies of popular songs.  这个节目滑稽地模仿了几首流行歌曲。|synonyms: spoof, burlesque, lampoon, caricature, travesty|antonyms: homage, tribute|noun

parodies: /ˈpærədiz/ (US), /ˈpærədiːz/ (UK)| v. |滑稽地模仿；拙劣地模仿|Produce a humorously exaggerated imitation of (a writer, artist, or genre).|The comedian parodied the president's mannerisms perfectly.  这位喜剧演员完美地模仿了总统的举止。|synonyms: mock, mimic, satirize, ape|antonyms: emulate, honor|verb
