fanzine:/ˈfænziːn/
n.
同人志；爱好者杂志
A non-official magazine or newspaper produced by fans of a particular cultural phenomenon (such as a literary character, rock group, or television show), for other fans.
Example: She publishes a fanzine dedicated to her favorite band. 她出版了一本专门介绍她最喜欢的乐队的同人志。
Example:  He discovered the band through an online fanzine. 他通过一个在线同人志发现了这个乐队。

近义词:magazine, journal, periodical
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "official publication")
词性: noun
