millstones:/ˈmɪlˌstoʊnz/| n.|磨石；沉重的负担|Large, heavy circular stones used for grinding grain. Also used figuratively to describe a heavy burden or responsibility.|The debt was a millstone around his neck.|Synonyms: burden, encumbrance, weight, load|Antonyms: asset, advantage, help, boon|noun
