misdemeanor: /ˌmɪsdɪˈmiːnər/ | n. | 轻罪；不端行为 | A misdemeanor is a minor wrongdoing. |  He was charged with a misdemeanor for shoplifting. 他因商店行窃被指控犯有轻罪。

| Example: Parking violations are considered misdemeanors. 违章停车被认为是轻罪。 | Synonym: offense, infraction, petty crime | Antonym: felony | noun
