breather:/ˈbriːðər/| n.|休息；喘息；短暂的缓解；呼吸器|A short rest or pause from an activity, providing an opportunity to relax or recover; a device that helps someone to breathe.|After running the marathon, she needed a breather. 跑完马拉松后，她需要休息一下。|Synonyms: rest, pause, respite, break, lull, intermission|Antonyms: exertion, strain, stress, labor|noun


breather:/ˈbriːðər/| n.|喘气的人；呼吸孔|A person who breathes; a vent or opening that allows air to circulate.|He's a heavy breather when he exercises. 他运动时呼吸很重。|Synonyms: respirator, ventilator|Antonyms:  (For a person) none; (For a vent) blockage, obstruction|noun
