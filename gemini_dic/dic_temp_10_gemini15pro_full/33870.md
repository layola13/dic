circumscriptively:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈskrɪptɪvli/
adverb
限制地；在限定范围内地
in a way that limits or restricts something; within clearly defined limits
He spoke circumscriptively, avoiding any mention of the controversial topic. 他说话很谨慎，避免提及任何有争议的话题。
Synonyms: restrictively, narrowly, limitedly
Antonyms: broadly, extensively, widely
词性: 副词

circumscribe:/ˈsɜːrkəmˌskraɪb/
verb
限制；限定
restrict (something) within limits
The government's powers are circumscribed by law. 政府的权力受法律限制。
Synonyms: restrict, limit, confine
Antonyms: expand, broaden, enlarge
词性: 动词

circumscription:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈskrɪpʃən/
noun
限制；限定；界限
the act of restricting something within limits; a boundary
The circumscription of his authority led to frustration. 对他权力的限制导致了挫折。
Synonyms: restriction, limitation, confinement
Antonyms: expansion, enlargement, broadening
词性: 名词
