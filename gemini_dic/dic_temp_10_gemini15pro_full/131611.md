noncompliances:/ˌnɒnkəmˈplaɪənsɪz/
n.
不符合，违规行为
Failure or refusal to comply with a rule, regulation, or request.
The company was fined for multiple noncompliances with safety regulations.  这家公司因多次违反安全规定而被罚款。
noncompliance, violation, breach, infringement, infraction
compliance, conformity, observance
noun
