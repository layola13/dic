nonabandonment:/ˌnɒnəˈbændənmənt/ | n. | 不放弃，不抛弃 | The principle of nonabandonment is crucial in healthcare, ensuring patients receive continuous care. |  The policy emphasizes the nonabandonment of clients, even in challenging circumstances. | 近义词: commitment, perseverance, dedication, support | 反义词: abandonment, desertion, neglect | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "abandonment" (from Old French "abandoner," meaning "to relinquish control").
