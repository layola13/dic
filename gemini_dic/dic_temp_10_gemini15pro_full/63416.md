entrances:/ˈɛntrənsɪz/
n.
入口，进入；进入权；开始，开端
The main entrance to the building is on Main Street.  这栋建筑的主入口在主街上。
The actress made a dramatic entrance. 女演员戏剧性地登场。
The book describes the entrance of women into the workforce. 这本书描述了妇女进入劳动大军的情况。

近义词: entry, doorway, access, ingress, beginning, commencement
反义词: exit, egress, departure, conclusion

词性: 名词 (plural of entrance)

词源:  From Old French *entrance (Modern French entrée), from entrer "to enter," from Latin intrare, from intra "within."
