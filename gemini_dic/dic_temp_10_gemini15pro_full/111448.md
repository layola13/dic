llog:/ˈlɒɡ/ | n. | 日志，记录 | A detailed record of events, data, or observations. |  Example: The captain kept a detailed log of the voyage. 船长详细记录了航海日志。

Example:  The system automatically generates a log of all user activity. 系统会自动生成所有用户活动的日志。 | Synonyms: record, journal, diary, chronicle | Antonyms:  None | Noun
