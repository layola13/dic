molecularity:/ˌmɒlɪˈkjʊlərɪti/ /ˌmoʊlɪˈkjʊlærɪti/| n. |分子数，分子性|The number of molecules involved in an elementary reaction step.  A reaction can be unimolecular, bimolecular, or termolecular.|The molecularity of this reaction is two.|近义词: reaction order (sometimes)|反义词: N/A|noun
