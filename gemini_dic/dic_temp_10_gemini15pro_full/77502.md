gazillion:/ɡəˈzɪljən/| n. | 极大量的，无数的 | An extremely large, indeterminate number.  A humorous exaggeration. |  I've told you a gazillion times to clean your room! 我已经告诉你无数次要打扫你的房间了！|  There were a gazillion mosquitos at the lake last night. 昨晚湖边有无数的蚊子。 | synonyms: zillion, jillion, umpteen, countless, myriad | antonyms: few, little, handful | noun
