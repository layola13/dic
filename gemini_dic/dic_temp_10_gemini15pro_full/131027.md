nonauthoritative:/ˌnɒnɔːˈθɒrɪtətɪv/
adjective
非权威的，非官方的
Not proceeding from or sanctioned by authority; not having the weight of authority; not official.
This website provides nonauthoritative information on medical conditions.  这个网站提供关于医疗状况的非权威信息。
The information found on this blog is nonauthoritative and should be verified elsewhere.  在这个博客上找到的信息是非权威的，应该在其他地方进行验证。
近义词: unofficial, informal, inauthentic, unreliable
反义词: authoritative, official, authentic, reliable
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From non- + authoritative.  "Authoritative" comes from Late Middle English "autoritatif," from Old French "autoritatif," from Medieval Latin "authoritativus," from Latin "auctoritas" ("authority").
