herl:/hɜːrl/ | n. | 羽毛纤维，毛羽，（尤指）苍鹭毛 | A long, narrow feather or fiber, especially from a heron, used in dressing artificial flies for fishing. | The fly fisherman carefully selected a herl for his latest creation. 这位飞蝇钓渔夫仔细地为他最新的作品挑选了一根羽毛纤维。|  She wrapped the herl around the hook to create the body of the fly. 她把羽毛纤维缠绕在鱼钩上，做成了鱼饵的主体。 | synonyms: feather, plume, barb | antonyms: N/A | noun
