bilirubin: /ˌbɪlɪˈruːbɪn/ | n. | 胆红素 | A reddish-yellow pigment formed in the liver by the breakdown of hemoglobin and excreted in bile. |  High levels of bilirubin in the blood can cause jaundice. 血液中胆红素含量高会导致黄疸。| synonyms: bile pigment | antonyms: None | noun
