electrobiologist:/ɪˌlɛktroʊbaɪˈɒlədʒɪst/ /ɪˌlɛktroʊbaɪˈɑːlədʒɪst/
n.|生物电学家|A scientist who studies the electrical properties of living organisms.|Electrobiologists are researching how electrical signals control cell growth. 生物电学家正在研究电信号如何控制细胞生长。|biophysicist, biochemist| N/A |noun
ETYMOLOGY->
electro- (prefix): from Greek ἤλεκτρον (ēlektron) meaning "amber," referring to static electricity.
bio- (prefix): from Greek βίος (bios) meaning "life."
-logist (suffix): from Greek -λόγος (-logos) meaning "one who studies" or "one who speaks about."
