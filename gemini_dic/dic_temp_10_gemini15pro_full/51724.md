devitrify:/diːˈvɪtrɪfaɪ/
v.
使失透，使反玻璃化，使脱玻
to change from the glassy state to a crystalline state; to lose the characteristics of glass
The porcelain devitrified during firing.  瓷器在烧制过程中失透了。

devitrify:/diːˈvɪtrɪfaɪ/
v.
使丧失活力，使无生气
to make (something) less lively or interesting
The city's spirit had been devitrified by years of economic hardship.  多年的经济困难使这个城市失去了活力。

devitrification:/diːˌvɪtrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
失透，反玻璃化，脱玻
The process of devitrifying.
The devitrification of the glass made it cloudy. 玻璃的失透使其变得浑浊。

devitrification:/diːˌvɪtrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
丧失活力
The process of losing liveliness or interest.
The devitrification of the city center was a concern for residents. 市中心的活力丧失让居民感到担忧。

近义词:crystallize, solidify, dull, deaden
反义词:vitrify, enliven, brighten
词性:v., n.
