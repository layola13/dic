defunction:/ˌdiːˈfʌŋkʃən/| v. |使失去功能；停止运作|To cause something to stop functioning; to make something obsolete.|The old factory was defunctioned after the company moved its operations overseas. 这家公司将业务转移到海外后，旧工厂就停止运作了。|近义词：disable, decommission, retire|反义词: activate, enable, commission|词性:动词
defunct:/dɪˈfʌŋkt/| adj. |失效的；停止运作的；不存在的|No longer functioning or existing; obsolete.|The defunct satellite is now just a piece of space junk.  这颗失效的卫星现在只是一块太空垃圾。|近义词：obsolete, extinct, inoperative|反义词: functioning, operating, extant|词性:形容词
