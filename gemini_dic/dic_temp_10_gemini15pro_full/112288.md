loquaciously:/loʊˈkweɪʃəsli/| adv.|喋喋不休地，饶舌地|In a talkative and fluent manner; excessively wordy.|She spoke loquaciously about her trip to Europe. 她喋喋不休地谈论她的欧洲之旅。|Synonyms: talkatively, volubly, garrulously|Antonyms: taciturnly, concisely, succinctly|adverb

loquacious:/loʊˈkweɪʃəs/| adj.|健谈的，多话的|Tending to talk a great deal; talkative.|He was a loquacious and entertaining dinner guest. 他是一位健谈且有趣的晚宴嘉宾。|Synonyms: talkative, volubly, garrulous|Antonyms: taciturn, reticent, laconic|adjective
