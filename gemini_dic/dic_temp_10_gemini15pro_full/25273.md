bufflehead: /ˈbʌfəlˌhɛd/ | n. | 小潜鸭 | A small North American diving duck (Bucephala albeola) with a large head and a short bill.  The male has a striking black and white plumage. |  We saw a pair of buffleheads diving for fish in the lake. 我们在湖里看到一对小潜鸭在潜水捕鱼。 | synonyms:  butterball, spirit duck | antonyms: N/A | noun

bufflehead: /ˈbʌfəlˌhɛd/ | n. | 傻瓜，笨蛋 (informal) | A stupid or foolish person. (This usage is less common than the bird meaning.) |  He acted like such a bufflehead when he locked his keys in the car. 他把钥匙锁在车里时，表现得像个傻瓜。 | synonyms:  idiot, fool, dolt | antonyms: genius, mastermind | noun
