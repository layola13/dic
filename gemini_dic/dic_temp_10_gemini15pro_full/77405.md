gavage:/ɡəˈvɑːʒ/ /ɡɑːˈvɑːʒ/| n. |强制喂食，管饲法|The act of force-feeding someone or something, typically through a tube.  This is often used in medical settings or for force-feeding animals like geese for foie gras.|The farmer used gavage to fatten the geese for foie gras. 农夫用管饲法让鹅变得肥胖以制作鹅肝酱。|Synonyms: force-feeding, tube feeding|Antonyms:  natural feeding|noun

gavage:/ɡəˈvɑːʒ/ /ɡɑːˈvɑːʒ/| v. |强制喂食，用管饲法喂|To force-feed someone or something, typically through a tube.|The vet gavaged the sick cat with a nutritional supplement.  兽医用管子给病猫强制喂食营养补充剂。|Synonyms: force-feed, tube feed|Antonyms:  naturally feed|verb
