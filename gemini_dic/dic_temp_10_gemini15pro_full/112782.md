lubricational:/ˌluːbrɪˈkeɪʃənəl/ |adj.|润滑的；润滑作用的|Relating to lubrication or serving as a lubricant.  Used to reduce friction between moving parts.| The lubricational properties of the oil are essential for the engine's smooth operation. 这款油的润滑性能对发动机的平稳运行至关重要。| 近义词: lubricating, lubricant | 反义词: abrasive, friction-increasing | 词性: adjective

词源: From lubrication + -al.  Lubrication ultimately comes from the Latin word "lubricare" meaning "to make smooth or slippery."
