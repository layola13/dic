panoramic:/ˌpænəˈræmɪk/ | adj. |全景的；全貌的|A panoramic view encompasses a wide area, often with a continuous, sweeping perspective.| From the top of the hill, we had a panoramic view of the city. 从山顶上，我们看到了这座城市的全景。|近义词：wide-angle, comprehensive, sweeping |反义词：limited, restricted |词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From French *panoramique*, from Ancient Greek *παν* (pan, “all”) + *ὅραμα* (hórama, “sight”).
