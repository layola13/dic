grunt:/ɡrʌnt/| n. vi. vt.|咕哝；呼噜声；嘟囔；|A low, guttural sound made by an animal or person.|The pig let out a loud grunt. 猪发出一声响亮的呼噜声。|近义词：groan, growl, murmur|反义词:whisper, murmur (轻声细语，但grunt强调喉音，所以反义词选择有限)|noun, verb
