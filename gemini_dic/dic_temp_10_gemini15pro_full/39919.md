constrainingly:/kənˈstreɪnɪŋli/
| adv. | 约束地，限制地 | In a way that limits or restricts freedom of action or thought. |  Her tight budget constrainingly limited her travel options.  她紧张的预算限制了她的旅行选择。 |  restrictively, limitingly, inhibitingly |  freely, liberally, permissively | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From constrain + -ing + -ly.  "constrain" comes from Old French "constraindre,"  from Latin "constringere"  meaning "to bind together," from "com-" (together) and "stringere" (to draw tight). "-ing" forms the present participle and "-ly" creates the adverb form.
