influxious:/ɪnˈflʌkʃəs/ | adj. | 流入的；涌入的 | Characterized by a flowing in, especially in abundance; tending to flow in.  Influxuous describes something that has the quality of flowing inward, often in large amounts. It can refer to tangible things like water or people, but also to intangible things like ideas or influences. | The influxuous nature of the river made it prone to flooding. 这条河流的流入特性使其容易发生洪水。|  The town experienced an influxuous wave of tourists during the summer months. 在夏季的几个月里，这个小镇经历了游客的涌入。 | 近义词: incoming, inflowing, inundating | 反义词: outflowing,  egressing | 词性: adjective
