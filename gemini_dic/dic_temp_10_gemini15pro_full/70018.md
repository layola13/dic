fetishly:/ˈfɛtɪʃli/ | adv. | 恋物地；迷恋地 | In a manner characterized by excessive or irrational devotion to a particular object or activity, treating it with reverence or obsession as if it were a fetish. | He gazed fetishly at his collection of vintage watches. 他迷恋地凝视着自己收藏的古董手表。|  She fetishly polished her car every weekend. 她每个周末都迷恋地擦拭她的车。 | 近义词: obsessively, fixatedly | 反义词: indifferently, casually | 词性: 副词

词源: From the noun "fetish" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). The word "fetish" ultimately derives from the Portuguese "feitiço," meaning "charm, sorcery, witchcraft," from the Latin "facticius," meaning "artificial, made by art."
