macrocosmical:/ˌmækroʊˈkɒzmɪkəl/| adj.|宏观的，宇宙的|Relating to the macrocosm; on a large or universal scale.  Concerned with the whole universe.|例句：His macrocosmical view of history encompassed millennia. 他对历史的宏观视角涵盖了数千年。|例句：She took a macrocosmical approach to the problem, considering its global implications. 她从宏观的角度处理这个问题，考虑了它的全球影响。|近义词：universal, cosmic, global, comprehensive|反义词：microcosmical, local, limited|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From macrocosm + -ical.  Macrocosm from Greek *makros kosmos* "great world,"  *makros* "long, large" + *kosmos* "order, world, universe."
