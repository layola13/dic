deambulate:/diˈæmbjʊleɪt/| v. | 漫步，闲逛 | To walk or move around, especially in a leisurely or aimless way. |  He deambulated through the park, enjoying the fresh air. 他在公园里漫步，享受着新鲜空气。

|  The patients were encouraged to deambulate after surgery to promote healing.  鼓励病人在手术后四处走动以促进愈合。 | 近义词：stroll, wander, saunter, amble | 反义词：rush, hurry, sprint, stay, sit | 词性：动词 | 词源：From Latin *deambulare, from de- + ambulare "to walk."
