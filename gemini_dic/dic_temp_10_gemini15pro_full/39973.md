consubstantially:/ˌkɒnsəbˈstænʃəli/ /ˌkɑːnsəbˈstænʃəli/
|adv.|同体的，同质的，同实体的|Having the same substance or essence; existing in the same substance.|The bread and wine in the Eucharist are believed by some to be consubstantially the body and blood of Christ. 圣餐中的面包和葡萄酒被一些人认为与基督的身体和血液同体。|Synonyms: essentially, intrinsically, inherently|Antonyms: differently, separately|Adverb
