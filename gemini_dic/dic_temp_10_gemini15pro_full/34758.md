cliffier:/ˈklɪfɪər/
adjective
更悬崖峭壁的; 更险峻的
Characterized by more or steeper cliffs; more precipitous.
The western coast became cliffier as we drove north.  我们越往北开，西海岸的悬崖就越陡峭。
The path grew cliffier and more dangerous. 这条小路变得越来越陡峭，越来越危险。
Synonyms: steeper, precipitous, sheer, craggy
Antonyms: gentler, sloping, gradual
词性: 形容词
