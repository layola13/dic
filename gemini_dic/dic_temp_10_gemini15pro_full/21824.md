bockland:/ˈbɒklænd/| n.|保有地，世袭地|Land held by charter, free of feudal service and subject only to the king.  This contrasts with folkland, which was subject to customary law and could be transferred only with the consent of the community.|King Alfred granted bockland to his loyal thanes. 阿尔弗雷德大帝将保有地赐予他忠诚的塞恩.|近义词： freehold, allodial|反义词：folkland|词性：名词
