bushfighter:/ˈbʊʃfaɪtər/| n.| 丛林战士，游击队员 | A soldier or guerrilla fighter who operates in wooded or jungle terrain. |  Example 1: The bushfighters were adept at camouflage and moving stealthily through the dense forest. 丛林战士擅长伪装，能够悄无声息地穿过茂密的森林。

Example 2:  The bushfighters relied on their knowledge of the local terrain to ambush the enemy. 游击队员依靠他们对当地地形的了解来伏击敌人。 | 近义词: guerrilla, partisan, insurgent | 反义词: regular soldier, conventional forces | 词性: noun
