catechisms:/ˈkætɪˌkɪzəmz/
n.
教义问答；问答集；问答式教程
a summary of the principles of Christian religion in the form of questions and answers, used for instruction.
The children memorized the catechisms. 孩子们记住了教义问答。
近义词:creed, dogma, doctrine, teaching
反义词:heresy
词性:noun
