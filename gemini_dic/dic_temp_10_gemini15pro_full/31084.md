champed:/tʃæmpt/
verb
咬碎；焦躁；急切
bite or chew on something noisily or impatiently.  Also, to be eager or impatient for something.
The horse champed at the bit, eager to start the race.  这匹马嚼着马衔，急于开始比赛。
近义词:  gnawed, chomped, chafed
反义词:  waited patiently
词性: verb


champed:/tʃæmpt/
adjective
(of a horse's bit) bitten or worn away by constant chewing. Also, eager or impatient.
The champed bit showed how nervous the horse was.  被咬坏的马衔表明这匹马有多紧张。
近义词:  worn, gnawed, eager
反义词:  smooth, patient
词性: adjective
