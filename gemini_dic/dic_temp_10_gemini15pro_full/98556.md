instinctively:/ɪnˈstɪŋktɪvli/
adverb
本能地；直觉地
Acting on an innate impulse without conscious reasoning.
She instinctively knew something was wrong. 她本能地知道出了什么问题。
近义词: intuitively, automatically, naturally, spontaneously
反义词: deliberately, consciously, rationally
词性: adverb

词源: From instinct + -ive + -ly.  "Instinct" comes from Late Latin instinctus "impulse, instigation," literally "incitement," from past participle stem of instinguere "to incite, impel," from in- "on, upon" + stinguere "prick, urge."
