collateral: /kəˈlætərəl/ | n. adj. | 抵押品，担保品；附属的，次要的 | Something pledged as security for repayment of a loan, to be forfeited in the event of a default.  (as adjective)  Accompanying or arising as a consequence of something else, though secondary or subordinate. |  例句：He used his house as collateral for the loan.  他用房子作贷款的抵押品。| 近义词：security, guarantee, pledge, secondary, ancillary | 反义词：primary, main | 词性: noun, adjective

collaterals: /kəˈlætərəlz/ | n. | 抵押品，担保品（复数）|  The plural form of "collateral," referring to multiple items pledged as security. | 例句：The bank seized the collaterals when the loan defaulted. 银行在贷款违约时扣押了抵押品。| 近义词：securities, guarantees, pledges | 反义词：N/A | 词性: noun


词源: From Late Latin *collateralis "situated at the side," from com- "together" + lateralis "of the side," from latus "side"
