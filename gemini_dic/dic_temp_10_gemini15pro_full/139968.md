odontornithes:/ˌoʊdɒnˈtɔːrnɪθiːz/
n.
齿鸟类
An extinct group of toothed birds from the Cretaceous period.  These birds possessed teeth in their beaks, unlike modern birds.
The Odontornithes, such as Hesperornis and Ichthyornis, represent a fascinating stage in avian evolution.  齿鸟类，例如黄昏鸟和鱼鸟，代表了鸟类进化中一个引人入胜的阶段。
Ichthyornis, a genus of Odontornithes, was a strong flier resembling modern seabirds. 鱼鸟，齿鸟类的一个属，是一种类似于现代海鸟的强壮飞行者。
近义词: toothed birds, prehistoric birds
反义词: modern birds
词性: noun
