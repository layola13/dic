dispost:/dɪˈspəʊz/
verb
处置，安排；使倾向于
to deal with or settle; to make someone inclined towards something
The governor is disposed to grant your request. 州长倾向于批准你的请求。
dispose of: 处理掉  They disposed of the body secretly. 他们秘密地处理了尸体。
近义词: arrange, settle, incline
反义词: disincline, dissuade
词性: verb
