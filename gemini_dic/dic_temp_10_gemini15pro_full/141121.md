ontologist:/ɒnˈtɒlədʒɪst/ /ɑːnˈtɑːlədʒɪst/| n.| 本体论学家，本体论专家|A specialist in ontology; someone who studies ontology.|The ontologist argued that abstract concepts like "love" and "justice" have a real existence. 本体论学家认为，“爱”和“正义”等抽象概念是真实存在的。|近义词：philosopher, metaphysician|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek ὄντος (óntos, “being, that which is”) (genitive singular of ὤν (ṓn)) + -λογία (-logía, “study of”).
