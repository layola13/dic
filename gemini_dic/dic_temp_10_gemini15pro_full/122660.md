modu:/ˈmoʊduː/ | n. | 模块，模组 | A self-contained unit or component of a larger system, often designed for easy assembly and replacement. |  This car is built with modular components, making repairs easier. 这辆车是用模块化组件制造的，使维修更容易。| synonym: unit, component, module, section, element | antonym: whole, entirety | noun

modu:/ˈmoʊduː/ | adj. | 模块化的 | Designed or constructed using modules. | The company offers a modular furniture system that can be customized to fit any space.  这家公司提供了一个模块化的家具系统，可以定制以适应任何空间。| synonym: modularized, sectional, component-based | antonym: integrated, monolithic, unified | adjective
