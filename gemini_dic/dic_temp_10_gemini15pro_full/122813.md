moldy:/ˈmoʊldi/ (US), /ˈməʊldi/ (UK)| adj.|发霉的；陈腐的|Covered with or smelling of mold; stale or musty.|The bread was moldy, so I threw it away. 面包发霉了，所以我把它扔掉了。|近义词：musty, mildewed, fusty|反义词:fresh, new|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Middle English *moldi, from Old English *moldig, from mold (“mold”) + -ig (“-y”).
