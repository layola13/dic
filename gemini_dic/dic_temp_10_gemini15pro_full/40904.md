coolabah: /ˈkuːləbɑː/ | n. |  库拉巴 (一种澳大利亚原住民使用的回旋镖) | A type of returning boomerang traditionally used by Aboriginal Australians. | He skillfully threw the coolabah, and it curved back to him. 他熟练地投掷库拉巴，它弯曲着飞回到他手中。| 近义词: boomerang, returning boomerang | 反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly non-returning boomerang/kylie) | 词性: noun
