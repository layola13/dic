panpresbyterian:/ˌpænˌprɛzbɪˈtɪəriən/
adjective
泛长老会的
Relating to or involving all Presbyterian churches or members of those churches; advocating the union of all Presbyterian bodies.
The panpresbyterian council met to discuss shared theological concerns. 泛长老会理事会开会讨论共同的神学关切。
The conference aimed to foster panpresbyterian unity.  这次会议旨在促进泛长老会的团结。
Synonym: interpresbyterian, ecumenical (within Presbyterianism)
Antonym: denominational (specifically relating to a single denomination within Presbyterianism)
词性: adjective
