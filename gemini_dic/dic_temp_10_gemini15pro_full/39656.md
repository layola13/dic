conservatively:/kənˈsɜːrvətɪvli/| adv.|保守地，谨慎地|In a way that avoids risk or extremes; cautiously.  It also refers to a style or manner that is traditional and not showy.|She dressed conservatively for the interview. 她面试时穿得很保守。|Synonyms: cautiously, prudently, moderately, traditionally|Antonyms: radically, liberally, extravagantly, progressively|adverb
