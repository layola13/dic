annuities:/əˈnjuːɪtiz/
n.
年金；养老金
A fixed sum of money paid to someone each year, typically for the rest of their life.
Example: She invested her inheritance in an annuity to provide a steady income stream. 她把遗产投资于年金，以提供稳定的收入来源。

Example:  He receives a monthly annuity from his retirement plan. 他从他的退休计划中每月领取年金。

近义词:pension, allowance, payment
反义词:lump sum, single payment
词性:noun
