park:/pɑːrk/ (UK), /pɑːrk/ (US)| n. v.|公园，停车|A large public green area in a town, used for recreation.  To stop and leave a vehicle in a particular place for a period of time.|We went for a walk in the park. 我们去公园散步了。|
近义词：garden, playground, recreation area|反义词：street, road|词性: noun, verb

park:/pɑːrk/ (UK), /pɑːrk/ (US)| v.|停车|To stop and leave a vehicle in a particular place for a period of time.|I parked the car outside the house. 我把车停在了房子外面。|
近义词：stop, leave|反义词：drive, go|词性: verb


词源: From Middle English *parrok, from Old North French parc, from Frankish *parruk (“enclosure”), from Proto-Germanic *parrukaz (“enclosure, paddock, park”), from Proto-Indo-European *perkʷ- (“oak, sacred tree; enclosed area”). Cognate with Old English pearroc (“park, enclosure”), Middle Dutch parke (“park, park forest”).
