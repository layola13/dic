palamedeae:/ˌpæləˈmiːdiːiː/ | n. |叫鹤科| A family of birds, the screamers, found in South America. They are large, long-legged wading birds with chicken-like bills and spurs on their wings. | The palamedeae are unique birds with a prehistoric look. 叫鹤科是具有史前外观的独特鸟类。 | The crested screamer belongs to the family palamedeae.  冠叫鹤属于叫鹤科。 | synonyms: Anhimidae, screamers | antonyms: N/A | noun
