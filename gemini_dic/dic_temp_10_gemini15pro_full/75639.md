fuguelike: /ˈfjuːɡlaɪk/ | adj. |赋格曲似的，像赋格曲的|Resembling or characteristic of a fugue, a musical form in which a theme is introduced by one part and then taken up and developed by others.|The piece had a fuguelike quality, with interwoven melodies. 这首曲子有一种赋格曲般的特质，旋律交织在一起。|Synonyms:  intricate, interwoven, contrapuntal|Antonyms: simple, monotonic, homophonic|adjective
