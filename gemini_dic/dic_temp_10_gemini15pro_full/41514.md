corollifloral: /ˌkɒrəˈlɪflɔːrəl/ /ˌkɔːrəˈlɪflɔːrəl/
adjective
花冠生的；合瓣花类的
Having flowers growing from the corolla, or inserted on the corolla.
The botanist identified the plant as corollifloral due to the position of its blossoms.  这位植物学家根据花的位置将这种植物鉴定为花冠生的。
The corollifloral arrangement of the petals was a key characteristic of the species.  花瓣的花冠状排列是该物种的一个关键特征。
近义词: gamopetalous, sympetalous
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "calycifloral" - having flowers arising from the calyx)
词性: adjective
