booker:/ˈbʊkər/| n.|预订员，订票员；记账员|A person who books something, especially seats on a plane or rooms in a hotel, or one who keeps accounts.|Example: The booker found us a great deal on a flight to Paris.  预订员帮我们找到了去巴黎的航班的优惠价。

Example:  The band's booker is responsible for arranging all their gigs.  乐队的预订员负责安排他们所有的演出。|近义词: reservation agent, booking clerk, accountant (for keeping accounts)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
