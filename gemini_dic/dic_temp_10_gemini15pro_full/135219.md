nonoxidizable:/ˌnɑːnˈɑːksɪdaɪzəbl/
adjective
不可氧化的, 不氧化的
not susceptible to oxidation; resistant to combining with oxygen.
Gold is a nonoxidizable metal. 金是一种不可氧化的金属。
Platinum is highly valued because it is nonoxidizable.  铂金因其不可氧化性而备受重视。

近义词:non-oxidizing, rustproof, corrosion-resistant
反义词:oxidizable, corrosive

词性:adjective

词源: non- + oxidizable.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "oxidizable" comes from "oxidize," which ultimately derives from the French "oxygène" (oxygen).
