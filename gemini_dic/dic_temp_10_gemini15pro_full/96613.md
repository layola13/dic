indulgement:/ɪnˈdʌldʒmənt/ | n. | 放纵；沉溺；享受；嗜好 | The act of indulging; allowing oneself to enjoy something, especially something considered pleasurable or wrong. |  She treated herself to a weekend of pure indulgence. 她让自己享受了一个纯粹放纵的周末。| synonyms: gratification, pleasure, luxury, treat, pampering | antonyms: abstinence, self-denial, restraint, austerity | noun

indulge: /ɪnˈdʌldʒ/ | v. | 放纵；沉溺；满足；纵容 | Allow oneself to enjoy the pleasure of. | I indulged in a long hot bath. 我尽情享受了一个长时间的热水澡。| synonyms:  gratify, satisfy,  wallow in, give in to  | antonyms:  resist, abstain from, deny oneself | verb
