ember:/ˈembər/| n.|余烬， embers(复数)；|A small piece of burning or glowing coal or wood in a dying fire.  一小块在快要熄灭的火中的燃烧或发光的煤或木头。|The last embers of the campfire glowed in the darkness.  篝火的余烬在黑暗中闪烁。|近义词：coal, cinder|反义词：flame, blaze|Noun
