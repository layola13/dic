americas: /əˈmɛrɪkəz/
n.
美洲（尤指北美和南美）
the continents of North and South America, especially when considered together.
The Americas were colonized by Europeans. 美洲曾被欧洲人殖民。
近义词: New World, Western Hemisphere
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider Eastern Hemisphere or Old World depending on context)
词性: noun

americas: /əˈmɛrɪkəz/
n.
(泛指) 美洲各国
the countries of North, Central, and South America.
He traveled extensively throughout the Americas. 他游遍了美洲各国。
近义词: American nations, American continents
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun


词源: From Amerigo Vespucci, an Italian explorer.
