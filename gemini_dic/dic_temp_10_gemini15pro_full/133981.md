nonhierarchical:/ˌnɒnhaɪəˈrɑːkɪkəl/ | adj. |非等级的，无等级的 |  Not organized in a hierarchy; lacking a hierarchical structure, where some elements are ranked above others. |  The company adopted a nonhierarchical structure, empowering all employees.  这家公司采用了非等级结构，赋予所有员工权力。 |  The team has a nonhierarchical approach to decision-making.  该团队采用非等级的决策方法。|  Synonyms: egalitarian, democratic, flat, peer-to-peer | Antonyms: hierarchical, stratified, ranked, graded | adjective
