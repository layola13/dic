in bulk:/ɪn bʌlk/| adv. adj.|大量，散装，|Buying in bulk is often cheaper.  大量购买通常更便宜。|近义词：wholesale, in large quantities, in mass|反义词:individually, separately, in detail|词性:副词, 形容词

Etymology:
From "bulk," meaning size or magnitude, from Old Norse *bulki ("cargo, heap"). The phrase "in bulk" emerged in Middle English, signifying "in large quantities."
