immigrate:/ˈɪmɪɡreɪt/| v. |移民，移居入境|To come to a country to live there permanently.|Many people immigrated to the United States in the 19th century. 许多人在19世纪移民到美国。|
Many people immigrated to Canada for a better life. 许多人为了更好的生活移民到加拿大。


|近义词：migrate, relocate, resettle|反义词:emigrate|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin immigratus, past participle of immigrare "to remove, go into," from in- "into" + migrare "to depart."
