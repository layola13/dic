biologist:/baɪˈɒlədʒɪst/ (UK) /baɪˈɑːlədʒɪst/ (US)| n. |生物学家|A scientist who studies living organisms. |例句：The biologist studied the behavior of the ants.  生物学家研究了蚂蚁的行为。

例句：She is a renowned biologist specializing in marine life. 她是一位著名的海洋生物学家。


近义词：life scientist, naturalist, zoologist (for animal biologists), botanist (for plant biologists)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From bio- (life) + -logy (study of) + -ist (one who studies).  "Bio-" comes from the Greek "bios" (life), and "-logy" comes from the Greek "-logia" (study of). "-ist" denotes a person who practices or is an expert in something.
