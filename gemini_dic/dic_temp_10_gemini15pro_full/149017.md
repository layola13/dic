owel:/ˈaʊəl/ | n. | 母音; 元音 | A speech sound produced by comparatively open configuration of the vocal tract, with vibration of the vocal cords but without audible friction.  |  Example: The word "owl" has two vowels.  单词"owl"有两个元音。 | Synonym: vowel sound | Antonym: consonant | noun
