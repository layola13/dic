loiterer:/ˈlɔɪtərər/ (US) /ˈlɔɪtərə(r)/ (UK)| n. |闲逛者，游荡者|A person who loiters; someone who stands around idly or lingers without apparent purpose.|The police questioned the loiterers outside the closed shop. 警察盘问了关门商店外的闲逛者。|近义词：loafer, idler, lingerer, dawdler|反义词：worker, hustler|noun
