greyer:/ˈɡreɪər/ (US), /ˈɡreɪə/ (UK)| adj.|更灰色的，更灰白的|Comparative form of grey: more grey.  Used to describe something that has become more grey than it was before.|例句：Her hair is greyer than it was last year. 她的头发比去年更灰白了。

例句：The sky became greyer as the storm approached. 随着暴风雨的临近，天空变得更加灰暗。|近义词：darker, duller, more gray|反义词：lighter, brighter, whiter|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Middle English *greyer, compartive of grey, from Old English grǣg.
