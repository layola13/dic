legator:/ˈleɡeɪtər/ | n. | 遗赠者；立遗嘱的人 | A person who makes a bequest or leaves a legacy.  | Example: The legator left a substantial sum to the university in his will. 遗嘱人给大学留下了一大笔钱。

Example: The legator's intentions were clearly stated in the testament. 立遗嘱者的意图在遗嘱中明确说明。 | Near Synonyms: testator, bequeather, donor | Antonyms: beneficiary, heir | noun
