interlanguage:/ˌɪntərˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ/| n. | 中介语；过渡语 |The linguistic system used by second language learners as they move towards proficiency in the target language. It is characterized by features of both the learner's native language and the target language, as well as features unique to the interlanguage itself. |  Her interlanguage was characterized by the omission of articles and incorrect verb conjugations. 她的中介语的特点是省略冠词和动词变形错误。 | synonyms: learner language, transitional competence | antonyms: native language, target language | noun
