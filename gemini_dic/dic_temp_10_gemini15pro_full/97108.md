infidelities: /ˌɪnfɪˈdɛlətiz/
n.
不忠，不贞；背信弃义
acts of being unfaithful to a spouse or other sexual partner
例句：His infidelities eventually led to the breakdown of his marriage. 他的不忠最终导致了他的婚姻破裂。

例句：She couldn't forgive his repeated infidelities. 她无法原谅他屡次的不忠。

近义词：unfaithfulness, adultery, cheating, disloyalty
反义词：faithfulness, loyalty, fidelity
词性：名词 (plural noun)


词源：Late Middle English: from Old French infidelite, from late Latin infidelitas, from infidelis ‘unfaithful’ (see infidel).
