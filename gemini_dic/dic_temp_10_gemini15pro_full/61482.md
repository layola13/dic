embread:/ɪmˈbrɛd/ |v. (past participle: embreaded, past tense: embreaded) |裹上面包屑|To coat or cover something with breadcrumbs.|例句：She embreaded the chicken before frying it. 她炸鸡前给鸡肉裹上面包屑。

例句：The chef embreaded the fish fillets and then baked them until golden brown.  厨师将鱼片裹上面包屑，然后烘烤至金黄色。|近义词：bread, coat, cover |反义词: uncover, expose |词性: 动词
