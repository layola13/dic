loom:/luːm/
n.|织布机|A machine for weaving cloth.|The weaver worked diligently at her loom.  织布工在她的织布机上辛勤地工作。|近义词: weaving machine|反义词: None|词性: 名词

loom:/luːm/
vi.|隐约出现；逼近|To appear large and indistinct; to come into view indistinctly, often threateningly.|Dark storm clouds loomed on the horizon.  乌云密布在地平线上。|近义词: emerge, appear, menace|反义词: recede, disappear|词性: 动词
