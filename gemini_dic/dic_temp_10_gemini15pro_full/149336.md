pachacuti:/ˌpɑːtʃɑːˈkuːtiː/| n.|帕查库蒂，世界翻转|A Quechua word meaning "world transformer" or "he who overturns time and space," referring to a cataclysmic event or a person who brings about radical change.  It is most commonly associated with Pachacuti Inca Yupanqui, the ninth Sapa Inca (emperor) of the Kingdom of Cusco, who initiated its transformation into the Inca Empire (Tawantinsuyu).|The reign of Pachacuti marked a pachacuti in Andean history. 帕查库蒂的统治标志着安第斯历史上的一个巨大转变。

The museum exhibit explored the concept of pachacuti through the lens of indigenous cosmology.  博物馆展览从土著宇宙学的角度探讨了帕查库蒂的概念。|近义词：revolution, transformation, upheaval|反义词:stasis, stagnation|词性:noun
