oversuspiciously:/ˌoʊvər səˈspɪʃəsli/ | adv. | 过分怀疑地，过于猜疑地 |  In a manner that exhibits excessive suspicion, distrust, or paranoia.  Characterized by an unreasonable or exaggerated degree of suspicion. | He eyed the newcomer oversuspiciously, clutching his bag tightly. 他过分怀疑地盯着新来的人，紧紧抓着他的包。| She oversuspiciously checked her locks three times before leaving the house. 她离开房子前过分谨慎地检查了三次门锁。 | 近义词: distrustfully, suspiciously, warily | 反义词: trustingly, naively, confidently | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From "over" (excessively) + "suspiciously" (in a suspicious manner).  "Suspiciously" comes from "suspicious," derived from Late Latin "suspiciosus," from Latin "suspicio" (suspicion, mistrust), from "suspicere" (to look up at, suspect), from "sub" (under) + "specere" (to look).
