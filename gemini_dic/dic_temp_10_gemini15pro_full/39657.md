conservativeness:/kənˈsɜːrvətɪvnəs/ |n.|保守主义，保守性|The quality or state of being conservative; avoidance of excess; moderation; caution; restraint.|Her conservativeness in financial matters is commendable. 她在财务方面的保守值得称赞。|近义词: caution, prudence, traditionalism, restraint|反义词: radicalism, liberalism, progressiveness|词性:名词

词源: From conservative + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin conservare (to preserve).
