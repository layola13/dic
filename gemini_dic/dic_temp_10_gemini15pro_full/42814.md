courageously:/kəˈreɪdʒəsli/
adv.
勇敢地
In a brave manner; with courage or bravery.
He courageously faced the danger. 他勇敢地面对危险。
近义词:bravely, valiantly, boldly, fearlessly
反义词:cowardly, timidly, fearfully
词性:副词

词源:
From courage + -ous + -ly.  "Courage" comes from Old French "corage," ultimately from Latin "cor" (heart).
