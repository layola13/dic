hyperrealizes:/ˌhaɪpərˈriːəlaɪzɪz/
verb
过度现实化；极度意识到
to realize something to an excessive degree, often to the point of anxiety or obsession.  This word emphasizes a heightened, almost exaggerated sense of awareness.
The protagonist hyperrealizes his mortality after a near-death experience. 主人公在一次濒死体验后，极度意识到自己的死亡。
He hyperrealizes every social interaction, analyzing every word and gesture. 他过度地关注每一次社交互动，分析每个字和每个手势。
Synonyms: overthink, obsess over, fixate on
Antonyms: ignore, disregard, overlook
词性: 动词
