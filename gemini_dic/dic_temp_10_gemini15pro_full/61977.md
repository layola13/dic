enabler:/ɪˈneɪblər/| n.|使能者，促成者；帮凶|Someone or something that makes something possible or easier.  Something that gives someone the ability or opportunity to do something.|The internet has been a great enabler for small businesses.|近义词：facilitator, catalyst, supporter|反义词：obstacle, hindrance, impediment|noun
