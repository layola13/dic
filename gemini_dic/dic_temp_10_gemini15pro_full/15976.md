baltimorean: /ˌbɔːltɪˈmɔːriən/ | n. adj. |巴尔的摩人；巴尔的摩的|A native or inhabitant of Baltimore, Maryland.  Also used as an adjective to describe something related to Baltimore.|例句：He's a Baltimorean through and through. 他是个地地道道的巴尔的摩人。|近义词：Baltimore resident, Marylander |反义词:N/A|词性:noun, adjective


baltimorean: /ˌbɔːltɪˈmɔːriən/ | adj. |巴尔的摩的|Relating to or characteristic of the city of Baltimore, Maryland.|例句：The Baltimorean accent is quite distinctive. 巴尔的摩口音很独特。|近义词：Baltimore-related, Baltimore-based |反义词:N/A|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Baltimore + -an.  Baltimore itself is named after Cecil Calvert, 2nd Baron Baltimore (1605-1675), who founded the Province of Maryland.
