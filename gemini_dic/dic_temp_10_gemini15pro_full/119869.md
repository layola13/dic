milieu:/miːlˈjɜː/ /mɪlˈjuː/
n.
环境；周围；社会背景
A person's social environment.

He grew up in a privileged milieu.
他在一个优越的环境中长大。

Artists thrive in a milieu that supports their creativity.
艺术家在支持他们创造力的环境中茁壮成长。

近义词：environment, atmosphere, setting, background, surroundings, context
反义词：N/A
词性：noun

词源：French, from mi 'middle' + lieu 'place'.
