annulata:/əˌnjuˈleɪtə/
adjective
环节的,环状的
Marked with rings; ringed.  Specifically, divided into ring-like segments; arranged in rings or ring-like structures.
The earthworm is an annulate animal. 蚯蚓是一种环节动物。
近义词:ringed, segmented, circular
反义词:unsegmented, linear
词性:adjective

annulata:/əˌnjuˈleɪtə/
noun
环节动物
An animal with a segmented body, like an earthworm or leech.
Annulata are characterized by their segmented bodies. 环节动物的特点是身体分节。
近义词:annelid
反义词: (No direct antonym, but could use non-segmented animals as a contrasting group)
词性:noun
