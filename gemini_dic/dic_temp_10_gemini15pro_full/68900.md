fasting:/ˈfɑːstɪŋ/ (US) /ˈfɑːstɪŋ/ (UK)| n. adj.|禁食，斋戒|The act of abstaining from all or some kinds of food or drink, especially as a religious observance.|例句：Many religions practice fasting. 许多宗教都有禁食的习俗。|近义词：abstinence, deprivation|反义词：feasting, gorging|词性：名词

fasting:/ˈfɑːstɪŋ/ (US) /ˈfɑːstɪŋ/ (UK)|adj.|禁食的，空腹的|Relating to or used during a period of fasting.|例句：He took a fasting blood test. 他做了一个空腹验血。|近义词：hungry, starved|反义词：fed, full|词性:形容词
