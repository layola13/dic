dutifulnesses:/ˌdjuːtɪfʊlnɪsɪz/
n.
责任感；尽职；忠实
The quality of being dutiful; conscientiousness in carrying out one's duties.
Her dutifulnesses to her family were admirable. 她对家庭的责任感令人钦佩。
近义词: conscientiousness, diligence, responsibility, obedience
反义词: negligence, irresponsibility, disobedience
词性: 名词 (plural)

dutiful:/ˈdjuːtɪfʊl/
adj.
尽职的；忠实的；顺从的
Performing one's duties willingly and well; obedient.
She is a dutiful daughter who always cares for her parents. 她是一个孝顺的女儿，总是关心她的父母。
近义词: obedient, respectful, responsible, conscientious
反义词: disobedient, disrespectful, irresponsible, negligent
词性: 形容词

duty:/ˈdjuːti/
n.
责任；义务；职责
A moral or legal obligation; a responsibility.
It is our duty to protect the environment. 保护环境是我们的责任。
近义词: responsibility, obligation, task, chore
反义词: exemption, irresponsibility
词性: 名词
