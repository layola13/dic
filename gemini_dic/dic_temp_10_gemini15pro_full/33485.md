chymification:/ˌkaɪmɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 乳糜化 | The process of converting food into chyme in the stomach. |  The process of chymification is essential for proper digestion. 乳糜化过程对正常的消化至关重要。| Example:  The stomach acids play a crucial role in chymification. 胃酸在乳糜化过程中起着至关重要的作用. | Synonyms: digestion, gastric digestion | Antonyms:  N/A | noun

chyme:/kaɪm/ | n. | 食糜 | The pulpy acidic fluid that passes from the stomach to the small intestine, consisting of gastric juices and partly digested food. | The chyme moves from the stomach into the duodenum. 食糜从胃移动到十二指肠。| Example: The consistency of chyme changes as digestion progresses. 随着消化的进行，食糜的稠度会发生变化。| Synonyms:  N/A | Antonyms:  N/A | noun
