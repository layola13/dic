livening:/ˈlaɪvənɪŋ/ | adj. | 使生动的，使活泼的 | Making something more lively or interesting. | The band played a livening tune. 乐队演奏了一首活泼的曲子。 |  The comedian's jokes were quite livening.  喜剧演员的笑话很热闹。 | synonyms: animating, invigorating, stimulating, exciting | antonyms: dulling, boring, depressing | adjective

liven:/ˈlaɪvən/ | v. | 使生动；使活泼 | To make something more lively or interesting. | The music livened up the party. 音乐使派对活跃起来。 | The addition of spices livened up the bland dish. 添加香料使清淡的菜肴变得鲜活起来。 | synonyms: animate, invigorate, stimulate, excite | antonyms: dull, bore, depress | verb


ETYMOLOGY->

From Middle English *livenen*, from Old English *libban* ("to live").  The "-en" suffix transforms the verb "live" into the adjective "livening" or the related verb "liven."  Essentially, it implies bringing something "to life" or making it more "alive."
