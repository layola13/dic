inoperative:/ɪnˈɒpərətɪv/ | adj. |无效的，不起作用的，失灵的 |Not working or taking effect; not having power or validity. |The old law is now inoperative. 这项旧法律现在已失效。| The elevator is currently inoperative. 电梯目前无法运行。 |近义词：invalid, ineffective, defunct, unusable, broken, out of order |反义词：operative, functional, working, valid, effective |词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + operative.  The "in-" prefix negates the meaning of "operative," which means "functioning" or "having effect."
