anticity:/ˌæntiˈsɪti/ | n. |反城市；对城市的厌恶|A feeling of dislike or opposition towards cities and urban life.  It often manifests as a preference for rural or natural environments.|  He felt a strong anticity sentiment after living in the crowded metropolis for a decade. 他在大都市拥挤的环境中生活了十年后，对城市产生了强烈的厌恶感。|  Example:  The poet's works often expressed a deep anticity sentiment, praising the tranquility of nature. 这位诗人的作品经常表达出一种深刻的反城市情绪，赞美大自然的宁静。|Synonyms:  anti-urbanism, dislike of cities |Antonyms: urbanophilia, love of cities| noun
