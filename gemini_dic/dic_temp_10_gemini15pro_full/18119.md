behind-your-back:/bɪˈhaɪnd jɔːr bæk/
| adv. | 背着你；在你背后；偷偷地 | Done or said without someone's knowledge or permission. | They were talking about me behind my back. 他们在背后议论我。| secretly, covertly, surreptitiously | openly, to one's face | adverb

behind-your-back:/bɪˈhaɪnd jɔːr bæk/
| adj. | 背后的；秘密的 | Done or existing without someone's knowledge.  | It was a behind-your-back deal.  这是一笔暗中进行的交易。| secret, clandestine | open, public | adjective
