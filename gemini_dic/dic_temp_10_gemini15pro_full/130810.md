nonapologetically:/ˌnɒnəˌpɒləˈdʒɛtɪkli/ /ˌnɑːnəˌpɑːləˈdʒɛtɪkli/ | adv. |毫不道歉地，理直气壮地|Without offering any apology or excuse; in a manner that shows no regret or remorse.  It combines the prefix "non-" (meaning "not"), the noun "apology," and the suffixes "-etic" (forming an adjective) and "-ally" (forming an adverb).|She nonapologetically defended her controversial views. 她理直气壮地捍卫她备受争议的观点。|She nonapologetically pursued her own ambitions. 她毫不歉疚地追求着自己的抱负。|近义词：unapologetically, unrepentantly, defiantly|反义词：apologetically, remorsefully, contritely|词性: 副词
