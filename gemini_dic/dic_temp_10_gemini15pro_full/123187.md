monocarbonic:/ˌmɒnəʊkɑːˈbɒnɪk/ |adj.|一碳的；含有一个碳原子的|Containing only one carbon atom.|Monocarbonic acid, like formic acid, plays a crucial role in certain chemical processes.  像甲酸这样的一碳酸，在某些化学过程中起着至关重要的作用。|Synonyms: single-carbon|Antonyms: polycarbonic (containing multiple carbon atoms)|adjective
