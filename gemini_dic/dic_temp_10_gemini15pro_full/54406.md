disincentive:/ˌdɪsɪnˈsɛntɪv/ | n. |抑制; 阻碍;  |Something that discourages a particular action or behavior. | High taxes are a disincentive to business. 高税收不利于商业发展。 |  The disincentive of a long commute made her reconsider the job offer. 长途通勤的阻碍让她重新考虑了这份工作邀请。 | synonym: deterrent, obstacle, impediment | antonym: incentive, encouragement, motivation | noun

disincentivize:/ˌdɪsɪnˈsɛntɪvaɪz/ | v. | 打消...的念头; 阻止; | To discourage (someone) from doing something, especially by removing incentives or creating disincentives. |  The new law disincentivizes businesses from polluting. 新法律阻止企业污染环境。 | High fines disincentivize speeding. 高额罚款阻止超速行驶。 | synonym: deter, discourage, hinder | antonym: incentivize, encourage, motivate | verb
