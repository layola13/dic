gleam:/ɡliːm/| n. vi.|微光，闪光；闪现，流露|A faint or brief light, or a brief or faint show of some quality, feeling, etc. |The moonlight cast a gleam on the water. 月光在水面上投下了一道微光。|近义词：shine, glimmer, flash, sparkle, glint|反义词：dullness, darkness|词性:名词,动词

gleam:/ɡliːm/| vi.|发出微光；闪现，流露|To shine faintly or briefly; to appear briefly or faintly|A gleam of hope appeared in his eyes.  一丝希望的光芒出现在他的眼中。|近义词：shine, glimmer, flash, sparkle, glint|反义词：fade, dim|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *glæm, from Proto-Germanic *glaimaz ("gleam, brightness"). Cognate with Old Norse gleima ("to gleam").
