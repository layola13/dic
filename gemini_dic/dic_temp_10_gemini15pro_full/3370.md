affrontedly:/əˈfrʌn.tɪd.li/
adverb
冒犯地，愤懑地
In a manner that shows resentment or indignation at having been offended.
She stared at him affrontedly when he suggested she was lying. 当他暗示她在撒谎时，她愤怒地盯着他。
He turned his back affrontedly and walked away.  他愤愤地转过身走开了。
近义词: indignantly, resentfully, angrily, offensively
反义词: politely, respectfully, submissively, humbly
词性: adverb
