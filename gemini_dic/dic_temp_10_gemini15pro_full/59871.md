ecumenically:/ˌɛkjuːˈmɛnɪkli/ | adv. |普世地，不分教派地| In a way that promotes or relates to unity among the world's Christian churches. |The Pope appealed ecumenically for peace. 教皇呼吁普世和平。| 近义词: universally, globally, catholically | 反义词: parochially, narrowly, sectarianly | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From Late Latin *oecumenicus*, from Ancient Greek οἰκουμενικός (oikoumenikós, “of or pertaining to the inhabited world; general, universal”), from οἰκουμένη (oikouméni, “the inhabited world”), feminine present passive participle of οἰκέω (oikéō, “I inhabit”), from οἶκος (oîkos, “house”).
