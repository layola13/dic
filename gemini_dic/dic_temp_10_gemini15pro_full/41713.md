corroborator:/kəˈrɒbəreɪtər/ | n. | 证实者；确证者 |  A person or thing that corroborates something. | The witness acted as a corroborator for the defendant's alibi.  该证人为被告的不在场证明提供了佐证。 | synonyms: confirmer, validator, authenticator, verifier | antonyms:  contradictor, refuter, denier | noun

corroborate:/kəˈrɒbəreɪt/ | v. | 证实；确证 | Confirm or give support to (a statement, theory, or finding). |  The evidence corroborates his story. 证据证实了他的说法。| synonyms: confirm, verify, validate, authenticate, substantiate | antonyms: contradict, refute, deny, disprove | verb
