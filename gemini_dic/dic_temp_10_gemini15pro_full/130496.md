nonacceptance:/ˌnɒnəkˈsɛptəns/ | /ˌnɑːnəkˈsɛptəns/ | n. | 不接受；拒收 | The nonacceptance of the offer was a disappointment.  |  The artist was upset by the gallery's nonacceptance of his latest work. | 近义词：rejection, refusal, denial | 反义词：acceptance, approval | 词性: 名词

词源:  From non- + acceptance.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not". "Acceptance" comes from the Old French "accepter" and ultimately from the Latin "accipere" meaning "to take or receive."
