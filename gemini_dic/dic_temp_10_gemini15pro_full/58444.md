ducklings:/ˈdʌklɪŋz/
n.
小鸭子（duck 的复数）
Young ducks, typically referring to those not yet fully grown.
The mother duck led her ducklings to the pond. 母鸭带着她的小鸭子们去了池塘。

ducklings:/ˈdʌklɪŋz/
noun
小鸭子
Baby ducks.
Several ducklings were swimming in the pond.  几只小鸭子在池塘里游泳。


近义词:baby ducks, chicks (泛指幼鸟)
反义词:(无明确的反义词，可考虑 adult ducks 成年鸭子)
词性:noun
