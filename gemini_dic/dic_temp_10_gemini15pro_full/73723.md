forkbeard:/ˈfɔːrkˌbɪərd/| n. |叉须鱼，一种海鱼|A marine fish with a forked beard-like appendage on its chin. It's a type of cod. |例句：The forkbeard is a popular fish for eating in some European countries. 叉须鱼在一些欧洲国家是一种受欢迎的食用鱼。

例句：The fisherman caught a large forkbeard in his net. 渔夫的网里捕到了一条大叉须鱼。|近义词：cod, hake|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific fish species) |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From 'fork' + 'beard', referring to the two barbels or whisker-like appendages on the fish's chin, resembling a forked beard.
