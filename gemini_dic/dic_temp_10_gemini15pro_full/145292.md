overbragging: /ˌoʊvərˈbræɡɪŋ/
noun
过度吹嘘，过分自夸
The act of bragging excessively, often to the point of being annoying or unbelievable.
He lost some friends due to his constant overbragging about his accomplishments.  他因为不断地吹嘘自己的成就而失去了一些朋友。
boasting, showing off, bragging excessively
modesty, humility
n.
