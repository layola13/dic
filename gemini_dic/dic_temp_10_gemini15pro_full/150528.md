pants's:/pæntsɪz/ | n. |裤子（错误用法，通常用pants）|This form is grammatically incorrect.  The possessive of "pants" is formed by adding an apostrophe after the "s" as it is already plural, like "pants'".  While "pants's" might be seen occasionally, it is considered nonstandard.  "Pants" itself refers to a garment covering the body from the waist to the ankles, covering each leg separately. | Incorrect Example: *The pants's pockets were ripped. | Correct Example: The pants' pockets were ripped. 裤子的口袋破了。|  Near synonyms: trousers, slacks | Antonym: shorts | noun
