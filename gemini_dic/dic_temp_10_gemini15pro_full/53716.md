disclosure:/dɪˈskloʊʒər/ | n. | 揭露；公开；披露 | The act of making something known or the fact that something has been made known. |  The company made a full disclosure of its financial status.  公司充分披露了其财务状况。 | Example:  The disclosure of the secret documents caused a scandal.  秘密文件的披露引发了一场丑闻。 | Synonyms: revelation, unveiling, exposure, divulgence | Antonyms: concealment, suppression, secrecy | noun

disclosure:/dɪˈskloʊʒər/ | n. | 被揭露的事情；被公开的事情 | Something that is made known or revealed. | The newspaper published disclosures about the politician's personal life.  报纸公布了有关这位政治家私生活的披露内容。 | Example: The disclosures in the report shocked the public.  报告中的披露内容震惊了公众。 | Synonyms: revelation, exposé | Antonyms: secret, cover-up | noun
