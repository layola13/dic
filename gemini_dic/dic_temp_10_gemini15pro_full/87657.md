hexadecane:/ˌhɛksəˈdeɪkeɪn/| n.|十六烷|A saturated aliphatic hydrocarbon with the chemical formula C16H34. It is a colorless liquid at room temperature and is a component of diesel fuel.|Hexadecane is commonly used as a solvent in organic chemistry. 十六烷通常用作有机化学中的溶剂。|Hexadecane is a major component of paraffin wax. 十六烷是石蜡的主要成分。|近义词:cetane|反义词:|名词
