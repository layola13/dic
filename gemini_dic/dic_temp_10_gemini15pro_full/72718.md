fodder:/ˈfɑːdər/ /ˈfɒdər/| n.|饲料，草料；素材，原料|Food, especially dried hay or feed, for cattle and other livestock.  Figuratively, anything used as material for a specific purpose.|The cows were given fodder. 牛被喂了饲料。|The tabloid newspapers are always looking for fodder for their next scandal.  这些小报总是在寻找下一个丑闻的素材。|近义词：feed, forage, provender|反义词:|noun
