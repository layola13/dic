ductility:/dʌkˈtɪləti/ | n. |延展性，韧性 |The ability of a material to be drawn out into a wire or thread without breaking; the ability to be deformed plastically without fracturing. |Copper has high ductility, making it ideal for wiring. 铜具有很高的延展性，使其成为理想的电线材料。|Synonyms: malleability, plasticity, flexibility |Antonyms: brittleness, fragility, stiffness | noun
