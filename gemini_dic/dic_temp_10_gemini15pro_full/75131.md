friskily:/ˈfrɪskɪli/| adv.|活泼地，嬉戏地|In a playful and energetic way, often suggesting a lighthearted or mischievous manner.|The lambs frisked friskily in the meadow. 羊羔在草地上嬉戏玩耍。|The puppies frisked friskily around their mother. 小狗们绕着它们的妈妈活泼地跳来跳去。|近义词：playfully, energetically, merrily|反义词：sluggishly, listlessly|词性: 副词

Etymology:  From frisk + -ly.  Frisk (v.) comes from Old French *frisque "fresh, lively," from Frankish *frisc, from Proto-Germanic *friskaz "fresh, new."
