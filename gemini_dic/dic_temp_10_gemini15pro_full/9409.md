antiracist:/ˈæntiˈreɪsɪst/ /ˌæntiˈreɪsɪst/
adjective| 反种族主义的|Actively opposing racism; supporting anti-racism.|例句：She's an antiracist activist working to dismantle systemic oppression. 她是一位致力于瓦解系统性压迫的反种族主义活动家。

antiracist:/ˈæntiˈreɪsɪst/ /ˌæntiˈreɪsɪst/
noun| 反种族主义者|A person who actively opposes racism.|例句：He considers himself an antiracist and strives to challenge prejudice whenever he encounters it. 他认为自己是一个反种族主义者，并努力在他遇到偏见时挑战它。


近义词:nonracist, egalitarian, integrationist
反义词:racist, bigot, prejudiced
词性:adjective, noun

词源:
anti- (prefix) : against, opposed to  (from Greek)
racist (noun/adjective): a person who believes that a particular race is superior to others  (from race + -ist)
