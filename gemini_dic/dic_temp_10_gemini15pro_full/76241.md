futuritial: /ˌfjuːtʃəˈrɪʃəl/ | adj. | 未来主义的，未来派的 | Relating to or resembling the future, especially in a way that is considered advanced or visionary. |  The architect's design was strikingly futuritial, incorporating sleek lines and innovative materials.  这位建筑师的设计惊人地具有未来感，融合了流畅的线条和创新的材料。

The movie presented a futuritial society where robots performed all menial tasks. 这部电影展现了一个未来主义的社会，在那里机器人执行所有琐碎的任务。 | 近义词: futuristic, forward-looking, avant-garde, visionary | 反义词: antiquated, outdated, old-fashioned, traditional | 词性: adjective
