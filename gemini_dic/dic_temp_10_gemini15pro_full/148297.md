oversoftly:/ˌoʊvərˈsɒftli/ | adv. | 过于柔软地，过分温柔地 | In a manner that is excessively soft or gentle. | She spoke oversoftly, and I could barely hear her. 她说话太轻了，我几乎听不见。|  He patted the puppy oversoftly, afraid to hurt it. 他极其温柔地拍了拍小狗，生怕弄疼它。 | 近义词: too softly, very softly, extremely softly | 反义词: harshly, loudly, firmly | 词性: 副词 | 词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessively") + softly (adverb)
