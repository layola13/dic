disreputability:/dɪsˌrepjətəˈbɪləti/
noun
不体面, 声名狼藉, 坏名声
The state of being disreputable; having a bad reputation.
His disreputability made it difficult for him to find work. 他的坏名声使他很难找到工作。
Synonyms: notoriety, ill repute, disgrace, dishonor, infamy
Antonyms: respectability, good repute, honor, prestige
词性: 名词


disreputable:/dɪsˈrepjətəbəl/
adjective
声名狼藉的，不体面的
Not considered respectable in character or appearance.
He frequented disreputable bars. 他经常光顾声名狼藉的酒吧。
Synonyms: shady, notorious, infamous, dishonorable, disgraceful
