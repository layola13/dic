let slip: /lɛt slɪp/ | v. | 无意中泄露，不小心说出 | to reveal something unintentionally, often a secret |  He let slip that he was planning to resign. 他无意中透露了他打算辞职的消息。

He let slip a confidential document to the press. 他不小心把一份机密文件泄露给了媒体。

近义词: divulge, reveal, disclose, blurt out | 反义词: conceal, hide, keep secret | 词性: 动词

词源:  From the Old English *lētan slīpan, meaning "to allow to slip away." The "let" part comes from Old English *lētan, meaning "to allow," and "slip" comes from Old English *slīpan, meaning "to slip."
