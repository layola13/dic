ecgonine:/ˈɛkɡəniːn/ |n.| 芽子碱; 莨菪酮|A tropane alkaloid found in coca leaves, it is a precursor to cocaine and a metabolite of cocaine. It is a controlled substance in many countries.|Ecgonine is the chemical backbone of cocaine. 芽子碱是可卡因的化学骨架。|  Ecgonine itself has little psychoactive effect. 芽子碱本身几乎没有精神活性作用。|Synonyms:  3-hydroxy-2-tropane carboxylic acid (chemical name)|Antonyms:  None|noun
