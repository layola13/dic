erythrochroism:/ɪˈrɪθrəkroʊɪzəm/
noun
红变色，红变现象
The property of some crystals of exhibiting a different color according to the direction of the transmitted light.
Example 1: The erythrochroism of the ruby was striking under the polarized light. 红宝石在偏振光下的红变色现象很引人注目。

Example 2:  The gemologist noted the distinct erythrochroism of the tourmaline. 宝石学家注意到了碧玺明显的红变色现象。
近义词: pleochroism, dichroism
反义词: (No direct antonym, but the absence of erythrochroism could be considered an opposite characteristic.)
