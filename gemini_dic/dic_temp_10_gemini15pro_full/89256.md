hominin:/ˈhɒmɪnɪn/ | n. | 人族；人类 | A primate of a family (Hominidae) that includes humans and their fossil ancestors. |  Early hominins used tools for hunting and gathering. 早期人类使用工具进行狩猎和采集。| synonym: human, hominid | antonym:  (no direct antonym, perhaps other primate families) | noun
