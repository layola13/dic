bandiest:/ˈbændɪɪst/| adj.|弯曲最多的；腿最罗圈的|Having the most bends or curves; most bowlegged.  Relating to something that has many turns or bends, especially legs.|例句：He was the bandiest cowboy I'd ever seen. 他是我见过的腿最罗圈的牛仔。

例句：The bandiest road through the mountains was also the most scenic.  山中最弯曲的道路风景也最优美。|近义词：curviest, crookedest, windingest|反义词：straightest, flattest|词性:形容词

词源:  From "bandy" + "-est" (superlative suffix). "Bandy" originates from the Old French "bander," meaning "to curve or bend," likely related to the Germanic word "band," meaning "ribbon" or "strap."
