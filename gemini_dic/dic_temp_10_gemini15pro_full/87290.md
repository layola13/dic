hermetically:/hɜːˈmetɪkli/ | adv. | 密封地，不透气地 | In a way that is completely airtight; sealed so that no air or gas can enter or escape. | The tomb was hermetically sealed for centuries. 墓穴被密封了几个世纪。| Synonyms: airtightly, tightly, securely | Antonyms: loosely, openly | adverb

hermetically:/hərˈmetɪkli/ | adj. | 密封的，不透气的 | Completely airtight; sealed so that no air or gas can enter or escape. | The food was preserved in a hermetically sealed container.  食物被保存在一个密封的容器里。| Synonyms: airtight, sealed, closed | Antonyms: open, unsealed, leaky | adjective
