exceptionalities:/ɪkˌsɛpʃənˈælɪtiz/| n. |特殊性，例外情况，异常情况|The state or quality of being exceptional; unusual qualities, abilities, or characteristics, especially relating to intelligence, learning, or behavior, often requiring specialized educational or support services.|The school caters to the exceptionalities of gifted children.  这所学校致力于满足资优儿童的特殊需求。|Synonyms: peculiarities, anomalies, idiosyncrasies, deviations, singularities|Antonyms: normalities, regularities, typicalities|noun

exceptionalities:/ɪkˌsɛpʃənˈælɪtiz/| n. |例外，特殊情况|Instances that deviate from the norm or standard.|  We must make exceptionalities for students with learning disabilities. 我们必须为学习障碍学生提供特殊照顾。|Synonyms: exemptions, exclusions, special cases|Antonyms: norms, standards, rules|noun
