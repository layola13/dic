ensiferous:/ɛnˈsɪfərəs/
adjective
带剑的;佩剑的;持剑的
Carrying a sword; sword-bearing.
The ensifrous knight stood guard at the castle gate.  带剑的骑士守卫着城堡大门。
近义词:sword-bearing, armed (with a sword)
反义词:unarmed, disarmed
词性:adjective
词源:From Latin *ensifer, from ensis (“sword”) + -fer (“bearing”).
