otoba:/oʊˈtoʊbə/| n.|奥托巴木, 奥托巴脂|A fragrant resin obtained from the Myristica otoba tree, native to tropical America. It is used in perfumery and traditional medicine.|The otoba resin has a warm, spicy aroma. 奥托巴脂有一种温暖的辛辣香味。|The indigenous people use otoba resin for medicinal purposes. 土著居民将奥托巴脂用于药用。|Synonyms: nutmeg oil, mace oil|Antonyms: N/A|noun
