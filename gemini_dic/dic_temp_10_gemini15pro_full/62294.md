endanger:/ɪnˈdeɪndʒər/ | vt. | 危害，使遭危险 | To put someone or something in a situation where they could be harmed or damaged. |例句：
The pollution is endangering the health of the local residents. 
污染正在危害当地居民的健康。

例句：
Smoking endangers your health.
吸烟危害你的健康。

近义词：threaten, jeopardize, imperil | 反义词：protect, safeguard | 词性：动词
