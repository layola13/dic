marsupial: /mɑːrˈsuːpiəl/ (US) /mɑːˈsjuːpiəl/ (UK)| n. adj.|有袋动物，有袋的|A mammal of an order whose members are born incompletely developed and are typically carried and suckled in a pouch on the mother's belly.  |例句：Kangaroos and koalas are marsupials. 袋鼠和考拉是有袋动物。

|例句：The Tasmanian devil is a carnivorous marsupial. 袋獾是一种食肉的有袋动物。|近义词：pouched mammal|反义词: placental mammal|词性: noun, adjective
