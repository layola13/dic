automatic:/ˌɔːtəˈmætɪk/ | adj. n. |自动的；无意识的；自动装置| operating by itself; involuntary; a device or process that operates automatically |  The car has an automatic transmission. 这辆车有自动变速器。| synonyms: automated, self-acting, involuntary, mechanical | antonyms: manual, deliberate, voluntary | adjective

automatic:/ˌɔːtəˈmætɪk/ | n. |自动装置；自动手枪| a device that operates automatically, especially a firearm | He pulled out his automatic. 他掏出了他的自动手枪。| synonyms: machine, appliance, pistol | antonyms: manual device | noun
