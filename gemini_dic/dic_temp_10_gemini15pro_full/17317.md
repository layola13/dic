bdellovibrio:/ˌbɛloʊˈvɪbrioʊ/
noun
蛭弧菌
A genus of small, highly motile, curved, gram-negative bacteria of the delta subgroup of proteobacteria that preys on other gram-negative bacteria, including many plant and animal pathogens.  Bdellovibrio are found in soil, sewage, and aquatic environments.
Bdellovibrio bacteriovorus is a predatory bacterium that attacks and kills other Gram-negative bacteria.
近义词:  predator (in a broader sense)
反义词:  prey
词性: n.
