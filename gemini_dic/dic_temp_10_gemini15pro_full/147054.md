overlearned:/ˌoʊvərˈlɜːrnd/| adj.|过度学习的，过度训练的|Learned or practiced to the point of automaticity; excessively learned.|例句：The pianist's performance was technically perfect, but some critics found it overlearned and lacking in spontaneity.  这位钢琴家的演奏技巧上很完美，但一些评论家认为它过度学习，缺乏自发性。

例句：His responses to the questions were so overlearned that they seemed almost robotic.  他对这些问题的回答是如此过度学习，以至于它们看起来几乎像机器人一样。|近义词：rote, ingrained, habitual|反义词：underlearned, unpracticed|词性:形容词
