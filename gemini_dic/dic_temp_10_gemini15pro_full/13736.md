auburn:/ˈɔːbɜrn/ (US), /ˈɔːbən/ (UK)| adj.|赤褐色的，红褐色的|A reddish-brown color, typically of hair.|例句：Her auburn hair gleamed in the sunlight. 她的赤褐色头发在阳光下闪闪发光。|例句：He admired her long, auburn locks. 他欣赏她那一头赤褐色的长发。|近义词：reddish-brown, chestnut, Titian|反义词:blond, light brown|词性:形容词
词源:From Old French *alborne, from Latin alburnus (“whitish”), from albus (“white”).  The sense shift from "whitish" to "reddish-brown" is uncertain, but may be due to association with the color of alburnum (sapwood).
