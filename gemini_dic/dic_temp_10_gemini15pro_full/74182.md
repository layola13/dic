four-wheeler:/ˈfɔːrˌwiːlər/| n.|四轮车，四轮驱动车|A vehicle with four wheels, especially an all-terrain vehicle (ATV).  A four-wheeler is often used for recreational off-road driving.|He took his four-wheeler out for a spin in the mud. 他开着他的四轮车在泥地里兜风。|近义词：ATV, quad bike, all-terrain vehicle|反义词: two-wheeler|词性:noun

four-wheeler:/ˈfɔːrˌwiːlər/| n.|四轮车，四轮驱动车|A vehicle with four wheels, especially a car or truck.  While less common than "ATV," it can be used to refer to any four-wheeled vehicle.|She drove her four-wheeler to the grocery store. 她开着她的四轮车去了杂货店。|近义词：car, truck, automobile, vehicle|反义词:  bicycle, motorcycle|词性:noun
