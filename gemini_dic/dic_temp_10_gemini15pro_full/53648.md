discharged:/dɪsˈtʃɑːrdʒd/
verb|释放，排出；卸货；解雇；放电|release, emit, unload, dismiss, fire
The hospital discharged him yesterday. 他昨天出院了。
The factory discharged waste into the river. 工厂向河里排放废物。
discharged:/dɪsˈtʃɑːrdʒd/
adjective|已释放的；已卸货的；已解雇的；已放电的|released, unloaded, dismissed, fired
The discharged patient went home. 出院的病人回家了。
The discharged battery needs to be recharged. 放完电的电池需要充电。


Etymology->
From Old French descharger ("to unload"), from des- ("from, away") + charger ("to load"). Ultimately from Latin carrus ("cart").

Synonyms->
release, emit, expel, unload, dismiss, fire, axe (informal), sack (informal), let go, free, liberate

Antonyms->
retain, hold, admit, hire, employ, charge (battery)
