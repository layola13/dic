abuzz:/əˈbʌz/| adj.|嗡嗡作响的，嘈杂的，兴奋的|Filled with a low, continuous humming sound; lively with excitement and activity.|The room was abuzz with excitement before the concert.  音乐会前，房间里充满了兴奋的嗡嗡声。

The office was abuzz with rumors of a merger. 办公室里充满了关于合并的传言。|近义词：humming, buzzing, lively, vibrant, active, animated|反义词：quiet, silent, still, inactive|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From a- + buzz.  "A-" acts as a prefix indicating a state or process. "Buzz" imitates the sound of a bee or other insect.  The word gained its figurative sense of excitement and activity later.

USAGE->
The room was abuzz with activity. = 房间里嗡嗡作响，一片繁忙。
The hive was abuzz with bees. = 蜂巢里蜜蜂嗡嗡作响。


The market was abuzz with shoppers. = 市场里熙熙攘攘，挤满了购物者。
The air was abuzz with anticipation. = 空气中弥漫着期待的气氛。


PHRASE->
keep/set something abuzz = 使某事充满活力和兴奋
get abuzz = 变得兴奋和活跃

注意事项:
abuzz 通常用来形容一个地方或气氛，而不是形容单个的人或物。
