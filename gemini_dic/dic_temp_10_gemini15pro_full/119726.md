midsummers:/ˈmɪdsʌmərz/| n.|仲夏，仲夏节|Midsummers is the period of time around the summer solstice.|例句：We always celebrate midsummers with a bonfire. 我们总是以篝火庆祝仲夏。|例句：The midsummers festival is a joyous occasion. 仲夏节是一个欢乐的节日。|近义词：midsummer|反义词:midwinter|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *mydsomer, from Old English midsumer (“midsummer”), from midde (“mid”) + sumer (“summer”). Cognate with Dutch midzomer, German Mittsommer.
