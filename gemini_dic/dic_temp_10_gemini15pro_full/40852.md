cooeeing:/ˈkuːˌiːɪŋ/
n.
呼喊;大声叫喊
a long, high-pitched cry or call, used especially in the Australian bush to attract attention.
例句：The cooeeing of the lost hiker echoed through the valley.  迷路徒步旅行者的呼喊声在山谷中回荡。

例句：We heard the cooeeing of children playing in the distance. 我们听到远处孩子们玩耍的叫喊声。

近义词：shouting, yelling, calling, hollering
反义词：whispering, murmuring

词性: noun (名词), and less commonly as a verb (动词) - to cooee

词源: From Dharuk *guwiː "come here".
