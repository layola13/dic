bequeathment:/bɪˈkwiːðmənt/
n.
遗产；遗赠；遗赠物
The act of bequeathing something (property, money, etc.) to someone after one's death, or the thing itself that is bequeathed.
His will included a bequeathment of $10,000 to his alma mater. 他的遗嘱包括向他的母校捐赠1万美元。
legacy, inheritance, bequest
donation, gift
