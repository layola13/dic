inexertion:/ˌɪnɪɡˈzɜːrʃən/
n.
lack of exertion; inactivity
The state of not exerting oneself; inactivity; lack of effort.
His increasing weight was due to inexertion.  他日益增长的体重是由于缺乏运动。
Near synonyms: inactivity, idleness, indolence, laziness
Antonyms: exertion, activity, effort
noun
