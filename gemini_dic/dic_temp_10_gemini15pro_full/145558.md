overcompetitive:/ˌoʊvərˌkəmˈpɛtɪtɪv/
adjective
过度竞争的；竞争过度的
Excessively competitive; too focused on winning or succeeding, often to the detriment of oneself or others.
He's become so overcompetitive that he can't enjoy playing games anymore. 他变得竞争心太强，以至于再也无法享受游戏的乐趣了。
The overcompetitive atmosphere at the school put a lot of pressure on the students.  学校竞争激烈的氛围给学生带来了很大的压力。
近义词: hypercompetitive, cutthroat, overly ambitious
反义词: cooperative, collaborative, laid-back
词性: adjective
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "competitive" (adjective from "compete" from Latin competere "to strive in common, agree, come together, contend")
