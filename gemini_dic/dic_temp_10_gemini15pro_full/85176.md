harefoot:/ˈheərfʊt/| n. |野兔, 胆小鬼, 逃兵|A hare's foot, sometimes carried as a good luck charm. Also, a person who runs or moves quickly, or someone timid or cowardly.|例句：He was so swift, they called him harefoot.  他跑得很快，他们叫他飞毛腿。

例句：The harefoot darted across the field.  那只野兔飞快地穿过田野。|近义词：coward, deserter, timid person, speedster|反义词:brave, courageous, hero|词性:noun
