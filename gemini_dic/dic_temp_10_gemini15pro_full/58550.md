duh:/dʌ/ | interj. | 哎哟，真是的；呃；废话 | Expressing obviousness or impatience.  Used to indicate that something is obvious or self-evident. | Duh, of course I know that! 真是的，我当然知道！ |  Synonyms: obviously, well, no kidding | Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, as it expresses a sentiment rather than a factual concept) | interjection
