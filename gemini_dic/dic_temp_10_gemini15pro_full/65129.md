ethically:/ˈeθɪkli/
adverb
伦理地；道德地
In an ethical manner; morally.
She acted ethically in donating the money to charity. 她把钱捐给慈善机构，行为合乎道德。
近义词:morally, virtuously, righteously
反义词:unethically, immorally, wrongly
词性:adverb

词源:
From "ethical" + "-ly".  "Ethical" comes from the Late Latin "ethicus," from the Greek "ēthikos," meaning "relating to moral character," from "ēthos," meaning "character, custom."
