outstepped:/ˌaʊtˈstɛpt/| v. |超越，超出…的限度|To go beyond the proper limits of; exceed.|He outstepped his authority when he fired the employee without consulting his superiors. 他在没有咨询上级的情况下解雇了这名员工，超出了他的权限。|The company outstepped its budget by a significant margin. 这家公司大大超出了预算。|Synonyms: exceed, overstep, transgress|Antonyms: observe, respect, comply|verb
