nonrefundabilities:/ˌnɒnriːˈfʌndəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 不可退款性；不退还款项 | The nonrefundabilities of the tickets caused a lot of frustration among the travelers.  机票的不可退款性让很多旅客感到沮丧。| Synonym: non-reimbursability | Antonym: refundability | Noun

Etymology:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "refund" (from re- "back" + fundere "to pour") + "-able" (suffix making an adjective meaning "capable of being") + "-ity" (suffix forming abstract nouns from adjectives).  Essentially, it describes the quality of not being able to be refunded.
