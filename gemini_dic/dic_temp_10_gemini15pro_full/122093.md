mission: /ˈmɪʃən/ (US) /ˈmɪʃn/ (UK)
mission: n. | 任务，使命；代表团；布道，传教 | A specific task or duty assigned to a person or group; a group of people sent to a foreign country to conduct negotiations, establish relationships, or offer aid; a strongly felt aim, ambition, or calling. |例句：The astronaut's mission was to collect rock samples from the moon. 宇航员的任务是从月球收集岩石样本。

mission: |例句：The diplomatic mission worked tirelessly to secure a peace treaty.  外交使团不知疲倦地工作以确保和平条约的签订。|近义词：assignment, task, duty, delegation, objective, purpose, calling, vocation |反义词: leisure, idleness, vacation |词性: noun
