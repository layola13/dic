dendritical:/dɛnˈdrɪtɪk(ə)l/
adjective
树枝状的；枝晶状的
Relating to or resembling the branching structure of a dendrite (a short branched extension of a nerve cell, along which impulses received from other cells at synapses are transmitted to the cell body).
The neuron's dendritic tree receives signals from other neurons.  这个神经元的树突接收来自其他神经元的信号。
The metal formed dendritic patterns as it cooled.  金属冷却时形成了树枝状的图案。
synonyms: branching, arborescent, tree-like, ramified
antonyms: linear, straight
词性: adjective
