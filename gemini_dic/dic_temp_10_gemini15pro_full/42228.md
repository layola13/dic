count's: /kaʊnts/ | n. |伯爵的；伯爵领地| Belonging to or associated with a count (a European nobleman).  | The count's castle overlooked the valley. 伯爵的城堡俯瞰着山谷。| synonyms: earl's, nobleman's | antonyms: commoner's, peasant's | noun (possessive)

count's: /kaʊnts/ | n. |计数；总数 | The total number of something. | The final count's still uncertain. 最终的计数仍然不确定。 | synonyms: total, tally, sum | antonyms: fraction, portion | noun (contraction of "count is")


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *conte*, from Latin *comes* ("companion"), later a title of high rank.  The possessive form is created by adding 's.
