bangers:/ˈbæŋərz/ | n. |香肠；（非正式）好听的歌曲；精彩的事物 | sausages; (informal) excellent songs or things; something exciting or excellent |  例句：We had bangers and mash for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了香肠和土豆泥。

例句：These new headphones are bangers! 这款新耳机太棒了！| 近义词：sausages (for food), hits (for songs), great things, excellent things | 反义词：flops (for songs), terrible things | 词性:名词

banger:/ˈbæŋər/ | n. |香肠；爆竹；旧车；精彩的事物 | sausage; firecracker; old car; something exciting or excellent | 例句：He lit a banger for the Fourth of July. 他为七月四日点燃了一个爆竹。
