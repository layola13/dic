multilobular:/ˌmʌltiˈlɒbjʊlə(r)/ /ˌmʌltiˈlɑːbjələr/ | adj. | 多叶的；多裂的 | Having many lobes or lobules.  A lobule is a small lobe, and the prefix "multi-" means many. Therefore, multilobular describes something that is divided into many small lobes. This term is frequently used in anatomy and medicine, particularly in describing organs or tissues. | The doctor identified a multilobular mass in the patient's lung. 医生在病人的肺部发现了一个多叶的肿块。

The multilobular structure of the liver allows for efficient blood filtration. 肝脏的多叶结构使其能够有效地过滤血液。 | multilobed, polylobular | unilobular | adjective
