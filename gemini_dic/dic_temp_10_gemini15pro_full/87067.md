heptametrical:/ˌhɛptəˈmɛtrɪkəl/
adjective
七音步的
Consisting of or written in heptameter (a line of verse consisting of seven metrical feet).
The poem is written in heptametrical verse, giving it a flowing rhythm. 这首诗是用七音步诗句写成的，赋予它流畅的节奏。
The poet experimented with heptametrical lines in his latest work. 这位诗人在他的最新作品中尝试了七音步诗行。
Near Synonyms: seven-foot, seven-measure
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but consider contrasting metrical terms like iambic pentameter, dactylic hexameter)
