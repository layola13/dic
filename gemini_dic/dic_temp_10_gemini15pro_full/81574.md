grazioso:/ɡrɑːtˈsjoʊzoʊ/ /ɡræˈtsjoʊzoʊ/
adjective
优美的，优雅的
Graceful; elegant.  Used as a musical direction.
The ballerina's grazioso movements captivated the audience. / 芭蕾舞演员优雅的动作吸引了观众。
She played the piece with a grazioso touch. / 她演奏这首曲子时带着优雅的触感。
近义词:graceful, elegant, stylish, refined
反义词:clumsy, awkward, ungainly
词性:adjective
词源:Italian, from grazia 'grace'.
