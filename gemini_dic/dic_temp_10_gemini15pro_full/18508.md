beneath:/bɪˈniːθ/ | prep. adv.|在...下面，低于，次于|Located or situated lower than something else; underneath. |The coin was found beneath the sofa.  硬币是在沙发下面找到的。

beneath:/bɪˈniːθ/ | prep. adv.|在...下面，低于，次于|Not worthy of; inferior to. |Such behavior is beneath you. 这种行为有失你的身份。

beneath:/bɪˈniːθ/ | prep. adv.|在...下面，低于，次于|Hidden by or covered by.|Beneath the surface of the water, the fish swam peacefully. 在水面下，鱼儿平静地游着。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *beneoðan (attested only in the dative byneoðan), from be- (by, near) + neoðan (below, beneath). Cognate with Old Frisian binethe, Old Saxon binithan, Old High German binidan, Old Norse niðan.


SYNONYM->
under, underneath, below, lower than


ANTONYM->
above, over, on top of


POS->
