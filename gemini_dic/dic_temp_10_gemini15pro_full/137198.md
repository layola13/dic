nonsuit:/ˌnɒnˈsuːt/ | n. v. |不起诉，驳回诉讼|A judgment against a plaintiff for failure to prosecute a suit or to introduce sufficient evidence. |The judge nonsuited the case due to lack of evidence.| 近义词:dismissal, rejection | 反义词:prosecution, acceptance | 词性:名词, 动词 | 词源:From Anglo-French *non suyte, literally, "not followed," past participle of non suivre "not to follow."
