beltways:/ˈbeltweɪz/
n.
环城公路，环状道路
A beltway is a highway that loops around a city.
The traffic on the beltway was particularly heavy this morning. 今早环城公路的交通特别拥堵。

The Capital Beltway encircles Washington, D.C. 首都环线环绕着华盛顿特区。
近义词:bypass, ring road, loop
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "city center" or "downtown")
词性:noun
