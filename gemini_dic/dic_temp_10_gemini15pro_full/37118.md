combustibles:/kəmˈbʌstɪblz/
|n.|易燃物，可燃物|Substances that can burn easily.|The garage was full of combustibles, making it a fire hazard.  车库里堆满了易燃物，很容易引发火灾。|Example:  All combustibles should be stored safely away from heat sources. 所有易燃物都应该安全地存放在远离热源的地方。|Synonyms: flammable materials, inflammables|Antonyms: non-combustibles, fire-retardant materials|noun

combustible:/kəmˈbʌstɪbl/
|adj.|易燃的，可燃烧的|Able to catch fire and burn easily.|Paper and wood are combustible materials. 纸和木头是易燃材料。|Example:  The fire spread quickly through the dry, combustible brush.  火势迅速蔓延过干燥易燃的灌木丛。|Synonyms: flammable, inflammable|Antonyms: non-combustible, fire-retardant, fireproof|adjective
