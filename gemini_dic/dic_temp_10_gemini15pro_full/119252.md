michaelmas:/ˈmɪkəlməs/| n.|米迦勒节|The feast of St. Michael and All Angels, celebrated on September 29th. It traditionally marks the end of the harvest.|例句：Michaelmas is a quarter day in England. 米迦勒节在英格兰是一个季度结算日。

例句：The university term begins at Michaelmas. 大学学期在米迦勒节开始。|近义词：Michaelmas Day|反义词: None|词性:名词

词源: From Old English *Michelesmæsse, composed of Michael (from Hebrew mīkhāʼēl, “who is like God?”) + mass (from Late Latin missa).
