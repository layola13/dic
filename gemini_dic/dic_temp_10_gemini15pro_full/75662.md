fulcible:/ˈfʌlsɪbəl/| adj.|可推动的，可压制的|Capable of being pushed, propelled, or pressed.  Relating to a fulcrum or leverage.|The fulcible nature of the piston allows it to compress the air in the cylinder.|近义词:pushable, compressible, leverageable|反义词:immovable, rigid, inflexible|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *fulcibilis, from fulcire "to prop, support," related to fulcrum "prop, support."
