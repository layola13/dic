brahmagupta:/ˌbrɑːməˈɡuːptə/
n.
婆罗摩笈多
An Indian mathematician and astronomer who lived in the 7th century CE.  He is the author of the Brāhmasphuṭasiddhānta (Correctly Established Doctrine of Brahma), a major work on mathematics and astronomy.
Brahmagupta is known for his work on zero and negative numbers, as well as his contributions to algebra and geometry.
例句：Brahmagupta made significant contributions to the field of mathematics. 婆罗摩笈多对数学领域做出了重大贡献。

例句：Brahmagupta's formula is used to find the area of a cyclic quadrilateral. 婆罗摩笈多的公式用于求圆内接四边形的面积。
近义词: mathematician, astronomer
