noncorporation:/ˌnɑːnkɔːrpəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 非公司，非法人团体 | An entity that is not incorporated as a legal corporation, lacking the legal status and protections of a corporation. | Example 1: The small business operated as a noncorporation, leaving the owners personally liable for debts. 这家小企业作为非法人团体运营，业主个人要对债务负责。

Example 2:  Many noncorporations are partnerships or sole proprietorships. 许多非法人团体是合伙企业或独资企业。 | Near Synonyms: unincorporated business, unincorporated association | Antonyms: corporation, incorporated entity | noun
