circumstantial:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈstænʃəl/ | adj. |  依情况而定的；详细的；间接的 | Relating to, dependent on, or derived from circumstances.  Detailed; giving full details. Based on inference rather than direct evidence. | The evidence against him was purely circumstantial.  针对他的证据完全是间接的。| synonyms: indirect, inferential, presumptive, incidental, detailed, particularized | antonyms: direct, conclusive, essential | adjective

circumstantials:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈstænʃəlz/ | n. | 细节；详情 | The details or facts of a situation, incident, or story. | The police are still piecing together the circumstantials of the crime. 警方仍在拼凑这起犯罪的细节。| synonyms: details, particulars, specifics, minutiae | antonyms: generalities, outlines | noun
