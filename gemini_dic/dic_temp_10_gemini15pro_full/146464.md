overfruitful:/ˌoʊvərˈfrʊtfəl/
adjective
过度多产的；结果过多的
Producing or yielding too much fruit; excessively fruitful.
The apple tree was overfruitful this year, and the branches were bending under the weight. 今年的苹果树结果过多，树枝都因负重而弯曲。
Synonyms: prolific, abundant, copious, exuberant
Antonyms: barren, infertile, unproductive
词性: 形容词
