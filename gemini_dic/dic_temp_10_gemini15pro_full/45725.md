curvicostate: /ˌkɜːrvɪˈkɒsteɪt/ | adj. | 肋骨弯曲的 | Having curved ribs. | Example: The fossil revealed a curvicostate structure, indicating the creature had unusually curved ribs. 这块化石展现出一种肋骨弯曲的结构，表明该生物的肋骨异常弯曲。

Example:  Curvicostate shells are a distinguishing feature of certain brachiopods. 肋骨弯曲的贝壳是某些腕足动物的显著特征。 | 近义词:  curved-ribbed | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly straight-ribbed, but this isn't a standard term) | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin "curvus" (curved) + "costa" (rib) + "-ate" (suffix forming adjectives).
