neared:/nɪrd/
verb
接近，靠近
came or drew nearer
例句：The train neared the station. 火车接近了车站。

例句：As the deadline neared, the team worked around the clock.  随着截止日期的临近，团队夜以继日地工作。

近义词：approached, closed in on
反义词：receded, retreated
词性：v.
