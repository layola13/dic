faze:/feɪz/| v. |使慌乱；使担忧；使不安|To disturb the composure of; disconcert.|The news didn't faze him. 这消息并没有让他感到不安。|近义词: disturb, unsettle, disconcert, ruffle, fluster|反义词:  calm, reassure, comfort|词性:动词

Etymology:  From Old English *fēsian "drive away, put to flight," causative of *fēon "to flee, vanish."  Related to foe.  The current meaning developed in American English in the late 19th century.
