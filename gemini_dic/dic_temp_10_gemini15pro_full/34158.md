clambake:/ˈklæmˌbeɪk/| n.| 野餐，海滩聚餐，(尤指)吃蛤蜊的聚会|A social gathering at which clams and other kinds of seafood are cooked and eaten, typically on the beach.|We're having a clambake on the beach this weekend. 我们这周末要在海滩上举行一次野餐。|Synonyms: beach party, cookout, picnic|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider "formal dinner")|Noun
