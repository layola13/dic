cigarette:/ˌsɪɡəˈrɛt/ | n. | 香烟；纸烟 | A small roll of finely cut tobacco enclosed in a paper cylinder for smoking. | He lit a cigarette. 他点燃了一支香烟。| synonym: fag, butt | antonym: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
