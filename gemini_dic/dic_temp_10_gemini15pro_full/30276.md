cementatory:/sɪˈmɛntətɔːri/
adjective
粘合的，结合的
Relating to cement; having the power to unite or bind.
The cementatory properties of the substance make it ideal for repairing broken pottery.  这种物质的粘合特性使其成为修复破碎陶器的理想选择。
The protein acts as a cementatory agent, binding the cells together.  这种蛋白质充当粘合剂，将细胞结合在一起。
Synonyms: adhesive, binding, cohesive, unifying
Antonyms: separating, dividing, disuniting
词性: adjective
