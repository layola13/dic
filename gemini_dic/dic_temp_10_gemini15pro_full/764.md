absolvable:/əbˈzɒlvəbl/ /æbˈsɑːlvəbl/| adj.|可赦免的，可免除的|Able to be absolved; pardonable.  Forgivable; excusable.|His actions, though regrettable, were ultimately absolvable. 他的行为虽然令人遗憾，但最终还是可以原谅的。

The priest absolved him of his sins. 神父赦免了他的罪过。|近义词：pardonable, forgivable, excusable, venial, remissible|反义词：unpardonable, unforgivable, inexcusable, unabsolvable|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French absolvable, from absolver, based on Latin absolvere ‘acquit’.
