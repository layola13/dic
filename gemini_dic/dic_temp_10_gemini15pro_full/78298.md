germiparity:/ˌdʒɜːrmɪˈpærɪti/
n.
生殖力；胎生
The condition or mode of reproduction in which young develop from eggs that are retained within the body of the parent until hatching or birth.
Guppies exhibit germiparity, giving birth to live young.  孔雀鱼表现出胎生，产下活的幼鱼。
近义词: viviparity
反义词: oviparity
词性: noun

Etymology: From Latin *germen* (bud, sprout) + *paritas* (equality, parity).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *(s)ger- (to sprout).
