indiaman:/ˈɪndiəmən/| n.|东印度公司商船|A large sailing ship formerly used by the East India Company for carrying cargo to and from India and East Asia.|The indiaman sailed from London to Calcutta, laden with goods.|Near synonyms: merchantman, trading vessel, East Indiaman|Antonyms: None (lack of a direct opposite for a specific type of ship)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From India + man.  Originally referring to any ship trading with India, it later specifically designated ships of the British East India Company.
