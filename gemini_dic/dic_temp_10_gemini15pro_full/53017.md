dint:/dɪnt/
noun
力量；影响力；凹痕
force; power; impact; a dent
He succeeded by dint of hard work. 他凭借努力工作取得了成功。
The hail left a dint in the car's hood. 冰雹在汽车引擎盖上留下了一个凹痕。
Synonyms: force, power, strength, impact, dent, impression
Antonyms: weakness, impotence, fragility
n.

dint:/dɪnt/
verb
使凹陷；留下痕迹
to make a dent in; to mark by a blow
The hammer dinted the metal plate. 锤子把金属板砸凹了。
His reputation was dinted by the scandal.  丑闻损害了他的名誉。
Synonyms: dent, gouge, impress, mark
Antonyms: smooth, flatten, repair
v.
