lemonade:/ˌleməˈneɪd/ | n. |柠檬水|A drink made from lemon juice, water, and sugar.|  例句：I love a glass of cold lemonade on a hot day. 我喜欢在大热天喝一杯冰凉的柠檬水。

例句：She made homemade lemonade with fresh lemons. 她用新鲜柠檬做了自制柠檬水。

近义词：lemon squash, lemon drink |反义词: None |词性: noun
