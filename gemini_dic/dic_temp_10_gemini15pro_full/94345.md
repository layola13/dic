impingers:/ɪmˈpɪndʒərz/| n.|撞击器，冲击器，影响者|Something that impinges strikes or has an effect on something else.  It often refers to something that encroaches or intrudes upon something else, often negatively. This can be a physical impact, or a more abstract impact like an idea or feeling.|The cold spray from the ocean impinged on my face.  海上的冷浪冲击着我的脸。|Synonyms: encroachers, intruders, infringers,  affectors|Antonyms: protectors, guardians|noun

impinges:/ɪmˈpɪndʒɪz/| v.|撞击，冲击，影响|To impinge on something means to have an effect or impact on it, often in a negative way. It suggests an intrusion or encroachment.|The noise of the construction impinged on our ability to concentrate. 建筑噪音影响了我们集中注意力的能力。|Synonyms:  encroaches, intrudes, affects, influences, infringes|Antonyms: protects, guards, aids, assists|verb
