outlet-river:/ˈaʊtlɛt ˈrɪvər/| n.|支流，出水河流|A river that serves as the outlet of a lake or other body of water.|The Mississippi River is the outlet-river for many smaller rivers and streams. 密西西比河是许多较小河流和溪流的出水河流。|Synonym: effluent stream, outflow river|Antonym: tributary, source river|noun
