horticultural:/ˌhɔːrtɪˈkʌltʃərəl/
adjective
园艺的
Relating to the art or practice of garden cultivation and management.
She studied horticultural techniques at the university.  她在大学学习了园艺技术。
Horticultural crops like fruits and vegetables are essential for a healthy diet.  水果和蔬菜等园艺作物对健康饮食至关重要。
近义词: gardening, agricultural
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *horticultura*, from hortus (“garden”) + cultura (“cultivation”).
