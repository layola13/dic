attributively:/əˈtrɪbjətɪvli/| adv.|定语地，属性地|Used to describe the way an adjective modifies a noun directly, without a linking verb.|He used the word "red" attributively, describing the "red car." 他用“红色”作为定语，描述了那辆“红色的车”。|Synonym:  descriptively|Antonym: predicatively|詞性: adverb
