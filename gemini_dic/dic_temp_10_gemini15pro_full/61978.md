v.|使能够，使成为可能|His wealth enables him to live a luxurious life. 他的财富使他能够过上奢侈的生活。|例句：The new technology enables doctors to perform complex surgeries. 新技术使医生能够进行复杂的手术。|近义词：allows, permits, facilitates, empowers|反义词：prevents, hinders, restricts|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *enablen, from Old French enabler, from en- (“in, into”) + able (“able, capable”). Equivalent to en- +‎ able.
