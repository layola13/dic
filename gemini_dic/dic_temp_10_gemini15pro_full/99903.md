internationalism:/ˌɪntərˈnæʃənəlɪzəm/
n.|国际主义|The principle of cooperation among nations, for the promotion of their common good, sometimes as contrasted with nationalism or patriotism.|例句：The organization promotes internationalism and cross-cultural understanding.  这个组织提倡国际主义和跨文化理解。

例句：Internationalism is a growing force in the modern world. 国际主义在现代世界中是一股不断壮大的力量。


近义词：globalism, cosmopolitanism, multilateralism
反义词：nationalism, isolationism
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From international + -ism.  International itself comes from inter- (between) + national (pertaining to a nation).
