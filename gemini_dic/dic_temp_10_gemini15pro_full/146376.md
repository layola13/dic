overfloods:/ˌoʊvərˈflʌdz/| v. | 泛滥，淹没；充满，过多 |  To flood or cover something completely, often excessively.  It can also refer to being overwhelmed with something, like emotions or information.| The river overfloods its banks every spring.  每年春天，河水都会泛滥成灾。| The market is overflooded with cheap imports.  市场上充斥着廉价的进口商品。|近义词：inundates, overwhelms, deluges|反义词: drains, dries|词性:动词

overfloods:/ˌoʊvərˈflʌdz/| n. | 泛滥，洪水；过剩，过量 | Instances of flooding or an excessive amount of something. | The overfloods caused significant damage to the town. 洪水给城镇造成了重大损失。| There's an overfloods of information available online. 网上充斥着过量的信息。|近义词：floods, deluges, excesses|反义词: shortages, droughts|词性:名词
