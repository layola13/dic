bipedally:/baɪˈpɛdəli/
adverb
双足地；用两足
Using two feet; on two feet.
Humans move bipedally. 人类双足行走。
Gorillas can occasionally walk bipedally. 大猩猩偶尔可以双足行走。
近义词:on two feet
反义词:quadrupedally
词性:adverb

Etymology:
From bi- (two) + pedal (of or relating to the foot) + -ly (adverbial suffix). Ultimately from Latin bi- (two) and ped- (foot).
