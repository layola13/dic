occupations:/ˌɑːkjəˈpeɪʃənz/ | n. |职业，工作|A person's job or profession; the activity in which one engages.|Many different occupations are represented at the conference. 许多不同的职业都在会议上有所体现。| professions, jobs, careers, vocations | unemployment, inactivity | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *occupacioun*, from Old French *occupation*, from Latin *occupātiō*, from *occupāre* (“to seize, take possession of, occupy”), from *ob-* (“towards, against”) + *capiō* (“I take, seize”).
