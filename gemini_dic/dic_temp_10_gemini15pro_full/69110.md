favelas:/fəˈvɛləz/
n.
贫民窟
A low-income, informal urban area in Brazil characterized by substandard housing and often lacking basic services.
The documentary explored the complex social dynamics within the favelas of Rio de Janeiro.  这部纪录片探讨了里约热内卢贫民窟复杂的社会动态。
slums, shantytowns
gated communities, suburbs
noun
