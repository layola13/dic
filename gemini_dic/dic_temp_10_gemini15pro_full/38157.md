comprisal: /kəmˈpraɪzəl/ | n. | 报复；报复行动 | an act of retaliation, especially in war; something unpleasant done to someone because of something unpleasant that they have done |  The bombing was a reprisal for the earlier attack.  这次爆炸是对先前袭击的报复。| synonyms: retaliation, revenge, retribution, vengeance | antonyms: forgiveness, pardon | noun

comprisal: /kəmˈpraɪzəl/ | n. | (通常复数)【正式】扣押；没收 | the forcible seizure of something, especially as compensation for something else taken or damaged | They threatened reprisals against UN personnel. 他们威胁要报复联合国人员。 | synonyms: seizure, confiscation, expropriation, appropriation | antonyms: restitution, return | noun
