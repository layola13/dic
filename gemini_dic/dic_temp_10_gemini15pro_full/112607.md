lovu:/ˈloʊvuː/
noun
Lovu (Fijian)
love, affection
例句：Au lomani iko. (Fijian)  I love you.
例句：Sa rui levu na lovu. (Fijian) There is so much love.
近义词：affection, fondness, care
反义词：hate, dislike, animosity
词性：n.
