inosite:/ˈɪnəˌsaɪt/ /ˈɪnoʊˌsaɪt/| n. |肌醇|A cyclic alcohol often found in plants and animals, important for various cellular functions.  It's often referred to as a vitamin-like substance.|Inositol plays a crucial role in cell signaling. 肌醇在细胞信号传导中起着至关重要的作用。|Synonyms: cyclohexitol, |Antonyms: (No direct antonym exists)|noun
