Bollandists:/ˈbɒləndɪsts/
n.
波兰迪斯特派学者；圣徒传学者
A group of Jesuit scholars who continued the work of Jean Bolland in compiling and critically editing the Acta Sanctorum (Acts of the Saints).
The Bollandists have spent centuries researching the lives of saints. 波兰迪斯特派学者花费了几个世纪研究圣徒的生平。
Synonyms: hagiographers,  church historians
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, possibly) secular historians
词性: noun


Bollandist:/ˈbɒləndɪst/
adj.
波兰迪斯特派的
Relating to the Bollandists or their work.
His Bollandist approach to the study of saints was meticulous. 他以波兰迪斯特派的方式研究圣徒，一丝不苟。
Synonyms: hagiographical
Antonyms: (No direct antonym)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
Named after Jean Bolland (1596-1665), a Jesuit hagiographer from Belgium who began the Acta Sanctorum project.
