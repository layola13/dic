bogusness:/ˈboʊɡəsnəs/
n.
虚假,伪造,欺骗
The quality or state of being bogus; spuriousness; falsity.
The lawyer questioned the bogusness of the evidence.  律师质疑证据的虚假性。
synonyms:  falsity, spuriousness, fraudulence, sham, artificiality, phoniness
antonyms: genuineness, authenticity, validity, reality
词性: 名词
