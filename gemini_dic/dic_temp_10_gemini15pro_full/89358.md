homomorphous:/ˌhɒməˈmɔːfəs/ /ˌhoʊməˈmɔːrfəs/| adj.|同形的，同晶形的|Having the same or similar form or structure, especially of crystals.|The two substances are homomorphous, making it difficult to distinguish them visually.|近义词：isomorphous, analogous, similar|反义词：heteromorphous, dissimilar|adjective
