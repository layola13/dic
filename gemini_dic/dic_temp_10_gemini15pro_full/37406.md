commercialistic:/kəˈmɜːrʃəlˈɪstɪk/
adjective
商业化的，唯利是图的
Characterized by or emphasizing commerce or financial gain, often at the expense of artistic or other values.
例句：The Christmas season has become increasingly commercialistic.  圣诞节越来越商业化了。

例句：Many people find the commercialistic aspects of modern sports off-putting. 很多人觉得现代体育的商业化令人反感。

近义词：mercenary, materialistic, profit-oriented
反义词：altruistic, non-profit, charitable
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From commercial + -istic.  Commercial comes from Late Latin commercium "commerce, trade," from com- "together" + merx (genitive mercis) "merchandise."  The suffix -istic forms adjectives meaning "relating to" or "characteristic of."
