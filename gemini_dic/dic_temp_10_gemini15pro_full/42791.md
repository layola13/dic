coupledom: /ˈkʌpəldəm/
n.
the state of being a couple; the time when two people are a couple
the state or period of being in a romantic relationship.
例句：They celebrated a decade of coupledom. 他们庆祝了十年的伴侣关系。

例句：After years of single life, she was finally ready to embrace coupledom. 在多年的单身生活之后，她终于准备好接受伴侣关系了。

近义词：relationship, partnership, togetherness
反义词：singlehood, being single
词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
couple + -dom.  "Couple" comes from Old French, ultimately from Latin *copula, meaning "link" or "tie". "-dom" is an Old English suffix denoting state or condition, realm, or dignity.
