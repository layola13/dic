hydrocorallia:/ˌhaɪdrəʊkəˈreɪliə/ | n. | 水螅珊瑚 | An order of colonial marine invertebrates, often mistaken for true corals, characterized by a calcareous skeleton and the presence of both feeding polyps and defensive polyps. |  The hydrocorallia formed a beautiful reef structure. 水螅珊瑚形成了美丽的礁石结构。 | The divers observed a variety of hydrocorallia on the shipwreck. 潜水员在沉船上观察到各种水螅珊瑚。 | 近义词: fire coral (specifically Millepora species) | 反义词: None | noun
