caterpillar:/ˈkætərˌpɪlər/| n. |毛毛虫，履带|The larva of a butterfly or moth.  A caterpillar eats leaves and eventually turns into a pupa, then a butterfly or moth.|例句：The caterpillar munched on a leaf. 毛毛虫啃食着一片叶子。

|例句：The tank moved slowly on its caterpillars. 坦克在履带上缓慢移动。|近义词：larva, worm|反义词:butterfly, moth|Noun
