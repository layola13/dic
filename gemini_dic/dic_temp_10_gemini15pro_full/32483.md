chindits: /ˈtʃɪndɪts/ | n. |钦迪特部队|A member of a specially trained force of Burmese and British guerrillas operating in Burma during World War II.|The Chindits, under the command of Orde Wingate, carried out daring raids behind Japanese lines. 钦迪特部队在奥德·温盖特的指挥下，在日军后方进行了大胆的突袭。|Synonyms: guerrillas, commandos, raiders|Antonyms: regular army, conventional forces|Noun
