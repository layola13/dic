commandant:/ˌkɒmənˈdɑːnt/ /ˌkɑːmənˈdænt/ | n. | 司令官；指挥官； commandant | A commandant is an officer who is in charge of a military organization or establishment. | The commandant of the marine base addressed the recruits.  海军基地的司令官向新兵讲话。

The commandant ordered the evacuation of the base. 司令员下令撤离基地。 | synonyms: commander, chief, officer, leader, director, superintendent, head, boss | antonyms: subordinate, follower | noun
