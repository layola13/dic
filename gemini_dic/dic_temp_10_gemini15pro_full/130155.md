non-representative:/ˌnɒnˌreprɪˈzentətɪv/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˌreprɪˈzentətɪv/| adj.|非代表性的，不典型的|Not typical of a particular group or thing; not accurately reflecting a larger group or situation.| This small survey is non-representative of national opinion.  这项小型调查不具有全国代表性。|  The sample group was non-representative of the population as a whole. 样本组不代表总体人口。|近义词：atypical, uncharacteristic, biased, skewed|反义词: representative, typical, characteristic|词性: 形容词

non-representative:/ˌnɒnˌreprɪˈzentətɪv/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˌreprɪˈzentətɪv/| n.|非代表性的人或事物|A person or thing that is not typical of a particular group or thing.|The study's findings were questioned because the participants were non-representatives of the target demographic. 这项研究的发现受到了质疑，因为参与者并非目标人群的代表。|The committee included a few non-representatives who had little understanding of the issues. 委员会中包括了一些不了解问题的人，他们并非真正意义上的代表。|近义词：anomaly, outlier|反义词: representative, exemplar|词性: 名词
