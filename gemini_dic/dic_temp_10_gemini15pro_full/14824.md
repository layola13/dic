axillary: /ˈæksɪləri/ (US), /ækˈsɪləri/ (UK)| adj. n.|腋窝的；腋生的；辅助的；腋窝；腋芽|Relating to the armpit, or an armpit; supplementary or supporting something else. |例句：The axillary artery supplies blood to the arm. 腋动脉向手臂供血。

|例句：The axillary buds of the plant developed into new branches. 植物的腋芽发育成新的枝条。|近义词：underarm, lateral, secondary|反义词：central, primary|词性：adjective, noun


axillars:  /ˈæksɪlɚz/ (US), /ækˈsɪlɚz/ (UK) | n. |腋窝（复数）; 腋毛|Plural form of axillary (noun), referring to the armpits.|例句：He shaved his axillars. 他剃了腋毛。

|例句：The sweat glands in the axillars produce a characteristic odor. 腋窝的汗腺会产生一种特殊的气味。|近义词：underarms|反义词：N/A|词性：noun


词源:  From Late Latin *axillaris, from Latin axilla (“armpit”).
