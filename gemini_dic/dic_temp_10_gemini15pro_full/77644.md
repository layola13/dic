gelatinization:/dʒəˌlætɪnəˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |糊化，胶凝化 |The process of converting starch into a gelatinous form by heating it with water. This causes the starch granules to swell and burst, resulting in a thickened, viscous solution.| The gelatinization of the starch is crucial in making a smooth gravy.  淀粉的糊化对于制作顺滑的肉汁至关重要。|近义词：gelling, thickening |反义词: liquefaction |词性: noun

gelatinize:/dʒəˈlætɪnaɪz/ | v. |糊化，胶凝 |To convert (starch) into a gelatinous form by heating with water.| The cornstarch gelatinized when heated with the milk, creating a pudding-like consistency.  玉米淀粉在与牛奶加热时糊化，形成了布丁状的稠度。|近义词：gel, thicken |反义词: liquefy |词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French gélatinisation, from gélatine ("gelatin") + -isation.  Ultimately from Latin gelātus ("frozen").
