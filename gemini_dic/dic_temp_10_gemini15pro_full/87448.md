hesitators:/ˈhezɪˌteɪtərz/
n.
犹豫者，迟疑者
People who hesitate or are slow to act or decide.
The hesitators stood back while the bolder ones rushed forward.  犹豫不决的人往后退，而胆大的人则向前冲。
近义词：waverers, doubters, procrastinators
反义词：deciders, actors, doers
词性：名词

hesitate:/ˈhezɪteɪt/
v.
犹豫，迟疑
To pause before saying or doing something, especially through uncertainty or unwillingness.
She hesitated before answering the question. 她犹豫了一下才回答问题。
近义词：pause, waver, falter
反义词：decide, act, proceed
词性：动词
