line-printers:/ˈlaɪn prɪntərz/| n. |行式打印机|A type of high-speed impact printer that prints an entire line of text at once.  They were commonly used in the past for printing large volumes of data. |The company used line-printers to produce reports. 公司使用行式打印机制作报告。|The old line-printers were incredibly noisy. 旧的行式打印机噪音非常大。|Synonyms: impact printer, printer|Antonyms: laser printer, inkjet printer|noun
