avulse: /əˈvʌls/ | vt. | 撕脱，扯下 | To tear away or separate forcibly. | The dentist avulsed the decayed tooth. 牙医拔掉了那颗蛀牙。| The storm avulsed the branches from the tree.  暴风雨把树枝从树上扯了下来。| 近义词: tear, rip, extract, detach | 反义词: attach, connect, join | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *avulsus*, past participle of *avellere* "to tear away," from *a-* "away" + *vellere* "to pluck, tear."
