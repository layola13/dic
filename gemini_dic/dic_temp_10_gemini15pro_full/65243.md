etudes:/ˈeɪtjuːdz/ (US), /ˈeɪtjuːdz/ (UK)| n. |练习曲；习作；(尤指)音乐练习曲|A short musical composition, typically for one instrument, designed as an exercise to improve the player's technique.| Chopin's etudes are known for their technical difficulty. 肖邦的练习曲以其技巧难度而闻名。| synonyms: exercises, studies, pieces, compositions | antonyms: performances, concerts, improvisations | noun

etude:/ˈeɪtjuːd/ (US), /ˈeɪtjuːd/ (UK)| n. |(尤指对某人或某事的)仔细研究；深入分析|(especially in French) a short literary composition |  He wrote a compelling etude on the life of a forgotten artist. 他写了一篇引人入胜的关于一位被遗忘的艺术家的生平研究。| synonyms: study, analysis, examination, investigation | antonyms: summary, overview, synopsis | noun
