codicillary: /ˌkɒdɪˈsɪləri/ /ˌkɑːdɪˈsɪləri/
adjective
遗嘱附录的；作为遗嘱附录的
Relating to or of the nature of a codicil (an addition or supplement that explains, modifies, or revokes a will or part of one).
The codicillary document added a provision for her favorite charity.  遗嘱附录文件增加了一条关于她最喜欢的慈善机构的条款。
近义词: supplementary, amending, additional
反义词: original, primary
词性: adjective
