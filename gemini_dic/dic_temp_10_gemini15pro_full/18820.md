bertillon:/bɜːrˈtɪljən/ |n.|贝尔蒂永测定法; 贝尔蒂永氏系|A system of criminal identification based on anthropometric measurements.  It was named after Alphonse Bertillon, a French police officer who developed the system in the late 19th century.|The police used the Bertillon system to identify the suspect. 警方使用贝尔蒂永测定法来识别嫌疑人。|Near synonyms: anthropometry, biometrics|Antonyms: None|noun
