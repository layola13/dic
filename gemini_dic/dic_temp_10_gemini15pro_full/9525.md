antisecrecy:/ˌæntiˈsiːkrəsi/ | n. | 反保密，反对保密 | The principle or practice of opposing secrecy, especially in government or business.  It promotes transparency and open access to information. |  The organization champions antisecrecy in government operations.  该组织倡导政府运作的透明化。

The journalist's commitment to antisecrecy led to the exposure of the scandal.  这位记者对反保密的承诺导致了丑闻的曝光。 |  transparency, openness,  public access, disclosure | secrecy, confidentiality, concealment | noun
