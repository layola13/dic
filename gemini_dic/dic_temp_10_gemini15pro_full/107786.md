lappaceous:/ləˈpeɪʃəs/
adjective
覆有粗糙碎片的；粗糙的；石头多的
covered with or resembling small, rough, loose fragments of stone.
The lappaceous soil made it difficult to grow crops. 这片满是碎石的土壤使得种植作物很困难。
rough, stony, gritty
smooth, fine
