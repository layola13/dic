amirate:/ˈemɪrət/ | n. |酋长国；酋长管辖区|A territory ruled by an emir. |The United Arab Emirates is a federation of seven emirates. 阿拉伯联合酋长国是由七个酋长国组成的联邦。|  The small amirate declared independence.  这个小酋长国宣布独立。| synonym: sheikdom, principality | antonym: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *émirat*, from Arabic *ʾimārah*  (إمارة) meaning "principality, emirate", from *ʾamīr* (أمير) meaning "commander, prince, emir". Ultimately from the Arabic root *ʾ-m-r* (أمر) meaning "to command".
