hyperlinks:/ˈhaɪpərˌlɪŋks/| n.|超链接|A link from a hypertext file or document to another location or file, typically activated by clicking on a highlighted word or image on the screen.|例句：This webpage contains many hyperlinks to other websites.  这个网页包含许多到其他网站的超链接。

例句：Clicking on the hyperlink will take you to the relevant page. 点击超链接会将您带到相关页面。|近义词：link, connection, URL|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
