paan:/pɑːn/ | n. | 槟榔; 嚼槟榔 | A preparation of betel leaf containing areca nut and sometimes tobacco and other ingredients, which is chewed as a stimulant. |  I saw a street vendor selling paan. 我看到一个街头小贩在卖槟榔。 | He offered me some paan, but I declined. 他给了我一些槟榔，但我拒绝了。 | 近义词: betel quid | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

词源: From Hindi पान (pān), from Sanskrit पर्ण (parṇa, “leaf”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *pornóm (“leaf, feather”).
