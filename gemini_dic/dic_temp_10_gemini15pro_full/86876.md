hemipeptone:/ˌhɛmiˈpɛptəʊn/ (UK) /ˌhɛmiˈpɛptoʊn/ (US)| n. |半蛋白胨|A mixture of polypeptides and amino acids formed by partial hydrolysis of protein.  It is commonly used in culture media for microorganisms.|The bacteria thrived in the hemipeptone broth.|Synonym: peptone, protein hydrolysate|Antonym: protein|noun
