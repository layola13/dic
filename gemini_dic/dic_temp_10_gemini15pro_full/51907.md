dhoorra:/ˈdʊərə/| n. |高粱|A type of sorghum, a grain crop grown in arid regions.|Example: Dhoorra is a staple food in many parts of Africa. 高粱是非洲许多地区的主食。|Example:  The farmers planted dhoorra to withstand the drought. 农民们种植高粱以抵御干旱。|近义词：sorghum, milo|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From Hindi दुर्रा (durrā), ultimately from Arabic ذرة (ḏuratun, “grain, seed”).
