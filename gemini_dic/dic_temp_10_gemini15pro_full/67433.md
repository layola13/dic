extinguishable:/ɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbl/
adjective
可熄灭的; 可扑灭的
able to be extinguished.
The fire was small and easily extinguishable. 这场火很小，很容易扑灭。
近义词:quenchable, suppressible
反义词: inextinguishable, unquenchable
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin extinguishibilis, from Latin exstinguere (to extinguish) + -ibilis (capable of being).  The root "stinguere" means "to quench" or "to put out."
