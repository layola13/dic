declivitous:/dɪˈklɪvɪtəs/
adjective
陡峭的；倾斜的
sloping downward; steep
The hikers struggled up the declivitous path.  徒步旅行者艰难地爬上陡峭的小路。
The declivitous terrain made the climb challenging.  陡峭的地形使攀登变得具有挑战性。
近义词: steep, precipitous, sloping
反义词: gentle, gradual, flat
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *dēclīvis, from dē- + clīvus (“slope”).
