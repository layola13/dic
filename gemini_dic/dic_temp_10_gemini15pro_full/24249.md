briefed:/briːft/| v.|已向…介绍情况，已向…简要汇报| to give someone detailed instructions or information about something|例句：The president was briefed on the latest developments. 总统听取了最新的情况汇报。|例句：She briefed the team on the project goals. 她向团队简要介绍了项目目标。|近义词：informed, updated, filled in|反义词: uninformed, ignorant|词性:动词

词源: From the noun "brief" (meaning a concise statement or summary), which ultimately comes from the Latin "brevis" meaning "short."
