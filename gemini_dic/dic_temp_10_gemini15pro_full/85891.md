header's:/ˈhɛdərz/ | n. | 标题; 页眉; 导语; 主要的; 最好的; |Headers are titles or short descriptions that appear at the top of a section of text, a page, or a table. | Example: The headers on this website are clearly labeled. 这个网站上的标题都清楚地标注了。

Example: She always reads the headers before deciding to read the whole article. 她总是在决定阅读整篇文章之前先看标题。 | Synonyms: titles, headings, captions | Antonyms: footers, conclusions | Noun
