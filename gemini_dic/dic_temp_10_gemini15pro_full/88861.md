hoidenish:/ˈhɔɪdənɪʃ/| adj.|像野丫头的；粗野的；不端庄的|Resembling or characteristic of a hoyden; boisterous and bold in a traditionally unfeminine way.|She had a hoidenish laugh that echoed through the halls. 她那粗野的笑声在走廊里回荡。|例句：Her hoidenish behavior often shocked the more conservative members of society.  她那粗野的行为经常让社会上较为保守的人感到震惊。


例句：Despite her elegant attire, she retained a certain hoidenish air. 尽管她衣着优雅，但她仍然保留着某种粗野的气质。


近义词：tomboyish, boisterous, unladylike, rambunctious, unruly|反义词:ladylike, demure, refined, graceful, well-behaved|词性:adjective


词源:From hoyden + -ish.  Hoyden itself is of uncertain origin, possibly from Dutch "heide" meaning "heath" suggesting a wild, rural girl.
