enveloper:/ɪnˈvɛləpər/ | n. | 包封者，包装者 |  A person or thing that envelops something.  Someone or something that wraps or covers something else. | The enveloper carefully sealed the letter. 包封者仔细地封好了信。 | synonyms: wrapper, coverer | antonyms: uncoverer, revealer | noun


envelop:/ɪnˈvɛləp/ | v. | 包围，笼罩 | To wrap up, cover, or surround completely. |  The fog enveloped the city. 雾笼罩着城市。 | synonyms: surround, enclose, encase | antonyms: expose, uncover, reveal | verb
