amphibiously:/æmˈfɪbiəsli/ /amˈfɪbiəsli/ | adv. |两栖地；水陆两用地 | In a manner that is suitable for both land and water; by means of both land and water. | The Marines landed amphibiously. 海军陆战队水陆两栖登陆。| 近义词: both on land and in water | 反义词: terrestrially, aquatically | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *amphibius, from Ancient Greek ἀμφίβιος (amphíbios, “living a double life”), from ἀμφί (amphí, “on both sides”) + βίος (bíos, “life”).
