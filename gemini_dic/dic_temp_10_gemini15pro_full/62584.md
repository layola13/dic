enervous:/ɪˈnɜːrvəs/| adj.|紧张不安的，焦虑的|Feeling or showing nervousness; easily agitated or alarmed.|例句：She was enervous about her upcoming exam. 她对即将到来的考试感到紧张不安。|例句：He felt enervous before his big presentation. 在重要的演讲之前，他感到很紧张。|近义词：nervous, anxious, apprehensive, uneasy|反义词：calm, composed, relaxed|词性:形容词


词源:  A blend of "energetic" and "nervous".  While not a standard English word, it is used informally to describe a state of nervous energy.
