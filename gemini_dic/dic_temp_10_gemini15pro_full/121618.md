mispackage:/ˌmɪsˈpækɪdʒ/
mispackage: v. |错误包装，包装错误|To package incorrectly, such as putting the wrong items in a package or using the wrong packaging materials.|The factory mispackaged several orders, leading to customer complaints. 工厂错误包装了几个订单，导致客户投诉。|mislabel, miswrap|package correctly|verb
mispackage:/ˌmɪsˈpækɪdʒ/
mispackage: n. |包装错误的物品|An item that has been packaged incorrectly.|The mispackage was returned to the sender.  包装错误的物品被退回给寄件人。|mislabeled item, miswrapped item|correctly packaged item|noun
