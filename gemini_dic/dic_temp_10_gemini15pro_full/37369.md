commensurately:/kəˈmɛnʃərətli/
adverb
相应地，相称地
in a corresponding or proportionate manner; to a commensurate degree.
Her salary is commensurate with her experience. 她的薪水与她的经验相称。
近义词:proportionately, correspondingly, appropriately, suitably
反义词:disproportionately, inadequately, insufficiently
词性:副词

commensurate:/kəˈmɛnʃərət/
adjective
相称的，相当的
corresponding in size or degree; in proportion.
The punishment should be commensurate with the crime. 惩罚应该与罪行相称。
近义词:proportional, equivalent, comparable, corresponding
反义词:disproportionate, unequal, inadequate
词性:形容词


词源:
From Late Latin commensuratus, past participle of commensurare "to measure together," from com- "together" + mensurare "to measure," from mensura "measure."
