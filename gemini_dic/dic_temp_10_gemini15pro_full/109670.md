leucon:/ˈljuːkɒn/ | n. |亮骨海綿；白骨海綿 |A type of sponge with a complex canal system.  A sponge of the class Calcarea, having a skeleton composed of calcareous spicules.| The leucon is the most complex sponge body type.  这种白骨海绵是最复杂的海绵体类型。|Synonym:  leuconoid | Antonym: ascon, sycon | noun
