outpractice:/ˌaʊtˈpræktɪs/| v.|以练习胜过，在实践中超过|To surpass or defeat someone through greater practice or experience.|He outpracticed his rivals and won the competition. 他通过比对手更多的练习赢得了比赛。|近义词：outperform, surpass, excel, outdo|反义词：underperform|词性：动词

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning exceeding or surpassing) + "practice" (noun meaning repeated exercise or performance of an activity)
