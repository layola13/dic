de-stressed:/diːˈstrɛst/
adjective
无压力的；放松的
Free from stress; relaxed.
After a long vacation, I felt completely de-stressed.  假期结束后，我感到完全放松了。
synonyms: relaxed, calm, composed, serene
antonyms: stressed, anxious, tense
词性: 形容词


de-stressed:/diːˈstrɛst/
verb
缓解压力
To relieve or reduce stress.
I de-stressed by listening to music. 我通过听音乐来缓解压力。
synonyms: relax, unwind, chill out
antonyms: stress, tense up, worry
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the prefix "de-" (meaning "remove" or "reverse") + "stressed" (past participle of the verb "stress").
