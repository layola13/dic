noctuid:/ˈnɒktʃuɪd/ (US) /ˈnɒktjuːɪd/ (UK)| n. adj.|夜蛾科昆虫；夜蛾科的|A moth of the family Noctuidae, typically of dull colouring and active at night.|例句：The researcher specialized in the study of noctuids.  这位研究员专门研究夜蛾科昆虫。

例句：The noctuid moth is attracted to light.  这种夜蛾会被光吸引。|近义词：moth, owlet moth|反义词: N/A|词性: noun, adjective


Etymology: From Latin *noctu-, stem of *nox "night" + -id.
