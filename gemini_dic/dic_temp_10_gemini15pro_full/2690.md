adrip:/əˈdrɪp/| adj.|湿淋淋的，滴着液体的|Covered or soaked with liquid; dripping.|The dog came in from the rain adrip with water.  这只狗淋雨进来，浑身湿透。|近义词：dripping, sopping, saturated|反义词:dry|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From a- (on, in, at) + drip (to fall in drops).  源自 a-（在...上，在...里，在...处）+ drip（滴落）。

USAGE->
The faucet was left on, leaving the sink adrip. 水龙头没关，水池里滴着水。

The survivors emerged from the river adrip and shivering. 幸存者从河里出来，浑身湿透，瑟瑟发抖。


PHRASE->
adrip with (something) = 滴着（某种液体）
soaked adrip = 湿透的
