letterbox: /ˈlɛtə(r)ˌbɒks/ (UK) /ˈlɛtərˌbɑːks/ (US)| n.|邮箱，信箱|A slot in a door or wall through which mail is delivered.|The postman delivered a package too big for the letterbox. 邮递员送来一个太大而放不进邮箱的包裹。|Synonyms: mailbox (US), postbox (UK)|Antonyms: None|noun

letterbox: /ˈlɛtə(r)ˌbɒks/ (UK) /ˈlɛtərˌbɑːks/ (US)| adj.|信箱式的，电影宽银幕的（指上下加黑边的格式）|Relating to a widescreen movie format with black bars at the top and bottom of the screen.|The film was shown in letterbox format to preserve the original aspect ratio.  这部电影以信箱格式放映，以保持原始的宽高比。|Synonyms: widescreen (for the film format)|Antonyms: fullscreen|adjective
