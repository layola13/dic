auszug:/ˈaʊstsuːk/| n.|摘录，摘要，引文，节选；搬出，迁出|An excerpt or selection from a larger work, often a book or musical composition. Also, the act of moving out of a residence.|例句：This auszug from the novel perfectly captures the author's style. 这部小说的摘录完美地体现了作者的风格。

例句：The auszug from Beethoven's Fifth Symphony was breathtaking. 贝多芬第五交响曲的节选令人叹为观止。|近义词：excerpt, extract, selection, passage, quotation, moving out, departure|反义词:inclusion, entry, arrival|词性:noun
