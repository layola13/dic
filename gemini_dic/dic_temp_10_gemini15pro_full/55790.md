divertible:/daɪˈvɜːrtəbl/ /dɪˈvɜːrtəbl/
adjective
可转移的；可娱乐的
able to be diverted or entertained.
例句：The river is divertible for irrigation purposes. 这条河可引水灌溉。

例句：The funds are divertible to other projects. 这些资金可以转用于其他项目。

近义词：deflectable, redirectable, alterable,  amusable, entertaining
反义词：fixed, inflexible, unchangeable, boring, tedious
词性：adjective
