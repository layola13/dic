dunelike:/ˈdjuːnlaɪk/| adj.|像沙丘的，沙丘似的|Resembling or characteristic of a dune.  Having the appearance or form of a dune.|The wind sculpted the snow into dunelike drifts. 风把雪雕刻成像沙丘一样的雪堆。|近义词：sandy, undulating, desertlike|反义词:flat, level|词性:形容词

Etymology:  From "dune" + "-like."  "Dune" comes from Middle Dutch "dūne," ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*dūnoz." "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
