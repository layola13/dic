ensuingly:/ɪnˈsuːɪŋli/
adverb
随之而来地，接着
Following as a consequence; subsequently.
He lost his job, and ensuingly, his house. 他失去了工作，随之而来的是，他也失去了房子。
The company went bankrupt, and ensuingly, many people lost their jobs. 公司破产了，随之而来的是，许多人失去了工作。
近义词: subsequently, consequently, thereafter, accordingly
反义词: previously, beforehand, earlier
