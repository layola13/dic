ethylic: /ɪˈθɪlɪk/ /ɛˈθɪlɪk/
adjective
乙基的；酒精的
Of or relating to ethyl alcohol; alcoholic.
Ethyl alcohol is the basis of ethylic beverages. 乙醇是酒精饮料的基础。
The ethylic content of the beverage was high. 这种饮料的酒精含量很高。
近义词: alcoholic, spirituous
反义词: non-alcoholic
词性: adjective
词源: From ethyl + -ic.  Ethyl comes from ether (because it was originally considered to be a "son of ether") + -yl (chemical suffix).
