manic-depressive:/ˌmænɪk dɪˈprɛsɪv/
adjective|躁狂抑郁的|Relating to or affected by manic depression, a mental illness characterized by extreme mood swings between periods of mania and depression.|He was diagnosed with manic-depressive illness.|近义词：bipolar|反义词：stable|词性: adjective

manic-depressive:/ˌmænɪk dɪˈprɛsɪv/
noun|躁狂抑郁症患者|A person affected with manic depression.|She is a manic-depressive and needs medication to manage her moods.|近义词：bipolar person|反义词：None|词性: noun
