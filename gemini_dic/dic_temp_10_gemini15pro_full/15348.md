backstitched:/ˌbækˈstɪtʃt/| v. adj.|回针缝，回针的|To sew backward over a seam to reinforce it or make it more secure; also used as an adjective to describe something sewn in this manner.|She backstitched the seam for extra strength. 她回针缝合了接缝以增加强度。|近义词：reinforced, secured, double-stitched|反义词:unsecured|verb/adjective

backstitched:/ˌbækˈstɪtʃt/| adj.|回针缝的|Sewn with backstitches, making it stronger and more durable.|The backstitched seam held up well under pressure. 回针缝合的接缝在压力下保持良好。|近义词：reinforced, secured, double-stitched|反义词:unsecured|adjective
