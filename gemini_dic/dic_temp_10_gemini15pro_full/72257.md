floroon:/flɔːˈruːn/
n.|花廊，阳台; (尤指) 剧院最高楼座|A gallery or balcony, especially the highest gallery in a theater.|The actors could barely see the audience in the floroon.|近义词:balcony, gallery, loge|反义词:stage, orchestra pit|词性:名词

Etymology:
From French *fleuron*, diminutive of *fleur* ("flower"), referring to the decorative elements often found on these upper levels.  The shift in meaning from "decoration" to "upper level" likely occurred due to the ornate nature of theater balconies.
