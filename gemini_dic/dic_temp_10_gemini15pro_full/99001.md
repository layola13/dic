interact:/ˌɪntərˈækt/ | vi. | 互动，相互作用，互相影响 | to communicate or react to each other | The two chemicals interact to form a new compound. 这两种化学物质相互作用形成一种新的化合物。 |  | synonyms: communicate, connect, react, mingle | antonyms: isolate, separate | verb
interact:/ˌɪntərˈækt/ | vi. | 交流，交往 | to talk or do things with other people | She interacts well with other children. 她和其他的孩子相处得很好。 | | synonyms: socialize, associate, fraternize | antonyms: avoid, shun | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *interāctus, past participle of interāgō (“I intervene, transact”), from inter (“between, among”) + āgō (“I do, drive, lead, act”).
