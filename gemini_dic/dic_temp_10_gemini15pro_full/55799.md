diverting:/daɪˈvɜːrtɪŋ/ | adj. | 有趣的，娱乐的；转移注意力的 | Causing amusement or entertainment; distracting. |  The magician's performance was diverting and kept the children entertained. 魔术师的表演很有趣，让孩子们很开心。

The interesting conversation was diverting and made me forget about my worries.  有趣的谈话分散了我的注意力，让我忘记了烦恼。

| 近义词：amusing, entertaining, engaging, distracting | 反义词：boring, tedious, dull, monotonous | 词性: 形容词
