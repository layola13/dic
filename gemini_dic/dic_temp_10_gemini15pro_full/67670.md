extruding:/ɪkˈstruːdɪŋ/
verb
挤压，推出，压出
to shape (metal, plastic, etc.) by forcing it through a die
例句：
The machine is extruding plastic pipes.
这台机器正在挤压塑料管。

The volcano was extruding lava.
火山正在喷出熔岩。

近义词：
forcing, pushing, ejecting, squeezing
反义词：
injecting, withdrawing, pulling

词性
v.

词源
From Latin extrūdō (“I thrust or push out”), from ex (“out”) + trūdō (“I thrust, push”).
