doa:/ˈdoʊə/ (US), /ˈdəʊə/ (UK)| n.|祈祷，恳求，祈愿|A prayer or invocation, especially in Islam.|Example: Muslims offer doa five times a day. 穆斯林每天祈祷五次。|Example:  The imam led the doa for the community. 伊玛目带领社区祈祷。|Synonym: prayer, supplication, invocation|Antonym: blasphemy, curse|noun
