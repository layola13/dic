increasingly:/ɪnˈkriːsɪŋli/ | adv. | 越来越;日益;更加 | more and more; to an increasing degree; progressively |  The world is becoming increasingly interconnected. 世界正变得越来越相互关联。| The problem is becoming increasingly difficult to solve. 这个问题越来越难解决。 | 近义词: progressively, more and more, increasingly | 反义词: decreasingly, less and less | 词性: 副词

词源:
From "increase" + "-ing" + "-ly".  "Increase" comes from Old French "encreiss-" (modern French "accroître"), from Latin "increscere" ("to grow," "increase"), from "in-" ("in," "into," "on," "upon," "against," etc.) + "crescere" ("to grow," "arise," "increase").  "-ing" is a suffix forming present participles and gerunds. "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs from adjectives.
