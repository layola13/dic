honorers:/ˈɑːnərərz/
noun
给予荣誉者; 尊敬者; 敬重者
People who give honor or respect to someone or something.
The honorers of the fallen soldiers gathered at the memorial.  在纪念碑前，悼念阵亡士兵的人们聚集在一起。
The queen was greeted by a crowd of honorers. 女王受到了一群敬重她的人的欢迎。

近义词: admirers, respecters, celebrants
反义词: detractors, critics, disparagers
词性: n. (noun)
