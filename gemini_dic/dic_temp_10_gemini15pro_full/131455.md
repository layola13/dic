noncognates:/ˌnɑːnˈkɑːɡneɪts/ | n. | 非同源词 | Words in different languages that look or sound similar but have different meanings and origins. |  The English word "bank" (a financial institution) and the French word "banque" (also a financial institution) are cognates, while "bank" (the side of a river) and "banque" are noncognates. 英语单词“bank”（金融机构）和法语单词“banque”（也是金融机构）是同源词，而“bank”（河岸）和“banque”是非同源词。|  false friends, false cognates | cognates | noun
