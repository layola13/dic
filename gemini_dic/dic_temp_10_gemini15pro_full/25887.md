burletta:/bərˈletə/ |n.|滑稽歌剧，诙谐小歌剧|A light, comic musical dramatic work, typically with a farcical plot.|The theater presented a lively burletta that had the audience in stitches. 剧院上演了一出活泼的滑稽歌剧，让观众笑得前仰后合。|synonyms: farce, parody, travesty|antonyms: tragedy, drama|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *burletta*, diminutive of *burla* (“joke, mockery”), from Old Italian *burlare* (“to joke, mock”). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *burjoną* (“to lift, raise”).
