chicanery:/ʃɪˈkeɪnəri/
n.
诡计；欺骗；狡辩
The use of trickery to achieve a political, financial, or legal purpose.
例句：The lawyer used legal chicanery to get his client off.  律师运用法律诡辩使其当事人脱罪。

例句：The businessman resorted to chicanery to close the deal.  这个商人为了完成交易 resorted to chicanery.

近义词：trickery, deception, subterfuge, duplicity, guile, fraud, dishonesty
反义词：honesty, integrity, forthrightness
词性：noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *chicanerie*, from *chicaner* "to quibble," of uncertain origin.
