outgush:/ˌaʊtˈɡʌʃ/| n. vi.|涌出，喷出|A sudden and forceful flow of liquid or emotion.|例句：There was an outgush of lava from the volcano. 火山喷出了熔岩。|近义词：gush, eruption, outburst, efflux, surge|反义词:trickle, seep, ooze|词性:名词, 动词
