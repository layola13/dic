betray:/bɪˈtreɪ/ /bɪˈtreɪ/| v. |背叛，出卖，辜负，暴露，流露|To be disloyal to someone or something, or to reveal something that should be kept secret.|He betrayed his country by selling secrets to the enemy. 他向敌人出卖情报，背叛了他的国家。

She betrayed her friend's trust. 她辜负了朋友的信任。

His trembling hands betrayed his nervousness. 他颤抖的双手暴露了他的紧张。|近义词：deceive, mislead, reveal, expose|反义词：support, protect, be loyal to|词性:动词

betrayal:/bɪˈtreɪəl/ /bɪˈtreɪəl/| n. |背叛，出卖，辜负|The act of betraying someone or something.|His betrayal of his friends shocked everyone. 他对朋友的背叛震惊了所有人。

The betrayal of secrets led to the company's downfall.  泄露机密导致了公司的倒闭。|近义词：treachery, disloyalty, deception|反义词：loyalty, fidelity|词性:名词
