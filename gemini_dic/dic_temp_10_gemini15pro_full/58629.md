词源:  "dumb" (unable to speak) + "ass" (donkey; a stupid person).  "Dumb" comes from Old English, while "ass" has a complex history, ultimately deriving from Latin "asinus".  The combination "dumb-ass" emerged in the 20th century as a derogatory term.
