oozy:/ˈuːzi/ | adj. | 渗出的；软泥的；泥泞的 |  (of a thick liquid) flowing slowly and gently; resembling or suggesting ooze | The wound was oozy and infected.  伤口渗出脓液并感染了。| The mud was thick and oozy. 泥浆又厚又黏。 | synonyms: slimy, viscous, sticky, gooey | antonyms: dry, hard, solid | adjective

oozy:/ˈuːzi/ | adj. | （感情等）充分流露的；过于多情的  | Excessively or insincerely emotional or sentimental |  He gave her an oozy, sentimental look. 他给了她一个多情而感伤的眼神。 | Her voice was oozy with false sympathy. 她的声音里充满了虚假的同情。 | synonyms: sentimental, mawkish, saccharine | antonyms: unsentimental, reserved | adjective
