philanthropy:/fɪˈlænθrəpi/| n.|慈善；博爱；仁慈|The act or gift of giving money, property, or work to help people who are poor or in trouble; the belief in or practice of helping other people.|例句：His philanthropy made him a beloved figure in the city. 他的慈善事业让他成为这座城市中受爱戴的人物。

例句：The university received a large donation from a local philanthropy. 这所大学收到了当地一家慈善机构的大笔捐赠。|近义词：charity, benevolence, altruism|反义词：misanthropy, selfishness, greed|词性:noun

Etymology: From Greek philanthrōpia, from philanthrōpos ‘loving mankind,’ from phil- ‘loving’ + anthrōpos ‘man.’
