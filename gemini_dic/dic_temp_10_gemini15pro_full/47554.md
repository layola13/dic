deauthorize:/diːˈɔːθəˌraɪz/ | v. | 取消授权，撤销授权 | To remove authorization or permission previously granted. |  The company deauthorized the third-party app from accessing user data.  该公司取消了第三方应用程序访问用户数据的授权。

The administrator deauthorized his access to the confidential files. 管理员取消了他访问机密文件的权限。 | synonyms: revoke, rescind, cancel, withdraw | antonyms: authorize, empower, enable | verb
