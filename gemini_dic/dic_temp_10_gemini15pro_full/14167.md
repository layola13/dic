autistic:/ɔːˈtɪstɪk/ | adj. | 自闭症的；患自闭症的 | Affected by autism, a developmental disorder that typically appears during the first three years of life and is marked by difficulties in social interaction and communication and by restricted or repetitive patterns of thought and behavior. |  He has an autistic child. 他有一个自闭症的孩子。 | My nephew is autistic. 我的侄子患有自闭症。| synonyms:  neurodivergent, on the spectrum | antonyms: neurotypical | adjective
