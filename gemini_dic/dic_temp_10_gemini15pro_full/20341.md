bismarckian:/bɪzˈmɑːrkiən/
adjective
俾斯麦式的，铁血宰相的
Relating to or characteristic of Otto von Bismarck, especially his policies of Realpolitik, marked by ruthless pragmatism and the use of force.
Bismarckian diplomacy was characterized by a combination of shrewd negotiation and the willingness to use military force. 俾斯麦式的外交手段的特点是精明的谈判和使用武力的意愿。
His policies were seen as distinctly Bismarckian. 他的政策被视为典型的俾斯麦式。
Synonyms: authoritarian, autocratic, dictatorial, iron-fisted, Machiavellian, pragmatic, ruthless
Antonyms: democratic, egalitarian, liberal
