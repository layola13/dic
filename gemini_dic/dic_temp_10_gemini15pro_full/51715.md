devisings:/dɪˈvaɪzɪŋz/
|n.|  设计，发明，策划；（尤指）诡计，计谋
|The act of inventing, contriving, or planning something, especially with ingenuity or cleverness.  Often used in the plural to refer to plans or schemes, especially those that are cunning or deceitful.|
|Her devious devisings ultimately led to her downfall. 她的阴谋诡计最终导致了她的垮台。|
|plans, schemes, machinations, inventions, contrivances|
|None (Direct opposite is difficult, lack of devising could be inaction, inertia)|
|noun|
