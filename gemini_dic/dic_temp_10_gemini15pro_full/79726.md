glues:/ɡluːz/| n. |胶水；粘合剂|An adhesive substance used for sticking things together.|She uses strong glues to mend broken toys. 她用强力胶水粘补破损的玩具。|近义词：adhesives, cements, binders|反义词:solvents, thinners|noun

glues:/ɡluːz/| v. (glue的第三人称单数)|粘合；粘贴|To stick something to something else using glue.|He glues the pieces of wood together. 他把木块粘在一起。|近义词：sticks, adheres, fastens|反义词:separates, detaches|verb
