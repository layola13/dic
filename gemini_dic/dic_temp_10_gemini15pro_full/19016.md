besuited:/bɪˈsuːtɪd/
adjective
穿着西装的
dressed in a suit.
The besuited businessman hurried to his meeting.  穿着西装的商人匆匆赶去开会。
近义词: suited, dressed up
反义词: casually dressed, unsuited
词性: adjective

词源: be- (prefix meaning "to make, cover with") + suited (past participle of suit, from Old French suite "following, retinue," hence "set of clothes")
