aletto:/aˈletto/ | n. | bed, crib (especially for a baby) | A small bed, often used for a child.  It derives from the Italian word "letto" meaning "bed". | The baby slept soundly in his aletto.  婴儿在他的小床上睡得很香。| synonyms: crib, cot, bassinet | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps floor, ground) | noun
