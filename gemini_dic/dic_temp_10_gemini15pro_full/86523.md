hejira:/hɪˈdʒɪərə/ /hɪˈdʒiːrə/
noun
迁徙，出走；移居
An exodus or migration.  Specifically, Muhammad's flight from Mecca to Medina in 622 AD, marking the beginning of the Muslim era.
例句：The family made their hejira from the war-torn country.  这个家庭从饱受战争蹂躏的国家迁徙。

例句：Many people see moving to a new city for work as a kind of personal hejira. 许多人将为了工作搬到一个新的城市视为一种个人的迁徙。
近义词：exodus, migration, flight, escape
反义词：return, immigration, arrival
词性:n.
