dirtily:/ˈdɜːrtɪli/ /ˈdɜːrtiːli/| adv.|肮脏地；下流地；卑鄙地|in a dirty manner; in a way that suggests or involves immoral or dishonest behaviour|He wiped his hands dirtily on his trousers. 他把他的手脏兮兮地在裤子上擦了擦。|The campaign was fought dirtily. 这场竞选进行得十分卑鄙。|近义词：filthily, squalidly, meanly|反义词：cleanly, purely, honorably|词性:副词
词源: From Middle English *dirtili, from Old English *diortilice, equivalent to dirty +‎ -ly.
