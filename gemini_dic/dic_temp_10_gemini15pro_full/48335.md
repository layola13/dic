decriminalizations:/diːˌkrɪmɪnələˈzeɪʃənz/| n.|非刑事化，合法化|The act of making something that was previously a crime no longer a crime.  It involves removing or reducing the criminal penalties associated with certain acts or behaviors.|The decriminalization of marijuana has been a hotly debated topic. 大麻合法化一直是一个备受争议的话题。|Synonyms: legalization, deregulation|Antonyms: criminalization, prohibition|noun
