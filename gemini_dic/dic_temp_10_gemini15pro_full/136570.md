nonsalubrious:/ˌnɒnsəˈluːbriəs/ | /ˌnɑːnsəˈluːbriəs/ | adj. | 不健康的，有害的 | Not conducive to good health; unhealthy. |  The family lived in a nonsalubrious, cramped apartment.  这个家庭住在一间不健康、狭小的公寓里。 |  The swamp gave off a nonsalubrious odor. 沼泽散发出一股难闻的气味。 | 近义词: unhealthy, insalubrious, noxious, detrimental | 反义词: healthy, salubrious, wholesome | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From non- + salubrious, from Latin *salūbriōsus, from salūs (“health”).
