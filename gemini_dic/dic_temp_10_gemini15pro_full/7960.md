antheriferous:/ˌænθəˈrɪfərəs/
adjective
有花药的；产花粉的
Bearing anthers; producing pollen.
The antheriferous part of the flower is essential for reproduction.  这朵花的花药部分对繁殖至关重要。
The botanist identified the antheriferous structures under the microscope.  植物学家在显微镜下识别出花药结构。
Near Synonyms: pollen-bearing, staminate
Antonyms:  pistillate, sterile (in the context of reproduction)
Etymology: From Latin *antherifer*  from *anthera* (anther) + *-fer* (bearing).
