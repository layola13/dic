collaboratives:/kəˈlæbərətɪvz/
n.|合作项目，协作作品|Projects or works created through collaboration.|例句：The museum is showcasing several exciting new collaboratives between artists and scientists.  博物馆正在展出艺术家和科学家之间几个令人兴奋的新合作项目。

例句：Online collaboratives allow people from all over the world to work together. 在线合作平台允许来自世界各地的人们一起工作。|近义词：joint ventures, partnerships, team projects|反义词:solo projects, individual works|词性:名词

collaborative:/kəˈlæbərətɪv/
adj.|合作的，协作的|Involving or done by two or more people or groups working together.|例句：The project was a collaborative effort between several departments. 这个项目是几个部门共同努力的结果。

例句：She has a collaborative working style and enjoys brainstorming with colleagues. 她有一个合作的工作风格，喜欢与同事们进行头脑风暴。|近义词：cooperative, joint, shared|反义词:independent, solo, individual|词性:形容词
