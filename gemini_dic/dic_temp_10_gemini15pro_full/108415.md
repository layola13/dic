lawnier:/ˈlɔːniər/ | adj. | 更像草坪的，草坪更多的 |  Comparative form of lawny: having more or resembling a lawn more closely. |  The front yard is looking lawnier this year thanks to the new fertilizer.  今年由于使用了新的肥料，前院看起来更像草坪了。 | more grassy, lusher | less grassy, barer | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From lawn + -ier (comparative suffix).  "Lawn" comes from Middle English *laune*, from Anglo-French *launde*, meaning "a glade, clearing".
