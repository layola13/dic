devotionary:/dɪˈvoʊʃənəri/ | n. adj. |祈祷书，灵修书籍；虔诚的，祷告的 | A book of prayers or meditations for private use; Characterized by or expressing devotion. |例句：She reads her devotional every morning. 她每天早上读她的灵修书籍。 |近义词：prayer book, spiritual, pious, devout |反义词：secular, worldly |词性：名词, 形容词
devotional:/dɪˈvoʊʃənl/ | n. adj. |祈祷式，灵修仪式；虔诚的，祷告的 | A religious service or exercise consisting chiefly of prayer and meditation; Characterized by or expressing devotion. |例句：The family holds a short devotional each evening.  这家人每天晚上都举行一个简短的祈祷仪式。 |近义词：prayer service, worship, reverent |反义词：irreligious, profane |词性：名词, 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *devotionalis, from Latin devotio (“dedication, consecration”), from devotus (“devoted”).
