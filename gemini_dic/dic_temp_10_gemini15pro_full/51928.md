diabetes:/ˌdaɪəˈbiːtiːz/ /ˌdaɪəˈbiːtis/
| n. |糖尿病|A disease in which the body’s ability to produce or respond to the hormone insulin is impaired, resulting in abnormal metabolism of carbohydrates and elevated levels of glucose in the blood and urine.|My grandmother has diabetes. 我的祖母患有糖尿病。|Synonyms: hyperglycemia|Antonyms: hypoglycemia|noun


diabetes mellitus:/ˌdaɪəˈbiːtiːz ˈmɛlɪtəs/ /ˌdaɪəˈbiːtis məˈlaɪtəs/
| n. |糖尿病|A group of metabolic disorders characterized by hyperglycemia resulting from defects in insulin secretion, insulin action, or both.|Diabetes mellitus is a serious health concern. 糖尿病是一个严重的健康问题。|Synonyms: diabetes, sugar diabetes|Antonyms: None|noun
