mopboard:/ˈmɒpbɔːd/ (UK) /ˈmɑːpbɔːrd/ (US)| n. |踢脚板|A board fixed to the bottom of a wall to protect it from dirt, damage, etc., especially during cleaning.| The mopboard was scuffed from the vacuum cleaner. 吸尘器把踢脚板刮花了。|Synonyms: baseboard, skirting board, |Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From mop + board.  Originally referred to a board that was specifically designed to be easy to clean with a mop, due to its projecting profile.
