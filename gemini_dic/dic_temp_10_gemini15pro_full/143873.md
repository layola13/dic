outpoison:/ˌaʊtˈpɔɪzn/
verb
比…更毒；毒害胜过
to poison something more than something else
The scorpion outpoisoned the spider.  这只蝎子比蜘蛛更毒。
近义词: outvenom, surpass in toxicity
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider "neutralize" or "cure")
词性: verb

词源:  out- + poison.  "Out-" acts as a prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing.
