mummifications: /ˌmʌmɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/
|n.| 木乃伊化（过程），防腐处理 |The process of preserving a body by embalming and wrapping it in cloth, as practiced in ancient Egypt and other cultures.  |The mummifications of the pharaohs were elaborate rituals. 法老的木乃伊化过程是精心设计的仪式。|  The museum exhibits several mummifications from different periods.  博物馆展出了不同时期的几具木乃伊。|embalmment, preservation|decomposition, decay|noun
