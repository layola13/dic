例句：His anticonventional approach to business has made him a millionaire. 他不落俗套的商业方法使他成为百万富翁。
例句：She has an anticonventional sense of style. 她有一种不落俗套的时尚感。|近义词：unconventional, unorthodox, nonconformist, iconoclastic|反义词：conventional, traditional, orthodox, conformist|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + conventional.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to," and "conventional" refers to what is generally accepted or agreed upon.
