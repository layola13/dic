automobile:/ˈɔːtəməˌbiːl/ (US) /ˈɔːtəməbiːl/ (UK)| n.|汽车|A road vehicle, typically with four wheels, powered by an internal combustion engine or electric motor, and used for transporting passengers.|He drove his automobile to work. 他开车去上班。|近义词：car, vehicle, motorcar|反义词:N/A|名词

auto:/ˈɔːtoʊ/ (US) /ˈɔːtəʊ/ (UK)| n.|汽车 (非正式)|A shortened form of "automobile".|He just bought a new auto. 他刚买了一辆新车。|近义词：car, automobile, vehicle|反义词:N/A|名词

词源:

"automobile" 源自法语单词 "automobile",  由希腊语 "autos" (self) 和拉丁语 "mobilis" (movable) 组成，意为“self-moving”。

"auto" 是 "automobile" 的缩写。
