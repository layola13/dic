floret:/ˈflɔːrət/ /ˈflɔːrɪt/ | n. |小花；小枝 |A small flower, especially one of the small flowers making up a composite flower head such as a daisy or a head of cauliflower. | The cauliflower was composed of hundreds of tiny florets.  这颗花椰菜是由数百朵小花组成的。|  The dandelion clock is made up of many individual florets. 蒲公英的绒球是由许多独立的小花组成的。| synonym: floweret, blossom | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "whole flower" in the context of a composite flower) | noun
