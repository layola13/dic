minim:/ˈmɪnɪm/ | n. | 最小量，微量; (音乐)二分音符 |A very small amount of something.  In music, a half note. | The doctor prescribed a minim of the powerful drug. 医生开了最小剂量的强效药。|She learned to identify minims and crotchets in her music theory class. 她在音乐理论课上学了如何辨认二分音符和四分音符。 | synonyms: drop, bit, iota, half note | antonyms: maximum, abundance | noun

minim:/ˈmɪnɪm/ | n. | (液量单位)一滴 | A unit of liquid volume. |  A minim is a very small unit of liquid measure. 一minim是非常小的液体计量单位。 | The recipe called for a minim of vanilla extract.  食谱要求加一滴香草精。| synonyms: drop, droplet | antonyms: gallon, liter | noun
