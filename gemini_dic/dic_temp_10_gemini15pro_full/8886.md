antifriction:/ˌæntiˈfrɪkʃən/
adjective
减摩的; 抗摩擦的
Reducing or preventing friction.
Antifriction bearings are crucial for smooth operation of machinery.  减摩轴承对于机器的平稳运行至关重要。
This antifriction coating reduces wear and tear on the engine.  这种减摩涂层减少了发动机的磨损。
Synonyms:  lubricating, nonabrasive
Antonyms:  abrasive, frictional
word class: adjective

Etymology:
From anti- "against" + friction.
