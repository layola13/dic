handspring:/ˈhændsprɪŋ/| n.|手翻，翻筋斗|An acrobatic movement in which the body turns forward or backward in a full circle from a standing position by putting the hands on the ground one at a time, kicking the legs over the head, and landing first on the feet and then on the hands.|She did a perfect handspring on the mat. 她在地垫上做了一个完美的手翻。|近义词：flip, somersault, cartwheel|反义词: (没有直接的反义词)|noun
