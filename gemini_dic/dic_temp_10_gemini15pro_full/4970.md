alkalimetrical:/ˌælkəlɪˈmɛtrɪkəl/
adjective
碱量测定的，酸碱测定法的
relating to or involving alkalimetry; used in alkalimetry.
This alkalimetrical method is used to determine the concentration of the acid.  这种碱量测定法用于测定酸的浓度。
The chemist performed an alkalimetrical titration.  化学家进行了碱量滴定。
synonyms:  acidimetric, titrimetric
antonyms: N/A
词性: adjective
