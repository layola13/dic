oversold:/ˌoʊvərˈsoʊld/ | adj. | 超卖的；卖空过度的；过分吹嘘的 |  Sold in excess of the available supply; excessively promoted or praised. | The concert tickets were oversold, and many people were turned away at the door.  音乐会的票超卖了，许多人在门口被拒之门外。 |  overvalued, hyped | undervalued | adjective

oversold:/ˌoʊvərˈsoʊld/ | adj. | 卖空过度的（指股票等）|  Having declined to a level that suggests the negativity is overdone and the price is likely to rise. | Analysts believe the stock is oversold and poised for a rebound. 分析师认为该股票被过度抛售，即将反弹。 | oversaturated, bearish  | bullish | adjective
