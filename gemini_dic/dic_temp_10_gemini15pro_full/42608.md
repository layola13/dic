counterquestioning:/ˌkaʊntərˈkwɛstʃənɪŋ/
n.|反诘；反问|The lawyer's counterquestioning exposed weaknesses in the witness's testimony.|律师的反问暴露了证人证词中的弱点。|
counterquestioning:|n.|The effective use of counterquestioning can dismantle a weak argument.|有效地运用反问可以瓦解一个站不住脚的论点。|近义词:cross-examination, questioning back|反义词:answering, responding|词性:名词

counterquestion:/ˌkaʊntərˈkwɛstʃən/
v.|反问；反诘|The witness counterquestioned the lawyer's interpretation of the evidence.|证人反驳了律师对证据的解释。|
counterquestion:|v.|He counterquestioned her about the inconsistencies in her story.|他就她故事中的矛盾之处对她进行了反问。|近义词:cross-examine, question back|反义词:answer, respond|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- + questioning.  "Counter-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "in opposition to." "Questioning" derives from the verb "question," ultimately from Latin "quaestio" meaning "a seeking, inquiry."
