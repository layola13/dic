earthrise: /ˈɜːθraɪz/ | n. | 地球升起（尤指从月球上看到的）| The photograph of the Earthrise is one of the most iconic images of the 20th century.  这张地球升起的照片是20世纪最具标志性的图像之一。 | The astronauts witnessed a breathtaking earthrise as they orbited the moon. 宇航员在绕月飞行时目睹了令人叹为观止的地球升起。| synonyms: dawning of Earth, Earth appearance | antonyms: Earthset | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Combination of "Earth" + "rise" (as in sunrise). Coined in the context of the Apollo 8 mission. "Earth" from Old English "eorðe," meaning "ground, soil, dry land." "Rise" from Old English "rīsan," meaning "to ascend, get up."
