ceilidh:/ˈkeɪli/ (US) /ˈkeɪlɪ/ (UK)| n.|凯利舞会；爱尔兰或苏格兰的传统社交聚会|A ceilidh is a social gathering which involves traditional Gaelic folk music and dancing, particularly in Ireland and Scotland. It's a lively and informal event where people of all ages and abilities can participate.|We went to a ceilidh last night and had a fantastic time. 昨晚我们去参加了一个凯利舞会，玩得很开心。

The village hall hosts a ceilidh every month. 村里的礼堂每个月都会举办一次凯利舞会。|近义词：party, gathering, social|反义词:None|词性:noun
