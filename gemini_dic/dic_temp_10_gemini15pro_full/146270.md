overfaintnesses: /ˌoʊvərˈfeɪntnɪsɪz/
| n. |过度昏厥; 频繁的昏厥| A state characterized by multiple instances of fainting or excessive faintness.  It suggests a recurring or prolonged experience of faintness. |  She suffered from overfaintnesses due to a heart condition. 她由于心脏疾病而经常昏厥。| Near synonyms:  syncope (medical term),  fainting spells, swooning, blackouts | Antonyms: alertness, consciousness, wakefulness
| Noun
