impromptu:/ɪmˈprɒmptjuː/ /ɪmˈprɑːmptuː/ | n. adj. adv.| 即兴演奏/表演，即兴的，即席的 |A short piece of music, especially for piano, that is performed without preparation or a performance or speech that is given without preparation; done without being planned or rehearsed.| He played an impromptu on the piano. 他即兴演奏了一曲钢琴曲。| synonyms: unrehearsed, spontaneous, extemporaneous, improvised, off-the-cuff | antonyms: planned, prepared, rehearsed | noun, adjective, adverb

impromptus:/ɪmˈprɒmptjuːz/ /ɪmˈprɑːmptuːz/ | n. | 即兴演奏/表演（复数）| Plural form of impromptu.
