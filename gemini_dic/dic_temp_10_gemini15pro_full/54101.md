disembowel:/dɪsɪmˈbaʊəl/| vt.|取出内脏，剖腹|To remove the internal organs of, especially from the abdomen.  It can be literal or figurative, meaning to deprive something of its essential part.|The ancient warriors would disembowel their enemies after battle. 古代武士会在战斗后取出敌人的内脏。|The new CEO disemboweled the company, stripping it of its core values and innovative spirit. 新任首席执行官掏空了公司，剥夺了它的核心价值观和创新精神。|eviscerate, gut| - |verb
disembowelment:/dɪsɪmˈbaʊəlmənt/| n.|取出内脏，剖腹|The act of removing the internal organs, especially from the abdomen.|The disembowelment of the victim was a gruesome sight. 受害者被剖腹的景象十分可怕。|evisceration, gutting|-|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing reversal or deprivation) + embowel (to remove the bowels of).  Ultimately from Latin *exinvolvere* (to roll out).
