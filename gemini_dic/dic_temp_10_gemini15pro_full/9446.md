antireductionism:/ˌæntiɹɪˈdʌkʃənɪzəm/
n.
反还原论
The philosophical position that a complex system is more than the sum of its parts and cannot be fully understood by reducing it to its individual components.
Antireductionism argues against explaining complex phenomena solely in terms of simpler ones. 反还原论反对仅仅用更简单的现象来解释复杂的现象。
The study of consciousness often embraces antireductionism, suggesting it's more than just brain activity. 意识研究通常包含反还原论，认为它不仅仅是大脑活动。
近义词:holism, organicism
反义词:reductionism
词性:noun
