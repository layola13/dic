gutturalness:/ˈɡʌtərəlnəs/
n.
喉音性；粗哑
The quality of being guttural; harshness or roughness of sound.
His voice had a certain gutturalness that made him sound intimidating. 他的声音带有一种喉音，使他听起来很吓人。
hoarseness, roughness, harshness
smoothness, clarity
noun
