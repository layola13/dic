facile:/ˈfæsaɪl/, /ˈfæsɪl/| adj.|容易的，肤浅的|Appearing neat and comprehensive only by ignoring the true complexities of an issue; superficial.|Her facile explanation of the complex problem was met with skepticism.|近义词：easy, simple, superficial, glib|反义词：difficult, complex, profound|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From French *facile*, from Latin *facilis* "easy," from *facere* "to do, make."
