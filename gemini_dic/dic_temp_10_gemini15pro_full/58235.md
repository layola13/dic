druidical:/druːˈɪdɪk(ə)l/
adjective
德鲁伊的；像德鲁伊的；与德鲁伊教有关的
Relating to the Druids, their practices, or their beliefs.  Characteristic of Druids.
The ancient stones formed a druidical circle.  这些古老的石头形成了一个德鲁伊式的圆圈。
The ceremony had a distinctly druidical feel.  这个仪式有一种明显的德鲁伊教的感觉。

近义词:pagan, ancient, mystical, ceremonial
反义词:modern, contemporary, secular

词性:adjective
词源:From Druid + -ical. Ultimately from Gaulish *druwids.
