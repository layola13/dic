bedridden:/ˈbedrɪdn/| adj.|卧床不起的，长期卧床的|Confined to bed by illness or infirmity.|例句：She was bedridden for months after the accident. 事故发生后，她卧床不起好几个月。

例句：His grandfather has been bedridden for years. 他的祖父已经卧床多年了。|近义词：confined, immobile, incapacitated|反义词:mobile, active, healthy|词性:形容词
Etymology: bed + ridden (past participle of ride). Originally meant "oppressed, tyrannized," as if ridden over by a nightmare or illness.  The current meaning, "confined to bed," emerged in the 16th century.
