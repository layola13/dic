almoravids:/ˌælməˈrɑːvɪdz/
n.
阿尔摩拉维德王朝
a Berber Muslim dynasty that ruled in Morocco and Spain in the 11th and 12th centuries.
The Almoravids conquered much of North Africa and Spain. 阿尔摩拉维德王朝征服了北非和西班牙的大部分地区。
The Almoravid empire stretched from Senegal to Spain. 阿尔摩拉维德帝国从塞内加尔延伸到西班牙。
synonyms:  Murabitun (Arabic name), Berber dynasty
antonyms: None (proper noun, referring to a specific historical entity).
