flyless:/ˈflaɪləs/| adj.|无飞行能力的，不会飞的|Unable to fly; lacking the ability or power of flight.|The kiwi is a flyless bird. 几维鸟是一种不会飞的鸟。|The dodo was a famously flyless bird. 渡渡鸟是一种著名的不会飞的鸟。|近义词：flightless, wingless|反义词：winged, volant|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From fly + -less.  The suffix "-less" is of Old English origin and means "without" or "lacking."  "Fly" in this context refers to the ability of flight, originating from Old English "flēogan."
