golem: /ˈɡoʊləm/ (US) /ˈɡɒlɛm/ (UK)| n.|魔像，泥人|An artificial being from Jewish folklore, magically created from inanimate matter, often clay or mud, and brought to life to serve its creator.|The rabbi animated the golem to protect the Jewish community.|Synonyms: automaton, robot, homunculus|Antonyms: human, organic|Noun
