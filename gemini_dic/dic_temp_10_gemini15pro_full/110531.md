lime:/laɪm/| n. adj.|石灰，酸橙，绿黄色|A white caustic alkaline substance consisting of calcium oxide, obtained by heating limestone.  A rounded citrus fruit similar to a small lemon but greener, more acidic, and with a distinctive flavour. A yellowish-green colour.|例句：He added lime to the soil. 他往土壤里加了石灰。|近义词：lemon, citrus|反义词:|n.

lime:/laɪm/| adj.|绿黄色的|Of a yellowish-green colour.|例句：She wore a lime green dress. 她穿了一件黄绿色的连衣裙。|近义词：chartreuse, yellowish-green|反义词:|adj.
