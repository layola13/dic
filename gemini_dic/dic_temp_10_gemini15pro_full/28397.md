carbonaceous:/ˌkɑːrbəˈneɪʃəs/
adjective
碳的；含碳的
containing carbon or coal
例句：Carbonaceous chondrites are a class of chondritic meteorites comprising at least 8 known groups and many ungrouped meteorites.  碳质球粒陨石是球粒陨石的一类，至少包含8个已知群和许多未分组的陨石。

例句：The carbonaceous film was analyzed using Raman spectroscopy.  用拉曼光谱分析了碳质薄膜。

近义词：carbonic, carbon, coaly
反义词：non-carbonaceous
