fluffed:/flʌft/
adjective|蓬松的，起绒的|Made light and airy, as by shaking or brushing.|The bird fluffed its feathers. 鸟儿抖松了羽毛。|近义词:fluffy, downy, puffy|反义词:flat, smooth|词性:adjective
词源:From fluff + -ed.  Fluff itself comes from Middle English *fluffen, probably of imitative origin.
