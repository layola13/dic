malapropist:/ˈmæləˌprɒpɪst/ | n. |  误用词语者，词语误用者 | A person who habitually misuses words, especially by confusing words that are similar in sound. |  "The malapropist insisted the alligator was a small amphibious reptile, clearly confusing it with a salamander."  "这位词语误用者坚持认为鳄鱼是一种小型两栖爬行动物，显然是把它与蝾螈混淆了。" | Synonyms: blunderer, misspeaker,  | Antonyms:  linguist, lexicographer | noun
