enlock: /ɪnˈlɒk/ | vt. | 锁住，关住；使固定 | To lock or fasten securely; to fix in place. |  He enlocked the jewels in the safe. 他把珠宝锁在保险箱里。

| The gears were enlocked, preventing the machine from working. 齿轮被锁住了，机器无法运转。 | 近义词：lock, secure, fasten, fix | 反义词：unlock, release, free | 词性: 动词


词源:

en- (prefix) + lock (verb)

"en-" is a prefix meaning "to put into or onto," "to cause to be," or "to make."  It comes from Old French and ultimately from Latin "in-".

"lock" comes from Old English "loc," meaning a fastening device.
