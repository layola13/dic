bending:/ˈbɛndɪŋ/| n. adj.|弯曲，弯曲的|The act of curving or causing something to curve.  The process of shaping something by curving it.|The bending of the metal was difficult.  金属的弯曲很困难。|curve, flex, bow|straightening|noun, adjective

bending:/ˈbɛndɪŋ/| n.|弯曲，转向|The curve or turn of something.|The road has a sharp bending. 这条路有一个急转弯。|curve, turn, bend|straightening|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *bendan "to bend, turn, make crooked," of Germanic origin; related to Dutch and German biegen, from PIE root *bheug- "to bend, curve."
