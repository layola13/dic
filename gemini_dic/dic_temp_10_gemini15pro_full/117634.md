megastome: /ˈmeɡəˌstoʊm/ | n. |巨口动物；大口 |An organism, especially a fish, having a very large mouth. |例句：The megastome shark is known for its enormous gape. 巨口鲨以其巨大的嘴而闻名。

例句：The anglerfish is a type of deep-sea megastome. 安康鱼是一种深海巨口动物。

近义词：large-mouthed, wide-mouthed |反义词: small-mouthed, narrow-mouthed| 词性: noun
