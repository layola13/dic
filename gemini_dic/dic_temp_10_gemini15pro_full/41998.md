costarring:/koʊˈstɑːrɪŋ/| adj. | 联合主演的 | Appearing in a movie or play along with the main star(s). | Example: She was costarring with Tom Hanks in the new film. 她与汤姆·汉克斯在新电影中联合主演。 | Example: The movie, costarring Meryl Streep and Robert De Niro, was a box office hit. 这部由梅丽尔·斯特里普和罗伯特·德尼罗联合主演的电影票房大卖。 | Near synonyms: sharing the lead, joint starring | Antonyms: leading, starring | word type: adjective

costarring:/koʊˈstɑːrɪŋ/| n. | 联合主演 | The act of appearing in a supporting role alongside a leading actor. | Example: The costarring of two popular actors helped the film's success.  两位受欢迎的演员的联合主演帮助了这部电影的成功。| Example:  Her costarring with him launched her career.  与他联合主演让她开启了职业生涯。| Near synonyms: joint performance, shared screen time | Antonyms: solo performance, lead role | word type: noun

costarring:/koʊˈstɑːrɪŋ/| adv. | 共同主演地 | In a costarring role.  (This usage is less common) | Example: He appeared costarring with several other famous actors. 他与其他几位著名演员共同主演。| Example: The film, costarring a young and unknown actress, surprised everyone with its success. 这部电影由一位年轻的不知名女演员共同主演，其成功让所有人感到惊讶。 | Near synonyms: jointly, together | Antonyms: solely, independently | word type: adverb
