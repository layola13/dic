metaplastic:/ˌmɛtəˈplæstɪk/| adj.|化生的，转化生的|Relating to or characterized by metaplasia (the change of cells from one differentiated type to another).|The biopsy revealed metaplastic changes in the esophageal lining.  活检显示食道内壁发生化生性改变。

The doctor explained that the patient's metaplastic cells were not cancerous. 医生解释说，病人的化生细胞并非癌细胞。|近义词：transformed, converted|反义词：anaplastic, undifferentiated|词性: adjective

词源:
From Greek meta- (change) + plastikos (capable of being molded).  来自希腊语 meta- (改变) + plastikos (可塑的)。
