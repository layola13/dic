词源: From Late Latin *consistentia, from Latin consistere "to stand firm, stand still, consist of," from com- "together" (see com-) + sistere "to cause to stand," from PIE root *sta- "to stand, make or be firm."


注意事项:  consistence 虽然可以表示"一致性"，但 consistency 更常用。consistence 多用于指物质的浓度或坚实度。
