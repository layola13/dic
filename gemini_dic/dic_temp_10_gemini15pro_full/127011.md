nascent:/ˈneɪs(ə)nt/ /ˈnæsənt/| adj.|初生的，新生的|Coming into existence; emerging. |例句：The nascent democracy is still fragile.  这个新生的民主仍然很脆弱。

例句：The company is investing in nascent technologies.  这家公司正在投资新兴技术。|近义词：emerging, developing, budding, incipient, embryonic|反义词：mature, developed, established, declining|词性:adjective

词源: From Latin *nascentem*, present participle of *nascī* (“to be born”).
