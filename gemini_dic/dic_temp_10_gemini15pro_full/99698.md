interlocking:/ˌɪntərˈlɒkɪŋ/ (US) /ˌɪntərˈlɑːkɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|联锁的，互相扣住的，紧密相连的|fitting together closely; joining or connecting in a way that forms a single unit|The toy bricks are interlocking, allowing children to build complex structures.  这些积木是互锁的，可以让孩子们搭建复杂的结构。|Synonyms: interconnected, intertwined, linked, interlaced|Antonyms: separate, detached, disconnected|adjective

interlocking:/ˌɪntərˈlɒkɪŋ/ (US) /ˌɪntərˈlɑːkɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|联锁，互锁装置|the act of interlocking or the state of being interlocked; a device that interlocks|The interlocking of the gears ensures smooth operation of the machine. 齿轮的联锁确保了机器的平稳运行。|Synonyms: connection, linkage, junction|Antonyms: separation, disconnection|noun
