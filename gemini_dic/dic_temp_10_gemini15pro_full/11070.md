archaeologist:/ˌɑːrkiˈɒlədʒɪst/ |n.|考古学家| A person who studies human history and prehistory through the excavation of sites and the analysis of artifacts and other physical remains.| The archaeologist carefully unearthed the ancient pottery. 考古学家小心翼翼地挖掘出古代陶器。|  The archaeologist specializes in the Bronze Age. 这位考古学家专攻青铜器时代。| synonyms: excavator, anthropologist, paleontologist (related) | antonyms: N/A | noun


Etymology: From French *archéologue*, from Greek *arkhaiología* "antiquarian lore," from *arkhaios* "ancient" + *-logía* "-logy."
