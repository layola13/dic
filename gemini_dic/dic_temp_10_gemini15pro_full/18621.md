benthic:/ˈbɛnθɪk/| adj.|底栖的，水底的|Of, relating to, or occurring at the bottom of a body of water.|例句：Benthic organisms play a vital role in the aquatic ecosystem.  底栖生物在水生生态系统中起着至关重要的作用。

例句：The study focused on the benthic community of the lake. 这项研究的重点是湖泊的底栖生物群落。|近义词：demersal, bottom-dwelling|反义词：pelagic|词性：形容词
