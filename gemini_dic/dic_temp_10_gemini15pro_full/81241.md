granduncle:/ˈɡrændʌŋkl/| n.|叔祖父；舅祖父|A granduncle is the brother of one's grandmother or grandfather.|I have fond memories of visiting my granduncle's farm when I was young. 我小时候去我叔祖父的农场玩耍的美好回忆。|近义词:great-uncle|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From grand- +‎ uncle.  "Grand-" acts as an intensifier, indicating one generation further removed. "Uncle" comes from Old French "uncle," ultimately from Latin "avunculus" (maternal uncle).
