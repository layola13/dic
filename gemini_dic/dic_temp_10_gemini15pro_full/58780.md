dunning:/ˈdʌnɪŋ/
n.|催款，催付|The persistent dunning of overdue accounts can damage customer relationships.  持续催缴逾期账款可能会损害客户关系。|
dunning:/ˈdʌnɪŋ/
adj.|催款的，催付的|The dunning letters were sent to customers with outstanding balances. 催款信寄给了余额未结清的客户。|
Etymology: From the verb "dun," which originated in the 17th century. Likely from the name "Dun," short for Joe Dun, a bailiff in Lincoln, England, known for his persistence in collecting debts.  源自动词 "dun"，起源于 17 世纪。可能来自名字 "Dun"，是英格兰林肯郡一位名为乔·邓的执达吏的简称，他以坚持不懈地收债而闻名。
Synonyms:  pressing, hounding, badgering, harassing, pestering|
Antonyms:  ignoring, neglecting, forgiving|
Word type: noun, adjective|
