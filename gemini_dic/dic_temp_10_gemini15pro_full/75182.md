frizzer:/ˈfrɪzər/| n.|卷发器，蓬松器|A device used to create volume or curls in hair. It can be a heated styling tool or a hair product.|She used a frizzer to add volume to her flat hair. 她用卷发器让扁塌的头发蓬松起来。|Synonyms: hair crimper, curling iron, volumizer|Antonyms: straightener, flat iron|noun

frizzy:/ˈfrɪzi/| adj.|卷曲的，蓬松的|Hair that is tightly curled and often dry and unruly.|Her hair gets frizzy in humid weather. 她的头发在潮湿的天气里会变得卷曲蓬松。|Synonyms: curly, kinky, unruly|Antonyms: straight, smooth, sleek|adjective
