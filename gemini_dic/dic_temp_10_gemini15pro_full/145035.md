overafflicts:/ˌoʊvərəˈflɪkts/| v.|过度折磨，使苦恼不堪|To afflict excessively or to an extreme degree.|The weight of his responsibilities overafflicted him, leading to a breakdown.|近义词：overburden, overwhelm, oppress|反义词:comfort, soothe|词性:动词

词源:
"Over-" (Old English *ofer) meaning "above, beyond, excessively" + "afflict" (from Latin afflictus, past participle of affligere "to dash to the ground, weaken, damage, injure, trouble, distress")
