cycloid:/ˈsaɪklɔɪd/| n. |摆线，旋轮线|A curve traced by a point on the circumference of a circle as it rolls along a straight line.|The path of the valve tip in a reciprocating pump is a cycloid. 往复泵中阀尖的路径是一条摆线。|近义词: roulette, trochoid |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
Etymology: From Modern Latin *cycloīdēs*, from Ancient Greek κύκλος (kúklos, “circle, wheel”) + -oid (“-like, -shaped”).
