air-to-surface:/ˌeər tə ˈsɜːrfɪs/ | adj. | 空对地的 | Designed to be launched from an aircraft at a target on the ground. | The fighter jet was equipped with air-to-surface missiles.  这架战斗机配备了空对地导弹。 | Synonyms: air-ground, ground-attack | Antonyms: surface-to-air, ground-to-air | adjective

Etymology:
"air" (Old French "air") + "to" (Old English "tō") + "surface" (Old French "sur-" + "face")
