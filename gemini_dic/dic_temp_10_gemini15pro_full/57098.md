doughy:/ˈdoʊi/| adj.|面团似的，柔软的，苍白的|Resembling dough, especially in being pale, soft, and thick.|The bread had a slightly doughy texture.  这面包的口感有点像生面团。|近义词：pasty, pasty-faced, pallid, soft, flabby|反义词:firm, tanned, robust|adjective

Etymology->
From dough + -y.  Ultimately from Old English *dāh.
