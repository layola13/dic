euphuist:/ˈjuːfjuɪst/ | n. | 修辞家；辞藻华丽的人 | A writer or speaker who uses an affectedly elegant and elaborate style, characterized by antithesis, alliteration, and far-fetched comparisons. |  John Lyly, the author of Euphues, is considered the archetypal euphuist. 约翰·礼利，《尤弗伊斯》的作者，被认为是典型的修辞家。

| Synonyms: stylist, rhetorician, verbalist | Antonyms: plain speaker, simple writer | noun
