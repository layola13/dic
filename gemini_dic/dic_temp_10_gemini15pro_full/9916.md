aper:/ˈeɪpər/| n.| 猿; 猴; 类人猿; 模仿者 |A primate mammal with long arms and no tail or a very short tail, esp. an ape, gorilla, chimpanzee, orangutan, or gibbon.  Someone who imitates or mimics another.| The young aper swung effortlessly through the trees.  这只小猿猴轻松地在树间荡来荡去。 He's such an aper, always copying what I do. 他真是个模仿者，总是学我做什么。 | 近义词：monkey, primate, mimic, imitator | 反义词:  (无明确的反义词，可考虑根据语境使用human, original等) | 词性: noun


Etymology->
From Old English *apa (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *apon, from Latin simia, from Greek πίθηκος (píthēkos).  Ultimately of uncertain origin.

注意事项:
虽然aper可以指多种灵长类动物，但更常用于指类人猿，尤其是在非正式场合。 在更正式的科学语境中，"ape"通常指没有尾巴的灵长类动物，例如黑猩猩、大猩猩、猩猩和长臂猿。
