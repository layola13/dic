anemic:/əˈniːmɪk/ | adj. | 贫血的；缺乏活力的；无精打采的 | Lacking vigor or energy; listless. Also, relating to or suffering from anemia. |  The doctor said she was anemic and needed iron supplements. 医生说她贫血，需要补铁。 | Synonyms: pale, feeble, weak, languid, listless | Antonyms: robust, vigorous, energetic, strong | adjective

anemic:/əˈniːmɪk/ | adj. | 贫血的；(比喻)缺乏力量的；没有生气的 |  Relating to or affected with anemia; lacking in vitality, force, or power; insipid.  | The play was criticized for its anemic plot and uninspired acting.  这出戏因其情节无力和表演缺乏灵感而受到批评。 | Synonyms: pallid, washed-out, bloodless, ashen, colorless; lifeless, spiritless, lackluster, vapid | Antonyms: vibrant, dynamic, energetic, robust | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *anémique*, from Late Latin *anaemicus*, from Greek *anaimos* "bloodless," from *an-* "without" + *haima* "blood."
