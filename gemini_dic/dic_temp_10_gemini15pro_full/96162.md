independences:/ˌɪndɪˈpendənsiz/
|n.|独立性；自主性|The state of being independent; freedom from outside control or support. |After years of struggle, the colony finally achieved its independence. 经过多年的斗争，殖民地终于获得了独立。|The American colonies fought for independence from Britain.  美国殖民地为脱离英国而战。|近义词：autonomy, self-reliance, sovereignty, liberty, freedom|反义词：dependence, reliance, subjection, subordination|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Old French independance, from Late Latin independentia, from independent- ‘not dependent’, from the verb independere, from in- ‘not’ + dependere ‘to hang down, depend’.
