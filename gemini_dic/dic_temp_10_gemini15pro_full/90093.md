hostler:/ˈhɒstlə(r)/ (UK), /ˈhɑːstlər/ (US)| n.|马夫；旅店老板；火车司机助手|A person employed to look after horses at an inn or stable, or a person who helps to service steam locomotives. |The hostler led the tired horse to the stable. 马夫把疲惫的马牵到马厩。

The hostler stoked the engine's fire. 火车司机助手给引擎加煤。|Synonyms: stableman, groom, ostler (UK)|Antonyms: guest, customer|noun


hostlers:/ˈhɒstləz/ (UK), /ˈhɑːstlərz/ (US)| n.|马夫们；旅店老板们；火车司机助手们|Plural form of "hostler"|The hostlers were busy tending to the horses. 马夫们正忙着照料马匹。

Several hostlers worked together to prepare the locomotive. 几位火车司机助手一起准备火车头。|Synonyms: stablemen, grooms, ostlers (UK)|Antonyms: guests, customers|noun
