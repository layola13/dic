empty-handed: /ˌɛmptiˈhændɪd/ | adj. | 空手的；一无所获的 | Having nothing in one's hands; having failed to obtain something desired or expected. | He returned from the fishing trip empty-handed. 他空手而归，钓鱼之旅一无所获。|  She went to the store for milk but came back empty-handed because they were sold out. 她去商店买牛奶，但因为卖完了而空手而归。 | 近义词: unsuccessful, fruitless, unfruitful | 反义词: successful, fruitful, with something to show for it | 词性: 形容词
