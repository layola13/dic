dressing-down:/ˈdrɛsɪŋ daʊn/ (US), /ˈdrɛsɪŋ daʊn/ (UK)| n.| 斥责，训斥|A scolding; a reprimand.  A severe criticism or telling-off.| He received a dressing-down from his boss for being late.| Synonyms: reprimand, rebuke, scolding, lecture, talking-to, telling-off | Antonyms: praise, commendation | noun
