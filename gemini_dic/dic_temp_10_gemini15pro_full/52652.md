digitally:/ˈdɪdʒɪtəli/
adverb
数字地，数位地
In a digital form, relating to or using calculations or data represented by digits
例句：
The information is stored digitally.  这些信息以数字方式存储。

The music is recorded digitally.  这音乐是数字录制的。


近义词：electronically, virtually, online
反义词:analogically, physically
词性:adverb
