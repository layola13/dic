nonspecificity:/ˌnɑːnˌspesɪˈfɪsɪti/ | n. | 非特异性，非专一性 | The lack of specific targeting or action; the quality of being applicable to a wide range of things or situations rather than a particular one. | The nonspecificity of the symptoms made diagnosis difficult.  症状的非特异性使得诊断变得困难。|  The test's nonspecificity led to many false positives.  这项测试的非特异性导致了许多假阳性。 | generality, lack of precision, broadness | specificity, precision, selectivity | noun

词源: non- (not) + specific + -ity (noun suffix)
