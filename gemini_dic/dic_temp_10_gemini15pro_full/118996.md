methicillins:/ˌmɛθɪˈsɪlɪnz/
noun
甲氧西林（复数）
A semisynthetic form of penicillin that is resistant to breakdown by bacterial enzymes.
Methicillins were once effective against staphylococcus aureus. 甲氧西林曾经对金黄色葡萄球菌有效。
Synonyms: meticillin,  (when referring to resistant bacteria) MRSA
Antonyms: None (specific antonyms for a particular antibiotic are uncommon)
