headquarters's:/ˈhedˌkwɔːrtərzɪz/
n.
总部；司令部
The possessive form of "headquarters," indicating ownership or association.  It refers to something belonging to or associated with the headquarters.
The headquarters's location is kept secret for security reasons. 总部的位置出于安全原因保密。
The general inspected the headquarters's communication systems. 将军视察了总部的通讯系统。
synonyms: head office, main office, center of operations
antonyms: branch, satellite office
noun
