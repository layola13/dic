exempting:/ɪɡˈzɛmptɪŋ/
verb
免除;豁免
Freeing or state of being freed from an obligation or liability imposed on others.
Students are exempted from paying the fee. 学生免交费用。
近义词:excusing, releasing, freeing
反义词:obligating, requiring, subjecting
词性:v.

exempt:/ɪɡˈzɛmpt/
adjective
免除的;豁免的
Free from an obligation or liability imposed on others.
Charities are exempt from paying tax. 慈善机构免税。
近义词: excused, released, free
反义词: liable, subject, obligated
词性:adj.

exemption:/ɪɡˈzɛmpʃn/
noun
豁免;免除
The process of freeing or state of being free from an obligation or liability imposed on others.
He was granted exemption from military service. 他获得了免服兵役的豁免。
近义词: immunity, release, freedom
反义词: obligation, liability, subjection
词性:n.


词源:
From Late Middle English *exempten*, from Old French exempter, from Latin exemptus, past participle of eximere (“to take out, remove”), from ex (“out of”) + emere (“to take, buy”).
