merino:/məˈriːnoʊ/| n. |美利奴羊；美利奴羊毛|A breed of fine-wooled sheep originally from Spain, now widely raised in Australia, New Zealand, and other countries.  Also refers to the wool of this sheep.|例句：This sweater is made of 100% merino wool.  这件毛衣是100%美利奴羊毛制成的。

例句：Merinos are prized for their soft, fine fleece. 美利奴羊因其柔软细腻的羊毛而备受珍视。|近义词：sheep, fleece|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑coarse wool 粗糙的羊毛)|词性:noun
