croconic:/kroʊˈkɑːnɪk/
adjective
番红花的，藏红花黄的，藏红花酸的
Relating to or derived from croconic acid, a yellow crystalline acid obtained from potassium carboxide.
Croconic acid forms intensely yellow salts. 藏红花酸形成深黄色的盐。
Croconic acid reacts with metals to form croconates. 藏红花酸与金属反应生成藏红花酸盐。
synonym:yellow, saffron-colored
antonym:colorless
adjective
