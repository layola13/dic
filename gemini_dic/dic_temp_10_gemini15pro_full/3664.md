agglomerator:/əˈɡlɒməreɪtə(r)/
n.|凝聚器，结块器，烧结机|A machine or device that causes small particles to adhere and form larger masses.|The agglomerator combines fine iron ore particles into pellets suitable for use in a blast furnace.|近义词：aggregator, collector, compiler|反义词：separator, disperser|词性:noun
Etymology: From the Latin word *agglomerāre*, meaning "to wind into a ball," from *ad-* ("to") + *glomus* ("ball").
