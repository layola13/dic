nonresident:/ˌnɒnˈrɛzɪdənt/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈrɛzɪd(ə)nt/ (UK)| n.|非居民|A person who does not live permanently in a particular place.|例句：The nonresidents were required to register with the local authorities. 非居民被要求向当地政府登记。|近义词：foreigner, alien, outsider|反义词:resident, local|词性:名词

词源:  From non- + resident.  "Resident" comes from the Latin "residentem," present participle of "residēre" (to remain, sit down, reside).
