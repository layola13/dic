incantation:/ˌɪnkænˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 咒语，符咒；吟诵 |  A series of words said as a magic spell or charm.  A ritual recitation of verbal charms or spells believed to have magical power. | The witch muttered an incantation over the bubbling cauldron. 女巫对着冒泡的大锅低声念着咒语。|  spell, charm, conjuration | counter-charm, exorcism | noun
