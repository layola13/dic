dus:/dʌs/ | n. | 灰尘，尘土 | Fine, dry powder consisting of tiny particles of earth or waste matter. | Example: The furniture was covered in a thick layer of dus. 家具上覆盖着一层厚厚的灰尘。| Example:  He wiped the dus off the table. 他擦掉了桌子上的灰尘。 | Synonyms: dust, grime, soot | Antonyms: cleanliness, purity | noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *dus*, from Old English *dūst*, from Proto-Germanic *dunstaz* (“vapor, dust”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰewh₂- (“dust, smoke, vapor”). Cognate with Dutch *duist* (“dust”), German *Dunst* (“vapor, steam”), Icelandic *dust* (“dust”).
