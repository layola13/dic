burgeon:/ˈbɜːrdʒən/| v. |萌芽，迅速发展|To begin to grow or increase rapidly; flourish.|例句：The city’s population has burgeoned in recent years.  近年来，该市的人口迅速增长。

例句：New suburbs have burgeoned on the edge of the city. 新的郊区在城市边缘迅速发展。|近义词：flourish, thrive, prosper, expand, boom|反义词：decline, dwindle, shrink, contract, wither|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *burjonen, from Old French burjoner, from burjon (“bud, sprout”), from Vulgar Latin *burrionem, from Late Latin burra (“wool, flock of wool”), from Latin burrus (“reddish-brown”).
