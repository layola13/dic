arytenoid: /ˌærɪˈtiːnɔɪd/ | adj. n. |杓状的；杓状软骨 |Relating to either of the two small cartilages at the back of the larynx to which the vocal cords are attached.  A pair of small, pyramid-shaped cartilages that are essential for voice production. | The arytenoid cartilages play a crucial role in phonation. 杓状软骨在发声中起着至关重要的作用。|  The doctor examined the patient's arytenoid area. 医生检查了病人的杓状软骨区域。|  Synonym:  laryngeal (relating to larynx) | Antonym:  None | Noun, Adjective
