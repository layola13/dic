outsmart:/ˌaʊtˈsmɑːrt/ | v. | 智胜，比…更聪明 | To defeat or outwit someone by being more clever or cunning. | She outsmarted her opponents with a clever strategy. 她用一个巧妙的策略智胜了她的对手。 |  He outsmarted the police by changing his car. 他通过换车智胜了警察。 | 近义词：outwit, outmaneuver, outfox | 反义词：be outwitted, be outsmarted | 词性: 动词

词源: out- + smart.  "Out-" prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing. "Smart" meaning clever or intelligent.
