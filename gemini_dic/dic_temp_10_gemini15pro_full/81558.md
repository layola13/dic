graynesses:/ˈɡreɪnɪsnɪsɪz/
noun
灰暗，阴沉；灰色的地方
The state or quality of being gray or greyish; multiple instances of grayness.
The photograph captured the graynesses of the winter sky. 这张照片捕捉到了冬日天空的灰暗。
近义词:drabness, dullness, gloominess
反义词:brightness, colorfulness, vibrancy
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From gray + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state, condition, or quality).  Gray itself has origins in Old English græg.
