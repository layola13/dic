endorsible:/ɪnˈdɔːrsəbl/ | adj. | 可背书的，可转让的 | Able to be endorsed; transferable.  Specifically referring to a document, typically financial, that can be legally transferred to another party by signing it. | The check was made endorsible to the bearer.  这张支票可以背书给持票人。|  The bond is endorsible, allowing for easy transfer of ownership.  该债券可背书转让，方便所有权转移。 |  transferable, negotiable | non-transferable, non-negotiable | adjective
