ovoviviparously:/ˌoʊvəˌvaɪvɪˈpærəsli/
adverb
卵胎生地
In a manner characterized by ovoviviparity, where eggs are hatched within the body of the parent.
The shark reproduced ovoviviparously, giving birth to live young.  鲨鱼以卵胎生的方式繁殖，产下幼鲨。
The boa constrictor ovoviviparously brings forth its young.  蟒蛇以卵胎生的方式产下幼崽。
Near Antonyms: oviparously, viviparously
Etymology: From ovoviviparous + -ly.  Ovoviviparous from Latin ovum "egg" + viviparous from vivus "living" + parere "to bring forth".
