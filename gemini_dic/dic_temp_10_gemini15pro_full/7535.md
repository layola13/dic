annexe:/ˈæneks/ (UK) /əˈneks/ (US)| n. |附属建筑，附件，附录|A building joined to or associated with a main building, providing additional space or accommodations.  An addition to a document.|The hospital has a new annex for outpatients. 医院新建了一个门诊部附楼。|Synonyms: addition, extension, appendage, wing, supplement|Antonyms: main building, core|noun

annex:/əˈneks/ (US) /əˈnɛks/ (UK) | vt. |吞并，附加|To add or attach, especially by force or without permission.|The country annexed the neighboring territory.  这个国家吞并了邻近的领土。|Synonyms: seize, conquer, appropriate, acquire, add, attach|Antonyms: detach, separate, relinquish|verb
