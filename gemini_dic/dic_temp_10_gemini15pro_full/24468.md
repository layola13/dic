britishly:/ˈbrɪtɪʃli/| adv.| 以英国人的方式；像英国人一样地 | In a way that is characteristic of British people or culture. | She spoke very britishly. 她说话很英国腔。| 近义词: typically British, in a British manner | 反义词: unbritishly, foreignly | 词性: 副词

词源:  Derived from the adjective "British" with the suffix "-ly" to form an adverb.  "British" itself comes from the Old English "Bryttisc," referring to the Britons, the native Celtic inhabitants of Great Britain.
