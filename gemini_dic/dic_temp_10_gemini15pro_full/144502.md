outtongued:/ˌaʊtˈtʌŋd/
|adj.|雄辩胜过；口才超过；说得…哑口无言|Having been defeated in debate; silenced by superior eloquence.|He was completely outtongued by the experienced lawyer. 他被这位经验丰富的律师说得哑口无言。|近义词:outspoken, eloquent, articulate|反义词:inarticulate, tongue-tied|词性:形容词

词源:  "out-" (超过) + "tongue" (舌头, 语言)  表示在语言能力上胜过对方.
