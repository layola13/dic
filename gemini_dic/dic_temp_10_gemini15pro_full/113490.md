lyrist:/ˈlɪrɪst/| n.|抒情诗人；作词家|A person who writes the words for songs, especially pop songs.  A poet, especially one who writes lyric poetry.|例句：The lyrist of the band wrote some beautiful and moving lyrics.  乐队的作词家写了一些优美动人的歌词。

例句：The renowned lyrist was known for his evocative and poignant verses.  这位著名的抒情诗人以其引人入胜和令人心酸的诗句而闻名。|近义词：poet, songwriter, versifier|反义词: (没有直接的反义词)|词性: noun
