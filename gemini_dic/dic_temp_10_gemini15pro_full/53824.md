disconnection's:/ˌdɪskəˈnekʃən'z/
noun
断开，连接中断
the act of disconnecting or the state of being disconnected.
The disconnection's impact was felt immediately. 连接中断的影响立即显现出来。
The internet disconnection's cause was a faulty cable.  网络连接中断的原因是电缆故障。
synonym: separation, severance, detachment
antonym: connection, union, link
词性: 名词
