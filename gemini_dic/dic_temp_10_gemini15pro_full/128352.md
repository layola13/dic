neutralizing:/ˈnjuːtrəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
抵消；使中和；使无效
Making something neutral, as in reaction or effect.  Making an acid or base chemically neutral.
The antibodies are neutralizing the virus.  抗体正在中和病毒。
The company issued a statement neutralizing the negative publicity. 公司发表声明以抵消负面宣传。
synonyms: counteracting, balancing, offsetting
antonyms: intensifying, strengthening, promoting
