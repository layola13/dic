attending:/əˈtɛndɪŋ/
attending|adj.|出席的，参加的；伴随的|Present participle of attend.  Describes the act of being present at an event or accompanying someone.|例句：The attending physician made the diagnosis. 主治医生做了诊断。|近义词：present, participating, accompanying|反义词：absent, missing|词性: adjective
attending:/əˈtɛndɪŋ/
attending|n.|出席；参加；护理；照料|The act of being present at a place or event, or providing care or service to someone.|例句：Her attending to the details impressed the manager. 她对细节的关注给经理留下了深刻印象。|近义词：presence, attendance, care|反义词：absence, neglect|词性: noun
