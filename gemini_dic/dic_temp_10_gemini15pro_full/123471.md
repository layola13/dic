monosporous:/ˌmɑːnəˈspɔːrəs/ |adj.|单孢子的|Producing or containing only one spore, typically in an ascus (a sac-like structure in fungi).|Example 1: The monosporous culture allowed for genetic analysis of the individual fungus.  单孢子培养允许对单个真菌进行基因分析。

Example 2: Monosporous asci are characteristic of certain species of yeast.  单孢子子囊是某些酵母菌种的特征。|Synonym: unisporous|Antonym: multisporous, polysporous|Etymology: "mono-" (Greek: one) + "spor-" (Greek: seed, spore) + "-ous" (suffix forming adjectives)|Word Type: Adjective
