/wɑːt/ (UK) /wɑːt/ (US)|pron. det. int. adv.| 什么，多么 |Used to ask for information about something; used to ask for repetition of something not heard or not understood; used to express surprise or disbelief. |
What a beautiful day! 多么美好的一天！
What time is it? 现在几点了？

近义词: which
反义词: (No single antonym for "what," as its meaning depends on context)
词性: pronoun, determiner, interjection, adverb


USAGE->

What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? = 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? = 她用这笔钱做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading this week? = 他这周一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃了什么？
What time did she arrive? = 她几点到的？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 pm last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I called? = 在我打电话之前你做了什么？
What had they discussed before the decision? = 在做决定之前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

一般将来时 (Simple Future):

What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
