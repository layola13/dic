confabulate:/kənˈfæbjʊleɪt/ /kənˈfæbjəleɪt/| v. | 闲聊，聊天；虚构，编造 | to chat or talk casually; to fabricate or make up (a story or an event)| We often confabulated about our childhood adventures. 我们经常聊起我们童年时代的冒险故事。

He confabulated a story about being kidnapped by aliens. 他编造了一个关于被外星人绑架的故事。| 近义词：chat, converse, fabricate, invent | 反义词：be silent, be quiet, tell the truth |词性：动词
