half-famished:/ˌhæf ˈfæmɪʃt/ | adj. | 极饿的，非常饥饿的 | Extremely hungry; very close to starving. |  The hikers arrived at the cabin half-famished after their long trek.  徒步旅行者长途跋涉后到达小屋时饿极了。

The half-famished children devoured the sandwiches. 饥肠辘辘的孩子们狼吞虎咽地吃着三明治。 | 近义词: ravenous, starving, very hungry | 反义词: full, satiated, well-fed | 词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From "half" + "famished".  "Famished" comes from the Old French "famesche," meaning "famished," which ultimately derives from the Latin "fames," meaning "hunger."
