backlists:/ˈbækˌlɪsts/| n.|黑名单；禁书目录|A list of books or authors that are disapproved of and not promoted or stocked by a particular library, bookstore, or publisher, often for political or moral reasons.|Many libraries refused to carry books on their backlists that dealt with controversial topics.|近义词：blacklist, banned list, censored list|反义词: approved list, recommended list|词性:名词
