ebrillade:/ˈɛbrɪleɪd/
noun
欢呼; 兴奋
A sudden burst of emotion or excitement.
The announcement of the winner was met with ebrillades from the crowd.  宣布获胜者后，人群中爆发出欢呼声。

The team's victory sparked ebrillades in the stadium. 球队的胜利引发了体育场内的欢呼。

Synonym:  rejoicing, jubilation, exhilaration
Antonym:  despair, gloom, dejection
Part of Speech: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *ébrillade*, from *ébriller* "to exhilarate," from Old French *esbriller*, from *es-* (out) + *briller* (to shine). Ultimately from Latin *berillus* (beryl).
