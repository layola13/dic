fairweather:/ˈfɛəˌwɛðər/| adj.|好天气的；晴朗的；只在情况好时才支持或友好的|Someone who is fairweather is only a friend or supporter when things are going well. |He's a fairweather friend; he's nowhere to be seen when you need him. 他是个酒肉朋友；当你需要他时，他无影无踪。|近义词：fickle, unreliable, inconstant|反义词：loyal, steadfast, true|词性:形容词

词源:
From "fair" (pleasant, agreeable) + "weather" (atmospheric conditions).  This compound word literally refers to pleasant weather but figuratively denotes someone who is only present or supportive during good times.
