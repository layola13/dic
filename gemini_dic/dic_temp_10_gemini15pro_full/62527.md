endure:/ɪnˈdjʊər(英式) / /ɪnˈdʊr(美式) /| v.| 忍受，忍耐；持续，持久|  To suffer (something painful or difficult) patiently over a long period; To remain in existence; last.|例句：They endured great hardship during the war.  他们在战争期间忍受了极大的困苦。

例句：The tradition has endured for centuries.  这个传统已经延续了几个世纪。|近义词：tolerate, bear, withstand, last, persist|反义词: succumb, yield, perish|词性:动词
