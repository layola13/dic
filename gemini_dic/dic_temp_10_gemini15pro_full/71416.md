flair:/fler/ | n. | 天赋，才华；鉴赏力；特别的才能；(尤指穿着的)潇洒，优雅 | A natural ability to do something well; a stylishness and originality. | She has a flair for languages. 她有语言天赋。| synonyms: talent, gift, aptitude, knack, ability, genius, skill, expertise, bent, style, elegance, panache, dash, swagger | antonyms: inability, incompetence, clumsiness | noun

flair:/fler/ | n. |  嗅觉；觉察力|  The ability to detect something | He had a flair for spotting profitable investments. 他有觉察获利投资的天赋。| synonyms: intuition, instinct, insight, perception, discernment | antonyms: obtuseness | noun
