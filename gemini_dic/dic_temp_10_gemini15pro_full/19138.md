bevel:/ˈbevəl/| n. v.|斜角，斜面；使成斜角，使成斜面|A surface or edge that is not horizontal or vertical; to cut or shape something so that it has a bevelled edge. |The mirror had a bevelled edge.  镜子边缘是斜切的。|He bevelled the edges of the wooden plank. 他把木板的边缘削成斜面。|近义词:slope, slant, chamfer|反义词:straight, level|词性:名词, 动词
bevels:/ˈbevəlz/| n.|斜角，斜面（复数）|The plural form of bevel, referring to multiple sloped surfaces or edges.|The picture frame's bevels added to its elegance.  画框的斜边增加了它的优雅。|近义词:slopes, slants, chamfers|反义词:straight lines, level surfaces|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *bivel, probably from Latin *bivium "fork in a road," from bi- "two" + via "way."  The sense development is likely from the idea of two surfaces meeting at an angle.
