beheadals:/biˈhedəlz/
n.
斩首;砍头
the act of cutting off someone's head
The beheadals were carried out in public. 这些斩首是在公共场合进行的。
近义词:decapitations, executions
反义词:spares, reprieves
词性:名词

词源:
From behead + -al (suffix forming nouns of action). Behead comes from be- (prefix meaning "completely") + head (noun).  Ultimately from Old English beheafdian, from be- + heafod "head."
