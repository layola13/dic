ichthyomorpha:/ɪkθiəˈmɔːrfə/ | n. | 鱼形类，鱼形动物 | An extinct subclass of tetrapods that were transitional between amphibians and reptiles, possessing characteristics of both. They were primarily aquatic and resembled fish in body shape. | Example: Ichthyomorpha were important in the evolutionary history of vertebrates. 鱼形类在脊椎动物进化史上很重要。 | Example:  The study of Ichthyomorpha fossils helps us understand the transition from water to land. 对鱼形类化石的研究有助于我们理解从水到陆地的过渡。 | 近义词:  temnospondyls (some overlap) | 反义词:  None (taxonomic group) | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Greek *ikhthys* (fish) + *morphē* (form).
