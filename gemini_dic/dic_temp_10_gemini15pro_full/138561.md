一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing right now? = 他现在在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she been up to lately? = 她最近在忙什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all week? = 你整个星期都在做什么？
What has he been reading recently? = 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say? = 你说了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about yesterday? = 她昨天在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you left? = 你离开之前做了什么？
What had they eaten before the movie? = 他们在看电影前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I arrived? = 我来之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been talking about for so long? = 他们一直在谈论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天要做什么？
What will the weather be like next week? = 下周天气会怎样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting tomorrow? = 明天会议上他们会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by next month? = 到下个月你将完成什么？
What will they have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底他们将取得什么成就？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
