palatalised:/ˈpælətəlaɪzd/ | adj. |腭化的；软腭化的|  Made to sound with the tongue closer to the hard palate, giving a "y" sound after the consonant.  This is a phonetic process where consonants are pronounced as if followed by a /j/ sound, like the "y" in "yes".| The "t" in "tune" is palatalised.  “tune”中的“t”是腭化的。|  The pronunciation of the Spanish "ñ" is a palatalised "n". 西班牙语中的“ñ”的发音是腭化的“n”。 | palatalized, softened |  hardened, velarized| adjective
