forgoer:/fɔːrˈɡoʊər/
n.
放弃者; 摒弃者
One who forgoes something; one who abstains or refrains from something.
Many smokers are now forgoers of cigarettes due to health concerns. 由于健康问题，许多吸烟者现在都放弃了香烟。
The forgoers of the festival missed a great performance. 放弃参加节日的人错过了一场精彩的演出。

近义词: abstainer, renouncer
反义词: indulger, partaker
词性: 名词
