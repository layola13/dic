monarchical:/məˈnɑːrkɪkəl/
adjective
君主制的；君主政体的
Of or relating to a monarch or monarchy. Characterized by or favoring monarchy.
The country transitioned from a monarchical system to a republic.  这个国家从君主制过渡到共和国。
The family held monarchical power for centuries.  这个家族拥有了几百年的君主权力。
Synonyms: regal, royal, sovereign, kingly, queenly
Antonyms: republican, democratic, egalitarian
词性: adjective
