A type of black tea from China, specifically the Wuyi Mountains, often considered a lower grade or coarser variety.

Bohea was once a popular tea in Europe. 武夷茶曾经在欧洲很受欢迎。

ETYMOLOGY->
From the Min Chinese pronunciation of 武夷 (Wuyi), the mountain range in Fujian, China, where the tea is grown.
