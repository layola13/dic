oenologists:/ˌiːˈnɒlədʒɪsts/ | n. | 葡萄酒学家；酿酒学家 | An expert in the science of winemaking, especially one who is concerned with the selection of grapes, their fermentation, and the proper aging of wine. | The oenologists at the vineyard carefully monitored the fermentation process. 葡萄园的酿酒学家仔细监测发酵过程。 |  Synonyms: winemaker, vintner | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
