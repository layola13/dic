nae:/neɪ/, /ne:/ | adj. | no (Scottish) |  A Scottish word meaning "no".  It's a more emphatic or informal way of saying no than simply using "no". |  "Are ye comin' tae the pub?" "Nae, I'm busy." 你要去酒吧吗？ 不，我很忙。| 近义词: no, nope | 反义词: aye (Scottish for yes), yes | 词性: adjective
