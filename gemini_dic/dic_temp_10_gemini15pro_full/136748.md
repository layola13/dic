nonsensuousnesses:/ˌnɒnˈsɛnsjʊəsnəsəz/ | n. | 无感觉；非感官性 | The state or quality of being nonsensuous; lack of appeal to the senses. |  His art explored nonsensuousnesses, focusing on abstract concepts rather than physical forms. 他艺术探索的是非感官性，专注于抽象概念而非物质形态。|  The philosopher discussed the nonsensuousnesses of pure thought. 这位哲学家讨论了纯粹思想的非感官性。 |近义词: immateriality, abstractness, incorporeality | 反义词: sensuality, physicality, materiality | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + sensuous (appealing to the senses) + -ness (forming nouns of quality or state) + -es (plural suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *sentire* "to feel".
