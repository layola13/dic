couvade:/kuːˈvɑːd/| n.| 父神妊娠，产翁幻痛，拟娩症候群| A custom in some cultures in which a man takes to his bed and undergoes ritual practices that imitate the symptoms of pregnancy and childbirth while his wife is pregnant or giving birth.  It refers to a range of sympathetic pregnancy symptoms experienced by the male partner of an expectant mother.| The anthropologist described the couvade rituals practiced by the tribe.  这位人类学家描述了该部落实行的产翁幻痛仪式。| Synonyms:  sympathetic pregnancy, false pregnancy, phantom pregnancy | Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, as it's a specific cultural phenomenon) | Noun
