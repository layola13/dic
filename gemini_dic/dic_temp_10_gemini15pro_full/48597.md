defect:/ˈdiːfekt/ (US) /ˈdɪːfekt/ (UK)| n. |缺点，缺陷，瑕疵|A shortcoming, imperfection, or lack in something.|The product was returned due to a manufacturing defect.  该产品因制造缺陷被退回。|近义词：flaw, imperfection, blemish, fault, bug|反义词：perfection, strength, virtue|noun

defect:/dɪˈfekt/ (US) /dɪˈfekt/ (UK)| vi. |叛逃，投敌|To abandon one's country or cause and join another, often opposing, one.|The soldier defected to the enemy side. 这名士兵叛逃到了敌方。|近义词：desert, abandon, forsake|反义词：support, defend, uphold|verb
