finishers:/ˈfɪnɪʃərz/| n.|完成者；终结者；整理工|People who complete something, especially a race or competition, or those who apply a final coat or treatment to a product.|The marathon finishers received medals. 马拉松完成者获得了奖牌。|近义词：completers, enders, finalizers|反义词: starters, beginners|noun

finishers:/ˈfɪnɪʃərz/| n.|精加工机; 整理机|Machines used in manufacturing to give products a final treatment or coating.|The factory uses laser finishers to engrave designs on metal parts. 工厂使用激光精加工机在金属部件上雕刻图案。|近义词：processing machines, finishing machines|反义词: N/A|noun
