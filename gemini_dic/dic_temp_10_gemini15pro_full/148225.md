oversilently:/ˌoʊvərˈsaɪləntli/
| adv. | 过于安静地，过分沉默地 |  Characterized by excessive silence, to a degree that is noticeable or unusual.  |  He moved oversilently through the house, trying not to wake anyone. 他过于安静地在房子里走动，尽量不唤醒任何人。 |  She oversilently accepted the news, her face betraying no emotion. 她过分沉默地接受了这个消息，脸上没有流露出任何情绪。 | 近义词：too quietly, excessively silently | 反义词：noisily, loudly | 词性:副词

词源:  "over-" (prefix indicating excess) + "silently" (adverb form of "silent").  "Silent" comes from Old French "silent," ultimately from Latin "silēre" (to be silent).
