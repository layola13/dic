angulating:/ˈæŋɡjʊleɪtɪŋ/
v.
使成角;弯曲;
Giving something an angle or bending it.
The surgeon was angulating the bone to set it properly.  外科医生正在使骨头成角以便正确复位。
The road angulated sharply to the left.  这条路向左急转弯。
近义词:bending, curving, inclining
反义词:straightening, flattening
词性:动词

angulated:/ˈæŋɡjʊleɪtɪd/
adj.
有角的;成角的;
Having angles or an angular shape.
The building had an angulated facade.  这座建筑有一个有角的正面。
The insect's legs were angulated.  昆虫的腿是弯曲的。
近义词:angled, bent, crooked
反义词:straight, smooth, unbent
词性:形容词


词源: From Latin *angulatus*, past participle of *angulare* "to make angular," from *angulus* "angle, corner."
