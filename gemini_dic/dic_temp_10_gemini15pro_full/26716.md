cadets:/kəˈdɛts/| n.|学员，军校学生|A person, especially a young man, who is training to become an officer in the armed forces or police.|The military academy trains hundreds of cadets each year.  军事学院每年训练数百名学员。|Example: The cadets marched in perfect formation.  学员们以完美的队形行进。|Synonyms: trainee, student, recruit|Antonyms: veteran, graduate, instructor|noun


cadet:/kəˈdɛt/| n.|（尤指年轻的）见习生，实习生|A young person who is training to do a particular job.|The company offers a cadet program for aspiring journalists. 这家公司为有抱负的记者提供见习项目。|Example:  He started as a police cadet and worked his way up to detective. 他从警校学员做起，一路晋升到侦探。|Synonyms: apprentice, intern, trainee|Antonyms: expert, professional, veteran|noun
