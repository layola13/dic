chaperon:/ˈʃæpəroʊn/ (US) /ˈʃæpərəʊn/ (UK)| n. vt.|陪伴人；监护人|A person, usually an older woman, who accompanies and supervises a young unmarried woman or a group of young people on social occasions.|例句：She acted as a chaperon to her younger sister at the school dance. 她在她妹妹的学校舞会上担任陪伴人。

例句：The teacher chaperoned the students on their field trip. 老师在学生的实地考察旅行中担任监护人。

|近义词：escort, attendant, guardian|反义词: N/A|词性: noun, verb
