boondoggle: /ˈbuːndɑːɡəl/ | n. | 浪费时间、金钱的无聊活动；华而不实的装饰品 | A project or activity that is wasteful or pointless but gives the appearance of having value. |  The construction of the new airport was seen as a massive boondoggle.  新机场的建设被视为巨大的浪费。| synonyms: waste, extravagance, frippery, gimmick, folly | antonyms: necessity, essential, requirement | noun

boondoggle: /ˈbuːndɑːɡəl/ | v. | 浪费时间做无意义的事情；制作华而不实的装饰品 | To engage in wasteful or pointless activity; to make something that is showy but useless. | The committee boondoggled for months without achieving anything concrete.  委员会浪费了几个月的时间，没有取得任何具体成果。| synonyms: waste time, dawdle, trifle, mess around | antonyms: work diligently, focus, concentrate | verb
