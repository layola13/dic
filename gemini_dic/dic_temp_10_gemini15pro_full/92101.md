hyperrhythmic:/ˌhaɪpərˈrɪðmɪk/ | adj. | 心律过速的，节律过快的 | Characterized by an abnormally rapid rhythm, especially of the heart.  Relating to or characterized by hyperrhythmia. |  The doctor diagnosed the patient with a hyperrhythmic heart condition. 医生诊断病人患有心律过速。|  His hyperrhythmic heartbeat was a cause for concern. 他过快的心跳令人担忧。| synonyms: tachycardic, fast, rapid | antonyms: bradycardic, slow | adjective
