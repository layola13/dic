entasia:/ɛnˈteɪʒə/ /ɛnˈteɪziə/
noun
内生菌，内生真菌
A fungus living within a plant without causing apparent disease.
The researchers discovered a new entasia in the leaves of the oak tree. 研究人员在橡树叶中发现了一种新的内生真菌。
Synonyms: endophyte, symbiotic fungus
Antonyms: pathogen, parasitic fungus
词性: 名词
