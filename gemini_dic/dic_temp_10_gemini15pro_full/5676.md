altruistically: /ˌæltruˈɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 无私地，利他地 | In an altruistic manner; with selfless concern for the well-being of others. | She altruistically donated her kidney to a stranger. 她无私地把自己的肾脏捐献给了一个陌生人。|  He altruistically volunteered his time to help the homeless. 他无私地奉献自己的时间去帮助无家可归的人。 | 近义词: selflessly, charitably, benevolently | 反义词: selfishly, egoistically | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From altruism + -ically.  Altruism is from French altruisme, coined by Auguste Comte from autrui "other people," from Old French autre "other." Ultimately from Latin alter "other."
