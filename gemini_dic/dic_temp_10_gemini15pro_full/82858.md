guller:/ˈɡʌlər/| n.|容易受骗的人；傻瓜|A person who is easily tricked or fooled. |He's such a guller, he believed everything she said.| 近义词: dupe, fool, sucker |反义词: skeptic, cynic |词性:名词

Etymology:
From the obsolete verb *gull* (to trick, cheat), which comes from the Old French *guiller*. Ultimately, it is believed to be related to words imitating the cries of seagulls, perhaps suggesting the ease with which these birds are sometimes deceived.
