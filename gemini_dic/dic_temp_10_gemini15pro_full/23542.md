braining:/ˈbreɪnɪŋ/
noun
动名词，名词
脑力激荡，思考，构思
The process of intense thinking or brainstorming.  The act of using one's brain, especially for creative or intellectual purposes.
We spent hours braining on the new project. 我们花了几个小时构思这个新项目。
synonyms: brainstorming, thinking, ideating, cogitating, pondering, contemplating
antonyms:  relaxing, idling, vegetating
noun


braining:/ˈbreɪnɪŋ/
verb
动词（非正式，罕用）
猛击头部，打伤头部
To hit someone on the head.
He brained him with a rock. 他用石头砸了他的头。
synonyms: bash, clobber, hit, strike
antonyms: heal, protect, defend
verb
