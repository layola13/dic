blester:/ˈblɪstər/| n. |水疱; 气泡; 起泡剂; (使)起泡|A blister is a small bubble on the skin that contains watery fluid and is caused by friction, burning, or other damage.|I got a blister on my heel from my new shoes. 我的新鞋把我的脚后跟磨出了水泡。|Synonyms: bleb, vesicle|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical blister)|noun


blester:/ˈblɪstər/| v. |(使)起泡; (使)起水疱; 猛烈批评; 痛斥|To blister means to form blisters or to cause blisters to form.|The hot sun blistered my skin.  炎热的太阳晒得我的皮肤起了水疱。|Synonyms:  scald, burn|Antonyms: soothe, heal|verb
