hastening:/ˈheɪsnɪŋ/| v. |加速，催促|Making something happen sooner or more quickly.  The ambulance was hastening to the scene of the accident.|例句：He was hastening to catch the last train. 他正赶着去赶最后一班火车。

例句：The project's completion was hastened by the arrival of additional workers.  更多工人的到来加快了项目的完成。|近义词：accelerating, speeding up, expediting, rushing|反义词：delaying, slowing down, hindering|词性: 动词 (present participle of hasten)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *hastenen*, from Old Norse *heista*, from Proto-Germanic *haistjaną* (“to urge on, incite”), from Proto-Indo-European *keyd- (“to excite, set in motion”). Cognate with Icelandic *heista* (“to hasten”), Swedish *hasta* (“to hasten”).
