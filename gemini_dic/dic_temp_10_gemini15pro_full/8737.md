antidepressant:/ˌæntiːdɪˈprɛsənt/ | n. adj. | 抗抑郁药；抗抑郁的 | A medication used to treat depression.  It works by affecting neurotransmitters in the brain. | She was prescribed an antidepressant to help manage her symptoms. 她被开了抗抑郁药来帮助控制她的症状。| The doctor recommended an antidepressant therapy. 医生建议进行抗抑郁治疗。 | Synonyms: mood elevator, mood stabilizer | Antonyms: depressant | noun, adjective
