fadable:/ˈfeɪdəbəl/| adj.|可褪色的，易褪色的|Likely to fade; capable of fading.  Something that is fadable is susceptible to losing its color or intensity over time, usually due to exposure to light or washing.|The dye in this fabric is fadable, so avoid leaving it in direct sunlight.  这种布料的染料容易褪色，所以避免阳光直射。|The vibrant colors of the mural are unfortunately fadable.  不幸的是，壁画鲜艳的色彩很容易褪色。|synonyms:bleachable, fading, transient, ephemeral|antonyms:permanent, indelible, fast (as in colorfast)|adjective
