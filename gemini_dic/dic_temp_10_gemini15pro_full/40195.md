conterminous:/kənˈtɜːrmɪnəs/ /ˌkɑːntərˈmɪnəs/
adjective
相邻的；接壤的；同一界限的
Sharing a common boundary or limit; bordering.
The conterminous United States excludes Alaska and Hawaii. 美国本土不包括阿拉斯加和夏威夷。
Synonyms: adjacent, bordering, contiguous, neighboring
Antonyms: distant, far, nonadjacent, separate
Etymology:  From Late Latin *conterminus, from Latin con- ‘together with’ + terminus ‘boundary, limit’.
