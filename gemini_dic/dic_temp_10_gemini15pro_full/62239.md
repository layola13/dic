encrusted:/ɪnˈkrʌstɪd/ | adj. | 镶嵌的；覆盖的；结壳的 | Covered with a hard crust or layer. | The old ship's hull was encrusted with barnacles.  老船的船体上覆盖着藤壶。| 近义词：coated, covered, caked | 反义词: clean, bare | 词性:形容词

encrusted:/ɪnˈkrʌstɪd/ |  v. (used as adj.) | 镶嵌；覆盖；结壳 | (Used as an adjective)  Having formed a crust or hard layer. | The encrusted jewels sparkled in the light. 镶嵌的珠宝在灯光下闪闪发光。| 近义词：coated, covered, caked | 反义词: clean, bare | 词性:动词(用作形容词)
