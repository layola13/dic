barel:/ˈbærəl/ | n. | 桶；枪管；一桶的量 | A cylindrical container with a flat bottom and typically a slightly bulging side, made of staves bound together with hoops, used for storing liquids or other substances.  Also, the metal tube of a gun through which the bullet or shell is fired. |  例句：The barel of oil is expensive these days.  这些日子油桶很贵。

例句：He cleaned the barel of his rifle. 他清理了他的步枪的枪管。| 近义词： cask, drum, keg, cylinder | 反义词: None | 词性: 名词

barrel:/ˈbærəl/ | v. |  高速行驶；飞奔 | To travel at a high speed. | 例句：The car barreled down the highway.  这辆车在高速公路上飞驰。

例句：He barreled through the crowd.  他飞快地穿过人群。 | 近义词： speed, rush, race, zoom | 反义词: crawl, creep | 词性: 动词
