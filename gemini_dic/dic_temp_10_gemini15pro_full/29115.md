cascaders:/kæsˈkeɪdərz/
| n. |  瀑布群；倾泻的人（或物）;级联器|A group of waterfalls; things or people that pour or flow downwards in stages, like a waterfall. |The cascaders of the mountain were a breathtaking sight. 山上的瀑布群景色壮丽。|近义词：waterfalls, series, chain, sequence |反义词: trickle, drip |词性:名词

cascader:/kæsˈkeɪdər/
| n. | 倾泻者；瀑布状物；级联放大器|Something that cascades; a person or thing that causes something to cascade; a type of electronic amplifier. |The flower cascader from the balcony created a beautiful display. 从阳台上倾泻而下的花朵构成了一幅美丽的景象。|近义词：waterfall, spillway, fountain |反义词: container, reservoir |词性:名词


Etymology:
From the verb "cascade" + "-er" (agent suffix), ultimately from Italian "cascata" (waterfall), from "cascare" (to fall), from Latin "cadere" (to fall).
