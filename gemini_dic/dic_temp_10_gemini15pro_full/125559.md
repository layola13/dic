multitasked: /ˌmʌltiˈtɑːskt/ /ˌmʌltiˈtæskt/ | v. adj.| 多任务处理的，执行多任务的 |  Performed multiple tasks at the same time.  Having the ability to perform multiple tasks concurrently. | She multitasked by answering emails while talking on the phone. 她一边打电话一边回复邮件，同时处理多项任务。|  He's a multitasked employee, capable of handling various projects simultaneously. 他是一位能够同时处理多个项目的多任务员工。| Synonyms: juggled, handled multiple things, performed several duties, worked on several things at once | Antonyms: single-tasked, focused on one thing | verb, adjective
