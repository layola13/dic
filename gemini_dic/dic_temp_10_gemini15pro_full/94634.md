impresser:/ɪmˈprɛsər/ | n. | 给人留下深刻印象的人；压印机 |  A person who makes a strong impression on others; a device used for impressing or printing. |  He was a real impresser at the job interview. 他在工作面试中给人留下了深刻的印象。 The printing press is a type of impresser. 印刷机是一种压印机。|  showman, influencer | wallflower, nobody | noun


impress:/ɪmˈprɛs/ | vt. | 使印象深刻；使铭记；盖印 | To affect deeply or strongly in mind or feelings; to fix firmly in the mind; to mark by pressure.  | The magician's tricks impressed the children. 魔术师的戏法给孩子们留下了深刻的印象。  She impressed the importance of the task upon him. 她让他铭记这项任务的重要性。|  affect, influence, imprint | bore, disappoint | verb
