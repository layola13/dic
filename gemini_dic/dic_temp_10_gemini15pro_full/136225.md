nonremittable:/ˌnɑːnrɪˈmɪtəbl/
adjective
不可汇款的，不可宽恕的
Not capable of being remitted or pardoned; unpardonable.
The judge declared the crime nonremittable and sentenced the defendant to life in prison. 法官宣布该罪行不可饶恕，判处被告终身监禁。
The company's debts were deemed nonremittable, forcing them into bankruptcy. 该公司的债务被认为是不可汇款的，迫使他们破产。
Synonyms: unpardonable, unforgivable, inexcusable
Antonyms: forgivable, pardonable, excusable
词性: adjective
