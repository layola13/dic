grater:/ˈɡreɪtər/| n.|磨碎器, 擦菜板|A kitchen utensil with a rough surface used for grating food.|I used a grater to shred the cheese for the pizza. 我用磨碎器把奶酪磨碎，用来做披萨。|近义词:shredder|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 grinder if 指的是磨成粉末)|词性:名词

grate:/ɡreɪt/| v.|磨碎, 擦碎|To rub food against a grater to reduce it to small shreds or powder. |She grated the carrots and added them to the salad. 她把胡萝卜磨碎，加到沙拉里。|近义词:shred, grind|反义词:(没有直接的反义词)|词性:动词
