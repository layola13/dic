counterdraw:/ˈkaʊntərˌdrɔː/
verb
反击；对抗性地模仿；以牙还牙
to draw in opposition or contrast; to imitate in a contrasting or opposing manner, often as a form of retaliation or competition.
He counterdrew his opponent's move with a clever strategy. 他用巧妙的策略反击了对手的行动。
Synonyms: retaliate, oppose, counteract, rival
Antonyms: cooperate, support, assist
词性: 动词
