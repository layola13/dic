nonseptate:/nɒnˈsɛpteɪt/
adjective
无隔膜的，不分隔的
Lacking septa or partitions.  A nonseptate fungus, for example, has hyphae that lack cross-walls.
例句：The nonseptate hyphae of the fungus allowed for rapid cytoplasmic streaming.  这种真菌的无隔膜菌丝允许快速的细胞质流动。

例句：Nonseptate fungal infections can be particularly aggressive.  无隔膜真菌感染可能特别具有侵略性。
近义词：aseptate, coenocytic
反义词：septate
词性：adjective
