hyperfemininities:/ˌhaɪpərˌfɛmɪˈnɪnɪtiz/| n. |极端女性化（特质、行为等）|Exaggerated or amplified representations and performances of femininity, often going beyond traditional gender norms.  It can be a conscious or unconscious performance, and can be used for various purposes, including empowerment, subversion, or conformity.|例句：The advertisement's hyperfemininities perpetuated harmful stereotypes.  该广告的极端女性化延续了有害的刻板印象。

例句：Her hyperfemininities were a performance designed to challenge societal expectations. 她的极端女性化是一种旨在挑战社会期望的表演。 |近义词：ultra-femininity, exaggerated femininity|反义词:masculinity, hypermasculinity|词性:noun (plural)
