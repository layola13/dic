gymnasiums: /dʒɪmˈneɪziəmz/ | n. |体育馆，健身房| A large room or building equipped for gymnastics, sports, or physical exercise. |  The school has two gymnasiums available for students. 学校有两个体育馆供学生使用。

The Olympic gymnasiums were state-of-the-art. 奥运会的体育馆是最先进的。 | 近义词：gym, fitness center, health club | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin gymnasium, from Greek gymnasion, from gymnazein "to train naked," from gymnos "naked."  Originally, a place for athletic exercises practiced naked.
