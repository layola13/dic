navigational:/ˌnævɪˈɡeɪʃənəl/| adj.|导航的；航行的|Of or relating to navigation; aiding or used in navigation. |例句：The ship's navigational equipment was state-of-the-art. 这艘船的导航设备是最先进的。|例句：Navigational errors can lead to disastrous consequences. 导航错误会导致灾难性的后果。|近义词：directional, guiding|反义词: aimless, undirected|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *navigationālis, from Latin navigātiō (“navigation”) + -ālis.  Ultimately from Latin navis (“ship”) + agere (“to drive, lead, act”).
