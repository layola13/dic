nonsuburbans:/ˌnɒn.səˈbɜːbənz/ |n.| 非郊区居民；居住在非郊区的人 |People who live outside of the suburbs, often associated with urban or rural areas. |  Many nonsuburbans prefer the vibrancy of city life. 许多非郊区居民更喜欢充满活力的城市生活。

The study focused on the lifestyle choices of nonsuburbans. 这项研究的重点是非郊区居民的生活方式选择。 | 近义词: urbanites, city dwellers, rural residents | 反义词: suburbanites | 词性: 名词

nonsuburban:/ˌnɒn.səˈbɜːbən/ | adj.| 非郊区的 | Not located in or characteristic of a suburb. |  They live in a nonsuburban area with easy access to both the city and the countryside. 他们住在非郊区，可以方便地前往城市和乡村。

The nonsuburban landscape was a mix of farmland and small towns. 非郊区的景观是农田和小城镇的混合体。| 近义词: urban, rural, exurban | 反义词: suburban | 词性: 形容词


词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "suburban" (relating to a suburb). The word "suburb" comes from the Latin "suburbium," meaning "an area close to a city."
