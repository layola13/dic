overreduction:/ˌoʊvərɪˈdʌkʃən/ | n. | 过度还原；超还原 | The process of reducing a chemical compound beyond the desired or intended point, often resulting in an undesired product. | The overreduction of the ketone resulted in the formation of an alcohol instead of the desired alkane.  过度还原酮导致形成了醇，而不是所需的烷烃。| over-reduction, excessive reduction | underreduction, insufficient reduction | noun

overreduction:/ˌoʊvərɪˈdʌkʃən/ | n. | 过度简化；过度缩减 | Simplifying something excessively, often to the point of misrepresenting or distorting the original. | The overreduction of complex scientific concepts in the textbook made them difficult to understand.  教科书中对复杂科学概念的过度简化使它们难以理解。| oversimplification, excessive simplification |  under-simplification | noun
