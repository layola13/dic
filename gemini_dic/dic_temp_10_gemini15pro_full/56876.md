dose:/doʊs/ (US) /dəʊs/ (UK)| n. vt.|剂量; 一服; 一剂; (尤指大量)给予，施加|A dose of medicine is a measured amount of it that is intended to be taken at one time.  一剂药是指一次服用的一定量的药。|The doctor prescribed a dose of antibiotics. 医生开了剂量的抗生素。|近义词：amount, quantity, portion|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 overdose 过量)|noun, verb
