descaled:/diːˈskeɪld/
adjective
除垢的，去除水垢的
Removed scale (a hard deposit of mineral salts) from.
The descaled kettle boiled water much faster.  除垢后的水壶烧水快多了。
近义词: cleaned, purged
反义词: scaled, encrusted
词性: adjective

descaled:/diːˈskeɪld/
verb
除垢，去除水垢
To remove scale from.
I descaled the coffee machine last week. 我上周清洗了咖啡机的水垢。
近义词: clean, purge
反义词: encrust
词性: verb
