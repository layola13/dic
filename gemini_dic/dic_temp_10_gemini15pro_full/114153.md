mahdiism:/mɑːˈdiːɪzəm/ | n. | 马赫迪主义；救世主论 | The belief in the coming of a Mahdi, a divinely guided leader who will restore justice and righteousness to the world, especially in Islam. |  Example 1: Mahdiism is a central tenet of Twelver Shia Islam. 十二伊玛目派什叶派伊斯兰教的核心教义是马赫迪主义。

Example 2:  The rise of Mahdiism led to several uprisings in the 19th century.  19世纪马赫迪主义的兴起导致了几次起义。 | 近义词: messianism | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists.  One could consider secularism or atheism as opposing ideologies.) | 词性: noun
