neutron:/ˈnjuːtrɒn/ (UK), /ˈnuːtrɑːn/ (US)|n.|中子|A subatomic particle of about the same mass as a proton but without an electric charge, present in all atomic nuclei except those of ordinary hydrogen.|Neutrons and protons make up the nucleus of an atom. 中子和质子构成原子的原子核。|The discovery of the neutron revolutionized nuclear physics. 中子的发现彻底改变了核物理学。|Synonym: neutral particle|Antonym: proton, electron|noun
