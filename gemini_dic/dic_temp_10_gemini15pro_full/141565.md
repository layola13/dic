opposites:/ˈɒpəzɪts/ | n. | 相反的事物；对立面 | things that are as different as possible from each other |  Heat and cold are opposites.  热和冷是相反的。 | synonyms: contraries, antitheses | antonyms: similar things, equivalents | noun

opposites:/ˈɑːpəzɪts/ | adj. |  相反的；对立的 | completely different | They have opposite views on the subject.  他们对这个问题的看法截然相反。 | synonyms: contrasting, conflicting | antonyms: same, identical | adjective
