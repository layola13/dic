bludgeoning:/ˈblʌdʒənɪŋ/ | n. | 棍棒殴打; 严厉批评 | The act of hitting someone repeatedly with a heavy object.  Also, a harsh or relentless verbal attack. | The witness described a brutal bludgeoning with a baseball bat. 目击者描述了用棒球棍进行的野蛮殴打。

The constant bludgeoning of negative news left her feeling depressed.  不断涌现的负面新闻让她感到沮丧。 | 近义词：beating, battering, pounding, lambasting, excoriating | 反义词：praising, commending | 词性：名词

bludgeon:/ˈblʌdʒən/ | v. | 棍棒殴打; 强迫; 严厉批评 | To hit someone repeatedly with a heavy object; to force someone to do something; to criticize harshly and relentlessly. | The victim was bludgeoned to death. 受害者被殴打致死。

He tried to bludgeon me into accepting his proposal. 他试图强迫我接受他的提议。

She bludgeoned him with accusations of incompetence. 她用指责他无能的言语来抨击他。 | 近义词：batter, beat, pummel, coerce, bully, lambaste | 反义词：persuade, coax, praise | 词性：动词
