ingoted:/ˈɪŋɡətɪd/
|adj.|铸成锭的；(喻)固定不变的|formed into an ingot (a block of metal); (figuratively) fixed and unchangeable|The ingoted gold shone brightly. 铸成金锭的黄金闪闪发光。|近义词：solidified, fixed, molded|反义词:molten, fluid, changeable
词性:形容词

ingoted:/ˈɪŋɡətɪd/
|v.|铸成锭|to form (metal) into an ingot|They ingoted the silver after refining it.  他们提炼完银子后，将其铸成银锭。|近义词：cast, mold, solidify|反义词:melt, liquefy
