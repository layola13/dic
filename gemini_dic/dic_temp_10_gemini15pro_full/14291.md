autokinesis:/ˌɔːtəʊkaɪˈniːsɪs/
|n.| 自身运动错觉|The apparent movement of a stationary point of light in the dark.|In the experiment, participants experienced autokinesis and reported the light moving, even though it was stationary. 在实验中，参与者经历了自身运动错觉，报告说光在移动，即使它实际上是静止的。|Example 2: Pilots flying at night sometimes experience autokinesis, which can be disorienting. 飞行员在夜间飞行有时会经历自身运动错觉，这可能会使他们迷失方向。|Synonyms: apparent motion, illusory motion, self-motion illusion|Antonyms: actual motion, real motion|noun
