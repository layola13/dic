molybdenous:/məˈlɪbdənəs/
adjective
含钼的；钼的
Containing or relating to molybdenum.
Molybdenous steel is a type of steel alloyed with molybdenum. 含钼钢是一种含钼的合金钢。
The molybdenous solution was used as a catalyst. 钼溶液用作催化剂。
近义词: molybdenum-containing
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "molybdenum-free")
词性: adjective
