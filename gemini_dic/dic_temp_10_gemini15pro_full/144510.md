outtraveled:/ˌaʊtˈtrævəld/
|v.| 旅行得比...多；比...更广博地游历 |Having journeyed more extensively than.| Example: She has outtraveled all her friends, visiting over 50 countries. 她游历过50多个国家，比她所有的朋友都多。 | Example: He felt outtraveled and out-experienced by his new colleagues. 他感觉他的新同事比他游历更广，经验更丰富。| Synonyms: out-journeyed, traveled more extensively than| Antonyms:  stayed put, remained local | Verb
