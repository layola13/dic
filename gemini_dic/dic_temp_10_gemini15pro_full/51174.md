desquamatory:/dɪˈskwɑːmətɔːri/
adjective
脱屑的，鳞屑状的
Characterized by or causing desquamation; peeling or shedding of the outer layer of skin.
The patient presented with a desquamatory rash on his hands and feet. 病人的手脚上出现了脱屑性皮疹。
desquamative, exfoliative, scaling
