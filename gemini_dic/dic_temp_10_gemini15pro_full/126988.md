nasal:/ˈneɪzl/ (US), /ˈneɪzəl/ (UK)| adj. n.|鼻的；鼻音的；鼻音|Relating to the nose; a sound produced by air passing through the nose.|
She has a nasal voice. 她嗓音带鼻音。

Her cold gave her a nasal tone.  她的感冒使她说话带鼻音。

The doctor examined his nasal passages. 医生检查了他的鼻腔。

He spoke with a nasal twang. 他说话带鼻音。

A nasal spray can help with congestion. 鼻喷雾可以帮助缓解鼻塞。


近义词:nose,  blocked, stuffy|反义词:oral|词性:adjective, noun
