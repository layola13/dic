conversing:/kənˈvɜːsɪŋ/
v.
交谈，谈话
engaging in conversation.
例句：They were conversing quietly in the corner. 他们在角落里安静地交谈。
例句：We spent a pleasant evening conversing about old times. 我们度过了一个愉快的夜晚，谈论着过去。
近义词：talking, chatting, discussing, speaking
反义词：silent, mute
词性：动词
