palmitoleic:/ˌpælməˈtoʊlɪk/ /ˌpælmɪˈtəʊlɪk/| adj.|棕榈油酸的；含有棕榈油酸的|An unsaturated fatty acid with 16 carbon atoms and one double bond.  It is found in various animal and vegetable fats and oils.|Macadamia nuts are a good source of palmitoleic acid.  澳洲坚果是棕榈油酸的良好来源。|Synonyms: hexadec-9-enoic acid (more technical/chemical name)|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific fatty acid.  Could consider saturated fatty acids as a broader contrasting category.)|adjective
