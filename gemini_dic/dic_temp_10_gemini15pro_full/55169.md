disseminated:/dɪˈsɛmɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
散布的，传播的
spread widely, distributed
The information was disseminated through the internet.  这个信息通过互联网传播开来。
The seeds are disseminated by the wind. 种子随风传播。
Synonyms: spread, distributed, circulated, broadcast, propagated
Antonyms: confined, restricted, suppressed, withheld
词性: 形容词
