cross-referring:/ˌkrɒs rɪˈfɜːrɪŋ/ | adj. | 交叉引用的，互相参照的 | Referring to another text or part of a text, typically given as a footnote or endnote. | This section is cross-referring to Chapter 3 for further explanation. 本节交叉引用了第三章以获得进一步的解释。| The author cross-referring to previous research to support his argument. 作者交叉引用了先前的研究来支持他的论点。 | synonyms: referencing, citing, alluding to | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "standalone" or "independent" in certain contexts) | adjective
