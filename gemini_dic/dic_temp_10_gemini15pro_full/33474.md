chylific:/kaɪˈlɪfɪk/ | adj. | 乳糜的；产生乳糜的 | Relating to chyle, a milky fluid consisting of fat droplets and lymph that is formed in the small intestine during digestion and taken up by lymph vessels. | The chylific vessels absorb fats from the small intestine. 乳糜管从小肠吸收脂肪。 | 近义词: chylous | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly consider terms relating to non-fat absorption) | 词性: adjective

chylification:/ˌkaɪlɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 乳糜化 | The process of forming chyle. | The chylification process is crucial for the absorption of dietary fats. 乳糜化过程对于膳食脂肪的吸收至关重要。 | 近义词: chyle formation | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
