nondescript:/ˌnɒndɪˈskrɪpt/ | adj. | 平凡的，毫无特色的，难以形容的 | Lacking distinctive or interesting features or characteristics; dull and unremarkable. | The building was a nondescript gray block. 这栋建筑是一座毫无特色的灰色街区。| The furniture was nondescript and old. 家具毫无特色，也很旧了。| 近义词：ordinary, unremarkable, commonplace, featureless, characterless | 反义词：distinctive, remarkable, unique, striking | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- +‎ descript, from Latin nōn (“not”) + dēscrīptus, past participle of dēscrībō (“describe”).
