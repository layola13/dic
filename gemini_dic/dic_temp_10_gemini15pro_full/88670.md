hoarstone:/ˈhɔːrstəʊn/| n. |界石，边界石|A stone placed as a boundary marker, often ancient and covered in moss or lichen.  It carries a sense of age and historical significance.|例句：The ancient hoarstone marked the boundary between the two kingdoms. 这块古老的界石标志着两个王国之间的边界。

例句：The hikers discovered a moss-covered hoarstone deep in the woods.  徒步旅行者在树林深处发现了一块覆盖着苔藓的界石。|近义词：boundary stone, landmark, milestone|反义词:|名词
