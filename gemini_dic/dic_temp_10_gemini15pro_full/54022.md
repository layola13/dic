discursion:/dɪˈskɜːrʒən/
noun
漫谈，离题，讨论
a turning aside (as from a main subject) in discourse; a digression
The professor's lecture was filled with discursions, making it hard to follow the main thread. 教授的讲座充满了离题的讨论，让人很难跟上主线。
The book contains many interesting discursions on related topics. 这本书包含许多关于相关主题的有趣讨论。
近义词:digression, excursion, rambling
反义词:focus, conciseness, brevity
词性:n.
