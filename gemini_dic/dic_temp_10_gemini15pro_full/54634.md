dismissing:/dɪsˈmɪsɪŋ/
v.
解雇;解散;驳回;不予理会;消除;挥去
Letting go, rejecting, or putting aside.
The company is dismissing several employees due to budget cuts. 公司由于预算削减正在解雇几名员工。
He dismissed the idea as ridiculous. 他认为这个想法很荒谬，不予理会。
synonyms: rejecting, discharging, firing, ignoring
antonyms: hiring, accepting, embracing, considering
verb
