lawyers:/ˈlɔɪərz/
n.
律师（复数）
Legal professionals who advise and represent clients in legal matters.
The lawyers presented their arguments in court. 律师们在法庭上陈述了他们的论点。
attorneys, solicitors, barristers
(无常用反义词)
