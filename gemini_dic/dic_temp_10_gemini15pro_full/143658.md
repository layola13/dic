outl:/ˈaʊtlaɪn/ | n. vt.|轮廓，概要，大纲；概述，略述| An outline is a general description or plan giving the essential features of something but not the detail. |The president gave a broad outline of his plans for the economy. 总统概述了他对经济的计划。| synonyms: summary, abstract, synopsis, sketch, draft | antonyms: detail, specifics | noun

outl:/ˈaʊtlaɪn/ | vt.|概述，略述；勾勒…的轮廓| To outline something is to give a summary of it. |She outlined the benefits of the insurance scheme. 她概述了这项保险计划的好处。| synonyms: summarize, sketch, delineate | antonyms: detail, elaborate | verb
