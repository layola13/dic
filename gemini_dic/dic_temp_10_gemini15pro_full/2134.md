adaptiveness:/əˌdæpˈtɪvnəs/| n. |适应性，适应能力|The adaptiveness of this species is remarkable, allowing it to thrive in diverse environments.  这个物种的适应性非常强，使其能够在不同的环境中繁衍生息。|例句：The company demonstrated great adaptiveness in responding to the changing market.  这家公司在应对变化的市场方面表现出了极大的适应性。

The adaptiveness of the human brain is what allows us to learn and grow throughout our lives. 人类大脑的适应性使我们能够终身学习和成长。|近义词：flexibility, plasticity, resilience, adaptability|反义词：rigidity, inflexibility|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From adapt (v.) + -ive (suffix forming adjectives) + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives).  Adapt comes from Latin adaptare "to fit, adjust," from ad "to" + aptare "to fit."
