capacitor:/kəˈpæsɪtər/ | n. | 电容器 | A device used to store an electric charge, consisting of one or more pairs of conductors separated by an insulator. |  The capacitor in the circuit stores energy. 电路中的电容器储存能量。| synonyms: condenser | antonyms: N/A | noun

capacitors:/kəˈpæsɪtərz/ | n. | 电容器（复数） | Plural form of capacitor.  |  The circuit board contains numerous capacitors.  电路板包含许多电容器。| synonyms: condensers | antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *capacitatem*  (capacity),  from *capax* (able to hold).  The term "condenser" was used earlier, but "capacitor" is now standard.
