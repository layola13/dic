original:/əˈrɪdʒənəl/ | adj. n. |原始的; 原创的; 原件; 原作|Something that is original is the first one of its kind, and has not been copied or imitated from something else.  An original piece of work, such as a painting, play, or piece of music, is produced by the artist and not a copy. An original is the first, true, or best form of something.|例句：This is an original painting by Van Gogh. 这是梵高的原作。|近义词：initial, primary, first, innovative, creative|反义词：copied, imitated, fake, unoriginal|词性:形容词, 名词

originals:/əˈrɪdʒənəlz/ | n. |原件; 原作; (尤指艺术作品、文件等的) 原版|The plural form of "original," referring to the first, authentic versions of something, especially artwork, documents, or recordings.|例句：The museum keeps the originals in a climate-controlled vault.  博物馆将原件保存在一个气候控制的保险库中。|近义词：first editions, master copies, prototypes|反义词：copies, reproductions, duplicates|词性:名词
