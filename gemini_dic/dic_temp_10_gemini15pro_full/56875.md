dosages:/ˈdoʊsɪdʒɪz/
noun
剂量；用量
the size and frequency of a dose of a medicine or drug.
The doctor prescribed her a high dosage of painkillers. 医生给她开了大剂量的止痛药。
synonyms: dose, amount, measure, quantity
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps underdose or insufficient amount)
n.
