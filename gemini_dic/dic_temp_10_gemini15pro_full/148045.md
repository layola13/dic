overrules:/ˌoʊvərˈruːlz/ /ˌəʊvəˈruːlz/
verb
推翻；否决；驳回
to reject a decision, especially a legal decision, made by someone with less authority than you
The judge overruled the objection. 法官驳回了异议。
overturn, reject, disallow, override
accept, approve, uphold
