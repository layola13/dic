nonlegitimate:/ˌnɒnlɪˈdʒɪtɪmət/
adj.
非法的；不合法的；私生的
not authorized by the law; not in accordance with accepted standards or rules; born of parents not married to each other

例句：
The government declared the election nonlegitimate.
政府宣布选举无效。

The business was involved in nonlegitimate activities.
这家企业参与了非法活动。

He discovered he was the nonlegitimate son of a wealthy businessman.
他发现自己是一个富商的私生子。


近义词：illegal, unlawful, illicit, bastard (for offspring)
反义词：legal, legitimate, lawful

词性: adjective
