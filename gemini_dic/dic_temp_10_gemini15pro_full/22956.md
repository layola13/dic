bottom-feeder:/ˈbɑːtəm ˌfiːdər/ | n. |底层食客; 食腐动物; 卑鄙小人|A creature that feeds on organic matter on the bottom of a body of water.  Figuratively, it refers to a person who exploits others or engages in unethical activities, often for personal gain. |例句：The catfish is a bottom-feeder, scavenging for food in the mud. 鲶鱼是一种底层食客，在泥里寻找食物。

例句：He was known as a bottom-feeder in the business world, always looking for a quick and easy profit. 在商界，他被认为是一个卑鄙小人，总是想快速轻松地获利。|近义词：scavenger, parasite, opportunist |反义词: philanthropist, benefactor |词性:noun


bottom-feeder:/ˈbɑːtəm ˌfiːdər/ | adj. |卑鄙的;  下等的|Describing something low or undesirable, often in a moral or ethical sense. |例句：He used bottom-feeder tactics to win the election. 他用卑鄙的伎俩赢得了选举。

例句：The company engaged in bottom-feeder pricing, undercutting all its competitors. 该公司采取低价策略，削弱了所有竞争对手。|近义词：unethical, unscrupulous, exploitative |反义词: ethical, principled |词性:adjective
