flexing:/ˈfleksɪŋ/| v. |炫耀；展示；弯曲；活动（关节或肌肉）|showing off or displaying something, often in a boastful or ostentatious way; bending or moving (a joint or muscle).|He was flexing his biceps in the mirror. 他对着镜子展示他的肱二头肌。|Synonyms: showing off, boasting, displaying, flaunting, bending, stretching|Antonyms: hiding, concealing, contracting, stiffening|verb

flexing:/ˈfleksɪŋ/| n. |炫耀；展示；弯曲；活动（关节或肌肉）|the act of showing off, displaying something boastfully, or bending/moving a joint or muscle.|His constant flexing on social media is annoying. 他在社交媒体上不断炫耀很烦人。|Synonyms: show-off, display, boast, flaunting, bending, stretching|Antonyms: hiding, concealing, contraction, stiffening|noun

词源: From the verb "flex," which comes from the Latin "flectere" meaning "to bend."
