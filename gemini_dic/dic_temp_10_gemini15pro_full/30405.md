centinody: /sɛntɪˈnoʊdi/
noun
百周年纪念; 百年期
A period of one hundred years; a centennial.
The nation celebrated its centinody with grand festivities.  这个国家以盛大的庆祝活动来庆祝其百年纪念。
The museum displayed artifacts from the past centinody.  博物馆展出了过去一百年的文物。
