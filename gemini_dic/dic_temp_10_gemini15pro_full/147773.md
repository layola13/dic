overpromised: /ˌoʊvərˈprɑːmɪst/ | adj. | 被过度承诺的 | Having been promised more than can or will be delivered. | The project is overpromised and under-delivered. 这个项目承诺过高，交付不足。 | Synonyms: overcommitted, overstated, exaggerated | Antonyms: underpromised, understated | adjective
