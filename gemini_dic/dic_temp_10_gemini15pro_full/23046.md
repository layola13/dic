bounce's:/ˈbaʊnsɪz/| n.|弹跳，反弹;活力，精力|The bounce's of the ball was impressive. 球的弹跳力令人印象深刻。|近义词：resilience, spring, energy, vitality|反义词：lethargy, dullness|词性:名词

词源：From Middle English *bunsen, *bossen (“to beat, strike, knock”), probably frequentative of bouncen (“to strike, knock”), from Old French bondir (“to resound, leap, jump”), from Frankish *buntōn (“to beat”), from Proto-Germanic *buntaną (“to beat, strike”). Cognate with Old High German buntōn (“to beat”), Middle Dutch bontsen (“to beat, strike”), Old Norse bólginn (“swollen”).
