overpersecute:/ˌoʊvərˈpɜːrsɪkjuːt/| vt. |过度迫害|To persecute excessively or beyond reasonable limits.|The religious minority group was overpersecuted by the ruling regime, leading to many fleeing the country. 这个少数宗教团体受到执政当局的过度迫害，导致许多人逃离该国。

The government was accused of overpersecuting political dissidents. 政府被指控过度迫害政治异议人士。|近义词：persecute, oppress, harass|反义词:protect, support, defend|词性:动词

词源:
From over- + persecute.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," and "persecute" comes from the Latin word "persequi," meaning "to follow persistently," "to pursue," or "to harass."
