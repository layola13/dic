nonpermanences:/ˌnɒnˈpɜːmənənsɪz/
n.
非永久性，暂时性
The nonpermanence of life is a constant reminder to cherish every moment.  生命的短暂性不断提醒我们要珍惜每一刻。
Life is full of nonpermanences, from the changing seasons to our own fleeting existence.  人生充满了无常，从四季的变化到我们自身短暂的存在。
Synonym: transience, temporariness, impermanence
Antonym: permanence, eternity, perpetuity
noun
