loaminess:/ˈloʊminəs/| n.|壤土性；肥沃；|The loaminess of the soil made it ideal for growing vegetables. 土壤的肥沃使其成为种植蔬菜的理想选择。|English Explanation: The quality of being loamy; resembling or containing loam.  Loam is a fertile soil of clay and sand containing humus.|例句：The gardener praised the loaminess of the soil. 园丁称赞了土壤的肥沃。

例句：The loaminess of the river valley supported a thriving agricultural community.  河谷肥沃的土壤支撑着一个繁荣的农业社区。|近义词：fertility, richness, fecundity|反义词：sterility, barrenness|词性:noun


loamy:/ˈloʊmi/| adj.|肥沃的；壤土的；|The loamy soil was perfect for the new seedlings.  肥沃的土壤非常适合新的幼苗。|English Explanation: Consisting of or resembling loam; fertile.|例句：The farmer was pleased with the loamy texture of his fields.  农民对他的田地肥沃的质地感到满意。

例句：Loamy soil is ideal for growing a wide variety of crops. 壤土非常适合种植各种各样的作物。|近义词：fertile, rich, productive|反义词：infertile, barren, sterile|词性:adjective


词源:
From loam + -y/-ness.  Loam comes from Middle English *lom, from Old English lām "clay, loam," of uncertain origin.
