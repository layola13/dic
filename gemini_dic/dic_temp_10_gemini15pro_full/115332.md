mannite:/ˈmænɪtaɪt/| n. |甘露醇|A sweet, crystalline alcohol, C6H14O6, derived from mannose or fructose and found in many plants, such as seaweed, olives, and manna. It is used in medicine as a diuretic and a laxative.|Mannitol is often used to reduce swelling in the brain. 甘露醇常用于减轻脑肿胀。|The doctor prescribed mannitol to treat the patient's cerebral edema. 医生开了甘露醇来治疗病人的脑水肿。|Synonyms: manna sugar, hexitol|Antonyms: None|Noun
