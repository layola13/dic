outbragging: /ˌaʊtˈbræɡɪŋ/
verb
夸耀；吹嘘；比…更会吹牛
The act of boasting or bragging more excessively than someone else.
He was outbragging his colleagues about his new car. 他对同事吹嘘他的新车，比他们都厉害。
Synonyms: outdoing, surpassing, exceeding, topping
Antonyms: downplaying, minimizing, understating
词性: 动词 (gerund or present participle)
