matured:/məˈtʃʊərd/ /məˈtʃʊrd/ | v. | （使）成熟，（使）酿熟 (past participle of mature) | to become fully developed or ripe; to reach a desired or final condition | The wine has matured in oak barrels for six months.  这葡萄酒在橡木桶中陈酿了六个月。| Synonyms: age, ripen, develop | Antonyms:  decay, rot, regress | verb
