loop:/luːp/| n. v. |环，圈；循环；弯曲；|A shape produced by a curve that bends round and crosses itself. |The road went in a big loop round the lake. 这条路绕着湖形成了一个大环。|近义词：circle, ring, coil, spiral|反义词:line, straight|名词
loop:/luːp/| v. |使成环；以环连接；使翻筋斗；|To make something into a loop or loops. |Loop the rope around the pole. 把绳子绕在杆子上。|近义词：circle, enclose, surround|反义词:uncoil, straighten|动词

词源:
From Middle Dutch *lōpe, from Old French loupe (“magnifying glass”).  The verb sense is derived from the noun. 最初指放大镜，后来引申为环状物，动词含义由名词衍生而来。
