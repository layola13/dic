grangerite:/ˈɡreɪndʒəˌraɪt/ | n. | 格兰杰石 | A pink, hydrous sodium iron silicate mineral, NaFe³⁺₂Si₈O₂₂(OH)₂.  It is typically found in metamorphic rocks. | Grangerite was discovered in a quarry in the French Alps.  格兰杰石是在法国阿尔卑斯山的一个采石场发现的。 |  Synonyms: None specifically, as it's a specific mineral.  Could be broadly grouped with other silicate minerals. | Antonyms: N/A | noun
