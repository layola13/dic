outworking:/ˌaʊtˈwɜːrkɪŋ/ | n. | 外加工;胜过;过度工作 |The act of working more or harder than someone else, or the product of this extra work. | The factory relies heavily on outworking to meet production demands. 这家工厂严重依赖外加工来满足生产需求。|  The quality of her outworking is truly exceptional. 她外包工作的质量真的很出色。| synonyms: outsourcing, subcontracting, externalization | antonyms: insourcing, internalization | noun

outworking:/ˌaʊtˈwɜːrkɪŋ/ | v. | 比…更努力工作;胜过 |To work harder or more effectively than someone else. | She outworked everyone on the team and achieved the highest sales figures.  她比团队里的任何人都更努力，并取得了最高的销售额。| He consistently outworks his competitors, which explains his success. 他一直比他的竞争对手更努力地工作，这解释了他的成功。| synonyms: outdo, outperform, surpass | antonyms: underperform, lag behind | verb
