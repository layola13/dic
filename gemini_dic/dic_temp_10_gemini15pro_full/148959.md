ovidian:/əˈvɪdiən/| adj.|奥维德式的，情爱的，好色的|Of or relating to the Roman poet Ovid or his works, especially his love poetry; characterized by sensual or amorous themes.|Her ovidian poetry explored the complexities of love and desire. 她的奥维德式诗歌探索了爱与欲望的复杂性。|近义词：amorous, erotic, sensual|反义词：chaste, platonic|词性:形容词
Etymology: From the name of the Roman poet Publius Ovidius Naso (43 BC – AD 17/18), known for his works on love and mythology.
