euhemerism:/juːˈhiːmərɪzəm/| n.|神话历史化；理性解释神话|The interpretation of myths as traditional accounts of historical persons and events.|Many scholars see euhemerism in the portrayal of King Arthur as a real historical figure.|近义词：rationalization, demythologization|反义词:mystification|noun
