leptocardia:/ˌlɛptəˈkɑːrdiə/ | n. |  无头类，也称为狭心纲 |A subphylum of invertebrate chordates comprising the lancelets, characterized by a body plan that resembles a simplified fish.|  Lancelets, members of the Leptocardia, are important for studying the evolution of vertebrates. 无头类动物，狭心纲的成员，对于研究脊椎动物的进化非常重要。 | synonym: Cephalochordata | antonym: Vertebrata | noun
