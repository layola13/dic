molarities:/məˈlærɪtiz/| n.|摩尔浓度(复数)|The concentration of a solution expressed in moles of solute per liter of solution.  It's a measure of how many solute molecules are present in a given volume of solution.|The molarities of the different solutions were carefully calculated. 不同溶液的摩尔浓度都经过仔细计算。|近义词：concentrations|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑dilution稀释)|词性:名词

molarities:/məˈlærɪtiz/| n.|摩尔浓度(复数)|The plural form of "molarity." Refers to the concentrations of multiple solutions, each expressed in moles of solute per liter of solution.|We compared the molarities of the acid and base solutions. 我们比较了酸溶液和碱溶液的摩尔浓度。|近义词：concentrations|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑dilution稀释)|词性:名词
