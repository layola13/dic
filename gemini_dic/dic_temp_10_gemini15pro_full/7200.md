angle:/ˈæŋɡəl/ (US), /ˈæŋɡl/ (UK)| n. v.|角，角度；观点，立场|A figure formed by two lines diverging from a common point.  A particular way of approaching or considering an issue.|The two walls met at a 90-degree angle. 这两面墙呈90度角相交。

He tried to approach the problem from a different angle. 他试图从不同的角度来处理这个问题。|近义词：perspective, viewpoint, standpoint|反义词: curve|noun

angle:/ˈæŋɡəl/ (US), /ˈæŋɡl/ (UK)| v. |钓鱼；谋取，设法得到|To fish with a hook and line. To try to obtain something indirectly or by devious means.|He angled for a promotion by flattering his boss. 他通过奉承老板来谋求升职。

She was angling for an invitation to the party. 她设法想得到参加聚会的邀请。|近义词：fish, seek, scheme|反义词: give|verb
