disesteemer:/ˌdɪsɪˈstiːmər/| n. |轻视者，不尊重者|One who disesteems or holds in low regard.  A person who lacks respect or esteem for someone or something.| He was a disesteemer of traditional values, preferring a more modern approach. 他轻视传统价值观，更喜欢现代方法。| 近义词: critic, detractor, scornful person | 反义词: admirer, respecter, supporter | 词性: noun

词源:  From dis- (expressing negation) + esteem (to respect).
