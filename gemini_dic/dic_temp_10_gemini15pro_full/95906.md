incremation:/ˌɪnsɪnəˈreɪʃən/ |/ˌɪn.sɪ.nəˈreɪ.ʃən/| n. |火化，焚化|The act of burning a dead body to ashes. |例句：Cremation is becoming an increasingly popular choice. 火化正成为越来越流行的选择。

例句：The family chose cremation over burial. 这家人选择了火化而不是土葬。


近义词：burning, incineration |反义词: burial |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *incrematio*, from incremat- (past participle stem of incremare ‘burn up’), from in- ‘into, completely’ + cremare ‘to burn’.
