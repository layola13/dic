concussion:/kənˈkʌʃən/| n.|脑震荡; 震动; 激烈的震荡|A concussion is a temporary injury to the brain caused by a blow to the head. | He suffered a concussion after falling off his bike. 他从自行车上摔下来后脑震荡。|Synonyms: impact, shock, blow|Antonyms: stability, stillness|noun

concussion:/kənˈkʌʃən/| n.|震荡; 冲击; 震动|The concussion from the explosion broke windows in nearby buildings. 爆炸的冲击波震碎了附近建筑物的窗户。|The building suffered structural damage from the concussion. 该建筑因震动而遭受结构性损坏。|Synonyms: tremor, vibration, jolt|Antonyms: calmness, quiet|noun
