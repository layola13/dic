nebulize:/ˈnɛbjʊˌlaɪz/ | v. | 喷雾；雾化 | To convert (a liquid) into a fine spray. | The doctor nebulized the medication for the patient to inhale.  医生将药物雾化以便病人吸入。| Synonyms: atomize, vaporize, spray | Antonyms: condense, solidify | verb

nebulize:/ˈnɛbjʊˌlaɪz/ | n. | 喷雾；雾化（名词形式较少用）| The act of converting a liquid into a fine spray. | The nebulize of the medicine created a fine mist.  药物的雾化产生了一种细雾。 (用法较少见) | Synonyms: atomization, vaporization, spraying | Antonyms: condensation, solidification | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
