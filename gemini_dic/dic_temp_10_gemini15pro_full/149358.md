pachymeter:/pæˈkɪmɪtər/| n. |角膜厚度计|An instrument for measuring the thickness of the cornea.|The ophthalmologist used a pachymeter to measure the patient's corneal thickness. 眼科医生使用角膜厚度计测量病人的角膜厚度。|The doctor used the pachymeter before the LASIK procedure. 医生在LASIK手术前使用了角膜厚度计。|Synonyms: corneal thickness gauge|Antonyms: N/A|noun
