misinformed:/ˌmɪsɪnˈfɔːrmd/| adj.|被误导的；错误信息的|Given incorrect or misleading information.|Many people are misinformed about the dangers of vaccination. 很多人被误导了关于疫苗接种的危险性。|Synonyms: misled, deceived, misguided|Antonyms: informed, enlightened, educated|adjective


misinformed:/ˌmɪsɪnˈfɔːrmd/| adj.|被误导的；错误信息的|Given incorrect or misleading information.|The article was poorly researched and misinformed the public. 这篇文章研究得很差，误导了公众。|Synonyms: misled, deceived, misguided|Antonyms: informed, enlightened, educated|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + inform (meaning "to give knowledge of something"). The word "inform" comes from Old French enformer, from Latin informare, meaning "to shape, train, instruct."
