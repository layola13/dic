dungy:/ˈdʌndʒi/ | adj. | 肮脏的，昏暗的，破旧的 | Dark, dirty, and dilapidated; gloomy and depressing. |  The room was small and dungy, with peeling wallpaper and a musty smell.  这房间又小又脏，墙纸剥落，还有一股霉味。

The abandoned factory was a dungy, dangerous place. 这家废弃的工厂是一个肮脏危险的地方。| 近义词：grimy, squalid, dingy, shabby, seedy | 反义词：clean, bright, pristine, immaculate | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *dungi*, from *dung* (“animal excrement”) + *-i* (adjectival suffix).
