honeying:/ˈhʌniɪŋ/ | v. | 甜言蜜语；奉承；（加蜜使）变甜 |  To speak sweetly and flatteringly to someone, often in a way that is not sincere. Also, to add honey to something, making it sweet. | He was honeying her with compliments, hoping to get a promotion.  他甜言蜜语地恭维她，希望能升职。| 近义词: flattering, sweet-talking, cajoling | 反义词: criticizing, insulting | 词性: 动词
