muskadel:/ˈmʌskədɛl/ /ˈmʌskədəl/
n.
麝香葡萄酒;  Muscat, a sweet dessert wine.
A sweet, aromatic wine made from muscat grapes.  It typically has flavors of orange blossom, apricot, and honey.

Muskadel is a luscious dessert wine, perfect with fruit and cheese.
麝香葡萄酒是一种甜美的餐后甜酒，与水果和奶酪搭配完美。

The vineyard is known for its award-winning muskadel.
这家葡萄园以其屡获殊荣的麝香葡萄酒而闻名。


近义词:Muscat, Moscato
反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific type of wine, perhaps consider "dry wine" as a contrasting style)


词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Dutch *muskadel*, from Middle French *muscadelle*, from Italian *moscadello*, ultimately from Latin *muscus* ("musk").  The name refers to the musky aroma of the grapes.
