blaze-away:/bleɪz əˈweɪ/| phrasal verb |连续射击，猛烈攻击，大声说话，痛骂|To shoot continuously or rapidly; to speak or criticize forcefully and angrily.|The soldiers blazed away at the enemy. 士兵们向敌人猛烈开火。|近义词：fire away, let fly, lash out|反义词: be quiet, hold back |词性:动词短语

blaze-away:/bleɪz əˈweɪ/| phrasal verb |开始; (尤指) 开始工作|To begin something, especially to begin working. | He blazed away at the piano, surprising everyone with his skill. 他开始弹钢琴，以其精湛的技艺让所有人大吃一惊。|近义词：start, begin, commence|反义词: stop, finish, end |词性:动词短语
