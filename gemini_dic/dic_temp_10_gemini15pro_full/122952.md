mommie:/ˈmɑːmi/ (US) /ˈmʌmi/ (UK)| n.|妈咪（孩子对母亲的称呼）|An informal, childish way of saying "mommy" or "mother."  It's a term of endearment used by young children.|例句：Mommie, can I have a cookie? 妈咪，我可以吃块饼干吗？

例句：The little girl ran to her mommie for a hug. 小女孩跑到她妈妈那里求抱抱。

近义词：mommy, mama, mother, mum (UK)|反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider terms like "father," "dad" depending on context)|词性:noun

词源:  "Mommie" is a variant of "mommy," which is a childish form of "mama."  Ultimately, these words derive from the sounds babies typically make when trying to call for their mothers.  These sounds are similar across many languages, leading to similar words for "mother" in many different cultures.
