moray:/ˈmɔːreɪ/ /məˈreɪ/ | n. |海鳝|A large voracious eel of warm seas, with strong sharp teeth.|例句：The moray eel lurked in the coral reef, waiting to ambush its prey. 海鳝潜伏在珊瑚礁中，等待伏击猎物。

例句：Divers should be cautious around morays, as their bite can be dangerous. 潜水员在海鳝周围应该小心，因为它们的咬伤可能很危险。|近义词：eel, conger|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a specific type of fish)|词性:noun
