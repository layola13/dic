catoptron:/kəˈtɒptrən/ | n. | 反射镜，镜 | A polished surface, especially of metal, that forms images by reflection.  |  Ancient civilizations used catoptrons made of bronze. 古代文明使用青铜制成的反射镜。 | synonym: mirror, reflector | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps absorber?) | noun
