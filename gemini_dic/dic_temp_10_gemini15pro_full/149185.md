oxygen:/ˈɒksɪdʒən/ (UK) /ˈɑːksɪdʒən/ (US)| n.|氧，氧气|A colorless, odorless gas that is essential for respiration in most living organisms.|Plants produce oxygen during photosynthesis. 植物在光合作用过程中产生氧气.|
The fire needs oxygen to burn. 火需要氧气才能燃烧.|synonyms:  air (loosely), O2 |antonyms:  anoxia, hypoxia|noun
