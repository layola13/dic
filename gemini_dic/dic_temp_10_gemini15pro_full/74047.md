fossane:/ˈfɒseɪn/ (UK) /fɑːˈseɪn/ (US)| n. |马岛獴，隐香狸|A carnivorous mammal (Cryptoprocta ferox) native to Madagascar, resembling a mongoose but belonging to a separate family (Eupleridae). It is the largest mammalian carnivore on the island.|Fossanes are nocturnal hunters, preying on lemurs and other small animals. 马岛獴是夜间活动的猎手，捕食狐猴和其他小型动物。|Near Synonyms: Malagasy mongoose, falanouc|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific animal.  Could use "herbivore" in a broader ecological context.)|noun
