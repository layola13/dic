backups:/ˈbækʌps/| n.|备份; 候补队员; 支持者; 后援; 拥堵; 备份副本|A backup is a copy of data or software that is made to protect against loss or damage.  Backups can also refer to supporting players on a sports team or people who provide assistance or support.|例句：I make regular backups of my computer files. 我定期备份我的电脑文件。

例句：The team has several good backups in case of injuries.  这个队伍有一些优秀的替补队员，以防出现伤病。|近义词：copies, reserves, duplicates, substitutes|反义词: originals|词性:noun
