loin:/lɔɪn/ | n. | 腰肉；(牛、羊等)腰部；(书籍)里脊 | The part of the body on either side of the spine between the lowest ribs and the hip bones, or the equivalent part in an animal.  Also, a cut of meat from this part of an animal. |  He injured his loin in the accident. 他在事故中伤了腰部。  The butcher prepared a beautiful loin of pork. 肉贩准备了一块漂亮的猪里脊肉。 | synonyms:  back, flank, haunch | antonyms:  head, limb | noun

loin:/lɔɪn/ | v. |  (旧) 渴望；贪恋 (archaic) | To desire strongly; to crave. | He loined for his lost love. 他渴望他失去的爱人。 (archaic usage) | synonyms: yearn, crave, desire, long for | antonyms:  abhor, detest, dislike | verb
