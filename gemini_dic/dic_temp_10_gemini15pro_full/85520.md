hat-in-hand:/ˌhæt ɪn ˈhænd/
adjective
低声下气地；谦卑地；恳求地
humbly and submissively, especially when requesting a favor.
He went hat-in-hand to his father, begging for another loan. 他低声下气地去找他父亲，请求再借点钱。
近义词: humbly, submissively, pleadingly, beseechingly
反义词: arrogantly, proudly, demandingly
词性: adjective


hat-in-hand:/ˌhæt ɪn ˈhænd/
adverb
低声下气地；谦卑地；恳求地
in a humble and submissive manner, especially when requesting a favor.
He went hat-in-hand to the bank manager, hoping to secure a loan.  他低声下气地去找银行经理，希望能获得贷款。
近义词: humbly, submissively, pleadingly, beseechingly
反义词: arrogantly, proudly, demandingly
词性: adverb

词源:  This idiom derives from the physical act of removing one's hat as a sign of respect or subservience, particularly when approaching someone of higher social standing to ask for something.  Holding one's hat in hand further emphasizes the supplicant's humility and dependence.
