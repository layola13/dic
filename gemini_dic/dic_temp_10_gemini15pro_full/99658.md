interlent:/ˈɪntərˌlent/ | adj. | 交互的，相互的 |  Existing or occurring between; reciprocal.  Interlent describes something shared or exchanged mutually. It implies a dynamic interaction or relationship. This word is archaic and rarely used in modern English. |  The two groups established an interlent agreement. 这两个团体建立了相互协议。 |  reciprocal, mutual, interactive |  one-sided, unilateral | adjective
