amphiarthrosis:/ˌæmfiɑːrˈθroʊsɪs/ (US), /ˌæmfiɑːˈθrəʊsɪs/ (UK)| n. | 微动关节 |A type of joint that permits slight movement, such as the joints between vertebrae.|
The amphiarthrosis between the vertebrae allows for flexibility in the spine.|
近义词: slightly movable joint|
反义词: diarthrosis (freely movable joint), synarthrosis (immovable joint)|
词性: noun


Etymology:
From Greek *ἀμφί* (amphi, “on both sides”) + *ἄρθρον* (arthron, “joint”) + *-osis*.
