druidish:/ˈdruːɪdɪʃ/ | adj. | 德鲁伊的；像德鲁伊的；德鲁伊教的 | Relating to Druids or their practices; characteristic of Druids. |  The ancient stones had a druidish feel. 这些古老的石头有一种德鲁伊的感觉。| Near Synonyms: Celtic, pagan, ancient, mystical | Antonyms: modern, contemporary | adjective

druidish:/ˈdruːɪdɪʃ/ | n. | 德鲁伊教 | The doctrines or practices of the Druids. | He was fascinated by druidish and its connection to nature. 他对德鲁伊教及其与自然的联系着迷。| Near Synonyms: Druidism | Antonyms: Christianity, Islam (as examples of organized religions) | noun
