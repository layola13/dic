例句：The spread of the virus proved to be a noncontrollable factor in the global economy.  事实证明，病毒的传播是全球经济中一个不可控的因素。|近义词：uncontrollable, unmanageable, inoperable|反义词：controllable, manageable, operable|词性:形容词
