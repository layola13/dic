overbrown:/ˈoʊvərbraʊn/| adj.| 烤得过焦的，过度褐变的|Overcooked to the point of becoming excessively brown.|The toast was overbrown and tasted burnt.  吐司烤得过焦，尝起来有烧焦的味道。| The steak was slightly overbrown but still juicy inside. 牛排有点烤过头了，但里面还是多汁的。| 近义词：burnt, overcooked, scorched| 反义词：undercooked, underdone|词性:形容词


词源：
"over-" (prefix) + "brown" (adjective/verb)
"over-" 表示“过度”，“超过”。
"brown"  表示“褐色的”或“变成褐色”。
