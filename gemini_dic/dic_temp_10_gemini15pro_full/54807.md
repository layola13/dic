disperse:/dɪˈspɜːrs/ (US) /dɪˈspɜːs/ (UK)| v. |分散，驱散|To scatter or distribute something over a wide area.|The police dispersed the crowd. 警察驱散了人群。|The seeds are dispersed by the wind. 种子被风吹散。|synonyms: scatter, distribute, dissipate, spread|antonyms: gather, collect, assemble, concentrate|verb

disperse:/dɪˈspɜːrs/ (US) /dɪˈspɜːs/ (UK)| adj. |分散的|Spread over a wide area.|The refugees are now dispersed throughout the country. 难民现在分散在全国各地。|The population is quite dispersed in this rural area.  这个农村地区的人口相当分散。|synonyms: scattered, distributed, dissipated, spread|antonyms: concentrated, clustered, gathered|adjective


Etymology: From Latin *dispergere*, from dis- "apart" + spargere "to scatter".
