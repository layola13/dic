heroes:/ˈhɪəroʊz/ (US) /ˈhɪərəʊz/ (UK)|n.|英雄们|Plural form of "hero," referring to more than one person admired for their courage, outstanding achievements, or noble qualities.|例句：The heroes of the battle were honored for their bravery. 战斗中的英雄们因其勇敢而受到表彰。

例句：Many children dream of becoming heroes when they grow up. 许多孩子梦想长大后成为英雄。|近义词：champions, idols, legends|反义词：villains, cowards|词性:名词

heroess:  This is not a standard English word.  The correct plural of "hero" is "heroes."
