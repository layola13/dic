bee-line:/ˈbiː laɪn/| n.|直线，捷径|A straight, direct course.  The shortest route between two points.|He made a bee-line for the refrigerator as soon as he got home. 他一回家就直奔冰箱。|synonyms: direct route, shortcut, beeline|antonyms: detour, indirect route|noun

bee-line:/ˈbiː laɪn/| v.|走直线，直奔|To go directly and quickly towards something or someone.|She bee-lined straight for the manager to complain. 她径直去找经理投诉。|synonyms: head straight for, make a beeline for, dash to|antonyms: meander, wander|verb
