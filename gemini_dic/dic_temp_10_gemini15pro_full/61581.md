emergency:/ɪˈmɜːrdʒənsi/ (US) /ɪˈmɜːdʒənsi/ (UK)| n.|紧急情况；突发事件|A serious, unexpected, and often dangerous situation requiring immediate action.|There was a medical emergency on the plane.  飞机上出现了医疗紧急情况。

The heavy rain caused a state of emergency. 暴雨造成了紧急状态。|近义词：crisis, exigency, difficulty|反义词：normality, routine|词性: noun
