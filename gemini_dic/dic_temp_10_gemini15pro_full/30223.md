celliferous:/səˈlɪfərəs/
adjective
产生细胞的；含细胞的
Containing cells; producing cells.
例句：Celliferous tissue is composed of living cells.  含细胞组织由活细胞组成。

例句：The celliferous structure of the plant was examined under a microscope.  植物的细胞结构在显微镜下进行了检查。

近义词：cellular, cell-bearing
反义词：acellular
词性：adjective
