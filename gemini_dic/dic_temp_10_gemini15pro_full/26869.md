cajole:/kəˈdʒoʊl/ /kəˈdʒoʊl/
verb
哄骗，劝诱
persuade someone to do something by sustained coaxing or flattery.
He cajoled her into agreeing to the plan. 他哄骗她同意了这个计划。
She cajoled him into buying her a new dress. 她劝诱他给她买了一件新裙子。
近义词: coax, persuade, wheedle, entice
反义词: coerce, force, threaten
词性: verb
词源: From French cajoler 'to chatter like a jay,' from Old French gaiole 'cage,' referring to the bird's chattering.
