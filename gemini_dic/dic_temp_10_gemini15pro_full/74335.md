fragment:/fræɡˈment/| v.|使成碎片，破碎|To break or cause to break into fragments.|The explosion fragmented the glass into tiny pieces. 爆炸把玻璃炸成了碎片。|近义词：shatter, break, splinter|反义词：mend, repair, join|verb
