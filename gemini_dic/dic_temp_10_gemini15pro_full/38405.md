concern:/kənˈsɜːrn/ (US), /kənˈsɜːn/ (UK)| n. vt.| 关心，担忧；涉及；关系到 |A matter of importance or worry to someone.  It can also mean to relate to or involve someone or something.|The safety of the children is my main concern. 孩子们的安全是我最主要的关切。|近义词：worry, anxiety, interest, regard, involve, affect|反义词：indifference, apathy|noun

concern:/kənˈsɜːrn/ (US), /kənˈsɜːn/ (UK)| vt.| 使担忧；使关心；关系到 |To cause worry or anxiety to someone. It can also mean to be the business or affair of someone or something.|The news of the layoffs concerned everyone in the company. 裁员的消息让公司里的每个人都很担心。|近义词：worry, bother, affect, involve|反义词：reassure, comfort|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *concerner, from Latin *concernere, from con- "together" + cernere "to sift, separate, decide."  The original sense was "to sift together," hence "to be of importance to."
