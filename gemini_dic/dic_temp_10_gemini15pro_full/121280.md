misfeasance:/ˌmɪsˈfiːzəns/
|n.|不当行为；渎职|The lawyer was sued for misfeasance.  这位律师因渎职而被起诉。|An act that is legal but performed improperly. |The councilor was accused of misfeasance in public office.  这位议员被指控在公职中行为不当。|malfeasance, misconduct, wrongdoing|correct conduct, proper administration|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Anglo-French *mesfaisance, from mes- "wrongly" + faisant "doing," present participle of faire "to do," from Latin facere "to make, do."
