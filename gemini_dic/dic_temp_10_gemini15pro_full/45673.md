curtation:/kɜːrˈteɪʃn/ |n.|屈膝礼|A gesture of respect or greeting made by women and girls, consisting of a bending of the knees and a lowering of the body.|She made a deep curtsy to the queen. 她向女王深深地行了一个屈膝礼。|近义词：bow, genuflection|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly standing erect)|noun
