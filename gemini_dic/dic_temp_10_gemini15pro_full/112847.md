luckier:/ˈlʌkiər/ | adj. | 更幸运的 | Comparative form of "lucky," meaning having more good fortune.  |  She was luckier than him, winning the lottery twice. 她比他更幸运，两次中了彩票。 |  Example:  I felt luckier today than yesterday. 我今天感觉比昨天更幸运。 | Synonyms: more fortunate, more blessed | Antonyms: unluckier, less fortunate |  adjective
