What is he cooking for dinner? = 他晚饭在做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she been up to lately? = 她最近在忙什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been reading recently? = 你最近一直在读什么？
What has he been working on all week? = 他整个星期一直在做什么？

What did you do last night? = 你昨晚做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

Past Continuous:

Past Perfect:

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么，这么长时间？
