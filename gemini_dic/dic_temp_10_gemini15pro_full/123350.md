monolithic:/ˌmɒnəˈlɪθɪk/ | adj. | 巨大的，单一的，整体的 | Formed of a single large block of stone.  Also, (figuratively) large, powerful, and indivisible. |  Example: The company's monolithic structure made it slow to adapt to change.  公司庞大单一的结构使其难以适应变化。 | Example:  The monolithic statue dominated the landscape. 巨大的雕像俯瞰着整个景观。 | Synonyms: massive, huge, inflexible, rigid, unified | Antonyms: small, flexible, adaptable, fragmented, diverse | word property: adjective
