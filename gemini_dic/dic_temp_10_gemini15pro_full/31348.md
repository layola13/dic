characteristics:/ˌkærəktəˈrɪstɪks/ | n. | 特征，特性 |  A distinguishing feature or quality, especially one that helps to identify a person or thing. |  One of the characteristics of this breed of dog is its long, silky coat. 这种犬种的特征之一是它又长又柔顺的毛。 | traits, features, attributes | opposites, differences | noun

characteristics:/ˌkærəktəˈrɪstɪks/ | adj. |  典型的，特有的 | Showing the qualities or features that are typical of a particular person, thing, or group. | This painting is characteristic of his early work. 这幅画是他早期作品的典型代表。 | typical, representative, distinctive | atypical, unusual | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *characteristicus, from Greek kharaktēristikos, from kharaktērizein "to engrave, characterize," from kharaktēr "engraving tool, distinctive mark, character," from kharassein "to scratch, engrave."
