disownments:/dɪsˈoʊnmənts/
n.
断绝关系；否认；抛弃
The act of refusing to acknowledge or accept something or someone as one's own.
His disownment of his family shocked everyone. 他与家人断绝关系震惊了所有人。
近义词: renunciations, repudiations, rejections
反义词: acknowledgments, acceptances, embraces
词性: 名词 (plural)

词源:  From disown + -ment.  Disown itself comes from the Old French desavouer, meaning "to deny."  The prefix "dis-" indicates negation or reversal.
