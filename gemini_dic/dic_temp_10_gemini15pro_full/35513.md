coadventure:/kəʊədˈvɛntʃər/ | n. | 共同冒险，合资经营 | A joint or shared adventure; a partnership in a risky undertaking. |  They embarked on a coadventure to explore the uncharted territories. 他们一起踏上了探索未知领域的冒险之旅。

| The two companies formed a coadventure to develop new software. 两家公司合资开发新软件。 | 近义词: joint venture, collaboration, partnership | 反义词: solo venture, independent undertaking | 词性: 名词


Etymology:
From co- (together) + adventure.  The prefix "co-" comes from the Latin "cum" meaning "with" or "together". "Adventure" comes from Old French "aventure" ultimately from Latin "adventura" meaning "a thing about to happen."
