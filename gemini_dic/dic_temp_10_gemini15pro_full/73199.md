force-outs:/ˈfɔːrs aʊts/ | n. | 强制出局 | The act of forcing a base runner out, especially in baseball. | The shortstop made a quick throw to second for the force-out.  游击手快速传球到二垒，强制出局。| synonyms: putout, out | antonyms: safe, advance | noun

force-outs:/ˈfɔːrs aʊts/ | n. | 强迫退场；强制离职 | The act of forcing someone to leave a position or job. |  There have been several force-outs at the company due to the restructuring.  由于公司重组，已有数人被迫离职。| synonyms: dismissal, discharge, ousting | antonyms: hiring, appointment | noun
