gloomily:/ˈɡluːmɪli/| adv.|忧郁地，闷闷不乐地|In a gloomy manner; sadly, dejectedly.  Expressing or reflecting gloom.|He sat gloomily in the corner, staring at the floor. 他忧郁地坐在角落里，盯着地板。|近义词：sadly, dejectedly, morosely, glumly|反义词：happily, cheerfully, brightly|词性:副词
词源: From *gloom* + *-ly*.  *Gloom* comes from Middle English *glom*, meaning "twilight, dusk," likely from Old Norse *glāmr*, "moonlight."
