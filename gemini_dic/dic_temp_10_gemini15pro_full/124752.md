mres:/ɛmˈrɛz/ | n. | 研究型硕士学位 (Master of Research) | A postgraduate degree that focuses primarily on research, typically involving a substantial research project and dissertation, rather than taught courses.  It's a common pathway to a PhD. |  Example: She's pursuing an MRes in marine biology. 她正在攻读海洋生物学研究型硕士学位。

Example: An MRes provides excellent training for a research career. 研究型硕士学位为研究生涯提供了极好的训练。 | Synonym: Master of Science by Research, MSc(Res), Research Master's | Antonym:  N/A | noun
