entropically:/ˌɛntrəˈpɪkəli/| adv.|熵增地，熵增式地|In a way that relates to or involves entropy, typically characterized by increasing disorder or randomness.|The system degrades entropically over time.  这个系统随着时间的推移熵增式地退化。|Example 2: Information spreads entropically through the network. 信息在网络中熵增式地传播。|Synonyms: randomly, chaotically, disorderedly|Antonyms: orderly, systematically, structured|Part of Speech: Adverb

entropy:/ˈɛntrəpi/| n.|熵|A measure of disorder or randomness in a system.|The entropy of the universe is constantly increasing. 宇宙的熵在不断增加。|Example 2:  The closed system reached a state of maximum entropy.  这个封闭系统达到了最大熵的状态。|Synonyms: disorder, randomness, chaos|Antonyms: order, structure, organization|Part of Speech: Noun
