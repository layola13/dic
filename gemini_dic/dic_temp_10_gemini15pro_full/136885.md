nonsociable:/ˌnɒnˈsəʊʃəbl/ /ˌnɑːnˈsoʊʃəbl/| adj.|不爱交际的，孤僻的|Not inclined to socialize; unsociable.|He was a nonsociable man who preferred to spend his evenings alone. 他是一个不爱交际的人，喜欢独自度过夜晚。|She's become quite nonsociable since the incident. 自从那件事发生后，她变得相当孤僻。|近义词：unsociable, introverted, withdrawn, reclusive|反义词：sociable, gregarious, outgoing, extroverted|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From non- + sociable.  "Sociable" comes from Late Latin *sociabilis, from Latin sociare "to associate," from socius "companion."
