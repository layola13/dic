maniacally:/məˈnaɪəkəli/ | adv. |疯狂地，狂热地 | In a wild or violently insane manner; like a maniac. | He laughed maniacally and ran off into the night. 他疯狂地大笑，然后跑进夜色中。|  She maniacally scrubbed the floor until it shone. 她狂热地擦洗地板，直到它闪闪发光。 | 近义词：frenziedly, wildly, insanely, hysterically | 反义词：calmly, rationally, sanely | 词性: 副词

词源:  From maniac + -ally.  Ultimately from Greek *manikos* “mad, insane”
