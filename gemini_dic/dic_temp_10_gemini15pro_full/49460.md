delude:/dɪˈluːd/| v. |欺骗，哄骗，迷惑|To make someone believe something that is not true.|He deluded himself into thinking he could win the lottery. 他欺骗自己，以为自己能中彩票。|Synonyms: deceive, mislead, trick, fool|Antonyms: enlighten, inform, undeceive|Verb

delude:/dɪˈluːd/| v. |欺骗，哄骗，迷惑|To cause someone to have a false belief.|The con man deluded many people into investing in his fake company.  这个骗子欺骗许多人投资他的假公司。|Synonyms: beguile, dupe, hoodwink|Antonyms: disabuse, disillusion|Verb
