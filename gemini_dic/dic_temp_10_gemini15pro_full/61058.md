hellebore:/ˈhɛlɪˌbɔːr/ (US), /ˈhɛlɪbɔː(r)/ (UK)| n.|嚏根草；圣诞玫瑰|A perennial plant of the buttercup family, typically having dark green, leathery leaves and bowl-shaped flowers in winter or early spring.  It is known for its toxicity and some species are used medicinally.|例句：The hellebores bloomed beautifully even in the cold winter. 即使在寒冷的冬天，嚏根草也开得很漂亮。

例句：She planted hellebores in her garden for winter interest.  她在花园里种植了嚏根草，以便在冬天增添趣味。|近义词：winter rose, Lenten rose (for certain species)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *ellebor, from Old French elebore, from Latin elleborus, from Ancient Greek ἔλλεβορος (élleboros).  The ultimate origin is uncertain, but may be related to a word meaning "food that injures".
