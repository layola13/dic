nondogmatic:/ˌnɒnˈdɒɡˈmætɪk/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˈdɔːɡˈmædɪk/| adj.|非教条的，不武断的|Not adhering rigidly to a set of beliefs or doctrines; open to different ideas and opinions.|She has a nondogmatic approach to teaching. 她有一种非教条式的教学方法。|The scientist presented his findings in a nondogmatic way, open to discussion and further research.  这位科学家以非教条的方式展示了他的发现，愿意接受讨论和进一步研究。|近义词：open-minded, tolerant, flexible, liberal, receptive|反义词：dogmatic, rigid, inflexible, doctrinaire|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + dogmatic.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "dogmatic" comes from the Greek "dogma," meaning "opinion, belief, or tenet."
