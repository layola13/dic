eccentrically:/ɪkˈsɛntrɪkli/ /ɛkˈsɛntrɪkli/| adv.|古怪地；反常地；不同寻常地|In a strange or unusual way, deviating from the norm. |She dressed eccentrically, favoring brightly colored scarves and hats. 她穿着古怪，喜欢鲜艳的围巾和帽子。|The old house was eccentrically decorated with mismatched furniture and bizarre artwork.  这栋老房子装饰得很古怪，家具不配套，艺术品也很奇特。|近义词：oddly, strangely, peculiarly, bizarrely, unusually|反义词：normally, conventionally, typically|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From eccentric + -ly.  "Eccentric" comes from Late Latin eccentricus, from Greek ekkentros "out of the center," from ek- "out of" + kentron "center."
