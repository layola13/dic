coffeehouse:/ˈkɔːfiːhaʊs/ (US) /ˈkɒfiːhaʊs/ (UK)| n. |咖啡馆，咖啡屋|A place where coffee and other beverages are sold, often providing a social environment for conversation and relaxation.|例句：The coffeehouse is a popular meeting spot for students. 这家咖啡馆是学生们聚会的热门地点。

例句：We spent hours chatting in the cozy coffeehouse. 我们在这个舒适的咖啡馆里聊了几个小时。|近义词：cafe, coffee shop, tearoom |反义词: bar, pub |词性: noun

Etymology:
From coffee + house.  The word "coffee" itself ultimately comes from the Arabic "qahwah".
