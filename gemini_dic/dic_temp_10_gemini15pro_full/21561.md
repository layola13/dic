blueprints:/ˈbluːˌprɪnts/
noun
蓝图，设计图
a detailed plan or drawing that shows how something is to be made
The architects presented the blueprints for the new stadium. 建筑师展示了新体育场的蓝图。

blueprints:/ˈbluːˌprɪnts/
noun
方案，计划
a plan or scheme
The government unveiled its blueprint for economic reform. 政府公布了其经济改革方案。


Etymology: blue + print. Originally, blueprints were a type of photographic print used for copying technical drawings, characterized by white lines on a blue background.


Synonyms: plan, design, scheme, draft, outline
Antonyms:  improvisation, randomness
