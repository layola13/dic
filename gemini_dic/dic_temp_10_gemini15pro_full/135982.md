nonradicalness:/ˌnɒnˈrædɪkəlnəs/
n.|非激进性；温和性|The nonradicalness of his proposals surprised many. 他的提议的温和性让很多人感到惊讶。|The government opted for a policy of nonradicalness in its approach to the economic crisis. 政府在应对经济危机时选择了一种温和的政策。|近义词:moderateness, mildness, conservatism|反义词:radicalism, extremism|词性:名词

Etymology:
non- + radical + -ness

non-:  prefix meaning "not" (from Latin *non)
radical: from Late Latin *radicalis "of or pertaining to the root," from Latin radix (genitive radicis) "root"
-ness: suffix forming nouns from adjectives, denoting a state or quality.
