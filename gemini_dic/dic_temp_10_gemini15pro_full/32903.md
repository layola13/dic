choo:/tʃuː/| v.| 咀嚼；细细品味；认真思考|To chew something slowly and carefully, often to enjoy the flavor or to think about something carefully.|The cow was chooing on its cud.  牛正在咀嚼反刍的食物。

He sat chooing over the problem for hours. 他坐着苦苦思索这个问题几个小时。|近义词：chew, mull over, ponder, contemplate|反义词:gobble, gulp|词性:动词
