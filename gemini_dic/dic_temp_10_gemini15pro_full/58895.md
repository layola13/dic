durga:/ˈdʊrɡə/| n.|杜尔迦，难近母|Durga is a major Hindu goddess, also known as Devi or Shakti.  She is a warrior goddess, often depicted riding a lion or tiger and wielding multiple weapons. She is revered as the protector of the righteous and the destroyer of evil.|例句：Durga Puja is a major festival in India. 杜尔迦节是印度的一个重要节日。

例句：Durga is often depicted with ten arms. 杜尔迦通常被描绘成有十只手臂。|近义词：Devi, Shakti, Parvati|反义词:None|词性:noun
