extravaganza:/ɪkˌstrævəˈɡænzə/
n.
盛大表演；铺排；盛宴
a large, elaborate, and spectacular entertainment or production.
例句：The opening ceremony was a dazzling extravaganza of music and dance. 开幕式是一场令人眼花缭乱的音乐和舞蹈盛宴。

例句：They put on a musical extravaganza for the visiting dignitaries. 他们为来访的贵宾举办了一场盛大的音乐表演。

近义词：spectacle, show, performance, celebration, fete, festival, gala, pageant
反义词：simplicity, modesty, understatement

词性: noun
