obstructed:/əbˈstrʌktɪd/
adjective
阻塞的，堵塞的
blocked, closed, or difficult to get through
The road was obstructed by a fallen tree. 道路被一棵倒下的树挡住了。
近义词: blocked, clogged, impeded, hindered
反义词: clear, open, unobstructed
词性: adjective

obstruct:/əbˈstrʌkt/
verb
阻碍，阻塞，妨碍
to block or close up by an obstacle
The protesters obstructed the entrance to the building. 抗议者堵住了大楼的入口。
近义词: block, impede, hinder, prevent
反义词: aid, assist, help, facilitate
词性: verb
