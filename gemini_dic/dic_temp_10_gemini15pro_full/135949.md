nonpurposiveness:/ˌnɒnˈpɜːpəsɪvnɪs/ | n. | 无目的性；非目的性 | Lack of purpose or intention; the state of not being directed towards a specific goal. |  His art embraced nonpurposiveness, focusing on the process rather than the outcome. 他的艺术拥抱无目的性，专注于过程而不是结果。

| Example: The nonpurposiveness of the child's play was charming.  这孩子的玩耍的无目的性很可爱。| Synonym: aimlessness, purposelessness | Antonym: purposefulness, intentionality | noun
