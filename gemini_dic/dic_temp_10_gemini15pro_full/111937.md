loller:/ˈlɒlə(r)/ (UK) /ˈlɑːlər/ (US)| n.|傻笑的人；狂笑的人|Someone who laughs a lot, often in a loud or uncontrolled way.  A person who lolls (is lazy or idle).  Historically, a vagrant or idle wanderer.|He's a real loller, always joking around. 他真是个爱笑的人，总是开玩笑。|Synonyms: laugher, guffawing person, idler, lounger|Antonyms: serious person, hard worker|noun

loll:/lɒl/ (UK) /lɑːl/ (US)| v.|懒洋洋地躺着；闲逛；耷拉|To sit or stand in a relaxed or lazy way; to hang loosely; to droop.|The dog lolled on the rug in the sun. 狗懒洋洋地躺在阳光下的地毯上。  Her head lolled against the window. 她的头靠在窗户上耷拉着。|Synonyms: lounge, recline, slouch, droop, dangle|Antonyms: stand erect, sit upright|verb
