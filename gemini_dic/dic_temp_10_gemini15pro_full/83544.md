Hadrianus: /ˌheɪdriˈeɪnəs/ | n. | 哈德良 | Roman emperor (AD 76-138), known for building Hadrian's Wall. | Hadrianus was one of the Five Good Emperors. 哈德良是五贤帝之一。 | Synonyms: Publius Aelius Hadrianus | Antonyms: N/A | Noun


Etymology:

From Latin *Hadrianus*, derived from the Roman family name *Hadrianus*.  Ultimately of uncertain origin, possibly related to the name of the town of *Hadria* in northern Italy.
