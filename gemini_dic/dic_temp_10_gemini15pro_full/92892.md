idealizes:/aɪˈdiːəlaɪzɪz/
verb
理想化，将…理想化
regards or represents as perfect or better than in reality.
She idealizes her childhood. 她将她的童年理想化了。
Synonyms: romanticize, glorify, exalt, extol
Antonyms: vilify, denigrate, disparage, criticize
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From ideal + -ize.  "Ideal" comes from Late Latin idealis "existing in idea," from idea. "-ize" is a suffix forming verbs from nouns and adjectives, meaning "to make into," "to treat as," "to cause to resemble."
