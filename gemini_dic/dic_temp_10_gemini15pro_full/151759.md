pasha:/ˈpæʃə/ | n. | 帕夏；巴夏；高级官员 | A high-ranking Turkish official or dignitary, historically often a provincial governor. |  He lived like a pasha in his opulent mansion. 他在他的豪华宅邸里过着像帕夏一样的生活。| Synonyms: dignitary, official, nobleman | Antonyms: commoner, peasant | noun

pashaw:/pəˈʃɔː/ | n. | 帕夏；巴夏；（尤指傲慢的）要人 |  A person of high rank or importance, especially one who is arrogant or overbearing (often used humorously or ironically). |  He strutted around like a pashaw, expecting everyone to cater to his whims. 他像个帕夏一样趾高气扬地走来走去，期望每个人都迎合他的突发奇想。| Synonyms: grandee, magnate, VIP | Antonyms: nobody, commoner | noun
