militation:/ˌmɪlɪˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 影响；作用（通常是不利的）| The act of fighting against something; counteracting influence. |  His poor health militated against his chances of promotion.  他的健康状况不佳，影响了他的晋升机会。|  近义词：opposition, resistance, detriment, hindrance | 反义词：support, advantage, benefit | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *militātiōn*, from Latin *militāre* ("to serve as a soldier") + *-tiōn*.  Ultimately from Latin *miles* ("soldier").
