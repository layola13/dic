decagynia:/dekəˈɡɪniə/
n.
十雌蕊
Having ten pistils or styles.
This rare flower exhibits decagynia, a fascinating botanical feature.  这种珍稀的花朵展现了十雌蕊，这是一个迷人的植物学特征。
Near Antonyms:monogynia, digynia, polygynia
Etymology: From Greek *deka* (ten) + *gyne* (woman, pistil)
