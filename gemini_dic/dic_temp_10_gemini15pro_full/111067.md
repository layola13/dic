listeningless: /ˈlɪsənɪŋləs/
adjective
漠不关心的；不倾听的；不愿倾听的
Unwilling or unable to listen; inattentive.
He gave a listeningless ear to her pleas. 他对她的恳求充耳不闻。
Synonyms: inattentive, unheeding, indifferent, deaf
Antonyms: attentive, listening, receptive, heedful
词性: 形容词
