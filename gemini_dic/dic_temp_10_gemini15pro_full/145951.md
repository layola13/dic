overdiscusses:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈskʌsɪz/
verb
过度讨论
to discuss something too much or for too long
They overdiscussed the issue and failed to reach a conclusion. 他们过度讨论了这个问题，未能达成结论。
The committee overdiscussed the budget and wasted valuable time. 委员会过度讨论了预算，浪费了宝贵的时间。
近义词: overanalyze, belabor
反义词: skim, ignore
词性: verb
