looper:/ˈluːpər/ (US), /ˈluːpə(r)/ (UK)| n.|循环器，环形器；[电影]环形使者|A device that plays a looped recording. In the film "Looper," it refers to an assassin who kills victims sent back in time.|The guitarist used a looper pedal to create layered sounds. 吉他手使用循环效果器来创建分层的声音。|The film "Looper" explores the paradoxes of time travel. 电影《环形使者》探讨了时间旅行的悖论。|synonyms: cycler, repeater|antonyms: N/A|noun
