efflate:/ɪˈfleɪt/
verb
放气;泄气;使萎缩
To deflate or cause to collapse, especially by letting out air or gas.
The balloon slowly efflated after the party.  派对结束后，气球慢慢地瘪了。
The doctor efflated the patient's lung to perform surgery. 医生为了进行手术，给病人的肺部放了气。
近义词:deflate, collapse, shrink
反义词:inflate, expand
词性:v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *efflatus*, past participle of *efflare* ("to blow or breathe out"), from *ex* ("out") + *flare* ("to blow").
