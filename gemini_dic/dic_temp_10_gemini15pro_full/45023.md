ctenoid:/ˈsiːtnɔɪd/| adj.|栉状的，栉鳞的|Relating to or denoting scales that have a comblike edge, characteristic of most bony fishes.|The perch has ctenoid scales.|近义词：scaly, imbricate|反义词:smooth, cycloid|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Greek *ktenoeidēs* (comb-like), from *kteis* (comb) + *-oeidēs* (like).
