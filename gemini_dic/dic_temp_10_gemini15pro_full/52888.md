diminutiveness:/dɪˈmɪnjətɪvnəs/ | n. | 微小，渺小 |The state or quality of being extremely small. |The diminutiveness of the hummingbird makes it a wonder of nature. 蜂鸟的微小使其成为自然界的奇迹。| synonyms: smallness, tininess, minuteness, littleness | antonyms: largeness, hugeness, vastness, immensity | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From diminutive + -ness.  Diminutive comes from Late Latin *dīminūtīvus, from Latin dēminuere  "to lessen," from dē- "from, down" + minuere "to diminish."
