clashing:/ˈklæʃɪŋ/
adjective
冲突的，不协调的
meeting with opposition; coming into conflict
Her clashing outfit made her stand out in the crowd. 她那身不协调的服装让她在人群中显得格外突出。
The clashing personalities of the two leaders made negotiations difficult.  两位领导人冲突的性格使得谈判变得困难。
近义词:conflicting, discordant, jarring, grating
反义词:harmonious, matching, coordinating, agreeing
词性:adjective

clashing:/ˈklæʃɪŋ/
noun
冲突，不协调
a loud, harsh, metallic sound
The clashing of swords echoed through the valley. 剑的撞击声在山谷中回荡。
The clashing of cymbals marked the end of the performance.  铙钹的撞击声标志着表演的结束。
近义词:conflict, disagreement, discord, disharmony
反义词:harmony, agreement, concord, accord
词性:noun
