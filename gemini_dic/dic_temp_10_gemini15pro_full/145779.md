overdated:/ˌoʊvərˈdeɪtɪd/| adj.|过时的，过期的|Something that is outdated is old-fashioned and no longer useful or relevant because something newer and better exists.  It can refer to information, technology, or ideas.|例句：The information in the textbook is outdated. 这本教科书里的信息已经过时了。

例句：His views on social issues are outdated. 他对社会问题的看法已经过时了。|近义词：obsolete, antiquated, outmoded, old-fashioned|反义词：modern, contemporary, up-to-date, current|词性:形容词
