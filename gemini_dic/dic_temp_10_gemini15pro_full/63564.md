envies:/ˈɛnviːz/
n.|羡慕，嫉妒|A feeling of discontent or covetousness with regard to another's advantages, success, possessions, etc.|She envies her sister's talent. 她嫉妒她姐姐的才华。|Synonyms: jealousy, covetousness |Antonyms: admiration, respect|noun


envy:/ˈɛnvi/
vt.|羡慕，嫉妒|To feel discontent or covetousness toward (someone) because of their advantages, success, possessions, etc.|I envy your ability to speak so many languages. 我羡慕你能说这么多语言。|Synonyms: be jealous of, covet |Antonyms: admire, respect|verb
