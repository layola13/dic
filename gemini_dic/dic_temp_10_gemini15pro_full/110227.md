lifeguard:/ˈlaɪfɡɑːrd/| n.|救生员|A person employed to rescue people in danger of drowning, especially at a beach or swimming pool.|The lifeguard saved a child from drowning. 救生员从溺水中救了一个孩子。|近义词：rescuer, lifesaver|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "drowner" but that implies causing drowning)|noun
