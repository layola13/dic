PHRASE->
(While "moi" can be grammatically inserted into English phrases, it sounds very unnatural and affected.  Therefore, phrases are provided in French, demonstrating how "moi" is used naturally.)

Moi aussi. = Me too. 我也是。
Pas moi. = Not me. 不是我。
À moi! = Mine! / To me! 我的！/ 给我！
Donne-moi ça. = Give me that. 把那个给我。
C'est à moi. = It's mine. 这是我的。

注意事项:

As noted, using "moi" in English is generally affected and unusual. It's mostly used for humorous or ironic effect.  Its natural usage is in French.
