interjection:/ˌɪntərˈdʒɛkʃən/ | n. |感叹词 |A word or phrase that expresses a strong feeling or emotion, often used alone. | Example: "Wow! That's amazing!" 哇! 太棒了! | Synonym: exclamation | Antonym: statement | noun

词源: From Late Latin *interjectiō*, from Latin *interjectus*, past participle of *intericere* (“to throw between”), from *inter-* (“between”) + *jacere* (“to throw”).


注意事项:  感叹词通常用逗号或感叹号隔开。  有些感叹词可以单独成句。  感叹词的用法比较灵活，主要取决于语境和说话者的情感。
