market:/ˈmɑːrkɪt/ (US) /ˈmɑːkɪt/ (UK)| n. v.|市场，集市；行情；销路|A place where people buy and sell goods.  A particular area, country, or group of people to whom something is sold. To advertise and offer a product for sale.|例句：The local farmers' market is open every Saturday. 当地的农贸市场每周六开放。|近义词：marketplace, bazaar, exchange|反义词:None|词性:noun, verb

market:/ˈmɑːrkɪt/ (US) /ˈmɑːkɪt/ (UK)| v.|推销，营销|To advertise and offer a product for sale.|例句：They are marketing a new brand of coffee. 他们在推销一个新的咖啡品牌。|近义词：promote, advertise, sell|反义词:None|词性:verb
