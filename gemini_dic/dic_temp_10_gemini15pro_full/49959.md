deniably:/dɪˈnaɪəbli/| adv.|不可否认地|In a way that cannot be denied; undeniably. It means something is so clearly true that it's impossible to dispute.|Her talent is undeniably impressive. 她的才华令人印象深刻，不可否认。|Synonyms: indisputably, undoubtedly, unquestionably, irrefutably, incontrovertibly|Antonyms: disputably, arguably, questionably, dubiously|adverb
