oversell:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛl/ | v. | 过度销售；吹嘘；夸大| To sell or promote more of something than can actually be delivered or is reasonably likely to be successful. | The company oversold its new product and couldn't meet the demand. 这家公司过度销售了它的新产品，无法满足需求。| The salesman oversold the car's features, leading to disappointment. 销售员夸大了汽车的功能，导致了失望。 | 近义词: exaggerate, overhype, overstate | 反义词: undersell, downplay | 词性: 动词

词源: over- + sell.  "Over-" prefix meaning "excessively" combined with "sell".
