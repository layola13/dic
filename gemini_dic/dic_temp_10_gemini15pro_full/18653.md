benzosol:/ˈbenzosɒl/ | n. | 苯佐溶胶，愈创木酚苯甲酸酯 |A white, crystalline powder used as an antiseptic and expectorant, especially in the treatment of tuberculosis.  It is the benzoic acid ester of guaiacol. |  Benzosol was prescribed for his persistent cough.  苯佐溶胶被用来治疗他持续的咳嗽。| synonym: guaiacol benzoate | antonym: N/A | noun
