bahaism:/ˈbɑːhaɪɪzəm/ |n.|巴哈伊教|A monotheistic religion emphasizing the spiritual unity of humanity.| Bahaism promotes the unity of all religions and peoples. 巴哈伊教提倡所有宗教和民族的团结。|Synonyms: Baha'i Faith|Antonyms: None|noun

baha'i:/bəˈhɑːi/ |n. adj.|巴哈伊信徒；巴哈伊的|A follower of Bahaism; relating to Bahaism.|He is a devout Baha'i. 他是一位虔诚的巴哈伊信徒。 The Baha'i temple is beautiful. 巴哈伊寺庙很漂亮。|Synonyms: Baha'ist (less common)|Antonyms: None|noun, adjective
