caatinga:/ˌkɑːəˈtɪŋɡə/| n. |卡廷加群落，旱生灌木林|A type of thorny scrub and woodland vegetation found in the semiarid northeastern region of Brazil.  It is characterized by small, thorny trees that shed their leaves seasonally to conserve water. |The caatinga is home to a unique variety of plants and animals. 卡廷加是各种独特动植物的家园。| The caatinga experiences extreme temperatures and drought conditions. 卡廷加经历极端的温度和干旱条件。|近义词:scrubland, bushland, thicket|反义词:rainforest, jungle|词性:noun
