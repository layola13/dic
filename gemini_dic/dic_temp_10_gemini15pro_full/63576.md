environmentalist:/ɪnˌvaɪrənˈmentəlɪst/ | n. | 环保主义者；环境保护论者 |  A person who is concerned about and advocates for the protection of the environment. | Example 1: The environmentalist protested the construction of the new factory. 环保主义者抗议建造新工厂。

Example 2:  Many environmentalists advocate for renewable energy sources. 许多环保主义者提倡可再生能源。 | 近义词: conservationist, ecologist, nature lover | 反义词: polluter | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From environment + -ist.  "Environment" comes from Old French environner "to surround,"  and "-ist" denotes a person who practices or believes in a particular doctrine or cause.
