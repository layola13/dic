overmilking:/ˌoʊvərˈmɪlkɪŋ/ | n. |过度挤奶；过度利用；榨取|The act of milking a cow or other animal excessively, or of exploiting a resource or situation to an excessive degree. |The dairy farmer was accused of overmilking his cows.  这个奶农被指控过度挤奶。|Overmilking the company's resources led to its eventual downfall. 对公司资源的过度利用导致了它最终的垮台。|近义词：overexploiting, overuse, exhausting |反义词: underusing, conserving |词性:名词

Etymology: "over-" (Old English *ofer, meaning "above, beyond") + "milking" (present participle of "milk" from Old English *meolcian, meaning "to milk")
