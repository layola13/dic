incredibly:/ɪnˈkrɛdəbli/| adv.|非常地，难以置信地| incredibly means to an extremely high degree.  It intensifies the adjective or adverb it modifies.|The view from the mountaintop was incredibly beautiful. 山顶的景色美得令人难以置信。|近义词：extremely, remarkably, unbelievably, astonishingly|反义词：ordinarily, commonly, slightly|词性:副词
Etymology: From in- (not) + credible (believable) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Latin "credere" (to believe).
