fuddy-duddy:/ˈfʌdiˌdʌdi/| n. |老顽固，守旧的人|A person who is old-fashioned and fussy, and disapproves of anything new or different.|例句：He's such a fuddy-duddy, he still uses a typewriter. 他真是个老顽固，还在用打字机。

例句：My grandfather is a bit of a fuddy-duddy when it comes to technology. 我祖父在科技方面有点守旧。

|近义词：conservative, traditionalist, stick-in-the-mud, old fogey|反义词:modernist, progressive, innovator|词性:名词
