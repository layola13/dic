open-endednesses:/ˌoʊpənˈɛndɪdnɪsɪz/| n.|开放性；未完成性|The open-endedness of the question allowed for a variety of responses.  这个问题的开放性允许各种各样的回答。|The survey questions emphasized open-endednesses to encourage detailed feedback.  调查问卷强调开放性，以鼓励详细的反馈。|近义词：flexibility, fluidity, ambiguity|反义词：restriction, rigidity, specificity|词性:noun

词源:  From open-ended + -ness.  Open-ended (adjective) meaning having no fixed limit or prescribed ending, from open + ended.
