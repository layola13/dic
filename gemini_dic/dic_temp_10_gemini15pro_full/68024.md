factorially:/fækˈtɔːriəli/ | adv. | 阶乘地，以阶乘的方式 | In mathematics, something calculated or expressed factorially involves the product of all whole numbers from 1 to a given number. | The number of possible arrangements of five books on a shelf can be calculated factorially (5!). | 近义词: exponentially (指数地), combinatorially (组合地) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially linearly 线性地) | 词性: 副词
