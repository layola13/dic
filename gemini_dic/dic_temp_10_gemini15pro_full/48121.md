decoct:/dɪˈkɒkt/ (UK) /dɪˈkɑːkt/ (US)| v. | 煎煮;熬制;浓缩 | To extract the essence from something by boiling or heating; to prepare a medicinal concoction by boiling. |  She decocted a potent herbal remedy using roots and leaves. 她用根和叶煎了一种强效的草药.

The chef decocted the bones for hours to make a rich broth.  厨师将骨头熬制了几个小时，做成了浓稠的肉汤. |  近义词: brew, infuse, extract, concentrate | 反义词: dilute | 词性: 动词
