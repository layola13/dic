nontestable:/ˌnɒnˈtɛstəbl/ | adj. | 不可测试的，无法检验的 | Not able to be tested or verified.  | This theory is nontestable because it relies on subjective experiences.  这个理论不可测试，因为它依赖于主观经验。 | The claim about the existence of ghosts is nontestable with current scientific methods.  关于鬼魂存在的断言用目前的科学方法无法检验。 | verifiable, testable | unprovable, unverifiable | adjective
