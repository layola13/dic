fideistic:/ˌfaɪˈdiːɪstɪk/ |adj.|盲信的，唯信主义的|Based on faith rather than reason or evidence. Relying on faith alone, especially in the absence of evidence or proof.|He held a fideistic view of religion, believing that reason was irrelevant to matters of faith.|近义词:credulous, trusting, devout|反义词:skeptical, rational, empirical|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *fides* (faith) + *-istic* (suffix forming adjectives).
