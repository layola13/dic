ascent:/əˈsent/| n.|晋升，提升|Advancement in social status or rank. |His rapid ascent to CEO surprised everyone. 他迅速晋升为首席执行官让每个人都感到惊讶。|近义词：promotion, advancement, progress|反义词: demotion, regression|noun
