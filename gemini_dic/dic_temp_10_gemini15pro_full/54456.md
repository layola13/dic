disinhibitory:/dɪsɪnˈhɪbɪtəri/
adjective
去抑制的，解除抑制的
Reducing or eliminating inhibition; tending to remove a restraint.
Alcohol has a disinhibitory effect on some people.  酒精对某些人有去抑制作用。

The drug has a disinhibitory effect on behavior. 这种药物对行为有去抑制作用。

近义词:  releasing, liberating, freeing
反义词:  inhibitory, restraining, suppressing
词性: adjective
