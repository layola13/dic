bumpinesses:/ˈbʌmpɪnəsɪz/
n.
崎岖不平，颠簸
The state of being bumpy; a series of bumps or unevenness.
The bumpinesses of the road made the journey uncomfortable. 路面的颠簸使得旅程很不舒服。
近义词:unevenness, roughness, irregularity
反义词:smoothness, flatness
词性:名词

bumpy:/ˈbʌmpi/
adj.
崎岖不平的，颠簸的
Having a rough or uneven surface.
The bumpy road made for a slow drive. 崎岖不平的道路使得车开得很慢。
近义词:uneven, rough, irregular
反义词:smooth, flat
词性:形容词

bump:/bʌmp/
n./v.
撞击，碰撞；隆起物
A light blow or collision; a rounded swelling or protuberance.
The car hit a bump in the road. 汽车撞到了路上的一个凸起。 /  I bumped into an old friend at the supermarket. 我在超市里偶然碰见了一位老朋友。
近义词:knock, jolt, lump
反义词:dip, hollow
词性:名词/动词


词源:  From bump (n.) + -y.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *bumpuz (“a swelling”).
