infracting:/ɪnˈfræktɪŋ/
verb
违反，违规
The act of breaking a rule or law.
He was infracting the company's policy by using his work computer for personal business. 他因将工作电脑用于私人事务而违反了公司政策。
synonyms: violating, breaching, breaking, transgressing, offending
antonyms: obeying, complying, adhering, observing
