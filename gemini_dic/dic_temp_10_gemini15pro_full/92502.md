hypothesises:/haɪˈpɒθəsaɪzɪz/ | v. | 假设，假说 |  To suggest a possible explanation for something, but without knowing whether it is true.  | Scientists hypothesise that there is life on other planets. 科学家们假设其他星球上有生命。

The police hypothesised that the thief escaped through the back door. 警方假设小偷是从后门逃跑的。 | synonyms: postulate, theorize, propose, suggest | antonyms: prove, demonstrate, confirm | verb


hypotheses:/haɪˈpɒθəsiːz/ | n. | 假设，假说 | A proposed explanation for something that is based on limited evidence and requires further investigation. | Several hypotheses have been put forward to explain the origin of the universe. 已经提出了几个假说来解释宇宙的起源。

His hypothesis about the cause of the disease proved to be correct. 他关于这种疾病起因的假设被证明是正确的。 | synonyms: theory, supposition, assumption, conjecture | antonyms: fact, proof, certainty | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek hypothesis (ὑπόθεσις) "a placing under, foundation, supposition," from hypo- (ὑπο-) "under" + thesis (θέσις) "a placing, proposition."
