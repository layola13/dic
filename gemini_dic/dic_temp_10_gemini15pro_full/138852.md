nursehound:/ˈnɜːrsˌhaʊnd/ /ˈnɝsˌhaʊnd/| n. |铰口鲨，猫鲨|A type of small shark (Scyliorhinidae family) found in shallow waters, known for its docile nature.  They are also called catsharks. |例句：The nursehound is a common sight in these waters.  铰口鲨在这些水域很常见。

例句：The diver encountered a friendly nursehound resting on the seabed. 潜水员遇到一条友好的铰口鲨在海底休息。|近义词：catshark|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps larger, aggressive shark species like "great white shark")|词性:noun
