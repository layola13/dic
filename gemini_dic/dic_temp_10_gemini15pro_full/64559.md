erroneous: /ɪˈroʊniəs/ (US) /ɪˈrəʊniəs/ (UK)| adj.|错误的，不正确的|Containing or characterized by error; incorrect. |例句：The report was based on erroneous information. 这份报告是基于错误的信息。|例句：His erroneous assumptions led to a disastrous outcome. 他错误的假设导致了灾难性的结果。|近义词：incorrect, false, mistaken, inaccurate, flawed|反义词：correct, accurate, true, right, valid|词性:形容词

词源:
From Late Latin *erroneus, from Latin errōneus (“wandering, straying”), from errō (“I wander, stray, err”).
