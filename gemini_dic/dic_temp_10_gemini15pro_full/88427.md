Example 2:  As a hispanicist, she was fascinated by the evolution of the Spanish language. 作为一名西班牙语言文学研究者，她对西班牙语的演变很着迷。 | Near Synonyms: Hispanophile,  scholar of Spanish, Spanish specialist | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps someone who dislikes or avoids Spanish culture) | Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Hispanic + -ist.  Hispanic ultimately derives from the Latin "Hispania," the Roman name for the Iberian Peninsula.
