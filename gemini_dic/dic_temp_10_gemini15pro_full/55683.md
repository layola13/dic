dittology:/dɪˈtɒlədʒi/
n.
二部曲；双联画
a work consisting of two parts, especially two dramatic plays or operas, often connected by having a single theme or the same characters.
The Odyssey and The Iliad form a famous dittology.  《奥德赛》和《伊利亚特》构成了一部著名的二部曲。
The artist presented a dittology depicting the creation of the world. 这位艺术家展示了一幅描绘世界创造的双联画。
synonym:  diptych, two-parter
antonym:  N/A
noun
