fountained:/ˈfaʊntɪnd/ | adj. | 喷泉式的，如喷泉般涌出的 | Having jets or streams of water like a fountain; spurting or gushing like a fountain. |  The elaborate dessert was fountained with melted chocolate.  这道精致的甜点淋上了如喷泉般涌出的融化巧克力。 |  The blood fountained from the wound.  鲜血从伤口喷涌而出。 | synonyms: gushing, spurting, jetting, spraying | antonyms: trickling, dripping | adjective
