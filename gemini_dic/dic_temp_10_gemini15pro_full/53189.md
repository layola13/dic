dipsosis:/dɪpˈsoʊsɪs/| n.|口渴，极度渴望（尤指对酒精的渴望）|An abnormal thirst, especially one that cannot be quenched.|He suffered from dipsosis after running the marathon. 他跑完马拉松后感到非常口渴。|Synonyms: thirst, dehydration, craving (especially for alcohol)|Antonyms: hydration, satiation|noun


dipsomania:/ˌdɪpsəˈmeɪniə/| n.| 饮酒狂，嗜酒症|An uncontrollable craving for alcoholic beverages.|His dipsomania led him to lose his job and family. 他的嗜酒症让他失去了工作和家庭。|Synonyms: alcoholism, alcohol addiction|Antonyms: sobriety, temperance|noun
