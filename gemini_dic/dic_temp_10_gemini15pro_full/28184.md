cappuccino:/ˌkæpʊˈtʃiːnoʊ/ (US) /ˌkæpʊˈtʃiːnəʊ/ (UK)| n.|卡布奇诺|An Italian coffee beverage consisting of espresso with steamed milk foam.  A cappuccino is typically one-third espresso, one-third steamed milk, and one-third foamed milk.|I ordered a cappuccino and a croissant for breakfast. 我早餐点了一杯卡布奇诺和一个羊角面包。|近义词:latte, macchiato|反义词:None|词性:noun

词源: From Italian cappuccino, literally 'Capuchin', from the resemblance of the colour of the coffee to the colour of the habits worn by Capuchin friars.
