palindromical:/ˌpælɪnˈdrɒmɪkl/ (UK) /ˌpælɪnˈdrɑːmɪkl/ (US)| adj.|回文的，镜像的|Relating to or being a palindrome, a word, phrase, or sequence that reads the same backward as forward.|例句：The word "madam" is palindromical.  “madam”这个词是回文的。

例句：The DNA sequence exhibited palindromical properties. 这段DNA序列展现出回文特性。|近义词：palindrome, mirrored, symmetrical|反义词:non-palindromic, asymmetrical|词性:adjective
