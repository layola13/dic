beatitude:/biˈætɪtjuːd/ /biˈætɪtuːd/
n.
至福;祝福;八福
Supreme blessedness or happiness. The Beatitudes are eight sayings of Jesus at the beginning of the Sermon on the Mount in the Gospel of Matthew. They describe the qualities of people who are blessed by God.
The Beatitudes teach us spiritual values. 八福教导我们精神价值。
She experienced a moment of perfect beatitude. 她体验到了片刻完美的幸福。
近义词:bliss, happiness, felicity
反义词:misery, unhappiness, sorrow
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin beatitudo (“blessedness”), from Latin beatus (“blessed, happy”).
