amethyst:/ˈæmɪθɪst/| n.|紫水晶|A transparent purple variety of quartz, used as a gemstone.|She wore an amethyst pendant. 她戴着紫水晶吊坠。|近义词：violet, purple, lavender (referring to the color)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *ametiste* and Latin *amethystus*, from Ancient Greek *ἀμέθυστος (améthystos)*, meaning "not drunken," from *ἀ- (a-)*, "not" + *μεθύσκω (methýskō)*, "to intoxicate."  It was believed that the stone could prevent intoxication.
