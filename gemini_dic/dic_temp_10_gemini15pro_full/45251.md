cultishly:/ˈkʌltɪʃli/
adverb
狂热地；着迷地；近乎崇拜地
In a manner resembling or characteristic of a cult, often excessively devoted or enthusiastic.
She cultishly follows every new trend in fashion. 她狂热地追逐每一个新的时尚潮流。
He cultishly collects vintage records. 他狂热地收集老唱片。
Synonym: fanatically, obsessively, devotedly, zealously
Antonym: indifferently, apathetically
词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From cult + -ish + -ly.  "Cult" comes from the Latin "cultus," meaning "care, cultivation, worship."  The suffix "-ish" forms adjectives meaning "somewhat like" or "having the quality of," and "-ly" forms adverbs.
