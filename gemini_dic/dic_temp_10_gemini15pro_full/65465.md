eurocentric:/ˌjʊərəˈsɛntrɪk/ | adj. |以欧洲为中心的；欧洲中心主义的|Focusing on European culture or history to the exclusion of a wider view of the world; implicitly regarding European culture as preeminent.|例句：A eurocentric view of history often overlooks the contributions of other civilizations.  以欧洲为中心的史观常常忽视其他文明的贡献。

例句：The curriculum was criticized for its eurocentric bias.  该课程因其欧洲中心主义的偏见而受到批评。|近义词：Western-centric, Occidentalist|反义词：multicultural, global|词性:形容词

Etymology: euro- (from Europe) + -centric (from center, meaning focused on a particular center) 词源：euro-（来自Europe欧洲） + -centric（来自center中心，意思是专注于某个特定中心）
