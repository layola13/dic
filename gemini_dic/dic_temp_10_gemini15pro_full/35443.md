co-signatory:/ˌkoʊˈsɪɡnətɔːri/ /ˌkəʊˈsɪɡnətəri/| n. |共同签署人；联署人|A person who signs a document along with another person or other people, especially a loan or other financial agreement.|例句：My father was the co-signatory on the loan. 我父亲是贷款的共同签署人。

例句：All co-signatories are equally responsible for the debt. 所有共同签署人都对债务负有同等责任。|近义词：joint signatory, co-signer|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

co-signer:/ˌkoʊˈsaɪnər/ /ˌkəʊˈsaɪnər/| n. |共同签署人；联署人|A person who signs a document along with another person or other people, especially a loan or other financial agreement.|例句：She needed a co-signer to get the loan approved. 她需要一个共同签署人才能获得贷款批准。
