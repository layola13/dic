oversupplied:/ˌoʊvər səˈplaɪd/| adj.|供应过剩的，供过于求的|Having a supply that is greater than the demand.|The market is oversupplied with low-quality goods.  市场上充斥着劣质商品。|oversaturated, glutted|undersupplied|adjective
词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessive") + supplied (past participle of supply)
