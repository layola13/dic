packagers:/ˈpækɪdʒərz/
n.
包装商，包装工人
People or companies that package goods.
The packagers worked quickly to prepare the products for shipping. 包装工人快速工作，为产品装运做准备。
The packagers carefully sealed each box. 包装工人仔细地封好每个盒子。

近义词: packers, wrappers
反义词: unpackers

词性: noun (plural)

词源: From package + -er (agent suffix).  Ultimately from the Old French word "pacquet," meaning "small bundle."
