drachm:/dræm/ | n. | 英制药重单位（打兰，等于1/8盎司或60喱）；少量 | A drachm is a unit of weight, equal to one eighth of an ounce, used especially for measuring medicines. Also, it can refer to a small amount of something. |  The doctor prescribed a drachm of the medicine. 医生开了60喱的药。

The recipe called for a drachm of salt.  食谱要求加少量盐。| 近义词: dram, drop, bit, ounce (as a unit of weight) | 反义词:  N/A (Units of measurement don't typically have antonyms) | 词性: noun

drachm:/drɑːm/ | n. | 古希腊钱币；少量 |  An ancient Greek coin and a small quantity of liquid. |  He offered a drachm for the information. 他用一枚古希腊钱币来换取信息。

She added a drachm of perfume to the water. 她往水里加了几滴香水。| 近义词: drop, bit, ounce (for liquid, in a historical context), dram | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun

词源:
From Late Latin *drachma, from Greek δραχμή (drakhmē, “handful, drachma”), from δράσσομαι (drassomai, “to grasp”).  Cognate with dram.
