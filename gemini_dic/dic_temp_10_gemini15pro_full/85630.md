haunt:/hɔːnt/ | v.  n. |  萦绕；困扰；常去；鬼魂 | To visit or inhabit in the form of a ghost; to appear repeatedly to. As a noun, it refers to a place frequented by a particular person or group. | The ghost of his father haunts him. 他父亲的鬼魂萦绕着他。 | synonyms: plague, torment, frequent | antonyms: avoid, shun | verb

haunt:/hɔːnt/ | n. | 常去的地方；聚集地 | A place frequented by a particular person or group. | The old library is a favorite haunt of his.  旧图书馆是他最爱去的地方。 | synonyms: hangout, resort, spot | antonyms:  None (as a noun, it lacks a direct opposite) | noun
