long-stay: /ˈlɒŋ steɪ/ (UK) /ˈlɔːŋ steɪ/ (US)
adjective|长期逗留的，长期居住的|Intended for or involving a stay of considerable duration.|例句：They offer long-stay parking at the airport. 他们在机场提供长期停车服务。|近义词：extended-stay, long-term|反义词：short-stay, short-term|词性:adjective
long-stay: /ˈlɒŋ steɪ/ (UK) /ˈlɔːŋ steɪ/ (US)
noun|长期逗留，长期住宿|A stay of considerable duration.|例句：Many hotels offer discounts for long-stays. 许多酒店为长期住宿提供折扣。|近义词：extended stay, long term stay|反义词：short stay, short term stay|词性:noun

Etymology:  "long" (Old English lang, long) + "stay" (Middle English staien, from Old French estai)
