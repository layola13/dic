gazetting:/ɡəˈzɛtɪŋ/ | n. | 公告；刊登；公布 | The act of publishing something in an official gazette. | The gazetting of the new law will take place next week.  下周将公布新法律。 | publication, announcement, proclamation | suppression, concealment | noun

gazetted:/ɡəˈzɛtɪd/ | adj. | 已公布的；已刊登的 | Officially published in a gazette.  | The gazetted list of successful candidates will be displayed on the notice board.  已公布的候选人名单将张贴在布告栏上。| published, announced, proclaimed | unpublished, unannounced | adjective
