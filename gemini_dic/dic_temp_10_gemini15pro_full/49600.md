domestic:/dəˈmɛstɪk/| adj. n.|家庭的，国内的；家养的；佣人|Relating to the running of a home or to family relations. Existing or occurring inside a particular country; not foreign or international. (of an animal) tame and kept by humans.|例句：She enjoys domestic life. 她喜欢家庭生活。|近义词：home, household, national, local, native, indigenous, tame|反义词：foreign, international, wild, feral|词性:形容词/名词

domestic:/dəˈmɛstɪk/|n.|佣人|A person who is paid to help with menial tasks such as cleaning.|例句：They employed a domestic to help with the housework. 他们雇了一个佣人来帮忙做家务。|近义词：servant, maid, helper|反义词：employer, master|词性:名词
