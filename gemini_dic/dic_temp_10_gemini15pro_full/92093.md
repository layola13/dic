hyperresonance:/ˌhaɪpərˈrezənəns/| n.|亢进音，过共振|An exaggerated chest resonance produced when air is trapped in the pleural space.|Example 1: The doctor detected hyperresonance in the patient's chest, indicating a possible pneumothorax. 医生在病人的胸部检测到亢进音，表明可能存在气胸。

Example 2:  Hyperresonance on percussion can be a sign of emphysema. 叩诊时出现亢进音可能是肺气肿的征兆。|近义词:tympany, overresonance|反义词:dullness, flatness|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- + resonance. Hyper- comes from the Greek prefix "hyper-" meaning "over, above, beyond". Resonance comes from Latin "resonantia",  meaning "echo".
