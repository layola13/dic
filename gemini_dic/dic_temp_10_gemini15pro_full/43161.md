cpa:/ˌsiː piː ˈeɪ/ /ˌsiː piː ˈeɪ/ | n. |注册会计师 (Certified Public Accountant)|A licensed accountant who has passed the Uniform CPA Examination and met other educational and experience requirements.  CPAs are qualified to perform audits of financial statements and provide other accounting and tax services. |She hired a CPA to help her with her taxes. 她聘请了一位注册会计师来帮她报税。|Synonym: accountant, auditor |Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "unqualified accountant")|noun
