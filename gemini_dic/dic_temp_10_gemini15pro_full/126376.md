myristin:/ˈmɪrɪstɪn/| n. |肉豆蔻酸甘油酯；十四烷酸甘油酯|A white crystalline fat, the triglyceride of myristic acid, found in many vegetable fats, especially nutmeg butter.  It is used in cosmetics and soaps.|Myristin is a common ingredient in many lotions. 肉豆蔻酸甘油酯是许多乳液中的常见成分。|Myristin is extracted from nutmeg. 肉豆蔻酸甘油酯是从肉豆蔻中提取的。|Synonyms: glyceryl trimyristate|Antonyms: N/A|noun
