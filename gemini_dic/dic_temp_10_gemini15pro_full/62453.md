endorsement:/ɪnˈdɔːrsmənt/ | n. | 赞同，支持；认可，批准；背书；代言 |An act of giving public approval or support to someone or something.| The product has received endorsements from several celebrities. 该产品获得了多位名人的代言。| 近义词: approval, support, backing, sanction, recommendation, testimonial | 反义词: disapproval, opposition, condemnation | 词性: 名词

endorsement:/ɪnˈdɔːrsmənt/ | n. | 签注，批注（尤指在支票背面）| A signature on the back of a check transferring ownership to someone else. | She wrote an endorsement on the back of the check. 她在支票背面写了背书。 | 近义词: signature, countersignature | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词


词源:  From endorse + -ment.  Endorse comes from Old French endosser "write on the back of," from en- "on" + dos "back."
