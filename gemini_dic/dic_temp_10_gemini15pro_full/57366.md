doxologically:/dɒksəˈlɒdʒɪkli/
adv.|颂赞地，以颂赞的方式|In a manner relating to or expressing doxology; with praise and thanksgiving to God. |The congregation responded doxologically, singing praises to the Almighty.  会众以颂赞的方式回应，歌颂全能者。|近义词：worshipfully, reverently, thankfully|反义词:blasphemously, irreverently|词性:副词
词源: From Greek "doxa" (glory, praise) + "logia" (speaking, study) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).
