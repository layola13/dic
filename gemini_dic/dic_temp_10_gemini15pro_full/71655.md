flatteries:/ˈflætəriːz/
n.|奉承；恭维话；阿谀|insincere or excessive praise.
He showered her with flatteries. 他对她大加奉承。
She wasn't fooled by his flatteries. 她没有被他说的奉承话所愚弄。
近义词: compliments, praise, adulation
反义词: criticisms, insults
词性:名词(plural noun)

flattery:/ˈflætəri/
n.|奉承；恭维；阿谀|Excessive and insincere praise, given especially to further one's own interests.
She used flattery to get what she wanted. 她用奉承来达到目的。
He was immune to flattery. 他对奉承无动于衷。
近义词: adulation, cajolery, blandishment
反义词: criticism, condemnation
词性:名词(singular noun)
