megalops:/ˈmeɡəlɒps/ (US) /ˈmɛɡəlɒps/ (UK)| n. | 大眼幼体；海鲢科鱼类的幼鱼 | A large-eyed larval form of certain fishes, especially the tarpon.  It resembles a transparent, ribbon-like fish. |  The megalops of the tarpon is often mistaken for a separate species.  海鲢的大眼幼体经常被误认为是独立的物种。

The fishermen caught a megalops in their net. 渔民在他们的网里捕到了一条大眼幼体。 | 近义词: leptocephalus (柳叶鳗，也是某些鱼类的幼体) | 反义词: adult (成鱼) | 词性: noun


Etymology:
From Greek *mega* "large" + *ops* "eye, face".
