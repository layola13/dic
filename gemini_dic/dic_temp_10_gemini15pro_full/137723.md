nonvacuum:/ˌnɒnˈvækjuːm/
noun
非真空
A state or environment that is not a vacuum, meaning it contains matter, such as air or gas.
The experiment was conducted in a nonvacuum environment.  这个实验是在非真空环境下进行的。
The air we breathe is a nonvacuum. 我们呼吸的空气是非真空的。
near synonyms: atmosphere, medium
antonyms: vacuum
