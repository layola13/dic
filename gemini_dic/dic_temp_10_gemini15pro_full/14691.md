awakened: /əˈweɪkənd/ | adj. | 觉醒的，醒着的 |  roused from sleep; made aware of something |  She felt awakened to the possibilities of a new life. 她感觉到了新生活的可能性，并为之振奋。| 近义词: awake, aroused, conscious, enlightened | 反义词: asleep, unconscious, unaware | 词性: 形容词

词源: From the verb 'awaken', which comes from Old English 'awacnian', meaning "to wake up."
