fastness:/ˈfæstnɪs/| n.|坚固性；牢固；不易褪色；要塞|The fastness of the dye was tested.  这种染料的牢固度经过了测试。|The rebels retreated to their mountain fastness. 叛军撤退到他们的山间要塞。|近义词：strength, durability, security, stronghold, fortress|反义词: weakness, instability, insecurity|词性:名词
Etymology: From Middle English *fastnes*, from Old English *fæstnes* (“firmness, steadfastness”), from *fæst* (“firm, fast”) + *-nes*.  Cognate with Dutch *vastheid*, German *Festigkeit*.
