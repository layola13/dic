incontiguous:/ˌɪnkənˈtɪɡjuəs/ | adj. | 不连续的，不相邻的 | Not touching; not in contact; separated. | The houses on the street are incontiguous, with empty lots between them. 街上的房子是不连续的，中间有空地。|  The islands are incontiguous, scattered across a vast ocean. 这些岛屿是不连续的，散布在广阔的海洋中。 | 近义词: disconnected, separate, disjunct, nonadjacent | 反义词: contiguous, adjacent, adjoining, touching | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From in- (not) + contiguous (touching), from Latin *contiguus, from contingere (to touch).
