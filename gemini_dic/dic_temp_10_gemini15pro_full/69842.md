ferrier:/ˈfɛriər/ | n. |蹄铁匠；装钉蹄铁的人|A person who shoes horses.|例句：The ferrier carefully shaped the horseshoe to fit the horse's hoof. 蹄铁匠仔细地把马蹄铁塑造成适合马蹄的形状。

例句：The old ferrier had been shoeing horses in the village for over fifty years.  这位老蹄铁匠在村里钉马掌已经五十多年了。|近义词：blacksmith, farrier |反义词: None |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *ferrier, from fer (“iron”) (from Latin ferrum) + -ier (agent suffix).
