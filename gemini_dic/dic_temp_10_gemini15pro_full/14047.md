austereness:/ɔːˈstɪrnəs/| n. |严肃性，朴素，简朴 |The state or quality of being austere; severity of manner, life, etc.; strictness; simplicity.|  The austerity of the monks' lifestyle was remarkable. 修道士们生活方式的简朴令人瞩目。 |  The room was furnished with an almost shocking austereness. 房间的陈设极其简朴，近乎令人震惊。|Synonyms: severity, strictness, simplicity, asceticism, rigor, harshness|Antonyms: luxury, lavishness, extravagance, indulgence, ornateness|noun

austere:/ɔːˈstɪr/| adj. | 严肃的，朴素的，简朴的 |Severe in manner or appearance; uncompromising; strict; forbidding; rigorously self-disciplined and severely moral; ascetic; abstinent; grave; sober; solemn; without excess, luxury, or ease; simple; plain.| He had an austere expression on his face. 他脸上带着严肃的表情。| The austere room had only a bed and a chair. 简朴的房间里只有一张床和一把椅子。|Synonyms: stern, strict, severe, simple, plain, unadorned, harsh|Antonyms: lenient, luxurious, ornate, extravagant, indulgent|adjective
