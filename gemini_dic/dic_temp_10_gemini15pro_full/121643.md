misperception:/ˌmɪspərˈsepʃn/ | n. |误解，错误的认识|A view or opinion that is incorrect because based on faulty thinking or understanding.|例句：There is a common misperception that older workers are less productive.  老年人工作效率较低的普遍误解。

例句：His comments are based on misperception and prejudice. 他的评论是基于误解和偏见。|近义词：misunderstanding, misconception, misapprehension|反义词：accurate perception, correct understanding|词性:noun
