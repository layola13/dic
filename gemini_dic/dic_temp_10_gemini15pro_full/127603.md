needfulness:/ˈniːdfʊlnəs/
n.
必要性；需要；贫困
The state or quality of being needful; necessity.
The needfulness of regular exercise is undeniable.  规律运动的必要性是不可否认的。
necessity, requirement, essentiality
unnecessariness, dispensability
noun
