germanify:/dʒɜːmənaɪfaɪ/ | vt. | 使德国化；使具有德国特色 | To make German in character; to imbue with German characteristics or customs. |  They tried to germanify the occupied territories during the war.  他们在战争期间试图使被占领土德国化。

| Example: The restaurant's menu was germanified with the addition of sauerkraut and schnitzel.  这家餐厅的菜单加入了酸菜和炸肉排，使其更具德国特色。
| Synonyms: Germanize | Antonyms:  Internationalize, globalize
| Word Class: Verb
