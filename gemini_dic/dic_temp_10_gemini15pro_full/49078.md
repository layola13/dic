dehydrogenation:/diːˌhaɪdrədʒəˈneɪʃən/| n.|脱氢，去氢|The process of removing hydrogen from a molecule.  从分子中除去氢的过程。|例句：Dehydrogenation of ethane produces ethylene. 乙烷脱氢生成乙烯。|例句：Catalytic dehydrogenation is an important industrial process. 催化脱氢是重要的工业过程。|近义词：oxidation, elimination|反义词：hydrogenation|noun
