hillwalker:/ˈhɪlˌwɔːkər/ | n. | 徒步旅行者；登山爱好者 | A person who walks in hilly areas for recreation. |  We met a group of hillwalkers on the trail. 我们在小路上遇到了一群徒步旅行者。| hiker, rambler, climber | couch potato, sedentary person | noun

Etymology:  'hill' (Old English *hyll) + 'walker' (from 'walk' - Old English wealcan "to roll, toss, turn, revolve")
