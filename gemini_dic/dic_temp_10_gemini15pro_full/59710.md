eclectic:/ɪˈklektɪk/| adj.|兼收并蓄的，博采众长的|deriving ideas, style, or taste from a broad range of sources.|She has an eclectic taste in music, enjoying everything from classical to hip-hop. 她对音乐的品味兼收并蓄，从古典到嘻哈，什么都喜欢。|近义词：diverse, varied, broad, wide-ranging|反义词：narrow, limited, restricted|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Greek eklektikos "selective," from eklegein "to pick out," from ek "out" + legein "to gather, choose."
