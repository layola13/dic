hinges:/ˈhɪndʒɪz/ (US), /ˈhɪndʒɪz/ (UK)| n. |铰链，枢纽，关键|A hinge is a jointed device that allows a door, gate, lid, etc., to swing open or closed.|The door creaked on its rusty hinges. 门上的生锈铰链发出吱吱嘎嘎的声音。|synonyms: joint, pivot, swivel|antonyms: none|noun

hinges:/ˈhɪndʒɪz/ (US), /ˈhɪndʒɪz/ (UK)| v. |给…装铰链，依…而定|To attach or be attached with a hinge.  To depend entirely on.|The outcome of the election hinges on the votes of undecided voters. 选举的结果取决于未决选民的选票。|synonyms: depend, turn, rely|antonyms: none|verb
