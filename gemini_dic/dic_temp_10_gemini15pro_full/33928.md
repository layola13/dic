cirrhosis:/sɪˈroʊsɪs/| n.|肝硬化|A chronic disease of the liver marked by degeneration of cells, inflammation, and fibrous thickening of tissue. It is typically a result of alcoholism or hepatitis.|
The patient's cirrhosis was caused by years of heavy drinking.  这位病人的肝硬化是由多年的酗酒引起的。

His cirrhosis is in its final stages. 他的肝硬化已到晚期。


|近义词：fibrosis, hardening of the liver|反义词: health, healthy liver|词性:noun|

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *cirrhosis*, from Greek *kirrhos* "tawny, orange-colored," because of the tawny color the liver assumes in this disease + -osis.
