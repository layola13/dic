cakewalker:/ˈkeɪkˌwɔːkər/| n.|蛋糕行者; 专吃蛋糕的人; (指生活舒适安逸的人);|A person who seems to have an easy or privileged life, often implying they are detached from reality or the struggles of others.|He's a real cakewalker; he's never had to work a day in his life. 他真是个蛋糕行者，一辈子都没工作过一天。|近义词：freeloader, dilettante|反义词：hard worker, striver|noun
