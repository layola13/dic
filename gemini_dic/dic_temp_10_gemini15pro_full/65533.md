euthanasia:/ˌjuːθəˈneɪʒə/ | n. | 安乐死 | The painless killing of a patient suffering from an incurable and painful disease or in an irreversible coma. The practice is illegal in most countries. |  He supported euthanasia in certain limited circumstances.  他支持在某些特定情况下实行安乐死。 | synonyms: mercy killing, assisted suicide | antonyms:  dysthesia | noun
