overvigorousnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈvɪɡərəsnəsəz/
noun
过度活力; 过度旺盛
excessive energy or intensity.
His overvigorousnesses in the gym resulted in a pulled muscle.  他在健身房过度用力导致肌肉拉伤。
近义词: exuberance, dynamism, hyperactivity
反义词: lethargy, inactivity, sluggishness
词性: noun

Etymology:
From over- + vigorous + -ness + -es (plural suffix). "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much." "Vigorous" comes from the Latin "vigor," meaning "liveliness, activity, force." "-ness" is a suffix that forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality. "-es" is the plural suffix.
