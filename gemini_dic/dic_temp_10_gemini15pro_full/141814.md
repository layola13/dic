orbited:/ˈɔːrbɪtɪd/
verb
绕轨道运行，环绕
moved in an orbit around a celestial body.
The satellite orbited the Earth for several years.  这颗卫星绕地球运行了好几年。
The Earth orbits the sun. 地球绕太阳运行。
synonyms: circled, revolved, rotated
antonyms: crashed, descended, fell
词性: 动词
