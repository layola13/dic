incorporeal:/ˌɪnkɔːrˈpɔːriəl/ /ˌɪnkɔːrˈpɔːriəl/
adjective
无形的；非物质的；精神上的
Lacking a physical body; not composed of matter.
例句：Ghosts are often described as incorporeal beings.  鬼魂常被描述为无形的生命。

例句：Love is an incorporeal concept, yet its power is undeniable.  爱是一个无形的概念，但它的力量是不可否认的。

近义词：intangible, immaterial, ethereal, spiritual, ghostly
反义词：corporeal, physical, tangible, material
词性：adjective
