anesthetized:/əˈnɛsθəˌtaɪzd/| adj.|麻醉的，失去知觉的|Deprived of feeling or awareness, typically by the administration of an anesthetic.  The process results in a temporary loss of sensation or awareness.|The patient was anesthetized before the surgery. 病人在手术前被麻醉了。|The anesthetized dog lay still on the operating table.  麻醉的狗一动不动地躺在手术台上。|近义词：numb, insensible, unconscious|反义词：conscious, awake, alert|词性:形容词
Etymology: From an- (without) + aisthesis (sensation) + -ize (to make) + -ed (past participle suffix).  Ultimately from Greek roots.
