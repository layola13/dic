acronymed: /əˈkrɒnɪmd/ (UK) /əˈkrɑːnɪmd/ (US)
adjective
缩写成首字母缩略词的；用首字母缩略词表示的
Made into or represented by an acronym.

The word "laser" was originally acronymed from "Light Amplification by Stimulated Emission of Radiation".  激光（laser）一词最初是由“受激辐射光放大”（Light Amplification by Stimulated Emission of Radiation）的首字母缩略而成。

NASA is a well-known acronymed agency. 美国国家航空航天局（NASA）是一个众所周知的首字母缩略词机构。

近义词: abbreviated, shortened
反义词: expanded, lengthened
词性: adjective
