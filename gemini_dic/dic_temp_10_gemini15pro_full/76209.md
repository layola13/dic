fustiness:/ˈfʌstɪnɪs/
noun
霉味；陈腐；污浊
a stale smell; a musty odor; a state of being old-fashioned and outdated.
The fustiness of the old library made him sneeze.  这间旧图书馆的霉味让他打了个喷嚏。
近义词：mustiness, staleness, stuffiness, old-fashionedness
反义词：freshness, modernity
