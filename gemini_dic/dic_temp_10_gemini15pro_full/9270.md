antiperspirant: /ˌæntiˈpɜːrspərənt/ | n. |止汗剂，防汗剂| A substance designed to reduce perspiration. |  I apply antiperspirant every morning. 我每天早上都涂止汗剂。|  Antiperspirants help control sweat. 止汗剂有助于控制汗液。|Synonyms:  deodorant, antiperspirant deodorant | Antonyms:  - (No direct antonym, but one could consider "perspirant" as a contrasting concept) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + perspirant.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to". "Perspirant" comes from the Latin word "perspirare" meaning "to breathe through" or "to exude".
