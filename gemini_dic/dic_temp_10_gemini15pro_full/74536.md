fraternizers:/ˈfrætərˌnaɪzərz/
n.
交好者，友好往来者
People who associate in a friendly way, especially with members of a hostile group.
The general forbade his soldiers from fraternizing with the local population. 将军禁止他的士兵与当地居民友好往来。
synonyms: socializers, mixers, minglers
antonyms: isolators, segregators
noun

fraternize:/ˈfrætərˌnaɪz/
v.
友好往来，交好
To associate or form a friendship with someone, especially when it is not expected or approved of.
The officers were warned not to fraternize with the enlisted men. 军官们被警告不要与士兵们称兄道弟。
synonyms: socialize, mingle, consort
antonyms: shun, avoid, isolate
verb
