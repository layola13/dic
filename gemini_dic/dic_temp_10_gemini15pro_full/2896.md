advertizements: /ˌædvərˈtaɪzmənts/ | n. | 广告 |  A public promotion of some product or service.  A notice or announcement in a public medium promoting a product, service, or event or publicizing a job vacancy. | We were bombarded with advertizements for the new phone. 我们被新手机的广告狂轰炸。

The company launched a series of advertizements to promote their latest product.  公司推出了一系列广告来推广他们的最新产品。

| 近义词: ads, commercials, publicity, promotion | 反义词: criticism, condemnation | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From advertise + -ment.  Ultimately from Latin advertere "to turn toward".

USAGE->
Plural form of advertisement.  Generally used to refer to multiple distinct advertisements.
