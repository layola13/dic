over-cleanness:/ˌoʊvərˈklɛnɪsnɪs/ |n.| 过度清洁；洁癖 |Excessive cleanliness, often to an unhealthy or obsessive degree. |Her over-cleanness made it difficult for anyone to visit her home. 她的洁癖让任何人去她家都很困难。|  She scrubbed the floor three times a day out of sheer over-cleanness. 她纯粹出于洁癖，一天擦三次地板。| 近义词: fastidiousness, over-tidiness |反义词: dirtiness, untidiness |词性: noun

over-clean:/ˌoʊvərˈkliːn/ |adj.| 过度清洁的 |Excessively clean, often to an unhealthy or obsessive degree. |The over-clean kitchen felt sterile and unwelcoming. 过于干净的厨房感觉很无菌，也不受欢迎。| His over-clean apartment lacked personality and warmth. 他过于干净的公寓缺乏个性和温暖。 |近义词: excessively clean, over-sanitized |反义词: dirty, untidy |词性: adjective
