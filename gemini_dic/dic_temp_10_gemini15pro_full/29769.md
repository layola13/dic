cattier:/ˈkætiər/| adj.|更刻薄的，更恶毒的|Comparative form of catty: more catty.  Meaning more spiteful or malicious, especially in a petty way, often involving gossip or backbiting.|例句：Her remarks were getting cattier and cattier as the night went on. 随着夜幕降临，她的话语越来越刻薄。|例句：He's become much cattier since his promotion. 自从升职以来，他变得刻薄多了。|近义词：bitchy, spiteful, malicious, mean, nasty|反义词：kind, pleasant, agreeable, nice|词性:形容词
