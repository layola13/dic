mopiest:/ˈmoʊpiɪst/ | adj. | 闷闷不乐的；沮丧的 | Extremely sad and despondent; gloomier than gloomy.  Feeling or showing extreme sadness. |  He was the mopiest person at the party, sitting alone in the corner. 他是聚会上最闷闷不乐的人，独自坐在角落里。| Example:  After her cat died, she was the mopiest I'd ever seen her.  在她猫死后，她是我见过的最沮丧的样子。 | Synonyms:  gloomy, dejected, downcast, depressed, melancholic | Antonyms: cheerful, happy, joyful, elated, upbeat | Word type: Adjective
