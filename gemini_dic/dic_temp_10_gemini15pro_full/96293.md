indigenous:/ɪnˈdɪdʒɪnəs/| adj.|土生土长的，本地的，原住民的|Originating or occurring naturally in a particular place; native.|例句：The kangaroo is indigenous to Australia. 袋鼠是澳大利亚土生土长的动物。|例句：The indigenous people of this land have a deep connection to nature.  这片土地的原住民与自然有着深厚的联系。|近义词：native, aboriginal, endemic|反义词：foreign, alien, exotic|词性:形容词
