ekblah:/ˈɛkblɑː/ |  n. |  胡说，废话，无意义的话 |  A nonsense word, gibberish, or meaningless utterance.  It is often used as a placeholder for actual words or to represent unintelligible speech.  It's similar to "blah" but with a more emphatic or exasperated connotation. | Example: He was just saying a bunch of ekblah, I couldn't understand a word. 他只是在说一堆废话，我一个字也听不懂。

Example:  Stop with the ekblah and tell me what really happened!  别胡扯了，告诉我到底发生了什么！ |  Synonyms: gibberish, nonsense, babble, drivel | Antonyms: sense, meaning, clarity |  noun
