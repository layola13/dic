burrito:/bəˈriːtoʊ/ (US), /bəˈrɪtəʊ/ (UK)| n. |墨西哥卷饼|A Mexican dish consisting of a flour tortilla wrapped around various fillings, such as meat, beans, rice, and cheese.|例句：I ate a delicious chicken burrito for lunch. 我午餐吃了一个美味的鸡肉卷饼。

例句：The burrito was filled with seasoned beef, rice, and beans.  这个墨西哥卷饼里包着调味牛肉、米饭和豆子。

近义词：wrap, taco|反义词: None|词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Mexican Spanish *burrito*, literally "little donkey," diminutive of burro "donkey."  The reason for the name is uncertain, but it may refer to the rolled-up shape resembling a donkey's ear or pack, or to the variety of ingredients that can be "packed" inside like a donkey carries goods.
