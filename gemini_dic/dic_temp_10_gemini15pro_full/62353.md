endframe:/ˈɛnd freɪm/| n.|结束帧，终止帧|A frame in a video or animation sequence that marks the end of a specific action, scene, or animation.  It signifies the completion of a particular segment.|The endframe of the animation showed the character waving goodbye.|近义词：final frame, last frame|反义词:start frame, beginning frame|noun
