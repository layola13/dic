deciduity:/dɪˈsɪdʒuɪti/ | n. | 落叶性；脱落性 |  The quality or state of being deciduous; the falling off or shedding of leaves seasonally or at a certain stage of development. |  The deciduity of the maple trees in autumn is a beautiful sight.  枫树在秋天的落叶性是一道美丽的风景。 | 近义词：abscission, shedding | 反义词：persistence, evergreenness | 词性: noun

deciduous:/dɪˈsɪdʒuəs/ | adj. | 落叶的；脱落的 |  (of a tree or shrub) shedding its leaves annually. Also: falling off or shed at a particular season, stage of growth, etc. | The oak is a deciduous tree. 橡树是一种落叶树。| 近义词：shedding, caducous | 反义词：evergreen, persistent | 词性: adjective
