dusken:/ˈdʌskən/ | v. | 变昏暗，(使)朦胧，(使)模糊 | to make or become dim or dark, as at dusk; to darken slightly; to obscure or make indistinct | The setting sun duskened the landscape. 落日使景色变得昏暗。 | 近义词: dim, darken, obscure, cloud | 反义词: brighten, lighten, illuminate | 词性: 动词

词源:  From Middle English *dusken, from dusk +‎ -en (infinitival suffix). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *duskaz (“dark, dusky”). Cognate with Dutch dusken (“to grow dusk, darken”).
