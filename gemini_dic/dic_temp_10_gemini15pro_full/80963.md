gracelike: /ˈɡreɪsˌlaɪk/
adjective
优雅的，优美的
Resembling or characteristic of grace; elegant and refined.
Her movements were gracelike, flowing and effortless. 她的动作优雅流畅，毫不费力。
She entered the room with a gracelike demeanor. 她优雅地走进房间。
近义词: graceful, elegant, refined, poised, elegant, stylish
反义词: awkward, clumsy, ungainly, graceless
词性: adjective
词源: grace + like.  "Grace" comes from the Latin "gratia," meaning "grace, favor, thanks."  "Like" comes from the Old English "lic," meaning "like, similar."
