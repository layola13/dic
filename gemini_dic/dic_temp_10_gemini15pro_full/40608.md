convenably:/kənˈviːniəbli/ | adv. | 适当地，方便地 | In a suitable or convenient manner. | The meeting was convenably scheduled for Tuesday afternoon. 会议被适当地安排在星期二下午。 |  The documents were convenably filed for easy access. 文件被方便地归档以便于取用。 | 近义词：suitably, appropriately, conveniently, handily | 反义词：inconveniently, inappropriately, unsuitably | 词性: 副词

词源:
From Late Middle English *convenable, from Anglo-Norman convenable, conveniable, from Old French convenable, from convenir ‘to agree, be suitable’, from Latin convenīre ‘to come together, assemble, agree’, from con- ‘together’ + venīre ‘to come’.
