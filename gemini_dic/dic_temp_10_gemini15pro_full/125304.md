multiglazed:/ˌmʌltiˈɡleɪzd/
adjective
多层玻璃的
Having multiple layers of glass, typically for insulation or soundproofing.
The multiglazed windows helped keep the house warm in the winter.  多层玻璃窗帮助房子在冬天保持温暖。
The studio had multiglazed windows to reduce noise pollution.  工作室有多层玻璃窗以减少噪音污染。
近义词:double-glazed, triple-glazed
反义词:single-glazed
词性:adjective
