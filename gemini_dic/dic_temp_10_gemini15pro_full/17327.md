be-of-service:/biː əv ˈsɜːrvɪs/
adjective phrase
乐于助人；提供服务
being helpful or useful to someone.

例句：
The staff are always happy to be of service.  员工总是乐于提供服务。

He was eager to be of service to his community. 他渴望为他的社区服务。


近义词：helpful, useful, accommodating, obliging
反义词：unhelpful, useless, inconsiderate

词性：adjective phrase
