extrapolation:/ɪkˌstræpəˈleɪʃn/| n.| 外推法；推断；推测 | The act or process of estimating something by assuming that existing trends will continue or similar methods will be applicable. |  Scientists used extrapolation to predict future climate patterns. 科学家们用外推法来预测未来的气候模式。| estimation, projection, inference | interpolation | noun
extrapolate:/ɪkˈstræpəleɪt/| v.| 外推；推断；推测 | To infer or estimate by extending or projecting known information. |  We can extrapolate from current trends to predict future sales. 我们可以根据目前的趋势推断未来的销售额。|  infer, deduce, project | interpolate | verb
