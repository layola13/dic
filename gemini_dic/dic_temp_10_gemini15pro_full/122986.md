monander:/məˈnændər/| adj.|單雄蕊的；只有一枚雄蕊的|Having a single stamen.|This flower is monander, meaning it has only one stamen.|近义词:unistaiminate|反义词:polyandrous|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Greek *monos* "single" + *aner* "man" (genitive *andros*), referring to the stamen.
