aphoristic:/ˌæfəˈrɪstɪk/ | adj. | 格言式的；警句式的 | Concise and forcefully expressive, like an aphorism. |  Her writing style is aphoristic and memorable. 她的写作风格简洁精辟，令人难忘。 | The professor's lectures were filled with aphoristic wisdom.  教授的讲座充满了格言式的智慧。 | 近义词: pithy, epigrammatic, concise, succinct, terse | 反义词: prolix, verbose, rambling |词性: adjective
