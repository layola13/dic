antambulacral:/ˌæntæmˈbjʊləkrəl/
adjective
管足的；反步带的
Relating to or situated on the side of a starfish or similar echinoderm opposite to that on which the ambulacral groove is located.
The antambulacral plates provide protection and support to the starfish's body.  管足板为海星的身体提供保护和支撑。
The antambulacral spines of this species are particularly long and sharp.  这个物种的反步带刺特别长且尖锐。
Synonyms: abactinal
Antonyms: ambulacral
词性: adjective
