nonintrospective:/ˌnɒnɪntrəˈspɛktɪv/
adjective
非内省的，不反省的
Not given to or characterized by introspection; not examining one's own thoughts and feelings.
He's a remarkably nonintrospective person, rarely dwelling on his own motivations. 他是一个非常不内省的人，很少思考自己的动机。
She led a busy, nonintrospective life, focused on external achievements. 她过着忙碌而不内省的生活，专注于外在的成就。
近义词: unreflective, outward-looking, unselfconscious
反义词: introspective, reflective, self-analyzing
词性: adjective
