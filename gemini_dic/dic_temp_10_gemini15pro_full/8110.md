anti-abortion:/ˌæntiəˈbɔːrʃən/ | adj. | 反堕胎的 | Opposed to abortion; advocating the legal prohibition of abortion. |  The anti-abortion group protested outside the clinic. 反堕胎团体在诊所外抗议。|  Pro-life | Pro-choice, abortion rights | adjective

anti-abortion:/ˌæntiəˈbɔːrʃən/ | n. | 反堕胎主义者；反堕胎运动 | A person or movement opposed to abortion.  | He's a vocal anti-abortion advocate. 他是一位直言不讳的反堕胎倡导者。| Pro-lifer | Pro-choicer | noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From anti- + abortion.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to," derived from Greek "anti." "Abortion" comes from the Latin "abortio," meaning "miscarriage" or "premature birth."
