nonrhymes:/ˈnɒnˌraɪmz/ (British) /ˈnɑːnˌraɪmz/ (American)| n. |非韵体，不押韵的诗|Words or lines that do not rhyme.  A poem or verse composed of nonrhyming lines.|Example: The poem is written in nonrhymes, giving it a modern feel. 这首诗是用非韵体写的，给它一种现代感。|Synonym: blank verse, free verse|Antonym: rhymes, rhyming verse|Noun
