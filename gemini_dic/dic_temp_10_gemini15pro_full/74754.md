freewheeling:/ˈfriːˌwiːlɪŋ/| adj.|自由放任的，无拘无束的，随心所欲的|acting in a relaxed and unconcerned way, without obeying any rules or restrictions|He has a freewheeling style of management. 他有一种自由放任的管理风格。|近义词:uninhibited, unrestrained, easygoing, relaxed|反义词:constrained, restricted, inhibited|词性:形容词

freewheeling:/ˈfriːˌwiːlɪŋ/| n.|自由放任，随心所欲|the act of behaving in a free and unrestrained way|The freewheeling of the 1960s led to significant social changes.  60年代的自由放任导致了重大的社会变革。|近义词:unconstraint, abandon|反义词:restriction, constraint|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From free + wheel + -ing.  "Free wheel" refers to a mechanism on a bicycle or other vehicle that allows the driven wheels to rotate independently of the pedals or engine. This mechanical sense led to the figurative meaning of acting independently and without restraint.
