embitter:/ɪmˈbɪtər/ | vt. | 使怨恨；使痛苦 | To cause (someone) to feel bitterness or resentment. |

The constant criticism embittered her against her boss.  她不断受到批评，使她对老板心生怨恨。

His failure to get the promotion embittered him.  他升职失败，使他感到痛苦。

近义词: aggravate, exasperate, sour, poison | 反义词: sweeten, gratify, please | 词性: 动词
