anti-western:/ˈæntiˈwɛstən/
adj.|反西方的，反对西方的|Opposed to or hostile towards the values, culture, or political systems of Western countries, particularly Europe and North America.|The anti-western sentiment is growing in some parts of the world. 世界某些地区反西方情绪日益高涨。|anti-West, non-Western|pro-Western, Westernized|adjective
anti-western:/ˌæntiˈwɛstən/
n.|反西方主义者|A person who is opposed to or hostile towards Western culture, values, and influence.|Some anti-westerns believe that globalization is a form of Western cultural imperialism. 一些反西方主义者认为全球化是西方文化帝国主义的一种形式。|opponent of Western influence, critic of Western culture|supporter of Western influence, advocate for Western culture|noun
