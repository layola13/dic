bondages:/ˈbɒndɪdʒɪz/
noun
束缚，奴役
the state of being bound or enslaved
The people lived in bondages for many years.  这些人被奴役了很多年。

近义词: captivity, slavery, confinement, restraint, oppression
反义词: freedom, liberty, liberation, independence
