mammonitish:/mæˈmɒnɪtɪʃ/
adjective
拜金的，贪婪的
Characterized by a devotion to wealth and material possessions; greedy.
He lived a mammonitish existence, caring only about accumulating more money. 他过着拜金的生活，只关心积累更多的钱。
The company's mammonitish pursuit of profit put the environment at risk. 该公司对利润的贪婪追求使环境处于危险之中。
近义词: avaricious, greedy, materialistic, mercenary
反义词: generous, altruistic, charitable
词性: adjective

词源: From Mammon, the Aramaic word for wealth, often personified as a false god.  The suffix "-ish" transforms it into an adjective meaning "characteristic of Mammon."
