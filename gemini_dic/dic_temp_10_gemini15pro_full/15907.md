ballpoint:/ˈbɔːlpɔɪnt/| n. |圆珠笔|A pen that has a tiny ball bearing as its writing tip. The ball rotates as you write, transferring ink from a cartridge onto the paper.|I prefer using a ballpoint for everyday writing. 我更喜欢用圆珠笔进行日常书写。|Synonyms: pen, biro|Antonyms: fountain pen, quill|noun

ballpoints:/ˈbɔːlpɔɪnts/| n. |圆珠笔（复数）|Plural form of "ballpoint," referring to more than one ballpoint pen.|I have several ballpoints in my desk drawer. 我的书桌抽屉里有几支圆珠笔。|Synonyms: pens, biros|Antonyms: fountain pens, quills|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "ball" + "point."  "Ball" referring to the small rotating ball at the tip, and "point" indicating the writing tip of the pen.  The term became popular in the mid-20th century with the widespread adoption of this type of pen.
