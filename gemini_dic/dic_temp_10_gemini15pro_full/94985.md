in-the-hay:/ɪn ðə heɪ/ | adj. | 在干草堆里；发生性关系；性交| Being in a bed of hay; having sexual intercourse. |  The couple were discovered in-the-hay in the barn. 这对情侣被发现在谷仓的干草堆里做爱。| 近义词: making love, having sex, sleeping with | 反义词:  abstinent, chaste | 词性: 形容词

词源: This idiom originates from the rural practice of using haylofts for romantic encounters. The softness and seclusion of the hay provided a discreet location for such activities.  "Hay" itself comes from Old English *hēg.
