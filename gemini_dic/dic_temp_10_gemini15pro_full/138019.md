noons:/nuːnz/
noun
中午; 正午
the time of day when the sun is highest in the sky
例句：We usually eat lunch at noons. 我们通常中午吃午饭。

例句：The temperature is highest at noons. 中午的温度最高。

近义词：midday, lunchtime
反义词：midnight
词性：noun
