half-acre:/ˌhæf ˈeɪkər/ |n.|半英亩|A unit of land area equal to half an acre (approximately 2,023 square meters).|They live on a half-acre lot. 他们住在一块半英亩的地皮上。|
half-acre:/ˌhæf ˈeɪkər/ |adj.|半英亩的|Relating to or consisting of half an acre.|They own a half-acre plot of land.  他们拥有一块半英亩的土地。


Etymology:
"half" (Old English healf) + "acre" (Old English æcer)
