bza:/ˈbzɑː/ | n. |苯并[a]蒽, 一种多环芳烃|A polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbon, a five-ring fused aromatic system consisting of a benzene ring fused to a naphthalene moiety. This molecule can be viewed as an anthracene molecule with an additional benzene ring fused to it.|Benzo[a]anthracene (Bza) is a potent mutagen and carcinogen found in cigarette smoke.|近义词: polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbon (PAH), benz[a]anthracene|反义词:  (No direct antonym for a chemical compound)|词性:noun
