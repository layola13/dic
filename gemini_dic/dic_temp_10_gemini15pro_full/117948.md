membraniferous:/ˌmɛmbrəˈnɪfərəs/
adjective
膜的；有膜的；产生膜的
Having or bearing a membrane or membranes.

The membraniferous wings of the bat allow it to fly.
蝙蝠的膜状翅膀使它能够飞行。

Certain cells are membraniferous, containing internal membranes that compartmentalize their functions.
某些细胞是有膜的，包含内部膜，这些膜将其功能区分开来。

近义词: membranous
反义词: non-membranous
词性: adjective
