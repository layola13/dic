martyrdoms:/ˈmɑːrtərdəmz/
n.
殉道；殉教；殉难
The suffering of death on account of adherence to a cause, especially to one's religious beliefs.
The early Christians suffered many martyrdoms. 早期的基督徒遭受了许多殉难。

近义词:sacrifices, immolations, sufferings
反义词:deliverances, rescues

词性:名词(plural)
