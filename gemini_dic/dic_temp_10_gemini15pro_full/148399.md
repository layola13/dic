overstarched:/ˌoʊvərˈstɑːrtʃt/
adjective
浆过头的；浆得硬挺的
Stiff and unyielding due to excessive starching.
Her overstarched collar chafed her neck. 她浆得硬挺的衣领磨擦着她的脖子。
近义词:stiff, rigid, inflexible
反义词:soft, limp, flexible
词性:adjective

词源:
From over- + starched, past participle of starch.  The prefix "over-" indicates an excess or too much.  "Starch" refers to a carbohydrate used to stiffen fabrics.
