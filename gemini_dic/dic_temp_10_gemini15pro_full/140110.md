off-putting:/ˌɒfˈpʊtɪŋ/ /ˌɔːfˈpʊtɪŋ/| adj.|令人不快的；倒胃口的；令人反感的|Unpleasant and discouraging; tending to repel or deter.|His off-putting manner made it difficult to make friends. 他令人反感的举止让他很难交到朋友。|

The smell of stale cigarettes was off-putting.  陈腐的香烟味令人作呕。|近义词：unpleasant, disagreeable, repellent, distasteful, repulsive|反义词：attractive, appealing, pleasant, inviting|词性:形容词
