capitalization:/ˌkæpɪtələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 大写；资本化；资本总额 | The act of writing or printing with an initial capital letter or in capital letters. The conversion of income or assets into capital. |  He insisted on the capitalization of the word "God." 他坚持“上帝”这个词的首字母要大写。| synonyms: uppercasing, writing in capitals | antonyms: lowercasing | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *capitalizatio, from Latin capitalizare "to provide with a head, to write with a capital letter," from capitalis "of the head," from caput "head."
