hermeneuticses:/ˌhɜːrməˈnjuːtɪksɪz/
n.
解释学；诠释学
The study and theory of interpretation, especially the interpretation of biblical texts or literary texts.
Hermeneuticses encompasses a wide range of approaches to understanding texts.  解释学包含了理解文本的各种方法。
Modern hermeneuticses goes beyond simply deciphering words. 现代解释学超越了简单的解读文字。
近义词：interpretation, exegesis
反义词:misinterpretation
词性:noun
