amobiography:/ˌeɪmoʊbaɪˈɒɡrəfi/
noun
自我传记；自传
A biography written by the subject about his or her own life.
Example: The celebrity's amobiography became a bestseller shortly after its release.  这位名人的自传在发行后不久就成为了畅销书。
Example:  Writing an amobiography can be a cathartic experience. 写自传可以是一种宣泄的经历。
Synonyms: autobiography, memoir, life story
Antonyms: biography
词性: 名词
