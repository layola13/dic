devoice:/diːˈvɔɪs/
verb
使...失声；使...无声
to deprive of voice or vocal sound; to make voiceless.
The surgery devoiced her completely.  手术使她完全失声了。
The villain in the movie had a devoiced laugh, adding to his creepiness.  电影中的反派笑声嘶哑，更增添了他的恐怖感。
synonyms: silence, mute, hush
antonyms: vocalize, voice, sound
词性: 动词
