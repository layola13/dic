nonflirtatiousnesses:/ˌnɑːnflɜːrˈteɪʃəsnəsəz/
noun
无调情；不轻浮
lack of flirtatious behavior; absence of coquetry.
Her nonflirtatiousnesses made some people think she was aloof. 她不轻浮的态度让一些人觉得她很冷漠。
Near Synonyms: seriousness, reserve, modesty, demureness
Antonyms: flirtatiousness, coquetry, suggestiveness
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + flirtatious (inclined to flirt) + -ness (state of being) + -es (plural).  Ultimately from Old Norse *flirt (to move quickly and lightly).
