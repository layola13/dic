antibillboard:/ˌæntiˈbɪlbɔːrd/
n.|反广告牌；抵制广告牌|A sign or campaign opposing the use of billboards.  It can also refer to an act of vandalism or art that obscures or alters a billboard's message.|The local artist created an antibillboard by painting a mural over a cigarette advertisement.|Synonyms: counter-advertising, billboard opposition|Antonyms: pro-billboard, billboard support|noun
Etymology->
From anti- + billboard.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to," originating from Greek. "Billboard" refers to a large outdoor advertising display.
