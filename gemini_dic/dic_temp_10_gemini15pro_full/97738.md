initialism:/ɪˈnɪʃəlɪzəm/
n.
首字母缩略词
An abbreviation consisting of initial letters pronounced separately (e.g. BBC).
The BBC is a well-known initialism. 英国广播公司是一个著名的首字母缩略词。
近义词: abbreviation, acronym
反义词: (No direct antonym for a linguistic term)
