burghmote:/ˈbɜːrɡmoʊt/| n.|市民大会，市政会议|A town meeting in a burgh, especially in Anglo-Saxon England, for discussing municipal affairs.|The burghmote met to discuss the new tax levies.|Synonyms: town meeting, municipal assembly, council|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "private gathering")|Noun
