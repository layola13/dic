oxygenicity:/ˌɒksɪdʒəˈnɪsɪti/
n.
氧性，产氧性，含氧量
The oxygenicity of water is crucial for aquatic life. 水的含氧量对水生生物至关重要。
The researchers measured the oxygenicity of the new compound. 研究人员测量了这种新化合物的产氧性。
近义词: oxygen content, oxygen level
反义词: anoxia, hypoxia
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From oxygen + -icity (suffix forming nouns of quality, state, or degree).
