intermedial:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdiəl/| adj.|中间的；媒介的；介于两种媒介之间的|Existing or occurring between different media.  Relating to or involving a combination of different media, such as film, video, and music.|The project explored the intermedial connections between literature and film.|近义词：interdisciplinary, multimedia, mixed-media|反义词: unimedial, single-medium|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From inter- + medial. "Inter-" is a Latin prefix meaning "between" or "among." "Medial" comes from the Latin word "medius," meaning "middle."
