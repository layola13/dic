overchildish: /ˌoʊvərˈtʃaɪldɪʃ/ | adj. | 过于幼稚的，孩子气的 | Excessively or inappropriately childish; behaving in a way that is too immature for one's age. |  His overchildish behavior embarrassed everyone at the meeting.  他过于幼稚的行为让会议上的每个人都感到尴尬。

His overchildish tantrum in the store was quite a scene. 他在商店里过于孩子气的发脾气弄得场面很难堪。| 近义词: immature, puerile, infantile | 反义词: mature, grown-up, adult | 词性: adjective

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "childish" (adjective meaning "like a child").  "Childish" comes from "child" (Old English cild) + "-ish" (suffix forming adjectives).
