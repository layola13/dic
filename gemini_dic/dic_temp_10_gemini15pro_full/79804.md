glycocholate: /ˌɡlaɪkoʊˈkoʊleɪt/ | n. | 甘氨胆酸盐 | A salt of glycocholic acid, a bile acid. It aids in the digestion and absorption of fats. | Bile salts such as glycocholate emulsify fats in the small intestine. 胆汁盐如甘氨胆酸盐在小肠中乳化脂肪。| Glycocholate is secreted into the bile and helps solubilize cholesterol. 甘氨胆酸盐分泌到胆汁中，有助于溶解胆固醇。 | synonyms: bile salt, cholates | antonyms: None | noun
