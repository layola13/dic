nibbing:/ˈnɪbɪŋ/| n. v.|轻咬；细啄；一点点地吃；小口地喝|The act of taking small bites or sips.  It can also refer to making small, critical remarks or complaints.|The rabbit was nibbling on a carrot. 兔子正在啃一根胡萝卜。|Synonyms:  picking at, pecking at, gnawing, snacking on, carping, criticizing|Antonyms:  gobbling, devouring, gulping, praising, complimenting|Noun/Verb

nibble:/ˈnɪbl/| n. v.|轻咬；细啄；小口；少量|A small bite or sip. Also, a small amount of food.|She took a nibble of the cheese. 她尝了一小口奶酪。|Synonyms: bite, morsel, taste, sip, snack|Antonyms:  gulp, mouthful, feast|Noun/Verb

nibble away at (something): 逐渐减少或消耗  He’s been nibbling away at his savings. 他一直在慢慢地消耗他的积蓄。
nibble on (something):  小口地吃  She nibbled on a piece of fruit. 她小口地吃了一块水果。
have a nibble:  吃一小口  Would you like to have a nibble of my cake? 你想尝尝我的蛋糕吗？

not nibble at (something): 对…不感兴趣  The fish weren't nibbling at the bait. 鱼对鱼饵不感兴趣。
