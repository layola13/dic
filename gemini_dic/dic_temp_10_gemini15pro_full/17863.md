beefiness:/ˈbiːfinəs/| n. |肌肉发达，强壮；（声音）浑厚 |The quality of being beefy; muscularity or strength.  It can also refer to a full, rich quality, especially of a voice.|He admired the beefiness of the weightlifter. 他很羡慕举重运动员的强壮体格。|近义词：muscularity, strength, huskiness (of voice)|反义词：frailty, weakness, thinness|词性:名词
