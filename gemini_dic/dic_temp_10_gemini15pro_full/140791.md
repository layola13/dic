on's:/wʌnz/ |pron. |某人的；属于某人的|Possessive form of "one," used to indicate ownership or belonging.  It's a less formal alternative to "one's." |That's not my responsibility, it's on's own problem. 那不是我的责任，那是某人自己的问题。|One's belongings were scattered across the room. 某人的物品散落在房间各处。|近义词: one's |反义词: nobody's, no one's|词性: 代词

注意事项:

"on's" is very informal and generally considered non-standard.  While you might hear it in casual spoken English, it's best avoided in formal writing or speech.  Using "one's" is generally preferred.
