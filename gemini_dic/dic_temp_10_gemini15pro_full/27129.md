calisaya:/ˌkælɪˈseɪə/
noun
金鸡纳树皮；金鸡纳树
The bark of the Cinchona calisaya tree, used medicinally.
Historically, calisaya bark was used to treat malaria.  历史上，金鸡纳树皮被用来治疗疟疾。
Synonyms: cinchona bark, Peruvian bark, Jesuit's bark
Antonyms: N/A
词性: 名词

Etymology: From Spanish *calisaya*, from Quechua *qalisaya*, *qali* "good, healthy" + *saya* "bark".
词源：源于西班牙语 *calisaya*，来自盖丘亚语 *qalisaya*，*qali* 意为“好的，健康的” + *saya* 意为“树皮”。
