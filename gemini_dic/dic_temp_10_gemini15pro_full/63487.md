entryway:/ˈɛntriˌweɪ/ | n. | 入口，门厅 | An entryway is an area, such as a hall or lobby, that is at the entrance to a building or room. |  The entryway was decorated with a beautiful vase of flowers. 门厅里装饰着一个漂亮的花瓶。

|  Please leave your shoes in the entryway. 请把你的鞋子放在入口处。| 近义词: entrance, foyer, vestibule, hall | 反义词: exit, way out | noun
