configuring:/kənˈfɪɡərɪŋ/| v.|配置，设定|The process of setting up or arranging something, typically a computer system or software, to operate in a particular way.  It involves selecting options, customizing settings, and integrating different components to meet specific requirements.|He is configuring the network settings. 他正在配置网络设置.|Synonyms: setting up, adjusting, customizing, arranging, tailoring|Antonyms:  dismantling, disassembling|Verb

configuring:/kənˈfɪɡərɪŋ/| adj.|配置的，调整中的|Being in the process of being set up or arranged.|The configuring system is currently unavailable. 正在配置的系统目前不可用。|Synonyms:  being set up, under adjustment|Antonyms:  established, configured|Adjective
