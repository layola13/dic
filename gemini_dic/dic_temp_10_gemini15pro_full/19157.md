bewailingly:/bɪˈweɪlɪŋli/
adverb
悲叹地，哀号地
in a way that expresses great sadness or disappointment
She wept bewailingly over the loss of her pet. 她为失去宠物而悲痛欲绝。
plaintively, mournfully, sorrowfully
stoically, cheerfully
adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From bewail + -ing + -ly.  Bewail comes from the Old English *biwæilan, from bi- (by, near) + wæil (wail).
