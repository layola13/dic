opinionative: /əˈpɪnjənətɪv/ | adj. | 固执己见的；武断的 | Holding stubbornly to one's own opinions, especially without considering the views of others. |  He's very opinionative and doesn't listen to other people's ideas. 他很固执己见，不听取别人的意见。 | Example: She was known for being opinionative and often argued with her colleagues.  她以固执己见而闻名，经常与同事争吵。 | Synonyms: dogmatic, assertive, insistent, adamant, inflexible | Antonyms: open-minded, receptive, flexible, accommodating, agreeable | word type: adjective
