brimstone:/ˈbrɪmˌstoʊn/| n. |硫磺；地狱之火|A yellow nonmetallic element that is very reactive, burns with a blue flame and a suffocating odor, and is used in making gunpowder and matches. Figuratively, it refers to hellfire or divine punishment.|The air reeked of brimstone after the volcano erupted. 火山爆发后，空气中弥漫着硫磺味。|Synonyms: sulfur, hellfire|Antonyms: heaven, paradise|noun

brimstones:/ˈbrɪmˌstoʊnz/| n. |硫磺（复数）；地狱之火（复数）|Plural form of brimstone, referring to multiple instances of sulfur or hellfire.|The preacher warned of the brimstones of hell awaiting sinners. 传教士警告说，地狱的硫磺之火正等待着罪人。|Synonyms: sulfurs, hellfires|Antonyms: heavens, paradises|noun
