highhats:/ˈhaɪˌhæts/| n.|踩镲, 高帽钹 (通常用复数形式)|A pair of small cymbals mounted on a stand, played by pressing a foot pedal.|The drummer's highhats were crisp and precise. 鼓手的踩镲又脆又准。|Synonyms: cymbals|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a musical instrument)|noun

high-hat:/ˈhaɪˈhæt/| adj.|自命不凡的，高傲的|Snobbish, condescending.|She gave him a high-hat look. 她高傲地看了他一眼。|Synonyms: snobbish, arrogant, condescending, haughty, supercilious|Antonyms: humble, modest, down-to-earth|adjective
