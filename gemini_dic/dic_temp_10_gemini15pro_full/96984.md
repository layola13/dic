infantilisms:/ˈɪnfənˌtɪlɪzəmz/ | n. |幼稚的言行；童稚症|The recurrence of infantilisms in her speech was concerning. 她的讲话中反复出现幼稚的言行令人担忧。|例句：His infantilisms became more pronounced under stress.  他在压力下变得更加幼稚。

例句：She exhibited several infantilisms, such as thumb-sucking and temper tantrums. 她表现出几种幼稚的行为，例如吮拇指和发脾气。 |近义词：childishness, immaturity, puerility|反义词: maturity, sophistication|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From infantile (幼稚的) + -ism (表示状态或行为的后缀).  Ultimately from Latin infans (unable to speak, infant).
