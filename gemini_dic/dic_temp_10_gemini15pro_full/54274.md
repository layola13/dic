disguisable:/dɪsˈɡaɪzəbl/| adj.|可伪装的，可假扮的|Able to be disguised or concealed; capable of being altered in appearance or nature so as to be unrecognizable.|She wore a disguisable wig and sunglasses. 她戴着可伪装的假发和太阳镜。|The spy's true identity was disguisable. 间谍的真实身份是可以伪装的。|synonyms: concealable, maskable, hidable|antonyms: obvious, apparent, noticeable|adjective
