interactable:/ˌɪntərˈæktəbl/ | adj. | 可交互的，可互动的 | Able to interact or be interacted with, especially relating to computer systems or devices. | Example: The game features highly interactable environments.  这个游戏拥有高度可交互的环境。| Example: The museum exhibit is designed to be interactable, encouraging visitors to touch and explore.  博物馆的展览被设计成可交互的，鼓励游客触摸和探索。| 近义词: interactive, responsive | 反义词: static, unresponsive | 词性: adjective

词源: From interact + -able.  Interact comes from Latin inter- (between) + agere (to do, to drive, to act).
