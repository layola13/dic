mainpin:/ˈmeɪnpɪn/| n.|主要支点，关键人物，核心人物|A main support or important person in a group or organization.  The key element on which something depends.|例句：He was the mainpin of the whole operation. 他是整个行动的关键人物。

例句：The mainpin of their argument was that the evidence was flawed.  他们论证的主要支点是证据有缺陷。|近义词：linchpin, cornerstone, keystone, kingpin|反义词:  minor figure, subordinate, unimportant person|词性: 名词
