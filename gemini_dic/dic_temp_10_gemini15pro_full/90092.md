hostler:/ˈhɒstlə(r) (UK), ˈhɑːstlər (US)| n.|马夫；旅店老板；大学宿舍管理员|A person who takes care of horses, especially at an inn or stable; a person who manages a hostel or dormitory.| The hostler led the horses to the stable. 马夫把马牵到马厩。|Synonyms: stableman, groom, innkeeper, manager|Antonyms: guest, lodger, boarder|noun


hostler:/ˈhɑːstlər (US)| n.|骗子；妓女(主要指男性)|A person who tricks others into giving them money; a prostitute (chiefly male).| The hostler cheated the tourists out of their money. 骗子骗走了游客的钱。|Synonyms: swindler, con artist, hustler, hooker (for prostitute)|Antonyms: philanthropist, benefactor (for swindler)|noun
