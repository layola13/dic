antiexpressionists:/ˌæntiɪkˈspreʃənɪsts/| n.|反表现主义者|Artists or writers who oppose or react against expressionism, often emphasizing formality or objectivity over emotional intensity.|The antiexpressionists favored a return to classical forms and a rejection of personal narratives.|近义词:formalists, classicists, objectivists|反义词:expressionists|词性:名词
