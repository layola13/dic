gabble:/ˈɡæbəl/| n. v. |快而含糊不清地说；急促而不清楚的话|rapid, indistinct, and often foolish talk; to speak rapidly and indistinctly|The auctioneer gabbled so fast I couldn't understand a word. 拍卖师说得太快了，我一个字也听不懂。|近义词：babble, chatter, prattle, jabber|反义词:articulate, enunciate|词性:名词,动词
