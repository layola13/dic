mistresshood:/ˈmɪstrɪshʊd/| n. |女主人身份，情妇身份|The state or position of being a mistress (either of a household or a lover).|She enjoyed the privileges of mistresshood without the responsibilities. 她享受着女主人的特权，却没有女主人的责任。| Her brief mistresshood ended badly. 她短暂的情妇生涯结局很糟糕。 |近义词：dominion, control (for mistress of a household);  concubinage (for mistress as a lover) |反义词: servitude, subordination (for mistress of a household)|词性: noun
