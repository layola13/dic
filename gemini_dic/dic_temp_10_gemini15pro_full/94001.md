impalement:/ɪmˈpeɪlmənt/| n. |刺穿，穿刺；（尤指）钉死|The act of impaling something or someone on a sharp object.  The impalement of a body on a stake. |The museum displayed gruesome exhibits, including the impalement of a medieval criminal.  博物馆展出了可怕的展品，包括一个中世纪罪犯被刺穿的场景。|近义词：piercing, transfixion, spearing|反义词: removal, extraction|noun
