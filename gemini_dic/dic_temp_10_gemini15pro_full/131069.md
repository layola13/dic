nonbaronial:/nɒnbəˈrəʊniəl/
adjective
非男爵的，不属于男爵的
Not of or pertaining to a baron or barony; not baronial.
The nonbaronial estate was sold to a wealthy merchant. 这片非男爵的庄园被卖给了一位富有的商人。
The family lived in a comfortable, though nonbaronial, country house.  这家人住在一栋舒适的乡间别墅里，虽然它不属于男爵。
Synonyms: non-noble, common, plebeian
Antonyms: baronial, aristocratic, noble
word class: adjective
