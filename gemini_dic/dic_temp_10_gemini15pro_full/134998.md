nonnebulously:/ˌnɒnˈnɛbjʊləsli/
adv.
清晰地，不含糊地
clearly, without vagueness or ambiguity
The instructions were written nonnebulously, leaving no room for misinterpretation.  说明书写得非常清晰，没有留下任何误解的余地。
He explained the complex concept nonnebulously, making it easy for everyone to understand. 他清晰地解释了这个复杂的概念，使每个人都容易理解。
近义词:clearly, distinctly, explicitly, lucidly, perspicuously
反义词:nebulously, vaguely, obscurely, ambiguously
词性:副词


词源:
non- (prefix): not, opposite of
nebulous (adjective): cloudy, hazy, vague
-ly (suffix): forming adverbs
