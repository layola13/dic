happens:/ˈhæpənz/| v.|发生，碰巧|Something unexpected happened. 发生了意想不到的事情。|近义词：occurs, takes place, transpires|反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，根据语境可用prevents, avoids等表示阻止发生)|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *happenen, from Old Norse *happna (“to happen, befall, turn out”), from happ (“chance, luck, fortune”).
