oblige:/əˈblaɪdʒ/ /əˈblaɪdʒ/ | vt.| 迫使；施恩惠于；答应，同意 |To compel someone to do something; to do a favor for someone; to make someone grateful.|  I felt obliged to help them. 我觉得有义务帮助他们。| 近义词：compel, force, require, favor, accommodate | 反义词：disoblige | 词性:动词

oblige:/əˈblaɪdʒ/ /əˈblaɪdʒ/ | vt.| 责成；使负有义务 |To bind morally or legally.| The law obliges parents to send their children to school. 法律规定父母必须送子女上学。 | 近义词：bind, compel, obligate | 反义词：exempt | 词性:动词


词源: From Old French obliger, from Latin obligare (“to bind, tie up, fasten”), from ob (“towards”) + ligare (“to bind”).
