counterinstances:/ˈkaʊntərˌɪnstənsɪz/
noun
反例，反证
Examples that contradict a general rule or claim.
This theory is disproved by numerous counterinstances. 这个理论被大量的反例证伪了。
The professor cited several counterinstances to refute the student's argument.  教授引用了几个反例来反驳学生的论点。
Synonyms: counterexamples, exceptions
Antonyms: examples, proofs
