non-violent:/ˌnɒnˈvaɪələnt/ | adj. | 非暴力的 | Not violent; peaceful. |  Example: They used non-violent methods of protest. 他们使用了非暴力的抗议方式。 | Example:  The movement advocated for non-violent resistance. 这场运动提倡非暴力抵抗。 | Synonyms: peaceful, pacifist, non-aggressive | Antonyms: violent, aggressive, forceful | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + violent.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not". "Violent" comes from the Latin "violentus", meaning "vehement, impetuous, boisterous; done by force, forcible".
