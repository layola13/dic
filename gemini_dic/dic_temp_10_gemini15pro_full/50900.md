desertlessly:/dɪˈzɜːtlɪsli/
adjective
无功受禄地，不应得地
without merit or deserving reward.
He desertlessly received a promotion, having contributed little to the project. 他不应得地得到了晋升，因为他对这个项目贡献甚微。
近义词: undeservedly, unworthily, unjustly
反义词: deservedly, worthily, justly
词性: adjective

词源:
From "desert" (meaning deserving reward or punishment) + "-less" (suffix indicating absence of something) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix forming an adjective).  Ultimately from Latin "deserere" (to abandon).
