overservilely:/ˌoʊvərˈsɜːrvɪlli/| adv.|过度殷勤地，奴颜婢膝地|Excessively eager to serve; in a fawning or subservient manner.  This word describes an action performed with excessive humility or deference, often to the point of being insincere or sycophantic.|He bowed overservilely to the king, hoping to gain favor. 他奴颜婢膝地向国王鞠躬，希望能得到青睐。|近义词：fawningly, obsequiously, subserviently|反义词: independently, assertively|词性:副词

Etymology:
From over- + servile + -ly.  "Over-" indicates excess, "servile" refers to a slave-like or excessively submissive behavior, and "-ly" forms the adverb.
