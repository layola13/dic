eveners:/ˈiːvənərz/
n.
平衡器，使相等的人或物
Tools or devices used to make things level or equal.
The carpenters used eveners to ensure the floorboards were perfectly aligned. 木匠们使用平衡器来确保地板完全对齐。
levelers, equalizers
