impoverish: /ɪmˈpɒvərɪʃ/ (British) /ɪmˈpɑːvərɪʃ/ (American) | vt. | 使贫穷；使贫瘠；使枯竭 | To make poor; to deprive of strength, richness, or fertility. | The war impoverished the country. 战争使这个国家变得贫穷。|  The overuse of fertilizers can impoverish the soil. 过度使用化肥会使土壤贫瘠。 | synonyms: deplete, exhaust, bankrupt, ruin | antonyms: enrich, enhance, improve | verb
