autoplastic:/ˌɔːtəˈplæstɪk/
adjective
自体成形的；自我调节的
Relating to adaptation by altering one's own bodily processes or behavior, rather than the external environment.  Adaptation achieved through internal change rather than changes in the environment.
The patient demonstrated autoplastic adaptation to stress by developing coping mechanisms.  这个病人通过发展应对机制表现出对压力的自体成形适应。
Autoplastic changes allowed the organism to survive in the harsh environment. 自体成形的变化使生物体能够在恶劣的环境中生存。
近义词: self-adjusting, self-regulating, adaptive
反义词: alloplastic
词性: adjective
