hereditary:/həˈredɪteri/ /hɪˈredɪteri/| adj.|遗传的；世袭的|Transmitted or capable of being transmitted genetically from parent to offspring. Passed down by inheritance.|例句：Baldness is often hereditary. 秃顶通常是遗传的。

例句：The disease is hereditary. 这种疾病是遗传性的。|近义词：genetic, inherited, inborn, congenital|反义词：acquired|词性：形容词
