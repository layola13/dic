hydriform:/ˈhaɪdrɪfɔːrm/
n.|氢化物|A binary compound of hydrogen with a more electropositive element or group.|The most common hydriform is water (H₂O). 最常见的氢化物是水。|
hydriform:/ˈhaɪdrɪfɔːrm/
n.|氢阴离子|A negatively charged ion consisting of a hydrogen atom and an extra electron (H⁻).|Hydriforms are important in certain chemical reactions. 氢阴离子在某些化学反应中很重要。|
hydriform:/ˈhaɪdrɪfɔːrm/
adj.|含氢的|Containing hydrogen.|Hydriform compounds are studied in chemistry. 含氢化合物在化学中被研究。|
