onenesses:/ˈwʌn.nɪs.ɪz/
n.
统一性，一致性，单一性
The state or quality of being one; unity.  The quality of being united or undivided.
The onenesses of mind and body.  身心合一
近义词：unity, unification, wholeness, cohesion, solidarity
反义词：division, separation, disunity, fragmentation
词性:名词
