callipee:/ˈkælɪpiː/
noun
羊肠线; (尤指缝合伤口用的)肠线
A callipee is a surgical suture made from the intestines of sheep, especially used for stitching wounds.
The surgeon used a callipee to close the incision.  外科医生用羊肠线缝合了切口。
近义词:catgut, suture
反义词: (无明显的反义词，可考虑与现代合成缝合材料对比，例如: synthetic suture)
词性:n.
