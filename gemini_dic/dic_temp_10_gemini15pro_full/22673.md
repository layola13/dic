borderer:/ˈbɔːrdərər/ | n. | 边民，边界居民；边境居民 | Someone who lives on or near a border between two countries or regions. |  He was a borderer, familiar with the customs of both nations. 他是一位边民，熟悉两国的风俗习惯。

| Example: The borderers often traded goods across the border illegally.  边民经常非法进行跨境商品交易。 | Synonym: frontiersman, inhabitant | Antonym: city dweller | Noun
