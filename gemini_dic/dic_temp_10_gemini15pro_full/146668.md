overhaul:/ˈoʊvərˌhɔːl/ (US), /ˌəʊvəˈhɔːl/ (UK)| vt. n.|彻底检修，大修；全面改革|To examine and repair something thoroughly, especially a machine or system; a thorough examination and repair.|例句：The engine was completely overhauled.  发动机被彻底检修了。|近义词：renovate, repair, restore, service|反义词：damage, neglect|词性：动词，名词

overhaul:/ˈoʊvərˌhɔːl/ (US), /ˌəʊvəˈhɔːl/ (UK)| n.|彻底检修，大修；全面改革|A thorough examination and repair of a machine or system.|例句：The car is due for an overhaul. 这辆车该大修了。|近义词：renovation, repair, restoration, service|反义词：damage, neglect|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "over" + "hale" (to pull, drag). Originally meant to haul something over for inspection or repair.
