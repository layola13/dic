co:/koʊ/ /kəʊ/ | prefix | 共同，一起 | A prefix meaning "together," "jointly," "mutually," "in common." Derived from Latin "cum" (with). It often assimilates to con-, col-, cor-, com- depending on the following letter. | co-author a book 共同撰写一本书 | 近义词: con-, col-, cor-, com- | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a prefix. Consider words with opposite meanings like "separate," "individual") | 词性: prefix
