nonegregiously:/ˌnɒnɪˈɡriːdʒəsli/ | adv. |不恶劣地，不严重地 | Not in a conspicuously bad or offensive way; mildly. | The mistake, though regrettable, was nonegregiously so.  这个错误虽然令人遗憾，但并不严重。| Her behavior, while unconventional, was nonegregiously offensive. 她的行为虽然不合常规，但并不算太冒犯。 | 近义词: mildly, inoffensively, unobjectionably | 反义词: egregiously, flagrantly, outrageously | 词性: 副词

词源:
nonegregiously 由 none- (not) + egregiously 构成。 egregiously 源自拉丁语 egregius (outstanding, distinguished)，但其现代含义已演变为 "conspicuously bad".
