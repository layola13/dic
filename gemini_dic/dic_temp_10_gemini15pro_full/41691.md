corridors:/ˈkɒrɪdɔːz/ (UK), /ˈkɔːrɪdɔːrz/ (US)| n. |走廊，通道；(比喻)狭长地带；(政治)过道|A long passage in a building or train, with doors and rooms on either side. Also used metaphorically to describe a narrow strip of land or a particular area of activity.|The hotel corridors were dimly lit.  酒店的走廊灯光昏暗。|synonyms: passageway, hallway, aisle, gallery|antonyms:  room, chamber, compartment|noun
