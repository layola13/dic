delve:/dɛlv/| v. |探究，钻研，深入研究|To research something carefully or to search deeply for information. |例句：He delved into the archives looking for clues. 他在档案中仔细搜寻线索。

例句：She delved into her handbag for her keys.  她在手提包里翻找钥匙。|近义词：research, investigate, explore, probe, examine|反义词: skim, glance, ignore|词性:动词
