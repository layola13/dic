demonstrative:/dəˈmɒnstrətɪv/| adj.|公开表露感情的; 感情外露的; 证明性的; 指示的| openly expressing feelings|She's always been a very demonstrative person. 她一直是一个很外向的人。|近义词：expressive, affectionate, effusive|反义词：reserved, undemonstrative, inhibited|词性:adjective
