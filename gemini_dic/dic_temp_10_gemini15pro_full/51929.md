diabetes: /ˌdaɪəˈbiːtiːz/ (US), /ˌdaɪəˈbiːtiːs/ (UK) | n. | 糖尿病 | A metabolic disease characterized by abnormally high blood sugar levels.  It is caused by defects in insulin secretion, insulin action, or both. |  My grandmother has diabetes and has to monitor her blood sugar levels regularly. 我的祖母患有糖尿病，必须定期监测血糖水平。| synonyms: hyperglycemia (high blood sugar is a symptom, not a synonym for the disease itself) | antonyms: hypoglycemia (low blood sugar, again a symptom, not an antonym for the disease) | noun
