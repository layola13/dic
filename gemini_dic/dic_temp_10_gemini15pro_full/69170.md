faxed:/fækst/
|v.|传真（fax的过去式和过去分词）|transmitted (a document or image) via fax machine.|I faxed the contract to the lawyer yesterday. 我昨天把合同传真给律师了。|近义词：sent, transmitted|反义词:received|动词

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I fax important documents every day.  我每天都传真重要文件。
She faxes her reports to the head office weekly. 她每周都把报告传真到总部。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am faxing the document right now. 我现在正在传真文件。
He is faxing the contract as we speak. 我们说话的时候他正在传真合同。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have faxed the documents already. 我已经传真过文件了。
She has faxed all the necessary paperwork. 她已经传真了所有必要的文书工作。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been faxing documents all morning. 我整个早上都在传真文件。
He has been faxing reports for the past hour.  过去一个小时他一直在传真报告。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I faxed the document yesterday. 我昨天传真了文件。
She faxed the information to her client last week. 她上周把信息传真给了她的客户。

I was faxing the document when the power went out.  我正在传真文件的时候停电了。
She was faxing the report when she got the call. 她接到电话时正在传真报告。

She had faxed the information before she left the office. 她在离开办公室之前传真了信息。

PHRASE->
faxed document  传真文件

词源：faxed来源于fax，fax是facsimile的缩写，facsimile来自拉丁语fac simile，意思是“make similar”，“制作相似的东西”。
