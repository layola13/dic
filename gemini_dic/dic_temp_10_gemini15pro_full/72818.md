foley:/ˈfoʊli/| n.|拟音，拟音效果|The foley artist created the sound of footsteps in the snow. 拟音师创作了雪地里脚步声的效果。|The film's foley work was particularly impressive. 这部电影的拟音效果尤其令人印象深刻。|近义词：sound effects|反义词:|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Named after Jack Foley (1891-1967), a Hollywood sound effects artist who developed many of the techniques used in creating sound effects for film.
