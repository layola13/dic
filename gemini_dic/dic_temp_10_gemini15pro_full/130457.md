nonabsorbing:/ˌnɒnəbˈzɔːbɪŋ/ | /ˌnɑːnəbˈsɔːrbɪŋ/ | adj. | 非吸收性的，不吸收的 | Not absorbing or tending to absorb; reflecting or impermeable. | The raincoat is made of a nonabsorbing material. 这件雨衣是用非吸收性材料制成的。 | The surface is nonabsorbing and easy to clean.  这个表面不吸水，很容易清洁。 | 近义词：impermeable, nonporous, repellent | 反义词：absorbing, permeable, porous | 词性：形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
absorbing (adjective): from absorb (verb), from Latin absorbere "to swallow down," from ab- "away" + sorbere "to suck up"
