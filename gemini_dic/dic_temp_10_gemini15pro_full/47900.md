decentest:/ˈdiːsəntist/ | adj. | 最正派的，最得体的 |  Superlative form of "decent":  morally good or acceptable; of an acceptable standard; following accepted practice and behavior | He's the decentest man I know. 他是我认识的最正派的人。|  She wore her decentest dress to the interview. 她穿着她最得体的衣服去面试了。| 近义词: most respectable, most proper, most honorable | 反义词: most indecent, most improper, most dishonorable | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Old French *decent, present participle of decere "be fitting, suitable," from Latin decere "be fitting, proper, becoming," related to decus "ornament, grace, honor," from PIE root *dek- "to take, accept."  The "-est" suffix is a superlative ending.
