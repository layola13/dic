dactyli:/ˈdæktɪlaɪ/
n.
dactyls (复数)
手指，趾；（诗歌的）长短短格
A metrical foot consisting of one long syllable followed by two short syllables or of one stressed syllable followed by two unstressed syllables.  (源自希腊语 daktylos 指“手指”，因其三节结构类似手指)
The poet used dactyli throughout the verse. 诗人整首诗都用了长短短格。
Synonyms: dactyls
Antonyms: None. (No direct antonym for a metrical foot)
