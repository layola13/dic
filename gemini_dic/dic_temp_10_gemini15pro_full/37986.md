complicateness:/kəmˈplɪkɪtnəs/
n.|复杂性，错综复杂|The complicateness of the situation made it difficult to find a solution.  情况的复杂性使得很难找到解决方案。|The sheer complicateness of the legal system can be overwhelming.  法律系统的绝对复杂性可能令人不知所措。|complexity, intricacy, elaborateness|simplicity, straightforwardness|noun


complicate:/ˈkɒmplɪkeɪt/
v.|使复杂化，使变得更困难|Don't complicate matters by bringing up the past. 不要提起过去，把事情复杂化。|The new regulations only complicate the process. 新的规章制度只会使流程更加复杂。|make complex, make difficult, muddle|simplify, clarify|verb


complicated:/ˈkɒmplɪkeɪtɪd/
adj.|复杂的，难懂的|The instructions were so complicated that I couldn't understand them.  说明书太复杂了，我看不懂。|The situation is more complicated than it appears. 情况比表面上看起来更复杂。|complex, intricate, involved|simple, easy, straightforward|adjective
