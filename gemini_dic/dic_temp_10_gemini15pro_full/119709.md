ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *midrif, from Old English *midhrif, from midd- (“mid-”) + hrif (“belly, womb”).  Cognate with Dutch middenrif (“diaphragm”), German Mittelriff (“midriff”). The anatomical sense relating to the diaphragm is older, and the external sense referring to the exposed abdomen is more recent.
