nuraghe:/nuˈraːɡeɪ/ /nuˈrɑːɡeɪ/
n.
努拉吉/努拉盖（一种巨石塔状建筑）
A nuraghe (plural nuraghi, or in Italian, nuragi) is the main type of ancient megalithic edifice found in Sardinia, Italy, developed during the Nuragic Age between 1900 and 730 BC.  They were built of dressed stones laid in courses without mortar (dry stone masonry).
例句：The nuraghe is a remarkable example of prehistoric architecture.  努拉吉是史前建筑的杰出典范。

例句：Many nuraghi are well-preserved and can be visited today. 许多努拉吉保存完好，今天仍然可以参观。
近义词：megalith, monument
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "modern building")
词性: noun
