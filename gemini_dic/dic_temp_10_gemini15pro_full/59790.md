ecotourist: /ˈiːkəʊtʊərɪst/ (UK) /ˈiːkoʊtʊrɪst/ (US) | n. | 生态游客 | A tourist who travels to natural areas, often to support conservation efforts and observe wildlife, while minimizing their environmental impact. | Example: The ecotourists were thrilled to see the rare birds in their natural habitat. 生态游客们很兴奋地在自然栖息地看到了珍稀鸟类。

Example:  Ecotourism is becoming increasingly popular as people seek more sustainable travel options. 随着人们寻求更可持续的旅行选择，生态旅游正变得越来越受欢迎。 | Synonym:  nature tourist, green tourist | Antonym: mass tourist | Noun
