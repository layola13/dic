numerative:/ˈnjuːmərəˌtɪv/ /ˈnuːmərətɪv/| adj.|数词的，表示数量的|Relating to or expressing number; numerical.|Example: Numerative adjectives like "two," "three," and "four" modify nouns to indicate quantity.  像“二”、“三”、“四”这样的数词形容词修饰名词以表示数量。

Example:  The numerative system in this ancient language is quite complex.  这种古代语言中的计数系统相当复杂。|近义词：numerical, quantitative|反义词: qualitative|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *numerativus, from Latin numeratus (past participle of numerare “to number, count”), from numerus "number."
