dashedly:/ˈdæʃɪdli/ | adv. | 猛冲地，急速地，轻率地 | In a manner characterized by sudden, impulsive action; hastily and carelessly. | He dashedly ran across the street without looking. 他轻率地跑过马路，没有看路。|  She dashedly agreed to the proposal without considering the consequences. 她轻率地同意了这个提议，没有考虑后果。 | 近义词： hastily, rashly, impetuously, recklessly | 反义词： cautiously, deliberately, carefully | 词性: 副词

词源: From dash + -ed + -ly.  "Dash" itself comes from Middle English dasshen, of uncertain origin.
