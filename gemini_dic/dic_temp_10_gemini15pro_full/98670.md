insulated:/ˈɪnsəˌleɪtɪd/ /ˈɪnsjəˌleɪtɪd/| adj.| 绝缘的，隔热的，隔离的 | Protected from heat, cold, or noise by a layer of material that prevents the passage of heat, cold, or sound. |  The house is well insulated against the cold. 这房子隔热良好，抵御寒冷。 | The wires are insulated with plastic. 电线用塑料绝缘。 | 近义词：protected, shielded, isolated | 反义词：exposed, unprotected, conductive | 词性: adjective

insulate:/ˈɪnsəˌleɪt/ /ˈɪnsjəˌleɪt/| vt.|  使绝缘，使隔热，隔离 | To cover or separate with a material that prevents the passage of heat, cold, or sound. |  We need to insulate the loft to reduce heat loss. 我们需要给阁楼隔热以减少热量损失。 |  His wealth insulated him from the realities of poverty. 他的财富使他与贫困的现实隔绝开来。 | 近义词：protect, shield, isolate | 反义词：expose | 词性: verb

insulation:/ˌɪnsəˈleɪʃn/ /ˌɪnsjəˈleɪʃn/| n.| 绝缘，隔热，隔离 |  Material used to prevent the passage of heat, cold, or sound. |  The insulation in the attic needs to be replaced. 阁楼里的隔热材料需要更换。 | Social insulation can lead to feelings of loneliness. 社会隔离会导致孤独感。 | 近义词：protection, shielding | 反义词：exposure | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *insulatus*, past participle of *insulare* "to make into an island, isolate," from *insula* "island."
