criminative:/ˈkrɪmɪnətɪv/| adj.|判罪的；定罪的；criminative 指的是与犯罪有关的，或者能够区分罪犯的。它强调的是与犯罪行为本身相关的，或者能够区分犯罪行为的。|The criminative evidence was enough to convict the suspect. 定罪证据足以给嫌疑人定罪。|The detective searched for criminative clues at the crime scene.  侦探在犯罪现场寻找定罪线索。|近义词：incriminating, accusatory, inculpatory|反义词：exculpatory, absolving|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *criminativus, from Latin criminari "to accuse, incriminate," from crimen "accusation, crime."
