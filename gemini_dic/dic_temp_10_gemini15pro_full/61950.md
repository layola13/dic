emulsifier:/ɪˈmʌlsɪfaɪər/ | n. | 乳化剂 | A substance that stabilizes an emulsion, preventing liquids that usually do not mix from separating. |  Lecithin is a common emulsifier used in food production.  卵磷脂是食品生产中常用的乳化剂。

|Synonyms: stabilizer, binding agent| Antonyms: demulsifier | noun
