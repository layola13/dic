fustigate:/ˈfʌstɪɡeɪt/| v. |严厉批评；鞭打；痛斥|To criticize severely; to beat with a stick as punishment.|The senator fustigated his opponent for his stance on healthcare.  参议员严厉批评了对手在医疗保健方面的立场。

The teacher fustigated the student for cheating on the exam. 老师因为学生考试作弊而严厉批评了他。|近义词：criticize, chastise, lambaste, censure, reprimand, scold, berate|反义词：praise, commend, laud|词性:verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fustigāre* "to cudgel, beat with sticks," from *fustis* "stick, club."
