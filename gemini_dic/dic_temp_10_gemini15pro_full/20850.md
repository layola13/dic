blastula: /ˈblæstjʊlə/ (US) /ˈblæstʃʊlə/ (UK)| n. |囊胚|An early stage of embryonic development in animals, consisting of a hollow sphere of cells. |The blastula is formed after the morula stage. 囊胚是在桑椹胚阶段之后形成的。| The mammalian blastula is called a blastocyst. 哺乳动物的囊胚被称为胚泡。|synonyms: blastocyst (in mammals)| antonyms: None| noun

Etymology: From Modern Latin blastula, diminutive of Greek blastos "sprout, germ"
