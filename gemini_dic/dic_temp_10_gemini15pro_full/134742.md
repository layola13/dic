nonmeditatively:/ˌnɒnˌmedɪˈteɪtɪvli/

Example 1: He worked nonmeditatively, focusing only on the task at hand.  他工作时没有沉思，只专注于手头的任务。

Example 2:  She spoke nonmeditatively, her words tumbling out without pause for reflection. 她不假思索地说，话语脱口而出，没有停顿反思。

Synonym:|thoughtlessly, mindlessly, automatically|
Antonym:|meditatively, contemplatively, thoughtfully|
Etymology:|non- (not) + meditative + -ly (adverbial suffix)|
