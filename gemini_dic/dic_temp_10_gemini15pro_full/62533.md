enduring:/ɪnˈdjʊərɪŋ/ /ɛnˈdʊrɪŋ/| adj.|持久的，持续的，不朽的|lasting for a long time; able to exist or continue for a long time|例句：He has an enduring love for his wife. 他对妻子的爱永恒不变。|近义词：lasting, persistent, abiding, permanent|反义词：temporary, fleeting, ephemeral|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *enduren*, from Old French *endurer*, from Latin *indūrāre* (“to harden”), from *in-* + *dūrus* (“hard”).
