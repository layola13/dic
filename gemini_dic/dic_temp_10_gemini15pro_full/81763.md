gregarious:/ɡrɪˈɡɛəriəs/| adj.|群居的；爱社交的|Fond of company; sociable.|例句：Gregarious animals tend to live in flocks or herds. 群居动物倾向于成群结队地生活。

例句：She's a gregarious person who enjoys parties and social gatherings. 她是一个喜欢参加聚会和社交活动的合群的人。|近义词：sociable, outgoing, convivial|反义词：solitary, antisocial, introverted|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *gregarius* "belonging to a flock or herd," from *grex* (genitive *gregis*) "flock, herd."
