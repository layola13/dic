The book includes extractions from Shakespeare's plays. 这本书包含莎士比亚戏剧的选段。|近义词：removal, withdrawal, excerpt, abstract|反义词:insertion, addition|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English extraccion, from Old French extraction, from Latin extractiō (“a drawing out”), from extractus, past participle of extrahō (“I draw out”).
