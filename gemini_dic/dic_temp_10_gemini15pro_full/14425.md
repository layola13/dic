autumnal: /ɔːˈtʌmnəl/ (US) /ɔːˈtʌmnl/ (UK)| adj.|秋天的；秋季的；像秋天的|Of or characteristic of autumn; occurring in autumn.|The autumnal leaves fell gently to the ground. 秋天的落叶轻轻地飘落在地上。|Synonyms: fall, harvest, autumny|Antonyms: vernal, spring, summery|adjective

autumnal: /ɔːˈtʌmnəl/ (US) /ɔːˈtʌmnl/ (UK)| n.|秋天的景色|The characteristic features of autumn.|The poet captured the beauty of the autumnal in his verses. 诗人在他的诗句中捕捉到了秋天的美景。|Synonyms: fall, harvest|Antonyms: spring, summer|noun (rare usage)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin autumnalis, from Latin autumnus (“autumn”).
