forkers:/ˈfɔːrkərz/
n.
分叉者；使用分叉的人；叉子使用者
People who use forks; those who create a fork (in software, a blockchain, or a project).
The forkers disagreed on the direction of the project. 分叉者们对项目的方向存在分歧。
近义词：developers, contributors, branchers (in software context)
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) centralizers, mergers
词性: 名词
