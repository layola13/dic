ETYMOLOGY->

From Middle English *fastnynge*, from Old English *fæstnung* (“a making fast, fastening, confirmation”), from *fæstnian* (“to fasten”), from Proto-Germanic *fastinōną* (“to fasten”), from *fastuz* (“fast, firm”), from Proto-Indo-European *pastós* (“firm, solid”).
