lightfully: /ˈlaɪtfʊli/ | adv. | 轻松地，轻快地 |  Delightfully is an adverb that describes something done in a light, cheerful, and pleasing manner. It often implies a sense of ease and enjoyment. | She danced lightfully across the stage. 她轻快地在舞台上跳舞。| The music played lightfully in the background.  音乐在背景中轻快地播放着。 | 近义词: cheerfully, airily, merrily, gaily, blithely | 反义词: heavily, gloomily, sadly | 词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From light + -fully.  "Light" in this context refers to a lack of burden or seriousness, not the absence of darkness.  The suffix "-fully" transforms the adjective "light" into an adverb.
