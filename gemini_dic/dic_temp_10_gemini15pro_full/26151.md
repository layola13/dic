busy: /ˈbɪzi/ (US) /ˈbɪzi/ (UK) | adj. | 忙碌的，繁忙的 | actively engaged in work or other activity; full of activity |  I'm busy with work today. 我今天工作很忙。

He had a busy day at the office. 他在办公室度过了忙碌的一天。

近义词: occupied, engaged, swamped, overworked

反义词: idle, free, unoccupied


词性: adjective

词源: From Old English *bisig (attested only in unbisig "idle," literally "not busy"), from Proto-Germanic *bisigaz, perhaps from PIE root *bheidh- "to split." The sense development might be "being about one's business," hence "actively engaged."
