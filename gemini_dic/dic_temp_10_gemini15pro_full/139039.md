oaps:/oʊps/ | n. | 老年人，退休人员 (plural) | Older Adults Protective Services.  This acronym is commonly used to refer to services designed to protect vulnerable older adults from abuse, neglect, and exploitation.  It can also refer generally to older adults/pensioners. |  The oaps enjoyed their afternoon tea. 老年人们享受着他们的下午茶。| seniors, elderly people, retirees | youngsters, children | noun
