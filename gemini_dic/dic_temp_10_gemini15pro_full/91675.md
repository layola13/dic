hyperclassical:/ˌhaɪpərˈklæsɪkəl/
adjective
超古典的，极端古典主义的
Excessively or extremely classical; adhering rigidly to classical principles or styles, often to an exaggerated or affected degree.
His music is hyperclassical, almost parodic in its adherence to strict form. 他的音乐极端古典，在坚持严格形式方面几乎到了滑稽的地步。

The architect's hyperclassical designs seemed out of place in the modern city.  这位建筑师的超古典主义设计在现代城市中显得格格不入。

近义词:ultraclassical, rigidly classical, neoclassical
反义词:modern, avant-garde, contemporary
词性:adjective

词源:
"hyper-" (prefix meaning "over, excessive") + "classical" (adjective derived from "classic")
