legislate:/ˈlɛdʒɪsleɪt/| v. |立法；制定法律|To make or enact laws.|Congress has the power to legislate on national issues. 国会拥有制定国家法律的权力。

The city council will legislate new parking regulations. 市议会将制定新的停车规定。

|近义词：enact, decree, ordain, make laws|反义词: repeal, abolish|词性:动词
