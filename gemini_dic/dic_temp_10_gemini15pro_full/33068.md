chowter:/ˈtʃaʊtər/| vi.| 喋喋不休;大声抱怨;大声责骂| To talk loudly and angrily; to complain loudly.| He chowtered on about the price of everything. 他不停地大声抱怨所有东西的价格.|近义词:rant, rave, complain, grumble|反义词:whisper, murmur|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
Likely of imitative origin, echoing the sound of loud, complaining speech.  Possibly related to "chatter."
