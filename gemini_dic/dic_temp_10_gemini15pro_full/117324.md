meddles:/ˈmɛdəlz/| v.|干涉，干预，管闲事|To interfere in something that is not one's concern.|She's always meddling in other people's affairs. 她总是干涉别人的事情.|近义词：interfere, intrude, tamper|反义词:ignore, neglect|词性:动词

词源:
From Middle English *medlen, from Anglo-Norman medler, from Old French mesler, medler (“to mix, mingle, meddle”), from Vulgar Latin *misculāre, from Latin miscēre (“to mix”).
