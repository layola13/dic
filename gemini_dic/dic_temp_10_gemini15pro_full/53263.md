dirge:/dɜːrdʒ/ | n. | 挽歌；哀歌；悲歌 | A mournful song or piece of music, especially one played at a funeral. |  The slow, mournful dirge echoed through the church. 缓慢而悲伤的挽歌在教堂里回响。 |  The bagpipes played a mournful dirge as the procession moved slowly through the streets. 当队伍缓慢穿过街道时，风笛演奏着悲伤的挽歌。 | Synonyms: lament, elegy, requiem | Antonyms: celebration, anthem, paean | noun
