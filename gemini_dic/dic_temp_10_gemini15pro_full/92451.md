hypopituitary:/ˌhaɪpoʊpɪˈtuːɪteri/
adjective
垂体功能减退的；垂体功能低下的
Relating to or characterized by decreased activity of the pituitary gland.
Hypopituitary patients often require hormone replacement therapy. 垂体功能减退的患者通常需要激素替代疗法。
Hypopituitarism can lead to a variety of hormonal deficiencies. 垂体功能减退会导致多种激素缺乏。
近义词:pituitary-deficient
反义词:hyperpituitary
词性:adjective
