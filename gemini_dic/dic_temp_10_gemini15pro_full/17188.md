baudrick:/ˈbɔːdrɪk/| n. | 腰带，绶带 | A baldric, also spelled baudrick, is a belt worn over one shoulder, usually to support a sword or other weapon, or as a decorative sash. | The knight wore a baudrick adorned with jewels. 骑士佩戴着一条饰有珠宝的绶带。 | Synonyms: baldric, sash, belt, girdle | Antonyms: None | noun
