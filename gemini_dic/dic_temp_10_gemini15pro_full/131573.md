noncommutativities:/ˌnɒnkəˌmjuːtəˈtɪvɪtiz/| n. |非对易性，非交换性|The property of an operation where changing the order of the operands changes the result.  This is often seen in matrix multiplication and quantum mechanics.|The noncommutativities of matrix multiplication are crucial in quantum mechanics.|近义词:non-commutability,  |反义词:commutativity, |noun
