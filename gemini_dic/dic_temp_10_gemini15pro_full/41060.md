coplanar:/koʊˈpleɪnər/| adj.|共面的|Lying in the same plane.  Existing or acting in the same plane.|The three points are coplanar. 这三个点共面。|近义词：concurrent, coincident|反义词:noncoplanar|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From co- (together) + planar (relating to a plane).
