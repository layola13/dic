nonpropagandists:/ˌnɒnˌprɒpəˈɡændɪsts/
n.
非宣传者，不进行宣传的人
People who do not spread propaganda; individuals who refrain from promoting particular doctrines or ideologies.
The group consisted largely of nonpropagandists, focused on humanitarian aid rather than political messaging.  这群人主要由非宣传者组成，专注于人道主义援助而不是政治宣传。
The conference brought together scientists, academics, and nonpropagandists to discuss the issue objectively. 会议汇集了科学家、学者和非宣传者，以客观地讨论这个问题。
近义词:neutrals,  bystanders
反义词:propagandists, advocates
词性:noun
