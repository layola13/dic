encratite:/ˈɛŋkrəˌtaɪt/
n.
陨硫铁
A stony meteorite consisting essentially of enstatite, a magnesium silicate.
Encratite meteorites are rare and are believed to have originated from the inner solar system.  陨硫铁陨石很罕见，据信起源于太阳系内部。
Enstatites are a rare type of meteorite believed to have formed in oxygen-poor environments.  顽火辉石球粒陨石是一种罕见的陨石类型，据信形成于缺氧的环境中。
Synonym:enstatite chondrite, aubrite
Antonym:N/A
