antiregulatory:/ˌæntiˈrɛɡjʊlətɔːri/
adjective
反监管的；反对监管的
Opposing or intended to reduce or eliminate regulation.
The antiregulatory stance of the new administration is concerning to environmental groups. 新政府的反监管立场令环保团体感到担忧。
近义词：deregulatory, laissez-faire
反义词：regulatory, pro-regulatory
词性：adjective
