documenters:/ˈdɒkjʊˌmɛntəz/ (UK) /ˈdɑːkjəˌmɛntərz/ (US)
n.
记录者，记录员
People who document something, typically in writing, film, or other media.
The documenters meticulously recorded the events of the conference.  记录员一丝不苟地记录了会议的事件。
The documenters filmed the protests for posterity. 记录者拍摄了抗议活动以供后世参考。
近义词: recorders, chroniclers, reporters, journalists
反义词: fabricators, liars
词性: noun


documenters:/ˈdɒkjʊˌmɛntəz/ (UK) /ˈdɑːkjəˌmɛntərz/ (US)
Etymology:  From the verb "document" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Document" comes from the Old French "document," ultimately from the Latin "documentum" meaning "lesson, proof, example."
词源: 来自动词 "document" + "-er" (表示行为者的后缀)。 "Document" 来自古法语 "document"，最终源于拉丁语 "documentum"，意思是“教训、证据、例子”。
