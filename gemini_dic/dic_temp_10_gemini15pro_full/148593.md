overteaches:/ˌoːvərˈtiːtʃɪz/
verb
过度教学，教得过多过细
To teach a subject in too much detail or with excessive thoroughness, often resulting in confusion or lack of engagement from the student.
The teacher overteaches the simple concept, causing the students to become bored and confused. 老师对这个简单的概念过度讲解，导致学生感到厌烦和困惑。
The new teacher tends to overteach, giving the students too much information at once.  这位新老师倾向于过度教学，一下子给学生太多的信息。
overexplain, over-instruct,  labor
