erred:/ɜːrd/
verb
犯错，出错
made a mistake or did something wrong
He erred in his calculations. 他在计算中犯了错。
The court erred in its judgment. 法院的判决错了。
synonyms:mistaken, blundered, slipped up, messed up
antonyms:succeeded, excelled, triumphed
