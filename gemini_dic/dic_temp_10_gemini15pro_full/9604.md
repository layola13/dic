antispeculative:/ˌæntiˈspɛkjʊlətɪv/
adjective
反投机的；防止投机的
Intended to discourage or prevent speculation.
The government introduced antispeculative measures to curb rising house prices. 政府出台了反投机措施以抑制房价上涨。
Near Synonyms:  regulatory, restrictive, controlling
Antonyms: speculative,  encouraging speculation
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to. From Greek *anti-
speculative: from Latin *speculatus*, past participle of *speculari* "to look at, observe, contemplate," from *specula* "watchtower, lookout."
