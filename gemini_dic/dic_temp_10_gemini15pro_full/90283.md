houselessnesses:/ˈhaʊslɪsnɪsɪz/
n.
无家可归的状态; 多次或长期无家可归
The state of being without a home, especially repeatedly or for long periods.
The report highlighted the increasing houselessnesses among young people.  这份报告强调了年轻人无家可归现象的日益增多。
Synonyms: homelessness, rooflessness, vagrancy
Antonyms: housed, sheltered, accommodated
Noun
