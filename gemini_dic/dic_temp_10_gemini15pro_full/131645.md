noncompulsively:/ˌnɑːnkəmˈpʌlsɪvli/
adverb
非强迫地，非冲动地
in a way that is not compulsive; without obsession
She checks her email noncompulsively, only a few times a day. 她不会强迫性地查看邮件，一天只查看几次。
He eats noncompulsively, listening to his body's hunger cues. 他饮食没有强迫性，会倾听身体的饥饿信号。
近义词:  moderately,  temperately,  reasonably
反义词: compulsively, obsessively
词性: adverb
词源:  From non- + compulsive + -ly.  Non- is a prefix meaning "not," compulsive refers to an irresistible urge, and -ly forms adverbs.
