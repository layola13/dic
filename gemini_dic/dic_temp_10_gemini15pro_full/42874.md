courting:/ˈkɔːrtɪŋ/ (US), /ˈkɔːtɪŋ/ (UK)| v. n.|求爱，追求；招致；设法获得|The process of trying to win the affections of someone with the goal of marriage or a romantic relationship.  Also, attempting to gain favor or support.|He was courting a wealthy heiress. 他正在追求一位富有的女继承人。|Synonyms: wooing, pursuing, romancing, seeking, soliciting|Antonyms: rejecting, spurning, discouraging|verb, noun

courting:/ˈkɔːrtɪŋ/ (US), /ˈkɔːtɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|求爱，追求|The period of time during which a couple develops a romantic relationship, especially with a view to marriage.|Their courting was brief, but intense. 他们的恋爱期很短，但很热烈。|Synonyms: courtship, wooing, romance|Antonyms: rejection, break-up|noun
