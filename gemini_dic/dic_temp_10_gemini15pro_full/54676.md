disorb:/dɪˈsɔːrb/ /dɪˈzɔːrb/
verb
解吸，脱附
The process of a substance releasing from a surface it was previously adsorbed onto.  The opposite of adsorption.
Gases disorb from the surface of the catalyst.  气体从催化剂表面解吸。
desorb, release
