marinate:/ˈmærɪneɪt/ /ˈmærɪnət/ | v. n. | 腌制，浸泡 | To soak food in a seasoned liquid before cooking. |  I marinated the chicken in a mixture of herbs and spices. 我把鸡肉浸泡在香草和香料的混合物中。

The steak was marinated overnight for maximum flavor.  牛排腌制了一整夜，以获得最佳风味。 |  soak, steep, pickle | dry, dehydrate | verb, noun
