gush:/ɡʌʃ/| vi. n.|涌出，迸发；滔滔不绝地说话|To flow out suddenly and in large amounts; to express a positive feeling, especially praise, in such a strong way that it does not sound sincere.|The blood gushed from the wound.  鲜血从伤口涌出。|She gushed about how much she loved my new dress. 她滔滔不绝地说她有多喜欢我的新裙子。|近义词：spurt, flow, pour, effuse|反义词:trickle, drip|verb

gush:/ɡʌʃ/| n.|涌出，迸发|A sudden and forceful flow of liquid; an excessive display of sentiment or enthusiasm.|There was a sudden gush of water from the broken pipe.  破裂的管道里突然涌出一股水。|Her gush of enthusiasm was overwhelming. 她热情洋溢，令人难以招架。|近义词：outpouring, surge, rush|反义词:trickle, drip|noun
