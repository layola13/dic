antiatheism:/ˌæntiˈeɪθiɪzəm/ | n. | 反无神论；反对无神论 | Opposition to atheism.  It often takes the form of arguing for the existence of God, or criticizing the arguments and effects of atheism. |  Many philosophers consider antiatheism to be distinct from theism. 许多哲学家认为反无神论不同于有神论。

Antiatheism can sometimes manifest as hostility towards atheists. 反无神论有时表现为对无神论者的敌意。 | 近义词: theism, deism | 反义词: atheism | 词性: noun
