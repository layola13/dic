hothouses:/ˈhɑːthaʊzɪz/ (US), /ˈhɒthaʊzɪz/ (UK)| n. |温室；温床|A glass building, usually heated, for growing plants.|The orchids thrived in the hothouses. 兰花在温室里茁壮成长。|Synonyms: greenhouse, conservatory, glasshouse|Antonyms: outdoors, open air|noun

hothouse:/ˈhɑːthaʊs/ (US), /ˈhɒthaʊs/ (UK)| adj. |温室的；（气氛）闷热的；（感情）过分热烈的|Relating to or characteristic of a hothouse; artificially stimulating or intense.|The hothouse atmosphere of the political debate was stifling. 政治辩论的热烈气氛令人窒息。|Synonyms: heated, intense, stifling|Antonyms: cool, temperate, moderate|adjective
