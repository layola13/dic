gooseberries:/ˈɡuːzbəriz/ /ˈɡuːsbɛriz/| n.|醋栗，鹅莓|Small, round, edible berries with a tart flavor, typically green when unripe and reddish or yellowish when ripe.  They are often used in desserts, jams, and sauces.|She made a delicious gooseberry pie. 她做了一个美味的醋栗派。|The gooseberries are ripe and ready to be picked. 这些醋栗熟了，可以采摘了。|近义词：currants, berries|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

词源:
From Middle English *goseberye, *gosberie, from Old English *gōsberian, *gōsbērie (“gooseberry”), equivalent to gōs (“goose”) + berie (“berry”).  The reason for the name is uncertain, but may be due to the berries being served with roast goose or the shape of the fruit resembling the bill of a goose.
