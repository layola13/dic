nonfluently:/nɒnˈfluːəntli/ /nɑːnˈfluːəntli/ | adv. | 不流利地 | In a manner that is not fluent; with hesitations and difficulty. | She spoke Spanish nonfluently, but could still communicate her needs. 她西班牙语说得不流利，但仍然能够表达她的需求。 | He read the poem nonfluently, stumbling over several words.  他念这首诗很不流利，磕磕绊绊地念了好几个词。 | 近义词：haltingly, hesitantly, awkwardly | 反义词: fluently, smoothly, effortlessly | 词性: 副词

词源:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + fluent (adjective meaning "able to speak or write a language easily and accurately") + -ly (adverb suffix)
