mucigenous:/mjuːˈsɪdʒɪnəs/
adjective
产生粘液的; 粘液性的
Producing or secreting mucus.
The mucigenous cells lining the stomach protect it from the corrosive effects of gastric acid. 胃壁上的粘液细胞保护胃部免受胃酸的腐蚀。
mucous, slimy, viscid
dry
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin mucus (“mucus”) + -genous (“producing”).
