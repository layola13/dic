mis-coining:/ˌmɪsˈkɔɪnɪŋ/
n.
错误地创造新词; 滥铸新词
The act of inventing a new word or phrase incorrectly, or using an existing word in a wrong way.
His essay was full of mis-coinings and grammatical errors. 他的文章充满了造词错误和语法错误。
neologism, coinage, innovation
solecism, barbarism
noun
