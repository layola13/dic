conspirators:/kənˈspɪrətərz/| n. |阴谋者，共谋者，同谋|A person who takes part in a conspiracy; a plotter.  A group of people who secretly plan together to do something unlawful or harmful.|The conspirators were arrested before they could carry out their plan.  这些阴谋者在实施计划之前就被逮捕了。|Synonyms: plotters, schemers, collaborators,  intriguers|Antonyms: loyalists, conformists|noun
