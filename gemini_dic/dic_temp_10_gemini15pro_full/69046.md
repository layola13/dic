faucet:/ˈfɔːsɪt (US) / /ˈfɔːsɪt (UK)/| n. |水龙头|A device that controls the flow of liquid, especially water, from a pipe.|She turned on the faucet and filled the glass with water. 她打开了水龙头，在杯子里注满了水。|近义词：tap, spigot|反义词: (none - a faucet can be off, but doesn't have a true opposite)|noun
