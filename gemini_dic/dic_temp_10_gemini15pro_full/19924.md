biochemically:/ˌbaɪoʊˈkɛmɪkli/
adverb
生物化学地，从生物化学角度
In terms of its chemical processes relating to living organisms.
This drug works biochemically by inhibiting a specific enzyme.  这种药物通过抑制一种特定的酶，以生物化学的方式起作用。
The two species are biochemically very similar. 这两个物种在生物化学方面非常相似。

Synonyms: biologically, organically, metabolically
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps physically or mechanically in certain contexts)
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
bio- (life) + chemical + -ly (adverbial suffix)
"bio-" comes from Greek "bios" meaning "life".
"-ly" transforms the adjective "biochemical" into the adverb "biochemically".
