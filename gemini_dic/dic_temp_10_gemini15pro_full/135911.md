nonpublication:/ˌnɒnpʌblɪˈkeɪʃn/
n.
不出版，未出版，不发表
The nonpublication of the manuscript was due to its controversial nature.  这份手稿由于其争议性而未出版。
The author requested nonpublication of the article. 作者要求不发表这篇文章。
近义词：suppression, withholding
反义词：publication, release
词性：名词

Etymology:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + publication (from Latin publicare "to make public")
