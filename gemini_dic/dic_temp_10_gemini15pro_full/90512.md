huddlingly:/ˈhʌdlɪŋli/
adverb
拥挤地， huddled 的副词形式
in a huddled manner; snugly together.  They sat huddlingly around the fire. 他们挤在一起围坐在火堆旁。
近义词: snugly, closely, tightly
反义词: loosely, separately, dispersedly
词性: adverb

词源: From huddle + -ing + -ly.  "Huddle" comes from the Low German "huddeln," meaning "to crowd together."
