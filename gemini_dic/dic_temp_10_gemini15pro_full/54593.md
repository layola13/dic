dismantler:/dɪsˈmæntlər/ | n. | 拆卸者，拆除者 | A person who dismantles something, especially machinery or structures. | The dismantler carefully took apart the engine.  拆卸者小心地拆卸了发动机。| synonyms: demolisher, disassembler | antonyms: assembler, constructor | noun

dismantle:/dɪsˈmæntl/ | vt. | 拆卸，拆除；解散 | To take apart a machine or structure so that it is in separate pieces; to end an organization or system gradually in an organized way. | They dismantled the old bridge. 他们拆除了旧桥。 The government has dismantled the national health service. 政府已经解散了国民保健服务。| synonyms: disassemble, take apart, break down, demolish | antonyms: assemble, construct, build, establish | verb
