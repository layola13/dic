luxuriation:/lʌɡˌʒʊəriˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 奢华， luxuriating;  a luxurious situation or surrounding; an instance of luxuriousness | The cat stretched out on the soft rug in complete luxuriation.  这只猫完全沉浸在奢华中，舒展地躺在柔软的地毯上。| 近义词: indulgence, reveling, wallowing, basking | 反义词: austerity, abstinence, self-denial | 词性: noun
词源: From Late Latin *luxuriationem,* accusative singular of *luxuriatio,* from Latin *luxuriare* "to be luxuriant," from *luxuria* "luxury, abundance."
