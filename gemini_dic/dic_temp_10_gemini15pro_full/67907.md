face-the-music:/feɪs ðə ˈmjuːzɪk/| idiom | 承担后果；接受批评；面对现实 | to accept the unpleasant consequences of one's actions |  He knew he had to face the music sooner or later. 他知道他迟早要承担后果。| synonyms: accept the consequences, take responsibility, pay the piper | antonyms: avoid responsibility, shirk one's duty, evade the consequences | idiom

face-the-music:/feɪs ðə ˈmjuːzɪk/| verb phrase | 承担后果；接受批评；面对现实 | to confront a difficult or unpleasant situation with courage | After failing the exam, she had to face the music and tell her parents. 考试不及格后，她不得不面对现实，告诉父母。| synonyms: confront, accept, deal with | antonyms: avoid, evade, escape | verb phrase
