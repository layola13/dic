etymological:/ˌɛtɪməˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 词源的，词源学的 | Relating to the origin and historical development of words. |  The Oxford English Dictionary provides etymological information about words. 牛津英语词典提供单词的词源信息。 |  The professor gave an etymological explanation of the word "democracy". 教授对“民主”一词进行了词源学解释。 |  derivational, linguistic | antonymous (rarely used as an antonym) | adjective
