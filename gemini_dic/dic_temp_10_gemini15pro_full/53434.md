disappointed:/ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪntɪd/| adj.|失望的，沮丧的|Feeling sad because something you hoped for did not happen.|I was disappointed that the concert was cancelled. 我对音乐会被取消感到很失望。|近义词：sad, upset, disheartened, let down|反义词：happy, pleased, delighted|adjective

disappoint:/ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪnt/| vt.|使失望|To fail to fulfill the hopes or expectations of (someone).|The movie disappointed me. 这部电影让我失望。|近义词：frustrate, let down|反义词：please, delight|verb
