overtwisted:/ˌoʊvərˈtwɪstɪd/ | adj. | 过捻的，过度加捻的 | Twisted excessively; overspun.  This term is primarily used in the context of yarn, rope, or fabric, indicating that the material has been twisted too much, which can weaken it or alter its properties. It can also be used metaphorically to describe something that is excessively complex or convoluted. | The overtwisted yarn snapped easily. 过捻的纱线很容易断裂。 | The plot of the novel was so overtwisted that it became difficult to follow. 这部小说的情节过于复杂，以至于难以理解。 |近义词：overspun, excessively twisted | 反义词：undertwisted | 词性: 形容词
