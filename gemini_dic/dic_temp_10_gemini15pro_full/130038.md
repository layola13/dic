non-partisanship:/ˌnɒnˈpɑːrtɪzənʃɪp/ /ˌnɑːnˈpɑːrtɪzənʃɪp/| n.|不偏不倚；无党派性；超党派性|The state of not supporting any particular political party or group.  The judge stressed the importance of non-partisanship in the court system.|例句：The organization prides itself on its non-partisanship.  这个组织以其不偏不倚为荣。|近义词：impartiality, neutrality, objectivity|反义词：partisanship, bias, prejudice|词性:名词

non-partisan:/ˌnɒnˈpɑːtɪzən/ /ˌnɑːnˈpɑːrtɪzən/| adj.|无党派的；不偏不倚的|Not supporting any particular political party or group. |例句：A non-partisan election is one in which candidates are not affiliated with any political party. 无党派选举是指候选人不隶属于任何政党的选举。|近义词：impartial, neutral, objective|反义词：partisan, biased, prejudiced|词性:形容词
