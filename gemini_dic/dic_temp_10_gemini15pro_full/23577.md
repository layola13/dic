brak: /bræk/
noun
故障，缺陷；刹车；断裂；裂缝
a fault or crack, especially in a bone or a piece of rock; a sudden loud noise
The car screeched to a halt, the brakes screaming in protest. 汽车猛地停了下来，刹车发出刺耳的抗议声。
synonyms:  fault, crack, fracture, flaw, defect, rupture, break
antonyms: repair, mend, fix
词性: 名词

brak: /bræk/
verb
(使)刹车；发出刺耳的声音
to make a sudden loud, harsh noise; to apply the brakes of a vehicle
The driver braked hard to avoid hitting the deer. 司机猛踩刹车以避免撞到鹿。
synonyms:  stop, halt, screech, grind, squeal
antonyms: accelerate, speed up
词性: 动词
