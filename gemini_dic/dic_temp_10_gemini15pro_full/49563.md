demedicates:/diːˈmɛdɪkeɪts/
|v.|停止用药; 使脱离药物治疗|To discontinue medication for someone or oneself; to cease taking medication.|The doctor demedicated the patient after seeing improvement in their condition. 医生看到病人情况好转后就停止给病人用药了。|The patient decided to demedicate themselves against medical advice. 病人不顾医嘱决定自行停药。|discontinue medication, wean off medication, cease medication|medicate, administer medication|verb
