anti-dynastical:/ˌænti daɪˈnæstɪkəl/
adjective
反王朝的，反对王朝统治的
Opposed to the rule of dynasties; against the hereditary succession of power within a single family or group.

The anti-dynastical movement gained momentum after the king's controversial decision.
反王朝运动在国王做出有争议的决定后获得了动力。

The protestors carried anti-dynastical banners and chanted slogans against the royal family.
抗议者们举着反王朝的旗帜，高呼反对王室的口号。

Synonym: anti-monarchical
Antonym: dynastic, pro-monarchy
word type: adjective
