eyewitness:/ˈaɪˌwɪtnəs/ | n. | 目击者；见证人 | A person who has personally seen something happen and so can give a first-hand description of it. |  The eyewitness described the robber as tall and thin.  目击者描述劫匪又高又瘦。

The eyewitness testimony was crucial to the case. 目击者的证词对这个案子至关重要。| 近义词: observer, witness, onlooker | 反义词:  N/A (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
