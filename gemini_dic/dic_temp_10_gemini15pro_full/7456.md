anisopetalous:/ˌænɪsoʊˈpɛtələs/ | adj. | 花瓣不等长的；不等瓣的 | Having unequal petals; of or relating to a flower having petals of unequal size or form. | The wild rose is anisopetalous, with petals of varying sizes. 野玫瑰的花瓣不等长，大小不一。| Example 2: Anisopetalous flowers are common in certain plant families.  某些植物科的花瓣不等长是很常见的。 | Near Synonyms: irregular, unequal, asymmetrical | Antonyms: regular, equal, symmetrical | word property: adjective
