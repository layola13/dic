felo:/ˈfiːloʊ/ | n. | 凶手，重罪犯 | A person who has committed a felony. | He was convicted as a felo in the murder case. 他在谋杀案中被判为重罪犯。| Synonym: criminal, culprit, offender | Antonym: law-abiding citizen | Noun

felo:/ˈfiːloʊ/ | adj. |  凶恶的，残忍的 (罕见用法，主要用于法律术语中描述罪行) |  Relating to or constituting a felony. | The felo act shocked the entire community.  这凶残的行为震惊了整个社区。 | Synonym: felonious, criminal, villainous | Antonym: innocent, virtuous | Adjective
