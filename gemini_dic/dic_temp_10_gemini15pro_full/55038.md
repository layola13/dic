disquietingly:/dɪsˈkwaɪətɪŋli/
adverb|令人不安地；令人忧虑地|In a manner that causes anxiety or uneasiness.|The silence in the room was disquietingly profound. 房间里静得令人不安。|例句：
He smiled disquietingly. 他笑得令人不安。
She looked at him disquietingly. 她不安地看着他。

近义词: disturbingly, unsettlingly, alarmingly, worryingly
反义词: calmly, peacefully, reassuringly
词性: adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From disquiet + -ing + -ly.  Disquiet comes from the Old French *desquiete, from des- "away, from" + quiet.
