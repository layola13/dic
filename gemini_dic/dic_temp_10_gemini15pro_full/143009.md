outbellows:/ˌaʊtˈbɛloʊz/
verb
大声吼叫，胜过…的吼叫声
to shout louder than someone or something else
The angry foreman outbellowed the noise of the machinery.  愤怒的工头的声音盖过了机器的噪音。
近义词: outshout, drown out
反义词: whisper, murmur
词性: verb

Etymology: out- + bellows (n.)
"out-"  prefix meaning "exceeding," "surpassing," "external," "outside"
"bellows" (n.) from Old English "bylġes," plural of "bylġ," meaning "bag, purse, skin." Later referred to a device for blowing air.
