mediocrist:/ˌmiːdiˈɒkrɪst/ | n. | 平庸之人；庸才 | A person of mediocre ability or achievement. |  He dismissed his rivals as mediocrists. 他把他的对手斥为庸才。| Synonyms: nonentity, undistinguished person, commoner | Antonyms: genius, expert, prodigy | Noun

mediocre:/ˌmiːdiˈəʊkə(r)/ | adj. | 平庸的；普通的；平常的 | Of only moderate quality; not very good. | The food was mediocre. 食物很一般。| Synonyms: ordinary, average, middling, run-of-the-mill | Antonyms: excellent, outstanding, exceptional | Adjective
