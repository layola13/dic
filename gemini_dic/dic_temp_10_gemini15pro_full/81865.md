grids:/ɡrɪdz/ | n. |网格，格子，输电网 | A network of lines that cross each other to form a series of squares or rectangles. | The city is laid out in a grid pattern.  这座城市以网格状布局。| synonyms: network, lattice, mesh | antonyms: disarray, chaos | noun

grids:/ɡrɪdz/ | v. |(给…)加网格，准备(尤指比赛) | To mark or divide with a grid.  |  He gridded the paper before drawing the graph. 他在画图表之前先在纸上画了网格。 | synonyms:  divide, partition, section | antonyms: combine, merge, unite | verb
