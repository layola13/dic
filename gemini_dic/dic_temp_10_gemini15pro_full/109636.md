letting-up:/ˈlɛtɪŋ ʌp/| n.|停止，减弱，缓和|A letting-up of the rain allowed us to go outside. 雨势减弱，我们得以出门。|近义词：decrease, reduction, easing, abatement, relaxation, lull, respite|反义词：increase, intensification, escalation|noun

letting-up:/ˈlɛtɪŋ ʌp/| n.|放松，松懈|There was no letting-up in the training schedule. 训练计划没有丝毫放松。|近义词：slackening, letup|反义词：tightening, intensification|noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From the phrasal verb "let up," which originates from "let" (Old English *lētan, meaning "to allow") and "up" (Old English *upp, meaning "upwards").  The meaning evolved from "to loosen" or "release" to "diminish" or "cease."
