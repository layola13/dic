artly:/ˈɑːrtli/
adjective
艺术地；精巧地；熟练地
In an artful or artistic manner; skillfully.
She artly dodged the question. 她巧妙地回避了这个问题。
近义词: skillfully, adeptly, cleverly, expertly, artfully
反义词: clumsily, awkwardly, ineptly
词性: adjective

词源: From art + -ly.  "Art" comes from Old French art, from Latin artem (nominative ars) "work of art; practical skill; a business, craft," from PIE root *ar- "to fit together." The suffix "-ly" is used to form adverbs and adjectives, and derives from Old English -lice, meaning "like, having the form or qualities of."
