harlech:/ˈhɑːrlɛk/ (UK), /ˈhɑːrlɛtʃ/ (US)| n. |哈勒克|A town in Gwynedd, northwest Wales, historically associated with a medieval castle. It is also the name of the famous Welsh march "Men of Harlech".|Harlech Castle is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. 哈勒克城堡是联合国教科文组织世界遗产地。

The song "Men of Harlech" commemorates the seven-year siege of Harlech Castle.  歌曲《哈勒克男儿》是为了纪念哈勒克城堡七年围攻战。|近义词: fortress, stronghold (referring to the castle) |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
