intellectualize:/ɪnˌtɛlɛktʃuəlaɪz/ | v. | 使理智化；使抽象化；用理智解释；过度分析 | To analyze or discuss something in an excessively abstract or rational way, often avoiding emotional considerations. |  He tends to intellectualize his feelings rather than express them openly. 他倾向于把自己的感受理智化，而不是公开表达出来。 |  She intellectualized her grief by reading books on philosophy. 她通过阅读哲学书籍来理性化她的悲伤。 |  rationalize, overanalyze, theorize |  emotionalize, feel | verb
