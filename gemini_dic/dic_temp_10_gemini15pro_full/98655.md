insufficient:/ˌɪnsəˈfɪʃənt/| adj.|不足的，不够的|Not enough; inadequate.|The funds are insufficient to cover the project costs.  资金不足以支付项目费用。|The evidence is insufficient to convict him.  证据不足以给他定罪。|近义词:inadequate, deficient, lacking, scarce, meager|反义词:sufficient, adequate, enough, ample|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *insufficient-, from in- ‘not’ + sufficient- ‘enough’.
