envolup:/ˌɑ̃vəˈluːp/ |v.| 包裹，覆盖，笼罩 | To wrap up, surround, or cover completely; to envelop in something. | The fog enveloped the city. 浓雾笼罩着城市。 |  The rich aroma of coffee enveloped the room. 浓郁的咖啡香气弥漫了整个房间。 | 近义词: surround, enshroud, enfold, encompass | 反义词: uncover, expose | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *envelopper*, from *en-* (in, into) + *voloper* (to wrap), from Old French *veloper*. Ultimately from Frankish or another Germanic source.
