insessor:/ɪnˈsɛsər/ | n. |栖木鸟；鸣禽|A bird that perches, especially a songbird.|The robin is a common insessor in many gardens. 知更鸟是许多花园里常见的栖木鸟。|近义词：percher, songbird |反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑非栖木鸟，如waterfowl 水禽) |词性: 名词

Etymology: From Latin *insessor*, from *insidēre* "to sit upon," from *in* "on" + *sedēre* "to sit."
