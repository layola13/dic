parachrose:/ˈpærəkroʊz/| n.| 副玫瑰树脂，副玫瑰油 |A deep red resinous exudation from the bark of the socotrine aloe.  It is used as a colorant and in medicine.| The ancient Egyptians used parachrose in embalming and as a dye.|近义词: aloe resin|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Greek para- (beside, near to) + rhodon (rose)
