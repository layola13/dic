enorthotrope:/iːnɔːrˈθoʊˌtroʊp/ | n. | 正交异性体 | A crystal, mineral, or rock that has different physical properties (such as elasticity or conductivity) in different directions, but with certain symmetrical relationships between these properties, especially having three mutually perpendicular axes of different properties. | This mineral is an enorthotrope, displaying distinct variations in its thermal conductivity along its three principal axes.  这种矿物是一种正交异性体，沿其三个主轴的热导率显示出明显的差异。| The scientists studied the enorthotrope to understand how its unique structure affected its behavior under pressure. 科学家们研究了正交异性体，以了解其独特的结构如何影响其在压力下的行为。 | synonym: anisotropic | antonym: isotropic | noun
