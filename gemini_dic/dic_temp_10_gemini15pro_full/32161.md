chewink:/ˈtʃiːwɪŋk/| n.|赤胸朱雀|A North American finch (Pipilo erythrophthalmus) having a distinctive chewink call and, in the male, black upper plumage, white wing bars, and reddish-brown underparts.|例句：The chewink hopped across the lawn, searching for seeds.  赤胸朱雀在草坪上跳来跳去，寻找种子。

例句：I heard the distinctive "chewink" call and knew the bird was nearby. 我听到了独特的“chewink”叫声，知道这只鸟就在附近。|近义词：towhee, rufous-sided towhee|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
