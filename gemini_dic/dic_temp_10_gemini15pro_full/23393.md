brachydiagonal:/ˌbrækiːdaɪˈæɡənəl/| adj. n.|短对角线的|Relating to or being a brachydiagonal; a shorter diagonal (as of a rhombus or rhombohedron).|The brachydiagonal of this rhombus is half the length of the other diagonal.|近义词:short diagonal|反义词:macrodiagonal|词性:adjective, noun

Etymology:
From Greek *brakhus* (short) + *dia* (through) + *gōnia* (angle, corner).
