aight:/aɪt/
|interj. adv. adj.|好的，知道了|A casual way of saying "all right."  It is a shortened form of "alright," which itself is a nonstandard spelling of "all right."|Aight, I'll see you later. 好的，回头见。|近义词：ok, alright, fine, cool|反义词：no, nope|词性：interjection, adverb, adjective
aight:/aɪt/
|interj. adv. adj.|可以，没问题|Expressing agreement or acceptance. Similar to "okay" or "fine."|Aight, that sounds like a plan. 可以，这听起来像个计划。|近义词：okay, sure, fine|反义词：no way, not at all|词性：interjection, adverb, adjective
aight:/aɪt/
|interj. adv. adj.|不错的，还行的|Used to express a moderate level of satisfaction or approval.|The food was aight. 食物还不错。|近义词：decent, okay, alright|反义词：terrible, awful|词性：interjection, adverb, adjective
