nonarticulate:/ˌnɒnɑːrˈtɪkjʊlət/ |adj.|不善表达的，口齿不清的|He was nonarticulate with rage. 他气得说不出话来。|inarticulate, unarticulate| articulate, eloquent | adjective

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non "not."
articulate (adjective/verb): From Latin articulatus, past participle of articulare "to utter distinctly, joint," from articulus "small joint," diminutive of artus "joint, limb."
