definer:/dɪˈfaɪnər/| n.|定义者; 确定者|Someone or something that defines something; that states or explains exactly the nature, scope, or meaning of something.|The dictionary is a definer of words. 这本词典是一本单词的定义者。|Synonyms: expounder, interpreter, explainer|Antonyms: obscurer, confuser|noun
