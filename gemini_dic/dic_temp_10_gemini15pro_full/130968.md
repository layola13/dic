nonassumptions:/ˌnɒnəˈsʌmpʃənz/
noun
non-assumptions (通常用复数形式)
lack of assumptions; avoiding making assumptions; the practice of not making assumptions.
We proceeded with the project based on nonassumptions, carefully verifying each step.  我们基于不作假设的原则推进项目，仔细核实每一步。
non-assumption (单数形式较少使用)
an instance of not making an assumption.
His non-assumption about her guilt proved to be wise. 他没有假设她有罪，证明是明智的。
near synonyms: open-mindedness, neutrality, objectivity
antonyms: prejudice, bias, preconception
