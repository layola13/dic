cosmopolitan:/ˌkɒzməˈpɒlɪtən/ /ˌkɑːzməˈpɑːlɪtən/
adjective|世界性的，全球性的；见多识广的；来自世界各地的|Familiar with and at ease in many different countries and cultures.|She has a very cosmopolitan outlook on life. 她的人生观非常国际化。|近义词:international, global, worldly, sophisticated|反义词:provincial, parochial, insular|词性:adjective

cosmopolitan:/ˌkɒzməˈpɒlɪtən/ /ˌkɑːzməˈpɑːlɪtən/
noun|世界主义者，四海为家的人；世界性大都市|A person who is familiar with and at ease in many different countries and cultures.|London is a cosmopolitan city. 伦敦是一个国际大都市。|近义词:citizen of the world|反义词:local|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *cosmopolita, from Ancient Greek κοσμοπολίτης (kosmopolítēs, “citizen of the world”), from κόσμος (kósmos, “world, universe, ornament”) + πολίτης (polítēs, “citizen”).
