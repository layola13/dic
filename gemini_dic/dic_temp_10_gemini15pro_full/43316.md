crakeberry:/ˈkreɪkˌbɛri/
n.
蔓越莓，酸果蔓
A small, sour, red berry that grows on a low, creeping plant in acidic bogs.
Cranberries are often used to make sauce, juice, and relishes.  蔓越莓经常被用来制作酱汁、果汁和调味品。
