leashing:/ˈliːʃɪŋ/
n.
拴住; 用皮带系住; 控制; 约束
The act of putting a leash on an animal, or the state of being restrained by a leash.
The dog enjoys leashing for his daily walk. 这只狗喜欢每天系上皮带散步。
control, restraint, confinement
freedom, liberation
noun
