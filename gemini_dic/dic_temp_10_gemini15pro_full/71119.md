fishgig:/ˈfɪʃɡɪɡ/| n.|鱼叉|A barbed spear or fork for catching fish.|He used a fishgig to spear a large salmon. 他用鱼叉叉住了一条大鲑鱼。|近义词：spear, harpoon|反义词: net|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From fish + gig (a pronged spear).  The "gig" part likely comes from an Old Norse word "giga," meaning "spear."
