mailshots:/ˈmeɪlʃɒts/ (UK) /ˈmeɪlʃɑːts/ (US)| n. |邮寄广告；群发邮件|A mass mailing of advertising or similar material. |例句：The company sent out thousands of mailshots to potential customers.  公司向潜在客户发送了数千封邮寄广告。

例句：We received a mailshot advertising a new range of garden furniture. 我们收到了一份宣传新系列花园家具的邮寄广告。|近义词：circulars, flyers, direct mail, mass mailing, junk mail|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "targeted advertising")|词性:noun
