breathablenesses:/ˈbriːðəbəlnəsɪz/
n.
透气性（复数）
The quality of being breathable; the extent to which something allows air to pass through it.
The breathablenesses of the different fabrics varied greatly. 不同面料的透气性差异很大。
breathability, permeability, porosity
impermeability, airtightness
noun
