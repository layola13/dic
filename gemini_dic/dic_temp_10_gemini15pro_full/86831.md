hematophilia:/ˌhiːməˈfɪliə/ (US) /ˌhiːməˈfɪliə/ (UK)| n. |血友病|A medical condition, typically inherited, in which the blood doesn't clot normally, leading to excessive bleeding from even minor injuries.|Example: Hemophilia is a genetic disorder that affects blood clotting. 血友病是一种影响血液凝固的遗传性疾病。

Example:  He was diagnosed with hemophilia as a child. 他小时候被诊断出患有血友病。|近义词:bleeding disorder|反义词:normal blood clotting|词性:noun
