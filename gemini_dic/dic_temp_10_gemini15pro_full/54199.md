disfame:/dɪsˈfeɪm/ | v. n.| 诽谤，中伤；污名| To damage the reputation of (someone or something) by saying or writing bad things that are not true. | He was disfamed by the newspaper article. 他被报纸上的文章诽谤了。 | 近义词：slander, libel, defame, malign, vilify | 反义词：praise, commend, laud, extol | 词性: 动词/名词


disfame:/dɪsˈfeɪm/ | n.| 诽谤，中伤；污名| The state of being disfamed. | The disfame he suffered ruined his career. 他遭受的诽谤毁了他的职业生涯。 | 近义词：disgrace, dishonor, ignominy, opprobrium | 反义词：honor, reputation, prestige | 词性: 名词


词源: Late Middle English: from Old French diffamer, based on Latin dis- ‘expressing reversal’ + fama ‘reputation’.
