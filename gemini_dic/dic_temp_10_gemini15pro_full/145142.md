overasserting:/ˌoʊvərəˈsɜːrtɪŋ/
| adj. | 过分自信的；过于武断的；|The act of asserting oneself or one's claims too forcefully or aggressively.  Expressing opinions or claims with excessive confidence or insistence, often to the point of being domineering or aggressive.| He was overasserting his authority, making his colleagues uncomfortable. 他过于强调自己的权威，让同事们感到不舒服。 |近义词：domineering, pushy, aggressive, overbearing|反义词：submissive, passive, reserved, timid|词性:形容词
overassert:/ˌoʊvərəˈsɜːrt/
| v. | 过分自信；过于武断；|To assert oneself or one's claims too forcefully or aggressively.  To express opinions or claims with excessive confidence or insistence, often to the point of being domineering or aggressive.| By overasserting her opinions, she alienated many of her friends. 她由于过分坚持自己的意见，疏远了许多朋友。|近义词：domineer, push, impose, bully|反义词：submit, yield, concede, defer|词性:动词
assert:/əˈsɜːrt/
| v. | 断言；主张；维护；坚持|State a fact or belief confidently and forcefully.|He asserted his innocence. 他坚称自己无辜。|近义词：declare, maintain, affirm, avow|反义词：deny, contradict, refute, gainsay|词性:动词
