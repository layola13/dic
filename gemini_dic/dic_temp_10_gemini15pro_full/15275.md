backlashing:/ˈbæklæʃɪŋ/
n.|强烈抵制；强烈反对；反冲；后冲；齿隙|A strong negative reaction by a large number of people, especially to a social or political change.|The government is facing a backlashing from voters over its new tax policy. 政府正面临选民对其新税收政策的强烈反对。|repercussion, reaction, resistance|support, acceptance
v.|发生后冲；产生反lash|Experiencing a backlash; reacting negatively.|The company backlashed against the negative publicity. 公司对负面宣传作出了强烈反应。|recoil, react, kick back|accept, embrace
adj.|反冲的；反lash的|Relating to or characterized by a backlash.|The backlashing effect of the new law was unexpected. 新法律的反冲效应出乎意料。|reactive, resistant, opposing|supportive, accepting
