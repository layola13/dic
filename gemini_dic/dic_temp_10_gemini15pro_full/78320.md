gerontologists:/ˌdʒɛrənˈtɒlədʒɪsts/ | n. |老年学家| Specialists who study the process of aging and the particular problems of old people. | Gerontologists are working to find ways to improve the quality of life for the elderly. 老年学家正在努力寻找提高老年人生活质量的方法。| Gerontologists conducted a study on the effects of social interaction on cognitive decline in older adults. 老年学家进行了一项关于社会互动对老年人认知能力下降的影响的研究。 | synonyms: aging specialists, geriatrists | antonyms: N/A | noun
