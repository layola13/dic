engraver:/ɪnˈɡreɪvər/| n.|雕刻师，雕刻匠；雕刻机|A person or machine that engraves.  An engraver is someone skilled in the art of carving designs into hard materials like metal, wood, or stone.|The engraver carefully etched the design onto the silver plate.|雕刻师小心翼翼地在银盘上蚀刻图案。|Synonyms: carver, etcher, sculptor|Antonyms: None|noun

engraver:/ɪnˈɡreɪvər/| n.|雕刻机|A machine used for engraving.|The engraver quickly personalized the trophy with the winner's name.|雕刻机迅速地将获胜者的名字刻在奖杯上。|Synonyms: etching machine, carving machine|Antonyms: None|noun
