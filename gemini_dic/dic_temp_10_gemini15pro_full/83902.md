half-baked:/ˌhæfˈbeɪkt/ | adj. | 半生不熟的；考虑不周的；不完善的 | Not fully thought out; poorly planned; incomplete. |  This is a half-baked idea, we need to develop it further.  这个想法还不成熟，我们需要进一步完善它。 | Example: He presented a half-baked plan to the board, and it was quickly rejected. 他向董事会提交了一个不成熟的计划，很快就被否决了。 | Synonyms: ill-conceived, underdeveloped, immature, raw | Antonyms: well-thought-out, thorough, complete, developed | Adjective

half-baked:/ˌhæfˈbeɪkt/ | adj. |  不成熟的；（尤指想法或计划）不完善的 | (especially of ideas or plans) not fully developed or thought through. |  The government's half-baked scheme to reduce traffic congestion only made things worse.  政府旨在减少交通拥堵的欠考虑的方案反而使情况更糟。 | Example:  She wasn't going to present her half-baked ideas to the team until they were more refined.  在她的想法更完善之前，她不打算把这些不成熟的想法提交给团队。 | Synonyms: ill-considered, undeveloped, incomplete, superficial | Antonyms: well-considered, developed, complete, thorough | Adjective
