atheistically:/ˌeɪθiˈɪstɪkli/| adv.|无神论地，不信神地|In a manner that denies or disbelieves in the existence of God.|He lived his life atheistically, never setting foot in a church. 他过着无神论的生活，从未踏进过教堂。|Example: She argued atheistically against the existence of a divine creator. 她以无神论的观点反驳了神圣造物主的存在。|Synonym: godlessly, irreligiously, secularly|Antonym: religiously, piously|词性:副词
