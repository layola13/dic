filatory:/ˈfɪləˌtɔːri/ /ˈfɪləˌtəri/ | adj. | 纺纱的；纤维的 | Relating to or used in spinning. | The filatory process transforms raw fibers into yarn.  纺纱过程将原料纤维转化成纱线。| The museum displayed antique filatory equipment.  博物馆展示了古老的纺纱设备。 | 近义词：spinning, textile | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin *filātōrius, from filātus (past participle of filāre ‘to spin’) + -ōrius. Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European root *gwīh₁- ‘to wind, turn’.
