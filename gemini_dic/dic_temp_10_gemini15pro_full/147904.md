overrefinements:/ˌoʊvər rɪˈfaɪnmənts/
|n.| 过度精细化；过度细致；矫枉过正|The overrefinements of the legal system can sometimes lead to injustice. 法律体系的过度精细化有时会导致不公正。|English Explanation: Excessive refinements or elaborations, often to the point of unnecessary complexity or obscurity.|Example: The architect's overrefinements made the design overly complicated and expensive.  建筑师的过度精细化使得设计过于复杂和昂贵。|Synonyms: subtleties, nuances, complexities, elaborations|Antonyms: simplifications, generalizations, crudities|Noun
