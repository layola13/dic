bait-and-switch:/ˈbeɪt ən ˈswɪtʃ/ | n. | 诱饵调包；虚假广告 | A deceptive commercial practice where customers are lured by an advertised bargain and then pressured to buy a more expensive product. |  The store advertised a low price on a popular toy, but it was a bait-and-switch; they were out of the cheap toy and tried to sell us a more expensive one. 这家商店以低价宣传一款流行玩具，但这是个诱饵调包的伎俩；他们缺货了便宜的玩具，试图向我们推销更贵的。


The car dealership's bait-and-switch tactic involved advertising a car at a ridiculously low price, only to tell customers it had already been sold. 汽车经销商的诱饵调包策略包括以低得离谱的价格宣传一辆汽车，结果却告诉顾客它已经被卖掉了。 | 近义词: deceptive advertising, false advertising, scam | 反义词: honest advertising, ethical marketing | 词性: noun

bait:/beɪt/ | n. vt. | 饵；诱饵；引诱 | Something used to attract or entice, especially to a trap. |  We used worms as bait to catch fish. 我们用蠕虫作诱饵来钓鱼。


He baited the trap with cheese. 他用奶酪作诱饵来设置陷阱. | 近义词: lure, enticement, decoy | 反义词: deterrent, repellent | 词性: noun, verb

switch:/swɪtʃ/ | n. v. | 开关；转换；交换 | A device for making and breaking the connection in an electric circuit.  To change or exchange. |  Please turn off the light switch. 请关掉电灯开关。


He switched his major from biology to chemistry. 他把专业从生物学换成了化学. | 近义词: change, shift, swap | 反义词: maintain, stay, remain | 词性: noun, verb
