digested:/daɪˈdʒestɪd/ /dɪˈdʒestɪd/| adj.|消化的；领会的；吸收的|Broken down by the digestive system; thoroughly understood; absorbed and incorporated.|例句：The digested food is absorbed into the bloodstream. 消化后的食物被吸收到血液中。|近义词：absorbed, assimilated, processed, understood|反义词：undigested, raw|词性：形容词

digested:/daɪˈdʒestɪd/ /dɪˈdʒestɪd/| n.|文摘；摘要|A summary or condensation of a larger work.|例句：I read a digested version of the report. 我读了这份报告的摘要版本。|近义词：summary, abstract, synopsis, compendium|反义词：full text, unabridged version|词性：名词
