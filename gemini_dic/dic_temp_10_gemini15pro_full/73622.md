forewomen:/ˈfɔːrwɪmɪn/
n.
女工头；女领班
A woman who is in charge of a group of workers, especially in a factory.
The forewomen ensured all the safety protocols were followed. 女工头确保所有安全协议都得到遵守。
The forewomen organized the work schedule for the week.  女领班安排了本周的工作计划。

近义词:supervisor, manager, overseer, superintendent
反义词:subordinate, worker, employee
词性:noun
