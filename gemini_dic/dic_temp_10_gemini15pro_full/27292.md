calver:/ˈkælvər/| n.|版本号; 版本控制; (尤指按日期) |A versioning scheme where the version number is based on the calendar date, typically in the YYYY.MM.DD format.|Example: We're using calver for our software releases, so the next version will be 2024.10.26.|Synonym: versioning, date-based versioning|Antonym: semantic versioning|noun
