nonindividualities:/ˌnɒnɪndɪˌvɪdʒuˈælɪtiz/
noun
非个体性；缺乏个性
The absence or suppression of individual characteristics, resulting in a lack of uniqueness or distinction among members of a group.
The dystopian society in the novel emphasized conformity and nonindividualities.  这部反乌托邦小说中的社会强调一致性，缺乏个性。
Mass-produced clothing can sometimes lead to a sense of nonindividualities.  批量生产的服装有时会让人感觉缺乏个性。
Synonyms: uniformity, conformity, homogeneity, sameness
Antonyms: individuality, uniqueness, distinctiveness, diversity
词性: 名词
