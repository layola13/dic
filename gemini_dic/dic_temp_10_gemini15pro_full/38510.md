conciliation:/kənˌsɪliˈeɪʃən/
n.
和解，调解，安抚
The act of placating and overcoming distrust or hostility.
例句：Conciliation between the two warring factions seemed impossible.  交战双方之间的和解似乎不可能。

例句：The union and management reached a conciliation after a long strike.  工会和管理层在长期罢工后达成了和解。

近义词：reconciliation, appeasement, mediation, compromise
反义词：aggravation, antagonism, hostility

词性: noun
