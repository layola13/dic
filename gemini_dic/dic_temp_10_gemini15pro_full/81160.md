grandchild:/ˈɡræn(d)tʃaɪld/ /ˈɡrændˌtʃaɪld/| n. |孙子；孙女；外孙；外孙女|A child of one's son or daughter.|例句：She adores her grandchildren. 她非常疼爱她的孙辈们。|例句：He has five grandchildren, three boys and two girls. 他有五个孙辈，三个男孩和两个女孩。|近义词：descendant, offspring|反义词:ancestor, forebear|词性:名词

词源:
From grand- +‎ child.
