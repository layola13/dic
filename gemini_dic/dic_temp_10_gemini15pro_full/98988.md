inter-variation:/ˌɪntərˌveəriˈeɪʃən/ |n.| 内部变异，种内变异 |The inter-variation within the species is significant, leading to diverse physical traits.  该物种内部的变异非常显著，导致了不同的物理特征。|Example:  Researchers studied the inter-variation in beak size among the finches. 研究人员研究了雀类喙大小的种内变异。 |Synonym: intraspecific variation, within-species variation |Antonym: interspecific variation, between-species variation| noun


Etymology:
"inter-" (Latin) = between, among
"variation" (from Latin "variatio") =  the act or process of varying; a change or difference
