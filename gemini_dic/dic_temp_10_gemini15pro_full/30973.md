chalcid: /ˈkælsɪd/ | n. | 寄生蜂；小蜂 | A small wasp of the superfamily Chalcidoidea, many of which are parasitic on other insects. |  例句：The chalcid wasp lays its eggs inside the larva of another insect.  寄生蜂把卵产在另一种昆虫的幼虫体内。

例句：Chalcid wasps are important for biological control of pests. 寄生蜂对害虫的生物防治很重要。 | 近义词：parasitoid wasp | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "host insect") | 词性: noun
