heptarchic:/hɛpˈtɑːrkɪk/
adjective
七王国时代的，七个统治者的
Relating to a group of seven rulers; specifically, relating to the period in Anglo-Saxon England when the country was divided into seven kingdoms.
The heptarchic period of English history is complex and often confusing.  英国历史上的七国时代是复杂且经常令人困惑的。
The historian specialized in heptarchic manuscripts and artifacts.  这位历史学家专门研究七国时代的文献和文物。
Near Synonyms: sevenfold, septuple
Antonyms: unified, singular
word class: adjective
