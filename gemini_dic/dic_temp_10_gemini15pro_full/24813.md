brouhaha:/ˌbruːˈhɑːhɑː/| n. |喧闹，骚动，纷扰|A noisy and overexcited reaction or response to something, typically something unimportant. |Example 1: The brouhaha over the new dress code eventually died down.  新着装规范引起的喧闹最终平息了。

Example 2:  There was a tremendous brouhaha when the star arrived unexpectedly.  当这位明星意外到来时，引起了一阵巨大的骚动。|近义词：commotion, uproar, hubbub, fuss, furor|反义词:calm, peace, quiet|词性:名词
