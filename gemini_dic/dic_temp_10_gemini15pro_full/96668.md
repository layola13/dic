industriously:/ɪnˈdʌstriəsli/| adv.|勤奋地，勤勉地|In a hard-working and diligent manner.|She worked industriously on her project, determined to finish it on time. 她勤奋地做她的项目，决心按时完成。|近义词：diligently, assiduously, laboriously|反义词：lazily, idly|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From industry + -ously.  "Industry" traces back to Latin "industria" meaning "diligence, activity." The suffix "-ously" forms adverbs from adjectives.
