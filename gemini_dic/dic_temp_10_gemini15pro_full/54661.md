disobeying:/ˌdɪsəˈbeɪɪŋ/
verb
不服从，违抗
present participle of disobey.  The act of not following rules or commands.
He was punished for disobeying a direct order. 他因不服从直接命令而受到惩罚。
Synonyms: defying, ignoring, violating, resisting, contravening
Antonyms: obeying, complying, following, heeding
词性: 动词 (present participle)
