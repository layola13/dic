dosage:/ˈdoʊsɪdʒ/ (US) /ˈdəʊsɪdʒ/ (UK)|n.|剂量; 用量|The amount of a medicine or drug that should be taken at one time or regularly during a period of time.|The doctor prescribed a dosage of 200mg three times a day.|synonyms: dose, amount, quantity|antonyms: (No direct antonym, but consider "lack," "deficiency," or "insufficiency" in certain contexts)|noun
