emetical:/ɪˈmɛtɪkəl/ | adj. | 催吐的；引起呕吐的 | Causing vomiting; emetic. |  This plant has emetical properties and should not be ingested.  这种植物有催吐特性，不应该被摄入。|  The doctor prescribed an emetical medicine to help the patient expel the poison. 医生开了一种催吐药帮助病人排出毒物。 | synonyms: emetic, vomitory, nauseant | antonyms: antiemetic | adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *ēmeticus, from Greek emetikós, from emeîn to vomit.
