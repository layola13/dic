lucklessly:/ˈlʌklɪsli/| adv.|不幸地，倒霉地|Without luck; unfortunately.  Characterized by a lack of luck.|He invested lucklessly in the stock market and lost a fortune. 他不幸地在股市投资失败，损失了一大笔钱。|Synonyms: unfortunately, unluckily, disastrously|Antonyms: luckily, fortunately|adverb

Etymology->
From luckless + -ly.  Luckless from luck + -less. Luck ultimately from Middle Dutch *luc, meaning "chance, fortune".
