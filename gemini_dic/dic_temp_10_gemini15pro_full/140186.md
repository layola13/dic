offertory:/ˈɔːfərtɔːri/ (US) /ˈɒfətəri/ (UK)
n.|奉献，奉献物，捐款，(弥撒中的)奉献经|The act of offering something, especially money, to a church or charity; the money or gifts offered. Also, the part of a church service when offerings are collected.|The offertory hymn was beautifully sung. 奉献圣歌唱得很美。|Synonyms: offering, collection, donation, contribution|Antonyms: withholding, refusal, denial|noun

offertory:/ˈɔːfərtɔːri/ (US) /ˈɒfətəri/ (UK)
n.|（弥撒中的）奉献礼，奉献仪式|The part of a church service, especially in the Roman Catholic and Anglican churches, when offerings are collected and presented.|The priest blessed the gifts during the offertory.  神父在奉献礼中祝福了礼物。|Synonyms: oblation, presentation|Antonyms: N/A|noun
