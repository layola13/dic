demisable:/dɪˈmaɪzəbl/ | adj. | 可转让的，可出租的 |  Subject to lease; capable of being demised or leased.  Referring to property, especially land or buildings, that can be legally transferred, typically through a lease. | The demisable premises included the main building and the adjacent parking lot.  可出租的房屋包括主楼和邻近的停车场。| The landlord confirmed that the property was demisable for a period of five years. 房东确认该房产可出租五年。 | leasable, rentable | non-demisable, non-leasable | adjective
