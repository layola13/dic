emmies:/ˈɛmiz/
noun
艾美奖
Emmy Awards, awards given annually for outstanding achievements in the American television industry.
The Emmys are considered the most prestigious awards in television. 艾美奖被认为是电视界最 prestigious 的奖项。
She won three Emmies for her role in the drama series. 她凭借在电视剧中的角色赢得了三项艾美奖。
synonyms: Emmy Awards, television awards
antonyms: (No direct antonym for an award)
