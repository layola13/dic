fueler:/ˈfjuːələr/| n.|加油员；加油机；加燃料者|A person or machine that fuels vehicles or equipment.  Someone who adds fuel to something.|The fueler filled the airplane's tanks. 加油员给飞机油箱加满了油。|Synonyms: gas attendant, refueler,  tanker|Antonyms: drainer, emptier|noun
