americanized:/əˈmɛrɪkənaɪzd/
| adj. |美国化的|Made or become American in character; assimilated to the customs and institutions of the U.S.|例句：The restaurant serves americanized Chinese food. 这家餐厅供应美式中餐。|例句：Many immigrants become americanized over time. 许多移民随着时间的推移变得美国化了。|近义词：westernized, modernized|反义词:traditional, indigenous|词性:形容词
americanize:/əˈmɛrɪkənaɪz/
|v. (tr.)|美国化|To make or become American in character; assimilate to the customs and institutions of the U.S.|例句：They americanized their names when they moved to the US.  他们搬到美国后把名字美国化了。|例句：The company's marketing strategy was americanized for the US market.  该公司的营销策略针对美国市场进行了美国化。|近义词：westernize, modernize|反义词:localize, traditionalize|词性:动词
