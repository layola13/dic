arachne:/əˈrækniː/| n.|阿拉克涅，蜘蛛|From Greek mythology, Arachne was a skilled weaver who challenged Athena to a weaving contest.  Athena, enraged by Arachne's depiction of the gods' flaws in her tapestry, transformed her into a spider.  The word "arachne" is often used in scientific contexts related to spiders.| Arachne's skill in weaving was unparalleled. 阿拉克涅的编织技艺无与伦比。| synonym: spider | antonym: none | noun
