cloacal:/kloʊˈeɪkəl/| adj.|泄殖腔的；污秽的|Relating to or resembling a cloaca, a common cavity into which the intestinal, urinary, and generative canals open in birds, reptiles, amphibians, fish, and monotremes; or foul or filthy like a sewer.|例句：The cloacal opening in birds serves multiple functions. 鸟类的泄殖腔开口有多种功能。

例句：The zoologist studied the cloacal anatomy of the reptile. 动物学家研究了爬行动物的泄殖腔解剖结构。|近义词：sewer-like, fecal, filthy|反义词:clean, pure|词性:形容词
