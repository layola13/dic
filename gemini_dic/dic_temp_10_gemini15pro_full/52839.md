dime-a-dozen:/ˌdaɪm ə ˈdʌzn/
adjective
不值钱的；普通的；多的
common; inexpensive; of little value.  Something that is so common that it is not valuable.
Generic sci-fi movies are a dime a dozen these days.  如今，普通的科幻电影多如牛毛。
Synonyms: commonplace, ordinary, run-of-the-mill, common, ubiquitous, plentiful, abundant
Antonyms: rare, scarce, uncommon, unusual, unique, valuable, precious
词性: adjective
