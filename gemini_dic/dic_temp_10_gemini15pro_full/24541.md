broadnesses:/ˈbrɔːdnəsɪz/
noun
宽度，广阔，宽容
The broadnesses of his views surprised many. 他的观点之宽广让很多人惊讶。
The broadnesses of the river made it difficult to cross. 这条河很宽，很难渡过。
She showed great broadnesses in forgiving him. 她原谅了他，表现出极大的宽容。
Synonyms: widths, expanses, tolerances, latitudes
Antonyms: narrownesses, restrictions, intolerances
n.

Etymology: From broad + -ness (suffix forming nouns of quality or state).  The word "broad" comes from Old English "brād," meaning wide.
