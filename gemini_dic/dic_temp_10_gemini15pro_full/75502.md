fryable:/ˈfraɪəbl/| adj.|可油炸的，适合油炸的|Suitable for frying; able to be fried.|Example: These potatoes are fryable. 这些土豆可以油炸。 Example: Is this fish fryable? 这条鱼可以油炸吗?|近义词：cookable, edible|反义词:uncookable|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From fry + -able.  "Fry" comes from Old French "frire" (to fry), ultimately from Latin "frigere" (to roast, fry).  "-able" is a suffix of Latin origin meaning "capable of being," "fit to be," or "worthy of being."
