nongelatinous:/nɒn-dʒɪˈlætɪnəs/
adjective
非胶状的，非凝胶状的
Not having the nature or characteristics of gelatin; not gelatinous.
The bacteria formed nongelatinous colonies on the agar plate.  这些细菌在琼脂板上形成了非胶状的菌落。
The substance had a nongelatinous, more liquid consistency.  这种物质具有非胶状的、更具流动性的一致性。
synonyms: nonjellylike, fluid, watery, runny
antonyms: gelatinous, jellylike, viscous, thick
