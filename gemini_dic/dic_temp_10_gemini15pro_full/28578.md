careered:/kəˈrɪrd/
verb
猛冲，飞驰
to move at high speed in an uncontrolled or dangerous way
The car careened down the hill. 汽车飞驰下山。
近义词：rush, speed, hurtle, career
反义词：crawl, creep
词性：verb
