hypothyroidic:/ˌhaɪpoʊθaɪˈrɔɪdɪk/
adjective
甲状腺功能减退的；甲状腺机能低下的
Relating to or affected with hypothyroidism, a condition in which the thyroid gland does not produce enough thyroid hormone.
My doctor thinks I might be hypothyroidic because I've been feeling tired and sluggish lately. 我医生认为我可能有甲状腺功能减退，因为我最近一直感觉疲倦和迟钝。
She was diagnosed as hypothyroidic and started medication immediately. 她被诊断为甲状腺功能减退，并立即开始服药。
近义词:thyroid-deficient
反义词:hyperthyroidic
