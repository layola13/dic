excrement:/ˈɛkskrɪmənt/
noun
排泄物，粪便
Waste matter discharged from the bowels; feces.
The dog left its excrement on the sidewalk. 狗在人行道上留下了粪便。
The analysis of the animal's excrement can reveal its diet. 对动物粪便的分析可以揭示它的饮食。
近义词:feces, dung, stool
反义词: (没有直接的反义词)
词性:noun


词源: From Late Latin *excrementosus, from Latin excrementum (“excrement”), from excernere (“to sift out, separate”), from ex- (“out”) + cernere (“to sift, separate”).
