branned:/brænd/| adj.|麸做的，麸皮的；有麸皮的|Made with or containing bran.  The bread is made with branned flour.  这种面包是用麸皮面粉做的。|例句：She prefers branned bread for its fiber content. 她更喜欢麸皮面包，因为它的纤维含量高。

例句：Branned muffins are a healthier alternative to regular muffins. 麸皮松饼是普通松饼的更健康替代品。|近义词：whole-wheat, wholemeal, high-fiber|反义词:white, refined|词性:形容词
