batshit:/ˈbætʃɪt/| adj.| 疯狂的，精神错乱的，完全荒谬的 | Extremely crazy, insane, or completely ridiculous.  Derived from "bat" (the animal) and "shit" (feces), implying that someone is so crazy they are covered in bat droppings. | That plan is completely batshit!  那计划简直疯了！|  He went batshit crazy when he found out she was cheating.  当他发现她出轨时，他气疯了。 |  crazy, insane, bonkers, nuts, loony | sane, rational, sensible | adjective
