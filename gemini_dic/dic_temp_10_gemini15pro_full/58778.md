dunnest:/ˈdʌnɪst/ | adj. | 最敦实的，最褐色的 (指马) |  Superlative form of "dun," referring to a dull grayish-brown or brownish-gray color, often used to describe horses. |  例句：That's the dunnest horse I've ever seen. 那是我见过的毛色最灰褐的马。| 近义词：brownest, darkest (in the context of horse coat color) | 反义词：lightest, palest (in the context of horse coat color) | 词性: 形容词

dun:/dʌn/ | adj. n. v. |  灰褐色的; 催讨债务的人;  不断索求 | From Old English *dunn, of uncertain origin, perhaps related to Old Norse dúnn (“dark-colored, swarthy”). | 例句1：The horse was a dun color. 这匹马是灰褐色的。 例句2：He works as a dun for a loan company. 他在一家贷款公司当催债人。 | 近义词：(adj.) grayish-brown, brownish-gray; (n.) debt collector | 反义词：(adj.) bright, colorful | 词性: 形容词，名词，动词
