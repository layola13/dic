nonappreciativeness:/ˌnɒnəˌpriːʃəˈtɪvnəs/ | n. | 不领情，不感激，缺乏欣赏 |  The state of being unappreciative or lacking gratitude; failure to recognize or value something. | Her nonappreciativeness of his efforts hurt his feelings. 她对他的努力不领情，伤害了他的感情。 | Synonyms: ingratitude, ungratefulness, thanklessness | Antonyms: gratitude, appreciation, thankfulness | noun

nonappreciative:/ˌnɒnəˌpriːʃəˈtɪv/ | adj. | 不领情的，不感激的，缺乏欣赏的 | Not showing gratitude or appreciation; failing to recognize or value something. | He was nonappreciative of her hard work. 他对她的辛勤工作不领情。 | Synonyms: ungrateful, unthankful, thankless | Antonyms: grateful, appreciative, thankful | adjective
