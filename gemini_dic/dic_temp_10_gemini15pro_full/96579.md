inducer:/ɪnˈd(j)uːsər/| n.|诱导物，诱导剂|Something that causes or brings about an effect, especially in a biological context.  It often refers to a molecule that initiates or increases the rate of a biological process.|The lactose inducer stimulated the production of the enzyme.|近义词：stimulant, activator, trigger|反义词:inhibitor, repressor, blocker|noun
