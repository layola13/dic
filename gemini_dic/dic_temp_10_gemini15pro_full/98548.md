instilling:/ɪnˈstɪlɪŋ/
verb
灌输, 注入
gradually but firmly establish (an idea or attitude, especially a desirable one) in a person's mind.
The coach is instilling a sense of discipline in his players. 教练正在向他的队员灌输纪律意识。
Synonyms:  implanting, inculcating, infusing, introducing, imbuing
Antonyms:  eradicating, removing, extracting, uprooting
verb
