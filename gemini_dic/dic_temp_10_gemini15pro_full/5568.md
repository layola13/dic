also-ran:/ˈɔːlsoʊ ræn/ | n. | 落选者；失败者 |  A person or thing that loses in a race or other competition, especially one that is not considered to have a serious chance of winning. |  He was an also-ran in the presidential election. 他在总统选举中落选了。 | synonyms: loser, failure, non-winner, underdog | antonyms: winner, victor, champion | noun

also-ran:/ˈɔːlsoʊ ræn/ | adj. | 落选的；失败的 |  Describing a person or thing that loses in a race or other competition, especially one that is not considered to have a serious chance of winning. | The also-ran candidate received little media attention.  这位落选的候选人几乎没有受到媒体的关注。| synonyms: losing, unsuccessful, defeated | antonyms: winning, successful, victorious | adjective
