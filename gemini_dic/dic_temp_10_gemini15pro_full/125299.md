multigene:/ˌmʌltiˈdʒiːn/ |adj.|多基因的|Containing or involving multiple genes.  Relating to or determined by several genes at different loci.|Researchers are studying the multigene family responsible for this trait. 研究人员正在研究负责这种性状的多基因家族。|polygenic|monogenic|adjective

multigene:/ˌmʌltiˈdʒiːn/ |n.|多基因|A group of genes that are similar in structure and function, often derived from a common ancestral gene through duplication events.|The multigene encoding for the protein was found to be highly conserved. 编码该蛋白质的多基因被发现是高度保守的。|gene family, gene cluster|-|noun
