commutual: /kəˈmjuːtʃuəl/
adjective
相互的，共同的
Characterized by sharing in common; reciprocal.
例句：They share a commutual love for classical music. 他们共同热爱古典音乐。

例句：The two families had a commutual respect for each other. 这两个家庭互相尊重。

近义词：mutual, reciprocal, shared, joint
反义词：separate, individual, independent
词性: adjective

词源:  A back-formation from "commute" + "-al," influenced by "mutual."  While "commute" often refers to travel, its older meaning of "exchange" or "interchange" is relevant here.  The prefix "com-" emphasizes the shared or joint nature of the exchange.
