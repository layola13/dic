confinedness's:/kənˈfaɪndnɪsɪz/
noun
限制的状态；被禁锢的状态
The state of being confined, limited, or restricted.
The confinedness's of the small apartment made her feel claustrophobic.  这间小公寓的局促感让她感到幽闭恐惧症。
Her injury resulted in the confinedness's of her movement. 她的伤导致她的行动受限。
近义词:restriction, constraint, limitation, confinement
反义词:freedom, liberty, openness, expanse
词性:名词

词源:
confinedness + 's (所有格)

confinedness 来自 confine + -ness

confine (v.) 来自古法语 confiner,  最终源自拉丁语 confinis “接壤的，相邻的”，由 com- “一起” + finis “边界，界限” 构成。

-ness (后缀)  构成名词，表示状态或性质。

's (后缀) 表示所有格。
