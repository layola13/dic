mammonism:/ˈmæmənɪzəm/
n.
拜金主义;  贪财;  爱钱如命
Excessive love of money or material wealth; greed.
He condemned the mammonism of modern society. 他谴责现代社会的拜金主义。
近义词:materialism, avarice, greed, cupidity
反义词:altruism, generosity, philanthropy
词性:noun

Etymology:
From Mammon, the Aramaic word for wealth, personified in the New Testament as a false god.
