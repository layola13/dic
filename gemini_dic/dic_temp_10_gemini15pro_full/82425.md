gruesomest:/ˈɡruːsəməst/| adj.|最可怕的，最令人毛骨悚然的|Superlative form of gruesome: most gruesome.  Causing repulsion or horror; grisly.|The gruesomest scene in the movie was the aftermath of the battle.|Synonyms: horrific, appalling, ghastly, macabre, hideous|Antonyms: pleasant, delightful, agreeable, beautiful|adjective
