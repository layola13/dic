non-reaction:/ˌnɒn riˈækʃən/ | n. | 无反应，不反应 | Lack of response or reaction to a stimulus or event.  |  The government's non-reaction to the crisis angered the public. 政府对危机的无动于衷激怒了公众。 |  inaction, indifference, apathy | reaction, response | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + reaction.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "lack of," derived from Latin. "Reaction" comes from the Late Latin "reactionem," meaning "action in response to some influence."
