over-fluent:/ˌoʊvərˈfluːənt/ | adj. | 过于流利的，流利得有些不自然的 | Speaking too smoothly and rapidly, often to the point of being unnatural or insincere. |  His over-fluent speech made me suspicious. 他过于流利的讲话让我起了疑心。|  The salesman's over-fluent pitch sounded rehearsed and unconvincing.  这个推销员过于流利的推销词听起来像是排练过的，很不令人信服。| 近义词: glib, voluble | 反义词: hesitant, halting | 词性: 形容词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "fluent" (from Latin "fluentem," present participle of "fluere" meaning "to flow")
