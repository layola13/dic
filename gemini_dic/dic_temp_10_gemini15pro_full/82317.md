grouters:/ˈɡraʊtərz/| n.|灌浆工；填缝工；灌浆机|A person or machine that applies grout (a thin mortar) to fill gaps between tiles, bricks, etc.|The grouters finished sealing the bathroom tiles. 灌浆工完成了浴室瓷砖的密封。|近义词：tile setters, tilers|反义词:|noun

grouters:/ˈɡraʊtərz/| n.|灌浆材料|Materials used for grouting.|The grouters were mixed according to the manufacturer's instructions. 灌浆材料按照制造商的说明进行混合。|近义词：grout, cement, mortar|反义词:|noun

词源:  From the verb "grout" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Grout" comes from Old English "grūt" meaning "coarse meal."
