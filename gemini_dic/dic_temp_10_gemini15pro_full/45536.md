curing:/ˈkjʊərɪŋ/| n. v. adj.|治愈，固化，腌制|The process of preserving or hardening something, often by drying, smoking, or chemical treatment.  It can also refer to the process of healing a disease or ailment.|例句：The curing of the concrete took several days.  混凝土的养护用了好几天。|近义词：healing, hardening, preserving, treating|反义词:worsening, decaying, rotting|词性:noun, verb, adjective
