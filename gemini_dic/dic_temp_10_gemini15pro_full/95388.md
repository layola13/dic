inched:/ɪntʃt/| v.|缓慢移动，一点点地移动|to move very slowly and carefully in a particular direction|The snail inched along the leaf.  蜗牛缓慢地沿着叶子爬行。|The line inched forward.队伍缓慢地向前移动。|近义词：crept, crawled, edged|反义词: sped, raced, dashed|词性:动词
词源: From inch (n.) + -ed, the past participle suffix forming verbs from nouns.  Ultimately from Latin "uncia" (twelfth part).
