denationalizes:/diːˌnæʃənəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使（企业、产业等）非国有化；取消…的国有化
To transfer (a business or industry) from public to private ownership.
The government denationalized the airline last year. 政府去年将这家航空公司私有化了。
The government is planning to denationalize the railway system. 政府正计划将铁路系统私有化。
privatizes, deregulates
