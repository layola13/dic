hominoid:/ˈhɑːmɪnɔɪd/ | adj. | 类人猿的；似人的 | Characteristic of or resembling the Hominoidea.  |  The hominoid skull structure is distinct. 类人猿的头骨结构很独特。 | synonyms: anthropoid, apelike | antonyms: (No direct antonym) | adjective
