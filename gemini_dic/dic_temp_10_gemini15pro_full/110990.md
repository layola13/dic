liquidizes:/ˈlɪkwɪˌdaɪzɪz/
verb
清算；变卖；使液化
To convert assets into cash; to make or become liquid.
The company liquidizes its assets to pay off its debts.  公司变卖资产以偿还债务。
The heat liquidizes the butter.  高温使黄油液化。
synonyms: dissolve, melt, liquefy
antonyms: solidify, freeze
词性: 动词
