augmentative:/ɔːɡˈmɛntətɪv/ | adj. | 增大的，扩大性的 |  Making or tending to make something greater; increasing. |  She has an augmentative personality, always trying to make things bigger and better. 她有着一种扩张性的性格，总是试图把事情做得更大更好。 |  The augmentative effect of the new policy was immediately apparent. 新政策的扩大效应立即显现出来。 |  expansive, increasing, magnifying, amplifying |  reductive, diminutive, lessening | adjective

augmentative:/ɔːɡˈmɛntətɪv/ | n. | 增大词，扩展词 | A word formed by adding a suffix to a base word, expressing greater size, importance, or intensity. | "Gigantic" is an augmentative of "giant."  “Gigantic” 是 “giant” 的增大词。| While less common in English, some languages use augmentatives regularly. 虽然在英语中不太常见，但有些语言经常使用增大词。 | magnification, enlargement, expansion | reduction, diminution | noun
