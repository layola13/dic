blackleg:/ˈblækˌleɡ/ | n. | 破坏罢工者；骗子；黑胫病（植物病害） | a person who continues to work when fellow workers are on strike; a swindler or cheat; a disease of plants, especially of cereals and potatoes, characterized by blackening of the stem |  The strikers were furious at the blacklegs who crossed the picket line. 罢工者对那些越过纠察线的人非常愤怒。| synonym: scab, strikebreaker, cheat, fraud, con artist | antonym: striker, unionist, honest person | noun

blackleg:/ˈblækˌleɡ/ | n. | （尤指牛）黑肢病 | (especially of cattle) a bacterial disease characterized by gas-filled swellings in the muscles, often fatal |  The farmer lost several cows to blackleg. 那个农民因为黑肢病损失了几头牛。| synonym: black quarter | antonym:  (No direct antonym for a disease) | noun
