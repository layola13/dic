censoriousness:/ˌsensɔːriəsnəs/
n.
苛刻，吹毛求疵
The state of being excessively critical or fault-finding.
Her censoriousness made it difficult for anyone to feel comfortable around her. 她的吹毛求疵让周围的人感到很不自在。
Synonyms: criticalness, faultfinding, hypercriticism,苛刻,挑剔
Antonyms: tolerance, leniency, acceptance,宽容,宽厚
词性: 名词

censorious:/senˈsɔːriəs/
adj.
吹毛求疵的，爱挑剔的
Severely critical of others.
He cast a censorious eye over the work of his juniors. 他以挑剔的眼光审视着下属们的工作。
Synonyms: critical, faultfinding, disapproving, judgemental
Antonyms: tolerant, lenient, accepting, approving
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *censoriosus, from Latin censor "censor" (from censēre "to assess, judge") + -osus "-ous."  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *kens- "to announce, proclaim."
