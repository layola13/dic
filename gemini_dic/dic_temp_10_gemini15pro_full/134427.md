nonirradiated:/ˌnɒnɪˈreɪdieɪtɪd/
adjective
未辐照的，未经辐射的
Not having been exposed to radiation.
例句：
Consumers are increasingly seeking out nonirradiated foods due to health concerns.  消费者越来越关注健康问题，因此寻求未辐照的食品。

The study compared the shelf life of irradiated and nonirradiated strawberries.  该研究比较了辐照草莓和未辐照草莓的保质期。

近义词：untreated, unexposed
反义词：irradiated
词性：adjective
