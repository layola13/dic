monandria:/məˈnændriə/
noun
单雄蕊植物；单雄蕊纲
Plants having a single stamen in each flower.  Belonging to the class Monandria (obsolete taxonomic classification).
The botanist categorized the flower as monandria due to its single stamen.  这位植物学家因其单雄蕊而将这朵花归类为单雄蕊植物。
