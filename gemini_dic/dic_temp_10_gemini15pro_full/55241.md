dissimilarly:/dɪˈsɪmɪlə(r)li/
adv.
不同地，相异地
in a way that is not similar
The twins, despite their identical appearance, behaved dissimilarly. 这对双胞胎尽管外貌相同，但行为却不同。
近义词: differently, distinctively, uniquely
反义词: similarly, alike, identically
词性: 副词


dissimilar:/dɪˈsɪmɪlə(r)/
adj.
不同的，相异的
not similar
Their tastes in music are dissimilar. 他们在音乐方面的品味不同。
近义词: different, unlike, distinct
反义词: similar, alike, identical
词性: 形容词


词源:
From dis- (expressing negation) + similar (alike, resembling) + -ly (adverbial suffix). Ultimately from Latin similis (like).
