conserve:/kənˈsɜːrv/ /kənˈsɝːv/| v. |保存，保护；节约|To protect something from being lost, wasted, or damaged; to avoid wasteful or destructive use.|We must conserve our forests for future generations. 我们必须为子孙后代保护我们的森林。|

The park was established to conserve the area's natural beauty.  这个公园是为了保护该地区的自然美景而建立的。


|近义词：preserve, protect, save|反义词:waste, squander, deplete|词性:动词|


conserve:/ˈkɒnsɜːv/ /ˈkɑːnsɝːv/| n. |蜜饯，果酱|A sweet food made by preserving fruit with sugar.|She enjoys homemade conserves. 她喜欢自制果酱。|

Apricot conserve is her specialty. 杏子果酱是她的特色菜。


|近义词：preserve, jam, jelly|反义词: N/A|词性:名词|
