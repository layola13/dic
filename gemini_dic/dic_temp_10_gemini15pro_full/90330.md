housing:/ˈhaʊzɪŋ/| n.| 住房，房屋，住宅| The city has a serious housing shortage. 这个城市住房严重短缺。| synonyms: accommodation, lodging, shelter | antonyms: homelessness | noun

Etymology: From the verb "to house," which comes from Old English "hūs" meaning "house." The suffix "-ing" turns the verb into a noun indicating the act or process of providing houses, or the houses themselves.
