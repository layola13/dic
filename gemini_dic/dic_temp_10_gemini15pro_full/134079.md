nonillustration:/ˌnɒnɪləˈstreɪʃn/ |n.|非例证，缺乏例证|Absence of illustration; lack of examples or supporting evidence.|The nonillustration of his argument made it unconvincing. 他的论点缺乏例证，使之难以令人信服。|Example:  The speaker's nonillustration of the complex theory left the audience confused. 演讲者没有举例说明这个复杂的理论，让听众感到困惑。
|Synonyms: lack of examples, absence of proof, unsupported assertion|Antonyms: illustration, example, proof, demonstration|noun
