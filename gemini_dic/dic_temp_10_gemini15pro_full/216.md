abbreviator:/əˈbriːvieɪtər/| n.|缩写者，缩写器|A person or thing that abbreviates.|The author used an online abbreviator to shorten the titles of the articles.|近义词:shortener, condenser|反义词:expander, elaborator|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin abbreviator, from Latin abbreviare (to shorten).

USAGE->
The abbreviator shortened the long title to just a few words.  =  缩写者将长标题缩短为几个字。

The automatic abbreviator saved me a lot of time. =  自动缩写器为我节省了很多时间。


She works as an abbreviator for a legal firm, summarizing lengthy documents. = 她在一家律师事务所担任缩写员，负责总结冗长的文件。

He used an abbreviator tool to create acronyms for the project's different phases. = 他使用缩写工具为项目的不同阶段创建首字母缩略词。
