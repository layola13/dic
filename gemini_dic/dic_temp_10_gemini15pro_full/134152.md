nonimpressionablenesses:/ˌnɒnɪmˈprɛʃənəbəlnəsɪz/
noun
不易受影响；无动于衷
The state or quality of being nonimpressionable; resistance to being influenced or affected.
His nonimpressionableness made him a difficult person to persuade. 他不易受影响的性格让他很难被说服。
Synonyms:  imperviousness, insensitivity, resistance,  unresponsiveness
Antonyms: impressionability, sensitivity, susceptibility, responsiveness
词性: 名词
