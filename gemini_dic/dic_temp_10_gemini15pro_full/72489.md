fluores:/flʊˈɔːrɛz/ | n. | 荧光 | The emission of light by a substance that has absorbed light or other electromagnetic radiation.  It is a form of luminescence. | The mineral fluoresced under UV light.  这种矿物在紫外线下发出荧光。 | 近义词: luminescence, glow | 反义词: darkness, blackness | 词性: 名词

fluorescent:/flʊˈɔːrɛsənt/ | adj. | 荧光的；发荧光的 | Having or showing fluorescence. |  A fluorescent light bulb.  荧光灯泡。| 近义词: luminous, glowing | 反义词: dull, dark | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *fluōrēscent- (stem of *fluōrēscēns), present participle of *fluōrēscere "begin to shine, shimmer," from fluōre "to flow" (see fluent) + -escere (inceptive suffix).
