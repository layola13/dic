dialogical:/ˌdaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. |对话的，对话式的| Relating to or in the form of a dialogue; characterized by dialogue. | Example: The play was highly dialogical, with witty banter between the two main characters.  这出戏充满了对话，两位主角之间妙语连珠。| Example:  Dialogical teaching methods encourage student interaction and discussion. 对话式教学方法鼓励学生互动和讨论。 | Synonyms: conversational, interactive, communicative | Antonyms: monological, unidirectional | adjective
