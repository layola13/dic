childproofing:/ˈtʃaɪldpruːfɪŋ/| n.|儿童安全防护，防儿童措施|The act of making something safe for children, by covering sharp corners, locking cupboards, etc.|例句：We need to do some serious childproofing before the baby starts crawling. 我们需要在宝宝开始爬行之前做好一些认真的儿童安全防护工作。

例句：Thorough childproofing can prevent many accidents. 彻底的儿童安全防护可以防止很多事故。|近义词：child safety, babyproofing|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps negligence or carelessness in context) |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
child (n.) + proof (v.) + -ing (suffix)

"child" comes from Old English "cild."
"proof" comes from Old French "prove," ultimately from Latin "probare" (to test, try, approve).
"-ing" is a suffix forming present participles and gerunds.
