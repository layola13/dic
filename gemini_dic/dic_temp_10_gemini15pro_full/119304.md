microanalysis:/ˌmaɪkroʊəˈnælɪsɪs/ | n. |微量分析；显微分析|The detailed analysis of a very small quantity of material, using chemical, physical, or microscopic techniques.  |  例句：Microanalysis of the soil revealed traces of pollutants. 土壤的微量分析揭示了污染物的痕迹。

|例句：The microanalysis confirmed the presence of the rare mineral. 显微分析证实了稀有矿物的存在。 | 近义词：microscopy, microexamination | 反义词: macroanalysis |词性: noun
