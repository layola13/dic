murexide:/mjuˈrɛksaɪd/ | n. | 骨螺紫，紫色染料 | a purple dye obtained from certain mollusks, historically used for dyeing textiles. | Murexide was a valuable commodity in ancient times.  骨螺紫在古代是一种珍贵的商品。| Near synonyms: Tyrian purple, royal purple | Antonyms: N/A | noun


murexoin:/mʊrɛkˈsɔɪn/ | n. | 骨螺紫指示剂，金属指示剂 | A metal indicator, also known as ammonium purpurate, used in complexometric titrations. It forms colored complexes with metal ions, allowing for the determination of their concentration. | Murexoin is used as an indicator in EDTA titrations. 骨螺紫指示剂用于EDTA滴定中作为指示剂。| Near synonyms: Ammonium purpurate, metal indicator | Antonyms: N/A | noun
