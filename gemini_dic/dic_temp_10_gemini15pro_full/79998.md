go kaput:/ɡoʊ kəˈpʊt/
|v.| 坏掉，出故障，完蛋|To break down; to stop functioning.  To fail completely. | My old car finally went kaput last week. 我的旧车终于在上周报废了。|
|v.|  The company went kaput after the stock market crash.  公司在股市崩盘后破产了。|
|近义词:| break down, fail, die, expire, collapse, conk out|
|反义词:| work, function, succeed, thrive|
|词性:|动词短语 (phrasal verb)|


go kaput:/ɡoʊ kəˈpʊt/
|adj.(informal)| 坏掉的，故障的|Broken; not working.| After being dropped, my phone went kaput. 我的手机摔了之后就坏了。|
|adj.(informal)| The project went kaput due to lack of funding.  这个项目由于缺乏资金而失败了。|
|近义词:| broken, defunct, out of order|
|反义词:| working, functional, operational|
|词性:|形容词 (informal usage)|
