overreact:/ˌoʊvərriˈækt/| vi.|反应过度，过激反应|To react or respond more strongly than is necessary or appropriate.|She overreacted to his innocent comment and started yelling. 她对他的无心之言反应过度，开始大喊大叫。|近义词：overdo, exaggerate|反义词: underreact|词性:动词
