as-big-as-life:/æz ˌbɪɡ əz ˈlaɪf/
|adj.|
|栩栩如生；活灵活现；千真万确；(尤指令人惊讶地)本人亲自；真真切切|
|Appearing in person, especially unexpectedly; actually present.  It emphasizes someone's presence, often implying surprise or disbelief.|
|I couldn't believe it, there he was, as big as life, standing right in front of me.|我简直不敢相信，他竟然活生生地站在我面前。

|And there she was, as big as life, on the front page of the newspaper.|她竟然上了报纸头版，而且照片那么大。

|近义词: in person, in the flesh, real, actual|
|反义词: absent, missing, imaginary|
|词性: adjective|
