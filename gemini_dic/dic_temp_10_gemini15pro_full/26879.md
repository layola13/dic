cakes:/keɪks/ | n. | 蛋糕（复数）| A sweet baked food made from a mixture of flour, sugar, eggs, fat, and other ingredients. |  She baked three delicious cakes for the party. 她为派对烤了三个美味的蛋糕。| Synonyms: pastries, desserts | Antonyms: None | Noun

cakes:/keɪks/ | v. | 结块，凝固 (third-person singular simple present cakes, present participle caking, simple past and past participle caked) | To form a hard or crusty surface or mass. | The mud caked on his boots. 泥巴在他靴子上结块了。 | Synonyms: harden, solidify, crust | Antonyms: soften, liquefy | Verb
