fathomed:/ˈfæðəmd/ | v. | 理解；了解；测深 | To understand or comprehend something complex or mysterious; to measure the depth of water. |  The scientist fathomed the mystery of the ancient artifact. 科学家解开了古代文物的奥秘。| Synonyms: understand, comprehend, grasp, gauge, plumb | Antonyms: misunderstand, misinterpret, confuse | verb
