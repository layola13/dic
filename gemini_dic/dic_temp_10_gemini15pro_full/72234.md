floriculturist:/ˌflɔːrɪˈkʌltʃərɪst/
n.|花卉栽培家；花匠|A person who cultivates or grows flowers, especially for commercial purposes. |例句：The floriculturist carefully tended to her prize-winning roses.  花卉栽培家精心照料着她那些获奖的玫瑰。

例句：He learned the art of floriculture from his grandfather, a renowned floriculturist. 他从他祖父——一位著名的花卉栽培家那里学习了花卉栽培的艺术。
