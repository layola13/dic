cool-it:/ˈkuːl ɪt/ | v. | 冷静下来；放松；别激动 | To calm down; to relax; to stop being agitated or excited. | Example 1: "Cool it, guys, there's no need to fight." 冷静点，伙计们，没必要打架。

Example 2:  "I told him to cool it with the jokes; they were getting offensive." 我叫他别再开玩笑了，那些笑话已经开始冒犯人了。 | Synonyms: calm down, relax, chill out, settle down | Antonyms:  get excited, get worked up | verb
