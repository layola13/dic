matchlessness:/ˈmætʃləsnəs/| n.|无与伦比，无比，卓越|The matchlessness of her beauty captivated everyone. 她的美貌无与伦比，迷住了所有人。|Synonyms:  incomparability, peerlessness, unrivaled, unequaled, unsurpassed|Antonyms: mediocrity, ordinariness, commonness|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From matchless + -ness.  Matchless itself comes from match (meaning "equal") + -less (meaning "without").  Ultimately derived from Old English *gemæcca.
