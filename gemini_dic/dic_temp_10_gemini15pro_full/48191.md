decompress:/ˌdiːkəmˈprɛs/ | v. | 减压，解压缩 | To release from pressure; to reduce the pressure on. decompression:/ˌdiːkəmˈprɛʃən/ | n. | 减压，解压缩 | The act or process of decompressing. |  Divers must undergo decompression after deep dives to avoid the bends. 潜水员深潜后必须进行减压以避免减压病。 | synonyms: relaxation, expansion, inflation | antonyms: compression, condensation, pressurization | noun
