overmoralized:/ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 过分道德化的，过分说教的 | Excessively moralizing; imposing or emphasizing moral principles to an excessive or unnecessary degree.  Characterized by excessive concern with morality, often to the point of being self-righteous or judgmental. |  He gave an overmoralized speech about the dangers of drinking. 他做了一个关于饮酒危害的过分说教的演讲。|  sanctimonious, preachy, moralistic |  amoral, immoral, unethical | adjective
