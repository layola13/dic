hortatory:/ˈhɔːrtətɔːri/ (US) /ˈhɔːtətəri/ (UK)
adjective
劝告的；激励的
tending or aiming to exhort; encouraging
The coach delivered a hortatory speech to his team before the big game. 教练在大赛前对他的队员们发表了鼓舞人心的讲话。
近义词: encouraging, inspiring, exhortative, urging
反义词: discouraging, dissuasive, deterrent
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *hortatorius, from hortator (“encourager”), from hortari (“to encourage, urge, exhort”), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *gher- (“to grasp, enclose”).
