fourest:/ˈfɔːrɪst/ |adj.| 最多森林的;最多树木的|  Superlative adjective of "forested," meaning having the most forests or trees. |The fourest region of the country is in the northwest. 这个国家森林最多的地区在西北部。| 近义词: most wooded, densest, thickest (when referring to tree cover) | 反义词: barest, least wooded |词性:形容词

词源:  Derived from "forest" + "-est" (superlative suffix). "Forest" comes from Old French "forest" (ultimately from Latin "foris," meaning "outside").
