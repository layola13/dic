ludic:/ˈluːdɪk/| adj.|嬉戏的，玩耍的，好玩的|Relating to or characterized by play; playful.|例句：The children's museum encouraged ludic learning through interactive exhibits. 孩子们的博物馆通过互动展览鼓励寓教于乐。

例句：Her ludic spirit made her a favorite among the children. 她爱玩的天性使她成为孩子们中最受欢迎的人。|近义词：playful, sportive, frolicsome, merry, jolly|反义词：serious, solemn, grave, earnest|词性:形容词
词源:From Latin *ludicus*, from ludus (“play, game, sport”).
