multi-partist:/ˌmʌltiˈpɑːtɪst/
|adj.|多党制的|Relating to or involving multiple political parties.|The country transitioned to a multi-partist democracy after the fall of the dictatorship. 这个国家在独裁统治垮台后过渡到多党民主制。|Synonyms: multiparty|Antonyms: single-party, one-party|adjective


multi-partist:/ˌmʌltiˈpɑːtɪst/
|n.|多党制拥护者|A supporter of a multi-party system.|Many multi-partists believe it is essential for a healthy democracy. 许多多党制拥护者认为多党制对健康的民主至关重要。|Synonyms: advocate of multiparty system|Antonyms: supporter of one-party system|noun
