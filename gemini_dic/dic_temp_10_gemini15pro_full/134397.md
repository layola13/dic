nonintrovertedly:/nɒnˌɪntrəˈvɜːtɪdli/ | adv. | 非内向地；外向地 | In a manner that is not introverted; outgoingly, sociably. | She spoke nonintrovertedly at the meeting, surprising her colleagues. 她在外向地在会议上发言，让她的同事们感到惊讶。| She nonintrovertedly joined the conversation. 她落落大方地加入了谈话。| 近义词:extrovertedly, outgoingly, sociably | 反义词:introvertedly, shyly, reservedly | 词性:副词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "introverted" (adjective meaning "shy and withdrawn") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  The word "introverted" itself comes from Latin "introvertere" meaning "to turn inwards."
