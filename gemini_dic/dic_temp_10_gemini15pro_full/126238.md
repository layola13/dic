mycologically:/ˌmaɪkəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ | adv. | 真菌学地，从真菌学的角度 |  In a way that relates to the study of fungi. | The mushrooms were mycologically examined. 这些蘑菇经过了真菌学检验。 | 近义词: fungally | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From *mycology* + *-ical* + *-ly*. *Mycology* comes from the Greek *mýkēs* (fungus) + *-logia* (study of). *-ical* forms adjectives from nouns, and *-ly* forms adverbs from adjectives.
