boxberry:/ˈbɒksˌbɛri/ /ˈbɑːksˌberi/| n. |冬青|A low-growing evergreen shrub (Gaultheria procumbens) of eastern North America, having white bell-shaped flowers, spicy red berries, and aromatic leaves that yield an oil used medicinally and as a flavoring.|The boxberry plant is a common sight in the forests of Maine. 冬青植物在缅因州的森林中很常见。|Synonyms: checkerberry, teaberry, wintergreen|Antonyms: N/A|noun

boxberry:/ˈbɒksˌbɛri/ /ˈbɑːksˌberi/| n. |冬青果|The edible, aromatic red berry of the boxberry plant.|Boxberries can be used to make tea and jam. 冬青果可以用来制作茶和果酱。|Synonyms: checkerberry, teaberry, wintergreen berry|Antonyms: N/A|noun
