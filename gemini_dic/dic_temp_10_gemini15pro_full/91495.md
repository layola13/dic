hymnals:/ˈhɪmnəlz/
noun
赞美诗集
a book containing a collection of hymns
The church provided new hymnals for the congregation.  教堂为会众提供了新的赞美诗集。
synonyms: hymn books, songbooks
antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider secular books)
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *ymnal, from Anglo-Norman ymnal, from Medieval Latin hymnale, from Late Latin hymnus (“hymn”).
