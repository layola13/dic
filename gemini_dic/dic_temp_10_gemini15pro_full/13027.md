ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *astragalus*, from Ancient Greek ἀστράγαλος (astrágalos, “anklebone, die, vertebra; astragal (architectural molding)”). The architectural sense is from the resemblance of the molding to a row of dice or vertebrae.
