accidentality: /ˌæksɪdɛnˈtælɪti/
n.
偶然性; 意外性
The quality or state of being accidental; the fact of occurring by chance.
The accidentality of the discovery led to further research. 这一发现的偶然性导致了进一步的研究。

The sheer accidentality of their meeting surprised them both. 他们相遇的纯粹偶然性让两人都感到惊讶。

近义词: chance, fortuity, randomness, contingency
反义词: intentionality, design, purpose, inevitability
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From accidental + -ity.  "Accidental" comes from Late Latin accidentalis, from accidens, accidentis "something happening," present participle of accidere "to happen," from ad- "to" + cadere "to fall."  The suffix "-ity" is used to form nouns expressing state or quality.
