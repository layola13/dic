nonsibilants:/nɑːnˈsɪbɪlənts/ | n. |非咝音|  Consonant sounds that are not sibilants, characterized by the absence of a high-pitched hissing sound.| The sounds /p/, /b/, /t/, /d/, /k/, /ɡ/, /m/, /n/, /ŋ/, /l/, /r/, /w/, and /j/ are nonsibilants in English. | /p/, /b/, /t/, /d/, /k/, /ɡ/, /m/, /n/, /ŋ/, /l/, /r/, /w/, 和 /j/ 在英语中是非咝音。 | 近义词:non-sibilant consonants | 反义词:sibilants | 词性:noun
