bilingual:/baɪˈlɪŋɡwəl/ | adj. n.| 双语的；能说两种语言的人 | Speaking two languages fluently; a person who speaks two languages fluently. | She is bilingual in English and Spanish. 她精通英语和西班牙语。| synonyms: multilingual, fluent in two languages | antonyms: monolingual | adjective, noun
bilingual:/baɪˈlɪŋɡwəl/ | n. | 能说两种语言的人 | A person who speaks two languages fluently. | The company is looking for bilinguals who can speak both English and Mandarin. 这家公司正在寻找会说英语和普通话的双语人才。| synonyms: multilingual, polyglot | antonyms: monolingual | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bi- (two) + lingual (related to language). Ultimately from Latin "lingua" (tongue, language).
