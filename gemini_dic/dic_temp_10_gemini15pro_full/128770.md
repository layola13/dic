nighing:/ˈnaɪɪŋ/| v.|嘶鸣；临近|The sound of horses nighing filled the air. 马嘶声弥漫在空气中。|近义词：approaching, nearing|反义词: receding, departing|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *nyghen, from Old English nēah, nīeh (“near, nigh”), from Proto-Germanic *nēhwaz (“near”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂engh- (“narrow, near”). Cognate with Scots nych (“near”), West Frisian nei (“near”), Dutch na (“after, to”), German nahe (“near”), Danish næ (“near, close”), Swedish nä (“near, close”), Icelandic ná (“near”).
