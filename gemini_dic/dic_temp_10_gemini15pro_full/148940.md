overwrest: /ˌoʊvərˈrest/ | vt. | 扭夺；过度用力；曲解 | To wrest (something) excessively; to distort or misinterpret. | He overwrest the meaning of the poem to fit his own theory. 他为了迎合自己的理论而曲解了这首诗的含义。 | The lawyer overwrest the witness's words. 律师曲解了证人的话。 | 近义词: distort, misinterpret, twist, pervert | 反义词: clarify, explain, interpret correctly | 词性: 动词

Etymology:
From over- + wrest.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much." "Wrest" comes from the Old English *wræstan and means "to twist or pull violently."
