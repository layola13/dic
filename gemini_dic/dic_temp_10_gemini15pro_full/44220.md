crocker:/ˈkrɑːkər/ (US), /ˈkrɒkər/ (UK)| n.|陶器商，陶器工人；<非正式>虚弱的人，有病的人|A person who makes or sells pottery, or (informally) a person who is weak or ill.|He was a skilled crocker, known for his intricate designs. 他是一位技艺精湛的陶工，以其复杂的图案而闻名。|The flu left him a crocker for weeks. 流感让他病了好几周。|近义词：potter, invalid (informal)|反义词: healthy person|noun
