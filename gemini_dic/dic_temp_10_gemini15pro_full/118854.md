metaphysically:/ˌmɛtəˈfɪzɪkli/| adv.|形而上学地，抽象地|In a way that relates to metaphysics; abstractly; dealing with the fundamental nature of reality.  It refers to considerations beyond the physical or material realm, often concerning abstract concepts like being, knowing, identity, time, and space.|例句：He spoke metaphysically about the nature of consciousness. 他形而上学地谈论了意识的本质。

例句：The question of free will can be considered metaphysically. 自由意志的问题可以从形而上学角度来考虑。|近义词：abstractly, philosophically, theoretically|反义词：physically, concretely, empirically|词性:副词
