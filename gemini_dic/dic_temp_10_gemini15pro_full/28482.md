carcinogen:/kɑːrˈsɪnədʒən/ /kɑːrˈsɪnəˌdʒɛn/| n.|致癌物|A substance capable of causing cancer in living tissue.|Exposure to certain carcinogens can significantly increase the risk of developing cancer. 接触某些致癌物质会显著增加患癌风险。|Synonym: cancer-causing agent, oncogen|Antonym:  anticarcinogen|noun


carcinogen:/kɑːrˈsɪnədʒən/ /kɑːrˈsɪnəˌdʒɛn/| adj.|致癌的|Having the potential to cause cancer.|Carcinogenic substances should be handled with extreme care. 致癌物质应极其小心地处理。|Synonym: cancer-inducing, oncogenic|Antonym:  anticarcinogenic|adjective
