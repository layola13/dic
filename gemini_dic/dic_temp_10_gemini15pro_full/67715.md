exuperate:/ɛksˈuːpəreɪt/ /ɪɡˈz(j)uːpəreɪt/
verb
超越；胜过；超过
to surpass; to excel; to exceed.
He exuperated all expectations with his performance. 他以其表现超越了所有人的期望。
近义词: excel, surpass, transcend, outdo, exceed
反义词: fail, fall short, underperform
词性: verb
