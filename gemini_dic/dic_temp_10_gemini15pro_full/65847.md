inevitably:/ɪnˈevɪtəbli/| adv.|不可避免地，必然地|Something that is inevitable is certain to happen and cannot be avoided. |例句：Inevitably, the conversation turned to politics.  对话不可避免地转向了政治。

例句：She knew that, inevitably, she would have to face him again. 她知道自己不可避免地将不得不再次面对他。|近义词：certainly, unavoidably, necessarily|反义词:avoidably,  preventably|词性:副词
