garaged:/ɡəˈrɑːʒd/

garaged:/ɡəˈrɑːʒd/
verb (past participle of "garage")|把…放入车库，将…停放在车库中|(of a vehicle) placed or stored in a garage|Having garaged the car, she walked into the house. 把车停好后，她走进了房子。|近义词：parked, stored, sheltered|反义词：removed, taken out|词性:verb
