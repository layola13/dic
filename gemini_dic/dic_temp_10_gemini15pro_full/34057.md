civetlike:/ˈsɪvɪtlaɪk/| adj.|像灵猫的，类似灵猫的|Resembling or characteristic of a civet, a nocturnal mammal known for its musk-like scent.|The civetlike odor permeated the air.  空气中弥漫着类似灵猫的气味。|The civetlike markings on the cat's fur were distinctive.  这只猫皮毛上类似灵猫的斑纹很独特。|近义词：musky, pungent|反义词：odorless, unscented|词性:形容词
