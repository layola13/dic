blandiloquious:/ˌblændɪˈloʊkwɪəs/| adj.|奉承的，甜言蜜语的|Characterized by flattering or ingratiating speech; smooth-tongued.|The salesman's blandiloquious manner convinced her to buy the expensive car.  销售员奉承的态度说服她买了那辆昂贵的车。|Synonyms: flattering, ingratiating, unctuous, smooth-tongued|Antonyms: blunt, frank, candid, forthright|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *blandiloquus*, from *blandus* (flattering, charming) + *loqui* (to speak).
