geishas:/ˈɡeɪʃəz/ | n. |艺妓| A Japanese woman who is trained to entertain men with conversation, music, dance, and games.  A traditional Japanese female entertainer who acts as a hostess. | The geishas entertained the guests with traditional music and dance. 艺伎们用传统的音乐和舞蹈来招待客人。| The geishas' elegant kimonos were a beautiful sight. 艺伎们优雅的和服是一道美丽的风景。|  synonyms: entertainers, hostesses | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "commoner," "ordinary woman") | noun
