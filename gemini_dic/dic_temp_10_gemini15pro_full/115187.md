manicheism:/ˈmænɪkiːɪzəm/| n.|摩尼教,二元论|A dualistic religious system with Christian, Gnostic, and pagan elements, originating in Persia in the 3rd century. It emphasized a cosmic struggle between good and evil, light and darkness.|例句：His philosophy reflects a strain of Manicheism, seeing the world in terms of a battle between good and evil. 他的哲学反映了一种摩尼教的倾向，将世界视为善恶之战。

例句：Manicheism spread widely throughout the Roman Empire, despite persecution. 尽管受到迫害，摩尼教仍然在罗马帝国广泛传播。|近义词：dualism, gnosticism|反义词:monism, holism|词性:noun
