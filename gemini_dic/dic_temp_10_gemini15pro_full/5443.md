almsdeed:/ˈɑːmzdiːd/
n.
施舍；善行
An act of giving money or goods to the poor and needy.
The old woman performed an almsdeed by giving the beggar some bread.  这位老妇人给了乞丐一些面包，做了一件善事。

The billionaire's foundation is known for its many almsdeeds.  这位亿万富翁的基金会以其众多的善举而闻名。

Synonym: charity, donation, benefaction, philanthropy
Antonym: selfishness, greed, avarice
词性: 名词
