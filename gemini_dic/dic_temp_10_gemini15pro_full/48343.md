decrumbing:/diːˈkrʌmɪŋ/
verb
去除面包屑; 去除…上的面包屑；（比喻）清理，清除（不想要的痕迹或影响）
The act of removing crumbs, or more figuratively, removing unwanted traces or influences.
She was decrumbing the tablecloth after lunch.  她在午饭后清理桌布上的面包屑。
The government is decrumbing the old laws. 政府正在清理旧法律。
synonyms: cleaning, clearing, purging
antonyms: soiling, messing, contaminating
