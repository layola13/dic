coffeeman:/ˈkɒfiːmæn/ (UK) /ˈkɔːfiːmæn/ (US)| n. |咖啡师，咖啡爱好者，经常喝咖啡的人|A person who makes and serves coffee (especially in a coffee shop), or a person who is very fond of or frequently drinks coffee. |例句：The coffeeman expertly crafted a latte with a beautiful swan design.  咖啡师熟练地制作了一杯带有漂亮天鹅图案的拿铁咖啡。

例句：He's such a coffeeman, he can't function without his morning cup. 他真是个咖啡迷，如果没有早上的那杯咖啡就无法正常工作。|近义词：barista, coffee lover, caffeine fiend |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps tea drinker) |词性:noun
