orthoform:/ˈɔːrθəfɔːrm/| n. |正字形，规范形式|A word's standard spelling or pronunciation, especially as established by a dictionary or other authority.|例句：The orthoform of "colour" in American English is "color".  美式英语中“colour”的正字形是“color”。

例句：The dictionary lists the preferred orthoform for each word.  字典列出了每个单词的首选正字形。|近义词：standard form, canonical form|反义词:variant, nonstandard form|词性:noun
