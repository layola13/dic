lapelless:/ləˈpɛllɪs/
| adj. | 无翻领的 | Without lapels (the folded flaps of cloth on the front of a jacket or coat). | She wore a lapelless jacket to the party. 她穿着一件无翻领的夹克去参加了聚会。 |  Example: The lapelless design of the cardigan gave it a modern look. 这件开襟羊毛衫的无翻领设计使其具有现代感。 | Near synonyms: collarless, open-necked | Antonym: lapeled | Word Class: Adjective
