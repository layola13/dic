arm-twistings:/ˈɑːrmˌtwɪstɪŋz/
noun
施压，强迫，逼迫
The act of pressuring someone to do something they don't want to do.
He finally agreed to the deal after much arm-twisting. 经过一番强迫后，他最终同意了这笔交易。
coercion, pressure, intimidation
persuasion, encouragement

词性: noun
