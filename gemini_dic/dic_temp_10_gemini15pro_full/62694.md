engin:/ˈɛndʒɪn/ | n. | 引擎，发动机；机车；火车头 |A machine with moving parts that converts power into motion. |The engin of the car is broken. 汽车的发动机坏了。| 近义词：motor, machine, locomotive | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 manual 手动的) | 词性：名词


Etymology:
From Old French engin "skill, wit, ingenuity; war machine," from Latin ingenium "natural disposition, talent, intelligence; clever invention." The meaning shifted from "skill" to "product of skill" and finally to "machine."
