nonslaveholding:/ˌnɑːnˈsleɪvˌhoʊldɪŋ/
adjective
非蓄奴的，不拥有奴隶的
Not owning or holding slaves.
The nonslaveholding states of the North were increasingly at odds with the South. 北方的非蓄奴州与南方越来越不合。
Nonslaveholding farmers often struggled to compete with the large plantations. 非蓄奴农民往往难以与大型种植园竞争。
近义词:free, abolitionist
反义词:slaveholding, pro-slavery
词性:adjective
