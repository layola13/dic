allegorist:/ˈælɪɡərɪst/ | n. | 寓言家；讽刺作家 | A person who uses allegory, especially in writing or speaking. |  John Bunyan, author of "The Pilgrim's Progress," is a famous allegorist.  约翰·班扬，《天路历程》的作者，是一位著名的寓言家。

|  The allegorist used animals to represent different human characteristics.  这位寓言作家用动物来代表不同的人类特征。| 近义词: fabulist, moralist, symbolist | 反义词: realist | 词性: noun
