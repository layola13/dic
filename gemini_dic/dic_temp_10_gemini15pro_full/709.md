absentation: /ˌæbsənˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 缺席；缺勤；心不在焉 |  The state of being absent from a place or duty; failure to attend.  | His frequent absences from class are worrying. 他经常缺课，令人担忧。

Her long absence from work was due to illness. 她长时间缺勤是因为生病。 | 近义词: absence, nonattendance, truancy | 反义词: presence, attendance | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin absentatio, from absentare "to be absent," from Latin absens, present participle of abesse "to be away," from ab "off, away from" + esse "to be."

USAGE->

Absentation from meetings is discouraged. = 不鼓励缺席会议。
His frequent absentation affects his performance. = 他经常缺勤影响了他的表现。
