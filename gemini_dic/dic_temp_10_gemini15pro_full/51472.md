detouring:/dɪˈtʊərɪŋ/
| n. |绕道，绕行|The act of taking a different route than the usual or most direct one.| Due to the road closure, we were detouring through the countryside. 由于道路封闭，我们绕道乡村行驶。|
| v. |绕道而行，绕路|To travel by a different route than the usual or most direct one.| We had to detour around the accident. 我们不得不绕过事故现场。|近义词：diverting, bypassing, rerouting|反义词：directing|词性:动名词

detoured:/dɪˈtʊərd/
| v. |绕道而行，绕路|(past tense of detour)| We detoured around the construction zone. 我们绕过了施工区域。|
| adj. |绕道的|Forced to take a different route.| The detoured traffic caused a lot of congestion. 绕行的车辆造成了严重的拥堵。|近义词：diverted, bypassed, rerouted|反义词：direct|词性:动词(过去式),形容词
