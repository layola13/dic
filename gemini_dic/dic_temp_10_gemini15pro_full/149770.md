palatize:/ˈpælətaɪz/ (US) /ˈpælətʌɪz/ (UK)| v. |使腭音化；使适应上层社会|To make a sound a palatal sound; to make something refined or sophisticated, often associated with upper-class tastes.|The "y" sound in "yes" is palatized. “yes”中的“y”音是腭音化的。

She tried to palatize her accent to fit in with the upper class. 她试图使自己的口音变得更上流社会化，以便融入上层社会。|近义词：refine, polish, cultivate|反义词: vulgarize, coarsen|词性:动词
