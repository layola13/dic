helped-out:/hɛlpt aʊt/
verb phrase
帮助摆脱困境，给予帮助
To give assistance to someone, especially in a difficult situation.
My neighbors helped me out when my car broke down. 我的邻居在我汽车抛锚时帮助了我。
The lifeguard helped out the struggling swimmer. 救生员帮助了挣扎的游泳者。
Synonyms: assist, aid, support, relieve
Antonyms: hinder, obstruct, impede
