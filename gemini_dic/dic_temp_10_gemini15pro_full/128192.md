neurasthenia: /ˌnʊərəsˈθiːniə/ | n. | 神经衰弱 | A condition characterized by fatigue, headache, and irritability, thought to result from exhaustion of the nervous system. |  She was diagnosed with neurasthenia after months of persistent fatigue. 她持续数月的疲劳后被诊断为神经衰弱。

She suffered from neurasthenia brought on by overwork. 她因为过度工作而患上了神经衰弱。 | 近义词: nervous exhaustion, fatigue, burnout | 反义词: vigor, energy | 词性: noun
