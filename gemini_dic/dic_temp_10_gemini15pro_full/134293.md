noninhibitory:/ˌnɒnɪnˈhɪbɪtəri/
adjective
非抑制性的
Not inhibiting or restraining; tending to promote or encourage.  Preventing something from being inhibited.
The drug has a noninhibitory effect on the enzyme. 这种药物对这种酶有非抑制性作用。
non-restrictive, promoting, stimulating, encouraging
inhibitory, restrictive, suppressing
