morphon:/ˈmɔːrfɒn/ (UK) /ˈmɔːrfɑːn/ (US) | n. | 语素，词素 | A minimal meaningful unit in a language.  A morpheme is the smallest unit of language that has meaning. It can be a word or a part of a word (such as a prefix or suffix).|  Example: The word "unbreakable" contains three morphemes: "un-", "break", and "-able".  这个单词“unbreakable”包含三个语素：“un-”，“break”和“-able”。

| Example: The morpheme "re-" in "rewrite" means "again".  语素“re-”在“rewrite”中表示“再次”。 | Synonym: morpheme | Antonym: N/A | Noun
