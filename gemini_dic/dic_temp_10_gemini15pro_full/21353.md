bloop:/bluːp/| n. vi. vt.|低沉的、间歇的声音；发出哔哔声；打出高飞短打|A low, intermittent sound, often electronic; to make such a sound; in baseball, to hit a short, high fly ball.|The radar blooped intermittently. 雷达断断续续地发出哔哔声.  He blooped a single to right field. 他打出一个右外野方向的高飞短打。|近义词：blip, beep, ping|反义词: silence, quiet|词性:名词，动词

词源:  Likely onomatopoeic, imitating the sound itself.  可能是拟声词，模仿声音本身。
