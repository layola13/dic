hybridizations:/ˌhaɪbrɪdɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. |杂交，混合| The process of combining different varieties of plants or animals to produce a hybrid; the state of being hybridized.  The act or product of hybridizing. |  Many plant hybridizations have resulted in disease-resistant crops. 许多植物杂交已经培育出抗病作物。| synonyms: crossings, crossbreeds, interbreedings | antonyms: purebreeding | noun
