moppet:/ˈmɒpɪt/ (UK) /ˈmɑːpɪt/ (US)|n.|小孩; 小家伙 (尤指可爱或可怜的小孩)|A small child, often endearingly or pityingly.|例句：She cuddled the little moppet. 她搂着那个可爱的小家伙。

例句：The poor little moppet lost her teddy bear. 可怜的小家伙把她的泰迪熊弄丢了.|近义词：child, toddler, kid, youngster, little one|反义词:adult, grown-up|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain. Possibly related to Dutch *moppe*, meaning "doll". First recorded in the 16th century.
