carpetmonger:/ˈkɑːrpɪtˌmʌŋɡər/ | n. | 地毯商；(尤指)追求女色的男人 | A man who pursues women aggressively, especially for sexual gratification.  Often implies a sleazy or unscrupulous character. |  He had a reputation as a bit of a carpetmonger, always flirting with the women in the office. 他在办公室里总是和女人调情，有点好色之徒的名声。| 近义词: womanizer, philanderer, playboy, lecher | 反义词:  loyal partner, faithful husband | 词性: noun


Etymology:
"Carpet" refers to the bedroom, implying sexual activity. "Monger" comes from the Old English "mangere," meaning trader or dealer.  Thus, a "carpetmonger" is someone who "trades" in sexual conquests.
