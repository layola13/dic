foliar:/ˈfoʊliər/ (US), /ˈfəʊliə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|叶的；叶状的；经由叶面施的|Relating to leaves or leaf-like structures; applied to or through the leaves of a plant.|例句：Foliar feeding is a quick way to provide nutrients to plants. 叶面施肥是为植物提供营养的快速方法。

例句：The botanist studied the foliar patterns of the fern. 植物学家研究了蕨类植物的叶片图案。

近义词：leafy|反义词:|词性:adjective
