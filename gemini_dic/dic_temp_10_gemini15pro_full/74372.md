frak:/fræk/ | n. | 哥特体小写字母f | A stylized form of the lowercase letter 'f', typically used in mathematical formulas. | The equation used a frak to represent the function.  这个等式用哥特体小写字母f来表示函数。| 近义词:  (无直接近义词，可描述为) stylized 'f', script 'f' | 反义词: (无反义词) | 词性: 名词

frak:/fræk/ | v. | 水力压裂 | The process of injecting liquid at high pressure into subterranean rocks, boreholes, etc., so as to force open existing fissures and extract oil or gas. |  They decided to frak the well to increase production. 他们决定对这口井进行水力压裂以提高产量。 | 近义词: hydraulic fracturing, hydrofracking | 反义词: (无反义词) | 词性: 动词
