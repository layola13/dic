overcures:/ˌoʊvərˈkjʊrz/| v. (overcures, overcuring, overcured) |过度固化，过度硫化|To cure (rubber, plastic, etc.) for too long a time or at too high a temperature.|例句：The tires were overcured and became brittle. 轮胎过度硫化变脆了。|例句：Overcuring the resin can lead to cracking.  过度固化树脂会导致开裂。|近义词：overbake, overcook|反义词：undercure|词性：动词

overcures:/ˌoʊvərˈkjʊrz/| n. (plural overcures) |过度固化，过度硫化的状态|The state of being overcured.|例句：The overcure made the material unusable. 过度固化使得材料无法使用。|例句：The overcure resulted in a loss of flexibility. 过度固化导致柔韧性损失。|近义词：overbaking, overcooking|反义词：undercure|词性：名词
