nightingale:/ˈnaɪtɪŋɡeɪl/ | n. |夜莺|A small migratory thrush of the Old World, noted for its rich melodious song, especially at night. |例句：The nightingale's song filled the night air. 夜莺的歌声充满了夜空。

例句：We heard a nightingale singing in the woods. 我们听到树林里有一只夜莺在歌唱。

近义词：songbird, thrush |反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑与嘈杂的鸟类对比，如crow（乌鸦）) |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *nihtegale, from niht (night) + galan (to sing).  Meaning "night singer."
