cocultivations:/ˌkəʊˌkʌltɪˈveɪʃənz/
|n.|共同培养，共培养物|The cocultivations of different bacterial species can lead to the discovery of new antibiotics. 不同细菌种类的共同培养可以导致新抗生素的发现。|例句：The cocultivations of the two fungi yielded interesting results.  这两种真菌的共同培养产生了有趣的结果。

Cocultivations of human cells with bacteria can mimic infection processes. 人类细胞与细菌的共培养可以模拟感染过程。|近义词：cocultures|反义词: monocultures|词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "co-" (together) + "cultivation" (the act of growing something). "Cultivation" comes from the Latin "cultivare" (to till, cultivate).
