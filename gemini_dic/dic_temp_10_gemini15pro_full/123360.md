monologuists: /məˈnɒləɡɪsts/ (UK) /məˈnɑːləɡɪsts/ (US)
|n.|独白者；进行独白的人|People who deliver monologues, often actors or performers.  A person who gives a long speech.|The play featured several talented monologuists who captivated the audience.  这出戏有几位才华横溢的独白演员，吸引了观众。|Near synonyms: solo performers, speakers, orators|Antonyms:  dialogue participants, conversationalists|Noun
