nirvanic:/nɪrˈvænɪk/ | adj. |涅槃的，极乐的| Relating to or characteristic of Nirvana; a state of perfect happiness and peace.|  She described a nirvanic state of mind after her meditation. 她描述了冥想后一种涅槃般的精神状态。| The music created a nirvanic atmosphere. 音乐营造了一种极乐的氛围。| 近义词: blissful, ecstatic, serene, tranquil | 反义词: agitated, distressed, turbulent | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Nirvana + -ic.  "Nirvana" comes from Sanskrit निर्वाण nirvāṇa, meaning "extinction, disappearance" (of suffering and the cycle of rebirth).
