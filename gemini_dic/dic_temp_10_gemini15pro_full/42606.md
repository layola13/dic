counterquestion:/ˈkaʊntərˌkwɛstʃən/ | n. |反问; 反诘|A question asked in response to a question, often to avoid answering the original question or to shift the focus of the conversation.| "When I asked him about his alibi, he responded with a counterquestion about my motives."  当我问他不在场证明时，他反问我我的动机是什么。|Synonyms: retort, rejoinder, riposte|Antonyms: answer, reply|noun

Etymology->
From counter- + question.  Counter- means "against" or "in opposition to."
