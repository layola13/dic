forbidder:/fərˈbɪdər/ | n. | 禁止者，阻止者 | Someone who forbids something.  | The forbidder of the party was the grumpy old man next door.  派对的禁止者是隔壁脾气暴躁的老人。 | synonyms:  prohibitor, interdictor, restrictor | antonyms:  permitter, allower, authorizer | noun

forbid:/fərˈbɪd/ | v. | 禁止，阻止 | To order (someone) not to do something; to refuse to allow (something). |  I forbid you to go. 我禁止你去。 | synonyms: proscribe, prohibit, ban | antonyms: permit, allow, authorize | verb
