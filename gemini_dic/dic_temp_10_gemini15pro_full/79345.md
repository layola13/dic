gleeman:/ˈɡliːmən/| n.|吟游诗人|A medieval minstrel or professional reciter of poems and ballads, often accompanying himself on a harp or other instrument.  A wandering musician.|例句：The gleeman entertained the court with tales of bravery and romance. 吟游诗人用勇敢和浪漫的故事娱乐宫廷。

例句：Traveling from village to village, the gleeman shared news and stories through his songs.  吟游诗人游走乡村，用歌声传递新闻和故事。|近义词：minstrel, bard, poet, musician|反义词:|词性:noun
