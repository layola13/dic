de-integrations:/diːˌɪntɪˈɡreɪʃənz/
| n. | 瓦解，解体；分解；去整合|The process of breaking down or separating something that was previously integrated.  The de-integrations of the Soviet Union led to the formation of several independent states.|例句：The de-integrations of the company's departments caused communication problems. 公司各部门的解体导致了沟通问题。

例句：The de-integrations of the community following the riot were devastating. 骚乱后社区的瓦解是毁灭性的。|近义词：disintegration, breakdown, separation, fragmentation|反义词: integration, unification, consolidation|词性:名词
