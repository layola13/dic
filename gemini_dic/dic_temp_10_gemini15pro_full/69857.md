ferromagnetic:/ˌfɛroʊmæɡˈnɛtɪk/| adj.|铁磁的，强磁性的|Exhibiting ferromagnetism; readily magnetized by induction and capable of retaining magnetization.|Iron, nickel, and cobalt are ferromagnetic materials. 铁、镍和钴是铁磁性材料。|Synonyms: magnetic, magnetizable|Antonyms: diamagnetic, paramagnetic|adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From ferro- + magnetic. Ferro- comes from Latin ferrum, meaning "iron."
