flail:/fleɪl/| n. v. |连枷; 乱打，挥动|A tool for threshing grain, consisting of a wooden staff with a short, heavy stick swinging from it. To wave or swing wildly.|The farmer used a flail to separate the wheat from the chaff. 农民用连枷把小麦和谷壳分开。 He flailed his arms wildly, trying to keep his balance. 他疯狂地挥舞着手臂，试图保持平衡。|近义词：thresh, beat, flog, swing, wave|反义词:control, steady|词性:名词，动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *flaiel, from Late Latin flagellum "whip, scourge," diminutive of Latin flagrum "whip."
