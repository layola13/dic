monocropping: /ˈmɒnəʊˌkrɒpɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnəʊˌkrɑːpɪŋ/ (US)
monocropping: n.
monocropping: 单作，单一栽培
monocropping: The agricultural practice of growing a single crop year after year on the same land, in the absence of rotation through other crops or growing multiple crops on the same land (polyculture).
monocropping: Monocropping of corn can deplete the soil of nutrients.  玉米的单作会耗尽土壤中的养分。

monocropping: Monocropping can lead to increased pest and disease problems. 单一栽培会导致病虫害问题加剧。

monocropping: 近义词：single cropping
monocropping: 反义词：polyculture, crop rotation, diversified farming
