engineless:/ˈendʒɪnlɪs/
adjective
无引擎的; 没有发动机的
lacking an engine; not equipped with an engine.
The engineless glider soared silently through the air.  无引擎的滑翔机静静地翱翔在空中。
Synonyms: unpowered, motorless
Antonyms: powered, motorized
词性: 形容词
