iconoclastic:/aɪˌkɑːnəˈklæstɪk/ | adj. |打破传统的，反传统的，攻击传统观念的 | Characterized by attacking cherished beliefs or institutions. |  Her iconoclastic views on education often put her at odds with the school board. 她对教育的打破常规的观点经常让她与学校董事会产生分歧。

| Example: The artist's iconoclastic work challenged conventional notions of beauty. 这位艺术家的反传统作品挑战了传统的审美观念。| Synonyms: heretical, unorthodox, rebellious, nonconformist, dissident | Antonyms: conventional, traditional, orthodox, conformist | Word type: Adjective
