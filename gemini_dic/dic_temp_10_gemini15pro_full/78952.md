girdlingly:/ˈɡɜːrdlɪŋli/
adverb
环绕地，束缚地
In a manner that encircles or constricts.
The python coiled girdlingly around its prey.  蟒蛇紧紧地缠绕着它的猎物。
The ivy grew girdlingly around the oak tree, slowly suffocating it.  常春藤环绕着橡树生长，慢慢地使它窒息。
近义词:  tightly, constrictingly, encirclingly
反义词: loosely, freely
