nonsuppositional:/ˌnɑːnsʌpəˈzɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 非预设的，不基于假设的 | Not based on or involving supposition or presumption; not taking something for granted. |  This nonsuppositional account of knowledge avoids many of the traditional problems.  这种非预设的知识论避免了许多传统问题。|  His nonsuppositional approach to philosophy was refreshing. 他的非预设哲学方法令人耳目一新。 | near-synonyms: axiomatic, self-evident, a priori | antonyms:  presumptive, conjectural, hypothetical | adjective
