cacique:/kəˈsiːk/ | n. |酋长；首领；(西印度群岛或中南美洲西班牙殖民时代)酋长 |A cacique was a leader of an indigenous group in the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America, particularly during the Spanish colonial era. | The cacique ruled over a large territory and commanded the respect of his people.  这位酋长统治着一片广阔的领土，并赢得了人民的尊敬。|The museum displayed artifacts from the time of the cacique.  博物馆展出了酋长时代的文物。| synonyms: chief, chieftain, leader, head, ruler | antonyms: follower, subordinate | noun
