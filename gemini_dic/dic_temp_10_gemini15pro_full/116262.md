massacring:/ˈmæsəkərɪŋ/ |v. |屠杀，残杀|The act of killing a large number of people indiscriminately and cruelly. |They were accused of massacring innocent civilians. 他们被指控屠杀了无辜的平民。|Synonyms: slaughtering, butchering, killing, murdering |Antonyms: saving, rescuing, protecting| Verb

massacre:/ˈmæsəkər/ |n. |大屠杀，惨败|The act of killing a large number of people indiscriminately and cruelly; a complete defeat or failure.|The massacre shocked the world. 这场大屠杀震惊了世界。 The game was a massacre; we lost 10-0. 这场比赛是一场惨败，我们以0比10输了。|Synonyms: slaughter, carnage, bloodbath, rout |Antonyms: victory, triumph| Noun
