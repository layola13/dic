It often implies a boisterous, jovial, and excessive indulgence in pleasure. |He displayed a true pantagruelism at the buffet, piling his plate high with every dish imaginable. 他在自助餐上表现出真正的贪吃无度，把盘子里堆满了各种各样的菜。

The sheer pantagruelism of the feast astonished the guests. 盛宴的奢华程度令客人们震惊不已。 | 近义词: gluttony, voracity, insatiable appetite | 反义词: asceticism, moderation, temperance | 词性: noun
