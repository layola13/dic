hyalite:/ˈhaɪəlaɪt/| n. |透明蛋白石，玻 Opal|A transparent, colorless variety of opal, resembling glass.|例句：The hyalite shimmered under the moonlight. 透明蛋白石在月光下闪闪发光。

例句：He found a small piece of hyalite embedded in the rock. 他在岩石中发现了一小块透明蛋白石。|近义词：glass opal, Muller's glass|反义词: None|词性:noun

Etymology: From German *Hyalith*, from Ancient Greek *ὕαλος* (*húalos*, “glass”) + *-ite*.
