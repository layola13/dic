champion:/ˈtʃæmpiən/ (US) /ˈtʃæmpɪən/ (UK)| n.  vt. adj.|冠军; 捍卫者; 拥护者|A person who has defeated or surpassed all rivals in a competition, especially in sports. A person who fights or argues for a cause or on behalf of someone else.|例句：She is the reigning Olympic champion. 她是卫冕奥运冠军。|近义词：winner, victor, advocate, supporter, defender|反义词：loser, opponent, rival|词性:名词

champion:/ˈtʃæmpiən/ (US) /ˈtʃæmpɪən/ (UK)| vt.|捍卫; 拥护|To defend or support a cause or person.|例句：He championed the rights of the disabled. 他捍卫残疾人的权利。|近义词：support, defend, advocate, promote|反义词：oppose, attack, criticize|词性:动词

champion:/ˈtʃæmpiən/ (US) /ˈtʃæmpɪən/ (UK)| adj.|一流的; 冠军的|Excellent; first-rate. Of a champion.|例句：He's a champion swimmer. 他是一名一流的游泳运动员。|近义词：excellent, first-rate, top-notch, leading|反义词：inferior, second-rate|词性:形容词


词源: From Old French *champion, from Late Latin campionem (nominative campio) "gladiator, fighter in the campus," from campus "field of battle."
