congruous: /ˈkɒŋɡruːəs/ (US) /ˈkɒŋɡruːəs/ (UK)| adj.|一致的，适合的，相称的|Being in agreement, harmony, or correspondence; appropriate or fitting.|Her behavior was perfectly congruous with her values. 她的行为与她的价值观完全一致。|The modern furniture seemed congruous with the minimalist decor of the apartment. 现代家具似乎与公寓的极简主义装饰相得益彰。|近义词：consistent, appropriate, fitting, suitable, harmonious, compatible|反义词：incongruous, inappropriate, unsuitable, incompatible, discordant|词性:形容词

词源:
Late Middle English: from Latin congruus, from congruere ‘agree’, from con- ‘together’ + ruere ‘fall, rush’.
