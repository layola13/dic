darth:/dɑːrθ/| n. |黑暗的，邪恶的|A fictional title used before the given name of a Sith Lord, especially in the Star Wars universe, signifying the character's adoption of the dark side of the Force.  It's often understood as a title similar to "Lord" or "Master".  While not strictly a word on its own, it functions as one within the Star Wars context. |例句：Darth Vader is one of the most iconic villains in cinematic history.  达斯·维达是电影史上最具标志性的反派之一。

例句：He was seduced by the dark side and became known as Darth Tyranus. 他被黑暗面诱惑，成为了达斯·泰拉努斯。|近义词：dark, evil, sinister |反义词:light, good, benevolent |词性: proper noun (functions as a title)
