dingo:/ˈdɪŋɡoʊ/ (US) /ˈdɪŋɡəʊ/ (UK)| n.|澳洲野犬|A wild or half-wild dog of Australia, reddish-brown or yellowish in color, believed to be descended from domestic dogs introduced to Australia thousands of years ago.|Dingoes are native to Australia. 澳洲野犬原产于澳大利亚。|The dingo howled at the moon. 澳洲野犬对着月亮嚎叫。|Synonyms: wild dog, feral dog|Antonyms: domestic dog|noun
