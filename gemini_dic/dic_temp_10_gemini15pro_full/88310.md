hipper:/ˈhɪpər/| adj.|更时髦的，更懂行的|More fashionable or aware of current trends, especially in music, fashion, and culture.  It's the comparative form of "hip."|例句：She's hipper than her older sister. 她比她姐姐更时髦。

例句：He's trying to be hipper by wearing those clothes. 他想通过穿那些衣服让自己看起来更时髦。|近义词：cooler, trendier, more stylish, more fashionable|反义词：unhip, square, out of touch, old-fashioned|词性:形容词
