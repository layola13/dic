fusarole: /ˈfjuːzəˌroʊl/ | n. | 梭子，纺锤，纺锤形的东西 | A weighted spindle used in the spinning of yarn or thread.  It gives momentum to the twisting action and acts as a temporary bobbin for the accumulating yarn. |  The archaeologist unearthed a clay fusarole, indicating textile production at the site. 考古学家出土了一个陶土梭子，表明该遗址曾进行过纺织生产。| Synonyms: spindle whorl, whorl | Antonyms: N/A | noun
