nonworker:/ˌnɒnˈwɜːkər/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˈwɜːrkər/| n. |非劳动者，失业者|A person who is not working, especially one who is unemployed or not part of the labor force. |Example: The government is providing training programs for nonworkers to help them acquire job skills. 政府正在为非劳动者提供培训项目，以帮助他们获得工作技能。|Example:  The percentage of nonworkers in the population has increased due to the economic downturn.  由于经济衰退，人口中非劳动者的比例有所增加。|近义词：unemployed, jobless, idler|反义词:worker, employee|词性:noun

nonworking:/ˌnɒnˈwɜːkɪŋ/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˈwɜːrkɪŋ/| adj. |不工作的，非工作的|Not working or not involved in work. |Example: The nonworking population includes retirees, students, and those unable to work. 不工作人口包括退休人员、学生和无法工作的人。|Example:  The survey collected data on both working and nonworking individuals.  这项调查收集了在职和非在职人员的数据。|近义词：unemployed, jobless, idle|反义词:working, employed|词性:adjective
