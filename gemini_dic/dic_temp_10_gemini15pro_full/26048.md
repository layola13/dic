bushfowl:/ˈbʊʃfaʊl/| n. |灌丛鸡|A type of bird, specifically a francolin, found in Africa. These birds are known for their ground-dwelling habits and are often hunted for game.  | We saw a covey of bushfowl scurrying into the undergrowth. 我们看到一群灌丛鸡匆匆跑进灌木丛中。 |  The bushfowl's call is a distinctive series of whistles. 灌丛鸡的叫声是一连串独特的口哨声。 | 近义词：francolin, spurfowl | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "bush" + "fowl." "Bush" refers to the bird's habitat, and "fowl" is a general term for birds, especially those used as game.
