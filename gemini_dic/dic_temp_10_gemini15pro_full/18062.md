beguilement:/bɪˈɡaɪlmənt/| n. |欺骗；诱惑；魅力|The act of beguiling someone; the state of being beguiled.  A charming or enchanting quality.| Her beguilement was such that he readily agreed to her request. 她的魅力如此之大，他欣然同意了她的请求。|Synonyms: charm, enchantment, allure, fascination, seduction|Antonyms: repulsion, aversion, disgust|noun

beguile:/bɪˈɡaɪl/| vt. |欺骗；诱惑；使着迷|To charm or enchant (someone), sometimes in a deceptive way. To trick (someone) into doing something.| He beguiled them with his charm and wit. 他用他的魅力和机智迷惑了他们。  He was beguiled into investing in the fraudulent scheme. 他被骗投资了这个骗局。|Synonyms: charm, enchant, allure, captivate, deceive, trick|Antonyms: repel, disgust, disenchant|verb
