gowned:/ɡaʊnd/
adjective
穿着长袍的，穿着礼服的
dressed in a gown, especially a long, flowing one.
The gowned figure glided across the stage.  穿着长袍的身影滑过舞台。
The graduates, all gowned in black, waited for the ceremony to begin. 毕业生们都穿着黑色长袍，等待着仪式的开始。
近义词: robed, clad
反义词: undressed, unclothed
词性: adjective

词源: From gown + -ed.  The word "gown" comes from the Middle English *goune*, from the Anglo-Norman *gune*, ultimately from Frankish *gunna*, a word of uncertain origin, possibly Celtic.
