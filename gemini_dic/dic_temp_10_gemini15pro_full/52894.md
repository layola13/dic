dimit:/dɪˈmɪt/ | v. |  辞退；免职；让…离开 | To resign from an office or position; to allow or cause to resign. |  He was dimitted from his post as CEO. 他被免去了首席执行官的职务。 | dismiss, discharge, release | appoint, retain | verb

Etymology: From Latin *dimittere*, from *di-* (apart, away) + *mittere* (to send).
