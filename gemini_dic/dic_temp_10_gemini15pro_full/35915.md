cocreation:/ˌkoʊkriˈeɪʃən/| n.|共同创造，共同创作|The act of jointly creating something, typically involving collaboration between a company and its customers or users.  It emphasizes shared responsibility and ownership in the creation process.|例句：Cocreation allows companies to tap into the collective intelligence of their customers. 共同创造使公司能够利用其客户的集体智慧。

例句：The new product was a result of cocreation between the company and its user community.  这款新产品是公司与其用户社区共同创造的成果。|近义词：collaboration, joint creation, partnership, teamwork|反义词：independent creation, solo work|词性:noun
