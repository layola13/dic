dote:/doʊt/| vi.| 溺爱；过分喜爱；痴迷 | to be extremely and uncritically fond of someone; adore. | She dotes on her grandchildren. 她溺爱她的孙子孙女们。|  He dotes on his new car. 他对他的新车爱不释手。 | synonyms: adore, cherish, idolize, treasure | antonyms: despise, dislike, loathe | verb
