indissolubly:/ˌɪndɪˈsɒljəbli/ | adv. | 不可分解地，牢不可破地 | In a manner that cannot be dissolved or separated. |  The two concepts are indissolubly linked. 这两个概念是不可分割地联系在一起的。 |  inseparably, permanently, inextricably | solubly, separably | adverb

indissolubly:/ˌɪndɪˈsɒljəbli/ | adv. | 不可溶解地 | In a manner that cannot be dissolved. | The chemical is indissolubly bound to the plastic.  这种化学物质与塑料不可溶解地结合在一起。 | inseparably, permanently, inextricably | solubly, separably | adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *indissolubilis, from in- (“not”) + dissolubilis (“dissoluble”), from dis- + solvere (“to loosen, dissolve”).
