clathrate:/ˈklæθreɪt/| n.|笼形化合物；包合物|A chemical substance consisting of a lattice of one type of molecule enclosing molecules of a second type.|Methane clathrate is found in large quantities under the ocean floor. 甲烷包合物在海底大量存在。|近义词：inclusion compound, complex|反义词: N/A|名词


clathrate:/ˈklæθreɪt/| adj.|笼形的|Resembling or relating to a clathrate.|Clathrate hydrates are ice-like solids. 笼形水合物是类似冰的固体。|近义词：cage-like|反义词: N/A|形容词
