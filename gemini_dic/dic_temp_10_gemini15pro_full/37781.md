compatriotism:/kəmˈpeɪtriətɪzəm/
n.
爱国主义; 同胞情誼
Love for one's country, especially as manifested in a group of compatriots.
Compatriotism is a powerful force that unites people in times of crisis. 同胞爱国主义是危机时期团结人民的强大力量。
The soldiers felt a strong sense of compatriotism as they fought for their country. 士兵们为祖国而战时，感到强烈的爱国主义情怀。
近义词: patriotism, nationalism, fellow feeling
反义词: xenophobia, hatred, animosity
词性: noun

词源:
From com- (together) + patriota (compatriot) + -ism (forming abstract nouns). Ultimately from Latin pater (father) and patria (fatherland).
