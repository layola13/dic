banlieue: /bɑ̃ˈljœ/ (French pronunciation)| n.|郊区，城郊|A suburb, especially of a large city in France.  It often carries connotations of being a less affluent or desirable area than the city center.| Many workers in Paris commute from the banlieues. 许多在巴黎工作的工人从郊区通勤。|synonyms: suburb, outskirts, periphery|antonyms: city center, downtown, urban core|noun
