inningses:/ˈɪnɪŋzɪz/| n.| 局（尤指板球或棒球比赛）|A period of play for a team or a player, especially in cricket or baseball.  A turn at batting in baseball or cricket. |例句：The team scored 150 runs in their first innings.  该队在第一局中得了150分。

例句：He played a brilliant innings and scored a century. 他打了一场精彩的局，并且得到了一个世纪。|近义词：turn, round, stint|反义词:|名词
