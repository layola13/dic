deeding:/ˈdiːdɪŋ/
n.| 契约转让；立契转让(财产)|The deeding of the property was finalized last week.  房产的转让手续已于上周完成。|近义词：conveyancing, transferring, granting|反义词：acquiring, receiving|词性:名词

deeding:/ˈdiːdɪŋ/
v.|立契转让(财产) (deeding的动名词形式，也可视为动词)|They are deeding the land to their children. 他们正在把土地转让给他们的孩子。|近义词：conveying, transferring, granting|反义词：acquiring, receiving|词性:动词

词源: From the verb "deed," which comes from Old English "dæd" meaning "act, deed, action."
