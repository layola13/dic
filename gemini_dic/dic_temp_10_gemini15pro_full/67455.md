exogenous:/ɛkˈsɒdʒɪnəs/ /ɛɡˈzɑːdʒɪnəs/| adj.|外生的，外源的|Originating or produced externally; caused by factors outside the organism or system.|例句：The exogenous growth hormone stimulated rapid development. 外源生长激素刺激了快速发育。

例句：Exogenous factors, such as sunlight and temperature, can influence plant growth. 外界因素，例如阳光和温度，会影响植物生长。|近义词：external, outer, outside, extrinsic|反义词：endogenous, internal, intrinsic|词性: adjective
