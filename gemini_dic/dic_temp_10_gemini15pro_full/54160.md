disentanglements:/dɪsɪnˈtæŋɡlmənts/| n. |解开；理清；摆脱|The act of disentangling something, or the state of being disentangled.  The process of freeing something from a tangled state, or the state of being freed from such a state.|

例句：
The disentanglement of the fishing nets took several hours.
解开渔网花了几个小时。

The successful disentanglement of the two companies' finances was crucial to their survival.
成功理清两家公司的财务状况对它们的生存至关重要。


近义词：separations, unravelings, resolutions, extrications|
反义词：entanglements, complications|
词性：noun


词源:
From dis- + entangle + -ment.  "Dis-" is a prefix meaning "apart," "asunder," or denoting reversal. "Entangle" comes from the Old French "entangler," meaning "to entangle" or "to ensnare." "-ment" is a suffix forming nouns of action or process.
