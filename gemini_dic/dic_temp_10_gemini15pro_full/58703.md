dumpling:/ˈdʌmplɪŋ/ | n. |饺子；汤圆；团子|A small ball of dough (= flour and water mixed) cooked by boiling or steaming, sometimes containing meat or fruit.| 例句：I love eating dumplings. 我喜欢吃饺子。|例句：She made delicious pork dumplings. 她做了美味的猪肉饺子。 |近义词：potsticker, ravioli, pierogi|反义词: (No direct antonym exists for a food item like dumpling.)|名词


dumpling:/ˈdʌmplɪŋ/ | n. |矮胖而结实的人（尤指儿童）|A short, fat, sturdy person, especially a child.| 例句：He's a cute little dumpling. 他是个可爱的小胖墩。 |例句：The baby is such a dumpling!  这个宝宝真是个小团子！|近义词：chubby, plump|反义词: skinny, thin|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From dump (v.) + -ling.  The original sense probably referred to a lump of dough dumped into boiling water.  First recorded in the 16th century.
