overmournfulness:/ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrnfʊlnəs/| n. |过度悲伤，极度悲痛|The state of being excessively mournful or sorrowful, often to an unhealthy or excessive degree.|Her overmournfulness after the death of her goldfish was a bit much. 她对金鱼死后的过度悲伤有点过分了。|近义词：excessive grief, extreme sorrow, inconsolable sadness|反义词：joy, happiness, cheerfulness|词性:noun
Etymology: over- (prefix meaning "excessive") + mournful (adjective meaning "feeling or expressing sorrow") + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality)
