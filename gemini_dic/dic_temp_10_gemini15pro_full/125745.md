mur:/mɜːr/ (UK), /mɝː/ (US)| n.|低沉的说话声；咕哝；喃喃低语|A low, indistinct, continuous sound, typically made by a crowd of people.|The crowd gave a low mur of disapproval. 人群发出低沉的反对声。|Synonyms: mutter, murmur, mumble|Antonyms: shout, yell|noun

mur:/mɜːr/ (UK), /mɝː/ (US)| v.|低沉地说；咕哝；喃喃自语|To say something in a low, indistinct voice.|He murred something about being too busy. 他咕哝着说他太忙了。|Synonyms: mutter, murmur, mumble|Antonyms: shout, yell|verb
