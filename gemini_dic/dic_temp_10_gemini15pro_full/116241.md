mass-marketed:/ˌmæsˈmɑːrkɪtɪd/ | adj. | 大众市场的；面向大众市场的 |  Produced or sold in large quantities and at low prices so as to be available to most people. | Example: Mass-marketed clothing is often less expensive than designer brands.  大众市场的服装通常比名牌服装便宜。| Example: The company successfully transitioned from niche products to mass-marketed goods.  公司成功地从利基产品转型为大众市场商品。 | Synonyms: mass-produced, commercial, popular, mainstream | Antonyms: niche, exclusive, limited-edition, bespoke | adjective
