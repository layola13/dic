amazing:/əˈmeɪzɪŋ/| adj.|令人惊奇的，惊人的，极好的|Causing great surprise or wonder; astonishing.|例句：The view from the mountaintop was amazing. 从山顶望下去的景色令人叹为观止。|例句：She's an amazing cook. 她是一位很棒的厨师。|近义词：astonishing, surprising, wonderful, incredible, remarkable, stunning|反义词：awful, terrible, dreadful, unimpressive|词性:形容词
词源:From amaze + -ing. Amaze comes from Old French *amaer "dismay, confound, perplex," perhaps from Vulgar Latin *exmagare "drive out by magic," from Latin ex- "out" + Late Latin magus "magician" (see magic).
