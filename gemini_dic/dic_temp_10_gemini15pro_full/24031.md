breedy:/ˈbriːdi/| adj.|纯种的，良种的；有…品种特征的；显示优良品种的；|Of, relating to, or characteristic of a particular breed, especially of domestic animals. Showing qualities typical of a good breed; thoroughbred. |例句：The farmer raises only pure-bred, or breedy, cattle.  这位农民只饲养纯种牛。|例句：She admired the breedy lines of the Arabian horse. 她很欣赏这匹阿拉伯马优良的品种特征。|近义词：pedigreed, purebred, thoroughbred|反义词：mongrel, crossbred|词性:形容词
