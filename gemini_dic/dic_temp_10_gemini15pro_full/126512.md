n'gana:/ˈnɑːɡənə/| n.|非洲锥虫病，昏睡病|A disease of cattle, horses, and other livestock in Africa, caused by trypanosomes and transmitted by tsetse flies.  It is characterized by fever, lethargy, and edema.|例句：The n'gana epidemic decimated the cattle population. 非洲锥虫病的流行使牛的数量大大减少。

例句：Researchers are working to develop a vaccine for n'gana. 研究人员正在努力开发一种针对非洲锥虫病的疫苗。|近义词：sleeping sickness, trypanosomiasis|反义词: (None -  it's a specific disease)|词性:noun
