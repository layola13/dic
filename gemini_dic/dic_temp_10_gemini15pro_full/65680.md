evener:/ˈiːvənər/| n.|均化器，平地机，使…均匀的人或物|A tool or device used to make something level or even.  A person or thing that makes something more equal or uniform.|The farmer used an evener to distribute the weight of the plow evenly across the oxen. 农民用均化器将犁的重量均匀地分布在牛身上。|Synonyms: leveler, grader, equalizer|Antonyms: roughener, unevener|noun

evener:/ˈiːvənər/| adj. (comparative evener, superlative evenest)|更均匀的；更平坦的|More even; flatter.|The ground is evener here than it was over there.  这里的地面比那边更平坦。|Synonyms: smoother, flatter, more uniform|Antonyms: rougher, bumpier, more uneven|adjective
