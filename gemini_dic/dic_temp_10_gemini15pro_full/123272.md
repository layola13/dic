monody:/ˈmɒnədi/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnədi/ (US)| n.|挽歌；独唱曲；哀歌；单调的歌曲|A poem or song of lament for a dead person; a long speech by one actor in a play or film; any composition, musical or otherwise, that is mournful in character.|The poet composed a moving monody for his deceased friend.|近义词：elegy, lament, dirge, requiem|反义词:paean, celebration|名词
