cantation:/kænˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 吟唱，歌唱；咒语 | a rhythmic recitation of words or sounds, often as part of a ritual or spell; a chant | The witch muttered a cantation over the bubbling cauldron. 女巫对着冒泡的大锅低声念着咒语。| 近义词：chant, incantation, song, hymn | 反义词: silence | 词性: noun

词源:  From Late Latin *cantationem, accusative singular of *cantatio (“singing, chanting”), from Latin cantare (“to sing”), frequentative of canere (“to sing”).
