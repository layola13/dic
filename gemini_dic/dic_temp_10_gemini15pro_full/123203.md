monochromatically:/ˌmɒnəkrəˈmætɪkli/ /ˌmɑːnəkrəˈmætɪkli/| adv.|单色地，单调地|In a way that uses only shades of one color, or that is dull and uninteresting.|The image was rendered monochromatically in shades of gray.|近义词：monotonously, dully, plainly|反义词: colorfully, vibrantly|词性: 副词

monochrome:/ˈmɒnəkrəʊm/ /ˈmɑːnəkroʊm/| adj. n.|单色的，单色画|Consisting of or displaying images in black and white or in varying tones of only one color; a painting or photograph in monochrome.|The artist preferred monochrome photography.|近义词：black and white, grayscale|反义词: polychrome, multicolored|词性: 形容词, 名词

词源: From Greek *monos* "single, alone" + *khrōma* "color"
