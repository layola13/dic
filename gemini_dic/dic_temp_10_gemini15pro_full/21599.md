blunderer:/ˈblʌndərər/| n. |笨拙的人，犯错的人|A person who makes blunders; a clumsy or inept person.|He was such a blunderer, he tripped over the rug and spilled his drink. 他真是个笨手笨脚的人，被地毯绊倒，把饮料洒了。|  He made a blunder and missed the opportunity. 他犯了个大错，错失了机会。|Synonyms: clumsy person, inept person, bungler, goofball, klutz|Antonyms: expert, adept, professional, proficient|noun

blunder:/ˈblʌndər/| n. vi. |错误，失误；犯大错|A stupid or careless mistake.|I made a terrible blunder by forgetting her birthday. 我把她的生日忘了，犯了一个严重的错误。|She blundered into the wrong room. 她误入了错误的房间。|Synonyms: mistake, error, gaffe, faux pas, slip-up|Antonyms: success, triumph, achievement|noun, verb
