flambes:/flɒmˈbeɪz/ /flæmˈbeɪz/| v. | 烧烤，用烈酒点燃| To cover (food) with liquor, ignite it, and serve it flaming.  The chef flambéed the cherries for the dessert.  厨师用烈酒点燃了樱桃作为甜点。|近义词： ignite, sear, flash-fry |反义词: extinguish, douse |词性: 动词

词源: From French *flamber*, from Old French *flamber*  "to burn, blaze," from *flambe* "flame".
