diapedesis:/ˌdaɪəpɪˈdiːsɪs/
noun
渗出; 血细胞渗出;  白细胞游出
The passage of blood cells through the intact walls of the capillaries, typically accompanying inflammation.
Leukocytes squeeze through spaces between endothelial cells during diapedesis.  白细胞在渗出过程中挤过内皮细胞之间的空隙。
extravasation, transmigration
