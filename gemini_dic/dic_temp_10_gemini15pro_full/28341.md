caranas:/kəˈrɑːnəs/| n. |卡拉纳斯树脂，卡拉纳斯香脂|A fragrant resin obtained from the Bursera graveolens tree, native to South America.  It is used in traditional medicine and as incense.|The caranas resin has a sweet, balsamic aroma. 卡拉纳斯树脂有一种甜美的香脂香味。|Synonym: Palo Santo, Copal|Antonym: N/A|noun

caranas:/kəˈrɑːnəs/| n. |卡拉纳斯树|The Bursera graveolens tree itself, which produces the caranas resin. |Caranas trees grow in dry, rocky terrain. 卡拉纳斯树生长在干燥的岩石地形中。|Synonym: Palo Santo tree|Antonym: N/A|noun
