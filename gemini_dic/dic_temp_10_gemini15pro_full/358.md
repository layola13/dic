abides by:/əˈbaɪdz baɪ/| v.|遵守，遵循|To accept or obey a rule, decision, or agreement. |The company abides by all local and federal regulations.  这家公司遵守所有地方和联邦法规。

The players must abide by the rules of the game. 运动员必须遵守比赛规则。

|近义词：comply with, adhere to, conform to, observe, follow, respect|反义词：disobey, violate, breach, ignore, disregard|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *abiden bi* (to remain near, dwell with), from Old English *ābidan bī* (to wait by, remain near), from *ābidan* (to await, endure, remain) and *bī* (by, near). The modern meaning of "to comply with" developed in the 19th century.
