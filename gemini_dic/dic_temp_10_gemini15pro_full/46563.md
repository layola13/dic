dalmanites:/ˌdælməˈnaɪtiːz/
n.
达尔曼虫（三叶虫的一种）
An extinct genus of trilobites, marine arthropods that lived from the Ordovician to Devonian periods, characterized by a large tail spine and often elaborate head ornamentation.
Dalmanites are commonly found as fossils in certain rock formations.  达尔曼虫的化石常在某些岩层中被发现。
Dalmanites fossils are prized by collectors. 达尔曼虫化石深受收藏家喜爱。
Near Synonyms: trilobite,  Phacops (related genus)
