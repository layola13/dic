beige:/beɪʒ/| adj. n.|米黄色，浅褐色|A pale sandy fawn colour.|The walls were painted a warm beige.|近义词:cream, buff, sand, tan|反义词:black, brown, vibrant|词性:形容词, 名词

Etymology:
From French beige, from the natural undyed color of wool. Ultimately from Late Latin *bigius.
