ammodyte: /ˈæməˌdaɪt/ | n. | 沙潜鱼；沙蜥 | A small, slender fish of the family Ammodytidae, found in coastal waters. They bury themselves in sand. |  例句：The ammodyte darted into the sand to escape the predator. 沙潜鱼猛地钻进沙子里躲避捕食者。

例句：Ammodytes are a common food source for many seabirds. 沙潜鱼是许多海鸟的常见食物来源。| 近义词：sand eel, lance | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From Ancient Greek *ἀμμώδυτος (ammṓdutos, “sand-burrower”), from ἄμμος (ámmos, “sand”) + δύω, δύνω (dúō, dúnō, “to enter, to dive, to set”).
