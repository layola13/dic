parcel:/ˈpɑːrsl/ (US) /ˈpɑːsəl/ (UK)| n. |包裹，小包；(尤指土地的) 一块，一片|A thing or collection of things wrapped in paper in order to be carried or sent by mail.|例句：I received a parcel from my mother yesterday. 我昨天收到了我妈妈寄来的包裹。

parcel:/ˈpɑːrsl/ (US) /ˈpɑːsəl/ (UK)| n. |包裹，小包；(尤指土地的) 一块，一片|A particular quantity of something.|例句：She owns several parcels of land in the area. 她在该地区拥有几块土地。

parcel:/ˈpɑːrsl/ (US) /ˈpɑːsəl/ (UK)| v. |打包，捆扎|Make (something) into a parcel.|例句：They parceled up the goods for delivery. 他们把货物打包以便递送。


parcel:/ˈpɑːrsl/ (US) /ˈpɑːsəl/ (UK)| v. |分配，分给|Divide (something) into portions and distribute them.|例句：The company parceled out the bonuses among the employees. 公司将奖金分配给员工。

近义词: package, bundle, lot, plot, divide, distribute
反义词: unwrap, combine
词性: noun, verb
词源: From Old French *parcele, diminutive of part (“part”), from Latin pars.
