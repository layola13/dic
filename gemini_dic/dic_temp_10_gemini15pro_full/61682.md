emissive:/ɪˈmɪsɪv/| adj.|发射的，散发性的|Emitting or having the power to emit something, such as light, heat, or radiation.|The emissive properties of the material allow it to glow in the dark. 这种材料的发射特性使它能在黑暗中发光。|近义词：radiant, luminous, bright|反义词：absorbent, non-emissive|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ēmissīvus*, from ēmissus (past participle of ēmittō “send forth”), from ē- “out” + mittō “send.”
