filminess:/ˈfɪlmɪnɪs/| n. |薄膜状；朦胧；模糊|The filminess of the old photograph gave it a dreamlike quality.  这张老照片的朦胧感赋予它一种梦幻般的品质。|例句：The filminess of her eyes suggested she hadn't slept well. 她眼神的朦胧表明她没睡好。

The filminess of the material made it unsuitable for curtains. 这种材料的薄膜状使其不适合做窗帘。|近义词：thinness, gauziness, transparency, diaphaneity |反义词: thickness, opacity|词性:noun

Etymology: From "filmy" + "-ness".  "Filmy" comes from "film" (referring to a thin layer) + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives). The ultimate origin of "film" is from Old English "fylmen" meaning "membrane".
