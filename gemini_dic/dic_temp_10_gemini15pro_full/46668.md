damped:/dæmpt/ | adj. | 抑制的，减弱的，潮湿的 | reduced in extent or intensity; slightly wet | The damped oscillations gradually died away. 抑制的振荡逐渐消失了。| 近义词: suppressed, muted, softened, moist, humid | 反义词: amplified, enhanced, dry | 词性: 形容词

damped:/dæmpt/ | v. | 抑制，减弱，使潮湿 (damp 的过去式和过去分词) | to reduce in extent or intensity; to make slightly wet |  He damped the fire down with ashes. 他用灰烬把火扑灭了。 | 近义词: suppress, mute, soften, moisten, humidify | 反义词: amplify, enhance, dry | 词性: 动词


词源:  From Middle English *dampnen, from Old North French *dampner, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old Norse dampi (“vapor, exhalation”), from Proto-Germanic *dampaz (“vapor”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰembʰ- (“to smoke, steam”).
