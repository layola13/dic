hyperemotiveness:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈmoʊʃənəsnəs/| n. |过度情绪化；情绪亢奋|The state of being excessively emotional or readily showing emotion.|Her hyperemotiveness made it difficult for her to handle stressful situations. 她过度的情绪化使她难以处理压力大的情况。|Example: The child's hyperemotiveness was evident in his frequent tantrums.  这个孩子情绪亢奋，经常发脾气。

Example:  His hyperemotiveness often led to misunderstandings with his colleagues. 他的过度情绪化经常导致与同事之间的误解。|近义词：hypersensitivity, emotionalism, excitability|反义词：stoicism, apathy, impassivity|词性:noun
