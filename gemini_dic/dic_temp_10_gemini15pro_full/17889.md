例句：We had been working on the project for weeks before it was cancelled. 在项目被取消之前，我们已经为它工作了几个星期。

PHRASE->
(Been is not typically part of set phrases. It's a grammatical element used to form verb tenses.)


注意事项:
As noted above, 'been' is the past participle of 'be' and isn't used independently. It's always used with auxiliary verbs like "have," "has," or "had" to form perfect tenses. It indicates a completed state or action.
