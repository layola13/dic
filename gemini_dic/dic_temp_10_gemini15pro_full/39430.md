connectedly:/kəˈnɛktɪdli/
adverb
连接地，连贯地
in a connected way; coherently.
The chapters of the book are connectedly written, flowing smoothly from one to the next. 这本书的章节写得很有条理，从一章到下一章流畅地衔接。
connectedly: The speaker presented her arguments connectedly, ensuring the audience could follow her reasoning. 演讲者连贯地陈述了她的论点，确保听众能够理解她的推理。
近义词: coherently, consecutively, continuously
反义词: disconnectedly, disjointedly, incoherently
词性: adverb

Etymology:
From connect + -ed + -ly.  "Connect" comes from the Latin "connectere," meaning "to bind together."
