bto:/ˈbiː tiː ˈoʊ/
abbreviation
business-to-opportunity
商机对商机（指企业之间通过合作创造新的商业机会）
BTO partnerships can help companies expand their reach and access new markets. 商机对商机的合作可以帮助公司扩大业务范围并进入新市场。
BTO collaborations often involve sharing resources and expertise. 商机对商机的合作通常涉及共享资源和专业知识。
synonyms:business partnership, strategic alliance
antonyms:N/A (反义词不适用缩写)
