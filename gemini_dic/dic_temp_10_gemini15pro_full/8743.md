antidesertification:/ˌæntiˌdɛzərtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n.|防治荒漠化|The act or process of preventing or reversing desertification.  The practice of trying to stop land from becoming desert. |例句：The government has invested heavily in antidesertification programs. 政府已大力投资于防治荒漠化项目。

例句：Antidesertification efforts are crucial for sustainable development. 防治荒漠化的努力对可持续发展至关重要。|近义词：desert control, land reclamation, combating desertification|反义词: desertification|词性:noun
