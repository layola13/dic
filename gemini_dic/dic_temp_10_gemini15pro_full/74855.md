frequentest:/ˈfriːkwəntɪst/
adjective
最频繁的
superlative adjective of frequent: occurring most often.
例句：This is the frequentest question I get. 这是我最常被问到的问题。
例句：He is the frequentest visitor to the library. 他是图书馆最频繁的访客。
近义词：most frequent, most common, most usual
反义词：rarest, least frequent, least common
词性：adjective
