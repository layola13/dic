cowpeas:/ˈkaʊˌpiz/| n. |豇豆，眉豆|A type of bean grown in warm climates, often used for food or as animal fodder.  They are also known as black-eyed peas when dried.|例句：Cowpeas are a staple food in many parts of Africa. 豇豆是非洲许多地区的主食。

例句：The farmer planted cowpeas to improve the soil.  这位农民种植豇豆以改良土壤。|近义词：black-eyed peas, field peas|反义词: N/A |词性:noun

Etymology: From the words "cow" and "pea". Originally called "cow pease," because they were used as fodder for cows.  Over time, "pease" (plural of "pea") became "peas."
