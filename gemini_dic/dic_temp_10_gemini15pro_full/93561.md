imaging's:/ˈɪmɪdʒɪŋz/
noun
成像技术; 影像;
The process of creating a visual representation of something.
Medical imaging's played a crucial role in diagnosing the disease. 医学成像技术在诊断疾病方面发挥了至关重要的作用。
近义词: imagery, visualization
反义词: None
词性: noun (名词)


Etymology:
From "imaging," which is the present participle of the verb "image" (meaning to create a visual representation), plus the possessive suffix -'s.  "Image" ultimately derives from the Latin "imago" (meaning likeness, picture, statue).
