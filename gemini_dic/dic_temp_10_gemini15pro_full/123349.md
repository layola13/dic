monolith:/ˈmɒnəlɪθ/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnəlɪθ/ (US)| n. |巨石，独块巨石; 庞然大物; 整体，单一庞大的组织（或系统）|A large single upright block of stone, especially one shaped into or serving as a pillar or monument. Also, a large, powerful, and indivisible entity, especially a business or political organization.|例句：The company was a monolith in the industry, dominating all aspects of production and distribution.  这家公司是该行业的巨头，掌控着生产和分销的各个方面。

例句：Ancient monoliths stand as silent witnesses to forgotten civilizations. 古老的巨石静静地矗立着，见证着被遗忘的文明。|近义词：obelisk, pillar, column, megalith, behemoth, giant |反义词:fragment, piece|词性:noun


monoliths:/ˈmɒnəlɪθs/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnəlɪθs/ (US)| n. |巨石（monolith的复数）|Plural form of monolith.|例句：The researchers studied the mysterious monoliths scattered across the plain. 研究人员研究了散落在平原上的神秘巨石。
