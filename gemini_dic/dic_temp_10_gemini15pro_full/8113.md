anti-aircraft:/ˌæntiˈeəˌkræft/ /ˌæntiˈɛərˌkræft/| adj. n.|高射的，防空的；高射炮，防空武器|Designed to destroy enemy aircraft.  Used to describe weapons, guns, missiles, etc., or defense systems designed to shoot down aircraft.|The anti-aircraft guns opened fire. 高射炮开火了。|Synonyms: flak, antiair|Antonyms: ground-based (when referring to weapons)|adjective, noun

anti-aircraft:/ˌæntiˈeəˌkræft/ /ˌæntiˈɛərˌkræft/| n.|高射炮，防空武器|A gun or missile system designed to destroy enemy aircraft.|The anti-aircraft battery was deployed on the hill. 防空炮兵阵地部署在山上。|Synonyms: AA guns, ack-ack guns|Antonyms: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"anti-" (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek "anti-")
"aircraft": a vehicle capable of flight (from "air" + "craft")
