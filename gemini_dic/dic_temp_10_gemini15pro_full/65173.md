ethnolinguist:/ˌeθnoʊˈlɪŋɡwɪst/| n.|民族语言学家|A specialist in ethnolinguistics, which is the study of the relationship between language and culture.|Ethnolinguists often conduct fieldwork in diverse communities. 民族语言学家经常在不同的社区进行实地考察。|Synonyms: anthropological linguist, linguistic anthropologist|Antonyms: N/A|noun
