acupuncturation: /ˌækjʊˈpʌŋktʃəreɪʃən/
noun
针灸疗法
The practice of acupuncture; treatment by acupuncture.  It refers to the entire process and system of acupuncture treatment, encompassing diagnosis, needle insertion, manipulation, and other related therapeutic techniques.
She underwent a series of acupuncturation sessions to alleviate her back pain. 她接受了一系列针灸疗程来缓解背痛。

Acupuncturation has been used for centuries to treat a variety of ailments.  针灸已经被使用了几个世纪来治疗各种疾病。

近义词: acupuncture, acupuncture treatment
反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider) conventional medicine, Western medicine
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin acus "needle" + punctura "pricking, puncture" + -ation (suffix forming nouns of action or process).
