mordor:/ˈmɔːrdɔːr/| n. |魔多|A fictional land in J.R.R. Tolkien's The Lord of the Rings, characterized by its volcanic landscape and association with the Dark Lord Sauron.  It is a place of darkness, evil, and industry devoted to Sauron's war effort.|The armies of Mordor marched across the plains.| 近义词:hell, inferno | 反义词:paradise, heaven |词性:noun
