constructible:/kənˈstrʌktɪbl/| adj.|可构造的，可建造的|Able to be constructed or built; capable of being formed or devised by combining parts or elements.|例句：The building is constructible within six months. 这座建筑可以在六个月内建成。|近义词：buildable, makeable, feasible|反义词：unconstructible, impossible|词性:形容词
constructible:/kənˈstrʌktɪbl/| n.|可构成的东西|Something that can be constructed.|例句：A regular pentagon is a constructible polygon using only a compass and straightedge. 正五边形是一个只用圆规和直尺就能构造的多边形。|近义词：construction|反义词：N/A|词性:名词
