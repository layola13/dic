annat: /ˈænæt/ | n. |年金; 年金收入; 年金人寿保险| An annuity is a fixed sum of money paid to someone each year, typically for the rest of their life.|  She receives an annuity of $10,000 per year. 她每年收到一万美元的年金。| synonym: pension, allowance | antonym: lump sum | noun

annat: /ˈænæt/ | adj. | 年金的 | Relating to an annuity. |  An annat policy provides a guaranteed income stream. 年金保险提供有保障的收入来源。| synonym: annual, yearly | antonym:  N/A (adjective form rarely used) | adjective

Etymology:  From Old French *annate* (Modern French *annate*), from Medieval Latin *annata* "annual payment," from Latin *annus* "year."
