individualizations:/ˌɪndɪˌvɪdʒuəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n. |个性化，个体化|The process of making something suitable for individual needs or characteristics.  The act of individualizing or the state of being individualized.|例句：Individualizations of treatment plans are essential for effective patient care.  治疗方案的个性化对于有效的病人护理至关重要。

例句：The company offers individualizations of its products to meet specific customer requirements.  该公司提供产品的个性化服务，以满足特定客户的需求。|近义词：personalization, customization, tailoring|反义词：standardization, uniformity|词性:noun
