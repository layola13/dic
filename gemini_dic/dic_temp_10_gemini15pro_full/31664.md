chattier:/ˈtʃætiər/ | adj. | 更健谈的，更爱聊天的 | More talkative or inclined to chat. | She's become much chattier since she started her new job. 她自从开始新工作后变得健谈多了。|  He was much chattier than usual last night. 他昨晚比平时健谈得多。 | 近义词：talkative, loquacious, communicative | 反义词：quiet, reserved, taciturn | 词性：形容词

chattier:/ˈtʃætiər/ | adj. | 更爱聊天的 | More inclined to chat. |  My neighbor's parrot is getting chattier every day.  我邻居的鹦鹉每天都越来越爱说话了。 | The children were chattier after having some candy. 孩子们吃了糖之后更爱说话了。 | 近义词：talkative, gabby, gossipy | 反义词：reticent, uncommunicative, silent | 词性：形容词

词源：chatty + -er (比较级后缀).  From chat (闲聊) + -y (形容词后缀) + -er (比较级后缀). Ultimately from the verb chatter (喋喋不休).
