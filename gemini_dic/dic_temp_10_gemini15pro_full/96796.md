inerrability:/ˌɪnɛrəˈbɪləti/
n.
无错误性；绝对正确
The quality or state of being inerrant; incapable of being wrong.
The inerrancy of the Bible is a central tenet of some Christian denominations. 圣经的无错误性是一些基督教派的核心信条。
近义词：infallibility, impeccability
反义词：fallibility, errancy
词性：名词

词源：Late 19th century: from in- ‘not’ + errant + -bility.
