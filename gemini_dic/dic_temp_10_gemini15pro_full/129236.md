noctivagant:/ˌnɒktɪˈveɪɡənt/| adj.|夜间活动的，夜游的|Roaming or traveling about at night.  Often used to describe animals, but can also describe people.|The noctivagant owl hunted for mice in the dark.  夜间活动的猫头鹰在黑暗中捕猎老鼠。|Synonyms: nocturnal, night-walking, night-prowling|Antonyms: diurnal, day-active|Adjective
