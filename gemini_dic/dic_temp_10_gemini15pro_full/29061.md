carunculated: /kəˈrʌŋkjʊleɪtɪd/
adjective
肉瘤状的；长着肉瘤的
Covered in small, fleshy protuberances.
The turkey's wattle was carunculated. 火鸡的肉垂长满了肉瘤。
caruncular, fleshy, bumpy, lumpy, nodular, warty
smooth, even
