halicarnassus:/ˌhælɪkɑːrˈnæsəs/
proper noun
哈利卡纳苏斯
An ancient Greek city in southwest Asia Minor (modern Bodrum, Turkey).  It was famous for the Mausoleum at Halicarnassus, one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World.
Herodotus, the “Father of History,” was born in Halicarnassus.  哈利卡纳苏斯是“历史之父”希罗多德的诞生地。
Synonym: Bodrum (modern name)

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek Ἁλικαρνασσός (Halikarnassós).
