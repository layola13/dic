coccolith:/ˈkɒkəlɪθ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːkəlɪθ/ (US)| n. |颗石|A minute calcareous plate, one of many which form the covering of a coccolithophore (a type of single-celled alga).|Coccoliths are so abundant that they form chalk deposits.|近义词:scale (in a biological context)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun

coccoliths:/ˈkɒkəlɪθs/ (UK) /ˈkɑːkəlɪθs/ (US)| n. |颗石(复数)|Plural of coccolith.|Coccoliths accumulate on the seafloor, eventually forming chalk.|近义词:scales (in a biological context)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
