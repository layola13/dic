anamorphosis:/ˌænəˈmɔːrfəsɪs/
n.
变形，失真，歪像
a distorted projection or drawing that appears normal only when viewed from a particular point or with a special instrument or mirror; a distorted image that appears in its true form when viewed in some unconventional way.
The artist created an anamorphosis of a skull on the pavement.  这位艺术家在人行道上创作了一个头骨的变形画。

distortion, deformation, warping
clarity, accuracy, normality
noun
