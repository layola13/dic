antitechnologists:/ˌæntiˌtɛkˈnɒlədʒɪsts/
n.
反对技术者；技术反对者
People who oppose technological development or its impact on society and the environment.
Antitechnologists argue that technological advancements often lead to social and environmental problems. 反对技术者认为，技术进步常常导致社会和环境问题。
The antitechnologists protested the construction of the new power plant. 技术反对者抗议建造新的发电厂。
Synonyms: Luddites, neo-Luddites, technophobes
Antonyms: technologists, technophiles, tech enthusiasts
noun



ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- (prefix meaning "against") + technology + -ist (suffix forming agent nouns).  The term "Luddite" is often used historically and sometimes as a synonym, referring to 19th-century English textile workers who destroyed machinery.
