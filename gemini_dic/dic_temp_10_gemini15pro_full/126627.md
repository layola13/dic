nailery:/ˈneɪləri/
n.
指甲美容店；制钉厂
A place where nails are made or sold, or a place where manicures and pedicures are done.
She got her nails done at the new nailery downtown. 她去市中心新开的指甲美容店做了指甲。
The old nailery closed down after the factory moved. 工厂搬迁后，那家老制钉厂倒闭了。
synonyms: nail salon, nail bar, manicure shop (for beauty services); nail factory (for manufacturing)
antonyms: None (no direct opposite for this specific meaning).
