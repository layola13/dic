inadequacies:/ɪnˈædɪkwəsiːz/| n. |不足，缺点，缺陷|The report highlighted the inadequacies of the current system.  报告强调了现有系统的不足之处。|例句：The inadequacies of the healthcare system were apparent during the pandemic.  疫情期间，医疗体系的不足之处显而易见。

inadequacy:/ɪnˈædɪkwəsi/| n. |不足，缺点，缺陷|The inadequacy of the funding hampered the project. 资金不足阻碍了该项目。|例句：She felt a deep sense of inadequacy in her new role.  她在新职位上感到深深的不足感。|近义词：shortcomings, deficiencies, flaws, weaknesses, limitations|反义词：strengths, advantages, sufficiency, adequacy|词性:名词

Etymology:  Late 16th century: from French inadéquat or Latin inadequatus, past participle of inaequare ‘make unequal,’ from in- ‘not’ + aequare ‘make equal’ (from aequus ‘equal’).
