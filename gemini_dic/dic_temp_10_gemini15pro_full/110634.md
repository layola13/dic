limpkin:/ˈlɪmpkɪn/ | n. | 秧鹤; 跛行鹤 | A large wading bird native to the southeastern United States, the Caribbean, and parts of South America, known for its distinctive limping gait and piercing cries. |  We saw a limpkin foraging for snails in the swamp. 我们看到一只秧鹤在沼泽地里觅食蜗牛。 |  The limpkin's call echoed through the mangroves. 秧鹤的叫声在红树林中回荡。 | 近义词: snail kite, crying bird | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From earlier *limpkin, perhaps from limp + -kin, due to its unusual gait.
