aspartame:/ˈæspərteɪm, əˈspɑːrteɪm/ | n. | 阿斯巴甜 | An artificial non-saccharide sweetener about 200 times as sweet as sucrose, and is commonly used as a sugar substitute in foods and beverages. |  This yogurt is sweetened with aspartame.  这种酸奶是用阿斯巴甜增甜的。 | synonyms: NutraSweet, Equal | antonyms: sugar, sucrose | noun
