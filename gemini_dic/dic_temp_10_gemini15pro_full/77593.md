geek:/ɡik/| n.|怪才，奇才；狂热爱好者；反常的人|A person who is very interested in and who knows a lot about a particular subject, especially computers or technology.  A person who is socially awkward and unpopular : a usually intelligent person who does not fit in with other people.| He's such a computer geek! 他真是个电脑高手！|近义词：nerd, dweeb, enthusiast|反义词:conformist, normal person|noun
