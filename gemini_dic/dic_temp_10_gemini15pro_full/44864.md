cruth: /kruːθ/ /krʌθ/ | n. | 克鲁斯琴（一种古代凯尔特弦乐器）| A cruth is an ancient Celtic stringed musical instrument.| He played a beautiful melody on the cruth. 他用克鲁斯琴演奏了一曲优美的旋律。|Near synonyms: lyre, harp | Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a musical instrument) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Irish *crot, from Proto-Celtic *kruttos.  Related to Welsh crwth and Breton kroud. Ultimately from a Proto-Indo-European root meaning "curved," perhaps referring to the instrument's shape.
