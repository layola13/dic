| Example: The artist's work was seen as nonsubversive and therefore acceptable to the conservative gallery.  这位艺术家的作品被认为是非颠覆性的，因此为保守的画廊所接受。

| Synonyms: harmless, innocuous, inoffensive, safe, compliant | Antonyms: subversive, revolutionary, rebellious, seditious, disruptive | Word type: adjective
