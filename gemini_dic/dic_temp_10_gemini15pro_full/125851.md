musard:/ˈmʌsərd/ | n. | 闲逛；游荡；懒散的人 | A leisurely stroll or promenade; a person who strolls. | He enjoyed a musard through the park on a sunny afternoon. 他喜欢在阳光明媚的下午在公园里闲逛。 | 近义词: stroll, saunter, ramble, loiterer | 反义词: rush, hurry, hasten | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *musarder*, from *muser* "to muse, dream," of uncertain origin.
