calcarine:/kælˈkɛərɪn/ | adj. | 距状的；禽距的；跟骨的 | Relating to the calcar avis, a spur-like elevation on the medial surface of the occipital lobe of the brain.  It is specifically related to the calcarine sulcus/fissure, which contains the primary visual cortex. | The calcarine sulcus is a prominent landmark on the medial surface of the brain. 距状沟是大脑内侧表面上的一个显著标志。 | The patient experienced visual disturbances due to a lesion in the calcarine cortex. 由于距状皮层的病变，病人出现了视觉障碍。 |  Synonym:  occipital (related to the occipital lobe), visual (related to vision) | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, as it's an anatomical term) | adjective
