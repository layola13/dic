manifestoing:/ˈmænɪˌfɛstoʊɪŋ/
v.
展现，显现，表明；使(某事)公开
The illness first manifested itself in severe headaches. 这种疾病最初表现为严重的头痛。
She manifested little enthusiasm for the project. 她对这个项目几乎没有表现出热情。
Synonyms: demonstrating, exhibiting, revealing, displaying, showing, expressing
Antonyms: concealing, hiding, suppressing
verb
