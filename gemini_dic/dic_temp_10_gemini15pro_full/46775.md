dangerousness:/ˈdeɪndʒərəsnəs/
n.|危险性，危险程度|The dangerousness of the situation was apparent to everyone.  局势的危险性对每个人来说都是显而易见的。|The assessment of dangerousness is crucial in deciding whether to release a prisoner. 评估危险性对于决定是否释放囚犯至关重要。|近义词: peril, hazard, risk, jeopardy|反义词: safety, security|词性:名词

Etymology: From "dangerous" + "-ness."  "Dangerous" comes from Old French "dangereus," from "danger."  Ultimately from Latin "dominus" meaning "lord, master," reflecting the power to inflict harm.
