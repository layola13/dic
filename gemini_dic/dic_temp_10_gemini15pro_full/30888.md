chaffinches:/ˈtʃæfɪntʃɪz/| n. |燕雀（复数）|A common European finch (Fringilla coelebs) with a cheerful song, the male having a reddish breast and cheeks.|例句：A flock of chaffinches flitted among the branches. 一群燕雀在树枝间飞来飞去。

例句：The chaffinches were singing merrily in the garden. 燕雀在花园里欢快地歌唱。

近义词：finch, bird|反义词:|词性:noun
