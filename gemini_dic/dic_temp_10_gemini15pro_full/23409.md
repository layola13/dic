bracingly:/ˈbreɪsɪŋli/ | adv. | 令人振奋地，清新地 | In a way that makes you feel refreshed and invigorated. | The cold wind blew bracingly across the moor. 冷风令人振奋地吹过荒野。| 近义词: refreshingly, invigoratingly, stimulatingly | 反义词: depressingly, tiringly | 词性: 副词
词源: From brace (v.) + -ing + -ly.  "Brace" originates from Old French "bracier" meaning "to embrace, hold fast."  Over time, the meaning evolved to include strengthening or supporting, and eventually to invigorate.
