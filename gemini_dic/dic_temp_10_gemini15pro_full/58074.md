dronte:/ˈdrɒntiː/ (UK), /ˈdrɑːntiː/ (US)| n. |渡渡鸟|An extinct flightless bird, Raphus cucullatus, that was endemic to the island of Mauritius. It was larger than a turkey, with blue-grey plumage, a large head, and a heavy hooked beak. It became extinct in the late 17th century.|The dodo, or dronte, was a symbol of extinction. 渡渡鸟，或者说dronte，是灭绝的象征。|Synonyms: dodo, Raphus cucullatus|Antonyms: N/A (extinct species have no antonyms)|Noun
