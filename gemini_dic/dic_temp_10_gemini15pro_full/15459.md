badderlocks:/ˈbædərˌlɒks/ (UK) /ˈbædərˌlɑːks/ (US)| n. | 海带, 昆布 (一种褐藻)|A kind of edible seaweed (brown algae), also known as dabberlocks or sweet kelp. It grows in the lower intertidal and upper sublittoral zones.|例句：The badderlocks were swaying gently in the tide.  昆布在潮水中轻轻摇曳。

例句：She added some badderlocks to the soup for flavor. 她往汤里加了些昆布提味。|近义词：kelp, seaweed, dabberlocks|反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑land plants 陆生植物作为广义上的反义词)|词性:名词
