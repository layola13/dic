dreckish:/ˈdrɛkɪʃ/| adj.|污秽的，垃圾的，劣质的|Something that is dreckish is of poor quality, worthless, or trashy.  It often carries a connotation of being distasteful or offensive.|The movie was a dreckish mess of clichés and bad acting. 这部电影是一堆陈词滥调和糟糕表演的垃圾。|Synonyms: trashy, junky, cheesy, tawdry, sleazy|Antonyms: high-quality, excellent, superb, first-rate|Adjective

dreckish:/ˈdrɛkɪʃ/| adj.|无聊的，沉闷的|Something that is dreckish can also be tedious, boring, or uninteresting.|The lecture was so dreckish that I almost fell asleep.  这讲座太无聊了，我差点睡着了。|Synonyms: tedious, boring, dull, uninteresting, monotonous|Antonyms: exciting, interesting, stimulating, engaging, captivating|Adjective
