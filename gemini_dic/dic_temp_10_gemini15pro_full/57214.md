downchange:/ˈdaʊnˌtʃeɪndʒ/| n.|降级，调低；缩减规模|A downchange is a change to a less demanding or less important job or way of life, often so that you have less stress or more time to spend with your family.|Example:  They made a conscious decision to downchange and move to the countryside. 他们有意识地决定过更简单的生活，搬到了乡下。

Example: The economic downturn forced many families to downchange their lifestyle. 经济衰退迫使许多家庭降低了生活水平。|近义词：downshift, scale back, simplify|反义词: upgrade, upscale|词性:名词
