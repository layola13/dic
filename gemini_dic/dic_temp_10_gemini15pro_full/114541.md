malapropistic:/ˌmæləˈprɒptɪstɪk/| adj.|滑稽的，用词错误的|Of or relating to, or characterized by malapropism; ludicrously inappropriate.  Characterized by the misuse of words, especially through confusion with similar-sounding words.|Her malapropistic use of "obtuse" instead of "acute" made everyone laugh. 她把“acute”（尖锐的）误用成“obtuse”（迟钝的），让大家都笑了。|Near synonyms:  inappropriate, incorrect, misused,  ludicrous, comical|Antonyms: accurate, appropriate, correct, proper|Adjective
