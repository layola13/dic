noncreditor:/nɒnˈkrɛdɪtər/ | n. | 非债权人 | A person or entity that is not a creditor; someone who has not lent money or extended credit to another party.  |  The noncreditors were not involved in the bankruptcy proceedings. 非债权人没有参与破产程序。|  Synonym: nonlender | Antonym: creditor | noun

Etymology: non- + creditor. "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "creditor" comes from the Latin "creditor," meaning "one who believes" or "one who trusts," from "credere," meaning "to believe" or "to trust."
