interchanges:/ˈɪntərˌtʃeɪndʒɪz/
n.
交流；交换；互换；立交桥
An act of giving and receiving something in return; the substitution of one thing for another.  A junction of two or more highways by a system of separate levels that permit traffic to pass from one to another without crossing the path of any other traffic stream.
The two governments agreed on an interchange of prisoners.  两国政府同意交换战俘。
The new highway interchange is finally open. 新的公路立交桥终于通车了。
近义词:exchange, swap, trade, intersection
反义词:retention, keeping

interchange:/ˌɪntərˈtʃeɪndʒ/
v.
交换；互换；交替发生
Put or take something in place of something else; to occur in alternate order.
We interchanged gifts at Christmas. 我们在圣诞节交换了礼物。
Sunny and rainy days interchanged throughout April.  整个四月晴天和雨天交替出现。
近义词:exchange, swap, alternate
反义词:retain, keep
