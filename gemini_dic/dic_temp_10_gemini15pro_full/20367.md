bister:/ˈbɪstər/| adj. n.|黄褐色的；暗棕色的； bistre颜料；|A bister landscape painting. 一幅黄褐色的风景画。|近义词：brown, sepia, umber|反义词：bright, vibrant|词性:形容词, 名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French bistre, from the town of Bistre (now part of  Montignac, Dordogne) in France, where the pigment was originally made.
