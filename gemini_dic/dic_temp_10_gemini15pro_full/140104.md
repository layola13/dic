off-loaded:/ˌɒfˈləʊdɪd/ /ˌɔːfˈloʊdəd/ | v. | 卸下；转移；摆脱 | To remove a load from something, especially a vehicle or ship; to transfer work or responsibilities to someone else. | The cargo was off-loaded at the port. 货物在港口卸下。 |  The company off-loaded some of its debts onto its subsidiaries. 公司将其部分债务转移到其子公司。| unload, discharge, transfer | load, burden | verb

off-loaded:/ˌɒfˈləʊdɪd/ /ˌɔːfˈloʊdəd/ | adj. | 卸下的；转移的 |  Having had a load removed; having transferred work or responsibilities. |  The off-loaded cargo was inspected by customs officials. 被卸下的货物由海关官员检查。 | The team felt relieved after off-loading some of their responsibilities. 卸下一些责任后，团队感到轻松。 | unloaded, unburdened | loaded, burdened | adjective
