garboard:/ˈɡɑːrbɔːrd/
noun
龙骨翼板，底板
The first plank of a ship's hull fastened to the keel.
The garboard plank was carefully fitted to the keel. 龙骨翼板被小心地安装到龙骨上。
The garboard strake needs replacing due to rot.  由于腐烂，底板需要更换。
synonyms: keelson, bottom plank
antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps upper strake/deck)
