brumaire:/bruːˈmɛər/| n. |雾月| Brumaire is the second month of the French Republican Calendar, roughly corresponding to October/November.  It is most famously known for the Coup of 18 Brumaire, when Napoleon seized power. | The Coup of 18 Brumaire marked a turning point in French history.  雾月十八日政变标志着法国历史的转折点。| synonym:  no direct English synonym, perhaps "autumn month" | antonym: no direct antonym | noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From French *brumaire*, from *brume* ("mist, fog"), ultimately from Gaulish *bruma* ("winter solstice").
