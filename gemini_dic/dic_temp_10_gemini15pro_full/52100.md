diametrical:/ˌdaɪəˈmetrɪkl/| adj.|完全相反的，截然对立的|Relating to or along a diameter; directly opposite.|The two candidates hold diametrical opposing views on the issue.  两位候选人对这个问题持截然相反的观点。|近义词：opposite, contrary, antithetical|反义词：similar, alike, identical|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin *diametralis*, from Latin *diametros* (“diameter”) + *-alis*. Equivalent to *diameter* + *-al*.
