cottise:/ˈkɒtɪs/ /ˈkɑːtɪs/| n. |小带，窄条；（纹章学）位于盾牌边缘的窄条|A cottise is a narrow strip, one third the width of the bendlet, next to the edge of a shield in heraldry.  纹章学中小带是指位于盾牌边缘、宽度为斜条三分之一的窄条。|The shield featured three gold cottises on a blue background.  盾牌的蓝色背景上有三条金色的小带。|近义词：barrulet, ribbon, stripe|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "field" referring to the main area of the shield)|词性:noun
