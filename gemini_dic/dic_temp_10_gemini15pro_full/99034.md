interalliances:/ˌɪntərəˈlaɪənsɪz/ | n. |联盟之间的; 各联盟间的|A system of alliances between different alliances.  It signifies a complex network of agreements and partnerships, often involving multiple nations or groups.| The intricate web of interalliances during the Cold War kept a fragile peace.| 近义词: coalitions, partnerships, leagues | 反义词: rivalries, hostilities, conflicts | 词性: 名词

Etymology:

* inter- (prefix): between, among (from Latin)
* alliance (noun): a union or association formed for mutual benefit, especially between countries or organizations (from Old French *aliance*, from *alier* "to ally," from Latin *alligare* "to bind to, unite," from *ad* "to" + *ligare* "to tie")
