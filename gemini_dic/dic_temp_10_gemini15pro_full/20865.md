blatteration:/ˌblætəˈreɪʃən/
noun
喋喋不休;废话;空话
Loud, empty talk or writing.
The politician's speech was full of blatteration and lacked any substance. 这位政客的演讲充满了空话，缺乏实质内容。
The endless blatteration from the television was driving me crazy. 电视里无休止的废话让我快疯了。
近义词:babble, blather, chatter, prattle, gibberish, nonsense
反义词:sense, conciseness, brevity
词性:n.

Etymology:
From Middle English *blateren*, from Old French *blaterer*, from *blater* (“to bleat”), from Late Latin *blattare*, from Latin *blatire*.  Ultimately of imitative origin.
