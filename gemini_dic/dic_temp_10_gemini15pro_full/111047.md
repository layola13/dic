(Liss is rarely used in phrases.  Its poetic nature makes it stand alone.  However, constructions like these are possible.)

注意事项:

Although "liss" can be used as a verb, its primary usage is as a noun, especially in modern English.  It is primarily found in poetry or literary contexts.  Using it in everyday conversation might sound archaic or overly formal.
