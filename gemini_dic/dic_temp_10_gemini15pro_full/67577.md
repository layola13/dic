extrapunitive:/ˌekstrəˈpjuːnɪtɪv/
adjective
外罚型，外向攻击型
Directing blame or aggression outward.  Focusing on punishing others for one's misfortunes rather than oneself.
After failing the exam, John became extrapunitive, blaming the teacher for setting unfair questions. 考试不及格后，约翰变得外罚型，责怪老师出题不公平。
He displayed extrapunitive behavior by yelling at his co-workers after his project failed. 项目失败后，他对同事大吼大叫，表现出外罚型行为。
synonyms: aggressive, outward-directed, other-blaming
antonyms: intropunitive, self-blaming
