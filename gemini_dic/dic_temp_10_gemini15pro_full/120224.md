minesweeper:/ˈmaɪnˌswiːpər/ | n. |扫雷舰；扫雷兵；扫雷器（游戏）| A ship or device used to remove mines from the sea or other body of water. Also refers to a popular computer game. |  The minesweeper carefully navigated the treacherous waters. 扫雷舰小心翼翼地在危险水域航行。| synonyms: minehunter, minesweeping vessel | antonyms: minelayer | noun

minesweepers:/ˈmaɪnˌswiːpərz/ | n. |扫雷舰；扫雷兵；扫雷器（游戏）（复数）| Plural form of "minesweeper." | Two minesweepers were deployed to clear the shipping lane.  两艘扫雷舰被部署去清理航道。| synonyms: minehunters, minesweeping vessels | antonyms: minelayers | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "mine" (explosive device) + "sweeper" (one who sweeps).  The term gained popularity with the advent of naval warfare and the need to clear mines.  The computer game "Minesweeper" is named after this activity.
