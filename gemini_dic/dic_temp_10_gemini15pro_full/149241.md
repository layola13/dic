oxytone: /ˈɒksɪˌtəʊn/ (UK) /ˈɑːksɪˌtoʊn/ (US)| n. |扬音词，重音在最后一个音节上的词|A word having the main accent on the final syllable. |Example: The word "machine" is an oxytone.  机器这个单词是一个扬音词。

Example:  The word "balloon" is an oxytone.  气球这个单词是一个扬音词。|近义词:  acrotonic |反义词: prooxytone, paroxytone |词性: noun
