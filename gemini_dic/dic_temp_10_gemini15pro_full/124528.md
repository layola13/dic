mountainer:/ˈmaʊntɪnɪr/ (US), /ˈmaʊntɪnə(r)/ (UK)| n. |登山者；山地居民|A person who climbs mountains, especially as a sport or profession; someone who lives in a mountainous region.|例句：The experienced mountaineer reached the summit after days of climbing. 这位经验丰富的登山者在攀登数日后到达了顶峰。|近义词：climber, alpinist, hillwalker|反义词:lowlander, plainsman|noun
