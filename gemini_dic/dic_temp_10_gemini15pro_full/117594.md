megalopsychy:/ˌmɛɡəloʊˈsaɪki/ | n. | 妄想自大，夸大狂 | A mental disorder characterized by delusions of grandeur.  A state of mind characterized by inflated self-importance or an exaggerated sense of power or importance. |  He suffered from megalopsychy, believing he was destined to rule the world. 他患有夸大妄想症，认为自己注定要统治世界。| Example: His megalopsychy led him to believe he was a god. 他的夸大妄想让他觉得自己是神。 | Synonyms: grandiosity, egomania, narcissism | Antonyms: humility, modesty | noun
