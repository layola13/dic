give-back:/ˈɡɪvˌbæk/ | n. | 回馈; 回报; 捐赠 | something that is returned to a group of people after it has first been taken away from them | The company's give-back to the community includes scholarships for local students.  这家公司对社区的回馈包括为当地学生提供奖学金。| synonyms: contribution, donation, restitution, return | antonyms:  taking, withholding, confiscation | noun

give-back:/ˈɡɪvˌbæk/ | adj. | 回馈的; 赠予的 | relating to or involving the act of giving back something | The company organized a give-back event where they donated a portion of their profits to charity. 公司组织了一次回馈活动，将部分利润捐赠给慈善机构。 | synonyms: charitable, philanthropic, reciprocal | antonyms: selfish, greedy, stingy | adjective
