attempters:/əˈtɛmptərz/
n.
尝试者；试图做某事的人
People who try to do something, especially something difficult.
Many attempters failed to climb the mountain. 许多尝试攀登这座山的人都失败了。
近义词:triers, aspirants
反义词:non-participants, abstainers
词性:名词

Etymology: From the verb "attempt," which comes from the Old French "attempter" and ultimately from the Latin "attentare," meaning "to try."  The "-er" suffix denotes a person who performs the action of the verb.
