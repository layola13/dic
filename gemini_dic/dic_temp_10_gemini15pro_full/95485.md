inclamation:/ˌɪnkləˈmeɪʃən/| n.|感叹; 感叹语|A sudden, sharp cry or remark, especially expressing surprise, anger, or pain. |例句：He gave an exclamation of surprise. 他惊呼了一声。|例句：Her exclamation of joy filled the room. 她喜悦的欢呼声充满了整个房间。|近义词：cry, shout, yell, shriek, ejaculation|反义词:whisper, murmur|noun
