anti-ecclesiastic:/ˌæntiɪˌkliːziˈæstɪk/
adjective
反教会的，反对教会的
Opposed to the church or its power and influence.
The writer's anti-ecclesiastic views were evident in his latest novel.  这位作家的反教会观点在他的最新小说中很明显。
His anti-ecclesiastic stance made him unpopular with the religious community. 他的反教会立场使他在宗教界不受欢迎。

近义词:anticlerical, irreligious, secular
反义词:religious, devout, pious
词性:adjective
