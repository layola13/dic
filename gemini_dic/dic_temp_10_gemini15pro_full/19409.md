biceps: /ˈbaɪseps/ | n. | 二头肌 |  A muscle having two points of attachment at one end.  The large muscle in the front of the upper arm. | He flexed his biceps to show off his strength. 他收缩二头肌以炫耀自己的力量。|  She tore a biceps during the weightlifting competition.  她在举重比赛中拉伤了二头肌。| synonyms: muscle, flexor | antonyms:  None (Biceps is a specific muscle; it doesn't have a direct opposite.) | noun
