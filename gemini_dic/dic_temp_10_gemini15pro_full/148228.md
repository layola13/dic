oversimpleness:/ˌoʊvərˈsɪmplɪsnəs/ | n. | 过度简化，过于简单 |  The oversimpleness of the explanation made it difficult for students to grasp the complex concept.  这个解释过于简单，让学生难以理解这个复杂的概念。 | Example: The oversimpleness of the model failed to account for all the variables.  这个模型过于简化，未能涵盖所有变量。| Synonyms: simplification, reductionism | Antonyms: complexity, sophistication | noun

oversimple:/ˌoʊvərˈsɪmpl/ | adj. | 过于简单的，过于简化的 | The explanation was oversimple and misleading. 这个解释过于简单，而且具有误导性。| Example:  His oversimple approach to the problem ignored crucial details. 他对这个问题过于简单的方法忽略了关键的细节。 | Synonyms: simplistic, naive, unsophisticated | Antonyms: complex, sophisticated, nuanced | adjective
