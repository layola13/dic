cageyness:/ˈkeɪdʒɪnɪs/
noun
谨慎, 拘谨, 保守
The quality of being cagey; cautiousness and reticence.
He answered questions with his characteristic cageyness. 他以其特有的谨慎回答了问题。
近义词: reticence, reserve, guardedness, secretiveness, discretion
反义词: openness, candor, frankness
词性: n.

Etymology:
From cagey + -ness.  Cagey itself is of uncertain origin, possibly related to Scots cadgie "wary, cautious," perhaps ultimately from Romani.
