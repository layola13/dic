elated:/ɪˈleɪtɪd/| adj.|兴高采烈的，欣喜若狂的|Feeling or expressing great happiness or triumph.|She was elated at the news of her promotion. 她听到升职的消息欣喜若狂。|近义词：joyful, thrilled, ecstatic, jubilant|反义词：depressed, downcast, despondent|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *elatus* past participle of *efferre* "to carry out, bring forth," from *ex- "out" + *ferre* "to carry." The sense development is from "lifted up" to "spiritually uplifted" to "joyful."
