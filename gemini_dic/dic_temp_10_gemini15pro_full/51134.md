despoil:/dɪˈspɔɪl/
verb
掠夺，抢劫
To deprive of something valuable by force; to plunder or pillage.
The invaders despoiled the city of its treasures.  侵略者掠夺了这座城市的宝藏。
The barbarians despoiled the village, leaving it in ruins. 野蛮人洗劫了村庄，把它夷为废墟。

synonyms: plunder, pillage, loot, rob, ransack, strip, ravage, devastate
antonyms: protect, preserve, conserve, restore, enrich

词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *despoillier, from Latin despoliare "to plunder, rob," from de- "from, away" + spoliare "to strip, plunder."
