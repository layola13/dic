dolmens:/ˈdɒl.mɛnz/ /ˈdoʊl.mɛnz/
n.
巨石墓，支石墓
A megalithic tomb with a large flat stone laid on upright ones.
例句：The dolmens of Ireland are ancient and mysterious. 爱尔兰的巨石墓古老而神秘。

例句：Archaeologists are studying the dolmens to learn more about the people who built them. 考古学家正在研究巨石墓，以了解更多关于建造它们的人们的信息。

近义词：megaliths, cromlechs
反义词: (No direct antonym exists for a physical object like a dolmen.)
词性: noun
