devotedly:/dɪˈvoʊtɪdli/
adverb
虔诚地；忠实地；一心一意地
in a devoted manner; lovingly and loyally
She devotedly cared for her sick mother. 她一心一意地照顾她生病的母亲。
Synonyms: faithfully, loyally, religiously, earnestly, wholeheartedly
Antonyms: halfheartedly, indifferently, apathetically, negligently
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "devoted" + "-ly."  "Devoted" comes from the past participle of the verb "devote," from Late Middle English, from Latin *dēvōtāre* "to vow, dedicate," from *dē-* "down, away" + *vōtāre* "to vow, promise solemnly."  The suffix "-ly" forms adverbs from adjectives.
