betroth:/bɪˈtrəʊθ/ (UK) /bɪˈtroʊθ/ (US)| vt.| 许配，和…订婚|To promise or engage (a person) to be married.|He betrothed his daughter to a wealthy merchant. 他把女儿许配给了一个富商。|例句：
The king betrothed his daughter to the prince. 国王把他的女儿许配给了王子。

She was betrothed to a man she had never met. 她被许配给一个她从未见过面的男人。


|近义词：engage, affiance, promise in marriage|反义词: jilt, break off an engagement|词性:动词
