fireguard:/ˈfaɪəɡɑːrd/| n.|防火罩，火炉围栏|A metal or other protective guard placed in front of a fireplace to prevent sparks or embers from flying out and causing a fire.|例句：We need a fireguard to protect the children from the open fire. 我们需要一个防火罩来保护孩子们不被明火烫伤。

例句：The fireguard was old and rusty, so we replaced it with a new one. 防火罩又旧又生锈，所以我们换了个新的。|近义词：firescreen, spark guard|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "fire starter" in a figurative sense)|词性:noun
