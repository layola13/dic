outquoted:/ˌaʊtˈkwoʊtɪd/ | adj. | 被引用次数超过的 | Surpassed in the number or quality of quotations; having had more or better quotations used against one in an argument or discussion. | He felt outquoted in the debate, unable to match his opponent's rapid-fire citations. 他在辩论中感觉自己被引用次数超过了，无法与对手快速的引用相匹敌。| synonyms: outmatched, surpassed, bested | antonyms: unsurpassed, unmatched, superior | adjective
