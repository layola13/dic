deadweight:/ˈdɛdˌweɪt/| n.|自重，净重；累赘，负担|A deadweight is the weight of an object itself, excluding the weight of its container or supporting structure. It can also refer to a person or thing that is useless and burdensome.|The truck's deadweight was 10 tons. 这辆卡车的自重是10吨。|  The project was becoming a deadweight on the company's resources.  这个项目正在成为公司资源的负担。|Synonyms: burden, drag, load, encumbrance|Antonyms: asset, advantage, benefit|noun

deadweight:/ˈdɛdˌweɪt/| adj.|无用的，累赘的|Something that is deadweight is useless and burdensome.|The team was carrying a lot of deadweight. 团队背负着很多无用的东西。|He felt like a deadweight to his family. 他觉得自己是家里的累赘。|Synonyms: burdensome, cumbersome, useless, unproductive|Antonyms: helpful, useful, productive, beneficial|adjective
