dreadfully:/ˈdredfəli/ | adv. |非常地；可怕地；极其地| Extremely; terribly; in a way that inspires dread. | The play was dreadfully boring.  这齣戏无聊得可怕。|  The house was dreadfully cold. 房子冷得要命。 | Synonyms: terribly, awfully, horribly, frightfully, extremely, exceedingly | Antonyms: wonderfully, delightfully, pleasantly | Adverb

Etymology: From *dreadful* + *-ly*. *Dreadful* comes from Old English *drēadlic*, meaning "inspiring dread."
