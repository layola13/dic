graphited:/ˈɡræfaɪtɪd/

graphited:/ˈɡræfaɪtɪd/
| v. | 用石墨涂抹或处理 (graphited, graphiting) | To coat or treat with graphite. | They graphited the mold to prevent sticking. 他们用石墨涂抹模具以防粘连。 | Synonyms: coat with graphite, treat with graphite | Antonyms: remove graphite | Word type: Verb (past participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From graphite + -ed.  "Graphite" itself comes from the Greek "graphein" meaning "to write," due to its use in pencils.
