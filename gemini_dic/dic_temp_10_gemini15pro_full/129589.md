non-commissioned:/ˌnɒn kəˈmɪʃənd/ | adj. | 非委任的；未经委任的 | Not holding a commission; not appointed by a commission.  A non-commissioned officer is an enlisted person of a rank below a commissioned officer. |  The non-commissioned officer led the training exercise.  这位未委任的军官领导了训练演习。 | The non-commissioned ranks include sergeants and corporals.  未委任军衔包括中士和下士。 | Synonyms: enlisted, nonofficer | Antonyms: commissioned, officer | adjective
