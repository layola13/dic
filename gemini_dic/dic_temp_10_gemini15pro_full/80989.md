gradatory:/ˈɡrædəˌtɔːri/ /ˈɡrædəˌtəri/
adjective
分级的，渐进的，逐步的
Proceeding by steps or degrees; gradual.
The ceremony featured a gradatory procession of dignitaries.  典礼的特色是政要们依次序的游行。
The drug's effect was gradatory, slowly building over time.  这种药物的作用是渐进的，随着时间的推移慢慢增强。
近义词: gradual, progressive, step-by-step, incremental
反义词: abrupt, sudden, immediate
词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *gradatorius, from gradus ("step, degree").
