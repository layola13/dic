outpraising: /ˌaʊtˈpreɪzɪŋ/
verb
过分赞扬; 比...更赞扬
praising someone or something more than another
He was outpraising his rival's product. 他对竞争对手的产品赞不绝口，甚至超过了自家的产品。

The marketing campaign was outpraising the actual benefits of the product.  营销活动对该产品实际益处的赞扬过高了。

Synonym: overpraising, exaggerating the praise of
Antonym: underpraising, downplaying
词性: 动词 (verb)
