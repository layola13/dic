chains:/tʃeɪnz/| n.| 链条，锁链；一系列；束缚；|A series of connected links or rings, typically made of metal, used for various purposes such as restraint, support, or transmission of power.  A sequence of connected things or events.|The dog was held on a chain. 狗被链子拴着。|synonyms: fetters, shackles, bonds, series, sequence|antonyms: freedom, liberty|noun

chains:/tʃeɪnz/| v. (chains, chaining, chained)|用链条拴住；束缚；|To fasten or secure with a chain.|They chained the bicycle to the railing.  他们把自行车锁在栏杆上。|synonyms: bind, restrain, fetter|antonyms: free, release, unchain|verb
