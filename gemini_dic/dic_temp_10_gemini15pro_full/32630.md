chirurgic:/kərˈɜːrdʒɪk/ /səːrˈdʒɪk/| adj.|外科的；手术的|Relating to surgery or surgeons.  Derived from Greek "kheirourgikos" (kheir = hand, ergon = work).  Often used to describe instruments, techniques, or procedures related to surgery. |The chirurgic instruments were sterilized before the operation. 外科手术器械在手术前已消毒。

The chirurgic procedure was successful.  外科手术很成功。

|近义词：surgical, operative|反义词: medical (in the sense of non-surgical)|词性: adjective
