fortuitousnesses:/fɔːrˌtuːɪtəsnəsəz/| n. |偶然性；意外事件|The quality or state of being fortuitous; a series of fortuitous events.|His success was due to a series of fortuitousnesses. 他的成功源于一系列的偶然事件。|近义词：coincidences, accidents, chances, flukes|反义词: inevitabilities, certainties|词性:名词

词源:
From fortuitous + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin *fortuitus (whence Old French and Middle French fortuit) + -ous + -ness.
