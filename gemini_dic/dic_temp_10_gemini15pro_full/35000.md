cloistress:/ˈklɔɪstrɪs/
n.
修女；隐居的女子
a woman living in or as if in a cloister; a nun.
The cloistress devoted her life to prayer and contemplation.  这位修女将一生奉献给了祈祷和沉思。
Synonyms: nun, recluse, anchorite
Antonyms:  worldling, socialite
词性: 名词
