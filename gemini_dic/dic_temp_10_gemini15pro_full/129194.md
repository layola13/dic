nobilitation:/ˌnoʊbɪlɪˈteɪʃən/ |n.|授予贵族称号；册封；提升地位|The act of raising someone to the nobility or to a higher social rank.  The nobilitation of the commoner was a surprise to everyone.|例句：His nobilitation came as a reward for his years of service to the crown. 他的册封是对其多年来为王室服务的奖赏。

例句：The family sought nobilitation through marriage into the aristocracy.  这个家族试图通过与贵族联姻来提升地位。|近义词：elevation, promotion, ennoblement|反义词：degradation, demotion|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *nōbilitātiōn- (stem of nōbilitātiō), from Latin nōbilitāre  "to make noble," from nōbilis  "noble."
