begone:/bɪˈɡɒn/ (UK), /bɪˈɡɔːn/ (US)| v. |走开，离开，消失|Used as an imperative to tell someone to leave, usually in an angry or impatient way.  It has a strong literary or archaic feel and is not commonly used in modern everyday conversation.|Begone, foul beast! 走开，你这肮脏的野兽！|近义词：depart, leave, get out, go away|反义词：arrive, come, stay, remain|Verb
