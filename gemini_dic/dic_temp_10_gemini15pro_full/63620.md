enzootic:/ɛnzoʊˈɒtɪk/
adjective
地方性的（疾病）；（疾病）限于特定地区的
Prevailing among animals in a certain locality; confined to a particular district or area (of a disease of animals).
The study found the virus to be enzootic in the deer population.  这项研究发现这种病毒是鹿群的地方性疾病。
The enzootic bovine leukosis was a concern for local farmers. 地方性的牛白血病是当地农民关心的问题。
近义词: endemic, regional, localized
反义词: pandemic, widespread, global
词性: adjective
词源: From Greek *en- 'in' + zō(o)- 'animal' + -otic
