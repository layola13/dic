newsome:/ˈnjuːsəm/ | n. | 纽瑟姆（姓氏） | A surname, most notably associated with Gavin Newsom, the current governor of California. | 例句：Newsom signed the bill into law. 纽瑟姆将该法案签署成为法律。 | 例句：I saw Newsom on television last night. 我昨晚在电视上看到了纽瑟姆。 | 近义词: (无，因为是专有名词) | 反义词: (无，因为是专有名词) | 词性: 名词


词源:
Newsome is a locational surname of English origin. It means "at the new houses," derived from the Old English elements "neowe" (new) and "husum" (houses, dative plural of "hus").  Over time, the pronunciation and spelling evolved to the current form.
