iconographic:/ˌaɪkənəˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. | 肖像的；图像的；图解的 | Relating to or using icons or other symbolic representations.  |  The altarpiece is rich in iconographic detail. | 近义词: symbolic, emblematic, pictorial | 反义词: aniconic, non-representational | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *iconographique*, from *icono-* (from Greek *eikōn* "image") + *-graphique* (from Greek *graphikos* "of or relating to painting or drawing").
