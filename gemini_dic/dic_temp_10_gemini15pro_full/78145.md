geophila:/ˌdʒiːˈɒfɪlə/ /ˌdʒiːˈoʊfɪlə/
n.|地生植物；土生植物|A plant that grows in the ground, especially one whose seeds germinate in the soil.|Geophila repens is a small creeping herb often used as a ground cover in tropical gardens.  匍匐地果是一种小型匍匐草本植物，常用于热带花园的地被植物。|Synonyms: terrestrial plant, ground plant|Antonyms: epiphyte, aquatic plant|noun
