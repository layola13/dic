bicapsular: /baɪˈkæpsjʊlər/
adjective
双囊的；具有两个囊的
Having two capsules or capsule-like structures.
The bicapsular joint allows for a wide range of motion.  这个双囊关节允许大范围的运动。
The bicapsular nature of the organ allows for separate functions within each chamber. 这个器官的双囊性质允许每个腔室内的独立功能。
近义词: double-capsuled
反义词: unicapsular (单囊的)
词性: adjective

词源:
bi- (prefix): two, twice, double (来自拉丁语)
capsule (noun): a small case or covering, often containing medicine or other substances (来自拉丁语 capsula,  意为小盒子)
-ar (suffix): relating to, having the character of (形成形容词)
