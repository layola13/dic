Noun
计算器; 计算表; (尤指) 计算结果
A calculary is a table or device used for calculation, especially a table of results.  This word is archaic and rarely used in modern English.
The astronomer consulted his calculary to predict the next eclipse. 天文学家查阅了他的计算表来预测下一次日食。
Synonym: calculator, computer, table (of results)
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "estimation" or "guesswork" as contrasting concepts)
