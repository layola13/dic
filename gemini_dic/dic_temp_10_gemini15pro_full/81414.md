grassplot:/ˈɡræsplɒt/ (US) /ˈɡrɑːsplɒt/ (UK)| n. |草坪，草地|A small area of grass, especially one that is kept closely mowed, as in a lawn or garden.|例句：The children were playing on the grassplot. 孩子们在草坪上玩耍。

例句：A picnic blanket was spread out on the grassplot.  野餐毯铺在草坪上。

近义词：lawn, patch of grass, sward|反义词: barren, wasteland |词性: noun
