functionalists:/ˈfʌŋkʃənəlɪsts/
n.
功能主义者
Advocates or followers of functionalism, a theory that emphasizes the interdependence of parts within a system and their contribution to the overall functioning of that system.
Functionalists believe that society is like a living organism with interdependent parts.  功能主义者认为社会就像一个有相互依存部分的有机体。

Functionalists often study the role of education in maintaining social stability. 功能主义者经常研究教育在维护社会稳定中的作用。

近义词:proponents, supporters, advocates
反义词:critics, opponents
词性:noun
