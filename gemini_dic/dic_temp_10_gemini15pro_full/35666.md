cobber:/ˈkɒbə(r)/ (UK), /ˈkɑːbər/ (US)| n.|朋友，伙伴，老兄|A close friend or companion, especially a male friend.  Originally Australian slang.|例句：He's a good cobber of mine. 他是我的好朋友。

例句：We went to the footy with a few cobbers. 我们和几个朋友去看澳式足球了。

近义词：friend, mate, pal, buddy|反义词:enemy, foe|词性:noun
