convertibility:/kənˌvɜːrtɪˈbɪləti/
n.
可兑换性，可变换性
The convertibility of currency is crucial for international trade. 货币的可兑换性对国际贸易至关重要。
The easy convertibility of the sofa into a bed makes it perfect for small apartments. 沙发易于转换成床，使其非常适合小公寓。
interchangeability, transformability
non-convertibility, inconvertibility
noun
