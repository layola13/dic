foreknow:/fɔːrˈnoʊ/| v.|预知，先知|To know beforehand; to have prior knowledge of. |例句：She seemed to foreknow what would happen. 她似乎预知了将要发生的事。

例句：He foreknew the difficulties that lay ahead. 他预知了未来的困难。

近义词：predict, anticipate, foresee|反义词: be unaware of, be ignorant of|词性:动词
