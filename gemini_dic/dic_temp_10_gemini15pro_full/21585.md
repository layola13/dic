bluffly:/ˈblʌfli/| adv.|唐突地；率直地；粗鲁地|In a blunt and abrupt manner, often appearing insensitive or rude.|He bluffly told her she was wrong. 他唐突地告诉她，她错了。|例句：She bluffly refused his offer. 她粗鲁地拒绝了他的提议。|近义词：abruptly, bluntly, curtly, roughly|反义词：tactfully, politely, diplomatically|词性:副词

bluffly:/ˈblʌfli/| adv.|虚张声势地|Pretending to be confident and strong, especially in order to deceive someone.|He bluffly stated he had a royal flush, but his hand trembled. 他虚张声势地说他拿到同花大顺，但他的手却在颤抖。|例句：He bluffly pushed all his chips into the pot. 他虚张声势地把所有筹码都推进了底池。|近义词：pretentiously, deceptively|反义词：honestly, sincerely|词性:副词


词源: From bluff (adjective) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Bluff" originates from the Dutch word "bluf" meaning "boastful."
