gehenna:/ɡɪˈhɛnə/| n.|地狱，阴间|A place of eternal punishment; hell.  It comes from the Hebrew name for a valley near Jerusalem that was used as a garbage dump and where fires perpetually burned. |The preacher warned of the horrors of gehenna awaiting sinners.|近义词：hell, inferno, abyss|反义词:heaven, paradise|名词
