agnostical: /æɡˈnɒstɪk(ə)l/ /æɡˈnɑːstɪk(ə)l/ | adj. |不可知的，怀疑论的 | Relating to agnosticism; holding the view that the existence of God or the supernatural is unknowable. |  He identified himself as an agnostic, unsure of the existence of a higher power. 他自认为是一个不可知论者，对更高力量的存在不确定。| Synonyms: skeptical, doubting, unbelieving | Antonyms: believing, faithful, devout | adjective

agnostic: /æɡˈnɒstɪk/ /æɡˈnɑːstɪk/ | n. |不可知论者 | A person who believes that nothing is known or can be known of the existence or nature of God or of anything beyond material phenomena; a person who claims neither faith nor disbelief in God. |  She was raised Catholic but now considers herself an agnostic. 她从小是天主教徒，但现在自认为是不可知论者。 | Synonyms: skeptic, doubter | Antonyms: believer, theist | noun
