oosporangium:/ˌoʊəspɔːˈrændʒiəm/ | n. | 卵孢子囊 | A sporangium (spore case) in certain algae and fungi that contains one or more large non-motile female gametes (oospheres). |  The oosporangium develops a thick wall for protection. 卵孢子囊会长出一层厚厚的壁来保护自己。| The oospores are formed within the oosporangium. 卵孢子在卵孢子囊内形成。 | synonym: oogonium | antonym: N/A | noun
