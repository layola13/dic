candelabrum:/ˌkændəˈlɑːbrəm/ /ˌkændəˈluːbrəm/ | n. |枝状大烛台；烛台|A large, branched candlestick or lampstand, often ornate and used for holding multiple candles. | The grand ballroom was illuminated by a magnificent candelabrum.  宏伟的宴会厅被一个华丽的枝状大烛台照亮。| The candelabrum on the dining table added a touch of elegance.  餐桌上的烛台增添了一丝优雅。 | 近义词：candlestick, chandelier | 反义词: lamp | 词性: 名词
