ethnocentric: /ˌeθnoʊˈsentrɪk/ | adj. | 民族中心主义的 | Believing that one's own ethnic group or culture is superior to all others and judging other cultures by the standards of one's own. |  Example 1: His ethnocentric views made it difficult for him to appreciate other cultures. 他的民族中心主义观点使他难以欣赏其他文化。

Example 2: The ethnocentric policies of the government led to discrimination against minority groups. 政府的民族中心主义政策导致了对少数群体的歧视。 | 近义词: prejudiced, biased, narrow-minded, insular | 反义词: open-minded, tolerant, cosmopolitan | 词性: adjective

ethnocentrism: /ˌeθnoʊˈsentrɪzəm/ | n. | 民族中心主义 | The belief in the inherent superiority of one's own ethnic group or culture. | Example 1: Ethnocentrism can lead to conflict and misunderstanding between different groups. 民族中心主义会导致不同群体之间的冲突和误解。

Example 2: Overcoming ethnocentrism is essential for building a harmonious society. 克服民族中心主义对于建设和谐社会至关重要。 | 近义词: prejudice, bias, bigotry | 反义词: cultural relativism, tolerance | 词性: noun
