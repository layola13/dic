heraldries: /ˈhɛrəldriz/
n.
纹章学；纹章；盾形纹章
The study of coats of arms and other heraldic bearings.  The design and display of armorial bearings, especially as symbols of a family, corporation, or nation. A collection or display of coats of arms.
The book provided a detailed explanation of medieval heraldries. 这本书详细解释了中世纪的纹章学。
The heraldries of the noble families were proudly displayed. 贵族家庭的纹章被骄傲地展示出来。
近义词:  coat(s) of arms,  armorial bearings, blazonry,  insignia
反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑  plainness, anonymity)
词性: 名词 (plural)

词源:
From Old French *heraldie, from herault "herald." Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *harja-waldaz "army commander."
