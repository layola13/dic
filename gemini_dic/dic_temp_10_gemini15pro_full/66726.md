exorbitant:/ɪɡˈzɔːr.bɪ.tənt/| adj.|过高的，过分的，离谱的|Going far beyond what is usual, reasonable, or proper; excessive.|The prices at that restaurant are exorbitant. 那家餐馆的价格高得离谱。|近义词：excessive, outrageous, unreasonable, preposterous|反义词: reasonable, moderate, affordable|词性:形容词
Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin exorbitant- ‘diverging from the track’, from the verb exorbitare, from ex- ‘out of’ + orbita ‘track, course’.
