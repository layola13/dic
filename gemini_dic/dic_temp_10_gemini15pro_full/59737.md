eco-label:/ˈiːkəʊˌleɪbəl/| n. |生态标签|A label which indicates that a product or service meets certain standards of environmental friendliness.|This product carries an eco-label, guaranteeing its low environmental impact.  这个产品带有生态标签，保证其对环境影响小。|Synonyms: green label, environmental label|Antonyms: N/A|noun
