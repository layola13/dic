dittoing:/ˈdɪtəʊɪŋ/ | v. | 同上，重复，附议 | Repeating something that has already been said or done, often in a formal context, especially in voting. | The motion was dittoed by several members.  几位成员附议了这项动议。| He just dittoed what his boss said. 他只是重复了他老板的话。 | 近义词: repeating, echoing, copying, concurring | 反义词: disagreeing, opposing | 词性: 动词

词源: From "ditto,"  which comes from Italian *detto*, the past participle of *dire* "to say."
