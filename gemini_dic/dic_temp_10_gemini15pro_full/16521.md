barkentine:/ˈbɑːrkənˌtiːn/
n.
三桅帆船，巴肯廷帆船
A sailing ship with three masts; the foremast square-rigged and the main and mizzen fore-and-aft rigged.
The barkentine sailed gracefully into the harbor.  这艘三桅帆船优雅地驶入港口。
近义词：barquentine, schooner
反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific ship type)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Dutch *barkentyn*, probably from *barke* ("barque") + *tyn* (diminutive suffix).  Ultimately related to Latin *barca* ("small boat").
