ballhawk:/ˈbɔːlhɔːk/| n.| 球鹰；(尤指棒球或美式足球中)擅长接球的人；|A ballhawk is a player, especially in baseball or American football, who is exceptionally skilled at catching balls, particularly those that are difficult to catch.  |例句：He's a real ballhawk in center field. 他是中外野一个真正的接球高手。

例句：The team's ballhawk intercepted the pass and secured the win. 球队的接球高手拦截了传球，确保了胜利。|近义词：interceptor, fielder (in baseball), defensive back (in football)|反义词: butterfingers|词性: noun
