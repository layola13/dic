foggily:/ˈfɒɡɪli/ (UK) /ˈfɑːɡɪli/ (US)| adv. | 模糊地，朦胧地 | In a way that is not clear or sharp, like something seen through fog.  It can refer to both literal and figurative obscurity. |  He looked at me foggily, as if he hadn't slept in days. 他眼神朦胧地看着我，好像好几天没睡似的。

She remembered the events foggily, as if in a dream. 她朦胧地记得那些事，就像在梦里一样。| 近义词: vaguely, obscurely, dimly, hazily | 反义词: clearly, distinctly, sharply | 词性: 副词
