high-definition:/ˌhaɪ defəˈnɪʃən/ | adj. | 高清的；高清晰度的 |  Producing images of high quality, with a large number of pixels per unit area, resulting in a sharp and detailed picture. |  We watched the movie in high-definition. 我们以高清格式观看了这部电影。 | high-resolution, sharp, clear | low-resolution, blurry, fuzzy | adjective

high-definition:/ˌhaɪ defəˈnɪʃən/ | n. | 高清晰度 | The quality of being high-definition. | The TV supports high-definition. 这台电视支持高清。 | high-resolution, clarity, sharpness | low-resolution, blurriness, fuzziness | noun
