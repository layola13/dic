oils:/ɔɪlz/
n.
油；油类；油画颜料
A viscous liquid derived from petroleum, plants, or animals, used for lubrication, fuel, or cooking, among other purposes. Also refers to oil paintings or oil-based paints.
The car needs an oil change. 这辆车需要换油。
lubricants, greases
solids
noun
