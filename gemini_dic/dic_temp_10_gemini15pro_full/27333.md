camaradas:/ˌkɑːməˈrɑːdəz/ /ˌkæməˈrɑːdəz/| n. |同志们；伙伴们|A term of address used by communists and socialists, expressing solidarity and comradeship.  It originates from the Spanish word "camarada," meaning "comrade."|例句：The speaker addressed the crowd as "camaradas." 演讲者称呼人群为“同志们”。

例句：The camaradas fought side by side in the revolution.  同志们在革命中并肩作战。|近义词：comrades, companions, friends, colleagues, associates|反义词：enemies, adversaries, opponents|词性:noun
