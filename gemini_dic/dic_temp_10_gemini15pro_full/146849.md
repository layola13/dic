overindividualization:/ˌoʊvərˌɪndɪˌvɪdʒuələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度个体化，过度个人主义 | The excessive emphasis on individual needs and desires, often at the expense of collective well-being or social cohesion.  It refers to a societal trend where individual rights and freedoms are prioritized to such an extent that it undermines social harmony and collective responsibility. |  The overindividualization of society has led to a decline in community spirit.  社会过度个体化导致了社区精神的衰落。

Overindividualization in education can sometimes hinder collaborative learning. 教育中的过度个体化有时会阻碍合作学习。 | 近义词:atomization, excessive individualism, hyper-individualism | 反义词: collectivism, communitarianism | 词性: noun
