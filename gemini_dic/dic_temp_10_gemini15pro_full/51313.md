detainder:/dɪˈteɪndər/
n.|扣押，拘留；扣押令|The unlawful act of detaining someone.  The writ or warrant authorizing such detention.|The suspect was held under a detainder.  嫌疑犯被拘留令扣押。|近义词：detention, imprisonment, confinement|反义词：release, liberation|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Anglo-French *deteyner, from Old French detenir, from Latin detinēre (“to hold back, detain”), from de- + tenēre (“to hold”).
