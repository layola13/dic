co-guardianship:/koʊˈɡɑːrdiənʃɪp/
n.
共同监护权
The sharing of legal guardianship responsibilities between two or more individuals, typically after a separation or divorce.
The parents agreed on co-guardianship of their children after the divorce. 离婚后，父母双方同意共同监护孩子。
The co-guardianship arrangement allows both parents to be actively involved in their child's life. 共同监护安排允许父母双方都积极参与孩子的生活。
近义词:joint custody, shared guardianship
反义词:sole custody
