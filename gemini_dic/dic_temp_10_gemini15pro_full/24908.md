brulee:/bruːˈleɪ/| adj. n.| 焦糖化的，烧焦的; 焦糖布丁 |A dessert, typically custard, topped with a layer of hard caramel.  The caramel is usually created by sprinkling sugar on top of the custard and then caramelizing it with a blowtorch or under a broiler.| I ordered the crème brûlée for dessert. 我点了焦糖布丁作为甜点。| 近义词: caramelized, torched | 反义词: uncooked, raw | 词性: adjective, noun | 词源: French, past participle of brûler "to burn"
