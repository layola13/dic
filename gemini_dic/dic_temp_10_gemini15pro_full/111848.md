logically:/ˈlɒdʒɪkli/ (UK) /ˈlɑːdʒɪkli/ (US)| adv.|逻辑上地，符合逻辑地|In a way that is expected or reasonable given the facts.  This adverb describes actions or thinking that is based on clear reasoning.|She logically concluded that he was lying, given his contradictory statements. 她根据他自相矛盾的陈述，逻辑地推断他在撒谎。|Synonyms: rationally, reasonably, sensibly|Antonyms: irrationally, unreasonably, illogically|adverb
