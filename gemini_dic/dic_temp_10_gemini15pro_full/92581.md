一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What's his name? = 他叫什么名字？
What's her favorite color? = 她最喜欢的颜色是什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What's happening outside? = 外面发生了什么事？
What's she doing right now? = 她现在在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What's been going on here? = 这里发生了什么事？
What's he accomplished so far? = 到目前为止他取得了什么成就？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What's she been working on all day? = 她一整天都在做什么？
What's he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What was his reaction? = 他的反应是什么？
What's happened to the car? = 这车怎么了？（虽然用has happened更常见，但what's happened在口语中也使用）


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were they doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候他们在做什么？
What was she thinking about yesterday? = 她昨天在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had happened before I left? = 我离开之前发生了什么事？
What had they discussed in the previous meeting? = 他们在上次会议中讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had he been doing before the interruption? = 打断之前他一直在做什么？
What had she been working on for so long? = 她一直以来都在做什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will be the outcome? = 结果会是什么？
What's going to happen tomorrow? = 明天会发生什么？（口语常用）

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 会议上他们会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will have changed by the end of the year? = 到年底会有什么变化？
What will they have achieved by then? = 到那时他们会取得什么成就？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for the past ten years by 2033? = 到2033年，你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What's new? = 有什么新鲜事？
What's the matter? = 怎么了？
What's going on? = 发生什么事了？
What's the point? = 有什么意义？


注意事项:

因为'i's's 不是一个独立的单词，而是 what is 或 what has 的缩写，所以没有直接的词源。它的组成部分 what 和 is/has 有各自的词源，可以分别查询。

'i's's 作为缩写，主要用于口语和非正式场合。
