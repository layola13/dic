echidna:/ɪˈkɪdnə/ | n. |针鼹，刺食蚁兽 |A spiny, egg-laying mammal native to Australia and New Guinea, characterized by its long snout and sharp claws. |例句：The echidna uses its long snout to probe for ants and termites. 针鼹用它长长的鼻子探测蚂蚁和白蚁。

例句：Echidnas are one of the few mammals that lay eggs. 针鼹是少数几种产卵的哺乳动物之一。|近义词：spiny anteater|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps placental mammal) |词性:noun
