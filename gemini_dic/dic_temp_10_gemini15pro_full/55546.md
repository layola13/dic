distributorship: /ˌdɪstrɪˈbjuːtərʃɪp/ (US), /ˌdɪstrɪˈbjuːtəʃɪp/ (UK) | n. |经销权，经销商资格；分销商；经销地区|The right or license granted by a manufacturer to an individual or company to sell its products within a specific geographic area.  A business that holds such a right. | He was granted a distributorship for the entire state.  他获得了整个州的经销权。|synonyms: franchise, dealership, agency | antonyms:  None (lack of a distributorship implies no right to distribute, but there's no single antonym) | noun
