outgaining:/ˌaʊtˈɡeɪnɪŋ/
verb
胜过，超过（在收益或产量方面）
Surpassing in gains or production.
Company A is outgaining Company B in sales this quarter.  A公司本季度的销售额超过了B公司。
The new factory is outgaining the old one in terms of efficiency. 新工厂在效率方面超过了旧工厂。
Synonyms: exceeding, surpassing, outperforming, outstripping
Antonyms: lagging behind, underperforming, trailing
词性: 动词
