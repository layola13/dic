ditokous:/daɪˈtɒkəs/
adjective
双胎的，产双胞胎的
Giving birth to two offspring at a time.
例句：The ditokous ewe gave birth to two healthy lambs. 这只产双胞胎的母羊生了两只健康的小羊羔。

例句：Ditokous births are more common in some sheep breeds than others.  在某些绵羊品种中，双胞胎出生比其他品种更常见。

近义词：twin-bearing, biparous
反义词：monotocous (producing single offspring at birth)
词性: adjective

词源: From Greek *di- "two" + tokos "birth, offspring."
