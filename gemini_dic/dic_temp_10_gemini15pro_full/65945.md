exactingness:/ɪɡˈzæktɪŋnɪs/| n.|严格；苛求；严谨|The state or quality of being exacting; strictness; meticulousness.  The exactingness of the professor's standards made the course very challenging.  教授严格的标准使得这门课程非常具有挑战性。|近义词：rigor, meticulousness, precision, strictness, demandingness|反义词：leniency, laxity, flexibility, tolerance|词性:noun
Etymology: From exacting + -ness.  Exacting from exact + -ing. Exact from Latin *exactus, past participle of exigō (“I demand, require, drive out, weigh”), from ex (“out”) + agō (“I drive, lead”).
