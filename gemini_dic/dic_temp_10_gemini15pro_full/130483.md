nonacademic:/ˌnɒnækəˈdɛmɪk/ | adj. | 非学术的；非学院的 | Not related to academic studies or the world of universities and scholarship.  Relating to practical skills and knowledge rather than theoretical subjects. |  Many students pursue nonacademic careers after graduation. 许多学生毕业后从事非学术性职业。 | The workshop focused on nonacademic skills, such as teamwork and communication.  该研讨会侧重于非学术技能，例如团队合作和沟通。 | 近义词: non-scholarly, practical, vocational, applied | 反义词: academic, scholarly, theoretical | 词性: adjective

词源:
From non- + academic.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "academic" refers to studies and scholarship.
