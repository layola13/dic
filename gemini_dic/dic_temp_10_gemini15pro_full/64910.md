essoign: /ɛˈsɔɪn/ | vt. | To offer an excuse for non-appearance; to excuse. | To formally excuse someone's absence. | He essoigned himself from the meeting due to a prior engagement.  由于事先有约，他为自己缺席会议找了借口。 | synonyms: excuse, exempt | antonyms:  summon, compel | verb
