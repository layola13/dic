antiblackness:/ˌæntiˈblæknɪs/
n.
反黑性；对黑人的歧视
The systematic oppression and dehumanization of Black people, based on the belief that Black people are inferior.
例句：Antiblackness is deeply ingrained in many societies.  反黑性在许多社会中根深蒂固。

例句：The protests highlighted the pervasiveness of antiblackness.  抗议活动突显了反黑性的普遍存在。

近义词：racism, discrimination, prejudice, bigotry, racial hatred
反义词：racial equality, racial justice, anti-racism
词性：名词
