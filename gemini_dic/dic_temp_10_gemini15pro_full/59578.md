ebony:/ˈɛbəni/| n. adj.|乌木；乌木制的；乌黑色的|A hard, heavy, durable wood, most varieties of which are black or very dark brown. Also, the color of this wood, or something that color.|The antique table was made of polished ebony.|近义词：black, dark, jet|反义词:white, light, pale|词性:名词, 形容词

ebony:/ˈɛbəni/| adj.|乌木制的；乌黑色的|Having the color of ebony; jet-black.|She had long, ebony hair.|近义词：black, dark, jet|反义词:white, light, pale|词性:形容词
