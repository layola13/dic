beadiest:/ˈbiːdiɪst/| adj.|最炯炯有神的，目光最锐利的|Having or showing an intense, sharp, and observant gaze, often suggesting shrewdness or suspicion.  Characterized by small, bright, bead-like eyes that seem to miss nothing.|He gave me a beadiest look, as if he knew my secret. 他给了我一个极其锐利的眼神，好像他知道我的秘密。|Synonyms: sharp, piercing, keen, hawk-like, penetrating, observant, shrewd, watchful|Antonyms: dull, unobservant, inattentive|adjective
