counterparty:/ˈkaʊntərˌpɑːrti/ (US), /ˈkaʊntəˌpɑːti/ (UK)| n. |交易对手，对方当事人|A counterparty is the other party involved in a financial contract or transaction.  It refers to the person or organization with whom one enters into an agreement or business deal.|例句：The bank acted as the counterparty in the swap transaction.  这家银行在互换交易中充当了交易对手。

例句：We need to assess the creditworthiness of the counterparty before entering into the agreement.  在签订协议之前，我们需要评估交易对手的信用度。|近义词：other party, opposite number|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
