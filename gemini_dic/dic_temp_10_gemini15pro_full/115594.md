marble:/ˈmɑːrbl̩/ (US) /ˈmɑːbl/ (UK)| n. adj.|大理石，弹珠；冷酷无情|A hard crystalline metamorphic form of limestone, typically white with coloured mottlings or streaks, which may be polished and used in sculpture and architecture. (一种坚硬的结晶变质石灰石，通常为白色，带有彩色斑点或条纹，可抛光用于雕塑和建筑。) Also, a small ball of glass, clay, etc., used in children's games. (也指一种用于儿童游戏的小玻璃球、泥球等。)|The Taj Mahal is made of white marble. (泰姬陵是用白色大理石建造的。)|近义词：stone, rock, gem, bead|反义词:softness, warmth|词性:名词，形容词

marble:/ˈmɑːrbl̩/ (US) /ˈmɑːbl/ (UK)| adj.|大理石的；冷酷无情的|Made of marble; resembling marble in coldness or hardness. (由大理石制成的；在冰冷或硬度方面类似大理石。)|He has a marble heart. (他有一颗冷酷无情的心。)|近义词：stone, stony, cold, hard|反义词:warm, compassionate|词性:形容词
