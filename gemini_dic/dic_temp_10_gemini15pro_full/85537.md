hatchel:/ˈhætʃəl/ (US), /ˈhætʃl/ (UK)| n. v.|梳麻机，梳理（亚麻、大麻等）|A tool with long metal teeth used for separating the fibers of flax, hemp, or jute.|The farmer used a hatchel to prepare the flax for spinning.|Synonyms: hackle, comb|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a tool. Could consider "tangle" as an antonym for the verb form)|Noun, Verb
