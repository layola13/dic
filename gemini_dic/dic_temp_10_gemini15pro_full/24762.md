brooklet:/ˈbrʊklɪt/| n. |小溪，溪流|A small stream; a rivulet. |例句：The brooklet flowed gently through the meadow. 小溪缓缓流过草地。

例句：The children paddled in the cool brooklet on a hot summer day.  炎热的夏天，孩子们在凉爽的小溪里嬉水。|近义词：stream, rivulet, creek |反义词:river|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *broklet, a diminutive of brook, from Old English brōc (“brook”).
