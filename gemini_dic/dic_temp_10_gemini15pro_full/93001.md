idiopathic:/ˌɪdiəˈpæθɪk/| adj.|特发性的，原因不明的|Relating to or denoting any disease or condition that arises spontaneously or for which the cause is unknown.|例句：The doctor diagnosed the patient with idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis. 医生诊断该病人患有特发性肺纤维化。|例句：Idiopathic hypersomnia is a rare neurological disorder. 特发性嗜睡症是一种罕见的神经系统疾病。|近义词：essential, primary, spontaneous, cryptogenic|反义词:secondary (when the cause is known)|词性:adjective
