bugler's:/ˈbjuːɡlərz/ |n. | 军号手的（东西）; 军号手所属的 |Something that belongs to or is associated with a bugler. | The bugler's uniform was impeccably clean. 军号手的制服干净整洁。| 近义词: trumpeter's | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: 名词所有格

词源:  From "bugler" + the possessive suffix "-'s".  "Bugler" comes from the French "bugle" (hunting horn), ultimately from Latin "buculus" (young bull, bullock), referring to the original horns made from bull horns.
