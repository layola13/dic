imaret:/ɪməˈrɛt/ | n. | 公共食堂，施粥场；旅客招待所 | A public soup kitchen or hostel in Turkey, especially one built as a charitable institution by a Muslim. | The imaret provided food and shelter to travelers and the poor. 这家公共食堂为旅行者和穷人提供食物和住所。| The weary travelers found refuge in the imaret.  疲惫的旅客在旅客招待所找到了避难所。| 近义词：hostel, soup kitchen, refuge, shelter | 反义词:  None | noun
