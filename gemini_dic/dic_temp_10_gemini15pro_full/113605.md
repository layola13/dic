maceration:/ˌmæsəˈreɪʃn/ | n. |浸渍，软化; (尸体)腐烂| The process of softening or breaking down by soaking in a liquid.  The maceration of the fruit made it easier to make jam. 水果的浸渍使其更容易制作果酱。| synonyms: soaking, steeping, softening, disintegration, decomposition | antonyms: dehydration, drying, hardening | noun


macerations:/ˌmæsəˈreɪʃnz/ | n. |浸渍，软化; (尸体)腐烂（复数）| The plural form of maceration. The macerations of the various herbs were combined to create the tincture. 各种草药的浸渍物被组合起来制成酊剂。| synonyms: soakings, steepings, softenings, disintegrations, decompositions | antonyms: dehydrations, dryings, hardenings | noun
