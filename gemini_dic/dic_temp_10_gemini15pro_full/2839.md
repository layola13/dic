adventuring:/ədˈvɛntʃərɪŋ/
n.|冒险; 冒险经历|The act of engaging in adventures.  The experience of having adventures.|They spent their youth adventuring in the Himalayas. 他们年轻时在喜马拉雅山探险。|Synonyms: exploring, venturing, roaming, traversing|Antonyms: staying put, remaining, settling down|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From adventure + -ing.  Ultimately from Latin adventurus.

USAGE->
As a gerund (noun form of a verb):

They loved adventuring in the wilderness. = 他们喜欢在野外冒险。
Adventuring in a foreign country can be both exciting and challenging. = 在国外冒险既令人兴奋又充满挑战。

As a present participle (part of a continuous verb tense):

They are adventuring through the Amazon rainforest. = 他们正在亚马逊雨林中冒险。
He was adventuring in South America when he discovered the lost city. = 他在南美洲探险时发现了失落的城市。


PHRASE->
go adventuring = 去冒险
adventuring spirit = 冒险精神
a life of adventuring = 冒险的生活
adventuring gear = 冒险装备
enjoy adventuring = 喜欢冒险
