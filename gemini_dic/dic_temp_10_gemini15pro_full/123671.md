moodies:/ˈmuːdiz/| n.|喜怒无常的人；情绪化的人|People who are moody and experience frequent shifts in their emotional state.  It's the plural form of "moody."|The teenagers were a bunch of moodies, their attitudes changing with the wind.|近义词：grumps, grouches|反义词：optimists, cheerful people|Noun
