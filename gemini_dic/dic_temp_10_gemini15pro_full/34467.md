claw:/klɔː/ (US) /klɔː/ (UK)| n. v. |爪子；攫取|A sharp, curved nail on an animal's foot, or a similar part on an insect or crustacean.  As a verb, it means to scratch or tear something with claws or fingernails.|
The cat sharpened its claws on the tree. 猫在树上磨爪子。

The eagle clawed at its prey.  老鹰用爪子抓捕猎物。

|近义词：nail, talon, grab, clutch|反义词: paw (not always a direct opposite, but refers to a softer pad)|词性: noun, verb
