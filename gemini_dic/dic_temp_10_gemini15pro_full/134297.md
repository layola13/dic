noninjurious:/ˌnɒnɪnˈdʒʊəriəs/
adjective
无害的，不致伤害的
Not harmful or injurious; causing no damage.
The substance is considered noninjurious to humans.  这种物质被认为对人体无害。
The doctor confirmed that the bump was noninjurious. 医生确认肿块是无害的。
近义词:harmless, safe, innocuous
反义词:harmful, injurious, damaging
词性:adjective

词源:
From non- (not) + injurious (causing injury).  "Injurious" comes from Latin "injuriosus" (insulting, injurious), from "injuria" (injury, wrong).
