grant:/ɡrɑːnt/ (US) /ɡrɑːnt/ (UK)| n. vt.|拨款，资助；授予，准予|To agree to give or allow (something requested) to someone.|The government granted him permission to build a house. 政府批准他建房子。|Synonyms: award, bestow, concede, confer |Antonyms: refuse, deny, withhold|noun, verb

grant:/ɡrɑːnt/ (US) /ɡrɑːnt/ (UK)| vt.|同意，准予|To agree or admit to (something).|I grant that you have a point. 我承认你有道理。|Synonyms: concede, admit, acknowledge |Antonyms: deny, dispute, contradict|verb
