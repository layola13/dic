nonagglomerative:/ˌnɒnəˈɡlɒmərətɪv/
adjective
非凝聚性的，非结块的
Not agglomerative; not tending to agglomerate or clump together.
The mixture was specifically designed to be nonagglomerative, ensuring easy flow.  这种混合物被特别设计成非凝聚性的，以确保容易流动。
The nonagglomerative nature of the powder made it ideal for use in the machine. 这种粉末的非凝聚性使其非常适合在机器中使用。
non-clumping, non-caking
agglomerative, cohesive
词性: 形容词
