misdemean:/ˌmɪsdɪˈmiːn/| v. |贬低；降低身份；举止不当|To behave in a way that is considered unacceptable or improper.|He misdemeaned himself by accepting bribes. 他因收受贿赂而降低了自己的身份。|Synonyms: debase, degrade, discredit|Antonyms: exalt, elevate, honor|verb

misdemeanor:/ˌmɪsdɪˈmiːnər/| n. |轻罪；不端行为|A minor wrongdoing; a nonindictable offense, less serious than a felony.|Parking violations are considered misdemeanors. 违规停车被认为是轻罪。|Synonyms: offense, transgression, infraction|Antonyms: felony, serious crime|noun
