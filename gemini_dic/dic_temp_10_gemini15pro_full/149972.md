palmatifid:/pælˈmætɪfɪd/| adj.|掌状浅裂的|Divided into lobes that extend less than halfway to the center or base, like the fingers of a hand spread apart but not fully extended.  Resembling a hand with the fingers spread open about halfway.|The leaves of the fig tree are palmatifid.|近义词：lobed, incised|反义词:entire, undivided|词性:adjective
