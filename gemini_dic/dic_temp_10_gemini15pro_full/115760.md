marinading:/ˈmærɪneɪdɪŋ/| n.|腌制，浸泡|The process of soaking food in a marinade.  The act of marinating something.|例句：The marinading of the chicken took several hours.  鸡肉的腌制花了几个小时。

例句：The chef is marinading the steak.  厨师正在腌制牛排。|近义词：soaking, immersing|反义词:drying|词性:noun (gerund)


marinade:/ˈmærɪneɪd/| n. vt.|腌泡汁，腌料；腌制|A sauce or mixture in which food is marinated; to soak food in a marinade.|例句：The marinade gave the meat a wonderful flavor.  腌泡汁给肉增添了绝妙的风味。

例句：He marinated the fish in lemon juice and herbs. 他用柠檬汁和香草腌制鱼。|近义词：sauce, mixture|反义词:N/A|词性:noun, verb


词源: From French *marinade*, from *mariner* "to pickle in seawater," from *marin* "sea," from Latin *marinus* "of the sea," from *mare* "sea."
