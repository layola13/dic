outstorms:/ˈaʊtˌstɔːrmz/
n.
胜过风暴; 比...更猛烈
surpasses or exceeds in force or intensity, especially a storm.
The ship outstormed the hurricane. 这艘船经受住了飓风的考验。
近义词: outweather, withstand, endure
反义词: succumb, yield

词性: 名词 (n.)

词源:  A combination of "out-" (meaning "exceeding" or "surpassing") and "storms" (referring to violent weather).  This creates a verb meaning to surpass or outlast a storm.  When used as a noun (less common), it refers to the act of outstorming.
