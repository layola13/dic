nonconsultatory:/ˌnɒnkənˈsʌltətəri/ | adj. | 非咨询性的，未经磋商的 | Not involving consultation; made or carried out without consulting others. | The decision was made in a nonconsultatory manner, angering many stakeholders.  这个决定是以非协商性的方式做出的，激怒了许多利益相关者。| The government imposed the new tax in a nonconsultatory way. 政府以非协商的方式征收了新税。 | 近义词: arbitrary, unilateral, undemocratic | 反义词: consultative, collaborative, democratic | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non "not."
consultatory (adjective):  From the verb "consult," which comes from Latin consultare "to consult, take counsel, deliberate."  The "-ory" suffix indicates relating to or characterized by.
