词源:  From inactive + -ness + -es (plural).  Inactive comes from the Latin prefix *in-* (not) + *activus* (active), ultimately from *agere* (to do, to drive). The suffix *-ness* forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality. The suffix *-es* makes the noun plural.
