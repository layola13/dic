dentality:/dɛnˈtælɪti/
n.
齿性; 牙齿的状态;
The condition or arrangement of the teeth in an animal.
The dentality of the ancient skull helped researchers determine its species.  这颗古代头骨的齿性帮助研究人员确定了它的种类。
denture, dentition
edentulousness
