diathermaneity:/ˌdaɪəθɜːrˈmeɪnɪti/ | n. | 透热性 | The property of transmitting radiant heat; transparency to heat radiation. | The diathermaneity of the material allows infrared radiation to pass through.  这种材料的透热性允许红外辐射穿过。| Synonym: transmissivity, permeability | Antonym: opacity, impermeability | noun


diathermanous:/ˌdaɪəθɜːrˈmeɪnəs/ | adj. | 透热的 | Allowing radiant heat to pass through easily. |  A diathermanous substance is used in the experiment to ensure efficient heat transfer.  实验中使用透热物质以确保有效的热传递。| Synonym: transparent (to heat), permeable (to heat) | Antonym: opaque (to heat), impermeable (to heat) | adjective
