counterspell:/ˈkaʊntərˌspɛl/| n. v. |反咒，对抗咒语|An action or spell that stops another spell from working.  It neutralizes or cancels the effect of the opposing spell.|The wizard cast a counterspell just as the goblin was about to unleash a fireball.|Synonyms: dispel, neutralize, nullify|Antonyms: cast, conjure, invoke|noun, verb
