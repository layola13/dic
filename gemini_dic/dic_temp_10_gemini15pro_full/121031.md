misconceit: /ˌmɪskənˈsiːt/
n.
自负，自大，骄傲自满
An excessive belief in one's own abilities or importance.
He was blinded by misconceived. 他被自负蒙蔽了双眼。

近义词: vanity, arrogance, pride, egotism, haughtiness
反义词: humility, modesty, humbleness
词性: 名词


词源:
From mis- (wrongly) + conceit (concept, opinion).  The word "conceit" originally meant "concept" or "opinion," and "misconceit" thus meant a wrong or false opinion, specifically about oneself.
