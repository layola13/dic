op-eds:/ˈɑːp ɛdz/ | n. | 观点版，评论版 | A shortened form of "opposite the editorial page," referring to a section in a publication (usually a newspaper or magazine) for signed articles expressing the opinions of writers who are usually unaffiliated with the publication's editorial board. | The newspaper publishes a variety of op-eds on current events. 这家报纸发表了各种关于时事的观点文章。 | synonyms: columns, commentaries, opinions, perspectives | antonyms: editorials, news reports | noun
