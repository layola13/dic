nontradition:/ˌnɒntrəˈdɪʃən/ | n. | 非传统，非惯例 |  A departure from tradition; something that breaks with established customs or practices. |  The architect's nontradition design was met with mixed reviews.  这位建筑师的非传统设计褒贬不一。 | synonyms: innovation, unconventionality, novelty, departure, break, modernization | antonyms: tradition, convention, custom, orthodoxy, conservatism | noun

nontraditional:/ˌnɒntrəˈdɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 非传统的，非惯例的 | Not following established traditions or customs. |  They took a nontraditional approach to education.  他们采取了一种非传统的教育方法。| synonyms: unconventional, unorthodox, innovative, modern, newfangled, avant-garde, progressive, groundbreaking | antonyms: traditional, conventional, customary, orthodox, conservative | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + tradition.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "tradition" refers to established customs or beliefs passed down through generations.
