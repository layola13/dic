forehand:/ˈfɔːrˌhænd/ (US), /ˈfɔːhænd/ (UK)| n. adj. adv.|正手；正手击球；正手的；用正手的|A stroke played with the palm of the hand facing forward.  It can also describe the side of the body on which the dominant hand is located.|He hit a powerful forehand down the line. 他用正手大力击球沿着边线得分。|近义词: front-handed|反义词: backhand|词性: noun, adjective, adverb

forehand:/ˈfɔːrˌhænd/ (US), /ˈfɔːhænd/ (UK)| adj.|正手的|Executed with the palm of the hand turned forward.|His forehand volley was perfectly timed. 他的正手截击时机掌握得很好。|近义词: front-handed|反义词: backhand|词性: adjective

forehand:/ˈfɔːrˌhænd/ (US), /ˈfɔːhænd/ (UK)| adv.|用正手|With the palm of the hand facing forward.|He played the shot forehand. 他用正手打了这一击。|近义词: front-handedly|反义词: backhand|词性: adverb
