inadmissible:/ˌɪnədˈmɪsəbl/ | adj. | 不可接受的；不允许的；不予采纳的 | Not allowed or acceptable, especially in a court of law. |  The judge ruled the evidence inadmissible. 法官裁定证据不可采纳。| Synonyms: unacceptable, invalid, prohibited, objectionable | Antonyms: admissible, acceptable, valid, permissible | 词性:形容词

inadmissible:/ˌɪnədˈmɪsəbl/ | adj. |  不合理的；不适当的 | Not reasonable or appropriate. | His behavior was completely inadmissible. 他的行为完全不可接受。 | Synonyms: inappropriate, unsuitable, improper, unreasonable | Antonyms: appropriate, suitable, proper, reasonable | 词性:形容词

词源:  Late 16th century: from in- ‘not’ + admissible.
