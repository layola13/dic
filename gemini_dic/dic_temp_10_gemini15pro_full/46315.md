dabbed:/dæbd/
|v.|轻拍，轻涂，轻触| to press against something lightly several times with a piece of cloth or other soft material in order to clean or dry it, or to apply a substance|She dabbed her eyes with a tissue. 她用纸巾轻擦了眼睛。|近义词：pat, tap, blot, touch|反义词：smear, rub|词性：动词

dabbed:/dæbd/
|v.|轻敷，轻点|to apply a substance with quick light strokes|She dabbed a little perfume behind her ears. 她在耳后轻点了一些香水。|近义词：apply, touch, daub|反义词：slather, coat|词性：动词
