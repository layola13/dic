cancriform:/ˈkæŋkrɪfɔːrm/| adj.|蟹形的，像蟹的|Resembling a crab; having a crab-like form.  Its shape is cancriform.  它的形状像螃蟹。|例句：The shell had a cancriform appearance.  这个壳看起来像螃蟹。

例句：The cancriform nebula is so named because of its crab-like shape. 蟹状星云因其像螃蟹的形状而得名。|近义词：crablike, crab-shaped|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective
Etymology: From Latin *cancriformis*, from *cancer* (“crab”) + *-formis* (“-shaped”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱerk- (“to turn, bend”).
