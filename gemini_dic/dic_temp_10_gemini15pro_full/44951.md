cryptopunks: /ˈkrɪptoʊˌpʌŋks/ | n. | 加密朋克 | A collection of 10,000 unique 24x24 pixel art images, generated algorithmically. They are NFTs and were among the earliest examples of this technology on the Ethereum blockchain. |  He bought a rare Cryptopunk for millions of dollars. 他花了几百万美元买了一个稀有的加密朋克。| Near synonyms: NFTs, digital art, collectibles | Antonyms:  physical art, traditional collectibles | noun
