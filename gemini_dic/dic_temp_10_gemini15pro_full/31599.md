chastening: /ˈtʃeɪsənɪŋ/ /ˈtʃeɪsnɪŋ/
adjective
使人反省的；使人变谦逊的；惩戒性的
Having a humbling or sobering effect, often as a result of some experience.
The team suffered a chastening defeat in the final.  这个队伍在决赛中遭受了一场令人反省的失败。
The experience was chastening for him.  这段经历让他变得谦逊。
近义词: humbling, sobering, subduing, disciplinary
反义词: encouraging, exhilarating, emboldening
词性: adjective

chasten: /ˈtʃeɪsn/ /ˈtʃeɪsən/
verb
惩戒；磨炼；使变乖
(of experience or suffering) have a restraining or moderating effect on.  /  Discipline; punish.
He was chastened by the experience. 他被这段经历磨炼了。
She chastened her son for his bad behavior. 她因儿子行为不端而责罚他。
近义词: discipline, punish, correct, subdue, humble
反义词: praise, encourage, reward
词性: verb

词源: From Old French 'chastier' (to punish, correct, chastise), from Latin 'castigare' (to punish, reprove). Ultimately from 'castus' (pure).
