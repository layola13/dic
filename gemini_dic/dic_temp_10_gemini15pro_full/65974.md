exaggerators:/ɪɡˈzædʒəreɪtərz/ | n. | 夸大者；夸张者 |  People who exaggerate; those who represent something as larger, better, or worse than it really is. |  The exaggerators made the fish they caught sound like a whale.  夸大其词的人把他们钓到的鱼说成像鲸鱼一样大。 | synonyms: hyperbolists, overstaters, embellishers | antonyms: understaters, minimizers | noun


exaggerate: /ɪɡˈzædʒəreɪt/ | v. | 夸大；夸张 | To represent something as larger, better, or worse than it really is. | He tends to exaggerate when talking about his accomplishments. 他在谈论自己的成就时往往会夸大其词。 | synonyms: overstate, amplify, embellish, inflate | antonyms: understate, downplay, minimize | verb
