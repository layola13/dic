bellowslike:/ˈbɛloʊzˌlaɪk/| adj.|像风箱一样的|Resembling a bellows, especially in its expanding and contracting movement or shape.  Often used to describe breathing or other rhythmic expansions and contractions.|The old man's bellowslike breathing filled the room. 老人的呼吸声像风箱一样充满了房间。|The fabric billowed in a bellowslike manner. 布料像风箱一样鼓了起来。|similar, resembling, analogous, akin|opposite, dissimilar, unlike|adjective
