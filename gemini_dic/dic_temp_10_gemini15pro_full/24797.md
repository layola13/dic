brotherlike: /ˈbrʌðərlaɪk/
adjective
像兄弟的；兄弟般的
Resembling or characteristic of a brother; fraternal.
He showed a brotherlike concern for his friend. 他对他的朋友表现出兄弟般的关心。

He has a brotherlike affection for his teammate. 他对他的队友有一种兄弟般的感情。

近义词: fraternal, brotherly
反义词: unfriendly, hostile
词性: adjective
