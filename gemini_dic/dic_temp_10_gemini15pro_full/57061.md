doubtlessness:/ˈdaʊtləsnəs/
noun
无疑；肯定
The state of being without doubt; certainty.
He spoke of the doubtlessness of their victory. 他谈到他们胜利的必然性。
Synonyms: certainty, assurance, conviction, confidence
Antonyms: doubt, uncertainty, skepticism
词性: 名词

doubtless:/ˈdaʊtləs/
adverb
无疑地；肯定地
Without doubt; certainly.
Doubtless, he will succeed.  他无疑会成功。
Synonyms: certainly, undoubtedly, assuredly, probably, likely
Antonyms: doubtfully, uncertainly, improbably, unlikely
词性: 副词
