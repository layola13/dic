behaviouristically: /bɪˈheɪvjərɪˈstɪkli/
| adv. | 行为主义地 | In a way that relates to behaviourism, a school of psychology that emphasizes observable behaviour. |  He approached the problem behaviouristically, focusing only on the child's actions and ignoring any underlying emotional issues. 他从行为主义的角度来处理这个问题，只关注孩子的行为，而忽略任何潜在的情绪问题。 |  
| synonym: behaviorally | antonym: cognitively, subjectively | word type: adverb

behaviouristically: /bɪˈheɪvjərɪˈstɪkli/
| adv. | 行为地 |  From the perspective of behaviourism. | The therapist interpreted the patient's actions behaviouristically, attributing them to learned responses to stimuli. 治疗师从行为主义的角度解释了病人的行为，将其归因于对刺激的习得反应。|
| synonym:  behaviorally | antonym:  mentally, subjectively | word type: adverb
