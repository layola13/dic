articulately:/ɑːrˈtɪkjələtli/
adv.|清晰地；明白地；表达清楚地|In a manner that is clear and easy to understand; using distinct syllables or words.|She spoke articulately about the complex issue, making it easy for everyone to grasp. 她清晰地阐述了这个复杂的问题，使每个人都容易理解。|clearly, distinctly, eloquently, fluently|inarticulately, unclearly, vaguely|副词

articulate:/ɑːrˈtɪkjəleɪt/
v.|清晰地表达；连接；明确有力地表达|To express (an idea or feeling) fluently and coherently.|He struggled to articulate his feelings about the loss. 他难以表达他对失去亲人的感受。|express, voice, utter, pronounce|mumble, slur, mutter|动词
articulate:/ɑːrˈtɪkjələt/
adj.|善于表达的；有关节的|Having or showing the ability to speak fluently and coherently.|She delivered an articulate and persuasive speech. 她发表了一场清晰且有说服力的演讲。|eloquent, fluent, expressive, communicative|inarticulate, mumbled, slurred|形容词
