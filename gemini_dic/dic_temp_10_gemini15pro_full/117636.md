megastores:/ˈmɛɡəˌstɔːrz/ /ˈmeɡəˌstɔːrz/
| n. |大型商店，大型零售店|A very large store, typically one of a chain, selling a wide variety of goods.| Many megastores offer a wide selection of products at competitive prices. 许多大型商店以具有竞争力的价格提供各种各样的产品。|  Megastores often have large parking lots and extended opening hours. 大型商店通常拥有大型停车场和延长的营业时间。 |superstores, hypermarkets, big-box stores|small shops, boutiques|noun
