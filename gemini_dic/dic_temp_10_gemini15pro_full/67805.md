eyesore:/ˈaɪˌsɔːr/| n.|眼中钉，碍眼的事物| something that is unpleasant to look at; an ugly thing that spoils the appearance of a place|例句：That abandoned factory is a real eyesore.  那座废弃的工厂真是个碍眼的东西。

例句：The graffiti on the walls is an eyesore. 墙上的涂鸦很不雅观。|近义词：blight, blemish, disfigurement|反义词:beauty, attraction
