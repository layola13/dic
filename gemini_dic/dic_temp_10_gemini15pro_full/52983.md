例句：He won the point with a clever dink over the net. 他用一个巧妙的轻扣球赢得了这一分。|近义词：tap, pat (for the sports meaning);  fool, idiot (for the informal meaning of "silly person")|反义词：(No direct antonym for DINK or the sports usage).  Genius, smart (for the informal meaning of "silly person")|词性:名词
