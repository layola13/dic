condoning:/kənˈdəʊnɪŋ/ | v. | 纵容；包庇；宽恕 |  Accepting or overlooking behaviour that is considered wrong. |  She refused to condone his violent behaviour. 她拒绝纵容他的暴力行为。 |  The school does not condone cheating. 学校不容忍作弊行为。 | synonyms: overlooking, excusing, pardoning, forgiving, accepting | antonyms: condemning, denouncing, censuring, criticizing, disapproving | verb

词源:
From Latin *condonare*, from *com-* (together) + *donare* (to give). Originally meant "to give jointly," later "to forgive a debt or offense."
