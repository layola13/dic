glidingly:/ˈɡlaɪdɪŋli/
adverb
流畅地，平滑地
Moving smoothly and effortlessly.
The skater moved glidingly across the ice.  滑冰者流畅地在冰面上滑行。
She spoke glidingly, her words flowing effortlessly. 她流畅地说着，话语毫不费力地流淌。
smoothly, effortlessly, gracefully
awkwardly, clumsily, jerkily
词性: 副词
