fusilli:/fuːˈsɪli/ /fjuːˈsɪli/
|n.|螺旋粉，螺旋面|A type of pasta that is formed into a spiral or corkscrew shape.|I made a fusilli salad for lunch. 我中午做了一份螺旋粉沙拉。|
|近义词:rotini, spirali, corkscrew pasta|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps straight pasta like spaghetti)|名词
