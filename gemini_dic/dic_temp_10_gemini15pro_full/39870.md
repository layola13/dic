constellation:/ˌkɑːnstəˈleɪʃən/ |n.| 星座；一群杰出人物；一系列（相关的想法、事物）|A group of stars forming a recognizable pattern that is traditionally named after its apparent form or identified with a mythological figure. A group of related ideas, things, or individuals.|例句：The constellation of Orion is easily recognizable.  猎户座很容易辨认。


例句：A constellation of Hollywood stars attended the premiere. 一群好莱坞明星出席了首映式。|近义词：cluster, collection, assembly, gathering|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
