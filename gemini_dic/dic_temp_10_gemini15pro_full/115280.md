manky:/ˈmæŋki/ | adj. | 肮脏的，破旧的，令人作呕的 | Disgusting, dirty, or in bad condition; unpleasant.  |  That old teddy bear is absolutely manky.  那只旧泰迪熊真是太脏了。 | Synonyms: dirty, filthy, grimy, grubby, scruffy, shabby, nasty | Antonyms: clean, pristine, immaculate | adjective

manky:/ˈmæŋki/ | adj. | (英国俚语) 糟糕的，不舒服的 | (British slang)  Bad, unwell, or in a bad mood. | I feel a bit manky today. 我今天感觉有点不舒服。| Synonyms: ill, unwell, poorly, rough, lousy | Antonyms: well, healthy, fine | adjective
