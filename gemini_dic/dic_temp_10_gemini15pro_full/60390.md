ehlite:/ˈeɪlaɪt/ | n. | 伊利石 | A group of clay minerals of general formula (K,H3O)(Al,Mg,Fe)2(Si,Al)4O10[(OH)2·H2O]. Ehlite is a hydrated calcium aluminum sulfate mineral.
