agriculturalists:/ˌæɡrɪˈkʌltʃərəlɪsts/
| n. | 农业学家；农学家 | People who work in agriculture, especially those who study farming scientifically.  |  Agriculturalists are experimenting with new ways to increase crop yields. 农业学家正在试验提高作物产量的新方法。 | synonyms: farmers, growers, cultivators | antonyms: consumers | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From agriculture + -ist.  "Agriculture" comes from the Latin "agricultura," from "ager" (field) and "cultura" (cultivation). "-ist" is a suffix denoting a person who practices or is skilled in something.
