counts:/kaʊnts/ (US), /kaʊnts/ (UK)| n. & v.|计数；数量；计算；认为；重要|Counts can be used as a noun or a verb, referring to the act of counting or the result of counting.  It can also mean to consider or be of importance.|The census counts everyone in the country. (人口普查统计了该国的所有人。)|Synonyms: tallies, totals, enumerates, figures, matters, signifies|Antonyms: disregards, ignores|Noun & Verb
