combed:/koʊmd/ (US) /kəʊmd/ (UK)| adj.|梳理过的，整齐的|Having been arranged with a comb; tidy and smooth.|She had long, combed hair. 她有一头梳理整齐的长发。|Her hair was neatly combed. 她的头发梳得整整齐齐。|近义词：groomed, neat, tidy, brushed|反义词：uncombed, tangled, messy|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Old English *cambed, past participle of *cambian "to comb," from Proto-Germanic *kambjan, from *kambaz "comb."
