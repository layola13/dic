meler: /ˈmiːlər/ | n. |磨坊主；磨坊工人|A person who owns or operates a mill, especially a grain mill. | The meler ground the wheat into flour. 磨坊主把小麦磨成了面粉。 | 近义词: miller, grinder | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

词源：From Middle English *meler, from Old English *melere, from Proto-Germanic *malijaną, from Proto-Indo-European *melh₂- (“to grind, crush”). Cognate with Dutch meler (“miller”), German Müller (“miller”), Swedish mjölnare (“miller”).
