duckiest:/ˈdʌkiɪst/| adj.|最可爱的，最像鸭子的|Superlative form of ducky: most ducky.  It means exceptionally cute, charming, or sweet, often in a way that evokes a small, fluffy duckling. It can also mean most resembling a duck.|例句：She has the duckiest little nose. 她有一个最可爱的小鼻子。

例句：That's the duckiest sweater I've ever seen. 那是我见过的最可爱的毛衣。|近义词：cutest, sweetest, most adorable, charming|反义词：ugliest, least attractive, homely|词性：形容词
