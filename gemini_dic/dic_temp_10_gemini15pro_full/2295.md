adept:/əˈdept/ | adj. n. | 熟练的；内行的；专家；能手 | Highly skilled and proficient. | She is adept at playing the piano. 她擅长弹钢琴。

He's adept at solving complex problems. 他擅长解决复杂的问题。 | 近义词：skilled, proficient, expert, masterful | 反义词：inept, unskilled, clumsy, awkward | 词性: 形容词, 名词

adept:/əˈdept/ | n. | 专家；能手 | A person who is highly skilled and proficient in something. | He is an adept in the art of negotiation. 他是谈判艺术的专家。

She's considered an adept at marketing. 她被认为是营销专家。| 近义词：expert, professional, master, specialist | 反义词：novice, beginner, amateur | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adeptus* "having attained," past participle of *adipisci* "to attain, obtain," from *ad* "to" + *apisci* "to reach, obtain."

USAGE->
形容词用法:

He is adept at using computers. = 他精通计算机。
She is an adept dancer. = 她是一位熟练的舞者。


名词用法:

He is an adept of the martial arts. = 他是武术大师。
The adepts of this ancient craft are few and far between. = 掌握这门古老技艺的专家寥寥无几。


PHRASE->
adept at = 擅长于
adept in = 精通
an adept of = ...的专家
adept with =  熟练使用
become adept at =  变得擅长
