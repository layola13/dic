calycifloral:/ˌkælɪsɪˈflɔːrəl/
adjective
萼状花冠的；合瓣花下位的
Having the petals and stamens attached to the calyx.
The rose family is a well-known example of a calycifloral plant group.  玫瑰科是萼状花冠植物群的一个著名例子。
Many members of the Rosaceae family exhibit calycifloral characteristics. 许多蔷薇科的成员都表现出萼状花冠的特征。
synonym: perigynous
antonym: hypogynous
word type: adjective
