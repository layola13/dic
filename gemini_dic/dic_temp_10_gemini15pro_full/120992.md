misclaims:/ˌmɪsˈkleɪmz/| n.| 错误的主张；虚报；误称|An incorrect or false claim.  The insurance company investigated the misclaims submitted after the hurricane.|例句：The company's financial report contained several misclaims about its profits. 这家公司的财务报告中包含几项关于其利润的虚报。

例句：Several misclaims were made about the product's effectiveness. 关于该产品功效的几项说法是错误的。|近义词：falsehoods, misrepresentations, inaccuracies, distortions, lies |反义词:truths, facts, accuracies|noun

词源: "mis-" (meaning "wrong" or "bad") + "claim" (meaning "to assert ownership of or demand")
