exsufflate:/ɛksˈsʌfleɪt/
verb
吹出；呼出
to blow out or expel (air or something else, such as mucus) from a body cavity
The doctor asked the patient to exsufflate forcefully to clear the airways. 医生让病人用力呼气以清除呼吸道。
The device exsufflates air to inflate the airbag.  该装置吹出空气以膨胀安全气囊。
近义词:expel, exhale, blow out
反义词:inhale, inspire, breathe in
词性:v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin exsufflāre (to blow out, puff out), from ex- (out) + sufflāre (to blow, puff).
