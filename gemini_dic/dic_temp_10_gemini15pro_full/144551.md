outvying:/ˌaʊtˈvaɪɪŋ/
verb
胜过，超过
surpassing or exceeding someone in achievement or success, often through competition.
He was always trying to outvie his brother in sports. 他总是试图在运动方面超过他的兄弟。
Synonyms: surpass, exceed, outdo, outstrip, eclipse
Antonyms:  underperform, lose to, fall behind
词性: 动词
