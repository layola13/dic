halolike:/ˈheɪloʊˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 像光晕的，像光环的 | Resembling a halo; having the appearance of a halo. |  The moon had a halolike ring around it. 月亮周围有一个像光环一样的环。| 近义词:aureole-like, nimbus-like, radiant | 反义词:dull, dim | 词性:形容词

词源: From "halo" + "-like." "Halo" comes from the Greek "halōs," meaning "disk of the sun or moon." "-like" is a suffix forming adjectives and meaning "resembling, similar to."
