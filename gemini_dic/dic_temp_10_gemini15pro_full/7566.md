annominate:/əˈnɒmɪneɪt/
verb
提名；指定
to nominate or appoint to an office or position.
The president will annominate a new Supreme Court justice. 总统将提名一位新的最高法院法官。
The committee annominated her for the award. 委员会提名她获奖。
Synonyms: nominate, appoint, designate, propose
Antonyms: dismiss, reject, remove
词性: 动词
