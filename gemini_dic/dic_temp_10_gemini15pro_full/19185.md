例句：The bewitched princess slept for a hundred years. 被施了魔法的公主沉睡了一百年。|近义词：enchanted, captivated, fascinated, charmed, spellbound|反义词：disenchanted, disillusioned, bored, uninterested|词性:形容词

PHRASE->
bewitched by = 被...迷住
utterly bewitched = 完全被迷住
bewitched and beguiled = 被迷住和欺骗
completely bewitched = 完全被迷住
bewitched by the beauty = 被美景迷住


词源: From be- + witch + -ed.  "be-" is a prefix meaning "thoroughly" or "completely." "witch" refers to a person who practices witchcraft. "-ed" is a suffix forming the past participle of verbs and also adjectives.  Therefore, "bewitched" literally means "completely affected by witchcraft."
