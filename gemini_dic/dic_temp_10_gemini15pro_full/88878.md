hoity-toityness:/ˌhɔɪtiˈtɔɪtinɪs/
noun
傲慢，自命不凡
the state or quality of being hoity-toity; haughty or snobbish behavior.
She displayed her hoity-toityness by refusing to speak to the staff. 她拒绝和员工说话，显露出她的傲慢。
近义词：haughtiness, arrogance, snobbishness, superciliousness
反义词：humility, modesty, humbleness
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From hoity-toity (adjective) + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  Hoity-toity itself is of uncertain origin, possibly imitative of haughty speech or behavior.  First recorded in the mid-18th century.
