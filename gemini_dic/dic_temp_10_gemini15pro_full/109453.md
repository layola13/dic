lepidine: /ˈlɛpɪdiːn/ | n. |  勒皮啶 | A crystalline alkaloid, C₁₀H₉N, obtained from coal tar and bone oil, and isomeric with quinoline.  It is used in making dyes and other organic compounds. | Lepidine is a useful building block in organic synthesis. 勒皮啶是有机合成中一种有用的结构单元。

Lepidine derivatives exhibit interesting biological activities. 勒皮啶衍生物表现出有趣的生物活性。| 近义词: 4-methylquinoline | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


词源: From Greek *λεπίς (lepís, “scale”) +‎ -idine, referring to the scaly appearance of certain lepidine salts.
