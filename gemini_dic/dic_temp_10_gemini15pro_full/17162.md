battlemented:/ˈbætəlməntɪd/
adjective
城垛式的，有城垛的
furnished with battlements; having battlements.  A battlement is a parapet at the top of a wall, usually of a fort or castle, that has regularly spaced gaps for shooting through.
The castle walls were high and battlemented. 城堡的墙壁高耸，筑有城垛。
The battlemented towers offered a commanding view of the surrounding countryside.  这些有城垛的塔楼可以俯瞰周围的乡村。

近义词:crenellated, fortified, castellated
反义词:undefended, unfortified

词性:adjective
词源:From battlement + -ed.  Battlement itself comes from Old French *batelment, from bataillier "to fortify."
