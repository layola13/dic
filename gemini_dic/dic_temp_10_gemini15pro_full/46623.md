dammit:/ˈdæmɪt/| int. | 该死；见鬼 | Expressing annoyance, frustration, or anger.  A euphemism for "damn it". | Dammit, I forgot my keys! 该死，我忘了带钥匙了！| 近义词：damn, blast, shoot, darn | 反义词: (No direct antonym for an interjection expressing frustration) | interjection
Etymology: Contraction of "damn it," with "damn" originating from the Latin "damnare" (to condemn).
