mansuetude:/ˈmænswiːtjuːd/ /ˈmænswjətjuːd/
n.|温顺，驯服，柔和|The quality of being gentle and tame; meekness.|Her mansuetude made her a beloved figure in the community. 她的温顺使她成为社区中受爱戴的人物。|近义词：gentleness, meekness, mildness, docility|反义词：ferocity, aggressiveness, wildness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *mansuetudo*, from Latin *mansuetus* (“tame, gentle, mild”), from *manus* (“hand”) + *suetus* (“accustomed, used to”).  The idea is of an animal accustomed to the hand, hence tame.
