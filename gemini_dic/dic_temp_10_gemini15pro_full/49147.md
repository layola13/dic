dejectedness:/dɪˈdʒɛktɪdnəs/| n. |沮丧，颓丧|The state of being dejected; low spirits.  A feeling of sadness and discouragement.|Her dejectedness was evident in her slumped posture and quiet voice. 她的沮丧从她颓丧的姿势和低沉的声音中显而易见。|近义词：despondency, depression, discouragement, gloom, melancholy|反义词：elation, joy, happiness, cheerfulness|词性:noun
Etymology: From "dejected" + "-ness". "Dejected" comes from the Latin "deicere" meaning "to throw down".
