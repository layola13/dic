curtailers:/kərˈteɪlərz/
n.
限制者，缩短者，削减者
People who curtail something; those who reduce or limit something.
The budget curtailers were unpopular with the department heads. 预算削减者不受部门主管的欢迎。

近义词: restrictors, reducers, limiters
反义词: expanders, increasers, promoters
词性: 名词 (plural)


curtail:/kərˈteɪl/
vt.
缩减，限制，削减
To reduce or limit something, especially something desirable.
The government curtailed spending on education. 政府削减了教育支出。

近义词: reduce, restrict, limit, decrease
反义词: increase, expand, extend, promote
词性: 动词
