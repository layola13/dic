cackerel: /ˈmækərəl/ | n. | 鲭鱼；马鲛鱼 | A fast-swimming, edible fish of the North Atlantic and other seas. |  We had grilled mackerel for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了烤鲭鱼。 | Synonyms: tuna, herring | Antonyms: None | Noun

cackerel: /ˈmækərəl/ | adj. | 斑驳的，有鲭鱼斑纹的 | Resembling the markings of a mackerel. |  The sky was a mackerel sky, full of small, high clouds. 天空是鲭鱼天空，充满了细碎的高云。 | Synonyms: mottled, dappled | Antonyms: plain, uniform | Adjective
