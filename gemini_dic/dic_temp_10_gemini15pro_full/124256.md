mostly:/ˈmoʊstli/| adv.|主要地，大部分地，通常|Mainly, for the most part, generally.  It indicates that something is true or happens most of the time, but not always.|I mostly eat at home, but sometimes I go out to restaurants. 我主要在家吃饭，但有时也出去餐馆。|Near synonyms: chiefly, primarily, mainly, largely, predominantly|Near antonyms: rarely, seldom, infrequently|adverb
