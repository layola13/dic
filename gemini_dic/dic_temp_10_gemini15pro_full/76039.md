furler:/ˈfɜːlə(r)/| n.|收帆器；卷帆装置|A device for rolling up a sail on a spar or stay.|The sailor used the furler to quickly reef the sail in the rising wind.  水手用收帆器快速地卷起帆以应对越来越大的风。|近义词：reefing gear|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 unfurling device  展开装置)|noun
