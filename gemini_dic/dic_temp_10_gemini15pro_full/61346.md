embellish: /ɪmˈbɛlɪʃ/ /ɛmˈbɛlɪʃ/ | vt. | 装饰；润色；对…加以渲染 | To make something more attractive or interesting by adding details, often ones that are not true. |  She embellished her story with details about her exciting travels. 她用她激动人心的旅行细节润色了她的故事。

He embellished his resume to make himself look more qualified. 他润色了他的简历，让自己看起来更有资格。

近义词: adorn, decorate, enhance, ornament, exaggerate, elaborate | 反义词: simplify, understate, minimize | 词性: verb (动词)
