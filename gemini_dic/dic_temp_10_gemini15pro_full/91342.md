hydroplane:/ˈhaɪdrəˌpleɪn/| vi. n.|水上滑行，水上飞机|To skim or slide on a wet surface, especially a road surface, because of a thin layer of water between the tires and the road, resulting in loss of control.|The car hydroplaned on the wet road and crashed into the barrier. 汽车在湿滑的路面上打滑，撞上了护栏。|Near synonyms: aquaplane, skid, slide|Opposite: grip, traction|verb

hydroplane:/ˈhaɪdrəˌpleɪn/| n.|水上飞机|An airplane designed to take off from and land on water.|The hydroplane landed gracefully on the lake. 水上飞机优雅地降落在湖面上。|Near synonyms: seaplane, floatplane|Opposite: airplane, aeroplane (general term)|noun
