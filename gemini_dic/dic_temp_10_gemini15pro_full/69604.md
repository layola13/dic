femalist:/ˈfɛməlɪst/
n.
女性至上主义者; 极端女权主义者
A person who believes in the superiority of women.  A person who advocates for the supremacy of women over men.
She was labeled a femalist by her opponents due to her radical views. 她的对手因为她激进的观点给她贴上了女性至上主义者的标签。
Some people consider the term "femalist" to be derogatory.  有些人认为“女性至上主义者”这个词带有贬义。
近义词：misandrist, sexist (if used generally)
反义词：misogynist
词性：noun
