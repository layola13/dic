decentralist:/diːˈsɛntrəlaɪst/ | n. |去中心化主义者； 分散主义者 | A person who advocates decentralization, especially of political or economic power. |  The decentralist argued that local communities should have more autonomy.  去中心化主义者认为地方社区应该有更多的自治权。| synonyms: autonomist, federalist | antonyms: centralist, authoritarian | noun

decentralize:/diːˈsɛntrəlaɪz/ | v. | 去中心化；分散 | To distribute the administrative powers or functions of (a central authority) among several local authorities. | The company decided to decentralize its operations to improve efficiency. 公司决定分散经营以提高效率。| synonyms: distribute, disperse, devolve | antonyms: centralize, consolidate | verb

decentralized:/diːˈsɛntrəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 去中心化的；分散的|  Having administrative powers or functions distributed among several local authorities. | The company has a decentralized organizational structure.  这家公司有一个分散的组织结构。| synonyms: distributed, dispersed, diffused | antonyms: centralized, concentrated | adjective
