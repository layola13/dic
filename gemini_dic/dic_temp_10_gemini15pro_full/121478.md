mislabeling:/ˌmɪsˈleɪbəlɪŋ/ | n. | 错误标记，贴错标签 | The mislabeling of the product led to a massive recall. 产品的错误标签导致大规模召回。| Example: The mislabeling of the medication could have serious consequences.  药物的错误标签可能会造成严重后果。 | Synonyms: misbranding, mismarking | Antonyms: correct labeling, accurate labeling | noun
