lavolta:/ləˈvɔːltə/ | n. | 拉沃尔塔舞, 一种快速的旋转舞 | A lively dance for two persons, consisting of quick, turning steps.  It was popular in the late 18th and early 19th centuries. |  They danced the lavolta with great enthusiasm. 他们热情地跳着拉沃尔塔舞。| 近义词: waltz, galop | 反义词: None | noun

lavolta:/ləˈvɔːltə/ | n. | (体操中的)空翻，腾跃 | An acrobatic leap or turn. This meaning is less common than the dance definition. | The gymnast performed a stunning lavolta. 体操运动员表演了一个惊艳的空翻。| 近义词: somersault, flip | 反义词: None | noun
