hyperconformities:/ˌhaɪpərˌkənˈfɔːrmɪtiz/
|n.|超整合面；超整合不整合|A surface of unconformity, where a considerable span of geologic time is missing, often combined with significant erosional relief.|The hyperconformity represents a time gap of millions of years.|Synonyms: unconformity, nonconformity|Antonyms: conformity, concordance|noun
