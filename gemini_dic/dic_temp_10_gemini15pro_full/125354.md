multilobe:/ˈmʌltiˌloʊb/ | adj. | 多裂的，多瓣的 | Having multiple lobes or rounded divisions. | The multilobe leaf of the oak tree is easily recognizable. 橡树的多裂叶子很容易辨认。| The doctor examined the patient's multilobe liver. 医生检查了病人多叶的肝脏。 | multilobed, multipartite | simple, entire | adjective

Etymology:
From "multi-" (meaning "many") + "lobe" (from Latin "lobus," meaning "pod, husk, lobe").
