overingenious:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʒiːniəs/
adjective
过分精巧的；过于巧妙的；过分机智的
Excessively or unnecessarily ingenious; too clever or elaborate.

The plot of the movie was overingenious, with twists and turns that ultimately made it confusing. 这部电影的情节过于复杂，曲折反复，最终让人感到困惑。

Her overingenious solution to the problem ended up creating more problems than it solved. 她对这个问题过于精巧的解决方案最终制造了比它解决的更多的问题。

近义词:contrived, elaborate, overwrought, oversubtle
反义词:simple, straightforward, artless


词性: adjective


词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "ingenious" (from Latin "ingeniosus," meaning "gifted with genius, clever").
