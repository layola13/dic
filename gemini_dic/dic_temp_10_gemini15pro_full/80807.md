gothicize: /ˈɡɒθɪsaɪz/ (US) /ˈɡɒθɪsaɪz/ (UK)| v. |使哥特式化，使具有哥特风格|To give something a Gothic character or appearance; to make something resemble Gothic architecture or art.|The architect gothicized the building by adding pointed arches and gargoyles. 建筑师通过添加尖拱和滴水嘴使建筑哥特化。|  The author gothicized the story with dark, mysterious elements. 作者用黑暗、神秘的元素使故事哥特化。 |近义词：medievalize, archaize|反义词: modernize, contemporize|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Gothic + -ize.  Gothic refers to the East Germanic tribes known as the Goths.  The term later became associated with a style of medieval architecture characterized by pointed arches, ribbed vaults, and flying buttresses.  The suffix "-ize" means "to make or become like."
