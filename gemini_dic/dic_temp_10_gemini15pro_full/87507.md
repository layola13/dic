heteroclite:/ˌhɛtərəˈklaɪt/| adj.|异类的，不规则的|Deviating from common forms or rules; irregular or abnormal, especially in grammar (inflection).  Referring to a word that does not follow the usual rules of inflection in its language.|The botanist discovered a heteroclite plant that didn't fit into any known family.  这位植物学家发现了一种不属于任何已知科的异类植物。|Synonyms: anomalous, atypical, aberrant, irregular, unconventional|Antonyms: typical, regular, normal, conventional|adjective
