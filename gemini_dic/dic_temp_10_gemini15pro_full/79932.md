gnomological:/ˌnɒməˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
格言学的；箴言学的
Relating to gnomology, the study of maxims or proverbs.
Gnomologic literature often explores universal truths about human nature. 格言学文献经常探讨关于人性的普遍真理。
The professor's gnomological analysis of the proverb revealed its deeper meaning.  教授对这句谚语的格言学分析揭示了它更深层的含义。
Near Synonyms: proverbial, aphoristic, sententious
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider) unexpressive, inarticulate
Word type: adjective
