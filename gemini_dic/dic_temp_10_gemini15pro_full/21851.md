bodice-ripping:/ˈbɒdɪs ˌrɪpɪŋ/
adjective
撕扯紧身胸衣的；激情四射的；浪漫刺激的
Characterized by scenes of passionate or melodramatic romance, often involving the tearing of clothing.  This term is often used humorously or ironically to describe overly dramatic or clichéd romantic fiction.
She enjoys reading bodice-ripping romance novels. 她喜欢读激情四射的浪漫小说。
近义词: passionate, melodramatic, steamy, romantic
反义词:  understated, subtle, realistic
