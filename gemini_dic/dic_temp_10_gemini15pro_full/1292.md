accountancy: /əˈkaʊntənsi/
n.
会计学；会计工作
The systematic recording and reporting of financial transactions of a business.
She studied accountancy at university. 她大学学习会计学。

He works in accountancy. 他从事会计工作。

近义词: accounting, bookkeeping
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps mismanagement of finances)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "aconter" (to count, reckon) which comes from Latin "computare" (to count, sum up).  The suffix "-ancy" denotes a state or quality.
