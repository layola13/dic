launcestons:/ˈlɔːnsɛstənz/ | n. |朗塞斯顿（澳大利亚塔斯马尼亚州一城市）|Launceston is a city in the north of Tasmania, Australia. It is the second largest city in the state after Hobart.|例句：Launceston is known for its beautiful parks and gardens. 朗塞斯顿以其美丽的公园和花园而闻名。

例句：We visited Launceston during our trip to Tasmania. 我们在塔斯马尼亚之旅期间参观了朗塞斯顿。

近义词:city, town (广义上的近义词，指城市或城镇)|反义词:|词性:名词

词源:  The city's name comes from Launceston, Cornwall, England, the birthplace of William Paterson, Lieutenant Governor of Van Diemen's Land (now Tasmania) from 1804 to 1808.
