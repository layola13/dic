overopinionatedly:/ˌoʊvərəˈpɪnjəneɪtɪdli/| adv. |过于武断地，固执己见地|In a manner characterized by expressing opinions too strongly or frequently, often without sufficient knowledge or consideration for others' views. | He overopinionatedly dismissed all suggestions that contradicted his own. 他武断地驳回了所有与他意见相左的建议。|Synonyms: dogmatically, opinionatedly, assertively, peremptorily | Antonyms: open-mindedly, receptively, humbly, modestly | adverb
