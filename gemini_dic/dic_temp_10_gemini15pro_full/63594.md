envois:/ˈɑːnvɔɪz/ | n. | 作品; 寄赠物; 赠诗; 附诗; |A work of literature, art, or music, especially one dedicated to someone.  A thing that is sent, especially a consignment of goods. |  Her latest envoi is a collection of poems about nature. 她最新的作品是一本关于自然的诗集。 | The envoi of coffee beans arrived from Colombia. 这批咖啡豆是从哥伦比亚运来的。 |  Near synonyms:  submission, offering, dedication, gift, consignment, shipment | Near antonyms:  receipt | noun
