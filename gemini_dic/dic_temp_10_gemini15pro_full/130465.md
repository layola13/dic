nonabstemiously: /ˌnɒnæbˈstiːmiəsli/ | adv. | 不节制地，放纵地 |  In a manner that is not characterized by abstinence or moderation, especially in eating or drinking.  It describes indulging in something, typically food or drink, excessively or without restraint. | He ate nonabstemiously, indulging in every dish on the buffet. 他毫无节制地吃着，自助餐上的每道菜都尝了个遍。| The guests feasted nonabstemiously, enjoying the abundance of food and wine. 客人们尽情享用着丰盛的食物和酒水，毫不节制。 | 近义词: immoderately, excessively, intemperately | 反义词: abstemiously, temperately, moderately | 词性: 副词

词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin *non < *ne oinom "not one."
abstemious (adjective): From Latin abstemius "sober, temperate," from ab- "away from" + temetum "strong drink," related to temulentus "drunken."
-ly (suffix):  Forms adverbs from adjectives.
