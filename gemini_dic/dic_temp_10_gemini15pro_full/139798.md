octet:/ˈɒktɛt/ /ɑːkˈtɛt/ | n. |八位字节；八重奏；八行诗| A group of eight bits or a byte, especially used in computing.  Also refers to a group of eight people or things, particularly in music or poetry. | Example 1:  The data is transmitted in octets. 数据以八位字节的形式传输。

Example 2: The octet performed beautifully at the concert. 八重奏在音乐会上表演得非常出色。 | 近义词: byte (in computing), group, ensemble | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


octet:/ˈɒktɛt/ /ɑːkˈtɛt/ | adj. |八位的；八个一组的 | Composed of eight parts or members. | Example 1: The system uses an octet-based network protocol.  该系统使用基于八位的网络协议。
