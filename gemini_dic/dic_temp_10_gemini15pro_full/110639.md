limps:/lɪmps/| v. | 跛行，蹒跚而行 | walks with difficulty because of an injured leg or foot |  The old dog limps painfully on its back leg. 老狗后腿受伤，走起来很痛苦。

The runner limped off the field after twisting his ankle.  跑步者扭伤脚踝后一瘸一拐地离开了场地。| 近义词：hobble, stumble | 反义词: stride, walk |词性:动词

limp:/lɪmp/| n. | 跛行 | a way of walking in which one leg is hurt and makes it difficult to walk | He has a noticeable limp. 他走路明显跛。

She walked with a slight limp after the accident. 事故发生后，她走路有点跛。| 近义词：hobble | 反义词: - | 词性:名词

limp:/lɪmp/| adj. | 柔软的，无力的 | lacking stiffness or firmness | The lettuce was old and limp. 生菜又老又软。

He felt limp and exhausted after the marathon. 马拉松结束后，他感到浑身无力，精疲力尽。| 近义词：flaccid, drooping | 反义词: firm, stiff | 词性:形容词
