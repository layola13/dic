chiefrie:/ˈtʃiːfriː/
n.
chiefdom; leadership; domain; territory
A territory or domain of a chief.  The area over which a chief has authority.  The position or dignity of a chief.
The chiefrie of Clan Donald spanned across the western isles and highlands of Scotland.  麦克唐纳家族的领地横跨苏格兰西部岛屿和高地。
