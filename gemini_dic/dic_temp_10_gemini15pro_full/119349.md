microchronometer:/ˌmaɪkroʊkrəˈnɒmɪtər/ | n. |微秒表，精密计时器 | An instrument for measuring very small intervals of time, often to the nearest microsecond.  | Example 1: The scientist used a microchronometer to measure the duration of the chemical reaction. 科学家用微秒表测量化学反应的持续时间。

Example 2:  The microchronometer recorded the athlete's time to the nearest millionth of a second.  微秒表记录了运动员的时间，精确到百万分之一秒。 | Synonym:  chronograph, high-speed timer, precision timer | Antonym:  N/A | noun
