cornucopian:/ˌkɔːrnjʊˈkoʊpiən/ | adj. |  丰饶的，大量的 | Relating to or resembling a cornucopia; abundant, overflowing. | The market offered a cornucopian array of fresh produce.  市场提供了琳琅满目的新鲜农产品。| Synonyms: abundant, plentiful, copious, lavish, profuse | Antonyms: scarce, meager, scanty, sparse | adjective

cornucopia:/ˌkɔːrnjʊˈkoʊpiə/ | n. |  丰饶角，大量 |  A horn overflowing with fruit, flowers, etc., symbolizing abundance; an abundant supply of good things of a specified kind. | The holiday table was a cornucopia of delicious food. 节日餐桌上摆满了美味佳肴。| Synonyms: abundance, profusion, bounty, plethora | Antonyms: scarcity, lack, dearth, paucity | noun
