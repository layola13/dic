overkicked: /ˌoʊvərˈkɪkt/
verb
过度踢，踢得过高（或过远）
To kick (a ball) higher or farther than intended.
The kicker overkicked the ball, and it went out of bounds.  踢球者把球踢得过高，球出了界。
overkicked the kickoff and it went out of bounds.  开球踢得过高，出了界。
近义词: mishit, miskick
反义词: underkick
