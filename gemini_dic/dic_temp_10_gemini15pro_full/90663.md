humble:/ˈhʌmbəl/ (US) /ˈhʌmbl/ (UK)| adj. vt.| 谦逊的，谦虚的；简陋的；（使）谦逊，（使）卑下 |  Having or showing a modest or low estimate of one's own importance. | He gave a humble apology for his mistake. 他为自己的错误谦逊地道歉了。 |  modest, unassuming, meek | arrogant, proud, haughty | adjective
humble:/ˈhʌmbəl/ (US) /ˈhʌmbl/ (UK)| vt.|  使谦卑，使感到卑微 |  Lower (someone) in dignity or importance. |  The defeat humbled the arrogant champion.  这场失败使傲慢的冠军变得谦逊了。 |  humiliate, abase, subdue | exalt, elevate, aggrandize | verb
