impulsion:/ɪmˈpʌlʃən/| n.|冲动，推动，驱使|The impulsion to create is strong within her. 她内心深处有着强烈的创作冲动。|例句：He felt an impulsion to help the injured man. 他感到一种想帮助那个受伤男人的冲动。

例句：She acted on impulsion, without thinking. 她一时冲动就行动了，没有思考。|近义词：impulse, urge, drive, motivation|反义词：hesitation, reluctance|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *impulsio, from Latin impulsus, past participle of impellere (“to push against, strike against, drive forward”), from in (“in, on, against”) + pellere (“to push, drive”).
