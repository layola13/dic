fam:/fæm/
|n.|家人，朋友（非正式）|Close friends, family, or people who are considered like family.  A shortened, informal version of "family."|例句：I'm going to the movies with my fam tonight. 今晚我要和我的家人（或朋友）一起去看电影。

|例句：My fam always supports me. 我的家人（或朋友）一直支持我。
|近义词：family, friends, crew, posse|反义词:enemies, strangers|词性:名词
