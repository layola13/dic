exostome:/ˈeksəˌstoʊm/| n. |外口；外胚孔|An opening in the outer layer of an embryo, such as the opening of the archenteron in the gastrula stage.|The exostome eventually develops into the anus in deuterostomes.|近义词:blastopore|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "archenteron" as the internal opening)|词性:noun
