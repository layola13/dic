bilin:/ˈbaɪlɪn/ | n. |胆汁，胆液；坏脾气，怨恨|A bitter greenish-brown alkaline fluid that aids digestion and is secreted by the liver and stored in the gallbladder.  Also, figuratively, anger or resentment.|例句：The bilin secreted by the liver is essential for fat digestion. 肝脏分泌的胆汁对脂肪消化至关重要。

例句：His bilin rose at the perceived injustice. 他对感觉到的不公正感到愤怒。|近义词：bile, gall, bitterness, resentment |反义词：goodwill, kindness|词性:名词
