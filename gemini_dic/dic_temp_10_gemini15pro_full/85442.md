hash-over:/ˈhæʃ ˌoʊvər/ |n.| 详细讨论；仔细审查；彻底检查 | A thorough discussion or examination of something, often a problem or disagreement. | We need to have a hash-over about the budget before we finalize it. 我们需要在最终确定预算之前对预算进行详细讨论。|  They had a long hash-over about the project's next steps. 他们就项目的下一步计划进行了长时间的仔细审查。 | 近义词: discussion, review, examination, analysis | 反义词: glance, skim | 词性: 名词
