coppice: /ˈkɒpɪs/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpɪs/ (US)| n. |矮林，萌生林|An area of woodland managed by cutting back trees and shrubs to ground level periodically, so that they produce new shoots from the base.|The farmer planted a coppice of willow for basket making. 农民种植了一片柳树矮林，用来制作篮子。|Synonyms: thicket, underwood, brushwood|Antonyms: forest, woodland|noun


coppice: /ˈkɒpɪs/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpɪs/ (US)| vt. |将…矮林化经营；修剪成矮林|To cut back (trees or shrubs) to ground level periodically to encourage new growth.|They coppiced the hazel trees every few years.  他们每隔几年就把榛树修剪成矮林。|Synonyms: pollard, trim, prune|Antonyms: plant, grow|verb
