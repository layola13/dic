compressibleness:/kəmˌpresəˈbɪlnəs/| n. |可压缩性；压缩性|The quality of being compressible; the capacity of being compressed.  The compressibleness of air allows it to be used in pneumatic tools.|例句：The compressibleness of the gas allowed it to be stored in a smaller container.  这种气体的可压缩性使其能够储存在更小的容器中。

例句：The compressibleness of the sponge made it ideal for cleaning spills.  海绵的可压缩性使其成为清洁溢出物的理想选择。|近义词：compressibility, squeezability|反义词：incompressibility, rigidity|词性: noun
