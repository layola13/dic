pacifist:/ˈpæsɪfɪst/| n. adj.|和平主义者；和平主义的|A person who believes that war and violence are always wrong and that all disputes should be settled peacefully. |例句：He was a committed pacifist all his life. 他一生都是一个坚定不移的和平主义者。

例句：Her pacifist beliefs prevented her from joining the army. 她的和平主义信仰使她无法参军。|近义词：peacemaker, dove, anti-militarist|反义词：warmonger, hawk, aggressor|词性: 名词，形容词

词源:  From French *pacifiste*, from Latin *pacificus* (“peace-making”) + *-ist*.
