anaglyptic:/ˌænəˈɡlɪptɪk/
adjective
雕刻的，浮雕的
Relating to or resembling anaglyphs; carved in low relief.
The museum displayed several anaglyptic friezes depicting scenes from ancient mythology.  博物馆展出了几幅描绘古代神话场景的浅浮雕饰带。
The artist created an anaglyptic portrait of the king. 艺术家创作了一幅国王的浮雕肖像。
近义词: embossed, carved, sculptured
反义词: smooth, flat
词性: adjective

anaglyph:/ˈænəɡlɪf/
noun
浮雕，雕刻
A carving or ornament in low relief.  A picture produced by anaglyph.
The anaglyph of a bird was beautifully carved into the wooden door. 鸟的浮雕被精美地雕刻在木门上。
We watched a 3D movie using anaglyph glasses. 我们用立体眼镜看了一部3D电影。
近义词: carving, sculpture, embossment
反义词: intaglio
词性: noun
