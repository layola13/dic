nonsolar:/ˌnɒnˈsəʊlə(r)/ | adj. | 非太阳的；非太阳能的 | Not relating to or derived from the sun; not powered by solar energy. |  Example: Nonsolar planets are those that do not orbit a star.  非太阳行星是指不绕恒星运行的行星。 | Example:  Nonsolar energy sources are becoming increasingly important. 非太阳能能源正变得越来越重要。 | Synonyms: non-solar, extrasolar | Antonyms: solar | word type: adjective
