monotone:/ˈmɒnətəʊn/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnətəʊn/ (US)| n. adj.|单调，单音调|a sound that stays on the same note without varying in pitch;  lacking in variety and interest; tedious|The lecturer spoke in a monotone, and the students struggled to stay awake. 演讲者用单调的声音说话，学生们很难保持清醒。|近义词:droning, tedious, boring, dull|反义词: varied, diverse, interesting, lively|noun, adjective

monotonic:/ˌmɒnəˈtɒnɪk/ (UK) /ˌmɑːnəˈtɑːnɪk/ (US)| adj.|单调的，单一的| (of a function or quantity) varying in such a way that it either never decreases or never increases.|The function is monotonic increasing. 这个函数是单调递增的。|近义词:unchanging, consistent, unvarying|反义词: fluctuating, varying, oscillating|adjective
