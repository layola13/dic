hybridisms:/ˈhaɪbrɪdɪzəmz/ | n. | 杂交词，混合词 |A word or form that is created by combining elements from different languages or dialects, or different registers within the same language.  | The word "smog" (from "smoke" and "fog") is a classic example of a hybridism.  烟雾（来自“smoke”和“fog”）是杂交词的一个典型例子。 | synonyms: blends, portmanteau words, mixes | antonyms: N/A | noun

hybridism:/ˈhaɪbrɪdɪzəm/ | n. | 杂交，混合 |The process or result of combining different elements, especially in language. | The novel displays a curious hybridism of styles. 这部小说展现了一种奇特的风格混合。 | synonyms: mixture, blend, combination | antonyms: purity, separation | noun
