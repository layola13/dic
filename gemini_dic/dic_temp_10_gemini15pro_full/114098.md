magnify:/ˈmæɡnɪfaɪ/ /ˈmæɡnəˌfaɪ/| v.| 放大，扩大，夸大 | To make something appear larger than it is, especially with a lens or microscope; to increase the importance or effect of something. |  She used a magnifying glass to examine the stamp. 她用放大镜检查邮票。

The media tends to magnify the dangers of air travel. 媒体倾向于夸大航空旅行的危险。 | synonyms: enlarge, amplify, augment, boost, exaggerate, heighten, inflate, intensify  | antonyms: diminish, minimize, reduce, shrink, understate | verb
