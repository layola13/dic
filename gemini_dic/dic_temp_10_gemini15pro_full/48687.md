defer:/dɪˈfɜːr/
verb
推迟，延期
to postpone something until a later time
例句：The decision has been deferred until next week.  这个决定被推迟到下周。

例句：They deferred the meeting to a later date. 他们把会议推迟到以后的日期。

近义词：postpone, delay, adjourn
反义词：expedite, hasten, advance
词性：v.


defer:/dɪˈfɜːr/
verb
遵从，听从
to yield to someone's wishes, opinion, or authority
例句：I defer to your judgment on this matter. 在这件事上，我遵从你的判断。

例句：He deferred to his parents' wishes. 他遵从了父母的意愿。

近义词：yield, submit, accede
反义词：resist, oppose, defy
词性：v.
