merchant:/ˈmɜːrtʃənt/ (US) /ˈmɜːtʃənt/ (UK)| n. | 商人，店主 | A person or company involved in wholesale trade, especially one dealing with foreign countries or supplying merchandise to a particular trade. | The merchants of Venice were renowned for their wealth. 威尼斯商人以其财富而闻名。|  The silk merchant displayed his wares.  丝绸商人展示了他的商品。 |近义词：trader, dealer, retailer, wholesaler | 反义词: customer, buyer | noun

merchant:/ˈmɜːrtʃənt/ (US) /ˈmɜːtʃənt/ (UK)| adj. | 商业的，商人的 | Of or relating to merchants or trade. | The merchant navy plays a vital role in international trade. 商船队在国际贸易中扮演着至关重要的角色。| The merchant class held considerable power in the city. 商人阶级在这个城市拥有相当大的权力。|近义词：commercial, trading | 反义词: N/A | adjective
