misstamping:/ˌmɪsˈstæmpɪŋ/
noun
错误盖章，盖错戳
The act of stamping something incorrectly, such as a document or letter.
The misstamping of the visa led to his denial of entry.  签证盖错戳导致他被拒绝入境。
misprinting, mismarking
