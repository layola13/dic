cloisterly: /ˈklɔɪstərli/ (US), /ˈklɔɪstəli/ (UK) | adj. | 修道院的；隐居的；与世隔绝的 | Suggestive of a cloister; secluded from the world; solitary. |  The scholar lived a cloisterly life, dedicated to his studies.  这位学者过着隐居的生活，专注于他的研究。|  The library had a cloisterly atmosphere, quiet and conducive to contemplation. 图书馆有一种修道院般的气氛，安静且有助于沉思。 | 近义词: secluded, reclusive, monastic, solitary, withdrawn | 反义词: gregarious, sociable, outgoing, extroverted | 词性: adjective

词源: From *cloister* + *-ly*. *Cloister* comes from Old French *cloistre*, ultimately from Latin *claustrum* ("lock, bolt, barrier").
