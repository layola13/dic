inexpedient:/ˌɪnɪkˈspiːdiənt/ | adj. | 不明智的，不适宜的，不方便的 | Not suitable or advisable; not practical or wise.  It refers to something that is not a good idea under the current circumstances, often because it would cause problems or be ineffective. |  It was inexpedient to raise the issue at that moment. 当时提出这个问题是不明智的。| synonyms: inadvisable, unwise, imprudent, impolitic, inappropriate | antonyms: expedient, advisable, wise, prudent, appropriate | adjective
