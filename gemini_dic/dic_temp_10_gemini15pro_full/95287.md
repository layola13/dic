incarcerating:/ɪnˈkɑːrsəreɪtɪŋ/
verb
监禁；禁闭
confining someone in prison or another place they cannot leave.
The judge sentenced him to five years incarcerating for armed robbery. 法官判他五年监禁，罪名是持械抢劫。
incarcerating: He is incarcerating in a high-security prison. 他被关押在一个高度设防的监狱里。
synonyms: imprisoning, confining, jailing, detaining
antonyms: releasing, freeing, liberating
