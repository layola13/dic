colorimeter:/ˌkʌlərˈɪmɪtər/ | n. | 比色计，色度计 | An instrument used to measure the intensity of color, often in terms of three primary colors. |  The chemist used a colorimeter to analyze the concentration of the dye solution.  化学家使用比色计分析染料溶液的浓度。| synonyms: color comparator, color meter, tintometer | antonyms: N/A | noun

colorimeter:/ˌkʌlərˈɪmɪtər/ | n. | 比色计，色度计 | An instrument for measuring the absorbance or transmission of light through a solution to determine the concentration of a substance. | The lab technician used a colorimeter to determine the iron content in the blood sample.  实验室技术员使用比色计来确定血液样本中的铁含量。| synonyms: photometer, spectrophotometer | antonyms: N/A | noun
