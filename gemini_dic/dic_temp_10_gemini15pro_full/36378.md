coinheritor:/ˌkoʊɪnˈhɛrɪtər/| n. |共同继承人|A person who inherits something jointly with another person or other people.|例句：She and her brother were coinheritors of their father's estate. 她和她哥哥是父亲遗产的共同继承人。

例句：The coinheritors decided to sell the property and split the proceeds. 共同继承人决定出售房产并平分收益。|近义词：joint heir, fellow heir|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
