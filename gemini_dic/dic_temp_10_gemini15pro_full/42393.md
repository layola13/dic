countercampaigns:/ˈkaʊntərˌkæmˈpeɪnz/
noun
反宣传活动，对抗运动
Organized efforts to oppose or counteract another campaign, typically a political or advertising campaign.
The company launched a series of countercampaigns to combat the negative publicity.  这家公司发起了一系列反宣传活动来对抗负面宣传。
The political party initiated several countercampaigns to challenge their opponent's claims. 该政党发起了一些反宣传活动来挑战对手的主张。
Synonyms: countermeasures, counteroffensive, counterattack
Antonyms: campaigns, promotion, support
