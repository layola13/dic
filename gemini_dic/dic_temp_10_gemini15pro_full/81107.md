grammarians:/ɡrəˈmɛəriənz/
n.
语法学家；语法研究者
Experts in grammar, often involved in defining and describing the rules of a language.
The grammarians debated the proper use of the subjunctive mood. 语法学家们就虚拟语气的正确用法进行了辩论。
synonyms: linguists, philologists
antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps) language amateurs, non-linguists
noun
