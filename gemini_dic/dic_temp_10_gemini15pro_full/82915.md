gummer:/ˈɡʌmər/| n.|牙龈；齿龈；无牙老人；嚼口香糖的人|A person who chews gum.  A person who has lost their teeth, especially an old person. Also refers to the gums themselves.|例句：The old gummer smiled toothlessly.  那个无牙老人咧嘴一笑，没有牙齿。

例句：He's a constant gummer, always chewing on something. 他总是在嚼东西，是个不停地嚼口香糖的人。|近义词：chewer, senior citizen (for a person), gingiva (technical term for gums)|反义词: (No direct antonym for gummer as a person,  for gums perhaps "teeth")|词性:noun
