heron:/ˈherən/ (US), /ˈhɛrən/ (UK)| n. |鹭|A long-legged wading bird with a long neck and a long pointed bill.|例句：We saw a blue heron fishing in the shallows. 我们看到一只蓝鹭在浅滩捕鱼。

例句：The heron stood motionless, waiting for its prey.  这只鹭一动不动地站着，等待猎物。


近义词：crane, egret, bittern|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
