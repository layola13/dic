desirousness:/dɪˈzaɪrəsnəs/
n.
渴望, 贪求
The state of being desirous; eagerness to possess or attain something.
His desirousness for power was evident in his every action. 他对权力的渴望体现在他的一举一动中。
desire, eagerness, longing, yearning, craving
indifference, apathy, lack of interest
noun

desire:/dɪˈzaɪr/
n. vt.
渴望, 欲望; 要求
A strong feeling of wanting to have something or wishing for something to happen.  To wish or hope for; to express a wish for.
She has a strong desire to travel the world. 她非常渴望环游世界。
I desire only to be left alone. 我只希望被留下独自一人。
wish, want, crave, long for, yearn for
dislike, aversion, indifference
noun, verb
