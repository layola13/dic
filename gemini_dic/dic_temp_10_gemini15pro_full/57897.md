drifting:/ˈdrɪftɪŋ/
n.|漂流，漂移|The drifting of the boat carried it far from shore. 船的漂流使它远离了海岸。
v.|漂流，漂移|The leaves were drifting on the water. 树叶在水上漂流。|
adj.|漂流的，漂移的|The drifting snow made visibility difficult. 飘落的雪使能见度降低。
|近义词：floating, wandering, roaming|
|反义词：anchored, fixed, stationary|
|词性: 名词, 动词, 形容词|
|词源: From Middle English *drifften, frequentative of *driven (“to drive”), from Old Norse drífa (“to drive”), from Proto-Germanic *drībananą (“to drive”). Cognate with Dutch drijven (“to drive, float”), German treiben (“to drive, float”), Gothic 𐌳𐍂𐌰𐌹𐌱𐌰𐌽 (draiban, “to drive”).|
