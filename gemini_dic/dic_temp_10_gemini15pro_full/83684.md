haggardly:/ˈhæɡərdli/ | adv. |憔悴地，疲惫不堪地|Looking thin and tired, often with dark circles under the eyes, typically as a result of illness, worry, or lack of sleep.|He stared haggardly at the camera after being held captive for three months.|近义词：gauntly, wearily, exhaustedly|反义词：healthily, robustly, vibrantly|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *hagard*, from Anglo-Norman *hagard*, of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to *hag*.
