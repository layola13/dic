conceives:/kənˈsiːvz/
verb
构想，设想；怀孕
form or devise (a plan or idea) in the mind. become pregnant with (a child).
She conceived a plan to escape. 她想出了一个逃跑的计划。
She conceived her first child at the age of 30. 她在30岁时怀上了第一个孩子。
近义词：imagine, devise, visualize, think up, become pregnant
反义词：miscarry
词性：v.
