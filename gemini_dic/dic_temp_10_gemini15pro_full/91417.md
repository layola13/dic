hyenaism:/ˌhaɪˈiːnɪzəm/
n.
鬣狗行径；食腐行为；卑鄙行径
The behavior of a hyena, especially its scavenging habits; figuratively, rapaciousness or abject subservience.
His hyenaism disgusted everyone at the funeral, as he joked and gossiped rather than offering condolences.  他的鬣狗行径让葬礼上的每个人都感到恶心，因为他不仅没有表示哀悼，反而在那里开玩笑和说闲话。
Near synonyms:  rapacity, scavenging, servility, obsequiousness, sycophancy
Antonyms:  generosity, altruism, selflessness, nobility
