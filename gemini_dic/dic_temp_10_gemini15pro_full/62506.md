endower:/ɪnˈdaʊər/| n.| 捐赠者；赋予者 |One who gives a dowry or makes an endowment, especially to a hospital or other institution.| The endower of the new hospital wing wished to remain anonymous.  这位新医院侧楼的捐赠者希望保持匿名。 | synonyms: benefactor, patron, donor | antonyms: recipient, beneficiary | noun

endow:/ɪnˈdaʊ/| vt.| 捐赠；赋予 | To provide with a permanent fund or source of income; to furnish with a quality, ability, or asset.|  Nature has endowed her with both beauty and intelligence.  大自然赋予了她美貌和智慧。 | synonyms: bestow, grant, confer, furnish, equip | antonyms: deprive, divest, strip | verb
