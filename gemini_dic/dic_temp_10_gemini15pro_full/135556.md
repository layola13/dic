nonpolarizing:/ˌnɒnpəʊləˈraɪzɪŋ/
adjective
非偏振的
Not causing polarization, especially of light.  A nonpolarizing lens does not restrict the vibration direction of light waves.
Nonpolarizing sunglasses reduce glare without affecting the color perception.  非偏光太阳镜减少眩光而不影响颜色感知。
Nonpolarizing beamsplitters are used in some optical systems. 非偏振分束器用于某些光学系统。
synonyms: non-polarising, isotropic (in some contexts)
antonyms: polarizing, polarising
词性: adjective
