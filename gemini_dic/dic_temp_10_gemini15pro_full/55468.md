distorters:/dɪˈstɔːrtərz/
| n. |失真器；扭曲者；歪曲者|People or things that distort something, typically information, sound, or images.|The media are often accused of being distorters of the truth. 媒体经常被指责歪曲事实。|
|Synonyms: falsifiers, misrepresenters, manipulators|
|Antonyms: clarifiers, rectifiers|
|词性: 名词|


distort:/dɪˈstɔːrt/
| vt. |扭曲；歪曲；使失真|To pull or twist out of shape; to give a false or misleading account of; to change the form of (an electrical signal or sound wave) in an unwanted way.|The loud music distorted the sound of her voice.  大声的音乐使她的声音失真了。|
|Synonyms: warp, twist, misrepresent|
|Antonyms: clarify, rectify|
|词性: 动词|
