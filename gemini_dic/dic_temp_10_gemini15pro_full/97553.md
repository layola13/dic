ingravidation:/ɪnˌɡrævɪˈteɪʃən/
|n.|失重，无重力状态|The process of becoming weightless or the state of being weightless.  The reduction or absence of the sensation of gravity, as in free fall.|例句：The astronauts experienced ingravidation during their spacewalk. 宇航员在太空行走时体验了失重。

例句：Ingravidation can have long-term effects on the human body. 失重会对人体产生长期影响。|近义词：weightlessness, zero gravity|反义词：gravity, heaviness
|词性:名词
