pal:/pæl/ | n. | 朋友，伙伴 | A friend or comrade. |  Example: He's been my pal since childhood. 他从小就是我的朋友。| Synonyms: friend, buddy, mate, chum | Antonyms: enemy, foe | noun

palpate:/ˈpælpeɪt/ | v. | 触诊，触摸 | To examine (a part of the body) by touch, especially for medical purposes. | Example: The doctor palpated the patient's abdomen. 医生触诊了病人的腹部。 | Synonyms: examine, feel, touch, explore | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "ignore," "neglect" in the context of medical examination) | verb
