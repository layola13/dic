babe-in-the-woods:/beɪb ɪn ðə wʊdz/| n. |涉世未深的人，天真无邪的人|A naive, innocent, or inexperienced person, easily fooled or taken advantage of.  This idiom refers to a child abandoned in a forest, vulnerable and unaware of the dangers.|She's a babe in the woods when it comes to office politics.|Synonyms: innocent, naive, ingenue, greenhorn|Antonyms: sophisticated, worldly, experienced|noun
