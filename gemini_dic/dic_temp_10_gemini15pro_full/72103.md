flocculus:/ˈflɒkjʊləs/ (US) /ˈflɑːkjʊləs/ (UK)| n. |小脑绒球，絮状物|A small lobe of the cerebellum involved in motor control, especially of eye movements and balance.  It can also refer to a small, fluffy mass, like a tuft of wool.|The flocculus plays a crucial role in the vestibulo-ocular reflex. 小脑绒球在前庭眼反射中起着至关重要的作用。|Synonyms: lobe, flake, tuft|Antonyms: N/A|noun

flocculi: /ˈflɒkjʊlaɪ/ (US) /ˈflɑːkjʊliː/ (UK)| n. |小脑绒球（复数）|Plural form of flocculus.|Damage to the flocculi can impair balance.  损害小脑绒球会损害平衡。|Synonyms: lobes, flakes, tufts|Antonyms: N/A|noun
