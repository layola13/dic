maybach: /ˈmaɪbɑːk/ (US) /ˈmaɪbæk/ (UK)| n.|迈巴赫|A brand of ultra-luxury automobiles, now a sub-brand of Mercedes-Benz, known for its exquisite craftsmanship, advanced technology, and opulent features.  Historically, Maybach was an independent manufacturer of high-end vehicles.|He arrived at the gala in a sleek Maybach.|近义词: Rolls-Royce, Bentley|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) economy car|Noun
