beacon:/ˈbiːkən/ | n. | 灯塔，信号灯，烽火，指路明灯 | A fire or light set up in a high or prominent position as a warning, signal, or celebration. | The lighthouse serves as a beacon for ships at sea. 灯塔充当海上船只的指路明灯。 | synonyms: signal, warning, guide, lighthouse | antonyms: obscurity, darkness | noun

beacon:/ˈbiːkən/ | v. | 发信号，指引 | To guide or summon with or as if with a beacon. |  The flashing lights beaconed us to the shore. 闪烁的灯光指引我们到岸边。| synonyms: guide, signal, direct | antonyms: mislead, confuse | verb
