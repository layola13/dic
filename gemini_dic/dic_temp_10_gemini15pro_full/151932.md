pasticheurs:/ˌpæsˈtiˈʃɜːrz/ | n. |模仿者；拼凑者；混合风格艺术家|  A person who creates pastiches, which are works that imitate the style of another artist, writer, or period. | The museum held an exhibition featuring works by several renowned pasticheurs.  这家博物馆举办了一个展览，展出了几位著名模仿者的作品。|  imitators, copyists, parodists | innovators, originators | noun
