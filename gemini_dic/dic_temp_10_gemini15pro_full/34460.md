claviform:/ˈklævɪfɔːrm/| adj.|锁骨形的，棒状的|Resembling a club or key; club-shaped. Specifically, designating a small, somewhat club-shaped bone, articulating with the sternum and scapula.|The claviform bone is a small bone found in some vertebrates.|近义词：club-shaped, key-shaped|反义词:|形容词
Etymology->
From Latin *clāviformis*, from clāvis (“key”) + -formis (“-shaped”).
