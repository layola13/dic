nonsententiousnesses:/ˌnɒnsɛnˈtɛnʃəsnɪsɪz/
n.
lack of sententiousness; avoidance of expressing moral judgments or giving advice.
Absence of being sententious; the quality of not being given to moralizing or pithy pronouncements.
Her nonsententiousnesses was refreshing in a world full of self-righteous pronouncements. 她在一个充满自以为是的宣言的世界里，不爱说教的特点令人耳目一新。
synonyms:  unpretentiousness, modesty, humility, simplicity, straightforwardness
antonyms: sententiousness, preachiness, moralizing, didacticism, pomposity
