ETYMOLOGY->

From mis- + proportion.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "wrong," "bad," or "wrongly." "Proportion" comes from the Old French "proporcion" and ultimately from the Latin "proportio," meaning "comparative relation," from "pro" (for) + "portio" (part, share).
