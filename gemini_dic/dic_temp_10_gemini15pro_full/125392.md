multinational:/ˌmʌltiˈnæʃənəl/ | adj. n. | 跨国的；多国的；跨国公司 | Involving several different countries. | Example: The conference attracted a multinational audience.  这次会议吸引了来自多个国家的观众。

multinational:/ˌmʌltiˈnæʃənəl/ | adj. n. | 跨国的；多国的；跨国公司 |  From "multi-" (many) + "national" (relating to a nation).  |  | 近义词: international, global, transnational, cosmopolitan | 反义词: domestic, local, national | 词性: 形容词, 名词
