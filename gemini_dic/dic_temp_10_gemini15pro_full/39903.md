|  The organization was criticized for its constitutionless operations, leading to accusations of corruption.  该组织因其无章程的运作而受到批评，导致腐败指控。 |Synonyms: unorganized, lawless, anarchic | Antonyms: constitutional, organized, lawful | adjective
