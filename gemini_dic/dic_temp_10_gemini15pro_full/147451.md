overocclusion:/ˌoʊvərəˈkluːʒən/
n.|覆牙合，深覆合|A condition in which the upper teeth overlap the lower teeth excessively when the jaws are closed.  The overlap is vertical, meaning the upper teeth cover a significant portion of the lower teeth.|His overocclusion made it difficult for him to chew properly. 他的深覆合使他难以正常咀嚼。|Synonyms: deep bite, closed bite, overbite|Antonyms: open bite, underbite|noun
