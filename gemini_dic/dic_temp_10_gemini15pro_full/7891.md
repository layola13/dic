anteflexion:/ˌæntiˈflɛkʃən/
n.
前屈，向前弯曲
The abnormal forward bending of an organ, especially the uterus.
例句：The doctor diagnosed her with uterine anteflexion. 医生诊断她患有子宫前屈。

例句：Anteflexion of the uterus can sometimes cause discomfort during pregnancy. 子宫前屈有时会在怀孕期间引起不适。
近义词：bending forward, forward curvature
反义词：retroflexion
词性：名词
