concussing:/kənˈkʌsɪŋ/
verb
使脑震荡, 震动
The blow concussed him badly. 这一击使他脑震荡很严重。
The explosion concussed several nearby residents. 爆炸使附近的几个居民脑震荡。
近义词:shaking, jarring, jolting
反义词:stabilizing, calming, soothing
词性:v.


concussion:/kənˈkʌʃən/
noun
脑震荡, 震动
He suffered a concussion in the accident. 他在事故中脑震荡。
The concussion from the blast broke windows in nearby buildings. 爆炸引起的震动震碎了附近建筑物的窗户。
近义词:impact, shock, blow
反义词:stability, calmness, stillness
词性:n.


词源: From Latin *concussus, past participle of concutere "to shake violently," from com- "together" + quatere "to shake."
