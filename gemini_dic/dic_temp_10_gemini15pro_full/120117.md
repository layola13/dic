mimicking:/ˈmɪmɪkɪŋ/ /ˈmɪmɪkɪŋ/| v. adj.|模仿，模拟|The act of imitating someone or something, often for comedic effect or to learn a skill.  It can also refer to an animal's camouflage or a biological process that resembles another.|He was mimicking his teacher's voice. 他在模仿他老师的声音。|Synonyms: imitating, copying, aping, impersonating|Antonyms: originating, innovating|Verb/Adjective

mimic:/ˈmɪmɪk/ /ˈmɪmɪk/| n. v. adj.|模仿者，模仿，模仿的|A person who imitates, the act of imitating, or an adjective describing something that imitates.|The comedian is a brilliant mimic. 这个喜剧演员是个很棒的模仿者。 |Synonyms: imitator, impersonator, copycat|Antonyms: originator, innovator|Noun/Verb/Adjective
