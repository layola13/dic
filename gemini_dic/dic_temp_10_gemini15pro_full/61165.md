elucidate:/ɪˈluːsɪdeɪt/ | v. | 阐明，解释 | To make something clear; to explain something in detail. |  The professor elucidated the complex theory with clear examples. 教授用清晰的例子阐明了这个复杂的理论。

| 近义词：clarify, explain, illuminate, expound | 反义词：obfuscate, confuse, obscure | 词性: verb

elucidate:/ɪˈluːsɪdeɪt/ | v. | 说明，解释 |  She elucidated her position on the issue in a lengthy speech.  她在一个长篇演讲中阐明了她在这个问题上的立场。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *elucidatus, past participle of elucidare "to make clear," from Latin e- "out" + lucidus "clear, bright," from lucere "to shine."
