disbursal:/dɪsˈbɜːrsəl/
n.|支付，支出，拨款|The disbursal of funds for the project has been delayed.  该项目的资金支付已被推迟。|
Etymology: From dis- "apart" + burse "to pay out," from Old French bourse "purse."|
The company handles the disbursal of pensions to retired employees. 公司负责向退休员工发放养老金。|
Synonyms: payment, payout, distribution, allocation|
Antonyms: collection, receipt, deposit|
noun

disburse:/dɪsˈbɜːrs/
v.|支付，支出，拨款|The government will disburse funds to schools in need. 政府将向有需要的学校拨款。|
Etymology: From dis- "apart" + burse "to pay out," from Old French bourse "purse."|
The charity disbursed over $1 million in aid last year.  去年，该慈善机构支付了超过100万美元的援助款。|
Synonyms: pay out, distribute, allocate, expend|
Antonyms: collect, receive, deposit|
verb
