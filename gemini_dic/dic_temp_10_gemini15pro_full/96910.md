inexplainable:/ˌɪnɪkˈspleɪnəbl/ | adj. | 无法解释的，难以理解的 | Not able to be explained or understood. |

例句：
The sudden disappearance of the ship remains inexplicable.
这艘船的突然失踪仍然无法解释。

His behavior was completely inexplicable.
他的行为完全令人费解。


近义词：
incomprehensible, unaccountable, mysterious, puzzling, baffling

反义词：
explainable, understandable, comprehensible, clear


词性:
adjective


词源:
From in- (not) + explainable (capable of being explained).  Ultimately from Latin *explanare, from ex- (out) + planare (to make plain).
