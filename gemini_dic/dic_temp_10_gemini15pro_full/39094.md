confrere:/ˈkɒnfriːə(r)/ /kɑːnˈfrɛər/| n.|同事；同行；同僚|A fellow member of a profession, especially journalism; a colleague.|例句：He lunched with his confrères at the club. 他和他的同事在俱乐部共进午餐。

例句：The journalist consulted his confrères about the developing story.  这位记者就正在发展的报道咨询了他的同行。|近义词：colleague, associate, peer, fellow|反义词:opponent, rival, adversary|词性:noun
