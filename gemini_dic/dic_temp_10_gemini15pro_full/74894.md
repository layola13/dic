freshish:/ˈfrɛʃɪʃ/ | adj. |  相当新鲜的，较新鲜的 | Moderately fresh; somewhat fresh.  | The bread was a bit freshish, not quite stale, but not ideal.  这面包有点不太新鲜，还没到不新鲜的地步，但也不是理想状态。 | The fruit was freshish, good enough for a pie. 水果还算新鲜，足够做馅饼了。| 近义词: fairly fresh, somewhat fresh, reasonably fresh | 反义词: stale, old, rotten | 词性: 形容词

词源:  fresh + -ish.  The suffix "-ish" is used to form adjectives indicating a moderate degree of the quality expressed by the base word.
