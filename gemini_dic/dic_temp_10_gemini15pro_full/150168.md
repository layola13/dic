panaceist:/ˌpænəˈsiːɪst/
noun
万能药信奉者；相信有万能药的人
A person who believes in or seeks a panacea, a remedy for all ills or difficulties.
Many view him as a panaceist, offering simplistic solutions to complex problems.  很多人认为他是个万能药信奉者，对复杂问题只提供简单的解决方案。
The panaceist claimed his new invention could cure everything from cancer to the common cold.  这位万能药信奉者声称他的新发明可以治愈从癌症到普通感冒的所有疾病。
近义词: utopian, idealist, dreamer, visionary
反义词: pragmatist, realist, skeptic
词性: n.

panacea: /ˌpænəˈsiːə/
noun
万能药; 灵丹妙药
A solution or remedy for all difficulties or diseases.
There is no single panacea for all social ills. 没有什么万能药可以解决所有社会弊病。
She foolishly believed money would be a panacea for all her problems. 她愚蠢地认为金钱是她所有问题的万能药。
近义词: cure-all, elixir, nostrum, remedy
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可用限定词修饰，例如 "specific remedy")
词性: n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek panakeia "cure-all," from pan- "all" + akos "cure."
