enarthrodia:/ˌɛnɑːrˈθroʊdiə/ | n. | 球窝关节 | A freely moving joint in which a rounded head of one bone fits into a cuplike socket of another bone, allowing movement in all directions. | The shoulder joint is an example of an enarthrodia, allowing for a wide range of motion. 肩关节是球窝关节的一个例子，允许大范围的运动。|  The hip joint is an enarthrodia and allows for a wide range of movement, including flexion, extension, abduction, adduction, and rotation. 髋关节是球窝关节，允许大范围的运动，包括屈曲、伸展、外展、内收和旋转。 | synonym: ball-and-socket joint | antonym: synarthrosis | noun
