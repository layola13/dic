infinity's:/ɪnˈfɪnɪtiz/| n.|无限，无穷大(的所有格)|The possessive form of "infinity," referring to something belonging to or associated with an unbounded or immeasurable extent.  It signifies ownership or possession related to the concept of infinity.|例句：Infinity's embrace is vast and mysterious. 无限的怀抱是浩瀚而神秘的。|近义词：eternity's, boundless's, limitless's|反义词: finity's, limited's|词性:名词

词源:  From Late Latin *īnfinītātem (nominative īnfinītas), from Latin īnfīnītus (“unlimited, boundless”) + -y.  The possessive form is created by adding 's.  Ultimately derived from in- (“not”) + fīnītus (“limited, bounded”).
