congruency:/kənˈɡruːənsi/ | n. |一致性，全等，适合|The state of being congruent; agreement or harmony.|  The congruency between the two triangles is evident. 这两个三角形之间的全等关系是显而易见的。|Synonyms: agreement, harmony, conformity, consistency, correspondence|Antonyms: disagreement, discord, incongruity, inconsistency|noun

congruent:/ˈkɒŋɡruənt/ | adj. |一致的，全等的，适合的|Agreeing or harmonious; corresponding; (of figures) identical in form.|The congruent triangles had the same angles and side lengths.  这些全等三角形有相同的角度和边长。|Synonyms: agreeing, harmonious, corresponding, consistent, matching|Antonyms: disagreeing, discordant, incongruous, inconsistent, mismatched|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *congruentia, from Latin congruēns, present participle of congruere "to agree, meet together, coincide," from com- "together" + -gruere, related to grus "crane" (birds that flock together).
