distinguish-by:/dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃ baɪ/| v. |通过...区分，凭借...辨别|To recognize or differentiate something based on a particular characteristic or feature.|例句：We can distinguish between the two species by their markings. 我们可以通过它们的斑纹区分这两个物种。

例句：It's difficult to distinguish him by his voice alone. 仅凭声音很难辨别出他。|近义词：differentiate by, identify by, recognize by|反义词：confuse, mistake|词性: 动词

distinguish:/dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃ/| v. |区分，辨别|To recognize or treat (someone or something) as different.|例句：He can distinguish right from wrong. 他能分辨是非。

例句：It's important to distinguish between fact and opinion. 区分事实和观点很重要。|近义词：differentiate, discern|反义词：confuse|词性: 动词
