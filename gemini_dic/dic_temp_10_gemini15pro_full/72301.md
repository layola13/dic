flour:/ˈflaʊər (US), ˈflaʊə(r) (UK)| n.| 面粉 |A fine powder obtained by grinding grain, especially wheat, used for making bread, cakes, pasta, etc.|She added two cups of flour to the mixture. 她往混合物里加了两杯面粉。|Synonym: meal, powder|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "whole wheat kernels" as an opposite concept)|noun

flour:/ˈflaʊər (US), ˈflaʊə(r) (UK)| vt.|撒粉; 用粉覆盖|To sprinkle or coat with flour.|She floured the chicken before frying it. 她炸鸡前给鸡肉撒上面粉。|Synonym: dust, dredge|Antonym: (No direct antonym for this verb form)|verb
