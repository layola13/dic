old-womanly:/ˌoʊldˈwʊmənli/ | adj. | 老太太似的，老气横秋的 | Resembling or characteristic of an old woman, especially in being fussy, timid, or overly cautious.  |  She had an old-womanly fear of the dark. 她像老太太一样害怕黑暗。 | 近义词：elderly, matronly, dowdy | 反义词：youthful, girlish, spirited | 词性: adjective
