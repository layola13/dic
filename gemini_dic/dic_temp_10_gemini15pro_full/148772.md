overurge:/ˌoʊvərˈɜːrdʒ/
verb
过度催促；过分怂恿
To urge excessively or beyond what is reasonable.
He overurged his horse, causing it to stumble. 他过度催促他的马，导致它绊倒了。
She overurged her son to apply to Harvard, even though he wasn't interested. 她过分怂恿儿子申请哈佛，即使他不感兴趣。
近义词: pressure, push, goad
反义词: discourage, dissuade
