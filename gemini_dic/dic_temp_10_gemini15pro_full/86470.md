hegira:/ˈhedʒɪrə/ /hɪˈdʒaɪrə/ | n. |迁徙，逃亡; (尤指)穆罕默德从麦加逃往麦地那|An exodus or migration, especially the flight of Muhammad from Mecca to Medina in AD 622. |例句：The refugees made a difficult hegira across the mountains.  难民们翻山越岭艰难地逃亡。|例句：His sudden departure felt like a hegira, leaving his family and friends behind. 他的突然离开就像一次逃亡，抛下了他的家人和朋友。|近义词：exodus, flight, migration, escape|反义词：immigration, return, arrival|词性:名词

hegiras:/ˈhedʒɪrəz/ /hɪˈdʒaɪrəz/ | n. |迁徙，逃亡; (尤指)穆罕默德从麦加逃往麦地那 (hegira的复数形式)|Multiple instances of exodus or migration, especially referring to Muhammad's flight from Mecca to Medina.|例句：History books record many hegiras of people seeking religious freedom. 历史书记录了许多人们寻求宗教自由的迁徙。|例句：The hegiras of various tribes marked a significant shift in the region's demographics. 各部落的迁徙标志着该地区人口结构的重大变化。|近义词：exoduses, flights, migrations, escapes|反义词：immigrations, returns, arrivals|词性:名词
