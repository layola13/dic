driveled:/ˈdrɪvəld/| adj.|胡言乱语的，愚蠢的|Characterized by foolish or incoherent talk.|His driveled pronouncements were ignored by everyone. 他胡言乱语的声明被所有人忽视了。|Synonyms: nonsensical, foolish, inane|Antonyms: sensible, coherent, intelligent|adjective
