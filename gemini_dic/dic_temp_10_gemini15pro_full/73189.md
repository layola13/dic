forby:/fɔːrˈbaɪ/| adv.|此外，而且，加之|besides, moreover, in addition to|例句：The food was good, and cheap forby. 食物很好，而且很便宜。|例句：She's clever, and hardworking forby. 她很聪明，而且很勤奋。|近义词：besides, moreover, additionally, as well, furthermore|反义词：however, nevertheless, nonetheless|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *forbi*, from Old Norse *fyrir* ("before") + *by* ("nearby").  Literally, "before by," meaning "close beside," which evolved into the sense of "in addition to."
