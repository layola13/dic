four-ply:/ˈfɔːrˈplaɪ/
adjective
四层的；四股的
Made of four layers or strands twisted together.
例句：She bought some four-ply yarn for knitting. 她买了一些四股纱线用来编织。

例句：Four-ply toilet paper is softer and more absorbent. 四层卫生纸更柔软，吸水性也更好。

近义词：quadruple-layered, four-fold
反义词:single-ply, two-ply
词性:adjective


four-ply:/ˈfɔːrˈplaɪ/
noun
四层织物；四股线
A fabric or yarn made of four layers or strands.
例句：This sweater is made of a warm four-ply. 这件毛衣是用保暖的四层织物制成的。

例句：Four-ply is a popular choice for baby blankets. 四股线是婴儿毯的常用选择。

近义词：four-fold, quadruple-layered material
反义词:single-ply yarn, two-ply fabric
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "four" + "ply" (meaning a layer or strand, from Old French "pli" meaning "fold").  The word "ply" ultimately derives from Latin "plicare" meaning "to fold."
