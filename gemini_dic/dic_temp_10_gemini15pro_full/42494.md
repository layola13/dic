counterimage:/ˈkaʊntərɪmɪdʒ/| n. |逆像，原像|The counterimage of a set under a function is the set of all elements in the domain that map to that set.|The counterimage of the interval [0, 1] under the function f(x) = x^2 is [-1, 1]. 函数 f(x) = x^2 下区间 [0, 1] 的逆像是 [-1, 1]。|preimage, inverse image| |noun|

counterimages:/ˈkaʊntərɪmɪdʒɪz/| n. |逆像，原像（复数）|The counterimages of the singleton sets under a function partition the domain. 函数下单例集的逆像划分定义域。|The counterimages of {2} and {3} under the function f(x) = x^2 are {√2, -√2} and {√3, -√3} respectively.  函数 f(x) = x^2 下 {2} 和 {3} 的逆像分别是 {√2, -√2} 和 {√3, -√3}.|preimages, inverse images| |noun|
