oared:/ɔːrd/
adjective
桨的；有桨的；用桨划的
having oars; propelled by oars.
The oared boat glided silently across the lake. 用桨划的船静静地滑过湖面。
近义词:rowed, paddled
反义词:motorized, sail-powered
词性:adjective

词源: From oar + -ed.  "Oar" comes from Old English *ār, from Proto-Germanic *ōrōn, from Proto-Indo-European *erē-, meaning "to row."  The "-ed" suffix forms adjectives from nouns, indicating possession or characteristic of what the noun describes.
