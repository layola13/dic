devas:/ˈdeɪvəz/| n.|天神，神灵|A Sanskrit word referring to deities or gods, often benevolent and associated with nature.|The devas are believed to inhabit higher realms of existence.|近义词：gods, deities|反义词：asuras, demons|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Sanskrit *devaḥ*, related to Latin *deus* (god).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *deywós* (god, shining sky).
