manhunter:/ˈmænˌhʌntər/ | n. | 猎人；追捕者；捕快 | Someone who hunts and captures people, especially criminals. | The police were relentless manhunters, determined to bring the fugitive to justice.  警方是不懈的追捕者，决心将逃犯绳之以法。 | synonyms: hunter, pursuer, tracker, headhunter | antonyms: prey, quarry | noun

manhunter:/ˈmænˌhʌntər/ | n. | 食人者 |  (literary) A cannibal. | In the darkest corners of the jungle lived a feared manhunter. 在丛林最黑暗的角落里住着一个令人恐惧的食人者。 | synonyms: cannibal, anthropophagus | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but could consider herbivore in a very broad sense) | noun
