nonresuscitation: /ˌnɒnriːˌsʌsɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 不复苏，放弃抢救 | The act of withholding cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR) or other life-saving measures.  It represents a decision to allow natural death. | The patient had a Do Not Resuscitate (DNR) order, so nonresuscitation was the medical team's course of action.  病人有“不进行心肺复苏术”的医嘱，所以医疗团队的做法是不进行复苏。

The family opted for nonresuscitation for their terminally ill loved one. 家属选择对他们病危的亲人不进行复苏。 | 近义词: do-not-resuscitate (DNR), no CPR | 反义词: resuscitation, life support | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
* non- : Latin prefix meaning "not"
* resuscitation: from Latin resuscitare "to revive, restore to life"
