elasipoda:/ɪˌlæˈsɪpədə/ | n. |海参纲|A taxonomic class within the phylum Echinodermata, commonly known as sea cucumbers. These marine animals are characterized by their elongated, worm-like bodies and leathery skin.|例句：Elasipoda are found in all oceans, from shallow coastal waters to the deep sea.  海参纲生物存在于所有海洋中，从浅海沿岸水域到深海都有分布。

例句：The elasipoda exhibit a remarkable diversity of forms and lifestyles. 海参纲生物展现出惊人的形态和生活方式多样性。|近义词：sea cucumbers|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


Etymology: From Greek *elasmos*  (strap, plate) + *pous* (foot) referring to the tube feet
