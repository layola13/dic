layette:/leɪˈet/| n.|婴儿的全套服装及用品|A complete set of clothing and accessories for a newborn baby.|例句：They received a beautiful layette as a baby shower gift. 他们收到了一个漂亮的婴儿用品套装作为迎婴派对的礼物。

例句：She carefully folded the tiny garments and placed them in the layette. 她仔细地叠好小衣服，放进婴儿用品套装里。|近义词：baby clothes, newborn essentials, baby kit|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *layette*, diminutive of *laie*, meaning 'box, chest'.  Originally referred to the chest or box in which the baby's clothes were kept.
