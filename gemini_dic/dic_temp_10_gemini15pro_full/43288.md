craftswoman:/ˈkræftsˌwʊmən/ | n. | 女工匠；手艺女人 | A woman who practices a craft with skill. |  例句：The craftswoman skillfully carved the wood into a beautiful bird.  这位女工匠巧妙地把木头雕刻成一只美丽的鸟。

例句：My grandmother was a skilled craftswoman, known for her intricate lacework. 我的祖母是一位技艺精湛的女工匠，以其复杂的蕾丝作品而闻名。 | 近义词：artisan, craftsperson, artist | 反义词: amateur, novice | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From craft + woman.  "Craft" comes from Old English *cræft (meaning "strength, skill")  and "woman" comes from Old English wīfmann (meaning "female human being").
