dandler:/ˈdændlər/| n. | 宠儿；逗乐(婴儿)的人；|A person who dandles a baby or young child.| She was a real dandler with her niece, always bouncing her on her knee. 她非常喜欢逗她的侄女，总是让她坐在膝盖上颠来颠去。| 近义词:  coddler, amuser | 反义词: neglecter |词性: 名词

dandle:/ˈdændl/ | vt. |  宠溺；逗乐(婴儿)；|To move (a baby or young child) up and down on one's knee or in one's arms.| He dandled the baby on his knee. 他把婴儿放在膝盖上逗乐。|近义词: bounce, jiggle | 反义词: ignore |词性: 动词
