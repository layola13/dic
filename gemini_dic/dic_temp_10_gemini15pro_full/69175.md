fay:/feɪ/
noun.
仙女, 小精灵
A mythical creature of folk tales, typically a small, human-like being with magical powers.
She believed she saw a fay dancing in the moonlight. 她相信她看到了一个月光下跳舞的小精灵。
synonyms: fairy, sprite, elf, pixie
antonyms: giant, ogre
词性: 名词


fay:/feɪ/
verb.
(古语)注定; 注定死亡
(Archaic) To condemn to death; to destine.
The knight was fayed to die in battle.  这个骑士注定要在战斗中死去。
synonyms: doom, condemn
antonyms: save, rescue
词性: 动词
