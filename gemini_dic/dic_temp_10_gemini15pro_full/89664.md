hoop:/hup/ | n. |箍; 环; 铁环; (篮球的)篮圈|A circular band of metal, wood, or similar material, especially one used for holding something in place. | The barrel was secured with iron hoops.  这个桶用铁箍固定。| synonyms: ring, band, circle, loop | antonyms: line, segment | noun

hoop:/hup/ | v. |用箍圈住; 加箍于|To put a hoop or hoops on something. |  He hooped the barrel. 他给桶加了箍。 | synonyms: encircle, surround, bind | antonyms: unbind, loosen | verb
