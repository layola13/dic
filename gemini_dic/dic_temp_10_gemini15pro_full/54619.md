dismemberers:/dɪsˈmɛmbərərz/
n.
肢解者，分割者
People who dismember something, usually a body.  This refers to the act of cutting or tearing apart the limbs or parts of a body.
The dismemberers were apprehended by the police.  肢解者被警方逮捕了。
The dismemberers left no trace of their gruesome work. 肢解者没有留下他们可怕工作的任何痕迹。
synonyms: mutilators, butchers, destroyers
antonyms: healers, restorers, assemblers
noun
