overinterferences:/ˌoʊvərɪntərˈfɪrənsɪz/ |n.|过度干扰|Excessive interference; interference that is too much or too frequent.|The overinterferences from the government stifled the company's growth. 政府的过度干预扼杀了公司的发展。|The constant overinterferences from his manager made it difficult for him to do his job effectively.  经理不断的过度干预让他难以有效地完成工作。|近义词: overintervention, excessive interference, meddling|反义词: non-interference, laissez-faire|词性: 名词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "interference" (from inter- "between" + ferire "to strike").  "Over-" 表示“过度”，"interference" 源于 inter- “之间” + ferire “打击”。
