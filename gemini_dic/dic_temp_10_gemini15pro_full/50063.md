denser:/ˈdensər/ (US) /ˈdensə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更稠密的，更浓的|Comparative form of dense: having parts or molecules packed closely together; crowded; thick.|The fog grew denser as the night wore on.  夜越来越深，雾也越来越浓。|The population is much denser in urban areas. 城市地区的人口密度要大得多。|近义词：thicker, heavier, more compact|反义词：thinner, lighter, less dense|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *dens*, from Old French *dens*, from Latin *densus* ("thick, dense, crowded").
