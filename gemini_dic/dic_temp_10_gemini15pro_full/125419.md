multiperil:/ˈmʌltɪˌpɛrəl/ | adj. n.| 多重风险的，多灾的；多重风险保险 |  Relating to or involving insurance against more than one kind of risk.|  The farmer bought a multiperil insurance policy to protect his crops. 农民购买了一份多重风险保险单来保护他的作物。| 近义词: comprehensive, all-risk | 反义词: single-peril, limited | 词性: 形容词，名词

Etymology:
From multi- (many) + peril (danger).
