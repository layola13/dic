hyperdevelopmental:/ˌhaɪpərˌdɪˌvɛləpˈmɛntl/
adjective
超速发展的，过度发展的
Characterized by unusually rapid or accelerated development, often exceeding typical norms or expectations.  This term often carries a connotation of being excessive or potentially problematic.
The child showed hyperdevelopmental language skills, speaking in complex sentences by age two.  这个孩子表现出超常的语言发展技能，两岁时就能说复杂的句子。

The hyperdevelopmental growth of the tech industry has led to both innovation and disruption. 科技行业的超速发展带来了创新和颠覆。

Near Synonyms: accelerated, advanced, precocious, rapid,  premature
Antonyms: delayed, slow, retarded, underdeveloped
词性: adjective
