copublishes:/ˌkoʊˈpʌblɪʃɪz/
verb
共同出版
publishes something together with another publisher.
Example:  Oxford University Press copublishes the book with a small independent press. 牛津大学出版社与一家小型独立出版社共同出版了这本书。

Example: The journal is copublished by two leading scientific societies. 这本期刊由两个主要的科学协会共同出版。
synonym:jointly publish, publish together
antonym:solely publish, publish alone
词性:动词
