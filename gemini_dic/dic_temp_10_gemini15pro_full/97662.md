inhibitingly:/ɪnˈhɪbɪtɪŋli/
adverb
抑制地，约束地
in a way that inhibits or restrains.
The medication inhibitingly affected her cognitive function.  这种药物抑制了她的认知功能。
His shyness acted inhibitingly on his ability to make friends. 他的羞怯抑制了他交友的能力。
近义词: restrictively, repressively, suppressively
反义词: encouragingly, stimulatingly, promotingly
词性: adverb

词源: From inhibit + -ing + -ly.  Inhibit comes from Latin inhibitus, past participle of inhibere "to restrain, hold back," from in- "in, on" + habere "to hold."
