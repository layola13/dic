anginose:/ˈændʒɪˌnoʊs/ /ˈændʒɪnəʊs/| adj.|心绞痛的，患心绞痛的|Relating to or resembling angina, especially angina pectoris; characterized by choking or suffocation.|例句：He experienced anginose pain in his chest. 他感到胸部心绞痛。

例句：Her symptoms were described as anginose. 她的症状被描述为心绞痛。|近义词：anginal|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "healthy," "asymptomatic")|词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *anginosus, from Latin angina (“quinsy, inflammation of the throat”), from angere (“to strangle, choke”).
