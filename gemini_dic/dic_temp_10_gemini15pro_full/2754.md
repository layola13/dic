adulterate:/əˈdʌltəreɪt/
verb
掺假，搀杂
To make (something, such as a food or drink) impure or weaker by adding something of poor quality.
The company was accused of adulterating its products with cheap ingredients.  该公司被指控在其产品中掺杂廉价成分。

The milk was adulterated with water.  牛奶里掺了水。

近义词:debase, contaminate, corrupt, doctor
反义词:purify, refine
词性:verb


adulterization:/əˌdʌltəˈreɪʃn/
noun
掺假，伪造
The act of adulterating something.
The adulteration of food is a serious crime. 食品掺假是严重的犯罪行为。

The investigation revealed widespread adulteration of olive oil. 调查揭露了橄榄油普遍掺假的现象。

近义词:contamination, debasement, corruption
反义词:purification, refinement
词性:noun
