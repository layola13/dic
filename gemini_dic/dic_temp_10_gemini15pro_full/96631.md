indument:/ɪnˈdjuːmənt/| n.| پوشش；（植物表面的）毛被；衣服|A covering, especially of hair, feathers, or scales on an animal or plant; clothing.|The fern's indument is composed of tiny, hair-like scales.|近义词：covering, vestment, attire, clothing|反义词: bareness, nudity|词性: noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *indumentum, from Latin induere "to put on," from in- "in, on" + duere "to put, place."
