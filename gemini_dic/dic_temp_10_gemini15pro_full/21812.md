bobtails:/ˈbɒbteɪlz (UK), ˈbɑːbˌteɪlz (US)| n. |短尾动物; 短尾猫; 澳大利亚牧牛犬; 短尾游戏|A bobtail is an animal, especially a cat or a horse, with a very short tail. It can also refer to an Australian Cattle Dog or a type of card game.|The farmer had a bobtail cat. 那个农民有一只短尾猫。|Synonyms: docked tail, stub tail|Antonyms: long tail|noun

bobtails:/ˈbɒbteɪlz (UK), ˈbɑːbˌteɪlz (US)| v. |截短尾巴|To bobtail means to shorten an animal's tail by cutting part of it off.|They bobtailed their dog's tail when it was a puppy.  他们在他们的小狗时期截短了它的尾巴。|Synonyms: dock, crop|Antonyms: lengthen|verb
