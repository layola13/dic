microdensitometry: /ˌmaɪkroʊˌdɛnsɪˈtɑːmətri/ | n. |显微光密度测定法;显微密度测定法 | The measurement of the optical density of very small areas, typically used to quantify the darkness of spots on photographic film or stained sections of biological tissue. | Microdensitometry was used to analyze the protein bands on the electrophoresis gel. 显微光密度测定法用于分析电泳凝胶上的蛋白质条带。 | 近义词: densitometry | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but potentially qualitative analysis) | 词性: noun


Etymology: micro- (small) + density + -metry (measurement)
