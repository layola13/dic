durance:/ˈdjʊərəns/ /ˈdʊrəns/| n. |监禁；禁闭；关押|Imprisonment; confinement.|He was held in durance for several months. 他被监禁了几个月。| The political prisoners languished in durance. 政治犯在牢狱中煎熬。|近义词：imprisonment, confinement, captivity, incarceration|反义词：freedom, liberty|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Old French *durance*, from *durer* "to last, endure," from Latin *dūrāre* "to harden, to last," from *dūrus* "hard."
