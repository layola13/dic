loosish:/ˈluːɪʃ/| adj.|略微松的，有点散漫的|Somewhat loose; not very tight or firm.  Relatively relaxed or casual. |The screws were a bit loosish, so I tightened them. 螺丝有点松，所以我拧紧了它们。

The atmosphere at the meeting was loosish and informal. 会议的气氛比较轻松随意。|近义词：slack, relaxed, casual, informal|反义词:tight, firm, strict, formal|词性:形容词


词源:  Derived from "loose" with the suffix "-ish" indicating a moderate degree of the quality.  From Old Norse *lauss, meaning "loose, free, unbound."
