courtbred:/ˈkɔːrtbrɛd/| adj.|受过良好教养的，有教养的；温文尔雅的|Of good manners and upbringing, refined.  Having the elegant manners and sophistication associated with high social position, especially of royal or aristocratic origin. |例句：Her courtbred manners were evident in her graceful movements and polite conversation. 她的良好教养在她优雅的举止和礼貌的谈吐中显而易见。

例句：The courtbred prince was known for his impeccable taste and diplomatic skills.  这位出身宫廷的王子以其无可挑剔的品味和外交技巧而闻名。|近义词：refined, polished, cultivated, sophisticated, well-bred, aristocratic|反义词：uncouth, vulgar, coarse, unrefined, ill-bred|词性:adjective
