countersecure:/ˌkaʊntər sɪˈkjʊr/ | adj. |反安全的，反保障的| Designed or intended to counteract security measures.  Meant to undermine or bypass existing security protocols or systems. | The hackers employed countersecure tactics to breach the firewall. 黑客们采用反安全策略来攻破防火墙。| The group developed countersecure software to expose vulnerabilities in popular operating systems.  该小组开发了反安全软件来揭露流行操作系统中的漏洞。 | 近义词: insecure, vulnerable, unsafe | 反义词: secure, safe, protected | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "Counter-" (prefix meaning "against" or "opposite") + "secure" (adjective meaning "safe" or "protected").
