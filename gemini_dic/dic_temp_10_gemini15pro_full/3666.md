agglutinant:/əˈɡluːtɪnənt/
n.|粘合剂，胶粘剂|A substance that causes adhesion; glue.|The postage stamp wouldn't stick to the envelope because it lacked agglutinant.|adhesive, glue, binder|solvent
adj.|粘合的，胶粘的|Having the properties of an adhesive; sticky.|Agglutinant substances are used in many industrial processes.|adhesive, sticky, gluey|non-adhesive


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *agglutinant- (stem of agglutinans), present participle of agglutinare "to glue to," from ad- "to" + glutinare "to glue," from gluten "glue."
