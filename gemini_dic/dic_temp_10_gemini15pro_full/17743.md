bedeviled: /bɪˈdɛvəld/ /biˈdevəld/
verb
中文翻译：1. （长期）困扰，折磨；2. 恶魔附身（旧时用法）
英文解析：1. To cause continual trouble or difficulty for. 2. (Archaic) possessed by a devil.
例句：
The project was bedeviled by technical problems.
这个项目一直受到技术问题的困扰。

He has been bedeviled by ill health for years.
他多年来一直体弱多病。

近义词：plagued, tormented, harassed, troubled, afflicted
反义词：blessed, aided, assisted
词性：v.
