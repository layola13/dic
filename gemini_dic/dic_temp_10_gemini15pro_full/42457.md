counterefforts:/ˈkaʊntərˌɛfərts/
noun
反制措施，对抗行动
Actions taken to oppose or nullify the efforts of another.
The government launched a series of counterefforts to combat the spread of misinformation. 政府发起了一系列反制措施来打击 misinformation 的传播。
The enemy's counterefforts were surprisingly effective. 敌人的反击出奇地有效。
synonyms: countermeasures, counteractions, countermoves
antonyms: efforts, initiatives, campaigns
