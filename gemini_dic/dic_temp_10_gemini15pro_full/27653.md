candida:/ˈkændɪdə/| n. |念珠菌|A yeast-like fungus, often part of the normal flora of the mouth, skin, intestinal tract, and vagina, that can cause infections, especially in people with weakened immune systems.|Candida albicans is the most common species that causes infections in humans. 白色念珠菌是引起人类感染最常见的种类。|Synonyms: yeast, fungus|Antonyms: bacteria, virus|Noun

candidas:/ˈkændɪdəz/| n. |念珠菌（复数）|Plural form of candida, referring to multiple species or strains of the yeast-like fungus.|Different candidas can cause various types of infections. 不同的念珠菌会引起各种类型的感染。|Synonyms: yeasts, fungi|Antonyms: bacteria, viruses|Noun
