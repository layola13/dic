deluded:/dɪˈluːdɪd/
adjective
受蒙蔽的，妄想的
believing something that is not true
例句：He's deluded himself into thinking he's a great artist. 他误以为自己是一位伟大的艺术家。

例句：The deluded man believed he could fly.  这个妄想的人相信自己能飞。

近义词：misguided, deceived, fooled, misled
反义词：disabused, enlightened, realistic, aware, informed
词性: adjective


delusion:/dɪˈluːʒən/
noun
错觉，妄想
a false belief or opinion about yourself or your situation
例句：He's under the delusion that he's attractive to women. 他误以为自己对女人很有吸引力。

例句：She suffers from delusions of grandeur. 她患有夸大妄想症。

近义词：illusion, fallacy, misconception, fantasy
反义词：reality, truth, fact
词性: noun
