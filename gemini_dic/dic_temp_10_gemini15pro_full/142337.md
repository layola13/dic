orthognathous: /ˌɔːrθəɡˈneɪθəs/ | adj. | 直颌的，正颌的 | Having a straight jaw; not prognathous.  Relating to a condition where the face is relatively flat, with the jaws projecting only slightly or not at all. |  "Modern humans are characterized by an orthognathous face." 现代人类的特征是直颌的面部。 |  "The skull of the Neanderthal was less orthognathous than that of a modern human." 尼安德特人的头骨不如现代人的直颌。 | Synonyms: straight-jawed | Antonyms: prognathous, retrognathous | adjective
