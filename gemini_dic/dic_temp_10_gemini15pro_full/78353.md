gestalts:/ɡəˈʃtɑːlts/ /ɡəˈʃtɔːlts/
n.
格式塔; 完形
an organized whole that is perceived as more than the sum of its parts.
The gestalt of the city is changing rapidly with all the new construction.  这座城市的整体面貌正随着所有新建筑的出现而迅速改变。
The painting lacked a cohesive gestalt; it felt like a collection of unrelated images. 这幅画缺乏一个连贯的整体; 它感觉像是一堆不相关的图像。
configuration, form, shape, structure, whole
fragment, part, piece
noun
