aldermanic:/ˌɔːldərˈmænɪk/
adjective
市政委员的；市议员的
Relating to or characteristic of an alderman.
The aldermanic council voted in favor of the new park. 市政委员会投票赞成新建公园。
The aldermanic elections are held every four years. 市议员选举每四年举行一次。

近义词: municipal, civic
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "private" in some contexts)
词性: adjective
