preparatory:/prɪˈpærətɔːri/ |adj.|准备的，预备的|Serving as or intended as preparation; introductory.|例句：Preparatory work is essential before starting any project.  项目开始前的准备工作至关重要。

例句：He attended a preparatory school before going to university. 他上大学前读了一所预备学校。 |近义词：introductory, preliminary, initial|反义词：final, concluding|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *praeparatorius, from Latin praeparator (“one who prepares”) + -orius (suffix forming adjectives of relation). Praeparator is derived from praeparare (“to prepare”), from prae (“before”) + parare (“to make ready”).
