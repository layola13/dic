impregnablenesses:/ɪmˌprɛɡnəbəlˈnɛsɪz/
|n.| 不可攻破性；坚不可摧性 |The impregnableness of the fortress made it a safe haven during the war.  这个堡垒的坚不可摧性使它在战争期间成为一个安全的避风港。| The impregnableness of his arguments made it difficult to counter him. 他论点的无懈可击使人难以反驳他。|
|近义词: invulnerability, invincibility, impenetrability|
|反义词: vulnerability, weakness, penetrability|
|词性: Noun|
|词源: From impregnable + -ness. Impregnable comes from Old French imprenable, from in- "not" + prenable "takeable," from prendre "to take," ultimately from Latin prehendere "to seize, grasp."|
