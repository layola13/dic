corgi: /ˈkɔːrɡi/ (UK) /ˈkɔːrɡi/ (US)| n. |柯基犬|A small breed of dog with short legs and a long body, originally from Wales. It comes in two varieties: the Pembroke Welsh Corgi and the Cardigan Welsh Corgi.|例句：The Queen is famous for her love of corgis. 女王以喜爱柯基犬而闻名。

例句：My corgi loves to herd sheep, even though he's never seen a real one. 我的柯基犬喜欢牧羊，即使它从未见过真正的羊。|近义词：dog, canine|反义词:cat (并非严格意义上的反义词，只是提供一种对比)|词性:noun
