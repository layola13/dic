beautifulnesses:/ˈbjuːtɪfʊlnəsɪz/
n.
美；美丽之处；美好的事物
the quality of being beautiful; the beautiful features of something.
The painting captured the beautifulnesses of the sunset. 这幅画捕捉到了日落的美丽。
近义词：beauty, loveliness, attractiveness
反义词：ugliness, unattractiveness
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From beautiful + -ness.  "Beautiful" comes from the Middle English *beautevous*, derived from the Old French *beauteus*.  Ultimately, the root is the Latin *bellus*, meaning "beautiful," "handsome," "fine," or "agreeable."  The suffix "-ness" is a native English suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, denoting a state, quality, or condition.
