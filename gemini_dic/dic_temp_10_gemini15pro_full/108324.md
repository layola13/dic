laus: /laʊs/ | n. | praise, acclaim, approval | An expression of commendation or approval. | The hero received laus for his bravery.  这位英雄因其勇敢而受到赞扬。 | Synonyms:  accolade, commendation, applause, plaudit, kudos | Antonyms: criticism, condemnation, censure | noun

Etymology: From Latin *laus*, from Proto-Italic *lausēs*, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱlewH-és-. Cognates include Greek κλέος (kléos, “glory, fame”).
