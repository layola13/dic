mulse: /mʌls/
n. |蜂蜜酒; 一种用蜂蜜发酵制成的酒精饮料|A fermented alcoholic beverage made from honey.|He brewed a batch of mulse using wildflower honey. 他用野花蜜酿造了一批蜂蜜酒。|近义词:mead|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑其他酒精饮料如beer啤酒, wine葡萄酒)|词性:名词

Etymology: From Old English *meodu, from Proto-Germanic *meduz, from Proto-Indo-European *médʰu (“honey; mead”). Cognate with Old Norse mjöðr, Old High German metu, Sanskrit मधु (madhu).
