immusical:/ɪmˈjuːzɪkəl/
adjective
不音乐的；不和谐的；不懂音乐的
Not musical; lacking in musical talent or appreciation; discordant.
Her singing voice was so immusical that it made everyone cringe. 她唱歌的声音很不和谐，让每个人都感到畏缩。
The immusical sound of the rusty swing set grated on her nerves. 生锈的秋千发出不和谐的声音，刺激着她的神经。
Synonyms: unmusical, inharmonious, discordant, tuneless, cacophonous
Antonyms: musical, harmonious, melodious, tuneful, euphonious
词性: 形容词
