coordination:/kəʊˌɔːdɪˈneɪʃn/ /koʊˌɔːrdɪˈneɪʃn/ | n. | 协调，调和;  phối hợp |The coordination of the different departments is essential for the success of the project.  不同部门之间的协调对于项目的成功至关重要。| 近义词: cooperation, collaboration, harmony, synchronization | 反义词: disorganization, discord, conflict | 词性: 名词

coordinated:/kəʊˈɔːdɪneɪtɪd/ /koʊˈɔːrdɪˌneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 协调的，配合的; phối hợp |Their movements were perfectly coordinated. 他们的动作配合得天衣无缝。| 近义词: harmonious, synchronized, organized | 反义词: uncoordinated, disorganized, chaotic | 词性: 形容词

coordinate:/kəʊˈɔːdɪneɪt/ /koʊˈɔːrdɪnət/ | v. | 协调，调节; phối hợp |We need to coordinate our efforts to achieve our goals. 我们需要协调努力以实现我们的目标。| 近义词: organize, harmonize, synchronize, manage | 反义词: disorganize, disrupt | 词性: 动词

Etymology: Late Middle English: from medieval Latin coordinatus, past participle of coordinare, from co- ‘together’ + ordinare ‘to arrange’.
