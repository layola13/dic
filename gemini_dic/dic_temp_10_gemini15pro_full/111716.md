locomoter:/ˌloʊkəˈmoʊtər/| adj. n.|运动的；移动的；机车|Relating to locomotion; an organ or apparatus used in locomotion.|例句：The locomoter system of a horse is highly developed. 马的运动系统高度发达。

例句：The train is a powerful locomoter. 火车是一种强有力的机车。|近义词：mobile, ambulatory, locomotive|反义词：immobile, stationary, sessile|词性：adjective, noun

词源: From Latin *locomotivus*, from *locus* ("place") + *motivus* ("moving"), from *movēre* ("to move").
