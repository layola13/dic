antisubsidy:/ˌæntiˈsʌbsədi/| adj.|反补贴的|Relating to or designed to counteract or prevent subsidies.  It describes actions, policies, or measures taken to oppose or neutralize the effects of subsidies.|The government imposed antisubsidy duties on imported steel. 政府对进口钢材征收反补贴税。|synonyms: countervailing, anti-dumping|antonyms: pro-subsidy, supporting|adjective

antisubsidy:/ˌæntiˈsʌbsədi/| n.|反补贴（措施）|A measure taken to counteract the effects of a subsidy, often in the form of a tariff or duty.|The World Trade Organization (WTO) has rules regarding the application of antisubsidies.  世界贸易组织(WTO)对反补贴措施的应用有相关规定。|synonyms: countervailing measure, countervailing duty|antonyms: subsidy, grant|noun
