bungle:/ˈbʌŋɡəl/| v. n.|笨拙地做；搞砸；失败|To carry out (a task) clumsily or incompetently, leading to failure or an unsatisfactory outcome.  A thing that is done badly or clumsily.|例句：I bungled the presentation and forgot half my lines. 我把演讲搞砸了，忘了一半的台词。|例句：The entire operation was a bungle from start to finish. 整个行动从头到尾都是一团糟。|近义词：mishandle, botch, mess up, fumble|反义词：succeed, achieve, accomplish|词性：动词，名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From probably Scandinavian origin; compare Norwegian dialect *bungla “to work clumsily,” Swedish dialect bungla “to bungle, to perform clumsily.”
