mislabels:/ˌmɪsˈleɪblz/| v. |贴错标签，错误标记|To apply a wrong label to something; to label incorrectly. | The factory mislabeled several boxes of products, sending them to the wrong stores. 工厂给几箱产品贴错了标签，把它们送到了错误的商店。

The clerk mislabeled the price of the item, causing confusion at the checkout.  店员给商品标错了价格，在结账时引起了混乱。 |近义词：misname, misidentify, misclassify |反义词: label correctly, identify correctly |词性:verb

mislabel:/ˌmɪsˈleɪbl/| n. |错误的标签|A label that is incorrect. |The mislabel on the package led to the product being recalled. 包装上的错误标签导致产品被召回。|近义词：incorrect label, wrong label |反义词: correct label |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (meaning "wrong" or "bad") + label.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Germanic origin. "Label" comes from Old French "label," meaning "ribbon, strip of parchment."
