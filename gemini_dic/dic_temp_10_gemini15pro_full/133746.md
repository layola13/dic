nonfuturistic:/ˌnɒnˌfjuːtʃəˈrɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非未来主义的；非未来派的 | Not relating to or characteristic of the future, especially not portraying a technologically advanced future. |  The design of the building was surprisingly nonfuturistic, with its traditional brick facade. 这座建筑的设计令人惊讶地非未来主义，带有传统的砖砌外墙。

The movie's setting, while in space, felt distinctly nonfuturistic due to its focus on basic human struggles.  这部电影的背景虽然设定在太空，但由于其关注基本的人类斗争，感觉明显是非未来主义的。 | 近义词: traditional, conventional, old-fashioned, dated | 反义词: futuristic, avant-garde, modern, cutting-edge | 词性: adjective
