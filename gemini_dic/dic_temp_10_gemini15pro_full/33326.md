chuffily:/ˈtʃʌfɪli/| adv.|不满地，不高兴地|In a bad-tempered or discontented way, often because one feels slighted or treated unfairly.|He crossed his arms chuffily and refused to speak. 他不高兴地交叉双臂，拒绝说话。|近义词：grumpily, sullenly, morosely|反义词：happily, cheerfully|词性:副词
词源: From the adjective "chuffy" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Chuffy" likely originates from a dialectal word meaning "swollen" or "puffed up," perhaps referring to the appearance of someone holding their breath in anger or sulking.
