discarnate:/dɪsˈkɑːrneɪt/
adjective
肉体脱离的；无形的；灵魂的
Not embodied in flesh; incorporeal.  Existing in spirit form, without a physical body.
例句：The discarnate spirit floated through the room.  无形的灵魂飘过房间。

例句：She claims to have communicated with discarnate entities. 她声称与脱离肉体的实体进行过交流。
近义词：incorporeal, disembodied, spiritual, ethereal
反义词：incarnate, embodied, physical, corporeal
词性: adjective


discarnate:/dɪsˈkɑːrneɪt/
verb
使脱离肉体
To free from the body; to separate the soul or spirit from the body.
例句：The ritual is believed to discarnate the soul.  人们相信这个仪式能使灵魂脱离肉体。

例句：Near-death experiences are often described as feeling discarnated from the physical body.  濒死体验通常被描述为感觉灵魂与肉体分离。
近义词： disembody
反义词：incarnate, embody
词性: verb



ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin dis- (apart, away) + caro, carnis (flesh)
