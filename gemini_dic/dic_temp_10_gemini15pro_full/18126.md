beholden:/bɪˈhəʊldən/
adjective
感激的，受恩惠的
obligated; indebted; owing thanks or having a duty to someone in return for help or a service
例句：I am beholden to you for your help. 我很感激你的帮助。

例句：She felt beholden to her family for their support. 她对家人的支持感到感激。

近义词：indebted, obligated, obliged
反义词：unobligated, ungrateful
词性: adjective
