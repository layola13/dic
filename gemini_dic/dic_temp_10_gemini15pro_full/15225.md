backers:/ˈbækərz/| n.|支持者，赞助者，资助者|A person or group of people who give financial or other support to something. |The project attracted backers from all over the world.  这个项目吸引了来自世界各地的支持者。|Synonyms: supporters, patrons, sponsors, financiers|Antonyms: opponents, detractors|noun


backers:/ˈbækərz/| n.|背书人；担保人|(finance) a person who underwrites something. |The backers of the loan were confident in the company's success. 这笔贷款的担保人对公司的成功充满信心。|Synonyms: guarantors, underwriters|Antonyms: debtors|noun
