immunosuppressants:/ˌɪmjuːnoʊsəˈprɛsənts/| n.|免疫抑制剂|Drugs that reduce or prevent the body's immune response, used to prevent organ rejection after transplantation or to treat autoimmune diseases.|例句：The patient was prescribed immunosuppressants after the kidney transplant. 患者在肾移植后被开了免疫抑制剂。

例句：Immunosuppressants can increase the risk of infections. 免疫抑制剂会增加感染的风险。|近义词：immunomodulators, antirejection drugs|反义词：immunostimulants|词性：名词

immunosuppressant:/ˌɪmjuːnoʊsəˈprɛsənt/| adj.|免疫抑制的|Relating to or denoting a substance that suppresses the body's immune response.|例句：The doctor prescribed an immunosuppressant medication. 医生开了免疫抑制药物。

例句：Immunosuppressant therapy is crucial for organ transplant recipients. 免疫抑制疗法对器官移植接受者至关重要。|近义词：immunosuppressive|反义词：immunostimulatory|词性：形容词

词源:
From immuno- + suppressant.  "Immuno-" comes from "immune," ultimately from Latin "immunis" (free from burden). "Suppressant" comes from "suppress," from Latin "suppressus," past participle of "supprimere" (to press down).
