calycate: /ˈkælɪkeɪt/ | adj. | 杯状的，萼状的 | Having a calyx or resembling a calyx; cup-shaped. | The calycate flower had delicate, overlapping petals.  这朵杯状的花有着精致重叠的花瓣。 | Example 2: The mushroom's cap was distinctly calycate, resembling a tiny cup.  这蘑菇的菌盖明显呈杯状，像一个小杯子。 |  Near synonyms: cup-shaped, cuplike, calyciform | Antonym: acalycine | Word type: Adjective

calyx: /ˈkeɪlɪks/ | n. | 花萼 | The sepals of a flower, typically forming a whorl that encloses the petals and forms a protective layer around a flower in bud. | The calyx of the rose was a deep green. 玫瑰的花萼是深绿色的。 | Example 2: The botanist carefully examined the calyx of the newly discovered plant. 植物学家仔细检查了新发现植物的花萼。 | Near synonyms: sepals |  | Word type: Noun
