bicched:/ˈbɪtʃt/| vt. adj. (slang, vulgar)|咒骂（尤指女性），卑鄙的，糟糕的|This word is a highly offensive slang term derived from "bitch," typically used as a verb to insult or demean someone, particularly a woman. It can also be used as an adjective to describe something unpleasant or difficult.  It carries a strong negative connotation and should be avoided in formal and most informal settings.|Example: He bicched about his boss all day. 他整天都在抱怨他的老板。

Example:  The traffic was bicched this morning. 今早交通糟透了。|近义词：(verb) curse, insult, berate, criticize harshly; (adjective) awful, terrible, difficult, unpleasant|反义词：(adjective) pleasant, agreeable, easy, wonderful|词性: verb, adjective
