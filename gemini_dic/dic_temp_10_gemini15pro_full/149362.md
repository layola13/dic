pacifiable:/ˌpeɪsəˈfaɪəbl/ /ˌpæsəˈfaɪəbl/| adj.|可安抚的，可平定的|Able to be pacified; appeasable.|例句：The protestors seemed pacifiable after the mayor addressed their concerns.  市长回应了抗议者的担忧后，他们似乎得到了安抚。

例句：The situation, though tense, proved pacifiable through diplomatic negotiations. 虽然局势紧张，但通过外交谈判最终得到了平息。|近义词：appeasable, conciliatory, placable, mollifiable|反义词：unappeasable, implacable, intransigent, unyielding|词性:形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French *pacifiable, from pacifier (see pacify).
