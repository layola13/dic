/ˈɪndɪvɪd/ | n. | 个人；个体 | An individual is a single human being, as distinct from a group. |  Example: Each individual has their own unique personality. 每个人都有自己独特的个性。

Example:  The rights of the individual are protected by law.  法律保护个人的权利。

| Synonym: person, being, human | Antonym: group, collective, society | noun
