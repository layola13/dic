oraculous:/ɔˈrækjʊləs/ | adj. | 预言的；神谕的；睿智的 | Resembling or characteristic of an oracle; prophetic; wise. | The oraculous pronouncements of the ancient priestess were highly regarded. 古代女祭司的预言备受重视。|  The old woman gave an oraculous prediction about the future king. 老妇人对未来国王做出了神谕般的预言。 | 近义词: prophetic, prescient, insightful, wise, sage | 反义词: unprophetic, shortsighted, foolish | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin ōrāculum (“oracle”) + -ous.
