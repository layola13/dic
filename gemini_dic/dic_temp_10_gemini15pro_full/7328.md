angustation:/ˌæŋɡʌˈsteɪʃən/| n. |极度痛苦，苦恼|Extreme anxiety, distress, or mental suffering. |例句：He experienced a period of profound angustation after the loss of his job. 他在失业后经历了一段极度痛苦的时期。

例句：The angustation of waiting for the test results was almost unbearable. 等待测试结果的焦虑几乎令人难以忍受。|近义词：anxiety, distress, anguish, torment, suffering|反义词：comfort, solace, peace, tranquility|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin angustātiō (“a narrowing, making narrow, straitening”), from angustus (“narrow, strait, limited”).
