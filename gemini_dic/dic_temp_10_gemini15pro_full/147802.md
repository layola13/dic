overproven:/ˌoʊvərˈpruːvən/
adjective
过度证明的
Having been proven excessively or unnecessarily; proven beyond a reasonable doubt.  The evidence is so overwhelming that it feels overproven.
The prosecution’s case felt overproven, with a mountain of evidence presented.  检方的案件感觉过度证明了，提出了大量的证据。
The theory is overproven by numerous experiments and observations.  该理论被大量的实验和观察过度证明了。
近义词:overstated, excessive
反义词:understated, insufficient
词性:adjective
