inimitability:/ɪnˌɪmɪtəˈbɪlɪti/
n.
inimitable quality; the state of being impossible to imitate
The inimitability of her style made her a literary icon.  她风格的独特性使她成为文学偶像。
Her voice had an inimitability that captivated audiences. 她的声音有一种独特的魅力，深深吸引着观众。
uniqueness, singularity, distinctiveness, irreplaceability
imitability, reproducibility, copycatting
noun
