in-midst:/ɪnˈmɪdst/ |prep. adv. n.|在…之中，在…中间|being in the middle of a place, a group of people or things, or a period of time|例句：In the midst of the chaos, she remained calm. 在一片混乱之中，她保持镇定。|例句：He stood in the midst of his cheering supporters. 他站在欢呼的支持者中间。|近义词：amid, among, amidst, in the middle of|反义词：outside, beyond|词性: 介词，副词，名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *in middes, equivalent to in + midst.  Midst is from Old English middest, the superlative of midde "middle".
