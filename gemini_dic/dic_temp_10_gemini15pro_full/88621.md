如果你是想了解疑问词 "what" 的用法，我可以提供以下信息：

what: /wɒt/ (UK) /wɑːt/ (US) | pron. det. adv. | 什么，多么 | used to ask for information about something | 例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？ | 近义词: which | 反义词:  无 | 词性: pronoun, determiner, adverb

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? = 你怎么想？
What does he do for a living? = 他做什么谋生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? = 她晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she been up to lately? = 她最近在忙什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on? = 你一直在做什么？
What has he been reading recently? = 他最近一直在读什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the wedding? = 她婚礼上穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you left? = 你离开之前做了什么？
What had they decided before the meeting? = 会议前他们决定了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I called? = 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about? = 他们一直在争论什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do this weekend? = 你这周末要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be presenting at the conference? = 他们在会议上将要展示什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by then? = 到那时会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习了什么多久？
