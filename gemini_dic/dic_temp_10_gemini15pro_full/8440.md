antialbumid:/ˌæntiælˈbjuːmɪd/
noun
抗白蛋白
A substance that reacts with or opposes the action of albumin.
The researcher injected the antialbumid to observe its effect on blood clotting. 研究人员注射了抗白蛋白以观察其对血液凝固的影响。
synonyms: antialbumin
antonyms: albumin
