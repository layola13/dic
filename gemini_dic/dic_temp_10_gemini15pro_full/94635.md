impressers:/ɪmˈprɛsərz/
n.
给人留下深刻印象的人或事物
People or things that make a strong impression.
The new product's sleek design and innovative features made it a real head-turner, attracting a crowd of impressers at the trade show.  这款新产品时尚的设计和创新功能使其成为真正的吸睛之作，在展会上吸引了一群留下深刻印象的人。
Her eloquence and confidence made her a powerful impresser during the debate.  她的雄辩和自信使她在辩论中给人留下了深刻的印象。
近义词: influencers, attention-grabbers
反义词: bores, dullards
词性: noun (plural)
