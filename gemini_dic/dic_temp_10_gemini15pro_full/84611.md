hamulose:/ˈhæmjʊloʊs/| n. |糖醛酸|A sugar acid derived from a hexose sugar by oxidation of the terminal -CH2OH group to a -COOH group. |Hamulose is an important intermediate in the biosynthesis of certain polysaccharides. 糖醛酸是某些多糖生物合成中的重要中间体。|  The specific hamulose involved determines the properties of the resulting polysaccharide.  所涉及的特定糖醛酸决定了所得多糖的特性。|synonym: uronic acid| antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps a specific sugar alcohol like sorbitol) | noun
