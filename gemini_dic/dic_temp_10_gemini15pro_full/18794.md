berried:/ˈber.id/ /ˈbɛr.id/
adjective
结满浆果的；有浆果的
Covered with berries.
The bush was berried with ripe raspberries. 灌木丛上结满了成熟的覆盆子。
近义词：fruitful, berry-laden
反义词：barren, fruitless
词性: adjective

词源：From berry + -ed.  The suffix "-ed" is used to form adjectives from nouns, indicating "having" or "characterized by" something.  In this case, "berried" means "having berries."
