microsthene:/ˈmaɪkrəsˌθiːn/ |n.|微sthène（厘米-克-秒制中磁极强度的单位）|A unit of magnetic pole strength in the centimeter-gram-second (CGS) system.  It is defined as the strength of a magnetic pole that exerts a force of one dyne on an equal pole placed one centimeter away.|The strength of the magnet was measured in microsthenes. 这块磁铁的强度是用微sthène测量的。|近义词: unit of magnetic pole strength|反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, as it's a unit of measurement. One might consider units of magnetic flux density like Tesla or Gauss as conceptually related opposites in some contexts.)|词性:noun
