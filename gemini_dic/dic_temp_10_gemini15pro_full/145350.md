overbumptious: /ˌoʊvərˈbʌmpʃəs/
adjective
傲慢的；自负的；专横的
Excessively confident and assertive; pushy.  Characterized by excessive self-assertion or an overbearing manner.
He was so overbumptious that nobody wanted to work with him. 他太傲慢了，以至于没人想和他一起工作。

The overbumptious young executive alienated his colleagues with his constant boasting.  这个傲慢的年轻主管不停地吹嘘，疏远了他的同事。

近义词: arrogant, presumptuous, conceited, pompous, overbearing, domineering
反义词: humble, modest, unassuming, meek, diffident
词性: adjective

词源:  "Over" (excessive) + "bumptious" (offensively self-assertive). "Bumptious" itself possibly comes from "bump" (to strike or knock against) + "-tious" (suffix forming adjectives).  The overall implication is of someone pushing themselves forward aggressively.
