angerful:/ˈæŋɡərfl/ | adj. |充满愤怒的，易怒的| Filled with anger; easily angered. | He gave her an angerful look. 他愤怒地瞪了她一眼。| She felt angerful and frustrated. 她感到愤怒和沮丧。| 近义词:angry, wrathful, irate, furious, indignant | 反义词:calm, peaceful, tranquil, serene | 词性:形容词

词源: anger (愤怒) + -ful (充满...的)
