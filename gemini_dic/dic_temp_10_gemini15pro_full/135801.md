nonprolificness:/ˌnɑːnproʊlɪˈfɪknəs/
n.
非多产性，缺乏创造力
The state or quality of not being prolific; lack of productivity or fruitfulness.
His recent nonprolificness has worried his editor. 他最近缺乏创造力让他的编辑很担心。
近义词: barrenness, infertility, unproductiveness
反义词: prolificacy, productivity, fruitfulness
词性: 名词

词源:
From non- + prolific + -ness.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Prolific" comes from the Late Latin "prolificus," meaning "producing offspring or fruit abundantly," from "proles" (offspring) + "-ficus" (making).  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
