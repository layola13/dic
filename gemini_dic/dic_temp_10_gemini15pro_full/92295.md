hyphens:/ˈhaɪfənz/ | n. |连字符，连接号 |A punctuation mark (-) used to join words or parts of words, or to divide a word at the end of a line. | Example: The word "up-to-date" uses hyphens.  这个词“up-to-date”使用了连字符。

| Example:  Use a hyphen to connect compound adjectives. 使用连字符连接复合形容词。| Synonyms: dash, minus sign | Antonyms: N/A | noun
