hierotheca:/ˌhaɪəroʊˈθiːkə/ | n. | 圣物库，圣器收藏室 | A place where sacred objects are kept, especially in a church or temple. |  The hierotheca contained relics of the saints. 圣物库里保存着圣徒的遗物。| synonym: reliquary, treasury | antonym: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek  ἱεροθήκη (hierothēkē)  'sacred depository', from  ἱερός (hieros)  'sacred' +  θήκη (thēkē)  'receptacle, box'.
