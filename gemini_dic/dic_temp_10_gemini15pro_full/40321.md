contouring:/ˈkɒntʊərɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntʊrɪŋ/ (US)| n.| 修容，轮廓塑形|The process of using makeup to define and enhance the structure of the face, creating the illusion of shadows and highlights.|She used contouring to make her cheekbones look more prominent. 她用修容使她的颧骨看起来更突出。|synonyms: shaping, sculpting, defining|antonyms: blending, smoothing|noun

contouring:/ˈkɒntʊərɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntʊrɪŋ/ (US)| adj.| 修容的，用于修容的|Relating to or used for contouring the face.|She bought a new contouring palette. 她买了一款新的修容盘。|synonyms: sculpting, shaping|antonyms: blending, smoothing|adjective
