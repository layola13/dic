busbar:/ˈbʌsbɑːr/ (US), /ˈbʌsbɑː/ (UK)| n.|汇流排，母线|A conductor or group of conductors forming a rigid connection between several circuits.|The main busbar in the power distribution panel provides electricity to the entire building. 配电盘中的主汇流排为整栋建筑供电。|The busbar overheated and caused a fire. 汇流排过热引发火灾。|Synonyms: bus, conductor bar|Antonyms: N/A|noun
