in-built:/ˈɪnbɪlt/ | adj. | 内置的，嵌入的 |  Existing as an essential or inseparable part of something.  Integrated within the structure or design of something, rather than added later. |  The car has an in-built sat-nav system. 这辆车有内置卫星导航系统。 |  The software has in-built security features.  这个软件有内置的安全功能。 | integrated, built-in, inherent | external, add-on | adjective
