overcare:/ˌoʊvərˈkɛr/| v.|过度关心，过分照顾|To care for someone or something excessively, often to the point of being detrimental.|She overcared for her plants, watering them so much that they drowned. 她过度照料她的植物，浇水太多以至于它们淹死了。|He overcared for his children, stifling their independence. 他过分照顾他的孩子，扼杀了他们的独立性。|近义词：overprotect, coddle, smother|反义词:neglect, ignore|verb
