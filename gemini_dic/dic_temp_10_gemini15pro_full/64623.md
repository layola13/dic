erysipelatoid:/ˌɛrɪsɪˈpiːləˌtɔɪd/ | adj. | 类丹毒的；类似丹毒的 | Resembling erysipelas, a bacterial skin infection characterized by redness and swelling. | The rash on his arm was erysipelatoid. 他手臂上的皮疹类似丹毒。 | 近义词: erysipelas-like | 反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider terms indicating healthy skin: clear, unblemished) | 词性: adjective


Etymology:
From erysipelas + -oid.  Erysipelas comes from the Greek  "erysipelaς" meaning "red skin."  The suffix "-oid" means "resembling" or "like."
