fingerprinting:/ˈfɪŋɡərˌprɪntɪŋ/| n.|指纹识别，指纹鉴定|The process of taking and analyzing fingerprints for identification purposes.  It involves recording the unique pattern of friction ridges on a person's fingertips.|The police used fingerprinting to identify the suspect. 警方用指纹识别技术确认了嫌疑人。|synonyms: dactyloscopy, identification, biometric authentication|antonyms: N/A|noun
