artifact:/ˈɑːrtɪfækt/ | n. | 人工制品，手工艺品，史前古器物 | An object made by a human being, typically an item of cultural or historical interest. | The museum displayed ancient Egyptian artifacts.  博物馆展出了古埃及的文物。| synonyms: relic, object, article, item, specimen, piece, antiquity | antonyms: natural object | noun

artifact:/ˈɑːrtɪfækt/ | n. | (科技)人为现象，假象 |  Something observed in a scientific investigation or experiment that is not naturally present but occurs as a result of the preparative or investigative procedure. | The distortion on the image is an artifact of the scanning process. 图像上的失真是扫描过程造成的假象。 | synonyms: anomaly, glitch, error, aberration | antonyms: genuine phenomenon | noun
