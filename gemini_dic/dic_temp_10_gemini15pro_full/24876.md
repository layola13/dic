broy:/brɔɪ/
noun
兄弟；哥们儿 (Informal, often used online)
A slang term used to address a male friend or acquaintance.
例句：Hey broy, what's up? 嘿，兄弟，怎么了？

例句：I'm going to the gym with my broys. 我要和我的兄弟们去健身房。

近义词：bro, dude, pal, mate
反义词:enemy, foe
词性:n.
