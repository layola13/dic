discordantly:/dɪˈskɔːrdəntli/ | adv. |不和谐地，刺耳地 |In a discordant manner; clashing or disagreeing.  Characterized by a lack of harmony or agreement. | The instruments played discordantly, creating a jarring sound. 乐器演奏得很不和谐，发出刺耳的声音。| The bright pink walls clashed discordantly with the antique furniture.  亮粉色的墙壁与古董家具很不协调。| synonym:  disharmoniously, jarringly, inharmoniously, conflictingly | antonym: harmoniously, concordantly, agreeably |词性: 副词
