eucharistize:/juːkərɪstaɪz/ | v. | 感恩; 行圣餐礼 | To celebrate the Eucharist; to give thanks. |  The priest eucharistized at the altar.  神父在祭坛上举行圣餐礼。

The pilgrims eucharistized for their safe arrival.  朝圣者们为他们的安全抵达而感恩。 | 近义词: give thanks, celebrate communion | 反义词: blaspheme, desecrate | 词性: verb

Etymology: From Late Latin *eucharistizare*, from Greek *eukharistein* "to give thanks," from *eu* "well" + *kharizesthai* "to show favor, gratify."
