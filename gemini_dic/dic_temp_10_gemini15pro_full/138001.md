nook:/nʊk/| n. |角落，隐蔽处|A secluded or sheltered place, often small and cozy.|例句：She found a quiet nook in the library to read. 她找到了图书馆一个安静的角落读书。

例句：The cat curled up in a sunny nook by the window. 猫蜷缩在窗边阳光照射的角落里。|近义词：corner, recess, cranny, alcove|反义词：exposure, open space|词性：名词

词源: From Middle English *noke, *nok, from Old Norse *hnǫk (“corner, angle”), from Proto-Germanic *hnukkaz (“hook, bend”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)neh₂k- (“to bend, turn”). Cognate with Dutch hoek (“corner, angle”), German Haken (“hook”).
