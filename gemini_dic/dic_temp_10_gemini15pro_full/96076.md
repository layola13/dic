indefatigability:/ˌɪndɪˌfætiɡəˈbɪləti/
n.
不倦；不知疲倦
The quality of being tireless and persistent.
Her indefatigability made her a successful marathon runner. 她不知疲倦的精神使她成为一名成功的马拉松选手。
Synonyms: tirelessness, persistence, tenacity, endurance
Antonyms: weariness, fatigue, exhaustion
noun
