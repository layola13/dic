glamorizes:/ˈɡlæməˌraɪzɪz/
verb
使…富有魅力；美化；颂扬
makes (something) seem glamorous or desirable, especially by presenting it in a romanticized way.
The movie glamorizes violence and makes it seem heroic.  这部电影美化了暴力，使暴力行为看起来像是英雄行为。

The advertisement glamorizes smoking. 这则广告美化了吸烟。

近义词: romanticizes, idealizes, glorifies, exalts
反义词: vilifies, denigrates, disparages, demeans
词性: verb
