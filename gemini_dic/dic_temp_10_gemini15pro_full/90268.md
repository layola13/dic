householder:/ˈhaʊsˌhoʊldər/ (US), /ˈhaʊsˌhəʊldə(r)/ (UK)| n. |住户，户主|A person who owns or rents a house and lives in it.|例句：The householder was responsible for maintaining the property.  户主负责维护房产。

例句：The survey was conducted among 1,000 householders.  这项调查是在1000名住户中进行的。|近义词：resident, occupant, homeowner, tenant|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *housholder*, equivalent to house +‎ holder.  Compare Dutch *huishouder*, German *Haushalter*.
