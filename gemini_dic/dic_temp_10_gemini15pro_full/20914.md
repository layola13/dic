bleaknesses:/ˈbliːknɪsɪz/
noun
荒凉；凄凉；黯淡；阴郁
The state or quality of being bleak; dreariness, gloominess.
The bleaknesses of the moorland in winter can be depressing. 冬天荒原的凄凉景象会让人沮丧。
desolation, barrenness, dreariness, gloominess
warmth, brightness, cheerfulness, hopefulness
