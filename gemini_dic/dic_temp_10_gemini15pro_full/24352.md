brindle:/ˈbrɪndl/| adj. n.|斑纹的，有斑纹的; 斑纹;|A brindle coat is a coat of mixed colors, usually tan and brown, that looks like stripes or streaks.|The boxer was a brindle, with dark stripes on a fawn background.|近义词：striped, streaked, tabby|反义词:solid-colored|词性:形容词/名词

词源:  From earlier brindled, from brinded, probably from obsolete brand 'sword', referring to the markings resembling burn marks made by a sword.
