crooning:/ˈkruːnɪŋ/| n. v.|低吟浅唱，哼唱|A soft, sentimental style of singing, often associated with jazz and popular music.|例句：He was crooning a love song to his girlfriend. 他正对着女朋友低吟一首情歌。|例句：The crooning of the singer lulled the baby to sleep. 歌手的低吟浅唱哄婴儿入睡了。|近义词：humming, singing softly, warbling|反义词:shouting, yelling|词性:noun, verb
croon:/ˈkruːn/| v.|低吟浅唱，哼唱|To sing in a soft, sentimental style.|例句：She crooned a lullaby to her baby. 她低声哼唱一首摇篮曲给她的宝宝。|例句：He crooned his way to stardom. 他一路哼唱，最终成为了明星。|近义词：hum, sing softly, warble|反义词:shout, yell|词性:verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Dutch *kroonen "to groan, lament," probably of imitative origin.  The sense shifted from groaning to soft singing, particularly of a sentimental nature.
