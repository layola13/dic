miscarriage: /ˈmɪskærɪdʒ/ | n. | 流产；失败；误送 | The spontaneous or induced expulsion of a fetus from the womb before it is viable. Also, a failure to achieve a desired outcome. | She suffered a miscarriage in her first trimester. 她怀孕早期流产了。

The project was a complete miscarriage of justice. 这个项目完全是冤假错案。 | 近义词: abortion (in the medical sense), failure, fiasco | 反义词: success, birth (in the medical sense) | 词性: noun

词源: From Old French *mescariage "misbehavior," from mes- "wrongly" + carier "to carry." The medical sense developed later, referring to the body "failing to carry" a pregnancy to term.
