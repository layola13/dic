mouldy:/ˈməʊldi/ (UK) /ˈmoʊldi/ (US)| adj.|发霉的，霉烂的|Covered with or smelling of mold; stale and musty.|The bread was mouldy and had to be thrown away. 面包发霉了，不得不扔掉。|Synonyms: mildewed, musty, fusty, stale|Antonyms: fresh, new, clean|adjective


mouldy:/ˈməʊldi/ (UK) /ˈmoʊldi/ (US)| n.|霉菌|A growth of minute fungi forming on vegetable or animal matter.|There was mouldy growing on the wall. 墙上长了霉。|Synonyms: mildew, fungus, mold|Antonyms: None|noun
