andesine:/ˈændɪˌzaɪn/| n. |中长石|A plagioclase feldspar mineral of the albite-anorthite series, containing between 30% and 50% anorthite.|Andesine is a common constituent of igneous rocks such as andesite. 中长石是火成岩如安山岩的常见成分。|The geologist identified the mineral as andesine. 地质学家将这种矿物鉴定为中长石。|近义词:plagioclase, feldspar|反义词:None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the Andes mountains, where it was first discovered, + -ine (a suffix used in mineralogy to form names of minerals).
