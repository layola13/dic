hellenic:/hɛˈlɛnɪk/
adjective
希腊的；希腊文化的
Relating to Greece, its people, or its language.
The Hellenic Association of Cultural Heritage promotes Greek history and art.  希腊文化遗产协会致力于推广希腊历史和艺术。
The museum houses a vast collection of Hellenic art.  这家博物馆收藏了大量的希腊艺术品。
synonyms: Greek, Grecian
antonyms: Roman, Latin
词性: adjective
