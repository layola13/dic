bawls:/bɔːlz/
verb
放声痛哭，号啕大哭
cries loudly and uncontrollably.
The baby bawls when it's hungry.  婴儿饿了就大声哭。
近义词:cries, sobs, wails, howls
反义词:giggles, laughs, chuckles
词性:verb

bawl:/bɔːl/
noun
大声哭，号啕
a loud cry or wail.
The child's bawl could be heard throughout the house.  孩子的哭声响彻整个房子。
近义词:cry, sob, wail, howl
反义词:giggle, laugh, chuckle
词性:noun

词源:  From Middle English *baullen, of imitative origin.  源自中古英语 *baullen，拟声词起源。
