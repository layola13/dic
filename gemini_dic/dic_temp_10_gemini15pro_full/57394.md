doziest:/ˈdoʊziɪst/| adj.|最困倦的，最想睡的|Superlative form of dozy: most dozy.  Means extremely sleepy or drowsy.|例句：After a long day of travel, he was the doziest passenger on the bus.  旅途劳顿了一整天后，他是车上最困倦的乘客。

例句：She felt doziest in the afternoons, even after her morning coffee. 她下午感觉最困，即使喝了早咖啡也是如此。|近义词：sleepiest, drowsiest, most lethargic|反义词：most alert, most awake, liveliest|词性:形容词

词源: From doze + -y + -est.  Ultimately from Old Norse *dusa.
