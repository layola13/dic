offertorial: /ˌɒfərˈtɔːriəl/
n.|付费宣传;软文|A type of paid advertisement disguised as editorial content.|Example: The website published an offertorial promoting a new weight loss supplement. 该网站发布了一篇推广新型减肥补充剂的软文。|
Example:  The offertorial blended seamlessly with the rest of the magazine content, making it difficult to distinguish as advertising. 这篇软文与杂志的其他内容完美融合，很难将其识别为广告。|
近义词: advertorial, sponsored content, native advertising|
反义词: editorial, news report|
词性: noun
