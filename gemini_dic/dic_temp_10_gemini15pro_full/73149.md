forager:/ˈfɔːrɪdʒər/| n. |觅食者，强征者|A person or animal that searches widely for food or provisions.|The forager returned to the camp with a basket full of berries. 觅食者带着满满一篮子的浆果回到了营地。|Synonyms: hunter-gatherer, scavenger, seeker, collector|Antonyms: provider, farmer, cultivator|noun

forage:/ˈfɔːrɪdʒ/| v. n. |觅食，搜寻；饲料| (v.) To search widely for food or provisions; (n.) Food for horses or cattle.| (v.) The deer foraged for acorns in the forest. 鹿在森林里觅食橡子。 (n.) The farmer stored hay for winter forage. 农民储存干草作为冬季饲料。|Synonyms: (v.) rummage, hunt, search, explore; (n.) feed, fodder, provender|Antonyms: (v.) provide, supply, cultivate; (n.) starvation, famine|verb & noun
