guanaco:/ɡwəˈnɑːkoʊ/ | n. |原驼, 野生美洲驼|A wild South American mammal (Lama guanicoe) related to the llama and alpaca, having long soft woolly hair. |例句：The guanaco is native to the Andes Mountains. 原驼原产于安第斯山脉。

例句：Guanacos are known for their fine wool. 原驼以其优质的羊毛而闻名。|近义词：llama, alpaca, vicuña |反义词: N/A |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *guanaco*, from Quechua *wanaku*.
