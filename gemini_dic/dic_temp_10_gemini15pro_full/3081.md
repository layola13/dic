aeronaut:/ˈɛərənɔːt/ | n. |  热气球驾驶员；飞行员；宇航员 | A person who operates or travels in an airship, balloon, or other aircraft. |  The aeronaut skillfully navigated the hot air balloon through the mountain pass.  热气球驾驶员熟练地驾驶热气球穿过山口。

The early aeronauts were true pioneers of aviation. 早期的热气球驾驶员是真正的航空先驱。 | 近义词: pilot, aviator, airman, astronaut | 反义词: passenger | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French aéronaute, from Greek ἀήρ (aḗr, “air”) + ναύτης (naútēs, “sailor”).
