contagiosity:/kənˌteɪdʒiˈɑːsəti/| n. |传染性，感染力|The quality of being contagious; the ease with which a disease or idea spreads.|The contagiosity of the flu made it spread rapidly through the school. 流感的传染性使其在学校迅速传播。|  The speaker's enthusiasm had a certain contagiosity that captivated the audience. 演讲者的热情具有一定的感染力，吸引了观众。|近义词：infectiousness, communicability, transmissibility|反义词：resistance, immunity|词性：名词
