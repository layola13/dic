avowedness:/əˈvaʊədnəs/
n.
公开承认; 公开表示
the state of being avowed; open acknowledgment or declaration.
He spoke with an avowedness that surprised everyone.  他坦率直言，让每个人都感到惊讶。
synonyms:  frankness, openness, candor, honesty, forthrightness
antonyms: secrecy, concealment, furtiveness, dissimulation, reticence
noun

avow:/əˈvaʊ/
vt.
公开承认；公开表示
to declare openly; to admit frankly.
She avowed her love for him. 她公开承认她爱他。
synonyms: declare, admit, confess, acknowledge, proclaim
antonyms: deny, conceal, disavow, repudiate, retract
verb
