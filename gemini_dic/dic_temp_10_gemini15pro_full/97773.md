injector:/ɪnˈdʒɛktər/ | n. |注射器；喷射器；注射者|A device that injects something, especially fuel into an engine or a drug into the body. |例句：The fuel injector wasn't working properly, causing the car to stall. 汽车的燃油喷射器工作不正常，导致汽车熄火。

例句：The nurse used a syringe injector to administer the vaccine. 护士用注射器注射了疫苗。|近义词：syringe, needle, nozzle, jet |反义词: extractor |noun
