fiat:/ˈfiːæt/ (US), /ˈfaɪæt/ (UK)| n.|法令，命令，许可|An authoritative decree, sanction, or order. A decree or order issued by someone in authority, especially a government.|The president issued a fiat banning smoking in public places. 总统发布了一项禁止在公共场所吸烟的法令。|The company's success was due to a fiat from the CEO. 公司的成功归功于首席执行官的命令。|edict, decree, order|allowance, permission|noun
