outtravels:/ˌaʊtˈtrævəlz/
verb
超过…的旅行距离；比…旅行更多
travels farther or more extensively than
例句：He outtravels all his colleagues, visiting new countries every month. 他比所有同事都旅行得多，每个月都去新的国家。

例句：Her adventurous spirit outtravels most people she knows.  她冒险的精神超越了她认识的大多数人。

近义词：surpasses, exceeds, outstrips
反义词：lags behind, falls short of
词性：v.
