hazier:/ˈheɪzɪər/ (US), /ˈheɪziər/ (UK)| adj.|更朦胧的，更模糊的|Comparative form of hazy: more hazy.  Meaning less clear, more misty, or more vague.|The view became hazier as the fog rolled in. 随着雾气涌入，视野变得更加模糊。|Synonyms: mistier, foggier, murkier, less clear, more obscure, more vague|Antonyms: clearer, brighter, sharper, more distinct, more transparent|adjective

haze:/heɪz/ (US), /heɪz/ (UK)| n. v.|薄雾，霾; 使模糊，使朦胧|n. A slight obscuration of the lower atmosphere, typically caused by fine suspended particles.  v. To become hazy or make something hazy.|n. The morning haze obscured the distant mountains. 早晨的薄雾遮蔽了远处的山脉。 v. The heat began to haze the horizon.  热气开始使地平线变得朦胧。|Synonyms: mist, fog, smog, obscurity|Antonyms: clarity, clearness, brightness|noun, verb
