fluctuant:/ˈflʌktʃuənt/| adj.|波动的，起伏的，变动的|Varying or tending to vary, especially irregularly and within certain limits.|The patient's temperature was fluctuant throughout the day. 病人的体温一整天都在波动。|Synonyms: variable, unstable, wavering, oscillating|Antonyms: stable, constant, steady|adjective

fluctuate:/ˈflʌktʃueɪt/| v.|波动，起伏，变动|To rise and fall irregularly in number or amount.|Stock prices fluctuated wildly throughout the year. 股票价格全年大幅波动。|Synonyms: vary, oscillate, waver, change|Antonyms: stabilize, remain constant|verb

词源:  From Latin fluctuans, present participle of fluctuare (“to fluctuate, wave, toss”), from fluctus (“wave, billow”).
