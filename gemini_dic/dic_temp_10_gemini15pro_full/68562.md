fangle:/ˈfæŋɡl/ | n. | 新玩意儿；新发明；新奇的装置 | A new invention or gadget, especially one that is not yet widely known or accepted. |  This new phone has all the latest fangles. 这款新手机拥有所有最新的玩意儿。 | novelty, gadget, gimmick, contrivance | classic, standard | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English *fanglen "to contrive, devise," perhaps frequentative of *fongen "to seize, take" (from Old English *fōn, from Proto-Germanic *fōnjana-), with the sense development perhaps influenced by or related to Old French *fangler "to catch, seize."
