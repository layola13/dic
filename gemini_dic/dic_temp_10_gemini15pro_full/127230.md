nausea:/ˈnɔːziə/ (US), /ˈnɔːsiə/ (UK)| n. |恶心，作呕|A feeling of sickness with an inclination to vomit.|例句：The smell of the hospital gave her a feeling of nausea. 医院的气味让她感到恶心。|例句：He experienced nausea after the chemotherapy treatment. 他在化疗后感到恶心。|近义词：sickness, queasiness, vomiting|反义词: wellness, comfort|词性:名词

ad nauseam:/æd ˈnɔːziəm/ (US), /æd ˈnɔːsiəm/ (UK)| adv. |令人作呕地，冗长乏味地|To a sickening or excessive degree; so as to cause disgust or boredom.|例句：The politician repeated his campaign slogans ad nauseam. 这位政治家令人作呕地重复着他的竞选口号。|例句：He talked about his new car ad nauseam. 他喋喋不休地谈论着他的新车。|近义词：excessively, tediously, repeatedly|反义词: briefly, concisely|词性:副词
