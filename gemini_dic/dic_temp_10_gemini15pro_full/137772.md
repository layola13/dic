nonvassals:/ˌnɒnˈvæsəlz/
n.
非附庸者；非臣属
People or groups who are not subject to the authority of a feudal lord.
The nonvassals were free to trade with whomever they chose.  非附庸者可以自由地与他们选择的任何人进行贸易。
The king's power did not extend to the nonvassals living beyond the mountains. 国王的权力并未延伸到居住在山外的非附庸者。
synonyms: freemen, commoners, independents
antonyms: vassals, subjects, dependents
