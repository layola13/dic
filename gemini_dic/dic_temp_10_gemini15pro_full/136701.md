nonsegregated: /ˌnɒnˈsɛɡrɪɡeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 未隔离的，不分开的 | Not separated or divided on the basis of race, religion, or other criteria. |  This is a nonsegregated school, where children of all backgrounds learn together. 这是一所不分种族的学校，各种背景的孩子都在一起学习。|  integrated, mixed, unified, desegregated | segregated, separated, divided | adjective

Etymology:
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + segregated (past participle of segregate, from Latin segregatus, past participle of segregare "to set apart," from se- "apart" + gregare "to flock, herd," from grex "flock, herd").
