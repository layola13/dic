byes:/baɪz/
n.
告别，再见
An informal, plural form of "bye," used to express farewell.
"See ya later, byes!" 再见啦！
近义词: farewells, goodbyes
反义词: greetings, hellos
词性: 名词

词源: Shortened form of "goodbyes," from "God be with you."
