papain:/pəˈpeɪɪn/ /ˈpɑːpeɪn/| n. |木瓜蛋白酶|A proteolytic enzyme obtained from the latex of the papaya, used to tenderize meat and as a digestive aid.|Papain is often used as a meat tenderizer.  木瓜蛋白酶经常被用作嫩肉剂。

Papain can also be used to treat certain medical conditions. 木瓜蛋白酶也可以用来治疗某些疾病。|近义词：protease, enzyme|反义词: (No direct antonym, but inhibitors exist like alpha-1 antitrypsin) |词性:noun
