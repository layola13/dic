leatheriness:/ˈleðərɪnɪs/
n.|坚韧；像皮革的性质；皮革状|The leatheriness of his skin was a result of years spent working outdoors. 他的皮肤粗糙是他多年在户外工作的结果。|例句：The old saddle had a certain leatheriness that spoke of years of use.  这旧马鞍有种皮革般的坚韧，表明它已经使用了多年。

例句：The leatheriness of the fruit made it difficult to peel. 这种水果的皮革般坚韧使得它很难剥皮。|近义词：toughness, firmness, rigidity|反义词:softness, suppleness, pliability|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From leather + -iness.  "leather" comes from Old English "lether"  meaning skin of an animal. The suffix "-iness" forms nouns denoting a state or quality.
