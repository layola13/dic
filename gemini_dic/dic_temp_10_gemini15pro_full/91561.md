hyper-reactive:/ˌhaɪpər riˈæk.tɪv/
adjective
过度反应的
Characterized by exaggerated or excessive reactions, often in response to stimuli that would not normally elicit such a strong response.
Her hyper-reactive immune system caused her to have allergies to many common substances. 她的过度活跃的免疫系统导致她对许多常见物质过敏。

The child's hyper-reactive behavior made it difficult for him to focus in class.  这个孩子过度活跃的行为使他难以集中注意力上课。

Synonym: oversensitive, hypersensitive
Antonym: unresponsive, insensitive
词性: adjective
