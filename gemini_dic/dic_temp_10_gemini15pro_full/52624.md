diggable:/ˈdɪɡəbl/| adj.|可理解的，易懂的 (informal)|Something that is diggable is easy to understand or appreciate. |His explanation of quantum physics was surprisingly diggable. 他对量子物理学的解释出奇地容易理解。|Synonyms: understandable, comprehensible|Antonyms: incomprehensible, obscure|adjective
