banquette:/bæŋˈkɛt/ | n. | 长椅，宴会 | A long upholstered bench, often placed against a wall, especially in a restaurant. |  The restaurant had cozy banquettes along one wall.  这家餐厅沿着一面墙摆放着舒适的长椅。|Synonyms: bench, seat, sofa | Antonyms: chair, stool | noun
