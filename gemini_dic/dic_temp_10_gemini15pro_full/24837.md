browned:/braʊnd/
|v. adj.| 变成褐色，褐色的|To make or become brown, typically by cooking or exposure to the sun.|The bread browned nicely in the toaster. 面包在烤面包机里烤得很好，变成了漂亮的褐色。|近义词：roasted, toasted, seared, tanned|反义词:whitened, bleached|词性:动词过去式/过去分词，形容词

browned:/braʊnd/
|adj.| 褐色的|Having a brown color, especially as a result of cooking or exposure to the sun.|She had browned skin from spending the summer at the beach.  她在海滩度过了夏天，皮肤晒成了褐色。|近义词：bronze, tan, tawny|反义词:pale, fair|词性:形容词

词源:  From the Old English word "brun", meaning "dark or dusky".  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*brūnaz".
