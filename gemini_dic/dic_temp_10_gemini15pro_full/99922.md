internecine:/ˌɪntərˈniːsaɪn/ | adj. |  内讧的；两败俱伤的；毁灭性的 | Mutually destructive; characterized by great slaughter and bloodshed within a group. | The internecine warfare within the company ultimately led to its downfall. 公司内部的两败俱伤的斗争最终导致了它的垮台。|  Example: The internecine struggles within the political party threatened to tear it apart.  党内斗争可能会导致该党分裂。 | Synonyms:  internal, fratricidal, deadly, ruinous | Antonyms:  harmonious, peaceful, constructive | Word Type: adjective
