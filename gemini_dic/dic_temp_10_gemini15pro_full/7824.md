antagonize: /ænˈtæɡənaɪz/ | vt. | 使对抗；引起…的敌意；激怒 | To cause someone to become hostile or unfriendly. |  His remarks antagonized many voters. 他的言论激怒了许多选民。|  She didn't want to antagonize her boss. 她不想惹恼她的老板。 | synonyms: alienate, provoke, irritate, offend | antonyms: appease, conciliate, placate | verb

antagonize: /ænˈtæɡənaɪz/ | vt. | 对…起反作用；中和 | To act in opposition to; counteract. |  The medication antagonized the effects of the poison. 这种药物抵消了毒药的作用。| This herb antagonizes the other ingredients in the mixture. 这种草药会中和混合物中的其他成分。 | synonyms: counteract, neutralize, oppose | antonyms: complement, enhance, reinforce | verb
