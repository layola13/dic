hutch:/hʌtʃ/ | n. | 笼子，棚屋，碗橱 | A box or cage, typically with a wire mesh front, for keeping rabbits or other small domesticated animals. Also, a small, simply built dwelling or shelter.  A cupboard, typically of plain design, especially one used for storing food or displaying china. | The rabbit lives in a hutch in the garden. 兔子住在花园里的笼子里。


The miners lived in simple hutches in the mountains. 矿工们住在山里简陋的棚屋里。


We keep our best china in a hutch in the dining room. 我们把最好的瓷器放在餐厅的碗橱里。 | 近义词：cage, pen, coop, cabin, shed, cupboard | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "mansion" for dwelling contrast) | 词性: 名词
