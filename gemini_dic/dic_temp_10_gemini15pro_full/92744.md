ichthyography: n.
ichthyography: the branch of zoology dealing with the description of fishes.
ichthyography:  The study or description of fishes.  It encompasses classification, anatomy, behavior, and distribution.
ichthyography:  His research in ichthyography led to the discovery of several new species.  他关于鱼类学的研使得他发现了几个新的物种。
ichthyography:  Near synonyms: fish biology, fish science.
ichthyography:  No direct antonym.
