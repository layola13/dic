neediness:/ˈniːdinəs/| n.|需要；贫困；依赖性|The state of being needy; a pressing need or want, especially for affection, attention, or reassurance.|Her constant neediness was exhausting. 她持续的需求感令人疲惫不堪。|Synonyms: dependence, insecurity, vulnerability|Antonyms: independence, self-sufficiency, security|noun

neediness:/ˈniːdinəs/| n.|需要；贫困；依赖性|A condition marked by a strong need for affection, attention, reassurance, or the like, often to an excessive degree.|He displayed a surprising neediness for someone so successful. 对于一个如此成功的人来说，他表现出惊人的依赖感。|Synonyms: clinginess, desperation, fragility|Antonyms: confidence, strength, resilience|noun
