hypervascularities:/ˌhaɪpərvæskjʊˈlærɪtiz/
noun
血管增生，血管过多
The presence of an excessive number of blood vessels in a tissue or organ.
MRI revealed multiple hepatic hypervascularities. 磁共振成像显示肝脏多发血管增生。
hypervascularization, neovascularization, angiogenesis
hypovascularity, avascularity
