hatchback:/ˈhætʃˌbæk/ | n. | 掀背车 | A car with a sloping back door that opens upwards.  A hatchback combines features of a sedan and a station wagon, offering more cargo space than a typical sedan but less than a station wagon. |  I prefer hatchbacks because they're easier to park. 我更喜欢掀背车，因为它们更容易停车。

 My family needs a hatchback for all our camping gear.  我家需要一辆掀背车来装我们所有的野营装备。 | 近义词: liftback, five-door | 反义词: sedan, coupe, convertible | 词性: noun
