cantonal:/ˈkæntənl/| adj.|州的；州级的；邦的|Relating to a canton (a subdivision of a country, especially Switzerland or France).|例句：The cantonal government is responsible for education. 州政府负责教育。|例句：Each cantonal bank operates independently.  每个州立银行都独立运营。|近义词：regional, state, provincial|反义词：federal, national|词性:形容词
Etymology: From French *cantonal*, from *canton* ("canton"), from Italian *cantone* ("corner, district"), ultimately from Vulgar Latin *canto* ("corner"), from Latin *cantus* ("corner, tire rim").
