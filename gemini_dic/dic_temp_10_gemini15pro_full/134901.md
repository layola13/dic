nonmultiplicational:/ˌnɒnˌmʌltɪplɪˈkeɪʃənəl/
| adj. |非倍增的，非乘法的|Not involving or characterized by multiplication; not increasing by a constant factor.|The growth was nonmultiplicational, increasing by a fixed amount each year rather than a percentage.|近义词:additive, linear|反义词:multiplicative, exponential|词性:形容词
词源:non- (not) + multiplicational (relating to multiplication)
