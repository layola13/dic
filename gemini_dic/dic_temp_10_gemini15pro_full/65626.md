evangelising:/ɪˈvændʒəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
传福音；宣扬福音；劝化
The act of preaching the gospel or spreading a particular belief system enthusiastically.
They were evangelising in the town square. 他们在城镇广场上传福音。
propagating, proselytizing, preaching
suppressing, concealing
v.
