attract:/əˈtrækt/| v. |引起；激起|To cause (something) to happen or be done.|His unusual behavior attracted the attention of the police. 他异常的行为引起了警方的注意。|Synonyms: draw, evoke, elicit|Antonyms: deter, discourage|verb
