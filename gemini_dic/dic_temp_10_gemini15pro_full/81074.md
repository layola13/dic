grains:/greɪnz/ | n. | 谷物；颗粒；少量；气质；纹理 | Small, hard pieces of something, such as sand or salt; seeds of plants such as wheat, corn, rice, etc., that are used for food. | The farmer scattered grains of wheat for the chickens. 农民撒了一些麦粒给鸡吃。| Synonyms: kernels, seeds, particles, bits | Antonyms: wholes, lumps, masses | noun

grains:/greɪnz/ | n. | 木纹；纹理；性格 | The arrangement of the lines and fibers in wood or other materials; the basic nature or character of someone or something. | The wood has a beautiful grain. 这块木头的纹理很漂亮。| Synonyms: texture, pattern, fiber, nature, temperament | Antonyms: smoothness, uniformity | noun
