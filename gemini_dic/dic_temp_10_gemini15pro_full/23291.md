boxinesses:/ˈbɒksɪnəsɪz/
noun
方形，箱状，笨重
The quality of being boxy; resembling a box in shape.
The old car's boxinesses made it difficult to park.  这辆旧车的笨重使其难以停车。
bulkiness, clumsiness, squareness
sleekness, streamlinedness
