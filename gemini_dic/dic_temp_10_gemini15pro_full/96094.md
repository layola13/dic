indefensive:/ˌɪndɪˈfɛnsɪv/
adjective
无防备的，不设防的
unable or unwilling to protect oneself from criticism or attack.
He looked young and vulnerable, almost indefensive. 他看起来很年轻，很脆弱，几乎没有防备。
近义词:vulnerable, unprotected, exposed
反义词:defensive, protected, guarded
词性:adjective
词源:in- (not) + defensive (protective)
