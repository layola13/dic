PHRASE->
campaign for = 为...而进行竞选活动
campaign against = 反对...而进行竞选活动
on the campaign trail = 在竞选途中
campaign headquarters = 竞选总部
campaign manager = 竞选经理

词源：campaign (n.) 来自法语 campagne "open country," 源自晚期拉丁语 campania "level stretch of country," 源自拉丁语 campus "level field."  -ing 是构成动名词和现在分词的后缀。
