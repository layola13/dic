malthusianisms:/mælˈθuːziənɪzəmz/
n.
马尔萨斯主义；人口过剩的学说
Theories derived from or related to the writings of Thomas Robert Malthus, particularly his pessimistic predictions about population growth exceeding resource availability.
The politician's malthusianisms about overpopulation were met with criticism.  这位政治家关于人口过剩的马尔萨斯主义观点遭到了批评。
Near Synonyms: Malthusian doctrines, Malthusian principles, overpopulation theories
Antonyms:  Cornucopianism, abundance theories
noun
