catechuic: /ˌkætɪˈtʃuːɪk/ | adj. | 儿茶的，含儿茶的 | Relating to, derived from, or resembling catechu; containing catechu. | Catechuic acid is a component of catechu. 儿茶酸是儿茶的一种成分。 | The catechuic tannins give the wood a reddish-brown color. 儿茶单宁使木材呈红褐色。 |  近义词: tannic, astringent | 反义词:  (No specific antonym) | 词性: adjective

词源: From catechu + -ic.  Catechu itself comes from Malay *kachu, from Tamil *kāttu.
