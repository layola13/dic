fee'd:/fiːd/
|v.|已付酬金的；已付费的|paid a fee; having received a fee|例句：The lawyer is already fee'd. 律师已经收到了酬金。|近义词：compensated, remunerated, paid|反义词：unpaid, owing|词性：动词(过去分词形式，用作形容词)


Etymology->
From Middle English *feen*, from Old French *feer*, from Medieval Latin *feudum*, from Frankish *fehu* ("cattle, property").  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *fehu* ("cattle, wealth"). Cognate with Dutch *vee* ("cattle"), German *Vieh* ("cattle"), Old English *feoh* ("cattle, property, money").  The meaning shifted from "property held in return for service" to "payment for services."
