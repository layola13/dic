exiles: /ˈeksaɪlz/ | n. | 流亡者，被流放的人 | people who are forced to live in a foreign country because they are not allowed to live in their own country |  Many political exiles sought refuge in the United States. 许多政治流亡者在美国寻求庇护。| synonyms: refugees, emigres, displaced persons | antonyms: citizens, nationals, residents | noun

exiles: /ˈeksaɪlz/ | v. | 流放，放逐 | to force someone to live in a foreign country because they are not allowed to live in their own country | The dictator exiled his opponents to a remote island.  独裁者将他的反对者流放到一个偏远的岛屿。| synonyms: banish, deport, expel | antonyms: repatriate, admit, welcome | verb
