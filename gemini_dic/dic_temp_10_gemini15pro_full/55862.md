divinistre: /dɪˈvɪnɪstər/ | n. | 占卜者，预言家 | A divinistre is someone who practices divination, or the art of foretelling the future.  |  The old divinistre predicted a great famine.  这位老预言家预言将有一场大饥荒。 | synonyms: soothsayer, seer, prophet, oracle, fortune teller | antonyms: skeptic, doubter | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *diviniste*, from Late Latin *divinista*, from Latin *divinus* (“divine”), from *divus* (“god”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\*deiwós* (“god”).
