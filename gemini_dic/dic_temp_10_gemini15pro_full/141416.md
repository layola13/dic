ophite:/ˈoʊfaɪt/ | n. | 辉绿岩 | A rock of diabasic or basaltic character, consisting essentially of plagioclase and augite, and containing also magnetite, biotite, or other minerals.  It is a variety of dolerite containing much augite. | The ophite formation is common in certain geological areas. 辉绿岩地层在某些地质区域很常见。| The geologist identified the sample as ophite. 地质学家将样品鉴定为辉绿岩。 | Synonyms: dolerite, diabase | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
