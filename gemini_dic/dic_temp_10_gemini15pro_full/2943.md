advocate: /ˈædvəkət/ (n.) /ˈædvəkeɪt/ (v.)|n. 提倡者，拥护者；辩护律师；v. 提倡，拥护，主张|n.  A person who publicly supports or recommends a particular cause or policy.  v. Publicly recommend or support.|n.  She is a strong advocate for animal rights. 她是一位坚定的动物权利倡导者。

v.  He advocates for stricter gun control laws. 他主张更严格的枪支管制法。|n. supporter, proponent, champion; lawyer, counsel|v. support, promote, champion, recommend|n. opponent, critic|v. oppose, denounce|noun & verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English advocat, from Latin advocatus (“one called to aid; advocate, intercessor”), past participle of advocare (“to call to one's aid”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + vocare (“to call”).

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He advocates for free education. = 他倡导免费教育。
She advocates a healthy lifestyle. = 她提倡健康的生活方式。

一般过去时:
They advocated for peace during the war. = 战争期间，他们倡导和平。
The group advocated changes to the law. = 该团体主张修改法律。

现在完成时:
He has advocated for this cause for many years. = 多年来，他一直倡导这项事业。
She has advocated several different approaches to the problem. = 她提出了几种不同的方法来解决这个问题。

过去完成时:
They had advocated for this policy before it was implemented. = 在这项政策实施之前，他们就一直倡导这项政策。
She had advocated for him even when others doubted him. = 即使别人怀疑他，她也一直为他辩护。

将来时:
I will advocate for your rights. = 我会维护你的权利。
They will advocate a new approach to the problem. = 他们将提倡一种解决问题的新方法。

虚拟语气:
If I were in charge, I would advocate for a different solution. = 如果我负责，我会提倡一个不同的解决方案。
I wish they would advocate for the rights of the poor. = 我希望他们能为穷人的权利辩护。

被动语态:
This policy is advocated by many experts. = 这项政策得到了许多专家的拥护。
A change in the law was advocated by the protesters. = 抗议者主张修改法律。

疑问句:
Do you advocate for animal rights? = 你支持动物权利吗？
What changes do they advocate? = 他们主张哪些改变？

否定句:
I do not advocate violence. = 我不提倡暴力。
They did not advocate for this policy. = 他们没有倡导这项政策。


PHRASE->
advocate for = 为...辩护/倡导
a strong advocate = 坚定的拥护者
an advocate of = ...的支持者/拥护者
advocate a policy = 提倡一项政策
advocate change = 提倡改变
