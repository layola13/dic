indetermined:/ˌɪndɪˈtɜːrmɪnd/| adj.|未确定的，不明确的|Not exactly known, established, or defined.|The cause of the fire remains undetermined. 火灾原因仍然不明。|近义词:uncertain, unknown, undefined, unresolved|反义词:determined, definite, known, resolved|词性:形容词
词源:Late Middle English: from Latin indeterminatus, from in- ‘not’ + determinatus (see determinate).
