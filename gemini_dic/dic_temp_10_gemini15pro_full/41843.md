corybant:/ˈkɔːrɪbænt/ | n. | 狂热的追随者；喧闹狂乱的人 | A frenzied follower; a wildly excited, noisy person.  Derived from the Corybantes, mythical attendants of the Phrygian goddess Cybele, known for their ecstatic dances and noisy music. | The politician's corybants cheered wildly at his every word. 这位政治家的狂热追随者对他说的每一个字都欢呼雀跃。 |  Synonyms: fanatic, zealot, enthusiast, devotee | Antonyms: critic, skeptic, opponent | Noun

corybantic:/ˌkɔːrɪˈbæn.tɪk/ | adj. | 狂热的；喧闹狂乱的 | Frenzied; wildly excited and noisy. | The corybantic music filled the air, inciting the dancers to even greater heights of frenzy.  喧闹的音乐充满了空气，煽动舞者达到更高的狂乱状态。 | The crowd reacted with corybantic enthusiasm to the announcement. 人群对这一消息的反应是狂热的。 | Synonyms: frantic, frenetic, ecstatic, wild | Antonyms: calm, peaceful, tranquil | Adjective
