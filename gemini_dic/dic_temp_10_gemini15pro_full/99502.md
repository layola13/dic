interfolded:/ˌɪntərˈfoʊldɪd/
adjective
交叠的，互相折叠的
Folded together; interwoven.  Layers of different materials are interfolded to create a strong composite.
The sheets of paper were carefully interfolded. 这些纸张被仔细地交叠在一起。
近义词: interlaced, interwoven, intertwined
反义词: unfolded, separated
词性: adjective
词源: From inter- + fold.  "Inter-" signifies "between" or "among," and "fold" refers to the act of bending something over on itself.
