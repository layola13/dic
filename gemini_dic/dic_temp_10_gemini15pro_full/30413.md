centonism: /ˈsɛntənɪzəm/ | n. | 集锦诗；集锦文；集锦画 | The act of creating a work of art or literature by piecing together lines or passages from other works. |  The professor criticized the student's paper, calling it a blatant example of centonism. 教授批评了学生的论文，称其为公然的集锦文。 |  She created a beautiful poem using centonism, weaving together lines from Shakespeare and Keats. 她用集锦诗的方式创作了一首美丽的诗，将莎士比亚和济慈的诗句交织在一起。 |  pastiche, collage, patchwork |  original work,  new creation | noun
