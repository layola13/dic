geologists:/dʒiˈɒlədʒɪsts/ | n. | 地质学家 | A scientist who studies the earth, its structure, history, and processes. |  The geologists studied the rock formations to determine the age of the mountain range. 地质学家们研究了岩层以确定山脉的年龄。

The geologists predicted the eruption of the volcano. 地质学家预测了火山爆发。 | 近义词: earth scientist, geoscientist | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


Etymology:
From geology + -ist. Geology comes from the Greek gē "earth" + -logia "study of," a suffix of nouns denoting fields of study, as in biology, theology, etc.
