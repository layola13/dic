frigging:/ˈfrɪɡɪŋ/| adj.|该死的，讨厌的，非常的 (粗俗用法)|A mild oath, used for emphasis or to express annoyance.  It's a euphemism for "fucking".|例句：This frigging computer is so slow! 这该死的电脑太慢了！

例句：It's frigging cold outside! 外面真他妈冷！|近义词：damn, bloody, blasted|反义词: (No direct antonym for this intensifier)|词性:adjective
