enzootic:/ɛnzoʊˈɒtɪk/ | adj.  n.| 地方性的（疾病）；地方性疾病 |  (of a disease) regularly affecting animals in a particular district or at a particular season.  A disease that is enzootic in a population. | 例句：The enzootic strain of the virus is relatively mild. 这种病毒的地方性菌株相对温和。| 例句：Enzootic bovine leukosis is a significant problem for cattle farmers. 牛地方性白血病是养牛户面临的一个重大问题。 | 近义词：endemic, native, indigenous | 反义词：epidemic, pandemic | 词性: adjective, noun
