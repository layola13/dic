cynicism: /ˈsɪnɪˌsɪzəm/ | n. | 愤世嫉俗；犬儒主义 | An attitude of distrust toward claimed ethical and social values; a belief that people are motivated only by selfishness. |  He displayed a growing cynicism about the prospects for peace. 他对和平前景表现出越来越愤世嫉俗的态度。|  pessimism, negativity, skepticism | optimism, idealism, faith | noun

cynical: /ˈsɪnɪkəl/ | adj. | 愤世嫉俗的；犬儒主义的 | Believing that people are motivated purely by self-interest; distrustful of human sincerity or integrity. |  She's become cynical about men. 她变得对男人很愤世嫉俗。 | pessimistic, skeptical, distrustful | optimistic, trusting, naive | adjective
