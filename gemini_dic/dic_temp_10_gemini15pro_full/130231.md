non-significant:/ˌnɒn sɪɡˈnɪfɪkənt/ (US) /ˌnɒn sɪɡˈnɪfɪkənt/ (UK)| adj.|无意义的；不重要的；不显著的|Not important or significant; having no noticeable or measureable effect.|The study found a non-significant difference between the two groups. 这项研究发现两组之间没有显著差异。|Synonyms: unimportant, trivial, negligible, insignificant, inconsequential|Antonyms: significant, important, substantial, crucial, vital|Adjective

non-significant:/ˌnɒn sɪɡˈnɪfɪkənt/ (US) /ˌnɒn sɪɡˈnɪfɪkənt/ (UK)| n.|不重要的东西|Something that is not significant.|The non-significants were discarded from the analysis. 不重要的东西被从分析中剔除。|Synonyms: trivia, minutiae|Antonyms: essentials, key factors|Noun (rare usage)
