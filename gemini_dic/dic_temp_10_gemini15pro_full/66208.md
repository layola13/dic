excitant:/ɪkˈsaɪtənt/| n. adj.|兴奋剂；刺激物；刺激的|Something that excites or stimulates.|The chef used chili peppers as an excitant in the dish.  厨师在菜里用辣椒作为刺激物。|  This new drug acts as a cardiac excitant. 这种新药充当心脏兴奋剂。 |近义词：stimulant, stimulus, aphrodisiac|反义词：depressant, sedative|词性:名词, 形容词
词源: From French excitant, present participle of exciter (“to excite”), from Latin excitāre (“to rouse, awaken, stimulate”), frequentative of exciēre (“to call forth, summon, rouse”), from ex (“out of, from”) + ciēre (“to set in motion, stir, rouse”).
