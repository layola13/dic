complainants:/kəmˈpleɪnənts/
noun
原告，申诉人，抱怨者
People who formally complain about something, especially to an authority.
The complainants alleged that the company had discriminated against them. 原告声称该公司歧视他们。
complainants filed a lawsuit against the city. 申诉人对该市提起了诉讼。

近义词: accusers, plaintiffs, objectors
反义词: defendants, respondents
