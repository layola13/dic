lucubrate: /ˈluːkjʊˌbreɪt/ (US), /ˈluːkjuːˌbreɪt/ (UK)
verb
刻苦钻研，埋头苦读；(尤指在夜间)工作或学习
to study or work, especially at night, with great concentration and effort
He would lucubrate upon his research for hours each evening. 他每晚都会花几个小时刻苦钻研他的研究。
Synonyms: study, work, toil, ponder, contemplate
Antonyms: idle, relax, procrastinate
词性：动词
