monomanias:/ˌmɒnəˈmeɪniəz/ /ˌmɑːnəˈmeɪniəz/
|n.|偏执狂，狂热|An exaggerated or obsessive enthusiasm for or preoccupation with one thing.|He had a monomania about cleanliness. 他有洁癖。|近义词:obsession, fixation, preoccupation|反义词: indifference, apathy|Noun
