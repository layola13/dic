hypochondriasm: /ˌhaɪpəˈkɒndrɪæzəm/ (US) /ˌhaɪpəˈkɒndrɪəzəm/ (UK)| n. |疑病症，臆想病|A psychological disorder characterized by excessive anxiety or fear about having a serious illness, despite medical reassurance.|例句：Her hypochondriasm led her to constantly seek medical attention. 她的疑病症导致她不断寻求医疗帮助。

例句：He was so consumed by hypochondriasm that he couldn't enjoy life.  他被疑病症折磨得无法享受生活。

近义词：health anxiety, illness anxiety disorder, nosophobia|反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider)  robust health, well-being |词性: noun


hypochondriac: /ˌhaɪpəˈkɒndrɪæk/ (US) /ˌhaɪpəˈkɒndrɪæk/ (UK)| n. adj. |疑病症患者，疑病的|A person who suffers from hypochondriasm. Also used as an adjective to describe someone exhibiting symptoms of hypochondriasm.|例句：He's a hypochondriac who always thinks he's dying. 他是个疑病症患者，总是觉得自己快死了。

例句：She had hypochondriac tendencies, constantly checking for lumps and bumps. 她有疑病症倾向，经常检查自己身上有没有肿块。

近义词：(n.) worrier, valetudinarian (adj.) anxious, neurotic |反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider)  carefree, stoic |词性: noun, adjective
