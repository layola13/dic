overexpresses:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspresiz/
verb
过度表达
causes (a gene) to be transcribed more actively than normal, resulting in an excessive amount of the encoded protein.
The tumor overexpresses a particular protein.  这个肿瘤过度表达一种特殊的蛋白质。
The genetically modified plants overexpress the enzyme responsible for drought tolerance.  这些转基因植物过度表达了负责耐旱性的酶。
synonyms: upregulate, overproduce
antonyms: downregulate, underexpress
v.
