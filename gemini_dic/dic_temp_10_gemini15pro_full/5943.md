amblyopia: /ˌæmbliˈoʊpiə/ (US), /ˌæmbliˈəʊpiə/ (UK)| n. |弱视|A condition in which the vision in one eye is reduced because the eye and brain are not working together properly.  It usually develops in early childhood.|Example 1: Early detection and treatment of amblyopia are crucial for preventing permanent vision loss. 早期发现和治疗弱视对于预防永久性视力丧失至关重要。

Example 2:  The child's amblyopia was corrected with an eye patch.  这个孩子的弱视通过眼罩矫正了。|Synonym: lazy eye|Antonym: normal vision|Noun
