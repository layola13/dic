gallivant: /ˈɡæləvænt/ | v. | 逍遥,游荡,寻欢作乐 | To go around from one place to another in a lively, excited way, usually looking for pleasure or adventure. | She spent her gap year gallivanting around Southeast Asia. 她用间隔年在东南亚四处游荡。 | 近义词: roam, wander, gad, carouse | 反义词: stay, remain, settle | 词性: 动词

词源:  Possibly from obsolete *gallivant "to behave like a gallant," from gallant "brave, showy," originally "stylish, splendid."  可能源于过时的 *gallivant “像个勇士一样行事”，来自 gallant “勇敢的，炫耀的”，最初意思是“时髦的，华丽的”。
