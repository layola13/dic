deluder:/dɪˈludər/| n.|欺骗者，诱惑者|Someone who deludes or deceives others. |例句：The con artist was a skilled deluder, easily tricking people out of their money.  这个骗子是个老练的欺诈者，很容易骗走人们的钱。

例句：She felt betrayed by the deluder who had promised her the world. 她感到被那个向她承诺了整个世界的人欺骗了。|近义词：deceiver, trickster, charlatan, fraud, imposter|反义词：honest person, truth-teller|词性: noun
