nonintuitiveness:/ˌnɒnɪnˈtjuːɪtɪvnəs/ | n. | 非直观性，反直觉性 | The quality of not being easily understood or grasped by intuition; the state of being counterintuitive. | The nonintuitiveness of quantum mechanics often baffles students. 量子力学的非直观性常常使学生感到困惑。

The nonintuitiveness of the software interface made it difficult to use.  这个软件界面的非直觉性使得它很难使用。 | 近义词: counterintuitiveness, opacity, complexity | 反义词: intuitiveness, obviousness, clarity | 词性: noun
