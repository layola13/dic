defalcator:/dɪˈfɔːlkeɪtər/
|n.| 亏空公款者；挪用公款者；贪污者|A person who embezzles or misappropriates money, especially public funds. | The defalcator was sentenced to ten years in prison. 亏空公款者被判处十年监禁。| Synonyms: embezzler, peculator, thief | Antonyms: benefactor, philanthropist | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin defalcator, from defalcare "to cut off, deduct," from de- "down, off" + falx (genitive falcis) "sickle."
