atrocious:/əˈtroʊʃəs/
adjective
残暴的，凶恶的；糟糕的，极坏的
Exceptionally bad or unpleasant.
The food at the restaurant was atrocious.  这家餐馆的食物糟透了。
The weather has been atrocious all week. 这周的天气一直很糟糕。
synonyms: terrible, awful, dreadful, appalling, abysmal, horrendous, heinous, monstrous, egregious
antonyms: excellent, wonderful, superb, magnificent, delightful, pleasant
词性: 形容词
