meanderers:/miˈændərərz/
n.
漫步者，蜿蜒曲折的东西
People who meander; things that meander.
The meanderers strolled along the riverbank. 漫步者沿着河岸漫步。
The river's meanderers created a beautiful landscape.  河流的蜿蜒曲折创造了美丽的风景。
近义词:wanderers, ramblers, strollers
反义词:rushers, sprinters
