mat:/mæt/| n.|垫子，席子，草席，(桌上的)餐垫|A piece of thick material used to cover a floor, protect a surface, or provide a soft surface.|例句：She wiped her feet on the door mat. 她用门口的垫子擦了擦脚。|例句：He placed the hot dish on a mat. 他把热菜放在垫子上。|近义词：rug, carpet, pad|反义词:(没有直接的反义词)|noun
