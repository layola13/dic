bottom-out:/ˈbɑːtəm aʊt/ /ˈbɒtəm aʊt/| v.|触底反弹；达到最低点|To reach the lowest point and then start to rise again.  A stock price may bottom out after a period of decline. |The housing market bottomed out last year and has since begun to recover.| 近义词:reach a low point, hit rock bottom, trough| 反义词:peak, climax, top out|词性:动词

bottom-out:/ˈbɑːtəm aʊt/ /ˈbɒtəm aʊt/| n.|底部；最低点|The lowest point of something.  The bottom-out of the recession marked a turning point for the economy.|The bottom-out price for the car was $5,000.| 近义词:nadir, low point, trough| 反义词:peak, apex, summit|词性:名词
