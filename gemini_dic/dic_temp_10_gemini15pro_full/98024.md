inordinacy:/ˌɪnɔːrˈdɪnəsi/ | n. | 过度，无节制，放肆 | Lack of moderation; excessiveness. |  His inordinacy in spending led him to bankruptcy. 他的过度消费导致他破产。|  The inordinacy of her grief surprised everyone. 她过度悲伤令所有人惊讶。| synonyms: excess, immoderation, extravagance, exorbitance | antonyms: moderation, restraint, temperance | noun

inordinate:/ɪnˈɔːrdɪnət/ | adj. | 过度的，过分的，无节制的 | Exceeding reasonable limits; excessive. | The restaurant charged an inordinate amount for a simple meal. 这家餐厅对一顿简单的饭菜收取过高的费用。 | She felt an inordinate amount of pressure to succeed. 她感到巨大的压力要成功。 | synonyms: excessive, immoderate, extravagant, exorbitant | antonyms: moderate, reasonable, temperate | adjective
