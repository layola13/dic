nectarine:/ˈnektərɪn/ /ˈnektəriːn/| n.|油桃，蜜桃|A smooth-skinned peach. A sweet, juicy fruit that is like a peach but has smooth skin.|例句：I prefer nectarines to peaches because I don't like the fuzzy skin. 我喜欢油桃胜过桃子，因为我不喜欢毛茸茸的表皮。

例句：The nectarines at the farmer's market were perfectly ripe.  农贸市场的油桃熟得恰到好处。|近义词：peach, apricot|反义词: None|词性:noun
