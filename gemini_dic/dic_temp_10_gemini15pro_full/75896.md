funder:/ˈfʌndər/ | n. | 资助者，赞助者 | A person or organization that provides funds for a project, cause, or organization. | The university received a large donation from a wealthy funder. 这所大学收到了一位富有资助者的大笔捐赠。|  近义词:sponsor, backer, benefactor, patron, donor | 反义词: recipient, beneficiary | 词性: 名词

funder:/ˈfʌndər/ | 词源: From the verb "fund" + "-er" (agent suffix), ultimately from Latin "fundus" (bottom, foundation). |  |  |  |  | 词性: 名词
