hydrophobic:/ˌhaɪdrəˈfəʊbɪk/ | adj. | 疏水的，憎水的 | Tending to repel or fail to mix with water. | Oil is hydrophobic and does not mix with water. 油是疏水的，不会与水混合。 |  Hydrophobic coatings are used to protect surfaces from water damage. 疏水涂层用于保护表面免受水损害。 | 近义词: water-repellent, nonpolar | 反义词: hydrophilic, polar | 词性: adjective
Etymology:  From Greek *hydro-* (water) + *-phobos* (fearing).
