assonance:/ˈæsənəns/ | n. |  类韵，谐音，音的相似 |  The repetition of vowel sounds in nearby words.  This is used as a literary device to create internal rhyming within phrases or sentences. |  The rain in Spain falls mainly on the plain is a classic example of assonance.  西班牙的雨主要下在平原上，这是一个典型的类韵例子。

The words "fade" and "pale" share assonance with the long "a" sound.  “fade”和“pale”这两个词都包含长元音“a”，构成类韵。 |  近义词：rhyme, resonance | 反义词：dissonance | 词性: noun
