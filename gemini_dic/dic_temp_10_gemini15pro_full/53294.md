dirtinesses:/ˈdɜːrtɪnɪsɪz/
n.
污秽，肮脏
The state of being dirty; filth.
The dirtinesses of the city shocked the tourists. 城市的污秽震惊了游客。

近义词:filth, grime, squalor, muck, dirt
反义词:cleanliness, purity, neatness
词性:名词(plural)


词源:
From Middle English *dirteness, equivalent to dirty +‎ -ness.
