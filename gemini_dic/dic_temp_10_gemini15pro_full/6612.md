anacreontic:/ˌænəkriˈɒntɪk/
adjective
欢宴的；酒神风格的；享乐的
Relating to or suggestive of the Greek lyric poet Anacreon; convivial; characterized by or celebrating love and wine.
The party had a decidedly anacreontic atmosphere, with music, dancing, and free-flowing wine.  这个派对充满了酒神风格的氛围，音乐舞蹈，美酒佳肴。
His anacreontic poetry celebrated the joys of wine and love. 他的享乐诗歌歌颂了美酒与爱情的乐趣。
Synonyms:convivial, bacchanalian, festive, hedonistic
Antonyms:ascetic, austere, puritanical
词性:adjective


anacreontic:/ˌænəkriˈɑːntɪk/
noun
以酒神风格写成的诗歌；欢宴诗
A poem in the style of Anacreon, typically celebrating love and wine.
He wrote several anacreontics in praise of his beloved. 他写了几首欢宴诗来赞美他的爱人。
The collection included a number of lighthearted anacreontics.  这本诗集包含了一些轻松愉快的欢宴诗。
Synonyms:ode, lyric, poem
Antonyms:N/A
词性:noun
