hydrometallurgical:/ˌhaɪdrəʊˌmɛtəlˈɜːrdʒɪkəl/
adjective
湿法冶金的
Relating to the extraction of metals from their ores by dissolving them in a suitable solvent and recovering them from the solution.
Hydrometallurgical processes are used to extract gold from low-grade ores. 湿法冶金工艺用于从低品位矿石中提取金。
Hydrometallurgy offers a more environmentally friendly way to extract certain metals. 湿法冶金提供了一种更环保的方法来提取某些金属。
Near Synonyms: chemical extraction, leaching
Antonyms: pyrometallurgical
词性: adjective
