cashless:/ˈkæʃləs/
adjective
无现金的
Relating to, or operating by, a system that does not use cash.
Sweden is moving towards a cashless society.  瑞典正在走向无现金社会。
Many businesses now operate in a cashless environment. 许多企业现在都在无现金环境中运营。
cashless transactions 无现金交易
近义词: electronic, digital
反义词: cash-based
