metallization:/ˌmɛtələˈzeɪʃən/
n.|金属化，金属镀层|The process of coating a non-metallic surface with a metal.  将非金属表面镀上一层金属的过程。|The metallization of the plastic components improved their conductivity.  塑料部件的金属化提高了它们的导电性。|plating, coating|non-metallization
n.|金属化|The conversion of something into metal. 将某物转化为金属。|The alchemists' dream was the metallization of base elements. 炼金术士的梦想是将贱金属转化为贵金属。|transformation, conversion|demetallization
