ferruginous:/fəˈrʊdʒɪnəs/
adj.
铁锈色的，含铁的
containing or consisting of iron oxide; resembling iron rust in color
The geologist identified the ferruginous soil. 地质学家识别出了含铁的土壤。
ferruginous rocks. 铁锈色的岩石。

近义词:rusty, iron-rich, iron-bearing
反义词:non-ferruginous


词性:adjective
