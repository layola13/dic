paralytically:/ˌpærəˈlɪtɪkli/ | adv. | 瘫痪地；麻痹地 | In a way that suggests paralysis, either physical or mental.  | He sat paralytically in the chair, unable to move or speak. 他瘫坐在椅子上，不能动也不能说话。 |  The news hit her paralytically, leaving her speechless and stunned.  这个消息让她感到震惊，说不出话来，目瞪口呆。 | 近义词: helplessly, powerlessly, numbly | 反义词: actively, energetically, capably | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From paralysis + -ly.  Paralysis comes from the Greek "paralusis," meaning "a loosening beside," from "para-" (beside) + "luein" (to loosen).
