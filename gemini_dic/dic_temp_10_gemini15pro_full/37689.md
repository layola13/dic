companionabilities:/kəmˈpænjənəˈbɪlɪtiz/
|n.| 交际能力；友善的品质；好相处|The qualities that make someone a good companion; pleasantness in company.|Her companionabilities made her a popular guest at parties. 她的友善使她成为聚会上受欢迎的客人。|近义词：sociability, affability, friendliness|反义词：unsociability, aloofness|词性: 名词

词源:
From companionable + -ities.  "Companionable" comes from "companion" (from Old French "compagnon," meaning "one who shares bread," from Latin "companio") + "-able" (suffix meaning "capable of, worthy of").
