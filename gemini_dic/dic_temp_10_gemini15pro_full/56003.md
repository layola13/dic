do-dos:/ˈduːduːz/ | n. | 待办事项；必须做的事情 | A list of tasks that need to be completed. | I have so many do-dos today, I don't know where to start. 我今天有好多事情要做，我都不知道从哪里开始了。|  Example: She added "grocery shopping" to her do-dos. 她把“买菜”添加到她的待办事项清单中。 | Synonyms: tasks, chores, errands, agenda | Antonyms: leisure, free time | Noun
