oversensitive:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsɪtɪv/ /ˌəʊvəˈsɛnsətɪv/| adj.| 过于敏感的，神经质的| Easily offended or upset; unduly sensitive.|例句：He's a bit oversensitive about his weight. 他对自己的体重有点过于敏感。|例句：She's oversensitive to criticism. 她对批评过于敏感。|近义词：touchy, sensitive, thin-skinned|反义词：insensitive, thick-skinned|词性:形容词

oversensitivity:/ˌoʊvərˌsɛnsɪˈtɪvɪti/ /ˌəʊvəˌsɛnsəˈtɪvəti/| n.| 过敏，过度敏感| The state or quality of being oversensitive.|例句：Her oversensitivity made it difficult to work with her. 她过度敏感，很难与她共事。|例句：He suffers from oversensitivity to certain foods. 他对某些食物过敏。|近义词：sensitivity, touchiness|反义词：insensitivity|词性:名词
