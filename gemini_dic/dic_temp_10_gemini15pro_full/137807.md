nonverminous:/ˌnɒnvɜːˈmɪnəs/| adj.|无虫害的，没有寄生虫的|Free from vermin; not infested with vermin.|The grain storage facility was certified as nonverminous, ensuring the quality of the stored crops.  这个粮仓被认证为无虫害的，确保了储存作物的质量。|例句：The newly renovated apartment was thankfully nonverminous.  谢天谢地，新装修的公寓没有虫害。|近义词：clean, hygienic, pest-free|反义词:verminous, infested|词性:形容词

词源:
non- (prefix): not, without  非，无
vermin (noun):  parasitic worms or insects; any noxious or disgusting animal  寄生虫；害虫
-ous (suffix):  full of; having the qualities of; characterized by  充满...的；具有...性质的；以...为特征的
