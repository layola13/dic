gestured:/ˈdʒɛstʃərd/
verb
做手势, 用动作示意
made a movement of the hand, head, or body to express an idea or meaning.
She gestured towards the door, indicating he should leave. 她朝门口做了个手势，示意他应该离开。
signaled, motioned, indicated
(antonym often depends on the specific gesture, but could be ignored, remained still)
词性: 动词
