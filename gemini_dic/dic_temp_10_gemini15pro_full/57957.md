drippier:/ˈdrɪpiər/ | adj. | 更滴水的，更潮湿的 | More dripping or wet than something else.  More saturated with liquid and prone to dripping. | The faucet was drippier today than it was yesterday. 水龙头今天比昨天漏水更严重了。 | The dog's fur was drippier after its swim in the lake.  狗在湖里游完泳后，毛发更湿了。 | 近义词: wetter, soggier, damper | 反义词: drier, less wet | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From drip (v.) + -ier (comparative suffix). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *drupjaną (“to drip”).
