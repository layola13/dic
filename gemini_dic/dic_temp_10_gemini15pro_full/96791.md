inergetically:/ˌɪnərˈdʒɛtɪkli/
adjective
惰性地; 不活泼地
lacking vigor or energy; inertly.  Characterized by inertia; sluggish.
The reaction proceeded inergetically due to the low temperature. 由于低温，反应进行得很缓慢。
近义词: inertly, sluggishly, passively, listlessly
反义词: energetically, actively, vigorously
词性: adjective

词源:
From inert + -ically. Inert comes from the Latin word "iners," meaning "idle" or "inactive."
