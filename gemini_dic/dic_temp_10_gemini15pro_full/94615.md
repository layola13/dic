impregnating: /ɪmˈprɛɡneɪtɪŋ/
verb
使充满；使饱和；浸渍；使怀孕
making something full of a particular substance; saturating; filling something with a feeling or quality; making a woman or female animal pregnant
The cloth was impregnated with resin. 这块布浸透了树脂。
His writings are impregnated with religious feeling. 他的作品充满了宗教情感。
She was impregnated by her boyfriend. 她被她男朋友搞怀孕了。
近义词: saturating, permeating, infusing, imbuing, fertilizing
反义词: draining, emptying, depleting
词性: Verb
