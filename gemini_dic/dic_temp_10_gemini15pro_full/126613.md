naiant:/ˈneɪənt/| adj.|游泳的，漂浮的 (尤指鱼类等水生动物在纹章中的姿势，水平游动)|A heraldic term for a fish depicted horizontally, as if swimming.|The family crest features three naiant trout.  家族的徽章上有三条水平游动的鳟鱼。|近义词：swimming, floating|反义词:erect, upright|词性:形容词

Etymology:  From Old French *naiant (present participle of naier “to swim”), from Latin natans (present participle of natare “to swim”).
