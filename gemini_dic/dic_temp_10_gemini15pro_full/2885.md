advertise:/ˈædvərtaɪz/ (US) /ˈædvətaɪz/ (UK)| v. |登广告；宣传；做广告|To make something known generally or in public, especially in order to sell it.|We advertised our car in the local newspaper. 我们在当地报纸上刊登了汽车广告。

They are advertising for a new sales manager. 他们在招聘新的销售经理。|近义词：promote, publicize, market|反义词：conceal, hide|词性:动词

advertise:/ˈædvərtaɪz/ (US) /ˈædvətaɪz/ (UK)| n. |广告;宣传|The activity or profession of producing advertisements for commercial products or services.|The company spent a lot of money on advertise. 这家公司在广告上花费了大量的金钱。|近义词：advertising, promotion, publicity|反义词：--|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *advertiser*, from Late Latin *advertere* "to turn towards," from *ad-* "to" + *vertere* "to turn."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
Companies advertise their products to attract customers. = 公司为吸引顾客而宣传他们的产品。
He advertises his services online. = 他在网上宣传他的服务。

一般过去时:
They advertised the job vacancy last week. = 他们上周刊登了招聘广告。
She advertised her new book on social media. = 她在社交媒体上宣传了她的新书。

现在完成时:
We have advertised this product for several months. = 我们已经宣传这个产品好几个月了。
He has advertised his business in various magazines. = 他在各种杂志上宣传了他的业务。

过去完成时:
The company had advertised the position before they found a suitable candidate. = 公司在找到合适的候选人之前就已经登过招聘广告了。
She had advertised her services extensively before she got her first client. = 她在获得第一个客户之前就已经广泛宣传了她的服务。

将来时:
We will advertise our new collection next spring. = 我们将在明年春天宣传我们的新系列。
They will advertise the event on television. = 他们将在电视上宣传这个活动。

被动语态:
The product was advertised on TV and radio. = 该产品在电视和广播上做广告。
The job vacancy is being advertised online. = 这个职位空缺正在网上招聘。


PHRASE->
advertise for = 登广告征求/招聘
advertise on = 在…上做广告
heavily advertised = 大力宣传的
falsely advertised = 虚假宣传的
widely advertised = 广泛宣传的
