hedge:/hedʒ/| n. v.|树篱，障碍，回避|A row of bushes or small trees planted close together, especially to form a boundary.|The garden is surrounded by a tall hedge. 花园被高高的树篱包围着。|近义词：barrier, fence, screen|反义词：opening|词性：名词

hedge:/hedʒ/| v.|限制，规避，保护|To limit or qualify (something) by conditions or exceptions.|He hedged his bets by applying to several colleges. 他为了保险起见，申请了几个大学。|近义词：limit, restrict, evade|反义词：expose|词性：动词
