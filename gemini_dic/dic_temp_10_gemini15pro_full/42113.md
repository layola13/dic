cotted:/ˈkɒtɪd/| adj.|覆盖着棉絮的；缠着棉花的；用棉絮填充的|Covered or wrapped with cotton wool; filled with cotton wadding.|The baby was wrapped in a cotted blanket.  婴儿被裹在一床棉絮毯子里。|The toy was cotted for softness. 这个玩具填充了棉絮使其柔软。|近义词：padded, cushioned, wadded|反义词:bare, uncovered|词性:形容词

词源: From the verb "cot" + "-ed" (past participle suffix).  The verb "cot" means "to mat or tangle together," and in this context refers to the process of preparing cotton fibers for spinning.
