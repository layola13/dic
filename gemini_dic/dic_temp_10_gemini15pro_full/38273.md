conan:/ˈkoʊnən/| n. |柯南|A masculine given name of Irish origin, meaning "little wolf" or "hound". Also famously associated with the fictional character Conan the Barbarian. |Conan Doyle was a famous author. 柯南·道尔是一位著名的作家。|近义词: (for Conan the Barbarian) barbarian, warrior|反义词: (for Conan the Barbarian)civilian, weakling|noun
