pace:/peɪs/| n. vi. vt.| 步伐，速度，节奏；踱步|A single step taken when walking or running.  The speed at which someone or something moves, or with which something happens or develops.|He quickened his pace. 他加快了步伐。|Synonyms: speed, rate, tempo, velocity|Antonyms: slowness, inactivity|noun

pace:/peɪs/| vi. vt.| 踱步，缓慢而行|To walk at a steady and regular speed, especially back and forth and as a way of showing that you are worried or nervous. To set the speed at which something happens or develops.|He paced nervously up and down the room. 他在房间里紧张地来回踱步。|Synonyms: stride, walk, step|Antonyms: stand still, stay put|verb
