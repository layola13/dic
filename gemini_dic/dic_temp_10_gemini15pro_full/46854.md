darbyite:/ˈdɑːrbaɪaɪt/
n.
达比派信徒；普利茅斯弟兄会成员
A member of a strict fundamentalist sect of the Plymouth Brethren, founded in the 19th century by John Nelson Darby.
John is a Darbyite and adheres strictly to the doctrines of his sect. 约翰是达比派信徒，严格遵守其教派的教义。
Synonyms: Plymouth Brother, Darbyist
Antonyms: None (lack of a widely recognized opposing group)
noun
