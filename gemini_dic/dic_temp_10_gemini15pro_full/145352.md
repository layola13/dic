overbumptiousness:/ˌoʊvərˌbʌmpʃəsnəs/| n. | 过分自信；傲慢自大；自以为是 | The state of being excessively or obnoxiously self-assertive or conceited.  | His overbumptiousness alienated his colleagues. 他的傲慢自大疏远了他的同事们。 |  近义词：arrogance, conceit, haughtiness, pomposity | 反义词：humility, modesty, diffidence | 词性: noun

Etymology:  "over" (excessive) + "bumptious" (offensively self-assertive) + "-ness" (forming a noun denoting a state or quality).  "Bumptious" itself comes from "bump" (to strike or knock against) + "-tious" (forming adjectives).  The implication is of someone pushing themselves forward aggressively.
