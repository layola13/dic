eyeglass: /ˈaɪɡlæs/ (US), /ˈaɪɡlɑːs/ (UK)| n. |眼镜 (单数，通常指单片眼镜)|A lens worn in front of the eye to improve vision.  Usually used in the plural (eyeglasses).|He lost his eyeglass while swimming. 他游泳时把单片眼镜弄丢了。|Near synonyms: monocle, lens|Antonyms: None (It's a tool, not a descriptive quality.)|Noun


eyeglasses: /ˈaɪˌɡlæsɪz/ (US), /ˈaɪˌɡlɑːsɪz/ (UK)| n. |眼镜|A pair of lenses worn in front of the eyes to improve vision.|She needs new eyeglasses. 她需要一副新眼镜。|Near synonyms: spectacles, glasses|Antonyms: None|Noun

Etymology:  From "eye" + "glass".  Middle English "ieglas," literally "eye glass."
