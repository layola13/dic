attractor:/əˈtræktər/| n. |吸引子，吸引物|Something that attracts people or things; a feature or quality that attracts. |The new shopping mall is a major attractor for tourists.  这个新的购物中心是吸引游客的主要景点。

The museum's main attractor is its collection of dinosaur skeletons.  这家博物馆最吸引人的地方是它的恐龙骨骼收藏。|近义词：magnet, draw, lure|反义词：repellent, deterrent|词性:noun

Etymology: From Latin *attractor*, from *attrahere* "to draw to," from *ad-* "to" + *trahere* "to draw."
