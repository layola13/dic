hinging:/ˈhɪndʒɪŋ/| v. |取决于, 依靠, 以…为转移|The whole argument hinges on a single premise. 整个论证取决于一个前提。|例句：The success of the project hinges on everyone's cooperation.  这个项目的成功取决于每个人的合作。

例句：Her decision hinges on the outcome of the meeting. 她的决定取决于会议的结果。

近义词：depending, pivoting, turning, revolving|反义词:independent|词性:动词

hinging:/ˈhɪndʒɪŋ/| adj. |关键的，枢纽的|This is the hinging point of the whole play. 这是整部戏的关键点。|例句：The hinging moment in the game was when the quarterback was injured.  比赛中的关键时刻是四分卫受伤的时候。

例句：The hinging factor in their decision was the cost.  他们决定的关键因素是成本。


近义词：crucial, pivotal, critical|反义词:unimportant, trivial|词性:形容词
