antiepileptics:/ˌæntiˌɛpɪˈlɛptɪks/ | n. | 抗癫痫药 | Medications used to prevent or control epileptic seizures. |  She was prescribed antiepileptics after her diagnosis. 她确诊后被开了抗癫痫药。 |  The doctor adjusted her dose of antiepileptics to control her seizures. 医生调整了她的抗癫痫药物剂量以控制她的癫痫发作。|Synonyms: anticonvulsants | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps proconvulsants but this refers to something that *causes* seizures) | noun
