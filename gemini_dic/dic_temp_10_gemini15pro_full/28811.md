carotid: /kəˈrɒtɪd/ | adj. n. | 颈动脉的；颈动脉 | Relating to the two main arteries that carry blood to the head and neck.  One on each side of the neck. |  The carotid artery supplies blood to the brain. 颈动脉向大脑供血。 | synonyms: None for the anatomical term. If used figuratively to mean vital, synonyms would be crucial, essential, vital. | antonyms: None for the anatomical term. | adjective, noun

carotid: /kəˈrɒtɪd/ | n. | 颈动脉 | Either of the two main arteries, one on each side of the neck, that carry blood to the head. | Blockage of the carotid artery can cause a stroke. 颈动脉阻塞会导致中风。 | synonyms: None for the anatomical term. | antonyms: None for the anatomical term. | noun
