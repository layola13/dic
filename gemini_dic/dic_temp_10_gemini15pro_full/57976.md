drive-by:/ˈdraɪv baɪ/ | adj. n. |路过射击；免下车；顺便访问 | A drive-by is a shooting committed from a moving vehicle. It can also refer to something done quickly or casually, often from a car. |  The gang committed a drive-by shooting.  该团伙实施了一起驾车枪击案。|  We did a drive-by of their new house on the way home. 我们在回家的路上顺便看了一下他们的新房子。 |  近义词:  cursory, quick, passing | 反义词:  thorough, deliberate, planned | 词性: adjective, noun


drive-by:/ˈdraɪv baɪ/ | n. |路过射击；免下车服务；顺便访问 | An instance of shooting from a moving vehicle; a service performed without needing to leave one's car; a brief, casual visit. |  The police are investigating a series of drive-bys in the area. 警方正在调查该地区发生的一系列驾车枪击事件。 | 近义词: (for shooting) ambush, attack; (for service) drive-through, curbside; (for visit) quick visit, pop-in | 反义词: (for shooting) planned assault, targeted attack; (for service/visit) sit-down, lengthy visit | 词性: noun

词源：drive-by是由动词 "drive" 和副词 "by" 构成的复合词，字面意思是“开车经过”。最初用于描述驾车枪击事件，后来引申指其他快速、随意完成的事情。
