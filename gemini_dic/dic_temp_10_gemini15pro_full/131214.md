noncabinet:/ˌnɑːnˈkæbɪnɪt/
adjective
非内阁的
Not belonging to or included in a political cabinet.
The noncabinet members of the administration also play an important role.  该行政部门的非内阁成员也发挥着重要作用。
The president appointed a noncabinet advisory committee. 总统任命了一个非内阁的顾问委员会。
近义词: non-ministerial, extra-cabinet
反义词: cabinet
