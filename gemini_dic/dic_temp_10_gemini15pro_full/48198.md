decompressive:/ˌdiːkəmˈprɛsɪv/ | adj. | 减压的；降压的 | Relating to or causing decompression, the reduction of pressure. |  The diver needed to make decompressive stops to avoid the bends. 潜水员需要进行减压停留以避免减压病。 | Synonyms: pressure-relieving, depressurizing | Antonyms: compressive, pressurizing | adjective

decompress:/ˌdiːkəmˈprɛs/ | v. | 减压；放松 | To release from pressure; to relax. | After a long day at work, I like to decompress with a good book.  在漫长的一天工作后，我喜欢读一本好书来放松一下。 | Synonyms: relax, unwind, de-stress | Antonyms: compress, pressurize, stress | verb
