foot:/fʊt/ (US), /fʊt/ (UK)| n. v.| 脚，英尺 |The bottom part of the leg, below the ankle; a unit of length equal to 12 inches (30.48 centimeters).|She hurt her foot playing soccer. 她踢足球时伤了脚。|The table is three feet long. 这张桌子有三英尺长。|近义词:base, bottom (as in the lowest part), unit (of measurement) |反义词:head, top|noun, verb

foot:/fʊt/ (US), /fʊt/ (UK)| v. |支付，承担费用|To pay a bill or expense.|I footed the bill for the dinner. 我付了晚餐的账单。|He had to foot the cost of repairs. 他不得不承担维修费用。|近义词:pay, cover, settle|反义词:charge, bill|verb
