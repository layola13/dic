foams:/foʊmz/ /fəʊmz/
noun|泡沫，泡沫塑料，泡沫状物|A mass of small bubbles formed on or in a liquid, typically by agitation or fermentation.  A lightweight form of rubber or plastic made by solidifying foam.|The beer had a thick head of foam.  啤酒上面有一层厚厚的泡沫。|synonyms: froth, lather, suds, bubbles|antonyms: liquid, solid|n.

foams:/foʊmz/ /fəʊmz/
verb|起泡沫，吐白沫|To form or produce foam.|The agitated horse foamed at the mouth. 激动的马口吐白沫。|synonyms: froth, lather, bubble|antonyms: flatten, settle|v.
