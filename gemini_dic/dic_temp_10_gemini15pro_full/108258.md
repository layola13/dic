launching:/ˈlɔːntʃɪŋ/ (US), /ˈlɔːntʃɪŋ/ (UK)
n.|发射，启动，发行|The launching of the new product was a success.  新产品的发布很成功。
v.|发射，发动，开始|They are launching a new marketing campaign. 他们正在发起新的营销活动。
|Derived from the verb "launch," ultimately from Old French "lancer" (to hurl, throw).  源自动词“launch”，最终来自古法语“lancer”（投掷，扔）。
|近义词：starting, initiating, commencing, releasing|
|反义词：ending, terminating, concluding, withdrawing|
词性:  动名词 (gerund) / 现在分词 (present participle)
