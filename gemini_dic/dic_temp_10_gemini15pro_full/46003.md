cybercafes:/ˈsaɪbərˌkæfeɪz/ (US), /ˈsaɪbəˌkæfeɪz/ (UK)| n. | 网吧 | A place that provides internet access to the public, typically for a fee.  It usually also serves food and drinks. |  Many students go to cybercafes to do their homework. 许多学生去网吧做作业。

Many cybercafes offer printing services. 许多网吧提供打印服务。

|近义词：internet cafe, net cafe|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "library" or "home office" in certain contexts) | noun
