experimentative:/ɪkˌspɛrɪˈmɛntətɪv/
adjective
实验性的；爱试验的；试验性的
Given to or characterized by experiment; experimental.
例句：
She has an experimentative approach to cooking. = 她做菜的方式很实验性。

He's very experimentative with his music. = 他的音乐风格非常具有实验性。

近义词：experimental, empirical, trial, test
反义词：theoretical, proven, established
词性：adjective
