distrusters:/dɪˈstrʌstərz/
n.
不信任者，怀疑者
People who distrust others; skeptics.
The distrusters questioned the motives of the new CEO. 怀疑者质疑新任首席执行官的动机。
近义词: skeptics, doubters, cynics
反义词: believers, trusters, optimists
词性: 名词

词源: From distrust (v.) + -er (suffix forming nouns denoting agents or persons performing an action).  Distrust itself comes from dis- (prefix indicating negation or reversal) + trust.
