menispermic:/ˌmɛnɪˈspɜːrmɪk/ | adj. |  弯胚的，月形胚的 | Relating to or characteristic of seeds with a curved or moon-shaped embryo, typical of the Menispermaceae family. | The plant's menispermic seeds are a key identifying feature.  这种植物弯胚的种子是其关键识别特征。| Synonym: campylotropous | Antonym: orthotropous | adjective
