goat:/ɡoʊt/ (US) /ɡəʊt/ (UK)| n.|山羊|A domesticated ruminant mammal with backward-curving horns, a beard, and (typically) a long coat.|The farmer has a herd of goats.|近义词：billy goat (male), nanny goat (female)|反义词: (No direct antonym for an animal. Could consider predator like wolf)|noun
