outbrazening: /ˌaʊtˈbreɪzənɪŋ/
verb
厚颜无耻地胜过; 在厚颜无耻方面超过
surpassing in boldness or impudence.
He outbrazened his rivals by claiming victory before the votes were counted. 他在计票前就宣称胜利，其厚颜无耻超过了他的对手。
近义词: outfacing, outdaring, surpassing, exceeding
反义词: yielding, submitting, deferring

词性: v.

词源:  From out- + brazen, meaning "made of brass." The sense evolved from the perceived hardness and impudence associated with brass.
