compensator:/ˈkɒmpənseɪtə(r) (UK), ˈkɑːmpənseɪtər (US)| n.|补偿器，均衡器，校正装置|A device or system that compensates for something, typically something undesirable.|The pilot used the compensator to adjust for the wind. 飞行员使用补偿器来调整风的影响。|Synonyms: equalizer, counterbalance, adjuster, regulator |Antonyms: amplifier, intensifier|noun

compensate:/ˈkɒmpənseɪt (UK), ˈkɑːmpənseɪt (US)| v.|补偿，赔偿，弥补|To give something, typically money, to someone in recognition of loss, suffering, or injury; to make up for something unwelcome or unpleasant by exerting an opposite force or effect.|The company compensated her for the damage to her car. 公司赔偿了她汽车的损失。|Synonyms: recompense, repay, atone, offset |Antonyms: penalize, forfeit|verb
