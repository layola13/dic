deported:/dɪˈpɔːrtɪd/
verb
被驱逐出境的
expelled from a country; banished.
The illegal immigrants were deported back to their country of origin.  非法移民被驱逐回原籍国。
synonyms: expelled, banished, exiled, extradited
antonyms: admitted, welcomed, repatriated
词性: Verb (Past Participle)

deport:/dɪˈpɔːrt/
verb
驱逐出境
to expel (a foreigner) from a country, typically on the grounds of illegal status or for having committed a crime.
The government deported the convicted criminal. 政府驱逐了这名被定罪的罪犯。
synonyms: expel, banish, exile, extradite
antonyms: admit, welcome, repatriate
词性: Verb
