equinity:/ɪˈkwɪnɪti/
n.
the quality of being equal or fair; fairness
the state or quality of being like or resembling a horse; horseness
Equity demands that everyone be treated fairly. 公平要求每个人都受到公平对待。
Her paintings captured the equinity of the wild horses. 她的画捕捉到了野马的马的特性。
The judge's decision was based on the principle of equinity. 法官的判决基于公平原则。
The sculpture emphasized the animal's equinity, its powerful, muscular form reminiscent of a wild stallion.  这件雕塑强调了这种动物的马性，它强壮的肌肉形态让人想起一匹野种马。


近义词:fairness, justice, impartiality, parity, resemblance (to a horse)
反义词:inequity, injustice, bias, disparity


词性:noun

词源:From Latin aequinitas ("equality, uniformity, conformity, similarity"), from aequus ("even, level, equal, like, similar")
