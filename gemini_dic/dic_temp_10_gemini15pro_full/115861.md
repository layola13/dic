markups:/ˈmɑːrkʌps/ /ˈmɑːrkʌps/| n.| 涨价，加价，标记 |An amount added to the cost price to determine the selling price. The difference between the cost of a product or service and its selling price.|The store has a high markup on designer clothes.  这家商店对名牌服装的加价很高。|synonyms: increase, boost, surcharge |antonyms: markdown, discount, reduction|noun

markups:/ˈmɑːrkʌps/ /ˈmɑːrkʌps/| n.| 利润，毛利 |The profit derived from markups.|High markups can lead to increased profits. 高利润率可以带来更高的利润。|synonyms: profit margin, gross profit |antonyms: loss, deficit|noun
