pantos:/ˈpæntəʊz/ (UK) /ˈpæntoʊz/ (US)| n.| 童话剧；哑剧（尤指圣诞节期间上演的）| A pantomime, often shortened to "panto", is a type of musical comedy stage production, designed for family entertainment. It is developed from the traditions of commedia dell'arte and British music hall, and combines topical humour with slapstick comedy and songs, incorporating audience participation. | Example 1: We're going to see the pantos at the theatre this Christmas.  我们今年圣诞节要去剧院看童话剧。

Example 2:  He always plays the dame in the local pantos. 他总是在当地的童话剧里扮演丑角。| 近义词: pantomime, play, show | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "tragedy" as a contrasting genre) | 词性: noun
