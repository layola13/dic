mammonization:/ˌmæmənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 拜金主义，物质至上 | The process of becoming obsessed with wealth and material possessions, often at the expense of spiritual or ethical values. |  The rampant mammonization of society has led to a decline in ethical behavior. 社会上猖獗的拜金主义导致了道德行为的衰落。

The increasing mammonization of professional sports is evident in the exorbitant salaries paid to athletes.  职业体育日益增长的拜金主义在运动员的高薪中显而易见。 | 近义词: materialism, commercialism, avarice | 反义词: altruism, philanthropy, asceticism | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From "Mammon," a term personifying greed and wealth, ultimately from Aramaic māmonā, meaning "wealth," "riches."
