congealable:/kənˈdʒiːləbl/
adjective
可凝固的
Capable of being congealed or solidified.
The liquid is congealable at low temperatures.  这种液体在低温下会凝固。
Congealable fat can clog arteries. 可凝固的脂肪会阻塞动脉。
近义词:coagulable, solidifiable
反义词:non-coagulating, fluid
词性:adjective
