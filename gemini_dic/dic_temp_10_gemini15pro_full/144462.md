outtalked:/ˌaʊtˈtɔːkt/
verb
胜过(某人)的讲话；说得比…久
to talk longer or more persuasively than someone else
He outtalked his opponents and won the debate. 他在辩论中说得比对手久，赢得了辩论。
outspoke, outargued
- (no clear opposite, perhaps "silenced")
