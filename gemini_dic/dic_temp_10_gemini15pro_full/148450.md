overstout:/ˈoʊvərstaʊt/| adj.|过胖的，过于肥胖的|Excessively stout; very fat or obese.|例句：He was a rather overstout gentleman with a red face. 他是一位面色红润、身材过于肥胖的绅士。|近义词：obese, corpulent, portly|反义词：thin, slim, slender|词性:形容词
Etymology: over- + stout. "Over-" acts as an intensifier, meaning "excessively" or "too much". "Stout" originally meant "strong" or "brave," but later developed the meaning of "thickset" and eventually "fat."
