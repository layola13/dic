coralligenous:/ˌkɒrəlɪˈdʒɛnəs/
adjective
珊瑚的；产珊瑚的；由珊瑚形成的
Composed of, containing, or producing coral.  Relating to a hard substrate in the Mediterranean Sea, formed by the build-up of calcareous algae and other organisms, and providing a habitat for a diverse range of marine life.
The coralligenous reefs of the Mediterranean are biodiversity hotspots. 地中海的珊瑚礁是生物多样性热点。
The study focused on the coralligenous communities of the Aegean Sea. 这项研究的重点是爱琴海的珊瑚礁群落。
Synonyms: coral-producing, coral-forming
Antonyms: non-coralligenous
