factual:/ˈfæktʃuəl/| adj.|真实的，事实的，实际的|Based on or concerned with facts.|例句：The report was factual and unbiased. 这份报告是真实的且公正的。|例句：She gave a factual account of the incident. 她对事件进行了真实的描述。|近义词：true, accurate, real, authentic|反义词：false, fictional, untrue, inaccurate|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *factualis, from Latin factum (“deed, act, feat; fact”) + -alis (adjective suffix). factum is the neuter past participle of facere (“to make, do”).
