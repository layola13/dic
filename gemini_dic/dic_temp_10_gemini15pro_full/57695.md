drawn-out:/ˈdrɔːn ˈaʊt/| adj.|冗长的，拖延的|Prolonged to a tedious degree; lasting longer than necessary or expected.  Extended in time; lengthy.|The meeting was drawn-out and unproductive. 会议冗长而没有成果。|The drawn-out negotiations finally ended in a compromise.  旷日持久的谈判最终以妥协告终。|近义词:prolonged, lengthy, extended, protracted|反义词:brief, short, concise, succinct|词性:形容词
词源:From the verb "draw out," meaning to extend in length or time.  The "drawn" part is the past participle of "draw," and "out" indicates the direction or extension.
