inconstantly:/ɪnˈkɒnstəntli/ /ɪnˈkɑːnstəntli/
adverb
反复无常地；易变地
Changing frequently and unpredictably.
He behaved inconstantly, showing affection one moment and indifference the next. 他表现反复无常，一会儿表示亲热，一会儿又漠不关心。
近义词：erratically, capriciously, fitfully, variably
反义词：constantly, steadily, consistently
词性：adverb (副词)
