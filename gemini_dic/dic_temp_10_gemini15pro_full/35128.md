cloudless:/ˈklaʊdləs/| adj.|无云的，晴朗的|Free from clouds; clear.|例句：We enjoyed a cloudless sky on our hike. 我们在徒步旅行时享受了晴朗的天空。|近义词：clear, sunny, bright, unclouded|反义词:cloudy, overcast|词性:形容词
Etymology: From cloud + -less.  "Cloud" comes from Old English clūd, meaning "mass of rock or earth, hill."  The meaning shifted to "rain cloud" likely due to the resemblance of cumulus clouds to mountains.  "-less" is a suffix of Old English origin meaning "without."
