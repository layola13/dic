fleshed:/fleʃt/
adjective
有血肉的；丰满的；充实的
covered in flesh; plump; well-developed
例句：
The fleshed-out skeleton provided a much clearer image of the dinosaur.
这具添上血肉的骨架让我们对恐龙有了更清晰的认识。

例句：
The author fleshed out the characters in her novel with vivid descriptions and backstories.
作者用生动的描写和背景故事充实了她小说中的人物。

近义词：
plump, full, well-developed, substantial, detailed, elaborate
反义词：
thin, gaunt, emaciated, skeletal, sketchy, incomplete
词性
adjective
