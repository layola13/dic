escapingly:/ɪˈskeɪpɪŋli/
adv.
逃避地，规避地
in a way that allows or involves escape
He glanced escapingly at the door. 他逃避似的瞥了一眼门口。
近义词: evasively, elusively
反义词: directly, facingly
词性: 副词

Etymology:  From escape + -ing + -ly.  "Escape" comes from Old French "escaper" (to escape), itself from Vulgar Latin *excappare (to slip out of one's cape), from Latin "ex" (out of) + "cappa" (cape).
