computist:/kəmˈpjuːtɪst/
n.
计算员，精于计算的人
A person skilled in computation; a calculator.
Early computists worked with slide rules and mechanical calculators. 早期的计算员使用计算尺和机械计算器。
Computists were essential for complex calculations before the advent of electronic computers. 在电子计算机出现之前，计算员对于复杂的计算至关重要。
近义词: calculator, computer, mathematician
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "innumerate person")
词性: noun
