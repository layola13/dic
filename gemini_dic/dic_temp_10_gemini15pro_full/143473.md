outgalloped:/ˌaʊtˈɡæləpt/
verb
跑过，超过…的速度
to gallop faster than another horse or rider.
The young horse easily outgalloped the older one. 这匹小马轻易地超过了那匹老马。
outpaced, outran, overtook
-
v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + gallop.  "Out-" prefix indicates exceeding or surpassing in the action of the verb.  "Gallop" comes from Old North French *galoper, of uncertain Germanic origin.
