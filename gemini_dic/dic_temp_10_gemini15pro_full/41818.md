cortisones:/ˈkɔːrtɪˌsoʊnz/ /ˈkɔːrtɪˌzəʊnz/
noun
皮质酮（复数）
A group of steroid hormones produced by the adrenal cortex.  They are involved in metabolism, immune response, and stress response.
She was prescribed cortisone injections for her arthritis. 她被开了可的松注射剂来治疗关节炎。
synonyms: corticosteroids, glucocorticoids
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a chemical substance.  One might consider antagonists that block cortisol receptors as functional antonyms.)
词性:noun


cortisone:/ˈkɔːrtɪˌsoʊn/ /ˈkɔːrtɪˌzəʊn/
noun
皮质酮
A steroid hormone produced by the adrenal cortex.  It is involved in metabolism, immune response, and stress response.

synonyms:  hydrocortisone (specifically referring to cortisol) , corticosteroid, glucocorticoid
antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a chemical substance. One might consider antagonists that block cortisol receptors as functional antonyms.)
词性:noun
