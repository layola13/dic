denuded:/dɪˈnjuːdɪd/
adjective
裸露的，光秃秃的
stripped bare, deprived of covering
The hillside was denuded of trees after the fire. 山坡在大火后变得光秃秃的，没有树木了。
The land was denuded of vegetation by overgrazing. 过度放牧使得这片土地寸草不生。
synonyms: bare, naked, stripped, exposed, barren
antonyms: covered, clothed, vegetated, forested
adjective
