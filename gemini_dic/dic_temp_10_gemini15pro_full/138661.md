nudibranchiate:/ˌnjuːdɪˈbræŋkiət/ /ˌnuːdɪˈbræŋkieɪt/ | adj. n. | 裸鳃类的，裸鳃动物 |  Relating to or denoting a subclass (Nudibranchia) of marine gastropods which lack a shell in the adult stage, and which have projections (cerata) on the upper surface of the body.
