maleic:/məˈleɪɪk/| adj.|马来酸的；顺丁烯二酸的|Relating to or derived from maleic acid, a dicarboxylic acid.  It's an unsaturated organic compound and is the cis isomer of butenedioic acid.|Maleic anhydride is used in the production of polyester resins. 马来酸酐用于生产聚酯树脂。|近义词: cis-butenedioic, toxilic|反义词: fumaric (its trans isomer)|词性: adjective
