diencephalon:/ˌdaɪɛnˈsɛfəlɒn/ (US), /ˌdaɪɛnˈsɛfəlɒn/ (UK)| n.|间脑|The diencephalon is a part of the forebrain, containing the thalamus, hypothalamus, epithalamus, and subthalamus.  它是前脑的一部分，包含丘脑、下丘脑、上丘脑和底丘脑。|The diencephalon plays a crucial role in relaying sensory information and regulating hormonal functions. 间脑在传递感觉信息和调节激素功能方面起着至关重要的作用。|Synonyms: interbrain (less common)|Antonyms: None|noun

diencephalic:/ˌdaɪɛnˈsɛˈfælɪk/ (US), /ˌdaɪɛnˈsɛˈfælɪk/ (UK)| adj.|间脑的|Diencephalic structures are involved in a wide range of functions. 间脑结构参与广泛的功能。|Damage to the diencephalic area can lead to various neurological disorders. 间脑区域的损伤会导致各种神经系统疾病。|Synonyms: interbrain (less common)|Antonyms: None|adjective
