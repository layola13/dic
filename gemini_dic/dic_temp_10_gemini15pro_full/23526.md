brainage: /ˈbreɪnɪdʒ/
n.
脑龄; 心智年龄
A person's mental capacity relative to their chronological age.
Her brainage is much higher than her actual age. 她的心智年龄比实际年龄高得多。
近义词: mental age
反义词: chronological age
词性: 名词


Etymology:  A blend of "brain" and "age".  Relatively recent coinage, likely emerging in the late 20th or early 21st century with the increased interest in cognitive science and brain health.
