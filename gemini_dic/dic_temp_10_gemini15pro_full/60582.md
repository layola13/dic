elatednesses:/ɪˈleɪtɪdnɪsɪz/
noun
兴高采烈，欣喜若狂
The state of being elated; extreme happiness and exhilaration.
Her elatedness was evident in her bright smile and bubbly laughter.  她灿烂的笑容和爽朗的笑声表明她欣喜若狂。
The team's elatedness after winning the championship was infectious.  团队赢得冠军后的喜悦很有感染力。
近义词：joy, euphoria, ecstasy, bliss
反义词：despair, sadness, gloom, depression
词性:n.


elated:/ɪˈleɪtɪd/
adjective
兴高采烈的；欣喜若狂的
Feeling or expressing great happiness or triumph.
He was elated at the news of his promotion. 他听到升职的消息欣喜若狂。
She felt elated after finishing the marathon. 完成马拉松后，她感到欣喜若狂。
近义词：joyful, jubilant, thrilled, ecstatic
反义词：depressed, sad, downcast, dejected
词性:adj.

词源: From Latin *elatus, past participle of efferre (“to carry out, bring forth”), from ex (“out”) + ferre (“to carry, bear”). Related to elate.
