arborical:/ɑːrˈbɔːrɪkəl/ | adj. | 树木的；树栖的 | Relating to trees; living in trees. |  The monkey's arborical lifestyle meant it rarely touched the ground.  这只猴子树栖的生活方式意味着它很少接触地面。| 近义词：arboreal, tree-dwelling | 反义词：terrestrial, ground-dwelling | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *arboricolus, from arbor "tree" + -cola "dweller".
