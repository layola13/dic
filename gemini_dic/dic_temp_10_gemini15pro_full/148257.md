overslept: /ˌoʊvərˈslɛpt/ | v. | 睡过头，睡得太久 | Slept longer than intended. | I overslept this morning and missed my train. 我今天早上睡过头了，错过了火车。| overslept is the past tense and past participle of oversleep | 近义词: slept in, slept late | 反义词: woke up early | 词性: verb

词源: "over-" (excessively) + "sleep" (rest in a state of unconsciousness).
