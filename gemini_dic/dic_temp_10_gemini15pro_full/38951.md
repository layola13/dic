confides:/kənˈfaɪdz/ | v. | 吐露；委托；信赖 | tell someone about a secret or private matter while trusting them not to repeat it to others. Also, entrust something to someone's care. | She confided her fears to her best friend. 她向她最好的朋友倾诉了她的恐惧。 He confided the task to his most trusted colleague. 他把这项任务委托给了他最信任的同事。 | synonyms: reveals, discloses, admits, entrusts, commits | antonyms: conceals, hides, withholds, distrusts | verb

confide (in):/kənˈfaɪd (ɪn)/ | v. |  吐露（心事）；信赖 | trust (someone) enough to tell them a secret or private matter. |  He confided in his therapist about his problems. 他向他的治疗师倾诉了他的问题。| synonyms: trust, unburden oneself to, open one's heart to | antonyms: distrust, be wary of, keep secrets from | verb
