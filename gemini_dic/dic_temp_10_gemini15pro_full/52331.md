dicrotal:/ˈdaɪkrətəl/| adj.|分叉的，叉状的|Relating to or resembling a rattlesnake's rattle; forked, bifurcated.|The snake's dicrotal tail made a distinctive rattling sound. 这条蛇分叉的尾巴发出独特的嘎嘎声。|Near Synonyms: forked, bifurcated, branched|Antonyms: straight, unbranched|adjective


dicrotism:/ˈdaɪkrətɪzəm/| n.|双脉搏|A condition in which the pulse is marked by a double beat for each cardiac cycle.|Dicrotism can be a sign of certain cardiovascular conditions. 双脉搏可能是某些心血管疾病的征兆。|Near Synonyms: double pulse|Antonyms: single pulse|noun
