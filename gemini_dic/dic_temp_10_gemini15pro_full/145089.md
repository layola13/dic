overapplauded:/ˌoʊvərəˈplɔːdɪd/
adjective
过分称赞的，被过度鼓掌的
Excessively applauded; applauded more than is warranted.
The overapplauded performance felt somewhat anticlimactic.  演出被过分称赞，反而感觉有点虎头蛇尾。
The overapplauded pop star seemed indifferent to the adoration.  这位被过度追捧的流行歌星似乎对众人的喜爱无动于衷。
Synonym: overpraised, overestimated
Antonym: underrated, underappreciated
词性: 形容词
