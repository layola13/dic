armour:/ˈɑːmə(r) (UK) /ˈɑːrmər (US)| n.|盔甲; 装甲;|A protective covering, typically made of metal, worn by soldiers or warriors or on vehicles to deflect or absorb blows from weapons. |The knight wore shining armour. 骑士穿着闪亮的盔甲。|The tank's armour protected it from enemy fire. 坦克的装甲保护它免受敌人炮火袭击。|近义词:protection, shield, defense|反义词:vulnerability|词性:名词

armored:/ˈɑːməd (UK)/ˈɑːrmərd (US)| adj.|装甲的; 装甲覆盖的;|Equipped with or protected by armour. |An armoured car waited outside the bank. 一辆装甲车停在银行外面。|The troops advanced in armoured vehicles. 部队乘坐装甲车前进。|近义词:protected, fortified, shielded|反义词:unprotected, unarmed, vulnerable|词性:形容词
