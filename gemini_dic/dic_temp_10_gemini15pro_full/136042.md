nonreasons:/nɒnˈriːznz/ | n. | 无效理由；站不住脚的理由 | Explanations or justifications that lack logic or validity and do not truly support a claim or action. | He offered a series of nonreasons for his lateness, none of which were believable. 他为自己的迟到提供了一系列站不住脚的理由，没有一个是可信的。| Synonyms: excuses, pretexts, justifications (when used negatively), fallacies | Antonyms: valid reasons, sound arguments, legitimate explanations | noun
