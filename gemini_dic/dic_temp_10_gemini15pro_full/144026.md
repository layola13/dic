outreproduce:/ˌaʊtriːprəˈdjuːs/
verb
过度繁殖，生育过多
To reproduce or multiply at a faster rate than.
The rabbits outreproduced the foxes, leading to an imbalance in the ecosystem. 兔子繁殖的速度超过了狐狸，导致生态系统失衡。
近义词: outbreed, overproduce
反义词: underproduce
词性: verb
