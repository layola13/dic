grison:/ˈɡrɪzən/ | n. | 格里森;鼬科动物;|A carnivorous mammal (Galictis vittata) of Central and South America, resembling a weasel with a greyish coat and black underparts. It is known for its ferocity and ability to kill snakes.| The grison hunted a venomous snake. 格里森猎杀了一条毒蛇。| 近义词:weasel, ferret, tayra | 反义词: None (no direct opposite for an animal name) | 词性: noun
Etymology: From French *gris*, meaning "grey." | 词源: 源自法语 *gris*，意为“灰色”。
