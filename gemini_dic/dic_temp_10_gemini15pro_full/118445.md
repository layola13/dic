mesaticephalous:/ˌmɛsɪtɪˈsɛfələs/| adj.|中头型的|Having a head of medium proportions; specifically : having a cephalic index between 75 and 80.|The skull found at the archaeological site was determined to be mesaticephalous.|Near synonyms: mesocephalic, medium-headed|Antonyms: brachycephalic (short-headed), dolichocephalic (long-headed)|adjective
