moldings:/ˈmoʊldɪŋz/ (US), /ˈməʊldɪŋz/ (UK)| n. |  线脚，造型，装饰线条 |A shaped strip of wood, plastic, or other material used for decoration or finishing, especially around doors, windows, or the edges of furniture. | The ornate moldings around the fireplace added a touch of elegance to the room.  房间壁炉周围华丽的线脚增添了一丝优雅。| Synonyms: trim, ornamentation, decoration | Antonyms: plainness, simplicity | noun

molding:/ˈmoʊldɪŋ/ (US), /ˈməʊldɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |  塑造，模制，浇铸 |The process of shaping a material, such as plastic or metal, by pouring it into a mold. | The molding process for this plastic component is quite complex.  这种塑料部件的成型工艺相当复杂。| Synonyms: casting, forming, shaping | Antonyms:  | noun
