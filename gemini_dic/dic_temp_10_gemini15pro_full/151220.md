pare-away:/ˌpɛər əˈweɪ/ | v. | 削减，减少，精简 | To reduce or diminish something gradually by cutting or trimming away parts. |  The sculptor pared away the excess clay. 雕塑家削去了多余的粘土。

The editor pared away unnecessary details from the article. 编辑从文章中删除了不必要的细节。 | 近义词: trim, reduce, cut back, whittle down | 反义词: augment, increase, expand, enlarge | 词性: 动词
