burghership:/ˈbɜːɡərʃɪp/
n.
市民权；公民身份；城市居民资格
The right of citizenship in a burgh, or the freedom of a burgh.  The state of being a burgess.
He was granted burghership after living in the town for five years. 他在镇上住了五年后被授予市民权。
synonyms: citizenship, freedom,  franchise
antonyms: alienage, disenfranchisement
noun
