hustlers:/ˈhʌslərz/ (US), /ˈhʌsləz/ (UK)| n.| 骗子；  hustler的复数形式； 非常积极进取的人|  People who work hard and are determined to succeed, often in a way that is seen as aggressive or ruthless. Can also refer to those who obtain money by fraud or deception. |Those hustlers on Wall Street made millions. 华尔街的那些拼命三郎赚了数百万美元。| synonyms: go-getters, strivers, grafters, entrepreneurs, swindlers, con artists | antonyms: idlers, slackers, laggards| noun
