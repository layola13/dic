cocultivate:/koʊˈkʌltɪveɪt/
verb
共同培养，协同种植
to cultivate (different kinds of organisms) together.  To grow two or more different kinds of organisms together.
Researchers cocultivated the bacteria with human cells to study the interaction. 研究人员将这种细菌与人类细胞共同培养，以研究它们之间的相互作用。
The farmers cocultivated corn and beans to improve soil fertility.  农民们将玉米和豆类共同种植以提高土壤肥力。

近义词:coculture, grow together, raise together
反义词: monoculture, separate culture
词性:verb
