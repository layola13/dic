multireflexes:/ˌmʌltiˈriːflɛksɪz/
noun
多反射
multiple reflexes; more than one reflex occurring simultaneously or in rapid succession.
The patient exhibited multireflexes when the tendon was tapped.  病人叩击肌腱时表现出多反射。
Near synonyms: multiple reflexes, polysynaptic reflexes
Antonyms: areflexia, hyporeflexia
