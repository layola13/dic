banning:/ˈbænɪŋ/| n.|禁止，禁令|The banning of smoking in public places has improved air quality. 在公共场所禁止吸烟改善了空气质量。|Synonyms: prohibition, forbidding, proscription|Antonyms: allowing, permitting, authorization|noun
banning:/ˈbænɪŋ/| adj.|禁止的|The banning order prevented him from entering the country.  禁止令阻止他进入该国。|Synonyms: prohibited, forbidden, proscribed|Antonyms: allowed, permitted, authorized|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "ban" (meaning "to prohibit"), which comes from Old French "banir" and ultimately from Frankish "*bannan" (to proclaim, summon, command).
