gratis: /ˈɡrætɪs/ (also /ˈɡreɪtɪs/) | adj. adv.| 免费的，无偿的 | Given or done free of charge. |  The drinks were gratis. 饮料是免费的。|  The software is available gratis.  这款软件是免费提供的。| 近义词: free, complimentary, for nothing | 反义词: paid, charged | 词性: 形容词，副词

gratis: /ˈɡrætɪs/ (also /ˈɡreɪtɪs/) |  n. | 免费赠送的东西 | Something given or done free of charge. | The appetizers were gratis.  这些开胃菜是免费赠送的。|  We received several gratis at the conference. 我们在会议上收到了一些免费赠品。| 近义词: freebie, giveaway | 反义词: purchase | 词性: 名词


词源: From Latin gratis "for nothing, freely, gratuitously," related to gratia "grace, favor, gratitude," from gratus "pleasing, agreeable, thankful."
