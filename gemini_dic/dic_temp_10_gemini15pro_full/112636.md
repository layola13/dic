low-resolution:/ˌloʊ ˌrɛzəˈluːʃən/| adj.|低分辨率的|Having a limited number of pixels, resulting in a lack of clarity or detail.|The image was low-resolution and blurry. 这张图片分辨率低，很模糊。|Synonyms: low-definition, pixelated, blurry, coarse|Antonyms: high-resolution, sharp, clear, crisp|Adjective
