affordances:/əˈfɔːrdənsɪz/| n. |功能可见性；可供性；提供可能性|The perceived and actual properties of something, primarily those fundamental properties that determine just how the thing could possibly be used.  It refers to the possibilities for action offered by objects and situations.|The chair's affordances include sitting, standing on, and hiding under.|近义词：possibilities, opportunities, potential, capabilities|反义词:limitations, restrictions|noun
