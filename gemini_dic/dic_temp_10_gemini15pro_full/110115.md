licorice: /ˈlɪkərɪʃ/ (US), /ˈlɪkərɪs/ (UK)| n. | 甘草，由甘草制成的糖果 | A confection made from the extract of licorice root. | I love black licorice candy. 我喜欢黑甘草糖。

licorice: /ˈlɪkərɪʃ/ (US), /ˈlɪkərɪs/ (UK)| n. | 甘草植物 | The plant Glycyrrhiza glabra, from whose root a sweet extract is derived. | Licorice is used in some herbal remedies. 甘草用于一些草药疗法。


近义词: liquorice (UK spelling),  anise (similar flavor)
反义词: None (no direct opposite for a plant or candy)
词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *licoresse*, from Old French *licoresse, licoreice*, from Late Latin *liquiritia*, from Greek *γλυκύρριζα* (glukurrhiza), from *γλυκύς* (glukus, “sweet”) + *ῥίζα* (rhiza, “root”).
