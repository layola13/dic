noncomplacently:/ˌnɒnkəmˈpleɪs(ə)ntli/
adverb
不满足地，不懈怠地
in a way that shows you are not satisfied with the way things are and want to improve them.
The team worked noncomplacently, always striving for better results.  该团队不懈努力，始终力争取得更好的成绩。
She noncomplacently pursued her goals, never settling for mediocrity. 她不满足地追求自己的目标，从不甘于平庸。
近义词: diligently, assiduously, tirelessly
反义词: complacently, passively, idly
词性: adverb

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + complacent + -ly (adverb suffix)
From Latin complacere "to please completely," from com- "with" + placere "to please."
