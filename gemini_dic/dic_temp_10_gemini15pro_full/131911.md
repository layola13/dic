noncontiguousness:/ˌnɒnkənˈtɪɡjuəsnəs/ | n. | 非连续性，不邻接 | The noncontiguousness of the data made analysis difficult. 数据的非连续性使分析变得困难。|  The noncontiguousness of the islands poses challenges for transportation. 这些岛屿的不相邻给交通运输带来了挑战。 | 近义词：discontinuity, separateness, brokenness | 反义词：continuity, contiguity, connectedness | 词性: noun

noncontiguous:/ˌnɒnkənˈtɪɡjuəs/ | adj. | 非连续的，不邻接的 |  The data were collected from noncontiguous locations.  数据是从不连续的地点收集的。 | The park is made up of several noncontiguous areas.  这个公园由几个不相连的区域组成。 | 近义词：discontinuous, separate, broken | 反义词：continuous, contiguous, connected | 词性: adjective
