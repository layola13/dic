antisensitivities:/ˌæntiˌsɛnsɪˈtɪvɪtiz/
noun|脱敏反应；降低敏感性；抗过敏性|The state of being less sensitive or reactive, especially to allergens or other stimuli. It can also refer to the methods or treatments used to achieve this state.|
After years of allergy shots, she developed antisensitivities to pollen.|
近义词: desensitization, tolerance, resistance|
反义词: sensitivity, hypersensitivity, reactivity|
词性: noun (plural)
