noncustodial:/ˌnɒnkʌˈstəʊdiəl/ | adj. | 非监护权的 | Not having custody (especially of children after a divorce or separation). | The noncustodial parent is usually required to pay child support.  非监护方通常需要支付子女抚养费。| Example: The noncustodial parent has visitation rights every other weekend. 非监护父母每隔一周有探视权。| Synonyms: non-custodial (alternative spelling), non-guarding | Antonyms: custodial | Adjective
