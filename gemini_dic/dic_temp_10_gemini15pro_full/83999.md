half-denuded:/ˌhæf dɪˈnjuːdɪd/
adjective
半裸的，部分裸露的
partly stripped bare; partially uncovered or exposed.
The half-denuded trees stood stark against the winter sky. 冬天光秃秃的树木在天空的映衬下显得格外醒目。
The half-denuded hillside showed the scars of recent logging. 半裸露的山坡上留下了最近伐木的痕迹。
Near synonyms: partially bare, partly uncovered, semi-nude
Near antonyms: fully clothed, completely covered, densely vegetated
word class: adjective
