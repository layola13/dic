dispersing:/dɪˈspɜːrsɪŋ/
verb
分散，散开
the act of spreading something over a wide area
The police fired tear gas to disperse the crowd. 警察发射催泪瓦斯驱散人群。
Synonyms: scattering, distributing, spreading
Antonyms: gathering, collecting, concentrating
verb

disperse:/dɪˈspɜːrs/
verb
分散，散开
to spread across or move away over a large area, or to make something do this
The crowd dispersed quickly. 人群很快就散开了。
Synonyms: scatter, distribute, spread
Antonyms: gather, collect, concentrate
verb

dispersion:/dɪˈspɜːrʒən/
noun
分散，散布
the action or process of distributing things or people over a wide area
The dispersion of seeds is important for plant reproduction.  种子的传播对植物繁殖很重要。
Synonyms: scattering, distribution, spreading
Antonyms: gathering, collection, concentration
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English dispersen, from Latin dispersus, past participle of dispergere (“to scatter”), from dis- (“apart, asunder”) + spargere (“to scatter”).
