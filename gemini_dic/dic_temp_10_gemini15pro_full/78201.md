GeoTIFF: /ˈdʒiːoʊtɪf/ | n. |地理标记的TIFF图像 | A type of TIFF image file that includes geographic metadata, allowing the image to be located spatially. | Example: The satellite imagery was provided as a GeoTIFF file so we could easily integrate it into our GIS system. 卫星图像以GeoTIFF文件的形式提供，因此我们可以轻松地将其集成到我们的GIS系统中。

Example: We converted the drone photos into a GeoTIFF for use in our mapping software. 我们将无人机照片转换为GeoTIFF格式，以便在我们的地图软件中使用。 | 近义词: georeferenced TIFF | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


Etymology:  "Geo" comes from the Greek word "gē" meaning "earth", indicating its geographical nature.  "TIFF" stands for Tagged Image File Format, a widely used raster graphics format.  Thus, GeoTIFF signifies a TIFF image with geographical tagging.
