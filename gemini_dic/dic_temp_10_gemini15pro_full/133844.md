nongraciously: /ˌnɒnˈɡreɪʃəsli/ (British) /ˌnɑːnˈɡreɪʃəsli/ (American) | adv. | 不礼貌地，粗鲁地 |  In a manner lacking grace, courtesy, or kindness.  It describes actions or words delivered in a rude or impolite way. | She nongraciously snatched the gift from his hand. 她粗鲁地从他手中抢走了礼物。| He nongraciously dismissed their concerns. 他很不客气地驳回了他们的担忧。 |  rudely, impolitely, ungraciously, discourteously, churlishly | graciously, politely, courteously, kindly | adverb
