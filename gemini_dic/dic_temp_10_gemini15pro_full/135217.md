nonowners:/ˈnɒnˌoʊnərz/ (US) /ˈnɒnˌəʊnəz/ (UK)| n. |非业主，非拥有者|People who do not own something, particularly referring to property like houses or cars.|例句：The survey targeted nonowners to understand their reasons for not buying a home.  这项调查针对非业主，以了解他们不买房的原因。

例句：Marketing campaigns often focus on converting nonowners into customers. 营销活动通常侧重于将非业主转化为客户。|近义词：non-possessors, renters (in the context of housing)|反义词:owners, possessors|词性:noun
