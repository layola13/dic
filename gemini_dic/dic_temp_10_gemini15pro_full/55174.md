disseminative:/dɪˈsɛmɪnətɪv/
adjective
传播的，散布的
Tending to disseminate; causing dissemination.
The internet has become a highly disseminative medium for news and information.  互联网已成为新闻和信息的强大传播媒介。
The organization plays a disseminative role in promoting health awareness. 该组织在促进健康意识方面发挥着传播作用。
近义词: spreading, distributive, dispersive
反义词: restrictive, suppressive, concealing
词性: adjective

disseminate:/dɪˈsɛmɪneɪt/
verb
传播，散布
To spread or scatter widely.
The government disseminated information about the new policy through the media. 政府通过媒体传播了有关新政策的信息。
The seeds are disseminated by the wind. 种子随风传播。
近义词: spread, distribute, circulate, propagate
反义词: suppress, conceal, withhold
词性: verb
