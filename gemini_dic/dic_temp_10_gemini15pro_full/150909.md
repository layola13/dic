parallelable:/ˈpærəleləbl/ | adj. | 可并行的，可并联的 |  Capable of being operated or processed in parallel; able to be done or occur at the same time as something else. |  These tasks are parallelable, so we can split them up among the team. 这些任务可以并行处理，所以我们可以把它们分给团队成员。| synonyms: concurrent, simultaneous, alongside | antonyms: sequential, consecutive | adjective

parallelable:/ˈpærəleləbl/ | adj. | 可比较的 | Comparable; able to be drawn a parallel to. | The situations in both countries are parallelable to some extent. 这两个国家的情况在某种程度上是可以比较的。 | synonyms: comparable, analogous, similar | antonyms: disparate, dissimilar | adjective
