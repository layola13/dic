badge:/bædʒ/| n.|徽章，证章，标记，象征|A distinctive emblem worn as a mark of office, membership, achievement, or identification. |She wore a badge identifying her as a security guard. 她佩戴着表明她是保安人员的徽章。|
badge:/bædʒ/| n.|（品质或特征的）标记，象征|A mark or sign of a quality or characteristic. |His bruised eye was a badge of honor after the fight. 他在打架后留下的乌眼青是荣誉的象征。|
badge:/bædʒ/| vt.|授予…徽章|To mark or identify with a badge. |The new recruits were badged and sent to their posts. 新兵被授予徽章并被派往各自的岗位。|

近义词: emblem, insignia, symbol, token, mark, label
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以根据语境选择表示“无标记”或“匿名”的词语，例如 unmarked, anonymous)
词性: noun, verb
