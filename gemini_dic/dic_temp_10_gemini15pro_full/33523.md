cicia:/ˈtʃitʃə/ |  n. | 昆虫；蝉  |  A type of insect known for its loud, buzzing song, especially common in warm climates.  From Italian "cicala". | The cicada's song filled the air on a hot summer afternoon. 夏日炎炎的午后，蝉鸣声充满了空气。|  Synonym:  locust | Antonym: None | noun

cicia:/ˈtʃitʃə/ |  n. | 闲聊；饶舌  |  Chatter; idle talk. From Italian "cicala". This meaning is less common than the insect meaning. |  The old women engaged in endless cicia about their neighbors. 老妇人们无休止地闲聊着她们的邻居。| Synonym: chatter, gossip | Antonym: silence | noun
