anti-puritan:/ˌæntiˈpjʊərɪtən/ | adj. n. | 反清教徒的，反对清教徒的人 | Opposed to or against the Puritans, their beliefs, or their practices.  A person opposed to the Puritans. |  He held strong anti-puritan views, believing their strict moral code to be oppressive. 他持有强烈的反清教徒观点，认为他们严格的道德准则具有压迫性。 |  The anti-puritan sentiment grew stronger as the king's power increased. 随着国王权力的增强，反清教徒的情绪也越来越强烈。 | 近义词: libertine, freethinker | 反义词: puritan, pietist | 词性: 形容词，名词
