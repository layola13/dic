frothier:/ˈfrɒθɪər (UK), ˈfrɔːθiər (US)| adj.|更加起泡的，更加轻浮的|More foamy; more frivolous and superficial.|The cappuccino was frothier than usual this morning. 今早的卡布奇诺比平时更起泡。|The politician's speech was frothier than expected, lacking substance. 这位政客的演讲比预期的更轻浮，缺乏实质内容。|近义词：foamier, bubblier, flightier, frivolous|反义词：flat, serious, substantive|词性:形容词
词源:From froth + -ier (comparative adjective suffix).  Froth comes from Old English *froþ, of Germanic origin.
