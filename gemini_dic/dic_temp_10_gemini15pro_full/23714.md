brassicas:/ˈbræsɪkəz/
n.
芸苔属植物；十字花科蔬菜
Plants of the genus Brassica; cruciferous vegetables, such as cabbage, broccoli, and Brussels sprouts.
Brassicas are a good source of vitamins and fiber. 芸苔属植物是维生素和纤维的良好来源。
Brassicas are grown worldwide for food. 全世界都种植芸苔属植物作为食物。
近义词:crucifers, cole crops
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps plants from a different family like Solanaceae (nightshades))
词性:noun
