crewmates: /ˈkruːmeɪts/ | n. |船员，同事，伙伴 | People who are members of the same crew, team, or group, especially in a collaborative or shared experience. |  例句：The crewmates worked together to repair the damaged engine. 船员们一起修理损坏的引擎。

例句：My crewmates on the project were incredibly supportive.  我在这个项目中的同事们非常支持我。 | 近义词：colleagues, teammates, partners, companions | 反义词：opponents, adversaries, enemies | 词性: 名词

词源: crew + mate. "Crew" comes from Old French "creue" meaning "growth, increase," later referring to a group of soldiers, and then to a ship's company. "Mate" comes from Middle Low German "mate" meaning "companion, comrade."
