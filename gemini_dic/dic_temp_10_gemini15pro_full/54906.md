disposable:/dɪˈspəʊzəbl/ /dɪˈspoʊzəbl/
adjective|一次性的，可任意处理的|Intended to be used once and then thrown away.|例句：Disposable diapers are convenient for busy parents.  一次性尿布对忙碌的父母来说很方便。|近义词：expendable, single-use, throwaway|反义词：reusable, permanent, durable
noun|一次性用品|Something designed to be thrown away after use.|例句：Many disposable products end up in landfills. 许多一次性产品最终被填埋。|近义词：throwaway|反义词：reusable


ETYMOLOGY->
From dispose + -able. Dispose comes from Old French *disposer (modern French disposer), from Latin dispōnere, from dis- "apart, away" + pōnere "to put, place."  The suffix -able means "capable of being." So, disposable literally means "capable of being disposed of."
