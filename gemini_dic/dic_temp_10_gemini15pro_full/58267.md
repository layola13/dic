drunk: /drʌŋk/ | adj. | 喝醉的；陶醉的 | Affected by alcohol to the extent of losing control of one's faculties or behavior. | He was so drunk he could barely stand. 他醉得几乎站不住了。|  I felt drunk with happiness. 我感到幸福得飘飘然。 | 近义词: intoxicated, inebriated, tipsy | 反义词: sober | 词性: 形容词

drunk: /drʌŋk/ | n. | 醉汉；酒鬼 | A person who is habitually drunk. | The drunk stumbled out of the bar. 醉汉踉踉跄跄地走出酒吧。 | 近义词: alcoholic, drunkard | 反义词: teetotaler | 词性: 名词


词源: From Middle English *dronken, from Old English *druncen, past participle of drincan (“to drink”). Cognate with West Frisian dronken (“drunk”), Dutch dronken (“drunk”), German trunken (“drunk”).
