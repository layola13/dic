grandad:/ˈɡrændæd/ (UK) /ˈɡrænˌdæd/ (US)| n.|爷爷；外公|An informal word for grandfather, often used by children.|例句：My grandad told me stories about his childhood. 我的爷爷给我讲了他童年时代的故事。

例句：I visited my grandad in the hospital. 我去医院看望了我的爷爷。

近义词：grandfather, grandpa, gramps|反义词:grandson, granddaughter|词性:名词
