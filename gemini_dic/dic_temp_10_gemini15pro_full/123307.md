monogoneutic: /ˌmɒnəɡəˈnjuːtɪk/ /ˌmɑːnəɡəˈnuːtɪk/
adjective
单性生殖的；单基因的
Reproducing asexually by developing from a single cell, or originating from a single gene.
例句：The monogoneutic life cycle of the parasite makes it difficult to control.  这种寄生虫的单性生殖生命周期使其难以控制。

例句：Monogoneutic traits are often easier to study in genetics. 单基因性状在遗传学中通常更容易研究。
近义词：asexual, parthenogenetic
反义词：sexual, digenetic
词性：adjective
