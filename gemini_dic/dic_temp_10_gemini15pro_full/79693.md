glowingly:/ˈɡloʊɪŋli/
adverb
热情洋溢地；容光焕发地
In a way that expresses great praise and enthusiasm; radiantly.
She spoke glowingly of her trip to Italy. 她热情洋溢地讲述了她的意大利之旅。
Synonyms: enthusiastically, warmly, favorably, brightly, radiantly
Antonyms: critically, negatively, dimly, dully
词性: 副词

Etymology:
From "glowing" + "-ly."  "Glowing" comes from the verb "glow," which has its roots in the Old English word "glōwan."
