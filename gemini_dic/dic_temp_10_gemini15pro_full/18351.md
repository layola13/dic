bellow:/ˈbeləʊ/ (UK) /ˈbɛloʊ/ (US)| v.|大声吼叫；怒吼；（牛等）哞哞叫|emit a deep loud roar, typically in pain or anger|The sergeant bellowed orders at his troops. 军士对着他的部队大声吼叫命令。|The bull bellowed in pain after being stung by a bee. 公牛被蜜蜂蛰了以后疼得哞哞直叫。|近义词：roar, shout, yell, bawl|反义词:whisper, murmur|词性:动词
Etymology: From Old English *bellan, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch bellen and German bellen ‘to bark’.
