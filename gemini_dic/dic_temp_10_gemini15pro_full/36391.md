coinventor:/ˌkoʊɪnˈvɛntər/| n.|共同发明人；共同发明者|A coinventor is someone who invents something together with one or more other people.|例句：She is listed as a coinventor on the patent. 她被列为该专利的共同发明人。

例句：The two brothers were coinventors of the new device. 这两兄弟是这项新装置的共同发明人。|近义词：collaborator, joint inventor|反义词:|noun
