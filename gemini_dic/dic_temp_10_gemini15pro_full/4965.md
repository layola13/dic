alkalies: /ˈæl.kə.laɪz/ | n. | 碱 |  A group of chemical substances that are soluble in water, have a pH greater than 7, and can neutralize acids.  Forming salts when reacting with acids. Typically, they feel slippery to the touch. |  Sodium hydroxide and potassium hydroxide are strong alkalies. 氢氧化钠和氢氧化钾是强碱。 | synonyms: bases | antonyms: acids | noun

alkali: /ˈæl.kə.laɪ/ | n. | 碱 | Any of various soluble mineral salts found in natural water and arid soils. Also, any substance that has basic properties. |  Baking soda is a mild alkali. 小苏打是一种弱碱。 | synonyms: base | antonyms: acid | noun
