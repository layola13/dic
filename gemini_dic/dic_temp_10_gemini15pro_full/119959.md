millenarism:/ˌmɪləˈnɛəriɪzəm/ | n. | 千年主义；千禧年主义 | The belief that a golden age or paradise will occur on Earth, especially in the near future.  This belief is often associated with the second coming of Christ. |  Many cults preach millenarism and the imminent end of the world. 许多邪教宣扬千年主义和世界末日即将来临。| synonyms: chiliasm, millennialism | antonyms: secularism | noun

millenarian:/ˌmɪləˈnɛəriən/ | adj. | 千年至福的; 千禧年的 | Relating to or believing in the millennium.  Also used to describe someone who believes in millenarism. | The preacher delivered a fiery sermon on millenarian prophecies.  这位传教士就千禧年的预言发表了激烈的布道。| synonyms: chiliastic, apocalyptic | antonyms: secular | adjective

millenarian:/ˌmɪləˈnɛəriən/ | n. | 千年至福论者；相信千年至福说的人 | A person who believes in millenarism. |  The millenarian predicted the world would end in the year 2000.  这位千年至福论者预言世界将在2000年终结。 | synonyms: chiliast | antonyms: secularist | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *millenarius "containing a thousand," from Latin mille "thousand."  The term is ultimately derived from the Book of Revelation in the New Testament, which speaks of a thousand-year reign of Christ on Earth.
