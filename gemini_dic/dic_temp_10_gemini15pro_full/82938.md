gumshoed:/ˈɡʌmʃuːd/| adj.|穿着胶底鞋的; 暗中侦查的，偷偷摸摸的|Wearing rubber-soled shoes; acting like a detective, especially in a secretive or sneaky way.|The gumshoed reporter followed the politician for days, hoping to uncover a scandal.  这个偷偷摸摸的记者跟踪了这个政客好几天，希望能发现丑闻。|Synonyms: secretive, sneaky, stealthy, clandestine, surreptitious|Antonyms: open, honest, forthright|adjective
