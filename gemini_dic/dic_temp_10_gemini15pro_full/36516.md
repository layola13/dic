collider:/kəˈlaɪdər/| n.|对撞机；碰撞器|A machine that accelerates particles to high speeds and then causes them to collide. |例句：The Large Hadron Collider is the world's largest and most powerful particle accelerator.  大型强子对撞机是世界上最大、能量最高的粒子加速器。

例句：Scientists use colliders to study the fundamental constituents of matter. 科学家使用对撞机来研究物质的基本组成部分。|近义词：accelerator|反义词:|noun
