dumbest:/ˈdʌməst/| adj.|最愚蠢的|Superlative form of dumb; most lacking in intelligence or good judgment.|That was the dumbest thing I've ever heard. 那是迄今为止我听过的最愚蠢的事情。|Example:  Choosing to walk in the rain without an umbrella was the dumbest decision she made all day. 选择在没有雨伞的情况下在雨中行走是她一整天做出的最愚蠢的决定。|Synonyms: stupidest, most foolish, silliest|Antonyms: smartest, brightest, wisest|词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *domb*, from Old English *dumb* (“mute, speechless, silent”), from Proto-Germanic *dumbaz* (“dumb, stupid”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰembʰ-* (“to be stunned, dazed”).  The meaning shifted from "unable to speak" to "unintelligent" over time.
