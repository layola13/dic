historian:/hɪˈstɔːriən/ | n. |历史学家；史学工作者|A person who studies and writes about the past.  A scholar of history.|例句：The historian specialized in the medieval period.  这位历史学家专攻中世纪时期。

例句：Historians debate the causes of the war. 历史学家们争论这场战争的起因。|近义词：chronicler, annalist, scholar|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
Etymology: Late Middle English: from Old French historien, from late Latin historiarius ‘belonging to history’, from historia (see history).
