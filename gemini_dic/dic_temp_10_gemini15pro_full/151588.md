parthenogenetic:/ˌpɑːrθənəʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk/
adjective
单性生殖的; 处女生殖的
Relating to or characterized by parthenogenesis.  Development of an egg without fertilization.
例句：Parthenogenetic reproduction is common in some insect species.  某些昆虫物种中单性生殖很常见。

例句：Scientists are studying the parthenogenetic capabilities of certain lizards. 科学家正在研究某些蜥蜴的单性生殖能力。

近义词：asexual, agamic
反义词：sexual
词性：adjective

parthenogenesis:/ˌpɑːrθənəʊˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
noun
单性生殖; 处女生殖
Reproduction from an ovum without fertilization, especially as a normal process in some invertebrates and lower plants.
例句：Parthenogenesis allows some species to reproduce even in the absence of males. 单性生殖允许一些物种即使在没有雄性的情况下也能繁殖。

例句：The study of parthenogenesis can offer insights into reproductive biology. 对单性生殖的研究可以为了解生殖生物学提供见解。

近义词：asexual reproduction, agamogenesis
反义词：sexual reproduction
词性：noun
