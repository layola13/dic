fishbowls:/ˈfɪʃboʊlz/| n. |鱼缸（复数）|A transparent container, typically spherical or rounded, filled with water and used to keep fish and other aquatic animals.|The fishbowls in the office were filled with colorful fish. 办公室里的鱼缸里满是色彩斑斓的鱼。|近义词：aquariums, tanks|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "dry land" in a metaphorical sense)|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From fish + bowl.  "Fish" comes from Old English "fisc", and "bowl" comes from Old English "bolla".  The compound word "fishbowl" likely emerged sometime in the Middle Ages.
