dactylopterous:/ˌdæktɪˈlɒptərəs/
adjective
指翼鱼的；有指状鳍的
Having finger-like fins or wings.  Relating to or characteristic of the fish family Dactylopteridae, which includes flying gurnards.
The dactylopterous fish glided along the ocean floor.  指翼鱼沿着海底滑翔。
Synonyms: finger-finned,  gurnard-like
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider fish without finger-like fins)
Etymology:  From Greek *daktylos* (finger) + *pteron* (wing, fin)
