hierographical:/ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪkəl/
adjective
象形文字的；与象形文字有关的
relating to hieroglyphics; expressed by hieroglyphics
The Rosetta Stone was crucial in deciphering hieroglyphical writing.  罗塞塔石碑对于破译象形文字至关重要。

The ancient Egyptians used a hieroglyphical system of writing. 古埃及人使用象形文字书写系统。

Synonym: hieroglyphic
Antonym: (No direct antonym, consider "alphabetic" or "modern")
Etymology:  From Greek hieros "sacred" + glyphein "to carve"
