conduce:/kənˈduːs/ /kənˈdjuːs/
verb
导致，有助于，有益于
to lead or contribute to a particular result; to be helpful in bringing something about.
Regular exercise conduces to good health.  规律的运动有益于健康。
His actions conduced to his downfall. 他的行为导致了他的垮台。
近义词: contribute, lead, promote, facilitate
反义词: hinder, obstruct, impede
词性: verb

词源:  From Latin conducere "to lead or bring together," from com- "together" (see com-) + ducere "to lead" (see duke).
