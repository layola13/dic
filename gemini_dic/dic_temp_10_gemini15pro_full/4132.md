air-drop:/ˈeəˌdrɒp/ (US) /ˈeəˌdrɒp/ (UK)| n. v.|空投，空降；(电子设备间)隔空投送|The act of dropping supplies from an aircraft or transferring data wirelessly between devices.|The air-drop was successful, and the supplies reached the stranded hikers. 空投很成功，物资送到了被困的徒步旅行者手中。|Can you air-drop that photo to my phone? 你能把那张照片隔空投送到我的手机上吗？|近义词：parachute, deliver, transfer (data), beam (data)|反义词: upload, receive (data)|词性: noun, verb

词源:  "air-" + "drop".  "air" refers to the sky or atmosphere, while "drop" refers to the act of falling or releasing something.  The term originated in the context of military operations, referring to delivering supplies by parachute from an aircraft.  Later, it was adapted to describe the wireless transfer of data between electronic devices.
