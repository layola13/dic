monoglacialist:/ˌmɒnəˈɡleɪʃəlɪst/ /ˌmɑːnəˈɡleɪʃəlɪst/
n.
冰期学家；单冰期论者
A scientist who specializes in the study of a single glacial period.  A person who believes that there was only one glacial period in the Earth's history.
The monoglacialist presented evidence supporting their theory of a single ice age.  这位冰期学家提出了支持单冰期理论的证据。
近义词:glacialist, ice age specialist
反义词:polyglacialist
