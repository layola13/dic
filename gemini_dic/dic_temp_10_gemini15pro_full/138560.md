nq:/ɛnˈkjuː/ |abbr.|  no questions|没有问题| NQ means "no questions". | Example: "Are you sure you understand?" "NQ."  “你确定你明白了吗？” “没有问题。” | Synonym: no problem, all clear| Antonym: uncertain, unclear | abbreviation

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What does he do for a living? = 他靠什么谋生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is she working on right now? = 她现在在做什么？


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done to improve her skills? = 她做了什么来提高她的技能？


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been doing since I last saw you? = 自从我上次见到你以来你一直在做什么？
What has he been working on for the past few months? = 过去几个月他一直在做什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 聚会上发生了什么？


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? = 我看到她的时候她在想什么？


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you came here? = 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had happened before the police arrived? = 警察到达之前发生了什么？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will happen if we don't act now? = 如果我们现在不采取行动会发生什么？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们将在下次会议上讨论什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将取得什么成就？
What will have changed by the time we get there? = 等我们到那里的时候会有什么变化？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2030? = 到2030年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时她将已经学习了什么多久了？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
What would have happened if I hadn't been there? = 如果我不在那里会发生什么？


被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？


疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？


否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what you're saying. = 她不明白你在说什么。
