nonextinctions:/ˌnɒnɪkˈstɪŋkʃənz/ | n. | 未灭绝；未绝种 |  The study of nonextinctions focuses on species that have persisted despite environmental challenges.  这项研究的重点是那些尽管面临环境挑战但仍然存在的物种。| 例句：The conservation efforts contributed to the nonextinctions of several endangered bird species. 保护工作促成了几种濒危鸟类的未灭绝。| 近义词：survival, persistence, continuation | 反义词：extinction, annihilation, extermination | 词性: 名词

词源: "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "extinction" (noun meaning the state or process of a species, family, or larger group ceasing to exist). 由表示“不”的前缀"non-"和名词"extinction"（意为一个物种、家族或更大群体停止存在的状态或过程）组成。
