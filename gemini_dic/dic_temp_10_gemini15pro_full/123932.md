moretto:/məˈrɛtoʊ/
n.
a dark-skinned child, especially one depicted in Italian Renaissance art.
A type of dark sweet cherry.
A small, dark-skinned person; a Moorish child represented in painting.
例句：The moretto in the painting is thought to be a portrait of a servant. 这幅画中的黑皮肤小孩被认为是一位仆人的肖像。

例句：Moretto cherries are known for their rich, sweet flavor.  莫雷托樱桃以其浓郁的甜味而闻名。

近义词：Moor, dark-skinned child, blackamoor (archaic and offensive)
反义词：(No direct antonym, consider "fair-skinned child")
词性：noun
