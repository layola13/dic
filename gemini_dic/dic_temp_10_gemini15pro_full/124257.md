mostra:/ˈmoʊstrə/ | n. | 展览，展览会 | An exhibition or show, especially of works of art or other items of interest. | Example: The artist's work was featured in a mostra at the local gallery.  这位艺术家的作品在当地画廊的展览中展出。

Example: We visited a mostra of vintage cars. 我们参观了一个老爷车展览。| 近义词: exhibition, show, display, exposition | 反义词: concealment | 词性: 名词

词源: From Italian *mostra*, from Latin *monstrare* "to show," frequentative of *monēre* "to warn, advise, remind."
