hoisting:/ˈhɔɪstɪŋ/ | n. | 提升，吊起;  (尤指在 JavaScript 中的)变量提升 | The hoisting of the flag marked the beginning of the ceremony. 升旗标志着仪式的开始。In JavaScript, variable declarations are hoisted to the top of their scope. 在 JavaScript 中，变量声明会被提升到其作用域的顶部。 | Example: The crane was used for hoisting heavy materials.  起重机被用来吊起重物。  Example:  Understanding hoisting is crucial for writing predictable JavaScript code. 理解变量提升对于编写可预测的 JavaScript 代码至关重要。 | 近义词: lifting, raising | 反义词: lowering | 词性: 名词
