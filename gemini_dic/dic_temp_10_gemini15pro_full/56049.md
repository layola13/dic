doc:/dɑːk/ /dɒk/ | n. | 文件；文档；医生（口语）| A document, especially one stored electronically.  A physician or surgeon (informal). | Example: Please send me the doc by email.  请通过电子邮件把文件发给我。  Example:  He went to see the doc about his back pain. 他去看医生治疗背痛。 | 近义词: file, document, physician, MD | 反义词: (For document) N/A, (For doctor) patient | noun
