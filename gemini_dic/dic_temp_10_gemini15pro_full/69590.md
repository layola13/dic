例句：The feltness of the blanket made it perfect for a cold night. 这条毯子的质感使它非常适合寒冷的夜晚。|近义词：softness, texture, fluffiness|反义词:hardness, roughness|词性:名词

词源: From felt + -ness.  来自 felt（毛毡）+ -ness（名词后缀，表示状态或性质）。
