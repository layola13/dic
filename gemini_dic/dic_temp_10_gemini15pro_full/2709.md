adsorb:/ædˈsɔːrb/ /ædˈzɔːrb/| v.|吸附|To attract and hold (molecules of a gas or liquid or solute) to a surface, resulting in a relatively high concentration of the molecules at the surface.|Activated charcoal is used to adsorb poisonous gases.  活性炭用于吸附有毒气体。

The filter adsorbs impurities from the water.  过滤器吸附水中的杂质。|近义词: soak up, take up, accumulate|反义词:desorb|词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sponge adsorbs water quickly. = 海绵快速吸水。
Activated carbon adsorbs odors. = 活性炭吸附异味。

一般过去时:
The cloth adsorbed the spilled liquid. = 布吸附了溢出的液体。
The soil adsorbed the fertilizer. = 土壤吸附了肥料。

现在完成时:
The plant has adsorbed nutrients from the soil. = 植物已经从土壤中吸附了营养物质。
The material has adsorbed a significant amount of moisture. = 该材料已经吸附了大量的水分。

过去完成时:
The filter had adsorbed impurities before the water reached the tap. = 在水到达水龙头之前，过滤器已经吸附了杂质。
The soil had adsorbed the pesticide before the rain washed it away. = 在雨水冲走农药之前，土壤已经吸附了农药。

将来时:
The new material will adsorb more pollutants. = 新材料将吸附更多污染物。
The system will adsorb excess moisture from the air. = 该系统将吸附空气中多余的水分。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
