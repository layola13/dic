dialectal:/ˌdaɪəˈlɛktəl/
adjective
方言的
Relating to a dialect; characteristic of a particular dialect.
Her accent was thick with dialectal peculiarities. 她的口音充满了方言特色。
近义词:vernacular, regional
反义词:standard, formal
词性:adjective

dialectal:/ˌdaɪəˈlɛktəl/
adjective
用方言的
Expressed or written in a dialect.
The poem was written in dialectal Scots. 这首诗是用苏格兰方言写的。
近义词:vernacular, regional
反义词:standard, formal
词性:adjective
