bobcat:/ˈbɑːbkæt/ (US) /ˈbɒbkæt/ (UK)| n. |山猫，猞猁|A North American wild cat (Lynx rufus) with a short tail and typically reddish-brown fur, marked with dark spots and bars. It is smaller than the Canada lynx.|We saw a bobcat while hiking in the mountains. 我们在山上徒步旅行时看到了一只山猫。|Near synonyms: lynx, wildcat|Antonym: (No direct antonym for an animal species, could use "domestic cat" for contrast)|noun
