coinciding:/ˌkoʊɪnˈsaɪdɪŋ/ | adj. | 一致的，同时发生的 | happening at the same time | The two events were coinciding, adding to the drama of the day.  这两个事件同时发生，增加了当天的戏剧性。 | synonyms: concurrent, simultaneous, synchronous | antonyms: separate, distinct, asynchronous | adjective

coincidence:/koʊˈɪnsɪdəns/ | n. | 巧合，一致 | a remarkable concurrence of events or circumstances without apparent causal connection | It was pure coincidence that we both wore the same outfit. 我们都穿了同样的衣服，纯属巧合。| synonyms: accident, chance, fluke | antonyms: intention, plan, design | noun
