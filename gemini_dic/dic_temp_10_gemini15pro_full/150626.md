papilliform:/ˌpæpɪˈlɪfɔːrm/
adjective
乳头状的，具乳突的
Resembling a papilla or nipple; covered with papillae.  Having a small, rounded protuberance.
The tongue's surface is papilliform, covered with tiny taste buds. 舌头的表面是乳头状的，覆盖着微小的味蕾。
The cactus has papilliform spines. 仙人掌有乳头状的刺。
近义词: papillary, mammillary, nipple-shaped
反义词: smooth, flat
词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin papilla (nipple) + -form (having the form of).
