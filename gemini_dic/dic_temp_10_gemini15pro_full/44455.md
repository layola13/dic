cross-tongue:/ˈkrɔːs tʌŋ/| n.|交叉吹奏; 交叉吐舌|A technique used by wind instrumentalists, especially brass players, where the tongue rapidly alternates between two positions in the mouth, creating a fluttering or trilling effect.|The trumpet player used cross-tonguing to create a unique sound. 小号手使用交叉吹奏技巧创造出一种独特的声音。|近义词:double-tonguing, flutter-tonguing|反义词:single-tonguing|noun

cross-tongue:/ˈkrɔːs tʌŋ/| v.|交叉吹奏; 交叉吐舌|To play a wind instrument using the cross-tonguing technique.|He cross-tongued the passage, giving it a distinctive character.  他用交叉吐舌的技巧演奏了这段乐句，赋予了它独特的个性。|近义词:double-tongue, flutter-tongue|反义词:single-tongue|verb
