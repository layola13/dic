nonbarbarousness:/ˌnɒnbɑːrˈbɛrəsnəs/
|n.| 非野蛮；文明 |The nonbarbarousness of their culture is reflected in their art and literature. 他们的文化并非野蛮，这体现在他们的艺术和文学中。|  Nonbarbarousness is a hallmark of civilized societies. 文明社会的标志之一就是非野蛮性。| civility, refinement, culture | barbarity, savagery | noun

Etymology:
From non- (not) + barbarous + -ness (state of being).  "Barbarous" ultimately derives from the Greek word "bárbaros," meaning "foreign," and later used by the Greeks and Romans to describe those whose languages they didn't understand and whom they considered uncivilized.
