overdisciplines:/ˌoʊvərˈdɪsəplɪnz/| v.|过度纪律；管教过严|To impose excessive discipline or control upon; to regulate or train too strictly.|The coach overdisciplined the team, leading to burnout and resentment. 教练对队员管教过严，导致队员倦怠和不满。|The school's overdisciplined environment stifled creativity. 学校过度纪律化的环境扼杀了创造力。|近义词：overregulate, overcontrol|反义词: underdiscipline, neglect|动词

overdiscipline:/ˌoʊvərˈdɪsəplɪn/| n.|过度纪律；过分管教|Excessive discipline or control.|The overdiscipline in the military can be both beneficial and detrimental. 军队中过度的纪律既有利也有弊。|The child suffered under the overdiscipline of his parents. 孩子在他父母过分严格的管教下受苦。|近义词：overregulation, overcontrol|反义词: underdiscipline, permissiveness|名词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "discipline" (from Latin "disciplina," meaning "instruction, knowledge").
