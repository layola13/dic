dogmaticalness:/dɒɡˈmætɪkəlnəs/ /dɑːɡˈmætɪkəlnəs/
n.
教条主义；武断；独断
The quality of being dogmatic; asserting opinions in a doctrinaire or arrogant manner; expressing personal beliefs or opinions as if they were unquestionable truths.
His dogmaticalness alienated many of his followers. 他的教条主义疏远了他的许多追随者。
近义词: dogmatism, rigidity, inflexibility, authoritarianism, doctrinaireism
反义词: open-mindedness, flexibility, tolerance, skepticism
词性: 名词
