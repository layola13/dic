palpations:/ˌpælpeɪˈʃənz/| n.|触诊，悸动|The doctor performed palpations on the patient's abdomen to check for any abnormalities. 医生对病人的腹部进行触诊，以检查是否有任何异常。|The patient reported experiencing heart palpitations. 病人报告说感到心悸。|近义词：examination, feeling, pulsations, throbbing|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 numbness 麻木)|noun

palpation:/ˌpælˈpeɪʃn/| n.|触诊|The doctor used palpation to assess the size of the liver. 医生用触诊来评估肝脏的大小。|Palpation of the lymph nodes revealed no swelling. 触诊淋巴结未发现肿胀。|近义词：examination, feeling|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 observation 观察)|noun

词源: From Late Latin *palpātiōn- (stem of palpātiō), from Latin palpō (“stroke, touch gently, caress, flatter”), perhaps from an imitative PIE root *pal- (“to touch, feel”).
