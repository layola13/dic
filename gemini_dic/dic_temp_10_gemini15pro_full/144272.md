outsought:/ˌaʊtˈsɔːt/
adjective
广受欢迎的；非常需要的；受追捧的
Highly sought after; in great demand.
The outsought antique vase was finally sold at auction for a record price. 这件广受欢迎的古董花瓶最终在拍卖会上以创纪录的价格售出。
outsought tickets for the concert.  演唱会的抢手门票。
近义词: sought-after, desired, coveted, in-demand
反义词: undesirable, unpopular, unwanted
词性: adjective

词源:  "Outsought" is formed from the prefix "out-" (meaning exceeding or surpassing) and the past participle of the verb "seek," which is "sought."  Therefore, it literally means "sought beyond others" or "exceedingly sought."
