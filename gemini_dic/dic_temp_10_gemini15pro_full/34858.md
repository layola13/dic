clinodome: /ˈklaɪnəˌdoʊm/ | n. |斜圆顶，倾斜穹顶 | A crystal form consisting of two or more intersecting inclined crystal faces.  It is a dome-like structure where the faces are not symmetrical about a vertical axis. |  The mineral calcite often exhibits clinodomes. 方解石经常呈现斜圆顶的形态。 | The clinodome faces on the gypsum crystal were clearly visible under the microscope.  在显微镜下，石膏晶体上的斜圆顶面清晰可见。 | Near synonyms: dome, cupola | Antonyms:  N/A | noun
