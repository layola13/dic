concludes:/kənˈkluːdz/
verb
结束，推断出
brings or comes to an end; finishes; infers
The meeting concludes at 5 pm. 会议下午5点结束。
The report concludes that the project is feasible. 报告的结论是该项目可行。
Synonyms:finishes, ends, terminates, closes, deduces, infers
Antonyms:begins, starts, commences, initiates
