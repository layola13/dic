cavalcade:/ˌkævəlˈkeɪd/| n.| 骑兵队伍；行列；盛大游行|A procession of people riding horses, or a series of related things.  A cavalcade of horsemen greeted the king.  国王受到了一队骑兵的欢迎。|例句：

A cavalcade of cars followed the president.
总统的车队随后跟上。

The museum boasts a cavalcade of antique toys.
这家博物馆以其丰富的古董玩具收藏而自豪。

|近义词：procession, parade, array, series, sequence|反义词:  N/A |词性:noun
