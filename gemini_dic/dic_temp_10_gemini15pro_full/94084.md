impasto:/ɪmˈpɑːstoʊ/ (US), /ɪmˈpæstoʊ/ (UK)
|n.| 厚涂颜料；厚涂法
|The technique of applying paint thickly, so that brushstrokes or palette knife marks are visible. It creates a textured surface. |
|The artist used impasto to create a sense of depth in the painting.| 艺术家使用厚涂法在画作中营造了一种深度感。

|
|The impasto in Van Gogh's paintings is very characteristic of his style.| 梵高绘画中的厚涂法非常具有他的风格特征。

|Synonym:|texture, layering, thickness
|Antonym:|smoothness, flatness
|noun|


impastos:/ɪmˈpɑːstoʊz/ (US), /ɪmˈpæstoʊz/ (UK)
|n. (plural)| 厚涂颜料（复数）; 厚涂法（复数）
|The artist's impastos added a dynamic quality to the canvas.| 艺术家的厚涂颜料为画布增添了动感。

|
|The various impastos in the artwork created a fascinating interplay of light and shadow.| 艺术品中各种各样的厚涂法创造了光影之间迷人的相互作用。


|Synonym:|textures, layers, thicknesses
|Antonym:|smoothnesses, flatnesses
|noun|
