anti-creational:/ˌænti-kriˈeɪʃənl/
| adj. | 反创造的，反对创造的 | Opposed to creation, typically in a philosophical or religious context.  Expressing or characterized by opposition to creation or creativity.  |  Some philosophers hold anti-creational views, believing the universe is eternal. 一些哲学家持有反创造的观点，认为宇宙是永恒的。 |  The artist rebelled against the anti-creational dogma of the regime.  这位艺术家反抗了该政权的反创造教条。 |  nihilistic, destructive, iconoclastic | creative, generative, constructive | adjective
