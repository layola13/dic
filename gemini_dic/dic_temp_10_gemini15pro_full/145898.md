overdiffused:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈfjuːzd/ | adj. | 过度扩散的；过度漫射的 |  Spread out too thinly or widely; excessively diffused.  This term is often used in technical contexts, like photography or semiconductor manufacturing, where a substance or effect is spread more than desired. | The overdiffused light made the photograph look hazy. 过度漫射的光线使照片看起来朦胧。| The dopants in the semiconductor were overdiffused, ruining the chip. 半导体中的掺杂剂过度扩散，损坏了芯片。 | synonyms: overspread, dispersed, scattered | antonyms: concentrated, focused | adjective
