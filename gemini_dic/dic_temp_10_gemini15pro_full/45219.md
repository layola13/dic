culmen:/ˈkʌlmən/| n.|顶点，最高点；鸟喙的脊；|The culmen of his career was winning the Nobel Prize.  他事业的顶峰是获得诺贝尔奖。|Synonyms: peak, apex, summit, pinnacle, climax|Antonyms: nadir, bottom, base|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *culmen*  "top, summit, roof," related to *collumen* "summit," from *columen* "support, supporting timber, prop," from *columna* "column."
