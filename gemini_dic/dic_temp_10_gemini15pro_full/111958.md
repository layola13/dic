lomatinous:/loʊˈmætɪnəs/| adj.|粘液的；被粘液覆盖的|Covered with or resembling mucus; slimy.|The lomatinous substance coating the frog's skin helped it retain moisture.  这层覆盖在青蛙皮肤上的粘液物质帮助它保持水分。|例句：The lomatinous secretion from the snail left a glistening trail.  蜗牛分泌的粘液留下了一条闪闪发光的痕迹。

例句：The wound was covered in a lomatinous discharge. 伤口上覆盖着一层粘稠的分泌物。|近义词：mucilaginous, slimy, viscous|反义词：dry, non-viscous|词性: adjective
词源: From Latin "lomentum" (a bean pod) + "-ous" (suffix forming adjectives).  源自拉丁语 "lomentum"（豆荚）+ "-ous"（构成形容词的后缀）。
