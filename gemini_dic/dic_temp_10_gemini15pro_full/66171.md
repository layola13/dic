excessive:/ɪkˈsesɪv/| adj.|过多的，过分的，极度的|Going to excess can be harmful.  过量有害。|The amount of force used was excessive.  使用的武力过大。|近义词：immoderate, inordinate, extravagant, extreme|反义词：moderate, reasonable, temperate|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *excessivus, from Latin excessus (“excess”), past participle of excedere (“to go beyond, surpass”).  Ultimately from ex (“out of, from”) + cedere (“to go, yield”).
