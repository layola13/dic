felicita:/fɛlɪˈtʃiːtɑː/ /fɛˌlɪtʃɪˈtɑː/
n.
幸福; 快乐; 祝贺; 贺词
Happiness; bliss; congratulations.  Expressing good wishes on a happy occasion.

Felicita is an Italian word that expresses happiness, bliss, and congratulations.  It can be used as a general term for happiness or to offer congratulations on a special occasion.

例句：
She found true felicita in her simple life.
她在简单的生活中找到了真正的幸福。

They sent their felicita to the newlyweds.
他们向新婚夫妇表达了祝贺。


近义词：happiness, joy, bliss, congratulations, felicitations
反义词：unhappiness, sorrow, misery

词性: noun
