forester:/ˈfɔːrɪstər/ (US) /ˈfɒrɪstə(r)/ (UK)| n. |森林管理员；护林员|A person who is in charge of caring for and managing a forest. |例句：The forester planted new seedlings in the cleared area. 护林员在清理过的区域种植了新的树苗。

例句：The forester patrolled the forest to prevent illegal logging.  护林员在森林里巡逻，防止非法砍伐。

近义词：ranger, woodsman, forest guard|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

forester:/ˈfɔːrɪstər/ (US) /ˈfɒrɪstə(r)/ (UK)| n. |林务员|A person employed to manage or oversee a forest. |例句：The forester marked the trees for selective harvesting. 林务员标记了树木以便进行选择性采伐。

例句：The forester educated the public about wildfire prevention. 林务员向公众宣传预防野火的知识。

近义词：forest manager, silviculturist|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
