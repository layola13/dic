nematode:/ˈnɛmətəʊd/ (UK) /ˈnɛməˌtoʊd/ (US)| n.|线虫；线形动物|A worm of the large phylum Nematoda, such as a roundworm or threadworm.|例句：Plant nematodes can cause significant damage to crops. 植物线虫会对农作物造成重大损害。

例句：The soil sample contained several different species of nematodes. 土壤样本中包含几种不同种类的线虫。|近义词：roundworm, threadworm|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

nematoid:/ˈnɛmətɔɪd/ (UK) /ˈnɛməˌtɔɪd/ (US)| adj.|线虫状的；线形的|Resembling or characteristic of a nematode.|例句：The parasite had a nematoid body shape. 寄生虫具有线虫状的身体形状。

例句：The scientist discovered a new nematoid species in the deep sea. 科学家在深海发现了一种新的线虫状物种。|近义词：wormlike, threadlike|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective
