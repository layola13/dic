organographist: /ˌɔːrɡəˈnɒɡrəfɪst/ | n. | 器官学家，器官学研究者 | A specialist in organography, the detailed description of the organs of plants or animals. |  The organographist meticulously documented the structure of the flower's reproductive organs. 器官学家一丝不苟地记录了花朵生殖器官的结构。|  The organographist compared the leaf structures of several different species. 器官学家比较了几种不同植物的叶子结构。 |  近义词: anatomist, morphologist | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun


Etymology:

From organography + -ist.

Organography: from  organo- + -graphy

organo-: from Greek órganon 'tool, instrument, organ'

-graphy: from Greek -graphía  'description of'

-ist:  a suffix forming agent nouns denoting a person who performs a specified action or activity.
