monoxylous:/ˌmɒnɒkˈsaɪləs/| adj.|单木的，由单块木材制成的|Made of a single piece of wood; consisting of a single piece of wood.  Relating to or designating a stem or axis formed of a single elongated cell or filament, as in some algae.|The craftsman created a beautiful monoxylous bowl from a single block of walnut.|Near Synonyms:  one-piece, solid, integral|Antonyms:  jointed, segmented, composite|Etymology:  From Greek *mónos* ("single, alone") + *xýlon* ("wood")|Word Type: Adjective
