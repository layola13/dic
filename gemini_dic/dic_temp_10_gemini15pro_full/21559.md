blueprinters:/ˈbluːˌprɪntərz/| n. |蓝图绘制者，设计者|People who create blueprints, plans or designs for something, often large-scale projects like buildings or strategies.  They are the architects of the future, laying the groundwork for what's to come.|The blueprints were carefully reviewed by the team of blueprinters before construction began. 蓝图在施工开始前由蓝图绘制者团队仔细审查过。|近义词：designers, planners, architects, drafters|反义词: implementers, builders, executors|Noun
