hemibranchi:/ˌhɛmiˈbræŋkaɪ/
noun
半鳃类动物
A fish of the order Hemibranchii, having a single gill on each side.
The seahorse is a hemibranchi. 海马是一种半鳃类动物。
近义词: syngnathid (海龙科动物, 更广义)
反义词:  (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑用拥有完整鳃的鱼类，例如 teleost 硬骨鱼)
词性: n.

hemibranchia:/ˌhɛmiˈbræŋkiə/
noun
半鳃
One of the two gill arches of a hemibranch.
The hemibranchia is located under the operculum. 半鳃位于鳃盖下。
近义词: half-gill
反义词: holobranch (全鳃)
词性: n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek hemi- (half) + brankhia (gills).
