ethicality:/ˌeθɪˈkælɪti/
n.
伦理性，道德性
The ethicality of his decision is questionable. 他的决定的伦理性值得怀疑。
The ethicality of animal testing is a hotly debated topic. 动物试验的伦理性是一个备受争议的话题。
近义词：morality, righteousness
反义词：immorality, unethicalness
词性: noun

词源：From "ethical" + "-ity". "Ethical" comes from the Greek word "ethikos" (ἠθικός), meaning "relating to character" or "moral," derived from "ethos" (ἦθος), meaning "character" or "custom."  The suffix "-ity" forms abstract nouns denoting a state or quality.
