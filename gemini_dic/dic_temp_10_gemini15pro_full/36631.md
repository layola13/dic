collectivistically:/kəˌlɛktɪˈvɪstɪkli/
| adv. |集体地；集体主义地|In a collectivist manner; as a collective.  Prioritizing the needs of the group over the individual.|例句：They worked collectivistically to achieve their shared goal. 他们集体工作以实现共同的目标。|例句：The community collectivistically decided on the new rules. 社区集体决定了新的规则。|近义词：cooperatively, jointly, communally|反义词：individually, independently|词性:副词

Etymology:
From "collectivist" + "-ically".  "Collectivist" comes from "collective" + "-ist". "Collective" comes from French "collectif," from Latin "collectivus," past participle of "colligere," meaning "to gather together."  "-ist" is a suffix forming nouns and adjectives denoting adherence to a doctrine or system.  "-ically" is a suffix forming adverbs from adjectives ending in "-ic".
