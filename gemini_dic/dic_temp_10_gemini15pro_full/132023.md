noncorrespondent:/ˌnɒnkɒrɪˈspɒndənt/ | adj. | 不一致的，不符的，不相符的 | Not matching or agreeing; different. |  The figures quoted in the two reports are noncorrespondent. 这两份报告中引用的数字不一致。 |  The witness's statement was noncorrespondent with the physical evidence. 目击者的证词与物证不符。 | Synonyms: dissimilar, divergent, incompatible, inconsistent, discrepant | Antonyms: correspondent, consistent, matching, similar, congruent | adjective
