needlessnesses:/ˈniːdlɪsnəsɪz/
n.
不必要；无用；多余
The state or quality of being needless or unnecessary.
The needlessnesses of the paperwork frustrated everyone.  这些文书工作的繁琐让每个人都很沮丧。
近义词：superfluities, redundancies, excesses
反义词：necessities, essentials
词性：名词 (plural noun)

needless:/ˈniːdlɪs/
adj.
不必要的；无用的
Not needed; unnecessary.
It is needless to say that we were all disappointed.  不用说，我们都很失望。
近义词：unnecessary, superfluous, redundant
反义词：necessary, essential, required
词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *nedles, from Old English *nēadlēas, from Proto-Germanic *nauþizlauzą (“useless, unnecessary”), equivalent to need +‎ -less. Cognate with Dutch noodloos, German nutzlos.
