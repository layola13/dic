incessant:/ɪnˈsesənt/| adj.|不停的，不断的|Continuing without interruption; ceaseless.|The incessant rain caused flooding in the area. 不停的雨造成了该地区的洪水。|近义词：continuous, constant, perpetual, unremitting|反义词：intermittent, occasional, sporadic|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin incessant-, incessans, present participle of incessare "to press on, not cease," from in- "not" + cessare "to cease, stop, be quiet," frequentative of cedere "to go, yield, withdraw."
