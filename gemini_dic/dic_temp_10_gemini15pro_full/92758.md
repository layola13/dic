ichthyophagist: /ɪkθiˈɒfədʒɪst/ | n. |食鱼者，鱼食者| A person or animal whose diet consists primarily or exclusively of fish. |  例句：The traditional diet of the islanders made them ichthyophagists. 岛民的传统饮食使他们成为食鱼者。

例句：The otter is an example of an ichthyophagist. 水獭是食鱼动物的一个例子。| 近义词：piscivore | 反义词: herbivore, carnivore (if not primarily fish-eating) | 词性: noun
