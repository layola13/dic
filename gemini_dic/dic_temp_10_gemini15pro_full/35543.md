coalesce:/ˌkoʊəˈlɛs/| v. |合并，联合，结合|to come together to form one mass or whole|The puddles on the sidewalk coalesced into a small stream. 人行道上的水坑汇聚成一条小溪。|synonyms: merge, unite, combine, blend, fuse|antonyms: separate, divide, split, disperse|verb

coalescence:/ˌkoʊəˈlɛsəns/| n. |合并，联合，结合|the act or process of coming together to form one mass or whole|The coalescence of the two companies created a powerful new corporation. 两家公司的合并创造了一个强大的新公司。|synonyms: merger, union, combination, fusion|antonyms: separation, division, splitting, dispersal|noun
