incorrigible:/ɪnˈkɒrɪdʒəbl/ /ɪnˈkɔːrɪdʒəbl/
adjective
无可救药的； incorrigible
not able to be corrected, improved, or reformed
He's an incorrigible flirt. 他就是个无可救药的花花公子。
synonyms: incurable, inveterate, irredeemable, hopeless
antonyms: corrigible, reformable, redeemable
词性: adjective
