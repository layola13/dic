ambaginous: /ˌæmbəˈɡaɪnəs/
adjective
迂回的，绕圈子的
circuitous; roundabout; indirect. Characterized by beating around the bush; not forthright.
Example: Her ambaginous explanation left me more confused than before. 她迂回的解释让我更加困惑了。

Example:  The politician gave an ambaginous answer to the reporter's question, avoiding a direct commitment.  这位政治家对记者的提问给出了一个绕圈子的答案，避免了直接承诺。

Synonym: circuitous, roundabout, indirect, evasive, meandering
Antonym: direct, straightforward, forthright, explicit, concise
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ambāginōsus, from ambāgēs (“circuitous route, detour”), from ambi- (“around”) + agō (“I drive, lead”).
