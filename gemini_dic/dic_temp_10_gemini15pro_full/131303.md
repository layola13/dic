noncertainty:/ˌnɒnsɜːrtnti/ | n. |不确定性，非确定性 |The state of not being certain; lack of certainty. |The noncertainty of the future makes planning difficult. 未来的不确定性使规划变得困难。| 近义词: uncertainty, doubt, ambiguity, vagueness | 反义词: certainty, assurance, conviction | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "certainty" (from Old French "certeinte," ultimately from Latin "certus" meaning "settled, fixed, sure").
