demister:/diːˈmɪstər/ | n. |除雾器，去雾器|A device used to remove droplets of liquid from a stream of gas or vapor.|The demister in the car windscreen helps to clear the condensation. 汽车挡风玻璃上的除雾器有助于清除冷凝水。|Synonyms: dehumidifier, defogger|Antonyms: humidifier|noun

demist:/diːˈmɪst/ | v. |除雾，去除雾气|To remove mist or condensation from something.|I need to demist the car windows before I can drive. 我需要先清除车窗上的雾气才能开车。|Synonyms: defog, clear|Antonyms: fog, mist|verb
