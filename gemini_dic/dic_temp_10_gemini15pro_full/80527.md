goodgeon:/ˈɡʊdʒən/| n.|容易上当的人；傻瓜|A person who is easily tricked or cheated; a gullible person.|He was a goodgeon and lost all his money in the scam. 他很容易上当受骗，在骗局中损失了所有钱。|近义词：sucker, dupe, chump, fool, naive person|反义词: shrewd person, skeptic|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *godgeon, perhaps from Old French goujon, diminutive of gojon "gudgeon (fish)," from Vulgar Latin *gobio, from Latin gobius. The figurative sense of "easily deceived person" arose from the fact that gudgeon fish are easily caught.
