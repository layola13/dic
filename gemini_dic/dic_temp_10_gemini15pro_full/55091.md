disrespectful:/ˌdɪsrɪˈspektfəl/
adjective
不尊重的，失礼的
Showing a lack of respect or courtesy.
His disrespectful behavior towards his elders was unacceptable. 他对长辈的不尊重行为是不可接受的。
The student's disrespectful comments angered the teacher.  这个学生无礼的评论激怒了老师。

近义词:rude, impolite, impertinent, insolent, discourteous
反义词:respectful, polite, courteous, reverent

词性:adjective

词源:From dis- + respectful, from respect + -ful.  "Dis-" prefix indicates negation or reversal.
