overaptly:/ˌoʊvərˈæptli/| adv.| 过分贴切地，过分恰当地| Excessively aptly; too suitably or fittingly.  It describes something that is so fitting or appropriate that it seems excessive or exaggerated. It often carries a connotation of being overly clever or contrived. | His words, though overaptly chosen, seemed to miss the mark. 他的话虽然选得过分贴切，但似乎没有说到点子上。|  The metaphor, while overaptly applied, felt somewhat forced.  这个比喻虽然运用得过分贴切，但感觉有点牵强。| 近义词： excessively aptly, too suitably, overly fittingly | 反义词： inappropriately, unsuitably, ineptly |词性: 副词
