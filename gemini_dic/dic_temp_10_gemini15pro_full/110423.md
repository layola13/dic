likeableness:/ˈlaɪkəbəlnɪs/
n.
可爱；讨人喜欢；亲和力
The quality of being pleasant and easy to like.
Her likeableness made her popular among her colleagues. 她的亲和力使她在同事中很受欢迎。
likeability, charm, affability
unpleasantness, obnoxiousness, repulsiveness
