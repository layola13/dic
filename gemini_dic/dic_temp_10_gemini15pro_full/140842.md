on-the-bench:/ɒn ðə bɛntʃ/ | adj. | 替补的，未上场的；待业的 | Not actively participating, especially in sports or work; sidelined.  Reserved for future use. | The quarterback was on the bench for the entire second half due to an injury.  由于受伤，四分卫整个下半场都坐在替补席上。| The company kept him on the bench, waiting for a suitable project. 公司让他待业，等待合适的项目。 | synonyms: sidelined, benched, inactive, unemployed, reserve | antonyms: active, playing, working, employed, starting | adjective


on-the-bench:/ɒn ðə bɛntʃ/ | n. | 替补席；冷板凳 | The place where substitute players sit during a game; a situation of being inactive or unemployed. |  He spent most of the season on the bench. 他大部分赛季都坐在替补席上。| Being on the bench for so long made him question his career choice.  长期坐冷板凳让他开始质疑自己的职业选择。 | synonyms: sidelines, reserves | antonyms: field, court, active duty, employment | noun
