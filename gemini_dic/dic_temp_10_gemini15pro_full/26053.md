bushier:/ˈbʊʃiər/ | adj. | 更浓密的，更茂盛的 | More bushy; having more bushes.  |  The plant is bushier this year than last. 今年这株植物比去年茂盛。 | denser, thicker, lusher | sparser, thinner, scanter | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From bushy + -er (comparative suffix).  Bushy itself comes from bush + -y (adjective-forming suffix).  Ultimately from Middle English busch, from Old Norse buskr.
