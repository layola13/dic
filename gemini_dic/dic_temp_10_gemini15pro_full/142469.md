osculatrix:/ˈɒskjʊlətrɪks/ | n. |密切圆,密切曲线;密切平面|A curve or surface that has a higher order of contact with another curve or surface at a given point than any other curve or surface of the same kind.|The osculatrix to the curve at P provides a good approximation of the curve near P.| 近义词: tangent, approximating curve | 反义词: None | noun

osculating:/ˈɒskjʊleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |密切的,吻合的|Having a high degree of contact, as between curves or surfaces.|The osculating circle shares the same tangent and curvature as the curve at that point.|近义词: tangent, contacting | 反义词: diverging | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *osculatrix*, feminine form of *osculator*, from *osculatus*, past participle of *osculari* "to kiss," from *osculum* "little mouth, kiss," diminutive of *os* "mouth."
