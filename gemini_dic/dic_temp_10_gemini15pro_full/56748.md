doorstepper:/ˈdɔːrˌstɛpər/| n.|挨家挨户上门推销或游说的人；上门采访的记者|A person who goes from door to door, typically to sell something, solicit votes or donations, or conduct interviews.|The politician employed several doorsteppers to canvass the neighborhood before the election.  这位政治家在选举前雇佣了几名挨家挨户上门游说的人在社区进行宣传。

|近义词：canvasser, solicitor, peddler|反义词:|词性:noun

doorstepper:/ˈdɔːrˌstɛpər/| n.|上门采访|A journalist who conducts interviews at people's homes, often unexpectedly.|The doorstepper persisted in questioning the celebrity despite being asked to leave.  尽管被要求离开，这名上门采访的记者仍然坚持向这位名人提问。


|近义词：reporter, journalist|反义词:|词性:noun
