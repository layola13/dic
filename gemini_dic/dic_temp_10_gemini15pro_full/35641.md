coauthoring:/koʊˈɔːθərɪŋ/| n. |共同创作，合著|The act of writing something together with one or more other people.|Example: Coauthoring a book can be a rewarding experience.  合著一本书可以是一次有益的经历。|Example:  The coauthoring of the scientific paper took several months. 这篇科学论文的合著花了几个月的时间。|Synonyms: joint authorship, collaboration, co-writing|Antonyms: sole authorship, independent writing|noun

co-author:/koʊˈɔːθər/| n. |合著者|A person who writes something together with one or more other people.|Example: She and her brother were co-authors of the play. 她和她哥哥是这出戏的合著者。|Example: He's listed as a co-author on the paper. 他被列为论文的合著者。|Synonyms: collaborator, joint author|Antonyms: sole author|noun

co-author:/koʊˈɔːθər/| v. |共同创作，合著|To write something together with one or more other people.|Example: They co-authored a research paper on climate change. 他们共同撰写了一篇关于气候变化的研究论文。|Example:  She co-authored the screenplay with her husband. 她和她丈夫共同创作了剧本。|Synonyms: collaborate, co-write|Antonyms: write alone|verb
