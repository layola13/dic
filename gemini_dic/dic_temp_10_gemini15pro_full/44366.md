cross-contaminate:/ˌkrɒs kənˈtæmɪneɪt/ /ˌkrɑːs kənˈtæmɪneɪt/| v. |交叉污染|To unintentionally transfer bacteria or other harmful substances from one surface or object to another, especially food.|例句：Raw chicken can easily cross-contaminate other foods if you're not careful. 如果你不小心，生鸡肉很容易交叉污染其他食物。|例句：Be sure to wash your hands thoroughly to avoid cross-contaminating the ingredients. 一定要彻底洗手，以免交叉污染食材。|近义词：contaminate, pollute, infect|反义词:sterilize, sanitize|词性:动词

cross-contamination:/ˌkrɒs kənˌtæmɪˈneɪʃən/ /ˌkrɑːs kənˌtæmɪˈneɪʃən/| n. |交叉污染|The process by which bacteria or other microorganisms are unintentionally transferred from one substance or object to another, with harmful effect.|例句：Cross-contamination is a serious issue in food preparation. 交叉污染是食品制备中的一个严重问题。|例句：The report highlighted the risks of cross-contamination in the hospital. 该报告强调了医院交叉污染的风险。|近义词：contamination, pollution, infection|反义词:sterilization, sanitization|词性:名词
