ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *clingen, from Old English clingan (“to adhere, stick, congeal, wither, shrink”), from Proto-Germanic *klinganą (“to stick, adhere”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)gley- (“to stick, smear”). Cognate with Dutch klingen (“to sound, ring, clink”), German klingen (“to sound, ring, clink”).
