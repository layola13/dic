claims:/kleɪmz/ | n. | 索赔，声明，要求 | An assertion of a right or demand, typically one made without proof. | He made several claims about his qualifications.  他声称自己具备多项资格。 | demands, assertions, requests | denials, concessions | noun

claims:/kleɪmz/ | v. |  声称，要求，认领 | State or assert that something is the case, typically without providing evidence or proof. |  She claims to have seen the accident. 她声称目睹了那场事故。| assert, maintain, declare | deny, refute | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *claimer (modern French clamer, "to cry out, proclaim, call"), from Latin clāmāre ("to shout, cry out").
