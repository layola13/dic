dissimuler:/dɪˈsɪmjʊleɪtər/ |n.| 掩饰者，伪装者 |A dissimuler is someone who hides their true feelings or intentions. | He was a master dissimuler, hiding his true motives behind a facade of charm. 他是个伪装高手，用魅力的外表掩盖了他真正的动机。|近义词：dissembler, hypocrite, pretender|反义词：truth-teller, honest person|词性：名词

dissimuler:/dɪˈsɪmjʊleɪt/ |v.| 掩饰，掩盖，假装 |To dissimulate is to conceal one's true feelings or intentions. |She dissimulated her anxiety with a forced smile. 她强颜欢笑以掩饰内心的焦虑。|近义词：conceal, disguise, mask, feign|反义词：reveal, disclose, expose|词性：动词

词源: From Old French dissimuler, from Latin dissimulare (“to disguise, conceal, dissemble”), from dis- (“not, un-”) + simulare (“to simulate, copy, resemble”).
