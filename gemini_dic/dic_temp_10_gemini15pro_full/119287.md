micro-electrophoretic: /ˌmaɪkroʊɪˌlɛktroʊfəˈrɛtɪk/ | adj. | 微电泳的 | Relating to or involving microelectrophoresis, a technique used to separate and analyze small particles based on their surface charge and size in an electric field. | Micro-electrophoretic analysis was used to determine the zeta potential of the nanoparticles.  微电泳分析用于测定纳米粒子的zeta电位。 | Near synonyms: capillary electrophoretic | Antonym: N/A | adjective

micro-electrophoresis: /ˌmaɪkroʊɪˌlɛktroʊfəˈriːsɪs/ | n. | 微电泳 | The technique of separating and analyzing small particles based on their surface charge and size in an electric field. | Micro-electrophoresis is a powerful tool for studying the surface properties of colloids. 微电泳是研究胶体表面性质的有力工具。 | Near synonyms: capillary electrophoresis | Antonym: N/A | noun
