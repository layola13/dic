akin:/əˈkɪn/| adj.|相似的，类似的；同类的，同族的|Similar in nature or character; related by blood.|例句：Their interests are akin to ours. 他们的兴趣与我们的相似。|例句：The two languages are closely akin. 这两种语言非常相似。|近义词：similar, analogous, related, kindred|反义词：dissimilar, different, unrelated|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *akin*, from Old English *gecynde* (“natural, native, innate, proper, right, due”), from Proto-Germanic *gakundiz* (“suitable, belonging to, fitting, related”), from *kunją* (“family, kin”). Cognate with Dutch *gekund* (“able, skilled”), German *kund* (“known, familiar”), Gothic *��������* (*kunds*). More at kind.
