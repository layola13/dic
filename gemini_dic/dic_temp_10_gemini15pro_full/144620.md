outwind:/ˌaʊtˈwɪnd/ | v. | 展开；放松；解开；吐露（心事） | To unwind something that is wound, such as a rope or a bandage; to relax and become less tense; to disclose or reveal something gradually, especially a secret or a story. |  He slowly outwound the fishing line. 他慢慢地放出钓鱼线。 | 近义词：unwind, unravel, unroll, disclose, reveal | 反义词：wind, tighten, conceal | 词性: 动词
