ingestive:/ɪnˈdʒestɪv/
adjective
摄取的；食用的
Relating to ingestion, the process of taking food or other substances into the body.
The digestive system plays a crucial role in the ingestive process. 消化系统在摄取过程中起着至关重要的作用。
Birds use their beaks for ingestive purposes. 鸟类用喙来摄取食物。
synonyms: oral, consummatory, dietary
antonyms: excretive, ejective
词性: adjective
