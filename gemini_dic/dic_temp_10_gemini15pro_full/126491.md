myUS:/maɪˈjuːˈɛs/ | n. | 美国转运公司 |  A private company that provides package forwarding services, primarily for international shoppers who purchase goods from US retailers that don't ship internationally.  | Example: I used myUS to ship my new shoes from America to Australia. 我用myUS将我的新鞋从美国运到澳大利亚。 | Example: MyUS offers various shipping options depending on your budget and delivery speed. myUS根据您的预算和交货速度提供各种运输选项。|  Synonym: package forwarder, shipping agent | Antonym: N/A | noun
