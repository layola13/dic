obituarists:/əˈbɪtʃuːərɪsts/
n.
讣告作者，撰写讣告的人
A person who writes obituaries, especially as a profession.
The newspaper’s obituarists were kept busy during the flu epidemic.  这家报纸的讣告作者在流感流行期间一直很忙。
synonyms: obituary writer, biographer, chronicler
antonyms:  None (No direct antonym for a profession)
noun
