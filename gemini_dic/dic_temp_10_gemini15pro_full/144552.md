outwit:/aʊtˈwɪt/| vt.| 智胜，瞒骗 | To get the better of by superior cleverness. | He outwitted his opponents by a clever strategy. 他以巧妙的策略智胜了对手。|  She outwitted the police and escaped. 她骗过了警察逃跑了。 | 近义词：outmaneuver, outsmart, trick, deceive | 反义词：be outwitted, be fooled | 词性: 动词

词源:

From out- + wit.  "Out-" prefix indicates exceeding or surpassing, and "wit" refers to intelligence or cleverness.  Therefore, "outwit" means to surpass someone in wit or cleverness.
