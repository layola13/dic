mistranslation:/ˌmɪstrænsˈleɪʃən/| n. |误译，翻译错误|An act or instance of translating incorrectly; an inaccurate translation.  The mistranslation caused a diplomatic incident.|例句：The mistranslation of the document led to a serious misunderstanding.  这份文件的误译导致了严重的误解。

例句：There were several mistranslations in the subtitles of the film.  这部电影的字幕中有几处翻译错误。|近义词：misinterpretation, wrong translation, inaccurate translation|反义词：accurate translation, correct translation|词性:noun

词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning "wrongly") + "translation" (from Latin "translatio," meaning "a carrying across").
