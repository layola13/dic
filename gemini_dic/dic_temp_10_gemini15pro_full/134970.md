nonnattiest: /nɒnˈnætɪɪst/ | adj. | 最不成熟的 |  Superlative form of nonnatus (not yet born; immature). Refers to someone or something being the least mature or developed among a group. |  He was by far the nonnattiest of the group, prone to childish tantrums. 他是小组里最不成熟的人，动不动就耍孩子脾气。|  immature, childish, juvenile | mature, sophisticated, adult | adjective
