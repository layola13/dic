dying-out:/ˈdaɪɪŋ aʊt/
adjective
逐渐消失的；灭绝的
becoming extinct; disappearing
The dying-out of traditional crafts is a great loss.  传统工艺的逐渐消失是一个巨大的损失。
Many languages are dying-out due to lack of speakers. 由于缺乏使用者，许多语言正在逐渐消失。
synonyms: disappearing, vanishing, fading, declining, dwindling
antonyms: thriving, flourishing, growing, expanding
adjective


dying-out:/ˈdaɪɪŋ aʊt/
noun
灭绝；消失
the process of becoming extinct
The dying-out of the dinosaurs occurred millions of years ago. 恐龙的灭绝发生在数百万年前。
The museum exhibition highlighted the dying-out of certain species. 博物馆展览突出了某些物种的灭绝。
synonyms: extinction, disappearance, demise, decline
antonyms: survival, growth, resurgence
noun
