nonfissile:/ˌnɒnˈfɪsaɪl/ /ˌnɑːnˈfɪsəl/
adjective
非裂变的
Not capable of undergoing fission (splitting into smaller parts) when bombarded with neutrons.
Depleted uranium, unlike enriched uranium, is nonfissile and therefore cannot sustain a chain reaction.  贫铀与浓缩铀不同，它是非裂变的，因此不能维持链式反应。

The reactor uses nonfissile isotopes as a coolant.  该反应堆使用非裂变同位素作为冷却剂。
synonym:nonfissionable
antonym:fissile
词性:adjective
