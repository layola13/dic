mistress:/ˈmɪstrəs/| n.|女主人，情妇，女教师，(尤指旧时的)女能手|A woman who is in a position of authority or control, especially in a household or over servants.  A woman other than his wife with whom a man has a continuing sexual relationship. A female teacher, especially in the past. A woman who is skilled in something. |例句：She is the mistress of the house. 她是这房子的女主人。

例句：He left his wife for his mistress. 他为了他的情妇离开了他的妻子。

|近义词：lover, paramour, concubine, teacher, instructress, expert, virtuoso|反义词:servant, employee, student, pupil|词性:noun
