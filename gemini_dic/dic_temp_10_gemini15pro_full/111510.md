loan:/loʊn/ (US) /ləʊn/ (UK)| n. vt.|贷款，借出|Something lent or borrowed, especially a sum of money that is expected to be paid back with interest.  A thing that is borrowed, especially a sum of money.|例句：I took out a loan to buy a car. 我贷款买了一辆车。|近义词：advance, credit, mortgage|反义词:repayment, deposit|n.
loan:/loʊn/ (US) /ləʊn/ (UK)| vt.|借出|To lend something.|例句：Can you loan me five dollars? 你能借我五美元吗？|近义词：lend, advance|反义词:borrow, receive|v.
