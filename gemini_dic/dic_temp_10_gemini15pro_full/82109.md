groggier:/ˈɡrɑːɡiər/ (US), /ˈɡrɒɡiə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更昏沉的，更迷糊的|Comparative form of groggy: more groggy.  Means feeling weak and unsteady, especially from illness, intoxication, or lack of sleep.  It implies a worsening of the groggy state.|例句：I felt even groggier after taking the cold medicine.  服用了感冒药后，我感觉更昏沉了。

例句：He woke up feeling groggier than he did the night before. 他醒来时感觉比前一天晚上更昏沉了。|近义词：drowsier, sleepier, woozier, fuzzier|反义词：alert, awake, clearheaded, energetic|词性:形容词
