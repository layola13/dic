flatulence:/ˈflætʃʊləns/ (US) /ˈflætjʊləns/ (UK)| n.|胀气，肠胃气胀|The act of passing intestinal gas.  It is a normal bodily function, although excessive flatulence can be a sign of a digestive issue.|He suffered from embarrassing flatulence after eating beans.|近义词: gas, wind, bloating|反义词: None|Noun

flatulent:/ˈflætʃʊlənt/ (US) /ˈflætjʊlənt/ (UK)| adj.|胀气的，产生气体的|Affected with or characterized by flatulence; causing gas or generating gas in the digestive system.|Eating too many beans can make you feel flatulent.|近义词: gassy, windy|反义词: None|Adjective
