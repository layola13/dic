dubbers:/ˈdʌbərz/
n.
配音演员；译制者
People who provide dubbed voices for films or other media.
The dubbers did an excellent job of matching the lip movements to the translated dialogue. 配音演员出色地完成了将唇部动作与翻译后的对话匹配的工作。

dubbers:/ˈdʌbərz/
n.
复制者；翻录者
People who make copies of recordings.
The dubbers were busy making copies of the latest album. 复制者忙着复制最新的专辑。


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "dub," meaning to give (a film, etc.) a new soundtrack in a different language, or to make a copy of a recording. "Dub" itself is likely shortened from "double," referring to the process of creating a second audio track.


SYNONYMS->
voice actors, voice artists (for film/media dubbing)
copiers, duplicators (for making copies)


ANTONYMS->
(No direct antonyms for the act of dubbing. One could consider "original artists/performers" as an antonym in the context of voice acting, or "originals" as opposed to copies.)
