bordereau:/ˌbɔːrdəˈroʊ/
n.
清单，明细表，报表
A detailed statement of account, especially one listing documents or goods.
The bordereau listed all the items shipped.  该报表列出了所有运送的物品。

The insurance company required a bordereau of all the damaged items. 保险公司要求提供一份所有受损物品的清单。
synonym: list, inventory, schedule, register, summary
antonym: N/A (Abstract noun, no direct opposite)
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *bordereau*, diminutive of *bord*, meaning "edge" or "margin," referring to the marginal notes or summaries often found on such documents.  源自法语 *bordereau*，是 *bord* 的缩小词，意思是“边缘”或“页边空白”，指的是此类文件上经常出现的页边注释或摘要。
