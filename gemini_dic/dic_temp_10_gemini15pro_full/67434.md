extinguished: /ɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃt/
adjective
熄灭的，消灭的
put out (a fire or light);quenched, put an end to
The fire was quickly extinguished.  火很快就被扑灭了。
近义词: quenched, smothered, doused, suppressed
反义词: ignited, kindled, lit

extinguish: /ɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃ/
verb
熄灭，扑灭，压制，使消失
to put out (a fire or light); to put an end to
The firefighters extinguished the blaze. 消防队员扑灭了大火。
近义词: quench, smother, douse, suppress
反义词: ignite, kindle, light

词性:  verb, adjective
