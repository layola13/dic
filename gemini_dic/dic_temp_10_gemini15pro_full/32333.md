childbearing:/ˈtʃaɪldˌberɪŋ/| n. adj.|生育，怀孕期|The act of bearing children.  Relating to the bearing of children.|例句：She decided to postpone childbearing until she finished her education. 她决定推迟生育直到完成学业。|近义词：reproduction, procreation, gestation, pregnancy|反义词:infertility, barrenness|词性:noun, adjective

childbearing:/ˈtʃaɪldˌberɪŋ/| adj.|生育的，适合生育的|Relating to the bearing of children.|例句：The survey focused on the health of women of childbearing age. 这项调查的重点是育龄妇女的健康。|近义词：reproductive, fertile|反义词:infertile, barren|词性:adjective

词源:  From 'child' + 'bearing' (present participle of 'bear' - to carry, give birth to).  'Bear' in this sense comes from Old English beran, of Germanic origin.
