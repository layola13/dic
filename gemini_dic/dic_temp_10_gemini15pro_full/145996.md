overdomesticated:/ˌoʊvərdoʊˈmɛstɪkeɪtɪd/
adjective
过度驯化的
Having been domesticated to an excessive degree, often resulting in undesirable traits or loss of original characteristics.
例句：The overdomesticated dog breeds are prone to certain health problems.  这些过度驯化的犬种容易出现某些健康问题。

例句：Some researchers believe that overdomesticated crops are less resilient to pests and diseases. 一些研究人员认为，过度驯化的作物对病虫害的抵抗力较弱。
近义词：overbred, excessively domesticated
反义词：wild, feral, undomesticated
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "domesticated" (from Latin "domesticus," meaning "belonging to the house").
