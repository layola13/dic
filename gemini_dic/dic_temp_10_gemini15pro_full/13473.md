atrophic:/əˈtrɒfɪk/ /əˈtroʊfɪk/| adj.|萎缩的，发育不全的|Relating to or characterized by atrophy; wasted away or shrunken.  A decrease in size of a normally developed organ or tissue.|The doctor noticed atrophic changes in the patient's muscles. 医生注意到病人肌肉出现了萎缩性变化。|The patient's leg muscles became atrophic after being in a cast for several months.  病人的腿部肌肉在打了几月的石膏后萎缩了。|近义词：shrunken, wasted, withered, degenerate|反义词：developed, healthy, growing, enlarged|词性: 形容词

Etymology: From French *atrophique*  and directly from Modern Latin *atrophicus*, from Greek *atrophiā*  "a wasting away" + *-ikos*  "-ic".
