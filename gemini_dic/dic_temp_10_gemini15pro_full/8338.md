anti-piracy:/ˌæntiˈpaɪrəsi/| n. |反盗版|Actions taken to prevent the unauthorized copying or distribution of copyrighted material.  |The software company invested heavily in anti-piracy measures. 这家软件公司在反盗版措施上投入了巨资。

The music industry is stepping up its anti-piracy campaign.  音乐产业正在加强其反盗版运动。|近义词：copyright protection, anti-counterfeiting|反义词：piracy, copyright infringement|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + piracy.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to," derived from Greek. "Piracy" comes from the Latin "pirata," meaning "pirate," ultimately from Greek "peirates," meaning "robber," "attacker."
