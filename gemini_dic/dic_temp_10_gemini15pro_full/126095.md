mutandis:/mjuːˈtændɪs/| adv.|相应地；比照办理；|with the necessary changes having been made;  Used in Latin phrases, especially *mutatis mutandis*, to indicate that a statement, etc., applies in a different situation with the necessary changes having been made.|The agreement can be applied to other companies *mutatis mutandis*.  该协议可以比照适用于其他公司。|近义词：correspondingly, similarly, accordingly|反义词:  N/A (as it is an adverb of qualification, it doesn't have a direct opposite.)|词性: adverb
