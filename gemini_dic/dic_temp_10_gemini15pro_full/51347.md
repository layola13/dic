detector:/dɪˈtɛktər/| n.|探测器，检测器|A device or instrument designed to detect the presence of something, such as smoke, gas, metal, or radiation.|The smoke detector went off when I burned the toast. 烤面包片烤糊了，烟雾探测器响了。|synonyms: sensor, monitor, scanner|antonyms: concealer, hider|noun
