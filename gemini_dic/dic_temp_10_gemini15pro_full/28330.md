caramel:/ˈkærəməl, ˈkɑːrməl/| n. adj.|焦糖，焦糖色的|A light brown confection made by heating sugar until it changes color and flavor.  Also, the color of this confection, a light golden brown.|例句：I love the caramel flavor in this latte. 我喜欢这杯拿铁里的焦糖味。

例句：She has caramel-colored hair. 她有一头焦糖色的头发。|近义词：butterscotch, toffee|反义词: N/A|词性: noun, adjective

Etymology: From Spanish *caramelo*, earlier *caramella*, probably from Late Latin *cannamella*, "sugar cane", from Latin *canna*, "reed, cane" + *mella*, "honey".
