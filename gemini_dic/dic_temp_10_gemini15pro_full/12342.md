asham:/əˈʃæm/| n.|羞愧，惭愧，内疚|A feeling of guilt or embarrassment about something you have done.| I felt a deep sense of asham after I lied to my friend. 我对我的朋友撒谎之后感到深深的羞愧。|近义词：guilt, shame, remorse|反义词：pride, honor|词性:名词


Etymology:
From Old English *æscāmian "to feel ashamed," from Proto-Germanic *skama "shame."  Related to Old Norse skamma "to disgrace," and German beschämen "to shame."  The noun form likely developed from the verb.
