allophonic:/ˌæləˈfɒnɪk/ (US) /ˌæləˈfɑːnɪk/ (UK)| adj.| 音位的，音位变体的|Relating to or being an allophone.  An allophone is one of a set of speech sounds that represent a single phoneme, such as the aspirated k in kit and the unaspirated k in skit, which are allophones of the phoneme /k/.|The aspirated and unaspirated /p/ are allophonic in English. 送气和不送气的/p/在英语中是音位变体。|Near synonyms: phonetic, phonological|Antonym: phonemic|adjective
