cross-border:/ˌkrɒsˈbɔːdə(r)/ /ˌkrɔːsˈbɔːrdər/ | adj.|跨境的；跨国界的|involving or relating to two or more countries|例句：Cross-border trade has increased significantly in recent years. 近年来，跨境贸易显著增长。|例句：The company specializes in cross-border mergers and acquisitions.  这家公司专门从事跨境并购。|近义词：international, transnational, global|反义词：domestic, national|词性:形容词

cross-border:/ˌkrɒsˈbɔːdə(r)/ /ˌkrɔːsˈbɔːrdər/ | n.|边界；国界|a line separating two countries, administrative divisions, or other areas|例句：Goods are transported across the cross-border. 货物被运过边境。 (这里cross-border更像指边境本身，用法比较少见)|例句：Regulations at the cross-border are strict. 边境的管理很严格。(这里cross-border更像指边境本身，用法比较少见)|近义词：border, frontier, boundary|反义词：N/A|词性:名词 (用法较少)
