neatness:/ˈniːtnəs/| n.|整洁，干净，利落|The neatness of her handwriting impressed me. 她的字迹工整给我留下了深刻的印象。|例句：She always keeps her house in a state of perfect neatness. 她总是把她的房子保持得非常整洁。

例句：He admired the neatness and order of the garden. 他欣赏花园的整洁有序。

近义词：tidiness, orderliness, cleanliness|反义词:messiness, disorder, untidiness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *netnesse, from Old English *nēatnes, from nēat (“cattle, beast; useful animal; property”) +‎ -ness.  Originally meant "the quality of being well-fed," then "the quality of being well-kept," and finally "tidiness, orderliness."
