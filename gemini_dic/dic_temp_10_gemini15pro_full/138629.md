nucleolated:/njuːˈkliːəˌleɪtɪd/
adj.
有核仁的；核仁明显的
Having a nucleolus or nucleoli, especially one that is prominent.
The nucleolated cells were clearly visible under the microscope. 在显微镜下，有核仁的细胞清晰可见。
Near synonyms: nucleolar
Antonyms: anucleolated, anucleolar
Part of speech: adjective
