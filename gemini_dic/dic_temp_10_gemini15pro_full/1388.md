accusation:/ˌækjuˈzeɪʃn/| n. |指控，控告，谴责|a statement saying that someone has done something wrong|The accusation of theft was deeply upsetting to him.  他对盗窃的指控深感不安。

He denied the accusations against him. 他否认了对他的指控。|近义词：charge, allegation, claim, indictment|反义词：defense, exoneration|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *accusatio(n-), from Latin accusare "to accuse," from ad- "to" + causa "cause, reason."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The accusation against him is serious. = 对他的指控很严重。
His accusation lacks evidence. = 他的指控缺乏证据。

一般过去时:
The accusation shocked the community. = 这项指控震惊了社区。
She faced an accusation of fraud. = 她面临欺诈指控。

现在完成时:
The accusation has damaged his reputation. = 这项指控损害了他的名誉。
He has denied the accusation repeatedly. = 他多次否认了这一指控。


过去完成时:
The accusation had been made before the trial began. = 指控在审判开始前就提出了。
They had investigated the accusation thoroughly. = 他们彻底调查了这项指控。

将来时:
The accusation will be investigated. = 该指控将被调查。
He will face serious consequences if the accusation is proven true. = 如果指控被证实，他将面临严重后果。


被动语态:
He was hurt by the false accusation. = 他被虚假的指控伤害了。
The accusation is being investigated by the police. = 警方正在调查这起指控。


疑问句:
What is the accusation? = 指控是什么？
Is there any truth to the accusation? = 指控属实吗？


否定句:
The accusation is not true. = 指控不属实。
He did not make the accusation lightly. = 他不是轻易提出指控的。

PHRASE->
