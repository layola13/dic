analogic:/ˌænəˈlɒdʒɪk/ | adj. | 相似的，类推的 | Based on analogy; expressing or implying analogy.  | This analogic reasoning helps us understand complex systems. 这种类比推理帮助我们理解复杂系统。 |  The analogic computer models the behavior of a real system. 这台模拟计算机模拟了真实系统的行为。 | 近义词：analogous, comparable, similar | 反义词：dissimilar, different | 词性: adjective

analogical:/ˌænəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 相似的，类推的 |  Relating to or based on analogy. | His argument is analogical, comparing the human brain to a computer. 他的论点是类比的，将人脑比作计算机。 | The teacher used an analogical example to explain the concept. 老师用了一个类比的例子来解释这个概念。 | 近义词：analogous, comparable, similar | 反义词：dissimilar, different | 词性: adjective


词源：From Late Latin *analogicus, from Greek analogikos, from analogia "proportion," from ana- "upon, according to" + logos "ratio."
