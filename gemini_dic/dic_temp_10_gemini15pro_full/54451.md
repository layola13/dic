disinherited:/ˌdɪsɪnˈherɪtɪd/
adjective
被剥夺继承权的
Deprived of the right to inherit property or a title, especially by being specifically excluded from a will.
例句：He felt bitter and disinherited after his father's will was read. 他在父亲的遗嘱宣读后感到痛苦和被剥夺了继承权。

例句：The disinherited son vowed to challenge the will in court.  被剥夺继承权的儿子发誓要在法庭上挑战遗嘱。

近义词：disowned, excluded
反义词：inherited, entitled

词性: adjective

词源：
From dis- + inherit + -ed.  "Dis-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of."  "Inherit" comes from Old French "enheriter,"  ultimately from Latin "heres," meaning "heir."
