illuxurious:/ˌɪlʌɡˈʒʊəriəs/ | adj. | 不奢华的，简朴的 | Not luxurious; lacking luxury or abundance; plain. |  The illuxurious cabin had only the bare necessities.  这间简朴的小屋只有最基本的必需品。

| The monk lived an illuxurious life, devoted to prayer and simple living.  这位僧侣过着简朴的生活，致力于祈祷和简朴的生活。 | Near synonyms: simple, plain, modest, austere, spartan | Antonyms: luxurious, opulent, lavish, extravagant | Word type: Adjective
