antisag:/ˈæntiˌsæɡ/
adjective
防下垂的，抗下垂的
Preventing or counteracting sagging.
This bra features antisag technology to provide extra support. 这款文胸采用防下垂技术，提供额外支撑。
近义词: supportive, firming
反义词: sagging, drooping
词性: adjective

antisag:/ˈæntiˌsæɡ/
noun
防下垂装置，抗下垂措施
A device or measure that prevents or counteracts sagging.
The manufacturer developed a new antisag for airplane wings. 制造商开发了一种新的飞机机翼防下垂装置。
近义词: support, reinforcement
反义词: weakness, droop
词性: noun

词源:  "anti-" (against) + "sag" (to droop or sink)
