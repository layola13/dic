footplate:/ˈfʊtpleɪt/| n. |踏板，脚踏板|A plate or platform on which to place the foot, especially: a. The flat part of a stirrup. b. The treadle of a sewing machine or other mechanical device. c. (British) The platform at the front or rear of a steam locomotive, on which the crew can stand.|The driver stood on the footplate of the engine.  司机站在机车的踏板上。|Synonyms: treadle, pedal, platform|Antonyms: N/A|noun

footplates:/ˈfʊtpleɪts/| n. |踏板，脚踏板（复数）|Plural of footplate.|The footplates of the old sewing machine were worn smooth.  老式缝纫机的踏板磨得很光滑。|Synonyms: treadles, pedals, platforms|Antonyms: N/A|noun
