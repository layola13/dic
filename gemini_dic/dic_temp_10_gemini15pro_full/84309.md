half-wildness:/ˌhæf ˈwaɪldnəs/ | n. | 半野生状态; 半驯服状态 | The state of being partially wild or untamed; a mixture of wildness and domestication. |  The horses on the island lived in a state of half-wildness, shy of humans but not entirely feral. 岛上的马处于半野生状态，怕人但并不完全野蛮。 | Near synonyms: semi-wildness, partial wildness | Antonyms: domestication, tameness | noun

half-wildness:/ˌhæf ˈwaɪldnəs/ | adj. | 半野生的; 半驯服的 | Partially wild or untamed; exhibiting a mixture of wildness and domestication. | The half-wildness of the cat made it a fascinating pet. 这只猫的半野生状态使它成为一只迷人的宠物。 | Near synonyms: semi-wild, partially wild | Antonyms: domesticated, tame | adjective
