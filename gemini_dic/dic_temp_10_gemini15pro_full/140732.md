omnipotent:/ɒmˈnɪpətənt/ /ɑːmˈnɪpətənt/
adjective
无所不能的；全能的
Having unlimited power; able to do anything.
God is often described as omnipotent and omniscient. 上帝常被描述为无所不能、无所不知的。

The CEO acts like he's omnipotent, making decisions without consulting anyone.  这位CEO表现得像自己无所不能一样，不咨询任何人就做决定。

近义词: all-powerful, almighty, supreme
反义词: powerless, impotent, weak
词性: adjective
