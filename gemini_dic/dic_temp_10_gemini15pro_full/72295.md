flounder:/ˈflaʊndər/ (US), /ˈflaʊndə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|比目鱼, 挣扎, 错乱|A flatfish, typically bottom-dwelling, with both eyes on one side of the head.  To struggle clumsily or helplessly; to move with difficulty. To speak or act in a hesitant or confused manner.|The flounder flopped on the deck of the boat. 比目鱼在船的甲板上扑腾。|近义词：(n.) flatfish, sole, halibut (v.) struggle, thrash, stumble|反义词: (v.) succeed, thrive, prosper|词性: 名词, 动词
