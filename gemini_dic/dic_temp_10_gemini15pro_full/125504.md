multiregional:/ˌmʌltiˈriːdʒənəl/ | adj. | 多区域的，多地区的 |  Relating to or occurring in several regions.  | The multiregional hypothesis proposes that modern humans evolved in different regions of the world.  多区域假说认为现代人类在世界不同地区进化而来。

The company adopted a multiregional marketing strategy.  这家公司采取了多区域营销策略。| 近义词:  global, worldwide, international | 反义词: local, regional | 词性: adjective


Etymology->
"multi-" (from Latin "multus" meaning "much, many") + "regional" (from "region" ultimately from Latin "regio")
