lessoning:/ˈlɛsənɪŋ/
verb
减少; 减轻; 缓和
reducing; diminishing; mitigating
The doctor gave him medication for lessening the pain. 医生给他开了药来减轻疼痛。
mitigate, alleviate, decrease
increase, aggravate, worsen
词性: 动词

noun
减少; 减轻; 缓和 (较少用作名词)
