inconsonancy:/ɪnkənˈsɑnənsi/ | n. | 不协调，不和谐，不一致 |lack of harmony or agreement; dissonance.  The inconsonancy between her words and actions made it hard to trust her. 她言行不一致，让人难以信任她。| 近义词：discord, disharmony, disagreement, incongruity, inconsistency | 反义词：harmony, consonance, agreement, concord, consistency | 词性: 名词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin inconsonantia, from in- ‘not’ + consonantia (see consonance).
