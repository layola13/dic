mooncake:/ˈmuːnkeɪk/| n.|月饼|A rich, round pastry traditionally eaten in China and other Asian countries during the Mid-Autumn Festival. It typically contains a sweet filling, such as lotus seed paste or red bean paste, and may also include nuts, dried fruit, or an egg yolk.|例句：We ate mooncakes during the Mid-Autumn Festival. 我们在中秋节吃了月饼。

例句：Mooncakes are a traditional treat for the Mid-Autumn Festival. 月饼是中秋节的传统美食。

近义词：pastry, dessert|反义词: None|词性: Noun
