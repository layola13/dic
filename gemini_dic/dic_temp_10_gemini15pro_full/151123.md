parastatal: /ˌpærəˈsteɪtl/ /ˌpærəˈsteɪtəl/ | n. adj. | 半国营的，准政府的；半国营公司，准政府机构 |A company or organization which is owned or controlled wholly or partly by the government.  It operates commercially but is often subject to some government regulation and may have social or political objectives in addition to profit. |  The government is planning to privatize several parastatals. 政府正计划将几家半国营公司私有化。 | The parastatal company is responsible for providing telecommunications services. 这家半国营公司负责提供电信服务。 | 近义词: government-owned corporation, state-owned enterprise, quasi-governmental organization | 反义词: private company, privately owned business | 词性: noun, adjective | 词源: para- (beside, alongside) + state
