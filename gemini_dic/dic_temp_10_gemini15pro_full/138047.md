nordvpn:/ˌnɔːrdviːpiːˈɛn/| n.|北方VPN|A virtual private network service offered by Nord Security. It allows users to create a secure connection to another network over the Internet. This can be used to bypass geographic restrictions on websites or content, as well as to protect users' browsing activity from prying eyes.|I use NordVPN to access streaming services that are not available in my country. 我使用NordVPN访问在我所在国家/地区不可用的流媒体服务。

NordVPN helps protect my online privacy. NordVPN有助于保护我的在线隐私。|近义词：VPN, virtual private network, ExpressVPN, Surfshark|反义词:None|词性:noun
