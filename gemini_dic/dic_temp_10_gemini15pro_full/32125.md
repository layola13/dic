chevalier:/ˌʃɛvəˈlɪər/ | n. |骑士，武士；爵士；（旧时法国的）绅士|A mounted soldier serving under a feudal superior; a knight.  A member of certain orders of knighthood or of merit, or a person honored with a title equivalent to knight. A gallant or chivalrous man.| He was a true chevalier, always ready to defend the weak. 他是一位真正的骑士，时刻准备着保护弱者。|Near synonyms: knight, cavalier, paladin|Near antonyms: villain, knave|noun
