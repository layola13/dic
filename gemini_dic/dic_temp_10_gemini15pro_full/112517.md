lout:/laʊt/
noun
笨蛋，粗鲁的人，流氓
A rude, aggressive, and noisy man.
例句：A group of louts started a fight in the pub. 一群流氓在酒吧里挑起了争斗。

例句：The louts were shouting abuse at passers-by. 这些流氓对着路人叫骂。

近义词：ruffian, hooligan, thug, hoodlum, vandal, yob, boor, oaf, brute
反义词：gentleman, lady
词性：n.
