n.
肥胖；脂肪过多
the state of being fat; obesity
Adiposity is a risk factor for many diseases. 肥胖是许多疾病的风险因素。

词性: 名词

词源:  From Latin *adipōsitās, from adipōsus (“fatty”), from adeps (“fat”).
