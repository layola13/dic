embattled:/ɪmˈbætld/ | adj. | 陷入困境的，卷入战争的 |  Involved in or beset by conflict or struggle.  Having a long or difficult struggle | The embattled president faced calls for his resignation.  陷入困境的总统面临着让他辞职的呼声。 | Synonyms: besieged, struggling, troubled, under fire, under attack | Antonyms: peaceful, secure, untroubled | adjective

embattled:/ɪmˈbætld/ | adj. | 备战的，严阵以待的 | Prepared for battle; fortified. |  The embattled troops prepared for the enemy's attack.  备战的部队准备迎接敌人的进攻。| Synonyms: armed, fortified, prepared, ready for battle | Antonyms: unarmed, unprepared, defenseless | adjective
