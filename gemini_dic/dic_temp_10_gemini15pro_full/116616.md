matronal:/məˈtroʊnl/ /ˈmeɪtrənl/ | adj. |  matronly的；像主妇的；（尤指年长的妇女）稳重的 | Of, relating to, or characteristic of a matron; dignified, stately.  Suitable for a matron, especially with reference to age and appearance.  Sometimes used to imply a woman is older than her years or overly serious. | 例句：She wore a matronal dress. 她穿着一件 matronly 的连衣裙。

例句：Her matronal air made her seem older than she was. 她那副主妇派头让她看起来比实际年龄大。


近义词：motherly, stately, dignified, matronly | 反义词：youthful, girlish, immature, childish | 词性：adjective
