brisknesses: /ˈbrɪsknɪsɪz/
noun
活泼, 爽快, 轻快
the quality of being brisk; liveliness and energy.
The briskness of the autumn air invigorated her. 秋天的凉爽空气使她精神焕发。
Synonyms: liveliness, vigor, energy, pep, zip, animation, sprightliness
Antonyms: sluggishness, lethargy, apathy, dullness
词性: 名词
