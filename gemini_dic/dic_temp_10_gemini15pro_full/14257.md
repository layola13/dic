autofecundation:/ˌɔːtəʊˌfiːkənˈdeɪʃən/ |n.| 自体受精 | Self-fertilization; fertilization of an ovum by a spermatozoon from the same individual. | Many plants rely on autofecundation to reproduce. 许多植物依靠自体受精繁殖。| The snail's reproductive strategy includes autofecundation.  这只蜗牛的繁殖策略包括自体受精。 | synonyms: self-fertilization, self-pollination | antonyms: cross-fertilization, allogamy | noun
