outpaces:/ˌaʊtˈpeɪsɪz/
verb
超过，胜过
to go faster, perform better, or develop more quickly than someone or something else.
Electric cars sales are outpacing those of gasoline-powered vehicles. 电动汽车的销量超过了汽油动力汽车。
The demand for skilled workers outpaces the supply. 对熟练工人的需求超过了供应。
近义词:surpass, exceed, outstrip, outrun
反义词:lag behind, trail
词性:v.
