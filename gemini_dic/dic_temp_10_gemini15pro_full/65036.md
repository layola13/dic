etas:/ˈiːtɑːz/ /ˈetəz/ | n. |预计到达时间; 预计到达时刻| Estimated Time of Arrival. It's commonly used in transportation and logistics to indicate when a vehicle, shipment, or person is expected to arrive at a destination. | The pilot announced the ETA as 3:00 PM.  飞行员宣布预计到达时间为下午3点。| Synonyms: arrival time, scheduled arrival, due time | Antonyms: ETD (Estimated Time of Departure) | noun
