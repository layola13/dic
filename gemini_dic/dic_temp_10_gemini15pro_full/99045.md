interanimation:/ˌɪntərænɪˈmeɪʃən/
noun
相互赋予生命，互相激励，相互影响
The process of giving life or spirit to each other; mutual stimulation or interaction.
The interanimation between the two artists led to the creation of a truly unique masterpiece.  两位艺术家之间的互相激励促成了一件真正独特的杰作。
Interanimation between the characters in the play made the story come alive. 剧中人物之间的相互影响使故事栩栩如生。
近义词: interaction, interplay, reciprocity, synergy
反义词: isolation, separation, apathy
词性: n.
