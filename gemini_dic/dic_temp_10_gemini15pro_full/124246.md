mosstrooper:/ˈmɒsˌtruːpər/ | n. | 劫匪，强盗（尤指苏格兰和英格兰边境地区的）| A bandit or raider, especially one who operated in the borderlands between Scotland and England. |例句：The mosstroopers terrorized the local villages.  强盗们恐吓了当地的村庄。

例句：Mosstroopers were known for their swift raids and daring escapes.  强盗们以其迅速的突袭和大胆的逃脱而闻名。|近义词：bandit, raider, outlaw, brigand |反义词:lawman, police |词性:noun
