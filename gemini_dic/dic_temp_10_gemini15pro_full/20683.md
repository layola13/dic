bladder-like:/ˈblædərˌlaɪk/
adjective
囊状的；像膀胱的
Resembling a bladder in form or function; thin-walled and inflatable.
The plant has bladder-like leaves that trap insects. 这种植物有囊状的叶子来捕捉昆虫。
The tumor was a bladder-like structure filled with fluid.  肿瘤是一个充满液体的囊状结构。
近义词:sac-like, pouch-like
反义词:solid, compact
词性:adjective
词源: bladder (from Old English blædre) + -like (from Old English -līc)
