oppressive:/əˈprɛsɪv/| adj.|压迫的；沉重的；压制性的；令人窒息的|Burdensome, unjustly harsh, or tyrannical. Causing discomfort by being excessive, intense, or stuffy.|The oppressive heat made it difficult to work. 闷热的天气使人难以工作。|近义词：tyrannical, harsh, cruel, brutal, unjust, burdensome, stifling, suffocating|反义词： liberating, freeing, gentle, mild, kind, lenient|词性:形容词

oppressive:/əˈprɛsɪv/| adj.|压抑的；令人沮丧的|Weighing heavily on the mind or spirits; depressing.|The atmosphere in the room was oppressive. 房间里的气氛令人压抑。|近义词：depressing, gloomy, disheartening|反义词： uplifting, cheerful, encouraging|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *oppressif*, from Old French *oppressif*, from Latin *oppressīvus*, from *oppressus*, past participle of *opprimere* (“to press against, suppress”), from *ob-* (“against”) + *premere* (“to press”).
