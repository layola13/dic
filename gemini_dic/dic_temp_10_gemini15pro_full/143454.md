outfront:/ˌaʊtˈfrʌnt/
adjective
在前面；公开的；坦率的
publicly and openly; in a leading position
The company has been outfront in its support of environmental causes.  这家公司一直公开支持环保事业。
近义词: upfront, forthright, candid, open, public
反义词: secretive, hidden, concealed
词性: adjective

outfront:/ˌaʊtˈfrʌnt/
adverb
在前面；公开地；坦率地
in a leading position
She's been outfront about her struggles with depression. 她一直坦率地讲述自己与抑郁症的斗争。
近义词: openly, publicly, frankly
反义词: secretly, privately
词性: adverb
