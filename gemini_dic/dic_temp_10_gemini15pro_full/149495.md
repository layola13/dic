paeanism:/ˈpiːənɪzəm/ | n. | 颂扬；赞美；歌颂 |  An excessive expression of praise or enthusiasm, often for a person or cause.  It implies a fervent and sometimes exaggerated admiration. |  The leader's followers engaged in constant paeanism, praising his every move. 领导者的追随者不断地歌颂他，赞扬他的一举一动。| synonyms: eulogy, adulation, glorification, exaltation | antonyms: criticism, condemnation, denunciation | noun
