outwood:/ˈaʊtwʊd/
noun
外围林地，郊区林地
A wood or forest situated outside a town or village, or on the outskirts.
The children played in the outwood, gathering berries and exploring. 孩子们在外围林地玩耍，采摘浆果和探险。
Synonyms: woodland, forest, outskirts woods
Antonyms: city center, urban area
词性: 名词
