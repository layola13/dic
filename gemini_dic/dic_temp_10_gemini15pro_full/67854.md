fabaceous:/fəˈbeɪʃəs/
adjective
豆科的；豆类的
Relating to or belonging to the Fabaceae family (also known as Leguminosae), which includes plants such as peas, beans, and clover.  Characterized by having pods containing seeds.
Many fabaceous plants are important sources of protein.  许多豆科植物是重要的蛋白质来源。

近义词:leguminous
反义词:(None -  there isn't a direct antonym for a taxonomic classification)
词性:adjective
