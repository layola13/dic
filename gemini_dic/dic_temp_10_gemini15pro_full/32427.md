chimango:/tʃɪˈmæŋɡoʊ/| n. |栗翅鹰|A small hawk-like bird of prey, *Milvago chimango*, found in open country in southern South America, having brown plumage and chestnut wing patches.|The chimango soared effortlessly above the pampas. 栗翅鹰毫不费力地在潘帕斯草原上空翱翔。|近义词：hawk, falcon, kestrel|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific bird species) |词性:noun

词源: From Spanish *chimango*, probably of Chilean Spanish origin.
