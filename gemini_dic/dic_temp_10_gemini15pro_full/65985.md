exaltedly:/ɪɡˈzɔːltɪdli/ | adv. |兴奋地，得意洋洋地|In a manner filled with great joy, triumph, or lofty emotion.  Characterized by high spirits and a sense of elation.|She spoke exaltedly of her recent promotion. 她兴奋地谈到她最近的升职。|Synonyms: triumphantly, jubilantly, ecstatically, rapturously|Antonyms: dejectedly, despondently, gloomily|adverb

Etymology:
From Late Latin exaltatus (past participle of exaltare, "to exalt"), from Latin ex-, "out" + altus, "high".  The "-ly" suffix transforms the adjective "exalted" into the adverb "exaltedly".
