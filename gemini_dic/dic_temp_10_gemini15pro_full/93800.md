immersion:/ɪˈmɜːrʒən/ /ɪˈmɜːrʃən/ | n. | 沉浸，浸入，专心 |  The state of being deeply engaged or involved in something. |  She experienced a total immersion in the local culture. 她完全沉浸在当地文化中。

| Example:  Language immersion is a great way to learn. 语言沉浸式学习是一个很好的学习方法。

| Example:  He enjoys the immersion of a good book. 他喜欢沉浸在一本好书中的感觉。

| Synonym: submersion, absorption, engagement | Antonym: detachment, disengagement | noun
