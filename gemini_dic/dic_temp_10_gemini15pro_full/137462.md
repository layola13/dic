nontimbered:/nɒnˈtɪmbəd/
adjective
非木材的；无立木的
Not covered with trees suitable for timber; not forested.
The nontimbered areas were used for grazing.  这些没有树木的区域被用来放牧。
The park includes both timbered and nontimbered areas.  这个公园包括有林地和无林地。
Synonyms: treeless, unforested, unwooded
Antonyms: timbered, forested, wooded
