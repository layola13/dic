dredge:/drɛdʒ/| n. v.| 挖泥机，疏浚；挖掘，疏浚 | A machine used for deepening or clearing out harbors, rivers, etc.  A boat or machine that removes mud from the bottom of a river, lake, etc. | The harbor was dredged to allow larger ships to enter. 港口被疏浚，以便更大的船只进入。 | They dredged the channel to make it deeper. 他们疏浚了河道，使它更深。 | synonyms: excavate, scoop, drag, clear | antonyms: fill, deposit | noun, verb

dredge:/drɛdʒ/| v. | 撒，洒；翻找，搜寻 | To sprinkle or coat (food) with a dry ingredient, such as flour or breadcrumbs. To search through something thoroughly. | Dredge the chicken pieces in flour before frying.  炸鸡块前，先把鸡块裹上面粉。 | She dredged up old memories of her childhood. 她翻出了儿时的旧记忆。| synonyms: sprinkle, coat, sift, rummage, search | antonyms: uncover, reveal | verb
