choliamb:/kəˈlaɪæmb/
noun
跛行诗; 跛行扬抑格; 抑扬格诗的最后音步由抑抑格代替
A verse or line of verse having a spondee or trochee replacing an iambus in the last foot.
Choliambs were often used by ancient Greek and Roman poets for satire. 跛行诗常被古希腊和罗马诗人用于讽刺。
