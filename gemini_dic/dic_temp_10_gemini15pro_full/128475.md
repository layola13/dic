newsmen:/ˈnjuːzmən/ | n. |新闻记者（尤指男性）| A journalist, especially a male one, who works for newspapers, magazines, or television or radio news programs. | Example 1: The newsmen crowded around the president, eager for a statement.  新闻记者们围着总统，渴望得到一份声明。

Example 2:  The newsmen were on the scene within minutes of the accident. 事故发生后几分钟内，新闻记者就赶到了现场。 | 近义词: journalists, reporters, correspondents | 反义词:  None (No direct opposite for a profession) | 词性: Noun
