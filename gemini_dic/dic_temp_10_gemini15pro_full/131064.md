nonbarbarously: /nɒnbɑːˈbɛrəsli/ | adv. | 非野蛮地，文明地 | In a civilized and humane manner; not cruelly or savagely. |  The prisoners, surprisingly, were treated nonbarbarously.  令人惊讶的是，囚犯们受到了文明的对待。|  The conquering army behaved nonbarbarously towards the defeated population. 征服军对战败的人民表现得并不野蛮。 | 近义词: humanely, civilly, kindly | 反义词: barbarously, cruelly, savagely | 词性: 副词

词源:
non- (prefix):  not, opposite of  (非，相反的)
barbarous (adjective):  extremely cruel or unpleasant; primitive and uncivilized (野蛮的，残忍的)
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives (副词后缀)
