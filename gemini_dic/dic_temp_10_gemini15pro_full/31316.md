chaprasi: /tʃəˈprɑːsi/ | n. | 茶房，勤杂工 | An office boy or attendant, especially in India.  Derived from Persian "chahar parasti" meaning "four worshippers," likely referring to the four points of the compass and implying a servant's wide availability. |  The chaprasi brought us tea. 茶房给我们送来了茶。|  The chaprasi delivered the mail. 茶房送来了邮件。 | Synonyms: peon, orderly, attendant | Antonyms: manager, executive | noun
