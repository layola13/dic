dictator:/ˈdɪkteɪtər/ | n. | 独裁者，专政者 | A ruler with total power over a country, typically one who has obtained control by force. | The dictator ruled with an iron fist.  这个独裁者用铁腕统治。| autocrat, tyrant, despot | democrat, president | noun

dictators:/ˈdɪkteɪtərz/ | n. | 独裁者们，专政者们 | Plural form of "dictator," referring to multiple individuals who hold total power over a country or organization. |  History is full of dictators who abused their power. 历史上充满了滥用权力的独裁者们。 | autocrats, tyrants, despots | democrats, presidents | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dictator*, from *dictare* "to say repeatedly, prescribe," frequentative of *dicere* "to say, speak."
