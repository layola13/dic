convocational:/ˌkɒnvəˈkeɪʃənəl/ /ˌkɑːnvəˈkeɪʃənl/ | adj. |  会话的；谈话的；交谈的 | Relating to or resembling conversation; characterized by informal exchange of ideas. | The atmosphere at the meeting was convivial and convocational.  会议上的气氛轻松愉快，大家畅所欲言。 | 近义词: conversational, dialogic, discursive | 反义词: formal, stilted, uncommunicative | 词性: 形容词

convocation:/ˌkɒnvəˈkeɪʃən/ /ˌkɑːnvəˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |  召集；会议；集会 |  The act of calling people together for a meeting, especially a large formal assembly.  | The university president addressed the convocation of graduating students.  大学校长在毕业生大会上致辞。| 近义词: assembly, gathering, meeting, congress | 反义词: dispersal, dismissal | 词性: 名词
