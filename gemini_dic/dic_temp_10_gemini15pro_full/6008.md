amebalike:/əˈmiːbəˌlaɪk/| adj.|变形虫似的，像变形虫的|Resembling an amoeba, especially in being shapeless or in changing shape readily.|The amebalike creature slowly engulfed its prey.  这种像变形虫的生物慢慢地吞噬了它的猎物。|Synonyms: amoeboid, amorphous, shapeless|Antonyms: formed, shaped, definite|adjective
