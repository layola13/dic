bloomed:/bluːmd/| v. |开花；繁荣，兴旺|to produce flowers; to develop successfully|例句：The cherry trees bloomed early this year. 今年樱花开得早。|近义词：flowered, blossomed, thrived, flourished|反义词：withered, faded, declined|词性:动词
Etymology: From Middle English *blo(o)men, from Old English *blōmōian (“to bloom, blossom”), from *blōma (“bloom”), from Proto-Germanic *blōmô (“flower, blossom”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰleh₁- (“to blow, bloom, blossom”). Cognate with Scots bloom (“bloom”), West Frisian blome (“to bloom”), Dutch bloeien (“to bloom, blossom”), German blühen (“to bloom, blossom”).
