maleficence:/məˈlɛfɪsəns/| n.|邪恶，恶意，罪恶|The act of committing harm or evil; wrongdoing, especially by sorcery or witchcraft.  It implies a conscious and deliberate choice to inflict harm or suffering on others. |His maleficence knew no bounds, as he delighted in causing pain and misery. 他的恶意没有界限，因为他以制造痛苦和苦难为乐。|Synonyms: evil, wickedness, malevolence, malignity, viciousness|Antonyms: benevolence, beneficence, goodness, kindness|Noun

maleficent:/məˈlɛfɪsənt/| adj.|有害的，恶毒的，邪恶的|Producing evil or harm; harmful or deadly.  It describes something that is likely to cause harm or destruction. |The witch cast a maleficent spell on the princess. 女巫对公主施了一个恶毒的咒语。|Synonyms: harmful, evil, wicked, malevolent, pernicious|Antonyms: beneficial, benign, good, kind|Adjective
