pahoehoe:/pɑːˈhoʊ.hoʊ.eɪ/ /pəˈhoʊ.hoʊ.eɪ/ | n. | 块状熔岩，绳状熔岩 | A type of lava flow with a smooth, billowy, or ropy surface.  It is formed by highly fluid basaltic lava. | The volcano erupted, producing vast fields of pahoehoe. 火山喷发，产生了大片的块状熔岩。


Pahoehoe lava is easier to walk on than a'a.  走过绳状熔岩比走过渣状熔岩更容易。 | 近义词:ropy lava | 反义词:a'a | 词性:名词
