gaggled:/ˈɡæɡ(ə)ld/
verb
成群结队地移动; 咯咯叫
moved or sounded like a gaggle of geese.
The geese gaggled loudly as they flew overhead.  这些鹅飞过头顶时大声咯咯叫。
The tourists gaggled excitedly as they entered the museum.  游客们兴奋地涌入博物馆。
近义词: flocked, swarmed, thronged
反义词: dispersed, scattered
词性:v.


gaggle:/ˈɡæɡ(ə)l/
noun
鹅群; (尤指嘈杂的)一群女人或女孩
a group of geese; a flock of geese, especially when on water; an informal term for a group of women or girls, especially a noisy or gossipy group
A gaggle of geese waddled across the road. 一群鹅摇摇摆摆地走过马路。
The gaggle of schoolgirls giggled as they walked past. 一群女学生走过时咯咯地笑着。
近义词: flock, group, crowd, cluster
反义词: individual
词性:n.


词源:
From the Middle English *gagelen, frequentative of gagen "to cackle," of imitative origin.
