例句：The androphagous practices of certain tribes horrified early explorers.  某些部落的食人行为使早期的探险家感到恐惧。|近义词：cannibalistic|反义词:vegetarian, herbivorous|词性:adjective
Etymology->
From Ancient Greek  *ἀνθρωποφάγος (anthrōpophágos)*  'man-eating', from  *ἄνθρωπος (ánthrōpos)*  'man, human being' +  *φαγεῖν (phageîn)*,  'to eat'.
