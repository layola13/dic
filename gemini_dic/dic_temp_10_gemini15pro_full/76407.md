gaged:/ɡeɪdʒd/
adj.
已抵押的，已订婚的
pledged as security; engaged to be married.
The house was gaged to the bank. 这房子抵押给了银行。
She is gaged to a young doctor. 她和一位年轻的医生订婚了。
近义词: betrothed, engaged, pledged, committed
反义词: unengaged, free
词性: 形容词

gaged:/ɡeɪdʒd/
v.
(gage的过去式和过去分词) 测量，估计；抵押，典当；雇佣；使…啮合
measured, estimated; pledged as security; employed; caused to mesh.
They gaged the depth of the river. 他们测量了河的深度。
He gaged his watch for a loan. 他用他的手表作抵押借款。
The company gaged a new engineer. 公司雇佣了一位新工程师。
The gears were gaged smoothly. 齿轮顺利啮合了。
近义词: measure, estimate, assess, appraise, pledge, pawn, hire, employ, engage, mesh
反义词: release, disengage
词性: 动词
