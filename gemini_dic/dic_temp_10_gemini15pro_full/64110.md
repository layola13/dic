epoxyed:/ɪˈpɒksid/ |v. | 环氧树脂涂覆的; 用环氧树脂粘合的 | The floor was epoxyed for a durable finish. 地板涂了环氧树脂以获得耐用的表面。|  The two pieces of metal were epoxyed together.  这两块金属用环氧树脂粘合在一起。 | 近义词：glued, bonded, cemented, sealed | 反义词：detached, separated | 词性: 动词 (past participle)

epoxy:/ɪˈpɒksi/ | n. | 环氧树脂 |  The epoxy resin is a two-part adhesive.  这种环氧树脂是一种双组分粘合剂。 | 例句:  The sculptor used epoxy to create a strong bond between the different materials.  雕塑家使用环氧树脂在不同材料之间建立牢固的连接。 | 近义词: resin, adhesive, glue | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 solvent 溶剂) | 词性: 名词

词源:  From *epoxy* + *-ed*. *Epoxy* is a shortening of *epoxy resin*, ultimately from *epi-* (upon, over) + *oxy-* (oxygen).
