nonperceptional:/ˌnɒnpərˈsɛpʃənəl/
adjective
非感知的；非感觉的
Not relating to or involving perception; not perceptible.
His theory relies on several nonperceptional factors. 他的理论依赖于几个非感知因素。
The coma patient was in a nonperceptional state.  昏迷的病人处于无知觉状态。
Synonyms: insensible, unconscious, unaware, insensitive
Antonyms: perceptual, sensory, conscious, aware
词性: adjective
