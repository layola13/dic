enjoyed:/ɪnˈdʒɔɪd/ | v. | 享受，喜爱，经历 | Past tense of "enjoy."  Indicates that someone experienced pleasure or satisfaction from something in the past. | I enjoyed the movie last night. 我昨晚很享受那部电影。| We enjoyed our vacation in Hawaii. 我们在夏威夷的假期玩得很开心。 |  近义词：liked, appreciated, relished, savored | 反义词：disliked, hated, loathed | verb
