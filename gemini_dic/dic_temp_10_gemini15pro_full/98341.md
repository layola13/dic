insinuation:/ɪnˌsɪn.juˈeɪ.ʃən/ | n. | 暗示，影射，含沙射影 | An indirect and unpleasant hint or suggestion of something bad. |  She resented the insinuation that she was lazy. 她对别人暗示她懒惰感到愤恨。

The article made insinuations about his personal life. 这篇文章影射了他的私生活。

| 近义词：implication, suggestion, innuendo, hint | 反义词：direct statement, explicit declaration | 词性：名词
