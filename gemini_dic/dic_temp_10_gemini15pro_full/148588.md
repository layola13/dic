overtaxation:/ˌoʊvərˌtækˈseɪʃən/| n. |过度征税，负担过重|The act of taxing excessively or imposing too heavy a burden.|The overtaxation of the small business sector stifled economic growth.  对小型企业部门的过度征税扼杀了经济增长。|Synonyms: excessive taxation, overburdening |Antonyms: tax relief, tax reduction|noun

overtax:/ˌoʊvərˈtæks/| vt. |过度征税，使负担过重|To tax excessively or impose too heavy a burden on.|The government overtaxed the people to fund the war. 政府过度征税以资助战争。|Synonyms: overburden, strain, overwork |Antonyms: undertax, ease, lighten|verb


ETYMOLOGY->

"overtaxation" comes from "over-" (meaning "excessive") + "taxation" (the act of levying a tax).  "Taxation" ultimately derives from the Latin "taxare" (to estimate, appraise, or charge).
