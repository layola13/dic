looking-back:/ˈlʊkɪŋ bæk/| n.|回顾，回首，反思|The exhibition offers a fascinating looking-back at the history of fashion.  这个展览引人入胜地回顾了时尚的历史。|例句：Looking back, I realize I made a few mistakes. 回顾过去，我意识到我犯了一些错误。|近义词：retrospection, review, reflection|反义词：anticipation, looking forward|词性:名词

Etymology: From the verb phrase "look back," composed of "look" (from Old English *lōcian "to see, look," from Proto-Germanic *lōkōjan) and "back" (from Old English bæc "backward," from Proto-Germanic *bak).
