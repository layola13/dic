dialis:/dīˈælɪs/ | adj. |  每日的，属于白天的 |  Relating to a day or occurring during the daytime.  Derived from Latin "dies" meaning "day". While "dialis" itself is not a common word in modern English, it forms the root of several more common words like "diurnal".  | The dialis activities of the bees ceased as the sun set. 蜜蜂的白日活动随着太阳落山而停止。 | diurnal | nocturnal | adjective
