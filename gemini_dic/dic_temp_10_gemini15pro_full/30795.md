cetin:/ˈsɛtɪn/| n.|鲸蜡; 鲸蜡醇; 十六烷醇|A waxy, white, crystalline alcohol, CH3(CH2)15OH, found in spermaceti and other natural waxes, used in cosmetics and other products.|The candle is made with cetin. 这蜡烛是用鲸蜡醇制成的。|Synonyms: cetyl alcohol, hexadecanol|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
