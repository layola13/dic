outsmiled:/ˌaʊtˈsmaɪld/
verb
胜过…的笑容；以微笑胜过
outdo someone in smiling; to smile more than someone else.
She outsmiled everyone at the party, radiating warmth and joy. 她以灿烂的笑容胜过派对上的每一个人，散发着温暖和快乐。
He outsmiled his opponent, showing he wasn't intimidated. 他以微笑回应对手，表明自己毫不畏惧。
近义词: outcharm, outshine
反义词: frown, scowl
词性: verb

词源:
"Out-" (prefix indicating surpassing or exceeding) + "smiled" (past tense of "smile")
