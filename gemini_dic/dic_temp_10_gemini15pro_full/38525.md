concise:/kənˈsaɪs/| adj.|简明的，简洁的|Giving a lot of information clearly and in a few words; brief but comprehensive.|The chairman's summary of the issues was concise and to the point. 主席对问题的总结简明扼要。|synonyms: brief, succinct, terse, compact, pithy|antonyms: lengthy, verbose, wordy, prolix|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *concisus*, past participle of *concidere* "to cut down, cut off, cut short," from *com-* "together" (see com-) + *caedere* "to cut" (see -cide).
