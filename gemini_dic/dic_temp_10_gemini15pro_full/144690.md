ouzo:/ˈuːzoʊ/ (US), /ˈuːzəʊ/ (UK)| n.|茴香酒;希腊茴香烈酒|A dry anise-flavored aperitif that turns milky white when water is added.|He ordered an ouzo before dinner. 他在晚餐前点了一杯茴香酒。|Synonyms: aperitif, anise liqueur|Antonyms: None|Noun

ouzos:/ˈuːzoʊz/ (US), /ˈuːzəʊz/ (UK)| n.|茴香酒（复数）|Plural form of ouzo, referring to multiple servings or types of ouzo.|They sampled several different ouzos at the Greek restaurant. 他们在希腊餐厅品尝了几种不同的茴香酒。|Synonyms: aperitifs, anise liqueurs|Antonyms: None|Noun
