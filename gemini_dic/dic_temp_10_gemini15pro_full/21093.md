blinken:/ˈblɪŋkən/| v. |眨眼，闪烁|To shut and open the eyes quickly.  To shine intermittently.|He blinked rapidly in the bright sunlight. 他在明亮的阳光下快速地眨眼。|The lights blinked on and off. 灯一闪一闪地亮着。|synonyms: wink, flicker, flash|antonyms: stare, gaze|verb
