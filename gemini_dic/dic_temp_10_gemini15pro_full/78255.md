germander:/dʒɜːrˈmændər/| n.|香科科属植物，石蚕|A plant of the genus Teucrium, which includes wall germander and wood germander.  These plants are often used in herbal medicine and as ornamental garden plants.|The bees were busy amongst the germander. 蜜蜂在香科科属植物间忙碌着。|Synonyms: teucrium, wood sage|Antonyms: N/A|noun


germander:/dʒɜːrˈmændər/| n.|不公正的选区划分|The manipulation of electoral district boundaries to favor one party or class.|The gerrymandering of the districts ensured the incumbent's re-election. 选区的划分保证了现任议员的连任。|Synonyms:  redistricting (when done for political advantage), manipulating|Antonyms: fair apportionment|noun
