debilitative:/dɪˈbɪlɪteɪtɪv/
adjective
使人衰弱的；削弱的
Tending to weaken or enfeeble.
The debilitating effects of the illness kept him bedridden for months.  这种疾病使人衰弱的影响让他卧床数月。
Synonyms: weakening, enervating, incapacitating, crippling
Antonyms: strengthening, invigorating, empowering
词性: 形容词
