dadaist:/ˈdɑːdəɪst/ | n. adj. |达达主义者；达达主义的|A follower or supporter of Dadaism, an avant-garde art movement that rejected logic and reason in favor of chaos and irrationality.  | Example: The dadaist artist created a collage of random images and text.  这位达达主义艺术家创作了一幅由随机图像和文字组成的拼贴画。| Example:  Her dadaist poetry challenged traditional notions of form and meaning. 她的达达主义诗歌挑战了传统的形式和意义的概念。 |Synonyms: avant-gardist, modernist, rebel, iconoclast | Antonyms: traditionalist, classicist | Noun, Adjective
