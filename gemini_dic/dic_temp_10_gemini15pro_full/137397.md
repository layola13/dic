nontensility:/ˌnɒnˈtɛnˈsɪləti/
noun
不可拉伸性; 非张力性
Lack of tensility; inability to be stretched.
The nontensility of the material made it unsuitable for bungee cords.  这种材料的不可拉伸性使得它不适合做蹦极绳。
The rope's nontensility caused it to snap under pressure.  绳子的不可拉伸性导致它在压力下断裂。
近义词: inflexibility, rigidity
反义词: tensility, elasticity, stretchability
词性: noun

Etymology:
From non- (not) + tensility (the state or property of being tensile).  Tensility derives from tensile, from Latin *tensilis, from tensus, past participle of tendere (to stretch).
