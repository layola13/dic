cirriferous:/ˌsɪrɪˈfɛrəs/
adjective
卷云状的，有卷须的
Bearing cirri.
例句：Cirriferous clouds often indicate a change in weather. 卷云状的云通常预示着天气变化。

The cirriferous fronds of the fern unfurled gracefully.  蕨类植物卷须状的叶子优雅地展开。

近义词：cirrose, tendrilled
反义词：smooth, bald
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *cirrifer, from cirrus (“curl of hair, tendril”) + -fer (“bearing”).
