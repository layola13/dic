arteriology: /ˌɑːtɪəriˈɒlədʒi/ (British), /ˌɑːrtɪriˈɑːlədʒi/ (American) | n. | 动脉学 | The study of the arteries, including their structure, function, and diseases. |  Arteriology plays a crucial role in understanding cardiovascular health.  动脉学在理解心血管健康方面起着至关重要的作用。 | 近义词: angiology | 反义词:  None (no direct antonym) | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Modern Latin *arteriologia, from arteria "artery" + -logia "-logy".  From Latin arteria "windpipe, artery" (originally believed to carry air), from Greek arteria "windpipe," later "artery," of unknown origin.
