marconigram:/ˈmɑːrkɒnɪɡræm/ |n.| 马可尼电报；无线电报 |A message sent by Marconi's wireless telegraphy system. | Example:  The ship sent a marconigram to report its position.  这艘船发送了一份无线电报报告它的位置。

Example:  Marconigrams were a crucial means of communication in the early 20th century.  在20世纪初，无线电报是一种至关重要的通讯方式。|Synonym: radiogram, wireless (message), telegram|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but consider) letter, physical message|noun
