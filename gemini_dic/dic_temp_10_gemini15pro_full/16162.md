bankability:/ˌbæŋkəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可融资性，银行可接受性 | The quality of being likely to bring profit and therefore attractive to financial institutions that might provide loans or investment. |  The project's bankability depends on securing sufficient funding.  这个项目的可融资性取决于能否获得足够的资金。| Synonyms: viability, feasibility, financeability | Antonyms: unviability, infeasibility | noun

bankability:/ˌbæŋkəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 获利能力，盈利能力 | The capacity of a project or venture to generate profit.  | The bankability of the new product line is still uncertain. 新产品线的盈利能力仍不确定。 | Synonyms: profitability, lucrativeness | Antonyms: unprofitability | noun
