manans: /ˈmænænz/ | n. | 甘露聚糖 | Manans are a type of polysaccharide found in plant cell walls, particularly in the endosperm of some seeds.  They are composed of mannose units. |  Guar gum contains manans which contribute to its thickening properties. 瓜尔豆胶含有甘露聚糖，这有助于其增稠特性。| synonyms: mannose polymers, hemicelluloses | antonyms: None | noun
