paranthracene:/ˌpærənˈθræsiːn/
noun
对蒽，二蒽
A dimer of anthracene, formed by irradiation of anthracene with ultraviolet light.
Paranthracene is formed by the photodimerization of anthracene. 对蒽是由蒽的光二聚作用形成的。
Paranthracene can revert to anthracene by heating or further irradiation with shorter-wavelength ultraviolet light. 对蒽可以通过加热或用更短波长的紫外线进一步照射而恢复成蒽。
synonym: dianthracene
antonym: anthracene
词性: noun
