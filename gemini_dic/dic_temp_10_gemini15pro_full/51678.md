deviling:/ˈdɛvəlɪŋ/
n.|魔鬼行为；调皮捣蛋；（烹饪）加辣味调料|The act of being mischievous or troublesome, often in a playful way; also, the culinary practice of adding spicy seasoning, typically to eggs or other dishes.|He was known for his deviling, constantly playing pranks on his classmates. 他以调皮捣蛋而闻名，经常对他的同学恶作剧。|近义词：mischief, prankishness, spicing|反义词: obedience, goodness|词性:名词

deviling:/ˈdɛvəlɪŋ/
v.|（烹饪）加辣味调料；使苦恼，折磨|Adding spicy seasoning, especially to eggs or other dishes; causing someone worry or distress.|She was deviling the eggs with paprika and cayenne pepper. 她正在用辣椒粉和辣椒给鸡蛋调味。|近义词：spicing, seasoning, tormenting|反义词: soothing, comforting|词性:动词

词源:
From "devil" + "-ing" suffix.  Derived from the notion of adding heat or spice, as if from the devil, or behaving in a mischievous way, like a little devil.
