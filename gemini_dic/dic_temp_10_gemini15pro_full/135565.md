nonpoliticians:/ˌnɑːnˌpɑːləˈtɪʃənz/
noun
非政治家；非政界人士
People who are not involved in politics, or who are not professional politicians.
Many nonpoliticians are active in community service. 许多非政界人士积极参与社区服务。
Nonpoliticians often bring a fresh perspective to policy debates. 非政界人士通常会为政策辩论带来新的视角。
Synonyms: civilians, laypeople, outsiders
Antonyms: politicians, statesmen, officeholders
词性: 名词
