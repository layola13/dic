abstract away from: /æbˈstrækt əˈweɪ frəm/ | phrasal verb | 从...抽象出来，脱离...的具体细节 | To consider something theoretically or separately from specific details or concrete instances.  |  例句：The artist abstracted away from the real-world appearance of the trees to create a more impressionistic painting. 艺术家从树木的真实世界外观中抽象出来，创作了一幅更具印象派风格的画作。

例句：In object-oriented programming, we abstract away from the implementation details to focus on the object's interface. 在面向对象编程中，我们从实现细节中抽象出来，专注于对象的接口。 | 近义词：generalize, conceptualize, simplify, distill | 反义词：specify, detail, concretize | verb phrase


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *abstractus*, past participle of *abstrahere* "to draw away," from *abs-* "away" + *trahere* "to draw." The sense development progressed from the literal "drawn away" to the figurative "separate (in thought)."
