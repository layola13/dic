free-to-air:/ˌfriː tuː ˈeər/
adjective
免费的（无线电视广播）
Available to the public without subscription or fee, typically referring to television or radio broadcasts.
Example: I watched the game on free-to-air television. 我在免费电视频道观看了比赛。
Example: Many channels switched from free-to-air to subscription services. 许多频道从免费广播转为订阅服务。
Synonym: free, over-the-air, broadcast
Antonym: subscription, pay-per-view, cable, satellite
词性: 形容词


free-to-air:/ˌfriː tuː ˈeər/
noun
免费电视/广播
Television or radio broadcasts that are available to the public without subscription or fee.
Example:  Free-to-air TV is still popular in many countries. 免费电视在许多国家仍然很受欢迎。
Example: The government regulates free-to-air broadcasting. 政府监管免费广播。
Synonym: broadcast television/radio, terrestrial television/radio
Antonym: subscription television/radio, cable television/radio, satellite television/radio
词性: 名词
