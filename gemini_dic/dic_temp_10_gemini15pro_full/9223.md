antipapal:/ˌæntiˈpeɪp(ə)l/
| adj. | 反教皇的；反对教皇权威的 | Opposed to the authority of the Pope. |  The king's antipapal stance led to a break with Rome. 国王的反教皇立场导致了与罗马的决裂。| synonyms: anti-papal, anticlerical | antonyms: pro-papal, papist | adjective


Etymology:
From anti- + papal.  "Anti-" is a prefix of Greek origin meaning "against," "opposite of," "instead of." "Papal" relates to the Pope or the papacy.
