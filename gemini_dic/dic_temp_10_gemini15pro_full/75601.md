fuero:/ˈfweroʊ/| n.|特权，管辖权，特许权|A jurisdiction or privilege granted by a king or lord, especially in Spain and Spanish America.  Historically, it referred to a body of laws or customs particular to a place or group of people. |例句：The town's fuero allowed it to govern itself independently. 这个城镇的特许权允许其独立自治。

例句：He claimed fuero, asserting that he was subject only to the jurisdiction of the church courts. 他声称拥有特权，断言他只服从教会法庭的管辖。|近义词：privilege, right, prerogative, jurisdiction, charter, immunity |反义词: liability, subjection|词性:noun
