overharsh:/ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrʃ/| adj. | 过于严厉的，过于苛刻的 | Excessively harsh; too severe.  Characterized by excessive severity or strictness. |  His criticism was overharsh, considering the student's effort. 他的批评过于严厉，考虑到学生的努力。|  The overharsh lighting made everyone look washed out. 过于刺眼的光线让每个人看起来都很苍白。 | severe, stringent, rigorous, draconian | lenient, gentle, mild | adjective
