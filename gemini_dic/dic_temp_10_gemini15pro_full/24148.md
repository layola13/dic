bribees:/braɪˈbiːz/
n.
受贿者
People who accept bribes, typically to perform an act that constitutes a breach of trust.
The bribees were caught red-handed accepting envelopes of cash. 受贿者当场被抓，手里拿着装满现金的信封。
The investigation revealed a network of corrupt officials and their bribees. 调查揭露了一个腐败官员及其受贿者的网络。
近义词:recipients of bribes, those bribed
反义词:bribers
词性:名词
