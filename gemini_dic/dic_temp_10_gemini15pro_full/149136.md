oxidizability:/ˌɒksɪdaɪzəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可氧化性 | The capability of a substance to be oxidized; the ease with which a substance loses electrons in a chemical reaction. |  The oxidizability of iron is higher than that of gold. 铁的可氧化性比金高。| Synonyms: oxidation potential, reactivity | Antonyms: inertness, stability | noun
