acupunctures: /ˈækjʊpʌŋktʃərz/
noun
针灸疗法
The practice of inserting thin needles into specific points on the body to alleviate pain and treat various health conditions.
She tried acupuncture for her back pain. 她尝试用针灸治疗背痛。
Acupuncture is often used to treat chronic pain. 针灸常用于治疗慢性疼痛。

近义词:acupuncture treatment, needling
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider conventional medicine or allopathy)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin acus "needle" + punctura "pricking," from pungere "to prick."  The English word comes from the French acupuncture, borrowed in the 17th century.
