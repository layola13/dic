nonrecoil:/ˌnɒnˈriːkɔɪl/ /ˌnɑːnˈriːkɔɪl/ | adj. n.|无后坐力的，无后坐力武器|A nonrecoil gun is a weapon designed to minimize or eliminate the recoil force felt by the shooter upon firing.
