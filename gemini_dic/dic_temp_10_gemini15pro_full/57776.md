dreariest:/ˈdrɪəriɪst/| adj.|最沉闷的，最阴郁的|Superlative form of dreary: most dreary.  It means extremely bleak and depressing.|The dreariest day of the year was brightened by the arrival of a surprise package.|近义词：gloomiest, bleakest, dullest, most depressing|反义词：brightest, cheeriest, happiest|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *dreriest*, superlative of *drery*, from Old English *drēorig* (“sad, sorrowful, grieved; tedious, tiresome, dreary”), from Proto-Germanic *drēuzigaz* (“mournful”), from Proto-Indo-European *dreu-* (“to be troubled, to flow”).
