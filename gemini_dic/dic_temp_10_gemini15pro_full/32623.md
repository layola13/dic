chirrupper:/ˈtʃɪrʌpər/ | n. | 吱喳叫的人或物；乐观向上的人 |Someone or something that chirps, especially a person who is cheerful and optimistic. | The chirrupper in the cage turned out to be a bright yellow canary.  笼子里吱吱喳喳叫个不停的原来是一只亮黄色的金丝雀。| 近义词：singer, warbler, optimist | 反义词：pessimist, complainer | 词性: noun

Etymology: From the verb "chirrup" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Chirrup" is imitative of the sound made by birds.
