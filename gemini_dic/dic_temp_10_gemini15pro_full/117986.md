memorial:/məˈmɔːriəl/ | n. adj. | 纪念物，纪念碑；纪念的 | something, such as a ceremony, that is intended to honor the memory of a person or event |  The town built a memorial to the soldiers who died in the war.  这个小镇为在战争中牺牲的士兵建造了一座纪念碑。 | synonyms: monument, tribute, commemoration | antonyms:  disregard, disrespect | noun

memorial:/məˈmɔːriəl/ | adj. | 纪念的 | serving to preserve or honor the memory of a person or event |  a memorial service  追悼会 | synonyms: commemorative | antonyms:  uncommemorated | adjective
