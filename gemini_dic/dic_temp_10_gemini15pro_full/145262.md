overboiled:/ˌoʊvərˈbɔɪld/| adj.|煮过头的，过度烹煮的|Cooked for too long, resulting in a mushy or undesirable texture.|The overboiled vegetables were mushy and tasteless. 煮过头的蔬菜软烂无味。|The pasta was completely overboiled and stuck together.  意大利面完全煮过头了，粘在一起了。|近义词：overcooked|反义词：undercooked|词性:形容词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "boiled" (past participle of "boil")
