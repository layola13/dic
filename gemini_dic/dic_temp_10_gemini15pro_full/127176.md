naturalize:/ˈnætʃrəlaɪz/ /ˈnætʃrəlaɪz/| v. |使归化；移植；使入籍；吸纳；（使）适应；（使）显得自然|To grant full citizenship to a foreigner.|例句：He was naturalized as a British citizen. 他被归化为英国公民。|近义词：acclimatize, adapt, assimilate|反义词:alienate, expatriate|词性:动词
naturalize:/ˈnætʃrəlaɪz/ /ˈnætʃrəlaɪz/| adj. |归化的；驯化的|Made to seem natural; adapted to a new environment.|例句：Naturalized plants often outcompete native species. 驯化的植物经常比本地物种更有竞争力。|近义词：domesticated, acclimated|反义词:wild, foreign|词性:形容词
