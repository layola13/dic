aftercare:/ˈæftərˌkɛr/| n.|善后护理；后续照料；后期保养|The provision of assistance and support to someone after a period of illness, treatment, or incarceration.|例句：The hospital provides excellent aftercare for its patients. 这家医院为病人提供优秀的善后护理。

例句：Aftercare for drug addicts is crucial for long-term recovery.  对吸毒者的后续照料对于长期康复至关重要。|近义词：post-treatment care, follow-up care, support, rehabilitation|反义词:neglect|词性:名词
