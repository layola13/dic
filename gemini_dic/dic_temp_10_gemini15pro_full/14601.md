avionics:/ˌeɪviˈɒnɪks/ /ˌeɪviˈɑːnɪks/| n. |航空电子设备；航空电子学|The study and application of electronics in aviation.  It encompasses all of the electronic systems used on aircraft, spacecraft, and missiles, including navigation, communication, and flight control systems.|例句：The plane's avionics were state-of-the-art. 这架飞机的航空电子设备是最先进的。|近义词：aircraft electronics, aerospace electronics|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
