legation:/lɪˈɡeɪʃən/
n.
外交使团；使节馆舍
a group of diplomats representing their country in a foreign country, or the building where they work
The French legation in Bern was closed down. 法国驻伯尔尼的使馆关闭了。
mission, embassy, consulate
---
legation:/lɪˈɡeɪʃən/
n.
派遣；委任
the act of sending someone to a particular place as a representative
His legation to the conference was unexpected.  他被派去参加会议是出乎意料的。
delegation, assignment, commission
---

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *legacion*, from Old French *legacion*, from Latin *lēgātiō* (“a sending, dispatch, embassy”), from *lēgātus* (“envoy, ambassador”), past participle of *lēgō* (“to send with a commission, depute, delegate, bequeath”)
