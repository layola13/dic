ouse:/aʊs/| n.| 老鼠；胆小鬼 |A small rodent that typically has a pointed snout, small rounded ears, a body-length scaly tail, and a high breeding rate.|The little girl screamed when she saw a mouse scurry across the floor. 小女孩看到一只老鼠在地板上窜过，尖叫起来。|近义词： rodent, vermin|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
