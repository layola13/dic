hexadactylous:/ˌheksəˈdæktɪləs/| adj.|六指（趾）的|Having six fingers or toes on each hand or foot.|例句：The hexadactylous cat was quite a curiosity at the pet show.  这只六指猫在宠物展上引起了不小的轰动。

例句：Hexadactylous traits can be inherited genetically. 六指（趾）特征可以遗传。|近义词：polydactyl, six-fingered, six-toed|反义词:pentadactyl (五指的)|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Greek *hexa-* (six) + *daktylos* (finger, toe).
