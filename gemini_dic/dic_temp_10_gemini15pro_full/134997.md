nonnebulous:/nɒnˈnɛbjʊləs/| adj.|清晰的，明确的，不含糊的|Not nebulous; clear, definite, and unambiguous.  It describes something that is not vague or unclear.|例句：The contract was nonnebulous, leaving no room for misinterpretation.  合同写得清清楚楚，没有留下任何误解的余地。

例句：Her nonnebulous instructions made the task easy to complete. 她明确的指示使任务很容易完成。|近义词：clear, precise, definite, explicit, unambiguous, distinct, lucid, perspicuous|反义词：nebulous, vague, ambiguous, obscure, unclear, hazy, indistinct, fuzzy|词性:形容词
