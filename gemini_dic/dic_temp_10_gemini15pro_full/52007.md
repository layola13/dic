diagonal:/daɪˈæɡənəl/ /daɪˈæɡənl/ | adj. n. |对角线的，斜线的；对角线，斜线 |A diagonal line or movement goes from one corner of something to the opposite corner. |例句：Draw a diagonal line across the square. 在正方形上画一条对角线。

例句：The pattern consists of diagonal stripes.  这个图案由斜条纹组成。|近义词：oblique, slanting, transversal|反义词：horizontal, vertical|词性：adjective, noun

diagonal:/daɪˈæɡənəl/ /daɪˈæɡənl/ | n. |对角线，斜线|A straight line joining two opposite corners of a square, rectangle, or other straight-sided shape.|例句：The carpenter measured the diagonal of the wooden plank. 木匠测量了木板的对角线。

例句：Fold the paper along the diagonal. 沿着对角线折叠纸张。|近义词：oblique line, slant|反义词：side, edge|词性：noun
