eking:/ˈiːkɪŋ/ | v. | 勉强得到，设法获得 | Supplementing his meager income by eking out a living as a street vendor.  他靠做街头小贩勉强维持生计，补贴微薄的收入。 | He managed to eke out a victory in the final seconds of the game.  他在比赛的最后几秒钟设法取得了胜利。 |  近义词：scrape, scratch, squeeze, wrest | 反义词: squander, waste | 词性: 动词

词源:
From Middle English *eken, from Old Norse *auka (“to increase, add”), from Proto-Germanic *aukjaną (“to increase, augment”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ewg- (“to increase, strengthen”). Cognate with Swedish öka (“to increase”), Danish øge (“to increase”), Icelandic auka (“to increase”), German auch (“also, too”).
