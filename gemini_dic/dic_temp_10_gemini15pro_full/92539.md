hypural:/ˈhaɪpjʊrəl/| n. |尾下骨；尾杆骨|A flattened, modified haemal spine of the caudal vertebrae that supports the caudal fin rays in bony fishes.|The hypural bones are crucial for the movement of the tail fin. 尾下骨对于尾鳍的运动至关重要。| The scientist examined the hypural plate of the fish. 科学家检查了鱼的尾杆骨板。 |synonyms: hypural bone, hypurals| antonyms: (No direct antonyms, as it's a specific anatomical feature)|noun
