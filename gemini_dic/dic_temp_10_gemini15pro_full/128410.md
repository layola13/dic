newell:/ˈnuːəl/ | n. | 旋钮；球形把手；新月形的装饰 | A knob or handle, typically round, especially on a door or a piece of furniture.  Also, a crescent-shaped ornamentation. | Example 1:  The newell post at the bottom of the banister was intricately carved.  楼梯底部的新月形装饰柱雕刻精美。 | Example 2: The brass newell on the door felt cool to the touch. 门上的黄铜球形把手摸起来很凉。 | 近义词: knob, handle, finial | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun


词源: From Middle English *knopwelle (“knob-shaped well, knobbed roller”), from knop (“knob”) + welle (“well, spring”).  More recent senses likely influenced by personal names.
