maieutics:/meɪˈjuːtɪks/ | n. | 助产术; 苏格拉底式问答法 | The art of assisting another in bringing forth ideas, especially by a method of questioning resembling that of Socrates. |  Socrates' method of teaching, known as maieutics, involved skillful questioning to help students discover truths for themselves. 苏格拉底的教学方法，被称为助产术，包括巧妙的提问，以帮助学生自己发现真理。 |  midwifery, Socratic method |  didactics, lecture | noun
