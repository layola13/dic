desirously:/dɪˈzaɪərəsli/| adv.|渴望地，想望地|In a manner characterized by strong desire or longing; eagerly.|She looked at the chocolate cake desirously. 她渴望地看着巧克力蛋糕。|The child desirously reached for the brightly colored toy. 孩子渴望地伸手去拿色彩鲜艳的玩具。| 近义词:eagerly, longingly, yearningly|反义词:apathetically, indifferently|词性:副词

词源: From the adjective "desirous," which comes from the Old French "desiros" (desiring, desirous), ultimately from the Latin "desiderare" (to long for, desire).
