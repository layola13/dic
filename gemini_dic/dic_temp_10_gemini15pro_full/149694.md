pakora:/pəˈkɔːrə/ (UK), /pəˈkɔːrɑː/ (US)| n. |油炸蔬菜丸子；裹面糊油炸的蔬菜或肉|A piece of vegetable or meat, coated in spiced batter and deep-fried. |例句：We ordered some vegetable pakoras as a starter. 我们点了一些蔬菜油炸丸子作为开胃菜。

例句：The chicken pakoras were crispy and delicious. 鸡肉油炸丸子酥脆可口。

近义词：fritter, bhaji|反义词: None|词性:noun
