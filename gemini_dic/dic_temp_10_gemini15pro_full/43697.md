credited:/ˈkrɛdɪtɪd/
verb (past participle)|记入贷方；归功于；认为…具有(某种特性)|
The goal was credited to the team captain. 这个进球记在了队长名下。
He is credited with inventing the telephone.  他被认为发明了电话。
近义词:attributed, ascribed, assigned
反义词:debited
词性:v.
