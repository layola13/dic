PHRASE->
greet with open arms  热情欢迎
例句：The community greeted the new teacher with open arms.  社区热情地欢迎了新来的老师。

greet with a smile  笑脸相迎
例句：She greeted me with a smile, making me feel instantly welcome. 她笑脸相迎，让我立刻感到宾至如归。

exchange greetings  互相问候
例句：They exchanged greetings before starting the meeting.  他们在会议开始前互相问候。

offer greetings  致以问候
例句：Please offer my greetings to your family. 请代我向你的家人问好。

例句：I was greeted by a chorus of boos when I walked on stage. 我走上舞台时，受到了嘘声的“欢迎”。
