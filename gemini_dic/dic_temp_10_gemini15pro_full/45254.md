cultism:/ˈkʌltɪzəm/ | n. | 邪教;狂热崇拜;膜拜| Excessive devotion to a person, idea, or thing, especially when regarded as misguided or dangerous. |例句：The cultism surrounding the new guru worried many people.  对新宗教导师的狂热崇拜让很多人感到担忧。

例句：The government is trying to crack down on cultism. 政府正在试图打击邪教活动。|近义词：fanaticism, zealotry, idolatry |反义词: moderation, skepticism |词性: noun
