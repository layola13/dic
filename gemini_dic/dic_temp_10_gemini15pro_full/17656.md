becking:/ˈbɛkɪŋ/ | n. | 示意；召唤；招手 | The act of beckoning someone, usually with a hand gesture. | She gave him a becking gesture to come closer. 她示意他过来。| 近义词: gesturing, signaling, waving, summoning | 反义词: dismissing, repelling | 词性: 名词

becking:/ˈbɛkɪŋ/ | adj. | 吸引人的；诱人的 | Appealing or attractive; enticing. | The becking lights of the city drew him in. 城市迷人的灯光吸引了他。| 近义词: alluring, tempting, enticing, attractive | 反义词: unappealing, repellent, repulsive | 词性: 形容词
