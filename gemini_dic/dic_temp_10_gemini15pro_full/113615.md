machete:/məˈʃɛti/ (US), /məˈʃeti/ (UK)| n. |大砍刀; 開山刀|A large, heavy knife with a broad blade, used for cutting vegetation, wood, etc., and as a weapon.|例句：The farmer used a machete to clear the brush.  农民用大砍刀清除灌木丛。|近义词：cutlass, cleaver|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "scalpel" for its precision and small size, though not truly opposite)|noun
