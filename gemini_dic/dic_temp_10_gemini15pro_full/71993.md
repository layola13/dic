fling-out:/ˈflɪŋ aʊt/| n.| 突然的发怒；勃然大怒；(尤指)愤然离去|a sudden angry outburst; a fit of temper, especially one in which someone leaves a place angrily|She had a fling-out and stormed out of the room. 她勃然大怒，冲出了房间。|近义词：outburst, tantrum, fit of temper|反义词: calmness, composure|noun
fling-out:/ˈflɪŋ aʊt/| v.| 突然发怒；勃然大怒；(尤指)愤然离去|to suddenly become angry and leave a place|He flung out of the meeting in a rage. 他怒气冲冲地离开了会议。|近义词：storm out, walk out, stomp out|反义词: remain calm, stay composed|verb
