ETYMOLOGY->
From freckled + -ness.  Freckled comes from  freckle (n.)  Old English *frecel, related to Old Norse freknur "freckles,"  ultimately from Proto-Germanic *frak-, source of Old High German phrehhan "to spot, speckle."  The -ness suffix forms nouns from adjectives, meaning "state or quality of being."
