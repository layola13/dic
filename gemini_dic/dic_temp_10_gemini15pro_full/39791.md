consonous:/ˈkɑːnsənəs/| adj.|和谐的，一致的，符合的|Agreeing or accordant; in agreement in sentiment, feeling, etc.; marked by harmony or agreement. |The team members were consonous in their decision to move forward with the project. 团队成员一致决定推进该项目。|近义词：harmonious, agreeable, concordant, consistent, compatible|反义词：discordant, dissonant, incompatible, inconsistent|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *consonus* ("sounding together, agreeing, harmonious"), from *con-* ("together") + *sonus* ("sound"). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *swen-* ("to sound").
