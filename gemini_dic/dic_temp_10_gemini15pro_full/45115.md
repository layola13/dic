cucquean: /ˈkʌkwiːən/
noun
女性戴绿帽者；被戴绿帽而默许甚至鼓励妻子与其他男人发生性关系的男子
a woman whose husband has sexual relations with other women, especially with her knowledge and consent; a man who permits or encourages his wife to have sexual relations with other men.
She felt like a cucquean when her husband openly flirted with other women at the party.  当她的丈夫在派对上公开与其他女人调情时，她觉得自己像个戴绿帽的女人。
He's a cucquean who gets off on watching his wife with other men. 他是一个喜欢观看妻子与其他男人在一起的戴绿帽的男子。
Synonyms: cuckold (for men), wittol
