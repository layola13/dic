hektograph: /ˈhɛktəɡrɑːf/ | n. | 复制器，油印机 | A duplicating machine that uses a gelatinous surface from which copies are made.  A gelatine pad which copies can be made by transferring ink to it and then pressing paper against it. |  The teacher used a hektograph to make copies of the worksheet. 老师用油印机复制了练习题。| synonyms: duplicator, mimeograph | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "printer" in a modern context, implying a more advanced technology) | noun

hektograph: /ˈhɛktəɡrɑːf/ | v. | 用复制器复制 | To copy using a hektograph. | The secretary hektographed the meeting minutes. 秘书用油印机复制了会议记录。 | synonyms: duplicate, mimeograph, copy | antonyms: (No direct antonym) | verb
