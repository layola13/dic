anthomania:/ˌænθəˈmeɪniə/ | n. | 对花的狂热；爱花成癖 | An excessive enthusiasm for flowers. | She has a true anthomania, her house is overflowing with blooms. 她有真正的爱花癖，她的房子里到处都是花。 | Example 2: His anthomania led him to cultivate rare orchids in his greenhouse. 他的爱花癖使他在温室里种植珍稀的兰花。 | 近义词: florimania | 反义词: apath, indifference | 词性: noun

词源: From Greek ἄνθος (ánthos, “flower”) +‎ -mania.
