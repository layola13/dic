nasopharyngeal:/ˌneɪzoʊfəˈrɪndʒiəl/ | adj. | 鼻咽的 | Relating to the nasopharynx (the upper part of the pharynx, connecting with the nasal cavity above the soft palate). |  Nasopharyngeal cancer is a type of cancer that starts in the nasopharynx. 鼻咽癌是一种始于鼻咽的癌症。| Synonym: rhinopharyngeal | Antonym:  (No direct antonym exists, could consider terms relating to other parts of the respiratory system, like "tracheal" or "laryngeal") | Word Type: Adjective

nasopharynx: /ˌneɪzoʊˈfærɪŋks/ | n. | 鼻咽 | The upper part of the pharynx, connecting with the nasal cavity above the soft palate. | The nasopharynx is located behind the nose and above the roof of the mouth. 鼻咽位于鼻子后面，口腔顶部上方。 | Synonym: epipharynx | Antonym: (No direct antonym exists, potentially  "oropharynx" or "laryngopharynx" for other pharyngeal regions) | Word Type: Noun
