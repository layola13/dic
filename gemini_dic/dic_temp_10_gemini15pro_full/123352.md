monologian: /ˌmɒnəˈləʊdʒɪən/ /ˌmɑːnəˈlɑːdʒɪən/
n.
独白者；擅长独白的人；喜欢发表长篇大论的人
A person who delivers a monologue; a person who is skilled at or enjoys delivering long speeches.
The comedian was a true monologian, captivating the audience with his witty observations for a full hour.  这位喜剧演员是一位真正的独白大师，用他机智的观察力让观众着迷了整整一个小时。
He's not much of a conversationalist, more of a monologian. 他不太健谈，更像是一个喜欢发表长篇大论的人。
近义词: monologist, speaker, orator
反义词: listener
词性: noun
