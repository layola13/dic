Example 2:  We need to deploy a hotfix to fix the server crash issue immediately. 我们需要立即部署一个热修复程序来解决服务器崩溃问题。|近义词：patch, update, quick fix|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

词源: "Hot" refers to the urgency of the fix, and "fix" refers to the solution to the problem.  The term arose in the context of software development, where a quick fix is often needed to address critical issues. "Hot" emphasizes the immediacy and importance of the fix.
