burdeners:/ˈbɜːrdənərz/
n.
施加负担的人;加重负担者
People who impose burdens (financial, emotional, or otherwise) on others.
The burdeners of the economy are often the working class. 经济的负担者往往是工人阶级。
The constant complainers were burdeners on the team's morale.  不停的抱怨者加重了团队的精神负担。
Synonyms: encumberers, weighers-down
Antonyms: helpers, supporters
