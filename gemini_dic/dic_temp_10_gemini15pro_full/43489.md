crateriform:/ˈkreɪtərɪfɔːrm/
adjective
坑状的，环形的
Resembling a crater; shaped like a crater.
The meteorite impact left a crateriform depression in the landscape.  陨石撞击在地面上留下了一个坑状的凹陷。
The top of the volcano was crateriform. 火山顶部呈环形。
Near synonyms: crater-shaped, craterlike, bowl-shaped
Antonyms: flat, level
adjective
