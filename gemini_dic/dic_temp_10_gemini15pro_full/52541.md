diffidentnesses:/ˈdɪfɪdəntnəsɪz/| n. |缺乏自信，胆怯，羞怯|The state or quality of being diffident; lack of self-confidence; shyness; hesitancy.|Her diffidentnesses prevented her from speaking up in class.|近义词：shyness, timidity, hesitancy, insecurity, self-doubt|反义词：confidence, boldness, assertiveness, self-assurance|词性:名词

Etymology:
From diffident + -ness.  Diffident comes from Late Latin diffīdent-, diffīdens, present participle of diffīdere "to distrust," from dis- "away" + fīdere "to trust."
