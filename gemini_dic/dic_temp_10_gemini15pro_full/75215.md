froglets:/ˈfrɒɡlɪts/ (US) /ˈfrɑːɡlɪts/ (UK)| n.|小青蛙; 幼蛙|Young frogs that have recently metamorphosed from tadpoles.|例句：The pond was teeming with froglets.  池塘里充满了小青蛙。|例句：The froglets were hopping about on the lily pads. 小青蛙在睡莲叶子上跳来跳去。|近义词：tadpole (prior stage), polliwog (prior stage), young frog|反义词:adult frog|词性:noun
