cairniest:/ˈkɛərniɪst/ | adj. | 最爱抱怨的，最爱挑剔的 | Most complaining; most fault-finding.  This word is not commonly used and might be considered dialectal or informal.  It likely derives from "cairny" meaning complaining or fretful, ultimately related to "care" in the sense of anxiety or worry. |  "He was the cairniest old man in the village, always grumbling about something." 他是村子里最爱抱怨的老人，总是嘟囔着什么。 |  "She's so cairniest about the weather, it's never right for her." 她对天气太挑剔了，对她来说从来都不合适。| 近义词：complaining, carping, querulous, fault-finding | 反义词：easygoing, tolerant, uncritical | 词性: 形容词
