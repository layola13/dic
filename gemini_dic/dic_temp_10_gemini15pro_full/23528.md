brainchild:/ˈbreɪntʃaɪld/| n. |智力产物，发明，创意|An idea or invention that is the result of someone's creative thinking.  A product of someone's imagination. |例句：This new software is the brainchild of a young programmer.  这款新软件是一位年轻程序员的创意。

例句：The project was the brainchild of the CEO. 这个项目是首席执行官的创意。

近义词：invention, creation, innovation, product, offspring, idea|反义词: - |词性: noun
