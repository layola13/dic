iconize:/ˈaɪkənaɪz/| vt.|使成为偶像，把…当作偶像，赋予…偶像地位|To treat someone as an icon, especially by idealizing or glorifying them. |例句：The media iconized the young athlete after his Olympic victory.  媒体在年轻运动员奥运夺冠后把他奉为偶像。

例句：Fans iconized the singer, showering her with gifts and adoration. 歌迷们把这位歌手奉为偶像，送给她大量的礼物和崇拜。

近义词：idolize, glorify, deify, exalt, worship|反义词: vilify, denigrate, condemn|词性: verb
