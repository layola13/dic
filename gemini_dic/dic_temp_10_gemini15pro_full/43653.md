creature:/ˈkriːtʃər/ (US), /ˈkriːtʃə(r)/ (UK)| n. |生物，动物，人|A living being, especially an animal.  Can also refer to a person, often in a specific context.|
The woods are full of strange creatures.  树林里充满了奇怪的生物。

The poor creature was lost and alone.  这可怜的生物又迷路又孤单。


|近义词：animal, being, organism, individual|反义词: - |noun|
