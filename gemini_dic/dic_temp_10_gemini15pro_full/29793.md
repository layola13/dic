caucus:/ˈkɔːkəs/ (US) /ˈkɔːkəs/ (UK)| n. vi.| 核心会议，党团会议，预选会议|A meeting of the members of a political party or other group to discuss policy or to select candidates for office.  A closed meeting of supporters or members of a specific political party or movement.|The Democrats held a caucus to choose their presidential candidate. 民主党召开了一次核心会议来选择他们的总统候选人。|synonyms: meeting, conference, assembly, gathering, convention|antonyms: general meeting, public forum|noun, verb
