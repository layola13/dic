例句：His limitive mindset prevented him from seeing other possibilities. 他限制性的思维方式使他看不到其他的可能性。|近义词：restrictive, confining, limiting, constraining|反义词：expansive, broad, unlimited, unrestricted|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *limitativus, from Latin limitare "to limit," from limes "boundary, limit."
