operatic:/ˌɒpəˈrætɪk/ /ˌɑːpəˈrætɪk/| adj.|歌剧的；夸张的，戏剧性的|Relating to or characteristic of opera; exaggerated and dramatic.|Her operatic performance was over the top. 她的歌剧表演太夸张了。|The singer gave an operatic sigh. 歌手夸张地叹了口气。|近义词：dramatic, theatrical, histrionic|反义词：understated, subtle, restrained|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From opera + -ic.  "Opera" comes from the Italian word "opera," which means "work" or "labor," and is the plural of "opus."
