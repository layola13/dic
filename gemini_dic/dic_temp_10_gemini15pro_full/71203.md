fistuliform:/ˈfɪstjʊlɪfɔːrm/
adjective
瘘管状的；似瘘管的
Resembling a fistula; shaped like a tube or pipe.
The doctor observed a fistuliform tract in the patient's wound. 医生观察到病人伤口处有一条瘘管状的管道。
The infection created a fistuliform opening in the skin. 感染在皮肤上形成了一个瘘管状的开口。
近义词: tubular, pipe-like
反义词: solid, closed
词性: adjective
