nonsecluded:/nɒnsɪˈkluːdɪd/ | adj. | 非隐蔽的，不僻静的 | Not secluded; open or accessible. |  The park was nonsecluded, with paths crisscrossing through it and people everywhere.  这个公园并不僻静，到处都是纵横交错的小路和熙熙攘攘的人群。

The beach, being nonsecluded, was crowded with tourists.  这个海滩不僻静，挤满了游客。 | 近义词：open, public, exposed, accessible | 反义词：secluded, private, hidden, isolated | 词性：形容词

Etymology:
* non-: Prefix meaning "not." From Latin *non*.
* secluded:  From the past participle of *seclude*, from Latin *secludere* "to shut off, separate," from *se-* "apart" + *claudere* "to shut."
