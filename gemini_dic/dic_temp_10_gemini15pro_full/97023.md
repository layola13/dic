infecters:/ɪnˈfɛktərz/
n.|感染者；传播者|People or things that transmit a disease or contaminate others.  Those who spread infection. |The infecters were quarantined to prevent further spread of the disease. 感染者被隔离以防止疾病进一步传播。|Synonyms: transmitters, carriers, spreaders|Antonyms: healers, curers|noun

infect:/ɪnˈfɛkt/
v.|感染；传染|To contaminate with a disease-causing organism or with a harmful or corrupting influence.|The virus infected the entire computer system. 病毒感染了整个计算机系统。|Synonyms: contaminate, taint, pollute|Antonyms: cleanse, purify, disinfect|verb
