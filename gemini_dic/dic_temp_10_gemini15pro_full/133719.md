nonfrugalness:/ˌnɑːnˈfruːɡəlnəs/ | n. | 不节俭, 奢侈, 浪费 | Lack of frugality; the quality or state of being nonfrugal.  It describes spending habits that are characterized by extravagance or a lack of thriftiness. | His nonfrugalness led him into debt. 他的不节俭让他负债累累。 |  Extravagance, lavishness, prodigality, spendthriftiness | Frugality, thrift, economy, prudence | noun
