congestive:/kənˈdʒɛstɪv/
adjective
充血的
Characterized by congestion; causing or relating to an excessive accumulation of blood or other fluid in a body part or blood vessel.
She was diagnosed with congestive heart failure. 她被诊断为充血性心力衰竭。
The patient experienced congestive headaches due to sinus pressure.  病人由于鼻窦压力而感到充血性头痛。
Synonyms: blocked, obstructed, overcrowded
Antonyms: clear, unobstructed, free-flowing
word class: adjective
Etymology: From Late Latin *congestīvus, from Latin congestus (past participle of congerō, “heap together”).
