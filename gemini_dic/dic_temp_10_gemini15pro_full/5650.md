altitudes:/ˈæltɪtjuːdz/
n.
海拔高度；高地；高处；(等级、地位等)高端
the height of an object or point in relation to sea level or ground level.  It can also refer to a high level or position.
The plane flew at an altitude of 30,000 feet.  飞机在30,000英尺的高度飞行。
Climbers must acclimatize to high altitudes.  登山者必须适应高海拔。
elevations, heights
depths, lowlands
noun
