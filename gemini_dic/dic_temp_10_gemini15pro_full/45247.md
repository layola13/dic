culter:/ˈkʌltər/| n. |刀,犁刀,耕刀;切刀|A part of a plow or other agricultural implement that cuts the soil.|The farmer used a new culter on his plow.|近义词:blade, knife|反义词:blunt (adj.)|词性:noun

Etymology: From Latin *culter* "knife, plowshare," from *colere* "to till, cultivate."
