impromptuary: /ɪmˈprɒmptʃʊəri/ /ɪmˈprɑːmptʃʊeri/
adjective
即兴的；无准备的
done without being planned, prepared, or rehearsed.
She gave an impromptuary performance at the party.  她在聚会上即兴表演了一段。
Near synonyms:  impromptu, extemporaneous, off-the-cuff, ad-lib, unrehearsed, improvised
Antonyms: planned, prepared, rehearsed
词性: adjective
