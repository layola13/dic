featherers:/ˈfeðərərz/
n.
羽毛匠; 装羽毛的人; 羽毛工; (鸟类的)长羽毛者
People who work with feathers, especially in making or repairing things decorated with feathers.  Also, birds that have long feathers.
The featherers were busy preparing the costumes for the carnival.  羽毛匠们正忙着为嘉年华准备服装。
Some birds are known as strong featherers because of their long tail feathers. 一些鸟类因其长尾羽而被称为强壮的长羽毛者。
近义词:plume makers, fletchers
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "plucker" in the context of removing feathers)
词性: Noun
