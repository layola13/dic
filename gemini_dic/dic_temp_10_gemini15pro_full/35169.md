clownishness:/ˈklaʊnɪʃnəs/
noun
滑稽；小丑行为；愚蠢
The quality of behaving in a silly or exaggerated way, like a clown.
His clownishness embarrassed his family at the formal dinner. 他的滑稽行为让他的家人在正式晚宴上感到尴尬。
Synonyms: buffoonery, silliness, foolishness, absurdity
Antonyms: seriousness, solemnity, dignity
词性: 名词
