literarinesses:/ˌlɪtəˈrɛrɪnɪsɪz/
n.
文学性（复数）
The quality or state of being literary; literary character or style.
The literarinesses of the different poems varied greatly, from highly stylized verse to more colloquial free form. 这些不同诗歌的文学性差异很大，从高度程式化的诗句到更口语化的自由体都有。
n.
literary qualities
The text displays many literarinesses, such as intricate metaphors and allusions to classical mythology. 这篇文章展现出许多文学性，例如复杂的隐喻和对古典神话的典故。

近义词: artistry, refinement, sophistication
反义词: simplicity, artlessness, plainness
词性: noun (plural)

词源:  From literariness + -es (plural suffix). Literariness is derived from literary (meaning "pertaining to literature") + -ness (a suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality).  Ultimately from Latin *litterarius* "of letters," from *littera* "letter."
