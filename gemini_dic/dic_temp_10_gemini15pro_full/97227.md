inflations:/ɪnˈfleɪʃənz/| n.|通货膨胀（复数）|A general increase in prices and fall in the purchasing value of money.|例句：The government is trying to control inflations. 政府正在努力控制通货膨胀。|近义词：price increases, rising prices, price hikes|反义词:deflation, disinflation|词性:名词

inflations:/ɪnˈfleɪʃənz/| n.|膨胀；扩大（复数）|The action of inflating something or the condition of being inflated.|例句：Inflations of the currency have damaged the economy.  货币膨胀损害了经济。|近义词：expansions, enlargements, swellings|反义词:contractions, deflations|词性:名词
