extends:/ɪkˈstɛndz/| v.| 延伸，扩展，延展；给予，提供；包括，涵盖| To stretch out or lengthen in space or time; to offer or give something to someone; to include or encompass something within its range. |例句：The road extends for miles. 这条路延伸数英里。

例句：The company extended its business into new markets.  公司将业务扩展到新的市场。|近义词：expand, prolong, lengthen, stretch, offer, grant|反义词：contract, shorten, limit, restrict|词性:动词
