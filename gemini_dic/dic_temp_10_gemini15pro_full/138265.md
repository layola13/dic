not turn a hair:/nɒt tɜːn ə heə(r)/ /nɑːt tɜːrn ə her/ | idiom |  不为所动；面不改色；毫不动容 | to show no reaction to something surprising or shocking; to remain calm and unruffled |  The explosion was so loud, but she didn't turn a hair. 爆炸声震耳欲聋，但她却面不改色。 | Synonyms: not bat an eyelid, keep one's cool, remain calm, stay composed | Antonyms: panic, freak out, lose one's composure | idiom
