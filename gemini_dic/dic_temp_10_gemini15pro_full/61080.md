ellipticalness:/ɪˈlɪptɪkəlnəs/ | n. |椭圆度，椭圆性|The quality or state of being elliptical.  The deviation of an ellipse or orbit from circularity. |The ellipticalness of the planet's orbit is very slight.  这颗行星轨道的椭圆度很小。|Near synonyms: ellipticity, eccentricity |Near antonyms: circularity, roundness |noun

ellipticalness:/ɪˈlɪptɪkəlnəs/ | n. |椭圆度，椭圆性|The degree to which something is elliptical.  A measure of how much an ellipse deviates from a perfect circle.|The comet's ellipticalness made it difficult to predict its trajectory. 这颗彗星的椭圆度使得难以预测它的轨迹。|Near synonyms: ovalness, oblongness |Near antonyms: sphericity, roundness |noun
