consigning:/kənˈsaɪnɪŋ/
verb
交付；托运；寄售
delivering something to a person's custody, typically in order for it to be sold
The farmer is consigning his produce to the local market. 这位农民正在把他的农产品运到当地市场。
synonyms: delivering, entrusting, handing over, transferring
antonyms: receiving, accepting, withholding, retaining
verb

consign:/kənˈsaɪn/
verb
交付；托运；寄售；打发；使…处于（不愉快的境地）
deliver something to a person's custody, typically in order for it to be sold; assign; commit decisively or permanently
She consigned the letter to the flames. 她把信投入火中。
synonyms: deliver, entrust, hand over, transfer, assign, commit
antonyms: receive, accept, withhold, retain
