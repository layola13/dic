equiradical:/ˌiːkwɪˈrædɪkəl/ | adj. | 等根的；同根的 | Having the same root or radical.  Relating to or denoting radicals or roots that are the same. |  The two equations are equiradical, meaning they have the same roots. 这两个方程是等根的，意味着它们有相同的根。|  The equiradical expressions simplified to the same value.  这些等根表达式简化为相同的值。 | synonymous:  same root, identical roots | antonymous: different roots, distinct roots | adjective
