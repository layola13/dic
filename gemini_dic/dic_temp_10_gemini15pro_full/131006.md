nonattributive:/ˌnɒnəˈtrɪbjʊtɪv/
adjective
非定语的，不能作定语的
A nonattributive adjective cannot be used before a noun to describe it.
This adjective is nonattributive and cannot be used before the noun.  这个形容词是非定语的，不能用在名词前面。
The word "asleep" is a nonattributive adjective; you can't say "an asleep child". “asleep”这个词是非定语形容词；你不能说“an asleep child”。
Synonym: predicative
Antonym: attributive
词性: adjective
