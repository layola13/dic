heuristically:/ˌhʊərˈɪstɪkli/ /ˌhjʊərˈɪstɪkli/| adv.|启发式地；凭经验地|Using or obtained by a heuristic method; proceeding to a solution by trial and error or by rules that are only loosely defined.|Example: The problem was solved heuristically, by trying different approaches until one worked.  这个问题是通过启发式方法解决的，尝试不同的方法直到找到一个有效的方法。

Example:  Heuristically, we can assume that the larger the city, the higher the crime rate. 凭经验，我们可以假设城市越大，犯罪率越高。|近义词：empirically, experimentally, practically|反义词：analytically, systematically, rigorously|词性:副词

词源: From Greek *heuriskein* "to find, discover."  来自希腊语 *heuriskein* “找到，发现”。
