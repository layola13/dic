|近义词：paracetamol (especially in UK), analgesic, antipyretic|反义词: N/A|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From a condensation of the chemical name *para-acetylaminophenol*.  *Para-* indicates the position of a particular group on the benzene ring, *acetyl-* refers to the acetyl group (CH3CO), *amino-* refers to the amino group (NH2), and *phenol* refers to the phenolic ring structure.
