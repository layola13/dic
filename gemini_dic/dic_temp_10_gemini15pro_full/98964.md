inter-influence:/ˌɪntərˈɪnfluəns/ | n. |相互影响 |The reciprocal action of two or more things on each other. |The inter-influence of supply and demand determines market prices. 供求之间的相互影响决定了市场价格。|  近义词：interaction, reciprocity, interplay | 反义词：independence, isolation | 词性: 名词

inter-influence:/ˌɪntərˈɪnfluəns/ | v. |相互影响 |To affect or be affected by something reciprocally. |Social media platforms inter-influence the way people communicate and form opinions. 社交媒体平台相互影响人们沟通和形成观点的方式。|近义词：interact, affect, impact | 反义词：isolate, separate | 词性: 动词
