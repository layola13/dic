overpenalizing: /ˌoʊvərˈpiːnəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度惩罚
Punishing someone too harshly or excessively for a particular offense.
The referee was accused of overpenalizing the visiting team.  裁判被指责对客队过度处罚。
The new law overpenalizes minor traffic violations.  新法律对轻微的交通违规处罚过重。
Synonyms: overpunishing, oversanctioning
Antonyms: underpenalizing, underpunishing
