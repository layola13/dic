menders:/ˈmɛndərz/| n. |修理工，修补者|People who mend things; those who repair or fix something.|The menders worked tirelessly to repair the damaged bridge. 修理工们不知疲倦地修补被毁坏的桥梁。|近义词：repairers, fixers, restorers|反义词：breakers, destroyers|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *mender*, from the verb *menden* "to mend," from Old English *gemendan*, from Proto-Germanic *gamundjaną* "to protect, take care of," from Proto-Indo-European *(s)mendʰ- "to improve, strengthen, make firm."*
