malfeasance:/ˌmælˈfiːzəns/
noun
渎职；不法行为；不正当行为
wrongdoing, especially by a public official
Several cases of malfeasance in the police department are currently under investigation. 警察部门的几起渎职案件目前正在调查中。


SYNONYM->
misconduct, wrongdoing, misbehavior, impropriety, corruption, abuse of power

ANTONYM->
lawful behavior, proper conduct, ethical behavior

ETYMOLOGY->
Late Middle English: from Old French malfaisance, from malfaisant ‘misdoing’, from mal- ‘badly’ + faisant ‘doing’, present participle of faire ‘to do’.
