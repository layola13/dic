hildred:/ˈhɪldrəd/ | n. | 家人；子孙；家族 | A household, family, or offspring; a company or assembly of people. |  The king greeted the hildred gathered in the great hall. 国王向聚集在大厅里的家人致意。| synonyms: family, kindred, offspring, progeny, household | antonyms: none | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hildrǣden "a company of warriors," from hild "battle, war" + rǣden "condition, state, law."  The meaning shifted over time from a war band to a household or family.
