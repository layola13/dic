anticness:/ˈæntɪknəs/
noun
古怪；滑稽；可笑
The quality of being old-fashioned or peculiar in an amusing way.
Her anticness made everyone laugh. 她的滑稽行为让每个人都笑了。
Synonyms: whimsicality, capriciousness, eccentricity, peculiarity, quirkiness
Antonyms: seriousness, normalcy, conventionality
