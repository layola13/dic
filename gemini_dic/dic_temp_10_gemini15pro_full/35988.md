codesigned:/ˌkoʊdˈsaɪnd/ | adj. | 共同签署的，代码签名的 |  Digitally signed by multiple parties, usually involving a developer and an authority like Apple. This ensures the software is from a verified source and hasn't been tampered with. | The codesigned app ran without any security warnings. 这个共同签署的应用程序运行时没有任何安全警告。 | Synonym: digitally signed, verified | Antonym: unsigned, unverified | adjective

codesigned:/ˌkoʊdˈsaɪnd/ | v. | 共同签署 | Past participle of "codesign," meaning to digitally sign software with multiple signatures. | The application was codesigned by the developer and Apple.  这个应用程序由开发者和苹果共同签署。 | Synonym: digitally signed, verified | Antonym: unsigned, unverified | verb
