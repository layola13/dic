fusile:/ˈfjuːsaɪl/ /ˈfjuːzɪl/ | adj. | 可熔的；易熔的；液化的 | Capable of being melted or liquefied by heat; molten.  Also, capable of being fused or joined together by melting. |  Lead is a more fusible metal than iron. 铅是一种比铁更容易熔化的金属。 | Synonyms: meltable, liquefiable, molten | Antonyms: infusible, refractory | Adjective

fusile:/ˈfjuːsaɪl/ /ˈfjuːzɪl/ | adj. | (of rock) formed by fusion; igneous. | Formed by the solidification of molten rock. |  Granite is a fusible rock. 花岗岩是一种由熔融岩石形成的岩石。| Synonyms: igneous, volcanic | Antonyms: sedimentary, metamorphic | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *fusilius, from Latin fusus, past participle of fundere “to pour, melt.”
