circumstantiate: /sərˈkʌmstænʃieɪt/ | vt. | 证实；用事实说明 | To provide detailed information about something or to support a claim with evidence. | The lawyer asked the witness to circumstantiate his claim. 律师要求证人提供证据证实他的说法。

The police needed more evidence to circumstantiate the charges against the suspect. 警方需要更多证据来证实对嫌疑人的指控。| 近义词：verify, confirm, corroborate, substantiate, prove | 反义词：disprove, refute, invalidate | 词性: 动词
