disaffirmation:/ˌdɪsəfərˈmeɪʃən/
n.
否认，废除，拒绝承认
The act of denying or rejecting something, especially a contract or agreement.
The minor's disaffirmation of the contract rendered it void.  未成年人对合同的否认使其无效。
The court upheld the disaffirmation of the agreement. 法院支持废除该协议。
近义词：repudiation, rejection, denial, revocation
反义词：affirmation, confirmation, ratification
词性：名词

disaffirm:/ˌdɪsəˈfɜːrm/
v.
否认，废除，拒绝承认
To deny or reject something, especially a contract or agreement.
He disaffirmed the contract as soon as he turned 18. 他一满18岁就否认了这份合同。
She disaffirmed her previous statements. 她否认了她之前的陈述。
近义词：repudiate, reject, deny, revoke
反义词：affirm, confirm, ratify
词性：动词
