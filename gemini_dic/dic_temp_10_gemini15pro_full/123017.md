monastically:/məˈnæstɪkli/ | adv. | 修道院般地；禁欲地；简朴地 | In a manner resembling or suggestive of a monk or monastery; characterized by seclusion, asceticism, or contemplation. | He lived monastically, dedicating his life to prayer and study. 他过着修道院般的生活，将一生奉献给祈祷和学习。|  She ate monastically, consuming only simple, unprocessed foods. 她吃得很简朴，只吃简单的、未经加工的食物。 | 近义词: ascetically, austerely, simply, plainly | 反义词: extravagantly, luxuriously, hedonistically | 词性: 副词

词源: From Late Latin *monasticālis, from Latin monasticus (“monastic”), from monachus (“monk”). Ultimately from Ancient Greek μοναχός (monachós, “solitary, single”).
