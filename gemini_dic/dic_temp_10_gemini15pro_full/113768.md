macroprism:/ˈmækroʊˌprɪzəm/| n. |宏棱镜；巨棱镜|A large prism, often used in optical instruments or for creating spectacular visual effects.  It combines the prefixes "macro-" (meaning large) with "prism" (a transparent optical element).|The macroprism scattered the laser beam into a rainbow of colors. 巨棱镜将激光束散射成彩虹般的颜色。

The photographer used a macroprism to create a unique distorted effect in his portrait. 摄影师用一个宏棱镜在他的肖像作品中创造了一种独特的扭曲效果。|近义词:large prism, giant prism|反义词:microprism|词性:noun
