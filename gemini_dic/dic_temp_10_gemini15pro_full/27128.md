calippic: /kəˈlɪpɪk/ | adj. | 讽刺的，挖苦的 | A calippic is a bitter, vehement verbal attack, especially one delivered publicly.  It often involves elaborate and sustained invective. |  His speech was a calippic against the current administration. 他的演讲是对现任政府的猛烈抨击。|  The disgruntled employee launched into a calippic against his boss.  心怀不满的员工对他的老板发起了一场猛烈的抨击。 | Synonyms: diatribe, tirade, invective, denunciation, harangue | Antonyms: eulogy, panegyric, encomium | adjective
