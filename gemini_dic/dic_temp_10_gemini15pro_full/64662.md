escalates:/ˈɛskəleɪts/| v.|升级，扩大，加剧|Third-person singular present tense form of "escalate," meaning to increase or become more intense, especially of a conflict, problem, or situation.|He escalates the situation every time he opens his mouth. 他每次开口都让情况恶化。|Near Synonyms:  intensifies,  aggravates,  heightens,  amplifies|Near Antonyms: de-escalates, decreases, diminishes, reduces|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *scalare* "to climb," from *scala* "ladder." The prefix *e-* (ex-) adds the sense of "out" or "up."
