frizz:/frɪz/| n. v. |卷发，毛躁；使卷曲|a mass of small, tight curls; make (something, especially hair) frizzy|The humidity gave me a frizz. 潮湿的天气让我的头发毛躁。|近义词：curl, kink, crinkle|反义词: straighten, smooth|词性:名词, 动词

词源:  From French friser "to curl, crisp," from Old French friser "to decorate with a border."
