nonresolution:/ˌnɒnˌrɛzəˈluːʃən/
n.
lack of resolution; failure to resolve an issue
The nonresolution of the conflict led to further tensions.  这个冲突未得到解决导致了进一步的紧张局势。
The committee's nonresolution on the budget was a major setback. 委员会未能就预算问题作出决议是一个重大挫折。
lack of determination; indecision
His nonresolution about his career path was frustrating. 他对职业道路的优柔寡断令人沮丧。
Her nonresolution in the face of the challenge was surprising. 面对挑战，她的犹豫不决令人惊讶。
near synonyms: indecision, irresolution, inaction, standstill, stalemate
antonyms: resolution, decision, determination, action
noun
