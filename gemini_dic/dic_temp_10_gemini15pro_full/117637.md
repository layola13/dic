megastructural:/ˌmɛɡəˈstrʌktʃərəl/ | adj. | 巨型结构的，特大结构的 | Relating to a megastructure, an extremely large artificial object. |  The megastructural engineering feat was unprecedented.  这项巨型结构工程壮举是史无前例的。 | The Dyson sphere is a hypothetical megastructural concept. 戴森球是一个假设的巨型结构概念。 |  massive, enormous, gigantic | small, tiny, miniature | adjective

Etymology:
mega- (prefix): from Greek *megas "great"
structural (adjective): from structure + -al
