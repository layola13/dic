marls:/mɑːrlz/
noun
泥灰岩；泥灰土
A calcareous clay rock, or mixture of clay and calcium carbonate, used especially as a fertilizer.
The farmer spread marls on his fields to improve the soil. 农民在田里撒了泥灰岩来改良土壤。
synonyms:clay, loam, soil
antonyms:sand, gravel
n.

marl:/mɑːrl/
noun
泥灰岩；泥灰土
A calcareous clay rock, or mixture of clay and calcium carbonate, used especially as a fertilizer.
The marl provided essential nutrients for the crops. 泥灰岩为农作物提供了必要的营养。
synonyms:clay, loam, soil
antonyms:sand, gravel
n.

marl:/mɑːrl/
verb
用泥灰土施肥
To fertilize with marl.
He marled the field to prepare it for planting. 他用泥灰土给田地施肥，为播种做准备。
synonyms:fertilize, enrich, amend
antonyms:deplete, impoverish
v.
