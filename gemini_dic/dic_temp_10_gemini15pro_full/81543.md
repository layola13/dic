graybeard:/ˈɡreɪˌbɪərd/| n.|灰胡子老人，经验丰富的人|An old man, especially one with a gray beard; a veteran.|The graybeards of the village council offered their wisdom.|近义词：veteran, old-timer, elder|反义词:youth, youngster|noun


Etymology->
From gray + beard, literally "gray beard."  The connection between age, a gray beard, and wisdom/experience is ancient and widespread across cultures.
