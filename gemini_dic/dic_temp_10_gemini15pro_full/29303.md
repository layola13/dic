casteism:/ˈkɑːstɪzəm/ /ˈkæstɪzəm/
n.
种姓制度;种姓歧视
Prejudice or discrimination on the basis of caste.

Casteism is a major social issue in some parts of the world.
在世界某些地区，种姓制度是一个主要的社会问题。

The government has implemented new laws to combat casteism.
政府已经实施了新的法律来打击种姓歧视。

近义词:discrimination, prejudice, bigotry, bias
反义词:equality, tolerance, acceptance

词性:noun
