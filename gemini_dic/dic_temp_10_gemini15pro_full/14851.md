axmen:/ˈæksmən/| n. |伐木工人；斧头帮|A group of men who work with axes, especially to fell trees. |The axmen cleared the forest for the new road. 伐木工人清理了森林，为新路腾出空间。| The axmen competed in a wood-chopping contest. 伐木工人参加了伐木比赛。|近义词：lumberjack, woodcutter, logger |反义词: None |Noun
