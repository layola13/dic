nonmilitant:/ˌnɒnˈmɪlɪtənt/ /ˌnɑːnˈmɪlɪtənt/
adjective
非好战的; 非战斗性的
Not militant; not inclined to fight or engage in war.
The group took a nonmilitant approach to protesting.  这个团体采取了非暴力抗议的方式。
The monks followed a nonmilitant philosophy. 这些僧侣信奉非战斗的哲学。
Synonyms: peaceful, pacifistic, nonaggressive, nonviolent, dovish
Antonyms: militant, aggressive, belligerent, warlike
adjective
