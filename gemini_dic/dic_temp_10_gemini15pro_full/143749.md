outmanipulates: /ˌaʊtməˈnɪpjuleɪts/ | v. | 巧妙地胜过，以策略取胜 | to defeat or gain advantage over someone by using more skill or cunning in manipulating or controlling a situation |  She outmanipulated her opponents in the negotiations and secured a favorable deal. 她通过谈判中更巧妙的策略胜过对手，达成了有利的协议。 | Example: He outmanipulated his rivals in the business world. 他在商界以智取胜，击败了竞争对手。| Synonyms: outmaneuver, outsmart, outwit | Antonyms: be outmaneuvered, be outsmarted, be outwitted | Verb
