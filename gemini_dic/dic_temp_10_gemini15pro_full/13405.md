atomistic:/ˌætəˈmɪstɪk/ | adj. |原子论的；原子式的；个人主义的|Relating to or based on the theory of atomism; composed of many separate, often disparate elements; prioritizing individual rights and self-reliance over collective needs or social responsibility. |例句：An atomistic view of society emphasizes individual freedom and self-reliance.  原子论的社会观强调个人自由和自力更生。

例句：The company adopted an atomistic approach to marketing, targeting individual consumers rather than broad demographic groups.  这家公司采取了原子化的营销方式，针对个人消费者而不是广泛的人口群体。|近义词：individualistic, fragmented, particulate, discrete|反义词：holistic, collective, unified|词性:形容词
