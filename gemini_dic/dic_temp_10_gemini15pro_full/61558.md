emend:/ɪˈmend/| vt.| 修改；修订；改进|To correct or improve a text, typically a literary work, by removing errors or making alterations.|The editor emended the manuscript before publication. 编辑在出版前修订了手稿。|近义词：correct, revise, amend, improve, rectify|反义词：corrupt, worsen, damage|verb

emendation:/ˌiːmenˈdeɪʃn/| n.| 修改；修订；改进|The act of correcting or improving a text.|The emendations to the text were made with great care.  对文本的修改非常谨慎。|近义词：correction, revision, amendment, improvement|反义词：corruption, damage|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ēmendāre, from Latin ēmendāre (“to remove faults, correct”), from ex- (“out, from”) + mendum (“fault”).
