coating:/ˈkoʊtɪŋ/| n.|涂层，覆盖物|A layer of a substance spread over a surface.|The car has a new coating of paint. 这辆车有一层新的油漆。|近义词：layer, film, covering|反义词:uncoating, bareness|noun

coating:/ˈkoʊtɪŋ/| n.|外衣，外套|An outer garment.|The dog has a thick winter coating. 这只狗有一件厚厚的冬衣。|近义词：coat, jacket|反义词:lining|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English cotyng, from cote (“coat”) + -ing. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *kotōn (“coarse cloth, coat”), from Proto-Indo-European *gʷṓdʰ-. Cognate with Old Frisian kote (“cottage, hut”), Dutch kot (“coat, tunic, cage, sty”), German Koth (“dirt, mud, excrement”).
