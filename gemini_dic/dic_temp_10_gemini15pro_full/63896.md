epinicial:/ˌɛpɪˈnɪʃəl/
adjective
附加的，写在后面的
added or written at the end
The epinicial note clarified the author's intent. 附加的注释阐明了作者的意图。
Epinicial information was added to the document after the initial draft. 初稿之后，文件中添加了附加信息。
synonyms: appended, supplementary, additional
antonyms: original, initial, primary
词性: 形容词

词源: From Greek *epi- "upon, in addition" + Latin *initialis* "of or pertaining to a beginning."
