linebackers:/ˈlaɪnbækərz/| n.|线卫|A defensive position in American football.  Linebackers typically play behind the defensive linemen and are responsible for tackling ball carriers, covering receivers, and blitzing the quarterback.|The team's linebackers are known for their aggressive playing style.  这支球队的线卫以其侵略性的打法而闻名。|近义词: defenders|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps offensively: quarterbacks, running backs)|词性: Noun
