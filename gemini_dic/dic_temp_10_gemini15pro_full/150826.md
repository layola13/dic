parageosynclinal:/ˌpærəˌdʒiːoʊˈsɪŋklɪnəl/
adjective|准地槽的|Relating to a parageosyncline, a sedimentary basin that develops adjacent to a geosyncline and is characterized by less intense deformation and metamorphism.|The parageosynclinal sediments were less deformed than those in the adjacent geosyncline.|近义词:miogeosynclinal|反义词: eugeosynclinal|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From *para-* (beside, alongside) + *geosyncline*.  *Geosyncline* comes from *geo-* (earth) + *syncline* (a trough or fold of stratified rock).
