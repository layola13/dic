insusceptible:/ˌɪnsəˈsɛptəbəl/
adjective
不易受影响的；无感觉的；不敏感的
Not susceptible; incapable of being affected, influenced, or moved.
He seems insusceptible to flattery. 他似乎对奉承无动于衷。
insusceptible to disease 不易感染疾病
近义词:impervious, resistant, immune
反义词:susceptible, vulnerable, sensitive
词性:adjective

词源:
Late 16th century: from in- ‘not’ + susceptible.
