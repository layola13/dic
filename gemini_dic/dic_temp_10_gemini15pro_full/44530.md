crosstown:/ˈkrɔːstaʊn/ (US) /ˈkrɒstaʊn/ (UK)| adj. adv.|跨城的，横穿城市的；|Relating to, or traveling across a city.  Going from one side of a city to the other.|The crosstown bus was packed during rush hour.  跨城公交车在高峰时间挤满了人。|Synonyms:  citywide, urban, intercity|Antonyms:  local, neighborhood, intracity|adjective, adverb
