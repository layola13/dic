panhellenism:/ˌpænˈhɛlɪnɪzəm/
|n.|泛希腊主义，希腊各邦统一运动|
|A political and cultural movement advocating the unity of all Greeks.|
|The Olympic Games were an early expression of panhellenism.| 奥林匹克运动会是泛希腊主义的早期体现。

|The rise of panhellenism led to several attempts to unite the Greek city-states.| 泛希腊主义的兴起导致了几次统一希腊城邦的尝试。
|Synonyms: unity, solidarity, unification|
|Antonyms: division, disunity, fragmentation|
|noun|
