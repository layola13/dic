hyperingenuities:/ˌhaɪpərɪndʒɪˈnjuːɪtiz/
n.
超级聪明; 过度精巧的设计; 过度的创造力
Excessive cleverness or ingenuity, often to the point of being impractical or needlessly complicated.
The inventor's hyperingenuities, while impressive, ultimately made the device too complex for the average consumer.  发明家的超级聪明，虽然令人印象深刻，但最终使该设备对普通消费者来说过于复杂。
近义词: over-elaborations, over-ingenuity, excessive cleverness
反义词: simplicity, practicality, straightforwardness
词性: noun


Etymology:
"hyper-" (Greek): over, excessive
"ingenuity" (Latin): ingenium - natural talent, ability, cleverness
