commitments:/kəˈmɪtmənts/
n.
承诺，保证；义务，责任；投入，花费；信仰，信念
an agreement or pledge to do something; an obligation or responsibility; the state or quality of being dedicated to a cause, activity, etc.; funds or resources allocated to a particular purpose
I have too many commitments to attend another meeting. 我有太多的事情要做，不能再参加另一个会议了。
近义词：obligations, pledges, duties, responsibilities, engagements
反义词：freedom, release
