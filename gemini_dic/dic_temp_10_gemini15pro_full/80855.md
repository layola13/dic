gourmand: /ˈɡʊərmɑːnd/ (US) /ˈɡɔːmənd/ (UK) | n. | 美食家；贪吃的人 | A person who enjoys eating and drinking, especially to excess; a glutton. | He's a gourmand who enjoys fine dining experiences. 他是一位喜欢精致餐饮体验的美食家。|  My uncle is a gourmand; he's always trying new restaurants and dishes.  我叔叔是个美食家；他总是尝试新的餐馆和菜肴。 | Synonyms: epicure, gourmet, glutton, connoisseur | Antonyms: ascetic, abstainer | noun
