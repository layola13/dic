monotocous: /ˌmɒnəˈtəʊkəs/ /ˌmɑːnəˈtoʊkəs/
adjective
单胎的
Producing usually a single offspring at a birth.
例句：Humans are typically monotocous, giving birth to one child at a time.  人类通常是单胎的，一次生育一个孩子。

例句：Unlike dogs, which have litters, sheep are monotocous mammals.  不像狗一窝生好几只，绵羊是单胎哺乳动物。
近义词：uniparous
反义词：polytocous
词性：adjective
