noncertitude:/ˌnɒnsɜːrˈtɪtjuːd/
n.
不确定性，无把握
lack of certainty; doubt
The noncertitude of the situation made it difficult to plan ahead.  情况的不确定性使得难以提前计划。
近义词：uncertainty, doubt, ambiguity, vagueness
反义词：certainty, assurance, conviction
