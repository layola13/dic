nonimpairments:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpeəmənts/ | n. | 无损伤；无损害；未受损害的状态 |  The absence of impairments or disabilities; the state of being free from any physical, mental, or sensory limitations.  It refers to the aspects of functioning that are *not* impaired.  | The report focused on the individual's nonimpairments and strengths, rather than solely on their limitations.  这份报告重点关注个人的未受损害的功能和优势，而不是仅仅关注他们的局限性。

The therapist assessed the patient's nonimpairments to develop a personalized rehabilitation plan. 治疗师评估了患者的未受损害的功能，以制定个性化的康复计划。 | 近义词: abilities, capabilities, strengths, functionalities | 反义词: impairments, disabilities, limitations, deficits | 词性: 名词

词源:  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "impairment" comes from the Old French "empeirer" meaning "to make worse."
