imprints:/ˈɪmprɪnts/| n. |印记，痕迹；影响，烙印|A mark made by pressing something onto a softer substance; a lasting effect or influence.|The horse's hooves left deep imprints in the mud. 马的蹄子在泥里留下了深深的印记。

The experience left a lasting imprint on his memory. 这段经历在他的记忆中留下了持久的烙印。|近义词：marks, impressions, traces, effects, influences|反义词:  erasures, deletions|noun

imprint:/ɪmˈprɪnt/| vt. |压印，刻印；铭刻，使铭记|To mark or impress something onto a surface; to fix something firmly in the mind.|The publisher's logo was imprinted on the cover of the book. 出版商的标志印在书的封面上。

The image of the accident was imprinted on her mind. 事故的画面深深地印在了她的脑海里。|近义词：impress, stamp, engrave, fix, instill|反义词: erase, forget|verb
