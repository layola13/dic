melancholies:/ˈmɛləŋkɒliz/ (UK) /ˌmɛlənˈkɑːliz/ (US)| n.|忧郁，悲哀，愁绪|A state of pensive sadness, typically with no obvious cause.  A feeling of thoughtful sadness, usually without any clear reason.|She suffered from deep melancholies after the death of her husband. 她丈夫去世后，她陷入了深深的忧郁之中。|Synonyms: sadness, gloom, depression, dejection, wistfulness|Antonyms: happiness, joy, elation, cheerfulness|noun
