break-down:/ˈbreɪkdaʊn/| n. |故障，崩溃，分解，分类，衰弱|A failure to function or continue normally.  The separation of something into its constituent parts. |The car suffered a breakdown on the motorway. 这辆车在高速公路上抛锚了。|synonyms: failure, malfunction, collapse, decomposition, analysis |antonyms: success, repair, construction, synthesis, strengthening| noun
break down:/breɪk daʊn/| v. |分解，发生故障，失败，崩溃，（情绪）失控|To stop functioning. To separate something into its constituent parts. To lose control emotionally. |The negotiations broke down. 谈判破裂了。|synonyms: fail, malfunction, collapse, decompose, analyze, cry |antonyms: succeed, repair, construct, synthesize, strengthen| verb
