allosauruses:/ˌæləˈsɔːrəsəz/ |n.|异龙（复数）|A large carnivorous dinosaur that lived during the Late Jurassic period.  It is characterized by its powerful legs, short arms, and three-fingered hands.|Example 1:  The allosauruses hunted large herbivores like stegosaurus. 异龙捕食像剑龙这样的大型食草动物。

Example 2: The museum exhibit featured a complete skeleton of one of the largest allosauruses ever discovered.  博物馆展览展出了一个迄今为止发现的最大的异龙之一的完整骨架。|近义词:carnosaurs (肉食龙), theropods (兽脚亚目恐龙)|反义词:N/A (No direct antonym for a specific dinosaur type)|词性:Noun


词源: From Greek *allos* "other" + *sauros* "lizard."
