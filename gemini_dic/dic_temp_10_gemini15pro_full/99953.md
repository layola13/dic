interobservers:/ˌɪntərəbˈzɜːrvərz/ | n. | 观察者间 |  People who observe the same phenomenon or event.  It typically refers to the situation where multiple observers independently record data and their observations are compared to assess the degree of agreement or reliability. | The interobservers recorded the children's behavior during playtime. 观察员记录了孩子们在游戏时间内的行为。 |  observers, raters, assessors | N/A | noun
