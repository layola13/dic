chillest:/ˈtʃɪlɪst/| adj.|最冷静的，最放松的|Superlative form of chill.  Meaning extremely relaxed, laid-back, and not easily upset.| That beach is the chillest spot I've ever been to. 那个海滩是我去过的最放松的地方。|Synonyms: calmest, coolest, most relaxed|Antonyms: most stressed, most anxious, most uptight|adjective
