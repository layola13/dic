defencelessness:/dɪˈfɛnsləsnəs/| n. |无助，无力自卫，毫无防御能力|The state of being unable to defend oneself.  This can refer to physical vulnerability, emotional fragility, or a lack of resources or support.|例句：The defencelessness of the refugees was heartbreaking.  难民的无助令人心碎。

例句：The small animal showed utter defencelessness against the predator. 小动物对捕食者表现出完全的无力自卫。|近义词：vulnerability, helplessness, weakness, impotence, exposure|反义词:strength, power, security, protection, invulnerability|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From defenceless + -ness.  Defenceless comes from defence (from Old French defense) + -less. Ultimately derived from Latin defendere "to ward off."
