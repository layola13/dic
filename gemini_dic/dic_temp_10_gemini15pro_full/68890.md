fast-forwarding:/ˌfæstˈfɔːrwərdɪŋ/ | n. | 快进，快速前进 | the act of advancing quickly through a recording or program |  I hate the commercials, so I'm always fast-forwarding through them. 我讨厌广告，所以我总是快进它们。| skipping, advancing | rewinding, reversing | noun
