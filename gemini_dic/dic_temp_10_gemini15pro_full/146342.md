overfierce: /ˌoʊvərˈfɪərs/ | adj. | 过于凶猛的，过分激烈的 | Excessively fierce; too intense. |  The competition was overfierce, with some players resorting to dirty tactics.  比赛过于激烈，有些选手 resorted to dirty tactics. | 近义词:  excessive, extreme, immoderate, inordinate, intense, too much, unduly | 反义词: gentle, mild, moderate, temperate | 词性: 形容词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "fierce" (adjective).  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning of "fierce," indicating an excessive or extreme degree of fierceness.
