enormity:/ɪˈnɔːrməti/ | n. | 邪恶;暴行;巨大;极恶;滔天大罪 | An enormity is an act of extreme wickedness, or something that is extremely wicked.  It can also refer to the great size of something, especially something unpleasant. | The enormity of the crime shocked the nation. 这起罪行的滔天罪行震惊了全国。The sheer enormity of the task daunted him. 任务的艰巨性使他感到畏惧。 | synonyms: atrocity, outrage, monstrousness, immensity, vastness | antonyms: triviality, insignificance, smallness | noun

enormities:/ɪˈnɔːrmətiz/ | n. |  (enormity的复数形式) 邪恶行径;暴行;巨大;极恶 | Plural form of enormity, referring to multiple acts of extreme wickedness or things that are extremely wicked. |  The enormities committed during the war will never be forgotten. 战争期间犯下的滔天罪行将永远不会被忘记。He was overwhelmed by the enormities of the universe. 宇宙的浩瀚让他不知所措。| synonyms: atrocities, outrages, monstrousnesses, immensities, vastnesses | antonyms: trivialities, insignificances, smallnesses | noun
