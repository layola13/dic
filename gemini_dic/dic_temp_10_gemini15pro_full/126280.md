mykiss:/ˈmaɪkɪs/ |n.|虹鳟鱼；迈奇斯鲑鱼|A species of trout (Oncorhynchus mykiss) native to tributaries of the Pacific Ocean in Asia and North America. |例句：The mykiss is a popular game fish. 虹鳟鱼是一种受欢迎的游钓鱼。

例句：We caught several mykiss in the river. 我们在河里钓到了几条虹鳟鱼。|近义词：rainbow trout, steelhead (when migrating to the ocean)|反义词: N/A |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Chinook Jargon "mɪ́xɪs," meaning "spotted."  Ultimately from Lower Chinook "mɪ́x," meaning "mark, spot."
