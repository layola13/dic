ft:/fʊt/ (plural feet /fiːt/) | n. | 英尺；脚 | A unit of length equal to 12 inches or 0.3048 meter.  The lower extremity of the leg below the ankle. |  The table is three feet wide.  桌子有三英尺宽。  My feet are cold. 我的脚很冷。 | synonyms: linear unit, base | antonyms: head, top | noun
