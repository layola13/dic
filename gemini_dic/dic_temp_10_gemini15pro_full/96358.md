indirectly:/ˌɪndɪˈrektli/ /ˌɪndaɪˈrektli/| adv.|间接地；迂回地|Not in a forthright or straightforward manner; in a roundabout way.  Implying something rather than stating it directly.|He indirectly criticized the government's policy by praising the opposition's approach. 他通过赞扬反对派的方案间接地批评了政府的政策。|Synonym: circuitously, obliquely, tacitly|Antonym: directly, explicitly|词性:副词
