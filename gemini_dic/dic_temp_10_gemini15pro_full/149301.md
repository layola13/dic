page:/peɪdʒ/| n. vt.|页，页面；侍从，男童；呼叫；|A sheet of paper in a book, or the equivalent online; to contact someone via pager.|She ripped a page from her notebook. 她从笔记本上撕下一页。|  The doctor was paged to the emergency room. 医生被呼叫到急诊室。 |近义词：leaf, sheet, folio (for paper); summon, call, contact (for verb) |反义词: (none for noun); ignore (for verb)| noun, verb
