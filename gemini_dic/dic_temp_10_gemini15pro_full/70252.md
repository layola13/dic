fictionalizes:/ˈfɪkʃənəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使小说化；虚构化
to make into fiction; to treat as fiction
The movie fictionalizes the life of a famous singer.  这部电影将一位著名歌手的生活小说化了。
The author fictionalizes historical events to create a compelling narrative.  作者将历史事件虚构化，以创造引人入胜的叙事。
Synonyms:  novelize, romanticize, dramatize, invent, fabricate
Antonyms:  document, report, factualize
词性: 动词
