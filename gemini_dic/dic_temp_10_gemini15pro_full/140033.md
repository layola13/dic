oenanthylous:/iːˈnænθɪləs/
adjective
庚醇的，庚基的，有酒味的
Having the smell or flavor of wine, specifically reminiscent of oenanthol, a fragrant, colorless liquid found in wine.
The oenanthylous aroma of the aged wine filled the cellar.  陈年葡萄酒的酒香味弥漫了整个酒窖。
The chemist identified an oenanthylous compound in the sample. 化学家在样品中鉴定出一种庚醇化合物。
近义词:vinous, winey
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider using terms like "odorless" or "flavorless" depending on context)
词性:adjective
