overspices: /ˌoʊvərˈspaɪsɪz/
verb
过度调味，加太多香料
To season excessively with spices.
The chef overspiced the dish, making it too hot to eat.  厨师在菜里放了太多的香料，辣得没法吃。
oversalts, overseasons
underseasons
