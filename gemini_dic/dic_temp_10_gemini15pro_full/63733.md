epiblema:/ˌɛpɪˈbliːmə/| n. |外皮层；表皮；（昆虫的）上唇；（书的）护封；（植物的）表皮|The epiblema of the root protects the delicate tissues beneath. 根的表皮保护下面的娇嫩组织。|例句：

The epiblema of this plant is unusually thick.
这种植物的表皮异常厚。

Damage to the epiblema can make the fruit susceptible to rot.
表皮的损伤会使水果容易腐烂。

|近义词：covering, skin, rind, layer|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἐπίβλημα (epíblēma, “something put on, covering”), from ἐπιβάλλω (epibállō, “I put on, apply”), from ἐπί (epí, “on, upon”) + βάλλω (bállō, “I throw, put”).
