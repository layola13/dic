novelistically:/ˌnɒvəlˈɪstɪkli/ /ˌnɑːvəlˈɪstɪkli/
adverb
以小说手法地；像小说那样地
in a way that resembles or suggests a novel; fictionally.
She described the events novelistically, adding dramatic details and emotional depth. 她以小说手法描述了这些事件，添加了戏剧性的细节和情感深度。
The film portrays the historical events novelistically, blending fact with fiction. 这部电影以小说的方式描绘了历史事件，将事实与虚构融合在一起。
Synonyms:  fictionally, imaginatively, creatively, dramatically
Antonyms: factually, realistically, plainly, simply
词性: 副词
