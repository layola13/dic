galvanizer:/ˈɡælvənaɪzər/| n.|镀锌工；电镀器；激励者|A person or thing that galvanizes something or someone.|The news galvanized them into action. 这消息激励他们行动起来。|近义词：motivator, stimulator, energizer|反义词：deterrent, inhibitor|noun

galvanize:/ˈɡælvənaɪz/| vt.|镀锌；激励；刺激|To coat (iron or steel) with zinc to protect it from rust. To shock or excite someone into taking action.|The threat of war galvanized the nation into action. 战争的威胁激励了全国人民采取行动。|近义词：stimulate, energize, arouse, invigorate|反义词：dampen, discourage, subdue|verb
