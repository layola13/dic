nimble:/ˈnɪmbəl/| adj.|敏捷的，灵活的|Quick and light in movement or action; agile.|The nimble fingers of the pianist flew across the keys.  钢琴家灵活的手指在琴键上飞舞。|近义词：agile, quick, deft, swift, spry|反义词：clumsy, awkward, slow|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *nemel*, from Old English *nǣmel* (“capable of catching, quick to grasp”), from Proto-Germanic *nāmilaz* (“able to seize, dexterous”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)nem-/* (“to take, distribute”). Cognate with German *nehmen* (“to take”).
