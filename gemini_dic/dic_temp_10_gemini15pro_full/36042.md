codling:/ˈkɒdlɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːdlɪŋ/ (US)| n.|小鳕鱼; 未成熟的苹果; |A young cod fish, typically less than two years old.  Also, an immature apple.|例句：The fisherman caught a codling in his net. 渔夫在他的网里捕到了一条小鳕鱼。

例句：The codling fell from the tree before it was ripe.  未成熟的苹果从树上掉了下来。|近义词：cod (for fish), crab apple (for apple) |反义词:mature cod (for fish), ripe apple (for apple)|词性:noun
