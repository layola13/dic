compaginate:/kəmˈpædʒɪneɪt/
verb
结合；组合；编排
to join or combine together; to arrange in pages
The printer compaginated the book before binding it. 印刷商在装订前先把书编排好。
近义词: combine, unite, join, assemble, collate
反义词: separate, divide, disconnect
词性: verb

compaginate:/kəmˈpædʒɪneɪt/
adjective
结合的；组合的；编排的
joined or combined together; arranged in pages
The compaginate sheets were ready for binding. 组合好的书页准备装订。
近义词: combined, united, joined, assembled, collated
反义词: separate, divided, disconnected
词性: adjective


词源:
From Late Latin *compaginatus, past participle of compaginare (“to join together”), from Latin compages (“a joining together”), from com- (“together”) + pangere (“to fasten”).
