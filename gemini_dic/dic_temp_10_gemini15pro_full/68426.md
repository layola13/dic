falsification:/ˌfɔːlsɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ (US) /ˌfɔːlsɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ (UK)| n. | 伪造；篡改；歪曲 | the act of falsifying something | The falsification of the evidence led to a mistrial.  证据的伪造导致审判无效。| synonyms: forgery, counterfeiting, misrepresentation, distortion | antonyms: verification, confirmation, authentication | noun
