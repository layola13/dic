ethylidene:/eθɪˈlɪdiːn/ | n. |亚乙基|A bivalent radical, CH3CH=, derived from ethane by removal of two hydrogen atoms from the same carbon atom.  It forms the basis for naming certain organic compounds.|The ethylidene group is present in many important chemical compounds. 亚乙基存在于许多重要的化合物中。| Ethylidene dichloride is used as an industrial solvent. 二氯化亚乙基用作工业溶剂。|近义词: (No direct synonym, as it is a specific chemical term) |反义词: (No antonym for a chemical radical)|词性: noun
