hypermodestly:/ˌhaɪpərˈmɒdɪstli/ | adv. | 过分谦虚地 |  In an excessively modest manner, often to the point of seeming insincere.  It implies an exaggerated display of humility. | He hypermodestly attributed his success to luck, despite years of hard work. 他过分谦虚地将他的成功归功于运气，尽管他付出了多年的努力。|  Near Synonyms: excessively modestly, overly humbly, affectedly modestly | Near Antonyms: boastfully, arrogantly, proudly |  adverb
