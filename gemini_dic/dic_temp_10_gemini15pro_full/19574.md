bigcoin:/ˈbɪɡkɔɪn/| n.|大币; 巨额比特币 (非正式, 通常指数量很大的比特币)|A large amount of Bitcoin.  This is not a formally recognized term, but used colloquially. |He made a bigcoin off that investment. 他从那笔投资中赚了一大笔比特币。|近义词：Bitcoin fortune, large Bitcoin holding|反义词:small Bitcoin holding, dust (指极少量比特币)|noun
