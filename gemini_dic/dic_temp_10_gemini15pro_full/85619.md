haulages:/ˈhɔːlɪdʒɪz/
noun
运输; 搬运; 拖运; (尤指长途)货物运输
The business of transporting goods by road or rail; the goods transported.
The company specializes in long-distance haulages.  这家公司专门从事长途货物运输。
Heavy haulages of timber are common on this road.  这条路上经常有大宗木材运输。
transportation, carriage, conveyance, freight
-
plural noun
