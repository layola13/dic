disforest:/diːˈfɔːrɪst/ /dɪˈfɔːrɪst/
verb
砍伐森林; 毁灭森林
To clear of forests; deprive of forests.
例句：
They are disforesting the Amazon at an alarming rate.
他们正在以惊人的速度砍伐亚马逊森林。

Rampant logging is disforesting the region.
猖獗的伐木正在毁坏这个地区的森林。

近义词：deforest, clear-cut
反义词：reforest, afforest
词性：v.
