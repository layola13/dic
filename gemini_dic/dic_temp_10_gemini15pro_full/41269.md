cordately:/ˈkɔːrdətli/| adj.|心形的；心形底的|Shaped like a heart, with a pointed end and rounded lobes at the base.  Specifically referring to the shape of a leaf.|The leaves of the morning glory are cordately shaped.  牵牛花的叶子是心形的。|近义词：heart-shaped, cordate|反义词:N/A|词性:adjective
词源:From Latin *cordātus, from cord-, cor "heart."
