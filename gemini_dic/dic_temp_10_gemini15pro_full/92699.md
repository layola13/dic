icehouses:/ˈaɪsˌhaʊzɪz/
n.
冰窖，冷藏库
A building used to store ice, especially before refrigeration.
The farmers filled their icehouses during the winter to preserve food throughout the year.  农民们在冬天把冰窖填满，以便全年保存食物。
refrigerators, freezers, cellars
warm places, ovens
noun
