galvanologist:/ˌɡælvəˈnɒlədʒɪst/
n.
电疗专家，电镀专家
A specialist in the use of galvanism for medical purposes or in electroplating.
Galvanologists were pioneers in understanding the effects of electricity on the human body.  电疗专家是理解电对人体影响的先驱。
The galvanologist carefully applied the electrodes to the patient's skin.  电疗专家小心翼翼地将电极贴在病人的皮肤上。
近义词: electrotherapist, electroplater
反义词:  (无明显的反义词)
词性: noun

galvanic:/ɡælˈvænɪk/
adj.
电流的，电击的，惊人的
Of or relating to galvanism; startling.
The galvanic current caused the muscles to contract.  电流使肌肉收缩。
The news of the accident had a galvanic effect on everyone. 事故的消息让每个人都感到震惊。
近义词: electric, shocking, startling
反义词: calming, soothing
词性: adjective

galvanize:/ˈɡælvənaɪz/
vt.
电镀，刺激，激励
Coat (iron or steel) with a protective layer of zinc; shock or excite (someone), typically into taking action.
The steel beams were galvanized to prevent rust. 钢梁经过镀锌以防止生锈。
The news galvanized them into action.  这个消息激励他们采取行动。
近义词: stimulate, excite, rouse, electrify, coat, plate
反义词: soothe, calm, pacify, demotivate
词性: verb

词源:  From Luigi Galvani (1737-1798), Italian physiologist, who discovered that a dead frog’s leg would twitch when touched by two different metals, thus demonstrating the existence of bioelectricity.  来源于意大利生理学家路易吉·伽伐尼 (1737-1798)，他发现用两种不同的金属接触死青蛙的腿会使其抽搐，从而证明了生物电的存在。
