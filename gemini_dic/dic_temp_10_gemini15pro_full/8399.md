anti-slavic:/ˌæntiˈslɑːvɪk/
adj.
反斯拉夫的；歧视斯拉夫人的
Opposed to, prejudiced against, or hostile towards Slavic peoples, their languages, and their cultures.
The politician was accused of making anti-Slavic remarks.  这位政治家被指控发表反斯拉夫言论。
The rise of anti-Slavic sentiment in the region is alarming.  该地区反斯拉夫情绪的抬头令人担忧。
近义词:racist, xenophobic, discriminatory, bigoted
反义词:pro-Slavic, Slavophile
词性:adjective
