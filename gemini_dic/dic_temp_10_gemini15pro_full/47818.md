decardinalize:/diːˈkɑːrdɪnəlaɪz/
verb
使不再重要；降低…的重要性
To deprive of cardinal importance or significance.
The new policy decardinalized the role of the department.  这项新政策降低了该部门的重要性。
The report decardinalized the traditional metrics of success.  这份报告降低了传统成功指标的重要性。
Synonyms: diminish, downplay, de-emphasize, minimize
Antonyms: emphasize, highlight, accentuate, maximize
