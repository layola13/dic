outlives:/ˌaʊtˈlɪvz/
verb
比...活得长; 比...持久
to live or exist longer than someone or something
例句：She outlived her husband by twenty years. 她比她丈夫多活了二十年。

例句：This building has outlived its usefulness. 这栋建筑已经失去了它的作用。

近义词：survive, outlast
反义词：predecease

词性:v.
