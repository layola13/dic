nonatomic:/ˌnɑːnəˈtɑːmɪk/ | adj. | 非原子的 | Not atomic; (in Objective-C) indicating that the compiler should not generate locking code to synchronize access to the associated property, thus making access faster but potentially unsafe in multithreaded environments. |  `@property (nonatomic, strong) NSString *myString;` (Declares a string property without atomic access.) |  non-blocking, thread-unsafe | atomic, thread-safe | adjective
