desperado:/ˌdespəˈrɑːdoʊ/ (US) /ˌdespəˈreɪdoʊ/ (UK)| n. |亡命之徒；不顾一切的人|A bold or reckless criminal, especially one of a group of outlaws.  A desperate person who is willing to take risks.|例句：The desperado held up the stagecoach. 亡命之徒劫持了驿站马车。

例句：He became a desperado after losing everything he owned.  失去所有财产后，他变成了一个亡命之徒。|近义词：outlaw, bandit, gangster, ruffian, villain|反义词：law-abiding citizen, conformist|词性: 名词

desperados:/ˌdespəˈrɑːdoʊz/ (US) /ˌdespəˈreɪdoʊz/ (UK)| n. |亡命之徒（复数）|Plural form of desperado.|例句：The sheriff was hunting a gang of desperados. 警长正在追捕一伙亡命之徒。

例句：The desperados rode off into the sunset. 亡命之徒们朝着夕阳骑马而去。|近义词：outlaws, bandits, gangsters, ruffians, villains|反义词：law-abiding citizens, conformists|词性: 名词
