carpet-bomb:/ˈkɑːrpɪt bɒm/ (US) /ˈkɑːpɪt bɒm/ (UK)| v. |地毯式轰炸|To bomb (an area) heavily and indiscriminately.|The air force carpet-bombed the city.| 近义词: blitz, bombard, saturate with bombs | 反义词: precision bombing |词性: 动词

carpet-bombs:/ˈkɑːrpɪt bɒmz/ (US) /ˈkɑːpɪt bɒmz/ (UK)| n. |地毯式轰炸|The act of carpet-bombing.|The carpet-bombs destroyed everything.| 近义词: saturation bombing, area bombing | 反义词: targeted airstrike |词性: 名词


词源:  "Carpet bombing" is a metaphorical term derived from the image of completely covering an area like a carpet. It emerged during World War II.
