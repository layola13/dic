outcheat:/ˌaʊtˈtʃiːt/
verb
以欺骗胜过
To surpass or defeat someone by cheating more effectively or cleverly than they do.
He outcheated his opponent in the poker game, winning the entire pot. 他在扑克游戏中以更巧妙的欺骗手段战胜了对手，赢得了全部赌注。
近义词: outsmart, outwit, outmaneuver
反义词: be outwitted, be outsmarted
