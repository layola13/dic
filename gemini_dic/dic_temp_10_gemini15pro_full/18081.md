behaved:/bɪˈheɪvd/
adjective
表现好的；行为端正的
Having good manners; acting in a proper way.
The children were very well behaved in the restaurant.  孩子们在餐厅里表现得很好。
近义词:well-mannered, polite, good, obedient
反义词:misbehaved, naughty, unruly, disobedient
词性:adjective

词源: From the verb "behave" + "-ed" (past participle suffix). "Behave" comes from "be-" (prefix indicating existence or action) + "have" (to hold oneself, to conduct oneself).
