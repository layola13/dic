cautioning:/ˈkɔːʃənɪŋ/| v.|警告，告诫|The act of giving a warning or expressing caution.|The teacher was cautioning the students about the dangers of the internet. 老师正在告诫学生们互联网的危险。|近义词：warning, admonishing, advising|反义词：encouraging, promoting|verb

Etymology: From the verb "caution," which comes from the Old French "caution" and the Latin "cautio" meaning "carefulness, precaution."
