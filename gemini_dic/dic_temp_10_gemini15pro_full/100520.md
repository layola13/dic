interviewing:/ˈɪntərvjuːɪŋ/
n.|面试；面谈|The process of asking someone questions to find out more about them, especially for a job or a news report.|The interviewing panel was very impressed with her qualifications. 面试小组对她的资历印象非常深刻。
v.|面试；采访；访问|The act of conducting an interview.|They are interviewing candidates for the new marketing position. 他们正在面试新营销职位的候选人。
gerund|正在面试|The present participle form of the verb "interview".|She is interviewing for a job at Google. 她正在谷歌面试一份工作。
Etymology: From the French "entrevue," meaning "a meeting between," from "entre-" (between) and "voir" (to see).
Synonyms: questioning, examining, assessing, surveying
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for the process itself, but for the purpose, it could be "ignoring," "neglecting")
Word Type: Noun, verb (present participle)
