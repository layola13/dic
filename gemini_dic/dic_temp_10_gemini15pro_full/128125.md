netball:/ˈnɛtbɔːl/| n.| 篮球 (一种主要由女性进行的球类运动)|A team sport played by two teams of seven players, the object being to shoot a ball through a hoop elevated on a post.|例句：She's a keen netball player. 她是一位热衷于篮球的运动员。

例句：The netball match was postponed due to rain. 篮球比赛因下雨而延期。|近义词：basketball (相似, 但规则不同)|反义词: 无|词性:noun
