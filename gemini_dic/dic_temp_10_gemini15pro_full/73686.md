forging:/ˈfɔːrdʒɪŋ/
n.|锻造；伪造； forging(名词) 指的是金属或其他材料通过加热和锤打成形的工艺，也指伪造文件或签名等。|The forging of steel requires high temperatures. 钢的锻造需要高温。|synonyms: shaping, fabrication, counterfeiting|antonyms: casting, molding
v.|锻造；伪造； forging(动词) 指的是通过加热和锤打来塑造金属或其他材料，也指非法复制或模仿某物以欺骗。|He forged a new career in the tech industry. 他在科技行业开创了一番新事业。  The blacksmith forged the iron into a horseshoe. 铁匠把铁锻造成马蹄铁。|synonyms:  create, fabricate, counterfeit|antonyms: destroy, dismantle

词性: 名词/动词
词源:  From Old French forger "to forge, fabricate," from Latin fabricare "to make, construct, fashion," related to faber "workman, craftsman."
