allopath:/ˈæləpæθ/| n.|对抗疗法医师；对抗疗法信奉者|A practitioner of conventional medicine; a believer in allopathy. |Example: The allopath prescribed antibiotics for the infection.  对抗疗法医师开了抗生素治疗感染。

Example: Some people prefer alternative medicine to seeing an allopath.  有些人更喜欢替代医学，而不是去看对抗疗法医师。|Synonym: conventional doctor, traditional physician|Antonym: homeopath, naturopath|Noun
