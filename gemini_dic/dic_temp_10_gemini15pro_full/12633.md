assaultable:/əˈsɔːltəbl/
adjective
可攻击的，易受攻击的
Able to be assaulted; vulnerable to attack.
The isolated farmhouse was assaultable from all sides.  这栋孤立的农舍四面八方都容易受到攻击。
The system is highly assaultable due to its weak security protocols. 由于安全协议薄弱，该系统极易受到攻击。
近义词: vulnerable, exposed, unprotected
反义词: secure, protected, safe
词性: adjective

词源:
From assault + -able.  来自 assault (攻击) + -able (可...的)
