nomady:/ˈnoʊmædi/| n.|游牧，流浪，漂泊|A way of life in which people do not live in one place but move around to find fresh pasture or food, or in search of seasonal work.|The Tuareg people of the Sahara are traditionally nomadic, moving their herds of camels and goats across the desert. 图阿雷格人是撒哈拉沙漠的传统游牧民族，他们赶着骆驼和山羊群穿越沙漠。|近义词：wandering, roaming, migration, itinerancy|反义词:sedentary, settled|词性:名词

词源: From French *nomade*, from Latin *nomas* (genitive *nomadis*), from Greek *νομάς* (genitive *νομάδος*), from *νέμειν* (nemein, “to pasture, to graze”).
