nickel-and-dime:/ˈnɪkəl ənd daɪm/
| adj.  v. |小气的，斤斤计较的；为小钱斤斤计较；|Charging small amounts, especially in a way that seems unfair or unreasonable. |The company is known for nickel-and-diming its customers with hidden fees. 这家公司以向客户收取隐性费用而闻名，斤斤计较。|They nickel-and-dimed me on every single charge. 他们在每笔费用上都跟我斤斤计较。|近义词：petty, miserly, stingy|反义词：generous, lavish|词性: adjective, verb

nickel-and-dime:/ˈnɪkəl ənd daɪm/
| n. |为小钱斤斤计较的行为|The act of charging small amounts, especially in a way that seems unfair or unreasonable.|The constant nickel-and-dime made me switch providers.  不断的斤斤计较让我换了供应商。|近义词：penny-pinching|反义词：generosity|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "nickel" (a US five-cent coin) and "dime" (a US ten-cent coin), representing small amounts of money.  源于“nickel”（美国五分硬币）和“dime”（美国十分硬币），代表少量钱。
