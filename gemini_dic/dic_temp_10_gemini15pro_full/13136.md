aswoon:/əˈswuːn/
adjective
昏厥的，神志不清的
in a swoon; faint from emotion.
She fell aswoon at the sight of blood. 她一看到血就晕了过去。
近义词:fainting, unconscious, insensible
反义词:conscious, awake, alert
词性:adjective

词源:
From Middle English *aswone, from Old English on swōne (“in a swoon”), from Proto-Germanic *in swōną (“in a faint”). Cognate with Dutch in zwijm (“in a faint”), German in Ohnmacht (“in a faint, unconscious”).  The word swoon itself comes from Old English swōgen, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *swōgjaną (“to suffocate, stifle”).
