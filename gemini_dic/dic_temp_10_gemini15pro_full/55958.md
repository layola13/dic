djinnee:/dʒɪˈniː/ /ˈdʒiːniː/
n.
精灵，妖怪
A supernatural creature in Arabian and Muslim folklore, often able to assume human or animal form and to grant wishes.
A djinn appeared from the lamp, offering the fisherman three wishes. 一个精灵从灯里出现，给渔夫三个愿望。
Synonyms: genie, jinni, jinn
Antonyms: None
noun
