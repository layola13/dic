half-formed:/ˈhɑːfˈfɔːrmd/
adj.
半成品的，未完全形成的
not fully formed; incompletely developed
The artist showed us a half-formed sculpture.  这位艺术家给我们看了一座未完成的雕塑。
The plan was still half-formed in his mind.  这个计划在他脑海里还只是个雏形。
incipient, rudimentary, undeveloped
developed, complete, finished
adjective
