bottleholder:/ˈbɒtlˌhəʊldə(r) (British), ˈbɑːtlˌhoʊldər (American)| n. |持瓶人，助手，支持者|A person who holds a bottle (especially for a boxer or other athlete), or, more commonly, a supporter or assistant.|例句：He was his best friend and bottleholder throughout the difficult campaign. 他是他最好的朋友，在整个艰难的竞选过程中都是他的支持者。|例句：The boxer's bottleholder offered him advice and encouragement between rounds.  拳击手的助手在回合之间给他提供建议和鼓励。|近义词：supporter, assistant, second, aide|反义词: opponent, adversary|词性:noun

bottleholder:/ˈbɒtlˌhəʊldə(r) (British), ˈbɑːtlˌhoʊldər (American)| n. |拎瓶器|A device for holding bottles.|例句：She used a bottleholder to carry the wine to the picnic. 她用拎瓶器把酒带去野餐。|例句：The bottleholder kept the champagne chilled.  这个拎瓶器让香槟保持冰镇。|近义词：bottle carrier, bottle rack|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
