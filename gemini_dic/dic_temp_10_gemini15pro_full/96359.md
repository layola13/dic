indirectness:/ˌɪndɪˈrɛktnəs/ | n. |间接，迂回 | the quality of being indirect; lack of straightforwardness | He achieved his goal through indirectness, hinting at his desires rather than stating them outright. 他通过间接的方式实现了目标，暗示而不是直接说出他的愿望。| synonyms: circumlocution, obliqueness, evasiveness | antonyms: directness, straightforwardness, frankness | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + direct + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality).  Ultimately from Latin *in- (not) + directus (straight).
