outlaying:/aʊtˈleɪɪŋ/
adjective
支出大量资金的；花钱多的

近义词:expensive, costly, exorbitant
反义词:inexpensive, cheap, economical
词性:adjective

outlaying:/aʊtˈleɪɪŋ/
adjective
远离中心的；边远的
situated far from a center; remote
The company is outlaying a substantial amount of money on research and development. 该公司正在研发方面投入大量资金。
近义词:remote, distant, peripheral
反义词:central, nearby, close
词性:adjective
