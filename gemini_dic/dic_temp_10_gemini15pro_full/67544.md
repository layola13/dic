extramission:/ˌɛkstrəˈmɪʃən/ | n. | 外射说 (一种古老的视觉理论，认为视觉是通过眼睛发射光线实现的) |The theory of extramission, now discredited, held that vision resulted from rays emitted from the eyes.  外射说，现在已经被推翻了，它认为视觉是由眼睛发出的光线造成的。| 例句：The ancient Greeks debated the merits of extramission versus intromission theories of vision. 古希腊人争论视觉的外射说和内射说的优劣。 | 近义词: emission (发射),  radiation (辐射) | 反义词: intromission (内射) | 词性: noun

词源: From Latin extra- "outward" + missionem "a sending."  来自拉丁语 extra- “向外” + missionem “发送”。
