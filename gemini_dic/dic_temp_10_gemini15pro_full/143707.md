outlier:/ˈaʊtˌlaɪər/| n.|异常值，离群值|A data point that differs significantly from other observations.|The study identified several outliers that skewed the overall results. 这项研究发现了几个使总体结果出现偏差的异常值。|synonyms: anomaly, aberration, deviation, exception, irregularity|antonyms: norm, standard, average|noun

outlier:/ˈaʊtˌlaɪər/| n.|局外人，圈外人|A person or thing situated away or detached from the main body or system.|He felt like an outlier in the close-knit community. 在这个联系紧密的社区里，他觉得自己像个局外人。|synonyms: outsider, misfit, nonconformist, maverick|antonyms: insider, conformist|noun
