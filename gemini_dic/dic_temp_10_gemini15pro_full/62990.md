enomoty: /ɪˈnɒməti/ | n. | 百人队 | A subdivision of an ancient Spartan regiment, consisting of about 100 men.  It was a tactical unit within the larger Spartan army. |  The enomoty formed a crucial part of the Spartan phalanx. | 近义词: company, unit, detachment | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From Ancient Greek ἐνωμοτία (enōmotía), from ἐν (en, “in”) + ὄμνυμι (ómnumi, “I swear”). Literally, "those who swear together".
