nitrate:/ˈnaɪtreɪt/ (US), /ˈnaɪtreɪt/ (UK)| n. |硝酸盐|A salt or ester of nitric acid.  |例句：Potassium nitrate is used in fertilizers. 硝酸钾用于肥料。

例句：High levels of nitrates in drinking water can be harmful.  饮用水中高含量的硝酸盐可能是有害的。|近义词：salt, ester (depending on context)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

nitrate:/ˈnaɪtreɪt/ (US), /ˈnaɪtreɪt/ (UK)| v. |硝化，用硝酸盐处理|To treat or combine with nitric acid or a nitrate. |例句：The soil was nitrated to increase its fertility. 土壤被硝化以增加肥力。


例句：Farmers nitrate their crops to promote growth. 农民给农作物硝化以促进生长。|近义词：fertilize (in some contexts)|反义词: N/A|词性:verb
