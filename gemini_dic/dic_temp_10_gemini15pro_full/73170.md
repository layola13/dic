forbiddenly:/fərˈbɪdnli/ | adv. | 禁止地，严禁地 | In a way that is forbidden; illicitly. |  She smiled forbiddenly at him across the crowded room. 她隔着拥挤的房间偷偷地对他笑了笑。 | 近义词: illicitly, wrongly, sinfully | 反义词: permissibly, legally, allowedly | 词性: 副词

词源:  From *forbidden* + *-ly*. *Forbidden* comes from the past participle of *forbid*, from Old English *forbēodan* "to prohibit," from *for-* "against" + *bēodan* "to command."
