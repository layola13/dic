bereaveds:/bɪˈriːvdz/| n. |丧亲者，死者家属|People who have recently lost a loved one, especially through death.  The bereaveds gathered for the funeral.|例句：The bereaveds were comforted by the support of their friends and family.  死者家属得到了亲朋好友的支持和安慰。

例句：The charity provides counselling services for the bereaveds.  该慈善机构为丧亲者提供咨询服务。|近义词：mourners, the grieving|反义词: None|词性:Noun


bereaved:/bɪˈriːvd/| adj. |丧失亲友的，痛失亲人的|Deprived of a close relation or friend through their death.|例句：The bereaved mother was inconsolable. 痛失爱子的母亲悲痛欲绝。

例句：He offered his condolences to the bereaved family. 他向死者家属表示哀悼。|近义词：grieving, mourning|反义词: None|词性:Adjective

词源: From the past participle of bereave, from Old English berēafian "to deprive of, rob, dispossess," from be- "completely" + rēafian "to rob, plunder."
