dishearten:/dɪsˈhɑːrtn/ /dɪsˈhɑːrʔn/| vt. |使沮丧，使灰心|To cause (someone) to lose determination or confidence.|The team was disheartened by the loss.|近义词：demoralize, discourage, depress|反义词:encourage, hearten, inspire|词性:动词

dishearten:/dɪsˈhɑːrtn/ /dɪsˈhɑːrʔn/| adj. |沮丧的，灰心的|(Usually used predicatively) Feeling or causing someone to feel discouraged.|She felt disheartened after failing the exam.|近义词：demoralized, discouraged, depressed|反义词:encouraged, heartened, inspired|词性:形容词
