nonequability:/ˌnɒnˌiːkwəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不均匀性，不稳定性 |  The state or quality of being not equable; unevenness, instability, changeableness.  | The nonequability of the climate made farming difficult.  气候的不稳定性使耕作变得困难。 | 近义词：variability, instability, unevenness, fluctuation, inconstancy | 反义词：stability, constancy, uniformity, evenness | 词性: 名词

词源:  From non- + equability, from equable + -ity.  Equable comes from Latin aequabilis, from aequare "to make equal," from aequus "equal."
