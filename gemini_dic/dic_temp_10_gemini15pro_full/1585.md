achromatous: /ˌeɪkrəˈmeɪtəs/ /ˌeɪkrəˈmɑːtəs/
adjective
无色的，消色的
Lacking color; colorless.  Free from chromatic aberration (of a lens).
The achromatous lens produced a clearer image.  消色差透镜产生了更清晰的图像。
The scientist studied the achromatous properties of the newly discovered mineral.  这位科学家研究了新发现的矿物的无色特性。

近义词: colorless, uncolored, hyaline
反义词: chromatic, colored
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *a-* (without) + *chrōma* (color) + *-ous* (adjective suffix).

USAGE->
The word "achromatous" is primarily used in scientific or technical contexts, especially in optics and biology.

Example 1:  The achromatous telescope lens minimized color distortion. 消色差望远镜镜头最大限度地减少了色差。

Example 2:  Achromatous cells lack pigment.  无色细胞缺乏色素。
