climax: /ˈklaɪmæks/ | n. | 高潮，顶点，极点 | The climax of the story was the hero's daring rescue.  故事的高潮是主人公英勇的救援。|  The music reached a powerful climax.  音乐达到了一个强有力的顶点。 | synonyms: peak, culmination, apex, zenith | antonyms: nadir, bottom, anticlimax | noun

Etymology: From Late Latin climax, from Greek klimax "ladder, staircase," from klinein "to slope, to lean."
