PHRASE->
what if = 如果...会怎么样
What if we investigate this further? 如果我们进一步调查会怎么样？

what for = 为了什么
What are you investigating this for? 你调查这个是为了什么？

no matter what = 无论如何
No matter what, we must investigate this thoroughly. 无论如何，我们必须彻底调查此事。

what's more = 而且；更重要的是
The evidence is compelling, and what's more, the police are now investigating. 证据确凿，而且，警方现在正在调查。

what about = ...怎么样
What about investigating the other suspects?  调查其他嫌疑人怎么样？
