hoodwinkable:/hʊdˈwɪŋkəbl/
adjective
易受骗的
Easily deceived or tricked.
He was so hoodwinkable that he believed the email promising him a million dollars. 他太容易上当了，竟然相信了那封承诺给他一百万美元的电子邮件。
近义词:gullible, naive, credulous
反义词: astute, discerning, perceptive
词性:adjective

hoodwink:/hʊdˈwɪŋk/
verb
欺骗，蒙蔽
To deceive or trick someone.
The con man hoodwinked the elderly woman into giving him her life savings. 骗子哄骗老妇人把毕生积蓄都给了他。
近义词:deceive, trick, dupe
反义词: enlighten, inform, expose
词性:verb
