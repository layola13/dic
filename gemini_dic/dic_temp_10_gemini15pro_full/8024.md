anthraconite:/ˌænθrəˈkɒnaɪt/ | n. |  沥青灰岩，含沥青的方解石 | A dark-colored variety of calcite containing bituminous matter. |  The geologist identified the sample as anthraconite due to its dark color and bituminous odor. 地质学家根据其深色和沥青气味将样品鉴定为沥青灰岩。|  Synonym: stinkstone, bituminous limestone | Antonym: (No direct antonym, could consider pure calcite or limestone) | noun
