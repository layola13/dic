dissent-from:/dɪˈsɛnt frʌm/| v.|不同意，持异议|To disagree with a majority opinion or decision.  Expressing disagreement or opposition to something.|例句：Two justices dissented from the majority opinion. 两位法官对多数意见表示异议。

例句：He dissented from the proposal to build a new shopping mall. 他不同意建造新购物中心的提议。|近义词：differ, disagree, object|反义词：agree, concur, assent|词性:动词

词源: From Latin "dissentire," from "dis-" (apart, away) + "sentire" (to feel).
