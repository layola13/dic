grubble:/ˈɡrʌbəl/| v.| 搜寻；翻找；摸索 | to search or rummage for something, especially by feeling or groping | He grubbled in his pocket for some change. 他在口袋里摸索找零钱。 | synonyms: rummage, fumble, search, grope | antonyms: organize, arrange | verb

grubble:/ˈɡrʌbəl/| n.| 搜寻；翻找；摸索 | the act of searching or rummaging for something, especially by feeling or groping |  After a grubble in the attic, she found the old photo album. 在阁楼里翻找一番后，她找到了那本旧相册。 | synonyms: rummage, search | antonyms: organization, arrangement | noun
