crossbow: /ˈkrɒsbəʊ/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsbəʊ/ (US)| n. | 十字弓，弩 | A medieval weapon consisting of a bow fixed transversely on a stock, having a groove for the arrow and a mechanism for drawing and releasing the string. | The hunter used a crossbow to take down the deer. 猎人用十字弓猎杀了鹿。|  The museum displayed a collection of ancient crossbows. 博物馆展出了一系列古代十字弓。 | 近义词: ballista | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "sling" as a simpler ranged weapon) | 词性: noun
