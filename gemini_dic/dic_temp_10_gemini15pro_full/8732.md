antidemocrat:/ˌæntiˈdɛməkræt/ | n. | 反民主主义者；反对民主的人 | Someone who is opposed to democracy. | He was labeled an antidemocrat for his views on limiting voting rights. 他因为限制投票权的观点而被贴上反民主主义者的标签。| The politician's anti-democratic stance caused controversy. 这位政治家的反民主立场引发了争议。 |  近义词: authoritarian, totalitarian, despot, tyrant | 反义词: democrat, egalitarian | noun

antidemocratic:/ˌæntiˌdɛməˈkrætɪk/ | adj. | 反民主的；非民主的 | Opposed to or violating the principles of democracy. | The new laws were seen as antidemocratic and a threat to civil liberties. 这些新法律被视为反民主的，是对公民自由的威胁。 |  The regime's antidemocratic practices included suppressing dissent and manipulating elections. 该政权的反民主做法包括压制异议和操纵选举。 | 近义词: authoritarian, totalitarian, illiberal, undemocratic | 反义词: democratic, liberal, egalitarian | adjective


Etymology:
anti- (prefix): from Greek anti "against, opposite of, instead of, in place of"
democrat: from French démocrate, from Greek dēmokratēs "democrat," from dēmos "people" + -kratēs "ruler, member of a specific class"
