currier:/ˈkɜːriər/ (US), /ˈkʌriər/ (UK)| n.|皮革工，制革工人；马具制造商|A person who curries and dresses leather, especially for use in saddles, harnesses, etc.  A person who makes or deals in leather goods.|The currier carefully applied oil to the leather. 制革工人小心地给皮革上油。|Synonyms: leatherworker, tanner|Antonyms: None|noun
