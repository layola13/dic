enharden:/ɪnˈhɑːrdn/ /ɛnˈhɑrdn/
verb
使变硬；使坚固；使冷酷
To make something harder, tougher, or more resistant.  Also, to make someone less compassionate or sympathetic.
The cold wind enhardened the ground. 寒风使地面变硬了。
Years of war had enhardened the soldiers. 多年的战争使士兵们变得冷酷无情。
近义词: harden, strengthen, toughen, solidify, steel
反义词: soften, weaken, melt
词性: verb
