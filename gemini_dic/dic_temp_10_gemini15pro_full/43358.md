cranelike: /kreɪnlaɪk/
adjective
像鹤的；鹤立的
Resembling a crane, especially in being tall and slender.
The ballerina stood in a cranelike pose, elegant and poised.  芭蕾舞演员以鹤立的姿势站着，优雅而沉着。
The building, tall and cranelike, dominated the skyline. 这座高耸如鹤的建筑占据了天际线的主导地位。
近义词: tall, slender, lanky, spindly
反义词: short, stocky, squat
词性: adjective

词源: crane + like.  Crane (n.) from Old English *cran, from Proto-Germanic *krano.  Like (suffix) from Old English -lic, from Proto-Germanic *-likan.
