concretes:/ˈkɒŋkriːts/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋkriːts/ (US)| n. |混凝土；具体物；(concretes)具体事例|A mixture of cement, sand, gravel, and water that hardens to a stone-like consistency. Also refers to tangible things or specific instances.|The builders used tons of concrete for the foundation.|近义词：cement, mixture, aggregate, specifics, examples, instances|反义词：abstractions, generalities|Noun

concretes:/ˈkɒŋkriːts/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋkriːts/ (US)| v. |(concretes)使凝固；使具体化|To make something concrete, either literally by mixing with cement or figuratively by making it definite and specific.|The plan was concreted after several meetings.|近义词：solidify, harden, define, specify|反义词：abstract, generalize|Verb
