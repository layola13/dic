booking-offices:/ˈbʊkɪŋ ˈɒfɪsɪz/ (UK) /ˈbʊkɪŋ ˈɔːfɪsɪz/ (US)
n.
售票处
A place where tickets are sold, especially for travel.
I bought my train tickets at the booking-offices. 我在售票处买了火车票。
The booking-offices were crowded with people trying to get tickets for the concert. 售票处挤满了想买音乐会门票的人。
synonyms: ticket office, box office, ticket counter
antonyms: N/A
