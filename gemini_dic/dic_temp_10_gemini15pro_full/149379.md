pacifism:/ˈpæsɪfɪzəm/| n.|和平主义|The belief that war and violence are unjustifiable and that all disputes should be settled by peaceful means.|例句：Pacifism is a philosophy that rejects violence. 和平主义是一种反对暴力的哲学。

例句：Her pacifism prevented her from supporting the war effort. 她的和平主义立场使她无法支持战争行动。|近义词：peacefulness, nonviolence, anti-militarism|反义词：militarism, belligerence|词性:noun
