corticiform: /kɔːrtɪsɪfɔːrm/
adjective
皮质状的，类似树皮的
Resembling or having the form of bark or cortex.
The lichen had a corticiform texture, rough and flaky like tree bark.  地衣有一种皮质状的纹理，粗糙且呈片状，像树皮一样。
The fungus developed a corticiform layer on its surface.  这种真菌的表面长出了一层皮质状的层。
近义词: bark-like, crustose
反义词: smooth, soft
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *corticiformis, from cortex, cortic- 'bark' + -formis '-shaped, -like'.
