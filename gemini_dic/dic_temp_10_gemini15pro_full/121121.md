misdeclared:/ˌmɪsdɪˈkleərd/ | adj. | 申报不实的，虚报的 | Incorrectly declared; falsely stated. | The customs officials seized the misdeclared goods. 海关官员扣押了申报不实的货物。 | The company was fined for misdeclared earnings.  该公司因虚报收益而被罚款。 | 近义词：falsely declared, misrepresented, understated, overstated | 反义词：correctly declared, accurately declared | 词性: adjective

词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + "declared" (past participle of the verb "declare," from Old French "declarer," from Latin "declarare" meaning "to make clear").
