disinvolve:/ˌdɪsɪnˈvɒlv/ | v. | 使脱离；使不参与；不再介入 | To detach or separate oneself from involvement or participation in something. |  She disinvolved herself from the project due to personal reasons. 她由于个人原因退出了这个项目。

He disinvolved himself from the family business to pursue his own interests. 他脱离了家族企业去追求自己的兴趣。| 近义词: detach, withdraw, extricate, disentangle | 反义词: involve, engage, participate | 词性: 动词
