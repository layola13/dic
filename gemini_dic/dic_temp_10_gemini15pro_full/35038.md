close-run:/ˌkloʊs ˈrʌn/ | adj. | 势均力敌的，难分胜负的 | A close-run contest or competition is one in which the competitors are very close and it is difficult to predict who will win. | The election was a close-run thing, with the winner declared only after a recount.  这场选举势均力敌，只有在重新计票后才宣布获胜者。|Synonyms: close, tight, neck and neck | Antonyms: one-sided, decisive | adjective

close-run:/ˌkloʊs ˈrʌn/ | n. | 势均力敌的比赛，难分胜负的比赛 | A close-run contest or competition. |  It was a close-run until the very last minute. 直到最后一分钟，比赛都难分胜负。|Synonyms: close contest, tight race | Antonyms: landslide victory | noun
