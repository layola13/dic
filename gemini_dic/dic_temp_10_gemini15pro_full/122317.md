mitu: /ˈmiːtuː/ |pron.|我，我们 (非正式，网络用语，受西班牙语影响)|A colloquial, internet-based form of "me too," often used to express agreement or shared experience, derived from Spanish.|Example: Mitu! I love that band too! 我也是！我也喜欢那个乐队！|近义词: me too|反义词: (No direct antonym)|pron.
