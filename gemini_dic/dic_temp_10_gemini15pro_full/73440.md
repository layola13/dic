foremention:/ˌfɔːrˈmɛnʃən/ | v. |预先提及，先前提到|To mention something or someone previously in a text or conversation.|As forementioned in the introduction, this study focuses on the effects of climate change.|近义词：premention, aforesaid, aforementioned|反义词: None|词性:动词

Etymology:
From fore- + mention.  "Fore-" is from Old English *fore, from Proto-Germanic *fura, meaning "before." "Mention" comes from Old French mencion, from Latin mentionem (nominative mentio) "a calling to mind, a speaking of, mention," from meminisse "to remember," related to mens "mind."
