contestingly:/kənˈtɛstɪŋli/
adv.
争辩地，竞争地
in a contesting manner; with contention or dispute.
The election results were contestingly debated.  选举结果引起了激烈的争论。
He contestingly argued his point, refusing to back down. 他争辩着自己的观点，拒绝让步。
近义词: disputatiously, argumentatively, contentiously
反义词: agreeably, amicably, harmoniously
词性: 副词
