glumelle:/ɡluːˈmɛl/| n. |颖壳，外稃|The glumelle is the lower of two bracts that enclose a grass floret. |The glumelle of this rice variety is particularly long.  这种水稻品种的颖壳特别长。|Synonyms: lemma, lower bract|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps palea as the opposing bract)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French glumelle, diminutive of glume (“glume”), from Latin glūma (“husk, hull”).
