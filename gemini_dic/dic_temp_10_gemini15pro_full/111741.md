locusta:/loʊˈkʌstə/ | n. | 蝗虫，蚱蜢 | A large, short-horned grasshopper that often migrates in destructive swarms.  A type of insect known for its swarming behavior and ability to cause significant damage to crops. |  Locusta migratoria is a major agricultural pest.  飞蝗是主要的农业害虫。|Synonyms: grasshopper, locust | Antonyms: None | Noun
