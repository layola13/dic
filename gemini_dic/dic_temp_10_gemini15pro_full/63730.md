epibenthic:/ˌepɪˈbɛnθɪk/ | adj. | 底栖的，生活在海底表面的 | Living on the surface of the bottom sediments of a body of water. |  Epibenthic organisms play a crucial role in the marine ecosystem. 底栖生物在海洋生态系统中起着至关重要的作用。|  Researchers studied the diversity of epibenthic invertebrates. 研究人员研究了底栖无脊椎动物的多样性。| 近义词:benthic | 反义词:pelagic | 词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From epi- (Greek: upon, on) + benthic (Greek: relating to the bottom of a sea or lake).
