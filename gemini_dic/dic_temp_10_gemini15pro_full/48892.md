defrauder:/dɪˈfrɔːdər/| n.|诈骗者，欺诈者|A person who defrauds someone of something.|The defrauder was eventually caught and sentenced to prison.  这个诈骗犯最终被抓获并判处监禁。|Synonyms: cheat, swindler, con artist|Antonyms: honest person, philanthropist|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "defraud" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Defraud" comes from the Old French "defrauder," from Latin "defraudare,"  meaning "to cheat, deceive."
