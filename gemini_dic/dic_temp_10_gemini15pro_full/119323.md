microbiologically:/ˌmaɪkroʊˌbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkli/
adverb|微生物学地；从微生物学的角度|In a way that relates to the study of microorganisms.
The sample was microbiologically tested.  该样品经过了微生物检测。
The food was microbiologically safe.  这种食物在微生物学方面是安全的。
近义词:bacteriologically|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps macroscopically)|词性:adverb
