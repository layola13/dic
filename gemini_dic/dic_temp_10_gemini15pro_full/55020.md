disputation:/ˌdɪspjuˈteɪʃn/ |n.| 争论，辩论 |A formal academic debate or argument.| The students engaged in a lively disputation about the merits of different philosophical theories. 学生们就不同哲学理论的优点进行了激烈的辩论。| synonyms: argument, debate, controversy, contention | antonyms: agreement, harmony, concord, accord | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *disputatio*, from Latin disputare "discuss, examine, argue," from dis- "apart" + putare "to think, consider, reckon, believe, suppose."
