limbus:/ˈlɪmbəs/| n.|边缘，界限；模糊不清的状态；地狱边境|A region on the border or edge of something; a state of uncertainty or neglect; in some Christian theologies, a place on the edge of hell where righteous pagans and unbaptized infants reside.|例句：The project lingered in limbo for months due to lack of funding.  这个项目由于缺乏资金而搁置了数月。

例句：He felt like he was living in a limbus, unsure of his future. 他感觉自己生活在一种不确定的状态，对未来感到迷茫。|近义词：border, edge, fringe, margin, periphery, verge, brink, threshold, purgatory|反义词:center, core, heart|词性:名词
