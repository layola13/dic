cockfighting:/ˈkɒkˌfaɪtɪŋ/ | n. |斗鸡 |  A blood sport in which two roosters, specially bred for aggressiveness, are placed in a pit to fight, often to the death, for the purposes of gambling.  | Cockfighting is illegal in many countries. 斗鸡在很多国家是非法的。|  Cockfighting was a popular pastime in ancient Rome. 斗鸡是古罗马时期一项流行的娱乐活动。 |  近义词: animal fighting, blood sport | 反义词: animal welfare, animal rights | 词性: 名词
