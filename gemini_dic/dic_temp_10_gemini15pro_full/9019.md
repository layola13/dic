antimacho:/ˌæntiˈmɑːtʃoʊ/
adj.
反对大男子主义的; 反对男子气概的
Opposed to traditional macho attitudes and behavior; rejecting exaggerated masculinity.
He has a very antimacho attitude towards relationships, believing in equality and shared responsibilities.  他对于两性关系持有非常反对大男子主义的态度，信奉平等和共同承担责任。
She wrote an antimacho article criticizing the glorification of violence in action movies. 她写了一篇反对大男子主义的文章，批评动作片中对暴力的美化。
近义词:feminist, egalitarian
反义词: macho, sexist
词性: 形容词


antimacho:/ˌæntiˈmɑːtʃoʊ/
n.
反对大男子主义的人
A person who opposes traditional macho attitudes and behavior.
He considers himself an antimacho and actively challenges gender stereotypes. 他认为自己是一个反对大男子主义的人，并积极挑战性别刻板印象。
The group of antimachos protested against the sexist advertisement. 这群反对大男子主义者抗议性别歧视的广告。
近义词: feminist, egalitarian
反义词: macho, sexist
词性: 名词


词源:
"anti-" (prefix meaning against) + "macho" (from Spanish, meaning masculine or manly)
