christianness:/ˈkrɪstʃənɪsnɪs/
n.
基督教精神; 基督教特质
The quality or state of being Christian; Christian character or spirit.
Her life was marked by a deep christianness that influenced everyone around her. 她的一生以深刻的基督教精神为标志，影响了她周围的每一个人。
近义词：Christianity, piety, devoutness, religiousness
反义词：ungodliness, impiety, irreligion
词性：名词

词源: From Christian + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality).  Ultimately from Late Latin christianus, from Greek Khristianos.
