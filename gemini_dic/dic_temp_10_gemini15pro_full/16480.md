bargainers:/ˈbɑːrɡənərz/
n.
谈判者，讲价者，讨价还价的人
People who engage in bargaining or negotiation.
The bargainers finally reached an agreement after hours of discussion. 谈判者经过数小时的讨论终于达成了一致。
The two bargainers haggled over the price of the car for a long time.  两个买主为了车的价格讨价还价了很长时间。
negotiators, hagglers, dealers
opponents, adversaries
noun
