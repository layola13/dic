achier: /əˈtʃiːər/
n.|获得者，完成者；成功者|Someone who achieves something, especially something difficult or impressive. |She was a high achiever in school and went on to a successful career. 她上学时成绩优异，后来事业有成。

He is a great achiever and has made a huge contribution to his society. 他是一个伟大的成就者，为他的社会做出了巨大贡献。
|近义词:winner, achiever, successor, accomplisher|反义词:loser, failure|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "achieve," which comes from Old French "achever" meaning "to finish, complete, carry out."  This in turn derives from the Vulgar Latin *accapāre, meaning "to come to a head, bring to an end," which is composed of ad- "to" + caput "head."

一般现在时:
An achiever is someone who sets goals and works hard to reach them. = 成功人士是设定目标并努力实现目标的人。
High achievers often have a strong work ethic. =  高成就者通常有很强的工作 ethic。

一般过去时:
He was an achiever in his field and left a lasting legacy. = 他在他所在的领域是一位成就卓著的人，并留下了持久的遗产。
She was considered an achiever from a young age, excelling in both academics and sports. = 她从小就被认为是一个有成就的人，在学业和体育方面都表现出色。


现在完成时:
He has been a consistent achiever throughout his career. =  在他的职业生涯中，他一直是一位始终如一的成就者。
She has achieved great success in her chosen field. =  她在自己选择的领域取得了巨大的成功。

过去完成时:
He had already been a recognized achiever before he took on this new role. = 在他担任这个新角色之前，他已经是一位公认的成就者。
She had achieved more by the age of 30 than most people do in their entire lives.  =  她在30岁之前取得的成就比大多数人一生取得的成就还要多。

将来时:
With her talent and dedication, she will undoubtedly be a great achiever. =  凭借她的才华和奉献精神，她无疑将成为一位伟大的成就者。
He will be remembered as an achiever who made a real difference in the world. =  他将被铭记为一位对世界做出真正改变的成就者。

虚拟语气:
