cashier:/kæˈʃɪr/ /kæˈʃɪə(r)/| n. v. |收银员，出纳员；收银；兑换|A person who collects payments in a shop/store, bank, etc.|例句：The cashier scanned the groceries. 收银员扫描了食品杂货。|近义词：teller, clerk, checkout operator|反义词:customer, client|noun
cashier:/kæˈʃɪr/ /kæˈʃɪə(r)/| v. |解雇，开除（尤指因不诚实）;兑现|To dismiss from employment, especially for dishonesty; to exchange a check or money order for cash.|例句：The bank cashier cashed the check. 银行出纳员兑现了支票。|近义词：dismiss, fire, sack|反义词:hire, employ|verb
