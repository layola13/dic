cortical:/ˈkɔːrtɪkəl/ (US), /ˈkɔːtɪkəl/ (UK)| adj.|皮质的；皮层的|Relating to the outer layer of an organ, especially the brain.|The cortical areas of the brain are responsible for higher-level thinking. 大脑皮层区域负责高级思维。|例句：

Damage to the cortical area can result in impaired cognitive function.  皮质区域的损伤会导致认知功能受损。

The study examined the cortical thickness in patients with Alzheimer's disease.  该研究检查了阿尔茨海默病患者的皮质厚度。|近义词：cerebral, outer|反义词：medullary, inner|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *corticalis, from Latin cortex (“bark, rind, shell”) + -alis (adjectival suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *kerp- (“to gather, pluck”).
