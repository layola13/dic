nonowning:/ˌnɒnˈəʊnɪŋ/
adjective
非拥有的;不拥有所有权的
Not having or involving ownership.
Smart pointers offer a nonowning way to manage resources.  智能指针提供了一种非拥有的资源管理方式。
Nonowning references are crucial for avoiding memory leaks. 非拥有引用对于避免内存泄漏至关重要。
Synonyms:  non-possessing, unowned
Antonyms: owning, possessing
词性: 形容词
