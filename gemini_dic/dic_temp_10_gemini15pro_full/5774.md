amalgamators:/əˈmælɡəˌmeɪtərz/
n.
合并者；混合者；融合者
People or things that amalgamate something.  Those who combine or merge different elements, often businesses or organizations.
The amalgamators of the two companies ensured a smooth transition.  两家公司的合并者确保了平稳的过渡。
The museum showcased the work of several amalgamators of different artistic styles.  博物馆展示了几位融合不同艺术风格的艺术家的作品。
synonyms: mergers, consolidators, integrators, combiners
antonyms: separators, dividers, splitters
noun
