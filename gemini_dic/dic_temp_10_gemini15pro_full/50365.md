depopulated:/diːˈpɒpjʊleɪtɪd/
adjective
人口减少的，荒芜的
Reduced in population; deserted.
The war depopulated the countryside. 战争使农村地区人口减少。
Synonyms: deserted, abandoned, uninhabited, empty
Antonyms: populated, inhabited, crowded
词性: adjective


depopulate:/diːˈpɒpjʊleɪt/
verb
使人口减少，减少人口
To reduce the population of (a place).
Disease depopulated the island. 疾病使岛上人口减少。
Synonyms: evacuate, empty, clear
Antonyms: populate, inhabit
词性: verb


Etymology:
From Late Latin *depopulatus, past participle of depopulari "to devastate, lay waste," from Latin de- "down, away from" + populari "to populate," from populus "people."
