antisocietal:/ˌæntiːsəˈsaɪɪtəl/
adjective
反社会的
Opposed or harmful to society.  Going against the norms and values of society.
His antisocial behavior led to his expulsion from school. 他的反社会行为导致他被学校开除。
synonyms: antisocial, asocial, misanthropic, rebellious, nonconformist, deviant, subversive
antonyms: prosocial, social, sociable, conformist, conventional
词性: adjective
