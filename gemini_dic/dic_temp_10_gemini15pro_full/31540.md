charterless:/ˈtʃɑːrtərlɪs/
adj.
无执照的，无特许状的，未经授权的
Lacking a charter or license; unauthorized.
The charterless fishing boat was seized by the coast guard.  那艘无执照的渔船被海岸警卫队扣押了。
近义词:unauthorized, unlicensed, illegal
反义词:chartered, licensed, authorized
词性:形容词

词源:
From "charter" + "-less".  "Charter" comes from Old French "chartre" and ultimately from Latin "charta" (paper, document).  "-less" is a suffix meaning "without".
