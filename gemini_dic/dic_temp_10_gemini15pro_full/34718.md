click's:/klɪks/| n.|点击次数，点击量|A click is the act of pressing a button on a computer mouse.  The plural "clicks" refers to multiple instances of this action, often used in the context of website traffic or online advertising. |The website received thousands of clicks in the first hour.  该网站在第一个小时内就收到了数千次点击。|近义词：hits, visits|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
