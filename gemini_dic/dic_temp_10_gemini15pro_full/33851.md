circumnutation:/ˌsɜːrkəmnyuˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 环旋运动，回旋转头运动 | The circumnutation of climbing plants helps them find support. | Example 1: The tendrils of the pea plant exhibited circumnutation as they searched for something to grasp.  豌豆植物的卷须在寻找可以抓住的东西时表现出回旋转头运动。

Example 2:  The circumnutation of the sunflower's head allows it to track the sun throughout the day. 向日葵头部的回旋转头运动使它能够整天追踪太阳。| Synonym:  rotation, gyration, twining | Antonym: stillness, immobility | noun
