fibsters:/ˈfɪbstərz/| n.|撒谎者；编造故事的人|A person who tells lies; a fabricator.  This word is informal and often used playfully.|例句：He's such a fibster! He told me he caught a fish this big! 他真是个爱撒谎的人！他告诉我他钓到了一条这么大的鱼！

例句：The kids are little fibsters, always making up stories about their imaginary friends.  这些孩子都是些小骗子，总是编造关于他们假想朋友的故事。|近义词：liar, prevaricator, storyteller, fabulist|反义词:truth-teller, honest person|词性:noun
