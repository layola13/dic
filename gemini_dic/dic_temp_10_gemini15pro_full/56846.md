dorsality:/dɔrˈsælɪti/
n.
背部, 背侧, 背面
The state or quality of being dorsal; relation to the back or upper surface of an organism.
The dorsality of the hand is the back of the hand.  手的背侧就是手背。
The hump on a camel's back contributes to its dorsality. 骆驼背上的驼峰增加了它的背部高度。
近义词: back, dorsal aspect
反义词: ventrality
词性: 名词
