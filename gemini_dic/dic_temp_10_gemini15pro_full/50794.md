descendibility:/dɪˌsɛndəˈbɪləti/| n. |可继承性，可传递性|The quality of being descendible; the capacity of being transmitted or passed down from parent to offspring or from ancestor to descendant.  It can refer to traits, titles, property, or even genetic information.|The descendibility of the family estate was challenged in court.  家族财产的可继承性在法庭上受到了质疑。|Synonyms: heritability, inheritability, transmissibility|Antonyms: non-heritability|noun
