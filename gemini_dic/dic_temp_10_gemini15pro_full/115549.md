mapping:/ˈmæpɪŋ/| adj.|测绘的，地图绘制的|Relating to the process of creating a map or plan.|Mapping software can be used to create detailed maps of any area.  测绘软件可以用来创建任何地区的详细地图。|近义词：cartographic, surveying|反义词: N/A| adjective
