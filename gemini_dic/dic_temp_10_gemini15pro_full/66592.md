exhumation: /ˌɛkshjuˈmeɪʃən/ | n. |发掘；掘尸；（秘密等）暴露|The act of digging up something buried, especially a corpse. The exhumation of the body was ordered by the court. 法院下令挖掘尸体。 | 近义词: disinterment, unearthing | 反义词: burial, interment | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Latin *exhumationem, from Latin exhumare (“to dig up, unearth”), from ex- (“out of”) + humus (“ground, earth”).
