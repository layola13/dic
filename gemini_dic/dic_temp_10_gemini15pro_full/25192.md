buddhistically:/ˌbʊdɪˈstɪkli/
adv.
佛教式地；从佛教的观点
in a Buddhist manner; from a Buddhist perspective
He approached the problem buddhistically, seeking enlightenment through meditation.  他以佛教的方式处理这个问题，通过冥想寻求启迪。
She interpreted the text buddhistically, focusing on the concepts of karma and rebirth. 她从佛教的角度解释了这篇课文，重点关注业力和轮回的概念。
Synonym: piously, religiously
Antonym: secularly, materialistically
词性: 副词
