cutling: /ˈkʌtlɪŋ/
n.
插枝; 插条; 扦插苗
A young plant produced from a cutting.
The gardener planted several cutlings in the new flowerbed. 园丁在新花坛里种了几株扦插苗。


近义词: cutting, slip, sprout
反义词: seed


词性: noun


词源:  From the verb "to cut" + the suffix "-ling" which denotes something small or young.
