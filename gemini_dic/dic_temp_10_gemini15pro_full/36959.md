colourlessly:/ˈkʌlələsli/ | adv. | 无颜色地；苍白地；平淡地 | Without color; in a pale or uninteresting way. | The story was written colourlessly, lacking any vivid descriptions.  这个故事写得毫无色彩，缺乏生动的描写。 | The actor delivered his lines colourlessly, failing to engage the audience. 演员的台词念得毫无生气，未能吸引观众。 | 近义词: blandly, dully, palely, uninterestingly | 反义词: vividly, brightly, colourfully | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From "colourless" + "-ly".  "Colourless" comes from "colour" (from Old French "colour," ultimately from Latin "color") + "-less" (a suffix meaning "without").
