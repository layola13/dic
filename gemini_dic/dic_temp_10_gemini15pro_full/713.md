absenteeism: /ˈæbsənˈtiːɪzəm/ (US) /ˌæbsənˈtiːɪzəm/ (UK)| n.|旷工，缺勤|The habitual failure to be present at work or school.  The act of being absent, especially from work or school, often without a good reason.|High levels of absenteeism are costing the company a lot of money. 高缺勤率使公司损失了很多钱。

Absenteeism among factory workers increased during the flu season. 流感季节期间工厂工人的缺勤率上升了。|近义词：truancy, non-attendance, absence |反义词：attendance, presence|noun
