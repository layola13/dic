channel:/ˈtʃæn(ə)l/ | v. | 引导；输送；开辟；形成河道 | To direct something into a particular course. | The company is channeling its resources into research and development.  这家公司正将其资源投入研发。 | synonym: direct, guide, convey, transmit | antonym: disperse, scatter | verb
