licensers:/ˈlaɪsənsərz/
noun
许可人；授权人
People or organizations that grant licenses.  Those who give permission to someone to do or use something.
The licensers of the software retain all copyrights.  该软件的许可人保留所有版权。
licensors:/ˈlaɪsənsərz/
noun
许可方
The party granting a license.
The licensers stipulated strict terms of use. 许可方规定了严格的使用条款。
licensors:/ˈlaɪsənsərz/
plural noun
(license的复数)
A legal document giving official permission to do something.
The licensers have the right to revoke the license at any time. 许可方有权随时吊销许可证。


近义词: grantors, authorizers
反义词: licensees
