hinders:/ˈhɪndərz/ (US), /ˈhɪndəz/ (UK)| v. |阻碍，妨碍|Creates difficulties for (someone or something), resulting in delay or obstruction.|The heavy traffic hindered our progress. 交通拥堵阻碍了我们的前进。|近义词：obstructs, impedes, hampers, restricts, blocks|反义词:facilitates, aids, assists, helps, promotes|Verb (third-person singular simple present hinders, present participle hindering, simple past and past participle hindered)

hindrance:/ˈhɪndrəns/ (US), /ˈhɪndrəns/ (UK)| n. |阻碍，障碍|A thing that provides resistance, delay, or obstruction to something or someone.|Lack of funding was a major hindrance to the project. 缺乏资金是该项目的主要障碍。|近义词：obstacle, impediment, obstruction, barrier, handicap|反义词:aid, assistance, help, support, advantage|Noun
