cymiferous: /saɪˈmɪfərəs/ | adj. |  结荚的；产豆荚的 | Bearing or producing cymes.  A cyme is a type of flowering shoot where the main stem ends in a flower, and any subsequent flowers develop on lateral stems. | Example: The cymiferous plant produced an abundance of seed pods.  这种结荚的植物结出了大量的豆荚。| Example:  Cymiferous plants are common in the legume family. 豆科植物中常见的植物是结荚植物。 | Synonym: pod-bearing, legume-bearing | Antonym: sterile, non-fruiting | word type: adjective
