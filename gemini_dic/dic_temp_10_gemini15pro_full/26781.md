caesural:/siˈʒʊərəl/| adj.| 休句的；诗行中间有休止的| Relating to or marked by a caesura. A caesura is a pause or break in a line of verse, often marked by punctuation.  It can be used to create a sense of rhythm or emphasis. | The poem's caesural pauses added to its melancholic tone. 这首诗的休止增加了其忧郁的基调。|  The caesural structure of the poem emphasized the contrast between life and death. 这首诗的休止结构强调了生与死的对比。 |Near synonyms: paused, rhythmic| Antonyms: continuous, unbroken | Part of Speech: Adjective
