calcivorous: /kælˈsɪvərəs/
adjective
食钙的; 嗜钙的
Feeding on calcium; calcium-eating.  Specifically refers to organisms that eat calcium carbonate, such as certain snails and earthworms.
Snails are often calcivorous, obtaining calcium for their shells from the rocks they graze on.  蜗牛通常是食钙的，它们从放牧的岩石中获取钙来构建外壳。
The calcivorous diet of some earthworms helps to break down limestone in the soil.  一些蚯蚓的食钙性饮食有助于分解土壤中的石灰石。
synonyms: calcium-eating, lime-eating
antonyms: (No direct antonym, but one could consider) non-calcivorous
