aperture:/ˈæpərtʃər/ (US), /ˈæpətʃə(r)/ (UK)| n.|孔，缝隙，光圈|An opening, hole, or gap, especially one that controls the amount of light that passes through a lens or into a camera.|The photographer adjusted the aperture to let in more light. 摄影师调整了光圈以获得更多光线。|Synonyms: opening, hole, gap, orifice, slit|Antonyms: closure, blockage|noun
