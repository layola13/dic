factorises:/ˈfæktəraɪzɪz/
verb
分解，因式分解
breaks (a number) down into factors, or represents (an algebraic expression) as a product of factors
The number 12 factorises into 2 x 2 x 3.  12可以分解成2 x 2 x 3。
The expression x² - 4 factorises as (x-2)(x+2).  表达式x² - 4可以分解为(x-2)(x+2)。
factor, resolves, decomposes
combine, synthesize
v.
