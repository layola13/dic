courtierly:/ˈkɔːrtjərli/ /ˈkɔːrtiərli/ | adj. |  宫廷式的；彬彬有礼的；献媚的 |  Polite and elegant, as befitting a royal court; excessively flattering. | He bowed courtierly and kissed her hand. 他彬彬有礼地鞠躬并吻了她的手。 | 近义词: polite, gracious, refined, elegant, flattering, obsequious | 反义词: rude, impolite, boorish, coarse | 词性: 形容词

courtier:/ˈkɔːrtjər/ /ˈkɔːrtiər/ | n. | 朝臣;廷臣 | A person who attends a royal court as a companion or adviser to the king or queen. | The courtiers vied for the king's favor. 朝臣们争夺国王的宠爱。| 近义词: attendant, lord, lady | 反义词: commoner, peasant | 词性: 名词
