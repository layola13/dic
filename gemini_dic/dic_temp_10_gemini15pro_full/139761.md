octahedral:/ˌɒktəˈhiːdrəl/ |adj.|八面体的，八面形的|Having the shape of an octahedron, a solid figure with eight plane faces.  Relating to or characterized by an octahedron.|例句：The diamond has an octahedral structure. 这颗钻石具有八面体结构。|例句：The octahedral complex ion has six ligands. 八面体络离子有六个配体。|近义词：eight-sided, cubic (in some contexts)|反义词:tetrahedral|词性:形容词
