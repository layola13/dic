ichthyopsida:/ˌɪkθiˈɒpsɪdə/ | n. | 鱼形类，鱼纲 | A class of vertebrates comprising the fishes.  This term is now considered outdated in modern taxonomy.  It traditionally included all fish-like aquatic vertebrates. |  Ichthyopsida, a group that included fish and amphibians, is no longer used in modern classification. 鱼形类，包括鱼类和两栖类，在现代分类中已不再使用。| 近义词: fish, aquatic vertebrates | 反义词: terrestrial vertebrates | noun
