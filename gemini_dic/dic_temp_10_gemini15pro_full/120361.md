minimizing: /ˈmɪnɪmaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使最小化；将…减到最少 | Reducing something to the smallest possible amount or degree. |  例句：The company is minimizing its losses by cutting staff. 公司正在通过裁员来减少损失。
