impulse:/ˈɪmpʌls/ (US), /ˈɪmpʌls/ (UK)| n.|冲动，脉冲，刺激|A sudden, strong urge or desire to act.|On impulse, I bought a plane ticket to Hawaii. 我一时冲动买了一张去夏威夷的机票。|近义词：urge, whim, instinct, spur|反义词：hesitation, inhibition|noun

impulse:/ɪmˈpʌls/ (US), /ɪmˈpʌls/ (UK)| v.|推动，激励|To drive or urge forward.|The new engine impulses the car forward with greater speed. 新引擎推动汽车以更快的速度前进。|近义词：propel, drive, motivate|反义词：restrain, hinder|verb
