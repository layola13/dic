business:/ˈbɪznəs/ (US), /ˈbɪznɪs/ (UK)| n.|生意，商业，事务，职责，企业|A person's regular occupation, profession, or trade.  The practice of making one's living by engaging in commerce.|例句：She runs her own business. 她经营着自己的生意。|近义词：commerce, trade, industry, work, affair|反义词:leisure, unemployment|noun


business:/ˈbɪznəs/ (US), /ˈbɪznɪs/ (UK)| adj.|商业的，事务的，公务的|Relating to or concerned with commerce or trade.|例句：This is a business meeting. 这是一个商务会议。|近义词：commercial, professional|反义词:personal, private|adjective
