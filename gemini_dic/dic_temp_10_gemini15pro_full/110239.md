lifelong:/ˈlaɪflɔːŋ/| adj.|终身的，毕生的|Lasting or continuing for a lifetime.  A lifelong dream has finally come true. 一个毕生的梦想终于实现了。|近义词: enduring, permanent, lasting|反义词: temporary, short-term|词性:形容词
Etymology: "life" + "long"  Old English līf "life, lifetime" + lang "long".
