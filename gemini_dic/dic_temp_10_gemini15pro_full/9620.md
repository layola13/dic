antispreading:/ˌæntiˈsprɛdɪŋ/
adjective
防扩散的，防止蔓延的
Preventing or designed to prevent something, typically a problem or disease, from spreading.
The government implemented antispreading measures to contain the virus. 政府实施了防扩散措施以控制病毒。
The antispreading properties of the new material are impressive.  这种新材料的防扩散性能令人印象深刻。

近义词:containment, restrictive, limiting
反义词:spreading, distributive, diffusive
