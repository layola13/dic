farzon: /ˈfɑːrzɒn/ | n. |  王子; 英雄; 勇士;  | A legendary or historical hero, especially in Persian literature.  It signifies a person of high rank, often associated with royalty or nobility, recognized for their bravery and prowess.  |  Example: The Shahnameh tells the stories of many farzons. 《列王纪》讲述了许多勇士的故事。

Example:  He was considered a farzon for his valiant defense of the city. 他因英勇保卫这座城市而被视为英雄。 | 近义词: hero, champion, warrior, knight | 反义词: coward, villain | 词性: 名词

词源: From Persian فرزند (farzand), meaning "child," "offspring," or "descendant," which evolved semantically to denote a noble or heroic figure.
