interacted:/ˌɪntərˈæktɪd/
verb
互动，交流，相互作用
past tense of "interact": to act in such a way as to have an effect on another; to act reciprocally.
The two chemicals interacted violently. 这两种化学物质剧烈地相互作用。
The website allows users to interact with each other.  这个网站允许用户之间互动。
近义词: reacted, communicated, engaged, cooperated
反义词: ignored, avoided, disregarded
词性: verb
