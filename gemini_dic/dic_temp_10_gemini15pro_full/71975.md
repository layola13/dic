flimsies:/ˈflɪm.ziz/
n.
薄纸;  flimsy的复数形式; 廉价印刷品;
Thin sheets of paper, especially those used for making carbon copies, or cheap printed matter.
The contract was printed on a few flimsies.  这份合同印在几张薄纸上。
近义词: thin papers, sheets
反义词: thick papers, cardstock
词性: 名词

flimsy:/ˈflɪm.zi/
adj.
薄的; 易损坏的; 脆弱的; 不可靠的;
Light and thin, easily damaged; weak; not reliable.
The evidence against him was rather flimsy.  针对他的证据相当薄弱。
近义词: fragile, weak, thin, insubstantial
反义词: strong, sturdy, robust, solid
词性: 形容词
