fistula:/ˈfɪstʃələ/ (US), /ˈfɪstjʊlə/ (UK)| n. |瘘管；瘘|An abnormal connection or passage between two internal organs or from an internal organ to the surface of the body.|The patient developed a fistula after the surgery.  病人手术后出现了瘘管。|Synonyms: channel, passage, tract|Antonyms: closure, blockage|Noun

fistulas:/ˈfɪstʃələz/ (US), /ˈfɪstjʊləz/ (UK)| n. |瘘管；瘘（fistula的复数形式）|Plural form of fistula, referring to multiple abnormal connections or passages.|The surgeon identified and repaired several fistulas.  外科医生识别并修复了几个瘘管。|Synonyms: channels, passages, tracts|Antonyms: closures, blockages|Noun
