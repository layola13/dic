discontinued:/ˌdɪskənˈtɪnjuːd/ | adj. | 停止的，中止的，中断的 | No longer produced or offered.  |  The company discontinued the production of that model last year.  这家公司去年停止了该型号的生产。 |  abandoned, ceased, defunct, dropped, terminated | continued, ongoing, current | adjective


discontinue:/ˌdɪskənˈtɪnjuː/ | vt. | 停止，中止，中断 | To stop doing, using, or providing something. | They discontinued the old software. 他们停止使用旧软件。 | abandon, cease, stop, terminate | continue, maintain, persist | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin discontinuare, from dis- "apart, not" + continuare "to continue," from continuus "continuous."
