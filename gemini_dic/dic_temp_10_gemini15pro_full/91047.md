husseini: /ˌhʊseɪˈniː/ | adj. n. | 侯赛因的；侯赛因派信徒 | Relating to or supporting Husayn ibn Ali, the grandson of the Prophet Muhammad, or his descendants; a member of the Shia Muslim group that reveres Husayn. |  Example 1: The Husseini procession commemorates the martyrdom of Husayn. (侯赛因游行是为了纪念侯赛因的殉难。)

Example 2:  He comes from a Husseini family. (他来自一个侯赛因家族。) | Synonym: Shiite, Shia | Antonym: Sunni (in a broad sectarian context) | adjective, noun
