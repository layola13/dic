biddably:/ˈbɪdəbli/| adv.|顺从地，听话地|In a manner that is obedient and compliant; readily yielding to the will of another.|The child biddably followed his mother's instructions.|近义词：obediently, compliantly, submissively, meekly|反义词：rebelliously, defiantly, uncooperatively|词性:副词

Etymology:
From "biddable" + "-ly".  "Biddable" comes from the verb "bid" (meaning to command or request) + "-able" (suffix meaning capable of being).  The verb "bid" has Old English roots.
