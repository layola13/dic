demoniacal:/ˌdiːməˈnaɪək(ə)l/
adjective
恶魔般的；凶恶的
Of or like a demon; characteristic of demons or evil spirits.
His demoniacal laughter echoed through the halls. 他的恶魔般的笑声回荡在大厅里。
The killer had a demoniacal glint in his eyes.  凶手眼中闪现着恶魔般的光芒。

近义词:fiendish, devilish, satanic, infernal
反义词:angelic, divine, heavenly

词性:adjective

词源:Late Middle English: from late Latin daemonialis, from Greek daimōnios (see demon) + -al.
