leaping:/ˈliːpɪŋ/| adj. v.|跳跃的，跳跃|Present participle of leap.  Describes the action of jumping or springing a long way. |The leaping flames reached for the sky.  跳跃的火焰直冲天空。|
The gazelle was leaping across the savanna.  瞪羚在草原上跳跃。|近义词：jumping, bounding, springing, hopping|反义词:falling, descending, dropping|词性:adjective, verb (present participle)
Etymology: From Middle English lepen, from Old English hlēapan (“to leap, jump, run, spring”), from Proto-Germanic *hlaupaną (“to run, leap”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)lew- (“to slide, flow”).
