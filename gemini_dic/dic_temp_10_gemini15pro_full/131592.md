noncompetitive:/ˌnɑːnkəmˈpɛtɪtɪv/
adjective
非竞争性的
Not involving or encouraging competition.
The grant program is noncompetitive; all eligible applicants will receive funding.  这个拨款项目是非竞争性的；所有符合条件的申请者都将获得资金。
Noncompetitive inhibition is a type of enzyme inhibition where the inhibitor reduces the activity of the enzyme. 非竞争性抑制是一种酶抑制，其中抑制剂降低酶的活性。
Synonyms: non-rivalrous, cooperative, collaborative
Antonyms: competitive, cutthroat, vying
词性: adjective
