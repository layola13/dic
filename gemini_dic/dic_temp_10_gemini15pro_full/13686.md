attribute:/ˈætrɪbjuːt/ (US) /əˈtrɪbjuːt/ (UK)
n.|属性，特性，特征|A quality, feature, or characteristic that belongs to a person or thing.|Her greatest attribute is her kindness. 她最大的优点是善良。
attribute:/əˈtrɪbjuːt/ (US) /əˈtrɪbjuːt/ (UK)
vt.|把…归因于，认为…是…的属性|To regard something as being caused by someone or something.|He attributes his success to hard work. 他将他的成功归因于努力工作。


SYNONYM->
n.|characteristic, trait, quality, property, feature|
vt.|ascribe, credit, assign, impute|
ANTONYM->
n.|defect, flaw, imperfection|


ETYMOLOGY->
Late Middle English: from Latin attribut- ‘assigned’, from the verb attribuere, from ad- ‘to’ + tribuere ‘assign’.
