after-shave: /ˈæftərˌʃeɪv/ (US), /ˈɑːftəʃeɪv/ (UK)| n. |须后水|A lotion, balm, or cream applied to the face after shaving, typically to soothe and moisturize the skin.|He splashed some after-shave on his face. 他往脸上涂了些须后水。|Synonyms: aftershave balm, aftershave lotion|Antonyms: N/A|noun

after-shave: /ˈæftərˌʃeɪv/ (US), /ˈɑːftəʃeɪv/ (UK)| adj. |须后水的|Relating to or used after shaving.|He prefers an after-shave balm to a lotion. 他喜欢用须后膏而不是须后水。|Synonyms: post-shave|Antonyms: N/A|adjective

Etymology:  A compound word formed from "after" and "shave."  "After" indicates the time of application, and "shave" refers to the act of removing facial hair.
