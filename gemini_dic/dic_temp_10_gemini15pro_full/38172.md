compromissorial:/ˌkɒmprəˈmaɪsɔːriəl/
adj.
妥协的，和解的
Relating to or involving compromise; of the nature of or involving a compromise.
The government made a compromissorial offer to the striking workers. 政府向罢工工人提出了妥协方案。
近义词:conciliatory, mediating
反义词:uncompromising, inflexible
词性:adjective

compromise:/ˈkɒmprəmaɪz/
n.
妥协，和解
An agreement or a settlement of a dispute that is reached by each side making concessions.
They reached a compromise over the disputed territory. 他们就争议领土达成了妥协。
v.
妥协，让步
Settle a dispute by mutual concession.
We are prepared to compromise on this issue. 我们准备在这个问题上妥协。
近义词:agreement, settlement, concession
反义词:disagreement, conflict, stand-off
词性:noun, verb


词源:
From Late Latin compromissorius, from compromittere (to make a mutual promise), from com- (together) + promittere (to promise).
