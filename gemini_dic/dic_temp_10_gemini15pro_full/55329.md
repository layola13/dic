dissuade:/dɪˈsweɪd/| vt.|劝阻，阻止|To discourage someone from doing something, usually by persuasion.|例句：I tried to dissuade him from quitting his job. 我试图劝他不要辞职。|近义词：deter, discourage, prevent|反义词:persuade, encourage|Verb

dissuader:/dɪˈsweɪdər/| n.|劝阻者，阻止者|A person or thing that dissuades.|例句：The high price was a strong dissuader.  高昂的价格是一个强有力的劝退因素。|近义词：deterrent|反义词:persuader, encourager|Noun
