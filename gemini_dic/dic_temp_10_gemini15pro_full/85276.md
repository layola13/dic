近义词: compatible, consistent, reconcilable, adaptable | 反义词: incompatible, inconsistent, irreconcilable | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From harmonize + -able.  Harmonize itself comes from Late Latin harmonizare, from Greek harmonizein, from harmonia "harmony."
