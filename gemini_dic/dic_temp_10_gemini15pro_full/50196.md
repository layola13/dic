deoxygenation:/diːˌɒksɪdʒəˈneɪʃən/
n.
脱氧，去氧
The process of removing oxygen from something.
The deoxygenation of the ocean is a serious environmental problem. 海洋脱氧是一个严重的环境问题。
The experiment involves the deoxygenation of water.  这个实验包括水的脱氧。
