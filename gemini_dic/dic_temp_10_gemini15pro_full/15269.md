backlands:/ˈbæklændz/
n.
内地，腹地，穷乡僻壤
The remote areas of a country, far from the coast or urban centers; sparsely populated rural areas.
The family lived in the backlands of Brazil.  这个家庭住在巴西的内地。
The missionaries traveled deep into the backlands to spread their faith.  传教士深入内地传播他们的信仰。
hinterland, outback, bush, wilderness
coast, city, urban area
noun
