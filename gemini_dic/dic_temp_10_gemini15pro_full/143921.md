outproduces:/ˌaʊtprəˈd(j)uːsɪz/
verb
生产过剩，产量超过
produces more than something else.
例句：China now outproduces the US in steel. 中国现在的钢铁产量超过了美国。

例句：The factory outproduces its competitors by a significant margin.  这家工厂的产量远远超过其竞争对手。

近义词：surpasses, exceeds, overtakes
反义词：underperforms, lags behind
词性：v.
