druidic:/druˈɪdɪk/ | adj. | 德鲁伊的；与德鲁伊教有关的 | Of or relating to the Druids, ancient Celtic priests. | The ancient stones held a druidic significance. 这些古老的石头具有德鲁伊教的意义。 |  The archaeologist unearthed a druidic ritual site. 考古学家发掘了一个德鲁伊仪式遗址。| 近义词: Celtic, pagan | 反义词: Christian, modern | 词性:形容词
词源: From Druid + -ic.  Druid comes from Gaulish and ultimately Proto-Celtic, possibly related to words for "oak" and "knowledge".
