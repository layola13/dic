eccentric:/ɪkˈsɛntrɪk/ /ɛkˈsɛntrɪk/| adj. n.|古怪的，异常的；古怪的人|Deviating from the recognized or customary character, practice, etc.; peculiar; odd: unconventional; a person of odd or unconventional habits.|例句：My eccentric aunt collects garden gnomes. 我古怪的阿姨收集花园侏儒。

例句：He was known for his eccentric behaviour, such as wearing a top hat and tails to the supermarket.  他以古怪的行为而闻名，例如戴着高顶礼帽和燕尾服去超市。|近义词：peculiar, odd, strange, unusual, quirky, unconventional, idiosyncratic, aberrant|反义词：normal, conventional, ordinary, usual, typical|词性:形容词, 名词

eccentric:/ɪkˈsɛntrɪk/ /ɛkˈsɛntrɪk/| n.|古怪的人|A person of odd or unconventional habits.|例句：She was considered an eccentric by her neighbours. 她的邻居认为她是个古怪的人。

例句：The town was full of eccentrics and artists.  这个小镇上到处都是古怪的人和艺术家。|近义词：character, oddball, nonconformist, maverick|反义词：conformist|词性:名词
