in-style:/ɪn ˈstaɪl/| adj.|流行的，时髦的|Fashionable and currently popular.|例句：Her dress was very in-style. 她的连衣裙很时髦。|近义词：fashionable, trendy, stylish, chic|反义词：outdated, old-fashioned, out-of-style|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From "in" + "style".  "In" indicating inclusion or belonging to, and "style" referring to a particular fashion or trend.
