boulter:/ˈboʊltər/| n.|博尔特; 筛子; 粗筛; |A person who bolts or sifts.  Specifically, a worker who separates the coarser part of meal, flour, etc., from the finer by means of a bolter (sieve). Also, a machine for bolting.|Example 1: The boulter sifted the flour, removing the bran.  筛子工用筛子筛面粉，去除了麦麸。

Example 2: The old boulter in the mill wasn't working efficiently anymore.  磨坊里的旧筛子效率不高了。|近义词：sifter, screener|反义词:None|词性:noun
