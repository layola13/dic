derring-do:/ˈdɛrɪŋ duː/
n.
勇敢的行为；大胆的举动
Valiant deeds in adventure or daring; heroic exploits.
He was known for his derring-do on the battlefield.  他因在战场上的英勇行为而闻名。
近义词:bravery, courage, heroism, valor, audacity, boldness
反义词:cowardice, timidity, fearfulness
词性:名词

词源:
From Middle English *derryng do*, from *derryng* (present participle of *durren*, "to dare") + *do*, "deed".  源自中古英语 *derryng do*，由 *derryng*（*durren* 的现在分词，意为“敢”）+ *do*（“行为”）组成。
