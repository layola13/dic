absorptiveness:/əbˈzɔːrptɪvnəs/
n.
吸收性；吸收能力
The absorptiveness of the sponge is impressive.  这块海绵的吸水性令人印象深刻。
The soil's absorptiveness is crucial for plant growth.  土壤的吸收能力对植物生长至关重要。
近义词: absorption, porosity, permeability
反义词: impermeability, impenetrability
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From absorb (吸收) + -ive (形容词后缀) + -ness (名词后缀).  "Absorb" comes from Latin "absorbēre," from "ab-" (from, away) + "sorbēre" (to suck in).

USAGE->
The absorptiveness of the material is tested regularly. =  这种材料的吸收性会定期进行测试。

The high absorptiveness of the towel makes it ideal for drying quickly. =  这条毛巾的高吸水性使其非常适合快速擦干。


PHRASE->
the absorptiveness of... (...的吸收性)
