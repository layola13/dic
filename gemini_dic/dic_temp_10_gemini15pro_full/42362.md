counterassassinations: /ˌkaʊntərəˌsæsɪˈneɪʃənz/ | n. | 反暗杀，反刺杀 | The act of assassinating someone in retaliation for a previous assassination.  This involves killing someone who has themselves carried out or planned an assassination. |  The government was concerned about the possibility of counterassassinations after the death of the dictator. 政府担心独裁者死后可能发生反暗杀。|  reprisals, retaliatory killings |  peacemaking, reconciliation | noun
