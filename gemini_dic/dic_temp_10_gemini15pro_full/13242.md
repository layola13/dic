ataxy:/əˈtæksi/ /eɪˈtæksi/| n.|共济失调，运动失调|Loss of full control of bodily movements.|The patient exhibited signs of ataxia after the stroke.  这位病人在中风后表现出共济失调的症状。|Synonyms: incoordination, unsteadiness, imbalance|Antonyms: coordination, balance, stability|noun

ataxic:/əˈtæksɪk/ /eɪˈtæksɪk/| adj.|共济失调的，运动失调的|Relating to or characterized by ataxia.|His ataxic gait made it difficult for him to walk. 他共济失调的步态使他行走困难。|Synonyms: uncoordinated, unsteady, imbalanced|Antonyms: coordinated, steady, balanced|adjective
