illuminous:/ɪˈluːmɪnəs/
adjective
明亮的，光辉的
emitting or reflecting light, bright; spiritually or intellectually enlightened
例句：The illuminous moon bathed the landscape in silver light. 明亮的月亮给大地披上了一层银色的光辉。

例句：Her face was illuminous with joy. 她的脸上洋溢着喜悦的光芒。
近义词：bright, radiant, luminous, brilliant
反义词：dark, dim, obscure
词性: adjective

illumine:/ɪˈluːmɪn/
verb
照亮，启发
to light up; to make clear or understandable
例句：The streetlights illumine the road at night. 路灯照亮了夜晚的道路。

例句：A sudden idea illumined his mind.  一个突然的想法启发了他的思路。
近义词：light up, brighten, illuminate, enlighten
反义词：darken, obscure
词性: verb
