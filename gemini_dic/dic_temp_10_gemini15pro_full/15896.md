balloted:/ˈbælətd/ | v. | 投票，抽签决定 |  To have voted or cast a ballot. The process of voting, typically secret, to choose a candidate, decide on a course of action, etc.  | The committee balloted for a new chairman. 委员会投票选举了一位新主席。|  The club members balloted on the proposed changes to the rules.  俱乐部成员对提议的规则修改进行了投票。 |  voted, polled, elected |  abstained | verb

ballot:/ˈbælət/ | n. | 选票，投票 | A process of voting, in writing and typically in secret. A piece of paper used to cast a vote. |  Everyone cast their ballots in the election. 每个人都在选举中投了票。|  The ballot box was sealed after the voting ended. 投票结束后，投票箱被封存了。 | vote, poll, election |  abstention | noun
