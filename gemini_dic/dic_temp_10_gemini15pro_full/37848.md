competitive:/kəmˈpɛtɪtɪv/| adjective |竞争的，有竞争力的|Relating to or characterized by competition.  Involving competition or competitiveness.|例句：The company faces competitive pressure from new startups.  这家公司面临来自新兴企业的竞争压力。|例句：He has a competitive edge over his rivals due to his experience.  由于他的经验，他在竞争对手中拥有优势。|近义词：rivalrous, combative |反义词：uncompetitive, cooperative |词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *competītīvus, from Latin competere ("to strive together, coincide"), from com- ("together") + petere ("to seek, strive for").
