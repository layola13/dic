completenesses:/kəmˈpliːtnəsɪz/| n.|完整性，完备性|The completeness of the data is crucial for accurate analysis. 数据的完整性对于准确分析至关重要。|例句：The archaeologist was impressed by the completeness of the skeleton. 考古学家对骨架的完整性印象深刻。

例句：He checked the completeness of his travel documents before leaving. 他在出发前检查了旅行证件的完整性。|近义词：integrity, wholeness, entirety|反义词:incompleteness, deficiency, lack|词性:名词

词源: From "complete" + "-ness".  "Complete" comes from the Latin "complētus," the past participle of "complēre" (to fill up, complete), from "com-" (together, with) + "plēre" (to fill). "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, denoting a state or quality.
