bridemaid:/ˈbraɪdzmeɪd/| n. |伴娘|A woman who attends the bride at a wedding.|例句：She asked her sister to be her bridesmaid. 她请她姐姐做她的伴娘。

例句：The bridesmaids wore matching pink dresses. 伴娘们穿着配套的粉色连衣裙。|近义词：maid of honor (首席伴娘), attendant|反义词: groomsman (伴郎)|词性:noun
