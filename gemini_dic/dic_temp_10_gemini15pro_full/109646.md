leu:/luː/ /ljuː/
noun
列伊（罗马尼亚货币单位）
Romanian currency, the basic monetary unit of Romania.
Example:  The price was 50 lei.  价格是50列伊。
Example:  He exchanged his dollars for lei.  他把美元兑换成了列伊。
Synonyms: RON (ISO currency code)
Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a currency unit)
词性: 名词
