engrained:/ɪnˈɡreɪnd/ /ɛnˈɡrend/| adj.|根深蒂固的；牢固的|firmly fixed or established; difficult to change|His prejudice against women was deeply ingrained. 他对女性的偏见根深蒂固。|近义词：embedded, fixed, ingrained, rooted, deep-seated|反义词：superficial, shallow|词性:形容词

engrain:/ɪnˈɡreɪn/ /ɛnˈɡrend/| vt.|使根深蒂固；牢记|establish firmly in the mind|The lessons of childhood are often deeply engrained. 童年的教训常常根深蒂固。|近义词：implant, instill, fix, embed|反义词：erase, forget|词性:动词
