haemorrhagic:/ˌheməˈrædʒɪk/ | adj. | 出血的，伴有出血的 | Relating to or characterized by hemorrhage (bleeding). |  The patient presented with haemorrhagic fever. 病人出现出血热症状。 |  The doctor quickly diagnosed the haemorrhagic stroke. 医生迅速诊断出病人患有出血性中脑。| Synonyms: bleeding, hemorrhaging | Antonyms: bloodless, non-hemorrhagic | adjective

haemorrhage:/ˈhemərɪdʒ/ | n. vi. | 出血；大量流失（资金、人员等）|  n. A profuse discharge of blood, as from a ruptured blood vessel; bleeding. | vi. To bleed profusely. |  The patient suffered a severe haemorrhage. 病人大量出血。 | The company haemorrhaged money after the failed product launch.  产品发布失败后，公司资金大量流失。 | Synonyms: bleeding, effusion | Antonyms: clotting, coagulation | noun, verb
