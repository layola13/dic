divulgater:/daɪˈvʌlɡeɪtər/ | n. | 泄露者；传播者；揭秘者 | Someone who divulges information; a person who makes something known, especially something previously secret. | The journalist acted as a divulgater of the classified documents. 这位记者充当了机密文件的泄露者。 |  The company was quick to find the divulgater of the confidential information.  公司很快就找到了泄露机密信息的人。| Synonyms: revealer, discloser, informant,  | Antonyms: concealer, suppressor | noun

divulge:/daɪˈvʌldʒ/ | v. | 泄露；透露；公开 |  Make known (private or sensitive information). |  I am too much of a gentleman to divulge her age. 我太绅士了，不会透露她的年龄。 | She refused to divulge the source of her information. 她拒绝透露消息来源。 | Synonyms: reveal, disclose, impart, leak, unveil | Antonyms: conceal, hide, suppress | verb
