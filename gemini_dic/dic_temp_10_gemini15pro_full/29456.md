catalysing:/ˈkætəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
催化；促进
The enzyme catalyses the breakdown of fats in the body.  这种酶催化体内脂肪的分解。
The government's policies are catalysing economic growth. 政府的政策正在促进经济增长。
近义词: accelerating, boosting, facilitating, stimulating, promoting
反义词: inhibiting, hindering, obstructing, retarding
词性: verb (present participle of catalyse)

catalyse:/ˈkætəlaɪz/
verb
催化；促进
A catalyst catalyses a chemical reaction without itself being changed. 催化剂催化化学反应而自身不发生变化。
The presence of a role model can catalyse significant personal growth. 榜样的存在可以促进显著的个人成长。
近义词: accelerate, boost, facilitate, stimulate, promote
反义词: inhibit, hinder, obstruct, retard
词性: verb

Etymology:  From the Greek "katalysis," meaning "dissolution," from "kata-" (down) + "lyein" (to loosen).
