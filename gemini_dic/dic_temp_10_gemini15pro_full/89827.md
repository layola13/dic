hornet:/ˈhɔːrnɪt/ (US) /ˈhɔːnɪt/ (UK)| n. |大黄蜂；马蜂|A large social wasp with a powerful sting.|例句：A hornet stung me while I was gardening.  我园艺的时候被一只大黄蜂蜇了。

例句：Hornets build large paper nests in trees and bushes. 大黄蜂在树上和灌木丛中筑造大型纸巢。|近义词：wasp, yellow jacket|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *hornet*, from Old English *hyrnet* (compare West Frisian *hoarnbyt*, Dutch *horzel*), from Proto-Germanic *hurznutą* (compare German *Hornisse*), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱerh₂- (“horn; head”).  The original meaning was probably "horned one," referring to the antennae.
