overtight:/ˌoʊvərˈtaɪt/| adj.|过紧的，拧得太紧的|Too tight; excessively tightened.  It describes something that has been tightened more than necessary. |The screws were overtight, and the wood cracked. 螺丝拧得太紧，木头裂开了。|The guitar strings were overtight, making it difficult to play. 吉他弦太紧了，很难弹奏。|近义词：too tight, excessively tight|反义词: loose, slack|词性:形容词
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "tight" (adjective meaning "firmly or closely fixed or held").  "Over-" comes from Old English "ofer," and "tight" comes from Old Norse "þéttr."
