nonlandowner:/ˌnɒnˈlændˌəʊnə(r)/ (US: /ˌnɑːnˈlændˌoʊnər/)
noun
非土地所有者
A person who does not own land.
The nonlandowners in the village relied on fishing and trade for their livelihoods. 村里的非土地所有者依靠渔业和贸易为生。
The government implemented policies to support nonlandowners in starting their own businesses. 政府实施了支持非土地所有者创业的政策。
Synonyms: tenant, renter, leaseholder
Antonyms: landowner, landholder, proprietor
