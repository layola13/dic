creviced: /ˈkrɛvɪst/ | adj. | 有裂缝的；多裂口的 | Having crevices; full of crevices. | The old wall was creviced and crumbling.  古老的墙壁布满了裂缝，正在崩塌。| The mountaineer carefully scaled the creviced rock face. 登山者小心翼翼地攀登着布满裂缝的岩壁。| 近义词: cracked, fissured, fractured | 反义词: smooth, seamless | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From crevice + -ed.  Crevice comes from Old French crevace "crack, chink," from crever "to break, burst," from Latin crepare "to crack, creak, rattle, break, burst."
