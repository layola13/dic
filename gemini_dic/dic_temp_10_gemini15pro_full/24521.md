broadcasting: /ˈbrɔːdkɑːstɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈbrɔːdkæstɪŋ/ (US) | n. | 广播; 播送 | the transmission of programmes or information by radio or television | The BBC's broadcasting of the event reached millions.  英国广播公司对该事件的广播覆盖了数百万人。| synonyms: transmission, airing, diffusion | antonyms: receiving, suppression | noun

broadcasting: /ˈbrɔːdkɑːstɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈbrɔːdkæstɪŋ/ (US)| adj. | 广播的; 播送的 | relating to broadcasting |  The broadcasting industry is highly competitive. 广播行业竞争激烈。 | synonyms:  televising, radio, transmitting | antonyms: receiving | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb 'broadcast', which originally meant to scatter seeds widely in a field. Later, the term was adopted for the widespread dissemination of information via radio waves, and then television.
