mingled:/ˈmɪŋɡəld/| v. adj.|混合，交融；交往，交际|To mix or combine, or to become mixed or combined; to move around and talk to different people at a social event.|The scents of woodsmoke and pine mingled in the air.  空气中弥漫着木烟和松树的气味。|
The two families mingled at the wedding reception. 这两个家庭在婚宴上互相交谈。|近义词：mixed, blended, combined, socialized, interacted|反义词：separated, segregated, isolated|词性:动词/形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *minglen*, frequentative of *mengen* (“to mix”), from Old English *mengen* (“to mix”), from Proto-Germanic *mangjan* (“to mix, knead”), from Proto-Indo-European *meyǵ- (“to mix, knead”).  Cognate with German *mengen* (“to mix”), Dutch *mengen* (“to mix”), Swedish *mänga* (“to mix”).
