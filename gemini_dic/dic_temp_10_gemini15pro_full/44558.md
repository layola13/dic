crotchetiness:/ˈkrɑːtʃɪtinəs/| n.|古怪; 暴躁; |A state of being easily annoyed or irritated, often about seemingly trivial matters.  Characterized by unpredictable changes in mood and a tendency to express displeasure or disapproval.|His crotchetiness made him difficult to work with. 他的古怪脾气让他很难与人共事。|近义词：irritability, grumpiness, cantankerousness, irascibility|反义词：amiability, affability, good humor, pleasantness|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From crotchet (a peculiar notion or whim) + -iness (suffix forming nouns of state or quality).  "Crotchet" itself comes from the Old French "crochet," meaning "a small hook," perhaps referring to a whimsical or twisted idea.
