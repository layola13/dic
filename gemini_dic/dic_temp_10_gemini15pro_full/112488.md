lounging: /ˈlaʊndʒɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
闲逛; 懒洋洋地躺着或坐着
relaxing or lying down in a casual way, often for a long time
She spent the afternoon lounging by the pool. 她整个下午都在泳池边闲逛。
He was lounging on the sofa, watching TV. 他懒洋洋地躺在沙发上看电视。
近义词: relaxing, idling, lolling
反义词: working, hurrying, rushing
词性: v.
