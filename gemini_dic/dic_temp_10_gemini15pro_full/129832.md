non-hesitant:/nɒnˈhɛzɪtənt/ | adj. | 不犹豫的，果断的 | Not hesitant; resolute and decisive. Showing no hesitation; acting quickly and confidently. |  She gave a non-hesitant reply. 她给出了一个毫不犹豫的答复。| Synonyms: decisive, resolute, prompt, unhesitating, forthright | Antonyms: hesitant, indecisive, wavering, reluctant, doubtful | adjective
