crochet:/kroʊˈʃeɪ/ (US) /ˈkrəʊʃeɪ/ (UK)| n. v. |钩针编织；钩针织物|A method of creating fabric by interlocking loops of yarn, thread, or other material using a hook.  The fabric resulting from this technique.|
She made a beautiful crochet blanket for the baby. 她为婴儿做了一条漂亮的钩针编织毯。
I learned to crochet from my grandmother. 我跟祖母学钩针编织。
|近义词：knit, tat|反义词:|词性:名词, 动词
