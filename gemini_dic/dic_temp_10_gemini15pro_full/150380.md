panning-out:/ˈpænɪŋ aʊt/
|v.|淘选; 成功; (摄像机) 镜头拉远, 全景拍摄; |To separate heavier particles in a mixture from lighter ones by washing in a pan, usually to separate gold from sand or gravel. Also, to develop or turn out successfully. In filmmaking, to move a camera so as to show a wider view. |例句1：The prospector was panning out for gold in the river.  勘探者在河里淘金。

例句2：The project panned out better than expected.  这个项目比预期的要好。

例句3：The camera panned out to reveal the vast landscape. 镜头拉远，展现了广阔的景色。|近义词：succeed, work out, develop, zoom out (for camera)|反义词：fail, zoom in (for camera)|词性: 动词
