multireligious:/ˌmʌlti rɪˈlɪdʒəs/
adjective
多宗教的
Characterized by the presence or involvement of multiple religions.
India is a multireligious country with people following Hinduism, Islam, Christianity, Sikhism, Buddhism, and Jainism.
印度是一个多宗教国家，人们信奉印度教、伊斯兰教、基督教、锡克教、佛教和耆那教。

The neighborhood is known for its multireligious community, with churches, mosques, and temples all within walking distance.
这个社区以其多宗教社区而闻名，教堂、清真寺和寺庙都在步行距离之内。

近义词:multifaith, pluralistic (in religious terms)
反义词:monoreligious, unireligious
词性:adjective
