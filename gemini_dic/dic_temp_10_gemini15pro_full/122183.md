mistypes:/ˌmɪsˈtaɪps/| n. |打字错误；输入错误|Typographical errors made while typing.  A mistype can range from a single incorrect letter to a whole word or phrase entered incorrectly. |I made a few mistypes in my email and had to resend it. 我在我的电子邮件中犯了几个打字错误，不得不重新发送。|Synonyms: typos, typing errors, keying errors, misspellings|Antonyms: accurate typing, correct input|noun
