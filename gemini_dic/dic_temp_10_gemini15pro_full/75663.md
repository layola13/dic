fulciment:/ˈfʌlkɪmənt/
noun
支点，支撑
a thing that provides support or acts as a foundation for something.
The central pillar acted as the fulciment for the entire structure.  中央支柱作为整个结构的支点。
support, prop, foundation, base
hindrance, obstacle
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin fulcimentum, from Latin fulcire "to prop, support."
