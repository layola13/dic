moli:/ˈmɔːliː/
noun
茉莉；茉莉花茶
A tropical shrub or climber with fragrant white or yellow flowers.  The flowers are used to make jasmine tea.
The air was filled with the scent of moli. 空气中弥漫着茉莉花的香味。
synonyms:jasmine
antonyms:N/A
词性:n.
