deans:/diːnz/| n.|院长（复数）|A dean is a senior official in a college or university who is responsible for a particular academic area, or the head of a faculty or department in some universities.|The deans of several colleges met to discuss the new curriculum. 几位学院院长开会讨论新课程。|近义词：heads, directors, administrators|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps subordinates)|noun

deans:/diːnz/| n.|教长（复数）|A dean is a clergyman, especially an Anglican clergyman, who is the head of the chapter of canons in a cathedral or collegiate church.|The deans of several cathedrals attended the conference. 几位大教堂的教长参加了会议。|近义词：canons, clergy|反义词: (No direct antonym) |noun
