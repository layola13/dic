interliner:/ˈɪntərˌlaɪnər/| n.|衬垫;衬里;隔离层;（书籍）行间注释;夹层纸|A layer of material placed between other materials, especially between the outer fabric and lining of a garment or between the leaves of a book. Also, a ship or aircraft operating on a route between intermediate places rather than making long journeys.|The tailor added an interliner to the coat for extra warmth. 裁缝在外套里加了一层衬里以增加保暖性。|Synonyms: lining, padding, filler, insert|Antonyms: exterior, cover|noun
