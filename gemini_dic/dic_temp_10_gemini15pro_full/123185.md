monobasic:/ˌmɒnəˈbeɪsɪk/ | adj. | 单碱的；一元的 | Containing only one hydrogen atom replaceable by a metal to form a salt. |  Hydrochloric acid is a monobasic acid. 盐酸是一种一元酸。| synonym: monoprotic | antonym: polybasic | adjective
