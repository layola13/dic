migrator:/ˈmaɪɡreɪtər/| n.| 迁徙者，移民|A person or animal that migrates.  A person who moves from one place to another, especially in order to find work or better living conditions.| Many birds are migrators, flying south for the winter. 许多鸟类都是迁徙者，冬天会飞往南方。|The economic migrators were hoping for a better life in the city. 经济移民希望在城市里过上更好的生活。|synonyms: migrant, traveler, immigrant, emigrant|antonyms: resident, native, settler|noun

migratory:/ˈmaɪɡrəˌtɔːri/| adj.| 迁移的，迁徙性的|Relating to migration. Habitually moving from place to place especially at regular intervals.|Swallows are migratory birds. 燕子是候鸟。|The migratory patterns of the monarch butterfly are truly fascinating.  帝王蝶的迁徙模式着实令人着迷。 |synonyms: mobile, nomadic, wandering, roving|antonyms: settled, resident, stationary, fixed|adjective
