homothermous:/ˌhɒməʊˈθɜːməs/ /ˌhoʊmoʊˈθɜːrməs/| adj.|恒温的，温血的|Maintaining a relatively constant body temperature despite changes in the surrounding temperature.  Able to regulate its body temperature.|例句：Humans are homothermous mammals. 人类是恒温哺乳动物。

例句：Birds and mammals are homothermous animals. 鸟类和哺乳动物是恒温动物。|近义词：endothermic, warm-blooded|反义词：poikilothermic, cold-blooded|词性:adjective
词源: From Greek *homoiothermous*  *homoio-* "similar" + *thermē* "heat" + *-ous*.
