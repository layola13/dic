nonsimilitude:/ˌnɒnsɪˈmɪlɪtjuːd/
n.
lack of resemblance; dissimilarity
The nonsimilitude between the twins was striking, despite their identical genes. 这对双胞胎尽管基因相同，但彼此之间的差异却很惊人。
Nonsimilitude in design philosophy led to conflicts within the team. 设计理念上的不同导致了团队内部的冲突。
Synonyms: difference, dissimilarity, disparity, divergence, unlikeness
Antonyms: similarity, resemblance, likeness, similitude

Etymology:  From non- + similitude.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "similitude" comes from the Latin "similitudo," meaning "likeness" or "resemblance."
