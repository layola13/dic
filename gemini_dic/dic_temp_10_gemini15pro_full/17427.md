beanbag-chair:/ˈbiːnˌbæɡ ˈtʃer/ /ˈbinˌbæɡ ˈtʃɛr/| n.|豆袋椅|A chair consisting of a fabric bag filled with dried beans, polystyrene beads, or similar material, conforming to the shape of the sitter.|I love relaxing in my beanbag chair after a long day. 我喜欢在漫长的一天后在我的豆袋椅上放松。|Synonyms: beanbag, sack chair|Antonyms: armchair, rocking chair|noun
