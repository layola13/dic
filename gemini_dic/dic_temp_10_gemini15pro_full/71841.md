fleme:/flem/ | n. | 粘液；痰 | Phlegm, thick mucus secreted in the respiratory passages. |  He coughed up some fleme. 他咳出了一些痰。 | 近义词: mucus, phlegm, sputum | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English *fleem, *flem, from Old English *flēam, from Proto-Germanic *flaumaz (“flowing, flood”), from Proto-Indo-European *pleu- (“to flow”). Cognate with Dutch vloem (“phloem”), German Fliem (“phloem”). More at phloem.
