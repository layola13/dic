impasting:/ɪmˈpɑːstɪŋ/ /ɪmˈpeɪstɪŋ/
n.
厚涂颜料；堆砌；涂厚；厚涂法
the process or technique of applying paint thickly so that it stands out from the surface
例句：The impasting of the oil paint created a textured effect. 油画的厚涂法创造了一种纹理效果。

例句：The artist used impasting to add depth to the painting.  这位艺术家用厚涂法来增加画的深度。

近义词：thickening, layering, daubing
反义词：thinning, smoothing

词性：名词

词源：From Italian *impastare* "to knead," from *in- "into" + *pasta* "paste."
