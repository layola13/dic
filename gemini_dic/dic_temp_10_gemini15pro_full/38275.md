conation:/kəˈneɪʃən/
n.
意动，努力，意志力
The mental faculty of purpose, desire, or will to perform an action; volition.
She had a strong conation to succeed in her career. 她有在事业上取得成功的强烈愿望。
近义词:volition, will, desire, drive, impulse
反义词:apathy, indifference, inertia
词性:名词

conation:/kəˈneɪʃən/
词源:Late 16th century: from late Latin conatio(n-), from conari ‘attempt’.
词性:名词
