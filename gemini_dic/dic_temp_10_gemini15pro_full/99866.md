intermunicipality:/ˌɪntərˌmjuːnɪsɪˈpælɪti/ | n. | 市政互助;  跨市; 市际 |  Cooperation or interaction between municipalities.  It refers to the relationships, agreements, and joint projects undertaken by two or more separate municipalities. |  The intermunicipality agreement allows for shared resources and services.  市际协议允许共享资源和服务。| The intermunicipality transportation plan aims to improve connectivity across the region. 市际交通计划旨在改善整个地区的连通性。 |  collaboration, partnership, joint action |  isolation, separation | noun
