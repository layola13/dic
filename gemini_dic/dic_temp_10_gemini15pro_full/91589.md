hyperadiposities:/ˌhaɪpərˌædɪˈpɑːsɪtiz/ | n. |过度肥胖，脂肪过多| Excessive accumulation of body fat, usually localized.  It refers to multiple areas of excessive fat accumulation. | Example: The patient presented with multiple hyperadiposities around the abdomen and thighs.  病人腹部和大腿周围有多处脂肪堆积。| Example:  Hyperadiposities can be a side effect of certain medications.  脂肪过多可能是某些药物的副作用。 | Synonym: obesity, adiposity, fat deposits | Antonym: leanness, thinness | noun
