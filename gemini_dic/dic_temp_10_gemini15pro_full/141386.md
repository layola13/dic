operculum:/oʊˈpɜːrkjʊləm/ /əˈpɜːkjʊləm/
n.
盖, 覆盖物, 鳃盖
A lid or covering, such as the gill cover of a fish or the flap of skin covering the entrance of a snail's shell.
The operculum of the fish helps it breathe. 鱼的鳃盖帮助它呼吸。
The snail retreated into its shell, sealing the opening with its operculum.  蜗牛缩回壳里，用盖子封住开口。
synonyms: covering, lid, flap, cap, plug
antonyms: opening, aperture

operculum:/oʊˈpɜːrkjʊləm/ /əˈpɜːkjʊləm/
n.
(某些苔藓植物的)蒴盖
The lid or covering of a moss capsule.
The operculum of the moss capsule detaches to release the spores. 苔藓蒴的盖子脱落以释放孢子。
The botanist examined the operculum under the microscope. 植物学家在显微镜下检查了蒴盖。
synonyms: cap, lid, covering
antonyms: base


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin operculum "cover, lid," diminutive of operis "work, effort, deed."  Related to opus "work."
