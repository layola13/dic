over-plowed:/ˈoʊvərˌplaʊd/
adjective
过度耕作的；耕作过度的
Excessively plowed, often leading to soil degradation.
The over-plowed fields were dry and dusty, susceptible to erosion.  过度耕作的田地干燥多尘，容易受到侵蚀。
The farmer realized he had over-plowed the land and it was now infertile.  农民意识到他过度耕作了土地，现在土地已经贫瘠了。
近义词: over-cultivated, over-tilled, exhausted
反义词: under-cultivated, fallow
词性: adjective

over-plowed:/ˈoʊvərˌplaʊd/
verb
过度耕作
To plow excessively, often leading to soil degradation.
The farmer over-plowed his fields, resulting in poor yields.  农民过度耕作了他的田地，导致产量低。
They had over-plowed the land for years, stripping it of nutrients.  他们多年来过度耕作这片土地，使其养分流失。
近义词: over-cultivate, over-till, exhaust
反义词: under-cultivate
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "plowed" (past participle of "plow," meaning "to turn up the earth with a plow").  源自“over-”（前缀，意为“过度地”）+“plowed”（“plow”的过去分词，意为“用犁翻土”）。
