monetizing:/ˈmɒnɪtaɪzɪŋ/ (US) /ˈmʌnɪtaɪzɪŋ/ (UK)
verb (present participle)
盈利; 变现
The process of converting something into money or making it profitable.
The company is exploring ways of monetizing its user base.  这家公司正在探索将其用户群变现的方法。
The website monetizes its content through advertising.  该网站通过广告盈利。
synonyms: commercializing, capitalizing, profiting from
antonyms:  devaluing, depreciating
