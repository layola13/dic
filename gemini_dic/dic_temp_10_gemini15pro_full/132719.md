nondisturbance:/ˌnɒndɪˈstɜːbəns/ | n. | 不干扰，不打扰; 平静，安宁 |  The lease included a nondisturbance clause, protecting the tenant from eviction if the building was sold.  该租约包含一项非干扰条款，如果建筑物被出售，可保护租户不被驱逐。 |  例句：The contract guaranteed nondisturbance of the existing tenants.  合同保证了现有租户的权益不受干扰。

例句：The wildlife sanctuary ensures the nondisturbance of the animals in their natural habitat.  野生动物保护区确保动物在其自然栖息地不受干扰。 | 近义词：peace, tranquility, quiet, calm | 反义词：disturbance, disruption, interruption | 词性: 名词
