homoplasmy:/ˈhɒməʊˌplæzmi/ /ˈhoʊməʊˌplæzmi/
noun
同质性，同质性
The presence of only one type of mitochondrial DNA within a cell or individual.
Mitochondrial diseases are often less severe in individuals with homoplasmy compared to those with heteroplasmy.  线粒体疾病在具有同质性的人中通常比异质性的人轻微。
Homoplasmy of the mitochondrial genome is crucial for proper cellular function. 线粒体基因组的同质性对细胞的正常功能至关重要。
近义词: uniformity, homogeneity
反义词: heteroplasmy
词性: n.
