haematolysis:/ˌhiːməˈtɒlɪsɪs/ /ˌhiməˈtɑːləsɪs/| n. | 溶血; 血细胞溶解 |The destruction or dissolution of red blood cells.  This can occur in a variety of conditions, including some autoimmune diseases and infections.|  Example 1: The venom of certain snakes can cause rapid haematolysis. 某些蛇的毒液会导致快速溶血。

Example 2: The lab results indicated haematolysis, suggesting a problem with the patient's blood. 实验室结果显示溶血，表明患者的血液有问题。|Synonym: hemolysis |Antonym: haemopoiesis/hemopoiesis (血细胞生成)|noun
