cruises:/kruːzɪz/ | n. | 巡航，航游，游弋 |A journey by sea, visiting several places, especially as a holiday/vacation.  A voyage for pleasure.| We went on a Caribbean cruise. 我们去加勒比海 cruise 了。|近义词：voyages, tours, trips, sailings |反义词: flights, stays | noun

cruise:/kruːz/ | v. | 巡航，航游，游弋，以平稳的速度行驶 |To travel or sail at a moderate speed, especially for pleasure or in search of something. To travel at a steady speed, especially over a long distance.| The taxi cruised through the streets of Paris. 出租车在巴黎街头缓缓行驶。|近义词：sail, voyage, tour, travel |反义词: race, speed, hurry | verb
