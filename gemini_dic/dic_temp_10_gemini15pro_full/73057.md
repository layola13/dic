footnote:/ˈfʊtnəʊt/ (British) /ˈfʊtnoʊt/ (American)| n. |脚注，注释|A note at the bottom of a page that gives more detailed information about something mentioned in the main text.|The book has many footnotes explaining the historical context.|synonyms: annotation, endnote, comment|antonyms:  (No direct antonyms exist for footnote.  One could consider "main text" a conceptual opposite.)|noun
