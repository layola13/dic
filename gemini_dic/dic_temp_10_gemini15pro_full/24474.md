britons:/ˈbrɪtənz/
noun
英国人
People of Great Britain or the United Kingdom.
Many Britons enjoy a cup of tea in the afternoon. 许多英国人喜欢在下午喝杯茶。
synonyms: British people, UK citizens, Brits (informal)
antonyms: foreigners, non-Britons
