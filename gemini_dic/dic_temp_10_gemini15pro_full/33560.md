cigars:/sɪˈɡɑːrz/
n.
雪茄
A tightly rolled bundle of dried and fermented tobacco leaves, smoked like a cigarette.
He lit a cigar and sat back in his chair. 他点燃一支雪茄，靠在椅子上。

近义词：stogies, cheroots
反义词: (No direct antonym for a physical object, could consider "cigarettes" as a somewhat different product)
词性: noun
