acromial: /əˈkroʊmiəl/
adjective|肩峰的，肩胛骨的|Relating to the acromion (the bony process at the top of the scapula).|The acromial end of the clavicle articulates with the scapula.  锁骨的肩峰端与肩胛骨相连。|The doctor pointed to the acromial region of my shoulder. 医生指了指我肩膀的肩峰区域。|近义词: scapular|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps caudal/inferior depending on context)|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *akrōmion* (shoulder point), from *akros* (highest, outermost) + *ōmos* (shoulder).

USAGE->
The acromial process is an important landmark for shoulder surgery. 肩峰是肩部手术的重要标志。

The acromial arch protects the rotator cuff tendons. 肩峰弓保护着肩袖肌腱。

The patient complained of pain in the acromial area. 病人抱怨肩峰区域疼痛。

An acromial fracture can be a serious injury. 肩峰骨折可能是一种严重的损伤。

The acromial end of the clavicle is prone to injury. 锁骨肩峰端容易受伤。


PHRASE->
acromial process = 肩峰
acromial arch = 肩峰弓
acromial angle = 肩峰角
acromial end = 肩峰端
acromial region = 肩峰区
