dunderpates:/ˈdʌndərˌpeɪts/| n.|傻瓜，笨蛋|A foolish or stupid person.  Often used playfully or affectionately. |例句：Those dunderpates locked their keys in the car again! 那些笨蛋又把钥匙锁在车里了！

例句：Don't be such a dunderpate! 别这么傻了！|近义词：dunce, dolt, idiot, fool, nitwit|反义词:genius, intellectual|词性:noun
