haemogram: /ˈhiːməɡræm/ (UK) /ˈhiməɡræm/ (US) | n. |血象，血图| A laboratory analysis of the cellular components of blood, including the numbers, percentages, and characteristics of red blood cells, white blood cells, and platelets. | Example: The doctor ordered a haemogram to check for signs of infection. 医生要求做血象检查以查看是否有感染迹象。

Example:  The haemogram showed a low white blood cell count. 血象显示白细胞计数低。

| Synonym:  blood count, complete blood count (CBC) | Antonym: N/A | noun
