grion:/ˈɡriːən/ | n. |灰尘，沙砾| Fine particles of rock, such as sand or gravel. | The grion from the construction site covered everything in a fine layer of dust. 建筑工地的灰尘覆盖了所有东西，形成一层薄薄的灰尘。| 近义词: dust, grit, gravel, sand | 反义词: cleanliness, purity | 词性: 名词

词源:  Likely a variant or dialectal form, possibly related to "grit" which comes from Old English "greot" meaning "sand, earth, dust." Its precise origin and etymology are uncertain and not well-documented in standard dictionaries.  It may also be a regional or archaic term.
