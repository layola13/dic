individualising:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
个性化；个别化
The process of making something suitable for the needs of a particular person or thing.
例句：The teacher individualised the learning plan for each student. 老师为每个学生制定了个性化的学习计划。

例句：They are individualising the training program to meet the specific needs of each employee.  他们正在根据每个员工的具体需求，对培训计划进行个性化调整。

近义词：personalizing, customizing, tailoring
反义词：standardizing, generalizing
词性：v.


individualised:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlaɪzd/
adjective
个性化的；个别化的
Made or changed to suit the needs of a particular person or thing.
例句：The school offers individualised instruction to students with learning disabilities. 学校为有学习障碍的学生提供个性化教学。

例句：Each patient receives individualised care based on their specific medical history. 每位患者都会根据其具体的病史接受个性化的护理。

近义词：personalized, customized, tailored
反义词：standardized, generalized
词性：adj.


individualisation:/ˌɪndɪˌvɪdʒuəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
noun
个性化；个别化
The act or process of individualising something.
例句：Individualisation of treatment is crucial for effective cancer therapy.  治疗的个性化对于有效的癌症治疗至关重要。

例句：The trend towards individualisation in education is gaining momentum. 教育个性化的趋势正在增强。

近义词：personalization, customization
反义词：standardization, generalization
词性：n.


词源: From individual + -ize + -ation.  Ultimately from Latin *individuus, "indivisible."
