fenian:/ˈfiːniən/| n.|芬尼亚分子，爱尔兰民族主义者|A member of a 19th-century revolutionary nationalist organization among the Irish in Ireland and the US, dedicated to overthrowing British rule in Ireland.|The Fenians planned an uprising against British rule. 芬尼亚分子计划发动起义反抗英国统治。|Synonym: nationalist, rebel, revolutionary|Antonym: loyalist|noun

fenian:/ˈfiːniən/| adj.|芬尼亚的，与芬尼亚运动有关的|Relating to the Fenian movement.|Fenian sentiment was strong among Irish immigrants. 爱尔兰移民中芬尼亚情绪高涨。|Synonym: nationalist, revolutionary|Antonym: loyalist|adjective
