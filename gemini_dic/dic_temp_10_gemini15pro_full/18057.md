begrudgingly:/bɪˈɡrʌdʒɪŋli/
adverb
勉强地；不情愿地
In a way that shows unwillingness or resentment.
He begrudgingly agreed to help.  他很不情愿地同意帮忙。
She begrudgingly handed over the money. 她不情愿地交出了钱。
近义词: reluctantly, unwillingly, resentfully
反义词: willingly, gladly, eagerly
词性: adverb

词源:
From  begrudge + -ing + -ly.  Begrudge comes from the Old French begruchier, meaning "to complain of."
