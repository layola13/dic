injuriousness:/ɪnˈdʒʊəriəsnəs/
noun
危害性，有害性
The quality or state of being injurious; harmfulness.
The injuriousness of smoking is well documented. 吸烟的危害性已被充分证明。
synonyms: harmfulness, noxiousness, detrimentalness, perniciousness, destructiveness
antonyms: harmlessness, innocuousness, beneficence
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From injurious + -ness.  Injurious comes from Late Middle English: from Old French injurieux, from Latin injuriosus ‘wrongful’, from injuria ‘wrong, injury’.
