nod:/nɒd/ (British) /nɑːd/ (American)| n. vi. vt.|点头，打盹，点头表示|A slight, quick downward bending of the head, as in assent, greeting, or command.  To lower and raise one's head briefly, especially in greeting, assent, or drowsiness.|He nodded in agreement. 他点头表示同意。|Synonyms: bow, incline, dip, beckon|Antonyms: shake (one's head), dissent|noun, verb, intransitive verb, transitive verb
