closeable:/ˈkləʊzəbl/ (US) /ˈkloʊzəbl/| adj.|可关闭的，可封闭的|Able to be closed.  Something that can be closed, typically by means of a lid, door, or other similar mechanism.|The box is closeable and keeps the contents safe.  这个盒子是可关闭的，可以保证里面的东西安全。|Synonyms: shuttable, sealable|Antonyms: open, unclosable|adjective
