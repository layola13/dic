parochialization: /pəˌroʊkiəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /pəˌrɒkiəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
| n. |狭隘化；地方化；教区化|The process of becoming narrow in scope or interest; limited to a small area or group; specifically, the process of transferring the administration of schools or other institutions from a central authority to local authorities. |The increasing parochialization of news coverage means people are less informed about global issues.  新闻报道日益狭隘化，意味着人们对全球性问题的了解越来越少。| localization, provincialization, insularity | cosmopolitanism, globalization | noun
