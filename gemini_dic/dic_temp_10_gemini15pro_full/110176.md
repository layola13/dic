lienteric:/ˌlaɪənˈtɛrɪk/ | adj. | 食糜样的，消化不良的 | Relating to or resembling partially digested food.  Characterized by the passage of undigested food in the feces. |  The patient's lienteric stools suggested a serious digestive problem. 病人的食糜样粪便表明存在严重的消化问题。|  The doctor noted the lienteric nature of the patient's bowel movements. 医生注意到病人排便的食糜样性质。 |Synonyms: undigested, chyme-like | Antonyms: digested | Adjective
