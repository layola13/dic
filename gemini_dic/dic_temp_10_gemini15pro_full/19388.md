bicameralist:/baɪˈkæmərəlɪst/
n.
两院制拥护者；支持两院制的人
A person who advocates or supports a bicameral legislature.
The bicameralist argued that two houses would provide better checks and balances.  两院制拥护者认为，两院制能更好地实现制衡。
The senator, a staunch bicameralist, defended the current legislative structure.  这位参议员是坚定的两院制拥护者，他捍卫了现行的立法结构。
近义词:supporter of a two-house legislature
反义词:unicameralist
