nonobsessional:/ˌnɒnɒbˈseʃənəl/ | adj. | 非强迫性的；无强迫观念的 | Not characterized by obsession; not obsessive.  |  She described herself as a nonobsessional collector of antique dolls. 她形容自己是一个非强迫性的古董娃娃收藏家。 | He has a nonobsessional interest in trains. 他对火车有一种非强迫性的兴趣。 | 近义词: casual, relaxed, moderate | 反义词: obsessive, compulsive | 词性: adjective

词源:  The prefix "non-" negates the adjective "obsessional," which derives from the noun "obsession," ultimately from the Latin "obsessio," meaning "siege" or "blockade."
