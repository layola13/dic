一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃什么？
What does she like to do in her free time? = 她空闲时间喜欢做什么？


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在看什么电视节目？
What is he cooking for dinner tonight? = 他今晚在做什么晚餐？


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she bought at the store? = 她在商店买了什么？


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been studying for the past few months? = 他过去几个月一直在学习什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? = 她对这部电影说了些什么？


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? = 我看到她的时候她在想什么？


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? = 她生病之前吃了什么？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the phone rang? = 电话响之前你一直在做什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after you graduate? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气怎么样？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting tomorrow? = 明天会议上他们会讨论什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have accomplished by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will they have finished building by next summer? = 到明年夏天他们将建成什么？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):

What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业的时候她将已经学习什么多久了？


虚拟语气:

What would she have done if she had known the truth? = 如果她知道真相，她会怎么做？

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？

疑问句:

否定句:
