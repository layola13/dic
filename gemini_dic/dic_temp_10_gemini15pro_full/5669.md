altostratus:/ˌæltoʊˈstrɑːtəs/ | n. | 高层云 | A middle-altitude cloud genus belonging to the stratiform physical category characterized by a generally uniform, gray to bluish-gray sheet or layer. |  The altostratus clouds obscured the sun. 高层云遮蔽了太阳。| Synonym: cloud cover, overcast | Antonym: clear sky | noun
