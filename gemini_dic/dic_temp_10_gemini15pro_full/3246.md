affiliate: /əˈfɪliət/ (US), /əˈfɪlieɪt/ (UK)| n. v. adj.|附属机构，分支机构；联营公司；加盟；隶属|A person or organization officially attached to a larger body.|Our local TV station is an affiliate of NBC. 我们当地的电视台是美国全国广播公司的附属机构。|synonyms: branch, subsidiary, associate, partner|antonyms: independent, separate|noun

affiliate: /əˈfɪliət/ (US), /əˈfɪlieɪt/ (UK)| v. |使隶属；使紧密联系|Officially attach or connect (a subsidiary group or a person) to an organization.|The college is affiliated with the University of London.  这所学院隶属于伦敦大学。|synonyms: associate, connect, join, link|antonyms: disaffiliate, separate|verb

affiliate: /əˈfɪliət/ (US), /əˈfɪlieɪt/ (UK)| adj. |附属的；联营的|Attached, associated, or connected as a member or branch.|He’s an affiliate member of the club. 他是俱乐部的附属会员。|synonyms: associated, connected, related, linked|antonyms: independent, separate|adjective
