brisky:/ˈbrɪski/| adj.|轻快的，活泼的，兴隆的|Energetic and quick; active and lively.  Can also refer to brisk business or trade.|The dog took a brisk walk in the park.  狗在公园里轻快地散步。|近义词：quick, lively, energetic, vigorous, active, fast, rapid, swift, speedy, nimble, agile|反义词：slow, sluggish, languid|词性:形容词
词源: From the earlier brisk (adjective and noun) meaning "to move quickly, be lively," probably of imitative origin.
