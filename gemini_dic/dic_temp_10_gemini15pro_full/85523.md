hataki: /ˈhɑːtɑːkiː/ | n. | 拂尘，掸子 |  A tool, often made of feathers, used for dusting or cleaning.  Derived from Japanese 帚 (hahaki), meaning broom. |  She used a hataki to dust the shelves. 她用拂尘掸了架子上的灰。| synonym: duster, feather duster | antonym: vacuum cleaner | noun
