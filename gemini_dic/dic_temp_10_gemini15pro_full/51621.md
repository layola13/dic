developing:/dɪˈvɛləpɪŋ/
adjective|发展中的，开发中的|Growing or expanding; in the process of becoming more advanced, complex, or elaborate.|例句：The company is investing heavily in developing new technologies.  这家公司正在大力投资开发新技术。|近义词：evolving, progressing, growing, advancing|反义词：stagnating, declining, regressing|词性: adjective
developing:/dɪˈvɛləpɪŋ/
verb|发展，开发，培养|To grow or cause to grow and become more mature, advanced, or elaborate.|例句：We are developing a new marketing strategy. 我们正在制定新的营销策略。|近义词：growing, expanding, evolving, progressing|反义词：shrinking, declining, regressing|词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *desveloper, from des- "un-, out" + voloper "to wrap," of uncertain origin. The sense of "to unfold, bring out gradually" evolved from the literal meaning of unwrapping.
