This word emphasizes a deliberate departure from accepted norms, often in a rebellious or challenging way.|He dressed antiorthodoxly, preferring bright colors and unusual patterns to the somber suits his colleagues wore. 他穿着非正统，喜欢鲜艳的颜色和不寻常的图案，而不是他的同事穿的深色西装。|Synonyms: unconventionally, heterodoxly,  unusually,  idiosyncratically|Antonyms: orthodoxly, conventionally, traditionally|adverb
