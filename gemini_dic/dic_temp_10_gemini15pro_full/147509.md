overpaint:/ˌoʊvərˈpeɪnt/| v. n.|过度涂抹，覆盖涂抹|To paint over something, usually another layer of paint.  The act of painting over something.|The artist overpainted the original background with a darker color.  艺术家用更深的颜色覆盖了原来的背景。|近义词：repaint, cover, coat|反义词:strip, uncover|v.
overpaint:/ˈoʊvərpeɪnt/| n.|覆盖涂抹（一层）|A layer of paint applied over another.|The overpaint was thick and cracked. 覆盖涂抹的颜料很厚，而且开裂了。|近义词：coating, layer|反义词:base coat|n.
