growan: /ˈɡroʊən/ (US) /ˈɡrəʊən/ (UK)| v. | 呻吟，抱怨 | To moan or complain, especially in a prolonged or low voice. | He was growaning in pain after he fell. 他摔倒后痛苦地呻吟。 | The old man was growaning about the price of groceries. 老人抱怨食品杂货的价格。|近义词：moan, groan, complain, grumble | 反义词：rejoice, celebrate |词性: 动词
