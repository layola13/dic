insectivorous:/ˌɪnsekˈtɪvərəs/ | adj. | 食虫的 | Feeding on insects; insect-eating. | Example 1: Bats are insectivorous mammals.  蝙蝠是食虫的哺乳动物。

Example 2: The Venus flytrap is an insectivorous plant.  捕蝇草是一种食虫植物。| Synonym: entomophagous | Antonym: herbivorous |词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *insectum* (insect) + *-vorus* (eating), from *vorare* (to devour).  源自拉丁语 *insectum*（昆虫）+ *-vorus*（吃），来自 *vorare*（吞食）。
