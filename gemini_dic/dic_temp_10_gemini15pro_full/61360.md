embezzle: /ɪmˈbɛzəl/ (US), /ɪmˈbɛzl/ (UK)| vt.| 盗用，挪用（公款）| To illegally take money that you are responsible for, usually from a company or organization.| The accountant embezzled $50,000 from the company. 会计从公司盗用了5万美元。| The former CEO was found guilty of embezzling millions of dollars. 前首席执行官被判犯有挪用数百万美元的罪行。| 近义词: misappropriate, pilfer, defraud | 反义词: donate, contribute | 词性: verb | 词源: From Old French *embesillier "to make away with," from en- "in" + besillier "to steal, plunder."
