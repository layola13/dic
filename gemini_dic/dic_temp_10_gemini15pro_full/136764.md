nonseparation:/ˌnɑːnˌsepəˈreɪʃən/ | n. |非分离，不分离，连续性|The state of being nonseparated; lack of separation.  The principle or practice of not separating things, especially things that are naturally or logically connected.| The therapist emphasized the nonseparation of mind and body in achieving overall wellness.  治疗师强调身心合一以达到整体健康。|近义词：unity, connection, integration, cohesion, inseparability|反义词：separation, division, disconnection, segregation, partition|词性: noun

Etymology:  From non- + separation.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "separation" comes from the Latin word "separatio," meaning "a parting, division."
