anthroposophy:/ˌænθrəˈpɒsəfi/| n.|人智学|A philosophy founded by Rudolf Steiner that postulates the existence of an objective, intellectually comprehensible spiritual world accessible to direct experience through inner development.|She's been studying anthroposophy for years. 她多年来一直在研究人智学。| Anthroposophical ideas are often applied in education and medicine. 人智学的思想常应用于教育和医学领域。|近义词:theosophy, spiritualism|反义词:materialism|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἄνθρωπος (ánthrōpos, “human being”) + σοφία (sophía, “wisdom”).
