nescience:/ˈnɛʃəns/ | n. | 无知，愚昧 | Lack of knowledge or awareness; ignorance. |  His nescience about local customs caused him some embarrassment. 他对当地习俗的无知让他有些尴尬。 |  The student’s nescience of basic algebra was concerning. 这个学生对基础代数的无知令人担忧。 | 近义词: ignorance, unawareness, incomprehension | 反义词: knowledge, awareness, wisdom | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *nescientia, from Latin nesciens, present participle of nescire "not to know," from ne- "not" + scire "to know."
