hermes:/ˈhɜːrmiːz/ (US) /ˈhɜːmiːz/ (UK)| n.|赫耳墨斯; 爱马仕|Hermes is the Greek god of trade, wealth, luck, fertility, animal husbandry, sleep, language, thieves, and travel. In addition, he is the messenger of the gods. Hermes is also a luxury brand known for its high-end fashion products, especially handbags.|例句：Hermes is often depicted wearing winged sandals. 赫耳墨斯通常被描绘成穿着带翅膀的凉鞋。
