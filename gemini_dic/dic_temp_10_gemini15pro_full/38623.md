concreture:/ˈkɒŋkrɪtʃə(r)/ /kɑːnˈkriːtʃər/
n.
凝结物; 固结; 具体化
A solid mass formed by coagulation or coalescence.  The act or process of becoming concrete or solid.  A specific or concrete form or instance of something.
The wound healed into a hard concreture.  伤口愈合成硬块。
The concreture of his ideas into a workable plan took several months.  他将想法具体化为可行的计划花了好几个月。
The artist's concreture of abstract emotions into physical forms was impressive.  艺术家将抽象情感具体化为实体形式，令人印象深刻。
近义词:coagulation, solidification, concretion, materialization
反义词:abstraction, dissolution, diffusion
词性:名词
