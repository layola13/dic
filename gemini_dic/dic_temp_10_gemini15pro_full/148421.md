oversteer:/ˌoʊvərˈstɪr/ /ˌəʊvəˈstɪə(r)/| n. vi.|转向过度|A condition in which the rear wheels of a vehicle lose traction in a turn, causing the rear end to swing out.|The car oversteered on the wet road and spun out. 汽车在湿滑的路面上转向过度，打滑了。|近义词：fishtail, slide|反义词: understeer|名词
