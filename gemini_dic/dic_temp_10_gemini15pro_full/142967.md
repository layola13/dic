outbabble: /ˌaʊtˈbæbəl/
verb
胜过…的饶舌；比…更健谈；滔滔不绝地压倒
to talk more loudly, longer, or more persuasively than someone else
例句：He outbabbled his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中以滔滔不绝压倒了对手。

例句：She outbabbled everyone at the party. 她在聚会上比任何人都健谈。

近义词：outtalk, outargue, overwhelm
反义词：be quiet, listen

词性：v.
