panade:/pəˈnɑːd/ /pəˈneɪd/| n.|糊状物，面包糊，软食|A thick mixture of breadcrumbs and liquid, often milk or stock, used to bind stuffing or coat food before frying.  It can also refer to a dish made of bread soaked in broth.|The chef prepared a panade for the turkey stuffing.  厨师准备了火鸡馅的糊状物。|She softened the stale bread in milk to make a panade for the meatballs. 她把不新鲜的面包泡在牛奶里，做成肉丸子的面包糊。|近义词：binder, thickener, stuffing|反义词:|noun
