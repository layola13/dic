counterstock:/ˈkaʊntərstɑːk/ | n. | 平衡抵消物；对冲股票；（尤指）与另一股票价格走势相反的股票 |  A stock or other investment position taken to offset a potential loss from another investment.  It's a way to hedge against market volatility by investing in something that is expected to move in the opposite direction of another investment. | Example 1: He bought counterstock to protect himself from losses in his main portfolio. 他购买了对冲股票以保护自己免受其主要投资组合的损失。

Example 2: The investor used counterstock to mitigate the risk of a market downturn.  投资者使用对冲股票来降低市场低迷的风险。 | Synonym: hedge, offset, counterbalance | Antonym:  correlated asset | Noun
