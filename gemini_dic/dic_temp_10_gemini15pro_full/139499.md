obstructionistic:/əbˌstrʌkʃəˈnɪstɪk/
adjective
阻挠的；妨碍的
Characterized by obstructionism; deliberately hindering or delaying progress.
The senator's obstructionistic tactics prevented the bill from passing.  参议员的阻挠策略阻止了该法案的通过。
Synonyms: obstructive, hindering, impeding, delaying, stalling
Antonyms: cooperative, helpful, facilitating, expediting
词性: 形容词


obstructionism:/əbˌstrʌkʃəˈnɪzəm/
noun
阻挠主义；妨碍主义
The practice of deliberately hindering or delaying progress, especially in political contexts.
The opposition party's obstructionism made it difficult to govern effectively. 反对党的阻挠主义使得有效执政变得困难。
Synonyms: obstruction, hindrance, impedance, delay, stalling
Antonyms: cooperation, help, facilitation, expediting
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From obstruct + -ion + -istic.  Obstruct comes from Latin obstructere, from ob- "against" + struere "to build."
