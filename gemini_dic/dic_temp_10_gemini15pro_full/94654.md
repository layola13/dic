impressionists:/ɪmˈprɛʃənɪsts/
n.
印象派艺术家；印象主义者
A group of artists who worked in the Impressionist style, particularly in France during the late 19th century.
The Impressionists revolutionized painting with their focus on light and color. 印象派艺术家通过对光线和色彩的关注，彻底改变了绘画。
The exhibition showcased works by several notable Impressionists.  展览展示了几位著名印象派艺术家的作品。

近义词：artists, painters
反义词：(No direct antonym, potentially "realists" or "classicists" depending on context)
词性:noun
