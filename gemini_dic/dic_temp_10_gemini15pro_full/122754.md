moisturise:/ˈmɔɪstʃəraɪz/ (UK) /ˈmɔɪstʃəˌraɪz/ (US)| v. |滋润，保湿|To make something less dry, especially skin, by putting a cream or liquid on it.|I moisturise my skin every day. 我每天都滋润我的皮肤。|She uses a special cream to moisturise her hands. 她用一种特殊的乳霜滋润她的双手。|近义词：hydrate, moisturize (US spelling), cream, lotion|反义词:dry, dehydrate|词性:动词

moisturizer:/ˈmɔɪstʃəraɪzər/ (UK) /ˈmɔɪstʃəˌraɪzər/ (US)| n. |保湿霜，润肤霜|A cream or liquid that you put on your skin to make it less dry.|I always carry a moisturizer in my bag. 我包里总是带着保湿霜。|This moisturizer is great for sensitive skin. 这款保湿霜对敏感肌肤非常有效。|近义词：cream, lotion, balm, emollient|反义词:drying agent, desiccant|词性:名词
