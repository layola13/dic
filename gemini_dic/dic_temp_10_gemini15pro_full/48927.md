deftness:/ˈdeftnəs/| n. |灵巧，熟练|skill and ease in physical movement, especially in using the hands|Her deftness with the needle was remarkable. 她用针的灵巧令人惊叹。|近义词：dexterity, adroitness, skillfulness, proficiency|反义词：clumsiness, awkwardness, ineptness|noun

deft:/deft/| adj. |灵巧的，熟练的|neatly skillful and quick in one's movements|a deft movement of his wrist 他手腕灵巧地一动|近义词：dexterous, adroit, skillful, proficient|反义词：clumsy, awkward, inept|adjective
