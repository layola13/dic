epidermatic:/ˌɛpɪdərˈmætɪk/| adj.|表皮的，外皮的|Relating to or affecting the epidermis (the outer layer of skin).|The epidermatic cells were damaged by the sun.  太阳晒伤了表皮细胞。|Synonyms: cutaneous, dermal|Antonyms: subcutaneous, internal|adjective

epidermatic:/ˌɛpɪdərˈmætɪk/| n.|表皮制剂|A medicine or cosmetic applied to the skin.|The doctor prescribed an epidermatic cream for her rash. 医生给她开了治疗皮疹的表皮乳膏。|Synonyms: topical medication, skin cream|Antonyms: internal medicine, injection|noun
