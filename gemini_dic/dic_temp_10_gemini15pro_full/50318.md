deplorableness:/dɪˌplɔːrəblˈnɛs/
noun
可悲; 悲惨; 凄惨
The state or condition of being deplorable; wretchedness.
The deplorableness of the living conditions in the refugee camp shocked the visitors.  难民营里悲惨的生活条件震惊了来访者。

The sheer deplorableness of the situation brought tears to her eyes.  情况的糟糕程度让她流下了眼泪。
synonym: wretchedness, misery, piteousness, patheticness
antonym:  blessedness, happiness, fortunate, wonderfulness
n.
