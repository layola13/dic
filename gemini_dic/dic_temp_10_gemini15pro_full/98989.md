inter-visited:/ˌɪntərˈvɪzɪtɪd/
adjective
互相访问的，互相拜访的
Having visited each other; mutually visited.
例句：The inter-visited families maintained close ties for years.  互相拜访的家庭多年来保持着密切的联系。

例句：The inter-visited nations agreed on a new trade deal. 互相访问的国家就一项新的贸易协议达成了一致。

近义词：mutually visited, reciprocally visited
反义词：unvisited, isolated


ETYMOLOGY->
"inter-" (prefix): from Latin, meaning "between," "among," "mutually," "reciprocally."
"visit" (verb): from Old French "visiter," ultimately from Latin "visitare" meaning "to go to see," frequentative of "visere" meaning "to go to see," related to "videre" meaning "to see."
"-ed" (suffix): past participle marker.
