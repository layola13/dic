kinglihood:/ˈkɪŋlɪhʊd/
n.
王位，君权；国王身分
The state of being a king or queen; royal power.
He ascended to kinglihood at the age of fifteen. 他十五岁登基。
近义词：kingship, sovereignty, monarchy, dominion
反义词：abdication, deposition

词性: noun
