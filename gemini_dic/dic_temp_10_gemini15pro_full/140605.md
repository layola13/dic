olivine:/ˈɑːləˌviːn/ /ˈɒlɪˌviːn/| n. |橄榄石|A magnesium iron silicate mineral, (Mg,Fe)₂SiO₄, occurring widely in basic and ultrabasic igneous rocks and some metamorphic rocks, typically translucent olive-green or yellowish-green in color.|Olivine is a common mineral in Earth's mantle. 橄榄石是地幔中常见的矿物。

Olivine is used in some refractory materials. 橄榄石用于某些耐火材料。

|近义词：forsterite (Mg-rich endmember), fayalite (Fe-rich endmember), peridot (gem-quality olivine)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
