ligament: /ˈlɪɡəmənt/| n. |韧带|A short band of tough, flexible, fibrous connective tissue that connects two bones or cartilages or holds together a joint.|The ligaments in his knee were torn during the football game.  他的膝盖韧带在足球比赛中撕裂了。|Synonym:sinew, tendon|Antonym:bone|noun


ligament: /ˈlɪɡəmənt/| n. |纽带，联系|Something that connects or unites.|Trade is an important ligament between the two countries. 贸易是两国之间重要的纽带。|Synonym:bond, tie, connection|Antonym:division, separation|noun
