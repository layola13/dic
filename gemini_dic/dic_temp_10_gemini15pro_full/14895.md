ayuntamiento: /aˌʝuntamˈjento/ (Spanish)
n.
town hall; city hall; municipal government
A building that houses the offices of a town or city government; the governing body of a town or city.
The ayuntamiento met to discuss the new budget.  市政厅开会讨论新的预算。
The Barcelona ayuntamiento is a beautiful building. 巴塞罗那市政厅是一座美丽的建筑。
近义词: municipality, city council, local government
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "state government" or "national government" as a contrasting concept)
词性: noun
