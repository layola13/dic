dryer: /ˈdraɪər/ (US) /ˈdraɪə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 干衣机；烘干机；吹风机 |A machine that dries things, especially clothes, hair, or hands.|  I put the wet clothes in the dryer. 我把湿衣服放进了烘干机。| synonyms: dehydrator, drying machine, blow-dryer | antonyms: humidifier, wetter | noun

dryer: /ˈdraɪər/ (US) /ˈdraɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj. (comparative dryer, superlative driest)| 更干燥的 |Used to describe something that is less wet than something else.| This towel is dryer than that one. 这条毛巾比那条干。| synonyms: drier, less wet | antonyms: wetter, more humid | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From dry + -er. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *drugaz.
