orthotropal:/ɔːˈθɑːtrəpəl/
adjective
正交各向异性的
Having properties that differ along three mutually perpendicular axes.  Specifically, in botany, describing an ovule that is straight, with the micropyle at the apex and the chalaza at the base.
The orthotropal ovule is the most primitive type of ovule.  正交胚珠是最原始的胚珠类型。
The researcher studied the development of the orthotropal ovule in the plant. 研究人员研究了该植物中正交胚珠的发育。
Near Synonyms:anisotropic, aeolotropic
Antonyms: isotropic
