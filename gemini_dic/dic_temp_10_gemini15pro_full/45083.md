cubit:/ˈkjuːbɪt/| n.|腕尺，肘，(长度单位)|An ancient unit of length, approximately equal to the length of a forearm from the elbow to the tip of the middle finger, typically about 18 inches (46 centimeters).|The ark was 300 cubits long, 50 cubits wide, and 30 cubits high. 方舟长300肘，宽50肘，高30肘。|Synonyms:forearm, ell|Antonyms: N/A|noun
