machinabilities:/məˌʃiːnəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 可加工性，机械加工性能 | The ease with which a material can be machined; factors include hardness, ductility, and composition. |  The machinabilities of different alloys vary greatly. 不同合金的可加工性差异很大。|  machinability, workability | inmachinability (rarely used) | noun
