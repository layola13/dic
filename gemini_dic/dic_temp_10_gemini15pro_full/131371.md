noncircuitousness:/ˌnɑːnsərˈkjuːɪtəsnəs/
noun
非迂回性,直接
The state or quality of being noncircuitous; directness.
His noncircuitousness was refreshing in a world of evasive politicians.  在一个充满回避的政治家世界里，他的直截了当令人耳目一新。
Synonyms: directness, straightforwardness, forthrightness
Antonyms: indirectness, circuitousness, roundaboutness, tortuousness
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + circuitous + -ness.  Circuitous from Latin circuitus, past participle of circuire "to go around," from circum "around" + ire "to go."
