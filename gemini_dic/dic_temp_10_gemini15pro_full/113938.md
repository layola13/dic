mag:/mæɡ/ | n. | 杂志 | Short for "magazine," a periodical publication containing articles and illustrations, often on a particular subject. |  I read a fashion mag on the train. 我在火车上看了一本时尚杂志。 | Synonym: periodical, journal | Antonym: book | noun


mag:/mæɡ/ | v. | (informal) 偷窃; 顺手牵羊 | To steal something.  Derived from "magpie," a bird known for taking shiny objects. | He magged a candy bar from the store. 他从商店里偷了一块糖果。 | Synonym: steal, pilfer, pinch | Antonym: give, return | verb
