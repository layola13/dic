fiefs:/fiːfs/
noun
封地；采邑
An estate of land, especially one held on condition of feudal service.
The king granted his loyal knights fiefs for their service. 国王将封地赐予他忠诚的骑士以表彰他们的功绩。

The duke controlled numerous fiefs across the province. 公爵控制着全省众多的封地。

近义词:  estates, manors, domains, territories
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider "public land" or "commons")
词性: noun
