impede:/ɪmˈpiːd/| v. |阻碍，妨碍|To delay or prevent someone or something by obstructing them; hinder.|The heavy snow impeded the progress of the rescue team. 大雪阻碍了救援队的进展。|例句：The construction work impeded traffic flow. 施工阻碍了交通通行。|例句：Bureaucracy can impede progress. 官僚主义会阻碍进步。|近义词：hinder, obstruct, hamper, block, thwart|反义词：facilitate, aid, assist, help, expedite|词性：动词|词源：From Latin impedire "to shackle the feet of," from in- "in, on" + pes (genitive pedis) "foot."
