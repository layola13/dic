coheiresses:/koʊˈɛrɪsɪz/
n.
女共同继承人
A female who inherits property jointly with another or others.
The coheiresses will split the family fortune. 女共同继承人们将平分家族财产。

The two sisters were coheiresses to their father's estate. 这两姐妹是她们父亲遗产的共同继承人。

Synonyms: joint heiress,  co-inheritor (female)
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, possibly "disinherited")
