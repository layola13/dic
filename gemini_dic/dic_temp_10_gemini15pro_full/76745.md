game-changing:/ˈɡeɪmˌtʃeɪndʒɪŋ/
adjective
改变游戏规则的; 具有颠覆性的
Having a significant and disruptive effect on the existing situation or market.
The development of the internet was a game-changing innovation. 互联网的发展是一项颠覆性的创新。
Revolutionary, transformative, groundbreaking, disruptive
Insignificant, minor, trivial
adjective
