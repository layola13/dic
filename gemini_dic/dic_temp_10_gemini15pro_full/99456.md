interfaced:/ˈɪntərfeɪst/
verb|连接，交互|To connect or interact with another system or person, often through a specific device or program.|The new software interfaced seamlessly with the existing system.  新软件与现有系统无缝连接。|synonym: connect, interact, integrate|antonym: disconnect, separate|v.

interfaced:/ˈɪntərfeɪst/
adjective|已连接的，交互的|Connected or interacting with another system or person.|The interfaced systems worked together efficiently.  连接的系统一起高效地工作。|synonym: connected, integrated|antonym: disconnected, separate|adj.

interface:/ˈɪntərfeɪs/
noun|界面，接口|A point where two systems, subjects, organizations, etc., meet and interact.|The user interface is very intuitive.  用户界面非常直观。|synonym: connection, junction, link|antonym: separation, division|n.
