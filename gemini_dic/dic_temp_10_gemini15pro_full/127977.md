neothalamic:/ˌniːoʊθəˈlæmɪk/
adjective
新丘脑的
Relating to the neocortex, the most recently evolved part of the cerebral cortex, and the thalamus, a relay center in the brain.  Specifically, referring to the connections and interactions between these two regions.
The neothalamic pathways are crucial for higher-order cognitive functions. 新丘脑通路对于高级认知功能至关重要。
Neocortical lesions can disrupt neothalamic communication, leading to cognitive deficits. 新皮质病变会扰乱新丘脑的交流，导致认知缺陷。
Synonyms: None (highly specific term)
Antonyms: None (highly specific term)
词性: adjective
