discophora:/dɪˈskɒfərə/ | n. |盘菌纲|A large class of Ascomycete fungi, formerly called Discomycetes, that produce their asci in an open ascocarp (the ascoma).|The morels and truffles belong to the discophora.|近义词: Discomycetes|反义词: N/A| noun
