heads-up:/ˈhɛdzˌʌp/ (US), /ˈhedzˌʌp/ (UK)| n.| 预先通知; 提醒; 警示 |A piece of advance information or a warning.| "Just a heads-up, the meeting has been moved to 3 pm."  提前通知一下，会议改到下午3点了。| synonyms: warning, alert, notification| antonyms: surprise, ambush| noun

heads-up:/ˈhɛdzˌʌp/ (US), /ˈhedzˌʌp/ (UK)| adj.| 警惕的; 注意的 |Alert and aware of your surroundings.| "Stay heads-up while walking at night."  晚上走路要保持警惕。| synonyms: alert, vigilant, attentive| antonyms: unaware, oblivious, inattentive| adjective
