camerae:/ˈkæmərɪː/
noun
chambers, rooms
A plural form of camera, meaning "rooms" or "chambers," primarily used in anatomical and biological contexts.
The heart has four camerae. 心脏有四个腔室。
The foraminifera builds its shell with multiple camerae. 有孔虫用多个腔室构建它的外壳。
