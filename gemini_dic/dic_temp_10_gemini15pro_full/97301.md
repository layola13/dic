infomania:/ˌɪnfəˈmeɪniə/ | n. | 信息癖；信息强迫症 | An excessive enthusiasm for acquiring and processing information, especially news and current affairs.  An obsessive need to acquire information. |  He suffers from infomania, constantly checking his phone for news updates. 他患有信息癖，不断查看手机上的新闻更新。| Example: She has infomania and is constantly checking her social media feeds. 她有信息癖，不断查看她的社交媒体订阅。| Synonyms: information overload, data addiction, news addiction | Antonyms: apathy, indifference | noun
