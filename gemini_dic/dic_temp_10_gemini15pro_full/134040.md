noniconoclastic:/ˌnɒnˌaɪkɒnəˈklæstɪk/
adjective
非偶像破坏的; 不反对传统信仰的
Not iconoclastic; not characterized by attack on cherished beliefs or institutions.
Her noniconoclastic approach to art was refreshing in a world obsessed with novelty.  她在艺术上不反对传统信仰的做法，在一个痴迷于新奇事物的世界上令人耳目一新。
He took a noniconoclastic stance on the issue, preferring to uphold tradition. 他在这个问题上采取了不反对传统信仰的立场，宁愿坚持传统。
synonym:conservative, traditional, orthodox, conventional
antonym:iconoclastic, radical, revolutionary, rebellious
词性:adjective
