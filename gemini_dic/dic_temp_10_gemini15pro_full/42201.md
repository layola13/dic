councillor:/ˈkaʊnsələr/ (UK) /ˈkaʊnsələr/ (US)| n. |议员，市政委员|A member of a council, especially a local government council. |例句：The councillor voted against the proposed development.  这位议员投票反对拟议的开发项目。

例句：The local councillors are responsible for providing services for their constituents. 当地议员负责为其选民提供服务。

近义词：councilman, councilwoman, council member|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *conseillier, from Latin consiliarius (“advisor”), from consilium (“council, deliberation”).
