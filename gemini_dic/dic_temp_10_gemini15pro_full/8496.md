antibodies:/ˈæntibɒdiz (UK) /ˈæntibɑːdiz (US)| n. |抗体|A blood protein produced in response to and counteracting a specific antigen. Antibodies combine chemically with substances which the body recognizes as alien, such as bacteria, viruses, and foreign substances in the blood.|例句：The antibodies attack the infection. 抗体攻击感染。

例句：Her body has developed antibodies against the disease. 她的身体已经产生了抵抗这种疾病的抗体。|近义词：immunoglobulins|反义词: antigens|词性:noun
