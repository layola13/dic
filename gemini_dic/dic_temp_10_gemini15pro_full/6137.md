amianth:/ˈæmiænθ/
n.
石棉
A fibrous mineral consisting of hydrated silicates of magnesium, calcium, and iron, used in fireproofing and insulation.
Asbestos, or amianth, was once a common building material. 石棉，或称作石棉，曾经是一种常见的建筑材料。
近义词:asbestos
反义词: (No direct antonym for a material)
