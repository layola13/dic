crap-shot:/ˈkræpˌʃɑːt/| n.|胡乱猜测，碰运气，冒险一试|A wild guess or a risky attempt with little chance of success; something done or said without much thought or planning.|He took a crap-shot and invested all his money in the new company. 他孤注一掷，把所有的钱都投资到这家新公司了。|近义词:long shot, gamble, guess|反义词:calculated risk, sure thing|名词

crap-shot:/ˈkræpˌʃɑːt/| adj.|碰运气的，冒险的|Done or said without much thought or planning; risky.|It was a crap-shot decision, but it paid off. 这是一个冒险的决定，但最终成功了。|近义词:risky, haphazard, speculative|反义词:calculated, planned, deliberate|形容词
