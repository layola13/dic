equals:/ˈiːkwəlz/ | v. | 等于；相当于 | is the same in number, amount, degree, rank, or quality as (something else) |  Five plus five equals ten. 五加五等于十。 | synonyms: is equal to, is equivalent to, amounts to, corresponds to, is tantamount to, adds up to, comes to | antonyms: differs from, is unequal to | verb

equals:/ˈiːkwəlz/ | n. | 相等物；等同物 | a person or thing that is equal to another. |  He treats all his employees as equals. 他平等地对待所有员工. | synonyms: peer, counterpart, equivalent | antonyms: opposite, unequal | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *equalis*, from Latin *aequālis* (“equal, similar, uniform, level”), from *aequus* (“level, even, equal, just, fair, calm”).
