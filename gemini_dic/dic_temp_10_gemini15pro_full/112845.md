lucked:/lʌkt/
verb
幸运的；侥幸的
past tense and past participle of luck
He lucked out and found a great job. 他很幸运，找到了一份很棒的工作。
近义词: fortunate, lucky
反义词: unfortunate, unlucky
词性: verb
