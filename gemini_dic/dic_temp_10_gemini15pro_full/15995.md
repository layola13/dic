例句：The con man used bamboozlement to trick the elderly woman out of her savings. 骗子用欺骗手段骗取了老妇人的积蓄。


近义词：deception, trickery, chicanery, hoodwinking, beguilement|
反义词：clarity, honesty, transparency|
