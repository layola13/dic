abhorrently:/əbˈhɔːrəntli/ /əbˈhɑːrəntli/
adverb
令人憎恶地；可恨地
in a manner that inspires disgust and loathing.
His racism was expressed abhorrently through his hateful speeches. 他令人憎恶地通过仇恨言论表达了他的种族主义。

She treated the homeless man abhorrently, refusing even to acknowledge his presence. 她以令人憎恶的方式对待那个无家可归的人，甚至拒绝承认他的存在。

近义词:detestably, hatefully, loathsomely, repugnantly, repellently, offensively
反义词:admirably, appealingly, attractively, delightfully, pleasantly
词性:副词

词源: From abhorrent + -ly.  Abhorrent comes from the Latin abhorrēns, present participle of abhorrēre "to shrink back from, shudder at," from ab- "away from" + horrēre "to bristle, shudder."
