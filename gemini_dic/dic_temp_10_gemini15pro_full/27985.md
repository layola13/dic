canvasing:/ˈkænvəsɪŋ/
v.|游说；拉票；兜售|The act of soliciting votes, opinions, or sales.|例句：They are canvasing the neighborhood for their candidate. 他们在社区为他们的候选人拉票。|近义词：soliciting, campaigning, surveying|反义词：None|词性:动词

词源：From the noun "canvas," referring to sailors' use of canvas sails, extended to mean "to spread or toss about" and then "to solicit."  源自名词 "canvas"（帆布），指水手使用帆布帆，引申为“散布或四处奔走”，然后是“征求”。
