capitalizing:/ˈkæpɪtəˌlaɪzɪŋ/ /ˈkæpɪtlˌaɪzɪŋ/
v.
大写；利用；使资本化
present participle of capitalize
Capitalizing the first letter of a sentence is a basic rule in English.  把句子的首字母大写是英语的基本规则。
He is capitalizing on his experience to start a new business. 他正在利用他的经验创办一家新公司。
近义词: uppercase, exploit, leverage
反义词: lowercase
