adoptabilities:/əˌdɒptəˈbɪlətiz/
noun
可采用性；可接受性
The quality of being adoptable; the state of being capable of being adopted.  This can refer to children, animals, ideas, technologies, etc.
The adoptabilities of the new software are high, making it attractive to businesses.  这款新软件的可接受性很高，对企业很有吸引力。
The agency assessed the adoptabilities of the children in their care.  该机构评估了他们所照顾的儿童的可收养性。
synonyms: suitability, acceptability, eligibility
antonyms: unsuitability, unacceptability, ineligibility
词性:n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From "adoptable" + "-ities" (suffix forming plural nouns of quality or state). "Adoptable" comes from "adopt" (from Latin "adoptare," frequentative of "ad-optare" - "to choose for oneself") + "-able" (suffix meaning "capable of being").
