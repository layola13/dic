amphitheatrical:/ˌæmfɪθiˈætrɪk(ə)l/
adjective
圆形剧场的，圆形剧场式的，像圆形剧场的
Resembling or characteristic of an amphitheater; having the form or arrangement of an amphitheater, especially in being semicircular or oval in shape and rising in tiers.
The seating arrangement was amphitheatrical, allowing everyone a good view of the stage.  座位安排是圆形剧场式的，每个人都能清楚地看到舞台。
The valley had an amphitheatrical shape, with the mountains rising steeply on all sides. 山谷呈圆形剧场状，四面群山陡峭。
近义词:  arena-like, stadium-like, tiered
反义词:  flat, level
