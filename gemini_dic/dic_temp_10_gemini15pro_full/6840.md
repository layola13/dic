anathematic:/ˌænəˈθɪmətɪk/ | adj. | 可憎的；被诅咒的；令人厌恶的 | Something that is anathematic is something that is loathed or cursed. It evokes strong feelings of disapproval or condemnation. |  His political views were anathematic to the majority of the voters. 他的政治观点令大多数选民深恶痛绝。 | Synonyms: accursed, execrable, abhorrent, detestable | Antonyms: blessed, sacred, hallowed | adjective


anathematic:/ˌænəˈθɪmətɪk/ | n. | 令人厌恶的人或事 | Something that is intensely disliked or loathed. | Racism is anathematic to a just society. 种族主义对于一个公正的社会来说是令人深恶痛绝的。| Synonyms: abomination, anathema | Antonyms: blessing, ideal | noun
