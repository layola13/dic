europium:/jʊˈrəʊpiəm/ | n. |  европий(évrobiĭ) |A chemical element, atomic number 63, a soft, silvery-white metal of the lanthanide series. It is used in some nuclear reactors and to make red phosphors for television screens. | Europium is used in control rods for nuclear reactors. 铕被用于核反应堆的控制棒。

Europium is also used in lasers. 铕也被用于激光器中。 | 近义词: (As an element, it doesn't have direct synonyms, but other lanthanides like gadolinium or samarium have some overlapping applications.) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
