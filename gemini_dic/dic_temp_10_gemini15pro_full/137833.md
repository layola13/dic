nonvibrations:/ˌnɒnvaɪˈbreɪʃənz/ /ˌnɑːnvaɪˈbreɪʃənz/
noun
无振动; 非振动
Absence or lack of vibrations.  The state of being free from vibrations or oscillations.
The sensor detected nonvibrations, indicating the machine was off. 传感器检测到无振动，表明机器已关闭。
The building's nonvibrations during the earthquake were surprising.  地震期间建筑物没有震动，真是令人惊讶。
近义词: stillness, quiet, inactivity
反义词: vibrations, oscillations, tremors
词性: noun


Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
vibration (noun): from Late Latin vibrationem (nominative vibratio) "a shaking, brandishing," noun of action from past-participle stem of vibrare "to shake, brandish," from PIE root *weip- "to turn, vacillate, tremble convulsively."
