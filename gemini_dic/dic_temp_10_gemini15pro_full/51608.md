devastavit:/ˌdeɪvəˈsteɪvɪt/ | v. | 摧毁，破坏（尤指土地或建筑物）| He devastated the town with his army. 他用他的军队摧毁了这个城镇。 |The hurricane devastated the coastal region. 飓风摧毁了沿海地区。| synonyms: destroyed, ravaged, ruined, wrecked | antonyms: built, created, constructed, repaired | verb

Etymology:
From Latin *devastare*, from *de-* (completely) + *vastare* (to lay waste), from *vastus* (empty, desolate).
