beeline:/ˈbiːlaɪn/| vi.|直奔，径直前往|To go directly and quickly towards something or someone.|He beelined for the exit as soon as the meeting was over. 他一开完会就直奔出口。|近义词：rush, dash, head straight for|反义词:meander, wander|verb

beeline:/ˈbiːlaɪn/| n.|直线，捷径|A straight line or direct route to something.|She made a beeline for the cake table. 她径直走向蛋糕桌。|近义词：shortcut, direct route|反义词:detour, indirect route|noun
