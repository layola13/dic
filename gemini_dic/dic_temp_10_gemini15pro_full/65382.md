eupathy:/ˈjuːpəθi/
n.
心情舒畅；幸福感；欣快症
A feeling of well-being or elation.
She experienced a sense of eupathy after completing the marathon.  她跑完马拉松后感到心情舒畅。
The medication induced a state of eupathy in the patient.  药物使病人产生了欣快感。
近义词：well-being, happiness, elation, euphoria
反义词：dysphoria, depression, sadness
