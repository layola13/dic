intermingles:/ˌɪntərˈmɪŋɡlz/ | v. | 混合，交织 |mixes or blends together | The story intermingles fact and fiction.  这个故事交织着事实和虚构。| synonyms: blend, mix, merge, combine | antonyms: separate, divide, segregate | verb

intermingled:/ˌɪntərˈmɪŋɡld/ | adj. | 混合的，交织的 | mixed or blended together | The dancers' movements were intermingled with the music. 舞蹈演员的动作与音乐交织在一起。 | synonyms: blended, mixed, merged, combined | antonyms: separate, divided, segregated | adjective

Etymology: From Middle English *intermenglen, from inter- + menglen (“to mingle”). Equivalent to inter- +‎ mingle.
