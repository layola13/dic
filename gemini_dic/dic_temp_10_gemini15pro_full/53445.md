disapprobation:/ˌdɪsæprəˈbeɪʃən/ | n. | 不赞成，非难，反对 | Strong disapproval; condemnation. |  Her disapproval was evident in her pursed lips. 她的不赞成从她紧闭的双唇中显露出来。

The government's new policy met with widespread disapprobation. 政府的新政策遭到了广泛的反对。| 近义词：disapproval, condemnation, censure, objection | 反义词：approval, praise, commendation | 词性: 名词

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin disapprobatio(n-), from the verb disapprobare, from dis- ‘not’ + approbare (see approve).
