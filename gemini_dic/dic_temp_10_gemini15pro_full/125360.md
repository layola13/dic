multiloquence:/ˌmʌltɪˈloʊkwəns/ | n. | 多言，饶舌，健谈 | The state or quality of talking much or at length. | His multiloquence made it difficult to get a word in edgewise.  他太健谈了，别人很难插话。| 近义词: loquacity, garrulity, volubility, talkativeness | 反义词: taciturnity, reticence, conciseness, brevity | noun

multiloquent:/ˌmʌltɪˈloʊkwənt/ | adj. | 多言的，饶舌的，健谈的 |  Given to much talking; loquacious. | The multiloquent speaker droned on for hours.  这位多言的演讲者喋喋不休地讲了几个小时。| 近义词: loquacious, garrulous, voluble, talkative | 反义词: taciturn, reticent, concise, brief | adjective
