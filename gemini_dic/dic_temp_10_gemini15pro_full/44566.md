crotonylene:/ˈkroʊtənɪˌlin/ | n. |巴豆炔，丁炔|An unsaturated hydrocarbon, CH₃C≡CH, of the acetylene series.  A colorless gas with a sweetish odor. Used in organic synthesis.|Crotonylene is a highly reactive compound used in the production of certain synthetic rubbers. 巴豆炔是一种高活性化合物，用于生产某些合成橡胶。|Synonym: butyne, 1-butyne|Antonym: N/A (Chemical compounds typically don't have direct antonyms)|noun
