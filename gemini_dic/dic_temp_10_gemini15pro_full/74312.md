fracture:/ˈfræktʃər/ (US), /ˈfræktʃə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|骨折；断裂；破裂；(使)断裂，(使)折断|A fracture is a break, usually in a bone. As a verb, it means to cause something to break.|
The X-ray showed a fracture in his leg.  X光显示他的腿骨折了。

He fractured his skull in the accident.  他在事故中颅骨骨折。

|近义词：break, crack, rupture, split|反义词：mend, repair, heal|词性: noun, verb
