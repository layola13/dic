exhume:/ɪɡˈzjuːm/ /ɛksˈhjuːm/ |v.|掘出;发掘;挖出|To dig out (something buried, especially a corpse) from the earth.|The archaeologists exhumed the ancient king's remains. 考古学家挖出了古代国王的遗骸。

The police exhumed the body to conduct further forensic tests. 警方挖出尸体进行进一步的法医鉴定。|近义词:disinter, unearth, uncover|反义词:bury, inter|词性:动词
