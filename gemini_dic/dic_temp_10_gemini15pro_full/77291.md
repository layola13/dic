gatecrashing: /ˈɡeɪtkræʃɪŋ/
n.
擅自闯入，不请自来
The act of entering a party or other event without an invitation.
He was caught gatecrashing a wedding. 他被抓到在婚礼上不请自来。
intruding, barging in, crashing
invited entry, planned attendance
noun

gate-crash: /ˈɡeɪtkræʃ/
v.
擅自闯入，不请自来
To enter a party or other event without an invitation.
They gate-crashed the party and were eventually asked to leave. 他们擅自闯入派对，最终被要求离开。
intrude, barge in, crash
be invited, attend with permission
verb
