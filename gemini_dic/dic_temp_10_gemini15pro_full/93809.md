immethodical:/ˌɪmɪˈθɒdɪkəl/
adjective
无条理的，没有方法的
Lacking method or order; unsystematic.
例句：His immethodical approach to studying resulted in poor grades. 他无条理的学习方法导致成绩很差。

例句：Her desk was an immethodical jumble of papers and books. 她的桌子乱七八糟地堆满了文件和书籍。

近义词：disorganized, unsystematic, haphazard, chaotic, random
反义词：methodical, systematic, organized, orderly


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + methodical (systematic, orderly).  Methodical comes from the Greek "methodos" (way, pursuit).
