nods:/nɒdz/ (British) /nɑːdz/ (American)| v. (third-person singular simple present nods, present participle nodding, simple past and past participle nodded)|点头，打盹，摆动|A slight, quick downward bending of the head, as in assent, greeting, or command.  Also, to fall asleep briefly or involuntarily.|She nods in agreement. 她点头表示同意。|Synonyms: bow, incline, dip, acknowledge, signal, doze|Antonyms: shake (one's head), dissent, disagree|verb
