glottis:/ˈɡlɒtɪs/ (US) /ˈɡlɑːtɪs/| n. |声门|The part of the larynx consisting of the vocal cords and the slit-like opening between them.|During swallowing, the glottis closes to prevent food from entering the trachea.  吞咽时，声门会关闭以防止食物进入气管。|Synonyms: rima glottidis|Antonyms: N/A|noun

glottic:/ˈɡlɒtɪk/ (US) /ˈɡlɑːtɪk/| adj. |声门的|Relating to the glottis.|Glottic closure is essential for producing certain sounds. 声门闭合对于发出某些声音至关重要。|Synonyms: laryngeal|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
