maceration:/ˌmæsəˈreɪʃn/| n.|浸渍，软化|The process of softening or breaking down by soaking in a liquid.  This can refer to softening food by soaking, or to the decomposition of tissues by soaking.|The chef used a maceration technique to tenderize the fruit.  厨师使用浸渍技术来软化水果。|The pathologist noted the maceration of the tissue sample. 病理学家注意到组织样本的软化。|soaking, steeping, softening|dehydration, drying|noun
