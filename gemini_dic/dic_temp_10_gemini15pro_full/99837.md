intermittency:/ˌɪntərˈmɪtənsi/ | n. | 间歇性，断断续续 |The intermittency of the signal made it difficult to understand. 信号断断续续，难以理解。|  The wind blew intermittently, starting and stopping throughout the day. 风断断续续地吹，一整天都在刮一阵停一阵。| synonyms: discontinuity, irregularity, sporadicity | antonyms: continuity, constancy, regularity | noun


intermittent: /ˌɪntərˈmɪtənt/ | adj. |  间歇的，断断续续的 | The intermittent rain showers throughout the day made it difficult to plan outdoor activities.  一整天的阵雨使得安排户外活动很困难。|  He suffered from intermittent headaches. 他患有间歇性头痛。 | synonyms: sporadic, occasional, periodic | antonyms: continuous, constant, incessant | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *intermittent- (stem of intermittens), present participle of intermittere “to discontinue, interrupt, cease,” from inter- “between” + mittere “to send, let go.”
