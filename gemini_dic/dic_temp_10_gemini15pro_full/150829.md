paraglided:/ˈpærəɡlaɪdɪd/
verb
滑翔伞飞行过；滑翔伞降落
descended to the ground by paraglider.
例句：He paraglidered down the mountain. 他乘滑翔伞飞下山。

例句：She had paraglidered across the valley many times.  她已经多次乘滑翔伞飞越山谷。

近义词：glided, soared, floated
反义词：climbed, ascended

词性: v.
