nondistributiveness:/ˌnɒndɪˈstrɪbjʊtɪvnəs/ | n. | 非分配性 | The property of a logical or mathematical operation where the operation on a whole is not equivalent to the operation performed on each part separately and then combined.  |  The nondistributiveness of multiplication over addition in some algebraic structures is a key concept.  非分配性在某些代数结构中乘法对加法的非分配性是一个关键概念。 | non-distributivity | distributiveness | noun

nondistributive:/ˌnɒndɪˈstrɪbjʊtɪv/ | adj. | 非分配的 |  Describing an operation that doesn't distribute over another operation. |  Subtraction is nondistributive over addition. 减法对加法是非分配的。 | non-distributive | distributive | adjective
