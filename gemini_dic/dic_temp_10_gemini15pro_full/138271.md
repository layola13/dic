notables:/ˈnoʊtəblz/| n.| 显要人物；名流；值得注意的人（或事物）| people or things that are important or famous | The guest list included notables from the world of art and politics. 嘉宾名单中包括来自艺术界和政界的名人。| synonyms: dignitaries, VIPs, luminaries, celebrities | antonyms: unknowns, nonentities | noun

notable:/ˈnoʊtəbl/| adj.| 值得注意的；显著的；著名的| deserving of attention or notice; remarkable |  A notable feature of the landscape is the ancient volcano.  该景观的一个显著特征是古老的火山。| synonyms: significant, important, prominent, remarkable, outstanding, distinguished | antonyms: insignificant, unremarkable, obscure, ordinary | adjective


Etymology:
From Old French notable, from Late Latin notabilis "remarkable," from Latin notare "to mark, note," from nota "mark, sign."
