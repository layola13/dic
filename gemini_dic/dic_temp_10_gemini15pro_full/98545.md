instillatory:/ɪnˈstɪlətɔːri/ |adj.|灌输的，逐渐灌输的|Relating to instillation; tending or serving to instill.|The teacher used instillatory methods to impart moral values to her students. 老师用循循善诱的方法向她的学生灌输道德价值观。|近义词:inculcating, implanting, infusing|反义词:extracting, removing|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin "instillatus," past participle of "instillare" (to instill), from "in-" (in, into) + "stilla" (drop).
