lychee:/ˈliːtʃiː/
n.
荔枝
A small rounded fruit with sweet white scented flesh, a brittle shell, and a large central stone.
The lychee is a popular fruit in Southeast Asia. 荔枝是东南亚的热门水果。
近义词:litchi
词性:名词
