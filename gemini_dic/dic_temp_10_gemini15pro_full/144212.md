outsize:/ˈaʊtsaɪz/| adj. n.|特大的，超大的；特大号|Exceeding the usual or standard size.  Something that is larger than the usual or standard size. |This store specializes in outsize clothing for larger men. 这家商店专门为身材高大的男士提供特大号服装。|The demand for outsize shoes has increased dramatically. 对特大号鞋的需求急剧增加。|近义词：oversized, jumbo, giant, huge, enormous|反义词：small, tiny, petite, miniature|词性:形容词,名词


outsizes:/ˈaʊtsaɪzɪz/| n.|特大号，特大尺寸|Plural form of "outsize," referring to multiple items that exceed the standard size.|This shop stocks outsizes in shoes and clothing.  这家商店备有特大号的鞋子和服装。|They specialize in outsizes for tall and large individuals.  他们专门为高大的人提供特大号服装。|近义词：oversized items, plus sizes, large sizes|反义词：small sizes, standard sizes|词性:名词

词源:  "Out-" (meaning "beyond" or "outside") + "size."  It literally means "outside the usual size."
