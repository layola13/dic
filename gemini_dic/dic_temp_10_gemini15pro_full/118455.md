mesembryanthemum:/ˌmɛzɛmbriˈænθɪməm/| n. | 松叶菊，日中花 |A genus of succulent plants, many of which are cultivated for their showy flowers that open in sunlight.  They are native to southern Africa. |  例句：The mesembryanthemum bloomed brightly in the afternoon sun.  午后的阳光下，松叶菊开得鲜艳夺目。

例句：My grandmother has a beautiful collection of mesembryanthemums in her garden. 我祖母的花园里有一系列美丽的松叶菊。 | 近义词：ice plant, fig-marigold (for some species) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

etymology: From Greek *mesēmbria* "midday" + *ánthemon* "flower."  Because some species' flowers open in the middle of the day.
词源: 来自希腊语 *mesēmbria* “中午” + *ánthemon* “花”。 因为某些品种的花在中午开放。
