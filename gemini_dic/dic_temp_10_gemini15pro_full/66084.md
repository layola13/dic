excavated:/ˈekskəveɪtɪd/| adj.|挖掘的，开凿的|Having been dug out of the ground; unearthed.|The archaeologists carefully examined the excavated artifacts. 考古学家仔细检查了挖掘出的文物。|近义词：unearthed, disinterred, exhumed|反义词:buried, interred|adjective


excavate:/ˈekskəveɪt/ | v. |挖掘，开凿|To dig out (earth, sand, etc.) especially to find something; to make (a hole or channel) by digging.|They excavated the site in search of ancient ruins. 他们在遗址挖掘，寻找古代遗迹。|近义词：dig, unearth, disinter, exhume|反义词:bury, interred|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *excavatus*, past participle of *excavare* "to hollow out," from *ex-* "out" + *cavare* "to hollow."
