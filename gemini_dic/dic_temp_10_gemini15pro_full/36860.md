colophony:/ˈkɑːləˌfɑːni/ /kəˈlɒfəni/| n.|松香，树脂|A translucent, yellowish-brown, brittle resin obtained from pine trees and other conifers, used in varnishes, inks, and adhesives.  It is also used by musicians on the bows of stringed instruments.|The violinist applied colophony to his bow.|近义词：rosin, resin|反义词: None|词性: noun
