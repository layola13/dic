ETYMOLOGY->
From the Old English *Grantabrycge* (Grant's Bridge), later shortened to *Cantebrigge* and eventually Cambridge.  The name refers to a bridge over the River Cam. 源自古英语 *Grantabrycge*（格兰特的桥），后来缩写为 *Cantebrigge*，最终成为剑桥。这个名字指的是横跨剑河的一座桥。
