ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hwæt (attested as hwæt), nominative and accusative neuter singular of hwā (“who”). Cognate with Old Frisian hwet, Old Saxon hwat, Old High German hwaz, Old Norse hvað, Gothic ƕa.
