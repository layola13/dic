deplore:/dɪˈplɔːr/ /dɪˈplɔr/
verb
公开谴责；强烈反对；对…深感遗憾
express strong disapproval of; regret deeply; lament
The violence was deplored by all right-thinking people. 所有正直的人都谴责这种暴力行为。
We deplore this act of terrorism. 我们强烈谴责这一恐怖主义行径。

synonym: lament, bemoan, bewail, condemn, denounce, deprecate
antonym: applaud, commend, praise, extol

Etymology:
From Old French deplorer, from Latin deplorare (“to weep for, lament, bewail”), from de- (“down, completely”) + plorare (“to weep”).
