limewater:/ˈlaɪmˌwɔːtər/| n.|石灰水|A saturated solution of calcium hydroxide in water, having an alkaline reaction and used in medicine, chiefly as an antacid.|Limewater turns milky when carbon dioxide is bubbled through it. 石灰水在通入二氧化碳时会变浑浊。|近义词:calcium hydroxide solution|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑酸性溶液acidic solution)|noun
