outsoars:/ˌaʊtˈsɔːrz/
verb
超越，胜过
to surpass or excel in flight or soaring; to go beyond or exceed in achievement or development
The new aircraft outsoars all previous models in terms of fuel efficiency.  这架新飞机在燃油效率方面超过了所有以前的型号。
outsoars all expectations 超越所有预期
近义词:surpasses, exceeds, transcends, outperforms
反义词:fails, underperforms, falls short of
词性:v.


outsoars:/ˌaʊtˈsɔːrz/
词源: out- + soar
"out-"  prefix meaning "beyond," "outside," or "exceeding."
"soar" meaning "to fly high in the air" or "to rise rapidly."
