metensomatosis:/ˌmɛtənˈsoʊməˈtoʊsɪs/ | n. |交代作用，变质交代作用|The process of rock alteration by hydrothermal and other fluids, involving the removal of some substances and the introduction of others, resulting in a change in the rock's chemical and mineralogical composition without melting.  |Example 1: The formation of skarns is a classic example of metensomatosis.  矽卡岩的形成是交代作用的典型例子。

Example 2: Metensomatosis played a crucial role in the formation of this ore deposit. 交代作用在这种矿床的形成过程中起了至关重要的作用。|Synonym: metasomatism|Antonym: N/A (This is a geological process; it doesn't have a direct opposite.)|noun
