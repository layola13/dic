obviate:/ˈɒbviˌeɪt/ (US) /ˈɒbvieɪt/ (UK)| vt. |避免，排除，取消|To remove a need or difficulty; to prevent something from happening by making it unnecessary.|Installing a new ventilation system obviated the need for air conditioning.  安装新的通风系统避免了安装空调的需要。

The new medicine obviates the need for surgery.  这种新药避免了手术的必要性。

|近义词：prevent, preclude, avert, avoid, eliminate|反义词：necessitate, require, cause|词性：动词
