noncolonials:/ˌnɒn.kəˈləʊ.ni.əlz/
noun
非殖民地居民；非殖民地的人们
People who are not from or living in a colony.
The conference brought together representatives from various noncolonial nations.  这次会议汇集了来自各个非殖民地国家的代表。
Noncolonial perspectives are crucial for understanding global history.  非殖民主义的视角对于理解全球历史至关重要。
近义词:independents, natives
反义词:colonists, settlers
词性:n.
