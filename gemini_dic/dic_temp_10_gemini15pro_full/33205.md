chronal:/ˈkrəʊnəl/ (US), /ˈkrɒnəl/ (UK)| adj.|时间上的，与时间有关的|Relating to time; temporal.|例句：Chronal anomalies are a common trope in science fiction. 时间异常是科幻小说中的常见比喻。|例句：The chronal displacement caused him to arrive a day later. 时间位移导致他晚了一天才到达。|近义词：temporal, time-related|反义词: atemporal, timeless|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Greek "chronos" (time) + "-al" (suffix forming adjectives).
