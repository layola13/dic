overgoverns:/ˌoʊvərˈɡʌvərnz/
|v.|过度管理，管得太多|To govern excessively or too strictly; to regulate or control to an excessive degree.|The city council overgoverns the local businesses, stifling innovation and growth. 市议会对当地企业管得太多，扼杀了创新和增长。|The strict school dress code overgoverns the students' self-expression. 学校严格的着装规定过度限制了学生的自我表达。|overregulate, micromanage|undergovern, deregulate|verb
