misworshipping:/ˌmɪswɜːrʃɪpɪŋ/
verb
错误地崇拜；崇拜不当之物
The act of worshipping something incorrectly or something that should not be worshipped.
They were accused of misworshipping pagan gods. 他们被指责错误地崇拜异教神。
Near synonyms:  idolizing falsely, venerating wrongly, revering incorrectly
Antonyms: worshipping correctly, revering appropriately, venerating properly
词性: verb
