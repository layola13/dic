ETYMOLOGY->
From deliberativeness + -es (plural suffix).  Deliberativeness is derived from deliberate (adjective), from Latin *deliberatus, past participle of deliberare "to weigh in mind, consider, deliberate," from de- "thoroughly" + librare "to weigh," from libra "balance, scales."
