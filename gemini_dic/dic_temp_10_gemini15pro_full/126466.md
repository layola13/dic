mythmaking:/ˈmɪθˌmeɪkɪŋ/
n.|神话创作，神话构建|The process of creating or propagating myths.  This can involve inventing stories, reinterpreting existing ones, or shaping narratives to serve particular cultural or political purposes.|The politician's speech was full of mythmaking about the country's history.|myth creation, fabrication, storytelling, legend-making|myth-breaking, debunking|noun
