innholder:/ˈɪnˌhoʊldər/| n.|旅馆老板，客栈老板|A person who owns or manages an inn.|The friendly innkeeper welcomed us with a warm smile.  友好的旅馆老板热情地微笑着欢迎我们。|Synonyms: innkeeper, publican, hotelier, landlord, landlady|Antonyms: guest, customer, patron|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *inne holder*, equivalent to *inn* + *holder*.  Compare Dutch *herbergier*, German *Herbergsvater*.
