holometabola:/ˌhɒləʊmɪˈtæbələ/ | n. | 全变态昆虫 | Insects that undergo complete metamorphosis, i.e., egg, larva, pupa, and imago (adult).| Butterflies are holometabola. 蝴蝶是全变态昆虫。| The beetle is a holometabola, going through a complete metamorphosis. 甲虫是全变态昆虫，经历完全变态。 |  Synonyms: Holometabola (often used as a synonym itself), Endopterygota |  Antonyms:  Hemimetabola (incomplete metamorphosis) | noun
