indomitable:/ɪnˈdɒmɪtəbl/ /ɪnˈdɑːmɪtəbl/| adj.|不屈不挠的，不屈服的，坚韧不拔的|Not easily discouraged, subdued, or defeated; unconquerable.|例句：She showed indomitable courage in the face of adversity.  她在逆境中表现出不屈不挠的勇气。|近义词：unyielding, resolute, tenacious, persistent, unwavering|反义词：yielding, submissive, weak, cowardly|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *indomptable*, from *in-* (not) + *dompter* (to tame), from Latin *domitare* (to tame), frequentative of *domare* (to tame, subdue).
