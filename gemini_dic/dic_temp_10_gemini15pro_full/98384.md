insolation:/ˌɪnsəˈleɪʃn/ | n. | 日晒，日照；曝晒 |  the amount of radiation from the sun that reaches the earth | The architect considered the insolation of the site when designing the house.  建筑师在设计房屋时考虑了场地的日照情况。| synonyms: sunlight, sunshine, solar radiation | antonyms: shade, darkness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *insolatio(n-), from insolare "to expose to the sun," from in- "in, on" + solare "to expose to the sun," from sol "sun."
