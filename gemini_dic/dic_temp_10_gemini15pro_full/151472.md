parquet:/pɑːrˈkeɪ/ /pɑːrˈkeɪ/| n.|镶木地板；拼花地板|A type of flooring made of wood blocks arranged in a geometric pattern.  It's often used in upscale homes and businesses.|The ballroom had gleaming parquet floors.  舞厅的地板闪闪发光。|近义词：wood flooring, hardwood flooring|反义词:carpet, tile|词性:noun

parquet:/pɑːrˈkeɪ/ /pɑːrˈkeɪ/| adj.|镶木地板的|Made of or relating to parquet flooring.|The parquet floor added a touch of elegance to the room. 镶木地板为房间增添了一丝优雅。|近义词：wooden, hardwood|反义词:carpeted, tiled|词性:adjective
