holloway:/ˈhɒləweɪ/ (UK) /ˈhɑːloʊweɪ/ (US)| n. |小路，山谷中的小路；(尤指)两边有堤岸或高地的凹路|A sunken road or track, typically one bordered by banks or hedges. |We walked along the holloway, shaded from the sun. 我们沿着两旁有堤岸的小路走，避开了阳光。|近义词：lane, path, track, byway|反义词:ridge, peak|词性:名词

Etymology: From Old English *holhweg "hollow way," from holh "hollow" + weg "way."  The word reflects the way these paths are often worn down below the surrounding land.
