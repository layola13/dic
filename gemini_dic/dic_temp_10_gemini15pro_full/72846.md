folk-rock:/ˈfəʊk rɒk/ (UK) /ˈfoʊk rɑːk/ (US)| n.|民谣摇滚|A genre of popular music that combines elements of folk music and rock music.|Bob Dylan's move to electric guitar is often seen as a pivotal moment in the development of folk-rock. 鲍勃·迪伦改用电吉他的举动通常被视为民谣摇滚发展中的关键时刻。

The Byrds were pioneers of the folk-rock sound in the mid-1960s.  伯兹乐队是20世纪60年代中期民谣摇滚的先驱。|近义词：roots rock, Americana|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider genres like heavy metal or electronic music)|词性:noun
