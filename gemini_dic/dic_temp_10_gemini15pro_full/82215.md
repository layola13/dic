grotesque: /ɡroʊˈtɛsk/ | n. | 怪诞风格；怪诞图案；风格怪诞的艺术品 | A style of ornamentation characterized by fanciful or grotesque figures and designs. | The museum houses a collection of medieval grotesques.  这家博物馆收藏了一批中世纪的怪诞风格艺术品。 |  He carved a grotesque out of wood. 他用木头雕刻了一个怪诞的形象。 | synonyms: caricature, gargoyle | antonyms: beauty, masterpiece | noun
