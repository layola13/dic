dismay:/dɪsˈmeɪ/ |vt.|使沮丧，使惊慌|The news of the defeat dismayed the entire nation. 战败的消息使整个国家感到沮丧。|近义词：dishearten, appall, daunt|反义词：encourage, hearten, reassure|词性:动词
