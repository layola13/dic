hicksite:/ˈhɪksaɪt/| n.|希克斯派教徒；希克斯派教友|A member of the Hicksite branch of the Society of Friends (Quakers).|Elias Hicks was a prominent Hicksite leader. 伊莱亚斯·希克斯是希克斯派一位杰出的领袖。|近义词：Quaker (broadly), Liberal Quaker|反义词：Orthodox Quaker|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Elias Hicks (1748–1830), an American Quaker minister whose followers separated from the Orthodox Quakers in the 1820s.
