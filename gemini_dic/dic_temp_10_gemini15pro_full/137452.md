nonthinking:/nɒnˈθɪŋkɪŋ/
adjective
无思想的；不思考的；不动脑筋的
Lacking the capacity for thought or consideration; unintelligent.
The nonthinking masses were easily swayed by propaganda. 无思想的群众很容易被宣传所左右。
Synonyms: unthinking, mindless, thoughtless, inconsiderate, irrational
Antonyms: thoughtful, considerate, intelligent, rational
词性: 形容词
