constipate:/ˈkɒnstɪpeɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnstɪpeɪt/ (US)| vt. |便秘；使迟滞；使停滞|to cause someone to be unable to empty their bowels easily|例句：Eating too little fiber can constipate you. 吃太少纤维会导致便秘。

例句：The traffic constipated the main street. 交通堵塞了主要街道。|近义词：obstruct, clog, block|反义词: loosen, relax, free|词性:动词

constipated:/ˈkɒnstɪpeɪɪtɪd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnstɪpeɪtɪd/ (US)| adj. |便秘的|unable to empty your bowels easily|例句：I felt bloated and constipated. 我感觉腹胀和便秘。

例句：He had been constipated for several days. 他已经便秘好几天了。|近义词：blocked, bound|反义词: loose, regular|词性:形容词

constipation:/ˌkɒnstɪˈpeɪʃn/ (UK) /ˌkɑːnstɪˈpeɪʃn/ (US)| n. |便秘|the condition of being unable to empty your bowels easily|例句：Chronic constipation can be a serious problem. 慢性便秘可能是一个严重的问题。

例句：She suffered from constipation after the surgery. 手术后她便秘了。|近义词：costiveness|反义词: diarrhea|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "constipatus," past participle of "constipare" ("to press closely together, crowd, condense"), from "com-" ("together") + "stipare" ("to pack, press, stuff").
