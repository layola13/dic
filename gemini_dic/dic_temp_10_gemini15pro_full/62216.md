The correct spelling is **increase**.

increase: /ɪnˈkriːs/ (British) /ɪnˈkriːs/ (American)| n. v. |增加，增长；增加量| to become or make something greater in size, amount, or degree | The population has increased dramatically. 人口急剧增加。|  The company increased its profits by 10% last year.  公司去年利润增长了10%。 |近义词：rise, grow, expand, augment, boost |反义词: decrease, decline, reduce, diminish |词性: 名词, 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *encreiss-, present participle stem of encreistre "to grow, increase," from Latin increscere "to grow in, grow upon, increase, swell," from in- "in, on" + crescere "to grow, arise."

PHRASE->
increase in = ...方面的增加  (e.g., an increase in sales 销售额的增长)
increase by = 增加了... (e.g., increase by 10% 增加了10%)
increase to = 增加到... (e.g., increase to 100 units 增加到100个单位)
increase with = 随着...而增加 (e.g., The risk increases with age. 风险随着年龄的增长而增加。)
on the increase = 正在增加 (e.g., Crime is on the increase in the city.  该市的犯罪率正在上升。)
