misinformant:/ˌmɪsɪnˈfɔːrmənt/ | n. | 误报者；提供错误信息的人 | A person who gives incorrect information, especially deliberately. | The journalist was misled by a misinformant. 记者被一个误报者误导了。| Synonyms: liar, deceiver, prevaricator | Antonyms: informant, truth-teller | noun

misinformant:/ˌmɪsɪnˈfɔːrmənt/ | n. |  misinformation 提供者 | Someone who provides misinformation, often unintentionally. |  He acted as a misinformant, though he didn't realize the information he was sharing was incorrect. 他充当了一个误报者的角色，尽管他没有意识到他分享的信息是不正确的。| Synonyms: fabricator, perjurer | Antonyms: witness, source | noun
