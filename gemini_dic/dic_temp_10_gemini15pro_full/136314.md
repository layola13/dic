nonreputably:/ˌnɒnˌrɛpjətəbli/ | adv. | 不名誉地，声名狼藉地 |  In a way that damages one's reputation; disreputably.  Describes actions or behavior performed in a manner that brings disgrace or ill repute. | He acted nonreputably by cheating on the exam. 他考试作弊，行为不光彩。|  The company gained money nonreputably through exploiting its workers. 这家公司通过剥削工人不光彩地赚钱。 | Synonyms: disreputably, dishonorably, shamefully, ignominiously | Antonyms: reputably, honorably, respectably | 词性: 副词
