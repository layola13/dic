duplicating:/ˈduːplɪkeɪtɪŋ/ /ˈdʊplɪkeɪtɪŋ/
verb
复制，拷贝
making an exact copy of something.
The secretary is duplicating the report. 秘书正在复制报告。
synonyms: copying, reproducing, replicating
antonyms: deleting, erasing
verb

duplicating:/ˈduːplɪkeɪtɪŋ/ /ˈdʊplɪkeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
复制的，副本的
being an exact copy of something.
The duplicating machine is broken. 复印机坏了。
synonyms: copied, reproduced, replicated
antonyms: original, unique
adjective
