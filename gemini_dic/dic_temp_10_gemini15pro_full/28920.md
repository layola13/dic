carried-forward:/ˈkærid ˈfɔːrwərd/ | adj.| 结转的，延后的 | An amount or figure that is carried forward is transferred from one column or page to the next. | The losses can be carried forward to offset profits in future years. 这些亏损可以结转以抵消未来几年的利润。| Synonyms: transferred, brought forward | Antonyms: written off | adjective

carried-forward:/ˈkærid ˈfɔːrwərd/ | n.| 结转，延后 | The act of transferring an amount or figure from one column or page to the next. | The carried-forward balance from last year was substantial.  去年结转的余额相当可观。| Synonyms: transfer, carryover | Antonyms: deduction, write-off | noun
