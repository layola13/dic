careering:/kəˈrɪərɪŋ/
verb
猛冲，飞驰
moving swiftly and in an uncontrolled way.
The car careened down the hill. 汽车沿着山坡飞驰而下。
近义词:hurtling, rushing, speeding, dashing, tearing, zooming, whizzing, flashing, bolting, charging, plummeting
反义词:crawling, creeping
词性:verb

careering:/kəˈrɪərɪŋ/
adjective
飞驰的
moving swiftly and in an uncontrolled way.
The careering car narrowly missed the pedestrians. 飞驰的汽车险些撞到行人。
近义词:speeding, runaway, uncontrolled, reckless, wild
反义词:slow, controlled
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "career," meaning "to go at full speed," ultimately from the Late Latin "carraria," meaning "carriage road."  The sense of moving rapidly and uncontrollably developed from the original meaning of simply moving along a road.
