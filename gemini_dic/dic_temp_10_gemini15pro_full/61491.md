embroidered:/ɪmˈbrɔɪdərd/ /ɛmˈbrɔɪdərd/ | adj. | 刺绣的；绣花的 | Decorated with needlework; embellished with ornamental needlework. |

例句：
She wore an embroidered dress. 她穿着一件绣花连衣裙。

The tablecloth was embroidered with flowers. 桌布上绣着花。


近义词：decorated, ornamented, embellished, stitched
反义词：plain, unembellished, undecorated
词性：形容词


词源：
From Middle English *embrouden, from Old French embroidier, embroder ("to embroider"), from en- ("in, on") + broider ("to embroider"), from broder ("a needle, goad, prickle"), from Frankish *brod ("point"), from Proto-Germanic *brōdiją ("point"). Cognate with Old High German brōt ("spike, point"), Old English brord ("point, spearhead"), Old Norse broddr ("spike, nail, point").
