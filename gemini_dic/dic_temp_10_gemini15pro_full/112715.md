词源:
From Old French *loial, from Latin lēgālis “legal,” from lex (genitive lēgis) “law.”  The sense shifted from “in accordance with the law” to “faithful to one's oaths.”
来源于古法语 *loial，来自拉丁语 lēgālis “合法的”，来自 lex（所有格 lēgis）“法律”。其含义从“符合法律”转变为“忠于誓言”。
