allonge:/əˈlɒndʒ/ | n. | 附加纸; 延长纸; | A slip of paper attached to a bill of exchange or promissory note, on which to write endorsements when there is not sufficient space on the bill itself. |  The bill of exchange required an allonge for the additional signatures.  汇票需要一张附加纸来签署额外的签名。| synonyms:  rider, attachment, extension, annex | antonyms: N/A | noun

allonge:/əˈlɒndʒ/ | n. | (尤指旧时女子戴的)假发, 接发 |  A hairpiece worn to give the appearance of longer hair, especially one worn by women in former times. |  The actress wore an allonge to create the illusion of long, flowing locks.  这位女演员戴了接发，以营造出长发飘飘的错觉。| synonyms: hairpiece, extension, wig, fall | antonyms: N/A | noun
