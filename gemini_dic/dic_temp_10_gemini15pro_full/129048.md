nitriferous:/naɪˈtrɪfərəs/
adjective
含氮的; 硝石的
Producing or containing nitrogen or nitrates.
Nitriferous bacteria play a crucial role in the nitrogen cycle.  含氮细菌在氮循环中起着至关重要的作用。
The nitriferous soil is ideal for growing certain crops.  这种含氮的土壤非常适合种植某些作物。
近义词:nitrogenous, nitrogen-bearing
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "nitrogen-deficient")
