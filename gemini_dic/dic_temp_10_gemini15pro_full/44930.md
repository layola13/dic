cryptogamist: /krɪpˈtɒɡəmɪst/ (US) /krɪpˈtɒɡəmɪst/ (UK)| n.| 隐花植物学家|A botanist specializing in the study of cryptogams (plants that reproduce by spores, such as ferns, mosses, algae, and fungi).|例句：The cryptogamist spent years cataloging the diverse mosses of the cloud forest.  隐花植物学家花了数年时间对云雾森林中种类繁多的苔藓进行编目。

|例句：A renowned cryptogamist discovered a new species of fern in the remote mountains. 一位著名的隐花植物学家在偏远的山区发现了一种新的蕨类植物。

|近义词：botanist, plant scientist|反义词: N/A|词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From cryptogam + -ist.
cryptogam: from Greek kryptos "hidden" + gamos "marriage" (referring to the hidden reproductive organs).
-ist: a suffix forming agent nouns, denoting a person skilled in or occupied with something.
