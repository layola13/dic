intercuts:/ˈɪntərkʌts/ | v. | 插入镜头；穿插；交切 | To insert (a shot, scene, etc.) between other shots or scenes in a film or television program. | The director intercuts scenes of the present with flashbacks to the character's childhood. 导演将现在的场景与角色童年的闪回镜头穿插在一起。 | The film intercuts between the two main characters' stories.  这部电影在两个主要角色的故事之间来回切换。 |  intersperses, inserts, interjects |  --- | verb

PHRASE->
intercut with:  穿插... | The scene is intercut with shots of the city.  场景中穿插着城市的镜头。
intercut between: 在...之间穿插 | The narrative intercuts between the past and the present. 叙事在过去和现在之间穿插。
intercut a scene with another: 将一个场景与另一个场景穿插 | The director decided to intercut the chase scene with a romantic flashback. 导演决定将追逐场景与浪漫的闪回穿插在一起。
intercut footage:  穿插镜头 | They intercut footage from different cameras to create a dynamic effect. 他们穿插来自不同摄像机的镜头以创造动态效果。
intercut shots: 穿插镜头 | The editor skillfully intercut shots of the two characters to build tension. 剪辑师巧妙地穿插了两个角色的镜头来营造紧张气氛。
