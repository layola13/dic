expandability:/ɪkˌspændəˈbɪləti/
n.|可扩展性，扩展性|The capacity of something to be expanded or increased in size, scope, or capacity. |This computer system boasts excellent expandability, allowing you to easily upgrade its components.  这套计算机系统拥有出色的可扩展性，可轻松升级其组件。|近义词：scalability, flexibility, extensibility|反义词：inelasticity, inflexibility, rigidity|词性:noun
Etymology: From "expandable" + "-ity". "Expandable" comes from "expand" (from Latin "expandere," meaning "to spread out") + "-able."
