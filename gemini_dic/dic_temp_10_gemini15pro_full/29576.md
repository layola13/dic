catchwork:/ˈkætʃwɜːrk/| n.|拼凑物，杂烩，粗制滥造的作品|Something made of a variety of different parts or pieces, often in a haphazard way. It can refer to a physical object or a piece of writing or music.|The quilt was a beautiful catchwork of different fabrics. 这床被子是由不同布料拼凑成的美丽作品。|synonyms: patchwork, hodgepodge, medley, jumble,  mishmash|antonyms:  unified, homogenous, consistent, seamless|noun
catchwork:/ˈkætʃwɜːrk/| adj.|拼凑的，粗制滥造的|Made up of various parts or elements in a haphazard way.|The report was a catchwork of hastily assembled data. 这份报告是由仓促收集的数据拼凑而成的。|synonyms:  patchwork,  hodgepodge,  piecemeal|antonyms: unified, homogenous, consistent, seamless|adjective
