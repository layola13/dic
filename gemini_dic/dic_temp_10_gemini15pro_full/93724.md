immanently:/ˈɪmənəntli/
adverb
内在地，固有地；即将发生地
Existing or operating within; inherent. Happening very soon.
The danger was immanently present. 危险迫在眉睫。
The system is immanently unstable. 这个系统本身就不稳定。
inherently, intrinsically, imminently
extrinsically, remotely
adverb
