outbeamed: /ˌaʊtˈbiːmd/
verb
发出更强的光，胜过
to surpass in beaming; to emit a stronger beam of light than.
The new lighthouse outbeamed the old one. 新灯塔的光比旧灯塔强。
outshine, eclipse, overshadow
dim, darken
