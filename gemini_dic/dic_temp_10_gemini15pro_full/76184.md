fussily:/ˈfʌsɪli/| adv.|挑剔地，过分讲究地|In a way that shows too much attention to small details and is often anxious and complaining.|She arranged the flowers fussily, constantly readjusting their positions. 她过分讲究地摆放着花，不停地调整它们的位置。|例句：He fussily straightened his tie.  他神经质地整了整领带。

例句：She fussily rearranged the cushions on the sofa. 她不停地摆弄沙发上的靠垫。|近义词：meticulously, fastidiously, painstakingly|反义词：carelessly, casually|词性：副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From fussy + -ly.  "Fussy" originates from the now obsolete "fust" meaning stale or musty, implying a person overly concerned with trivial matters as if detecting subtle flaws like a stale smell.
