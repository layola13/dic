owner-occupied:/ˈoʊnər ˈɑːkjəpaɪd/ | adj. | 自住的，业主自住的 |  Used to describe a property (usually a house or apartment) that is lived in by the person who owns it. |  例句：The bank offers lower interest rates on owner-occupied properties.  银行对自住房产提供较低的利率。

例句：The majority of houses in this neighborhood are owner-occupied.  这个社区的大部分房屋都是业主自住的。| 近义词：owner-inhabited, resident-owned | 反义词：rented, leased, tenant-occupied | 词性: adjective
