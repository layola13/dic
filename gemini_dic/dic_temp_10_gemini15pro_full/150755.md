paracymene:/ˈpærəˈsaɪmiːn/ | n. | 对伞花烃;对异丙基甲苯 |A naturally occurring aromatic hydrocarbon, p-cymene is a constituent of a number of essential oils, most notably cumin and thyme oils. It is a clear, colorless liquid with a pleasant odor. |Paracymene is used as a solvent and in the production of fragrances and resins.|例句：Paracymene is a common component of essential oils. 对伞花烃是精油的常见成分。
