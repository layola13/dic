nonrevealing:/ˌnɒnriˈviːlɪŋ/ | adj. | 不泄露的；不暴露的；不明显的 | Not revealing private information or secrets; not exposing something to view. |  She wore a nonrevealing dress to the business meeting. 她穿着一件不暴露的连衣裙去参加商务会议。 | The magician's tricks were nonrevealing, leaving the audience wondering how he did them. 魔术师的戏法不暴露任何秘密，让观众想知道他是怎么做到的。 | 近义词: discreet, modest, concealing, unexposing | 反义词: revealing, exposing, transparent | 词性: adjective


词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"  from Latin
revealing (present participle of reveal): from Old French *reveler, from Latin revelare "to unveil, reveal," from re- "back, again" (see re-) + velare "to veil, cover" (see veil (n.)).
