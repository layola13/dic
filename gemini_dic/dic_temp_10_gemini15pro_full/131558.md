noncommunal:/ˌnɒnkəˈmjuːnl/
adjective
非公共的，非社区的
Not shared by or involving all members of a community; private; not communal.
This area offers noncommunal laundry facilities for each apartment.  这个区域为每个公寓提供独立的洗衣设施。
The artist preferred a noncommunal studio space to foster individual creativity. 这位艺术家更喜欢一个非公共的工作室空间来培养个人创造力。
近义词:private, individual, separate, independent
反义词:communal, shared, collective, public
词性:adjective
