miso:/ˈmiːsoʊ/ (US), /ˈmiːsəʊ/ (UK)| n.|味噌|A fermented paste made primarily from soybeans, used in Japanese cooking for flavoring soups, sauces, and marinades.|例句：I added a spoonful of miso to the broth for extra flavor. 我在汤里加了一勺味噌来提味。

例句：Miso soup is a staple of Japanese cuisine. 味噌汤是日本料理的主食。

近义词：soybean paste, fermented soybean paste|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
