papboat:/ˈpæpˌboʊt/| n.|喂食小船，糊状食物喂食器|A small, shallow boat-shaped dish with a spout, used for feeding soft or liquid food to infants and invalids.|The nurse used a papboat to feed the weak patient.  护士用喂食小船给虚弱的病人喂食。|Synonyms: feeder, spoon|Antonyms: None|Noun
