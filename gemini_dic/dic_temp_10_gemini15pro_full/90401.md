howling:/ˈhaʊlɪŋ/| adj. n.| 嚎叫的，嗥叫；嚎叫声，怒吼|A long, loud, mournful cry or wail, like that of a dog or wolf.  The sound carries a sense of pain, distress, or wildness. It can also refer to something (like the wind) making a similar sound. |The howling wind kept me awake all night.  狂风呼啸让我彻夜未眠。|近义词：wailing, shrieking, roaring, yelling|反义词: whispering, murmuring|词性:adjective/noun
howling:/ˈhaʊlɪŋ/| adv.|(informal) extremely; very|Used to emphasize the degree of something, typically something negative.  Similar to "extremely" or "very."|It was a howling success. 这非常成功。|近义词：extremely, very, exceptionally|反义词: slightly, moderately|词性:adverb
