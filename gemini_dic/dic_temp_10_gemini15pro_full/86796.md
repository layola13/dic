hemaphaein:/ˌhɪməˈfeɪɪn/
| n. |嗜血;吸血鬼|A creature that consumes blood; a vampire. |The legends speak of a hemaphaein that stalked the village at night. 传说有一个嗜血的生物在夜间潜伏在村庄里。|近义词：vampire, bloodsucker|反义词: none (no direct opposite exists) |词性: noun
Etymology: From Greek "haima" (blood) + "phagein" (to eat).
