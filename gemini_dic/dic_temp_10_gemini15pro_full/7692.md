anolis: /əˈnoʊ.lɪs/ (US) /əˈnɒl.ɪs/ (UK)| n.|变色蜥，安诺蜥蜴|A lizard of the genus *Anolis*, found in warmer parts of the Americas.  These lizards are known for their ability to change color.|例句：The anolis darted across the branch, its skin flashing green. 变色蜥蜴飞快地穿过树枝，它的皮肤闪烁着绿光。

例句：Several species of anolis are popular pets. 几种变色蜥蜴是受欢迎的宠物。|近义词：lizard, chameleon|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From New Latin *Anolis*,  from French *anoli*. Ultimately of unknown origin. Some sources suggest a possible Carib origin.
