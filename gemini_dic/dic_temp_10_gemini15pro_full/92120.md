hypersceptical:/ˌhaɪpərˈskeptɪkəl/
adjective
极度怀疑的；超级怀疑的
Excessively or extremely skeptical; unwilling to believe claims or explanations.
He was hypersceptical about the government's promises. 他对政府的承诺极度怀疑。
The scientist took a hypersceptical approach to the new theory.  这位科学家对新理论采取了极度怀疑的态度。

近义词: incredulous, distrustful, doubting, cynical
反义词: credulous, trusting, gullible
词性: adjective

词源:
From hyper- + skeptical.  "Hyper-" is a prefix meaning "over, above, beyond," derived from Greek. "Skeptical" comes from Greek "skeptikos," meaning "inquiring, reflective," ultimately from "skeptesthai" "to look at, consider."
