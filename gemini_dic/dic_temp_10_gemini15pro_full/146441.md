overfrailties: /ˈoʊvərˈfreɪltiz/ | n. | 过度的脆弱，过分的虚弱 |  Excessive or extreme frailties; the state of being excessively or unusually weak, especially due to old age or illness. This word emphasizes the degree of frailty, suggesting it is beyond the typical or expected level. |  His overfrailties made it difficult for him to live independently.  他过分的虚弱使他难以独立生活。 |  infirmities, weaknesses, vulnerabilities | robustness, strength, resilience | noun
