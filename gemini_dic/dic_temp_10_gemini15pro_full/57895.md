driftier: /ˈdrɪftiər/ | adj. | 更漂泊的，更随波逐流的 | More inclined to drift; more subject to drifting. | The driftier clouds moved slowly across the sky.  更漂泊的云朵在天空中缓缓移动。| The driftier population tended to move from town to town. 更漂泊不定的人群倾向于从一个城镇迁移到另一个城镇。 | Synonyms: rootless, nomadic, wandering | Antonyms: settled, rooted, anchored | adjective

drift: /drɪft/ | n. v. | 漂流，漂移；趋势，倾向 |  From Old Norse *dript (unattested) meaning "a driving," related to drífa ("to drive"). | The boat drifted aimlessly on the sea. 小船漫无目的地在海上漂流。 | He drifted from job to job. 他不停地换工作。 | Synonyms: float, wander, roam, trend | Antonyms: anchor, settle, fix | noun, verb
