outclasses:/ˌaʊtˈklæsɪz/
verb
超越，胜过
to be much better than someone or something
例句：The new computer outclasses all the others in speed and versatility.  这台新电脑在速度和多功能性方面胜过所有其他电脑。

例句：She easily outclassed her opponents in the debate.  她在辩论中轻松击败了对手。

近义词：surpass, excel, outstrip, outdo, outperform
反义词：be inferior to, be outdone by
词性：v.
