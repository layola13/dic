ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *Judas*, from Greek *Ἰούδας* (*Ioudas*), from Hebrew *יהודה* (*Yehudah*), meaning "Judah".  The name was common in ancient Judea, but became synonymous with treachery because of the biblical figure.
