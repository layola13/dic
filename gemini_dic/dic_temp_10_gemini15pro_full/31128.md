chancellorships: /ˈtʃɑːnsələʃɪps/
n.
大学总理或校长职位；总理或校长的任期
The position or term of office of a chancellor, especially of a university.
She held two chancellorships concurrently. 她同时担任两个大学的校长。
Synonyms:  leadership, administration, directorship, headship
Antonyms:  subordination, followership
