coholder:/ˈkoʊˌhoʊldər/| n. |共同持有者，共同继承人|A person who shares ownership or possession of something with another person, especially property or a title.|例句：The coholders of the patent decided to license the technology. 专利共同持有人决定授权该技术。

例句：She and her brother are coholders of their parents' estate. 她和她的兄弟是父母遗产的共同继承人。|近义词：joint owner, co-owner, sharer|反义词:sole owner, sole proprietor|noun
