hypertropia:/ˌhaɪpərˈtroʊpiə/ | n. | 上斜视 | A type of strabismus (eye misalignment) in which one eye is higher than the other when looking straight ahead. |  The child's hypertropia was corrected with surgery.  这个孩子的上斜视通过手术得到了矫正。 | Synonym: vertical strabismus | Antonym: hypotropia | noun
