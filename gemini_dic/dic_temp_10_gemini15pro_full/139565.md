obtusity:/əbˈtjuːsɪti/
n.|迟钝，愚钝；不尖，钝|The obtusity of the student made it difficult for him to grasp the complex concepts. 这个学生的迟钝使他难以理解复杂的.概念。|
|例句：His obtusity prevented him from understanding the subtle humor in the play. 他的迟钝使他无法理解戏剧中微妙的幽默。

His political obtusity made him unaware of the public's growing discontent. 他的政治迟钝使他没有意识到公众日益增长的不满。
|近义词：dullness, stupidity, insensitivity, obtuseness|反义词：acuteness, sharpness, intelligence, sensitivity|词性:名词
