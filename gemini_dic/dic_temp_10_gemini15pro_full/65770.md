everydaynesses:/ˈɛvriˌdeɪnɪsnɪsɪz/
n.
日常琐事；平凡生活
the quality or state of being everyday or ordinary; the collection of everyday occurrences
She found beauty in the everydaynesses of life. 她从生活的日常琐碎中发现了美。
synonyms: ordinariness, commonness, routine, banality, humdrum
antonyms: extraordinariness, uncommonness, novelty, excitement
