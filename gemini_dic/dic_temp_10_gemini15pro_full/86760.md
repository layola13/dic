helpless:/ˈhɛlpləs/| adj.|无助的，无能为力的|Unable to help oneself; powerless or incompetent.|The injured bird lay helpless on the ground. 受伤的小鸟无助地躺在地上。|近义词：powerless, impotent, weak, defenseless|反义词：capable, competent, powerful, strong|词性:形容词
helplessness:/ˈhɛlpləsnəs/| n.|无助，无力|The state of being helpless.|She felt a sense of helplessness when her car broke down in the middle of nowhere. 她的车在荒郊野外抛锚时，她感到一阵无助。|近义词：powerlessness, impotence, weakness|反义词：capability, competence, power, strength|词性:名词
