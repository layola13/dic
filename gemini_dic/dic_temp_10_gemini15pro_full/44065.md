crinkle:/ˈkrɪŋkl/| n. v.|皱纹，褶皱；发出沙沙声；使起皱，使沙沙作响|A small wrinkle or crease. To become or cause something to become covered in wrinkles or creases or to make a rustling sound.|The crinkle of the paper bag reminded her of childhood picnics. 纸袋的沙沙声让她想起了童年时的野餐。|近义词：wrinkle, crease, rumple, rustle|反义词:smooth, flatten|词性:名词,动词

crinkle:/ˈkrɪŋkl/| v.|使起皱，使沙沙作响|To become or cause something to become covered in wrinkles or creases or to make a rustling sound.|The leaves crinkled under our feet. 叶子在我们脚下沙沙作响。|近义词：rustle, crackle|反义词:smooth, flatten|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *crinklen, frequentative of crinken (“to bend, twist”), from Old English crincan (“to bend, yield, cringe, shrink”), from Proto-Germanic *kringaną (“to turn, bend, twist”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)ker- (“to turn, bend”).  Cognate with Dutch krinkelen (“to curl, crinkle”), German krinkeln (“to crinkle”), Swedish krinka (“to shrink, crumple”).
