hedged:/hɛdʒd/
verb|限制，保护，避免，用树篱围|The company hedged its bets by investing in a variety of stocks.  这家公司通过投资各种股票来规避风险。|近义词：protected, guarded, shielded, limited, avoided|反义词：exposed, risked, unprotected|词性:v.
hedged:/hɛdʒd/
adjective|被树篱围住的，有所保留的，不直接的|Her response to the question was hedged and noncommittal. 她对这个问题的回答含糊其辞，不置可否。|近义词：guarded, cautious, noncommittal, evasive, qualified|反义词：direct, forthright, unequivocal, unqualified|词性:adj.
