defusers:/dɪˈfjuːzərz/| n.| 扩散器; 缓解器; 消散器|People or things that cause something to become less intense or widespread.  Something that spreads something else more thinly or widely. |The defusers helped to calm the tense situation. 缓解人员帮助缓和了紧张的局势。|The essential oil defuser filled the room with a calming scent.  精油扩散器使房间里充满了令人平静的香味。|synonyms: mitigators, dispersers, dissipaters|antonyms: concentrators, intensifiers|noun
