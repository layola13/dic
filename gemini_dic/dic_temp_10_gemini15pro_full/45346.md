cumulonimbus:/ˌkjuːmjʊləˈnɪmbəs/ | n. |积雨云|A type of dense, towering vertical cloud, forming from water vapor carried by powerful upward air currents. These clouds are associated with thunderstorms and atmospheric instability.| We could see the cumulonimbus building in the distance. 我们能看到远处积雨云正在形成。 | Near synonyms: thunderhead, storm cloud | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider "fair-weather cloud" for contrast) | Noun
