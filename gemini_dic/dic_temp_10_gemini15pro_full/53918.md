discouraging:/dɪˈskʌrɪdʒɪŋ/
adjective
令人沮丧的，使人泄气的
Having the effect of reducing someone's confidence or enthusiasm; depressing.
The news from the front lines was discouraging.  前线传来的消息令人沮丧。
The team's performance was discouraging.  球队的表现令人泄气。

synonyms: disheartening, dispiriting, demoralizing, depressing, dampening
antonyms: encouraging, heartening, inspiring, uplifting

ETYMOLOGY->
From discourage + -ing.  Discourage comes from the Old French descourager, from des- (expressing negation) + corage (courage).
