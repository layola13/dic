cinquecento:/ˌtʃɪŋkwəˈtʃɛntoʊ/ | n. | 十六世纪, 意大利文艺复兴时期(尤指艺术和文学)  |  Italian word referring to the 16th century, especially in relation to Italian art and literature of the High Renaissance. It literally means "five hundred" (1500s). |  The art of the *cinquecento* is characterized by its grandeur and harmony.  十六世纪的艺术以其宏伟与和谐为特征。

The *cinquecento* witnessed the flowering of Renaissance art. 十六世纪见证了文艺复兴艺术的繁荣。 | 近义词: High Renaissance, 16th century | 反义词:  Quattrocento (15th century) | 词性: 名词
