maidan:/maɪˈdɑːn/ | n. |广场，露天市场，(尤指)城市中心的广场|A large open space in a town, used for public meetings or as a market, especially in Eastern Europe or Asia. |例句1：The protesters gathered in the maidan to voice their dissent. 示威者聚集在广场上表达他们的异议。

例句2：The maidan was bustling with activity, filled with vendors selling their wares. 广场上熙熙攘攘，到处都是兜售商品的小贩。|近义词：square, plaza, public space|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑) confined space, enclosed area |词性: 名词
