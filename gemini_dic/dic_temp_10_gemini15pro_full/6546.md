amylogenesis:/ˌæmɪloʊˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/| n. |淀粉生成,淀粉形成|The process by which starch is formed, especially in plants.|例句：Amylogenesis is crucial for energy storage in plants.  植物的能量储存依赖于淀粉生成。

例句：Researchers are studying amylogenesis to improve crop yields.  研究人员正在研究淀粉生成以提高作物产量。|近义词：starch biosynthesis, starch formation|反义词:amylolysis|noun
