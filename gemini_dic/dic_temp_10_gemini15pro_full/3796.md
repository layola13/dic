agnate:/ˈæɡneɪt/| adj. n.|父系的，同族男子，|Related on the father's side. A male relative related through the male line. |例句：He was recognized as an agnate of the royal family. 他被认为是王室的父系亲属。

例句：Agnatic succession prioritized male heirs. 父系继承优先考虑男性继承人。|近义词：kinsman, relative, paternal|反义词：cognate|形容词，名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *agnatus*, past participle of *agnasci* ("to be born to, to be born in addition to"), from *ad* ("to") + *gnasci* ("to be born"), from the same root as *natus* ("born").
