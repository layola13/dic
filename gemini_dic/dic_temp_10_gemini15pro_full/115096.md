manganesian:/ˌmæŋɡəˈniːʒən/| adj.|锰的，含锰的|Containing or relating to manganese.  Relating to or containing manganese, especially in its higher oxidation states.|Manganese dioxide is a manganesian mineral. 二氧化锰是一种含锰的矿物。|manganese-containing, manganous (for Mn2+ compounds)|ferric (for Fe3+ compounds)|adjective
