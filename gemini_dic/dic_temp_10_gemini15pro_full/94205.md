imperfectibility:/ˌɪmpərˌfektɪˈbɪləti/
n.
不可完善性，不完美
The imperfectibility of human nature is a recurring theme in literature.  人类本性的不可完善性是文学中反复出现的主题。
The philosopher discussed the imperfectibility of any utopian system. 哲学家讨论了任何乌托邦制度的不完美性。
近义词:flawedness, imperfection, fallibility
反义词:perfectibility, flawlessness
词性:名词


Etymology:
From Late Latin *imperfectibilitas, from imperfectibilis (“incapable of being perfected”), from in- (“not”) + perfectibilis (“capable of being perfected”), from perfectus, past participle of perficere (“to complete, perfect”), from per- (“thoroughly”) + facere (“to do, make”).
