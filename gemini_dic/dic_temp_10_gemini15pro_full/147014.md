overlard:/ˌoʊvərˈlɑːrd/
verb
涂抹过多的油脂; 过分修饰; 使过分华丽
to cover or decorate something with too much of something rich or elaborate; to embellish excessively.
The writer overlarded his prose with flowery language.  作者用华丽的辞藻过度修饰了他的散文。
overwhelm, exaggerate, embellish
understate, simplify
v.
