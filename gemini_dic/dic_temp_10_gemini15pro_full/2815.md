advenient:/ˈædˌviːniənt/| adj.|来临的，到来的，外来的|Coming to; arriving, especially from outside; additional.|例句：The advenient technology revolutionized the industry. 这项新兴技术彻底改变了这个行业。

The family prepared a feast for their advenient guests. 这家人为即将到来的客人准备了盛宴。|近义词：arriving, incoming, approaching, imminent, supervening|反义词:departing, outgoing, receding|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adveniens*, present participle of *advenīre* (“to arrive”), from *ad-* (“to”) + *venīre* (“to come”).
USAGE->
Attributive Usage:

The advenient storm caused widespread damage.  即将到来的暴风雨造成了广泛的破坏。
They eagerly awaited the advenient spring. 他们热切地盼望着春天的到来。


Predicative Usage (less common):

Change is advenient. 变革即将到来。
With the new year, hope is advenient. 随着新年的到来，希望也随之而来。



PHRASE->
the advenient of = ...的到来
advenient technology = 新兴技术
advenient change = 即将到来的变化
advenient opportunities = 新出现的机会
advenient challenges = 新出现的挑战
