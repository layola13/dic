over-tinted:/ˌoʊvərˈtɪntɪd/
adjective
过度着色的，染色过深的
having too much color added, especially of a photograph or film.
The photograph was over-tinted, making the subject look unnatural.  这张照片颜色过深，使拍摄对象看起来不自然。
The windows were over-tinted, making it difficult to see out. 窗户颜色太深，难以看清外面。
overcolored, oversaturated
undertinted, subtly tinted
adjective
