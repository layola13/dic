curler:/ˈkɜːrlər/ (US) /ˈkɜːlə(r)/ (UK)| n.|卷发器；卷发的人；冰壶运动员|A device used for curling hair, someone who curls hair, or a person who plays curling. |She used a heated curler to create bouncy curls. 她用加热的卷发器做出了弹性的卷发。|Synonym: curling iron, curling tongs (for the device), stylist (for the person)|Antonym: straightener|noun

curler:/ˈkɜːrlər/ (US) /ˈkɜːlə(r)/ (UK)| n.|冰壶运动员|A person who plays the sport of curling. |The curler skillfully delivered the stone.  这位冰壶运动员巧妙地投出了冰壶石。|Synonym: curling player|Antonym: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "curl" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Curl" comes from Middle English *curle*,  from Old North French *curler* or Anglo-Norman *cruler*, ultimately from Old French *croeillier*, meaning "to bend, twist".
