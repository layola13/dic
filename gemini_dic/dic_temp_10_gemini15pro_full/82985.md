gunnels:/ˈɡʌnəlz/ | n. | 船舷；龙骨；最底部；(复数) | The sides of a ship at the level of or just above the deck. Also refers to the bilge, or the lowest internal part of a ship's hull.  | The sailor scrubbed the gunnels until they were spotless. 水手擦洗了船舷，直到它们一尘不染。 |  The bilge water sloshed around in the gunnels. 舱底的水在船底晃来晃去。 | synonyms: bulwarks, sides, hull, bilge | antonyms: deck, topside | noun


gunwale:/ˈɡʌnwəl/ | n. | 船舷；舷缘 | The upper edge of the side of a boat or ship. |  He leaned against the gunwale and watched the dolphins swim. 他靠在船舷上，看着海豚游来游去。 | The waves crashed over the gunwale, soaking the passengers. 海浪冲过船舷，淋湿了乘客。 | synonyms: gunnel, bulwark, rail | antonyms: keel | noun
