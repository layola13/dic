bandicoot:/ˈbændɪkuːt/| n. |袋狸|A small, omnivorous marsupial native to Australia and New Guinea, characterized by its pointed head, strong claws, and striped back.|例句：The bandicoot scurried across the forest floor. 袋狸飞快地穿过森林地面。

|例句：We saw a bandicoot foraging for food in our garden. 我们看到一只袋狸在我们的花园里觅食。|近义词：bilby, quoll, marsupial|反义词: None (no direct opposite for a specific animal)|词性:noun
