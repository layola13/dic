anachoretical:/ˌænəkəˈrɛtɪkəl/
adjective
隐居的；遁世的；苦行的
characteristic of an anchorite or hermit; solitary and withdrawn from the world.
例句：He lived an anachoretical existence in the mountains. 他在山中过着隐居的生活。

例句：Her anachoretical lifestyle was a stark contrast to her previous life in the city. 她隐居的生活方式与她以前在城市里的生活形成鲜明对比。

近义词：hermitic, ascetic, reclusive, secluded, solitary
反义词：gregarious, sociable, outgoing

词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *anachorēticus, from Ancient Greek ἀναχωρητικός (anakhōrētikós, “fit for withdrawing”), from ἀναχωρέω (anakhōréō, “I withdraw”).
