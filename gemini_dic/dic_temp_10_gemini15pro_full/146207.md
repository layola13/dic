overexpectation:/ˌoʊvərˌɛkspɛkˈteɪʃən/| n. |过度期望，期望过高|The state of expecting more than is likely or reasonable.|His overexpectation led to disappointment when the project didn't meet his unrealistic goals. 他期望过高，导致项目未能达到他不切实际的目标时感到失望。|近义词：excessive expectation, unrealistic expectation|反义词:underexpectation, low expectation|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + expectation.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much". "Expectation" comes from the Latin "expectare" meaning "to look out for,"  "await," or "anticipate."
