nonrival:/nɑːnˈraɪvəl/
adjective
非竞争性的；非对抗性的
Not subject to rivalry; that is, one person's consumption of the good does not diminish the amount available for others to consume.  A characteristic of some goods where the consumption of the good by one person does not reduce the amount available for consumption by another person.
例句：Public parks are considered nonrival goods because one person's enjoyment doesn't diminish another's.  公园被认为是非竞争性商品，因为一个人的享受不会减少另一个人的享受。

例句：Information is a classic example of a nonrival good; sharing it doesn't reduce its value.  信息是非竞争性商品的典型例子；分享它并不会降低它的价值。

近义词：non-competitive, non-exclusive
反义词：rival, competitive, exclusive
词性: adjective
