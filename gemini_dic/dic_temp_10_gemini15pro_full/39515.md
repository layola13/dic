nominees:/ˌnɒmɪˈniːz/
n.
被提名者；候选人
People who have been nominated for an award, position, or honor.
The nominees for Best Picture were announced. 最佳影片的提名名单已经公布。
近义词：candidates, contenders
反义词：non-nominees (not a standard word), losers (in the context of a competition)
词性：Noun (plural)
