outrebounded:/ˌaʊtrɪˈbaʊndɪd/
|v.|超过…的篮板球数|To have gathered more rebounds (in basketball) than an opponent.|The Lakers outrebounded the Celtics, securing their victory. 湖人队的篮板球超过了凯尔特人队，确保了他们的胜利。|例句：The Warriors outrebounded their opponents by a significant margin. 勇士队的篮板球数量远远超过了他们的对手。|近义词：outplayed, dominated|反义词: out-rebounded (passive voice)|词性:动词
