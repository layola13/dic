incondensible:/ˌɪnkənˈdɛnsəbl/ | adj. | 不可冷凝的，不能液化的 | Not able to be condensed (changed from a gas or vapor to a liquid).  This usually refers to gases that cannot be liquefied by ordinary methods, often requiring extremely low temperatures or high pressures. | The gas emitted from the industrial process was incondensible under normal atmospheric conditions. 工业过程中排放的气体在正常大气条件下不可冷凝。

The incondensible gases were vented from the system. 不可冷凝的气体从系统中排出。 | 近义词：noncondensable, non-liquefiable | 反义词：condensable, liquefiable | 词性：形容词

词源: in- (not) + condensable (capable of being condensed)  Ultimately from Latin "condensare" (to make dense).
