condole:/kənˈdoʊl/ /kənˈdəʊl/| v. |哀悼，慰问|to express sympathy to someone who is grieving|例句：She condoled with him on the death of his mother. 她对他母亲的去世表示慰问。

例句：Friends condoled with the bereaved family. 朋友们慰问了这个丧失亲人的家庭。

近义词：sympathize, commiserate, console|反义词:rejoice, celebrate|词性:动词
