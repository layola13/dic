earner:/ˈɜːrnər (UK), ˈɜːrnər (US)| n.|挣钱的人，赚钱者|Someone who earns money, especially a large amount of money.|例句：He's a high earner in the financial sector. 他是金融行业的高收入者。

例句：She's the main earner in the family. 她是家里主要的经济支柱。|近义词：breadwinner, provider, wage-earner|反义词:dependent, freeloader|词性:noun

词源：From the verb "earn" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting a person or thing that performs a specified action).  "Earn" comes from Old English *earnian "to deserve, merit, earn," from Proto-Germanic *aznōjan "to harvest, earn," ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *es- "to be."
