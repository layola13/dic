chondrule:/ˈkɒndr(j)uːl/ (US) /ˈkɑːndr(j)uːl/| n. |球粒|A small, round, glassy body found in some meteorites, believed to have formed in the solar nebula.|The meteorite contained numerous chondrules, providing clues to the early solar system.  这颗陨石包含大量的球粒，为了解早期太阳系提供了线索。|Synonyms: spherule, granule|Antonyms: N/A|noun
