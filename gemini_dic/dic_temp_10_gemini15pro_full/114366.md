majorism: /ˈmeɪdʒərɪzəm/ | n. | 多数主义；多数统治 | The belief that the majority of people in a country or group should have the power to make decisions. |  The country operates under the principle of majoritarianism. 这个国家实行多数主义原则。| synonyms: majority rule, popular sovereignty | antonyms: minority rule, elitism | noun

majorism: /ˈmeɪdʒərɪzəm/ | n. | （尤指美国南北战争后南方）白人至上主义 | (especially in the southern US after the Civil War) the belief that white people are superior to other races. |  Majorism was a powerful force in the post-Civil War South. 白人至上主义在内战后的南方很有影响力。 | synonyms: white supremacy, racism | antonyms: racial equality, integration | noun
