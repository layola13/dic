autotransplantation:/ˌɔːtoʊˌtrænsplænˈteɪʃən/ | n. |自体移植| The surgical transfer of tissue or an organ from one part of the body to another in the same individual. |  The patient underwent an autotransplantation of skin from his thigh to his arm. 病人接受了从大腿到手臂的皮肤自体移植。| synonyms: autograft | antonyms: allotransplantation, xenotransplantation | noun
