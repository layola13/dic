nonvictim:/ˌnɒnˈvɪktɪm/ | n. | 非受害者 |  A person who is not a victim, especially one who is seen as not having suffered harm or disadvantage in a particular situation. It can also refer to someone who takes responsibility for their actions and doesn't adopt a victim mentality. |  Example:  He argued that many so-called "disadvantaged" individuals are actually nonvictims who have the power to change their circumstances.  他认为许多所谓的“弱势”群体实际上是非受害者，他们有能力改变自己的处境。

Example:  She promotes a nonvictim mentality, encouraging people to take ownership of their lives. 她提倡一种非受害者的心态，鼓励人们掌控自己的生活。 | 近义词: survivor, thriver | 反义词: victim | 词性: noun
