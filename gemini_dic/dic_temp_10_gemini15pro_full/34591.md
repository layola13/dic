clearway:/ˈklɪəweɪ/ | n. | 清路;畅行道 | A stretch of road, especially near an airport, where parking is prohibited to ensure free flow of traffic. |  The police kept the clearway open for emergency vehicles.  警察让清路保持畅通，以便紧急车辆通行。| synonym: throughway, expressway | antonym: traffic jam, bottleneck | noun

clearway:/ˈklɪəweɪ/ | n. | (空中)航路 |  A designated air route free from obstructions. | The pilot requested a clearway for landing. 飞行员请求一条畅通的着陆航路。| synonym: airway, flight path | antonym: no-fly zone | noun
