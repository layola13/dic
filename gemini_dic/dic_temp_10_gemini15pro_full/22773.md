borstal:/ˈbɔːrstəl/| n.|青少年犯教养院|A type of youth detention centre that existed in Britain and some other countries.  These institutions aimed to rehabilitate young offenders through education and vocational training.|He was sent to borstal for three years for car theft. 他因为偷车被送进了青少年犯教养院三年。|Synonyms: reformatory, youth detention center, juvenile detention center|Antonyms:  None (lack of direct antonym.  Could consider "freedom" or "liberty" in a broad sense).|Noun


borstals:/ˈbɔːrstəlz/| n.|青少年犯教养院（复数）|Plural form of "borstal," referring to multiple youth detention centres.|Several borstals were closed down due to budget cuts.  由于预算削减，几家青少年犯教养院被关闭。|Synonyms: reformatories, youth detention centers, juvenile detention centers|Antonyms: None (same as singular form).|Noun
