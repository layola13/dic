lightings:/ˈlaɪtɪŋz/
n.
照明设备；灯光效果；舞台灯光
The system of lights in a building, street, etc. or the equipment that produces them, or the quality of the light produced.
The stage lightings for the play were dramatic and effective.  这出戏的舞台灯光效果引人注目，非常有效。
illumination, lights
darkness
