bather:/ˈbeɪðər/ (US), /ˈbeɪðə/ (UK)| n.|沐浴者；游泳者；洗海水浴的人|A person who bathes, especially one who swims or bathes in the sea.|The beach was crowded with bathers enjoying the sunshine. 海滩上挤满了享受阳光的游泳者。|Synonyms: swimmer, sunbather, paddler|Antonyms: non-swimmer|Noun
