dictatorship:/dɪkˈteɪtərʃɪp/ | n. |独裁，专政 |A form of government in which one person or a small group possesses absolute power without effective constitutional limitations. |例句：The country suffered under a brutal dictatorship for decades.  这个国家在残酷的独裁统治下遭受了数十年的苦难。

例句：The dictator used force to maintain his dictatorship.  独裁者使用武力来维持他的独裁统治。|近义词：tyranny, autocracy, despotism |反义词：democracy, republic |词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin dictātūra ("dictatorship, office of dictator"), from dictātor ("dictator") + -ūra (noun-forming suffix).
