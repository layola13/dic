internecine:/ˌɪntərˈnɛsaɪn/ | adj. |  内讧的；两败俱伤的；毁灭性的 | Marked by slaughter or bloodshed within a group; mutually destructive. |  The internecine warfare within the company led to its downfall. 公司内部的内讧导致了它的垮台。 | Example: The internecine struggles within the political party threatened to tear it apart.  政党内部的斗争有可能将其分裂。| Synonyms: internal, fratricidal, deadly, ruinous | Antonyms: peaceful, harmonious, constructive, unifying | Word Type: Adjective

Etymology: From Latin *internecinus* "mutually destructive," from *inter-* "between, among" + *necare* "to kill"
