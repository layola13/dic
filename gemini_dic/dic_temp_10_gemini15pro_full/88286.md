hintonborg:/ˈhɪntənbɔːrɡ/ | n. | 辛顿堡 | A portmanteau of Geoffrey Hinton and cyborg, referring to a hypothetical future where humans have integrated AI technology developed by Hinton into their brains, enhancing their cognitive abilities. |  The concept of the hintonborg emerged after Hinton left Google and voiced concerns about the potential misuse of AI. | 近义词: AI-human hybrid, augmented human | 反义词: natural human, unaugmented human | 词性: noun
