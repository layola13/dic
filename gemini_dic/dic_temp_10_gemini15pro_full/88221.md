hinderingly:/ˈhɪndərɪŋli/
adv.
阻碍地，妨碍地
in a way that hinders or obstructs.
His large backpack was hinderingly bulky as he tried to navigate the crowded bus.  他那巨大的背包碍手碍脚的，让他很难在拥挤的公交车上行走。
The construction work was hinderingly loud, making it impossible to concentrate.  施工的声音非常吵闹，令人无法集中注意力。
近义词: obstructively, impedinly, restrictively
反义词: helpfully, supportively
词性: 副词
