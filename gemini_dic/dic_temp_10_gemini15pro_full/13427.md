atone:/əˈtoʊn/| vi.|赎罪，弥补|To make amends or reparation for a wrong or injury; to expiate.|He atoned for his sins by giving to charity. 他通过慈善捐赠来赎罪。|近义词：expiate, redeem, make amends for|反义词:offend, sin|动词

atone:/əˈtoʊn/| vi.|和解，调和|To become reconciled; to make peace.|The two families finally atoned after years of feuding.  两个家族在多年的不和之后终于和解了。|近义词：reconcile, make peace|反义词:alienate, estrange|动词
