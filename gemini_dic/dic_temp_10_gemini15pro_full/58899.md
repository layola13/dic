durians:/ˈdʊəriənz/ /ˈdjʊəriənz/| n. |榴莲|A large, oval, spiky fruit containing a creamy, pungent-smelling pulp.|例句：Durians are known for their strong smell. 榴莲以其强烈的气味而闻名。

例句：Some people love durians, while others find the smell overpowering.  有些人喜欢榴莲，而另一些人觉得它的气味太浓烈了。|近义词: (No direct synonym for the fruit itself; could list other tropical fruits like jackfruit, mangosteen depending on context) |反义词:  (No direct antonym) |词性:noun
