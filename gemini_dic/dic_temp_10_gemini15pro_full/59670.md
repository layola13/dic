echinoidea:/ɪˌkaɪnɔɪˈdiːə/ | n. |海胆纲|A class of marine invertebrates, with a spherical or flattened shell (test) covered in movable spines, and tube feet for locomotion. They belong to the phylum Echinodermata.|例句：Echinoidea are commonly found on the seabed.  海胆纲动物常见于海床上。
