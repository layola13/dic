hemiopsia:/ˌhɛmiˈɒpsiə/ /ˌhiːmiˈɑːpsiə/
noun|偏盲|Blindness or defective vision in half of the visual field.|The patient experienced hemiopsia after the stroke.  病人中风后出现偏盲。|hemianopsia,hemianopia|normal vision|n.
Etymology: From Greek ἡμι- (hemi-) "half" + ὄψις (opsis) "sight, vision".
