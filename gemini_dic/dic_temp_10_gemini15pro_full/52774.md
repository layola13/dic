dilatory:/ˈdɪləˌtɔːri/ (US), /ˈdɪlətri/ (UK)| adj.|拖延的，迟缓的|Slow to act or proceed; intended to cause delay.|例句：The committee's dilatory tactics prevented a vote on the issue. 委员会的拖延战术阻止了对该问题的投票。

例句：His dilatory response to the crisis angered many people. 他对危机的拖延反应激怒了很多人。

近义词：slow, tardy, procrastinating, lagging, sluggish|反义词：prompt, swift, expeditious, efficient, punctual|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dilatorius*, from dilator "delayer, procrastinator," from dilatus, past participle of differre "to postpone, defer" (see differ).
