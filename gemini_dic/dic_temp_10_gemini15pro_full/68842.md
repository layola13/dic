fashion-conscious:/ˈfæʃən ˈkɑːnʃəs/ | adj. |时尚意识强的；注重时尚的 | Aware of and interested in fashion; following current trends. | She's very fashion-conscious and always wears the latest styles. 她非常注重时尚，总是穿着最新的款式。|  He's not particularly fashion-conscious and prefers comfortable clothes over stylish ones. 他并不特别注重时尚，更喜欢舒适的衣服而不是时尚的衣服。 | style-conscious, trendy, fashionable | unfashionable, indifferent to fashion | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
fashion (n.) from Old French "façon" meaning "make, shape, form, manner" + conscious (adj.) from Latin "conscius" meaning "knowing, aware, privy to"
