indies: /ˈɪndiz/
n.
独立电影; 独立音乐; 独立游戏; 独立开发者
Independent films, music, video games, or developers, especially when seen as creative and original.
Example:  She's into indie music and films. 她喜欢独立音乐和电影。

Example: The indie game scene is thriving right now.  独立游戏界现在很繁荣。
近义词: independent, alternative
反义词: mainstream
词性: 名词 (plural noun)


ETYMOLOGY->
Shortened form of "independents," ultimately from in- (not) + dependent (relying on).
