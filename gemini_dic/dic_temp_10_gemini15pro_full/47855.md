deceitfully:/dɪˈsiːtfəli/
adverb
欺骗地；虚伪地
In a way that is intended to deceive; dishonestly.
He deceitfully obtained the money from his grandmother. 他欺骗地从他祖母那里得到了钱。
She smiled deceitfully, hiding her true intentions. 她带着欺骗的微笑，掩盖了她的真实意图。
近义词:dishonestly, fraudulently, deceptively, craftily, cunningly
反义词:honestly, truthfully, frankly, openly
词性:adverb

deceit:/dɪˈsiːt/
noun
欺骗；欺诈；诡计
The action or practice of deceiving someone by concealing or misrepresenting the truth.
His deceit was eventually discovered. 他的欺骗最终被发现了。
She was a victim of deceit and manipulation. 她是欺骗和操纵的受害者。
近义词:fraud, deception, trickery, chicanery, guile
反义词:honesty, truthfulness, frankness
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *deceit*, borrowed from Old French *deceite*, *decepte*, from Latin *dēceptus*, past participle of *dēcipiō* (“I deceive, cheat, ensnare, beguile”), from *dē-* + *capiō* (“I take, seize, capture”).
