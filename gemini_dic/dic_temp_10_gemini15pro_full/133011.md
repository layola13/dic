nonentertainment:/ˌnɒnˌentəˈteɪnmənt/
noun
非娱乐
Something that is not designed for entertainment purposes; typically serious or functional in nature.
The documentary was nonentertainment, focusing on the harsh realities of war.  这部纪录片不是娱乐性的，重点关注战争的残酷现实。
Near Synonyms: non-amusement, non-diversion, non-recreation
Antonyms: entertainment, amusement, diversion
