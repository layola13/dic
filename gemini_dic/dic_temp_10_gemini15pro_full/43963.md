cricketed:/ˈkrɪkɪtɪd/| adj.|精疲力竭的，疲惫不堪的|Exhausted, worn out, especially after playing cricket.  It describes a state of extreme tiredness, often associated with physical exertion, similar to being "knackered" or "shattered." Although etymologically related to the sport of cricket, its use isn't strictly confined to it.|例句：After a long day of moving furniture, I felt completely cricketed.  搬了一天家具后，我感到筋疲力尽。

例句：The team was cricketed after their five-hour match.  五小时的比赛后，队员们都累垮了。|近义词：exhausted, knackered, shattered, worn out|反义词：energetic, refreshed, invigorated|词性:形容词
