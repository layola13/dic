anglicises:/ˈæŋɡlɪsaɪzɪz/
verb
使英语化; 英文化
To make or become English in form or character.
The Japanese company anglicises its name for the Western market.  这家日本公司将其名称英语化以适应西方市场。
He anglicised his name when he moved to England. 他移居英国后把名字改成了英文名字。
synonyms: anglify, adapt, modify
antonyms: translate, localize
词性: 动词
