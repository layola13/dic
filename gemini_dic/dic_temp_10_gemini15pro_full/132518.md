nondeterminations:/ˌnɒndɪˌtɜːmɪˈneɪʃənz/ | n. | 非确定性，不确定性 |  The lack of certainty or predictability; the state of being undetermined.  It often refers to situations with multiple possible outcomes or where the outcome is influenced by chance or unknown factors. |  The quantum world is full of nondeterminations. 量子世界充满了不确定性。 The nondeterminations of the market make investing risky.  市场的不确定性使得投资有风险。 | 近义词: uncertainties, indeterminacies, ambiguities | 反义词: certainties, determinations | 词性: 名词

词源:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + determination (noun from the verb "determine," ultimately from Latin "terminare" - to limit, bound)
