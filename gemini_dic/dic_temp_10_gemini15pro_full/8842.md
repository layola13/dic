antiexpressiveness:/ˌænˌtɪɪkˈspresivˌnɛs/ |n.| 反表达性，抑制表达，不善表达 |The quality or state of being unexpressive or inhibiting expression.  A tendency to avoid or suppress emotional expression. |His antiexpressiveness made it difficult to know what he was thinking. 他的不善表达让人很难知道他在想什么。|Near Synonyms: inexpressiveness, reserve, reticence, taciturnity |Antonyms: expressiveness, effusiveness, volubility |noun

Etymology:
"anti-" (prefix of Greek origin meaning "against, opposite of") + "expressive" (from Latin "expressus," past participle of "exprimere" - to press out, represent) + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality).
