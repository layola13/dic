biblical: /ˈbɪblɪkəl/ | adj. | 圣经的；像圣经故事的 | Relating to the Bible; resembling a story from the Bible | The story had biblical proportions. 这个故事具有圣经故事般的规模。 | Near Synonyms: scriptural, religious, sacred | Antonyms: secular, worldly | adjective

biblical: /ˈbɪblɪkəl/ | adj. |  巨大的; 惊人的 |  Figuratively used to describe something of immense scale or impact. | The flood caused a disaster of biblical proportions. 这场洪水造成了圣经般规模的灾难。 | Near Synonyms: colossal, immense, enormous | Antonyms: tiny, minuscule, insignificant | adjective
