mollitude:/ˈmɒlɪˌtjuːd/ /ˈmɑːlɪˌtuːd/| n. |温柔，温和，柔弱|a quality of softness, smoothness, or weakness|Her mollitude made her an easy target for bullies. 她的柔弱使她很容易成为恃强凌弱者的目标。|Synonyms: softness, gentleness, mildness, tenderness, weakness|Antonyms: hardness, harshness, severity, strength, firmness|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *mollitūdō,* from Latin *mollis* (“soft”) + *-itūdō* (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality).
