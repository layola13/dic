gangrel:/ˈɡæŋɡrəl/| adj. n.|流浪的，无家可归的；流浪汉，无家可归的人|A wandering, homeless person; a vagabond.  It also has connotations of being wild, rough, or disreputable.|He lived a gangrel life, drifting from town to town.|近义词：vagabond, vagrant, wanderer, nomad, rogue, tramp|反义词：settled, resident, domiciled|词性: adjective, noun

词源:
From Middle English *gangrel, gangrell, from gang (“to go, walk”) + -rel, a diminutive suffix.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *gangą (“to go”).
