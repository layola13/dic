overplay:/ˌoʊvərˈpleɪ/| v.|过分强调；夸张；表演过火|To emphasize or exaggerate something to an excessive degree, especially in acting or performance.|He overplayed his role and it came across as insincere. 他表演得太过火了，让人觉得不真诚。|近义词：exaggerate, overact, overemphasize|反义词：underplay, downplay|词性:动词

overplay:/ˈoʊvərpleɪ/| n.|过分强调；夸大|An instance of overemphasizing or exaggerating something.|The overplay of his emotions made the scene feel melodramatic.  他情绪的过度夸张使这场戏显得过于戏剧化。|近义词：exaggeration, overacting, overemphasis|反义词：understatement, downplaying|词性:名词
