litholatry: /ˌlɪθˈɒlətri/ (US) /ˌlɪθˈɒlətri/ (UK)| n.| 石头崇拜; 拜石|The worship of stones, especially as objects having inherent power or as symbolic representations of a deity.|  Ancient cultures practiced litholatry, believing certain stones held divine power. 古代文化实行石头崇拜，相信某些石头拥有神圣的力量。

Litholatry was a common practice in many prehistoric societies. 石头崇拜在许多史前社会中是一种常见的习俗。|近义词: idolatry, fetishism|反义词: iconoclasm|词性: noun
