allelomorph:/əˈliːləmɔːrf/ | n. | 等位基因 | One of two or more alternative forms of a gene that arise by mutation and are found at the same place on a chromosome. | Example 1: The allelomorphs for eye color in fruit flies can result in red or white eyes. 果蝇眼睛颜色的等位基因可以导致红色或白色的眼睛。

Example 2: Different allelomorphs can produce variations in traits like height or flower color. 不同的等位基因可以产生性状的变异，如身高或花的颜色。 | Synonym: allele | Antonym: N/A | Noun
