fraternises:/ˈfrætərnʌɪzɪz/
verb
交往；亲善；友好往来
associates or forms a friendly relationship with someone, especially when one is not supposed to
The soldiers were warned not to fraternise with the enemy. 士兵们被警告不要与敌人 fraternize。
近义词: associate, socialize, mingle
反义词: shun, avoid, ostracize
词性: verb


fraternise:/ˈfrætərnʌɪzɪz/
verb
兄弟般交往
To associate or mingle as brothers would.
例句：The two groups, despite their differences, fraternized easily.  这两个团体尽管存在分歧，却很容易亲如兄弟般交往。
近义词: socialize, mingle, consort
反义词: shun, avoid, ostracize
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French fraterniser, from fraternité (“brotherhood”), from Latin frater (“brother”).
