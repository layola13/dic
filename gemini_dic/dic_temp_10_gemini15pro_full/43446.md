crapulous:/ˈkræpjʊləs/ | adj. | 恶心反胃的；饮食过度的；宿醉的 | Suffering from excessive eating or drinking; feeling sick or ill from overindulgence in food or alcohol. |  He felt utterly crapulous after the all-you-can-eat buffet. 他在自助餐后感到非常恶心。| Synonyms: nauseated, queasy, sick, ill, indisposed, hungover | Antonyms: healthy, well, sober, temperate | adjective

Etymology->
From Latin *crāpulōsus, from crāpula (“intoxication, drunkenness”), from Greek κραιπάλη (kraipálē, “headache after drunkenness”).
