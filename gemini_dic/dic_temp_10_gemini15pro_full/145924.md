overdirect:/ˌoʊvərdaɪˈrekt/ /ˌəʊvədaɪˈrekt/
|v.|过度指导；过分指挥|To direct or manage with excessive control or attention to detail, often hindering effectiveness.|The manager overdirected his team, stifling their creativity and initiative.|micromanage, overmanage|underdirect
|词性: 动词|

overdirect:/ˌoʊvərdaɪˈrektɪd/ /ˌəʊvədaɪˈrektɪd/
|adj.|被过度指导的|Managed with excessive control, often to the point of inefficiency.|The overdirected employees felt stifled and unable to take initiative.|micromanaged, overmanaged|underdirected, empowered
|词性: 形容词|
