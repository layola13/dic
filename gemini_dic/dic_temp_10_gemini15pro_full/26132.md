Etymology:
From bust + -y + -est.  "Bust" originally referred to a sculpted representation of a person's head and shoulders, and later came to refer to the chest or breast area.  "-y" is a suffix used to form adjectives indicating possession or abundance of a quality. "-est" is the superlative suffix.
