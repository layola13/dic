gent:/dʒɛnt/
n.|绅士；先生 (informal, often humorous or slightly ironic)|A man of refinement, traditionally one who is well-bred, courteous, and fashionable.  Often used humorously or ironically today.|He's quite the gent, always opening doors for ladies.|近义词：gentleman, fellow, chap|反义词:boor, lout|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
Shortened form of "gentleman," from Old French "gentil homme."
