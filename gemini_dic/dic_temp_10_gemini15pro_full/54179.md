diser:/ˈdaɪzər/| n.|诋毁者，诽谤者|Someone who disparages or belittles someone or something.|He was a known diser of the government's policies. 他是出了名的诋毁政府政策的人。|近义词：detractor, critic, slanderer, backbiter|反义词：supporter, admirer, advocate|词性：名词
Etymology: From the verb "disparage" + "-er" (agent suffix).  The verb "disparage" comes from Old French "desparagier" (to marry unequally), from "des-" (expressing negation or reversal) + "parage" (rank, lineage).
