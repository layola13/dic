bot:/bɑːt/ (US) /bɒt/ (UK) | n. | 机器人；自动程序；网络机器人 | A computer program that performs automatic tasks, especially one designed to simulate human behavior. |  例句：The bot automatically responded to customer inquiries. 这个机器人自动回复客户的询问。

例句：I use a bot to schedule my social media posts. 我使用机器人来安排我的社交媒体帖子。| 近义词：robot, automaton, program, crawler, spider | 反义词: human, person | 词性: noun
