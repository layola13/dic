associatively:/əˈsoʊʃiətɪvli/ /əˈsəʊʃɪətɪvli/
| adv. | 结合地，关联地 | In mathematics, an operation is associative if the order in which the operations are performed does not change the result.  |  Addition is associative: (2 + 3) + 4 = 2 + (3 + 4). 加法是结合的：(2 + 3) + 4 = 2 + (3 + 4). | synonym: jointly, combinedly | antonym: independently, separately | adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From associate + -ive + -ly.  Ultimately from Latin *associatus*, past participle of *associare* "to join to, unite, attach," from *ad-* "to" + *socius* "companion."
