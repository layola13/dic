horseknop:/ˈhɔːrsˌnɒp/ (UK) /ˈhɔːrsˌnɑːp/ (US)| n.|马结; 平结;|A knot, typically used to tie a horse's halter to a post or rail, made by passing the end of the rope under and then over and through the loop.  A simple overhand knot. Also called a thumb knot.|例句：He secured the horse with a horseknop. 他用一个马结拴住了马。

例句：The sailor quickly tied a horseknop to secure the line. 水手快速地打了一个马结来固定绳子。|近义词：overhand knot, thumb knot|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
