outwrote:/ˌaʊtˈroʊt/
verb
比…写得多；写作胜过
surpass in writing; write more than
Shakespeare outwrote all his contemporaries.  莎士比亚的作品超过了他所有的同时代作家。
近义词:surpassed, outproduced
反义词:was outwritten by
词性:verb

PHRASE->

what if = 如果...会怎么样
What if it rains tomorrow?  如果明天下雨怎么办？

what for = 为了什么
What did you do that for? 你为什么那样做？

no matter what = 无论如何
No matter what happens, I'll be there for you.  无论发生什么事，我都会在你身边。

He's intelligent, and what's more, he's very kind. 他很聪明，而且很善良。

what about = ...怎么样
What about going to the cinema tonight? 今晚去看电影怎么样？
