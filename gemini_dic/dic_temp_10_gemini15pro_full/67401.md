externalised:/ɪkˈstɜːnəlaɪzd/ /ɛkˈstɜːrnəˌlaɪzd/| adj.|外化的，外部化的|Made external or manifested externally; put into an outward form; given outward expression.|Her internalised anger was eventually externalised in a series of angry outbursts. 她内心的愤怒最终在一系列的愤怒爆发中外化了出来。|Synonyms: expressed, manifested, outward, external, overt|Antonyms: internalized, suppressed, repressed|adjective

externalised:/ɪkˈstɜːnəlaɪzd/ /ɛkˈstɜːrnəˌlaɪzd/| v.|外化，外部化|(past tense) Made something external or manifested it externally; put something into an outward form; gave outward expression to something.|The company externalised its IT department to save costs.  该公司将IT部门外包以节省成本。|Synonyms: outsourced,  divested, expressed, manifested|Antonyms: internalized, absorbed, integrated|verb
