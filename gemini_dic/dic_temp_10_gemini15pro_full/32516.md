chintz:/tʃɪnts/| n.|印花棉布，色彩鲜艳的印花棉布|A glazed, brightly printed cotton fabric, typically with a floral design.|The curtains were made of a cheerful chintz.|Synonyms: calico, glazed cotton|Antonyms: plain cloth|Noun

chintzy:/ˈtʃɪntsi/| adj.|印花棉布制的，俗气的，廉价的|Resembling or made of chintz; cheap and gaudy.|The furniture was chintzy and unattractive.|Synonyms: gaudy, tacky, tawdry|Antonyms: elegant, refined, tasteful|Adjective
