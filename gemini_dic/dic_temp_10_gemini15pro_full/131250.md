noncarcinogen:/ˌnɒnkɑːˈsɪnədʒən/ | adj. |非致癌的|Not causing or tending to cause cancer.| This product is made with noncarcinogenic materials.  这个产品是用非致癌材料制成的。| synonyms: non-cancerous, innocuous | antonyms: carcinogenic, cancer-causing | adjective
