boded:/ˈboʊdɪd/| v.|预示，预兆|The dark clouds boded rain.  乌云预示着要下雨。|The economic indicators bode well for the future. 经济指标预示着未来会很好。|foretold, presaged|auspicious, fortunate (if meaning good)|v.
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *boden, from Old English bodian "to announce, proclaim, foretell," from Proto-Germanic *bodjanan, from *baud- "bid, command."  Related to bid.
