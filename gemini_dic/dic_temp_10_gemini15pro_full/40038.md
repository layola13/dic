consummate:/ˈkɒnsəmət/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsəmət/ (US)| vt.|完成；使完美|To make (a marriage or relationship) complete by having sexual intercourse. To bring to a state of perfection; fulfill.|例句：Their happiness was consummated with the birth of their first child.  第一个孩子的出生使他们的幸福圆满。|近义词：complete, finish, conclude, finalize, perfect|反义词：begin, start, initiate|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin consummatus, past participle of consummare "to sum up, complete, perfect," from com-, intensive prefix (see com-), + summa "sum, total" (see sum).
