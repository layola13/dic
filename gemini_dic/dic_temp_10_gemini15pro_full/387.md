abiological:/ˌeɪˌbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ /ˌeɪˌbaɪəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/
adjective
非生物的；无生命的
Relating to things that are not living.  Not caused by or related to biological processes.
The erosion of the rock formations was due to abiological processes like wind and rain.  岩石的侵蚀是由风和雨等非生物过程造成的。

The abiological synthesis of organic molecules is a key area of research in the origins of life. 有机分子的非生物合成是生命起源研究的一个关键领域。

近义词:non-biological, non-living, inorganic
反义词:biological, living, organic
词性:adjective
