coasted:/ˈkoʊstɪd/ (US) /ˈkəʊstɪd/ (UK)| v. |滑行，惯性前进，轻松前进|moved effortlessly or smoothly, often without power; proceeded without exertion|The car coasted down the hill. 汽车顺着山坡滑行下去。|The cyclist coasted along the flat road.  骑自行车的人沿着平坦的道路滑行。|近义词：glide, drift, freewheel, sail|反义词：struggle, labor, pedal|词性:动词

词源: From coast (n.), from Old French coste "rib, side, coast," from Latin costa "rib, side." The verb sense developed from the idea of sailing along the coast, then generalized to mean moving effortlessly.
