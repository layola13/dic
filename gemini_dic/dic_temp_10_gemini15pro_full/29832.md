caulocarpous:/ˌkɔːloʊˈkɑːrpəs/ | adj. |茎花植物的；树干生花的 | Having flowers and fruits developing from the main stem or trunk, as in the cacao tree. |  The cacao tree is a caulocarpous species, with its pods emerging directly from the trunk. 可可树是一种茎花植物，其果荚直接从树干上长出来。| Example 2:  Caulocarpous trees are a fascinating adaptation, allowing large fruits to develop without the need for strong branches. 茎花树是一种迷人的适应，允许大型水果在不需要强壮树枝的情况下发育。 | 近义词:trunk-flowering | 反义词: (no direct antonym, possibly acrocarpous - fruiting at the apex) | 词性: adjective
