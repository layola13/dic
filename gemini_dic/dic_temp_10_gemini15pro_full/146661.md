overhastinesses: /ˌōvərˈheɪstinəsiz/
| n. | 过于匆忙，草率 | The state of being excessively hasty; undue haste.  Characterized by acting too quickly and without sufficient thought or consideration. |  His overhastinesses in making decisions often led to mistakes. 他做决定过于仓促，经常导致错误。|  rashness, precipitateness, impetuosity |  carefulness, deliberation, prudence | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + hastiness + -es (plural suffix).  "Hastiness" comes from "hasty" which derives from Middle English *hastif, from Old Norse *hastr, meaning "violent," "vehement."
