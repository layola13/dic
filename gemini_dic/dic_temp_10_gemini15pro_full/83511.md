hackster:/ˈhæk.stər/ | n. | 黑客;  科技爱好者 |  Someone who enjoys exploring the capabilities of software and hardware, often in a creative or unconventional way; a technology enthusiast. |  The hackster spent all weekend building a robot from scratch.  这个科技爱好者花了整个周末从零开始建造一个机器人。 | Example: The local hackster group meets every month to share their latest projects. 当地的科技爱好者小组每个月都会聚会分享他们最新的项目。| Synonym: maker, tinkerer, hobbyist, enthusiast | Antonym:  Luddite (反对技术进步的人) | noun


hackster:/ˈhæk.stər/ | n. | 黑客 | Someone who gains unauthorized access to computer systems. (This meaning is less common in modern usage, particularly when referring to hardware enthusiasts.)  | The hackster broke into the company's server. 这个黑客入侵了公司的服务器。 | Example:  The hackster was able to bypass the security system.  这个黑客能够绕过安全系统。 | Synonym: hacker, cracker, intruder  | Antonym: security expert | noun
