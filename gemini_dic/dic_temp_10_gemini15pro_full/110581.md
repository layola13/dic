limited-government:/ˈlɪmɪtɪd ˈɡʌvərmənt/ | adj. | 有限政府 | A political system in which the government's power is restricted by law, usually a written constitution.  It emphasizes individual liberties and economic freedom. | The United States is an example of a limited government, where the Constitution outlines specific powers for the government and protects individual rights. | Synonyms: constitutional government, restricted government | Antonyms: unlimited government, authoritarian government | adjective

limited:/ˈlɪmɪtɪd/ | adj. | 有限的 | Restricted in size, amount, or extent; not great in quantity or range. |  The company had limited resources for the project. 公司的项目资源有限。| Synonyms: restricted, confined, finite | Antonyms: unlimited, infinite, vast | adjective

government:/ˈɡʌvərmənt/ | n. | 政府 | The system by which a state or community is governed. | The government announced new economic policies. 政府宣布了新的经济政策。| Synonyms: administration, regime, authority | Antonyms: anarchy, disorder, chaos | noun
