lcds:/ˌɛl siː ˈdiːz/ | n. |液晶显示器; 液晶显示屏 | Liquid Crystal Displays.  Flat-panel displays that use the light-modulating properties of liquid crystals. LCDs do not produce light directly. | The laptop has a bright LCD screen. 这台笔记本电脑有一个明亮的液晶显示屏。| synonym: display, screen | antonym: CRT (Cathode Ray Tube) | abbreviation
