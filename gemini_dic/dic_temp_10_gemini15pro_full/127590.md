nedder:/ˈnɛdər/| n.|毒蛇；蝰蛇；卑鄙的人|A venomous snake, especially an adder; a contemptible person.|例句：The nedder coiled and struck. 毒蛇盘绕起来，发动了攻击。

例句：He called him a nedder for betraying their trust. 他称他为毒蛇，因为他背叛了他们的信任。|近义词：adder, viper, snake, villain, scoundrel|反义词:hero|词性:名词
