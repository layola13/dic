paragoning:/ˈpærəɡɒnɪŋ/ | v. | 比作；与…相比；将…视为典范 | Comparing or likening something to something else considered to be an ideal or excellent example. | He was paragoning her to a goddess. 他把她比作女神。 | 近义词: comparing, likening, equating, matching | 反义词: contrasting, differentiating | verb

paragon:/ˈpærəɡɒn/ | n. | 典范；模范；完人 | A person or thing regarded as a perfect example of a particular quality. | She is a paragon of virtue. 她是一位品德高尚的典范。 | 近义词: model, exemplar, ideal, epitome | 反义词: imperfection, flaw | noun
