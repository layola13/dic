opposedness:/əˈpoʊzdnɪs/
n.
对立; 反对; 对抗
The state of being opposed or opposite.
His opposedness to the new policy was clear from his speech. 他在讲话中清楚地表明了他对新政策的反对。
Near Synonyms: opposition, antagonism, hostility, resistance, contrariety
Antonyms: agreement, harmony, concord, accord, consensus
