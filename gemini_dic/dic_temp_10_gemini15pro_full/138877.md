nurture: /ˈnɜːrtʃər/ (US) /ˈnɜːtʃə(r)/ (UK)| n. vt.| 养育，培育；营养物|The nurture of children is very important.  孩子的养育非常重要。|  To nurture a talent requires patience and dedication. 培养才能需要耐心和奉献。|近义词：cultivate, foster, cherish, nourish|反义词：neglect, abandon|词性:名词,动词

Etymology:  From Middle English *norture, from Old French norture,  from Late Latin nutritia (“nourishment”), from Latin nutrire (“to nourish”), from nutris (“nourishing”).
