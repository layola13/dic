lilied:/ˈlɪlid/| adj.|百合花的，百合花状的，像百合花一样洁白的|Adorned or covered with lilies; resembling a lily, especially in purity or whiteness.|Her lilied hands gracefully held the bouquet. 她那像百合花一样洁白的手优雅地拿着花束。|近义词：lily-white, pure, white|反义词:dark, stained|词性:形容词

词源: From "lily" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Old English "lilie," from Latin "lilium," and from Greek "leirion."
