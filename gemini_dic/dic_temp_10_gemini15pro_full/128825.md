nightmarishly:/naɪtˈmɛərɪʃli/| adv.|噩梦般地，可怕地|In a way that resembles a nightmare; terribly.  It describes something extremely unpleasant, frightening, or difficult.|The traffic was nightmarishly slow. 交通拥堵得可怕。|Synonyms: terribly, awfully, horribly, dreadfully, frightfully|Antonyms: pleasantly, delightfully, wonderfully|Adverb
