downturning:/ˌdaʊnˈtɜːnɪŋ/
adjective
下降的，衰退的
Characterized by a decline or downturn.
The downturning economy caused many businesses to close.  经济下滑导致许多企业倒闭。
declining, weakening, slumping
rising, improving, booming
