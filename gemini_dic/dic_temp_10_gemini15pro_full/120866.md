misbestow:/ˌmɪsbɪˈstoʊ/
verb
错误地授予；滥用；浪费
to give something, especially love, affection, or praise, to an undeserving person or thing.
He misbestowed his affections on a woman who did not deserve them. 他把他的爱错付给了一个不值得爱的女人。
The award was misbestowed on an unknown actress.  这个奖项被错误地颁给了一个不知名的女演员。
Synonyms: misapply, misuse, squander, waste
Antonyms: allocate correctly, use wisely, grant appropriately
词性: 动词
