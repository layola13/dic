floatability:/ˌfloʊtəˈbɪləti/| n.|浮力，漂浮性|The floatability of an object depends on its density and the density of the liquid it is in. 一个物体的浮力取决于它自身的密度和它所在液体的密度。|The life jacket's floatability ensured the swimmer's safety. 救生衣的浮力确保了游泳者的安全。|近义词：buoyancy|反义词: sinkability|词性:noun
