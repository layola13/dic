misjudgment:/ˌmɪsˈdʒʌdʒmənt/
n.
误判，判断错误
An act of judging wrongly or inaccurately.
His misjudgment of the situation led to disastrous consequences. 他对形势的误判导致了灾难性的后果。
miscalculation, error, mistake
sound judgment, accurate assessment
noun
