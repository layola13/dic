non-exportation:/ˌnɒnˌɛkspɔːˈteɪʃən/ |n.| 不出口；禁止出口 |The non-exportation agreement was a key part of the colonies' resistance.  殖民地抵制的重要组成部分是不出口协议。| The non-exportation of certain goods is crucial for national security.  某些商品的不出口对国家安全至关重要。|近义词：embargo, boycott|反义词：exportation, export|词性: 名词

Etymology: non- (prefix meaning "not") + exportation (from export, from Latin exportare "to carry out," from ex- "out of" + portare "to carry")
