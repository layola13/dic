autochthon:/ˈɔːtɒkθən/ (US) /ɔːˈtɑːkθən/| n. |土著，原住民|A person, plant, or animal that is native to a particular place.|The  research focuses on the languages of the autochthons of this region. 这项研究的重点是该地区土著居民的语言。|Synonyms: native, aboriginal, indigenous|Antonyms: immigrant, foreigner, alien|noun

autochthonous:/ɔːˈtɒkθənəs/ (US) /ɔːˈtɑːkθənəs/| adj. |土生的，原产的|Indigenous rather than descended from migrants or colonists.|The kangaroo is autochthonous to Australia. 袋鼠是澳大利亚的本土动物。|Synonyms: native, endemic, indigenous|Antonyms: foreign, alien, introduced, exotic|adjective
