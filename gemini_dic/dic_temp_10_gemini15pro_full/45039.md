cub:/kʌb/| n.|幼兽（尤指熊、狮、狼等猛兽的幼崽），新手，幼童|A young animal of certain carnivorous mammals such as the bear, lion, or wolf, or a young fox, whale, etc. Also, an inexperienced person, especially a young reporter or athlete.|The lioness protected her cubs fiercely. 母狮凶猛地保护着她的幼崽。|Synonyms: young, offspring, whelp, pup|Antonyms: adult, mature|Noun
