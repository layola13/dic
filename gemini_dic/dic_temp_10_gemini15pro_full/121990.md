misspeaking:/ˌmɪsˈspiːkɪŋ/
n.|失言，说错话|The incident was due to a misspeaking on my part. 这件事是因为我的失言造成的。
|Synonyms: slip of the tongue, verbal blunder, gaffe, error, mistake|
|Antonyms: accuracy, correctness, precision|
|noun|


misspeak:/ˌmɪsˈspiːk/
v.|说错话，失言|I misspoke earlier; I meant to say "Tuesday," not "Thursday." 我之前说错了；我的意思是“星期二”，而不是“星期四”。
|Synonyms:  blunder, err, stumble, slip up|
|Antonyms: articulate clearly, express correctly|
|verb|


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- + speak.  Mis- is a prefix meaning "wrong" or "badly." Speak comes from Old English *sprecan, of Germanic origin.
