neaped:/niːpt/| adj.|小潮的，受小潮影响的|Left aground by the neaped tide. |例句：The ship was neaped in the harbor. 船在港口遇上了小潮。

例句：The neaped tide prevented the boat from leaving the harbor. 小潮阻止了船离开港口。|近义词：stranded, grounded|反义词： afloat|词性:形容词
词源: From "neap" (noun, referring to a tide just after the first or third quarters of the moon, when there is the least difference between high and low water) + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives). Ultimately from Old English "nepflod".
