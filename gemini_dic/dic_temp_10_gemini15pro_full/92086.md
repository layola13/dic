hyperreal:/ˌhaɪpərˈriːəl/ | adj. | 超真实的，超现实的 | Exaggerated in comparison to reality, often to the point of being unbelievable or surreal.  Characterized by heightened or amplified qualities of reality. | The movie presented a hyperreal vision of the future, with flying cars and robot servants. 这部电影呈现了对未来的超现实愿景，有飞行汽车和机器人仆人。 | The airbrushed photos created a hyperreal image of the model.  这些经过喷枪修饰的照片创造了模特超真实的形象。 | 近义词: surreal, unreal, exaggerated, heightened | 反义词: real, realistic, natural | 词性: 形容词

词源: 
From hyper- + real.  "Hyper-" is a prefix meaning "over, above, beyond," derived from Greek *hupér*.
