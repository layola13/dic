migrant:/ˈmaɪɡrənt/| n. adj.|移民，迁徙的|A person who moves from one place to another, especially in order to find work or better living conditions.  A migrant worker/bird|例句：The migrant workers were exploited by their employers. 这些移民工人被他们的雇主剥削了。

例句：Migrant birds fly south for the winter.  候鸟冬天飞往南方。|近义词：immigrant, emigrant, traveler, nomad|反义词：native, resident, local|词性:名词，形容词

migrant:/ˈmaɪɡrənt/| adj.|迁徙的，流浪的|Moving from one place to another, especially in order to find work or better living conditions.|例句：The documentary followed the arduous journey of migrant farmworkers.  这部纪录片跟踪拍摄了移居农场工人的艰辛旅程。

例句：Migrant butterflies travel thousands of miles. 迁徙的蝴蝶飞行数千英里。|近义词：migratory, itinerant, wandering, nomadic|反义词：settled, stationary, resident|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *migraunt*, from Latin *migrāns*, present participle of *migrāre* (“to migrate”).
