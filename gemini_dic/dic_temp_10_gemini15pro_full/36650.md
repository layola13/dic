college:/ˈkɒlɪdʒ/ (British) /ˈkɑːlɪdʒ/ (American)| n. |学院，大学，高等教育机构|A place of higher education, typically offering undergraduate degrees and sometimes postgraduate degrees.  An organized body of people with shared functions and privileges, such as a college of physicians.|She's going to college next year. 她明年要去上大学。|The Royal College of Surgeons is a prestigious institution. 皇家外科医学院是一家享有盛誉的机构。|university, academy, institute|high school|noun
