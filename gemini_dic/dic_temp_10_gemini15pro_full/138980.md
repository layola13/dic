nylons:/ˈnaɪlɑːnz/
n.
尼龙; 尼龙织物; 尼龙长袜
A strong, elastic synthetic fiber.  (Usually used in the plural.)  Made from polyamides.
例句：She bought a pair of nylons. 她买了一双尼龙长袜。

例句：Nylons are often used in clothing. 尼龙常用于服装制造。

近义词：stockings, tights, hosiery
反义词：(No direct antonym for the material itself, perhaps "natural fibers" like cotton or wool)
词性: noun
