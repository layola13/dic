denizens:/ˈdɛnɪzənz/
noun
居民，栖息动物
inhabitants or occupants of a particular place.
The denizens of the forest were startled by the sudden noise. 森林里的居民被突如其来的噪音吓了一跳。
inhabitants, occupants, residents, dwellers
aliens, foreigners, strangers
