lend an ear:/lɛnd ən ɪr/
|idiomatic phrase|
|倾听；听取|
|To listen attentively and sympathetically to someone.|
|例句：Could you lend an ear? I need to talk to someone about this problem. 你能听我说说话吗？我需要和人谈谈这个问题。|
|例句：She always lends an ear to her friends when they are in trouble. 当她的朋友遇到麻烦时，她总是会倾听。|
|近义词：listen, pay attention, heed|
|反义词：ignore, disregard|
|phrase|
