nonmaturely:/ˌnɒn.məˈtʊə.li/ | adv. | 不成熟地 | In a way that lacks maturity; not fully developed. | He acted nonmaturely by throwing a tantrum. 他耍脾气，表现得很不成熟。|  The project was nonmaturely handled, leading to several setbacks.  这个项目处理得不够成熟，导致了一些挫折。| 近义词: immaturely, childishly, foolishly | 反义词: maturely, responsibly, wisely | 词性: 副词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "mature" (adjective meaning "fully developed") + "-ly" (adverb suffix).
