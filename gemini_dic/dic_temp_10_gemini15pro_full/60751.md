ETYMOLOGY->
From electro- + encephal- + -gram.  Electro- comes from Greek ēlektron, meaning "amber," referencing the static electricity produced by rubbing amber. Encephal- comes from Greek enkephalos, meaning "brain."  -gram comes from Greek gramma, meaning "something written."
