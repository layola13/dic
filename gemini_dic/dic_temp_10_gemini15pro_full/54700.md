disorganizer:/dɪsˈɔːrɡəˌnaɪzər/| n.|扰乱者，混乱制造者|A person or thing that causes disorganization.|例句：He's such a disorganizer; his office is always a mess. 他真是个制造混乱的人，他的办公室总是乱糟糟的。

例句：The sudden storm was a disorganizer of the picnic. 突如其来的暴风雨扰乱了野餐。|近义词：mess maker, disruptor|反义词：organizer, planner|词性:noun
