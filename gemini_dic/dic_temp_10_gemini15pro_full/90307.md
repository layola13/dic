houses:/ˈhaʊzɪz/ | n. |房屋，住宅|Plural form of "house," referring to more than one dwelling place.  A building for human habitation, especially one that consists of a ground floor and one or more upper storeys.|例句：The houses on this street are all very similar. 这条街上的房子都很相似。|例句：They are building new houses near the river. 他们在河边建造新房子。 |近义词：residences, dwellings, homes|反义词: - |noun

Etymology: From Old English "hūs," from Proto-Germanic "*husan," from Proto-Indo-European "*k̂ews-."  Cognates include German "Haus" and Dutch "huis."
