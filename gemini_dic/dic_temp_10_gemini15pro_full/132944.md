nonemergencies:/ˌnɑːnɪˈmɜːrdʒənsiz/ | n. | 非紧急情况 | Situations that do not require immediate or urgent attention; events that are not life-threatening or do not pose an immediate danger. |  The nurse triaged the patients, dealing with the emergencies first and the nonemergencies later. 护士对病人进行了分类，先处理紧急情况，然后再处理非紧急情况。| synonyms: non-urgent situations, ordinary matters, routine cases | antonyms: emergencies, crises, urgent situations | noun

nonemergency:/ˌnɑːnɪˈmɜːrdʒənsi/ | adj. | 非紧急的 | Not requiring immediate or urgent attention; not life-threatening or posing an immediate danger. |  The hospital has a separate entrance for nonemergency patients. 医院为非急诊病人设置了单独的入口。| synonyms: non-urgent, routine, ordinary | antonyms: emergency, urgent, critical | adjective
