dibbler:/ˈdɪblər/| n.|袋食蚁兽;  一种用于挖洞种植的小工具|A small, nocturnal marsupial native to Australia that feeds on insects and other invertebrates. Also, a pointed hand tool used for making holes in the ground for planting seeds or bulbs.|The dibbler is an endangered species. 袋食蚁兽是一种濒危物种。

The gardener used a dibbler to plant the tulip bulbs. 园丁用挖洞器种植郁金香球茎。|近义词：(animal)antechinus,  (tool) dibber, planter|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


dibble:/ˈdɪbəl/| v.|用小工具挖洞; 点播|To make small holes in the ground for planting seeds or bulbs, typically using a pointed tool.|She dibbled holes for the seeds. 她用小工具挖洞播种。

He dibbled the seedlings into the soil. 他把幼苗点播到土壤里。|近义词:plant, sow, drill|反义词:N/A|词性:verb
