bana:/ˈbɑːnə/ | n. | A type of plantain or cooking banana |  A starchy, low-sugar type of banana used in cooking, particularly in tropical regions.  It is typically eaten cooked, not raw. | Example: We made banana bread with ripe bananas, but used green banas for the curry. 我们用成熟的香蕉做了香蕉面包，但咖喱用的是绿色的芭蕉。| Example: Banas are a staple food in many parts of Africa. 芭蕉是许多非洲地区的主食。 | Synonyms: plantain, cooking banana | Antonyms: dessert banana, Cavendish banana | noun
