bodysuit: /ˈbɒdiˌsuːt/ (UK) /ˈbɑːdiˌsuːt/ (US)
n.
紧身衣裤
A one-piece, close-fitting garment that covers the torso and crotch, often extending down the legs like a leotard or shorts.
She wore a black bodysuit under her sheer dress. 她穿了一件黑色紧身衣在透明连衣裙里面。
The dancer's bodysuit allowed for a full range of motion.  舞者的紧身衣可以做各种动作。
近义词:leotard, unitard, catsuit
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider general clothing items like dress, pants, shirt)
