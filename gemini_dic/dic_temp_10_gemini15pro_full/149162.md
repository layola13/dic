oxpecker:/ˈɒkspɛkə(r)/ (UK) /ˈɑːkspɛkər/ (US)| n. |啄牛鸟|A bird native to Africa, known for its symbiotic relationship with large mammals, feeding on ticks, flies, and other parasites found on their skin.|The oxpecker diligently picked parasites off the rhinoceros's back. 啄牛鸟勤奋地从犀牛背上啄食寄生虫。|例句：
Oxpeckers are commonly seen on the backs of zebras and giraffes.
啄牛鸟常见于斑马和长颈鹿的背上。

The oxpecker's sharp beak is perfect for removing ticks.
啄牛鸟锋利的喙非常适合去除蜱虫。|近义词：tickbird|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "predator" in a loose sense)|词性:noun
