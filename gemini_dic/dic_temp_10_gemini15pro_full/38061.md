composite:/ˈkɒmpəzɪt/ (UK) /kəmˈpɑːzɪt/ (US)| adj. n.|合成的，复合的；复合材料|Made up of various parts or elements.|例句：The report is a composite of several different studies. 这份报告是由几项不同的研究合成的。|近义词：compound, combined, complex|反义词：simple, basic|词性: adjective, noun

composite:/ˈkɒmpəzɪt/ (UK) /kəmˈpɑːzɪt/ (US)| n.|复合材料|A material made from two or more constituent materials with significantly different physical or chemical properties that, when combined, produce a material with characteristics different from the individual components.|例句：Carbon fiber is a popular composite material used in aerospace engineering. 碳纤维是一种在航空航天工程中常用的复合材料。|近义词：compound, mixture|反义词：element|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin compositus "composed," past participle of componere "to put together," from com- "together" + ponere "to put, place."
