chromophane:/ˈkroʊməfeɪn/| n. |变色物质，色彩显示剂|A substance that exhibits chromophany, a change in color with temperature.  It refers to a reversible change in color of a material with a change in temperature.|The chromophane in the mug changed from blue to pink as hot water was poured in.|近义词：thermochromic material|反义词:|noun
