insatiability:/ɪnˌseɪʃiəˈbɪləti/ | n. |贪得无厌，不知足 |The state of being insatiable; incapable of being satisfied. |Her insatiability for knowledge led her to pursue multiple degrees. 她对知识的渴望驱使她攻读多个学位。| Synonyms: greed, rapacity, avarice, voracity | Antonyms: contentment, satisfaction, moderation | noun

insatiable:/ɪnˈseɪʃəbl/ | adj. |贪得无厌的，不知足的| Impossible to satisfy. |He had an insatiable appetite for power. 他对权力有着永不满足的欲望。| Synonyms: greedy, ravenous, voracious, unquenchable | Antonyms: satisfied, content, moderate | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin insatiabilis, from in- "not" + satiabilis "capable of being satisfied," from satiare "to satisfy," from satis "enough."
