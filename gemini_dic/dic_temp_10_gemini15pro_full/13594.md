attenuating:/əˈtenjuːeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 衰减的，减弱的 | Reducing the force, effect, or value of something. | The thick walls attenuated the sound. 厚厚的墙壁减弱了声音。 |  The lawyer tried to attenuate the seriousness of the crime. 律师试图减轻罪行的严重性。 | 近义词: weakening, diminishing, lessening, reducing | 反义词: amplifying, increasing, strengthening, intensifying | 词性: 形容词

attenuate:/əˈtenjuːeɪt/ | v. | 使减弱，使降低 | To make something less strong, less important, or less serious. |  The drug attenuates the effects of the virus. 这种药物减弱了病毒的影响。 | The artist attenuated the colors in the painting. 艺术家淡化了画作中的颜色。| 近义词: weaken, diminish, lessen, reduce | 反义词: amplify, increase, strengthen, intensify | 词性: 动词

词源: From Latin attenuatus, past participle of attenuare "to make thin, weaken," from ad- "to" + tenuis "thin."
