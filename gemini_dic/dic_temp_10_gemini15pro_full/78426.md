get-the-hook:/ɡet ðə hʊk/| idiom |被解雇，被撤职；被赶下台；(演员)被取消演出；被抛弃|To be dismissed from a job or position, often suddenly or unexpectedly; to be removed from a performance or show; to be rejected romantically. |例句：The lead actor got the hook after a series of poor performances. 主演因一连串糟糕的表演而被撤换。|近义词：be fired, be dismissed, be sacked, be let go, be dumped |反义词：be hired, be employed| idiom

get-the-hook:/ɡet ðə hʊk/| idiom |被迫停止，被打断| To be forced to stop doing something, often abruptly. |例句：I was enjoying my presentation, but I got the hook when the fire alarm went off. 我正享受我的演讲，但火警响起时我被迫停止了。|近义词：be interrupted, be cut short|反义词：continue, proceed| idiom
