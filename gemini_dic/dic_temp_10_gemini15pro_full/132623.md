nondisarmament:/ˌnɒndɪsˈɑːməmənt/ | n. |不裁军，不解除武装|The state of not disarming; a lack of disarmament.  It refers to the continued possession or even build-up of weapons, especially military ones.|例句：The nondisarmament of certain nations is a cause for concern.某些国家不裁军令人担忧。|例句：Their nondisarmament policy has led to increased tensions in the region.他们的不裁军政策导致该地区紧张局势加剧。|近义词：armament, militarization|反义词:disarmament|词性:名词
