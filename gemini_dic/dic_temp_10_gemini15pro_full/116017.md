marshmallow:/ˈmɑːrʃˌmæloʊ/ (US) /ˈmɑːʃˌmæləʊ/ (UK)| n.| 棉花糖 |A soft, spongy confection made from sugar, gelatin, and often corn syrup. | I love toasting marshmallows over a campfire. 我喜欢在篝火上烤棉花糖。| synonym:confection, sweet | antonym:None | noun

marshmallow:/ˈmɑːrʃˌmæloʊ/ (US) /ˈmɑːʃˌmæləʊ/ (UK)| adj.|  棉花糖般的; 柔软的 |Resembling a marshmallow in texture; soft and spongy. | The sofa had marshmallow-soft cushions. 沙发上有棉花糖般柔软的垫子。| synonym:soft, spongy, fluffy | antonym:hard, firm | adjective
