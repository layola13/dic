depict:/dɪˈpɪkt/| v. |描绘，描述，刻画|to represent or show something in a picture, story, movie, etc.; to describe something using words in a way that creates a clear image in someone's mind.|The novel depicts life in a small village in the 19th century. 这部小说描绘了19世纪一个小村庄的生活。|The painting depicts a scene from the Bible. 这幅画描绘了圣经中的一个场景。|近义词：portray, represent, illustrate, describe|反义词：misrepresent, distort|词性:动词

depicture:/dɪˈpɪktʃər/| v. |描绘，描述，刻画|To represent in a picture or sculpture; to describe vividly in words.|The author depictured the hardships of life during the war. 作者描绘了战争期间生活的艰辛。|The mural depictured scenes from local history.  壁画描绘了当地历史的场景。|近义词：depict, portray, represent, illustrate|反义词：misrepresent, distort|词性:动词


词源: From Late Latin *depictus, past participle of depingere, from de- + pingere "to paint."
