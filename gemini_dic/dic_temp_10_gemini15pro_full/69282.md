ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fetely, from Old French *fait, *fet (Modern French fait), past participle of faire (“to do, to make”), from Latin factum, neuter past participle of faciō (“I do, I make”).  More at fact, do.  The suffix -ly is a common adverbial ending.
