brasseries:/bræsəri/ | n. |啤酒屋；小餐馆|A restaurant serving beer, wine, and simple meals.  A brasserie is often less formal than a restaurant. |例句：We had a delicious meal at the French brasserie. 我们在法国小餐馆吃了一顿美味的佳肴。

例句：The brasserie offers a wide selection of beers on tap. 这家啤酒屋提供各种各样的散装啤酒。|近义词：restaurant, bistro, cafe, pub |反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑一些高档餐厅类型) fine dining restaurant


ETYMOLOGY->
From French "brasserie," meaning "brewery," from Old French "bracier," meaning "to brew." Ultimately from Vulgar Latin "*braciare," from Latin "brasium," meaning "malt."
