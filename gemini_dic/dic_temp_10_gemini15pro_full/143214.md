outcry:/ˈaʊtkraɪ/| n.|强烈抗议，大声疾呼|a strong expression of public disapproval or anger|例句：There was a public outcry against the new tax.  公众对新税强烈抗议。

例句：The proposed cuts in education spending led to a national outcry.  削减教育经费的提议引起了全国性的强烈抗议。

近义词：protest, clamor, uproar, commotion|反义词: silence, acceptance|词性:noun
