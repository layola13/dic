outfed: /ˌaʊtˈfɛd/
verb
喂得比…多，胜过
fed more than; surpassed in feeding.
The farmer outfed his neighbor's prize-winning pig.  这位农民把猪喂得比他邻居的获奖猪还好。
outdo, surpass, excel
be outdone by, fall behind

ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + feed.  Out-  表示“超越，胜过”。 feed 表示“喂养”。
