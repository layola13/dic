ordainable:/ɔːrˈdeɪnəbl/
adjective
可授予圣职的；可被任命的
Suitable or eligible to be ordained as a priest or minister.
He felt a calling to the priesthood and considered himself ordainable. 他感觉自己被召唤去当神父，认为自己可以被授予圣职。
近义词:eligible, suitable, qualified
反义词: ineligible, unsuitable, unqualified
词性: adjective

ordain:/ɔːrˈdeɪn/
verb
授予圣职；任命；规定；注定
1. To officially make someone a priest or minister in a religious ceremony.
2. To order or decree something officially.
3. (often passive) to destine or predestine.

词源:  From Old French *ordeiner, from Latin ordinare “to set in order, arrange, regulate,” from ordo “order, row, series.”
