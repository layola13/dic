demerited: /dɪˈmɛrɪtɪd/
adjective
受处分的，被降级的
Having received a demerit or demerits; deserving of blame or censure.
The demerited soldier was demoted in rank.  这个受处分的士兵被降级了。
近义词: penalized, censured, reprimanded
反义词: commended, praised, rewarded
词性: adjective

词源:  From "demerit" + "-ed". "Demerit" comes from Old French "demerite" meaning "ill desert," from "de-" (down, away) + "merite" (merit).
