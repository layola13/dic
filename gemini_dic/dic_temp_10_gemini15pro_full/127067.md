natantly:/ˈneɪtəntli/| adv.|天生地，天然地|In a natural manner; innately.  Existing naturally; not acquired or learned. |The child natantly understood the concepts of sharing and kindness.  这个孩子天生就懂得分享和友善的概念。|近义词：innately, naturally, inherently|反义词：acquiredly, learnedly|词性:副词
词源: From Latin *natans, present participle of natō (“I am born, I spring forth”), from PIE *gn̥-tós (“born”).
