misplant:/ˌmɪsˈplɑːnt/
verb
错误地种植; 移栽不当
to plant (something) in the wrong place or at the wrong time
The gardener misplanted the roses in the shade, and they didn't thrive. 园丁把玫瑰错种在阴凉处，导致它们长势不好。

verb
(比喻) 放置不当; 安置不当
to put (something) in an unsuitable or inappropriate place
He felt he had been misplanted in the corporate world. 他觉得自己被放错了地方，不适合在企业界工作。

近义词: transplant incorrectly, plant in the wrong place
反义词: plant correctly

词性: verb
词源: mis- (prefix meaning "wrong" or "badly") + plant (verb meaning "to put (a seed, plant, or bulb) into the ground so that it can grow")
