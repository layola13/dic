connectability:/kəˌnɛktəˈbɪləti/ | n. |可连接性，连接能力|The ability of something to be connected to something else, especially to a network or system. |The new software improved the connectability of devices within the network.  这个新软件提高了网络中设备的连接能力。|Synonyms: interoperability, compatibility, linkability | Antonyms:  incompatibility, isolation | noun

connectability:/kəˌnɛktəˈbɪləti/ | n. |连接性，连通性|The state or extent of being connectable. |The connectability of the region has been greatly improved by the new highway. 新的高速公路极大地提高了该地区的连通性。|Synonyms: accessibility, reachability, networkability | Antonyms: inaccessibility, remoteness | noun
