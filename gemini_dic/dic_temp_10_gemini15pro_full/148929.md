overwithered:/ˌoʊvərˈwɪðərd/| adj.|过度枯萎的，极其憔悴的|Excessively withered; dried up and shriveled beyond the normal point of withering.|The overwithered flowers crumbled at my touch. 这些过度枯萎的花一碰就碎了。|The overwithered leaves fell from the branches. 过度枯萎的叶子从树枝上掉落下来。|近义词：withered, shriveled, desiccated, parched|反义词：flourishing, thriving, lush, vibrant|词性:形容词
词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "withered" (past participle of "wither," meaning "to dry up or shrivel")
