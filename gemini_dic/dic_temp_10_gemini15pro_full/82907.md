gumby:/ˈɡʌmbi/| n.|冈比，橡皮泥人；笨拙的人|A Gumby is a green, humanoid clay animation character. 冈比是一个绿色的、人形的黏土动画角色。|例句：He's as flexible as a Gumby. 他像冈比一样灵活。|近义词：klutz, goofball|反义词:genius, expert|noun

gumby:/ˈɡʌmbi/| adj.|笨拙的，愚笨的|Describing someone clumsy or foolish, like the clay animation character. 形容某人笨拙或愚蠢，就像那个黏土动画角色一样。|例句：He made a gumby move and spilled his coffee. 他笨手笨脚地打翻了咖啡。|近义词：clumsy, awkward|反义词:graceful, coordinated|adjective
