directness:/dɪˈrɛktnɪs/, /daɪˈrɛktnɪs/ | n. |直线性；直达性 |The quality of being direct or going straight to a destination. |The directness of the route saved us a lot of time. 这条路线的直达性为我们节省了很多时间。| synonyms: straightness, linearity | antonyms: indirectness, circuitousness | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From direct + -ness.  "direct" comes from the Latin word "directus," the past participle of "dirigere," meaning "to guide or direct."  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
