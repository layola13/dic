inconvincibly:/ˌɪnkənˈvɪnsɪbli/
adverb
无法反驳地，无可辩驳地
in a way that cannot be convincingly argued against.
Her argument was presented so inconvincibly that no one could disagree. 她的论点如此无可辩驳地提出，以至于没有人能够不同意。
The evidence proved his innocence inconvincibly.  证据无可辩驳地证明了他的清白。


近义词:irrefutably, undeniably, indisputably
反义词: disputably, questionably


词源:
From in- (not) + convincible (capable of being convinced) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Latin convincere (to conquer, overcome, convince).
