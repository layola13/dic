concordancing:/kənˈkɔːrdənsiŋ/ | n. |  索引；词汇索引；共现分析 | The process of finding and listing all the occurrences of a particular word or phrase in a text or corpus, along with its immediate context. |  Researchers used concordancing software to analyze the use of metaphors in Shakespeare's plays. 研究人员使用索引软件来分析莎士比亚戏剧中隐喻的用法。| synonyms: indexing, collocation analysis | antonyms: N/A | noun

concordancer:/kənˈkɔːrdənsər/ | n. |索引程序;索引编制者 | A computer program or a person who creates a concordance. | The concordancer quickly generated a list of all instances of the word "love" in the novel. 索引程序快速生成了小说中所有“爱”字实例的列表。 | synonyms: indexing software, indexer | antonyms: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English *concordancia*, from Latin *concordantia* "agreement, harmony,"  from *concordant-* (stem of *concordans*) "agreeing," present participle of *concordare* "to agree," from *com-* "together" + *cord-* (stem of *cor*) "heart".  The sense of "an alphabetical index of the principal words of a book" is first recorded in the early 15c.
