depolishes:/diːˈpɒlɪʃɪz/
verb
擦掉光泽，使失去光泽
removes the polish from something.  This can refer to the literal removal of a shiny surface or, figuratively, to the diminishing of something's prestige or elegance.
The harsh cleaner depolishes the wood.  这种强力清洁剂会使木头失去光泽。
Years of neglect had depolished the grand piano.  多年的疏忽使这架大钢琴失去了光泽。
synonyms: dulls, tarnishes, dims
antonyms: polishes, shines, burnishes
v.
