mother-like:/ˈmʌðərˌlaɪk/ | adj. |像母亲的，慈母般的| Resembling or characteristic of a mother, especially in terms of kindness, care, and protectiveness. | She has a mother-like affection for all her students. 她对所有的学生都有一种慈母般的关爱。 | The nurse provided mother-like care to the injured child.  护士对受伤的孩子给予了母亲般的照顾。 | 近义词: maternal, nurturing, motherly | 反义词: uncaring, neglectful | 词性:形容词

词源:
"mother" (n.) + "-like" (suffix)
"mother" comes from Old English "mōdor," from Proto-Germanic "*mōdēr," from Proto-Indo-European "*māter-."
"-like" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling," "characteristic of," from Old English "-līc."
