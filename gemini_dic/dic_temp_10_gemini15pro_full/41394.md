cornamute: /ˈkɔːrnəˌmjuːt/ | n. |弱音器；（尤指）铜管乐器弱音器|A device used to muffle or soften the sound of a musical instrument, especially a brass instrument.  It is typically inserted into the bell of the instrument.|The trumpet player used a cornamute to practice quietly.  小号演奏者使用弱音器安静地练习。|Synonyms: mute, silencer|Antonyms: amplifier|noun
