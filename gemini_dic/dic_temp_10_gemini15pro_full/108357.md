lavisher: /ˈlævɪʃər/ | n. | 挥霍者，奢侈者 |  A person who spends money or resources extravagantly. | He was a lavisher of time and money on his hobbies. 他在他的爱好上挥霍时间和金钱。 | 近义词：spendthrift, prodigal, squanderer | 反义词：saver, miser | 词性: noun

lavish: /ˈlævɪʃ/ | adj. | 奢华的，丰富的 |  Characterized by extravagance and abundance. | They gave a lavish party. 他们举办了一个奢华的派对。 | 近义词：luxurious, opulent, extravagant | 反义词：meager, sparse, frugal | 词性: adjective

lavish: /ˈlævɪʃ/ | v. | 慷慨地给予，挥霍 | To give or bestow something generously or extravagantly. | She lavished affection on her grandchildren. 她对孙辈们倾注了无限的爱。 | 近义词：shower, bestow, heap | 反义词：stint, withhold, begrudge | 词性: verb
