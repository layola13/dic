breastpin: /ˈbrɛstˌpɪn/ (US), /ˈbrɛstˌpɪn/ (UK)
n.
胸针，领针
A decorative pin worn on the breast.
She wore a beautiful antique breastpin on her lapel. 她在外套翻领上戴了一枚漂亮的古董胸针。
