liberator:/ˈlɪbəreɪtər/| n.|解放者，释放者|A person who liberates a person or place, especially from imprisonment or foreign control.|例句：The liberators were welcomed with open arms by the jubilant citizens.  解放者受到欢欣鼓舞的市民的热烈欢迎。

例句：She is remembered as a great liberator of her people. 她被铭记为她的人民的伟大解放者。|近义词：rescuer, emancipator, deliverer, savior|反义词：oppressor, captor, enslaver|词性:noun
