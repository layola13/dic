immobilizer:/ɪˈmoʊbɪˌlaɪzər/ | n. | 防盗器，固定器 |A device that prevents something, especially a vehicle, from being used or moved. |  The car thief couldn't start the engine because of the immobilizer. 汽车窃贼由于防盗器的缘故无法启动引擎。| synonyms: anti-theft device, security system | antonyms: activator, enabler | noun

immobilize: /ɪˈmoʊbəˌlaɪz/ | vt. | 使无法移动，使固定 | To prevent something or someone from moving or operating. | The doctor immobilized his broken leg with a cast. 医生用石膏固定了他断掉的腿。| synonyms: restrict, disable | antonyms: mobilize, activate | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From im- (not) + mobilize (to make mobile) + -er (agent suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *mobilis* (movable).
