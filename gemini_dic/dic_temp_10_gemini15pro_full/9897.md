apathistical:/ˌæpəˈθɪstɪkəl/
adj.
冷漠的，无动于衷的
Having or showing little or no emotion; indifferent.
He remained apathistical despite the pleas for help. 他对求助的请求仍然无动于衷。
近义词：indifferent, uninterested, listless, passive
反义词：enthusiastic, passionate, concerned, interested
词性：adjective

Etymology:
From a- (without) +‎ path- (feeling) +‎ -ical (suffix forming adjectives). Ultimately from Ancient Greek ἀ- (a-, “without”) + πάθος (páthos, “suffering, feeling”).
