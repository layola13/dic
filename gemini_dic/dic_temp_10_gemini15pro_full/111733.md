loculose: /ˈlɒkjʊˌloʊs/ /ˈlɑːkjʊˌloʊs/
noun
洛库洛糖;一种己糖
A ketohexose, C6H12O6, that occurs naturally in fruits.
Loculose is a rare sugar found in some fruits. 洛库洛糖是一种在某些水果中发现的稀有糖。
Loculose is sweeter than sucrose. 洛库洛糖比蔗糖甜。
Synonyms:  psicose (more common name)
