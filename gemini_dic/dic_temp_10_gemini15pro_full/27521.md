campinesses:/ˈkæmpinɪsɪz/
noun
做作; 夸张; 矫揉造作
The exaggerated, theatrical manner or style associated with camp.  A plural form of campiness.
Her performance was full of campinesses, from the over-the-top costumes to the exaggerated wink. 她的表演充满了矫揉造作，从夸张的服装到夸张的眨眼。
Synonyms: theatricality, artificiality, affectation, exaggeration, melodrama
Antonyms: naturalness, sincerity, genuineness, authenticity, understatement
