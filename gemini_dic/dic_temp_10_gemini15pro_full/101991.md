isochronism:/aɪˈsɒkrənɪzəm/ /aɪˈsɑːkrənɪzəm/
noun
等时性
The quality or state of having equal duration or occurring at regular intervals.
The isochronism of a pendulum is affected by gravity. 钟摆的等时性受重力影响。
isochrony, uniformity, regularity
anisochronism, irregularity
n.
