chirologist:/kɪˈrɒlədʒɪst/ /kaɪˈrɑːlədʒɪst/ | n. | 手相学家 | A person who practices chirology; one who reads palms. |  She visited a chirologist to have her palm read. 她拜访了一位手相学家，让他解读她的掌纹。

| Example: The chirologist predicted a long and prosperous life based on the lines on her hand.  手相学家根据她手上的纹路预测她会长寿且富足。 | Synonym: palm reader, hand reader | Antonym: N/A | noun

chirology:/kɪˈrɒlədʒi/ /kaɪˈrɑːlədʒi/ | n. | 手相学 | The study of the hand, especially the lines and mounts of the palm, as a means of character analysis and fortune-telling. |  Chirology is considered a pseudoscience by many. 许多人认为手相学是伪科学。

| Example: He studied chirology in his spare time, fascinated by the idea that the hand could reveal a person's destiny. 他在业余时间学习手相学，对手可以揭示一个人命运的想法着迷。 | Synonym: palmistry | Antonym: N/A | noun
