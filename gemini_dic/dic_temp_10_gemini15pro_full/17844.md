beeches:/ˈbiːtʃɪz/
n.
山毛榉树（复数）
A large tree with smooth grey bark, found in temperate regions.  The wood of the beech tree is used for making furniture and flooring.
The beeches swayed gently in the breeze. 山毛榉树在微风中轻轻摇曳。

近义词:trees, hardwoods
反义词:(无直接反义词，可以考虑shrubs 灌木)
词性:noun
