microdotting: /ˈmaɪkrəʊˌdɒtɪŋ/ | n. | 微缩点印; 微点印刷 | The process of reducing images or information to such a small size that they are almost invisible to the naked eye, often used for security purposes. |  The passport used microdotting as a security measure.  这本护照使用微点印刷作为安全措施。 | synonyms: microminiaturization, miniaturization | antonyms: magnification, enlargement | noun

microdotting: /ˈmaɪkrəʊˌdɒtɪŋ/ | v. | 将…缩印成微点; 微点印刷 | The act of applying microdots to a document or object. | They are microdotting all official documents. 他们正在对所有官方文件进行微点印刷。 | synonyms: microminiaturizing, miniaturizing | antonyms: magnifying, enlarging | verb
