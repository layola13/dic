fosterers:/ˈfɒstərərz/ (UK) /ˈfɑːstərərz/ (US)| n.|寄养者，抚育者|People who temporarily care for children or animals that cannot live with their own families. |例句：The fosterers provided a loving home for the abandoned kittens. 寄养者为被遗弃的小猫提供了一个充满爱的家。

例句：The organization is always looking for more fosterers for rescued dogs.  这个组织一直在寻找更多愿意寄养获救犬只的人。


近义词：carers, guardians, custodians|反义词: biological parents|词性:noun
