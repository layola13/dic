batlike:/ˈbætˌlaɪk/| adj.|像蝙蝠的，蝙蝠似的|Resembling a bat in appearance or behavior. |The creature had batlike wings and emitted high-pitched squeaks.  这生物有着像蝙蝠一样的翅膀，发出尖锐的叫声。|Synonyms: bat-winged, chiropteran|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, consider describing the opposite quality, e.g., birdlike, angelic)|adjective
