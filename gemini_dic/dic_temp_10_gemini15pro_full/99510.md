interfraternal:/ˌɪntərfrəˈtɜːrnl/ | adj. | 兄弟会之间的；互助会的 | Existing or occurring between fraternities. |  The interfraternal council organized a campus-wide charity event.  兄弟会间理事会组织了一场校园范围的慈善活动。 |  intersociety, interorganizational |  intrafraturnal | adjective

Etymology:
From inter- + fraternal.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," deriving from Latin. "Fraternal" comes from the Latin "fraternus," meaning "brotherly," related to "frater" (brother).
