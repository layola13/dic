baha'isms:/bəˈhaɪɪzəmz/
n.
巴哈伊教义；巴哈伊信仰
the religious beliefs and practices of Baha'ism, a monotheistic religion emphasizing the spiritual unity of all humankind.
Baha'isms emphasize the oneness of God and the unity of humanity. 巴哈伊教义强调上帝的唯一性和人类的团结。
The core Baha'isms include the belief in one God and the unity of all religions.  巴哈伊信仰的核心包括相信唯一的上帝和所有宗教的统一。
近义词: Baha'i Faith, Baha'i teachings
反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider opposing religious doctrines)
词性: noun
