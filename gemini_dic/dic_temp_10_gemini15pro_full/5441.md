alms:/ɑːmz/ | n. |施舍，救济金|Something given to the poor as charity. |例句：The beggar asked for alms. 乞丐乞讨施舍。

例句：They distributed alms to the needy. 他们向穷人分发救济品。

近义词：charity, donation, contribution, offering |反义词:greed, selfishness|词性:noun
