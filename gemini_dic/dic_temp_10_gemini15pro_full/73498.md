foresee:/fɔːrˈsiː/ /fɔːrˈsiː/
verb
预见，预知
to see or know something that will happen in the future.
It's difficult to foresee what will happen next year. 很难预见明年会发生什么。
The architect foresaw the problem with the design. 建筑师预见到了设计中的问题。

近义词:predict, anticipate, envisage, forecast
反义词:retrospect

词性:v.
