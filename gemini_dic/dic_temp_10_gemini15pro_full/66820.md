expansions:/ɪkˈspænʃənz/ /ɛkˈspænʃənz/| n. |扩张，扩展，扩充；膨胀；展开式|The company is planning expansions into new markets.  该公司正计划拓展新市场。|例句：The rapid expansion of the city caused housing shortages. 城市的快速扩张导致住房短缺。|近义词：growth, development, enlargement, extension, increase|反义词: contraction, reduction, decrease|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French expansion, or from late Latin expansio(n-), from the verb expandere (see expand).
