deliciouses:/dɪˈlɪʃəsɪz/
n.
美味佳肴，好吃的食物（复数）
This is a rare usage; "delicious" is not typically pluralized.  It would generally refer to a variety of delicious foods.
She brought a plate of delicioses to the party. 她带了一盘美味佳肴来参加聚会。
Near synonyms: treats, goodies, delicacies
Antonym: (singular) disgusting, unappetizing
