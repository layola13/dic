borderline:/ˈbɔːrdərlaɪn/| n. adj.|边界线，界限；临界的，边缘的|A line that separates one area, country etc from another; between two different conditions or states|The animal was a borderline case of rabies. 这只动物是狂犬病的临界病例。|Synonyms: boundary, limit, edge, marginal|Antonyms: center, core, interior|noun

borderline:/ˈbɔːrdərlaɪn/| adj.|临界的，边缘的；刚够格的|Between two different conditions or states; barely acceptable|His performance was borderline. 他的表现勉强合格。|Synonyms: marginal, borderline, debatable|Antonyms: definite, clear, unambiguous|adjective

borderlines:/ˈbɔːrdərlaɪnz/| n.|边界线，界限|Plural form of borderline (noun)|The borderlines between countries are often disputed. 国与国之间的边界线经常存在争议。|Synonyms: boundaries, limits, edges|Antonyms: centers, cores, interiors|noun
