high-hatted:/ˈhaɪˌhætɪd/| adj.|傲慢的，自负的|Wearing a top hat (literally or figuratively) and behaving in a snobbish or supercilious manner.  Often implies a sense of superiority and disdain for others.|例句：The high-hatted critic dismissed the performance with a sniff.  这位傲慢的评论家轻蔑地对表演嗤之以鼻。

例句：He acted high-hatted after his promotion, ignoring his former colleagues.  升职后他变得傲慢，对以前的同事不屑一顾。|近义词：snobby, arrogant, supercilious, condescending, haughty|反义词：humble, modest, down-to-earth|词性:形容词
