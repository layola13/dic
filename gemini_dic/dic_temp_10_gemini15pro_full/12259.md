ascensions: /əˈsɛnʃənz/
n.
上升，登高，提升
the act of rising or climbing to a higher position or level.
The balloon's slow ascension was mesmerizing.  气球缓慢的上升令人着迷。
The mountain climbers celebrated their ascension to the summit. 登山者庆祝他们登顶。
synonyms: rise, climb, ascent, elevation
antonyms: descent, fall, decline
noun
