forktail:/ˈfɔːrkˌteɪl/ | n. | 叉尾鸟；燕尾服 | A bird of the Old World flycatcher family, typically having a long forked tail.  A type of formal coat with two long pointed tails at the back, resembling a forked tail. | 例句：The forktail darted through the forest, catching insects on the wing.  叉尾鸟在森林中飞来飞去，捕捉飞虫。

例句：He wore a stylish forktail to the opera. 他穿着一件时髦的燕尾服去歌剧院。| 近义词：swallowtail (for the coat), flycatcher (for the bird) | 反义词: N/A | 词性：noun
