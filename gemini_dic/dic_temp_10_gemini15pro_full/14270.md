autogenetic:/ˌɔːtəʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk/| adj.|自体发生的，自生的|Self-generated; originating within the organism itself.  Relating to or denoting the theory that the continental landmasses originated from within the earth's crust and not from the fragmentation of a pre-existing single continent.|The study suggested an autogenetic origin of the tumor.|近义词：endogenous, innate, self-generated|反义词：exogenous, acquired|词性:adjective
