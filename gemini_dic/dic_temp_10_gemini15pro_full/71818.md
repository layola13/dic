词源：
"Fleen" is an archaic word derived from the Middle English word "flien," which in turn comes from the Old English "flēon."  It shares a common ancestor with the modern word "flee."


注意事项:

“fleen”是一个古语，在现代英语中很少使用。“flee”是更常用的现代词汇。
