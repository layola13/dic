cephalotomy:/ˌsefəˈlɒtəmi/ |n.| 头颅切开术 |Surgical incision into the head, especially of a fetus to facilitate delivery.  It is a procedure used in obstructed labor when the fetal head is too large to pass through the birth canal. | Cephalotomia was performed due to cephalopelvic disproportion. 由于头盆不称，进行了头颅切开术。|Synonym: craniotomy | Antonym: N/A | noun
