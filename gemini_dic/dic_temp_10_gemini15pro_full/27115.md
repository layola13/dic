caligula:/kəˈlɪɡjʊlə/ | n. | 卡里古拉（罗马帝国皇帝，以残暴闻名） | Gaius Caesar Germanicus, Roman emperor (AD 12-41), known for his cruelty and tyranny.  His reign was marked by extravagance, cruelty, and sexual perversion. | Caligula was a notoriously cruel emperor. 卡里古拉是一位臭名昭著的残暴皇帝。 |  Nero, tyrant, despot |  benevolent ruler, just leader | noun
