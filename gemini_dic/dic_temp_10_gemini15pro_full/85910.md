headinesses:/ˈhɛdinɪsɪz/
n.
兴奋, 晕眩，轻率
The state of being heady; exhilaration or lightheadedness.
The headinesses of youth often lead to impulsive decisions. 年轻人的冲动常常导致草率的决定。
近义词: dizziness, giddiness, lightheadedness, euphoria, excitement
反义词: calmness, sobriety, levelheadedness
词性: 名词 (plural)

headiness:/ˈhɛdinɪs/
n.
兴奋, 晕眩，轻率
The state of being heady; exhilaration or lightheadedness.
The headiness of victory was short-lived. 胜利的喜悦是短暂的。
近义词: dizziness, giddiness, lightheadedness, euphoria, excitement
反义词: calmness, sobriety, levelheadedness
词性: 名词

heady:/ˈhɛdi/
adj.
令人兴奋的, 晕眩的, 轻率的
Intoxicating; exhilarating; tending to make one dizzy or lightheaded.
The heady aroma of the wine filled the room.  令人陶醉的酒香弥漫了整个房间。
近义词: intoxicating, exhilarating, potent, strong
反义词: calming, soothing, mild
词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *hedy, from Old English hēadig (“heady, headlong, precipitate”), equivalent to head +‎ -y. Cognate with Middle Dutch hedigh, Middle Low German hedich.
