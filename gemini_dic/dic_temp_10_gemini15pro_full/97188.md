inflection:/ɪnˈflɛkʃən/| n. |屈折变化，音调变化，词尾变化|The process by which the form of a word is changed to indicate grammatical features such as number, tense, person, mood, or voice.|The inflection of the verb "to be" changes depending on the subject.|synonyms: conjugation, declension, modification, variation|antonyms: invariance, uniformity|noun

inflect:/ɪnˈflɛkt/| v. |使屈折，使弯曲，改变音调|To change the form of (a word) by inflection; to modulate (the voice).|He inflected his voice to express sarcasm.|synonyms: modulate, vary, alter|antonyms: maintain, fix, stabilize|verb
