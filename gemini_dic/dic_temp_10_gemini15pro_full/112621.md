low-end:/ˌloʊˈɛnd/| adj.|低端的，廉价的|Relating to or denoting the cheaper or less sophisticated products in a range.|例句：They targeted the low-end market with their budget smartphones. 他们用廉价智能手机瞄准低端市场。|例句：The low-end model of this car lacks many features. 这款车的低端型号缺少很多功能。|近义词：cheap, budget, inexpensive, bottom-of-the-line|反义词：high-end, premium, luxury, top-of-the-line|词性:adjective
词源:Compound of "low" and "end". "Low" referring to a lower price or quality, and "end" referring to the part of a range or spectrum.
