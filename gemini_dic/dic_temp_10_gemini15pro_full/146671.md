overhauling:/ˌoʊvərˈhɔːlɪŋ/| n. |彻底检修，大修|A complete examination and repair of a machine or system.|The car is in the garage for an overhauling. 这辆车正在车库里进行大修。|近义词：repairing, renovating, restoring, reconditioning|反义词:damaging, neglecting|noun

overhaul:/ˌoʊvərˈhɔːl/| vt. |彻底检修，大修|To examine and repair a machine or system thoroughly.|The mechanic overhauled the engine. 机械师彻底检修了发动机。|近义词：repair, renovate, restore, recondition|反义词:damage, neglect|verb
