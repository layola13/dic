fulfil:/fʊlˈfɪl/ (US) /fʊlˈfɪl/ (UK)| vt.|履行，实现，完成|To carry out or bring to completion; to satisfy or meet (a requirement, condition, or need).|例句：He fulfilled his promise to visit his grandmother. 他履行了去看望他祖母的承诺。|例句：She fulfilled her dream of becoming a doctor. 她实现了成为一名医生的梦想。|近义词：accomplish, achieve, complete, execute, realize, satisfy|反义词：fail, neglect, abandon|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fulfillen*, from Old English *fullfyllan* (“to fill full, fulfill, complete”), equivalent to *full* + *fill*. Cognate with Dutch *vervullen* (“to fulfill”), German *erfüllen* (“to fulfill”).
