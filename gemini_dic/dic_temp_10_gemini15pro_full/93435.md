illuminating:/ɪˈluːmɪneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
启发性的，启迪的; 使人了解的; 照亮的
Making something clearer and easier to understand.  Shedding light on (literally or figuratively).
The documentary was illuminating, providing new insights into the artist's life.  这部纪录片很有启发性，提供了对艺术家生活的新见解。
Her explanation was illuminating. 她的解释使人茅塞顿开。
The streetlights were illuminating the path ahead. 路灯照亮了前方的道路。
synonyms: enlightening, informative, insightful, revealing, clarifying, elucidating
antonyms: confusing, obscure, misleading, darkening
词性: 形容词
