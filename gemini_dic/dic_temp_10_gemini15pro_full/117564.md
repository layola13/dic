megafarad:/ˈmegəˌfæræd/ | n. |兆法拉|A unit of capacitance equal to one million farads.  It is a very large unit and is typically used for very large capacitors. |This capacitor has a capacitance of one megafarad. 这个电容器的电容为一兆法拉。|Synonym: none (unit of measurement)|Antonym: none (unit of measurement)|noun
