ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *mutilationem, accusative of mutilatio (“maiming, mutilation”), from Latin mutilare (“to maim, mutilate”), from mutilus (“maimed, mutilated, without horns”), probably from PIE *mēu- (“to cut off, shorten”).
