gallops:/ˈɡæləps/ (US) /ˈɡælɒps/ (UK)| n. v.| 疾驰，飞奔；飞快地进行|A fast gait of a horse or other quadruped in which all four feet are off the ground together in each stride. (As a verb) To run or move at a gallop.|The horse galloped across the field.  这匹马飞奔穿过田野。|近义词：runs, sprints, dashes, races|反义词:crawls, creeps, walks, plods|词性:noun, verb
词源:From Old French *galoper, of Germanic origin; related to wallop.
