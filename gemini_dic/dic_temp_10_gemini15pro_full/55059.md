disarray:/dɪsəˈreɪ/
|n.|混乱，紊乱|A state of disorder or confusion.|The room was in complete disarray after the party. 房间在聚会后一片狼藉。|近义词：disorder, chaos, confusion, mess|反义词：order, tidiness, organization|名词
disarray:/dɪsəˈreɪ/
|vt.|使混乱，使紊乱|To throw into disorder or confusion.|The sudden news disarrayed their plans. 突如其来的消息打乱了他们的计划。|近义词：disorganize, disorder, confuse, muddle|反义词：organize, arrange, order|动词
