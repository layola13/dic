nonpersonifications:/ˌnɒnpərˌsɒnɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 非人格化，不赋予人格 |  The avoidance of giving human qualities to inanimate objects, abstract concepts, or animals. | The poem relies on nonpersonifications, focusing on the raw beauty of nature without imbuing it with human emotions. 这首诗依靠非人格化，专注于自然的原始之美，而没有赋予它人类的情感。| 近义词: objectification, depersonalization | 反义词: personification, anthropomorphism | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "personification" (from "personify" - to give human qualities to something nonhuman).  "Personify" comes from Latin "persona" (mask, character) + "-fy" (suffix meaning "to make").
