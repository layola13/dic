askingly:/ˈɑːskɪŋli/| adv.|询问地；探询地|In a way that shows you are asking a question, especially by the expression on your face or in your voice.  It implies a subtle, non-verbal questioning through expression or tone.|She looked at him askingly. 她探询地望着他。|近义词：inquiringly, questioningly, curiously|反义词: assertively, declaratively|词性:副词
Etymology: From "asking" + "-ly" (suffix forming adverbs). "Asking" comes from the Old English "ascian" (to ask). The "-ly" suffix comes from the Old English "-lice," related to "like."
