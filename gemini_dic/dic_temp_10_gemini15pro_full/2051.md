acupressures: /ˈækjʊˌprɛʃərz/
noun
指压；穴位按摩
Acupressure is a traditional Chinese medicine technique similar to acupuncture, but instead of needles, it uses finger pressure on specific points on the body.
She uses acupressure to relieve her headaches. 她用指压来缓解头痛。
Acupressure can be used to treat a variety of ailments. 指压可以用来治疗各种疾病。
近义词: acupuncture, massage therapy, shiatsu
反义词: (No direct antonym, but potentially) conventional medicine, allopathy
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From acu- (needle, sharp) + pressure.  "Acu-" comes from Latin "acus" (needle).
