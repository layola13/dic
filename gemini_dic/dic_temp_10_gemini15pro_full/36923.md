colossally:/kəˈlɒsəli/ /kəˈlɑːsəli/| adv.|巨大地，非常地|To a colossal extent; enormously.  Meaning "on a huge scale; extremely."|The project was colossally expensive. 这个项目耗资巨大。|
|synonyms: enormously, immensely, hugely, vastly, tremendously, exceedingly, extremely, massively, monumentally, gigantesquely, gargantuanly, titanically|antonyms: minutely, slightly, insignificantly|adverb|

colossal:/kəˈlɒsəl/ /kəˈlɑːsəl/| adj.|巨大的，庞大的|Extremely large or great.|They have a colossal statue in the town square.  他们在城镇广场上有一座巨大的雕像。|
|synonyms: enormous, immense, huge, vast, tremendous, gigantic, gargantuan, mammoth, monumental, titanic|antonyms: tiny, small, minuscule|adjective|


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *colossus* ("gigantic statue"), from Greek *κολοσσός* (*kolossós*), of unknown pre-Greek origin.
