mosques:/mɒsks/ | n. |清真寺|A building used for public worship by Muslims.|Many beautiful mosques can be found throughout the world. 世界各地有很多美丽的清真寺。|The mosque is a central place of worship for the Muslim community. 清真寺是穆斯林社区的中心礼拜场所。|synonyms: Islamic center, masjid|antonyms: None|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French mosquée, from Old Italian moschea, from Vulgar Latin *moschita, from Arabic masjid "place of prostration (in prayer)," verbal noun of sajada "he prostrated himself in worship."
