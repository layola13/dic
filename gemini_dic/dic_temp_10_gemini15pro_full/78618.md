gibbous:/ˈɡɪbəs/ | adj. | 凸圆的，隆起的（尤指月亮）|  (of the moon or a planet) More than half but less than fully illuminated.  Having a hump.|
The gibbous moon shone brightly in the night sky. 凸圆的月亮在夜空中闪耀。|
The camel has a gibbous back. 骆驼有隆起的背。|
Near synonyms:  convex, rounded, bulging, protuberant |
Antonyms:  concave, hollow, flat |
词性: 形容词
