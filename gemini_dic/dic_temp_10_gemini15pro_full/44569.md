crouchant:/ˈkraʊtʃənt/| adj.|蹲伏的，蜷伏的|Being in a crouching or squatting position.|The lion lay crouching in the grass, ready to pounce. 狮子蹲伏在草丛中，准备突袭。|近义词：squatting, crouched, recumbent|反义词：standing, upright|adjective

crouchant:/ˈkraʊtʃənt/| n.|蹲伏的动物（纹章学用语）|An animal depicted in a crouching position in heraldry.|The crest featured a crouchant lion.  徽章上是一头蹲伏的狮子。|近义词：N/A (in this heraldic sense)|反义词：rampant (in heraldry)|noun


词源: From Old French *crouchier, ultimately from Vulgar Latin *coactiare "to press together", from Latin cogere "to compel, force".
