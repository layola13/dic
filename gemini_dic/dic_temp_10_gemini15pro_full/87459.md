hesperornis:/ˌhɛspərˈɔːrnɪs/
n.
黄昏鸟
an extinct, flightless, toothed aquatic bird of the Cretaceous period, fossils of which have been found in North America.
The Hesperornis, with its powerful legs and reduced wings, was a remarkable example of adaptation to a marine environment. 黄昏鸟凭借其强壮的腿和退化的翅膀，是适应海洋环境的一个显著例子。

Hesperornis fossils provide valuable insights into the evolution of birds. 黄昏鸟化石为了解鸟类的进化提供了宝贵的见解。

近义词:  diving bird, aquatic bird (broadly)
反义词:  terrestrial bird, flying bird
词性: noun


词源: From Greek ἕσπερος (hesperos, “western, evening”) + ὄρνις (ornis, “bird”). Literally "western bird" or "evening bird."
