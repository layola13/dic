formation:/fɔːrˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 形成，构成；形成物；排列，队形 | The process of forming something or of being formed.  The way in which something is formed or arranged. |例句：The formation of the mountains took millions of years. 山脉的形成花费了数百万年。

例句：The cloud formations were spectacular. 云的形状非常壮观。|近义词：development, creation, structure, arrangement |反义词：destruction, demolition | 词性: 名词

词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French, or from late Latin formatio(n-), from the verb formare (see form).
