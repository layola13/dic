dissimulative:/dɪˈsɪmjʊlətɪv/
adjective
掩饰的，装作不知情的
concealing one's true feelings or intentions.
Her dissimulative nature made it hard to know what she was really thinking. 她掩饰的天性让人很难知道她真正在想什么。
Synonyms: deceptive, artful, sly, cunning, secretive, furtive
Antonyms: open, honest, frank, candid, forthright
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From Late Latin dissimulatus, past participle of dissimulare "to disguise, conceal," from dis- "not, opposite of" + simulare "to copy, imitate, pretend," from similis "like."
