bad-mouthers:/ˈbædˌmaʊðərz/
n.|诽谤者，说坏话的人|People who speak negatively or critically about someone or something, often unfairly or maliciously.  They spread rumors or make derogatory remarks to damage a person's reputation.|Those bad-mouthers are spreading lies about the company. 那些说坏话的人正在散播关于公司的谎言。|critics, detractors, slanderers|supporters, admirers|noun
