insubjection:/ˌɪnsəbˈdʒɛkʃən/
n.|不服从，不顺从；反抗|The act or state of not being subjected or subordinate; disobedience; resistance.|His insubjection to authority led to his dismissal. 他对权威的反抗导致了他的解雇。|Near synonyms: disobedience, defiance, rebellion, resistance, noncompliance|Near antonyms: obedience, submission, compliance|noun
