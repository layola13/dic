martyr:/ˈmɑːrti/| v.|（为事业或信仰）折磨，使殉难|To kill someone because of their beliefs.|He was martyred for his faith. 他因其信仰而殉难。|synonyms: persecute, torture, kill|antonyms: protect, save|verb
