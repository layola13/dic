flashcards:/ˈflæʃˌkɑːrdz/ | n. | 抽认卡 | Small cards with information on them, used for learning and memorizing facts. |  I'm using flashcards to study for my vocabulary test. 我正在用抽认卡学习我的词汇测试。|  study cards, note cards | textbooks, dictionaries | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "flash" (quick, brief glimpse) + "cards". The term refers to the quick presentation of information on the cards.
