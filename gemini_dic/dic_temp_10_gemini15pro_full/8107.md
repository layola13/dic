anthypochondriac:/ˌæntiˌhaɪpəˈkɒndriæk/ |  n. |反疑病症患者，不相信自己有病的人 | A person who is unduly worried about their health, often fearing or imagining they have a serious illness. |  He was such an anthypochondriac that he refused to see a doctor even when he was clearly ill. 他如此不相信自己有病，即使明显生病了也拒绝去看医生。 | 近义词: optimist, denier | 反义词: hypochondriac, worrier | 词性: noun


anthypochondriac:/ˌæntiˌhaɪpəˈkɒndriæk/ | adj. |不相信自己有病的，反疑病症的 | Relating to or characteristic of an anthypochondriac. | Her anthypochondriac tendencies led her to dismiss her symptoms as mere stress. 她不相信自己有病的倾向使她把自己的症状仅仅视为压力。 | 近义词: optimistic, unconcerned | 反义词: hypochondriacal, anxious | 词性: adjective

词源:
From anti- + hypochondriac.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposite". "Hypochondriac" comes from Greek "hypochondria," referring to the area of the abdomen believed in ancient times to be the seat of melancholy and hypochondria.
