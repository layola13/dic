misbeseem:/ˌmɪsbɪˈsiːm/
verb
不适合，不相称
to be inappropriate or unsuitable for; to not befit
It misbeseems a gentleman to behave in such a manner.  一位绅士举止如此，实属不该。
近义词:misbecome, ill-suit
反义词:suit, befit, become
词性:v.
