misprovoking:/ˌmɪsprəˈvoʊkɪŋ/
adjective
令人恼怒的；挑衅的
Tending or serving to provoke wrongly; giving rise to irritation or resentment through misinterpretation or misunderstanding.
His misprovoking behavior led to a heated argument. 他的挑衅行为导致了一场激烈的争论。
Near Synonyms: irritating, annoying, vexing, exasperating, galling
Near Antonyms: soothing, calming, placating, mollifying
词性: adjective
