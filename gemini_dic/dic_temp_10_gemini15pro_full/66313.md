excrescency: /ɪkˈskresənsi/ /ekˈskresənsi/ | n. |  (不正常的)增生，瘤，赘生物;  多余的、不正常的部分 | An abnormal outgrowth or protuberance, especially on the body; a superfluous or disfiguring addition. |  The doctor removed the small excrescence from her hand. 医生从她手上切除了那个小赘生物。 | Synonyms: outgrowth, protuberance, lump, growth, tumor, wart | Antonyms: indentation, concavity, depression | noun

excrescence: /ɪkˈskresənsi/ /ekˈskresənsi/ | n. |  (作品、制度等的)不和谐部分，累赘 | A disfiguring and unnecessary addition to something. | The unnecessary ornamentation on the building was considered an architectural excrescence.  建筑物上不必要的装饰被认为是建筑上的累赘。| Synonyms: superfluity, redundancy, excess, addendum | Antonyms: essential, necessity, core | noun
