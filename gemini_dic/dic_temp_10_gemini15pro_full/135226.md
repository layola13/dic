nonpacifists:/ˌnɒnˈpæsɪfɪsts/ | /ˌnɑːnˈpæsɪfɪsts/ | n. | 非和平主义者 | People who do not believe in pacifism (the belief that war and violence are unjustifiable). |  The nonpacifists argued for strengthening the military.  非和平主义者主张加强军队。 | Synonyms: warmongers, hawks, militants | Antonyms: pacifists, peacemakers, doves | noun

nonpacifist:/ˌnɒnˈpæsɪfɪst/ | /ˌnɑːnˈpæsɪfɪst/ | adj. | 非和平主义的 |  Not pacifistic; advocating or supporting war or violence. |  He took a nonpacifist stance on the conflict.  他对这场冲突采取了非和平主义的立场。 | Synonyms:  militant, hawkish | Antonyms: pacifist, peaceful | adjective
