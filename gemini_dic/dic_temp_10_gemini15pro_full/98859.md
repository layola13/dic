intemperately: /ɪnˈtɛmpərɪtli/
adverb
放纵地；无节制地；过度地
without restraint; excessively.
He drank intemperately. 他过度饮酒。
Synonyms: excessively, immoderately, unrestrainedly, inordinately
Antonyms: moderately, temperately, restrainedly


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin intemperatus "immoderate," from in- "not" + temperatus "moderate" (see temperate).
