centralising:/ˈsɛntrəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
集中化；中央集权化
The act of bringing something under central control.
The government is centralising control of education. 政府正在集中教育控制权。
The company is centralising its operations in one location.  这家公司正在将其业务集中在一个地点。
centralizing (American spelling)
decentralising, dispersing
v.
