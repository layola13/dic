outdriving:/ˌaʊtˈdraɪvɪŋ/
v.
胜过驾驶；在驾驶方面超过
Surpassing someone in driving skill or distance covered.
He was outdriving everyone on the track. 他在赛道上胜过所有人。
近义词: outpacing, surpassing
反义词: lagging, trailing
词性: 动词

outdriving:/ˌaʊtˈdraɪvɪŋ/
n.
胜过驾驶的行为；在驾驶方面超过的行为
The act of surpassing someone in driving skill or distance covered.
His outdriving of the competition was impressive.  他在驾驶方面超过对手令人印象深刻。
近义词: outpacing, surpassing
反义词: lagging, trailing
词性: 名词 (由动词转化而来)
