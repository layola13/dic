crudy:/ˈkruːdi/| adj.| 粗糙的，简陋的；未加工的；粗鲁的；不成熟的|Crude and rudimentary, lacking refinement or sophistication.  Also, unprocessed or raw; vulgar or offensive; immature or underdeveloped.|The furniture was crudy and poorly made.  这些家具做工粗糙，质量很差。|The crudy shelter barely protected them from the storm.  简陋的棚子几乎无法保护他们免受暴风雨的侵袭。|近义词:rough, primitive, raw, unrefined, coarse, vulgar, immature|反义词:refined, sophisticated, polished, processed, elegant, mature|词性:形容词

crudy: /ˈkruːdi/ | n. | 粗糙的东西; 粗鲁的人|Something that is crude or unrefined; a vulgar or unmannerly person.|The miner showed us some crudys he'd dug up. 矿工给我们看了一些他挖出来的粗糙的矿石。|Don't be such a crudy! 别这么粗鲁！|近义词:rough stuff, lout, boor|反义词:gentleman, lady, refined person|词性:名词
