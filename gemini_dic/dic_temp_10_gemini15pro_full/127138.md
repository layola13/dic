nats:/næts/ | n. |纳特（信息系统），北约空中预警管制系统| North Atlantic Treaty Organization's Airborne Warning and Control System (NATO AWACS), a modified Boeing E-3 Sentry aircraft.  These aircraft monitor airspace for air and maritime traffic and coordinate battlefield management. It can also refer more generally to the system itself or the personnel involved. | The nats reported increased enemy air activity.  纳特报告敌方空中活动增加。| Synonym: AWACS, airborne surveillance,  | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "ground surveillance") | noun
