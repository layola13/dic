ejectable:/ɪˈdʒɛktəbl/
adjective
可弹出的，可喷射的
Capable of being ejected or thrown out.
The pilot activated the ejectable seat. 飞行员启动了弹射座椅。
The plane is equipped with ejectable fuel tanks. 这架飞机配备了可抛弃式油箱。
Synonyms: removable, detachable, extractable
Antonyms: fixed, permanent, non-removable
词性: 形容词
