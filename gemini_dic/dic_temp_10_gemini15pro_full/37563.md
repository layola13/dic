commote:/kəˈmoʊt/
n.
行政区域，百户区
A subdivision of a Welsh kingdom or principality.
The commote was the basic unit of local administration in medieval Wales.  这个百户区是中世纪威尔士地方行政的基本单位。
The prince held court in each commote. 王子在每个百户区都设立了法庭。
近义词: district, territory, hundred (historical)
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly something like "whole kingdom/principality")
词性: noun

commote:/kəˈmoʊt/
v. (archaic, rare)
召集，聚集
To summon or call together, especially for military service.
The lord commoted his men for the upcoming battle.  领主召集他的部下准备即将到来的战斗。
The sheriff commoted the posse. 警长召集了民兵队。
近义词: summon, assemble, muster
反义词: disperse, dismiss
词性: verb
