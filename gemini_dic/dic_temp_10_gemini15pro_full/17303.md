bccing: /ˌbiː siːˈsɪŋ/ | v. | 密送；暗送 | The act of sending an email or letter to someone without letting the main recipient(s) know. | I'm bccing John on this email so he's aware of the situation. 我正在密送这封邮件给约翰，让他了解情况。 |  emailing secretly, copying blindly | disclosing openly | verb
