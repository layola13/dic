offer:/ˈɒfə(r)/ (British), /ˈɔːfər/ (American)| n. vt.|提议，提供；报价；出售；出现|An offer is a proposal to do something or to give something to someone.|

例句：
He made an offer to buy the house.
他出价买这栋房子。

They offered me a job.
他们给我提供了一份工作。


近义词：proposal, suggestion, bid, tender, present, proffer
反义词：refusal, decline, rejection
词性: noun, verb


offer:/ˈɒfə(r)/ (British), /ˈɔːfər/ (American)| vt.|提议，提供；报价；出售；出现|To offer is to present something for someone to accept or reject.|

例句：
I offered him a cup of coffee.
我给他提供了一杯咖啡。

She offered to help me with the project.
她主动提出要帮我做这个项目。


近义词：propose, suggest, bid, tender, present, proffer
反义词：refuse, decline, reject
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *offren, *offere, from Old French offrer, from Latin offerre (“to present, bestow, furnish, exhibit”), from ob (“towards”) + ferre (“to bear, carry”).
