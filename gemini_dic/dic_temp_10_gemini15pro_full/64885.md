essaying:/ɛˈseɪɪŋ/
v.
尝试；试验；试图(尤指文艺创作)
Trying to do something, especially in writing or other artistic forms.
She is essaying a new approach to painting. 她正在尝试一种新的绘画方法。
近义词：attempting, trying, endeavoring
反义词：abandoning, neglecting
词性：动词

词源：
From Old French *essaiier, from essai "trial, attempt," from Latin exagium "weighing, balance."
