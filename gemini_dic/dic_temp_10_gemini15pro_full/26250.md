butties:/ˈbʌtiz/ (UK) /ˈbʌdiz/ (US)| n.|三明治(尤指英式), 朋友, 伙伴|A sandwich, especially one made with two slices of buttered bread and a filling. Also used informally to refer to a friend or companion.|例句：I packed two cheese and pickle butties for lunch. 我打包了两个奶酪酸黄瓜三明治作为午餐。

例句：He went to the pub with his butties after work. 他下班后和他的朋友们去了酒吧。|近义词：sandwiches, friends, mates, pals|反义词:enemies, foes|Noun
