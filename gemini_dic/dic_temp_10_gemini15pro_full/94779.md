impudency:/ɪmˈpjuːdənsi/| n. |厚颜无耻；放肆；无礼|The state or quality of being impudent; impertinence or effrontery.  A bold or disrespectful act or utterance.|His impudency shocked the entire courtroom. 他的厚颜无耻震惊了整个法庭。|Synonyms: insolence, audacity, impertinence, boldness, brazenness, cheek, nerve, presumption, disrespect |Antonyms: respect, courtesy, humility, modesty, diffidence|noun
