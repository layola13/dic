firewall:/ˈfaɪərˌwɔːl/| vt.|设置防火墙|To protect a network with a firewall.|They firewalled their server to prevent hacking attempts. 他们为服务器设置了防火墙以防止黑客攻击。|synonyms: protect, secure, defend|antonyms: expose, open, unlock|verb
