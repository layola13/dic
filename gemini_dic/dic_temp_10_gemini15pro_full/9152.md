antinaturalistic:/ˌæntɪˌnætʃərəˈlɪstɪk/
adj.
反自然主义的
Opposing or rejecting naturalism, especially in art or literature.  Rejecting the view that everything arises from natural properties and causes, and supernatural or spiritual explanations are excluded.
His antinaturalistic paintings emphasized abstract forms and vibrant colors. 他反自然主义的绘画强调抽象的形式和鲜艳的色彩。
The author's antinaturalistic writing style incorporated elements of fantasy and mythology.  这位作者的反自然主义写作风格融入了奇幻和神话的元素。
近义词:artificial, contrived, nonrealistic, unrealistic
反义词:naturalistic, realistic
词性:adjective
