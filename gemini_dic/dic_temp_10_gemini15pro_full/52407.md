diddums:/ˈdɪdəmz/| n.| 小可怜，小宝贝 (child's word)|A term of endearment or sympathy for a child, often used when they are upset.  It suggests a slightly condescending or humorous attitude.|例句：Aww, diddums, did you fall down?  噢，小可怜，你摔倒了吗？

例句：Don't be such a diddums, it's just a little scratch.  别那么娇气，只是一个小划痕而已。|近义词：poor thing, sweetie, darling|反义词: (No direct antonym.  One might consider words implying strength or resilience, like "tough guy" but these are not true opposites.)|词性:Noun
