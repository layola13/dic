exemplar: /ɪɡˈzɛmplər/ (US), /ɪɡˈzɑːmplər/ (UK) | n. | 范例；模范；样本 | a person or thing serving as a typical example or appropriate model. |  This painting is an exemplar of the artist's early work. 这幅画是这位艺术家早期作品的范例。| synonyms: example, model, paradigm, archetype | antonyms: anomaly, exception | noun

exemplar: /ɪɡˈzɛmplər/ (US), /ɪɡˈzɑːmplər/ (UK) | adj. |  (古语) 值得效仿的；可作楷模的 | serving as a desirable model; exemplary. | His behavior was exemplar, a model for all to follow. 他的行为堪称楷模，值得所有人效仿。  | synonyms: exemplary, model, ideal | antonyms: flawed, imperfect | adjective
