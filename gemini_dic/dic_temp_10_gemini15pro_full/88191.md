himalayan:/ˌhɪməˈleɪən/ | adj. n. | 喜马拉雅的；喜马拉雅山脉的；(H-)喜马拉雅山人；喜马拉雅猫 | Of or relating to the Himalayas.  A native or inhabitant of the Himalayas.
