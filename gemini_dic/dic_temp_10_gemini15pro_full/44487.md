crosse:/krɒs/ (US) /krɒs/ (UK) | n. | 球棍；曲棍球棒 | A stick with a netted pouch at one end, used for catching and throwing a ball in lacrosse. | He expertly scooped the ball with his crosse. 他熟练地用球棍舀起了球。 | The crosse is the most important piece of equipment in lacrosse. 球棍是曲棍球运动中最重要的一件装备。 | synonyms: lacrosse stick | antonyms: N/A | noun
