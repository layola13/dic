bile:/baɪl/| n.|胆汁；愤怒，怨恨|A bitter greenish-brown alkaline fluid that aids digestion and is secreted by the liver and stored in the gallbladder.|The patient was vomiting bile. 病人正在吐胆汁。|Synonyms: venom, bitterness, rancor|Antonyms: amity, benevolence|noun

bile:/baɪl/| n.|坏脾气；愤怒|Intense anger or bitterness.|He vented his bile on the unfortunate waiter. 他把怒气发泄在了那个不幸的服务员身上。|Synonyms: anger, fury, wrath|Antonyms: calmness, peacefulness|noun
