expires:/ɪkˈspaɪərz/ /ɛkˈspaɪərz/
verb
期满，失效，终止
comes to an end; ceases to be valid
The contract expires next month.  合同下个月到期。
My passport expires in two years. 我的护照两年后到期。
synonyms: terminates, runs out, lapses, ceases
antonyms: renews, extends, continues
词性: 动词
