bedrid:/ˈbedrɪd/| adj.|卧床不起的，长期卧床的|Confined to bed by illness or infirmity; unable to get out of bed.|例句：He was bedrid for months after the accident. 事故发生后，他卧床不起好几个月。

例句：Her grandmother has been bedrid for years. 她祖母卧床不起已经好几年了。|近义词：bedridden, bedfast|反义词: healthy, mobile|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Old English *bedrida, from bed + rida "rider," literally "bed rider."  The original sense was "one who rides a bed," hence "one confined to bed."
