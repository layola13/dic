huffs:/hʌfs/ | v. | 愤怒地喘气，喷出气息；吹嘘 | blows air out forcefully, often as an expression of annoyance or frustration; brags | She huffed and puffed as she climbed the stairs. 她爬楼梯时气喘吁吁。|近义词：pants, puffs, blows, brags, boasts | 反义词：inhales, whispers | 词性：动词
