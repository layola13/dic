connectional:/kəˈnɛkʃənl/
adjective
连接的；联络的；关联的
Relating to or involving connection or connections.  Characterized by interconnections.
例句：The brain's connectional architecture is incredibly complex.  大脑的连接结构极其复杂。

例句：The researchers studied the connectional pathways between different brain regions. 研究人员研究了不同大脑区域之间的连接通路。

近义词：connected, related, linked, associated, affiliated
反义词：disconnected, unrelated, separate, isolated
词性: adjective
