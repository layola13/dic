comper:/kəmˈpɛər/ | n. |主持人，司仪| A person who introduces the acts in a variety show or other entertainment. |例句：The comper kept the audience entertained between acts.  司仪在节目表演的间隙让观众保持愉悦。

例句：The comper announced the next singer with great enthusiasm.  司仪满怀热情地宣布了下一位歌手。|近义词：host, emcee, master of ceremonies |反义词: audience, guest |词性: noun
