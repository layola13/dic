nonmoderates:/nɒnˈmɒdərɪts/ | n. | 非温和派；非稳健派 |  People who hold extreme views, either to the left or right of the political spectrum, and who reject moderate positions.  |  The nonmoderates in both parties were unwilling to compromise.  两党中的非温和派都不愿妥协。 | synonyms: extremists, radicals, zealots | antonyms: moderates, centrists | noun

nonmoderate:/nɒnˈmɒdərɪt/ | adj. | 非温和的；非稳健的 | Not moderate; extreme or radical.  | The nonmoderate stance of the new party leader alienated many voters. 新党魁的非温和立场疏远了许多选民。 | synonyms: extreme, radical, immoderate, extremist | antonyms: moderate, centrist, temperate | adjective
