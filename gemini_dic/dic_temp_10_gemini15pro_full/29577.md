catchy:/ˈkætʃi/| adj.|朗朗上口的；引人注目的；容易记住的|Pleasing and easily remembered, especially of a tune or slogan.|That new song has a very catchy tune. 那首新歌的旋律很朗朗上口。|近义词：memorable, appealing, infectious|反义词：forgettable, dull, unmemorable|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From catch + -y.  Originally referring to something that is easily caught, like a disease. The meaning evolved to describe things that easily "catch" the attention or memory.
