blurbist:/ˈblɜːrbɪst/| n.|推荐者；吹捧者；(尤指)写广告短文的人|A blurbist is someone who writes blurbs, short promotional pieces of writing, often found on the back of books.|The blurbist praised the novel as a masterpiece of contemporary literature.  推荐者将这部小说誉为当代文学的杰作。|近义词：publicist, promoter, advertiser|反义词: critic, detractor|noun
