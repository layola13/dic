nassa:/ˈnæsə/| n.|鱼梁，一种捕鱼的陷阱|A nassa is a type of fish trap, typically a basket or wickerwork construction with a funnel-shaped entrance.|Fishermen used nassas to catch small fish in the river. 渔民们用鱼梁在河里捕小鱼。|近义词：fish trap, weir, pound|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "open water")|noun
