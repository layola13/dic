asylee: /əˌsaɪˈliː/ /əˌsaɪˈli/
n.
寻求政治庇护者，难民
A person who is seeking or has been granted political asylum.
The asylee fled his country due to political persecution.  这名寻求政治庇护者因政治迫害逃离了他的国家。
The government granted asylum to the asylee. 政府给予了这名寻求政治庇护者庇护。
近义词: refugee, exile
反义词: citizen, national
词性: 名词
