cowquake:/ˈkaʊ.kweɪk/| n.|牛震；牛群集体移动造成的震动|A cowquake is a seismic event, usually minor, caused by the movement of a large herd of cattle.|The seismograph registered a small cowquake when the herd stampeded.|近义词: tremor, microquake|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, potentially "stillness," "quiet")|词性:noun

Etymology:  A blend of "cow" and "earthquake."  It's a humorous and informal term.
