debaser:/dɪˈbeɪsər/
n.|贬低者，降低者|Someone who debases something, especially a currency. |The king punished the debaser of the royal currency. 国王惩罚了降低皇家货币价值的人。|synonyms: degrader, corrupter, adulterator|antonyms: elevator, enhancer, upgrader|noun

debase:/dɪˈbeɪs/
vt.|贬低，降低，贬损|To reduce the quality or value of something.|The politician was accused of debasing the office he held.  这位政治家被指控降低了他所担任的职位的价值。|synonyms: degrade, cheapen, demean|antonyms: elevate, enhance, ennoble|verb
