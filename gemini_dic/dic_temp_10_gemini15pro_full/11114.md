archdeaconess:/ˌɑːrtʃˈdiːkənɪs/ | n. |女执事长；副主教夫人|A female archdeacon, a senior administrative official in certain Christian churches.  A woman who assists a bishop, especially in Episcopal churches, typically having responsibility for pastoral care and church administration within an archdeaconry. |The archdeaconess visited the parish on Sunday. 女执事长星期天访问了教区。|The new archdeaconess is known for her compassionate outreach programs. 新任副主教夫人以其富有同情心的外联项目而闻名。|Synonyms: church leader, administrator, cleric |Antonyms: layman, parishioner |noun
