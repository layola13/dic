chesterlite: /ˈtʃɛstərˌlaɪt/
n.
方沸石，钙霞石
A variety of microcline feldspar, sometimes used as a gemstone. It is usually white, grayish, or reddish in color.
Chesterlite is a rare mineral found in Chester County, Pennsylvania.  方沸石是一种稀有矿物，发现于宾夕法尼亚州的切斯特县。
Chesterlite can exhibit a play of colors. 方沸石可以展现色彩的变幻。
近义词: microcline, feldspar
