brownwort:/ˈbraʊnwɜːrt/| n. |藿香，藿香属植物|A plant of the genus *Prunella*, especially *Prunella vulgaris* (self-heal), with purple flowers. It has been used in traditional medicine.|The brownwort grew wild along the edges of the forest.|近义词：self-heal, heal-all|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
Etymology:  From Middle English *brounwort*, from Old English *brūnwyrt*, from *brūn* (“brown”) + *wyrt* (“wort,” “plant,” “herb”). The name refers to the brown color of the dried leaves and flowers.
