consolers:/kənˈsoʊlərz/
|n.|安慰者，慰问者|People who offer comfort or solace to someone who is grieving or distressed.  Those who console others in times of sadness or disappointment.|Her friends and family were her consolers during her difficult time.  她的朋友和家人在她困难时期是她的安慰者。|comforters, supporters, sympathizers|critics, detractors|noun
