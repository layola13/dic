down-and-outer:/ˌdaʊn ən ˈaʊtər/| n.|穷困潦倒的人；失意的人|A person who is down on their luck, typically unemployed and without prospects.|He was a down-and-outer, sleeping on park benches and begging for change. 他是一个穷困潦倒的人，睡在公园长椅上，乞讨零钱。|近义词：bum, derelict, vagrant|反义词: successful person, fortunate person|Noun

down-and-out:/ˌdaʊn ən ˈaʊt/| adj.|穷困潦倒的；失意的|Destitute and having no prospects.|She felt down-and-out after losing her job and her apartment.  她失业又没了公寓，感到穷困潦倒。|近义词：destitute, impoverished, penniless|反义词: prosperous, affluent|Adjective
