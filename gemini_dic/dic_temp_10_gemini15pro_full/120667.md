misadaptation:/ˌmɪsædæpˈteɪʃən/| n. | 适应不良，不适应|The polar bear's thick fur, a useful adaptation in the Arctic, becomes a misadaptation in a warmer climate. 北极熊厚厚的皮毛在北极是一种有用的适应性特征，但在气候变暖的情况下就变成了适应不良。| The company's misadaptation to the changing market led to its downfall.  公司对不断变化的市场的适应不良导致了它的垮台。| 近义词: maladaptation, inadaptability | 反义词: adaptation, adaptability | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From mis- (expressing wrongness or error) + adaptation.  "Mis-" comes from Old French "mes-" from Latin "minus" meaning "less". "Adaptation" comes from Late Latin "adaptātiōn-" from "adaptāre"  meaning "to adjust".
