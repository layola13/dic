noncommunity:/ˌnɑːnkəˈmjuːnɪti/
adjective
非社区的；非共同体的
Not of or relating to a community; not shared by all members of a community.
The hospital serves a large noncommunity population. 这家医院服务于大量的非社区人口。
The noncommunity water supply is contaminated. 非社区供水受到污染。
