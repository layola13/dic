overabsorptions:/ˌoʊvərəbˈzɔːrpʃənz/ |n.| 过度吸收; 超额吸收| The situation where costs allocated are greater than actual costs incurred.  This typically happens in cost accounting when using predetermined overhead rates. |  The overabsorptions of factory overhead led to an understatement of the product cost. 工厂间接费用的过度吸收导致产品成本低估。| synonyms: over-recovery, excess absorption | antonyms: underabsorptions, under-recovery | noun
