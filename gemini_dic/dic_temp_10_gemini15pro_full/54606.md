dismayed:/dɪsˈmeɪd/ | adj. | 沮丧的，惊慌的，灰心的 | Filled with consternation and distress; discouraged. |  She was dismayed by the news of her friend's illness. 她对朋友生病的消息感到沮丧。

She was dismayed to discover that her wallet was missing.  她沮丧地发现她的钱包不见了。

近义词: disheartened, discouraged, appalled, alarmed, distressed, upset | 反义词: encouraged, heartened, pleased, delighted, cheerful | 词性: 形容词
