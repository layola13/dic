carboyed:/ˈkɑːrˌbɔɪd/
verb
用大玻璃瓶装
transferred or stored in a carboy (a large glass bottle).
The wine was carboyed for aging.  这酒被装入大玻璃瓶陈酿。
The chemicals were carefully carboyed for transport. 这些化学品被小心地装入大玻璃瓶以便运输。
近义词: bottled, decanted
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "poured out", "dispensed")
词性: verb (past tense and past participle)
