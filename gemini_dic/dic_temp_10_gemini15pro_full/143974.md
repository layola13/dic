outrageously:/aʊtˈreɪdʒəsli/
adverb
令人愤慨地，骇人听闻地
in a way that is shocking and unacceptable
She was outrageously dressed for a funeral. 她穿得如此不得体，竟然去参加葬礼。
Synonyms: shockingly, scandalously, unbelievably, excessively, exorbitantly
Antonyms: reasonably, moderately, appropriately, acceptably, decently
词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From outrage + -ous + -ly.  Outrage comes from Old French outrage, from ultra "beyond" (from Latin ultra "beyond") + rage "rage."
