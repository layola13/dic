overgesticulates: /ˌoʊvərˈdʒɛstɪkjʊleɪts/
verb
做过多手势；过分打手势
Uses excessive hand gestures, often to the point of distraction.
He overgesticulates when he's nervous, making it hard to focus on what he's saying. 他紧张的时候会做过多手势，让人很难集中注意力听他说话。
She overgesticulated throughout her presentation, waving her arms wildly.  她在整个演讲过程中都过分地打手势，疯狂地挥舞着手臂。
Synonyms:  emotes excessively, flails, gesticulates wildly
Antonyms:  remains still, gestures subtly, under-emotes
词性: 动词
