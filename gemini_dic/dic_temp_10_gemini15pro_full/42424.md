countercultural:/ˌkaʊntərˈkʌltʃərəl/| adj.|反文化的|Relating to or expressing a culture with values and beliefs differing from or opposing those accepted by most of society.|The hippie movement of the 1960s was a countercultural phenomenon.  20世纪60年代的嬉皮士运动是一种反文化现象。|Synonyms:  rebellious, nonconformist, alternative, underground|Antonyms:  mainstream, conventional, orthodox|adjective

counterculture:/ˈkaʊntərˌkʌltʃər/| n.|反文化|A way of life and set of attitudes opposed to or rejecting the prevailing social norms and values.|The counterculture of the 1960s challenged traditional authority.  20世纪60年代的反文化挑战了传统权威。|Synonyms:  underground, subculture|Antonyms:  mainstream culture, establishment|noun
