enfolden:/ɪnˈfoʊldən/ /ɛnˈfoʊldən/
verb
包裹，拥抱，覆盖
To wrap up; to envelop or embrace.
The soft blanket enfoldened the sleeping child.  柔软的毯子裹住了熟睡的孩子。
The mountains were enfoldened in mist. 群山笼罩在薄雾之中。
Synonyms: envelop, embrace, shroud, cloak
Antonyms: uncover, expose, reveal
词性:动词
