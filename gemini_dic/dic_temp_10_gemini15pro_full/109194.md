legroom:/ˈleɡruːm/ /ˈleɡˌruːm/| n.|腿部空间|The amount of space available for your legs, especially in a car or airplane.  The space in front of your seat in which you can stretch your legs.|例句：There wasn't much legroom in the back of the car. 车后座的腿部空间不大。

例句：I booked an exit row seat for extra legroom on the flight. 我预订了一个靠近紧急出口的座位，以便在飞机上获得额外的腿部空间。|近义词：space, room|反义词:crampedness|词性:名词
