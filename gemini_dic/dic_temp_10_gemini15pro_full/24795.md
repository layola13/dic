brotherhood:/ˈbrʌðərhʊd/| n.|兄弟情谊；手足之情；同业，同僚；博爱，四海之内皆兄弟|The close brotherhood of the team was evident in their performance. 球队的兄弟情谊在他们的比赛中显而易见。|近义词：fraternity, fellowship, camaraderie, kinship|反义词:animosity, hostility|noun

Etymology->
From Old English brōðorhād, from brōðor (“brother”) +‎ -hād (“-hood”). Cognate with Old Saxon brōðarhēd, Old Frisian brōderskap (“brotherhood”), Dutch broederschap (“brotherhood, fraternity”), German Bruderschaft (“brotherhood, fraternity”), Old Norse brœðralag (“brotherhood”).
