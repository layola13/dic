forgiven:/fərˈɡɪvən/
adjective|被原谅的，被宽恕的|Having been pardoned or absolved of an offense or debt.|例句：He was forgiven for his mistakes. 他因犯错而被原谅。|近义词：pardoned, excused, absolved|反义词：unforgiven, condemned|词性:形容词

forgive:/fərˈɡɪv/
verb|原谅，宽恕|To grant pardon for or remission of (an offense, debt, etc.); excuse.|例句：I forgive you for lying to me. 我原谅你对我说谎。|近义词：pardon, excuse, absolve|反义词：condemn, resent|词性:动词
