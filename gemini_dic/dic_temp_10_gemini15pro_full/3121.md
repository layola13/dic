aery:/ˈeəri/ (US), /ˈɛəri/ (UK)| adj. n.|空中的，高耸的；鹰巢|High in the air; resembling an eagle's nest; ethereal or unearthly. |The castle stood on an aery cliff overlooking the sea. 城堡矗立在俯瞰大海的高耸悬崖上。|The music had an aery, otherworldly quality. 这音乐有一种空灵的、超凡的品质。|近义词：lofty, airy, elevated, ethereal|反义词：grounded, low, earthly|词性: adjective, noun

aery:/ˈeəri/ (US), /ˈɛəri/ (UK)| n.|鹰巢|A nest of a bird of prey, especially an eagle, typically built high in a tree or on a cliff.|The climbers spotted an aery high on the cliff face. 登山者在悬崖峭壁上发现了一个鹰巢。|The eagle returned to its aery with a fish in its talons.  老鹰带着一条鱼爪回到了它的巢穴。|近义词：nest, eyrie|反义词：None|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *eyrie*, from Old French *aire*, from Late Latin *agrāria* "field, landed property," from Latin *ager* "field." The sense shifted from "field" to "nest in a field" to "bird's nest," especially of an eagle.
