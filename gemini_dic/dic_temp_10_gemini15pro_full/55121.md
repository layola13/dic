disruptively:/dɪsˈrʌptɪvli/| adv.|扰乱地，破坏性地|In a way that causes disruption or interruption.  It describes actions or events that significantly alter the normal course or function of something.|The new technology disruptively changed the industry.  这项新技术颠覆性地改变了这个行业。|Synonyms: disturbingly, destructively, radically, revolutionarily|Antonyms: constructively, helpfully, beneficially|Derivation: From disrupt (verb) + -ive (suffix forming adjectives) + -ly (suffix forming adverbs)|Word Type: Adverb
