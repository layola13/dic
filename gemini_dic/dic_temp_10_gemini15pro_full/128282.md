neuroprosthetics:/ˌnʊəroʊprɒsˈθɛtɪks/ | n. | 神经修复术; 神经义肢; | Artificial devices that replace lost neural function.  It involves connecting the nervous system to an external device, such as a prosthetic limb, to restore lost function or enhance existing capabilities. |  Researchers are making significant advances in the field of neuroprosthetics. 研究人员在神经修复领域取得了重大进展。| synonyms: neural prostheses,  brain-computer interfaces,  BCIs | antonyms:  natural limbs, organic functions | noun
