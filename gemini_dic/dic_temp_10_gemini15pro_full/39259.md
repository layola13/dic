congregative:/ˈkɒŋɡrɪɡətɪv/ /ˈkɑːŋɡrɪɡeɪtɪv/| adj.|聚集的，群居的|Tending to congregate; assembling.|例句：Congregative animals, such as ants, work together in colonies.  群居动物，例如蚂蚁，在群体中一起工作。

例句：The congregative nature of bees allows them to create complex hives. 蜜蜂的群居特性使它们能够建造复杂的蜂巢。|近义词：gregarious, social, collective|反义词：solitary, individualistic|词性: adjective

congregate: /ˈkɒŋɡrɪɡeɪt/ /ˈkɑːŋɡrɪɡeɪt/| v.|聚集，集合|Come together; assemble.|例句：People congregated in the town square. 人们聚集在城镇广场上。

例句：Birds congregate in large flocks during migration. 鸟类在迁徙期间聚集在一起形成大型的鸟群。|近义词：assemble, gather, cluster|反义词：disperse, scatter|词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *congregativus, from Latin congregatus (past participle of congregare "to flock together"), from com- "together" + greg- (stem of grex "flock").
