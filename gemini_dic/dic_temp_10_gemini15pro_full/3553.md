agama:/əˈɡɑːmə/ | n. | (蜥蜴类)飞蜥; 岩蜥;  |A lizard of the family Agamidae, found in warm regions of the Old World. | The agama basked in the sun on a rock. 岩蜥在一块岩石上晒太阳。|Synonym: lizard | Antonym: None | Noun


agama:/əˈɡɑːmə/ | n. | (印度尼西亚、马来西亚)龙目岛人 |A person from the island of Lombok, Indonesia. | The agama fisherman returned with a full net. 龙目岛渔夫带着满满一网鱼回来了。| Synonym: Lombok Islander | Antonym: None | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From New Latin Agamidae, from genus name Agama, possibly from Greek ἀγαμός (agamos, “unmarried”), perhaps referring to the lack of femoral pores in some species.
