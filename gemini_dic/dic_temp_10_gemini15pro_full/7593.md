announces:/əˈnaʊnsɪz/
verb
宣布；通告
make a public and typically formal declaration about a fact, occurrence, or intention.
The company announced a new product launch.  该公司宣布推出新产品。
The mayor announced his resignation. 市长宣布辞职。
Synonyms: proclaim, declare, publicize, broadcast
Antonyms: conceal, hide, suppress
词性:动词
