outsparkled:/ˌaʊtˈspɑːrkəld/
verb
胜过，光芒盖过
outdid in brilliance or showiness.  Surpassed in sparkle or glitter.
Her dress outsparkled all the others at the party. 她的礼服在派对上比其他所有人的都闪亮。
outshone, eclipsed, surpassed
dimmed, overshadowed
