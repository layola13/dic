dotehead: /ˈdɔːtˌhɛd/
noun
老糊涂；傻子；痴呆的人
A person who is considered to be senile or foolish, especially an old person.
He's getting to be a bit of a dotehead these days, forgetting where he put his glasses and all that. 他这些天变得有点糊涂了，总是忘记把眼镜放在哪里之类的。
synonyms: dotard, codger, old fool, simpleton
antonyms: genius, mastermind
n.
