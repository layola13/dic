backstory:/ˈbækˌstɔːri/ |n.| 背景故事，幕后故事，隐情|The backstory of the character is quite complex.  这个角色的背景故事相当复杂.|Example: The movie provided a compelling backstory for the villain, explaining his motivations. 这部电影为反派提供了令人信服的背景故事，解释了他的动机。

Example:  Knowing the backstory of the company helps understand its current challenges. 了解公司的背景故事有助于理解其当前的挑战。|Synonyms: background, history, origin, context|Antonyms: foreground|Noun
