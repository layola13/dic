hylarchical:/ˌhaɪˈlɑːrkɪkəl/ | adj. | 分层的，等级制的 | Arranged in a hierarchy or according to levels of subordination. | The hylarchical structure of the organization allows for efficient communication.  这个组织的等级结构可以有效沟通。| hierarchical, stratified, layered | non-hierarchical, egalitarian | adjective

hylomorphic:/ˌhaɪləˈmɔːrfɪk/ | adj. | 质料与形式的 | Relating to or characterized by hylomorphism (the doctrine that physical objects consist of matter and form). | Aristotle's hylomorphic theory explains change in terms of matter and form.  亚里士多德的质料形式论用质料和形式来解释变化。 | - | - | adjective
