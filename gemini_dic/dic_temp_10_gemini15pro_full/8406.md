anti-Spanish:/ˌæntiˈspænɪʃ/
adjective
反西班牙的；仇视西班牙的

He held strong anti-Spanish sentiments due to historical conflicts.  他由于历史冲突而持有强烈的反西班牙情绪。
The anti-Spanish propaganda fueled the war. 反西班牙的宣传加剧了战争。
近义词: anti-Hispanic
反义词: pro-Spanish
词性: adjective
