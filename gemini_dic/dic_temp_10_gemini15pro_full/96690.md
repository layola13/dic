inebrious:/ɪˈniːbriəs/| adj.|醉的；使人陶醉的|Characterized by or producing intoxication; drunk.|例句：The inebrious partygoers danced until dawn. 醉醺醺的参加派对的人跳舞到天亮。|例句：The inebrious fumes of the wine filled the cellar.  酒窖里充满了令人陶醉的酒香。|近义词：drunk, intoxicated, tipsy|反义词：sober|词性:形容词
Etymology: Late 16th century: from Latin inebriatus ‘made drunk’, from the verb inebriare, from in- ‘into’ + ebrius ‘drunk’.
