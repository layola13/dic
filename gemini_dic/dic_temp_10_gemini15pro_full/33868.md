circumscription:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈskrɪpʃən/ | n. | 界限，限制；定义；外接(几何) | The act of drawing a line around; limiting; restricting within limits; the line or boundary that encloses a figure or area. | The circumscription of the president's power is defined by the constitution. 总统的权力受宪法限制。| synonyms: limitation, restriction, confinement, definition, boundary, perimeter | antonyms: expansion, extension, liberation, freedom | noun

circumscribe:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈskraɪb/ | vt. | 限制，限定；画…的外接圆；包围 | To draw a line around; to encircle; to confine within limits; to define or mark off carefully. |  His role in the project was carefully circumscribed. 他在这个项目中的角色被仔细地限定了。 | synonyms: restrict, limit, confine, define, encircle, enclose | antonyms: expand, extend, liberate, free | verb
