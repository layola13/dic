nonaluminum:/ˌnɒn əˈlʊmɪnəm/ | adj. | 非铝的；非铝制的 | Not made of aluminum; other than aluminum. |  Example:  They opted for nonaluminum siding for their house. 他们选择用非铝质的壁板来装饰他们的房子。

Example: Nonaluminum cookware is often preferred by chefs.  厨师通常更喜欢非铝制的炊具。| Near synonyms: non-aluminium, aluminum-free | Antonyms: aluminum, aluminium | adjective
