overspeculates: /ˌoʊvərˈspɛkjəˌleɪts/
verb
过度推测，过分臆测
To engage in speculation to an excessive or unwarranted degree; to speculate more than is reasonable or wise.
He overspeculates on the future of the stock market. 他过度推测股市的未来。
overspeculate, conjecture excessively, over-conjecture
underspeculate
