acidities:/əˈsɪdətiz/| n.|酸度；酸性|The acidity of the soil is too high for most plants. 土壤的酸度对大多数植物来说太高了。|The wine's acidity gives it a crisp, refreshing taste.  这种酒的酸度赋予它清爽宜人的味道。|近义词：sourness, tartness|反义词：alkalinity, basicity|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *acidité*, from Late Latin *aciditas*, from Latin *acidus* (sour).

USAGE->
The acidities of the different fruits varied. = 不同水果的酸度各不相同。
The chemist measured the acidities of the solutions. = 化学家测量了溶液的酸度。

PHRASE->
gastric acidities = 胃酸度
soil acidities = 土壤酸度
the acidities of the beverages = 饮料的酸度
the acidities of the wines = 葡萄酒的酸度
measuring acidities = 测量酸度
