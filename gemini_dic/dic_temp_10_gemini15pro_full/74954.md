friar:/ˈfraɪər/ (US), /fraɪə(r)/ (UK)| n. |修士，修道士|A member of a religious order, especially the mendicant orders (e.g., Franciscans, Dominicans, Carmelites, Augustinians).| Friar Tuck was a character in the Robin Hood stories.  塔克修士是罗宾汉故事中的一个人物。|
|Synonyms: monk, brother, religious|Antonyms: layman, secular|noun|

friar:/ˈfraɪər/ (US), /fraɪə(r)/ (UK)| n. |托钵僧|A mendicant friar relies on charitable donations. 托钵僧依靠慈善捐赠为生。|
|Synonyms: mendicant, beggar|Antonyms: rich man, nobleman|noun|
