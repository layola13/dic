heavenwardly:/ˈhɛvənwərdli/| adv.|向天堂地，向上地|Toward heaven; in a direction leading to heaven.  It describes movement or orientation in the direction of heaven, both literally and figuratively (spiritually or aspirationally).|Her gaze drifted heavenwardly as she contemplated the vastness of the universe. 她凝视着浩瀚的宇宙，目光飘向天空。

The smoke from the incense rose heavenwardly, filling the temple with a sweet fragrance. 香的烟雾袅袅升起，使寺庙充满了甜美的香味。|近义词：skyward, upward, heavenwards|反义词：earthward, downward|词性: 副词
