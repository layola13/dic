hance:/hɑːns/ /hæns/ | v. |  增强，提高，增进 | To enhance something is to increase or improve its value, quality, or attractiveness. |  The soft lighting enhanced her beauty. 柔和的灯光更衬托出她的美丽。

The new features enhance the functionality of the software. 新功能增强了软件的功能。 | 近义词: improve, augment, boost, strengthen, heighten | 反义词: diminish, decrease, reduce, weaken, detract | 词性: 动词
