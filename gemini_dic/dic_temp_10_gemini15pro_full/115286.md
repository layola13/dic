manlier:/ˈmænliər/| adj.|更男子气概的，更有男子汉气概的|Comparative form of manly: more manly.  Having or showing qualities traditionally associated with men, such as strength, courage, and independence, to a greater degree. |He looked manlier with a beard. 他留了胡子看起来更有男人味了。|Synonyms:  more masculine, stronger, tougher, brawnier,  sturdier|Antonyms:  less manly, effeminate, weaker,  frailer, more feminine|adjective
