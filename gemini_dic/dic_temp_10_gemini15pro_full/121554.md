mismeshed:/ˌmɪsˈmɛʃt/
adjective
啮合不良的，不协调的
not meshing or fitting together properly; out of harmony or synchronization.
The gears were mismeshed, causing a grinding noise. 齿轮啮合不良，发出刺耳的噪音。
mismatched, incongruous, discordant
harmonious, compatible
词性:形容词
