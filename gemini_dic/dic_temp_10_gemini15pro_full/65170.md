ethnographically:/ˌeθnəˈɡræfɪkli/
adverb
人种志地，民族志地
in a way that relates to the scientific description of peoples and cultures with their customs, habits, and mutual differences
The community was studied ethnographically to understand their traditions.  这个社区被人种志地研究以了解他们的传统。
The researcher ethnographically documented the rituals of the tribe. 研究人员用人种志方法记录了该部落的仪式。
Synonym: anthropologically, culturally
Antonym:  (No direct antonym exists.  One could consider terms like "statistically" or "quantitatively" as contrasting approaches, but not true opposites.)
词性: 副词
