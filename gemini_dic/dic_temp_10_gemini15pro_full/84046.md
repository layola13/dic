half-fictitious:/ˌhæf fɪkˈtɪʃəs/
adjective
半虚构的
Partly real and partly imagined; based partly on fact and partly on fiction.
His account of the journey was half-fictitious, embellished with tales he'd heard from others. 他对这次旅行的描述是半虚构的，点缀着他从别人那里听来的故事。
semi-fictional, quasi-fictional
factual, real, true
adjective
