noncultivation:/ˌnɒnkʌltɪˈveɪʃn/ | n. | 非耕作；未开垦 | The noncultivation of the land led to its becoming overgrown with weeds.  这块土地没有耕作，导致杂草丛生。 |  The area has been designated for noncultivation to allow native plants to regenerate.  该区域已被指定为非耕作区，以允许本地植物再生。 | 近义词: abandonment, neglect, fallow | 反义词: cultivation, farming | 词性: 名词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + cultivation (from cultivate, from Latin cultivare "to till, cultivate," from cultus "care, cultivation," past participle of colere "to till, cultivate, inhabit, protect, honor")
