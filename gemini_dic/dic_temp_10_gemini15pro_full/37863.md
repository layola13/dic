compilations:/ˌkɒmpɪˈleɪʃənz/ /ˌkɑːmpɪˈleɪʃənz/| n. | 合集；汇编；编辑物 |A collection of things, especially pieces of writing, music, etc., that have been gathered together and published in a single work. | This album is a compilation of their greatest hits. 这张专辑是他们的精选集。| The book is a compilation of essays by various authors. 这本书是由不同作者的文章汇编而成的。|  synonyms: collection, anthology, album, digest, compendium | antonyms: separation, division | noun
