descriers:/dɪˈskraɪərz/
|n.|描述者，识别者|People who describe or identify something or someone.  Those who can discern and articulate the qualities of a subject.|The descriers of the ancient artifact noted its intricate carvings and symbolic markings.  古代文物的描述者注意到其复杂的雕刻和象征性标记。|observers, narrators, commentators, analysts|concealers, obfuscators|noun
describe:/dɪˈskraɪb/
|v.|描述，形容|To give a detailed account of something.|Can you describe the suspect to the police? 你能向警方描述嫌疑犯吗？|portray, depict, characterize, explain|obscure, conceal|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *descrien*, from Old French *descrivre*, from Latin *describere* ("to write down, copy, sketch, describe"), from *de-* ("down") + *scribere* ("to write").
