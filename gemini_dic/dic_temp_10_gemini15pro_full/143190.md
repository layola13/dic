outcompetes:/ˌaʊtkəmˈpiːts/
verb
胜过，比…更具有竞争力
to compete more effectively than (someone or something)
例句：The larger company outcompetes the smaller startups in the market.  这家大公司在市场上胜过那些较小的初创公司。

例句：In the struggle for survival, the fittest individuals outcompete the weaker ones. 在生存竞争中，最适者胜过较弱者。

近义词：outdo, surpass, beat, defeat, triumph over, prevail over
反义词：lose to, be defeated by, succumb to
词性：v.
