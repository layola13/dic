manipulativeness:/məˌnɪpjʊˈleɪtɪvnəs/| n. |操控性，操纵性|The quality of being manipulative; characterized by unscrupulous control of others.|Her manipulativeness made it difficult to trust her. 她的操控性让人很难信任她。|例句：His manipulativeness was evident in the way he controlled the conversation. 他的操控性在他控制谈话的方式中显而易见。

例句：The employee's manipulativeness ultimately led to his dismissal.  这个员工的操控性最终导致他被解雇。


|近义词：cunning, scheming, deviousness, craftiness|反义词:honesty, sincerity, forthrightness|词性:noun
