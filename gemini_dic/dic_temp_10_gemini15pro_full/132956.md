nonemotiveness:/ˌnɒnɪˈməʊtɪvnəs/
n.|无感情; 冷漠; 平静|The state of not showing or experiencing emotion.  Lack of emotional responsiveness.|His nonemotiveness made him seem distant and uncaring. 他缺乏感情使他看起来冷漠疏离。|Synonyms: apathy, indifference, impassivity, detachment|Antonyms: emotionality, expressiveness, passion, fervor|noun
