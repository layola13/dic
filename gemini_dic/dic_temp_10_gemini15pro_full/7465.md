anisotropous:/ˌænɪˈsɒtrəpəs/ | adj. | 各向异性的 | Exhibiting properties with different values when measured along axes in different directions.  Having different physical properties in different directions. | Wood is an anisotropous material, its strength is greater along the grain than across it. 木材是一种各向异性材料，其沿纹理方向的强度比垂直于纹理方向的强度更大。 | The conductivity of this crystal is anisotropous.  这种晶体的导电性是各向异性的。 | anisotropic | isotropic | adjective
