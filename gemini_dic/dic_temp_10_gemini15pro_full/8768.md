antidotally:/ˌænɪkˈdəʊtəli/ | adv. | 传闻地；轶事地 | Based on personal accounts or stories rather than on systematic scientific study. |  Example: Antidotally, garlic is said to ward off colds.  传闻说，大蒜可以预防感冒。 | Example: The drug's effectiveness has been reported antidotally, but more research is needed.  这种药物的有效性是传闻报道的，但还需要更多的研究。 | 近义词: anecdotally, informally, unofficially | 反义词: scientifically, empirically, formally | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From anecdote + -al + -ly.  Anecdote comes from the Greek anekdota, meaning "things unpublished."
