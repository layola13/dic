deallocation's:/ˌdiːæləˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 释放，解除分配 | The deallocation's of memory is crucial for preventing memory leaks.  内存的释放对于防止内存泄漏至关重要。| 近义词: release, freeing | 反义词: allocation | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From de- (prefix meaning "removal, reversal") + allocation (from allocate, from Latin allocare "to assign, allot," from ad- "to" + locare "to place," from locus "place").  The -'s is the possessive marker.
