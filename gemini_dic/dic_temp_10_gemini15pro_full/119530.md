microscopic:/ˌmaɪkrəˈskɒpɪk/ (US) /ˌmaɪkrəˈskɑːpɪk/| adj.|微观的；极小的；只能用显微镜才能看到的|relating to or involving a microscope; extremely small; visible only by means of a microscope|The bacteria were microscopic and could not be seen with the naked eye. 细菌非常微小，肉眼无法看到。|Microscopic examination revealed the presence of cancerous cells.  显微镜检查显示存在癌细胞。|近义词：minute, tiny, minuscule|反义词：macroscopic, huge, gigantic|词性:形容词

词源:
From French *microscopique*, from micro- + -scopique “relating to viewing”.  Ultimately from Greek mikros "small" + skopein "to look at."
