incrustation:/ɪnˌkrʌˈsteɪʃən/ | n. |结壳，外壳，镶嵌装饰 |A hard coating or deposit, especially of minerals or other materials. |The ancient vase was covered in incrustations of salt from years buried at sea. 这座古老的花瓶上覆盖着多年来埋在海底形成的盐壳。

The pipes were clogged with incrustations of lime. 管道被石灰垢堵塞了。|近义词：deposit, coating, covering, layer, accumulation, accretion, scale, crust |反义词： erosion, abrasion| 词性：名词

incrust:/ɪnˈkrʌst/ | vt. |包壳，镶嵌 |To cover or decorate with a hard coating or crust. |The jeweler incrusted the gold ring with diamonds. 珠宝商在金戒指上镶嵌了钻石。

She incrusted the frame with seashells. 她用贝壳装饰了相框。|近义词： coat, cover, encrust, embed, inlay |反义词：strip, uncover| 词性：动词
