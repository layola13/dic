dissertate:/ˈdɪsərˌteɪt/ /dɪˈsɜːrteɪt/| v. | 发表论文；详细论述；写博士论文 | to speak or write authoritatively or at length about a subject | She disserted on the history of medieval architecture for over an hour. 她详细论述了中世纪建筑史一个多小时。 | 近义词: discuss, expound, elaborate, lecture | 反义词: summarize, condense | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dissertatus, past participle of dissertare (“to discuss, debate”), from dis- (“apart, thoroughly”) + serere (“to join, link, interweave, plant, sow”).
