encyclopedic:/ˌɪnsaɪkləˈpiːdɪk/ | adj. | 百科全书式的；包罗万象的；知识渊博的 | Containing or covering a wide range of knowledge, like an encyclopedia. |  Her knowledge of history is encyclopedic. 她对历史的知识非常渊博。| comprehensive, exhaustive, vast, wide-ranging | limited, narrow, specialized | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *encyclopédique*, from *encyclopédie* (“encyclopedia”) + *-ique* (English *-ic*). *Encyclopédie* comes from  Ancient Greek  ἐγκύκλιος παιδεία (*enkýklios paideía*) "general education," literally "circular/complete education," from ἐν (*en*) "in" + κύκλος (*kýklos*) "circle" + παιδεία (*paideía*) "education, training."
