overrestrain:/ˌoʊvərɪˈstreɪn/| v. |过度约束，过度限制|To restrain too much; to control or limit excessively.|He overrestrained his children, causing them to rebel in their teenage years. 他过度约束他的孩子们，导致他们在青少年时期反叛。| The government was criticized for overrestraining the press. 政府因过度限制新闻自由而受到批评。| 近义词: overcontrol, restrict, suppress, curb|反义词:  free, liberate, loosen, encourage|词性:动词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "restrain" (from Old French "restreindre," from Latin "restringere" -  "to bind back tightly")
