overcoldly:/ˌoʊvərˈkoʊldli/ | adv. | 过于冷淡地，冷漠地 |  In a manner that is excessively cold or unfeeling. | She overcoldly dismissed his apology. 她冷漠地拒绝了他的道歉。| He greeted his old friend overcoldly, barely making eye contact.  他冷漠地迎接了他的老朋友，几乎没有眼神交流。 | 近义词: frigidly, icily, aloofly | 反义词: warmly, cordially, affectionately | 词性: 副词

词源:  "over-" (excessively) + "coldly" (in a cold manner). "Coldly" derives from "cold" (lacking warmth), ultimately from Proto-Germanic *kaldaz.
