cauterizes:/ˈkɔːtəraɪzɪz/
verb
烧灼，烙
burns (a wound or snakebite) with a hot iron or caustic substance to stop bleeding and prevent infection.
The doctor cauterizes the wound to stop the bleeding. 医生烧灼伤口以止血。
cauterize, sear, burn
heal, soothe
v.
