navarino:/ˌnævəˈriːnoʊ/ | n. |羊肉燴飯；羊肉雜燴 | A ragout or stew of lamb or mutton, with tomatoes, onions, and usually eggplant or zucchini. | We had a delicious navarino for dinner last night. 昨晚我们吃了一顿美味的羊肉燴飯。| Example: The navarino was seasoned perfectly with herbs and spices. 羊肉雜燴用香草和香料調味得恰到好处。 | Synonym: stew, ragout, casserole | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "simple dish" or "plain meal") | Noun
