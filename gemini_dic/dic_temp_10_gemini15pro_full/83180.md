gutturals:/ˈɡʌtərəlz/
noun
喉音；咽喉音
Sounds produced in the throat.
The guttural sounds in Arabic can be difficult for English speakers to master.  阿拉伯语中的喉音对于英语使用者来说很难掌握。
近义词:throaty sounds, glottal sounds
反义词:None
n.

gutturals:/ˈɡʌtərəlz/
adjective
(声音)沙哑的,喉音的
Relating to or formed in the throat; harsh-sounding.
His voice had a guttural quality that made him sound intimidating. 他的声音有一种沙哑的特质，使他听起来很吓人。
近义词:harsh, rasping, gravelly
反义词:smooth, melodious, mellifluous
adj.
