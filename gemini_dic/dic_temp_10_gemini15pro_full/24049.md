breezing:/ˈbriːzɪŋ/| v. |轻松地完成，轻而易举地做；吹着微风|Moving or acting with effortless speed and grace; blowing a gentle wind.|He breezed through the exam. 他轻松地完成了考试。|She was breezing along the beach. 她沿着海滩吹着微风散步。|近义词：cruising, coasting, sailing, gliding,  |反义词:struggling, toiling|verb

breezy:/ˈbriːzi/| adj. |轻松愉快的；有微风的|Cheerfully informal; pleasantly windy.|He had a breezy manner. 他态度轻松愉快。|It was a breezy afternoon. 那是一个有微风的下午。|近义词：airy, light, fresh, carefree|反义词:stuffy, oppressive, serious, gloomy|adjective
