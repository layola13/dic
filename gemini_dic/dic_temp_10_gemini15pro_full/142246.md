orometer: /ɒˈrɒmɪtə(r)/ /oʊˈrɒmɪtər/
noun
高度计；测高仪
An instrument for measuring altitude, especially above sea level.
The orometer indicated an altitude of 3,000 meters.  高度计显示高度为3000米。
The pilot checked the orometer to determine their current altitude. 飞行员查看了高度计以确定他们当前的高度。
Synonyms: altimeter,  hypsometer
Antonyms: depth gauge, bathymeter
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek óros (mountain) + -meter (measure)
