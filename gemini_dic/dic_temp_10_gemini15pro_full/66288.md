excommunication:/ˌɛks.kə.ˌmju.nɪˈkeɪ.ʃən/
n.
逐出教会; 开除教籍
The act of officially excluding someone from participation in the sacraments and services of the Christian Church.
He was threatened with excommunication. 他被威胁要逐出教会。
近义词:exclusion, ousting, banishment
反义词:inclusion, admittance, reinstatement
词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *excommunicatio*, from excommunicare "to excommunicate," from ex- "out" + communicare "to share, communicate," from communis "common."
