inaccessibility:/ˌɪnəkˌsesəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可接近性；无法得到；难以理解 |The inaccessibility of the remote mountain village made it difficult to provide aid.  这个偏远山村的难以接近使得提供援助很困难。| inaccessibility comes from the negation of "accessibility," which means the quality of being able to be reached or entered.  不可接近性来源于“可接近性”的否定，可接近性指的是能够被到达或进入的性质。|  例句：The inaccessibility of the summit discouraged many climbers.  顶峰的难以接近性使许多登山者望而却步。

|近义词：unavailability, impenetrability, remoteness |反义词：accessibility, availability, closeness | 词性: 名词


inaccessible:/ˌɪnəkˈsesəbl/ | adj. | 不可接近的；达不到的；难以理解的 | The island was inaccessible due to the strong currents. 由于强劲的水流，该岛屿无法到达。| inaccessible is formed by adding the prefix "in-" (meaning "not") to "accessible," which derives from the Latin "accessus," meaning "approach."  “inaccessible”是由前缀“in-”（表示“不”）加在“accessible”上构成的，“accessible”源于拉丁语“accessus”，意思是“接近”。| 例句：The information was inaccessible to the public.  这些信息公众无法获取。

|近义词：unreachable, unattainable, remote |反义词：accessible, reachable, available | 词性: 形容词
