otocranial:/ˌoʊtoʊˈkreɪniəl/
adjective
耳颅的
Relating to the ear and the cranium (skull).  Specifically, pertaining to the bony capsule enclosing the inner ear, which is part of the temporal bone of the skull.
The otocranial development is crucial for hearing and balance. 耳颅发育对听力和平衡至关重要。
The study focused on the otocranial anatomy of various mammals. 这项研究集中于各种哺乳动物的耳颅解剖结构。
Synonyms:  None specifically, might use "auditory" or "otic" in some contexts relating to the ear, and "cranial" relating to the skull.
Antonyms: None
