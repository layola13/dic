misnavigation:/ˌmɪsnævɪˈɡeɪʃən/
n.|错误导航，导航错误|The misnavigation led them miles off course. 导航错误使他们偏离航线数英里。|例句：The ship's misnavigation resulted in it running aground.  这艘船的导航错误导致它搁浅。

例句：Due to the misnavigation of the aircraft, the flight was delayed by several hours. 由于飞机导航错误，航班延误了几个小时。


近义词：misdirection, misguidance, error in navigation
反义词：accurate navigation, correct navigation
词性: noun
