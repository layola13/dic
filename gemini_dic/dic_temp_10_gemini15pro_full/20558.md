blackballed:/ˌblækˈbɔːld/ | v. | 阻止；拒绝；排斥 | To exclude or reject someone from a group or society, typically by means of a secret ballot. | He was blackballed by the club for his controversial views. 他因为有争议的观点而被俱乐部拒之门外。 | The committee blackballed her application. 委员会拒绝了她的申请。 | 近义词: ostracize, exclude, reject, bar, blacklist | 反义词: accept, admit, welcome, include | 词性: 动词

blackballed:/ˌblækˈbɔːld/ | adj. | 被拒绝的；被排斥的 | Excluded or rejected, typically by a secret ballot. | He felt blackballed and isolated after the incident. 事件发生后，他感到被排斥和孤立。 | Her blackballed status made it difficult to find work in the industry. 她被拒绝的状态使她很难在这个行业找到工作。 | 近义词: ostracized, excluded, rejected | 反义词: accepted, welcomed, included | 词性: 形容词
