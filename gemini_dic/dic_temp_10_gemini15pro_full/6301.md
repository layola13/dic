amortisable:/əˈmɔːrtaɪzəbl/ /əˈmɔːrtɪzəbl/
adjective
可摊销的
Able to be amortized; that can be gradually written off.
The cost of new equipment is amortisable over five years. 新设备的成本可以在五年内摊销。
Synonyms: depreciable, write-offable
Antonyms: non-amortisable, non-depreciable
词性: adjective
