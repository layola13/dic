clear-headedness:/ˌklɪərˈhɛdɪdnəs/ | n. | 清醒；头脑清晰 | The state of being clear-headed; having a clear and focused mind. |  After a good night's sleep, she woke with a sense of clear-headedness.  睡了一夜好觉后，她醒来时头脑清醒。| clarity, lucidity, alertness, composure | confusion, muddleheadedness, fogginess | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From clear + -headed + -ness.  Clear (from Old French *cler from Latin clarus) meaning bright, transparent, or easy to understand. -headed is a suffix meaning "having a head of a specified kind". -ness is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, denoting a state or quality.
