hellebore: /ˈhɛlɪˌbɔːr/ (US), /ˈhɛlɪˌbɔː(r)/ (UK)| n. |嚏根草； Hellebore |A perennial plant of the buttercup family, typically having palmately lobed leaves and large bowl-shaped flowers.  Hellebores are known for blooming in winter or early spring. |例句：The hellebores in my garden are already blooming.  我家花园里的嚏根草已经开花了。

例句：She planted a variety of hellebores for winter color. 她种了各种各样的嚏根草来增添冬天的色彩。|近义词：winter rose, Lenten rose (for specific types)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *ellebor, from Old French elebore, from Latin helleborus, from Greek ἑλλέβορος (helléboros).  The Greek word is of uncertain origin, possibly related to ἑλεῖν (heleîn, “to injure”) and βορά (borá, “food”), referring to its poisonous properties.
