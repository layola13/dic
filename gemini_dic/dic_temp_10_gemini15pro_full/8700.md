anticreative:/ˌænti kriˈeɪtɪv/ | adj. | 反创造力的，扼杀创造力的 | Opposing or hindering creativity; tending to stifle innovation or imaginative thinking. | The school's strict rules and emphasis on rote learning were seen as anticreative. 学校严格的规章制度和对死记硬背的强调被认为是扼杀创造力的。

The company's anticreative environment led to a high turnover rate among its designers.  这家公司扼杀创造力的环境导致其设计师的高流失率。


近义词:unimaginative, uninspired, stifling, restrictive | 反义词:creative, imaginative, inspiring, innovative | 词性:adjective
