declaiming:/dɪˈkleɪmɪŋ/
verb
慷慨陈词；朗诵；演讲；高谈阔论
delivering speech or a poem in an impassioned, rhetorical style.
He was declaiming against the injustices of the system. 他正在慷慨激昂地控诉该制度的不公。
Synonyms: orating, speechifying, reciting, holding forth
Antonyms: whispering, muttering, murmuring
词性: 动词 (verb)

declaim:/dɪˈkleɪm/
verb

She declaimed against the evils of alcohol. 她高谈阔论酒精的危害。
