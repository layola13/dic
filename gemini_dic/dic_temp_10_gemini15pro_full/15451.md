badam:/ˈbɑːdɑːm/ |n.|巴旦木；杏仁|Badam is a type of tree nut that is popular in many cuisines. 巴旦木是一种在许多菜肴中很受欢迎的坚果。|She added some slivered badam to the rice dish. 她在米饭里加了一些杏仁片。|Synonyms: almond, tree nut|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific nut)|Noun

Etymology:
From Hindi बादाम (bādām), from Persian بادام (bâdâm), from Middle Persian *wādām, from Old Iranian *wāδama-, from Proto-Iranian *wāδama-.  Ultimately from the same source as the English word "almond".
