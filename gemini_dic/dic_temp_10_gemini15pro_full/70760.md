fineries:/ˈfaɪnəriːz/
noun
精致的服饰；华丽的装饰
fine clothes and jewellery, especially those worn for special occasions
She wore her finest fineries to the ball. 她穿着她最精致的服饰去参加舞会。
近义词: best clothes, Sunday best, fancy clothes, elegant attire
反义词: rags, everyday clothes, casual wear
词性:n.

词源: From *fin*, meaning "refined," "elegant," or "of high quality."  The suffix *-ery* denotes a collection or state of being, thus "finery" refers to a collection of fine things.  The plural form, *fineries*, specifically refers to a collection of fine clothing and jewelry.
