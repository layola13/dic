antitheologian:/ˌæntiθiːəˈloʊdʒən/
n.|反神学家|A person who opposes theology or the influence of organized religion.|He was considered an antitheologian for his criticisms of traditional doctrines. 他因批评传统教义而被认为是反神学家。|
近义词: freethinker, skeptic, atheist, agnostic
反义词: theologian, devotee, believer
词性: noun

antitheologian:/ˌæntiθiːəˈloʊdʒən/
n.|反神学|Opposition to theology or the influence of organized religion.|His writings expressed a strong antitheologian sentiment. 他的著作表达了强烈的反神学情绪。|
近义词: atheism, secularism, freethinking
反义词: theism, religiosity, faith
词性: noun


词源:  "anti-" (against) + "theologian" (one who studies theology).  "Anti-" comes from Greek "anti" (against, opposite). "Theologian" comes from Greek "theologia" (the study of God), from "theos" (god) + "-logia" (study).
