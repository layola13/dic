nonconceptual:/ˌnɒnkənˈsɛptʃuəl/ | adj. | 非概念的，非观念的 | Not related to or based on concepts; not involving or requiring the use of concepts. |  Her art is nonconceptual, focusing on the emotional impact rather than intellectual meaning. 她的艺术是非概念性的，侧重于情感冲击而不是智力意义。|  The child's understanding of the world is largely nonconceptual at this stage.  在这个阶段，孩子对世界的理解很大程度上是非概念性的。 |  non-representational, abstract, intuitive | conceptual, intellectual | adjective
