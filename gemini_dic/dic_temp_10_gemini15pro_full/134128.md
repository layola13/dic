nonimperativeness:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpɛrətɪvnəs/
|n.|非强制性，非命令性|The nonimperativeness of the suggestion allowed for flexibility in its implementation.  这个建议的非强制性使其在执行过程中具有灵活性。|The system's nonimperativeness is a key feature, allowing users to choose their own paths. 该系统的非强制性是一个关键特征，允许用户选择自己的路径。|近义词：optionality, discretion, flexibility|反义词：obligation, necessity, requirement|词性:名词

词源:  From non- + imperative + -ness.  "Non-" prefix meaning "not," combined with "imperative" (meaning expressing a command), and the suffix "-ness" which forms nouns expressing a state or quality.
