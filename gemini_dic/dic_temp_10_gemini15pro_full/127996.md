nephelometer:/ˌnɛfəˈlɒmɪtər/ (US) /ˌnɛfəˈlɒmɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |浊度计；散射光度计|An instrument for measuring the scattering of light by particles suspended in a liquid or gas, used to determine the turbidity of a substance.|The nephelometer measured the concentration of suspended particles in the water sample.|Synonyms: turbidimeter,  opacity meter|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps clarity meter if such a device exists)|noun
