dicotyledon: /ˌdaɪˌkɒtɪˈliːdən/ (UK) /ˌdaɪˌkɑːtɪˈliːdən/ (US)| n. |双子叶植物|A flowering plant with two embryonic seed leaves or cotyledons. Dicotyledons typically have broad leaves with netlike veins, a main root system, and flower parts in multiples of four or five. |例句：Roses and beans are examples of dicotyledons. 玫瑰和豆类是双子叶植物的例子。

|例句：Dicotyledons are one of the two main groups of flowering plants. 双子叶植物是开花植物的两大类群之一。

|近义词：dicot|反义词: monocotyledon|词性:noun
