antacids:/ˌæntˈæsɪdz/ | n. | 抗酸剂；解酸药 | Medications used to neutralize stomach acid. |  She took some antacids after eating spicy food. 她吃了辛辣食物后服用了一些抗酸药。| Synonyms: antacid tablets, acid neutralizers, stomach settlers | Antonyms: acids | noun

antacid:/ˌæntˈæsɪd/ | adj. | 抗酸的 | Having the ability to neutralize acids. | Antacid tablets can provide relief from heartburn. 抗酸片可以缓解胃灼热。| Synonyms: alkaline, basic, neutralizing | Antonyms: acidic | adjective
