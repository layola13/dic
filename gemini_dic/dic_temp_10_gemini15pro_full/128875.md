nilgai:/ˈnɪlɡaɪ/| n.|蓝牛, 尼尔盖(一种大型亚洲羚羊)|A large bluish-gray antelope (Boselaphus tragocamelus) of India.|例句：We saw a herd of nilgai grazing in the grassland. 我们看到一群蓝牛在草原上吃草。|例句：The nilgai is the largest antelope in Asia. 蓝牛是亚洲最大的羚羊。|近义词：antelope, blue bull|反义词:None (no direct opposite for an animal species)|词性:noun

Etymology: From Hindi नीलगाय (nīlgāy), from नीला (nīlā, “blue”) + गाय (gāy, “cow”).
