half-radically:/ˌhæf ˈrædɪkəli/ | adv. | 半彻底地，不完全彻底地 |  Partially radical; advocating or implementing radical changes in some areas but not others.  This word suggests a hesitant or incomplete approach to radicalism. |  The government adopted a half-radically approach to education reform, changing the curriculum but not the funding model. 政府对教育改革采取了半彻底的做法，改变了课程，但没有改变资金模式。 | The party's platform was half-radically, appealing to both progressives and moderates. 该党的纲领并不完全彻底，对进步人士和温和人士都有吸引力。 |  Near synonyms: partially radical, semi-radical, incompletely radical | Near antonyms: fully radical, thoroughly radical, completely radical | adverb
