co-production:/ˌkoʊ prəˈdʌkʃən/| n.| 联合制作，共同制作|The film was a co-production between France and Italy. 这部电影是法国和意大利的合拍片。|例句：The co-production between the two studios was a huge success.  两家工作室的联合制作取得了巨大的成功。

例句：This documentary is a co-production of several international broadcasters. 这部纪录片是由几家国际广播公司联合制作的。


|近义词：joint production, collaboration, partnership|反义词:|noun
