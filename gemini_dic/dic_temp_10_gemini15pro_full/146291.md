overfatigue:/ˌoʊvərˈfəˈtiːɡ/| n.|过度疲劳|Excessive tiredness; exhaustion.|He collapsed from overfatigue after working for three days straight. 他连续工作了三天，因过度疲劳而晕倒了。|例句：The doctor diagnosed her with overfatigue. 医生诊断她过度疲劳。|近义词：exhaustion, weariness, burnout|反义词:energy, vigor|名词


overfatigue:/ˌoʊvərˈfəˈtiːɡ/| vt.|使过度疲劳|To make someone excessively tired.|The long journey overfatigued the horses. 长途旅行使马匹过度疲劳。|例句：Don't overfatigue yourself with too much exercise. 不要运动过度而使自己过度疲劳。|近义词：exhaust, wear out, overwork|反义词:refresh, energize|动词
