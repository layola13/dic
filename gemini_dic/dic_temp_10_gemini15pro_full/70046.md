fetters:/ˈfɛtərz/| n.| 枷锁，束缚 |chains or shackles placed on the ankles or feet of a prisoner; anything that confines or restrains| The fetters were removed from the prisoner's legs.  囚犯腿上的镣铐被 removed除了。|  The old traditions were fetters on his creativity.  旧的传统束缚了他的创造力。| synonyms: shackles, chains, bonds, restraints | antonyms: freedom, liberty | noun

fetter:/ˈfɛtər/| v.| 束缚，限制 |to restrain with chains or shackles; to restrict or confine|  His fear of failure fettered his ambition. 他对失败的恐惧束缚了他的雄心。|  The company was fettered by outdated regulations.  公司受到过时规定的限制。 | synonyms: restrain, restrict, confine, hamper, impede | antonyms: liberate, free, unleash | verb
