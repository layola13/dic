harfang:/ˈhɑːrfæŋ/ | n. | 雪鸮 | Snowy owl, a large white owl native to Arctic regions. | The harfang silently swooped down on its prey. 雪鸮悄无声息地俯冲向它的猎物。|  The harfang blended in perfectly with the snowy landscape. 雪鸮与雪地完美地融合在一起。| 近义词: snowy owl | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

词源: From Canadian French *harfang*,  from Old French *harfang* "gyrfalcon," probably of Scandinavian origin.
