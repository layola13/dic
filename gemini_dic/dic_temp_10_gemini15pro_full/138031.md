nop:/nɒp/| v. |不做任何事；拒绝|To do nothing; to refuse to act.|Example:  He decided to nop the request and move on to the next task. 他决定拒绝这个请求，继续下一个任务。|Synonym:  ignore, skip, decline, refuse|Antonym:  act, perform, accept|verb
