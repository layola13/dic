concordable:/kənˈkɔːrdəbəl/
adjective
可一致的，可和谐的
Capable of being in agreement or harmony; harmonious.
例句：The two parties found their views concordable on most issues. 双方发现他们在大多数问题上的观点是一致的。

例句：The concordable atmosphere in the meeting led to a productive outcome. 会议和谐的气氛带来了富有成效的结果。
近义词：compatible, agreeable, harmonious, consistent
反义词：incompatible, discordant, conflicting, contradictory
词性: adjective
词源:  From Late Latin *concordabilis, from Latin concordāre (“to agree”), from com- + cord- (“heart”).  Essentially, "able to be of one heart."
