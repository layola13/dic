overplants:/ˌoʊvərˈplænts/ | n. | 过量种植，过度种植 |  Planting more than is necessary or desirable, often resulting in overcrowding and reduced yields.  |  The farmer overplants his fields every year, hoping for a bumper crop, but often the plants compete for resources and the yield is lower. 农民每年都过度种植他的田地，希望能丰收，但通常植物会争夺资源，导致产量下降。 |  Overplanting led to stunted growth and a poor harvest. 过度种植导致生长发育不良和歉收。 | 近义词: overcrowd, oversow | 反义词: underplant | 词性: 名词

overplant:/ˌoʊvərˈplænt/ | v. | 过量种植，过度种植 | To plant more than is necessary or desirable, often resulting in overcrowding. |  He overplanted the garden with tomatoes, and they didn't have enough space to grow properly. 他在花园里种了太多的西红柿，它们没有足够的空间正常生长。 | The farmer overplanted his fields, which ultimately reduced his yield. 农民过度种植了他的田地，最终降低了他的产量。 | 近义词: overcrowd, oversow | 反义词: underplant | 词性: 动词
