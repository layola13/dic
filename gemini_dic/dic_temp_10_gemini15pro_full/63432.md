entreatance:/ɪnˈtriːtəns/
n.
恳求, 乞求
An earnest request or petition.
He made entreatance to the king for mercy. 他恳求国王开恩。
Synonyms: supplication, plea, appeal, petition
Antonyms: demand, command, order
词性: 名词
