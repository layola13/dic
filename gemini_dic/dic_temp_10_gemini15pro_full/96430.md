indistinct:/ˌɪndɪˈstɪŋkt/ | adj. | 模糊的，不清楚的 | Not clearly defined or easy to perceive or understand. | The photograph was blurred and indistinct.  照片模糊不清。|  近义词：blurred, faint, vague, obscure, fuzzy | 反义词：clear, distinct, sharp, precise | 词性: 形容词

indistinct:/ˌɪndɪˈstɪŋkt/ | adj. | 微弱的，不清楚的 | Not clearly audible or easy to hear. |  His voice was indistinct, lost in the wind. 他的声音模糊不清，被风吹散了。 | 近义词：muffled, low, faint | 反义词：loud, clear, audible | 词性: 形容词


词源:  Late Middle English: from in- (expressing negation) + distinct.
