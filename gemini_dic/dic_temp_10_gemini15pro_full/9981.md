aphorismic:/ˌæfəˈrɪzmɪk/
adjective
格言式的；警句式的
terse and witty, like an aphorism.  Expressing a general truth or astute observation in a concise and pithy manner.
His writing style is aphorismic, packed with wisdom in short, memorable sentences. 他的写作风格是格言式的，用简短而令人难忘的句子表达了丰富的智慧。

Her aphorismic pronouncements were both witty and wise. 她格言式的言论既机智又明智。

synonym: pithy, concise, epigrammatic, sententious
antonym: prolix, verbose, rambling
