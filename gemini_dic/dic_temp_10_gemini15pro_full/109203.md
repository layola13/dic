legwork:/ˈleɡwɜːrk/
noun
跑腿的工作，实地调查
The job involved a lot of legwork, going from house to house.  这项工作需要大量的跑腿工作，挨家挨户地走访。
She did all the legwork for the senator's campaign. 她为参议员的竞选活动做了所有的跑腿工作。
fieldwork, groundwork, footwork
deskwork
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From leg + work.  Meaning “work that involves much walking or running about” is from 1840.
