additionally:/əˈdɪʃənəli/
adv.
此外，另外
Used to introduce something further, typically another point or fact.
Additionally, I would like to point out that... 此外，我想指出...
He was fined, and additionally, his driving license was suspended. 他被罚款，此外，他的驾驶执照也被吊销了。
近义词: also, furthermore, moreover, in addition, besides
反义词: however, nevertheless, on the contrary
词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *additionalis, from Latin additio (addition) + -alis (suffix forming adjectives).

USAGE->
Introducing a new point:
Additionally, the company offers excellent benefits. = 此外，该公司提供优厚的福利。
The food was delicious; additionally, the service was impeccable. = 食物很美味；此外，服务也很周到。

Adding information:
He's a talented musician and additionally a skilled painter. = 他是一位才华横溢的音乐家，此外还是一位技艺精湛的画家。
She speaks French fluently, and additionally, she understands Spanish. = 她法语流利，此外，她还懂西班牙语。

After a semicolon:
The project is behind schedule; additionally, we're over budget. = 项目进度落后；此外，我们超出了预算。
He's always late for work; additionally, he never apologizes. = 他上班总是迟到；此外，他从不道歉。

At the beginning of a sentence:
Additionally, we need to consider the environmental impact. = 此外，我们需要考虑环境影响。
Additionally, I'd like to thank everyone for their hard work. = 此外，我要感谢大家的辛勤工作。

In formal writing:
Additionally, the report highlights the need for further research. = 此外，该报告强调了进一步研究的必要性。
Additionally, the committee recommends implementing new safety measures. = 此外，委员会建议实施新的安全措施。


PHRASE->
additionally to this = 除此之外
in addition to = 除...之外
not additionally = 不再另外
additionally noteworthy = 此外值得注意的是
additionally included = 此外还包括
