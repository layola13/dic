nonsober:/nɒnˈsəʊbə(r)/ | adj. | 不清醒的，醉酒的 | Not sober; intoxicated.  | Example: He was clearly nonsober and shouldn't be driving. 他显然醉酒了，不应该开车。 | Example: Her nonsober behavior embarrassed everyone at the party.  她醉酒后的行为让派对上的每个人都感到尴尬。 | 近义词: drunk, intoxicated, inebriated | 反义词: sober, temperate | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + sober.
