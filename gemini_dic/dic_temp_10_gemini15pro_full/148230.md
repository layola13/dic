oversimplicities:/ˌōvərˌsɪmplɪˈsɪtiz/| n.| 过分简化；过于简单化 |The report suffered from several oversimplicities, failing to capture the nuances of the situation.  这份报告过于简化，未能捕捉到情况的细微之处。|  These oversimplicities can lead to misunderstandings and poor decision-making. 这些过分简化会导致误解和糟糕的决策。 |近义词：simplifications, generalizations|反义词： complexities, nuances | noun

oversimplify:/ˌōvərˈsɪmplɪfaɪ/| vt.| 过分简化；过于简单化 |It's easy to oversimplify complex issues.  很容易将复杂的问题过分简化。| Politicians often oversimplify economic problems to appeal to voters. 政客们经常为了迎合选民而过分简化经济问题。|近义词：simplify excessively, generalize |反义词： complicate, elaborate | verb

oversimplified:/ˌōvərˈsɪmplɪfaɪd/| adj.| 过分简化的；过于简单化的 |The explanation was oversimplified and misleading. 这个解释过于简化，而且具有误导性。| An oversimplified view of history can be dangerous. 对历史的过于简化的看法可能是危险的。| 近义词： simplistic, reductive| 反义词： complex, nuanced| adjective
