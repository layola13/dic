misbegin:/ˌmɪsbɪˈɡɪn/
verb
错误地开始；开始得不顺利
to begin incorrectly or unluckily
The project misbegan with a series of misunderstandings.  这个项目一开始就遭遇了一系列的误解。
They misbegan their journey by taking the wrong train. 他们的旅程一开始就错了，坐错了火车。
近义词: start badly, get off to a bad start
反义词: begin well, start successfully
词性: verb
