genteelness:/ˌdʒɛnˈtiːlnəs/| n.|文雅；有教养；上流社会的风度；|The genteelness of her manners impressed everyone. 她的举止文雅，给大家留下了深刻的印象。|English Explanation:  The quality of being genteel; refinement and politeness in manners and behavior, often associated with a higher social class.  It suggests an affected or overly refined politeness.|例句：She was raised with a certain genteelness that was evident in her every action. 她从小就被培养出一种文雅的气质，这在她的一举一动中都显而易见。

例句：His genteelness was a stark contrast to the roughness of the other men. 他的文雅与其他男子的粗鲁形成了鲜明的对比。|近义词：refinement, politeness, courtesy, elegance, grace, sophistication|反义词：rudeness, coarseness, vulgarity, crudeness|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From genteel + -ness.  Genteel itself comes from French gentil, ultimately from Latin gentilis "of the same clan or family," later "noble, of good family."
