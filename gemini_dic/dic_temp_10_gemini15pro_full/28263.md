captions:/ˈkæpʃənz/| v.| 为…加字幕，加上说明文字 | To provide (an illustration) with a caption.  (Often used in the passive form "is captioned")|The video is captioned in English and Spanish.|Synonyms: subtitle, annotate, label, title |Antonyms:  uncaptioned, blank|verb
