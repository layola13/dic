desecration:/ˌdɛsɪˈkreɪʃən/| n. |亵渎，玷污|The act of damaging or disrespecting something sacred or highly valued.|The desecration of the temple shocked the community.  寺庙被亵渎震惊了整个社区。|近义词：profanation, defilement, violation, sacrilege|反义词：reverence, veneration|词性: noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *dēsacrātiōn*, from Latin dēsacrāre (“to deconsecrate”), from dē- (“un-”) + sacrāre (“to consecrate”). Ultimately from Latin sacer (“sacred”).
