elastomeric:/ɪˌlæstəˈmɛrɪk/ |adj.|弹性的，橡胶似的|Made of or resembling an elastomer; elastic.|例句：The elastomeric material allows the phone case to stretch and protect the phone from drops.  这种弹性材料使手机壳能够拉伸并保护手机免受跌落损坏。

例句：Elastomeric bearings are used in bridges to absorb seismic shocks.  弹性轴承用于桥梁以吸收地震冲击。|近义词：elastic, resilient, rubbery, flexible|反义词：rigid, inflexible, stiff|词性: 形容词

Etymology->
From elastomer + -ic.  Elastomer is derived from elastic + -mer (denoting a polymer). Ultimately from Greek *elastos "malleable, ductile"
