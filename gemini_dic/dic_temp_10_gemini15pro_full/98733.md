insurmountableness:/ˌɪnsərˈmaʊntəbəlnəs/
n.
不可克服性；无法超越性
The quality or state of being insurmountable; impossibility of being overcome or passed over.
The insurmountableness of the obstacle made them give up.  这个障碍的不可克服性使他们放弃了。
近义词: insuperability, invincibility, unconquerability
反义词: surmountability, conquerability, vincibility
词性: noun

insurmountable:/ˌɪnsərˈmaʊntəbl/
adj.
不可克服的；无法超越的
Too great to be overcome.
They were faced with seemingly insurmountable obstacles.  他们面临着似乎不可克服的障碍。
近义词: insuperable, invincible, unconquerable
反义词: surmountable, conquerable, vincible
词性: adjective

surmount:/sərˈmaʊnt/
v.
克服；战胜；越过
To overcome (a difficulty or obstacle).
She surmounted the obstacle with great courage. 她以极大的勇气克服了障碍。
近义词: overcome, conquer, vanquish
反义词: succumb, yield, surrender
词性: verb

mount:/maʊnt/
v.
登上；骑上；增加；上升
To go up or climb.  To increase in amount or extent.
The tension mounted as the deadline approached. 随着截止日期的临近，紧张气氛加剧。
近义词: ascend, climb, increase
反义词: descend, decrease, decline
词性: verb

-mount (suffix)
suffix
构成动词和名词，表示“登上，骑上”
Forms verbs and nouns meaning "to go up or climb," "to increase in amount or extent."
