nonescalating: /ˌnɒnˈɛskəleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 非升级的，不恶化的 | Not becoming more intense or serious; not escalating. | The situation remained nonescalating despite the initial tension.  尽管最初局势紧张，但并未恶化。|  The two countries engaged in nonescalating border skirmishes. 两国进行了不会升级的边境小冲突。| 近义词：stable, contained, controlled, limited | 反义词：escalating, intensifying, worsening, deteriorating | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "escalating" (from "escalate," from Latin "scala" meaning "ladder" or "stairs," referring to the act of climbing or increasing). 词源：“non-”（前缀，意为“不”）+“escalating”（来自“escalate”，源自拉丁语“scala”，意为“梯子”或“楼梯”，指的是攀爬或增加的行为）。
