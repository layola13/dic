alongshoreman: /əˌlɔːŋˈʃɔːrmən/ /əˌlɔːŋˈʃɔːrmæn/
n.
码头工人，搬运工
A dockworker who loads and unloads ships.
例句：The alongshoremen were busy unloading the cargo from the ship. 码头工人正忙着从船上卸货。

例句：The alongshoreman skillfully maneuvered the crane to lift the heavy container.  码头工人熟练地操纵起重机吊起沉重的集装箱。

近义词：docker, dockworker, stevedore, lumper
反义词:  None (No direct antonym; perhaps "shipowner" or "cargo owner" in certain contexts)
词性: noun
