displeasure:/dɪsˈpleʒər/
noun
不愉快，不高兴，生气
a feeling of annoyance and disapproval
The boss showed his displeasure by frowning. 老板皱着眉头表示不满。


近义词: annoyance, dissatisfaction, disapproval, irritation, anger, resentment
反义词: pleasure, satisfaction, approval, delight, happiness

词源:
From Middle English *displesaunce, from Anglo-Norman displeisaunce, from displeisir (“to displease”), from des- + plaisir (“pleasure”).  Ultimately from Latin displicēre.
