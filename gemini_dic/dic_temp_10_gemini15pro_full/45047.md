cubbed:/kʌbd/
adjective|成立方体的，切成小方块的|shaped like a cube; cut into small cubes|The carrots were cubbed and added to the stew. 胡萝卜被切成小块，加到了炖菜里。|近义词：diced, cubed|反义词: whole, uncut|词性: adjective
cubbed:/kʌbd/
verb|切成小方块|cut into small cubes|The chef cubbed the potatoes for the soup.  厨师把土豆切成小块做汤。|近义词：dice, chop|反义词: leave whole|词性: verb
