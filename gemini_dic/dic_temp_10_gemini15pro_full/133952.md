nonheinous: /ˌnɑːnˈheɪnəs/ | adj. | 非十恶不赦的；非罪大恶极的 | Not heinous; not grossly wicked or reprehensible. |  The crime, while serious, was judged nonheinous. 这项罪行虽然严重，但被判定为非罪大恶极。| Synonyms: minor, venial, unimportant, insignificant, trifling, petty | Antonyms: heinous, atrocious, monstrous, egregious, flagrant | adjective

Etymology:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "heinous" (from Old French "haineus," meaning "hateful," ultimately from "haine," meaning "hatred").
