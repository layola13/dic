cornflower:/ˈkɔːrnflaʊər/ (UK) /ˈkɔːrnflaʊər/ (US)| n. |矢车菊，玉米粉|A plant with bright blue flowers, or the flower itself. Also, a bright, vivid blue color like that of the cornflower.  The finely ground flour/starch made from corn (maize), used in cooking as a thickener.|例句：
The field was ablaze with cornflowers. 田野里盛开着矢车菊。

She wore a dress the color of cornflowers. 她穿着一件矢车菊蓝的连衣裙。

|近义词：bachelor's button, bluebottle (for the flower), azure, cerulean (for the color)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


cornstarch:/ˈkɔːrnstɑːrtʃ/ (UK) /ˈkɔːrnstɑːrtʃ/ (US)| n. |玉米淀粉|A fine, white powder made from corn, used for thickening sauces and gravies.|例句：
Mix the cornstarch with a little cold water before adding it to the hot sauce.  在加入热酱汁之前，将玉米淀粉与少量冷水混合。

Cornstarch is a common ingredient in many Chinese dishes. 玉米淀粉是许多中国菜的常见成分。
|近义词：cornflour (UK)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
