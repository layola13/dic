nettle:/ˈnetl/ (US), /ˈnetəl/ (UK)| n. v. |荨麻；激怒|A herbaceous plant which has jagged leaves covered with stinging hairs.  As a verb, it means to irritate or annoy someone.|例句：The nettles stung my legs as I walked through the tall grass.  走过高高的草丛时，荨麻刺痛了我的腿。|近义词：irritate, annoy, vex, provoke, sting (v.)|反义词：soothe, calm, please (v.)|词性:noun, verb
