centrolineal:/ˌsɛntrəˈlɪniəl/
adjective
中心线的；共线的
Relating to or situated on a central line; collinear.
The centrolineal arrangement of the points allowed for a simplified calculation.  这些点的共线排列使得计算得以简化。

The architect designed the building with a centrolineal emphasis. 建筑师设计了这座建筑，强调中心线。

近义词:axial, collinear
反义词:offset, non-collinear
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *centrum* (center) + *linea* (line).
