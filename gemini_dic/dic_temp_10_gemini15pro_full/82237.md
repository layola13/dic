grouching:/ˈɡraʊtʃɪŋ/| n. |抱怨，发牢骚|The act of complaining or grumbling persistently.|He's been grouching all day about the weather. 他整天都在抱怨天气。|近义词：complaining, grumbling, whining|反义词：praising, complimenting|词性:noun (gerund or present participle)
grouching:/ˈɡraʊtʃɪŋ/| adj. |抱怨的，爱发牢骚的|Given to complaining or grumbling.|She gave him a grouching look. 她给了他一个抱怨的眼神。|近义词：grumpy, complaining|反义词：cheerful, happy|词性:adjective (present participle)
