palification: /ˌpælɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
| n. | 打桩; 筑栅栏; 用栅栏围护 | The act or process of constructing a palisade or fence, or strengthening something with piles. | The palification of the fort made it more secure.  堡垒的打桩使其更加安全。| 近义词：fencing, fortification, piling | 反义词：demolition, destruction | 词性: 名词

palification: /ˌpælɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
| n. |  (解剖学) 栅状排列，栅状结构 |  Arrangement resembling a palisade.  (Especially in anatomy, referring to a row of elongated structures, like the palisade cells in a leaf). | The microscopic image revealed the palification of the tissue. 显微图像显示了组织的栅状结构。| 近义词：arrangement, alignment, formation | 反义词: disorder, disarray | 词性: 名词


词源:  From French *palification*, from *palificer* "to palisade," from Latin *palus* "stake."
