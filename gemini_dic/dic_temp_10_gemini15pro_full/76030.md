furioso:/ˌfjʊəriˈoʊsoʊ/ /ˌfʊəriˈoʊsoʊ/
adjective
狂怒的，激烈的
Marked by fury; furious.  Also, in music, played with great energy and speed.
The conductor directed the orchestra in a furioso passage. 指挥家指挥乐队演奏一段激烈的乐章。
Near synonyms:  furious, enraged, vehement, passionate, impetuous
Antonyms: calm, peaceful, tranquil, serene
词性: adjective


furioso:/ˌfjʊəriˈoʊsoʊ/ /ˌfʊəriˈoʊsoʊ/
adverb
狂怒地，激烈地
In a furious manner; with great energy and speed (music).
He played the final movement furioso. 他狂暴地演奏了最后乐章。
Near synonyms: furiously, vehemently, passionately, impetuously
Antonyms: calmly, peacefully, tranquilly, serenely
词性: adverb


furioso:/ˌfjʊəriˈoʊsoʊ/ /ˌfʊəriˈoʊsoʊ/
noun
狂怒的人，狂暴的段落（音乐）
A furious person; a passage in a musical composition to be performed with great energy and speed.
The furioso in the opera was particularly dramatic. 歌剧中的狂暴段落特别引人注目。
Near synonyms: rage, outburst, frenzy (for the person);  crescendo, climax (for the music)
Antonyms: lull, pause, interlude (for the music)
词性: noun


词源: Italian, from Latin *furiosus, from furia "fury."
