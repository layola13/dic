infirmarian:/ˌɪnfərˈmɛəriən/ | n. |医务室管理员；医院护士|A person who cares for the sick or injured, especially in a school or institution. | The school infirmarian tended to the student's scraped knee. 学校的医务室管理员处理了学生擦伤的膝盖。| 近义词: nurse, medic, attendant | 反义词: patient | 词性: 名词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin *infirmarius, from Latin infirmus ‘weak, not firm.’
