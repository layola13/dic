outthwack:/ˌaʊtˈθwæk/
verb
彻底打败，胜过
to hit someone harder than they hit you
例句：He outthwacked his opponent in the final round. 他在最后一轮彻底击败了对手。
例句：She outthwacked all the other competitors and won the championship. 她击败了所有其他竞争者，赢得了冠军。
近义词：outdo, surpass, defeat, trounce, vanquish
反义词：lose to, be defeated by
词性：v.
