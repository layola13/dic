adventuresomeness:/ədˌvɛntʃərəsnəs/
n.|冒险精神，大胆，爱冒险|The quality or state of being adventuresome; a willingness to take risks and try new things.|She showed great adventuresomeness by traveling to the remote jungle. 她展现了巨大的冒险精神，去了偏远的丛林旅行。|近义词：daring, boldness, audacity, spirit of adventure|反义词:timidity, cautiousness, fearfulness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From adventuresome + -ness.  Adventuresome is derived from adventure + -some.  Adventure ultimately comes from Latin adventura, meaning "that which is about to happen."

USAGE->
She admired his adventuresomeness in trying new foods. = 她很欣赏他勇于尝试新食物的冒险精神。

He demonstrated his adventuresomeness by quitting his job and starting his own business. = 他辞掉工作，开始自己创业，这证明了他的冒险精神。
