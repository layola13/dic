leeangle:/ˈliːˌæŋɡəl/ | n. | 背风角，下风处 |  The sheltered side of something; the side away from the wind. | The boat anchored in the leeangle of the island.  船停泊在岛的背风处。| 近义词: sheltered side, lee side | 反义词: windward side | 词性: 名词

词源: "Lee" comes from Middle English *lē*, from Old English *hlēow*, meaning "shelter, protection." "Angle" comes from Old French *angle*, from Latin *angulus*, meaning "corner, angle."  Thus, "leeangle" refers to the angled area sheltered from the wind.
