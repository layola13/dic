biblike:/ˈbɪblaɪk/| adj.|圣经的，像圣经的|Of, relating to, or resembling the Bible.|The language used was biblike in its formality.  所使用的语言在形式上像圣经一样。|例句：His pronouncements were biblike in their authority. 他的声明在权威性方面如同圣经。

例句：The flood was of biblike proportions. 这场洪水规模巨大，如同圣经中描述的一样。|近义词：scriptural, biblical|反义词：secular, worldly|词性：adjective
