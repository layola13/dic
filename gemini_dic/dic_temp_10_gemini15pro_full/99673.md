interlineation:/ɪnˌtɜːrˌlɪnɪˈeɪʃn/ | n. |插入；插写；行间书写；加行文字 |The act of writing something between the lines of a text, often as an addition or correction. |例句：The interlineation in the contract was barely noticeable.  合同中的插写几乎看不出来。

例句：The lawyer questioned the validity of the contract due to the interlineation.  由于存在插写，律师质疑合同的有效性。

近义词：insertion, addition, interpolation |反义词: deletion, erasure |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *interlineatio, from Latin interlinēāre (“to write between lines”), from inter (“between”) + linea (“line”).
