destroyingly:/dɪˈstrɔɪɪŋli/
adverb
毁灭性地；破坏性地
in a way that causes a lot of damage or destruction.
The fire spread destroyingly through the forest. 大火毁灭性地在森林中蔓延。
近义词: devastatingly, ruinously, catastrophically
反义词: constructively, beneficially
词性: adverb

destroyingly:/dɪˈstrɔɪɪŋli/
词源: From destroying + -ly.  "Destroying" comes from the verb "destroy," which has its roots in Old French "destruire" and ultimately from Latin "destruere" (de- "down" + struere "to build").
中文翻译:毁灭性地；破坏性地
英文解析: In a manner that destroys or ruins something completely.  It describes the way in which an action is carried out, emphasizing the destructive nature of that action.
例句:
The hurricane swept through the town, destroyingly affecting homes and businesses.  飓风席卷了整个城镇，对房屋和企业造成了毁灭性的影响。

The criticism was delivered destroyingly, shattering her confidence.  批评以毁灭性的方式提出，粉碎了她的自信。

反义词: constructively, beneficially, helpfully
