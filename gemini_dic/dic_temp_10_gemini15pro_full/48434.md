deductible:/dɪˈdʌktəbl/ | n. adj.|免赔额，可扣除的|The amount you have to pay out-of-pocket for expenses before your insurance coverage kicks in.  指保险生效前你需要自己支付的费用金额。|例句：My health insurance has a $500 deductible. 我的健康保险有500美元的免赔额。|近义词：excess, out-of-pocket expense|反义词：benefit, coverage|n.

deductible:/dɪˈdʌktəbl/ | adj.|可扣除的|Expenses that can be subtracted from your income when calculating taxes. 指计算税收时可以从收入中扣除的费用。|例句：Charitable donations are often deductible from your taxes.  慈善捐款通常可以从你的税款中扣除。|近义词：subtractable, allowable|反义词：non-deductible, taxable|adj.
