cassava:/kəˈsɑːvə/ | n. | 木薯，树薯 | A tropical plant with thick roots that are eaten as a vegetable.  The roots are also ground to make flour, tapioca, and other products. | Cassava is a staple food in many tropical countries. 木薯是许多热带国家的 staple food 主食。

Cassava flour is a good gluten-free option. 木薯粉是很好的无麸质选择。| 近义词: yuca, manioc | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun
