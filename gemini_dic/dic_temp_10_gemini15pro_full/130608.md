nonadherents:/ˌnɒnədˈhɪərənts/
n.
不遵守者；不服从者；未加入者
People who do not adhere to a particular belief, rule, or cause.
The nonadherents of the new policy voiced their concerns.  新的政策的未遵守者表达了他们的担忧。
The church welcomed both members and nonadherents to the community event. 教堂欢迎成员和非成员参加社区活动。
synonyms: dissenters, objectors, opponents, apostates, heretics, unbelievers, nonconformists, rebels, recusants, resisters
antonyms: adherents, followers, supporters, believers, conformists
