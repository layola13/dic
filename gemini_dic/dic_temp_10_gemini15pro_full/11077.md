archaeozoic:/ˌɑːrkiːəʊˈzoʊɪk/ | adj. n.| 太古代的；太古代 | Relating to or denoting the earliest eon in the history of the Earth, from its formation until about 2,500 million years ago.  Also can be used as a noun, referring to the Archaeozoic Eon itself. | The archaeozoic era saw the formation of the first continents. 太古代见证了最初大陆的形成。| The archaeozoic rocks are the oldest on Earth. 太古代的岩石是地球上最古老的。 | synonyms: Precambrian, primordial | antonyms: modern, recent | adjective, noun
