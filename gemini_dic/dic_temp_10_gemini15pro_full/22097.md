bolognese:/ˌbɒləˈneɪz/ /ˌboʊləˈneɪz/| adj. n.|博洛尼亚的；博洛尼亚肉酱|A rich meat sauce, typically used in pasta dishes, originating from Bologna, Italy.  It usually includes ground meat (often beef or pork), tomatoes, onions, and herbs.|例句：I made spaghetti bolognese for dinner. 我晚饭做了意大利肉酱面。|近义词：meat sauce, ragu|反义词:None|词性:adjective, noun
