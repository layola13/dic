demotist: /ˈdɛməˌtɪst/ | n. |平民主义者；民众主义者| A person who believes in or advocates for demotism (rule by the people, especially the common people). |  The demotist argued for greater representation of the working class in government.  平民主义者主张工人阶级在政府中有更大的代表性。 | synonym: populist, democrat | antonym: elitist, aristocrat | noun
