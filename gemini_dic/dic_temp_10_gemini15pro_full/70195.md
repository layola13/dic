fibrins:/ˈfaɪbrɪnz/
n.
纤维蛋白
Insoluble fibrous protein formed from fibrinogen by the action of thrombin, forming the essential portion of the blood clot.
A mesh of fibrin forms to stop bleeding.  纤维蛋白网状结构形成以止血。
Fibrin is essential for blood clotting. 纤维蛋白对血液凝固至关重要。
Near Synonyms:  fibrinogen,  coagulant
Antonyms: anticoagulant
noun
