burgess:/ˈbɜːrdʒɪs/| n.|议员|A member of a legislative body, typically representing a borough.|He served as a burgess for many years. 他担任议员多年。|Synonyms: representative, councilor, assemblyman, legislator|Antonyms: constituent, voter, elector|noun
