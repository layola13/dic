elusion:/ɪˈluːʒən/ | n. | 逃避；躲避；回避|The act of escaping or avoiding something, especially by cleverness or deceit. | He managed to create an elusion of the paparazzi by ducking into a side street. 他巧妙地躲进一条小巷，避开了狗仔队的追踪。|  His sudden disappearance was a clever elusion of his responsibilities. 他突然消失是巧妙地逃避责任。 | synonyms: evasion, avoidance, escape, dodging | antonyms: confrontation, facing, meeting | noun

elusion:/ɪˈluːʒən/ | n. | 幻觉；错觉 | A misleading impression or belief. | The magician created the elusion of sawing a woman in half. 魔术师制造了把一个女人锯成两半的幻觉。| He was under the elusion that he was popular. 他错误地认为自己很受欢迎。| synonyms: illusion, deception, delusion, fallacy | antonyms: reality, truth, fact | noun
