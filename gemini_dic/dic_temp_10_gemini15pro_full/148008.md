overrigorous:/ˌoʊvərˈrɪɡərəs/| adj.| 过于严谨的，过于严格的| Excessively rigorous; too strict or precise.  Characterized by an excessively high standard of precision or accuracy, often to the point of being impractical or unnecessarily demanding.|  His overrigorous approach to grammar made his writing stiff and formal. 他对语法过于严谨的态度使他的写作显得僵硬刻板。|  The overrigorous training regime led to several injuries among the athletes. 过于严苛的训练方案导致几名运动员受伤。| 近义词:  strict, stringent, demanding, exacting, inflexible, rigid| 反义词: lenient, lax, flexible, tolerant| 词性: 形容词
