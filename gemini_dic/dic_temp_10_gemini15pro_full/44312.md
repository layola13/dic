crooningly:/ˈkruːnɪŋli/ | adv. | 哼着地，低吟地 | In a soft, gentle singing voice.  | He crooningly sang a lullaby to his baby daughter. 他低声哼唱着摇篮曲哄他的宝贝女儿睡觉。| 近义词：softly, gently, murmuringly | 反义词：loudly, harshly | 词性: 副词

crooningly:/ˈkruːnɪŋli/ | adv. | 哼着地，低吟地 | In a soft, gentle singing voice, often with a sentimental quality.  | She crooningly delivered the romantic ballad, captivating the audience. 她低吟浅唱着那首浪漫的民谣，深深吸引了观众。| 近义词：melodically, sweetly | 反义词：shrilly, raucously | 词性: 副词

词源: From croon + -ing + -ly.  Croon comes from Middle Dutch *kronen, of imitative origin.
