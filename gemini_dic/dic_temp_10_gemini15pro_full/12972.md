asthmas:/ˈæzməz/ |n.|哮喘（复数，医学上更常用单数形式 asthma）|A chronic respiratory condition marked by spasms in the bronchi of the lungs, causing difficulty in breathing. It is usually connected with allergic reactions or other forms of hypersensitivity.|His asthmas were acting up because of the pollen. 他的哮喘由于花粉而发作。|近义词: asthma (singular form, generally preferred), respiratory disease|反义词: healthy lungs, normal breathing|词性: noun

词源: From the Greek word *ázma*, meaning "panting" or "shortness of breath."
