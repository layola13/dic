nonpantheistic:/ˌnɒnpænˈθiːɪstɪk/
adjective
非泛神论的
Not pantheistic; characterized by the belief that God is distinct from the universe.
The philosopher argued for a nonpantheistic worldview, emphasizing the separation of the creator from creation.
近义词:non-pantheistic, non-panentheistic
反义词:pantheistic, panentheistic
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From non- + pantheistic.  "Pantheistic" comes from the Greek "pan" (all) + "theos" (god), meaning "all is god."  The prefix "non-" negates this, meaning "not all is god."
