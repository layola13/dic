nonvitality:/ˌnɒnvaɪˈtælɪti/
noun
缺乏活力;死气沉沉
Lack of vitality or liveliness; the state of being nonvital.
The nonvitality of the town was palpable, with closed shops and empty streets.  这个城镇死气沉沉，商店关门，街道空空荡荡。
Synonyms: lifelessness, inertia, inactivity, dullness
Antonyms: vitality, liveliness, energy, vigor
