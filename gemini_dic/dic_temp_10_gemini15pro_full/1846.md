acromegaly: /ˌækrəʊˈmɛɡəli/
n.
肢端肥大症
A hormonal disorder that develops when your pituitary gland produces too much growth hormone during adulthood. When this happens, your bones increase in size, including those of your hands, feet and face.
Acromegaly is a rare condition that affects about 60 people per million. 肢端肥大症是一种罕见的疾病，每百万人中约有 60 人患病。

Acromegaly is often difficult to diagnose in the early stages because the changes in facial features are often subtle. 肢端肥大症通常在早期难以诊断，因为面部特征的变化通常很微妙。

近义词: gigantism (especially in children)
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider dwarfism for contrasting condition)
词性: noun
