nonforfeiture:/ˌnɑːnfɔːrˈfɪtʃər/ | n. | 不可没收性，不丧失权利 | The condition of an insurance policy that allows the policyholder to retain certain benefits even if premium payments are discontinued.  A policyholder may be able to receive a reduced paid-up policy, extended term insurance, or a cash surrender value. |  Example: The nonforfeiture clause in my life insurance policy guarantees I won't lose all my investment if I miss a few payments. 我的寿险保单中的不可没收条款保证了即使我错过几次付款，我也不会损失所有的投资。

Example:  Understanding the nonforfeiture options is crucial when choosing a life insurance policy.  了解不可没收选项对于选择人寿保险单至关重要。| 近义词: incontestability, guaranteed value | 反义词: forfeiture, lapse | 词性: 名词
