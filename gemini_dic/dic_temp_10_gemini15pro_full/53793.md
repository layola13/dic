discomposure:/ˌdɪskəmˈpoʊʒər/ (US), /ˌdɪskəmˈpəʊʒə(r)/ (UK)| n. |不安；慌乱；沉不住气|A state of being flustered, agitated, or unsettled; lack of composure.|例句：His discomposure was evident in his trembling hands. 他的双手颤抖，显得很不安。

例句：The unexpected news caused her considerable discomposure.  这突如其来的消息使她非常不安。

近义词：agitation, perturbation, anxiety, uneasiness, distress|反义词：composure, calmness, tranquility, serenity|词性：名词
