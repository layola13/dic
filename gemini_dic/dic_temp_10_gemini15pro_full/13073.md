astrologic:/ˌæstrəˈlɑːdʒɪk/ | adj. |  占星的，占星术的 | Relating to or based on astrology; astrological. |例句：She made an astrologic chart for her newborn baby. 她为她刚出生的宝宝制作了一张占星图。|例句：He has an astrologic interpretation of the current events. 他对时事有占星学的解读。| 近义词：astral, horoscopic | 反义词：scientific, empirical | 词性: adjective

astrology:/əˈstrɑːlədʒi/ | n. | 占星术，占星学 | The study of the movements and relative positions of celestial bodies interpreted as having an influence on human affairs and the natural world. |例句：Many people find comfort and guidance in astrology. 很多人在占星学中找到安慰和指引。|例句：She's been studying astrology for over ten years. 她研究占星学已经超过十年了。| 近义词：horoscopy | 反义词：astronomy | 词性: noun
