Present Continuous:
He is apparently working on a new project. 他看来正在做一个新项目。
They are apparently having a good time. 他们似乎玩得很开心。

Present Perfect:

She has apparently forgotten about the meeting. 她似乎已经忘记了会议。

Present Perfect Continuous:

Past Continuous:

Past Perfect:

Simple Future:

She will apparently be traveling next week. 她显然下周要去旅行。


Future Continuous:
He will apparently be working late tomorrow. 他明天显然会工作到很晚。

Future Perfect:

Future Perfect Continuous:

Subjunctive Mood:
If he were apparently richer, he would buy a new car. 如果他看起来更富有，他会买一辆新车。

Passive Voice:

Interrogative Sentence:
Is he apparently happy? 他看起来快乐吗？
Was she apparently upset? 她看起来难过吗？


Negative Sentence:
He is apparently not happy. 他看起来不快乐。
She was apparently not upset. 她看起来不难过。
