iconoclast: /aɪˈkɒnəklæst/ (UK) /aɪˈkɑːnəklæst/ (US)| n.| 破坏偶像者；打破传统观念者|A person who attacks cherished beliefs or institutions.|The young director was an iconoclast, challenging the traditional methods of filmmaking.|Synonyms: rebel, dissenter, nonconformist, radical, revolutionary|Antonyms: conformist, traditionalist|noun


iconoclastic: /aɪˌkɒnəˈklæstɪk/ (UK) /aɪˌkɑːnəˈklæstɪk/ (US)| adj.|  打破传统观念的；偶像破坏的|Characterized by attack on cherished beliefs or institutions.|Her iconoclastic views on education often sparked controversy.|Synonyms: revolutionary, rebellious, unconventional, unorthodox|Antonyms: conventional, orthodox, traditional|adjective
