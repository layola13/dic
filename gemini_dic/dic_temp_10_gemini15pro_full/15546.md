baggily:/ˈbæɡɪli/| adv.|松垮地；袋状地|in a baggy way; loosely and hanging in folds. |Her jeans hung baggily on her thin frame. 她的牛仔裤松垮地挂在她瘦削的身上。|loosely, slackly|tightly, snugly|副词
baggily:|/ˈbæɡɪli/|adv.|松垮地；袋状地|Characterized by looseness or hanging folds, like a bag.  The sweatpants hung baggily on his legs.  他的运动裤松垮地挂在腿上。|loosely, slackly|tightly, snugly|副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From baggy + -ly.  Baggy itself comes from bag, referring to something resembling or shaped like a bag.
