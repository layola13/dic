discorporate:/dɪskɔːrpəreɪt/
verb
剥夺法人资格；使脱离公司关系；使脱离实体
To deprive of corporate rights and privileges; to separate from a body or corporation.
The government discorporated the failing company. 政府取消了这家倒闭公司的法人资格。
The soul is said to discorporate at death. 据说灵魂在死亡时脱离肉体。
disestablish, disembody, disincorporate
incorporate, embody
