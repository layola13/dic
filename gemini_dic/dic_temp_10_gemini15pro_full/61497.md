embroiders:/ɪmˈbrɔɪdərz/
verb
刺绣；渲染；润色
decorates with needlework; embellishes a story
She embroiders beautiful designs onto silk scarves. 她把美丽的图案绣在丝绸围巾上。
近义词: stitches, decorates, embellishes, adorns
反义词: simplifies, strips (down), understates
词性: verb (动词)

embroidery:/ɪmˈbrɔɪdəri/
noun
刺绣；刺绣品；润色
decorative needlework; embellishment of a story
The embroidery on her dress was exquisite. 她衣服上的刺绣精美绝伦。
近义词: needlework, needlepoint, adornment, embellishment
反义词: plainness, simplicity
词性: noun (名词)

词源:
From Old French *embroidure, from embroder "to embroider," from en- "in, on" + broder "to border, edge," from bord "border."
