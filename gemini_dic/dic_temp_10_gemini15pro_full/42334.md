counter-walk:/ˈkaʊntərˌwɔːk/ /ˈkaʊntərˌwɑːk/ | n. |逆行，反方向行走|The act of walking in the opposite direction of the usual flow of pedestrian traffic. | Example:  Counter-walk can be dangerous in crowded areas. 在拥挤的地方逆行很危险。

Example:  He made his way through the bustling market by counter-walk. 他逆行穿过繁忙的市场。| 近义词: walk against the flow, reverse walk | 反义词: walk with the flow, follow the flow | 词性: 名词
