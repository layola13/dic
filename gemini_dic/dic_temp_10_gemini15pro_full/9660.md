antisyndicalists:/ˌæntiˈsɪndɪkəlɪsts/
|n.|反工团主义者；反对工会的人|People who oppose syndicalism, a political movement advocating worker control of industry through labor unions.|The antisyndicalists believed that unions posed a threat to the established social order.|近义词:anti-unionists, conservatives (in some contexts)|反义词:syndicalists, unionists, labor activists|noun
