macadam:/məˈkædəm/ | n. | 碎石路 | A road surface made of small pieces of broken stone, often bound together with tar or bitumen. |  The old macadam road was bumpy and uneven.  这条古老的碎石路崎岖不平。| synonyms:  gravel road, stone road | antonyms: asphalt road, paved road | noun

macadamize:/məˈkædəˌmaɪz/ | vt. | 用碎石铺路 | To cover (a road) with macadam. | They macadamized the lane leading to the farm.  他们用碎石铺设了通往农场的道路。 | synonyms: pave, surface | antonyms: dig up, excavate | verb
