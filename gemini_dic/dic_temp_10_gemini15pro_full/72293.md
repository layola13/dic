flouncier:/ˈflaʊnsiər/
adjective
更加炫耀的；更爱虚张声势的
more flouncy; more ostentatious
The dress she wore to the gala was even flouncier than last year's. 她参加晚会的礼服比去年的还要华丽。
He became even flouncier after receiving the promotion.  升职后他变得更加炫耀了。
Near Synonyms: showier, flashier, more ostentatious
Near Antonyms: simpler, plainer, more understated
word class: adjective


flounce:/flaʊns/
noun
荷叶边；裙褶；装饰褶边
a strip of gathered or pleated material attached to a garment, typically around the hem, for decoration
Her skirt had a delicate flounce at the bottom. 她的裙子下摆有一圈精致的荷叶边。
The curtains were trimmed with a velvet flounce. 窗帘上装饰着天鹅绒的褶边。
Near Synonyms: ruffle, frill, furbelow
Near Antonyms: plain edge, straight hem

verb
(因生气或不耐烦而) 猛地移动，跺(脚)，(鸟)扑动翅膀
to move impatiently or angrily with exaggerated movements; (of a bird) to flap the wings
She flounced out of the room in a huff. 她怒气冲冲地跺着脚走出房间。
The bird flounced its wings and took flight. 那只鸟扑打着翅膀飞了起来。
Near Synonyms:  storm, stomp, swagger, (for birds) flutter
Near Antonyms: tiptoe, glide, slink
