lepidoganoid:/ˌlɛpɪdoʊˈɡænɔɪd/
adjective
鳞状硬鳞的；具有硬鳞的
Covered with thick, bony, rhomboid scales, characteristic of certain primitive fishes.  These scales are composed of a bony basal layer, a layer of dentine, and a superficial layer of ganoine.
The coelacanth is a lepidoganoid fish.  腔棘鱼是一种硬鳞鱼。
近义词:ganoid, scale-covered
反义词:scaleless
词性:adjective
