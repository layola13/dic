cybernetically:/ˌsaɪbərˈnɛtɪkli/
adverb
控制论地；通过控制论
In a way that relates to cybernetics, the study of control and communication systems in living organisms and machines.
The robot arm was cybernetically controlled.  机械臂由控制论控制。
The artist explored the idea of a cybernetically enhanced human. 艺术家探索了控制论增强人类的想法。

近义词: automatically, electronically
反义词: manually, naturally
词性: adverb

词源:  From cybernetics + -ally.  Cybernetics from Greek kubernetes (steersman, pilot, governor).
