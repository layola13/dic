hanukkah:/ˈhɑːnəkə/ (US), /ˈhɑːnʊkə/ (UK)| n.|光明节|A Jewish festival lasting eight days and nights, commemorating the rededication of the Second Temple in Jerusalem in the second century BC.|We celebrate Hanukkah by lighting candles on the menorah. 我们通过在烛台上点燃蜡烛来庆祝光明节。|Near Synonyms: Feast of Dedication, Festival of Lights|Antonym: N/A|noun
