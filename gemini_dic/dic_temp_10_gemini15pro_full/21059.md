blindfolded:/ˈblaɪndˌfoʊldɪd/| adj.|蒙住眼睛的，被蒙住眼睛的|With one's eyes covered with a piece of cloth, unable to see. |She walked a tightrope blindfolded. 她蒙着眼睛走钢丝。|
The children played a game of blindfolded tag. 孩子们玩蒙眼抓人游戏。|
Synonyms: sightless, unseeing, eyeless (in this context)|Antonyms: sighted, seeing|adjective
