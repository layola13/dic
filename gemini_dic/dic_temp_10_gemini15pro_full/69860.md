ferromagnets:/ˌferoʊˈmæɡnɪts/ | n. | 铁磁体 | Materials that exhibit strong attraction to magnets and can retain their magnetic properties after being magnetized.  |  Iron, nickel, and cobalt are common ferromagnets. 铁、镍和钴是常见的铁磁体。 | synonyms: magnetic materials,  | antonyms: diamagnets, paramagnets | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From ferro- + magnet.  "Ferro-" comes from Latin "ferrum" meaning "iron".
