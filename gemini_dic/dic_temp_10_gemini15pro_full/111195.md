lithological:/ˌlɪθəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
岩性的
Relating to the study of rocks, especially their formation, composition, and classification.
The lithological characteristics of the area suggest volcanic activity millions of years ago.  该地区的岩性特征表明数百万年前曾有火山活动。
近义词:petrographic, petrologic
反义词:None
词性:adjective

lithology:/lɪˈθɒlədʒi/
noun
岩性学
The study of rocks, especially their formation, composition, and classification.
The geologist specialized in the lithology of sedimentary rocks. 这位地质学家专门研究沉积岩的岩性。
近义词:petrography, petrology
反义词:None
词性:noun
