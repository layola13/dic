misreported:/ˌmɪsrɪˈpɔːr.tɪd/
|adj.|错误报道的；失实报道的|reported incorrectly or falsely. |The incident was misreported in the media.  这事件被媒体错误报道了。|Synonyms: distorted, falsified, inaccurate, untrue|Antonyms: accurate, correctly reported, truthful, veracious
|verb (past participle)|

misreport:/ˌmɪsrɪˈpɔːrt/
|vt.|错误地报道；失实地报道|to report (something) incorrectly or falsely.|The journalist misreported the facts of the case. 记者错误地报道了案件的事实。|Synonyms: distort, falsify, misrepresent|Antonyms: report accurately, report truthfully
|verb|
