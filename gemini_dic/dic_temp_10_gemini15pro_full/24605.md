broil:/brɔɪl/| v. n.|烧烤，烤制，炙烤|To cook by direct exposure to radiant heat, as over a grill or under a broiler.|例句：I broiled the steak for five minutes on each side. 我把牛排两面各烤了五分钟。

例句：We're going to broil some burgers tonight. 我们今晚要烤一些汉堡。|近义词：grill, barbecue, roast|反义词：freeze, chill|词性：动词，名词
