al gusto:/æl ˈɡuːstoʊ/ /æl ˈɡʌstoʊ/
|adv.| 适量地，随意地；合口味地|To one's liking; according to one's taste.|The chef seasoned the dish al gusto. 厨师根据自己的口味给菜肴调味。|
|Synonyms: to taste, as you like, to one's liking|
|Antonyms: excessively, immoderately|
|词性: 副词 (adverb)|
