concession:/kənˈseʃən/ | n. | 让步，妥协；特许权；优惠；承认；（体育比赛等的）场地使用权 | Something granted, especially in response to demands; a thing conceded.  A preferential allowance or rate given by an organization. | The company made several concessions to the workers.  公司对工人们做出了一些让步。| synonyms: compromise, allowance, adjustment, exception | antonyms: disagreement, refusal, denial | noun

concession:/kənˈseʃən/ | n. |  （尤指体育比赛等的）场地使用权；销售权；经营权 | A business operating within the premises of a larger concern. | The stadium has several food concessions. 体育场有几个食品摊位。| synonyms: franchise, lease, permit | antonyms: prohibition, restriction | noun
