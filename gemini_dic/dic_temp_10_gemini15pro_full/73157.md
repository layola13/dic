forbearance:/fɔːrˈbɛərəns/ | n. |  宽容；忍耐；克制 |  The act of refraining from exercising a legal right, especially enforcing the payment of a debt.  Patience, tolerance, or self-control, especially in not retaliating. | The bank showed forbearance by not foreclosing on their mortgage when they lost their jobs. 银行在他们失业时没有取消抵押品赎回权，表现出了宽容。 |  patience, tolerance, leniency, indulgence |  impatience, intolerance, harshness | noun
