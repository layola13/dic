overthoughtfulnesses: /ˌoʊvərˈθɔːtfʊlnəsɪz/ | n. | 过度思虑，想太多 | The state of having thought about something too much, often to the point of anxiety or indecision.  Excessive contemplation or reflection. | Her overthoughtfulnesses prevented her from making a quick decision. 她想太多，以至于无法快速做出决定。 | Synonyms: rumination, brooding, agonizing,  excessive deliberation | Antonyms: decisiveness, spontaneity, impulsiveness | noun
