haya:/ˈhaɪə/| n.|哈雅，羞耻，谦逊|A feeling of shyness and modesty; a reluctance to act in a presumptuous or forward manner. |Example: She accepted the compliment with haya. 她谦虚地接受了赞美。|Example:  He displayed great haya in the presence of his elders.  他在长辈面前表现得非常谦逊。|Synonyms: modesty, humility, diffidence, shyness|Antonyms: boldness, arrogance, presumptuousness|noun
