clucky:/ˈklʌki/| adj.|母鸡似的；咯咯叫的；渴望做母亲的|Of a hen, brooding or inclined to brood; (of a woman) feeling broody or maternal.|例句：The hen became clucky and started sitting on the eggs. 母鸡变得咯咯叫，开始孵蛋。

例句：She's been feeling very clucky since her sister had a baby. 自从她姐姐生了孩子后，她就一直很想当妈妈。|近义词：broody, maternal|反义词:apathetic, indifferent|词性:adjective
clucking:/ˈklʌkɪŋ/ |n.|咯咯声；咯咯叫|The characteristic sound made by a hen, especially when brooding.|例句：The clucking of the hens was a comforting sound. 母鸡的咯咯声令人感到安慰。

例句：I could hear the soft clucking of a hen nearby. 我能听到附近母鸡轻轻的咯咯叫声。|近义词：cackling|反义词:silence|词性:noun
