biofouler:/ˈbaɪoʊˌfaʊlər/| n. |生物污损生物|An organism that contributes to biofouling, the accumulation of unwanted organisms on submerged surfaces.|Barnacles are common biofoulers on ship hulls. 藤壶是船体上常见的生物污损生物。|Synonyms: fouling organism, biofouling organism|Antonyms: N/A|noun
