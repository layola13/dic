besetter:/bɪˈsɛtər/
noun
困扰者，折磨者
One that besets; a besieger or attacker.
The knight errant faced his besetter, a fearsome dragon.  这位游侠骑士面对着他的困扰者，一条可怕的巨龙。
besieger, attacker, harasser, annoyer
protector, defender, guardian

Etymology:
From the verb "beset" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Beset" comes from Old English "besettan," meaning "to set around," from "be-" (around) + "settan" (to set).
