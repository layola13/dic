nonreceptiveness:/ˌnɑːnrisɛpˈtɪvnəs/ | n. |  不接受；不接纳; 冷漠 | The state of being unwilling or unable to receive or accept something, particularly ideas, suggestions, or criticism. | His nonreceptiveness to new ideas hindered the project's progress.  他对于新想法的不接受阻碍了项目的进展。| synonyms: unresponsiveness, resistance, indifference, hostility, closed-mindedness | antonyms: receptiveness, openness, acceptance, willingness, enthusiasm | noun
