overcloying: /ˌoʊvərˈklɔɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 过于甜腻的，令人腻烦的 | Excessively sweet, rich, or sentimental to the point of being unpleasant.  Derived from "cloy," meaning to cause disgust or aversion through excess.  | The overcloying sweetness of the dessert made me feel sick. 这道甜点甜得发腻，让我觉得恶心。 |  The romance novel was so overcloying that I couldn't finish it. 这部爱情小说太腻歪了，我没能看完。 | 近义词: saccharine, cloying, sickly sweet, treacly | 反义词:  subtle, understated, bland, dry | 词性: adjective
