non-hygienic:/ˌnɒn haɪˈdʒiːnɪk/ /ˌnɑːn haɪˈdʒenɪk/| adj.|不卫生的，不健康的|Not clean or sanitary; likely to cause disease.|例句：The restaurant was closed down due to non-hygienic conditions.  这家餐厅由于不卫生的条件而被关闭。

例句：Storing raw meat next to cooked food is non-hygienic. 将生肉储存在熟食旁边是不卫生的。|近义词：insanitary, unhealthy, unsanitary|反义词：hygienic, sanitary, clean|词性:形容词
