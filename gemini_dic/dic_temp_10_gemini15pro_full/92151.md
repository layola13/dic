hypersensuous:/ˌhaɪpərˈsɛnʃuəs/| adj.|过度感官的，极度感性的| Appealing strongly to the senses; excessively refined or aesthetically sensitive.  Characterized by an extreme awareness and appreciation of beauty, often to the point of being overly refined or decadent.|The poet's hypersensuous language evoked a world of intoxicating beauty. 这位诗人极度感性的语言唤起了一个令人陶醉的美丽世界。|The film is a hypersensuous feast for the eyes. 这部电影是一场视觉的盛宴。|近义词：sensual, voluptuous, decadent|反义词：ascetic, austere|词性: adjective
Etymology:  From hyper- "over, excessive" + sensuous.
