eulogizer:/ˈjuːləˌdʒaɪzər/
noun
颂扬者，赞美者
A person who delivers a eulogy, a speech or piece of writing that praises someone or something highly, typically someone who has recently died.
The eulogizer spoke movingly about the deceased's contributions to the community. 这位颂扬者动情地讲述了死者对社区的贡献。
synonyms: praiser, lauder, encomiast, panegyrist
antonyms: critic, detractor, denouncer
