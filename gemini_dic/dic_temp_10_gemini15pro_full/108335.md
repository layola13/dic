lavaret:/ˈlævərɪt/ | n. |lavaret; whitefish; gwiniad|A freshwater fish of the salmon family, found in lakes in northern Europe.  Specifically, it refers to several forms of whitefish in the *Coregonus* genus.|例句：The lavaret is a popular game fish in Finland.  拉瓦雷特是芬兰一种受欢迎的游钓鱼。

例句：Lavaret are often caught using nets. 拉瓦雷特经常用网捕捞。|近义词：whitefish, cisco, coregonus|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
