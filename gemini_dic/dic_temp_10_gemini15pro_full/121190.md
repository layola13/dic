词源:  "mis-" (badly, wrongly) + "do" (act, deed).  "Mis-" comes from Old English "mis-", meaning "wrongly, badly, amiss." "Do" comes from Old English "dōn," meaning "to do, perform, make."  Therefore, "misdo" literally means a "wrong deed."
