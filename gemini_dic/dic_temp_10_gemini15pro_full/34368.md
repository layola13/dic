classicist:/ˈklæsɪsɪst/| n.|古典主义者；古典学者|A person who studies classical literature, art, or culture, especially that of ancient Greece or Rome.  A scholar or artist who works in a classical style or tradition.|例句：The classicist argued that the poem was a perfect example of epic form.  这位古典主义学者认为这首诗是史诗的完美典范。

例句：She's a renowned classicist, specializing in Roman architecture. 她是一位著名的古典学者，专攻罗马建筑。|近义词：scholar, humanist, traditionalist|反义词：modernist, avant-gardist|词性: Noun
