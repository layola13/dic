insincerity:/ˌɪnsɪnˈsɛrəti/
n.
不真诚，虚伪
lack of sincerity; the quality of not expressing genuine feelings
Her insincerity was evident in her forced smile. 她的假笑显露出她的不真诚。

近义词:dishonesty, falseness, hypocrisy, duplicity
反义词:sincerity, honesty, genuineness, candor
词性:noun
