agenesis:/eɪˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
n.
先天性器官缺失，发育不全
The absence or partial development of an organ or body part during fetal development.
Congenital absence of the corpus callosum is a rare brain malformation called agenesis of the corpus callosum.  先天性胼胝体缺失是一种罕见的脑畸形，称为胼胝体发育不全。
Renal agenesis is a birth defect in which one or both kidneys fail to develop. 肾发育不全是一种出生缺陷，其中一个或两个肾脏未能发育。
近义词: aplasia, hypoplasia
反义词: hyperplasia
词性: noun

词源:  From a- (“without”) +‎ -genesis (“origin, development”).  来自 a-（“没有”）+ -genesis（“起源，发展”）。
