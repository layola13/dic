misjudgers:/ˌmɪsˈdʒʌdʒərz/
|n.|误判者，判断错误的人|People who make incorrect judgments or assessments of something or someone.  Those who miscalculate or form inaccurate opinions.|The misjudgers underestimated the strength of their opponent and lost the match. 误判者低估了对手的实力，输掉了比赛。|Synonyms: misinterpreters, miscalculators, blunderers|Antonyms: assessors, evaluators, judges|Noun
