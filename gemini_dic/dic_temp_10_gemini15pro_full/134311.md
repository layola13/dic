noninsistent:/ˌnɒnɪnˈsɪstənt/ /ˌnɑːnɪnˈsɪstənt/| adj.|不坚持的，不强求的|Not insistent; not pressing or demanding.|She made a noninsistent request for a raise. 她提出了一个不强求的加薪请求。|He offered a noninsistent suggestion. 他提出了一个不坚持的建议。|近义词：unassuming, modest, undemanding|反义词：insistent, persistent, demanding|词性:形容词

词源:
From non- + insistent.  "Insistent" comes from the Latin "insistere" meaning "to stand upon, persist."  The prefix "non-" negates the meaning.
