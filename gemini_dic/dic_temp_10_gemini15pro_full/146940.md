overinterestedly: /ˌoʊvərˈɪntrɪstɪdli/ | adv. | 过分感兴趣地，过分关注地 | Showing excessive or undue interest.  It implies a level of interest that is inappropriate or intrusive. | He stared overinterestedly at the private conversation across the table. 他过分关注地盯着桌子对面私密的谈话。 | She questioned him overinterestedly about his personal life. 她过分关注地询问他的私人生活。 | 近义词: nosily, inquisitively, intrusively | 反义词: apathetically, indifferently | 词性: 副词

over-: /ˈoʊvər/ | prefix | 过于，过度 |  A prefix indicating "too much," "excessively," or "beyond a normal or desirable limit." |  overeat (吃得太多), oversleep (睡过头)

interestedly: /ˈɪntrɪstɪdli/ | adv. | 感兴趣地 | In a way that shows interest or curiosity. |  She listened interestedly to his story. 她饶有兴趣地听着他的故事。 |  He watched the game interestedly. 他饶有兴趣地观看了比赛。

interested: /ˈɪntrɪstɪd/ | adj. | 感兴趣的 | Showing curiosity or concern about something or someone. | I'm interested in learning more about history. 我有兴趣了解更多关于历史的知识。

interest: /ˈɪntrɪst/ | n. | 兴趣，利益 |  The feeling of wanting to know or learn about something or someone. | He has a keen interest in music. 他对音乐很感兴趣。 | 词源: From Old French interesse, ultimately from Latin interesse "to be of importance."
