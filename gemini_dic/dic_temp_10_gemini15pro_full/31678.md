chauffer: /ˈʃoʊfər/ (US) /ˈʃəʊfər/ (UK)| n. v.|司机；驾驶；加热|A person employed to drive a private or hired car.  To act as a chauffeur for someone. To warm something through.|例句：He works as a chauffeur for a wealthy family. 他在一个富裕家庭当司机。|近义词：driver, operator|反义词:passenger|词性: noun, verb

chauffer: /ˈʃoʊfər/ (US) /ˈʃəʊfər/ (UK)| v.|加热|To warm something through, especially wine.|例句：The wine was slightly chauffeured before serving.  这酒上桌前稍微加热了一下。|近义词：warm, heat|反义词:cool, chill|词性: verb
