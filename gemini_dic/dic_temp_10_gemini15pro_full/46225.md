cystitis:/sɪˈstaɪtɪs/
noun
膀胱炎
Inflammation of the urinary bladder.
She was prescribed antibiotics for her cystitis. 她被开了抗生素治疗膀胱炎。
近义词:bladder infection, urinary tract infection (UTI)
反义词:(None, as it's a medical condition)
词性:noun
cystitis:/sɪˈstaɪtɪs/
词源:From Greek kystis "bladder" + -itis "inflammation".
