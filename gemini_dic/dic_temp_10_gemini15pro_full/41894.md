cosily:/ˈkəʊzɪli/ (UK) /ˈkoʊzɪli/ (US)| adv.|舒适地，惬意地|In a cosy manner; snugly and comfortably.|We sat cosily by the fire. 我们舒适地坐在火炉旁。|例句：The cat curled up cosily on the rug. 猫咪舒服地蜷缩在地毯上。|近义词：snugly, comfortably, warmly|反义词：uncomfortably, coldly|词性:副词
词源: From cosy + -ly.  Cosy (also cozy) has a complex and debated etymology, possibly related to Scots cozie "snug, comfortable," of unknown origin.
