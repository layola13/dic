further:/ˈfɜːrðər/ (British) /ˈfɜːrðər/ (American)| adj. adv. v.|更远的，进一步的；促进，增进|A distance or space that is greater or longer.  To help the progress or development of something.|例句：We need to discuss this matter further. 我们需要进一步讨论这件事。|近义词：additional, more, advance, promote|反义词：less, nearer, hinder|词性:形容词, 副词, 动词
Etymology: From Middle English *ferther, furthre, from Old English furðor (“further, more advanced”), from Proto-Germanic *furþiz (“forth”). Cognate with Dutch verder (“further”), German verderb (“corrupted, spoiled”), Gothic 𐍆𐌰𐌹𐍂𐍂𐌰 (fairra, “far”).
