frisee:/friːˈzeɪ/| n. |菊苣，苦苣|A curly-leaved variety of endive.  A type of salad green with curly, frizzy leaves. |例句：The frisee salad with poached egg was delicious.  菊苣沙拉配水煮蛋很美味。

例句：She added some frisee to her mixed greens salad. 她在混合蔬菜沙拉里加了一些菊苣。|近义词：endive, chicory|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

Etymology: From French *frisée*, feminine past participle of *friser* "to curl," from Frankish *frisan*.
