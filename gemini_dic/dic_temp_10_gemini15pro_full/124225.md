mosquito:/məˈskiːtoʊ/ (US) /məˈskiːtəʊ/ (UK)| n. |蚊子|A small flying insect that bites people and animals and sucks their blood. Some types of mosquitoes spread diseases such as malaria.|
例句：
The mosquito buzzed annoyingly around my head.
蚊子在我头上嗡嗡作响，真烦人。

例句：
Mosquitoes are attracted to standing water.
蚊子会被积水吸引。

近义词：gnat, midge|反义词: (No direct antonym for a living creature) |词性:noun
