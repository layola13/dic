nestling:/ˈnestlɪŋ/| n.|雏鸟; 尚未自立的幼小动物(或幼儿);|A young bird that has not yet left the nest.  一只还未离巢的小鸟。|例句：The nestlings were chirping loudly for food. 雏鸟们大声唧唧喳喳地叫着要食物。

例句：The mother bird carefully fed the nestlings. 母鸟小心翼翼地喂养雏鸟。|近义词：chick, fledgling, young bird|反义词:adult bird|noun
