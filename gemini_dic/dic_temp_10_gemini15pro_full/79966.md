go-at:/ˈɡoʊæt/| n. | 攻击；尝试；努力 | An attempt or effort to do something. | He had a go at fixing the broken chair. 他尝试修理那把坏掉的椅子。 |  He made a go at starting his own business.  他尝试创办自己的企业。 |近义词：attempt, try, effort, endeavor |反义词: avoidance, inaction |词性:名词

Etymology:
From the verb "go" + "at" (preposition indicating direction or target).  The phrase originated in the late 19th century.
