metamorphoser:/ˌmɛtəˈmɔːrfəzər/| n.|变形者，使变形的人|A metamorphoser is someone or something that transforms or changes something else significantly.|The alchemist, a true metamorphoser, turned lead into gold.  炼金术士，一个真正的变形者，把铅变成了金。|The caterpillar is a natural metamorphoser, transforming into a butterfly. 毛毛虫是自然界的变形者，会变成蝴蝶。|Synonym: transformer, transmuter|Antonym: preserver, stayer|noun
