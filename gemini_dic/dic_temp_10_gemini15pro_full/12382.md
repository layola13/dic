ashtray:/ˈæʃtreɪ/| n.|烟灰缸|A small container for cigarette ashes and butts.|He tapped his cigarette ash into the ashtray. 他把烟灰磕到烟灰缸里。|近义词:butt can, ash receiver|反义词: None|词性:noun
Etymology: ash + tray.  "Ash" comes from Old English "æsce," meaning "ash." "Tray" comes from Old English "treg," meaning "wooden board."
