inscrutablenesses:/ɪnˈskruːtəbl̩nəsɪz/ | n. | 不可思议，难以理解，神秘莫测 |the quality of being impossible to understand or interpret |  His face remained an expression of complete inscrutableness. 他的脸上依然是一副完全让人摸不透的表情。| mystery, enigma, impenetrability | clarity, obviousness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From inscrutable + -ness.
Inscrutable:  Late Middle English: from late Latin inscrutabilis, from in- ‘not’ + scrutari ‘to search’ (from scruta ‘trash, rags’, perhaps influenced by the notion of searching through rubbish).
