mameluke:/ˈmæməluːk/| n.|马穆鲁克，奴隶兵|A member of a former military caste originally composed of slave soldiers from various ethnic backgrounds, notably Turkic, Circassian, and Georgian, who rose to prominence in the Islamic world, particularly in Egypt and Syria.|The Mamelukes ruled Egypt for centuries. 马穆鲁克统治了埃及几个世纪。|Synonyms: slave soldier, mercenary, warrior|Antonyms: civilian, citizen|Noun
