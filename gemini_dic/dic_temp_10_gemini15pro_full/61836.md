**2. Employed (English)**

employed: /ɪmˈplɔɪd/
adjective
Having a job; working for someone or a company.
Someone who is employed has a job.
例句：She is currently employed as a teacher. 她目前受雇为教师。

例句：He was employed by a large corporation. 他受雇于一家大公司。

近义词: working, hired
反义词: unemployed, jobless
词性: adjective
