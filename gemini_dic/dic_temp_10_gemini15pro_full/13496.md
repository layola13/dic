PHRASE->
attach to = 附属于;  He is attached to the marketing department. 他隶属于市场部。
attach importance to = 重视;  They attach great importance to customer service. 他们非常重视客户服务。
attach oneself to = 加入; 跟随;  The journalist attached himself to the army unit. 记者跟随部队一起行动。
attach significance to = 认为…重要;  I don't attach much significance to his opinion. 我不认为他的意见很重要。
attach blame to = 归咎于;  It's difficult to attach blame to any one person. 很难将责任归咎于任何一个人。
