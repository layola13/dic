flog:/flɒɡ/ (British), /flɑːɡ/ (American)| v. |鞭打，抽打；（非正式）严厉批评；（非正式）努力推销|To beat with a whip or stick, especially as punishment; to criticize harshly; to try hard to sell something.|The slave was flogged for disobedience.  奴隶因不服从而被鞭打。

The salesman flogged the new product at every opportunity.  推销员抓住每一个机会推销新产品。|近义词：whip, lash, beat, chastise, criticize, sell, promote|反义词：praise, commend|词性:动词
