greatcoat:/ˈɡreɪtˌkoʊt/| n. |大衣；厚外套|A heavy overcoat, typically double-breasted.|He wore a thick greatcoat against the biting wind. 他穿了一件厚厚的大衣以抵御刺骨的寒风。|Synonyms: overcoat, topcoat, ulster|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps light jacket, summer clothes)|noun
