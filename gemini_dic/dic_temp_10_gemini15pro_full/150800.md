paradoxides:/ˌpærəˈdɒksaɪdiːz/ | n. | 悖论虫属（三叶虫的一种）| An extinct genus of large trilobites, found as fossils in Middle Cambrian rocks.  It's known for its large size and distinctive cephalon (head shield). | Example: Paradoxides davidis is one of the largest trilobites ever found.  悖论虫戴维斯种是已发现的最大的三叶虫之一。
