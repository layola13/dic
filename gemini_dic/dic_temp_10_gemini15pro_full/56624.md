dongle: /ˈdɒŋɡəl/ (UK) /ˈdɑŋɡəl/ (US)| n. |加密狗；电子狗|A small piece of hardware that connects to a computer, usually by USB, to unlock software or access protected content. |

例句：
I need a dongle to use this software.
我需要一个加密狗来使用这个软件。

例句：
The dongle acts as a security key, preventing unauthorized access.
加密狗充当安全密钥，防止未经授权的访问。

近义词：adapter, hardware key, security key|
反义词: N/A|
词性: noun
