bourgeoisification:/ˌbʊərʒwɑːzɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
|n.|中产阶级化，资产阶级化|The process by which a neighborhood or city becomes more middle-class, often resulting in increased property values and displacement of lower-income residents.|The bourgeoisification of the neighborhood has led to a rise in rents. 这个街区的资产阶级化导致租金上涨。|gentrification,  upscaling|proletarianization, impoverishment|noun

bourgeoisified:/ˌbʊərʒwɑːzɪfaɪd/
|adj.|中产阶级化的，资产阶级化的|Having undergone bourgeoisification; characterized by the values and lifestyle of the middle class.|The once working-class neighborhood is now thoroughly bourgeoisified. 曾经的工薪阶级社区现在已经完全中产阶级化了。|gentrified, upscale|working-class, proletarian|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *bourgeoisification*, from *bourgeois* (bourgeois) + *-ification* (suffix forming nouns of action or process). Ultimately from Late Latin *burgensis* (inhabitant of a borough).
