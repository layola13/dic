depersonalised:/diːˈpɜːrsənəlaɪzd/ /diːˈpɜːrsnəˌlaɪzd/
adjective
去个性化的，失去人情味的
Divested of human characteristics or individuality; made impersonal.  The hospital felt cold and depersonalised.
The process had become increasingly depersonalised, with customers treated as numbers rather than individuals.
Synonym: impersonal, anonymous, inhuman, robotic, mechanized
Antonym: personal, individualised, humanized
词性: adjective
