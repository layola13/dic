bane:/beɪn/
n.
祸根；灾星；毒药
something that causes misery or death
例句：
The bane of his existence was his noisy neighbor.
他生活中最大的祸根就是他那吵闹的邻居。

Gambling is the bane of many lives.
赌博是许多人生活的祸根。

近义词：curse, plague, scourge, affliction, blight
反义词：blessing, boon, benefit, advantage

bane:/beɪn/
n.
(古语) 毒药；死亡
(archaic) poison; death
