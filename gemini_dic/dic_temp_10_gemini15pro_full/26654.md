cocoon:/kəˈkuːn/
n.
茧; 安乐窝
A silky case spun by the larva of many insects, as a protective covering during the pupal stage.  A place or situation of comfort and safety.
The caterpillar spins a cocoon around itself. 毛毛虫吐丝结茧把自己裹起来。
He wrapped himself in a cocoon of blankets. 他用毯子把自己裹得像个茧一样。
近义词:chrysalis, shell, sanctuary, refuge
反义词:exposure, vulnerability
词性: 名词
