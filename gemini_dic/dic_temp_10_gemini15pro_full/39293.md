conicality:/kɒnɪˈkælɪti/ /kɑːnɪˈkælɪti/
noun
锥度，锥形度
The property of being conical; the degree to which something is conical.  A measure of the angle of a cone.
The conicality of the drill bit must be precise for it to function correctly.  钻头的锥度必须精确才能正常工作。
conicity, taper
cylindricality, flatness
n.
