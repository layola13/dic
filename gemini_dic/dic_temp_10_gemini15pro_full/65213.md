etiolate: /ˈiːtiəˌleɪt/ /ˈiːtiəˌlɑːt/
| v. adj.| (使)变白；(使)变弱；(使)丧失活力 |To make or become pale and weak, especially through lack of sunlight. |The seedlings etiolated in the dark.  这些幼苗在黑暗中变得衰弱苍白。|近义词：blanch, bleach, whiten, pale, weaken|反义词: strengthen, invigorate, darken|词性:动词,形容词
etiolation: /iːtiəˈleɪʃn/  /iːtiəˈlɑːʃn/
| n. | 变白；变弱；丧失活力|The process of becoming pale and weak, especially through lack of sunlight. |The etiolation of the plant was caused by insufficient light.  这种植物的变白是由于光照不足造成的。|近义词：blanching, bleaching, whitening, paling, weakening|反义词: strengthening, invigoration, darkening|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *étioler*, from *étiole*, a variant of *sthole*, ultimately from Frankish *stuba* "stubble"
