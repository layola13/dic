dewires:/diːˈwaɪərz/
|v.|拆除（设备或场所）的电线|To remove the wires from (equipment or a place).|The electricians dewired the old building before demolition.  电工在拆除旧建筑之前拆除了电线。|The company dewires deactivated satellites to prevent space debris.  该公司拆除了停用的卫星的电线，以防止产生太空碎片。|近义词：disconnect, dismantle|反义词:wire, connect|词性:动词

词源: de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + wires (plural of wire)
