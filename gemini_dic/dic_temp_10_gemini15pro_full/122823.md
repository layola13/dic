molehill:/ˈmoʊlhɪl/| n.|鼹鼠丘，小土堆，小问题|A small mound of earth thrown up by a mole.  Figuratively, a small difficulty or problem. |The problem is just a molehill; don't make a mountain out of it.  这个问题只是一个小问题；别小题大做。|Synonyms: anthill, mound, trifle, triviality|Antonyms: mountain, obstacle, hurdle|noun

molehill:/ˈmoʊlhɪl/| n.|无足轻重的事，小事|Something insignificant or trivial.|Don't worry about such molehills.  别担心这些小事。|Synonyms: bagatelle, nothing, trifle|Antonyms: crisis, emergency, catastrophe|noun
