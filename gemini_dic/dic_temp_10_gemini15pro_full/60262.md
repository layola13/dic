EFTA: /ˈɛftə/
noun
European Free Trade Association
欧洲自由贸易联盟 (一个由未加入欧盟的欧洲国家组成的区域贸易组织)
EFTA promotes free trade and economic cooperation between its member states. 欧洲自由贸易联盟促进其成员国之间的自由贸易和经济合作。
EFTA members enjoy tariff-free trade with each other. 欧洲自由贸易联盟成员彼此之间享有免关税贸易。
synonyms:  EEA (somewhat, as some EFTA states are also EEA members), free trade area
antonyms:  EU (partially, as it represents a different approach to European integration), customs union
