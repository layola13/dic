The guide authoritatively directed the tourists to the ancient ruins. 导游权威地指挥游客前往古遗址。

| 近义词: definitively, decisively, confidently, assertively, imperiously | 反义词: hesitantly, tentatively, uncertainly, meekly, submissively | 词性: 副词
