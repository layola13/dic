frontiersmen:/ˌfrʌnˈtɪərzmən/
noun
拓荒者；边疆居民
People who live and work on a frontier, especially in the American West during the period of westward expansion.
The frontiersmen faced many dangers, including hostile Native American tribes and harsh weather conditions.  拓荒者面临许多危险，包括敌对的美洲原住民部落和恶劣的天气条件。

The frontiersmen were skilled hunters and trappers.  拓荒者是熟练的猎人和捕兽者。

pioneer, settler, colonist
