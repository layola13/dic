musicomania: /ˌmjuːzɪkəˈmeɪniə/ | n. | 音乐狂 | An excessive enthusiasm for music. | He has a musicomania; he listens to music all day long. 他有音乐狂，整天都在听音乐。 | 近义词: melomania | 反义词: musical apathy | 词性: noun

词源:  From Greek *mousikomania*, from *mousikē* (music) + *mania* (madness).
