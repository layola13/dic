argala:/ˈɑːrɡələ/ | n. | 阿伽罗，沉香木，鹫巢 | A fragrant wood, especially agarwood, also referring to a vulture's nest. |  The argala is highly prized for its aromatic properties. 阿伽罗因其芳香特性而备受推崇。

| The ancient texts mention the use of argala in perfumes and incense. 古代文献提到了阿伽罗在香水和香料中的用途。 | 近义词: agarwood, aloeswood | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Sanskrit अगुरु (aguru) meaning "not heavy," referring to the lightweight nature of the wood.
