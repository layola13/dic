candlestick:/ˈkændlstɪk/| n. |烛台；蜡烛架|A holder for a candle, typically made of metal or wood and having a socket or spike for the candle. |The dining table was elegantly set with silver candlesticks and white linen. 餐桌上摆放着银质烛台和白色亚麻布，布置得很雅致。

The antique candlestick was worth a fortune.  这古董烛台价值连城。|近义词：candleholder|反义词: None|词性:noun


Etymology: From Middle English *candelstykke*, equivalent to *candle* + *stick*.  Ultimately from Old English *candelsticca*.
