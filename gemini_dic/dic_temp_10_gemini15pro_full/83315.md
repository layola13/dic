gynophore:/ˈɡaɪnəfɔːr/ /ˈdʒɪnəfɔːr/
n.
雌蕊柄；子房柄
A stalk bearing the ovary above the level of insertion of the other floral parts.
The peanut flower develops a gynophore, which elongates and pushes the ovary into the soil where the fruit develops. 花生开花后会长出一个雌蕊柄，它会伸长并将子房推入土壤中，果实就在那里发育。
The gynophore of the passion flower is clearly visible. 西番莲的雌蕊柄清晰可见。

近义词: stipe, stalk
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek γυνή (gunḗ, “woman, female”) +‎ -phore,  from φέρειν (phérein, “to bear”).  Literally "female-bearing".
