overtaking:/ˌəʊvəˈteɪkɪŋ/ /ˈoʊvərˌteɪkɪŋ/ | n. | 超车，赶超 | The act of passing another vehicle or person that is moving in the same direction. |  The overtaking maneuver was executed perfectly. 超车动作执行得很完美。

overtaking:/ˌəʊvəˈteɪkɪŋ/ /ˈoʊvərˌteɪkɪŋ/ | adj. |  超车的，赶超的 | Relating to the act of passing another vehicle or person. | The overtaking car had its headlights on. 超车的车辆打开了前灯。


overtaking:/ˌəʊvəˈteɪkɪŋ/ /ˈoʊvərˌteɪkɪŋ/| n. |超车，赶超 | From over + take + -ing.  "Overtake" itself comes from Old Norse *auka-taka "to take in addition," from *auka "to increase" + taka "to take." | Overtaking on a blind corner is extremely dangerous. 在视线盲区超车极其危险。


近义词: passing, outstripping
反义词: lagging, falling behind
词性: noun, adjective (primarily used as a noun)
