husbander:/ˈhʌzbəndər/ | n. | 节俭的人，管理人，经营者 | A person who manages and conserves resources, especially in a careful or thrifty way.  |  The farmer was a good husbander of his land, rotating crops and using sustainable practices.  这位农民是一位优秀的土地经营者，他会轮作作物并采用可持续的耕作方式。 | 近义词: manager, economizer, conserver, steward | 反义词: spendthrift, squanderer | noun

husbander:/ˈhʌzbəndər/ | v. | 节俭地使用；妥善管理；经营 | To use or manage resources economically; to conserve. |  She carefully husbanded her remaining energy for the final sprint. 她小心翼翼地保存剩余的精力，以备最后的冲刺。 | 近义词: conserve, manage, economize, preserve | 反义词: waste, squander | verb
