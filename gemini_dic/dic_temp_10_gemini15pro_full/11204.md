architects:/ˈɑːrkɪtekts/| n.|建筑师|A person who designs buildings and in many cases also supervises their construction.|The architects designed a stunning modern art museum. 建筑师们设计了一座令人惊叹的现代艺术博物馆。|Synonyms: designer, planner, builder|Antonyms: demolisher, destroyer|Noun

Etymology:
From Old French architecte, from Latin architectus, from Ancient Greek ἀρχιτέκτων (arkhitéktōn), from ἀρχι- (arkhi-, “chief”) + τέκτων (téktōn, “builder, carpenter”).
