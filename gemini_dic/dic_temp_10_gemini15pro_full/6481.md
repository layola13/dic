amputations:/ˌæmpjʊˈteɪʃənz/| n.|截肢（手术），切除（手术）|The surgical removal of a limb or other body part.  The patient underwent multiple amputations after a severe accident. 病人在一场严重的事故后经历了多次截肢手术。|Synonyms: excisions, removals|Antonyms: attachments, additions|noun
