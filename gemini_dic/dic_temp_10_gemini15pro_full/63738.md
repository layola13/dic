epical:/ˈɛpɪkəl/| adj.|史诗般的；壮丽的；宏大的|Relating to or characteristic of an epic or epics; heroic or grand in scale or character.|例句：The battle was of epical proportions. 这场战斗规模宏大。|例句：She told an epical tale of adventure. 她讲述了一个史诗般的冒险故事。|近义词：heroic, grand, majestic, monumental|反义词：mundane, ordinary, trivial|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin *epicalis*, from Latin *epicus* (“epic”) + *-alis*. Ultimately from Ancient Greek *ἐπικός (epikós)*.
