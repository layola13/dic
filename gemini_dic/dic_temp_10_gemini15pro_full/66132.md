exception:/ɪkˈsepʃn/ (US), /ɪkˈsɛpʃn/ (UK)| n.|例外，除外；反对；异议|something that does not follow the normal rules or pattern; an objection or protest|例句：There is an exception to every rule. 每条规则都有例外。|近义词：exclusion, anomaly, objection, dissent|反义词：norm, rule, standard|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *exceptiōn-, from Latin excipere (“to take out, except”), from ex- (“out”) + capere (“to take”).
