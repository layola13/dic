desiring:/dɪˈzaɪərɪŋ/
adjective
渴望的，想望的
Having a strong feeling of wanting something.
She cast a desiring glance at the handsome stranger. 她对那个英俊的陌生人投以渴望的目光。
Synonyms: covetous, yearning, longing, craving, wishful
Antonyms: indifferent, apathetic, uninterested
词性: 形容词


desiring:/dɪˈzaɪərɪŋ/
verb
渴望，想望 (present participle of desire)
To strongly wish for or want something.
Desiring a better life, she moved to the city. 渴望更好的生活，她搬到了城市。
Synonyms: wanting, wishing, craving, longing for
Antonyms: rejecting, refusing, spurning
词性: 动词
