ecostate:/ˈiːkoʊsteɪt/| n.|生态区|A major ecological region with characteristic flora and fauna, often encompassing multiple ecosystems.|例句：The Amazon rainforest is a vast and diverse ecostate. 亚马逊雨林是一个广阔而多样的生态区。

例句：Protecting ecostates is crucial for biodiversity conservation. 保护生态区对生物多样性保护至关重要。|近义词：bioregion, ecoregion|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

Etymology:  From eco- (ecological) + -state (as in state of a system).  The term was coined in the late 20th century.
