defensative:/dɪˈfɛnsətɪv/ | adj. | 防御性的，防守的；辩护的 | Acting or designed to defend; protective.  Relating to or intended for defense.  Also, excessively concerned with justifying one's actions; touchy. |  He immediately became defensative when I questioned his decision.  他对我质疑他的决定立即采取了防御姿态。|  protective, shielding, apologetic, guarded | aggressive, offensive | adjective
