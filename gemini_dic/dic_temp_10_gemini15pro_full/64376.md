erection:/ɪˈrɛkʃ(ə)n/ /ɪˈrɛkʃən/

The erection of the new bridge was completed ahead of schedule. 新桥的建造提前完成了。

He awoke with an erection. 他醒来时勃起了。

erect:/ɪˈrɛkt/
vt.|建造；竖立；使直立|To build or establish something; to cause something to stand upright.
They erected a statue in his honor. 他们为他竖立了一座雕像。

He erected a fence around his property.  他在他的房产周围竖立了围栏。

|近义词:build, construct, assemble, raise, put up|反义词:demolish, destroy, dismantle, take down|词性:动词

erect:/ɪˈrɛkt/
adj.|直立的；竖直的；笔直的|Standing upright; vertical; rigid.
He stood erect, his back straight. 他直立着，背挺得很直。

The soldiers stood in erect posture. 士兵们直立站着。

|近义词:upright, vertical, straight, perpendicular|反义词:bent, stooped, drooping, horizontal|词性:形容词
