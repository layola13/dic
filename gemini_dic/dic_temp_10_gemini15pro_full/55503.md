distrainor:/dɪˈstreɪnər/| n.|扣押人，执行扣押令的人|A person who distrains, especially an official authorized to seize someone's property to compel payment of rent or other debts.|The distrainer seized the farmer's equipment for unpaid rent. 扣押人因未付租金而扣押了农民的设备。|近义词：seizer, confiscator|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "debtor" in some contexts)|noun
