exploiting: /ɪkˈsplɔɪtɪŋ/
verb
利用; 剥削
Making full use of a resource or situation in a way considered unfair or selfish.
The company is accused of exploiting its workers.  该公司被指控剥削工人。
He exploited his father's name to get the job. 他利用他父亲的名声得到了这份工作。
synonyms: utilizing, harnessing, capitalizing on, taking advantage of
antonyms:  squandering, neglecting, wasting, overlooking, helping
verb
