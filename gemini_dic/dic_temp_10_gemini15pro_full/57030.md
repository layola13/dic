doublespeakers:/ˈdʌblˌspiːkərz/
noun
双重标准者；言行不一者
Someone who speaks insincerely or hypocritically, saying one thing but meaning another, often for deceitful or manipulative purposes.
He's such a doublespeaker, promising loyalty while secretly plotting against us. 他真是个言行不一的人，一边承诺忠诚，一边却秘密策划反对我们。
synonyms: hypocrite, liar, deceiver, prevaricator, equivocator
antonyms: honest person, truthful person, straight shooter
