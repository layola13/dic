nutriceuticals:/ˌnjuːtrəˈsuːtɪkəlz/ | n. |营养保健品|A food or food product that provides health and medical benefits, including the prevention and treatment of disease.| Example:  Many people take nutriceuticals like vitamins and supplements to improve their health. 很多人服用维生素和补充剂等营养保健品来改善健康。

Example:  The company specializes in developing new nutriceuticals for the prevention of chronic diseases. 这家公司专门开发用于预防慢性病的新型营养保健品。|近义词:functional foods, dietary supplements, nutraceuticals |反义词:junk food |词性:noun
