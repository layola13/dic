heliocentricity:/ˌhiːliəʊˈsɛntrɪsɪti/ /ˌhiːlioʊˈsɛntrɪsɪti/| n.|日心说|The astronomical model in which the Earth and planets revolve around the Sun at the center of the Solar System.| Copernicus's theory of heliocentricity revolutionized astronomy. 哥白尼的日心说理论彻底改变了天文学。|近义词: heliocentrism|反义词: geocentricity|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἥλιος (hḗlios, “sun”) +‎ κέντρον (kéntron, “center”) +‎ -ity.
