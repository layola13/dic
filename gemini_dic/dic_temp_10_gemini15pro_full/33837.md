circumlocutory:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈlɒkjʊtəri/ /ˌsɜːrkəmˈloʊkjʊtɔːri/
adjective
迂回的，冗长的
Characterized by indirect or roundabout language; verbose.
He gave a circumlocutory explanation, avoiding the main point. 他给了一个迂回的解释，避开了要点。

The politician gave a circumlocutory answer to the journalist's question.  这位政治家对记者的提问给出了一个迂回的答案。

近义词:periphrastic, verbose, roundabout, indirect, prolix
反义词:direct, concise, succinct, straightforward, brief
词性:adjective

词源:From Latin circumlocutio (“a roundabout way of speaking”), from circum (“around”) + locutio (“speech, speaking”).
