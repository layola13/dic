antide:/ˈæn.taɪd/ |n.| 抗体; 抗毒素 |A substance formed in the blood that destroys or neutralizes an antigen (such as a toxin or the surface of an infectious agent).| The antide helped her body fight off the infection. 她的抗体帮助她的身体战胜了感染。|  Antibodies are produced by the body's immune system to fight off harmful substances.  抗体是由身体的免疫系统产生的，用来对抗有害物质。 | synonyms: antibody, antitoxin | antonyms: toxin, antigen | noun
