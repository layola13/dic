non-orientability:/ˌnɒnˌɔːriənˈtəbɪləti/ |n.| 不可定向性 |The property of a surface or shape where it is impossible to consistently define a "clockwise" or "counterclockwise" rotation across the entire surface.  A Möbius strip is a classic example of a non-orientable surface.| A Klein bottle is an example of a non-orientable surface. 克莱因瓶是不可定向曲面的一个例子。|non-directedness|orientability|noun
