clong:/klɒŋ/| n.| 叮当声，铿锵声 |A metallic ringing sound. | The clong of the hammer hitting the anvil echoed through the forge. 锤子敲击铁砧的铿锵声在锻造坊里回荡。|近义词：clang, ring, chime|反义词: thud, thump| noun

词源：
Likely imitative of the sound.  The word is relatively modern and doesn't have a deep etymological history.
