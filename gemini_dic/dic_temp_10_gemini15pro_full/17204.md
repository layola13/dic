bauxite:/ˈbɔːksaɪt/ | n. |铝土矿 |A rock consisting mainly of hydrated aluminum oxides, the principal ore of aluminum. | Bauxite is the main source of aluminum. 铝土矿是铝的主要来源。| Bauxite deposits are often found in tropical regions. 铝土矿矿床经常在热带地区被发现。 | synonyms: aluminum ore | antonyms: N/A | noun
