debarred:/dɪˈbɑːrd/
verb
禁止，阻止
officially prevent someone from doing something
例句：He was debarred from entering the competition. 他被禁止参加比赛。

例句：She was debarred from practicing law. 她被禁止从事律师职业。

近义词：prohibited, forbidden, banned, excluded, precluded
反义词：permitted, allowed, authorized
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *desbarrer, from des- (expressing removal) + barre (bar, barrier).
