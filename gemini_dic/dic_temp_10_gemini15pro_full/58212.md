drug-on-the-market:/drʌɡ ɒn ðə ˈmɑːkɪt/ /drʌɡ ɑːn ðə ˈmɑːrkɪt/| n.|市售药物，已上市的药物|A pharmaceutical product that is legally available for sale and use by the public.  It refers to a medication that has received regulatory approval (like from the FDA in the US) and can be purchased by consumers.|The new drug-on-the-market has shown promising results in treating the disease.  这种新上市的药物在治疗这种疾病方面显示出良好的效果。|Synonyms: marketed drug, available medication, approved drug|Antonyms: investigational drug, experimental drug|noun
