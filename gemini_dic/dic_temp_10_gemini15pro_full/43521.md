cravingnesses:/ˈkreɪvɪŋnəsɪz/| n. |渴望，热望|The state of having strong desires or cravings, especially for something unhealthy or undesirable.  This word is not commonly used; "cravings" is the standard plural form.|His cravingnesses for sweets were becoming a problem. 他对甜食的渴望成了一个问题。|Near synonyms: cravings, desires, yearnings, longings|Antonym: indifference|noun
