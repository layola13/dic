overglutting:/ˌoʊvərˈɡlʌtɪŋ/
| n.| 供过于求，过度饱和 |The overglutting of the market with cheap imports led to a decline in domestic production.  廉价进口商品充斥市场导致国内产量下降。|  The overglutting of information online can make it difficult to find what you need.  网上信息过载使得很难找到你需要的东西。|oversupply, saturation, surplus| shortage, scarcity, dearth |noun
overglut:/ˌoʊvərˈɡlʌt/
| vt.|  使…供应过剩，使…过度饱和|The company overglutted the market with its new product, causing prices to fall.  该公司的新产品充斥市场，导致价格下跌。|They overglutted themselves with food at the buffet. 他们在自助餐上吃得太多。|oversupply, saturate, flood| deprive, starve, undersupply |verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + glut.  "Over-" signifies "excessively" or "too much." "Glut" comes from the Old French "glotir" meaning "to swallow" or "to devour," ultimately from the Latin "glutire." Thus, "overglut" literally means to "over-swallow" or "over-devour."
