fascistically:/fæˈʃɪstɪkli/
adv.
法西斯地；极权主义地
in a fascist manner; relating to or characteristic of fascism.
He ruled the country fascistically, suppressing all dissent.  他以法西斯的方式统治国家，压制所有异议。
The government acted fascistically by censoring the press. 政府通过审查新闻媒体，采取了法西斯主义的行动。
近义词:totalitarianly, dictatorially, tyrannically, oppressively
反义词:democratically, liberally
词性:adverb
