bummer:/ˈbʌmər/| n.|令人失望的事情；扫兴的事情|A bummer is something disappointing or unpleasant.|例句：It's a real bummer that the concert was canceled.  演唱会被取消真是太扫兴了。

例句：Losing my wallet was a major bummer. 丢了钱包真倒霉。

近义词：disappointment, setback, drag, downer|反义词:joy, blessing|词性:名词
