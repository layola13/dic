coralline:/ˈkɒrəlaɪn/ /ˈkɔːrəlaɪn/| adj. n.|珊瑚的，珊瑚色的；珊瑚虫|Made of coral or resembling coral.  Relating to or consisting of coral.|The divers explored the coralline reefs. 潜水员探索了珊瑚礁。|近义词：coral, coral-like|反义词:|adjective, noun

coralline:/ˈkɒrəlaɪn/ /ˈkɔːrəlaɪn/| n.|珊瑚藻|A red alga that secretes calcium carbonate, contributing to the formation of coral reefs.|The coralline algae were abundant in the shallow waters.  浅水区珊瑚藻很丰富。|近义词：nullipore|反义词:|noun
