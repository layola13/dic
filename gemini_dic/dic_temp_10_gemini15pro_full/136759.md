nonseparable:/nɒnˈsɛp(ə)rəb(ə)l/ /nɑːnˈsɛpərəbəl/ | adj. | 不可分的，不可分离的 | Not able to be separated or treated as separate parts. |  The prefix and the root word are nonseparable in this case.  在这个例子中，前缀和词根是不可分的。 | inseparable, indivisible | separable, divisible | adjective

nonseparable:/nɒnˈsɛp(ə)rəb(ə)l/ /nɑːnˈsɛpərəbəl/ | n. |  不可分割的事物 | Something that cannot be separated. | While less common as a noun, one might refer to a nonseparable as a single unit.  虽然作为名词不太常见，但可以将不可分割的事物称为一个单元。| unit, whole | parts, components | noun
