non-hero:/ˌnɒnˈhɪərəʊ/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈhɪroʊ/ (US)| n.| 非英雄;平凡人;普通人|A person who lacks the qualities traditionally associated with a hero, such as courage, exceptional ability, or noble character.  |He's a non-hero, just an ordinary man trying to do the right thing. 他不是英雄，只是一个努力做正确事情的普通人。|Synonyms: ordinary person, average Joe, civilian, nobody |Antonyms: hero, champion, legend| noun


non-heroic:/ˌnɒn.hɪˈrəʊ.ɪk/ (UK)  /ˌnɑːn.hɪˈroʊ.ɪk/ (US) | adj.| 非英雄的;平凡的;不英勇的|Not heroic; lacking the characteristics of a hero. |The film focuses on the non-heroic aspects of war. 这部电影聚焦于战争中非英雄的一面。|Synonyms: ordinary, mundane, unheroic, commonplace, average |Antonyms: heroic, brave, courageous, valiant| adjective
