micromanipulate:/ˌmaɪkroʊməˈnɪpjʊleɪt/| v. |微操，显微操作|To manipulate or control something very small, typically using a microscope and specialized instruments.  It refers to the precise manipulation of very small objects, often at the cellular or subcellular level, using specialized tools and techniques.|Scientists micromanipulate DNA molecules to study their structure. 科学家们对DNA分子进行微操以研究其结构。|manipulate, control|macro-manipulate|verb
