antipasto:/ˌæntiˈpɑːstoʊ/ (US), /ˌæntiˈpæstoʊ/ (UK)| n. |开胃菜，前菜|A small dish of food eaten before the main part of a meal.|We started with a delicious antipasto of cured meats and olives. 我们以美味的腌肉和橄榄开胃菜开始用餐。|Synonyms: appetizer, starter, hors d'oeuvre|Antonyms: dessert, main course|noun

Etymology: From Italian *antipasto*, from *anti-* (before) + *pasto* (meal), ultimately from Latin *pastus* (pasture, food).
