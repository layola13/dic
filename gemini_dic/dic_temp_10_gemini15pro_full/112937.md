lugger:/ˈlʌɡər/| n.|小型帆船；拖网渔船；强壮的人；耳垂|A small sailing vessel with four-cornered lugsails set fore and aft; a fishing boat that drags nets along the seabed; a strong person (informal); an earlobe (informal).|例句：The old lugger sailed gracefully across the bay. 那艘老式帆船优雅地驶过海湾。

例句：The lugger brought in a large haul of fish. 这艘拖网渔船捕获了大量的鱼。

近义词：sailing vessel, fishing boat, trawler, workhorse (for a person)|反义词:|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From probably Low German or Dutch *logger, from loggen (“to sail slowly”), from Middle Low German loggen.
