bludger: /ˈblʌdʒə(r)/ (UK), /ˈblʌdʒər/ (US)| n.|懒汉，寄生虫；（澳大利亚俚语）棍棒，球棒|Someone who avoids work or responsibility; (Australian slang) a short, heavy club.|He's a real bludger, never lifting a finger to help. 他真是个懒汉，从来不帮忙。|  The cricket team needed a new bludger.  这支板球队需要一根新的球棒。|Synonyms: loafer, idler, shirker, parasite, sponger (UK), dole bludger (Australian, derogatory); (Australian slang) club, cudgel|Antonyms: hard worker, grafter|noun
