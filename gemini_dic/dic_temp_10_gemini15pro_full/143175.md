outclimbed: /ˌaʊtˈklaɪmd/
verb
胜过攀登，爬得比…高
surpassed in climbing; climbed higher or faster than.
The younger climber outclimbed his older rival on the difficult rock face.  年轻的登山者在艰难的岩壁上胜过了他年长的对手。
Synonyms: surpassed, bested, exceeded, topped
Antonyms: lost to, trailed, lagged behind
词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + climb + -ed (past tense suffix). "Out-" acts as a prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing in the action of the verb it modifies.
