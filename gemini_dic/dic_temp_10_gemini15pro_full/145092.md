overapplication:/ˌoʊvərˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən/
|n.|过度应用，过量施用|The overapplication of fertilizer can harm the environment. 过量施用化肥会危害环境。|
|例句：The overapplication of pesticides can lead to resistant strains of insects. 过量使用杀虫剂会导致昆虫产生抗药性。|
|例句：Overapplication of sunscreen can sometimes clog pores.  过量涂抹防晒霜有时会堵塞毛孔。|
|近义词：overuse, excessive application|
|反义词：underapplication, insufficient application|
|词性：noun|


overapply:/ˌoʊvərəˈplaɪ/
|v.|过度应用，过量施用|Don't overapply the cream, a small amount is enough. 不要过度涂抹面霜，少量就足够了。|
|例句：He tends to overapply the rules, leading to unnecessary bureaucracy. 他倾向于过度应用规则，导致不必要的官僚主义。|
|例句：She overapplied for the job, sending in multiple resumes and cover letters. 她过度申请了这份工作，提交了多份简历和求职信。|
|近义词：overuse, overdo|
|反义词：underapply, underuse|
|词性：verb|
