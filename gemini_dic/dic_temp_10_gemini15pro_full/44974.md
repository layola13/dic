crystallising:/ˈkrɪstəlaɪzɪŋ/
v.
结晶；使明确；使具体化
The solution is slowly crystallising.  溶液正在慢慢结晶。
His vague ideas began to crystallise into a definite plan. 他模糊的想法开始具体化为一个明确的计划。
Synonyms: solidify, harden, concretize
Antonyms: dissolve, melt, liquefy
verb

crystallize:/ˈkrɪstəlaɪz/
v.
结晶；使明确；使具体化

Synonyms: solidify, harden, concretize
Antonyms: dissolve, melt, liquefy
