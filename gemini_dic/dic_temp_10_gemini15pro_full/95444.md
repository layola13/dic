incise:/ɪnˈsaɪz/| v. |雕刻，切割|To cut into a surface, especially precisely and with a sharp instrument.|The surgeon incised the skin with a scalpel.  外科医生用手术刀切开了皮肤。|例句：The artist incised the design into the wood.  艺术家把图案刻在木头上。|近义词：carve, engrave, etch|反义词:join, unite|词性:动词
incision:/ɪnˈsɪʒən/| n. |切口，雕刻|A cut, especially one made surgically.|The surgeon made a small incision to drain the abscess. 外科医生做了一个小切口来排出脓肿。|例句：The incision healed quickly. 切口愈合得很快。|近义词：cut, gash, slit|反义词:closure, suture|词性:名词
