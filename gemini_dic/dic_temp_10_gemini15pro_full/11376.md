一般现在时 (Simple Present):
They are students. = 他们是学生。
We are going to the park. = 我们要去公园。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
They are playing football now. = 他们现在正在踢足球。
The birds are singing beautifully. = 鸟儿们正在优美地歌唱。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):  "are" is not used in the present perfect. The present perfect tense of "be" uses "have been" for plural subjects.
They have been studying English for three years. = 他们学习英语已经三年了。
We have been waiting here for an hour. = 我们在这里等了一个小时了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous): "are" is not used in the present perfect continuous. The present perfect continuous tense of "be" uses "have been being" (rarely used) or implies a continuous action with another verb as in the examples below.
They have been playing games all day. = 他们玩了一整天游戏. (implies continuous action)
We have been working on this project for months. = 我们几个月来一直在做这个项目。（implies continuous action)

一般过去时 (Simple Past): The past tense form is "were."
They were at the cinema last night. = 他们昨晚在电影院。
We were tired after the long journey. = 长途旅行后我们很累。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous): The past continuous form is "were being".
They were playing outside when it started to rain. = 开始下雨时，他们正在外面玩。
We were watching a movie when the phone rang. = 电话响的时候，我们正在看电影。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect): The past perfect uses "had been".
They had been living in London for five years before they moved to Paris. = 在搬到巴黎之前，他们在伦敦住了五年。
We had been waiting for hours before the train finally arrived. = 火车终于到达之前，我们已经等了几个小时了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):  The past perfect continuous also uses "had been" + present participle.
They had been studying all night before the exam. = 考试前他们整夜都在学习。
We had been driving for hours before we stopped for a break. = 我们开了几个小时的车才停下来休息。

一般将来时 (Simple Future): The future tense uses "will be".
They will be here soon. = 他们很快就会到这里。
We will be ready to leave in five minutes. = 我们五分钟后就可以出发了。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous): The future continuous uses "will be" + present participle.
They will be playing football tomorrow afternoon. = 明天下午他们将进行足球比赛。
We will be flying to Spain next week. = 下周我们将飞往西班牙。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect): The future perfect uses "will have been".
By next year, they will have been married for ten years. = 到明年，他们将结婚十年了。
We will have been living here for five years by the time the new house is built. = 新房子建成的时候，我们将在这里住了五年。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous): The future perfect continuous uses "will have been" + present participle.
By next year, they will have been living in London for five years. = 到明年，他们将在伦敦生活五年了。
We will have been traveling for a month by the time we get home. = 等我们到家的时候，我们已经旅行了一个月了。
