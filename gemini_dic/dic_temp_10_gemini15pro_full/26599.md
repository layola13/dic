cachalot:/ˈkæʃəlɒt/ (US) /ˈkæʃəlɒt/ (UK)| n. |抹香鲸|A large toothed whale, the largest of the toothed whales, known for its spermaceti organ.|例句：The cachalot dives deep to hunt squid. 抹香鲸潜入深海捕食乌贼。

例句：Whalers once hunted the cachalot for its valuable oil. 捕鲸者曾经捕猎抹香鲸以获取其珍贵的油脂。|近义词：sperm whale|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
