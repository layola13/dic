nonagreement:/ˌnɒnəˈɡriːmənt/ | n. |不一致，分歧，缺乏协议|lack of agreement or harmony; failure to reach an understanding or consensus. |例句：There was significant nonagreement between the two parties on the key issues. 双方在关键问题上存在重大分歧。

例句：The nonagreement over the contract terms led to the deal falling through.  合同条款上的分歧导致交易失败。|近义词：disagreement, discord, difference, dissent, conflict, variance |反义词：agreement, concord, harmony, accord, consensus |词性:名词
