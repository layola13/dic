nexuses:/ˈnɛksəsɪz/
n.
连接,枢纽;中心,核心
a connection or series of connections linking two or more things. a central or focal point.
例句：The city is a nexus of transportation routes. 这座城市是交通路线的枢纽。

例句：The university is a nexus of intellectual activity. 这所大学是知识活动的中心。

近义词：center, hub, focus, core, junction, link, connection
反义词：periphery, edge, fringe
词性: 名词 (plural of nexus)
