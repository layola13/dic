gut-wrenching:/ˈɡʌtˌrɛntʃɪŋ/| adj.|令人心痛的，极其痛苦的|Causing extreme distress; emotionally upsetting.|Seeing the injured animal was a gut-wrenching experience. 看见受伤的动物是一次令人心碎的经历。|The news of his death was gut-wrenching. 他去世的消息令人悲痛欲绝。|近义词:heartbreaking, devastating, harrowing, agonizing|反义词:comforting, reassuring, pleasant|词性:形容词
词源:gut (内脏) + wrenching (扭绞的).  The imagery evokes a physical sensation of pain and twisting in the stomach, reflecting the emotional turmoil.
