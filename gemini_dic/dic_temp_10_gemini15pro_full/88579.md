hithermost:/ˈhɪðəməʊst/| adj.|最靠近这边的；最近的| Situated nearest to the speaker or the point of reference.|The hithermost houses were already engulfed in flames. 最靠近这边的房子已经被大火吞噬了。|The hithermost edge of the forest. 森林最近的边缘。|近义词：nearest, closest, adjacent|反义词：farthest, furthest|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old English "hider" (to here) + "-most" (superlative suffix).  Essentially means "most here" or "closest here."
