decipherer:/dɪˈsaɪfərər/
n.
解密者，破译者
A person who deciphers something, especially a coded message.
The decipherer worked tirelessly to break the enemy's code.  解密者不知疲倦地工作，以破解敌人的密码。
The archaeologist was a skilled decipherer of ancient hieroglyphs.  这位考古学家是一位熟练的古代象形文字破译者。
decoder, interpreter, translator
encoder, encryptor
noun
