columbine: /ˈkɒləmbaɪn/ (UK) /ˈkɑːləmbaɪn/ (US) | n. | 耧斗菜；鸽子 | A plant with flowers shaped like bells, typically of a bluish-purple colour. Also, a symbol of peace and the Holy Spirit. | The columbine bloomed in vibrant shades of purple and blue. 耧斗菜开出了鲜艳的紫色和蓝色。| The columbine is often associated with innocence.  耧斗菜常与纯真联系在一起。 | synonym: dove flower, granny's bonnet | antonym: (No direct antonym for a plant.  Perhaps a contrasting flower like a rose, symbolizing passion, could be considered) | noun
