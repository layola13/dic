felicitas:/fɛlɪˈsɪtɑːs/
|n.|幸福，幸运，繁荣|Happiness, good fortune, prosperity.  It refers to a state of blessedness or a condition of being fortunate.  It emphasizes a sense of deep and lasting happiness, often associated with divine favor or a prosperous life.|The ancient Romans prayed to the goddess Felicitas for felicitas in their lives. 古代罗马人向幸福女神祈祷，祈求生活幸福。|Synonyms: bliss, blessedness, happiness, prosperity, fortune|Antonyms: misfortune, unhappiness, adversity|Noun
