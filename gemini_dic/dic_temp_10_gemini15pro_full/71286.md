fixedness:/ˈfɪksɪdnəs/
n.
固定性；固执；刻板性
The state of being fixed or unchanging.  An inability to see a problem from a fresh perspective.
His fixedness on the idea prevented him from considering other options. 他对这个想法的固执使他无法考虑其他选择。

近义词: rigidity, inflexibility, obstinacy, stubbornness
反义词: flexibility, adaptability, open-mindedness
