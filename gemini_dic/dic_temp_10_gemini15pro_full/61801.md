emphaticalness:/ɪmˈfætɪkəlnəs/| n. |强调,坚决,着重|The quality of being emphatic; forcefulness of expression.  The state of being expressed with emphasis.|She delivered her speech with great emphaticalness, leaving no doubt about her position. 她以非常强调的语气发表了讲话，毫不怀疑她的立场。|近义词：emphasis, forcefulness, insistence, vehemence|反义词：hesitancy, uncertainty, vagueness, meekness|词性:noun
Etymology: From emphatic + -ness.  Emphatic derives from Late Latin emphaticus, from Greek emphatikos "expressive," from emphainein "to exhibit," from en- "in" + phainein "to show."
