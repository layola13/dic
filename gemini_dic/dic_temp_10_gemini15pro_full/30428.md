centrality:/sɛnˈtrælɪti/
n.|中心性，集中性|The quality of being central or essential.|The centrality of this issue cannot be overstated.  这个问题的重要性怎么强调都不为过。|importance, significance, key role|periphery, marginality|noun

centrality:/sɛnˈtrælɪti/
n.|中心地位，核心地位|The state of being central or in the middle of something.|The new shopping mall enjoys centrality in the town.  新的购物中心占据了城镇的中心位置。|center, core, heart|outskirts, edge|noun
