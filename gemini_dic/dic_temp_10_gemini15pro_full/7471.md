anito:/əˈniːtoʊ/| n. |神灵，祖先，偶像|A spirit, particularly an ancestral spirit, revered especially in the Philippines.|例句：The villagers offered sacrifices to the anito of the mountain. 村民们向山神献祭。

例句：They prayed to their anitos for a bountiful harvest.  他们向祖先祈祷丰收。|近义词：deity, god, spirit, idol|反义词:demon, devil|词性:noun
