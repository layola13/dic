mailing:/ˈmeɪlɪŋ/
n.
邮件, 邮寄, 邮递
the act of sending something by mail.
例句：The mailing of the invitations took several days.  寄请柬花了几天时间。

例句：The company handles the mailing of its own catalogs.  这家公司自己处理商品目录的邮寄。

近义词：posting, dispatch, sending
反义词: receiving

词性: 名词
