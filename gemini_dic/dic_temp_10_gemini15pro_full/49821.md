demonstrator:/ˈdɛmənˌstreɪtər/ | n. | 示威者；演示者；论证者 | A person who takes part in a public protest.  A person who explains or shows how something works. A person who proves or makes clear a fact, theory, etc. |  例句：The demonstrators marched through the city streets. 示威者在城市街道上游行。

例句：The demonstrator showed the audience how to use the new software. 演示者向观众展示了如何使用新软件。 | 近义词：protester, marcher, exponent, explainer | 反义词：opponent, critic | 词性: noun


demonstrate:/ˈdɛmənˌstreɪt/ | v. |  демонстрировать, показывать, доказывать| To show or make something clear; to take part in a public protest. |  例句：The students demonstrated against the tuition increase. 学生们示威抗议学费上涨。

例句：He demonstrated how to use the new machine. 他演示了如何使用这台新机器。 | 近义词：show, exhibit, display, prove, manifest | 反义词：conceal, hide | 词性: verb
