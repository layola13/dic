nonprovable:/ˌnɒnˈpruːvəbl/ | adj. | 不可证的，无法证明的 | Not able to be proved; incapable of proof. |  This conjecture, while widely believed to be true, remains nonprovable.  这个猜想虽然被广泛认为是正确的，但仍然无法证明。

The existence of God is a nonprovable concept for many people. 对许多人来说，上帝的存在是一个无法证明的概念。 |  undemonstrable, unprovable, incapable of being proved | provable, demonstrable | adjective
