gemination:/ˌdʒɛmɪˈneɪʃən/
n.
双生，成对；重复，语音的叠音
the act of doubling a consonant or vowel sound.
The gemination of the consonant 'n' in 'unnamed' changes its pronunciation.
“unnamed”中辅音“n”的叠音改变了它的发音。
duplication, repetition, doubling
reduction, shortening
noun
