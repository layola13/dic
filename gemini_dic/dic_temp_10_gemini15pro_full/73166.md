forbid:/fərˈbɪd/ /fɔːrˈbɪd/| v. |禁止，不许|To command someone not to do something; to not allow something.|例句：My parents forbid me from going to the party. 我的父母禁止我去参加聚会。

例句：Smoking is forbidden in the hospital. 医院禁止吸烟。

近义词：prohibit, ban, disallow|反义词：allow, permit, authorize|词性：动词
