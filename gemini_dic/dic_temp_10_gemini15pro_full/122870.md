mollifying:/ˈmɒlɪfaɪɪŋ/ /ˈmɑːlɪfaɪɪŋ/| adj.|安抚的，抚慰的，缓和的|Reducing anger or anxiety; appeasing.|Her mollifying tone calmed the angry customer. 她安抚的语气让愤怒的顾客平静下来。|近义词:soothing, appeasing, pacifying, conciliatory|反义词:agitating, provoking, irritating|adjective

mollify:/ˈmɒlɪfaɪ/ /ˈmɑːlɪfaɪ/| vt.|安抚，抚慰，缓和|To soothe in temper or disposition; appease.|The manager tried to mollify the upset employees. 经理试图安抚那些沮丧的员工。|近义词:soothe, appease, pacify, conciliate|反义词:agitate, provoke, irritate|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *mollificāre, from Latin mollificō ("make soft, soften"), from mollis ("soft") + -ficō (suffix forming verbs meaning "make" or "cause to be").
