iching: /ˈiː tʃɪŋ/ | n. | 易经 |  An ancient Chinese divination text and philosophical system, also known as the Book of Changes. It is based on a system of 64 hexagrams, each representing a different life situation or state of being. |  She consults the I Ching for guidance. 她查阅易经寻求指引。 |  synonyms: Book of Changes | antonyms: N/A | noun
