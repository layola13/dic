hird:/hɜːd/ | n. | 牧群，兽群；一大群人 | A large group of animals, especially hoofed mammals, that live together; a large group of people. |  The shepherd led his hird to the pasture. 牧羊人把他的羊群带到牧场。|  The tourists arrived in a hird. 游客们成群结队地到达。| synonyms: flock, herd, drove, swarm, throng | antonyms: individual, loner | noun
