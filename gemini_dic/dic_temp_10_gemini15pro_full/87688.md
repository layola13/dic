hexametaphosphate:/ˌhɛksəˌmɛtəˈfɒsfeɪt/ | n. | 六偏磷酸盐 | A polymeric phosphate salt containing a cyclic or polymeric anion consisting of six PO4 tetrahedra linked together.  | Sodium hexametaphosphate is used as a water softener. 六偏磷酸钠用作水软化剂。 |  Synonyms:  polyphosphate, glassy phosphate | Antonyms: None | noun


hexametaphosphate:/ˌhɛksəˌmɛtəˈfɒsfeɪt/ | n. | 六偏磷酸盐 | A type of phosphate polymer used in various applications, including water treatment, food processing, and as a sequestrant. |  Calcium hexametaphosphate is sometimes added to toothpaste. 六偏磷酸钙有时会被添加到牙膏中。 | Synonyms: Calgon (a common trade name, though Calgon can refer to other polyphosphates),  polyphosphate | Antonyms: None | noun
