combines:/kəmˈbaɪnz/ | v. | 结合，联合，合并 |  joins or mixes two or more things together. | This machine combines several functions. 这台机器结合了多种功能。

The company combines traditional craftsmanship with modern technology.  这家公司将传统工艺与现代科技相结合。| 近义词：mixes, unites, merges, blends, integrates | 反义词：separates, divides, disconnects | 词性: 动词


词源:
From Middle English *combinen, from Old French combiner, from Latin combināre (“to unite, join two by two”), from com- (“together”) + bīnī (“two by two”).
