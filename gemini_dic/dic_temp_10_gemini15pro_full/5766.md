amalgamated:/əˈmælɡəmeɪtɪd/
adjective
合并的，融合的
combined or united, especially by merging businesses or organizations
The company amalgamated with several smaller firms to form a large corporation. 这家公司与几家小公司合并成了一家大公司。
amalgamated:/əˈmælɡəmeɪtɪd/
verb (past participle)
合并，融合
to combine or unite to form one organization or structure
Several local businesses have amalgamated to form a larger company. 几家当地企业合并成了一家更大的公司。


近义词:combined, merged, united, consolidated, blended
反义词:separated, divided, disunited
词性:adjective, verb (past participle)
