coromandel:/ˌkɒrəˈmændəl/ | n. |科罗曼德尔海岸（印度东南部海岸）;  科罗曼德尔木（一种硬木，可用于制作家具和装饰品）|A region on the southeastern coast of India, known for its scenic beauty and historical significance. Also refers to a type of hardwood from the region, prized for its durability and distinctive grain patterns. | We spent our honeymoon on the Coromandel Coast. 我们在科罗曼德尔海岸度蜜月。 | The antique chest was made of beautiful Coromandel wood.  这个古董箱子是用美丽的科罗曼德尔木制成的。| synonyms: coast, shore (for the geographical location); hardwood, timber (for the wood) | antonyms: inland (for the geographical location) | noun
