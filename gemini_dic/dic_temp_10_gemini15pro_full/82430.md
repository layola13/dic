gruffest:/ˈɡrʌfɪst/| adj.|最粗暴的，最生硬的|Superlative form of gruff: most gruff.  It describes a voice or manner that is rough and unfriendly.|He answered in his gruffest voice, clearly annoyed by the interruption. 他用最粗暴的声音回答，显然对被打扰感到恼火。|Example: The gruffest bouncer I've ever seen guarded the club entrance.  我见过的最粗暴的保镖守卫着俱乐部的入口。|Synonyms: harshest, roughest, surliest, brusquest|Antonyms: gentlest, kindest, sweetest, smoothest|adjective
