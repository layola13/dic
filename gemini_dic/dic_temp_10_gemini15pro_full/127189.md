natureless:/ˈneɪtʃərlɪs/
adjective
无自然的；缺乏自然美的；不自然的
lacking natural qualities; artificial
The concrete jungle felt natureless and oppressive.  这片钢筋水泥的丛林让人感觉毫无自然气息，令人压抑。
The artist's work was criticized for its natureless, sterile aesthetic.  这位艺术家的作品因其缺乏自然美感和毫无生气的审美而受到批评。
近义词: artificial, unnatural, sterile, lifeless
反义词: natural, authentic, organic, vibrant
词性: adjective
