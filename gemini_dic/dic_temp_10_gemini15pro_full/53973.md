discrepancy:/dɪˈskrepənsi/| n.|差异，不符，不一致|a difference or inconsistency between two or more facts, figures, or claims|There was a discrepancy between the amount of money deposited and the amount recorded.  存入的金额与记录的金额之间存在差异。|例句：The police noticed a discrepancy in his statements. 警方注意到他的陈述中存在矛盾之处。|近义词：difference, inconsistency, variation, divergence, disparity|反义词：agreement, consistency, conformity|词性:名词

discrepancies:/dɪˈskrepənsiz/| n.|差异，不符，不一致（复数）|differences or inconsistencies between two or more facts, figures, or claims|There were several discrepancies between the two reports. 这两份报告之间有一些差异。|例句：Investigators found discrepancies in the witness accounts. 调查人员发现证人陈述中存在出入。|近义词：differences, inconsistencies, variations, divergences, disparities|反义词：agreements, consistencies, conformities|词性:名词
