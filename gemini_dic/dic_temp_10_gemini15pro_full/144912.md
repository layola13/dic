over-suspicious: /ˌoʊvər səˈspɪʃəs/ | adj. | 过分怀疑的，过于多疑的 | Excessively suspicious; inclined to suspect without sufficient reason. | He was over-suspicious of everyone he met, always assuming they had ulterior motives. 他对他遇到的每个人都过分怀疑，总是认为他们别有用心。|  The over-suspicious detective saw a conspiracy in every coincidence.  过于多疑的侦探在每个巧合中都看到了阴谋。| 近义词: distrustful, wary, skeptical, paranoid | 反义词: trusting, gullible, naive | 词性: 形容词

词源:

From over + suspicious.  "Over" acts as an intensifier, meaning "excessively" or "too much." "Suspicious" comes from the Latin word "suspiciosus," meaning "full of suspicion."
