mundil:/ˈmʊndɪl/ | n. | 世界；宇宙；人类 |  In Norse mythology, Mundilfari was a personification of the world, and Mundil was one of his two children (the other being Sol, the sun).  The name means "time-measurer" or "the one who moves according to time". | In Norse myth, Mundil was placed in the sky by the gods to drive the moon's chariot. | 近义词: world, cosmos, universe | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
