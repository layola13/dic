doghood:/ˈdɒɡhʊd/ /ˈdɑːɡhʊd/ | n. |狗的时期，狗的状态|The state or time of being a dog.  It refers to the period of a dog's life or the characteristics and experiences associated with being a dog. |He spent his childhood surrounded by animals, and reached a sort of happy doghood, always with canine companions.|近义词:caninity, dogdom|反义词: (None -  there's no direct opposite to the state of being a dog) | Noun
