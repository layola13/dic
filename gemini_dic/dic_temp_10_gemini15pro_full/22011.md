boilingly:/ˈbɔɪlɪŋli/
adverb
沸腾地，热情地
in a boiling manner; with intense heat or passion.
The tea was boilingly hot.  这茶烫得像开水一样。
She spoke boilingly about the injustice. 她愤怒地谈论着不公正。
近义词:fervently, passionately, intensely
反义词:coldly, apathetically
词性:adverb

词源: From boil + -ing + -ly.  来自 boil（沸腾）+ -ing（现在分词后缀）+ -ly（副词后缀）。
