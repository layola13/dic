menispermine:/məˈnɪspərˌmiːn/
noun
美尼士宁
An alkaloid, C₁₈H₂₄N₂, found in the seeds of *Anamirta cocculus* (fish berries) and related plants.
Menispermine is a toxic substance that can cause convulsions. 美尼士宁是一种有毒物质，会导致抽搐。
Menispermine is used in some traditional medicines. 美尼士宁用于一些传统药物中。
Synonyms: cocculine, picrotoxin
Antonyms: None
