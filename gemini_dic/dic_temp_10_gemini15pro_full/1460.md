acequia: /əˈseɪkiə/ (US), /əˈsekiə/ (UK)| n.| 灌溉渠；沟渠| An irrigation ditch or canal.| The farmers share the water from the acequia. 农民们共享来自沟渠的水。

The acequia is essential for irrigating the fields. 这条沟渠对灌溉田地至关重要。|近义词：canal, ditch, channel, furrow|反义词: None|词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *acequia*, from Arabic *as-saqiya* (الساقية), meaning "watercourse" or "that which waters".  Ultimately from the Arabic root *s-q-y*, related to watering or irrigation.
