binotonous: /baɪˈnɒtənəs/ | adj. | 双音的，单调的 | Having two tones, especially two musical tones, or characterized by monotony. | The binotonous call of the cuckoo echoed through the forest. 布谷鸟双音的叫声在森林中回荡。| The speaker's binotonous voice made the lecture incredibly boring. 演讲者单调的声音使讲座极其无聊。 | 近义词：monotonous, dull, tedious, boring | 反义词：varied, diverse, interesting, stimulating | 词性: adjective

词源: "bi-" (two) + "tonous" (toned). From Latin "bi-" (two) + Greek "tonos" (tone).
