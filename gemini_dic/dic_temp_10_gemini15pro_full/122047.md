mistaken:/mɪˈsteɪkən/| adj.|错误的，弄错的|Wrong in one's opinion or judgment. Having misunderstanding or misconception about something.|例句：I was mistaken about the time of the meeting. 我弄错了会议的时间。|近义词：wrong, incorrect, erroneous, inaccurate|反义词：correct, right, accurate|词性:形容词

mistaken:/mɪˈsteɪkən/| v. |误解，弄错（mistake的过去分词）|To understand, interpret, or identify incorrectly.| 例句：She mistook him for her brother. 她把他误认为是她的兄弟。|近义词：misunderstand, misinterpret, misidentify|反义词：understand, comprehend|词性:动词
