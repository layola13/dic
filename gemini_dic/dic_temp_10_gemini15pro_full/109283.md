lemonish:/ˈlɛmənɪʃ/| adj.|略带柠檬味的；有点像柠檬的|Somewhat resembling a lemon in taste, color, or smell.|The drink had a lemonish tang.  这饮料略带柠檬味。|Synonyms: citrusy, tart, tangy|Antonyms: sweet, bland|adjective

lemonish:/ˈlɛmənɪʃ/| adj.|略带柠檬色的；淡黄色的|Somewhat resembling a lemon in color.|The walls were painted a lemonish yellow. 墙壁被漆成了淡柠檬黄。|Synonyms: yellowish, pale yellow, light yellow|Antonyms: dark, deep, rich (color)|adjective
