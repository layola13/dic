hat:/hæt/
n.
帽子
A covering for the head, often made of felt, straw, or cloth, and typically having a shaped crown and brim.
She wore a wide-brimmed hat to protect herself from the sun. 她戴了一顶宽边帽来遮阳。

近义词:cap, bonnet, headgear
反义词:(无直接反义词)
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hæt (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *hattuz (compare West Frisian hat, Dutch hoed, German Hut), from Proto-Indo-European *kadʰ- (“to cover, protect”).
