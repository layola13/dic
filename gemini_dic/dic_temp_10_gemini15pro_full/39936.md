constructability:/kənˌstrʌktəˈbɪləti/| n. |可施工性，可建造性|The ease with which a structure can be built, taking into account factors such as design, materials, and site conditions.|The architect considered the constructability of the bridge design before finalizing the plans.  建筑师在最终确定计划之前考虑了桥梁设计的可施工性。|Synonyms: buildability, feasibility|Antonyms: impracticability, unbuildability|noun
