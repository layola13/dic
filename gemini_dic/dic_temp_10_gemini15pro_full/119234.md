miasmatist:/maɪˈæzmətɪst/| n.|瘴气论者，瘴气学家|A person who believes in the miasma theory (the theory that diseases are caused by miasma, a noxious form of "bad air").|The miasmatist argued that the cholera outbreak was due to the foul smells emanating from the river.  瘴气论者认为霍乱的爆发是由于河里散发出的恶臭造成的。|近义词:contagionist|反义词:None|Noun

miasm:/ˈmaɪæzmə/| n.|瘴气，臭气，不良影响|A noxious or poisonous atmosphere formerly thought to rise from swamps and putrid matter and cause disease. Also, a pervasive corrupting influence.|The miasm from the swamp was unbearable. 沼泽地里的瘴气令人难以忍受。|近义词:stench, effluvium, fetor|反义词:fragrance, perfume|Noun
