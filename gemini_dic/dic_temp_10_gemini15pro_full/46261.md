cytoplasmically: /ˌsaɪtəʊˈplæzmɪkli/ | adv. | 细胞质地，在细胞质中 |  Existing or occurring within the cytoplasm of a cell. |  The protein is cytoplasmically localized.  这种蛋白质位于细胞质中。 | synonym: intracellularly | antonym: extracellularly | adverb

cytoplasm: /ˈsaɪtəʊplæzəm/ | n. | 细胞质 | The material or protoplasm within a living cell, excluding the nucleus. | The cytoplasm contains various organelles. 细胞质包含各种细胞器。 | synonym: protoplasm | antonym: nucleus | noun
