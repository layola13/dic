darned:/dɑːrnd/ | adj. | 讨厌的，该死的；缝补过的 | used to emphasize how annoying something is; sewn up |  例句：This darned computer keeps crashing! 这该死的电脑老是死机！

例句：He has darned socks. 他缝补了袜子。

近义词：cursed, damned, blasted, annoying, irritating, mended, repaired | 反义词：blessed, loved, fortunate | 词性：形容词
