canvases:/ˈkænvəsɪz/
noun
帆布（复数）;油画（复数）
Pieces of cloth on which artists paint, or the paintings themselves.
The artist stretched the canvases before beginning to paint. 艺术家在开始绘画之前先绷紧了画布。
She had several canvases on display in the gallery. 她在画廊里展出了几幅油画。
synonyms: paintings, pictures, artworks
antonyms: (For the material) None.  (For the artwork) sculptures, installations.
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French canevas, from Late Latin cannabis "hemp, canvas," from Greek kannabis.
