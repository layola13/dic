boisterous:/ˈbɔɪstərəs/| adj.|喧闹的；狂暴的；爱吵闹的|Full of noisy energy and high spirits.  Characterized by or expressive of exuberance and high spirits.|The boisterous crowd cheered for their team. 喧闹的人群为他们的队伍欢呼。|The party was a boisterous affair with loud music and dancing. 派对很热闹，有大声的音乐和舞蹈。|近义词：noisy, loud, rowdy, rambunctious|反义词：quiet, calm, peaceful|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle French *boistreus*, from Old French *boiste*, "box, case, container". The original sense was "rough, clumsy," perhaps from the notion of bumping into things like a box being moved, later evolving to "noisy, unruly."
