dodoisms:/ˈdoʊdoʊɪzəmz/ | n. |  过时观念，愚蠢的行为 |  Outmoded or foolish beliefs or practices, especially those characteristic of the dodo.  These ideas are real dodoisms. 这些想法真是过时。|  "Clinging to those outdated marketing strategies is pure dodoism in today's digital age."  在当今数字时代，坚持那些过时的营销策略纯粹是愚蠢的行为。 | 近义词：anachronisms, absurdities, follies | 反义词：wisdom,  modernity | 词性: 名词

词源：From "dodo" + "-ism," referring to the extinct dodo bird, often seen as a symbol of stupidity due to its inability to adapt and survive.
