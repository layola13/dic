cagiest:/ˈkeɪdʒiɪst/| adj.|最谨慎的，最守口如瓶的|Superlative adjective of cagey: most cagey.  Characterized by great caution and reticence, especially in revealing information or intentions.|He gave a cagy response, revealing nothing about his plans. 他给出了一个谨慎的回答，没有透露他的计划。|近义词：secretive, guarded, wary, noncommittal|反义词：open, frank, candid|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From "cage," referring to keeping something enclosed or hidden + "-y" (forming adjectives indicating a characteristic) + "-est" (superlative suffix).
