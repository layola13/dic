hajj:/hædʒ/ | n. | 朝觐，麦加朝圣 |  A pilgrimage to Mecca, Saudi Arabia, considered the holiest city in Islam.  It is one of the Five Pillars of Islam and is a religious duty for all Muslims who are physically and financially able to undertake the journey, at least once in their lifetime. |  Example:  My grandfather made his hajj to Mecca last year.  我祖父去年去麦加朝圣了。

Example:  Performing the hajj is a deeply spiritual experience for Muslims.  对于穆斯林来说，进行朝圣是一次深刻的精神体验。 |  Synonym: pilgrimage | Antonym: N/A | noun
