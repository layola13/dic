descantest: /dɪˈskæntɪst/ | n. | 歌手；演奏者；评论者 | A person who sings or plays a descant; one who comments or discourses. |  He was a renowned descantest, known for his intricate melodies. 他是一位著名的歌手，以其复杂的旋律而闻名。 |  She performed as a descantest at the local church. 她在当地教堂担任歌手。 | synonyms: singer, musician, commentator, analyst | antonyms: listener, audience | noun


Etymology: From the Old French "deschanter" meaning "to sing," ultimately from Latin "dis-" (apart) + "cantare" (to sing).
