coloradoite:/kɒləˈrædəʊaɪt/ /kɑːləˈrædoʊaɪt/
noun
科罗拉多矿
A rare telluride mineral, mercury telluride (HgTe),  that crystallizes in the isometric system. It is named after the state of Colorado, USA, where it was first discovered.
Coloradoite is a rare mineral found in hydrothermal veins. 科罗拉多矿是一种罕见的矿物，发现于热液矿脉中。
Coloradoite is a valuable ore of mercury. 科罗拉多矿是重要的汞矿石。
近义词: telluride (碲化物), calaverite (卡拉维矿)
反义词: None (无)
词性: n.

etymology->
From Colorado + -ite (mineral suffix).  源自Colorado（科罗拉多州）+ -ite（矿物后缀）。
