malnourishment:/ˌmælnəˈrɪʃmənt/ | n. |营养不良|The state of having poor nutrition because of an insufficient or poorly balanced diet or faulty absorption of nutrients.|The child suffered from severe malnourishment. 这个孩子患有严重的营养不良。|Synonyms: undernourishment, malnutrition, starvation|Antonyms: nourishment,  nutritiousness|noun

malnourished:/ˌmælnəˈrɪʃt/ | adj. |营养不良的|Suffering from malnourishment.|Many children in developing countries are malnourished. 许多发展中国家的儿童营养不良。|Synonyms: underfed, undernourished, starved|Antonyms: nourished, well-fed|adjective
