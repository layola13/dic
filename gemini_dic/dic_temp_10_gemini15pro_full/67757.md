eyecup:/ˈaɪkʌp/| n.|目镜罩，眼罩|A cup-shaped device attached to a camera viewfinder or telescope eyepiece to shield the eye from stray light and help maintain proper eye position.|He used an eyecup on his camera to block out the glare of the sun. 他在相机上使用了目镜罩来遮挡阳光的眩光。|近义词：eye shield, viewfinder shield|反义词:|名词
