commencement:/kəˈmɛnsmənt/ /kəˈmɛnsmənt/| n.|开始，毕业典礼|a beginning or start; a ceremony in which degrees or diplomas are conferred on graduating students|例句：The commencement of the project has been delayed due to funding issues. 项目的开始由于资金问题被推迟了。

例句：Her commencement speech was inspiring and moving. 她的毕业典礼演讲鼓舞人心，感人至深。|近义词：beginning, start, initiation, inception, graduation (ceremony)|反义词：end, conclusion, termination|词性:noun

commence:/kəˈmɛns/ /kəˈmɛns/| v.|开始，着手|begin; start|例句：The construction work will commence next week. 建筑工程将于下周开始。

例句：They commenced negotiations for a new contract. 他们开始就新合同进行谈判。|近义词：begin, start, initiate, inaugurate|反义词：end, conclude, finish, terminate|词性:verb
