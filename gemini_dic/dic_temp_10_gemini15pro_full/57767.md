词源：The term "Dreamtime" is a translation of the word *alcheringa*, from the Arrernte language of Central Australia.  It became widely adopted by anthropologists and the public in the mid-20th century.  It's important to note that different Aboriginal groups have their own specific terms and beliefs related to this concept.
