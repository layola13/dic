dustsheet: /ˈdʌstʃiːt/
n.
防尘布；盖布
A large piece of cloth used to protect furniture, floors, etc. from dust, paint, etc.
She spread a dustsheet over the sofa before starting to paint. 她开始粉刷前在沙发上铺了一块防尘布。

近义词: drop cloth, dust cover
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps something like "cleaning cloth" in a specific context)
词性: noun
