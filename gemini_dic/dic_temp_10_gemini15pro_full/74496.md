frantic:/ˈfræntɪk/| adj.|疯狂的，狂乱的，手忙脚乱的|Marked by uncontrolled excitement or emotion. Showing wild or distraught agitation.|She made a frantic search for her keys. 她疯狂地寻找她的钥匙。|近义词：frenzied, desperate, hectic, wild|反义词：calm, composed, peaceful|词性:形容词
frantic:/ˈfræntɪk/| adj.|（因恐惧、焦虑）发狂似的|Distraught with fear, anxiety, or other emotion.|He was frantic with worry. 他担心得发狂。|近义词：distraught, beside oneself, agitated|反义词：calm, collected, serene|词性:形容词

词源:
From French *frénétique*, from Late Latin *phreneticus*, from Greek *phrenetikos* (“delirious, mad”), from *phrenitis* (“frenzy”), from *phren* (“mind, diaphragm”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\*ḱerh₂-*.
