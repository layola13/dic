overconsumes:/ˌoʊvər kənˈsuːmz/
verb
过度消费
Consumes something to excess.
例句：He overconsumes sugary drinks, which is bad for his health. 他过度饮用含糖饮料，这对他健康有害。

例句：The country overconsumes resources, leading to environmental problems.  这个国家过度消耗资源，导致环境问题。

近义词：overspends, overindulges, squanders
反义词：conserves, saves, underconsumes
词性：v.
