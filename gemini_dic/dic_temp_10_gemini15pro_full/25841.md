burgoos:/ˈbɜːrɡwɑːz/ /ˈbʊərʒwɑː/ | n. adj.| 资产阶级；中产阶级；庸俗的；市侩的 |  From French *bourgeois*, from Old French *burgeis*, inhabitant of a *burg* (fortified town), from *burg* "town, borough". Originally, a member of the middle class, particularly one associated with trade or commerce in a town. Can be used as a noun to refer to the social class or as an adjective describing something as characteristic of that class, often with connotations of materialism, conventionality, and a lack of social conscience. | Example: The restaurant was popular with the local *bourgeois*. 这家餐馆很受当地中产阶级的欢迎。| Example: He dismissed their concerns as *bourgeois* sentimentality. 他认为他们的担忧不过是资产阶级的多愁善感。 | Synonym: middle-class, materialistic, conventional | Antonym: proletarian, bohemian, unconventional | Noun, Adjective
