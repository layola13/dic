comanagers:/ˌkoʊˈmænɪdʒərz/
n.
共同经理；共同管理者
Two comanagers were appointed to oversee the project.  两位共同经理被任命来监督这个项目。
The comanagers worked well together, complementing each other's strengths. 这两位共同经理合作得很好，彼此优势互补。
Synonyms: co-directors, joint managers
Antonyms: sole manager, single director
noun
