ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hlēapan, from Proto-Germanic *hlaupaną (“to run, jump, leap”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)lewə- (“to slide, flow”). Cognate with Dutch lopen (“to walk, run”), German laufen (“to run”), Gothic  hlaupan (“to run, leap, jump”).
