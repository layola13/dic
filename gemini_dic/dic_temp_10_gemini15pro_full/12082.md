artiness:/ˈɑːrtinəs/
noun
做作;附庸风雅;装腔作势
The quality of being excessively or affectedly artistic.  A deliberate display of artistic sensibility or refinement, often seen as pretentious or superficial.
Her artiness was evident in her bohemian clothing and constant talk of obscure films. 她穿着波西米亚风格的衣服，总是谈论一些晦涩难懂的电影，这充分展现了她的做作。
The restaurant's decor had a certain artiness that felt contrived. 这家餐厅的装潢有一种刻意营造的艺术感，让人感觉很做作。
pretentiousness, affectation, artificiality
naturalness, genuineness, sincerity
