half-praising:/ˌhɑːfˈpreɪzɪŋ/| adj.|半表扬的，略带赞扬的|Expressing mild or qualified praise; praising with reservations.|Her review of the book was half-praising, acknowledging its strengths while pointing out its flaws. 她对这本书的评论是半表扬的，既承认了它的优点，也指出了它的缺点。|Near Synonyms: lukewarm, faintly praising, qualified|Antonyms: wholeheartedly praising, effusive, enthusiastic|Adjective
