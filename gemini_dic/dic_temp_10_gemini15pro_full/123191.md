monocarpic: /ˌmɒnəʊˈkɑːpɪk/ /ˌmɑːnəʊˈkɑːrpɪk/
adjective
单次结实性的
(of a plant) flowering only once and then dying.
例句：The agave plant is monocarpic, meaning it blooms only once in its lifetime. 龙舌兰是单次结实植物，意味着它一生只开一次花。

例句：Many bamboo species are monocarpic and may take several decades to flower. 许多竹子种类都是单次结实性的，可能需要几十年才会开花。

近义词：hapaxanth, semelparous
反义词：polycarpic, iteroparous
词性: adjective
