fess:/fɛs/

fess:/fɛs/
|n. |供认; 坦白; 费用; 会费|a confession; a fee|The club has a yearly fess of $50.  这个俱乐部每年的会费是50美元。|近义词：confession, admission, fee, dues|反义词：denial, concealment|noun
