diffidence:/ˈdɪfɪdəns/| n.|缺乏自信；胆怯；羞怯|Modesty or shyness resulting from a lack of self-confidence.|She overcame her natural diffidence and spoke with great conviction. 她克服了天生的胆怯，充满自信地发言。|近义词：shyness, timidity, hesitancy, insecurity, self-doubt|反义词：confidence, boldness, assertiveness, self-assurance|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from Latin diffidentia, from diffidens ‘distrustful’, from the verb diffidere, from dis- (expressing reversal) + fidere ‘to trust’.
