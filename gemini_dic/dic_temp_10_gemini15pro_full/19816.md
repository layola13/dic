bimedial:/ˌbaɪˈmiːdiəl/
| adj. |双中线的；二中数的| Relating to or involving two medials; (Geometry) designating or of a magnitude which is the product of two medial straight lines.| The bimedial line is of interest to geometers.  这条双中线对几何学家来说很有趣。| medial, intermediate |  N/A | adjective
