heterochromic:/ˌhɛtərəˈkrɒmɪk/ /ˌhɛtərəˈkroʊmɪk/ | adj. | 异色的，杂色的 | Having two different-colored eyes or having different colors within one eye. |  Example: The cat was heterochromic, with one blue eye and one green eye.  这只猫有异色症，一只眼睛是蓝色的，另一只眼睛是绿色的。

Example:  Heterochromic iridium can be a striking feature. 异色虹膜可以是一个引人注目的特征。 | Near Synonyms:  variegated, multicolored, polychromatic | Antonym: homochromic | Word type: adjective
