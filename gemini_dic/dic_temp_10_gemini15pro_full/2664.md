adorned: /əˈdɔːrnd/ (UK) /əˈdɔːrnd/ (US)| adj.|装饰的，修饰的|Made more beautiful or attractive by adding something.|例句：She wore an adorned dress with intricate beading. 她穿着一件饰有复杂珠饰的华丽连衣裙。

The Christmas tree was adorned with colorful lights and ornaments. 圣诞树上装饰着彩灯和饰品。|近义词：decorated, embellished, ornamented, beautified|反义词：plain, simple, unadorned, bare|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the late Middle English word *adornen*, which comes from the Old French word *adorner*, meaning "to adorn, decorate, embellish."  Ultimately, it derives from the Latin *adōrnāre*, composed of *ad-* (to, towards) and *ōrnāre* (to equip, furnish, adorn), related to *ōrnāmentum* (equipment, ornament).
