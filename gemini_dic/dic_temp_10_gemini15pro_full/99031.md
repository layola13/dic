interallelic:/ˌɪntərəˈliːlɪk/
| adj. |等位基因间的|Occurring between different alleles of a gene.|Interallelic complementation can restore partial function. 等位基因间互补可以恢复部分功能。|
|近义词: intergenic (although not strictly synonymous, refers to interactions *between* genes rather than within)|反义词: intra-allelic (referring to interactions within the same allele)|词性: adjective
