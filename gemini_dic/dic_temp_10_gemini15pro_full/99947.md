internuncial:/ˌɪntərˈnʌnʃəl/
adjective
中间神经元的；介于神经元之间的
Connecting two or more neurons; situated between two other neurons and conveying impulses from one to the other.
The internuncial neurons relay signals between sensory and motor neurons.  中间神经元在感觉神经元和运动神经元之间传递信号。
The drug blocks the action of internuncial neurons in the spinal cord.  这种药物阻断了脊髓中中间神经元的活动。
Synonyms: connecting, intermediary, relay
Antonyms: terminal, end, peripheral
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From Latin *inter* (between) + *nuntius* (messenger) + *-al* (suffix forming adjectives).
