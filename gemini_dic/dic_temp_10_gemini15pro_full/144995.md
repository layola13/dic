overactivenesses:/ˌoʊvərækˈtɪvnɪsɪz/| n. |过度活跃，活动过度|The state of being excessively or abnormally active.  It refers to instances of heightened activity beyond what is considered typical or normal.|His overactivenesses in class disrupted the other students. 他在课堂上的过度活跃扰乱了其他学生。|近义词：hyperactivity, restlessness, excitability|反义词：inactivity, lethargy, calmness|词性: Noun
Etymology: From "overactive" + "-ness" + "-es" (plural). "Over-" is an Old English prefix meaning "excessive," "active" comes from the Latin "activus," meaning "busy," and "-ness" is an Old English suffix forming nouns of state or quality. The "-es" makes the word plural.
