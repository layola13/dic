limestone:/ˈlaɪmˌstoʊn/| n. |石灰岩，石灰石|A sedimentary rock consisting mainly of calcium carbonate, formed from the remains of marine organisms.|The White Cliffs of Dover are made of limestone. 多佛白崖是由石灰岩构成的。|Synonyms: calcite, chalk, marble|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a type of rock)|noun
