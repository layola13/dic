massedly:/ˈmæsɪdli/
adverb
大量地，集体地，成群地
in a massed or massive way; in great numbers.
The troops were massedly deployed along the border. 部队大量部署在边境线上。
The protesters massedly gathered in front of the government building. 抗议者成群结队地聚集在政府大楼前。
近义词: en masse, collectively, in droves, in force
反义词: sparsely, individually, scatteredly
词性: 副词

词源:  From "massed" + "-ly". "Massed" comes from the verb "mass," meaning to gather or form into a mass, which ultimately derives from the Latin "massa" meaning "lump" or "kneaded dough."  The suffix "-ly" transforms adjectives into adverbs.
