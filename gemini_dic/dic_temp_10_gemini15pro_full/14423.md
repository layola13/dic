autumn: /ˈɔːtəm/ (British) /ˈɑːtəm/ (American) | n. | 秋天，秋季 | The season after summer and before winter, when the leaves change color and fall from the trees. | The leaves turn beautiful shades of red and gold in autumn. 秋天树叶变成了美丽的红色和金色。|  The crisp air of autumn is invigorating. 秋天清爽的空气令人精神振奋。 | synonyms: fall (American English), autumnal season | antonyms: spring, summer, winter | noun
