buoyant:/ˈbɔɪənt/ | adj. | 有浮力的；快乐的；上涨的 | Able to float easily; cheerful and optimistic; (of a market or prices) tending to rise. |  The life jacket kept her buoyant in the water. 救生衣让她在水中漂浮。 | synonyms: cheerful, optimistic, lighthearted, jaunty, perky, airy, bouncy, resilient, floating, floatable, light | antonyms: depressed, gloomy, pessimistic, sinking, heavy | etymology: From Old French *boiant "floating," present participle of boier "to float," from Gallo-Romance *buoyare, from Vulgar Latin *boviare, from Latin boia "fetter, chain, collar," later "buoy." | property: adjective
