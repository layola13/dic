nestorian:/nɛˈstɔːriən/ /nɛˈstɔːriən/| adj. n.|涅斯托利派的，涅斯托利派教徒|Relating to the doctrines of Nestorius, a 5th-century patriarch of Constantinople, who held that Christ had distinct human and divine natures.  A follower of Nestorius.|例句：The Nestorian church spread eastward through Persia and into China.  涅斯托利派教会向东传播，经波斯传入中国。

|例句：He was accused of being a Nestorian heretic. 他被指控为涅斯托利派异端。
|近义词：heretical, heterodox|反义词：orthodox|词性：adjective, noun
