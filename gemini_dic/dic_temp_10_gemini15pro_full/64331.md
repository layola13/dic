eradicants:/ɪˈrædɪkənts/| n.| 根除剂；消灭剂 |Substances used to eradicate something, typically a disease or pest.| The farmer used powerful eradicants to eliminate the fungal infection affecting his crops. 农民使用强力根除剂来消除影响他作物的真菌感染。|近义词： pesticides, fungicides, herbicides |反义词:  promoters, enhancers |词性: 名词

eradicate:/ɪˈrædɪkeɪt/| vt.| 根除；消灭；扑灭 | To remove or destroy completely.|  The government launched a campaign to eradicate malaria. 政府发起了一场消灭疟疾的运动。 |近义词：eliminate, exterminate, abolish |反义词: cultivate, promote |词性: 动词
