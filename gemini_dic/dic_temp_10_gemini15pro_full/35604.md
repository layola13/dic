coas: /kəʊst/ /koʊst/ | n. | 海岸，沿海地区 | The coastline is rugged and beautiful.  海岸线崎岖而美丽。| The ship sailed along the coast. 这艘船沿着海岸航行。 | 近义词: shore, seashore, coastline | 反义词: inland | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *coste, from Old French coste, from Latin costa (“rib, side, flank”), from Proto-Italic *kostā, from Proto-Indo-European *kost-. Cognate with Italian costa, Spanish costa, Portuguese costa, Romanian coastă, Occitan costa, Catalan costa.
