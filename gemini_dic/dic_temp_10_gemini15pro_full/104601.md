kanzu:/ˈkɑːnzuː/
n.
长袍，罩袍
A long, white robe worn by men in East Africa, particularly in Uganda and Tanzania.
He wore a traditional kanzu for the ceremony.  他穿着一件传统的坎祖参加仪式。
garment, robe, gown
cloak, coat
