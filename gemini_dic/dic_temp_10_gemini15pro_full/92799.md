icky-poo:/ˈɪki puː/| adj.|令人作呕的，恶心的|Something unpleasant or disgusting, often used in a childish or humorous way.  It combines "icky" (itself a childish term for disgusting) with "poo," a euphemism for feces.|例句： "Ew, that's icky-poo!"  “哎，那真恶心！”|近义词：gross, disgusting, yucky, nasty|反义词:pleasant, appealing, nice|adjective
