non-physical:/ˌnɒnˈfɪzɪkəl/
adjective
非物质的；非实体的
Not consisting of matter; not perceptible by the senses; not physical.
例句：
Many religions believe in a non-physical soul. 许多宗教信仰非物质的灵魂。

例句：
The effects of stress can be non-physical, such as anxiety and depression.  压力的影响可以是非物质的，例如焦虑和抑郁。

近义词：immaterial, intangible, spiritual, metaphysical
反义词：physical, material, tangible
词性: adjective
