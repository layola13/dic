glew:/ɡluː/| v. | 粘合；黏住 | To stick or adhere with glue or a similar sticky substance. |  He glewed the pieces of wood together. 他把几块木头粘在一起。

| Example: She glewed the broken vase back together. 她把破花瓶粘回去了。 |  Synonyms: glue, paste, stick, adhere, bond | Antonyms: separate, detach, unglue | verb
