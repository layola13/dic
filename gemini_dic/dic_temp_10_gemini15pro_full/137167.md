nonsuccesses:/ˌnɒn.səkˈses.ɪz/ | n. | 失败；未成功；没有成就 | Lack of successes; instances of not achieving a desired outcome. | The report highlighted the nonsuccesses of the project.  报告强调了该项目的失败之处。|  The team analyzed the reasons behind their nonsuccesses. 团队分析了他们失败背后的原因。 | 近义词: failures, setbacks, defeats | 反义词: successes, achievements, triumphs | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "success" + "-es" (plural suffix).  "Success" comes from Old French "succes" and ultimately from Latin "successus," meaning "advance, progress, a good result."
