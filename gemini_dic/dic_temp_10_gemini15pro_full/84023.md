half-educated:/ˌhæf ˈɛdʒʊkeɪtɪd/
adj.
半吊子; 受过一点教育的; 一知半解的
Having some education but not enough to be considered well-educated or fully knowledgeable.
He made some half-educated guesses about the meaning of the poem. 他对这首诗的含义做了一些一知半解的猜测。
Near synonyms: poorly educated, partially educated, undereducated, smatteringly educated
Antonyms: well-educated, highly educated, fully educated, erudite, knowledgeable
