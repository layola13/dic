bogofs:/ˈbɒɡɒfs/ (UK) /ˈbɑːɡɑːfs/ (US)| n.|买一送一（商品促销方式）|Buy One Get One Free (often used in the plural to refer to multiple such offers).  A sales promotion where a customer buys one item and gets a second identical item free.|例句：The supermarket is advertising lots of bogofs this week. 这家超市这周有很多买一送一的促销活动。|例句：I stocked up on pasta thanks to the bogofs. 多亏了买一送一活动，我囤了很多意大利面。|近义词：two for one, BOGO, special offer|反义词:standard price, regular price|词性:noun
