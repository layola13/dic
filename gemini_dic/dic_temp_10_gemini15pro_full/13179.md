at full speed:/æt fʊl spiːd/
|adv.|全速地；以最高速度；飞快地|Moving or operating as fast as possible. |The car raced down the track at full speed. 汽车全速在赛道上飞驰。|The ambulance rushed to the hospital at full speed. 救护车全速赶往医院。|Synonym: rapidly, quickly, swiftly, speedily, hastily|Antonym: slowly, gradually, leisurely|adverb
