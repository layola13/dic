beanfest:/ˈbiːnfɛst/| n.|豆子盛宴，豆子节|A festival or celebration centered around beans or dishes made with beans. It can be a casual gathering or a more organized event.|例句：The annual beanfest is a highlight of the summer.  一年一度的豆子节是夏季的亮点。

例句：They had a beanfest in the backyard, with chili, baked beans, and all sorts of bean salads. 他们在后院举办了一个豆子盛宴，有辣椒、烤豆子，还有各种各样的豆子沙拉。|近义词：potluck, feast, celebration|反义词:|词性:名词
