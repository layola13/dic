connoisseurships:/kənˌnɔɪˈsɜːrʃɪps/| n. |鉴赏力；鉴赏家的身份；专家意见|The state or quality of being a connoisseur; expertise or refined taste in a particular field. |His connoisseurship in wine is well-known. 他在葡萄酒方面的鉴赏力是众所周知的。|近义词：expertise, refinement, discernment|反义词：ignorance, inexperience|词性:名词

词源: From French *connoisseur*, ultimately from Latin *cognoscere* "to know."
