ephoral:/ˈɛfərəl/| adj.|短暂的；执政官的；监督者的|Relating to an ephor (a magistrate or overseer in ancient Sparta), or characterized by the qualities of an ephor, such as strictness or authority. Also, short-lived or transient.|The ephoral powers of the council were extensive.  委员会的监督权力很大。|Synonyms: transient, ephemeral, fleeting, short-lived|Antonyms: permanent, enduring, lasting|Adjective
