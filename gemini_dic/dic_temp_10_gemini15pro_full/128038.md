nerdy:/ˈnɜːdi/ (US) /ˈnɜːdi/ (UK)| adj.|书呆子气的；沉迷于特定（通常不受欢迎）兴趣的；缺乏社交技巧的|Characterized by great enthusiasm for and knowledge about a particular subject, especially one of specialist or niche interest. Often associated with a lack of social skills or fashion sense.|He's a bit nerdy, but he's really smart. 他有点书呆子气，但他真的很聪明。|例句：
She's got a nerdy obsession with Star Wars. 她对《星球大战》有着书呆子般的痴迷。
