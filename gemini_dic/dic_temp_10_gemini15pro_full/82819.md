gujaratis:/ˌɡuːdʒəˈrɑːtiz/ | n. |古吉拉特人| People from the state of Gujarat in India, or people of Gujarati descent. | Many Gujaratis have businesses in East Africa. 许多古吉拉特人在东非有生意。| Gujaratis are known for their entrepreneurial spirit. 古吉拉特人以其企业家精神而闻名。 |Indians, Asians| None |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Gujarati, the name of the state and language of western India. Ultimately from Gurjara, the name of a people who ruled the area from the 6th-12th centuries.
