nubuck:/ˈnjuːbʌk/| n.|磨砂皮，绒面革|A top-grain cattle leather that has been sanded or buffed on the grain side, or outside, to give a slight nap of short protein fibers, producing a velvet-like surface.|例句：I prefer nubuck shoes because they are soft and comfortable. 我更喜欢磨砂皮鞋，因为它们柔软舒适。

例句：Nubuck leather is more delicate than traditional leather. 磨砂皮革比传统皮革更娇贵。|近义词：suede, buckskin|反义词:full-grain leather|词性:noun
