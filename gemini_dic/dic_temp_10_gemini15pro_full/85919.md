headlessness:/ˈhedləsnəs/| n. |无头状态，无领导状态|The state of being without a head or leader. This can refer to a literal lack of a head (as in a decapitation) or a figurative one (as in an organization lacking a leader).|The sudden headlessness of the company caused chaos.|Near synonyms: leaderlessness, decapitation, acephaly|Near antonyms: leadership, direction|noun
