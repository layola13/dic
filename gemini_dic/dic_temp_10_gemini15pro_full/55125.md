disrupts:/dɪsˈrʌpts/| v. |扰乱，中断，打乱|to interrupt the normal progress or activity of something|The loud noise disrupted my concentration. 嘈杂的声音扰乱了我的注意力。|近义词：interrupt, disturb, upset, interfere with|反义词:organize, arrange, facilitate|词性:动词
Etymology: From Latin *disruptus*, past participle of *disrumpere*  ("to break apart"), from *dis-* ("apart") + *rumpere* ("to break").
