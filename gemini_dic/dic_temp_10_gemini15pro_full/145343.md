overbulk:/ˈoʊvərˌbʌlk/| vt. n.|过大，体积过大，过载|To make or become too bulky or large; to overload.  To exceed the bulk allowed or required.|The shipping company refused to accept the crate because it was overbulked.  运输公司拒绝接受这个板条箱，因为它体积过大。|近义词：overload, overburden|反义词：underload|词性:动词,名词

overbulk:/ˈoʊvərˌbʌlk/| n.|过剩的体积，过大的尺寸|Excessive bulk or size.|The overbulk of the package made it difficult to handle.  包裹体积过大，难以搬运。|近义词：excess, surplus|反义词：deficiency, shortage|词性:名词
