felter:/ˈfɛltər/| n. v.|毡匠，制毡工人；使缠结；使毡化|A person who makes felt. To become matted or tangled. To process (wool, hair, or other fiber) into felt.|例句：The felter carefully shaped the wool into a hat. 毡匠仔细地把羊毛塑造成一顶帽子。  The cat's fur felted together in the rain. 猫的毛在雨中缠结在一起了。|近义词：mat, tangle, compress (v.); feltmaker (n.)|反义词:smooth, untangle, separate (v.)|n.
felter:/ˈfɛltər/| v. |毡化，缠结；(声音)颤抖，(步伐)蹒跚|To become matted or tangled, like felt; to speak or move in a hesitant or unsteady way.|例句：His voice feltered as he tried to explain. 当他试图解释的时候，他的声音颤抖了。 The hiker's steps feltered as he climbed the steep mountain.  登山者在攀登陡峭的山峰时步伐蹒跚。|近义词：falter, stammer, stumble|反义词:steady, smooth, flow|v.
