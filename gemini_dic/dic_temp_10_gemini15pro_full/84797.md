handsomest:/ˈhændsəməst/ | adj. | 最英俊的 | Superlative form of handsome; most attractive (typically used of a man).  | He was the handsomest man in the room. 他是房间里最英俊的男人。 |  He was voted the handsomest boy in school. 他被选为学校里最帅的男孩。 | 近义词: most attractive, best-looking, gorgeous, stunning | 反义词: ugliest, homeliest | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *handsom*, from hand + -some (suffix meaning "characterized by, having the quality of"). Originally meant "easy to handle, convenient," later evolving to mean "dexterous, skillful," and finally "pleasing in appearance."
