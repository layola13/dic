glomerule:/ˈɡlɒmərjʊl (British), /ˈɡlɑːmərjʊl (American)| n.|肾小球|A cluster of capillaries around the end of a kidney tubule, where waste products are filtered from the blood.|The glomerule filters waste products from the blood. 肾小球过滤血液中的废物。|The glomeruli in his kidneys were damaged.  他的肾脏中的肾小球受损了。|近义词: renal corpuscle|反义词: 无|词性: noun
