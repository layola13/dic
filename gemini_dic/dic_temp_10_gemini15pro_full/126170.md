mutual-funds: /ˈmjuːtʃuəl fʌndz/ | n. | 共同基金 | An investment program funded by shareholders that trades in diversified holdings and is professionally managed. |  Example 1:  He invested a large portion of his savings in mutual funds. 他把他的大部分积蓄投资于共同基金。

Example 2: Mutual funds offer a way to diversify your investment portfolio. 共同基金提供了一种使你的投资组合多样化的方式。 | Synonyms: investment fund, unit trust | Antonyms: individual stocks, bonds | noun
