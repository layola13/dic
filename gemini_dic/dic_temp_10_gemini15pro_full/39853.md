constabulatory:/kənˈstæbjʊləˌtɔːri/ /ˌkɑːnstəˈbjuːlətɔːri/| adj.|巡逻的，维持治安的|Relating to a constable or the duties of a constable, especially relating to the police force.|例句：The constabulatory force patrolled the streets regularly. 巡逻队定期在街上巡逻。|近义词：police, law enforcement|反义词：criminal, lawless|词性:形容词

constabulatory:/kənˈstæbjʊləˌtɔːri/ /ˌkɑːnstəˈbjuːlətɔːri/| n.|警察局，警务|A police force or the building where they work.|例句：The suspect was taken to the local constabulatory. 嫌疑人被带到当地警察局。|近义词：police station, precinct|反义词：N/A|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *constabularia, from Latin constabulus "count of the stable" (see constable).  Originally referred to the quarters of the imperial guard, later to any police force.
