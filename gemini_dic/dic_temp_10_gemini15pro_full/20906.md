bleaching:/ˈbliːtʃɪŋ/
n.|漂白; 漂白作用|The act of whitening something by removing its color, typically using chemicals. |The bleaching of coral reefs is a serious environmental problem. 珊瑚礁的漂白是一个严重的环境问题。|synonyms: whitening, decolorizing|antonyms: dyeing, coloring|noun

bleaching:/ˈbliːtʃɪŋ/
adj.|漂白的|Whitened by a chemical process.|She wore a bleaching white dress. 她穿着一件漂白过的白裙子。|synonyms: whitened, decolorized|antonyms: dyed, colored|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From bleach (v.) + -ing.  Ultimately from Old English *blēcan "to make pale, bleach," from Proto-Germanic *blaikijaną "to bleach," related to Old Norse bleikja "to bleach."
