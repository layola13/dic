nonsusceptible:/ˌnɒnsəˈsɛptəbl/ /ˌnɑːnsəˈsɛptəbl/
adjective
不易感染的；不受影响的
Not susceptible; resistant, immune.  Unable to be affected by something.
The virus is known to infect various species, but humans appear to be nonsusceptible.  这种病毒已知会感染多种物种，但人类似乎不易感染。
This material is nonsusceptible to rust. 这种材料不易生锈。
近义词:immune, resistant, impervious
反义词:susceptible, vulnerable, sensitive
词性:adjective

词源:
From non- + susceptible.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "susceptible" comes from the Latin word "suscipere," meaning "to take up" or "to undergo."
