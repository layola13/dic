externalises:/ɪkˈstɜːnəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使外化；使客观化；外包
to express (a thought or feeling) outwardly
She externalized her anger by yelling at her brother. 她通过对哥哥大喊大叫来发泄她的愤怒。
express, vent, articulate
