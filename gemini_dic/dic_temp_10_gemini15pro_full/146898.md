overinsisting:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈsɪstɪŋ/
adjective
过度坚持的；过分强调的
The act of insisting too much or too strongly on something.
He annoyed everyone by overinsisting on his point of view. 他过分强调自己的观点，惹恼了所有人。
近义词：overemphasizing, harping on, dwelling on
反义词：downplaying, understating

词源：over- + insisting.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "too much" or "excessively". "Insisting" comes from the verb "insist", which means "to demand something forcefully, not accepting refusal".  Therefore, "overinsisting" means insisting too much.
