homm:/hɒm/ | n. |家，住所|A dwelling; a place where one lives. |例句：There's no place like homm.  金窝银窝不如自己的狗窝。

例句：He returned to his childhood homm. 他回到了他童年的家。|近义词：home, house, residence, abode, dwelling |反义词: exile, foreign land | 词性:名词


词源: "homm" appears to be a misspelling of "home."  The word "home" comes from Old English "hām," meaning "house, dwelling, village."  It is related to Old Norse "heimr" and Gothic "hams," both also meaning "home."
