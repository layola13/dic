impart:/ɪmˈpɑːrt/ /ɪmˈpɑːrt/| vt.|传授，告知；给予，赋予|To communicate information, knowledge, or a feeling to someone; to bestow a quality on something.|She imparted her wisdom to her students. 她把自己的智慧传授给学生们。|Synonyms: communicate, convey, transmit, bestow, grant|Antonyms: withhold, conceal, suppress|verb

impart:/ɪmˈpɑːrt/ /ɪmˈpɑːrt/| n.|传授，告知|The act of communicating information, knowledge, or a feeling.|The impart of knowledge is the primary function of a teacher.  知识的传授是教师的主要职能。|Synonyms: communication, conveyance, transmission|Antonyms: withholding, concealment, suppression|noun
