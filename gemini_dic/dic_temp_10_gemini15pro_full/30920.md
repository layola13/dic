chainless:/ˈtʃeɪnlɪs/
adj.
无链的；自由的；不受束缚的
Not restrained or bound by chains; free from restraint.
The cyclist rode a chainless bicycle up the hill.  骑自行车的人骑着一辆无链条的自行车上山。
The poet felt chainless in his creativity. 诗人觉得他的创作是自由的。
近义词: unchained, free, unbound, unrestrained
反义词: chained, bound, restrained, restricted
词性: 形容词

词源:
From chain + -less.  来自 chain（链条）+ -less（无...的）。
