hawkish:/ˈhɔːkɪʃ/| adj.|鹰派的；强硬的|Favoring aggressive policies, especially in foreign affairs.  Like a hawk; predatory.|例句：The senator's hawkish stance on defense spending alarmed some voters.  这位参议员在国防开支上的强硬立场让一些选民感到震惊。

例句：He took a hawkish position on the trade dispute. 他在贸易争端中采取了强硬的立场。|近义词：aggressive, belligerent, militant|反义词：dovish, conciliatory, peaceful|词性:adjective
词源:From hawk + -ish.  Relating to the perceived aggressiveness of hawks.
