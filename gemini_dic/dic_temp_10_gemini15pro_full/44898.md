cryology:/kraɪˈɒlədʒi/
n.
低温学；冻学
The study of low temperatures, their production, and their effects.
Cryology plays a crucial role in preserving biological samples. 低温学在保存生物样本方面起着至关重要的作用。
Cryology is a fascinating field with applications in medicine and space exploration. 低温学是一个引人入胜的领域，在医学和太空探索方面都有应用。
近义词:cryogenics
反义词:thermology
