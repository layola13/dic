clearway:/ˈklɪəweɪ/ | n. | 清除路段，畅通路段 | A stretch of road where parking is prohibited, especially during rush hour, to allow for a free flow of traffic. |  The police kept the clearway open for emergency vehicles. 警察让畅通路段保持畅通，以便紧急车辆通行。 | synonyms: throughway, expressway, arterial road | antonyms: traffic jam, congestion, bottleneck | noun
