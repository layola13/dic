hibernation:/ˌhaɪbərˈneɪʃn/ | n. | 冬眠 | A state of inactivity and metabolic depression in endotherms.  Animals that hibernate conserve energy during winter when food is scarce. | Many bears hibernate during the cold winter months. 许多熊在寒冷的冬季冬眠。| The dormouse went into hibernation as the weather grew colder.  随着天气变冷，睡鼠进入了冬眠。 | 近义词：dormancy, sleep, torpor | 反义词：activity, wakefulness | noun
