earth-shattering:/ˈɜːrθˌʃætərɪŋ/ | adj. | 极其重大的，惊天动地的 | Extremely surprising and important; having a momentous impact. | The discovery of penicillin was an earth-shattering medical advancement.  青霉素的发现是一个惊天动地的医学进步。

| Example 2: The news of the president's resignation was earth-shattering. 总统辞职的消息令人震惊。 | Synonym: momentous, groundbreaking, revolutionary, transformative | Antonym: insignificant, trivial, minor, unimportant | adjective
