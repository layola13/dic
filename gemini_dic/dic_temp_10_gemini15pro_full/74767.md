freeze-dry:/ˈfriːz draɪ/ (US) /friːz draɪ/ (UK)| v.  n. | 冷冻干燥 | To preserve (food) by freezing it rapidly and then subjecting it to a high vacuum that removes ice by sublimation.  (v.) The process of freeze-drying. (n.) | 例句：They freeze-dried the coffee to preserve its flavor. 他们冷冻干燥咖啡以保持其风味。 | 近义词：lyophilize | 反义词: hydrate |词性：动词, 名词

freeze-dried:/ˈfriːz draɪd/ (US) /friːz draɪd/ (UK)| adj. | 冷冻干燥的 | Preserved by freeze-drying. | 例句：Freeze-dried food is lightweight and easy to store.  冷冻干燥的食物重量轻，易于储存。| 近义词：lyophilized | 反义词: fresh, hydrated |词性：形容词
