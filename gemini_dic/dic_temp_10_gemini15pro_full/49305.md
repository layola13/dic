delegate: /ˈdɛlɪɡeɪt/ (US), /ˈdɛlɪɡeɪt/ (UK)| vt.|委派，授权|Entrust (a task or responsibility) to another person, typically one who is less senior than oneself.|He delegated the task of writing the report to his assistant. 他把写报告的任务委托给了他的助手。| synonyms: assign, entrust, commit, allocate, consign| antonyms: retain, withhold| verb
