Example 2:  A group of gongoozlers gathered at the lock gates, eager to see the barge navigate through. 一群观船者聚集在闸门旁，渴望看到驳船穿过。| 近义词: canal watcher, boat watcher, bystander, onlooker | 反义词: participant, worker | 词性: 名词
