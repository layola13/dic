detractingly:/dɪˈtræktɪŋli/
adv.
贬低地，减损地
in a way that makes something seem less good or impressive
Her constant complaints were detractingly from the overall positive atmosphere. 她不断的抱怨降低了整体积极的氛围。
Synonyms: disparagingly, unfavorably, negatively
Antonyms: favorably, positively, enhancingly
词性: 副词


detract:/dɪˈtrækt/
v.
减损，降低
reduce or take away the worth or value of
The scandal detracted from his reputation. 这桩丑闻损害了他的名誉。
Synonyms: diminish, lessen, reduce
Antonyms: enhance, increase, improve
词性: 动词
