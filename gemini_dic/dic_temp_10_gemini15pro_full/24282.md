brigandage:/ˈbrɪɡəndɪdʒ/| n.|土匪行为；抢劫；盗匪行径|The act of brigands; robbery and plundering.|The area was plagued by brigandage and lawlessness. 该地区饱受盗匪行径和无法无天的困扰。|Synonyms: banditry, highway robbery, plundering, pillage, looting|Antonyms: lawfulness, order, peace, security|noun

Etymology:
From French *brigandage*, from *brigand* ("brigand"), from Italian *brigante*, from a Romance root *briga* ("strife, quarrel"). Ultimately from a Gaulish word, possibly related to Irish *brig* ("force, power").
