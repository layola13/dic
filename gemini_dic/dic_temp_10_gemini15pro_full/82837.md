gule:/ɡuːl/ | n. | 欺骗；诡计；贪婪 | Deceit; trickery; greed.  A strong desire for something, especially wealth or food. |  He was driven by gule and a thirst for power. 他被贪婪和对权力的渴望所驱使。 | 近义词: greed, avarice, cupidity, rapacity | 反义词: generosity, altruism | 词性: noun

词源: From Old French *goule "throat, gullet," from Latin gula. Originally referred to the throat or gullet, then extended to gluttony and greed.
