comprehensives:/ˌkɒmprɪˈhɛnsɪvz/ | n. | 综合学校；综合考试 |  A secondary school in Britain that accepts pupils of all abilities. |  My son goes to the local comprehensive. 我儿子在当地的一所综合中学上学。 | synonyms: comprehensive school, comprehensive examination | antonyms: selective school, specialized test | noun

comprehensives:/ˌkɒmprɪˈhɛnsɪvz/ | adj. | 综合的；广泛的；理解的 | Covering or including everything. | The police conducted a comprehensive search of the area. 警察对该地区进行了全面搜查。 | synonyms: complete, thorough, extensive, inclusive | antonyms: limited, narrow, partial, incomplete | adjective
