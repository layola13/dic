guerrillaism:/ɡəˈrɪləɪzəm/
|n.|游击主义，游击战术|The use of unconventional warfare tactics, typically involving small, independent groups operating in occupied territory.|The rebels employed guerrillaism to harass the occupying army. 叛军采用游击战术骚扰占领军。|近义词：guerrilla warfare, unconventional warfare|反义词:conventional warfare|词性:名词
Etymology: From guerrilla + -ism.  Guerrilla itself comes from Spanish *guerrilla*, meaning "little war," a diminutive of *guerra*, meaning "war." Ultimately from  West Germanic *werra*
