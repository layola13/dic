disimprove:/ˌdɪsɪmˈpruːv/
verb
使恶化；使退步；使变坏
to become or make something worse
The weather has disimproved. 天气变坏了。
近义词:worsen, deteriorate, decline
反义词:improve, enhance, better
词性:v.
