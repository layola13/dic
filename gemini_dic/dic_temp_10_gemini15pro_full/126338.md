myotic:/maɪˈɒtɪk/| adj.|近视的；缩瞳的；|Relating to, or affected by, myosis (excessive contraction of the pupil of the eye); causing or characterized by myosis. |Example 1: The drug caused a myotic reaction in the patient's eyes. 这种药物引起了病人眼睛的缩瞳反应。

Example 2: His myotic vision made it difficult to see distant objects clearly. 他的近视使他很难看清远处的物体。|近义词：short-sighted, nearsighted (for vision), miotic|反义词: hyperopic, farsighted (for vision), mydriatic|词性:adjective
