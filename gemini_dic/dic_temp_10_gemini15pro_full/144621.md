outwing:/ˌaʊtˈwɪŋ/
verb
超越，胜过，在侧翼飞行并超越
to surpass or defeat, especially by having a longer wingspan or more advanced technology; to fly on the flank and outstrip
The new aircraft easily outwinged its competitors.  这架新飞机轻松超越了它的竞争对手。
Our team outwinged the opponents in the final match. 我们的团队在决赛中胜过了对手。
近义词:surpass, outstrip, outperform, exceed
反义词:lag behind, fall behind
词性:v.


outwing:/ˌaʊtˈwɪŋ/
noun
（飞机的）外翼；侧翼
the outer part of a wing, especially of an aircraft; a flanking force
Damage to the outwing caused the plane to crash. 外翼的损坏导致飞机坠毁。
The general positioned his cavalry on the outwing. 将军把他的骑兵部署在侧翼。
近义词:wingtip, flank
反义词:center, core
词性:n.
