paralyzes:/ˈpærəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使麻痹，使瘫痪
To cause (a person or part of the body) to lose the ability to move or feel.
The accident paralyzed him from the waist down.  事故使他腰部以下瘫痪。
synonyms: disable, immobilize, incapacitate
antonyms: enable, empower, activate
词性: 动词(第三人称单数)
