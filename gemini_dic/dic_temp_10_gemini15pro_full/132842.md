noneducative:/ˌnɒnˈɛdʒʊkeɪtɪv/
adjective
非教育性的，无教育意义的
Not educational; not contributing to education or development.
That movie was purely for entertainment and completely noneducative. 那部电影纯粹是为了娱乐，完全没有教育意义。
The video game was criticized for its violent and noneducative content.  这款电子游戏因其暴力和无教育意义的内容而受到批评。
近义词: uneducational, uninstructive
反义词: educational, instructive
词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + educative (adjective meaning "tending or contributing to educate")
