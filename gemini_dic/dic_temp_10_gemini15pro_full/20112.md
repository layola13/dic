biotic:/baɪˈɑːtɪk/
adjective
生物的，生命的
Relating to living organisms or their life processes.
The biotic factors in an ecosystem include all the living things.  生态系统中的生物因素包括所有生物。
The pond contains many biotic elements, such as fish, frogs, and plants.  池塘里包含许多生物元素，例如鱼、青蛙和植物。
近义词:living, organic
反义词:abiotic
词性:adjective
