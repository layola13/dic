overpreach:/ˌoʊvərˈpriːtʃ/ | v. | 过度布道，喋喋不休地说教 | To preach excessively or to a tedious extent; to preach too much or too often.  | The pastor overpreached about the importance of tithing, causing some parishioners to feel uncomfortable. 牧师喋喋不休地讲什一税的重要性，让一些教区居民感到不舒服。

| The politician overpreached his message, turning off potential voters.  这位政客过度宣扬他的理念，导致潜在的选民失去兴趣。 | 近义词：moralize, sermonize, lecture | 反义词: underemphasize, downplay | 词性: 动词
