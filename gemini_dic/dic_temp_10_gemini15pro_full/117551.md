megacephalic:/ˌmɛɡəsɪˈfælɪk/ | adj. |巨头的，头大的| Having an abnormally large head.  A megacephalic individual may have a head circumference significantly larger than average for their age and sex. |  Example 1: The doctor diagnosed the child as megacephalic after measuring his head circumference. 医生在测量了孩子的头围后，诊断他为巨头症。

Example 2:  Megacephalic conditions can be caused by a variety of factors. 巨头症的病因有很多种。 | Synonym: macrocephalic | Antonym: microcephalic | adjective
