bhang:/bæŋ/
n.
印度大麻
A narcotic preparation made from the leaves and flowers of the Indian hemp plant.
She drank a cup of bhang and felt euphoric. 她喝了一杯印度大麻，感到欣快。

近义词:cannabis, marijuana, hemp
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps sobriety or abstinence)
词性:noun
