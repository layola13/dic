abietinic:/ˌeɪbiːˈtɪnɪk/
adjective
松香的；枞酸的
Relating to or derived from abietic acid, a resin acid found in rosin.
Abietinic acid is a major component of rosin.  枞酸是松香的主要成分。
The abietinic resin was used in the varnish.  这种枞树脂被用于清漆中。
近义词:resinous, resiny
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider terms like "non-resinous")
词性:adjective

From Latin *abiēs, abiet- (“fir tree”) + -inic (suffix forming adjectives).
