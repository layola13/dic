excerpt:/ˈeksɜːrpt/ /ˈɛksɜːrpt/| n. vt.|摘录；引用；选段|A passage or quotation taken or selected from a book, film, piece of music, etc.|例句：The following excerpt is taken from her new novel.  以下摘录选自她的新小说。

例句：He excerpted a few lines from the poem. 他从这首诗中摘录了几行。|近义词：extract, passage, quotation, selection|反义词: whole, entirety|词性:名词, 动词
