hermitically:/hɜːrˈmetɪkli/ | adv. | 密封地，不透气地 | In a way that is completely airtight; sealed so that no air or gas can enter or escape. | The tomb was hermitically sealed for centuries.  这座坟墓被密封了几个世纪。 | Synonym: airtightly, imperviously, tightly | Antonym: loosely, openly | adverb

hermetic:/hɜːrˈmetɪk/ | adj. | 密封的，不透气的 | Completely airtight; sealed so that no air or gas can enter or escape. | The food was stored in hermetic containers. 食物被储存在密封的容器里。 | Synonym: airtight, sealed, impervious | Antonym: leaky, permeable, porous | adjective
