painstaking:/ˈpeɪnzteɪkɪŋ/ | adj. | 辛苦的， painstaking的 | Done with or employing great care and thoroughness.  Characterized by extreme care and great effort. |  She completed the painting with painstaking attention to detail. 她一丝不苟地完成了这幅画。| synonyms: meticulous, thorough, careful, diligent, assiduous | antonyms: careless, negligent, sloppy | adjective

painstakingly:/ˌpeɪnzˈteɪkɪŋli/ | adv. | 辛苦地， painstakingly地 | In a painstaking manner; with great care and thoroughness. | He painstakingly reconstructed the ancient vase from hundreds of fragments.  他煞费苦心地用数百个碎片重建了这件古代花瓶。 | synonyms: meticulously, thoroughly, carefully, diligently, assiduously | antonyms: carelessly, negligently, sloppily | adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "pains" (meaning efforts or trouble) + "taking" (present participle of "take," meaning to expend).  Originally referred to the effort taken, now often implies the care and thoroughness resulting from that effort.
