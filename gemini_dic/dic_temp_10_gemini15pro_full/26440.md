byproduct:/ˈbaɪˌprɑːdʌkt/ | n. | 副产品；副作用 | something produced in addition to the main product |  The factory produces chemicals, and one of the byproducts is a gas that smells terrible. 这家工厂生产化学品，其中一种副产品是一种气味难闻的气体。 | synonyms: side effect, spin-off, offshoot, consequence | antonyms: main product, primary product | noun
