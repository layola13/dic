misspeak:/ˌmɪsˈspiːk/| v. |说错话，失言|To say something incorrectly, unintentionally, or inappropriately.| I misspoke earlier; I meant to say "next Tuesday," not "this Tuesday." 我刚才说错话了；我的意思是说“下周二”，而不是“本周二”。|近义词：slip of the tongue, blunder, err|反义词: articulate correctly, express clearly|verb
