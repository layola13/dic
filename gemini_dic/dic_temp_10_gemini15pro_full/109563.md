let-go:/lɛt ˈɡoʊ/ | n. | 放手，释放 | The act of releasing something or someone. | After holding on for so long, she finally decided it was time for a let-go.  在坚持了这么久之后，她最终决定是时候放手了。 | synonyms: release, surrender, relinquishment | antonyms: grip, hold, grasp | noun

let-go:/lɛt ˈɡoʊ/ | v. | 放开，放弃，释放 | To release one's hold on something or someone; to stop thinking about or being involved with something. |  He let go of the rope and the kite soared. 他放开了绳子，风筝飞了起来。| synonyms: release, relinquish, abandon | antonyms: hold, grasp, retain | verb
