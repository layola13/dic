cenotaphic:/ˌsɛnəˈtæfɪk/ |adj.|空的，衣冠冢的，纪念碑的|Relating to or in the nature of a cenotaph; a monument erected in honour of a person or group of people whose remains are elsewhere.|The cenotaphic inscription commemorated the soldiers lost at sea.  这块纪念碑上的铭文是为了纪念在海上牺牲的士兵们。|例句：The town square features a cenotaphic memorial to the fallen soldiers of World War I.  小镇广场上有一座纪念第一次世界大战阵亡士兵的衣冠冢。

例句：The cenotaphic service was held on Remembrance Day. 在阵亡将士纪念日举行了衣冠冢仪式。 |近义词：memorial, commemorative|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "actual burial site")|词性: adjective
