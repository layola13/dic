brookite:/ˈbrʊkaɪt/
n.
板钛矿
A polymorph of titanium dioxide, TiO₂, occurring as orthorhombic crystals, typically brown, reddish brown, or black.
Brookite is a relatively rare titanium mineral.  板钛矿是一种相对稀有的钛矿物。
Brookite, anatase, and rutile are the three mineral forms of titanium dioxide.  板钛矿、锐钛矿和金红石是二氧化钛的三种矿物形式。

近义词:anatase, rutile (as polymorphs of TiO2)
反义词:None (as it's a specific mineral)
词性:noun
