antiptosis:/æntiˈtoʊsɪs/
n.
下垂矯正，眼瞼下垂矯正術
The surgical correction of ptosis (drooping) of an organ, especially the eyelid.
Antiptosis surgery helped improve her vision by lifting her droopy eyelids.  下垂矯正手術通過抬起她下垂的眼瞼幫助改善了她的視力。

Antiptosis crutches can provide support for a drooping limb. 下垂矯正拐杖可以為下垂的肢體提供支撐。
近义词:  blepharoplasty (specifically for eyelid ptosis),  elevation, suspension
反义词: ptosis, descent
