overdenunciation: /ˌoʊvərdiˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃən/
noun
过度发音；过分清晰的发音
The act of pronouncing words or sounds more distinctly than is natural or necessary.
His overdenunciation of every syllable made him sound artificial. 他对每个音节都过度发音，让他听起来很不自然。
hyperarticulation, overpronunciation
underarticulation, mumbling
