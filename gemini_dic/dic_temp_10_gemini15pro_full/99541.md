intergovernmental:/ˌɪntərɡʌvərnˈmentl/
adjective
政府间的；跨政府的
Relating to or involving the governments of two or more countries.
The intergovernmental panel on climate change released a new report.  政府间气候变化专门委员会发布了一份新报告。
The organization promotes intergovernmental cooperation on environmental issues.  该组织促进各国政府在环境问题上的合作。
近义词: international, multinational
反义词: domestic, internal
词性: adjective

词源: inter- (between) + governmental (relating to government)
