monamide:/ˈmɒnəmaɪd/ /ˈmɑːnəmaɪd/ | n. | 一酰胺，单酰胺 | An organic compound derived from a carboxylic acid by replacing the hydroxyl group (-OH) with an amino group (-NH2).  It contains a single amide group (-CONH2). |  This reaction produces a monamide and water. 这个反应产生一酰胺和水。| synonyms:  acid amide, carboxamide | antonyms: N/A | noun
