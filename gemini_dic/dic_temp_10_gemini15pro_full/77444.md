gawking:/ˈɡɔːkɪŋ/| adj. | 呆呆地看的 | staring stupidly | He gave a gawking stare. 他呆呆地盯着看。 | staring, gaping, ogling | glancing, glimpsing| adjective

词源: From the verb "gawk," which likely originates from a Scandinavian word meaning "to stare foolishly."  来自动词 "gawk"，可能源于一个斯堪的纳维亚语单词，意思是“愚蠢地盯着看”。
