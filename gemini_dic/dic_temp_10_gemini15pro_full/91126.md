hybrid:/ˈhaɪbrɪd/ | n. adj. | 混合物，杂种；混合的 |Something that is made by combining two different elements; a mixture. |  The car is a hybrid, powered by both petrol and electricity. 这辆车是混合动力车，由汽油和电力驱动。
