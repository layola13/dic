discourser:/dɪˈskɔːrsər/ /dɪˈskɜːrsər/| n. | 演讲者，论述者，著作者 | A person who discourses; one who speaks or writes formally and at length. |  He was a skilled discourser, captivating his audience with his eloquence. 他是一位技艺精湛的演讲者，以其雄辩的口才吸引着观众。

The discourser presented a compelling argument for his theory.  这位论述者为他的理论提出了令人信服的论证。 | 近义词：speaker, orator, writer, author | 反义词: listener, reader | 词性: noun
