hyperconjugation:/ˌhaɪpərˌkɑːndʒʊˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. | 超共轭 | The delocalization of electrons in a sigma bond (usually C-H) with an adjacent empty or partially filled p-orbital or a pi orbital, leading to an extended molecular orbital that increases the stability of the system. |  The stability of carbocations is often explained by hyperconjugation.  碳正离子的稳定性通常用超共轭来解释。| 近义词: resonance, delocalization | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- (meaning "over, beyond") + conjugation (referring to the interaction of orbitals).
