alarums:/əˈlɑːrəmz/ | n. | 警报；惊慌；骚动 | A sudden loud noise or outcry, especially one signifying the approach of danger; a call to arms. Signals of alarm, usually in the form of loud noises.  A state of alarm or apprehension caused by the awareness of danger. | The alarums of war echoed through the valley.  战争的警报声回荡在山谷中。 | Synonyms: alarms, warnings, alerts, signals, calls to arms | Antonyms: peace, quiet, tranquility | noun
