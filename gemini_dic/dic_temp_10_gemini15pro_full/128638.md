nickels-and-dimes:/ˈnɪkəlz ənd daɪmz/| idiom|斤斤计较；为小钱操心；吝啬|To be concerned with or preoccupied with small amounts of money; to be frugal or stingy.|例句：He's always nickels-and-diming, even with his friends. 他总是斤斤计较，即使是对他的朋友也一样。

例句：The company is so nickels-and-dimes that they charge for everything. 这家公司太吝啬了，什么都要收费。|近义词：penny-pinching, frugal, stingy, thrifty|反义词：generous, extravagant|词性：idiom
