necessarily:/ˌnesəˈserəli/ /ˌnesəˈsɛrəli/ | adv. | 必然地，不可避免地 |  Means something is unavoidable or inevitable, or that it is logically required.  |例句：That's not necessarily true. 那不一定是真的。|例句： Increased production doesn't necessarily lead to increased profits.  增加产量并不一定会带来利润的增长。 | 近义词：inevitably, unavoidably, certainly, definitely | 反义词: optionally, possibly, perhaps | 词性: 副词
