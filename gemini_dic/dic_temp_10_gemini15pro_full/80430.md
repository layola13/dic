gondolet:/ˈɡɒndələt/ (UK), /ˈɡɑːndələt/ (US)| n. |贡多拉式缆车，吊箱式缆车 |A type of cable car suspended from a continuously circulating cable, typically used to transport skiers up mountains or sightseers to scenic viewpoints.  It's enclosed, unlike a chairlift. |例句：We took a gondolet up the mountain to enjoy the breathtaking views. 我们乘坐贡多拉式缆车登上山顶，欣赏令人叹为观止的景色。
