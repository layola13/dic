Etymology:
From park + -y + -est.  "Park" in this sense likely derives from the sense of being "brisk" or "alert," potentially related to the idea of a horse being "high-parked," meaning spirited and ready to go.  The suffix "-y" forms adjectives, and "-est" indicates the superlative form.
