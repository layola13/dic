nonvictims:/nɒnˈvɪktɪmz/
noun
非受害者
Those who are not victims of a particular crime or circumstance, or those perceived as not being victims.  It can sometimes carry a connotation of blaming or questioning the experiences of those who do identify as victims.
The media often portrays certain groups as nonvictims, overlooking the harm they experience. 媒体经常将某些群体描绘成非受害者，忽视他们所遭受的伤害。
Near Synonyms:  perpetrators, aggressors, privileged, empowered
Antonyms: victims, sufferers, injured parties
