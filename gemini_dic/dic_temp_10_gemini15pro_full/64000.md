epistolic: /ˌɛpɪˈstɒlɪk/ /ˌɛpɪˈstɑːlɪk/
adjective
书信体的；用书信写成的
Relating to or written in the form of letters.
Example:  The novel is written in an epistolary style, using letters and diary entries to tell the story. 这部小说是以书信体写成的，用信件和日记来讲述故事。

Example:  His epistolary novel captured the hearts of readers with its intimate portrayal of love and loss.  他书信体的小说以其对爱与失去的细腻刻画打动了读者的心。

近义词: letter-based, correspondence-based
反义词: non-epistolary
