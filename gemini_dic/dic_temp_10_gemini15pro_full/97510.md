inglobate:/ɪnˈɡloʊbeɪt/
verb
包含；包容；合并
to incorporate (something) within something else so that it becomes an indistinguishable part of it.
The new company inglobated several smaller businesses.  这家新公司合并了几家小企业。
The empire inglobated many different cultures.  这个帝国融合了多种不同的文化。
近义词: incorporate, include, encompass, absorb, integrate
反义词: exclude, separate, divide
词性: verb

inglobate:/ɪnˈɡloʊbeɪt/
adjective
球状的；包入的
(rare) formed into a globe or ball.
The artist created an inglobate sculpture.  这位艺术家创作了一个球状的雕塑。
The cells were arranged in an inglobate mass. 细胞排列成球状。
近义词: spherical, globular, round
反义词: flat, uneven
词性: adjective


词源:  From Latin *in* (in, into) + *globus* (globe, sphere).
