overdrip:/ˈoʊvərdrɪp/| n.| 过度滴落，过度饱和 (尤指咖啡)|An overdrip occurs when too much water is used in brewing coffee, resulting in a weak and watery beverage.  过度滴落是指在冲泡咖啡时使用了过多的水，导致咖啡味道淡而 watery。|The overdrip ruined the coffee, making it taste bland. 过度滴落毁了咖啡，使它尝起来很淡。|近义词: overextraction|反义词: underextraction|词性:名词

词源:  "Over" (过度) + "drip" (滴落).  A straightforward compound word. 由"over" (过度) 和 "drip" (滴落) 组成的一个直接的复合词。
