idiographic:/ˌɪdiəˈɡræfɪk/
adjective
独特的，个体化的
Relating to the study of particular facts or events, rather than general laws.  Focusing on individual cases or specific details, rather than general principles.
Idiographic research focuses on individual cases, like a detailed biography of a historical figure.  这种独特的个体化研究专注于个别案例，例如对历史人物的详细传记。
Idiographic approaches are often used in qualitative research. 个体化方法常用于定性研究中。
Synonyms: individual, particular, specific, unique
Antonyms: nomothetic, general, universal
词性: 形容词
