hardscrabble:/ˈhɑːrdˌskræbəl/| adj.|艰苦的，贫瘠的，勉强维持的|Characterized by poverty and difficult living conditions; involving a desperate struggle for survival.|They lived a hardscrabble existence in the Appalachian Mountains. 他们在阿巴拉契亚山脉过着艰苦的生活。|The farmer eked out a hardscrabble living from the dry, rocky soil.  这位农民从干燥的岩石土壤中勉强维持生计。|近义词：difficult, impoverished, meager, struggling, tough, grueling, arduous|反义词: easy, affluent, prosperous, comfortable|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From "hard" + "scrabble" (to scratch or scrape), implying scratching a meager living from poor soil.
