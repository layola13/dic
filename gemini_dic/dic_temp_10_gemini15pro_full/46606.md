dambonite:/ˈdæmbəˌnaɪt/
noun
丹波尼特（一种从西非产的某种藤本植物中提取的白色结晶有机化合物）
A white crystalline organic compound extracted from a kind of liana found in West Africa.
Dambonite is a naturally occurring dimethyl ether of inositol. 丹波尼特是一种天然存在的肌醇二甲醚。
Dambonite was first isolated from the sap of the Gabonese rubber tree. 丹波尼特最初是从加蓬橡胶树的汁液中分离出来的。
近义词:dimethyl ether of inositol (更偏向化学名称)
反义词: 无
词性:n.
