equiangular:/ˌiːkwɪˈæŋɡjʊlər/
| adj.| 等角的| Having all angles equal. | An equiangular triangle is also equilateral. 等边三角形也是等角三角形。|
|近义词: equilateral (when referring to triangles), isogonal |反义词: oblique, scalene |词性: adjective
