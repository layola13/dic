discina:/dɪˈskaɪnə/
n.
盘菌属; 杯状真菌
A genus of fungi (cup fungi) in the family Discinaceae.
The Discina perlata is a type of cup fungus. 盘菌属的佩尔拉塔是一种杯状真菌。
近义词: cup fungus
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a genus of fungi)
词性: noun


discina:/dɪˈskaɪnə/
n.
(古罗马)餐盘；圆盘
A small, flat, round dish or plate used by the ancient Romans.
The archeologists unearthed a discina made of bronze. 考古学家出土了一个青铜制成的圆盘。
近义词: plate, dish, platter
反义词: (No direct antonym for a small dish)
词性: noun
