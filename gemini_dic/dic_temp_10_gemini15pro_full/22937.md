bottle-up:/ˈbɑːtl ʌp/ (US) /ˈbɒtl ʌp/ (UK)| phrasal verb | 抑制，压抑（感情）；憋在心里 | To repress or suppress (feelings, emotions, etc.); to keep inside without expressing them. | He bottled up his anger for days before finally exploding. 他把怒气憋了好几天，最后终于爆发了。 | repress, suppress, contain, conceal, internalize | express, vent, release, unleash, divulge | verb

bottle-up:/ˈbɑːtl ʌp/ (US) /ˈbɒtl ʌp/ (UK)|  adjective |  被抑制的，被压抑的 |  Describing feelings or emotions that are being repressed or suppressed. |  She had a bottled-up rage that was slowly eating away at her. 她内心压抑着愤怒，这股怒火正慢慢地吞噬着她。 | repressed, suppressed, pent-up, contained | expressed, released, open | adjective
