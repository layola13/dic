bushmaster:/ˈbʊʃˌmæstər/ | n. | 巨蝮；粗鳞矛头蝮；灌木丛主宰者 | A large, venomous pit viper found in Central and South America, known for its potent venom and aggressive nature.  It can also refer to a person skilled in navigating or surviving in the bush. | Example 1: The bushmaster is one of the most dangerous snakes in the Americas. 巨蝮是美洲最危险的蛇类之一。

Example 2: He was a true bushmaster, able to survive for weeks in the wilderness with minimal supplies. 他是一个真正的丛林主宰者，能够在荒野中仅靠少量补给生存数周。 | 近义词:pit viper, fer-de-lance (for the snake) , survivalist, woodsman (for the person) | 反义词: N/A | 词性:noun
