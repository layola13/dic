hire:/ˈhaɪər (US)/ /ˈhaɪə(r) (UK)/
|n. vt.|雇佣，租用；租金，雇用金|The act of employing someone for a short time to do a particular job, or the amount of money paid for this.  The company plans to hire 200 new employees this year.  这家公司计划今年新招聘200名员工。|Synonyms: employ, recruit, engage|Antonyms: fire, dismiss, lay off|noun
hire:/ˈhaɪər (US)/ /ˈhaɪə(r) (UK)/
