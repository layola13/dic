flankers:/ˈflæŋkərz/| n.|侧翼队员，侧卫，侧翼攻击者|People or things positioned on the sides of something or someone.|The president was surrounded by his flankers.|近义词：wingmen, escorts, guards|反义词:center, middle|noun

flank:/flæŋk/| n. v.|侧翼，侧面， flank somebody/something  包抄…的侧翼，攻击…的侧翼|The side of something, especially a military formation or building. To be positioned at the side of someone or something.|The army attacked the enemy's left flank.  The building is flanked by two towers.|近义词：side, wing|反义词:front, rear|noun, verb
