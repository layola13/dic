grizzles:/ˈɡrɪzəlz/ | n. | 灰白色, (尤指熊的)灰白色毛发, 鬓发斑白,  不满, 抱怨 |  Grizzles refers to gray or partly gray hairs, especially in the fur of animals like bears. It can also mean a state of discontent or complaining. | The old man's grizzles showed his age.  这位老人的灰白头发显示了他的年龄。| synonyms: gray hairs, complaints, grumbles | antonyms: youth, contentment | noun

grizzle:/ˈɡrɪzl/ | v. | (使)变灰白,  哭泣,  抱怨 | To become or cause to become gray or partly gray.  To cry or complain fretfully. | The cold wind grizzled his hair. 寒风使他的头发变灰白了。| synonyms: gray, complain, whine | antonyms: darken, rejoice, praise | verb
