nettly:/ˈnet(ə)li/| adj.| covered with stinging nettles; like a nettle in causing a stinging or irritating sensation; bad-tempered and irritable.| 覆盖着荨麻的；像荨麻一样引起刺痛或刺激感的；脾气暴躁易怒的。| The path was nettly and overgrown. 小路长满了荨麻，杂草丛生。| 近义词：prickly, stinging, irritable, irascible | 反义词：smooth, pleasant, amiable | 词性:形容词

nettle:/ˈnet(ə)l/| n. v.| a herbaceous plant which has jagged leaves covered with stinging hairs; irritate or annoy (someone).| 荨麻（一种草本植物，叶子呈锯齿状，覆盖着刺毛）；激怒或惹恼（某人）。|  I brushed against a nettle and got stung. 我碰到了荨麻，被蜇了。| 近义词：(n.) stinging nettle, (v.) irritate, annoy, provoke | 反义词：(v.) soothe, placate | 词性:名词,动词


词源:
From Old English *netele, from Proto-Germanic *natilō, from Proto-Indo-European *nēdlā (“needle”). Cognate with Dutch netel, German Nessel, Danish nælde.  The verb is a back-formation from the noun.
