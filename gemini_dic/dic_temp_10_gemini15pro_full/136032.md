nonreason:/ˌnɒnˈriːzn/ | n. | 非理性，缺乏理性 | Absence or lack of reason; irrationality. |  His decision was based on pure nonreason. 他的决定完全基于非理性。 |  Nonreason led him down a dangerous path. 非理性使他走上了一条危险的道路。 | 近义词: irrationality, illogicality, absurdity, unreasonableness | 反义词: reason, logic, rationality | 词性: 名词

词源:
non- (prefix) + reason (noun)

"non-" comes from Latin, meaning "not."
"reason" comes from Old French "raison," ultimately from Latin "ratio" meaning "calculation, reasoning."
