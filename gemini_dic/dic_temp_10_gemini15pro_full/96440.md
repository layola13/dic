indistinguishably:/ˌɪndɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbli/
adverb|无法区分地，难以辨别地|in a way that makes it impossible to tell the difference between two or more things or people.|The twins were dressed so similarly they were almost indistinguishably.|Synonyms: identically, alike, similarly|Antonyms: differently, distinctly|词性: 副词

indistinguishably:/ˌɪndɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbli/
adjective (derived from the adverb)|无法区分的，难以辨别的|(While not a standard adjectival form, it can occasionally be found used this way, describing something that cannot be distinguished.)| Their handwriting was so similar, their signatures were practically indistinguishably.|Synonyms: identical, alike, similar|Antonyms: different, distinct|词性: 形容词 (非标准用法)

词源:
