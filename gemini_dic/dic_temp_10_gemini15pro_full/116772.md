mauveine:/moʊˈviːn/ | n. |苯胺紫，淡紫色染料| A synthetic organic dye, the first commercially successful synthetic dye. | Perkin discovered mauveine accidentally while trying to synthesize quinine.  珀金在试图合成奎宁时偶然发现了苯胺紫。

Mauveine was the first synthetic dye used to color clothing. 苯胺紫是第一个用来给衣服染色的合成染料。 | 近义词:aniline purple, Perkin's mauve | 反义词: None | 词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *mauveine*, from *mauve* ("mallow, the plant; mauve, the color") + *-ine* (suffix forming abstract nouns). The color name *mauve* derives from the Latin *malva*.
