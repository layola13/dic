deconstructible:/dɪkənˈstrʌktəbəl/
adjective
可解构的
able to be deconstructed.  Deconstruction involves analyzing something, like a text or an idea, to understand its hidden meanings and assumptions.  This adjective describes something that can be subjected to this kind of analysis.
The architect designed a deconstructible building, allowing for easy dismantling and repurposing of materials.  这位建筑师设计了一座可拆解的建筑，以便于拆卸和材料的再利用。

The philosopher argued that the social construct of gender is deconstructible. 这位哲学家认为性别的社会建构是可以解构的。

近义词: analyzable,  dismantlable
反义词:  unassailable, indivisible
词性: adjective
