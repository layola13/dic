metalinguistically:/ˌmɛtəˌlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪkli/
| adv. | 元语言地 |  In a manner that uses language to describe or analyze language itself.  It refers to the use of language to talk about language. |  She spoke metalinguistically about the use of metaphors in the poem, explaining how they contributed to the overall meaning. 她元语言地谈论了诗歌中隐喻的运用，解释了它们是如何对整体意义做出贡献的。 | Example:  The teacher asked the students to think metalinguistically about the grammar rules they were learning. 老师要求学生们元语言地思考他们正在学习的语法规则。 | Synonyms:  analytically (regarding language), reflexively (regarding language) | Antonyms:  unconsciously (regarding language), implicitly (regarding language) |  adverb
