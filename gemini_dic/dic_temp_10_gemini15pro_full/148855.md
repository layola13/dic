overwax:/ˌoʊvərˈwæks/| v. |过度涂蜡；过分夸张；|To coat or polish excessively with wax; to embellish or exaggerate unduly.|He overwaxed his car, making it look greasy. 他把车打蜡打得太多了，看起来油腻腻的。|The salesman overwaxed the benefits of the product.  推销员过分夸大了产品的好处。|近义词：overdo, exaggerate, embellish|反义词: understate, downplay|词性:动词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "wax" (verb meaning "to coat or polish with wax").  由前缀"over-"（意思是“过度地”）和动词"wax"（意思是“用蜡涂抹或抛光”）组成。
