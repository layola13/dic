diseasedness:/dɪˈziːzdnɪs/

It refers to the extent or severity of the disease's impact on an organism or a part of it.|The advanced diseasedness of the plant made it unlikely to recover.  这株植物病情严重，不太可能恢复了。|近义词：illness, sickness, morbidity|反义词：health, wellness|词性: noun

词源:  From diseased + -ness.  "Diseased" comes from the Old French "desaisi" meaning dispossessed, deprived, or ill. "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
