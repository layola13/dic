cockchafer: /ˈkɒkˌtʃeɪfər/ (UK) /ˈkɑːkˌtʃeɪfər/ (US)| n. |金龟子；五月虫|A large brown European beetle that flies at twilight in May and June and whose larvae are destructive to the roots of plants.
