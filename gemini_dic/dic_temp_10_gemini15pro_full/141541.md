opportunist:/ˈɒpətʃuːnɪst/ (UK), /ˌɑːpərˈtuːnɪst/ (US)| n.|机会主义者；投机分子|A person who exploits circumstances to gain immediate advantage rather than being guided by consistent principles or plans.|He's a ruthless opportunist who will stop at nothing to get what he wants. 他是一个冷酷无情的机会主义者，为了得到他想要的东西会不择手段。


He seized the opportunity to advance his career. 他抓住了机会提升自己的事业。


近义词:exploiter, self-seeker, timeserver|
反义词:principled person, idealist|
词性:noun


opportunistic:/ˌɒpətʃuːˈnɪstɪk/ (UK), /ˌɑːpərtuːˈnɪstɪk/ (US)| adj.|机会主义的；投机的|Exploiting chances offered by immediate circumstances without reference to a general plan or principle.|Opportunistic infections are common in people with weakened immune systems. 免疫系统较弱的人很容易发生机会性感染。


The company took an opportunistic approach to the market. 该公司对市场采取了机会主义的方式。


近义词:exploitative, timeserving, expedient|
