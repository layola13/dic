incinerator:/ɪnˈsɪnəreɪtər/| n. |焚化炉；焚烧装置|A furnace for burning waste material, especially industrial waste, at high temperatures until it is reduced to ash.|The city built a new incinerator to handle its growing waste problem.  这座城市建造了一座新的焚化炉来处理日益增长的垃圾问题。|Synonym: burner, furnace, destructor|Antonym: composter, recycler|noun

incinerate:/ɪnˈsɪnəreɪt/| v. |焚化；烧成灰烬|Destroy (something, especially waste material) by burning.|Medical waste must be incinerated. 医疗废物必须焚化。|Synonym: burn, cremate|Antonym: extinguish, preserve|verb
