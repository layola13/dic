doodlesack:/ˈduːdəlsæk/| n. |风笛;一种简易风笛|A simple bagpipe. A child’s toy wind instrument, similar to a bagpipe, made from a bladder or skin and a reed pipe.|The child was happily playing a doodlesack he'd made out of a balloon and a straw.  这孩子高兴地玩着他用气球和吸管做的简易风笛。|Synonyms: bagpipe, pipe|Antonyms: None|Noun
