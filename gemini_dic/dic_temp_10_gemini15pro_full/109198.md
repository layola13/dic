legumen:/ˈlɛɡjuːmɛn/ /ləˈɡjuːmən/
n.
豆类；豆科植物；豆荚
a seed, pod, or other edible part of a leguminous plant used as food.
Legumes, such as beans and lentils, are a good source of protein. 豆类，例如豆类和扁豆，是蛋白质的良好来源。
Pulses are the dried edible seeds of plants in the legume family. 豆类是豆科植物的干燥可食用种子。

近义词:pulse, bean, pea
反义词:(无明确的反义词)
词性:noun
