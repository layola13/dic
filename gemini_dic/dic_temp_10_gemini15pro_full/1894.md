act: /ækt/
v.|扮演；表现；行动；假装|To perform a role; to behave in a certain way; to take action; to pretend|例句：He acted bravely in the face of danger. 面对危险他表现得很勇敢。

act: /ækt/
v.|扮演；表现；行动；假装|To perform a role; to behave in a certain way; to take action; to pretend|例句：She acted as if she didn't know me. 她表现得好像不认识我一样。

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *actus*, past participle of *agere* "to do, drive, lead, act".

SYNONYMS->
(n.) action, deed, law, statute, performance
(v.) perform, behave, operate, function, pretend

ANTONYMS->
(n.) inaction, idleness
(v.) refrain, desist, cease


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He acts like a child sometimes. = 他有时表现得像个孩子。
The new law acts against illegal immigration. = 这项新法律旨在打击非法移民。

一般过去时:

Congress acted quickly to pass the bill. = 国会迅速采取行动通过了该法案。

现在完成时:
He has acted in several movies. = 他出演过几部电影。

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
