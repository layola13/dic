intermezzo:/ˌɪntərˈmetsoʊ/ (US), /ˌɪntəˈmetsəʊ/ (UK)| n. |间奏曲，幕间剧; (复数) intermezzi 或 intermezzos |A short piece of entertainment performed between the acts of a play or opera, or a short connecting movement in a longer musical work. |The opera included a delightful intermezzo featuring a solo flute. 歌剧中有一段精彩的间奏曲，以长笛独奏为特色。| synonym: interlude, interval, entr'acte | antonym: main act, principal performance | noun

intermezzi:/ˌɪntərˈmetsē/ (US), /ˌɪntəˈmɛtsi/ (UK)| n. |间奏曲，幕间剧（intermezzo的复数形式）|The plural form of "intermezzo", referring to multiple short pieces of entertainment performed between acts. |The intermezzi provided comic relief between the tragic scenes of the play.  这些幕间剧在悲剧场景之间提供了喜剧效果。| synonym: interludes, intervals, entr'actes | antonym: main acts, principal performances | noun
