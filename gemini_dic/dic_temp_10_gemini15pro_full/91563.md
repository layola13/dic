hyper-salinity:/ˌhaɪpər səˈlɪnɪti/ | n. |高盐度，超盐度 |  Excessive salinity; a concentration of salt in water or soil that is higher than normal. | The hyper-salinity of the Dead Sea makes it difficult for most organisms to survive. 死海的高盐度使得大多数生物难以生存。| The hyper-salinity of the soil stunted the growth of the plants. 土壤的高盐度阻碍了植物的生长。 | hypersalinity, high salinity | low salinity, hyposalinity | noun
