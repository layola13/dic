get-it:/ˈɡɛt ɪt/ (US), /ˈɡet ɪt/ (UK)| idiom |理解, 明白, 懂得|To understand something; to comprehend the meaning or situation.|例句：I finally get it! I understand now. 我终于明白了！现在我懂了。

例句：She explained it clearly, and I think he gets it. 她解释得很清楚，我想他明白了。

近义词：understand, comprehend, grasp, catch, see, figure out|反义词：misunderstand, misinterpret|词性：idiom

get-it:/ˈɡɛt ɪt/ (US), /ˈɡet ɪt/ (UK)| n. |理解, 领悟 (较少用作名词)|The act of understanding or comprehending something.  (Less common usage)|例句：His get-it of the situation was quick. 他对情况的理解很快。（较少用）

例句：It took a while, but the get-it finally came. 花了一段时间，但最终还是明白了。（较少用）

近义词：understanding, comprehension, grasp|反义词：misunderstanding, misinterpretation|词性：noun (less common)
