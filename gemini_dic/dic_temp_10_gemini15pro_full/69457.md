feigning:/ˈfeɪnɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
假装的，装作的
Pretending to feel or be affected by (something).
He was feigning indifference, but I knew he cared. 他假装漠不关心，但我知道他在乎。

feigning:/ˈfeɪnɪŋ/
adjective
假装的
Pretending to feel or be affected by (something).
Her feigning smile didn't fool anyone. 她假装的笑容并没有骗到任何人。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *feynen*, from Old French *feindre*, from Latin *fingere* ("to mold, fashion, invent, feign").

SYNONYMS->
pretending, simulating, faking, sham, dissembling

ANTONYMS->
genuine, sincere, honest, real

PART OF SPEECH->
Verb (present participle), Adjective
