montiform:/ˈmɒntɪfɔːm/ | adj. | 山形的，像山的 | Having the shape of a mountain; resembling a mountain. |  The montiform cloud formation was an ominous sign. 山形的云层是一个不祥的征兆。| The terrain was characterized by montiform dunes.  地形以山形沙丘为特征。| 近义词: mountain-shaped, mountainous | 反义词: flat, level | 词性: 形容词
词源: From Latin "mons" (mountain) + "-formis" (shaped like).
