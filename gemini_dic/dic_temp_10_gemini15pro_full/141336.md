openwork:/ˈoʊpənˌwɜːrk/| n. adj.|镂空细工；透雕细工|Ornamental work, especially of metal or lace, consisting of a pattern of openings.  Something that is openwork has a pattern of holes in it.|The balcony had an intricate openwork railing.  阳台有一个复杂的镂空栏杆。|近义词：filigree, lattice, tracery|反义词：solid|词性：名词, 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From open + work.  "Open" meaning having gaps or holes, and "work" referring to the crafted or constructed nature of the piece.
