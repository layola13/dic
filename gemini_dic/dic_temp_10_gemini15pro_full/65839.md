eviscerate:/ɪˈvɪsəˌreɪt/ | v. | 去除内脏；取出…的内脏；掏空；彻底摧毁 | To remove the internal organs of a body; to deprive something of its essential part. |  The surgeon eviscerated the fish before grilling it.  外科医生在烤鱼之前先把鱼的内脏掏空了。

The explosion eviscerated the building, leaving only a shell. 爆炸摧毁了这栋建筑，只剩下一个空壳。| 近义词: disembowel, gut, devastate, destroy | 反义词: fill, replenish, construct | 词性: 动词

eviscerated:/ɪˈvɪsəˌreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 被取出内脏的；被掏空的；被彻底摧毁的 | Having had the internal organs removed; deprived of essential content or force. | The eviscerated chicken was ready for stuffing.  掏空内脏的鸡准备好了填料。

The eviscerated town offered a grim reminder of the war's devastation.  被摧毁的城镇残酷地提醒着人们战争的破坏力。 | 近义词: gutted, disemboweled, devastated, destroyed | 反义词: full, intact, whole | 词性: 形容词
