idoling: /ˈaɪdəlɪŋ/ | n. | 偶像崇拜；闲逛；无所事事 | The act of excessively admiring or obsessing over someone, often a celebrity; spending time doing nothing productive. | She spent her teenage years idoling over boy bands. 她十几岁的时候都在迷恋男孩乐队。| He was idoling around the town square. 他在城镇广场闲逛。 | synonyms: worshipping, adoring, lazing, loafing | antonyms: working, ignoring, disdaining | noun
