catcalling: /ˈkætˌkɔːlɪŋ/ (US), /ˈkætˌkɔːlɪŋ/ (UK)
|n.|
|调戏；吹口哨；(尤指男性对女性的) 街头性骚扰|
|The act of shouting sexually suggestive comments or whistles at someone, especially a woman, in a public place.|
|She experienced frequent catcalling while walking home from work.|她下班回家时经常遭到调戏。

|She was upset by the catcalling she received while jogging in the park.|她在公园慢跑时受到的调戏让她感到很不安。


|近义词：street harassment, sexual harassment, verbal abuse|
|反义词:| (No direct antonym, consider respectful behavior, appropriate conduct)
|词性: noun|
