gesso:/ˈdʒɛsoʊ/ | n. | 石膏粉，底料 | A mixture of plaster of Paris, glue, and whiting, used to prepare surfaces, especially wood panels, for painting.  | The artist applied a layer of gesso to the canvas before beginning to paint. 艺术家在开始绘画之前在画布上涂了一层石膏粉。 | synonyms: primer, ground | antonyms: N/A | noun

gesso:/ˈdʒɛsoʊ/ | v. | 用石膏粉涂 | To apply gesso to a surface. | He gessoed the wooden panel before painting it. 他在画木板之前先涂上了石膏粉。 | synonyms: prime, prepare | antonyms: N/A | verb
