parovarium:/ˌpærəʊˈvɛəriəm/ | n. | 副卵巢 | A rudimentary organ that lies in the broad ligament of the uterus, between the fallopian tube and the ovary. It is a remnant of the Wolffian duct in the female. |  The doctor identified a cyst in the parovarium. 医生在副卵巢中发现了一个囊肿。| synonym: epoophoron | antonym:  (No direct antonym for an anatomical structure) | noun
