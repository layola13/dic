palissander:/ˌpælɪˈsændər/ | n. | 巴西紫檀，东印度紫檀（木材） | A dense, reddish-brown wood from several tropical trees, used in making furniture and musical instruments. |  This guitar is made of high-quality palissander. 这把吉他是用优质的巴西紫檀木制成的。

The rich, warm tones of the palissander wood made it a favorite for violin makers. 巴西紫檀木丰富温暖的音色使其成为小提琴制作商的最爱。 | 近义词：rosewood, Brazilian rosewood | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a type of wood) | 词性: noun
