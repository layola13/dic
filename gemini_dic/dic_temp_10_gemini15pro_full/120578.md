mirit: /ˈmɪrɪt/ | v. | 值得，应得 | If someone or something merits a particular treatment, they deserve it. |  His performance merited a standing ovation. 他的表演值得起立鼓掌。 | synonyms: deserve, earn, warrant, justify | antonyms: forfeit | verb
