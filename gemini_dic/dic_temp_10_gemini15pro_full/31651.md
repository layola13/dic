chattels:/ˈtʃætlz/| n.|动产，财产（尤指动产）|Items of personal property that are movable, as opposed to real estate.  Chattels can include furniture, vehicles, livestock, and other tangible assets.|例句：The farmer's chattels included several cows and a tractor.  这位农民的动产包括几头奶牛和一台拖拉机。

例句：Upon her death, her chattels were divided amongst her children. 她死后，她的动产被分给了她的孩子们。|近义词：goods, possessions, belongings, effects, movables|反义词:real estate, property (immovable)|词性:noun
