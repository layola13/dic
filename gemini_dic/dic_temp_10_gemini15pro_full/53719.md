discloud:/dɪsˈklaʊd/
verb
驱散云彩; 使云消散
To cause clouds to disperse or disappear.
The wind disclouded the sky.  风驱散了天上的云。
近义词:clear, brighten
反义词:cloud, obscure
词性:v.

词源:  dis- (prefix meaning "apart, away") + cloud (n.)
