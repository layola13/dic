overkilling:/ˌoʊvərˈkɪlɪŋ/ | n. | 过度杀伤；矫枉过正 | The marketing campaign was overkill; they spent way too much money on advertising.  市场营销活动矫枉过正；他们在广告上花了太多钱。|  He brought five suitcases for a weekend trip – that's overkill. 他周末旅行带了五个手提箱——这太过分了。 | 近义词：excess, superfluity, overkill | 反义词: underkill, insufficiency | 词性: 名词

overkill:/ˌoʊvərˈkɪl/ | v. | 过度杀伤；做得过分 |  Don't overkill it with the spices; a little goes a long way.  不要过度使用香料；少许就足够了。 | They completely overkilled the presentation with too many slides and statistics. 他们的演示文稿用了太多的幻灯片和统计数据，做得太过火了。 |近义词：exaggerate, overdo | 反义词: underdo | 词性: 动词
