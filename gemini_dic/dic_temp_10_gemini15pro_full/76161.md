fusilier:/ˌfjuːzəˈlɪər/ | n. | 火枪手；轻步兵 | A soldier armed with a fusil (a light musket).  Originally, a soldier specifically trained to use a fusil. Now, often used to refer to a member of a particular regiment. | The fusiliers marched into battle.  火枪手们列队进入战场。| The Royal Welsh Fusiliers are a renowned regiment. 皇家威尔士火枪团是一支著名的军团。 | Synonyms: infantryman, rifleman, soldier | Antonyms: civilian | noun

fusilier:/ˌfjuːzəˈlɪər/ | n. | 火枪 | A light musket or flintlock rifle.
