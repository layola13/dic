faineant:/ˈfeɪnɪənt/| n. adj.|懒汉，懒惰的|A person who is unwilling to work; idle; lazy.|He was such a faineant that he lost his job. 他太懒了，以至于丢了工作。|近义词：idler, loafer, sluggard|反义词：hard worker, diligent person|词性:名词，形容词|词源: From French *fainéant*, from *fai néant* "doing nothing," from *faire* "to do" and *néant* "nothing."
