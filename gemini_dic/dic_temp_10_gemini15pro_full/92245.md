hypertrophous:/haɪˈpɜːrtrəfəs/
adjective
肥大的，过度生长的
Enlarged or overgrown beyond normal size; excessively developed.
The bodybuilder's hypertrophous biceps bulged impressively.  健美运动员肥大的二头肌令人印象深刻地鼓起。
