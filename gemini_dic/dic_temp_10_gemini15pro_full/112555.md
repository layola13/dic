lovelier: /ˈlʌvliər/ (US), /ˈlʌvliə(r)/ (UK)
|adj.|更可爱的，更美丽的|The comparative form of "lovely," meaning more lovely or beautiful.|She wore a lovelier dress than her sister. 她穿了一件比她姐姐更漂亮的裙子。|The sunset was even lovelier than the night before.  日落比前一天晚上更美。|近义词：prettier, more beautiful, more attractive, more charming|反义词：uglier, less attractive, less beautiful, plainer|词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *loveliker, comparative of lovely; equivalent to lovely + -er.  Ultimately from Old English luflic (“loving, friendly, amiable”).
