divesture:/daɪˈvɛstʃər/| n.|剥离，撤资|The company's divestiture of its non-core assets allowed it to focus on its main business.  该公司剥离非核心资产使其能够专注于其主要业务。|
The divestiture of the subsidiary resulted in a significant profit.  出售子公司带来了巨额利润。|
Synonyms: sale, disposal, shedding, spin-off|
Antonyms: acquisition, investment, purchase|
