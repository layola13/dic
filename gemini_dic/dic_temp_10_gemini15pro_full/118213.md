mephitism: /ˈmɛfɪtɪzəm/
noun
瘴气，毒气
A noxious or poisonous exhalation from the earth.
The cave was filled with a dangerous mephitism. 这个洞穴里充满了危险的瘴气。
The mephitism emanating from the volcano made it difficult to breathe. 火山散发出的毒气使人难以呼吸。
近义词:  miasma, effluvium, noxious fumes, toxic gas
反义词:  fresh air, pure air
词性:  noun

Etymology: From French *méphitisme*, from *méphitique* "mephitic", from Latin *mephitis* "noxious exhalation from the earth."  Ultimately from a presumed Etruscan source.
