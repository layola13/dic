forgive:/fərˈɡɪv/ (US), /fəˈɡɪv/ (UK)| vt.|原谅，宽恕|To grant pardon for or cease to feel resentment against (an offense, injury, etc.); to pardon or excuse (a person, error, fault, etc.).|She forgave him for his mistake. 她原谅了他的错误。|He forgave the debt. 他免除了债务。|近义词：pardon, excuse, absolve|反义词：blame, condemn|词性:动词

词源:
From Old English *forgiefan (attested only in forgiefenes "forgiveness"), from for- "away, off, against" + giefan "to give".  The sense development is thus "to give up, give away (resentment)."
