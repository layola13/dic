hunker:/ˈhʌŋkər/| v. |蹲下，蜷缩；顽固地坚持|To crouch down or squat close to the ground; to hold stubbornly to a position or opinion.|例句：The soldiers hunkered down in the trenches. 士兵们蜷缩在战壕里。

例句：He's hunkering down, determined to finish the project. 他蹲下来，决心完成这个项目。|近义词：crouch, squat, huddle, dig in|反义词:stand, rise|词性:动词

hunker:/ˈhʌŋkər/| n. |蹲下，蜷缩|The act of crouching down or squatting close to the ground.|例句：He took a hunker behind the wall. 他在墙后蹲了下来。

例句：The cat settled into a comfortable hunker. 猫舒适地蜷缩了起来。|近义词：crouch, squat|反义词:standing|词性:名词
