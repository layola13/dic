capability:/ˌkeɪpəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 能力，才能，性能 | The ability or power to do something. | This software has the capability to process large amounts of data.  这个软件有处理大量数据的能力。| capacity, ability, potential, competence | inability, incompetence | noun

capability:/ˌkeɪpəˈbɪləti/ | n. |  容量;生产力;生产能力 | The amount that something can produce or contain; the maximum amount that something can produce or contain. | The factory has a production capability of 10,000 units per month. 这家工厂每月有生产10000台的能力。 | capacity, potential, output | limitation, restriction | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *capabilitas, from Latin capabilis (“receptive, able to grasp or hold”), from capere (“to take, seize, grasp, hold”).
