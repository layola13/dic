dictatus:/dɪkˈteɪtəs/ | n. | 指令；命令；规定；声明 | A decree or authoritative statement. | The king issued a dictatus outlining the new laws. 国王发布了一项指令，概述了新的法律。 |  The pope's dictatus asserted his authority over secular rulers. 教皇的声明宣称他对世俗统治者拥有权力。 | 近义词: decree, edict, mandate, proclamation, dictum | 反义词: request, plea, petition | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Latin dictatus, past participle of dictare ("to dictate").  Ultimately from dicere ("to say").
