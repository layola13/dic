glose:/ɡloʊs/ | v. | 注释；掩饰；敷衍；| to provide an explanation for a word or phrase, often one that is difficult to understand; to make something seem less bad than it really is |  He glossed over the company's declining profits in his presentation.  他在演讲中掩饰了公司利润下降的事实。 | synonyms: explain, interpret, annotate, comment on, elucidate, expound, paraphrase, clarify, illuminate; disguise, conceal, cover up, whitewash, varnish over | antonyms: obscure, confuse, complicate, obfuscate | verb

glose:/ɡloʊs/ | n. | 注释；解释；| an explanation or interpretation of a word or phrase | The editor added a glose to the difficult passage. 编辑在难懂的段落添加了注释。| synonyms: explanation, interpretation, annotation, commentary, elucidation, exposition, paraphrase, clarification, illumination | antonyms: obfuscation, confusion, complication | noun
