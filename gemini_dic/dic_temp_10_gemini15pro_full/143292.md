outdraw:/ˌaʊtˈdrɔː/
verb
胜过；超过（尤指在决斗中拔枪更快）; 吸引更多观众；比…更受欢迎
to be quicker than someone else at drawing a gun, especially in a fight in the American Wild West; to attract larger audiences than something else
例句：He outdrew his opponent and fired first. 他比对手拔枪更快，先开了一枪。

例句：The new movie outdrew all the others at the box office. 这部新电影的票房超过了所有其他电影。
近义词：surpass, exceed, outdo, beat, triumph over
反义词：lose to, be defeated by
词性：v.

词性：v.
