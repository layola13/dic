anaesthesiologist's:/ˌænɪsˌθiːziˈɒlədʒɪsts/ | n. | 麻醉师的，麻醉医生的 |  Something that belongs to or is associated with an anaesthesiologist. ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀναισθησία (anaisthesía, “lack of sensation”) + -ist + -'s.  ἀ- (a-, “without”) +  αἴσθησις (aísthēsis, “sensation, feeling”).
