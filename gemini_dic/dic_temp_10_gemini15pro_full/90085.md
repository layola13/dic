hostile:/ˈhɒstaɪl/ (British), /ˈhɑːstaɪl/ (American)| adj.|敌对的，敌意的；不友好的；怀敌意的|Showing or feeling opposition or dislike; unfriendly.|例句：The local people were hostile to the soldiers. 当地居民对士兵怀有敌意。|例句：He gave me a hostile look. 他狠狠地瞪了我一眼。|近义词：unfriendly, antagonistic, aggressive, inimical|反义词：friendly, amicable, welcoming|词性:形容词

hostile:/ˈhɒstaɪl/ (British), /ˈhɑːstaɪl/ (American)| n.|敌对的人；敌方|A person or thing that is unfriendly or shows dislike.|例句：The hostages were surrounded by hostiles. 人质被敌方包围。|例句：The ship was attacked by hostiles. 船只遭到敌对者的袭击。|近义词：enemy, opponent, adversary|反义词：friend, ally, supporter|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *hostilis, from hostis (“enemy, stranger”).
