makebate:/ˈmeɪkbeɪt/ | n. | 挑拨离间的人；搬弄是非的人 | A person who stirs up trouble or discord between others. |  He's such a makebate, always whispering lies and causing arguments. 他真是个搬弄是非的人，总是散布谎言，挑起争端。 | 近义词: troublemaker, mischief-maker, agitator, instigator | 反义词: peacemaker, mediator | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From Middle English *makebate, equivalent to make + bate "strife, contention."  Ultimately from Old French *batre "to beat."
