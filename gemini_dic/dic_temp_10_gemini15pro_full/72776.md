fohn:/fɜːn/ /foʊn/| n. |焚风; 干燥热风|A warm dry wind that blows down the leeward side of mountains, sometimes melting snow and causing avalanches.|例句：The fohn wind was so strong that it knocked down trees. 焚风强劲得把树都吹倒了。

例句：The melting snow caused by the fohn led to flooding in the valley. 焚风融化的雪导致山谷洪水泛滥。|近义词：chinook, foehn, Santa Ana wind|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
