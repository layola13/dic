commuting:/kəˈmjuːtɪŋ/ /kəˈmjutɪŋ/
n.
通勤，上下班往返
the activity of travelling regularly between work and home
例句：My morning commute takes about an hour. 我每天早上通勤大约需要一个小时。

例句：Commuting by car can be stressful, especially during rush hour. 开车通勤可能会很有压力，尤其是在高峰时段。

近义词：traveling, journeying
反义词：staying, residing
词性：noun (名词)


commuting:/kəˈmjuːtɪŋ/ /kəˈmjutɪŋ/
adj.
通勤的
traveling regularly between work and home
例句：Commuting costs can be quite high in big cities.  在大城市，通勤成本可能相当高。

例句：He has a long commuting distance. 他的通勤距离很长。

近义词：traveling, journeying
反义词：resident, local
词性：adjective (形容词)
