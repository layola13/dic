conflagrative:/kənˈflæɡrətɪv/
adjective
燃烧的；煽动性的
Relating to or causing a large fire; tending to incite conflict.
The conflagrative nature of the dry brush made it easy for the wildfire to spread. 干灌木丛易燃的特性使野火很容易蔓延。
The speaker's conflagrative rhetoric incited violence in the crowd. 演讲者煽动性的言辞引发了人群中的暴力事件。
近义词:incendiary, inflammatory, combustible
反义词:fire-retardant, nonflammable, peaceful
词性:adjective

词源:
From Latin conflagratus, past participle of conflagrare ("to burn completely"), from con- ("together") + flagrare ("to burn").
