bracketing:/ˈbrækɪtɪŋ/ | n. |括号，分组，包围，归类|The process of placing something within brackets, either literally or figuratively.  Enclosing something within a pair of brackets.  Grouping similar things together.|例句：The bracketing of the two candidates in the polls suggests a close race.  民意调查中两位候选人的支持率接近，表明竞争激烈。

例句：The bracketing of these two ideas under one heading is misleading.  将这两个观点归入同一标题下具有误导性。|近义词：grouping, classifying, enclosing, encompassing|反义词: separating, dividing|词性:noun

bracketing:/ˈbrækɪtɪŋ/ | v. |用括号括起来，把…归入同一类，将…相提并论|To enclose something within brackets. To group similar things together.|例句：He bracketed the two numbers together in the equation. 他在方程式中将两个数字用括号括起来。

例句：The media bracketed the two teams as the favorites to win. 媒体将这两支队伍相提并论，认为它们是夺冠热门。|近义词：grouping, classifying, enclosing, pairing |反义词: separating, distinguishing|词性:verb
