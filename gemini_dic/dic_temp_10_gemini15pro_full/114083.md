magnificat:/mæɡˈnɪfɪkæt/| n.|尊主颂|A hymn expressing praise of the Virgin Mary, taken from Luke 1:46–55.  It is traditionally recited or sung at Vespers in the Western church and at various other services in the Orthodox Church.|The choir sang the Magnificat beautifully.  唱诗班优美地唱了尊主颂。|Near synonyms: canticle, hymn, anthem|Antonym: N/A (Antonyms are not readily applicable to a specific hymn.)|noun
