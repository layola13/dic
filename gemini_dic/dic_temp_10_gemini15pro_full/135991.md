nonrandom:/ˌnɑːnˈrændəm/ | adj. | 非随机的 | Not occurring by chance; deliberate or planned. | The selection of participants for the study was nonrandom, focusing on specific demographics. 这项研究的参与者选择是非随机的，侧重于特定的人口统计数据。|  The placement of the products on the shelves was nonrandom, designed to maximize sales. 货架上产品的摆放是非随机的，旨在最大限度地提高销售额。| 近义词: deliberate, intentional, planned, systematic | 反义词: random, chance, haphazard | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- + random.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "random" refers to something happening by chance.
