graven:/ˈɡreɪvən/| adj.|雕刻的；铭刻的；深深印入的|Carved or impressed deeply, as if on stone.  It can refer to physical carvings or to something deeply impressed on the mind or memory.|例句：The graven image of the god was worshipped by the tribe.  部落崇拜着神明的雕像。

例句：His face was graven with lines of worry. 他脸上刻满了忧虑的皱纹。|近义词：carved, engraved, etched, inscribed, imprinted|反义词：smooth, unblemished|词性:形容词
