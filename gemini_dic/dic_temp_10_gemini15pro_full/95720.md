inconsequence:/ɪnˈkɒnsɪkwəns/ /ɪnˈkɑːnsɪkwəns/| n. |不重要；无关紧要；逻辑混乱|Lack of importance or relevance; illogicality.|Her lateness was of no inconsequence. 她的迟到并不重要。|Synonyms: insignificance, triviality, irrelevance, illogicality|Antonyms: importance, significance, relevance, logic|noun


inconsequential:/ˌɪnkɒnsɪˈkwenʃəl/ /ˌɪnkɑːnsɪˈkwenʃəl/| adj. |不重要的；无关紧要的；不合逻辑的|Not important or significant; illogical.|The details seemed inconsequential at the time. 这些细节在当时看来无关紧要。|Synonyms: insignificant, trivial, irrelevant, illogical|Antonyms: important, significant, relevant, logical|adjective
