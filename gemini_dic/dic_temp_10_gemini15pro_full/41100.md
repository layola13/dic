copresenting:/ˌkoʊprɪˈzɛntɪŋ/
|n.|共同呈现；共同主持|Their copresenting of the award ceremony was well-received.|他们共同主持颁奖典礼，反响良好。|
Etymology: co- (together) + presenting (from present, meaning "to offer for observation")
Synonyms: cohosting, joint presentation
Antonyms: solo presentation, independent presentation
Word Type: Verb, Adjective, Noun
