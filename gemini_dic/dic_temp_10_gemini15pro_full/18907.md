besnow:/bɪˈsnoʊ/
|v.|用雪覆盖|To cover with snow; to snow upon.|The fields were besnowed overnight. 田野一夜之间被雪覆盖了。|
|v.|使变白如雪|To make white like snow.|Her hair was besnowed with age. 她的头发因年老而变得雪白。|
|近义词：cover with snow, blanket with snow|反义词：melt, thaw|词性:动词
