gentled:/ˈdʒɛnt(ə)ld/

|adj.|温和的；驯服的；文雅的|Having been made gentle; tame; calm.|Her gentled voice soothed the crying baby. 她温柔的声音抚慰了哭泣的婴儿。|Synonym: gentle, tame, calm, meek|Antonym: wild, fierce, unruly|Adjective
