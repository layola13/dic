carriageable:/ˈkæridʒəbl/
adj.
可搬运的，适于运输的
Suitable for being carried or transported; portable.
The furniture was not carriageable, so they had to hire movers.  这些家具无法搬运，所以他们不得不雇搬运工。
Synonyms: portable, transportable, movable
Antonyms: unmovable, immobile, fixed
词性: 形容词


carriageable:/ˈkæridʒəbl/
adj.
(指女性)适于结婚的，可娶的 (旧时用法)
(of a woman) Suitable for marriage; marriageable (old-fashioned).
She was considered a carriageable young lady from a good family. 她被认为是一位出身名门的适婚女子。
Synonyms: marriageable, eligible
Antonyms: unmarriageable, ineligible
词性: 形容词
