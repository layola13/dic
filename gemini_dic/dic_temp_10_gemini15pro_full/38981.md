confining:/kənˈfaɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 限制的；局限的；狭窄的 | Restricting freedom of movement; limiting |  例句：The illness was confining, keeping her in bed for weeks.  这种疾病限制了她的行动，让她卧床数周。

例句：Living in a small apartment can feel confining. 住在小公寓里会让人感觉很局限。 | 近义词：restricting, limiting, restraining, cramped, narrow | 反义词：liberating, freeing, expansive, open, spacious | 词性: 形容词

confine: /kənˈfaɪn/ | v. | 限制；局限；禁闭 | Keep within limits; restrict; imprison | 例句：Please confine your remarks to the topic under discussion. 请把你的发言限制在讨论的主题范围内。

例句：He was confined to a wheelchair after the accident.  事故发生后，他只能坐轮椅。 | 近义词：restrict, limit, restrain, imprison, enclose | 反义词：free, liberate, release, expand | 词性: 动词

词源: From Old French *confiner, from Latin confinis (“bordering upon”), from con- (“together”) + finis (“end, boundary, limit”).
