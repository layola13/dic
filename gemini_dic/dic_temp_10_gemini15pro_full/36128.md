coestate:/koʊɪˈsteɪt/| n. |共同继承人，共同遗产拥有人|A person who inherits property jointly with another or others.|John and his sister are coestates of their father's farm.  约翰和他的妹妹是他父亲农场的共同继承人。|Near synonyms: joint heir, coheir|Antonym: sole heir|noun

coestate:/koʊɪˈsteɪt/| adj. |共同拥有遗产的|Owning an estate jointly with another or others.|They are coestate owners of the property. 他们是该房产的共同所有者。|Near synonyms: joint, shared|Antonym: sole|adjective
