cowrote:/ˌkoʊˈroʊt/
verb
共同撰写
wrote something together with someone else
Shakespeare cowrote *Two Noble Kinsmen* with John Fletcher. 莎士比亚与约翰·弗莱彻共同创作了《两位贵族亲戚》。
co-authored, collaborated on
(no single antonym applicable)
