fanfare:/ˈfænfeə(r)/| n.| fanfare |A short loud flourish of trumpets or other brass instruments, typically one played to announce the arrival of someone important.  A showy display or celebration, especially designed to attract attention.|The president was greeted with a fanfare of trumpets. 总统在响亮的喇叭声中受到欢迎。|The new product launch was accompanied by much fanfare. 新产品的发布伴随着大量的宣传。|synonyms: flourish, blare, blast, peal, chorus, fanfare of trumpets |antonyms: silence, quiet |noun
