outlustre:/ˌaʊtˈlʌstər/
verb
使黯然失色；胜过；比…更有光彩
to surpass in brilliance or excellence
Her performance completely outlustred everyone else's. 她的表演完全胜过了其他人。

近义词:outshine, eclipse, surpass, exceed
反义词:dim, dull, obscure
词性:v.
