assuredness:/əˈʃʊərdnɪs/
noun
自信；把握；确信
A state of being confident and certain.
She spoke with the assuredness of a seasoned performer. 她以老练表演者的自信进行了演讲。

He approached the task with complete assuredness. 他以十足的把握来处理这项任务。

近义词: confidence, certainty, self-assurance, poise, aplomb
反义词: insecurity, uncertainty, doubt, hesitation, diffidence
词性: noun
