leasts:/liːsts/| adj. n.| 最少的，最小的；最小值；最少的事物 |Of all the options, this one is the least appealing. 在所有选项中，这个是最不吸引人的。|近义词：fewest, smallest, minimal, slightest|反义词：most, greatest, largest|词性:形容词，名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *lǣst, superlative of *lȳtel (“little”), from Proto-Germanic *laisists (“least”), superlative of *litaz (“small, little”). Cognate with Dutch least (“least”), German letzt- (as in letztendlich "finally"), letzt (“last”). Related also to last.
