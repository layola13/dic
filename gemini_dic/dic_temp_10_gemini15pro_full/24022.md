breed:/briːd/
n.|品种，种类；血统，出身|A particular type of animal or plant.|例句：This breed of dog is known for its loyalty. 这种狗以其忠诚而闻名。|近义词：type, kind, variety, strain|反义词: - |名词
breed:/briːd/
v.|繁殖，饲养；产生，孕育|To produce offspring; to raise (animals).|例句：They breed horses on their farm. 他们在农场养马。|近义词：raise, rear, propagate, generate|反义词:extinguish, destroy|动词
