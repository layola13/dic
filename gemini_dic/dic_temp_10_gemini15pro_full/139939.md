odometries:/oʊˌdɒmɪtriz/
n.
里程计读数；测程法；里程测量
The process of determining the position and orientation of a mobile robot by measuring the rotation of its wheels.
The robot uses odometries to navigate the maze.  这个机器人使用里程计读数在迷宫中导航。
The odometries data was corrupted, leading the robot astray. 里程计数据损坏了，导致机器人误入歧途。

近义词:dead reckoning, wheel odometry
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "absolute positioning")
