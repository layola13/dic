half-winged:/ˌhæfˈwɪŋd/ | adj. | 半翅的；半翅目的 | Having wings that are only partially developed or of a different texture from the rest of the wing.  Relating to or characteristic of insects of the order Hemiptera. |  Many half-winged insects, like aphids, are agricultural pests. 许多半翅目昆虫，例如蚜虫，都是农业害虫。 | short-winged, incompletely winged | full-winged, fully winged | adjective
