non-darwinian:/ˌnɒnˈdɑːˈwɪnɪən/ | adj. | 非达尔文主义的 | Not related to or consistent with the theory of evolution by natural selection as proposed by Charles Darwin.  |  Example: Some scientists propose non-Darwinian mechanisms for evolutionary change. 一些科学家提出了非达尔文式的进化变化机制。 | Example:  The theory of punctuated equilibrium is sometimes considered a non-Darwinian evolutionary theory. 间断平衡理论有时被认为是非达尔文进化论。 | Synonyms:  non-selectionist,  Lamarckian (sometimes) | Antonyms:  Darwinian, selectionist | adjective

non-Darwinian:/ˌnɒnˈdɑːˈwɪnɪən/ | n. | 非达尔文主义者 | A person who does not subscribe to the theory of evolution by natural selection. | Example:  Some non-Darwinians believe in intelligent design. 一些非达尔文主义者相信智能设计。 | Example:  The conference attracted both Darwinians and non-Darwinians.  这次会议吸引了达尔文主义者和非达尔文主义者。 | Synonyms:  non-selectionist | Antonyms: Darwinian, selectionist | noun
