deform:/dɪˈfɔːrm/ | v. | 使变形，使畸形 | to spoil the natural or normal shape or form of something. | The intense heat deformed the plastic.  高温使塑料变形。| synonyms:  distort, twist, contort, warp, buckle | antonyms:  fix, repair, straighten, perfect | verb
