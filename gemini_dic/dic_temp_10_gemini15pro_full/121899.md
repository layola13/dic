misscript:/ˈmɪsˌskrɪpt/
noun
错误的文本; 写错的文字; 抄写错误
a text, especially a manuscript, containing an error or errors.
The monk realized he had made a misscript when copying the ancient text.  这个僧侣意识到他在抄写古文时犯了一个抄写错误。
The publisher found several misscripts in the final draft. 出版商在最终稿中发现了一些错误的文本。
miswriting, error, mistake, typo
correct text, accurate text
