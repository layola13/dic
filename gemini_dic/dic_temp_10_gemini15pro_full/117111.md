mealworm:/ˈmiːlwɜːrm/| n. |面包虫，黄粉虫|A small, yellowish-brown beetle larva, often used as food for pet reptiles and birds, and increasingly for human consumption due to its high protein content.|例句：I feed my bearded dragon mealworms every other day. 我每隔一天喂我的鬃狮蜥面包虫。

例句：Mealworms are a sustainable source of protein. 黄粉虫是可持续的蛋白质来源。|近义词：grub, larva|反义词:None|词性:noun
