hyperconformists:/ˌhaɪpərˈkənˈfɔːrmɪsts/
n.
超级遵奉者；过度顺从者
People who conform to an excessive degree, often beyond what is considered normal or necessary.  They adhere rigidly to rules, norms, or expectations, sometimes out of fear of deviating or a desire for acceptance.
The study suggests that hyperconformists may be more susceptible to propaganda. 这项研究表明，过度顺从者可能更容易受到宣传的影响。
Hyperconformists tend to avoid rocking the boat and readily accept the status quo. 过度顺从者往往避免制造麻烦，很容易接受现状。
synonyms: conformists, followers, yes-men, sheep
antonyms: nonconformists, rebels, mavericks, individualists
noun
