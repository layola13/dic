dispassionatenesses:/dɪsˈpæʃənɪtnɪsɪz/
n.
冷静；客观；不动声色
the state of being dispassionate; calmness, objectivity, lack of emotional involvement
The judge listened to the arguments with utter dispassionateness.  法官极其冷静地听取了双方的辩论。
近义词:calmness, composure, objectivity, detachment, impartiality
反义词:passion, fervor, bias, prejudice
词性:名词


dispassionate:/dɪsˈpæʃənɪt/
adj.
冷静的；客观的；不动声色的
not influenced by strong emotion, and so able to be rational and impartial
He gave a dispassionate account of the incident. 他对事件作了冷静的描述。
近义词:calm, composed, objective, detached, impartial
反义词:passionate, emotional, biased, prejudiced
词性:形容词


词源:
From dis- + passionate + -ness.  "dis-" 表示否定，"passionate" 表示充满激情的，"-ness" 名词后缀，表示状态。
