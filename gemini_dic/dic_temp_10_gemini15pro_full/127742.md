negotiation:/nɪˌɡoʊʃiˈeɪʃən/ (US), /nɪˌɡəʊʃiˈeɪʃən/ (UK)| n.| 谈判，协商|The negotiation between the two companies lasted for months.  两家公司之间的谈判持续了数月。|例句：The successful negotiation resulted in a profitable contract for both parties. 成功的谈判为双方带来了有利可图的合同。

例句：The negotiation for a pay raise was unsuccessful.  加薪谈判没有成功。

近义词：discussion, bargain, deliberation, conference|反义词：dispute, argument, conflict|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *negociacion, from Old French negociacion, from Late Latin negotiatio (“business, trade, traffic”), from Latin negotiari (“to do business, trade, traffic”), from negotium (“business, occupation, employment”), from nec otium (“not leisure, not idleness”), from nec (“not, nor”) + otium (“leisure, ease, idleness”).
