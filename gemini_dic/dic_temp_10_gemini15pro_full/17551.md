beatings:/ˈbiːtɪŋz/| n. | 打，殴打，击打; (有规律的)敲击声，跳动声 |Repeated beatings left the child traumatized.  反复的殴打给孩子留下了心理创伤。| Synonyms: thrashings, whippings, floggings, poundings, pulsations | Antonyms:  caresses, strokes | noun

Etymology: From Middle English *betyng*, verbal noun from *beten* (“to beat”).
