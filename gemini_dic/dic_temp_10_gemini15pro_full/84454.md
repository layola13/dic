haloid:/ˈheɪlɔɪd/| adj. n.|卤素的，卤化物的；卤化物|Resembling a salt; specifically, consisting of a binary compound of a halogen with a more electropositive element or radical.  A haloid salt.|Silver bromide is a haloid that is used in photography. 溴化银是一种用于摄影的卤化物。|近义词：halide|反义词:|adjective, noun

haloid:/ˈheɪlɔɪd/| n.|卤化物|A binary compound of a halogen with a more electropositive element or radical.|Sodium chloride is a common haloid. 氯化钠是一种常见的卤化物。|近义词：halide|反义词:|noun
