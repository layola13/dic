nonfragrant:/nɑːnˈfreɪɡrənt/
adjective
无香味的；无香气的
Lacking fragrance; not fragrant.
The mushrooms were nonfragrant but delicious. 这些蘑菇没有香味，但是味道鲜美。
近义词:odorless, scentless, unscented
反义词:fragrant, aromatic, scented
词性:adjective

词源:
non- (prefix): not, without  + fragrant (adjective): having a pleasant smell
