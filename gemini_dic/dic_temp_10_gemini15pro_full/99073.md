It often refers to the times when multiple people or resources are free at the same time.| We are trying to find interavailabilities in our schedules to meet next week. 我们正在努力寻找下周开会的空闲时间。| The software helps coordinate the interavailabilities of the team members.  这个软件帮助协调团队成员的空闲时间。|近义词：available times, free time, openings, slots|反义词:unavailabilities, booked times|词性:noun
