gabel:/ˈɡeɪbl/ (US), /ˈɡeɪbəl/ (UK)| n.|餐叉；(尤指)两齿叉，鱼叉；鹿角的分叉|A gabel is a fork, especially a two-pronged fork used for eating fish, or a forked part of a deer's antler.|He used a gabel to serve the fish. 他用一把两齿叉来盛鱼。|Synonyms: fork, prong|Antonyms: spoon (loosely)|noun

gabel:/ˈɡeɪbl/ (US), /ˈɡeɪbəl/ (UK)| v.|用叉子吃；用叉举起|To gabel something is to eat it with a fork or to lift it with a fork.|She gabled a piece of asparagus. 她用叉子叉起了一根芦笋。|Synonyms: fork, spear|Antonyms: spoon (loosely)|verb
