anamorphism:/ˌænəˈmɔːrfɪzəm/ | n. | 变形，失真；歪像 | A distorted projection or drawing that appears normal when viewed from a particular point or with a suitable mirror or lens. | Example 1: The artist used anamorphism to create a hidden image in the painting. 艺术家在画中使用了变形手法来隐藏图像。

Example 2: The anamorphic image appeared stretched and distorted until viewed through a cylindrical mirror.  这幅变形图像看起来被拉伸和扭曲，直到通过圆柱形镜子观看。 | 近义词: distortion, deformation | 反义词:  N/A | noun
