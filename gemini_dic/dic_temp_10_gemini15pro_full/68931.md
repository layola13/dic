fatally:/ˈfeɪtəli/| adv.|致命地；灾难性地；不幸地|In a way that causes death or disaster.  Resulting in death; causing death.|The driver was fatally injured in the crash.  司机在车祸中受致命伤。|Synonyms: lethally, mortally, terminally|Antonyms: harmlessly, safely|adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *fataliter, from Latin fatalis (“ordained by fate”), from fatum (“fate”).
