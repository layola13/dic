hardinesses:/ˈhɑːrdinəsiz/
n.
耐寒性，强健性，坚韧性
The ability of plants to survive adverse winter conditions.  The hardinesses of different rose varieties vary greatly.
These new varieties of wheat have increased hardinesses to drought.
近义词:robustness, resilience, toughness, durability
反义词:fragility, weakness, vulnerability
词性:名词(复数)

Etymology: From hardy + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  "Hardy" traces back to Old Norse "harðr" meaning "hard."
