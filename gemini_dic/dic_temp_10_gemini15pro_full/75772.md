fulsomeness:/ˈfʊlsəmnəs/
n.
过分恭维;令人作呕的甜蜜;腻烦;油腔滑调
Excessive or insincere praise; sickening sweetness; disgustingly excessive
Her fulsomeness made me uncomfortable. 她的过分恭维让我很不舒服。

近义词:excessiveness, overabundance, superfluity, effusiveness, lavishness,  adulation, flattery
反义词:restraint, moderation,  sincerity, genuineness

词性:noun

词源:From Middle English *fulsumnes, from Old English fūlsumnes (“abundance, satiety, plenty”), from fūl (“full”) + -nes (“-ness”). Cognate with German Füllsel (“filling, stuffing, padding”).
