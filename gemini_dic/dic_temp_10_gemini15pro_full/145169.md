overassurednesses:/ˌoʊvərəˈʃʊərdnɪsɪz/
noun
过分自信，过于自信的态度
The state of being excessively confident or self-assured, often to the point of arrogance.
His overassuredness led him to make several costly mistakes. 他的过分自信导致他犯了几个代价高昂的错误。
Near synonyms: arrogance, presumptuousness, conceitedness, hubris
Antonyms: humility, modesty, diffidence, self-doubt
n.
