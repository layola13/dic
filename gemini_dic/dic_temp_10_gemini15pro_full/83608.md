haematoscope:/ˌhiːməˈtɒskəʊp/ (UK) /ˌhiməˈtɑːskoʊp/ (US)| n.| 血细胞计数器；验血器 |An instrument for examining blood, especially for counting blood cells.| Doctors used a haematoscope to analyse the patient's blood sample. 医生用验血器分析了病人的血样。|Synonyms: hemocytometer, blood counter| Antonyms: None| noun
