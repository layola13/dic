headwater:/ˈhɛdwɔːtər/ (US), /ˈhɛdwɔːtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |源头，上游，水源|The source of a stream or river.|例句：The dam was built near the headwaters of the river.  大坝建在河的源头附近。

例句：Protecting the headwaters is crucial for the health of the entire river system. 保护水源对整个河流系统的健康至关重要。

近义词：source, origin, fountainhead|反义词:mouth, outlet|词性:名词
