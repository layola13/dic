die:/daɪ/
verb
死亡，消亡
to stop living; to become dead
He died of a heart attack. 他死于心脏病发作。

die:/daɪ/
noun
骰子
a small cube with each side marked with a different number of spots, used in games
He rolled the die and got a six. 他掷骰子掷了个六。


近义词:decease, perish, expire (v.); cube, block (n.)
反义词:live, survive (v.); born (v.)
词性:v. n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *dēgan "to die," of Germanic origin; related to Old Norse deyja, Old High German touwen. The noun meaning "dice" comes from Old French de, from Latin datum "something given," which is related to the verb dare "to give."
