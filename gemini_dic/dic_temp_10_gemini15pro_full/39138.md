confusing:/kənˈfjuːzɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人困惑的；难以理解的 | Causing confusion; difficult to understand. | The instructions were very confusing, and I couldn't figure out how to assemble the furniture.  说明书非常令人困惑，我不知道该如何组装家具。| synonyms: perplexing, bewildering, baffling, unclear, ambiguous | antonyms: clear, obvious, straightforward, understandable | adjective


confuse:/kənˈfjuːz/ | v. | 使困惑；混淆 | To make (someone) bewildered or perplexed. |  The similar names confused me.  相似的名字把我弄糊涂了。 | synonyms: disorient, muddle, perplex, bewilder | antonyms: clarify, explain, enlighten | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *confusen*, from Old French confondre, from Latin confundere (“to pour together, mix, mingle, blend, confuse”), from con- (“together”) + fundere (“to pour, melt”).
