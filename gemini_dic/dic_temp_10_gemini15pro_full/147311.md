overmonopolize: /ˌoʊvər məˈnɑːpəˌlaɪz/ | v. | 过度垄断，过分独占 | To monopolize something to an excessive degree, effectively excluding all or most competitors. |  The company overmonopolized the market, driving smaller businesses into bankruptcy.  该公司过度垄断了市场，导致小型企业破产。 | Example: Their attempts to overmonopolize the pharmaceutical market stifled innovation and limited consumer choice. 他们的企图过度垄断医药市场的行为扼杀了创新，限制了消费者的选择。| Synonyms: dominate, control, corner | Antonyms: share, distribute, liberalize | Verb

overmonopolization: /ˌoʊvər məˌnɑːpələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度垄断，过分独占 | The act of overmonopolizing something. | The overmonopolization of the media by a few large corporations is a concern for many. 少数大型企业对媒体的过度垄断是许多人关心的问题。 | Example: The government is investigating the overmonopolization of the telecommunications industry. 政府正在调查电信行业的过度垄断情况。| Synonyms: domination, control | Antonyms: sharing, distribution | Noun
