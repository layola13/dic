miscitation:/ˌmɪsaɪˈteɪʃən/
noun
错误引用；误引
The act of misquoting someone or something.
He was accused of miscitation of legal precedent. 他被指控错误引用了法律先例。
近义词: misquotation, misattribution
反义词: accurate citation, correct quotation
词性: noun

miscitation:/ˌmɪsaɪˈteɪʃən/
verb
错误地引用；误引
To misquote someone or something.
The journalist miscited the scientist's findings.  这位记者错误地引用了科学家的发现。
近义词: misquote, misattribute
反义词: accurately cite, correctly quote
词性: verb
