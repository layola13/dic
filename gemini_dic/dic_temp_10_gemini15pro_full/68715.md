farflung:/ˌfɑːrˈflʌŋ/| adj.|广泛的；分布广的；遥远的|Extending or reaching over a large area; remote.|例句：The company has farflung operations in Asia and Europe. 这家公司在亚洲和欧洲的业务广泛。

例句：She traveled to farflung corners of the world. 她前往了世界各地遥远的角落。|近义词：widespread, extensive, remote, distant|反义词：local, nearby, close|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From far + flung (past participle of fling, meaning "to throw or cast").  The image is of something being thrown or spread over a wide area.
