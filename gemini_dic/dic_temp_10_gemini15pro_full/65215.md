etiolation:/ˌiːtiəˈleɪʃn/
n.
黄化，苍白
The process in plants of becoming pale and drawn out due to a lack of light.
The seedlings showed signs of etiolation after being kept in the dark for a week.  这些幼苗在黑暗中放置一周后出现了黄化的迹象。
blanching, whitening
greening, healthy growth
noun
