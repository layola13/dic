multipersonal:/ˌmʌltiˈpɜːrsənəl/ | adj. | 多人参与的; 多人性格的 |  Relating to or involving more than one person, especially in a psychological context; exhibiting multiple personalities. |  Example: Therapists specializing in dissociative identity disorder often work with multipersonal patients. 治疗分离性身份识别障碍的治疗师经常与多重人格的病人打交道。|  Example:  The novel explored the complexities of a multipersonal existence. 这部小说探讨了多重人格存在的复杂性。 | Synonym: multifaceted, multiple | Antonym: singular, individual | adjective
