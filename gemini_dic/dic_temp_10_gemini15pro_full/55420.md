distilled:/dɪˈstɪld/ | adj. | 蒸馏的；提纯的；精炼的 | Purified or concentrated by distillation; refined or extracted to its essence. |  The chemist analyzed the distilled water. 化学家分析了蒸馏水。 | refined, purified, concentrated | raw, unrefined, crude | adjective

distill:/dɪˈstɪl/ | v. | 蒸馏；提炼；渗出 | Purify (a liquid) by heating it so that it vaporizes, then cooling and condensing the vapor and collecting the resulting liquid; extract the essential meaning or most important aspects of something. | They distill whiskey from corn. 他们用玉米酿造威士忌。 | refine, purify, extract, concentrate | dilute, contaminate, adulterate | verb
