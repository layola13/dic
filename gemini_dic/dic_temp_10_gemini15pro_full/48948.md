defy's:/dɪˈfaɪz/ | v. | 挑战；违抗；不服从 |  This action defies explanation. 这一举动难以解释。 |例句：His attitude defies all description. 他的态度难以形容。| 近义词: resist, oppose, challenge | 反义词: obey, submit, comply | 词性:动词 (Third-person singular simple present form of defy)

词源: From Old French *desfier, from des- (expressing reversal) + fier (“to trust, confide in”), from Latin fidere.
