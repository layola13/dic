corban:/ˈkɔːrbæn/ (US), /ˈkɔːbæn/ (UK)| n. |献礼，奉献物|A gift dedicated to God; something devoted to a sacred purpose; by extension, anything forbidden or prohibited.|Example 1: He declared his house a corban, meaning it could no longer be used by his family. 他宣布他的房子是奉献给神的，这意味着他的家人不能再使用它了。

Example 2:  The Pharisees were criticized for using corban as a loophole to avoid their responsibilities to their parents. 法利赛人被批评利用“奉献物”作为逃避赡养父母责任的漏洞。|近义词：offering, dedication, gift|反义词: appropriation, secular|词性:名词
