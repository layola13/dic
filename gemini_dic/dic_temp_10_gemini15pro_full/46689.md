dams:/dæmz/| n.|水坝，堤坝|A barrier constructed across a waterway to control the flow or raise the level of water.|The Hoover Dam is a marvel of engineering.  胡佛水坝是工程学的奇迹。|
The Three Gorges Dam is the world's largest power station. 三峡大坝是世界上最大的发电站。|
synonyms: barriers, embankments, levees|antonyms: breaches, openings|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *dam*, from Old English *damm* (“dam, bank, dike”), from Proto-Germanic *dammaz (“dam”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰemh₂- (“to build, construct”). Cognate with Dutch *dam* (“dam”), German *Damm* (“dam, causeway, embankment”), Icelandic *dammr* (“dam”).
