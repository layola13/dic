parfait:/pɑːrˈfeɪ/ (US), /ˈpɑːfeɪ/ (UK)| n.|芭菲，冻糕|A layered dessert typically made with yogurt, granola, and fruit, often served in a tall glass.  It can also refer to a savory dish made with meat or vegetables in aspic.|例句：The strawberry parfait looked delicious. 草莓芭菲看起来很美味。

例句：She ordered a chicken parfait for lunch. 她午餐点了一份鸡肉冻糕。


|近义词：dessert, sundae, trifle|反义词: (No direct antonym for a food item)|词性:noun
