outvoyaging:/ˌaʊtˈvɔɪɪdʒɪŋ/
verb
超越航行，远航
Surpassing or exceeding in voyaging; traveling farther or more extensively than others.
He was outvoyaging all his contemporaries, exploring uncharted waters. 他超越了所有同时代的人，探索着未知的水域。
The new ship was capable of outvoyaging any other vessel in the fleet. 这艘新船能够比舰队中的任何其他船只航行得更远。
synonyms:  outsailing, outtraveling, exploring, venturing
antonyms: staying, remaining, lingering
