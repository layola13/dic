passeriform:/ˈpæsərɪfɔːrm/ | adj. n. | 雀形目的，雀形目鸟类 |Of or relating to the largest order of birds, Passeriformes, which includes perching birds and songbirds, characterized by having four toes, three pointing forward and one back, adapted for perching. |  The robin is a common passeriform bird found in gardens. 知更鸟是一种常见的在花园中发现的雀形目鸟类。|  Sparrows are passeriform birds known for their seed-eating habits. 麻雀是雀形目鸟类，以其食种子的习性而闻名。 | 近义词: perching bird, songbird | 反义词:  (无明确的反义词，可以考虑非雀形目鸟类，例如：waterfowl 水禽) | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From Latin *passer "sparrow" + -formis "having the form of".
