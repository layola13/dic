merchanthood:/ˈmɜːrtʃənthʊd/
n.
商人身份，商业，商界
the state of being a merchant; the business of buying and selling commodities for profit.
He inherited his father's business and entered into merchanthood at a young age. 他继承了父亲的生意，很年轻就进入了商界。
近义词：trade, commerce, business
反义词：unemployment
