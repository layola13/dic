empaneling, empanelling: /ɪmˈpænəlɪŋ/ | n. | 遴选陪审团；组成专门小组 | The empaneling of the jury took longer than expected.  遴选陪审团的时间比预期的要长。|  The empaneling of a grand jury is a significant step in a legal case. 组成大陪审团是法律案件中的一个重要步骤。 | 近义词：selection, formation, choosing | 反义词：dismissal, disbandment | 词性：名词 (derived from verb "empanel")

empanel, empannel: /ɪmˈpænəl/ | vt. | 遴选（陪审团）；组成（专门小组） | The judge empaneled a jury of twelve people. 法官遴选了一个由十二人组成的陪审团。 |  The mayor empaneled a committee to investigate the issue. 市长组成一个委员会来调查这个问题。 | 近义词：select, appoint, choose | 反义词：dismiss, disband | 词性：动词

词源: From Anglo-French *empaneller, from en- + pannel ‘piece of parchment, schedule, list’ (from medieval Latin panellus ‘small piece of cloth, rag, list’, diminutive of pannus ‘cloth’).
