nyctalopia:/ˌnɪktəˈloʊpiə/ | n. |夜盲症 |Inability to see clearly in dim light or at night, due to a deficiency of vitamin A. |  He suffered from nyctalopia and couldn't drive at night. 他患有夜盲症，晚上不能开车。|  night blindness |  day vision | noun

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek νυκτάλωψ (nuktálōps, “night-blind”), from νύξ (núx, “night”) + ὤψ (ṓps, “eye, face”).
