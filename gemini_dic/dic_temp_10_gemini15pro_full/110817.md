linnean:/lɪˈniːən/
adjective
林奈的；林奈学说的
Relating to or following the classification system of the Swedish botanist Carolus Linnaeus.
The linnean system of classification is still used today, although it has been modified. 林奈分类系统至今仍在使用，尽管它已被修改。
