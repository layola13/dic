hoary:/ˈhɔːri/ (US); /ˈhɔːri/ (UK)| adj.|灰白的，古老的，陈腐的|Covered with or colored like hoar; extremely old; ancient; overused and unoriginal.|例句：The hoary old man sat by the fire. 灰白头发的老人坐在火炉旁。|例句：They told a hoary old joke. 他们讲了一个老掉牙的笑话。|近义词：ancient, old, venerable, white, gray, grey|反义词：new, young, modern, fresh|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old English *hārig, from Proto-Germanic *hairaz ("gray, hoary"), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱoyros, from *ḱey- ("grayish-white, ash-colored").  Cognate with Old High German hēr ("gray"), Old Norse hárr ("gray-haired").
