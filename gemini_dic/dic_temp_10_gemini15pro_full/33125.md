christlikeness:/ˈkraɪstˌlaɪknəs/
noun
像基督一样；基督的精神
The quality of resembling Christ in character or spirit.
She exemplified christlikeness in her selfless service to others. 她对他人无私的奉献体现了基督的精神。
Near Synonyms: piety, devoutness, holiness, godliness
Antonyms: impiety, ungodliness, wickedness, worldliness
词性: 名词
