groomless:/ˈɡruːmlɪs/| adj.|没有新郎的；没有伴郎的|Without a groom; lacking a groom.  Referring to a bride who does not have a groom, or a wedding without a groom.  Can also, less commonly, refer to a man without a best man or groomsmen.|The groomless bride walked down the aisle alone.  没有新郎的新娘独自走过红毯。

The magazine article featured several groomless weddings, highlighting the growing trend of women celebrating their independence. 这篇杂志文章刊登了几场没有新郎的婚礼，突出了女性庆祝独立的日益增长的趋势。|Near Synonyms: unwed, single, solo|Antonyms: married, wedded|adjective
