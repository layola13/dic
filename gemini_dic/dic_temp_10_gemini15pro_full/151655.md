particulates:/pɑːrˈtɪkjələts/ | n. | 微粒；颗粒物 | Tiny particles of solid or liquid matter suspended in a gas or liquid. |  The air was thick with particulates from the nearby factory. 空气中充满了附近工厂排放的颗粒物。| synonyms: particles, granules, specks | antonyms: whole, complete | noun


particulates:/pɑːrˈtɪkjələts/ | adj. | 微粒的；颗粒状的 | Existing as or composed of tiny separate particles. | Particulate matter can be harmful to human health. 颗粒物可能对人体健康有害。| synonyms: granular, powdery | antonyms: solid, continuous | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "particula" (small part) + "-ate" (forming adjectives)
