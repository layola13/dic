maia:/ˈmaɪə/ | n. | Maia, 玛雅 | In Greek mythology, Maia is the eldest of the Pleiades, daughter of Atlas and Pleione, and mother of Hermes by Zeus.  She is associated with spring and nurturing.  In Romanian, "Maia" means "May". It can also be a girl's name. | Maia is a beautiful name, originating from Greek mythology. 玛雅是一个美丽的名字，源于希腊神话。
