autocratrix:/ˈɔːtəkrætrɪks/
n.
女独裁者; 女专制君主
A female autocrat; a woman who rules with absolute power.
Catherine the Great was considered by some to be an autocratrix.  凯瑟琳大帝被一些人认为是女独裁者。
The autocratrix demanded complete obedience from her subjects.  这位女独裁者要求臣民绝对服从。
近义词：dictator, tyrant, despot
反义词：democrat
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From auto- (self) + -crat (ruler) + -rix (female suffix).  源自 auto- (自己) + -crat (统治者) + -rix (女性后缀)。
