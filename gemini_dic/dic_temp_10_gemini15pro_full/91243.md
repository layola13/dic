hydrocyanide:/ˌhaɪdrəˈsaɪənaɪd/| n. |氢氰酸|A solution of hydrogen cyanide (HCN) in water.  It is a highly poisonous, weak acid.|Potassium cyanide reacts with acids to produce hydrogen cyanide. 氰化钾与酸反应生成氢氰酸。|Synonym: prussic acid|Antonym: N/A (no direct chemical antonym)|noun
