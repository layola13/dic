convenors:/kənˈviːnəz/
|n.|召集人，会议召集人|A person who organizes a meeting or conference.|The convenors of the conference did a great job organizing the event. 会议的召集人出色地组织了这次活动。|synonyms: organizers, coordinators, chairmen, chairwomen, chairpeople|antonyms: attendees, participants|noun


convenor:/kənˈviːnə(r)/
|n.|召集人，会议召集人|A person who organizes a meeting or conference.|The convenor of the meeting ensured everyone had a chance to speak. 会议召集人确保每个人都有机会发言。|synonyms: organizer, coordinator, chairman, chairwoman, chairperson|antonyms: attendee, participant|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "convene" + "-or" (agent suffix). "Convene" comes from Old French "convenir" and ultimately from Latin "convenīre" (to come together, agree).
