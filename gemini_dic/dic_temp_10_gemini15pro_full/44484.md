crossbreeding:/ˈkrɒsˌbriːdɪŋ/ (US) /ˈkrɒsˌbriːdɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|杂交育种|The act or process of breeding animals or plants of different breeds, varieties, or species to produce a hybrid.|Farmers have been crossbreeding different types of corn for centuries to improve yield.  农民们几个世纪以来一直在对不同类型的玉米进行杂交育种以提高产量。|近义词：hybridization, interbreeding|反义词：inbreeding, purebreeding|词性: noun

crossbreed: /ˈkrɒsˌbriːd/ (US) /ˈkrɒsˌbriːd/ (UK)| n. v.|杂交品种，杂交; 使杂交|n. An animal or plant produced by crossbreeding. v. To breed animals or plants of different breeds, varieties, or species.|n. The dog is a crossbreed, a mix of Labrador and German Shepherd. 这只狗是一个杂交品种，拉布拉多犬和德国牧羊犬的混种。 v.  They crossbred the two varieties of rose to create a new color. 他们将两个品种的玫瑰杂交以创造新的颜色。 |近义词：hybrid, hybridize|反义词：purebred, purebreed|词性: noun, verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From "cross-" (meaning "across" or "between") + "breeding" (the process of producing offspring).  "Cross-" ultimately comes from Old Norse "kross," related to Old English "crux" (cross). "Breeding" comes from Old English "brēdan" (to breed).
