neurography:/nʊˈrɒɡrəfi/ /nʊˈrɑːɡrəfi/
noun
神经造影术;神经描记法
The process of producing images or graphical representations of nerves, typically for diagnostic purposes.  This can involve various techniques to visualize nerve structure and function.
Neurography helped identify the location of the nerve compression.  神经造影帮助确定了神经受压的部位。
The neurography revealed no significant nerve damage.  神经描记显示没有明显的神经损伤。
Synonyms: electroneurography, nerve conduction study
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but one might consider terms related to obscuring nerve visualization, such as "neuro-obfuscation" – though this is not a standard medical term.)
词性: 名词
