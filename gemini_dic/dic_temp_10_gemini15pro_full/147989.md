overridden:/ˌoʊvərˈrɪdn/| adj.|被覆盖的，被重写的|Overridden is the past participle of the verb "override," meaning to supersede or replace a previous decision, setting, or value.|The default settings were overridden by the user's preferences. 用户的偏好覆盖了默认设置。|Synonyms: superseded, replaced, overuled|Antonyms: upheld, sustained|Verb (past participle)

overridden:/ˌoʊvərˈrɪdn/| adj.|无效的，被否决的|Overridden can also mean that something has been made invalid or ineffective by a higher authority or a more powerful force.|The judge's decision was overridden by the higher court. 法官的判决被上级法院否决了。|Synonyms: invalidated, nullified, cancelled|Antonyms: validated, ratified|Verb (past participle)
