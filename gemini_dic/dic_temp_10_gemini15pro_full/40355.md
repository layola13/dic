contracting-out:/kənˈtræktɪŋ aʊt/ | n. | 外包；承包出去 | The practice of hiring an external organization to perform some business functions. | Many companies are contracting-out their IT services to specialized firms. 许多公司将它们的IT服务外包给专业公司。| outsourcing, subcontracting | insourcing, in-house | noun


contracting-out:/kənˈtræktɪŋ aʊt/ | adj. | 外包的 | Relating to the practice of contracting out. | The contracting-out process can be complex and time-consuming. 外包过程可能复杂且耗时。| outsourced, subcontracted | insourced, in-house | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From contract (v.) + out (adv.). The verb "contract" ultimately derives from Latin "contrahere," meaning "to draw together, conclude a bargain."  "Out" indicates the transfer of work outside of the organization.
