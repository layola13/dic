over-sweetness:/ˈoʊvərˈswiːtnəs/| n.| 过甜，甜度过高|An excess of sweetness, often considered unpleasant or cloying.|The over-sweetness of the dessert made me feel sick. 这道甜点太甜了，让我感到恶心。|近义词：excessive sweetness, cloying sweetness|反义词: blandness, lack of sweetness|词性:名词

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi) meaning "above, beyond, excessively" + "sweetness" (from "sweet" + "-ness")
