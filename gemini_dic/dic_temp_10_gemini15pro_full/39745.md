consolation:/ˌkɒnsəˈleɪʃn/ (UK) /ˌkɑːnsəˈleɪʃn/ (US)| n.|安慰，慰藉；令人安慰的事物|Something that provides comfort or solace in a time of grief, disappointment, or trouble.|例句：Her children were her only consolation after her husband died.  她的孩子们是她丈夫去世后唯一的安慰。

例句：The team took consolation in the fact that they had played well despite losing.  尽管输了，球队还是从他们表现良好的事实中得到了安慰。|近义词：comfort, solace, relief, reassurance|反义词：distress, grief, sorrow|词性: 名词
