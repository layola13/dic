gadling:/ˈɡædlɪŋ/| n. | 流浪汉；游手好闲的人；闲逛者 | A person who travels around, often without a settled home or job; a vagrant. | He was a gadling, always moving from town to town. 他是个流浪汉，总是从一个镇子搬到另一个镇子。| Example: The old gadling wandered the streets, begging for food.  那个老流浪汉在街上游荡，乞讨食物。| Synonym: vagrant, wanderer, rover, drifter, nomad | Antonym: settler, resident, stay-at-home | noun
