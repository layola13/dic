arboricultural:/ˌɑːrbərɪˈkʌltʃərəl/
adjective
树木栽培的;  树木学的
Relating to arboriculture; the cultivation of trees.
例句：Arboricultural practices are crucial for maintaining healthy urban forests.  树木栽培实践对于维护健康的城市森林至关重要。

例句：He studied arboricultural techniques at the university. 他在大学学习了树木栽培技术。

近义词:dendrological,  silvicultural
反义词:N/A
词性:adjective
