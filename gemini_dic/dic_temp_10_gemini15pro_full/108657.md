leaflessness: /ˈliːfləsnəs/
noun
无叶状态; 光秃秃
the state of being without leaves
The leaflessness of the trees in winter gives the landscape a stark beauty. 冬天树木的无叶状态赋予了景色一种荒凉的美。

The leaflessness of the branches made them look like skeletal fingers reaching for the sky. 光秃秃的树枝看起来像骨骼手指伸向天空。

近义词: bareness, nudity
反义词: leafiness, foliage
词性: noun
