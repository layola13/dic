englut:/ɪnˈɡlʌt/ | v. |使充满；使过饱；充斥 | to fill or flood completely, especially with more than is needed or wanted; to supply or provide (someone or something) with much more than is needed or wanted | The market was englutted with cheap imports. 市场充斥着廉价的进口商品。|  synonyms: flood, glut, gorge, inundate, oversupply, saturate, surfeit | antonyms: deprive, drain, empty, starve | verb

englut:/ɪnˈɡlʌt/ | v. |使过量；使过度 | to fill to excess; to cloy | He englutted himself with rich food. 他吃了太多油腻的食物。 | synonyms: gorge, satiate, stuff, surfeit | antonyms: abstain, fast, starve | verb
