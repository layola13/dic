defendable:/dɪˈfɛndəbl/
adjective
可辩护的，可防御的
Capable of being defended, justified, or protected.
His actions were morally defendable. 他的行为在道德上是站得住脚的。

defendable:/dɪˈfɛndəbl/
adjective
可防御的
Capable of being protected from attack.
The city's walls made it defendable against invaders.  这座城市的城墙使其能够抵御入侵者。


近义词:justifiable, supportable, maintainable, protectable
反义词:indefensible, unjustifiable, vulnerable

词性:adjective
