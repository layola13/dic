brazier:/ˈbreɪʒər/| n. |火盆，炭盆|A metal container that holds burning coals or charcoal, used for cooking or heating.|例句：The brazier glowed warmly in the corner of the room. 房间角落里的火盆发出温暖的光芒。

例句：We cooked our dinner over a makeshift brazier. 我们用一个临时做的火盆煮晚餐。|近义词：fire pit, hearth, barbeque|反义词:|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *brasier, from braise ("burning embers"), from Frankish *brasa, from Proto-Germanic *brasǭ ("firebrand"). Cognate with Old English brǣse ("fire"), Middle Dutch brase ("ember"), Old High German brasa ("firebrand"). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰregʰ- ("to shine, gleam, burn").
