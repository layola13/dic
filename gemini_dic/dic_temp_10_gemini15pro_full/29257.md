cassino:/kəˈsiːnoʊ/ /kæˈsiːnoʊ/
noun
赌场；纸牌游戏
A place where gambling games are played.  A card game in which players try to take cards by matching the rank of a card played with one on the table or by combining cards that total that rank.
例句：They spent the weekend at a casino in Las Vegas. 他们在拉斯维加斯的赌场度过了周末。

例句：We played a few rounds of cassino after dinner. 晚饭后我们玩了几局赌场纸牌游戏。

近义词：gambling house, gaming house (for the place);  rummy (for the card game)
反义词：None
词性：n.
