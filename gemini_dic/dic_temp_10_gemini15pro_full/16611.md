barometz:/ˈbærəmɛts/ | n. | 植物羊，蕨类植物 | A mythical plant believed to grow sheep as fruit. Also called the Scythian lamb. |  Legend has it that the barometz produced sheep as its fruit.  传说植物羊会结出羊作为果实。| Synonym: vegetable lamb, Scythian lamb | Antonym: N/A | noun

barometz:/ˈbærəmɛts/ | n. | 羊齿植物（尤指金毛狗蕨） | A type of fern, specifically *Cibotium barometz*, also known as the woolly fern or golden chicken fern. | The barometz fern is known for its woolly rhizome.  金毛狗蕨以其毛茸茸的根茎而闻名。| Synonym: woolly fern, golden chicken fern, Cibotium barometz | Antonym: N/A | noun
