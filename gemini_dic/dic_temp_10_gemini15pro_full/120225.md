minesweeping:/ˈmaɪnˌswiːpɪŋ/
n.|扫雷，排雷|The act of clearing an area of mines, especially naval mines.|例句：Minesweeping operations are crucial for ensuring safe navigation.  扫雷行动对于确保航行安全至关重要。

例句：The navy conducted minesweeping exercises in the region. 海军在该区域进行了扫雷演习。
|近义词：mine clearance, demining|反义词:mining (laying mines)|词性:名词
