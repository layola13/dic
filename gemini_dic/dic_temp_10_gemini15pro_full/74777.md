freezing:/ˈfriːzɪŋ/ /ˈfriːzɪŋ/| adj.| 极冷的；冰冻的| Extremely cold.|  It's freezing outside! 外面冷极了！|  The lake was freezing over. 湖水正在结冰。|近义词：icy, frigid, chilly, cold|反义词：boiling, hot, warm|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Old English *frēosan "to freeze," from Proto-Germanic *freusan, from Proto-Indo-European *preus- "to freeze."  The present participle "freezing" became used adjectivally.
