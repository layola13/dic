oncost:/ˈɒnkɒst/ (UK), /ˈɑːnkɔːst/ (US)| n.|间接费用，附加费用|An oncost is an expense related to producing a product or service, but not directly attributable to a particular project or item. It represents the general overhead expenses of running a business. |The oncost for manufacturing the widgets includes factory rent and utilities.  制造这些小部件的间接费用包括工厂租金和水电费。|synonyms: overhead, indirect cost, burden, expense|antonyms: direct cost, revenue, profit|noun
