frizz:/frɪz/| n. v.|鬈曲，卷发；使卷曲|A mass of tight curls; the state of hair being very curly. (n.) To form tight curls; to make hair curly. (v.)|Her hair has a natural frizz. 她的头发天生卷曲。|近义词：curl, kink, fuzz|反义词:straighten, smooth|noun, verb

frizzy:/ˈfrɪzi/| adj.|卷曲的，毛躁的|Having a mass of tight curls; being very curly.|Her hair gets frizzy in humid weather. 她的头发在潮湿的天气里会变得毛躁。|近义词：curly, kinky, fuzzy|反义词:straight, smooth|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From the French friser, meaning "to curl or crimp."  Ultimately from Frankish *frisan.
