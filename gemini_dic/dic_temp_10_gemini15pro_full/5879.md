ambassadrix: /æmˈbæsədrɪks/
noun
女大使；女性大使
A female ambassador.  This term is used to explicitly denote a female ambassador, often in contexts emphasizing gender inclusivity.
The ambassadrix presented her credentials to the president. 女大使向总统递交了国书。
The new ambassadrix is known for her work in human rights.  新任女大使以其在人权领域的工作而闻名。
近义词: ambassador, envoy
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly consider rival diplomat)
词性: noun
