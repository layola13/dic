chalon:/ʃæˈlɒn/ | n. | 平底驳船，大型平底船 | A large, flat-bottomed boat used for carrying goods, especially on rivers and canals. | The chalon was laden with cargo and slowly made its way down the river. 平底驳船装满了货物，沿着河慢慢地往下游走。| The chalon was used to transport goods across the canal. 这艘平底驳船被用来运送货物穿过运河。| 近义词：barge, lighter, scow | 反义词: None | 词性: 名词

词源: From French *chaland*, from Middle Dutch *schale*, meaning "shell, husk".  This likely refers to the shallow, shell-like hull of the boat.
