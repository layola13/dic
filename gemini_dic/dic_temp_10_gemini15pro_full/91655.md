adjective
过度肉欲地；极端肉体地

His behavior was described as hypercarnal, focused solely on base instincts. 他的行为被描述为过度肉欲的，只专注于基本的本能。
The novel explored the hypercarnal desires of the protagonist. 这部小说探讨了主人公极端的肉体欲望。
近义词:licentious, lascivious, lewd, debauched
反义词:chaste, pure, ascetic, abstinent
词性:adjective

词源:

"Carnal" (Latin: "carnalis"): relating to the physical body; especially sexual desires
