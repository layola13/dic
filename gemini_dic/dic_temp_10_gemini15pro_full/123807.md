moplah: /ˈmoʊplɑː/ | n. | 马拉巴尔穆斯林 (历史上对印度喀拉拉邦穆斯林的称呼，现被认为带有贬义) | A term historically used to refer to Muslims from the Malabar region of Kerala, India, now often considered offensive. |  The British colonial administration often referred to the Mappila community as Moplahs. 英国殖民政府经常将马皮拉社区称为莫普拉人。
