cisser:/ˈsɪzər/| n.|剪刀；剪切者；修剪者|A tool used for cutting cloth, paper, and other materials, consisting of two blades laid one on top of the other and fastened in the middle so that the sharp edges can be opened and closed.|She used a pair of scissors to cut the fabric. 她用剪刀剪布料。|近义词：shears, clippers, snips|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与其功能相反的工具，例如：glue 胶水) |词性:noun
