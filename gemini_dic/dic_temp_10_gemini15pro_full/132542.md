nondevotionally: /ˌnɒndɪˈvəʊʃənəli/ /ˌnɑːndɪˈvoʊʃənəli/
adverb
不虔诚地，不专注地
without devotion or piety; in a way that lacks dedication or commitment.
He performed his duties nondevotionally, his mind clearly elsewhere.  他心不在焉地履行职责，心思显然在别处。
The artist worked nondevotionally, merely going through the motions.  这位艺术家不专心地工作，只是敷衍了事。
近义词: irreverently, perfunctorily, halfheartedly, carelessly
反义词: devoutly, piously, dedicatedly, diligently
词性: adverb
词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + devotion + -ally (adverbial suffix)
