fluxional:/ˈflʌkʃənəl/ |adj.|流动的，易变的，不稳定的|Subject to constant change or fluctuation; likely to vary or change.|The political situation in the country is highly fluxional.  这个国家的政治局势高度不稳定。|Synonyms: changeable, volatile, fluid, unstable, dynamic|Antonyms: stable, fixed, constant, static|adjective

fluxional:/ˈflʌkʃənəl/ |n.|流数术|A method of calculating, developed by Isaac Newton, that deals with continuously changing quantities.|Newton's method of fluxions was a precursor to calculus. 牛顿的流数术是微积分的先驱。|Synonyms: calculus (in a historical context)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
