nicknaming:/ˈnɪkˌneɪmɪŋ/
n.|取绰号，起昵称|The act of giving someone a nickname. |He's good at nicknaming people. 他很擅长给人起绰号。|近义词:  name-calling, labeling|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider: formal naming, official designation)|词性: 名词 (gerund)


nicknaming:/ˈnɪkˌneɪmɪŋ/
v.|给...起绰号|The act of giving someone a nickname. |They started nicknaming him "Speedy" after he won the race.  他赢得比赛后，他们开始叫他“飞毛腿”。|近义词: dubbing, labeling|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性: 动词 (present participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From nickname + -ing.  Nickname itself comes from Middle English "eke-name," meaning "additional name," with "eke" meaning "also" or "in addition."
