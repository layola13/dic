loud-hailer:/ˌlaʊdˈheɪlər/ | n. |扩音器；喇叭|A device that amplifies the voice so that it can be heard over a wide area. |The police used a loud-hailer to address the crowd. 警察用扩音器向人群喊话。

|近义词：megaphone, bullhorn, amplifier, speaker|反义词: microphone (opposite in function) |词性: noun


loud-hailer:/ˌlaʊdˈheɪlər/ | n. |扬声器；喇叭|An electronic device that converts electrical signals into sound, typically used for public address or music reproduction. |The band's music was blasted through the loud-hailer. 乐队的音乐通过扬声器播放出来。
