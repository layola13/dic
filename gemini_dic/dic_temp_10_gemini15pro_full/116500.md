The word "materious" is not a standard English word.  It's likely a misspelling of "material" or perhaps intended to be "materialistic."  Therefore, I will provide analyses for both "material" and "materialistic."

**Material:**

/məˈtɪəriəl/ | adj. n. | 材料；物质的；重要的 |  Relating to physical matter; relevant; important. |  例句：The material for the dress is silk. 这件衣服的材料是丝绸。|  近义词：fabric, substance, important, relevant | 反义词：immaterial, irrelevant, spiritual | 词性: adjective, noun

/məˈtɪəriəl/ | n. | 材料；素材 | The matter from which a thing is or can be made. | 例句：We need more raw materials to finish the project. 我们需要更多原材料来完成这个项目。 | 近义词：ingredients, components, supplies | 反义词：product, result | 词性: noun


**Materialistic:**

/məˌtɪəriəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 物质主义的 | Excessively concerned with material possessions; money-oriented. | 例句：She's become very materialistic since she got her promotion. 自从升职后，她变得非常物质主义。 | 近义词：greedy, acquisitive, consumerist | 反义词：spiritual, altruistic, selfless | 词性: adjective
