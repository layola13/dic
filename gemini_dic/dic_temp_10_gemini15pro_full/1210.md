accommodates: /əˈkɒmədeɪts/ (UK) /əˈkɑːmədeɪts/ (US)
verb
容纳；提供住宿；使适应；顾及
(1) provide lodging or sufficient space for.  
(2) fit in with the wishes or needs of.

例句：
The hotel accommodates up to 500 guests. = 这家酒店最多可容纳500位客人。

The new design accommodates the needs of disabled users. = 新的设计考虑到了残疾用户的需求。

近义词：house, lodge, hold, fit, suit, adapt
反义词：exclude, reject
词性：verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin accommodatus, past participle of accommodare (“to fit, adapt”), from Latin ad (“to, towards”) + commodare (“to make fit”), from commodus (“suitable, convenient”).

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hall accommodates 500 people. = 这个大厅能容纳500人。
This hotel accommodates pets. = 这家酒店允许携带宠物。

一般过去时:
The hotel accommodated all the conference attendees. = 这家酒店容纳了所有与会者。
The schedule accommodated her request for time off. =  时间表考虑到了她请假的要求。

现在完成时:
We have accommodated their needs as best we can. = 我们已经尽力满足他们的需求了。
The company has accommodated the changing market. = 公司已经适应了不断变化的市场。

过去完成时:
They had accommodated the extra guests in the annex. = 他们在附属建筑中安置了额外的客人。
The company had accommodated his disability by providing special equipment. = 公司通过提供特殊设备来适应他的残疾。

将来时:
The new stadium will accommodate thousands of spectators. = 新体育场将容纳数千名观众。
We will accommodate your request if possible. = 如果可能，我们将满足您的要求。


被动语态:
All the guests were accommodated in the hotel. = 所有客人都被安排住进了酒店。
His needs were accommodated by the company. = 公司满足了他的需求。


疑问句:
Can this room accommodate another bed? = 这个房间还能放一张床吗？
Does the price accommodate breakfast? =  这个价格包含早餐吗？

否定句:
This small car cannot accommodate five people. = 这辆小车容纳不了五个人。
The current system doesn't accommodate the new regulations. =  当前系统不符合新规定。

PHRASE->
