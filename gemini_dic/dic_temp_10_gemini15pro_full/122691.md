moev:/muːv/ | v. | 移动，感动，提议，促使，前进，进行，行动，搬家 | To change the position of something, to cause someone to feel a particular emotion, to propose something formally, to encourage or cause something to happen, to make progress, to proceed, to take action, to change residence. |  例句：Could you please move your car? 你能把你的车挪一下吗？

例句：The movie moved me to tears. 这部电影感动得我热泪盈眶。

近义词：shift, transfer, relocate, stir, touch, propose, suggest, prompt, propel, advance, progress, proceed, act, operate

反义词：stay, remain, stop, halt, freeze

词性：动词


词源：From Middle English *moven, from Old French mover, from Latin movēre (“to move”), from Proto-Indo-European *mew- (“to push away”).
