infinitesimal: /ˌɪnfɪnɪˈtɛsɪməl/ | adj. n. | 极小的，无限小的；无量小；无穷小 |  Extremely small; too small to be measured.  A quantity that approaches zero as a limit. |  The effect of the change was infinitesimal. 改变的效果微乎其微。 | synonym: tiny, minute, microscopic, negligible | antonym: huge, enormous, vast, immense | adjective

infinitesimal: /ˌɪnfɪnɪˈtɛsɪməl/ | n. | 无穷小；极小量 | An infinitely small quantity. |  Calculus deals with infinitesimals. 微积分处理无穷小量。 | synonym: iota, jot, smidgen, whit | antonym: infinity, vastness | noun
