pasturages:/ˈpɑːstʃərɪdʒɪz/ /ˈpæstʃərɪdʒɪz/
n.
牧场；草地；牧草
Land covered with grass or other plants suitable for grazing animals.
The sheep were grazing on the lush pasturages. 羊群在茂盛的牧场上吃草。
pastures, fields, grasslands
barren lands, deserts
noun
