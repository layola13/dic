dubitancy:/ˈduːbɪtənsi/| n. |犹豫，迟疑不决|The state of being uncertain or hesitant.|His dubitancy about accepting the job offer was evident. 他对于接受这份工作邀请的犹豫显而易见。|近义词：hesitancy, indecision, uncertainty|反义词：certainty, decisiveness, resoluteness|词性:noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *dubitantia, from Latin dubitans, present participle of dubitare "to doubt," from dubius "doubtful."
