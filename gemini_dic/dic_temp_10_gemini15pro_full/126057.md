mustard: /ˈmʌstərd/ (US), /ˈmʌstəd/ (UK)| n. adj.|芥末，芥末黄|A pungent-tasting yellow or brown paste made from the crushed seeds of certain mustard plants, typically eaten with meat or used as a cooking ingredient.  Also, a dark yellow color resembling this paste.|I like to put mustard on my hot dogs. 我喜欢在我的热狗上放芥末。|近义词: condiment, relish |反义词:  (No direct antonym for the condiment. For the color, perhaps "pale") |词性: noun, adjective
