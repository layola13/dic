cockpits:/ˈkɒkpɪts/ (UK) /ˈkɑːkpɪts/ (US)| n. |驾驶舱，座舱；战场|An enclosed area in an aircraft or spacecraft where the pilot sits and controls the vehicle. Also, a place where a lot of activity or fighting happens. |The pilots checked the instruments in their cockpits before takeoff.  飞行员在起飞前检查了驾驶舱内的仪器。| synonyms: flight deck, cabin, control center, arena, battleground | antonyms: passenger cabin, grandstand | noun

cockpit:/ˈkɒkpɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːkpɪts/ (US)| n. |斗鸡场|A pit or enclosed area for cockfighting.|(Archaic usage) The crowd roared in the cockpit as the roosters fought. (古语) 当公鸡搏斗时，斗鸡场里的人群发出怒吼。| synonyms: arena, ring | antonyms: peace sanctuary | noun
