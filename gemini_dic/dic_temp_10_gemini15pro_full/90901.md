hunting:/ˈhʌntɪŋ/| n. & adj.|狩猎，打猎; 寻找|The act of chasing and killing wild animals for food or sport; the act of searching for something.|例句：He enjoys hunting deer in the fall. 他喜欢在秋天猎鹿。|近义词：shooting, poaching, stalking, pursuing, seeking, searching|反义词:preserving, conserving|noun

hunting:/ˈhʌntɪŋ/| adj.|狩猎的; 找寻的|Used for hunting; relating to hunting.|例句：He wore his hunting boots. 他穿着他的猎靴。|近义词：predatory|反义词:N/A|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *huntian, from hunta (“hunter”), of Germanic origin. Cognate with Dutch hunten (“to hunt”), German hunten (“to hunt”).
