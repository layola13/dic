collectivizes:/kəˈlɛktɪvaɪzɪz/
verb
集体化
To organize (something) on the basis of collectivism.  To bring under collective ownership or control, especially by the state.
The government collectivized agriculture, taking land from private owners and creating collective farms. 政府将农业集体化，从私人业主手中夺取土地，建立集体农庄。
The Soviet Union collectivized farms in the 1930s.  苏联在20世纪30年代将农场集体化。
Synonyms: nationalize, communalize, socialize
Antonyms: privatize, individualize
词性: 动词
