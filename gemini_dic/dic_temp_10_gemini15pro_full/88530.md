hit-list:/ˈhɪt lɪst/ | n. |暗杀名单；打击目标名单 | A list of people or things intended to be dealt with in a particular way, often violently or destructively. | The dictator had a hit-list of political opponents.  独裁者有一份政治对手的暗杀名单。

The company's hit-list included several underperforming branches. 该公司的打击目标名单包括几个表现不佳的分支机构。| synonyms: blacklist, target list, death list | antonyms: whitelist, safe list | noun


hit-lists:/ˈhɪt lɪsts/ | n. |暗杀名单（复数）；打击目标名单（复数）| Plural form of "hit-list," referring to multiple lists of people or things targeted for elimination or other negative actions. |  The spy agency maintained several hit-lists for different operations.  间谍机构为不同的行动维护着几个暗杀名单。

Rival gangs exchanged hit-lists, escalating the violence. 敌对帮派交换了打击目标名单，加剧了暴力事件。 | synonyms: blacklists, target lists, death lists | antonyms: whitelists, safe lists | noun
