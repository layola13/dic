bunk:/bʌŋk/| n. vi.|床铺；铺位；废话；逃课；<口>住处|A narrow bed, typically one built into a wall, as on a ship or train; nonsense; to stay away from school or work without permission; a place to sleep.|例句：The children slept in bunks. 孩子们睡在双层床上。

He bunked off school to go to the football match. 他逃学去看足球比赛了。|近义词：bed, berth, cot, nonsense, rubbish, bunk off (v.) - skip, play truant|反义词:sense, truth|词性:noun, verb

bunk:/bʌŋk/| vi.|逃课；睡在铺位上|To stay away from school or work without permission; to sleep in a bunk.|例句：He bunked off work to go to the beach. 他逃班去海滩了。

We bunked down for the night in a cheap hotel. 我们在一个便宜的旅馆过夜。|近义词：skip, play truant, dodge, sleep|反义词:attend|词性:verb
