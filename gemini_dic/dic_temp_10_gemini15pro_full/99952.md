interobserver:/ˌɪntərəbˈzɜːrvər/| adj.|观察者间的，不同观察者之间的|Relating to or involving different observers.  It describes the variation or agreement between the results obtained by different people observing the same phenomenon.|The study examined interobserver reliability in diagnosing the disease.  这项研究检验了诊断这种疾病时观察者间的一致性。|近义词：interrater|反义词：(No direct antonym, but consider "intraobserver")|词性:形容词

interobserver reliability:/ˌɪntərəbˈzɜːrvər rɪˌlaɪəˈbɪləti/| n.|观察者间信度|The degree of agreement among raters. It gives a score of how much homogeneity, or consensus, there is in the ratings given by judges.|High interobserver reliability is crucial in scientific studies.  在科学研究中，观察者间信度高至关重要。|近义词：interrater reliability|反义词：(No direct antonym)|词性:名词
