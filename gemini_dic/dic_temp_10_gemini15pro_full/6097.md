americanizing:/əˈmɛrɪkəˌnaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使美国化
The process of making something or someone more American in character or style.
Many immigrants are gradually americanizing their lifestyles. 许多移民正在逐渐使他们的生活方式美国化。

The company is americanizing its products to appeal to the US market.  这家公司正在将其产品美国化，以吸引美国市场。

Synonym: westernizing, assimilating
Antonym:  localizing, indigenizing
词性: 动词 (verb)
