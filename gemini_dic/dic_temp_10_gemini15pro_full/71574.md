flashinesses:/ˈflæʃɪnɪsɪz/
noun
炫耀；浮华；俗丽
the quality of being too brightly coloured or decorated, or too obviously designed to attract attention
例句：The flashinesses of the Las Vegas strip can be overwhelming. 拉斯维加斯大道的浮华可能会让人不知所措。

例句：He toned down the flashinesses of his outfit for the funeral. 他为了参加葬礼而淡化了服装的浮华。

近义词：ostentation, showiness, gaudiness, flamboyance
反义词：simplicity, modesty, understatement, subtly
词性：n. (plural noun)
