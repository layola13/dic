needlelike:/ˈniːdlˌlaɪk/| adj.|针状的，像针一样的|Resembling a needle in shape; slender and pointed.|The plant had needlelike leaves.  这种植物长着针状的叶子。|Synonyms: acicular, acerose, needle-shaped, spiky, pointy|Antonyms: blunt, rounded|adjective
