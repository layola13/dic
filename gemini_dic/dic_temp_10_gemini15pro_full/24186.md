bricole:/brɪˈkəʊl/ (US) /brɪˈkoʊl/| n.|砖块；木板；(台球)三颗星|A heavy wooden block, especially one used as a counterpoise in a drawbridge or portcullis.  A shot in billiards in which the cue ball strikes two cushions before contacting the object ball. |The bricole swung upwards as the portcullis was raised. 吊桥升起时，砖块向上摆动。|synonyms: block, timber, counterweight|antonyms: (For the counterweight sense) support, anchor|noun

bricole:/brɪˈkəʊl/ (US) /brɪˈkoʊl/| n.|（旧时）投石器|A military engine for casting stones.|The army used a bricole to hurl rocks at the castle walls.  军队用投石器向城墙投掷石块。|synonyms: catapult, trebuchet, sling|antonyms: N/A|noun
