cointense:/ˌkoʊɪnˈtɛns/
adjective
共同强烈的，共同紧张的
Existing or occurring together with the same degree of intensity; jointly intense.  This term is often used in specialized fields like mathematics and physics to describe relationships between variables or phenomena.
The cointense pressure and heat caused the material to melt.  共同强烈的压力和热量导致材料融化。
The researchers observed a cointense relationship between the two variables. 研究人员观察到两个变量之间存在共同强烈的关系。
近义词:concurrent, simultaneous, coincident, concomitant
反义词:separate, independent, divergent
词性:adjective
词源: "co-" (together, jointly) + "intense" (of extreme force, degree, or strength)
