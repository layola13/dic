overcarry:/ˌoʊvərˈkæri/
verb
过度携带；过分用力；（体育运动）越位
to carry something too far; to exaggerate or overdo something; (in sports) to cross a boundary line while in possession of the ball or puck
The quarterback overcarried the ball and fumbled. 四分卫带球过猛，结果弄丢了球。
The comedian overcarried his jokes and the audience didn't laugh.  这位喜剧演员把笑话讲得太过火了，观众都没有笑。
The hockey player overcarried the puck across the blue line.  这名曲棍球运动员带球越过了蓝线。
近义词: exaggerate, overdo, overstep
反义词: understate, underplay
词性: verb
