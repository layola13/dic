hoppo:/ˈhɒpoʊ/| n.|海关官员，(旧时中国)海关监督|A hoppo was a high-ranking customs official in Imperial China, responsible for collecting duties and taxes on foreign trade. |The hoppo controlled all trade entering and leaving the port.  海关监督控制着所有进出港口的贸易。| The emperor appointed a new hoppo to Canton. 皇帝任命了一位新的广州海关监督。|Synonyms: customs official, superintendent of customs|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
