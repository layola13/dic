alloy:/ˈælɔɪ/ (US), /ˈælɔɪ/ (UK)| n. v.|合金；混合物；(金属的)成色；降低…的纯度|A metal made by combining two or more metallic elements, especially to give greater strength or resistance to corrosion. To mix (a metal) with another metal or substance.|例句：Steel is an alloy of iron and carbon. 钢是铁和碳的合金。|近义词：mixture, blend, compound, composite|反义词：pure substance, element|n.

alloy:/əˈlɔɪ/ (US), /əˈlɔɪ/ (UK)| v. |掺杂，融合；降低…的纯度（或质量）|To debase by mixing with something inferior.|例句：Copper is often alloyed with tin to make bronze. 铜经常与锡混合制成青铜。|近义词：mix, blend, combine, adulterate|反义词：purify, refine|v.
