negator:/ˈnɪɡeɪtər/| n.|否定者；反驳者；否定词|A person or thing that negates something.  Something that denies or refutes something else.  In logic, a word or symbol that expresses negation.|例句：The lawyer acted as a negator, refuting all the claims made by the prosecution. 律师扮演了反驳者的角色，驳斥了控方提出的所有主张。

例句： "Not" is a common negator in English. “Not”在英语中是一个常见的否定词。|近义词：denier, contradictor, opponent, refuter|反义词：affirmer, supporter, proponent|词性: noun
