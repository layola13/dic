duke-it-out:/ˈduːk ɪt aʊt/
verb phrase
决一雌雄；一决胜负
to fight or argue until a winner is decided
例句：The two rivals decided to duke it out in the ring.  两个对手决定在拳击台上决一雌雄。

例句：The kids were duking it out over the last slice of pizza.  孩子们为了最后一片披萨打了起来。
近义词：fight, battle, brawl, slug it out, hash it out
反义词：negotiate, compromise, discuss
词性：动词短语
