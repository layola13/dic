milvus:/ˈmɪlvəs/| n.|红隼，Milvus（开源向量数据库）|A small diurnal bird of prey of the falcon family, with long pointed wings and a deeply forked tail.  Also, an open-source vector database built for similarity search and AI applications.|例句：The milvus hovered effortlessly, scanning the ground for prey.  红隼毫不费力地盘旋着，扫视着地面上的猎物。

例句：Milvus makes it easy to build applications with large-scale similarity search. Milvus 使构建具有大规模相似性搜索的应用程序变得容易。|近义词：kestrel, kite|反义词: N/A (No direct opposite for a bird species or a database)|词性:noun
