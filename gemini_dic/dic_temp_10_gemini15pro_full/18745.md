bergys:/ˈbɜːrɡiz/ | n. | berries (plural) |  This is the plural form of "bergy", a less common variant of "berg" meaning a large mass of floating ice, typically one broken off from a glacier or ice sheet.  It can also refer to small pieces of ice broken off from a larger berg.  When pluralized as "bergys," it implies multiple small pieces of ice. | The ship navigated through the dangerous bergys. 船在危险的碎冰中航行。 | The bergys clinked against the hull of the boat. 碎冰块撞击着船体。| 近义词: brash ice, growlers | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
