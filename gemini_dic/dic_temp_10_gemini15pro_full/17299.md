bcc'ed:/ˌbiː siː ˈsiːd/ | v. | 密送；暗送 |  Past participle of "bcc," meaning to include someone in an email using the blind carbon copy field.  This hides their address from other recipients. | I was bcc'ed on the email about the project. 我被密送了关于这个项目的邮件。 | I bcc'ed my boss on the email to keep him informed. 我把邮件密送给了我的老板，让他随时了解情况。 | 近义词: blind copied | 反义词: cc'ed (carbon copied) | 词性: 动词

bcc:/ˌbiː siː ˈsiː/ | v. | 密送；暗送 | To send an email to someone using the blind carbon copy field, hiding their address from other recipients. | I will bcc you on the email. 我会把邮件密送给你。 |  Please bcc me when you send the invitation. 你发送邀请函时请密送我一份。 | 近义词: blind copy | 反义词: cc (carbon copy) | 词性: 动词
