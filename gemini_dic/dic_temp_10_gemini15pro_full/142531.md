ossifrage: /ˈɒsɪfrɪdʒ/ (UK), /ˈɑːsɪfrɪdʒ/ (US) | n. | 骨雕, 胡秃鹫 | A large bird of prey, formerly believed to break open bones by dropping them from a height.  It is now usually identified with the lammergeyer or bearded vulture. |  In ancient legends, the ossifrage was said to carry bones to great heights before dropping them to shatter on the rocks below.  在古代传说中，据说骨雕会把骨头带到很高的地方，然后再把它们扔下去，让它们在下面的岩石上摔碎。|  例句：The ossifrage, a powerful bird of prey, is often depicted in medieval bestiaries. 骨雕，一种强大的猛禽，经常出现在中世纪的动物寓言集中。

例句：Modern ornithologists identify the ossifrage with the lammergeyer. 现代鸟类学家将骨雕与胡秃鹫视为同一种鸟。
