curdler:/ˈkɜːrdlər/| n.|凝乳剂，凝固剂|Something that causes milk or other liquids to coagulate and form curds.|Rennet is a common curdler used in cheesemaking.  凝乳酶是奶酪制作中常用的凝乳剂。|The acid in the lemon juice acted as a curdler for the milk. 柠檬汁中的酸充当了牛奶的凝固剂。|synonyms: coagulant, thickener|antonyms: thinner, diluent|noun
