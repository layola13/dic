assumptive:/əˈsʌmptɪv/| adj.| 假定的，设想的，自以为是的|Making an assumption about something without proof; characterized by presumption; arrogant.|He had an assumptive air about him, as if he already knew all the answers. 他一副自以为是的姿态，好像他已经知道所有答案了。|近义词：presumptuous, arrogant, conceited, self-assured, presumptive|反义词：humble, modest, unassuming, diffident|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Late Latin *assumptivus*, from Latin assumptus, past participle of assumere "to take up, adopt," from ad- "to" + sumere "to take."
