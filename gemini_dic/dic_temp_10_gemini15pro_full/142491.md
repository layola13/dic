osmious:/ˈɒzmiəs/ /ˈɑːzmiəs/| adj.|渗透的；有渗透性的|Permeable to water or other liquids, especially by osmosis.| Osmosis is the movement of water molecules across a semipermeable membrane from a region of higher water concentration to a region of lower water concentration.|The plant's roots are osmious, allowing water to be absorbed from the soil. 植物的根具有渗透性，可以从土壤中吸收水分。

The osmious membrane allowed the passage of small molecules.  渗透膜允许小分子通过。

|近义词：permeable, porous|反义词：impermeable|词性: adjective
