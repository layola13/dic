nonuniformity:/ˌnɒnˌjuːnɪˈfɔːmɪti/ | n. |不均匀性，不一致性 |lack of uniformity; the state of being nonuniform; uneven distribution or variation |The nonuniformity of the surface caused problems with the paint application. 表面的不均匀性导致油漆施工出现问题。|近义词：irregularity, unevenness, inconsistency, disparity, variation, heterogeneity |反义词：uniformity, consistency, homogeneity, evenness| noun

non-uniform:/ˌnɒnˈjuːnɪfɔːm/ | adj. |不均匀的，不一致的 |not uniform; varying or differing, especially in size, degree, or quality |The non-uniform distribution of resources led to economic disparity. 资源分配不均导致了经济差距。|近义词：irregular, uneven, inconsistent, disparate, varying, heterogeneous |反义词：uniform, consistent, homogeneous, even | adjective
