geyser:/ˈɡaɪzər/ (US), /ˈɡiːzə(r)/ (UK)| n.|间歇泉；热水器|A spring that throws forth intermittent jets of heated water and steam.|例句：The geyser erupted, shooting a plume of hot water and steam high into the air. 间歇泉喷发了，一股热水和蒸汽喷向高空。

例句：We were amazed by the power of the geyser. 我们对间歇泉的力量感到惊叹。|近义词：hot spring, thermal spring|反义词:|noun
