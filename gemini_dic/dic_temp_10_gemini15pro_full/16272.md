baptise:/bæpˈtaɪz/ (UK) /ˈbæpˌtaɪz/ (US)| vt. |施洗；(尤指)给…施洗礼|To administer the Christian rite of baptism to.|The priest baptised the baby. 牧师给婴儿施洗。|Synonyms: christen|Antonyms: None|verb
