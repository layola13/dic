outcharge:/ˌaʊtˈtʃɑːrdʒ/
verb
过高收费，收费超过
To charge (someone) more than is reasonable or usual.
The hotel outcharged us for the room service.  这家酒店的客房服务收费过高。
The taxi driver tried to outcharge the tourists. 出租车司机试图向游客多收钱。
近义词: overcharge, gouge
反义词: undercharge, discount
词性: verb
