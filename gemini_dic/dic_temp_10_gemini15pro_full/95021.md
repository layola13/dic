inabusively:/ˌɪnəˈbjuːsɪvli/
| adv. |无侵犯地；不唐突地|In a way that does not intrude or cause offense; discreetly and respectfully.| He inabusively inquired about her health. 他小心翼翼地询问了她的健康状况。|例句：She inabusively placed the gift on the table. 她轻轻地把礼物放在桌子上。|近义词：discreetly, respectfully, unobtrusively, subtly, delicately|反义词：intrusively, offensively, rudely, abruptly, aggressively|词性:副词
词源:  From "in-" (not) + "abusive" (using harsh and insulting language).  "Abusive" comes from Latin "abusus," past participle of "abuti" (to misuse), from "ab-" (away) + "uti" (to use).
