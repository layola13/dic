inlays:/ˈɪnleɪz/ (US), /ˈɪnleɪz/ (UK)| n. |镶嵌物；镶嵌装饰；镶嵌细工|A decorative material (like wood, metal, or ivory) set into a surface of another material.|The antique table featured intricate inlays of mother-of-pearl. 这张古董桌子有复杂的珍珠母镶嵌装饰。|近义词：insetting, insertion, mosaic|反义词: - |noun

inlays:/ˈɪnleɪz/ (US), /ˈɪnleɪz/ (UK)| v. |镶嵌；嵌入|To set (something) into a surface as a decoration.|He inlaid the wooden box with silver wire. 他用银丝镶嵌木盒。|近义词：embed, insert, set in|反义词: extract, remove|verb
