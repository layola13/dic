extrajudicially: /ˌɛkstrədʒuˈdɪʃəli/
adverb
在法庭外；非经司法途径
outside of court; without legal proceedings
The matter was settled extrajudicially. 这件事在庭外和解了。
The dispute was resolved extrajudicially through mediation.  这起纠纷通过调解在庭外解决了。
Synonyms: informally, privately, out of court
Antonyms: judicially, legally, in court
词性: 副词
