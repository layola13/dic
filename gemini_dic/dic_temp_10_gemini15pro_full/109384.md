lenticel:/ˈlɛntɪsɛl/ | n. | 皮孔 | A small raised pore in the stem of a woody plant that allows gases to pass between the atmosphere and the internal tissues. |  The lenticels on the cherry tree branches are very prominent.  樱桃树枝上的皮孔非常明显。|  pore, stoma | N/A | noun

lenticel:/lɛnˈtɪsl/ | n. | 透镜体 | A lens-shaped or lentil-shaped body.  |  The geologist identified a lenticel of volcanic rock within the sedimentary layer. 地质学家在沉积层中发现了一个透镜状的火山岩体。 | lens-shaped body | N/A | noun

From Latin *lenticella*, diminutive of *lens*, *lent-* "lentil".  源自拉丁语 *lenticella*，是 *lens*, *lent-* "扁豆" 的缩小词。
