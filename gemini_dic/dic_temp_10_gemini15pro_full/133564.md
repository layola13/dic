nonfixation:/ˌnɒn.fɪkˈseɪ.ʃən/ | n. | 非固定; 不固定化 |The nonfixation of nitrogen in the soil led to poor crop yields. 土壤中氮的非固定化导致作物产量低。|  lack of fixation, absence of fixation | fixation | noun
Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "fixation" (from Latin "fixatio,"  act of fastening or making firm).
