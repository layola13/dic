nonreligious:/ˌnɒnrɪˈlɪdʒəs/ /ˌnɑːnrɪˈlɪdʒəs/
adjective
非宗教的;世俗的
Not having or showing any concern with religion.
例句：
He was raised in a nonreligious household.
他在一个非宗教的家庭长大。

例句：
The school promotes nonreligious values such as tolerance and respect.
学校提倡诸如宽容和尊重之类的非宗教价值观。

近义词：secular, lay, worldly, temporal
反义词：religious, devout, pious
词性：adjective
