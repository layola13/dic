disburse:/dɪsˈbɜːrs/| v.|支付，支出，付款|To pay out (money) from a fund.|The company disbursed $10 million in bonuses. 公司支付了1000万美元的奖金。|近义词：pay out, expend, distribute|反义词：receive, collect|词性:动词
disbursed:/dɪsˈbɜːrst/| v. |已支付的，已支出的|Past participle of disburse.|The funds were disbursed to the beneficiaries last week.  资金已于上周支付给受益人。|近义词：paid out, expended, distributed|反义词：received, collected|词性:动词(过去分词)

词源:
From Anglo-French *desborser, from des- (expressing removal) + borse (purse).
