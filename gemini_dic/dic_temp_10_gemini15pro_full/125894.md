muscularity:/ˌmʌskjʊˈlærɪti/
n.
肌肉发达；强壮
The state or quality of having well-developed muscles.
He admired the muscularity of the weightlifters.  他羡慕举重运动员的肌肉发达。
近义词:brawn, strength, power
反义词:weakness, frailty, feebleness
