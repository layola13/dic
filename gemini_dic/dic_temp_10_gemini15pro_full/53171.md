dippy:/ˈdɪpi/ | adj. | 愚蠢的，傻的 | Foolish, silly, slightly crazy.  |  That's a dippy idea. 那是个愚蠢的主意。 | She's a bit dippy sometimes, but she's a good person. 她有时有点傻，但她是个好人。 | 近义词: silly, foolish, daft | 反义词: sensible, wise, intelligent | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "Dippy" likely originates from "dip," referring to a brief immersion, perhaps implying a brief lapse of sanity or intelligence.  "Dippier" is simply the comparative form of "dippy."
