litigiously:/lɪˈtɪdʒəsli/
adjective
好诉讼地；爱打官事地
in a litigious manner; inclined to engage in lawsuits.  Disposed to settle disputes by legal action.
He is known for his litigious nature, always ready to sue someone. 他以好诉讼的性格而闻名，总是准备起诉别人。
The company has a reputation for being litigious and aggressively pursuing its legal rights. 这家公司以好诉讼和积极维护其合法权利而闻名。
近义词:contentious, quarrelsome, argumentative
反义词:amicable, conciliatory, peaceable
词性:adjective
词源:From Latin *litigiosus, from litigium (“lawsuit”) + -osus.
