disenchained:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtʃeɪnd/
adj.
解脱的，释放的
Freed from chains or shackles, either literally or figuratively; liberated.
He felt disenchained from the worries of his former life. 他感觉从先前生活的忧虑中解脱了出来。
Synonyms: liberated, freed, released, unfettered
Antonyms: enslaved, bound, shackled, constrained
词性: 形容词
