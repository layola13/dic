dipterygian:/dɪpˈtɛrɪdʒɪən/
| adj. n.|双翅目的；双翅目昆虫|Relating to or characteristic of the Diptera, an order of insects including flies, mosquitoes, gnats, and midges.  Characterized by having a single pair of wings.|例句：The dipterygian insect hovered near the fruit.  这只双翅目昆虫在水果附近盘旋。|近义词：fly, mosquito, gnat, midge|反义词:|adjective, noun
