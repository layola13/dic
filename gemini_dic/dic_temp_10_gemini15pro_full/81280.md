grantable:/ˈɡræntəbl/| adj.|可授予的，可给予的|Something that is grantable is able to be granted or bestowed. |The scholarship is grantable to students who demonstrate financial need.  这笔奖学金可授予有经济需要的学生。

The land is grantable by the government for development. 这块土地可由政府批准开发。|Synonyms: awardable, bestowable, conferrable|Antonyms: non-grantable, unawardable|adjective
