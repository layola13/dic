busies:/ˈbɪziz/| v.|使忙碌，忙于|She busies herself with housework. 她忙于家务。|近义词：occupies, engrosses, preoccupies|反义词: idles, relaxes|verb
Etymology: From Middle English *busien, from Old English bysgian (“to occupy, engage, trouble”), from Proto-Germanic *busgijaną (“to be busy, to be anxious”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰewg- (“to bend, to turn, to swell”).  The modern form is a back-formation from busy.
