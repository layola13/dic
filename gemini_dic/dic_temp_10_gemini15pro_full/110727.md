linesman:/ˈlaɪnzmən/ (US), /ˈlaɪnzmən/ (UK)| n. |巡边员，线务员|A person who assists a referee in a sport such as football, hockey, or tennis, especially by deciding whether the ball has gone out of play; A person who installs or repairs electrical or telephone lines.|例句：The linesman raised his flag for offside. 巡边员举旗示意越位。|近义词：assistant referee, umpire (in tennis)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
