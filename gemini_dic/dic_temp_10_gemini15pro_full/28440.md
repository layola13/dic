carboy:/ˈkɑːrˌbɔɪ/ | n. |大玻璃瓶；大肚瓶|A large glass bottle or container with a narrow neck, typically protected by a wicker or other covering, used for holding liquids, especially acids or chemicals.|The chemist carefully poured the acid into the carboy.|近义词：demijohn, flagon, jug |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
Etymology: From Persian *karāba*, from *karā* "large" + *ba* "bottle".
