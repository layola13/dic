index-linked:/ˈɪndɛks lɪŋkt/
adjective
指数挂钩的
Linked to an index, typically a price index, so that the value rises and falls in line with the index.
Her pension is index-linked, protecting her against inflation. 她的养老金与指数挂钩，可以抵御通货膨胀。
Index-linked gilts offer protection against rising inflation. 与指数挂钩的政府债券可以抵御通货膨胀。
近义词:inflation-proofed, inflation-protected
反义词:fixed, unlinked
词性:adjective
