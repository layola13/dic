goujere:/ɡuˈʒɛər/ | n. | 泡芙，奶酪泡芙 | A small, savory choux pastry puff made with cheese, often Gruyère. |例句：The goujeres were light, airy, and bursting with cheesy flavor.  奶酪泡芙口感轻盈，充满奶酪香味。

例句：We served goujeres as an appetizer before the main course. 我们在上主菜之前先上了奶酪泡芙作为开胃菜。| 近义词：cheese puff, choux pastry | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From French *goujère*, ultimately from Vulgar Latin *goffaria*, meaning "waffle," related to Old French *gofre*.
