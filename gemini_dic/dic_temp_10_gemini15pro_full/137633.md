nontypicalnesses:/ˌnɑːnˈtɪpɪkəlnəsɪz/
noun
非典型性，异常性
The state or quality of being nontypical; atypicality.  The collection or aggregation of nontypical instances or characteristics.
His nontypicalnesses in behavior made him stand out from the crowd. 他的行为异常使他从人群中脱颖而出。
Synonyms: atypicalities, abnormalities, irregularities, eccentricities, peculiarities
Antonyms: typicalities, normalities, regularities, commonalities
