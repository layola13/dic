calabash:/ˈkæləbæʃ/| n.|葫芦，瓢|A large, round fruit with a hard shell, used for making bowls, pipes, etc.  Also refers to the plant itself.  |The old man smoked his tobacco in a calabash pipe. 老人用葫芦做的烟斗抽烟。|Synonyms: gourd, bottle gourd|Antonyms: N/A|noun

calabash:/ˈkæləbæʃ/| n.|葫芦，瓢|A tropical American evergreen tree. |Calabash trees can grow up to 30 feet tall. 葫芦树可以长到30英尺高。|Synonyms: Crescentia cujete (scientific name)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
