overresponding:/ˌōvərərɪˈspɑːndɪŋ/ | adj. |反应过度|Reacting excessively or disproportionately to a stimulus or situation.|She's overresponding to the criticism; it wasn't that bad. 她对批评反应过度了；其实并没有那么糟。|The market overresponded to the news, causing a sharp drop in prices.  市场对这个消息反应过度，导致价格暴跌。|近义词：overreacting, hypersensitivity|反义词：underreacting,  unresponsive|词性:形容词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "responding" (present participle of "respond," from Latin "respondere," meaning "to answer")
