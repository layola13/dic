paraphrase:/ˈpærəfreɪz/ | v. n. | 转述，意译；释义 | To express something using different words, especially in order to make it easier to understand. |  He paraphrased the poem in modern English. 他用现代英语解释了这首诗。 | synonyms: reword, rephrase, restate, summarize, gloss | antonyms: quote, plagiarize | verb
paraphrase:/ˈpærəfreɪz/ | n. | 转述，意译；释义 | A rewording of something written or spoken by someone else. | The teacher asked for a paraphrase of the paragraph. 老师要求对这段文字进行释义。| synonyms: rewording, rephrasing, rendition, version, interpretation | antonyms: quotation, verbatim | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *paraphrasis, from Greek paráphrasis "a telling in other words," from paraphrazein "to tell in other words," from para- "beside" + phrazein "to tell."
