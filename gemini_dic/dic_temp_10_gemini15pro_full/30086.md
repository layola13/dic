cease:/siːs/
verb
停止，终止
To stop happening or existing; to stop something from happening or existing.
The fighting ceased when the truce was declared.  战斗在宣布休战后停止了。
The company has decided to cease operations. 该公司已决定停止运营。
synonyms: stop, halt, end, terminate, discontinue
antonyms: start, begin, commence, continue, persist
词性: 动词
