paced:/peɪst/| adj.|步调的，节奏的；定速的|Moving or progressing at a steady and consistent speed.  Measured and regular in movement or development; deliberately slow. |The professor delivered a paced lecture, ensuring everyone could follow. 教授以稳定的节奏授课，确保每个人都能跟上。


The runner maintained a paced stride throughout the marathon.  这位跑步者在整个马拉松比赛中保持着稳定的步伐。|近义词：measured, steady, regular, even, deliberate, unhurried|反义词：rushed, hurried, hasty, frenetic, erratic|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "pace" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Pace" ultimately comes from the Old French "pas" meaning "step," derived from the Latin "passus."
