delimitation:/diːˌlɪmɪˈteɪʃn/
n.
限定，界限
the act of setting boundaries or limits
The delimitation of the country's borders was a complex process.  这个国家的边界划分是一个复杂的过程。

近义词: boundary, limit, demarcation, restriction
反义词: expansion, extension, enlargement
