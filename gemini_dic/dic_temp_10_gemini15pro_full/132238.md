nondeciduous:/ˌnɑːndɪˈsɪdʒuəs/
adjective
非落叶的，常绿的
Not shedding leaves seasonally; evergreen.
The live oak is a nondeciduous tree, meaning it retains its leaves year-round.  活橡树是一种常绿树，这意味着它一年四季都保留着叶子。
Magnolia trees are nondeciduous and provide beautiful green foliage even in winter.  木兰树是常绿的，即使在冬天也能提供美丽的绿色叶子。
synonym: evergreen, persistent
antonym: deciduous
词性: adjective
