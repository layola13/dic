accost: /əˈkɒst/ (UK) /əˈkɔːst/ (US)| vt.| 搭讪；上前攀谈；（尤指带有敌意地）走近；对…进行乞讨| To approach and address (someone) boldly or aggressively, especially with a demand or request. |A man accosted me in the street and asked for money. 一个男人在街上走近我，向我要钱。

The beggar accosted passersby for spare change. 乞丐向路人乞讨零钱。

|近义词：approach, confront, address, solicit, buttonhole|反义词：avoid, evade, shun|词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *acoster* "to come alongside," from *coste* "rib, side, coast," from Latin *costa* "rib, side."  The original sense was nautical, meaning to come alongside another ship. The meaning shifted to "approach and speak to" in the 16th century.

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He often accosts tourists to sell them souvenirs. = 他经常上前和游客搭讪，向他们兜售纪念品。
She never accosts strangers, even to ask for directions. = 她从不跟陌生人搭讪，即使是问路也不会。

一般过去时:
A stranger accosted him in the park and asked for a cigarette. = 一个陌生人在公园里和他搭讪，向他要一根烟。
The journalist accosted the politician as he was leaving the building. = 记者在政治家离开大楼时上前和他攀谈。

现在完成时:
Several people have accosted me today asking for money. = 今天已经有几个人上前向我要钱了。
She has accosted several celebrities for autographs. = 她已经向好几个名人索要签名了。

过去完成时:
The police officer had accosted the suspect before he could escape. = 警察在嫌疑人逃跑之前就上前拦住了他。
She had accosted the store manager several times about the faulty product. = 她已经就这个有缺陷的产品跟店长交涉过好几次了。

将来时:
I will accost the manager if I don't get a refund. = 如果我得不到退款，我就要去找经理。
If he accosts me again, I will call the police. = 如果他再纠缠我，我就报警。

虚拟语气:
If someone were to accost me in that alley, I would be terrified. = 如果有人在那条小巷里跟我搭讪，我会吓坏的。
I wish he wouldn't accost me every time I walk past his store. = 我希望他不要每次我经过他的店都来跟我搭讪。

被动语态:
He was accosted by a group of beggars. = 他被一群乞丐围住了。
She was accosted by a fan who wanted her autograph. = 她被一个想要她签名的粉丝拦住了。


PHRASE->
be accosted by someone = 被某人搭讪
accost someone for something = 为某事而搭讪某人
accost someone with something = 用某物搭讪某人 (e.g., accost someone with a question)
accost someone in the street = 在街上搭讪某人
accost someone aggressively =  气势汹汹地搭讪某人
