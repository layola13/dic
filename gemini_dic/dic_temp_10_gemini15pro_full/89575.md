USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Who knows the answer? = 谁知道答案？
Who wants ice cream? = 谁想要冰淇淋？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Who is knocking at the door? = 谁在敲门？
Who is talking so loudly? = 谁在大声说话？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Who has finished their homework? = 谁完成了作业？
Who has been to Paris? = 谁去过巴黎？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
Who has been using my computer? = 谁一直在用我的电脑？
Who has been singing all morning? = 谁一早上都在唱歌？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
Who called last night? = 昨晚谁打电话来了？
Who broke the vase? = 谁打碎了花瓶？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
Who was sleeping in my bed? = 谁在我的床上睡觉？
Who were you talking to on the phone? = 你在和谁打电话？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
Who had already left before the party started? = 派对开始前谁已经离开了？
Who had eaten all the cookies? = 谁吃了所有的饼干？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
Who had been waiting for the bus for an hour? = 谁等了一个小时的公交车？
Who had been working on this project before me? = 在我之前谁一直在做这个项目？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
Who will win the game? = 谁会赢得比赛？
Who will be the next president? = 谁将是下一任总统？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
Who will be attending the meeting tomorrow? = 明天谁会参加会议？
Who will be cooking dinner tonight? = 今晚谁做饭？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
Who will have finished the race by noon? = 中午之前谁会完成比赛？
Who will have graduated by next year? = 到明年谁会毕业？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
Who will have been living here for ten years by 2030? = 到2030年谁将已经在这里住了十年？
Who will have been studying English for five years by then? = 到那时谁将已经学习英语五年了？


虚拟语气:
If I knew who was responsible, I would tell you. = 如果我知道谁负责，我会告诉你。
I wish I knew who to ask for help. = 我希望我知道该向谁寻求帮助。

被动语态:
By who was this book written? = 这本书是谁写的？（正式用法，通常用"Who was this book written by?"）
Who is being interviewed right now? = 现在谁在接受采访？


疑问句:
Who are you? = 你是谁？
Who is she? = 她是谁？


否定句:
I don't know who he is. = 我不知道他是谁。
She didn't say who she was with. = 她没说她和谁在一起。
