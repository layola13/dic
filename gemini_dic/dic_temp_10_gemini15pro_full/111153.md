litherly:/ˈlɪðərli/| adv.|轻快地，灵活地，敏捷地|In a light or nimble manner; actively.  Referring to physical movement or action, suggesting ease and grace.|She danced litherly across the stage. 她轻快地在舞台上跳舞。|近义词：nimbly, agilely, lightly, swiftly|反义词:clumsily, awkwardly, heavily|词性:副词

Etymology:
From Middle English *litherli, from Old English *liðerlice, from liðe ("gentle, mild, meek; pleasant, agreeable; soft, smooth; flexible, pliant") +‎ -lice (adverbial suffix).  The root is related to the modern English word "lithe."
