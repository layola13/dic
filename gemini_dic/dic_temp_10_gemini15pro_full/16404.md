barcode:/ˈbɑːrkoʊd/ (US) /ˈbɑːkəʊd/ (UK)| n.|条形码|A machine-readable code in the form of numbers and a pattern of parallel lines of varying widths, printed on and identifying a product.|The cashier scanned the barcode on the cereal box. 收银员扫描了麦片盒上的条形码。|近义词：UPC (Universal Product Code), scan code|反义词: None|Noun
