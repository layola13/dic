hilting:/ˈhɪltɪŋ/| adv.|拼命地，彻底地，完全地|Fighting to the hilting describes a fight where both sides are giving everything they have, often to the death.  It comes from the idea of fighting so fiercely that your hand is gripping the hilt of your sword so tightly that it can't go any further down.|They argued their case to the hilting.|近义词: fiercely, relentlessly, determinedly|反义词: halfheartedly, weakly, apathetically|词性: 副词
