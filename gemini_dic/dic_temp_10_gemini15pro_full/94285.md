imperspicuous:/ɪmpərˈspɪkjuəs/| adj.|晦涩难懂的，费解的|Not easily understood; obscure or confusing.|His imperspicuous writing style made it difficult for students to grasp the concepts. 他晦涩难懂的写作风格让学生难以理解这些概念。|The contract was filled with imperspicuous legal jargon.  这份合同充满了晦涩难懂的法律术语。|近义词：obscure, ambiguous, unclear, vague, confusing, enigmatic|反义词：clear, lucid, perspicuous, transparent, intelligible|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *imperspicuus*, from *in-* (not) + *perspicuus* (clear, transparent), from *per-* (through) + *spicere* (to look).
