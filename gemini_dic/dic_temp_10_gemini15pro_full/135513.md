nonplastics:/ˌnɑːnˈplæstɪks/
noun
非塑料制品
materials that are not made of plastic
例句：The company is exploring the use of nonplastics in its packaging.  这家公司正在探索在其包装中使用非塑料制品。

例句：Consumers are increasingly seeking nonplastics alternatives to reduce their environmental impact. 消费者越来越多地寻求非塑料替代品以减少对环境的影响。

近义词：alternatives, substitutes
反义词：plastics
词性：n.
