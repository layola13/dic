communital: /kəˈmjuːnɪtəl/
adj.
公共的，社区的
Of or relating to a community; shared by a community.
例句：Communital harmony is essential for a thriving society.  社区和谐对于繁荣的社会至关重要。

例句：The communital garden provided fresh produce for the neighborhood.  社区花园为邻里提供了新鲜的农产品。

近义词：community, collective, shared, common, public
反义词：private, individual, personal
词性：adjective

词源: From "community" + "-al". "Community" comes from Late Latin *communitas, from Latin communis "common".
