dress: /dres/ (US) /dres/ (UK) | v.|  包扎(伤口)；处理；准备 | To apply a dressing to a wound; to prepare something, such as food or a carcass, for cooking or use.  | The nurse dressed the wound carefully. 护士仔细地包扎了伤口。 | He dressed the salad with olive oil and vinegar. 他用橄榄油和醋拌沙拉。 | 近义词: bandage, treat, prepare | 反义词: N/A | 词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *dresser* (to straighten, arrange, prepare), from Vulgar Latin *directiare*, frequentative of Latin *dirigere* (to direct, guide).
