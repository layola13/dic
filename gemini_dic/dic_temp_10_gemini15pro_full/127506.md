necklines: /ˈnɛkˌlaɪnz/ | n. | 领口， neckline的复数形式 | The shape of the opening around the neck of a garment.  The dress had a plunging neckline. | 例句：She chose a dress with a flattering neckline. 她选择了一件领口很衬托她的连衣裙。

例句：The shop offers a variety of necklines to suit different tastes. 这家店提供各种各样的领口以满足不同的口味。  | 近义词：neck openings, collars (sometimes) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps high neckline could be considered in some contexts) | 词性: noun
