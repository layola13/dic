moeman:/ˈmoʊˌɛmən/ | n. | 萌妹子；可愛的女孩 | A term used primarily online to describe a young, cute, and often fictional female character, typically in anime or manga, designed to elicit feelings of affection or protectiveness.  It can be considered a sub-category of kawaii (cute) in Japanese culture. | Example:  The artist drew a moeman with big eyes and colorful hair.  这位艺术家画了一个大眼睛、彩色头发的萌妹子。| Synonym:  kawaii girl, cute girl | Antonym:  unattractive girl | noun
