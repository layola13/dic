directorially:/dɪˌrɛkˈtɔːriəli/
|adv.|导演地；指导地|In a manner relating to the work or skill of a director. |The film was directorially stunning, showcasing innovative camera angles and lighting. 这部电影在导演方面令人惊艳，展示了创新的拍摄角度和灯光。|The play was directorially weak, lacking a clear vision. 这部戏导演方面很弱，缺乏清晰的视野。|近义词:artistically, creatively, skillfully|反义词:amateurishly, clumsily, ineptly|词性:副词
Etymology: From "director" + "-ial" + "-ly". "Director" comes from the Late Latin "director," meaning "guide" or "ruler."  "-ial" forms adjectives from nouns, and "-ly" forms adverbs from adjectives.
