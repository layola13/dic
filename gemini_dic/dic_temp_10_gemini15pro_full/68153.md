failingness:/ˈfeɪlɪŋnɪs/
n.
失败；缺点；弱点
The state or quality of being prone to failure or having shortcomings.
His failingness to meet deadlines cost him his job. 他未能按时完成任务导致他丢了工作。
failing, shortcoming, weakness
strength, success
noun
