disacidify:/diːəˈsɪdɪfaɪ/| vt.|去酸，脱酸|To remove acid from something; to neutralize acid.|The winemaker disacidified the wine to improve its flavor.  酿酒师对葡萄酒进行脱酸以改善其风味。|The soil was disacidified by adding lime. 通过添加石灰来降低土壤酸度。|neutralize, deacidify|acidify|verb
ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- + acidify.  "Dis-" is a prefix meaning "reversal or removal of," and "acidify" means "to make or become acid." Thus, "disacidify" means "to remove acidity."
