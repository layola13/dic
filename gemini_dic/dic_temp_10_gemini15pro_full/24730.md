bronzing:/ˈbrɒnzɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbrɒnzɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |古铜色，晒黑，青铜化|The process of becoming bronze or bronze-colored, either naturally (as with a suntan) or artificially (as with makeup).  It can also refer to the application of bronze powder or paint, or the development of a bronze-like patina on a metal.|She achieved a healthy bronzing from her vacation in the Caribbean. 她加勒比海度假回来，晒得一身健康的古铜色。|近义词：tanning, sunbathing|反义词：whitening, bleaching|词性: noun

bronzing:/ˈbrɒnzɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbrɒnzɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. |古铜色的，用于晒黑的|Having a bronze color or finish.|She used a bronzing lotion to give her skin a sun-kissed look. 她用了一种古铜色的乳液，让她的皮肤看起来像被阳光亲吻过一样。|近义词：bronze, tanned|反义词：pale, white|词性: adjective
