docudramatist:/ˈdɒkjuːˈdrɑːmətɪst/ | n. |纪录片剧作家；纪实剧作家 | A writer who creates docudramas, which are dramatized accounts of real events. |  Example 1: The docudramatist meticulously researched the historical period to ensure accuracy in the script.  这位纪实剧作家仔细研究了历史时期，以确保剧本的准确性。

Example 2: The docudramatist faced criticism for taking liberties with the facts in their portrayal of the event.  这位纪实剧作家因在刻画事件时歪曲事实而受到批评。 |  Synonym:  documentary playwright, screenwriter (for documentaries)| Antonym:  fiction writer, playwright (of fictional works) | noun
