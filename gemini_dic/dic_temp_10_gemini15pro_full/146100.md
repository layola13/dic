overelaborates:/ˌoʊvərɪˈlæbəreɪts/| v.| 过分详尽阐述，过度修饰|  explains or describes something in excessive detail, often making it more complicated than necessary. | He overelaborates his points, making the presentation tedious. 他把他的观点解释得过于详细，使得演讲很乏味。|近义词：overexplain, belabor, dwell on|反义词: simplify, condense, summarize|词性:动词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "elaborate" (from Latin elaborare, "to work out")
