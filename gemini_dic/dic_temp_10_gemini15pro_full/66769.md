exoteric:/ˌeksəˈtɛrɪk/| adj.| 外界的，公开的，通俗的 | Intended for or likely to be understood by the general public. | Example: The exoteric doctrines of the Pythagorean school were those which were openly professed and taught.| Synonym: public, popular, common, accessible, understandable, intelligible | Antonym: esoteric, arcane, obscure, private, hidden, confidential | 词性: adjective

exoteric:/ˌeksəˈtɛrɪk/| n.| 外行，俗人 | A person not belonging to a particular group, profession, etc. | Example:  The meaning was clear to the initiated, but remained opaque to the exoteric.| Synonym: layman, outsider, nonprofessional, novice | Antonym: expert, insider, professional, specialist | 词性: noun
