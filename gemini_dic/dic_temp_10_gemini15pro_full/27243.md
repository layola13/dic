caloriduct:/ˈkælərɪˌdʌkt/ | n. | 输热管，导热管 |A duct or passage for conveying heat.  A theoretical or fictional passage for conveying heat, especially in the context of science fiction or fantasy. | The caloriduct efficiently transferred heat from the reactor to the living quarters.  输热管有效地将热量从反应堆转移到生活区。
