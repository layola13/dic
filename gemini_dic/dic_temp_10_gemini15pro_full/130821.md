nonappealable:/ˌnɒnəˈpiːləbl/
adjective
不可上诉的
Not subject to appeal; final.
The judge's decision is nonappealable. 法官的判决是不可上诉的。
The arbitrator's ruling was deemed nonappealable. 仲裁员的裁决被认为是不可上诉的。
近义词:final, conclusive, definitive, unchallengeable
反义词:appealable
