fouler:/ˈfaʊlər/| n.|犯规者；弄脏的人或物|A fouler is someone or something that makes something dirty or commits a foul. |The referee penalized the fouler for tripping. 裁判因绊人对犯规者进行了处罚。|近义词：offender, culprit, polluter, contaminant|反义词:cleaner, purifier|noun
fouler:/ˈfaʊlər/| v.|弄脏；犯规|To foul or make dirty; to commit a foul. |The factory fouled the river with its waste. 这家工厂用废料污染了河流。|近义词：contaminate, pollute, soil, defile, infringe|反义词:clean, purify|verb
