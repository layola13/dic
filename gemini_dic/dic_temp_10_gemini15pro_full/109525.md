lesion: /ˈliːʒən/ (US) /ˈliːʒn/ (UK)| n.|损害，损伤；病灶；病变|An area of diseased or injured tissue.  A lesion can be caused by various factors, including trauma, infection, or disease.|The doctor discovered a lesion on her lung during the X-ray. 医生在X光检查中发现了她肺部的一个病灶。|Synonyms: wound, injury, sore, ulcer, abscess|Antonyms: healing, recovery|Noun
