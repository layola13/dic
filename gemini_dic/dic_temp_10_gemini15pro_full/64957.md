esthetically:/ɛsˈθɛtɪkli/ | adv. | 美学地；审美地；雅致地 |  In a way that relates to beauty or appearance, pleasing in appearance. | The room was aesthetically pleasing with its soft lighting and carefully chosen artwork. 房间里的灯光柔和，艺术品也是精心挑选的，看起来赏心悦目。| The building was aesthetically designed. 这座建筑设计美观。 | synonyms: artistically, beautifully, tastefully | antonyms: uglily, hideously, unattractively | 词性: 副词
