buzzy:/ˈbʌzi/| adj.|嗡嗡的，嘈杂的，兴奋的，热闹的|Making a low, continuous humming sound.  Also, lively, exciting, or popular.|The room was buzzy with conversation. 房间里嗡嗡的谈话声。|近义词：vibrant, lively, exciting, bustling, energetic|反义词：quiet, still, dull, boring|词性:形容词
Etymology: From buzz + -y.  Buzz ultimately imitates the sound.
