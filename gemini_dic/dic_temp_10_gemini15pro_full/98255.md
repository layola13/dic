inseparables: /ɪnˈsɛpərəblz/
| n. |形影不离的人（或物）; 不可分离的事物|People or things that are very close and cannot be separated. |They've been inseparables since they met in college.  他们自从大学相遇就一直形影不离。|近义词：bosom friends, close friends, inseparable friends, best friends|反义词：estranged, separated|词性:名词

inseparable: /ɪnˈsɛpərəbl/
| adj. |不可分离的; 形影不离的; 密切相关的;  |Not able to be separated. |The two friends were inseparable. 这两个朋友形影不离。|近义词：indivisible, unbreakable,  intimate|反义词：separable, divisible|词性:形容词


Etymology:
From Late Latin inseparabilis, from in- "not" + separabilis "separable," from separare "to separate."
