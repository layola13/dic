autoharp:/ˈɔːtəʊhɑːrp/ | n. | 自鸣竖琴 | A musical instrument, similar to a zither, having a series of chord bars that are pressed to dampen unwanted strings, leaving the desired chord to be strummed. |  She played a beautiful melody on the autoharp. 她用自鸣竖琴演奏了一曲优美的旋律。 |  The autoharp's gentle sound filled the room.  自鸣竖琴柔和的声音充满了整个房间。 | synonym: zither, chord zither | antonym: (No direct antonym for a musical instrument) | noun
