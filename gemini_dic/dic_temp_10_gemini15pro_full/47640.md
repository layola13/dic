debited:/ˈdɛbɪtɪd/
verb|借记，扣除（钱款）| to enter a sum of money as a debit in an account; charge (an amount) against (a person or account).|  Your account has been debited with $50. 你的账户已被扣除50美元。|charged, deducted|credited|v.
debited:/ˈdɛbɪtɪd/
adjective|借记的，扣除的| (of an account) having had a specified sum charged against it.| My debited account showed a negative balance. 我的借记账户显示余额为负。|charged, deducted|credited|adj.
