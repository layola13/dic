byssolite:/ˈbɪsəˌlaɪt/| n. |绒石，细长石棉|A fine, silky variety of amphibole, occurring in delicate fibers or plumose masses, usually white or yellowish. It is a kind of asbestos.|Example: The byssolite found in the cave was incredibly delicate and shimmered in the dim light.  洞穴中发现的绒石非常精细，在昏暗的光线下闪烁。
