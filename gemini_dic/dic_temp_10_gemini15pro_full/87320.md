herniated:/ˈhɜːrniˌeɪtɪd/| adj.|疝气的，突出|A herniated disc occurs when the soft center of a spinal disc pushes through a crack in the tougher exterior casing.  椎间盘突出症发生在脊椎盘的柔软中心穿过较硬的外壳裂缝时。|He has a herniated disc in his lower back. 他的腰椎间盘突出。|Synonyms: ruptured, prolapsed|Antonyms: normal, intact|adjective

herniate:/ˈhɜːrniˌeɪt/| v.|形成疝气，突出|The impact caused the disc to herniate. 撞击导致椎间盘突出。|The sudden strain herniated his abdominal wall.  突然的拉伤导致他的腹壁突出。|Synonyms: rupture, protrude|Antonyms: retract, recede|verb
