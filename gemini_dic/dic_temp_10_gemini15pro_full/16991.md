bastinado:/ˌbæstɪˈneɪdoʊ/ /ˌbæstɪˈnɑːdoʊ/
|n. vt.|杖刑；棒打；拷打|The act of beating the soles of someone's feet as a form of punishment or torture.  Also, the punishment itself.|The thief received a bastinado for his crimes. | 近义词：flogging, whipping, caning, scourging|反义词: reward, praise|noun
bastinado:/ˌbæstɪˈneɪdoʊ/ /ˌbæstɪˈnɑːdoʊ/
|vt.|杖打；棒打；拷打|To beat the soles of someone's feet as a form of punishment or torture.|The guards bastinadoed the prisoner for information. |近义词：flog, whip, cane, scourge|反义词: reward, praise|verb
