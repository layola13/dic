canalization:/ˌkænələˈzeɪʃn/| n.|渠化；管道化；(胚胎发育的)稳态化；(行为或性格的)定型化|The process of forming a canal or channel, or the state of being canalized. Also refers to the tendency of development to follow a particular pathway, despite genetic or environmental variations, or the restriction of phenotypic expression to a limited number of outcomes.|The canalization of the river provided a new route for trade. 这条河的渠化为贸易提供了新的途径。|近义词：channeling, routing, directing, stabilization|反义词: diversification, variability|词性:名词

canalization:/ˌkænələˈzeɪʃn/| n.|管道化；(行为或性格的)定型化 |The restriction of phenotypic expression to a limited number of outcomes, or the channeling of behavior into specific patterns.|Early childhood experiences can lead to the canalization of certain personality traits. 童年早期的经历会导致某些性格特征的定型化。|近义词: fixation, restriction, channeling |反义词: flexibility, adaptability |词性:名词
