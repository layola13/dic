nonembryonically:/ˌnɒnˌɛmbrɪˈɒnɪkli/
adjective
非胚胎地
Not related to or derived from an embryo; occurring or developing outside the embryo.
Nonembryonic stem cells can be derived from adult tissues.  非胚胎干细胞可以从成体组织中获得。
These cells differentiate nonembryonically. 这些细胞以非胚胎方式分化。
Synonyms: extraembryonic, postembryonic
Antonyms: embryonic
词性: adjective
