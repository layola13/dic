hourglasses:/ˈaʊərˌɡlæsɪz/
n.
沙漏（hourglass 的复数）
instruments for measuring time, consisting of two bulbs of glass connected by a narrow neck, allowing sand to flow from the upper bulb to the lower one in a fixed amount of time.
The scientist used hourglasses to measure the duration of the experiments. 科学家用沙漏来测量实验的时间。
The museum displayed several antique hourglasses. 博物馆展出了几个古董沙漏。
synonyms: sandglasses, sand timers, timers
antonyms: clocks, watches
noun
