indistancy:/ˌɪndɪˈstənsɪ/ |n.|  不清晰，模糊不清; 近距离;  |The indistancy of the photograph made it difficult to identify the subject.  照片的模糊性使得难以识别拍摄对象。|Nearness, proximity; indistinctness, vagueness| Remoteness, distance; clarity, sharpness | noun

Etymology:  From in- (not) + distance + -cy (suffix forming nouns of state or quality).  Essentially, the lack of distance, either literally (meaning nearness) or figuratively (lack of clarity, as if seen from a close but unfocused perspective).
