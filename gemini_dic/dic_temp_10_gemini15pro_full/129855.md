non-incidental:/ˌnɒn ɪnsɪˈdentl/ /ˌnɑːn ɪnsɪˈdentl/ | adj. | 非偶然的；非附带的；重要的；主要的 | Not happening by chance; essential; important.  Relating to the primary subject or purpose and not occurring merely as a consequence of something else. |  This is a non-incidental finding of the study, highlighting a crucial aspect of the disease.  这是这项研究的一个重要发现，突出了该疾病的一个关键方面。| Example: The non-incidental expenses of running a business can be significant. 经营一家企业的必要开支可能很大。 | Synonym: essential, crucial, important, vital, primary, key, integral, inherent | Antonym: incidental, accidental, minor, trivial, unimportant, peripheral, secondary | adjective
