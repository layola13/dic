godforsakenness:/ˌɡɒd.fəˈseɪ.kən.nəs/ /ˌɡɑːd.fərˈseɪ.kən.nəs/
n.
被上帝遗弃的状态; 荒凉; 凄凉
the state of being deserted and desolate, as if abandoned by God.
The godforsakenness of the remote island made the castaways despair.  这偏远岛屿的荒凉让漂流者感到绝望。
The traveler was struck by the godforsakenness of the landscape. 旅行者被这片土地的荒凉所震撼。
desolation, barrenness, remoteness
comfort, prosperity, civilization
noun
