meowing:/miˈaʊɪŋ/ | v. | （猫）喵喵叫 |The act of a cat making its characteristic sound. | The cat was meowing at the door to be let in. 猫在门口喵喵叫，想进来。| 近义词: purring, caterwauling | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 being silent 保持沉默) | 词性: 动词 (现在分词)

词源:  From meow + -ing.  Ultimately imitative.
