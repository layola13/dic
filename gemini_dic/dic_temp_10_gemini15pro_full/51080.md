despatch's:/dɪˈspætʃɪz/
noun
派遣;急件;发送;新闻报道;
The despatch's arrival was eagerly awaited. 急件的到来备受期待。
The general read the despatches from the front line. 将军阅读了前线的新闻报道。
Synonyms: shipment, sending, dispatch, message, report
Antonyms: receipt, arrival, holding, retention
词性: 名词 (Noun)


despatch:/dɪˈspætʃ/
verb
派遣;发送;迅速处理;杀死;
The troops were despatched to the border. 部队被派往边境。
He despatched the task quickly and efficiently. 他快速高效地完成了任务。
Synonyms: send, dispatch, forward, transmit, kill, execute
Antonyms: receive, hold, delay, keep, revive
词性: 动词 (Verb)


Etymology:
From Old French despescher "to hasten, expedite," from des- "out, away" + pescher "to hinder, impede" (from Latin impedicare "to entangle").  The sense shift arose from the idea of getting rid of impediments.
