degerm:/ˌdiːˈdʒɜːrm/ | v. | 去除胚芽; 除去细菌 | To remove the germ from grain, especially wheat. Also, to remove or destroy germs; disinfect. |  They degerm the wheat to make white flour. 他们去除小麦的胚芽来制作白面粉。

The company degerms the equipment regularly. 这家公司定期给设备消毒。 | 近义词: sterilize, sanitize, disinfect | 反义词: contaminate, infect | 词性: 动词
