benthal:/ˈbɛnθəl/| adj.|深海底的，深海的|Of, relating to, or occurring at the bottom of a body of water.|例句：Benthal organisms thrive in the deep ocean. 深海生物在深海中繁衍生息。

例句：The benthal zone is characterized by low light and high pressure. 深海区以光线昏暗和高压为特征。|近义词：abyssal, deep-sea, submarine|反义词：pelagic, surface|词性:形容词

benthos:/ˈbɛnθɒs/| n.|底栖生物，水底生物|The flora and fauna found on the bottom, or in the bottom sediments, of a sea, lake, or other body of water.|例句：The benthos of this lake is diverse and includes many species of invertebrates. 这个湖的底栖生物种类繁多，包括许多无脊椎动物。

例句：Pollution can have a devastating impact on the benthos. 污染会对底栖生物造成毁灭性的影响。|近义词：bottom fauna, bottom dwellers|反义词：plankton, nekton|词性:名词
