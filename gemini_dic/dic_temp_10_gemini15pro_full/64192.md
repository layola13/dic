equestrienne:/ɪˌkwɛstriˈɛn/| n.|女骑手；马术女运动员|A female rider or performer on horseback.  A woman skilled in horse riding, especially one who rides in competitions or exhibitions.|例句：The equestrienne performed a daring jump. 女骑手表演了一次大胆的跳跃。

例句：She is a renowned equestrienne, known for her elegant style. 她是一位著名的女骑手，以其优雅的风格而闻名。|近义词：horsewoman, rider|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *équestre* (masculine) and *équestrienne* (feminine), ultimately from Latin *equester* ("horseman"), from *equus* ("horse").
