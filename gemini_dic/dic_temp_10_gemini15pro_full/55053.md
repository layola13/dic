disquisitorial:/ˌdɪskwɪzɪˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
爱议论的；好钻研的；探讨的
Given to or characterized by formal and lengthy discussion or argumentation.
The professor launched into a disquisitorial discourse on the nuances of existential philosophy. 教授开始就存在主义哲学的细微之处进行了一番长篇大论的探讨。
His disquisitorial style made the lecture rather tedious. 他好钻牛角尖的风格使得讲座相当乏味。
近义词: analytical, argumentative, critical, discursive, examining, exploratory, inquisitive, investigative, probing, searching
反义词: concise, succinct, brief
