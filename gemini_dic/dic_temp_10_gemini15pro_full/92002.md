hyperobtrusive:/ˌhaɪpərəbˈtruːsɪv/ | adj. | 过分突出的；过分惹眼的；过分强加于人的 | His hyperobtrusive manner made him unpopular. 他过分强加于人的态度让他不受欢迎。|  Pushy, intrusive, overbearing, blatant |  Unobtrusive, subtle, discreet | adjective
