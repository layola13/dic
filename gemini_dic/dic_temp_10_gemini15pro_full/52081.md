dialyze:/ˈdaɪəlaɪz/ (US), /ˈdaɪəlaɪz/ (UK)| v. |透析，渗析|To separate substances from a liquid by dialysis.  The process of removing waste products and excess fluid from the blood when the kidneys are not working properly.| The patient's blood was dialyzed to remove toxins.  病人的血液被透析以去除毒素。| synonyms: purify, filter | antonyms: contaminate, pollute | verb

dialysis:/daɪˈælɪsɪs/ (US), /daɪˈælɪsɪs/ (UK)| n. |透析，渗析|The separation of substances from a liquid by dialysis. The clinical purification of blood by dialysis, as a substitute for the normal function of the kidney.| He needs dialysis three times a week. 他每周需要透析三次。| synonyms: filtration, purification | antonyms: contamination, pollution | noun
