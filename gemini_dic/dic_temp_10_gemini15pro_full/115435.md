mantology:/mænˈtɒlədʒi/ | n. | 占卜术，预言 | The study or practice of divination by inspecting entrails, especially the livers of sacrificed animals.  It can also more broadly refer to any form of divination. |  Ancient Romans practiced mantology to predict the outcome of battles. 古罗马人通过占卜术来预测战争的结果。 |  He consulted a mantologist before making important decisions. 他在做重要决定之前咨询了一位占卜师。 |  divination, prophecy, augury | N/A | noun
