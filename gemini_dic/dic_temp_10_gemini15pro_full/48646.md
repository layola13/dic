defensed:/dɪˈfɛnst/ | v. | 防御，保卫 |  past tense and past participle of *defend* |  He defensed his actions in court.  他在法庭上为自己辩护。 | 近义词：protected, guarded, shielded | 反义词：attacked, assaulted | 词性: 动词

词源: From Middle English *defensen*, from Old French *defendre*, from Latin *dēfendere* (to ward off, protect).
