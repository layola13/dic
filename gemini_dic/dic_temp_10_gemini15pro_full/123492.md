monotessaron:/ˌmɒnəˈtɛsərɒn/ | n. | 四福音合参，四福音书 | A gospel harmony, or a continuous narrative of the life of Jesus Christ constructed from the four Gospels. |  The early church father Tatian compiled a monotessaron.  早期教会教父塔提安编纂了一部四福音合参。 | synonym: gospel harmony, diatessaron | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps individual Gospels) | noun
