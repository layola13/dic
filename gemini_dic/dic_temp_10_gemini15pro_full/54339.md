disheritor:/dɪsˈɪnˌhɛrɪtər/
n.
剥夺继承权的人
One who disinherits another; one who deprives another of an inheritance.
The disheritor was ostracized by the family after his actions became known.  剥夺继承权的人的行为被曝光后，他被家族排斥了。
His greedy uncle, the disheritor, left him with nothing but debt. 他贪婪的叔叔，剥夺了他的继承权，只给他留下了债务。
近义词:depriver
反义词:heir, beneficiary
