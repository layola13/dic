exudate:/ˈeksjʊdeɪt/ /ˈɛksjʊˌdeɪt/| n.|渗出物，分泌物|A substance that has oozed forth from a tissue or its capillaries, or from a wound. |The wound was seeping a thick, yellowish exudate.  伤口渗出浓稠的黄色分泌物。|近义词：discharge, secretion, effusion|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑吸收 absorb)|词性: noun
