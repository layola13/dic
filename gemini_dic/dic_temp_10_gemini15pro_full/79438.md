glitch:/ɡlɪtʃ/| n.|小故障，失灵，技术上的小毛病|A sudden, usually temporary malfunction or irregularity in a system, especially in electronics or computing. |The computer program had a glitch that caused it to crash.  电脑程序出现了一个小故障，导致它崩溃了。

The space mission was delayed due to a technical glitch. 太空任务由于技术故障而延迟。|近义词：bug, error, fault, malfunction, problem|反义词：perfection, smoothness|词性：名词
