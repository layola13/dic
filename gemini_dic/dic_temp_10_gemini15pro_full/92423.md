hypomaniac:/ˌhaɪpəˈmeɪniæk/ | n. adj. |轻躁狂患者; 轻躁狂的|A person experiencing hypomania; mildly manic.  Characterized by hypomania.|

例句：
He was diagnosed as hypomaniac after exhibiting periods of elevated mood and increased activity.
他因表现出情绪高涨和活动增多的时期而被诊断为轻躁狂。

例句：
Her hypomaniac episodes were marked by excessive spending and risky behavior.
她的轻躁狂发作的特点是过度消费和冒险行为。


近义词：manic, overexcited, elated|
反义词：depressed, lethargic|
词性：noun, adjective|
词源：From Greek *hupo* (under) + *mania* (madness)|
