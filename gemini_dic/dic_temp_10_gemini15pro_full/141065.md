onomasticon: /ˌɒnəˈmæstɪkən/ | n. | 人名辞典，专名辞典 | A book or list of names, especially of people or places. |  例句：The researcher consulted the onomasticon to verify the historical accuracy of the names in the novel. 研究人员查阅了人名辞典，以核实小说中名字的历史准确性。

例句：The onomasticon provided a comprehensive list of the deities worshipped in ancient Greece. 这本人名辞典提供了古希腊崇拜的神灵的完整清单。 | 近义词：name list, glossary of names, dictionary of names | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
