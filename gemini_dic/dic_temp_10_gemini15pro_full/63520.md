enunciate: /ɪˈnʌnsieɪt/ (US), /ɪˈnʌnʃɪeɪt/ (UK)| v. |清晰地发音；阐明；宣布|To pronounce words or parts of words clearly.|He enunciated each syllable of the word carefully. 他仔细地念出了这个单词的每个音节。|The spokesperson enunciated the company's new policy. 发言人宣布了公司的新政策。|近义词：articulate, pronounce, declare, express|反义词：mumble, slur|词性：动词

enunciated: /ɪˈnʌnsieɪtɪd/ (US), /ɪˈnʌnʃɪeɪtɪd/ (UK)| adj. |清晰表达的|Expressed or pronounced clearly.|His speech was carefully enunciated and easy to understand. 他的演讲清晰易懂。 |The principles of the new policy were clearly enunciated in the document. 新政策的原则在文件中得到了清晰的阐述。|近义词：articulated, clear, distinct|反义词：unclear, mumbled, slurred|词性：形容词
