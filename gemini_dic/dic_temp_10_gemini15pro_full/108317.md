laurinitis:/ˌlɔːrɪˈnaɪtɪs/
n.
月桂酸酯炎; 肉豆蔻酸酯炎; 十二酸酯炎
Inflammation caused by lauric acid, myristic acid, or dodecanoic acid.  Specifically refers to inflammation of the lamina within the hoof of a horse.
Laminitis can be a debilitating condition for horses.  马蹄叶炎对马来说可能是一种使人衰弱的疾病。
Near synonyms: hoof founder, inflammation of the lamina
No direct antonyms, but a healthy hoof would be the opposite state.
