overcompliant:/ˌoʊvərˌkəmˈplaɪənt/
| adj. | 过分顺从的，过度服从的 | Excessively compliant; obeying or agreeing too readily, often to the point of being detrimental to oneself. |  She was so overcompliant that she let her colleagues take advantage of her. 她太顺从了，以至于让同事们占了她的便宜。| 近义词: docile, submissive, acquiescent, obedient | 反义词: assertive, defiant, rebellious | 词性: adjective

overcompliant:/ˌoʊvərˌkəmˈplaɪənt/
| adj. | 过分服从的，过度遵从的 |  Inclined to comply excessively with rules or requests, sometimes to an unnecessary or harmful degree. | The company's overcompliant attitude towards regulations stifled innovation.  这家公司对规章制度过度服从的态度扼杀了创新。| 近义词: subservient, yielding | 反义词: resistant, noncompliant | 词性: adjective
