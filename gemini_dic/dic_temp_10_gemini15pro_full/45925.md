cutting-edge:/ˈkʌtɪŋ ɛdʒ/| adj.|尖端的；前沿的；领先的|The most advanced or innovative stage of development in a particular field or area. |This company is known for its cutting-edge technology. 这家公司以其尖端技术而闻名。

The hospital has invested in cutting-edge medical equipment.这家医院投资了尖端的医疗设备。


近义词：advanced, innovative, leading-edge, state-of-the-art, pioneering|反义词:outdated, obsolete, antiquated, old-fashioned|词性:形容词


词源: From the literal edge of a cutting tool, which is the working part.  The figurative meaning developed in the mid-20th century.
