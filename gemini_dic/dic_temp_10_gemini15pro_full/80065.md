goalpost:/ˈɡoʊlˌpoʊst/ (US) /ˈɡəʊlˌpəʊst/ (UK)| n. |球门柱|One of the two vertical posts that form part of a goal, especially in football (soccer).|The ball hit the goalpost and bounced out.  球击中了球门柱弹了出来。

The striker's shot rebounded off the goalpost.  前锋的射门从门柱上反弹了回来。

|近义词：upright|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
