flopper's:/ˈflɒpəz/ (US) /ˈflɒpəz/ (UK)| n. |失败者(们)的；容易失败的人(们)的；笨拙的人(们)的|Something that belongs to or is associated with a flopper (someone who flops or fails).  It's the possessive form of "flopper".|

例句：
The flopper's attempt at a slam dunk ended with him on the floor.
失败者的扣篮尝试以他摔倒在地板上而告终。

The flopper's excuse for being late was unbelievable.
这个总是失败的人迟到的借口令人难以置信。


近义词：loser's, failure's, klutz's|
反义词: winner's, success's, expert's|
词性: possessive noun (所有格名词)|
