ingenerate:/ɪnˈdʒɛnəreɪt/ | adj.| 内在的，固有的，天生的 | Existing or generated within; innate; inborn. |  例句：An ingenerate desire for knowledge.  对知识的内在渴望。

例句：Ingenerate fear of the dark.  天生对黑暗的恐惧。 | 近义词：innate, inherent, congenital, inborn | 反义词：acquired, learned, extrinsic | 词性：形容词


词源:

Late Latin *ingeneratus, from Latin in- + generatus, past participle of generare to beget — more at kin
