intermolecularly:/ˌɪntərmoʊˈlɛkjələrli/ | adv. | 分子间地 | Occurring between molecules. | The atoms are bonded intramolecularly to form a molecule, and the molecules themselves can interact intermolecularly.|  分子内的原子键合形成分子，而分子本身可以在分子间相互作用。


Hydrogen bonds can form intermolecularly between water molecules. | 水分子之间可以形成分子间氢键。


近义词: between molecules | 反义词: intramolecularly | 词性: 副词
