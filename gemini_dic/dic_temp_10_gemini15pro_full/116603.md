matrilineally:/ˌmætərɪˈlɪniəli/
adv.
母系地，通过母系
In a matrilineal society, descent and inheritance are traced through the mother's line.
在母系社会中，血统和继承是通过母系传承的。
She inherited the property matrilineally.
她通过母系继承了这笔财产。
近义词: through the female line
反义词: patrilineally
词性: adverb
