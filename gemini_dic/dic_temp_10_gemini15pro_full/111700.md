lockout: /ˈlɒkaʊt/ (UK) /ˈlɑːkaʊt/ (US)
n.|停工，闭厂；封厂； lockout|A lockout is a work stoppage initiated by the management of a company during a labor dispute.  |The company initiated a lockout after negotiations with the union failed. 公司在与工会谈判失败后，启动了停工。|
lockout: /ˈlɒkaʊt/ (UK) /ˈlɑːkaʊt/ (US)
n.|停赛，禁赛|In sports, a lockout can refer to a period when games are not played due to a dispute between owners and players.  |The NBA lockout in 1998 shortened the season. 1998年的NBA停摆缩短了赛季。|
lockout: /ˈlɒkaʊt/ (UK) /ˈlɑːkaʊt/ (US)
