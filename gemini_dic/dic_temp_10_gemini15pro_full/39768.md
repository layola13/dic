consolidator:/kənˈsɒlɪdeɪtər/ /kənˈsɑːlɪdeɪtər/| n. |合并者；统一者；债务整理人|A person or company that consolidates something, especially by combining a number of things into a single more effective or coherent whole.|The company acted as a consolidator in the fragmented software market.  这家公司在分散的软件市场中扮演了整合者的角色。|Synonyms: integrator, unifier, merger, combiner|Antonyms: divider, separator, splitter|noun

consolidate:/kənˈsɒlɪdeɪt/ /kənˈsɑːlɪdeɪt/| v. |巩固；合并；统一|To make something stronger or more solid; to combine a number of things into a single more effective or coherent whole.|The company consolidated its position in the market by buying out its main competitor.  该公司通过收购其主要竞争对手巩固了其市场地位。|Synonyms: strengthen, reinforce, secure, merge, combine|Antonyms: weaken, undermine, separate, divide|verb
