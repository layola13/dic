ostracize: /ˈɒstrəsaɪz/ (UK) /ˈɑːstrəsaɪz/ (US)
|v.|排斥，放逐；孤立|to exclude from a group by common consent|She was ostracized by her colleagues for whistleblowing.|Synonyms: shun, exclude, banish, blackball, boycott|Antonyms: include, welcome, embrace|Verb

ostracize: /ˈɒstrəsaɪz/ (UK) /ˈɑːstrəsaɪz/ (US)
|n.|排斥，放逐；孤立 (This noun form is less common)|the act of ostracizing someone|His ostracization from the community made him feel isolated.|Synonyms: exclusion, banishment|Antonyms: inclusion, welcome|Noun (less common)
