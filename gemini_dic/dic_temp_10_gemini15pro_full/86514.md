heisenberg:/ˈhaɪzənbɜːrɡ/
n.|海森堡；海森堡不确定性原理|A German physicist who made foundational contributions to quantum mechanics and is best known for the uncertainty principle, which states that it is impossible to know both the position and momentum of a particle with perfect accuracy.|Werner Heisenberg is a pivotal figure in the history of physics. 海森堡是物理学史上的关键人物。| The Heisenberg uncertainty principle is a cornerstone of quantum mechanics. 海森堡不确定性原理是量子力学的基石。|synonym: physicist, scientist|antonym: N/A|noun
