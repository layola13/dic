outgambles:/ˌaʊtˈɡæmbəlz/
verb
以赌博方式胜过；赌赢
to defeat someone by making better gambles or taking greater risks than they do.
He outgambled his opponents and won the tournament. 他在赌博中胜过对手，赢得了比赛。
outplay, outmaneuver, outsmart
