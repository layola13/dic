giggler:/ˈɡɪɡlər/| n.|咯咯笑的人；爱傻笑的人|A person who giggles frequently, especially a girl or woman.|She's such a giggler, always laughing about something. 她真是个爱傻笑的人，总是咯咯地笑个不停。|Synonyms: laugher, chuckler|Antonyms:  solemn person, serious person|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "giggle" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Giggle" comes from Middle English *gigelen, of imitative origin.
