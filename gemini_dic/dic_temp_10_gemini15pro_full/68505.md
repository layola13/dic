fanal:/fæˈnɑːl/ | n. | 灯塔，信号灯，尾灯 | A lighthouse or signal light, especially one used on ships or railroads. | The fanal flashed brightly, warning ships of the approaching shore. 灯塔闪耀着明亮的光芒，警告船只即将靠岸。 | The train's fanal was a dim red glow in the distance. 火车的尾灯在远处发出微弱的红光。 | Synonyms: beacon, signal, light | Antonyms: darkness, obscurity | noun

fanal:/fæˈnɑːl/ | adj. | 信号的，尾部的 | Relating to a signal or the rear part of something.  This usage is less common. |  The fanal apparatus of the ship was malfunctioning. 船上的信号装置发生故障。 | The fanal carriage of the train detached unexpectedly. 火车的尾部车厢意外脱落了。 | Synonyms: signaling, rear, tail | Antonyms: front, head | adjective
