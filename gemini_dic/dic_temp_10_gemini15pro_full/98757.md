intactly:/ɪnˈtæktli/| adv.|完整地，原封不动地|in a way that is not damaged or altered in any way.  It means completely whole and undamaged.|The package arrived intactly, with no signs of tampering. 包裹完好无损地到达了，没有任何被篡改的痕迹。|近义词：wholly, entirely, completely, perfectly|反义词: brokenly, damagedly, imperfectly|词性:副词
词源: From the adjective "intact" + "-ly". "Intact" comes from the Latin "intactus", meaning "untouched," from "in-" (not) + "tangere" (to touch).
