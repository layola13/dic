orycterope:/ˌɔːrɪkˈtɪrəˌpiː/ /ˌɔːrɪkˈtɪrəˌpoʊp/ | n. |土豚|A nocturnal, burrowing mammal native to Africa, with a long snout, large ears, and powerful claws for digging.
