epigenesis:/ˌɛpɪˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
n.
渐成论，后成论
The theory that an embryo develops progressively from an undifferentiated egg cell.  The development of an organism from an undifferentiated cell or cells by a series of steps in which cells differentiate and organs form.
Many studies support the theory of epigenesis. 许多研究支持渐成论。
synonyms: development, evolution, formation, growth, morphogenesis
antonyms: preformation,  preformism
noun
