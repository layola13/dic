baaing:/ˈbeɪɪŋ/ | n. | 羊叫声，咩咩叫 | The sound a sheep makes. |  The baaing of the sheep kept me awake all night. 羊的叫声让我整夜没睡。| 近义词：bleating | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a sound) | 词性: noun

baaing:/ˈbeɪɪŋ/ | adj. (less common) |  咩咩叫的 | Characterized by the sound of sheep. |  The baaing flock moved slowly across the field.  咩咩叫的羊群缓缓地穿过田野。| 近义词：bleating | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a sound) | 词性: adjective

词源：拟声词，模仿羊的叫声。
