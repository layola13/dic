overrigged:/ˌoʊvərˈrɪɡd/
adjective
装备过多的，帆具过大的
Having too much rigging or too many sails.  A ship that is overrigged is difficult to handle, especially in strong winds.
The ship was overrigged and capsized in the storm. 这艘船帆具过多，在暴风雨中倾覆了。
