hardy:/ˈhɑːrdi/ (US); /ˈhɑːdi/ (UK)| adj.|强壮的，坚强的；耐寒的|Able to survive difficult conditions, especially cold weather; strong and healthy; robust.|例句：These plants are hardy enough to survive the winter. 这些植物足够耐寒，可以过冬。|近义词：robust, sturdy, tough, resilient, strong, vigorous|反义词：fragile, weak, delicate, tender|词性:形容词
hardy:/ˈhɑːrdi/ (US); /ˈhɑːdi/ (UK)| n.|强壮的人，耐寒的动植物|A hardy person or plant.|例句：The local museum keeps an archive of information on native hardies. 当地博物馆保存着当地耐寒动植物信息的档案。|近义词：veteran, survivor|反义词: weakling|词性:名词
