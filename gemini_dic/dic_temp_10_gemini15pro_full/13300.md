athetic: /əˈθɛtɪk/
adjective
冷漠的；缺乏感情的；无动于衷的
Lacking feeling or emotion; apathetic.  Showing or feeling no enthusiasm, interest, or concern.
例句：He remained atheistic to the plight of the refugees. 他对难民的困境漠不关心。
例句：She gave an atheistic shrug. 她冷漠地耸了耸肩。
近义词：apathetic, indifferent, uninterested, unconcerned, impassive
反义词：sympathetic, compassionate, empathetic, concerned, passionate
词性: adjective


词源:  From Greek *athetos "without position or place, unsettled," also "without feeling or emotion."  From a- "without" + thetos, verbal adjective of tithenai "to put, place, set."  The meaning shifted from "unsettled" to "indifferent" in Late Latin.  A related word is athetesis "rejection of a text as spurious."
