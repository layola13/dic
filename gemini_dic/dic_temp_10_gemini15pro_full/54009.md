discriminous:/dɪˈskrɪmɪnəs/
adjective
有识别力的；有偏见的；歧视的
Showing or involving the ability to recognize small differences between things; making or showing an unfair or prejudicial distinction between different categories of people or things.
例句：She has a discriminous palate and can distinguish between subtle flavors. 她味觉敏锐，能分辨出细微的味道。

例句：The company has a discriminous hiring policy that favors younger applicants. 这家公司有歧视性的招聘政策，偏向年轻的求职者。
近义词：prejudiced, biased, discriminatory, selective, discerning
反义词：unbiased, impartial, fair, indiscriminate, undiscerning
词性: adjective
