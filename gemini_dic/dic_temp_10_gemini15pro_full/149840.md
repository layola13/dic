paleotype: /ˈpeɪliəʊtaɪp/ | n. |古型；古体；古型标本；古生物型|A paleotype is an early or original form or type of something, especially in biology, referring to an ancient specimen or form of a species. |The museum displayed a paleotype of a trilobite, an extinct marine arthropod.  博物馆展出了一种三叶虫的古型标本，这是一种已灭绝的海洋节肢动物。|Synonyms: archetype, prototype, |Antonyms: neotype|noun


paleotype: /ˈpeɪliəʊtaɪp/ | adj. |古型的；古体的| Relating to or resembling an ancient form or type. |The paleotype features of the fossil suggested it was a transitional species.  这块化石的古型特征表明它是一个过渡物种。|Synonyms: archaic, primitive |Antonyms: modern, contemporary|adjective
