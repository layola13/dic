consolator:/ˈkɒnsəleɪtə(r)/ (US) /ˈkɑːnsəleɪtər/| n. |安慰者，慰藉者|Someone or something that consoles or comforts. |例句：She was a true consolator in his time of grief.  她在他悲痛的时候是一个真正的安慰者。

例句：Music can be a great consolator during difficult times.  音乐在困难时期可以成为一种极好的慰藉。

近义词：comforter, soother, reliever |反义词:aggravator, annoyer|词性:noun
