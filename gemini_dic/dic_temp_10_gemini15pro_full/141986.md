organicalness:/ɔːrˈɡænɪkəlnəs/
|n.|
|有机性；天然性|
|The state or quality of being organic; the degree to which something is organic.|
|The organicalness of the soil contributes to the healthy growth of the plants. 土壤的有机性有助于植物的健康生长。|


|The farm boasts the organicalness of its produce, using only natural fertilizers.  这家农场以其产品的有机性为荣，只使用天然肥料。|
|近义词：naturalness, organic nature|
|反义词：artificiality, synthetic nature|
|词性：名词|



Etymology:

organic + -al + -ness

* **organic:** From Late Middle English *organik*, from Late Latin *organicus*, from Latin *organum* ("instrument, tool, organ of the body"), from Ancient Greek *ὄργανον* (*órganon*, "instrument, tool, organ").
* **-al:**  A suffix forming adjectives from nouns, meaning "of, relating to, like, or characteristic of".  From Middle English *-al*, *-el*, from Old French *-al* (Modern French *-el*), from Latin *-ālis*.
* **-ness:** A suffix forming nouns from adjectives, meaning "state, quality, or condition of being". From Old English *-nes*, *-nis*.
