emble:/ˈembləm/| n.|象征，标志，徽章|Something that visually represents something else, often an idea, group, or nation.  It can be an object, image, or symbol.|The national flag is an emblem of the country. 国旗是国家的象征。|Synonym: symbol, badge, logo, insignia|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, consider using words like "counter-symbol" or describing the lack of representation, e.g., "meaningless")|Noun
