mispackages:/ˌmɪsˈpækɪdʒɪz/
| n. | 包装错误的货物 | Goods that have been incorrectly packaged, often containing the wrong items or quantities. | The warehouse issued a recall due to numerous customer complaints about mispackages.  仓库因顾客对包装错误的货物的大量投诉而发布了召回通知。|  mispackaged items, wrongly packaged goods | correctly packaged goods | noun


mispackage:/ˌmɪsˈpækɪdʒ/
| vt. | 包装错误 | To package something incorrectly, often resulting in the wrong items or quantities being included. | The factory mispackaged several orders, leading to customer dissatisfaction. 工厂包装错了几个订单，导致顾客不满意。| mislabel, package incorrectly | package correctly | verb


mispackaged:/ˌmɪsˈpækɪdʒd/
| adj. | 被包装错误的 | Incorrectly packaged. | The mispackaged products were returned to the supplier. 包装错误的产品被退回给了供应商。 | wrongly packaged, incorrectly packaged | correctly packaged | adjective


Etymology:
mis- (prefix): Old English, meaning "wrongly," "badly," or "incorrectly."
package (noun/verb): From Middle French *pacage, ultimately from Late Latin *paccare "to pack," from Latin pacare "to pacify, appease."
