antihygienic:/ˌæntiˌhaɪˈdʒiːnɪk/ |adj.|不卫生的，有害健康的|Not hygienic; likely to promote disease or infection.|  The kitchen was in an antihygienic state, with dirty dishes piled high and food scraps everywhere. 厨房里很不卫生，脏盘子堆积如山，到处都是食物残渣。

The antihygienic conditions in the refugee camp led to an outbreak of cholera. 难民营不卫生的条件导致了霍乱的爆发。|近义词: insanitary, unhealthy, unsanitary, germ-ridden|反义词: hygienic, sanitary, clean|词性: 形容词

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek anti-)
hygienic (adjective): pertaining to hygiene, clean (from Greek hygieinos, relating to Hygieia, the goddess of health)
