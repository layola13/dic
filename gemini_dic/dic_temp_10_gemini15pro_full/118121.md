menopoma:/mɛnəˈpoʊmə/ | n. | 美洲大鲵，地狱弯曲者 | A large aquatic salamander of the eastern United States, having a broad flattened head and wrinkled skin.  It is also known as hellbender. |  例句：The menopoma is a fascinating creature to observe in its natural habitat.  美洲大鲵在其自然栖息地中是一种引人入胜的生物。

例句：The hellbender, or menopoma, is an important indicator species for healthy freshwater ecosystems.  地狱弯曲者，或美洲大鲵，是健康淡水生态系统的重要指示物种。 | 近义词：hellbender, mud-devil, snot otter | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
