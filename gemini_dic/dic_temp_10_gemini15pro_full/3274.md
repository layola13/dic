affirmed:/əˈfɜːrmd/
verb
确认；断言；证实
state emphatically or publicly that something is true or that one believes it to be true.
The suspect affirmed his innocence. 嫌疑人声称自己是无辜的。
The court affirmed the lower court's decision. 法院维持了地方法院的判决。
synonyms: assert, declare, state, maintain, aver, avow, profess, proclaim, pronounce, attest, testify, swear, vouch for, confirm, endorse, ratify, approve, sanction
antonyms: deny, contradict, refute, disavow, repudiate
v.
