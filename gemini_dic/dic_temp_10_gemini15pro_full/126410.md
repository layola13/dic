mystagogic:/ˌmɪstəˈɡɒdʒɪk/ /ˌmɪstəˈɡɑːdʒɪk/| adj.|神秘的，引人入胜的，有关宗教入会仪式的|Relating to or characteristic of a mystagogue; initiating into religious rites or mysteries; spiritually symbolic or suggestive.|The mystagogic symbolism of the ritual was lost on most of the participants.|近义词：mystical, esoteric, occult, symbolic|反义词：mundane, prosaic, clear|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From French *mystagogique*, from Late Latin *mystagogicus*, from Greek *mystagōgikos*, from *mystagōgos* "one who initiates into mysteries," from *mystagōgein* "to initiate into mysteries," from *mystēs* "one initiated into mysteries" + *agōgos* "leading, leader."
