improvise:/ˈɪmprəvaɪz/ (US) /ˈɪmprəvʌɪz/ (UK)| v. | 即兴创作，临时凑合 | To create or perform something spontaneously, without preparation. |  The musicians improvised a bluesy jam session. 音乐家们即兴演奏了一段布鲁斯音乐。

He had to improvise a shelter from branches and leaves. 他不得不临时用树枝和树叶搭建一个避难所。


|近义词：extemporize, ad-lib, wing it, make do |反义词: plan, prepare, rehearse |词性: verb
