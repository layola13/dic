mesomyodian:/ˌmɛzoʊˈmaɪoʊdiən/ |adj.|中肌型的，中肌纲的|Relating to or denoting mesomyodians, a group of bivalve molluscs characterized by a simple hinge structure and an internal nacreous layer.|Mesomyodian bivalves are found in various marine environments. 中肌纲双壳类软体动物发现于各种海洋环境中。|Near synonyms:  (No exact single-word synonyms, but related terms include) bivalve, mollusc, pelecypod|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms exist for this taxonomic term)|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From  *meso-* (middle) + *myo-* (muscle) + *-dian* (suffix forming adjectives).  Refers to the arrangement of the adductor muscles in these bivalves.
