overdainty:/ˌoʊvərˈdeɪnti/ | adj. | 过于讲究的，过分挑剔的，娇气的 | Excessively delicate or fastidious, especially in matters of taste or behavior.  Characterized by an undue sensitivity or finickiness. | She was too overdainty to eat the street food, fearing it wasn't clean enough. 她太讲究了，不敢吃街边小吃，怕不干净。|  He found her overdainty manners rather affected. 他觉得她过分讲究的举止有点做作。 | 近义词: fastidious, finicky, picky, squeamish, fussy | 反义词: unfussy, easygoing, undemanding, tolerant | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From Middle English *overdainty, from over- + dainty.  "Dainty" itself comes from Old French daintie "delight, pleasure," ultimately from Latin dignitas "worthiness, dignity." The prefix "over-" adds the sense of excess.
