mergers:/ˈmɜːrdʒərz/
n.
合并，并购
The act or process of combining two or more businesses into one.
The company announced a merger with its biggest rival.  该公司宣布与其最大的竞争对手合并。
Many smaller companies are swallowed up in mergers. 许多小公司在合并中被吞并。
synonyms: amalgamation, consolidation, union, integration
antonyms: separation, division, split, demerger
noun
