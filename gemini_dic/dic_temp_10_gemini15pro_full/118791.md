metalman: /ˈmɛtlmæn/ | n. | 金属人；钢铁侠；机器人；铁石心肠的人 | A person made of metal, or a person who works with metal, or someone unemotional or insensitive. |  The comic book featured a metalman who could bend steel with his bare hands. 这本漫画书的主角是一个可以用双手弯曲钢铁的金属人。

The metalman expertly welded the two pieces of metal together.  这位金属工匠熟练地将两块金属焊接在一起。

He's such a metalman; he didn't even flinch when he heard the bad news. 他真是个铁石心肠的人；即使听到坏消息，他也没有退缩。 | 近义词: robot, automaton, ironman, hard-hearted person | 反义词: softie, emotional person | 词性: 名词

词源:  Compound word formed from "metal" and "man". "Metal" comes from the Old French "metal" and ultimately from the Greek "metallon" (mine, quarry, metal). "Man" comes from Old English "mann".
