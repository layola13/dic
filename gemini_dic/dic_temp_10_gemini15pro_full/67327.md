extemporization:/ɪkˌstɛmpəraɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ɛkˌstɛmpərɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
即兴创作；即兴表演；即兴演讲
the act of composing, performing, or speaking extemporaneously; improvisation.
He gave a brilliant extemporization on the topic of climate change. 他就气候变化主题做了精彩的即兴演讲。

extemporization:/ɪkˌstɛmpəraɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ɛkˌstɛmpərɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
即兴作品
something extemporized, such as a musical piece or a speech.
Her extemporization on the piano was breathtaking. 她在钢琴上的即兴演奏令人叹为观止。

extemporization:/ɪkˌstɛmpəraɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ɛkˌstɛmpərɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
即兴的才能
the ability to extemporize.
His extemporization skills are highly impressive. 他的即兴创作能力令人印象深刻。


近义词: improvisation, ad-libbing, off-the-cuff remarks
反义词: preparation, rehearsal, premeditation
词性: noun
