hemoradiotherapy:/ˌhiːmoʊˌreɪdioʊˈθɛrəpi/ | n. | 放化疗，血液放射疗法 | Treatment that combines radiotherapy and chemotherapy, often used in cancer treatment.  It specifically refers to the combination of radiation therapy with systemic chemotherapy (chemotherapy that affects the whole body).| The patient underwent concurrent hemoradiation for advanced lung cancer. 该病人因晚期肺癌接受了同步放化疗。| Hemoradiotherapy is a crucial part of the treatment plan. 放化疗是治疗计划的重要组成部分。| synonyms: chemoradiotherapy, radiochemotherapy | antonyms: N/A | noun


hemoradiation:/ˌhiːmoʊˌreɪdiˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 放化疗，血液放射疗法 |  A shortened form of hemoradiatherapy, referring to the combined use of radiation therapy and chemotherapy. | The hemoradiation was successful in shrinking the tumor. 放化疗成功地缩小了肿瘤。| The doctors recommended hemoradiation as the best course of action. 医生建议放化疗为最佳疗程。| synonyms: chemoradiotherapy, radiochemotherapy, hemoradiatherapy | antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
hemo- (from Greek haima, meaning "blood") + radio- (from Latin radius, meaning "ray") + -therapy (from Greek therapeia, meaning "treatment").  So, literally "blood ray treatment," reflecting the systemic nature of the chemotherapy component affecting the bloodstream and the use of radiation.
