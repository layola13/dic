inequilateral:/ˌɪnɪˈkwɪlætərəl/
adjective
不等边的；不规则的
Having unequal sides or angles (especially of a triangle).
例句：The architect designed a building with an inequilateral facade. 建筑师设计了一座正面不等边的建筑。

例句：An inequilateral triangle has sides of different lengths.  一个不等边三角形的三条边长度不同。

近义词：asymmetrical, irregular, uneven
反义词：equilateral, regular
词性：adjective
