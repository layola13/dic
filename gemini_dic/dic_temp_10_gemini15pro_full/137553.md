nontransiently:/nɒnˈtrænziəntli/ | adv. | 非瞬时地，非短暂地 | In a manner that is not transient; persistently, enduringly.  It describes something that continues to exist or have an effect for a significant period of time, rather than fading away quickly. | The pain lingered nontransiently, lasting for several days.  疼痛持续不断，持续了几天。| Synonym: persistently, enduringly, continuously, permanently | Antonym: transiently, briefly, temporarily, fleetingly | 词性: 副词
