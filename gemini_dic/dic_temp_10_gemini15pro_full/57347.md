dowry:/ˈdaʊri/ | n. |嫁妆； приданое|  Property or money brought by a bride to her husband on their marriage.|  She brought a large dowry to the marriage. 她带着丰厚的嫁妆结婚。 | synonyms:  marriage portion, trousseau, dot | antonyms:  N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *douarie*, from Vulgar Latin *dōtārium* (“dower”), from Latin *dōs* (“dowry, endowment”).
