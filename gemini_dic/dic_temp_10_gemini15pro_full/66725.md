exorbitant:/ɪɡˈzɔːrbɪtənt/ | adj. | 过高的；过分的；| The price of the apartment was exorbitant. 公寓的价格过高。| Synonyms: excessive, outrageous, preposterous | Antonyms: reasonable, moderate, affordable | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *exorbitantia, from Latin exorbitans, present participle of exorbitare ("to deviate from the track, go out of the rut"), from ex- ("out of") + orbita ("track, rut").
