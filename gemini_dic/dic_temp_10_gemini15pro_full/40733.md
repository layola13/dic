convey:/kənˈveɪ/| v.|传达，表达，运送|to make (an idea, impression, or feeling) known or understandable to someone; to take or carry (someone or something) from one place to another|The flowers conveyed my sympathy. 这些花表达了我的同情心。|The train conveys passengers from Boston to New York. 火车把乘客从波士顿送到纽约。|synonyms: communicate, transmit, transport, carry|antonyms: withhold, suppress, retain|verb

conveyance:/kənˈveɪəns/| n.|运送，运输工具，表达|the act of conveying; something that is used to convey people or goods|The conveyance of goods by rail is efficient. 铁路运输货物效率很高。|His car is his primary means of conveyance. 他的车是他主要的交通工具。|synonyms: transportation, transmission, vehicle|antonyms: N/A|noun
