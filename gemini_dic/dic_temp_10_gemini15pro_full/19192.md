bewitching:/bɪˈwɪtʃɪŋ/
adjective
迷人的，令人着迷的
Captivating, enchanting, fascinating; having the power to charm or fascinate someone as if by magic.
Her bewitching smile captivated everyone in the room. 她迷人的微笑吸引了房间里所有的人。
The bewitching melody stayed in my head for days.  这首令人着迷的旋律在我脑海中萦绕了好几天。
近义词: enchanting, captivating, mesmerizing, alluring, fascinating
反义词: repulsive, repellent, unappealing, unattractive
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From bewitch + -ing.  Bewitch comes from the Old English *bewiccian "to use witchcraft on, enchant," from be- "thoroughly" + wiccian "to practice witchcraft."
