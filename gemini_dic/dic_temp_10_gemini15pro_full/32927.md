choots:/tʃuːts/ | n. |鞋子 (非标准英语，俚语) | Shoes (slang, non-standard English).  A shortened and informal version of "chucks," which itself is slang for "sneakers" or "athletic shoes," particularly Converse Chuck Taylor All-Stars. | Example: "Nice choots, man!"  伙计，你的鞋子真不错！ | Example: "I gotta get some new choots for the game." 我得为比赛买双新鞋。 | 近义词: sneakers, trainers, shoes, chucks | 反义词:  (No direct antonym)
