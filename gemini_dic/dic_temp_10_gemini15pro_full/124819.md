muciparous:/ˌmjʊːˈsɪpərəs/| adj.|分泌粘液的；产粘液的|Producing or secreting mucus.|The muciparous glands in the lining of the intestine secrete mucus to aid digestion.|近义词：mucous, slimy|反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps "dry")|词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin "mucus" (mucus) + "-parous" (producing).  "-parous" comes from Latin "parere" (to bear, to produce).
