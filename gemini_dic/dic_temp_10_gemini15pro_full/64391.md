eremitical:/ˌɛrɪˈmɪtɪkəl/
| adj. |隐居的，遁世的，像隐士的|Characterized by solitude and contemplation, like a hermit. |He lived an eremitical life in the mountains, far from the hustle and bustle of the city. 他在山上过着隐居的生活，远离城市的喧嚣。|The old cabin had an eremitical feel, isolated and peaceful.  这间古老的小屋有一种隐居的感觉，孤立而宁静。|近义词：reclusive, solitary, ascetic, secluded|反义词：gregarious, sociable, outgoing|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin *eremiticus*, from Greek *erēmītikos*, from *erēmitēs* "hermit," from *erēmos* "desert, lonely place."
