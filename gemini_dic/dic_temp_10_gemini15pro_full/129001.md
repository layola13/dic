nippy:/ˈnɪpi/| adj.|寒冷刺骨的；敏捷的；辛辣的|1. (of the weather or the air) cold in a way that makes you feel uncomfortable.  2. quick; nimble. 3. sharp or pungent in taste.|1. The air was nippy this morning. 今晨空气寒冷刺骨。 2.  A nippy little sports car. 一辆快速的小跑车。 3. A nippy cheese. 一种辛辣的奶酪。|近义词: chilly, brisk, quick, agile, pungent, sharp|反义词: warm, mild, slow, sluggish, bland|词性:形容词

nippy:/ˈnɪpi/| n.|A nippy person or thing, especially a small, quick-moving child or animal.|一个敏捷的人或物，尤指快速移动的小孩或动物。|The nippy little dog darted across the road. 那只敏捷的小狗飞快地穿过马路。|近义词: scamp, rascal|反义词: slowpoke|词性:名词
