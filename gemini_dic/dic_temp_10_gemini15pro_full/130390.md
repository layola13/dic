non-verifiable:/ˌnɒn ˈvɛrɪfaɪəbl/ | adj. | 不可验证的，无法核实的 | Not able to be checked or proved to be true. |  例句：The information he provided was non-verifiable, so we couldn't use it in our report. 他提供的信息无法核实，所以我们不能在报告中使用它。

例句：Many of the claims made in the advertisement were non-verifiable. 广告中的许多说法都无法证实。

近义词：unprovable, unsubstantiated, unconfirmed | 反义词：verifiable, provable, confirmed | 词性：形容词
