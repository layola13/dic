natural:/ˈnætʃrəl/ (US) /ˈnætʃʊrəl/ (UK)| adj.| 自然的，天然的；天赋的；正常的；不做作的|Existing in or caused by nature; not made or caused by humankind.  Inherent; innate. |It's perfectly natural to feel nervous before a big exam.  在大型考试前感到紧张是很自然的。|Synonyms:  normal, typical, innate, inherent, instinctive, organic, genuine|Antonyms: artificial, synthetic, unnatural, acquired, affected|adjective

natural:/ˈnætʃrəl/ (US) /ˈnætʃʊrəl/ (UK)| n.| 自然的事情；生就具有特定技能的人|Something that is natural or someone with natural talent or skill. |He's a natural at playing the piano.  他天生就擅长弹钢琴。|Synonyms:  talent, prodigy, genius, aptitude|Antonyms:  amateur, novice|noun
