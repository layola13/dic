lubricity:/luːˈbrɪsɪti/
n.
润滑性；光滑；流畅
the state or quality of being slippery or smooth; the ability to reduce friction between surfaces.
The lubricity of the oil ensured the smooth operation of the engine.  这种油的润滑性确保了发动机的平稳运转。
Synonyms: slipperiness, smoothness, slickness
Antonyms: friction, roughness, stickiness
noun
