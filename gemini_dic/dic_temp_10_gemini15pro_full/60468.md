ejaculating: /ɪˈdʒækjʊˌleɪtɪŋ/
verb
射精; (突然)说出
(of a man or male animal) eject semen from the body.  Also, to utter something suddenly and forcibly.
He ejaculated a curse. 他咒骂了一声。
He was ejaculating when he climaxed. 他达到高潮时射精了。
synonyms:  cumming,  spurting,  climaxing,  orgasming, exclaiming, bursting out, crying out
antonyms:  none (for the physiological sense).  For the utterance sense:  whispering, muttering
