chimneyed:/ˈtʃɪmnid/
adj.

Having chimneys.
The chimneyed houses lined the street, smoke curling from their rooftops.  那些有烟囱的房屋排列在街道两旁，烟雾从屋顶袅袅升起。
The old chimneyed cottage looked cozy and inviting.  古老的有烟囱的小屋看起来舒适宜人。
Near synonyms:  smokestacked, with chimneys
Antonyms:  chimneyless, without chimneys
Word Family: chimney (n.), chimney sweep (n.), chimney stack (n.)
