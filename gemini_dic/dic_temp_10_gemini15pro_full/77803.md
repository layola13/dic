generalizes:/ˈdʒɛnərəˌlaɪzɪz/
|v.|概括，归纳；推广；普及|This theory generalizes to a wider range of cases. 这个理论可以推广到更广泛的案例中。|例句：The scientist generalized from his experiments to draw a conclusion about human behavior. 科学家从他的实验中进行概括，从而得出关于人类行为的结论。|近义词：summarize, conclude, infer, extrapolate, extend|反义词：specify, particularize|词性:动词

generalizes:/ˈdʒɛnərəˌlaɪzɪz/
|v.| (指人) 妄下断论，以偏概全|It's dangerous to generalize about entire groups of people. 对整群人妄下断论是危险的。|例句：You can't generalize about all women based on your experience with one person. 你不能根据你与一个人的经历就概括所有女性。|近义词：stereotype, oversimplify|反义词：differentiate, distinguish|词性:动词
