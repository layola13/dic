overservicing:/ˌoʊvərˈsɜːrvɪsɪŋ/
n.
过度服务
The act of providing more services than are necessary or desired, often leading to increased costs and inefficiency.
Banks have been accused of overservicing wealthy clients. 银行被指责对富裕客户提供过度服务。

overservicing:/ˌoʊvərˈsɜːrvɪsɪŋ/
n.
过度维修
The act of performing more maintenance or repairs than are necessary, often as a way to generate extra revenue.
The mechanic was accused of overservicing the car.  这名机械师被指责过度维修汽车。


近义词:overdoing, excessive service
反义词:underservicing, neglect
