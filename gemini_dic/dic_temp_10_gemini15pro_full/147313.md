overmonopolizes: /ˌoʊvərˈmɒnəpəˌlaɪzɪz/ /ˌoʊvərˈmɑːnəpəˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
过度垄断
To monopolize something to an excessive degree; to control something completely and unfairly, preventing others from participating or benefiting.
The large corporation overmonopolizes the market, stifling competition and innovation.  这家大公司过度垄断了市场，扼杀了竞争和创新。
Certain companies are accused of overmonopolizing the app store market.  某些公司被指控过度垄断了应用商店市场。
近义词: dominates, controls excessively
反义词: shares, opens up
