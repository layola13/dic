glorified:/ˈɡlɔːrɪfaɪd/| adj.|美化的；赞美的；荣耀的；吹嘘的|Made to seem more important or better than it really is.  Elevated to a higher status or given undue importance, often through exaggeration or idealization.|He has a glorified job as a clerk, but it's really just data entry. 他有一份被吹嘘得很厉害的职员工作，但实际上只是数据录入。| 近义词：exalted, elevated, aggrandized, lauded, praised|反义词：debased, degraded, diminished, belittled|词性:形容词
