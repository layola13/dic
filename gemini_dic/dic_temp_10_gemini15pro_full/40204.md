contestants:/kənˈtɛstənts/ | n. |参赛者，竞争者|People who take part in a contest or competition. |The contestants for the prize all gave their best.  参赛者们为了奖项都尽力了。|  The contestants were nervous before the final round.  决赛前，参赛者们都很紧张。 |competitors, participants, candidates, rivals| opponents, adversaries | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English: from Old French contestant, present participle of contester ‘to contest,’ from Latin contestari ‘call to witness, invoke,’ from con- ‘together’ + testari ‘bear witness.’
