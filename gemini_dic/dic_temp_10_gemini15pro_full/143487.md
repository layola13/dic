outgive: /ˌaʊtˈɡɪv/
verb
超过…的捐赠；比…捐赠更多
to surpass in giving; to give more than
He outgave all the other donors to the charity. 他给慈善机构的捐款超过了所有其他捐赠者。
Synonyms: outdo, surpass, exceed
Antonyms: be outdone, be surpassed
词性: 动词
