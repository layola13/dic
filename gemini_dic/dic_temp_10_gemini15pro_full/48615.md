defectors:/dɪˈfɛktərz/
n.
叛逃者，变节者
People who abandon their country or cause for another.
Many defectors from the communist regime sought asylum in the West. 许多从共产主义政权叛逃的人都到西方寻求政治庇护。
synonyms: deserters, traitors, renegades, turncoats
antonyms: loyalists, patriots
