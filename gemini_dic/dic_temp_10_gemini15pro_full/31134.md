chancery: /ˈtʃɑːnsəri/ (US) /ˈtʃɑːnsəri/ (UK)| n.| 大法官法庭；衡平法院；档案室；档案馆；（某些国家的）大使馆|A court of equity, as distinguished from a common-law court.  A place where public records or historical documents are kept.  The office or department of a chancellor. In some countries, the building or offices occupied by an embassy.|例句：The case was heard in chancery.  这个案件在大法官法庭审理。

例句：The documents were found in the chancery archives. 这些文件是在档案馆里找到的。

近义词：court, tribunal, record office, archives, embassy|反义词:|词性:noun


chancery: /ˈtʃɑːnsəri/ (US) /ˈtʃɑːnsəri/ (UK)| adj. (古旧用法)| 大法官法庭的；衡平法的|Relating to or proceeding from a court of chancery or equity.|例句：It was a chancery case.  这是一个衡平法案件。

例句：They sought a chancery remedy. 他们寻求衡平法的补救措施。
