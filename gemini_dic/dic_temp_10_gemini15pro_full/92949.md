ideographic:/ˌaɪdiəˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. | 表意的；象形的 | Relating to or denoting a system of writing in which each character represents an idea or concept. |  Chinese characters are ideographic. 汉字是表意的。 |  The ancient Egyptians used an ideographic script. 古埃及人使用象形文字。 | synonyms: symbolic, pictorial, emblematic | antonyms: phonetic, alphabetic | adjective
