n.

The Andes form the longest cordillera in the world.  安第斯山脉形成了世界上最长的科迪勒拉山系。
The climbers attempted to scale the treacherous peaks of the cordillera.  登山者试图攀登科迪勒拉山脉险峻的山峰。
