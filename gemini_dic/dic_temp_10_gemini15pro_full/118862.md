metapode: /ˈmɛtəˌpoʊd/| n. |后足, 跖部|The metapode is the part of an animal's limb between the ankle or wrist and the digits. |The horse's metapode sustained an injury during the race.  这匹马的跖部在比赛中受伤了。|Near synonyms: metatarsus, metacarpus|Antonym: (No direct antonym, potentially proximal limb segments)|noun

metapode: /ˈmɛtəˌpoʊd/| n. |掌骨, 跖骨|In anatomy, metapode refers to the bones of the hand (metacarpals) or foot (metatarsals) between the wrist/ankle and the fingers/toes.|The fracture was located in the metapode of the bird's wing.  骨折位于鸟翅膀的掌骨。|Near synonyms: metacarpals, metatarsals|Antonym: (No direct antonym, potentially carpus/tarsus)|noun
