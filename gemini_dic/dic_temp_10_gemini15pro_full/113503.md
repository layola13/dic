ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English _hwæt_, neuter of _hwā_ ("who"), from Proto-Germanic *_hwat_, from Proto-Indo-European *_kʷod_. Cognates include German _was_, Dutch _wat_, Latin _quod_, and Sanskrit _kad_.
