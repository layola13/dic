conservancies:/kənˈsɜːrvənsiz/
n.
自然保护区；(尤指)野生动物保护区
areas of land, often with plants or animals of a particular type, that are protected
例句：Many conservancies are working to protect endangered species. 许多自然保护区正在努力保护濒危物种。

例句：The conservancies in Africa play a vital role in preserving wildlife.  非洲的自然保护区在保护野生动物方面发挥着至关重要的作用。

近义词：reserves, sanctuaries, preserves
反义词：N/A
词性：名词
