actor:/ˈæktər/ (US) /ˈæktə(r)/ (UK)| n.|演员|A person who performs in plays, movies, or television shows.|He's a talented actor. 他是一位才华横溢的演员。

The lead actor gave a powerful performance.  主角的表演非常精彩。

近义词:performer, player, artist|反义词:audience, spectator|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *actor*, from *agō* "I do, I drive, I act."
