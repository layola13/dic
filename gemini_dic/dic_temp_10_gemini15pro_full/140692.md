omittance: /əˈmɪtəns/
n.
遗漏；省略；疏忽
The omission of her name from the list was a mistake.
她名字的遗漏是错误的。
The report was criticized for its omission of key facts.
报告因省略关键事实而受到批评。
近义词: exclusion, oversight, neglect, failure
反义词: inclusion, addition
