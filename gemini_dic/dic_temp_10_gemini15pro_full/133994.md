nonhits:/ˈnɒnhɪts/ (US) /ˈnɒnhɪts/ (UK)| n.|未命中; 未击中; 未成功; (网络搜索)不匹配的结果|A nonhit is something that is not a hit, such as a baseball swing that misses the ball, a song that isn't popular, or a web search that doesn't return relevant results.|The batter swung hard but it was a nonhit. 击球手用力挥棒，但没有击中。|Near synonyms: miss, failure, unsuccessful attempt|Antonyms: hit, success, triumph|noun
