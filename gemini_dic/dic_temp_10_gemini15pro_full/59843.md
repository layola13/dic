ectoplasm:/ˈɛktəˌplæzəm/| n. | 外质；细胞外质 |  A viscous, gelatinous substance believed to emanate from the body of a medium during a spiritualistic trance.  A substance that is supposed to surround all living cells. | The medium claimed that the ghostly figure was formed of ectoplasm.  这灵媒声称鬼魂是由外质构成的。| The amoeba extended a pseudopod of ectoplasm to engulf its prey. 变形虫伸出外质伪足吞噬猎物。| synonyms:  protoplasm, cytoplasm | antonyms: None | noun
