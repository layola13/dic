goitrigenous: /ˌɡɔɪtrɪˈdʒɛnəs/
adjective
致甲状腺肿的; 由甲状腺肿引起的
Causing goiter; originating in the goiter.
Goitrigenous substances in the diet can interfere with thyroid function.  饮食中致甲状腺肿的物质会干扰甲状腺功能。
近义词: goitrogenic
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider terms like "thyroid-supporting" or "anti-goitrogenic")
词性: adjective
