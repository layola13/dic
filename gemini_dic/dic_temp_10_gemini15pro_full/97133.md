infiltrating:/ˈɪnfɪltreɪtɪŋ/
verb| 渗透，潜入|Entering or gaining access to a place or organization surreptitiously, often with hostile intent.|The rebels were infiltrating the government army. 叛军正在渗透政府军。|Synonyms: permeating, penetrating, invading|Antonyms: withdrawing, retreating|v.

infiltrating:/ˈɪnfɪltreɪtɪŋ/
adjective| 渗透的，潜入的|Relating to or characterized by infiltration.|The infiltrating agent was discovered and arrested.  潜入的特工被发现并逮捕了。|Synonyms: pervasive, invasive, permeating|Antonyms: superficial, external|adj.
