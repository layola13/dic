longipennate:/ˌlɒndʒɪˈpɛneɪt/ /ˌlɔːndʒɪˈpɛneɪt/
adjective
长翼的，长翅的
Having long wings or feathers.  Specifically refers to birds with long, pointed wings adapted for soaring and gliding.
例句：The albatross is a longipennate bird, capable of remaining aloft for hours. 信天翁是一种长翼鸟，能够在高空停留数小时。

例句：Longipennate birds are often seen soaring effortlessly above the ocean. 长翼鸟经常被看到毫不费力地在海洋上空翱翔。
近义词：long-winged
反义词: short-winged (not a formally recognized word, but conveys the opposite meaning)
词性: adjective
