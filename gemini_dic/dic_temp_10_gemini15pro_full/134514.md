nonliberal:/ˌnɑːnˈlɪbərəl/ | adj. | 非自由主义的；非自由派的 | Not liberal; opposed to liberal principles; illiberal. |  Many nonliberal governments are authoritarian. 许多非自由主义政府都是专制的。|  The nonliberal policies of the regime drew criticism from international human rights organizations. 该政权的非自由主义政策招致了国际人权组织的批评。| 近义词: illiberal, conservative, reactionary, authoritarian | 反义词: liberal, progressive, left-wing | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  From Latin *non "not."  Indicates negation or absence.
liberal (root):  From Latin liberalis "noble, befitting a freeman, generous," from liber "free."
