bestowing:/bɪˈstoʊɪŋ/
verb
授予，给予
presenting as a gift or an honor; conferring.
The queen is bestowing knighthoods on several distinguished scientists. 女王将授予几位杰出科学家爵位。
近义词:conferring, granting, awarding
反义词:withholding, denying, revoking
词性:verb (动名词)

bestow:/bɪˈstoʊ/
verb
授予，给予
confer (a title, right, or honour) on someone.
The university bestowed an honorary degree on the renowned author. 大学授予这位著名作家荣誉学位。
近义词:confer, grant, award
反义词:withhold, deny, revoke
词性:verb

Etymology:
From Middle English *bistowen*, from Old English *bestandan* (“to bestow, give, apply, use, occupy”), equivalent to *be-* +‎ *stand*. Cognate with Dutch *besteden* (“to spend, employ”), German *bestehen* (“to exist, consist of, pass”). More at stand.
