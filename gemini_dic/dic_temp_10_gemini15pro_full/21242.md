blom:/blʊm/ | n. | 花；开花；最盛期；青春；健康有光彩的脸色| A flower, especially one cultivated for its beauty.  The state or period of flowering. The time of greatest beauty, vigor, or influence. A healthy, rosy complexion. | The cherry trees are in full blom. 樱花正盛开。| synonyms: blossom, flower, bloom, efflorescence | antonyms: decay, decline, wither, fade | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *blome, from Old French blome, from Frankish *blōmō, from Proto-Germanic *blōmô ("flower, blossom"). Cognate with Old High German bluoma (German Blume), Old English blōma (English bloom).
