barracuda:/ˌbærəˈkuːdə/ | n. |梭鱼；|A large, predatory ray-finned fish found in tropical and subtropical oceans worldwide, known for its elongated body, powerful jaws, and sharp teeth. |例句：The barracuda flashed its sharp teeth as it swam past. 梭鱼游过时露出了锋利的牙齿。

例句：Barracuda are known to be aggressive hunters. 梭鱼是出了名的凶猛猎手。|近义词：mackerel, pike|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑prey猎物) |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *barracuda*, from Taíno *barrakúda*.
