oxalic:/ɒkˈsælɪk/ | adj. | 草酸的；含草酸的 | Relating to, derived from, or containing oxalic acid. |  Oxalic acid is found in many plants, including spinach and rhubarb. 草酸存在于许多植物中，包括菠菜和大黄。|  Oxalic acid can be toxic in large amounts. 大量摄入草酸是有毒的。 | synonymous:  sour, acidic | antonymous: alkaline, basic | adjective
