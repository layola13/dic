chattelism: /ˈtʃætəlɪzəm/
n.
奴隶制，动产制
The system of treating people as chattels or property.
Slavery was a brutal system of chattelism. 奴隶制是一种残酷的动产制。
近义词: slavery, bondage, enslavement
反义词: freedom, liberty
词性: noun
