noncomplying:/ˌnɒnkəmˈplaɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 不遵守的，不顺从的 | Not complying with rules, standards, or requirements. |  The noncomplying factories were shut down by the authorities.  当局关闭了不符合规定的工厂。 | synonyms: disobedient, rebellious, resistant, defiant, recalcitrant | antonyms: compliant, obedient, conforming, submissive | adjective

noncomplying:/ˌnɑːnkəmˈplaɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 不遵守的，不顺从的 | Not complying with rules, standards, or requirements. |  Several noncomplying businesses were fined heavily.  几家不遵守规定的企业被处以重罚。 | synonyms: disobedient, rebellious, resistant, defiant, recalcitrant | antonyms: compliant, obedient, conforming, submissive | adjective

词源:
From non- + complying, the present participle of comply.  "Comply" comes from the Old French "complir," meaning "to fulfill, complete," ultimately from the Latin "complēre," meaning "to fill up, complete."
