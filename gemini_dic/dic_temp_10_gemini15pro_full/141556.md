opposes:/əˈpoʊzɪz/
verb
反对，抵制
resists or is against something or someone.
The senator opposes the new tax law.  参议员反对新的税法。
近义词:resists, objects to, challenges, combats,  fights
反义词:supports, favors, advocates, promotes, champions
词性:verb
