availability: /əˌveɪləˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可用性，可获得性 | The availability of fresh produce varies throughout the year.  新鲜农产品的供应情况全年都在变化。|  The availability of affordable housing is a major issue in this city.  经济适用房的供应是这个城市的一个主要问题。 | 近义词: accessibility, attainability, presence | 反义词: unavailability, scarcity, absence | 词性: 名词

词源:  From available + -ity.  Available comes from the Old French *available, from the verb *availer "to be of use, profit," which itself comes from the Latin advalere "to be strong, to be worth."
