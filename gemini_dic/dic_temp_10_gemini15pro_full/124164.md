mortuary:/ˈmɔːrtʃʊəri/ (US) /ˈmɔːtʃʊəri/ (UK)| n. |太平间；殡仪馆|A place where dead bodies are kept before burial or cremation.|The body was taken to the mortuary after the accident. 尸体在事故发生后被送往太平间。|Synonym: morgue, funeral home|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "living quarters" in a contrasting sense) |noun


mortuary:/ˈmɔːrtʃʊəri/ (US) /ˈmɔːtʃʊəri/ (UK)| adj. |死亡的；丧葬的|Relating to death or burial.|He worked in the mortuary affairs office. 他在丧葬事务办公室工作。|Synonym:  funereal, deathly|Antonym: vital, lively |adjective
