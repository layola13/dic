linnaeans:/lɪˈniːənz/
n.|林奈学派; 林奈分类法的拥护者|Followers or supporters of the taxonomic system developed by Carl Linnaeus.|Many linnaeans resisted the new cladistic approach to taxonomy.|近义词:taxonomists, biologists|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly opponents of Linnaean taxonomy)|词性:名词

linnaean:/lɪˈniːən/
adj.|林奈的; 与林奈有关的;  (尤指动植物分类)林奈体系的|Relating to Carl Linnaeus or his system of classifying living things.|The linnaean system of classification is based on hierarchical categories.|近义词:taxonomic, Linnean (noun form as synonym for linnaeans)|反义词:(No direct antonym, possibly non-Linnaean or cladistic)|词性:形容词
