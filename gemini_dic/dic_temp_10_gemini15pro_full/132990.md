nonenergetically:/ˌnɒnˌɛnəˈdʒɛtɪkli/
| adv. | 非能量地；不积极地；不费力地 |  In a way that does not involve or require much energy or effort. |  He moved nonenergetically, as if exhausted. 他行动迟缓，好像筋疲力尽。|  lethargically, listlessly, apathetically | energetically, vigorously, actively | adverb

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin *non < *noenum < ne oinom "not one thing."
energetic (adjective): From French *énergétique* and directly from Late Latin *energeticus*, from Greek *energetikos* "active, operative," from *energes* "active, working," from *en- "in" + *ergon* "work."
-ally (suffix): Forming adverbs from adjectives, from Old English *-lice*, from Proto-Germanic *-liko*, related to Old English *lic* "body, form."
