aligners:/əˈlaɪnərz/| n.|矫正器，对准器|Devices, typically made of plastic, used to straighten teeth by applying gentle pressure.  They are a popular alternative to traditional braces.|She started wearing clear aligners to correct her overbite. 她开始戴透明矫正器来矫正她的覆咬。|Synonyms: braces, retainers|Antonyms: N/A|noun
