mortgaging:/ˈmɔːrɡɪdʒɪŋ/
v.
抵押；以…作抵押
The act of using property as security for a loan.
They are mortgaging their home to start a business. 他们正在抵押他们的房子来创业。
近义词: pledging, pawning
反义词: redeeming
