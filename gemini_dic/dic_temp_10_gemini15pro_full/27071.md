calgarian: /kælˈɡɛəriən/ | n. | 卡尔加里人；卡尔加里居民 | A person who lives in or comes from Calgary, Alberta, Canada. |  She's a proud Calgarian, born and raised. 她是一位土生土长的卡尔加里人，为此感到自豪。|  Calgary resident, Calgarian citizen |  None | noun
Etymology:  Derived from Calgary, the name of the city.  Calgary's name itself comes from the Gaelic name *Cala Gairraidh*, meaning "bay farm" or "garden cove".
