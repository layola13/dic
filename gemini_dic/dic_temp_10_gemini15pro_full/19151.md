bevyest:/ˈbɛviɪst/ | adj. | 最多的，最大群的 |  This word is likely a superlative form of "bevy" intended to mean "largest bevy" or "most numerous bevy".  However, it's non-standard and not found in established dictionaries.  "Bevy" itself refers to a large group, especially of birds or women.  Using "bevyest" is not recommended in formal writing. |  (Example using "bevy" instead)  The meadow was filled with the bevy of butterflies. 草地上满是蝴蝶。  (Example using "largest bevy" instead)  The largest bevy of quail I've ever seen gathered near the feeder. 我见过的最大的鹌鹑群聚集在喂食器附近。 | 近义词: largest, most numerous, greatest | 反义词: smallest, fewest, least | 词性: 形容词


词源: Likely derived from "bevy" (from Anglo-French *bevee, meaning "drink, drinking bout") which later evolved to mean a group or company.  "-est" is a common superlative suffix.
