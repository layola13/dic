affectionated:/əˈfekʃənət/ | adj. | 深情的，充满爱的 | Showing, feeling, or characterized by affection; loving. | She gave her dog an affectionate pat. 她深情地拍了拍她的狗。|
近义词: loving, tender, fond, caring, warm, devoted | 反义词: cold, indifferent, uncaring, aloof | 词性: 形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin *affectionatus, from Latin affect- ‘disposed towards’ (see affect) + -atus.
