dentate:/ˈdɛnˌteɪt/
adjective
齿状的；有齿的
Having a toothed margin or tooth-like projections.
The leaf margins are dentate. 叶子边缘呈锯齿状。

dentate:/ˈdɛnˌteɪt/
adjective
齿状的；有齿的
Toothed; notched.
The fern had dentate fronds.  这种蕨类植物有齿状的叶子。


dentate:/ˈdɛnˌteɪt/
adjective
齿状的；有齿的
Resembling a tooth in shape or having teeth.
The dentate edge of the saw made quick work of the wood. 锯子齿状的边缘很快就把木头锯开了。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin dentātus (“toothed”), past participle of dentō (“I furnish with teeth”), from dens (“tooth”).

SYNONYMS->
toothed, serrated, jagged, crenate

ANTONYMS->
smooth, even, entire
