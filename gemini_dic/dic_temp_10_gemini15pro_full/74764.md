PHRASE->
freeze over: (湖、河等)结冰  The pond froze over last night. 池塘昨晚结冰了。
freeze to death:  冻死  He almost froze to death in the blizzard. 他在暴风雪中差点冻死。
freeze up:  (机器、引擎等)因结冰而停止运转  The engine froze up in the cold weather. 发动机在寒冷的天气里冻住了。
freeze someone out:  排挤某人  They froze her out of the conversation. 他们把她排除在谈话之外。
freeze assets: 冻结资产  The government froze the company's assets. 政府冻结了公司的资产。
