mnemonical: /nɪˈmɒnɪkəl/ (US) /nɪˈmɒnɪkl/ (UK) | adj. | 助记的; 记忆的 | Assisting or intended to assist memory. |  She used mnemonical devices to help her remember people's names. 她用助记法来帮助她记住人们的名字。 | Example:  He created a mnemonical rhyme to memorize the periodic table. 他创作了一首助记韵文来记住元素周期表。 | Synonyms: memory-aiding, mnemonic | Antonyms: forgetful | Adjective

mnemonically: /nɪˈmɒnɪkli/ (US) /nɪˈmɒnɪkli/ (UK)| adv. |  记忆地; 助记地 | In a way that assists memory. | Example: The information was presented mnemonically, using vivid imagery and rhymes.  这些信息是用生动的图像和押韵的方式来呈现的，以便于记忆。 | Synonyms: memorably | Antonyms: forgettably | Adverb
