nurserymen:/ˈnɜːrs(ə)rimən/
noun
苗圃工人；园艺师
A person who owns or works in a plant nursery, cultivating and selling young plants.
The nurserymen grafted the apple trees to produce a new variety. 苗圃工人嫁接苹果树以培育新品种。
Synonyms:  gardeners, horticulturalists, plantsmen
Antonyms: customer, buyer
n.
