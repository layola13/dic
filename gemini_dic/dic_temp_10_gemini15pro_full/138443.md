nouveau:/ˈnuːvəʊ (UK), nuːˈvoʊ (US)| adj.|新近到达的；新近生产的；新颖的；新式的|Newly arrived or recently produced; modern; up-to-date|例句：The restaurant has a nouveau riche clientele. 这家餐厅的顾客都是些暴发户。|近义词：new, modern, contemporary, recent, fresh, novel, original|反义词：old, traditional, ancient, established|词性:形容词

词源: From French *nouveau*, masculine form of *nouvelle* "new," from Latin *novellus*, diminutive of *novus* "new."
