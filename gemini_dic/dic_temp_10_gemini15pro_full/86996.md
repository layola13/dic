hennon:/ˈhɛnən/ | adj. n. |  亨农的；与亨农吸引子相关的；亨农吸引子 |  Relating to the Hénon map, a discrete-time dynamical system exhibiting chaotic behavior. It's commonly used as a test case for chaotic dynamics. Hénon also refers to the Hénon attractor, a strange attractor generated by this map. | The Hénon map is a classic example of a chaotic system. 亨农映射是混沌系统的典型例子。

The Hénon attractor is a fractal structure. 亨农吸引子是一种分形结构。| 近义词: chaotic (when referring to the map) | 反义词: stable, predictable (when referring to the map) | 词性: adjective, noun
