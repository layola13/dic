intentionality:/ɪnˌtenʃəˈnæləti/
n.|意向性，目的性|The quality of mental states (e.g., thoughts, beliefs, desires, hopes) that consists in their being directed toward some object or state of affairs.|She spoke with clear intentionality, making sure her message was understood. 她说话的目的性很明确，确保她的信息被理解。|Synonyms: purposefulness, purposiveness, design, intent, aim|Antonyms: aimlessness, randomness, purposelessness|noun

intentionality:/ɪnˌtenʃəˈnæləti/
n.|故意|The fact of being intended or planned; deliberate.|The police are investigating the intentionality of the fire. 警方正在调查火灾是否故意造成。|Synonyms: deliberateness,  premeditation|Antonyms: accident, chance, coincidence|noun
