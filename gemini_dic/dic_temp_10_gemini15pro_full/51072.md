despairful:/dɪˈspeərfəl/
adjective
绝望的，充满绝望的
Filled with or expressing despair.
She cast a despairful glance at the locked door. 她绝望地看了一眼锁着的门。
近义词:despondent, hopeless, desperate
反义词:hopeful, optimistic
词性:adjective
词源:From despair + -ful.
