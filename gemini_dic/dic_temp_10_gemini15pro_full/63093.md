ensemble:/ɑːnˈsɑːmbl/ /ɒnˈsɒmbl/| n.|全体，整体；剧团；合奏组|A group of musicians, actors, or dancers who perform together.  A complete outfit of clothing. A group of items viewed as a whole rather than individually. |The string ensemble played beautifully.  弦乐团演奏得非常优美。

She bought a new ensemble for the wedding. 她为婚礼买了一套新衣服。

The building, gardens, and sculptures make a pleasing ensemble.  建筑、花园和雕塑构成了一幅赏心悦目的景象。|近义词：group, collection, troupe, outfit, set|反义词: individual, solo|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French ensemble, from en- (“in”) + sembler (“to seem”).  Ultimately from Latin simul (“together”).
