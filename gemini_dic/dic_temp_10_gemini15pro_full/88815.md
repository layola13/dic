hogan:/ˈhoʊɡɑːn/| n.|霍根(纳瓦霍人传统的民居)|A traditional Navajo dwelling, usually made of logs and earth.|Example: The family gathered in their hogan for the evening meal.  家人聚集在他们的霍根屋里吃晚饭。|Example:  The hogan provided shelter from the harsh desert climate. 霍根屋提供了躲避恶劣沙漠气候的庇护所。|Synonym: dwelling, house, lodge|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, could be considered opposite of "homelessness")|noun
