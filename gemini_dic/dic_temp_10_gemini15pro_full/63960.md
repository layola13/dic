episiotomies:/ɪˌpɪziˈɒtəmiz/ /ɪˌpɪziˈɑːtəmiz/| n.| 外阴切开术；会阴切开术 |A surgical cut made at the opening of the vagina during childbirth, to aid a difficult delivery and prevent rupture of tissues.|The doctor performed an episiotomy to prevent tearing during the delivery. 医生进行了外阴切开术以防止分娩时撕裂。| Synonyms: perineotomy | Antonyms: None | Noun
