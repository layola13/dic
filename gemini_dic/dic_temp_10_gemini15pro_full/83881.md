half-altered:/ˌhæf ˈɔːltərd/ | adj. | 半改变的，部分改变的 | Partly or incompletely changed; modified to some extent but not completely. | The building was half-altered, with some parts modernized and others left untouched.  这栋建筑半改造了，一些部分现代化了，其他部分保持原样。| The recipe was half-altered, using some of the original ingredients and adding some new ones.  食谱被部分修改了，使用了一些原始配料并添加了一些新配料。 | 近义词: partially changed, partly modified, semi-altered | 反义词: completely altered, fully modified, totally changed | 词性: 形容词

half:/hæf/
altered:/ˈɔːltərd/
词源: "half-" comes from Old English "healf." "altered" comes from the Old French "alterer," ultimately from Latin "alterare," meaning "to change."
