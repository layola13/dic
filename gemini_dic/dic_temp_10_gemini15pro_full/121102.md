miscreating:/ˌmɪskrɪˈeɪtɪŋ/
Verb
错误地创造；歪曲；败坏
To create badly or improperly; to distort or pervert.
He miscreated the character, turning a hero into a villain. 他歪曲了这个角色，把一个英雄变成了一个恶棍。
Near Synonyms: distorting, perverting, corrupting, deforming
Antonyms: perfecting, improving, enhancing
Word type: Verb
