cup-tie:/ˈkʌp taɪ/ |n.| 杯赛;淘汰赛|A match in a knockout competition, especially in football (soccer).|例句：The team was eliminated from the cup-tie in the first round.  这支队伍在杯赛第一轮就被淘汰了。

例句：They won their cup-tie against a lower-league team. 他们赢了与低级别联赛球队的杯赛。|近义词：knockout match, elimination match|反义词:league match|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->

From "cup" + "tie".  "Tie" in this context refers to a match where the winner progresses and the loser is eliminated.  The term originated in association with football (soccer) competitions where a trophy (cup) is awarded to the winner.
