flooding:/ˈflʌdɪŋ/| n.|洪水；泛滥；涌入|An overflowing of water onto land that is normally dry.  The heavy rain caused widespread flooding.|例句：The flooding caused extensive damage to homes and businesses. 洪水对房屋和企业造成了广泛的破坏。

例句：The town experienced severe flooding after the hurricane.  飓风过后，小镇经历了严重的洪水。|近义词：inundation, deluge, overflow|反义词:drought, drainage|词性:noun

flood:/flʌd/| n. v.|洪水；泛滥；涌入；淹没|A large amount of water covering an area that is usually dry. / To cover or submerge with a large amount of water.|例句：The river flooded its banks after the heavy rain.  大雨过后，河水漫过了河岸。

例句：The basement flooded during the storm.  暴风雨期间地下室被淹了。|近义词：inundate, deluge, overflow|反义词:drain, recede|词性:noun, verb
