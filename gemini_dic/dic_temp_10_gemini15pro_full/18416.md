belswagger:/ˈbɛlˌswæɡər/
verb
大摇大摆；耀武扬威
to walk or behave in a way that shows you are very confident and important, often in a way that annoys other people
He belswaggered into the room, demanding attention. 他大摇大摆地走进房间，要求大家注意他。
近义词: strut, swagger, prance
反义词: cower, shrink
词性: verb
