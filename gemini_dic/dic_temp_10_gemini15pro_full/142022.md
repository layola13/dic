organizer:/ˈɔːrɡənaɪzər/ (US) /ˈɔːɡənaɪzə(r)/ (UK)| n.|组织者；安排者；整理器|A person or thing that arranges something. |例句：The organizer of the conference did a fantastic job. 会议的组织者做得非常出色。

例句：She's a very efficient organizer. 她是一位非常高效的组织者。|近义词：planner, coordinator, arranger|反义词: disorganizer, mess maker|Noun
