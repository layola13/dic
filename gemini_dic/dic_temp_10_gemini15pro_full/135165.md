nonopposing:/ˌnɒnəˈpəʊzɪŋ/
adjective
不反对的，不抵抗的
Not opposing; offering no resistance.
The nonopposing faction surrendered peacefully. 不抵抗派和平地投降了。
Synonyms: nonresistant, passive, acquiescent, compliant, yielding
Antonyms: opposing, resistant, combative, antagonistic
词性: 形容词
