hebraism:/ˈhiːbreɪɪzəm/ | n. |希伯来语的表达方式；希伯来风格|A characteristic feature of the Hebrew language; a Hebrew idiom or expression; a mode of thought or style of writing characteristic of the Hebrews. |The author's prose was full of hebraisms, reflecting his deep study of the Old Testament.  作者的散文充满了希伯来语的表达方式，反映了他对《旧约》的深入研究。|synonyms:  Hebraic idiom, Hebrew expression |antonyms: Hellenism|noun
