counterpetitioned:/ˌkaʊntər pəˈtɪʃənd/
|v.|反诉，反请求|To file a petition in response to another petition, usually opposing it.|The company counterpetitioned, arguing that the original claim was frivolous.|Synonyms: counterclaim, oppose, challenge|Antonyms: support, endorse|verb
