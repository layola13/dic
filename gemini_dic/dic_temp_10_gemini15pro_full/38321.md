concede:/kənˈsiːd/ /kənˈsid/ | v. | 承认，让步，给予 | to admit that something is true or valid; to surrender or yield something |例句：He conceded defeat in the election. 他承认在选举中失败了。 |例句：The government conceded to the protesters' demands. 政府对示威者的要求做出了让步。 | 近义词：admit, acknowledge, grant, yield | 反义词：deny, refuse, dispute | verb

concede:/kənˈsiːd/ /kənˈsid/ | v. | 容许 | to allow someone to have something, even if you do not want to |例句：He conceded the point to his opponent. 他把这一点让给了他的对手。 |例句：She conceded that she might have been wrong. 她承认她可能是错的。 | 近义词：allow, grant | 反义词：deny, refuse | verb
