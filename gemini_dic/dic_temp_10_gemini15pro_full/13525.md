ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *ateinte*, past participle of *ateindre* "to reach, attain, touch," from Latin *attingere*, from *ad* "to" + *tangere* "to touch."  The legal sense developed from the idea of "reaching" or "attacking" someone's reputation or legal standing.
