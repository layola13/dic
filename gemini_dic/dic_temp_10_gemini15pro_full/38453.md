concessionaries:/kənˈseʃəneriz/
n.
特许经营者， concessionaire 的复数形式
People who have been granted a concession, especially to operate a business within a particular area.
The concessionaries at the national park run the restaurants and gift shops. 国家公园的特许经营者经营着餐厅和礼品店。
synonyms: franchisees, licensees, operators, vendors
antonyms:  N/A (No direct antonym for a person holding a concession)
noun


concessionaire:/kənˌseʃəˈner/
n.
特许经营者
A person or company to whom a concession has been granted, especially to operate a business within a particular area.
The concessionaire for the stadium's food vendors raised prices this year.  体育场食品摊贩的特许经营者今年提高了价格。
synonyms: franchisee, licensee, operator, vendor
antonyms: N/A (No direct antonym for a person holding a concession)
noun
