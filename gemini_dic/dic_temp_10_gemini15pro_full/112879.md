luculent:/ˈluːkjʊlənt/| adj.|清晰的，明白易懂的；（文章）流畅的；明亮的|Clear, easy to understand; (of speech or writing) fluent and elegant;  bright or luminous.|Her luculent prose made even the most complex scientific concepts accessible to the general reader. 她清晰流畅的散文使普通读者也能理解最复杂的科学概念。|synonyms: clear, lucid, articulate, eloquent, transparent, luminous|antonyms: obscure, opaque, unclear, ambiguous, inarticulate, murky|adjective
