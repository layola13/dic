inflow:/ˈɪnfloʊ/| n.|流入，注入；流入物|The inflow of foreign investment has boosted the local economy. 外国投资的流入促进了当地经济。|例句：The dam regulates the inflow of water into the river.  大坝调节了流入河流的水量。

近义词： influx, arrival, intake|反义词: outflow, efflux|词性:名词

inflow:/ˈɪnfloʊ/|词源：in- (into) + flow (流动)|
