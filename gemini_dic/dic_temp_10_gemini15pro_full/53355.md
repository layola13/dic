disadvise:/ˌdɪsədˈvaɪz/ | vt. |劝阻，建议不要|To recommend against; to counsel someone not to do something.| I strongly disadvised him from investing in that company. 我强烈建议他不要投资那家公司。|The doctor disadvised her from travelling while she was ill. 医生建议她生病期间不要旅行。| synonyms:  caution against, warn against, deprecate, discourage | antonyms: advise, encourage, recommend | verb
