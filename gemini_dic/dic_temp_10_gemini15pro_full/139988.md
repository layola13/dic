odourful:/ˈəʊdəfʊl/ (UK) /ˈoʊdərfʊl/ (US)| adj.|有气味的，有香味的，（常指）难闻的|Having a distinct odour, often unpleasant.  The adjective can describe both pleasant and unpleasant smells, but in modern usage, it more frequently leans towards unpleasant smells.|例句：The odourful cheese filled the room.  房间里充满了难闻的奶酪味。

例句：The odourful blossoms of the jasmine perfumed the night air. 浓郁的茉莉花香弥漫在夜空中。


近义词：aromatic, fragrant, smelly, pungent, musty, fetid|反义词: odourless, scentless, unscented|词性:adjective


词源: From Middle English *odourful, equivalent to odour +‎ -ful.
