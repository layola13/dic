octopodia: /ɒkˈtɒpədiə/ /ɑːkˈtoʊpədiə/
| n. |章鱼（复数）|Plural form of octopus, referring to more than one octopus. octopi: /ˈɒktəpaɪ/ /ˈɑːktəpaɪ/
| n. |章鱼（复数）|Another plural form of octopus. Less common than "octopuses" but still considered acceptable. octopus: /ˈɒktəpəs/ /ˈɑːktəpəs/
| n. |章鱼|A cephalopod mollusc with eight arms, each with two rows of suckers, and a soft, unsegmented body. Known for their intelligence and ability to camouflage.|The octopus changed color to blend in with the coral. 章鱼改变颜色以融入珊瑚。|Synonyms: cephalopod|Antonyms: N/A|noun
