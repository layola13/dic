lo-res:/ˈləʊˈrez/ | adj. | 低分辨率的 |  Short for "low resolution," referring to images or displays with a relatively small number of pixels, resulting in less detail and sharpness. | The image was lo-res and blurry. 这张图片分辨率低，很模糊。|Synonyms: low-resolution, pixelated | Antonyms: high-resolution, hi-res | adjective

lo-res:/loʊˈrez/ | n. | 低分辨率 | Short for "low resolution," referring to the state or quality of having a relatively small number of pixels. | The lo-res of the security camera footage made it difficult to identify the suspect.  监控录像的低分辨率使得难以识别嫌疑人。| Synonyms: low resolution | Antonyms: high resolution, hi-res | noun
