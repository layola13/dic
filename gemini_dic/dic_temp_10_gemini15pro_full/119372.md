microcosmically:/ˌmaɪkrəʊˈkɒzmɪkli/| adv.|微观地；缩影地|In a way that relates to or represents a microcosm; on a small scale representing a larger entity.|He examined the situation microcosmically, focusing on a single family's experience to understand the broader societal trends.|Synonyms: representatively, typically, epitomically|Antonyms: macroscopically, broadly|词性: 副词
