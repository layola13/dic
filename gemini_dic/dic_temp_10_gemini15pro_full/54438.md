disinformation:/ˌdɪsɪnfərˈmeɪʃən/
n.
虚假信息，假情报
The spread of disinformation can have serious consequences.
虚假信息的传播可能会造成严重后果。
The government was accused of spreading disinformation about the war.
政府被指控散布关于战争的虚假信息。
近义词：misinformation, propaganda, lies, falsehoods
反义词：truth, facts, accuracy
