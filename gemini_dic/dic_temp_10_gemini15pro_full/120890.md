misbuttoned: /ˌmɪsˈbʌtnd/
| adj. | 扣错的；扣错扣子的 | fastened incorrectly, with the buttons in the wrong holes |  She noticed her blouse was misbuttoned and quickly fixed it. 她注意到她的衬衫扣错了，赶紧把它弄好。 |
| The child's shirt was misbuttoned.  这孩子的衬衫扣错了。 |
| Near synonyms: wrongly buttoned, incorrectly fastened |
| Antonyms: correctly buttoned, properly fastened |
| word property: adjective |
