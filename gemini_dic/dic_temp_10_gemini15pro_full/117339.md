medevacs:/ˈmɛdɪvæks/ | n. | 医疗后送，医疗撤离 |The military carried out several medevacs after the battle.  军队在战斗后进行了几次医疗后送。| Evacuation of casualties for medical care. |例句：The helicopter crew performed a daring medevac in the middle of the storm. 直升机机组人员在暴风雨中进行了一次大胆的医疗后送。

medevac:/ˈmɛdɪvæk/ | v. | 医疗后送，医疗撤离 | They medevacked the wounded soldier to the nearest hospital. 他们用直升机把受伤的士兵送到最近的医院。| To evacuate someone for medical care. |例句：The pilot medevacked the injured hikers from the mountain. 飞行员用直升机将受伤的徒步旅行者从山上撤离。
