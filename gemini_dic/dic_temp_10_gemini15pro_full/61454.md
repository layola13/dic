embower:/ɪmˈbaʊər/| vt.|使…深藏于树丛中；荫蔽；环绕；|To enclose in or cover with a bower; to shelter, as in a bower.  To surround with foliage or trees, creating a secluded or sheltered space.|The cottage was embowered in roses. 这座小屋被玫瑰环绕。|近义词：enclose, shelter, surround, screen, conceal|反义词：expose, reveal|词性：动词
词源：From em- (meaning "in" or "into") + bower (a shady, leafy recess or shelter). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *bauraz (meaning "dwelling").
