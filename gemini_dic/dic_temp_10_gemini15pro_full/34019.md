citizenries: /ˈsɪtɪzənriz/
noun
公民身份（复数）
The collective body of citizens of a nation or state, considered as a group.
The citizenries of several countries protested the war.  几个国家的公民抗议这场战争。

近义词: citizens, populace, public
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-citizens" or "foreigners" in some contexts)
词性: noun
