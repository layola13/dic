deglazes:/diːˈɡleɪzɪz/
v.
给…去釉，去除…的釉
To loosen and dissolve browned food residue from a pan by adding liquid, often wine or stock, and heating while stirring and scraping.
The chef deglazed the pan with white wine to make a flavorful sauce.  厨师用白葡萄酒给锅去釉，做成美味的酱汁。
v.
(冰川)消融，融化
The glacier deglazed the valley, leaving behind a fertile landscape. 冰川融化了山谷，留下肥沃的土地。
synonym: dissolve, loosen, melt
antonym: freeze, solidify
verb
