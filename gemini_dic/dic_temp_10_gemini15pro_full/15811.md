balkiness:/ˈbɔːkɪnɪs/
balkiness|n.|顽固，倔强；不愿合作；迟疑|The balkiness of the mule made it difficult to plow the field.  骡子的倔强使得耕地很困难。|Etymology: From balky + -ness.  Balk, meaning "to stop short and refuse to proceed," comes from the Middle English balken, "to pile up in ridges," referring to obstacles in plowing.  The "-ness" suffix forms nouns indicating a state or quality.|Examples: The balkiness of the old car engine was a constant source of frustration. 老式汽车发动机的启动困难一直令人沮丧。 His balkiness about signing the contract delayed the project. 他对签署合同的迟疑延误了项目。|Synonyms: reluctance, resistance, unwillingness, obstinacy, stubbornness|Antonyms: willingness, cooperation, compliance, amenability|词性:名词

balky:/ˈbɔːki/
balky|adj.|顽固的，倔强的；不愿合作的；迟疑的|The balky engine finally sputtered to life.  那台启动困难的发动机终于突突地发动起来了。|Etymology: From balk + -y. Balk, meaning "to stop short and refuse to proceed," comes from the Middle English balken, "to pile up in ridges," referring to obstacles in plowing. The "-y" suffix forms adjectives.|Examples:  The balky door wouldn't open. 那扇难开的门打不开。  He was balky about answering my questions. 他不愿回答我的问题。|Synonyms: reluctant, resistant, unwilling, obstinate, stubborn|Antonyms: willing, cooperative, compliant, amenable|词性:形容词
