collet:/ˈkɒlɪt/ (UK), /kɑːˈlɛt/ (US)| n.|夹头，套筒|A metal ring or clamp used for holding a workpiece or tool, especially in a lathe or other machine.|The collet held the drill bit securely in place. 夹头将钻头牢固地固定到位。|The watchmaker used a collet to hold the tiny screw. 钟表匠用夹头夹住小螺丝。|近义词：chuck, clamp, holder|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
