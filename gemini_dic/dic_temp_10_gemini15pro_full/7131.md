angeleno:/ˌænʤəˈliːnoʊ/
noun
洛杉矶人；洛杉矶居民
A native or inhabitant of Los Angeles, California.
Example:  Many Angelenos enjoy the city's diverse culinary scene. 许多洛杉矶人喜欢这座城市的多元烹饪景象。

Example:  Traffic is a common complaint among Angelenos. 交通拥堵是洛杉矶居民普遍抱怨的问题。

Synonym: Los Angeleno, resident of Los Angeles
Antonym: (No direct antonym, could be a resident of another city, e.g., New Yorker)
