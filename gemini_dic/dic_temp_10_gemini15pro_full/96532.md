indoles:/ˈɪndoʊlz/ | n. | 吲哚 | A class of aromatic heterocyclic organic compounds containing a benzene ring fused to a pyrrole ring.  Derived from the indigo dye, from which it was first isolated. |  Indoles are important building blocks in many pharmaceuticals and natural products. 吲哚是许多药物和天然产物的重要组成部分。|  Tryptophan, an essential amino acid, is an indole derivative. 色氨酸，一种必需氨基酸，是一种吲哚衍生物。|  synonyms:  heterocycles,  aromatic compounds | antonyms:  None (lacks a direct opposite) | noun
