menstruated:/ˈmenstrueɪtɪd/
verb
月经来潮
Having experienced menstruation; having had a period.
She menstruated for the first time when she was twelve. 她十二岁第一次来月经。
近义词:had her period, had a period
反义词:(none - a direct antonym doesn't exist, but one could consider pre-menarcheal or post-menopausal as contrasting states)
词性:verb (past tense of menstruate)
