ollie: /ˈɑːli/ (US), /ˈɒli/ (UK) | n. v. | 空翻，滑板腾空 | An acrobatic maneuver, especially in skateboarding, in which the rider and board leap into the air without the use of the rider's hands. |

He landed a perfect ollie over the gap. 他完美地空翻越过了缺口。

Tony Hawk is known for his impressive ollies. 托尼·霍克以其令人印象深刻的滑板腾空而闻名。

近义词: aerial, jump, flip | 反义词: landing, fall | 词性: noun, verb
