daung: /daʊŋ/
n.
缅甸长裙，筒裙
A longyi, a sheet of cloth worn as a skirt in Myanmar.
She wore a beautiful silk daung to the festival. 她穿着漂亮的丝绸长裙参加节日。
Near synonyms: longyi, sarong
Antonyms: trousers, pants
noun
