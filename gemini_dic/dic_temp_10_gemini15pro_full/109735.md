leverage: /ˈliːvərɪdʒ/ | v. | 利用；举债经营 | Use (something) to maximum advantage. |  The company leveraged its resources to achieve rapid growth.  这家公司利用其资源实现了快速增长。 | synonyms: capitalize on, exploit, utilize | antonyms: waste, neglect | verb
