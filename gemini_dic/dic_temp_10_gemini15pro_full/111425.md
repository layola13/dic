lizardly:/ˈlɪzərdli/| adj.|像蜥蜴的，蜥蜴般的；敏捷的，鬼鬼祟祟的|Resembling or characteristic of a lizard, especially in being quick, agile, or furtive.  Often implies a sense of coldness or detachment.|His lizardly eyes darted around the room.  他那双像蜥蜴一样的眼睛在房间里扫来扫去。|近义词：reptilian, serpentine, sly, furtive|反义词:warm, open, honest|词性:形容词

词源: From lizard + -ly.  "Lizard" comes from Old French "lisarde".
