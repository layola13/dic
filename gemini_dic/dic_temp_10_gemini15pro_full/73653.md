forgathering:/fɔːrˈɡæðərɪŋ/
n.
集会，聚会
A forgathering is a gathering or assembly of people.
The annual forgathering of the clan was a much-anticipated event.  家族一年一度的聚会是一个备受期待的活动。
The forgathering of tech enthusiasts drew a large crowd.  科技爱好者的聚会吸引了大批人群。
近义词：gathering, assembly, meeting, reunion
反义词：dispersal, separation, scattering
词性: 名词


Etymology:
From for- + gathering.  "For-" in this context acts as an intensifier.  "Gathering" comes from the Old English "gadrian," meaning "to collect."
