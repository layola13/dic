agritourists:/ˈæɡrɪˌtʊərɪsts/ | n. | 农业游客 | Tourists who visit farms or agricultural areas for recreational or educational purposes.  They may participate in activities like picking fruits, learning about farming practices, or enjoying the rural environment. | Example: The agritourists enjoyed picking strawberries at the local farm. 农业游客们喜欢在当地农场采摘草莓。

Example: Many local farms are now catering to agritourists, offering tours and activities. 许多当地农场现在都为农业游客提供服务，提供旅游和活动。 | Synonym: farm tourists, rural tourists | Antonym: urban tourists | noun
