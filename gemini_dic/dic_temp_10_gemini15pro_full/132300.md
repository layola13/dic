nondefiantly:/ˌnɒndɪˈfaɪəntli/
adverb|非对抗地，不挑衅地|In a way that is not defiant; not exhibiting defiance.  Showing cooperation and submission.|He nondefiantly accepted the officer's instructions. 他顺从地接受了警官的指示。|近义词:submissively, obediently, meekly, cooperatively|反义词:defiantly, rebelliously, resistantly|词性:副词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin, meaning "not"
defiant (adjective): from Old French *defiant, present participle of defier "to challenge," from Latin diffidare "to distrust, renounce," from dis- "apart, away" + fidare "to trust," from fides "faith"
-ly (suffix): adverbial suffix
