countertrends:/ˈkaʊntərtrɛndz/
n.
逆势；反趋势
A trend that goes against the prevailing trend.
Many investors are looking for countertrends in the market. 许多投资者正在寻找市场上的逆势。

countertrend:/ˈkaʊntərtrɛnd/
n.
逆势；反趋势
A trend that goes against the prevailing trend.
The current countertrend in fashion is towards more classic styles.  当前时尚界的反趋势是趋向更经典的风格。

countertrend:/ˈkaʊntərtrɛnd/
adj.
逆势的；反趋势的
Going against the prevailing trend.
Countertrend investing can be risky, but also very rewarding. 逆势投资可能会有风险，但也非常有回报。


近义词:contrarian trend, reverse trend, opposite trend
反义词:trend, prevailing trend
词性: noun, adjective
