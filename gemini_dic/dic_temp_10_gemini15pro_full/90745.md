hummer:/ˈhʌmər/ (US) /ˈhʌmə(r)/ (UK)| n.|悍马; 嗡嗡声; 振翅声; 哼唱者|A large, military-style SUV. Also refers to the sound of humming.  A type of hummingbird. Someone who hums.|例句：He drives a Hummer.  他开一辆悍马。

例句：The hummer hovered near the flowers.  蜂鸟在花朵附近盘旋。

近义词：SUV (for the vehicle), drone, buzz (for the sound)|反义词:whisper (for the sound)|词性:noun


hum:/hʌm/ (US) /hʌm/ (UK)| v.  n.|哼唱; 嗡嗡作响; 忙碌; 哼(曲); 嗡嗡声; 嘈杂声|To sing with closed lips, or to make a low, continuous sound. Also can refer to a state of activity.  The sound itself.|例句：She hummed a cheerful tune. 她哼着一首欢快的曲子。

例句：The factory hummed with activity. 工厂里一片繁忙的景象。

近义词：sing, drone, buzz|反义词:be silent|词性:verb, noun
