creme:/kreːm/ /krɛm/ | n. | 奶油，乳霜 | A thick, smooth substance used in cooking or as a cosmetic.  A dessert or sweet sauce made with cream. | The cake was covered in a rich chocolate creme. 蛋糕上覆盖着浓郁的巧克力奶油。 |She used a creme cleanser to wash her face. 她用乳霜洁面乳洗脸。| 近义词：cream, lotion, ointment | 反义词: (没有明确的反义词) | 词性: 名词

词源:  From French crème, from Latin cremāre (“to burn, to skim”). Cognate with cream.  Essentially a variant spelling of "cream".
