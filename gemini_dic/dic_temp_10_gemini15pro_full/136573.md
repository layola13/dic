nonsalubriousnesses:/ˌnɒnsæljuːˈbrɪəsnəsɪz/
noun
不健康；有害；(尤指地方)污秽
The state or quality of being nonsalubrious; unhealthfulness.
The nonsalubriousnesses of the slums contributed to the spread of disease.  贫民窟不卫生的环境导致了疾病的蔓延。
Synonyms: unhealthfulness, insalubrity, noxiousness, pestilence
Antonyms: healthfulness, salubrity, wholesomeness
词性: noun
