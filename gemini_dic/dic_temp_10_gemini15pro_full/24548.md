broadside:/ˈbrɔːdsaɪd/ (US) /ˈbrɔːdsaɪd/ (UK)| n. adj. adv.|舷侧齐射；猛烈抨击；单页印刷品|A firing of all the guns on one side of a warship; a strong verbal attack; a large printed sheet (usually only printed on one side)|The ship delivered a devastating broadside. 这艘船进行了毁灭性的舷侧齐射。|近义词：barrage, volley, attack, denunciation|反义词:compliment, praise|词性:noun, adjective, adverb

broadside:/ˈbrɔːdsaɪd/ (US) /ˈbrɔːdsaɪd/ (UK)| adj.|侧面的，横向的|Directed to or from the side.|The car skidded and hit the tree broadside. 汽车打滑，侧面撞到了树上。|近义词：sideways, lateral|反义词:head-on|词性:adjective

broadside:/ˈbrɔːdsaɪd/ (US) /ˈbrɔːdsaɪd/ (UK)| adv.|侧面地，横向地|In a sideways direction.|The truck hit the wall broadside. 卡车侧面撞上了墙。|近义词：sideways, laterally|反义词:head-on|词性:adverb
