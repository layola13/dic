edvard: /ˈɛdvɑːrd/ | n. | 爱德华（男子名） | A male given name, the Scandinavian form of Edward. | Edvard Munch was a famous Norwegian painter. 爱德华·蒙克是一位著名的挪威画家。| Edvard Grieg composed the famous Peer Gynt Suite.  爱德华·格里格创作了著名的《培尔·金特》组曲。 | 近义词: Edward | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

词源: From the Old Norse name *Eðvarðr, composed of the elements *auða- "wealth, prosperity" and *varðr "guardian".  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *auþa- "property, inheritance" and *warduz "guard".
