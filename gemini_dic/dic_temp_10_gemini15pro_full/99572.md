interindicated:/ˌɪntərˈɪndɪkeɪtɪd/
adjective
相互禁忌的，互相抵触的
Indicating that (a treatment or procedure) should not be used in conjunction with another treatment or procedure because they may interact in a harmful or counterproductive way.
The use of warfarin and aspirin is interindicated because of the increased risk of bleeding.  华法林和阿司匹林的使用是相互禁忌的，因为会增加出血的风险。
Certain medications are interindicated in patients with kidney disease.  某些药物对于肾病患者是禁忌的。
近义词: contraindicated, incompatible
反义词: indicated, compatible
词性: adjective

词源:
inter- (prefix): between, among, mutually, reciprocally (来自拉丁语)
indicate (verb): to point out, to show (来自拉丁语 indicare)
