ganoidal:/ˈɡæˌnɔɪdəl/| adj.|硬鳞的，闪光的|Covered with or resembling ganoid scales; bright, shining, or glistening.|例句：The ganoidal scales of the gar gave it a prehistoric appearance.  雀鳝的硬鳞使其具有史前外观。

例句：The ganoidal sheen of the fish's scales caught the light.  鱼鳞的闪光反射着光线。|近义词：shiny, glistening, bright, scaly|反义词：dull, matte|词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *ganoïde*, from Greek γάνος (gános, “brightness, sheen”) + -oid. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰen- (“to give birth, beget; know”).
