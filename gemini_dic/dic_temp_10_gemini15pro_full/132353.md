nondelegations:/ˌnɒndɛlɪˈɡeɪʃənz/ | n. | 不可委托事项；不准授权事宜 | The principle of nondelegation holds that one branch of government cannot authorize another entity to exercise the power which it is constitutionally authorized to exercise itself. |  Congress often includes nondelegations clauses in legislation to prevent agencies from overstepping their authority. 国会经常在立法中加入不准授权条款，以防止机构越权。| synonyms: non-transferable powers, reserved powers | antonyms: delegations, transferable powers | noun

nondelegation:/ˌnɒndɛlɪˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. | 不可委托；不准授权 | The nondelegation doctrine prohibits Congress from transferring its legislative power to another branch of government. | The president argued that the nondelegation doctrine prevented him from giving the agency more authority. 总统认为，不准授权原则阻止他赋予该机构更多权力。| synonyms: non-transferability, non-assignment | antonyms: delegation, assignment | noun
