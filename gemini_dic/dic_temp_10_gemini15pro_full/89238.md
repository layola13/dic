homiletic:/ˌhɒmɪˈlɛtɪk/ /ˌhoʊmɪˈlɛtɪk/| adj.|说教的；讲道的；布道的|Of or relating to sermons or homilies; characterized by a moralizing or didactic tone.|The preacher's homiletic style was both engaging and thought-provoking.  牧师的讲道风格既引人入胜又发人深省。|近义词：didactic, moralistic, preachy, sermonic|反义词：conversational, informal|词性:形容词
homiletics:/ˌhɒmɪˈlɛtɪks/ /ˌhoʊmɪˈlɛtɪks/| n.|讲道法；讲道术|The art of preaching or writing sermons.|He studied homiletics in seminary. 他在神学院学习讲道法。|近义词：preaching, sermonizing|反义词：(No direct antonym)|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *homileticus*, from Greek homilētikos (“conversational, social”), from homilia (“conversation, discourse”), from homilein (“to converse, associate with”), from homilos (“assembly, crowd”), from homos (“same”).
