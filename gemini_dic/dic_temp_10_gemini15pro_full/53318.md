disabusal:/ˌdɪsəˈbjuːzl/
n.
醒悟; 彻底了解真相; 纠正
The act of freeing someone from a misconception or deception.
例句：His disabusal was painful, but necessary.  他的醒悟是痛苦的，却是必要的。

例句：The letter provided her disabusal about his true intentions. 这封信让她彻底了解了他的真实意图。

近义词：enlightenment, clarification, disillusionment,  undeceiving
反义词：deception, illusion, delusion
