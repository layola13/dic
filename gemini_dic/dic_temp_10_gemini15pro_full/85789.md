haystacks:/ˈheɪstæks/ | n. | 干草堆，草垛 | A large pile of hay, typically stacked outdoors and used as fodder. | The farmer loaded the haystacks onto the wagon. 农民把干草堆装到马车上。|  The children played hide-and-seek in the haystacks. 孩子们在干草堆里玩捉迷藏。 | 近义词: hayrick | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑loose hay 散装的干草) | 词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English *heystak, equivalent to hay + stack.
