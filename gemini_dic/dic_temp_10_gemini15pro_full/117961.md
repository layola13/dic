mementos:/məˈmɛntoʊz/
noun
纪念品；纪念物
An object kept as a reminder or souvenir of a person or event.
She kept the ticket as a memento of her trip to Europe. 她把票留作她欧洲之行的纪念品。
souvenirs, keepsakes, reminders
