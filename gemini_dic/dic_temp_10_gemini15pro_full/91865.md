hyperinflationary:/ˌhaɪpərɪnˈfleɪʃəneri/
adjective
恶性通货膨胀的
marked by or causing hyperinflation; relating to or characteristic of hyperinflation.  Characterized by extremely rapid and uncontrollable inflation.
The country's hyperinflationary economy made it difficult for businesses to operate.  这个国家的恶性通货膨胀经济使得企业难以经营。
The hyperinflationary spiral caused prices to double every few days. 恶性通货膨胀的螺旋式上升导致物价每隔几天就翻一番。
近义词: inflationary, runaway
反义词: deflationary
词性: adjective

词源:
hyper- (prefix meaning "excessive, above") + inflation + -ary (suffix forming adjectives)
From hyper- +‎ inflation +‎ -ary.
