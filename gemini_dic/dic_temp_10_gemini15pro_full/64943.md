esteemed:/ɪˈstiːmd/ | adj. | 受人尊敬的，受敬重的 | Held in great respect; admired. |例句：He is an esteemed colleague. 他是一位受人尊敬的同事。|例句：She is an esteemed member of the community. 她是社区里受人尊敬的成员。| 近义词：respected, admired, honored, revered | 反义词：despised, scorned, disrespected |词性:形容词

esteemed:/ɪˈstiːmd/ | v. (past participle of esteem) | 尊重，尊敬 | To regard highly or favorably. (This is less common as a verb, primarily used as an adjective.) | 例句：The judge was highly esteemed for her fairness.  这位法官因其公正而备受尊敬。 | 例句：He was esteemed by all who knew him. 所有认识他的人都尊敬他。| 近义词：respected, admired, honored, revered | 反义词：despised, scorned, disrespected | 词性:动词(esteem的过去分词)
