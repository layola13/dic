noun|巴达维亚人|A native or inhabitant of Batavia, a former name for Jakarta, Indonesia.|Many Batavians speak Dutch. 许多巴达维亚人说荷兰语。|近义词：Javanese, Indonesian|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Batavia, the Latinized form of Betuwe, a region in the Netherlands.  The Dutch East India Company established a trading post in the 17th century in what is now Jakarta, and named it Batavia after this region.
