gastroscopy:/ɡæsˈtrɒskəpi/ /ɡæˈstrɑːskəpi/| n.|胃镜检查；胃镜|An examination of the inside of the stomach using a gastroscope. |The doctor recommended a gastroscopy to investigate his stomach pain. 医生建议做胃镜检查以调查他的胃痛。|Gastroscopy revealed a small ulcer in the stomach lining. 胃镜检查发现胃粘膜有小溃疡。|synonyms: endoscopic examination of the stomach, stomach endoscopy|antonyms: N/A|noun
