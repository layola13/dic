housecoat:/ˈhaʊskoʊt/ (US) /ˈhaʊskəʊt/ (UK)| n. |家居服，晨衣|A loose, informal robe worn at home, typically in the morning or evening.|She slipped on her housecoat before making breakfast. 她穿上了晨衣，然后开始做早餐。|例句：My grandmother always wore a floral housecoat. 我的祖母总是穿一件花卉图案的晨衣。

例句：He likes to relax in his housecoat on Sunday mornings. 他喜欢在星期天早上穿着晨衣放松。|近义词：dressing gown, robe, bathrobe|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly street clothes/outdoor clothes)|词性:noun
