embogue:/ɪmˈboʊɡ/| v.|流出，注入；排出|To discharge or empty, as a stream into a larger body of water; to flow out.|The river embogues into the sea. 这条河流入大海。|近义词：discharge, empty, flow, debouch|反义词: fill|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *embouguer*, from *en-* (in) + *bougue* (mouth), of uncertain origin.
