outmoving:/ˌaʊtˈmuːvɪŋ/
adjective
向外移动的；迁出的
Relating to or involving the act of moving out of a place, especially a residence.
The outmoving tenants left the apartment spotless. 迁出的房客把公寓打扫得一尘不染。
近义词: departing, exiting, vacating
反义词: incoming, arriving, moving in
词性: adjective

outmoving:/ˌaʊtˈmuːvɪŋ/
noun
迁出；搬出
The act of moving out of a place, especially a residence.
The outmoving of the company's headquarters caused a lot of disruption. 公司总部的搬迁造成了很大的混乱。
近义词: departure, exit, move-out
反义词: arrival, move-in
词性: noun


词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "outward, away from") + "moving" (present participle of "move").  Essentially, it combines the idea of "moving" with the direction "out."
