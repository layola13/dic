insecure:/ˌɪnsɪˈkjʊr/ (US), /ˌɪnsɪˈkjɔː(r)/ (UK)| adj.|不安全的，不牢靠的；缺乏信心的，不自信的|Not confident or assured; uncertain of oneself; apprehensive or anxious about oneself.|例句：She felt insecure about her appearance. 她对自己的外貌感到不自信。|近义词：anxious, apprehensive, uncertain, doubtful, hesitant, timid, shy, diffident|反义词：secure, confident, self-assured, assertive|词性:形容词

insecure:/ˌɪnsɪˈkjʊr/ (US), /ˌɪnsɪˈkjɔː(r)/ (UK)| n.|不安全感|Lack of confidence or assurance; self-doubt.|例句：He overcame his insecurities and became a successful businessman. 他克服了自己的不安全感，成为了一名成功的商人。|近义词：anxiety, apprehension, uncertainty, doubt|反义词：security, confidence, self-assurance|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + secure (safe, reliable).  The word "secure" ultimately comes from Latin "securus" (free from care), from "se-" (without) + "cura" (care, concern).
