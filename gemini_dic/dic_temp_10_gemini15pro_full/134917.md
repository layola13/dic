nonmutable:/ˌnɒnˈmjuːtəbl/ | adj. | 不可变的 | Not able to be changed; immutable. |  Example: Strings in Python are nonmutable, meaning you cannot change them in place. Python中的字符串是不可变的，意味着你不能就地修改它们。 | Example:  The contract contains several nonmutable clauses.  合同中包含一些不可修改的条款。 | 近义词: immutable, unchangeable, fixed, constant, permanent | 反义词: mutable, changeable, variable, alterable | 词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin, meaning "not"
mutable: from Latin "mutabilis," meaning "changeable," from "mutare" (to change).
