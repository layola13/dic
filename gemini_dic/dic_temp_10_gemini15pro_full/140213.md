officialism: /əˈfɪʃəlɪzəm/
noun
官僚主义；官僚作风
Excessive reliance on or reverence for official rules and regulations; the rigid and inflexible administration of rules.
The project was bogged down by endless officialism.  这个项目因无休止的官僚作风而陷入困境。
Bureaucracy and red tape are hindering progress. 官僚主义和繁文缛节阻碍了进步。

近义词: bureaucracy, red tape, formalism
反义词: flexibility, informality, dynamism
词性: noun
