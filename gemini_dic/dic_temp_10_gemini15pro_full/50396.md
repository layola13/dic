deposing:/dɪˈpoʊzɪŋ/
verb
罢免; 宣誓作证
the act of removing someone important from a powerful position, or the act of making a statement under oath for use in a law court.
The rebels are deposing the dictator. 反叛者正在罢免独裁者。
He spent the morning deposing before the investigating committee. 他整个上午都在调查委员会面前作证。
Synonyms: removing, ousting, dethroning, dismissing, unseating,  testifying, swearing
Antonyms: appointing, electing, installing, inaugurating

depose:/dɪˈpoʊz/
verb

to remove someone important from a powerful position, or to make a statement under oath for use in a law court.

He was deposed as a witness at the trial. 他在审判中被传唤作证。
Synonyms: remove, oust, dethrone, dismiss, unseat, testify, swear
Antonyms: appoint, elect, install, inaugurate
