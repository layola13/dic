blocs:/blɒks/ | n. | 集团，阵营，联盟 | A group of nations or parties with common interests or aims, often political. | The Western blocs imposed sanctions. 西方阵营实施了制裁。|  The two blocs agreed to a ceasefire. 这两个阵营同意停火。| synonyms: alliances, coalitions, factions, groups | antonyms: individuals, splinter groups | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French "bloc," meaning "block," referring to a cohesive group. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*blukkaz," meaning "lump, block."
