mortiferous: /mɔːrˈtɪfərəs/ | adj. | 致命的，致死的 | Causing or likely to cause death; deadly. |  The Black Death was a mortiferous plague. 黑死病是一种致命的瘟疫。|  The venom of the cobra is mortiferous.  眼镜蛇的毒液是致命的。| 近义词: deadly, fatal, lethal | 反义词: life-giving, beneficial, harmless | 词性: 形容词 | 词源: From Latin *mortifer*, from *mors* (death) + *-fer* (bearing).
