bonibell:/ˈbɒnɪˌbɛl/ (US) /ˈbɒniˌbel/ (UK)| n.|美女; 迷人的女子|A bonibell is a beautiful and charming young woman.  This word is somewhat dated and not commonly used in modern English.|例句：She was considered a true bonibell in her youth. 她年轻时被认为是一位真正的美女。

例句：The bonibell captivated everyone at the ball.  这位美女在舞会上迷住了所有人。


近义词：beauty, belle, charmer, stunner|反义词:hag, crone|词性:名词

词源:  From the French "bonne et belle," meaning "good and beautiful."
