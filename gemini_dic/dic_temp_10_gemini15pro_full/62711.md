enginery:/ˈɛndʒɪnri/ | n. | 机械, 机械装置; 器械 | Machinery, especially large and complex machinery; apparatus. |  The complex enginery of the dam fascinated the tourists. 大坝复杂的机械装置吸引了游客。| The enginery required to build such a massive structure was extensive. 建造如此庞大的结构所需的机械设备非常广泛。 | 近义词: machinery, equipment, apparatus, mechanisms | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑tool, implement等单个工具) | 词性: 名词

词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French enginerie, based on engin 'skill', also 'war machine', from Latin ingenium ‘natural talent’.
