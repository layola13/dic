muzzling:/ˈmʌzəlɪŋ/
n.|压制言论; 封口; 戴口套|The act of preventing someone from speaking or expressing their opinions, or the act of putting a muzzle on an animal.|The government's muzzling of the press was widely condemned. 政府对新闻界的压制受到了广泛谴责。
muzzling:|v.|压制言论; 封口; 戴口套|Preventing someone from speaking or expressing their opinions, or putting a muzzle on an animal.|The new law effectively muzzles any criticism of the government.  这项新法律有效地压制了任何对政府的批评。
muzzling:|adj.|压制言论的|Descriptive of something that prevents speech or expression.|The muzzling effect of the policy was clear. 这项政策的压制效果是显而易见的。
