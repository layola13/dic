geotechnology:/ˌdʒiːoʊtekˈnɑːlədʒi/ | n. |地理信息技术，地球空间信息技术 |The study and application of scientific and engineering principles to the acquisition, processing, interpretation, display, and utilization of data relating to the earth.  It integrates disciplines like surveying, remote sensing, GIS, GPS, and computer cartography to understand and manage geographical information. | Geotechnology plays a crucial role in urban planning and environmental management. 地理信息技术在城市规划和环境管理中起着至关重要的作用。|  Geotechnology is used to create detailed maps for navigation. 地理信息技术被用来创建用于导航的详细地图。| synonyms: geomatics, geospatial technology | antonyms: N/A | noun
