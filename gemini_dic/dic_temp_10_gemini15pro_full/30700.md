certificatory:/sərˈtɪfɪkətɔːri/
adjective
证明的；授予证书的
Relating to or serving as a certificate.
This course offers a certificatory program. 这门课程提供一个认证项目。
The judge issued a certificatory order. 法官发布了认证令。
synonyms: certifying, credentialing, validating
antonyms: uncertified, invalid, unvalidated
词性: adjective
