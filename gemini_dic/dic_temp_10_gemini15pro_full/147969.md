overrestrains:/ˌoʊvərɪˈstreɪnz/| v. |过度限制，过度约束|To restrain excessively; to impose too many or too strict limitations on.|例句：The government overrestrains the press, limiting freedom of speech. 政府过度限制新闻界，限制了言论自由。

例句：Overrestraining children's creativity can stifle their development. 过度限制孩子的创造力会扼杀他们的发展。|近义词：restrict, constrain, curb, stifle, suppress|反义词:liberate, free, encourage, empower|词性:动词
