ergoism: /ˈɜːrɡəʊɪzəm/ | n. |利己主义; 我行我素 | The doctrine that individual self-interest is the valid end of all actions.  A system of ethics holding that the pursuit of one's own self-interest is both rational and moral. | He lived a life of pure ergoism, caring little for the needs of others. 他过着纯粹利己的生活，很少关心别人的需要。|  egoism, self-interest, selfishness | altruism, selflessness | noun

egoist: /ˈiːɡəʊɪst/ | n. | 利己主义者; 自我主义者 | A person who is excessively conceited or absorbed in themselves; self-centered.  |  He was an egoist, always putting his own needs first. 他是一个利己主义者，总是把自己的需求放在第一位。| egotist, narcissist, self-seeker | altruist, humanitarian | noun

egoistic: /ˌiːɡəʊˈɪstɪk/ | adj. | 利己主义的; 自我中心的 |  Motivated by the belief that one's own interests are supreme. | His egoistic behavior alienated his friends. 他利己的行为疏远了他的朋友。 | selfish, self-centered, egocentric | selfless, altruistic | adjective
