huffily:/ˈhʌfɪli/| adv.|生气地，傲慢地|In a way that shows you are annoyed or offended.|She turned and walked huffily out of the room. 她生气地转身走出房间。|例句：He huffily rejected their offer. 他气呼呼地拒绝了他们的提议。|近义词：angrily, indignantly, resentfully, sullenly|反义词：cheerfully, pleasantly, good-naturedly|词性:副词

词源: From huff (n.) + -ily (adverbial suffix).  The noun "huff" originates from imitative of the sound of forceful exhalation expressing annoyance.
