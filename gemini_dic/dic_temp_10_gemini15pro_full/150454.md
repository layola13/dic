pantelegraph:/ˈpæn.tə.lə.ɡræf/ | n. | 潘特莱格拉夫（一种早期传真机） | An early form of facsimile machine that could transmit handwriting, signatures, and simple drawings over telegraph wires. It used a pendulum system to scan the original and transmit its movements electrically. | The pantelegraph was a remarkable invention for its time, allowing for the transmission of handwritten documents over long distances. 潘特莱格拉夫在当时是一项了不起的发明，可以远距离传输手写文件。| The museum displayed a working pantelegraph, demonstrating how it could send signatures across wires.  博物馆展示了一台工作的潘特莱格拉夫，演示了它如何通过电线发送签名。 | synonyms: facsimile machine, fax machine, teleautograph | antonyms: N/A | noun
