notice:/ˈnoʊtɪs/ (US) /ˈnəʊtɪs/ (UK)| n. vt.|通知，注意，察觉|A written or spoken announcement giving information or warning about something.  The act of paying attention to something or someone.  To become aware of something.|The company gave notice of the impending layoffs. (公司发布了即将裁员的通知。) I noticed a strange smell in the room. (我注意到房间里有一股奇怪的味道。)  She noticed him looking at her. (她注意到他在看着她。)|近义词：announcement, information, warning, observation, perception|反义词:ignore, overlook|noun, verb
