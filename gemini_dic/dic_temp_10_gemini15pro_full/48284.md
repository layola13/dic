decoying:/dɪˈkɔɪɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
诱骗；用诱饵吸引
luring someone or something into a trap or dangerous situation by using a decoy.
The hunters were decoying ducks with realistic-looking models. 猎人用逼真的模型诱捕野鸭。
The police officer decoyed the criminal into confessing. 警察用计诱使罪犯认罪。
Synonyms:  luring, enticing, tempting, baiting
Antonyms:  repelling, deterring
