chloroformic:/ˌklɔːrəˈfɔːrmɪk/ | adj. |氯甲酸的；氯甲酰的| Relating to or containing the chloroformyl group (COCl). | Chloroformic acid is unstable. 氯甲酸不稳定。|Chloroformic esters are important reagents in organic synthesis. 氯甲酸酯是有机合成中的重要试剂。| 近义词: chloroformyl | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective

chloroformyl:/ˌklɔːrəˈfɔːrmɪl/ | n. adj. |氯甲酰基；氯甲酰的| The univalent radical COCl, formally derived from formic acid by replacing the hydroxyl group with chlorine.  Also used as an adjective: containing the chloroformyl group. | Chloroformyl chloride is another name for phosgene. 氯甲酰氯是光气的别名。 | The chloroformyl group is highly reactive. 氯甲酰基具有很高的反应活性。 | 近义词: None | 反义词: None | 词性: noun, adjective
