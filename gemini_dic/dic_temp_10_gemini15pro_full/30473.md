centripetalism:/ˌsɛntrɪˈpiːtəlɪzəm/ | n. | 向心力，向心性 | The tendency to unify a country or group by focusing on a common center or concern. | The government's policies promoted centripetalism, emphasizing national unity and shared values. 政府的政策促进了向心力，强调国家统一和共同价值观。|  The new national anthem fostered a sense of centripetalism among the diverse population. 新的国歌在不同人群中培养了一种向心力。 | synonyms: centralization, unification, integration | antonyms: centrifugalism, separatism, fragmentation | noun

centripetal:/ˌsɛnˈtrɪpɪtəl/ | adj. | 向心的 | Moving or tending to move towards a center. | The centripetal force keeps the planets in orbit around the sun. 向心力使行星绕太阳运行。 | The design of the city, with its central plaza, creates a centripetal effect. 城市的设计，以其中心广场，创造了一种向心的效果。 | synonyms: converging, centralizing | antonyms: centrifugal, diverging | adjective
