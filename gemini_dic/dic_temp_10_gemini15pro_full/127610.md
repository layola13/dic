needily:/ˈniːdɪli/| adv.|贫困地；缺乏地；需要地；依赖地|In a needy manner; in a way that shows a great need for something, especially affection, attention, or money.  表示非常需要某物，尤指感情、关注或金钱。|She looked at him needily. 她可怜巴巴地看着他。|近义词：desperately, urgently, imploringly, clingingly|反义词：independently, self-sufficiently|词性:副词

词源: From "needy" + "-ly."  来自形容词 "needy" 加后缀 "-ly"。
