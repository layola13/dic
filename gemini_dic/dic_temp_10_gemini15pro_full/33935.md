cirriform:/ˈsɪrɪfɔːrm/
adjective
卷云状的，纤维状的
Resembling a cirrus cloud; having a delicate, hairlike, or feathery appearance.
例句：The cirriform clouds drifted lazily across the sky. 卷云状的云朵懒洋洋地飘过天空。

例句：The artist used cirriform brushstrokes to create a sense of movement. 艺术家用卷云状的笔触来营造一种动感的画面。

近义词：wispy, feathery, filamentous
反义词：cumuliform, stratus
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cirriformis, from cirrus (“curl of hair”) + -formis (“-shaped”).
