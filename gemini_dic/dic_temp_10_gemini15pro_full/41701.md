corrivalship:/kəˈraɪvəlʃɪp/
n.
竞争； соперничество
the state or fact of being rivals; competition.
The two companies have a long history of corrivalship. 这两家公司有着长期的竞争历史。

近义词: rivalry, competition, contention
反义词: cooperation, collaboration, partnership
词性: noun
