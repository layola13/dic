a-frames: /ˈeɪ freɪmz/
n.
A-字框架，A型架
A-frames are triangular structures, often used for supporting roofs or signs.
We rented an A-frame cabin in the mountains. 我们在山上租了一个A字形小屋。
The shop had an A-frame sign out front.  这家商店前面有一个A字形招牌。
近义词: A-frame structure, A-shaped frame
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider contrasting shapes like rectangular frame)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the shape resembling the capital letter "A".  源自类似于大写字母“A”的形状。



USAGE->
These cabins are built with sturdy a-frames. 这些小屋是用坚固的A形框架建造的。

A-frames are popular for vacation homes. A型框架房屋是度假屋的热门选择。



PHRASE->
a-frame house = A型框架房屋
a-frame cabin = A型框架小屋
a-frame sign = A型框架标志
a-frame tent = A型框架帐篷
a-frame swing set = A型框架秋千架
