selfishness:/ˈsɛlfɪʃnɪs/
n.
自私，利己主义
The state or quality of being selfish; exclusive regard to one's own interest or happiness.
Her selfishness made it difficult for her to maintain friendships.  她的自私让她难以维持友谊。
egoism, self-centeredness, self-seeking
altruism, selflessness
noun
