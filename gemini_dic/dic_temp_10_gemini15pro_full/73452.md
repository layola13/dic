forenoons:/ˈfɔːrnuːnz/| n.|上午;午前|The mornings are busy, but the afternoons are relatively free. 上午很忙，但下午比较空闲。|例句：I usually work in the forenoons and relax in the afternoons. 我通常上午工作，下午休息。

例句：She spends her forenoons gardening. 她上午都在做园艺。|近义词：mornings|反义词:afternoons|词性:名词
