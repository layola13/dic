fruit:/fruːt/| n.| 水果，果实；成果，结果 |The tree bears delicious fruit.  这棵树结出美味的果实。|近义词: produce, crop, harvest, outcome, result |反义词: barrenness, sterility | noun


Etymology->
From Old French *fruit* (Modern French *fruit*), from Latin *fructus*, meaning "enjoyment, delight, pleasure, satisfaction; produce, crops, fruit."  The Latin word is related to *frui*, meaning "to use, enjoy."
