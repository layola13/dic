colligations:/ˌkɒlɪˈɡeɪʃənz/ | n. | 搭配；组合；词语搭配 |The study of colligations helps learners understand which words typically occur together.  词语搭配研究帮助学习者理解哪些词通常一起出现。| Colligations in English, such as "strong tea" and "heavy rain," are often arbitrary and must be learned.  英语中的搭配，例如“浓茶”和“大雨”，通常是任意的，必须学习。 | collocations, combinations |  - | noun
