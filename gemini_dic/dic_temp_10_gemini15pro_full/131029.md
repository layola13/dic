nonauthoritativeness:/ˌnɒnɔːˌθɒrɪˈteɪtɪvnəs/
n.|非权威性，非官方性，不确定性|The nonauthoritativeness of the source made it unreliable for research.  该来源的非权威性使其在研究中不可靠。|lack of authority, unofficial nature, uncertainty|authoritativeness, certainty, definiteness|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + authoritative + -ness.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not". "Authoritative" comes from "authority", which ultimately derives from the Latin word "auctoritas", meaning "origin, influence, power". The suffix "-ness" turns an adjective into a noun, indicating a state or quality.
