denationalising:/diːˌnæʃənəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使非国有化; 私有化
The act of transferring ownership or control of an industry or service from the government to private individuals or companies.
The government is denationalising the railway system. 政府正在将铁路系统私有化。
Synonyms: privatizing, deregulating
Antonyms: nationalizing, socializing
v.
