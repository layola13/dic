lobeliaceous:/ˌloʊˌbiːlɪˈeɪʃəs/
adjective
半边莲科的; 山梗菜科的
Relating to or denoting plants of the Lobeliaceae family, which includes herbs and shrubs with two-lipped, tubular flowers.
The small blue flowers are characteristic of many lobeliaceous plants.  这些蓝色的小花是许多半边莲科植物的特征。
Lobelia cardinalis is a popular lobeliaceous garden plant.  红衣主教花是一种很受欢迎的半边莲科园林植物。
Near synonyms:  campanulaceous
Antonyms:  None (lack of a specific antonym for a taxonomic adjective)
词性: adjective
