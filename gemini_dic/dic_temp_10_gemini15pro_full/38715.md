condimental:/ˌkɒndɪˈmɛntəl/ | adj. | 调味用的；作佐料的 | Relating to, or used as a condiment; serving to season food. |  The chef added various condimental herbs to enhance the flavor of the dish.  厨师添加了各种调味香草来提升菜肴的风味。 | Synonyms: seasoning, flavoring, spicy, savory | Antonyms: bland, tasteless | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *condimentālis, from condimentum (“spice, seasoning”), from condīre (“to pickle, preserve, season”), from com- (“together”) + -dīre, related to dare (“to give”).
