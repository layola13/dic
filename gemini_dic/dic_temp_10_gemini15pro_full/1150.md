accipenser: /ækˈsɪpənsər/
noun
鲟鱼
A genus of fish that includes the sturgeon.  These fish are valued for their roe (caviar) and isinglass.
The beluga sturgeon, an accipenser, is the source of the most prized caviar.
白鲟，一种鲟鱼，是最珍贵鱼子酱的来源。

Accipenser sturgeon are found in both freshwater and marine environments.
鲟鱼可以在淡水和海洋环境中找到。

近义词: sturgeon
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific genus of fish)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *accipēnser*, from *accipiter* (“hawk”) +‎ *-ēnser*, perhaps comparing the fish's bony plates to a hawk's feathers or talons.
