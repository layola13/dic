anti-royalist:/ˌæntiˈrɔɪəlɪst/
noun
反保皇党人；反君主制者
A person who opposes monarchy or royal rule.
例句：The anti-royalists gained power during the revolution.  革命期间反保皇党人获得了权力。

例句：He was a known anti-royalist and advocated for a republic. 他是一位知名的反保皇党人，拥护共和制。
近义词：republican, democrat, non-monarchist
反义词：royalist, monarchist
词性:n.


anti-royalist:/ˌæntiˈrɔɪəlɪst/
adjective
反保皇党的；反君主制的
Opposed to monarchy or royal rule.
例句：Anti-royalist sentiment was growing in the country.  反保皇党情绪在这个国家日益增长。

例句：The newspaper published several anti-royalist articles. 这家报纸发表了几篇反君主制的文章。
近义词：republican, democratic, non-monarchist
反义词：royalist, monarchist
词性:adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix): from Greek anti- "against, opposite"
royalist: from royal + -ist (suffix forming agent nouns)
royal: from Old French roial, from Latin regalis "regal," from rex "king"
