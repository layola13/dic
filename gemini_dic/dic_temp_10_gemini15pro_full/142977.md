outbaking:/ˌaʊtˈbeɪkɪŋ/
verb
比...烤得更好, 在烘焙方面胜过
Surpassing someone in baking skills.
She outbaked her mother with her prize-winning apple pie. 她用她那屡获殊荣的苹果派在烘焙方面胜过了她母亲。
近义词: outdo, surpass
反义词: be outdone by, be surpassed by
词性: verb


Etymology:
"out-" (prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing) + "bake" (verb meaning to cook food by dry heat without direct exposure to a flame, typically in an oven)
