exposure: /ɪkˈspəʊʒə(r)/ (UK) /ɪkˈspoʊʒər/ (US)| n.| 暴露；曝光；揭露；接触；体验；(尤指危险的)处境|The act of exposing something to something else. The state of being exposed to something. |例句：Prolonged exposure to the sun can cause skin cancer.  长时间暴露在阳光下会导致皮肤癌。

例句：The exposure of the politician's corruption led to his resignation.  这位政客的腐败行为被曝光后，他被迫辞职。|近义词：uncovering, revelation, disclosure, subjection|反义词：concealment, protection, shelter|词性:noun
