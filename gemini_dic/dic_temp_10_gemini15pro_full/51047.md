desolately:/ˈdesələtli/ /ˈdɛsələtli/| adv.|凄凉地，荒凉地，孤寂地|In a desolate manner; lonely; sadly; hopelessly.  Characterized by emptiness, loneliness, or despair.|The wind howled desolately across the empty moor. 风凄凉地呼啸着掠过空旷的荒野。|Synonyms: bleakly, forlornly, miserably, drearily|Antonyms: cheerfully, happily, joyfully|adverb

desolate:/ˈdesələt/ /ˈdɛsələt/| adj.|荒凉的，凄凉的，孤寂的|Deserted and barren; lonely; miserable.|The desolate landscape stretched before them, empty and forbidding. 荒凉的景色在他们面前延伸，空旷而险恶。|Synonyms: barren, bleak, deserted, forlorn|Antonyms: cheerful, fertile, populated|adjective
