brimmingly:/ˈbrɪmɪŋli/
adverb
充满地，洋溢地
Full to the brim; overflowing.  Filled with or showing an abundance of something, especially a positive emotion.
Her eyes were brimmingly with tears of joy. 她的眼中充满了喜悦的泪水。
Near synonyms: overflowing, brimming over, abundantly, copiously
Antonyms: sparsely, meagerly, sparingly
词性: 副词
