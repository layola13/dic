dent's:/dɛnts/| n.|凹痕的；凹陷的；齿的|dent's is the possessive form of "dent," meaning belonging to or associated with a dent.  It indicates ownership or origin related to a hollow or depression in a surface.|The dent's location on the car door made it difficult to open.  车门上凹痕的位置使它难以打开。|近义词：depression's, hollow's, indentation's|反义词:bulge's, protrusion's|词性:名词(possessive)

dent's:/dɛnts/| n.|牙齿的|dent's can informally refer to something related to teeth, particularly dentures. This usage is much less common than the possessive of "dent" meaning a hollow.|My grandma's dent's weren't fitting properly today. 我奶奶的假牙今天不太合适。|近义词：teeth's, dentures'|反义词: N/A|词性:名词(possessive, informal)
