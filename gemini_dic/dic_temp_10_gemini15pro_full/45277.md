cultriform: /ˈkʌltrɪfɔːrm/
adjective
刀形的，似刀形的
shaped like a knife or dagger; resembling a coulter (a blade attached to the beam of a plough)
The surgeon made a cultriform incision.  外科医生做了一个刀形的切口。
The plant's leaves are cultriform.  这种植物的叶子是刀形的。
近义词: knife-shaped, blade-shaped, lanceolate
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider shapes opposite of elongated and pointed, e.g., round, ovate)
词性: adjective
