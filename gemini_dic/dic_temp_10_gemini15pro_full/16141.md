baning:/ˈbeɪnɪŋ/
baning | adj. | 禁止的，取缔的  |  Relating to the act of prohibiting or forbidding something. | The government issued a baning order on the sale of certain products. 政府发布了禁止销售某些产品的命令。 | 近义词：prohibitory, prohibitive | 反义词：permissive, allowing | 词性: 形容词 (derived from the verb "to ban")


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "ban," which originates from Old English "bannan" meaning "to summon, proclaim, command."  Later, it evolved to mean "prohibit" or "forbid."
