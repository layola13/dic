excrescential:/ˌɛkskrɪˈsɛnʃəl/
adjective
异常生长的，赘生的
growing out of something else in an abnormal way; superfluous
The excrescential growth on the tree looked like a giant wart.  树上异常生长的东西看起来像一个巨大的疣。
The politician's excrescential commentary was irrelevant to the debate.  这位政客多余的评论与辩论无关。
近义词: superfluous, extraneous, adventitious
反义词: inherent, essential, integral
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin excrescentia (“growth, excrescence”), from excrescens, present participle of excrescere (“to grow out”), from ex- (“out”) + crescere (“to grow”).
