enzoo: /ɛnˈzuː/
noun
圈养的动物;动物园里的动物
An animal that lives in a zoo.
The enzoo suffered from stress due to the constant noise.  这只动物园里的动物因为持续的噪音而感到压力很大。
近义词: captive animal, zoo animal
反义词: wild animal
词性: 名词


词源:  "enzoo" is a relatively modern word, likely a back-formation from "zoological garden".  It combines "en-" (meaning "in" or "within") and "zoo" (short for "zoological garden").
