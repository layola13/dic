paragutta:/ˌpærəˈɡʌtə/ | n. | 巴拉圭橡胶树，巴拉圭胶 | A South American tree (Hevea brasiliensis) that is the chief source of natural rubber.  It is also cultivated in other tropical areas. |  The paragutta tree thrives in the Amazon rainforest. 巴拉圭橡胶树在亚马逊雨林中茁壮成长。|  The demand for paragutta increased with the invention of the pneumatic tire.  随着充气轮胎的发明，对巴拉圭橡胶树的需求增加了。 | synonyms: rubber tree, Hevea brasiliensis,  caoutchouc tree | antonyms: N/A | noun
