pacesetters:/ˈpeɪsˌsɛtərz/
n.
领跑者；标兵；带头人
Those who set the pace, especially in terms of achievement or innovation.  Those who establish a standard of performance for others to follow.
Tesla is often seen as a pacesetter in the electric vehicle industry.  特斯拉通常被视为电动汽车行业的领跑者。
The company is a pacesetter in the development of new technologies.  该公司在新技术开发方面处于领先地位。
Synonyms: leader, pioneer, innovator, trailblazer, vanguard
Antonyms: follower, laggard, straggler
noun
