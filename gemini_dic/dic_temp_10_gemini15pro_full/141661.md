Also, the best or most favorable condition, degree, or amount.|例句：The optimus temperature for growing these plants is 20 degrees Celsius.  这些植物生长的最佳温度是20摄氏度。

例句：We need to find the optimus solution for this problem. 我们需要找到解决这个问题的最佳方案。|近义词：best, ideal, optimal, prime|反义词：worst, poorest, least favorable|词性:形容词/名词


Etymology->
From Latin *optimus*, superlative of *bonus* ("good"). Related to English *boon*.
