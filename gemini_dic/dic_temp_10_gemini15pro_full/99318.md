intercrusting:/ˌɪntərˈkrʌstɪŋ/
n.
结壳；硬壳形成；壳间填充物
The formation of a hard crust or layer between other layers or on a surface.
The intercrusting of minerals formed a beautiful pattern on the cave walls.  矿物质的结壳在洞壁上形成了美丽的图案。

近义词:incrustation, layering, deposition
反义词:erosion, dissolution
词性:名词
