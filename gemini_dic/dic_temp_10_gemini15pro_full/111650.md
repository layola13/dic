locationally:/loʊˈkeɪʃənəli/
adv.
位置上地，根据位置地
Regarding or based on location; in terms of location.
The data is locationally specific, showing variations across different regions. 这些数据是根据位置而定的，显示了不同区域的差异。
The resources are distributed locationally to maximize efficiency. 这些资源按位置分布，以最大限度地提高效率。
Near synonyms: geographically, positionally, spatially
Antonyms: generally, universally
词性: 副词
