disadvantageously:/ˌdɪsədvænˈteɪdʒəsli/
adverb
不利地，劣势地
in a way that causes disadvantage; in a way that is not favorable.
He was disadvantageously placed in the competition. 他在比赛中处于不利地位。
近义词: unfavorably, detrimentally, poorly, badly
反义词: advantageously, favorably, beneficially
词性: adverb

Etymology:
From disadvantage + -ous + -ly.  Disadvantage comes from the Old French desavantage, from des- (expressing negation) + avantage (advantage).
