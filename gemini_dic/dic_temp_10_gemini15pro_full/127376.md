neander:/niˈændər/ /ˈneɪændər/ | n. |尼安德特人| A Neanderthal (Homo neanderthalensis or Homo sapiens neanderthalensis) is an extinct species or subspecies of archaic humans who lived in Eurasia until about 40,000 years ago. |例句：Neanderthals were skilled hunters and toolmakers. 尼安德特人是熟练的猎人和工具制造者。

例句：Recent research suggests that Neanderthals interbred with Homo sapiens. 最近的研究表明，尼安德特人与智人杂交。|近义词：Neanderthal, Homo neanderthalensis |反义词: Homo sapiens (in the sense of a distinct lineage) |词性: 名词
