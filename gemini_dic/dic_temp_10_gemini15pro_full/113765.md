macropodal: /ˌmækrəˈpoʊdəl/ | adj. | 大脚的；长脚的 | Having large or long feet.  Relating to or resembling a macropod (such as a kangaroo or wallaby). |  The macropodal creature bounded across the plains.  这只长脚的生物跳跃着穿过平原。 | The macropodal kangaroo easily hopped over the fence. 这只大脚的袋鼠轻松地跳过了栅栏。 |  long-legged, large-footed | short-legged, small-footed | adjective
