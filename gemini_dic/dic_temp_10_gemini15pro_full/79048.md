giveaways:/ˈɡɪvəˌweɪz/
n.
赠品；泄露；免费赠送的商品；（尤指比赛中的）致胜因素，决定性因素
Something that is given away free, especially something that a company gives away to advertise its products; an unintentional revelation or betrayal.
The radio station is having a giveaway.  这家广播电台正在进行赠品活动。
His nervous laugh was a dead giveaway. 他紧张的笑声完全暴露了他。
近义词：freebies, prizes, promotions, gifts, reveals, clues, hints, indicators
反义词：purchases, secrets, concealments
词性: 名词 (plural)
