immersive:/ɪˈmɜːsɪv/| adj.|沉浸式的，身临其境的|Fully involving the senses and creating a feeling of being present in an environment or experience.|例句：The new video game is incredibly immersive.  这款新的电子游戏非常具有沉浸感。

例句：The virtual reality experience was truly immersive, making me feel like I was actually there. 虚拟现实体验真的身临其境，让我感觉自己真的在那里。|近义词：engrossing, captivating, absorbing|反义词：superficial, shallow, uninvolving|词性:形容词
