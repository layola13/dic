ETYMOLOGY->
From neighbor + -liness.  "neighbor" comes from Old English "nēahgebūr," meaning "near dweller," from "nēah" (near) + "gebūr" (dweller). "-liness" is a suffix forming abstract nouns denoting a state or quality.
