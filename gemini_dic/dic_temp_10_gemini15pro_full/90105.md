hot-bedding:/ˈhɒtˌbɛdɪŋ/ (US) /ˈhɒtˌbedɪŋ/ (UK)
noun
轮床制，多人共用床位（尤指在医院或监狱）; 温床，滋生地
The practice of allocating a bed to more than one person over a 24-hour period, especially in hospitals or prisons, typically by assigning different shifts to different individuals. Also refers to something that fosters the growth or development of something else, often something undesirable.
The hospital had to resort to hot-bedding because of the overwhelming number of patients.  这家医院由于病人太多，不得不采取轮床制。
Extremism finds a hotbed in conditions of poverty and inequality. 极端主义在贫穷和不平等的环境中找到了滋生的温床。
近义词: shared accommodation, bunk sharing, breeding ground
反义词: private room, single occupancy
