bandle:/ˈbændl/| n. |捆, 束, 卷|A bandle is a small bundle or package, usually of something soft or flexible, like yarn, straw, or hair.|She carried a bandle of straw under her arm. 她胳膊下夹着一捆稻草。|近义词：bundle, sheaf, bunch|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


Etymology:
From Scots <i>bandle</i>, probably a diminutive of <i>band</i>, from Old Norse <i>band</i> (“a bond, tie”).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic <i>*bandą</i> (“something that binds”).
