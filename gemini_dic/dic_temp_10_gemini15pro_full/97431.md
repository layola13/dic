infuriate:/ɪnˈfjʊərieɪt/
verb
激怒，使大怒
to make someone extremely angry and impatient
His constant lateness infuriated her. 他总是迟到让她很生气。
近义词: enrage, anger, madden, incense
反义词: calm, soothe, placate
词性:v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *infuriatus, past participle of infuriare “to enrage,” from in- “in” + Latin furor “rage, madness.”
