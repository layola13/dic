nonprocurements:/ˌnɑːnprəˈkjʊrmənts/
noun
非采购
Items or services that are not obtained through a formal purchasing process.  This often refers to items received through donation, transfer, or other means besides a standard procurement contract.
The university received several nonprocurements of lab equipment from a partnering institution. 大学从合作机构那里收到了一些非采购的实验室设备。
The report detailed both procurements and nonprocurements for the fiscal year.  该报告详细列出了本财政年度的采购和非采购项目。
synonyms: donations, gifts, transfers, non-purchases
antonyms: procurements, purchases, acquisitions
