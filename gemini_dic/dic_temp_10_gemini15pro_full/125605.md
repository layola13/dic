multivariate:/ˌmʌltɪˈveəriət/ | adj. | 多变量的，多维的 |  Involving or relating to multiple variables or factors.  It describes something that considers or analyzes many different elements or aspects simultaneously. |  Multivariate analysis was used to study the relationship between lifestyle factors and disease risk.  多变量分析被用来研究生活方式因素与疾病风险之间的关系。

Multivariate data is complex and requires specialized statistical methods. 多变量数据很复杂，需要专门的统计方法。|  multidimensional, multiple-variable | univariate, single-variable | adjective
