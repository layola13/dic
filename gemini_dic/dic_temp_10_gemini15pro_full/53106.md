diphthongizing:/dɪfˈθɒŋɡaɪzɪŋ/
| v. |双元音化|The process of changing a single vowel sound into a diphthong.|The Great Vowel Shift in English involved the diphthongizing of several long vowels.|近义词: changing, altering, modifying|反义词: monophthongizing|verb


diphthongization:/dɪfˌθɒŋɡaɪˈzeɪʃən/
| n. |双元音化|The act or process of diphthongizing.|The diphthongization of vowels played a significant role in the evolution of the English language.|近义词: alteration, modification, change|反义词: monophthongization|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From diphthong + -ize + -ing/-ation.  Diphthong comes from Greek *diphthongos*, from *di-*, "two" + *phthongos*, "sound."
