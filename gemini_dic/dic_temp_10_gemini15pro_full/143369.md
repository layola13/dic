outfeasted: /ˌaʊtˈfiːstɪd/
verb
胜过宴请; 宴饮超过
to surpass in feasting; to have eaten more than another at a feast.
He outfeasted all the other guests, consuming a whole boar by himself. 他在宴会上吃得比所有其他客人都多，一个人吃掉了一整头野猪。
近义词: out-eat, surpass in eating
反义词: be out-eaten


ETYMOLOGY->
From "out-" (meaning exceeding, surpassing) + "feast" (a large meal, especially a celebratory one).  "Out-" as a prefix is of Old English origin, while "feast" comes from Old French "feste."
