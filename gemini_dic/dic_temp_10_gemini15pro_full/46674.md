dampen:/ˈdæmpən/ | v. | 使潮湿；抑制；减弱 | to make something slightly wet; to make something less strong | The rain dampened the ground. 雨水打湿了地面。|  synonyms: moisten, dampen, stifle, subdue, restrain | antonyms: dry, encourage, intensify | verb
