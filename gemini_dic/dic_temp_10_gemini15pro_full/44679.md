cruciform:/ˈkruːsɪfɔːrm/ | adj. n. | 十字形的；十字形物 | Having the shape of a cross. |  The church has a cruciform layout. 教堂的布局是十字形的。| The building is cruciform in shape. 这座建筑呈十字形。 | cross-shaped, crosswise |  - | adjective, noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cruciformis*  (cross-shaped), from *crux* (cross) + *-formis* (having the form of).
