antilogy:/ænˈtɪlədʒi/ | n. |三部曲；对照；矛盾 | A series of three literary or dramatic works that are connected, often thematically. A contradiction between two beliefs, ideas, or statements. | Example 1: The author is planning an antilogy exploring the themes of love, loss, and redemption.  作者正在计划一个探索爱、失去和救赎主题的三部曲。

Example 2: His philosophy contains an antilogy, claiming both free will and determinism. 他的哲学包含一个矛盾，同时声称自由意志和决定论。| Near Synonyms: trilogy, series, contradiction, inconsistency | Antonyms: consistency, agreement, harmony | noun
