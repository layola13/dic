They interaccused each other of lying. 他们互相指责对方撒谎。
interaccuse (third-person singular simple present interaccuses, present participle interaccusing, simple past and past participle interaccused)
例句：The two suspects interaccused each other of starting the fight.  两个嫌疑人互相指责对方挑起了争斗。

例句：The politicians interaccused each other of corruption during the debate.  政客们在辩论中互相指责对方腐败。

近义词：blame each other, accuse each other, point fingers at each other
反义词：exonerate each other, defend each other
词性：动词
