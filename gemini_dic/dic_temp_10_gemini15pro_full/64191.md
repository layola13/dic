equestrian:/ɪˈkwɛstriən/ /ɪˈkwestriən/
noun|骑马的；骑术的；骑手|A person who rides on horseback; relating to horseback riding.|She is a skilled equestrian. 她是一位技艺娴熟的骑手。|
近义词:rider, horseman, horsewoman|反义词:pedestrian|词性:n. adj.

equestrian:/ɪˈkwɛstriən/ /ɪˈkwestriən/
adjective|骑马的；骑术的|Relating to horseback riding.|Equestrian sports are popular in many countries. 马术运动在许多国家都很流行。|近义词:riding|反义词:walking|词性:adj.
