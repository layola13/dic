collaborationist:/kəˌlæbəˈreɪʃənɪst/
n.
通敌者，合作者，协助者
A person who cooperates traitorously with an enemy; a person who collaborates with an enemy occupying force.
The Vichy government in France included many collaborationists who worked with the Nazis.  法国维希政府包括许多与纳粹合作的通敌者。
The newspaper editor was accused of being a collaborationist during the war.  这家报纸的编辑被指控在战争期间是通敌者。
近义词:  traitor, quisling, cooperator
反义词:  resister, partisan, patriot
词性: noun


Etymology:
From French *collaborationniste*, from *collaboration* (collaboration) + *-iste* (suffix forming agent nouns).  Ultimately from Latin *collaborare* (to work together).
