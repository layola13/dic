duke:/djuːk/| n.|公爵，君主，大公|A duke is a male member of the nobility, ranking below a prince and above a marquis. 公爵是贵族中男性成员，地位低于王子，高于侯爵。|The Duke of Cambridge attended the ceremony. 剑桥公爵出席了仪式。|近义词:nobleman, lord, peer|反义词:commoner|noun

duke:/djuːk/| v.|挥拳猛击|To duke it out means to fight or compete fiercely. 挥拳猛击意味着激烈地战斗或竞争。|They duked it out in the boxing ring. 他们在拳击台上决一死战。|近义词:fight, battle, brawl|反义词:yield, surrender|verb
