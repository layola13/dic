mules:/mjuːlz/| n.|骡子；拖鞋；顽固的人|A mule is the offspring of a male donkey and a female horse. It is known for its strength and surefootedness but is sterile.|The farmer used mules to plow his fields.  农民用骡子犁地。|Synonyms: hybrid, crossbreed, slippers (when referring to shoes), stubborn person|Antonyms: horse, donkey, open-toed shoes (when referring to shoes), compliant person|noun

mules:/mjuːlz/| n.|拖鞋|Mules are a type of backless shoe.|She wore a pair of stylish mules to the party. 她穿着一双时髦的拖鞋去参加聚会。|Synonyms: slippers, slides|Antonyms: boots, closed-toe shoes|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *mul, from Latin mulus "mule."
