espousers:/ɪˈspaʊzərz/
n.
拥护者，支持者
People who support or advocate for a particular cause or belief.
The espousers of this new technology believe it will revolutionize the industry.  这种新技术的支持者认为它将彻底改变这个行业。
supporters, advocates, proponents, champions
opponents, detractors, antagonists
noun
