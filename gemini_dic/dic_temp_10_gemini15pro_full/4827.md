aliases:/ˈeɪliəsɪz/ | n. |别名，化名|  A name that is not someone's official or legal name, but that they are known by. | Clark Kent is Superman's alias.  克拉克·肯特是超人的化名。 | synonyms: pseudonym, assumed name, nom de plume, sobriquet | antonyms: real name, legal name | noun

aliases:/ˈeɪliəsɪz/ | v. |取别名，用别名| To use a different name, especially to hide your real identity. | He aliased himself as John Smith. 他用约翰·史密斯的别名。 | synonyms:  masquerade as, pose as, go by, be known as | antonyms:  use one's real name, identify oneself | verb
