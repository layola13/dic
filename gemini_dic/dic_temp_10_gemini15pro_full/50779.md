descanted:/dɪˈskæntɪd/
verb
详述；评论；演唱；高谈阔论
to speak or write at length on a particular subject, especially in a learned or elaborate way; to sing a descant.
He descanted on the merits of organic farming.  他详细阐述了有机农业的优点。
The professor descanted on the history of the Roman Empire for hours. 教授详细讲述了几个小时的罗马帝国历史。
近义词: elaborated, expounded, commented, discoursed
反义词: summarized, abbreviated
