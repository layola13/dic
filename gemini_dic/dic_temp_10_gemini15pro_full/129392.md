nomenclature:/ˈnoʊmənˌkleɪtʃər/ | n. |命名法；术语；命名系统 | A system of names used in a particular specialist field. |  Example: The nomenclature of organic chemistry is complex.  有机化学的命名法很复杂。

Example:  The scientific nomenclature can be confusing for beginners.  对于初学者来说，科学命名法可能会令人困惑。 | Synonyms: terminology, classification, system, taxonomy | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "misnomer," which refers to an incorrect name.) | noun
