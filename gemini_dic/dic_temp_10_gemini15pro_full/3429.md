africana:/ˌæfrɪˈkɑːnə/ /ˌæfrɪˈkænə/ | n. | 非洲文物；非洲文献；非洲研究|  Materials relating to Africa, especially historical and cultural materials. |  The library has a vast collection of Africana, including rare books and manuscripts. 图书馆收藏了大量的非洲文献，包括珍本和手稿。 | 近义词: African studies, African history, African culture | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From the Latin word "Africanus" meaning "African," with the added suffix "-ana" which indicates a collection of materials related to a particular subject or place.  源自拉丁语 “Africanus”，意为“非洲的”，加上后缀“-ana”，表示与特定主题或地点相关的材料集合。
