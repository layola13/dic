agrimony:/ˈæɡrɪməni/| n.|龙牙草；仙鹤草|A perennial herbaceous plant of the rose family, with small yellow flowers and spiky burrs that cling to clothing. It has been used in traditional medicine.|例句：Agrimony was used to treat minor wounds. 龙牙草被用来治疗轻微的伤口。

例句：She picked some agrimony for her herbal tea. 她摘了一些龙牙草来泡茶。|近义词：herb, plant|反义词: N/A |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *agrimoine*, from Old French *agrimoine*, from Latin *agrimonia*, perhaps ultimately from Greek *argemon*, a poppy-like plant. The change from *argemon* to *agrimonia* may be due to folk etymology associating it with *ager* ("field").
