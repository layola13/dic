deflorate:/diːˈflɔːreɪt/
verb
剥夺…的童贞；夺去…的纯洁；使…凋谢
to deprive of virginity; to take away the purity of something; to cause something to wither or lose its freshness.
The young woman was deflorated on her wedding night.  这位年轻女子在新婚之夜失去了童贞。
The harsh winds deflorated the delicate blossoms.  狂风使娇嫩的花朵凋谢了。
近义词:ravish, violate, defile
反义词:preserve, protect
词性:v.
