brainwashed:/ˈbreɪnwɒʃt/ | adj. | 洗脑的；被灌输的 | subjected to intensive indoctrination and propaganda, often resulting in an uncritical acceptance of a particular set of beliefs | Example: The cult leader had completely brainwashed his followers.  邪教头目彻底给他的追随者洗脑了。 | Example:  He felt brainwashed by the constant stream of propaganda on television. 他感觉自己被电视上连绵不断的宣传洗脑了。 | Synonyms: indoctrinated, programmed, conditioned, manipulated | Antonyms: independent-minded, critical, skeptical | word property: adjective
