celebrate: /ˈsɛləbreɪt/ (US), /ˈsɛlɪbreɪt/ (UK) | v. | 庆祝 | To mark a special occasion or event with festivities, ceremonies, or other observances. | We celebrated his birthday with a big party. 我们为他举办了一个盛大的生日派对。 | The team celebrated their victory with champagne.  团队用香槟庆祝他们的胜利。 |  近义词: commemorate, observe, honor | 反义词: mourn, grieve | 词性: 动词

Etymology:  From Latin celebratus, past participle of celebrare "to frequent, honor, praise," from celeber "frequented, populous, famous."
