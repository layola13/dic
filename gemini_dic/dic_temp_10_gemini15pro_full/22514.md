boomers:/ˈbuːmərz/| n.|婴儿潮一代|People born during a period of increased birth rate, typically following a war or other major social upheaval. Specifically refers to the generation born between approximately 1946 and 1964.|Example: The boomers are now reaching retirement age. 婴儿潮一代现在正在步入退休年龄。

Example:  Many boomers remember the Vietnam War. 许多婴儿潮一代都记得越南战争。|近义词:baby boomers|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider Gen X, Gen Y, or Millennials as subsequent generations) |词性:noun
