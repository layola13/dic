glycoluric:/ˌɡlaɪkəˈljʊrɪk/
adjective
糖尿的；尿中含糖的
Relating to or characterized by the presence of sugar in the urine.
Glycoluric acid is found in the urine of mammals.  哺乳动物的尿液中含有糖尿酸。
The patient's glycoluric condition indicated a problem with their blood sugar regulation. 病人的糖尿状况表明他们的血糖调节有问题。
近义词: glycosuric
反义词: aglycosuric
词性: adjective
