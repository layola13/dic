hibernate:/ˈhaɪbərneɪt/| v. |冬眠；蛰伏；(喻)停止活动；|To spend the winter in a dormant state.  Bears hibernate during the winter months. 熊在冬季冬眠。|例句：The dormouse hibernates in winter. 榛睡鼠冬天冬眠。|例句：Many insects hibernate during the cold winter months. 许多昆虫在寒冷的冬季冬眠。|近义词：sleep, overwinter, lie dormant|反义词：awaken, revive|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *hibernare*, from *hibernum* "winter," related to *hiems* "winter."
