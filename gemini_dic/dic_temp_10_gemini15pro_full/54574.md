disloyalty:/dɪsˈlɔɪəlti/ | n. |不忠诚，背叛|The act of being disloyal; unfaithfulness.  A breach of trust or allegiance. |His disloyalty to the company was evident when he leaked confidential information to a competitor. 他对公司的背叛在他向竞争对手泄露机密信息时显而易见。|Near synonyms: treachery, betrayal, perfidy, infidelity |Antonyms: loyalty, fidelity, allegiance| noun
