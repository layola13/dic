The quality of being universal or all-embracing.
The museum's collection displays the catholicity of 20th-century art.  这家博物馆的藏品展现了20世纪艺术的包罗万象。
The library boasts a catholicity of resources, from ancient manuscripts to modern e-books.  该图书馆拥有丰富的资源，从古代手稿到现代电子书应有尽有。
近义词: universality, comprehensiveness, breadth, inclusiveness
反义词: narrowness, restrictiveness, limitedness, exclusiveness
