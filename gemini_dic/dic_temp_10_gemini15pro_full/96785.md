ineradicabilities:/ˌɪnɪˈrædɪkəˈbɪlɪtiz/
|n.| 不可根除性；无法消除性|The ineradicabilities of certain prejudices are a persistent social problem.  某些偏见的不可根除性是一个持续存在的社会问题。|The report highlighted the ineradicabilities of poverty in certain regions. 报告强调了某些地区贫困的顽固性。|近义词: immutability, permanence, fixity|反义词: eradicability, removability, transience|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From ineradicable + -ities.
Ineradicable: from in- (not) + eradicable (capable of being eradicated).
Eradicable: from eradicate + -able.
Eradicate: from Latin eradicatus, past participle of eradicare (to root out, pluck up by the roots), from ex- (out) + radix (root).
