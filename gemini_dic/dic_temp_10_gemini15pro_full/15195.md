backchat:/ˈbækˌtʃæt/ | n. |闲聊，轻松的谈话| Light, informal conversation; banter. |  We enjoyed some friendly backchat during our lunch break.  我们在午休时间进行了一些友好的闲聊。 | synonyms: retort, repartee, impudence, banter, chat | antonyms: respect, obedience | noun
