mudslinging:/ˈmʌdˌslɪŋɪŋ/
noun
诽谤，恶意中伤
The act of making malicious and untrue statements about an opponent, especially in politics.
The election campaign descended into mudslinging. 选举活动沦为互相诽谤。
近义词:smear, slander, defamation, libel, character assassination
反义词:praise, commendation, acclaim, adulation
词性:noun

Etymology:
From mud + slinging (present participle of sling).  The image is of throwing mud to soil or damage someone's reputation.
