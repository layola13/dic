papacies: /ˈpeɪpəsiːz/
| n. |教皇职位，教皇的任期；教皇权威|The papacies of the Renaissance were a time of great artistic patronage. 文艺复兴时期的教皇职位是艺术赞助的伟大时代。|The museum holds artifacts from various papacies.  博物馆收藏了来自不同教皇任期的文物。|近义词：pontificate, reign|反义词: 无|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *papasee, from Old French papauté, from Medieval Latin pāpatus, from Latin pāpa (“pope”).
