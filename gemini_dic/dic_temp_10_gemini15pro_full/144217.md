outskirmish: /ˌaʊtˈskɜːrmɪʃ/
verb
以小规模战斗胜过;在小冲突中击败
to defeat in a skirmish; surpass in minor fighting
例句：The smaller, faster boats were able to outskirmish the larger warships. 那些更小更快的船能够在小规模战斗中击败更大的战舰。

例句：The rebel forces outskirmished the government troops. 叛军在小冲突中击败了政府军。

近义词：outmaneuver, outfight, defeat, overcome
反义词：lose to, be defeated by
词性：verb
