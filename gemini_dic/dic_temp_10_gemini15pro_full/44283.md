crony:/ˈkroʊni/| n.|密友，亲信，死党|A close friend or companion, especially one who may be suspected of receiving favors due to the relationship.|He got the job because his crony is the mayor. 他得到这份工作是因为他的死党是市长。|Synonyms: chum, pal, buddy, mate|Antonyms: enemy, foe, rival|noun
