nonconsonances:/nɒnˈkɒnsənənsɪz/
| n. |不和谐音；非辅音|Lack of harmony or agreement, especially in sounds; specifically, a combination of sounds that are not pleasing to the ear. Also, absence of consonants.|The piece was full of nonconsonances, making it difficult to listen to.  这首曲子充满了不和谐音，让人难以入耳。|近义词：discord, dissonance, disharmony|反义词：harmony, consonance, concord|noun
