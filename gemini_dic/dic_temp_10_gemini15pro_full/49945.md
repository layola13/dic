dendroid:/ˈdendrɔɪd/| adj. n.|树状的，树枝状的；树状物|Resembling a tree in form; branching like a tree.  A plant that resembles a tree, especially a small tree or shrub.|The coral had a dendroid structure. 这珊瑚有着树状的结构。|The dendroid fossil was millions of years old.  这块树状化石有数百万年的历史了。|近义词：arborescent, treelike, branching, ramified|反义词:linear, straight|词性:adjective, noun|词源:From Greek *dendron* "tree" + *-oid* "resembling."
