fessitude:/ˈfesɪtjuːd/
n.
倦怠；疲乏；没精打采
A state of physical or mental weariness; lack of energy.
She was overcome by a sudden fessitude. 她突然感到一阵疲乏。
Near synonyms: weariness, fatigue, lassitude, lethargy, languor, exhaustion
Antonyms: energy, vigor, vitality
词性: 名词
