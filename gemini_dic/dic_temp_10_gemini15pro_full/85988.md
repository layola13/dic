headstall:/ˈhɛdstɔːl/ (US) /ˈhɛdstɔːl/ (UK)| n.|马笼头|The part of a bridle that fits around a horse's head.|
The horse's headstall was too tight and he was uncomfortable.|马的笼头太紧了，它很不舒服。|
The headstall was adorned with silver conchos.|笼头上装饰着银质的圆形饰物。|
Synonyms: bridle, headpiece|
