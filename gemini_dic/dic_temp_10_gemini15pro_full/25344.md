builder:/ˈbɪldər/| n.| 建造者，建设者；开发者|A person or company whose job is to build or construct something, such as houses, offices, bridges, etc.| The builder is finishing the roof today.  今天建筑工人正在完成屋顶的建造。| synonyms: constructor, developer, erector, architect | antonyms: demolisher, destroyer | noun

Etymology: From Middle English *bildere, from Old English *byldere, from Proto-Germanic *bildōną (“to build”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰel- (“to swell, grow”).
