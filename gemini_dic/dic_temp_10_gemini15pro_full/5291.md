allotheism:/ˌæləˈθiːɪzəm/
n.
异神崇拜；崇拜异教神
The belief in or worship of a god or gods other than one's own.
例句：Allotheism is the opposite of monotheism. 异神崇拜是与一神论相反的。

例句：Her studies focused on the allotheism practiced by ancient Romans. 她的研究集中于古罗马人所信奉的异神崇拜。
近义词：polytheism, paganism
反义词：monotheism
词性：名词
