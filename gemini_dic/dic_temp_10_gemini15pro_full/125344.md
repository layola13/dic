multilineal:/ˌmʌltiˈlɪniəl/ | adj. | 多系的；多谱系的；多线的 | Descended from many lines of ancestry.  Relating to or involving several lines of descent. |  The family's multilineal heritage included ancestors from several different countries.  这个家族的多系传承包括来自几个不同国家的祖先。 | polyphyletic, multi-ancestral | unilineal | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From multi- (many) + lineal (of or in a line)
