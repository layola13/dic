dogcart:/ˈdɒɡkɑːt/ (UK) /ˈdɔːɡkɑːrt/ (US)| n. |狗车；轻便双轮马车|A light, open, two-wheeled carriage, pulled by one horse, originally designed for carrying sporting dogs but later used for general transport.|He drove his dogcart through the village. 他驾着他的狗车穿过村庄。|Synonyms: carriage, buggy, cart|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "automobile" as a modern replacement)|Noun

dogcart:/ˈdɒɡkɑːt/ (UK) /ˈdɔːɡkɑːrt/ (US)| n. |狗推车|A cart designed for a dog to pull.|The children harnessed the dog to the dogcart and played in the park. 孩子们把狗套到狗推车上，在公园里玩耍。|Synonyms: cart, wagon|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym)|Noun
