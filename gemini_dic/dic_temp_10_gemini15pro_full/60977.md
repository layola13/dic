elicitation:/ɪˌlɪsɪˈteɪʃən/
n.|启发; 引出; 诱出|The elicitation of information from the witness was crucial to the case. 从证人那里获取信息对于这个案子至关重要。|derivation: from elicit (v.), from Latin elicere "to draw out, entice," from ex- "out" + lacere "to entice, ensnare."|Examples: The teacher used various techniques for the elicitation of responses from her students. 老师用各种技巧来引出学生的回答。 The researchers focused on the elicitation of requirements for the new software. 研究人员专注于引出新软件的需求。|Synonyms: extraction, obtaining, evoking|Antonyms: suppression, concealment, repression|noun
