nonsurvivors:/ˌnɒn.səˈvaɪ.vəz/ | n. | 非幸存者，未幸存者 | People who did not survive a particular event, such as a disaster or illness.  Those who perished. |  The nonsurvivors of the plane crash were mourned by the nation.  这个国家为飞机失事的遇难者哀悼。| Synonyms: casualties, victims, deceased, fatalities | Antonyms: survivors | Noun
