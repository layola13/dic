contabescence:/ˌkɒntəˈbɛsəns/
noun
衰弱；消瘦；憔悴
The process of wasting away; a decline in health and strength.
The doctor observed the patient's rapid contabescence with concern. 医生关切地观察到病人迅速的衰弱。
The contabescence of the empire was evident in its crumbling infrastructure and declining military power.  帝国的衰弱在其崩溃的基础设施和日益衰退的军事力量中显而易见。
近义词:emaciation, atrophy, decline, deterioration
反义词:growth, development, improvement
词性:noun


词源: From Latin *contabescere (“to melt away, waste away”), from com- (“together, with”) + tabescere (“to waste away, melt away”).
