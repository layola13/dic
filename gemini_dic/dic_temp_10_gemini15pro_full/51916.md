dhurnas:/ˈdʊrnəz/
n.
抗议静坐，绝食静坐
A form of non-violent protest in which people sit or fast in a public place.
例句：The workers staged a dharna outside the factory gates. 工人们在工厂大门外举行静坐抗议。

例句：The dhurnas continued for several weeks until the government agreed to negotiate. 静坐抗议持续了数周，直到政府同意谈判。

近义词：sit-in, protest, demonstration
反义词：N/A
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi धरना (dharnā), from Sanskrit धारणा (dhāraṇā), meaning "holding, maintaining, persistence".  The word entered English during the British Raj.
