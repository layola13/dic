foreword:/ˈfɔːrwɜːrd/ (US), /ˈfɔːwɜːd/ (UK)| n. |前言，序言|An introductory note at the beginning of a book, typically written by someone other than the author.|例句：The foreword of the book was written by a famous scholar. 这本书的序言是由一位著名学者撰写的。

例句：I skipped the foreword and went straight to the first chapter. 我跳过了前言，直接开始读第一章。


近义词：preface, introduction, prologue|反义词: afterword, epilogue|词性: 名词
