frescoist:/ˈfrɛskoʊɪst/
n.
壁画画家
An artist who paints frescoes.
The renowned frescoist spent years perfecting his technique.  这位著名的壁画画家花了数年时间完善他的技法。
The frescoist carefully prepared the wall before applying the plaster. 壁画画家在涂抹灰泥之前仔细地准备了墙壁。
近义词: muralist
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "oil painter" if contrasting techniques)
词性: noun

词源:
From Italian *fresco* "fresh," referring to the fresh plaster on which frescoes are painted.  -ist is a suffix denoting a person who practices or is skilled in something.
