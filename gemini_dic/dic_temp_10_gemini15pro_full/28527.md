cardigan:/ˈkɑːrdɪɡən/ (US), /ˈkɑːdɪɡən/ (UK)| n.|羊毛衫；开衫毛衣|A knitted garment, such as a sweater or jacket, that opens down the front.|She wore a cardigan over her dress. 她穿着一件羊毛衫套在连衣裙外面。|近义词:sweater, jacket, jumper|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly shorts/t-shirt for contrasting clothing)|noun
