circumduce: /ˌsɜːrkəmˈd(j)uːs/ /ˌsɝːkəmˈduːs/
verb
环绕；旋转；画圆；（尤指外科手术中）切除包皮
To draw (a circle or other figure) with a compass or other instrument; to trace the outline of; to encircle. In surgery, to remove (foreskin) by circular incision.
The surgeon circumcised the baby.  外科医生给婴儿做了包皮环切手术。
The teacher circumduced a circle on the blackboard with a compass. 老师用圆规在黑板上画了一个圆。

近义词: encircle, circumscribe, rotate
反义词: - (No direct antonym, depends on the context.  Consider "straighten" if referring to foreskin, or "leave intact")
词性: verb
