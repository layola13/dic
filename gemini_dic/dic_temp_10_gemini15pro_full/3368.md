affront:/əˈfrʌnt/ | n. vt. | 冒犯；侮辱；当面冲撞 | An open or intentional insult; a slight.  An action or remark that causes outrage. To offend the dignity or self-respect of. |  His loud laughter was an affront to the solemnity of the occasion. 他的大声笑声是对这个严肃场合的冒犯。 |  He affronted her by ignoring her greeting. 他不理睬她的问候，这冒犯了她。 | Synonyms: insult, offense, slight, indignity | Antonyms: compliment, praise, honor, respect | noun
affront:/əˈfrʌnt/ | vt. | 冒犯；侮辱；当面冲撞 | To offend the dignity or self-respect of.
