bifronted:/baɪˈfrʌntɪd/
adjective
两面的，双面的，表里不一的
Having two fronts or faces; two-faced.  Figuratively, deceitful or hypocritical.
The bifronted politician spoke out of both sides of his mouth, promising different things to different groups.  这个两面三刀的政客言行不一，向不同的群体承诺不同的事情。

The Roman god Janus is often depicted as bifronted, looking both to the past and the future. 罗马神雅努斯通常被描绘成两面神，同时注视着过去和未来。

近义词: two-faced, hypocritical, duplicitous, deceitful
反义词: honest, sincere, straightforward


词性: adjective


词源:  From Latin *bifrons*, from bi- "two" + frons "forehead, face".
