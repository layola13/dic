pal's:/pælz/ | n. |朋友的；伙伴的|Possessive form of "pal," meaning belonging to or associated with a friend. | That's my pal's car. 那是我的朋友的车。| My pal's dog is very friendly. 我的朋友的狗很友好。 |近义词：friend's, buddy's, mate's |反义词:enemy's, foe's |词性:名词所有格

词源:
Shortened form of "pally," which itself is likely a shortening of "paladin," meaning a trusted companion or knight.  The modern usage emerged in the late 19th century.
