gregge:/ˈɡrɛdʒ/| n. | flock, herd |A group of animals, especially sheep or birds, that stay close together.|The shepherd led his gregge across the fields. 牧羊人带领他的羊群穿过田野。|近义词：flock, herd, drove |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "scattered") |noun


gregge:/ˈɡrɛdʒ/| n. | group, collection |A collection of people or things of a particular type.|A gregge of tourists gathered outside the museum. 一群游客聚集在博物馆外。|近义词：group, collection, assembly |反义词: individual |noun
