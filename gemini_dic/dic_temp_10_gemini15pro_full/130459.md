nonabsorptions:/ˌnɒnəbˈsɔːrpʃənz/ | n. | 非吸收; 不吸收 | The process or state of not being absorbed.  The experiment measured the nonabsorptions of certain wavelengths of light.  这个实验测量了某些波长的光的不吸收。 | 近义词: non-absorption (singular), lack of absorption | 反义词: absorptions, absorption | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "absorption" (from Latin "absorbere" - to swallow down)
