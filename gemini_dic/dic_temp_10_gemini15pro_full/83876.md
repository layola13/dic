half-admittedly:/ˌhæf ədˈmɪtɪdli/ | adv. | 半承认地，不完全承认地 | Partially or reluctantly admitted; acknowledging something with reservation or qualification. |  He half-admittedly enjoyed the attention. 他半推半就地享受着关注。 | Near Synonyms:  partly, partially, somewhat, qualifiedly, grudgingly | Antonyms:  fully, completely, wholeheartedly, unreservedly | word property: adverb

Etymology:
"half-" (Old English healf) + "-admittedly" (from "admit" - Latin admittere - to send to, let in, allow to enter + "-ed" past participle suffix + "-ly" adverb suffix)
