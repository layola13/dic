hyperparameters:/ˌhaɪpərˈpəræmɪtərz/ | n. | 超参数 |  Parameters that are not learned from the data but are set before the learning process begins. They control the learning process itself. | The learning rate and the number of hidden layers are examples of hyperparameters.  学习率和隐藏层数是超参数的例子。 | synonyms: meta-parameters | antonyms: parameters | noun

hyperparameter:/ˌhaɪpərˈpəræmɪtər/ | n. | 超参数 | A single setting that controls the learning process of a machine learning algorithm. |  The choice of hyperparameter can significantly impact the model's performance. 超参数的选择会显著影响模型的性能。| synonyms: meta-parameter | antonyms: parameter | noun
