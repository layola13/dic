dirigible:/ˈdɪrɪdʒəbəl/ /ˈdɪrɪdʒɪbəl/
n.|飞艇|A lighter-than-air aircraft that can be steered, typically elongated in shape and containing gas such as helium.|The dirigible floated gracefully across the sky.|近义词：airship, zeppelin|反义词: N/A|词性: 名词
dirigible:/ˈdɪrɪdʒəbəl/ /ˈdɪrɪdʒɪbəl/
adj.|可操纵的|Capable of being steered or directed.|The dirigible balloon was a marvel of engineering.|近义词：steerable, controllable|反义词: unsteerable, uncontrollable|词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French dirigible, from diriger "to direct" + -ible "-able," ultimately from Latin dirigere "to direct."
