misprinting:/ˌmɪsˈprɪntɪŋ/
n.
印刷错误，印错
an error in printing; a typographical error
The book was full of misprintings, making it difficult to read.  这本书里到处都是印刷错误，很难读懂。
misprint, typographical error, erratum
correct printing, accurate printing
noun
