grouched:/ɡraʊtʃt/ | adj. | 抱怨的，不高兴的 | Feeling or expressing discontent or irritability. | He came home grouched because he'd had a bad day at work. 他回家后很不高兴，因为他在工作中度过了糟糕的一天。 |  She's been grouched all week. 她整个星期都闷闷不乐。 | 近义词：grumpy, sulky, irritable, bad-tempered | 反义词：cheerful, happy, content | 词性:形容词

grouche:/ɡraʊtʃ/ | n. | 抱怨，不高兴 | A complaint or grumble. | He had a grouche about the service at the restaurant. 他抱怨餐厅的服务。 | My main grouche is that the room is too small. 我的主要抱怨是房间太小了。 | 近义词：complaint, grumble, grievance | 反义词：praise, compliment | 词性:名词

grouche:/ɡraʊtʃ/ | vi. | 抱怨，发牢骚 | To complain or grumble. | He's always grouching about something. 他总是抱怨些什么。| She grouched about the cold weather. 她抱怨天气寒冷。 | 近义词：complain, grumble, moan | 反义词：praise, compliment | 词性:动词


词源: From the French "grouchier" (to grumble).
