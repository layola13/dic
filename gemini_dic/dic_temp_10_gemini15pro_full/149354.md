pachydermoid:/ˌpækɪˈdɜːrmɔɪd/
adjective
厚皮类的；似厚皮类的
Resembling or characteristic of a pachyderm; thick-skinned.
Elephants have pachydermoid skin.  大象有厚皮类的皮肤。
The rhinoceros, with its pachydermoid hide, is difficult to injure.  犀牛有着厚皮类的兽皮，很难受伤。
近义词: thick-skinned, tough-hided
反义词: thin-skinned, delicate
词性: adjective
词源: From Greek pakhýdermos 'thick-skinned', from pakhýs 'thick' + dérma 'skin'.

pachydermoid:/ˌpækɪˈdɜːrmɔɪd/
noun
厚皮动物
A pachyderm.  (Less common usage)

近义词: elephant, rhinoceros, hippopotamus
反义词: N/A (No direct antonym for an animal group)
词性: noun
