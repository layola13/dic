marlite:/ˈmɑːrlaɪt/| n. |马莱特板，一种装饰板材|A hard, glossy, easily cleaned wall covering, often used in bathrooms and kitchens. It is typically made of a composite material with a baked enamel finish.|The bathroom walls were covered in marlite, making them easy to wipe down. 浴室的墙壁覆盖着马莱特板，很容易擦干净。|近义词: wallboard, paneling, laminate |反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "tile" or "wallpaper" as alternatives) |词性:noun
