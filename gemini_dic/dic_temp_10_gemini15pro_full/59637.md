ecclesiology: /ɪˌkliːziˈɒlədʒi/ /ɪˌkliːʒiˈɑːlədʒi/
n.
教会学; 教会组织;
The study of the Christian Church, its nature, structure, and function.  This includes its history, its doctrine, its worship, its organization, and its relation to the world.
He is a renowned scholar of ecclesiology. 他是一位著名的教会学学者。
synonyms: church history, theology, doctrine
antonyms: secularism, atheism
noun
