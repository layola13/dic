groundlessness:/ˈɡraʊndləsnəs/
n.
无根据；无稽；空穴来风
lack of justification or supporting evidence.
The groundlessness of his accusations was apparent to everyone. 他的指控毫无根据，这一点大家都很清楚。
近义词: baselessness, unsubstantiatedness, unfoundedness, falsity
反义词: validity, soundness, truth, legitimacy
词性: 名词

groundlessness:/ˈɡraʊndləsnəs/
词源:  From groundless + -ness.  Groundless comes from ground (meaning "foundation") + -less (meaning "without").  Ultimately from Old English grund.
英文解析: The state or quality of being groundless; lack of grounds or foundation; lack of supporting evidence or justification.
例句：The lawyer demonstrated the groundlessness of the plaintiff's claims. 律师证明了原告的主张毫无根据。
例句：The rumor's groundlessness became apparent when the facts were revealed. 当事实真相大白后，谣言的无稽之谈就显而易见了。
近义词: baselessness, unfoundedness, invalidity
反义词: validity, soundness, truthfulness
词性: 名词
