nonresonant:/nɒnˈrɛzənənt/ | adj. | 非共振的，非谐振的 | Not resonant; not exhibiting resonance.  A nonresonant system does not oscillate at its natural frequency when subjected to a periodic force. | The nonresonant frequency response of the system was flat. 系统的非共振频率响应是平坦的。| The bridge was designed to be nonresonant to avoid vibrations caused by wind. 这座桥被设计成非共振的，以避免风引起的振动。 |近义词: non-vibrating, damped |反义词: resonant, vibrating |词性: adjective
