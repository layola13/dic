coaled:/koʊld/| v. |加煤；供以煤；涂以煤；|The ship was coaled in preparation for its long voyage. 这艘船加满了煤，准备进行长途航行。|近义词：fueled, supplied with coal|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 depleted of coal - 煤耗尽)|verb

coaled:/koʊld/| adj. |涂有煤的；加过煤的；|The coaled face of the miner was grim. 矿工那张沾满煤灰的脸显得阴沉。|近义词：coal-covered, coal-blackened|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 clean - 干净的)|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From coal + -ed.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *kula-, meaning "glowing ember."
