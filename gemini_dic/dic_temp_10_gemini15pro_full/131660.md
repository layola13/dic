nonconcentration:/ˌnɒnkɒnsənˈtreɪʃn/ | n. |缺乏集中, 不集中|Lack of concentration; inability to focus one's attention.|His nonconcentration during the lecture resulted in poor grades. 他上课不集中导致成绩很差。|近义词：inattention, distraction, absent-mindedness|反义词: concentration, focus, attention|词性:名词

词源:  From non- (prefix meaning "not") + concentration.  "Concentration" comes from Late Latin *concentrātiōn-, from concentrāre "to concentrate," from com- "together" + centrum "center."
