dare:/dɛər/ (US), /deə(r)/ (UK)| v. aux. n.| 敢，胆敢；挑战；激将 | To have the courage to do something; to challenge someone to do something; a challenge.|

例句：
He dared to climb the highest mountain. 他敢于攀登最高的山峰。

She dared him to jump off the cliff. 她激他跳下悬崖。


近义词：challenge, defy, venture, risk|
反义词：fear, cower, avoid|
词性: 动词 (v.), 助动词 (aux.), 名词 (n.)


dare:/dɛər/ (US), /deə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 挑战；激将 | An act of daring; a challenge.|

例句：
He accepted her dare to eat the whole chili. 他接受了她吃掉整个辣椒的挑战。

It was a dare, and he couldn't back down. 那是一个挑战，他不能退缩。
