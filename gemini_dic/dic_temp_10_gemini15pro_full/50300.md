deplant:/diːˈplænt/ | v. | 移植；迁移；强迫迁离 | To remove a plant from the ground or from a pot. To remove a person or group of people from their home or country, often by force. |  The gardener carefully deplanted the rose bush. 园丁小心翼翼地把玫瑰丛移植了。 | synonyms: uproot, displace, remove, relocate, transplant | antonyms: plant, settle, establish | verb

deplant:/diːˈplænt/ | v. | 撤职；使去职 | To remove someone from a position of power or authority. | The CEO was deplanted after the scandal.  丑闻之后，首席执行官被撤职了。 | synonyms: oust, remove, dismiss, dethrone | antonyms: appoint, install, promote | verb
