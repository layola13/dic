inrushing:/ˈɪnrʌʃɪŋ/| adj.|涌入的，急速涌入的|A sudden and rapid inward flow, often of air, water, or people.  Describes something that is rushing in. |例句：The inrushing tide flooded the beach. 涌入的潮水淹没了海滩。

例句：We felt the inrushing cold air as we opened the door to the freezer. 我们打开冰箱门时感觉到了涌入的冷空气。|近义词：inflowing, surging, flooding|反义词：outgoing, receding, ebbing|词性：形容词
