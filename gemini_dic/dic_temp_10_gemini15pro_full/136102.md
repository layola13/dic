nonrecuperatory:/ˌnɒnriːˈkʌpərətɔːri/ | adj. | 非恢复性的 | Not able to recover or be recovered; irreversible.  Referring to processes or systems that do not return to their original state after a change or disturbance. |  The environmental damage caused by the oil spill was nonrecuperatory.  石油泄漏造成的环境破坏是不可恢复的。|  The patient suffered nonrecuperatory brain damage.  病人遭受了不可逆转的脑损伤。| 近义词: irreversible, irreparable, permanent | 反义词: reversible, recoverable, temporary | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  From "non-" (not) + "recuperatory" (from Latin "recuperare" - to recover).
