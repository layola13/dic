antiphase:/ˈæntiˌfeɪz/ /ˈæntaɪˌfeɪz/ | n. |反相，逆相|A stage in a regularly recurring cycle or process, such as a wave form, that is separated by half a cycle from another such stage; the relation between two such stages.|The two signals were in antiphase.|近义词：opposite phase |反义词:in phase| noun
