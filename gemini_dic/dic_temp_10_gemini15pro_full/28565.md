cards:/kɑːrdz/ | n. | 卡片；纸牌；明信片；贺卡 | A small rectangular piece of stiff paper, plastic, or cardboard used for various purposes, such as playing games, sending messages, or storing information. |  I sent her a birthday card. 我给她寄了一张生日贺卡。 | synonyms:  postcards, greetings cards, playing cards, business cards, credit cards | antonyms: letter (sometimes), email | noun

cards:/kɑːrdz/ | v. (card, cards, carding, carded) |  梳理（羊毛等）；记下…以备后用 | To comb or disentangle fibers, especially wool or cotton, with a card. Also, to record something for future use. | The workers were carding the wool. 工人们正在梳理羊毛。 | synonyms: comb, disentangle, record, document | antonyms: tangle, mess up | verb
