nonincrimination:/ˌnɑːnɪnˌkrɪmɪˈneɪʃn/
n.
不控告；不自证其罪；免于自证其罪
The principle that a person cannot be compelled to incriminate themselves.
The Fifth Amendment of the U.S. Constitution guarantees the right of nonincrimination.  美国宪法第五修正案保障了不自证其罪的权利。

The suspect invoked his right to nonincrimination. 嫌疑人援引了不自证其罪的权利。

immunity, privilege
self-incrimination, confession
noun
