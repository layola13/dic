militates:/ˈmɪlɪteɪts/
verb
不利于，阻碍，产生不利影响
(of a fact or circumstance) be a powerful or conclusive factor in preventing.
His poor health militates against his chances of success. 他糟糕的健康状况不利于他成功的机会。
近义词: works against, counts against, operates against, weighs against, goes against, acts against, tells against, argues against, prejudices, biases, disfavors
反义词: helps, assists, aids, supports, benefits, favors
词性: verb

militates:/ˈmɪlɪteɪts/
词源:  From Latin *militatus, past participle of militare (“to serve as a soldier”), from miles (“soldier”).
英文解析:  (of a fact or circumstance) be a powerful or conclusive factor in preventing.  It suggests a strong influence working against something.
例句:  The lack of funding militates against further research. 缺乏资金阻碍了进一步的研究。

例句:  Her shyness militates against her making friends easily.  她的害羞使她难以交到朋友。
近义词:  hinders, obstructs, impedes, hampers, undermines
反义词:  supports, promotes, encourages, facilitates, furthers
词性: verb
