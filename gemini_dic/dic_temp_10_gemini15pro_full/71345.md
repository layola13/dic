flaccid:/ˈflæsɪd/| adj.|松弛的，软弱的，无力的|hanging loosely or limply; lacking firmness; weak; lacking force or effectiveness|The flowers were flaccid from lack of water. 这些花因为缺水而变得枯萎了。|limp, soft, flabby|firm, hard, taut|adjective


flaccid:/ˈflæsɪd/| adj.|不结实的，不坚挺的|lacking firmness or resilience; not stiff or rigid|His handshake was surprisingly flaccid. 他的握手出奇地无力。|weak, feeble, spiritless|strong, robust, vigorous|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin flaccidus, from flaccēre (to be flabby, to droop).
