guardroom:/ˈɡɑːrdruːm/ | n. | 警卫室；卫兵室 | A room used by guards, especially in a castle, prison, or other large building. | The prisoner was taken to the guardroom.  囚犯被带到了警卫室。|  guardhouse, security room |  None | Noun
Etymology: From 'guard' + 'room'. 'Guard' comes from Old French 'garder' (to protect). 'Room' comes from Old English 'rūm' (space).
