nobber:/ˈnɒbə(r)/ (UK) | n. | 傻瓜，笨蛋；（尤指）有钱有势的傻瓜 | A foolish or stupid person, especially one who is rich or influential. | He acted like a complete nobber, throwing his money around like it grew on trees. 他表现得像个十足的傻瓜，到处乱花钱，好像钱是从树上长出来的一样。|  Synonyms: idiot, fool, twit, prat, dolt | Antonyms: genius, intellectual, mastermind | noun
