illogical:/ɪˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ /ɪˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/| adj.|不合逻辑的；不合理的|Not logical; lacking sense or reason.|It's illogical to assume that just because something is expensive, it's good quality.  假设仅仅因为某物昂贵就质量好是不合逻辑的。

It's illogical to think you can succeed without hard work. 认为不努力就能成功是不合逻辑的。|近义词：irrational, unreasonable, absurd|反义词：logical, rational, reasonable|词性:形容词
