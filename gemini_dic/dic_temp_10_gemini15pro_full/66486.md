exertive:/ɪɡˈzɜːrtɪv/| adj.|费力的， strenuous; demanding.|Characterized by or requiring exertion; requiring effort.|Shoveling snow is exertive work. 铲雪是一项费力的工作.|近义词：strenuous, arduous, laborious, demanding, taxing|反义词：easy, effortless, simple|词性:形容词

词源: From exert + -ive.  "Exert" comes from the Latin "exserere," meaning "to put forth," from "ex-" (out) + "serere" (to join, link).
