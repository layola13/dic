blacksmith:/ˈblækˌsmɪθ/| n.|铁匠|A person who makes and repairs things in iron by hand.|The blacksmith hammered the hot metal into shape. 铁匠把烧热的金属锤打成形。|近义词：metalworker, smith|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与其工作相反的职业，例如 software engineer 软件工程师)|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *blæcsmiþ (attested only as a surname, Blæcsmið), from blæc "black" + smiþ "smith". The "black" refers to the black fire scale that forms on the surface of the metal during heating.
