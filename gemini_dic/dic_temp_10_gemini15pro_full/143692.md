outleaps:/ˌaʊtˈliːps/
verb
超越，超过
surpasses, exceeds, jumps further than
His new novel outleaps his previous works in both style and depth. 他的新小说在风格和深度上都超过了他以前的作品。
The athlete easily outleaps all his competitors.  这位运动员轻松地超过了他的所有竞争对手。

近义词:surpasses, exceeds, transcends, outstrips, outdoes
反义词:lags behind, falls short of
词性:v.
