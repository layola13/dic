hand-and-glove:/ˈhænd ən ɡlʌv/| adj.|密切合作的；配合默契的；狼狈为奸的|Working in close association or collaboration, like a hand fits perfectly in a glove.  Implies a very close and often secretive relationship.|The two companies worked hand-and-glove on the project. 这两家公司在这个项目上密切合作。|Synonyms: closely, intimately, in cahoots, thick as thieves|Antonyms: independently, separately, at odds|adjective
