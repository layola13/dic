multipli:/ˈmʌltɪplaɪ/| adj.|多重的，多样的|Multiple means having many parts or elements. It refers to something that is repeated or exists in several forms.  It can also imply a large number or quantity.|例句：The company faced multiple challenges during the recession.  公司在经济衰退期间面临多重挑战。

例句：She suffered multiple injuries in the car accident. 她在车祸中遭受多处损伤。|近义词：numerous, various, several, many|反义词：single, sole, unique|词性:形容词

词源: From Late Latin *multiplus, from Latin multus ("much, many") + -plus ("-fold").
