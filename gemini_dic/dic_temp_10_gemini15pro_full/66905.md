expeditionist:/ˌɛkspɪˈdɪʃənɪst/
n.
探险家，远征队员
A person who participates in or leads expeditions, especially to remote or unexplored regions.
She's a renowned expeditionist, having climbed Mount Everest twice. 她是一位著名的探险家，曾两次攀登珠穆朗玛峰。
近义词:explorer, adventurer, traveler
反义词:homebody, stay-at-home
词性:名词
