cohost:/ˈkoʊhoʊst/ (US) /ˈkəʊhəʊst/ (UK)| n. v.|共同主持人，联合举办|A person who hosts something jointly with another or others. |例句：She cohosted the awards ceremony with a famous comedian. 她和一位著名的喜剧演员共同主持了颁奖典礼。

例句：They cohosted a successful fundraiser for the local hospital. 他们共同举办了一场成功的当地医院募捐活动。|近义词：joint host, co-presenter|反义词:guest, audience|词性:名词,动词
