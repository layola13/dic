amianthus:/ˌæmiˈænθəs/
n.
石棉
a fibrous mineral (hydrated magnesium silicate) that separates easily into long flexible fibers, formerly used for making fireproof materials but now known to cause lung cancer and other diseases.
Asbestos, a fibrous silicate mineral with insulating properties, was once widely used in building materials but is now known to cause serious health problems, such as lung cancer and mesothelioma.  石棉是一种具有绝缘性能的纤维状硅酸盐矿物，曾经广泛用于建筑材料，但现在已知会导致严重的健康问题，如肺癌和间皮瘤。
Exposure to amianthus fibers can lead to asbestosis.  接触石棉纤维会导致石棉肺。
Synonym: asbestos
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps "safe building material" could be considered a contextual antonym)
