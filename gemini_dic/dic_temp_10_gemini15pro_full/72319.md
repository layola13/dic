flout:/flaʊt/| v. |蔑视，嘲笑，公然违抗|openly disregard (a rule, law or convention).|The driver flouted the traffic laws by speeding through a red light. 司机无视交通法规，闯红灯超速行驶。

|例句：She flouted convention by wearing jeans to the formal event.  她穿着牛仔裤出席正式场合，公然违反惯例。

|例句：He flouted the school rules by smoking on campus. 他在校园里吸烟，违反了校规。


|近义词：disregard, defy, scorn, mock, disobey|反义词: obey, respect, observe, comply|词性:动词
