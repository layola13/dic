nondefiniteness:/ˌnɒndɪˈfɪnɪtnəs/ /ˌnɑːndɪˈfɪnɪtnəs/
n.
非限定性；不确定性；不明确性
The nondefiniteness of the contract terms led to disputes.  合同条款的不确定性导致了争议。
The artist embraced the nondefiniteness of the abstract form.  这位艺术家欣然接受了抽象形式的不确定性。
Synonyms: indefiniteness, uncertainty, vagueness, ambiguity
Antonyms: definiteness, certainty, clarity, precision
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + definiteness.  Definiteness comes from the Latin "definitus," past participle of "definire" (to define).
