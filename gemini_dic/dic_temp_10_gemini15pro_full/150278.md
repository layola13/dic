panegyric: /ˌpænɪˈdʒɪrɪk/ | n. | 颂词，赞美词， панегирик | A formal speech or piece of writing that praises someone or something highly. |  The CEO delivered a panegyric on the company's founder at the memorial service.  首席执行官在追悼会上发表了对公司创始人的颂词。

The book is a panegyric to the natural beauty of the countryside. 这本书是对乡村自然美景的赞美。 | 近义词：eulogy, encomium, tribute, accolade | 反义词：diatribe, criticism, denunciation | 词性：noun

panegyrics: /ˌpænɪˈdʒɪrɪks/ | n. | 颂词，赞美词（复数） | Plural form of panegyric.  | He offered panegyrics to all the award winners. 他向所有获奖者致颂词。

The book is full of panegyrics about the author's favorite artists. 这本书充满了对作者最喜欢的艺术家的赞美之词。| 近义词：eulogies, encomiums, tributes, accolades | 反义词：diatribes, criticisms, denunciations | 词性：noun
