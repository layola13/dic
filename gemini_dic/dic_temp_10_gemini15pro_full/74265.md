foxtrots:/ˈfɑːkstrɑːts/ /ˈfɒkstrɒts/| n. v.|狐步舞,跳狐步舞|A ballroom dance for couples, characterized by gliding steps and slow, elegant movements.  It can also be used as a verb meaning to dance the foxtrot.|例句：They danced a graceful foxtrot. 他们跳了一支优雅的狐步舞。

例句：He foxtrotted her across the dance floor. 他和她跳着狐步舞穿过舞池。|近义词：waltz, quickstep|反义词:(No direct antonym for a specific dance)|词性:noun, verb
