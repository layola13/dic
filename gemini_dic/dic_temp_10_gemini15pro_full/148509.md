oversubtleties:/ˌoːvərˈsʌt(ə)ltiz/ | n. | 过于精细；过度微妙之处 | Excessive subtleties; nuances or distinctions that are too fine or delicate to be readily perceived or appreciated. |  The novel was filled with oversubtleties that were lost on most readers. 这部小说充满了大多数读者无法理解的过度微妙之处。| synonyms: nuances, refinements, intricacies | antonyms: bluntness, obviousness, simplicity | noun

oversubtle:/ˌoʊvərˈsʌt(ə)l/ | adj. | 过于精细的；过度微妙的 | Excessively subtle; characterized by nuances or distinctions that are too fine or delicate to be readily perceived or appreciated. | His oversubtle humor was often misinterpreted. 他过于微妙的幽默经常被误解。| synonyms: nuanced, refined, intricate | antonyms: blunt, obvious, simple | adjective
