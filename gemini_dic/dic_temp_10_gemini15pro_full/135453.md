nonpertinently:/ˌnɒnpɜːrtnəntli/
adverb
无关地；不切题地
Not relevant to the subject matter; irrelevantly.
He rambled on nonpertinently about his childhood. 他漫无边际地讲述了他的童年，与主题无关。
She nonpertinently interrupted the meeting with a question about lunch.  她用一个关于午餐的问题不适时地打断了会议。
近义词: irrelevantly, inappropriately, immaterialy
反义词: relevantly, pertinently, appropriately
词性: adverb

词源:
From non- + pertinent + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Pertinent" comes from the Latin "pertinēre," meaning "to pertain" or "to belong."  "-ly" is a suffix that forms adverbs.
