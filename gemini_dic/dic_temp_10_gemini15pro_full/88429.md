hispanicized:/hɪˈspænɪsaɪzd/
adjective
西班牙化的，受西班牙文化影响的
Influenced by or having the characteristics of Spanish culture or language.
Many towns in California have hispanicized names.  加州的很多城镇都有西班牙化的名字。
The region's culture has become increasingly hispanicized over the centuries.  几个世纪以来，该地区的文化越来越西班牙化。
近义词:Hispanified, Latinized
反义词:Anglicized
词性:adjective
