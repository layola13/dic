interinfluencing:/ˌɪntərˈɪnfluənsɪŋ/
| adj. |相互影响的|Exerting influence on each other; mutually influencing.|
|The interinfluencing factors of climate and vegetation shaped the landscape.|气候和植被相互影响的因素塑造了景观。|
|The two communities lived in close proximity, their cultures interinfluencing each other for centuries.|这两个社区比邻而居，它们的文化几个世纪以来一直相互影响。|
|近义词：interacting, interdependent, interconnected|
|反义词：independent, separate, unconnected|
|词性:形容词|


interinfluence:/ˌɪntərˈɪnfluəns/
| v. |相互影响|To exert influence on each other; to mutually influence.|
|Social media and traditional news outlets interinfluence each other.|社交媒体和传统新闻媒体相互影响。|
|Economic and political factors interinfluence to shape national policy.|经济和政治因素相互影响，从而塑造国家政策。|
|近义词：interact, affect, impact|
|反义词：isolate, separate|
|词性:动词|

词源:
"inter-" (prefix) + "influence"
"inter-" comes from Latin, meaning "between," "among," "mutually," "reciprocally."
"influence" comes from Old French "influence," ultimately from Latin "influentia," meaning "a flowing in."
