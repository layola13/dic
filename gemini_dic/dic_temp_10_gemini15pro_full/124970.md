muggard:/ˈmʌɡərd/| n.| 吝啬鬼，守财奴|A miserly or stingy person; a skinflint.|He's such a muggard, he wouldn't even buy his own mother a birthday present. 他真是个吝啬鬼，连自己母亲的生日礼物都不肯买。|近义词：miser, skinflint, tightwad, cheapskate|反义词:spendthrift, philanthropist|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain. Possibly related to mug (face), suggesting a sullen or disapproving expression associated with miserliness.  也可能与dialectical mug  (to cheat or swindle) 有关.
