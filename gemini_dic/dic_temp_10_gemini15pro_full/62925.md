enmarble:/ɪnˈmɑːrbl/| adj.(rare, poetic)|像大理石的，坚硬的|Resembling marble, especially in hardness or coldness.|Her enmarbled gaze betrayed no emotion.|近义词：marble-like, stony|反义词:soft, yielding|adjective
