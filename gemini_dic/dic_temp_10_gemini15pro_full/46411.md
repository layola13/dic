daftest:/ˈdæftɪst/| adj.|最愚蠢的|Most foolish or silly.|That's the daftest idea I've ever heard! 那是
我听过的最愚蠢的想法！|Synonyms: silliest, stupidest, most foolish|Antonyms: wisest, smartest, most intelligent|adjective

Etymology:
From Middle English *dafte*, from Old Norse *daftr*  meaning “mild, meek,” later “stupid.”  The superlative form "-est" is a standard English suffix.
