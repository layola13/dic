nonsecessions:/ˌnɒnsɪˈseʃənz/
noun
非分裂, 不脱离
The absence of secession; a state of remaining within a larger political entity.
The treaty ensured the nonsecession of the territories.  该条约确保了这些领土不会脱离。
The period of nonsecession was marked by economic growth. 非分裂时期经济增长显著。
Near Antonyms: secessions, withdrawals, departures
Antonyms: secessions
