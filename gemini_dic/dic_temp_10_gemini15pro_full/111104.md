literacies:/ˈlɪtərəsiːz/
noun
读写能力；多种读写能力
The ability to read, write, and use language effectively in various contexts, often including digital and cultural literacy.
Colleges are increasingly concerned with students' digital literacies.  大学越来越关注学生的数字素养。
skills, competencies, capabilities
illiteracy, ignorance
