indeterminate:/ˌɪndɪˈtɜːrmɪnɪt/ | adj. | 不确定的，不明确的 | Not precisely determined or established; vague. | The future remains indeterminate. 未来依然是不确定的。| 近义词: undefined, uncertain, vague | 反义词: definite, certain, precise | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Late Latin *indeterminatus, from in- ‘not’ + determinatus ‘determined’.
