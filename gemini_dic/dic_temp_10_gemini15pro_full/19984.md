biogeochemical:/ˌbaɪoʊˌdʒiːoʊˈkɛmɪkəl/
adjective|生物地球化学的|Relating to the study of the chemical, physical, geological, and biological processes that govern the composition of the natural environment.|例句：Biogeochemical cycles are crucial for maintaining life on Earth. 生物地球化学循环对于维持地球上的生命至关重要。|例句：Scientists study biogeochemical processes to understand climate change. 科学家研究生物地球化学过程以了解气候变化。|近义词：ecological, environmental|反义词：(No direct antonym exists, potentially "abiotic")|词性:adjective

词源:
bio- (life) + geo- (earth) + chemical
