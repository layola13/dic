cylindricity:/ˌsɪlɪnˈdrɪsɪti/
n.
圆柱度，圆柱性
The state or quality of being cylindrical.  The degree to which a surface resembles a true cylinder.
The cylindricity of the piston is crucial for the engine's performance.  活塞的圆柱度对发动机的性能至关重要。

The manufacturer checks the cylindricity of each pipe. 制造商检查每根管子的圆柱度。

近义词:circularity, roundness
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) irregularity, asymmetry
词性:noun
