mutch:/mʌtʃ/ | n. |  a large amount; a great deal (chiefly Scottish and Northern English) | A dialectal variant of "much," meaning a large amount or a great deal. |  He made a mutch of money in the oil business. 他在石油生意中赚了很多钱。|  He's got a mutch of work to do. 他有很多工作要做。| Synonyms: much, a lot, lots, plenty | Antonyms: little, few | noun
