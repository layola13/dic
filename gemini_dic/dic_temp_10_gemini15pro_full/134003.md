nonhomologous:/ˌnɒnhəˈmɒləɡəs/ | adj. | 非同源的 | Not derived from the same ancestral structure or gene.  Not corresponding in structure, position, or origin. |  Nonhomologous chromosomes do not pair during meiosis. 非同源染色体在减数分裂过程中不会配对。 | The wings of a bat and the wings of a butterfly are nonhomologous structures. 蝙蝠的翅膀和蝴蝶的翅膀是非同源结构。 | analogous, similar | homologous | adjective
