heave-to:/hiːv tuː/
verb phrase
停下，顶风停航
To bring a ship to a standstill, facing into the wind.
The captain ordered the crew to heave-to and wait for the storm to pass. 船长命令船员顶风停航，等待暴风雨过去。
The ship hove-to off the coast. 船在海岸边顶风停航。
