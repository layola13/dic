maasai: /mɑːˈsaɪ/ /məˈsaɪ/ | n. adj.| 马赛人；马赛人的；|A member of an indigenous ethnic group inhabiting southern Kenya and northern Tanzania.  Known for their distinctive customs and dress, particularly their red shukas and beaded jewelry.  Also used adjectivally to describe things related to the Maasai people. |例句：The Maasai are known for their cattle herding. 马赛人以牧牛而闻名。

例句：Maasai culture is rich in tradition. 马赛文化富有传统。|近义词：Masai|反义词: N/A|词性:noun, adjective
