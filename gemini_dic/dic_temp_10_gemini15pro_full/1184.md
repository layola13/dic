acclimatization: /əˌklaɪmətaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 适应，驯化 | The process of adapting or adjusting to a new climate, environment, or situation.  |  The acclimatization period for new students can be challenging. 新生的适应期可能充满挑战。

The climbers needed a week of acclimatization to the high altitude before attempting the summit.  登山者在尝试登顶之前需要一周时间来适应高海拔。 | 近义词：adaptation, adjustment, acclimation | 反义词: maladaptation | 词性: 名词

acclimatizations: /əˌklaɪmətaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 适应，驯化（复数） | Multiple instances of the process of adapting or adjusting to new climates, environments, or situations. | The book detailed the various acclimatizations of different species to changing environments. 这本书详细描述了不同物种对环境变化的各种适应。

The team studied the acclimatizations of plants in different regions. 该团队研究了植物在不同地区的适应情况。 | 近义词：adaptations, adjustments, acclimations | 反义词: maladaptations | 词性: 名词
