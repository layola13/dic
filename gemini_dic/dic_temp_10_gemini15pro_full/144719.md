ovato:/oʊˈvɑːtoʊ/
adjective
卵形的，椭圆形的
Having an oval outline or shape, like an egg.
The artist preferred to use ovato frames for his miniature portraits.  这位艺术家喜欢用椭圆形的画框来装裱他的微型肖像画。
The leaves of the plant are ovato.  这种植物的叶子是卵形的。
近义词:oval, elliptic, ovate, egg-shaped
反义词:round, circular, square
词性:adjective

ovato:/əˈveɪtoʊ/
adjective
卵形的，椭圆形的
(especially of a leaf) egg-shaped with the broader end at the base.
The botanist identified the species by its distinctive ovato leaves.  植物学家通过其独特的卵形叶子识别了该物种。
The petals of the flower are a delicate shade of pink and distinctly ovato in shape.  这朵花的花瓣是淡粉色的，形状明显是卵形的。
近义词:oval, elliptic, ovate, egg-shaped
反义词:round, circular, square
词性:adjective


词源: From Latin *ōvātus, past participle of *ōvāre (“to make egg-shaped”), from ōvum (“egg”).
