cognisee:/ˌkɒɡnɪˈziː/ /ˌkɑːɡnɪˈziː/
n.
被认知者；认知对象
The person or thing that is perceived or known by the cognizer.
The cognisee in this experiment was a group of monkeys.  这个实验中的被认知者是一群猴子。
The painting is the cognisee, and the viewer is the cognizer. 这幅画是被认知的对象，而观赏者是认知者。
近义词:percept, object of perception
反义词:cognizer
词性:noun
