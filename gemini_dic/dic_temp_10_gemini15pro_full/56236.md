doffs:/dɒfs/ (UK), /dɑːfs/ (US)| v. (third-person singular simple present doffs, present participle doffing, simple past and past participle doffed)|脱下（帽子、衣服等）; 摘下; 摆脱; 放弃|To remove (one's hat or clothing); to take off; to get rid of; to cast off|He doffed his cap in greeting. 他脱帽致意。|Synonyms: remove, take off, shed, discard|Antonyms: don, put on, wear|verb
