decentralize:/diːˈsɛntrəlaɪz/ | v. | 分散，去中心化 | to distribute or spread over a wide area; to transfer (authority) from central to local government. | The company decided to decentralize its operations. 公司决定将其运营分散化。 | distribute, disperse, devolve | centralize, consolidate | verb
