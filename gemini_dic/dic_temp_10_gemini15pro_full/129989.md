non-national:/nɒnˈnæʃ(ə)n(ə)l/ /nɑːnˈnæʃ(ə)n(ə)l/
noun, adjective
非国民的，非本国的；外国人，非本国人
A person who is not a citizen of a particular country.  Not connected with or belonging to a particular nation.
例句：The non-nationals were required to register with the local authorities.  非国民被要求向当地政府登记。

例句：The government introduced new restrictions on non-national workers. 政府对非本国工人实行了新的限制。

近义词：foreigner, alien, immigrant
反义词：national, citizen
词性：noun, adjective
