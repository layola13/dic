liablest:/ˈlaɪəblɪst/| adj.|最有可能的；最易受影响的|Most likely to be affected by something unpleasant. |例句：He is the liablest person to catch a cold. 他是最容易感冒的人。|近义词：prone, susceptible, vulnerable|反义词：resistant, immune|词性:形容词
词源: From liable + -est (superlative suffix).  "Liable" comes from Old French "liable" (obliged, subject to), from Latin "ligare" (to bind).
