inscribed:/ɪnˈskraɪbd/| adj.|刻写的，铭刻的|Marked or written on a surface.  Derived from Latin "inscribere" (in + scribere), meaning "to write on."|例句：His name was inscribed on the trophy. 他的名字刻在奖杯上。|例句：The ring was inscribed with a personal message. 戒指上刻着一条私人信息。|近义词：engraved, etched, carved|反义词: erased, unmarked|词性:形容词
