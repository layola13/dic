overphilosophizing: /ˌoʊvərˌfɪləˈsɑːfaɪzɪŋ/ | n. | 过度哲学化；过度思辨 | The act of thinking too much or too deeply about something, often to the point of confusion or inaction.  | He was prone to overphilosophizing about life's meaning, which often left him feeling more lost than enlightened. 他容易过度思辨人生的意义，这常常让他感到更加迷茫而不是顿悟。|近义词：overthinking, ruminating, brooding |反义词:  practicality, action | 词性: 名词 (gerund)


overphilosophize: /ˌoʊvərˌfɪləˈsɑːfaɪz/ | v. | 过度哲学化；过度思辨 | To think too much or too deeply about something, often to the point of confusion or inaction. | Don't overphilosophize the situation; just do what feels right.  不要过度思辨情况；做你觉得对的事就好。 | 近义词：overthink, ruminate, brood | 反义词: act, simplify | 词性: 动词
