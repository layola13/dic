codille:/kəˈdɪl/ | n. |  科迪尔（一种纸牌游戏中赢得额外分数的情况）| A term in the card game of ombre and quadrille, referring to a situation where a player wins a hand after initially seeming unlikely to do so, thus earning extra points.  |  She won the hand, much to everyone's surprise, and scored a codille. 她赢了这一手牌，让每个人都大吃一惊，并赢得了一个科迪尔。 |  Near synonyms: slam, coup | Antonym: loss | Noun
