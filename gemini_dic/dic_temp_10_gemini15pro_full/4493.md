albinistic:/ˌælbɪˈnɪstɪk/
adj.
白化的，患白化病的
Affected by albinism; lacking pigment in the skin, hair, and eyes.
例句：The albinistic rabbit stood out against the dark foliage.  这只白化的兔子在深色的树叶衬托下格外显眼。

例句：Albinistic animals are often more vulnerable to predators. 白化动物通常更容易受到捕食者的攻击。

近义词：albino, leukistic, amelanistic
反义词：pigmented
词性：adjective
