fisheries:/ˈfɪʃəriz/ | n. | 渔业; 渔场; 水产业 |  The business or practice of catching fish, either for food or as a sport.  A place where fish are reared for food.  An area of water where fish are caught. | The decline of coastal fisheries is a serious concern. 沿海渔业的衰退是一个严重的问题。|  The government is investing in sustainable fisheries management. 政府正在投资可持续渔业管理。 | synonym: fishing industry, aquaculture, fish farms | antonym:  (No direct antonym, but consider "terrestrial farming" for contrast) | noun
