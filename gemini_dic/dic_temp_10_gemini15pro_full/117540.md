meets:/miːts/| v. |遇见，满足，符合| This road meets the highway a mile ahead. 这条路在一英里外与高速公路相交。|近义词：encounters, satisfies, fulfills|反义词: misses, fails, avoids|词性:动词

Etymology: From Old English *mētan (to meet), from Proto-Germanic *mōtijaną (to meet, find), from Proto-Indo-European *mot- (to assemble).
