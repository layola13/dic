obolary: /ˈoʊbəˌlɛri/ | n. |  词汇，词汇量 |  A person's vocabulary; the total stock of words used in a particular language, by a particular person or group, or at a particular period in time. | He has a limited obolary. 他的词汇量有限。| Synonyms: vocabulary, lexicon, wordstock | Antonyms:  lack of vocabulary, limited vocabulary | noun
