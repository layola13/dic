mistrains:/ˌmɪsˈtreɪnz/ | vt. | 误导；错误地训练；训练不当 | To train incorrectly or impart faulty methods or information to. |  He mistrains his dog by rewarding bad behavior. 他因奖励不良行为而错误地训练了他的狗。

The coach mistrained the athletes, leading to their poor performance. 教练对运动员进行了错误的训练，导致他们表现不佳。 | 近义词：misinstruct, miseducate, mislead | 反义词：train properly, educate correctly | 词性: 动词
