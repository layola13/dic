addressable:/əˈdrɛsəbl/| adj.|可寻址的，可访问的|Able to be addressed; capable of being found or accessed, especially by a computer or other electronic device.  Specifically refers to something that can be individually identified and accessed, such as a device on a network or a specific memory location.|Each device on a network has a unique addressable IP address.  每个网络上的设备都有一个唯一的可寻址IP地址。

The memory locations are addressable by the CPU.  这些内存位置可由CPU寻址。|近义词：accessible, reachable, locatable|反义词：inaccessible, unreachable|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From address + -able.  "Address" comes from Old French "adresser" meaning "to direct, guide."  "-able" is a suffix meaning "capable of being."

USAGE->
Directly modifying a noun:
Addressable memory is a key feature of modern computers. = 可寻址内存是现代计算机的一个关键特性。
The company sells addressable advertising solutions. = 这家公司出售可寻址广告解决方案。

Used with "to":
The problem is addressable to further research. = 这个问题可以通过进一步研究来解决。
The issue is not addressable to a quick fix. = 这个问题无法快速解决。

Used in passive voice:
The individual components of the system are addressable by the central controller. = 系统的各个组件可由中央控制器寻址。
The specific needs of each customer can be addressable through personalized services. = 每位顾客的具体需求都可以通过个性化服务来满足。

Used with "make":
The new technology makes individual customers addressable. = 新技术使个体客户可寻址。
Improvements in data analytics make specific target audiences addressable. = 数据分析的改进使得特定的目标受众可寻址。
