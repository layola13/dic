cryogen:/ˈkraɪədʒən/ | n. | 冷冻剂，低温剂 | A substance used to produce very low temperatures. |  Example 1: Liquid nitrogen is a common cryogen used in scientific research. 液氮是科研中常用的冷冻剂。

Example 2:  The cryogen was carefully stored in a specialized tank. 冷冻剂被小心地储存在专门的罐子里。 | 近义词: refrigerant, coolant | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun

cryogenic:/ˌkraɪəˈdʒɛnɪk/ | adj. | 冷冻的，低温的 | Relating to or involving very low temperatures. | Example 1: Cryogenic freezing is used to preserve biological samples. 冷冻冷藏用于保存生物样本。

Example 2: The cryogenic engine powered the rocket into space. 低温发动机为火箭进入太空提供动力。| 近义词: freezing, frigid | 反义词:  hot, thermal | 词性: adjective
