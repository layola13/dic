bet:/bet/ (US) /bet/ (UK)| n. v.|赌注，打赌，确信|An agreement in which people try to guess what will happen and the person who guesses wrong has to give something, such as money, to the person who guesses right.|

I placed a bet on the horse race. 我在赛马上下了赌注。

He bet me $20 that I couldn't finish the race. 他和我赌 20 美元我跑不完比赛。


|近义词：wager, gamble, stake|反义词：-|noun

bet:/bet/ (US) /bet/ (UK)| v.|打赌，确信|To risk money on the result of an event or a competition, such as a horse race, in the hope of winning more money.|

I bet you $50 that it will rain tomorrow. 我和你赌 50 美元明天会下雨。

I bet she'll be late. 我敢肯定她会迟到。


|近义词：wager, gamble|反义词：-|verb
