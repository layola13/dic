comember:/koʊˈmɛmbər/| n. |共同成员，同事|A fellow member of a group or organization.  A person belonging to the same group or organization as another person.|例句：She is a comember of the board of directors. 她是一位董事会成员。

例句：He and I are comembers of the same golf club.  我和他是同一个高尔夫俱乐部的会员。|近义词：colleague, associate, fellow member|反义词:nonmember, outsider|词性:名词
