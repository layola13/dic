misregulating: /ˌmɪsˈrɛɡjʊleɪtɪŋ/
verb
失调;  调节失当
Failing to regulate properly; regulating badly or incorrectly.
The misregulating gene caused severe developmental problems.  这个失调的基因导致了严重的发育问题。
The body's temperature is misregulating due to the illness. 由于疾病，身体的体温调节失常。
Synonyms: malfunctioning, dysregulating, deranging
Antonyms: regulating, controlling, managing
词性: 动词(present participle)
