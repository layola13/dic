impetrative:/ɪmˈpɛrətɪv/| adj. n.|必要的，紧急的；命令的；祈使语气|Of vital importance; crucial. Expressing a command; peremptory.|例句：It is imperative that we act now. 我们现在必须行动起来。|近义词：essential, crucial, vital, urgent, mandatory, compulsory, required|反义词:optional, unnecessary, unimportant|词性:adjective

impetrative:/ɪmˈpɛrətɪv/| n.|命令；祈使语气|A command; something that demands attention or action; the imperative mood.|例句：He issued an imperative to his troops to advance. 他向他的部队发出了前进的命令。|近义词：command, order, decree, directive|反义词:request, plea|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *imperātīvus*, from Latin imperō (“I command”).
