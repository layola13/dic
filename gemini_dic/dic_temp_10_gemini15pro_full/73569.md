forestick:/ˈfɔːrˌstɪk/ | n. |引火棒，点火棒 |A piece of wood or other flammable material used for kindling a fire.| He used a forestick to start the campfire. 他用一根引火棒点燃了篝火。|近义词: kindling, tinder |反义词: extinguisher |词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *forestikke*, from Old English *fōrstice* ("forestick"), from *fōr* ("fore, before") + *sticca* ("stick").  Literally, a "stick placed before" the fire.
