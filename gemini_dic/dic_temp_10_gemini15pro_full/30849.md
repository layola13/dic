chad:/tʃæd/| n.|小伙子; 男子汉;  (尤指)自信而有男子气概的年轻人;  (网络用语)成功人士;  (2000年美国总统选举中)因选票重复计算问题而引起争议的穿孔卡片|A young, typically white, heterosexual male perceived as being entitled and privileged, often associated with traditional masculine stereotypes.  Originally used in a more neutral or even positive way, particularly in Chicago, the term has evolved to often carry negative connotations of arrogance and insensitivity.  It also refers to a small piece of paper that is punched out of a voting card, notably relevant during the 2000 U.S. presidential election. |He's such a chad, always bragging about his car. 他真是个小伙子，总是吹嘘他的车。|Synonyms: bloke, dude, guy, hunk (sometimes), alpha male (sometimes) |Antonyms:  N/A (Direct antonyms are difficult; one might consider terms like "nerd," "geek," or "beta male" as contrasting figures, but these are not direct opposites.)|noun
