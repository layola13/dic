cloudily:/ˈklaʊdɪli/| adv.|阴沉地，多云地|In a cloudy manner; with clouds.  Covered or filled with clouds; overcast.|The sky was cloudily gray. 天空阴沉沉的，一片灰色。|近义词：overcastly, gloomily, darkly|反义词：brightly, clearly, sunnily|词性:副词
词源: From cloud + -y + -ly.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *klud-, meaning "lump, rock, hill."
