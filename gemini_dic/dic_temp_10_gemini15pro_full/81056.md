graille:/ɡʁaj/ (French)| n.|食物; 伙食 (informal, often pejorative)|Food; grub.  Often implies food that is plentiful but of poor quality or messy to eat.|例句：On a mangé de la bonne graille hier soir.  We ate some good grub last night.|例句：Ce restaurant sert une graille infâme. This restaurant serves awful grub.|近义词：bouffe (French, informal), food, grub|反义词:delicacy, gourmet food|词性:noun

注意事项:
The word "graille" is primarily used in informal French and often carries a slightly negative or pejorative connotation, implying that the food is plentiful but not necessarily of high quality.  It's not typically used in formal settings.  The etymology is uncertain, but it's believed to be of onomatopoeic origin.
