disdainfully:/dɪsˈdeɪnfʊli/ | adv. | 轻蔑地，鄙夷地 | showing contempt or lack of respect | She disdainfully rejected his offer of help. 她轻蔑地拒绝了他的帮助。 |  He tossed the crumpled letter disdainfully into the trash. 他轻蔑地把揉皱的信扔进了垃圾桶。 |  scornfully, contemptuously | respectfully, admiringly | adverb

Etymology:
From Middle English desdeynousli, from Old French desdeignossement, from desdeigner (“to disdain”), from des- (“dis-”) + deigner (“to deign”).
