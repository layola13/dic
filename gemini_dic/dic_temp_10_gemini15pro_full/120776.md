misappreciation:/ˌmɪsəˌpriːʃiˈeɪʃən/| n.|误解，低估，错误评价|An act of misappreciating something or someone; failure to appreciate correctly.  A lack of appreciation or an inaccurate understanding of something's true worth or value.|She suffered from a misappreciation of her own abilities. 她对自己能力的误判让她很受挫。|The misappreciation of the painting's artistic value led to its sale for a pittance.  对这幅画艺术价值的低估导致它以极低的价格出售。|近义词：misunderstanding, undervaluation, misevaluation|反义词: appreciation, recognition, understanding|词性:noun
