overdistant:/ˌoʊvərˈdɪstənt/| adj.|过于疏远的，冷漠的|Excessively distant, either physically or emotionally; aloof.|例句：Their relationship became overdistant after the argument. 他们的关系在争吵后变得过于疏远了。

例句：He maintained an overdistant demeanor throughout the meeting.  他在整个会议过程中都保持着一种过于疏远的举止。|近义词：remote, aloof, detached, standoffish|反义词：close, intimate, warm, friendly|词性:形容词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "distant" (adjective meaning "far away in space or time")
