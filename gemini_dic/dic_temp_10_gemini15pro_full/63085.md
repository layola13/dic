ensconce: /ɪnˈskɒns/ (UK), /ɛnˈskɑːns/ (US) | v. | 安置；使…舒适地安顿下来 |  To settle securely or snugly in a place; to establish or settle (someone) in a comfortable, safe, or secret place. |  She ensconced herself in a comfortable armchair with a good book. 她舒适地坐在扶手椅上，拿着一本好书。|  He ensconced his family in a secluded villa. 他把家人安置在一栋僻静的别墅里。 |  settle, install, nestle, snuggle |  dislodge, remove | verb
