concurrent:/kənˈkɜːrənt/ /kənˈkʌrənt/| adj.|同时发生的，并存的|Happening or existing at the same time.|The concurrent rise in unemployment and inflation is a serious problem for the government. 失业率和通货膨胀率的同时上升对政府来说是一个严重的问题.|synonyms: simultaneous, coincident, contemporaneous|antonyms: non-concurrent, successive, asynchronous|adjective

concurrent:/kənˈkɜːrənt/ /kənˈkʌrənt/| n.|同时发生的事物|Something that happens at the same time as something else.|The judge imposed concurrent sentences for the two crimes. 法官对这两项罪行判处了合并执行的刑罚。|synonyms: coincidence, conjunction|antonyms: divergence, separation|noun
