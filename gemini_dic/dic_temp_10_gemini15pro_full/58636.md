dumber:/ˈdʌmər/ | adj. | 更笨的，更愚蠢的 | More stupid or unintelligent than before or than someone else. | He made a dumber decision than I thought he would. 他做的决定比我想象的还要愚蠢。|  synonyms: stupider, more foolish, more idiotic | antonyms: smarter, brighter, more intelligent | adjective

dumber:/ˈdʌmər/ | adj. | 更笨的，更愚蠢的 | Comparative form of "dumb," meaning more lacking in intelligence. |  She felt dumber after failing the test.  考试不及格后，她觉得自己更笨了。| synonyms: more obtuse, more dense, less sharp | antonyms: wiser, more astute, more perceptive | adjective
