lacerative: /ˈlæsəreɪtɪv/
adjective
撕裂的; 伤口撕裂的
Tending to lacerate; causing laceration.
The barbed wire caused a lacerative wound.  带刺的铁丝网造成了撕裂的伤口。
lacerating, tearing, ripping
healing, soothing
