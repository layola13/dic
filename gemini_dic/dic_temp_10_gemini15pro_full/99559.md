interhybridize:/ˌɪntərˈhaɪbrɪdaɪz/
verb
杂交；进行种间杂交
to hybridize between species or varieties
Scientists have successfully interhybridized different species of orchids.  科学家们成功地将不同种类的兰花进行了杂交。
Different varieties of corn are sometimes interhybridized to produce more resilient crops.  不同品种的玉米有时会被杂交以生产更有抗逆性的作物。
近义词: crossbreed, hybridize
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "inbreed")
词性: verb
词源: inter- (between) + hybridize (to crossbreed)
