anglicizations: /ˌæŋɡlɪsaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 英文化；英语化 | The process of making something more English in form or character.  This can include adapting foreign words, names, or customs to English norms. | The anglicizations of many Irish place names have obscured their original Gaelic meanings. 许多爱尔兰地名的英语化掩盖了它们最初的盖尔语含义。|  The restaurant's menu featured several anglicizations of classic French dishes. 这家餐厅的菜单上有几道经典法国菜的英文化版本。 | synonyms: adaptations, modifications, transformations | antonyms: nativizations, vernacularizations | noun
