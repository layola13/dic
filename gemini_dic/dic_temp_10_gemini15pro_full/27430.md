camisated:/ˈkæmɪˌseɪtɪd/
adjective
被套上的，包住的
Enclosed in a close-fitting case or sheath.  Specifically, of a blood vessel: enclosed in a sheath, especially of connective tissue.
The camisated artery showed no signs of rupture. 这条被包裹的动脉没有显示出破裂的迹象。
encased, sheathed, enveloped
exposed, bare
