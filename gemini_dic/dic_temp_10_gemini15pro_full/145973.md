overdiversifying:/ˌoʊvərdaɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪɪŋ/
verb
过度多元化
Investing in too many different assets, thus reducing the potential for higher returns and increasing the complexity of managing the portfolio.
He worried about overdiversifying his investments and losing potential gains. 他担心过度分散投资而损失潜在收益。
Spreading your investments too thinly across numerous asset classes can lead to overdiversifying and diminished returns. 将你的投资过于分散到众多资产类别会导致过度多元化和收益减少。

近义词: overextending, overspreading
反义词: underdiversifying, concentrating
