ailanthus:/eɪˈlænθəs/ | n. | 臭椿，樗 | A fast-growing tree native to China and Taiwan, known for its unpleasant smell. | The ailanthus tree is considered an invasive species in many parts of the world. 臭椿在世界许多地方被认为是入侵物种。|  The ailanthus can thrive in even the most polluted urban environments. 臭椿即使在污染最严重的城市环境中也能茁壮成长。 | synonyms: tree of heaven, Chinese sumac | antonyms: N/A | noun
