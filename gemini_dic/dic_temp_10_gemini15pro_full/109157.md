legit:/ləˈdʒɪt/| adj.|合法的；正规的；真的；地道|Something that is legit is considered authentic, genuine, or conforms to the rules.  It originated as slang, a shortened form of "legitimate".|That concert ticket is totally legit. 那张演唱会门票绝对是真的。|Synonyms: genuine, authentic, real, valid, bona fide|Antonyms: fake, counterfeit, bogus, phony, illegitimate|adjective

legit:/ləˈdʒɪt/| adv.|合法地；正规地；真正地；地道地|In a legit way; legitimately.  It is the adverbial form of "legit."|They're doing business legit now. 他们现在合法地做生意。|Synonyms: legitimately, genuinely, authentically, really, truly|Antonyms: illegally, falsely, fraudulently|adverb
