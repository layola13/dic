hygroscopicity:/ˌhaɪɡrəˈskɒpɪsɪti/ | n. | 吸湿性；吸水性 | The property of a substance to readily absorb moisture from the air. |  Table salt has high hygroscopicity. 食盐有很强的吸湿性。| synonyms: moisture absorption, water absorption | antonyms: water resistance, hydrophobicity | noun

hygroscopic:/ˌhaɪɡrəˈskɒpɪk/ | adj. | 吸湿的；吸水的 |  Describing a substance that readily absorbs moisture from the air. | Silica gel is a hygroscopic material often used as a desiccant. 硅胶是一种吸湿性物质，常用作干燥剂。| synonyms: moisture-absorbing, water-absorbing | antonyms: water-resistant, hydrophobic | adjective
