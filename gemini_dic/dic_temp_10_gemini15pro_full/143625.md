outjourney:/ˈaʊtˌdʒɜːrni/
noun
外出旅行，远行
An outward journey; a journey away from home.
We began our outjourney to the mountains early in the morning. 我们一大早开始了前往山区的旅程。
synonyms: trip, excursion, expedition, journey
antonyms: return, homecoming, inbound journey
n.
