caballing:/kəˈbæliŋ/
verb
勾结，密谋，策划
Secretly conspiring or plotting together, typically to achieve something illicit or immoral.
They were accused of caballing against the CEO. 他们被指控密谋反对首席执行官。
caballing against the government to overthrow it.  密谋推翻政府。

近义词:conspiring, plotting, intriguing, scheming
反义词:cooperating, collaborating

词性:v.
