commercial:/kəˈmɜːrʃl/ (US) /kəˈmɜːʃəl/ (UK)| adj. n.|商业的；商业广告|Relating to or engaged in commerce; an advertisement broadcast on radio or television.|The company is facing commercial pressure to increase profits. 这家公司面临着提高利润的商业压力.|
commercial:/kəˈmɜːrʃl/ (US) /kəˈmɜːʃəl/ (UK)| adj. n.|商业的；商业广告|A new commercial for the car aired during the Super Bowl. 超级碗期间播放了一则新的汽车广告。|近义词:business, mercantile, advertisement|反义词:noncommercial, non-profit|词性:adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *commercialis, from Latin commercium (“commerce, trade”), from com- (“together”) + merx (“merchandise”).
