delineator:/dɪˈlɪnɪeɪtər/| n. |界定器，分隔物，轮廓标|Something that clearly shows the edge or shape of something.  A person or thing that describes or portrays something precisely.|The delineators on the side of the highway help drivers stay in their lanes. 高速公路两侧的界定器帮助司机保持在车道内。|Synonyms: marker, boundary, divider, separator|Antonyms: blender, merger, integrator|noun

delineator:/dɪˈlɪnɪeɪtər/| n. |描绘者，解释者|A person or thing that describes or portrays something precisely.|The author acts as a delineator of social inequalities in her novel. 作者在小说中充当了社会不平等的描绘者。|Synonyms: describer, portrayer, explainer, interpreter|Antonyms: obfuscator, confuser|noun
