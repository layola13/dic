legalistic:/ˌliːɡəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
墨守成规的，拘泥于法律条文的
excessively concerned with or adhering to the letter or formal interpretation of the law, often to the detriment of its spirit or intent.
The lawyer's legalistic approach often alienated his clients.  这位律师拘泥于法律条文的方法常常疏远了他的客户。
近义词:formalistic, by-the-book, rigid, inflexible
反义词:flexible, pragmatic, reasonable, lenient
词性:adjective

legalistic:/ˌliːɡəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
法律的，法理的
of or relating to law or legalism.
His legalistic arguments were difficult for the jury to understand. 他的法律论证让陪审团难以理解。
近义词:legal, judicial, juridical
反义词:illegal, unlawful
词性:adjective
