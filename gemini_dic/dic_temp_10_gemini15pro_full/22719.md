boring:/ˈbɔːrɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbɔːrɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|无聊的，令人厌烦的|Causing weariness and restlessness through lack of interest.|The lecture was so boring that I almost fell asleep.  讲座太无聊了，我差点睡着了。|Synonyms: tedious, dull, monotonous, uninteresting|Antonyms: interesting, exciting, stimulating, engaging|adjective
