palaverist:/pəˈlævərɪst/
noun
空谈家，爱闲聊的人
A person who engages in excessive or idle talk; a chatterbox.
He's such a palaverist, always going on about nothing. 他真是个空谈家，总是喋喋不休地说着无关紧要的事情。
Synonyms: chatterbox, windbag, gossipmonger
Antonyms: reticent person, quiet person
n.

palaver:/pəˈlævər/
noun & verb
冗长的讨论；闲聊 (noun);  进行冗长的讨论；闲聊 (verb)
A long discussion or conference, especially one between people of different cultures or levels of status (noun); To hold a long discussion or conference (verb).
After much palaver, they finally reached an agreement.  经过长时间的讨论，他们终于达成了协议。
The tribal elders palavered for hours before making a decision. 部落长老们讨论了几个小时才做出决定。
Synonyms: discussion, conference, talk, chat (noun); discuss, talk, chat, confer (verb)
Antonyms: silence, quiet (noun); be silent, listen (verb)
n. v.
