nonindustriously:/ˌnɒnɪnˈdʌstriəsli/
adverb|不勤奋地，懒惰地|Not industriously; in a lazy or unproductive manner.|He worked nonindustriously, often taking long breaks and achieving little. 他工作不勤奋，经常休息很长时间，几乎没有什么成就。|She approached her studies nonindustriously, leading to poor grades.  她学习不勤奋，导致成绩很差。|近义词:lazily, idly, slothfully|反义词:industriously, diligently, assiduously|词性:副词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non "not."
industriously (adverb): From industry + -ously.  Industry from Latin industria "diligence, activity." -ously is an adverbial suffix.
