dampener:/ˈdæmpənər (US) / ˈdæmpnə(r) (UK)| n.|抑制剂; 减震器; 扫兴的人或事| something that makes feelings, especially of excitement or enjoyment, less strong| The bad news put a dampener on the celebrations.  这个坏消息给庆祝活动泼了冷水。|synonyms: deterrent, discouragement, restraint, curb, check, constraint, damper, killjoy, spoilsport, wet blanket| antonyms: encouragement, stimulant, catalyst| noun
