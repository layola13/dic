hobnailed:/ˈhɒbneɪld/ (UK) /ˈhɑːbneɪld/ (US)| adj.|带钉的；粗糙的；|Covered with hobnails, especially of boots; (figuratively) rough or coarse.|例句：He wore hobnailed boots. 他穿着带钉的靴子。|例句：She had a hobnailed voice from years of smoking. 多年的吸烟使她的嗓音变得粗糙。|近义词：studded, spiked, rough, coarse|反义词:smooth, soft|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From hobnail + -ed.  Hobnail itself comes from hob (a peg, projection) + nail.  The "hob" element is of uncertain origin, possibly related to a hobgoblin or a hob (a part of a fireplace).
