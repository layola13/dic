incoherent:/ˌɪnkəʊˈhɪərənt/ /ˌɪnkəˈhɪrənt/
adjective
不连贯的，语无伦次的
(of language or thought) expressed in a confusing or illogical way; unable to express yourself clearly
例句：His speech was rambling and incoherent. 他的讲话漫无边际，语无伦次。
例句：She became incoherent with rage. 她愤怒得语无伦次。
近义词：confused, illogical, rambling, disjointed, unintelligible
反义词：coherent, lucid, articulate, clear
词性: adjective

incoherence:/ˌɪnkəʊˈhɪərəns/ /ˌɪnkəˈhɪrəns/
noun
不连贯，语无伦次
the quality of being illogical, confused, or unclear in speech or thought
例句：The incoherence of his explanation made it impossible to understand. 他的解释语无伦次，让人无法理解。
例句：There was an incoherence in his thinking that worried his colleagues. 他思维的混乱让他的同事们感到担忧。
近义词：confusion, illogicality, rambling, disjointedness
反义词：coherence, clarity, lucidity, articulation
词性: noun
