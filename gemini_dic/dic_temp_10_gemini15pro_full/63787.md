epicycle:/ˈɛpɪˌsaɪkəl/| n. | 本轮; 周转圆 |A small circle whose center moves around the circumference of a larger one.  In ancient astronomy, epicycles were used to model the apparent motions of planets. |  Ptolemy used epicycles to explain the retrograde motion of Mars. 托勒密用本轮来解释火星的逆行运动。

The planet's orbit was modeled using a system of epicycles. 这颗行星的轨道是用本轮系统来模拟的。|  近义词:circle, orbit, cycle| 反义词: N/A | noun
