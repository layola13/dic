miasma: /maɪˈæzmə/ /miˈæzmə/ | n. | 瘴气，臭气，不良气氛 | An unpleasant or unhealthy smell or atmosphere. |  A miasma of stale cigarette smoke hung in the air. 空气中弥漫着一股难闻的香烟味。

The financial scandal left a miasma of distrust over the entire industry. 金融丑闻给整个行业蒙上了一层不信任的阴影。 | 近义词: stench, stink, odor, reek, effluvium | 反义词: fragrance, perfume, aroma | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin miasma, from Greek *miasma, from *miainein "to stain, pollute."
