dukhobors:/duːkəˈbɔːrz/
n.|杜霍波尔人|A religious group of Russian origin, known for their pacifism and communal living.|The Dukhobors emigrated to Canada in the late 19th and early 20th centuries. 杜霍波尔人在19世纪末和20世纪初移民到加拿大。|近义词:pacifists,communalists|反义词:militarists,individualists|词性:noun

dukhobors:/duːkəˈbɔːrz/
n.|杜霍波尔派教徒|Members of the Dukhobor religious sect.|Some Dukhobors practiced nudism as a form of protest. 一些杜霍波尔派教徒以裸体作为抗议的一种形式。|近义词:sectarians, dissenters|反义词:conformists|词性:noun
