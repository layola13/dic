ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *Caspium (mare) "Caspian (sea)," from Greek Kaspion (pelagos) "Caspian (sea)," from Kaspioi, a people who lived southwest of the sea in ancient times.  The ultimate origin of the name is unknown.
