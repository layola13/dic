nonrotating:/ˌnɒnˈrəʊteɪtɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːnˈroʊteɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非旋转的，不旋转的
Not rotating; not turning on or around an axis.
例句：A nonrotating space station would not experience artificial gravity. 一个不旋转的空间站将不会体验到人造重力。
例句：The Earth is not nonrotating, it spins on its axis. 地球不是不旋转的，它绕着它的轴旋转。
近义词：stationary, fixed, immobile
反义词：rotating, spinning, revolving
词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix) + rotating (present participle of rotate)
"non-" comes from Latin, meaning "not". "Rotate" comes from Latin "rotatus," the past participle of "rotare," meaning "to turn round," from "rota," meaning "wheel."
