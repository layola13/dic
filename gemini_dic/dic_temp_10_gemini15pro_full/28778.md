caroched:/kəˈroʊʃt/ |v. | 乘马车；狂欢 | To travel in a horse-drawn carriage, especially in a leisurely or celebratory way.  Often implies a sense of revelry or noisy, drunken merrymaking.| They caroched through the countryside, singing loudly. 他们乘着马车穿过乡间，高声歌唱着。 | The winning team caroched through the streets, celebrating their victory. 获胜的队伍乘着马车穿过街道，庆祝他们的胜利。 | 近义词: rode, drove, cavorted | 反义词: walked, trudged | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From French *carocher*, from Italian *carroccio*, "war chariot".
