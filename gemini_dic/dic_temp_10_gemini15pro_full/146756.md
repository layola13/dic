overhunt:/ˌoʊvərˈhʌnt/| v.|过度捕猎|To hunt a species to the point of endangering its population or causing local extinction.|They overhunted the deer in that area, and now they are scarce.|Synonyms: overexploit, deplete|Antonyms: conserve, protect|verb

overhunt:/ˌoʊvərˈhʌnt/| n.|过度捕猎|Excessive hunting that depletes a population.|The overhunt of whales in the 19th century nearly drove them to extinction.|Synonyms: overexploitation, depletion|Antonyms: conservation, protection|noun
