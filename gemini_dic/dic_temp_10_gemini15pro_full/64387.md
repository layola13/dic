eremacausis:/ˌɛrɪməˈkɔːsɪs/ | n. |  缓慢燃烧; 腐败; 朽化 | Slow combustion or oxidation of organic matter, especially in dead plant material, at ordinary temperatures with the evolution of heat, but without visible fire. |  The eremacausis of fallen leaves enriches the soil. 落叶的朽化使土壤肥沃。 | 近义词: decomposition, decay, putrefaction, rotting | 反义词: growth, synthesis | 词性: noun

词源: From Greek *ērēmakausia*, from *ērēmos* "quiet, still" + *kausis* "burning".
