fippenny:/ˈfɪpni/ (UK) /ˈfaɪvpɛni/ (US)| adj.|五便士的；微不足道的；不值钱的|Relating to or costing five pence (historically); trifling; insignificant.  Derived from "five" and "penny". Originally referred to a five-penny piece. Now mostly used figuratively.|He dismissed it as a fippenny affair. 他认为这是一件微不足道的事情。|近义词：cheap, insignificant, trifling, paltry|反义词：valuable, expensive, significant|词性:形容词
