benzol:/ˈbɛnzɒl/ | n. | 苯 | An old name for benzene, a colorless volatile liquid hydrocarbon present in coal tar and petroleum, used in the manufacture of many organic chemicals. | Benzol was once widely used as a solvent. 苯曾经被广泛用作溶剂。 | 近义词: benzene | 反义词:  (无常用反义词) | 词性: noun

词源: From German *Benzol*, from *Benz(oin)* (benzoin, a resin from which benzene was first obtained) + *-ol* (indicating an alcohol or phenol).  This term is now largely obsolete and replaced by "benzene."
