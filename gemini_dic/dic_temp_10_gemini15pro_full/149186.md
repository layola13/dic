oxygen-bars:/ˈɒksɪdʒən bɑːrz/ (UK) /ˈɑːksɪdʒən bɑːrz/ (US)| n. |氧吧|A place where customers pay to breathe oxygen, often scented with aromas, for a short period.  It's promoted as relaxing and energizing, though the health benefits are disputed.| I went to an oxygen bar after a stressful day at work. 我在工作压力大的一天后去了氧吧。|Synonyms: oxygen cafe, oxygen spa|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "polluted area" in a contextual sense)
