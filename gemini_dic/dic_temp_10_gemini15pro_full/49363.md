deliquesce:/ˌdɛlɪˈkwɛs/ |vi.| 潮解，溶解 |To absorb moisture from the air and dissolve into a liquid solution.  |Example 1: The calcium chloride deliquesced in the humid air, forming a puddle. 氯化钙在潮湿的空气中潮解，形成一滩液体。

Example 2:  The salt crystals began to deliquesce after being left out in the rain.  盐晶体在雨中放置一段时间后开始潮解。 | 近义词: dissolve, melt, liquefy | 反义词: solidify, harden, dry | verb
