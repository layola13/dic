chiromancers:/ˈkaɪrəmænsərz/ | n. | 手相家，掌纹算命者 | A person who practices chiromancy; a palm reader.  They claim to be able to tell the future by reading the lines on someone's palm. |  The chiromancers at the carnival claimed to know your destiny. 嘉年华上的手相家声称知道你的命运。

The king consulted with his chiromancers before making important decisions. 国王在做重要决定之前会咨询他的手相师。| 近义词: palm reader, palmist, fortune teller | 反义词: skeptic | 词性: noun
