nonsegregation: /ˌnɑːnˌseɡrɪˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. | 非隔离，不分隔 | The absence of segregation; the state of being integrated or mixed. |  The school's policy of nonsegregation ensures that all students have equal access to resources. 这所学校的非隔离政策确保所有学生都能平等地获得资源。

The city's commitment to nonsegregation is reflected in its diverse neighborhoods. 这座城市对非隔离的承诺体现在其多元化的社区中。 | 近义词：integration, desegregation, mixing | 反义词：segregation, separation, discrimination | 词性：名词

Etymology:  From non- + segregation.  "Segregation" comes from the Latin word "segregare," meaning "to set apart."  Therefore, "nonsegregation" literally means "not setting apart."
