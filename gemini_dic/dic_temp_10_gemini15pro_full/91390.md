hydrothorax:/ˌhaɪdrəˈθɔːræks/
noun
胸腔积液
An abnormal accumulation of serous fluid in the pleural cavity.
Hydrothorax is a condition characterized by excess fluid buildup in the pleural cavity, the space surrounding the lungs.
胸腔积液是一种以胸膜腔（肺周围的空间）积液过多为特征的疾病。

The patient presented with symptoms of shortness of breath and chest pain, and was subsequently diagnosed with hydrothorax.
病人出现呼吸急促和胸痛的症状，随后被诊断为胸腔积液。


Near synonyms: pleural effusion, water on the chest
近义词: 胸膜积液, 胸腔积水

Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but the opposite state would be a normal pleural cavity with no excess fluid)
反义词: （没有直接的反义词，但相反的状态是胸膜腔正常，没有积液）
