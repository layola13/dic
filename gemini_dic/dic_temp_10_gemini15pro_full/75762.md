fulminatory:/ˈfʊlmɪneɪtəri/ /ˈfʊlmɪnətɔːri/
adjective
爆发性的；雷霆万钧的；激烈的
coming on suddenly and with great severity; explosive
The disease is characterized by a fulminatory onset, often leading to rapid deterioration. 这种疾病的特点是爆发性发作，通常会导致病情迅速恶化。
His fulminatory denunciation of the government shocked everyone. 他对政府的猛烈抨击震惊了所有人。

近义词:explosive, sudden, violent, severe, fierce, vehement, raging, furious
反义词:gradual, mild, moderate, slow

词性:adjective
词源:From Latin fulminatus, past participle of fulminare "to strike with lightning," from fulmen "lightning bolt."
