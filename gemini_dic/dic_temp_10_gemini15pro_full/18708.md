bereave: /bɪˈriːv/ /bəˈriːv/
verb
剥夺，使丧失，使痛失（尤指亲人）
deprive (someone) of a loved one through death.
She was bereaved by her husband's death. 她因丈夫去世而悲痛欲绝。

The accident bereaved them of their only child.  这场事故使他们失去了唯一的孩子。

synonym: deprive, rob, strip, dispossess
antonym: provide, give, bestow
词性: 动词 (v.)


bereavement: /bɪˈriːvmənt/ /bəˈriːvmənt/
noun
丧亲之痛；丧失亲人之苦
the state of being bereaved.
She never fully recovered from the bereavement of her mother. 她从未从丧母之痛中完全恢复过来。

He suffered a profound bereavement when his wife died. 妻子的去世使他遭受了巨大的悲痛。

synonym: grief, loss, mourning, sorrow
antonym: joy, happiness, comfort
词性: 名词 (n.)
