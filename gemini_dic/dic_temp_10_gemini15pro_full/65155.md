ethnical: /ˈɛθɪkəl/ | adj. |伦理的，道德的| Relating to moral principles or the branch of philosophy dealing with right and wrong conduct.|例句：The company's decision to close the factory raised some ethical questions.  这家公司关闭工厂的决定引发了一些伦理问题。

例句：Is it ethical to use animals for medical research?  用动物进行医学研究合乎道德吗？

近义词：moral, virtuous, righteous|反义词：unethical, immoral, wrong|词性:形容词
