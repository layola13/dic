maidenliness:/ˈmeɪdnlinəs/
n.
少女般的娴静, 贞淑, 处女风范
The quality of being maidenly; the state or character of a maiden; modesty.
She possessed a certain maidenliness that was both charming and intriguing. 她拥有一种既迷人又引人入胜的少女般的娴静。
The novel explored themes of maidenliness and societal expectations placed on young women. 这部小说探讨了少女贞洁以及社会对年轻女性的期望。
近义词: modesty, chastity, purity, innocence
反义词: immodesty, boldness, brazenness
词性: noun

Etymology:
From maiden + -li- + -ness.  "Maiden" comes from Old English "mægden" meaning "virgin, young unmarried woman."  The suffix "-li-" forms adjectives and "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives.
