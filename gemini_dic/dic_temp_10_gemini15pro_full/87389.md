herpetologist:/ˌhɜːrpɪˈtɒlədʒɪst/ /ˌhɜːrpəˈtɑːlədʒɪst/| n. |爬虫学家|A biologist specializing in the study of reptiles and amphibians.|例句：The herpetologist carefully documented the behavior of the venomous snake.  爬虫学家仔细记录了毒蛇的行为。

例句：My dream is to become a herpetologist and study lizards in the rainforest. 我的梦想是成为一名爬虫学家，在雨林中研究蜥蜴。|近义词：reptile and amphibian specialist|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
