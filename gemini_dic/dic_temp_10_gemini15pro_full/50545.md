deputable:/dɪˈpjuːtəbl/ | adj. | 可委任的，可派遣的 | Capable of being deputed or delegated.  Able to be appointed as a deputy or representative. | The manager's assistant was deputable to sign contracts in his absence.  经理的助手在他不在的时候被授权签署合同。 | Synonyms: delegable, assignable, appointable | Antonyms: non-delegable, non-transferable | Word type: adjective

depute:/dɪˈpjuːt/ | v. | 委任，派遣 | To appoint someone as a deputy or representative. To delegate authority or a task to someone. | The CEO deputed his vice president to handle the negotiations. 首席执行官委派他的副总裁处理谈判。| Synonyms: delegate, assign, commission, appoint | Antonyms: retain, keep, withhold | Word type: verb
