interlayers:/ˈɪntərˌleɪərz/
n.
中间层；夹层；中间层物质
A layer of a different material between two other layers of a different material.
The plywood is made of five interlayers of wood veneer.  这种胶合板由五层木板贴面构成。
The cake had delicious cream interlayers.  这个蛋糕有美味的奶油夹层。
synonyms: layer, stratum, lamina, sheet, ply
antonyms: exterior, surface
