gynaeceum:/ˌɡaɪnɪˈsiːəm/ /ˌdʒaɪnɪˈsiːəm/ | n. | 雌蕊群；妇女室，女眷居室 |The innermost whorl of a flower, consisting of one or more pistils.  A part of a house in ancient Greece reserved for women. | The botanist examined the gynaeceum of the orchid. 植物学家检查了兰花的雌蕊群。| The women gathered in the gynaeceum to spin wool. 妇女们聚集在女眷居室纺羊毛。| synonyms: pistil, carpel | antonyms: androecium | noun
