galloping:/ˈɡæləpɪŋ/| adj. n.| 飞驰的，疾驰的；飞奔；|moving or progressing very quickly.|The galloping inflation is causing widespread concern.  飞涨的通货膨胀正在引起广泛关注。|The horse broke into a galloping pace. 这匹马开始飞奔起来。|近义词：racing, speeding, dashing, bolting, cantering|反义词：slow, crawling, creeping|词性: adjective, noun (gerund/present participle)
Etymology: From Middle English *galopen, from Old French galoper, of Germanic origin.
