infernally:/ɪnˈfɜːnəli/| adv.|极其地，非常地，可怕地|Intensely, extremely, in a hellish manner.  Derived from "infernal" which means relating to hell.  It emphasizes the extreme nature of something, often unpleasant.|The traffic was infernally slow. 交通慢得要命。|Synonym: extremely, awfully, terribly, devilishly|Antonym: mildly, slightly|Adverb
