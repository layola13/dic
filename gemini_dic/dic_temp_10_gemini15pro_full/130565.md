nonactionably:/ˌnɒnˈækʃənəbli/
adjective
不可控告地，无行为能力地
Not capable of being acted upon or giving grounds for legal action.
The report concluded that the company's actions were nonactionably negligent. 报告的结论是，该公司的行为是不可控告的疏忽。
The lawyer determined that the case was nonactionably weak. 律师认定这个案件因证据不足而无法进行诉讼。
近义词: unimpeachable, unchallengeable, invulnerable
反义词: actionable, liable, culpable
词性: adjective
词源:  non- (not) + actionable (-able capable of being acted upon)
