asperser:/əˈspɜːrsər/ | n. |诽谤者，污蔑者 |Someone who attacks or criticizes the reputation or integrity of someone else. | The online asperser hid behind a fake profile.  网上诽谤者躲在虚假的个人资料后面。| Synonyms: slanderer, defamer, calumniator | Antonyms: defender, advocate | noun

asperse:/əˈspɜːrs/ | vt. | 诽谤，污蔑，中伤 | To attack or criticize the reputation or integrity of someone or something. |  His rivals aspersed his character during the election.  他的竞争对手在选举期间诽谤他的人格。| Synonyms: slander, defame, calumniate, denigrate, vilify | Antonyms: praise, commend, laud, extol | verb
