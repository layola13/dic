anastatic:/ˌænəˈstætɪk/ | adj. |  复印的；能复写的；再生的 |Relating to or denoting a method of printing from a design drawn or written on a zinc plate with a greasy ink, which is then etched with dilute nitric acid and printed lithographically. | The anastatic process was an early form of photocopying.  这种复印法是一种早期的影印形式。| The museum displayed several anastatic prints of ancient manuscripts. 博物馆展出了几幅古代手稿的复印作品。 | synonyms:  reproducible, replicable, duplicable | antonyms: original, unique | adjective
