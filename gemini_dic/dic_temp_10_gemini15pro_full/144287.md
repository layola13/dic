outspeeded:/ˌaʊtˈspiːdɪd/
verb
超过速度, 比...更快
To move faster than someone or something else.
The cheetah outspeeded the gazelle. 猎豹的速度超过了瞪羚。
His new car easily outspeeded mine on the highway. 他的新车在高速公路上轻松地超过了我的车。
近义词: outrun, outpace, surpass
反义词: lag behind, trail
