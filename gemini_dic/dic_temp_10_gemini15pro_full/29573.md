catchweight:/ˈkætʃˌweɪt/| n. |约定重量，比赛时的体重限制|A weight agreed upon for a boxing or wrestling match, especially one between contestants of widely differing weights, or a weight limit for such a match.|The fight was set at a catchweight of 160 pounds. 这场比赛的约定重量为160磅。|Synonyms: agreed weight, stipulated weight|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps "open weight" in some contexts)|noun
