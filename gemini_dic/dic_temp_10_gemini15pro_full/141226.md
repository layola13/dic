oosporic:/ˌoʊəˈspɔːrɪk/
adjective|卵孢子的，由卵孢子产生的|Relating to or derived from an oospore, a thick-walled sexual spore that develops from a fertilized oosphere.|Oosporic reproduction is common in certain algae and fungi.|近义词: oosporogenous|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider terms related to asexual reproduction, e.g., vegetative, asexual)|词性: adjective
