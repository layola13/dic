nonjoiners:/ˌnɒnˈdʒɔɪnəz/
|n.|不加入者；非会员|People who choose not to join a group, organization, or activity are referred to as nonjoiners.  They may have various reasons for not participating.|例句：The survey revealed a significant number of nonjoiners among the eligible population.  这项调查显示，在符合条件的人群中，不加入者的人数相当多。

例句：The union leaders are concerned about the high percentage of nonjoiners in the workforce. 工会领导人对劳动大军中不加入者的比例很高表示担忧。
|近义词：non-members, abstainers, outsiders|反义词: members, participants, joiners|词性: 名词
