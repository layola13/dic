metallophone:/ˈmɛtəl əˌfoʊn/ | n. | 金属乐器，钢片琴 | A musical instrument consisting of a series of metal bars of graduated lengths, played with mallets. |  The children enjoyed playing the metallophone in music class. 孩子们喜欢在音乐课上演奏钢片琴。

The street musician played a lively tune on his metallophone.  街头音乐家用他的钢片琴演奏了一段欢快的曲子。

| 近义词：glockenspiel, xylophone | 反义词:  (无明确的反义词) | 词性: noun


Etymology: From French *métallophone*, from métal (“metal”) + -phone, from Greek phōnḗ (“sound”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰwón- (“to sound, to call”).
