orthotone:/ˈɔːrθəˌtoʊn/| n. |正调字，同调词|A word having the same tone or stress throughout; a word without accent.|The word "orthotone" itself is not an orthotone because it has stress on the second syllable.|近义词: monotonic (in terms of sound) |反义词: heterotone, polytone|noun
