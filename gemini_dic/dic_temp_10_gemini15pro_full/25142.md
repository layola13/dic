buckle:/ˈbʌkəl/ (US) /ˈbʌkl/ (UK)| n. v.|扣环，搭扣；扣住，系牢|A metal or plastic fastener for a belt or strap, typically one in which a prong is inserted into a slot or other opening.  To fasten or be fastened with a buckle.|例句：He buckled his belt. 他系上了皮带。|近义词：fasten, clasp, secure|反义词:unbuckle, unfasten|noun
buckle:/ˈbʌkəl/ (US) /ˈbʌkl/ (UK)| v.|扣住，系牢；屈服，垮掉|To fasten or be fastened with a buckle; to bend, warp, or collapse under pressure or strain.|例句：The bridge buckled under the weight of the truck. 桥在卡车的重压下垮塌了。|近义词：fasten, clasp, secure, yield, collapse|反义词:unbuckle, unfasten, straighten|verb
