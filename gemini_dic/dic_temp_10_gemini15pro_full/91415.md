hyena:/haɪˈiːnə/ |n.|鬣狗|A doglike carnivorous mammal of Africa and southern Asia, having coarse fur, a sloping back, and powerful jaws. It produces a distinctive laugh-like howl.|The hyena laughed as it scavenged for food. 鬣狗在觅食时发出笑声。|近义词:  scavenger|反义词: prey|词性: noun

词源: From Late Latin *hyaena, from Ancient Greek ὕαινα (húaina), from ὗς (hûs, “pig, sow”).  The similarity in appearance and bristly hair led to the comparison with pigs.
