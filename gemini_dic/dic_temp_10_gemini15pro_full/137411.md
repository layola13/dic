nonterminative:/nɒntɜːrmɪnətɪv/
| adj. |非限定的，未完成的|Not coming to an end; inconclusive.  Referring to a verb form that does not express a completed action.|The nonterminative aspect of the verb suggests an ongoing process.|近义词：incomplete, unfinished, ongoing, continuous, progressive|反义词：terminative, completed, finished, concluded|词性:形容词
Etymology: From non- + terminative, from Latin *terminativus*, from terminare "to limit, bound," from terminus "boundary, limit."
