USAGE->
Simple Present:
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does he do for a living? 他做什么工作？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? 她晚饭在做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has she bought at the store? 她在商店买了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading this week? 他这周一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? 她对这部电影说了些什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you eaten before you got sick? 你生病前吃了什么？
What had she done before she left? 她离开前做了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I arrived? 我来之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before changing her major?  她转专业前一直在学什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气怎么样？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到今年年底你会完成什么？
What will they have finished by tomorrow? 到明天他们会完成什么？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for five years by then? 到那时你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时，她将已经学了什么多久了？
