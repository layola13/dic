nontemperately:/nɒnˈtɛmpərɪtli/
adverb
不节制地，无节制地
acting or done in a way that is not moderate or restrained; excessively
He indulged nontemperately in rich foods and desserts. 他毫无节制地沉溺于丰富的食物和甜点。
Near Antonyms: moderately, temperately, abstemiously
Antonyms: temperately
词性: 副词
