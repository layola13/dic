It refers to multiple clubs, typically social or sports organizations.|Os clubes de futebol são muito populares no Brasil. (Football clubs are very popular in Brazil.)|Near Synonyms: associations, societies, organizations|Antonym: (No direct antonym for a plural noun, but conceptually) individual, loner|Noun
