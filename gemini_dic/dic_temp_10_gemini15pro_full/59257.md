e-zine:/ˈiːziːn/ |n.|电子杂志|An electronic magazine, typically distributed via the internet.|Example 1: I subscribe to several e-zines about photography. 我订阅了几本关于摄影的电子杂志。

Example 2:  Many e-zines offer interactive features that print magazines can't. 许多电子杂志提供印刷杂志无法提供的互动功能。|近义词:online magazine, webzine, digital magazine|反义词:print magazine|词性:noun
