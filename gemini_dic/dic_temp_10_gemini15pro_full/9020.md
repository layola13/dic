antimagistrical: /ˌæntiˌmædʒɪˈstrɪkəl/
adjective
反对政府的；反抗权威的
opposed to magistrates or their authority; rebellious against authority.
The antimagistrical sentiments of the crowd grew as the unjust laws were enforced.  人群的反政府情绪随着不公正法律的实施而增长。
The pamphlet contained several antimagistrical arguments.  这本小册子包含了几篇反对政府的文章。
近义词: rebellious, seditious, insurrectionary, insubordinate, defiant
反义词: obedient, submissive, compliant, deferential, loyal
词性: adjective
词源: anti- (against) + magistrical (relating to a magistrate)
