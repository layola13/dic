hornpike:/ˈhɔːrnpaɪk/| n.|收费公路，收费站|A main road, especially one on which a toll is collected.|We drove along the hornpike to avoid the traffic in the city. 我们沿着收费公路行驶以避开市区的交通。|例句：The hornpike was congested due to the holiday traffic. 由于假日交通拥堵，收费公路非常拥堵。|近义词：toll road, turnpike, expressway|反义词:freeway, back road|名词

hornpike:/ˈhɔːrnpaɪk/| n.|收取过路费的栅栏|A barrier placed across a road to stop traffic until a toll is paid.|The car stopped at the hornpike until the driver paid the fee. 车在收费站停下，直到司机付了钱。|例句：The hornpike was lifted after the toll was paid. 付费后，收费站的栅栏升起了。|近义词：tollgate, tollbooth|反义词: None|名词
