maximally:/ˈmæksɪməli/| adv.|最大限度地，最大程度地|To the greatest possible extent; to the maximum degree.|She wanted to utilize her skills maximally. 她想最大限度地利用她的技能。|The engine is designed to perform maximally under pressure.  这台发动机设计成在压力下发挥最佳性能。|近义词：optimally, fully, completely, totally, utterly|反义词：minimally, slightly, partly|词性:副词
词源: From Late Latin *maximalis, from Latin maximus (“greatest”).
