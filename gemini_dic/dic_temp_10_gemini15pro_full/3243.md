affiliable:/əˈfɪliəbəl/
adjective
可加入的，可附属的
capable of being affiliated; suitable for affiliation.
This club is affiliable to a national organization.  这个俱乐部可以加入一个全国性的组织。
Local businesses are affiliable with the chamber of commerce.  当地企业可以加入商会。
近义词:associable, attachable, joinable
反义词:independent, unaffiliable
词性:adjective

词源:
From affiliate + -able.  来自 affiliate (加入，附属) + -able (可...的)  Ultimately from Latin affiliare, from ad- + filius ‘son’. 最终来自拉丁语 affiliare，由 ad- + filius “儿子”组成。
