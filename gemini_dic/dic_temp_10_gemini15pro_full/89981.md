horsewomen:/ˈhɔːrsˌwɪmɪn/
n.
女骑手；骑马的女人
A woman who rides horses.
The horsewomen gathered for the annual fox hunt. 女骑手们聚集在一起参加一年一度的猎狐活动。

The skilled horsewomen navigated the challenging terrain with ease.  熟练的女骑手们轻松地驾驭了充满挑战的地形。

近义词：rider, equestrian
反义词：(无直接反义词，可以考虑 non-rider, pedestrian)
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From horse + woman + -en (plural suffix).  "Horse" comes from Old English "hors." "Woman" comes from Old English "wīfman" (wife + man). The plural suffix "-en" is an older form, now largely replaced by "-s," but retained in a few words like "oxen" and "children."
