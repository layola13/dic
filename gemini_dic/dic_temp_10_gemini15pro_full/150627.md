papilloma:/ˌpæpɪˈloʊmə/ (US), /ˌpæpɪˈləʊmə/ (UK)| n. |乳头状瘤|A small, benign tumor of the skin or mucous membrane, typically having a rough or cauliflower-like surface.  It is caused by a virus.|She had a papilloma removed from her finger.|Synonyms: wart, growth, lesion|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

papillomas:/ˌpæpɪˈloʊməz/ (US), /ˌpæpɪˈləʊməz/ (UK)| n. |乳头状瘤（复数）|Plural form of papilloma.|He had multiple papillomas on his back.|Synonyms: warts, growths, lesions|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
