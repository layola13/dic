albinisms:/ˈælbɪnɪzəmz/
n.
白化病（复数）
A group of inherited disorders characterized by little or no melanin production.
Albinisms affect people of all races. 白化病影响所有种族的人。
Several types of albinisms exist. 存在几种类型的白化病。
Synonyms: achromia, achromasia
Antonyms: pigmentation
Noun
