imbizo:/ɪmˈbiːzoʊ/
noun
集会，大会，部落会议
A Zulu word for a gathering or meeting, especially a traditional assembly called by a chief or leader.
The chief called for an imbizo to discuss the drought.  酋长召集了一次部落会议来讨论旱灾。
The annual imbizo is an important cultural event.  一年一度的部落会议是一项重要的文化活动。
