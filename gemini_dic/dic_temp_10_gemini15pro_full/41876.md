cosentient:/ˌkoʊˈsɛnʃɪənt/ | adj. | 有共同感觉的；同感的；共同感知的 | Sharing the same feelings or awareness; perceiving or feeling together. |  例句：The cosentient beings communicated through telepathy.  这些有共同感觉的生物通过心灵感应进行交流。

例句：They developed a cosentient understanding of the universe. 他们对宇宙产生了共同的理解。 | 近义词：sympathetic, empathetic, like-minded | 反义词：apathetic, indifferent, unsympathetic | 词性：形容词

词源: From Latin *consentiens*, present participle of *consentire* ("to feel together"), from *com-* ("together") + *sentire* ("to feel").
