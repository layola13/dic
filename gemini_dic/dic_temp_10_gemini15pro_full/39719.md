consignments:/kənˈsaɪnmənts/
n.
货物;托运的物品
a batch of goods destined for or delivered to someone
例句：The company received two large consignments of computer equipment. 公司收到了两大批电脑设备。

例句：The latest consignment of clothes has arrived. 最新一批服装已到货。

近义词：shipments, deliveries, batches
反义词：N/A
词性：名词
