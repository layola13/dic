abdominoscopy: /æbˌdɒmɪˈnɒskəpi/ /æbˌdɑːmɪˈnɑːskəpi/
noun
腹腔镜检查
Examination of the abdominal cavity by means of an endoscope.
The abdominoscopy revealed no abnormalities. 腹腔镜检查未发现异常。
The patient underwent an abdominoscopy to investigate the cause of the pain. 病人接受了腹腔镜检查以查明疼痛的原因。

synonyms: laparoscopy, celioscopy
antonyms: N/A
词性: n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin abdomen ("belly") + -scopy (from Greek skopein, "to view").
