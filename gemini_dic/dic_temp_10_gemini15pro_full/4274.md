airtightness:/ˈɛərtɑɪtnəs/
n.
气密性，密封性
The quality of being airtight; impenetrability to air or gas.
The airtightness of the container ensured the food stayed fresh.  这个容器的气密性确保了食物保持新鲜。
近义词:impermeability, seal
反义词:leakiness, porosity
词性:名词

Etymology:
From air + tight + -ness.
