coadjutorship:/ˌkoʊəˈdʒuːtərʃɪp/
n.
助手职位；副主教职位
The position or office of a coadjutor.  A coadjutor is an assistant, especially to a bishop.
He was appointed to the coadjutorship of the diocese. 他被任命为教区副主教。
近义词:assistantship, auxiliary position
反义词:leadership, head position
词性:noun

coadjutrix:/ˌkoʊəˈdʒuːtrɪks/
n.
女性助手；女性副主教
A female coadjutor.  Specifically, a female assistant, especially to a bishop.
She served as coadjutrix for many years before becoming bishop herself. 她担任了多年的女性副主教，之后自己成为了主教。
近义词:female assistant, female auxiliary
反义词:female leader, female head
词性:noun
