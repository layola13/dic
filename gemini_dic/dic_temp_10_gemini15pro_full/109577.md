let-up:/ˈlɛtˌʌp/| n.| 暂停；减弱；缓和|a pause or reduction in something, typically something unpleasant|The rain continued all day without let-up. 雨一直下了一整天，没有停歇。|synonyms: respite, break, lull, pause, relief|antonyms: continuation, increase, intensification|noun

let-up:/ˈlɛtˌʌp/| n.| 松懈；放松|a relaxation of effort or severity|There was no let-up in the pressure he was under. 他承受的压力丝毫没有减轻。|synonyms: relaxation, slackening, easing|antonyms: tightening, strengthening|noun
