intelligenced:/ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒənst/ | adj. |  赋予智能的，有智能的 | Provided with intelligence, especially artificial intelligence. |  例句：The intelligenced robot could perform complex tasks.  这个智能机器人可以执行复杂的任务。 | 近义词：smart, intelligent, AI-powered | 反义词：unintelligent, dumb, non-intelligenced | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From "intelligence" (noun) + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives). "Intelligence" comes from Old French "intelligence"  and ultimately from Latin "intelligentia,"  from "intelligere" ("to understand"), formed from "inter-" ("between") + "legere" ("to choose, gather, read").
