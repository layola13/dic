flunk-out:/ˈflʌŋkˈaʊt/
verb|退学，因成绩不及格而被学校开除|To fail a course or exam and be dismissed from school or a program. |He flunked out of college after his first semester. 他第一个学期后就因成绩不及格被大学开除。|fail, drop out|graduate, pass|v.

flunk-out:/ˈflʌŋkˈaʊt/
noun|退学生，因成绩不及格而被学校开除的学生|A student who has been dismissed from school or a program due to failing grades. |The flunk-outs were often seen hanging around the local pool hall. 那些退学生经常被看到在当地的台球馆闲逛。|dropout, failure|graduate, success|n.
