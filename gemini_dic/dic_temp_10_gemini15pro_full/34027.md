citrate:/ˈsɪtreɪt/ | n. | 柠檬酸盐；柠檬酸酯 | A salt or ester of citric acid. |  Sodium citrate is commonly used as a food additive. 柠檬酸钠通常用作食品添加剂。

Citrates are important intermediates in the Krebs cycle. 柠檬酸盐是克雷布斯循环中的重要中间体。| 近义词: citric acid salt | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From citric acid + -ate. Citric acid from Latin citrus "citrus tree."
