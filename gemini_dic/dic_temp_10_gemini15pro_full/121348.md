misgoverning:/ˌmɪsˈɡʌvərnɪŋ/
verb
管理不善；治理不当
ruling badly or unjustly.
The country was plagued by years of misgoverning. 这个国家多年来一直受到管理不善的困扰。
Misgoverning led to widespread corruption. 管理不善导致腐败猖獗。
Near Synonyms: mismanaging, misruling, abusing power
Antonyms: governing well, ruling justly, administering effectively
