coombe:/kuːm/| n. |小谷，狭窄的山谷；|A deep narrow valley on the side of a hill or down. |例句：The houses nestled in the coombe were sheltered from the wind.  房屋依 nestled 在山谷里，避开了风。

例句：The stream flowed down the coombe to the river.  溪流沿着山谷流向河流。|近义词：valley, dell, ravine, gorge |反义词:peak, summit, ridge|名词
