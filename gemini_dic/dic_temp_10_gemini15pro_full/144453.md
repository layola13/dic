outswirling: /ˌaʊtˈswɜːrlɪŋ/
adjective
向外旋转的，螺旋状向外运动的
moving in a spiral outwards
例句：
The outswirling smoke from the chimney created a mesmerizing pattern against the twilight sky.
从烟囱里旋转向外升起的烟雾在暮色天空下构成了一幅迷人的图案。

The outswirling leaves danced in the autumn wind.
旋转飞舞的落叶在秋风中飘荡。

近义词：spiraling outwards, whirling outwards, swirling outwards
反义词：inswirling, inward-spiraling
词性：adjective
