lignified:/ˈlɪɡnɪfaɪd/
adjective
木质化的，木化的
Turned into wood; made woody.
The lignified cell walls provide structural support to the plant.  木质化的细胞壁为植物提供结构支撑。
The tree trunk is composed of lignified tissue. 树干由木质化组织组成。
近义词:woody, ligneous
反义词:non-lignified, herbaceous
词性:adjective

词源:
From Latin lignum (“wood”) + -fy + -ed.
