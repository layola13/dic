lavine:/ləˈviːn/ /ˈlæviːn/ | n. |雪崩，山崩|A large mass of snow, ice, and rocks falling rapidly down a mountainside.|例句：The skiers were caught in a sudden lavine. 滑雪者突然遭遇了雪崩。

例句：The lavine buried the entire village. 雪崩掩埋了整个村庄。|近义词：avalanche, snowslide, landslide|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用表示稳定、静止的词) stability, stillness|词性:名词
