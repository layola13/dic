homotropous:/hɒˈmɒtrəpəs/ /hoʊˈmɒtrəpəs/
adjective
直生的，顺生的 (植物学)
Having the same basic structure or form; (of a plant ovule) inverted so that the micropyle is close to the funiculus (the stalk that attaches the ovule to the placenta).
The homotropous arrangement of ovules is considered primitive in flowering plants.  直生胚珠的排列被认为是开花植物的原始特征。
The lily exhibits homotropous ovule development. 百合展现了直生胚珠的发育。
近义词: atropous (although technically the opposite, it refers to a different ovule orientation), orthotropous
反义词: anatropous, campylotropous
词性: adj.
