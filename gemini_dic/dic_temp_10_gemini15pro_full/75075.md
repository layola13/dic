frigidly:/ˈfrɪdʒɪdli/| adv.|冷淡地，冷漠地|In a frigid manner; coldly.  Showing or expressing coldness of feeling or manner.|She frigidly refused his offer of help. 她冷淡地拒绝了他的帮助。|Synonyms: coldly, icily, aloofly, distantly|Antonyms: warmly, kindly, affectionately|Adverb

frigid:/ˈfrɪdʒɪd/| adj.|寒冷的，冷淡的|Very cold in temperature; showing or expressing coldness of feeling or manner.|The air was frigid. 空气很寒冷。|Synonyms: cold, icy, freezing|Antonyms: warm, hot, passionate|Adjective
