fryers:/ˈfraɪərz/| n.|炸锅；炸鸡店；油炸食品；供油炸的小鸡(或鱼等)|A kitchen appliance used for deep frying food. Also refers to young chickens suitable for frying, or a place that sells fried food.|例句：She bought a new set of fryers for her restaurant. 她为她的餐厅买了一套新的炸锅。|例句：The fryers were busy all day at the county fair. 县集市上的炸鸡店整天都很忙。|近义词：deep fryer, frying pan, skillet|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 oven 烤箱)|词性:noun


fryer:/ˈfraɪər/ |n.|小鸡；鱼苗；较小的东西（尤指供煎炸的）；油炸食品店|A young chicken suitable for frying; a small fish; something small, especially suitable for frying. Can also refer to a place that sells fried food. | 例句：The chef prepared the fryers for the evening meal.  厨师准备了小鸡作为晚餐。|例句：These fryers are perfect for a quick and easy meal. 这些小鱼非常适合做一顿快捷方便的餐食。|近义词：chick, fingerling (for fish)|反义词: (没有直接的反义词)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *friere*, from Old French *freire*, from Late Latin *frīgere* "to fry," from Latin *frīgēre* "to roast, parch, fry."
