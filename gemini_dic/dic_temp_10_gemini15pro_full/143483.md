outgazed:/ˌaʊtˈɡeɪzd/
verb
以眼神胜过，凝视得比…久
To look at someone steadily for a longer time than they can look at you.
He outgazed his opponent in a silent battle of wills. 他在无声的意志较量中以眼神胜过对手。
Synonyms: outstare, outface
Antonyms: avert one's gaze, look away
词性: 动词
