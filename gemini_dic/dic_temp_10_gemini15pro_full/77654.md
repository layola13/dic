geldable:/ˈɡɛldəbəl/
adjective
可征税的; 应纳税的
Subject to taxation; taxable.
The king declared all land geldable. 国王宣布所有土地都要纳税。
Synonyms: taxable, assessable, dutiable
Antonyms: non-taxable, exempt
