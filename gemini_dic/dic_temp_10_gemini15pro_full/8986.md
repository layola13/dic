antiliberalistic:/ˌæntiˌlɪbərəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
反自由主义的
Opposed to the principles of liberalism, which emphasizes individual rights, freedoms, and limited government intervention.
His antiliberalistic views often clashed with the progressive ideals of his colleagues. 他反自由主义的观点经常与他同事的进步理念发生冲突。
anti-liberal, illiberal, reactionary, conservative
liberal, progressive, radical
