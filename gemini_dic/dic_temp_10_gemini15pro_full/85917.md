headlands:/ˈhedləndz/
n.
海岬；岬角
A narrow piece of land that projects from a coastline into the sea.
The ship rounded the rocky headlands. 这艘船绕过了岩石岬角。

近义词:cape, promontory, point
反义词:bay, cove, gulf
词性:noun
