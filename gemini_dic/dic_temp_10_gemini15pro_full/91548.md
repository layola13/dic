hyper-excited:/ˌhaɪpərɪkˈsaɪtɪd/
adjective
极度兴奋的，过度兴奋的
Extremely excited; excessively enthusiastic.
The children were hyper-excited about going to the amusement park. 孩子们对去游乐园感到非常兴奋。
She was hyper-excited to finally meet her favorite singer. 她见到自己最喜欢的歌手后欣喜若狂。
近义词:overexcited, thrilled, exhilarated
反义词:calm, relaxed, apathetic
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From hyper- + excited, from excite + -ed. Hyper- is a prefix meaning "over, above, beyond," from Greek hyper "over, beyond."  Excite comes from Latin excitare "to rouse, stir up," from ex- "out" + citare "to summon, rouse."
