emptiness:/ˈɛmptinəs/| n.|空虚，空旷，空洞|a feeling of great sadness and loneliness|例句：The emptiness of the house was unsettling. 房子的空旷令人不安。|例句：She felt an emptiness in her life after her children left home. 孩子们离家后，她感到生活空虚。|近义词：void, hollowness, vacuum, vacancy|反义词: fullness, abundance, plenitude|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *emptines*, from Old English *æmtignes*, from *æmtig* (“empty”) + *-nes* (suffix forming abstract nouns). Equivalent to empty +‎ -ness.
