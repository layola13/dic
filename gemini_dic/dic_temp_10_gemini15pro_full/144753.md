over-claim:/ˈoʊvərkleɪm/| v. |过分要求，夸大索赔|To demand or assert more than is justified or due, especially in terms of compensation or credit.|He over-claimed expenses on his business trip. 他在出差时虚报了费用。|近义词：exaggerate, inflate, overstate|反义词: understate, downplay|词性:动词

over-claim:/ˈoʊvərkleɪm/| n. |过分要求，夸大索赔|An excessive or inflated claim.|The insurance company rejected his over-claim. 保险公司拒绝了他的过高索赔。|近义词：exaggeration, inflation, overstatement|反义词: understatement|词性:名词


词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much") + "claim" (verb meaning "to demand or assert as a right")
