packings:/ˈpækɪŋz/
n.
包装材料; 包装物; 填料
Materials used to protect goods during transport, or the process of packing these materials.
The company uses biodegradable packings for all its products.  这家公司所有产品都使用可生物降解的包装材料。
We need to buy some packings for the move. 我们需要为搬家买些包装材料。
packaging, wrapping, stuffing, filling
unpackings
