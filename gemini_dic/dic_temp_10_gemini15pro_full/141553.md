opposeless:/ˌɒpəˈzɛslɪs/
adj.
无敌的，不可阻挡的
without opposition; unable to be opposed
The opposeless army swept through the country, conquering all in its path.  这支不可阻挡的军队席卷全国，征服了所有阻碍。
The new technology seemed opposeless in the market. 这项新技术在市场上似乎是无敌的。
近义词:invincible, unstoppable, irresistible, unchallengeable
反义词:vulnerable, weak, defenseless, resistible
词性:形容词


词源:
"opposeless" comes from "oppose" + "-less."  "Oppose" comes from Old French "opposer," from Latin "opponere" (to place against).  "-less" is a suffix meaning "without."
