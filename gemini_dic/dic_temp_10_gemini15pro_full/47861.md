deceivable:/dɪˈsiːvəbl/
adjective
可欺骗的；易受骗的
Capable of being deceived or misled.
That  convincing counterfeit money is highly deceivable. 那张以假乱真的假币很容易骗人。
The advertisement uses deceivable tactics to lure customers.  这则广告使用了欺骗性的策略来吸引顾客。

近义词:gullible, misleading, deceptive
反义词:honest, genuine, undeceptive
词性:adjective


词源:
From Late Middle English *deceivable, from Old French decevable, from decever (“to deceive”) + -able.
