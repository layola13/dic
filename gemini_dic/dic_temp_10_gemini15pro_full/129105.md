nitwittedness:/ˌnɪtˈwɪtɪdnəs/| n. |愚蠢，傻气|The state of being a nitwit; stupidity.|His nitwittedness led him to believe the obvious scam. 他的愚蠢让他相信了这个明显的骗局。|Synonyms: stupidity, foolishness, idiocy, silliness, absurdity|Antonyms: intelligence, wisdom, cleverness, brilliance, astuteness|noun


Etymology:
From nitwit + -ed + -ness.  Nitwit itself is likely a humorous formation based on nit, meaning "a louse egg," suggesting something small and insignificant, and wit, meaning "intelligence."  Thus, a nitwit is someone with very little wit.
