flail:/fleɪl/
verb
挥动；乱打；挣扎
to wave or swing something wildly
The drowning man flailed his arms in the water. 溺水的人在水中挥舞着手臂。

近义词: thrash, flounder, struggle
反义词:  control, be still


flail:/fleɪl/
noun
连枷
a tool for threshing grain, consisting of a long handle with a free-swinging stick at the end
Farmers used flails to separate the grain from the stalks. 农民用连枷将谷粒从茎秆上分离出来。

近义词: thresher
反义词:  N/A
