elucidator:/ɪˈluːsɪdeɪtər/
n.
阐明者；解释者
Someone or something that elucidates or makes something clear.
The professor, a renowned elucidator of complex scientific concepts, made the topic easily understandable for the students.  这位教授是一位著名的复杂科学概念阐明者，他使学生们很容易理解这个主题。
The commentary provided by the editor served as an elucidator for the difficult passages in the novel.  编辑提供的评论对小说中难懂的段落起到了阐释作用。
synonyms: explainer, interpreter, clarifier, illuminator
antonyms: obfuscator, obscurer, confuser
noun
