coleopterous:/ˌkɒliˈɒptərəs/| adj.|鞘翅目的；甲虫类的|Relating to or belonging to the order Coleoptera, which includes beetles. |Coleopterous insects have hard, shell-like front wings. 鞘翅目昆虫有坚硬的、壳状的前翅。|Synonyms: beetle-like, sheathed-winged|Antonyms: None|Adjective
