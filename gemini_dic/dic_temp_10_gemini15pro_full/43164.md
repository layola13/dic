cpg:/siːpiːdʒiː/ | n. | 消费者包装商品; 快速消费品 | Consumer Packaged Goods, products that are sold quickly and at a relatively low cost. These goods are frequently purchased and consumed.  | Example:  CPG companies often use targeted advertising to reach specific consumer demographics. 消费者包装商品公司经常使用定向广告来覆盖特定的消费者群体。

Example: The cpg industry is highly competitive. 快速消费品行业竞争非常激烈。 | 近义词: FMCG (Fast Moving Consumer Goods) | 反义词: durable goods | 词性: noun
