administrants:/ədˈmɪnɪstrənts/| n. |管理者，行政人员|People who administer something, typically a business, organization, or system.  Those responsible for managing and overseeing operations.|The administrants of the program ensured its smooth execution. 该项目的管理人员确保其顺利执行。
The hospital administrants made a difficult decision regarding budget cuts. 医院的管理人员就预算削减做出了一个艰难的决定。|近义词：administrators, managers, directors, executives, officials, supervisors|反义词: subordinates, employees, staff|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *administrant*, from Latin *administrāns*, present participle of *administrō* (“I manage, direct, govern”), from *ad* (“to, towards”) + *ministrō* (“I serve, attend, furnish”).
