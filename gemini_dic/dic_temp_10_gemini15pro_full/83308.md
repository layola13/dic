gynephobia:/ˌɡaɪnəˈfoʊbiə/ | n. | 女性恐惧症 | An abnormal or irrational fear of women. |  He suffers from gynephobia and avoids interacting with women as much as possible. 他患有女性恐惧症，尽量避免与女性互动。

| Example: His gynephobia made it difficult for him to work in a predominantly female office. 他的女性恐惧症让他难以在一个女性为主的办公室工作。

| Synonym: fear of women, feminophobia | Antonym: philogyny (对女性的喜爱) | Noun
