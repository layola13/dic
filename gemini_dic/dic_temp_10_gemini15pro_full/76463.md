gaited:/ˈɡeɪtɪd/ | adj. |步态优美的，有特定步态的 |Having or marked by a particular gait or way of walking or moving. |The horse was a beautiful, high-stepping, gaited animal.  这匹马是一匹漂亮的、步态优美的、步伐高的动物。|例句：The trainer specialized in gaited horses.  这位训练员专攻步态优美的马匹。|近义词：graceful, rhythmic, coordinated |反义词: clumsy, awkward |词性:形容词
词源: From *gait* + *-ed*.  来自 *gait*（步态）+ *-ed*（形容词后缀）。
