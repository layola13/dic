cessation:/seˈseɪʃən/| n.|停止，终止|The cessation of hostilities brought much-needed relief to the war-torn region. 停火给饱受战争蹂躏的地区带来了急需的救济。|例句：The doctor recommended the complete cessation of smoking. 医生建议彻底戒烟。

例句：The factory announced the cessation of production due to lack of demand.  工厂因需求不足宣布停产。

近义词：stopping, halting, ending, discontinuation|反义词: commencement, beginning, start, continuation|词性: noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *cessatio, from Latin cessare "to cease," frequentative of cedere "to go, withdraw, yield."
