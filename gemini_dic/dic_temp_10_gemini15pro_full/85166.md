hardworking: /ˌhɑːrdˈwɜːrkɪŋ/ | adj. | 勤奋的，努力工作的 |  Someone who is hardworking puts a lot of effort into their work. |  She is a hardworking student and always gets good grades. 她是一个勤奋的学生，总是取得好成绩。| diligent, industrious, assiduous | lazy, indolent, slothful | adjective

Etymology: "hard" (Old English heard "firm, solid, severe") + "working" (present participle of "work" - Old English weorc "work, action, deed")
