huronian:/hʊˈroʊniən/ /hjʊˈrəʊniən/
adjective|休伦纪的，休伦系的|Relating to or denoting the Huronian geological period or system, typically dated to between 2.5 and 2.2 billion years ago.|例句：Huronian rocks are some of the oldest on Earth. 休伦纪的岩石是地球上最古老的岩石之一。|近义词：Precambrian, Proterozoic|反义词：modern, recent|词性:adjective
noun|休伦纪，休伦系|The Huronian geological period or system, typically dated to between 2.5 and 2.2 billion years ago.|例句：The Huronian saw the development of early life forms. 休伦纪见证了早期生命形式的发展。|近义词：Precambrian, Proterozoic|反义词：modern era, recent era|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Lake Huron, where rocks of this age are found, + -ian.
