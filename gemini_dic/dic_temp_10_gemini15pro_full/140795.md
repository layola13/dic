on all hands:/ɒn ɔːl hændz/
adverb|在各方面，到处，全体一致地|On all hands it was agreed that the plan was a failure. 各方面一致认为这项计划失败了。|例句：The new policy was condemned on all hands. 这项新政策遭到了各方的谴责。

例句：The project was praised on all hands.  这个项目受到了各方的赞扬。


近义词：everywhere, unanimously, universally
反义词：nowhere, partially
词性：adverb
