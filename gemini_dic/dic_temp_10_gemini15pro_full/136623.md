nonsaturation:/ˌnɒnˌsætʃəˈreɪʃn/ |n.|不饱和; 非饱和性|The nonsaturation of the fatty acids is important for their health benefits. 脂肪酸的不饱和性对其健康益处很重要。|The degree of nonsaturation in a molecule can influence its reactivity. 分子中的不饱和度会影响其反应性。|近义词: unsaturation|反义词: saturation|词性: 名词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + saturation (from Latin saturare, "to fill")
