evolve:/ɪˈvɒlv/ (British) /iˈvɑːlv/ (American)| v. |进化，发展，演变|To develop gradually, especially from a simple to a more complex form.|Over millions of years, humans evolved from apes. 数百万年来，人类是从猿类进化而来的。

The company has evolved from a small startup to a multinational corporation.  这家公司已经从一家小型初创企业发展成为一家跨国公司。|近义词：develop, progress, advance, mature|反义词：devolve, regress, degenerate|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *evolvere* "to unroll, unfold, open," from *ex-* "out" + *volvere* "to roll."
