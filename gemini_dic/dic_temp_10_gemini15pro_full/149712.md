palaeographist: /ˌpeɪliˈɒɡrəfɪst/ /ˌpæliˈɑːɡrəfɪst/
n.
古文字学家；古文书学家
A scholar who studies ancient writing systems and manuscripts.
The palaeographist dated the manuscript to the 12th century.  这位古文字学家将这份手稿的年代确定为12世纪。
The palaeographist deciphered the ancient inscription.  这位古文书学家破译了古代铭文。
Synonym: paleographer
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps one could consider a "forger" of ancient texts)
词性: noun
