centipede:/ˈsɛntɪpiːd/ | n. | 蜈蚣 | A long, flattened, many-segmented arthropod with one pair of legs per body segment.  |  例句：I saw a centipede crawling across the bathroom floor. 我看到一只蜈蚣爬过浴室地板。

例句：Centipedes are predatory arthropods. 蜈蚣是掠食性节肢动物。| 近义词：myriapod | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
