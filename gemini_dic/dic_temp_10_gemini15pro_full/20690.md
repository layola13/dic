blade:/bleɪd/| n.|刀片，叶片，桨叶|A thin, flat cutting edge of a knife, sword, or other tool; the flat, wide part of a leaf or the leaf of a grass; a rotating part in a machine, such as a propeller or turbine.|The chef sharpened the blade of his knife. 厨师磨快了他的刀刃。|近义词：edge, cutter, knife, sword, vane|反义词:bluntness|noun
