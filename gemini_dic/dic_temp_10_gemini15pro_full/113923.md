maestoso:/maɪˈstosoʊ/ /mɑːeˈstəʊsoʊ/| adj. |庄严的，雄伟的|Marked by stateliness and dignity; majestic.  It is often used as a musical direction, indicating a stately and dignified pace.|The symphony's opening movement was marked maestoso, creating a sense of grandeur. 交响曲的开场乐章被标记为“庄严的”，营造出一种宏伟的感觉。|Synonyms: majestic, stately, grand, noble, dignified|Antonyms: humble, simple, unpretentious, modest|Adjective
