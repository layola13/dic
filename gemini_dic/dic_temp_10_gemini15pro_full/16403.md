barck:/bɑːrk/| n. | 船 | The hull of a ship, especially a small sailing vessel.  A barque, or bark, is a sailing vessel with three or more masts, having the foremast and mainmast square-rigged and only the mizzenmast fore-and-aft rigged. |The barck sailed smoothly across the calm waters. 这艘船平稳地航行在平静的水面上。|近义词：vessel, ship, boat, hull|反义词: N/A|词性: 名词
barck: /bɑːrk/ |v. |剥树皮;  大声喊叫,咆哮|To strip the bark from (a tree). To speak sharply or yell loudly.| He barcked his shins climbing over the fence. 他爬过篱笆时擦破了胫骨。 The sergeant barcked orders at the recruits. 中士对着新兵大声吼叫着命令。|近义词：(v. to strip bark) peel, skin; (v. to yell) shout, yell, roar|反义词: whisper|词性: 动词
