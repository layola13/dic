loopier:/ˈluːpiər/| adj.|更疯狂的，更古怪的|Comparative form of loopy: more loopy.  Meaning even more crazy, eccentric, or silly.|He's gotten even loopier since he quit his job. 他自从辞职后变得更古怪了。|Synonyms: crazier, more eccentric, zanier, more outlandish|Antonyms: saner, more rational, more sensible|adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From loopy + -er (comparative suffix).  Loopy itself likely originates from "loop," referring to something that goes in circles, hence suggesting disorientation or craziness.
