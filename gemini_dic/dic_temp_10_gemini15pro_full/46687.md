damps:/dæmps/ | n. | 瘴气，湿气，沮丧|depressing or discouraging influence; an atmosphere of gloom and depression; (usually used in the plural) noxious vapors or gases, formerly believed to arise from swamps and cause disease | The damps of the prison had a terrible effect on his spirits. 监狱里的潮湿空气对他的精神造成了可怕的影响。| 近义词:gloom, dejection, miasma | 反义词:cheer, elation |词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *damp, from Old English *damp, from Proto-Germanic *dampaz, of uncertain origin. Related to Middle Low German damp "vapor, steam."
