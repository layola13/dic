lenu: /ˈleɪnuː/
noun
levee, embankment
A raised structure, usually made of earth, built to prevent a river from overflowing.
The levee protected the town from the rising floodwaters.  堤坝保护着小镇免受上涨的洪水侵袭。
synonyms: dike, embankment, barrier
antonyms: ditch, trench
n.
