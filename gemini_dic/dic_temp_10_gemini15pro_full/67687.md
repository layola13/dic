exuccous:/ɛkˈsʌkəs/
adjective
无汁的; 干燥的
Having lost moisture; dry.  Derived from Latin *exsuccus, from ex- 'out' + succus 'juice'.
The exuccous leaves crumbled in my hand.  干枯的叶子在我手中碎裂了。
近义词: dry, arid, desiccated, parched
反义词: succulent, moist, juicy
词性: adjective
