demisis:/dɪˈmaɪsɪs/
n.
死亡，逝世
death, decease
例句：The demise of the company was a shock to everyone.  公司的倒闭让每个人都感到震惊。

例句：His sudden demise left his family heartbroken. 他的突然离世让他的家人悲痛欲绝。

近义词：death, decease, passing
反义词：birth, beginning


词源：From Old French *demisse*, from Late Latin *demissio* "lowering, sinking," from Latin *demittere* "to send down," from *de-* "down" + *mittere* "to send."
