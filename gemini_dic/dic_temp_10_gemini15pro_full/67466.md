extolment:/ɪkˈstəʊlmənt/ /ɪkˈstoʊlmənt/| n.|赞扬，颂扬|The poet received much extolment for his latest work. 这位诗人最新的作品受到了很多赞扬。|例句：The king's extolment of the hero's bravery was heard throughout the land. 国王对英雄勇敢的赞扬传遍了全国。

His extolment of her beauty was quite excessive. 他对她的美貌的赞美太过分了。|近义词：praise, acclaim, laudation, commendation|反义词：criticism, condemnation, censure|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French estolement, from estoler ‘exalt’, based on Latin extollere (see extol).
