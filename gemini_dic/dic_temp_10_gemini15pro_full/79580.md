gloomth:/ɡluːmθ/| n.|忧郁，阴郁，悲伤；哥特式暗黑风格|A feeling of sadness and darkness, often associated with the gothic subculture.  It blends "gloom" and "goth".|She dressed in gloomth, wearing black lace and dark makeup. 她穿着哥特式暗黑风格的衣服，化着深色的妆容。|近义词：melancholy, dejection, gloom, darkness|反义词：joy, happiness, cheerfulness|词性:名词

词源:  A blend of "gloom" and "goth".  "Gloom" comes from Old English *glōm meaning "twilight, dusk". "Goth" refers to the Gothic subculture.
