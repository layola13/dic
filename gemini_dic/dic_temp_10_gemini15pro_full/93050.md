idolastre:/aɪˈdɒləstreɪ/ /aɪˈdɑːləstreɪ/
noun
偶像崇拜；盲目崇拜
Excessive or blind adoration; worship of an idol.
He was accused of idolastre, worshipping false gods. 他被指控崇拜偶像，崇拜假神。
The fans' idolastre of the pop star was overwhelming.  粉丝对这位流行歌星的狂热崇拜令人难以置信。
near synonym: idolatry, worship, adoration
antonym: disdain, contempt, disregard
词性: 名词
