obituary: /əˈbɪtʃuˌeri/ (US), /əˈbɪtʃʊəri/ (UK)| n. |讣告，讣闻|A notice of a death, especially in a newspaper, typically including a brief biography of the deceased person.|例句：The newspaper published a lengthy obituary for the renowned scientist. 这家报纸刊登了这位著名科学家的长篇讣告。

例句：Her family placed an obituary in the local paper after her passing. 她去世后，家人在当地报纸上刊登了讣告。|近义词：death notice, necrology|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
