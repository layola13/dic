mistura:/mɪsˈtjʊərə/ /mɪsˈtʃʊrə/
noun
混合物，混合
a substance made by mixing other substances together.
The chemist carefully prepared the mistura.  化学家仔细地准备了混合物。
blend, mix, combination, composite, concoction
separation, division, parting

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *mistūra*, from *mixtūra*, from *miscere* "to mix".
