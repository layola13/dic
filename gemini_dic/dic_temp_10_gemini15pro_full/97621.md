inharmoniousnesses:/ˌɪnhɑːrˈmoʊniəsnəsəz/ | n. |不和谐，不协调|The state or quality of being inharmonious; lack of harmony; discord.  A collection of inharmonious elements or instances. |The inharmoniousnesses of the musical piece were jarring to the ear.  这首音乐作品的不和谐之处令人感到刺耳。|Synonyms:  discords, dissonances, clashes, conflicts, inconsistencies|Antonyms: harmonies, consonances, agreements, consistencies|noun
