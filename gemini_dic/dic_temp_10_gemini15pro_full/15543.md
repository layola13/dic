baggier:/ˈbæɡiər/ | adj. | 更宽松的，更袋状的 | More baggy; looser and hanging in folds. |  Her jeans were much baggier than mine. 她的牛仔裤比我的宽松得多。

| Example: He prefers baggier clothes for comfort. 他为了舒适喜欢穿更宽松的衣服。 | Synonyms: looser, roomier, slouchier | Antonyms: tighter, snugger, fitted | adjective


baggier:/ˈbæɡiər/ | adj. | (informal) 更糟糕的，更差的 | Worse; of poorer quality. | The traffic is even baggier today than it was yesterday.  今天的交通比昨天更糟糕。

| Example: The economic situation is getting baggier by the day. 经济形势日益恶化。 | Synonyms: worse, poorer, inferior | Antonyms: better, improved, superior | adjective



ETYMOLOGY->
From baggy + -er (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from bag.
