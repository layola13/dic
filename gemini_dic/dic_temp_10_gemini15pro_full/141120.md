ontologism:/ˌɒntəˈlɒdʒɪzəm/
n.
本体论主义
The philosophical doctrine that the human intellect has a direct or intuitive knowledge of God's existence and nature.
Ontologism was a controversial philosophical movement in the 19th century. 本体论主义是19世纪一个有争议的哲学运动。
The priest's arguments rested heavily on ontologism.  神父的论点很大程度上依赖于本体论主义。
近义词:idealism, transcendentalism
反义词:materialism, empiricism
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *ontologisme*, from *ontologie* "ontology" + *-isme* "-ism."  *Ontologie* from Ancient Greek ὄντος (óntos) "being, present participle of εἰμί (eimí) "I am," + -λογία (-logía) "-logy."
