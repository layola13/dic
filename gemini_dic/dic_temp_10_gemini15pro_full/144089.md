outsainted:/ˌaʊtˈseɪn.tɪd/
verb
超越圣人, 比圣人还圣洁, 过分虔诚
surpass in sanctity or saintliness
Mother Teresa outsainted all. 特蕾莎修女比所有圣人都圣洁。
近义词: outdo, surpass, excel
反义词: sin, corrupt
词性: verb
