overspread:/ˌoʊvərˈsprɛd/| v. |蔓延，覆盖，布满|To cover the surface of something completely.|例句：A blush overspread her cheeks. 一阵红晕在她脸颊上蔓延开来。|例句：The sky was overspread with clouds. 天空乌云密布。|近义词：cover, blanket, coat, envelop|反义词：expose, uncover|词性：动词

词源: From Old English *ofer-sprǣdan (unattested), from ofer- 'over' + sprǣdan 'to spread'.
