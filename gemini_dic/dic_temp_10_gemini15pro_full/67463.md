extollment:/ɪkˈstəʊlmənt/ /ɪkˈstoʊlmənt/ |n.|赞美；颂扬；称赞|The extollment of the hero's bravery was heard throughout the land. 英雄的勇敢事迹在全国各地传颂。|The poem was an extollment of nature's beauty. 这首诗是对自然之美的赞颂。| 近义词:praise, commendation, laudation, acclaim, eulogy|反义词:criticism, condemnation, censure, denunciation|词性:名词


Etymology:
From Late Middle English *extollen, from Anglo-Norman extoller, alteration of Old French estoler (“to raise, lift up, exalt”), from Latin extollō (“I raise, lift up, elevate, extol”), from ex (“out, up”) + tollō (“I raise, lift up”).
