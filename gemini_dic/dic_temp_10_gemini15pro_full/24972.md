superlative adjective of brusque: most brusque.  Marked by rude or peremptory shortness.  It is the most extreme form of brusque.

Synonyms: abruptest, curtest, rudest, bluntest, sharpest
Antonyms: kindest, gentlest, politest, most courteous, most considerate
