for-sure:/fɔːr ˈʃʊr/ /fər ˈʃʊr/ | adv. | 肯定地；无疑地；当然 |  Used to emphasize that something is definitely true or will definitely happen.  It's informal.  Originates from the phrase "for certain." |  "Are you going to the party?" "For sure!"  “你会去派对吗？” “当然！” | certainly, definitely, absolutely | perhaps, maybe | adverb
