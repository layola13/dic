incommodious:/ˌɪnkəˈməʊdiəs/| adj.|不方便的，不舒适的，狭小的|Not spacious; inconveniently small or cramped.  Lacking sufficient space or room; inconvenient. |例句：The incommodious apartment made it difficult to entertain guests.  这狭小的公寓使得招待客人很困难。

例句：The attic room was dark and incommodious. 阁楼房间又黑又窄。|近义词：cramped, confined, small, uncomfortable, inconvenient|反义词:spacious, roomy, comfortable, convenient|词性:adjective
