flavin:/ˈfleɪvɪn/ | n. | 黄素; 核黄素 | Any of a class of organic compounds that are based on the heterocyclic isoalloxazine ring system, usually yellow in colour and readily reduced to colourless leuco derivatives. Many flavins are coenzymes concerned in biological oxidation-reduction processes. | Riboflavin is a flavin. 核黄素是一种黄素。| synonyms: riboflavin, vitamin B2 | antonyms: None | noun


flavin:/ˈfleɪvɪn/ | adj. | 黄素的 | Relating to or containing flavin. | Flavin nucleotides are coenzymes involved in redox reactions. 黄素核苷酸是参与氧化还原反应的辅酶。| synonyms: riboflavin-related | antonyms: None | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *flāvus* ("yellow") + *-īn* (suffix forming abstract nouns).
