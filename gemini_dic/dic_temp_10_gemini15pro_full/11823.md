arrear:/əˈrɪr/ | n. | 拖欠，欠款；积压未办事项 | Money that is owed and should have been paid earlier.  A state of being behind in the fulfillment of obligations. | The rent is three months in arrears. 租金拖欠了三个月。 | synonyms: debt, default, overdue, outstanding, unpaid | antonyms: prepayment, advance, credit | noun

arrear:/əˈrɪr/ | adj. | 拖欠的 | Being in arrears; overdue. |  His payments are in arrear. 他的付款逾期了。| synonyms: overdue, outstanding, unpaid, delinquent | antonyms: current, paid-up, up-to-date | adjective
