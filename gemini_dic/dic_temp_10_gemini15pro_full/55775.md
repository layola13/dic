diversionary:/daɪˈvɜːrʃəneri/  /dɪˈvɜːrʒəneri/
adjective
转移注意力的；牵制的
intended to distract attention from something more important
例句：The government used diversionary tactics to draw attention away from the scandal. 政府使用了转移注意力的策略，以将注意力从丑闻中转移开。

例句：He made a diversionary move to distract the guard while his accomplice snatched the jewels. 他做了一个声东击西的动作来分散警卫的注意力，与此同时他的同伙抢走了珠宝。

近义词： distracting, deceptive, misleading, evasive
反义词：  straightforward, honest, forthright
词性：adjective
