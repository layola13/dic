actinia:/ækˈtɪniə/| n. |海葵|A sea anemone, a marine predatory animal of the order Actiniaria.|The actinia swayed gently in the ocean current. 海葵在洋流中轻轻摇摆。|近义词：sea anemone|反义词:|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *actinia*, from Ancient Greek ἀκτῖνος (aktînos, “ray”), related to ἀκτίς (aktís, “ray of light”).  The name refers to the radial symmetry of the animal's tentacles.
