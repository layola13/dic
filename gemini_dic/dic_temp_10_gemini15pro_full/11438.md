argot:/ˈɑːrɡoʊ/ (US), /ˈɑːɡɒt/ (UK)| n.|暗语，行话，黑话|The special vocabulary of a particular group, often used to exclude or mislead outsiders.  A secret language used by a particular group of people.|例句：Thieves' argot is incomprehensible to most people. 小偷的黑话大多数人听不懂。

例句：The computer hackers spoke in an argot that made no sense to me.  电脑黑客们用一种我听不懂的行话交谈。|近义词：jargon, slang, cant, vernacular|反义词:standard language, formal language|词性:noun
