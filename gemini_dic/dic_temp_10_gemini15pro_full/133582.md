nonflatulence:/ˌnɒnˈflætʃʊləns/ /ˌnɑːnˈflætʃələns/| n. |无胀气；不排气|The absence of flatulence; the state of not expelling intestinal gas.|After the surgery, the patient experienced a period of nonflatulence.|近义词: absence of flatus, lack of gas|反义词: flatulence, flatus|词性:名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non "not"
flatulence (noun): from Latin *flatus "a blowing, blast," from flare "to blow"
