damagers:/ˈdæmɪdʒərz/
n.
损害者，造成损害的人或物
Those responsible for causing harm or damage.
The damagers were held accountable for the destruction they caused.  损害者要为他们造成的破坏负责。
The insurance company is pursuing the damagers. 保险公司正在追查肇事者。
Synonyms: saboteurs, destroyers, vandals, perpetrators
Antonyms: protectors, guardians, repairers
