hydropneumatic:/ˌhaɪdrəʊnjuːˈmætɪk/ (US) /ˌhaɪdroʊnuːˈmætɪk/| adj.|液压气动的；液气联动的|Of or relating to a system that uses both liquid and gas (typically air) to generate force or pressure.  Operating by a combination of fluid and air pressure.|The Citroen car is famous for its hydropneumatic suspension.  雪铁龙汽车以其液压气动悬架而闻名。|hydraulic, pneumatic|N/A|adjective
Etymology->
From hydro- + pneumatic.  "Hydro-" comes from Greek "hydōr" (water), and "pneumatic" comes from Greek "pneuma" (breath, air).
