orangeman:/ˈɔːrɪndʒmən/ | n. |奥兰治人，橙衣人| A Protestant supporter of William of Orange, especially a member of an Orange Order lodge. Historically, a supporter of the House of Orange. |  Example 1: The Orangemen marched through the streets. 橙衣人游行穿过街道。

Example 2:  He was a proud Orangeman and never missed a parade. 他是一位自豪的橙衣人，从不错过任何一次游行。 | Synonym: Protestant, Unionist (in Northern Ireland) | Antonym: Catholic, Nationalist (in Northern Ireland) | noun
