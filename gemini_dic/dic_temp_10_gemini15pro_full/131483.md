noncoking:/ˌnɑːnˈkoʊkɪŋ/ | adj. | 非焦化的，非炼焦的 | Not producing coke when heated.  Relating to coal that does not agglomerate or fuse when heated. | This power plant uses noncoking coal.  这家发电厂使用非焦煤。|  Non-coking coal is used for steam raising and electricity generation. 非焦煤用于蒸汽生产和发电。| Near synonyms: non-caking, free-burning | Antonym: coking | Adjective
