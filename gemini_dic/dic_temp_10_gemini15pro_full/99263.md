intercondenser:/ˌɪntərˈkənˈdɛnsər/| n.|中间冷凝器，级间冷凝器|A condenser between stages, especially in a multi-stage steam turbine or refrigeration system, used to cool the working fluid between expansions or compressions to improve efficiency.|The intercondenser cools the partially compressed gas before it enters the next stage of compression, thereby reducing the work required.|近义词:intermediate condenser|反义词:None|词性:名词
