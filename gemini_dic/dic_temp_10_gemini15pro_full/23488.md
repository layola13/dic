brahman:/ˈbrɑːmən/ /ˈbrɑːhmən/| n.|婆罗门，宇宙本体，梵|A member of the highest Hindu caste, traditionally priests or scholars.  In Hinduism, Brahman is the ultimate reality, the impersonal, divine ground of all existence.|例句：The brahman chanted the ancient verses. 婆罗门吟诵着古老的经文。

例句：The concept of Brahman is central to Hindu philosophy. 梵的概念是印度教哲学的核心。|近义词：priest, sage, absolute, ultimate reality|反义词:outcast (社会地位)|词性:noun


brahman:/ˈbrɑːmən/ /ˈbrɑːhmən/| n.|婆罗门牛，瘤牛|A breed of cattle originating in India, known for its hump and resistance to heat.|例句：The brahman cattle grazed peacefully in the field. 婆罗门牛在田野里平静地吃草。

例句：Brahman bulls are often used in rodeos. 婆罗门公牛经常用于牛仔竞技表演。|近义词：zebu, sacred cow|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
