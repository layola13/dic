boson:/ˈboʊzɑːn/ (US), /ˈbəʊzɒn/ (UK)| n.|玻色子|A subatomic particle, such as a photon, that has zero or integral spin and follows the statistical description given by S. N. Bose and Einstein.|Photons and gluons are examples of bosons.|synonyms: elementary particle, subatomic particle|antonyms: fermion|noun
