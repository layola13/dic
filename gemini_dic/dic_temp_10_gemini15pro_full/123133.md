monist:/ˈmɒnɪst/ /ˈmɑːnɪst/| n.|一元论者；单一主义者|A person who believes in monism (the doctrine that only one supreme being exists).| Spinoza was a monist, believing that mind and matter were aspects of the same substance.|近义词: Unitarian|反义词: dualist, pluralist|词性:noun

Etymology: From Ancient Greek μόνος (mónos, “alone, only, single”) + -ist.
