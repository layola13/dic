melampode:/ˌmɛləmˈpəʊdiː/
n.
黑眼苏珊, 金光菊
A plant of the genus Melampodium, native to tropical America, typically having bright yellow daisy-like flowers.
Melampodes attract butterflies and other pollinators to the garden.  黑眼苏珊吸引蝴蝶和其他传粉者到花园里来。
Melampodes are a cheerful addition to any summer garden. 金光菊是任何夏季花园的愉快补充。
synonym:  black-eyed Susan, gold medallion flower
antonym:  N/A
