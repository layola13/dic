oversceptically:/ˌoʊvərˈskɛptɪkli/
adverb|过于怀疑地|in a way that shows too much skepticism; with excessive doubt.|He oversceptically dismissed the evidence, refusing to believe it could be true. 他过于怀疑地驳回了证据，拒绝相信它可能是真的。|Example: She oversceptically questioned every aspect of the proposal. 她对提案的每个方面都提出了过多的质疑。|Synonyms: incredulously, suspiciously, distrustfully|Antonyms: trustingly, credulously, naively|词性: 副词
