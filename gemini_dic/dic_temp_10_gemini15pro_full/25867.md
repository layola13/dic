Burkinabe: /ˌbɜːrkiˈnɑːbeɪ/ /ˌbɜːrkiˈnɑːbeɪ/ | n.|布基纳法索人|A person from Burkina Faso. |例句：Many Burkinabe work in agriculture. 许多布基纳法索人从事农业。|近义词：citizen of Burkina Faso |反义词:N/A
词性:noun


词源: From "Burkina" (meaning "upright people" in Moore) and "Faso" (meaning "fatherland" in Dioula), the two major languages of Burkina Faso. The term "Burkinabe" replaced the former demonym "Upper Voltan" after the country's name change in 1984.
