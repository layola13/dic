antiromanticism:/ˌæntiroʊˈmæntɪsɪzəm/
n.
反浪漫主义
A movement or attitude that rejects or opposes romanticism, especially in literature and the arts.
Antiromanticism emphasizes realism, objectivity, and reason over emotionalism, idealism, and subjectivity.
The rise of antiromanticism in the 20th century can be seen as a reaction against the excesses of romantic art.  20世纪反浪漫主义的兴起可以被看作是对浪漫主义艺术过度的一种反应。
His paintings reflect a strong sense of antiromanticism. 他的绘画反映了强烈的反浪漫主义意识。
近义词: realism, classicism
反义词: romanticism
词性: noun
