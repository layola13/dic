cochampion:/ˌkoʊˈtʃæmpiən/| n.|共同冠军|A person or team that shares a championship with another.|例句：The two teams were declared co-champions after the tied game.  两支队伍在比赛打平后被宣布为共同冠军。

例句：They finished the season as co-champions. 他们在赛季末成为共同冠军。|近义词：joint champion, co-winner|反义词:runner-up|词性:名词
