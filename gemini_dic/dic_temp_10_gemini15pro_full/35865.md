cocksure:/ˌkɒkˈʃʊə(r)/ /ˌkɑːkˈʃʊr/ | adjective | 自以为是的；过分自信的；狂妄自大的 | Too confident, especially in a way that is annoying. Characterized by excessive confidence, often combined with arrogance.  Derives from "cock" (male chicken, often used to suggest boldness) + "sure".  Implies an often misplaced certainty and can be seen as negative. | He strutted into the room, cocksure of his victory. 他大摇大摆地走进房间，对自己的胜利充满自信。 | confident, arrogant, smug, overconfident, brash | humble, modest, diffident, uncertain, hesitant | adjective
