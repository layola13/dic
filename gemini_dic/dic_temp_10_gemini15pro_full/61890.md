emptily:/ˈemptɪli/
adv.
空虚地，空洞地
in an empty way; without real meaning or sincerity
She smiled emptily at him. 她对他空洞地笑了笑。
近义词:vacantly, hollowly, blankly
反义词: meaningfully, sincerely, expressively
词性:副词
