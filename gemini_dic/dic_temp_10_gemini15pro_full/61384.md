emblazonment:/ɪmˈbleɪzənmənt/| n. |纹章装饰；铭刻；鲜明展示|The act of emblazoning something, typically with heraldic devices or other decorative elements. It can also refer to a striking or prominent display.|The knight's shield bore a proud emblazonment of his family crest. 骑士的盾牌上骄傲地装饰着他家族的纹章。

The emblazonment of the company logo on the building was very noticeable. 公司标志在大楼上的醒目展示非常引人注目。|近义词：decoration, adornment, ornamentation, display|反义词:concealment, hiding|词性:noun

emblazon:/ɪmˈbleɪzən/| v. |用纹章装饰；铭刻；鲜明地展示|To decorate or adorn something, typically with heraldic devices. It can also mean to display something prominently or vividly.|The banner was emblazoned with the school's motto.  横幅上铭刻着学校的校训。

His name was emblazoned across the headlines. 他的名字赫然印在报纸头条上。|近义词：decorate, adorn, ornament, display|反义词:conceal, hide|词性:verb
