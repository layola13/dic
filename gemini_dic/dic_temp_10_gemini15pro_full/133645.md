nonformations:/ˌnɒnfɔːrˈmeɪʃənz/ | n. | 非构词形式，未被认可的词 | Words that are not formed according to the usual rules of word formation in a language, or are not accepted as standard vocabulary. | Many neologisms initially start as nonformations, only gaining acceptance through widespread use. 许多新词最初都是非构词形式，只有通过广泛使用才能被接受。 |  Nonformations can sometimes be understood in context, but they are not considered dictionary-worthy. 非构词形式有时可以在上下文中理解，但它们不被认为值得收入词典。 |  neologisms, coinages | standard words, established vocabulary | noun
