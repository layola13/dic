airborne:/ˈɛərˌbɔːrn/ (US), /ˈeəˌbɔːn/ (UK)| adj.|空运的；空气传播的；在空中的|Carried by air; transported by aircraft. Also, carried by the wind, as pollen or dust; and, in flight, as a plane.|The paratroopers were airborne and on their way to the drop zone.  伞兵已升空，正前往空投区。|The airborne pollen caused her allergies to flare up. 空气中传播的花粉使她过敏发作。|Synonyms: aloft, flying, in flight|Antonyms: grounded, landed|adjective

airborne:/ˈɛərˌbɔːrn/ (US), /ˈeəˌbɔːn/ (UK)| n.|空降兵|Troops trained to be deployed by parachute.|The airborne were crucial to the success of the operation. 空降兵对行动的成功至关重要。|Synonyms: paratroops, paratroopers|Antonyms: None|noun
