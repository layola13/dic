overaccumulated: /ˌoʊvərəˈkjuːmjəleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过度积累的 | Having accumulated excessively; more than necessary or desirable. | The warehouse was overaccumulated with unsold merchandise. 仓库里积压了大量未售出的商品。| The company overaccumulated inventory during the pandemic.  这家公司在疫情期间过度积累了库存。 | 近义词: overstocked, surplus, excessive | 反义词: depleted, scarce, insufficient | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + accumulate + -ed.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much." "Accumulate" comes from the Latin "accumulare," meaning "to heap up."  "-ed" is a suffix forming the past participle of verbs, here creating an adjective.
