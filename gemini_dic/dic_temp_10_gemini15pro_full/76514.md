ETYMOLOGY->
From Galen, the name of a Greek physician and writer (Claudius Galenus, c. 130–c. 200 AD), whose theories dominated Western medicine for centuries.  The term "galenic" refers to the system of medicine he developed, which emphasized herbal remedies and the balance of the four humors (blood, phlegm, yellow bile, and black bile).
