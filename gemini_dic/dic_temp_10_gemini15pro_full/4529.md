ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish Albuquerque, from Arabic  البرقوقي (al-barqūqī, “plum tree”), from Latin prunus.  The Spanish duke of Alburquerque held extensive lands in Spain.  Spanish explorers later named the city in New Mexico after him.
