businesswomen:/ˈbɪznɪsˌwɪmɪn/| n. |女商人，商界女性|A woman or women engaged in commercial or industrial enterprises. |例句：The businesswomen met to discuss investment strategies. 女商人聚在一起讨论投资策略。

例句：Successful businesswomen often serve as role models for younger generations.  成功的商界女性经常成为年轻一代的榜样。|近义词：female entrepreneurs, business executives|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From business + women. "Business" comes from Old English  *bisignis*  "care, anxiety, occupation," from busy + -ness. "Women" comes from Old English *wīfmann*, from *wīf* "woman" + *mann* "human being."
