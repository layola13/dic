lumberingly:/ˈlʌmbərɪŋli/
| adv. |笨拙地，缓慢地|Moving in a slow, heavy, and awkward way.|The elephant lumberingly made its way through the dense jungle. 大象笨拙地穿过茂密的丛林。|例句：The bear lumberingly climbed the tree. 熊笨拙地爬上了树。|近义词：clumsily, awkwardly, heavily, ponderously|反义词：gracefully, nimbly, agilely|词性: 副词

lumberingly:/ˈlʌmbərɪŋli/
