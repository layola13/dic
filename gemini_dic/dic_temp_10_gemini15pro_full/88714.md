hobbyist:/ˈhɒbɪɪst/ (UK) /ˈhɑːbiɪst/ (US)| n. | 爱好者，业余爱好者 | A person who engages in a particular activity, especially one that is pursued for pleasure rather than for profit. | He's a model railway hobbyist and spends hours working on his layout. 他是一位铁路模型爱好者，花费数小时在自己的模型布局上。

My aunt is a keen hobbyist photographer. 我阿姨是一位热衷摄影的爱好者。


近义词：amateur, enthusiast, devotee, buff
反义词：professional
词性：noun
