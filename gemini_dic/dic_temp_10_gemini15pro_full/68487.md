famine:/ˈfæmɪn/| n. |饥荒，灾荒|extreme scarcity of food | The famine caused widespread suffering. 饥荒造成了 widespread 的痛苦。|  近义词：starvation, hunger, scarcity | 反义词: abundance, plenty | noun
famine:/ˈfæmɪn/| n. |缺乏，匮乏|a great lack of something | There is a famine of affordable housing in the city.  这个城市缺乏负担得起的住房。|近义词：dearth, shortage, lack | 反义词: surplus, excess | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *famine (Modern French famine), from Latin famēs (“hunger, famine”), related to famulus (“servant, slave”) and possibly to Greek θάμβος (thámbos, “amazement, astonishment”).
