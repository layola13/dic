He whined babyishly about having to go to bed. 他孩子气地抱怨要上床睡觉。

She clung to her mother's arm babyishly. 她孩子气地 clinging to her mother's arm. 

|近义词：childishly, immaturely, naively|反义词：maturely, sophisticatedly|词性：副词
