conjecture:/kənˈdʒɛktʃər/ | n. | 推测，猜想 | An opinion or conclusion formed on the basis of incomplete information. |  Many conjectures about the future of the company were made after the CEO resigned.  公司首席执行官辞职后，人们对公司的未来做了很多猜测。 | synonyms: hypothesis, theory, speculation, guess, supposition | antonyms: fact, proof, certainty | noun
conjecture:/kənˈdʒɛktʃər/ | v. | 推测，猜想 | Form an opinion or supposition about (something) on the basis of incomplete information. |  Experts conjectured that the painting was a forgery.  专家推测这幅画是赝品。 | synonyms: hypothesize, theorize, speculate, guess, surmise | antonyms: know, prove, ascertain | verb
