misproduces:/ˌmɪsprəˈdjuːsɪz/ | v. | 生产错误，制造不良,产量不足| To produce incorrectly or inadequately; to yield a faulty product. | The factory misproduces a large number of units each year due to outdated equipment.  这家工厂由于设备老化，每年都会生产大量不合格的产品。

The play was misproduced and poorly received by critics. 这部戏制作得很差，受到了评论家的差评。 | synonyms: mismanufacture, miscreate, bungle, botch | antonyms: produce, manufacture, create, fabricate | verb
