adipsous: /əˈdɪpsəs/
adj.
解渴的; 止渴的
Quenching thirst; thirst-quenching.
The adipsous lemonade was perfect on a hot summer day.  炎炎夏日，一杯止渴的柠檬水真是太棒了。
The hiker found an adipsous spring in the desert.  徒步旅行者在沙漠中发现了一口能解渴的泉水。
近义词: thirst-quenching, refreshing
反义词: dehydrating, thirst-inducing
词性: 形容词

词源:
From Greek *adipson*, neuter of *adipsos* "quenching thirst," from *a-* "not" + *dipsa* "thirst."
