fugitiveness:/ˌfjuːdʒɪˈtɪvnəs/
n.
短暂性，逃亡，易逝
the quality or state of being fugitive; transience.
The fugitiveness of youth reminds us to cherish every moment.  青春的短暂性提醒我们要珍惜每一刻。
The fugitiveness of the criminal made it difficult for the police to catch him.  罪犯的逃亡使得警方很难抓到他。
synonyms: transience, ephemerality, impermanence, brevity
antonyms: permanence, eternity, durability
noun
