anachronous:/əˈnækrənəs/| adj.|时代错误的；年代失调的|Belonging to a period other than that being portrayed; out of chronological order; chronologically misplaced.|例句：The film is full of anachronisms, like characters using cell phones in ancient Rome. 这部电影充满了时代错误，例如古罗马人物使用手机。

例句：His anachronous views on race made him an outcast in modern society. 他关于种族的过时的观点使他在现代社会中被孤立。|近义词：outdated, obsolete, anachronistic, untimely|反义词：contemporary, modern, timely, synchronous|词性:adjective
