ornithomancy:/ˈɔːrnɪθəʊˌmænsi/ | n. | 鸟卜 | Divination by observing the flight or behaviour of birds. |  Example 1: The ancient Greeks practiced ornithomancy to predict the future. 古希腊人通过鸟卜来预测未来。

Example 2:  Ornithomancy is considered a form of divination. 鸟卜被认为是一种占卜形式。 | Synonym: augury, divination | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps scientific observation) | Noun
