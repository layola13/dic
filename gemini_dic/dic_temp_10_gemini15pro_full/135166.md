nonopposition:/ˌnɒnˌɒpəˈzɪʃən/ | n. | 不反对，不抵抗，默认 | Lack of opposition; absence of resistance or dissent. |  The bill passed due to the nonopposition of the majority party.  该法案由于多数党的默认而获得通过。 | The government faced nonopposition on the new policy. 政府在这项新政策上没有遇到反对。 | 近义词: acquiescence, acceptance, compliance, agreement | 反义词: opposition, resistance, defiance, objection | 词性: 名词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + opposition (from Old French oposicion, from Latin oppositio, from opponere "to place against")
