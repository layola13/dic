nonactivity:/ˌnɒnækˈtɪvɪti/
n.
不活动；闲置；停滞
Lack of activity; inactivity.
The long period of nonactivity caused the muscles to atrophy. 长期不活动导致肌肉萎缩。
近义词:inactivity, idleness, dormancy
反义词:activity, action, movement
词性:名词

nonactivity:/ˌnɒnækˈtɪvɪti/
n.
不活动, 闲置
The state of not being active.
Due to the injury, he had a period of enforced nonactivity. 由于受伤，他有一段被迫不活动的时间。
近义词:inaction, stillness
反义词:activity, action
词性:名词
