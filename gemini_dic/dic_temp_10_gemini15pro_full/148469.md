overstream:/ˈoʊvərˌstriːm/| v. |过度流式传输；过度直播；过度串流|To transmit or broadcast (especially live video content) excessively, often to the point of saturation or diminishing returns.|The influencer overstreamed last week, posting multiple live videos daily, and her viewership dropped significantly.  这位网红上周过度直播，每天发布多个直播视频，导致她的观看人数大幅下降。
|Synonyms: overbroadcast, oversaturate|Antonyms: understream, underbroadcast|verb


overstream:/ˈoʊvərˌstriːm/| n. |过度流式传输；过度直播；过度串流|An instance of excessive streaming, often resulting in saturation or diminished returns.|The platform experienced an overstream during the major sporting event, leading to buffering issues for many users.  该平台在大型体育赛事期间经历了过度串流，导致许多用户出现缓冲问题。|Synonyms: oversaturation, glut|Antonyms: undersupply, scarcity|noun
