havala:/həˈvɑːlə/| n.|哈瓦拉, 非正式的价值转移系统|An informal value transfer system based on trust, used primarily in South Asia, the Middle East, and North Africa, whereby money is transferred through a network of brokers (hawaladars) without the actual physical movement of cash.|Example:  The migrant workers used havala to send money back to their families.  移工使用哈瓦拉系统把钱寄回家。

Example:  Havala is often preferred for its speed and low fees. 哈瓦拉系统因其速度快、费用低而受到青睐。|近义词：remittance, money transfer|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From Hindi/Urdu हवाला (havālā), meaning "transfer," "trust," or "bill of exchange," ultimately from Arabic حوالة (hawāla), meaning "transfer" or "referral."
