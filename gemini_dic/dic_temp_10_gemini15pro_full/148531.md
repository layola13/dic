oversureties:/ˌōvərˈʃʊrətiz/
noun
过度担保，过多的保证人
Excessive sureties or guarantees, typically financial.
The loan agreement required three oversureties, which seemed excessive. 这份贷款协议要求三个担保人，这似乎太多了。
Oversureties can create a complex web of financial obligations. 过多的担保会造成复杂的财务义务网络。
synonyms: excessive guarantees, redundant sureties
antonyms: insufficient guarantees, lack of security
