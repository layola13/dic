expends:/ɪkˈspɛndz/
verb
花费，消耗
pays out (money); uses up (energy, resources, etc.)
The company expends large sums on research and development.  这家公司在研发上花费巨资。
The athletes expended all their energy in the final race.  运动员们在决赛中耗尽了所有精力。
spend, use, consume
save, conserve
词性：动词
