nonbibliographic:/ˌnɑːnˌbɪbliəˈɡræfɪk/
adjective
非书目的，非文献的
Relating to information or resources that are not found in books or other traditional published materials.
The museum's nonbibliographic collection includes photographs, recordings, and artifacts.  博物馆的非书目藏品包括照片、录音和文物。
The archive contains a wealth of nonbibliographic materials, such as maps and films.  档案馆包含大量的非书目资料，例如地图和电影。
synonyms: non-literary, non-textual, multimedia
antonyms: bibliographic, literary, textual
