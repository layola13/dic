grabber:/ˈɡræbər/ (US), /ˈɡræbə(r)/ (UK)| n.|抓取器；吸引人的东西；抓住…的人|Something that grabs or is intended to grab, either physically or figuratively.  A tool or device for grabbing or gripping something.  Something that attracts attention or interest.|

例句：
The baby's grabber toy helped develop his fine motor skills.
婴儿的抓握玩具帮助他发展精细运动技能。

The opening scene of the movie was a real grabber.
这部电影的开场戏非常吸引人。

近义词：catcher, gripper, hook, claw, attention-getter|
反义词：releaser|
词性: noun
