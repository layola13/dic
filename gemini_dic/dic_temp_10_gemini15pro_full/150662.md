paps:/pæps/ | n. | 狗仔队；偷拍照片（常用复数）| Paparazzi, informal, often used in the plural. Refers to freelance photographers who pursue celebrities to get photographs of them, often in intrusive ways. | The paps were hounding the movie star all day. 狗仔队整天都在跟踪这位电影明星。|  The paps camped outside her house hoping for a scoop. 狗仔队在她家外面扎营，希望能拍到独家新闻。| 近义词：paparazzi | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: Noun
