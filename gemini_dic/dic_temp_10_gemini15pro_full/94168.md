impenitently:/ɪmˈpɛnɪtəntli/
adverb
不悔改地，顽固地
without remorse or regret; stubbornly
He impenitently continued his bad behavior, ignoring everyone's pleas to stop. 他頑固地繼續他的不良行為，無視所有人的停止請求。
The thief impenitently denied stealing the jewels despite the overwhelming evidence against him. 儘管有壓倒性的證據證明他偷了珠寶，但他仍拒不認罪。
Synonyms: unrepentantly, remorselessly, obdurately, stubbornly
Antonyms: repentantly, remorsefully, contritely
词性: 副词
