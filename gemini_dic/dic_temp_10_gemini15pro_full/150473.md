pantherine:/ˈpænθəˌraɪn/| adj.|豹亚科的，像豹的|Of or relating to the Panthera genus of felids, which includes lions, tigers, leopards, jaguars, and snow leopards.  Characterized by a robust body, powerful limbs, and often a spotted or striped coat.|The pantherine roar echoed through the jungle. 豹子的吼声在丛林中回荡。|近义词：feline, leopard-like|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "canine" for a contrasting family)|词性: adjective

pantherine:/ˈpænθəˌraɪn/| n.|豹亚科动物|An animal belonging to the Panthera genus of felids.|The zoo houses several pantherines, including a majestic lion.  这家动物园养了几种豹亚科动物，包括一头雄伟的狮子。|近义词：big cat|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "small cat" for a contrasting size)|词性: noun
