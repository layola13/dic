grizzling:/ˈɡrɪzəlɪŋ/ | adj. | 灰白的，(尤指头发)花白的；(天气)阴沉的；令人沮丧的 |  Turning gray, especially of hair; (of the weather) bleak and dismal; causing grief or depression |  The grizzling sky threatened rain. 阴沉的天空预示着要下雨。 | 近义词：gray, greyish, dismal, depressing | 反义词：bright, cheerful, sunny | 词性:形容词

grizzling:/ˈɡrɪzəlɪŋ/ | n. | (熊的)低吼；抱怨 | The act of a bear growling or making low guttural sounds; complaining constantly or whining | The grizzling of the bears echoed through the forest. 熊的低吼声在森林中回荡。 | 近义词：growling, grumbling, complaining, whining | 反义词：praising, complimenting | 词性:名词

词源: From *grizzle* (verb), frequentative of *grisen*, from Old English *grīsan* (“to turn gray”), from Proto-Germanic *grīsaną* (“to turn gray”).
