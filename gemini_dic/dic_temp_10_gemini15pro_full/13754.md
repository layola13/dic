aucupation:/ˌɔːkjuˈpeɪʃən/
n.
鸟类捕捉; 捕鸟; 设陷阱捕捉鸟
the act of fowling or entrapping birds.
He studied the ancient art of aucupation, learning the ways of the falconer and the trapper. 他研究了古老的捕鸟艺术，学习了猎鹰者和诱捕者的方法。
近义词:fowling, birdcatching, trapping
反义词:release, liberation
词性:noun
