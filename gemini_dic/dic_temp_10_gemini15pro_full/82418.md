grueling:/ˈɡruːəlɪŋ/ (US), /ˈɡruːəlɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.| 艰苦的，令人精疲力竭的|Extremely tiring and demanding; punishing.|The marathon was a grueling test of endurance. 马拉松是一场对耐力的严峻考验。|Synonyms: arduous, exhausting, punishing, strenuous|Antonyms: easy, simple, effortless|adjective


gruelly:/ˈɡruːəli/ (US), /ˈɡruːəli/ (UK)| adv.| 艰苦地，令人精疲力竭地|In a grueling or exhausting manner.|He trained gruelly for months before the competition. 他在比赛前几个月进行了艰苦的训练。|Synonyms: arduously, exhaustingly, punishingly, strenuously|Antonyms: easily, simply, effortlessly|adverb
