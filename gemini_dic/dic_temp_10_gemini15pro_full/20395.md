bitchin:/ˈbɪtʃɪn/| adj.|极好的，很棒的；抱怨的，牢骚满腹的|From "bitching," present participle of "bitch" (v.), meaning "to complain." Originally referred to a dog's whining, then extended to general complaining and later, ironically, to something excellent.|That car is bitchin'! 那辆车真棒！|近义词：awesome, excellent, cool, fantastic, amazing, great, superb, wonderful, outstanding, terrific, splendid, marvelous, magnificent|反义词：awful, terrible, bad, horrible, lousy|词性:形容词

bitchin:/ˈbɪtʃɪn/| n.|抱怨，牢骚|From "bitching," the act of complaining excessively.|He did a lot of bitchin' about the food. 他对食物抱怨了很多。|近义词：complaining, whining, griping, moaning, grousing|反义词：praising, complimenting|词性:名词
