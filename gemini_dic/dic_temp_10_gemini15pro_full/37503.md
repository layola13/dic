commodate:/əˈkɑːmədeɪt/ |v. |容纳；提供住宿；适应；|to provide with a place to live or to be stored in; to do something helpful for someone or change your plans in order to help them; to change to suit a new situation| The hotel can accommodate up to 500 guests. 这家酒店最多可容纳500位客人。

The schedule can be adjusted to accommodate your needs.  时间表可以调整以满足您的需求。|近义词：house, lodge, adapt, adjust|反义词:displace, eject|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin accommodatus, past participle of accommodare  "to make fit, adapt,"  from Latin ad- "to" + commodare "to make suitable," from commodus "suitable, convenient," from com- "together" + modus "measure, manner."
