freudians:/ˈfrɔɪdiənz/
n.
弗洛伊德学派; 精神分析学家（尤指弗洛伊德学派的）
Followers or students of Sigmund Freud; adherents of Freudian theories.
例句：Many Freudians believe that dreams reveal unconscious desires. 许多弗洛伊德学派的人相信梦揭示了潜意识的欲望。

例句：The Freudians interpreted the patient's symptoms as manifestations of repressed childhood trauma. 弗洛伊德学派的人将病人的症状解释为被压抑的童年创伤的表现。

近义词：psychoanalysts, Freudian adherents
反义词: (No direct antonym exists, but one might consider "Jungians" or "Adlerians" as representing different schools of psychoanalytic thought.)
词性: noun (plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Sigmund Freud (1856-1939), Austrian neurologist and founder of psychoanalysis + -ian (suffix forming adjectives and nouns meaning "of or belonging to," "like," "one connected with").
