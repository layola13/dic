ingravidate: /ɪnˈɡrævɪˌteɪt/
verb
使失重; 使漂浮
To cause to experience weightlessness or to float.
例句：The astronauts were ingravidated during the spacewalk. 宇航员在太空行走时处于失重状态。

例句：The scientist ingravidated the particles in the experiment.  科学家在实验中使粒子失重。

近义词：levitate, float, suspend
反义词：weigh down, ground
词性：verb
