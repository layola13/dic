garbo:/ˈɡɑːrboʊ/| n.|垃圾; 废物; (尤指) 厨房垃圾|Something that is discarded as useless or unwanted, especially kitchen refuse. |例句：The garbo comes on Tuesdays.  垃圾车星期二来。

例句：Don't forget to take out the garbo.  别忘了把垃圾拿出去。

近义词：garbage, refuse, trash, rubbish, waste|反义词:treasure, valuables|词性:名词

garbo:/ˈɡɑːrboʊ/| n.|格丽泰·嘉宝 (Greta Garbo)|A Swedish-born actress, known for her beauty and dramatic film roles.  |例句：She had a mysterious allure, just like Garbo.  她有一种神秘的魅力，就像嘉宝一样。

例句：He's as reclusive as Garbo. 他和嘉宝一样深居简出。

近义词：icon, star, legend|反义词:nobody, unknown|词性:名词
