bulimic: /buːˈlɪmɪk/ | adj. n. | 贪食的；暴食症患者 |  Relating to or affected by bulimia, an eating disorder characterized by episodes of binge eating followed by purging (e.g., vomiting, laxative use, excessive exercise).  |  She was diagnosed as bulimic after years of struggling with her weight. 她与体重抗争多年后被诊断为贪食症。|  She felt bulimic after eating the entire cake.  吃完整个蛋糕后，她感觉自己像暴食症患者一样。| 近义词: gluttonous, voracious | 反义词: anorexic | 词性: adjective, noun
