orthodromic:/ˌɔːrθəˈdrɒmɪk/ |adj.|顺向的，沿最短路径的|Following the shortest path between two points on a sphere, especially along a great circle.|Ships and aircraft generally follow orthodromic routes to save time and fuel.|近义词:great-circle, direct|反义词:indirect|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Greek *orthos* (straight) + *dromos* (running, course).
