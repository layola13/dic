dissidents:/ˈdɪsɪdənts/ | n. | 持不同政见者；异议者 | A person who publicly disagrees with and criticizes their government. |  The government imprisoned several dissidents for speaking out against the regime.  政府将几名公开反对政权的异议人士投入监狱。 | synonyms:  objector, protestor, rebel, nonconformist, iconoclast, freethinker | antonyms: conformist, loyalist, supporter | noun

dissident:/ˈdɪsɪdənt/ | adj. | 持不同政见者的；异议的 | Characterized by or expressing dissent. | The dissident voices grew louder as the government's policies became more oppressive. 随着政府政策越来越专制，异议的声音也越来越大。 | synonyms: dissenting, critical, rebellious, nonconformist, unorthodox | antonyms: conforming, orthodox, compliant | adjective
