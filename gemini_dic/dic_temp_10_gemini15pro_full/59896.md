eddo:/ˈɛdoʊ/
noun
芋头；小芋头
A tropical plant with a starchy root, grown for food.
Eddoes are a staple food in many tropical countries. 芋头是许多热带国家的主食。
The eddo is similar to a potato in texture. 芋头的质地和土豆相似。
Synonyms: taro, dasheen
Antonyms: None.
