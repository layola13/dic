interentanglement:/ˌɪntərɪnˈtæŋɡlmənt/ | n. |相互纠缠，互相缠绕 |The interentanglement of the branches made it difficult to separate them.  树枝相互缠绕，很难把它们分开。|  The interentanglement of political and economic issues makes the situation complex. 政治和经济问题相互交织，使局势变得复杂。 | 近义词：intertwining, interweaving, entanglement | 反义词: separation, disentanglement | 词性: 名词

词源:  "inter-" (prefix) meaning "between, among, mutually" + "entanglement" from "entangle" (verb) meaning "to twist or twine together in a tangled mass; involve in difficulties or complications". Ultimately from the Old French word "entanglier".
