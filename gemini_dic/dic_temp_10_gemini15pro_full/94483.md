importability:/ɪmˌpɔːrtəˈbɪləti/| n.|可进口性；进口可能性|The importability of certain goods is restricted due to safety regulations. 某些商品的可进口性由于安全规定而受到限制。|The government is considering the importability of genetically modified crops. 政府正在考虑转基因作物的进口可能性。|近义词：importableness|反义词：non-importability|词性: noun

词源:  From import + -ability.  "import" comes from the Old French "importer" meaning "to bring in," ultimately from the Latin "importare"  (in- + portare "to carry"). "-ability" is a suffix denoting capability or possibility.
