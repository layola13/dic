epigram:/ˈɛpɪɡræm/ | n. | 警句，箴言，讽刺短诗 | A short, witty, and often paradoxical saying or remark. |  Example 1: Oscar Wilde was famous for his epigrams. 奥斯卡·王尔德以他的警句而闻名。

Example 2:  The book began with an epigram about the nature of life. 这本书以一句关于生命本质的箴言开头。 | synonym: quip, witticism, aphorism, maxim, adage | antonym:  statement, treatise, dissertation | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *epigramma, from Greek epigramma "inscription (on a monument, statue, etc.)," from epigraphein "to write upon, inscribe," from epi "upon" + graphein "to write."
