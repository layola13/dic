doornail:/ˈdɔːrneɪl/| n. |门钉|A large-headed nail used in doors, now primarily found in the idiom "dead as a doornail".  Originally referred to the nails used to secure the door knocker plate, which were hammered in from one side and bent over on the other, making them impossible to remove.|The miser was dead as a doornail.  那个守财奴死得透透的。|近义词:none (in the context of the idiom)|反义词:alive|noun
