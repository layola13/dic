masterful:/ˈmɑːstərfʊl/ /ˈmæstərfl/| adj.|精湛的，熟练的，高超的|Showing great skill or control, especially in an artistic performance or a difficult situation. |例句：She gave a masterful performance on the piano. 她钢琴演奏得非常精湛。

例句：His masterful handling of the crisis averted a disaster. 他对危机的娴熟处理避免了一场灾难。|近义词：skilled, expert, proficient, adept, accomplished, virtuoso|反义词：clumsy, inept, unskilled, amateurish|词性:形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from master + -ful.
