fellowship:/ˈfeləʊʃɪp/ (UK) /ˈfɛloʊʃɪp/ (US)| n.|友谊，交情；团体，协会；奖学金|A friendly association with others; a group of people sharing a common interest or pursuit; a sum of money awarded to a graduate student for further study.|例句：They enjoyed the fellowship of their friends. 他们享受着朋友间的友谊。|近义词：companionship, camaraderie, fraternity, sorority, scholarship, grant|反义词：isolation, loneliness, enmity|词性：名词

fellowship:/ˈfeləʊʃɪp/ (UK) /ˈfɛloʊʃɪp/ (US)| n.|会员资格|The status of being a member of a group or organization.|例句：She was awarded a fellowship at the university. 她获得了大学的会员资格。|近义词：membership|反义词：exclusion|词性：名词
