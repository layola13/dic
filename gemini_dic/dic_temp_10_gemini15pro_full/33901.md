circumvent:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈvent/ /ˌsɝːkəmˈvɛnt/ | vt. | 绕行，规避，回避 |To avoid something, especially cleverly or illegally, or to get around rules, problems, etc.|例句：They found a way to circumvent the law. 他们找到了规避法律的方法。

例句：The company circumvented the trade restrictions by shipping the goods through a third country.  这家公司通过经由第三国运输货物来规避贸易限制。


近义词：avoid, evade, bypass, dodge, sidestep | 反义词：confront, face, comply with | 词性：动词
