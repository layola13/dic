misbuttoning: /ˌmɪsˈbʌtənɪŋ/
verb
扣错扣子
fastening buttons incorrectly.
He was so flustered that he ended up misbuttoning his shirt. 他太慌乱了，结果把衬衫扣错了。
The child is still learning how to dress himself and often ends up misbuttoning his coat.  这个孩子还在学习如何自己穿衣服，经常把外套扣错。
近义词: misfastening
反义词: buttoning correctly
词性: verb (动名词)


misbuttoning: /ˌmɪsˈbʌtənɪŋ/
noun
扣错纽扣的行为
the act of fastening buttons incorrectly.
Her misbuttoning of her blouse went unnoticed until she was halfway through the meeting. 她衬衫扣错了，直到会议进行到一半才被注意到。
The toddler's misbuttoning made his outfit look quite comical.  幼儿把扣子扣错了，使他的衣服看起来很滑稽。
近义词: misfastening
反义词: correct buttoning
词性: noun
