offloaded:/ˌɒfˈləʊdɪd/ /ˌɔːfˈloʊdəd/
verb|卸载，卸货，转嫁（负担等）|To remove a load from something, especially a vehicle or ship; to transfer (work, duties, etc.) to someone else.|The ship offloaded its cargo at the port. 这艘船在港口卸下了货物。|synonyms: unload, discharge, transfer, unburden|antonyms: load, burden|v.

offloaded:/ˌɒfˈləʊdɪd/ /ˌɔːfˈloʊdəd/
adjective|卸载的|Having had a load removed.|The offloaded truck was much lighter. 卸载后的卡车轻多了。|synonyms: unloaded, empty|antonyms: loaded, full|adj.
