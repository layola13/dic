forthright:/ˈfɔːrθraɪt/ | adj. | 直率的，坦诚的 |  Direct and outspoken; straightforward and honest. Proceeding straight to the point. |  She was forthright in her criticism of the proposal. 她直率地批评了这项提议。| synonyms: frank, candid, blunt, direct, outspoken | antonyms: evasive, indirect, devious, deceitful | adjective

forthright:/ˈfɔːrθraɪt/ | adv. | 直率地，坦诚地 | Directly and frankly; straightforwardly. | He answered her questions forthrightly. 他坦率地回答了她的问题。 | synonyms: frankly, candidly, directly, openly | antonyms: evasively, indirectly, subtly | adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English forth-right, equivalent to forth + right.  Originally meaning "going straight ahead," it evolved to describe directness in speech and manner.
