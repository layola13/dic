nongeminate:/nɒnˈdʒɛmɪnət/ | adj. | 非双辅音的；非重叠的 | Not doubled; consisting of a single sound or letter, especially a consonant.  Refers to a sound that is pronounced once, not twice in a row.  Contrasts with a geminate consonant which is held for a longer duration. | The word "better" has a nongeminate /t/ sound, unlike "butter" which has a geminate /t/ sound.  | The pronunciation of "running" includes a nongeminate /n/ sound. | 近义词: single, simple | 反义词: geminate, double | 词性: adjective
