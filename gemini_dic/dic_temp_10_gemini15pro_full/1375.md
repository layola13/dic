accrue:/əˈkruː/
verb
累积，增加
(especially of money or interest) be received by someone in regular or increasing amounts over time.
Interest will accrue if you leave the money in the account. 如果你把钱留在账户里，就会产生利息。
The company accrued huge debts during the recession.  经济衰退期间，该公司累积了巨额债务。

近义词:accumulate, gather, collect, amass
反义词:decrease, diminish, dwindle, lessen
词性:verb

USAGE->
一般现在时:
Interest accrues on this account monthly. =  这个账户每月都会累积利息。
My vacation time accrues at a rate of two days per month. = 我的假期以每月两天的速度累积。


一般过去时:
Unpaid interest accrued over several years. = 未付利息累积了数年。
The benefits accrued slowly but steadily. = 收益缓慢而稳定地增长。


现在完成时:
A significant amount of interest has accrued on the investment. = 这项投资已经累积了大量的利息。
He has accrued a substantial amount of wealth over the years. = 多年来，他已经积累了大量的财富。


过去完成时:
By the time he retired, he had accrued enough vacation time for a three-month trip. = 到他退休的时候，他已经积累了足够三个月旅行的假期时间。
She had accrued considerable debt before she graduated. = 在她毕业之前，她已经积累了相当多的债务。


将来时:
More benefits will accrue to members in the coming years. = 未来几年，会员将获得更多福利。
Significant savings will accrue if we implement these changes. = 如果我们实施这些改变，将会累积可观的节省。


虚拟语气:
If the funds were invested wisely, more interest would accrue. = 如果资金投资得当，将会累积更多的利息。
If she had left the money in the account, it would have accrued more interest. = 如果她把钱留在账户里，它本来会累积更多利息。


被动语态:
Interest is accrued on a daily basis. = 利息按日累积。
A substantial amount of wealth was accrued by the company last year. = 去年该公司积累了大量财富。


疑问句:
How much interest will accrue on this investment? = 这项投资会累积多少利息？
How quickly do vacation days accrue? = 假期天数累积的速度有多快？


否定句:
The debt didn't accrue overnight. = 债务不是一夜之间累积起来的。
The interest doesn't accrue if the account balance is below the minimum. = 如果账户余额低于最低限额，则不会累积利息。
