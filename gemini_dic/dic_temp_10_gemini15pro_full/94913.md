in-groups:/ˈɪn ɡruːps/ | n. | 群内群体；内群体 |  A social group to which a person psychologically identifies as being a member. |  She felt comfortable sharing her opinions within her in-groups. 她感觉在自己的群体内很自在的分享观点。 | in-group, clique | out-groups, out-group | noun

in-group:/ˈɪn ɡruːp/ | adj. | 群内的；内群体的 | Belonging to or characteristic of an in-group. |  The in-group members shared a strong sense of camaraderie. 内群体成员之间有很强的同志情谊。|  inner, internal | outer, external | adjective
