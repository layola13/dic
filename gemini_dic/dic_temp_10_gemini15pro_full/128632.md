nickelized:/ˈnɪkəlaɪzd/
adjective
镀镍的；用镍处理的
coated or treated with nickel.
The car had nickelized bumpers. 这辆车的保险杠是镀镍的。
Synonyms: nickel-plated, nickel-clad
Antonyms: unplated, bare
词性: adjective
