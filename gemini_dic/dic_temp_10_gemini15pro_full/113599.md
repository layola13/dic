macerate:/ˈmæsəreɪt/| v. adj.|浸渍，软化；消瘦；憔悴|To soften or break down by soaking in liquid; to become thin and weak.|The apricots were macerated in brandy overnight. 杏子在白兰地里浸泡了一夜。|近义词：soak, steep, soften, weaken|反义词:harden, strengthen|词性：动词

macerated:/ˈmæsəreɪtɪd/| adj.|浸渍的，软化的；消瘦的；憔悴的|Softened or broken down by soaking in liquid; thin and weak.|The macerated fruit was used in the cake.  浸软的水果被用来做蛋糕。|近义词：soaked, steeped, softened, weakened|反义词:hardened, strengthened|词性：形容词

词源：From Latin *maceratus*, past participle of *macerare* ("to make soft, steep, soak"), from *macies* ("leanness").
