macrolinguistics:/ˌmækroʊlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪks/ | n. |宏观语言学|The study of language in relation to broad social, cultural, and behavioral contexts, rather than focusing on the internal structure of language itself.  It encompasses areas like sociolinguistics, psycholinguistics, anthropological linguistics, and dialectology.|Macrolinguistics examines the role of language in society. 宏观语言学研究语言在社会中的作用。|Near synonyms: language studies, linguistic anthropology |Antonym: microlinguistics | noun
