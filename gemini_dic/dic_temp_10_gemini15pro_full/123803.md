mopily:/ˈməʊpɪli/| adv.| 闷闷不乐地，沮丧地 | In a sad and gloomy manner; dejectedly. | He sat mopily in the corner after losing the game.  他输了比赛后闷闷不乐地坐在角落里。| 近义词：glumly, sadly, dejectedly, despondently | 反义词：cheerfully, happily, joyfully | 词性: 副词

mopily:/ˈmoʊpɪli/|  adv. | 闷闷不乐地，沮丧地 | Characterized by or expressing dejection or sadness. | She walked mopily through the park, lost in thought. 她闷闷不乐地走在公园里，陷入了沉思。 | 近义词：sullenly, morosely, gloomily | 反义词：brightly, merrily, joyously | 词性: 副词


词源:  From mope + -ly. Mope (verb) comes from the Middle Dutch word *māpen, meaning "to gape, to stare foolishly."  The suffix "-ly" is commonly used to form adverbs from adjectives.
