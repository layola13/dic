noncontradictories:/ˌnɒnkɒntrəˈdɪktərɪz/ | n. |非矛盾命题; 不矛盾者|Statements or propositions that are not contradictory to each other, meaning they can both be true at the same time.|The two witness statements were noncontradictories, so both could be considered valid. 这两个证人陈述并不矛盾，因此都可以被认为是有效的。|Synonyms: consistent, compatible, harmonious |Antonyms: contradictory, inconsistent, conflicting |Noun

noncontradictory:/ˌnɒnkɒntrəˈdɪktərɪ/ | adj. |不矛盾的; 一致的|Not contradictory; consistent. |His actions were noncontradictory to his words. 他的行为与他的言辞一致。|Synonyms: consistent, compatible, harmonious |Antonyms: contradictory, inconsistent, conflicting |Adjective
