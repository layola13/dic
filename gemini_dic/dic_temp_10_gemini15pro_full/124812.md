muciform:/ˈmjuːsɪfɔːrm/
adjective
粘液状的，似粘液的
Resembling mucus; covered with or secreting mucus.
The muciform layer of the stomach protects it from digestive acids. 胃的粘液层保护它免受消化酸的侵蚀。
Synonyms: mucous, mucilaginous, slimy, viscid
Antonyms: dry, parched

Etymology:
From Latin mucus ("mucus") + -form ("having the form of").
