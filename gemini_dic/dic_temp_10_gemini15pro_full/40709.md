convertible:/kənˈvɜːrtɪbl/ | adj. n. | 可转换的；敞篷车 | able to be changed from one form to another; a car with a roof that can be folded back |  This sofa is convertible into a bed.  这张沙发可以转换成床。| synonyms: adaptable, transformable, changeable | antonyms: fixed, permanent, immutable | adjective

convertible:/kənˈvɜːrtɪbl/ | n. | 敞篷车 | a car with a roof that can be folded back | He drove up in a red convertible. 他开着一辆红色的敞篷车来了。 | synonyms: cabriolet, roadster | antonyms: sedan, coupe | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *convertibilis, from Latin convertere "to turn around, transform," from com- "together" + vertere "to turn."
