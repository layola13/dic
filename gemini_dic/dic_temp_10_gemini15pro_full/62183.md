encomion:/ɛnˈkoʊmiəm/ | n. | 颂词，赞美 | A formal expression of high praise; a eulogy. | He delivered a moving encomion at his mentor's funeral.  他于其导师的葬礼上致了感人的颂词。 |  Synonyms: eulogy, panegyric, tribute, accolade, commendation | Antonyms: criticism, condemnation, censure, denunciation | noun
