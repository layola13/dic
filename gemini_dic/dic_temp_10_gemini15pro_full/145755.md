overcultivation:/ˌoʊvərˌkʌltɪˈveɪʃən/ | n. | 过度耕作，过度种植 | The act of cultivating land excessively, leading to depletion of soil nutrients and reduced fertility.  It involves farming land too intensively, without giving it enough time to recover. | Overcultivation has led to desertification in some regions. 过度耕作导致一些地区沙漠化。

The overcultivation of the land resulted in poor yields.  过度耕作导致产量低下。 | 近义词：overfarming, overuse | 反义词: undercultivation, sustainable agriculture | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From 'over-' (prefix meaning "excessive") + 'cultivation' (from Latin 'cultivare', meaning "to till, cultivate").
