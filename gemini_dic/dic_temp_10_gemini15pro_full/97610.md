inhaler:/ɪnˈheɪlər/ | n. | 吸入器；喷雾器 | A device holding medicine that is breathed in, used for treating asthma and other respiratory problems.  A small, portable device used to deliver medication in aerosol form directly to the lungs. |  She always carries her inhaler in case of an asthma attack. 她总是随身携带吸入器以防哮喘发作。| synonym: puffer, respirator | antonym: N/A | noun
