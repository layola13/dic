nativity: /nəˈtɪvɪti/ /neɪˈtɪvɪti/
n.
出生;诞生;耶稣诞生;诞生场景(画);圣诞剧
The birth of a person or animal.  The Christian festival or season of Christmas, celebrating the birth of Jesus. A depiction of the birth of Jesus Christ, especially in art.
例句：They celebrated the nativity of Jesus.  他们庆祝耶稣的诞生。

例句：We went to see the nativity play at the church. 我们去教堂看了圣诞剧。
近义词：birth, origin, genesis
反义词：death, demise
词性：名词
