outgas:/ˌaʊtˈɡæs/ /ˌaʊtˈɡæs/ | v. | 除气，放气 | To release or give off a gas or gases, especially slowly or in small amounts. |  The comet outgassed water vapor as it approached the sun.  彗星接近太阳时释放出水蒸气。

The new furniture outgassed a strong chemical smell. 新家具散发出一股强烈的化学气味。 | 近义词：emit, release, vent | 反义词: absorb | 词性: 动词
