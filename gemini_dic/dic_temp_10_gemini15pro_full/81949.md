grimier:/ˈɡraɪmiər/ | adj. | 更脏的，更油腻的 | comparative form of "grimy," meaning more covered in dirt or grease | The mechanic's hands were grimier after working on the engine.  技工修完发动机后，手更脏了。| The kitchen floor was grimier than usual. 厨房的地板比平时更脏。 | 近义词: dirtier, filthier, muckier | 反义词: cleaner, purer | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From grime + -y + -er.  Grime comes from Middle English *grime, of uncertain origin, perhaps from a Scandinavian source related to Norwegian grima ("a black spot, soot").
