inseminating:/ɪnˈsɛmɪneɪtɪŋ/
|v.|授精；使受精|introducing semen into the vagina or uterus of a female animal or plant, by natural or artificial means. |The farmer was inseminating the cows. 农民正在给母牛授精。|Synonyms: impregnating, fertilizing, siring |Antonyms: sterilizing, castrating|Verb

insemination:/ɪnˌsɛmɪˈneɪʃən/
|n.|授精；受精|the introduction of semen into the vagina or uterus of a female animal or plant, by natural or artificial means.|Artificial insemination is a common practice in animal husbandry. 人工授精是畜牧业中的常见做法。|Synonyms: impregnation, fertilization |Antonyms: sterilization|Noun
