arboreality:/ˌɑːrbɔːriˈælɪti/
n.
树栖性，树栖生活
The state of living in trees.
The monkey's arboreality allows it to easily escape predators.  这只猴子的树栖性使它能够轻易地躲避捕食者。
The sloth's arboreality is a key adaptation to its rainforest habitat.  树懒的树栖性是对其雨林栖息地的一种关键适应。
近义词：tree-dwelling, arboreal lifestyle
反义词：terrestriality, ground-dwelling
词性：noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *arborealis* ("of or belonging to a tree"), from *arbor* ("tree") + *-alis* (suffix forming adjectives of relation).  The English suffix *-ity* transforms the adjective into a noun denoting the state or quality.
