milit:/ˈmɪlɪt/
词性: Combining form
中文翻译: 兵役的；战士的；军事的
英文解析:  A combining form meaning "soldier," "military," used in the formation of compound words.  It's derived from the Latin word "miles" (soldier).
例句：
The militia was called up to quell the riot. 民兵被召集起来平息骚乱。

The paramilitary group was heavily armed.  这支准军事团体全副武装。

近义词: martial, warrior
反义词: civilian, peaceful
