boniform:/ˈbɒnɪfɔːm/
adjective
骨状的; 善良的
having the form of bone; good in nature.
This rare mineral has a boniform structure. 这种稀有矿物具有骨状结构。
He had a boniform face and a gentle smile. 他有一张善良的脸和温柔的微笑。
近义词:osseous, bony, kind, benevolent
反义词:malignant, unkind
词性:adjective

词源: From Latin "bonus" (good) + "-formis" (shaped like).
