antecedental:/ˌæntisiˈdentl/| adj.| 先前的，前身的，在先的；祖先的|Existing, happening, or coming before something else, especially the cause of something else. |The antecedental events leading up to the war are complex and multifaceted. 战争爆发前的事件错综复杂，涉及多方面。| The professor explained the antecedental principles of modern physics. 教授解释了现代物理学的先导性原理。|近义词：previous, prior, foregoing, precedent, precursor|反义词：subsequent, following, later, posterior|词性: adjective

antecedent:/ˌæntiˈsiːdnt/| n.| 先行词，前情，祖先|A thing or event that existed before or logically precedes another.|The pronoun "he" refers to its antecedent, "John". 代词“他”指的是它的先行词“约翰”。|Respect for elders is an important antecedent of a harmonious society. 尊敬长辈是构建和谐社会的重要前提。|近义词：predecessor, forerunner, ancestor|反义词：descendant, successor|词性: noun
