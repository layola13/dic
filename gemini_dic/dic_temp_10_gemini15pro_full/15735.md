blackshirts:/ˈblækʃɜːrts/ /ˈblækʃɝːts/| n.|黑衫党（复数）|Members of any of various political organizations who wear black shirts as a distinctive uniform, especially members of the Italian Fascist party before World War II. 指的是穿着黑色衬衫作为特殊制服的各种政治组织的成员，尤指二战前意大利法西斯党的成员。|Mussolini's blackshirts terrorized the opposition. 墨索里尼的黑衫党恐吓反对派。|近义词：fascists, |反义词:antifascists| noun
