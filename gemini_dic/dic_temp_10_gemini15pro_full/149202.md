oxygenize:/ˈɒksɪdʒənaɪz/ /ˈɑːksɪdʒənaɪz/| v. | 氧合；使氧化 | To supply, treat, or combine with oxygen. |  The blood is oxygenized in the lungs. 血液在肺部被氧合。

The machine oxygenizes the patient's blood. 这台机器为病人的血液供氧。 | 近义词: aerate, oxygenate | 反义词: deoxygenate |词性:动词

oxygenize:/ˈɒksɪdʒənaɪz/ /ˈɑːksɪdʒənaɪz/| adj. | 氧化的 | Containing or supplying oxygen. | Oxygenized water is sometimes used as a disinfectant. 含氧水有时被用作消毒剂。


This product contains oxygenized ingredients. 该产品含有氧化成分。| 近义词: aerated, oxygenated | 反义词: deoxygenated |词性:形容词
