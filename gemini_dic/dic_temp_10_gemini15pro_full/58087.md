droop:/druːp/
verb
下垂，低垂
to bend or hang down heavily
The flowers drooped in the heat. 花儿在高温下低垂。

近义词: sag, wilt, flag
反义词: rise, perk up
词性: verb


droop:/druːp/
noun
下垂，萎靡
a drooping posture or condition
There was a droop to his shoulders. 他的肩膀耷拉着。

近义词: sag, wilt
反义词: firmness, perkiness
词性: noun
