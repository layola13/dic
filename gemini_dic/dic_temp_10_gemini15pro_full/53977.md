discretely:/dɪˈskriːtli/
adv.
谨慎地，小心地
in a careful way, so as not to cause embarrassment or attract too much attention
She discretely wiped a tear from her eye. 她小心翼翼地擦去了眼角的泪水。
近义词: subtly, carefully, cautiously, privately, secretly
反义词: openly, publicly, blatantly, conspicuously
词性: 副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin discretus "separated, distinct," past participle of discernere "to separate, distinguish," from dis- "apart" + cernere "to sift, separate, distinguish." The adverbial form discretely arises directly from this adjective.
