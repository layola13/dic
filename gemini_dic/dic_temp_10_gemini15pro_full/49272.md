deliberateness:/dɪˈlɪbərɪtnəs/
n.
故意; 慎重; 从容
the quality of being done consciously or intentionally
She tied the knot with deliberateness. 她故意把结扎紧。

The deliberateness of her movements was unnerving. 她行动的从容不迫让人不安。

intention, purposefulness, calculation, care, caution
hastiness, carelessness, impulsiveness, rashness
