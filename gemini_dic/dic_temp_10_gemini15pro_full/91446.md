hygrometer:/haɪˈɡrɒmɪtər/ (US) /haɪˈɡrɑːmɪtər/ (UK)| n. |湿度计|An instrument for measuring the humidity of the air or a gas.|The hygrometer indicated a high level of humidity in the room. 房间里的湿度计显示湿度很高。|Synonyms: moisture meter, humidistat|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps a dehumidifier as an opposing device)|noun
