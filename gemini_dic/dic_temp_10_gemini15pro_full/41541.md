coronets:/ˈkɒrənɪts/ (UK), /ˈkɔːrənɪts/ (US)| n. |小冠冕，花冠|A small crown or a crownlike ornament.|The princess wore a coronet of flowers. 公主戴着花冠。|Synonyms: crown, tiara, diadem|Antonyms: none|Noun

coronets:/ˈkɒrənɪts/ (UK), /ˈkɔːrənɪts/ (US)| n. |（鸟类头上的）冠羽|A crown or tuft of feathers on the head of a bird.|The hoopoe is easily recognizable by its distinctive coronet. 戴胜鸟很容易通过其独特的冠羽辨认出来。|Synonyms: crest, plume|Antonyms: none|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "coronete," diminutive of "corone" (crown), from Latin "corona."
