color-blind:/ˈkʌlər blaɪnd/ (US) /ˈkʌlə blaɪnd/ (UK)| adj.|色盲的|Unable to distinguish certain colors, typically red and green.|例句：He's color-blind and can't tell the difference between red and green. 他是色盲，分不清红色和绿色。

例句：My grandfather is color-blind, so he has trouble matching his clothes. 我祖父是色盲，所以他搭配衣服有困难。

近义词：color-deficient|反义词：N/A|词性: adjective
