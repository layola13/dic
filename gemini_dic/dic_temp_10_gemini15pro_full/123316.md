monogram: /ˈmɑːnəɡræm/ | n. | 字母组合，花押字 | A design of two or more letters, typically the initials of a person's name, used as a logo or to identify personal possessions. |  She had her monogram embroidered on her handkerchiefs. 她把她的名字缩写绣在手帕上。 | synonyms: initials, cipher, logo | antonyms: N/A | noun

monogram: /ˈmɑːnəɡræm/ | v. |  用字母组合标记 | To mark or decorate with a monogram. | The hotel monogrammed all its towels. 这家酒店的所有毛巾都标有字母组合。 | synonyms: initial, personalize, customize | antonyms: N/A | verb
