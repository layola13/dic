eta:/ˈiːtə/ (US), /ˈiːtɑː/ (UK)| n.|预计到达时间|Estimated Time of Arrival.  It is often used in transportation and logistics to indicate when a vehicle, shipment, or person is expected to arrive at a destination.|The pilot announced the eta as 3 PM. 飞行员宣布预计到达时间为下午3点。|Synonyms: arrival time, due time|Antonyms: departure time|noun
