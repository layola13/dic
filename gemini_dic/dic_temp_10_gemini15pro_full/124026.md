morosis:/məˈroʊsɪs/ /mɔːˈrəʊsɪs/| n. | 忧郁，闷闷不乐 | A state of gloominess and sullenness. |  He sank into a deep morosis after losing his job. 他失业后陷入了深深的忧郁之中。|  The rainy weather only deepened his morosis.  阴雨天气加深了他的忧郁。|近义词：gloom, melancholy, dejection, sullenness |反义词：cheerfulness, happiness, elation| 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *morōsis, from Latin morōsus (“peevish, fretful”), from mōs (“manner, custom, habit”) + -ōsus.
