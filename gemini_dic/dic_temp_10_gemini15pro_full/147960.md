overresists:/ˌoʊvər rɪˈzɪsts/
verb
过度抵抗，抵抗过分
To resist more than is necessary or advisable.
The lawyer overresisted the judge's questioning, ultimately hurting his client's case. 律师对法官的提问过度抵抗，最终损害了他客户的案件。
近义词: overreact, overdo
反义词: yield, submit
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + resist.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," "too much," or "beyond."  "Resist" comes from the Latin "resistere," meaning "to stand back," "to withstand," or "to oppose."
