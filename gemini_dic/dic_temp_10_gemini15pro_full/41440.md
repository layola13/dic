cornfield: /ˈkɔːrnfiːld/ (UK) /ˈkɔːrnfiːld/ (US)
n.
玉米田，麦田
A field planted with corn.
The children got lost in the vast cornfield. 孩子们在广阔的玉米田中迷路了。
The scarecrow stood guard over the cornfield. 稻草人守护着玉米田。
synonyms: maize field, grain field
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider barren field, empty field)
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English "cornfeld," a compound of "corn" (grain, especially wheat or barley) and "feld" (field).  The meaning shifted to refer primarily to fields of maize (American corn) in American English.
