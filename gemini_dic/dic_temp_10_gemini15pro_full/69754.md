fergusonite:/ˈfɜːrɡəsənˌaɪt/ | n. | 弗格森矿 |A brownish-black mineral containing yttrium, cerium, erbium, niobium, tantalum and titanium. It is a radioactive ore of yttrium and other rare-earth elements.|Fergusonite is a relatively rare mineral found in pegmatites. 弗格森矿是一种相对稀有的矿物，发现于伟晶岩中。| Fergusonite can be a source of valuable rare earth elements. 弗格森矿可以作为一种有价值的稀土元素来源。 | Synonyms: None (as it's a specific mineral name) | Antonyms: None | noun
