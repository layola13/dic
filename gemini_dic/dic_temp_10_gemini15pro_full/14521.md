aver:/əˈvɜːr/ (US) /əˈvɜː(r)/ (UK)
verb
断言；坚称；声称
to state firmly and positively that something is true
The defendant averred that he was innocent.  被告坚称自己是无辜的。
She averred her innocence. 她声称自己是无辜的。
assert, declare, affirm, maintain
deny, refute, contradict
v.
