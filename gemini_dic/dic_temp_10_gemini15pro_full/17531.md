beatable:/ˈbiːtəbl/| adj.|可击败的，可战胜的|Able to be defeated or overcome.|例句：The champion is beatable, but it won't be easy.  这位冠军是可以被打败的，但这并不容易。|近义词：defeatable, conquerable, vincible, surmountable|反义词: unbeatable, invincible, insurmountable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "beat" (Old English *bēatan "to strike") + "-able" (suffix denoting capability).
