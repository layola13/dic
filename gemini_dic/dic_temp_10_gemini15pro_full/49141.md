deixis:/ˈdaɪksɪs/ | n. | 指示 | The use of a word or expression whose meaning is dependent on the context in which it is uttered, such as 'here', 'you', or 'tomorrow'. |  "In the sentence 'I'm here now', the words 'I', 'here', and 'now' are examples of deixis." 我现在在这里，“我”，“这里”和“现在”是指示语的例子。

| 近义词: reference,指示 | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
