albinism:/ˈælbɪnɪzəm/
n.
白化病
A group of inherited disorders characterized by little or no melanin production.
Albinism affects people of all races. 白化病影响所有种族的人。
The child's albinism made him sensitive to sunlight.  这个孩子的白化病使他对阳光敏感。
近义词: achromia, hypomelanism
反义词: melanism
词性: noun
