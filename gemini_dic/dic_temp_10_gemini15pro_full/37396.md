commerce: /ˈkɑːmɜːrs/ (US) /ˈkɒmɜːs/ (UK)| n.|商业，贸易|The exchange or buying and selling of commodities on a large scale. |International commerce is essential to a globalized economy. 国际贸易对全球化经济至关重要。|例句：The city is a center of commerce and industry.  这座城市是商业和工业中心。|近义词：trade, business, industry|反义词:stagnation, inactivity|词性:noun

Etymology: From Old French "commerce,"  from Latin "commercium"  "trade, traffic,"  from "com-" (together) + "merx" (merchandise).
