colostrum:/kəˈlɑːstrəm/ | n. | 初乳 | The first milk produced by mammals after giving birth, rich in antibodies and nutrients. |  Colostrum is crucial for newborns' immune system development. 初乳对新生儿的免疫系统发育至关重要。

Colostrum is often called "liquid gold" because of its yellowish color and health benefits. 初乳因其淡黄色和健康益处常被称为“液体黄金”。 | 近义词: first milk, beestings | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *colostra, plural of *colostrum, related to Latin colus ("sieve, strainer")
