clavus:/ˈkleɪvəs/ /ˈklɑːvəs/| n. |胼胝，鸡眼;嵌甲|A hard, thickened area of skin, especially on the foot, caused by friction or pressure.  Also, an ingrown toenail.|例句：The hiker developed a painful clavus on his heel after the long trek.  徒步旅行者长途跋涉后脚跟上长了一个疼痛的鸡眼。

例句：The doctor diagnosed her condition as a clavus and recommended a treatment plan. 医生诊断她的病情为嵌甲，并推荐了治疗方案。|近义词：callus, corn (for skin condition), ingrown nail|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
