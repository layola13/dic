congeneric:/ˌkɒndʒɪˈnɛrɪk/ | adj. |同属的，同类的|Of the same genus or kind; having a common ancestor. |例句：The congeneric species of birds shared similar plumage patterns. 这些同属的鸟类有着相似的羽毛图案。

例句：The study compared the behavior of several congeneric primate species. 这项研究比较了几种同属灵长类动物的行为。|近义词：related, kindred, cognate, similar|反义词：unrelated, dissimilar, different|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *congener* (of the same kind), from *con-* (together, with) + *genus* (kind, race, stock).  The English word was borrowed directly from Latin.
