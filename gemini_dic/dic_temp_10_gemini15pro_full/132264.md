nondeducible:/ˌnɒndɪˈdjuːsəbl/ /ˌnɑːndɪˈduːsəbl/| adj.|不可推断的，不能演绎的|Not able to be deduced or inferred; not derivable by reasoning.|The cause of the error was nondeducible from the available data.  这个错误的原因从现有数据中无法推断出来。| The theorem is nondeducible from the axioms.  这个定理无法从公理中推导出来。|近义词：uninferable, inconcludable, nonprovable|反义词：deducible, inferable, provable|词性:adjective
词源:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + deductible (from Latin deducere, "to lead down/away," meaning "able to be inferred")
