adjugate: /ˈædʒʌɡət/ /əˈdʒʌɡət/ | n. | 共轭矩阵; 伴随矩阵 |A square matrix obtained from a given square matrix and having the property that its product with the given matrix is equal to the determinant of the given matrix times the identity matrix. | The adjugate of matrix A is denoted adj(A).  共轭矩阵A表示为adj(A)。

The adjugate matrix is used to find the inverse of a matrix. 伴随矩阵用于求矩阵的逆矩阵。| synonym: adjoint, classical adjoint | antonym: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *adjugatus, past participle of adjugare (“to yoke to, join, attach”), from ad- (“to”) + jugum (“yoke”).  源自晚期拉丁语 *adjugatus，adjugare（“结合，连接，附加”）的过去分词，来自 ad-（“到”）+ jugum（“轭”）。



USAGE->
The adjugate matrix is rarely used outside of linear algebra and matrix theory.  共轭矩阵很少在线性代数和矩阵理论之外使用。


Example 1:
The adjugate of a 2x2 matrix A = [[a, b], [c, d]] is [[d, -b], [-c, a]].
2x2矩阵A = [[a, b], [c, d]]的共轭矩阵是[[d, -b], [-c, a]]。


Example 2:
If the determinant of a matrix is zero, its adjugate matrix will be singular (non-invertible).
如果矩阵的行列式为零，则其共轭矩阵将是奇异的（不可逆的）。
