honeycombed:/ˈhʌnikəʊmd/ (UK) /ˈhʌnikomd/ (US)| adj.|蜂窝状的；充满孔洞的|Resembling a honeycomb in structure; full of holes or cavities.|例句：The cliff face was honeycombed with caves.  悬崖面上布满了洞穴。

例句：The old wood was honeycombed by insects.  这块老木头被虫子蛀得满是洞。

|近义词：porous, pitted, cavernous|反义词:solid, compact|词性:形容词
