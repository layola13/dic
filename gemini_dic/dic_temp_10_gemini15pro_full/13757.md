audaciousness:/ɔːˈdeɪʃəsnəs/| n. |大胆；鲁莽；放肆|The quality of being daring and fearless, sometimes to the point of recklessness. |Her audaciousness in challenging the CEO surprised everyone. 她挑战CEO的大胆让每个人都感到惊讶。|Synonyms: boldness, daring, nerve, temerity, chutzpah|Antonyms: timidity, caution, meekness, hesitancy|noun

audacity:/ɔːˈdæsɪti/| n. |大胆；鲁莽；放肆|The willingness to take bold risks. |He had the audacity to interrupt the president. 他竟然胆敢打断总统讲话。|Synonyms: boldness, daring, nerve, temerity, chutzpah|Antonyms: timidity, caution, meekness, hesitancy|noun
