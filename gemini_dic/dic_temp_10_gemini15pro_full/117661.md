meh:/meɪ/ /mɛ/
interjection| 漠不关心的；无动于衷的；（表示不感兴趣或不兴奋）|An expression of indifference or boredom.  It suggests a lack of enthusiasm or interest.|"Meh," he shrugged, when asked about the movie. "It was okay."  他被问到电影怎么样时耸了耸肩说：“还行吧。”|近义词：so-so, indifferent, apathetic, unimpressed|反义词: enthusiastic, excited, impressed|词性:感叹词
词源:The word "meh" is of relatively recent origin, likely emerging in the late 20th century.  While its exact etymology is uncertain, it's thought to be influenced by Yiddish, where similar-sounding words express disinterest or displeasure.  It gained widespread popularity through its use on the animated television show "The Simpsons."
