airless:/ˈeərlɪs/| adj.|无空气的；不通风的；沉闷的|Lacking fresh air or ventilation; stuffy.|The airless room made it difficult to breathe. 这个不通风的房间让人难以呼吸。|Synonyms: stuffy, stifling, suffocating, close|Antonyms: airy, breezy, ventilated|adjective

Etymology->
From air + -less.  The suffix "-less" means "without" or "lacking." Thus, "airless" literally means "without air."
