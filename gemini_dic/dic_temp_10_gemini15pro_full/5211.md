alligatoring: /ˈælɪɡeɪtərɪŋ/
n.
龟裂；鳄鱼皮状裂纹
The formation of cracks in a surface, resembling the hide of an alligator.
The paint on the old car was alligatoring badly.  这辆旧车的油漆严重龟裂。
cracking, crazing, checking
smoothness
noun
