miniaturizations: /ˌmɪnɪətʃərəˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. |小型化，微型化|The process of making something much smaller than its usual size.  The act or process of miniaturizing something. |The miniaturizations of electronic components have revolutionized the computer industry. 电子元件的小型化彻底改变了计算机行业。|miniaturizing, shrinking, reducing | magnification, enlargement | noun
