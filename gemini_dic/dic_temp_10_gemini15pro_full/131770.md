nonconjugations:/ˌnɑːnkɑːndʒʊˈɡeɪʃənz/ | n. | 非共轭 |  Forms of verbs that do not show tense, person, or number.  These include infinitives, participles, and gerunds. |  The teacher emphasized the importance of understanding nonconjugations in English grammar. 老师强调了理解英语语法中非共轭的重要性。|  Learning nonconjugations is essential for mastering English verb forms. 学习非共轭对于掌握英语动词形式至关重要。 | synonyms: nonfinite verbs, verbals | antonyms: finite verbs | noun
