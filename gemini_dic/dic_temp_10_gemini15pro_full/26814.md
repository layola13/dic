cagelike:/ˈkeɪdʒˌlaɪk/
adjective
像笼子一样的；像牢笼一样的
Resembling a cage, especially in feeling confining or restrictive.
The small, windowless room felt cagelike.  这个小而无窗的房间感觉像笼子一样。
The bars of the balcony made the apartment feel cagelike. 阳台的栏杆让公寓感觉像笼子一样。
confining, restrictive, claustrophobic, imprisoning
open, spacious, airy, free
adjective
