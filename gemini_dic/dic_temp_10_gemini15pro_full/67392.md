extermination:/ɪkˌstɜːmɪˈneɪʃn/

v.|灭绝，根除，消灭|We need to exterminate the termites before they destroy the house. 我们需要在白蚁摧毁房子之前消灭它们。|例句：The government is trying to exterminate the invasive species. 政府正在努力消灭入侵物种。|近义词：eradicate, annihilate, destroy|反义词:preserve, conserve|词性:动词
