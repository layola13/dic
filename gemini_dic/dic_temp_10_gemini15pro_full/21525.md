bluebell:/ˈbluːbel/ | n. | 蓝铃花；风信子 | A perennial bulbous plant with bell-shaped blue flowers, native to Europe. |  The bluebells carpeted the woodland floor. 蓝铃花覆盖了林地。| synonym: harebell, campanula | antonym: N/A | noun

bluebell:/ˈbluːbel/ | n. | 蓝铃花色；天蓝色 | A pale purplish-blue colour. |  Her dress was a delicate shade of bluebell. 她的连衣裙是淡雅的天蓝色。| synonym: azure, sky-blue, periwinkle | antonym: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Old English 'blæc' (dark) + 'belle' (bell), referring to the shape and colour of the flower.  Later the 'blæc' element transformed to 'blue'.
