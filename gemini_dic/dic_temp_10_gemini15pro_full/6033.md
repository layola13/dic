amenability:/əˌmiːnəˈbɪləti/
n.
顺从；服从；可管理性
the quality of being open to suggestion or persuasion; willingness to cooperate.
The amenability of the staff made the project run smoothly.  员工的配合让项目进展顺利。
synonyms: receptiveness, responsiveness, tractability, compliance, cooperativeness
antonyms:  resistance, defiance, intractability, uncooperativeness
noun
