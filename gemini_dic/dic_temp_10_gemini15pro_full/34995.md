cloisterless:/ˈklɔɪstərlɪs/
adjective
无修道院的; 不受修道院限制的; 世俗的
Not confined to a cloister; secular.  Free from the seclusion or restrictions of a cloister.
He lived a cloisterless life, embracing the world and its experiences. 他过着不受修道院限制的生活，拥抱世界及其经历。
The cloisterless writer found inspiration in the bustling city streets.  这位不受修道院限制的作家在繁华的城市街道中找到了灵感。
近义词: secular, worldly, unrestrained, free
反义词: cloistered, monastic, secluded, confined
词性: adjective

词源:
From "cloister" (from Old French "cloistre," ultimately from Latin "claustrum" meaning "lock, bolt, barrier, enclosed place") + "-less" (a suffix meaning "without" or "lacking").
