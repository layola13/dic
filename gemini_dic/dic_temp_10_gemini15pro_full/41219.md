coral-like:/ˈkɔːrəlˌlaɪk/ |adj.|珊瑚状的；像珊瑚的|Resembling coral in appearance, form, or color.  Having a structure or texture similar to that of coral.|The intricate, coral-like formations in the cave were breathtaking.  洞穴里错综复杂的珊瑚状构造令人叹为观止。|The virus created coral-like growths on the host tissue. 病毒在宿主组织上形成了珊瑚状的生长物。|近义词：coralliferous, coralline, coralloid|反义词: smooth, flat|词性: 形容词

词源:  From "coral" + "-like". "Coral" comes from Old French "coral" and Latin "corallum," ultimately from Greek "korallion." "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
