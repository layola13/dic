midlife:/ˈmɪdˌlaɪf/ | n. adj.|中年，中年生活；中年的|The period of life from approximately 45 to 65 years of age.  指大约45岁到65岁这段人生时期|She's going through a bit of a midlife crisis. 她正经历中年危机。|Synonyms: middle age, mid-life |Antonyms: youth, old age |n.

midlife:/ˈmɪdˌlaɪf/ | adj.|中年的|Relating to or characteristic of middle age.  与中年有关的，中年的|He bought a sports car as a midlife crisis purchase. 他买了一辆跑车，这是他中年危机时期的购物。|Synonyms: middle-aged |Antonyms: young, elderly |adj.
