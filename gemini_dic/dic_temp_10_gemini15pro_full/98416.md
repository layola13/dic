insouciant:/ɪnˈsuːsiənt/ | adj. | 漫不经心的；无忧无虑的；漠不关心的 | Showing a casual lack of concern; indifferent. |  He strolled into the exam room with an insouciant air, as if it were just another day. 他漫不经心地走进考场，好像只是普通的一天一样。|  She met the news of her job loss with an insouciant shrug. 她对失业的消息耸了耸肩，一副漠不关心的样子。 | 近义词: nonchalant, carefree, unconcerned, indifferent | 反义词: anxious, worried, concerned, solicitous | 词性: 形容词

insouciance:/ɪnˈsuːsiəns/ | n. | 漫不经心；无忧无虑；漠不关心 |  The state of being insouciant. | His insouciance about the risks involved worried his parents. 他对所涉及风险的漫不经心让他的父母很担心。 |  Her insouciance in the face of danger was remarkable.  她在危险面前的漠不关心令人吃惊。 | 近义词: nonchalance, carelessness, indifference | 反义词: anxiety, worry, concern, solicitude | 词性: 名词
