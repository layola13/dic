impresario:/ɪmprɛˈsɑːrioʊ/ /ɪmprɛˈsɑːrɪəʊ/
|n.|演出承办人；经理人；主办者|A person who organizes and often finances concerts, plays, or operas.|The impresario brought the famous opera singer to town for a performance.  这位演出承办人把著名的歌剧演唱家带到镇上演出。|Example: The impresario was renowned for discovering new talent.  这位经理人以发掘新 talent 而闻名。|Synonyms: manager, producer, promoter, director, organizer|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps employee or subordinate)|noun
