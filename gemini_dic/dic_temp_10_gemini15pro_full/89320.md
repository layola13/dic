homogeneousness:/ˌhoʊmoʊdʒɪˈniːɪtnəs/ |n.|同质性，同种性，均匀性|The homogeneousness of the sample ensured accurate results. 样品的同质性确保了结果的准确性。|The mixture lacked homogeneousness, with clumps of undissolved material. 这种混合物缺乏均匀性，有一些未溶解的物质块。|近义词：uniformity, consistency, homogeneity|反义词：heterogeneity, diversity, variety|词性:名词

Etymology:
From homogeneous + -ness.  Homogeneous comes from the Late Latin homogēneus, from Greek homogenēs, from homos "same" + genos "kind, race."
