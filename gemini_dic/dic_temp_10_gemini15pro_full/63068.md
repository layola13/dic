enrolment:/ɪnˈrəʊlmənt/ (UK) /ɪnˈroʊlmənt/ (US)| n.|入学，注册，登记|The act of enrolling or being enrolled. |例句：Enrolment at the university has increased this year. 今年大学的入学人数增加了。

例句：The enrolment process was quick and easy. 注册过程快速简便。

近义词：registration, admission, signup|反义词: withdrawal, cancellation|词性:noun


enrol:/ɪnˈrəʊl/ (UK) /ɪnˈroʊl/ (US)| v.|注册，登记，入学|To officially register as a member of an institution or for a course. |例句：I enrolled in the online course. 我注册了在线课程。

例句：He enrolled his daughter in a private school. 他让女儿在一所私立学校注册。

近义词：register, sign up, join|反义词: withdraw, unregister|词性:verb
