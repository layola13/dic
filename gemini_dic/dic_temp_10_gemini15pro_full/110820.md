lino:/ˈliːnoʊ/ /ˈlaɪnoʊ/
n.
linoleum
油毡，油地毡
Linoleum is a floor covering made from solidified linseed oil, pine resin, ground cork dust, wood flour, and pigments.
油毡是一种用凝固的亚麻籽油、松树脂、磨碎的软木粉、木粉和颜料制成的地板覆盖物。
The kitchen floor was covered in worn lino.
厨房地板铺着破旧的油毡。
近义词: linoleum
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps carpet, wood flooring)
词性: noun
