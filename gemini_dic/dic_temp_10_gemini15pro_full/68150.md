failest:/ˈfeɪlɪst/| v.|你最失败 (第二人称单数，现在时)|You failest in this endeavor.  This is the archaic second person singular present tense form of "fail." It means "you fail" in modern English. |例句：Thou failest to grasp the gravity of the situation. 你未能理解情况的严重性。

例句：Thou failest to see the truth. 你没有看到真相。


|近义词：fall short, miss, blunder|反义词:succeed, triumph|词性:动词
