abstractness:/ˈæbstræktnɪs/ |n.|抽象性，抽象度|The abstractness of the concept made it difficult to grasp. 这个概念的抽象性使它难以理解。|The artist explored the abstractness of time in his paintings. 这位艺术家在他的绘画中探索了时间的抽象性。|近义词：abstraction, generality, vagueness|反义词：concreteness, specificity, particularity|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From abstract + -ness.  Abstract comes from Late Latin abstractus, past participle of abstrahere "to draw away," from Latin abs-, variant of ab- "away from" + trahere "to draw."
