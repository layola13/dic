dichlamydeous:/ˌdaɪkləˈmɪdiəs/
| adj. |双被的，具有双层花被的|Having two whorls of floral envelopes in the perianth, namely, both calyx and corolla.|The rose is a dichlamydeous flower, possessing both sepals and petals.|近义词:  bipetalous, dichlamydic|反义词: monochlamydeous, achlamydeous|词性: adjective

词源: From Greek *di-* (two) + *chlamys* (cloak, mantle) + *-ous* (adjectival suffix).
