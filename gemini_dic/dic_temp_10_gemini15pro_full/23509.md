braille:/breɪl/| n. adj.|盲文；布莱叶盲文|A system of writing for the blind, using raised dots that can be read with the fingers.  It is named after its inventor, Louis Braille.|例句：The book was printed in braille. 这本书是用盲文印刷的。

例句：She learned to read braille when she was five. 她五岁时学会了阅读盲文。|近义词：blind writing|反义词:print (印刷字体)|词性:名词, 形容词
