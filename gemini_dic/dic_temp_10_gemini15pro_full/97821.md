inkiness:/ˈɪŋkɪnɪs/
noun
墨黑，漆黑
The quality or state of being inky; blackness.
The inkiness of the night sky was breathtaking. 夜空的墨黑令人叹为观止。
synonyms: blackness, darkness, murkiness, obscurity
antonyms: brightness, lightness, paleness
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From inky + -ness.  Inky from ink + -y.  Ink from Old French "enque," from Late Latin "encaustum," from Greek "enkauston" (burned in), neuter of "enkaustos."
