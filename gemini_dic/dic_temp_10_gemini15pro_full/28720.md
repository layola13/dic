carmines:/ˈkɑːrmɪnz/ /ˈkɑːrmaɪnz/| n. |胭脂红; 深红色|A vivid crimson or scarlet pigment made from cochineal.  A rich red color.|The artist used carmines to paint the sunset.|近义词：crimson, scarlet, vermilion|反义词: (No direct antonym for a color, could consider contrasting colors like) cyan, teal|noun
carmine:/ˈkɑːrmɪn/ /ˈkɑːrmaɪn/| adj. |胭脂红的; 深红色的|Of a vivid crimson or scarlet color.|She wore a carmine dress to the gala.|近义词：crimson, scarlet, vermilion|反义词: (No direct antonym for a color, could consider contrasting colors like) cyan, teal|adjective
