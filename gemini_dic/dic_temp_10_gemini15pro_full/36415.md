cokenay:/ˈkəʊkni/| n. adj.|伦敦东区人，伦敦东区方言|A native or inhabitant of the East End of London; also, the dialect or accent traditionally spoken there.  A Cockney is traditionally defined as someone born within earshot of the Bow Bells (the bells of St. Mary-le-Bow church in Cheapside).|例句：He spoke with a broad Cockney accent. 他说话带着浓重的伦敦东区口音。

例句：She's a true Cockney, born and bred in the East End. 她是一个地道的伦敦东区人，在东区出生和长大。|近义词：East Ender, Londoner|反义词:None (lacks a direct antonym)|词性: Noun, Adjective
