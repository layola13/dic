housewifelinesses:/ˈhaʊswɪflɪnɪsɪz/
|n.|家庭主妇气质，贤妻良母的特质|The qualities traditionally associated with a housewife, such as domestic skills, nurturing, and homemaking.|She exuded an air of housewifeliness. 她散发着一种贤妻良母的气质。|近义词：domesticity, homemaking skills|反义词：career-mindedness|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From housewife + -li- + -ness.  Housewife (from house + wife) refers to a married woman whose main occupation is caring for her family and home. The suffix "-li-" forms adjectives, and "-ness" forms nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition. Thus, "housewifeliness" represents the state or quality of being a housewife.
