aphorize:/ˈæfəˌraɪz/| v. |格言化；用格言表达|To express a general truth or observation in a concise and witty manner. | He aphorized the lesson of the story in a single sentence. 他用一句话概括了故事的寓意。| synonyms: epitomize, summarize, encapsulate | antonyms: expand, elaborate, detail | verb

aphorizer:/ˈæfəˌraɪzər/ | n. |格言家；用格言表达的人|A person who expresses themselves in aphorisms. |  The philosopher was a known aphorizer, his works filled with pithy sayings. 这位哲学家是一位著名的格言家，他的作品充满了精辟的格言。 | synonyms: sage, maxim writer | antonyms:  N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *aphorizāre, from Greek ἀφορίζειν (aphorízein, “to define, delimit, separate”), from ἀπό (apó, “from, away from”) + ὁρίζειν (horízein, “to bound, limit”).
