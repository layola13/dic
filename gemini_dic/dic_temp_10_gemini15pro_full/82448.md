grumbling:/ˈɡrʌmblɪŋ/ | n. |抱怨，牢骚 |the expression of dissatisfaction or protest|The constant grumbling of the customers annoyed the staff.  顾客不断的抱怨惹恼了员工。|近义词：complaining, whining, moaning|反义词：praising, complimenting|词性:名词

grumble:/ˈɡrʌmbl/ | v. |抱怨，发牢骚|to complain in a bad-tempered way|He grumbled about the cold weather. 他抱怨天气冷。|近义词：complain, whine, moan|反义词：praise, compliment|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *grumbeln, *gromel, from Old French grommeler (“to grumble, mutter”), of imitative origin.
