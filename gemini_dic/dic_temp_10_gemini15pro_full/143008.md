outbellowing: /ˌaʊtˈbɛloʊɪŋ/
verb
大声吼叫，胜过…的吼叫
To shout louder than someone or something else.
The captain outbellowed the storm, his voice carrying above the wind and waves. 船长的声音盖过了风暴，他的声音响彻风浪。
The angry protesters outbellowed the police, their chants echoing through the streets.  愤怒的抗议者们的声音盖过了警察，他们的口号在街道上回荡。
近义词: outshout, drown out
反义词: whisper, murmur
词性: verb

Etymology:
From out- + bellow.  "Out-" acts as a prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing something else. "Bellow" means to emit a loud, deep roar.
