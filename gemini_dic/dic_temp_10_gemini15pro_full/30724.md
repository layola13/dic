cerusite:/ˈsɛːrʊsaɪt/ /ˈsɪrəsaɪt/ | n. |白铅矿|A mineral consisting of lead carbonate (PbCO3), typically occurring as colourless or white crystals and being an important ore of lead. |Cerusite is a common secondary mineral found in oxidized lead deposits.|近义词：lead carbonate, white lead ore|反义词: None (as it's a specific mineral)|词性:noun
