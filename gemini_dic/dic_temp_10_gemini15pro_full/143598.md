outinfluence:/ˌaʊtˈɪnfluəns/
verb
超过…的影响力；比…更有影响力
To have more influence than someone or something else.
The new CEO quickly outinfluenced his predecessor, shaping the company's direction in innovative ways. 新任首席执行官迅速超越了其前任的影响力，以创新的方式塑造了公司的发展方向。

The social media campaign outinfluenced traditional advertising methods, reaching a wider audience and generating more engagement. 社交媒体宣传活动的影响力超过了传统的广告方法，覆盖了更广泛的受众，并产生了更多的互动。

近义词: surpass, exceed, overshadow, dominate
反义词: be overshadowed by, be outdone by
词性: verb
