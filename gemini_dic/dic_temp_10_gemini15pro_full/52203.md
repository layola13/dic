diastem:/daɪˈæstəm/| n.|间隙，空隙; (解剖学)齿隙; (语音学)分音|A gap or space, especially between two teeth. In phonetics, the pronunciation of a vowel with a slight separation of the tongue from the roof of the mouth, resulting in a more open sound.|The child has a noticeable diastem between her two front teeth.  这个孩子的两颗门牙之间有一个明显的牙缝。|Synonyms: gap, space, interval, aperture, cleft, fissure|Antonyms: closure, bridge, connection|noun
