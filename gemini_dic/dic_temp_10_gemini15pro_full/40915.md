cooler:/ˈkuːlər/ (US) /ˈkuːlə(r)/ (UK)| n. adj.|冷却器；冷藏箱；(非正式)监狱|A container filled with ice for cooling food and drinks; also used informally to refer to a jail or prison.|We brought a cooler full of drinks to the beach. 我们带了一个装满饮料的冷藏箱去海滩。|近义词:refrigerator, ice chest, chiller, (for jail) prison, lockup|反义词:heater, warmer|词性:名词, 形容词

cooler:/ˈkuːlər/ (US) /ˈkuːlə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更凉爽的；更冷静的|Relatively low in temperature; calmer; less excited.|The evening air was cooler than the afternoon. 晚上的空气比下午凉爽。|近义词:chillier, colder, calmer|反义词:hotter, warmer, more excited|词性:形容词
