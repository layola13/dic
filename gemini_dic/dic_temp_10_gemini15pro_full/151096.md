parascenium:/ˌpærəˈsiːniəm/
n.|舞台前部|The part of a stage in front of the curtain; the proscenium.|The actors took a bow on the parascenium after the final curtain.|近义词:apron, forestage, proscenium|反义词:backstage|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *parascaenium, from Ancient Greek παρασκήνιον (paraskḗnion, “backstage area”), from παρά (pará, “beside”) + σκήνη (skḗnē, “stage”).  Doublet of parascenia.
