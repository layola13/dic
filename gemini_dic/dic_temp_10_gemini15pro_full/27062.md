calescence:/kæˈlɛsəns/
n.
升温，发热
The process of becoming warm; increasing in temperature.
The calescence of the metal during forging made it malleable.  金属在锻造过程中升温使其具有延展性。

近义词:heating, warming
反义词:cooling, refrigeration
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *calescere, from calēre "to be warm."  源自拉丁语 *calescere，来自 calēre “变暖”。
