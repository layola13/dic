maltier:/ˈmɔːltiər/ | adj. | 更麦芽的；麦芽味更浓的 | Having a stronger flavor of malt; more like malt. | This beer is maltier than the one I had last week. 这款啤酒比我上周喝的麦芽味更浓。 |The bread had a pleasantly maltier flavor.  这面包有一种令人愉悦的更浓郁的麦芽味。| Near Synonyms: richer, more flavorful, nuttier | Antonyms: less malty, blander | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From malt + -ier (comparative suffix).  "Malt" comes from Old English "mealt,"  meaning "melted, softened," referring to the process of germinating grain.
