cocounseled:/ˌkoʊˈkaʊnsəld/
verb
共同担任法律顾问；共同咨询
acted as cocounsel in the case
在此案中担任共同法律顾问
例句：They cocounseled on the complex litigation. 他们在复杂的诉讼中共同担任法律顾问。

例句：She cocounseled with a senior partner on the merger. 她与一位资深合伙人就合并事宜共同咨询。

近义词：collaborated, worked together, jointly advised
反义词：worked alone, independently advised
词性:v.
