necrophagan: /ˈnɛkrəˌfeɪɡən/ | n. | 食腐动物; 腐生生物; 以尸体为食的生物 |  A necrophage is an organism that feeds on dead or decaying animal flesh.  It comes from the Greek words "nekros" (corpse) and "phagein" (to eat). | Vultures are necrophages that play an important role in the ecosystem. 秃鹰是食腐动物，在生态系统中扮演着重要的角色。 |The burying beetle is a necrophage that buries small carcasses to feed its larvae.  埋葬甲虫是一种食腐动物，它会埋葬小尸体来喂养幼虫。 | synonyms: scavenger, carrion-eater | antonyms: predator, herbivore | noun
