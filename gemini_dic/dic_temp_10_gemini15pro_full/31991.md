chemotherapists: /ˌkiːmoʊˈθɛrəpɪsts/ | n. | 化疗师，化疗专家 | A medical professional specializing in the administration and management of chemotherapy. | The chemotherapists explained the treatment plan to the patient. 化疗师向病人解释了治疗方案。| The chemotherapists monitored the patient's vital signs during the infusion. 化疗师在输液过程中监测病人的生命体征。 | Synonyms: oncologist (肿瘤学家, 涵盖更广), cancer specialist (癌症专家) | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps alternative medicine practitioner 可作为相对的概念) | noun
