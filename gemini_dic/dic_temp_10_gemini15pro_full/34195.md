clamper:/ˈklæmpər/| n.|夹子，钳子；[电子]箝位电路|A device that holds or fastens something with a clamp. clamper:/ˈklæmpər/| v.|夹紧，固定；[电子]箝位|To fasten or hold something with a clamp. To limit the voltage or current in a circuit to a certain level.|He clamped the papers together so they wouldn't blow away. 他把文件夹在一起，这样它们就不会被吹走了。|近义词：clamp, fasten, secure|反义词:release, loosen|verb
