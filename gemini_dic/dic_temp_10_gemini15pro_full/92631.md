Simple Present:
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she study at university? 她在大学里学什么专业？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? 他晚饭在做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done to help? 她做了些什么来帮忙？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading all day? 他一整天都在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do last night? 你昨晚做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? 她对这部电影说了些什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 开会的时候她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you arrived? 你来之前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? 她生病之前吃了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I interrupted you?  在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major?  在她换专业之前她一直在学什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后打算做什么？
What will she bring to the party? 她会带什么来参加聚会？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be studying next semester? 她下学期会学什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will she have learned by the time she finishes the course?  到她完成课程时她将学到什么？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for five years by then? 到那时你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying for by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时她将已经学习什么多久了？
