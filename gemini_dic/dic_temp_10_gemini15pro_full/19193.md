bewitchingly:/bɪˈwɪtʃɪŋli/| adv.|迷人地，令人着迷地|In a manner that is enchanting and captivating, as if by magic.|She smiled bewitchingly at him. 她对他妩媚一笑。|近义词：charmingly, enchantingly, alluringly, captivatingly|反义词：repulsively, unattractively|词性:副词

Etymology:
From bewitching + -ly.  Bewitching comes from the verb "bewitch," from be- + witch. "Be-" is a prefix meaning "thoroughly" or "completely," and "witch" refers to a person who practices witchcraft or magic.  Therefore, "bewitch" means to affect as if by magic.
