loos:/luːz/
n.
厕所，洗手间
A British term for a toilet, especially one in a public place.
例句：
Excuse me, where are the loos?
打扰一下，请问洗手间在哪里？

I need to use the loos.
我需要用一下洗手间。

近义词：toilet, restroom, bathroom, lavatory, WC
反义词:  (loos没有直接的反义词)
词性:名词
