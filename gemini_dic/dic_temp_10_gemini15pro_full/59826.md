ectoblast:/ˈɛktəˌblæst/ | n. | 外胚层 | The outermost of the three primary germ layers of an embryo, from which the epidermis, nervous system, and sense organs develop. | The ectoblast gives rise to the skin and nervous system. 外胚层会发育形成皮肤和神经系统。|  The neural tube is formed from the ectoblast. 神经管由外胚层形成。 | synonyms: epiblast | antonyms: endoblast, hypoblast, mesoblast | noun
