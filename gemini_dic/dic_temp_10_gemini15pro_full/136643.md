nonschooling:/nɑːnˈskuːlɪŋ/
noun
非学校教育；在家教育
The practice of educating children outside of the traditional school system, typically at home by parents or tutors.
Many families are choosing nonschooling as an alternative to traditional education. 许多家庭选择非学校教育作为传统教育的替代方案。
Homeschooling often follows a curriculum, while nonschooling allows children to learn through self-directed exploration.  在家上学通常遵循课程，而非学校教育则允许儿童通过自主探索来学习。
近义词: homeschooling, unschooling, deschooling
反义词: schooling, traditional education
词性: noun

Etymology: non- (prefix meaning "not") + schooling (noun meaning "education in a school")
