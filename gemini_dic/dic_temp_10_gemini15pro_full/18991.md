bestowals:/bɪˈstoʊəlz/| n. |授予，赠与，给予(bestowal的复数形式)|The bestowals of honors and awards recognize outstanding achievements.  授予荣誉和奖项是对杰出成就的认可。|例句：The king's bestowals of land and titles created a new aristocracy. 国王的土地和头衔的授予创造了一个新的贵族阶级。

例句：The annual bestowals of scholarships help many students afford college. 每年颁发的奖学金帮助许多学生支付大学学费。|近义词：gifts, grants, awards, donations|反义词：denials, refusals, withholdings|词性:名词
