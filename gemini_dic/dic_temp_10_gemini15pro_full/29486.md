cataphysical:/ˌkætəˈfɪzɪkəl/
adjective
超自然的，形而上的
relating to metaphysics or the realm of ultimate reality, often spiritual or transcendental.
例句：Cataphysical experiences are often described as mystical or otherworldly. 超自然体验通常被描述为神秘的或超凡脱俗的。

例句：His cataphysical beliefs led him to explore various spiritual practices. 他的形而上信仰使他探索各种精神实践。
近义词：metaphysical, transcendental, supernatural, spiritual
反义词：physical, material, mundane, earthly
词性: adjective
