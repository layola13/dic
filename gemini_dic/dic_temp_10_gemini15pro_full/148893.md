overwhelmingness:/ˌoʊvərˈwelmɪŋnəs/| n. |势不可挡；压倒性；|The overwhelmingness of the task ahead made her feel paralyzed.  面前任务的艰巨性让她感到不知所措。|近义词：intensity, immensity, vastness|反义词:insignificance, triviality|Noun

Etymology->
From overwhelming + -ness.  Overwhelming itself comes from the verb overwhelm, which traces back to the Middle English *overwhelmen*, from over- + whelmen "to turn over."
