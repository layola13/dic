autochronograph:/ˌɔːtəˈkrɒnəɡræf/ |n.|自动计时码表；自动计时器|A watch that records the time of a happening, especially the start and finish of a race, and also shows the ordinary time.|The autochronograph started as the cars crossed the finish line.|近义词:chronograph, stopwatch, timer|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly clock which only tells current time)|noun
