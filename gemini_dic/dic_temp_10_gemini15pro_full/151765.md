pasigraphical:/ˌpæsɪˈɡræfɪkəl/| adj.|以图形文字表达的；使用图形文字的|Relating to or denoting a system of writing that uses pictures or symbols to represent ideas or concepts, rather than a phonetic alphabet.|The ancient Egyptians used a pasigraphical system of writing known as hieroglyphics. 古埃及人使用一种称为象形文字的图形文字书写系统。|
The artist's work was highly pasigraphical, relying on symbols and imagery to convey complex emotions.  这位艺术家的作品非常图形化，依靠符号和图像来传达复杂的情感。|近义词:ideographic, symbolic, pictorial|反义词:alphabetic, phonetic|词性:形容词

词源: From Greek *pasigraphos* "writing for all," from *pan-* "all" + *graphe* "writing."  源自希腊语 *pasigraphos* “为所有人写作”，由 *pan-* “所有” + *graphe* “写作”组成。
