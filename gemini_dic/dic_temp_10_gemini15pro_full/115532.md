map-projection:/ˈmæp prəˈdʒɛkʃən/ | n. |地图投影|A systematic transformation of the latitudes and longitudes of locations from the surface of a sphere or an ellipsoid into locations on a plane.|Cartographers use different map projections to represent the Earth's surface on a flat map.  地图学家使用不同的地图投影将地球表面呈现在平面地图上。|The Mercator projection is commonly used for navigation. 墨卡托投影通常用于航海。| synonyms: cartographic projection | antonyms: globe | noun
