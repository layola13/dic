blebby:/ˈblebi/| adj.|肿胀的，松弛的|(of skin) puffy; flabby.|The skin around the wound was red and blebby. 伤口周围的皮肤又红又肿。|近义词：puffy, swollen, flabby|反义词:taut, firm|词性:形容词
