hypnum:/ˈhɪpnəm/| n. |羽藓属；青藓属|A genus of mosses belonging to the family Hypnaceae, commonly known as feather mosses or hypnum mosses.  These mosses are typically found in damp, shady environments and are characterized by their feathery or creeping growth habit.|The hypnum moss created a soft, green carpet on the forest floor.|近义词：moss, bryophyte|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
词源: From Latin *hypnum*, from Ancient Greek ὕπνος (húpnos, “sleep”).  Originally referred to a type of mossy plant thought to induce sleep.
