nonsanely:/nɒnˈseɪnli/
adverb
不理智地，疯狂地
Not in a sane or rational manner; insanely.
He acted nonsanely, driven by grief and rage. 他因悲伤和愤怒而行为疯狂。
He laughed nonsanely at the absurd situation.  他对着荒谬的场景疯狂地大笑。
近义词: insanely, madly, crazily, foolishly, irrationally
反义词: sanely, rationally, sensibly, wisely

词性
adverb
