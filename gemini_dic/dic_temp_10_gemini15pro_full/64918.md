established:/ɪˈstæblɪʃt/ | adj. | 已建立的，已确立的；被认可的 |  Having been in existence for a long time and therefore generally accepted or respected. |  The company is an established leader in the industry.  这家公司是业内公认的领导者。 |  recognized, accepted, proven |  new, unproven, emergent | adjective

established:/ɪˈstæblɪʃt/ | v. | 建立，创立；确立；证实 | To start or create an organization, system, etc. that is meant to last for a long time; to show something to be true. |  The business was established in 1822. 该企业成立于1822年。 |  found, create, set up, prove | abolish, destroy, disprove | verb
