maginot:/ˈmædʒɪnoʊ/ | n. | 马其诺防线 | A line of concrete fortifications, obstacles, and weapon installations built by France in the 1930s to deter invasion by Germany. |  The Maginot Line proved ineffective when Germany bypassed it through the Ardennes Forest.  马其诺防线在德国绕过阿登森林进攻时被证明是无效的。 |  Example: The Maginot Line was a symbol of French defensive strategy before World War II. 马其诺防线是二战前法国防御战略的象征。| synonyms: fortification, defense, barrier, rampart | antonyms: vulnerability, weakness | noun
