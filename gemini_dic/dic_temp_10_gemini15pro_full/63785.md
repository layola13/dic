epicurism: /ˌɛpɪˈkjʊərɪzəm/ | n. | 享乐主义，美食主义 | The pursuit of pleasure, especially of the senses; the philosophy of Epicurus.  It often refers to a love of fine food and drink. | He lived a life of epicurism, indulging in fine wines and gourmet meals. 他过着享乐主义的生活，沉迷于美酒和美食。|  Example:  His epicurism was evident in his extensive wine cellar and frequent dinner parties. 他的享乐主义体现在他丰富的酒窖和频繁的晚宴上。| Synonyms: hedonism, sensualism, indulgence | Antonyms: asceticism, abstinence, self-denial | noun
