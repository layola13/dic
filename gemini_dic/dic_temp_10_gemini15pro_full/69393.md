feeders:/ˈfiːdərz/ (US), /ˈfiːdəz/ (UK)| n.|食客;吃货|People who eat a lot or with enthusiasm.|He's one of the biggest feeders I know. 他是我认识的最能吃的人之一。|synonyms: eaters, diners, gourmands|antonyms: dieters, abstainers|noun
