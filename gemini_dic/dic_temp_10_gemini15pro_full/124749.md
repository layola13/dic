mraa:/ˈmrɑː/| n. | 低级硬件访问库 (Low Level Hardware Access library) |  A C/C++ library with Python bindings for accessing sensors, actuators, and other peripherals on embedded platforms like Intel Galileo, Edison, and other IoT devices. It provides an abstraction layer for interacting with hardware, simplifying development for various platforms. |  Example:  The developer used mraa to control the GPIO pins on the Intel Edison board. 开发者使用 mraa 控制英特尔 Edison 开发板上的 GPIO 引脚。

 Example:  Mraa made it easy to read data from the temperature sensor.  Mraa 使得从温度传感器读取数据变得容易。 | 近义词: WiringPi, pigpio, libgpiod | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but could be contrasted with higher-level frameworks that don't directly interact with hardware) | 词性: noun
