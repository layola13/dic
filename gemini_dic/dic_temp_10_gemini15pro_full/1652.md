acknowledge:/əkˈnɒlɪdʒ/ (UK) /əkˈnɑːlɪdʒ/ (US)| vt.|承认；确认；答谢|To accept or admit the truth or existence of something.  To express gratitude or appreciation for something.|例句：She acknowledged her mistake. 她承认了自己的错误。

He acknowledged the applause with a smile. 他微笑着回应掌声。|近义词：admit, recognize, accept, confirm, concede|反义词：deny, dispute, reject|词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He acknowledges the importance of education. = 他承认教育的重要性。
She acknowledges the receipt of the letter. = 她确认已收到信。

一般过去时:
They acknowledged their defeat. = 他们承认了失败。
He acknowledged having received the gift. = 他承认收到了礼物。


现在完成时:
The company has acknowledged the problem. = 公司已经承认了这个问题。
She has acknowledged his contribution to the project. = 她已经认可了他对这个项目的贡献。


过去完成时:
He had acknowledged his error before the meeting. = 他在会议前已经承认了他的错误。
They had acknowledged the risks involved. = 他们已经承认了所涉及的风险。


将来时:
I will acknowledge your email as soon as I receive it. = 我一收到你的邮件就会回复确认。
The winner will be acknowledged at the ceremony. = 获胜者将在仪式上被表彰。


虚拟语气:
If I were to win the lottery, I would acknowledge my family's support. = 如果我中了彩票，我会感谢家人的支持。
I wish he would acknowledge his mistakes. = 我希望他能承认自己的错误。


被动语态:
The importance of regular exercise is widely acknowledged. = 经常锻炼的重要性被广泛认可。
His contribution was acknowledged by the committee. = 他的贡献得到了委员会的认可。


疑问句:
Do you acknowledge the seriousness of the situation? = 你认识到情况的严重性了吗？
Did they acknowledge your complaint? = 他们承认你的投诉了吗？


否定句:
He did not acknowledge his guilt. = 他不承认自己的罪行。
She doesn't acknowledge the need for change. = 她不承认需要改变。
