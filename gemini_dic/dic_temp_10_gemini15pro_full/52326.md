diclinous:/daɪˈklaɪnəs/
adjective
雌雄异株的，单性花的
Having the male and female reproductive organs in separate flowers on separate plants.
The willow tree is a diclinous plant, meaning its male and female flowers grow on separate trees.  柳树是一种雌雄异株的植物，这意味着它的雄花和雌花生长在不同的树上。
Dioecious plants like hollies are diclinous. 冬青等雌雄异株的植物是雌雄异株的。
synonym: dioecious, unisexual
antonym: monoclinous, hermaphroditic
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From di- (two) + Greek kline (bed, couch)  + -ous (adjective suffix).  The "bed" or "couch" refers to the receptacle of the flower.
