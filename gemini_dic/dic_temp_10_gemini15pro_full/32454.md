chimneylike:/ˈtʃɪmniˌlaɪk/
adj.
像烟囱的
Resembling a chimney in shape or structure.
The chimneylike factory stacks belched black smoke into the air. 工厂像烟囱一样的烟囱向空中喷出黑烟。

The rock formation was chimneylike, rising high and narrow from the canyon floor.  岩层像烟囱一样，从峡谷底部高高耸立，狭窄。
近义词:columnar, towering, shaftlike
反义词:low, flat, spreading
词性:形容词
