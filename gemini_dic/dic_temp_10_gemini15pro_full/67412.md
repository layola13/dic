externally:/ɪkˈstɜːnəli/ /ɛkˈstɜːrnəli/| adv.|外部地，外在地，外表上|From the outside; on the outside.  Relating to the outside or outer part of something.|The wound was treated externally with an antiseptic cream.  伤口用抗菌药膏进行了外部处理。|近义词：outwardly, superficially, externally|反义词:internally, inwardly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *externaliter, from Latin externus (“outward”).
