elaborateness:/ɪˈlæbərɪtnəs/| n. | 复杂精细；精心制作；详尽|the quality of being elaborate or detailed|Her costumes are known for their elaborateness and intricate detail. 她的服装以其复杂精细和错综复杂的细节而闻名。|近义词：complexity, intricacy, sophistication, detail, ornateness|反义词：simplicity, plainness, austerity, crudeness|词性:noun

elaborate:/ɪˈlæbərət/| adj. | 复杂的；精细的；详尽的|involving many carefully arranged parts or details; detailed and complicated in design and planning|They had prepared an elaborate meal. 他们准备了一顿丰盛的大餐。|近义词：complex, intricate, sophisticated, detailed, ornate|反义词：simple, plain, austere, crude|词性:adjective

elaborate:/ɪˈlæbəreɪt/| v. | 详细阐述；精心制作|to add more detail to something; to develop or present (a theory, policy, or system) in detail|Could you elaborate on that point, please? 请你详细阐述一下那点吧？|近义词：expand on, expound, develop, flesh out, detail|反义词：simplify, condense, summarize, abbreviate|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin elaboratus, past participle of elaborare "to work out," from Latin e- "out" + laborare "to work," from labor "work."
