igloo:/ˈɪɡluː/| n.|圆顶冰屋|A dome-shaped house or shelter built of blocks of hard snow, typically by the Inuit people of northern Canada and Greenland.|The Inuit families built igloos to shelter from the harsh winter weather. 因纽特人家庭建造冰屋以躲避严酷的冬季天气。|近义词：snow house, snow hut|反义词:house, building|名词
