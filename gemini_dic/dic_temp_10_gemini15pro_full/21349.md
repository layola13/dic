bloop:/bluːp/| n. | 哔声; 滴声; 冒泡声 | A short, low-pitched sound, often made by electronic equipment or bubbles.  | Example 1: The old computer made a bloop sound every time it started up. 老式电脑每次启动都会发出哔的一声。

Example 2: I heard a bloop as the air bubble rose to the surface of the water. 我听到一个冒泡声，当气泡升到水面时。 | Near Synonyms: blip, beep, plop |Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a sound, consider silence, quiet) | Noun
