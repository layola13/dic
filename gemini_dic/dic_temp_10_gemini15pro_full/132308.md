nondefilements:/ˌnɒndɪˈfaɪlmənts/ | n. | 无垢，清净；(佛教) 戒 | Absence of defilements; purity, especially in a spiritual or moral sense.  Specifically in Buddhism, freedom from mental impurities or defilements (kleshas). |  His teachings focused on achieving nondefilements through meditation and mindful living. 他的教导集中于通过冥想和正念生活来达到无垢之境。| 近义词: purity, immaculacy, cleanliness, sanctity | 反义词: defilements, impurities, corruption, sin | 词性: 名词

词源:  "Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "defilement" (from Old French "defouler" meaning "to trample down").
