ghoulishness:/ˈɡuːlɪʃnəs/
|n.|令人毛骨悚然; 可怕; 阴森森|The ghoulishness of the scene shocked everyone.  现场的恐怖景象震惊了所有人。| The movie's ghoulishness was a bit too much for the young children. 这部电影的恐怖程度对小孩子来说有点过分了。 |gruesomeness, macabre quality, creepiness|pleasantness, cheerfulness|noun


Etymology:
From ghoul + -ish + -ness.  Ghoul comes from Arabic غول (ġūl), a desert demon believed to rob graves and feed on corpses.
