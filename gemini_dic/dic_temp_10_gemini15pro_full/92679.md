ice-skated:/ˈaɪsˌskeɪtɪd/
verb
滑冰
past tense and past participle of "ice-skate": to move on ice wearing ice skates.
She ice-skated gracefully across the frozen pond. 她优雅地在结冰的池塘上滑冰。
He had ice-skated competitively for many years. 他参加竞技滑冰很多年了。
近义词: skated
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "walked")
词性: verb
