epicenism:/ɪˈpɪsənɪzəm/ /ˈɛpɪˌsiːnɪzəm/
n.|雌雄同体；两性兼具；无性|The state or condition of having only one set of grammatical genders, typically masculine or feminine, used for all nouns.  A system of grammar in which nouns and pronouns do not distinguish between male and female genders.|The French language exhibits epicenism, using the same articles and adjectives for both masculine and feminine nouns referring to people.|近义词：common gender, gender neutrality|反义词：gender distinction|词性:名词
