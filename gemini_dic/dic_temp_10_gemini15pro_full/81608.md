greatish:/ˈɡreɪtɪʃ/| adj.|相当好的，还不错的|Moderately great; fairly good.|例句：The food was greatish. 食物还不错。|近义词：pretty good, fairly good, quite good, decent, acceptable|反义词:awful, terrible, bad, poor|词性:形容词
词源：great + -ish (suffix indicating somewhat, rather).  From "great" meaning large or important, with the suffix "-ish" added to indicate a lesser degree.
