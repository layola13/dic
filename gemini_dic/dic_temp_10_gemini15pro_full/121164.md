misdiagnosing:/ˌmɪsˌdaɪəɡˈnoʊzɪŋ/
|v.|误诊|The act of diagnosing incorrectly; making a wrong diagnosis.|Doctors were accused of misdiagnosing patients’ illnesses. 医生被指控误诊了病人的病情。|misjudging, misinterpreting|correctly diagnosing|verb (present participle)

misdiagnosis:/ˌmɪsˌdaɪəɡˈnoʊsɪs/
|n.|误诊|An incorrect diagnosis.|The misdiagnosis led to inappropriate treatment. 误诊导致了不恰当的治疗。|mistake, error|correct diagnosis|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (wrongly) + diagnosing (present participle of diagnose).  Diagnose comes from the Greek dia- (through) + gignōskein (to know).
