nonjuridical:/ˌnɒnjuˈrɪdɪkəl/
adjective
非法律的；非司法性的
Not related to law or jurisprudence; outside the scope of legal proceedings.
This organization handles nonjuridical disputes between neighbors.  这个组织处理邻居间的非法律纠纷。
The community established a nonjuridical council to resolve internal conflicts. 社区建立了一个非法律委员会来解决内部冲突。

近义词: extralegal, nonlegal, informal
反义词: juridical, legal

词性: adjective
词源:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "juridical" (from Latin "iuridicus" meaning "pertaining to law").
