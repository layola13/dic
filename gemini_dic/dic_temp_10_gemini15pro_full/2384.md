adipolysis:/ˌædɪˈpɒlɪsɪs/
noun
脂肪分解
The breakdown of fats and other lipids by hydrolysis to release fatty acids.
Exercise promotes adipolysis.  运动促进脂肪分解。

Increased adipolysis is a common side effect of this medication. 这种药物的常见副作用是脂肪分解增加。

近义词: lipolysis, fat breakdown
反义词: lipogenesis, fat synthesis
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From adipo- (relating to fat) + -lysis (decomposition).  Adipo- comes from Greek *lipos* meaning "fat." -lysis comes from Greek *lusis* meaning "loosening" or "dissolution."
