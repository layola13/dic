outsiderish:/ˌaʊtˈsaɪdərɪʃ/| adj.|局外人的；不合群的；像个局外人的|Characteristic of or resembling an outsider; feeling or appearing detached or excluded from a group. | He had an outsiderish quality that made it hard for him to fit in. 他有一种不合群的气质，让他很难融入。|She always felt outsiderish at these corporate events. 她总觉得在这些公司活动中格格不入。|近义词：alienated, detached, excluded, unsociable, withdrawn|反义词：integrated, included, sociable, gregarious, belonging|词性:形容词
