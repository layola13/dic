decalled:/diːˈkæld/
verb
转移印花;贴花;移印的
Transferred a design or image onto a surface.
The model airplane was decalled with the airline's logo.  这架模型飞机贴上了航空公司的标志。
decorated, adorned, transferred
plain, undecorated
v.
