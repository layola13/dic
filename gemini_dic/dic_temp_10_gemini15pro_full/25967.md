burrowers:/ˈbʌrəʊərz (UK), ˈbɜːroʊərz (US)| n.|穴居动物；挖洞者|Animals that dig burrows, often for shelter or to find food.|例句：The burrowers had created a complex network of tunnels beneath the ground. 穴居动物在地下建造了一个复杂的隧道网络。

例句：Many desert animals are burrowers to escape the intense heat. 许多沙漠动物都是穴居动物，以躲避酷热。|近义词：diggers, excavators, tunnelers|反义词:surface dwellers|词性:名词
