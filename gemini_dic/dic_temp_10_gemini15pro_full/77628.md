gelable:/ˈdʒɛləbəl/
adjective
可凝胶的，能胶化的
Capable of forming a gel.
This substance is gelable when cooled.  这种物质冷却后会胶化。
The pectin is gelable and used in jams.  果胶可以胶化，用于制作果酱。
近义词:gelling, coagulable
反义词:ungelable, non-gelling
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From gel + -able.  From the verb "gel" (to form a gel) plus the suffix "-able" which denotes capability or possibility.
