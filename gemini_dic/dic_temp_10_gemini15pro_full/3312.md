affluent:/ˈæfluːənt/ | adj. | 富裕的，丰富的 | Having a lot of money or possessions; wealthy.  Characterized by abundance. |  She lives in an affluent neighborhood. 她住在富人区。|  The river is affluent with fish. 这条河鱼很多。 | synonyms: rich, wealthy, prosperous, opulent | antonyms: poor, impoverished, destitute | adjective


affluent:/əˈfluːənt/ | n. | 支流 | A stream or river that flows into a larger one. | The Missouri River is an affluent of the Mississippi. 密苏里河是密西西比河的支流。 | synonyms: tributary, branch | antonyms: main stream | noun
