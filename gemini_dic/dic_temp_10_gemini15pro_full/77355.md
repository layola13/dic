gaudy:/ˈɡɔːdi/ | adj. | 华丽的，俗丽的；花哨的 | Excessively showy or brightly colored, typically in a way that is considered tasteless.  |  The dress was so gaudy that it was almost painful to look at.  这件衣服太俗艳了，几乎让人不忍直视。 |  She wore a gaudy outfit to the party, complete with a feathered boa and sparkly tiara. 她穿着一套花哨的服装去参加派对，还配了一条羽毛围巾和闪闪发光的头饰。 | synonyms:  garish, flashy, ostentatious, flamboyant | antonyms:  understated, subtle, elegant, refined | word type: adjective
