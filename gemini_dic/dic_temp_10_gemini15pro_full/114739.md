malnutrition:/ˌmælnuˈtrɪʃən/ | n. | 营养不良 |  lack of proper nutrition, caused by not having enough to eat, not eating enough of the right things, or being unable to use the food that one does eat. | Many children in developing countries suffer from malnutrition. 许多发展中国家的儿童营养不良。 | synonyms: undernourishment, starvation, famine | antonyms: nourishment,  nutritiousness | noun


malnutrition:/ˌmælnuˈtrɪʃən/ | n. | 营养失调 |  A state of poor nutrition, often caused by a lack of essential nutrients in the diet. | The doctor diagnosed the child with malnutrition due to a severely restricted diet. 医生诊断出这名儿童由于饮食严重受限而营养不良。 | synonyms: dystrophy, undernutrition | antonyms:  eutrophy,  well-nourished | noun
