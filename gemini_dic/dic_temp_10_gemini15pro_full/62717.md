engirdle: /ɪnˈɡɜːrdl/ | vt. | 围绕；包围；环绕 | To surround; to encircle. |  例句：Mountains engirdle the valley. 群山环绕着山谷。

例句：A high wall engirdles the castle. 一堵高墙环绕着城堡。

近义词：encircle, surround, encompass, enclose | 反义词：expose, uncover | 词性：动词
