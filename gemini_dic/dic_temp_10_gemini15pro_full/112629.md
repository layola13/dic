low-lying:/ˈloʊˌlaɪɪŋ/ /ləʊˈlaɪɪŋ/| adj.|低洼的，地势低的|Situated close to the level of the sea or other body of water, and thus prone to flooding.|例句：The low-lying areas were flooded after the hurricane.  飓风过后，低洼地区被洪水淹没了。

例句：Low-lying coastal communities are particularly vulnerable to rising sea levels.  地势低洼的沿海社区尤其容易受到海平面上升的影响。|近义词：low, lowland, bottom,  |反义词:high, elevated, highland|词性:形容词
