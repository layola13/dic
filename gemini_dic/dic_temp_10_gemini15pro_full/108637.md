leady:/ˈledi/ /ˈliːdi/| adj.|铅色的；沉闷的；像铅的；含铅的|Resembling lead in color, dull gray; containing lead.|The sky was a leady gray. 天空是铅灰色的。|近义词：gray, dull, drab, leaden|反义词：bright, vibrant, colorful|词性:形容词

词源: From lead (n.) + -y (adj. suffix).  The word "lead" itself comes from Old English "lēad."
