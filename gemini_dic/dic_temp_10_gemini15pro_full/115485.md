manurance:/məˈnʊərəns/ |n.|施肥，肥料|The application of manure to soil to enhance its fertility.  The act of applying manure or fertilizers to land.|例句：The farmer practiced regular manurance to improve crop yields. 农民定期施肥以提高作物产量。

例句：Proper manurance is essential for healthy plant growth.  适当的施肥对植物健康生长至关重要。|近义词：fertilization, fertilizing|反义词:None|词性:名词


词源:  From Anglo-French *manurance, from Old French manure, from manuvrer “to cultivate by hand,” from Latin manu operari “to work with the hand,” from manu, ablative of manus “hand” + operari “to work.”
