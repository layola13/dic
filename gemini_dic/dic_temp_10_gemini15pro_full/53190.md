dipstick:/ˈdɪpstɪk/| n. |油尺；量油尺；试纸|A graduated rod for measuring the depth of a liquid, especially oil in a vehicle's engine.|例句：The dipstick showed that the oil level was low. 油尺显示油位低。

例句：He checked the oil level with the dipstick. 他用油尺检查了油位。|近义词：gauge, indicator, measuring rod|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
词源:dip (v.) + stick (n.)  "dip" meaning to immerse briefly, and "stick" referring to a slender piece of wood.  Essentially, a stick that is dipped.
