cosmonauts: /ˈkɒzmənɔːts/ (UK) /ˈkɑːzmənɔːts/ (US) | n. | 宇航员（尤指苏联或俄罗斯的）| A traveler in space; specifically : a Soviet or Russian astronaut. |  Yuri Gagarin was the first cosmonaut to orbit the Earth. 尤里·加加林是第一个绕地球飞行的宇航员。| synonyms: astronaut, space traveler | antonyms: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Russian "kosmonavt," a blend of "kosmo" (cosmos) and "navt" (navigator).  Ultimately from Greek "kosmos" (universe, order) and "nautes" (sailor).
