autocracy: /ɔːˈtɒkrəsi/ (UK) /ɔːˈtɑːkrəsi/ (US)| n. |独裁; 独裁统治; 独裁政府; |A system of government by one person with absolute power. |例句：The country was ruled by autocracy for decades.  这个国家被独裁统治了几十年。

例句：The autocracy suppressed all dissent.  独裁政府压制所有异议。|近义词：dictatorship, tyranny, despotism |反义词：democracy, republic |词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *autocratia*, from Ancient Greek αὐτοκρατία (autokratía, “absolute rule, autocracy”), from αὐτός (autós, “self”) + κράτος (krátos, “strength, power”).
