dewlapped:/ˈdjuːlæpt/ | adj. | 有垂肉的，垂肉发达的 | Having a dewlap; characterized by a dewlap.  A dewlap is a loose fold of skin hanging from the neck or throat of an animal, especially certain cattle, lizards, or birds. |  The dewlapped lizard puffed out its throat in a territorial display.  这只长着垂肉的蜥蜴鼓起喉咙，以宣示领地。 | Synonyms: wattled, fleshy, pendulous | Antonyms: smooth, tight | adjective

dewlapped:/ˈdjuːlæpt/ | n. |  有垂肉的动物 | An animal that has a dewlap. | We saw a dewlapped iguana basking in the sun. 我们看到一只长着垂肉的鬣蜥在阳光下晒太阳。| Synonyms:  none (this usage is rare) | Antonyms: none | noun
