clammy:/ˈklæmi/| adj.|湿冷的，黏糊糊的|Damp, sticky, and slightly cold to the touch.|Her hands were clammy with fear. 她的双手因害怕而湿冷。|The air in the basement was clammy and unpleasant. 地下室的空气潮湿而令人不快。|近义词：damp, sticky, moist|反义词：dry, warm|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *clammi, from Old English clǣmig (“sticky, viscous, clammy”), equivalent to clam +‎ -y. Cognate with Middle Low German klam (“sticky”). Related to Old English clām (“clay”).
