field-testing:/ˈfiːld ˌtɛstɪŋ/ | n. | 现场测试，实地测试 | The process of testing something (e.g., a product, a theory, or a procedure) in a real-world environment, rather than in a laboratory or controlled setting.  It allows for observation and evaluation under actual conditions of use. | The new software underwent extensive field-testing before its official release.  这款新软件在正式发布前经过了广泛的现场测试。 | field trial, beta testing, pilot study | laboratory testing, simulation | noun

field-test:/ˈfiːld ˌtɛst/ | v. | 现场测试，实地测试 | To test something in the field, under real-world conditions. |  They field-tested the new car in extreme weather conditions. 他们在极端天气条件下对新车进行了现场测试。 | field trial, beta test, pilot | simulate, model | verb
