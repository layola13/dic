nonredemptive:/ˌnɒnrɪˈdɛmptɪv/
adjective
无救赎的，无法弥补的
Not serving to redeem; offering no redemption, improvement, or hope.
His actions were seen as nonredemptive and only worsened the situation. 他的行为被认为是无法弥补的，只会让情况更糟。
The film offered a bleak and nonredemptive portrayal of human nature. 这部电影对人性进行了惨淡和无救赎的刻画。
Synonyms: irredeemable, hopeless, unforgivable, damning
Antonyms: redemptive, hopeful, forgiving, restorative
词性: adjective
