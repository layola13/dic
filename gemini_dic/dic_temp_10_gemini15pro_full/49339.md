delineable:/dɪˈlɪniəbl/
adjective
可描绘的，可界定的
able to be delineated; that can be outlined or represented clearly.
The boundaries of the property are clearly delineable on the map.  这处房产的边界在地图上清晰可辨。
The artist's style is delineable by its bold use of color. 这位艺术家的风格以其大胆的用色而著称。
Synonyms: definable, describable, discernible, distinguishable
Antonyms: vague, indistinct, blurry, obscure
词性: adjective
