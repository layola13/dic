gypsine:/ˈdʒɪpsɪn/ | n. | 石膏 |A soft white or yellowish mineral consisting of hydrated calcium sulfate. It occurs chiefly in sedimentary rocks and is used to make plaster of Paris and fertilizers. | Gypsum is a common mineral used in construction. 石膏是一种常用的建筑材料。| The walls were made of gypsine.  这些墙是用石膏做的。 | synonyms: gypsum, plaster stone, alabaster | antonyms: N/A | noun
