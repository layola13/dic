ahu:/ˈɑːhuː/ | n. | 祭坛，神殿（波利尼西亚语） | A stone platform or heap used as a place of worship or sacrifice, especially in Polynesian cultures. |  The ancient ahu stood silhouetted against the setting sun. 古老的祭坛在落日映衬下轮廓分明。

The archaeologists carefully excavated the ahu, hoping to find artifacts. 考古学家仔细挖掘祭坛，希望能找到文物。 | 近义词: altar, shrine, temple | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun


词源: From Polynesian (Hawaiian, Tahitian, Maori, etc.) *ahu, meaning "heap, mound, altar."
