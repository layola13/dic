ailan:/ˈeɪlən/ | n. | 臭椿 |A fast-growing East Asian tree (Ailanthus altissima) that has become naturalized in many other parts of the world and is often considered an invasive weed due to its ability to spread rapidly and suppress native vegetation. | The ailan tree is known for its unpleasant smell. 臭椿以其难闻的气味而闻名。| The city is trying to eradicate the invasive ailan. 这座城市正试图根除入侵的臭椿。 | synonyms: tree of heaven, Chinese sumac | antonyms: N/A | noun
