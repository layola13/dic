natatorium: /ˌneɪtəˈtɔːriəm/ (US) /ˌnætəˈtɔːriəm/ (UK)
n.
室内游泳池，游泳馆
An indoor swimming pool designed for swimming or other water activities.
The hotel boasts a luxurious natatorium with a retractable roof.  这家酒店拥有一个带可伸缩屋顶的豪华游泳馆。
The university's natatorium hosted the regional swimming competition.  这所大学的游泳馆举办了地区游泳比赛。
