multi-axially:/ˌmʌltiˈæksiəli/ | adv. | 多轴地，多方向地 |  Having or relating to multiple axes.  It describes something that operates or is arranged along several axes or directions. |  The material is multi-axially reinforced for increased strength.  这种材料是多轴加固的，以提高强度。 | The robot arm can move multi-axially.  这个机械臂可以多轴运动。 | multi-directional, polyaxial | uniaxial, single-axis | adverb
