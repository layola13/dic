emendation:/ˌiːmenˈdeɪʃən/ |n.| 校订；修改；修正|The text requires some emendation. 这篇文章需要一些修改。|例句：The editor made several emendations to the manuscript. 编辑对原稿做了几处修改。

例句：The emendation of the old laws is necessary for modern society.  旧法律的修改对于现代社会来说是必要的。

近义词：correction, revision, amendment, improvement|反义词：corruption, deterioration|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ēmendātiō (“a correction”), from ēmendātus, past participle of ēmendō (“I correct, free from fault”), from ex- (“out”) + mendum (“fault”).
