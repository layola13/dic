humin:/ˈhjuːmɪn/ | n. |腐殖质|A dark-colored substance that forms as plant and animal matter decays.  It is an important part of soil and helps it hold water and nutrients. |The soil was rich in humin, making it ideal for growing vegetables.|近义词:humus, organic matter|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "inorganic matter" in specific contexts) | noun
