overrestraints:/ˌoʊvərɪˈstreɪnts/
n.
过度限制，过度约束
Excessive restraints or limitations.
The overrestraints on the child's behavior stifled his creativity.  对孩子行为的过度限制扼杀了他的创造力。
Overrestraints on trade can hinder economic growth. 对贸易的过度限制会阻碍经济增长。
近义词: restrictions, limitations, constraints
反义词: freedoms, liberties
