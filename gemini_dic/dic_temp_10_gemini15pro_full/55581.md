distyle:/ˈdaɪstaɪl/
adjective
双柱式的，有两根柱子的
Having two columns.  Specifically, in classical architecture, having two columns across the front.
The temple was built in a distyle in antis configuration. 这座寺庙采用双柱式门廊结构建造。
近义词: two-columned
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider terms like "multi-columned" or specific architectural terms like "tetrastyle" for four columns)
词性: adjective
词源: From Greek *δίστυλος (dístylos)  meaning "having two columns", from *δίς (dís) "twice" + *στῦλος (stŷlos) "column".
