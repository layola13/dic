fair-minded:/ˌfeərˈmaɪndɪd/
|adj.|
|公正的；公平的；无偏见的|
|Impartial and just in one's opinions or dealings.|

|例句：A fair-minded judge is essential for a just legal system.  一个公正的法官对于一个公正的法律体系至关重要。|

|例句：She is known for being a fair-minded manager who treats all her employees equally. 她以是一位公正的经理而闻名，她平等地对待所有员工。|

|近义词：impartial, unbiased, just, equitable, objective|
|反义词：biased, prejudiced, unfair, partial, unjust|
|词性: adjective|
