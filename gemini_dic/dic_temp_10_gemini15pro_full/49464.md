deluders:/dɪˈluːdərz/| n. |欺骗者，迷惑者|People who deceive or mislead others.  Those who create false beliefs or impressions.|The deluders preyed on vulnerable individuals seeking easy solutions. 这些欺骗者专门捕猎那些寻求简单解决方法的弱势群体。|Synonyms: deceivers, tricksters, charlatans, fraudsters|Antonyms: truth-tellers, honest people|Noun (plural)
