bestreak: /bɪˈstriːk/
verb
涂抹条纹; 布满条纹
To mark with streaks.
The rain bestreaked the windows. 雨水在窗户上留下了条纹。
The sky was bestreaked with pink and gold at sunset. 日落时天空布满了粉红色和金色的条纹。
streak, stripe, daub
none
