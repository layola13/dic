hyperdeify:/ˌhaɪpərˈdiːɪfaɪ/| vt.| 过度神化，极度神化|To deify beyond all reason or measure; to treat as a god with excessive reverence. |  The cult hyperdeified their leader, believing him to be the incarnation of a deity.  这个邪教过度神化了他们的领袖，认为他是神灵的化身。

|例句：The fan club hyperdeified the young pop star, showering her with gifts and adoration.  粉丝俱乐部过度神化了这位年轻的流行歌星，向她赠送礼物并表达爱慕之情。|近义词：idolize, worship, exalt, glorify|反义词：denigrate, belittle, disparage|词性：动词
