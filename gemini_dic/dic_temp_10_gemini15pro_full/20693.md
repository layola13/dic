bladefish:/ˈbleɪdˌfɪʃ/| n. |刀鱼，剃刀鱼|A fish with a compressed, knife-like body.  These fish are known for their silvery appearance and sharp, bony keel. Several unrelated fish families are referred to as bladefish.|Example 1: The bladefish darted through the coral, its thin body barely visible. 刀鱼在珊瑚中飞快地游动，它纤细的身体几乎看不见。

Example 2:  The fisherman caught a large bladefish, its silver scales shimmering in the sun. 渔夫捕获了一条大刀鱼，它的银色鳞片在阳光下闪闪发光。|近义词：cutlassfish, ribbonfish|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
