faxable:/ˈfæksəbl/
adjective
可传真的
able to be sent by fax.
例句：Is this document faxable?  这份文件可以传真吗？

例句：The contract is faxable, so you can send it over immediately.  合同可以传真，所以你可以立即发送过来。

近义词：transmittable, sendable
反义词: non-faxable


ETYMOLOGY->
From fax + -able.  Fax comes from facsimile, from Latin fac simile 'make similar'.
