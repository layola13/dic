chainlike:/ˈtʃeɪnlaɪk/| adj.|链状的，像链条一样的|Resembling a chain; arranged like links in a chain.|The bacteria formed a chainlike structure. 这些细菌形成了链状结构。|The polymer has a chainlike molecule.  这种聚合物具有链状分子。|近义词：catenary, concatenated, linked|反义词:unconnected, separate|词性:形容词
