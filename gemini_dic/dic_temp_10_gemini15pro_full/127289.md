navigated:/ˈnævɪɡeɪtɪd/
verb
已航行，已导航
past tense and past participle of "navigate": to plan and direct the course of a vehicle or vessel, or to find one's way
The captain skillfully navigated the ship through the treacherous waters. 船长熟练地驾驶着船只穿过危险的水域。
He successfully navigated the complex maze of city streets. 他成功地穿过了城市街道复杂的迷宫。
近义词: steered, piloted, directed, guided
反义词: drifted, wandered, lost
