gong:/ɡɒŋ/ (UK), /ɡɑːŋ/ (US)| n.|锣; (某些比赛的)开始信号|A large metal disc that makes a loud noise when hit, especially one used in some ceremonies or as a signal. |The gong sounded, signaling the start of the meal. 锣声响起，标志着开饭了。|Synonyms: cymbal, bell|Antonyms: silence, quiet|noun

gong:/ɡɒŋ/ (UK), /ɡɑːŋ/ (US)| n.|勋章; 奖章|A medal or award.|He received a gong for his bravery. 他因勇敢而获得一枚奖章。|Synonyms: medal, award, prize|Antonyms: penalty, punishment|noun
