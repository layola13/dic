electro-optics:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈɑːptɪks/ |n.|电光学|The branch of physics and engineering that deals with the interactions between light and electric fields, especially as they affect the design and operation of optical devices.|Electro-optics plays a crucial role in modern communication systems. 电光学在现代通信系统中起着至关重要的作用。|Synonyms: optoelectronics, photonics|Antonyms: None|Noun

electro-optical:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈɑːptɪkəl/ |adj.|电光的|Relating to or involving the interaction of light and electric fields.|Electro-optical devices are used in a wide range of applications. 电光器件应用广泛。|Synonyms: optoelectronic, photonic|Antonyms: None|Adjective
