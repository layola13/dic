enfolded:/ɪnˈfəʊldɪd/
verb|包围，包裹，拥抱|Wrapped or enclosed completely.|She enfolded the child in her arms. 她把孩子抱在怀里。|Synonyms: embrace, hug, clasp, envelop, surround|Antonyms: unfold, release, uncover
verb|包含，包括|To contain or include something.|The darkness enfolded the city. 黑暗笼罩着城市。|Synonyms: encompass, comprise, include, contain|Antonyms: exclude, omit

词源：From en- + fold.  "En-" acts as a prefix meaning "in" or "to put in." "Fold" refers to the act of bending something over on itself.  Therefore, "enfold" literally means to fold something inwards or to wrap something up.
