engined:/ˈendʒɪnd/| adj.|装有发动机的；由…发动机驱动的|Having an engine or engines of a specified kind.|例句：A twin-engined aircraft. 一架双引擎飞机。|近义词：motorized, powered|反义词：unpowered|词性:形容词
词源: From engine + -ed.  The suffix "-ed" is used to form adjectives from nouns, indicating "having" or "equipped with."  The word "engine" itself has a complex etymology, ultimately deriving from Latin "ingenium" meaning "inborn quality, talent, mental power, intelligence."
