obligingly:/əˈblaɪdʒɪŋli/
adverb|殷勤地，乐意地，顺从地|In a way that shows a willingness to do favors or help others; helpfully.|She obligingly offered to drive me to the airport. 她殷勤地提出开车送我去机场。|Synonym:helpfully, willingly, graciously, kindly, considerately|Antonym:unwillingly, reluctantly, grudgingly
adverb
