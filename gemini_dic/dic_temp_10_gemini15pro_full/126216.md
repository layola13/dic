mya:/ˈmaɪə/ | n. | 百万年 (百万年前) |  Abbreviation for "million years ago," a unit of time used in geology, paleontology, and other sciences to denote a period of one million years in the past. | The dinosaur fossils are dated to 65 mya.  这些恐龙化石的年代可追溯到6500万年前。 |  Synonyms: million years ago | Antonyms: N/A | Noun

mya:/ˈmaɪə/ | adj. | 百万年前的 | Describes something that occurred or existed millions of years in the past. | The mya extinction event wiped out the dinosaurs. 百万年前的灭绝事件消灭了恐龙。| Synonyms: million-year-old | Antonyms: modern, recent, contemporary | Adjective
