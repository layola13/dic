elative: /ɪˈleɪtɪv/ | adj. | 比较的；最高级的；兴奋的；得意洋洋的 |  Expressing or indicating a high or the highest degree; marked by high spirits; exultant. |  He spoke in elative terms about the future of the company. 他兴高采烈地谈论着公司的未来。 | 近义词: euphoric, elated, jubilant | 反义词: depressed, dejected, gloomy | 词性: adjective

elative: /ɪˈleɪtɪv/ | n. | 最高比较级 |  A grammatical form expressing the highest degree of comparison. |  The elative form of "good" is "best".  "good"的最高级是"best"。 | 近义词: superlative | 反义词: positive (as in positive degree of comparison) | 词性: noun
