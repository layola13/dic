meritorious:/ˌmerɪˈtɔːriəs/| adj.|值得称赞的；有功的|Deserving praise, reward, or honor because of outstanding qualities or achievements.|(of an action or activity) deserving reward or praise.|
例句：Her meritorious service to the community was recognized with an award. 她对社区的功绩得到了奖励。

例句：He received a meritorious award for his bravery. 他因勇敢而获得了荣誉奖。

近义词：praiseworthy, commendable, laudable, deserving, worthy|
反义词：blameworthy, discreditable, unworthy, reprehensible|
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *meritorius, from Latin meritum (“merit, service, reward”) + -orius (suffix forming adjectives of relation or possession).
