magmatist:/ˈmæɡmətɪst/| n.|岩浆学家；火成论者|A scientist who studies magma and igneous rocks.  A person who believes in the theory of magmatism.|例句：The magmatist presented her research on the formation of granite. 岩浆学家介绍了她对花岗岩形成的研究。

例句：The magmatist argued that the volcano was formed by intrusive magmatic activity.  岩浆学家认为这座火山是由侵入性岩浆活动形成的。|近义词：volcanologist, petrologist, geologist|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "Neptunist," a historical opponent to the magmatist theory)|词性:noun
