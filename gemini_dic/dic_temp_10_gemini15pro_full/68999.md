fatly:/ˈfætlɪ/
adverb|肥胖地；油腻地；丰厚地|in a fat way; thickly or richly|She spread the butter thickly and evenly, or, as the recipe dictated, "fatly." 她把黄油涂得又厚又匀，或者像食谱上说的那样，“肥肥地涂”。|thickly, richly, heavily, plumply, obese|thinly, meagerly, sparingly, leanly|副词

fatly:/ˈfætlɪ/
adjective|肥胖的；油腻的；丰厚的 (古用法，现已罕见)|having a lot of fat; greasy or oily; rich or abundant|This sense is now archaic.  This meaning is rarely used now.|thick, rich, heavy, plump, obese|thin, meager, sparse, lean|形容词 (古用法)
