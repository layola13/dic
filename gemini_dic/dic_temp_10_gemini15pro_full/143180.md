outcoaches:/ˌaʊtˈkoʊtʃɪz/
v.|以更好的教练方式胜过|To surpass or defeat someone by having superior coaching strategies and tactics.
He outcoached his rival in the championship game, leading his team to a decisive victory.|他在冠军赛中以更好的教练方式击败了他的对手，带领他的球队取得了决定性的胜利。
The experienced coach outcoached the rookie, anticipating his every move.|经验丰富的教练以更好的教练方式胜过了新手教练，预判了他的每一个举动。
近义词:outmaneuver, outsmart, outwit
反义词:be outcoached
词性:动词
