leasehold:/ˈliːshəʊld/ (British), /ˈliːshoʊld/ (American)| n. |租赁产权; 租赁期限|The ownership of property for a fixed period of time under a lease agreement.  The lessee (tenant) has the right to use the property, but ownership reverts to the lessor (landlord) at the end of the lease term.|She bought a leasehold property for 99 years. 她购买了99年的租赁产权房产。|Synonym: tenancy, lease |Antonym: freehold|noun


leasehold:/ˈliːshəʊld/ (British), /ˈliːshoʊld/ (American)| adj. |租赁的|Held under a lease.  Refers to property or land owned under a lease agreement.|The leasehold apartment was conveniently located near the city center. 这套租赁公寓地理位置优越，靠近市中心。|Synonym: leased, rented|Antonym: freehold, owned|adjective
