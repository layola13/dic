bedew:/bɪˈdjuː/
verb
沾湿；使 покрывать росой
To cover with drops of dew or something resembling dew.
The grass was bedewed with morning mist. 早晨的薄雾沾湿了草地。

The spider's web was bedewed with tiny droplets.  蜘蛛网上沾满了小水滴。

近义词:moisten, dampen, drench
反义词:dry
词性:v.
