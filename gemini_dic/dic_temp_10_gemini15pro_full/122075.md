misthread:/ˌmɪsˈθred/
verb
错误地穿线；穿错线
To thread something incorrectly, such as a needle or a film projector.
She misthreaded the needle and had to start again. 她把针穿错了，不得不重新开始。
misthread
verb
弄错；理解错误
To misunderstand or misinterpret something.
He misthreaded the argument and came to the wrong conclusion. 他误解了论点，得出了错误的结论。


近义词: misinterpret, misunderstand, fumble, bungle
反义词: thread correctly, understand, grasp
词性: verb
