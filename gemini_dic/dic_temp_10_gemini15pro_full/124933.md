muesli: /ˈmjuːzli/ (UK) /ˈmjuːsli/ (US)| n. | 麦片；什锦早餐| A breakfast cereal made from rolled oats, nuts, dried fruit, and seeds, typically eaten with milk or yogurt. | 例句：I had muesli for breakfast this morning. 我今天早上吃了麦片。

例句：She prefers muesli to cooked cereals. 她比起热麦片更喜欢什锦早餐。

近义词：granola, cereal | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider cooked breakfast as a contrasting option) | 词性: noun
