geeing:/ˈdʒiːɪŋ/
verb
驾驭；激励；使（马）前进
driving or urging (a horse) onward.
The jockey was geeing his horse along.  骑师正策马前进。
近义词: urging, driving, encouraging, spurring
反义词: restraining, holding back
词性: verb


geeing:/ˈdʒiːɪŋ/
noun
(马的)驾驭声；激励
the act of urging a horse onward with a word of command
The geeing of the driver encouraged the horses to pull harder.  车夫的吆喝声鼓励马儿更用力地拉车。
近义词: urging, encouragement, exhortation
反义词: discouragement, restraint
词性: noun


词源:  Likely imitative of the sound used to encourage a horse.  很可能是模仿用来鼓励马匹的声音。
