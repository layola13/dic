mono-cable:/ˈmɒnəʊˌkeɪbəl/ /ˈmɑːnoʊˌkeɪbəl/
mono-cable|n.|单缆; 单线|A single cable used for transmitting multiple signals, often power and data.|The ski lift uses a mono-cable system. 滑雪缆车使用单线系统。|single cable, combined cable|dual-cable|noun
mono-cable|adj.|单缆的; 单线的|Utilizing or relating to a single cable for multiple functions.|The mono-cable gondola offers a smooth ride.  单线缆车提供平稳的行驶体验。|single-cable, combined-cable|dual-cable|adjective
