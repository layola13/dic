besiegingly:/bɪˈsiːdʒɪŋli/
| adv. | 围攻地，包围地 | In a manner suggestive of a siege; as if surrounding and attacking.  Like an army besieging a castle, the fans besiegingly surrounded the stage door. 像军队围攻城堡一样，歌迷们围攻着舞台门口。|例句1：The reporters besiegingly questioned the politician after the scandal broke.  丑闻曝光后，记者们围攻般地质问这位政客。

例句2：He stared besiegingly at her, waiting for an answer. 他围攻似地盯着她，等待着答案。|近义词：oppressively, aggressively, insistently |反义词：gently, subtly, respectfully |词性:副词


Etymology:
From "besiege" + "-ing" + "-ly". "Besiege" comes from Old French "asegier" (to besiege), from "a-" (to) + "sege" (seat, siege).
