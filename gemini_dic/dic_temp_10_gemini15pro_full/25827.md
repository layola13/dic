burglar:/ˈbɜːrɡlər/ (US), /ˈbɜːɡlə(r)/ (UK)| n. |窃贼，入室盗窃者|A person who commits burglary, which is the act of illegally entering a building with the intent to commit a crime, typically theft.|The burglar was caught trying to break into the house. 窃贼在试图闯入房子时被抓住了。|Synonyms: thief, robber, housebreaker, intruder|Antonyms: law enforcement officer, police officer|noun

burglarize:/ˈbɜːrɡləraɪz/ (US), /ˈbɜːɡləraɪz/ (UK)| vt. |入室盗窃|To illegally enter a building and steal something.|Our house was burglarized while we were on vacation. 我们度假时，房子被盗了。|Synonyms: rob, burgle, break into|Antonyms: protect, secure|verb
