bacchic:/ˈbækɪk/ | adj. | 酒神的；狂欢的；纵酒的 | Relating to Bacchus, the Roman god of wine; characterized by or given to drunken revelry; orgiastic. | The bacchic revels lasted all night long. 酒神狂欢持续了整夜。|  Synonyms: Dionysian, orgiastic, drunken, riotous | Antonyms: sober, restrained, moderate | adjective


bacchanal:/ˈbækənəl/ | n. | 狂欢作乐；狂饮作乐者 | A wild, drunken revelry; a participant in such a revelry. |  The bacchanal in the streets went on until dawn. 街上的狂欢一直持续到黎明。 | Synonyms: orgy, revelry, carousal, debauch | Antonyms:  prayer meeting, temperance | noun


bacchanalia:/ˌbækəˈneɪliə/ | n. | (通常指古罗马的) 酒神节；狂欢会 | A Roman festival of Bacchus celebrated with dancing, song, and revelry; any drunken or orgiastic revelry. | The annual bacchanalia was a time of unrestrained revelry. 每年的酒神节都是一个放纵狂欢的时刻。 | Synonyms: orgy, revel, saturnalia, debauchery | Antonyms:  abstinence, sobriety | noun
