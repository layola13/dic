nonfervidness:/ˌnɑːnˈfɜːrvɪdnəs/ | n. | 冷静，缺乏热情 | Lack of fervor or passion; the state of being not fervid. |  His nonfervidness about the project worried the team. 他对这个项目缺乏热情，这让团队很担心。 | The speaker's nonfervidness made the lecture rather dull. 演讲者缺乏热情，使讲座相当枯燥。 | 近义词: apathy, indifference, coolness, detachment | 反义词: fervor, passion, enthusiasm, zeal | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (not) + "fervid" (passionate) + "-ness" (noun suffix indicating a state or quality)
