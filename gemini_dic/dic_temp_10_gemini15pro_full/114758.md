malpractice: /ˌmælˈpræktɪs/ | n. |  渎职；玩忽职守；医疗事故 | Improper, illegal, or negligent professional activity or treatment, especially by a medical practitioner, lawyer, or public official. |  The doctor was sued for malpractice after the patient died during a routine surgery.  这位医生在病人死于常规手术后被控医疗事故。|  negligence, misconduct, wrongdoing |  due diligence, best practice | noun

malpractice: /ˌmælˈpræktɪs/ | n. |  弊端；不正之风 |  Any instance of improper conduct within a profession, not limited to medical, legal, or public official contexts. | The company's accounting malpractices led to a major financial scandal. 该公司的不正当会计行为导致了一起重大的财务丑闻。 |  abuse, corruption, fraud |  integrity, honesty, probity | noun
