insociably:/ɪnˈsoʊʃəbli/
adverb|不善交际地，孤僻地|In a manner that avoids social interaction; unsociably.|He lived insocibly, rarely leaving his apartment. 他过得很孤僻，很少离开他的公寓。|Near Synonyms: unsociably, aloofly, reclusively, solitarily|Near Antonyms: sociably, gregariously, affably|词性: 副词

insociable:/ɪnˈsoʊʃəbl/
adjective|不善交际的，孤僻的|Not inclined to socialize; reserved or antisocial.|He was an insociabe man who preferred his own company. 他是一个不善交际的人，喜欢独处。|Near Synonyms: unsociable, antisocial, reserved, withdrawn, aloof|Near Antonyms: sociable, gregarious, outgoing, affable|词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + sociable (from Late Latin sociabilis, from Latin socius "companion") + -ly (adverb/adjective suffix).
