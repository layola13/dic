nonsanguineness:/ˌnɒnsæŋˈɡwɪnɪsnəs/
noun
缺乏信心;不乐观
lack of hopefulness or confidence; pessimism
Her nonsanguineness about the future of the company was contagious. 她对公司未来的不乐观情绪很有感染力。
近义词: pessimism, gloominess, negativity, cynicism
反义词: optimism, hopefulness, sanguinity, positivity
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From non- (not) + sanguineness (hopefulness, optimism). Sanguineness comes from Latin sanguis (blood), reflecting the ancient belief that a person's temperament was determined by the balance of bodily fluids, with blood associated with a cheerful disposition.
