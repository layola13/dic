bulbar:/ˈbʌlbɑːr/ (US), /ˈbʌlbə/ (UK)| adj.|球根的，延髓的|Relating to the medulla oblongata (the bulb of the brain) or any bulb-shaped part.  It also refers to muscles innervated by the medulla oblongata.|The patient exhibited bulbar palsy.  病人表现出延髓麻痹。|Near synonyms: medullary, bulbous|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, consider antonyms for specific functions, e.g., cortical for relating to the cortex instead of the medulla)|adjective

bulbar:/ˈbʌlbɑːr/ (US), /ˈbʌlbə/ (UK)| n.|球茎植物|A plant that grows from a bulb.|Tulips and daffodils are popular bulbar plants. 郁金香和水仙花是受欢迎的球茎植物。|Near synonyms: bulb, tuber|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms)|noun
