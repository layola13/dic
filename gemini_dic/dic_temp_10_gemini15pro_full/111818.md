logcock:/ˈlɒɡkɒk/ (UK) /ˈlɔːɡkɑːk/ (US)| n. |啄木鸟|A bird, typically with a strong bill, stiff tail feathers, and long extensible tongue, which it uses to extract insects from bark and wood.  Specifically, it often refers to the pileated woodpecker (Dryocopus pileatus).|例句：The logcock hammered at the tree trunk. 啄木鸟敲击着树干。

例句：We heard the distinctive drumming of a logcock in the woods. 我们在树林里听到了啄木鸟独特的敲击声。|近义词：woodpecker, pileated woodpecker|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
