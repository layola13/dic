indispensable:/ˌɪndɪˈspɛnsəbl/| adj.|不可缺少的，必不可少的|Absolutely necessary; essential.|A good dictionary is indispensable for learning a new language.  一本好词典对于学习一门新语言是必不可少的。|近义词：essential, crucial, vital, necessary|反义词： dispensable, unnecessary, nonessential|词性: adjective
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin indispensabilis, from in- ‘not’ + dispensare ‘dispense with’ (see dispense).
