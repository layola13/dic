goodlyhead:/ˈɡʊdlihed/ | n. | 好人，正派人 (古语，现已少用) | A person of good character, especially a man (archaic and now rare). |  He was considered a goodlyhead in his community. 他在他的社区被认为是一个好人。| 近义词: good person, righteous person | 反义词: bad person, villain | 词性: 名词


Etymology:
From Middle English *godlihed*, equivalent to goodly +‎ -head.  Goodly itself comes from Old English *gōdlīc*  meaning “good-like, beautiful, splendid, excellent, virtuous.”
