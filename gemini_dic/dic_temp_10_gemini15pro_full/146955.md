overinvolve: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈvɑːlv/ | v. | 过度介入，过度参与 | To involve oneself too much in something, especially in the affairs of others. | She tends to overinvolve herself in her children's lives. 她倾向于过度介入孩子的生活。|  He overinvolved himself in the project, working late every night. 他过度投入到这个项目中，每天晚上都工作到很晚。 | 近义词: meddle, interfere, intrude | 反义词: neglect, ignore | 词性: 动词

overinvolved: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈvɑːlvd/ | adj. | 过度介入的，过度参与的 | Excessively or unnecessarily involved. | He was overinvolved in his daughter's wedding plans. 他过度参与了他女儿的婚礼计划。| The overinvolved parents hovered around their children constantly.  这些过度介入的父母总是围着他们的孩子转。 | 近义词: meddling, interfering, intrusive | 反义词: detached, uninvolved | 词性: 形容词


词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "involve" (from Latin "involvere," meaning "to wrap up, envelop")
