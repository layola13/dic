moorpan:/ˈmɔːrpæn/| n.|硬磐，铁盘层|A hardened, impermeable layer in soil, typically found in semi-arid regions, cemented by iron oxides and organic matter.  It restricts water infiltration and root penetration.|The moorpan prevented the crops from getting enough water. 硬磐阻止了农作物获得足够的水分。|The moorpan made it difficult for trees to establish deep roots. 硬磐使得树木难以扎根。|近义词：hardpan, ironpan|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly well-drained soil)|名词
