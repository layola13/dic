marish:/ˈmɛərɪʃ/ /ˈmærɪʃ/| adj.|沼泽的；沼泽似的|Characteristic of a marsh; swampy.  Relating to or inhabiting a marsh.|The air hung heavy and marish in the humid swamp.  潮湿的沼泽里，空气沉闷，弥漫着沼泽的气息。|近义词：swampy, boggy, fenny, miry|反义词：dry, arid|词性:adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *marisch, from Old English *merscisc, from mersc (“marsh”). Cognate with Old Frisian mersc (“marsh”), Middle Dutch maersc, mersc (“marsh”), Old High German marisc (“marsh”), German Marsch (“marsh”).
