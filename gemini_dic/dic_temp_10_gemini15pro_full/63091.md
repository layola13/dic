enseel:/ɪnˈsiːl/ | vt. | 封印，密封；保密 | To enclose or secure with a seal; to keep secret. |  The king ensealed the decree with his royal signet. 国王用他的皇家图章密封了法令。| 近义词：seal, close, confine, secure | 反义词: open, reveal, disclose | 词性: 动词

词源: From en- + seal, from Old French seel, from Latin sigillum "small image, seal."
