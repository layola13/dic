gestant:/ˈdʒɛstənt/
adjective
怀孕的；孕育的
Carrying a fetus or embryo within the body; pregnant.
例句：The gestant mare was carefully monitored by the veterinarian.  怀孕的母马受到兽医的密切监视。

例句：The article discussed the hormonal changes in a gestant woman.  这篇文章讨论了孕妇的荷尔蒙变化。

近义词：pregnant, gravid, expecting
反义词：nonpregnant, barren
词性：adjective
