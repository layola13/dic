adipoma:/ˌædɪˈpoʊmə/ /ˌædəˈpəʊmə/| n. |脂肪瘤|A benign tumor composed of fatty tissue.  A growth of fat cells in a thin, fibrous capsule, usually found just beneath the skin.|The doctor diagnosed the lump as an adipoma. 医生诊断该肿块为脂肪瘤。

The surgeon removed a small, painless adipoma from her back.  外科医生从她背部切除了一個小的、无痛的脂肪瘤。|近义词：lipoma, fatty tumor|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin, from adip- (from Latin adeps, adipis "fat") + -oma "tumor, swelling."

USAGE->
The doctor found an adipoma on his shoulder. = 医生在他的肩膀上发现了一个脂肪瘤。
Adipomas are generally harmless and do not require treatment. = 脂肪瘤通常是无害的，不需要治疗。


PHRASE->
multiple adipomas = 多发性脂肪瘤
adipoma removal = 脂肪瘤切除
subcutaneous adipoma = 皮下脂肪瘤
adipoma of the breast = 乳房脂肪瘤
spinal adipoma = 脊柱脂肪瘤
