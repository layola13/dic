malambo:/məˈlɑːmboʊ/| n.|马兰博舞（阿根廷牛仔的传统舞蹈）|A fast and percussive Argentinian folk dance, traditionally performed by men, characterized by intricate footwork, often including zapateo (tap-like steps).|
Example 1: The gauchos showcased their skills in a vibrant malambo performance.  牛仔们在一场充满活力的马兰博舞表演中展示了他们的技艺。

Example 2:  She was captivated by the rhythm and energy of the malambo. 她被马兰博舞的节奏和活力深深吸引。

|Synonym:  zapateo (sometimes), Argentine folk dance|Antonym: N/A|noun|
