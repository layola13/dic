Easily influenced by feelings or emotions; readily affected.
Her impressible nature made her susceptible to the influence of others. 她易受影响的天性使她容易受到他人的影响。

近义词:impressionable, susceptible, sensitive, receptive
反义词:unimpressionable, insensitive, resistant

词性:adjective
词源:From Late Latin *impressibilis, from Latin impressus, past participle of imprimere “to impress” (from in- “into” + premere “to press”).
