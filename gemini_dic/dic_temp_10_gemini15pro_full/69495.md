Her felicitousness in choosing the perfect gift was remarkable. 她挑选礼物的恰到好处令人惊叹。|近义词：aptness, appropriateness, suitability, perfection, happiness, bliss|反义词：inappropriateness, unsuitability, infelicity, misfortune, misery|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
