flammiferous:/fləˈmɪfərəs/
adjective
产火焰的；火焰似的
Bearing or producing flames; flamelike.
The flammiferous dragon breathed a torrent of fire. 这条喷火的龙喷出一股火焰。
近义词:fiery, flaming, igneous
反义词:nonflammable, fireproof
词性:adjective

词源:
From Latin *flammifer, from flamma (“flame”) + -fer (“-bearing”).
