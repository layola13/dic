gratifyingly:/ˈɡrætɪfaɪɪŋli/| adv.|令人满意地；欣慰地|in a way that gives pleasure and satisfaction|Her hard work paid off gratifyingly. 她的努力得到了令人欣慰的回报。|The project was gratifyingly successful.  这个项目取得了令人满意的成功。|satisfactorily, pleasingly, rewardingly|disappointingly, unsatisfactorily|adverb

词源:
From gratify (v.) + -ing (suffix forming present participles and verbal nouns) + -ly (suffix forming adverbs).  Gratify comes from the Late Latin gratificare "to give pleasure to," from gratus "pleasing, agreeable, thankful" + -ficare (from facere "to make").
