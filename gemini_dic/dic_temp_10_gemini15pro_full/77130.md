gasbags:/ˈɡæsˌbæɡz/
noun
气囊；夸夸其谈的人；空话连篇的人
A person who talks at length but says little of substance; a pompous or verbose person.  Also refers to literal gasbags, like those used in airships.

例句：
He's just a gasbag who loves to hear himself talk.
他只是一个喜欢自吹自擂的空话篓子。

The hot air balloon rose gracefully, its gasbags filled to capacity.
热气球优雅地上升，它的气囊充满了气体。


近义词：windbag, blowhard, braggart, boaster
反义词：quiet person, listener
