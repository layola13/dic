caulk:/kɔːk/ |v. n.| 堵缝，填塞；填隙料；堵缝|To seal a gap or joint with caulking.|He caulked the windows to keep out the draft. 他给窗户填缝以防穿堂风。|近义词:seal, fill, plug|反义词:open, uncover|词性:动词, 名词

caulked:/kɔːkt/ |v. | 堵缝的，填塞过的 (caulk的过去式和过去分词)|Sealed with caulking.|The windows were caulked to prevent leaks. 窗户被填塞以防止漏水。|近义词:sealed, filled, plugged|反义词:unsealed, open|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Northern French *cauquer, from Latin calcare "to tread, press down."  Related to "chalk."
