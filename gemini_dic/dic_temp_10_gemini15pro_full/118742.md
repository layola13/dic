metalbumin:/ˌmetælˈbjuːmɪn/
n.
变性白蛋白;偏白蛋白
A derivative of albumin, especially one found in pathological fluids.  Metalbumin is a complex of albumin and heme. It is found in certain pathological conditions such as hemorrhagic pancreatitis and pseudocysts.
Metalbumin is found in ascitic fluid in cases of haemorrhagic pancreatitis.  在出血性胰腺炎病例中，腹水中发现了偏白蛋白。

Metalbumin levels can be used to assess the severity of pancreatitis.  偏白蛋白水平可用于评估胰腺炎的严重程度。
