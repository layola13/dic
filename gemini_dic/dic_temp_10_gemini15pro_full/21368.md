blot-out:/ˈblɒt aʊt/ (US) /ˈblɒt aʊt/ (UK)| v.|遮蔽，抹去，忘却|To obscure something completely; to erase or obliterate something, often a memory.|The heavy clouds blotted out the sun. 厚厚的云层遮住了太阳。|Synonyms: obscure, obliterate, erase, expunge, efface|Antonyms: reveal, uncover, illuminate|verb

blot-out:/ˈblɒt aʊt/ (US) /ˈblɒt aʊt/ (UK)| n.|遮蔽，抹去，忘却|The act of obscuring or obliterating something.|The blot-out of the sun by the eclipse was breathtaking. 日食遮蔽太阳的景象令人叹为观止。|Synonyms: obscuration, obliteration, erasure|Antonyms: revelation, uncovering, illumination|noun
