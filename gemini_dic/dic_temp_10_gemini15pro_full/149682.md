pajamas:/pəˈdʒɑːməz/ /pəˈdʒæməz/| n. |睡衣，睡裤|Loose-fitting nightclothes consisting of trousers and a jacket, typically made of cotton or silk.|She changed into her pajamas and got ready for bed. 她换上睡衣，准备睡觉。|Synonyms: nightclothes, pyjamas, nightwear|Antonyms: daywear, street clothes|Noun


pajamases: This word is not standard English.  The correct plural form of "pajamas" is simply "pajamas".  While some might informally say "pajamases," it's not considered grammatically correct.
