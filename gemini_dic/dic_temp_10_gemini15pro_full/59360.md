earners:/ˈɜːrnərz/| n.| 挣钱的人；赚钱者 |People who earn money, especially those who earn a lot of money. |High earners often invest their money wisely. 高收入者通常会明智地投资他们的钱。|
The company rewarded its top earners with bonuses. 公司用奖金奖励了收入最高的员工。|近义词：income earners, wage earners, breadwinners|反义词: dependents, unemployed| noun (plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "earn," which comes from Old English *earnian "to merit, deserve, earn," related to Old Norse *ǫrnaz, Gothic *aznjan, from Proto-Germanic *aznojan "to harvest, reap, earn," from PIE root *es- "to be."  The suffix "-er" denotes a person or thing that performs the action of the verb.
