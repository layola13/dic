non-topographical:/ˌnɒnˌtɒpəˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
非地形学的；非地貌的
Not related to or based on the physical features of a place or region.
The painting was non-topographical, focusing on emotional expression rather than depicting a specific location. 这幅画是非地形学的，专注于情感表达而不是描绘特定地点。
The artist's work is increasingly non-topographical, exploring themes of memory and identity. 这位艺术家的作品越来越非地形学，探索记忆和身份的主题。
Synonyms: abstract, conceptual, non-representational
Antonyms: topographical, geographical, representational
adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + topographical.  "Topographical" comes from the Greek "topos" (place) and "graphein" (to write). "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not".
