falterers:/ˈfɔːltərərz/ | n. | 犹豫者；支吾者；步履蹒跚者 | People who falter; those who hesitate or stumble. |  The falterers in the race soon fell behind. 赛跑中犹豫不决的人很快就落后了. | Synonyms: hesitators, waverers, stumblers | Antonyms:  determined, resolute, confident | noun

falter:/ˈfɔːltər/ | v. | 犹豫；支吾；蹒跚；衰弱 | To hesitate or waver in action, purpose, or speech; to stumble or lose strength. |  His voice faltered as he spoke about the accident.  当他谈到事故时，他的声音颤抖了。 | Synonyms: hesitate, waver, stumble, weaken | Antonyms: persevere, continue, strengthen | verb
