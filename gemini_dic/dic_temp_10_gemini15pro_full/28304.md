car-pooled:/ˈkɑːrpuːld/
verb (past tense)|拼车，合伙用车|To travel to a destination together in one car, typically to reduce costs or environmental impact.  The act of sharing a car with others for commuting or other travel.|We car-pooled to work to save on gas. 我们拼车上班以节省汽油.|Synonyms: ride-shared, shared a ride|Antonyms: drove alone, drove separately|v.

car-pooling:/ˈkɑːrpuːlɪŋ/
noun|拼车，合伙用车|The practice of sharing a car with others for commuting or other travel.|Car-pooling is a great way to reduce traffic congestion. 拼车是减少交通拥堵的好方法。|Synonyms: ride-sharing, lift-sharing (British)|Antonyms: solo driving, driving alone|n.
