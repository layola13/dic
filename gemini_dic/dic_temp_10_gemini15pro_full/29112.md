cascaded:/kæˈskeɪdɪd/| adj. |层叠的，串联的，瀑布式的|arranged in a series or succession of stages so that each stage derives from or acts upon the product of the preceding. |The data is cascaded through a series of filters. 数据通过一系列过滤器进行级联。| The waterfall cascaded down the rocks. 瀑布顺着岩石倾泻而下。|近义词：tiered, layered, series, successive|反义词: single, isolated|词性:形容词

cascade:/kæˈskeɪd/| n. v. |小瀑布，倾泻，串联|n. a small waterfall, typically one of several that fall in stages down a steep rocky slope. v. (of water) pour downwards rapidly and in large quantities.|例句：The water cascaded over the rocks. 水从岩石上倾泻而下。|The news cascaded through the office. 消息在办公室里迅速传播。|近义词：waterfall, torrent, flood, pour, stream|反义词: trickle, drip|词性:名词，动词
