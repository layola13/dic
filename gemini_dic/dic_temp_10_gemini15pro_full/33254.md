chronotropic:/ˌkrɑːnəˈtrɑːpɪk/ | adj. | 变时性的，影响心率的 | Affecting the rate of time or, more specifically, the heart rate. |  Chronotropic drugs can increase or decrease the heart rate. 变时性药物可以增加或降低心率。| Synonyms: cardioaccelerator (for positive chronotropic effect), cardioinhibitor (for negative chronotropic effect) | Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but drugs with opposite effects exist) | adjective

chronotropism: /krəˈnɒtrəpɪzəm/ | n. | 变时性 | The influence of something on the rate of time or, more specifically, the heart rate.  | The chronotropism of caffeine is positive, meaning it increases the heart rate. 咖啡因的变时性是正的，这意味着它会增加心率。 | Synonyms:  (No direct synonyms, but related terms include dromotropism, inotropism) | Antonyms: N/A | noun
