mesosiderite:/ˈmɛzoʊˈsaɪdəˌraɪt/| n. |中铁陨石|A type of stony-iron meteorite composed of approximately equal parts of metallic nickel-iron and silicate minerals.|Mesosiderites are rare and fascinating meteorites. 中铁陨石是稀有且迷人的陨石。|The museum's collection includes a large mesosiderite.  博物馆的收藏包括一块巨大的中铁陨石。|Synonym: stony-iron meteorite|Antonym: N/A|noun
