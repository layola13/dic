bubba:/ˈbʌbə/| n.|兄弟，老兄，老朋友（尤指男性之间）,哥们儿|A term of endearment, often used between men, similar to "buddy" or "pal." It can also be used condescendingly.|例句：Hey bubba, how's it going? 嘿，老兄，最近怎么样？

例句：He called his old friend "bubba." 他称呼他的老朋友为“哥们儿”。

近义词：buddy, pal, friend, mate, dude|反义词:enemy, foe|词性:noun
