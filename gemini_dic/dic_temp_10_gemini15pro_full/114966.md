manchesterization:/ˌmæntʃɛstəraɪˈzeɪʃən/
noun
曼彻斯特化
The process of becoming industrialized and urbanized, often with negative connotations of pollution, overcrowding, and social problems, based on the historical example of Manchester, England during the Industrial Revolution.
Manchesterization led to significant air and water pollution in many 19th-century cities. 曼彻斯特化导致许多19世纪的城市出现了严重的空气和水污染。

The rapid manchesterization of the region resulted in widespread poverty and disease. 该地区快速的曼彻斯特化导致了普遍的贫困和疾病。

synonyms: industrialization, urbanization
antonyms: ruralization, de-industrialization
