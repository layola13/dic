anthropoidal:/ˌænθrəˈpɔɪdl/
adjective
类人的；似人的
resembling an anthropoid ape; apelike.  Having humanlike characteristics.
例句：The robot had an anthropoidal face.  这个机器人有一张类人的脸。

例句：The creature's anthropoidal movements were uncanny.  这个生物的类人动作令人不可思议。

近义词：apelike, simian, humanoid
反义词：nonhuman, beastly

词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From anthropoid + -al.  "Anthropoid" comes from the Greek "anthrōpos" (human) + "-eides" (form, shape).
