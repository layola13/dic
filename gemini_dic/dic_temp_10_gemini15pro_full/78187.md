geostatic:/ˌdʒiːəʊˈstætɪk/ | adj. | 地球静止的，对地静止的 |  Relating to or denoting a satellite that orbits the earth at the same speed as the earth's rotation, and therefore appears to remain stationary above a fixed point on the equator. |  A geostatic satellite provides continuous coverage of a specific area. 地球静止卫星可以持续覆盖特定区域。|  Geostationary orbits are highly sought after for communication satellites. 对地静止轨道是通信卫星的热门之选。 | synonyms: geostationary | antonyms: orbiting, revolving | word type: adjective
