pambi:/ˈpɑːmbi/| interj.|我的天啊；哇；表示惊讶或兴奋|An exclamation expressing surprise or excitement, similar to "wow" or "oh my god".|Example 1: Pambi! That's a huge spider! 我的天啊！那是一只巨大的蜘蛛！

Example 2: Pambi, I can't believe I won the lottery! 哇，我不敢相信我中了彩票！|近义词：wow, gosh, oh my god, jeepers|反义词: (No direct antonym, as it's an exclamation) |词性:感叹词

注意事项:

"Pambi" is primarily used in Filipino Tagalog.  While grammatically correct in various tenses, its usage is predominantly for expressing surprise or excitement, and therefore most natural in present and past tenses describing immediate reactions.  It's less common in future tenses or complex grammatical constructions.
