epitaphless:/ˌepɪˈtæfləs/
adjective
无墓碑铭的，没有墓志铭的
Without an epitaph; lacking a tombstone inscription.
The soldier was buried in an epitaphless grave.  这名士兵被葬在一个没有墓碑铭的坟墓里。
Many victims of the disaster lie in epitaphless graves. 许多灾难的受害者都躺在没有墓志铭的坟墓里。
unmarked, nameless, anonymous
inscribed, marked, named
