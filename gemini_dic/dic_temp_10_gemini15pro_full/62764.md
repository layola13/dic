词源：
From Old French *engraillier (unattested), from en- + graille (“gravel, coarse sand”).  Ultimately from Frankish *greot (“gravel, grit”), from Proto-Germanic *greutaz (“gravel, grit”), from Proto-Indo-European *gʰrew- (“to rub, grind”).
来源于古法语 *engraillier（未经证实），由 en- + graille（“砾石，粗砂”）组成。最终来自法兰克语 *greot（“砾石，沙砾”），来自原始日耳曼语 *greutaz（“砾石，沙砾”），来自原始印欧语 *gʰrew-（“摩擦，研磨”）。
