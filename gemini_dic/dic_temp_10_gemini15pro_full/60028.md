edt:/ˌiːdiːˈtiː/ | abbreviation | 东部夏令时间 (Eastern Daylight Time) | Eastern Daylight Time is the local time observed during the summer months in eastern North America. | EDT is four hours behind Coordinated Universal Time (UTC).  东部夏令时间比协调世界时晚四个小时。| EDT is used in many parts of North America. 东部夏令时间在北美许多地区使用。 | 近义词: EST (Eastern Standard Time) | 反义词: PST (Pacific Standard Time) | noun
