acclimatised: /əˈklaɪmətaɪzd/ (UK) /əˈklaɪməˌtaɪzd/ (US)
adjective
适应气候的; 适应环境的
Adapted to a new climate or environment.
The climbers needed a few days to become acclimatised to the altitude. 这些登山者需要几天时间来适应高海拔。

She quickly acclimatised herself to the hot weather. 她很快就适应了炎热的天气。

近义词: adapted, adjusted, habituated
反义词: unaccustomed, unadjusted

词性: adjective
词源: From French acclimater, from à to + climat climate.
