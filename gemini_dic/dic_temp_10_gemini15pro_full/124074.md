morphs:/mɔːrfs/ | n. | 形态学单位，词素 | A morph is the smallest meaningful unit in a language.  A word can be made up of one or more morphs. |  Example: The word "unbreakable" has three morphs: "un-", "break", and "-able".  “unbreakable”这个单词由三个词素构成：“un-”，“break”和“-able”。 | Example: The plural suffix "-s" in "dogs" is a morph.  单词“dogs”中的复数后缀“-s”是一个词素。 | Synonyms: morpheme, form, shape | Antonyms: whole, entirety | Noun
