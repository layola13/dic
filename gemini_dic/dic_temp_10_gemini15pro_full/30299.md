cenobitic:/sɪnəˈbɪtɪk/
adjective
修道院的；集体生活的
Living in a community, especially in a monastery or convent.
The monks lived a cenobitic life, sharing meals and praying together.  修道士们过着集体生活，一起吃饭和祈祷。
Monasteries are known for their cenobitic lifestyle.  修道院以其集体生活方式而闻名。
近义词：communal, monastic, conventual
反义词：eremitic, solitary, anchoritic
词性:adjective

cenobitic:/sɪnəˈbɪtɪk/
noun
过集体生活的人
A person who lives in a community, especially in a monastery or convent.
The cenobites dedicated their lives to prayer and study.  过集体生活的人将他们的生命奉献给了祈祷和学习。
He became a cenobite after leaving the secular world. 他离开世俗世界后成为了一个过集体生活的人。
近义词：monk, nun, friar
反义词：hermit, recluse
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *coenobita, from Greek koinobios "living in a community," from koinos "common" + bios "life."
