ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ebba, from Proto-Germanic *abjō ("ebb, reflux"). Cognate with Old Saxon ebbi, Old Frisian ebbe, Middle Dutch ebbe, eb (Dutch eb), Old High German ebba (German Ebbe). Related also to Old Norse efja, Gothic 𐌰𐍆 (af, "after, behind").
