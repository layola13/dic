ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *calmen, *camen, from Old Norse *kalma ("to cool, to still"), from Proto-Germanic *kalmaz ("cold, cool"), from Proto-Indo-European *gel- ("cold, to freeze"). Related to cold. The sense development is from "to cool down, to become cool" to "to become calm."
