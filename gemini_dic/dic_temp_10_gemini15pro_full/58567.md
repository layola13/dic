dulcamarin:/dʌlkəˈmærɪn/ | n. |苦茄碱; 苦甜茄碱 |A poisonous glycoside, C₂₂H₃₄O₁₀, obtained from the woody nightshade (bittersweet). It is crystalline, bitter, and then sweet, and is used as a diuretic and diaphoretic. |The dulcamarin in the plant can be toxic if ingested in large quantities.  这种植物中的苦茄碱如果大量摄入是有毒的。|Synonym: glucoside|Antonym: N/A| noun
