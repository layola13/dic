dulcetnesses: /ˈdʌlsɪtnɪsɪz/
noun
甜美, 悦耳, 美妙
the quality of being dulcet; sweetness of sound.
Her voice, full of dulcetnesses, calmed the crying baby.  她的声音充满了甜美，使哭闹的婴儿平静下来。
The dulcetnesses of the music lulled me to sleep.  音乐的甜美让我昏昏欲睡。
synonyms: sweetness, melodiousness, euphony
antonyms: harshness, dissonance, cacophony
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *doucetnes, from Old French doucete, from doux (“sweet”) + -ete (suffix forming abstract nouns).  Ultimately from Latin dulcis (“sweet”).
