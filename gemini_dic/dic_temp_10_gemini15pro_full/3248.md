affiliation:/əˌfɪliˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 附属关系，隶属关系，联系 |  A connection or association with a group or organization. |  Example 1: His affiliation with the university ended when he retired.  他与这所大学的附属关系在他退休时结束了。

Example 2: The group has affiliations with several international organizations.  该团体与几个国际组织有联系。 | Synonyms: association, connection, membership, attachment, alliance | Antonyms: disassociation, separation, independence | noun
