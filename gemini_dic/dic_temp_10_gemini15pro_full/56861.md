dorsoventral:/ˌdɔːrsəʊˈventrəl/ | n. | 背腹轴 | The axis extending from the back to the belly. | The study focused on the development of the dorsoventral axis in the embryo. 这项研究的重点是胚胎背腹轴的发育。| Synonym: dorsal-ventral axis | Antonym: ventrodorsal axis | noun
