nasalizing:/ˈneɪzəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb, adjective|鼻音化，使带鼻音的|The act of making a sound nasal, or the quality of being nasalized.  This involves lowering the velum, allowing air to escape through the nose.|She is nasalizing her vowels.|近义词：nasal, twanging|反义词:oral|v. adj.

nasalization:/ˌneɪzələˈzeɪʃən/
noun|鼻音化|The process or result of making a sound nasal.|The nasalization of the vowel was very noticeable.|近义词：nasality|反义词:orality|n.
