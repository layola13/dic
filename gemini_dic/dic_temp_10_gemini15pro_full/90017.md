hosanna:/hoʊˈzænə/ | n. int.|和散那; 欢呼声; 赞美诗|An exclamation of praise and adoration, especially one used in religious services.|例句：The crowd shouted "Hosanna!" as Jesus entered Jerusalem. 群众在耶稣进入耶路撒冷时高喊“和散那！”

例句：They sang hosannas to the king. 他们向国王唱赞歌。|近义词：praise, acclaim, adoration, hallelujah|反义词：curse, condemnation|词性:名词,感叹词
