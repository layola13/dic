intermeation:/ˌɪntərˈmiːʃən/| n.|渗透，渗入，弥漫|The intermeation of water into the concrete caused structural damage. 水渗入混凝土导致结构损坏。|例句：The intermeation of gas through the membrane is a key factor. 气体透过膜的渗透是一个关键因素。|例句：The intermeation of new ideas into the company culture is essential for growth. 新思想渗入公司文化对于发展至关重要。|近义词：penetration, permeation, infiltration|反义词: sealing, blockage|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "inter" (between, among) + "meare" (to pass, to go).
