ovenbird:/ˈʌvənˌbɜːrd/| n. |灶鸟，烤炉鸟|A small, brownish-grey American warbler (Seiurus aurocapilla) that builds a dome-shaped nest on the ground, resembling a Dutch oven.  It sings a loud, ringing "teacher, TEACHER, TEACHER" song.|The ovenbird is a common sight in eastern North American forests.  灶鸟在北美东部森林中很常见。|The ovenbird scurried through the underbrush, searching for insects. 灶鸟在灌木丛中匆匆穿过，寻找昆虫。|Synonyms:  wood warbler,  golden-crowned warbler|Antonyms: N/A|noun
