armor:/ˈɑːrmər/ (US), /ˈɑːmə(r)/ (UK)| n.|盔甲;装甲|A protective covering, typically made of metal, leather, or other strong material, worn to protect the body in battle or other dangerous situations.|The knight wore shining armor. 骑士穿着闪亮的盔甲。|Synonyms: protection, shield, mail, armour (UK)|Antonyms: vulnerability, weakness|noun
