autocephalous:/ˌɔːtəˈsɛfələs/| adj.|自主的，独立的（指教会）|Self-governing; independent (of a church).  Specifically, referring to an Eastern Orthodox church that is independent of external patriarchal authority.|The autocephalous Orthodox Church of Greece has its own archbishop.|Synonyms: independent, autonomous, self-governing|Antonyms: dependent, subordinate|Adjective
