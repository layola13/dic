factitious:/fækˈtɪʃəs/
adjective
人为的；虚假的；不自然的
Artificial; not natural; produced by humans rather than by natural forces.
The lawyer presented factitious evidence to the jury. 律师向陪审团出示了伪造的证据。
synonyms: artificial, contrived, bogus, sham, spurious, feigned
antonyms: genuine, authentic, natural, real
词性: 形容词


factitious:/fækˈtɪʃəs/
adjective
人为的；虚假的；不自然的
Made; done; or contrived in order to deceive; not spontaneous or genuine; artificial.
Her enthusiasm was factitious, a mere pretense to impress the boss. 她的热情是装出来的，只是为了给老板留下好印象。
synonyms:  manufactured, fabricated, forged, simulated
antonyms:  unfeigned, sincere, genuine
词性: 形容词
