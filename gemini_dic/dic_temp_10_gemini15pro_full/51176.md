dessert:/dɪˈzɜːrt/ (US) /dɪˈzɜːt/ (UK)| n.| 甜点，甜食|A sweet dish served at the end of a meal.|例句：I always save room for dessert. 我总是留肚子吃甜点。|例句：The restaurant has a wide variety of desserts. 这家餐厅有各种各样的甜点。|近义词：sweet, pudding, afters|反义词: appetizer, starter, main course|词性:noun
Etymology: From Old French desservir "to clear the table," from des- "un-, reverse of" + servir "to serve." Originally referred to the course served after the table was cleared of the main meal.
