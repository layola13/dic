disown:/dɪsˈoʊn/| vt. |否认，拒绝承认，断绝关系|To refuse to acknowledge or accept as one's own; to renounce or repudiate.|He disowned his son after he was convicted of the crime. 他在儿子被判有罪后与他断绝了父子关系。|例句：The family disowned their daughter when she eloped with a man they disapproved of.  当他们的女儿与一个他们不喜欢的男人私奔时，家人与她断绝了关系。

例句：She disowned her previous statements, claiming she had been misquoted. 她否认了她之前的声明，声称她被错误引用了。|近义词：deny, renounce, repudiate, reject|反义词：acknowledge, accept, embrace, claim|词性:动词
