interdealer:/ˌɪntərˈdiːlər/| adj.|交易商间的，经销商间的|Occurring between or involving dealers.  Relating to transactions between dealers.|例句：Interdealer brokers facilitate trading between large financial institutions. 交易商间经纪人促进了大型金融机构之间的交易。

例句：The interdealer market is where dealers buy and sell securities among themselves. 交易商间市场是交易商之间买卖证券的地方。|近义词：interbank, between dealers|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "retail" in some contexts) |词性: adjective
