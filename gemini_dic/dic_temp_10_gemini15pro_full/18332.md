bellicosities:/ˌbelɪˈkɒsətiz/
n.
好战性，交战状态
A state of being belligerent, hostile, or warlike.
The bellicosities between the two nations led to war.  这两个国家之间的敌对状态导致了战争。
近义词: hostilities, aggressiveness, belligerence
反义词: peacefulness, pacifism
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

bellicoseness:/ˌbelɪˈkɒsnəs/
n.
好战，好斗
The quality or state of being bellicose.
His bellicoseness made him unpopular among his colleagues. 他的好斗让他在同事中不受欢迎。
近义词: aggressiveness, pugnacity, belligerence
反义词: peacefulness, gentleness
词性: 名词

bellicose:/ˈbelɪˌkəʊs/
adj.
好战的，好斗的
Inclined to start quarrels or wars.
The general made bellicose statements about the neighboring country. 将军对邻国发表了充满敌意的声明。
近义词: aggressive, hostile, belligerent
反义词: peaceful, pacifistic
词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin bellicosus ("warlike"), from bellum ("war").
