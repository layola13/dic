agreeability:/əˌɡriːəˈbɪləti/
n.|随和，同意，宜人|the quality of being pleasant and easy to get along with; the extent to which something is acceptable or agreeable.|Her agreeability made her a popular member of the team. 她的随和让她成为团队中受欢迎的成员。|近义词：amiability, pleasantness, affability|反义词：disagreeableness, hostility, unpleasantness|词性:名词

agreeable:/əˈɡriːəbl/
adj.|令人愉快的，同意的，适合的|pleasant and easy to get along with; willing to agree or consent to something; suitable or acceptable.|We spent a most agreeable evening together. 我们一起度过了一个非常愉快的夜晚。|近义词：pleasant, amenable, acceptable|反义词：disagreeable, unpleasant, unacceptable|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *agreable*, from Old French *agreable*, from *agreer* "to please," from Latin *agrāre* "to make pleasant, to enhance," from *ad-* "to" + *grātus* "pleasing, agreeable, thankful."
