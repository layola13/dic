half-hard:/ˈhɑːf hɑːrd/| adj.|半硬的|Partially hardened; midway between soft and hard.  Refers to the temper of a metal, particularly non-ferrous metals like copper, aluminum, and their alloys. It indicates a specific level of hardness achieved through cold working or heat treatment.|Copper pipes are often supplied in a half-hard state for plumbing applications.  铜管通常以半硬状态供应用于管道应用。

The half-hard aluminum sheet was easier to bend than the fully hardened one.  半硬铝板比全硬铝板更容易弯曲。|近义词:semi-hard, moderately hard|反义词:soft, hard|词性:形容词
