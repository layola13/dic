cruiserweight:/ˈkruːzərˌweɪt/| n.|巡洋舰级，次重量级|A weight class in boxing and other combat sports, typically between light heavyweight and heavyweight.  A boxer or other combat sports competitor in this weight class.|例句：The cruiserweight champion defended his title. 巡洋舰级冠军卫冕了他的冠军头衔。

例句：He moved up to cruiserweight after dominating the light heavyweight division.  在统治了轻重量级之后，他升到了巡洋舰级。|近义词：light heavyweight, junior heavyweight|反义词:heavyweight|词性:noun
