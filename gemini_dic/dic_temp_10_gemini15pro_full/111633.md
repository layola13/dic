localize:/ˈloʊkəˌlaɪz/ /ˈləʊkəlaɪz/| v. |本地化；局限；定位|To adapt something to a particular place or market, or to confine something to a specific area.|The company decided to localize its products for the Asian market. 公司决定将其产品本地化以适应亚洲市场。|The infection was localized to a small area. 感染被局限在一个小区域。|adapt, confine, restrict|globalize, spread, disperse|verb
localize:/ˈloʊkəˌlaɪz/ /ˈləʊkəlaɪz/| adj. |局部的；地方性的|Confined or restricted to a particular place.|The pain was localized to his lower back. 疼痛局限于他的下背部。|local, regional, confined|general, widespread, global|adjective
