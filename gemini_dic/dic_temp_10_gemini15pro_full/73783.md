formalized:/ˈfɔːrməlaɪzd/ | adj. |正式化的，形式化的|Made formal or official; given a definite structure or form.  The agreement was formalized with signed contracts.  协议以签署合同的方式正式化。|例句：The process was formalized to ensure consistency.  这个过程被正式化以确保一致性。

例句：They formalized their relationship with a wedding ceremony. 他们用婚礼仪式正式确立了他们的关系。

近义词：official, structured, systematized, codified|反义词：informal, unstructured, casual|词性:形容词
