overrighteously:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪtʃəsli/ | adv. | 过分正直地，假正经地 | Excessively self-righteous; acting as if morally superior to others.  Displaying a righteousness that is excessive or hypocritical. | He overrighteously condemned her behavior, ignoring his own flaws. 他假正经地谴责她的行为，却无视自己的缺点。|  She overrighteously declared she would never tell a lie, conveniently forgetting the many times she had. 她假正经地宣称自己从不说谎，却轻易地忘记了自己撒过多少次谎。| 近义词: sanctimoniously, holier-than-thou, self-righteously | 反义词: humbly, modestly | 词性: 副词
