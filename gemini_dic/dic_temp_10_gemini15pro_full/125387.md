multimotor:/ˌmʌltiˈmoʊtər/ | adj. | 多发动机的 | Having more than one motor, especially of an aircraft. |  Example: The multimotor aircraft was able to fly even after one engine failed.  多引擎飞机即使在一个引擎失效后也能飞行。

Example: Multimotor designs became increasingly common in the early days of aviation. 多引擎设计在航空早期变得越来越普遍。 | Synonym: multi-engine | Antonym: single-engine | word type: adjective
