maintains:/meɪnˈteɪnz/
|v.|维持，保持；维修；保养；断言；主张|Keeps something in its existing state or condition;  repairs and keeps something in good working order; declares something to be true.|The mechanic maintains the company's fleet of trucks.  机械师维护着公司的卡车车队。|近义词:sustains, preserves, upholds, asserts|反义词:neglects, abandons, denies|词性:动词
词源:From Old French maintenir (to hold in the hand, to uphold), from Latin manu tenere (to hold in the hand).
