aloha:/əˈloʊhɑː/ /ɑːˈloʊhɑː/
n. int.
爱，你好，再见
A Hawaiian word used as a greeting or farewell, expressing love, affection, peace, or compassion.
Example: Aloha, welcome to Hawaii!  爱，欢迎来到夏威夷！

Example: We said our alohas and boarded the plane. 我们道别后登上了飞机。
近义词: hello, goodbye, greetings, farewell
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) hostility, animosity
词性: noun, interjection
