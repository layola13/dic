anomuran:/ˈænəmərən/ | n. | 寄居蟹类 |A group of decapod crustaceans, including hermit crabs, king crabs, and squat lobsters, characterized by a soft abdomen often asymmetrical and hidden in a shell or other shelter.|Hermit crabs are anomurans that often inhabit discarded snail shells. 寄居蟹是寄居蟹类动物，经常栖息在废弃的蜗牛壳中。|近义词:decapod crustacean |反义词: (无明确的反义词) |词性: 名词


Etymology: From Greek *ἀνώμαλος (anōmalos, “uneven, irregular”) + -ουρα (-oura, “tail”)
