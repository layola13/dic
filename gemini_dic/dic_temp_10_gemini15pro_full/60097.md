词源:  "Eenie, meenie, miney, mo" is a counting-out rhyme with origins that are difficult to pinpoint. It is thought to be derived from older counting rhymes and has variations in different cultures and languages.  Its etymology is obscure, and the words themselves likely have no inherent meaning outside the rhyme.
