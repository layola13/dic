caviller:/ˈkævɪlər/| n. |吹毛求疵的人；苛责者|A caviller is someone who raises petty objections or makes frivolous criticisms.|He's a caviller who always finds something to complain about. 他是一个吹毛求疵的人，总是能找到抱怨的事情。|近义词：critic, nitpicker, quibbler|反义词:supporter, admirer|词性:noun

cavil:/ˈkævəl/| v. |吹毛求疵；苛责|To cavil is to raise petty objections or make frivolous criticisms.|They cavilled at the price of the meal. 他们对这顿饭的价格吹毛求疵。|近义词：criticize, nitpick, quibble|反义词:praise, approve|词性:verb

词源: From Middle French *caviller*, from Latin *cavillārī* (“to jest, mock, banter”), frequentative of *calvārī* (“to deceive, trick”).
