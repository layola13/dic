ASML:/ˌeɪ ɛs ɛmˈɛl/ | n. | 阿斯麦 |  Dutch multinational company that designs, develops, manufactures, markets, and services advanced semiconductor equipment systems, especially lithography systems used in integrated circuit manufacturing.  | ASML is a key supplier to semiconductor manufacturers. 阿斯麦是半导体制造商的关键供应商。 |  Synonym: semiconductor equipment supplier | Antonym: (No direct antonym, but perhaps a competing company like Nikon or Canon could be considered a business antonym) | noun
