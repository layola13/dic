cornigerous:/kɔːrˈnɪdʒərəs/
adjective
有角的；角状的
Bearing horns; horned.
例句：The cornigerous beast lowered its head and charged. 这头有角的野兽低下头冲锋陷阵。

例句：Ancient myths often depict cornigerous deities. 古代神话中经常描绘有角的神灵。

近义词：horned, antlered (specifically for deer)
反义词：hornless

词性: adjective
