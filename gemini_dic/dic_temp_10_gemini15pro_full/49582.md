demerger:/diːˈmɜːrdʒər/ /diːˈmɜːrdʒər/
n.|公司分拆；业务剥离|The act of separating a part of a company from the main organization, creating a new independent entity.|The demerger of the company's telecommunications division resulted in two separate publicly traded companies. 公司电信部门的分拆导致形成了两家独立的上市公司。|
近义词：spin-off, divestiture, separation|反义词:merger, acquisition, consolidation|词性:名词

demerger:/diːˈmɜːrdʒər/ /diːˈmɜːrdʒər/
v.|分拆；剥离|To separate a part of a company from the main organization.|The board decided to demerge the manufacturing business from the retail operations. 董事会决定将制造业务从零售业务中剥离出来。|近义词：spin off, divest, separate|反义词:merge, acquire, consolidate|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- + merger.  "De-" is a prefix meaning "reverse" or "remove". "Merger" refers to the combining of two or more entities. Thus, "demerger" literally means the reverse of a merger.
