overpassionateness:/ˌoʊvərˈpæʃənətnəs/
|n.|过度热情，过分激情|The state of being excessively passionate or overly emotional.|Her overpassionateness about the cause sometimes alienated potential supporters. 她对这项事业的过度热情有时会疏远潜在的支持者。|近义词：excessiveness, fervor, zeal|反义词：apathy, indifference, coldness|词性: 名词
Etymology: over- + passionateness.  "Over-" is an Old English prefix meaning "above, beyond, excessively." "Passionateness" derives from "passionate," ultimately from Latin "passio" meaning "suffering, enduring," later evolving to encompass strong emotions.
