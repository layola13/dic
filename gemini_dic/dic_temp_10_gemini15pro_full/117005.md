McDougall: /məkˈduːɡəl/ | n. | 麦克杜格尔（姓氏） | A Scottish surname.  It is also used as a brand name for various food products, especially those related to a starch-free diet advocated by Dr. John McDougall. |  Example: My neighbor's last name is McDougall.  我的邻居姓麦克杜格尔。

Example:  I'm trying the McDougall diet. 我正在尝试麦克杜格尔饮食法。 | Synonym: (for the surname - none, as it's a proper noun)  (for the diet) plant-based, vegan | Antonym: (no direct antonym) | Noun
