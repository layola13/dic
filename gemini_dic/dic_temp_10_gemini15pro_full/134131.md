nonimperialistically:/nɒnɪmˈpɪəriəˈlɪstɪkli/
adverb
非帝国主义地
In a manner that does not promote or involve imperialism; without seeking to establish or maintain an empire.
The country decided to engage with other nations nonimperialistically, focusing on cooperation and mutual benefit. 这个国家决定以非帝国主义的方式与其他国家交往，注重合作和互利。
The organization promotes nonimperialistically cultural exchange programs. 该组织以非帝国主义的方式推动文化交流项目。
Synonyms: peacefully, cooperatively, equitably
Antonyms: imperialistically, expansionistically, aggressively
词性: 副词
