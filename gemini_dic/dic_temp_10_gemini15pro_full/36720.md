collocation:/ˌkɑːləˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 搭配；组合；词语搭配 |  The habitual juxtaposition of a particular word with another word or words with a frequency greater than chance. |  Example: "Heavy rain" is a common collocation in English.  暴雨在英语中是一个常见的搭配。 | synonym: combination, association, pairing | antonym: disassociation, separation | noun
