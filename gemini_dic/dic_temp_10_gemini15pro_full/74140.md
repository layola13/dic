词源：
foundation (n.) + -less (suffix) + -ness (suffix)

*   foundation: from Old French *fondacion, from Latin fundatio "a founding, laying a foundation," from fundare "to found," from fundus "bottom, base"
*   -less: Old English -leas "free from, without,"
*   -ness: Old English -nes(s), forming abstract nouns from adjectives and participles,
