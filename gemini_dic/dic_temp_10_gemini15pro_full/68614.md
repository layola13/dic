fantasticalness:/fænˈtæstɪkəlnəs/
n.
奇异；奇妙；不可思议
The quality of being fantastical; extraordinary strangeness.
The fantasticalness of the story captivated the children.  这个故事的奇幻色彩深深吸引了孩子们。
近义词：whimsicality, extraordinariness, surrealness, otherworldliness
反义词：ordinariness, commonness, mundanity, realism
词性:名词

fantastical:/fænˈtæstɪkl/
adj.
奇异的；奇妙的；荒诞的
Imaginative and unrealistic; bizarre.
He told a fantastical story about a unicorn. 他讲了一个关于独角兽的奇幻故事。
近义词：whimsical, extraordinary, surreal, otherworldly
反义词：ordinary, common, mundane, realistic
词性:形容词
