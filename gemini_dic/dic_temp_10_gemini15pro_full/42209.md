councilors:/ˈkaʊnsələrz/ (US), /ˈkaʊnsələz/ (UK)
n.
议员；顾问
A member of a council, especially a local government council.  A person who gives advice.
例句：The councilors voted to approve the new budget. 议员们投票通过了新的预算。

例句：The councilors advised the mayor on the matter. 顾问们就此事向市长提出了建议。

近义词：councilmembers, representatives, advisors
反义词:  None (No direct opposite for a person holding a position)
词性: Noun
