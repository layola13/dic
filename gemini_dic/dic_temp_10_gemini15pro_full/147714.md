overpress:/ˌoʊvərˈprɛs/| v. n.|过压，超压；加压过大|To apply excessive pressure to something; to pressurize something beyond its safe or normal limit.  The act of applying excessive pressure.|The engineer warned against overpressing the hydraulic system. 工程师警告不要对液压系统加压过大。|The overpress in the pipeline caused a rupture. 管道中的过压导致了破裂。|synonyms: overpressure, pressurize excessively|antonyms: depressurize, underpressurize|verb, noun
