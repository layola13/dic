fortnightlies:/ˈfɔːrtˌnaɪtliz/
noun
双周刊；每两周发生一次的事情
publications that are issued every two weeks; events that occur every two weeks
We subscribe to several fortnightlies. 我们订阅了几种双周刊。
The fortnightlies meetings are essential for team communication.  这些每两周一次的会议对于团队沟通至关重要。
synonyms: biweeklies
antonyms: N/A
n.


fortnightly:/ˈfɔːrtˌnaɪtli/
adjective
每两周一次的；双周的
happening every two weeks
We have fortnightly meetings to discuss progress. 我们每两周举行一次会议来讨论进展情况。
The magazine is published fortnightly. 这本杂志是双周刊。
synonyms: biweekly
antonyms: weekly, monthly
adj.


fortnightly:/ˈfɔːrtˌnaɪtli/
adverb
每两周一次地
every two weeks; biweekly
The newsletter is published fortnightly.  这封通讯每两周发布一次。
We meet fortnightly to discuss project updates. 我们每两周开会讨论项目更新。
synonyms: biweekly
antonyms: weekly, monthly
adv.


ETYMOLOGY->
From "fourteen nights," reflecting the original meaning of a period of two weeks.  "Fortnight" itself comes from Old English "fēowertyne niht," literally "fourteen nights."
