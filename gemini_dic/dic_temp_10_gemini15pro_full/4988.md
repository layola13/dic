alkargen:/ælˈkɑːrdʒən/
noun
烷基胂
an organic compound containing arsenic
The chemist synthesized a new alkargen.  化学家合成了一种新的烷基胂。
近义词: organoarsenic compound
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly inorganic arsenic compound)
词性: n.

Etymology:  From alkyl + arsenic + -gen (suffix forming nouns).  Alkyl refers to a univalent radical derived from an alkane by removal of a hydrogen atom. Arsenic is the chemical element. The suffix -gen indicates "something that produces or is produced".
