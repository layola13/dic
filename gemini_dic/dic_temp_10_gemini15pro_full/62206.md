encouraged:/ɪnˈkʌrɪdʒd/
|adj.|受到鼓舞的；被鼓励的|Having been given confidence, hope, or support.|She felt encouraged by the positive feedback. 她受到了积极反馈的鼓舞。|近义词：inspired, motivated, heartened|反义词：discouraged, disheartened|词性: 形容词
encourage:/ɪnˈkʌrɪdʒ/
|vt.|鼓励；促进；支持|To give support, confidence, or hope to (someone).|The teacher encouraged her students to ask questions. 老师鼓励学生提问。|近义词：support, promote, foster|反义词：discourage, hinder, obstruct|词性: 动词

词源:
From Old French *encoragier, from en- "in" + corage "courage," from Vulgar Latin *coraticum, from Latin cor "heart."
