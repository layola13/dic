gloriously:/ˈɡlɔːriəsli/
adverb
辉煌地；光荣地；荣耀地
in a glorious manner; splendidly.
The sun shone gloriously. 太阳辉煌地照耀着。
gloriously beautiful scenery  无比美丽的景色
Synonyms: magnificently, splendidly, superbly, triumphantly, resoundingly
Antonyms: ignominiously, shamefully, disgracefully, dishonorably, basely
Part of Speech: adverb
