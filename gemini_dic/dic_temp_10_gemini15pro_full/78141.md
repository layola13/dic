geometry: /dʒiˈɒmɪtri/ (UK) /dʒiˈɑːmətri/ (US) | n. | 几何学 | The branch of mathematics concerned with the properties and relations of points, lines, surfaces, solids, and higher dimensional analogs. |  Example: Euclidean geometry is the study of plane and solid figures. 欧几里得几何学是对平面和立体图形的研究。

Example:  She excelled in geometry in high school. 她在高中几何学方面很出色。
