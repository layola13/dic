nanotechnologist: /ˈnænəʊˌtekˈnɒlədʒɪst/ (British) /ˈnænəʊˌtekˈnɑːlədʒɪst/ (American) | n. | 纳米技术专家；纳米科技人员 | A scientist or engineer who specializes in nanotechnology, the manipulation of matter on an atomic and molecular scale. |  Dr. Smith is a leading nanotechnologist in the field of materials science. 史密斯博士是材料科学领域领先的纳米技术专家。

Nanotechnologists are developing new ways to deliver drugs directly to cancer cells. 纳米技术专家正在开发将药物直接输送到癌细胞的新方法。


近义词: nanoscientist, nanotechnician | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps one could consider "macro-scale researcher") | 词性: noun
