manichaeanisms:/ˌmænɪˈkiːənɪzəmz/
|n.|摩尼教主义；二元论；绝对的善恶观|A system of thought that divides the world into good and evil, light and darkness, often with a rigid and uncompromising view.|His philosophy, with its stark contrasts and lack of nuance, has been criticized for its Manichaeanisms.|近义词：dualism, absolutism|反义词：holism, relativism|词性:名词
