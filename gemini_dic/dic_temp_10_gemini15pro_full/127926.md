neomorph:/ˈniː.əʊ.mɔːrf/ | n. |新形态，新生体|A new and distinct form, especially in biology; a structural modification unlike anything previously seen in the evolutionary history of the lineage. | The scientists discovered a neomorph in the insect population, a wing structure never before observed in this species. 科学家在昆虫种群中发现了一种新的形态，一种在这个物种进化史上从未见过的翅膀结构。 | Example 2: The neomorph, a completely unexpected adaptation, puzzled the researchers.  这种全新的形态，一种完全出乎意料的适应性变化，让研究人员感到困惑。 | 近义词：mutant, variation, sport | 反义词: archetype, original form | noun
