deciduous:/dɪˈsɪdʒuəs/| adj.|落叶的，脱落的|Shedding foliage at the end of the growing season.  Trees that lose their leaves in the fall are deciduous. |例句：The maple tree in my yard is deciduous. 我院子里的枫树是落叶的。

例句：Deciduous trees provide beautiful autumn colors. 落叶树木在秋季呈现出美丽的色彩。|近义词：caducous, shedding|反义词：evergreen, persistent|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *deciduus*, from decidere "to fall off," from de- "down, off" + cadere "to fall."
