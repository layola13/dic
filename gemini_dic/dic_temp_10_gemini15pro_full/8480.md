antiaustrian:/ˌæntiˈɔːstriən/
adjective
反奥地利的
opposed to Austria, its policies, or its people.
His antiaustrian sentiments were well known. 他反奥地利的情绪是众所周知的。
近义词: anti-Habsburg
反义词: pro-Austrian, Austrian
词性: adjective
