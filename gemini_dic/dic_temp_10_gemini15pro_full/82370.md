growth:/ɡroʊθ/| n.|增长；生长；发展；发育；种植；肿瘤|An increase in size, number, or importance.  The process of developing or maturing physically, mentally, or spiritually.|例句：The growth of the economy has been impressive. 经济增长令人印象深刻。|例句：We track the growth of plants over time. 我们追踪植物的生长过程。|近义词：development, expansion, increase, progress, maturation|反义词: decline, decrease, shrinkage, reduction|词性:noun

Etymology: From Middle English *growthe*, from Old English *grōwþ*, from *grōwan* ("to grow"). Cognate with Dutch *groei* (“growth”), German *Gewächs* (“growth, plant”), Swedish *grodd* (“sprout, germ”).
