defuels:/diːˈfjuːəlz/ | v. |  去除燃料; 卸除燃料 | To remove fuel from something, such as a vehicle or aircraft. | The aircraft was defueled before maintenance.  飞机在维护前被卸除了燃料。| The ground crew began defueling the rocket after the launch was scrubbed.  发射取消后，地勤人员开始从火箭中卸除燃料。 | synonyms: drain, empty, unload | antonyms: fuel, refuel | verb

defuels:/diːˈfjuːəlz/ | n. | 去燃料; 卸除燃料（的行为）| The act of removing fuel from something. |  The defuels took several hours to complete. 卸除燃料花了好几个小时才完成。 | The quick defuels allowed the plane to be moved to a safer location.  快速卸除燃料使飞机能够被转移到一个更安全的地方。| synonyms: drainage, emptying, unloading | antonyms: fueling, refueling | noun
