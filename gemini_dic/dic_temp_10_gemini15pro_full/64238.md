equinumerous:/ˌikwiˈnjuːmərəs/ | adj. | 等数的，数量相等的 | Having the same number of elements.  | The two sets are equinumerous, meaning they have the same number of members. 这两个集合等数，意味着它们具有相同数量的成员。 | The set of even numbers is equinumerous with the set of odd numbers. 偶数集与奇数集等数。 | Synonyms: equal in number, numerically equivalent | Antonyms: unequal in number, numerically different | adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *aequi- (equal) + numerus (number).
