graduating:/ˈɡrædʒueɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 毕业的；即将毕业的 | Completing a course of study and receiving a diploma or degree. | Example:  She is graduating from college next year. 她明年大学毕业。

Example:  The graduating class is excited for the ceremony. 毕业班对毕业典礼感到兴奋。


|近义词: finishing, completing, concluding |反义词: commencing, beginning, starting |词性: adjective
