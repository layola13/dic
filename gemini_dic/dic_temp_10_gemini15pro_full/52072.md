dialoguing:/ˈdaɪəlɒɡɪŋ/ (UK), /ˈdaɪəlɔːɡɪŋ/ (US)| n./v. (present participle)|对话，交流|The act of engaging in a conversation or discussion, often with the aim of mutual understanding or resolving differences.  It can also refer to the process of exploring ideas or themes through conversation or written exchange.|例句：They are dialoguing with community leaders to address the concerns. 他们正在与社区领导对话，以解决这些担忧。|近义词：conversing, discussing, communicating, interacting|反义词：monologuing, lecturing|词性: noun/verb (present participle)
