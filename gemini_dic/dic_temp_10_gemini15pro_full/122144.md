mistresses:/ˈmɪstrɪsɪz/| n.|女主人，情妇，女教师，女艺术家|A woman who is in a position of authority or control.|例句：She is the mistress of the house. 她是这房子的女主人。

例句：He left his wife for his mistress. 他为了他的情妇离开了他的妻子。

近义词：lover, paramour, concubine, girlfriend, kept woman (for mistress in the sense of "lover")|反义词:servant, employee (for mistress in the sense of "woman in a position of authority")|词性:名词


mistresses:/ˈmɪstrɪsɪz/| n.|女主人，情妇，女教师，女艺术家|A skilled woman in a particular activity.|例句：She is a mistress of disguise. 她是个伪装高手。

例句：She was a mistress of the art of persuasion. 她精通说服的艺术。

近义词：expert, virtuoso, adept, proficient|反义词:amateur, novice (for mistress in the sense of "skilled woman")|词性:名词
