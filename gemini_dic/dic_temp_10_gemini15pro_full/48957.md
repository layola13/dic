degeling: /ˈdɛɡəlɪŋ/ | n. | 解冻，融化 (特指冰雪融化) | Thawing, melting, especially of ice and snow.  The degeling of the permafrost is a major concern for scientists. 永久冻土的解冻是科学家们主要关心的问题。| 近义词: thawing, melting | 反义词: freezing | 词性: 名词


词源:  From the Dutch verb "degelen," meaning "to thaw" or "to melt."  It's related to the German word "tauen" and the English word "thaw."
