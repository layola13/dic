goofing:/ˈɡuːfɪŋ/
verb (present participle of goof)
胡闹，开玩笑，犯傻
acting silly; making mistakes in a playful or harmless way
He was goofing around with his friends. 他和他的朋友们在一起胡闹。
近义词: messing around, fooling around, playing around, joking
反义词: being serious, working hard
词性: 动词(现在分词)


goof:/ɡuːf/
noun
傻瓜，错误
a silly or stupid person; a mistake
He made a goof and forgot to lock the door. 他犯了个傻，忘记锁门了。
近义词: blunder, mistake, error, slip-up
反义词: success, perfection
词性: 名词


goof:/ɡuːf/
verb
犯傻，开玩笑
to make a silly mistake; to spend time idly or foolishly
I goofed and wore mismatched socks. 我犯了个傻，穿了不成对的袜子.
近义词: blunder, err, mess up, fool around
反义词: succeed, excel
词性: 动词

词源:
Possibly from *gauf, a variant of Middle English gouph, "clown," perhaps of Scandinavian origin.
