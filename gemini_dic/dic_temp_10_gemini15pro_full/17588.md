beautiest:/ˈbjuːtiɪst/ |adj.|最美丽的|This is a non-standard superlative form of "beautiful." While grammatically incorrect, it might be used informally for emphasis or in a playful way.  It attempts to create a superlative form where "most beautiful" is already the standard. |She declared her cat the beautiest creature in the world. 她宣称她的猫是世界上最美丽的生物。|Near synonyms: most beautiful, fairest, prettiest|Antonym: ugliest|Adjective
