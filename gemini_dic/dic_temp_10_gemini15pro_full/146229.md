overexploits:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈsplɔɪts/ | v. | 过度开发，过度利用 | To make excessive use of something, especially natural resources, to the point of depletion or damage. |  The fishing industry has overexploited many fish populations, leading to their decline.  渔业过度捕捞了许多鱼类种群，导致它们数量下降。

| 近义词: overuse, misuse, abuse, exhaust | 反义词: conserve, preserve, protect, underutilize | 词性: verb

overexploit:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈsplɔɪt/ | n. | 过度开发，过度利用 | The excessive use of something, especially natural resources. | The overexploit of natural resources is a serious environmental problem.  自然资源的过度开发是一个严重的环境问题。
