counterdistinction:/ˌkaʊntər dɪˈstɪŋkʃən/| n.|对比; 对照;  |A distinction made by contrasting one thing with another.|He drew a counterdistinction between poetry and prose. 他对比了诗歌和散文。| 近义词: contrast, differentiation, juxtaposition | 反义词: similarity, resemblance | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- + distinction.  "Counter-"  means "opposite" or "contrary."  "Distinction" refers to the act of distinguishing or noting a difference.
