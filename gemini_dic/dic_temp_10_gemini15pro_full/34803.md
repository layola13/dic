climber:/ˈklaɪmər/| n.|攀登者；攀缘植物；向上爬的人或物|A climber is a person who climbs mountains or rocks, or a plant that climbs up walls or other supports. |The climber reached the summit after a grueling ascent.  这位登山者在艰苦的攀登后到达了顶峰。|The rose is a beautiful climber. 这玫瑰是一种美丽的攀缘植物。|近义词：mountaineer, alpinist, vine|反义词:descender|词性:noun
