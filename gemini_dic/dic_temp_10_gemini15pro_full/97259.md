inflicting:/ɪnˈflɪktɪŋ/
verb (present participle of inflict)
施加，使遭受，给予(打击)
Causing someone to experience something unpleasant or harmful.
The army inflicted heavy casualties on the enemy.  军队给敌人造成了重大伤亡。
He inflicted his terrible singing on everyone within earshot. 他把糟糕的歌声强加给所有听得见的人。
近义词: imposing, causing, dealing, administering
反义词: alleviating, soothing, relieving
词性: verb (present participle)

inflict:/ɪnˈflɪkt/
verb
施加，使遭受，给予(打击)
Cause (something unpleasant or painful) to be suffered by someone or something.
They inflicted a humiliating defeat on the home team. 他们给主队带来了耻辱性的失败。
Heavy casualties were inflicted on the enemy. 敌人遭受了重大伤亡。
近义词: impose, wreak, visit
反义词: alleviate, soothe, relieve
词性: verb

词源: From Latin inflictus, past participle of infligere "to strike or dash against, inflict," from in- "in, on, upon" + fligere "to strike."
