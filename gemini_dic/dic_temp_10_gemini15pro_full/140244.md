PHRASE->
offload onto =  转嫁给; 卸下到…上  He offloaded his work onto his colleagues. 他把他的工作转嫁给了他的同事们。
offload something to someone = 把…转嫁给… She offloaded her problems to her therapist. 她把她的问题告诉了她的治疗师。
offload goods/cargo = 卸货 The ship will offload its goods at the port. 这艘船将在港口卸货。
offload assets = 出售资产 The company is planning to offload some of its assets. 该公司正计划出售部分资产。
offload responsibilities =  卸下责任  The manager offloaded some of his responsibilities to his assistant. 经理把一些责任卸给了他的助手。
