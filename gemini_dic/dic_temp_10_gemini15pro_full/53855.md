discontentment:/ˌdɪskənˈtɛntmənt/
n.
不满，不满足
a feeling of wanting something more or different from what you have
例句：Her discontentment with her current job led her to search for a new one. 她对目前的工作不满，导致她开始寻找新工作。

例句：There was widespread discontentment among the workers about the new company policy.  工人们对公司的新政策普遍不满。

近义词：dissatisfaction, unhappiness, disaffection
反义词：contentment, satisfaction, happiness
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation) + contentment.  Contentment itself comes from Old French contentement, from contenter "to content," from Latin contentus, past participle of continere "to hold together, contain," from com- "together" + tenere "to hold."
