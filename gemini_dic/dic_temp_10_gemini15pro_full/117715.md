melancholily: /ˌmɛlənˈkɒlɪli/ | adv. | 忧郁地，悲哀地 | In a melancholic manner; sadly and pensively. | She smiled melancholily, remembering happier times. 她忧郁地笑了笑，回想起更快乐的时光。|  He walked melancholily through the empty streets. 他忧郁地走在空荡的街道上.| 近义词: sadly, gloomily, mournfully, pensively | 反义词: cheerfully, happily, joyfully | 词性: 副词

词源:  From melancholic + -ly.  Ultimately from the Greek melas (black) + kholé (bile), referring to the ancient belief that an excess of black bile caused melancholy.
