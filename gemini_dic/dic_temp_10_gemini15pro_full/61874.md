empowers:/ɪmˈpaʊərz/| v.|授权，使能够，赋予权力|To give someone the authority or power to do something; to make someone stronger and more confident, especially in controlling their life and claiming their rights.|Education empowers people to take control of their lives. 教育使人们能够掌控自己的生活。|近义词：authorize, enable, strengthen|反义词: weaken, disable, disempower|词性:动词
