nonpalliation:/ˌnɒnpæliˈeɪʃən/ | n. |  无缓解；不姑息；不缓和 |  The absence of palliation or relief of symptoms, especially in a medical context.  It refers to a situation where treatment is not focused on relieving symptoms but potentially on curing the underlying disease or addressing its cause. | The patient chose nonpalliation and aggressive treatment for the cancer.  病人选择了不姑息疗法和积极的癌症治疗。

The doctor's approach was one of nonpalliation, aiming for a complete cure.  医生的方法是不姑息疗法，旨在完全治愈。 | 近义词: nonrelief,  no alleviation | 反义词: palliation, alleviation, relief | 词性: noun
