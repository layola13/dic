dissonances:/ˈdɪsənənsɪz/
n.
不和谐音；不协调；冲突；矛盾
a combination of sounds that is unpleasant; disagreement or conflict between people or things.
The dissonance between the government's policies and the needs of the people is growing. 政府的政策与人民的需求之间的矛盾日益加剧。
The painting's bright colors create a striking dissonance. 这幅画鲜艳的色彩形成了鲜明的对比。

近义词:discord, conflict, clash, friction, disagreement, incongruity, inconsistency
反义词:harmony, consonance, agreement, concord, accord
