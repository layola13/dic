PHRASE->

demonstrate against (抗议):  Students demonstrated against the tuition increase. 学生们抗议学费上涨。

demonstrate for (支持，赞成): They demonstrated for better working conditions.  他们示威要求更好的工作条件。

demonstrate with (用...演示):  He demonstrated the principle with a simple experiment. 他用一个简单的实验演示了这个原理。

demonstrate how (演示如何):  She demonstrated how to use the new software. 她演示了如何使用新软件。

demonstrate that (证明): The study demonstrated that regular exercise is beneficial for health.  这项研究表明，经常锻炼有益于健康。
