gleeing:/ˈɡliːɪŋ/| adj.|幸灾乐祸的，欢欣鼓舞的|Showing great delight, especially from another person's misfortune. |He was gleeful at the news of his rival's failure. 他听到对手失败的消息欣喜若狂。|近义词：joyful, delighted, elated, jubilant|反义词：sad, sorrowful, dejected, miserable|词性:形容词
词源:From glee + -ing.  Ultimately from the Old French "glie," meaning "joy, amusement."
