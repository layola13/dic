amplexicaul: /ˌæmˈplɛksɪˌkɔːl/
adjective
抱茎的
(of a leaf or stipule) clasping or embracing the stem.
The plant's amplexicaul leaves gave it a distinctive appearance.  这种植物的抱茎叶赋予了它独特的外观。
amplexicaul leaves  抱茎叶

近义词: clasping, stem-clasping
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "petiolate" - having a leaf stalk)
词性: adjective
