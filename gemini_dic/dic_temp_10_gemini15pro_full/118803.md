metamerically:/ˌmɛtəˈmɛrɪkli/
adjective
分节地，体节地
arranged in metameres; segmented.  Relating to or exhibiting metamerism, the repetition of body parts along the length of an animal's body.
The earthworm's body is metamerically segmented. 蚯蚓的身体是分节的。
近义词: segmentally, serially
反义词: unsegmented
词性: adjective
