overrate:/ˌoʊvərˈreɪt/ | v. | 高估；过高评价 | To estimate the value or importance of something too highly. |  He tends to overrate his own abilities. 他往往高估自己的能力。 | overestimate, overvalue | underestimate, undervalue | verb

overrated:/ˌoʊvərˈreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 被高估的；评价过高的 | Rated or valued too highly. | The restaurant was overrated; the food was mediocre. 这家餐馆被高估了；食物很一般。 | overestimated, overvalued | underestimated, undervalued | adjective
