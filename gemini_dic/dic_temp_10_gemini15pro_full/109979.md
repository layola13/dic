liberties:/ˈlɪbərtiz/| n.|自由，自由权；特权；放肆，失礼| freedoms or rights, especially those guaranteed by law; the power or scope to act as one pleases; an action going beyond the usual limits of social acceptability; an act of undue intimacy. |例句：They fought for their liberties. 他们为他们的自由而战。

|例句：He took the liberty of borrowing your car. 他擅自借用了你的车。

|近义词：freedoms, rights, privileges, license|反义词：restrictions, constraints, limitations|词性:名词
