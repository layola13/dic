metrician:/məˈtrɪʃən/
n.
韵律学家；诗体学者；精通韵律的人
A specialist in metrics, especially in poetry or music.
The metrician analyzed the poem's complex rhythmic structure.  这位韵律学家分析了这首诗复杂的节奏结构。
prosodist, rhythmist
N/A
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin metricus (“metrical”), from Latin metrum (“meter”), from Ancient Greek μέτρον (métron, “measure”).
