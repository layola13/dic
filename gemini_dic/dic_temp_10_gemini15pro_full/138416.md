noumenon:/ˈnuːmɪnɒn/ (UK), /ˈnuːməˌnɑːn/ (US)| n. | 本体；自在之物 |A thing as it is in itself, as distinct from a phenomenon.  Something that can be known only by the mind, not through the senses.  A posited object or event known (if at all) without the use of the senses.|  Kant's theory distinguished between the phenomenon, which is the thing as it appears to us, and the noumenon, the thing as it is in itself.  康德的理论区分了现象，即事物在我们看来是什么样子，和本体，即事物本身是什么样子。|  近义词: thing-in-itself,  essence | 反义词: phenomenon | 词性: noun

noumena:/ˈnuːmɪnə/ (UK), /ˈnuːmənə/ (US)| n. | 本体；自在之物（复数） | Plural of noumenon.| The noumena are beyond our direct experience.  本体是我们无法直接体验到的。| 近义词: things-in-themselves, essences | 反义词: phenomena | 词性: noun
