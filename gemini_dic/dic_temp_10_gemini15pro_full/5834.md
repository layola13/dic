amatorian: /ˌæməˈtɔːriən/ | adj. n. | 业余的；业余爱好者 |  Of or relating to an amateur; not professional.  A person who engages in a pursuit, especially a sport, on an unpaid rather than a professional basis. | He's an amatorian golfer. 他是一位业余高尔夫球手。|  The painting was clearly the work of an amatorian. 这幅画显然是出自一位业余爱好者之手。 | 近义词: nonprofessional, dilettante, hobbyist | 反义词: professional, expert | 词性: adjective, noun | 词源: From French *amateur*, from Latin *amator* "lover," from *amare* "to love."
