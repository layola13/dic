embalming:/ɪmˈbɑːmɪŋ/ /ɛmˈbɑːmɪŋ/
n.|防腐，尸体保存|The embalming process preserves the body for viewing at the funeral. 防腐过程是为了在葬礼上瞻仰遗体而保存尸体。|例句：The embalming of the pharaoh was a complex ritual. 法老的防腐是一个复杂的仪式。|近义词：preservation, mummification|反义词:decomposition, decay|词性:名词
etymology: From Late Latin *imbalsamare, from in- "in" + balsamum "balsam."|词源：来自晚期拉丁语 *imbalsamare，由 in- “在” + balsamum “香脂” 组成。
