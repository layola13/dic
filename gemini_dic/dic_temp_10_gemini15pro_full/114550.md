malarious:/məˈleəriəs/| adj.|疟疾的；有疟疾的；容易患疟疾的|Relating to, infected with, or characterized by malaria.  Affected by malaria or susceptible to it.|例句：The region is known for its malarious swamps.  这个地区以疟疾肆虐的沼泽而闻名。|例句：Travelers should take precautions against malarious mosquitoes. 旅行者应该采取预防措施来防范携带疟疾的蚊子。|近义词：infected, unhealthy, miasmic, pestilential|反义词:healthy, salubrious|词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *malariosus, from Latin malaria (“bad air”).
