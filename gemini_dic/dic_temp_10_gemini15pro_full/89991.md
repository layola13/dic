horsing:/ˈhɔːrsɪŋ/| v. |胡闹，嬉戏；（尤指粗鲁地）摆弄，玩弄|To engage in rough or playful behavior, often involving physical contact; to handle or manipulate something, especially in a rough or careless way. |例句：The boys were horsing around in the backyard. 男孩子们在后院嬉闹。

例句：Stop horsing around with that expensive camera! 别乱动那个昂贵的相机！|近义词：fooling around, playing around, messing around, monkeying around|反义词：behaving, being serious|词性：动词
