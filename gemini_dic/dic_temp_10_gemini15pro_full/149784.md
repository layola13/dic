palaverment:/pəˈlævəmənt/
n.
空谈，冗长的讨论
A lengthy and often tedious discussion or conference, especially one that achieves little.
Example: After hours of palaverment, the committee still hadn't reached a decision. 经过数小时的空谈，委员会仍然没有达成任何决定。

Example: The endless palaverment about the budget left everyone frustrated. 无休止的预算扯皮让每个人都很沮丧。

近义词: discussion, conference, debate, negotiation, parley
反义词: agreement, decision, action


词性: noun

词源: From the verb "palaver,"  which comes from Portuguese "palavra" (word). Ultimately from Greek "parabola" (comparison, parable).
