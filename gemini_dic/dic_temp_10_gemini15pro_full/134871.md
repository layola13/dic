nonmonistic:/ˌnɑːnˈmɑːnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非一元论的 | Not pertaining to or characterized by monism; rejecting the doctrine that there is only one ultimate substance or principle. |  Philosophers who reject monism are by definition nonmonistic.  那些拒绝一元论的哲学家，根据定义就是非一元论者。|  Her nonmonistic worldview incorporates elements of both idealism and materialism. 她的非一元论世界观融合了唯心主义和唯物主义的元素。 |  pluralistic, dualistic | monistic | adjective
