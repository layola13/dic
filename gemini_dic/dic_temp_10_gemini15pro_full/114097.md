The media tends to magnify the dangers of air travel. 媒体往往夸大航空旅行的危险。|近义词：enlarge, amplify, expand, exaggerate, increase, intensify, boost|反义词：reduce, diminish, minimize, decrease, shrink, lessen|词性:动词
