binate:/ˈbaɪneɪt/| adj.|双生的，成对的|Growing in pairs; geminate.  Having two leaflets, as a compound leaf.|The binate leaves of the plant were a vibrant green. 这种植物的双生叶是鲜艳的绿色。|近义词：double, paired, geminate|反义词: single, solitary|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *binatus*, from bini (“two each, two by two”). Related to binī (“two, a pair”), from Proto-Indo-European *dwóh₁ (“two”).
