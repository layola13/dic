bug:/bʌɡ/| n. v. |昆虫；故障；窃听器；烦扰|A small insect. A fault or defect in a computer program, system, or machine. To annoy or bother someone.|例句：There was a bug in the software that caused the system to crash. 软件中有一个错误导致系统崩溃。|近义词：insect, glitch, error, defect, annoy, pester|反义词:feature, fix, repair|n.
bug:/bʌɡ/| v. |烦扰；装窃听器|To annoy or bother someone. To install a listening device in a place.|例句：He kept bugging me to go to the movies with him. 他一直缠着我跟他一起去看电影。|近义词：annoy, pester, bother, irritate|反义词:soothe, please|v.
