in retrospect:/ˌɪn ˈrɛtrəˌspɛkt/| adv.|回顾；回想起来|Looking back on past events, especially with the benefit of hindsight.|In retrospect, I should have taken that job offer. 回想起来，我应该接受那份工作.|近义词：in hindsight, looking back, on reflection|反义词:foresight, anticipation|词性:副词

词源: From Latin *retrospectus*, past participle of *retrospicere* "to look back," from *retro* "backward" + *specere* "to look."
