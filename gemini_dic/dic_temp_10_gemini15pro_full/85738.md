hawkeye:/ˈhɔːkaɪ/| n.|鹰眼; 视力敏锐的人;  神射手;  (尤指)鹰眼;  (Hawkeye) 鹰眼 (美国漫威漫画人物);|A hawkeye is a person who has very good eyesight, like a hawk.  A hawkeye can also refer to a skilled marksman. In popular culture, Hawkeye is a Marvel comics character known for his archery skills.|Clint Barton, also known as Hawkeye, is a skilled archer in the Avengers.  克林特·巴顿，也被称为鹰眼，是复仇者联盟中一名技艺高超的弓箭手。

The scout had a hawkeye and could spot enemy movement from miles away.  这位侦察兵目光敏锐，能从几英里外发现敌人的动向。|近义词：sharp-sighted, keen-sighted, eagle-eyed|反义词:blind, nearsighted|词性:noun
