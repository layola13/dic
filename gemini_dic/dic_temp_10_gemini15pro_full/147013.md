overlap:/ˌoʊvərˈlæp/ /ˌəʊvəˈlæp/| v. n.|重叠，交叠；部分相同；|The shingles on the roof overlap each other. 屋顶上的瓦片相互重叠。|Synonyms: coincide, intersect,  converge|Antonyms: diverge, separate|verb

overlap:/ˌoʊvərˈlæp/ /ˌəʊvəˈlæp/| n.|重叠部分；共同之处；|There is some overlap between the two subjects. 这两个科目有一些重叠之处。|Synonyms: intersection, convergence, common ground|Antonyms: divergence, separation|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + lap (v.).  "Over-" signifies "across" or "above," while "lap" refers to extending over something.
