doggrels:/ˈdɒɡrəl/ (US) /ˈdɑːɡrəl/ (UK)| n. |打油诗，蹩脚的诗|Trivial, poorly constructed verse, often deliberately humorous.|例句：He entertained the children with his silly doggrels. 他用他愚蠢的打油诗逗孩子们开心。

例句：The poem was nothing more than a collection of clumsy doggrels. 这首诗只不过是一堆笨拙的打油诗。|近义词：verse, rhyme, jingle, ditty|反义词:poetry, sonnet, epic|词性:noun
