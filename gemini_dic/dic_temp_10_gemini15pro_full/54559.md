dislocative:/ˌdɪsləˈkeɪtɪv/
adjective
移位的，导致脱位的
removing from the usual or proper place; causing dislocation.
The earthquake had a dislocative effect on the community, scattering families and destroying homes.  地震对社区造成了移位的影响，使家庭分散，房屋被毁。
The injury caused dislocative damage to his shoulder.  这伤导致他的肩膀脱位。
近义词:displacing, disruptive, disturbing
反义词:locating, placing, ordering
词性:adjective
