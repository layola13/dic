dopes:/doʊps/| n. |笨蛋，傻瓜；毒品；兴奋剂|A stupid person. Drugs, especially illegal drugs, that affect the user's mind and behaviour|He's such a dope, he locked his keys in the car. 他真是个笨蛋，把钥匙锁在车里了。|近义词：fools, idiots, drugs, narcotics|反义词:geniuses, smart people|noun
dopes:/doʊps/| v. |给…服用兴奋剂|To give someone drugs, especially illegal drugs, that affect the user's mind and behaviour|The racehorse was doped before the competition.  这匹赛马在比赛前被服用了兴奋剂。|近义词：drug, administer drugs|反义词:detoxify|verb
