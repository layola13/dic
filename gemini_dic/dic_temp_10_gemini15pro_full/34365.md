classicals:/ˈklæsɪkəlz/
noun
古典作品；古典音乐；古典文学作品
Works of art, literature, or music generally considered to be of the highest quality and lasting value, particularly those of ancient Greece or Rome.
She has a large collection of classicals on vinyl. 她收藏了大量的古典黑胶唱片。
The orchestra played several classicals during the concert. 乐队在音乐会上演奏了几首古典音乐作品。
synonyms: masterpieces, classics, canonical works
antonyms: contemporary works, modern works
