crownet:/ˈkraʊnɪt/| n.|小冠冕，小花冠|A small crown, often used in heraldry or as a decorative motif.  It can also refer to a garland or wreath worn on the head.|The knight's helmet was adorned with a golden crownet.|近义词：coronet, circlet, chaplet, garland|反义词: N/A|词性:名词


Etymology:
From Middle English *corounet, *corownet, from Old French coronete, diminutive of corone (“crown”), from Latin corona.
