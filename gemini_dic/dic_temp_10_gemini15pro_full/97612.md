inhaling:/ɪnˈheɪlɪŋ/
v.
吸入，吸气
The act of breathing in.
She was inhaling the fresh mountain air. 她正在呼吸新鲜的山间空气。
Synonyms: breathing in, inspiring
Antonyms: exhaling, breathing out
verb
