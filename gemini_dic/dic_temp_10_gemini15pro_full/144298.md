outspirited:/ˌaʊtˈspɪrɪtɪd/
adjective
沮丧的，气馁的
Deprived of spirit or courage; disheartened.
The team was outspirited after their crushing defeat.  该队在惨败后士气低落。
The constant criticism left him feeling outspirited and defeated.  不断的批评让他感到沮丧和挫败。
synonyms: dejected, disheartened, demoralized, discouraged, dispirited
antonyms: encouraged, spirited, heartened, emboldened
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + spirited, past participle of spirit (verb).  Meaning "deprived of spirit or courage" is from 1640s.
