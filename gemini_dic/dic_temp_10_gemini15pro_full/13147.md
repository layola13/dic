asymptomatic: /ˌeɪsɪmptəˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 无症状的 | Showing no symptoms of a particular disease. |  Many people with COVID-19 are asymptomatic, meaning they don't show any symptoms. 许多 COVID-19 患者是无症状的，这意味着他们没有表现出任何症状。

| Example 1: He was asymptomatic for weeks before finally developing a cough. 他在最终出现咳嗽之前数周都无症状。

| Example 2: The asymptomatic carriers of the virus are a major concern for public health officials. 病毒的无症状携带者是公共卫生官员的主要担忧。

| Synonym: subclinical, inapparent | Antonym: symptomatic | Word type: Adjective
