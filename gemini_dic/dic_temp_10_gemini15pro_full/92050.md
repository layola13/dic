hyperpigmentation: /ˌhaɪpərˌpɪɡmənˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 色素沉着过度 | Darkening of the skin or nails caused by increased melanin production. | Example:  Sun exposure can cause hyperpigmentation.  阳光照射会导致色素沉着过度。| Example:  She sought treatment for the hyperpigmentation on her face. 她寻求治疗脸上色素沉着过度的办法。 | Synonyms:  darkening, discoloration, melasma, chloasma | Antonyms: hypopigmentation | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- + pigmentation.  "Hyper-" is a prefix meaning "over" or "excessive."  "Pigmentation" refers to the coloring of tissue by pigment.
