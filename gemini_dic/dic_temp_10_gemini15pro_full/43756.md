creeping:/ˈkriːpɪŋ/| adj.|爬行的，蔓延的，缓慢发展的|moving slowly and quietly; spreading gradually|例句：The creeping ivy covered the wall. 爬山虎爬满了墙。|近义词：crawling, spreading, gradual|反义词：rapid, sudden|词性:形容词
creeping:/ˈkriːpɪŋ/| n.|爬行，缓慢移动|the act of creeping|例句：The creeping of the fog made everything seem eerie. 雾的蔓延让一切都显得怪异。|近义词：crawling|反义词：running|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *crepen*, from Old English *crēopan* (“to creep”), from Proto-Germanic *kreupaną* (“to creep”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)ker- (“to turn, bend”).
