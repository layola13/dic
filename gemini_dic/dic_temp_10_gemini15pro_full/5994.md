ambushlike:/ˈæmbʊʃˌlaɪk/
adjective
像埋伏的; 伏击似的
Resembling or characteristic of an ambush;  suggestive of a hidden attack.  Having the qualities of an ambush, such as suddenness, surprise, and concealment.
The soldiers moved through the forest with ambushlike stealth. 士兵们像埋伏一样潜行穿过森林。
The cat waited in ambushlike stillness before pouncing on the mouse. 猫像埋伏一样静静地等待，然后扑向老鼠。
近义词:  surreptitious, stealthy, furtive, clandestine
反义词: overt, open, obvious


ETYMOLOGY->
From ambush + -like.  "Ambush" comes from Old French "embusche," meaning "to place in ambush," from "en-" (in) + "busche" (woods).  "-like" is a suffix meaning "resembling," "characteristic of."
