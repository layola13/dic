emends:/ɪˈmɛndz/
verb
修改；修订；订正
to make corrections or improvements to a text or piece of writing
The editor emended the manuscript before publication. 编辑在出版前修订了稿件。
Synonyms: correct, revise, amend, improve, rectify
Antonyms: worsen, corrupt, damage, distort
词性: 动词
