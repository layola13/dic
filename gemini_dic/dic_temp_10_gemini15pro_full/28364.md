caravanserai:/ˌkærəˈvænsəˌraɪ/ | n. | 商队旅馆，大旅馆 | A large inn or roadside hotel in some Eastern countries, with a central courtyard for accommodating caravans. |  The weary travelers found respite in the caravanserai.  疲惫的旅行者在商队旅馆找到了喘息的机会。

The caravanserai offered shelter from the scorching desert sun. 商队旅馆提供了躲避沙漠烈日的庇护所。 | 近义词：inn, hostel, lodge | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Persian کاروانسرا kārvānsarā, from kārvān "caravan" + sarāy "palace, inn".
