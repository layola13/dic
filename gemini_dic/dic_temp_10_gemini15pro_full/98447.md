inspirable:/ɪnˈspaɪərəbl/
adj.
可激发灵感的；令人振奋的
Capable of being inspired; serving or tending to inspire.
Her courage is inspirable. 她的勇气令人振奋。
近义词:inspiring, motivating, uplifting
反义词:uninspiring, demotivating, depressing
词性:形容词

词源:
From inspire + -able.  "Inspire" comes from Latin *inspirare "to breathe or blow into," from in- "in" + spirare "to breathe."  The suffix "-able" forms adjectives meaning "capable of being," "worthy of being," or "tending to."
