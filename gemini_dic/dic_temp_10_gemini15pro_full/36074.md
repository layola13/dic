coefficients:/ˌkoʊɪˈfɪʃənts/ | n. | 系数 |A numerical or constant quantity placed before and multiplying the variable in an algebraic expression (e.g., 4 in 4x y).|The coefficients in the equation 2x + 3y = 6 are 2 and 3.  方程式 2x + 3y = 6 中的系数是 2 和 3。| synonyms: factors, multipliers | antonyms: N/A | noun
