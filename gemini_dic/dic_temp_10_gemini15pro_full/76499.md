galaxies:/ˈɡæləksiz/ | n. |星系|A system of millions or billions of stars, together with gas and dust, held together by gravitational attraction. |例句：The Hubble Telescope has captured stunning images of distant galaxies. 哈勃望远镜捕捉到了遥远星系的惊人图像。

例句：Scientists are studying the formation and evolution of galaxies. 科学家正在研究星系的形成和演化。|近义词：nebulae, star systems |反义词: None |词性:Noun
