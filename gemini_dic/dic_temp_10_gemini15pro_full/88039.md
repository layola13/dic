highbrow:/ˈhaɪbraʊ/ | n. | 高雅人士；自以为有文化的人 | A person who has or affects superior intellectual or cultural interests and tastes. |  She dismissed the critics as a bunch of highbrows. 她认为那些评论家只是一群自命不凡的家伙。 | synonyms: intellectual, aesthete | antonyms: lowbrow, philistine | noun

highbrow:/ˈhaɪbraʊ/ | adj. | 高雅的；曲高和寡的 |  Relating to or typical of a highbrow.  | The gallery hosted an exhibition of highbrow art.  这家画廊举办了一场高雅艺术展。| synonyms: intellectual, sophisticated, refined | antonyms: lowbrow, popular, unsophisticated | adjective
