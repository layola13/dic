estancia:/esˈtɑːnsjə/ /esˈtænsiə/
n.
大牧场；农场；庄园
A large estate or ranch in South America, especially in Argentina, Uruguay, or Paraguay.
The gauchos worked on the vast estancia. 高乔人在这片广阔的牧场上工作。

近义词:ranch, farm, estate, hacienda
反义词: (No direct opposite, possibly "smallholding")
