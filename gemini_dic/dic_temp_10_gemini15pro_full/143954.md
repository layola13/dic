outqueried:/ˌaʊtˈkwɪərid/
verb
胜过提问；在提问方面超过
To surpass or defeat someone in questioning or inquiry.
He outqueried his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中胜过对手的提问。
近义词: outsmart, outwit, surpass
反义词: lose to, be defeated by
词性: verb
