paraphimosis:/ˌpærəfɪˈmoʊsɪs/
n.
包茎嵌顿
Retraction of the foreskin of the penis resulting in constriction and swelling, preventing the foreskin from being returned to its normal position.
Paraphimosis is a urological emergency that requires prompt medical attention. 包茎嵌顿是一种泌尿科急症，需要立即就医。
The doctor quickly treated the paraphimosis to prevent further complications. 医生迅速治疗了包茎嵌顿，以防止进一步的并发症。
近义词:  phimosis (though not interchangeable, as phimosis refers to the inability to retract the foreskin)
反义词:  None (There isn't a direct opposite medical condition)
词性: Noun

Etymology:
From para- + Greek phimosis “a muzzling,” from phimoein “to muzzle, stop up,” from phimos “muzzle, gag.”
