busily:/ˈbɪzɪli/| adv.|忙碌地，勤奋地|In a busy manner; actively engaged in work or activity.|She was busily preparing for her exam. 她正忙着准备考试。|The bees busily buzzed around the flowers. 蜜蜂忙碌地在花丛中飞来飞去。|近义词：actively, diligently, industriously|反义词：lazily, idly|词性:副词
Etymology: From busy + -ly.  "Busy" comes from Old English *bysig, meaning "occupied, anxious, diligent," from Proto-Germanic *busiz. The "-ly" suffix is a common adverbial ending derived from Old English -lice.
