overtakes:/ˌoʊvərˈteɪks/ | v. | 超越，赶上，突然降临 | To catch up with and pass, as in a race; to come upon suddenly and unexpectedly. |  The car overtakes the truck on the highway. 这辆车在高速公路上超过了卡车。

The feeling of dread overtook him. 恐慌感突然降临到他身上。 | 近义词：pass, surpass, outstrip, overwhelm | 反义词: lag behind, trail |词性：动词
