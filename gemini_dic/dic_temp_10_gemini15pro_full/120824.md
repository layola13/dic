misauthorization:/ˌmɪsˌɔːθəraɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
未授权，错误授权
The act of authorizing something incorrectly or without proper authority.
Due to a misauthorization, the funds were transferred to the wrong account. 由于错误授权，资金被转移到了错误的账户。
misappropriation, unauthorized use, unlawful access
authorization, approved access
noun
