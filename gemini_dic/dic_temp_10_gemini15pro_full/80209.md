godmothers: /ˈɡɒdmʌðəz/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːdmʌðərz/ (US)| n. |教母（复数）|A female sponsor at a Christian baptism who promises to help with the child's religious education and general upbringing.  Also, a close female friend of a child's parents, especially in some cultures, who is expected to take an interest in the child's welfare.| My daughter has three godmothers. 我的女儿有三个教母。| sponsor, mentor, guide | N/A | noun
