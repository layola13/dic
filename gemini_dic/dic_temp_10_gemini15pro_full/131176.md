nonbristled:/nɒnˈbrɪsəld/
adjective
无刚毛的，不带鬃毛的
lacking bristles; smooth
The nonbristled brush is gentler on delicate skin.  无刚毛的刷子对娇嫩的皮肤更温和。
The worm had a smooth, nonbristled body.  这条虫子有一个光滑的、没有刚毛的身体。
Synonyms: smooth, hairless, glabrous
Antonyms: bristled, hairy, hirsute
word class: adjective
