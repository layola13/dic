oxidized:/ˈɒksɪdaɪzd/ (American) /ˈɒksɪdʌɪzd/ (British)| adj.|氧化的，被氧化的|Combined chemically with oxygen.  Having undergone a reaction in which electrons are lost to another substance (an oxidizing agent).|例句：The oxidized metal turned a rusty brown.  被氧化的金属变成了锈棕色。

例句：The apple quickly oxidized and turned brown after being cut. 苹果切开后迅速氧化变成棕色。|近义词：rusted, corroded, tarnished|反义词：reduced|词性:形容词
