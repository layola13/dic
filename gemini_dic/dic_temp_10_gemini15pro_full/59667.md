echinodermatous:/ɪˌkaɪnəˈdɜːrmətəs/
adjective
棘皮动物的
Relating to or belonging to the phylum Echinodermata, a group of marine invertebrates characterized by radial symmetry and a water vascular system.
例句：Starfish are echinodermatous animals. 海星是棘皮动物。

例句：The echinodermatous creatures exhibit radial symmetry. 这些棘皮动物呈现出辐射对称性。

近义词：spiny-skinned
