overfulfilling: /ˌoʊvərˌfʊlˈfɪlɪŋ/
adjective
过度完成的；超额完成的
Exceeding the requirements or expectations of a task or goal.
The team was commended for overfulfilling their sales quota.  该团队因超额完成销售指标而受到表彰。

She consistently overfulfills her duties and goes above and beyond. 她总是超额完成她的职责，并且做得非常出色。


近义词: exceeding, surpassing, outperforming
反义词: underperforming, underachieving
词性: adjective


词源:
"Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "fulfilling" (present participle of "fulfill," meaning "to carry out or achieve").  "Over-"（前缀，意思是“过度地”）+ "fulfilling"（"fulfill" 的现在分词，意思是“执行或实现”）。
