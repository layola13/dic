indier:/ˈɪndiər/ | adj. | 更独立的，更另类的 | More independent or alternative, especially in music, film, or fashion.  It's the comparative form of "indie." |  The band's new album is much indie than their previous work.  这个乐队的最新专辑比他们以前的作品更独立/另类。 |  They went to a screening of an indie film. 他们去看了一场独立电影的放映。 |近义词：alternative, unconventional, non-mainstream | 反义词：mainstream, commercial | 词性:形容词
