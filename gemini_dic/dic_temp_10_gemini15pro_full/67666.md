extrude:/ɪkˈstruːd/ (US) /ɪkˈstruːd/ (UK)| v. |挤压；推出；使突出|to shape (metal, plastic, etc.) by forcing it through a die or to force out or push forth|The machine extrudes the plastic into long strips.  这台机器将塑料挤成长条。
