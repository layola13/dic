over-morally:/ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrəli/
adj.
过度道德的，过分拘泥于道德的
Excessively or unduly concerned with morality; exhibiting an exaggerated sense of moral righteousness.
He criticized the over-morally judgmental attitude of some religious groups. 他批评了一些宗教团体过分拘泥于道德评判的态度。
Her over-morally stance on the issue alienated many of her supporters.  她在这个问题上过分道德的立场疏远了许多支持者。
近义词: moralistic, puritanical, self-righteous
反义词: amoral, immoral, unethical
词性: adjective

词源:
"over-" (prefix) + "morally" (adverb)
"over-" 表示“过度，过分”
"morally" 源自 "moral" (道德的)，最终源自拉丁语 "moralis" (与习俗有关的).
