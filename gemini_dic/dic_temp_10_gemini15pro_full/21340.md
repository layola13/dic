blooming:/ˈbluːmɪŋ/| adj.|盛开的；繁荣的；非常的（用于加强语气）| flourishing, thriving; used for emphasis, often expressing annoyance |The garden is full of blooming flowers. 花园里开满了鲜花。|近义词：flourishing, thriving, blossoming, blooming|反义词：withered, fading, declining, dying|词性:形容词
Etymology: From bloom (n.) + -ing (suffix indicating present participle or gerund).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *bloma.
