noninvestor:/ˌnɑːnɪnˈvɛstər/ | n. | 非投资者 | A person who does not invest money in stocks, bonds, or other securities. | Example: Many noninvestors are intimidated by the complexities of the stock market. 许多非投资者都被股票市场的复杂性吓倒了。

Example:  Financial literacy programs can help noninvestors understand the benefits of investing. 金融知识普及项目可以帮助非投资者了解投资的好处。| 近义词: non-shareholder | 反义词: investor | 词性: noun
