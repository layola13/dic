gordian:/ˈɡɔːrdiən/| adj.|戈迪亚斯的；难解的；复杂的|Extremely difficult to solve; intricate; involved.  Relating to the Gordian Knot.|例句：The negotiators faced a Gordian knot of conflicting interests.  谈判代表们面临着错综复杂的利益冲突。

例句：Cutting the Gordian knot can sometimes be the most effective solution.  快刀斩乱麻有时是最有效的解决办法。|近义词：intricate, complex, involved, knotty|反义词：simple, easy, straightforward|adjective
