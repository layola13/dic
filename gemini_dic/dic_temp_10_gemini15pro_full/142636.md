ostler:/ˈɒstlə(r)/ (US: /ˈɑːstlər/)| n. |马夫；旅店里的马夫|A person who takes care of horses at an inn or stable.|The ostler led the weary travelers' horses to the stable.  旅店里的马夫把疲惫的旅客的马牵到马厩。|Synonyms: stableman, groom, hostler|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps guest, traveler as someone served by an ostler)|noun
