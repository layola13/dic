fleetingnesses:/ˈfliːtɪŋnɪsɪz/
n.
短暂性；飞逝；转瞬即逝
The quality of being fleeting; transience.  The fleetingnesses of youth are often lamented in old age.
The fleetingnesses of fashion trends are well-known.  时尚潮流的短暂性是众所周知的。
Synonyms: transience, ephemerality, brevity, impermanence
Antonyms: permanence, eternity, durability, longevity
noun


fleeting:/ˈfliːtɪŋ/
adj.
短暂的；飞逝的；转瞬即逝的
Lasting for a very short time.  A fleeting glimpse of a rare bird.
She had a fleeting moment of doubt. 她有一瞬间的怀疑。
Synonyms: brief, transient, ephemeral, momentary
Antonyms: lasting, enduring, permanent, prolonged
adjective
