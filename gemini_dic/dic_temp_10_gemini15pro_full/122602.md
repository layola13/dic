modernization:/ˌmɒdənaɪˈzeɪʃn/ (UK) /ˌmɑːdərnaɪˈzeɪʃn/ (US)| n.|现代化|The process of adapting something to modern needs or standards, often involving new technologies and ways of thinking.|The modernization of the factory increased productivity significantly. 工厂的现代化大大提高了生产力。|synonyms: updating, upgrading, improvement, development, innovation|antonyms: stagnation, regression, deterioration|noun
