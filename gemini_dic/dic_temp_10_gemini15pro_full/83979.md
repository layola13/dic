half-critically:/ˌhɑːfˈkrɪtɪkli/ | adv. | 半批评地；略带批评地 |  Expressing mild or partial criticism; not fully or severely critical.  It suggests a mixture of appreciation and critique. |  "He reviewed the performance half-critically, praising the actors' energy but questioning the director's choices." 他略带批评地评价了这场演出，赞扬了演员们的活力，但质疑了导演的选择。 |  near synonyms: partly critically, mildly critically, qualifiedly, with reservations |  antonyms: uncritically, enthusiastically, completely approvingly | adverb

half:/hɑːf/ | adj. n. adv. | 一半的；半个 | One of two equal or approximately equal parts of a divisible whole. | I ate half the cake. 我吃了半个蛋糕。| near synonyms: semi-, demi-, partly | antonyms: whole, fully, entirely | adjective/noun/adverb
critically:/ˈkrɪtɪkli/ | adv. | 批评地；批判性地；危急地 | In a way that expresses disapproval or finds fault.  Also, in a way that analyzes and evaluates. Additionally, in a way that indicates a serious or dangerous situation. | "She looked at the painting critically, noticing the uneven brushstrokes." 她批判性地看了看这幅画，注意到了笔触不均匀。 | near synonyms: disapprovingly, judgmentally, analytically, seriously, gravely | antonyms: approvingly, uncritically, superficially, lightly | adverb
