meiostemonous:/ˌmaɪoʊˈstɛmənəs/
adjective
少雄蕊的；半雄蕊的
Having fewer stamens than petals, or half as many stamens as petals.
The flower is meiostemonous, with only five stamens compared to its ten petals.  这朵花是少雄蕊的，只有五个雄蕊，而花瓣有十个。
The botanist identified the plant as meiostemonous due to its stamen count.  植物学家根据其雄蕊数量将该植物鉴定为少雄蕊的。
近义词:oligandrous, oligostamenous
反义词:polyandrous, polystamenous
词性:adjective
词源:From Greek *meio- (less) + stemon (stamen)
