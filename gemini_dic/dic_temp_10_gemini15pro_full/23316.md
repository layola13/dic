boycottism:/ˈbɔɪkɒtɪzəm/ (US) /ˈbɔɪkɒtɪzəm/ (UK)| n.|抵制，联合抵制|The act of boycotting; a refusal to buy, use, or participate in something as a way of protesting.  This can refer to goods, services, organizations, or even individuals.|The boycottism of the company's products significantly impacted their sales.  该公司产品的抵制对其销售产生了重大影响。|Synonyms: boycott, embargo, sanction|Antonyms: patronage, support|noun
