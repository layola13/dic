censused:/ˈsɛnsəst/ | v. | 已统计的，被统计的 | past participle of "census" meaning that someone or something has been officially counted or included in a census.  | The population was censused last year. 去年人口普查完成了。| Synonym: counted, enumerated, surveyed | Antonym: uncounted, omitted | Verb (past participle)

census:/ˈsɛnsəs/ | n. vt. | 人口普查，统计；调查 | an official count or survey of a population, typically recording various details of individuals. |  The government conducts a census every ten years. 政府每十年进行一次人口普查。| The researchers censused the bird population in the area. 研究人员统计了该地区的鸟类数量。 | Synonym: count, survey, poll  | Antonym:  None (A census is a specific type of counting, so a direct antonym isn't applicable) | Noun, Verb
