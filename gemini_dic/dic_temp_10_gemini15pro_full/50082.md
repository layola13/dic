dentation:/dɛnˈteɪʃən/
n.
齿状; 牙列; 锯齿状
the arrangement or condition of the teeth in a particular species or individual.
Example 1: The dentist examined the patient's dentation.  牙医检查了病人的牙列。
Example 2:  The dentation of a shark is quite different from that of a human. 鲨鱼的齿列与人类的齿列截然不同。
Synonym: teeth, dentition
Antonym: edentulousness (lack of teeth)
noun
