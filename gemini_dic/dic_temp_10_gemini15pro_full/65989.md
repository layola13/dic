exalter:/ɪɡˈzɔːltər/| n.| 提升者，赞扬者| someone who praises or glorifies something or someone|The poet was an exalter of nature's beauty. 这位诗人是歌颂自然之美的倡导者。|例句：The king's exalter praised his wisdom and bravery. 国王的赞美者称赞他的智慧和勇敢。

例句：She is known as an exalter of traditional values. 她以倡导传统价值观而闻名。|近义词：praiser, lauder, eulogist|反义词：critic, detractor, denigrator|词性:noun

exalt:/ɪɡˈzɔːlt/| vt.| 提升，赞扬，使得意|to raise in rank, power, or character; to praise highly; to make joyful|The news of their victory exalted the spirits of the soldiers. 胜利的消息鼓舞了士兵们的士气。|例句：The company exalted him to the position of CEO. 公司将他提升为首席执行官。

例句：The singer's voice exalted the audience. 歌手的歌声使观众陶醉。|近义词：praise, glorify, elevate, acclaim|反义词：debase, degrade, humble|词性:verb
