multi-partism:/ˈmʌltiˌpɑːtɪzəm/ | n. | 多党制 | A system in which multiple political parties have the capacity to gain control of government offices, separately or in coalition. |  Many European countries operate under multi-partism. 许多欧洲国家实行多党制。| synonyms: multi-party system, pluralistic system | antonyms: one-party system, single-party system | noun
