maturescent:/ˌmætʃʊˈrɛsənt/ | adj. | 成熟中的，接近成熟的 | Becoming mature; approaching maturity. | The maturescent fruit began to change color. 成熟中的果实开始变色。| The vineyard was filled with the sweet scent of maturescent grapes. 葡萄园里充满了成熟葡萄的甜香。 | 近义词: ripening, maturing, developing | 反义词: immature, unripe, nascent | 词性: adjective
Etymology: From Latin *maturescent-, present participle of maturescere "to grow ripe," from maturus "ripe."
