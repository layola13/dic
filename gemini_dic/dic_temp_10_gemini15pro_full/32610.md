chirpier:/ˈtʃɜːrpiər/ | adj. | 更快乐的，更活泼的 | Comparative form of chirpy: more chirpy.  Meaning having or showing a cheerful and lively mood. | She sounded much chirpier after her holiday. 她度假回来后听起来快乐多了。 |  The birds were chirpier this morning. 今早鸟儿们更活泼了。 | synonyms:  more cheerful, more lively, brighter, perkier, sunnier, more upbeat, more buoyant | antonyms: gloomier, sadder, more depressed, less lively | adjective
