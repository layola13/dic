felspar:/ˈfɛlspɑːr/ (US), /ˈfɛlspɑː/ (UK)| n. |长石|A group of rock-forming tectosilicate minerals that make up as much as 60% of the Earth's crust.  They are found in igneous, metamorphic, and sedimentary rocks.|Feldspar is a common component of granite. 长石是花岗岩的常见成分。|
