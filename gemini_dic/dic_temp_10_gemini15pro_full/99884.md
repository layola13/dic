internalities:/ɪnˌtɜːrˈnælɪtiz/ | /ɪnˌtɜːrˈnælətiz/
n.|内部因素，内在性|The factors within a system or organization, such as its structure, culture, or processes.  These are often contrasted with externalities, which are factors outside the system. |The company's internalities, such as its inefficient management and outdated technology, contributed to its decline.|Synonyms: inner workings, internal factors, internal mechanisms|Antonyms: externalities, external factors|Noun
