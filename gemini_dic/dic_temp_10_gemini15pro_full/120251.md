mingy:/ˈmɪndʒi/| adj.|吝啬的，小气的|Unwilling to give or spend money; mean.|He's so mingy, he never buys anyone a drink. 他太小气了，从不请任何人喝饮料。|近义词：stingy, miserly, tight-fisted, parsimonious|反义词：generous, liberal, lavish|词性:形容词
词源:  Likely a blend of "mean" and "stingy."
