antiacademic:/ˌæntiˌækəˈdɛmɪk/
adjective
反对学术的；非学院派的；不注重学术的
Opposed to or inconsistent with the principles or practices of an academy or school; not scholarly.
His antiacademic attitude made him unpopular with his professors. 他对学术的反对态度让他不受教授们的欢迎。

近义词:unscholarly, nonacademic, intellectual,  
反义词:academic, scholarly, intellectual

词性:adjective
