grapheme:/ˈɡræfiːm/ | n. | 字素；书写单元 | A grapheme is the smallest unit in a writing system. It can be a letter, a number, a punctuation mark, or any other symbol.  | The word "cat" contains three graphemes: c, a, and t.  单词"cat"包含三个字素：c、a和t。 | synonym: character, letter, symbol | antonym: phoneme (although they are contrasting concepts, not true opposites) | noun

graphemes:/ˈɡræfiːmz/ | n. | 字素（复数） | Graphemes are the smallest units in a writing system. They can be letters, numbers, punctuation marks, or other symbols.  |  The English alphabet contains 26 graphemes. 英文有26个字素。 | synonym: characters, letters, symbols | antonym: phonemes (although they are contrasting concepts, not true opposites) | noun
