denticulate:/dɛnˈtɪkjʊleɪt/
adjective
有小齿的，细齿状的
Having small tooth-like projections.
The denticulate leaf margin of the plant helped identify it.  这种植物的细齿状叶缘有助于识别它。

The edge of the stamp was denticulate.  邮票的边缘呈锯齿状。
synonym: toothed, serrated, crenate
antonym: smooth, entire
adjective
