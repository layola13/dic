nonhumanistic:/ˌnɒnˌhjuːməˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非人文主义的 | Not humanistic; lacking humanism; not concerned with human interests or values.  Characterized by a lack of empathy, concern for human welfare, or appreciation for human culture and values. |  The nonhumanistic approach to animal testing prioritizes scientific advancement over animal welfare.  非人文主义的动物试验方法将科学进步置于动物福利之上。

The corporation's nonhumanistic policies led to widespread employee dissatisfaction. 该公司非人道主义的政策导致员工普遍不满。 | Near synonyms:  inhuman, impersonal, mechanistic, cold, unfeeling | Antonyms: humanistic, humane, compassionate, empathetic, caring | Word Type: Adjective
