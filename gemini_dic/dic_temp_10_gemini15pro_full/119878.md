militantly:/ˈmɪlɪtəntlɪ/
adverb|好战地， воинственно (Russian), 战斗性地|Fighting aggressively and determinedly for a political or social cause. |The union has been campaigning militantly for better pay. 工会一直在积极争取更高的薪酬。|
The protesters marched militantly through the streets. 抗议者 воинственно (Russian, militantly) 走上街头。
|Synonyms: aggressively, combatively, belligerently|Antonyms: peacefully, passively, nonviolently|
