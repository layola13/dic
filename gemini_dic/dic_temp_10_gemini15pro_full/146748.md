overhumanized:/ˌoʊvərˈhjuːmənaɪzd/
adjective
过度人化的，过分人性化的
Having been given or attributed too many human characteristics, especially to the point of being unrealistic or sentimental.
The portrayal of animals in children's books is often overhumanized, giving them complex emotions and motivations they don't possess.  这些儿童读物中对动物的刻画往往过于人性化，赋予它们并不具备的复杂情感和动机。
The movie's villain was overhumanized, making it difficult to see him as truly evil.  这部电影中的反派角色被过度人性化了，以至于很难将他视为真正的邪恶。
近义词:anthropomorphized, sentimentalized
反义词:dehumanized, objectified
词性:adjective
