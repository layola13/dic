improvability:/ɪmˌpruːvəˈbɪləti/
n.
可改进性, 可提升性
The quality of being able to be improved.
The improvability of the team's performance is evident in their recent wins.  这个团队的成绩的可提升性在他们最近的胜利中显而易见。
synonyms:  enhanceability,  upgradability,  perfectibility,  amenability
antonyms:  incorrigibility,  immutability,  fixity,  unalterability
noun
