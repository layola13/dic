deepened:/ˈdiːpənd/ | v. | 加深，加剧，变深 |The crisis deepened as more countries became involved. 危机随着更多国家的卷入而加深了。|例句：The river deepened after the heavy rain.  暴雨过后，河水变深了。

例句：His frown deepened as he read the letter. 他读信时，眉头皱得更紧了。|近义词：intensified, increased, magnified, strengthened |反义词： lessened, decreased, reduced, lightened |词性：动词

词源: From Middle English *depnen, from Old English dēopnian (“to deepen, make deep”), from dēop (“deep”).
