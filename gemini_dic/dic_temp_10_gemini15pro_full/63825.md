epidural:/ˌɛpɪˈdjʊərəl/ /ˌɛpɪˈdʊrəl/| adj. n.|硬膜外的，硬膜外麻醉|An epidural is a type of regional anesthesia that blocks pain in a particular region of the body. The goal of an epidural is to provide analgesia, or pain relief, rather than anesthesia, which leads to total lack of feeling.|She had an epidural during labor. 她分娩时进行了硬膜外麻醉。|The epidural provided excellent pain relief. 硬膜外麻醉提供了极好的疼痛缓解。|近义词：spinal anesthesia|反义词:general anesthesia|词性:adjective, noun
