bookbindery:/ˈbʊkˌbaɪndəri/
n.
装订厂，书籍装订作坊
A workshop or factory where books are bound.
The old bookbindery still uses traditional methods.  这家老装订厂仍然使用传统方法。
The bookbindery produced beautiful, hand-crafted books.  这家装订厂生产精美的手工书籍。
synonyms: bindery
antonyms: N/A
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From book + bindery (from bind + -ery).  "bind" comes from Old English "bindan," meaning "to tie, fasten." "-ery" is a suffix denoting a place where a specific activity is carried out.
