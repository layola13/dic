all-over:/ˈɔːl ˈoʊvər/ | adj. | 全身的，遍布的 | Covering the whole surface; affecting the whole body. |  I have an all-over ache. 我浑身都疼。 |  Widespread, overall, general | Localized, specific | adjective


all-over:/ˌɔːl ˈoʊvər/ | adv. | 遍及，到处 | Covering or affecting all parts. | The rash spread all over his body.  皮疹蔓延到他全身。 | Everywhere, throughout | Nowhere, locally | adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "all" + "over". "All" meaning the whole quantity, extent, or duration of something, and "over" meaning extending or occurring throughout a place or period.
