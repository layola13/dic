attestation:/ˌætɛˈsteɪʃən/ | n. |证明，证词，证据|The document served as an attestation of his birth. 这份文件用作他出生的证明。|例句：The lawyer presented the attestation of the witness in court. 律师在法庭上出示了证人的证词。

例句：Her signed attestation confirmed the authenticity of the document. 她签字的证明书确认了文件的真实性。

近义词：testimony, evidence, proof, verification, confirmation |反义词：denial, refutation, contradiction |词性：名词

attest:/əˈtɛst/ | v. |证明，证实|I can attest to the quality of their work. 我可以证明他们的工作质量。|例句：Several witnesses can attest to his presence at the scene. 几位目击者可以证明他在现场。

例句：The lawyer asked her to attest to the validity of the signature. 律师要求她证明签名的有效性。

近义词：confirm, verify, testify, corroborate, substantiate |反义词：deny, contradict, refute |词性：动词
