frontispiece:/ˈfrʌntɪspiːs/
noun
卷首插画;扉页
An illustration facing the title page of a book.
The frontispiece of the book was a striking portrait of the author. 这本书的卷首插画是作者引人注目的肖像。
Synonyms: illustration, plate, engraving, picture
Antonyms: back cover, epilogue
