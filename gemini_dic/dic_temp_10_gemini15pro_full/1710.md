acoumeter: /əˈkuːmɪtər/
n.
听力计；测听计
An instrument used to measure the acuity of hearing.
The doctor used an acoumeter to test the patient's hearing. = 医生用听力计测试病人的听力。

The audiologist used a variety of acoumeters to assess the child's hearing loss. = 听力学家使用各种听力计来评估孩子的听力损失。

近义词: audiometer
反义词: None
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French acoumètre, from Greek akouein (to hear) + -meter (measure).

USAGE->
The acoumeter is an important tool in audiology. = 听力计是听力学中的重要工具。
The use of the acoumeter allows for a quick and basic assessment of hearing. = 使用听力计可以快速、基本地评估听力。

The doctor held the acoumeter near the patient's ear. = 医生将听力计放在病人耳朵附近。
She couldn't hear the acoumeter at certain frequencies, indicating hearing loss. = 她听不到某些频率的听力计，表明听力损失。
