ectocuneiform:/ˌɛktoˈkjuːnɪfɔːrm/
n.
外楔骨
The lateral or third cuneiform bone of the tarsus.  It articulates with the cuboid, navicular, intermediate cuneiform, and third metatarsal bones.
The ectocuneiform bone, also known as the lateral cuneiform, is located in the foot.
