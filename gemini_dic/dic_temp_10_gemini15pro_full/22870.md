botanists: /ˈbɒtənɪsts/ (British) /ˈbɑːtənɪsts/ (American) | n. | 植物学家 | A scientist who studies plants. | Example: The botanists discovered a new species of orchid in the rainforest.  植物学家在雨林中发现了一种新的兰花品种。

| Example:  Many botanists are concerned about the effects of climate change on plant life. 许多植物学家担心气候变化对植物生命的影响。


| 近义词: plant scientists, phytologists | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
