misname:/ˌmɪsˈneɪm/
verb|错误命名；叫错名字|To give someone or something a wrong name.|He misnamed the file, causing confusion later. 他给文件起了个错误的名字，后来造成了混乱。|Example: The journalist misnamed the senator in his article.  记者在文章中把参议员的名字写错了。

Example:  I accidentally misnamed him, calling him "David" instead of "Daniel." 我不小心叫错了他的名字，把他叫成了“大卫”，而不是“丹尼尔”。
|synonyms: miscall, mislabel, mistitle|antonyms: name correctly, identify correctly|词性:动词

misname:/ˌmɪsˈneɪm/
noun|名字的误用; 错误的名字|A wrong name or the act of giving a wrong name.|The use of "John Smith" was a misname; his real name was "Tom Jones." 使用“约翰·史密斯”是错误的名称；他的真名是“汤姆·琼斯”。|Example: The misname on the invitation caused some embarrassment. 邀请函上的名字错误引起了一些尴尬。

Example: Using the wrong title for her was a serious misname. 用错她的头衔是一个严重的错误。
|synonyms: misnomer, wrong name, incorrect name|antonyms: correct name, right name|词性:名词
