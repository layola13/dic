expurgation:/ˌekspərˈɡeɪʃn/
n.
净化；删节；清除
the removal of matter thought to be objectionable or unsuitable from a book or account.
The editor made several expurgations to the manuscript before publication. 编辑在出版前对稿件进行了几处删节。
近义词：censorship, bowdlerization, purging, cleansing
反义词：inclusion, addition, insertion
词性: noun

expurgate:/ˈekspərɡeɪt/
v.
净化；删节；清除
remove matter thought to be objectionable or unsuitable from (a book or account).
The publisher expurgated the novel to avoid offending religious readers. 出版商删去了小说中一些可能冒犯宗教读者的内容。
近义词：censor, bowdlerize, purge, cleanse
反义词：include, add, insert
词性: verb

词源:  From Latin *expurgatus*, past participle of *expurgare* (“to cleanse, purify”), from *ex-* (“out of”) + *purgare* (“to purge, cleanse”).
