neuvaine:/nʊˈveɪn/ |n.| 九日敬礼，九日祈祷 |A neuvaine is a Roman Catholic devotion consisting of prayers or services on nine consecutive days.  It is a public or private devotion in the Catholic Church to obtain special graces.| My grandmother always says a neuvaine before important family events. 我的祖母总是在重要的家庭活动前进行九日祈祷。|Synonym: devotion, prayer |Antonym: blasphemy, irreverence |noun
