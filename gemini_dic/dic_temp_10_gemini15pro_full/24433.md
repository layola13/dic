bristletail:/ˈbrɪsəlteɪl/
n.
衣鱼；缨尾虫
A small, wingless insect with three long appendages at the rear of its abdomen.  These insects are primitive and are often found in damp places.
Bristletails are often found in bathrooms.  衣鱼经常出现在浴室里。

Silverfish are a type of bristletail.  衣鱼是一种缨尾虫。
近义词:silverfish, firetail
反义词:None
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bristle + tail.  The name refers to the three bristle-like appendages at the end of the insect's abdomen.
