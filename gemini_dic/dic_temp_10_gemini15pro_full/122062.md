mistemper:/mɪsˈtɛmpər/| n. vt.|坏脾气，怒气；使发脾气|A state of bad temper or anger.  To put in a bad temper; irritate.|例句：His mistemper was evident in his slamming of the door. 他的坏脾气从他摔门的声音中显露出来。|近义词：anger, irritability, irascibility, peevishness, crossness|反义词：good humor, calmness, placidity|n.
mistemper:/mɪsˈtɛmpər/| vt.|使发脾气；使恼怒|To put in a bad temper; irritate.|例句：The constant noise mistempered him.  持续不断的噪音让他发脾气。|近义词：anger, irritate, annoy, vex|反义词：calm, soothe, pacify|vt.
