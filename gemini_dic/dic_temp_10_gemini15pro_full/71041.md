first-aider:/ˈfɜːrst ˈeɪdər/ (UK) /ˈfɝːst ˈeɪdər/ (US)| n.|急救员|A person trained to give first aid.|例句：The first-aider quickly assessed the injured man. 急救员迅速评估了受伤男子的情况。

例句：A trained first-aider can be invaluable in an emergency.  在紧急情况下，训练有素的急救员是无价之宝。

近义词：paramedic, EMT (emergency medical technician)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun

词源:  Compound of "first" and "aider," where "aider" is derived from the verb "aid" with the suffix "-er" indicating a person who performs the action.  "Aid" comes from Old French "aider," ultimately from Latin "adjutare" meaning "to help."
