macropodian: /ˌmækrəˈpoʊdiən/
adjective|长足类的；大足的|Relating to or belonging to the Macropodia, a group of crustaceans characterized by long legs. |Macropodian crabs are often found in shallow waters. 长足蟹通常在浅水区被发现。|long-legged, large-footed|short-legged, small-footed|adjective

macropodian: /ˌmækrəˈpoʊdiən/
noun|长足动物|A member of the Macropodia, a group of crustaceans characterized by long legs. |The macropodian scuttled across the seabed. 这只长足动物在海床上快速移动。|long-legged creature, large-footed animal|short-legged creature, small-footed animal|noun


Etymology: From Greek "makros" (large) + "pous" (foot).
