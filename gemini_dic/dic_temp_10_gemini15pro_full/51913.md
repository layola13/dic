dhow: /daʊ/ | n. |单桅帆船，阿拉伯帆船 | A lateen-rigged sailing vessel used in the Indian Ocean.  It is traditionally made of wood and has one or more masts with triangular sails. |  Example: The dhow sailed gracefully across the Arabian Sea.  阿拉伯帆船优雅地航行穿过阿拉伯海。

Example:  We saw several dhows fishing near the coast. 我们看到几艘单桅帆船在海岸附近捕鱼。


| 近义词: sailboat, sailing vessel | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps steamship or motorboat as representing modern powered vessels) | 词性: noun
