in-reality:/ɪn riˈælɪti/ | adv. | 实际上，事实上 | In reality, the situation is much more complex. 实际上，情况要复杂得多。 |  The advertising promised a luxury hotel, but in reality it was a run-down motel. 广告承诺的是豪华酒店，但实际上却是一家破旧的汽车旅馆。 |  actually, in fact, truly |  ideally, theoretically | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From in + reality. "Reality" comes from Late Middle English: realte, from Old French: realité, from late Latin: realitas, from Latin: realis “relating to things,” from res “thing.”
