antisexuality:/ˌæntiˌsɛkʃuˈælɪti/ |n.| 反性欲，性冷淡 |A lack of sexual attraction or interest in sex. It can be a lifelong orientation or a temporary phase. | Some people experience antisexuality after trauma, while others identify as asexual from a young age. | 近义词: asexuality | 反义词: hypersexuality | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek anti-)
sexuality (noun): capacity for sexual feelings
