incommunicable:/ˌɪnkəˈmjuːnɪkəbl/
adjective
无法表达的，难以言传的
Not able to be communicated to others.
Her grief was so profound and personal, it felt utterly incommunicable. 她悲伤如此深切和私密，以至于完全无法表达。
The sense of peace and tranquility she experienced was incommunicable. 她所体验到的平和与宁静的感觉难以言传。
Synonyms: inexpressible, unutterable, indescribable
Antonyms: communicable, expressible, describable
词性: 形容词
