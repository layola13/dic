pachalic: /pɑːʃəˈlɪk/ | n. |帕夏的辖区；帕夏领；帕夏管辖的地区|A territory ruled by a pasha. |  The Ottoman Empire was divided into several pachalics. 奥斯曼帝国被划分为几个帕夏领。

The paschalic of Egypt was a powerful region within the empire. 埃及帕夏领是帝国中一个强大的地区。 | 近义词:province, territory, region | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Turkish *paşalık*, from *paşa* (“pasha”) +‎ *-lık* (a suffix forming abstract nouns). Ultimately from Persian *پادشاه* (pādešāh, “emperor”).
