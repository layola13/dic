hypocretin:/ˌhaɪpəˈkriːtɪn/ | n. |食欲素，下丘脑泌素| A neuropeptide hormone produced in the hypothalamus that plays a crucial role in regulating wakefulness, appetite, and arousal.  It's deficiency is associated with narcolepsy. |  Researchers believe that a lack of hypocretin in the brain causes narcolepsy. 研究人员认为，大脑中缺乏食欲素会导致嗜睡症。

The study found a link between hypocretin levels and sleep quality.  这项研究发现了食欲素水平与睡眠质量之间的联系。 |  Synonym: orexin | Antonym: N/A | noun
