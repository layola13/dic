dole-out:/doʊl aʊt/| v.|发放;少量给予;施舍|To distribute something sparingly or in small portions.|The government doled out emergency supplies to the flood victims. 政府向洪灾灾民发放了紧急物资。|例句：The soup kitchen doled out hot meals to the homeless.  施粥场向无家可归的人分发热食。

|近义词：distribute, dispense, allot, ration|反义词:hoard, withhold|词性:动词

dole:/doʊl/| n.|失业救济金|Money received from the government when unemployed.|He's been on the dole for six months. 他已经领取失业救济金六个月了。|例句：She was forced to go on the dole after losing her job. 她失业后被迫领取失业救济金。|近义词：benefit, allowance, welfare|反义词:earnings, income|词性:名词
