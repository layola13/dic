disparatenesses:/dɪˈspærətnəsɪz/ | n. | 差异，不同，悬殊 | The state or quality of being disparate; difference; dissimilarity.  | The disparatenesses between their lives were striking; he lived in luxury, while she struggled to make ends meet.  他们生活上的悬殊令人震惊；他过着奢侈的生活，而她却在为生计挣扎。 | Synonyms:  differences, dissimilarities, disparities, inequalities, variations | Antonyms: similarities, likenesses, uniformity, homogeneity | noun

disparate:/ˈdɪspərət/ | adj. | 不同的，异类的，迥异的 | Essentially different in kind; not allowing comparison. |  The company merged two disparate businesses, a clothing retailer and a software developer. 这家公司合并了两个完全不同的业务，一个服装零售商和一个软件开发商。| Synonyms: different, dissimilar, distinct, diverse, various | Antonyms: similar, alike, homogeneous, uniform | adjective
