mouthing:/ˈmaʊðɪŋ/ | n. | 空说，做口型，无声地说 | the act of forming words with the lips without making a sound | She was mouthing the words to the song. 她正在无声地跟着歌曲做口型。 |  lip-synching, miming | speaking, vocalizing | n.
