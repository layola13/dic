catheter:/ˈkæθɪtər/ (US), /ˈkæθɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|导管|A thin tube inserted into a body cavity, duct, or vessel to permit injection or withdrawal of fluids or to keep a passage open.|The doctor inserted a catheter into the patient's bladder. 医生将导管插入病人的膀胱。|近义词: tube, cannula|反义词: None|词性: noun
