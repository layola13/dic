beggarhood:/ˈbeɡərhʊd/| n. |乞丐身份，乞讨生活|The state or condition of being a beggar; the act of begging.  The collective body of beggars.|He was reduced to beggarhood after losing his job and home. 他失业又失去住所后沦为乞丐。|Synonyms: beggary, mendicancy, pauperism|Antonyms: wealth, affluence|noun
