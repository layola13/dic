green-lights:/ˈɡriːn laɪts/ |v. (transitive) n. (plural)|批准，准许；绿灯|To give permission for something to start or continue; a signal to proceed. |The council green-lighted the new housing development.  市议会批准了新的住房开发项目。|Synonyms: approve, authorize, sanction, permit|Antonyms: disapprove, reject, veto, forbid|verb

green-lights:/ˈɡriːn laɪts/ |n. (plural)|绿灯；通行信号|A signal to proceed, often literally a green light.|The project got the green-lights and is ready to begin. 该项目获得了批准，准备开始。|Synonyms: go-ahead, approval, authorization|Antonyms: red light, stop, prohibition|noun
