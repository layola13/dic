ooesporangium: /ˌoʊoʊˌspɔːˈrændʒiəm/
noun
卵孢子囊
A sporangium in certain fungi and algae that contains one or more large, nonmotile asexual spores called oospores.
Oomycetes produce oospores in structures called oosporangia. 卵菌在称为卵孢子囊的结构中产生卵孢子。
The oosporangium provides protection for the developing oospores. 卵孢子囊为发育中的卵孢子提供保护。
Synonym: oogonium (sometimes used interchangeably, though technically distinct)
Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps zoosporangium which produces motile spores)
词性: noun


Etymology:
From oo- (egg) + sporangium (spore case)
