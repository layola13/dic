breadfruit:/ˈbredˌfruːt/| n. |面包果|The starchy fruit of a tropical tree, which can be cooked and eaten as a vegetable.|例句：Breadfruit is a staple food in many Pacific Island cultures. 面包果是许多太平洋岛国文化中的主食。

例句：We had roasted breadfruit for dinner last night. 我们昨晚晚餐吃了烤面包果。|近义词：plantain, jackfruit|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bread + fruit.  So named because when cooked, the starchy inside resembles freshly baked bread.
