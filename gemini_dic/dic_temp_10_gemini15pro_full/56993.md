double-density:/ˌdʌbəl ˈdɛnsɪti/| adj.|双倍密度的|Having twice the storage capacity of standard density.  It refers to a storage medium, like floppy disks, that can hold more data than previous versions.|This double-density disk can hold twice as much data as the old one.  这个双倍密度的磁盘可以比旧磁盘存储两倍的数据。|high-density|low-density|adjective
