daubry:/ˈdɔːbri/ |n.|涂抹；涂鸦；拙劣的艺术作品；粗制滥造的东西|A daubry is something that is crudely or unskillfully made, especially a painting or piece of writing.  It can also refer to the act of daubing or smearing.| The child's painting was a charming, if somewhat messy, daubry.  孩子的画很可爱，虽然有点乱，像是一幅涂鸦。|Synonyms:  smear, smudge, botch, mess, bungle|Antonyms: masterpiece, work of art|Noun
