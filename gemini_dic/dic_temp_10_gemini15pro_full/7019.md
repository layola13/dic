androphore:/ˈændrəfɔːr/
n.
雄蕊柄，花丝连合柱
The stalk bearing the stamens in certain flowers.
The androphore of this hibiscus flower is particularly long.  这种芙蓉花的雄蕊柄特别长。

近义词: staminal column
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps gynoecium if contrasting male and female flower parts)
词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *andros* (man, male) + *-phoros* (bearing)
