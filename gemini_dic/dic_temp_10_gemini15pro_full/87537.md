heterogeneous:/ˌhetərəˈdʒiːniəs/| adj.|异质的，由不同成分组成的|Consisting of parts or elements that are dissimilar; diverse in character or content.|The classroom was a heterogeneous mix of students from different backgrounds.  这个教室里是来自不同背景的学生的混合体。|The city is a heterogeneous mix of cultures and ethnicities. 这座城市是文化和种族的异质混合体。|近义词：diverse, varied, mixed, dissimilar, composite|反义词：homogeneous, uniform, similar|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *heterogeneus*, from Ancient Greek ἑτερογενής (heterogenḗs, “of another race or kind”), from ἕτερος (héteros, “other, different”) + γένος (génos, “kind, race”).
