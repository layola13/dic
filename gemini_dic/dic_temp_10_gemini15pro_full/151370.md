parlante:/pɑːrˈlɑːnteɪ/ /pɑːrˈlɑːnti/
adjective|说话的，发音的；表达能力强的|able to speak, especially fluently or expressively; articulate.|He is a parlante individual, always able to express himself clearly. 他是一个表达能力很强的人，总是能清楚地表达自己。|近义词:articulate, eloquent, fluent, expressive|反义词:inarticulate, tongue-tied, speechless|词性:形容词

parlante:/pɑːrˈlɑːnteɪ/ /pɑːrˈlɑːnti/
noun|说话的人；演讲者|a person who speaks, especially one who speaks a particular language or speaks in a particular way.|The parlante delivered a captivating speech. 演讲者发表了引人入胜的演讲。|近义词:speaker, orator|反义词:listener, audience|词性:名词
