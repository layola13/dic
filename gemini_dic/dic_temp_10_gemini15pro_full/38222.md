computational:/ˌkɑːmpjuˈteɪʃənl/ | adj. | 计算的；计算机的 | Relating to or involving the use of computers, especially in scientific calculations. |  例句：Computational linguistics is a fascinating field. 计算语言学是一个引人入胜的领域。

例句：The project required significant computational resources.  这个项目需要大量的计算资源。


近义词：calculated, computing, algorithmic | 反义词：non-computational, theoretical, manual | 词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "compute" + "-ational". "Compute" comes from the Latin "computare," meaning "to reckon, sum up, compute," from "com-" (together) + "putare" (to prune, cleanse, reckon).
