hadeses:/ˈheɪdiːziːz/
n.
冥界（复数）
plural of Hades, the underworld in Greek mythology.
The poets wrote of heroes venturing into the hadeses. 诗人们写了英雄冒险进入冥界的故事。
近义词：underworlds, netherworlds
反义词：heavens, paradises
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ᾍδης (Háidēs).
