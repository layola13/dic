词源： "coldie" is a colloquial shortening of "cold one," which itself is a metonym, referring to a cold beverage, typically beer, by its temperature.  The use of "coldie" to refer to a cold or the flu is a further extension of this meaning, likely based on the feeling of being cold when ill.
