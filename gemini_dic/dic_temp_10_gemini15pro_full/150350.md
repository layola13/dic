panier:/ˈpænɪə(r)/ /ˈpɑːnjeɪ/| n. |篮子，购物篮|A basket, especially one used for holding bread or shopping. |例句：She filled her panier with fresh vegetables from the market. 她用市场上的新鲜蔬菜装满了篮子。

例句：The baker carried a panier full of warm croissants. 面包师提着一篮子热腾腾的可颂面包。|近义词：basket, hamper |反义词:  (No direct antonym for a physical object like a basket) |词性:noun
