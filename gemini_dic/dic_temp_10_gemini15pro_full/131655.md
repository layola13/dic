nonconcealment:/ˌnɒnkənˈsiːlmənt/
n.
不隐瞒，公开
The state of not concealing something; openness.
His nonconcealment of his past mistakes was admirable.  他毫不隐瞒自己过去犯的错误，令人钦佩。

近义词: openness, frankness, candor, honesty, transparency
反义词: concealment, secrecy, hiding, deception, dissimulation
词性: noun
