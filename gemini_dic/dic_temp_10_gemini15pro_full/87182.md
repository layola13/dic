herding:/ˈhɜːrdɪŋ/ | n. | 放牧；牧畜；群集 | The act of bringing together and controlling a group of animals, especially livestock. Also refers to the grouping together of people or things. | The shepherd was responsible for the herding of the sheep. 牧羊人负责放羊。 | synonyms:  shepherding, pasturing, rounding up, gathering, assembling | antonyms: scattering, dispersing | noun

herding:/ˈhɜːrdɪŋ/ | adj. | 放牧的；群集的 | Related to or characteristic of herding. |  He exhibited a herding instinct, always trying to keep the group together. 他表现出一种群集本能，总是试图让大家团结在一起。 | synonyms: pastoral, gregarious | antonyms: solitary, individualistic | adjective
