cottonwood:/ˈkɑːtnˌwʊd/ (US) /ˈkɒtnˌwʊd/ (UK)| n.|棉白杨，三叶杨|A fast-growing North American poplar tree with seeds covered in white cottony hairs.|The cottonwood trees lined the riverbank. 棉白杨树排列在河岸边。|Synonyms: poplar, aspen|Antonyms: N/A|noun

cottonwood:/ˈkɑːtnˌwʊd/ (US) /ˈkɒtnˌwʊd/ (UK)| adj.|棉白杨制的|Made from the wood of a cottonwood tree.|He built a raft from cottonwood logs. 他用棉白杨原木做了一个木筏。|Synonyms: poplar, aspen (when referring to the wood)|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
