holster:/ˈhəʊlstə(r)/ (British) /ˈhoʊlstər/ (American)| n.|枪套；皮套|A holster is a protective covering for a handgun, often made of leather or synthetic materials, designed to be worn on a belt or under clothing.|The police officer adjusted the holster on his hip. 警察调整了他臀部的枪套。|She drew her pistol from its holster. 她从枪套里拔出了手枪。|近义词：sheath, case|反义词: None|noun


holsters:/ˈhəʊlstəz/ (British) /ˈhoʊlstərz/ (American)| n.|枪套（复数）|Plural form of "holster," referring to multiple protective coverings for handguns.|The shop sold a variety of holsters for different types of firearms. 这家商店出售各种不同类型枪套。|He cleaned all his holsters meticulously. 他一丝不苟地清洁了他的所有枪套。|近义词：sheaths, cases|反义词: None|noun
