gapingstock:/ˈɡeɪpɪŋ stɒk/ (US) /ˈɡeɪpɪŋ stɑːk/| n. |笑柄，众矢之的|A person who is subject to general ridicule or contempt due to some blunder or misfortune.|例句：After his disastrous presentation, he became the gapingstock of the entire company. 在他灾难性的演讲之后，他成了整个公司的笑柄。|例句：She tripped and fell on stage, turning herself into a gapingstock for the audience. 她在舞台上绊倒了，成了观众的笑柄。|近义词：laughingstock, spectacle, butt of jokes, object of ridicule|反义词:dignified person, respected individual|词性:名词
