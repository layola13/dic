overtime:/ˈoʊvərtaɪm/ (US), /ˈəʊvətaɪm/ (UK)| n. adj. adv.|加班时间；超时；加班地；超时的|Work done in addition to regular working hours, or the payment for this work.  It can also be used as an adjective or adverb describing something happening during this extra time.|
He earns a lot of money in overtime. 他加班赚了很多钱。

She worked overtime to finish the project. 她加班完成了这个项目。

The game went into overtime. 比赛进入了加时赛。

近义词: extra time, extended hours|
反义词: regular time, standard hours|
词性: noun, adjective, adverb


overtime:/ˈoʊvərtaɪm/ (US), /ˈəʊvətaɪm/ (UK)| adj.|加班的；超时的|Relating to or occurring during overtime.|
He gets overtime pay. 他得到加班费。

It was an overtime goal that won the game.  是一个加时进球赢得了比赛。


近义词: extra, extended|
反义词: regular, standard|
词性: adjective


overtime:/ˈoʊvərtaɪm/ (US), /ˈəʊvətaɪm/ (UK)| adv.|加班地；超时的|Working or happening during overtime.|
He's been working overtime all week. 他整个星期都在加班。

The project ran overtime.  这个项目超时了。


近义词: extra, beyond regular hours|
反义词: within regular hours|
词性: adverb


词源: From "over" + "time," meaning beyond the regular allotted time.
