glareous:/ˈɡlɛəriəs/| adj.|耀眼的，炫目的|Emitting or reflecting a strong or dazzling light.  Characterized by a harsh, bright light that is difficult to look at.|The glareous sun reflected off the snow, making it difficult to see.|近义词：blinding, dazzling, bright, shining, radiant|反义词：dim, dull, dark|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *glāreōsus, from glāreō (“to shine, gleam”), related to glārē (“amber”).
