词源：
The origin of the word "hoagie" is uncertain, but the most widely accepted theory links it to the Hog Island shipyard in Philadelphia during World War I.  Workers there, known as "hoggies," would often eat large sandwiches, which became known as "hoggies" and eventually "hoagies."
