noneconomies:/ˌnɒnɪˈkɒnəmiz/ /ˌnɑːnɪˈkɑːnəmiz/
|n.| 非经济因素；不经济的因素；规模不经济|Factors that hinder or reduce economic efficiency or productivity; diseconomies of scale.|The factory suffered from noneconomies due to its remote location.|Synonyms: diseconomies, inefficiencies|Antonyms: economies, efficiencies|noun

noneconomy:/ˌnɒnɪˈkɒnəmi/ /ˌnɑːnɪˈkɑːnəmi/
|n.|不经济；非经济状态；浪费|A lack of economy or thrift; wastefulness; a situation that is not economically sound.|The noneconomy of the project led to its cancellation.|Synonyms: wastefulness, extravagance, inefficiency|Antonyms: economy, thrift, efficiency|noun
