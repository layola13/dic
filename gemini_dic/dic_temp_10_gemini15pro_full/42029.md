costlier:/ˈkɒstlɪə(r)/ (UK), /ˈkɑːstlɪər/ (US)| adj.|更昂贵的，更代价高的|Comparative form of costly: more costly.  Meaning more expensive or involving greater loss or sacrifice.|例句：This new car is costlier than my old one. 这辆新车比我的旧车更贵。|近义词：more expensive, pricier, dearer|反义词:cheaper, less expensive|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *costlyer, comparative of costly, from cost + -ly + -er. Ultimately from Latin constare (“to stand firm, cost”).
