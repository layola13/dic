granulite:/ˈɡræn.jə.laɪt/ | n. |麻粒岩|A metamorphic rock composed mainly of granular minerals such as feldspar, quartz, and garnet, formed under conditions of high temperature and pressure. |The geologist identified the sample as a granulite.  地质学家将该样本鉴定为麻粒岩。|

The Himalayas are known to contain extensive granulite formations.  众所周知，喜马拉雅山脉包含大量的麻粒岩地层。|近义词:gneiss(片麻岩) |反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific rock type) |词性:noun
