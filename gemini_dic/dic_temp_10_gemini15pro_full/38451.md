concessionaires:/kənˌseʃəˈnɛərz/
| n. |特许经营者， concessionaire的复数形式|A person or company that has been granted a concession, especially to operate a subsidiary business within a larger concern.|The concessionaires at the national park run the restaurants and gift shops. 国家公园的特许经营者经营着餐厅和礼品店。|Synonyms: franchisee, licensee, operator, vendor|Antonyms: licensor, franchisor|Noun


concessionaire:/kənˌseʃəˈnɛər/
| n. |特许经营者|A person or company that has been granted a concession, especially to operate a subsidiary business within a larger concern.|The concessionaire at the stadium sells hot dogs and beer. 体育场内的特许经营者出售热狗和啤酒。|Synonyms: franchisee, licensee, operator, vendor|Antonyms: licensor, franchisor|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *concessionnaire*, from *concession* (concession), from Late Latin *concessio* (permission, grant), from Latin *concedere* (to yield, grant), from *com-* (together) + *cedere* (to go, yield).
