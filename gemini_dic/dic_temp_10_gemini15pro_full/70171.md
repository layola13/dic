fibr:/ˈfaɪbər/ (US) /ˈfaɪbə(r)/ (UK)| n.|纤维; 细丝; 纤维制品|A thread or filament from which a vegetable tissue, mineral substance, or textile is formed.|例句：The cable is reinforced with glass fibrs. 这条电缆用玻璃纤维加固。

例句：Dietary fibr is important for digestive health. 膳食纤维对消化系统健康很重要。|近义词：filament, strand, thread|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
