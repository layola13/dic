exhilaratingly:/ɪɡˈzɪləreɪtɪŋli/
adverb
令人振奋地；欢欣鼓舞地
in a way that makes one feel very happy, animated, or elated; thrillingly
例句：The music was exhilaratingly fast-paced.  这音乐的快节奏令人振奋。

例句：She ran exhilaratingly through the fields. 她兴奋地在田野里奔跑。

近义词：thrillingly, excitingly, joyfully, exuberantly
反义词：depressingly, disappointingly, boringly
词性: adverb

词源: From exhilarate + -ing + -ly.  Exhilarate comes from Latin exhilaratus, past participle of exhilarare "to gladden, cheer," from ex- "out" + hilarare "to cheer," from hilaris "cheerful."
