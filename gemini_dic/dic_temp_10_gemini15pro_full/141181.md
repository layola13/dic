oohs:/uːz/ | n. | 惊叹声，感叹声 |  Sounds of surprise, delight, or pain, often expressing admiration or sympathy. | The oohs and aahs of the crowd filled the stadium as the fireworks began.  人群的惊叹声随着烟花的开始充满了体育场。 | 近义词: gasps, exclamations | 反义词: (No direct antonym, could be considered the absence of sound - silence) | 词性: Noun
