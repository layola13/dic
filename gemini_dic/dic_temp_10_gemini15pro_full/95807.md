incony:/ˈɪnkəni/| adj.|珍贵的，罕见的，可爱的|Rare, precious, or delightful; unusual and attractive.|例句：She has an incony collection of antique dolls. 她收藏了一批珍贵的古董娃娃。|例句：He gave her an incony smile. 他给了她一个可爱的笑容。|近义词：rare, precious, delightful, charming, sweet, dear, unusual, uncommon, atypical|反义词：common, ordinary, usual, typical|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *incognizable*, from in- "not" + cognizable, from Late Latin *cognoscibilis* "knowable," from Latin cognoscere "to get to know."  The original meaning was "unknown, strange," later shifting to "rare, curious," and finally to "fine, delicate, pretty."
