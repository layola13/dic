arthrospore:/ˈɑːrθrəˌspɔːr/| n. |节孢子|A spore formed by the breaking up of a hypha into individual cells.  A type of fungal spore formed by fragmentation of hyphae.|The fungus reproduces by arthrospores.|Synonym: oidium, conidium|Antonym: N/A|noun
