absonant: /ˈæbsənənt/
adjective
刺耳的，不和谐的；荒谬的
sounding harsh and unpleasant; incongruous
The absonant music made me want to leave the concert.  这刺耳的音乐让我想要离开音乐会。
His absonant laughter echoed through the silent halls. 他那刺耳的笑声在寂静的大厅里回荡。


近义词: discordant, dissonant, jarring, grating, inharmonious, incongruous, absurd
反义词: harmonious, melodious, euphonious, consonant, congruous

词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *absonans*, present participle of *absonare* (“to sound differently, to disagree in sound”), from *ab* (“away from, off”) + *sonare* (“to sound”).

USAGE->
Attributive usage:

The absonant chord jarred the otherwise peaceful melody.  刺耳的和弦破坏了原本平静的旋律。
Her absonant behavior at the formal dinner was shocking.  她在正式晚宴上不和谐的行为令人震惊。

Predicative usage:

The music was absonant and unpleasant to the ear. 音乐刺耳，听起来很不舒服。
His theories about the universe seemed utterly absonant. 他关于宇宙的理论似乎完全荒谬。

PHRASE->
absonant sound = 刺耳的声音
absonant note = 不和谐的音符
absonant chord = 不和谐的和弦
absonant behavior = 不和谐的行为
absonant ideas = 荒谬的想法
