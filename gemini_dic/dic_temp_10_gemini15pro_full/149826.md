paleontography:/ˌpeɪliɒnˈtɒɡrəfi/ | n. | 古生物地层学 | The study of the distribution of fossils in rocks, used to determine the relative ages of rock formations and the history of life on Earth. |  Paleontography helps us understand the evolution of life on our planet. 古生物地层学帮助我们理解地球上生命的进化。|  The paleontography of the region revealed a rich diversity of ancient marine life. 该地区的古生物地层学揭示了古代海洋生物的丰富多样性。| Synonyms: paleobiogeography, stratigraphic paleontology | Antonyms: None | noun
