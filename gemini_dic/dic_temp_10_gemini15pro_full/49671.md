demission:/dɪˈmɪʃən/
noun
辞职，放弃
the act of resigning from a job or office
例句：The minister announced his demission from the government. 部长宣布辞去政府职务。

例句：Her demission from the company was unexpected. 她从公司辞职出乎意料。

近义词：resignation, abdication, withdrawal
反义词：accession, assumption, appointment
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English demission, from Old French demission, from Latin dēmissiō (“a lowering, sinking, letting down, sending down”), from dēmīss (“sent down, lowered”), perfect passive participle of dēmittō (“I send down, lower, let down”), from dē (“down, away”) + mittō (“I send”).
