non-limiting:/ˌnɒnˈlɪmɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 非限制性的，非限定性的 |  Used to indicate that a list of examples does not include all possibilities.  Used in legal or technical contexts to clarify that a given list of items is not exhaustive. |  This list of potential side effects is non-limiting.  这些潜在的副作用列表并非详尽无遗。 | synonyms: inclusive, non-exhaustive | antonyms: limiting, restrictive, exhaustive | adjective
