hippocampus:/ˈhɪpəˌkæmpəs/ (US), /ˌhɪpəˈkæmpəs/ (UK)| n.|海马体|A complex neural structure (shaped like a seahorse) consisting of gray matter and located on the floor of each lateral ventricle of the brain. It is primarily associated with memory, particularly long-term memory, and spatial navigation.|The hippocampus plays a crucial role in forming new memories. 海马体在新记忆的形成中起着至关重要的作用。|synonyms:  cornu ammonis (technical)|antonyms: N/A|noun

hippocampi:/ˌhɪpəˈkæmpaɪ/ (plural)|n.|海马体（复数）|The plural form of hippocampus, referring to the two hippocampi, one in each hemisphere of the brain.|Damage to both hippocampi can result in severe memory impairment.  对两个海马体的损害会导致严重的记忆障碍。|synonyms: N/A|antonyms: N/A|noun
