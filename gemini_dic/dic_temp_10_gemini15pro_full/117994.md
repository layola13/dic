memorialized: /məˈmɔːriəlaɪzd/ /məˈmɔːriːəlaɪzd/
Verb
纪念；使永垂不朽
To preserve the memory of someone or something, especially by creating a monument or writing a tribute.
The fallen soldiers were memorialized with a statue in the town square.  阵亡的士兵们在小镇广场上被一座雕像纪念。

The museum memorialized the history of the city.  博物馆纪念了这座城市的历史。

commemorated, celebrated, remembered
forgotten, ignored
v.
