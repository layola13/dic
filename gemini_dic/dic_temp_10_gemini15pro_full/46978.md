darwinian:/dɑːrˈwɪniən/ | adj. | 达尔文的；达尔文主义的；适者生存的 | Of, relating to, or being the theories of Charles Darwin concerning evolution, especially the theory of natural selection. |  例句：Darwinian evolution is based on the concept of natural selection. 达尔文进化论是基于自然选择的概念。

例句：The company operates on a Darwinian principle, where only the strongest survive. 这家公司奉行达尔文主义原则，只有强者才能生存。 | 近义词：evolutionary, natural selection | 反义词：creationist, Lamarckian | 词性: adjective
