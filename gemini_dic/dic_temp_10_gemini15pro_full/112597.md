loving-up:/ˈlʌvɪŋ ˈʌp/| phrasal verb |溺爱；过分宠爱；大献殷勤|To show someone a lot of affection, often to an excessive degree, sometimes with an ulterior motive.|He's been loving her up all week, hoping she'll lend him some money. 他整个星期都对她大献殷勤，希望她能借他一些钱。|例句：She was loving up her new boyfriend. 她正溺爱着她的新男友。|近义词：pampering, spoiling, doting on, fawning over|反义词：neglecting, ignoring|词性：短语动词

loving-up:/ˈlʌvɪŋ ˈʌp/| n. |溺爱；过分宠爱；大献殷勤 (用作名词的情况较少)|The act of showing someone a lot of affection, often excessively.|All that loving-up made her suspicious. 所有这些溺爱让她起了疑心。|例句：He gave her a lot of loving-up. 他对她百般宠爱。|近义词：pampering, spoiling|反义词：neglecting|词性：名词
