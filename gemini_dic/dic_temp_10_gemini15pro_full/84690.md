handfish:/ˈhændfɪʃ/| n. |手鱼|A type of fish that uses its fins to "walk" on the ocean floor.|The handfish is a rare and fascinating creature. 手鱼是一种稀有而迷人的生物。|The spotted handfish is critically endangered.  斑点手鱼极度濒危。|近义词：anglerfish (somewhat similar in appearance), bottom-dweller|反义词: (No direct opposite, perhaps pelagic fish (open ocean fish))|noun
