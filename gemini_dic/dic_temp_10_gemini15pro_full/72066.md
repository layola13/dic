flitted:/ˈflɪtɪd/
verb
掠过，轻快地移动
moved swiftly and lightly
The butterfly flitted from flower to flower. 蝴蝶在花丛中飞来飞去。
近义词: darted, skimmed, whisked
反义词: lumbered, plodded
词性: 动词


flit:/flɪt/
verb
掠过，轻快地移动
move swiftly and lightly
A thought flitted through her mind. 一个念头闪过她的脑海。
近义词: dart, skim, whisk
反义词: lumber, plod
词性: 动词
