eradicative:/ɪˈrædɪkeɪtɪv/
adjective
根除的，消除的
tending or serving to eradicate.
The government is taking eradicative measures to eliminate the invasive species. 政府正在采取根除措施以消除入侵物种。
Eradicative treatment is necessary to cure the disease. 根除性治疗对于治愈这种疾病是必要的。

antonyms: constructive, preservative, supportive
