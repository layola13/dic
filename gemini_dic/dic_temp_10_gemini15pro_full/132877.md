nonelastically:/ˌnɑːnɪˈlæstɪkli/
adverb
非弹性地
in a way that is not elastic; inelastically.  This means without the ability to return to its original shape or size after being deformed, or without the ability to absorb impact or shock without permanent deformation.
The car crashed nonelastically into the wall. 汽车非弹性地撞到墙上。
The clay deformed nonelastically under pressure.  黏土在压力下非弹性地变形。
Synonyms: inelastically, rigidly, inflexibly
Antonyms: elastically, flexibly, resiliently
词性: adverb
