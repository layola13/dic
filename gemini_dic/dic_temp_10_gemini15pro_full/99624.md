interknow:/ˌɪntərˈnoʊ/
verb
互相了解；彼此心照不宣
to have mutual understanding or knowledge, often without needing to communicate verbally.
They had an interknowing glance, understanding each other without words. 他们交换了一个心照不宣的眼神，彼此无需言语便能理解。
近义词: understand, empathize, communicate, connect, intuit
反义词: misunderstand, misinterpret, disconnect


ETYMOLOGY->
