hecticness:/ˈhɛktɪknəs/
n.
忙乱；繁忙
The state of being hectic; a busy and frenetic quality.
The hecticness of modern life can be overwhelming.  现代生活的繁忙可能会让人难以承受。
My schedule has been insane lately due to the sheer hecticness of the project.  由于项目的极度忙碌，我最近的日程安排非常疯狂。
近义词: frenzy, bustle, chaos, flurry
反义词: calmness, tranquility, peace, quiet
词性: noun

Etymology: From hectic + -ness.  Hectic from Greek *hektikos "habitual," from *hexis "habit, state of body," from *ekhein "to have, hold."
