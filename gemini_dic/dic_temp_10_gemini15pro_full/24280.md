brigading:/brɪˈɡeɪdɪŋ/| n.| 集体行动，网络串联，网络围攻 |The act of organizing a group of people to vote or comment on something online, often with the intent to manipulate public opinion or harass someone.  |  Example: The politician accused his opponents of brigading his social media posts with negative comments.  这位政治家指责他的对手在他的社交媒体帖子中集体发布负面评论。

Example:  Brigading is a common tactic used in online forums to silence dissenting voices.  网络串联是在网络论坛中压制异见的常用策略。| 近义词：collusion, ganging up, coordinated attack, online harassment (when used in a negative context) | 反义词: individual action, independent voting | 词性:noun
