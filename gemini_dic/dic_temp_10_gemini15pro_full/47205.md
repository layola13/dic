daylighting:/ˈdeɪˌlaɪtɪŋ/| n.|采光; 日光照明|The use of natural light to illuminate interior spaces.  The building features innovative daylighting strategies to reduce energy consumption.|Example 1: The architect maximized daylighting in the office to create a brighter and more pleasant work environment.  建筑师最大限度地利用办公室的自然采光，创造了一个更明亮、更舒适的工作环境。

Example 2:  Daylighting is a key element of sustainable building design.  采光是可持续建筑设计的一个关键要素。|Synonyms: natural lighting, illumination, sunlighting|Antonyms: artificial lighting, electric lighting|noun
