answered:/ˈɑːnsəd/ /ˈænsərd/| v. adj.|回答，答复；已回答的|A question was asked and answered. 提出了一个问题并得到了回答。|近义词：replied, responded|反义词：unanswered, questioned|词性:动词/形容词

Etymology: From Middle English *answeren*, from Old English *andswarian* ("to answer, respond to"), from *andswaru* ("an answer, reply"), from *and-* ("against, in opposition to, in return") + *swaru* ("affirmation, assertion").  Cognate with Old High German *antwurten* ("to answer"). The *-w-* disappeared in Middle English.
