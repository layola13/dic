collaboration: /kəˌlæbəˈreɪʃn/ | n. | 合作，协作 |  The act of working together with one or more people in order to achieve something. |  The project was a collaboration between the two universities. 这个项目是两所大学的合作项目。 |  cooperation, partnership, teamwork |  opposition, antagonism | noun

collaboration: /kəˌlæbəˈreɪʃn/ | n. |  勾结，通敌 |  The act of working with an enemy, especially during a war.  | The investigation revealed evidence of collaboration with the enemy. 调查揭示了与敌人勾结的证据。| collusion, conspiracy | resistance, defiance | noun
