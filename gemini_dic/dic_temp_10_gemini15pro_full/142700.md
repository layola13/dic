otherness:/ˈʌðərnəs/
n.
差异，不同，他性
The state or quality of being different or distinct; the quality of being someone or something else.
She felt a sense of otherness in the new city.  她在新城市感到一种格格不入的感觉。
His paintings explore themes of alienation and otherness. 他的画作探索了疏离和他性的主题。
近义词: difference, distinctness, diversity, foreignness, strangeness, alienness, alterity
反义词: sameness, similarity, likeness, identity
词性: 名词
