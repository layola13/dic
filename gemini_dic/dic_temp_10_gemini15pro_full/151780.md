pass-fail:/ˈpæsˈfeɪl/| n.|及格/不及格制；合格/不合格制|A system of grading in which students receive either a passing grade or a failing grade, without letter grades or percentages.|Many students opt for the pass-fail option for elective courses. 许多学生选择选修课的及格/不及格选项。|The pass-fail system relieves some of the pressure of getting good grades. 及格/不及格制减轻了一些获得好成绩的压力。|近义词:credit/no credit system, satisfactory/unsatisfactory system|反义词:letter grading system, traditional grading system|词性:名词
