impertinently:/ɪmˈpɜːrtnəntli/
adverb|无礼地，鲁莽地|Not showing proper respect; rude.|She impertinently interrupted the speaker. 她无礼地打断了演讲者。|He impertinently asked about her salary. 他鲁莽地问起她的薪水。|rudely, insolently, impudently|respectfully, politely|adverb

impertinent:/ɪmˈpɜːrtnənt/
adjective|无礼的，鲁莽的，不相关的|Not showing proper respect; rude.  Not pertinent to a particular matter; irrelevant.|It was impertinent of him to ask such personal questions. 他问这样的私人问题很无礼。|Her comments were impertinent to the discussion. 她的评论与讨论无关。|rude, insolent, impudent, irrelevant|respectful, polite, relevant|adjective
