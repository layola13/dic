fraternizing:/ˈfrætərnaɪzɪŋ/
| n. | 交往；友好相处；亲善 | The act of associating or interacting with others in a friendly or informal way, especially with people considered to be of a different social group or with enemies. |  Their fraternizing with the enemy was considered treason.  他们与敌人称兄道弟被认为是叛国。 | 近义词：socializing, mingling, associating | 反义词：shunning, avoiding | 词性: 动名词 (gerund)

fraternize:/ˈfrætərnaɪz/
| v. | 交往；友好相处；亲善 | To associate or interact with others in a friendly or informal way, especially with people considered to be of a different social group or with enemies. | The soldiers were warned not to fraternize with the locals.  士兵们被警告不要与当地人 fraternize。| 近义词：socialize, mingle, associate | 反义词：shun, avoid | 词性: 动词 (verb)
