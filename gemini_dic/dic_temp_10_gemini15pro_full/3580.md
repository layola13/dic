agastric:/eɪˈɡæstrɪk/ | adj. | 无胃的，缺乏胃的 | Lacking a stomach or digestive cavity.  |  Example: Certain parasitic organisms are agastric and absorb nutrients directly through their body walls.  某些寄生生物是无胃的，直接通过体壁吸收营养。

Example:  The agastric flatworm relies on diffusion for nutrient uptake.  无胃的扁形虫依靠扩散来吸收营养。 | Synonym: stomachless | Antonym: gastric | word type: adjective
