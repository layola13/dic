faze:/feɪz/
verb
打扰；使慌乱；使担忧
to disturb or disconcert; unsettle
例句：
The news didn't faze him.
这消息并没有让他感到不安。

He was completely fazed by the exam.
他完全被考试吓到了。

近义词：disconcert, disturb, unsettle, ruffle, bother, perturb
反义词：calm, reassure, comfort
词性：v.
