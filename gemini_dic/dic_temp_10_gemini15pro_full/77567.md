gearhead:/ˈɡɪərˌhed/| n.|汽车迷；机械爱好者|A person who is enthusiastic about cars, motorcycles, or other machinery, especially one who enjoys working on them.|He's a real gearhead and spends every weekend tinkering with his car. 他是一个真正的汽车迷，每个周末都在摆弄他的车。|近义词：motorhead, car enthusiast, mechanic, tinkerer|反义词: Luddite (反对技术进步的人)|词性:名词
