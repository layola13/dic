balkanism: /ˈbɔːlkənɪzəm/ | n. | 巴尔干化，巴尔干半岛的民族纷争 | fragmentation of a region into smaller, often hostile, political units, especially along ethnic or religious lines, often involving violent conflict.  Derived from the history of the Balkan peninsula in the early 20th century. | The balkanism of the region threatens international stability.  该地区的巴尔干化威胁着国际稳定。| synonyms: fragmentation, division, factionalism, disintegration, sectarianism | antonyms: unification, integration, consolidation, harmony | noun

balkanize: /ˈbɔːlkənaɪz/ | vt. | 使巴尔干化，使分裂 | to divide (a region or body) into smaller mutually hostile states or groups. |  Political rivalries threaten to balkanize the country. 政治上的敌对势力有可能使这个国家分裂成若干小国。 | synonyms: divide, fragment, shatter, splinter, partition | antonyms: unite, integrate, consolidate | verb

balkanized: /ˈbɔːlkənaɪzd/ | adj. | 巴尔干化的，分裂的 | divided into small, mutually hostile units. | The formerly unified nation became a balkanized collection of warring states.  这个以前统一的国家变成了一个由交战国组成的分裂的集合体。| synonyms: fragmented, divided, fractured, splintered | antonyms: unified, integrated, consolidated | adjective
