mooncalf:/ˈmuːnkɑːf/ | n. | 笨蛋，傻瓜；白痴；梦想家 | A foolish person; a simpleton; a daydreamer. | He was considered a mooncalf by his peers because he always had his head in the clouds.  他的同龄人认为他是个傻瓜，因为他总是心不在焉。| Synonyms: simpleton, fool, dolt, nitwit, dreamer, idealist | Antonyms: genius, intellectual, pragmatist | noun

mooncalf:/ˈmuːnkɑːf/ | n. |  畸形胎儿 | A monstrous fetal form. |  Older texts mention the belief in mooncalves as deformed fetuses affected by the moon. 古老的文献提到人们相信月亮会影响胎儿导致畸形。 | Synonyms:  monster, deformity | Antonyms:  normal birth | noun
