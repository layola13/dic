disprison:/dɪsˈprɪzn/
verb
释放，解除监禁
to release from imprisonment
The governor disprisoned the wrongly convicted man. 州长释放了被冤枉的男子。
Synonyms: release, liberate, free, discharge
Antonyms: imprison, incarcerate, confine, detain
词性: 动词

disprison:/dɪsˈprɪzn/
noun
释放，解除监禁 (较少用作名词)
release from imprisonment
His disprison came after years of wrongful confinement. 他在被冤枉监禁多年后获释。
Synonyms: release, liberation, freedom, discharge
Antonyms: imprisonment, incarceration, confinement, detention
词性: 名词
