downriver:/ˌdaʊnˈrɪvər/| adv. adj.|顺流而下，在下游的|Situated or moving towards the mouth of a river.|We took a boat downriver to the next town. 我们乘船顺流而下到下一个城镇。|近义词：downstream|反义词:upriver|词性:副词,形容词

downriver:/ˌdaʊnˈrɪvər/| n.|下游地区|The part of a river nearer its mouth.|The downriver towns were flooded. 下游的城镇被淹没了。|近义词：lower reaches|反义词:upriver|词性:名词
