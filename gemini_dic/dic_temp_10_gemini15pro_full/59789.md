ecotourism:/ˈiːkəʊtʊərɪzəm/
n.
生态旅游
Tourism directed toward exotic, often threatened, natural environments, especially to support conservation efforts and observe wildlife.
例句：Ecotourism can benefit local communities and protect endangered species. 生态旅游可以造福当地社区并保护濒危物种。

例句：Costa Rica is a popular destination for ecotourism. 哥斯达黎加是生态旅游的热门目的地。

近义词：sustainable tourism, nature tourism, green tourism
反义词：mass tourism, conventional tourism
词性：noun

Etymology:  eco- + tourism.  "Eco-"  is a combining form extracted from "ecology," from Greek *oikos "house" + -logia "study of." "Tourism" comes from French tourisme, from tour "tour."
