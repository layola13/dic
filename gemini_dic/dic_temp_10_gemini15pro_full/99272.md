interconnected:/ˌɪntərˈkəˈnektɪd/
adjective
相互连接的，互相联系的
mutually connected; linked together.
The global economy is a complex web of interconnected systems. 全球经济是一个相互联系的复杂系统网络。
The various departments within the company are highly interconnected. 公司内部各个部门之间联系紧密。
interrelated, linked, networked, joined
isolated, separate, detached, independent
adjective
