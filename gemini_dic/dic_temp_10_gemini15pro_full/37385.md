commentator:/ˈkɒmənteɪtə(r) (UK), ˈkɑːmənteɪtər (US)| n.|评论员，解说员|A person who comments on events or on a text.|The commentator described the game in detail. 解说员详细地描述了这场比赛.|synonyms: analyst, reporter, announcer, pundit|antonyms: listener, audience, participant|noun

commentator:/ˈkɒmənteɪtə(r) (UK), ˈkɑːmənteɪtər (US)| n.|讲解员，注释者|A person who provides a commentary on something.|The commentator explained the historical context of the poem. 讲解员解释了这首诗的历史背景.|synonyms: interpreter, expounder, annotator|antonyms:  N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin commentatus, past participle of commentari "to explain, write notes on," from Latin commentum "invention, fiction, fabrication; interpretation, explanation," from comminisci "to devise, invent, contrive," from com- "together" + *men- "to think."
