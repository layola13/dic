breading:/ˈbrɛdɪŋ/| n.|面包屑，裹粉|A coating of breadcrumbs, or other crumbs, used to coat food before frying or baking.|例句：The chicken had a crispy breading. 这鸡肉有一层酥脆的裹粉。|例句：She dipped the fish in the breading before frying it. 她把鱼在裹粉里蘸了一下，然后炸了。|近义词：coating, crumb, crust|反义词:|noun
