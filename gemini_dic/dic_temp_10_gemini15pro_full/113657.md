machines:/məˈʃiːnz/ | n. |机器; 机械装置|A device that uses power to apply forces and control movement to perform an intended action. |The factory is full of noisy machines. 工厂里满是嘈杂的机器。

Machines help us do work more efficiently. 机器帮助我们更高效地工作。

|近义词：apparatus, device, mechanism, appliance, tool, instrument, engine, motor|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
