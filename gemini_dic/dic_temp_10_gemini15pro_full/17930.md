beetled:/ˈbiːtld/| adj.|甲虫似的，突出的，像甲虫的|Having a projecting brow; overhanging.  Resembling a beetle, especially in having a hard, rounded shell-like appearance.  |The old man had beetled brows and a stern expression.  这位老人眉毛突出，表情严肃。| The car's beetled hood gave it a sporty look. 这辆车的甲壳虫状引擎盖使它看起来很运动。 |Synonyms: protruding, overhanging, prominent, bulging|Antonyms: recessed, sunken, flat|adjective
