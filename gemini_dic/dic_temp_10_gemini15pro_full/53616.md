discardure:/dɪsˈkɑːrdʒər/
n.
排泄物；废弃物
The act of discarding something, or something that has been discarded.
The gardener removed the discardure from the flower beds. 园丁把花坛里的废弃物清除了。
近义词：waste, refuse, rubbish, trash, garbage, debris
反义词：retention, preservation

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *discarditura, from Latin discardō (“I throw away”) + -ūra (suffix forming nouns of action).
