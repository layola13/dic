deauthorizations:/diːˌɔːθəraɪˈzeɪʃənz/
n.|取消授权，撤销授权|The act of withdrawing authorization or permission, often related to access or privileges.|The company issued deauthorizations for several former employees to access the secure server.  该公司对几名前雇员发出了取消访问安全服务器的授权。|revocations, cancellations|authorizations|noun
