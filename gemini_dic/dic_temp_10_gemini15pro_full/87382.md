herpes:/ˈhɜːrpiːz/ (US) /ˈhɜːpiːz/ (UK)| n. |疱疹|A viral disease causing small painful blisters and inflammation, most commonly at the junction of skin and mucous membrane in the mouth or on the genitals.|She was diagnosed with genital herpes.|Synonyms: cold sore (for oral herpes), blisters|Antonyms: None|Noun
