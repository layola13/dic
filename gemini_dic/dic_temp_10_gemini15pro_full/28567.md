care-for:/ˈker fɔːr/ /ˈkɛər fɔr/| v.|关心；照顾；喜欢；想要|To feel affection or concern for; to look after; to like or desire.|She cared for her elderly mother until her death. 她照顾她年迈的母亲直到她去世。|近义词：look after, tend, like, fancy|反义词:neglect, dislike, hate|词性:动词

care-for:/ˈker fɔːr/ /ˈkɛər fɔr/| n.|关心；照顾|Affection and concern; the act of looking after someone or something.|The children were in need of care and attention. 孩子们需要照顾和关注。|近义词：attention, support, concern|反义词:neglect, indifference|词性:名词
