index: /ˈɪndɛks/ | n. |索引; 指数; 标志; 指示| A list of names or topics referred to in a book, arranged alphabetically or topically at the end of the book, giving the number of the page on which each item occurs. Also, a statistical measure of changes in a representative group of individual data points. | The index lists all the topics covered in the book.  索引列出了书中涵盖的所有主题。| synonyms: table of contents, guide, directory  | antonyms:  disorder, chaos | noun
