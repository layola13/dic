forgent:/ˈfɔːrdʒənt/ | n. | 伪造者 | A person who makes something false; a counterfeiter. | The forgent was apprehended and charged with fraud.  伪造者被逮捕并被指控犯有欺诈罪。| 近义词：counterfeiter, faker, forger | 反义词：N/A | 词性：名词


词源:
From Middle English *forgent, from Old French forgent, present participle of forger (“to forge”), from Vulgar Latin *fodicāre, from Latin fodīcāre (“to poke, prod, pierce”), from Latin fodix (“pointed stick, spade”).
