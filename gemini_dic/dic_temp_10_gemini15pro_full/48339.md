decriminalizing:/diːˈkrɪmɪnəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使非刑事化；将…合法化
The act of removing or reducing the criminal penalties associated with a particular action or behavior.
Many countries are considering decriminalizing the possession of small amounts of marijuana. 许多国家正在考虑将持有少量大麻合法化。
