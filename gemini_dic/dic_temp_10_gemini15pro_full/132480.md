nonderogatory:/ˌnɒndɪˈrɑːɡətɔːri/ | adj. | 非贬义的；不损毁名誉的 | Not intended to disparage or belittle; neutral or positive. |  He used nonderogatory language to describe the situation, focusing on the facts rather than personal opinions. 他用非贬义的语言描述了情况，侧重于事实而不是个人意见。

The article provided a nonderogatory account of the politician's career, highlighting both successes and failures. 这篇文章对这位政治家的职业生涯进行了非贬义的描述，突出了成功和失败。| 近义词：neutral, complimentary, respectful, inoffensive | 反义词：derogatory, pejorative, disparaging | 词性: adjective
