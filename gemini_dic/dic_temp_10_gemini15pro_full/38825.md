conepatl:/koʊˈneɪpɑːtl/| n. |墨西哥糖松|A tall evergreen tree native to Mexico, *Pinus ayacahuite*, with long, drooping needles and edible seeds.  Also refers to the edible seeds themselves.|The conepatl's seeds are a traditional food source in some parts of Mexico.|近义词：Mexican white pine, ayacahuite pine|反义词:|名词
