misgendering:/ˌmɪsˈdʒɛndərɪŋ/ | n. |  错误称呼性别 | Referring to someone using a gendered term (e.g., pronouns, titles) that does not align with their gender identity. |  Misgendering someone can be hurtful and disrespectful. 错误称呼某人的性别可能具有伤害性和不尊重性。

Misgendering him made him feel invalidated. 错误称呼他的性别让他感到不被认可。| 近义词: deadnaming | 反义词: respecting pronouns | 词性: noun
