monographical:/ˌmɒnəˈɡræfɪkəl/ | adj. | 专著的；专题研究的 | Relating to a monograph; pertaining to a detailed written study of a single specialized subject or an aspect of it. |  This monographical study provides a comprehensive overview of the artist's early work. 这部专题研究著作对这位艺术家的早期作品进行了全面的概述。

| Example: The professor assigned a monographical paper on the impact of social media on political discourse. 教授布置了一篇关于社交媒体对政治话语影响的专题论文。 |Synonyms: specialized, in-depth, focused, exhaustive, detailed, comprehensive | Antonyms: general, superficial, broad, cursory |词性: adjective
