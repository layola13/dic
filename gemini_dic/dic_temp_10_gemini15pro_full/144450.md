outswindling:/ˌaʊtˈswɪndlɪŋ/
verb
通过欺骗胜过
surpassing in swindling; cheating more successfully than.
He outswindled the other con men and made off with the entire fortune. 他比其他骗子更胜一筹，卷走了全部财产。
近义词: outcheating, outsmarting, defrauding
反义词: being honest, being fair
