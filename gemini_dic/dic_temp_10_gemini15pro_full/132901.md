nonelectronics:/ˌnɒnɪlɛkˈtrɒnɪks/
n.
非电子产品；非电子器件
Products or devices that do not use electronic components.
Many traditional toys are nonelectronics, offering a different kind of play experience. 许多传统的玩具是非电子产品，提供了一种不同的游戏体验。
Traditional board games are considered nonelectronics.  传统的棋盘游戏被认为是非电子产品。
近义词:non-electric, mechanical, analog
反义词:electronics, electric, digital
词性:名词

词源：
"nonelectronics" is a combination of the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the word "electronics". "Electronics" itself comes from "electron" + "-ic" + "-s".  The word "electron" has its roots in Greek, from "ēlektron" meaning "amber", as static electricity was first observed by rubbing amber.
