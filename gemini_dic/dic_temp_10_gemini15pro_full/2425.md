adjudge:/əˈdʒʌdʒ/
verb
判决，裁定，宣判；认为
To pronounce or decree judicially; to award or assign judicially. To deem; to consider or judge to be.
The court adjudged him guilty of the crime. 法庭宣判他有罪。
The prize was adjudged to the youngest contestant.  奖品判给了最年轻的参赛者。

近义词:decide, decree, rule, judge, deem
反义词:absolve, acquit
词性:verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *adjugen*, from Old French *ajujer*, from Latin *adjudicare* (“to award, assign by judgment”), from *ad-* (“to”) + *judicare* (“to judge”), from *judex* (“judge”).
