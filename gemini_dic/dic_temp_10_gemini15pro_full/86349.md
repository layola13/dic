hectic:/ˈhɛktɪk/| adj.|忙乱的；紧张的；狂热的|Full of activity, or very busy and fast.|例句：I've had a hectic week. 我这周过得很忙。|近义词：frantic, chaotic, busy, bustling|反义词：calm, peaceful, quiet|词性：形容词
Etymology: From Greek *hektikós* "habitual, consumptive," from *hexis* "habit, state of body." Originally referred to a persistent fever, later extended to any feverish activity.
