cold:/koʊld/ (American) /kəʊld/ (British)| adj. n.|寒冷的，冷的；感冒|Lacking warmth; having a low temperature. Can also refer to a common illness.|例句：The weather is cold today. 今天天气很冷。

例句：I have a cold. 我感冒了。

近义词：chilly, freezing, frigid, icy (adj.), flu (n.)|反义词:hot, warm|词性:形容词, 名词


cold:/koʊld/ (American) /kəʊld/ (British)| n.|寒冷，冷淡|The state of being cold; a low temperature.|例句：The cold made my teeth chatter. 寒冷使我的牙齿打颤。

例句：She gave him the cold shoulder. 她对他很冷淡。

近义词：chill, iciness|反义词:heat, warmth|词性:名词


词源:From Old English *ceald, from Proto-Germanic *kaldaz, from Proto-Indo-European *gel- (“cold”).
