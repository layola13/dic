descendants:/dɪˈsɛndənts/
noun
后裔；子孙
A person, plant, or animal that is descended from a particular ancestor.
Her descendants still live in the area. 她的后裔仍然住在该地区。
synonyms: offspring, progeny, heirs, successors
antonyms: ancestors, forebears
