angiomonospermous: /ˌændʒi.oʊˌmɒn.oʊˈspɜːm.əs/ | adj. | 被子植物单子叶的 | Having the characteristics of both angiosperms (flowering plants) and monocotyledons (plants with a single seed leaf).  It refers to a plant that has both enclosed seeds (characteristic of angiosperms) and a single cotyledon (characteristic of monocots). |  The corn plant is angiomonospermous, exhibiting features of both flowering plants and monocots. | 近义词: monocotyledonous | 反义词: dicotyledonous | 词性: adjective


Etymology:
From "angio-" (vessel, referring to the enclosed seed), "mono-" (one), and "sperm" (seed).
