affecters:/əˈfɛktərz/
| n. |影响者；感染者|Those who affect or influence something or someone; those who are infected by something.|The affecters of change in the organization were the new executives.|近义词：influencers, agents, actors|反义词:unaffected (adj.)|词性:名词

Etymology:
From the verb "affect" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting a person or thing that performs the action of the verb).  "Affect" comes from the Latin "afficere" (to act on, influence), from "ad-" (to, towards) + "facere" (to do, make).
