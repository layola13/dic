biofuel:/ˈbaɪoʊˌfjuːəl/| n. |生物燃料|A fuel produced from renewable biological resources, such as vegetable oils, animal fats, or agricultural waste.| Example 1: Biofuel is becoming increasingly popular as a sustainable energy source. 生物燃料作为一种可持续能源正变得越来越受欢迎。

Example 2:  The use of biofuel can help reduce greenhouse gas emissions. 使用生物燃料可以帮助减少温室气体排放。|Synonym: bioenergy, renewable fuel|Antonym: fossil fuel|noun
