molt:/moʊlt/ | v. n. | 蜕皮;换羽;脱毛 | To shed periodically feathers, skin, hair, or other outer covering, which is then replaced by a new growth. | The snake molted its skin. 这条蛇蜕了皮。| 近义词: shed, exuviate | 反义词: grow | 词性: 动词, 名词

molter:/ˈmoʊltər/ | n. | 正在蜕皮的动物 | An animal that is currently molting. | The molter was vulnerable while its new skin hardened. 这只正在蜕皮的动物在新皮变硬之前很脆弱。| 近义词: shedder | 反义词: None | 词性: 名词

词源: From Old English *mūtian* "to change," of Germanic origin; related to Dutch *muiten* "to mutiny," German *mausern* "to molt."
