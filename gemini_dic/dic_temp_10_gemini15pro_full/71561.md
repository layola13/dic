例句：The flashdrive is full, I need to delete some files.  U盘满了，我需要删除一些文件。|近义词：USB drive, thumb drive, pen drive, memory stick|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "hard drive" as a less portable alternative) |词性:noun
