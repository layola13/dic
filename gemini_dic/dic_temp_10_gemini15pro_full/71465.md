flameproofing:/ˈfleɪmpruːfɪŋ/
n.|防火处理，阻燃处理|The process of treating materials with a substance that makes them resistant to catching fire.|The curtains had undergone flameproofing treatment. 窗帘经过了防火处理。|近义词:fireproofing|反义词:combustibility|词性:名词

flameproof:/ˈfleɪmpruːf/
adj.|防火的，阻燃的|Resistant to catching fire.|Flameproof clothing is essential for firefighters. 对于消防员来说，防火服至关重要。|近义词:fireproof, fire-retardant|反义词:flammable, combustible|词性:形容词

flameproof:/ˈfleɪmpruːf/
v.|使...防火，使...阻燃|To treat (a material) with a substance that makes it resistant to catching fire.|They flameproofed the children's pajamas. 他们对儿童睡衣进行了防火处理。|近义词:fireproof|反义词:ignite|词性:动词
