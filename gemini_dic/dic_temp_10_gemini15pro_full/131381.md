noncircumspectly:/ˌnɒnsɜːrkəmˈspɛktli/
adverb
轻率地，不谨慎地
without attention to potential consequences; rashly or imprudently.
He acted noncircumspectly when he invested all his money in a single stock. 他把所有的钱都投资在一支股票上，真是轻率。
Synonyms: rashly, recklessly, imprudently, heedlessly, incautiously
Antonyms: cautiously, carefully, prudently, thoughtfully, discreetly
词性: adverb
