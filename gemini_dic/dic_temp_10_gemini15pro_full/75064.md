frightfulness:/ˈfraɪtfʊlnəs/| n. |恐怖; 可怕; 骇人听闻|The frightfulness of the war left many scarred for life. 战争的恐怖让许多人终生伤痕累累。|例句：The frightfulness of the accident shocked everyone.  这起事故的恐怖震惊了所有人。

The sheer frightfulness of the scene made her turn away.  这场景的骇人听闻让她转过身去。|近义词：terribleness, dreadfulness, horror, awfulness|反义词：pleasantness, beauty, delightfulness|词性:名词

Etymology: From Middle English *frightfulnes*, derived from frightful + -ness.  Frightful itself comes from fright + -ful, with fright ultimately originating from Old English *fyrhto*, meaning "fear."
