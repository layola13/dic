carbazole: /ˈkɑːrbəˌzoʊl/ (US), /ˈkɑːbəˌzɒl/ (UK)| n.|咔唑|A colorless crystalline organic compound used in the manufacture of pigments and dyes, having the molecular formula C12H9N. It is a heterocyclic aromatic organic compound.  A nitrogen atom is present in the structure and contributes two electrons to the aromatic system.|Carbazole derivatives are used in the production of certain antihistamines.|近义词: dibenzopyrrole|反义词: None|词性: noun
