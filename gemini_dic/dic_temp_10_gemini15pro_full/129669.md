non-delinquent:/ˌnɑːn dɪˈlɪŋkwənt/ | adj. | 未拖欠的，无违约的 | Not delinquent; having fulfilled all obligations, especially financial ones.  |  例句：The bank approved his loan application because he had a non-delinquent credit history.  银行批准了他的贷款申请，因为他有良好的信用记录，没有拖欠记录。

例句：Non-delinquent taxpayers receive a discount.  未拖欠税款的纳税人可以享受折扣。

近义词：current, up-to-date, paid-up | 反义词：delinquent, overdue, in arrears | 词性：形容词

词源：non- (prefix meaning "not") + delinquent (from Latin delinquere, "to fail in duty")
