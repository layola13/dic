foretastes:/ˈfɔːrˌteɪsts/ /ˈfɔːrˌteɪsts/
n.|预示，先兆，预先体验|An advance indication, sample, or warning of something to come. |The appetizer was a foretaste of the delicious meal to come. 开胃菜预示着接下来美味的正餐。| synonyms: preview,  presage,  inkling,  glimpse,  sample,  teaser,  aperitif | antonyms: aftertaste,  sequel,  conclusion|noun

foretastes:/ˈfɔːrˌteɪsts/ /ˈfɔːrˌteɪsts/
v.|预先体验|To experience or taste something before it is fully realized or available.|The trailer foretastes the excitement of the movie.  预告片预先让人体验了电影的精彩。|synonyms: preview, presample |antonyms:  reminisce,  review|verb
