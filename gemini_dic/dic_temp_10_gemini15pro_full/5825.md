amateurishnesses:/ˈæmətərɪʃnɪsɪz/| n. |业余爱好；不熟练；幼稚| The amateurishnesses of the performance were evident in the clumsy choreography and forgotten lines.  演出中的不熟练之处体现在笨拙的编舞和忘记台词上。|例句：The amateurishnesses of his early paintings are now endearing. 他早期绘画的稚嫩现在看来很可爱。

The numerous amateurishnesses in the report made it clear it wasn't written by a professional. 报告中大量的业余之处表明它不是由专业人士撰写的。|近义词：inexperience, clumsiness, awkwardness, naiveté |反义词: professionalism, skill, expertise, sophistication |词性: noun

词源: From amateur + -ish (suffix forming adjectives meaning 'somewhat like', 'having the nature of', 'given to', 'tending to') + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives).
