hyperorthodox:/ˌhaɪpərˈɔːrθədɑːks/ | adj. |极端正统的，极端保守的 | Extremely orthodox; adhering rigidly and strictly to traditional beliefs and practices, often beyond what is considered normative. |  The hyperorthodox community shunned all forms of modern technology.  极端正统的社区排斥一切形式的现代科技。

The rabbi's hyperorthodox views alienated many younger members of the congregation.  拉比极端正统的观点疏远了许多年轻的会众。 | 近义词:ultra-orthodox, fundamentalist, zealot | 反义词:liberal, progressive, reformist | 词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- + orthodox.  "Hyper-" is a Greek prefix meaning "over, above, beyond." "Orthodox" comes from Greek "orthos" (right, correct) + "doxa" (opinion, belief).
