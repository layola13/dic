cadaverine:/kəˈdævəˌriːn/
n.
尸胺
A foul-smelling compound produced by the decomposition of animal tissue.
例句：Cadaverine is one of the amines responsible for the putrid smell of decaying flesh.  尸胺是造成腐肉恶臭的胺类物质之一。

例句：The presence of cadaverine can be an indicator of spoilage in food.  尸胺的存在可以作为食物腐败的指标。
近义词：putrescine
