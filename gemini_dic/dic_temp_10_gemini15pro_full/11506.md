aridnesses:/əˈrɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
干旱，干燥
The state or quality of being arid; dryness.
The aridnesses of the desert made it difficult for anything to grow. 沙漠的干旱使得任何东西都很难生长。

The prolonged aridnesses devastated the crops. 长期的干旱摧毁了农作物。

近义词: dryness, drought, barrenness
反义词: humidity, wetness, moisture
词性: 名词 (plural)
