antiasthmatics:/ˌæntiæsˈmætɪks/ | n. | 抗哮喘药 | Medications used to treat asthma. |  The doctor prescribed antiasthmatics to help control her breathing. 医生开了抗哮喘药来帮助她控制呼吸。| Synonyms: asthma medications, bronchodilators, corticosteroids | Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but one might consider irritants or allergens that trigger asthma attacks) | noun

Etymology:
anti- (prefix) : against, opposing (Greek origin)
asthma (noun): a respiratory condition marked by spasms in the bronchi of the lungs (Greek origin)
-tic (suffix): relating to, or affected with (Greek origin)
-s (suffix) plural marker
