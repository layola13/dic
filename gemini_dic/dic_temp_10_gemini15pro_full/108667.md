leafletting:/ˈliːflɪtɪŋ/
n.
散发传单；发传单的活动
the activity of distributing leaflets to people
The group organized a leafletting campaign to raise awareness about the issue.  这个团体组织了一场散发传单的活动，以提高人们对这个问题的认识。

近义词: distributing flyers, handing out pamphlets, flyer distribution
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) information suppression, censorship
