graciously: /ˈɡreɪʃəsli/ (US) /ˈɡreɪʃəsli/ (UK)
adverb
亲切地；和蔼地；优雅地
In a kind, polite, and pleasant way, especially in a way that shows willingness to help.

She graciously accepted the gift. 她优雅地收下了礼物。
He graciously offered to help me with my luggage. 他亲切地提出帮我拿行李。


近义词: politely, kindly, courteously, generously, graciously
反义词: rudely, unkindly, discourteously, ungraciously

词性: adverb (副词)

词源:  From Middle English *graciousli, from Old French gracieusement, from gracieux (“gracious”) + -ment (“-ly”).  Ultimately from Latin gratia (“grace, favor, thanks”).
