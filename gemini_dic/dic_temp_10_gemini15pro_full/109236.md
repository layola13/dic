leisured:/ˈliːʒərd/ /ˈleʒərd/ | adj. | 悠闲的，有闲的 | Having plenty of leisure; not occupied with work. |  She led a leisured life, spending her days reading and gardening. 她过着悠闲的生活，每天读书和园艺。|  He enjoyed a leisured breakfast, savoring every bite. 他享用了一顿悠闲的早餐，细细品味每一口。| 近义词: relaxed, unhurried, leisurely | 反义词: busy, hurried, occupied | 词性: 形容词

词源: From leisure + -ed.  "Leisure" comes from Old French "leisir" meaning "permission, opportunity," ultimately from Latin "licēre" meaning "to be permitted."
