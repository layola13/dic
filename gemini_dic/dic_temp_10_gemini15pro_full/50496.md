depressurized:/diːˈprɛʃəraɪzd/ | adj. | 减压的，卸压的 |  Reduced in pressure, especially having had air or gas removed. | The aircraft cabin was depressurized before landing.  飞机舱在着陆前减压了。| synonyms: decompressed, vented, evacuated | antonyms: pressurized, compressed | adjective

depressurize:/diːˈprɛʃəraɪz/ | v. |  减压，卸压 | To reduce the pressure of a sealed container, such as an aircraft cabin or a pressure cooker. |  They had to depressurize the cabin before opening the door. 他们必须在打开门之前降低机舱压力。 | synonyms: decompress, vent, evacuate | antonyms: pressurize, compress | verb
