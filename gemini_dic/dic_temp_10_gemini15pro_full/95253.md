incanous:/ɪnˈkeɪnəs/| adj.|灰白色的，头发灰白的|Covered with short, fine, whitish hairs or down.|例句：The incanous leaves of the plant shimmered in the sunlight.  这种植物灰白的叶子在阳光下闪闪发光。

例句：The old man's incanous beard reached down to his chest. 老人的灰白胡须垂到胸前。|近义词：hoary, greyish, canescent|反义词: dark, black|词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *incanus, from in- (“in, on”) + canus (“white, gray”).
