fishwives:/ˈfɪʃˌwaɪvz/
noun
鱼妇们；卖鱼女；泼妇
a woman who sells fish, especially one who is loud and quarrelsome.
The fishwives at the market were known for their sharp tongues and quick wit.  市场上的鱼妇们以她们的伶牙俐齿和机智闻名。
The fishwives argued loudly over the price of the catch.  鱼妇们大声争论着渔获的价格。

近义词:shrew, virago, termagant
反义词:lady

词性:n. (plural)
