noncommendably:/ˌnɒnkəˈmɛndəbli/
adverb
不值得称赞地，不值得推荐地
in a manner that is not deserving of praise or recommendation
His behavior was noncommendably rude to the guests. 他对客人的行为非常粗鲁，不值得称赞。
The task was performed noncommendably, resulting in several errors. 这项任务完成得不好，导致了几个错误。

Synonyms:  discreditably,  unworthily,  reprehensibly,  blameworthy, objectionably
Antonyms: commendably, laudably, praiseworthily, admirably

词性: adverb
