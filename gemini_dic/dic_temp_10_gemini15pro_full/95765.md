inconsumable:/ˌɪnkənˈsuːməbl/
adjective
不可消耗的;不能用尽的
Not able to be consumed, especially not able to be eaten or destroyed by use.
例句：
Virtual goods are often inconsumable, meaning they can be used repeatedly without being depleted.
虚拟商品通常是不可消耗的，这意味着它们可以重复使用而不会耗尽。

例句：
The energy source they discovered was seemingly inconsumable, providing power for generations.
他们发现的能源似乎是取之不尽的，为几代人提供了动力。

近义词:inexhaustible, enduring, permanent, everlasting
反义词:consumable, expendable, perishable, finite
词性:adjective
