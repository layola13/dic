linchpin:/ˈlɪntʃpɪn/| n.|关键人物；核心；关键|A person or thing vital to an enterprise or organization.  Something that holds the various elements of a complicated structure together.|例句：She is the linchpin of the whole project. 她事整个项目的核心人物。

例句：The central axle is the linchpin of the entire vehicle. 中心轴是整辆车的关键。|近义词：keystone, cornerstone, centerpiece, pivot, essential |反义词: inessential, unimportant, peripheral|词性:noun
