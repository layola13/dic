nonsuggestive:/ˌnɒnsəˈdʒestɪv/
adjective
非暗示性的；不具挑逗性的
Not tending to suggest something improper or indecent.
The movie was rated PG because it was nonsuggestive and appropriate for children.  这部电影被评为PG级，因为它没有暗示性内容，适合儿童观看。
The advertisement used nonsuggestive imagery. 这则广告使用了非暗示性的图像。

近义词:clean, decent, wholesome, unobjectionable
反义词:suggestive, indecent, provocative, risqué
词性:adjective

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + suggestive (from suggest + -ive)
