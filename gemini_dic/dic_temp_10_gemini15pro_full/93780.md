immenser:/ɪˈmɛnsər/| adj.|巨大的，无限的|Extremely large; immeasurable.|The universe is immensly large. 宇宙浩瀚无垠。|Synonyms: enormous, vast, huge, gigantic, colossal|Antonyms: tiny, small, minuscule, insignificant|adjective

Etymology->
From French *immense*, from Latin *immensus*, from *in-* (not) + *mensus* (measured).  Literally "unmeasured".
