didym:/ˈdɪdɪm/| n. |双晶;孪晶|A double crystal; a twin crystal.  Specifically, a crystal consisting of two similar halves united, usually with the twin plane parallel to a primary face.|The didym found in the cave was a rare and beautiful specimen. 在山洞里发现的孪晶是一件稀有而美丽的标本。|近义词：twin, doublet|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially "single crystal")|名词
