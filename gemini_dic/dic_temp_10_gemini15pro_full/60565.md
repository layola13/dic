elastically:/ɪˈlæstɪkli/| adv.|弹性地，有弹性地|in a flexible and resilient manner, capable of returning to its original shape or size after being stretched or compressed.|The rubber band stretched elastically.  橡皮筋很有弹性地伸展。|The gymnast landed elastically on the mat. 体操运动员弹性地落在垫子上。|近义词：flexibly, resiliently, springily|反义词：rigidly, inflexibly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From elastic + -ly.  Elastic comes from Late Latin elasticus, from Greek elastikos “propulsive,” from elaunein “to drive, beat out,” from PIE root *el- “to strike, throw.”
