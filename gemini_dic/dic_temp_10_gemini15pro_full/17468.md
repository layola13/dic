beardie:/ˈbiədi/| n.|大胡子男人；留胡须的人；（尤指）蓄山羊胡的人；（某些）有须毛的动物（尤指鸟类和蜥蜴）|A person, especially a man, with a beard, especially a goatee beard. Also refers to some animals, especially birds and lizards, that have whisker-like hairs.|He's a real beardie, with a long, flowing beard. 他是个真正的大胡子，留着长长的飘逸的胡须。|The beardie at the zoo seemed quite content basking in the sun. 动物园里的长胡子蜥蜴似乎很满足于晒太阳。|近义词：bearded man, bearded person|反义词: clean-shaven man|noun

beardie:/ˈbɪədi/| n.|长胡子龙；鬃狮蜥|A common name for the Pogona vitticeps, a species of lizard.|My beardie loves to eat crickets. 我的鬃狮蜥喜欢吃蟋蟀。|She took her beardie to the vet for a checkup. 她带她的鬃狮蜥去看兽医检查。|近义词：Pogona vitticeps, bearded dragon|反义词: N/A|noun
