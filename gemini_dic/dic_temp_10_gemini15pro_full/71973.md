flimflam:/ˈflɪmˌflæm/
n.|欺骗；花招；胡言乱语|a trick or deception; nonsense|He tried to sell me some flimflam about a miracle cure, but I wasn't falling for it. 他试图向我兜售一些关于神奇疗法的胡言乱语，但我没有上当。|synonyms: trickery, deception, fraud, hoax, humbug|antonyms: honesty, truth, sincerity|noun

flimflam:/ˈflɪmˌflæm/
vt.|欺骗；哄骗|to deceive or trick someone|They flimflammed the old lady out of her life savings. 他们骗走了老太太的毕生积蓄。|synonyms: deceive, trick, cheat, swindle, defraud|antonyms: be honest, be truthful|verb
