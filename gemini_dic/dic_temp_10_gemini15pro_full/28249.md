captainess:/ˈkæptɪnɪs/| n. |女船长，女舰长|A woman who is a captain, especially of a ship. |例句：The captainess steered the ship through the storm. 女船长驾驶着船只穿越了暴风雨。

例句：The captainess gave the order to abandon ship. 女船长下令弃船。|近义词：captain, skipper (often used for smaller vessels)|反义词: crew, passenger|词性:noun

词源: From captain + -ess (a suffix used to form feminine nouns).
