caudated:/ˈkɔːdeɪtɪd/| adj.|有尾的，尾状的|Having a tail or a tail-like appendage.  Relating to the caudate nucleus (a part of the brain).|The caudate nucleus plays a vital role in how the brain learns. 尾状核在大脑学习中起着至关重要的作用。

The sperm of the species is caudated.  该物种的精子是有尾的。|近义词：tailed, caudate|反义词: acaudate, tailless|词性: adjective
Etymology: From Latin *caudātus* (having a tail), past participle of *caudāre* (to provide with a tail), from *cauda* (tail).
