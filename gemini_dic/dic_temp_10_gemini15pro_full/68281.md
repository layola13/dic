falafel:/fəˈlɑːfəl/ (US), /fəˈlæfəl/ (UK)| n.|沙拉三明治;油炸鹰嘴豆丸子|A Middle Eastern dish of spiced mashed chickpeas or fava beans formed into balls or patties and deep-fried. |例句：I had a delicious falafel sandwich for lunch. 我午餐吃了一个美味的沙拉三明治。

例句：The falafel were crispy on the outside and soft on the inside. 这些油炸鹰嘴豆丸子外脆里嫩。

近义词：fritter, croquette|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
