inklings:/ˈɪŋklɪŋz/
n.
暗示; 跡象; 略知; 模糊的想法
a slight knowledge or suspicion; a hint.
I had no inkling of what was going on. 我对发生的事情一无所知。
synonyms: hint, suggestion, suspicion, impression, notion, idea, sense, feeling, premonition, hunch
antonyms: certainty, knowledge, proof
noun
