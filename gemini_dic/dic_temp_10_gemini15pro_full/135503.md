nonpictorial:/ˌnɒnpɪkˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
非图画的；非绘画的
Not consisting of or represented by pictures or images; abstract.
The report contained dense, nonpictorial descriptions of the equipment.  这份报告包含了对设备的密集的、非图画式的描述。
His writing style is nonpictorial and focuses on abstract ideas. 他的写作风格是非图画式的，专注于抽象的概念。
近义词:abstract, nonrepresentational, non-visual
反义词:pictorial, visual, graphic
词性:adjective

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non "not."
pictorial (adjective): From Latin pictorius "of or pertaining to painting," from pictor "painter," from pingere "to paint."
-ial (suffix):  Forming adjectives meaning "having the character or form of," from Latin -ialis.
