dwarfs:/dwɔːrfz/| n.|矮人；侏儒；矮小的动植物|A person, animal, or plant that is much smaller than the usual size of its kind.  It is also a verb meaning to make something seem small by comparison.|The mountains dwarfed the houses below. 群山使下面的房屋显得矮小。|Synonyms: midget, pygmy|Antonyms: giant, colossal|Noun, Verb

dwarfs:/dwɔːrfz/| v.|使相形见绌；使显得矮小|To cause to seem small or insignificant in comparison.|Her new achievements dwarfed her earlier successes. 她的新成就使她早期的成功相形见绌。|Synonyms: outshine, overshadow|Antonyms: enhance, magnify|Verb
