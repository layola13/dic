brain-dead:/breɪnˈdɛd/| adj.|脑死亡的；无思维能力的；愚蠢的|A person who is brain-dead has permanent and irreversible loss of all brain function.|The doctors declared him brain-dead after the accident. 医生在事故后宣布他脑死亡。|Synonyms: comatose, unconscious, vegetative|Antonyms: conscious, alive, alert|adjective

brain-dead:/breɪnˈdɛd/| n.|脑死亡的人|A person who is brain-dead.|The family had to make the difficult decision to take the brain-dead patient off life support. 家属不得不做出艰难的决定，将脑死亡的病人撤离生命维持系统。|Synonyms: (none for this noun sense)|Antonyms: (none for this noun sense)|noun
