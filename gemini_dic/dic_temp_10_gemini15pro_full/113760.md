macrophyllous: /ˌmækroʊˈfɪləs/ | adj. | 大叶的；巨叶的 | Having large leaves.  Specifically, in the Raunkiaer system, having leaves with areas greater than 18,225 square millimeters (greater than 28.26 square inches). |  Example 1:  The rainforest was filled with macrophyllous plants, their broad leaves casting deep shadows. 雨林中充满了大叶植物，它们宽阔的叶子投下深深的阴影。

Example 2: Macrophyllous ferns unfurled their fronds in the humid air.  大叶蕨类植物在潮湿的空气中舒展着它们的叶子。 | Synonym: megaphyllous, large-leaved | Antonym: microphyllous, small-leaved | word type: adjective
