erethism: /ˌerəˈθɪzəm/ | n. |过度兴奋，兴奋异常; (尤指由汞中毒引起的)震颤谵妄 | A state of abnormal irritability or excitement, especially from mercury poisoning.  |  He exhibited signs of erethism, including tremors and anxiety. 他表现出震颤谵妄的迹象，包括颤抖和焦虑。 | The patient's erethism was attributed to mercury exposure.  该病人的过度兴奋被认为是汞接触引起的。| 近义词: irritability, excitability, restlessness | 反义词: calmness, tranquility | 词性: 名词

词源: From Greek *erethismos*, from erethizein 'to irritate, provoke', from ereth- 'irritant'.
