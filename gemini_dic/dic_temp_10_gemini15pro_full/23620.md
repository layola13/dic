branchier:/ˈbræntʃiər/| adj.|更多枝杈的；分支更多的；更复杂的|Having more branches; more subdivided or complex. |The branchier the tree, the more birds it can house.  树枝越多，能容纳的鸟就越多。|The company's organizational structure became branchier as it grew.  随着公司的发展，其组织结构变得更加复杂。|近义词：more complex, more intricate, more subdivided|反义词：simpler, less complex, less branched|词性:形容词

词源: From branch + -ier (comparative adjective suffix).  来自于 branch（树枝） + -ier（比较级形容词后缀）。
