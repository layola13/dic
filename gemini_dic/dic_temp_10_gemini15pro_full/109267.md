lemmings:/ˈlɛmɪŋz/
n.
旅鼠
Small rodents known for their periodic mass migrations and, mistakenly, for mass suicide.
Lemmings are small rodents that live in the Arctic. 旅鼠是生活在北极的小型啮齿动物。
Large numbers of lemmings migrate together when food is scarce. 当食物匮乏时，大量的旅鼠会一起迁徙。
近义词:vole, rodent
反义词:(No direct antonym for an animal species.  Could consider "predator" in a loose sense)
词性:noun
