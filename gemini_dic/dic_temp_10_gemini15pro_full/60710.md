electro-hydraulic:/ɪˌlektroʊ haɪˈdrɔːlɪk/
adjective
电动液压的
Operated by a combination of electric and hydraulic power.
The electro-hydraulic steering system provides precise control. 电动液压转向系统提供了精确的控制。

ETYMOLOGY->
electro- (prefix): from Greek *ēlektron* meaning "amber," referring to static electricity.
hydraulic (adjective): from Greek *hydraulikos* meaning "water organ,"  from *hydōr* "water" + *aulos* "pipe."
