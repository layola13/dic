overindexing:/ˌoʊvərˈɪndɛksɪŋ/ | n. |过度索引；过度代表；过度重视| The act of giving something more importance or representation than it statistically warrants. |  Marketing companies often focus on overindexing on specific demographics to target their advertising campaigns. 营销公司经常专注于特定人群的过度索引，以定位其广告活动。

The report showed an overindexing of millennial women in the online gaming community.  这份报告显示，千禧一代女性在网络游戏社区中过度代表。|近义词：overrepresentation, overemphasis |反义词: underrepresentation, underemaphasis |词性:名词

Etymology:  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much") + "index" (from Latin "indicare," meaning "to point out") + "-ing" (present participle suffix).
