disabuse:/ˌdɪsəˈbjuːz/
|vt.|纠正，使醒悟，打消…的错误念头|To free someone from a misconception or deception.|He disabused me of the notion that I could get rich quickly.|近义词:undeceive, enlighten, correct, set straight|反义词:deceive, mislead, delude|verb
