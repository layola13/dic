casias: /ˈkɑːsiəs/ /ˈkeɪʒəs/ | n. |木麻黄属植物；木麻黄树| Cassias are a genus of flowering plants in the family Fabaceae, commonly known as cassias. They are native to tropical and subtropical regions. | The cassias along the road provided welcome shade. 路边的木麻黄树提供了受欢迎的阴凉。|Synonym: senna | Antonym: N/A | Noun
