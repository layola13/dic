arsenic:/ˈɑːrsnɪk/ /ˈɑːrsənɪk/| n. adj.|砷，砒霜；含砷的|A chemical element that is a metalloid, meaning it has properties of both metals and nonmetals. It is highly toxic and is often used in pesticides and insecticides. As an adjective, it describes something containing arsenic.|例句：Arsenic is a highly poisonous substance. 砷是一种剧毒物质。|近义词：poison, toxin|反义词:(no direct antonym, possibly "harmless" in some contexts) |词性:noun, adjective
