lithodomus:/ˌlɪθəˈdoʊməs/| n. |石蛏|A marine bivalve mollusk of the family Mytilidae, typically burrowing into rock or other hard substrates.  It's a type of mussel that bores into rocks.|例句：Lithodomus can cause significant damage to coastal structures. 石蛏会对沿海建筑造成重大损害。

例句：The Lithodomus secretes an acid that dissolves the rock. 石蛏分泌一种酸来溶解岩石。|近义词：date mussel, rock-boring mussel|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps free-swimming mussel)|词性:noun
