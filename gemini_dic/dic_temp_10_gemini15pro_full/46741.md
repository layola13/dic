dandling:/ˈdændlɪŋ/
Verb|摇晃，轻摇；逗弄|Moving something gently back and forth, often a baby.|She was dandling the baby on her knee. 她正把婴儿放在膝盖上摇晃。|Synonyms: swinging, rocking, swaying|Antonyms: stilling, holding steady|v.

dandling:/ˈdændlɪŋ/
Adjective|(尤指婴儿)被摇晃的，被逗弄的|Being moved gently back and forth.|The dandling motion soothed the crying baby. 摇晃的动作使哭闹的婴儿平静下来。|Synonyms: swaying, rocking|Antonyms: still, motionless|adj.
