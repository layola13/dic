obduracy:/ˈɒbdjʊrəsi/ (US) /ˈɑːbdʒərəsi/ (UK)| n. |顽固，执拗|The obduracy of the president prevented any compromise. |例句：His obduracy in the face of overwhelming evidence was astonishing.  面对压倒性的证据，他的顽固令人震惊。

obdurate:/ˈɒbdjʊrət/ (US) /ˈɒbdʒərət/ (UK)| adj. |顽固的，执拗的|He remained obdurate, refusing to listen to our pleas. |例句：The obdurate old man refused to change his mind.  固执的老人拒绝改变主意。
