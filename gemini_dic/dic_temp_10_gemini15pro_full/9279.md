antiphilosophical:/ˌæntiˌfɪləˈsɒfɪkəl/ (US) /ˌæntiˌfɪləˈzɒfɪkl/ (UK)| adj.|反哲学的，反对哲学的|Opposed to or rejecting philosophy or philosophical principles.  Characterized by a distrust of or aversion to reasoned inquiry or abstract thought.|His antiphilosophical stance prevented him from engaging in any serious intellectual discussion. 他反哲学的立场使他无法进行任何严肃的智力讨论。|近义词：unphilosophical, irrational, dogmatic|反义词：philosophical, rational, open-minded|词性:形容词
