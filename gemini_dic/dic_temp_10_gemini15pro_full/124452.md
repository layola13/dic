motorway:/ˈməʊtəweɪ/ (UK) /ˈmoʊtərˌweɪ/ (US)| n. |高速公路|A major road designed for fast traffic, typically with multiple lanes and limited access points. |例句：We took the motorway to London. 我们走高速公路去伦敦。

例句：The motorway was closed due to an accident.  由于事故，高速公路关闭了。|近义词：freeway, expressway, highway, turnpike (US)|反义词: side road, back road |词性:noun
