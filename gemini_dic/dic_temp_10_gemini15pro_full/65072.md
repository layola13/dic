eternize:/ɪˈtɜːrnɪzaɪz/ /ɪˈtɝːnɪzaɪz/
verb
使永恒，使不朽，使永垂不朽
to make something last forever; to immortalize
The poet's work was eternized by future generations. 这位诗人的作品被后世所铭记。
Their love was eternized in the monument. 他们的爱情在这个纪念碑中得到永存。
近义词:immortalize, perpetuate, memorialize
反义词:forget, obliterate
词性:v.
