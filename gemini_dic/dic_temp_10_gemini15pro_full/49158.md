dejeuner:/deʒøne/ /dɪˈʒɜːneɪ/
noun
午餐;早餐
Midday meal, lunch, sometimes also breakfast.
例句：We had a delightful déjeuner at a charming bistro. 我们在一家迷人的小酒馆享用了愉快的午餐。

例句：They invited us for a déjeuner sur l'herbe. 他们邀请我们去草地上野餐。

近义词：lunch, midday meal, breakfast (sometimes)
反义词：dinner, supper
词性：noun

词源:
From French déjeuner, from Old French desjeuner, from des- (prefix indicating reversal or cessation) + jeuner (to fast).  Originally meant "to break one's fast," hence "breakfast."  The meaning shifted to "lunch" in modern French.
