overtediousness: /ˌoʊvərˈtiːdiəsnəs/ | n. | 过于冗长，过于乏味 | The overtediousness of the lecture caused many students to fall asleep.  这冗长乏味的讲座让许多学生昏昏欲睡。 |  Excessive length or dullness; the quality of being too tedious.  过长或沉闷；过于乏味的性质。| The overtediousness of the three-hour meeting left everyone drained.  三个小时冗长乏味的会议让每个人都筋疲力尽。 | 近义词: tedium, boredom, dullness, monotony | 反义词: excitement, interest, stimulation | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From "over" (excessive) + "tedious" (tiresome, dull) + "-ness" (forming nouns of quality or state). 源于“over”（过度的）+“tedious”（乏味的，沉闷的）+“-ness”（构成性质或状态的名词）。
