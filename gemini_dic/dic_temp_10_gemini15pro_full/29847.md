causationist:/kɔːˈzeɪʃənɪst/
n.
因果论者，原因论者
Someone who believes in causationism, the philosophical doctrine that everything has a cause.
The causationist argued that every event is predetermined.  因果论者认为每个事件都是预先决定的。
The judge, a strict causationist, wanted to know the root cause of the crime.  法官，一个严格的因果论者，想要知道犯罪的根源。
近义词:determinist
反义词:indeterminist
词性:noun
