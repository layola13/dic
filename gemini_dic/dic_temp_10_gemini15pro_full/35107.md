clotting:/ˈklɒtɪŋ/ (US) /ˈklɑːtɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|凝血，凝结|The process of forming a clot (a semi-solid mass of blood). This is a vital process to prevent excessive bleeding after an injury.|The doctor explained that the medication could affect blood clotting. 医生解释说这种药可能会影响血液凝固。|近义词：coagulation, thickening|反义词：thinning, dissolution|词性:noun

clotting:/ˈklɒtɪŋ/ (US) /ˈklɑːtɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|凝血的|Relating to or causing clotting.|Clotting factors are essential for stopping bleeding. 凝血因子对于止血至关重要。|近义词：coagulant, coagulative|反义词：anticoagulant|词性:adjective
