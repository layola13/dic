medium:/ˈmiːdiəm/ (US), /ˈmiːdɪəm/ (UK)| n. adj.|媒介，方法；中等|Something that acts as a means or agency: "using the latest technology as a medium for communication".  A middle state or condition: "a happy medium".|例句：Television is a powerful medium for advertising.  电视是一个强大的广告媒介。|近义词：means, channel, agency, method, average, intermediate|反义词：extreme|词性：名词、形容词

mediums:/ˈmiːdiəmz/ (US), /ˈmiːdɪəmz/ (UK)| n.|媒介，灵媒|Plural form of "medium". Refers to multiple means, agencies, or people claiming to communicate with spirits.|例句：She consulted several mediums in an attempt to contact her deceased husband. 她咨询了几位灵媒，试图联系她已故的丈夫。|近义词：channels, agencies, psychics, clairvoyants|反义词：-|词性：名词
