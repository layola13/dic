nonprolixnesses:/ˌnɒnproʊˈlɪksnəsɪz/
n.
简洁, 简明扼要
the quality or state of being concise and not wordy.
His nonprolixness was appreciated in the meeting, as everyone was short on time.  他的简洁在会议上受到赞赏，因为每个人都时间紧迫。
The editor praised the nonprolixnesses of the author's writing style. 编辑称赞了作者简洁的写作风格。
近义词: conciseness, brevity, succinctness
反义词: prolixity, verbosity, wordiness
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + prolixity (wordiness).  Prolixity comes from Latin prolixus "extended," from pro- "forth" + liquere "to be fluid."
