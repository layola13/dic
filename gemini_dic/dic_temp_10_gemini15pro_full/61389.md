emblem:/ˈembləm/| n.|象征; 徽章; 符号; |A badge or other object used as a symbol of a group, nation, or organization. |The eagle is the national emblem of the United States.  鹰是美国的国家象征。

The dove is an emblem of peace. 鸽子是和平的象征。|近义词：symbol, badge, logo, insignia, crest|反义词：N/A|词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *emblome*, from Late Latin *emblema* "inlaid work, relief, mosaic," from Greek *émblēma* "something inserted," from *emballein* "to throw in, insert," from *en- "in" + *ballein* "to throw."
