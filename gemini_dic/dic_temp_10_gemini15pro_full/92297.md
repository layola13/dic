hypidiomorphic:/ˌhaɪpɪdiəˈmɔːrfɪk/ | adj. | 半自形 | Partially or imperfectly euhedral; between euhedral and anhedral. Describes a mineral grain that only partially shows its characteristic crystal form because its growth was impeded by the surrounding minerals. | The quartz crystals in this rock are hypidiomorphic.  这块岩石中的石英晶体是半自形的。 |  subhedral, subidiomorphic | anhedral, allotriomorphic | adjective
