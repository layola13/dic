hydrostatic:/ˌhaɪdrəˈstætɪk/ |adj.| 静水力的；流体静力学的 |Relating to the pressure of liquids at rest or the study of such pressure.  The hydrostatic pressure at the bottom of the dam is immense.  大坝底部的静水压力是巨大的。|Synonyms: hydraulic, fluid-static |Antonyms: hydrodynamic, aerostatic |adjective


hydrostatics:/ˌhaɪdrəˈstætɪks/ |n.| 静水力学 |The branch of physics that deals with the pressure of liquids at rest. Hydrostatics is a crucial subject for civil engineers designing dams. 静水力学是土木工程师设计大坝的关键学科。|Synonyms: fluid statics |Antonyms: hydrodynamics, aerodynamics |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From hydro- (water) + -static (relating to equilibrium).  Ultimately from Greek 'hydōr' (water) and 'statikos' (causing to stand).
