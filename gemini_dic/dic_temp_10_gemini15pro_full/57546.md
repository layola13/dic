draintrap: /ˈdreɪnˌtræp/ | n. | 排水管存水弯; 存水弯; |A draintrap is a device shaped like a U or S, found under sinks and other plumbing fixtures, that holds a small amount of water to prevent sewer gases from entering the building. |  The draintrap under the sink is clogged. 水槽下的存水弯堵塞了。| synonym: trap, p-trap | antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps an open drain pipe) | noun

draintrap: /ˈdreɪnˌtræp/ | n. | 集油池，油水分离器 |A draintrap is a device designed to intercept and retain fats, oils, and grease (FOG) from wastewater before it enters the sewer system.|The restaurant's draintrap needs to be cleaned regularly to prevent blockages.  这家餐馆的集油池需要定期清理以防止堵塞。|synonym: grease trap, interceptor | antonym: (No direct antonym) | noun
