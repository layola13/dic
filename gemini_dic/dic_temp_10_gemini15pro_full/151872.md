passive-aggressive:/ˈpæsɪv əˈɡrɛsɪv/| adj.|消极攻击的，被动攻击型的|Showing aggression toward others indirectly through stubbornness, sullenness, procrastination, or deliberate inefficiency.|He gave her the passive-aggressive treatment, refusing to answer her calls.|近义词：indirectly aggressive, obstructive, uncooperative, resentful|反义词：assertive, cooperative, direct|词性:形容词

passive-aggressive:/ˈpæsɪv əˈɡrɛsɪv/| n.|消极攻击的人|A person who expresses aggression toward others indirectly through stubbornness, sullenness, procrastination, or deliberate inefficiency.|She's such a passive-aggressive!  She never says what she really means.|近义词：obstructionist|反义词：communicator|词性:名词
