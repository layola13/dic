decimetre:/ˈdɛsɪmiːtər/ (US) /ˈdɛsɪˌmiːtər/ (UK)| n. |分米|A unit of length in the metric system, equal to one tenth of a metre (10 centimetres).|The table is 10 decimetres long.  桌子长10分米。|Synonym: 10 centimeters|Antonym: dekameter|noun

decimetres:/ˈdɛsɪmiːtərz/ (US) /ˈdɛsɪˌmiːtərz/ (UK)| n. |分米（复数）|Plural form of decimetre.|The bookcase has shelves spaced at intervals of 30 decimetres.  书架的隔板间距为30分米。|Synonym: 30 centimeters|Antonym: dekameters|noun
