garrison: /ˈɡærɪsən/ (US) /ˈɡærɪzn/ (UK)| n. vt.|驻军，守备部队；卫戍区|A body of troops stationed in a fortified place.  A military post, especially one that is permanently established.|The general inspected the garrison. 将军视察了驻军。|The army garrisoned the town. 军队驻扎在这个城镇。|近义词：troops, soldiers, detachment, outpost|反义词: civilian population|noun, verb
