medium-term:/ˈmiːdiəm tɜːm/ (UK) /ˈmiːdiəm tɝːm/ (US)| adj. n.|中期，中期的|Relating to a period of time that is not short but not very long, usually between two and five years.  A medium-term goal is one that you plan to achieve in a few years, not immediately or over a very long period.  As a noun, it refers to this period of time itself.|例句：The company's medium-term strategy focuses on expanding into new markets. 该公司的中期战略重点是拓展新市场。

例句：They are looking for medium-term financing for the project. 他们正在为该项目寻找中期融资。|近义词：intermediate-term, near-term (sometimes)|反义词:long-term, short-term|词性:形容词, 名词
