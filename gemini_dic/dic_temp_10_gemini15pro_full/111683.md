locked-up:/ˌlɒkt ˈʌp/ (UK), /ˌlɑːkt ˈʌp/ (US)| adj.|被锁住的；被监禁的；被关闭的；被抑制的|Confined, especially in prison; also, unavailable for use or inaccessible.|The stolen goods were found in a locked-up warehouse. 被盗的货物在一个锁着的仓库里被找到了。|The family’s emotions were locked up after the tragedy.  这家人在悲剧发生后压抑着情感。|近义词：imprisoned, confined, secured, closed, unavailable|反义词：free, open, released, available|词性:形容词

locked-up:/ˌlɒkt ˈʌp/ (UK), /ˌlɑːkt ˈʌp/ (US)| n.|监狱；精神病院|(informal) A prison or psychiatric hospital.|He spent ten years in the locked-up. 他在监狱里待了十年。|近义词：jail, prison, asylum|反义词：freedom|词性:名词
