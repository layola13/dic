discarded:/dɪˈskɑːrdɪd/
adjective
丢弃的，废弃的
thrown away or rejected
The discarded tires were piled up behind the garage. 废弃的轮胎堆在车库后面。
近义词:abandoned, rejected, dumped, jettisoned, scrapped
反义词:kept, saved, retained, cherished

discarded:/dɪˈskɑːrd/
verb
丢弃，抛弃
to throw something away or get rid of it because it is no longer wanted or needed
He discarded his old clothes. 他丢弃了他的旧衣服。
近义词:reject, abandon, dump, jettison, scrap
反义词:keep, save, retain, cherish


ETYMOLOGY->
From the late 16th century.  From the verb discard, which comes from the French  *descarter* meaning "to discard a card," from *des-* (meaning "from, away") + *carte* (meaning "card").  The original meaning related to card games, and later expanded to mean discarding anything unwanted.
