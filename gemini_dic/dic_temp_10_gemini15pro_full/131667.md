nonconcentrically:/ˌnɒnkənˈsentrɪkli/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈsentrɪkli/
adjective
非同心地
not having the same center
The two circles were drawn nonconcentrically. 这两个圆画得不同心。

The pipes were fitted nonconcentrically, causing leakage. 这些管道安装得不同心，导致漏水。

近义词: eccentrically, off-center
反义词: concentrically

词性: adjective
