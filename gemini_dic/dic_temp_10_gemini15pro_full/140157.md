n.
冒犯；得罪；生气
The state of being offended.
His offendednesses at her remark was obvious. 他对她的言论感到生气，显而易见。
近义词：resentment, umbrage, indignation, pique
反义词：pleasure, delight, satisfaction
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

词源: From offended (past participle of offend) + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality).  Ultimately from Latin *offendere, frequentative of offendere "to strike against, hurt, offend."
