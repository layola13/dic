nonsalutarilyness:/nɒnsæljuˈtɛrɪlɪnɪs/ | n. | 非有益性；无益性 |  The state or quality of being nonsalutary; lack of benefit or harmfulness.  This word is not commonly used and might be considered nonstandard. It's constructed by adding multiple suffixes (-ary, -ly, -ness) to the base word "salutary," which means beneficial or wholesome. |  The nonsalutarilyness of his actions ultimately led to his downfall. 他的行为的无益性最终导致了他的垮台。|  The report highlighted the nonsalutarilyness of the proposed policy. 报告强调了拟议政策的无益性。 | Near synonyms: harmfulness, detriment, disadvantage | Antonyms:  salutariness, benefit, advantage | Noun
