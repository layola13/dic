discretions:/dɪˈskreʃənz/
n.|谨慎，慎重；自行决定的自由；判断力|The matter is left to your discretion. 这件事留给你自行处理。|近义词:prudence, judgment, caution, wisdom|反义词:indiscretion, imprudence|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Old French *discrecion* (modern French *discrétion*)  and directly from Latin *discrētiō* "separation, distinction, discernment," from *discrētus*, past participle of *discernere* "to separate, distinguish," from *dis-* "apart" + *cernere* "to sift, distinguish."
