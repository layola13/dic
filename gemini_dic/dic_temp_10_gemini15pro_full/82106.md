grog:/ɡrɒɡ/ (UK) /ɡrɑːɡ/ (US)| n.|格洛格酒；烈酒兑水|An alcoholic drink made of rum diluted with water, and often flavored with lemon or lime juice.|Sailors were given a daily ration of grog. 水手们每天都配给格洛格酒。|近义词：rum, liquor, alcoholic beverage|反义词:water, juice|noun


groggy:/ˈɡrɒɡi/ (UK) /ˈɡrɑːɡi/ (US)| adj.|醉醺醺的；走路不稳的；昏昏沉沉的|Dazed, weak, or unsteady, especially from illness, intoxication, or lack of sleep.|I felt groggy after the long flight. 长途飞行后我感到昏昏沉沉的。|近义词：dizzy, unsteady, faint|反义词:alert, awake, clear-headed|adjective
