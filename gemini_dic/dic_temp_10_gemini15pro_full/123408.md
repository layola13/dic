monophthongs:/ˈmɒnəfθɒŋz/ (UK), /ˈmɑːnəfθɑːŋz/ (US)| n. |单元音|A vowel sound that is spoken with one mouth position, as opposed to a diphthong, where the mouth position changes.|The word "cat" has the monophthong /æ/.  "猫"这个单词包含单元音/æ/。|Example: The vowel sound in "feet" /iː/ is a monophthong. 例如，“feet”中的元音/iː/是一个单元音。|Synonyms: pure vowel|Antonyms: diphthong|noun
