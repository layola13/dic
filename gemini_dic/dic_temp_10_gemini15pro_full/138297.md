notcher:/ˈnɒtʃər/ (US) /ˈnɒtʃə(r)/ (UK)| n.|刻痕器；切口工具；计数器|A tool or device used for making notches or cuts, especially for keeping a tally or record.  It can also refer to a person who makes notches.|The carpenter used a notcher to create a decorative edge on the wood. 木匠用刻痕器在木头上刻出装饰性的边缘。|Synonyms: cutter, scorer, marker, counter|Antonyms: smoother, filler|noun
