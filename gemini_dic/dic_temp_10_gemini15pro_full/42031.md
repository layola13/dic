costliness:/ˈkɔːstlɪnɪs/
n.
昂贵，高价
The costliness of the project caused concern among investors.
这个项目的昂贵令投资者担忧。
High cost; expensiveness.
The costliness of the antique furniture made it a risky investment.  这件古董家具价格昂贵，使其成为一项风险投资。
近义词:expense, expensiveness, dearness, high cost
反义词:cheapness, affordability, inexpensiveness
