conditionally:/kənˈdɪʃənəli/| adv.|有条件地；附带条件地|If something is done conditionally, it is done only if something else happens or is done. |例句：The offer was made conditionally upon her finishing college.  这个录取通知是有条件的，前提是她完成大学学业。

例句：He agreed to help, conditionally. 他同意帮忙，但有条件。|近义词：provisionally, dependently, subject to|反义词：unconditionally, absolutely|词性:副词
