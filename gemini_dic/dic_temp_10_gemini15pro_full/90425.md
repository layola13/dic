如果你是想了解单词 "what" 的用法，我可以提供以下信息：

what: /wɒt/ (British) /wʌt/ (American) | pronoun, determiner, adverb | 什么，多么 | used to ask for information about something | 例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？ | 近义词：which, how | 反义词:  (What 没有直接的反义词) | 词性: pronoun, determiner, adverb


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? 你觉得怎么样？
What does she do for a living? 她做什么工作？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? 你在看什么？
What is he doing here? 他在这里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you done with my keys? 你把我的钥匙放哪儿了？
What has she been up to lately? 她最近在忙什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on? 你一直在忙什么？
What has she been reading lately? 她最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say? 你说什么？
What happened last night? 昨晚发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about? 她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had she eaten for dinner? 她晚饭吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I called? 在我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about? 他们一直在争论什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do this weekend?  你这周末要做什么？
What will happen next? 接下来会发生什么？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time tomorrow? 明天这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting? 他们会在会议上讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by Friday? 到星期五你会完成什么？
What will they have accomplished by the end of the year? 到年底他们会完成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2030? 到2030年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业的时候，她将已经学习了什么多久？
