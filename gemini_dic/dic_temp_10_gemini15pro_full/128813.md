nightliest:/ˈnaɪtliɪst/ | adj. | 最像夜晚的；最黑暗的 |  Superlative form of "nightly," meaning most resembling or characteristic of night; darkest. | The nightliest hour is just before dawn.  最黑暗的时刻就在黎明之前。| The forest grew nightliest as the moon disappeared behind the clouds.  当月亮消失在云层后面时，森林变得更加黑暗。| 近义词: darkest, murkiest, gloomiest | 反义词: brightest, lightest, daylit | 词性: 形容词

词源: From "night" + "-ly" (adverb/adjective suffix) + "-est" (superlative suffix).  Ultimately from Old English "niht."
