archaisms: /ˈɑːrkeɪɪzəmz/
| n. | 古语；古体 | words and expressions that are no longer in common use but are sometimes used to give a sense of old-fashioned style |  The author used archaisms like "thee" and "thou" to create a historical atmosphere. 作者使用像“thee”和“thou”这样的古语来营造历史氛围。 | synonyms: antiquated language, obsolete words, old-fashioned terms | antonyms: modern language, contemporary terms, current usage | noun
