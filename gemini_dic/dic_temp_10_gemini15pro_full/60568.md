elasticities: /ɪˌlæstɪˈsɪtiz/ | n. | 弹性（复数） | The responsiveness of one variable to changes in another.  It is often expressed as a percentage change in one variable divided by the percentage change in another variable. | The price elasticities of demand for luxury goods are generally higher than those for necessities. 奢侈品的需求价格弹性通常高于必需品。| Synonyms: flexibilities, responsiveness, sensitivities | Antonyms: rigidities, inflexibilities | Noun
