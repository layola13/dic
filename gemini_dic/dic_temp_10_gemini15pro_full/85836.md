HBr:/ˌeɪtʃ biː ˈɑːr/ | n. | 溴化氢;氢溴酸 | Hydrogen bromide is a diatomic molecule with the formula HBr. It is a colorless gas that dissolves in water, forming hydrobromic acid. | Hydrogen bromide is highly corrosive. 溴化氢具有强腐蚀性。|  Hydrogen bromide is used in the production of inorganic bromides. 溴化氢用于生产无机溴化物。 | synonyms:  hydrobromic acid (when dissolved in water) | antonyms: None (as a chemical compound) | noun
