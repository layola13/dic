bonassus:/ˈbəʊnəsəs/ | n. | 欧洲野牛 | A large wild ox of Europe, also called the wisent or European bison. It is the largest land mammal in Europe. |  例句：The bonassus is a protected species in many European countries. 欧洲野牛在许多欧洲国家都是受保护的物种。

例句： Efforts are underway to reintroduce the bonassus to its former habitat. 人们正在努力将欧洲野牛重新引入其以前的栖息地。 | 近义词：wisent, European bison | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps domesticated cattle) | 词性: noun
