misnavigated: /ˌmɪsnævɪˈɡeɪtɪd/
verb
错误导航，导航错误
To navigate incorrectly; to take a wrong course or route.
The ship misnavigated and ended up in the wrong port. 这艘船导航错误，最终到达了错误的港口。
The driver misnavigated the city's one-way streets. 司机在城市单行道上导航错误。
近义词: lost, strayed, went astray
反义词: found the way, navigated correctly
词性: verb
