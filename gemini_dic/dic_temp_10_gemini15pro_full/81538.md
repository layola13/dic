gravity: /ˈɡrævɪti/ (US), /ˈɡrævəti/ (UK) | n. | 重力；地心引力；严重性；庄重 | The force that attracts a body towards the centre of the earth, or towards any other physical body having mass.  A serious situation. |例句：The apple fell to the ground due to gravity.  苹果由于重力落到地上。

|例句：The gravity of the situation was apparent to everyone.  情况的严重性对每个人来说都是显而易见的。 |近义词：gravitation, seriousness, weight, importance |反义词：levity, frivolity |词性: noun
