histologists:/hɪˈstɑːlədʒɪsts/
n.
组织学家
A specialist in the study of tissues.
Histologists examine tissue samples under a microscope to diagnose diseases. 组织学家在显微镜下检查组织样本以诊断疾病。
synonyms: tissue specialists,  microscopic anatomists
antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider broader fields like "general practitioners")
noun
