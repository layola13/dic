leaderboard:/ˈliːdərbɔːrd/| n. |排行榜，积分榜|A board displaying the names and current rankings of leading competitors, as in a tournament or contest. |例句：He checked the leaderboard to see his ranking. 他查看了排行榜以确认自己的排名。

例句：The leaderboard shows the top ten players in the game. 排行榜显示了游戏中前十名的玩家。


近义词：ranking, scoreboard, standings|反义词: |词性:noun
