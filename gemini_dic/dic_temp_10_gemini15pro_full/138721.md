numbedness:/ˈnʌmnəsnəs/| n.|麻木，麻痹|A state of reduced sensation or feeling, often caused by cold, pressure, or injury.|The numbness in my fingers from the cold was alarming. 我的手指冻得麻木了，这很令人担忧。|After the accident, he experienced a period of emotional numbness. 事故发生后，他经历了一段情感麻木的时期。|近义词：insensibility, deadness, apathy|反义词：sensitivity, feeling, responsiveness|词性: 名词

词源: From numb + -ness.  "Numb" comes from Old English *numen, past participle of *niman "to take," related to German nehmen "to take."  The original sense was "taken away," hence "benumbed."
