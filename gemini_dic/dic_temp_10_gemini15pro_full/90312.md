housewife:/ˈhaʊsˌwaɪf/ (US) /ˈhaʊswɪf/ (UK)| n.|家庭主妇|A woman whose main occupation is caring for her family and home.|She was a typical 1950s housewife. 她是一位典型的20世纪50年代的家庭主妇。|Example:  After her children grew up, the housewife took a part-time job. 孩子们长大后，这位家庭主妇找了一份兼职工作。|Synonym: homemaker, stay-at-home mom|Antonym: career woman|noun
