interfluency:/ˌɪntərˈfluːənsi/
n.
不流利，语言不流利
The lack of fluency or smooth flow in speech, often characterized by hesitations, repetitions, and self-corrections.
His interfluency made it difficult to understand him. 他的不流利使他难以被理解。
近义词：disfluency, hesitancy, non-fluency
反义词：fluency, eloquence
词性: 名词
