drip-dries:/ˈdrɪp draɪz/
drip-dries:v.|滴干，晾干| (of a garment) dry by dripping, without being wrung out or needing to be ironed. |This shirt drip-dries, so you don't need to iron it. 这件衬衫滴干即可，你不需要熨烫它。|Synonym: air-dry |Antonym: tumble-dry
drip-dries:n.|滴干的衣服| clothes that drip dry | I packed mostly drip-dries for the camping trip. 我为露营旅行打包了大部分滴干的衣服。|Synonym: non-iron clothes |Antonym: clothes requiring ironing
drip-dries:adj.|滴干的，免熨的|able to be dried by dripping, without the need for wringing or ironing. |This fabric is drip-dry, which is very convenient. 这种织物是滴干免熨的，非常方便。|Synonym: non-iron |Antonym: requiring ironing
