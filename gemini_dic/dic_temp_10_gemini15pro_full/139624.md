occiduous:/ɒkˈsɪdʒuəs/| adj.|落叶的，脱落的|Shedding leaves annually; not evergreen.  Falling off at maturity. |例句：Oak trees are occiduous, losing their leaves each fall. 橡树是落叶树，每年秋天都会落叶。

例句：The occiduous teeth of a child are eventually replaced by permanent teeth.  孩子的乳牙最终会被恒牙取代。|近义词：deciduous, shedding|反义词:evergreen, persistent|词性:adjective
