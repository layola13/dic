deprecation:/ˌdeprəˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 贬低，反对，弃用 |  The act of expressing disapproval of something; the act of withdrawing official support for something.  | The library marked the deprecation of the old version of the software. 图书馆标记了旧版软件的弃用。|  The teacher's deprecation of the student's work discouraged him. 老师对学生作品的贬低使他灰心丧气。| disapproval, belittling, criticizing | approval, praise | noun
