buttering:/ˈbʌtərɪŋ/
n.|涂黄油；奉承；拍马屁|The act of spreading butter on something. Also, figuratively, the act of flattering someone excessively.|He was buttering his toast. 他正在给他的吐司抹黄油。|近义词：flattering, fawning, adulating|反义词：criticizing, condemning|词性:名词/动词的现在分词

buttering:/ˈbʌtərɪŋ/
v.|涂黄油；奉承；拍马屁|To spread butter on something. Also, figuratively, to flatter someone excessively.|He was buttering up the boss hoping for a promotion. 他正在拍老板的马屁，希望能升职。|近义词：flattering, fawning, adulating|反义词：criticizing, condemning|词性:动词

词源: From butter + -ing.  Ultimately from Latin *butyrum, from Greek βούτυρον (bouturon), "butter."
