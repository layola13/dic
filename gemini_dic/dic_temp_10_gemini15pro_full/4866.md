aliennesses:/ˈeɪliənnɪsɪz/
n.
异质性；陌生感；外来性
The state or quality of being alien; foreignness; strangeness.
The aliennesses of the new environment made her feel uneasy.  新环境的陌生感让她感到不安。
Near Synonyms: foreignness, strangeness, otherness, unfamiliarity, exoticism
Antonyms: familiarity, closeness, kinship, sameness
