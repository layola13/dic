echinoid:/ˈɛkɪnɔɪd/ | n. |海胆；海胆纲动物|A sea urchin; a member of the class Echinoidea, characterized by a spherical or flattened body enclosed in a hard shell covered with movable spines.|例句：The echinoid’s spines protect it from predators. 海胆的刺保护它免受捕食者的侵害。

例句：We found several different types of echinoids during our dive. 在我们潜水期间，我们发现了几种不同类型的海胆。|近义词：sea urchin|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ekhinos* ("hedgehog, sea urchin") + *-oeidēs* ("resembling, having the form of")
