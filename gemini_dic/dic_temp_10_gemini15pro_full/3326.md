affordably:/əˈfɔːrdəbli/
| adv. | 经济实惠地；能够负担得起地 | In a way that is not expensive |  We ate affordably at the local cafe. 我们在当地咖啡馆吃得很实惠。| cheaply, reasonably, economically | expensively, extravagantly
| adverb

词源:
From afford + -ably.  "Afford" comes from Old English *geforðian "to promote, perform, execute, accomplish," from forð "forth."  The suffix "-ably" indicates "in a manner that can be."
