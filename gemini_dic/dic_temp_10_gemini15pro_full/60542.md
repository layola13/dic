elanet:/ˈelənɛt/ | n. | 弹性网 |  A regularized regression method that linearly combines the L1 and L2 penalties of the lasso and ridge methods.  |  The researchers used an elanet model to analyze the high-dimensional data. 研究人员使用弹性网模型来分析高维数据。 | synonyms: elastic net regularization | antonyms:  N/A | noun

elanet:/ˈelənɛt/ | n. | 弹性网络 | A type of network with flexible and adaptable properties. |  The elanet framework allows for dynamic scaling and resource allocation. 弹性网络框架允许动态扩展和资源分配。| synonyms: flexible network, adaptable network | antonyms: rigid network | noun
