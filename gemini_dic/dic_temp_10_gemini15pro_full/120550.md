miraculously:/mɪˈrækjələsli/
adverb
奇迹般地;神奇地
In a way that suggests a miracle.
He miraculously survived the crash. 他奇迹般地在坠机事故中幸存下来。
The child miraculously recovered from a serious illness.  这个孩子奇迹般地从一场重病中康复了。
近义词:amazingly, incredibly, astonishingly, wonderfully, supernaturally
反义词:ordinarily, normally, expectedly
词性:adverb

词源:
From Late Latin *miraculosus, from Latin miraculum (“miracle”) + -osus.
