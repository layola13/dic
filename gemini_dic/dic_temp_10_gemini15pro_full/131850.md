nonconstraints:/nɒn.kən.streɪnts/ | n. | 非约束，无约束 | Absence of constraints or limitations.  Things that do not restrict or limit. | The algorithm operates efficiently under nonconstraints, exploring a vast solution space.  这个算法在无约束条件下高效运行，探索巨大的解空间。 | synonyms: freedoms, liberties | antonyms: constraints, restrictions | noun

nonconstraint:/nɒn.kən.streɪnt/ | adj. | 无约束的，非约束性的 | Not subject to constraints or limitations. |  A nonconstraint optimization problem allows variables to take on any value. 一个无约束优化问题允许变量取任何值。| synonyms: unrestricted, unconstrained, unbound | antonyms: constrained, restricted, bound | adjective
