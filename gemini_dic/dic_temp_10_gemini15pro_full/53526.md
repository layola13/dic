disattention:/ˌdɪsəˈtɛnʃən/| n.| невнимание, рассеянность (Russian), 不注意, 心不在焉|Lack of attention; the state of being inattentive. |His disattention during the meeting led to him missing crucial information. 他在会议期间的心不在焉导致他错过了关键信息。| The teacher reprimanded the student for his disattention in class. 老师批评了这个学生上课不专心。|近义词：inattention, heedlessness, abstraction, negligence|反义词：attention, attentiveness, concentration, focus|词性:noun

词源:  From dis- "not, opposite of" + attention.  The prefix "dis-" negates the meaning of "attention."
