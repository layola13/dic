alleviating:/əˈliːvieɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
减轻，缓和
Making something less severe
Taking aspirin is effective in alleviating headaches. 服用阿司匹林可以有效缓解头痛。
synonyms: reducing, easing, mitigating, lessening
antonyms: aggravating, worsening, intensifying
verb
