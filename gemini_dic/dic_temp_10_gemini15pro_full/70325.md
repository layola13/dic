field-tests:/ˈfiːld tɛsts/
n.|现场测试，实地试验|The software underwent rigorous field-tests before its release.  该软件在发布前经过了严格的现场测试。|
field-test:/ˈfiːld tɛst/
v.|进行现场测试，实地试验|They field-tested the new car in extreme weather conditions. 他们在极端天气条件下对新车进行了现场测试。|
Synonyms: field trials, practical tests, real-world tests
Antonyms: lab tests, theoretical tests, simulations
Etymology:  'field' (Old English 'feld' - open country) + 'test' (Old French 'test' - earthen pot used in assaying metals) -  originally referring to testing something in its intended environment, as opposed to a laboratory.
