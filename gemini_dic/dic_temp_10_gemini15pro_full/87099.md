herald: /ˈherəld/ (US) /ˈhɛrəld/ (UK)| n. vt.| 预兆，先驱，传令官；预示，预告 |A person or thing viewed as a sign that something is about to happen.  To be a sign that (something) is about to happen.|The robin is a herald of spring. 知更鸟是春天的预兆。 | synonyms: precursor, forerunner, harbinger, messenger | antonyms: follower, successor | noun

herald: /ˈherəld/ (US) /ˈhɛrəld/ (UK)| vt.| 预示，预告| To make known the approach of; to usher in.| The warm weather heralded the start of spring.  温暖的天气预示着春天的开始。 | synonyms: announce, proclaim, portend, foreshadow | antonyms: conceal, hide | verb
