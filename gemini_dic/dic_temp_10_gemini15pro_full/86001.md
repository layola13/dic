headstrongnesses:/ˈhɛdstrɒŋnɪsɪz/ /ˈhɛdstrɔŋnɪsɪz/
n.
固执，倔强，顽固
The quality of being determined to do what you want, even if other people advise you not to.
Her headstrongnesses led her to make some poor decisions in her youth. 她年轻时的固执导致她做出了一些糟糕的决定。
obstinacy, stubbornness, willfulness
compliance, obedience, docility
noun
