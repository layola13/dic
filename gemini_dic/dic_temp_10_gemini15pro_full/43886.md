crests:/krɛsts/| n.|顶峰，浪尖，山脊，羽冠|A high point of a mountain or hill; the top of something, especially a wave; a plume of feathers on a bird's head.|The climbers finally reached the crest of the mountain. 攀登者最终到达了山顶。|Synonyms: peak, summit, top, apex, crown, ridge|Antonyms: trough, valley, bottom, base|noun

crests:/krɛsts/| v.|到达顶峰，到达顶点|To reach the highest point; to surge or swell to a peak.|The wave crested and then crashed onto the shore. 海浪到达顶峰，然后拍打在岸边。|Synonyms: peak, climax, culminate|Antonyms: decline, decrease, ebb|verb
