disconcertingly:/ˌdɪskənˈsɜːrtɪŋli/ | adv. | 令人不安地，令人 disconcertingly 尴尬地 |  In a way that makes someone feel slightly confused, embarrassed, or worried. |  He smiled disconcertingly. 他令人不安地笑了笑。 |  The silence was disconcertingly long.  这沉默长得令人不安。| 近义词: disturbingly, unsettlingly, upsettingly | 反义词: reassuringly, calmingly | 词性: 副词

disconcert:/ˌdɪskənˈsɜːrt/ | v. | 使不安，使尴尬 | To make someone feel slightly confused, embarrassed, or worried. | The sudden question disconcerted her. 突如其来的问题让她感到不安。|  He was disconcerted by the news. 他被这个消息弄得心烦意乱。 | 近义词: disturb, unsettle, upset | 反义词: reassure, calm | 词性: 动词

词源: From French *desconcerter*, from des- (expressing reversal) + concerter (to arrange, agree).
