honeypot:/ˈhʌnipɒt/ (UK) /ˈhʌnipɑːt/ (US)| n.|蜜罐；诱捕器；圈套|A honeypot is a computer system or network site designed to attract and trap potential hackers or malicious actors, allowing security professionals to study their methods and gather intelligence.|The company set up a honeypot to lure and identify cybercriminals. 这家公司设置了一个蜜罐来引诱和识别网络犯罪分子。|synonyms: trap, lure, decoy|antonyms: shield, protection|noun

honeypot:/ˈhʌnipɒt/ (UK) /ˈhʌnipɑːt/ (US)| v.|用蜜饯保存|To preserve or cook something in honey.|She honeypotted the figs for a unique flavor. 她用蜂蜜保存无花果，以获得独特的风味。|synonyms: preserve, candy|antonyms: spoil, decay|verb
