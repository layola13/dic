imprevalency:/ɪmˈprɛvələnsɪ/
noun
罕见;不流行
The state or quality of being not prevalent; uncommonness; rarity.
The imprevalency of this disease makes it difficult to study.  这种疾病的罕见性使其难以研究。
近义词:rarity, uncommonness, infrequency, scarcity
反义词:prevalence, commonness, frequency, abundance
词性:n.

Etymology:
From in- (not) + prevalent (widespread) + -cy (noun suffix).  Prevalent ultimately derives from Latin praevalēre (to be more powerful, prevail).
