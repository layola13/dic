infuriated:/ɪnˈfjʊərieɪtɪd/| adj.|被激怒的，暴怒的|Filled with fury; extremely angry.|例句：The customer was infuriated by the poor service. 顾客对糟糕的服务感到愤怒。

例句：His constant interruptions infuriated me. 他不停地打断我，让我很生气。|近义词：enraged, furious, livid, outraged|反义词：calm, pleased, happy|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *infuriatus*, past participle of *infuriare* (“to enrage”), from *in-* (“in, into”) + *furia* (“fury, rage”).
