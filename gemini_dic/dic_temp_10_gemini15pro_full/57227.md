downgrader:/ˈdaʊnˌɡreɪdər/| n.|降级者，降级程序，降低…等级的人（或物）|Someone or something that downgrades something.  A person who reduces the quality, rank, or importance of something.|The software downgrader made the old version of the program compatible with the new system.  这个软件降级程序使旧版本的程序与新系统兼容。|近义词：degrader, reducer|反义词:upgrader, enhancer|词性:noun

downgrade:/ˈdaʊnˌɡreɪd/| vt.  n.|降级，降低，贬低|To reduce the quality, rank, or importance of something;  a reduction in quality, rank, or importance.|The airline downgraded my ticket from business class to economy. 航空公司将我的机票从商务舱降级为经济舱。|近义词：degrade, demote, reduce|反义词:upgrade, promote, enhance|词性:verb, noun
