outvaluing:/ˌaʊtˈvæljuɪŋ/
verb
超过…的价值; 比…更有价值
Surpassing or exceeding in value.
This new technology is outvaluing traditional methods.  这项新技术比传统方法更有价值。
The company's assets are outvaluing its liabilities. 该公司的资产价值超过了其负债。
近义词: exceeding, surpassing, outstripping, outweighing
反义词: undervaluing, devaluing
词性: v.
