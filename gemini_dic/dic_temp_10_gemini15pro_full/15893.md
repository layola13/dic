ballotade:/ˌbæləˈtɑːd/ /ˌbæləˈteɪd/ | n. | 跳跃，腾跃 (芭蕾舞) | A jumping step in ballet in which the dancer jumps into the air with one leg extended forward and the other bent backward, bringing the feet together in midair before landing. | The ballerina performed a graceful ballotade.  芭蕾舞女演员表演了一个优雅的跳跃。

The ballotade is a difficult step that requires strength and precision.  跳跃是一个需要力量和精确度的困难步骤。 | 近义词：jeté, leap, bound | 反义词: landing, descent | 词性: noun

Etymology: French, from Italian *ballata "dance," from ballare "to dance," from Late Latin ballare, of Germanic origin; akin to Old High German ballan "to dance."
