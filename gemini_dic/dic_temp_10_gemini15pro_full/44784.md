crunched:/krʌntʃt/| adj.|嘎吱作响的，被压碎的，紧缩的|Crushed or squeezed, often making a crunching sound; also used figuratively to describe a situation where resources are limited or time is short.|The car's fender was crunched in the accident. 汽车的挡泥板在事故中被压扁了。|近义词：crushed, compacted, compressed, strained|反义词:smooth, expanded, relaxed|adjective


crunched:/krʌntʃt/| v.|嘎吱作响地咀嚼，压碎，处理大量数据|Past tense and past participle of "crunch," meaning to crush or grind with the teeth or to process large amounts of data.|I crunched on a crisp apple. 我嘎吱嘎吱地嚼着一个脆苹果。|近义词：chew, grind, process, analyze|反义词: expand, smooth|verb
