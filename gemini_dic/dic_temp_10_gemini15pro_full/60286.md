egghead:/ˈeɡhed/| n.|书呆子，知识分子；过于重视理论的人|A person who is highly intellectual or academic, often seen as lacking practical skills or social graces. |He's a real egghead, always buried in his books. 他真是个书呆子，总是埋头读书。| My brother is such an egghead; he spends all his time studying. 我弟弟真是个书呆子，他所有的时间都花在学习上了。|近义词：intellectual, academic, highbrow, nerd, geek|反义词：dummy, ignoramus, simpleton|词性:noun
