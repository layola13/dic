embellisher:/ɪmˈbɛlɪʃər/| n.|装饰者，润色者|Someone or something that embellishes something.|The writer used a lot of  embellishers to make the story more interesting.  作者用了很多润色手法使故事更有趣。|Synonyms: decorator, adornist, ornamenter|Antonyms: simplifier, minimalist|noun

embellish:/ɪmˈbɛlɪʃ/| v.|装饰，润色，修饰|To make something more attractive by adding decorative details or features; to add details to a story to make it more interesting.|He embellished his speech with a few jokes. 他在演讲中加了一些笑话作为点缀。|Synonyms: decorate, adorn, ornament, enhance, elaborate|Antonyms: simplify, strip, diminish|verb
