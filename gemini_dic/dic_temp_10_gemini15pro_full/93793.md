immersable:/ɪˈmɜːsəbl/| adj.|可浸入的，可沉浸的|Able to be immersed; capable of being submerged.  Something that can be fully involved or absorbed in an activity or experience.|The new virtual reality headset creates an immersable gaming experience.|近义词：submersible, submergible, dipable|反义词:non-immersable, nonsubmersible|词性:形容词
词源: From immerse + -able. Immerse comes from Late Latin immersus, past participle of immergere "to plunge in," from in- "in" + mergere "to dip, plunge."
