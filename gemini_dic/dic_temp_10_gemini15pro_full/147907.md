overreflective:/ˌoʊvərɪˈflɛktɪv/| adj.|过度反思的，过度反省的|Characterized by excessive reflection or introspection, often to the point of hindering action or causing anxiety.|例句：He became overreflective and anxious, constantly second-guessing his decisions. 他变得过度反思和焦虑，不断地怀疑自己的决定。

例句：Her overreflective nature often prevented her from taking risks. 她过度反思的性格常常使她不敢冒险。|近义词：introspective, ruminative, self-analyzing|反义词：impulsive, unreflective, thoughtless|词性:形容词

词源: over- + reflective

over-: prefix meaning "excessive," "too much"

reflective: adjective meaning "characterized by or given to reflection or contemplation"
