newts:/njuːts/| n. |蝾螈|A small semiaquatic amphibian with soft skin and short legs, similar to a lizard but typically spending more time in water.|例句：Newts are often found in ponds and streams. 蝾螈经常在池塘和溪流中被发现。

例句：The newts were swimming in the shallows. 蝾螈在浅水区游泳。|近义词：salamander, eft|反义词: None|Noun
