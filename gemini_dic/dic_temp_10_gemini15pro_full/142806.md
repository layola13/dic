The word "oursel" doesn't exist in standard English.  The closest correct word is "ourselves", which is what will be analyzed below:

ourselves: /ɑːrˈsɛlvz/ | pron. | 我们自己 |  reflexive form of "we," used when the subject and the object of the verb are the same.  It can also be used for emphasis. | We made the cake ourselves.  我们自己做了蛋糕。 | We ourselves are responsible for our actions. 我们自己要对我们的行为负责。|  近义词: (none - reflexive pronouns are unique) | 反义词: (none - reflexive pronouns are unique) | 词性: pronoun
