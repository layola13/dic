concupiscent:/kənˈkjuːpɪsənt/ /kɑːnˈkjuːpɪsənt/| adj.|贪色的，好色的|Filled with or exhibiting strong sexual desire; lustful.|例句：He gave her a concupiscent look. 他向她投以贪婪的目光。

例句：The concupiscent nature of the character was evident in his actions. 这个角色好色的本性在他的行为中显露无遗。|近义词：lustful, lecherous, lascivious, libidinous|反义词：chaste, virtuous, ascetic|词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *concupiscent- (stem of concupiscens), present participle of concupiscere "to long for eagerly, covet," from com- "together" (see com-) + cupiscere "to desire," related to cupidus "eager, desirous."
