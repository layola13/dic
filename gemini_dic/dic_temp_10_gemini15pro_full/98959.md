inter-firm:/ˌɪntərˈfɜːrm/
adjective
公司间的，企业间的
Existing or occurring between firms.
例句：Inter-firm competition is fierce in the tech industry. 科技行业的企业间竞争非常激烈。

例句：Inter-firm collaboration can lead to innovation. 企业间的合作可以带来创新。
近义词：intercompany, between companies
反义词：intrafirm
词性：adjective
