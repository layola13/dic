half: /hæf/ | n. adj. adv. |一半，半个|One of two equal or approximately equal parts of a divisible whole;  a part or portion equal to one of two equal parts into which something is or can be divided. |She ate half the cake. 她吃掉了半个蛋糕。|Synonyms: moiety, part, portion |Antonyms: whole, double | noun

half: /hæf/ | adj. |一半的，半个的|Consisting of, forming, or equal to one of two equal parts into which something is or can be divided. |He filled the glass half full. 他把杯子装了一半。|Synonyms: partial, incomplete |Antonyms: whole, complete | adjective

half: /hæf/ | adv. |一半地|To the extent of one half. |The door was half open. 门半开着。|Synonyms: partly, partially |Antonyms: fully, completely | adverb
