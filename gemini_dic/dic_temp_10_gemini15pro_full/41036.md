copeman:/ˈkəʊpmən/| n.|商人，小贩；(尤指旧时的)流动商贩|A copeman is a merchant, especially a traveling merchant, often used in historical contexts. |例句：The copeman traveled from village to village selling his wares. 这个小贩走村串乡地卖他的商品。

例句：In medieval times, copemen were important for trade between different regions. 在中世纪，流动商贩对于不同地区之间的贸易至关重要。|近义词：merchant, trader, peddler, vendor|反义词:buyer, customer|词性:noun
