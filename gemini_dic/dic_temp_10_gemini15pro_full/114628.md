malengine: /mælˈɛndʒɪn/ | n. | 恶意引擎; 恶意软件引擎 | A software engine designed to create or deploy malware.  It can be a toolkit used by hackers or a component of a larger malware system. |  The hacker used a malengine to generate a new strain of ransomware.  黑客使用恶意引擎生成了一种新的勒索软件。

The security researchers analyzed the malengine to understand its capabilities. 安全研究人员分析了恶意引擎以了解其功能。| 近义词: malware generator, malware toolkit | 反义词: antivirus engine, security software | 词性: 名词


词源:  A portmanteau of "malicious" and "engine."  "Malicious" comes from Old French "malicius," ultimately from Latin "malitia" meaning "badness" or "wickedness." "Engine" comes from Old French "engin," ultimately from Latin "ingenium" meaning "inborn quality, talent, intelligence, clever invention."
