outdrags:/ˌaʊtˈdræɡz/
verb
胜过，超过 (在耐力方面)
outlasts or surpasses in endurance
The Kenyan runner outdragged his rivals in the final lap.  肯尼亚选手在最后一圈超过了他的对手。
近义词: outlasts, surpasses, outruns, beats
反义词: loses to, trails, falls behind
词性: 动词
