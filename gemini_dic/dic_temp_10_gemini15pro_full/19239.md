bhagavad:/ˈbʌɡəvəd/ | adj. | 神圣的，至尊的 |  Relating to or spoken by a god, especially Krishna. Derived from Sanskrit "bhagavat" meaning "blessed, fortunate, divine, holy, adorable, venerable." Often used as part of the title "Bhagavad Gita." | The Bhagavad Gita is a revered Hindu scripture. 薄伽梵歌是一部受人尊敬的印度教经文。| 近义词: divine, holy, sacred | 反义词: profane, unholy | 词性:形容词
