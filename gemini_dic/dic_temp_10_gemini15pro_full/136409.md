nonretention:/ˌnɑːnrɪˈtɛnʃən/ |/ˌnɒnrɪˈtɛnʃən/| n. |未保留，未保持；不保留，不保持|The nonretention of experienced staff is a serious problem for the company.  公司留不住有经验的员工是一个严重的问题。|例句：The nonretention of water in the soil is causing the plants to die. 土壤无法保水，导致植物死亡。|近义词：loss, release, discharge|反义词:retention, keeping, preservation|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + retention.  "Retention" comes from Latin "retentio,"  from "retinere" (to hold back), from "re-" (back) + "tenere" (to hold).
