acetifier:/əˈsiːtɪfaɪər/| n.|醋化器，酸化器|A device or apparatus used for acetifying, or converting fermented liquors into vinegar. It facilitates the oxidation of ethanol to acetic acid, the primary component of vinegar.|The acetifier was constantly monitored to ensure optimal vinegar production.  这个醋化器被持续监控以确保最佳的醋生产。

The old acetifier was replaced with a more modern and efficient model.  旧的醋化器被更现代化、更高效的型号所取代。|近义词:vinegar generator, vinegar maker|反义词:|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *acétifier*, from *acéte* (vinegar) + *-fier* (to make), ultimately from Latin *acetum* (vinegar).

USAGE->
The acetifier is essential for vinegar production. = 醋化器对于醋的生产至关重要。

The new acetifier increased the efficiency of the vinegar-making process. = 新的醋化器提高了醋制造过程的效率。
