overbrave:/ˌoʊvərˈbreɪv/
adjective
过分勇敢的；鲁莽的
Excessively brave; rash or reckless.
He made an overbrave attempt to rescue the drowning child. 他过分勇敢地试图救起溺水的孩子。
The overbrave knight charged into battle without thinking. 这位鲁莽的骑士不假思索地冲进了战斗。
近义词:reckless, rash, foolhardy, impetuous
反义词:cautious, prudent, timid, cowardly
词性:adjective

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *ubar "over, above") + "brave" (from Old French brave "splendid, valiant, worthy," from Italian bravo "wild, savage, courageous," perhaps ultimately from Latin barbarus "barbarian")
