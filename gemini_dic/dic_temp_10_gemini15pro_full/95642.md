incompleteness:/ˌɪnkəmˈpliːtnəs/| n. |不完整，不完全，未完成|The incompleteness of the data makes it difficult to draw firm conclusions.  数据的残缺使得难以得出确切的结论。|例句：The incompleteness of the fossil record makes it hard to trace the evolution of certain species. 化石记录的不完整性使得追踪某些物种的进化变得困难。|近义词：lack, deficiency, imperfection, partialness|反义词:completeness, wholeness, fullness|词性:noun

incompleteness:/ˌɪnkəmˈpliːtnəs/| n. |未完成，不完善|The project suffered from incompleteness due to lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，该项目未完成。|例句：The sense of incompleteness haunted her until she finished the novel. 未完成的感觉一直困扰着她，直到她完成了小说。|近义词：unfinishedness, fragmentariness|反义词:completion, perfection, totality|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + complete + -ness (state of being).  Ultimately from Latin completus (filled). 来自 in-（不）+ complete + -ness（状态）。最终来自拉丁语 completus（填充）。
