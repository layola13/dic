excitedness:/ɪkˈsaɪtɪdnəs/| n. |兴奋，激动|The state of being excited.  The excitedness in the air was palpable.|例句：Her excitedness about the trip was contagious. 她对旅行的兴奋之情很有感染力。

例句：His excitedness at seeing his family after so long was evident.  与家人久别重逢，他的兴奋之情溢于言表。

近义词：excitement, exhilaration, enthusiasm|反义词:calmness, apathy, indifference|词性:noun

词源: From "excited" + "-ness". "Excited" comes from the Latin "excitare," meaning "to rouse, stir up."  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, denoting a state or quality.
