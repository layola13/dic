excreting:/ɪkˈskriːtɪŋ/
verb
排泄，分泌
the process of removing waste products from the body.
The kidneys are responsible for excreting waste products from the blood. 肾脏负责从血液中排出废物。
The skin excretes sweat. 皮肤分泌汗液。
synonyms: discharging, eliminating, expelling
antonyms: absorbing, retaining
verb
