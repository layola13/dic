albescent:/ælˈbɛsənt/
adjective
变白的，发白的
Becoming white or whitish; slightly white.
The albescent leaves of the plant shimmered in the moonlight.  这种植物发白的叶子在月光下闪闪发光。
The fungus developed an albescent coating as it aged.  随着时间的推移，真菌表面长出了一层白色的涂层。
近义词:whitish, pale, bleaching
反义词:darkening, blackening
词性:adjective
词源:From Latin *albescentem, present participle of albescere ("to grow white"), from albus ("white").
