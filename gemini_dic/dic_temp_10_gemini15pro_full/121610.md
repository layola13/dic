misorganizes:/ˌmɪsˈɔːrɡənaɪzɪz/
verb
组织混乱，组织不善
To organize something badly or inefficiently.
He consistently misorganizes his paperwork, leading to frequent delays. 他总是把文件整理得乱七八糟，导致经常延误。
The event was misorganized and chaotic.  这次活动组织得很糟糕，一片混乱。
mismanage, disorganize
organize, arrange
词性: 动词
