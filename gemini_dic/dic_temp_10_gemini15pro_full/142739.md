otology:/oʊˈtɑːlədʒi/ /ɒˈtɒlədʒi/
n.
耳科学
The study of the ear and its diseases.
Otology is a branch of medicine specializing in the diagnosis and treatment of ear disorders. 耳科学是专门诊断和治疗耳朵疾病的医学分支。
He is a renowned specialist in otology. 他是一位著名的耳科专家。
近义词:audiology
反义词:None
词性:noun
