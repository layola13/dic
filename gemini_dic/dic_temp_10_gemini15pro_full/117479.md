medle:/ˈmɛdəl/| v.|干涉，干预，掺杂|To interfere in something that is not one's concern.|He shouldn't medle in other people's affairs. 他不应该干涉别人的事情。|近义词：interfere, intervene, tamper, intrude|反义词:ignore, abstain|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *medler (compare Old French mesler, modern French mêler), from Latin misceō (“I mix, mingle”), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *meyḱ- (“to mix”). Doublet of mix.
