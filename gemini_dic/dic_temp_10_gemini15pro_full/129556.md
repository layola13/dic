non-capital:/ˌnɒnˈkæpɪtəl/ | adj. | 非资本的；非首府的；小写的 | Not of or relating to capital; not a capital letter. |  Many businesses have non-capital expenses. 许多企业都有非资本支出。| synonyms: non-major, lowercase, small letter | antonyms: capital, major, uppercase | adjective

non-capital:/ˌnɒnˈkæpɪtəl/ | n. | 非资本项目；非首府城市 | Something that is not capital, such as a non-capital expense or a city that is not a capital.  | The company's non-capitals include office supplies. 该公司的非资本项目包括办公用品。| synonyms: non-asset, lowercase letter | antonyms: capital asset, capital city, uppercase letter | noun
