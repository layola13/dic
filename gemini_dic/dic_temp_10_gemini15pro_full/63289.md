enthrone:/ɪnˈθroʊn/ (US), /ɪnˈθrəʊn/ (UK)| vt.| 登基；使…登上王位；推崇；奉为…|To place (someone, especially a monarch) on a throne, or in a position of authority or dignity.  Also, to exalt to a high position; to glorify. | The archbishop enthroned the new king.  大主教为新国王加冕。 | Synonyms: crown, invest, install, exalt | Antonyms: dethrone, depose, oust | verb

enthronement:/ɪnˈθroʊnmənt/ (US), /ɪnˈθrəʊnmənt/ (UK)| n.| 登基；加冕；就任 |The act or ceremony of enthroning someone, especially a monarch. |The enthronement ceremony was a grand spectacle.  登基典礼是一场盛大的场面。| Synonyms: coronation, inauguration, investiture | Antonyms: deposition, dethronement | noun
