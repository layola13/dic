obdurateness:/ˈɒbdjʊrətnəs/ /ˈɑːbdʊrətnəs/| n. |顽固，执拗，冷酷无情|The obdurateness of the king made any compromise impossible.|国王的顽固使得任何妥协都不可能。|Synonyms: stubbornness, inflexibility, intransigence, implacability|Antonyms: flexibility, compliance, yielding, compassion|noun


obdurate:/ˈɒbdjʊrət/ /ˈɑːbdʊrət/| adj. |顽固的，执拗的，冷酷无情的|He remained obdurate, refusing to listen to our pleas.|他仍然固执己见，拒绝听取我们的请求。|Synonyms: stubborn, inflexible, intransigent, implacable|Antonyms: flexible, compliant, yielding, compassionate|adjective


Etymology:  From Latin *obdūrātus*, past participle of *obdūrāre* (“to harden”), from *ob-* (“against, towards”) + *dūrāre* (“to harden, last, endure”), from *dūrus* (“hard”).
