nacreous:/ˈneɪkriəs/| adj.|珍珠似的，有珍珠光泽的|Resembling nacre; iridescent. Having a pearly luster.|The inside of the shell had a nacreous sheen. 贝壳的内部有着珍珠般的光泽。|近义词：pearlescent, iridescent, opalescent|反义词：dull, matte|词性:形容词
Etymology: Late 16th century: from French nacré, from nacre ‘mother-of-pearl’ + -ous.|词源：16世纪晚期：源自法语nacré，来自nacre“珍珠母”+ -ous。
