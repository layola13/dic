inspirer:/ɪnˈspaɪərər/| n.|激励者，鼓舞人心的人|Someone who inspires others.  A person who fills others with the urge or ability to do or feel something, especially to do something creative.| Nelson Mandela was an inspirer to millions.  纳尔逊·曼德拉激励了数百万人。|近义词：motivator, influencer, role model|反义词：demotivator, discourager|noun
