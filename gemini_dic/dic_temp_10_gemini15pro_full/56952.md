dottrel:/ˈdɒtrəl/ (UK) /ˈdɑːtrəl/ (US)| n. |点斑；金鸻；愚人；容易受骗的人|A small wading bird with a distinctive breast band, known for its easily approachable nature, or a person easily fooled or cheated.|例句：The dottrel is known for its tameness and can be easily approached. 金鸻以其温顺而闻名，很容易接近。

例句：He's such a dottrel, he believed everything she told him. 他真是个傻瓜，他相信她所说的一切。|近义词：dupe, gull, fool, simpleton|反义词：skeptic, cynic|词性:名词
