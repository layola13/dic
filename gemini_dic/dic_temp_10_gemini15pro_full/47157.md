dawdles:/ˈdɔːdəlz/ (US), /ˈdɔːd(ə)lz/ (UK)| v. | 闲逛，磨蹭，拖延 | To waste time or move slowly. | He dawdled over his breakfast, reluctant to go to work. 他磨磨蹭蹭地吃早饭，不愿去上班。| dawdle along the riverbank 沿着河岸闲逛 | synonyms: linger, loiter, tarry, delay, procrastinate | antonyms: hurry, rush, hasten | verb

dawdler:/ˈdɔːdlər/ (US), /ˈdɔːd(ə)lər/ (UK)| n. | 磨蹭的人，闲逛的人 | Someone who dawdles. | Stop being such a dawdler and get ready! 别磨蹭了，赶紧准备好！|  The dawdlers held up the entire group. 磨蹭的人拖了整个团队的后腿。| synonyms: loiterer, lingerer, procrastinator | antonyms: go-getter, hustler | noun
