nonspeculatively:/ˌnɒnˈspɛkjʊlətɪvli/
adverb
非投机性地，非推测性地
In a way that avoids speculation or conjecture; based on concrete evidence or facts.
He invested his money nonspeculatively in government bonds. 他以非投机的方式将资金投资于政府债券。
Synonyms: practically, pragmatically, realistically, empirically
Antonyms: speculatively, theoretically, hypothetically
词性: 副词
