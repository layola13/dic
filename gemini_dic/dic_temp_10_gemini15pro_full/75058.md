frighteningly:/ˈfraɪtnɪŋli/| adv.|令人恐惧地，可怕地|In a way that causes fear or alarm.  Derived from "frightening" (adjective) + "-ly" (adverb suffix).  The root word "fright" comes from Old English *fyrhtu, meaning "fear, terror."|The dog growled frighteningly at the stranger.  那只狗对着陌生人发出可怕的低吼。|horrifyingly, terrifyingly, alarmingly|reassuringly, calmly|adverb
