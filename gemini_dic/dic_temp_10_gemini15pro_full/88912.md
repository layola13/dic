hold-in:/ˈhoʊldɪn/| n.|抑制；克制；聚会；(尤指罢工期间的)静坐示威|An act of holding in; a restraint. Also, a gathering, especially a sit-in, as during a strike.|例句：The workers staged a hold-in at the factory to protest the new regulations. 工人们在工厂举行静坐示威，抗议新规。|例句：She couldn't contain her laughter; it was a real hold-in. 她忍不住笑了；这真是一种克制。|近义词：restraint, suppression, sit-in, demonstration|反义词：release, outburst, expression|词性:名词

hold-in:/ˈhoʊldɪn/| adj.|抑制的；克制的|Characterized by holding in or restraining.|例句：His hold-in emotions eventually caused him to break down. 他压抑的情绪最终导致他崩溃了。|例句：It was a hold-in reaction, but she knew she couldn't express her anger. 这是一种克制的反应，但她知道她不能表达她的愤怒。|近义词：restrained, suppressed, controlled|反义词：unrestrained, expressive, open|词性:形容词
