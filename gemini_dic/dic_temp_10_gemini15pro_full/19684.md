bildstein:/ˈbɪldʃtaɪn/| n.|雕像，塑像，石像|A sculpted stone figure, often religious or commemorative. |例句：The bildstein of the Virgin Mary stood in the garden. 圣母玛利亚的石像矗立在花园里。

例句：Ancient bildsteins adorned the entrance to the temple. 古老的石像装饰着寺庙的入口。|近义词：statue, sculpture, effigy, monument|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "fragment" if the statue is broken)|词性:noun
