nondeliberateness:/nɑːndɪˈlɪbərɪtnɪs/
|n.|非故意性；无意识；非深思熟虑|Lack of deliberation; unintentional or unconsidered action or quality.|Her nondeliberateness led to a series of unfortunate events. 她的无意识导致了一系列不幸事件。|Synonyms: unintentionality, inadvertence, unplannedness, thoughtlessness|Antonyms: deliberateness, intentionality, premeditation, purposefulness|noun

词源:  "Non-" (not) + "deliberate" (from Latin "deliberatus," past participle of "deliberare" - to weigh in mind, consider) + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality).  Therefore, "nondeliberateness" refers to the state or quality of not being deliberate.
