nonparalytics:/ˌnɑːnˌpærəˈlɪtɪks/
|n.|非麻醉性镇痛药|Drugs that reduce pain without causing paralysis.  These medications allow patients to remain conscious and breathe on their own while relieving discomfort.|The doctor prescribed nonparalytics for post-operative pain management. 医生开了非麻醉性镇痛药来控制术后疼痛。

The patient was given nonparalytics to manage their pain during the procedure. 在手术过程中，病人服用了非麻醉性镇痛药来控制疼痛。|近义词:analgesics, pain relievers|反义词:paralytics, anesthetics|词性:名词
