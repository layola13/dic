obeyed:/əˈbeɪd/| v.|服从，遵守|complied with or followed a command, instruction, or request.|The soldiers obeyed the general's orders. 士兵们服从了将军的命令。|近义词：complied, followed, adhered to|反义词：disobeyed, defied, ignored|词性:动词

词源:
From Old French *obeir, from Latin oboedīre (“to obey, be obedient to, give ear to”), from ob- (“towards”) + audire (“to hear”).
