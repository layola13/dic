oases:/əʊˈeɪsiːz/
n.
绿洲；舒适的地方；(困境中的) 缓解
A fertile spot in a desert where water is found. A place of peace or relief in a difficult or unpleasant situation.
The travelers found an oasis in the desert after days of wandering. 旅行者在沙漠中流浪数日后找到了一个绿洲。
The library was an oasis of calm in the bustling city. 图书馆是喧嚣城市中一片宁静的绿洲。
近义词: haven, refuge, sanctuary
反义词: desert, wasteland
词性: 名词(复数)
