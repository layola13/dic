horometer: /hɔːˈrɒmɪtər/ (British) /hɔːˈrɑːmɪtər/ (American) | n. |地平仪，水平仪,边界测量仪|An instrument used to measure the dip of the horizon, especially at sea, for determining latitude. It can also refer to an instrument for measuring horizontal distances or angles. |例句：The sailor used a horometer to determine the ship's latitude. 水手用地平仪确定船的纬度。

例句：Surveyors sometimes use a horometer to measure horizontal distances. 测量员有时会使用水平仪测量水平距离。|近义词：clinometer, level, inclinometer |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
