detonator:/ˈdɛtəˌneɪtər/| n.|雷管，引爆器|A device used to set off an explosive charge, such as dynamite.|The detonator initiated the explosion. 雷管引发了爆炸。|Synonyms: fuse, primer, trigger|Antonyms: N/A|noun

detonate:/ˈdɛtəˌneɪt/| v.|引爆，爆炸|To cause (something, such as an explosive) to explode.|The bomb detonated prematurely. 炸弹过早地爆炸了。|Synonyms: explode, blow up|Antonyms: defuse, disarm|verb
