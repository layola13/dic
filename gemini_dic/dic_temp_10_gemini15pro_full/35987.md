codesign:/ˈkoʊdsaɪn/| v. |代码签名|The process of digitally signing software to guarantee its authenticity and integrity.  It assures users that the software hasn't been tampered with and originates from a known source.|Example 1:  The developer had to codesign the app before distributing it on the App Store. 开发者必须在应用商店发布应用程序之前对其进行代码签名。

Example 2: Codesigning helps prevent malware from masquerading as legitimate software. 代码签名有助于防止恶意软件伪装成合法软件。|近义词: digitally sign, authenticate|反义词: N/A|词性: verb
