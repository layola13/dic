bowsprit: /ˈboʊsprɪt/ (US), /ˈbaʊsprɪt/ (UK)| n. |船首斜桅|A spar projecting forward from the stem of a sailing ship, to which the forestays are attached. |The bowsprit extended out over the waves. 船首斜桅延伸到波浪之上。|The ship's bowsprit was damaged in the storm. 船的船首斜桅在暴风雨中受损了。|近义词: jibboom |反义词:  (No direct antonym) |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *boghespriet, from bogen (“to bow, to bend”) + spriet (“spar”).
