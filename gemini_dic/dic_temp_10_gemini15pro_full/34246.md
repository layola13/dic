clap-stick's:/ˈklæpˌstɪks/ | n. |击板;拍板|A pair of sticks held in one hand and clapped together to make a percussive sound. Typically used in pairs, one held in each hand.|例句：The musician played a lively rhythm on the clap-sticks.  音乐家用击板演奏了一段欢快的节奏。

例句：She danced with energy, her clap-sticks clicking rhythmically. 她充满活力地跳舞，她的拍板发出有节奏的咔哒声。|近义词：claves, rhythm sticks|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "silent instrument")|词性:noun
