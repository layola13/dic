hyperpyrexia:/ˌhaɪpərpaɪˈrɛksiə/| n.|极度高热，超高热|A very high fever, above 41.5°C (106.7°F), which is a medical emergency.|The patient presented with hyperpyrexia and seizures. 病人出现极度高热和癫痫发作。|近义词:hyperthermia|反义词:hypothermia|词性:名词

Etymology:
From hyper- (Greek: 'over, above') + pyrexia (Greek: 'fever').
