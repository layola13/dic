gravamen:/ˈɡrævəmen/| n.|主要 grievance; the essence or most serious part of a complaint or accusation| the essence of a charge or complaint|例句：The gravamen of the plaintiff's argument was that the defendant had breached the contract. 原告论点的核心是，被告违反了合同。

例句：The gravamen of the lawsuit was the company's alleged negligence.  这起诉讼的核心是该公司被指控的过失。|近义词：gist, substance, core, crux, essence|反义词：insignificance, triviality|noun
