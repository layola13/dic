garter-belts:/ˈɡɑːrtər bɛlts/
noun
吊袜带
Garter belts are an undergarment worn to hold up stockings.
She wore a black lace garter belt. 她穿了一条黑色蕾丝吊袜带。
Synonyms: suspender belt, stocking suspender
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but perhaps "pantyhose" as an alternative)
