muffler:/ˈmʌflər/ (US) /ˈmʌflə(r)/ (UK)| n.|消声器；围巾；手笼|A device that reduces noise, especially on a car exhaust. Also, a scarf worn around the neck for warmth, or a tube-shaped piece of fur or fabric for keeping the hands warm.|
例句：The car's muffler needs to be replaced. 汽车的消声器需要更换。

例句：She wrapped a thick muffler around her neck to keep warm. 她用一条厚厚的围巾裹住脖子保暖。


近义词：silencer (for exhaust), scarf, neck warmer, hand muff|反义词: amplifier (opposite of silencer)|词性:noun


mufflers:/ˈmʌflərz/ (US) /ˈmʌfləz/ (UK)| n.|消声器（复数）；围巾（复数）；手笼（复数）|Plural form of "muffler".|
例句：The mufflers on these motorcycles are very loud. 这些摩托车的消声器很吵。

例句：They sell beautiful wool mufflers at the market. 他们在市场上卖漂亮的羊毛围巾。


近义词：silencers, scarves, neck warmers, hand muffs|反义词: amplifiers|词性:noun
