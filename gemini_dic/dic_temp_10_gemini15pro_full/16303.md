baracus:/bəˈrækəs/| n.|巴拉克斯 (通常指电视连续剧《天龙特攻队》中的角色 B. A. Baracus)| B. A. Baracus, played by Mr. T, is a fictional character and member of the A-Team from the television series of the same name. He is known for his distinctive mohawk hairstyle, gold chains, and fear of flying.|例句：Baracus is famous for his catchphrase, "I ain't gettin' on no plane!" 巴拉克斯以他的口头禅“我不会上任何飞机！”而闻名。

例句：Many people remember Baracus for his mechanical skills and tough-guy persona. 很多人记得巴拉克斯的机械技能和硬汉形象。|近义词：mechanic, tough guy|反义词: coward|词性:noun
