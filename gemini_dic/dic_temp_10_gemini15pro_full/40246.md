contingencies:/kənˈtɪndʒənsiz/
n.
意外事件；可能性；偶然性
Possible future events or circumstances that cannot be predicted with certainty; the possibility of something happening.
The budget includes contingencies for unexpected expenses. 预算包括了意外开支的准备金。
synonyms: possibilities, eventualities, uncertainties, eventualities
antonyms: certainties, inevitabilities, predictabilities
noun

contingency:/kənˈtɪndʒənsi/
n.
意外事件；可能性；偶然性
A future event or circumstance which is possible but cannot be predicted with certainty.
We must consider all possible contingencies. 我们必须考虑所有可能发生的意外情况。
synonyms: possibility, eventuality, uncertainty, chance
antonyms: certainty, inevitability, predictability
noun
