exuperable:/ɛkˈs(j)uːpərəb(ə)l/
adjective
可克服的，可超越的
able to be overcome or exceeded.
例句：With enough effort, the challenge proved exuperable.  足够的努力证明了这个挑战是可以克服的。

例句：Even the most daunting obstacles are exuperable with perseverance. 即使是最令人生畏的障碍，只要坚持不懈也能克服。

近义词：surmountable, conquerable, vincible
反义词：insurmountable, unconquerable, invincible
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *exsuperabilis, from exsuperare (“to overcome, surpass”), from ex (“out of, from”) + superare (“to overcome, surpass”).
