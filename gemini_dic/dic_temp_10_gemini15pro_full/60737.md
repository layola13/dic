electrocute:/ɪˈlɛktrəˌkjut/ | vt. |电死，以电刑处死 | To kill or injure someone by passing a strong electric current through their body. | The condemned prisoner was electrocuted.  这名被判刑的囚犯被处以电刑。

The faulty wiring electrocuted the dog.  错误的线路导致狗被电死了。 | 近义词: execute, kill | 反义词: revive, resuscitate | verb

electrocution:/ɪˌlɛktrəˈkjuːʃən/ | n. | 电刑，电死 | The act of killing or injuring someone by electric shock. |  His electrocution was swift and painless. 他的电刑执行得迅速而无痛。

The investigation ruled the death an accidental electrocution.  调查裁定这起死亡是意外触电身亡。 | 近义词: execution, death | 反义词: resuscitation, revival | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From electro- (combining form meaning "electric") + -cute (from execute).  The word was coined in the late 19th century.
