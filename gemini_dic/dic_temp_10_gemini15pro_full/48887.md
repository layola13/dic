deframing:/diːˈfreɪmɪŋ/
v.|去框架化，打破框架|The process of challenging and dismantling rigid ways of thinking, seeing, or understanding something, often related to social norms, cultural biases, or personal limitations.  It involves questioning assumptions and exploring alternative perspectives.|Therapy helped her in deframing her negative experiences and seeing them in a new light. 治疗帮助她重新构建了她的负面经历，并以新的眼光看待它们。|reframing, restructuring, rethinking|framing, limiting|verb
