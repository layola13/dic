paddocking:/ˈpædəkɪŋ/
n.
围场；将…置于围场
The act of confining a horse or other animal to a paddock.  The area where horses are saddled and paraded before a race.
The horse was restless during paddocking.  这匹马在围场里焦躁不安。
The paddocking area was crowded with trainers and jockeys. 围场里挤满了驯马师和骑师。
enclosure, confinement
release
