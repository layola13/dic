aerobatics: /ˌɛrəˈbætɪks/ | n. | 特技飞行；特技飞行表演 |  The spectacular aerobatics thrilled the crowd.  壮观的特技飞行表演使观众激动不已。 |  stunts, aerial acrobatics |  - | noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From French *aérobatie*, from *aéro-* (from Greek *aḗr* "air") + *-batie* (from Greek *batós* "passable," from *bainein* "to walk, go, step").  Coined in French in 1913.

USAGE->

The air show featured breathtaking aerobatics. = 航空展的特色是惊险的特技飞行。
His aerobatics were daring and skillful. = 他的特技飞行既大胆又娴熟。

Possessive form:
The pilot's aerobatics were impressive. = 飞行员的特技飞行令人印象深刻。
The plane's aerobatics defied gravity. = 飞机的特技飞行无视重力。

As subject of a sentence:
Aerobatics requires great skill and precision. = 特技飞行需要高超的技巧和精准度。
Aerobatics is a thrilling spectator sport. = 特技飞行是一项惊险的观赏运动。

As object of a sentence:
He practiced aerobatics for hours. = 他练习特技飞行好几个小时。
The crowd watched the aerobatics with awe. = 观众敬畏地观看着特技飞行表演。

With prepositions:
The plane performed aerobatics in the sky. = 飞机在空中进行特技飞行。
She has a passion for aerobatics. = 她对特技飞行充满热情。

With adjectives:
