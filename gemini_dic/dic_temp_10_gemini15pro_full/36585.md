collaterally:/kəˈlætərəli/
adv.| 附带地，作为抵押| In addition to the main subject or indirectly; as security.
He mentioned the issue collaterally in his speech. 他在演讲中附带提到了这个问题。
The house was collaterally secured against the loan. 这房子被抵押担保了贷款。
近义词: incidentally, indirectly, secondarily, tangentially
反义词: directly, primarily, principally
词性: 副词


collateral:/kəˈlætərəl/
n.| 抵押品，担保品，旁系亲属|Something pledged as security for repayment of a loan, a person having the same ancestor as another but not in a direct line of descent.
The bank required collateral for the loan. 银行要求贷款提供抵押品。
He and his cousins are collateral relatives. 他和他的堂兄弟是旁系亲属。
近义词: security, guarantee, pledge, cousin
反义词:  (For security sense) debt, liability
词性: 名词


collateral:/kəˈlætərəl/
adj.| 附带的，次要的，旁系的|Secondary or subordinate; descended from the same ancestor but in a different line.
The collateral effects of the drug are minimal. 药物的副作用很小。
She inherited the estate as a collateral heir. 她作为旁系继承人继承了遗产。
近义词: secondary, subordinate, ancillary, indirect, tangential
反义词: primary, main, principal, direct
词性: 形容词



词源:  From Late Latin *collateralis, from com- "together" + lateralis "of the side".
