buddleias:/ˈbʌdliəz/ | n. |醉鱼草（属植物）| A genus of shrubs and small trees in the figwort family (Scrophulariaceae), native to Asia, Africa, and the Americas. They are known for their fragrant, colorful flowers that attract butterflies. | The buddleias in my garden are attracting lots of butterflies.  我的花园里的醉鱼草吸引了许多蝴蝶。| Synonym: butterfly bush | Antonym: N/A | noun
