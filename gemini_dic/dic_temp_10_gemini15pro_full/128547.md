NGOs:/ˌɛnˌdʒiːˈoʊz/
|n.|非政府组织|Non-governmental organizations; organizations that are not part of a government or a for-profit business.  They often work in areas such as human rights, environmental protection, or development.|Many NGOs are working to provide aid to refugees. 许多非政府组织正在努力向难民提供援助。|Synonyms:  non-profit organizations, charities, voluntary organizations|Antonyms: governmental organizations, for-profit businesses|noun
