naturopathies:/ˌneɪtʃəˈrɒpəθiz/ | n. | 自然疗法 |  A system of alternative medicine based on the theory that diseases can be successfully treated or prevented without the use of drugs, by techniques such as control of diet, exercise, and massage. |  She explored various naturopathies to alleviate her chronic pain. 她探索了各种自然疗法来缓解她的慢性疼痛。 |  Alternative medicine, holistic medicine, complementary medicine | Conventional medicine, allopathic medicine | noun
