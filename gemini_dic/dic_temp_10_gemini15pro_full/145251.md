overblind:/ˈoʊvərblaɪnd/
adjective
过度盲目的
Excessively or uncritically accepting; lacking in discernment or judgment.  Influenced by excessive enthusiasm or emotion, preventing clear judgment. Built from "over" meaning excessive and "blind" meaning lacking sight or understanding.
例句：His overblind faith in the company led to significant financial losses. 他对公司过度盲目的信任导致了巨大的经济损失。

例句：The overblind optimism of the investors blinded them to the risks of the venture. 投资者过度盲目的乐观使他们对风险视而不见。
近义词：infatuated, obsessed, biased, prejudiced
反义词：discerning, critical, objective, judicious
词性: adjective
