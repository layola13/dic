ingenerability:/ɪnˌdʒɛnərəˈbɪləti/
n.
不能产生;不毛;
The ingenerability of the soil made farming impossible. 这片土地的贫瘠使得耕作成为不可能。
The ingenerability of the land led to famine.  这片土地的荒芜导致了饥荒。
近义词：sterility, barrenness, infertility
反义词：fertility, fecundity
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ingenerabilis, from in- "not" + generabilis "capable of being generated," from generare "to beget, produce," from genus "birth, descent, race, stock, kind."
