nonresponsibility:/ˌnɒnrɪˌspɒnsɪˈbɪləti/ |n.|无责任，不负责，免责|lack of responsibility; the state of not being responsible for something. |The contract included a clause of nonresponsibility for damage caused by natural disasters.|近义词: irresponsibility, unaccountability, blamelessness|反义词: responsibility, accountability, liability|词性:名词

词源: non- + responsibility.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Responsibility" comes from the Late Latin "responsabilitatem," meaning "the state of being answerable."
