dag's:/dæɡz/ | n. | 白天；日；工作日 (plural, informal, chiefly Southern US) |  A contraction of "dag's work," meaning a day's work. It refers to the portion of a day allotted to work, or the entire day itself. |  例句：I've done a good dag's work today. 我今天干了一天的活。

例句：He put in a hard dag's work. 他辛苦地干了一天活。| 近义词：day, workday | 反义词: night | 词性: 名词
