defender:/dɪˈfɛndər/ | n. | 防御者，守卫者，拥护者 | Someone who protects or supports something or someone. |  The defenders of the castle held off the enemy for weeks. 城堡的守卫者抵挡敌人长达数周。| synonyms: protector, guardian, advocate, supporter | antonyms: attacker, assailant, opponent, critic | noun

defender:/dɪˈfɛndər/ | n. | (体育)防守队员 | A player whose role is to prevent the opposing team from scoring. | The team's best defender was injured in the first half.  该队最好的防守队员在上半场受伤了。| synonyms: fullback, centre-back, guard | antonyms: forward, striker, attacker | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *defendeor*, from Latin *dēfēnsor* "protector," agent noun from past participle stem of *dēfendere* "to ward off, protect," from *de-* "off, away" + *fendere* "to strike, push."
