nicotic: /nɪˈkɒtɪk/ | adj. | 尼古丁的；与尼古丁有关的 | Relating to or containing nicotine. |  Nicotine is a nicotic alkaloid. 尼古丁是一种尼古丁生物碱。|  He developed a nicotic addiction. 他染上了尼古丁瘾。| 近义词: nicotine-containing, nicotinic | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用nicotine-free 无尼古丁的) | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From French *nicotique*, from *nicotine* + *-ique*. Ultimately from  Jean Nicot (French ambassador to Portugal), who introduced tobacco to France.
