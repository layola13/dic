brokenhearted:/ˈbroʊkənˌhɑːrtɪd/| adj.| 伤心欲绝的，心碎的 | Overwhelmed by grief or disappointment, especially due to the loss of love or a romantic relationship. | She was brokenhearted when her fiancé called off the wedding. 她未婚夫取消婚礼时，她伤心欲绝。|  Near Synonyms: heartbroken, devastated, inconsolable, grief-stricken | Antonyms: joyful, elated, happy, cheerful | Adjective

Etymology:  From *broken + hearted*, from Middle English *brokenherted* "discouraged, dejected," from Old English *brocenheort*.  The meaning shifted to "overwhelmed by grief" in the 16th century.
