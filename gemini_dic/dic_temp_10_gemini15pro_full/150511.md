pantomimic:/ˌpæntəˈmɪmɪk/| adj.|哑剧的，默剧的；通过哑剧表演的|Expressed or performed by pantomime; relating to or resembling pantomime.|She gave a pantomimic performance of opening a gift. 她用哑剧的形式表演了打开礼物的动作。|Synonyms: mimetic, gesticulatory, silent, unspoken|Antonyms: spoken, verbal|adjective

pantomime:/ˈpæntəmaɪm/| n.  vi. vt.|哑剧；哑剧表演；打手势|A dramatic entertainment, originating in Roman mime, in which performers express meaning through gestures accompanied by music.|The children enjoyed the Christmas pantomime. 孩子们喜欢圣诞哑剧。|Synonyms: mime, dumb show, charade|Antonyms: dialogue, speech|noun, verb
