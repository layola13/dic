nonsticky:/ˌnɒnˈstɪki/ /ˌnɑːnˈstɪki/| adj.|不粘的；不粘锅的|Not sticky; designed to prevent food from adhering.|例句：I always use a nonstick pan for frying eggs. 我煎鸡蛋总是用不粘锅。

例句：This nonstick spray makes cleaning up a breeze.  这种不粘喷雾使清洁变得轻而易举。


近义词：nonstick, non-stick|反义词:sticky|词性:adjective
