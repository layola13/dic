fogs:/fɒɡz/ (British), /fɑːɡz/ (American)| n.|雾(复数)|A thick cloud of tiny water droplets suspended in the atmosphere at or near the earth's surface that obscures or restricts visibility.|The heavy fogs rolled in from the sea. 浓雾从海上滚滚而来。|近义词：mists, hazes|反义词: clarity, sunshine|noun
