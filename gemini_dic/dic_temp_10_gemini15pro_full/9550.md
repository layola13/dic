antiseptically:/ˌæn.tɪˈsep.tɪk.li/ | adv. | 抗菌地，消毒地 | In a way that prevents infection by inhibiting the growth of microorganisms. | The wound was cleaned antiseptically.  伤口被抗菌地清洁了。| 近义词: sterily, hygienically | 反义词: septicly, unhygienically | 词性: 副词
词源: From antiseptic + -ally. Antiseptic comes from Greek anti (against) + septikos (causing putrefaction).
