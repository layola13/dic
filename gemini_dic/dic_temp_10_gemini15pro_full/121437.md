misintend:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtɛnd/ | v. | 误解，曲解 | To understand (something) incorrectly; to mistake the meaning of. | I think you misintend my words; I didn't mean to offend you. 我想你误解了我的话；我不是故意要冒犯你的。

She misintended his silence as agreement. 她把他的沉默误解为同意。

| 近义词：misunderstand, misinterpret, misconstrue | 反义词：understand, comprehend, grasp | 词性：动词
