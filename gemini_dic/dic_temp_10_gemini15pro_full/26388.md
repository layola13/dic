by-passing:/baɪˈpɑːsɪŋ/ | n. | 绕过，旁路，忽视 | The act of going around or avoiding something, often an obstacle or a procedure. |  The new highway bypasses the town, reducing traffic congestion. 新高速公路绕过了城镇，减少了交通拥堵。

The chairman bypassed the usual procedures and went straight to a vote. 主席绕过了通常的程序，直接进行了投票。 | synonyms: circumventing, avoiding, dodging, sidestepping | antonyms: confronting, facing, addressing | verb (present participle)

by-pass:/ˈbaɪpɑːs/ | n. | 旁路，绕行线路 | A road or route that goes around a town or other busy area. | The bypass allows drivers to avoid the city center. 这条旁路可以让司机避开市中心。

Heart bypass surgery is a common procedure. 心脏搭桥手术是一种常见的手术。 | synonyms: detour, alternative route, shunt | antonyms: main route, direct route | noun

bypass:/baɪˈpɑːs/ | v. | 绕过，避开，忽视 | To go around or avoid something. | They bypassed the traffic jam by taking a back road. 他们走了一条小路绕过了交通堵塞。
