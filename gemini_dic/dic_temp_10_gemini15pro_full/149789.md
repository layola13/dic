pale:/peɪl/| adj.|苍白的，暗淡的；浅色的|Lacking color or intensity. Appearing lighter than usual due to illness, fear, or other emotional distress.  Can also describe something light in color.|She looked pale and tired after the long journey. 她长途旅行后看起来脸色苍白，疲惫不堪。|The walls were painted a pale shade of blue. 墙壁被漆成了淡蓝色。|近义词：wan, ashen, light, faint|反义词：bright, vivid, flushed, ruddy|词性:形容词
