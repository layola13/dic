impairers:/ɪmˈpeərərz/| n.|损害物，损害因素|Something that impairs; something that diminishes or weakens, especially something that damages or weakens a person's physical or mental abilities.|Excessive alcohol consumption is one of the major impairers of driving ability. 过量饮酒是损害驾驶能力的主要因素之一。|The new regulations aim to reduce environmental impairers impacting air quality. 新法规旨在减少影响空气质量的环境损害因素。|synonyms: damagers, weakeners, deteriorators, inhibitors|antonyms: enhancers, improvers, strengtheners|noun
