The librarian was a mild-mannered woman who always spoke softly.  这位图书管理员是一位温文尔雅的女士，总是轻声细语。

| 近义词: gentle, meek, polite, unassuming, quiet | 反义词: aggressive, assertive, boisterous, rude, pushy | 词性: 形容词
