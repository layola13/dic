overcivilizes:/ˌoʊvərˈsɪvɪlaɪzɪz/
|v. (vt.)|过度文明化；过分教化|To civilize excessively; to impose an elaborate code of manners or behavior upon.|例句：Some anthropologists argue that overcivilizing indigenous tribes can erode their traditional cultures.  一些人类学家认为，过度文明化土著部落会侵蚀他们的传统文化。

例句：He felt the school had overcivilized the children, stifling their creativity and natural curiosity. 他觉得学校过度教化了孩子们，扼杀了他们的创造力和天然的好奇心。|近义词：refine, overcultivate, overrefine|反义词：barbarize, brutalize|词性：动词
