backlogging:/ˈbæklɒɡɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbæklɒɡɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|积压，积压的工作|The act of accumulating a backlog, a deferred or unfinished task or workload.|例句：The company is facing a significant backlogging of orders.  这家公司面临着严重的订单积压。

例句：The backlogging of paperwork caused delays in processing the applications. 文件工作的积压导致了申请处理的延误。

近义词：accumulation, buildup, deferral|反义词:completion, clearance|词性:名词
