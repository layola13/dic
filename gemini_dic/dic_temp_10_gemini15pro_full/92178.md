The report was criticized for its hyperspeculativenesses about the future of the economy.  该报告因其对未来经济的过度推测而受到批评。 | 近义词: overspeculation, conjecture, surmise, supposition | 反义词:  caution, prudence, restraint, groundedness | 词性: noun (plural)
