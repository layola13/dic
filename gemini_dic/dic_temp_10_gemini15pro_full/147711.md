overprescribes: /ˌoʊvərprɪˈskraɪbz/
verb
过度处方；过量开药
To prescribe (medication) in excessive amounts or for too long a period.
Doctors are often accused of overprescribing antibiotics. 医生经常被指责过度开抗生素。
overmedicate, overtreat
underprescribe
v.
