hyperconfidence:/ˌhaɪpərˈkɒnfɪdəns/ | n. |过度自信|  Excessive confidence, often unwarranted and leading to misjudgment. | He displayed hyperconfidence in his abilities, despite his lack of experience.  他表现出对自己能力的过度自信，尽管他缺乏经验。 | 近义词: overconfidence, arrogance, hubris | 反义词: insecurity, self-doubt | 词性: noun

词源: "hyper-" (Greek: over, excessive) + "confidence" (Latin: confidentia, from confidere, to trust)
