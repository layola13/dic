console:/ˈkɒnsəʊl/ (UK) /kənˈsoʊl/ (US)| n. vt.|安慰，慰藉；控制台，操纵台|A console is a panel or unit that contains the controls for a machine, electrical system, computer, etc.  As a verb, it means to comfort someone at a time of grief or disappointment.|
例句：He tried to console her after her cat died. 他试图在她猫死后安慰她。

例句：The pilot adjusted the controls on the console. 飞行员调整了控制台上的控制器。

近义词：(n.) control panel, dashboard, instrument panel; (v.) comfort, solace, soothe|
反义词：(v.) distress, upset|
词性: noun, verb
