beguiling:/bɪˈɡaɪlɪŋ/
adjective
迷人的，诱人的
Charming and attractive, often in a deceptive way.
Her beguiling smile made him forget his worries. 她迷人的微笑让他忘记了烦恼。

The salesman had a beguiling manner that made customers trust him.  这个销售员有一种迷人的举止，让顾客信任他。

近义词:charming, enchanting, alluring, captivating, seductive
反义词:repulsive, off-putting, unappealing

词性:adjective

词源:From Middle English *begylen, from be- + gilen (“to deceive, beguile”).  The word's original meaning was closer to "deceiving," but it has evolved to emphasize the charming aspect of deception.
