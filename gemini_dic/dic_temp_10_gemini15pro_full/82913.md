gummatous:/ˈɡʌmətəs/| adj.|树胶样的，树胶肿的|Relating to or resembling a gumma, a soft, gummy tumor characteristic of the tertiary stage of syphilis.|The patient presented with gummatous lesions on his skin. 病人的皮肤上出现了树胶样病变。|Synonyms: gummy, rubbery, elastic|Antonyms: hard, rigid|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin gummatosus, from gumma (“gumma”) + -osus (suffix forming adjectives meaning "full of," "prone to," "like," or "characterized by").  Gumma itself is of uncertain origin, possibly from Late Latin gummi (“gum”), from Greek κόμμι (kómmmi).
