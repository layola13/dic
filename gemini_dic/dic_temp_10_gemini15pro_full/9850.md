aortae:/eɪˈɔːrtiː/| n. |主动脉（复数）|The plural form of "aorta," referring to the main artery carrying blood from the left ventricle of the heart to the rest of the body.  It's less commonly used than the singular form.|Dissecting aneurysms of the aortae are rare. 主动脉夹层动脉瘤很少见。|Near synonyms:  main arteries (more general), great arteries (more general)|Antonyms: None (anatomical term, no direct opposite)|Noun
