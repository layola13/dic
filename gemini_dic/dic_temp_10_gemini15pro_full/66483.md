exert:/ɪɡˈzɜːt/
verb
施加，运用，发挥
From Latin *exserere, present active infinitive of exsero (“I put forth, thrust out”), from ex (“out of, from”) + sero (“I join, connect; I sow, plant”).
例句：He exerted all his influence to get his son the job.  他动用了他所有的影响力为他儿子谋得这份工作。
例句：The company exerted pressure on its employees to accept the new contract. 公司向其员工施加压力，要求他们接受新合同。
近义词：apply, employ, utilize, wield
反义词：relax, ease, withdraw
词性：v.
