词源:  "Nonhereditariness" is formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to "hereditariness," which itself derives from "hereditary."  "Hereditary" comes from the Latin word "hereditarius," meaning "pertaining to inheritance," ultimately from "heres," meaning "heir."
