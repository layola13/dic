deoppilation:/diːˌɒpɪˈleɪʃən/
noun
脱毛，疏通毛孔
The act of clearing obstructions from pores or removing hair.
Deoppilation is sometimes used as a treatment for acne.  脱毛有时被用作治疗痤疮的方法。
Deoppilation can be achieved through various methods, including waxing and laser treatments.  脱毛可以通过多种方法实现，包括打蜡和激光治疗。
Synonym: epilation, hair removal
Antonym:  hypertrichosis (毛发增多)
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *deōppilātiō, from de- (removal) + ōppilātiō (obstruction, blockage), from ōppilāre (to stop up, obstruct).
