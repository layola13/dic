explication:/ˌɛksplɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 解释，说明，阐释 | A detailed explanation or interpretation of a text, theory, or idea. |  The professor's explication of the poem was very helpful. 教授对这首诗的讲解很有帮助。|  explanation, interpretation, elucidation, clarification | obfuscation, confusion, mystification | noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *explicātiōn- (stem of explicātiō), from Latin explicātus, past participle of explicāre (“to unfold, explain”).
