overmeekness:/ˌoʊvərˈmiːknəs/
|n.| 过分温顺，过分谦恭|The quality of being excessively meek; extreme submissiveness.|His overmeekness made him an easy target for bullies. 他过分的温顺使他很容易成为恃强凌弱者的目标。|
|近义词:submissiveness, humility, timidity|反义词: assertiveness, boldness, aggressiveness|词性:名词|


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + meekness.  "Over-" acts as an intensifier, implying an excessive degree of meekness. Meekness itself comes from the Old Norse *mjúkr meaning "soft."
