grueling:/ˈɡruːəlɪŋ/ /ˈɡruːlɪŋ/| adj.| 艰苦的，繁重费力的|Extremely tiring and demanding; exhausting.|The athletes faced a grueling 100-mile race. 运动员们面临着一场艰苦的100英里比赛。

The hike up the mountain was a grueling experience. 这次爬山是一次艰苦的经历。

|近义词：exhausting, arduous, strenuous, taxing, demanding|反义词：easy, effortless, simple, relaxing|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->

From the verb "gruel" (to punish or exhaust), which itself likely comes from the Old French word "grueler" (to torment).  The original sense of the word was related to physical punishment, but it evolved to encompass any kind of exhausting or demanding activity.
