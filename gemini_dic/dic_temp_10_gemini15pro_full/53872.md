discontinuity:/ˌdɪskɒntɪˈnjuːɪti/ /ˌdɪskɑːntɪˈnjuːɪti/| n.|不连续性，中断，间断|A break or interruption in the continuity of something.|The geological record shows several discontinuities, suggesting periods of upheaval.|近义词：break, interruption, gap, hiatus|反义词:continuity, connectedness|词性:名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin discontinuitatem, from discontinuus (“discontinuous”), from dis- (“not”) + continuus (“continuous”).
