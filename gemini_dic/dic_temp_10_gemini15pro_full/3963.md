agrostographical:/ˌæɡrɒstəˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
农业地理学的
Relating to agrostography, the study of the geographical distribution of grasses.
Agrostographical studies are crucial for understanding grassland ecosystems.  农业地理学研究对于理解草原生态系统至关重要。
The agrostographical survey revealed the presence of rare grass species in the region.  农业地理调查揭示了该地区存在稀有草种。
Synonyms: phytogeographical, geobotanical
Antonyms: None
词性: adjective
