garrulously:/ˈɡærələsli/ | adv. | 喋喋不休地，絮絮叨叨地 | In a garrulous manner; excessively talkative. | He spoke garrulously about his travels. 他喋喋不休地谈论他的旅行。| 近义词：loquaciously, volubly, talkatively | 反义词： taciturnly, succinctly, concisely | 词性: 副词

词源: Late Middle English: from Latin garrulus ‘talkative’ (from garrire ‘chatter’) + -ly.
