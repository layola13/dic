firkin:/ˈfɜːrkɪn/| n. |小桶，弗金（容量单位）|A firkin is a small cask used especially for liquids such as beer or ale. It is also a unit of volume, equal to a quarter of a barrel.|The pub served its best ale in firkins.|近义词：cask, keg, barrel|反义词:|名词
