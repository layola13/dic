fly-beam:/ˈflaɪ biːm/ | n. | 飞梁，飞桁 | A horizontal beam supported at only one end, or a beam projecting beyond its support, especially in a timber-framed building. | The fly-beam in the old barn sagged under the weight of the hay. 老谷仓里的飞梁在干草的重压下下垂了。| The architect incorporated a decorative fly-beam into the design of the new house.  建筑师在新房子的设计中加入了一根装饰性的飞梁。| 近义词: cantilever, jetty | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "supporting beam") | 词性: noun
