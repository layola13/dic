divergency:/daɪˈvɜːrdʒənsi/ /dɪˈvɜːrdʒənsi/| n. |分歧，差异，背离|A difference or variation between things; the act of diverging.|The divergency of their opinions led to a heated debate. 他们的意见分歧导致了一场激烈的辩论。|近义词：difference, disagreement, deviation, variance, disparity|反义词：convergence, agreement, similarity, uniformity|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *divergentia, from Latin divergō (“I diverge”) + -ia.
