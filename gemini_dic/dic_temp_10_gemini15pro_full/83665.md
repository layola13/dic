haffle:/ˈhæfəl/| v.|拖延，推诿；支吾其词；胡扯|To prevaricate or speak evasively; to waffle.  The witness haffled when asked about his whereabouts on the night of the crime.|例句：He haffled for a few minutes before finally admitting he had lost the money. 他支吾了几分钟，最后才承认他把钱弄丢了。

例句：She haffled her way through the interview, never directly answering the interviewer's questions.  她在面试中含糊其辞，从来没有直接回答面试官的问题。|近义词：prevaricate, equivocate, waffle, stall, hedge|反义词: be forthright, be direct|词性: verb
