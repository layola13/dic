gallature:/ɡæˈlætʃər/ | n. |瘿瘤；虫瘿|A gall, especially one made by insects.  A swelling of plant tissue caused by insects, mites, fungi, or bacteria.| The oak leaves were covered in gallatures. 橡树叶上布满了虫瘿。 |Synonym: gall, cecidium| Antonym: (No direct antonym, could consider "healthy plant tissue") | Noun
