enswathement:/ɪnˈsweɪðmənt/
n.
包裹，缠绕
the act of wrapping or swathing something, especially a baby, tightly in cloth
例句：The enswathement of the newborn in a soft blanket provided comfort and warmth.  新生儿被包裹在柔软的毯子里，感到舒适和温暖。

例句：The artist's work explored the themes of confinement and enswathement.  这位艺术家的作品探讨了禁锢和束缚的主题。

近义词：wrapping, swaddling, enveloping, shrouding
反义词：unwrapping, uncovering, exposing
词性：noun
