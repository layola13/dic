evaporation:/ɪˌvæpəˈreɪʃn/ | n. | 蒸发；消失 | The process by which a liquid changes to a gas or vapor. | The evaporation of water from the lake cools the surrounding air. 湖水蒸发使周围的空气冷却。| Synonyms: vaporization, dehydration, drying | Antonyms: condensation, precipitation | noun
