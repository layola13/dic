flickered:/ˈflɪkərd/ (US), /ˈflɪkəd/ (UK)| v.|闪烁，忽隐忽现，摇曳|To shine or burn unsteadily; to appear briefly or intermittently.|The candle flickered in the wind.  蜡烛在风中摇曳。|The images flickered on the screen.  图像在屏幕上闪烁。|synonyms: shimmer, glimmer, flutter, waver|antonyms: steady, constant, remain, persist|verb
