heritability:/ˌhɛrɪtəˈbɪləti/ /ˌhɛrɪdəˈbɪlədi/| n.|遗传力，遗传性|The proportion of variation among individuals that we can attribute to genes. The heritability of a trait may vary, depending on the range of populations and environments studied.|The heritability of intelligence is a controversial topic. 智力的遗传性是一个有争议的话题。|Synonyms: inheritability,  transmissibility|Antonyms: non-heritability (not a commonly used word)|noun
