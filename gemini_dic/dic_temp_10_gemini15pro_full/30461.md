centrifugal force:/ˌsɛnˈtrɪfjʊɡəl fɔːrs/ /ˌsɛnˈtrɪfjuɡl fɔːrs/ | n. | 离心力 | An apparent force that acts outward on a body moving around a center, arising from the body's inertia. | The centrifugal force pushes the passengers outwards when the car turns sharply. 汽车急转弯时，离心力将乘客向外推。 | synonyms: outward force, inertial force | antonyms: centripetal force | noun

centrifugal forces:/ˌsɛnˈtrɪfjʊɡəl ˈfɔːrsɪz/ /ˌsɛnˈtrɪfjuɡl ˈfɔːrsɪz/ | n. | 离心力 | The plural form of centrifugal force, referring to multiple instances of this apparent outward force. | The centrifugal forces acting on the rotating blades keep them extended. 作用在旋转叶片上的离心力使它们保持伸展状态。| synonyms: outward forces, inertial forces | antonyms: centripetal forces | noun
