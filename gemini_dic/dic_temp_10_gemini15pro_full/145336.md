overbrutalizes:/ˌoʊvərˈbruːtəˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
过度残暴对待，极度野蛮化
To brutalize excessively; to treat with extreme cruelty and violence beyond what is considered brutal.
The dictator's regime overbrutalized the political prisoners, subjecting them to horrific torture and inhumane conditions.  独裁者的政权过度残暴地对待政治犯，使他们遭受可怕的酷刑和不人道的待遇。

The video game was criticized for overbrutalizing its depiction of violence, showing gratuitous gore and suffering.  这款电子游戏因其对暴力的过度残暴描写而受到批评，展现了无端的流血和痛苦。

Synonyms:  savages,  maltreats,  torments,  abuses
Antonyms:  cherishes,  protects,  nurtures,  comforts
词性: 动词
