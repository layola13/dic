epagoge:/ɛpəˈɡoʊdʒi/ /ɛpəˈɡɔːɡi/ | n. | 归纳法，归纳推理 |  A method of reasoning that moves from specific instances to a general conclusion. It is the opposite of deduction. | Example: Scientists often use epagoge to form hypotheses based on observed data. 科学家经常使用归纳法根据观察到的数据形成假设。

Example:  Through epagoge, she concluded that all swans are white after observing numerous white swans. 通过归纳法，她在观察了无数只白天鹅后得出结论，所有天鹅都是白色的。 | 近义词: induction, generalization | 反义词: deduction | 词性: noun
