gamin:/ˈɡæmɪn/| n.|流浪儿；顽童；小淘气|A child who spends their time playing on the streets, especially one who is homeless or neglected. Also used to describe a playful, mischievous, or boyish young woman. |The street gamin was quick and nimble, dodging through the bustling marketplace. 这个街头顽童动作敏捷，在熙熙攘攘的市场上穿梭自如。|Synonyms: urchin, waif, street child, imp, rogue, tomboy|Antonyms: well-behaved child, lady|noun
