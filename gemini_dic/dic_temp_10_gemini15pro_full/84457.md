halomancy:/ˈhæləˌmænsi/ | n. |盐占卜| Divination by means of salt.  It involves observing the patterns formed by salt crystals, or by interpreting the crackling sounds of salt thrown onto a fire. |  Ancient priests practiced halomancy to predict the future. 古代祭司通过盐占卜来预测未来。|  The villagers relied on halomancy to determine the best time for planting. 村民们依靠盐占卜来确定最佳播种时间。|  近义词: divination, fortune-telling | 反义词: None | noun
