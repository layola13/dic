criticizer: /ˈkrɪtɪsaɪzər/ | n. | 批评者，批评家 | Someone who expresses disapproval of someone or something. |  He's a harsh criticizer of the government's policies. 他是政府政策的严厉批评者。

The play attracted both admirers and criticizers.  这部戏剧既吸引了赞赏者，也吸引了批评者。


近义词: critic, faultfinder, detractor | 反义词: supporter, advocate, admirer | 词性: noun
