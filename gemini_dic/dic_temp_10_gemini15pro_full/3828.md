ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀγωνισμός (agōnismós, “contest, struggle”), from ἀγωνίζομαι (agōnízomai, “I contend for a prize”), from ἀγών (agṓn, “contest, struggle”). Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European root *h₂eǵ- (“to drive, draw”).
