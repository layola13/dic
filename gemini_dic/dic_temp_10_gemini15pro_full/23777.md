brawling: /ˈbrɔːlɪŋ/ | adj.| 喧闹的，爱打架的 |  Inclined to fight noisily and disruptively. |  A brawling drunk stumbled out of the bar. 一个爱闹事的醉汉从酒吧里踉跄而出。 | synonyms: quarrelsome, pugnacious, belligerent | antonyms: peaceful, calm | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *braulen*, from Old French *brailler* "to cry out," of imitative origin.
