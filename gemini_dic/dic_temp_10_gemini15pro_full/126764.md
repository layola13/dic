nanny:/ˈnæni/| n.|保姆；奶妈|A person, typically a woman, employed to care for a child or children in their own home.|例句：My sister works as a nanny for a family with two young children. 我的妹妹在一个有两个小孩的家庭里做保姆。

例句：The nanny took the children to the park. 保姆带孩子们去了公园。

近义词：childminder, au pair, babysitter|反义词: parent|词性: noun
