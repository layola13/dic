mourned:/mɔːrnd/ /mʊrnd/| v. |哀悼，悲痛|To feel or express deep sorrow for someone's death or a loss.|She mourned the death of her husband. 她哀悼她丈夫的去世。|The nation mourned the loss of its leader. 全国人民哀悼他们领袖的逝世。|近义词：grieved, lamented, sorrowed|反义词：celebrated, rejoiced|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *mournen*, from Old French *mornir* (Modern French *morner*), from Vulgar Latin *dolere*, from Latin *dolēre* ("to grieve, suffer, feel pain").
