partita:/pɑːrˈtiːtə/ | n. | 组曲；帕蒂塔 | A suite of related instrumental pieces, particularly common in the Baroque period.  A partita differs from a suite in that its movements are more closely related, often sharing thematic material. | Bach's partitas for keyboard are among his most famous works. 巴赫的键盘组曲是他最著名的作品之一。 | suite, set, series | symphony, concerto | noun

partita:/pɑːrˈtiːtə/ | n. |  比赛；(尤指)体育比赛(尤用于意大利语) | A match, game, or contest, especially in sports (primarily used in Italian).  | The football partita ended in a draw. 这场足球比赛以平局结束。| match, game, contest |  forfeit | noun
