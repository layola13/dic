nonprime:/ˌnɑːnˈpraɪm/ | adj. | 非质数的，合数的 | Not a prime number; composite.  A nonprime number can be divided evenly by a number other than 1 or itself. |  Example: 12 is a nonprime number because it is divisible by 2, 3, 4, and 6. 12是一个合数，因为它可以被2、3、4和6整除。| Example:  The security code uses a nonprime number for added complexity.  安全码使用了一个非质数来增加复杂性。 | Synonym: composite | Antonym: prime | word type: adjective
