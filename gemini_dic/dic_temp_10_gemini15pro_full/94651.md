impressionist:/ɪmˈprɛʃənɪst/| n. adj.| 印象主义者；印象派的|A painter, writer, or composer who is an exponent of impressionism.| Monet was a famous Impressionist. 莫奈是一位著名的印象派画家。|近义词：artist, painter, composer|反义词: realist|词性: noun, adjective

impressionistic:/ɪmˌprɛʃəˈnɪstɪk/| adj.| 印象派的；印象主义的|Resembling or suggestive of the techniques of impressionism.| The music was impressionistic and evocative. 这首音乐是印象派的，引人入胜。|近义词： evocative, suggestive, atmospheric|反义词: realistic|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *impressionniste*, from *impression* (“impression”) + *-iste* (suffix forming agent nouns).  *Impression* ultimately derives from Latin *impressio* (“a pressing into, impression”), from the verb *imprimō* (“I press into, impress”).
