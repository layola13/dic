|English Explanation: The quality of being excessively precise or overly attentive to minor details.  It often implies a tendency towards pedantry or an excessive focus on rules and regulations, sometimes to the detriment of practicality or common sense.|
|Example 1: Her overprecisenesses in following the recipe resulted in a dry and tasteless cake. 她一丝不苟地按照食谱操作，结果烤出了一个又干又没味的蛋糕。|

|Example 2: His overprecisenesses in grammar sometimes obscured the meaning of his writing. 他对语法的过度严谨有时反而掩盖了他写作的意义。|
