focuser:/ˈfoʊkəsər/ (US), /ˈfəʊkəsə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 调焦器；聚焦器；集中注意力的人或物 |A device that adjusts the focus of a beam of light or other radiation, or a person or thing that helps to concentrate attention on something.| The astronomer adjusted the focuser on the telescope to get a clearer image of the nebula. 天文学家调整了望远镜上的调焦器，以便更清晰地观察星云。| 近义词: focusing device, adjuster, concentrator | 反义词: diffuser, scatterer | 词性: 名词

focuser: /ˈfoʊkəsər/ (US), /ˈfəʊkəsə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 调焦者; 对焦者 | Someone or something that focuses or concentrates.| He is a real focuser and gets things done efficiently. 他是一个非常专注的人，做事效率很高。| 近义词: concentrator | 反义词: distractor | 词性: 名词
