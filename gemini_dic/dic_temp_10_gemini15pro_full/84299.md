half-volley:/ˈhɑːf ˌvɑli/ /ˈhæf ˌvɑli/| n.|半截击球|A shot in tennis or other racket sports where the ball is hit immediately after it bounces, before it has risen to its full height.|Federer played a superb half-volley to win the point. 费德勒打出一个漂亮的半截击球赢得了这一分。

|近义词: drop shot, volley|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, could consider 'lob')|noun

half-volley:/ˈhɑːf ˌvɑli/ /ˈhæf ˌvɑli/| v.|打半截击球|To hit a half-volley.|He half-volleyed the ball back over the net. 他把球半截击回网对面。

|近义词: volley|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, could consider 'lob')|verb
