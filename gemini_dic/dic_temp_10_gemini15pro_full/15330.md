backsliding: /ˈbækslaɪdɪŋ/
backsliding: n.
backsliding:  倒退，故态复萌，堕落
backsliding:  The act of reverting to bad habits or a worse state, especially after partial reformation.
backsliding: After years of sobriety, he experienced a period of backsliding and began drinking again. 多年戒酒后，他又故态复萌，开始酗酒。
backsliding: relapse, regression, lapse, deterioration
backsliding:  progress, improvement
