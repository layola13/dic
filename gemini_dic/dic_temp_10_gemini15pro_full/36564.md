collapsibility:/kəˌlæpsəˈbɪləti/| n. |可折叠性；可塌陷性|The quality of being easily collapsible; the tendency to collapse.|The collapsibility of the new stroller makes it easy to store.  这款新的婴儿车可折叠性很好，便于存放。|synonyms: foldability, portability |antonyms: rigidity, inflexibility |noun
