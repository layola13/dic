letuary:/ˈlɛtʃʊəri/ | n. | 药剂，糖浆，蜜剂 | A medicinal concoction, often sweet or syrupy, consisting of a mixture of powdered herbs or other ingredients in a base of honey, syrup, or molasses. |  In medieval times, letuaries were a common way to administer medicine. 在中世纪，药剂是常用的给药方式。| synonyms: electuary, confection, syrup, potion | antonyms: poison, toxin | noun

letuary:/ˈlɛtʃʊəri/ | n. | (比喻) 令人愉悦的事物  | Something, especially a piece of writing, that is soothing or delightful. | Her prose is a letuary for the soul. 她的散文是对灵魂的慰藉。 | synonyms: balm, solace, comfort, delight | antonyms: irritant, affliction | noun
