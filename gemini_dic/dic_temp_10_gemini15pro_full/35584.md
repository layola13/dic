coanchors:/ˈkoʊˌæŋkərz/| n.|共同主持人，联合主播|Two journalists coanchored the evening news. 两位记者共同主持了晚间新闻。|Example: The two coanchors had great chemistry on screen.  两位联合主播在屏幕上配合默契。|Synonyms: co-presenters, joint anchors|Antonyms: solo anchor, single presenter|Noun

coanchor:/ˈkoʊˌæŋkər/| v.|共同主持，联合主播|They decided to coanchor the morning show. 他们决定共同主持晨间节目。|Example:  Experienced journalists will coanchor the special report. 经验丰富的记者将联合主持特别报道。|Synonyms: co-present, jointly host|Antonyms: present solo, host alone|Verb
