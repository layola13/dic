amerciament:/əˈmɜːrsiəmənt/
n.
罚金，罚款
a pecuniary penalty; a fine imposed at the mercy of the court or other authority.
The amerciament for parking illegally was steep.  非法停车的罚款很重。
synonyms: fine, penalty, forfeiture, mulct
antonyms: reward, prize, bonus
noun
