nuttiest:/ˈnʌtɪɪst/| adj.|最疯狂的，最古怪的|superlative adjective of nutty; extremely silly, foolish, or strange.|例句：That's the nuttiest idea I've ever heard! 那是我听过的最疯狂的想法！|例句：He had the nuttiest collection of old cars. 他收藏了一堆最古怪的老爷车。|近义词：craziest, wildest, strangest, wackiest|反义词：sanest, most sensible|词性:形容词

nutty:/ˈnʌti/| adj.|疯狂的，古怪的；坚果味的|crazy, silly, or strange; having the flavor of nuts.|例句：He has some pretty nutty ideas. 他有一些非常疯狂的想法。|例句：This cake has a lovely nutty flavor. 这个蛋糕有一种可爱的坚果味。|近义词：crazy, eccentric, strange|反义词：sane, sensible|词性:形容词

词源: From nut (n.) + -y (adj. suffix).  The sense of "crazy" arose in the mid-20th century, perhaps from the idea of someone being "off their nut" (meaning "off their head" or "crazy").
