aberdeen: /ˌæbərˈdiːn/ (UK), /ˌæbərˈdin/ (US)| n. |阿伯丁|A port city in northeast Scotland. It is the third most populous city in Scotland.  |例句：I visited Aberdeen last summer and enjoyed the coastal scenery. 我去年夏天去了阿伯丁，很喜欢那里的海岸风光。

Aberdeen is known as the oil capital of Europe.  阿伯丁被称为欧洲的石油之都。|近义词：city, port, town|反义词:countryside|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Scottish Gaelic "Obar Dheathain", meaning "mouth of the River Dee".  来源于苏格兰盖尔语“Obar Dheathain”，意为“迪河口”。
