capitulation:/kəˌpɪtʃʊˈleɪʃn/
n.
投降，屈服
The act of surrendering or yielding; the terms of surrender.
After a long siege, the city finally announced its capitulation. 经过长时间的围困，这座城市最终宣布投降。
The enemy's capitulation came sooner than expected.  敌人的投降比预期的要早。
surrender, submission, yielding
resistance, defiance
noun
