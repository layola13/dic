panegyrically:/ˌpænɪˈdʒɪrɪkəli/
adverb
颂扬地，赞美地
In a manner expressing high praise or eulogy.
He spoke panegyrically about his mentor, praising his wisdom and guidance. 他颂扬了他的导师，赞扬他的智慧和指导。
The author panegyrically described the beauty of the natural landscape. 作者赞美了自然景观的美丽。
近义词:eulogistically, laudatorily, glowingly
反义词:critically, disparagingly, negatively
词性:adverb

词源:
From Late Latin *panegyricus + -ally. From Ancient Greek πανηγυρικός (panēgyrikós, “fit for a public assembly, festive”), from πανήγυρις (panḗgyris, “a public gathering, especially a festival”), from πᾶν (pân, “all”) + ἄγυρις (águris, “assembly”).
