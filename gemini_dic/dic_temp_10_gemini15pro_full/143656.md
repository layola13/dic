outkitchens:/ˈaʊtˌkɪtʃɪnz/
n.
户外厨房
An outdoor kitchen, typically located in a patio or garden, equipped with appliances and features for cooking and entertaining outdoors.
We built an outkitchen so we could enjoy cooking and dining outside during the summer. 我们建了一个户外厨房，这样我们就可以在夏天享受户外烹饪和用餐了。
