amalgamate:/əˈmælɡəˌmeɪt/ | v. | 合并，混合 | to combine or unite to form one organization or body | The two companies amalgamated to form a larger corporation.  两家公司合并成了一家更大的公司。| synonyms: merge, combine, unite, consolidate, blend | antonyms: separate, divide, split, disintegrate | verb

amalgamater:/əˈmælɡəˌmeɪtər/ | n. | 合并者，混合者 | a person or thing that amalgamates | The amalgamater successfully merged the three departments.  合并者成功地合并了三个部门。| synonyms: merger, unifier, consolidator | antonyms: separator, divider, splitter | noun

amalgamation:/əˌmælɡəˈmeɪʃn/ | n. | 合并，混合 | the action, process, or result of combining or uniting | The amalgamation of the two companies created a stronger entity. 两家公司的合并创造了一个更强大的实体。| synonyms: merger, union, consolidation, blend, mixture | antonyms: separation, division, split, disintegration | noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *amalgamare, from Latin amalgama (“amalgam”), from Medieval Greek μάλαγμα (málagma, “softening agent”).
