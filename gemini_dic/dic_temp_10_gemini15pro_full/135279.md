nonparochial:/ˌnɒnpəˈrəʊkiəl/ /ˌnɑːnpəˈroʊkiəl/ | adj. | 非教区的；不狭隘的 | Not limited to a narrow or local perspective; broad-minded; secular.  Not connected with or controlled by a church or religious group. | Example 1: Her nonparochial views on education allowed her to consider alternative learning environments. 她不狭隘的教育观使她能够考虑其他的学习环境。

Example 2: The university's nonparochial approach to research encourages interdisciplinary collaboration. 这所大学不狭隘的研究方法鼓励跨学科合作。 | Synonym: broad-minded, liberal, open-minded, cosmopolitan, secular, ecumenical, universal | Antonym: parochial, narrow-minded, insular, provincial, sectarian | word property: adjective
