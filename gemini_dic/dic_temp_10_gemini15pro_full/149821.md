paleolithic:/ˌpeɪlɪəˈlɪθɪk/| adj. n.| 旧石器时代的；旧石器时代|Relating to or denoting the early phase of the Stone Age, lasting about 2.5 million years, when primitive stone implements were used.|Paleolithic cave paintings depict scenes of hunting and daily life.  旧石器时代的洞穴壁画描绘了狩猎和日常生活的场景。|近义词：Stone Age, prehistoric|反义词：modern, contemporary|词性:形容词, 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *paléolithique*, from Greek *palaios* "old" + *lithos* "stone".
