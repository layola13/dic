floweriest:/ˈflaʊə.ri.ɪst/ |adj.|最华丽的；最辞藻华丽的|Having the most elaborate or ornate language; excessively flowery. |Her acceptance speech was the floweriest I've ever heard. 她的获奖感言是我听过的最华丽的。|The writer's floweriest prose obscured the simple message.  这位作家的华丽辞藻掩盖了简单的信息。| 近义词: ornate, elaborate, embellished, flamboyant |反义词: plain, simple, unadorned, austere|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From flower + -y + -est.  "Flower" in this context refers to figurative language, implying a blossoming of elaborate expression. "-y" forms an adjective meaning "full of" or "characterized by," and "-est" is the superlative suffix.
