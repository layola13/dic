deadline:/ˈdɛdlaɪn/| n. |截止日期，最后期限|A date or time by which something must be completed.|The deadline for submitting the application is May 1st. 申请提交的截止日期是5月1日。|Synonyms: due date, cutoff date, time limit|Antonyms: start date, commencement|noun
