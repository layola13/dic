banco:/ˈbæŋkoʊ/ (US), /ˈbæŋkəʊ/ (UK)| n.| 银行；(尤指赌博的)庄家；长凳，长椅|A financial institution that accepts deposits and channels the money into lending activities.  Also, in gambling, the person or entity that holds the stakes.|例句：I need to go to the banco to deposit some money. 我需要去银行存一些钱。

例句：The banco in this poker game is very aggressive.  这场扑克游戏中的庄家非常激进。

近义词：bank, financial institution (for financial sense); dealer, house (for gambling sense); bench, seat (for furniture sense)|反义词：(No direct antonym, depends on context)|词性:noun
