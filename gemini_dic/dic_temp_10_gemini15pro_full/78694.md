gigabyte: /ˈɡɪɡəˌbaɪt/ | n. | 千兆字节，十亿字节 | A unit of information equal to one billion bytes or 1024 megabytes. |  My new hard drive has a capacity of two terabytes, which is equivalent to 2048 gigabytes. 我的新硬盘容量为 2 TB，相当于 2048 GB。

| Example:  The file is too large; it's over 10 gigabytes.  这个文件太大了，超过 10GB 了。 | Synonym:  GB | Antonym:  kilobyte (KB), megabyte (MB) | noun
