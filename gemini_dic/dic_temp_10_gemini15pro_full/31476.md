charlock: /ˈtʃɑːrlɑːk/ | n. | 野芥菜，田芥菜 | A wild mustard plant (Sinapis arvensis) common as a weed in grain fields. It has yellow flowers and produces seeds that can contaminate crops.  | The field was infested with charlock. 这田里长满了野芥菜。| Synonyms: wild mustard, field mustard | Antonyms: cultivated plant | Noun

charlock: /ˈtʃɑːrlɑːk/ | n. | (figurative) Something unwanted or troublesome; a nuisance. | The constant spam emails were a charlock in his inbox.  不断涌入的垃圾邮件是他收件箱里的一大麻烦。 | Synonyms: nuisance, pest, bother | Antonyms: benefit, advantage | Noun
