nonstructurally:/ˌnɒnˈstrʌktʃərəli/
nonstructurally|adv.|非结构地，非构造地|Not related to or affecting the structure of something.  Specifically used in engineering and construction to refer to elements that do not contribute to the stability or load-bearing capacity of a building or other structure.|The building was nonstructurally damaged by the earthquake.  这栋建筑在地震中受到了非结构性破坏。|non-structurally|structurally
nonstructurally|adverb|非结构地|Modifying a verb, adjective, or another adverb to indicate that something is not related to or affecting the structure of something.|The walls were nonstructurally attached to the frame.  这些墙是非结构性地连接到框架上的。|non-structurally|structurally
