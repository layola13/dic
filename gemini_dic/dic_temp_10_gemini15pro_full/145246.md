overblanket: /ˈoʊvərˌblæŋkɪt/
Noun
过厚的毯子; 多余的毯子
A blanket that is too thick or heavy, or an unnecessary extra blanket.
The hotel room was stiflingly hot, as they had provided an overblanket in addition to the regular comforter.  酒店房间热得令人窒息，因为除了普通的羽绒被外，他们还提供了一条过厚的毯子。
The child kicked off the overblanket in the middle of the night.  孩子半夜把多余的毯子踢掉了。
Synonyms: extra blanket, thick blanket
Antonyms: thin blanket, light blanket
n.
