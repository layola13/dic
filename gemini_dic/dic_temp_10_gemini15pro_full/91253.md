hydroferrocyanic:/ˌhaɪdrəʊˌfɛrəʊsaɪˈænɪk/ | adj. |  氢铁氰的；六氰合铁(II)酸的 | Relating to or containing the anion [Fe(CN)₆]⁴⁻ derived from hydroferrocyanic acid (also called hexacyanoferrate(II)).  It forms intensely colored precipitates with most metal cations. |  Potassium hydroferrocyanide is used as an anti-caking agent. 氢铁氰化钾用作抗结块剂。| Synonym: hexacyanoferrate(II) | Antonym: hexacyanoferrate(III) (ferricyanic) | adjective
