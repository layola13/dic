overoperate:/ˌoʊvərˈɑːpəreɪt/ | v. |过度手术，过度治疗|To perform more surgical procedures than are medically necessary. | The doctor was accused of overoperating on his patients.  医生被指控对病人进行过度手术。| The surgeon tends to overoperate, performing unnecessary procedures.  这位外科医生倾向于过度手术，进行不必要的程序。 | 近义词: overtreat | 反义词: undertreat |词性:动词

词源: over- (prefix) + operate (verb)
"over-"  表示 "过度"， "过分"
"operate" 表示 "操作"，"手术"
