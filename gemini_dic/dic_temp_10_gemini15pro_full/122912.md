molters:/ˈmoʊltərz/| n.|蜕皮动物；换羽动物|Animals that are currently molting, shedding their outer layer (skin, feathers, etc.).|The molters were kept in a separate enclosure to avoid disturbing the other animals. 蜕皮的动物被单独饲养，以免打扰其他动物。|The crab molters are vulnerable during this period.  正在蜕壳的螃蟹在这个时期很脆弱。|近义词：shedders|反义词:|noun


molt:/moʊlt/| v.  n.|蜕皮；换羽|The process of shedding an outer covering such as skin, feathers, or hair, to be replaced by a new one.|The snake molted its skin. 蛇蜕了皮。|The birds molt their feathers in the fall. 鸟儿在秋天换羽。|近义词：shed, slough|反义词:|verb, noun
