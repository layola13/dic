ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *gravere*, from Anglo-Norman *gravour*, from Old French *graveur*, agent noun from *graver* (“to engrave”), from Latin *gravare* (“to burden, weigh down”), from *gravis* (“heavy”).  The adjective is a comparative form of *grave*.
