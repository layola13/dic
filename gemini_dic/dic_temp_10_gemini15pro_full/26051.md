bushido:/ˈbuːʃiːdoʊ/ /ˈbʊʃiːdoʊ/
n.
武士道，武士道精神
The code of the samurai, stressing loyalty, courage, and self-discipline.
He lived by the strictures of bushido. 他遵循武士道精神的戒律。
近义词: chivalry, code of honor
反义词: cowardice, dishonor
词性: noun

Etymology:
From Japanese 武士道 (bushidō), from 武士 (bushi, “warrior”) + 道 (dō, “way, path, code”).
