commandership:/kəˈmændərʃɪp/ /kəˈmɑːndərʃɪp/
n.
指挥权，统帅(职位)
The position or authority of a commander.  The skill or quality of being a commander.
He assumed commandership of the army. 他担任了军队的指挥官。
The king entrusted him with the commandership of the northern forces. 国王将北方军队的指挥权托付给了他。
Synonym: leadership, captaincy, control, authority
Antonym: subordination,  obedience
词性: 名词
