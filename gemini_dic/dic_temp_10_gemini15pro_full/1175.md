acclimate: /əˈklaɪmət/ (US) /əˈklaɪmeɪt/ (UK)| v. |适应，适应气候|To become accustomed to a new climate or environment.|The climbers needed a few days to acclimate to the high altitude. 登山者需要几天时间来适应高海拔。

The new employee acclimated quickly to the company culture.  新员工很快适应了公司文化。

|近义词：adapt, adjust, accustom|反义词: unaccustomed|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French acclimater, from à (to) + climat (climate). Ultimately from Greek klima (region, climate).

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He acclimates quickly to new situations. = 他很快就能适应新环境。
She acclimates easily to different cultures. = 她很容易适应不同的文化。


一般过去时:
It took me a while to acclimate to the heat. = 我花了一段时间才适应炎热的天气。
They acclimated to the local customs surprisingly fast. = 他们很快就适应了当地的风俗习惯，令人惊讶。


现在完成时:
The team has acclimated well to the new coach. = 团队已经很好地适应了新教练。
She has acclimated to the city life after living in the countryside for years. = 在乡下住了几年后，她已经适应了城市生活。


过去完成时:
By the time we arrived, they had already acclimated to the altitude. = 当我们到达时，他们已经适应了海拔高度。
He had acclimated to the cold weather before he moved to Alaska. = 在他搬到阿拉斯加之前，他已经适应了寒冷的天气。


将来时:
The puppies will acclimate to their new home soon. = 小狗们很快就会适应它们的新家。
I think I will acclimate to the new job quickly. = 我想我会很快适应新工作的。


虚拟语气:
If I were to move to a different country, I hope I would acclimate easily. = 如果我要搬到另一个国家，我希望我能轻松适应。
I wish I could acclimate to this humidity faster. = 我希望我能更快地适应这种湿度。


被动语态:
The plants are being acclimated to the greenhouse environment. = 这些植物正在适应温室环境。
The animals were acclimated to the new enclosure gradually. = 这些动物逐渐适应了新的围栏。


疑问句:
How long does it take to acclimate to a new time zone? = 适应新的时区需要多长时间？
Do you think he will acclimate well to the new school? = 你认为他会很好地适应新学校吗？


否定句:
She didn't acclimate to the new job and decided to quit. = 她没有适应新工作，决定辞职。
I haven't acclimated to the spicy food here yet. = 我还没有适应这里的辛辣食物。



PHRASE->
acclimate to (something) = 适应（某事）
acclimate oneself to (something) = 使自己适应（某事）
become acclimated to = 适应
fully acclimated = 完全适应
slowly acclimating = 慢慢适应
