mired:/maɪərd/ | adj. | 陷入困境的；深陷泥潭的 | Stuck in a difficult situation, typically one that involves a lot of problems or confusion. | The company is mired in debt. 这家公司深陷债务危机。 |  bogged down, entangled, trapped | free, liberated | adjective


mired:/maɪərd/ | v. | 使…陷入困境；使…深陷泥潭 (mire的过去式和过去分词) | To cause someone or something to become stuck in a difficult situation. |  The car got mired in the mud. 汽车陷在泥里了。| bog down, entangle, trap | free, liberate | verb



ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *miren, from Old Norse *mȳrr, from Proto-Germanic *mūriją (“bog, swamp”). Cognate with Dutch moer (“mire”), German Moor (“moor”).
