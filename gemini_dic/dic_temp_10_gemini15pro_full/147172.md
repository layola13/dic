overlubricates:/ˌoʊvərˈluːbrɪkeɪts/
verb
过度润滑
Applies too much lubricant.  The act of lubricating something excessively.
The mechanic overlubricated the engine, causing it to malfunction.  机械师给发动机过度润滑，导致它发生故障。
overgreases, oversaturates
