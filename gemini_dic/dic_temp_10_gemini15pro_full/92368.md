hypochondrias:/ˌhaɪpəˈkɒndriəs/ /ˌhaɪpəˈkɑːndriəs/| n. |疑病症，臆想病|A psychological disorder characterized by excessive worry about having a serious illness, even when there are no medical findings to support the concern.  It involves misinterpreting minor bodily sensations or functions as signs of severe disease.|He suffers from hypochondrias and is always convinced he has some terrible disease.|近义词：hypochondria, health anxiety, illness anxiety disorder|反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider) robust health, well-being|词性:noun
