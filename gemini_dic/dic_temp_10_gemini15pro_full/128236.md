neurofilament: /ˌnjʊərəˈfɪləmənt/ | n. | 神经丝 | A type of intermediate filament found in neurons, providing structural support to axons and regulating axon diameter. | Example 1: Neurofilaments are essential for the proper functioning of nerve cells.  神经丝对神经细胞的正常运作至关重要。

Example 2:  Damage to neurofilaments can lead to neurological disorders.  神经丝损伤会导致神经系统疾病。 | Synonym: intermediate filament (in this context) | Antonym:  N/A | noun
