nonpublic:/ˌnɒnˈpʌblɪk/ | adj. | 非公开的，私有的 | Not open to or available to the general public; private. |  The meeting was nonpublic, only for board members.  会议是非公开的，仅限董事会成员参加。|  The company's nonpublic information was leaked. 该公司的非公开信息被泄露了。| 近义词：private, confidential, proprietary | 反义词：public, open | 词性: 形容词

词源:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + public (from Latin publicus)
