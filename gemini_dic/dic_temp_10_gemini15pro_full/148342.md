overspecializations: /ˌoʊvərˌspɛʃələˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 过度专业化；过分专门化 | The act of specializing to an excessive degree, often resulting in a lack of adaptability or broader understanding. |  The overspecialization of skills in the modern workforce can lead to difficulties when companies restructure. 现代劳动力的技能过度专业化会导致公司重组时出现困难。

Overspecialization in medical training can sometimes lead to doctors overlooking common ailments. 医学培训的过度专业化有时会导致医生忽视常见疾病。 | 近义词: overspecialisation, hyperspecialization | 反义词: generalization, diversification | 词性: noun
