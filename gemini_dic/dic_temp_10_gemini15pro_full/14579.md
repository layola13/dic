avicula:/əˈvɪkjʊlə/ | n. | 小鸟，小翼 |A small bird or a small wing.  Derived from Latin "avis" meaning "bird." Often used in scientific or poetic contexts to refer to a small, delicate bird. | The avicula flitted among the blossoms. 小鸟在花丛中飞舞。| 近义词: bird, birdie, fledgling | 反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps large bird or predator) | 词性: noun
