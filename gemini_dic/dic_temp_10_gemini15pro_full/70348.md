fiending:/ˈfiːndɪŋ/| n. | 渴望，瘾; 痴迷 | An intense craving or desire, especially for something addictive. | He had a fiending for a cigarette. 他非常想抽烟。| synonyms: craving, yearning, hunger, thirst, jones, itch | antonyms: aversion, distaste, indifference | noun


fiending:/ˈfiːndɪŋ/| adj. |(非正式)极度渴望的; 痴迷的 |  (Informal) Having an intense craving or desire. | She was fiending for a new pair of shoes. 她非常想要一双新鞋。| synonyms: craving, yearning, desperate | antonyms: indifferent, apathetic | adjective
