musculatures:/ˈmʌskjələtʃərz/
n.
肌肉组织；肌肉系统
The system or arrangement of muscles in a body or body part; the muscular apparatus.
The athlete's musculature was incredibly well-developed.  这位运动员的肌肉组织非常发达。
近义词：muscles, muscular system
反义词：(没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用weakness 虚弱)
词性: 名词

词源:
Late 19th century: from French musculature, from Latin musculus ‘muscle’.
