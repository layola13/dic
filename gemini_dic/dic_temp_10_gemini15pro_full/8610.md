anticoagulating:/ˌæntikoʊˈæɡjəleɪtɪŋ/
adjective|抗凝的|Preventing or reducing coagulation of the blood.|例句：The patient was given anticoagulating medication to prevent blood clots.  病人被给予抗凝药物以防止血栓形成。|近义词：antithrombotic|反义词: coagulating, procoagulant|词性: adjective


anticoagulant:/ˌæntikoʊˈæɡjələnt/
noun|抗凝剂|A substance that prevents or reduces coagulation of the blood.|例句：Warfarin is a commonly used anticoagulant.  华法林是一种常用的抗凝剂。|近义词：antithrombotic|反义词: coagulant, procoagulant|词性: noun


词源:
anti- (prefix): against, opposing (来自希腊语 anti-)
coagulate (verb): to change from a liquid to a thickened or solid state (来自拉丁语 coagulare)
-ing (suffix): forms the present participle of verbs, and can also function as an adjective
