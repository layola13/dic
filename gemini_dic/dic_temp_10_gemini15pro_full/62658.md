engagednesses:/ɪnˈɡeɪdʒɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
投入，参与，订婚（状态）（复数）
The state of being engaged or involved in something; the degree or extent of engagement or involvement.  This word is rarely used and considered awkward.  It's better to use "engagement" or a more specific term.
Their engagednesses in the project varied greatly. 他们对这个项目的投入程度差别很大。 (This example is grammatically correct but sounds unnatural.)
近义词：engagement, involvement, commitment, dedication
反义词：apathy, indifference, detachment
词性: noun
