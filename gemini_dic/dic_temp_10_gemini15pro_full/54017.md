disculpatory:/dɪˈskʌlpətɔːri/
adjective
辩解的，开脱罪责的
Clearing or tending to clear from blame or guilt.
The lawyer presented disculpatory evidence in an attempt to free his client.  律师出示了辩解性证据，试图让他的客户脱罪。
The defendant offered a disculpatory statement to the police. 被告向警方提供了一份辩解声明。

近义词:exonerative, justificatory, vindicating, absolving
反义词:incriminating, accusatory

词性:adjective
词源:From Late Latin *disculpātōrius, from Latin disculpāre (to free from blame) + -tōrius.
