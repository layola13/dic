daycare:/ˈdeɪˌker/| n.|日托；托儿所；日间护理|A place where young children are cared for during the day while their parents are at work.|例句：My son goes to daycare three days a week.  我的儿子一周去三天日托。

例句：The daycare provides a stimulating environment for the children. 这家日托中心为孩子们提供了一个充满刺激的环境。|近义词：childcare, nursery school, preschool, creche |反义词: None|noun
