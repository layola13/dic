broish:/ˈbrɔɪʃ/ | adj. |  稍微的，有点 |  A Scottish and Northern English dialect word meaning slightly or a little.  It often describes a feeling or state of being. | I'm feeling a bit broish today. 我今天感觉有点不舒服。|  He was looking a wee bit broish after the long journey.  长途旅行后，他看起来有点疲惫。| 近义词: slightly, a little, somewhat, rather, kind of | 反义词: very, extremely, completely | 词性:形容词

词源:  Likely derived from the Scots word "broch," meaning weak or frail.
