interfactional:/ˌɪntərˈfækʃənəl/| adj.|派系间的，派系斗争的|Existing or occurring between factions (within an organization).|例句：The interfactional rivalry threatened to tear the party apart.  派系间的竞争有可能把这个政党分裂。

例句：Interfactional disputes hindered the progress of the project.  派系间的纠纷阻碍了项目的进展。|近义词：internal, sectarian|反义词：unified, harmonious|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From inter- (between) + faction (a dissenting group within a larger one) + -al (forming adjectives).  The word "faction" ultimately comes from Latin "factio" (a making, a doing).
