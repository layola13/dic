nonregressions:/ˌnɒn rɪˈɡrɛʃənz/ | n. | 非回归 |The absence of regressions; the state of not having regressed or deteriorated in functionality or performance.  Specifically in software development, it refers to ensuring that new code changes do not break existing functionality. | We run extensive nonregression tests before releasing any new software updates. 我们在发布任何新的软件更新之前都会运行大量的非回归测试。 | synonyms: stability, maintenance of function, consistency | antonyms: regressions, deterioration, decline | noun

nonregression:/ˌnɒn rɪˈɡrɛʃən/ | adj. | 非回归的 | Relating to or designed to prevent regressions; ensuring that new changes do not negatively impact existing functionality.  Commonly used in the context of software testing. | We employ nonregression testing strategies to maintain the integrity of our codebase. 我们采用非回归测试策略来维护代码库的完整性。| synonyms: preventative, protective, safeguarding | antonyms: regressive, deteriorating, declining | adjective
