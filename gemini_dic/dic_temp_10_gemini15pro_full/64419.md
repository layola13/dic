ergonomically:/ˌɜːrɡəˈnɒmɪkli/ /ˌɜːrɡəˈnɑːmɪkli/
| adv. | 人体工程学地；符合人体工程学地 | Designed to minimize physical strain and maximize efficiency and comfort in the working environment, based on the principles of ergonomics. | This chair is ergonomically designed to support your back. 这把椅子符合人体工程学设计，可以支撑你的背部。 | The keyboard is ergonomically positioned to prevent wrist strain.  这个键盘符合人体工程学定位，可以防止手腕劳损。 | comfortably, efficiently | uncomfortably, awkwardly | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From ergonomic + -ally.  Ergonomic comes from Greek ergon "work" + nomos "law".
