oary:/ˈɔːri/ | adj. | 桨的；有桨的；像桨的 | Resembling or having oars; propelled by oars. | The boat was oary, with two large oars on each side.  这艘船有桨，两侧各有两个大桨。 |  The Viking longship, oary and swift, raided the coastal villages.  维京人的长船，桨叶众多，速度飞快，袭击了沿海村庄。 | 近义词: oar-like, rowed | 反义词: oarless | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From oar + -y.  Oar comes from Old English *ār, from Proto-Germanic *ōrōm.
