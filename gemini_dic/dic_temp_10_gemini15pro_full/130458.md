nonabsorption:/ˌnɒnəbˈsɔːrpʃn/ |n.| 非吸收; 不吸收|The nonabsorption of nutrients can lead to health problems. 营养物质的不吸收会导致健康问题。|The experiment demonstrated the nonabsorption of the liquid by the material.  实验表明该材料不吸收液体。|近义词: non-assimilation, failure to absorb|反义词: absorption, assimilation|词性: 名词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + absorption (from Latin absorbere, "to swallow up")
