disconfirmable:/ˌdɪskənˈfɜːrməbl/ | adj. | 可证伪的，可反驳的 |  Able to be proven false or wrong; capable of being refuted by evidence. |  The scientist proposed a disconfirmable theory, allowing for the possibility that future experiments might prove it wrong.  科学家提出了一个可证伪的理论，允许未来实验可能证明它是错误的。 | synonyms: refutable, falsifiable, testable | antonyms:  unfalsifiable, irrefutable, unquestionable | word type: adjective

disconfirmable:/ˌdɪskənˈfɜːrməbl/ | adj. | 可证伪的，可反驳的 | Able to be disproven or shown to be false.  This hypothesis is disconfirmable through empirical observation. 这个假设可以通过经验观察来证伪。 | synonyms: refutable, falsifiable | antonyms: irrefutable, verifiable | word type: adjective
