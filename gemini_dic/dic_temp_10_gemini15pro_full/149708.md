palaeographer:/ˌpeɪliˈɒɡrəfər/ /ˌpælɪˈɑːɡrəfər/
n.
古文字学家；古文书学家
A scholar who studies ancient writing systems and manuscripts.
例句：The palaeographer dated the manuscript to the 12th century.  古文字学家将这份手稿的年代确定为12世纪。

例句：The palaeographer deciphered the faded inscription on the ancient tomb. 古文字学家破译了古墓上褪色的铭文。

近义词：paleographer,  scrivener,  archivist
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps) forger
