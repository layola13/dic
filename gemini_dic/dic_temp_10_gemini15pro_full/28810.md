carotenes:/ˈkærəˌtiːnz/ | n. |胡萝卜素|Any of several orange or red pigments found in carrots and many other plants, important in animal diets as a source of vitamin A. | Carrots are a good source of carotenes. 胡萝卜是胡萝卜素的良好来源。| Carotenes are essential for healthy vision. 胡萝卜素对健康视力至关重要。| 近义词: carotenoids | 反义词: None | noun
