n.
Alcanna Inc. (formerly Liquor Stores N.A. Ltd.)
阿尔坎纳公司（前身为北美酒类商店有限公司）
Alcanna Inc. was a Canadian private liquor retailer. It was acquired by Sundial Growers in 2022.
阿尔坎纳公司是一家加拿大私营酒类零售商。它于2022年被Sundial Growers收购。
Example: Alcanna operated numerous liquor stores across Canada.  阿尔坎纳公司在加拿大各地经营着许多酒类商店。
Example: The acquisition of Alcanna by Sundial Growers marked a significant shift in the Canadian cannabis market.  Sundial Growers 收购 Alcanna 标志着加拿大 cannabis 市场发生了重大转变。
Synonym: liquor retailer, alcohol retailer
近义词：酒类零售商
Antonym:  N/A (Proper noun)
反义词：无（专有名词）
