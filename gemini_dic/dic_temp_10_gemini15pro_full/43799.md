crenate:/ˈkriːneɪt/| adj.|圆齿状的，钝锯齿状的|Having a scalloped or rounded toothed edge, like a leaf.|The leaf margin was crenate, with small rounded teeth.|近义词：scalloped, serrated, notched|反义词:smooth, entire|adjective

crenation:/kriːˈneɪʃn/| n.|圆齿状，钝锯齿状，（细胞）收缩|The process of becoming crenate, or having a scalloped edge, especially of cells shrinking in a hypertonic solution. |The crenation of the red blood cells was visible under the microscope. |近义词:shrinking, contracting|反义词:swelling, expanding|noun
