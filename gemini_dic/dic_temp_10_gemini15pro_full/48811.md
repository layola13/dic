defluoridation:/diːˌflʊərɪˈdeɪʃən/| n. |除氟，脱氟|The process of removing excess fluoride from water.  This is important in areas where water naturally contains high levels of fluoride, which can lead to dental and skeletal fluorosis.|例句：Defluoridation of drinking water is crucial in some regions to prevent health problems.  在一些地区，饮用水的除氟对于预防健康问题至关重要。

例句：Various methods of defluoridation are used depending on the fluoride concentration and the resources available. 根据氟化物浓度和可用资源，使用各种除氟方法。|近义词：fluoride removal|反义词:fluoridation|词性:noun
