nontangential:/ˌnɒntænˈdʒɛnʃəl/
adjective
非切线的；直接的；相关的
Relating to or along a line that is not tangent to a curve or surface; directly related or relevant to the subject.
His questions were nontangential and focused on the core issues. 他的问题直切主题，集中在核心问题上。

The lawyer's nontangential questioning quickly revealed the truth.  律师直接的提问很快就揭示了真相。

近义词:relevant, pertinent, direct, straightforward
反义词:tangential, irrelevant, digressive, circuitous
词性:adjective
