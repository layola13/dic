duma:/ˈduːmə/ | n. | 杜马（俄罗斯议会）| A legislative body in Russia, specifically the lower house of the Federal Assembly. |  Example 1: The Duma passed a new law regarding education. 杜马通过了一项新的教育法。

Example 2:  The upcoming Duma elections are expected to be closely contested. 即将举行的杜马选举预计将竞争激烈。 | Synonym: parliament, assembly, legislature | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "executive branch") | noun

Etymology: From Russian дума (duma), meaning "thought," "consideration," from думать (dumat') "to think," from Proto-Slavic *dumati. |
