gorget:/ˈɡɔːrdʒɪt/ | n. | 护喉, (尤指古代士兵的)金属护喉, (鸟的)彩色喉部羽毛 | A piece of armor protecting the throat, especially one worn by medieval soldiers; a patch of colored feathers on the throat of a bird. | The knight polished his gorget until it gleamed. 骑士把他的护喉擦得闪闪发光。| The hummingbird's gorget shimmered in the sunlight.  蜂鸟的彩色喉部羽毛在阳光下闪闪发光。 | synonyms: neck armor, throat guard, bib, ruff (for birds) | antonyms:  N/A | noun
