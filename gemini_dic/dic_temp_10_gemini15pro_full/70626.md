filoplumaceous:/ˌfɪləʊpluːˈmeɪʃəs/
adjective
丝状羽的，毛状羽的
Having or resembling filoplumes; covered with filoplumes.  These feathers are hairlike or downy.
例句：The bird's filoplumaceous feathers provided excellent insulation.  这种鸟的丝状羽提供了极佳的保温效果。

例句：The chick's skin was covered in a fine, filoplumaceous down. 这只雏鸟的皮肤覆盖着一层细密的绒毛。

近义词：downy, hairlike, plumaceous
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider) pennaceous (referring to flight feathers)
