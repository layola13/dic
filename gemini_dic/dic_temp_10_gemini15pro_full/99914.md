internationalizing:/ˌɪntərˈnæʃənəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
国际化
The process of making something suitable for use in different countries.
Many companies are internationalizing their products to reach a wider market. 许多公司正在将其产品国际化，以开拓更广阔的市场。
The software company is internationalizing its user interface.  这家软件公司正在将其用户界面国际化。
近义词:globalizing, localizing
反义词: restricting, limiting
词性: verb (动名词形式)

Etymology:
From international + -ize + -ing.  "International" ultimately derives from Latin "inter" (between) and "natio" (nation).
