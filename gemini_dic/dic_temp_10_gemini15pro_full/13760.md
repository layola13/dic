audacity:/ɔːˈdæsɪti/ /ɑːˈdæsɪti/| n.|大胆；鲁莽；厚颜无耻|A willingness to take bold risks.  The state or quality of being audacious, especially with regard to action or behavior.|She had the audacity to ask for a raise after only a week on the job. 她竟然敢在工作才一周后就要求加薪。|Synonyms: boldness, daring, nerve, chutzpah, temerity|Antonyms: timidity, shyness, meekness, humility|noun
