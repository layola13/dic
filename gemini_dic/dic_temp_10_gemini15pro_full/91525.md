hyoscyamine:/ˌhaɪoʊˈsaɪəmiːn/| n.|莨菪碱|A poisonous crystalline alkaloid, C₁₇H₂₃NO₃, obtained from henbane and other plants of the nightshade family, used as an anticholinergic and antispasmodic.|Hyoscyamine is used to treat stomach and intestinal cramping. 莨菪碱用于治疗胃肠痉挛。|Synonyms:  atropine sulfate, scopolamine|Antonyms: N/A|noun
