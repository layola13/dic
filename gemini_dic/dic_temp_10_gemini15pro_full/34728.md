clickless:/ˈklɪklɪs/| adj.|无点击的，无需点击的|Relating to or denoting a system or device that does not require the user to click a mouse or other pointing device.|This new clickless interface allows users to navigate with just their eyes.|Near synonyms: hands-free, automatic, touchless|Antonyms: manual, click-based|adjective
