overmortgages:/ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrɡɪdʒɪz/
n.
超额抵押，过度抵押
A situation where the total amount of mortgages secured on a property exceeds its market value.  This can occur when property values decline, leaving the borrower owing more than the property is worth.
The recent housing market crash has led to a surge in overmortgages. 近期的房地产市场崩盘导致超额抵押激增。
近义词: excessive mortgages
反义词: undermortgages
词性: noun


overmortgage:/ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrɡɪdʒ/
v.
超额抵押，过度抵押 (To secure a mortgage on a property for an amount exceeding its value.)
Some homeowners were encouraged to overmortgage their properties during the boom years. 在经济繁荣时期，一些房主被鼓励过度抵押他们的房产。
近义词: overborrow
反义词: undermortgage
词性: verb
