outdoer:/ˌaʊtˈdɔːrər/
noun
户外活动爱好者
A person who enjoys spending time outdoors, especially engaging in recreational activities.
She's a true outdoer and loves hiking and camping. 她是一个真正的户外活动爱好者，喜欢徒步旅行和野营。
近义词: nature lover, outdoorsman, outdoorswoman
反义词: homebody, indoorsy person
词性:n.
