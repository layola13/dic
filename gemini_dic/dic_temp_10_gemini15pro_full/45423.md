cuprous:/ˈkjuːprəs/| adj.|亞銅的，一價銅的|Containing or relating to copper, especially with valence 1.  It refers to compounds containing copper in its +1 oxidation state.|例句：Cuprous oxide is a red powder. 氧化亞銅是一種紅色粉末。|例句：Cuprous chloride is a white solid. 氯化亞銅是一種白色固體。|近義詞：copper(I)|反义词：cupric|詞性:形容詞
