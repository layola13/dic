enliveningly:/ɪnˈlaɪvənɪŋli/ | adv. | 令人振奋地，活泼地 |  Making something more lively or interesting; in a way that makes something more lively or interesting. | Her speech was delivered enliveningly, captivating the audience. 她的演讲生动活泼，吸引了观众。|  animatedly, stimulatingly, vibrantly | dully, boringly, tediously | adverb

Etymology:
From enliven + -ing + -ly.  "Enliven" comes from the Old French "enlivner," meaning "to give life to," from "en-" (in) + "live" (alive).
