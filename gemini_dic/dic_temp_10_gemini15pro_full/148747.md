overtrusting:/ˌoʊvərˈtrʌstɪŋ/
adjective
过于信任的
inclined to trust others too readily or easily; too credulous.
She was overtrusting and naive, which made her an easy target for con artists. 她过于信任别人且天真，这使她很容易成为骗子的目标。
近义词:credulous, naive, gullible
反义词:suspicious, wary, cautious
词性:adjective
