liberating:/ˈlɪbəreɪtɪŋ/| adj.|解放的，使人自由的|Making one feel free and unrestrained.|例句：The experience of traveling alone can be incredibly liberating.  独自旅行的经历可以令人难以置信地感到自由。

例句：Breaking free from the constraints of societal expectations can be a truly liberating experience.  摆脱社会期望的束缚可以是一次真正解放的经历。|近义词：freeing, emancipating, empowering|反义词：restricting, confining, oppressive|词性:形容词

词源: From the Latin "liberare" (to free), derived from "liber" (free).
