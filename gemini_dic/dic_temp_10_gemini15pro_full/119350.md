microcinematographic: /ˌmaɪkroʊˌsɪnɪmætəˈɡræfɪk/
adjective
显微电影摄影的;显微电影术的
Relating to or involving the making of films through a microscope.
This microcinematographic study reveals the intricate movements of cells.  这项显微电影摄影研究揭示了细胞的复杂运动。
Microcinematographic techniques were employed to capture the process of cell division.  显微电影摄影技术被用来捕捉细胞分裂的过程。
近义词: microscopic filming, microphotographic
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider macroscopic)
词性: adjective
