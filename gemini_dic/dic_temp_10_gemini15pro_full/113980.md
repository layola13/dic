magically:/ˈmædʒɪkli/ | adv. |神奇地，魔术般地 | In a way that resembles magic; as if by magic. | The door magically opened. 门神奇地打开了。| The pain magically disappeared. 疼痛神奇地消失了。 | 近义词: miraculously, enchantingly, supernaturally, mysteriously | 反义词: normally, ordinarily, naturally | 词性: 副词

词源:
From magic + -al + -ly.  Ultimately from the Greek *magikos* (magical), related to *magos* (magician, a member of a priestly caste of ancient Persia).
