gatekeeper:/ˈɡeɪtˌkiːpər/| n.|守门人，看门人；把关人|A person who controls access to something, whether it's a physical place or information.|The gatekeeper wouldn't let us in without a ticket.  守门人不让我们进去，因为我们没有票。|The editor acts as a gatekeeper, deciding which stories get published. 编辑充当把关人，决定哪些故事可以发表。|Synonyms: guardian, custodian, watchman, controller|Antonyms:  None (The concept doesn't have a direct opposite, but one might consider intruder or trespasser as conceptually opposing actions.)|noun
