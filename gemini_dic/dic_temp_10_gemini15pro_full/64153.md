equalizer:/ˈiːkwəlaɪzər/| n.|均衡器，平衡器|A device that allows you to adjust the balance of different sound frequencies.|The sound engineer adjusted the equalizer to boost the bass. 音响工程师调节均衡器以增强低音。|synonyms: balancer, leveler, regulator|antonyms: amplifier, booster (in some contexts)|noun

equalizer:/ˈiːkwəlaɪzər/| n.|平衡因素，使平等之物|Something that makes things equal or similar.|Education is seen as a great equalizer in society. 教育被视为社会中伟大的平衡因素。|synonyms: leveler,  compensator|antonyms: differentiator, discriminator|noun
