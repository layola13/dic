normalizes:/ˈnɔːməlaɪzɪz/
verb
使正常化；使标准化
To bring or return something to a normal or standard condition or state.
The new medication normalizes blood pressure.  这种新药能使血压恢复正常。
The peace treaty normalized relations between the two countries. 和平条约使两国关系正常化。
standardizes, regulates, adjusts
deviates, distorts, perverts
词性: 动词(verb)

normalizes:/ˈnɔːrməlaɪzɪz/
verb
使(数据)标准化
To adjust data to a common scale or standard.
The software normalizes the audio levels.  该软件将音频电平标准化。
The researchers normalized the data to account for differences in sample size. 研究人员对数据进行了标准化处理，以考虑样本大小的差异。
standardizes, calibrates, adjusts
distorts, skews, biases
词性: 动词(verb)
