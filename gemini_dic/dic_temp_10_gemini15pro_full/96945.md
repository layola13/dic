inextricably:/ˌɪnɪkˈstrɪkəbli/
adverb
无法分开地，密不可分地
in a way that is impossible to disentangle or separate.
例句：
The two families were inextricably linked by marriage. 这两个家族因婚姻而密不可分。

His name is inextricably linked with the environmental movement. 他的名字与环保运动紧密相连。


近义词：inseparably, tightly, closely
反义词：separately, loosely
词性：副词
