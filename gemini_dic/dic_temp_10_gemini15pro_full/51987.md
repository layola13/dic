diageotropic:/ˌdaɪəˌdʒiːoʊˈtrɒpɪk/
adjective
横生地性的，背地性的
Growing or tending to grow in a direction at right angles to the force of gravity.
The diageotropic roots of the mangrove tree grow horizontally.  红树林的横生地性根水平生长。

The rhizomes of some ferns are diageotropic, growing horizontally beneath the soil surface. 一些蕨类植物的根状茎是横生地性的，在地表下水平生长。

近义词: plagiotropic
反义词: orthotropic, geotropic


词性: adjective
