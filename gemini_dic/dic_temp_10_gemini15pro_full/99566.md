interimistic:/ˌɪntərɪˈmɪstɪk/
adjective
临时的，过渡的
Existing or occurring for an interim or transitional period; temporary.
An interimistic government was put in place until elections could be held. 在举行选举之前，成立了一个临时政府。
interim, provisional, temporary
permanent, lasting
