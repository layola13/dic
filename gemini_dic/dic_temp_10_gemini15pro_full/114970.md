manchineel:/mænˌtʃəˈniːl/ | n. |毒番石榴树；马疯大戟木|A tropical American tree (Hippomane mancinella of the spurge family) that produces a milky sap that is extremely poisonous on contact and whose applelike fruit is toxic.|The manchineel tree is considered one of the most dangerous trees in the world.  毒番石榴树被认为是世界上最危险的树之一。

The manchineel's sap can cause severe burns and blindness.  毒番石榴树的汁液会导致严重的烧伤和失明。|近义词:beach apple (this refers to the fruit),  |反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific tree species)|词性:noun
