maids:/meɪdz/
n.
女佣，侍女
Female servants, especially those who clean or do housework.
The maids cleaned the entire house before the guests arrived. 女佣在客人到来之前打扫了整栋房子。

近义词: servants, housekeepers, domestics
反义词: masters, employers, mistresses
词性: 名词

词源:
From Middle English *mayde, maide, from Old English mægden (“maiden, virgin, girl, female servant”), from Proto-Germanic *magwiþō (“maiden”), from Proto-Indo-European *maǵʰ- (“young person, kinsman”). Cognate with Scots maid, may (“maid, girl, unmarried woman”), West Frisian faam (“maid, female servant”), Dutch meid (“girl”), German Magd (“maidservant”), Icelandic mey (“maiden, virgin”), Gothic 𐌼𐌰𐌲𐌰𐌸𐍃 (magaþs, “girl, virgin”).
