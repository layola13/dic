incompliable:/ˌɪnkəmˈplaɪəbl/
adjective
不服从的；不顺从的；不配合的
Not willing to comply; disobedient, unyielding.
He was known for his incompliable nature, refusing to follow even simple instructions. 他以不服从的天性而闻名，即使是简单的指示也拒绝遵守。
The incompliable child refused to eat his vegetables.  这个不听话的孩子拒绝吃蔬菜。

近义词: uncooperative, intractable, obstinate, recalcitrant, refractory
反义词: compliant, cooperative, obedient, docile, amenable
词性: adjective
