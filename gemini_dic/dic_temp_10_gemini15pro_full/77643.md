gelatinity:/ˌdʒɛləˈtɪnɪti/
n.
胶凝性，胶状
The state or quality of being gelatinous; resemblance to gelatin or jelly.
The gelatinity of the substance increased with the addition of pectin.  这种物质的胶凝性随着果胶的添加而增加。
近义词：gelatinousness, jelly-like consistency
反义词：fluidity, liquidity
词性: noun

gelatin:/ˈdʒɛlətɪn/
n.
明胶，凝胶
A translucent, colorless, brittle (when dry), flavorless solid substance, principally derived from collagen obtained from various animal by-products.
The dessert was made with gelatin. 这道甜点是用明胶做的。
近义词：jelly, agar-agar
反义词：liquid
词性: noun
