nephrology: /nɪˈfrɒlədʒi/ (UK) /nɪˈfrɑːlədʒi/ (US)| n. |肾脏学|The branch of medicine concerned with the kidneys. |He specializes in nephrology and treats patients with kidney disease. 他专攻肾脏学，治疗肾病患者。|近义词: renal medicine |反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider areas outside of renal medicine like cardiology or neurology) |词性: noun

Etymology:  From  Greek _nephros_ (kidney) +  _-logy_ (study of).
