diets:/ˈdaɪɪts/ (US) /ˈdaɪəts/ (UK)| n.| 饮食；节食；规定食谱|The plural form of "diet".  It refers to the kinds of food that a person or animal habitually eats or is prescribed to eat.  It can also refer to a special course of food to which a person restricts themselves, either to lose weight or for medical reasons. |She tried many different diets to lose weight. 她尝试了许多不同的饮食来减肥。

Many athletes follow strict diets. 许多运动员遵循严格的饮食。|近义词：regimens, nutritional plans, food plans|反义词: binging, gluttony|Noun
