interinvolve:/ˌɪntərɪnˈvɒlv/ | v. | 使互相参与；使互相影响 | To involve mutually or reciprocally; to cause to be involved with one another. | The two departments are interinvolved in the project. 这两个部门互相参与到这个项目中。|  The success of the two companies is interinvolved.  这两家公司的成功息息相关。 | 近义词: interconnect, interrelate, intertwine | 反义词: separate, disconnect | 词性: 动词

词源:  From inter- + involve.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between, among, mutually, reciprocally," and "involve" means "to engage as a participant."
