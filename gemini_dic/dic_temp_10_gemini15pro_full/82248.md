groundbreakings: /ˈɡraʊndˌbreɪkɪŋz/
| n. | 开创性的工作；突破；创新 | Innovative or pioneering achievements or discoveries. | The company is known for its groundbreakings in the field of artificial intelligence.  这家公司以其在人工智能领域的突破而闻名。 |
| | |  Her research led to several groundbreakings in cancer treatment. 她的研究导致了癌症治疗方面的几项突破。 | 近义词：innovations, advancements, breakthroughs | 反义词：stagnation, regression | 词性: 名词(复数)

groundbreaking: /ˈɡraʊndˌbreɪkɪŋ/
| adj. | 开创性的；突破性的 | Innovative; pioneering. |  The groundbreaking research has the potential to revolutionize the industry. 这项开创性的研究有可能彻底改变该行业。 |
| | | The scientist made a groundbreaking discovery in the field of genetics.  这位科学家在遗传学领域取得了突破性发现。 | 近义词：innovative, pioneering, revolutionary | 反义词：conventional, traditional, outdated | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Compound of "ground" (referring to the earth's surface) and "breaking" (from the verb "break"). Originally used literally to describe the act of breaking ground for construction, it later developed a figurative meaning of initiating something new and innovative.
