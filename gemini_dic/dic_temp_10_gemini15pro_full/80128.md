gobshites: /ˈɡɒbʃaɪts/| n.| 笨蛋，傻瓜，白痴 (粗俗) |A highly offensive term referring to a stupid or foolish person.  This word is considered vulgar and should be avoided in polite conversation.| "Those gobshities messed up the whole project." 那些笨蛋搞砸了整个项目。| 近义词：idiot, moron, fool, imbecile, jerk, twit | 反义词: genius, intellectual | 词性: 名词

词源:
Originates from Irish English, combining "gob" (mouth) with "shite" (excrement).  The implication is that the person speaks nonsense or rubbish.
