counterquestions:/ˈkaʊntərˌkwɛstʃənz/
noun
反诘；反问
Questions posed in response to another question, often to avoid answering the original question directly.
"The politician responded to the journalist's probing questions with a series of counterquestions."  这位政客用一系列反问来回应记者的探索性问题。
synonyms:  cross-questions, return questions, queries, inquiries
antonyms: answers, responses, replies
n.


counterquestion:/ˈkaʊntərˌkwɛstʃən/
verb
反问；反诘
To respond to a question with another question.
"Instead of answering directly, she counterquestioned him about his motives." 她没有直接回答，而是反问他的动机。
synonyms:  cross-examine, question back, interrogate
antonyms: answer, respond, reply
v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- + question.  "Counter-" acts as a prefix meaning "against" or "in opposition to."
