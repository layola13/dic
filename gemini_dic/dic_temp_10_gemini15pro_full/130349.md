non-trust:/ˌnɒnˈtrʌst/ | adj. | 非信任的，不信任的 | Lacking trust; characterized by distrust or suspicion. |  The non-trust environment made collaboration difficult.  非信任的环境使合作变得困难。

The survey revealed a high level of non-trust between management and employees.  调查显示管理层和员工之间存在高度不信任。| 近义词: distrustful, suspicious, skeptical | 反义词: trusting, trustful, confident | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "Non-" (prefix) from Latin meaning "not" + "trust" (noun/verb) from Old Norse "traust" meaning "confidence, reliance."
