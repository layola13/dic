overpedalling:/ˈoʊvərˌpɛdəlɪŋ/ /ˈəʊvəˌpɛdəlɪŋ/
| n. |过度踩踏; 过度用力蹬车|The act of pedalling a bicycle too fast, especially at a high cadence with little resistance, often resulting in reduced efficiency.|He was overpedalling up the hill and quickly tired out. 他上坡时过度用力蹬车，很快就累了。|近义词:spinning out, inefficient pedalling|反义词:mashing, grinding (referring to low cadence, high force pedalling)|词性:名词
