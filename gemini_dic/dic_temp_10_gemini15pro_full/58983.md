dutifully:/ˈdjuːtɪfli/ /duːˈtɪfəli/| adv.|尽职尽责地，忠实地|In a way that shows respect and obedience to someone or something. |She dutifully performed her chores every day. 她每天尽职尽责地做家务。|例句：He dutifully attended every meeting. 他尽职尽责地参加了每次会议。|近义词：obediently, respectfully, conscientiously, diligently|反义词：negligently, carelessly, irresponsibly|词性:副词

dutifully:/ˈdjuːtɪfli/ /duːˈtɪfəli/| adv.|忠实地|Faithfully; in a loyal manner. |He dutifully followed his captain's orders. 他忠实地执行了船长的命令。|例句：She dutifully recorded every transaction. 她忠实地记录了每一笔交易。|近义词：loyally, faithfully, truly|反义词：treacherously, disloyally, unfaithfully|词性:副词

词源:
From duty + -fully.  Duty comes from Old French deu, deü "due, owed," from Latin debitum "something owed," past participle of debere "to owe." -fully is a suffix meaning "in a specified manner," from full + -ly.
