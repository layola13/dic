decontaminate:/ˌdiːkənˈtæmɪneɪt/| v. |净化，清除污染，消毒|To remove contaminants from something, making it safe to handle or use.|例句：The workers decontaminated the area after the chemical spill. 工人们在化学品泄漏后对该区域进行了净化。

例句：The equipment must be decontaminated before it can be used again. 设备必须先净化才能再次使用。|近义词：cleanse, purify, sanitize, sterilize|反义词：contaminate, pollute|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (meaning "remove") + contaminate (from Latin contaminatus, past participle of contaminare, meaning "to pollute").
