birthplaces:/ˈbɜːrθpleɪsɪz/ (US) /ˈbɜːθpleɪsɪz/ (UK)| n.|出生地，诞生地|The places where someone or something was born or originated.|例句：Florence is the birthplace of the Renaissance. 佛罗伦萨是文艺复兴的发源地。

例句：Many famous artists' birthplaces have become museums. 许多著名艺术家的出生地都变成了博物馆。

近义词：origins, cradles, homes, sources|反义词: deathplace|词性:名词
