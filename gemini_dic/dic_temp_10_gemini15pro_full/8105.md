anthropy:/ˈænθrəpi/
n.
人类活动对环境的影响；人为变化
The influence of human beings on nature or the environment, typically with reference to the detrimental effects of pollution.
The increasing anthropy of the planet is evident in the rising global temperatures. 地球上日益增长的人为变化在全球气温上升中显而易见。
近义词：human impact, anthropogenic influence
反义词: natural processes
