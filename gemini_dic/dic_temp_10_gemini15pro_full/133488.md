nonfeminists:/ˌnɒnˈfɛmɪnɪsts/ | n. |非女权主义者| People who do not subscribe to feminist ideologies or actively oppose them.  Those who do not identify as feminists. |Many nonfeminists misunderstand the goals of the feminist movement.| Synonyms: anti-feminists, traditionalists (in some contexts) | Antonyms: feminists | Noun

nonfeminist:/ˌnɒnˈfɛmɪnɪst/ | adj. |非女权主义的| Not pertaining to or supporting feminism. |  He held some nonfeminist views on traditional gender roles. | Synonyms: anti-feminist | Antonyms: feminist | Adjective
