deteriorator:/dɪˈtɪəriəreɪtər/
noun
恶化物；恶化剂；劣化剂
Something that causes deterioration.
Exposure to sunlight is a major deteriorator of rubber.  阳光照射是橡胶老化的主要原因。
synonyms:  degrading agent, worsening agent, damaging agent, weakening agent, impairing agent
antonyms:  preservative, enhancer, improver, restorative
词性: 名词


deteriorate:/dɪˈtɪəriəreɪt/
verb
恶化；变坏；退化
To become progressively worse.
Her health deteriorated rapidly. 她的健康状况迅速恶化。
synonyms:  worsen, decline, degenerate, decay, crumble
antonyms:  improve, enhance, ameliorate, recover
词性: 动词
