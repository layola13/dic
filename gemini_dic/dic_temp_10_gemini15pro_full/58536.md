duffs:/dʌfs/
n.
屁股；糕点；拙劣的尝试；失败；混乱

successes, achievements, triumphs
noun

Doughnuts, muffins, pastries, sweet treats.  A general term for flour-based desserts, often simple ones. Can also refer to personal belongings, especially in a military context.  Informally, it can mean a mess, a mistake, or a failure.
He packed all his duffs in a duffel bag. 他把所有的个人物品都打包在一个行李袋里。
The camping trip was a complete duffs due to the torrential rain. 因为暴雨，露营旅行彻底失败了。

Synonyms: belongings, possessions, gear, stuff, kit (for personal items);  mess, shambles, disaster, fiasco, blunder (for failure/mess)
Antonyms: successes, achievements, order, organization (for failure/mess)


ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain. Possibly from Dutch "dof" meaning "dough," or related to "duff" meaning "decayed wood," referring to the spongy texture of some cakes.  The military slang for personal belongings is likely a shortening of "duffel bag."
