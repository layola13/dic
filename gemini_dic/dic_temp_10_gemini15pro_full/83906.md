half-barrels:/ˌhɑːf ˈbærəlz/ | n. | 半桶 | A barrel sawn in half lengthwise or across to make a container, planter, or other object. | The brewery delivered several half-barrels of beer for the festival. 酒厂为节日运送了几半桶啤酒。| The flowers were planted in brightly painted half-barrels. 花朵被种在漆成鲜色的半桶里。| synonyms: half-casks, tubs, planters | antonyms: full barrels | noun
