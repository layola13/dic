/kəˈkɛksiə/ /kəˈkɛkʃə/
n.
恶病质，羸弱，衰竭
A state of ill health involving significant weight loss, muscle atrophy, fatigue, weakness, and loss of appetite in someone who is not actively trying to lose weight. This syndrome is often associated with chronic diseases such as cancer, AIDS, or heart failure.
The patient with advanced cancer exhibited signs of cachexia.  这位晚期癌症病人表现出恶病质的迹象。
Cachexia can significantly impact a patient's quality of life. 恶病质会严重影响患者的生活质量。

近义词: wasting, atrophy, emaciation, debility
反义词: robustness, vigor, health
词性: noun
