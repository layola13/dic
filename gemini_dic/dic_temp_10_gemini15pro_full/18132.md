behoof:/bɪˈhuːf/| n.|利益，好处|advantage, profit; something that is helpful or useful|He acted in the behoof of his client.|近义词:benefit, advantage, interest, welfare|反义词:detriment, disadvantage|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *behōf (“advantage, profit, use”), from be- (“by, about”) + hōf (“fitting, suitable”). Cognate with Dutch behoeven (“to need”).
