cloudy:/ˈklaʊdi/ (US) /ˈklaʊdi/ (UK)| adj.|多云的，阴天的|Covered with clouds; overcast.|The sky is cloudy today. 今天天空多云。|It was a cloudy and cool day. 那是一个阴冷的一天。|近义词：overcast, gray, hazy|反义词：clear, sunny, bright|词性:形容词

词源: From Old English *cludīg, from clūd "cloud" + -ig, a suffix forming adjectives from nouns.  The Old English clūd is related to Dutch kluit "clod, lump," and German Klotz "block, lump." The basic sense is "mass, lump."
