georgette:/ʒɔːrˈʒɛt/ | n. |乔其纱；乔其绉绸|A thin, sheer fabric of silk or rayon, often used for dresses and blouses.|例句：She wore a beautiful georgette dress to the party. 她穿了一件漂亮的乔其纱连衣裙去参加聚会。

例句：Georgette is a popular choice for summer clothing due to its lightweight and breathable nature. 乔其纱因其轻盈透气的特性成为夏季服装的流行选择。|近义词：chiffon, crepe |反义词: (No direct antonym for a fabric type, perhaps something heavy like "tweed" or "denim") tweed, denim |词性:noun
