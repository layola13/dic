multimedia:/ˌmʌltiˈmiːdiə/
adjective|多媒体的|Using more than one medium of expression or communication.|The museum has a multimedia exhibit on the history of flight.|Synonyms: interactive, digital, mixed-media|Antonyms: traditional, unimedia|词性:形容词

multimedia:/ˌmʌltiˈmiːdiə/
noun|多媒体|The combined use of several media, as sound and full-motion video in computer applications.|The website uses multimedia to enhance the learning experience.|Synonyms: mixed media, hypermedia|Antonyms: single medium|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From multi- (many) + media (medium).
