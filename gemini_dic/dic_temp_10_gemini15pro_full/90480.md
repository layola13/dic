hubris: /ˈhjuːbrɪs/ (US) /ˈhjuːbrɪs/ (UK)| n.|傲慢；狂妄自大；| Excessive pride or self-confidence, often leading to downfall.  A feeling of invincibility that can lead to arrogant behavior and ultimately, failure. | His hubris led him to believe he could defeat anyone. 他的傲慢使他相信自己可以打败任何人。

| He was blinded by hubris and refused to listen to advice. 他被傲慢蒙蔽了双眼，拒绝听取任何建议。

| 近义词：arrogance, pride, vanity, conceit, haughtiness | 反义词：humility, modesty, diffidence | 词性: noun
