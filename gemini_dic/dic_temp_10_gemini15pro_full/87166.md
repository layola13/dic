herbs:/ɜːrbz (UK), /ɝːbz (US)| n. |草药，香草|Plants used for flavoring food, medicine, or fragrances.  They are typically valued for their savory or aromatic qualities.|She grows herbs in her garden, such as basil and thyme. 她花园里种植草药，如罗勒和百里香。|synonyms: spices, seasonings, aromatics|antonyms: poisons, toxins|noun
