coagulate:/koʊˈæɡjʊleɪt/| v. | 凝结，凝固 | To change from a liquid to a thickened or solid state. | The blood began to coagulate around the wound. 血液开始在伤口周围凝结。|  The milk coagulated when we added lemon juice.  我们加了柠檬汁后，牛奶凝固了。 | 近义词：clot, thicken, solidify, congeal | 反义词：liquefy, dissolve |词性：动词

coagulate:/koʊˈæɡjʊleɪt/| n. | 凝结物 | A thickened or solid mass formed by coagulation. | The coagulate was removed from the wound.  凝结物已从伤口处移除。| The chef carefully removed the coagulate from the sauce.  厨师小心地将凝结物从酱汁中取出。| 近义词：clot, curd | 反义词：liquid, solution |词性：名词


词源:  From Late Latin coagulātus, past participle of coagulāre (“to curdle, congeal”), from Latin co- + agulāre, frequentative of agere (“to drive, lead, do”).
