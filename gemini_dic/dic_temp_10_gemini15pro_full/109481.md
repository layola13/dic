leprosy:/ˈlɛprəsi/| n. |麻风病；受排斥的状态|A chronic infectious disease mainly affecting the skin, nerves, and mucous membranes, characterized by the formation of granulomas and nodules.|例句：Leprosy is a curable disease now. 现在麻风病是可以治愈的。

例句：He was ostracized by the community because of his leprosy. 他因为患麻风病而被社区排斥。|近义词：Hansen's disease|反义词: health|词性:noun
