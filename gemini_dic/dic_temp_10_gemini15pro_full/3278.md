affirmingly:/əˈfɜːrmɪŋli/
adverb
肯定地；断然地
in a way that expresses agreement or support
He nodded affirmingly. 他肯定地点了点头。
近义词:positively, definitely, assertively, resolutely
反义词:negatively, hesitantly, uncertainly
词性:adverb

词源:
From affirm + -ing + -ly.  "Affirm" comes from the Latin "affirmare," meaning "to strengthen, make firm, declare solemnly."
