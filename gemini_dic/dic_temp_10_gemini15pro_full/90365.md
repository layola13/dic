how:/haʊ/| adv. conj. n.|如何，怎样，多么，多少|Used to ask about the way something happens or is done, or about the condition or quality of something.|How are you? 你好吗？|近义词:in what way, by what means|反义词: N/A|adverb, conjunction, noun


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
How do you get to school? 你怎么去上学？
How does this machine work?  这个机器怎么运作？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
How are you feeling today? 你今天感觉怎么样？
How is she coping with the stress? 她如何应对压力？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
How have you been? 你最近怎么样？
How has the project progressed? 项目进展如何？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
How have you been spending your time? 你这段时间都在做什么？
How has he been managing the workload? 他一直是如何处理工作量的？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
How did you get here? 你是怎么来到这里的？
How did the accident happen? 事故是怎么发生的？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
How were you feeling yesterday? 你昨天感觉怎么样？
How was she handling the situation? 她当时是如何处理情况的？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
How had you prepared for the exam? 你考试前是如何准备的？
How had the situation deteriorated so quickly? 情况是如何恶化得这么快的？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
How had you been coping with the pressure? 你之前一直是如何应对压力的？
How had they been managing the project before he left? 他离开前他们是如何管理项目的？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
How will you get to the airport? 你将如何去机场？
How will the new policy affect us? 新政策将如何影响我们？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
How will you be spending your vacation? 你假期将如何度过？
How will they be handling the increased demand? 他们将如何应对增长的需求？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
How will you have completed the project by then? 到那时你将如何完成这个项目？
How will they have achieved their goals? 到那时他们将如何实现他们的目标？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
How will you have been traveling for so long? 到那时你将如何旅行这么长时间？
How will she have been studying for the exam? 到那时她将如何准备考试？
