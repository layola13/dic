newsiness:/ˈnjuːzinəs/| n.|新闻价值；新闻性|The newsiness of a story is determined by its impact, timeliness, proximity, prominence, and human interest.  一个故事的新闻价值取决于其影响力、时效性、接近性、显著性和人类兴趣。|The editor questioned the newsiness of the article. 编辑质疑这篇文章的新闻价值。|近义词：newsworthiness, topicality|反义词：insignificance, triviality|词性：名词

Etymology:  From "news" + "-y" (forming adjectives meaning 'having the quality of') + "-ness" (forming nouns from adjectives).  源自“news”（新闻）+“-y”（构成形容词，意为“具有...的性质”）+“-ness”（由形容词构成名词）。
