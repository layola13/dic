mot:/moʊ/ (US) /məʊ/ (UK)| n.| 主题; 词; 格言; 短歌| A recurring subject, theme, or idea, especially in a literary, artistic, or musical work.  A word or phrase, especially one that is characteristic of a particular person, group, or occasion.  A pithy observation or general truth, expressed in a memorable form. A short lyric or song.|

例句：
The mot of the conference was "Innovation."
会议的主题是“创新”。

The author's favorite mot was "carpe diem."
作者最喜欢的格言是“及时行乐”。


近义词：theme, subject, topic, slogan, motto, aphorism, maxim, adage
反义词: (No direct antonym, depends on the specific meaning of "mot")
