noninstinctual:/ˌnɒnɪnˈstɪŋktʃuəl/
adjective
非本能的；非直觉的
Not based on instinct; learned or acquired.
His reaction was noninstinctual, a product of years of training. 他的反应并非出于本能，而是多年训练的结果。
learned, acquired, conditioned
instinctive, innate, inherent
