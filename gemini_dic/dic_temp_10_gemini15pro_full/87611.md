heterozygotic:/ˌhɛtərəʊzaɪˈɡɒtɪk/ (US) /ˌhɛt̬əroʊzaɪˈɡɑːtɪk/| adj.|杂合的|Having two different alleles of a particular gene or genes.|Example:  The heterozygotic individual carried one dominant and one recessive allele for eye color.  这个杂合子个体携带一个显性等位基因和一个隐性等位基因，用于眼睛颜色。

Example: Heterozygotic plants often display hybrid vigor. 杂合植物通常表现出杂种优势。|近义词:hybrid|反义词:homozygotic|词性:adjective
