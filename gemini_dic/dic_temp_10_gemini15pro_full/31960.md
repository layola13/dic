chemically:/ˈkɛmɪkli/| adv.|化学地，用化学方法|In a way that relates to chemistry; using chemical processes or substances. |例句：The food was chemically analyzed.  这种食物经过了化学分析。

例句：The two substances reacted chemically. 这两种物质发生了化学反应。|近义词：synthetically, artificially|反义词:naturally, organically|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From chemical + -ly.  "Chemical" ultimately derives from the Arabic word "al-kīmiyā," meaning alchemy.
