obstreperosity: /əbˌstrɛpəˈrɑːsəti/ /əbˌstrɛpəˈrɒsəti/
n.
喧闹；吵闹；任性
The state of being noisy and difficult to control.
Example: The obstreperosity of the children made it difficult to concentrate. 孩子们的吵闹声使人难以集中注意力。

Example:  The teacher struggled to control the obstreperosity of the class during the field trip.  老师在实地考察旅行中努力控制班级的喧闹。


Synonym: boisterousness, clamor, tumult, uproar, rowdiness
Antonym: quiet, calmness, peacefulness, silence, tranquility
词性: 名词


obstreperous: /əbˈstrɛpərəs/ /əbˈstrɛpərəs/
adj.
喧闹的；吵闹的；任性的
Noisy and difficult to control.
Example: The obstreperous crowd cheered wildly after the game. 吵闹的人群在比赛结束后疯狂欢呼。

Example:  The obstreperous child refused to listen to his parents. 任性的孩子拒绝听父母的话。


Synonym: unruly, boisterous, clamorous, tumultuous, vociferous
Antonym: quiet, calm, peaceful, silent, well-behaved
词性: 形容词
