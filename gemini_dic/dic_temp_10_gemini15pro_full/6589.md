anacardium:/ˌænəˈkɑːrdiəm/| n. |腰果树；腰果|A tropical American tree (Anacardium occidentale) that produces the cashew nut and cashew apple.|例句：The anacardium tree thrives in warm climates. 腰果树在温暖的气候中茁壮成长。

例句：Cashews are the nuts of the anacardium tree. 腰果是腰果树的果实。|近义词：cashew tree|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
