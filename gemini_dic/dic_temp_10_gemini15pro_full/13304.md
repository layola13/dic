athirst:/əˈθɜːrst/| adj.|渴望的，口渴的|Feeling a strong need or desire for something, especially for a drink; thirsty.|例句：The travelers were athirst after their long journey across the desert. 旅行者们在穿越沙漠的长途跋涉后感到口渴。|近义词：thirsty, eager, craving, yearning|反义词：satisfied, quenched, sated|词性：形容词

Etymology:
From Old English *ofþyrst, from of- "intensive prefix" + þyrst "thirst".  Cognate with German dürstig.
