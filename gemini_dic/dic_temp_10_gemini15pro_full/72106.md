flocked:/flɑːkt/ | adj. | 成群的，聚集的；绒状的，有绒毛的 | Covered with or decorated with a flock; gathered or moving in a flock. | The hills were flocked with sheep. 山上聚集着成群的绵羊。 |  The wallpaper is flocked with velvet.  墙纸上覆盖着天鹅绒。| synonyms:  crowded, gathered, assembled, flocked, velvety, downy, fuzzy | antonyms:  empty, deserted, bare, smooth, bald | word type: adjective

flocked:/flɑːkt/ | v. | 聚集，成群结队；涌向 | Past tense and past participle of "flock" which means to gather or move in a large group. | People flocked to the beach on the hot day.  炎热的天气里，人们成群结队地涌向海滩。 | Tourists flocked to see the famous painting. 游客们涌去看那幅名画。 | synonyms:  converge, gather, swarm, throng | antonyms:  disperse, scatter, leave | word type: verb
