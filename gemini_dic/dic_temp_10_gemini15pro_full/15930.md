balmer(s):/ˈbɔːlmər(z)/| n.|巴尔默系，巴尔默线|A series of spectral lines in the visible region of the hydrogen spectrum, resulting from electron transitions to the second energy level.  It is named after Johann Balmer, a Swiss mathematician and physicist who discovered the formula describing the series in 1885.|The Balmer series is important in astronomy for identifying hydrogen in stars.|近义词:spectral lines, hydrogen lines|反义词: None|词性:noun
