arie:/ˈɑːriə/ /ˈeəriə/
n.
咏叹调，唱腔
A long, accompanied song for a solo voice, typically one in an opera or oratorio.
例句：The soprano's aria was the highlight of the opera.  女高音的咏叹调是歌剧的亮点。

例句：He performed a beautiful aria from "La Bohème." 他演唱了《波西米亚人》中的一段优美的咏叹调。

近义词：solo, song, air
反义词:chorus, ensemble
词性:noun
