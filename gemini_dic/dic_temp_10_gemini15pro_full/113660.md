machining:/məˈʃiːnɪŋ/| n.|机械加工，机加工|The process of shaping metal or other materials by removing material using machine tools such as lathes, milling machines, and drills.|The machining of this part requires high precision.  这个零件的加工需要很高的精度。|Synonyms:  milling, turning, drilling, shaping, fabrication|Antonyms:  casting, molding, forging|noun

machining:/məˈʃiːnɪŋ/| adj.(less common)|用于机加工的，适合机加工的，通过机加工制成的|Machining methods are constantly evolving. 机加工方法在不断发展。|Synonyms: machinable (more common for this sense)|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
