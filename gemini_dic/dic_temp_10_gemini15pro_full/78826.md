gimmicker:/ˈɡɪmɪkər/| n.|噱头制造者，耍花招的人|Someone who uses gimmicks, especially to attract attention or publicity.|The marketing campaign was orchestrated by a gimmicker who promised unbelievable results.|近义词:trickster, con artist, fraudster|反义词:honest person, straightforward person|词性:noun
Etymology: From "gimmick" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Gimmick" is of uncertain origin, possibly from gamblers' slang.
