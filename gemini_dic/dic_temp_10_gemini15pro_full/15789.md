balistoid:/ˈbælɪstɔɪd/| adj. n.|像弩炮的；弩炮鱼；|Resembling a crossbow or triggerfish; a fish of the family Balistidae (triggerfish).|The balistoid fish darted through the coral. 这条弩炮鱼在珊瑚中飞窜。|近义词：triggerfish, filefish|反义词:|adj. n.
Etymology: From Greek *ballistes* (catapult, crossbow) + *-oid* (resembling).
