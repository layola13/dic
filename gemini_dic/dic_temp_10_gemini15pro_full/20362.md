bisque:/bɪsk/| n. |浓汤，浓味鱼虾汤|A thick, creamy soup, typically made with shellfish.  It is often served as the first course in a formal meal.|例句：The lobster bisque was rich and flavorful. 龙虾浓汤味道浓郁鲜美。

例句：She ordered the bisque as a starter. 她点了一份浓汤作为开胃菜。|近义词：soup, chowder, potage|反义词:|词性:noun

词源: From French *bisque*, from *Biscaye* (Biscay), a province in Spain.  It is believed that the name originated because the soup is often made with shellfish from the Bay of Biscay.
