loyalty:/ˈlɔɪəlti/| n.|忠诚，忠实，忠心|The dog's loyalty to its owner was unquestionable.  这只狗对其主人的忠诚是毋庸置疑的。|例句：He showed unwavering loyalty to his friends. 他对朋友表现出 unwavering loyalty. 他对朋友表现出坚定不移的忠诚。

例句：Her loyalty to the company was rewarded with a promotion. 她对公司的忠诚得到了晋升的奖励。|近义词：fidelity, allegiance, devotion, constancy|反义词：treachery, betrayal, disloyalty|词性:名词

词源:  From Old French *lealté, from leal "loyal," from Latin legalis "legal, pertaining to law," from lex (genitive legis) "law."  The notion is of adherence to the law or to one's feudal obligations.
