coming:/ˈkʌmɪŋ/| adj.|即将来临的，到来的|Something that is coming is going to happen or arrive soon.  It is the present participle of "come".|
The coming week will be busy.  接下来的这一周将会很忙碌。
Winter is coming. 冬天来了。|近义词：approaching, forthcoming, imminent, impending|反义词：past, gone, bygone|词性:adjective

coming:/ˈkʌmɪŋ/| n.|到来，来临|The arrival of something or someone.|
The coming of spring is always welcome. 春天的到来总是令人欣喜的。
We eagerly await the coming of the new year. 我们热切地期盼着新年的到来。|近义词：arrival, advent|反义词：departure, going|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *cumende, present participle of cuman "to come," from Proto-Germanic *kweman, from Proto-Indo-European *gʷem- "to come, go."
