dwindling:/ˈdwɪndlɪŋ/
adjective
逐渐减少的，逐渐变小的
becoming smaller or weaker; decreasing
The town's population is dwindling.  这个镇的人口正在减少。
Their dwindling resources became a major problem. 他们日益减少的资源成了一个主要问题。
synonyms: decreasing, diminishing, shrinking, declining, contracting
antonyms: increasing, growing, expanding, rising, escalating
词性: 形容词
