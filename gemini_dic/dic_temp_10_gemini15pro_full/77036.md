garlanded:/ˈɡɑːləndɪd/
adjective
戴着花环的；饰以花环的
Adorned or decorated with garlands.
The victorious athletes were garlanded with laurel wreaths.  获胜的运动员戴着月桂花环。
The bride's hair was garlanded with fragrant flowers.  新娘的头发上戴着芬芳的花环。
Synonyms: wreathed, crowned, festooned, decorated
Antonyms: bare, undecorated
词性: adjective
