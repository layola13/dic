词源:
From Old English *baðian (attested only in the past participle gebaðod), from Proto-Germanic *baþōną (“to bathe”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰeh₂- (“to warm”). Cognate with Old Norse baða (“to bathe”), Dutch baden (“to bathe”), German baden (“to bathe”).
