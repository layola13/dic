inexpertness:/ˌɪnɛkˈspɜːrtnɪs/ | n. |缺乏专业知识；不熟练；笨拙 |Lack of expertise or skill; clumsiness. |  Her inexpertness in the kitchen resulted in a burnt dinner. 她在厨房里的不熟练导致晚餐烧焦了。| inexperience, awkwardness, clumsiness, incompetence | expertise, skill, proficiency, adeptness | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + expert + -ness (noun suffix forming abstract nouns from adjectives).  "Expert" comes from Old French "expert," past participle of "experir" (to try, to test), from Latin "experiri" (to try).
