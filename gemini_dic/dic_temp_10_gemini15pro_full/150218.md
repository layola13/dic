pandarize:/ˈpændəˌraɪz/| v. |迎合；拉皮条|To cater to or exploit the weaknesses of others, especially for personal gain.  To act as a pimp. |He pandarized to the wealthy tourists, offering them whatever they desired. 他迎合富有的游客，为他们提供任何他们想要的东西。| The club owner was arrested for pandarizing.  这家俱乐部的老板因拉皮条而被捕。 |Synonyms: cater to, indulge, exploit, pander|Antonyms: challenge, confront, resist|verb
