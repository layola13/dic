overrideable:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪdəbl/| adj.|可覆盖的，可重写的|able to be overridden; capable of being superseded.  Something that can be replaced or changed by something else, especially in programming where a method can be redefined in a subclass.|The default settings are overrideable by the user.  用户可以覆盖默认设置。|synonyms: replaceable, modifiable, alterable|antonyms: fixed, unchangeable, permanent|adjective

override:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪd/| v.|覆盖，重写，推翻|to replace or change something, especially in programming where a method can be redefined in a subclass. Also, to use one's authority to reject a decision or refuse a request.|The manager decided to override the committee's decision. 经理决定推翻委员会的决定。|synonyms: overrule, supersede, cancel|antonyms: uphold, maintain, confirm|verb
