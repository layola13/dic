anthropophagy:/ˌænθrəˈpɒfədʒi/
n.
食人，食人肉，同类相食
The practice of eating human flesh by human beings.
Ancient texts suggest that some tribes practiced anthropophagy.  古代文献表明，一些部落实行食人。
Cannibalism was a dark chapter in the history of anthropophagy. 食人是食人史上的黑暗篇章。
