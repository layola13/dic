embroglio:/ɪmˈbroʊljoʊ/ /ɛmˈbroʊljoʊ/
noun
纠纷，混乱，复杂的情况
a complicated or confusing situation, especially one involving disagreement or intrigue.
The political embroilment threatened to destabilize the entire region. 政治纠纷有可能破坏整个地区的稳定。
The inheritance became an embroilment, with various family members contesting the will.  遗产继承变成了一个纠纷，不同的家庭成员对遗嘱提出异议。
近义词: predicament, quandary, muddle, tangle, confusion
反义词: order, clarity, solution, harmony, agreement
词性: n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *imbroglio*, from *imbrogliare* "to confuse, entangle," from *in-* "in" + *broglio* "confusion, intrigue," perhaps ultimately from Vulgar Latin *brogilus* "small woods," of Germanic origin.
