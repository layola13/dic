methadone:/ˈmɛθəˌdoʊn/ (US), /ˈmɛθədən/ (UK)| n.|美沙酮|A synthetic opioid drug used as a pain reliever and as a treatment for heroin or other opioid addiction.|The doctor prescribed methadone to help him overcome his heroin addiction. 医生给他开了美沙酮来帮助他克服海洛因成瘾。|Synonyms: Dolophine, Amidone|Antonyms:  Naloxone, Naltrexone (These are opioid antagonists used to reverse opioid overdose)|noun
