defibrinate:/diːˈfaɪbrɪneɪt/ |v. |去纤维蛋白|To remove fibrin from (blood).|The scientist defibrinated the blood sample before conducting the experiment. 科学家在进行实验前去除血液样本中的纤维蛋白。|
The blood was defibrinated to prevent clotting.  血液被去纤维蛋白以防止凝结。|
Synonyms: declot |Antonyms: clot, coagulate |Verb|

defibrination:/diːˌfaɪbrɪˈneɪʃən/ |n. |去纤维蛋白（作用）|The process of removing fibrin from blood.|Defibrination is a crucial step in preparing certain blood products. 去除纤维蛋白是制备某些血液制品中的关键步骤。|The defibrination of the blood sample took several hours. 血液样本的去纤维蛋白过程花了几个小时。|Synonyms: declotting |Antonyms: clotting, coagulation |Noun|
