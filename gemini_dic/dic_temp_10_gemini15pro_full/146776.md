overidealized:/ˌoʊvər aɪˈdiːəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 过于理想化的 |  Characterized by representing something in an overly perfect or desirable way, disregarding its flaws or complexities. |  The movie presented an overidealized version of war, ignoring the brutal realities. 这部电影呈现了一个过于理想化的战争版本，忽略了残酷的现实。 |  The politician's overidealized promises failed to resonate with voters.  这位政客过于理想化的承诺未能引起选民的共鸣。 | idealistic, romanticized, utopian | realistic, pragmatic, cynical | adjective
