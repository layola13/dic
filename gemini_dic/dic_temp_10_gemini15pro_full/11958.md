artboards:/ˈɑːrtbɔːrdz/
n.
画板
A designated area on a screen in a graphics editing program representing a page, spread, or other discrete design element.  This allows designers to work on multiple layouts within a single document.
I created three different artboards in Illustrator for the website homepage, mobile version, and tablet version. 我在Illustrator中为网站主页、移动版和平板电脑版创建了三个不同的画板。
canvases, worksheets
(no direct antonym)
noun
