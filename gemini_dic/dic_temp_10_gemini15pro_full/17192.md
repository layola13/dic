bauhauses: /ˈbaʊˌhaʊzɪz/
n.
包豪斯风格的建筑物（复数）; 包豪斯学派（复数）
Bauhaus buildings or schools; adherents of the Bauhaus school.
The bauhauses of Dessau and Weimar are iconic examples of modernist architecture. 德绍和魏玛的包豪斯建筑是现代主义建筑的标志性范例。
The exhibition featured works by several prominent bauhauses.  展览展出了几位杰出的包豪斯学派成员的作品。
近义词: Bauhaus schools, Bauhaus movement
反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑一些对立的艺术流派，例如) Art Deco, Victorian architecture
词性: noun


Etymology:
From German Bauhaus, from Bau "building" + Haus "house".
