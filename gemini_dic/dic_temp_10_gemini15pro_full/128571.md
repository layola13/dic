nibful:/ˈnɪbfʊl/| n. |笔尖蘸满的墨水量；一口（食物或饮料）|The amount of ink held in the nib of a pen. Also, a small amount of food or drink. |She dipped her pen and took a nibful of ink. 她蘸了蘸笔，吸了一点墨水。|Synonyms: mouthful, sip, drop, bit|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps "gulf," "abundance")|noun

nibful:/ˈnɪbfʊl/| n. |一口（食物或饮料）|A small amount of food or drink, as much as can be taken in one mouthful. |He savored the nibful of rich chocolate cake. 他细细品尝了一口浓郁的巧克力蛋糕。|Synonyms: mouthful, bite, sip|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, perhaps "feast," "banquet")|noun
