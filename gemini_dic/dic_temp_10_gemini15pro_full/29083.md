carvist: /ˈkɑːrvɪst/ | n. | 雕刻家；雕刻匠 | A person who carves wood, stone, or other materials into artistic forms. |  He is a skilled carvist, known for his intricate wood carvings. 他是一位技艺精湛的雕刻家，以其复杂的木雕而闻名。| synonym: sculptor, engraver, woodcarver, stone carver | antonym:  None (No direct antonym for a profession) | noun

Etymology: From "carve" + "-ist" (suffix denoting a person who performs an action or practices a skill).  "Carve" comes from Old English *ceorfan, from Proto-Germanic *kerfaną, meaning "to cut, notch."
