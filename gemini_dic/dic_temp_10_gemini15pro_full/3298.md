afflictedness:/əˈflɪktɪdnəs/| n. |痛苦，苦恼，受折磨的状态|The afflictedness of the refugees was heartbreaking.  难民们的苦难令人心碎。|例句：The afflictedness caused by the chronic illness made it difficult for her to work.  慢性病带来的痛苦使她难以工作。

Her afflictedness was evident in her gaunt face and weary eyes. 她的痛苦在她憔悴的面容和疲惫的眼神中显而易见。|近义词：suffering, misery, distress, anguish|反义词: happiness, joy, comfort, well-being|词性:noun
词源: From afflicted + -ness.  Afflicted 源自拉丁语 afflictus，过去分词 affligere，意思是“打击，削弱”。
