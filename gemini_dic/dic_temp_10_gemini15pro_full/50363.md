depopularizing:/diːˈpɒpjʊləˌraɪzɪŋ/
verb
使不受欢迎；降低人气
making something less popular
The new parking regulations are depopularizing the downtown shopping district.  新的停车规定正在降低市中心购物区的人气。
depopularizing:/diːˈpɒpjʊləˌraɪzɪŋ/
adjective
使...不受欢迎的;降低人气的
making something less popular; describing the process of making something less popular
The depopularizing effect of the new tax was immediately felt. 新税收降低人气的影响立即显现出来。


近义词:marginalizing, alienating, isolating
反义词:popularizing, promoting, boosting
词性: verb, adjective (present participle)
词源:  From de- (prefix meaning "reverse" or "remove") + popularize + -ing (present participle suffix).  "Popularize" comes from "popular" + -ize (verb-forming suffix). "Popular" ultimately derives from Latin "populus" (people).
