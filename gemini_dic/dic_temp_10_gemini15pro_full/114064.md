magnetized:/ˈmæɡnɪtaɪzd/
adjective
已磁化的，带磁性的
Having been made magnetic.
The magnetized needle of a compass points north. 指南针磁化的指针指向北方。

近义词:magnetic, charged
反义词:demagnetized, unmagnetized
词性:adjective

词源:
From magnetize + -ed.  Ultimately from Latin magnes “magnet,” from Greek Magnēs (λίθος) “(stone) of Magnesia,” referring to a region in Thessaly where magnetic stone was found.
