imputrescible:/ˌɪmpjʊˈtrɛsəbəl/
adjective
不腐败的，不会腐烂的
Not subject to putrefaction; incorruptible.
The scientist searched for an imputrescible material to build the time capsule.  科学家寻找一种不腐烂的材料来建造时间胶囊。
The ancient Egyptians believed that certain herbs were imputrescible and could preserve the body after death. 古埃及人相信某些草药是不腐败的，可以在死后保存尸体。
近义词:incorruptible, imperishable, undecaying
反义词:putrescible, perishable, decomposable
词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *imputrescibilis, from Latin in- (“not”) + putrescere (“to rot”) + -ibilis (suffix forming adjectives).
