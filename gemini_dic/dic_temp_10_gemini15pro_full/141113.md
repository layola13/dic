ontogenetic:/ˌɒntədʒəˈnɛtɪk/ | adj. | 个体发生的，个体发育的 | Relating to the development of an individual organism from its earliest stage to maturity. |  The study explored the ontogenetic development of the human brain.  这项研究探讨了人脑的个体发育。| synonyms: developmental, maturational | antonyms: phylogenetic | adjective

ontogeny:/ˌɒnˈtɒdʒəni/ | n. | 个体发育，个体发生 | The development of an individual organism or anatomical or behavioral feature from the earliest stage to maturity. | The ontogeny of a butterfly is a fascinating process. 蝴蝶的个体发育是一个迷人的过程。| synonyms: development, maturation | antonyms: phylogeny | noun
