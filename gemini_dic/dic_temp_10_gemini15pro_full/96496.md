indo-african:/ˌɪndoʊˈæfrɪkən/
adjective
印非的；印度和非洲的
Relating to both India and Africa, or to the people or cultures of both regions.
The conference focused on indo-african trade relations.  这次会议的重点是印非贸易关系。
The indo-african community in London is quite vibrant.  伦敦的印非社群非常有活力。
近义词: Asian-African, Indian-African
反义词:  None (This word describes a combined geographical and cultural area; it doesn't have a direct opposite.)
词性: adjective
