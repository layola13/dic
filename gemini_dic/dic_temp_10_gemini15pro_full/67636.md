extricable:/ˈɛkstrɪkəbl/ | adj. | 可解救的，可摆脱的，可脱出的 | Able to be freed or removed from a difficult situation or entanglement. |  The knot was complex, but thankfully extricable.  这个结很复杂，但幸好可以解开。 |  The car was extricable from the mud. 这辆车可以从泥里拉出来。 | 近义词：rescuable, retrievable, releasable | 反义词：inextricable, trapped, entangled | 词性:形容词

词源: From Latin extricabilis, from extricare "to disentangle," from ex- "out" + tricae "perplexities, hindrances."
