inkers:/ˈɪŋkərz/
noun
墨水工，印刷工，纹身师
A person who applies ink, especially in printing or tattooing.
The inkers were busy preparing the printing press. 印刷工们正忙着准备印刷机。
The inkers at the tattoo parlor were highly skilled artists.  纹身店里的纹身师都是技艺高超的艺术家。
Synonyms: printer, tattoo artist
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, possibly) cleaner, eraser
词性: 名词(n.)
