off-the-air:/ˌɔːf ði ˈɛər/ | adj. | 停止播出的，停播的 | Not being broadcast on radio or television. | The show went off-the-air after five seasons.  这档节目播出了五季后停播了。| Synonyms:  unbroadcast, not on the air | Antonyms: on-the-air, broadcasting | adjective


off-the-air:/ˌɔːf ði ˈɛər/ | adv. |  停止播出地，停播地 | In a manner that is not being broadcast.  |  The interview was conducted off-the-air.  采访是在非直播状态下进行的。| Synonyms: privately, not publicly broadcast  | Antonyms: on-the-air, publicly  | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
"off" (Old English "of," meaning "away from, off") + "the" + "air" (from Old French "air," ultimately from Greek "aer," meaning "air")  The phrase developed with the advent of radio broadcasting.
