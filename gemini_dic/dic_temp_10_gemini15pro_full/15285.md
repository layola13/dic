backlisting: /ˈblækˌlɪstɪŋ/ | n. | 列入黑名单，禁止，抵制 | The act of putting someone or something on a blacklist.  This usually means refusing to hire, buy from, or associate with the person or entity. |  The company faced backlisting after the scandal. 这家公司在丑闻曝光后遭到抵制。|  boycotting, banning, ostracizing | whitelisting, approving | noun
