husbandly:/ˈhʌzbəndli/| adj.|丈夫的；像丈夫的；节俭的|Of or befitting a husband; pertaining to a husband or to the duties of a husband.  Thrifty; frugal.|例句：He displayed husbandly virtues of providing and caring. 他展现了养家糊口和关怀备至的丈夫美德。

例句：She appreciated his husbandly concern for her well-being. 她感激他对她健康的丈夫般的关心。|近义词：marital, matrimonial, thrifty, frugal|反义词：unhusbandly, extravagant, wasteful|词性：形容词
