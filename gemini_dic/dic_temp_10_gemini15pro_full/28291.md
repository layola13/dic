capuccio:/kəˈpuːtʃoʊ/ (US), /ˌkæpʊˈtʃiːnoʊ/ (UK)| n. |卡布奇诺|A coffee beverage made with espresso, hot milk, and steamed milk foam.|例句：I ordered a cappuccino and a croissant for breakfast. 我早餐点了一杯卡布奇诺和一个羊角面包。

例句：The barista made a beautiful cappuccino with latte art.  咖啡师制作了一杯漂亮的带有拉花艺术的卡布奇诺。|近义词：latte, macchiato|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps espresso as a simpler alternative)|词性:noun
