hagler:/ˈheɪɡlər/| n. |讨价还价者;  经常争论的人|A person who argues persistently, especially about the cost of something; a hard bargainer.|He was a haggler, always trying to get a lower price. 他是个爱讨价还价的人，总是想方设法压低价格。|近义词：bargainer, negotiator, quibbler|反义词:accommodator, pushover|词性:noun
