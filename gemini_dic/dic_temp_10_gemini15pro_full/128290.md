neuropterous:/njʊˈrɒptərəs/ | adj. |神经翅目的；脉翅目的 | Belonging or pertaining to the Neuroptera, an order of insects having four net-veined wings. | Example:  The lacewing is a neuropterous insect. 草蛉是一种脉翅目昆虫。

Example:  Neuropterous insects are known for their delicate wings. 脉翅目昆虫以其精致的翅膀而闻名。 | 近义词:  net-winged | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From  neuro-  +‎ -pterous.  neuro- comes from Greek νεῦρον (neûron), meaning "nerve, tendon, sinew". -pterous comes from Greek πτερόν (pterón), meaning "wing".
