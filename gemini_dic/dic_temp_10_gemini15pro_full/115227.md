manifoldness:/ˈmænɪˌfəʊldnəs/ | n. | 多样性，多方面性 | The state or quality of being manifold; multiplicity; variety. | The manifoldness of his talents impressed everyone. 他的多才多艺给每个人都留下了深刻的印象。| 近义词：multiplicity, variety, diversity, plenitude | 反义词：uniformity, homogeneity, sameness | 词性：名词

词源:  From manifold + -ness.  "Manifold" itself comes from Middle English *manyfold, from Old English manigfeald, from Proto-Germanic *managfalþaz.
