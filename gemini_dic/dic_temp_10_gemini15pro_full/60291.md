egging:/ˈeɡɪŋ/| n.|怂恿，煽动；扔鸡蛋|The constant egging from his friends finally convinced him to do it.  朋友们不断的怂恿最终说服了他去做这件事。|例句：The egging of the politician's car was a sign of public disapproval.  向政客的汽车扔鸡蛋是公众不满的一种表现。|近义词：incitement, goading|反义词：dissuasion|词性:名词

词源: From the verb "egg," meaning "to urge or incite," which comes from the Old Norse word "eggja," meaning "to edge, incite."  源自动词"egg"，意思是“敦促或煽动”，它来自古诺尔斯语单词“eggja”，意思是“边缘，煽动”。
