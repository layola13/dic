ferroprussiate:/ˌfɛroʊˈprʌʃiˌeɪt/ | n. adj. |亚铁氰化物，深蓝色 |A salt containing the ferrocyanide anion. As an adjective, it describes something treated with or containing a ferrocyanide. | This blueprint is a ferroprussiate print. 这张蓝图是亚铁氰化物印刷品。 | synonym:  cyanotype (specifically for the blue photographic print process), Prussian blue (for the pigment) | antonym: N/A | noun, adjective

ferroprussiate:/ˌfɛroʊˈprʌʃiˌeɪt/ | adj. |亚铁氰酸盐的 |Treated with or containing a ferrocyanide. | The ferroprussiate paper turned deep blue upon exposure to light.  亚铁氰酸盐纸在曝光后变成了深蓝色。| synonym: cyanotype (specifically for the blue photographic print process) | antonym: N/A | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From *ferro-* (iron) + *prussiate* (from Prussian blue, a deep blue pigment containing iron and cyanide).  Prussian blue was originally made in Berlin, then capital of Prussia.
