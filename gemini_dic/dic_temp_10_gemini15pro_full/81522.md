gravitas:/ˈɡrævɪtɑːs/
noun
庄严；严肃；认真
Dignity, seriousness, or solemnity of manner.
The judge spoke with gravitas, conveying the seriousness of the situation.  法官讲话庄严肃穆，表达了情况的严重性。
Synonyms: seriousness, solemnity, dignity, weight, importance, significance
Antonyms: frivolity, levity, flippancy
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin gravitas ("weight, heaviness, seriousness"), from gravis ("heavy").  Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European root *gʷerə- ("heavy").
