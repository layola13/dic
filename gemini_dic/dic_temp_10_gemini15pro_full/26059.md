bushing:/ˈbʊʃɪŋ/| n.|衬套; 套筒; 轴衬; 绝缘套管|A bushing is a sleeve or lining used to constrain, guide, or reduce friction in a machine.|The mechanic replaced the worn bushing in the suspension. 机械师更换了悬架中磨损的衬套。|The electrical bushing insulated the wire.  绝缘套管将电线绝缘。|近义词：sleeve, liner, bearing, grommet|反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "gap" in certain contexts)|Noun

bushing:/ˈbʊʃɪŋ/| v.|装衬套; 使用衬套|To fit or install a bushing.|They bushed the holes in the frame to reduce wear. 他们在框架上的孔中安装了衬套以减少磨损。|He is bushing the connection to ensure a tight fit. 他正在给连接处装衬套以确保紧密贴合。|近义词：fit, install, line|反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "remove" in certain contexts)|Verb
