calmy:/ˈkɑːmli/| adv.|平静地，镇定地|In a calm manner; peacefully; quietly.  It describes the manner in which something is done. |She spoke calmly despite the chaos around her. 她尽管周围一片混乱，但她仍然平静地说话。| calmly, peacefully, quietly, serenely| excitedly, nervously, anxiously |adverb
Etymology: From calm (adjective) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  The word "calm" ultimately derives from the Latin "calma" (heat, sultriness of the weather), which later developed the sense of stillness or tranquility.
