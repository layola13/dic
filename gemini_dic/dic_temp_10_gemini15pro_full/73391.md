forehew: /fɔːrˈhjuː/ /fɔːrˈhjuː/
n.
预感，预兆
an intuition or presentiment of something, especially of something unpleasant.
He had a forehew that something bad was going to happen.  他预感到要发生不好的事情。
近义词: premonition, presentiment, foreboding
反义词: hindsight
