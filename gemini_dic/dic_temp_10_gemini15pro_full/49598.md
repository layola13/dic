demesne:/dɪˈmeɪn/ /dɪˈmiːn/
n.|领地，地产；领域，范围|A piece of land attached to a manor and retained for the owner's own use.  The area over which a ruler or government has control or jurisdiction; realm. |The king surveyed his vast demesne. 国王巡视了他的广阔领地。|Synonyms: estate, domain, territory, realm, land, property |Antonyms: nothingness, void
词性: 名词
