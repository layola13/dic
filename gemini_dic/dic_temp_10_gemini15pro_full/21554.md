bluenoser:/ˈbluːˌnoʊzər/ | n. | 蓝鼻子，保守派（尤指新斯科舍省人），（非正式）清教徒 | A native or resident of Nova Scotia, Canada.  Often used informally to refer to someone who is perceived as strict, conservative, or puritanical. |  例句：He’s a bluenoser from Halifax. 他是来自哈利法的保守派。

例句：She was raised in a bluenoser family, so she's always been quite traditional.  她在一个清教徒家庭长大，所以她一直都很传统。 | 近义词：conservative, puritan, Nova Scotian | 反义词：liberal, radical | 词性：名词
