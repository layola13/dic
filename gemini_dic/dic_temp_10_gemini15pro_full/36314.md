cohobation:/ˌkoʊhoʊˈbeɪʃən/
noun
反复蒸馏
the repeated distillation of a liquid, especially in alchemy.
The alchemist practiced cohobation to purify the substance. 炼金术士反复蒸馏以提纯物质。
redistillation, repeated distillation
