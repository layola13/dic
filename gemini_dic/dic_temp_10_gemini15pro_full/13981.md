auric:/ˈɔːrɪk/| adj.|金的，金色的，三价金的|Of or relating to gold, especially trivalent gold.|The auric chloride solution shimmered in the sunlight. 氯化金溶液在阳光下闪闪发光。|Synonyms: golden, gold, aurous (related but refers to monovalent gold)|Antonyms: non-metallic, non-golden|adjective
