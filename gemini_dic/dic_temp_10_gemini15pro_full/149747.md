palatalities:/ˌpæləˈtælɪtiz/| n.|腭音化；腭音|The sound change involving palatalization is common in many languages. 许多语言中都存在涉及腭音化的语音变化。|Palatalization is a sound change in which a consonant becomes more like a neighboring palatal sound, typically /j/.  It often involves a shift in the place of articulation towards the palate.|例句：The pronunciation of "did you" often becomes "didja" through palatalization.  "did you" 的发音经常通过腭音化变成 "didja"。

例句：The influence of the palatal vowel resulted in the palatalization of the preceding consonant. 腭元音的影响导致了前面辅音的腭音化。|近义词：palatalization|反义词:|noun
