explosive:/ɪkˈsploʊsɪv/ /ɪkˈspləʊsɪv/| adj. n.|爆炸的；爆炸物|Relating to or involving an explosion; a substance that explodes.|例句：The explosive device was detonated remotely.  爆炸装置被远程引爆了。|近义词：volatile, inflammable, eruptive|反义词：stable, inert|词性：形容词

explosive:/ɪkˈsploʊsɪv/ /ɪkˈspləʊsɪv/| n.|爆炸物|A substance that explodes.|例句：Dynamite is a powerful explosive. 炸药是一种强力炸药。|近义词：bomb, dynamite, TNT|反义词： (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用safety device 安全装置)|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *explosīvus, from Latin explōdere (to explode), from ex- (out) + plaudere (to clap, applaud).
