homophobic:/ˌhəʊməˈfəʊbɪk/ (UK) /ˌhoʊməˈfoʊbɪk/ (US)| adj.| 恐同的，恐同症的 | Having or showing a dislike of or prejudice against homosexual people. |  Example 1: He made several homophobic remarks during the meeting. 他在会议上发表了几句恐同言论。

Example 2:  The homophobic graffiti on the wall was disgusting. 墙上的恐同涂鸦令人作呕。 | 近义词：anti-gay, prejudiced against homosexuals | 反义词: gay-friendly, tolerant of homosexuals | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  From homo- (combining form meaning "same," from Greek homos) + -phobic (suffix meaning "fearful of," from Greek phobos "fear").
