nodule:/ˈnɒdʒuːl/ (US) /ˈnɑːdʒuːl/| n. |小结，小瘤，节结|A small swelling or lump. |例句：The doctor discovered a nodule on her thyroid gland. 医生在她的甲状腺上发现了一个小结。

例句：The plant's roots were covered in nitrogen-fixing nodules.  这种植物的根上覆盖着固氮根瘤。

近义词：lump, bump, swelling, growth, node|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps concavity/depression if referring to shape)|词性: noun
