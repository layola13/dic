magnetite:/ˈmæɡnɪtaɪt/| n. |磁铁矿|A black iron oxide that is the most common magnetic mineral.  A naturally magnetic mineral.|例句：Magnetite is a common source of iron. 磁铁矿是铁的常见来源。

例句：The compass needle was attracted to the magnetite in the rock. 指南针被岩石中的磁铁矿吸引。|近义词：lodestone, iron oxide |反义词: None (No direct antonym for a specific mineral) |词性:noun
