nontheistically:/ˌnɒnˌθiːɪˈstɪkəli/
|adv.|非神论地，无神论地|Without reference to or belief in a god or gods; from a secular perspective.|He argued nontheistically that morality doesn't require a divine source. 他从非神论的角度论证，道德不需要神圣的来源。|
|Synonyms: secularly, humanistically|
|Antonyms: theistically, religiously|
|词性: 副词|

|Synonyms: secular, humanist, unbelieving|
|Antonyms: theistic, religious, believing|
