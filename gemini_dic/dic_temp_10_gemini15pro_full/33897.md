circumvallate:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈvæleɪt/ /ˌsɝːkəmˈvæleɪt/| adj.|环状的，被墙包围的|Surrounded by a wall or rampart, especially of a particular shape.  Specifically referring to a type of papillae on the tongue.|The city was circumvallated by a high wall. 这座城市被高墙包围。|Synonyms: encircled, encompassed, surrounded, enclosed|Antonyms: open, exposed|adjective

circumvallate:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈvæleɪt/ /ˌsɝːkəmˈvæleɪt/| v.|包围，环绕|To surround with a rampart or wall.|The Roman army circumvallated the enemy city. 罗马军队包围了敌人的城市。|Synonyms: encircle, encompass, surround, besiege|Antonyms: liberate, free|verb
