outlinger:/ˌaʊtˈlɪŋɡər/| n.| 幸存者；比别人活得更久的人；残留物；遗留物 |Someone or something that continues to exist or remain after others have gone or ceased to exist. | The outlinger of a once great civilization clung to their traditions.  那个曾经伟大文明的残存者坚守着他们的传统。 | survivors, remnants, relics, vestiges | predecessors, forerunners | noun
outlinger:/ˌaʊtˈlɪŋɡər/| v.| 比...活得更久；比...持续更长 | To live or last longer than someone or something else. | The old oak tree outlingered all the others in the forest. 这棵老橡树比森林里所有其他树都活得更久。| outlive, survive, last longer than | precede, predate | verb
