deas 不是一个标准的英文单词。 你可能是想问 "ideas"。如果是这样，以下是"ideas"的解析：

/aɪˈdɪəz/ | n. | 想法；主意；概念 |  A thought or suggestion as to a possible course of action. |  She has some great ideas for the new project. 她对这个新项目有一些很棒的想法。 | synonyms: thoughts, concepts, notions, plans, suggestions | antonyms: realities, facts, actualities | noun

/aɪˈdɪəz/ | n. | 意见；看法 | An opinion or belief. | He has strong ideas about education. 他对教育有很强烈的看法。 | synonyms: opinions, beliefs, views, convictions | antonyms: uncertainties, doubts | noun

/aɪˈdɪəz/ | n. | 模糊的印象；感觉 | A vague impression or notion. | I had no idea what time it was. 我不知道现在几点。 | synonyms: impressions, notions, feelings, senses | antonyms: certainties, knowledge | noun
