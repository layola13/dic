depositor:/dɪˈpɒzɪtər/ /dɪˈpɑːzɪtər/| n. |存款人；储户|A person who puts money into a bank account. |例句：The depositor withdrew all his savings from the bank. 储户从银行提取了所有存款。

例句：The bank offered a higher interest rate to attract new depositors. 为了吸引新的储户，银行提供了更高的利率。|近义词：saver, investor, contributor|反义词：borrower, debtor|词性:noun
