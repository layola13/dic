contrabasso:/ˌkɒntrəˈbæsəʊ/ (UK), /ˌkɑːntrəˈbɑːsoʊ/ (US)| n.|低音提琴；倍大提琴|A large stringed instrument, the largest and lowest-pitched of the violin family.|The contrabasso provides the bass line in the orchestra. 低音提琴在管弦乐队中提供低音线。|The orchestra’s contrabasso section was superb.  这支管弦乐队的低音提琴部分非常出色。|Synonyms: double bass, bass viol, string bass|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, perhaps piccolo or violin as highest pitched instruments)|noun
