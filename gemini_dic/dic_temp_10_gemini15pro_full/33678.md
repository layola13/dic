cinnabar:/ˈsɪnəˌbɑːr/ | n. | 硃砂，辰砂;  鲜红色 | A bright red mineral consisting of mercury sulfide, used as a pigment. | The artist used cinnabar to create a vibrant red in the painting. 艺术家用硃砂在画中创造了鲜艳的红色。| Synonyms: vermilion, scarlet | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for the color, but perhaps pale, dull) | noun
