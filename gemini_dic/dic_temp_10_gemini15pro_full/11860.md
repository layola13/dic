arriswise:/ˈærɪswaɪz/| adv.| 棱边朝上地，斜着地 | Placed diagonally or with the arris or edge uppermost. | The bricks were laid arriswise to create a decorative pattern.  砖块斜着铺设以形成装饰图案。 | diagonally, obliquely | straight | adverb
Etymology:  From "arris" (meaning a sharp edge, especially of a building stone) + "-wise" (a suffix indicating manner or direction).  "Arris" ultimately comes from Old French "arest."
