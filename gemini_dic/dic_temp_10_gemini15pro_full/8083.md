anthroponomy: /ˌænθrəˈpɒnəmi/
n.
人类命名学; 人名学
The study of human names, their origins, meanings, and cultural significance.
Anthroponomy is a fascinating field that explores the history and evolution of personal names. 人类命名学是一个迷人的领域，探索人名的历史和演变。
The anthroponomy of ancient Greece reveals much about their social structures. 古希腊的人名学揭示了他们的社会结构。
近义词: onomastics, name study
反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, but one might consider anonymity as a contrasting concept)
词性: noun
