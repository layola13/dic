assessments:/əˈsɛsmənts/| n.|评估，评价，估价|The process of evaluating or estimating the nature, quality, ability, extent, or significance of something. |The teacher gave us regular assessments to track our progress. 老师定期对我们进行评估以跟踪我们的学习进度。|synonyms: evaluations, appraisals, estimations|antonyms:  guesses, conjectures|noun
