enlighten:/ɪnˈlaɪtn/ | vt. | 启发，启迪，开导；照亮 | To give someone greater knowledge and understanding about a subject or situation. | The teacher enlightened the students about the history of the war. 老师向学生们讲解了战争的历史。

The experience enlightened me about the difficulties faced by refugees. 这段经历让我了解了难民面临的困境。

近义词: educate, inform, illuminate, edify | 反义词: confuse, bewilder, obscure | 词性: 动词
