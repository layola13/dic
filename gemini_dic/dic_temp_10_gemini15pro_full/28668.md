caring:/ˈkɛərɪŋ/| adj.|关怀的，体贴的，有爱心的|Showing kindness and concern for others. |She is a caring and compassionate nurse. 她是一位充满爱心和同情心的护士。|例句：He is a caring father who always puts his children first. 他是一位慈爱的父亲，总是把孩子放在第一位。

例句：The nurses provided caring support to the patients. 护士们为病人提供了关怀备至的支持。


近义词：compassionate, kind, sympathetic, empathetic, solicitous, tender, loving|反义词：uncaring, indifferent, callous, heartless, cold, cruel|词性:形容词
