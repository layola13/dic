crinoidean:/kraɪˈnɔɪdiən/| n.|海百合|A sea lily or feather star.|例句：The museum displayed a beautiful crinoidean specimen. 博物馆展出了一个美丽的海百合标本。|近义词：crinoid|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *crinoïde*, from Ancient Greek κρῑνοειδής (krīnoeidḗs, “lily-like”), from κρῑνον (krīnon, “lily”) + -ειδής (-eidḗs, “-like, -oid”).
