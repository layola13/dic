overproud:/ˌoʊvərˈpraʊd/| adj.| 过于骄傲的，自负的 | Excessively proud; arrogant.  Having or showing too much pride in oneself or one's achievements.  | He was overproud of his new car and boasted about it constantly.  他对他那辆新车过于骄傲，并且不断地吹嘘。 | She's overproud of her son's accomplishments. 她对她儿子的成就过于自豪。 |  近义词：arrogant, haughty, conceited, vain | 反义词：humble, modest | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above") + "proud" (from Old French prud, prod "valiant, brave," from Vulgar Latin *prodem, from Latin prode "to be useful, to benefit," originally "to be of value in front," from pro- "forth" + esse "to be")
