deritualize:/diːˈrɪtʃuəlaɪz/
verb
使非仪式化；使世俗化
To divest of ritual or ritualistic character; to make secular.
The modern wedding ceremony has been largely deritualized.  现代婚礼仪式在很大程度上已经世俗化了。
They are trying to deritualize religion and make it more accessible to young people. 他们正试图使宗教仪式世俗化，以便让年轻人更容易接受。
近义词: secularize, demystify, desacralize
反义词: ritualize, sanctify
词性: verb

Etymology:  de- (prefix meaning "remove, reverse") + ritual + -ize (suffix forming verbs)
