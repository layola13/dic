altimetry: /ælˈtɪmətri/ | n. | 测高学，测高术 | The science of measuring altitude, especially above sea level. | Example 1: Altimetry is crucial for aircraft navigation. 测高术对于飞机导航至关重要。

Example 2: Satellite altimetry is used to map the ocean floor. 卫星测高术被用来绘制海底地图。 | Synonym: hypsometry | Antonym: bathymetry | noun
