interdepends:/ˌɪntər dɪˈpɛndz/
verb
互相依赖，相互依存
To rely on each other; to be mutually dependent.
The two species interdepend on each other for survival. 这两个物种相互依存以求生存。
Synonyms: rely on each other, be interconnected, be mutually dependent
Antonyms: be independent, be self-sufficient
词性:v.
