monodelph:/ˈmɒnəˌdɛlf/ /ˈmɑːnəˌdɛlf/ | n. |单孔目动物|A mammal of the infraclass Monodelphia; a placental mammal.  Placental mammals nourish their unborn offspring through a placenta.|The kangaroo is a monodelph, giving birth to relatively undeveloped young that continue development in a pouch. 袋鼠是单孔目动物，产下相对未发育的幼崽，并在育儿袋中继续发育。|近义词：placental mammal, eutherian|反义词:prototherian (e.g., monotreme), metatherian (e.g., marsupial)|词性:noun

Etymology: From  Greek *mónos* (“single, alone”) + *delphýs* (“womb”).  Refers to the single vagina characteristic of placental mammals.
