leucoturic:/ˌluːkəˈtjʊrɪk/| adj.|亮白色的，与尿酸有关的|Relating to or designating a white crystalline substance obtained by the oxidation of uric acid.|Leucoturic acid is a product of uric acid oxidation. 亮白尿酸是尿酸氧化后的产物。|Synonyms: white, achromatic|Antonyms: colored, chromatic|adjective
