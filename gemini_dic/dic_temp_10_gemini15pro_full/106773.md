labed:/ˈleɪbd/
verb
labeled (US), labelled (UK)
贴上标签；标记；把…称为
She labeled the box "fragile." 她把盒子贴上“易碎”的标签。
近义词: tagged, marked, designated, identified
反义词: unlabeled, unmarked


labed:/ˈleɪbd/
adjective
labeled (US), labelled (UK)
贴上标签的；标记的；被称作…的
The product was labeled "organic." 该产品被标记为“有机”。
近义词: tagged, marked, designated, identified
反义词: unlabeled, unmarked
