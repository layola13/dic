durst:/dərst/ | v. | 敢，胆敢 | dared (past tense of dare) -  had the courage to do something | He durst not speak his mind. 他不敢说出自己的想法。 | 近义词: dared, ventured, risked | 反义词: feared, hesitated, cowered | 词性: 动词 (past tense of dare)
词源: From Middle English *dursten*, from Old English *dorste* (past tense of *durran*), from Proto-Germanic *daursti*, from Proto-Indo-European *dher-*, meaning “to hold firm, be steadfast.”
