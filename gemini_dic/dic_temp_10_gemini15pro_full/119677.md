midgarth:/ˈmɪdɡɑːrθ/
n.
人间；地上世界；人类居住的世界
The world inhabited by humans, the earth.
The hero returned from his adventures to the familiar comforts of midgarth.  这位英雄在冒险结束后回到了熟悉而舒适的人间。
The tales spoke of strange creatures dwelling beneath midgarth.  这些故事讲述了居住在人间之下的奇怪生物。
Synonyms: earth, world, human world
Antonyms: heavens, underworld, afterlife
