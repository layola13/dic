dilemmatically:/ˌdɪləˈmætɪkli/
| adv. |进退两难地，左右为难地|In a way that relates to or involves a dilemma; in a manner characterized by a difficult or perplexing situation involving a choice between equally undesirable alternatives.|She was dilemmatically caught between two equally appealing job offers. 她左右为难地陷于两个同样吸引人的工作机会之间。|Synonym: awkwardly, uncertainly, hesitantly|Antonym: decisively, resolutely, confidently|词性:副词

dilemma:/dɪˈlɛmə/
| n. |困境，两难境地|A situation in which a difficult choice has to be made between two or more alternatives, especially equally undesirable ones.|He faced the dilemma of whether to tell the truth and risk losing his job, or lie and keep his job. 他面临着是否说出真相并冒着失去工作的风险，或者撒谎并保住工作的两难境地。|Synonym: predicament, quandary, Catch-22|Antonym: solution, advantage, benefit|词性:名词
