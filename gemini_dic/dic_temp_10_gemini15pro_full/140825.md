on our part:/ɒn ɑːr pɑːrt/ | adverbial phrase |在我们这边，就我们而言|  Refers to actions or responsibilities that belong to us or are expected of us. It emphasizes the speaker's or their group's role in a situation. | The delay was entirely on our part.  延误完全是我们的责任。| Synonyms: on our behalf, from our side, as far as we're concerned | Antonyms: on their part, on your part | phrase
