demurrage:/ˈdɛmərɪdʒ/| n.|滞期费，延滞费|A charge payable to the owner of a chartered ship on failure to load or discharge the ship within the time agreed.|The company had to pay demurrage charges for the delay in unloading the goods.  公司不得不支付货物卸载延误的滞期费。|Example: The shipping company charged us a hefty demurrage fee due to the port congestion.  由于港口拥堵，航运公司向我们收取了高额的滞期费。|Synonyms: detention charges, delay penalty|Antonyms: dispatch money, premium|Noun

demurrage:/dɪˈmʌrɪdʒ/| n.|滞期，延滞|The period of time for which demurrage is payable.|The demurrage on the vessel was considerable due to unforeseen circumstances. 由于不可预见的情况，这艘船的滞期时间相当长。|Example: The contract stipulated a demurrage period of five days. 合同规定滞期时间为五天。|Synonyms: delay, detention|Antonyms: expedition, dispatch|Noun
