gramophone: /ˈɡræməfəʊn/ (UK), /ˈɡræməfoʊn/ (US) | n. | 留声机 | A machine that reproduces sound by placing a needle in contact with a grooved rotating disc.  |  We listened to old records on my grandfather's gramophone. 我们用我祖父的留声机听了些老唱片。 |  record player, phonograph |  - | noun
Etymology: From Greek *γράμμα (gramma, “letter, writing”) + φωνή (phōnē, “sound, voice”).
