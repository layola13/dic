glaciate:/ˈɡleɪʃieɪt/ /ˈɡlæsieɪt/| v. |冰川作用；被冰川覆盖|To cover with glaciers or ice sheets; to subject to glacial action.|The valleys were glaciated during the last ice age. 这些山谷在上一个冰河时代被冰川覆盖。|近义词：freeze, ice over|反义词:melt, thaw|词性：动词

glaciate:/ˈɡleɪʃieɪt/ /ˈɡlæsieɪt/| adj. |冰川的；冰河时代的|Pertaining to glaciers or ice ages; covered with glaciers or ice sheets.|The researchers discovered glaciated landscapes in the region. 研究人员在该地区发现了冰川化的地貌。|近义词：glacial, icy|反义词:tropical, temperate|词性：形容词

词源：From Latin *glaciātus*, past participle of *glaciāre* (“to freeze, turn to ice”), from *glacies* (“ice”).
