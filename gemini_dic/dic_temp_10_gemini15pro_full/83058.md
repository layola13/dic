gurings:/ˈɡʊərɪŋz/
n.
加强筋，支撑物
Reinforcements or supports, often diagonal, used to strengthen a structure.
The bridge's gurings provided extra stability against strong winds.  桥梁的支撑物提供了额外的稳定性以抵御强风。
Synonyms: bracing, supports, struts
Antonyms: weakening, undermining
词性:名词
