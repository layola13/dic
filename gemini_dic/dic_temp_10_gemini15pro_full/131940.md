noncontradictions:/ˌnɒnkɒntrəˈdɪkʃənz/ | n. |无矛盾，一致性|The principle of noncontradictions states that something cannot be both true and false at the same time and in the same respect.| The lawyer argued that there were no noncontradictions in his client's testimony. 律师辩称，他当事人的证词中没有矛盾之处。|Synonyms: consistency, agreement, harmony, concordance |Antonyms: contradictions, inconsistencies, discrepancies, conflicts |noun
