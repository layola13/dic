nonanalogously:/ˌnɒnənˈæləɡəsli/ | adv. | 非类比地，不相似地 | In a way that is not analogous; dissimilarly; differently.  This means in a way that does not involve a comparison or correspondence between two things.  It suggests a difference or lack of similarity in how something operates or is structured. | The two systems function nonanalogously, despite superficial similarities. 这两个系统运作方式不同，尽管表面上相似。|  The brain processes visual and auditory information nonanalogously. 大脑处理视觉和听觉信息的方式并不相同。 | synonym: dissimilarly, differently, distinctively | antonym: analogously, similarly, comparably | adverb
