carry-on:/ˈkæriˌɒn/ (US) /ˈkæriˌɒn/ (UK)| n. adj.|随身行李；(飞机上)可携带登机的行李；喧闹，骚动|A carry-on is a small suitcase or bag that you can take onto a plane with you. It can also refer to a fuss or commotion.|例句：I only brought a carry-on bag for my weekend trip. 我周末旅行只带了一个随身行李包。|近义词：hand luggage, cabin baggage|反义词:checked baggage|词性:名词

carry-on:/ˈkæriˌɒn/ (US) /ˈkæriˌɒn/ (UK)| adj.|可随身携带的|Suitable for being carried onto an airplane.|例句：Is this bag carry-on size? 这个包是可随身携带的尺寸吗？|近义词：portable|反义词:oversized|词性:形容词

词源:  "Carry-on" originates from the verb phrase "carry on," meaning to continue or proceed.  Its use as a noun referring to luggage likely arose from the idea of luggage that one can "carry on" with them onto a vehicle, especially an airplane.
