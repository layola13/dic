cute: /kjut/ | adj. | 可爱的，漂亮的 | Delightfully pretty or attractive. |  She has a cute little dog. 她有一只可爱的小狗。

|  The baby is wearing a cute outfit.  这个婴儿穿着一套可爱的衣服。


| 近义词: adorable, charming, lovely, pretty, sweet | 反义词: ugly, unattractive, hideous | 词性: 形容词
