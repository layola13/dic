advised:/ədˈvaɪzd/
verb 
建议，劝告
past tense and past participle of advise.  It means to offer suggestions about the best course of action to someone.
I advised him to see a doctor. = 我建议他去看医生。
She advised against travelling alone at night. = 她建议不要在夜间独自旅行。
近义词:recommended, suggested, counseled
反义词:dissuaded, discouraged
词性:verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *aviser "to consider, deliberate, inform," from Vulgar Latin *advisare, from Latin ad- "to" + visum, past participle of videre "to see."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He advises her on financial matters. = 他在财务问题上给她提供建议。
The doctor advises patients to eat healthy foods. = 医生建议病人吃健康食品。

一般过去时:
I advised him to be careful. = 我劝他小心。
They advised against the merger. = 他们建议不要合并。

现在完成时:
She has advised me on several occasions. = 她已经多次给我提过建议。
The committee has advised the president on the issue. = 委员会已经就这个问题向总统提出了建议。

过去完成时:
I had advised him to leave before it got dark. = 我曾建议他在天黑前离开。
They had advised against the investment before the market crashed. = 在市场崩盘之前，他们就建议不要投资。

将来时:
I will advise you on the best course of action. = 我会建议你采取最佳行动方案。
She will advise him to seek legal counsel. = 她会建议他寻求法律顾问。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would advise against it. = 如果我是你，我会建议不要这样做。
I wish he had advised me sooner. = 我希望他早点建议我。

被动语态:
He was advised to take some time off work. = 有人建议他休假一段时间。
The students were advised of the changes to the exam schedule. = 学生们被告知了考试时间表的变更。

疑问句:
Who advised you to do that? = 谁建议你这样做的？
What did the doctor advise? = 医生建议了什么？

否定句:
I did not advise him to quit his job. = 我没有建议他辞职。
She did not advise against the trip. = 她没有建议不要去旅行。


PHRASE->
be advised that =  请注意/被告知
as advised =  如建议
ill-advised =  不明智的
well-advised =  明智的
poorly advised =  建议不当
