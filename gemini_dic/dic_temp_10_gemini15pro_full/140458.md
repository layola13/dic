old-fashioned:/ˌoʊldˈfæʃənd/ /ˌəʊldˈfæʃənd/ | adj. |老式的，过时的| Characteristic of a past era; not modern. |例句：She wore an old-fashioned dress. 她穿着一件老式的连衣裙。|例句：His views on marriage are rather old-fashioned. 他对婚姻的看法相当过时。 |近义词：outdated, antiquated, traditional, vintage, retro |反义词: modern, contemporary, trendy, fashionable |词性:形容词
词源:  From "old" + "fashioned" (past participle of "fashion," meaning "to shape or make").  The term implies something made according to the styles of a previous time.
