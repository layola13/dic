词源:  geek + -dom.  "Geek" comes from  Low German *geck (fool, freak). "-dom" is a suffix forming nouns meaning "state or fact of being," "domain," "dignity," "rank," "office," "condition," "judgment," "all the people collectively who are in a certain condition."
