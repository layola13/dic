hang-in:/ˈhæŋɪn/| phrasal verb |坚持下去，不放弃|To persevere or remain steadfast in a difficult situation.  | Example: "Things are tough, but hang in there, it will get better."  事情很艰难，但是坚持下去，会好起来的。| Example: "Don't give up now, just hang in there a little longer."  现在不要放弃，再坚持一会儿。 |Synonyms: persevere, persist, endure | Antonyms: give up, quit |verb phrase

hang-in:/ˈhæŋɪn/| n. |(非正式)聚会，联谊会 | An informal gathering or social event. |Example: "We're having a hang-in at my place tonight, want to come?" 今晚我们在我家有个聚会，你想来吗？| Example: "It was a casual hang-in with some friends."  这是一个和一些朋友的随意聚会。|Synonyms: get-together, gathering | Antonyms: N/A | noun
