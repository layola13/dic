inquirer:/ɪnˈkwaɪərər/ (US), /ɪnˈkwaɪərə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 询问者，打听者，调查者|A person who asks for information; someone who investigates something.|例句：The inquirer persisted in asking questions.  询问者坚持问问题。

例句：The journalist, a seasoned inquirer, uncovered the truth.  这位经验丰富的记者，一个老练的调查者，发现了真相。|近义词：questioner, investigator, researcher, querist|反义词: respondent, answerer|词性:noun
