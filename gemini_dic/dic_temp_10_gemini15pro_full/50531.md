depulps:/diːˈpʌlp/ | v. | 去浆; 除去果肉 |  To remove the pulp or fleshy part from a fruit, vegetable, or other plant material. | The machine depulps the coffee cherries and separates the beans. 这台机器可以去除咖啡果的果肉并分离出咖啡豆。 | The factory depulps the fruit to extract the juice.  工厂去除了水果的果肉来榨汁。| 近义词: pulp, core, pit | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 动词

词源: de- (prefix meaning "remove") + pulp (noun meaning "the soft, fleshy part of a fruit")
