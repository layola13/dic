misstyling:/ˌmɪsˈstaɪlɪŋ/
n.
错误的样式; 不合适的风格
the act of styling something incorrectly or inappropriately.
Her misstyling of the outfit made her look much older. 她对这套衣服的错误搭配让她看起来老了很多。
近义词：mishandling, mismanaging
反义词：styling, designing
