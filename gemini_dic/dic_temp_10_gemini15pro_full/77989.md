gentilize:/ˈdʒɛntɪlaɪz/ | v. |使文雅；使高贵；使温和；净化；精炼 | To make gentle or refined in manner, taste, or behavior. To render aristocratic; ennoble. |  The artist attempted to gentilize the rough cowboys in his painting.  这位艺术家试图在他的画作中将粗犷的牛仔们描绘得文雅一些。

She tried to gentilize her accent before the interview.  她在面试前试图使她的口音更文雅。 | 近义词: refine, civilize, polish, cultivate | 反义词: coarsen, vulgarize, brutalize | 词性: verb

词源: From French *gentiliser*, from gentil (“noble, gentle”) + -iser (suffix forming verbs from adjectives).  Ultimately from Latin gentilis (“of the same clan or family”).
