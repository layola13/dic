engrailment:/ɪnˈɡreɪlmənt/| n. |锯齿状，凹陷，（纹章学）燕尾形|A series of concave curves along an edge, resembling a row of arches; in heraldry, a line of partition having such curves.|The engrailment on the shield signified the knight's lineage.  盾牌上的锯齿状图案象征着骑士的血统。|近义词：indentation, notch, serration, crenation|反义词:smoothness, projection|noun

engrail:/ɪnˈɡreɪl/| v. |刻成锯齿状，使成凹陷|To cut or carve with a series of concave curves along an edge.|The craftsman engrailed the edge of the silver platter.  工匠在银盘的边缘刻上了锯齿状的图案。|近义词：indent, notch, serrate, crenate|反义词:smooth, flatten|verb
