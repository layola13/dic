linnaeite:/ˈlɪnɪˌaɪt/| n. | 硫钴矿 | A steel-gray mineral, Co₃S₄, a source of cobalt.  A metallic, isometric sulfide mineral composed of cobalt and sulfur, Co3S4. It is an important ore of cobalt. | Linnaeite is found in hydrothermal veins and contact metamorphic deposits. 硫钴矿发现于热液脉和接触变质矿床中。| Linnaeite is a valuable ore mineral due to its cobalt content. 由于其钴含量，硫钴矿是一种有价值的矿石矿物。 |Synonyms: cobalt glance, cobalt pyrite| Antonyms: N/A | noun
