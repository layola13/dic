byproduct:/ˈbaɪˌprɑːdʌkt/ | n. | 副产品；副作用 | something produced in the making of something else; a secondary result |  The company uses the byproduct of its manufacturing process to create fertilizer.  这家公司利用其生产过程中的副产品制造肥料。|

byproduct:/ˈbaɪˌprɑːdʌkt/ | n. | 副产品；副作用 |  an incidental or secondary result | Learning a new language can have positive byproducts, such as improved cognitive function. 学习一门外语可以带来积极的副作用，例如提高认知功能。| 近义词: spin-off, side effect, consequence, result | 反义词: main product, primary product | 词性: noun
