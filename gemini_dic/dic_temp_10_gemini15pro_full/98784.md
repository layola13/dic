integrally:/ɪnˈtɛɡrəli/| adv.|完整地；不可分割地；作为整体|As a whole; in an integral way.  It describes something that is essential to the whole and cannot be separated from it.|The two companies are now integrally linked. 这两家公司现在紧密相连。|Synonyms: completely, entirely, fully, totally, wholly, thoroughly|Antonyms: partially, separately|adverb
