dehaired:/diːˈhɛəd/
adjective
去毛的，脱毛的
Having had the hair removed.
The dehaired hide was then tanned. 去毛后的皮革随后被鞣制。
近义词: hairless, depilated
反义词: hairy
词性: adjective
