oophoridium:/ˌoʊəfəˈrɪdiəm/| n. |卵巢小体；卵巢滤泡|A small, fluid-filled sac in the ovary that contains an immature egg (oocyte). It is the functional unit of the ovary.|The oophoridium plays a crucial role in ovulation. 卵巢小体在排卵中起着至关重要的作用。|The doctor identified several oophoridia on the ultrasound. 医生在超声波上识别出几个卵巢小体。|Synonym: ovarian follicle|Antonym: N/A|noun
