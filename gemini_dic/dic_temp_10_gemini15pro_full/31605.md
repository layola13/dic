chastest:/ˈtʃeɪstɪst/| adj.|最贞洁的，最纯洁的|Superlative form of chaste: most chaste.  Meaning morally pure; not having sexual activity, especially outside of marriage.|She led a chaste life devoted to her religious beliefs. 她过着贞洁的生活，致力于她的宗教信仰。|Synonyms: pure, virtuous, decent, modest, immaculate, uncorrupted|Antonyms: immoral, impure, promiscuous|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *chast*, from Old French *chaste*, from Latin *castus* ("morally pure, clean").
