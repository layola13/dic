coercion:/koʊˈɜːrʃən/| n.|胁迫，强迫|The confession was obtained under coercion.  这份供词是在胁迫下获得的。|例句：He claimed the police had used coercion to obtain the confession. 他声称警方使用胁迫手段获得了供词。

例句：The government was accused of using coercion to silence its critics. 政府被指控使用胁迫手段压制批评者。|近义词：compulsion, force, duress, intimidation|反义词：persuasion, encouragement, freedom|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French coercion, from late Latin coercio(n-), from the verb coercere, from co- ‘together’ + arcere ‘restrain.’
