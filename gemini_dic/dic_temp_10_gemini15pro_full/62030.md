enation:/iːˈneɪʃən/|n.|突出物，生长物；【植】羽状分裂|An outgrowth, especially from a leaf or stem, or the process of forming such an outgrowth.  A botanical term referring to the formation of leaf-like outgrowths or appendages. It can also refer more generally to any outgrowth or process of growing out.|The fern's enations gave it a feathery appearance. 蕨类植物的羽状分裂使其外观呈羽毛状。|outgrowth, appendage, projection|indentation, depression|noun
