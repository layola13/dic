gazon:/ɡæˈzɔːn/ (US), /ˈɡæzɒn/ (UK)| n. |草坪，草地|A stretch of short, mown grass, typically in a garden or park.|例句：The children were playing on the gazon. 孩子们在草坪上玩耍。

例句：The gazon needs mowing. 草坪需要修剪。|近义词：lawn, turf, grass|反义词: wasteland, barren land|词性:noun
Etymology: From French *gazon*, from Old North French *gasun*, *gasun*, from Frankish *gas*, "sod, turf," from Proto-Germanic *gatjaną* "to stick together," akin to Old English *gad* "goad, spike."
