mailings:/ˈmeɪlɪŋz/
noun
邮件; 邮寄; 邮件发送; 邮递
the act of sending something by mail, or the things that are sent
例句：The company does several large mailings every year. 这家公司每年都要进行几次大规模的邮件发送。

例句：The mailings were sent out to all our customers. 这些邮件已发送给所有客户。

近义词：dispatches, shipments, posts
反义词:receipts

词性:n.
