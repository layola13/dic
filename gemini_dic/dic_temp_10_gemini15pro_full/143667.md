outlandishly:/aʊtˈlændɪʃli/
adjective
古怪地；奇异地；异乎寻常地
in a bizarre or strikingly unconventional manner
例句：She was dressed outlandishly in a bright pink tutu and cowboy boots. 她穿得古怪极了，粉红色的芭蕾舞短裙配牛仔靴。

例句：He told an outlandishly funny story about his trip to the zoo. 他讲述了一个关于他去动物园的滑稽古怪的故事。

近义词：bizarrely, eccentrically, strangely, weirdly, unusually
反义词：normally, conventionally, ordinarily
词性：adverb

outlandish:/aʊtˈlændɪʃ/
adjective
古怪的；奇异的；异乎寻常的
looking or sounding bizarre or unfamiliar
例句：Her outlandish hairstyle attracted a lot of attention.  她古怪的发型吸引了很多人的注意。

例句：He had some outlandish ideas about how to run the business. 他对如何经营这家公司有一些古怪的想法。

近义词：bizarre, eccentric, strange, weird, unusual
反义词：normal, conventional, ordinary
词性：adjective


词源：
From outlandish, from outland + -ish.  Outland meaning foreign land, from out + land.
