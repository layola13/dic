fertilely:/ˈfɜːrtɪlli/ /ˈfɝːtɪlli/| adv.|肥沃地，丰饶地，多产地|In a fertile manner; productively.  Characterized by abundance and growth.|The valley fertilely produced a wide variety of crops.  这个山谷肥沃地生产了各种各样的农作物。|Synonyms:  abundantly, prolifically, fruitfully, richly|Antonyms:  barrenly, sparsely, unproductively|Adverb

Etymology:
From "fertile" + "-ly".  "Fertile" comes from Middle English *fertil*, from Old French *fertil*, from Latin *fertilis* "bearing in abundance, fruitful, fertile," from *ferre* "to bear, carry, bring forth."
