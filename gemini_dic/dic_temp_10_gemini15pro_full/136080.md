nonrecital:/ˌnɑːnriˈsaɪtl/
|n.|非叙述；不背诵；不列举|A nonrecital clause in a contract means that the contract itself does not list all prior agreements or discussions.  It prevents parties from later claiming that something not specifically included in the written contract was agreed upon.|This contract includes a nonrecital clause, so previous discussions are not legally binding.| 近义词: nondisclosure, omission| 反义词: recital, enumeration| noun

nonrecital:/ˌnɑːnriˈsaɪtl/
|adj.|非叙述的；不背诵的；不列举的|A nonrecital clause is a nonrecital provision that prevents prior agreements from being considered.|The nonrecital nature of the agreement simplifies the process.| 近义词: undisclosed, omitted| 反义词: recited, enumerated| adjective
