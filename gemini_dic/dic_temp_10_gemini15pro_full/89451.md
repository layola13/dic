honest:/ˈɑːnɪst/ | adj. | 诚实的，正直的 | Free from deceit and untruthfulness; sincere. | She is an honest person. 她是一个诚实的人。| He gave an honest account of what happened. 他如实讲述了事情的经过。 | truthful, sincere, upright, honorable | dishonest, deceitful, untruthful | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *honest*, from Old French *honeste*, from Latin *honestus* (“honorable, respectable, dignified”), from *honos* (“honor, reputation, public office”).
