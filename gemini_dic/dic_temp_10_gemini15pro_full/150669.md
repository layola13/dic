papulous:/ˈpæpjʊləs/| adj.|丘疹状的，有丘疹的|Covered with or characterized by papules (small, raised bumps on the skin).|The rash was papulous and itchy.|近义词：pimply, bumpy, pustular|反义词:smooth, clear|词性:adjective

Etymology: From Latin *papula* (pimple, pustule) + *-osus* (suffix forming adjectives meaning "full of," "abounding in").
