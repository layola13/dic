endurance:/ɪnˈdjʊərəns/ (US) /ɪnˈdjʊərəns/ (UK)| n. |忍耐力，持久力|The ability to withstand hardship, adversity, or stress for a prolonged period.|Marathon runners need incredible endurance. 马拉松运动员需要惊人的耐力。|近义词: stamina, resilience, perseverance, tenacity|反义词: weakness, fragility|词性:名词

endure:/ɪnˈdjʊər/ (US) /ɪnˈdjʊə(r)/ (UK)| v. |忍受，持久|To suffer (something painful or difficult) patiently over a prolonged period.|She endured years of hardship. 她忍受了多年的苦难。|近义词: tolerate, withstand, bear, suffer|反义词: surrender, succumb|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *endurance*, from endurer "to harden, endure," from Latin indurare "to harden," from in- "in" + durare "to harden, last," from durus "hard."
