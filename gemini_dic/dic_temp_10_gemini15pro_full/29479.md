cataphor:/ˈkætəfɔːr/| n.|前指, 先行词|A cataphor is a word or expression that refers to a later word or expression in the text.|Here's an example:  After he bought it, John took the car for a drive. ("it" is a cataphor referring to "the car").|近义词：forward reference, pro-form|反义词: anaphor|noun
