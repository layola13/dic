headstone:/ˈhedˌstəʊn/ (US) /ˈhedˌstoʊn/| n. |墓碑|A slab of stone set up at the head of a grave, bearing an inscription.|例句：The headstone was engraved with her name and dates of birth and death. 墓碑上刻着她的姓名、出生日期和死亡日期。

例句：He visited his father's grave and placed flowers by the headstone. 他去探望了他父亲的坟墓，并在墓碑旁放了鲜花。

|近义词：gravestone, tombstone, monument|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a physical object)|词性: Noun
