ethnology:/ɛθˈnɒlədʒi/ /ɛθˈnɑːlədʒi/
n.
民族学
The study of the characteristics of various peoples and the differences and relationships between them.
Ethnology helps us understand the diversity of human cultures. 民族学帮助我们理解人类文化的多样性。
Ethnologists often conduct fieldwork to study different cultures. 民族学家经常进行实地考察来研究不同的文化。
anthropology, cultural anthropology
---
Etymology:
From French ethnologie, from Greek ethnos "nation" + -logia "-logy."
