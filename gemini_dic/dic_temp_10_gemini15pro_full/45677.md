curtes:/ˈkɜːrtiːz/ | adj. | courteous, polite | Having or showing good manners; polite. |  He gave a curtes nod to the ladies as he entered the room. 他进入房间时向女士们点头致意。 | synonyms: polite, civil, respectful, gracious | antonyms: rude, impolite, disrespectful, discourteous | adjective

Etymology: From Old French *corteis, from cort "court," ultimately from Latin cohors "enclosure, company."  The word originally described behavior fitting for a royal court, hence polite and refined.
