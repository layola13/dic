hyperlipidaemia: /ˌhaɪpərˌlɪpɪˈdiːmiə/ (UK) /ˌhaɪpərˌlɪpɪˈdiːmiə/ (US) | n. |高脂血症|  A condition in which there are abnormally high levels of fats (lipids) in the blood.  This includes cholesterol and triglycerides. | High cholesterol is a major risk factor for heart disease, and hyperlipidaemia significantly increases this risk. 高胆固醇是心脏病的主要危险因素，而高脂血症会显著增加这种风险。|  Her doctor told her she had hyperlipidaemia and needed to change her diet. 她的医生告诉她，她患有高脂血症，需要改变饮食。 | hyperlipemia, hyperlipidemia (US spelling), high cholesterol, dyslipidemia | hypolipidaemia | noun
