arizona:/ˌærɪˈzoʊnə/ (US), /ˌærɪˈzəʊnə/ (UK)| n.|亚利桑那州|A state in the southwestern United States, bordering Mexico.|例句：Arizona is known for its desert landscapes. 亚利桑那州以其沙漠景观而闻名。

例句：The Grand Canyon is located in northern Arizona.  大峡谷位于亚利桑那州北部。
