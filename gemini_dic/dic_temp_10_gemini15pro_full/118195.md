mentorships:/ˈmɛntərʃɪps/
n.
指导；导师制；辅导期
A relationship in which a more experienced or more knowledgeable person helps to guide a less experienced or less knowledgeable person.
The company offers mentorships to new employees.  这家公司为新员工提供指导。
The mentorship program was a great success.  这个导师计划非常成功。
guidance, coaching, tutoring, apprenticeship
n/a
noun
