construe:/kənˈstruː/ /ˈkɑːnstruː/
verb
解释，理解，分析
to interpret or understand the meaning of something, especially in a particular way
She construed his silence as a sign of disapproval. 她把他的沉默理解为不赞成。
interpret, understand, analyze, explain, decipher
misinterpret, misunderstand, misconstrue

verb
construe something as something
认为…是…
His words could be construed as a threat. 他的话可以被理解为一种威胁。
interpret, understand, take, see, regard
misinterpret, misunderstand, misconstrue
