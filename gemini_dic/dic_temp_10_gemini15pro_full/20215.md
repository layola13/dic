birdless:/ˈbɜːrdləs/| adj.|无鸟的；寂静的|Without birds; lacking birdsong, therefore quiet.|The silent forest was strangely birdless that morning. 那片寂静的森林那天早上异常地没有鸟叫。|Near synonyms: deserted, silent, quiet, still|Antonyms: bird-filled, lively, noisy|adjective
