abelian:/əˈbiːliən/
adjective
阿贝尔的；交换的；阿贝尔群的
Relating to or involving commutative operations, especially in the context of group theory, where the order of operations does not affect the result.  Named after Norwegian mathematician Niels Henrik Abel.
The integers form an abelian group under addition. 整数在加法下构成一个阿贝尔群。

Abelian varieties are a major topic of study in algebraic geometry.  阿贝尔簇是代数几何中的一个主要研究课题。
近义词:commutative
反义词:non-abelian, noncommutative
词性:adjective
