organo:/ɔːrˈɡænoʊ/ /ɔːˈɡɑːnəʊ/
| adj. | 有机的；器官的 | relating to or derived from living matter; of or relating to a bodily organ |  Organo-metallic compounds are widely used in catalysis. 有机金属化合物广泛用于催化。| organic, biological | inorganic, synthetic | adjective

organo:/ɔːrˈɡænoʊ/ /ɔːˈɡɑːnəʊ/
| n. | 管风琴；机构；机关 | a large musical instrument with pipes supplied with wind by bellows and played using a keyboard or keyboards; a part of an organism which is typically self-contained and has a specific vital function; a group of people who work together, especially one that is part of a larger organization | The church organo filled the air with music. 教堂的管风琴充满了空气中的音乐。| instrument, body part, organization | none | noun
