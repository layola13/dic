nonloser:/ˌnɒnˈluːzər/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈluːzə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 非失败者; 未输者; 赢家 |A person who has not lost; a winner. nonloser:/ˌnɒnˈluːzər/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈluːzə(r)/ (UK)| adj.(informal, rare) | 未失败的; 赢的 |Describing something or someone that hasn't lost, often used informally. |He had a nonloser attitude, always striving for success. 他有一种不败的态度，总是力争上游。 |Near Synonyms: winning, successful, undefeated |Antonyms: losing, defeated, failing| Adjective
