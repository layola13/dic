cherokees:/ˈtʃɛrəˌkiz/ | n. |切罗基人；切罗基语|A member of an Iroquoian people originally inhabiting the southern Appalachian Mountains. They were forced to migrate to Oklahoma in the 1830s on the Trail of Tears. |例句：The Cherokees were forced to relocate to Oklahoma.  切罗基人被迫迁移到俄克拉荷马州。

例句：She is learning to speak Cherokee. 她正在学习说切罗基语。 |近义词：Cherokee people, Cherokee Nation |反义词:  (无，因为是专有名词) |词性:noun
