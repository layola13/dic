antiegalitarian:/ˌæntiɪˌɡælɪˈtɛəriən/
| adj. n.| 反平等主义的；反平等主义者 | Opposed to or hostile towards the principle of equality for all people, especially in political, social, or economic life. |  The antiegalitarian policies widened the gap between the rich and the poor. 这些反平等主义政策加剧了贫富差距。|  He's known for his antiegalitarian views. 他以其反平等主义观点而闻名。| 近义词：elitist, hierarchical, discriminatory | 反义词：egalitarian, democratic, equitable | 词性: 形容词, 名词
