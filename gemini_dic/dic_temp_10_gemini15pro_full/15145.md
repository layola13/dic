back-date:/ˌbækˈdeɪt/
verb
回溯；追溯生效
to put an earlier date on (something, such as a check or contract) than the actual date
例句：The contract was backdated to January 1st.  合同的生效日期回溯至1月1日。

例句：They backdated the document to make it appear as if it had been signed earlier. 他们把文件日期提前，使其看起来像是早些时候签署的。
近义词：anachronize, predate
反义词：postdate
词性：v.
