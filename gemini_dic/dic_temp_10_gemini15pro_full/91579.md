hyperactively:/ˌhaɪpərˈæktɪvli/
| adv. |过度活跃地，极度活跃地|In a manner exhibiting excessive activity and restlessness. |The child was hyperactively running around the room.  这个孩子在房间里过度活跃地跑来跑去。|
例句：
He paced hyperactively around the room. 他在房间里踱来踱去，显得非常焦躁。
He tapped his foot hyperactively throughout the meeting. 他在整个会议期间焦躁不安地敲着脚。
|近义词：restlessly, energetically, frenetically|反义词：calmly, passively, lethargically|词性: 副词
