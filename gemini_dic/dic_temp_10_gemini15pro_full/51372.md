determent:/dɪˈtɜːrmənt/
n.
阻碍物，损害物
Something that deters or hinders; an impediment.
The high cost of housing is a major determent to young people buying their first home. 高昂的房价是年轻人购买首套住房的主要障碍。
近义词:hindrance, obstacle, impediment, disadvantage
反义词:advantage, benefit, asset, aid
