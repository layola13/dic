dialogistic:/ˌdaɪəˈlɒdʒɪstɪk/ |adj.|对话式的，对话体的|Relating to or characterized by dialogue; characterized by an exchange of ideas or opinions.|Bakhtin's work emphasizes the dialogistic nature of language. 巴赫金的作品强调了语言的对话性。|近义词:conversational, interactive|反义词:monologic|词性:形容词

dialogistic:/ˌdaɪəˈlɒdʒɪstɪk/ |n.|对话主义|A philosophy of language and communication that emphasizes the importance of dialogue and interaction.|Dialogistic approaches to education value student interaction and discussion. 对话式教育方法重视学生的互动和讨论。|近义词:interpersonalism|反义词:monologism|词性:名词
