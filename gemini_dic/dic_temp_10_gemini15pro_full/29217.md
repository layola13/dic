casketing: /ˈkæskɪtɪŋ/
n.
装殓；入殓；棺材业务
The act of placing a body in a casket.
The casketing of the body took place in a private ceremony. 遗体的装殓是在一个私人仪式上进行的。
The family chose a simple casketing for their loved one. 家人为他们所爱的人选择了一种简单的装殓方式。
embalming, laying out, preparing for burial
