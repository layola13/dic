nonattention:/ˌnɒnəˈtɛnʃən/ | n. |不注意，疏忽|Lack of attention; failure to heed or notice.|例句：His nonattention to detail resulted in several errors. 他对细节的疏忽导致了几个错误。

例句：The student's nonattention in class led to poor grades.  这个学生上课不专心导致成绩很差。


|近义词：inattention, neglect, disregard, indifference|反义词:attention, heed, regard, mindfulness|词性:名词
