etacist:/ˈiːtəsɪst/| n.|艾塔化主义者；使用或过度使用长元音η的人|A person who uses or overuses the long vowel eta (η).|The etacist pronunciation was common in some dialects of ancient Greek.|近义词:None (highly specific term)|反义词:None (highly specific term)|词性:noun

Etymology: From Greek *ētā* (η), the seventh letter of the Greek alphabet, + *-ist*, a suffix denoting a person who practices or is associated with something.
