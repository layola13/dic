escritoire:/ˌeskrɪˈtɔːr/ /ˌeskrɪˈtwɑːr/
noun
写字台，书桌
A writing desk, often with drawers and compartments.
She sat at her escritoire, penning a letter. 她坐在写字台前，写着一封信。
The antique escritoire was inlaid with mother-of-pearl.  这古董写字台镶嵌着珍珠母。

synonyms: writing desk, desk, bureau, secretary
antonyms: N/A
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *escritoire*, from Old French *escretoire*, from *escrire* "to write," from Latin *scribere*. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\skreibh-* "to cut, scratch, draw, write."
