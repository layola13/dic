dispurveyance:/dɪsˌpɜːrˈveɪəns/ |n.|取消供应，停止供应|The dispurveyance of essential goods caused widespread panic. 基本生活物资的停止供应造成了 widespread 恐慌。|The sudden dispurveyance of fuel led to long queues at gas stations. 燃料的突然停止供应导致加油站排起了长龙。|近义词: discontinuation, cessation, termination|反义词: provision, supply|词性:名词

词源:  From dis- + purveyance.  "Dis-" is a prefix indicating negation or reversal. "Purveyance" comes from Old French "purveance," meaning "provisions."  Therefore, "dispurveyance" literally means the opposite of providing or supplying.
