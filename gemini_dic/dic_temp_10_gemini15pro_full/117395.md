medicare:/ˈmɛdɪˌkɛr (US)/| n. |医疗保险（尤指美国政府为老年人和残疾人提供的医疗保险）|A system of government-funded health insurance for people over 65 and some younger people with disabilities in the US. |例句：Medicare helps senior citizens afford healthcare. 医疗保险帮助老年人负担医疗保健费用。

例句：She qualified for Medicare after her disability diagnosis. 她因残疾诊断而有资格享受医疗保险。

近义词：health insurance, national health service (in other countries)|反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider) private healthcare|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Combination of "medical" and "care".  Coined in the United States in the 1960s.
