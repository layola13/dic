gaffed:/ɡæft/ | v. | 用鱼钩钩住；（非正式）犯错，搞砸 |  To hook (a fish) with a gaff. (Informal) To make a blunder; spoil. |  He gaffed a huge tuna. 他用鱼钩钩住了一条巨大的金枪鱼。  The actor gaffed his lines in the final scene.  演员在最后一幕说错了台词。 | 近义词：hook, snag, blunder, mess up | 反义词: succeed, perform well | 词性: 动词

gaffed:/ɡæft/ | adj. | （非正式）搞砸了的 | (Informal) Spoiled or ruined by a blunder. | The whole play was gaffed by the lead actor forgetting his lines. 整个戏剧都被主角忘词给搞砸了。 | 近义词：ruined, spoiled, botched | 反义词: successful, flawless | 词性: 形容词
