floodwater:/ˈflʌdwɔːtər/ /ˈflʌˌdwɔːdər/| n. |洪水|The water that has overflowed from a river, lake, or sea, typically during a flood. |The floodwater reached the rooftops of some houses. 洪水淹到了有些房子的屋顶。|Synonyms: deluge, inundation, torrent |Antonyms: drought, dryness |noun

floodwater:/ˈflʌdwɔːtər/ /ˈflʌˌdwɔːdər/| n. |积水|Water that has accumulated due to heavy rain or flooding, even if not directly from a river, lake, or sea.|The floodwater in the streets made it difficult to drive. 街上的积水使得开车很困难。|Synonyms: overflow, high water |Antonyms: low water, drainage |noun
