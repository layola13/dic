half-fertile:/ˌhæf ˈfɜːrtaɪl/
adjective
半育的; 部分可育的
Partially fertile; capable of producing some offspring, but not as many as a fully fertile organism.
The hybrid plants were half-fertile, producing only a few seeds. 这些杂交植物是半育的，只产生少量种子。
The half-fertile queen bee struggled to maintain the hive's population.  这只半育的蜂王难以维持蜂巢的种群数量。
Near synonyms: partially fertile, semi-fertile, subfertile
Antonyms: fertile, sterile, infertile
adjective
