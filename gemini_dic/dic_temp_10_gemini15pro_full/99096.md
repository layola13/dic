intercalary:/ɪnˈtɜːrkəleri/ (US), /ɪnˈtɜːkələri/ (UK)| adj.|闰的，插入的|Inserted into the calendar, especially of a day or month; pertaining to something inserted or added.|例句：The Julian calendar included an intercalary day every four years.  儒略历每四年包含一个闰日。

例句：February 29th is an intercalary day. 2月29日是闰日。|近义词：inserted, added, extra, interpolated|反义词：omitted, removed|词性:形容词
