barbarities:/bɑːrˈbærɪtiz/
noun
野蛮行径；残暴行为
extreme cruelty or brutality
The barbarities committed during the war shocked the world.  战争期间犯下的暴行震惊了世界。
近义词: atrocities, cruelties, savagery
反义词: kindness, compassion, humanity
词性:n. (noun)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *barbarité*, from Latin *barbaritas*, from *barbarus* (“barbarian”).
