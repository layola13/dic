allhallow:/ˈɔːlˌhæloʊ/
n.
All Hallows' Day; All Saints' Day
万圣节前夕; 诸圣节
All Hallows' Day is a Christian festival celebrated on November 1st.  诸圣节是11月1日庆祝的基督教节日。
All Hallows' Eve is celebrated on October 31st. 万圣节前夕在10月31日庆祝。
synonym: All Saints' Day, Hallowmas
antonym: (No direct antonym for a specific holiday)
