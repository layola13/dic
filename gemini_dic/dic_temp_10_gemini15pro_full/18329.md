bellicose: /ˈbelɪˌkoʊs/ (US) /ˈbelɪkəʊs/ (UK)| adj.|好战的，好斗的|Inclined or eager to fight; aggressively hostile. |例句：His bellicose rhetoric stirred up the crowd. 他的好战言辞激怒了人群。|例句：The bellicose nation threatened its neighbors.  这个好战的国家威胁着它的邻国。|近义词：aggressive, hostile, pugnacious, belligerent, warlike|反义词：peaceful, pacifistic, conciliatory, amicable|词性: 形容词
Etymology: From Latin "bellicosus," from "bellicus" ("of war") + "-osus" (suffix forming adjectives of fullness). Ultimately from "bellum" ("war").
