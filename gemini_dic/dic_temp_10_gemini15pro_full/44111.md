crispier:/ˈkrɪspɪər/| adj.|更脆的|Comparative form of crispy: more crispy.  Having a firm, brittle texture and making a sharp snapping sound when broken or crushed. |The chips are crispier than the fries. 这些薯片比薯条更脆。| The lettuce is crispier after being refrigerated. 生菜冷藏后更脆。|近义词：crunchier, firmer, brittle|反义词:soggy, soft, chewy|词性:形容词
crispier:|词源: From crispy + -er (comparative suffix). Ultimately from Old English "crisp" meaning curled. |
