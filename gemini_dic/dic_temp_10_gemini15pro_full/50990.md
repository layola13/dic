desirelessness:/dɪˌzaɪərlɪsnəs/
n.
无欲；不执著；淡泊
The absence or suppression of desire, especially craving or attachment.
Buddhist philosophy emphasizes desirelessness as a path to enlightenment.  佛教哲学强调无欲是通往 enlightenment 的道路。
He achieved a state of desirelessness through meditation. 他通过冥想达到了无欲的境界。
Synonyms: detachment, non-attachment, dispassion, indifference
Antonyms: desire, craving, attachment, longing
noun
