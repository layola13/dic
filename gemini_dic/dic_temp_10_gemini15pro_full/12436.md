asking:/ˈæskɪŋ/| v.|询问，请求|The act of requesting or inquiring about something.|He kept asking questions. 他不停地问问题。|近义词：inquiring, questioning, requesting|反义词：answering, replying|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ascian (attested only in the past participle ascod), from Proto-Germanic *askōjaną, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂es- (“to seek, request”). Cognate with Old Frisian askia, Old Saxon escon, Old High German eiscōn, German heischen (“to demand”), Dutch eischen (“to demand”).
