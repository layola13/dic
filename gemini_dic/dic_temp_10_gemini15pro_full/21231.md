blogosphere:/ˈblɒɡəsfɪə(r)/ /ˈblɑːɡəsfɪr/
n.
博客圈；博客世界
The blogosphere is the collective term for all blogs and their interconnections.  博客圈是指所有博客及其相互联系的统称。
The news quickly spread across the blogosphere. 这条新闻迅速在博客圈传播开来。
近义词:blogging community, online community
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "offline world")
词性:noun
