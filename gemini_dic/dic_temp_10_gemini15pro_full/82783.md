guiltless:/ˈɡɪltləs/| adj.|无罪的；无辜的；没有过失的|Free from guilt; innocent.|例句：He was found guiltless of the crime. 他被判无罪。|例句：She had a guiltless pleasure in her success. 她对自己的成功感到由衷的高兴。|近义词：innocent, blameless, sinless|反义词：guilty, culpable|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *giltles*, from Old English *gȳltlēas* (“free from guilt, innocent”), equivalent to *gȳlt* (“guilt”) + *-lēas* (“-less”). Cognate with Scots *giltless* (“guiltless”), West Frisian *skuldlaas* (“innocent”), Dutch *schuldloos* (“guiltless”), German *schuldlos* (“guiltless”), Swedish *skuldlös* (“guiltless”).
