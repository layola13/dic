mogger:/ˈmɒɡə(r)/ (UK) /ˈmɑːɡər/ (US)| n.| 击败者，压倒性胜利者，(尤指电子游戏或体育比赛中) 高手 |Someone who defeats another person decisively, especially in a video game or sport; a highly skilled player.| He's a real mogger at chess. 他真是个象棋高手。| 近义词: champion, master, victor, expert, pro, ace | 反义词: loser, amateur, novice, beginner | 词性: 名词

词源:  Originated in online gaming communities, potentially as a blend of "monster" and "bogger" (itself a slang term for someone clumsy or inept). This ironic usage eventually evolved into its current meaning.
