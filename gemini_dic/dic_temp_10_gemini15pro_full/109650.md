leucaniline:/ˌluːkəˈnɪliːn/ | n. | 无色品红,还原品红 |A colorless crystalline compound, C20H21N3, the leuco base of rosaniline, formed by the reduction of rosaniline.  It is readily oxidized to rosaniline, forming a deep red solution. |  Leucaniline is an important intermediate in the synthesis of certain dyes. 无色品红是合成某些染料的重要中间体。| synonyms: triaminotriphenylmethane | antonyms: rosaniline | noun
