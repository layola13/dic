misrelation:/ˌmɪsrɪˈleɪʃn/
n.
错误的关系; 不协调; 不相称
A misrelation between two things is a lack of proper connection or agreement between them.
例句：There is a misrelation between the amount of work he does and the amount he gets paid. 他所做的工作量与他得到的报酬不相称。

例句：The misrelation between supply and demand caused prices to fluctuate wildly. 供需之间的不协调导致价格剧烈波动。

近义词：disparity, disproportion, incongruity, imbalance, mismatch
反义词：harmony, agreement, balance, correspondence
词性: 名词
