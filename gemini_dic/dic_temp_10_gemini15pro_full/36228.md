cognatus:/kɒɡˈneɪtəs/ /kɑːɡˈneɪtəs/| adj.|同源的，同族的|Related by blood; descended from the same ancestor.  Sharing a common ancestor (linguistics).|例句：English and German are cognate languages. 英语和德语是同源语言。

例句：The word "night" in English is cognate with the German word "Nacht".  英语单词"night"与德语单词"Nacht"同源。|近义词：related, kindred, allied, affiliated|反义词:unrelated, dissimilar|词性:adjective
Etymology->
From Latin *cognātus (“born together, related by blood”), perfect passive participle of *cognōscō (“I get to know; I recognize”), from *con- (“with, together”) + *gnōscō (“I know, I learn”). Related to genus.
