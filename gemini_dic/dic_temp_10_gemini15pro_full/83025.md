gunsmithing:/ˈɡʌnˌsmɪθɪŋ/
n.
枪械制造;枪械修理;枪械工艺
The art or trade of designing, making, and repairing firearms.
He learned gunsmithing from his father. 他从他父亲那里学习了枪械制造。
armory, gunmaking
N/A
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From gunsmith + -ing.  Gunsmith from gun + smith (metal worker).
