caroller's:/ˈkærələrz/ |n.|颂歌者的；唱圣诞歌的人的| possessive form of caroller, meaning "belonging to a caroller" or "of a caroller." | The caroller's voice filled the church with joy. 颂歌者的声音充满了教堂的喜悦。| synonyms: singer's, chorister's | antonyms: listener's, audience's |词性: 名词所有格

Etymology:
From Middle English *caroler, from Old French carole (“a ring-dance accompanied by song”), from Medieval Latin carola, of uncertain origin.
