catechism:/ˈkætɪˌkɪzəm/ | n. | 教义问答；问答式教学；一连串问题 | a summary of the principles of Christian religion in the form of questions and answers, used for instruction |  The children learned their catechism by rote. 孩子们死记硬背地学习教义问答。| synonyms:  primer, manual, instruction, doctrine | antonyms:  heterodoxy, heresy | noun

catechize:/ˈkætɪˌkaɪz/ | v. |  教导；盘问；追问 | to instruct someone in the principles of a Christian religion by means of questions and answers | The priest catechized the children before their first communion.  神父在孩子们初领圣餐前对他们进行了教理问答。 | synonyms:  instruct, teach, examine, interrogate, quiz | antonyms:  misinform, mislead | verb
