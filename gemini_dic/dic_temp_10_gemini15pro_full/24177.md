bricklike:/ˈbrɪklaɪk/| adj.|像砖一样的，坚固的|Resembling a brick in shape, hardness, or other qualities.  Having the appearance or consistency of a brick.|The cake was dense and bricklike. 这块蛋糕又硬又像砖头一样。|近义词：brick-shaped, hard, solid, dense|反义词:soft, crumbly|词性:adjective

Etymology:
brick + like.  "Brick" comes from Middle Dutch *bricke "brick," and "like" from Old English *lic "body, form; alike, similar."
