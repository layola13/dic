brightish:/ˈbraɪtɪʃ/
adjective
稍微明亮的，有点亮的
Moderately bright; somewhat bright.
The room was brightish, even without the lights on. 房间即使没开灯也稍微有点亮。
Near Synonyms: fairly bright, somewhat bright, moderately bright
Antonyms: dim, dark, dull
词性: 形容词
