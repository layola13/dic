floccular:/ˈflɒkjʊlə(r)/ (US) /ˈflɑːkjələr/ (UK)| adj.|絮状的；小脑绒球的|Resembling or relating to a flocculus, a small lobe of the cerebellum, or characterized by tufts or flakes.  It can also describe something that looks like or is related to a flocculus, which is a small lobe of the cerebellum responsible for balance and eye movements. In a more general sense, it can refer to anything that has a fluffy or flaky appearance.|The floccular lobe of the cerebellum plays a crucial role in motor coordination. 小脑的絮状叶在运动协调中起着至关重要的作用。

The doctor identified a floccular lesion in the patient's brain scan. 医生在病人的脑部扫描中发现了一个絮状病灶。|Near synonyms: flaky, fluffy, tufted, woolly. |Antonyms: smooth, solid, compact.|adjective
