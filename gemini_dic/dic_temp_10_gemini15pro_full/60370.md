egriot:/ˈiːɡriɒt/ /ˈeɪɡriɑːt/ | n. | 酸樱桃,欧洲酸樱桃 | A sour cherry, especially the Morello cherry. | The egriot is tart and makes excellent pies. 欧洲酸樱桃很酸，做馅饼很棒。 | 近义词：Morello cherry, sour cherry | 反义词: sweet cherry | 词性:noun

Etymology: From French *égriot*, diminutive of *aigre* (“sour”), from Latin *ācer* (“sharp, pungent, sour”).
