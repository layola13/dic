doling:/ˈdoʊlɪŋ/
v.
施捨;少量发放
distributing something in small amounts.
The government was doling out food to the refugees. 政府正在向难民分发食物。
The charity worker is doling out blankets to the homeless.  慈善工作者正在向无家可归者分发毯子。

synonyms: distributing, dispensing, allotting, allocating, apportioning
antonyms: hoarding, withholding, keeping, retaining
verb
