inlace:/ɪnˈleɪs/
v.
交织；编织；使交错；缠绕
To interweave or intertwine; to lace together.
The craftsman inlaced the gold threads into the tapestry.  工匠将金线交织到挂毯中。

The vines inlaced around the old oak tree. 藤蔓缠绕着古老的橡树。

近义词: intertwine, interweave, braid, plait
反义词: disentangle, separate, unravel
词性: 动词(v.)

词源: From in- + lace, from Old French lacier, from Latin laqueus "noose, snare."
