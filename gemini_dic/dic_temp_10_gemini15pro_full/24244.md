bridoon:/ˈbraɪˌduːn/ | n. | 轻勒; 小勒衔 | A bridle with reins, used in addition to an ordinary bridle, especially on a warhorse.  A smaller, lighter bridle typically worn with a double bridle, primarily for show or dressage. |  The horse was fitted with a double bridle, including a bridoon, for the dressage competition.  这匹马为参加盛装舞步比赛配备了双勒，包括一个轻勒。| synonym: snaffle, curb bit | antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "main bridle") | noun
