birth:/bɜːrθ/ (UK) /bɜːrθ/ (US)| n.|出生，诞生；起源，根源|The birth of a child is a joyous occasion.  孩子的出生是件喜事。|例句：The birth of the internet changed the world. 互联网的诞生改变了世界。

Her birth certificate confirms she was born in 1998. 她的出生证明证实她出生于1998年。|近义词：origin, beginning, nativity|反义词:death, demise|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *birþ (attested only in compounds such as gebyrd 'birth,' cildbirþ 'childbirth'), from Proto-Germanic *burþiz (source also of Old Saxon giburd, Old Norse burðr, Old Frisian birth, Dutch geboorte, German Geburt, Gothic gabaurþs), from PIE root *bʰer- (2) "to carry, bear."
