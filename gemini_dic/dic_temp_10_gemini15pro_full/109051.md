legalising:/ˈliːɡəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
合法化；将…合法化
The act of making something legal.
Many countries are legalising the use of cannabis for medical purposes. 许多国家正在将医用大麻合法化。
Synonyms: legitimizing, authorizing, decriminalizing
Antonyms: prohibiting, banning, criminalizing
词性: 动词(gerund or present participle)


legalise:/ˈliːɡəlaɪz/
verb
合法化；将…合法化
To make something legal.
The government has decided to legalise same-sex marriage. 政府已决定将同性婚姻合法化。
Synonyms: legitimize, authorize, decriminalize
Antonyms: prohibit, ban, criminalize
词性: 动词
