orchestral:/ɔːrˈkɛstrəl/ |adj.|管弦乐的；管弦乐队的|Relating to or composed for an orchestra.|The orchestral score was magnificent. 管弦乐谱宏伟壮丽。|近义词:symphonic, philharmonic|反义词:solo, a cappella|词性:形容词

Etymology: From French *orchestral*, from *orchestre* (orchestra) + *-al*. Ultimately from Greek *orkhēstra* (the space in front of the stage in a theater where the chorus danced and sang).
