affined:/əˈfaɪnd/ | adj. |密切相关的，相似的，有亲和力的 |Closely related or connected by affinity; having an inherent similarity or compatibility. |例句：Their research interests are closely affined. 他们的研究兴趣密切相关。

例句：The two companies are affined through a strategic partnership. 这两家公司通过战略伙伴关系紧密相连。|近义词：related, connected, similar, akin, cognate |反义词：unrelated, dissimilar, different |词性：形容词

词源：From Latin affinis ("bordering on, adjacent to, neighboring," also "related by marriage"), from ad- ("to, towards") + finis ("boundary, limit, end").
