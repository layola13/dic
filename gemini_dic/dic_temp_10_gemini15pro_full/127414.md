nearsightedness:/ˌnɪrˈsʌɪtɪdnəs/ (US), /ˌnɪəˈsʌɪtɪdnəs/ (UK)| n. |近视|The inability to see distant objects clearly. It occurs when the eye is too long or the cornea is too curved, causing light to focus in front of the retina rather than directly on it.| She wears glasses because of her nearsightedness. 她因为近视而戴眼镜。|近义词：myopia, shortsightedness|反义词：farsightedness, hyperopia|名词
