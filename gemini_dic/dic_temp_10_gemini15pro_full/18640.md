benzile:/ˈbɛnzɪl/ /ˈbɛnzaɪl/| n.|二苯甲酮; 苯偶酰|A yellow crystalline aromatic ketone, C₁₃H₁₀O, used in perfumery and the manufacture of some pharmaceuticals.|Benzile is an important precursor to other organic compounds.|二苯甲酮是一种重要的有机化合物前体。

Benzile is used in the synthesis of certain drugs.|二苯甲酮用于合成某些药物。
