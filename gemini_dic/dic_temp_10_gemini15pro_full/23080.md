boundingly:/ˈbaʊndɪŋli/
adverb|跳跃地，有弹性地|In a bounding manner; with leaping or bouncing movements.|The deer ran boundingly through the forest. 鹿跳跃着穿过森林。|近义词：leapingly, springily, energetically|反义词：sluggishly, slowly|词性:副词
词源: From bound (v.) + -ing + -ly.  "Bound" comes from the Old French "bondir" (to leap, jump).
