decree:/dɪˈkriː/
v. |颁布，下令；裁定|To officially order or decide something.|The court decreed that the company must pay compensation. 法院裁定该公司必须支付赔偿金。|The government decreed a state of emergency. 政府颁布了紧急状态。|order, command, rule, dictate|countermand, revoke, rescind|verb

decreed:/dɪˈkriːd/
adj. |已颁布的，已裁定的|Ordered or decided officially.|It is decreed that all citizens must carry identification. 所有公民必须携带身份证件，这是法令规定的。|The decreed changes will take effect next month. 已颁布的变更将于下月生效。|ordained, mandated, stipulated|unofficial, unsanctioned|adjective
