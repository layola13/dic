ethnocentrisms: /ˌeθnoʊˈsɛntrɪzəmz/
n.
民族中心主义（复数）
The belief in the inherent superiority of one's own ethnic group or culture.  A tendency to view alien groups or cultures from the perspective of one's own.
Example 1:  Ethnocentrisms can lead to prejudice and discrimination against other cultures. 民族中心主义会导致对其他文化的偏见和歧视。
Example 2:  The anthropologist studied the various ethnocentrisms prevalent in the region.  这位人类学家研究了该地区普遍存在的各种民族中心主义。
近义词:  cultural bias, prejudice, bigotry, racism (when race is the focus)
反义词: cultural relativism, cultural sensitivity, tolerance
