implacableness:/ɪmˈplækəbəlnəs/
n.
无情；不妥协；固执
The state of being implacable; unyielding severity.
His implacability was frightening; he would not be swayed. 他的无情令人恐惧；他不会动摇。

近义词:inflexibility, relentlessness, rigidity, stubbornness, intransigence
反义词:flexibility, leniency, mercy, forgiveness, compassion
词性:名词

implacable:/ɪmˈplækəbl/
adj.
无法平息的；不妥协的；固执的
Unable to be appeased or pacified; relentless.
He was implacable in his pursuit of justice. 他在追求正义方面毫不妥协。

近义词:unyielding, relentless, inflexible, unbending, adamant
反义词:yielding, flexible, lenient, merciful, forgiving
词性:形容词
