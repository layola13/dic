palliations:/ˌpæliˈeɪʃənz/
n.
缓解，减轻，缓和（痛苦、症状等）
the act of making something less severe or unpleasant without removing the cause.
The doctor focused on palliations of his symptoms rather than a cure. 医生专注于缓解他的症状而不是治愈。
relief, mitigation, alleviation
exacerbation, aggravation
noun
