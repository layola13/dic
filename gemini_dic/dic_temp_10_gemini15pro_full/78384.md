gesundheit:/ɡəˈzʊntˌhaɪt/
interjection
祝你健康 (用于别人打喷嚏后)
A German word, literally meaning "health," used as a blessing after someone sneezes.
Example:  "Achoo!" "Gesundheit!"  “啊啾！”“祝你健康！”
Synonym: bless you
Antonym: N/A
word class: interjection

(Gesundheit is generally used on its own and doesn't form common phrases. The following are related concepts rather than direct phrases.)

Saying "Gesundheit" is a common courtesy. 说“祝你健康”是一种常见的礼貌。
"Gesundheit" is often used in response to a sneeze. “祝你健康”通常用于回应打喷嚏。
You might hear "Gesundheit" in American English, borrowed from German. 你可能会在美国英语中听到“祝你健康”，它是从德语借来的。
"Gesundheit" literally means "health" in German.  “祝你健康”在德语中的字面意思是“健康”。
While "bless you" is more common, "Gesundheit" is a perfectly acceptable alternative. 虽然“bless you”更常见，但“Gesundheit”是一个完全可以接受的替代词。
