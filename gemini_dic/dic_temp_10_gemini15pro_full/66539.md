exhaustless:/ɪɡˈzɔːstləs/ /ɪɡˈzɑːstləs/
adjective
无穷无尽的；用不完的
inexhaustible; that cannot be exhausted or used up
Her energy seemed exhaustless. 她似乎有用不完的精力。

The artist had an exhaustless supply of creativity. 这位艺术家有着无穷无尽的创造力。

inexhaustible, limitless, boundless, unlimited, infinite
finite, limited, exhaustible
