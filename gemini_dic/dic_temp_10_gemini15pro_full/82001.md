griot:/ˈɡriːoʊ/ (US), /ˈɡriːət/ (UK)| n.| 歌者，吟游诗人，西非的口述历史学家|A West African storyteller, singer, musician, and oral historian who perpetuates the oral traditions and history of a family or village.|例句：The griot told tales of ancient kings and heroes. 歌者讲述了古代国王和英雄的故事。

例句：The griot's songs preserved the history of the tribe. 歌者的歌曲保存了部落的历史。|近义词：storyteller, bard, historian, poet, musician|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "liar" or "fabricator" in a context where the griot's truthfulness is questioned)|词性: noun
