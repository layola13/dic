cupule:/ˈkjuːpjuːl/ /ˈkʌpjuːl/
n.
壳斗; 杯状结构
A small cup-shaped structure, especially the cup of an acorn or other nut, or a structure of similar appearance.
The acorns fell from the oak tree, their cupules still attached.  橡子从橡树上掉下来，壳斗仍然附着在上面。
The botanist examined the cupule of the beech nut.  植物学家检查了山毛榉坚果的壳斗。
synonym: husk, shell, involucre
antonym: (No direct antonym, consider using terms like "seed" or "nut" to refer to the enclosed part rather than the enclosing structure)
