pamphleteering:/ˌpæmflɪˈtɪərɪŋ/
n.
中文翻译：小册子写作；发放小册子；用小册子进行宣传
英文解析：The activity of writing or distributing pamphlets, especially to promote a political cause or point of view.
例句：Pamphleteering played a significant role in the spread of revolutionary ideas.  用小册子进行宣传在革命思想的传播中起了重要作用。

例句：The government tried to suppress pamphleteering by the opposition. 政府试图压制反对派的小册子宣传。
近义词：propaganda, advocacy, campaigning
反义词: censorship, suppression
词性：名词

pamphlet:/ˈpæmflɪt/
n.
中文翻译：小册子
英文解析：A small booklet or leaflet containing information or arguments about a single subject.
例句：He picked up a pamphlet about local tourist attractions. 他拿起了一本关于当地旅游景点的宣传册。

例句：The group distributed pamphlets promoting their cause.  该组织分发了宣传他们事业的小册子。
近义词：brochure, leaflet, booklet
反义词: book, tome
词性：名词
