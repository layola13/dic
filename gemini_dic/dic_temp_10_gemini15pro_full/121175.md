misdirect:/ˌmɪsdɪˈrekt/ | /ˌmɪsdəˈrekt/ | vt. | 误导，指错方向 | To guide or send (someone or something) in the wrong direction, either deliberately or accidentally. | The magician misdirected the audience's attention while he palmed the card. 魔术师在藏牌时误导了观众的注意力。

The complicated road signs misdirected many drivers. 复杂的路标误导了很多司机。

| 近义词: mislead, deceive, misguide | 反义词: guide, direct, lead | verb
