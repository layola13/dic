nonrepairable:/ˌnɒnrɪˈpeərəbl/ /ˌnɑːnrɪˈperəbl/
adjective
不能修理的，无法修复的
Not able to be repaired.
The damage to the car was nonrepairable, so I had to buy a new one. 这辆车的损坏无法修复，所以我不得不买一辆新的。
The antique vase was nonrepairable after it fell and shattered.  古董花瓶掉落摔碎后无法修复。

近义词:irreparable, unfixable, beyond repair
反义词:repairable, fixable, mendable
词性:adjective
