fosterer:/ˈfɒstərər/ (UK) /ˈfɑːstərər/ (US)| n.|寄养者，抚育者|A person who takes care of a child or animal temporarily, usually providing a home for them.|例句：The fosterer provided a loving home for the abandoned puppy. 寄养者为被遗弃的小狗提供了一个充满爱的家。

例句：She has been a fosterer for over ten years and has cared for many children. 她从事寄养工作已经超过十年，照顾过很多孩子。

近义词：caregiver, guardian, custodian|反义词:biological parent|词性:noun
