breathalyse:/ˈbreθəlaɪz/ (UK) /ˈbrɛθəˌlaɪz/ (US)| v.| 对…进行呼气酒精测试| To test someone's breath to see how much alcohol they have drunk. | The police officer breathalysed the driver after the accident.  警察在事故发生后对司机进行了呼气酒精测试。| The driver was breathalysed at the roadside.  司机在路边接受了呼气酒精测试。| synonyms: test for alcohol, check for alcohol | antonyms: N/A | verb

breathalyser:/ˈbreθəlaɪzər/ (UK) /ˈbrɛθəˌlaɪzər/ (US)| n.| 呼气酒精测试仪| A device used to test the amount of alcohol in someone's breath. | The police officer used a breathalyser to test the driver. 警察用呼气酒精测试仪测试了司机。| The breathalyser showed that the driver was over the legal limit. 呼气酒精测试仪显示司机超过了法定限度。| synonyms: breath test device,  alcohol testing device| antonyms: N/A | noun
