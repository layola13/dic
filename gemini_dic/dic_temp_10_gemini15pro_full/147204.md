overmanage:/ˌoʊvərˈmænɪdʒ/ | v. |过度管理；管理过细|To manage too closely or excessively, often stifling creativity and initiative.|The company was overmanaged, with too many layers of bureaucracy.  这家公司管理过度，官僚层级太多。| The micromanaging boss overmanaged every project, leaving his team feeling demoralized.  这个事无巨细的老板对每个项目都管理过细，让他的团队感到 demoralized。| 近义词: micromanage, overcontrol | 反义词: undermanage, neglect | verb

overmanage:/ˌoʊvərˈmænɪdʒ/ |  |过度管理的|Managed too closely or excessively.|An overmanaged team can become demotivated and unproductive. 一个被过度管理的团队可能会变得缺乏动力，生产力低下。|The overmanaged project eventually failed due to lack of flexibility. 这个过度管理的项目最终由于缺乏灵活性而失败。|近义词: overcontrolled | 反义词: undermanaged | adjective
