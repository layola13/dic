auctioneer:/ˌɔːkʃəˈnɪər/ | n. |拍卖师|A person who conducts auctions by accepting bids and declaring goods sold.|The auctioneer banged the gavel and declared the painting sold. 拍卖师敲响木槌，宣布这幅画售出。|Synonyms:  seller, broker, agent|Antonyms: bidder, buyer|noun

auctioneer:/ˌɔːkʃəˈnɪr/ | v. |拍卖|To sell something at an auction.|He auctioneered off his collection of vintage cars. 他拍卖了他收藏的老爷车。|Synonyms: sell, vend, auction|Antonyms: buy, purchase|verb
