eolis:/ˈiːəlɪs/| n.|风成作用；风蚀作用|The process by which wind erodes, transports, and deposits sediment.|The eolis shaped the landscape into strange formations. 风蚀作用把地貌塑造成奇特的形状。|近义词：wind erosion|反义词:deposition|词性:noun
Etymology: From Greek *Aiolos*, the god of the winds.
