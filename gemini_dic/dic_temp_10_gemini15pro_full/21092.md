blinked:/blɪŋkt/ | v. | 眨眼，闪烁 | closed and opened the eyes quickly |  She blinked rapidly as she stepped out into the bright sunlight. 她走出屋外，来到灿烂的阳光下，快速地眨了眨眼睛。

He blinked in surprise when he heard the news. 当他听到这个消息时，惊讶地眨了眨眼睛。 | 近义词: winked, flickered, flashed | 反义词: stared, gazed | 词性: 动词

词源: From Middle English *blinken, frequentative of  blinke (“to gleam, shine, glitter”), from Old English *blincan (“to blink, twinkle”), from Proto-Germanic *blinkaną (“to blink, gleam”). Cognate with Dutch blinken (“to shine, flash, twinkle”), German blinken (“to blink, flash”), Swedish blinka (“to blink, twinkle”).
