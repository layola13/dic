homotaxial:/ˌhɒməˈtæksiəl/ | adj. | 同轴的，同向的 | Having the same taxonomic arrangement, or axes similarly arranged. | The homotaxial deposits revealed similar fossil assemblages, suggesting contemporaneous environments.  同轴沉积物揭示了相似的化石组合，表明它们是同时代的环境。| Example 2: The homotaxial nature of the rock layers allowed geologists to correlate them across vast distances. 岩层的同轴性质使地质学家能够在广阔的距离上关联它们。 | synonym: coaxial, conformable | antonym: unconformable | adjective
