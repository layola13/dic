dereliction:/ˌdɛrəˈlɪkʃən/
n.
失职, 疏忽, 废弃, 破败
Willful dereliction of duty is a serious charge. 故意玩忽职守是一项严重指控。
The house fell into a state of dereliction. 这房子变得破败不堪。
Synonyms: negligence, abandonment, neglect, dilapidation
Antonyms: care, attention, maintenance, repair
