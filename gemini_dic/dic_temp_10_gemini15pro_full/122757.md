moisturising: /ˈmɔɪstʃəraɪzɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈmɔɪstʃəˌraɪzɪŋ/ (US)| adj.|保湿的，润肤的|Adding moisture to something, especially skin. Making something less dry.|例句：I use a moisturising cream every day. 我每天都使用保湿霜。|近义词：hydrating, emollient|反义词：drying, dehydrating|词性:形容词

moisturize: /ˈmɔɪstʃəraɪz/ (UK) /ˈmɔɪstʃəˌraɪz/ (US)| v.|保湿，滋润|To make something less dry, especially skin.|例句：You should moisturize your skin after showering. 你应该在淋浴后滋润你的皮肤。|近义词：hydrate, soften|反义词：dry, dehydrate|词性:动词
