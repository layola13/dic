fulminuric:/ˌfʊlmɪˈnjʊərɪk/
adjective
雷酸的；异氰尿酸的
Relating to or derived from fulminic acid, a highly explosive and unstable acid.  Characterized by sudden violence or explosiveness.
Fulminuric acid is a chemical compound.  Its silver salt is explosive.
近义词: explosive, volatile, unstable
反义词: stable, inert
词性: adjective
