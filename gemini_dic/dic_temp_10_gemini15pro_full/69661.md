fencelike:/ˈfɛnsˌlaɪk/
adjective
像栅栏的，类似栅栏的
Resembling a fence; having the appearance or function of a fence.
The bushes grew thick and fencelike, creating a natural barrier. 灌木丛长得又密又像栅栏，形成了一道天然屏障。
Near synonyms: fence-like, palinglike, palisadelike, fencey, barrier-like
Antonyms: open, unobstructed
词性: adjective
