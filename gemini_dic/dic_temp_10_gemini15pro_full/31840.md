cheekless:/ˈtʃiːklɪs/
adjective
无耻的，厚颜无耻的；没有脸颊的
Lacking cheeks; also, figuratively, impudent or shameless.
The cheekless boy lied to his mother.  这个厚颜无耻的男孩对他妈妈撒谎。
The artist sculpted a cheekless face, conveying a sense of unease. 艺术家雕刻了一张没有脸颊的脸，传达出一种不安的感觉。

synonym: shameless, impudent, brazen, bold, audacious
antonym: shy, modest, reserved, bashful, diffident
word type: adjective
