attracts:/əˈtrækts/ | v. | 吸引，引起 | Draws attention, interest, or liking; causes something to come closer | The museum attracts many visitors.  这家博物馆吸引了许多游客。| synonyms: allures, charms, entices, captivates | antonyms: repels, repulses | verb

Etymology:
From Late Latin *attractare, frequentative of Latin attrahere "to draw to," from ad- "to" + trahere "to draw."
