mandarinisms:/ˈmændərɪnɪzəmz/ | n. | 官话；中国官话；（尤指）北京官话 | Words, phrases, or idioms characteristic of Mandarin Chinese, especially when used in other languages. |  Her writing was peppered with mandarinisms that made it difficult for non-Chinese speakers to understand. 她的写作中充斥着中式英语，这让非中文使用者难以理解。| synonyms: Chinese expressions, Sinicisms | antonyms: (No direct antonym, could be considered opposite to colloquialisms or slang) | noun
