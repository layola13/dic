antisensuousness:/ˌæntiˌsɛnʃuːəsnəs/
|n.|反感官性，禁欲主义|The state or quality of being opposed to or indifferent to sensual pleasures.|He embraced a life of antisensuousness, rejecting the temptations of the material world. 他过着禁欲的生活，拒绝物质世界的诱惑。|Example:  The monk's antisensuousness was reflected in his simple life and devotion to prayer.  这位僧侣的禁欲主义体现在他简朴的生活和对祈祷的虔诚上。

Example:  Her antisensuousness led her to reject the lavish gifts and focus on inner peace. 她的禁欲主义使她拒绝了奢华的礼物，专注于内心的平静。|近义词：asceticism, austerity, puritanism|反义词：sensuality, hedonism, indulgence|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + sensuousness.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to."  "Sensuousness" refers to the enjoyment or appreciation of sensual pleasures.
