creditress:/ˈkredɪtrəs/
n.
女债权人
A woman to whom money or goods are owed.
The creditress demanded full payment of the debt.  这位女债权人要求全额偿还债务。
近义词: creditor
反义词: debtor
词性: noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *creditourresse*, from Anglo-Norman *creditriis*, *creditresse*, and Old French *credeorise*, feminine of *credeor*, from Latin *creditor*, from *crēdō* ("I believe, trust, lend"). The -ess suffix is a feminine marker.
