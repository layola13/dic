microcracking:/ˈmaɪkroʊkrækɪŋ/| n. |微裂纹，微裂化|The formation of minute cracks in a material, often caused by stress or temperature changes.|例句：Microcracking in the concrete can lead to structural weakness. 混凝土中的微裂纹会导致结构性弱点。

例句：The researchers observed microcracking in the ceramic after repeated heating and cooling cycles. 研究人员在反复加热和冷却循环后观察到陶瓷中的微裂纹。|近义词：crazing, cracking, fissure|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 intactness 完整性)|词性:noun
