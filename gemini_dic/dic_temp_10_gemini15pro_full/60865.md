elegiac: /ɪˌliːdʒɪˈæk/ | adj. n. | 哀歌的，挽歌的；哀伤的；挽歌 | Expressing sorrow or lamentation, especially for something now past.  A poem of mourning. |  The music had an elegiac quality, reminding us of all we had lost. 这首音乐有一种哀伤的调子，使我们想起了失去的一切。|  She wrote an elegiac poem for her deceased grandmother. 她为她已故的祖母写了一首挽歌。 | mournful, plaintive, melancholic, lamenting | joyful, celebratory | adjective, noun
