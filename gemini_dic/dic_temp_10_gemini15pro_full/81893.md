griffin:/ˈɡrɪfɪn/ /ˈɡrɪfən/| n. |狮鹫，格里芬|A mythical creature with the body of a lion and the head and wings of an eagle.|The griffin is a prominent figure in heraldry. 狮鹫是纹章中的一个突出形象。|例句：The griffin guarded the treasure fiercely. 狮鹫凶猛地守护着宝藏。

例句：Legends say that griffins are incredibly strong and loyal creatures. 传说狮鹫是非常强壮和忠诚的生物。|近义词：hippogriff, wyvern|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

词源: From Old French *grifon*, from Late Latin *gryphus*, from Greek *γρύψ* (*grūps*) meaning 'hooked nose'. Ultimately probably from the same Proto-Indo-European root as *grip*.
