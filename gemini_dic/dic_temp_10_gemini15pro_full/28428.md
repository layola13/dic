carbonizing:/ˈkɑːrbənaɪzɪŋ/
verb
碳化，炭化
The process of heating a substance in the absence of air to decompose it into carbon and other volatile products.
The wood was carbonized in a kiln to produce charcoal. 木材在窑中碳化以生产木炭。
charring, coking
calcining
v.
