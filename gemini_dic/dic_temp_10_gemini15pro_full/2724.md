adzuki:/æˈdzuːkiː/ /əˈdzuːki/
noun
红豆
A small, round, dark red bean grown chiefly in East Asia and used in Japanese, Chinese, and Korean cuisine.
Adzuki beans are often used in sweet desserts.  红豆常用于甜点中。

She made a delicious adzuki bean paste. 她做了一种美味的红豆沙。

近义词:red bean, azuki bean
反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific type of bean)
词性:noun
