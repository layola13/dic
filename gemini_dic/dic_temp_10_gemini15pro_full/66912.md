PHRASE->
expel from: 从...开除  He was expelled from school. 他被学校开除了。
expel air/gas/smoke: 排出空气/气体/烟雾  The engine expelled smoke. 发动机排出了烟雾。
be expelled for: 因为...被开除 He was expelled for cheating. 他因为作弊被开除了。
expel a foreign body: 排出异物 The doctor expelled a foreign body from his eye. 医生从他眼睛里取出了一块异物。
expel forcefully/violently: 强行/猛烈地排出 The volcano expelled lava forcefully. 火山猛烈地喷出熔岩。
