henna:/ˈhɛnə/|n.|指甲花，散沫花|A reddish-brown dye made from the henna plant, used to color hair and decorate the skin.|She used henna to create intricate patterns on her hands. 她用指甲花在手上绘制了复杂的图案。|近义词:dye|反义词:bleach|词性:名词

hennaed:/ˈhɛnəd/|adj.|用指甲花染色的|Decorated or colored with henna.|Her hands were hennaed with beautiful designs. 她的双手用指甲花染上了美丽的图案。|近义词:dyed, stained|反义词:uncolored, plain|词性:形容词
