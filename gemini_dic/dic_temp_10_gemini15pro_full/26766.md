caernarfon: /kɑːrˈnɑːrvən/ | n. |卡那封|A town and royal borough in Gwynedd, northwest Wales. It is located on the southern bank of the Menai Strait, and historically served as the county town of Caernarfonshire. Caernarfon Castle, built by Edward I, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.|例句：Caernarfon Castle is a magnificent example of medieval architecture. 卡那封城堡是中世纪建筑的宏伟典范。

例句：We visited Caernarfon during our trip to Wales. 在我们去威尔士的旅行中，我们参观了卡那封。|近义词：city, town, borough|反义词: countryside|词性: noun
