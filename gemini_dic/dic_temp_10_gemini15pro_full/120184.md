mindlessness:/ˈmaɪndləsnəs/| n.| 心不在焉；无意识；不留心 |The state of being unaware or inattentive.  A lack of conscious awareness or attention to the present moment.| He ate the entire bag of chips in a state of complete mindlessness. 他心不在焉地吃完了整袋薯片。|Synonyms: inattention, absent-mindedness, preoccupation, heedlessness, thoughtlessness|Antonyms: mindfulness, awareness, attentiveness, alertness, concentration|noun

mindless:/ˈmaɪndləs/| adj.| 无意识的；不留心的；机械的 | Done or acting without thought; not mindful. Characterized by a lack of conscious awareness or attention.|  She scrolled through social media in a mindless way. 她漫不经心地翻阅社交媒体。|Synonyms: inattentive, unthinking, careless, heedless, thoughtless|Antonyms: mindful, aware, attentive, alert, careful|adjective
