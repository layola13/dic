paraphraser:/ˈpærəˌfreɪzər/| n.|释义者，复述者，改写者|A paraphraser is a tool or a person that rewords a text while maintaining its original meaning.|She used a paraphraser to simplify the complex academic text. 她使用了一个释义工具来简化复杂的学术文本。|Synonyms: reworder, interpreter, expounder|Antonyms: plagiarist, copier|noun

paraphrase:/ˈpærəˌfreɪz/| v. n.|释义，复述，改写|To paraphrase is to express the meaning of something using different words, especially to achieve greater clarity.|He paraphrased the poem in modern English. 他用现代英语改写了这首诗。|The judge asked the witness to paraphrase the defendant's statement. 法官要求证人复述被告的陈述。|Synonyms: reword, restate, rephrase|Antonyms: quote, copy, plagiarize|verb, noun
