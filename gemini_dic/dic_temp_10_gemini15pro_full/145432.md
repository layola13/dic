overcharities:/ˈoʊvərˌtʃærɪtiz/
noun
过度慈善；过分的仁慈
Excessive acts of charity or generosity, often to the point of being detrimental or enabling.
His overcharities towards his irresponsible son only worsened the young man's financial problems. 他对他那不负责任的儿子的过度仁慈只会加剧这个年轻人的经济问题。
Near Synonyms: excessive generosity, overindulgence, misplaced kindness
Antonyms: stinginess, miserliness, parsimony
