computerist: /kəmˈpjuːtərɪst/
noun
计算机专家; 计算机爱好者
A person skilled in or enthusiastic about using computers.
Example: He's a real computerist, always tinkering with new software and hardware. 他是一位真正的计算机专家，总是摆弄新的软件和硬件。
Example: The computerist explained the intricacies of the new operating system.  这位计算机专家解释了新操作系统的复杂性。

近义词: programmer, software engineer, computer scientist, computer buff, techie, geek
反义词: computer illiterate, technophobe
词性: noun
