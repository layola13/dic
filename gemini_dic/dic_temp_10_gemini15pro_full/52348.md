dictational:/dɪkˈteɪʃənəl/
adjective
支配的; 专横的;  听写式的
Relating to or characteristic of dictation, especially in being domineering or authoritarian.
His leadership style was dictational and inflexible. 他的领导风格专横而僵化。
autocratic, authoritarian, tyrannical, despotic, imperious
democratic, collaborative, participative
adjective
