nucleotide: /ˈnjuːklɪəˌtaɪd/ | n. | 核苷酸 | A compound consisting of a nucleoside linked to a phosphate group. Nucleotides form the basic structural unit of nucleic acids such as DNA. | DNA is made up of nucleotides. DNA由核苷酸组成。 |  Synonym: base pair | Antonym: None | Noun
