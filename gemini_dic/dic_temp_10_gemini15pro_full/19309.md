词源：
From Middle English *bibben, frequentative of *bibben (“to drink”), of imitative origin. Related to Old English *bibo (“a beaver”), from Proto-Germanic *bebruz (“beaver”), so called for its association with water.  The meaning "Bible" is a humorous shortening of "Bible."
