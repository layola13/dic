hexagram:/ˈhɛksəɡræm/| n. |六角星; 六线形; 六卦|A figure formed of six straight lines, specifically one of the figures used in divination by the I Ching.|The I Ching uses 64 hexagrams to represent different life situations.  《易经》用64个卦象来代表不同的人生境况。| synonyms: six-pointed star, hexagram figure| antonyms: N/A| noun
