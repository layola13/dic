banjorine:/ˌbændʒəˈriːn/ | n. | 班卓林琴 | A small, four-stringed musical instrument of the banjo family, tuned an octave higher than a banjo. It is typically played with a plectrum. | He played a lively tune on his banjorine. 他用他的班卓林琴演奏了一首轻快的曲子。 |She brought her banjorine to the bluegrass festival. 她带着她的班卓林琴去了蓝草音乐节。|  近义词: banjo ukulele, tenor banjo | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a musical instrument) | 词性: noun
