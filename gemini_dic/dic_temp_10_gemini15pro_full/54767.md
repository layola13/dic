dispauperize:/dɪsˈpɔːpəraɪz/
|vt.|使摆脱贫困；使富裕|To lift from poverty; to make rich or richer.|The government implemented programs to dispauperize the impoverished rural communities. 政府实施了旨在使贫困农村社区摆脱贫困的计划。|The inheritance dispauperized her overnight.  这笔遗产让她一夜之间摆脱了贫困。|近义词:enrich, uplift, elevate|反义词:impoverish, pauperize|词性:动词
Etymology: From dis- + pauperize, from pauper + -ize.  "Dis-" is a prefix meaning "reversal or removal of," and "pauper" means a very poor person. So, "dispauperize" literally means to remove someone from the state of being a pauper.
