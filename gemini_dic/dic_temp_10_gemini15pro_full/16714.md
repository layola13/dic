barroom:/ˈbɑːrˌruːm/ | /ˈbɑːrˌrʊm/ | n. |酒吧间；酒馆 |A room or establishment where alcoholic drinks are served over a counter. | He met his future wife in a dimly lit barroom. 他在昏暗的酒吧间里遇到了他未来的妻子。| The barroom was filled with the smell of stale beer and cigarette smoke. 酒吧间里弥漫着陈腐啤酒和香烟的味道。| 近义词：bar, pub, saloon, tavern | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From bar + room.  "Bar" in this sense refers to the counter where drinks are served.
