musclebound: /ˈmʌsəlˌbaʊnd/ | adj. | 肌肉发达的，肌肉僵硬的 |  Having large, well-developed muscles that sometimes restrict movement.  | He was a musclebound weightlifter, unable to touch his toes. 他是一位肌肉发达的举重运动员，无法摸到自己的脚趾。 |  muscular, brawny, bulky, well-built |  weak, flabby, skinny | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From muscle + bound.  "Bound" in this context means constrained or restricted.  The word suggests that the muscles are so large that they limit flexibility.
