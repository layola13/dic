collectibles:/kəˈlɛktəblz/
noun
收藏品
items that are collected, often because they are considered to be valuable or interesting
He has a vast collection of sports memorabilia and other collectibles. 他收藏了大量的体育纪念品和其他收藏品。
synonyms: collections, valuables, treasures, curiosities
antonyms: junk, trash, disposables
