carelessnesses:/ˈkɛrləsnəsɪz/
n.
粗心大意，疏忽；马虎
instances of being careless; lack of proper care or attention
Her carelessnesses led to several mistakes in the report. 她的粗心大意导致报告中出现了几个错误。
近义词：negligence, oversight, inattention, heedlessness
反义词：carefulness, diligence, attention, caution
词性: 名词(plural)

词源: From Middle English *carelesnes*, equivalent to careless +‎ -ness. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *karō ("care") + *-laus ("-less") + *-inassō ("-ness").
