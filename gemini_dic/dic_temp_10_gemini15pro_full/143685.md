outlaw: /ˈaʊtlɔː/ | n.  | 歹徒；逃犯；亡命之徒 | A person who has broken the law and is hiding to avoid being caught. |  The outlaws hid in the mountains. 这些歹徒藏匿在山中。 | synonyms: criminal, fugitive, bandit, desperado | antonyms: law-abider, citizen | noun

outlaw: /ˈaʊtlɔː/ | vt.  | 禁止；剥夺…的法律保护 | To make something illegal or unacceptable. | The government outlawed smoking in public places. 政府禁止在公共场所吸烟。 | synonyms: ban, prohibit, forbid, illegalize | antonyms: legalize, permit, allow | verb
