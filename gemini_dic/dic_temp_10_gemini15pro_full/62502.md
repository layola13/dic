endothermisms:/ˈɛndoʊˌθɜːrmɪzəmz/
n.
地方性物种；特有种
Organisms that are restricted to a particular geographic region.
Endothermisms are unique species found only in specific locations.  这些特有种只在特定地区被发现。
The island's diverse habitats support a rich array of plant endothermisms.  这个岛屿多样化的栖息地孕育了丰富的植物特有种。
近义词：endemic species, indigenous species
反义词: cosmopolitan species
词性: noun
