dissective:/dɪˈsɛktɪv/
adjective
解剖的；分析的
Relating to or involving dissection; analytically detailed.
The surgeon employed a dissective approach to examine the tissue.  外科医生采用解剖方法检查组织。
The professor's dissective analysis of the poem revealed hidden meanings.  教授对这首诗的分析性解读揭示了隐藏的含义。
近义词: analytical, surgical, critical
反义词: synthetic, holistic
