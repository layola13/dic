ennui:/ˌɒnˈwiː/ (UK), /ˌɑːnˈwiː/ (US)| n.|厌倦，倦怠，无聊|A feeling of listlessness and dissatisfaction arising from a lack of occupation or excitement.|例句：She suffered from a deep sense of ennui. 她深感倦怠。|例句：The endless days of rain filled him with ennui.  连日阴雨让他感到无聊。|近义词：boredom, tedium, listlessness, apathy, languor|反义词：enthusiasm, excitement, interest, zeal|词性:noun
词源:From French ennui, from Old French enui, from anuier ‘annoy’, based on Latin in odio habere ‘to regard with hatred’.
