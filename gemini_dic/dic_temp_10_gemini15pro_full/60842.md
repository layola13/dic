electrothermally:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈθɜːrməli/
adjective
电热的; 通过电热方式的
heated or operated by means of electricity
The material is electrothermally cured.  这种材料是用电热固化的。
Electrothermally actuated microgrippers are becoming increasingly popular. 电热驱动的微型夹持器正变得越来越流行。
Synonyms: electrically heated, Joule heated
Antonyms: chemically heated, mechanically heated
词性: adjective
