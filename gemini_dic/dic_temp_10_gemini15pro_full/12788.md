assimilativeness:/əˌsɪməˈleɪtɪvnəs/
n.
同化性;吸收性;同化能力
The capacity or tendency to assimilate.  The ability to absorb and integrate new information, ideas, or cultures.
Her assimilativeness allowed her to quickly adapt to the new culture. 她很强的同化能力让她能快速适应新的文化。
近义词: adaptability, receptiveness, absorption, integration
反义词: resistance, rejection, intolerance

assimilate:/əˈsɪməleɪt/
v.
吸收;同化;消化
To absorb and integrate (people, ideas, or culture) into a wider society or culture. To fully understand and incorporate something.
The immigrants quickly assimilated into the local community. 移民们迅速融入了当地社区。
He assimilated the new information easily. 他很容易地吸收了新信息。
近义词: absorb, integrate, incorporate, digest
反义词: reject, exclude, segregate
词性：动词

assimilative:/əˈsɪməˌleɪtɪv/
adj.
同化的;吸收的
Relating to or characterized by assimilation. Having the ability to assimilate.
The assimilative processes of the human mind are complex. 人类思维的同化过程很复杂。
The company has an assimilative culture, welcoming employees from diverse backgrounds.  这家公司拥有包容的文化，欢迎来自不同背景的员工。
近义词: integrative, absorbent, incorporative
反义词: resistant, rejecting
词性：形容词
