conferring:/kənˈfɜːrɪŋ/
verb
授予，商谈，商议
present participle of confer
例句：The university is conferring an honorary degree on her.  这所大学将授予她荣誉学位。

例句：He spent hours conferring with his lawyers. 他花了几个小时与他的律师商谈。

近义词：discussing, consulting, awarding, bestowing
反义词：withholding, refusing, taking away
词性: v. (verb)


confer:/kənˈfɜːr/
verb
授予，赋予；商谈，商议
from Old French conferrer, from Latin conferre  'bring together, compare, contribute,' from con- 'together' + ferre 'bring, carry.'
例句：The Queen conferred a knighthood on him. 女王授予他爵位。

例句：I need some time to confer with my colleagues on this matter. 我需要一些时间和我的同事商量这件事。

近义词：consult, discuss, award, bestow
反义词：withhold, refuse, deprive
词性: v. (verb)
