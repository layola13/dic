counterrevolutionary:/ˌkaʊntərˌrɛvəˈluːʃəˌnɛri/
n.|反革命分子|A person who opposes or acts against a revolution, especially one who tries to overthrow a revolutionary government.|例句：The government labeled him a counterrevolutionary and imprisoned him. 政府给他贴上反革命分子的标签并将他监禁。|近义词：reactionary, rebel, insurgent|反义词：revolutionary, radical|词性:名词

counterrevolutionary:/ˌkaʊntərˌrɛvəˈluːʃəˌnɛri/
adj.|反革命的|Opposing or acting against a revolution.|例句：The counterrevolutionary forces attempted a coup. 反革命势力企图发动政变。|近义词：reactionary, subversive|反义词：revolutionary, progressive|词性:形容词
