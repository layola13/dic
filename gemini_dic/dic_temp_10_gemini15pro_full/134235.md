noninertnesses:/ˌnɒnɪˈnɜːtnɪsɪz/
n.|非惰性; 非不活泼性|The noninertnesses of these chemicals make them dangerous to handle. 这些化学物质的非惰性使它们处理起来很危险。|例句：The noninertnesses of the alkali metals are well documented. 碱金属的非惰性已被充分证明。

例句：The noninertnesses of certain gases contribute to air pollution. 某些气体的非惰性导致了空气污染。

近义词：reactivity, activity
反义词：inertness, inactivity
词性：noun (plural)

词源：
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "inert" (adjective meaning "lacking the ability or strength to move") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns from adjectives)
