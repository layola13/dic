overbrutalize:/ˌoʊvərˈbruːtəlaɪz/
verb
过度残暴对待，过度虐待
To brutalize excessively; to treat with extreme and unnecessary cruelty.
The dictator's regime overbrutalized the political prisoners.  独裁者的政权过度残暴地对待政治犯。
The captured soldiers were overbrutalized by their enemies.  被俘的士兵遭到了敌人的过度虐待。
近义词:  mistreat, abuse, maltreat, torture
反义词:  protect, cherish, nurture
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + brutalize.  "Brutalize" comes from the adjective "brutal," derived from the French word "brutal," ultimately from the Late Latin "brutalis," meaning "like a brute."  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning, indicating an excess of brutality.
