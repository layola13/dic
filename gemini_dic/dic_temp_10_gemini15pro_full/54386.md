disillusioning:/ˌdɪsɪˈluːʒənɪŋ/
adjective
令人失望的，令人醒悟的
Causing someone to stop believing that something is good, valuable, or true.
The experience was deeply disillusioning.  这次经历令人深感失望。
The trip was a disillusioning experience.  这次旅行是一次令人失望的经历。

近义词:disappointing, disheartening, disenchanting
反义词:encouraging, inspiring, heartening
词性:adjective

词源:
From dis- (expressing negation) + illusion (false belief) + -ing (present participle suffix).  Ultimately from Latin dis- (not) + illudere (to mock).
