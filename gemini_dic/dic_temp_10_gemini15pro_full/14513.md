avens:/ˈeɪvənz/| n. |水杨梅|Avens are a group of flowering plants in the rose family (Rosaceae), primarily of the genus *Geum*. They are herbaceous perennials with typically yellow, red, or white flowers.|The avens bloomed brightly in the meadow.  水杨梅在草地上开得鲜艳夺目。|The avens attracted a variety of pollinators.  这些水杨梅吸引了各种各样的传粉者。|近义词：wildflower, geum|反义词:|n.
