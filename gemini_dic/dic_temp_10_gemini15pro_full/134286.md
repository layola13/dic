| n. | 不可继承性；无法遗传性 |  The noninheritabilities of acquired characteristics is a cornerstone of modern evolutionary theory.  The traits acquired during an organism's life, like learned skills or muscle growth, are not passed down to its offspring. |  The study highlighted the noninheritabilities of certain behavioral traits. 这项研究强调了某些行为特征的不可遗传性。 |  non-hereditability, uninheritability | heritability, inheritability | noun
