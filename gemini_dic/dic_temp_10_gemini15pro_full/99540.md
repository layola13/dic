intergossips:/ˌɪntərˈɡɒsɪps/ | n. | 私下闲聊，流言蜚语 |  Exchange of gossip between people, especially in a private or secretive way. This word is not standard English and is rarely used.  It's likely a neologism formed by combining "inter-" (meaning "between") and "gossips" (plural of "gossip"). |  "The intergossips among the staff about the new boss were rampant." 员工之间关于新老板的私下闲聊很猖獗。 (This example uses the word even though it's not standard.) | 近义词：whispers, rumors, scuttlebutt | 反义词:  official announcements, facts | 词性: 名词
