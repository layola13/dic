overoptimistically: /ˌoʊvərˌɑːptɪˈmɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 过于乐观地 | In a way that is excessively or unrealistically optimistic. | He overoptimistically predicted a quick victory. 他过于乐观地预测了一场速胜。 | Synonyms: excessively optimistically, unrealistically optimistically, unduly optimistically | Antonyms: pessimistically, realistically, cautiously | Word Type: Adverb

Etymology:
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "optimistic" (adjective meaning "hopeful and confident about the future") + "-ally" (suffix forming adverbs).  "Optimistic" itself comes from the Latin "optimus" meaning "best."
