albino:/ælˈbaɪnoʊ/ (US), /ælˈbiːnəʊ/ (UK)| n. adj.|白化病人，白化体；白化的|A person or animal with albinism, a condition characterized by a lack of pigment in the skin, hair, and eyes.|例句：The albino rabbit stood out against the dark grass. 白兔在深色的草丛中格外显眼。|近义词：person/animal with albinism|反义词:pigmented|词性:名词, 形容词

albino:/ælˈbaɪnoʊ/ (US), /ælˈbiːnəʊ/ (UK)| adj.|白化的|Having albinism.|例句：Albino animals are rare. 白化动物很罕见。|近义词：leucistic, amelanistic|反义词:pigmented|词性:形容词
