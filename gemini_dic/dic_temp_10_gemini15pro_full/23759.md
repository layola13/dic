bravi: /ˈbrɑːviː/ | n. | 好极了！；bravo的复数形式 | An exclamation of praise, especially to a performer or performers.  It's the plural form of "bravo". | The audience shouted "Bravi!" at the end of the opera.  观众在歌剧结束时高呼“好极了！” | 近义词: well done, excellent, superb | 反义词: terrible, awful | 词性: 名词

词源: From Italian *bravo*, literally "brave, valiant, wild".

PHRASE->
While less common as a phrase, "Hearty bravi!" is sometimes used for extra emphasis. 热烈的“好极了！” 有时用于额外的强调。
"Bravi, bravi!" can be used to express even greater enthusiasm.  “好极了！好极了！” 可以用来表达更大的热情。
"Bravi tutti!" means "Bravo everyone!"  “好极了，各位！” 意思是“大家都很棒！”
"A chorus of bravi" describes the sound of many people shouting "bravi" at once.  “一片好极了的声音” 描述了许多人同时高呼“好极了”的声音。
"The performers deserved the bravi they received." 表演者值得他们收到的“好极了！”喝彩。
