downmarket:/ˌdaʊnˈmɑːrkɪt/ /ˌdaʊnˈmɑːrˌkɪt/ | adj. adv.|低档的，面向低端市场的；低档地，向下游市场地 |Relating to or appealing to the less affluent part of the market; of lower quality or status than average. |例句：The company is moving downmarket with its new range of budget products.  该公司正通过其新的廉价产品系列进军低端市场。|近义词：cheap, budget, inexpensive, low-end |反义词: upmarket, upscale, high-end, luxury |词性: adjective, adverb
