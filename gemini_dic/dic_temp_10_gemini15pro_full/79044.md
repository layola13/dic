give-way:/ˈɡɪv weɪ/ (US), /ˈɡɪv weɪ/ (UK)| n.|让路；屈服；让步；支路|A place where one road joins another and traffic on the smaller road must yield to traffic on the larger road; The act of yielding or conceding.|例句：At the give-way, I slowed down to let the other car pass. 在让路标志处，我减速让另一辆车先行。|近义词：yield, concession, surrender|反义词：insistence, resistance|词性:名词

give way:/ɡɪv weɪ/ (US), /ɡɪv weɪ/ (UK)| v.|让路；倒塌；屈服；让步；破裂|To yield to another vehicle or pedestrian; to collapse or break down; to surrender or concede.|例句：The old bridge finally gave way under the weight of the truck. 老桥最终在卡车的重压下坍塌了。|近义词：yield, collapse, surrender, concede|反义词：resist, withstand, insist|词性:动词
