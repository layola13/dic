barbacanage:/bɑːrbəkənɪdʒ/
n.
城垛；城墙上的小孔
A fortification consisting of openings in a parapet wall to allow defenders to fire on attackers below.
The castle's barbacanage provided excellent defensive positions for archers. 城堡的城垛为弓箭手提供了绝佳的防御阵地。
近义词: battlement, embrasure, loophole
反义词: (无明确的反义词)
词性: 名词

barbacanage:/bɑːrbəkənɪdʒ/
n.
射击孔，城垛
Openings in a parapet or wall for firing weapons.
The barbacanage allowed the soldiers to defend the castle effectively. 射击孔使士兵能够有效地保卫城堡。
近义词: crenel, merlon
反义词: (无明确的反义词)
词性: 名词

词源:
From Middle French *barbacane, from Old French barbacane, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Persian  بربخانا (barbakāna, “outer wall of a fort”).
