basinlike:/ˈbeɪsɪnlaɪk/
adjective
像盆一样的，盆状的
Resembling a basin in shape; shaped like a basin.
The valley was basinlike, surrounded by high mountains.  这个山谷像盆地一样，四周环绕着高山。
The crater was basinlike, deep and circular.  这个火山口像盆地一样，又深又圆。
近义词: bowl-shaped, concave, depressed
反义词: convex, protruding


ETYMOLOGY->
From basin + -like.  "Basin" comes from Old French "bacin" ultimately from Late Latin "baccinus," a large water vessel. "-like" is a suffix meaning "resembling," "similar to," from Old English "-lic."
