blanquette:/blɑ̃ˈkɛt/ (French), /blæŋˈkɛt/ (English)| n.|奶油炖肉(通常是小牛肉或鸡肉), 白汁烩肉|A white stew, typically of veal or chicken, in a creamy white sauce.|例句：We had a delicious blanquette de veau for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了美味的小牛肉奶油炖肉。


例句：The blanquette was rich and flavorful. 这道白汁烩肉浓郁而美味。


近义词：stew, fricassée|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "grilled meat" or "roasted meat" as contrasting cooking methods)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French "blanquette," meaning "little white thing," from "blanc" (white), ultimately from Frankish *blank "white, shining," from Proto-Germanic *blankaz "white, bright," from PIE root *bʰel- "to shine, flash, burn."  The name refers to the white color of the sauce.
