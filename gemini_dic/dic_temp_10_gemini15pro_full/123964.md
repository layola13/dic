morion:/ˈmɔːriən/ | n. |  钢盔，头盔 | A type of open helmet with a curved peak in front and back, popular in the 16th and 17th centuries. | The soldier wore a morion to protect his head in battle.  士兵戴着钢盔在战斗中保护头部。 | Synonyms: helmet, casque, headpiece | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps bareheadedness as a state) | noun
