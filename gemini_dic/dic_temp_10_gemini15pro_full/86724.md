helminthological:/ˌhɛlmɪnθəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/| adj.|蠕虫学的；寄生虫学的|Relating to the study of helminths, which are parasitic worms.|Example: Helminthological research is crucial for controlling parasitic infections in livestock. 蠕虫学研究对于控制牲畜寄生虫感染至关重要。

Example: The helminthological examination revealed the presence of hookworms. 蠕虫学检查显示存在钩虫。|Synonym: parasitological|Antonym: N/A|Word type: adjective
