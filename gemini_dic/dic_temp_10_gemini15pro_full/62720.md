{engl}: what /wɒt (英式) /hwɑːt, wʌt (美式)/| pron. det. adv. int.|什么，多么|A function word used in questions to ask for specific information about something.|What is your name? 你叫什么名字？|Synonyms: which, how, whatever|Antonyms:  N/A (What doesn't have a direct opposite.)|pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? = 你认为怎样？
What does she want? = 她想要什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing here? = 他在这里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned? = 你学到了什么？
What has she been working on? = 她一直在做什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been reading lately? = 你最近一直在读什么？
What has he been doing all this time? = 他这段时间一直在做什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about earlier? = 她之前在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had she eaten for dinner? = 她晚饭吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been working on before you left? = 你离开之前一直在做什么？
What had she been studying for the past few years? =  过去几年她一直在学习什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? = 你下周末要做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time tomorrow? = 明天这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting? = 会议上他们会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by Friday? = 你到星期五会完成什么？
What will she have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底她会取得什么成就？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? = 到2033年你将已经做了什么十年了？
What will he have been studying by the time he graduates? = 到他毕业时，他将已经学习什么多久了？
