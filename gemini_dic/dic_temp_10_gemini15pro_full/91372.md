hydrostatics:/ˌhaɪdrəˈstætɪks/| n. |流静力学；静水力学|The branch of physics that deals with the characteristics of fluids at rest.  It encompasses the study of pressure in fluids and the conditions of equilibrium of floating and submerged bodies.|例句：Hydrostatics explains why a ship floats. 流体静力学解释了为什么船会漂浮。

例句：Archimedes' principle is a fundamental concept in hydrostatics. 阿基米德原理是流体静力学中的一个基本概念。|近义词：fluid mechanics, fluid statics|反义词:hydrodynamics, hydraulics|词性:noun
