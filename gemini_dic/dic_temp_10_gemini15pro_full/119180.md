mezzanines:/ˈmɛzənɪnz/ /ˈmɛzəˌninz/ | n. | 中层楼，夹楼，中间层 | A low story between two main stories of a building; an intermediate floor. | The office has a mezzanine level for extra storage space. 办公室有一个夹层用于额外的存储空间。 | The books are stored on the mezzanine. 书存放在夹层里。| synonyms: balcony, loft, gallery, landing | antonyms: basement, cellar | noun

mezzanine:/ˈmɛzənɪn/ /ˈmɛzəˌnin/ | adj. | 夹层的，中层的 | Relating to or located on a mezzanine. | The mezzanine floor provides additional office space. 夹层楼提供了额外的办公空间。| The mezzanine seating offered a great view of the stage. 夹层座位提供了很好的舞台视野。 | synonyms: intermediate, middle | antonyms: ground, top | adjective
