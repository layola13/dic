idrialite:/ɪˈdriəlaɪt/ | n. | 矿物，伊德里亚石 | A rare hydrocarbon mineral found in mercury deposits, typically as minute yellowish crystals.  It fluoresces bright blue-violet under UV light. |  Idrialite is a valuable indicator mineral for cinnabar deposits. 伊德里亚石是辰砂矿床的宝贵指示矿物。 |  The miners discovered a vein of idrialite alongside the cinnabar ore.  矿工们在辰砂矿石旁边发现了一条伊德里亚石矿脉。 | synonyms: None (highly specific mineral) | antonyms: None | noun
