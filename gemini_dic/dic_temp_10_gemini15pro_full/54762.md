dispatchful:/dɪˈspætʃfʊl/
adjective
迅速的，高效的
Characterized by efficiency and promptness in action; expeditious.
The dispatchful delivery service ensured the package arrived on time.  这家高效的快递服务确保包裹准时到达。
近义词:efficient, prompt, expeditious, swift, quick
反义词:slow, inefficient, dilatory, sluggish
词性:adjective

词源:
From dispatch + -ful.  Dispatch itself comes from the Old Italian word *dispacciare, meaning "to expedite."
