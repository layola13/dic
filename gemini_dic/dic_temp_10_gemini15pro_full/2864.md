adverse:/ˈædvɜːrs/ (US) /ˈædvɜːs/ (UK)| adj.|不利的，相反的，敌对的|Preventing success or development; harmful; unfavorable.|The drug has adverse side effects.  这种药物有不良副作用。

Heavy snow caused adverse driving conditions. 大雪导致了不利的驾驶条件。|近义词：unfavorable, negative, detrimental, hostile|反义词：favorable, beneficial, positive, advantageous|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adversus* ("turned towards, opposite, unfavorable, hostile"), past participle of *advertere* ("to turn towards"), from *ad-* ("to, towards") + *vertere* ("to turn").

USAGE->
Attributive usage (before a noun):

Adverse weather conditions forced the cancellation of the flight.  恶劣的天气条件迫使航班取消。
The company faced adverse market conditions.  公司面临不利的市场环境。

Predicative usage (after a verb like "be," "seem," "become"):

The report was adverse to his interests.  这份报告对他的利益不利。
His reaction to the news was adverse. 他对这个消息的反应是不利的。


PHRASE->
adverse to:  反对...；对...不利
adverse effect: 不良反应；副作用
adverse impact:  负面影响
adverse reaction: 不良反应
adverse conditions:  不利条件
