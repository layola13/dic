aide:/eɪd/| n.|助手，助理，副官|A person who acts as an assistant to another person, especially to someone important.|The president's aide arranged his schedule. 总统的助手安排了他的日程。|近义词：assistant, helper, supporter|反义词:opponent, adversary|noun

aide:/eɪd/| vt.|帮助，援助|To give help or support to someone or something.|The organization aides refugees.  这个组织援助难民。|近义词：assist, help, support|反义词:hinder, obstruct|verb
