ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + recalcitrance.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "lack of". "Recalcitrance" comes from the Latin "recalcitrare" meaning "to kick back" (from "re-" meaning "back" and "calcitrare" meaning "to kick" from "calx" meaning "heel").
