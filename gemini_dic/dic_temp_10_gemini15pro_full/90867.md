hungry:/ˈhʌŋɡri/| adj.|饥饿的；渴望的|Feeling a need or desire to eat food. Also, having a strong desire for something.|例句：I'm so hungry I could eat a horse. 我饿得能吃下一匹马。|近义词：starving, ravenous, famished, eager, desirous|反义词:full, satiated, satisfied|adjective
