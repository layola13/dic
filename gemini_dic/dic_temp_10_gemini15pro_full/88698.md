PHRASE->
hobble along: 蹒跚而行  The old woman hobbled along the sidewalk. 老妇人沿着人行道蹒跚而行。
be hobbled by: 受…阻碍  The project was hobbled by lack of funding. 该项目因缺乏资金而受阻。
hobble together:  勉强拼凑  He hobbled together a meal from leftovers. 他用剩菜剩饭拼凑了一顿饭。
hobble one's progress:  阻碍某人的进步 The new regulations hobble our progress. 新规定阻碍了我们的进步。
hobble someone's efforts:  阻碍某人的努力 The lack of resources hobbled their efforts to rebuild. 资源匮乏阻碍了他们重建的努力。
