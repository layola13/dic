dweebier:/ˈdwiːbiər/| adj.|更呆的，更书呆子气的|Comparative form of dweeby: more dweeby.  Indicates a greater degree of social awkwardness, unfashionable appearance, or obsessive interest in intellectual or academic pursuits.|He became even dweebier in high school, always carrying a stack of textbooks.|近义词：nerdier, geekier, more awkward|反义词：cooler, more popular|词性:形容词
