overripen:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪpən/| v. |过熟，熟过头|To ripen beyond the point of prime edibility; to become too ripe.|The peaches overripened on the tree and fell to the ground. 桃子在树上熟过头了，掉到了地上。|近义词：overmature|反义词:underripe|动词

overripe:/ˈoʊvərraɪp/| adj. |过熟的，熟过头的|Ripened beyond the point of prime edibility; too ripe.|The overripe bananas were brown and mushy. 熟过头的香蕉是棕色的，而且很软。|近义词：overmature|反义词:underripe|形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + ripen.  "Over-" signifies "excessive" or "too much". "Ripen" comes from the Old English "rīpian," meaning "to grow ripe."
