lowc: /loʊk/
adjective|低矮的；低沉的；卑贱的；粗俗的；沮丧的；衰弱的；（价格）低廉的；位置低的；（声音）轻柔的；低下的；（牌戏中）点数低的|low in stature, position, or value; not high or tall; depressed in spirits; lacking in strength or vigor; (of a sound) quiet and deep; vulgar or indecent; of low rank or social standing; inexpensive; situated at a small distance above the ground or a base|例句：The table is too low for me. 这张桌子对我来说太矮了。
