glyoxal:/ˈɡlaɪɒksæl/ | n. |乙二醛|A yellow crystalline dialdehyde, C₂H₂O₂, used chiefly in making resins and in treating textiles.  It is the smallest dialdehyde (two aldehyde groups).|Glyoxal is used in the paper industry to improve wet strength.|近义词: oxaldehyde (less common) |反义词: N/A | noun
