demotions:/dɪˈmoʊʃənz/
n.
降级，降职
a reduction in rank or position.
He faced demotions and salary cuts. 他面临降职和减薪。
n.
降级
The act of reducing someone to a lower grade, rank, or position.
The demotions were seen as a necessary step to restructure the company.  这些降级被视为公司重组的必要步骤。

近义词:degradations, downgradings, reductions
反义词:promotions, advancements, elevations
词性:名词(plural noun)
