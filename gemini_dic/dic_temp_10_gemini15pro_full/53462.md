disard:/ˈdɪzɑːrd/ | n. | 笨蛋，傻瓜 | A foolish or stupid person. | He acted like a complete disard, forgetting his own phone number. 他表现得像个十足的笨蛋，连自己的电话号码都忘了。 |  例句：She called him a disard for making such a silly mistake. 她因为他犯了如此愚蠢的错误而称他为笨蛋。| 近义词：fool, idiot, dunce, simpleton, nincompoop | 反义词：genius, savant | 词性：名词

词源: From Middle English *disard, *dysard, from Old French *disard, *dysard (“foolish, stupid”), from des- (“dis-”) + -ard (“pejorative suffix”).
