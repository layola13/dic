dupe:/dʒuːp/ /duːp/| n. vt.|欺骗，上当者；复制，复制品|A person who is easily tricked or fooled. To deceive or trick someone.|例句：He was a dupe in their scheme. 他是他们诡计中的受骗者。

The forger duped the museum with his fake painting.  伪造者用他的假画欺骗了博物馆。|近义词：fool, gull, trick, deceive, copy, replicate|反义词:perceptive, astute|词性:名词，动词
