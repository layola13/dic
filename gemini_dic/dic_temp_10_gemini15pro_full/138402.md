notwheat:/nɑːtˈwiːt/ | n. | 非小麦制品;  小麦替代品 | A general term for grains and other foods that are not wheat, used especially by people with a wheat allergy or intolerance. |  Example:  She uses notwheat flour for baking. 她用非小麦粉烘焙。 | Synonym: wheat alternative, gluten-free grains (if gluten is the concern), alternative grain | Antonym: wheat | noun
