nonmicrobic:/ˌnɑːnmaɪˈkroʊbɪk/ | adj. | 非微生物的，非由微生物引起的 | Not caused by or related to microbes (microscopic organisms such as bacteria, viruses, or fungi). | The inflammation was determined to be nonmicrobic, likely caused by physical irritation.  这种炎症被确定为非微生物性的，可能是由物理刺激引起的。|  例句：The doctor ruled out a microbic infection and concluded the illness was nonmicrobic. 医生排除了微生物感染，并得出结论，这种疾病是非微生物引起的。 | 近义词: abiotic, sterile (in some contexts) | 反义词: microbic, microbial | 词性: 形容词
