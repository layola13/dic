flighting:/ˈflaɪtɪŋ/
n.|试播；阶段性投放；（广告）短期集中投放|A marketing technique in which advertising is scheduled in short, intense bursts alternating with periods of no advertising at all.|The company uses flighting in its advertising strategy, concentrating its spending in key periods.|近义词：bursting, pulsing|反义词:continuous advertising|词性:名词

词源: From the verb "to flight," meaning to put to flight or to flee.  In marketing, the term likely arose from the analogy of birds migrating in bursts, alternating periods of intense activity with periods of rest.
