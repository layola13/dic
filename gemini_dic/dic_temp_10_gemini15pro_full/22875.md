botanomancy:/ˌbɒtənˈɒmənsi/
n.
植物占卜
Divination by means of plants.
Botanomancy was practiced in ancient Greece.  在古希腊，人们会进行植物占卜。
The villagers relied on botanomancy to predict the harvest. 村民们依靠植物占卜来预测收成。
近义词: divination, augury, soothsaying
反义词:  N/A
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek *βοτανoμαντεία (botanomanteria) from *βοτάνη (botánē, “plant, herb”) + *μαντεία (manteía, “divination”).
