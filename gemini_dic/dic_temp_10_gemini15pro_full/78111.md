geologically:/ˌdʒiːəˈlɒdʒɪkli/
adjective
地质学上地；地质地
Relating to the study of the earth's physical structure and substance, its history, and the processes that act on it.
The area is geologically diverse, with a range of rock formations.  这个地区地质多样，拥有一系列的岩层。
The canyon was formed geologically over millions of years.  这个峡谷在地质上是经过数百万年形成的。
近义词:structurally, tectonically
反义词:None
词性:adjective

词源:
From geology + -ical + -ly.  Geology comes from the Greek words gē (earth) and -logia (study of).
