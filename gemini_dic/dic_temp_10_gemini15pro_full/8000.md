anthophilous: /ænˈθɒfɪləs/ | adj. | 喜花的，由昆虫授粉的 | Attracted to or pollinated by insects, especially bees.  Relating to or dependent on flower pollination by insects. | Bees are anthophilous insects, drawn to the sweet nectar and bright colors of flowers. 蜜蜂是喜花的昆虫，被花朵甜蜜的花蜜和鲜艳的颜色所吸引。

The anthophilous nature of many plants ensures their reproduction. 许多植物的喜花特性确保了它们的繁殖。| 近义词：entomophilous | 反义词: anemophilous (风媒的) | 词性: adjective
