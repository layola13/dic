empower:/ɪmˈpaʊər/ (US), /ɪmˈpaʊə(r)/ (UK)| vt.|授权；赋能；使能够|To give someone the authority or power to do something; to make someone stronger and more confident, especially in controlling their life and claiming their rights.|例句：Education empowers people to make informed decisions.  教育使人们能够做出明智的决定。

例句：The movement empowered women to fight for their rights. 这场运动使妇女能够为自己的权利而斗争。

近义词：authorize, enable, strengthen, fortify|反义词：weaken, disable, disempower|词性:动词
