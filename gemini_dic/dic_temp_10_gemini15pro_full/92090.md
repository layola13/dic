hyperrealized:/ˌhaɪpərˈriːəlaɪzd/
adjective
超现实化的，极度逼真的
Characterized by an intense, exaggerated sense of reality, often exceeding the bounds of normal perception.  It can describe something that appears more real than real, or a heightened awareness of reality itself.
The hyperrealized graphics in the video game were breathtaking.  这款电子游戏里超逼真的图像令人叹为观止。
The artist's paintings capture a hyperrealized vision of the urban landscape.  这位艺术家的绘画捕捉到了城市景观的超现实景象。
Synonyms:  surreal, heightened, exaggerated, intensified, vivid, ultra-realistic
Antonyms:  unrealistic, dreamlike, fantastical, vague, blurred, muted
词性: 形容词
