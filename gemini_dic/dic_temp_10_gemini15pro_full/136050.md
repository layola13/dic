nonrebels:/ˌnɑːnˈrɛbəlz/
noun|非叛乱者，顺从者|People who do not participate in a rebellion or uprising; those who conform to established authority.|The government offered amnesty to the nonrebels.|近义词：conformists, loyalists, adherents|反义词:rebels, insurgents, revolutionaries|词性:n.


Etymology:
non- (prefix):  From Latin *non, "not".  This prefix negates the meaning of the word it attaches to.
rebel (noun): From Old French rebelle, from Latin rebellis "making war again," from re- "again" + bellum "war".
