curculio:/kɜːrˈkjuːlioʊ/ | n. | 象鼻虫 | A weevil; a beetle of the family Curculionidae, having a prominent snout. |  The curculio is a pest that damages crops. 象鼻虫是一种危害农作物的害虫。| synonym: weevil | antonym: N/A | noun
