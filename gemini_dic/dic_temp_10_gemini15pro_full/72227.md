华丽的；装饰华丽的；花状的
Decorated with floral designs or ornaments; resembling a flower in shape.

The architect designed a floriated archway. 建筑师设计了一个花状的拱门。
近义词: ornate, embellished, flowery
反义词: plain, simple, unadorned
词性: adjective
