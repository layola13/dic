candlemas: /ˈkændlməs/ | n. | 圣烛节 | A Christian holy day celebrated on February 2nd. It marks the presentation of Jesus at the Temple. |  Candlemas is celebrated forty days after Christmas. 圣烛节在圣诞节后四十天庆祝。|  The farmers hoped for good weather after Candlemas.  农民们希望圣烛节后天气好。 |近义词: Feast of the Presentation of Jesus Christ, Purification of the Virgin Mary | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Old English *Candelmæsse, from *candel (candle) + mæsse (mass).  The word reflects the blessing of candles on this day.
