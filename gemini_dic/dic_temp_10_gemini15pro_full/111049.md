The lissom gymnast performed a series of breathtaking backflips.  灵活的体操运动员表演了一系列惊险的后空翻。| 近义词：lithe, supple, agile, nimble, graceful | 反义词：stiff, rigid, clumsy, awkward | 词性: 形容词
