booby's:/ˈbuːbiz/| n.|呆子的（东西）;笨蛋的（东西）;傻瓜的（东西）|Booby's refers to something that belongs to a booby, a term for a foolish or naive person.  It's generally considered offensive.|That's a booby's move. 那是傻瓜才会做的举动。|近义词：fool's, idiot's, dunce's|反义词:genius's, sage's|词性:名词


词源:
From "booby," a term for a foolish person, plus the possessive suffix -'s. The word "booby" likely originates from Spanish "bobo," meaning "fool," "stupid," or "dunce."
