detractory:/dɪˈtræktəri/
adjective
贬低的，诽谤的
Tending to detract; lessening the value or reputation of something or someone.
Her detractory remarks about her colleagues damaged her reputation. 她对她同事的贬低性言论损害了她的名誉。
近义词: disparaging, belittling, denigrating, defamatory
反义词: complimentary, laudatory, praising, flattering
词性: adjective

detractory:/dɪˈtræktəri/
noun
诽谤者，贬低者
A person who detracts.
His detractors claimed he was only interested in personal gain. 他的批评者声称他只对个人利益感兴趣。
近义词: critic, detractor, slanderer
反义词: admirer, supporter, advocate
词性: noun
