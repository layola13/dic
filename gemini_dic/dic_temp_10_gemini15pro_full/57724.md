dreadingly:/ˈdrɛdɪŋli/
adverb|可怕地，恐惧地|in a way that fills you with dread; terribly|例句：She dreadingly awaited the results of the medical tests. 她恐惧地等待着医疗检查的结果。|近义词：fearfully, apprehensively, anxiously|反义词：calmly, confidently, serenely|词性: 副词
Etymology: From dread (verb) + -ing + -ly.  Dread comes from Old English *drǣdan, from Proto-Germanic *drōzjaną (“to be anxious, troubled”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰrēw- (“to trouble, disturb”).
