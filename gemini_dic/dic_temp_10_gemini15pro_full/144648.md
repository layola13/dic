outwrangle:/ˌaʊtˈræŋɡəl/
verb
胜过；以智取胜
To surpass or defeat someone in wrangling or arguing; to outmaneuver or outsmart someone through skillful debate or negotiation.
He outwrangled his opponents in the courtroom. 他在法庭上以智取胜了他的对手们。
outsmart, outmaneuver, defeat
- (no clear antonym)
