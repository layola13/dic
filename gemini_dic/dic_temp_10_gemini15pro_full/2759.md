ETYMOLOGY->
From adulthood + -s (plural marker). Adulthood is derived from adult + -hood. Adult comes from the Latin adultus (past participle of adolescere, "to grow up"), related to alescere ("to grow"). -hood is an Old English suffix denoting a state, condition, or period of being.
