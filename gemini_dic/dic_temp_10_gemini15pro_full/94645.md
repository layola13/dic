impressionableness:/ɪmˈprɛʃənəb(ə)lnəs/
n.
易受影响，可塑性
The quality of being easily influenced or impressed.
Her impressionableness made her vulnerable to peer pressure. 她容易受影响的性格使她很容易受到同伴的压力。
susceptibility, plasticity, malleability
resistance, imperviousness
noun
