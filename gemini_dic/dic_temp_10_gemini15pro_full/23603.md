brambly:/ˈbræmbli/| adj.|多刺的，荆棘丛生的|Overgrown with brambles; thorny.|The path was brambly and difficult to follow.  这条小路荆棘丛生，难以通行。|Synonyms: thorny, prickly, spiny, briery|Antonyms: smooth, clear, unobstructed|adjective

brambly:/ˈbræmbli/| adj.|棘手的，麻烦的|Figuratively, full of difficulties or complications.|The legal situation was brambly and required expert advice.  法律情况错综复杂，需要专家的建议。|Synonyms: difficult, complicated, thorny, tricky|Antonyms: simple, easy, straightforward|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *bræmelīg, from bræmel (bramble) + -īg (suffix forming adjectives). Cognate with Dutch braamlig, German brombeerig.
