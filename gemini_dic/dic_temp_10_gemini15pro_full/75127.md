frisket:/ˈfrɪskɪt/| n.|遮蔽膜，保护膜|A thin sheet of metal, plastic, or paper used to mask off areas of a drawing or artwork that should not be exposed to ink, paint, or other substances.|The artist used a frisket to protect the white areas of the paper while she sprayed the background.|近义词：mask, stencil, template|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'exposure')|Noun
