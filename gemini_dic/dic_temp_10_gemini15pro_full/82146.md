groomer:/ˈɡruːmər/ (US), /ˈɡruːmə/ (UK)| n.| 美容师；动物美容师；马夫；新郎的伴郎|A person who cleans and cares for animals, especially horses or dogs. Also, someone who prepares someone else for a special occasion, particularly a bride or bridegroom before their wedding.  In a negative context, it can refer to someone who builds a relationship with a child or young person with the intention of exploiting or abusing them. |例句：The dog groomer trimmed our poodle's fur.  狗美容师修剪了我们贵宾犬的毛。

例句：The bride's groomer helped her into her dress.  新娘的伴娘帮她穿上了婚纱。|近义词：stylist, handler, attendant|反义词: (No direct antonym, depends on the context)|词性:noun
