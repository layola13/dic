bloodstock:/ˈblʌdstɒk/ (US) /ˈblʌdˌstɒk/ (UK)| n. |纯种马；良种家畜；血统；种族|Thoroughbred horses, or other animals kept for breeding purposes, especially racehorses.  It can also refer to the pedigree or lineage of a particular animal or group of animals.|例句：The farm is renowned for its excellent bloodstock.  这个农场以其优良的纯种马而闻名。

例句：He invested heavily in bloodstock. 他在良种家畜上投入了巨资。|近义词：pedigree, lineage, ancestry, breeding stock|反义词: None (no direct opposite for a noun referring to breeding animals)|词性: Noun
