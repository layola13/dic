dictum:/ˈdɪktəm/| n.|格言；声明；断言|A formal pronouncement from an authoritative source; a short statement that expresses a general truth or principle.|The professor's dictum was that all good writing is rewriting.  教授的格言是，所有好的写作都是反复修改的。|synonym: pronouncement, maxim, saying, precept, adage|antonym: question, query|noun
