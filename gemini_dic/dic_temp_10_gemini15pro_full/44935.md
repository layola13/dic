cryptographer:/krɪpˈtɒɡrəfər/ /krɪpˈtɑːɡrəfər/ | n. |密码学家；密码破译者|A person who studies or practices cryptography, the art and science of making and breaking codes.|例句：The cryptographer worked tirelessly to decipher the enemy's message.  密码学家孜孜不倦地破译敌人的信息。

例句：She's a renowned cryptographer, specializing in post-quantum cryptography. 她是一位著名的密码学家，专攻后量子密码学。|近义词：codebreaker, cryptanalyst, cipher expert|反义词: None|词性: noun
