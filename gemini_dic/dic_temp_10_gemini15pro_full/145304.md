overbridge:/ˈəʊvəbrɪdʒ/ | n. | 天桥，陆桥 | A bridge that carries a road or railway over another road or railway. |  The train rumbled across the overbridge. 火车隆隆地驶过天桥。

| Example: We walked across the overbridge to avoid the busy traffic below. 我们走过天桥，以避开下面繁忙的交通。| Synonyms: flyover, bridge, viaduct | Antonyms: underpass, subway | Noun
