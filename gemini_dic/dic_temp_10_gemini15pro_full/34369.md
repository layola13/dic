classicistic: /ˌklæsɪˈsɪstɪk/ | adj. | 古典主义的，古典风格的 |  Relating to or in the style of classicism, particularly in art, literature, or architecture, emphasizing traditional forms, order, and restraint. |  The building's facade is strikingly classicistic, with its symmetrical columns and pediment.  这座建筑的正面是引人注目的古典主义风格，有着对称的柱子和山形墙。| The composer's classicistic style contrasted sharply with the romanticism of his contemporaries.  这位作曲家的古典主义风格与他同时代人的浪漫主义形成鲜明对比。| 近义词: classical, neoclassical | 反义词: romantic, modern, baroque | 词性: 形容词
