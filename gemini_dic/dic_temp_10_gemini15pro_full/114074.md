magnetomotive:/ˌmæɡˌniːtəʊˈməʊtɪv/
|adjective|
|磁动势的，产生磁通量的|
|Relating to or denoting magnetomotive force.|
|The magnetomotive force is the force that produces magnetic flux.|磁动势是产生磁通量的力。

|The magnetomotive force in a solenoid is proportional to the number of turns of wire and the current flowing through it.|螺线管中的磁动势与线圈匝数和流过它的电流成正比。

|近义词:magnetic|
|反义词:nonmagnetic|
|词性:adjective|
