chlorophane:/ˈklɔːrəfeɪn/| n. |绿萤石，萤石的一种|A variety of fluorspar which exhibits a green phosphorescent light when heated or struck.  It emits a green light when heated. |The chlorophane glowed eerily in the darkened cave. 绿萤石在黑暗的洞穴里发出诡异的光芒。

The miner discovered a vein of chlorophane. 矿工发现了一条绿萤石矿脉。|近义词：fluorspar, fluorite|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From chloro- (Greek χλωρός, khlōrós, "green") + -phane (Greek φαίνειν, phainein, "to show, to appear").  Essentially meaning "green appearing".
