cil:/sɪl/ | n. |纤毛，睫毛 |A short, hairlike vibrating structure, found in large numbers on the surface of certain cells, either causing currents in the surrounding fluid, or, in some protozoans and other small organisms, providing locomotion. |The paramecium uses its cilia for movement. 草履虫利用纤毛移动。|cilia, flagella | - | noun
