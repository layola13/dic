PHRASE->
A pat on the back:  鼓励或赞扬 (encouragement or praise)
Pat answer:  预先准备好的、敷衍的回答 (a glib, pre-prepared answer)
Pat oneself on the back:  自吹自擂 (to congratulate oneself)
Have something down pat:  精通某事 (to have something mastered)
Stand pat:  坚持立场 (to hold firm to one's position)


词源：
From Old French *pater, frequentative of *patar "to paw," from patte "paw."  Ultimately of imitative origin.
