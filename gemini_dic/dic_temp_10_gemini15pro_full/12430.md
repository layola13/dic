asker's:/ˈæskərz/| adj. pron.|提问者的，提问人的|Belonging to or associated with the person who asked a question. |This is the asker's first question on the platform. 这是提问者在这个平台上的第一个问题。|近义词：inquirer's, questioner's|反义词：answerer's, respondent's|词性:所有格代词/所有格形容词

词源: From "asker" + "'s" (possessive suffix). "Asker" comes from the verb "ask," which has Old English roots.
