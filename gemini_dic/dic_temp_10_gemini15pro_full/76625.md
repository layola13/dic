gallopingly:/ˈɡæləpɪŋli/
adverb
飞快地，急速地
At a galloping pace; very rapidly.
The disease spread gallopingly through the population.  这种疾病在人群中迅速蔓延。
近义词: rapidly, swiftly, quickly, speedily, hastily, at breakneck speed
反义词: slowly, gradually
词性: adverb
词源: From gallop + -ing + -ly.  来自 gallop（飞奔） + -ing（现在分词后缀） + -ly（副词后缀）。
