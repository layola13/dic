necropsy: /ˈnɛkrɒpsi/ (US) /ˈniːkrɒpsi/ (UK) | n. | 尸检，尸体解剖 | An examination of a dead body to determine the cause of death.  | The necropsy revealed the cause of death to be a heart attack. 尸检显示死因是心脏病发作。

| The necropsy on the dolphin showed it had ingested plastic. 对海豚的尸检表明它吞食了塑料。| synonym: autopsy, postmortem examination | antonym:  N/A | noun


necropsies: /ˈnɛkrɒpsiz/ (US) /ˈniːkrɒpsiz/ (UK) | n. | 尸检（复数）| Plural form of necropsy. |  Multiple necropsies were performed to understand the spread of the disease.  进行了多次尸检以了解疾病的传播方式。

| The necropsies of the birds showed signs of avian flu.  这些鸟类的尸检显示出禽流感的迹象。 | synonym: autopsies, postmortem examinations | antonym: N/A | noun
