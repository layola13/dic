grate:/ɡreɪt/| v. n.|磨碎，擦碎；使人烦躁；装格栅| to rub food against a grater in order to cut it into small pieces; to make a harsh, rasping sound; to annoy or irritate someone; a metal frame with bars across it|例句：She grated the cheese onto the pasta. 她把奶酪磨碎撒在意大利面上。

|近义词：shred, grind, rasp, irritate, annoy|反义词：soothe, please|词性：动词，名词

grate:/ɡreɪt/| n.|格栅，炉栅|a frame of metal bars for holding fuel in a fireplace, etc.|例句：The fire glowed brightly in the grate. 火在炉栅里熊熊燃烧。

|近义词：grill, grid|反义词：N/A|词性：名词
