overplausible: /ˌoʊvərˈplɔːzəbəl/
adjective
过于貌似有理的，表面上可信的
appearing reasonable or probable but open to doubt or suspicion.
His explanation was overplausible, making me suspect he was hiding something. 他的解释过于貌似有理，让我怀疑他隐瞒了什么。
The salesman's overplausible pitch made me wary of the product.  销售员过于貌似可信的推销让我对产品产生了警惕。
synonyms: specious, too good to be true, unconvincing, suspicious
antonyms: genuine, authentic, credible, believable
