arachnophobes:/əˈræknəˌfoʊbz/
|n.|蜘蛛恐惧症患者|People who have an extreme or irrational fear of spiders are called arachnophobes.|Many arachnophobes avoid going into basements or attics for fear of encountering spiders.|近义词:spider-phobics|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps someone who loves spiders)|词性:名词
