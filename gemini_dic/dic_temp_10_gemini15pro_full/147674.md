overpotential:/ˌoʊvərpoʊˈtɛnʃəl/ | n. |过电位，超电势 |The extra voltage required beyond the thermodynamically determined value to cause a reaction to occur at a desired rate.  It represents the additional driving force needed to overcome kinetic barriers in an electrochemical process.| The overpotential for hydrogen evolution on platinum is low. 铂上析氢的过电位很低。| synonyms: overvoltage | antonyms: N/A | noun

overpotential:/ˌoʊvərpoʊˈtɛnʃəl/ | n. |过电位，超电势 | The difference between the actual electrode potential and the thermodynamically determined equilibrium potential for a given electrochemical reaction. It signifies the extra energy required to drive the reaction at a specific rate.  | High overpotentials can lead to energy losses in electrochemical systems.  高过电位会导致电化学系统中的能量损失。 | synonyms: overvoltage | antonyms: N/A | noun
