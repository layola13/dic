mercerize: /ˈmɜːrsəraɪz/ | v. | 丝光处理，使丝光泽 | To treat (cotton thread or fabric) with caustic soda under tension, so as to increase its strength and give it a silky luster. | The company mercerizes cotton fabrics to give them a luxurious sheen.  这家公司对棉织物进行丝光处理，使其具有奢华的光泽。| synonyms:  gloss, polish, sheen | antonyms: dull, roughen | verb

mercerized: /ˈmɜːrsəraɪzd/ | adj. | 丝光化的，丝光的 | Treated by mercerization. |  She wore a mercerized cotton dress. 她穿着一件丝光棉布连衣裙。| synonyms:  glossy, polished, sheeny | antonyms: dull, roughened | adjective
