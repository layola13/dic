floyd:/flɔɪd/| n.|弗洛伊德 (人名；也可指弗洛伊德算法)|A surname, also referring to the Floyd-Warshall algorithm, a shortest path algorithm in graph theory.|例句：Floyd Mayweather is a famous boxer. 弗洛伊德·梅威瑟是一位著名的拳击手。

例句：The Floyd-Warshall algorithm is used to find the shortest paths in a weighted graph. 弗洛伊德算法用于在加权图中查找最短路径。|近义词： (For the algorithm) Dijkstra, Bellman-Ford|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
