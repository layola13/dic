falanaka: /fæləˈnækə/ | n. | 法拉纳卡（马达加斯加语，一种木质的棋盘游戏）| Falanaka is a traditional Malagasy board game played on a wooden board with seeds or stones as pieces.  It is similar to Mancala games. |  Example: We played falanaka at the community center yesterday.  我们昨天在社区中心玩了法拉纳卡。 | Synonym:  Mancala (general category), Fanorona (another Malagasy game) | Antonym: N/A | Noun
