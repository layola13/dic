motionless:/ˈmoʊʃənləs/| adj.|静止的，不动的|Without any movement; still.|The cat sat motionless, staring at the bird. 猫一动不动地坐着，盯着鸟。|The motionless figure stood in the shadows.  一动不动的身影站在阴影里。|近义词：still, stationary, immobile, static|反义词：moving, active, dynamic|词性:形容词
Etymology: From motion + -less. Motion comes from Old French "mocien" from Latin "motio," meaning "a moving."  The suffix "-less" indicates absence, derived from Old English "-lēas."
