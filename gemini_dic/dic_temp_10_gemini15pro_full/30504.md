centuriate:/sɛnˈtʊəriət/ /sɛnˈtjʊəriət/| n. |百人队队员，百夫长|A member of a centuria, a military unit of the Roman army originally comprising one hundred men.  A centurion commanded a centuria.|The centuriate assembly played a key role in Roman politics. 百人队会议在罗马政治中扮演着关键角色。|近义词：soldier, legionary|反义词:civilian|noun


centuriate:/sɛnˈtʊəriət/ /sɛnˈtjʊəriət/| adj. |百人队的，按百人队组织的|Divided into or organized by centuries or hundreds.|The centuriate organization of the Roman army was a key to its success. 罗马军队的百人队组织是其成功的关键。|近义词：organized, structured|反义词:disorganized|adjective
