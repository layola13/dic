hectorism:/ˈhɛktərɪzəm/
n.
恃强凌弱；威吓；恐吓
bullying or intimidation; the act of hectoring.
He displayed pure hectorism, shouting down anyone who disagreed with him. 他表现出十足的恃强凌弱，大声呵斥任何不同意他的人。
bullying, intimidation, browbeating, domineering, blustering
conciliation, appeasement, diplomacy
noun
