carpintero:/kɑːrpinˈtɛroʊ/ (US), /ˌkɑːpɪnˈtɛəroʊ/ (UK)| n.|木匠，木工|A person who makes and repairs wooden objects and structures.|例句：The carpintero built a beautiful wooden table. 木匠做了一张漂亮的木桌。

例句：My grandfather was a skilled carpintero. 我的祖父是一位技艺精湛的木匠。

近义词：woodworker, joiner, carpenter|反义词:|词性:noun

carpintero:/kɑːrpinˈtɛroʊ/ (US), /ˌkɑːpɪnˈtɛəroʊ/ (UK)| adj.|木匠的|Relating to carpentry or a carpenter.  (Less common usage)|例句：He used carpintero tools to fix the chair. 他用木工工具修理椅子。

例句：She admired the carpintero skills of her neighbor. 她钦佩邻居的木工技能。

近义词：woodworking|反义词:|词性:adjective


词源: From Spanish *carpintero*, ultimately from Late Latin *carpentārius* (“cartwright, wagon-maker”), from Latin *carpentum* (“wagon, cart”).
