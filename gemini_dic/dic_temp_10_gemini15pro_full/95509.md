incliners:/ɪnˈklaɪnərz/ | n. | 倾斜装置；斜坡输送机；倾斜器 |  A mechanical device or sloping surface for raising or lowering people or objects. | The incliners transported skiers up the mountain.  倾斜装置将滑雪者运送到山上。| synonyms: ramps, lifts, elevators, escalators | antonyms: declines, descents | noun

incliners:/ɪnˈklaɪnərz/ | n. | 倾斜的人或物 | People or things that incline or lean. | The incliners towards a more liberal view were vocal. 倾向于更自由观点的人表达得很直白。 | synonyms: leaners, tilters | antonyms: - | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "incline" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Incline" comes from Old French "encliner" and ultimately from Latin "inclinare," meaning "to bend or lean toward."
