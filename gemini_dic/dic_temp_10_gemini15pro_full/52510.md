differential:/ˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃəl/| n. adj.|差速器；微分；差额；差别|A small difference or distinction between similar things or a quality that differentiates between similar things.  In mathematics, it refers to an infinitely small change in a variable.|例句：The differential between the two offers was negligible.  两个报价之间的差额微不足道。|近义词：distinction, difference, variance, disparity, deviation|反义词：similarity, resemblance|noun
differential:/ˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃəl/| adj.|差别的；特异的；微分的|Relating to or showing a difference; constituting a difference.|例句：Differential treatment of employees based on their performance is acceptable. 基于员工绩效的差别待遇是可以接受的。|近义词：distinctive, distinguishing, varied, diverse|反义词：uniform, similar, identical|adjective
