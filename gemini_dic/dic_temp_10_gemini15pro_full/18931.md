bespeckle:/bɪˈspɛkəl/| vt.|点缀；散布；弄上斑点|To cover or mark with speckles; to dot.|例句：The artist bespeckled the canvas with paint. 艺术家在画布上点缀着颜料。|例句：The sky was bespeckled with stars. 天空点缀着星星。|近义词：dot, fleck, spot, stipple|反义词：clear, plain|词性：动词
