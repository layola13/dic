liberty's:/ˈlɪbərtiz/| n.|自由;自由权（liberty的名词所有格）|It represents liberty's triumph over tyranny. 它代表着自由战胜了暴政。|近义词:freedom's, independence's|反义词:captivity's, slavery's|词性:名词所有格

词源:
From Old French "liberte" and directly from Latin "libertas," meaning "freedom, condition of a free man."  The "-y" suffix transforms the adjective "liber" (free) into a noun, and the apostrophe-s indicates possession.
