diethylamine:/ˌdaɪˈɛθəlˌæmiːn/| n.|二乙胺|A secondary amine with the chemical formula (C2H5)2NH. It is a colorless, volatile liquid with a strong, fishy odor.  It is miscible with water and most organic solvents.|Diethylamine is commonly used as a corrosion inhibitor and in the production of certain pesticides and pharmaceuticals.|Diethylamine is a versatile chemical used in various industrial processes.|近义词: N,N-Diethylamine|反义词: None|noun
