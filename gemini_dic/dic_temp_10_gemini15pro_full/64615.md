erupts:/ɪˈrʌpts/| v.|爆发，喷发|suddenly becomes active and releases something, often violently.  The volcano erupted with tremendous force. |例句：The volcano erupted with a deafening roar. 火山爆发出一阵震耳欲聋的咆哮声。

例句：Violence erupted in the city after the controversial decision.  有争议的决定宣布后，城市里爆发了暴力事件。|近义词：explode, burst, blow up|反义词:subside, die down|词性:动词

erupt:/ɪˈrʌpt/| v.|（指感情）爆发，迸发|to suddenly express a strong emotion. He erupted in anger when he heard the news. |例句：She erupted in laughter at his silly joke. 她被他愚蠢的笑话逗得大笑起来。

例句：The crowd erupted in cheers as the team scored the winning goal. 球队攻入制胜一球时，人群爆发出欢呼声。|近义词：explode, burst out, flare up|反义词:calm down, restrain|词性:动词
