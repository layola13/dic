magnolia: /mæɡˈnoʊliə/ (US) /mæɡˈnəʊliə/ (UK)| n.|木兰，玉兰花|A tree or shrub with large, fragrant flowers, typically white, pink, or purple.  The flowers often bloom before the leaves appear.|例句：The magnolia tree in my garden is in full bloom.  我家花园里的玉兰树盛开了。

例句：She wore a dress with a magnolia print. 她穿着一件玉兰花印花的连衣裙。|近义词：blossom, flower|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a type of flower)|Noun
