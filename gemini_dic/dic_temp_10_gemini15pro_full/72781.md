foilable:/ˈfɔɪləbl/
adjective
可挫败的，可阻挠的
Capable of being foiled or thwarted.
His plans were foilable, but only with great effort. 他的计划是可以被挫败的，但需要付出巨大的努力。
The thief's attempt was foilable thanks to the quick-thinking security guard. 多亏了机智的保安，小偷的企图被挫败了。
synonyms:thwartable, frustratable, defeatable
antonyms:unstoppable, irresistible, invincible
