establishable:/ɪˈstæblɪʃəbl/
adjective
可建立的，可确立的
Capable of being established; possible to establish.
It is establishable that the suspect was at the scene of the crime.  可以确定嫌疑犯在犯罪现场。
The link between smoking and lung cancer is firmly establishable.  吸烟和肺癌之间的联系是可以确定的。
近义词: demonstrable, provable, verifiable
反义词: unprovable,  undemonstrable
