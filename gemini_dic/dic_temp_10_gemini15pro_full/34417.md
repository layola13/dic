claude:/klɔːd/| n. |克劳德（男子名）|A masculine given name, derived from the Roman family name Claudius.  It's ultimately of Latin origin and means "lame" or "crippled," though the negative connotation has long since faded.  It is associated with several saints.|例句：Claude Monet was a famous French painter. 克劳德·莫奈是一位著名的法国画家。

例句：My grandfather's name is Claude. 我祖父的名字是克劳德。|近义词: Claudius (more formal, less common now)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
