opaqued:/oʊˈpeɪkt/ | adj. |不透明的，不透光的 | Made opaque or less transparent, especially by adding a color or other substance. | The windows were opaqued with a frosted film. 窗户上贴了一层磨砂膜，变得不透明了。 | The artist opaqued the background of the painting with a layer of white. 艺术家在画的背景上涂了一层白色，使其不透明。 | 近义词: nontransparent, obscure, cloudy | 反义词: transparent, clear, translucent | 词性: 形容词
词源: From opaque + -ed, forming the past participle of the verb *opaque* "to make opaque," from French *opaquer,* from Latin *opacus* "shaded, dark, obscure."
