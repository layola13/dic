demarcates:/ˈdɪːmɑːrkeɪts/
verb
划定界限; 区分; 划分
set the boundaries or limits of; separate or distinguish from
例句：A fence demarcates the boundary between the two properties.  一个栅栏划定了这两处房产之间的界限。

例句：The river demarcates the two countries. 这条河是两国的分界线。
synonym: separates, distinguishes, delimits, defines, outlines
antonym: unites, merges, combines, blends
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *démarquer*, from *dé-* (expressing removal) + *marquer* "to mark," from Latin *marcare* "to mark."
