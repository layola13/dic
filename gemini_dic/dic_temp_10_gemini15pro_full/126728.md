namoto:/ˈnɑːmɔːtoʊ/ |n.| 祈禱詞，佛號 | A Buddhist chant, specifically "Nam-myoho-renge-kyo."  It is a central practice of Nichiren Buddhism. | Example:  The members chanted "Nam-myoho-renge-kyo" rhythmically.  成员们有节奏地唱诵“南無妙法蓮華經”。 | Synonym: chant, invocation | Antonym: N/A | noun

namoto:/ˈnɑːmɔːtoʊ/ |interj.|  (用于问候或告别) | Used as a greeting or farewell, particularly within the Soka Gakkai International (SGI) Buddhist community.  | Example: "Namoto!" he called out as he entered the meeting. 他走进会议室时喊道：“南無妙法蓮華經！” | Synonym: greetings, hello, goodbye | Antonym: N/A | interjection
