announceable:/əˈnaʊnsəbl/
adjective
可公布的；可宣布的
Suitable or fit to be announced.
The engagement is now announceable.  订婚现在可以宣布了。
近义词:declarable, publishable
反义词:unannounceable, secret
词性:adjective
