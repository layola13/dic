nonmalleable:/ˌnɒnˈmæliəbl/ /ˌnɑːnˈmæliəbl/ | adj. | 不可延展的；固执的；难塑造的 | Not capable of being hammered or rolled into a thin sheet; inflexible; unyielding. | The sculptor found the material nonmalleable and difficult to work with.  雕塑家发现这种材料不可延展，难以加工。 | Synonyms: inflexible, rigid, unyielding, intractable | Antonyms: malleable, pliable, flexible, ductile | Adjective
