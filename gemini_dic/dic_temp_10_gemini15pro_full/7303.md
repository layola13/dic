anguineal:/æŋˈɡwɪniəl/ | adj. |血红色的，血染的；乐观的，充满希望的| Of or relating to blood; having the color of blood; optimistic, cheerful. | The setting sun cast an anguineal glow across the sky. 落日给天空染上了一层血红色。| 近义词: sanguine, blood-red, crimson, optimistic, hopeful | 反义词: pale, pessimistic, gloomy | 词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *anguinealis, from Latin anguineus (“of a snake”), from anguis (“snake”). The connection is likely due to the perceived color of snake blood.  The "optimistic" meaning is derived from the ancient belief that a sanguine (blood-red) complexion indicated a cheerful disposition.
