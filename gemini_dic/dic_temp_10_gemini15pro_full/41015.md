copartner:/ˈkoʊˌpɑːrtnər/ | n. | 合伙人，共同出资人 | A person who shares in a business undertaking with another or others, especially as a joint owner. |  Example: She and her copartner started a bakery together. 她和她的合伙人一起开了一家面包店。

Example: The copartners shared the profits and losses equally.  合伙人平分利润和损失。 | Synonym: partner, associate, collaborator | Antonym: competitor, rival | noun
