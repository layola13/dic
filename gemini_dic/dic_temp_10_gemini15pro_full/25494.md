bullfrog:/ˈbʊlˌfrɔːɡ/ (US), /ˈbʊlfrɒɡ/ (UK)| n.|牛蛙|A large North American frog with a deep resonant croak.|We heard the bullfrogs croaking in the pond. 我们听到池塘里牛蛙的叫声。|The bullfrog leaped from the bank into the water. 牛蛙从岸边跳入水中。|近义词：frog, toad|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From bull + frog, so called from the loud, bellowing croak of the male.
