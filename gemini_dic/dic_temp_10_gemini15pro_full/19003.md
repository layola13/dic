bestride:/bɪˈstraɪd/| vt.|跨越，横跨；支配，控制|To sit or stand with one leg on each side of something; to dominate or control.|例句：He bestrode his horse. 他骑着马。|近义词：straddle, span, dominate, control|反义词：submit to|词性：动词
