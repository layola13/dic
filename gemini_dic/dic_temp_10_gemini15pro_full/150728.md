parachronism:/pærˈækrənɪzəm/
n.
时代错误；年代误植；时间误置
An error in chronology, especially in placing an event later than it actually happened.
The novel is full of parachronisms, placing 21st-century technology in the Victorian era. 这部小说充满了时代错误，把21世纪的技术放在了维多利亚时代。
近义词：anachronism
反义词：synchronism
