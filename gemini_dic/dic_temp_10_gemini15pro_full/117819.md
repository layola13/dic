词源: From Middle English *mellen, from Old French meller (“to mix, stir, meddle”), from Late Latin misculāre, from Latin miscēre (“to mix”). More at mix.  Related to medley. Originally in the sense of "meddle" (as in mix). The sense of “smell" might be influenced by Latin olfacere.
