leeway:/ˈliːweɪ/
n.
自由活动空间; 余地; 允许偏差的范围;
Extra space or freedom to act or move in a particular way; margin of safety.
The ship had 500 feet of leeway in which to maneuver.  这艘船有500英尺的活动空间来操纵。
latitude, tolerance, flexibility
restriction, limitation
noun


leeway:/ˈliːweɪ/
n.
(比喻) 自由; 灵活性;
(Figurative) freedom to act or think in the way you want to.
The government has very little leeway in foreign policy. 政府在外交政策上几乎没有回旋余地。
latitude, room, scope
restriction, limitation
noun
