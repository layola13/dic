decarbonate:/diːˈkɑːrbəneɪt/
verb
脱碳
To remove carbon from something, especially from the economy or a process.
The government has pledged to decarbonize the economy by 2050. 政府已承诺到 2050 年实现经济脱碳。
decarbonise, reduce emissions
increase emissions
v.
