homeys:/ˈhoʊmiz/ | n. | 哥们儿，老铁，密友（复数） | Close friends, pals, buddies. Often used informally and with affection.  Derived from "homeboy" which originated in African American Vernacular English (AAVE). Refers to someone from one's same neighborhood or social group.  The plural form "homeys" extends this sense of close friendship to a group of people. | Example: I'm going to the game with my homeys tonight. 今晚我要和我的哥们儿一起去看比赛。| Synonym: friends, buddies, pals, mates, crew | Antonym: enemies, foes, rivals | Noun
