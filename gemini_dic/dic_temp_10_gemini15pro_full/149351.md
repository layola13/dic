pachydermata:/ˌpækɪˈdɜːrmətə/ | n. | 厚皮动物（旧称）|  A former classification of non-ruminant hoofed mammals, including elephants, rhinoceroses, and hippopotamuses, characterized by their thick skin. This term is now considered outdated and taxonomically inaccurate. | Elephants, rhinoceroses, and hippopotamuses were once classified as pachydermata. 大象、犀牛和河马曾经被归类为厚皮动物。 |  proboscideans, perissodactyls |  (No direct antonym as it's an outdated classification) | noun
