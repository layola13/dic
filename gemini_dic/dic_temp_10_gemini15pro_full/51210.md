destitute: /ˈdɛstɪtjuːt/ /ˈdɛstɪtuːt/
adjective
赤贫的，一无所有的；缺乏的
lacking the necessities of life; lacking something needed or desirable
The earthquake left many people destitute. 地震使许多人一无所有。
synonyms: impoverished, penniless, indigent, needy, poverty-stricken
antonyms: wealthy, affluent, rich, prosperous
词性: 形容词
