balti:/ˈbɔːlti/| n.|巴尔蒂咖喱，巴尔蒂餐馆|A type of curry served in a thin, pressed-steel wok-like bowl called a balti.  It is of disputed origin, claimed by both Birmingham, England and the Baltistan region in Pakistan. |We went for a balti last night. 我们昨晚去吃了巴尔蒂咖喱。|The balti was delicious. 这巴尔蒂咖喱真好吃。|近义词:curry|反义词:|名词
