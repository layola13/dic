heterogamous:/ˌhetərˈɒɡəməs/ | adj. |异配的，异型婚配的 | Relating to heterogamy; in which reproduction involves the union of dissimilar gametes.  | Example:  Many species of algae exhibit heterogamous reproduction. 许多藻类物种表现出异配生殖。

Example: Heterogamous marriage, where partners are significantly different in age or social background, is becoming more common.  异型婚，即伴侣在年龄或社会背景方面存在显著差异的婚姻，正变得越来越普遍。 | 近义词:anisogamous | 反义词:isogamous, homogamous | 词性: adjective

词源:
From hetero- (different) + -gamous (relating to marriage or reproduction).  Ultimately from Greek heteros (other) + gamos (marriage).
