disagreeablenesses: /dɪsəˈɡriːəbəlnəsɪz/
noun
不愉快，令人不快的事情；分歧
The state or quality of being disagreeable; unpleasantness.  A disagreeable quality, habit, or aspect.
Her constant disagreeablenesses made her unpopular. 她不断的惹人不快让她不受欢迎。
近义词: unpleasantries, disputes, quarrels, conflicts, differences
反义词: pleasantries, agreements, harmonies, accords, similarities
词性: noun (plural)

disagreeableness: /dɪsəˈɡriːəbəlnəs/
noun
不愉快，令人不快的事情；乖戾的性情
The state or quality of being disagreeable; unpleasantness. An instance of disagreeable behavior.
He exhibited a surprising disagreeableness. 他表现出惊人的乖戾。
近义词: unpleasantness, hostility, rudeness, bad temper, ill nature
反义词: pleasantness, agreeableness, friendliness, good temper, good nature
词性: noun

词源: From disagreeable + -ness.  Disagreeable is from dis- + agreeable. Agreeable is from Old French agreable, from agreer "to please," from a- "to" + gre "pleasure, liking, gratitude," from Latin gratus "pleasing, thankful."
