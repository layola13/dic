heteromorphy:/ˌhɛtərəˈmɔːrfi/ | n. |异形性，异态性| The state or condition of being heteromorphic; difference in form or structure.  |  The heteromorphy of the larval and adult stages of this insect is striking.  这种昆虫幼虫期和成虫期的异形性非常显著。 | synonyms: heteromorphism, polymorphism, allomorphy | antonyms: isomorphism, homomorphy | noun

heteromorphy:/ˌhɛtərəˈmɔːrfi/ | n. |异质同晶|  The crystallization of a substance in two or more different crystal systems. |  Diamond and graphite are an example of heteromorphy. 钻石和石墨是异质同晶的例子。 | synonyms: polymorphism, allotropy | antonyms: isomorphism, monomorphism | noun
