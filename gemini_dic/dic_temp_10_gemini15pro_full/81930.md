词源：From Middle English *grim, *grimme, from Old English grim, grimm (“fierce, cruel, savage, dire, painful, evil, fierce in disposition, raging, furious, grim, sinister”), from Proto-Germanic *grimmaz (“fierce, furious, grim”), from Proto-Indo-European *ghrem- (“to be angry, snarl”). Cognate with Dutch grim (“fierce, cruel”), German grimm (“grim, fierce, cruel”), Icelandic grimmr (“fierce, cruel”), Swedish grymm (“cruel, fierce”). Related also to Old English grama (“anger, fury”), gram (“angry, fierce”).
