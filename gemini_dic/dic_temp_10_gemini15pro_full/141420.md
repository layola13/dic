ophiuroid:/ˌɒfiˈjʊərɔɪd/| n.|蛇尾纲动物，蛇尾|A marine invertebrate of the class Ophiuroidea, having a flattened, star-shaped body and five slender, flexible arms.| Brittle stars and basket stars are ophiuroids.|近义词:brittle star, serpent star|反义词: N/A|词性: 名词
Etymology: From Ancient Greek ὀφίουρος (ophíouros, “snake-tailed”) + -oid.  ὀφίουρος is a compound of ὄφις (óphis, “snake”) + οὐρά (ourá, “tail”).
