dietarian:/ˌdaɪəˈtɛəriən/ | n. |营养学家；饮食学家|A person who is an expert on diet and nutrition.|Example: The dietarian recommended a balanced diet with plenty of fruits and vegetables. 营养学家建议均衡饮食，多吃水果和蔬菜。
Example:  She consulted a dietarian to help her lose weight. 她咨询了一位营养学家来帮助她减肥。|近义词: nutritionist, dietitian |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps someone promoting unhealthy eating habits) glutton |词性: noun
