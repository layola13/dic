indolent: /ˈɪndələnt/ | adj. | 懒惰的；好逸恶劳的 | Wanting to avoid activity or exertion; lazy. |  He was an indolent young man, who had no interest in work.  他是一个懒惰的年轻人，对工作毫无兴趣。 |  The hot weather made everyone feel indolent. 炎热的天气让每个人都感到懒洋洋的。 |  lazy, slothful, idle, lethargic |  energetic, industrious, diligent, active | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *indolent*, from Latin *indolent* (“insensitive to pain”), from *in-* (“not”) + *dolent-* (“suffering, grieving”), equivalent to *in-* + *dolent*.  More at dole.
