antireformer:/ˈæntiˌrɪˈfɔːrmər/ | n. | 反改革者，反对改革的人 | Someone who opposes reform or change, especially in social or political matters. |  The antireformers blocked the passage of the new law. 反改革者阻止了新法律的通过。

The conference was disrupted by a group of vocal antireformers. 会议被一群大声疾呼的反改革者扰乱了。 | 近义词：conservative, reactionary, diehard | 反义词: reformer, progressive, liberal | 词性: 名词
