ETYMOLOGY->
From "golden" + "-ness". "Golden" comes from Old English "gylden," derived from Proto-Germanic "gulþinaz," ultimately from Proto-Indo-European "ghel-," meaning "yellow, green, bright."  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, denoting a state or quality.
