actuates:/ˈæktʃueɪts/| v. |驱动，促使，激励|To cause (a machine or device) to operate.|例句：The button actuates the elevator.  这个按钮启动电梯。

例句：Fear actuated her to run.  恐惧驱使她逃跑。|近义词：activate, trigger, motivate, prompt, impel, drive|反义词：deactivate, stop, hinder|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin actuatus, past participle of actuare "to put into action," from Latin actus "a doing, a driving, impulse," from agere "to do, drive, act."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The thermostat actuates the heating system. = 恒温器启动了供暖系统。
His desire for power actuates his every move. = 他对权力的渴望驱动着他的一举一动。

一般过去时:
A lever actuated the mechanism. = 一个杠杆启动了这个装置。
Curiosity actuated him to open the box.  好奇心驱使他打开了盒子。


现在完成时:
The sensor has actuated the alarm. = 传感器已经触发了警报。
His ambition has actuated him to work tirelessly. = 他的雄心壮志促使他不知疲倦地工作。


过去完成时:
The timer had actuated the sprinkler system before we arrived. = 在我们到达之前，计时器已经启动了洒水系统。
Her conscience had actuated her to confess. = 她的良心促使她坦白。


将来时:
The switch will actuate the motor. = 开关将启动电机。
A small change in pressure will actuate the valve. =  压力的微小变化将启动阀门。


虚拟语气:
If the button were to be pressed, it would actuate the machine. = 如果按下按钮，它将启动机器。
I wish something would actuate him to take action. = 我希望有什么东西能促使他采取行动。


被动语态:
The machine is actuated by a lever. = 这台机器由一个杠杆驱动。
The alarm was actuated by the smoke detector. = 警报是由烟雾探测器触发的。


疑问句:
What actuates the opening of the valve? = 什么驱动阀门打开？
Does the button actuate the machine directly? =  这个按钮是直接启动机器吗？


否定句:
The switch doesn't actuate the light. = 开关不能启动灯。
The lever didn't actuate the mechanism properly. = 杠杆没有正确地启动机构。


PHRASE->
actuate by = 由...驱动
actuate a machine = 启动机器
actuate a device = 启动设备
actuate a system = 启动系统
actuate a mechanism = 启动机构
