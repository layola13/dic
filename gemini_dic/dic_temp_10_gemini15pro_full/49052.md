dehort:/dɪˈhɔːrt/
verb
劝阻，阻止， dissuade
To strongly discourage someone from doing something; to advise against.
The doctor dehorted his patient from smoking. 医生劝告病人不要吸烟。
He was dehorted from joining the army by his family. 他的家人劝他不要参军。

synonyms: dissuade, discourage, advise against, warn against, caution against
antonyms: encourage, urge, persuade, exhort, advocate
