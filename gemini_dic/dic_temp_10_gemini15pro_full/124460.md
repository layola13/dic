mottled:/ˈmɑːtld/ (US) /ˈmɒtld/ (UK)| adj.|斑驳的，杂色的|Marked with spots or patches of different colors or shades.|The old wall was mottled with green and brown patches. 老墙上布满了绿色和棕色的斑块。|例句：The sky was mottled with grey clouds. 天空布满了灰色的云朵。|近义词：spotted, speckled, blotched, dappled, variegated|反义词：uniform, solid, consistent|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *motled, *moteled, from Old French *mastelé, *mastelé, past participle of *masteler "to spot, stain," of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to Old French mascle "spot, mesh of a net."
