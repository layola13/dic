linden: /ˈlɪndən/ (US), /ˈlaɪndən/ (UK)| n. |椴树；菩提树|A deciduous tree with heart-shaped leaves and fragrant yellowish flowers.  The wood is soft and easily worked, used for carving and making boxes.|The linden tree in the park provides welcome shade in the summer. 公园里的菩提树在夏天提供了受欢迎的阴凉。|Synonym: lime tree, basswood|Antonym: N/A|noun


linden: /ˈlɪndən/ (US), /ˈlaɪndən/ (UK)| adj. |椴树的；菩提树的|Made of or relating to linden wood.|The linden wood carving was intricately detailed.  椴木雕刻非常精致。|Synonym: lime, basswood|Antonym: N/A|adjective
