nonliterarinesses:/ˌnɒnlɪtəˈrɛərɪnɪsɪz/
noun
非文学性；非文学状态
The state or quality of not being literary; instances of this.
Examples of nonliterariness in popular culture are abundant.  流行文化中非文学性的例子比比皆是。
The critic pointed to several nonliterarinesses in the author's supposedly serious work.  评论家指出了这位作家所谓严肃作品中的几处非文学性。
Synonyms: unliterary qualities, non-literary characteristics
Antonyms: literary qualities, literariness
词性: 名词
