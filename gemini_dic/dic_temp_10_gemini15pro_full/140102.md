off-limits:/ˌɒf ˈlɪmɪts/ /ˌɔːf ˈlɪmɪts/ | adj. |禁止进入的，禁止使用的| Not allowed to be entered or used; forbidden. | Example: The construction site is off-limits to the public.  建筑工地禁止公众进入。

Example:  His private study was off-limits to everyone but his wife. 除了他妻子，任何人都不得进入他的私人书房。


近义词:forbidden, prohibited, restricted, out of bounds
反义词:permitted, allowed, accessible
词性:adjective
