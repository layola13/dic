desynonymize:/diːsɪˈnɒnɪmaɪz/ | v. | 去同义化，消除同义词 | To replace synonyms with a single preferred term, often for clarity or consistency. | The editor desynonymized the document to ensure consistent terminology.  编辑去同义化了文档以确保术语一致。 | Example: The company's style guide requires writers to desynonymize their work. 公司的风格指南要求作者对他们的作品进行去同义词化。| Synonyms: standardize, unify, consolidate | Antonyms: diversify, vary | verb
