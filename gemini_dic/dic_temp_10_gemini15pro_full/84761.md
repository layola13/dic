handoff:/ˈhændˌɔːf/ (US), /ˈhændɒf/ (UK)| n. |交接，移交，切换|The act of passing something, such as responsibility or control, from one person or entity to another.|The quarterback made a smooth handoff to the running back. 四分卫顺利地把球交给了跑锋。|synonyms: transfer, handover, exchange|antonyms: retention, takeover, keeping|noun


handoff:/ˈhændˌɔːf/ (US), /ˈhændɒf/ (UK)| v. |交接，移交，传递|To pass something, such as responsibility or control, from one person or entity to another.|The project manager will handoff the project to the new team leader next week. 项目经理下周将把项目移交给新的团队负责人。|synonyms: transfer, handover, pass on|antonyms: retain, keep, hold onto|verb
