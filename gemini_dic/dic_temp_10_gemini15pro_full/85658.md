haustorium:/hɔːˈstɔːriəm/
noun
吸器
A specialized structure of a parasitic plant or fungus, typically a slender projection from the root or hypha, that penetrates the tissues of a host organism and absorbs nutrients.
The haustorium of the dodder plant penetrates the stem of its host.  菟丝子的吸器穿透宿主的茎。
haustoria (plural)
absorbing organ, sucker
