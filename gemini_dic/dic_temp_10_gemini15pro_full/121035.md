misconceivers:/ˌmɪskənˈsiːvərz/
n.
错误理解者；误解者
Those who misconceive something; people who hold incorrect beliefs or understandings.
Many misconceivers believe the Earth is flat. 很多误解者认为地球是平的。
The misconceivers spread false information about the vaccine. 误解者散播了关于疫苗的错误信息。
misunderstanders, misinterpreters
(No commonly used antonyms)
