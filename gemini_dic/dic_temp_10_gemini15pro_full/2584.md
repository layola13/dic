admonishments: /ˌædməˈnɪʃmənts/
n.
警告，劝诫，训诫
counsels, warnings or reprimands
He received several admonishments from his teacher for not completing his homework. 他因为没有完成作业而受到了老师的几次警告。

The coach delivered some stern admonishments to the team after their poor performance. 教练在球队表现糟糕后向他们发出了严厉的警告。

近义词: warnings, reprimands, cautions, rebukes, reproaches
反义词: praise, commendation, encouragement
词性: 名词(plural)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *amonestment*, from Old French *amonestement*, from *amonester* "to admonish," from Late Latin *admonestare* "to remind, urge," from Latin *ad* "to" + *monestare* "to warn," from *monere* "to advise, warn."
