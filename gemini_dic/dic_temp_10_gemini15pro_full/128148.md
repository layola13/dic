netted:/ˈnɛtɪd/
adjective
净得的，净赚的；用网覆盖的
covered with a net; having gained a particular amount of money after all costs and expenses have been deducted.
The company netted a profit of $1 million last year.  该公司去年净赚100万美元。
The netted fruit trees were protected from the birds. 用网覆盖的果树得到了保护，免受鸟类侵害。
Synonyms: gained, earned, clear, net, covered, meshed
Antonyms: gross, lost, uncovered
词性: 形容词


netted:/ˈnɛtɪd/
verb (past tense of net)
用网捕捉；净赚；净得
to catch something in a net; to earn or receive a particular amount of money as a profit after tax or other costs have been removed
The fishermen netted a large haul of salmon. 渔民用网捕获了大量的鲑鱼。
She netted $50,000 from the sale of her house. 她卖掉房子净赚了5万美元。
Synonyms: caught, trapped, earned, gained, cleared
Antonyms: lost, released, freed
词性: 动词
