attributivenesses:/əˌtrɪbjuːtɪvnɪsɪz/ | n. | 属性；归属；归因 | The quality of being attributive; the state of being an attribute. | The attributivenesses of the adjectives in the sentence helped clarify the meaning of the nouns. 句子中形容词的属性有助于阐明名词的含义。 | synonyms: attributes, characteristics, properties, qualities | antonyms: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From attributive + -ness.  Attributive comes from Late Latin *attributīvus, from Latin attributus (past participle of attribuere, "to assign, ascribe"), from ad- ("to") + tribuere ("to allot, assign").
