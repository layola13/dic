dorado:/dɔːˈrɑːdoʊ/ (US), /dɔːˈrɑːdəʊ/ (UK)| n.| 鲯鳅鱼; 海豚鱼 (指鱼); 理想中的黄金国 (El Dorado)|A large, colorful, edible fish found in tropical and subtropical waters, also known as mahi-mahi or dolphin fish (not related to the mammal). Also refers to a legendary city of gold sought by Spanish explorers in South America.|例句：The dorado leaped out of the water, flashing its brilliant colors.  鲯鳅鱼跃出水面，闪耀着绚丽的色彩。

例句：They caught a large dorado while deep-sea fishing.  他们在深海钓鱼时捕到了一条大鲯鳅鱼。|近义词：mahi-mahi, dolphin fish (referring to the fish)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
