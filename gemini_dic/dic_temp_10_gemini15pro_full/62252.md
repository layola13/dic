encumbered:/ɪnˈkʌmbərd/
adj.
负担沉重的，受妨碍的
Loaded down; burdened.  Hindered; restricted.
The hikers were encumbered by heavy backpacks.  这些徒步旅行者被沉重的背包所累。
近义词：burdened, hampered, hindered, weighed down
反义词：unburdened, free, unimpeded
词性: 形容词

encumber:/ɪnˈkʌmbər/
v.
妨碍，阻碍
To burden or weigh down. To impede or hinder.
Legal restrictions encumbered the sale of the property. 法律限制阻碍了房产的出售。
近义词：burden, hamper, hinder, obstruct
反义词：facilitate, aid, assist
词性: 动词

词源：From Old French *encombrer*, from *combre* "obstacle, dam," ultimately from Latin *cumulus* "heap."
