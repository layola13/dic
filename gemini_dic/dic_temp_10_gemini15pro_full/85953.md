headmost:/ˈhedˌmoʊst/| adj.|最前面的，最主要的|Situated at the very front; foremost.|The headmost car in the race took an early lead. 赛跑中最前面的车取得了领先。|近义词：foremost, leading, first, chief|反义词：rearmost, last, hindmost|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old English *hēafodmest, a superlative of hēafod 'head'.
