normalisation:/ˌnɔːməlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ (UK) /ˌnɔːrmələˈzeɪʃən/ (US)| n.| 归一化，标准化|The process of bringing or returning something to a normal condition or state.  It often involves establishing a common standard or scale.|Data normalisation is crucial for efficient database management. 数据规范化对于有效的数据库管理至关重要。|近义词：standardization, regularization|反义词: deviation, abnormality|noun
