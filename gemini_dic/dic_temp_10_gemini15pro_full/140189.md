offhandish:/ˌɒfˈhændɪʃ/ /ˌɔːfˈhændɪʃ/ | adj. | 漫不经心的；随便的；怠慢的 | Showing little interest or concern; casual and slightly rude. |  He gave an offhandish reply, as if he didn't care about the question. 他漫不经心地回答，好像根本不在乎这个问题。

"Sorry, I forgot your birthday," she said, with an offhandish wave. “对不起，我忘了你的生日了，”她漫不经心地挥了挥手说道。

近义词: casual, curt, dismissive, aloof, indifferent | 反义词: attentive, considerate, thoughtful, polite, courteous | 词性: adjective
