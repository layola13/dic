foresightednesses:/fɔːrˈsaɪtɪdnɪsɪz/
|n.|远见卓识，先见之明|The quality of being able to anticipate future needs or events.|His foresightednesses in investing allowed him to retire early. 他在投资方面的远见卓识使他能够提前退休。|synonyms: foresight, prescience, vision|antonyms: shortsightedness, myopia|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From foresightedness + -es (plural suffix).  Foresightedness comes from foresighted + -ness. Foresighted comes from fore- (before) + sighted (having sight).
