decentralises: /diːˈsɛntrəlaɪzɪz/ | v. | 分散，去中心化 | To distribute the administrative powers or functions of (a central authority) over a less concentrated area or group.  | The company decentralised its operations to empower regional offices.  公司将运营分散到各个地区办事处，以赋予它们权力。 | The new CEO decentralised decision-making, giving more autonomy to individual teams. 新任首席执行官将决策权下放，赋予各个团队更大的自主权。 | synonyms: distribute, disperse, devolve | antonyms: centralise, consolidate | verb
