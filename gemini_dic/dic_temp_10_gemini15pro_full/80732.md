gorsier:/ˈɡɔːrsiər/ (US), /ˈɡɔːzɪə/ (UK)| adj.|更粗的，更粗糙的；更刺耳的；更沙哑的|rougher or harsher, especially in sound; having a rough or grating quality|Her voice was gorsier than usual after shouting at the game.  她的声音在比赛中喊叫后比平时更沙哑了。|The fabric felt gorsier than I expected. 这块布料摸起来比我想象的要粗糙。|近义词：hoarser, rougher, coarser, harsher|反义词：smoother, softer, mellower|词性:形容词

词源:
From the French word "gorger" meaning "throat," and related to the Old French word "gorge," meaning "throat" or "gullet." The "-ier" ending is a comparative suffix, meaning "more."  源自法语单词 "gorger"，意为“喉咙”，与古法语单词 "gorge" 相关，意为“喉咙”或“食道”。“-ier” 结尾是一个比较后缀，意思是“更”。
