hyperbarbarous:/ˌhaɪpərˈbɑːrbərəs/
adjective
极其野蛮的；极度粗俗的
excessively barbarous; extremely crude or unrefined
例句：The hyperbarbarous treatment of prisoners of war was condemned by international organizations.  这些战俘遭受的极其野蛮的待遇受到了国际组织的谴责。

例句：His hyperbarbarous language shocked even the most seasoned sailors. 他极其粗俗的语言甚至让最有经验的水手都感到震惊。

近义词：savage, brutal, barbaric, cruel, inhuman
反义词：civilized, refined, cultured, humane
