boke:/boʊk/ | n. | 失焦；模糊 | A photograph or part of a photograph that is intentionally out of focus. |  The background of the portrait was a soft boke.  肖像的背景是柔和的失焦效果。| synonyms: blur, fuzziness | antonyms: focus, sharpness | noun
