fonding:/ˈfɒndɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈfɑːndɪŋ/ (US)| n.|喜爱；溺爱；抚摸|The act of showing affection, especially by touching or caressing. |She showed her fondness for the cat by constant fonding. 她不断地抚摸着猫，表达对它的喜爱。|近义词：caressing, petting, stroking, cuddling|反义词：neglect, abuse|词性:noun

fonding:/ˈfɒndɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈfɑːndɪŋ/ (US)| adj.|喜爱的；溺爱的|(Often used before a noun) having a liking or affection for (someone or something). This meaning is less common. |He gave her a fonding look. 他给了她一个充满爱意的眼神。|近义词：affectionate, loving, doting|反义词：uncaring, indifferent|词性:adjective
