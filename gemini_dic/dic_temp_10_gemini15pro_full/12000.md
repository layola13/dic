arthrodia:/ɑːˈθrəʊdiə/| n. |关节窝|A shallow joint socket.  A type of diarthrosis (freely movable joint) in which the articular surfaces are flat or nearly flat, enabling only gliding movements.|The acromioclavicular joint is an example of an arthrodia. 肩锁关节是关节窝的一个例子。|Synonyms: gliding joint, plane joint|Antonyms: synarthrosis (immovable joint)|noun

arthrodial:/ɑːˈθrəʊdiəl/| adj. |关节窝的|Relating to or resembling an arthrodia; having a shallow joint socket.|The arthrodial surfaces of the carpals allow for a wide range of wrist motion. 腕骨的关节窝表面允许手腕进行大范围的运动。|Synonyms: plane, gliding|Antonyms: fixed, fused|adjective
