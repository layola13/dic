outwinging:/ˌaʊtˈwɪŋɪŋ/
verb
胜过，以更宽的翼展超越
surpassing or exceeding in wingspan or scope.  Extending beyond the wings of something else.
The new aircraft is outwinging all its competitors in terms of fuel efficiency.  这架新飞机在燃油效率方面胜过所有竞争对手。
The condor was outwinging the eagle, soaring high above the mountains.  秃鹰的翼展超过了老鹰，在群山上空翱翔。
Synonyms: surpassing, exceeding, outstripping, outdoing
Antonyms: lagging, trailing, falling behind
词性: 动词 (verb)

outwinging:/ˌaʊtˈwɪŋɪŋ/
adjective
翼展更大的，超过…翼展的
Having a greater wingspan than something else. Extending beyond the wings of something else.
The outwinging aircraft quickly gained altitude.  这架翼展更大的飞机迅速升空。
The outwinging branches of the tree provided ample shade.  这棵树向外伸展的树枝提供了充足的阴凉。
Synonyms: wider, broader, larger, more expansive
Antonyms: narrower, smaller, less expansive
词性: 形容词 (adjective)
