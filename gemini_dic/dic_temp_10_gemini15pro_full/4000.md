ahaz:/ˈeɪhæz/| n.|亚哈斯（犹大王）|Ahaz was a king of Judah who reigned c. 735 – c. 715 BCE. He is portrayed in the Bible as a wicked king who introduced pagan practices into the kingdom.|Ahaz refused to heed the prophet Isaiah's warning. 亚哈斯拒绝听从先知以赛亚的警告。|Synonym: king|Antonym: commoner|noun
