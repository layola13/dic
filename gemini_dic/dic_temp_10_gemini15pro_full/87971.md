high-grading:/ˈhaɪˌɡreɪdɪŋ/ | n. | 高品位开采；拣精矿；优中选优；拔尖 | The selective mining of the richest ore or material, often illegally or unethically. | Miners were caught high-grading, stealing the most valuable gold nuggets. 矿工们被抓到拣精矿，偷走了最有价值的金块。 | 近义词: cherry-picking, cream-skimming, selective mining | 反义词: low-grading | 词性: 名词

high-grade: /ˈhaɪˌɡreɪd/ | adj. | 高品位的；高级的；优等的 | Of superior quality or grade. |  The company only uses high-grade materials in its products. 这家公司只在其产品中使用高级材料。 | 近义词: superior, top-quality, premium | 反义词: low-grade, inferior | 词性: 形容词

high-grade: /ˈhaɪˌɡreɪd/ | v. | 提高…的等级；精炼；拣精矿 | To improve the grade or quality of something; to selectively extract the best parts of something. |  They high-graded the ore before shipping it to the refinery. 他们在将矿石运送到炼油厂之前对其进行了精炼。 | 近义词: refine, upgrade, improve | 反义词: downgrade | 词性: 动词
