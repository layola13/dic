mistrain:/ˌmɪsˈtreɪn/| vt.|错误地训练；训练不当|To train incorrectly or improperly, often resulting in bad habits or flawed technique.|The athlete was mistrained in his youth, leading to chronic injuries later in his career.  这位运动员年轻时训练不当，导致他职业生涯后期慢性损伤。|The dog was mistrained and would bite strangers. 这只狗被错误地训练过，会咬陌生人。|近义词：misteach, miseducate|反义词: train properly, educate correctly|词性:动词
