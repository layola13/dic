offence:/əˈfens/ (US) /əˈfɛns/ (UK)| n.|冒犯；罪行；进攻|An act that breaks a law or rule, or causes anger or upset.  An attack.|例句：His words were taken as a grave offence. 他的话被认为是严重的冒犯。

例句：Driving under the influence of alcohol is a criminal offence.  酒后驾车是刑事犯罪。|近义词：crime, misdemeanor, transgression, insult, attack, assault|反义词：defense, compliment|词性: noun


offense:/əˈfens/ (US) /əˈfɛns/ (UK)| n.|进攻；攻势|The players on a sports team whose job is to try to score points.|例句：The team’s offence was surprisingly weak.  该队的进攻出人意料地弱。

例句：Their offense struggled in the second half. 他们的进攻在下半场很吃力。|近义词：attack, assault|反义词：defense|词性: noun
