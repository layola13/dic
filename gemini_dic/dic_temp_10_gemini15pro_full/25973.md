burs:/bɜːrz/| n. |囊；滑囊|A small sac containing synovial fluid, located at points of friction, especially in joints, to facilitate motion.|The horse developed burs on its knees from kneeling on hard ground. 这匹马因为跪在坚硬的地面上，膝盖上长出了滑囊。|Synonyms: sac, pouch|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a physical structure like a bursa)|Noun
