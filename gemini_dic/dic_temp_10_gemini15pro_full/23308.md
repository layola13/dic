boybands:/ˈbɔɪbændz/| n. |男团，男子乐队|A group of young male singers who play pop music and are seen as attractive by young women. |例句：The 1990s saw a surge in the popularity of boybands. 20世纪90年代见证了男团的盛行。

例句：Many teenage girls were obsessed with the new boyband. 许多十几岁的女孩痴迷于这个新男团。|近义词：boy group, male group, pop group|反义词:girlband (女子组合)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
boy + band.  "Boy" referring to a young male and "band" referring to a musical group.  The term gained popularity in the late 20th century with the rise of manufactured pop groups.
