can-do:/ˈkænˈduː/| adj.|积极进取的；有能力的；乐于助人的|Having a positive, confident attitude and believing that you can succeed; willing and eager to help or achieve something.|She has a real can-do attitude and I'm sure she'll do well in the new job. 她有一种积极进取的态度，我相信她会在新工作中做得很好。|synonyms: positive, proactive, willing, eager, resourceful|antonyms: negative, pessimistic, unwilling, apathetic, unhelpful|adjective

can-do:/ˈkænˈduː/| n.|积极进取的态度；自信心|A positive, confident attitude and a willingness to achieve something.|Her can-do helped her overcome many challenges. 她的积极进取帮助她克服了许多挑战。|synonyms: positivism, optimism, confidence, initiative, drive|antonyms: negativity, pessimism, apathy, inertia|noun
