oraison:/ɔːˈreɪzən/ /əˈreɪʒn/
noun
祈祷，祷告，演说
A prayer or petition; an address or discourse.
She offered an oraison for the sick. 她为病人做了祷告。
The preacher delivered a moving oraison at the funeral. 牧师在葬礼上发表了感人的演说。
Synonyms: prayer, supplication, petition, invocation, address, discourse, sermon
Antonyms: blasphemy, curse, imprecation
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *oraison*, from Latin *ōrātiō* (“prayer, plea, discourse”), from *ōrāre* (“to pray, plead, speak”), from *ōs* (“mouth”).
