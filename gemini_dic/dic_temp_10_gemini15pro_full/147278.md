overmix:/ˌoʊvərˈmɪks/| v. |过度混合，混合过度|To mix ingredients, especially the ingredients of a cake, for too long.|例句：I think I overmixed the batter, the cake is a little tough. 我想我把面糊搅拌过度了，蛋糕有点硬。|例句：Be careful not to overmix the dough, or the bread will be dense.  小心不要过度揉捏面团，否则面包会很硬。|近义词：overbeat, overwork|反义词: undermix|词性:动词
