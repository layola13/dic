homopteran:/hɒˈmɒptərən/ /hɑːˈmɑːptərən/
n.
同翅目昆虫
An insect of the order Homoptera, such as an aphid, cicada, or leafhopper.  These insects typically have sucking mouthparts and forewings that are uniform in texture.
Aphids are common homopteran pests in gardens. 蚜虫是花园里常见的同翅目害虫。
近义词: hemipteran,  auchenorrhynchan (more specific)
反义词: N/A (no direct opposite for a taxonomic group)
词性: noun


Etymology:
From Ancient Greek ὁμόπτερος (homópteros, “having the same wings”), from ὁμός (homós, “same”) + πτερόν (pterón, “wing”).
