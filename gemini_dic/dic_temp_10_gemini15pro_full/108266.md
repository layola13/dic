launderable:/ˈlɔːndərəbl/ /ˈlɑːndərəbl/| adj.|可洗涤的，耐洗的|Able to be washed, especially in a washing machine, without being damaged.|This dress is launderable, so you don't need to dry clean it. 这件衣服可以水洗，所以你不需要干洗。|The tablecloth is made of a launderable fabric. 这块桌布是由可洗涤的织物制成的。|近义词：washable, cleanable|反义词：non-washable, unwashable|词性:形容词

launder:/ˈlɔːndər/ /ˈlɑːndər/| v. n.|洗涤；洗黑钱|1.(v.) To wash and iron clothes or other items made of cloth. 2. (v.) To conceal the source of illegally obtained money, typically by transferring it through a series of complex transactions.|1. I need to launder my dirty clothes. 我需要洗我的脏衣服。 2. He was accused of laundering drug money. 他被指控洗钱。|近义词：(for washing) wash, clean; (for illegal money) cleanse, filter|反义词：(for washing) soil; (for illegal money) expose|词性:动词、名词
