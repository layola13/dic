aboss: /əˈbɒs/ (UK) /əˈbɔːs/ (US) | n. | 老板; 工头; 头目 | A person who is in charge of a worker or organization. |  He's the aboss of a big company. 他是一家大公司的老板。

He's my aboss, so I have to do what he says. 他是我的老板，所以我必须听他的。 | 近义词: boss, chief, manager, supervisor, superintendent | 反义词: employee, worker, subordinate | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Dutch word "baas," meaning "master."  Originally entered English as "baas" and later evolved into "aboss."
