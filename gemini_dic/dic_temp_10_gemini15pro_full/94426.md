imploded:/ɪmˈploʊdɪd/
verb
内爆；崩溃；瓦解
collapsed violently inwards.
例句：The submarine imploded under the immense pressure of the deep ocean. 潜水艇在深海巨大的压力下内爆了。

例句：His carefully constructed plans imploded when his main investor pulled out. 当他的主要投资者退出时，他精心制定的计划破产了。
近义词：collapsed, crumbled, disintegrated, shattered
反义词：exploded, expanded, thrived
词性：v.
