bandaging:/ˈbændɪdʒɪŋ/
n.|包扎；绷带|The act of applying a bandage to a wound.|The nurse was skilled in bandaging. 护士很擅长包扎。
bandaging:/ˈbændɪdʒɪŋ/
adj.|用于包扎的|Relating to or used for bandaging.|Bandaging materials are essential in a first-aid kit.  急救箱里必须要有包扎用品。


Etymology->
From bandage + -ing.  Bandage itself comes from Old French "bandage," ultimately from Late Latin "binda" (a strip of cloth).


Synonyms->
dressing, wrapping, binding

Antonyms->
unwrapping, uncovering

Word Type->
Noun, Adjective (less common)
