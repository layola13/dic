dog-eared:/ˈdɒɡɪəd/ (UK) /ˈdɑːɡɪrd/ (US)| adj.|卷角的；翻旧的；（书页）折角的；（比喻）破旧的|Having the corners of the pages turned down, typically from much use or reading.  Figuratively, it can also mean worn out or shabby.|例句：The dog-eared book on his nightstand was a testament to how many times he'd read it. 他床头柜上那本卷角的书证明了他读过多少遍。

例句：Her favorite childhood toy was a dog-eared teddy bear. 她最喜欢的童年玩具是一个破旧的泰迪熊。|近义词：worn, tattered, battered, thumbed|反义词：pristine, new, mint-condition|词性：形容词
