caveatee:/ˌkeɪviːˈtiː/ | n. | 受警告人，被提醒者 | A person to whom a caveat has been given; one who receives notice of a caveat. |  The caveatee was informed of the legal proceedings. 受警告人被告知了法律程序。 | 近义词: alertee, notified party | 反义词: caveator | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Latin *caveatus*, past participle of *caveo* ("I am wary, I take heed"), from which also "caveat".
