ichthyopterygium:/ɪkθiɒptəˈrɪdʒiəm/ | n. | 鱼龙鳍 |A modified limb or fin of an ichthyosaur.  It resembles the flipper of a dolphin or whale but contains bones homologous to those in the forelimbs or hindlimbs of terrestrial vertebrates. | The ichthyopterygium of the ichthyosaur was well-adapted to life in the water. 鱼龙的鱼龙鳍非常适应水中生活。|Synonym: flipper, paddle | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps limb)| noun
