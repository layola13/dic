clearance:/ˈklɪərəns/| n.|清除，清理；许可，批准；间隙，余隙|The act of clearing or the state of being cleared; permission to proceed; the space between two objects.|例句：The store is having a clearance sale.  这家商店正在进行清仓大甩卖。

例句：We need security clearance to enter the building. 我们需要安全许可才能进入大楼。|近义词：authorization, permission, approval, sale, gap, space|反义词:obstruction, blockage, denial|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "clear" + "-ance" (suffix forming nouns of action or process).  "Clear" comes from Old French "cler" (bright, shining, clear), ultimately from Latin "clarus" (clear, bright).
