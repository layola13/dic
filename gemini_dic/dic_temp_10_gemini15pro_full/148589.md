overtaxed:/ˌoʊvərˈtækst/| adj.|负担过重的，课税过重的，过度疲劳的|Overtaxed means burdened with excessive demands or work; excessively taxed.|The overtaxed healthcare system is struggling to cope with the pandemic. 负担过重的医疗系统正努力应对疫情。|The citizens felt overtaxed by the new levies imposed by the government. 市民们感到政府新征的税让他们负担过重。|近义词：overburdened, strained, overwhelmed|反义词:underutilized, rested, relaxed|词性:形容词
词源: From over- + tax.  "tax" comes from Old French "taxer" meaning "to assess, impose a tax."  "Over-" signifies "excessively".
