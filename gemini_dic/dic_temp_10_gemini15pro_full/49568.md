dementia:/dɪˈmenʃə/ /dɪˈmɛnʃə/| n. |痴呆，失智|A chronic or persistent disorder of the mental processes caused by brain disease or injury and marked by memory disorders, personality changes, and impaired reasoning.|Her grandmother suffers from dementia. 她祖母患有痴呆症。|Synonyms: Alzheimer's, senility, mental decline|Antonyms: lucidity, mental acuity|noun


demented:/dɪˈmɛntɪd/| adj. |疯狂的，精神错乱的|Driven to behave irrationally due to anger, distress, or excitement.|He had a demented look in his eyes. 他的眼神看起来很疯狂。|Synonyms: insane, mad, crazy, deranged|Antonyms: sane, rational, lucid|adjective
