corrugant:/ˈkɔːrəɡənt/ /ˈkɔːrʊɡənt/ | adj. | 波纹状的；有皱纹的；起皱的 |  wrinkled or furrowed; formed into ridges and grooves. | The caterpillar's skin was corrugant, covered in tiny folds. 毛毛虫的皮肤皱巴巴的，布满了细小的褶皱。| The old woman's face was corrugant with age. 老妇人的脸因年老而布满皱纹。 |  ridged, grooved, wrinkled, furrowed, creased | smooth, flat | adjective
