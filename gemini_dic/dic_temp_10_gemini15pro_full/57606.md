drapey:/ˈdreɪpi/| adj.|悬垂的，披肩似的，宽松的|Hanging loosely or in folds; flowing.  Characterized by soft, flowing folds of fabric.|Her dress was a drapey silk that flowed beautifully as she moved. 她的裙子是用悬垂的丝绸制成的，走动时非常飘逸。|近义词：flowing, loose, flowing, loose-fitting, billowing|反义词:tight, fitted, stiff|adjective

drapey:/ˈdreɪpi/| n.|悬垂的织物，披肩似的衣服|A piece of fabric or clothing that hangs loosely or in folds.|She wore a beautiful drapey over her shoulders. 她肩上披着一件漂亮的悬垂式披肩。|近义词：wrap, shawl, stole|反义词: - |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From drape + -y.  "Drape" comes from the Old North French word "draper" meaning "to make cloth," ultimately from the Latin "drappus," meaning "cloth."  The suffix "-y" is used to form adjectives indicating a quality or characteristic, in this case, having the quality of draping.
