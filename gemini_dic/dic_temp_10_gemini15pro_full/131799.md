nonconscription:/ˌnɒnkənˈskrɪpʃən/ | n. | 免役，不征兵 | The absence of conscription, or the system where military service is not compulsory. |  Many countries have moved towards nonconscription and maintain a professional army. 许多国家已经转向不征兵，并保持一支职业军队。|  The principle of nonconscription is central to libertarian ideals.  免役的原则是自由主义者理想的核心。 | synonyms: exemption from military service, voluntary military service | antonyms: conscription, draft, compulsory military service | noun
