bipedal:/baɪˈpiːdl/| adj.|两足动物的，用两足行走的|Using two feet for walking.  Relating to, or involving two feet.|Humans are bipedal. 人类是用两足行走的。|biped, two-footed|quadrupedal, four-footed|adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *bīpēs* ("two-footed"), from *bī-* ("two") + *pēs* ("foot").
