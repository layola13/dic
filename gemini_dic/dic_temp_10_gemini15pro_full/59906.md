edemata:/ɪˈdiːmətə/
n.
水肿（复数）
Swelling, typically of the limbs, due to an accumulation of fluid in the tissues.
The patient presented with severe edemata in both legs. 病人的双腿出现了严重的水肿。
edemas, swellings
atrophy, shrinkage
noun
