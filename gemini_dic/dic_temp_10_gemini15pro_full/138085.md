norman:/ˈnɔːrmən/ | n. adj.|诺曼人，诺曼式的|A member of a people of Viking origin who settled in northern France (Normandy) in the 10th century and under William the Conqueror, conquered England in 1066; relating to the Normans or their language, culture, or history.|例句：The Norman Conquest had a profound impact on England. 诺曼征服对英格兰产生了深远的影响。|近义词：Norman French (language) |反义词:Anglo-Saxon|词性：名词，形容词
