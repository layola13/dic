airily:/ˈɛərɪli/| adv.|轻快地，无忧无虑地，漫不经心地|In a lighthearted and carefree manner; without serious thought or concern.|She airily dismissed his concerns. 她漫不经心地打消了他的顾虑。|Synonyms: lightly, casually, flippantly, nonchalantly|Antonyms: seriously, gravely, solemnly|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From airy + -ly.  Airy from air + -y.  Ultimately from Latin *aerius.
