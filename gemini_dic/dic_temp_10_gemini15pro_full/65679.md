evened-out:/ˈiːvənd aʊt/| adj.| 平衡的，均匀的，平稳的 | Made smooth, uniform, or balanced.  The road was finally evened-out after years of neglect.  多年来一直被忽视的道路终于被整平了。 | Synonyms: balanced, level, uniform, smooth, stable | Antonyms: uneven, bumpy, irregular, fluctuating, volatile | adjective

evened-out:/ˈiːvənd aʊt/| v.| (使) 平衡，(使) 均匀，(使) 平稳 | To make something smooth, uniform, or balanced.  The chef evened-out the batter in the pan. 厨师把锅里的面糊抹平。 | Synonyms: level, smooth, balance, stabilize, equalize | Antonyms: unbalance, roughen, destabilize | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "even out," which combines "even" (meaning smooth, level) and "out" (indicating a change to a more complete or balanced state). "Even" ultimately comes from Old English "efen," meaning level.
