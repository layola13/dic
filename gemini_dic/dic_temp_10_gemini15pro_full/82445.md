grumblesome: /ˈɡrʌmb(ə)lsəm/ | adj. | 爱抱怨的，爱发牢骚的 | Inclined to grumble; complaining. |  He was a grumblesome old man, never satisfied with anything. 他是一个爱抱怨的老人，对任何事都不满意。|  She gave him a grumblesome look. 她给了他一个抱怨的眼神。 | 近义词: grumpy, cantankerous, querulous | 反义词: cheerful, good-natured, agreeable | 词性: 形容词

词源: From grumble (v.) + -some (suffix forming adjectives meaning 'characterized by').  Grumble itself comes from Middle English *grumbelen, of imitative origin.
