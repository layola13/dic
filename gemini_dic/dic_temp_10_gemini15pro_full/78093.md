geognosy:/dʒiˈɒɡnəsi/
noun
地质构造学；地球构造学
The study of the structure and composition of the earth, especially the parts of the earth accessible by direct observation.
Geognosy played a crucial role in the early development of geology.  地质构造学在地质学的早期发展中起到了至关重要的作用。
The old prospector had a deep geognosy of the region. 老探矿者对该地区的地质构造了解很深。
近义词: geology, geomorphology
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun

Etymology: From German *Geognosie*, from Ancient Greek *γῆ* (gê, “earth”) + *γνῶσις* (gnôsis, “knowledge”).
