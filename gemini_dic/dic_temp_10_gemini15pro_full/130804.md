nonantibiotics:/ˌnɒnˌæntɪbaɪˈɒtɪks/
noun
非抗生素药物
Substances that are not antibiotics, used for purposes other than treating bacterial infections.
The doctor prescribed nonantibiotics for the viral infection.  医生给病毒感染开了非抗生素药物。
synonyms: non-antibacterial agents, non-antimicrobial drugs
antonyms: antibiotics, antibacterial agents
n.
