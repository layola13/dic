co-educationally:/ˌkoʊˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃənəli/
adverb
共同教育地；男女同校地
In a way that involves both male and female students being taught together.
The school is run co-educationally. 这所学校是男女合校的。
Synonyms: together, jointly
Antonyms: separately, independently
词性: 副词


Etymology:
From "co-educational" + "-ly."  "Co-educational" comes from "co-" (together) + "educational" (relating to education).  "Educational" ultimately derives from the Latin "educatus," the past participle of "educare" (to bring up, to educate).
