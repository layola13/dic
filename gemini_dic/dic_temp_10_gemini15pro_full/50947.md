designated hitter:/ˌdɛzɪɡˌneɪtɪd ˈhɪtər/| n.|指定打击员|A player in baseball who bats in place of the pitcher.|The designated hitter hit a home run in the bottom of the ninth. 指定打击员在第九局下半场击出一支本垒打。|Synonym: DH|Antonym: Pitcher|Noun

designated hitters:/ˌdɛzɪɡˌneɪtɪd ˈhɪtərz/| n.|指定打击员们|Plural form of "designated hitter," referring to multiple players who bat in place of the pitcher.|Both designated hitters struggled at the plate tonight. 两位指定打击员今晚的打击表现都不佳。|Synonym: DHs|Antonym: Pitchers|Noun
