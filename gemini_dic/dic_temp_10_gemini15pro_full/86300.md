hebdomatical:/hɛbˈdɒmətɪk(ə)l/
adj.
每周的，一周一次的
Relating to a week or period of seven days; weekly.
The hebdomatical report summarized the team's progress.  每周报告总结了团队的进展。
The monks held a hebdomatical meeting to discuss spiritual matters.  僧侣们每周举行一次会议讨论精神事务。
近义词:weekly, septenary
反义词:daily, monthly, annual
词性:形容词
