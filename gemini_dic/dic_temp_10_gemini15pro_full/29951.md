caveman: /ˈkeɪvˌmæn/ (US), /ˈkeɪvˌmæn/ (UK)
n.
穴居人；野蛮人
A person who lived in a cave in prehistoric times.  A person who behaves in a rough or uncivilized way.
The museum exhibit featured tools used by cavemen.  博物馆的展览以穴居人使用的工具为特色。
He acted like a caveman, grabbing food off other people's plates.  他的行为像个野蛮人，从别人的盘子里抢食物。
近义词: troglodyte, Neanderthal
反义词: modern man, civilized person
词性: noun


Etymology:
From cave + man.  First recorded in the late 19th century.
