ascocarp:/ˈæskəˌkɑːrp/ | n. | 子囊果 |The fruiting body of an ascomycete fungus, containing asci and ascospores. |  The ascocarp of the morel is highly prized by gourmet cooks. | 羊肚菌的子囊果很受美食家推崇。 | synonym: fruiting body | antonym:  N/A (No direct antonym for a fruiting body structure) | noun

Etymology: From New Latin *ascocarpus, from ascus + -carpus ("fruit").  Ascus comes from the Greek "askos" meaning "sac" or "bag," referring to the sac-like shape of the ascus.
