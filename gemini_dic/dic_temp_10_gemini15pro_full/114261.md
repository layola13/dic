maim:/meɪm/| vt.|使残废，使残缺|The accident maimed him for life.|这场事故使他终身残疾。|Synonyms: cripple, disable, mutilate|Antonyms: heal, cure|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *mahaignier* (modern French *mahaigner*), from *mahain*, meaning "defect, blemish." Ultimately from Frankish *maham*, meaning "blemish, bodily defect."
