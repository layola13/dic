neutralist:/ˈnjuːtrəlɪst/| n. adj.|中立主义者，中立的|A person or country that does not support or help either side in a conflict, war, etc.; not supporting or helping either side in a conflict, war, etc.|例句：Switzerland has a long history of being a neutralist in European conflicts. 瑞士在欧洲冲突中长期保持中立。|近义词：nonaligned, impartial, uninvolved|反义词：partisan, belligerent, biased|词性: noun, adjective
Etymology: From neutral + -ist.  "neutral" comes from Late Latin neutralis, from neuter.
