livre:/liːvrə/ | n. | 书; (旧时法国货币单位) 法郎; |A book, especially one bound in leather or with a hard cover.  Formerly, the basic monetary unit of France. |例句：He found an old livre in the attic. 他在阁楼里发现了一本古老的书。|例句：The price was listed as twenty livres. 价格标为二十法郎。 |近义词：book, volume |反义词: None (for book);  (No direct antonym for a unit of currency) |词性: noun
