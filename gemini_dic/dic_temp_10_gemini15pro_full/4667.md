alehouse: /ˈeɪlˌhaʊs/ | n. | 酒馆，啤酒屋 | A place where ale (beer) is sold and drunk; a pub. |  例句：The weary travelers stopped at an alehouse for a pint. 疲惫的旅行者在一家小酒馆停下来喝了一品脱啤酒。 | 近义词：pub, tavern, inn | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Middle English *alehus, from Old English ealhūs (ale + house).
