antitypous:/ænˈtɪtɪpəs/ | adj. | 相反的，对立的 |  Of opposite type or character; antithetical.  Derived from Greek *antitypos* "a copy, image,"  from *anti* "against, opposite" + *typos* "impression, type".  Often used in theological contexts. |  The antitypous relationship between the Passover lamb and Christ.  逾越节的羔羊和基督之间的对立关系。 | synonyms: opposite, contrary, antithetical | antonyms: similar, alike, analogous | adjective
