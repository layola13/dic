friable:/ˈfraɪəbl/ (US) /ˈfraɪəbəl/ (UK)| adj.|易碎的，脆的|Easily crumbled or broken; brittle.|The soil is dry and friable. 土壤干燥易碎。|The cake was light and friable.  蛋糕松软易碎。|近义词：crumbly, brittle, fragile|反义词：tough, hard, compact|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *friabilis, from Latin friāre (“to rub, crumble”), from friāre (“to rub, crumble”).
