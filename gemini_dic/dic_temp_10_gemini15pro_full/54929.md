dispositionally:/ˌdɪspəˈzɪʃənəli/
adv.
气质上地，性格上地
By temperament or constitution.
He is dispositionally inclined to be optimistic. 他性格上倾向于乐观。
Synonyms: inherently, naturally, innately, temperamentally
Antonyms: situationally, environmentally, externally
