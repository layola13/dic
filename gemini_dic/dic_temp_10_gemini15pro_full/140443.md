OKR:/oʊ keɪ ˈɑːr/ | n. | 目标与关键成果; Objectives and Key Results | A goal-setting framework that helps companies define measurable goals and track their outcomes.  OKRs consist of an Objective, which tells you *where* you want to go, and several Key Results, which are measurable milestones that tell you *how* close you are to achieving your Objective. | We implemented OKRs to improve our team's performance. 我们实施了 OKR 以提高团队绩效。|  Synonyms: goals, targets, aims, objectives | Antonyms: N/A | noun
