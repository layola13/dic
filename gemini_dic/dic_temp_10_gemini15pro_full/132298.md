nondefiances:/ˌnɒndɪˈfaɪənsɪz/
n.
不服从；不顺从；不反抗
lack of defiance; obedience or submission
His nondefiance surprised everyone, as he was known for his rebellious nature. 他的不反抗让所有人感到惊讶，因为他一向以叛逆的性格而闻名。

近义词: obedience, submission, compliance, acquiescence
反义词: defiance, resistance, rebellion, disobedience
词性: 名词 (plural)
