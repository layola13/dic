heptathlon: /hɛpˈtæθlɒn/ | n. | 七项全能 | An athletic contest for women, consisting of seven track and field events held over two days. The events are, in order: 100-meter hurdles, high jump, shot put, 200-meter run, long jump, javelin throw, and 800-meter run. |  She competed in the heptathlon at the Olympics. 她参加了奥运会的七项全能比赛。| synonym: combined events, decathlon (for men) | antonym: N/A | noun
