hopers:/ˈhoʊpərz/| n.|希望者；抱有希望的人|People who maintain hope, especially in difficult circumstances.  They are the hopers, clinging to the belief that things will improve. 他们是希望者，紧紧抓住事情会好转的信念。|The hopers gathered for the announcement, eager to hear good news. 希望者们聚集在一起等待公告，渴望听到好消息。|Near synonyms: optimists, believers, aspirants|Near antonyms: pessimists, cynics|Noun
