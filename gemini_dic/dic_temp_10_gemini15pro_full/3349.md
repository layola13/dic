affreight:/əˈfreɪt/
verb
租船；包租（船舶或交通工具）；运送
to hire (a ship or other means of transport) for carrying goods.
The company affreighted a vessel to transport the goods overseas.  该公司租了一艘船将货物运往海外。
They affreighted a plane to deliver emergency supplies.  他们租了一架飞机运送紧急物资。
Synonym: charter, hire, lease
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, consider "unload", "discharge" in specific contexts)
词性: verb (动词)
