outhearing: /ˌaʊtˈhɪərɪŋ/
n.
胜过听力; 听得更远
The act of hearing something further or better than someone else.
He demonstrated his outhearing by detecting the faint sound of an approaching vehicle before anyone else. 他通过比其他人更早地察觉到接近的车辆微弱的声音来展示他的超强听力。
近义词: overhearing, eavesdropping
反义词: not hearing, deafness
词性: 名词
