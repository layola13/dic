The meeting focused on actional steps to improve the situation. 会议重点讨论了改善局势的行动步骤。|近义词：active, practical, operational|反义词：passive, inactive, theoretical|词性:形容词

词源: From action + -al.  Derived from the noun "action," with the suffix "-al" indicating "relating to."
