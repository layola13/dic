halophytism:/ˌhæloʊˈfaɪtɪzəm/ | n. | 盐生植物性，耐盐性 | The adaptation of plants to saline environments, including physiological mechanisms and morphological characteristics that enable them to thrive in high salt concentrations. |  Mangroves exhibit remarkable halophytism, thriving in coastal saltwater swamps.  红树林表现出非凡的耐盐性，在沿海咸水沼泽中茁壮成长。

| Synonym: salt tolerance, halotolerance | Antonym: glycophytism | noun
