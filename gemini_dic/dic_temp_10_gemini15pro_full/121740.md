misproposal:/ˌmɪsprəˈpoʊzəl/
n.
错误的提议，不恰当的建议
an inappropriate or ill-conceived proposal
He made a misproposal that was quickly rejected by the board. 他提出了一个很快就被董事会否决的错误提议。
近义词：bad proposal, inappropriate suggestion, flawed proposal
反义词：good proposal, appropriate suggestion, sound proposal
词性: 名词


词源:
mis- (prefix): wrong, bad, wrongly  错误的，坏的，错误地
proposal (noun): a plan or suggestion, especially a formal one, put forward for consideration or discussion 提议，建议，尤指正式提出的供考虑或讨论的计划或建议
