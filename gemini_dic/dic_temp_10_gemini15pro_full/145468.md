overclassifications: /ˌoʊvərˌklæsɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 过度分类 | The act of classifying something too specifically or into too many categories. |  Excessive division into classes or groups, often resulting in unnecessary complexity. |

例句：
The overclassifications of the data made the analysis more difficult than it needed to be.
数据过度分类使得分析比实际需要的更困难。


The overclassifications in the library system made it hard to find the book I was looking for.
图书馆系统中的过度分类使我很难找到我想要的书。


近义词: overcategorization, oversubdivision | 反义词: underclassification, simplification | 词性: noun
