camphoraceous:/ˌkæmfəˈreɪʃəs/ | adj. |樟脑的，有樟脑味的|Having the smell of camphor.| The ointment had a strong, camphoraceous scent.  这种药膏有一种强烈的樟脑味。|camphoric, camphory|  (No common antonym) |adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From camphor + -aceous.  Camphor ultimately derives from Sanskrit *karpūra*, meaning "camphor."  The suffix -aceous means "of or pertaining to," "resembling," or "having the nature of."
