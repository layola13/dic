inaudibility:/ˌɪnɔːdəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可闻性；听不见 |The inaudibility of the whisper made it even more mysterious.  耳语的听不见让它更加神秘。 | The high frequency sound is beyond the range of human inaudibility. 高频声音超出了人类听觉范围。| 近义词：unhearability, silence, faintness | 反义词：audibility, loudness | 词性: 名词

inaudible:/ɪnˈɔːdəbl/ | adj. | 听不见的；不可闻的 | The music was almost inaudible over the noise of the traffic.  在交通噪音中，音乐几乎听不见。 | Her voice was inaudible at the back of the room.  在房间的后面，她的声音听不见。 | 近义词：unhearable, faint, muffled | 反义词：audible, loud, clear | 词性: 形容词
