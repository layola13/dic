excitator:/ɪkˈsaɪtətər/ | n. | 激励者；刺激物；兴奋剂 | Something that excites or stimulates something else. |  The caffeine in coffee acts as an excitator, keeping people awake. 咖啡因在咖啡中充当兴奋剂，使人们保持清醒。

The coach's enthusiastic speech was an excitator for the team. 教练热情洋溢的讲话激励了整个团队。 | 近义词：stimulant, stimulus, instigator, motivator | 反义词：inhibitor, suppressant, depressant | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From the Late Latin word *excitator*, from the Latin verb *excitare* "to rouse, awaken, call out," from *ex-*, "out" + *citare* "to summon, rouse".
