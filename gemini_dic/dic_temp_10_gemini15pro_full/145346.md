overbulkinesses:/ˌōvərˈbʌlkɪnəsɪz/
n.
过度庞大；过度笨重
The state of being excessively bulky or large.
The overbulkinesses of the furniture made it difficult to move around the apartment.  家具过于笨重，使得在公寓里移动很困难。
The new packaging's overbulkinesses increased shipping costs. 新包装的过度庞大增加了运输成本。
Synonyms: bulkiness, massiveness, hugeness, largeness
Antonyms: compactness, smallness, slightness
noun
