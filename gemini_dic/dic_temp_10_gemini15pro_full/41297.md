cordon off:/ˈkɔːrdən ɒf/ (British) /ˈkɔːrdən ɔːf/ (American)
verb
隔离；封锁
To enclose or separate an area with a rope or barrier, often to prevent people from entering.
Police cordoned off the area after the explosion. 警方在爆炸发生后封锁了该区域。
The crime scene was cordoned off with yellow tape.  犯罪现场用黄色胶带封锁了。
近义词:block off, close off, seal off, shut off
反义词:open up
词性:verb
