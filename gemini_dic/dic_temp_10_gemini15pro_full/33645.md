cinematic:/ˌsɪnəˈmætɪk/| adj.|电影的；电影艺术的；像电影的|Relating to the cinema or filmmaking; having qualities characteristic of the cinema, especially in being visually impressive.|The movie had a very cinematic feel, with sweeping landscape shots and dramatic lighting. 这部电影有一种非常电影化的感觉，有广阔的风景镜头和戏剧性的灯光。|近义词：filmic, theatrical, dramatic|反义词：unremarkable, mundane|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cinématique*, from Greek *kínēma* (“movement”) + *-ikos* (adjectival suffix). Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰey- (“to go, come”).
