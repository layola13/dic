coniferin:/kəˈnɪfərɪn/
n.
松柏苷
A glucoside found in the sap of conifers, especially in the cambium, that yields coniferyl alcohol on hydrolysis.
Coniferin is extracted from the bark of certain conifers. 松柏苷是从某些针叶树的树皮中提取的。
Coniferin plays a crucial role in lignin biosynthesis. 松柏苷在木质素生物合成中起着至关重要的作用。
