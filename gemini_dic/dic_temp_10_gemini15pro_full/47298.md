de-ices:/diːˈaɪsɪz/
verb
除冰
removes ice from something
The airport crew de-ices the planes before takeoff. 机场工作人员在飞机起飞前进行除冰。
The pilot de-iced the wings of the aircraft. 飞行员给飞机的机翼除冰。
Synonym: thaw, defrost
Antonym: freeze, ice up
词性：动词

de-icer:/diːˈaɪsər/
noun
除冰器，除冰剂
a substance or device used to remove ice
We used a de-icer spray on the car windshield. 我们在汽车挡风玻璃上使用了除冰喷雾。
The airport uses a special de-icer fluid on the runway. 机场在跑道上使用特殊的除冰液。
Synonym: antifreeze, thawing agent
Antonym: freezing agent
词性：名词

词源：
From de- + ice,  "ice" comes from Old English *īs (attested only in compounds), from Proto-Germanic *isaz. "de-" is a prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse".
