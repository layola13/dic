/nəʊt/ (British) /noʊt/ (American)| n. vt.|笔记，记录；注释；便条；音符；钞票；v. 注意到；记录|A brief record of something written down to assist the memory or for future reference. |Please make a note of the meeting time. 请记下会议时间。|Synonyms: memo, record, annotation, observation, remark |Antonyms: ignore, disregard|noun
/nəʊt/ (British) /noʊt/ (American)| vt.|注意到；记录|To observe carefully; to record in writing. |He noted her careful attention to detail. 他注意到她对细节的认真关注。|Synonyms: observe, perceive, remark, record |Antonyms: ignore, overlook|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "note" (noun), "noter" (verb), from Latin "nota"  "mark, sign, character," originally a shorthand mark for a word in writing.
