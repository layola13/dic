Baybars: /ˈbeɪbɑːrz/ | n. | 巴伊巴尔斯 |  A Mamluk sultan of Egypt (reigned 1260–77). He reorganized the Egyptian army and administration and led the Muslims to victory against the Mongols and the Crusaders. | Baybars is considered one of the greatest medieval Muslim rulers. 巴伊巴尔斯被认为是中世纪最伟大的穆斯林统治者之一。| synonyms: Sultan, ruler | antonyms: subject, servant | noun


Etymology: From Arabic  بَيْبَرْس (baybars), possibly meaning "lord panther".
