padge:/pædʒ/
n.|垫子; 衬垫; 填充物|A padge is a soft mass of material used for padding or stuffing something.|The chair seat was filled with a thick padge. 椅子座位上填满了厚厚的衬垫。|近义词: padding, stuffing, cushion, wadding|反义词: None|词性:名词

Etymology:
Likely a variant or dialectal form of "padding," from the verb "pad" (meaning to stuff or fill with soft material).  The verb "pad" has its roots in the Middle Dutch word "padde," meaning "toad" or "frog," likely referring to the soft, padded bodies of these animals.
