agonizing:/ˈæɡənaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 极度痛苦的，折磨人的 | Causing extreme physical or mental suffering. |  She described the agonizing pain in her back. 她描述了她背部极度的疼痛。

The waiting for the results was agonizing. 等待结果的过程十分煎熬。

近义词: excruciating, tormenting, harrowing, painful | 反义词: pleasant, soothing, comforting | 词性: adjective

词源: From the verb "agonize," from Late Latin *agonizare, from Greek agonizesthai "to contend for a prize, struggle, suffer," from agon "contest, struggle."
