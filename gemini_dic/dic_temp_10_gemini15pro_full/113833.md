maddingly:/ˈmædɪŋli/| adv.|令人发狂地，极度地|In a manner that causes extreme annoyance or frustration.  Derived from "mad" (insane) + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverb suffix).|The traffic was moving maddingly slowly. 交通慢得令人发狂。|Synonyms: infuriatingly, exasperatingly, frustratingly|Antonyms: calmly, soothingly|Adverb
