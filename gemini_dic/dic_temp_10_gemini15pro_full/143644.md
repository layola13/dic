outkill:/ˌaʊtˈkɪl/
verb
超过…的杀伤量；比…杀伤更多
To kill a greater number of (something) than someone or something else.
The army outkilled the enemy by a ratio of two to one. 这支军队对敌人的杀伤力是二比一。
Synonyms: surpass in killing, exceed in killing, outdo in killing
Antonyms: be outkilled by, be surpassed in killing by
词性: 动词
