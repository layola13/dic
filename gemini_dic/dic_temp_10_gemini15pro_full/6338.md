amperages:/ˈæmpərɪdʒɪz/
n.
电流强度；安培数
The strength of an electric current expressed in amperes.
The amperages of the circuits in this building are too low for our equipment.  这栋楼里电路的电流强度对于我们的设备来说太低了。
