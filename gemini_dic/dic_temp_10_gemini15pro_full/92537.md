hypsometrical:/ˌhɪpsəˈmɛtrɪk(ə)l/
adjective
测高的，高度测量的
relating to the measurement of altitude
例句：Hypsometrical curves show the distribution of land area according to elevation.  测高曲线显示了土地面积随海拔高度的分布。

例句：The hypsometrical study revealed significant variations in the terrain.  测高研究揭示了地形上的显著变化。

近义词：altimetric, altitudinal
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *hypsos* (height) + *metron* (measure)
