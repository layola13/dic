overluxuriously: /ˌoʊvərˌlʌɡˈʒʊəriəsli/
adverb
过度奢华地，极尽奢华地
in an extremely luxurious manner; excessively luxuriously
He lived overluxuriously, spending vast sums on frivolous items.  他生活极其奢华，在琐碎的东西上花费巨额资金。
She furnished her apartment overluxuriously, with silk drapes and gold-plated fixtures. 她把她的公寓布置得极其奢华，用丝绸窗帘和镀金的固定装置。
近义词: extravagantly, lavishly, sumptuously
反义词: simply, modestly, frugally
词性: adverb

词源:
From over- + luxuriously.  Over-  表示"过度", luxuriously 源自 luxury (奢华) + -ly (副词后缀).
