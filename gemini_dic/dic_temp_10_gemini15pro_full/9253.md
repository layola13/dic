antipatriarch:/ˌæntiˈpeɪtriɑːrk/ | adj. n. | 反父权制的，反父权制者 | Opposed to or challenging patriarchy, a social system in which men hold primary power.  |  例句：The antipatriarchal movement seeks to dismantle systems of male dominance. 反父权运动旨在瓦解男性主导的体系。

例句：She identified as an antipatriarch. 她自认为是一个反父权制者。 | 近义词：feminist, anti-sexist | 反义词：patriarchal, sexist | 词性：形容词，名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + patriarch.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to." "Patriarch" comes from Greek "patriarkhēs" meaning "father of a family, clan, or tribe; founder, chief."
