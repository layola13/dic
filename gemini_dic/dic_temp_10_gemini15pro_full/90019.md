hosanna:/hoʊˈzænə/ /hɒˈzænə/| n. int.|和散那（表示赞美、崇拜的欢呼）|An exclamation of praise and adoration, especially in Christian worship.  It is used to express adoration, praise, or joy, originally in reference to the triumphal entry of Jesus into Jerusalem. |The crowd shouted "Hosanna!" as Jesus rode into Jerusalem.  群众在耶稣骑马进入耶路撒冷时高呼“和散那！”| The choir sang hosannas.  唱诗班唱着赞美诗。 | synonyms: praise, acclamation, adoration, laudation | antonyms: condemnation, criticism, denunciation| noun, interjection
