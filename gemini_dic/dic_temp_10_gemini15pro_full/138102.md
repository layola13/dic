normocalcaemic:/ˌnɔːrməʊkælˈsiːmɪk/
| adj. | 血钙正常的 | Having normal levels of calcium in the blood. |  The patient presented with normocalcaemic hyperparathyroidism.  这位病人表现出血钙正常的甲状旁腺功能亢进。 |
|例句：
Her blood tests showed she was normocalcaemic. 她的血液检查显示她的血钙正常。

The patient is currently normocalcaemic and requires no further treatment. 该患者目前血钙正常，不需要进一步治疗。
|近义词：eucalcemic|反义词：hypercalcemic, hypocalcemic|词性:形容词

词源:
normo- (normal, standard) + calc- (calcium) + -emic (relating to the blood)
