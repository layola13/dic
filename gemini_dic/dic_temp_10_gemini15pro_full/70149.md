fiar:/ˈfɪər/ /ˈfaɪər/ | n. | 集市，庙会；义卖会 | A gathering of stalls and amusements for public entertainment. |  The county fiar is always a popular event. 县里的庙会一直很受欢迎。| fair, carnival, festival, bazaar | market | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *feire, from Latin feria "holiday, festival day," plural feriae "holidays," from ferire "to strike, hit," related to festus "festive." Originally, fairs were held on holidays.
