deepest:/ˈdiːpɪst/| adj.|最深的，最深处的|At the deepest point of the ocean, the pressure is immense.  在海洋的最深处，压力是巨大的。|例句：The deepest part of the ocean remains largely unexplored. 海洋最深处很大程度上仍未被探索。|近义词：profoundest, furthest, bottommost|反义词：shallowest, highest, surface|词性:形容词
词源：From Old English *dēopest, superlative of dēop (“deep”). Cognate with West Frisian djipst, Dutch diepst, German tiefst.
