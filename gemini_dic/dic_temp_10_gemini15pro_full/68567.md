fangor:/ˈfæŋɡɔːr/
n.
方戈尔（波兰画家）
Wojciech Fangor (1922-2015) was a Polish painter, sculptor, and graphic artist.
Wojciech Fangor (1922-2015) 是一位波兰画家、雕塑家和平面艺术家。
His works are often characterized by vibrant colors and abstract forms.
他的作品通常以鲜艳的色彩和抽象的形式为特征。
