lipothymy: /ˌlaɪˈpɒθɪmi/ (UK) /ˌlɪˈpɑːθɪmi/ (US)| n. |昏厥，晕厥|A sudden faintness or lightheadedness.|

例句：
The patient experienced a brief episode of lipothymy.
病人经历了一阵短暂的晕厥。

She felt a wave of lipothymy come over her.
她感到一阵晕厥袭来。


近义词：fainting, syncope, lightheadedness|
反义词: consciousness, alertness|
词性: noun


Etymology:
From French *lipothymie*, from Ancient Greek λειποθυμία (leipothymía, “swooning, fainting”), from λείπειν (leípein, “to leave, quit”) + θυμός (thymós, “spirit, soul, breath, life”).
