incites: /ɪnˈsaɪts/ | v. | 煽动，激起 | encourages or stirs up (violent or unlawful behavior) | The politician's speech incited the crowd to riot. 这位政治家的演讲煽动了群众暴乱。 |  例句：The article incited a lot of anger among readers. 这篇文章激起了读者们的愤怒。

例句：His words incited them to violence. 他的话煽动他们使用暴力。

近义词：provoke, instigate, stir up, agitate, foment | 反义词：calm, soothe, pacify, quell, suppress | 词性：动词
