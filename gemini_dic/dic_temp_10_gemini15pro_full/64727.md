|n.|回避，规避；戒绝|The monk's life is marked by eschewals of worldly pleasures.  僧侣的生活特点是戒绝世俗的享乐。|The company's eschewals of certain investment opportunities proved to be wise. 公司对某些投资机会的回避被证明是明智的。|近义词：avoidance, abstaining, shunning, refraining|反义词：embracing, indulging, acceptance|词性:名词

eschewal:/ɪsˈtʃuːəl/
|n.|回避，规避；戒绝|His eschewal of alcohol stemmed from a troubled past. 他对酒精的戒绝源于一段不堪回首的过去。|Her eschewal of social media allows her more time for reading. 她对社交媒体的回避让她有更多时间阅读。|近义词：avoidance, rejection, abstinence|反义词：acceptance, embrace, indulgence|词性:名词

词源:  From Middle English *escheuen, from Old French eschiver, from Frankish *skiuhan (“to shun, avoid”), from Proto-Germanic *skeuhaną (“to shy away, avoid”). Cognate with Dutch schuwen (“to shun”), German scheuen (“to shy away from, be afraid of”), Scots eschewe (“to eschew”).
