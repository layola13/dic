half-roasted:/ˌhæf ˈroʊstɪd/ | adj. | 半烤的，半熟的 | Partly roasted; not fully cooked through roasting. | The chicken was half-roasted, so the inside was still pink.  这只鸡烤得半生不熟，里面还是粉红色的。| The potatoes were half-roasted, leaving them slightly firm. 土豆烤得半熟，吃起来还有点硬。| Near synonyms:  partially roasted, undercooked, par-roasted | Antonyms: fully roasted, well-done, overcooked | adjective
