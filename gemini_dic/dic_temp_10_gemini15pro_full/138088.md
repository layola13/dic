normanesque:/ˌnɔːrmənˈesk/
adjective
诺曼式的；具有诺曼风格的
Relating to or resembling the style of architecture, art, or literature prevalent in Normandy and England under Norman rule, characterized by rounded arches, massive walls, and geometric ornamentation.
The church's normanesque architecture, with its thick walls and rounded arches, spoke of an earlier time.  这座教堂的诺曼式建筑，以其厚厚的墙壁和圆拱，诉说着一个更早的时代。
Durham Cathedral is a magnificent example of Normanesque architecture.  达勒姆大教堂是诺曼式建筑的宏伟典范。
Synonyms: Romanesque, Norman
Antonyms: Gothic, Modern, Contemporary
词性: adjective
