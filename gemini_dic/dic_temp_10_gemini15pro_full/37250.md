comic:/ˈkɒmɪk/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmɪk/ (US)| n.|连环画，漫画|A comic is a magazine or book that contains a set of stories told in pictures.|例句：I used to read comics every week as a child. 我小时候每周都看漫画书。|近义词：comic book, comic strip, graphic novel|反义词：None|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the Greek word "komikos" meaning "of or pertaining to comedy," from "komos," meaning "revel, merrymaking."
