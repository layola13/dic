countersense:/ˈkaʊntɚsɛns/| n.|反义词，反面意思，对立面|An idea that is the opposite of another idea, often used in philosophy.  A meaning that is the reverse of what is expected or intended.|The philosopher explored the countersense of the original argument.|近义词:opposite, antithesis, contrary, converse|反义词:sense, meaning|名词
