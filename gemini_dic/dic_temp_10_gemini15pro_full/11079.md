archaic:/ɑːrˈkeɪɪk/ | adj. | 古老的，过时的 | Very old or old-fashioned. No longer in everyday use but sometimes used to impart an old-fashioned flavour.  |  Example: "Thou art" is an archaic form of "you are".  “Thou art”是“you are”的古体。

Example:  The company's archaic computer system needs an upgrade.  这家公司的古老计算机系统需要升级。| 近义词: obsolete, antiquated, ancient, outdated, old-fashioned | 反义词: modern, contemporary, current, new | 词性: adjective
