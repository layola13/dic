overexquisite:/ˌoʊvərˈɛkskwɪzɪt/| adj.| 过于精致的，过分精美的| excessively refined or elaborate; too delicate or subtle | The overexquisite decorations made the room feel stuffy.  过于精致的装饰让房间感觉很闷。| The cake decorations were overexquisite, bordering on gaudy. 蛋糕装饰过于精致，近乎俗丽。 | 近义词：overrefined, overelaborate, too delicate, fussy | 反义词：simple, plain, austere, understated | 词性: adjective

词源:
"over-" (prefix) + "exquisite" (adjective)

"over-" comes from Old English "ofer", meaning "above, beyond, across".

"exquisite" comes from Latin "exquisitus", past participle of "exquirere", meaning "to search out, seek diligently".
