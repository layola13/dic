desolatenesses:/ˌdɛsələtnəsɪz/
n.
荒凉，凄凉，孤寂
The state of being desolate; bleakness and emptiness.
The desolatenesses of the abandoned town were striking.  被遗弃的城镇一片荒凉，景象令人触目惊心。
The photograph captured the utter desolatenesses of the Arctic landscape.  这张照片捕捉到了北极景观的极度荒凉。
近义词: bleakness, barrenness, desolation, loneliness, emptiness
反义词: vibrancy, liveliness, cheerfulness, exuberance, fertility
词性: noun (plural)

desolate:/ˈdɛsəlɪt/
adj.
荒凉的，凄凉的
(of a place) deserted of people and in a state of bleak and dismal emptiness.
a desolate landscape  一片荒凉的景象
She felt desolate and alone.  她感到孤独和凄凉。
近义词: barren, bleak, deserted, empty, lonely
反义词: populous, cheerful, lively, fertile, vibrant
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *dēsōlātus* (past participle of *dēsōlāre* "to abandon, forsake"), from *dē-* "from, away" + *sōlāre* "to make alone," from *sōlus* "alone."
