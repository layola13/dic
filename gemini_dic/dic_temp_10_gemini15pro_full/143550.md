outhastens:/ˌaʊtˈheɪsənz/
verb
超过…的速度；行动比…快
To go faster than or act more quickly than someone or something else.
The gazelle outhastened the lion. 瞪羚比狮子跑得快。
He outhastened his rivals to the finish line. 他比他的对手更快到达终点线。
近义词: outpace, outstrip, surpass
反义词: lag behind, trail
