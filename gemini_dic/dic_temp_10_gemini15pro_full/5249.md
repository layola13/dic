allochroous:/əˈlɒkrəs/
adjective
异色的，变色的
Having various colors; changeable in color.  Relating to or exhibiting allochroism.
The allochroous mineral displayed a fascinating array of colors under ultraviolet light. 在紫外线下，这种异色矿物呈现出一系列迷人的颜色。
Synonyms: multicolored, polychromatic, variegated, iridescent
Antonyms: monochromatic, unicolor, homochromatic
词性: adjective
