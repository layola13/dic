oxygenous:/ɒkˈsɪdʒɪnəs/
adjective
含氧的; 产生氧气的
Containing or generating oxygen.
例句：Oxygenous photosynthesis is the most common type of photosynthesis. 含氧光合作用是最常见的光合作用类型。

例句：The oxygenous atmosphere of Earth allows for complex life to thrive. 地球的含氧大气允许复杂生命繁衍。
近义词：aerobic
反义词：anaerobic
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ὀξύς (oxús, “sharp, acid”) + γενής (genḗs, “producing, generating”).  The term reflects the early belief that oxygen was essential for the formation of acids.
