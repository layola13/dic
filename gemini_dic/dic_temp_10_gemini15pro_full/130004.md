non-normans:/nɒnˈnɔːrmənz/ | n. | 非诺曼人 | People who were not Normans, typically referring to those living in England before or after the Norman Conquest of 1066.  Those who were not of Norman descent. |  After the Norman Conquest, non-Normans faced significant social and political disadvantages. 诺曼征服后，非诺曼人面临着重大的社会和政治劣势。

The non-Normans continued to speak their own languages and maintain their own customs. 非诺曼人继续说着他们自己的语言，保持着他们自己的习俗。 | 近义词: Anglo-Saxons, English (in the historical context) | 反义词: Normans | 词性: 名词
