brinish:/ˈbraɪnɪʃ/| adj.|咸的，略带咸味的|Somewhat salty.|The air was brinish near the sea. 海边的空气略带咸味。|近义词：salty, brackish|反义词:fresh, sweet|词性:形容词
词源:From brine + -ish.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *brunjoną (“to burn”).
