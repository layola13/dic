importless:/ɪmˈpɔːrtlɪs/| adj.|无进口的，不进口的|Not importing or not involving imports.  Referring to a country, economy, or situation where goods are not brought in from other countries.|例句：The country aimed to become importless in certain sectors to boost domestic production.  这个国家旨在某些领域实现无进口，以促进国内生产。

例句：Their importless business model focuses on locally sourced materials.  他们不进口的商业模式专注于本地采购的材料。|近义词：self-sufficient, autarkic, independent|反义词：import-dependent, reliant on imports|词性:adjective
