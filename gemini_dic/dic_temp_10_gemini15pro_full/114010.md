magmas:/ˈmæɡməz/| n. |岩浆（magma的复数形式）|Molten rock material within the Earth's crust, from which igneous rock is formed by cooling. |The geologist studied the magmas from the recent volcanic eruption.  地质学家研究了最近火山喷发出的岩浆。|近义词：lava, molten rock|反义词:solid rock|Noun
