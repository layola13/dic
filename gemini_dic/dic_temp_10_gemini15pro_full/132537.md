nondevious:/ˌnɑːnˈdiːviəs/
adjective
正直的，坦率的
straightforward; not shifty or crooked
Her nondevious approach to the problem was refreshing. 她坦率地处理这个问题的方式令人耳目一新。
synonyms: straightforward, honest, direct, frank, open, candid, forthright, artless, guileless, ingenuous, innocent
antonyms: devious, deceitful, dishonest, indirect, shifty, crooked
词性: adjective
