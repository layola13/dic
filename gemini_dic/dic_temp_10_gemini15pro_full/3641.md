agents:/ˈeɪdʒənts/| n.|代理人，代理商，特工|A person who acts on behalf of another person or group.|The travel agents booked our flights and hotels. 旅行社帮我们预订了航班和酒店。|synonyms: representatives, deputies, brokers|antonyms: principals, clients|noun


agents:/ˈeɪdʒənts/| n.|作用物，药剂|A substance that produces a particular effect or change.|Cleaning agents can remove stubborn stains. 清洁剂可以去除顽固污渍。|synonyms: substances, chemicals, reagents|antonyms: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *agent* (noun), from Old French *agent*, from Latin *agentem* (nominative *agēns*), present participle of *agō* (“I do, act, drive, lead”).
