flosser:/ˈflɑːsər/ (US), /ˈflɒsə(r)/ (UK)| n.|牙线棒，用牙线的人|A person or device that uses dental floss to clean between teeth.|例句：She uses a flosser every day to keep her teeth clean. 她每天都用牙线棒保持牙齿清洁。

例句：He's a diligent flosser and rarely gets cavities. 他是一个勤于使用牙线的人，很少蛀牙。|近义词：interdental cleaner, dental floss user|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps someone who neglects oral hygiene)|词性:noun
