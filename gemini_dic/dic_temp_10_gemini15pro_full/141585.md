oppressiveness:/əˈprɛsɪvnəs/
|n.|压迫感，沉闷感|The oppressiveness of the humid heat was stifling.  潮湿的热气令人窒息。|
|近义词：tyranny, repression, severity, harshness|
|反义词：freedom, liberty, lightness, relief|
|词性：名词|
|词源：From oppress + -ive + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin *ob- "against" + premere "to press".|
