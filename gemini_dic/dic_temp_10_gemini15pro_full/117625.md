megaseme:/ˈmɛɡəsiːm/| n. |超级语义/巨义|A unit of meaning larger than a single word, often encompassing a complex concept or cultural idea.  It is a theoretical construct used in some fields of linguistics and anthropology to describe how words and concepts relate to broader cultural understandings.|The concept of "honor" could be considered a megaseme, encompassing a range of related ideas and behaviors.|近义词: concept, idea, notion|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
