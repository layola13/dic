hesperidin:/ˌhɛspəˈrɪdɪn/ | n. | 芸香苷，橙皮苷 | A flavanone glycoside found in citrus fruits, especially oranges and grapefruit. It is used as a dietary supplement and may have antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties. |  Oranges are a good source of hesperidin. 橙子是芸香苷的良好来源。|  Hesperidin supplements are available in health food stores.  芸香苷补充剂可以在保健食品商店买到。 | synonyms: flavanone glycoside, citrus bioflavonoid | antonyms: N/A | noun
