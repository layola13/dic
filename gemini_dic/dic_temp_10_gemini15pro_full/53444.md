disappreciate:/ˌdɪsəˈpriːʃiˌeɪt/
verb
轻视，低估，不赏识
To fail to appreciate; undervalue or underestimate.
He felt disappreciated by his boss, who never acknowledged his hard work. 他觉得老板不重视他，从不认可他的辛勤工作。
近义词: undervalue, underestimate, belittle, disparage, slight
反义词: appreciate, value, esteem, admire
词性: verb
