haruspice: /ˈhærəspaɪs/ | n. | 占卜者；预言家 | A haruspice was a Roman priest who practiced a form of divination called haruspicy, which involved examining the entrails of sacrificed animals to interpret omens. |  The haruspice examined the liver of the sacrificed ox. 占卜者检查了献祭的牛的肝脏。 |  soothsayer, diviner, prophet, oracle |  - | noun
