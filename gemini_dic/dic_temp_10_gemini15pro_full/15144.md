back-burner:/ˈbækˌbɜːrnər/ |n.|次要位置，搁置，暂缓处理|Something that is on the back burner is temporarily put aside, typically because it is not urgent or important.|The project was put on the back burner due to lack of funding.  这个项目由于缺乏资金而被搁置了。|synonyms:  back shelf, pending, deferred, tabled|antonyms:  priority, forefront, immediate, urgent|noun

back-burner:/ˈbækˌbɜːrnər/ |adj.|次要的，搁置的|Relating to something that is temporarily put aside, typically because it is not urgent or important.|He has a few back-burner projects he works on in his spare time. 他有一些在业余时间做的次要项目。|synonyms: secondary, subordinate, non-essential|antonyms: primary, main, essential, crucial|adjective
