chilli:/ˈtʃɪli/ (UK) /ˈtʃɪli/ (US)| n.|辣椒; 辣椒粉|A small hot-tasting pod of a variety of capsicum, used in cooking. Also called chili pepper. |Example 1: I like to add chilli to my curry. 我喜欢在我的咖喱里加辣椒。

Example 2:  She sprinkled some chilli powder on her tacos.  她在她的玉米饼上撒了些辣椒粉。|Synonym: chili pepper, hot pepper, cayenne pepper|Antonym: mild pepper, bell pepper|noun


chilli:/ˈtʃɪli/ (UK) /ˈtʃɪli/ (US)| adj.|辣的; 加了辣椒的|Containing or flavored with chillies. |Example 1: This chilli sauce is very hot.  这辣椒酱很辣。

Example 2: We had a chilli con carne for dinner. 我们晚饭吃了辣肉酱。|Synonym: spicy, hot, peppery|Antonym: mild, bland|adjective
