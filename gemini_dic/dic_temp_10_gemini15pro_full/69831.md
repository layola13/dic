ferret:/ˈferɪt/ (US), /ˈferɪt/ (UK)| v. |搜寻; 探索; 侦查; 刺探|To search tenaciously for something; to hunt with ferrets.|She ferreted around in her handbag for her keys. 她在手提包里搜寻钥匙。|近义词：search, rummage, hunt, explore|反义词: conceal, hide|Verb


ETYMOLOGY->

From Old French *furet "ferret" (Modern French furet), diminutive of furon "thief," from Vulgar Latin *furonem, accusative of *furo, from Latin fur "thief."  The animal was named for its thieving habits.  The verb sense developed from the animal's hunting behavior.
