nonenemies:/nɒnˈɛnɪmiz/ | n. | 非敌人; 既非敌人也非朋友的人 | People who are not enemies but also not friends.  Those in a neutral or ambivalent relationship. |  The two countries are nonenemies, maintaining a cautious but not hostile relationship. 这两个国家是非敌对关系，保持着谨慎但并非敌对的关系。

The conference brought together business leaders and nonenemies from various sectors. 这次会议汇集了来自各行各业的商界领袖和非敌对人士。 | 近义词: neutrals, non-adversaries | 反义词: enemies, foes | 词性: 名词

词源:  "Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "enemies" (plural of "enemy").  A relatively recent coinage, likely arising in the late 20th or early 21st century to describe the complex relationships of the modern world.  非- (表示“不”的前缀) + 敌人（enemy 的复数）。一个相对较新的词汇，可能出现在 20 世纪末或 21 世纪初，用于描述现代世界的复杂关系。
