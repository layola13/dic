breede:/briːd/| v.|繁殖，饲养，产生，教养|To produce (offspring); procreate. To raise (animals) by breeding. To cause or give rise to.|例句：The farmer breeds cattle for beef. 农民饲养牛以获取牛肉。

例句：Poverty breeds crime. 贫穷滋生犯罪。|近义词：procreate, generate, raise, rear, produce|反义词:extinguish, destroy|词性:动词
