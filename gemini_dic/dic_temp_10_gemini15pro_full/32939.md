ETYMOLOGY->
From "chop" (meaning short, irregular waves) + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns of state or quality).  "Chop" itself likely originates from a Low German or Dutch word related to "choppen" (to chop or hack), referring to the appearance of the water's surface.
