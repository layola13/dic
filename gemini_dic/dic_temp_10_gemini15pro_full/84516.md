hamate: /ˈheɪmeɪt/ (US), /ˈheɪmət/ (UK)| adj. n.|钩状的，钩骨|A bone in the wrist, specifically the hamate bone, which is distinguished by its hook-like projection.  It can also mean shaped like a hook.|The hamate bone is easily fractured during a fall. 钩骨在摔倒时很容易骨折。|近义词:hooked, uncinate|反义词:straight|词性:adjective, noun
