moderatenesses:/ˈmɒdərɪtnɪsɪz/ /ˈmɑːdərɪtnɪsɪz/
n.
适度；温和；节制
The quality of being moderate; avoidance of extremes.
Her moderateness in eating helped her maintain a healthy weight.  她的饮食节制帮助她保持了健康的体重。
moderation, temperance, restraint
extremism, immoderation, excess
noun
