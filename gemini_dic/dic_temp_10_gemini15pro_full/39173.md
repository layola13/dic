congealedness:/kənˈdʒiːldnɪs/
n.
凝固，凝结
The state of being congealed; thickness or solidity.
The congealedness of the gravy made it difficult to pour. 肉汁太稠了，难以倒出来。
solidification, coagulation, thickness
fluidity, liquidity
