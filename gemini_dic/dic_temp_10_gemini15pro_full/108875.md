lection:/ˈlɛkʃən/| n.|阅读；选读；读物；（尤指宗教的）训诫，教训|A reading, especially of a religious nature, or a selection of readings.  A lection can also refer to a selection of text chosen to be read aloud, particularly in a church service.|例句：The lection for today's service is from the Book of Psalms. 今天的礼拜仪式选读的是诗篇。

例句：He chose a lection from Shakespeare for his audition. 他选择了一段莎士比亚的作品来试镜。|近义词：reading, excerpt, passage, selection, extract|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps omission)|词性:noun
