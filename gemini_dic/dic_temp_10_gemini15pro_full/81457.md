gratitude:/ˈɡrætɪtjuːd/ (US), /ˈɡrætɪtʃuːd/ (UK)| n.|感激，感谢|The feeling of being grateful; thankfulness.|She expressed her gratitude with a heartfelt letter. 她用一封真诚的信表达了她的感激之情。|近义词：thankfulness, appreciation, gratefulness|反义词：ingratitude, resentment, thanklessness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *gratitud*, from Old French gratitude, from Latin grātitūdō (“thankfulness, gratefulness”), from grātus (“pleasing, agreeable, thankful”), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ǵwerH- (“to favour, to commend”).
