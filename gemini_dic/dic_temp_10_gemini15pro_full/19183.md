bewitch:/bɪˈwɪtʃ/| vt.|使着迷，迷惑，蛊惑|To cast a spell on; to enchant and fascinate someone completely.|She was bewitched by his charm. 她被他的人格魅力迷住了。|Synonyms: enchant, fascinate, captivate, charm, mesmerize|Antonyms: repel, disgust, disenchant|Verb

bewit:/bɪˈwɪt/| vt.|(古语) 欺骗，愚弄|To trick, deceive, or bewilder.  This word is now largely archaic.|He bewitted his opponents with a clever ruse. 他用巧妙的计策愚弄了他的对手。|Synonyms: deceive, trick, dupe, hoodwink, outwit|Antonyms: enlighten, inform, guide|Verb
