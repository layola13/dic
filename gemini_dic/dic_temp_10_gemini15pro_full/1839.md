acrolect: /ˈækrəʊlɛkt/
noun
高层方言；标准方言
The most prestigious dialect or variety of a language, often associated with education and formality.
The acrolect is often used in formal settings and by educated speakers.  高层方言常用于正式场合和受过教育的人。

The newsreader spoke in a clear acrolect.  新闻播音员用清晰的标准方言播报。

近义词: standard language, standard dialect
