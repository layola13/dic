heyman:/ˈheɪmæn/| n.|伙计，哥们，老兄|(often used as a friendly greeting or form of address between men)  A term of address used between men, often expressing camaraderie or solidarity.|例句：Heyman, what's up? 伙计，怎么了？

例句：Heyman, wanna grab a beer? 哥们，想喝杯啤酒吗？|近义词：dude, buddy, pal, fella, man|反义词: (No direct antonym, but could be considered informal and therefore inappropriate in formal settings)|noun
