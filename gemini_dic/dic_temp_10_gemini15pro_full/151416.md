parochialize:/pəˈroʊkiəlaɪz/
verb
使狭隘；使地方化；使像教区
to make or become narrow or restricted in scope or outlook; to give a parochial character to
The media's focus on local issues has parochialized the national debate. 媒体对地方问题的关注使得全国性的辩论变得狭隘了。
The school's curriculum has been parochialized over the years.  多年来，学校的课程变得越来越狭隘。
近义词: narrow, restrict, limit, confine
反义词: broaden, expand, widen, enlarge
词性: verb

parochial:/pəˈroʊkiəl/
adjective
狭隘的；地方性的；教区的
relating to or supported by a parish; limited in scope or outlook; narrow or provincial
He holds some rather parochial views. 他持有一些相当狭隘的观点。
The school has a parochial outlook. 这所学校的视野很狭隘。
近义词: narrow-minded, provincial, insular, local
反义词: cosmopolitan, broad-minded, liberal, universal
词性: adjective
