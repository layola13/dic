fabricate:/ˈfæbrɪkeɪt/ (US) /ˈfæbrɪkeɪt/ (UK)| v. | 制造；捏造；组装 | To construct or manufacture (something, especially an industrial product) from prepared components; to invent or concoct (something), typically with deceitful intent. |例句：The company fabricates steel components for bridges. 这家公司制造桥梁用的钢构件。

例句：He fabricated a story about being robbed. 他编造了一个关于被抢劫的故事。|近义词：manufacture, construct, build, invent, forge, devise, concoct |反义词：dismantle, demolish, destroy|词性:动词

fabrication:/ˌfæbrɪˈkeɪʃn/ (US) /ˌfæbrɪˈkeɪʃn/ (UK)| n. | 制造；捏造；伪造物 | The action or process of manufacturing or inventing something. |例句：The fabrication of the aircraft took several years. 这架飞机的制造花了数年时间。

例句：His claim was a complete fabrication. 他的说法纯属捏造。|近义词：manufacturing, construction, production, invention, falsehood, lie |反义词：destruction, demolition|词性:名词
