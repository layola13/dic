fop:/fɑːp/ | n. | 花花公子，纨绔子弟 | A man who is devoted to or vain about his appearance and clothes. | He was a notorious fop, always preening himself in front of the mirror. 他是个臭名昭著的花花公子，总是在镜子前精心打扮自己。 |  近义词：dandy, coxcomb | 反义词：sloven | 词性: 名词

fops:/fɑːps/ | n. | 花花公子们，纨绔子弟们 | Plural form of "fop". | The fops of the court were more interested in fashion than politics. 宫廷里的花花公子们对时尚比对政治更感兴趣。 | 近义词：dandies, coxcombs | 反义词：slovens | 词性: 名词
