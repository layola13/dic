counter-clockwise:/ˈkaʊntərˈklɒkwaɪz/ (UK) /ˈkaʊntərˈklɑːkwaɪz/ (US)| n.|逆时针方向|The direction opposite to the rotation of the hands of a clock.|The carousel rotated in a counter-clockwise direction. 旋转木马逆时针旋转。|Synonyms: anticlockwise (UK), leftward rotation|Antonyms: clockwise, rightward rotation|Noun
