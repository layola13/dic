epiphyllospermous:/ˌɛpɪˈfɪləˌspɜːrməs/
adjective
叶上生种子的；叶表生种子的
Bearing seeds on the leaves, as certain ferns.
Epiphyllospermous ferns reproduce by developing spores on the surface of their leaves. 叶上生种子的蕨类植物通过在其叶片表面发育孢子来繁殖。
Some species of ferns are epiphyllospermous.  某些蕨类植物是叶上生种子的。
近义词: foliar
反义词: (None readily available.  The opposite condition would be having seeds develop elsewhere on the plant, which is typical for most seed-bearing plants. A specific term for this isn't widely used.)
词性: adjective
