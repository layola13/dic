loir:/lwɑːr/ (French pronunciation)| n.|睡鼠|A small, nocturnal, arboreal rodent with large eyes and a bushy tail, native to Europe and Asia. It hibernates for long periods.|The loir is a close relative of the dormouse.|近义词：dormouse|反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps "diurnal animal" as a contrast to its nocturnal nature)|noun
