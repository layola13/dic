flankerback:/ˈflæŋkərbæk/| n.|侧翼后卫，边后卫|A flankerback in rugby is a position in the backline, typically responsible for covering the sides of the field and supporting the wings.  在橄榄球中，侧翼后卫是后防线上的一个位置，通常负责覆盖球场的两侧并支援边锋。|例句：The flankerback made a crucial tackle to prevent a try.  这位侧翼后卫完成了一次关键的擒抱，阻止了对方达阵。

例句：The flankerback is known for his speed and agility.  这位侧翼后卫以速度和敏捷著称。|近义词：wing, three-quarter, fullback|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps prop or lock as they play in the forwards) |词性: noun
