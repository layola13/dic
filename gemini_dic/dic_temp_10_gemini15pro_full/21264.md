blooded:/ˈblʌdɪd/| adj.|血统的，血腥的，冷血的|Having a specified kind of blood or temperament; involving bloodshed.|例句：He committed a cold-blooded murder. 他犯下了一起冷血的谋杀案。|近义词：bloody, gory, ruthless|反义词:merciful, compassionate|词性:形容词
Etymology: From blood + -ed.  The suffix "-ed" forms adjectives from nouns, indicating "having, characterized by, or provided with."
