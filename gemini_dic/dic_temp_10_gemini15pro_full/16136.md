bangs:/bæŋz/
noun
刘海
An area of hair cut short and straight that covers the forehead.
Her bangs were cut straight across her forehead. 她的刘海齐齐地剪在额头上。
fringe, forelock
---
ETYMOLOGY->
From the earlier form "bang" meaning "a sudden loud noise," perhaps from Scandinavian sources, later extended to mean a short, abrupt haircut.
