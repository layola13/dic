hemiholohedral:/ˌhɛmihɒləˈhiːdrəl/
|adj.|半全面体的|A crystal form exhibiting half the faces of the holohedral form within the same crystal system.|Crystals of chalcopyrite are hemiholohedral. 黄铜矿晶体是半全面体的。|Near synonyms: merohedral|Antonym: holohedral|adjective
