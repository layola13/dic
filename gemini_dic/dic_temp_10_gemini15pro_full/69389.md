feedbacking:/ˈfiːdbækɪŋ/
v.
提供反馈，反馈信息
The process of providing feedback.  This often involves giving someone information about their performance or behavior so they can improve.
We are feedbacking on the new proposals. 我们正在就新的提案提供反馈。
近义词: responding, reacting, reviewing, commenting
反义词: ignoring, disregarding
词性: 动词
