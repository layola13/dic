monarch:/ˈmɒnək/ (UK), /ˈmɑːnɑːrk/ (US)| n.| 君主，帝王 |A sovereign head of state, especially a king, queen, or emperor. | Queen Elizabeth II was a monarch for over 70 years.  伊丽莎白二世女王是一位在位超过 70 年的君主。

| 近义词: sovereign, ruler, king, queen, emperor | 反义词: subject, commoner | 词性: noun


monarch:/ˈmɒnək/ (UK), /ˈmɑːnɑːrk/ (US)| adj.(rare) | 至高无上的，主要的 |Supreme or dominant. (This usage is less common.) | The monarch butterfly is a monarch of the insect world.  帝王蝶是昆虫世界的霸主。(这个用法比较少见) | 近义词: supreme, dominant, principal | 反义词: subordinate, minor | 词性: adjective (rare)
