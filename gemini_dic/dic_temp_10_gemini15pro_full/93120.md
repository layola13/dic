iffy:/ˈɪfi/| adj.|不确定的，可疑的，不稳定的|Subject to conditions; uncertain; doubtful.  Dependent on something else happening.|The plan is a bit iffy at the moment, as we don't have all the funding yet.  这个计划目前还有点不确定，因为我们还没有拿到全部资金。|近义词：uncertain, doubtful, precarious, questionable, risky|反义词：certain, definite, sure, stable, reliable|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From if + -y (forming adjectives meaning 'characterized by').  Ultimately from Old English gif.
