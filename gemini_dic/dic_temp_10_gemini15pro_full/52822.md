dilutenesses:/daɪˈluːtɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
稀薄；淡薄；稀释度
The state of being diluted; weakness or thinness of consistency.
The dilutenesses of the paint made it difficult to apply evenly.  油漆的稀薄使得它难以均匀涂抹。
近义词:thinness, weakness, rarity
反义词:thickness, strength, concentration
词性:名词
