frontages:/ˈfrʌntɪdʒɪz/
n.
正面；临街面；沿街地带
The length of a property's boundary that faces a street or road.  The property has extensive frontages onto both streets.
The hotel has a frontage of 100 meters on the beach.  这家酒店在海滩上有100米的正面。
facades, fronts
backs, rears
noun
