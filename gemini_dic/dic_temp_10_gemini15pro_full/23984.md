breathalyser:/ˈbrɛθəlaɪzər/ (US) /ˈbrɛθəlaɪzə/ (UK)| n.|呼气酒精测试仪|A device used by police for measuring the amount of alcohol in a driver's breath.|The police officer asked him to blow into the breathalyser. 警察让他吹气酒精测试仪。|Synonyms: breathalyzer (US spelling), breath test, intoxilyzer|Antonyms: None|noun

breathalysers:/ˈbrɛθəlaɪzərz/ (US) /ˈbrɛθəlaɪzəz/ (UK)| n.|呼气酒精测试仪（复数）|Plural form of breathalyser, referring to multiple devices used for measuring the amount of alcohol in a driver's breath.|The police department purchased new breathalysers. 警察局购买了新的呼气酒精测试仪。|Synonyms: breathalyzers (US spelling), breath tests, intoxilyzers|Antonyms: None|noun
