nonhousing:/nɒnˈhaʊzɪŋ/
adjective
非住房的
Not related to housing or residences; used to describe expenses, costs, or sectors that are not directly connected to residential properties.
The city council approved a budget that included significant increases in nonhousing expenditures.  市议会批准了一项预算，其中包括非住房支出的大幅增加。

The nonhousing sector of the economy has shown steady growth this quarter. 本季度经济的非住房部门显示出稳定增长。
