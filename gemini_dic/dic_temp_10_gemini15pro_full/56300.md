doggonest:/ˈdɒɡənɪst/ (US) /ˈdɒɡənɪst/ (UK)| adj.|最...的；非常的；（加强语气）|Used to emphasize how great or extreme something is.  It's often used humorously or informally.  It functions as a superlative adjective, though the specific adjective it modifies is often implied rather than stated. |That's the doggonedest fish I ever did see! 那是我见过的最大的鱼！|近义词：darnedest,  blasted,  absolute, utter|反义词:least, slightest|词性:adjective

词源:
The word "doggonest" originated in the 19th century as a euphemism for "goddarnedest," avoiding the direct use of "God" in an oath.  It's part of a family of similar euphemisms like "doggone," "doggone it," and "dagnabbit," all used to express surprise, frustration, or emphasis.
