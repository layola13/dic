oversentimentality:/ˌoʊvərˌsɛntɪmɛnˈtælɪti/
n.
过度感伤，多愁善感
Excessive sentimentality; expressing or showing too much emotion, especially sadness or tenderness.
Her oversentimentality made it difficult for her to cope with even minor setbacks. 她过度感伤，以至于连小小的挫折都难以应付。
The film's oversentimentality made it feel contrived. 这部电影的过度煽情让人觉得很做作。

近义词:mawkishness, mushiness, sappiness
反义词:stoicism, impassivity, coldness
词性:noun
