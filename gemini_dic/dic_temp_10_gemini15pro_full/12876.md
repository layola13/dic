assuasive:/əˈsweɪsɪv/| adj.|安慰的；劝慰的；缓和的|Having a soothing or calming effect; tending to dispel doubts or anxieties.|Her assuasive words calmed the frightened child. 她安慰的话语使受惊的孩子平静下来。|近义词：soothing, calming, comforting, pacifying, reassuring, mollifying|反义词：agitating, disturbing, upsetting|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *assuasīvus*, from Latin *assuāsus*, past participle of *assuāre* "to accustom, soothe," from *ad-* "to" + *suāvis* "sweet, pleasant."
