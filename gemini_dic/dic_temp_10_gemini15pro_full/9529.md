antiseizure:/ˌæntiˈsiːʒər/ | adj. |抗癫痫的，抗惊厥的 |  Preventing or reducing the frequency or severity of seizures (sudden attacks of uncontrolled electrical activity in the brain). |  This medication is an effective antiseizure drug.  这种药物是一种有效的抗癫痫药物。 | synonyms: anticonvulsant, antiepileptic | antonyms: convulsant, epileptogenic | adjective


Etymology:
anti- (prefix) : against, opposing (from Greek "anti")
seizure (noun): a sudden attack of illness, especially one affecting the brain (from Old French "seisir" meaning "to seize, grasp")
