ganglioform:/ˈɡæŋɡliəfɔːrm/| adj.|神经节状的，似神经节的|Resembling a ganglion in form.  Having the form of a ganglion; ganglion-like.|The ganglioform swelling was observed under the microscope.|近义词：ganglionar, ganglionic|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From *ganglio-* (from Greek *γάγγλιον* (ganglion) meaning "knot, swelling, nerve ganglion") + *-form* (from Latin *-formis*, meaning "having the form of").
