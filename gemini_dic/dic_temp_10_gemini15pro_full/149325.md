pacemaker: /ˈpeɪsmeɪkər/ | n. | 起搏器；标兵；领跑者 | A device that uses electrical impulses to regulate the heart rhythm, or someone/something that sets the speed or standard for others. |  He needs a pacemaker to regulate his heartbeat. 他需要一个起搏器来调节他的心跳。| synonyms: pacesetter, regulator, controller | antonyms: follower, laggard | noun

pacemaker: /ˈpeɪsmeɪkər/ | n. | 标兵；领跑者 | Someone who sets a fast pace at the beginning of a race. | The pacemaker set a blistering pace for the first mile. 领跑者在第一英里设定了一个极快的速度。| synonyms: frontrunner, leader | antonyms: straggler | noun
