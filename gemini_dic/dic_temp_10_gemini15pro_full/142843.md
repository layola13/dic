out-feeding:/ˌaʊtˈfiːdɪŋ/
n.
外饲；户外喂食
The act of feeding animals outdoors, especially livestock or wildlife.
Farmers are practicing out-feeding to supplement the grazing of their cattle during winter.  农民们在冬天实行户外喂食以补充牛的放牧。
The out-feeding station attracted a variety of birds. 户外喂食站吸引了各种各样的鸟类。
supplementary feeding, outdoor feeding
indoor feeding
noun
