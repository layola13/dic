morbid:/ˈmɔːrbɪd/ (US) /ˈmɔːbɪd/ (UK)| adj.|病态的，不健康的；（对死亡等）着迷的|Characterized by or appealing to an abnormal and unhealthy interest in disturbing and unpleasant subjects, especially death and disease. |He had a morbid fascination with serial killers. 他对连环杀手有着病态的迷恋。|Synonyms: macabre, gruesome, unhealthy, ghoulish, lurid|Antonyms: healthy, wholesome, cheerful|adjective


morbidity:/mɔːrˈbɪdɪti/ (US) /mɔːˈbɪdɪti/ (UK)| n.|病态；发病率|The condition of being diseased or morbid; the rate of disease or illness in a population. |The morbidity rate for this particular disease is quite high. 这种特殊疾病的发病率相当高。|Synonyms: prevalence, incidence, sickness, unhealthiness|Antonyms: health, wellness|noun
