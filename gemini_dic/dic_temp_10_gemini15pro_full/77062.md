garnishable:/ˈɡɑːrnɪʃəbl/
adjective
可装饰的
Suitable or intended for use as a garnish.
The salad was garnishable with edible flowers.  沙拉可以用可食用的花来装饰。
近义词:decorative, embellishable
反义词:plain, undecorated
词性:adjective


garnishment:/ˈɡɑːrnɪʃmənt/
noun
装饰物
Something used to garnish food, such as a sprig of parsley or a slice of lemon.
A simple garnishment of chopped herbs can elevate a dish.  简单的切碎香草装饰可以提升一道菜。
近义词:decoration, embellishment
反义词:plainness
词性:noun

词源:
From garnish (v.) + -able/-ment.  Ultimately from Old French garnir "to furnish, equip, supply."
