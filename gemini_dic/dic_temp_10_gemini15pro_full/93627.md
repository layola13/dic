imblaze:/ɪmˈbleɪz/| vt.|使燃烧；照亮；激起|To set something on fire, or to make something bright with light or color; to inspire strong feelings, especially of love or anger.|The setting sun imblazed the sky with vibrant hues. 夕阳用鲜艳的色彩照亮了天空。|近义词：inflame, kindle, ignite, light up|反义词：extinguish, quench|verb
