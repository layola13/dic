nonimperious:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpɪəriəs/
adjective
非专横的；不傲慢的；谦逊的
Not domineering or arrogant; modest.
She spoke in a nonimperious tone, requesting rather than demanding assistance. 她以一种不专横的语气说话，请求而不是要求帮助。
He has a nonimperious leadership style, valuing collaboration and input from his team. 他有一种不专横的领导风格，重视团队合作和意见。
Synonyms: humble, modest, unassuming, unpretentious
Antonyms: imperious, arrogant, haughty, domineering
词性: 形容词
