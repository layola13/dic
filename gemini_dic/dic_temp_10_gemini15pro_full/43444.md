crapulence:/ˈkræpjələns/ | n. | 恶心，宿醉，暴饮暴食 | sickness or indisposition resulting from excessive eating or drinking, especially excessive drinking of alcohol. | He suffered from crapulence after the all-night party. 他在通宵派对后感到恶心宿醉。|  Example 2: The crapulence he felt the next morning was a stark reminder of his overindulgence. 第二天早上他感到的恶心是他过度放纵的一个明显提醒。 | 近义词：hangover, nausea, sickness, indigestion | 反义词: sobriety, temperance | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Latin *crāpulentia, from crāpulōsus (“sick from overeating or drinking”), from crāpula (“intoxication”).
