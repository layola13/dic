battlefatigued: /ˈbæt(ə)lˌfəˈtiːɡd/ | adj. | 战斗疲劳的，战争疲劳的 | Exhausted and emotionally drained as a result of prolonged exposure to the stresses of combat or other intensely demanding situations.  It describes a state of mental and physical exhaustion beyond ordinary tiredness. |  The battlefatigued soldiers stumbled back to camp.  战斗疲劳的士兵跌跌撞撞地回到了营地。

The doctor diagnosed the aid worker as battlefatigued after months in the refugee camp. 医生诊断这位救援人员在难民营待了几个月后患上了战争疲劳症。 | 近义词:  burnt-out, exhausted, shell-shocked, traumatized, weary | 反义词: refreshed, energized, invigorated | 词性: 形容词
