drafter:/ˈdræftər/ /ˈdrɑːftər/ | n. |起草人，制图员|A person who draws plans or designs, especially of buildings or machinery.  A person who writes a first version of a document. |例句：The drafter prepared the blueprints for the new bridge.  制图员准备了新桥的蓝图。

例句：The legal drafter is working on the contract. 法律起草人正在撰写合同。|近义词：designer, writer, author, composer, architect|反义词:None|词性:Noun

drafter:/ˈdræftər/ /ˈdrɑːftər/ | n. |应征入伍者，被征召者|A person who is drafted into military service. |例句：The young drafter was nervous about going to war.  年轻的应征入伍者对参战感到紧张。

例句：Many drafters were called up during the war. 战争期间，许多人被征召入伍。|近义词：conscript, recruit|反义词:volunteer|词性:Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From draft + -er.  "draft" comes from the Old Norse "draga" meaning "to draw, pull, drag."
