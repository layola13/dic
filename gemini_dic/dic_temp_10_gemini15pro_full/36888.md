colorful: /ˈkʌlərfəl/ (US) /ˈkʌləfʊl/ (UK)| adj.|色彩缤纷的；鲜艳的；有趣的；生动的|Full of bright colors or having a lot of different colors.  Figuratively, it can also mean full of variety or exciting.|例句：The birds have colorful feathers. 这些鸟有着色彩艳丽的羽毛。|例句：She led a colorful life, full of adventure. 她过着丰富多彩、充满冒险的生活。|近义词：bright, vibrant, vivid, rich, multicolored|反义词：dull, drab, colorless, monochrome|词性: adjective

词源: color (颜色) + -ful (充满...的)  The suffix "-ful" comes from Old English "-ful" and means "full of" or "characterized by."
