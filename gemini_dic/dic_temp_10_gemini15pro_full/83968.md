half-convinced:/ˌhæf kənˈvɪnst/
| adj. | 半信半疑的；不太确定的 | Partly convinced; not fully persuaded. | I was half-convinced by his explanation, but still had some doubts. 我对他的解释半信半疑，但仍然有一些疑问。 |  I'm half-convinced that she's lying. 我有点怀疑她在撒谎。 | 近义词：doubtful, skeptical, uncertain | 反义词：convinced, certain, sure | 词性: 形容词

词源: "half-" (Old English healf) + "convinced" (past participle of convince, from Old French convaincre, from Latin convincere "to overcome, conquer," from com- "together" + vincere "to conquer").
