deifies:/ˈdiːɪfaɪz/
verb
神化；将…奉若神明
treats or regards someone or something as a god
The ancient Romans deified their emperors. 古罗马人神化他们的皇帝。
exalts, glorifies, idolizes
belittles, denounces
