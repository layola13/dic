needlepointing:/ˈniːdlˌpɔɪntɪŋ/| n.|针绣；绣花|The act of embroidering canvas with yarn using a needle.  It involves creating designs and patterns on a stiff canvas mesh using a special needle and various types of yarn.|She spent her evenings needlepointing a cushion cover. 她晚上都在绣一个靠垫套。|近义词：embroidery, needlework, cross-stitch|反义词: None|noun
