chemos:/ˈkiːmoʊz/| n. |化疗（chemotherapy的缩写）|Chemotherapy, often shortened to "chemo," is a type of cancer treatment that uses one or more anti-cancer drugs (chemotherapeutic agents) as part of a standardized chemotherapy regimen.| My grandma is undergoing chemos for her cancer. 我奶奶正在接受癌症化疗。|近义词：chemotherapy, chemo treatment|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 alternative therapies，例如 surgery, radiation therapy)|词性: noun (abbreviation)
