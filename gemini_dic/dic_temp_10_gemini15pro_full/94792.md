impugner:/ɪmˈpjuːnər/| n.|质疑者，非难者|Someone who impugns; someone who challenges the truth, validity, or honesty of something or someone.|The politician's integrity was questioned by an impugner who presented evidence of past misdeeds.  这位政客的正直被一位质疑者质疑，他提供了过去不当行为的证据。|Synonyms: critic, attacker, challenger, assailant|Antonyms: supporter, defender, advocate|noun
