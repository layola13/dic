drat:/dræt/
interjection
该死；讨厌；见鬼
Expressing mild annoyance or impatience.
Drat! I've lost my keys again. 讨厌！我又把钥匙弄丢了。
Synonyms: darn, blast, bother, confound
Antonyms: bless
词性:感叹词


drat:/dræt/
verb
诅咒；咒骂
To curse.  (Often used humorously or mildly.)
He dratted the traffic jam that made him late. 他咒骂导致他迟到的交通堵塞。
Synonyms: curse, damn
Antonyms: bless
词性:动词
