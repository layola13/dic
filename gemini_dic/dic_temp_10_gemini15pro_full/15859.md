balletic:/bæˈlɛtɪk/
adjective
芭蕾舞的；像芭蕾舞的
Resembling or characteristic of ballet.
Her movements were balletic, graceful and precise. 她的动作像芭蕾舞一样，优雅而精准。
The dancer's balletic pose was captured perfectly in the photograph.  照片完美地捕捉到了舞者芭蕾舞般的姿势。
近义词: graceful, elegant, fluid, dancelike
反义词: clumsy, awkward, ungainly
词性: adjective

balletic:/bæˈlɛtɪk/
noun
芭蕾舞艺术；与芭蕾舞有关的事物 (较少用作名词)
Matters relating to or characteristic of ballet.  (Less common usage)
He dedicated his life to the balletic, mastering every nuance of the art form. 他毕生致力于芭蕾舞艺术，掌握了这种艺术形式的每一个细微之处。
Her knowledge of the balletic was extensive. 她对芭蕾舞艺术的了解非常广泛。
近义词: ballet
反义词:  (No direct antonym as a noun)
词性: noun
