associated: /əˈsoʊʃiˌeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 相关的，关联的 | Connected, linked, or related |  The risks associated with smoking are well documented.  吸烟的相关风险已有充分的文献记载。| synonyms: related, connected, linked, correlated | antonyms: unrelated, unconnected, independent | adjective

associated: /əˈsoʊʃiˌeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 联合的，合伙的 | Joined together for a particular purpose | He is associated with a law firm in Boston. 他与波士顿的一家律师事务所联合。| synonyms: affiliated, allied, partnered | antonyms: separate, independent, unaffiliated | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *associatus, past participle of associare "to join with," from Latin ad- "to" + sociare "to unite," from socius "companion."
