diphthongalize:/dɪfˈθɒŋɡəlaɪz/ /dɪfˈθɑːŋɡəlaɪz/
verb
双元音化; 将…变成双元音
To cause a single vowel sound to become a diphthong.
The Great Vowel Shift diphthongalized many Middle English long vowels.  大元音推移使许多中古英语的长元音变成了双元音。
The pronunciation of "house" has diphthongalized over time. “house” 的发音随着时间的推移变成了双元音。
近义词:  vocalize
反义词:  monophthongize
词性: verb
