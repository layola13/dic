muncher:/ˈmʌntʃər/| n. |大吃大嚼的人；吃…的人|A person who eats something, often noisily and enthusiastically.  Someone who munches (chews food steadily and often noisily).|例句：He's a real muncher – he can finish a whole bag of chips in one sitting. 他真是个大胃王，一袋薯片一下子就能吃完。

例句：My rabbit is a noisy muncher of carrots. 我的兔子吃胡萝卜的时候嘎吱嘎吱响。|近义词：eater, consumer, gourmand, gobbler|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "dieter" in some contexts) |词性:noun
