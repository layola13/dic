cytogenesis:/ˌsaɪtoʊˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
n.
细胞生成;细胞发生
The formation of cells.
例句：Cytogenesis is a crucial process in the development of an organism. 细胞生成是生物体发育的关键过程。

例句：The study of cytogenesis helps us understand how cells multiply and differentiate.  细胞生成的研究帮助我们理解细胞如何增殖和分化。

近义词：cell formation, cell development
反义词: cell death, apoptosis
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
cyto- + genesis
cyto- : from Greek "kytos" meaning "vessel" or "container," later referring to a "cell."
genesis : from Greek "genesis" meaning "origin," "creation," or "generation."
