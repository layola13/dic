cavo:/ˈkɑːvoʊ/ (US), /ˈkævəʊ/ (UK)| n.| 洞穴，凹处；（尤指葡萄酒）酒窖|A hollow or a natural underground space, especially one used for storing wine.|例句：The wine is stored in a cool, dark cavo.  葡萄酒储存在阴凉黑暗的酒窖里。

例句：The hikers discovered a hidden cavo in the mountainside.  徒步旅行者在山腰发现了一个隐藏的洞穴。|近义词：cave, cellar, grotto|反义词: mound, hill|词性:名词
