hemorrhoids:/ˈheməˌrɔɪdz/ (US), /ˈhɛmərɔɪdz/ (UK)| n.|痔疮|Swollen veins in the anus and lower rectum, sometimes causing pain, itching, and bleeding.  They can be internal (inside the anus) or external (under the skin around the anus).| He suffered from painful hemorrhoids. 他患有疼痛的痔疮。|Synonyms: piles|Antonyms: (None, as it's a medical condition)|Noun
