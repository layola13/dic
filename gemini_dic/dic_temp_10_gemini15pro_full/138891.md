nutant:/ˈnjuːtənt/ /ˈnuːtənt/
|adj.|下垂的，低垂的|Drooping, hanging downward, usually of a plant or flower.|The snowdrops were nutant, their delicate white bells bowed towards the earth.  雪花莲低垂着，它们精致的白色铃铛朝向地面低垂。|The ripe ears of wheat were nutant under their own weight. 成熟的麦穗在自身重量下低垂。| 近义词:drooping, pendulous, cernuous|反义词:erect, upright|词性:形容词
