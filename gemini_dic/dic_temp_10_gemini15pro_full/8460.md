antiaristocracy:/ˌæntiˌærɪˈstɒkrəsi/
noun
反对贵族统治；反贵族主义
Opposition to aristocracy or aristocratic principles.
The French Revolution was fueled by strong antiaristocracy sentiments. 法国大革命是由强烈的反贵族情绪推动的。
His antiaristocracy views led him to support the republican cause. 他的反贵族观点使他支持共和事业。
Synonyms:  anti-elitism, egalitarianism
Antonyms:  aristocraticism, elitism
