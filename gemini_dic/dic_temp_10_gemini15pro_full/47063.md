datedly:/ˈdeɪtɪdli/
adv.
过时地，陈旧地
in a way that shows the style or fashions of a particular time in the past, especially so that it now seems old-fashioned
Her clothes looked datedly. 她的衣服看起来过时了。
近义词:old-fashionedly, archaically, anachronistically
反义词:modernly, contemporarily
词性:副词
