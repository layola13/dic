encampments:/ɪnˈkæmpmənts/
n.
营地；扎营
A place where a group of people, especially soldiers or refugees, live in tents or other temporary shelters.
The refugees set up encampments along the border.  难民们沿着边界设立了营地。

The area was dotted with encampments of homeless people. 这个地区到处都是无家可归者的营地。
近义词:camps, settlements, bivouacs
反义词:N/A
词性:noun
