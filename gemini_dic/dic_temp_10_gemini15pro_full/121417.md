misinferring:/ˌmɪsɪnˈfɜːrɪŋ/
verb
错误地推断；曲解
Drawing a wrong conclusion from something; interpreting something incorrectly.
He was misinferring her silence as agreement. 他把她的沉默误解为同意。
misinterpreting, misunderstanding, misconstruing
correctly inferring, correctly interpreting, understanding
verb
