diligently:/ˈdɪlɪdʒəntli/| adv.|勤奋地，勤勉地|In a way that shows care and conscientiousness in one's work or duties.|She worked diligently on her project, determined to finish it on time. 她勤奋地做着她的项目，决心按时完成。|近义词:assiduously, industriously, carefully, painstakingly|反义词:lazily, carelessly, negligently|词性:副词

词源:
From Middle English *diligentli, from Old French diligemment, from Latin dīligenter, from dīligēns (“diligent”).
