impalas:/ɪmˈpɑːləz/ | n. |黑斑羚，高角羚|A graceful, medium-sized antelope found in eastern and southern Africa, known for its leaping ability. |例句：A herd of impalas grazed peacefully on the savanna. 一群黑斑羚在热带草原上平静地吃草。

例句：The impala leaped over the bush with incredible agility.  这只黑斑羚以惊人的敏捷跳过了灌木丛。|近义词：antelope, gazelle|反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑predator 捕食者) |词性:noun
