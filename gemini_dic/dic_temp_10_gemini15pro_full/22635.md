boracay:/bɔːrəˈkaɪ/
proper noun
长滩岛
A small island in the central Philippines, famous for its white-sand beaches.
例句：Boracay is a popular tourist destination. 长滩岛是一个热门的旅游目的地。

例句：We spent a week relaxing on the beaches of Boracay. 我们在长滩岛的海滩上放松了一个星期。
近义词：island, resort
反义词: mainland, city
词性: proper noun
