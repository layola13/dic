hadar:/ˈheɪdɑːr/ (US), /ˈhɑːdɑːr/ (UK)| n.|哈达尔|A star in the constellation Beta Centauri, also known as Beta Centauri A. It is a triple star system and one of the brightest stars in the night sky. | Example: Hadar is visible from the Southern Hemisphere. 哈达尔在南半球可见。| Example: Astronomers have studied the Hadar system extensively. 天文学家对哈达尔系统进行了广泛的研究。|Synonym: Beta Centauri A, Agena|Antonym: N/A|Noun
