ministryship:/ˈmɪnɪstriʃɪp/
n.
牧师的职务；牧师的任期
The position or tenure of a minister.
He began his ministryship in a small rural church.  他在一个乡村小教堂开始了他的牧师生涯。
synonyms: ministry, pastorate, priesthood, chaplaincy
antonyms: laity
