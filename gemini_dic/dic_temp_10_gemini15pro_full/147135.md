overlogicalness:/ˌoʊvərˈlɒdʒɪkəlnəs/ | n. | 过于逻辑化；过度理性 | The state of being excessively logical or relying too heavily on logic, often to the detriment of intuition, empathy, or practical considerations. | His overlogicalness made him unable to appreciate the emotional nuances of the situation. 他过于逻辑化，使他无法理解情况中的情感细微之处。 | 近义词：hyper-rationality, excessive logic, rigidity | 反义词：emotionalism, irrationality, intuition | 词性: 名词

overlogical:/ˌoʊvərˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 过于逻辑的；过度理性的 | Excessively logical or relying too heavily on logic. |  His overlogical approach to problem-solving often alienated his colleagues. 他过于逻辑的问题解决方法经常疏远他的同事。| 近义词：hyper-rational, excessively logical | 反义词：emotional, irrational, intuitive | 词性: 形容词


词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "logical" + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns from adjectives).  由表示“过度”的前缀"over-"，形容词"logical"（逻辑的）以及名词后缀"-ness"构成。
