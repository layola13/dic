fives:/faɪvz/
n.
一种用手掌击球的游戏
A game played by hitting a small, hard ball with the hand against a wall.
We played fives in the schoolyard. 我们在学校操场上玩手球。
近义词: handball (a similar game)
反义词: (No direct antonym for a game)
词性: noun
