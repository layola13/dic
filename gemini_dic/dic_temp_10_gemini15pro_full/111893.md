logotype:/ˈlɒɡəʊtaɪp/ /ˈloʊɡətaɪp/| n.|标识，商标，标志|A logotype is a graphic symbol or emblem that represents a company, brand, or product. It is often used interchangeably with the term "logo."|The company's logotype is instantly recognizable. 该公司的标识一眼就能认出来。|近义词：logo, emblem, trademark, symbol|反义词:None|词性:noun

logotype:/ˈlɒɡəʊtaɪp/ /ˈloʊɡətaɪp/| n.|（报刊的）刊名，报头|The distinctive name of a newspaper or periodical, printed in a special typeface at the top of the first page. |The newspaper's logotype was redesigned last year. 这家报纸的报头去年重新设计了。|近义词：masthead, banner|反义词:None|词性:noun
