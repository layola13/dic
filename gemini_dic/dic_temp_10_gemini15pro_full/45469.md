curatory:/kjʊˈreɪtəri/ |adj. |策展的，馆藏的|Relating to or characteristic of a curator or their work. |The museum's curatorial team carefully selected the artifacts for the exhibition.  博物馆的策展团队精心挑选了展览的文物。|Synonyms:  museum, archival, collection, gallery |Antonyms: unorganized, chaotic|adjective


curatory: /kjʊˈreɪtəri/ |n. |策展工作，馆藏管理|The activity or profession of a curator. |She has years of experience in curatory and museum management. 她在策展和博物馆管理方面有多年的经验。|Synonyms: curation, museum management, collection management |Antonyms: neglect, abandonment|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "curate" (v.) + "-ory" (suffix forming adjectives and nouns). "Curate" comes from Late Latin "curatus," past participle of "curare" (to take care of), from Latin "cura" (care, concern). The suffix "-ory" indicates a place or function related to the base word.
