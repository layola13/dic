birthrights:/ˈbɜːθraɪts/| n.|与生俱来的权利；天赋的权利|A birthright is a right or privilege that you have from birth, especially one that is given to all members of a particular group or nation. |例句：Education is a birthright of every child.  每个孩子都有受教育的权利。|近义词：prerogative, privilege, right|反义词:ineligibility |noun
