hendiadys:/hɛnˈdaɪədɪs/ | n. |  偏义复词，重言修饰 | A figure of speech in which two words connected by a conjunction (usually “and”) express a single complex idea.  It is a form of redundancy. |  Example: He came with might and main (meaning great force). 他带着强大的力量来了。 | Synonym: redundancy, pleonasm, tautology | Antonym: conciseness, brevity | noun
