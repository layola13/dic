indefeasibility:/ˌɪndɪˌfiːzəˈbɪləti/ | n. |不可废止，不可推翻，不可剥夺|The quality of being indefeasible; incapable of being annulled or voided or undone. |The indefeasibility of the title is guaranteed by the government.  政府保证所有权不可剥夺。|Synonyms: inviolability, impregnability, security |Antonyms: defeasibility, vulnerability, invalidity|noun
