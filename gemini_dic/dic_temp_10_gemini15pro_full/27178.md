calligraphical:/ˌkælɪˈɡræfɪkəl/| adj.|书法艺术的；精妙的|Relating to calligraphy; beautifully written. |Her calligraphical skills are impressive. 她的书法技艺令人印象深刻。|The contract was written in elegant, calligraphical script. 这份合同是用优美、精妙的书法字体书写的。|近义词：artistic, elegant, beautiful, fine, exquisite|反义词:sloppy, messy, illegible|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From calligraphy + -ical.  Calligraphy comes from Greek kalligraphia "beautiful writing," from kallos "beauty" + graphein "to write."
