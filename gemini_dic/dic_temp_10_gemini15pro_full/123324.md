monographically:/ˌmɒnəˈɡræfɪkli/ /ˌmɑːnəˈɡræfɪkli/
|adv.| 以专论的方式；专门地 |  In a manner of a monograph; dealing with a single specialized subject in detail. | He studied the topic monographically, focusing on its historical development. 他以专论的方式研究了这个主题，重点关注其历史发展。| The artist monographically explored the theme of light and shadow in his latest exhibition.  这位艺术家在其最新的展览中专门探讨了光与影的主题。 |  intensively, exhaustively, comprehensively, thoroughly, in depth | cursorily, superficially, briefly | adverb
