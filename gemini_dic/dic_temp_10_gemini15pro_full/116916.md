mayordomo:/ˌmaɪɔːrˈdoʊmoʊ/ (US), /ˌmeɪɔːrˈdəʊməʊ/ (UK)| n. |管家，总管|A chief steward or butler, especially in a large household.  A person who manages a household. |例句：The mayordomo oversaw all the staff and ensured the smooth running of the estate.  管家监督所有员工，并确保庄园的顺利运作。

例句：The mayordomo announced the arrival of the guests.  管家宣布客人到来。|近义词：steward, butler, manager|反义词:servant, maid|词性:noun
