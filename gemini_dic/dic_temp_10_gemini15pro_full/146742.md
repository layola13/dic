overhugenesses:/ˌoʊvərˈhjuːdʒnəsɪz/
|n.|巨大；庞大；极大|The sheer overhugeness of the universe is difficult to comprehend. 宇宙的浩瀚难以理解。|例句：The overhugeness of the task before them was daunting. 面对如此巨大的任务，他们感到畏惧。|近义词：immensity, vastness, enormity|反义词：smallness, tininess, minuteness|词性:名词

Etymology:  "over-" (excessive) + "huge" (very large) + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality).  From Middle English "huge," from Old French "ahuge," ultimately of Germanic origin.
