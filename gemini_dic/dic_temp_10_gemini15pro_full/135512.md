nonplasticity:/ˌnɒnplæˈstɪsɪti/
n.
非塑性；无塑性
The nonplasticity of the material made it unsuitable for molding.  这种材料的非塑性使其不适合模塑。
The nonplasticity of the clay made it difficult to shape.  这种粘土的非塑性使其难以塑形。
lack of plasticity, rigidity, stiffness
plasticity, malleability
noun
