fricassee: /ˈfrɪkəˌsiː/ /ˈfrɪkəsiː/ | n. vt.|  fricassé; 油炸肉块；炖肉块；|A dish of meat, especially chicken or veal, cut into small pieces, fried, and then stewed in a sauce. |例句：We had fricassee of chicken for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了鸡肉块。|近义词：stew, braise |反义词: roast, grill| noun
fricassee: /ˈfrɪkəˌsiː/ /ˈfrɪkəsiː/ | vt.| 油炸后再炖；|To cook (meat, especially chicken or veal) by cutting it into small pieces, frying it, and then stewing it in a sauce. |例句：She fricasseed the rabbit with mushrooms. 她用蘑菇炖了兔子肉。|近义词：stew, braise |反义词: roast, grill| verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *fricasée*, from Old French *friquasser* (to fry), of uncertain origin.
