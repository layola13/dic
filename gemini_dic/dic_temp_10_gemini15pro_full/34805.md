ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English climben, from Old English climban (“to climb, mount, ascend”), from Proto-Germanic *klamjaną (“to climb”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)klembh- (“to lean, bend”). Cognate with Scots clim (“to climb”), West Frisian klimme (“to climb”), Dutch klimmen (“to climb”), German klimmen (“to climb”), Danish klatre (“to climb”), Swedish klättra (“to climb”), Icelandic klífa (“to climb”).
