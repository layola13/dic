growingly: /ˈɡroʊɪŋli/ | adv. | 越来越；日益 | Increasingly; to an increasing extent. | The situation is growingly complex.  局势越来越复杂。| The problem is becoming growingly serious. 这个问题日益严重。 | 近义词: increasingly, progressively, more and more | 反义词: decreasingly, less and less | 词性: 副词

词源:  From "growing" (present participle of "grow") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  来自于动词grow的现在分词growing加上副词后缀-ly.
