agouti:/ˈæɡuːti/ | n. | 刺豚鼠; (某些)灰褐色 | A rodent of tropical America and the West Indies, typically having coarse, grizzled fur. | 例句1：The agouti scurried through the undergrowth. 刺豚鼠窜过灌木丛。

例句2: The coat of the dog was an agouti color. 这只狗的皮毛是刺豚鼠灰褐色。| 近义词：paca, acouchi | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From French agouti, from Tupi-Guarani agutí.
