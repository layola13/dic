motoneuronal:/ˌməʊtəʊˈnjʊərənəl/ |adj.|运动神经元的|Relating to or involving a motor neuron, a nerve cell that transmits signals from the central nervous system to muscles, causing movement.|Motoneuronal dysfunction can lead to muscle weakness. 运动神经元功能障碍会导致肌肉无力。|The study focused on motoneuronal control of breathing. 这项研究的重点是运动神经元对呼吸的控制。|Synonyms: motor, efferent|Antonyms: sensory, afferent|adjective
