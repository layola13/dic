oblige:/əˈblaɪdʒ/
|vt.|迫使；施恩惠于；|To compel or constrain someone to do something by force, necessity, or moral obligation. Also, to do a favor or service for someone.|例句：The law obliges parents to send their children to school. 法律规定父母必须送孩子上学。

例句：I'm much obliged to you for your help. 非常感谢你的帮助。|近义词：compel, force, require, bind, favor, accommodate|反义词：excuse, release, free|词性：动词
obliges:/əˈblaɪdʒɪz/
|v.|（第三人称单数）迫使；施恩惠于；|The third-person singular present tense form of "oblige". It means to compel or constrain someone to do something, or to do a favor for someone.|例句：She always obliges her friends when they need help. 当她的朋友需要帮助时，她总是乐于帮忙。

例句：The contract obliges him to pay a penalty if he breaks the agreement. 合同规定，如果他违反协议，必须支付罚金。|近义词：compels, forces, requires, binds, favors, accommodates|反义词：excuses, releases, frees|词性：动词
