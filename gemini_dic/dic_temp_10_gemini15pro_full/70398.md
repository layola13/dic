fiftyfold:/ˈfɪftiˌfoʊld/
adjective
五十倍的；五十重的
multiplied by fifty; having fifty parts.
The company's profits increased fiftyfold last year.  这家公司的利润去年增长了五十倍。
Her investment had grown fiftyfold in ten years. 她的投资十年内增长了五十倍。
近义词:fifty times,  fifty-times
反义词: (no direct opposite, consider fractions like one-fiftieth)
词性:adjective
