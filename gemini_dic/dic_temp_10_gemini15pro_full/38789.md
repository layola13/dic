conduction:/kənˈdʌkʃən/| n.|传导，导电，导热|The process by which heat or electricity is directly transmitted through a substance when there is a difference of temperature or of electrical potential between adjoining regions, without movement of the material.|Heat conduction in metals is typically very efficient. 金属的热传导效率通常很高。|Synonyms: transmission, conveyance, transfer|Antonyms: insulation, resistance|noun

conduction:/kənˈdʌkʃən/| n.|引导，带领|The act of leading or guiding someone or something.|The conduction of the tour was very professional.  这次旅行的引导非常专业。|Synonyms: guidance, leading, escorting|Antonyms: N/A|noun
