discifloral:/dɪsɪˈflɔːrəl/
adjective
花盘状的；盘状花的
Having a disc-shaped floral structure; relating to or resembling a disc floret.
The sunflower exhibits a discifloral head composed of numerous small florets.  向日葵展现出一个由许多小花组成的盘状花序。
The botanist specialized in discifloral plants, particularly those of the Asteraceae family.  这位植物学家专门研究盘状花科植物，尤其是菊科植物。
近义词: discoid, disc-shaped
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider terms like 'ray floret' or 'ligulate' for contrasting flower structures.)
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From "disci-" (combining form of "discus," meaning disc) + "-floral" (relating to flowers).  Ultimately from Latin "discus" (disc) and "flos" (flower).
