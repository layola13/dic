haloed:/ˈheɪloʊd/ /ˈheɪləʊd/| adj.|神圣的，光环笼罩的|Surrounded by a halo; holy or sacred.|The saint was depicted in a painting, haloed and serene. 这幅画描绘了这位圣人，他头戴光环，神情安详。|The haloed figure of the angel appeared in the sky. 天使的光环笼罩的身影出现在天空中。|近义词：sacred, holy, divine, blessed|反义词：profane, unholy, secular|词性:形容词

词源: From halo + -ed.  "Halo" comes from the Greek word "halos," meaning "threshing floor," which later referred to a circular area of light around the sun or moon.
