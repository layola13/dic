disinvestment:/dɪsɪnˈvɛstmənt/ | n. |撤资，减少投资| The act of reducing investment in something, often by selling assets.  | The company's disinvestment in research and development could hurt its long-term growth.  该公司在研发方面的撤资可能会损害其长期增长。 | synonyms: divestiture, liquidation, withdrawal | antonyms: investment, reinvestment | noun
