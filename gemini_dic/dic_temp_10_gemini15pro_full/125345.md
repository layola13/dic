multilinear:/ˌmʌltiˈlɪniər/| adj.|多重线性的|Relating to a function that is linear in each of its arguments separately.  A function f(x1, x2, ..., xn) is multilinear if it is linear in each variable xi when all other variables are held constant.|The determinant of a matrix is a multilinear function of its rows (and columns). 行列式的值是其行（和列）的多重线性函数。

The dot product is a multilinear map. 点积是一个多重线性映射。|近义词: N/A (highly specific mathematical term)|反义词: N/A (highly specific mathematical term)|词性: adjective
