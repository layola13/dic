mythes:/mɪθs/| n. |神话，传说|A traditional story, especially one concerning the early history of a people or explaining some natural or social phenomenon, and typically involving supernatural beings or events. |例句：Ancient Greek myths are full of gods and goddesses. 古希腊神话充满了神和女神。

例句：The myth of the Loch Ness Monster continues to attract tourists. 尼斯湖水怪的传说继续吸引着游客。|近义词：legend, fable, lore, story, tale|反义词：reality, truth, fact|词性:noun
