bromalin:/ˈbroʊməlɪn/ /ˈbrɒməlɪn/| n.|菠萝蛋白酶|An enzyme found in pineapples that breaks down proteins. It is used in medicine to reduce inflammation and swelling.|Pineapple contains bromalin, which can help tenderize meat. 菠萝含有菠萝蛋白酶，可以用来嫩化肉类。|近义词：enzyme, protease|反义词: inhibitor (抑制剂)|noun
