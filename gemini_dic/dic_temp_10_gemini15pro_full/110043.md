licensees:/ˌlaɪsənˈsiːz/
|n.|被许可人，获许可方|A person or entity granted a license, giving them permission to do something specific.|The licensees were required to display their permits prominently. 被许可人被要求显著地展示他们的许可证。|
|近义词：permit holders, franchisees|反义词：licensors|词性: 名词|

licensees:/ˌlaɪsənˈsiːz/
|n.|特许经营商|A business that has been granted a license to operate under a specific brand or system.|The restaurant chain had numerous licensees across the country. 这家连锁餐厅在全国各地有很多特许经营商。|
|近义词：franchisees, concessionaires|反义词：licensors|词性: 名词|
