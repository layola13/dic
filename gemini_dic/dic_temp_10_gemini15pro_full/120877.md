misborn:/ˌmɪsˈbɔːrn/
adjective
天生有缺陷的；畸形的
born with defects; malformed
例句：The misborn lamb was rejected by its mother. 这只天生有缺陷的羔羊被它的母亲遗弃了。

例句：The charity provides support for families with misborn children.  这个慈善机构为有畸形孩子的家庭提供支持。

近义词：deformed, malformed, defective, monstrous
反义词：healthy, normal, perfect
词性：adjective

词源：From mis- + born.  "Mis-" is a prefix of negative or pejorative force.
