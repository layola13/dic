encloses:/ɪnˈkloʊzɪz/
verb
包围，围住
surrounds and confines something
The fence encloses the entire garden.  篱笆围住了整个花园。
The letter enclosed a check. 这封信里附了一张支票。
近义词: surround, encompass, contain, enclose
反义词: open, release, expose
词性: verb


enclosure:/ɪnˈkloʊʒər/
noun
围场，附件
an area that is surrounded by a wall or fence; something enclosed with a letter
The animals were kept in a secure enclosure. 动物们被关在一个安全的围栏里。
Please find the enclosure with this letter.  请查看这封信的附件。
近义词: compound, enclosure, attachment, insert
反义词: opening, exterior
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "enclore" (to enclose), from Latin "includere" (to shut in), from "in-" (in) + "claudere" (to close).
