inconcrete:/ɪnˈkɒŋkriːt/ | adj. | 非具体的，无形的，抽象的 | Not concrete; not perceptible by the senses; intangible; abstract. |  His plans were still inconcrete, lacking specific details. 他的计划仍然很抽象，缺乏具体的细节。| The artist tried to express inconcrete emotions through color. 这位艺术家试图通过色彩来表达抽象的情感。 | 近义词：abstract, intangible, immaterial, formless, vague | 反义词：concrete, tangible, material, definite, specific | 词性：形容词

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin *in concretus ‘not grown together’, from in- ‘not’ + concretus (see concrete).
