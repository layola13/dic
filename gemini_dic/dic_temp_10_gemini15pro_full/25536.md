bullshitting: /ˈbʊlʃɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 胡说八道；瞎扯；忽悠 | Talking nonsense; lying or exaggerating; speaking insincerely or without regard for the truth. | He was bullshitting about his accomplishments to impress the interviewer.  他为了给面试官留下深刻印象，就在吹嘘自己的成就。|  lying, fabricating, exaggerating, bluffing | telling the truth, being honest | verb

bullshit: /ˈbʊlʃɪt/ | n. | 废话；胡说；谎言 | Nonsense; lies; something untrue or insincere. | That's a load of bullshit!  那是一派胡言！ | nonsense, rubbish, lies, claptrap | truth, facts | noun
