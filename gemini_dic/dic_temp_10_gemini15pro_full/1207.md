accommodated: /əˈkɒmədeɪtɪd/
verb
容纳；提供住宿；使适应；顾及
to provide someone with a place to stay or live, or to provide enough space for a number of people
The hotel can accommodate up to 500 guests.  这家酒店最多可容纳500位客人。

The new policy accommodates the needs of working parents. 新政策顾及了在职父母的需求。

accommodate: /əˈkɒmədeɪt/
verb
容纳；提供住宿；使适应；顾及
to provide someone with a place to stay or live, or to provide enough space for a number of people
We need to find a hotel that can accommodate pets. 我们需要找到一家可以带宠物入住的酒店。


The conference hall can accommodate 1000 people.  这个会议厅可以容纳1000人。



近义词: house, lodge, suit, fit, adapt
反义词: refuse, reject
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin accommodatus, past participle of accommodare "to make fit, adapt," from Latin ad- "to" + commodare "to make fit," from commodus "suitable, convenient" (see commodious). The sense of "furnish with lodgings" first appeared in the 16th century.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hotel accommodates guests from all over the world. = 这家酒店接待来自世界各地的客人。
This room accommodates up to four people. = 这个房间最多可容纳四人。


一般过去时:
The hotel accommodated all the refugees. = 这家酒店安置了所有的难民。
They accommodated us with a room for the night. = 他们为我们提供了一个房间过夜。


现在完成时:
We have accommodated their request. = 我们已经满足了他们的要求。
The university has accommodated a growing number of international students. = 这所大学接纳了越来越多的国际学生。


过去完成时:
They had accommodated his needs before he even asked. = 他们甚至在他提出要求之前就已经满足了他的需求。
The school had accommodated several disabled students in the past. = 这所学校过去曾接收过几名残疾学生。


将来时:
We will accommodate your request as soon as possible. = 我们将尽快满足您的要求。
The new building will accommodate more students. = 新大楼将容纳更多的学生。


被动语态:
All the guests were accommodated in the hotel. = 所有客人都被安置在酒店里。
Special dietary needs can be accommodated. = 可以满足特殊的饮食需求。



PHRASE->
accommodate to = 适应
accommodate with = 向…提供；供应
accommodate oneself to = 使自己适应于…
be accommodated with = 得到…的供应
be accommodated in = 被安置在…
