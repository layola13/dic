destocking:/diːˈstɒkɪŋ/ (US) /diːˈstɒkɪŋ/ (UK)
n.|去库存，库存减少|The act of reducing the amount of goods held in stock.
The company engaged in aggressive destocking to clear out old inventory.  公司积极去库存，清理旧库存。
synonyms: inventory reduction, stock reduction, liquidation
antonyms: stocking, accumulation, inventory buildup
noun

destock:/diːˈstɒk/ (US) /diːˈstɒk/ (UK)
v.|去库存，减少库存|To reduce the amount of goods held in stock.
Retailers are destocking winter clothes to make way for spring fashions. 零售商正在减少冬季服装的库存，以便为春季时装腾出空间。
synonyms: reduce inventory, liquidate stock, clear out stock
antonyms: stock up, build up inventory, accumulate stock
verb
