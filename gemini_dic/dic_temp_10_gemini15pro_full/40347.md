contractible:/kənˈtræk.tə.bəl/ | adj. | 可收缩的，可缩小的，可缔结的 | Capable of being contracted or shrunk; capable of being legally enforced. |  The balloon is contractible.  这个气球是可以收缩的。 | synonyms: shrinkable, reducible, compressible | antonyms: inextensible, non-contractible, expanding | adjective

contractible:/kənˈtræk.tə.bəl/ | n. | 可收缩的东西 | Something that can be contracted.  | This muscle is a contractible. 这块肌肉是可收缩的。 | synonyms: retractable | antonyms: fixed, rigid | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *contractibilis, from Latin contractus (past participle of contrahere “to draw together, contract”), from com- (“together”) + trahere (“to draw, pull”).
