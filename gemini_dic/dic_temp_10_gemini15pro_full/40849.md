cooed:/kuːd/
verb
轻声细语地说；咕咕地叫
to speak softly and gently, especially to a baby or child; (of a dove) to make a soft murmuring sound.
The baby cooed and gurgled in her mother's arms. 婴儿在母亲的怀里轻声细语地说着，咯咯地笑着。
The doves cooed softly in the trees. 鸽子在树上轻轻地咕咕叫。
近义词: murmured, whispered, trilled
反义词: shouted, yelled
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *couen, from Old English *cūwan, of imitative origin.
