approving:/əˈpruːvɪŋ/
adjective
赞同的；认可的
Expressing or showing approval.
She gave me an approving nod. 她赞许地对我点了点头。
synonyms:  supportive, encouraging, positive, favorable, consenting
antonyms: disapproving, critical, negative, condemning, objecting
词性: 形容词
