admires:/ədˈmaɪərz/
verb
钦佩，赞赏
regards with respect or warm approval.
She admires her grandmother's strength and resilience. 她钦佩她祖母的坚强和韧性。

He admires the way she handles difficult situations. 他钦佩她处理困境的方式。

近义词: respects, esteems, appreciates, venerates
反义词: scorns, despises, loathes
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *admirer*, from Latin admirari "to wonder at, marvel at," from ad- "to" + mirari "to wonder."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He admires his father's work ethic. = 他钦佩他父亲的职业道德。
She admires the beauty of nature. = 她欣赏大自然的美。

一般过去时:
I admired his courage in the face of danger. = 我钦佩他在危险面前的勇气。
They admired the intricate details of the painting. = 他们欣赏这幅画作的复杂细节。

现在完成时:
I have always admired her dedication to her craft. = 我一直钦佩她对技艺的奉献精神。
He has admired that musician for years. = 多年来他一直很欣赏那位音乐家。

过去完成时:
She had long admired the author's work before she finally met him. = 在最终见到这位作家之前，她早就钦佩他的作品了。
They had admired the architecture of the building for many years before they finally visited it. = 在最终参观这座建筑之前，他们多年来一直钦佩它的建筑风格。


将来时:
I will admire anyone who stands up for what they believe in. = 我会钦佩任何一个坚持自己信仰的人。
Future generations will admire the achievements of our time. = 后代将会钦佩我们时代的成就。

虚拟语气:
If I were to meet the queen, I would admire her grace and poise. = 如果我能见到女王，我会钦佩她的优雅和沉着。
I wish I had a talent I could truly admire in myself. = 我希望我有一个能真正让自己钦佩的才能。

被动语态:
The artist's work is admired by many. = 这位艺术家的作品受到很多人的钦佩。
Her bravery was admired by everyone who knew her. = 认识她的人都钦佩她的勇敢。

疑问句:
Who do you admire most? = 你最钦佩谁？
What qualities do you admire in a person? = 你钦佩一个人身上的哪些品质？

否定句:
I don't admire people who are cruel to animals. = 我不钦佩那些对动物残忍的人。
He doesn't admire those who cheat their way to success. = 他不钦佩那些靠欺骗获得成功的人。
