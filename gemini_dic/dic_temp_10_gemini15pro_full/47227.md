daze: /deɪz/
n.| 昏迷；迷乱；迷糊|A state of stunned confusion or bewilderment.|He walked in a daze after being hit on the head. 他头部被击中后昏昏沉沉地走着。|近义词: stupor, trance, confusion|反义词: alertness, awareness|词性:名词

daze: /deɪz/
vt.| 使茫然；使眩晕|To stun or bewilder.|The explosion dazed him for a moment. 爆炸让他一时昏了过去。|近义词: stun, bewilder, confuse|反义词: enlighten, clarify|词性:动词
