nebulae's:/ˈnɛbjʊliːz/
n.|星云的|The nebulae's vibrant colors captivated astronomers.  星云鲜艳的色彩吸引了天文学家。|
English Explanation: The possessive form of "nebulae," meaning "belonging to the nebulae" or "of the nebulae." Nebulae is the plural of nebula, which refers to a cloud of gas and dust in space.
Example: The nebulae's swirling gases created a breathtaking spectacle. 星云的旋涡状气体创造了令人叹为观止的景象。
Synonym: nebula's, nebulas' (less common)
Antonym: N/A (Possessive forms don't typically have antonyms)
Word type: Possessive Noun
