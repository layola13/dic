endoparasite:/ˈɛndoʊˈpærəsaɪt/| n. |体内寄生虫|An organism that lives inside another organism (the host) and derives nourishment at the host's expense.|Example 1: Tapeworms are common endoparasites that can infect humans. 绦虫是常见的可以感染人类的体内寄生虫。

Example 2: The veterinarian found endoparasites in the dog's intestines. 兽医在狗的肠道内发现了体内寄生虫。|近义词：internal parasite|反义词：ectoparasite|词性:noun
