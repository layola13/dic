chivalric:/ʃɪˈvælrɪk/| adj.|骑士的；骑士精神的；侠义的|Relating to or characteristic of knights or knighthood; marked by honor, courtesy, and valor.|例句：He displayed a chivalric attitude by opening the door for her. 他展现出骑士风度，为她开门。

例句：The chivalric code emphasized bravery and loyalty. 骑士准则强调勇敢和忠诚。|近义词：gallant, courteous, honorable, knightly|反义词：cowardly, dishonorable, rude, churlish|词性:形容词

词源:
From Old French chevalerie "knighthood," from Late Latin caballerius "horseman," from Latin caballus "horse."  The meaning shifted from simply "pertaining to horsemen" to "having the qualities of an ideal knight."
