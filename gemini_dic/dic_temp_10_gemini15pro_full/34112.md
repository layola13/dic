cla:/klɑː/ | adj. | 粘土的；泥土的 | Made of or containing clay. | The soil is quite claggy after the rain. 雨后土壤很粘。 | 近义词: clayey, earthen | 反义词: sandy | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Old English clæg, from Proto-Germanic *klajjaz, from Proto-Indo-European *gley- (“to stick, glue”). Cognate with Scots clag (“sticky earth”), West Frisian klaai (“clay”), Dutch klei (“clay”), German Klei (“clay”), Danish ler (“clay”), Swedish lera (“clay”).
