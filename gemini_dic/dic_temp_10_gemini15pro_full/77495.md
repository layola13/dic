gazette:/ɡəˈzɛt/ | n. | 公报；报纸 | An official journal or newspaper. | Example: The results of the election were published in the government gazette.  选举结果刊登在政府公报上。

Example:  The local gazette reported on the town's upcoming festival.  当地报纸报道了小镇即将举行的节日。| Synonym: journal, newspaper, publication, periodical | Antonym:  None (Gazettes are a specific type of publication, not a concept with a direct opposite.) | noun
