brickwork:/ˈbrɪkwɜːk/| n.|砖砌体；砖瓦结构；砖工|The brickwork needs repairing.  砖砌体需要修理。|例句：The old house had beautiful, intricate brickwork. 这栋老房子有着漂亮而复杂的砖砌体。

例句：The brickwork around the windows was crumbling. 窗户周围的砖砌体正在 crumbling。|近义词：masonry, bricklaying|反义词: None|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From brick + work.  "brick" comes from Middle Dutch *bricke "brick" (compare West Frisian briek, Dutch brik). "work" comes from Old English weorc "work, action, deed; military fortifications," from Proto-Germanic *werkam "work" (source also of Old Saxon werk, Old Frisian werk, Dutch werk, Old High German werc, German Werk, Old Norse verk).
