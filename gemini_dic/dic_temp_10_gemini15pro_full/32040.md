Chernenko: /tʃɜːrˈnɛŋkoʊ/ | n. | 切尔年科 |  Konstantin Ustinovich Chernenko (1911 – 1985) was a Soviet politician and the fifth General Secretary of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. He led the Soviet Union for only 13 months, from February 1984 until his death in March 1985. | Chernenko succeeded Yuri Andropov as General Secretary. | 近义词: Soviet leader, politician | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
