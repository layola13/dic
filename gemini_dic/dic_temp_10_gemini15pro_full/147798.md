overprotects: /ˌoʊvərprəˈtekts/
| v. | 过度保护 | To protect someone excessively, often to the point of hindering their independence or development. |  She overprotects her children, never letting them experience any risk. 她过度保护她的孩子，从不让他们经历任何风险。|近义词: cossets, shields, shelters | 反义词: neglects, ignores | verb

overprotect: /ˌoʊvərprəˈtekt/
| v. | 过度保护 | To protect someone excessively, often to the point of hindering their independence or development. | He overprotected his car, keeping it in the garage all the time. 他过度保护他的车，一直把它放在车库里。| 近义词: cosset, shield, shelter | 反义词: neglect, ignore | verb

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi) + "protect" (from Latin prōtegō, prōtectere, "to cover in front, shield," from pro- "before" + tegere "to cover").
