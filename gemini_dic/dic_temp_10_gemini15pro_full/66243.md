exclamatorily: /ˌɛkskləˈmætərɪli/ /ˌɛkskləˈmeɪtərɪli/ | adv. |感叹地，呼喊地 | In a manner that expresses strong emotion, typically surprise, excitement, or anger.  Used to describe how something is said or done.  It implies a forceful and emphatic delivery. | "Stop!" she shouted exclamatorily.  “停下！”她喊道，语气激动。| Near Synonyms: excitedly, emphatically, vehemently, passionately | Near Antonyms: calmly, quietly, indifferently | word type: adverb
