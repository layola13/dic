discovenant:/dɪsˈkʌvənənt/
n.
违约，毁约；不履行契约
An act of breaking a covenant or agreement.
The discovenant of the treaty led to war.  该条约的违反导致了战争。

近义词: breach, violation, infringement, contravention
反义词:  compliance, adherence, observance
词性: 名词


discovenant:/dɪsˈkʌvənənt/
vt.
违反，背弃（契约）
To break or violate a covenant or agreement.
The king discovenanted the treaty with the neighboring kingdom. 国王违背了与邻国的条约。

近义词: break, violate, infringe, contravene
反义词:  honor, fulfill, keep, observe
词性: 动词


词源:
From dis- (expressing negation) + covenant (agreement).  由dis-（表示否定）+ covenant（协议）组成。
