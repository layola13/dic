hyping:/ˈhaɪpɪŋ/
verb
炒作; 大肆宣传
The act of promoting or publicizing something, often excessively, to generate excitement or interest.
They are hyping their new product with a massive advertising campaign.  他们正在大规模的广告宣传活动中炒作他们的新产品。
The movie studio is hyping the film as the biggest blockbuster of the year.  电影制片厂正在将这部电影宣传为年度最大巨片。
synonyms: promoting, publicizing, boosting, advertising
antonyms: downplaying, understating, minimizing
v.
