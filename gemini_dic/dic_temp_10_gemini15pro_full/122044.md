mistakable:/mɪˈsteɪkəbl/| adj.|容易弄错的，明显的，不会被误解的|Easily recognized or identified; obvious.|Her happiness was unmistakable. 她的快乐是显而易见的。|The symptoms of the disease are unmistakable. 这种疾病的症状是明显的。|近义词：obvious, apparent, clear, evident, noticeable, distinct, recognizable|反义词：unclear, vague, ambiguous, indistinct, subtle|词性:形容词
词源: From mistake + -able.  Ultimately from Latin *mistacare, frequentative of *mistare “to be wrong,” related to mis- "wrongly."
