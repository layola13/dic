editor:/ˈedɪtər/ (US), /ˈedɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|编辑，编者|A person who prepares written material for publication, by correcting, condensing, or otherwise modifying it.|The editor reviewed the manuscript carefully before publication. 编辑在出版前仔细审阅了稿件。|近义词: reviser, compiler, proofreader|反义词: author, writer|noun
