freewheels:/ˈfriːwiːlz/| v. | 空转，滑行; 自由发展，不受约束 |  to move forward without applying power or effort, especially in a vehicle; to proceed without control or restraint. | The cyclist freewheeled down the hill.  骑自行车的人顺着山坡滑行而下。| coast, roll, drift |  pedal, drive | verb
freewheels:/ˈfriːwiːlz/| n. | 空转装置，飞轮 | a device, typically on a bicycle, that allows the driven wheel to rotate independently of the pedals. | The freewheel on my bike is broken. 我的自行车的飞轮坏了。| coaster brake, ratchet mechanism | fixed gear | noun
