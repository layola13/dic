ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *krimpen, frequentative of *krimmen (“to shrink, shrivel”), related to Middle Low German krimpen (“to shrink, wrinkle, crimp”). Cognate with German krimpen (“to shrink, shrivel”).
