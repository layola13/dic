gooselike:/ˈɡuːslaɪk/| adj.|像鹅的，鹅似的|Resembling a goose in appearance, behavior, or sound. |Her walk was stiff and gooselike. 她的走路姿势僵硬，像鹅一样。|近义词：awkward, ungainly|反义词：graceful, elegant|词性:形容词
Etymology: goose + like.  "Goose" comes from Old English "gōs," and "like" comes from Old English "gelīc."
