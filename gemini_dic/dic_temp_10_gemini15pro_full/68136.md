fahrenheit:/ˈfærənhaɪt/ /ˈfɑːrənhaɪt/| n. adj.|华氏度，华氏温度计|A scale for measuring temperature, in which water freezes at 32 degrees and boils at 212 degrees.|The temperature today is 80 degrees Fahrenheit. 今天气温是80华氏度。|Celsius, Centigrade|Kelvin|noun, adjective
