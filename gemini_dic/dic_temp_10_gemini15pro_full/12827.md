associable:/əˈsoʊʃiəbl/ /əˈsəʊʃəbl/
adjective
可联想的；可交际的；友善的
Capable of being associated; sociable; friendly.
People with similar interests are often associable.  兴趣相投的人通常很容易相处。
This idea is associable with his earlier work.  这个想法可以与他早期的作品联系起来。
近义词: sociable, affable, companionable, gregarious, friendly
反义词: unsociable, unfriendly, aloof, reserved
词性: adjective
