neighbour:/ˈneɪbər (US)ˈneɪbər/ /ˈneɪbə(r) (UK)/| n.|邻居；邻国；邻近的人或物|A person who lives next door or very near to you.| My neighbours are very friendly. 我的邻居们非常友好。|Synonyms:  resident, local, inhabitant|Antonyms: stranger, foreigner| noun

neighbourly:/ˈneɪbərli/| adj.|邻近的；友好的；睦邻的|Friendly and helpful to your neighbours.|We have a very neighbourly community. 我们有一个非常友好的社区。|Synonyms:  friendly, amiable, amicable, cordial|Antonyms: unfriendly, hostile| adjective
