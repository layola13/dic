noose: /nuːs/
n.
绳套；绞索；束缚
A loop with a running knot, tightening as the rope or wire is pulled and typically used to hang people or trap animals.
The cowboy lassoed the calf with a noose. 牛仔用绳套套住了小牛。
Synonyms: snare, trap, lasso, rope
Antonyms: freedom, liberation
