cosmopolitanly:/ˌkɒzməˈpɒlɪtənli/ | adv. | 世界性地，国际化地 | In a way that includes people from many different countries and cultures; in a sophisticated manner. | She dressed cosmopolitanly, favoring Parisian styles. 她穿着打扮很有国际范儿，偏爱巴黎风格。|  He spoke cosmopolitanly, referencing experiences from around the globe.  他谈吐不凡，提及了世界各地的经历。 | 近义词: internationally, globally, broadly, worldly | 反义词: provincially, narrowly, locally | 词性: 副词

cosmopolitan:/ˌkɒzməˈpɒlɪtən/ | adj. | 世界性的，国际性的 | Including people from many different countries and cultures.  | London is a very cosmopolitan city. 伦敦是一个非常国际化的城市。| New York is known for its cosmopolitan atmosphere. 纽约以其国际化的氛围而闻名。| 近义词: international, global, worldly | 反义词: provincial, insular, local | 词性: 形容词

cosmopolitan:/ˌkɒzməˈpɒlɪtən/ | n. | 四海为家的人，世界主义者 | A person who is at home all over the world. | He considers himself a cosmopolitan, having lived in five different countries. 他认为自己是一个世界主义者，在五个不同的国家生活过。|  She's a true cosmopolitan, comfortable in any culture. 她是一个真正的世界公民，在任何文化中都感到自在。 | 近义词: internationalist, globe-trotter | 反义词: provincial, local | 词性: 名词


词源:  From Greek *kosmopolítēs* "citizen of the world," from *kosmo- "world"* + *polítēs* "citizen."
