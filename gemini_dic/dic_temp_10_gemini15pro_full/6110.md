ameritus:/əˈmɛrɪtəs/| adj.|退休的，荣誉退休的|Retired from active professional service but retained to an honorary position.|例句：Professor Smith is now emeritus professor of physics. 史密斯教授现在是物理学荣誉退休教授。

例句：She is an emeritus director of the company. 她是公司的荣誉退休董事。


近义词：retired, honorary|反义词:active|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *emeritus*, past participle of *emerēre* "to serve out one's time," from *e-* "out" + *merēre* "to deserve, earn."
