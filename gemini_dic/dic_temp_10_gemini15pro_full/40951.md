cooperate:/koʊˈɑːpəreɪt/ (US) /kəʊˈɒpəreɪt/ (UK)| v. |合作，协作|To work together towards a common goal or purpose.|例句：The two companies cooperated on the project. 两家公司合作开展这个项目。


例句：We need to cooperate with each other to achieve success. 我们需要互相合作才能取得成功。


近义词：collaborate, work together, team up, join forces|反义词：obstruct, hinder, impede, resist|词性：动词


词源：Late Middle English: from Latin cooperat- ‘worked with’, from the verb cooperari, from co- ‘together’ + operari ‘to work’.
