horologiographer:/ˌhɔːrəˈlɒdʒəɡrɑːfər/
n.
钟表学家，钟表制造者
A person who makes clocks or watches, especially one who designs or constructs the mechanism.
The horologiographer meticulously assembled the tiny gears of the antique clock.  这位钟表学家一丝不苟地组装了古董钟的微小齿轮。
Near Synonyms:  clockmaker, watchmaker,  timepiece maker
Antonyms:  None (It's difficult to have a direct opposite of someone who makes clocks)
Word Origin: From Greek *hōra* "hour" + *-logos* "speech, discourse, reason"  + *-graphos* "writer, describer"
