devouringness:/dɪˈvaʊərɪŋnɪs/
n.
贪婪; 吞噬的特性; 狼吞虎咽
The quality of being devouring or consuming eagerly and completely.
He displayed a devouringness for knowledge, reading every book he could get his hands on. 他对知识表现出极大的渴求，读遍了所有能找到的书。
voracity, rapacity, greed
satisfaction, moderation
