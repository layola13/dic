balkily:/ˈbɔːlkɪli/| adv.|倔强地；不愿地；犹豫地|In a manner that shows unwillingness or hesitation to proceed or comply.|The horse balked balkily at the jump. 这匹马在跳跃时犹豫不决地止步了。|Synonyms: reluctantly, hesitantly, unwillingly, stubbornly|Antonyms: willingly, readily, eagerly|Adverb
