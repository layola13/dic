nonpliantnesses:/nɒnˈplaɪəntnɪsɪz/
n.
不服从，不顺从；顽固；倔强
Lack of pliancy; unyielding or unbending quality.
Her nonpliantness made negotiations difficult. 她的不服从使得谈判变得困难。
Synonyms: inflexibility, rigidity, stubbornness, obstinacy, recalcitrance
Antonyms: flexibility, pliability, compliance, docility, obedience
