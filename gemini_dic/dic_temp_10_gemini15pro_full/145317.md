overbroaden:/ˌoʊvərˈbrɔːdən/
verb
过度扩展；过分概括
to broaden something excessively, making it too general or inclusive
The definition of "art" has been overbroadened to include almost anything. “艺术”的定义被过度扩展，几乎包含了任何东西。
overextend, overgeneralize
restrict, narrow, specify
v.
