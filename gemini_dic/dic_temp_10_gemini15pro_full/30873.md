chafed:/tʃeɪft/| adj.|擦伤的；恼怒的；烦躁的|Feeling irritated or annoyed.|He was chafed by their constant interruptions. 他被他们不断的打扰激怒了。|synonyms: irritated, annoyed, vexed, irked|antonyms: pleased, content, satisfied|adjective
