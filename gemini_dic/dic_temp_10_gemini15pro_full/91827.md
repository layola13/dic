hypergenetical:/ˌhaɪpərjəˈnɛtɪkəl/| adj.|超遗传的|Relating to or involving hypergenesis, the hypothetical influence of ancestral germ plasm on the development of an individual.|The scientist proposed a hypergenetical theory to explain the unusual trait.  这位科学家提出了一个超遗传理论来解释这种不寻常的特征。|近义词: transgenerational, epigenetic |反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider terms like congenital, acquired)|词性: adjective
