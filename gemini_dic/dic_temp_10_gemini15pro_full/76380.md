gadsman:/ˈɡædzmən/
n.
闲荡者；游手好闲的人
A person who roams about idly; a loafer.
He was known as a gadsman, never settling down in one place. 他被称为游手好闲的人，从不在一个地方安顿下来。
Synonyms: loafer, idler, vagrant, wanderer
Antonyms: worker, busybody
