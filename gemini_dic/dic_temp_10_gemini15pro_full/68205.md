fair-trades:/ˈfeər treɪdz/| n.|公平交易|Trade in which fair prices are paid to producers in developing countries.|I always try to buy fair-trade coffee. 我总是尽量买公平贸易的咖啡。|近义词：ethical trade, responsible trade|反义词：free trade, exploitative trade|noun


fair-trade:/ˈfeər treɪd/| adj.|公平交易的|Relating to or promoting fair trade.|This shop sells fair-trade products. 这家商店出售公平贸易产品。|近义词：ethical, responsible|反义词：unethical, exploitative|adjective
