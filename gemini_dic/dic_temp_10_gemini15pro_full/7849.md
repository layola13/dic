Antarctica: /ænˈtɑːrktɪkə/ | n. | 南极洲 | A continent surrounding the South Pole, almost entirely covered by ice. |  Antarctica is the coldest, driest, and windiest continent.  南极洲是最冷、最干燥、风最大的大陆。

Antarctica is home to penguins and seals. 南极洲是企鹅和海豹的家园。 | 近义词: South Pole (南极) | 反义词: Arctic (北极) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek ἀνταρκτικός (antarktikos, “opposite to the Arctic”), from ἀντί (anti, “opposite, against”) + ἀρκτικός (arktikos, “of the north, arctic”).
