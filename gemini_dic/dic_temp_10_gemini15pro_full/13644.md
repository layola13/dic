at once:/æt wʌns/| adv.|立刻，马上；同时|immediately; without delay; at the same time|例句：Come here at once! 马上过来！

例句：He tried to do two things at once. 他试图同时做两件事。|近义词：immediately, instantly, promptly, directly, forthwith|反义词：later, gradually, eventually|词性:副词
