overcrediting: /ˌoʊvərˈkrɛdɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 过度授信，过高评价 | The act of giving someone more credit than they deserve, either financially or figuratively. |  The bank was accused of overcrediting risky borrowers.  这家银行被指控向高风险借款人过度放贷。 | Synonyms: overestimating, overvaluing, overpraising | Antonyms: underestimating, undervaluing, underpraising | Verb

overcrediting: /ˌoʊvərˈkrɛdɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 过度授信，过高评价 | The act of giving someone more credit than they deserve, either financially or figuratively.  | The overcrediting of subprime mortgages contributed to the financial crisis. 次级抵押贷款的过度授信导致了金融危机。 | Synonyms: overestimation, overvaluation, overpraise | Antonyms: underestimation, undervaluation, underpraise | Noun
