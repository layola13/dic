be-all-ears:/biː ɔːl ɪərz/
idiomatic_adjective
全神贯注地听着；洗耳恭听
attentively listening; eager to hear what someone has to say
"Tell me everything, I'm all ears."  告诉我一切，我洗耳恭听。
Synonyms: listening intently, attentive, keen to hear
Antonyms: inattentive, indifferent, uninterested
idiom
