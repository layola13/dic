graved:/ɡreɪvd/| adj.|雕刻的，铭刻的；严肃的，庄重的|Carved or engraved; serious and dignified in appearance or demeanor.|The inscription was graved on the tombstone.  墓志铭刻在墓碑上。|Synonyms: engraved, carved, etched, inscribed, solemn, serious, dignified|Antonyms: frivolous, lighthearted|adjective

graved:/ɡreɪvd/| v.(grave的过去式和过去分词)|雕刻，铭刻；铭记|Past tense and past participle of "grave" (to carve or engrave).  Also, to fix deeply (in the mind).|He graved his initials on the tree. 他把他的姓名首字母刻在树上。|Synonyms: engraved, carved, etched, inscribed|Antonyms: erase, obliterate|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *grafan, from Proto-Germanic *grabanan, from Proto-Indo-European *ghrebh- (to dig, scratch).  Related to "groove."
