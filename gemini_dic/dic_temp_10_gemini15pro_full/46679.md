ETYMOLOGY->
From damp + -ish.  Damp (n.) from Middle Low German damp, from Proto-Germanic *dampaz.  The suffix -ish forms adjectives from nouns or adjectives, meaning "somewhat," "rather," "somewhat like," "characteristic of."
