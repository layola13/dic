nonconsequentially:/ˌnɒnkɒnsɪˈkwɛnʃəli/ /ˌnɑːnkɑːnsɪˈkwɛnʃəli/
adverb
无关紧要地，没有逻辑后果地
in a way that is not important or significant; in a way that does not follow logically
He nonconsequentially added that he liked the color blue.  他漫不经心地补充说他喜欢蓝色。
The topic drifted nonconsequentially from the main point of the meeting.  话题不知不觉地偏离了会议的主题。
近义词:insignificantly, unimportantly, irrelevantly, immateriallly
反义词:significantly, importantly, consequentially
词性:adverb
