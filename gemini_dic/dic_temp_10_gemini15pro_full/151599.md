partialism:/ˈpɑːrʃəlɪzəm/ | n. |偏爱；偏袒；对事物一部分的特殊爱好|An inclination to favor one side or aspect of a subject or question; bias. A special fondness for a particular part of something.|He showed a partialism towards abstract art. 他对抽象艺术表现出偏爱。|Near synonyms: bias, favoritism, preference, prejudice, predilection|Near antonyms: impartiality, neutrality, objectivity|noun
