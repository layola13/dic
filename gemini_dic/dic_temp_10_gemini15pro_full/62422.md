endomorph:/ˈɛndəˌmɔːrf/| n.|内胚层体型者；肥胖体型者|A person with a soft, round body build and a high proportion of body fat.|Example: Endomorphs tend to gain weight easily. 内胚层体型的人容易发胖。
Example: He's a classic endomorph, with a stocky build and a tendency to store fat. 他是典型的内胚层体型，体格粗壮，容易储存脂肪。|近义词:mesomorph(中胚层体型者)|反义词:ectomorph(外胚层体型者)|词性:名词
