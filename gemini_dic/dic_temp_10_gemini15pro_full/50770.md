desaturating:/diːˈsætʃəreɪtɪŋ/ | v. |  (使)去饱和，(使)降低饱和度 | Reducing the saturation of something, especially a color. | The photographer was desaturating the image to give it a vintage feel. 摄影师正在降低图像的饱和度，使其具有复古感。| synonyms: dulling, muting, fading, bleaching | antonyms: saturating, intensifying, brightening | verb

desaturated:/diːˈsætʃəreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 去饱和的，降低饱和度的 | Having had saturation reduced, especially of a color. | The desaturated colors of the painting created a calming effect. 这幅画去饱和的颜色营造出一种平静的效果。| synonyms: dull, muted, faded, bleached | antonyms: saturated, intense, bright | adjective
