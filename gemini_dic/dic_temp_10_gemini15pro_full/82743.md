guilder:/ˈɡɪldər/| n.|荷兰盾|The basic monetary unit of the Netherlands, formerly used in Suriname and the Netherlands Antilles.  A former gold coin current in the Netherlands and other countries.|例句：He paid ten guilders for the book. 他花了十荷兰盾买了这本书。

例句：The painting was valued at thousands of guilders. 这幅画估价数千荷兰盾。|近义词：florin, Dutch guilder|反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of currency) |词性:noun
