impressionability:/ɪmˌprɛʃəˈnəbɪləti/
n.
易受影响；可塑性；敏感性
The quality of being easily influenced or impressed.
Her youthful impressionability made her vulnerable to peer pressure. 她年轻时的易受影响使她很容易受到同伴的压力。
suggestibility, sensitivity, plasticity, receptiveness, responsiveness
resistance, immunity, imperviousness, insensitivity
noun
