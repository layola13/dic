aliener:/ˈeɪliənər/ | adj. | 更疏远的，更陌生的 |  Making something or someone feel more alienated or estranged.  It implies an increasing sense of separation or isolation. | The new policy further aliened the minority groups.  这项新政策进一步疏远了少数群体。 |  estranged, isolated, distant | closer, connected | adjective
Etymology:  From the Latin word "alienus" meaning "belonging to another," "foreign," or "strange."  The "-er" suffix denotes a comparative degree, meaning "more alien."
