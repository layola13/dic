bloat:/bloʊt/| v. n.|膨胀，肿胀；使膨胀，使肿胀|To swell or cause to swell, typically by filling with liquid or gas.  To make something larger or more important than it needs to be.|The dead whale bloated and floated to the surface. 死鲸膨胀起来，漂浮到水面。|synonyms: swell, distend, inflate, expand |antonyms: deflate, shrink, contract|verb

bloat:/bloʊt/| n.|膨胀；肿胀|The state of being swollen or distended.|The bloat was evident in his face and ankles. 他的脸和脚踝明显肿胀。|synonyms: swelling, distension, inflation|antonyms: deflation, contraction|noun
