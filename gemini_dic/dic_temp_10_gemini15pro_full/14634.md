avoiding:/əˈvɔɪdɪŋ/
verb (present participle)|避免，回避|The act of keeping away from something or someone.|He's avoiding eye contact. 他在避免眼神接触.|eschewing, dodging, eluding, evading|confronting, encountering
word origin-> From Middle English avoiden, avoyden, from Anglo-Norman avoider, esveider, from Vulgar Latin *exvidāre, from Latin ex- + viduāre (“to deprive, bereave, make desolate, make empty”)

USAGE->
