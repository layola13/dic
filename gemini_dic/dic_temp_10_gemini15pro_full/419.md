abjure: /əbˈdʒʊr/ /əbˈdʒər/ | vt. |放弃，弃绝，公开放弃|To solemnly renounce or give up; to reject or deny under oath. |He abjured his former beliefs. 他放弃了他以前的信仰。

The prince abjured his claim to the throne. 王子放弃了对王位的要求。

近义词: renounce, repudiate, forsake, relinquish
反义词: embrace, affirm, uphold, maintain
词性: 动词
