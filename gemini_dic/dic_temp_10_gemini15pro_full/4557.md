alchemistic:/ˌælˈkɛmɪstɪk/
adjective
炼金术的; 炼丹术的
Relating to or characteristic of alchemy or alchemists.
The alchemistic search for the philosopher's stone.  炼金术士对点金石的探寻。

The novel describes an alchemistic process for transforming lead into gold. 这部小说描述了一个将铅转化为金的炼金术过程。

Synonyms:  magical, mystical, occult, esoteric
Antonyms: scientific, empirical, rational

Etymology: From Late Latin alchemisticus, from alchemia "alchemy."
