anthropomorphism: /ˌænθrəpəˈmɔːrfɪzəm/ | n. |拟人化，人格化|The attribution of human characteristics or behavior to a god, animal, or object.|例句：The cartoon uses anthropomorphism to give the animals human-like qualities. 这部动画片运用拟人化赋予动物们人类的品质。

例句：Anthropomorphism is a common literary device used to make animals more relatable. 拟人化是一种常见的文学手法，用于使动物更具亲和力。|近义词：personification, humanization|反义词: objectification, dehumanization|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἄνθρωπος (ánthrōpos, “human being”) + μορφή (morphḗ, “form, shape”).
