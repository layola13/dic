lianam: /liˈænəm/ | n. | 藤本植物; 藤蔓 | A woody climbing plant that hangs from trees. |  The monkey swung from lianam to lianam. 猴子从一根藤蔓荡到另一根藤蔓。| 近义词: vine, creeper | 反义词: tree, shrub | 词性: 名词

词源：Lianam comes from a Carib word meaning "to tie, to bind."  It entered English via French *liane*.
