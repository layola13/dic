overutilizing: /ˌoʊvərˈjuːtəˌlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 过度使用，过度利用 | Using something too much or too often. |  The company is overutilizing its resources and needs to find more sustainable practices.  这家公司过度使用其资源，需要寻找更可持续的做法。 | overusing, exploiting | underutilizing, neglecting | verb

overutilizing: /ˌoʊvərˈjuːtəˌlaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度使用的，过度利用的 (较少用作形容词，通常使用 present participle form "overutilizing" 作形容词) | Characterized by excessive use. | The overutilizing of antibiotics can lead to drug resistance.  过度使用抗生素会导致耐药性。| overused, exploited | underutilized, neglected | adjective
