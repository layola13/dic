equivocates:/ɪˈkwɪvəkeɪts/
|v.|含糊其辞，模棱两可，说模棱两可的话|To use ambiguous language so as to conceal the truth or avoid committing oneself.|He equivocated when asked about his stance on the issue, refusing to give a direct answer. 他在被问及他对这个问题的立场时含糊其辞，拒绝给出直接的答复。|The politician equivocated about the scandal, hoping to avoid further scrutiny.  这位政治家对丑闻含糊其辞，希望避免进一步的审查。|prevaricates, hedges, avoids|states clearly, affirms, commits|verb
