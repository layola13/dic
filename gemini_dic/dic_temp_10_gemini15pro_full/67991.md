factionalisms:/ˈfækʃənəlɪzəmz/
n.
派系主义；派性斗争
The tendency to form or promote factions; the state of being split into factions.
The party was torn apart by factionalisms.  这个党被派系斗争撕裂了。
synonyms:  infighting, dissension, strife, discord, division, schism, sectarianism
antonyms: unity, harmony, agreement, consensus, solidarity
noun
