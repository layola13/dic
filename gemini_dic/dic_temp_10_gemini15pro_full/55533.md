distributional:/ˌdɪstrɪˈbjuːʃənl/ | adj. | 分布的，分配的 | Relating to the distribution of something, especially income or wealth, or the way in which something is spread over an area. |  The study examined the distributional effects of the new tax policy. 这项研究考察了新税收政策的分配效应。| synonyms: distributive, allocative, apportioned | antonyms: concentrated, centralized | adjective

distributional:/ˌdɪstrɪˈbjuːʃənl/ | adj. |  (语言学)分布的 | Relating to the distribution of linguistic elements (sounds, words, grammatical structures) in a language or corpus.  |  Distributional analysis can reveal patterns in language use. 分布分析可以揭示语言使用中的模式。| synonyms: positional, arrangement, layout | antonyms:  N/A (This sense doesn't have a direct antonym) | adjective
