nonreducing:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈdjuːsɪŋ/
adjective
非还原性的
Not causing reduction, especially not able to donate electrons to other substances in a chemical reaction.
Sucrose is a nonreducing sugar. 蔗糖是一种非还原性糖。
Nonreducing disaccharides like sucrose do not react with Benedict's reagent.  像蔗糖这样的非还原性二糖不会与本尼迪克特试剂反应。
近义词: non-oxidizing
反义词: reducing
