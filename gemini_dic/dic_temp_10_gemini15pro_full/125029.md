muji:/ˈmuːdʒiː (moo-jee)/
n.|无印良品 (品牌名), 无印, 无牌, 素净|A Japanese retail company known for its minimalist design and philosophy of "no-brand" quality goods.|I bought a new sweater from Muji. 我从无印良品买了一件新毛衣。|Synonyms: plain, unbranded, unmarked, brandless|Antonyms: branded, labeled, marked|noun
