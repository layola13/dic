exedent:/ɛkˈsiːdənt/ | adj. | 突出,超过的；多余的 | Projecting or extending beyond the normal or usual limit; exceeding what is necessary. | The rabbit's exedent teeth were very noticeable. 兔子的突出牙齿很明显。  The exedent growth on the tree needed to be trimmed. 树上多余的生长需要修剪。 | 近义词: protruding, prominent, superfluous, excessive | 反义词: receding, deficient, insufficient | 词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *exedent-, present participle of *exedere "to project," from ex- "out" + edere "to eat."  Originally referred to teeth that projected beyond the normal line, allowing for continuous gnawing.  The meaning later broadened to include anything that projects or extends beyond a normal limit.
