haddie:/ˈhædi/
noun
熏黑线鳕
smoked haddock, a type of fish
She ordered haddie and chips for dinner. 她晚餐点了熏黑线鳕和薯条。
近义词:smoked fish, finnan haddie
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用fresh fish 表示未经熏制的鱼)
词性:n.


Etymology:
From Scots *haddie, *haddow, from Middle Dutch schellevis, literally "shellfish", probably influenced by Middle English haddock.
