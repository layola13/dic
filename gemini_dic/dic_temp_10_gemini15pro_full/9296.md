antiphysical:/ˌæntiˈfɪzɪkəl/
adjective
反物质的; 反物理的; 非物质的; 超自然的
opposed to or rejecting the physical or material.  Characterized by a disregard for the laws of physics. Also, relating to things beyond the physical realm.
His antiphysical beliefs led him to believe he could levitate. 他反物理的信仰使他相信自己可以漂浮。
The spiritualist described an antiphysical realm beyond human comprehension.  灵媒描述了一个人类无法理解的超自然领域。
近义词: immaterial, spiritual, metaphysical, supernatural
反义词: physical, material, tangible
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + physical.  "Anti-" is a prefix of Greek origin meaning "against," "opposite of," or "opposed to." "Physical" derives from the Late Latin "physicus" and ultimately from the Greek "physikos," meaning "of nature."
