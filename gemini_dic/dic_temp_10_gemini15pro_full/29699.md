cathodograph:/ˈkæθədəɡræf/ | n. |阴极射线示波图；示波图| A visual record or image produced by a cathode-ray oscilloscope.  It displays the variations of an electrical quantity as a function of time or another quantity.| The cathodograph showed a sharp spike in voltage. 阴极射线示波图显示电压急剧上升。 | synonym: oscilloscope trace, waveform display | antonym: (No direct antonym exists, perhaps consider terms like "static image" or "unchanging value") | noun
