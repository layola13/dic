ego-trippers:/ˈiːɡoʊˌtrɪpərz/| n.|  自我陶醉者；自恋狂；| People who are excessively focused on themselves and their own importance, often seeking admiration and validation from others.  They tend to boast about their accomplishments and downplay the contributions of others.| He's such an ego-tripper, always talking about himself and his achievements. 他真是个自恋狂，总是谈论他自己和他的成就。|The awards ceremony was full of ego-trippers, each vying for attention.  颁奖典礼上挤满了自恋狂，每个人都在争夺关注。|synonyms: narcissist, egotist, show-off, braggart| antonyms:  humble person, altruist| noun
