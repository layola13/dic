mediaeval:/ˌmediˈiːvəl/ /ˌmɪdˈiːvəl/| adj.|中世纪的，中古时代的|Of or relating to the Middle Ages (c. AD 476–1453).|例句：Mediaeval architecture is characterized by soaring arches and stained glass. 中世纪建筑的特点是高耸的拱门和彩色玻璃。

例句：He was a scholar of mediaeval history. 他是一位中世纪历史学者。


近义词：medieval|反义词:modern, contemporary|词性:adjective
