exorcisory:/ˈeksɔːrsɪzəri/ /ˈeksɔːrsəˌtɔːri/| adj.|驱魔的，驱邪的|Relating to or used in exorcism; tending to drive out evil spirits.|The priest performed an exorcisory ritual.  神父举行了驱魔仪式。|Synonyms: cleansing, purifying, ridding|Antonyms:  inviting, welcoming|adjective

exorcism:/ˈeksɔːrsɪzəm/ /ˈeksɔːrˌsɪzəm/| n.|驱魔，驱邪|The act or practice of driving out evil spirits from a person or place, especially by incantation or prayer.|The family called in a priest to perform an exorcism. 这家人请了一位牧师来驱魔。|Synonyms: purification, cleansing, deliverance|Antonyms: possession, summoning|noun
