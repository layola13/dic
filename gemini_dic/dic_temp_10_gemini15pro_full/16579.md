barning: /ˈbɑːrnɪŋ/
adjective
燃烧的; 强烈的; 急切的
burning describes something on fire, or a very strong emotion or sensation.
The house was burning fiercely. 房子猛烈地燃烧着。
She had a burning desire to travel the world. 她渴望环游世界。
synonyms: blazing, fiery, intense, passionate
antonyms: extinguished, cold, apathetic
词性: 形容词
