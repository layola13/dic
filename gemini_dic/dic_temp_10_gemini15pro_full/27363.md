cambridge:/ˈkeɪmbrɪdʒ/| n.|剑桥|A city in eastern England, home to the University of Cambridge.|I studied at Cambridge. 我在剑桥大学学习。|近义词:university town|反义词:rural area|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *Grantabrycg "bridge over the Granta" (the river's original name), later Grantebrycge, Cantebrigge, and then Cambridge. The river later became known as the Cam, likely a back-formation from the name of the town.
