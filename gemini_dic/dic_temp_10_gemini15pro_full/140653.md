omasum:/oʊˈmeɪsəm/ | n. |重瓣胃；牛百叶|The third compartment of the ruminant stomach, between the reticulum and the abomasum. It receives food from the reticulum and passes it to the abomasum.  It absorbs water and some nutrients. | The omasum in cows helps them digest tough plant material.  牛的重瓣胃帮助它们消化坚韧的植物材料。| Synonym: psalterium, manyplies | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for an anatomical part) | noun
