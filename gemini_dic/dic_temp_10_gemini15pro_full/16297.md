bar-coding:/ˈbɑːrˌkoʊdɪŋ/ | n. | 条形码编码，条码化 | The process of representing data with a machine-readable pattern of parallel lines and spaces of varying widths. |  Supermarkets use bar-coding to speed up checkout. 超市使用条形码来加快结账速度。| synonyms: bar code scanning, optical scanning | antonyms: manual data entry | noun

bar-coding:/ˈbɑːrˌkoʊdɪŋ/ | n. | 条码技术 | The technology or system of bar-coding. | The introduction of bar-coding revolutionized inventory management. 条形码技术的引入彻底改变了库存管理。| synonyms: bar code system, optical scanning technology | antonyms: manual inventory system | noun
