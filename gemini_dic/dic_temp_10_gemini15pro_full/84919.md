hanoverian:/ˌhænəˈvɪəriən/
adjective
汉诺威的；汉诺威王朝的；汉诺威马的
Relating to Hanover, Germany, its ruling dynasty, or its breed of horses.
The Hanoverian dynasty ruled Great Britain and Ireland from 1714 to 1901.  汉诺威王朝从1714年到1901年统治着大不列颠和爱尔兰。
He admired the elegant gait of the Hanoverian horse. 他欣赏汉诺威马优雅的步态。
synonyms:German, Teutonic
antonyms:non-German, foreign
noun
汉诺威人；汉诺威马
A native or inhabitant of Hanover, Germany; a horse of a breed originating in Hanover, known for its strength and elegance.
The Hanoverian was known for his horsemanship.  这位汉诺威人以其骑术而闻名。
She rode a magnificent Hanoverian in the dressage competition.  她在盛装舞步比赛中骑着一匹雄壮的汉诺威马。
synonyms:horse, steed
antonyms:None
