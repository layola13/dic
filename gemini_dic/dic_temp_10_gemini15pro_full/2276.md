adenocarcinoma:/ˌædɪnoʊˌkɑːrsɪˈnoʊmə/| n. |腺癌|A type of cancerous tumor that originates in glandular tissue, which is tissue that lines certain organs and makes and releases substances such as mucus, digestive juices, or other fluids. |例句：Lung adenocarcinoma is the most common type of lung cancer. 肺腺癌是最常见的肺癌类型。

Her biopsy revealed an adenocarcinoma of the breast. 她的活检显示乳腺腺癌。|近义词：cancer, carcinoma, tumor, malignancy |反义词: benign tumor|词性:noun
