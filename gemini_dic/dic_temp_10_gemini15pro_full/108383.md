law-worthy:/ˈlɔːˌwɜːrði/
adjective
值得立法的；适合入法的
Fit to be made or become law; suitable for legislation.
例句：The committee deemed the proposal law-worthy and recommended it to the legislature.  委员会认为该提案值得立法，并将其推荐给了立法机关。

例句：Their actions, though unconventional, were deemed law-worthy due to the unique circumstances. 他们的行为虽然不寻常，但由于特殊情况被认为适合入法。

近义词：legitimate, legal, justifiable
反义词：illegal, unlawful, illegitimate
词性: adjective
