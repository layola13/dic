malice:/ˈmælɪs/| n.|恶意，怨恨|The crime was committed with malice aforethought.  这起犯罪是预谋的。|Desire to inflict injury, harm, or suffering on another, either because of a hostile feeling or out of deep-seated meanness.|She spread the rumors out of pure malice. 她纯粹出于恶意散播谣言。|近义词：spite, malevolence, animosity, rancor|反义词：benevolence, goodwill, kindness|noun
