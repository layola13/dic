chirographical:/ˌkaɪrəˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
手写的；书法体的
Relating to handwriting; written by hand.
His chirographical skills were impeccable, each letter perfectly formed. 他的书法技巧无可挑剔，每个字母都写得完美无缺。
The chirographical evidence suggested the letter was authentic. 笔迹证据表明这封信是真的。
Synonyms: handwritten, penned, scriptory, manuscript
Antonyms: typed, printed
词性: 形容词


chirography:/kaɪˈrɑɡrəfi/
noun
书法；笔迹
Handwriting; the art or style of handwriting.
Her chirography was elegant and flowing. 她的书法优雅流畅。
The expert in chirography examined the signature carefully. 书法专家仔细检查了签名。
Synonyms: handwriting, penmanship, script
Antonyms: typing, printing
词性: 名词
