connusant:/ˈkɒnəsənt/ (US) /ˈkɑːnəsənt/| adj.|知晓的，了解的|Having knowledge of something; well-informed about a particular subject or situation.|She was connusant of all the latest developments in the field. 她了解该领域的所有最新发展。|近义词：aware, informed, knowledgeable, cognizant|反义词：ignorant, unaware, uninformed|词性:形容词

词源: From Late Latin *connūsāns*, present participle of *connōscere* (“to know well”), from Latin *con-* (“with, together”) + *nōscere* (“to get to know”).
