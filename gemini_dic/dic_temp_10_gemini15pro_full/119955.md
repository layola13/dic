millefiore:/ˌmɪleɪˈfjɔːreɪ/ /ˌmɪləˈfjɔːri/
noun
千花玻璃；千花粉饰；千花锦
A glassmaking technique in which rods of colored glass are fused together, then sliced to create patterns, especially floral ones. Also, decorative objects made using this technique.
例句：The museum boasts a stunning collection of millefiore paperweights. 博物馆收藏了令人惊叹的千花玻璃镇纸。

例句：She wore a beautiful millefiore pendant. 她戴着一个漂亮的千花玻璃吊坠。
近义词：mosaic, tessellation
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "monochromatic" in terms of visual effect)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *millefiori*, from *mille* ("thousand") + *fiori* ("flowers").
