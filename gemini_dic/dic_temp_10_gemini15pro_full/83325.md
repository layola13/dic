gypsy: /ˈdʒɪpsi/ (US), /ˈdʒɪpsi/ (UK)| n. |吉普赛人；流浪者；<贬>骗子|A member of a traditionally nomadic people originating in northern India, now living mostly in Europe and North America and noted for their traditional skills as musicians, fortune-tellers, and metalworkers.  This term is now considered outdated and often offensive, and Roma or Romani is preferred. |例句：The gypsy told my fortune. 吉普赛人给我算命。

例句：They lived a gypsy life, traveling from town to town. 他们过着吉普赛式的生活，从一个城镇到另一个城镇旅行。|近义词：Roma, Romani, traveler, nomad|反义词:settled person|词性:noun
