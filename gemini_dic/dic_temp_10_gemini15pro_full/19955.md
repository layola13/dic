biodynamics:/ˌbaɪoʊdaɪˈnæmɪks/
n.
生物动力学; 生物力学
A system of farming that treats the farm as a unified and largely self-sustaining organism. It emphasizes the use of manures and composts and excludes the use of artificial fertilizers, pesticides, and herbicides.
例句：
The vineyard uses biodynamics practices to cultivate their grapes.
这个葡萄园采用生物动力法种植葡萄。

Biodynamics is gaining popularity among environmentally conscious consumers.
生物动力法越来越受到环保意识消费者的欢迎。

近义词：organic farming, sustainable agriculture
反义词：conventional farming
词性：名词
