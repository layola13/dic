barothermograph:/ˈbɛrəʊˈθɜːməɡrɑːf/ /ˈbɛroʊˈθɜːrmoʊɡræf/
n.|气压温度记录器|An instrument that records both atmospheric pressure and temperature on a single chart.|The barothermograph showed a steady drop in pressure and a rise in temperature before the storm. 气压温度记录器显示，暴风雨来临前，气压持续下降，温度上升。|barograph, thermograph,  hygrothermograph| None|noun
Etymology: baro- (pressure) + thermo- (heat) + -graph (writing instrument)
