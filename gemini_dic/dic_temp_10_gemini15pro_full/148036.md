overrude: /ˌoʊvərˈruːd/ | adj. | 过分粗鲁的，极其无礼的 | Excessively rude; displaying a marked lack of politeness or consideration for others.  It combines "over" (meaning excessively) and "rude." |  His behavior was overrude, even for him. 他的行为太粗鲁了，即使对他来说也是如此。

She was overrude to the waiter, complaining loudly about everything. 她对服务员极其粗鲁，大声抱怨所有事情。 | 近义词：insolent, impertinent, impudent, disrespectful, cheeky | 反义词：polite, courteous, respectful | 词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (Old English *ofer-, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "above, beyond") + "rude" (Middle English rued, from Old French rude, ultimately from Latin rudis, meaning "rough, crude, unlearned").
