noosed:/nuːst/
adjective
套索的；被套索套住的
Having a noose or having been caught in a noose.
The noosed rope hung ominously from the tree.  套索的绳子不祥地悬挂在树上。
The cowboy skillfully noosed the calf. 牛仔熟练地用套索套住了小牛。
Synonyms: trapped, ensnared, lassoed
Antonyms: freed, released
词性: adjective
