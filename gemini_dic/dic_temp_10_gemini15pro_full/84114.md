half-liberally:/ˌhæfˈlɪbərəli/ | adv. | 半自由地，不完全自由地 |  Done or given in a way that is only partly generous or open-minded; not completely liberal.  It suggests a reluctance or reservation in being liberal. | He half-liberally offered to help, but with so many conditions attached that it was practically useless. 他勉强提出帮忙，但附加了这么多条件，实际上毫无用处。 |  The government half-liberally loosened some restrictions, but kept tight control over other aspects. 政府不情愿地放宽了一些限制，但在其他方面仍然保持着严格的控制。 | 近义词: partially liberally, somewhat liberally, not quite liberally | 反义词: liberally, generously, wholeheartedly | 词性: 副词
