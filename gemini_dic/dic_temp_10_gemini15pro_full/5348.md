alluding:/əˈluːdɪŋ/
verb
暗指，间接提到
suggesting something indirectly; hinting at
He was alluding to the recent scandal. 他在暗指最近的丑闻。
synonyms: hinting, suggesting, implying, intimating
antonyms: stating, declaring, specifying, articulating
v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin alludere "to play with, jest, allude," from ad- "to" + ludere "to play."
