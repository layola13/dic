burdock:/ˈbɜːrdɒk/ (UK) /ˈbɜːrdɑːk/ (US)| n. |牛蒡|A large, coarse plant with prickly flower heads that stick to clothing and fur.  The root of some kinds of burdock is eaten as a vegetable.|The boy's sweater was covered in burdocks after his walk through the field.  男孩的毛衣在穿过田野后沾满了牛蒡。|Synonyms:  cocklebur, clotbur|Antonyms: N/A|noun

burdocks:/ˈbɜːrdɒks/ (UK) /ˈbɜːrdɑːks/ (US)| n. |牛蒡（复数）|Plural form of burdock, referring to more than one burdock plant.|The farmer had to clear the field of burdocks before plowing.  农民在犁地之前必须清除田里的牛蒡。|Synonyms: cockleburs, clotburs|Antonyms: N/A|noun
