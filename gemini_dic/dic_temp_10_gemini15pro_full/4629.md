alder:/ˈɔːldər/ (US) /ˈɔːldə(r)/ (UK)| n.|桤木; 赤杨|A tree of the birch family that grows in wet ground, typically having catkins and small rounded fruits. The wood is often used for furniture and construction because of its resistance to rot in water.|The alder trees lined the riverbank. 河岸边排列着桤木。|Synonyms: birch, hazel|Antonyms: N/A|noun


alderman:/ˈɔːldərmən/ (US) /ˈɔːldəmən/ (UK)| n.|市议员; 市政官|A member of a municipal council, especially in England, Wales, and Ireland.|The alderman addressed the town's concerns. 市议员处理了镇上的问题。|Synonyms: councilor, councilman, selectman|Antonyms: N/A|noun
