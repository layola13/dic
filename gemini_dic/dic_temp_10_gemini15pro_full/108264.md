launderabilities:/ˌlɔːndərəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 可洗性；耐洗性 | The launderabilities of this fabric are excellent, meaning it can withstand frequent washing without losing its shape or color.  这种织物的耐洗性非常好，这意味着它可以承受频繁的洗涤而不变形或褪色。| This shirt's launderabilities are poor; it shrinks after just one wash. 这件衬衫的耐洗性很差，洗一次就缩水了。| 近义词：washability, durability | 反义词: fragility, delicateness | 词性: 名词

词源:  From "launder" (to wash and iron clothes) + "-able" (suffix indicating capability) + "-ities" (suffix indicating plural).  源自“launder”（洗涤和熨烫衣服）+“-able”（表示能力的后缀）+“-ities”（表示复数的后缀）。
