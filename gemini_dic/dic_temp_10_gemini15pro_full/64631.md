erythric:/ɪˈrɪθrɪk/ | adj. |  红色的，红褐色的 | Relating to or containing erythrite, a reddish mineral, or any of its derivatives;  of or relating to the color red. | The erythric stage of sunburn is characterized by redness and pain.  晒伤的红斑期以发红和疼痛为特征。| synonyms: reddish, ruby, scarlet | antonyms: pale, colorless | adjective

erythrite:/ˈɛrɪθraɪt/ | n. |  钴华，砷钴矿 | A pink to crimson hydrated cobalt arsenate mineral. | Erythrite is often found as a secondary mineral in cobalt deposits. 钴华通常作为次生矿物存在于钴矿床中。| synonyms: cobalt bloom | antonyms: N/A | noun
