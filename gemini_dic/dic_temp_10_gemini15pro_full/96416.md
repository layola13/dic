indisputablenesses:/ˌɪndɪˈspjuːtəbəlnəsɪz/ | n. | 不可争辩性；不容置疑性 | The quality or state of being indisputable; the fact that something cannot be questioned or argued against. |  The indisputablenesses of the evidence led to a swift conviction.  这些证据的无可争议性导致了迅速的定罪。| synonym: certainty, conclusiveness, irrefutability | antonym: disputability, uncertainty, questionability | noun

indisputable: /ˌɪndɪˈspjuːtəbəl/ | adj. | 不可争辩的；不容置疑的 |  Not open to question; undeniable. | His talent is indisputable. 他的才华无可争辩。 | synonym: undeniable, unquestionable, irrefutable, incontestable | antonym: disputable, questionable, debatable, contestable | adjective
