half-demolished:/ˌhɑːf dɪˈmɒlɪʃt/
adj.
半拆除的，部分拆毁的
partly demolished; torn down or destroyed partially.
The half-demolished building stood as a stark reminder of the war.  这栋半拆毁的建筑是战争的残酷提醒。
The earthquake left many houses half-demolished. 地震使许多房屋半拆毁。
Synonyms: partially demolished, partly destroyed, partly wrecked
Antonyms: intact, complete, whole
词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
half (from Old English healf) + demolished (from French démolir, from Latin demoliri "to tear down," from de- "down" + moliri "to build," from moles "mass, structure")
