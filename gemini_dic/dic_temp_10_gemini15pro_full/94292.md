impertinent: /ɪmˈpɜːrtɪnənt/ (US) /ɪmˈpɜːtɪnənt/ (UK)
adjective
不恰当的，粗鲁的，无礼的
Not showing proper respect; rude.
例句：It was impertinent of him to ask such personal questions.  他问这么私人的问题很不礼貌。

例句：She found his tone impertinent and offensive. 她觉得他的语气傲慢无礼，令人反感。

近义词：rude, insolent, disrespectful, impudent, cheeky
反义词：polite, respectful, courteous, civil
词性：adjective
