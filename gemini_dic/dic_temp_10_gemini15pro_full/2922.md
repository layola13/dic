advising:/ədˈvaɪzɪŋ/
v.
劝告；忠告；通知；建议
present participle of advise
He is advising me on how to invest my money. = 他正在就如何投资我的钱向我提供建议。
She spent the afternoon advising students on their course choices. = 她下午花了时间为学生们提供课程选择的建议。
近义词:counseling, guiding, recommending, suggesting
反义词:dissuading
词性:动名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is advising his clients on financial matters. = 他正在为他的客户提供财务方面的建议。
She advises students on their career choices. = 她为学生提供职业选择方面的建议。

一般过去时:
He advised me to invest in the stock market. = 他建议我投资股票市场。
They advised against traveling during the storm. = 他们建议不要在暴风雨期间旅行。

现在完成时:
She has been advising the company for several years. = 她已经为这家公司提供咨询服务好几年了。
I have advised him to be more careful with his spending. = 我建议他要更谨慎地花钱。

过去完成时:
He had been advising her for months before she finally made a decision. = 在她最终做出决定之前，他已经建议她好几个月了。
They had advised against the merger before the deal was finalized. = 在交易最终敲定之前，他们曾建议反对合并。

将来时:
I will be advising him on his next steps. = 我将就他的下一步行动给他提供建议。
She will advise us on the best course of action. = 她会建议我们采取最佳行动方案。

虚拟语气:
If I were advising him, I would tell him to quit his job. = 如果是我在建议他，我会告诉他辞职。
I wish I had been advising her earlier. = 我希望我早点给她建议。

被动语态:
I was advised to seek legal counsel. = 我被建议寻求法律顾问。
He is being advised by several experts. = 他正在接受几位专家的建议。


PHRASE->
advising on = 就...提供建议
advising against = 建议反对...
is advising = 正在建议
was advising = 过去正在建议
will be advising = 将会建议
