manned:/mænd/
adj.
载人的；由人操纵的
Operated or controlled by a human being; occupied by a human crew.
The first manned mission to Mars is planned for the next decade.  第一个载人火星任务计划于未来十年进行。
The control room monitored the manned spacecraft. 控制室监控着载人航天器。

近义词: crewed, human-operated, piloted
反义词: uncrewed, unmanned, robotic, automated
词性: adjective
