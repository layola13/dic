centenarians:/ˌsɛntɪˈnɛəriənz/
n.
百岁老人
people who have reached the age of 100 or more.
There are more centenarians now than ever before. 现在百岁老人比以往任何时候都多。
The study focused on the lifestyle of centenarians. 这项研究的重点是百岁老人的生活方式。
近义词:none (a precise synonym is rare)
反义词:none (a precise antonym is rare)
词性:noun


Etymology:
From Latin *centenarius* "containing a hundred," from *centum* "hundred."
