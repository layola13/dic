noncontinuance:/ˌnɒnkənˈtɪnjʊəns/ | n. | 中止，停止；不继续 | The noncontinuance of the program was due to lack of funding.  该计划的终止是由于缺乏资金。 |  The noncontinuance of his employment came as a shock. 他被解雇的消息令人震惊。 | 近义词: discontinuation, cessation, termination | 反义词: continuation,  persistence | 词性: 名词

词源:  From non- + continuance.  "Continuance" comes from Old French "continuance," from "continuer" (to continue), ultimately from Latin "continuare" (to make continuous).
