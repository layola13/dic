cocky:| /ˈkɒki/ (UK) /ˈkɑːki/ (US)| adj.|骄傲自大的，过于自信的|Derived from "cock" (male chicken), referring to the strutting and self-assured demeanor of a rooster.  Over time, the meaning extended to describe people exhibiting similar behavior.|She had a cocky attitude after winning the competition. 她在赢得比赛后表现得非常自大。|例句：His cocky demeanor rubbed some people the wrong way. 他自大的举止让一些人很不舒服。|近义词：brash, bold, audacious|反义词：diffident, timid, insecure|词性:形容词
