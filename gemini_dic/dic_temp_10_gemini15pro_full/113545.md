macacus: /məˈkeɪkəs/
noun
猕猴
A genus of Old World monkeys including rhesus monkeys and macaques.
The macacus is a common sight in many parts of Asia.  猕猴在亚洲许多地方很常见。
Synonyms: macaque, monkey
Antonyms: None (Animals don't typically have direct antonyms)
