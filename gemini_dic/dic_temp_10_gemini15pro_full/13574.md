attendingly:/əˈtɛndɪŋli/
|adv.|专心地，聚精会神地|paying close attention; attentively.|She listened attendingly to the speaker. 她专心致志地听着演讲者讲话。|近义词: attentively, carefully, heedfully, observantly|反义词: inattentively, distractedly, carelessly|词性:副词
词源: From attend + -ing + -ly.  Attend comes from the Latin attendere "to stretch toward, give heed to," from ad- "to" + tendere "to stretch."
