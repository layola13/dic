bellboys: /ˈbelbɔɪz/ | n. | 男服务员（通常指酒店的）| A hotel employee, usually male, who performs services for guests, such as carrying luggage and taking messages. | The bellboys quickly whisked our bags up to our room. 男服务员迅速把我们的行李送到房间。|  The bellboy helped us check in and explained the hotel facilities. 男服务员帮我们办理入住，并讲解了酒店的设施。| Synonyms: porter, page | Antonyms: guest | noun
