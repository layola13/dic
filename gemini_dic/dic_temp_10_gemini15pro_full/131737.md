nonconfirmations:/ˌnɑːnkɑːnfərˈmeɪʃənz/| n.|不符合项；不一致项；未确认项|Instances where something does not meet the expected standards, requirements, or specifications.  It refers to situations where there is a discrepancy or disagreement, or where something lacks official validation.|The audit revealed several nonconfirmations in the company's financial records. 审计揭示了公司财务记录中的几处不符合项。|synonyms: discrepancies, inconsistencies, variances, exceptions, deviations|antonyms: confirmations, agreements, consistencies, compliances|noun
