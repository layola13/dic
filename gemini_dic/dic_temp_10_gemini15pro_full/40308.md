contortion:/kənˈtɔːrʃən/ /kənˈtɔːrʃn/
n.
扭弯，扭曲；歪曲
the act of twisting or bending something out of its normal shape.
The contortion of the metal bars was caused by the intense heat. 金属棒的扭曲是由高温造成的。

contortion:/kənˈtɔːrʃən/ /kənˈtɔːrʃn/
n.
（身体的）扭曲，弯曲
a twisted or bent condition; a twisting or writhing movement.
The gymnast's contortions were amazing to watch. 体操运动员的弯曲动作令人惊叹。

Etymology: Late 16th century: from French contorsion, from late Latin contortio(n-), from contort- ‘twisted’, from the verb contorquere.

近义词:twist, bend, warp, deformation, distortion
反义词:straightening, alignment

词性: noun
