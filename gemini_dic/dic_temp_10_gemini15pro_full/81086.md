gralline:/ˈɡrælaɪn/ | n. | 灰线，细线；（尤指）地图上的等深线，等高线 | A thin line, especially a contour line on a map. | The grallines on the map showed the different depths of the ocean. 地图上的等深线显示了海洋的不同深度。| Example: The hikers followed the grallines on their map to reach the summit.  徒步旅行者沿着地图上的等高线到达山顶。|Synonyms: contour line, isopleth | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "area" or "region" as opposites of a line) | noun
