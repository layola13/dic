hypostoma:/haɪˈpɒstəmə/ | n. |口下片，下唇，（三叶虫的）唇瓣|A plate-like structure forming part of the mouthparts of certain invertebrates, such as trilobites and some crustaceans.  It is situated on the ventral side of the head, below the mouth.  |The hypostoma of this trilobite is well-preserved.  这只三叶虫的口下片保存完好。|Synonyms: labrum (in some contexts), lip (informal) |Antonyms: N/A |noun
