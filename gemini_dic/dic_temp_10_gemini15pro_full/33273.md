chrysogen:/ˈkraɪsɒdʒən/ | n. |金黄色素;菊花黄|A substance that stimulates the formation of carotenoids and thus the yellowing of flowers, especially in chrysanthemums.|Chrysogen is used to enhance the yellow color in certain flowers. 菊花黄被用来增强某些花的黄色。|Chrysogen promotes the biosynthesis of carotenoids in plants, leading to yellow pigmentation. 菊花黄促进植物中类胡萝卜素的生物合成，从而导致黄色色素沉着。 |Near synonyms: carotenoid precursor, xanthophyll precursor|Antonym: N/A|noun
