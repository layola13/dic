nonrepresentatively: /ˌnɒnˌrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪvli/ | adv. | 非典型地，不具有代表性地 |  In a way that is not representative; not typical of a particular group or thing.  |  The survey was conducted nonrepresentatively, so the results are not reliable. 这项调查进行得没有代表性，所以结果不可靠。 | Synonyms: atypically, untypically | Antonyms: typically, representatively | adverb
