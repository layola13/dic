The state of being hale and healthy.
His halenesses surprised everyone after his long illness. 他久病之后恢复健康令所有人惊讶。
近义词:health, wellness, fitness, soundness

词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *halnes, from Old English *hālnes (“wholeness, soundness, health”), from hāl (“whole, sound, healthy”) + -nes, equivalent to hale +‎ -ness. Cognate with Scots haleness (“health, soundness”), West Frisian healens (“health”), Dutch heil (“salvation”) (obsolete “health”), German Heil (“health, salvation, cure, remedy”). More at hale, -ness.
