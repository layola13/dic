americans:/əˈmɛrɪkənz/
n.
美国人
People who live in or are citizens of the United States of America.
Many Americans enjoy watching baseball. 很多美国人喜欢看棒球。
近义词: US citizens, people of the US
反义词: foreigners, non-Americans
词性: 名词

Etymology: American + -s (plural marker)
From American,  ultimately from Amerigo Vespucci + -an.  The -s is a standard plural marker in English.
