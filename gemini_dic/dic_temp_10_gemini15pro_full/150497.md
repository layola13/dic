pantographic:/ˌpæn.təˈɡræf.ɪk/
adj.
缩放的，比例放大的，由缩放仪绘制的
Relating to or resembling a pantograph; capable of being enlarged or reduced proportionally, as by a pantograph.
The pantographic arm allowed the artist to easily enlarge the small sketch onto a large canvas.  缩放仪的机械臂让艺术家可以轻松地将小草图放大到大型画布上。

The map was created using a pantographic process. 这张地图是使用缩放仪绘制的。
proportional, scalable, enlarging, reducing
