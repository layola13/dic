legumes:/ˈlɛɡjuːmz/ (also /ləˈɡuːmz/) | n. |豆类；豆科植物|Plants of the pea family, with seeds that grow in pods. These include beans, peas, lentils, soybeans, peanuts, etc.  They are a good source of protein and fiber.|例句：Legumes are an important part of a healthy diet. 豆类是健康饮食的重要组成部分。

例句：Farmers in this region grow a variety of legumes.  这个地区的农民种植各种豆类。|近义词：pulses, beans, peas|反义词: (No direct antonym.  Perhaps "non-legumes" in a specific context) |词性:noun
