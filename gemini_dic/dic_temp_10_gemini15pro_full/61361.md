embezzlement:/ɪmˈbɛzlmənt/ | n. | 贪污，盗用 | The act of illegally taking money that you are responsible for, usually from a company or organization. |  He was charged with embezzlement of company funds. 他被指控盗用公司资金。| synonyms: misappropriation, fraud, theft, pilfering | antonyms: restitution, repayment | noun

embezzle:/ɪmˈbɛzl/ | v. | 贪污，盗用 | To illegally take money that you are responsible for, usually from a company or organization. | She embezzled thousands of dollars from her employer. 她从雇主那里盗用了数千美元。| synonyms: misappropriate, defraud, steal, pilfer | antonyms: repay, reimburse | verb
