inconclusiveness:/ˌɪnkənˈkluːsɪvnəs/
n.
不确定性；无结果；未定
The quality or state of being inconclusive.
The inconclusiveness of the evidence led to the case being dismissed.  证据不确定导致案件被驳回。

近义词:indeterminacy, ambiguity, uncertainty, vagueness
反义词:conclusiveness, certainty, definitiveness
词性:noun
