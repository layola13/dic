amaranth: /ˈæmərænθ/ | n. |  苋菜；不凋花；紫红色；苋属植物 | A plant of the genus Amaranthus, which has small green, red, or purplish flowers. Also refers to an imaginary unfading flower and a purplish-red color. |  The ancient Greeks associated amaranth with immortality. 古希腊人把苋菜和永生联系在一起。| synonyms:  everlasting flower,  pigweed | antonyms:  mortal,  fading | noun

amaranth: /ˈæmərænθ/ | adj. |  紫红色的；不凋谢的 |  Of a purplish-red color; unfading. |  She wore an amaranth gown to the ball. 她穿着一件紫红色的礼服去参加舞会。| synonyms: purplish-red,  undying | antonyms: fading,  transient | adjective
