influencer:/ˈɪnfluənsər/| n.|影响者，有影响力的人|A person who affects or changes the way that other people behave, for example through their use of social media|Many beauty influencers recommend this product. 许多美妆达人推荐这款产品。|synonyms: leader, trendsetter, opinion leader|antonyms: follower, nobody|noun
