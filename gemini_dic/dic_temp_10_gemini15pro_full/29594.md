catechu: /ˈkætɪtʃuː/ /ˈkætɪʃuː/ | n. | 儿茶，儿茶膏 | A reddish-brown astringent extract from acacia trees, used in medicine, tanning, and dyeing. |  Catechu was traditionally used to treat diarrhea. 儿茶传统上用于治疗腹泻。

Catechu gives a rich brown color to textiles. 儿茶赋予纺织品丰富的棕色。 | 近义词: tannin, cutch | 反义词: None | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From Malay *kachu, from Sanskrit कत्थ  (kattha).
