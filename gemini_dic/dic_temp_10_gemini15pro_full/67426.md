extinct:/ɪkˈstɪŋkt/| adj.|灭绝的，绝种的；熄灭的|No longer in existence; having no living members.|The dodo is an extinct bird. 渡渡鸟是一种已灭绝的鸟类。|近义词：vanished, defunct, died out|反义词：extant, existing, living|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin extinctus, past participle of extinguere "to extinguish, quench, put out; destroy, abolish," from ex- "out" (see ex-) + stinguere "to quench, extinguish" (from PIE root *steig- "to stick; pointed").
