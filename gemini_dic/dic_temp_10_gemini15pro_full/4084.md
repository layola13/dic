aileron: /ˈeɪləˌrɑːn/ (US), /ˈeɪləˌrɒn/ (UK)| n. |副翼，扰流板|A hinged control surface on the trailing edge of an aircraft wing, used to control roll. |The pilot used the ailerons to bank the plane. 飞行员使用副翼使飞机倾斜。|The ailerons helped stabilize the aircraft during the turbulent flight.  副翼在湍流飞行中帮助稳定飞机。|近义词:control surface, flap|反义词:None|词性:noun


Etymology: From French *aileron*, diminutive of *aile* "wing," from Latin *āla*.
