copresents:/koʊˈprɪˈzɛnts/| v. |共同呈现；共同主持；共同赠送|To present or host something jointly with another person or entity.  It can also mean to give a gift jointly with another person.|例句：The two organizations copresented a conference on climate change.  这两个组织共同举办了一场关于气候变化的会议。

例句：They copresented their findings to the board of directors. 他们共同向董事会提交了他们的调查结果。|近义词：cohost, joint present|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "present alone")|词性:Verb
