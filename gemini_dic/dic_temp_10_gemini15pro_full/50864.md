desensationalize:/diːˌsɛnsəˈʃənəlaɪz/
verb
使不耸人听闻；使不引起轰动；降低…的轰动效应
To deprive of sensational qualities; to cause (something regarded as sensational) to appear less exciting or shocking.
The media tried to desensationalize the story, focusing on the facts rather than the emotional aspects. 媒体试图淡化该故事的耸人听闻之处，将重点放在事实而不是情感方面。

The government tried to desensationalize the news of the epidemic. 政府试图淡化疫情消息的轰动性。


近义词: downplay, minimize, de-emphasize, understate
反义词: sensationalize, dramatize, exaggerate, hype up
词性: verb
