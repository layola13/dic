encircling:/ɪnˈsɜːrkəlɪŋ/
verb
包围，环绕
forming a circle around; surrounding.
The enemy troops were encircling the city. 敌军正在包围这座城市。
The police encircling the building. 警察包围了大楼。
synonyms: surrounding, enclosing, encompassing, ringing
antonyms: releasing, freeing, liberating
v.


encircling:/ɪnˈsɜːrkəlɪŋ/
adjective
包围的，环绕的
surrounding or enclosing something.
The encircling mountains provided a natural barrier. 环绕的山脉形成了一道天然屏障。
The encircling wall protected the city.  环绕的城墙保护着这座城市。
synonyms: surrounding, enclosing, encompassing, circling
antonyms: open, exposed
adj.
