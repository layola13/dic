imbricative:/ɪmˈbrɪkətɪv/| adj.|覆瓦状的，叠瓦状的|Overlapping or layered like tiles or shingles.|The imbricative arrangement of the feathers helps the bird to stay dry.|近义词：overlapping, imbricate, shingled|反义词:separate, disjoint|adjective

imbricate:/ˈɪmbrɪkeɪt/| adj. v.|覆瓦状的，叠瓦状的；使成覆瓦状|Overlapping or layered like tiles or shingles; to arrange in an overlapping manner.|The roof tiles were imbricated to prevent leaks.  The artist imbricated the pieces of metal to create a textured surface.|近义词：overlapping, imbricative, shingled|反义词:separate, disjoint|adjective, verb


词源: From Latin *imbrica*, from *imbrex* "curved tile," related to *imber* "rain."  The notion is of tiles overlapping to shed rain.
