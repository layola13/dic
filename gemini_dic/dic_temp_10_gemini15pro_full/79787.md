gluttonous:/ˈɡlʌtənəs/ | adj. | 贪吃的；贪婪的；暴食的 | Excessively greedy; given to overindulgence in food or drink.  Characterized by voracious eating and drinking. | He has a gluttonous appetite. 他胃口很大很贪吃。| The king lived a gluttonous life, indulging in feasts every night. 国王过着贪图享乐的生活，每晚都尽情享用盛宴。 | synonyms: greedy, voracious, ravenous, insatiable | antonyms: moderate, temperate, abstemious | adjective


gluttonish:/ˈɡlʌtənɪʃ/ | adj. | 贪吃的；贪婪的 |  Similar to gluttonous, emphasizing the quality of being like a glutton.  |  She cast a gluttonish eye on the dessert tray. 她贪婪地盯着甜点盘。 |  He had a gluttonish desire for power. 他对权力有着贪婪的渴望。| synonyms: greedy, voracious, ravenous, insatiable, gluttonous | antonyms: moderate, temperate, abstemious | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *gluttonosus, from Latin glutto (“glutton”), from gluttīre (“to gulp down, swallow”).
