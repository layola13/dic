docetism:/ˈdoʊsɪtɪzəm/
n.
幻影说，基督一性论
The doctrine, important in Gnosticism, that Christ's body was not human but either a phantom or of real but celestial substance, and that therefore his sufferings were only apparent.
Docetism is a heresy denying the full humanity of Jesus Christ.  幻影说是一种否认耶稣基督完全人性的异端邪说。

Docetic views were common in the early centuries of Christianity.  幻影说的观点在基督教早期很普遍。

近义词:Gnosticism, illusionism
反义词:realism, physicalism
词性:noun
