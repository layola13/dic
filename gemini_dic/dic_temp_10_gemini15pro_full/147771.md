overprominently: /ˌoʊvərˈprɒmɪnəntli/ | adv. | 过于突出地，过分显著地 |  In a way that is too noticeable or emphasized.  It's formed by adding the prefix "over-" (meaning "excessive" or "too much") to "prominent" (meaning "standing out") and the suffix "-ly" (forming adverbs).  The word "prominent" comes from the Latin "prominere," meaning "to project" or "to jut out." |  He overprominently displayed his wealth, wearing flashy jewelry and driving expensive cars.  他过于炫耀自己的财富，佩戴着华丽的珠宝，开着昂贵的汽车。 |  conspicuously, noticeably, ostentatiously | subtly, discreetly | adverb
