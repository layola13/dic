exclusivism: /ɪkˈskluːsɪvɪzəm/ /ɪkˈskluːsɪvɪzəm/
n.
排他主义;排外主义
The practice of excluding certain groups, especially from participation in religious or social activities.
Many countries are trying to avoid religious exclusivism. 许多国家正试图避免宗教排他主义。
Exclusivism in private clubs is being challenged more and more. 私人俱乐部的排他性正受到越来越多的挑战。

近义词: intolerance, bigotry, discrimination, sectarianism
反义词: inclusiveness, tolerance, acceptance, liberalism
词性: noun
