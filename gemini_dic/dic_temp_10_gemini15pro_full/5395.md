almagest:/ˈælmədʒɛst/| n.|天文学大成；至大论|A comprehensive treatise on astronomy, written by Ptolemy in the 2nd century AD.|例句：The Almagest was the standard astronomical text for over a thousand years.  天文学大成是1000多年来的标准天文学文本。

例句：Scholars still study the Almagest to understand ancient astronomical theories.  学者们仍在研究天文学大成以了解古代天文学理论。|近义词：compendium, treatise|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Middle French *almageste, from Arabic  الكتاب المجسطي‎ (al-kitāb al-majisṭī, “The Great Book”), from Ancient Greek  Ἡ Μεγίστη Σύνταξις (Hē Megístē Sýntaxis, “The Greatest Compilation”)
