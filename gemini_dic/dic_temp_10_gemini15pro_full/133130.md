nonesthetic:/nɒnɛsˈθɛtɪk/ | adj. | 非审美的，不美的 |  Lacking aesthetic qualities; not pleasing to the eye or ear; unattractive. | The nonesthetic design of the building made it an eyesore in the neighborhood. 这栋建筑毫无美感的设计使它成为社区中的一个眼中钉。| The sound of nails scratching on a chalkboard is a nonesthetic experience for most people.指甲刮黑板的声音对大多数人来说是一种不美的体验。| 近义词: unaesthetic, unappealing, unsightly, ugly | 反义词: aesthetic, beautiful, pleasing, attractive | 词性: adjective

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + aesthetic (from Greek aisthētikos, "perceptive, sensitive," from aisthanesthai, "to perceive").
