overexerts:/ˌoʊvərɪɡˈzɜːrts/ | v. | 过度劳累，用力过度 | To exert oneself beyond one's strength or capacity. | He overexerted himself during the marathon and collapsed at the finish line. 他在马拉松比赛中用力过度，在终点线晕倒了。|  The weightlifter overexerted himself and injured his back.  举重运动员用力过度，伤了背部。 |Synonyms: strain, overstrain, overwork, overdo | Antonyms: relax, rest | verb
