fascinator:/ˈfæsɪneɪtər/| n.|头饰，女帽，令人着迷的人或事物|A fascinator is a headpiece, typically a decorative design attached to a band or clip, worn by women.  It's often smaller and less elaborate than a hat. It can also refer to something or someone that fascinates.|She wore a beautiful fascinator to the wedding. 她戴了一个漂亮的头饰参加婚礼。|Synonym: headpiece, ornament, accessory |Antonym: (For object) nothing specific, (For person) bore|noun
