devilishly:/ˈdɛvəlɪʃli/ /dɪˈvɪlɪʃli/ | adv. | 恶魔般地；极度地；非常地 | In a devilish manner; extremely; very. |  She grinned devilishly. 她露出了恶魔般的笑容。

| 近义词: wickedly, mischievously, fiendishly, extremely, exceedingly | 反义词: angelically, divinely, saintly | 词性: 副词
