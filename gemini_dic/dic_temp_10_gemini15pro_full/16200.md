banner-ad:/ˈbænər æd/ | n. | 横幅广告 | A graphical web advertising unit, typically rectangular in shape, displayed at the top or bottom, or sometimes on the sides, of a web page. | Example 1: The banner-ad at the top of the website was flashing and distracting.  网站顶部的横幅广告闪烁不停，让人分心。

Example 2:  Clicking on the banner-ad took me to a different website. 点击横幅广告后，我跳转到了另一个网站。 | 近义词: web banner, display ad, online advertisement | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider) print ad, television commercial | 词性: noun
