cordate:/ˈkɔːrdeɪt/ /ˈkɔːrˌdeɪt/| adj.|心形的，心脏形的|Shaped like a heart, with a pointed end and a rounded base.|The leaves of this plant are cordate. 这种植物的叶子是心形的。|近义词：heart-shaped, cordiform|反义词：oblong, lanceolate|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *cordātus, from cor, cord- ‘heart’.
