chrysaloid:/ˈkrɪsəlɔɪd/| adj. n.|蛹的，蛹状的；处于过渡期的|Resembling or suggestive of a chrysalis; being in a transitional phase. |The company is in a chrysaloid state, about to emerge as a major player in the market.  这家公司正处于蛹期，即将成为市场上的主要参与者。

The butterfly, still chrysaloid, clung to the branch.  蝴蝶仍然处于蛹的状态，紧紧地 clinging在树枝上。|近义词：transitional, nascent, developing, metamorphic, pupa-like|反义词：mature, developed, complete, finished|词性: adjective, noun

chrysaloid:/ˈkrɪsəlɔɪd/| n.|蛹；处于过渡期的事物|A chrysalis; something in a transitional phase.|The project is still in its chrysaloid, with much work to be done before completion.  这个项目仍处于蛹期，在完成之前还有很多工作要做。

The chrysaloid of a new political movement is forming. 一个新的政治运动的雏形正在形成。|近义词：chrysalis, pupa, germination, infancy|反义词：maturity, completion, adulthood|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *khrysallis*  (golden, referring to the color of some butterfly chrysalises) + -oid (resembling). Ultimately from *khrusos* (gold).
