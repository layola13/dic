donet:/ˈdʌnɪt/ |adj.| 完成的，做完的 | Completed, finished. | 例句：The task is donet. 任务完成了。 |近义词：finished, completed, done, over, through | 反义词：unfinished, incomplete, ongoing, pending| 词性:形容词

词源:  "donet" is an archaic or dialectal variant of "done," the past participle of the verb "do."  It comes from Old English "gedōn."
