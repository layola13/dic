clocking-in:/ˈklɒkɪŋ ɪn/ (US) /ˈklɒkɪŋ ɪn/ (UK)| n.| 打卡上班|The act of recording the time one arrives at work, typically by using a time clock or electronic system. |例句：Clocking-in is mandatory for all employees. 所有员工都必须打卡上班。|近义词：signing in, checking in|反义词：clocking-out, checking out|词性: 名词 (Gerund/Verbal Noun)


clocking-in:/ˈklɒkɪŋ ɪn/ (US) /ˈklɒkɪŋ ɪn/ (UK)| adj.| 打卡上班的|Relating to the act of clocking in.  (Less common usage than as a noun/gerund.) |例句：The clocking-in procedure has been updated. 打卡上班的程序已更新。|近义词：sign-in, check-in|反义词：clocking-out, check-out|词性: 形容词 (Less common)
