filchingly:/ˈfɪltʃɪŋli/
| adv. |偷偷摸摸地，鬼鬼祟祟地|In a manner resembling someone who filches; stealthily and quietly, often with the intention of stealing something.|He filchingly slipped the candy into his pocket when no one was looking. 他趁没人注意，偷偷地把糖果塞进了口袋。|近义词：stealthily, surreptitiously, furtively|反义词：openly, honestly|词性:副词

词源:
From filch + -ing + -ly.  Filch itself comes from Middle English filchen, of uncertain origin.
