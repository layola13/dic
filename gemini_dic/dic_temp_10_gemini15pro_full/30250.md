cellulosing:/ˈsɛljʊˌloʊzɪŋ/
noun
纤维素化；纤维素形成
The process of forming cellulose.  This word is less common than "cellulose formation."
Cellulosing of the plant cell walls provides structural support.  植物细胞壁的纤维素化提供了结构支撑。
近义词: cellulose formation
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider "decomposition" or "breakdown" in some contexts)
词性: noun
