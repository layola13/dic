lalo:/ˈlɑːloʊ/ | n. | 拉洛;人名; 拉洛·斯基弗林 (Lalo Schifrin)| A diminutive of Eduardo, commonly used in Spanish-speaking countries. It is also the professional name of Argentine composer and conductor Lalo Schifrin, known for his film and television scores. | Lalo is a talented musician. 拉洛是个有才华的音乐家。| My friend Lalo is visiting from Mexico. 我的朋友拉洛从墨西哥来访。 | synonyms: Eduardo (formal), Lalito (more diminutive) | antonyms: N/A | noun
