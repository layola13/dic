gibbousnesses:/ˈɡɪbəsnəsɪz/
noun
凸起，隆起；（尤指月亮）凸圆
The state or quality of being gibbous; a rounded swelling or protuberance.
The astronomer noted the gibbousnesses of the moon's surface. 天文学家注意到月球表面的隆起。
Synonyms: protuberances, swellings, bulges, convexities
Antonyms: concavities, depressions, hollows
n.


gibbousness:/ˈɡɪbəsnəs/
noun
凸起，隆起；（尤指月亮）凸圆
The state or quality of being gibbous; a rounded swelling or protuberance.
The gibbousness of the moon was clearly visible. 月亮的凸圆清晰可见。
Synonyms: convexity, protuberance, bulge, swelling
Antonyms: concavity, depression, hollowness
n.

gibbous:/ˈɡɪbəs/
adjective
凸起的，隆起的；（尤指月亮）凸圆的
(especially of the moon) convex; more than half full but less than full
The gibbous moon shone brightly in the night sky. 凸圆的月亮在夜空中闪耀。
Synonyms: convex, bulging, rounded, protuberant
Antonyms: concave, hollow, depressed
adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin gibbosus (“humpbacked”), from Latin gibbus (“hump, hunch”).
