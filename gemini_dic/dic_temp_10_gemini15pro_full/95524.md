including:/ɪnˈkluːdɪŋ/ | prep. | 包括，包含 |  Used to introduce things that are part of a larger group or amount. | Ten people attended the meeting, including myself. 十个人参加了会议，包括我自己。| synonyms: comprising, incorporating, containing | antonyms: excluding, omitting | preposition

/ɪnˈkluːdɪŋ/ | adj. | 包括在内的 |  Containing as part of a whole. | The price is £12.99, including postage and packing. 价格是12.99英镑，包括邮资和包装。| synonyms: inclusive | antonyms: exclusive | adjective
