douchebaggery:/ˈduːʃˌbæɡəri/
n.
愚蠢的行为；混蛋行为；令人讨厌的行为
The act of being a douchebag; obnoxious or contemptible behavior.
His latest stunt was pure douchebaggery. 他最新的噱头纯粹是混蛋行为。
近义词: jerkiness, assholery, idiocy, foolishness
反义词: kindness, politeness, consideration
词性: 名词

词源:
From *douche bag* + *-ery*. *Douche bag* originally referred to a small syringe for vaginal douching, later becoming a derogatory slang term for an unpleasant person.  *-ery* is a suffix forming nouns meaning "the action, practice, or condition of," as in *slavery* or *robbery*.
