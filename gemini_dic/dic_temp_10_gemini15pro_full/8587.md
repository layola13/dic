anticlassicists:/ˌæntiˈklæsɪsɪsts/
| n. |反古典主义者|People who oppose or reject the principles of classicism, especially in art, literature, and architecture.  They often favor more modern or romantic styles.|The anticlassicists of the early 20th century embraced new forms of expression.  20世纪早期的反古典主义者拥护新的表达形式。|近义词:modernists, romantics|反义词:classicists|Noun
