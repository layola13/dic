nonpolitically:/ˌnɑːnpəˈlɪtɪkli/
adverb
非政治地；不涉及政治地
Not involving or influenced by politics.
The issue was handled nonpolitically to avoid controversy.  这个问题被非政治地处理以避免争议。
The organization distributed aid nonpolitically, based solely on need.  该组织非政治地分发援助，完全基于需求。
Synonyms: neutrally, impartially, objectively
Antonyms: politically, partisanly
词性: 副词
