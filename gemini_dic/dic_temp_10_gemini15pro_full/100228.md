interrogatory:/ˌɪntəˈrɑːɡətɔːri/ /ɪnˈterɒɡətəri/ | adj. n. | 询问的，疑问的；质问书 |  Of, relating to, or conveying a question.  A formal question, especially one put in writing as part of a legal process. |  The lawyer submitted a list of interrogatories to the witness. 律师向证人提交了一系列质问书。| synonyms: questioning, inquisitive, inquiring | antonyms: declarative, affirmative | adjective, noun

interrogatory:/ˌɪntəˈrɑːɡətɔːri/ /ɪnˈterɒɡətəri/ | adj. n. | 询问的，疑问的；质问书 | Her tone was interrogatory. 她的语气是疑问的。 | synonyms: questioning, inquisitive, inquiring | antonyms: declarative, affirmative | adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin interrogātōrius (“of asking, questioning”), from Latin interrogō (“I ask, question”), from inter- (“between”) + rogō (“I ask”).
