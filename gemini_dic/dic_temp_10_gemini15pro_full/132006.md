noncorpuscular:/ˌnɒnkɔːrˈpʌskjʊlə(r)/ | adj. | 非微粒的，非粒子性的 | Not consisting of particles; not of a corpuscular nature. |  Light was once thought of as noncorpuscular, a wave phenomenon, rather than a stream of photons. 光曾经被认为是非粒子性的，是一种波的现象，而不是光子流。 | Light exhibits both wave-like and corpuscular properties, leading to the concept of wave-particle duality. 光既表现出波状特性又表现出粒子特性，这导致了波粒二象性的概念。 | 近义词: wave-like, non-particulate | 反义词: corpuscular, particulate | 词性: 形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  Latin, meaning "not"
corpuscular (adjective): From Latin "corpusculum" (small body), diminutive of "corpus" (body).
