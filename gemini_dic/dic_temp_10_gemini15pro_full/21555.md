bluepoll:/ˈbluːpəʊl/ (US) /ˈbluːpɒl/ (UK)| n.|蓝顶蓝饰雀|A small, brightly colored Australian bird (Malurus cyaneus) known for its vibrant blue plumage, especially in the breeding male.|The male bluepoll is a striking sight with its bright blue crown and ear coverts. 雄性蓝顶蓝饰雀有着鲜艳的蓝色冠羽和耳羽，十分引人注目。|The bluepolls were flitting about in the bushes. 蓝顶蓝饰雀在灌木丛中飞来飞去。|synonyms: superb fairy-wren, blue wren|antonyms: N/A (No direct antonym for a specific bird species)|noun
