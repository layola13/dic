countercoup:/ˈkaʊntərkuː/| n.|反政变，反颠覆|A coup against those who have just staged a coup.|例句：The countercoup restored the former government to power. 反政变使前政府恢复了权力。|例句：The military launched a countercoup just days after the initial takeover. 軍方在最初的接管几天后發動了反政變。|近义词：counter-revolution, putsch|反义词:coup, revolution|名词

countercoup:/ˈkaʊntərkuː/| n.|反击，对抗措施|An action taken to counteract or oppose another.|例句：His countercoup in the debate silenced his opponent.  他在辩论中的反击使他的对手哑口无言。|例句：The company launched a marketing countercoup to their competitor's new product. 该公司针对竞争对手的新产品发起了一场营销反击。|近义词：retaliation, countermeasure|反义词:initiative, attack|名词
