chapless:/ˈtʃæplɪs/
adjective
无护颊的，无面甲的
Lacking chaps (protective leg coverings worn over trousers, typically by cowboys).  It can also mean, more broadly, lacking a covering or protection, though this usage is less common.
The cowboy wore chapless trousers, leaving his legs vulnerable to the brush.  牛仔穿着没有护腿的裤子，让他的腿容易被灌木丛划伤。
Near synonyms: unprotected, exposed, uncovered
Antonyms: protected, covered, shielded
词性: adjective
