disanalogy:/ˌdɪsəˈnælədʒi/
n.|不同，不相 analogy|Lack of analogy; difference or dissimilarity.  The disanalogy between the two situations makes direct comparison difficult.|The disanalogy between bird flight and insect flight makes it hard to design a universal flying machine. 鸟类飞行和昆虫飞行之间的差异使得设计通用的飞行器变得困难。|difference, disparity, dissimilitude, divergence|analogy, similarity, resemblance|noun
