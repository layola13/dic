fraughtest:/ˈfrɔːtɪst/
adjective
充满的；焦虑的
filled with something unpleasant such as danger or worry; causing or affected by anxiety or stress
例句：The atmosphere was fraughtest with tension. 气氛非常紧张。

例句：This is the fraughtest mission we've ever undertaken. 这是我们承担过的最危险的任务。
synonym: tense, anxious, stressed, strained, charged, worried, uneasy, apprehensive, fearful, nerve-racking
antonym: calm, relaxed, peaceful, tranquil, serene, carefree, untroubled

词性: adjective
