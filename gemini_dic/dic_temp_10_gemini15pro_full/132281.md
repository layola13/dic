nondefendant:/ˌnɑːndɪˈfɛndənt/
n.
非被告人；未被控告者
A person or entity not named as a defendant in a legal proceeding.
The nondefendant witness corroborated the plaintiff's testimony.  非被告证人证实了原告的证词。
Synonyms: non-party, not accused
Antonyms: defendant, accused
noun
