insessorial:/ɪnsəˈsɔːriəl/
adjective
适于栖息的；（鸟类）适于栖树的；攀禽类的
Adapted for perching; relating to or characteristic of perching birds.
例句：The robin has insessorial feet, perfectly designed for gripping branches. 知更鸟有适于栖息的脚，非常适合抓住树枝。

例句：Insessorial birds often have three toes pointing forward and one toe pointing backward. 攀禽通常有三个脚趾向前，一个脚趾向后。

近义词：perching, arboreal
反义词: terrestrial, cursorial
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *insessor* (“one who sits upon”), from *insidēre* (“to sit upon”), from *in* (“on, upon”) + *sedēre* (“to sit”).
