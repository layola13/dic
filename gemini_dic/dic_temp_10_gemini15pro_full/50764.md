(Since "des" is a French word, it doesn't have direct English phrases.  However, here are some French phrases using "des":)
des gens = some people
des fois = sometimes
des tas de = lots of
des quantités de = large quantities of
des millions de = millions of


注意事项:

The word "des" is archaic in English and rarely used outside of very formal or literary contexts. Its usage is primarily found in older texts or those attempting to evoke a specific style.  It's much more common in modern French.
