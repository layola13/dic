liability:/ˌlaɪəˈbɪləti/
n.|责任，义务；负债；[复数]债务|Something one is obligated to do or pay; a debt; something disadvantageous.|The company has liabilities of over $1 million. 这家公司的负债超过100万美元。|responsibility, obligation, debt, burden|asset, advantage|noun

liability:/ˌlaɪəˈbɪləti/
n.|倾向；易遭受…之事|A tendency to be affected by something unpleasant.|His liability to depression makes it difficult for him to hold a steady job. 他容易抑郁，这使得他很难保住一份稳定的工作。|tendency, inclination, proneness, susceptibility|resistance, immunity|noun
