overreflectivenesses:/ˌoʊvərˌriːflɛkˈtɪvnɪsɪz/
|n.|过度反思; 过度反省|The state of engaging in excessive reflection or introspection, often to the point of hindering action or causing anxiety.|Her overreflectivenesses prevented her from making a decision.|近义词: rumination, introspection, self-analysis|反义词: impulsiveness, thoughtlessness|词性: 名词


Etymology:
"over" + "reflectiveness" + "-es"

"over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much".

"reflectiveness" comes from "reflective," which stems from the Latin "reflectere" meaning "to bend back."  The "-ness" suffix turns the adjective into a noun.

"-es" is the plural suffix.
