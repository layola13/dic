conchylious:/kɒŋˈkɪliəs/ /kɑːŋˈkɪliəs/
adjective
贝壳状的;似贝壳的
Resembling or having the nature of a shell, especially of a mollusk.  Made up of or containing shells.
例句：The fossilized remains were conchylious, indicating the presence of ancient marine life.  这些化石遗骸呈贝壳状，表明存在古代海洋生物。

例句：The artist created a conchylious sculpture using hundreds of seashells.  这位艺术家用数百个贝壳创作了一个贝壳状的雕塑。
近义词：shell-like, shelly
反义词: shell-less
