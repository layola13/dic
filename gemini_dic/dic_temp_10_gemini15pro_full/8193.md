词源:
"anti-" (prefix): from Greek "anti" meaning "against, opposite of"
"domestically" (adverb): from "domestic" (adjective) + "-ly" (adverb suffix). "Domestic" ultimately derives from Latin "domesticus" meaning "belonging to the house".
