marinades:/ˈmærɪneɪdz/| n.|腌泡汁，腌料|A mixture, typically of oil, vinegar, spices, and herbs, in which meat, fish, or other food is soaked before cooking in order to flavor or tenderize it.|She marinated the chicken in a mixture of lemon juice, garlic, and herbs. 她把鸡肉浸泡在柠檬汁、大蒜和香草的混合物中腌制。|近义词：sauces, seasonings|反义词:|n.


marinades:/ˈmærɪneɪdz/| v.|腌制|To soak (meat, fish, or other food) in a marinade.|He marinated the steak overnight. 他把牛排腌制了一夜。|近义词：soak, steep|反义词:|v.


词源：From French marinade, from mariner ‘to pickle in sea water’, from marin ‘sea’.  来自法语 marinade，源自 mariner “用海水腌制”，来自 marin “海”。
