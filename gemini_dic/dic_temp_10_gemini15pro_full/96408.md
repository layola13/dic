indisposedness:/ˌɪndɪˈspəʊzdnəs/
n.
不适，不愿意
The state of being indisposed; slight illness or unwillingness.
Her indisposedness prevented her from attending the meeting. 她身体不适，无法参加会议。
Synonyms: illness, ailment, sickness, reluctance, unwillingness
Antonyms: wellness, health, willingness, eagerness
noun


indisposed:/ˌɪndɪˈspəʊzd/
adj.
不愿意的，身体不适的
Slightly unwell or reluctant.
He felt indisposed to go to work. 他不太想去上班。
Synonyms: unwell, ill, sick, reluctant, unwilling
Antonyms: well, healthy, willing, eager
adjective
