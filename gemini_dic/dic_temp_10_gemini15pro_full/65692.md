evenly:/ˈiːvənli/ | adv. | 平均地，均匀地，平稳地 | In a smooth, regular, and balanced way.  | The cake was evenly sliced. 蛋糕被均匀地切片了。| The weight was evenly distributed. 重量被均匀地分布。 | 近义词: uniformly, equally, smoothly | 反义词: unevenly, irregularly, roughly | 词性: 副词

词源: From Middle English *evenly, from Old English *efenlīce, equivalent to even +‎ -ly.
