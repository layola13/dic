exploit:/ɪkˈsplɔɪt/ | n. | 功绩，勋绩；英勇事迹 | A brilliant, daring, or heroic feat. |例句：The soldier's exploits in battle were legendary.  这位士兵的战功显赫。| 近义词：feat, accomplishment, achievement, deed | 反义词：failure, defeat | noun

exploit:/ɪkˈsplɔɪt/ | v. | 利用；剥削；开发 | To make full use of a resource or situation, sometimes in a way considered unfair or unethical. | 例句：The company exploited its workers by paying them low wages. 这家公司剥削工人，支付低工资。 | 近义词：utilize, capitalize on, take advantage of, misuse | 反义词：preserve, protect, neglect | verb
