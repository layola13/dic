atomicity:/ˌætəˈmɪsɪti/| n. |原子性，不可分割性|The property of a transaction to be treated as a single, indivisible unit of work.  A transaction is either fully completed or not completed at all.|Database transactions must have atomicity to ensure data integrity. 数据库事务必须具有原子性以确保数据完整性。|synonyms: indivisibility, all-or-nothing, unity|antonyms: divisibility, separability|noun
