outquote:/ˈaʊtkwoʊt/
noun
外引语，引文
A passage or expression that is quoted or cited, especially from a written work.
The article included several outquotes from the CEO's speech. 这篇文章引用了首席执行官讲话中的几段话。
synonyms: quotation, excerpt, citation
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but consider: paraphrase, summary)
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "out-" (meaning outside or external) + "quote" (from Latin "quotare" meaning to number or reckon).  Indicates a quote taken "out" of a larger text.
