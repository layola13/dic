administer:/ədˈmɪnɪstər/
verb
管理，执行，给予
to manage and be responsible for the running of (a business, organization, etc.); to control the operation or arrangement of something; to give or provide something, especially in a formal way
The program is administered by the Department of Education.  这个项目由教育部管理。

The nurse administered the medicine to the patient. 护士给病人服了药。

近义词:manage, direct, govern, execute, dispense, apply
反义词:neglect, mismanage
词性:verb

The principal administers the school. = 校长管理学校。
