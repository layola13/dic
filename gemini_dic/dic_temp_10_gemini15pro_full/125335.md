multilateralists:/ˌmʌltiˈlætərəlɪsts/| n.|多边主义者|People who support multilateralism, the principle of cooperation between multiple countries.|例句：The multilateralists argue that global challenges require global solutions. 多边主义者认为，全球挑战需要全球解决方案。

例句：Multilateralists advocate for international cooperation on issues like climate change and trade. 多边主义者倡导在气候变化和贸易等问题上进行国际合作。|近义词：internationalists, cosmopolitans|反义词：unilateralists, isolationists|词性:Noun
