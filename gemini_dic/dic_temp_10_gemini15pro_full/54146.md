disengages:/dɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒɪz/
verb
脱离，解开，退出
to release from something that holds fast, connects, or entangles
The pilot disengaged the autopilot. 飞行员解除了自动驾驶。
detach, disconnect, withdraw
engage, connect, attach
