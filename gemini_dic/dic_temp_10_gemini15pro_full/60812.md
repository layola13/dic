electrophorus:/ɪˈlɛktrəfərəs/
n.|起电盘|A device used to generate static electricity, typically consisting of a dielectric plate and a metal plate with an insulated handle.|The students used an electrophorus to demonstrate the principles of electrostatic induction. 学生们用起电盘演示了静电感应的原理。|electroscope, generator|None|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἤλεκτρον (ḗlektron, “amber”) + -phorus  (-φόρος, from φέρω (phérō, “I carry”)).  Literally "amber-carrier".
