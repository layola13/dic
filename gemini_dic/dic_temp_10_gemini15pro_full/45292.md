culturist:/ˈkʌltʃərɪst/| n.|文化主义者；文化优越论者；健身爱好者|A person who promotes or advocates a particular culture, often believing in its superiority, or someone dedicated to physical culture, particularly bodybuilding.|He's a real culturist, always talking about the superiority of his nation's art and music. 他是一位真正的文化主义者，总是谈论他的民族艺术和音乐的优越性。

She's a dedicated culturist, spending hours in the gym every week. 她是一位专注的健身爱好者，每周都要在健身房里待上几个小时。|近义词：traditionalist, purist (for cultural sense), bodybuilder, weightlifter (for fitness sense)|反义词：globalist, cosmopolitan (for cultural sense) |词性: noun
