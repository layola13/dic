mesogastric:/ˌmɛzəˈɡæstrɪk/
adjective
中胃的
Relating to or situated in the middle region of the abdomen, between the epigastric and hypogastric regions.
The mesogastric region contains the small intestine, transverse colon, and part of the duodenum.
近义词:midabdominal
反义词:None
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From meso- (middle) + gastric (relating to the stomach)
