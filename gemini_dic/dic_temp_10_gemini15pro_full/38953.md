confidingly:/kənˈfaɪdɪŋli/
adverb
信任地，吐露秘密地
in a way that shows trust and that you want to tell someone personal things
She smiled confidingly at him. 她信任地对他笑了笑。
近义词:intimately, privately, secretly, trustfully
反义词:distrustfully, suspiciously
词性:adverb

词源:
From confide + -ing + -ly.  Confide comes from the Latin "confidere," meaning "to trust entirely."  The "-ing" suffix creates the present participle, which can function as an adjective.  The "-ly" suffix transforms the adjective into an adverb.
