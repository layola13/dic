miscuts:/ˈmɪskʌts/
noun
错误剪切，剪裁错误
incorrectly cut pieces, usually of paper, fabric, or film.
The miscuts on the fabric made it unusable for the project.  这些布料上的剪裁错误使得它无法用于该项目。
The printing company had to reprint the brochures due to several miscuts.  由于一些剪裁错误，印刷公司不得不重印宣传册。
近义词: misprints, errors, mistakes, flaws, defects
反义词: perfect cuts, accurate cuts
词性: noun (plural)
