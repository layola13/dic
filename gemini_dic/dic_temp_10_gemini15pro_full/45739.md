curvingly:/ˈkɜːrvɪŋli/
adv.
弯曲地，曲线地
in a curving manner; with curves
The road wound curvingly through the mountains. 这条路蜿蜒曲折地穿过群山。
近义词：sinuously, windingly
反义词：straightly, directly
词性：副词

Etymology:
From curve + -ing + -ly.  "Curve" comes from Late Latin *curvare "to bend," from Latin curvus "bent, crooked."
