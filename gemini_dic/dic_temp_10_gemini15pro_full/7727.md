anonymize:/əˈnɒnɪmaɪz/ | v. | 匿名化，使匿名 | To remove identifying details from something, such as a document, so that individuals cannot be recognized. | Example: The data was anonymized to protect the privacy of the participants.  数据被匿名化以保护参与者的隐私。| Example:  They anonymized the survey results by removing names and addresses. 他们通过删除姓名和地址将调查结果匿名化。 | Synonyms: de-identify, pseudonymize | Antonyms: identify, personalize | verb

/əˈnɑːnɪmaɪz/ (US) | v. | 匿名化，使匿名 | To remove identifying details from something, such as a document, so that individuals cannot be recognized. | Example: The website anonymizes user data before sharing it with advertisers. 该网站在与广告商共享用户数据之前将其匿名化。 | Example:  Researchers anonymized the medical records before analyzing them. 研究人员在分析医疗记录之前将其匿名化。| Synonyms: de-identify, pseudonymize | Antonyms: identify, personalize | verb
