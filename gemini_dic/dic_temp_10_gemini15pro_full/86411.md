heebie-jeebies:/ˈhiːbi ˈdʒiːbiz/| n.|紧张不安；神经紧张；恐惧感|A feeling of nervousness, apprehension, or fear.|例句：I get the heebie-jeebies just thinking about spiders. 我一想到蜘蛛就浑身起鸡皮疙瘩。

例句：The dark, empty house gave me the heebie-jeebies.  黑暗空荡的房子让我感到毛骨悚然。

|近义词：jitters, creeps, willies|反义词:calm, composure|词性:名词
