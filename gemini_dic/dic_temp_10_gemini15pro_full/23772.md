brawest:/ˈbrɔːɪst/| adj.|最勇敢的，最强壮的，最优秀的|superlative form of braw;  meaning the most brave, strong, or excellent.  Often used in Scottish English. |He was the brawest lad in the village. 他是村里最勇敢的小伙子。|近义词：bravest, strongest, finest, best|反义词：weakest, most cowardly, worst|词性:形容词

词源:
From Scots *braw, from Middle English *brau, probably from Old Norse *bragr (“beautiful, excellent, proud”), from Proto-Germanic *bragjaz (“to shine, glitter”).  Cognate with Icelandic bragr (“best”), Swedish bra (“good, fine”), Norwegian bra (“good, fine”).
