mynah:/ˈmaɪnə/ (US), /ˈmaɪnɑː/ (UK)| n. |八哥；鹩哥|A bird of the starling family, especially of tropical Asia, several species of which are noted for their ability to mimic sounds, including human speech.|The mynah mimicked the shopkeeper's call perfectly. 这只八哥完美地模仿了店主的叫声。|近义词：starling, mimic bird|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
