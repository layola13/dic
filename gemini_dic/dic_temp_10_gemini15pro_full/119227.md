miasm:/ˈmaɪæzəm/ | n. | 瘴气；不良影响；腐朽气息 | A poisonous or noxious atmosphere or influence. |  The miasm of corruption hangs heavy over the city. 腐败的瘴气笼罩着整个城市。

The miasm from the swamp made them feel ill. 沼泽的瘴气让他们感到不适。 | 近义词: pollution, contamination, stench, effluvium | 反义词: purity, cleanliness | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *miasme*, from Late Latin *miasma*, from Greek *míasma* "stain, pollution," from *miaínein* "to pollute."
