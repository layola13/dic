cultest: /ˈkʌltɪst/
n.|信徒; 狂热追随者|A member of a cult; a person who is excessively devoted to a particular activity or cause.|He was branded a cultest for his unwavering devotion to the obscure philosopher.|近义词:fanatic, devotee, zealot|反义词:skeptic, critic|词性:名词

Etymology:  From "cult" + "-ist". "Cult" comes from Latin "cultus" meaning "care, cultivation, worship."  "-ist" is a suffix forming agent nouns.
