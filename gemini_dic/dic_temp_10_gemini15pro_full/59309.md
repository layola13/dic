earbashing:/ˈɪəˌbæʃɪŋ/| n.|唠叨，喋喋不休|A prolonged and often tedious or unwelcome talk.  The act of talking to someone incessantly and often about trivial matters.|例句：I got an earbashing from my mother about my messy room. 我妈妈因为我的房间乱七八糟而对我唠叨个没完。

例句：He gave me a real earbashing about the importance of punctuality. 他就守时的重要性对我喋喋不休地讲了一大通。

近义词：lecturing, nagging, scolding, haranguing, badgering|反义词: silence, quiet|词性:noun
