overserving:/ˌōvərˈsɜːrvɪŋ/
verb
过度服务；服务过分
Providing too much service or attention, often to the point of being intrusive or excessive.
The restaurant staff were overserving, constantly interrupting our conversation to ask if we needed anything.  这家餐厅的服务员过度服务，不断打断我们的谈话，询问我们是否需要什么。
The waiter was overserving, hovering around our table and refilling our water glasses every two minutes. 服务员服务过分殷勤，在我们桌旁徘徊，每两分钟就给我们续杯水。
overservicing, over-attending
underserving
v.
