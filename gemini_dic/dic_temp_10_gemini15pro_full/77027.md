gargoyled:/ˈɡɑːrɡɔɪld/ | adj. |  滴水嘴状的；丑陋的 | Decorated with or resembling gargoyles. | The old building was gargoyled with grotesque figures. 这座古老的建筑装饰着怪诞的滴水嘴状雕像。 | 近义词: grotesque, hideous | 反义词: beautiful, attractive | 词性: 形容词

词源: From "gargoyle" (n.) + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives). "Gargoyle" comes from Old French "gargouille" meaning "throat," related to the verb "gargouiller" meaning "to gurgle." This refers to the water spouts on buildings that were often carved into grotesque figures.
