oversystematically:/ˌoʊvərˌsɪstəˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 过于系统地；过度系统化地 |  In a way that is excessively systematic, often to the point of being inflexible or impractical.  Applying a system too rigidly or comprehensively. | He oversystematically organized his pantry, labeling every spice jar with both the common name and its Latin equivalent. 他过度系统地整理了他的食品储藏室，用常用名称和拉丁名称标记了每个香料罐。|  She oversystematically categorized her books, dividing them not only by genre but also by the color of their covers. 她过度系统地将她的书分类，不仅按类型划分，还按封面的颜色划分。 | 近义词: excessively methodically, too rigidly, over-organized | 反义词: haphazardly, randomly, disorganizedly | 词性: 副词
