extraneity:/ɛkˈstreɪnɪti/
n.
外来性；无关性；非本质性
The quality of being extraneous; irrelevance.
The extraneity of the evidence led to its exclusion from the trial.  该证据的无关性导致其被排除在审判之外。
extraneousness, irrelevance, unimportance
relevance, pertinence
noun
