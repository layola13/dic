nonlanguage:/nɒnˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ/ | adj. | 非语言的 | Not involving or using language; nonverbal.  Relating to communication without the use of words. |  She gave me a nonlanguage cue to be quiet. 她给了我一个非语言的提示，让我安静下来。|  He is skilled in nonlanguage communication. 他擅长非语言交流。| 近义词: nonverbal, unspoken, wordless | 反义词: verbal, linguistic, spoken | 词性: adjective

词源: "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "language"
