hard-fought:/ˈhɑːrd fɔːt/
adjective
艰苦奋斗的，来之不易的
Won after a great deal of effort or struggle.
The team secured a hard-fought victory in the final.  该队在决赛中取得了来之不易的胜利。

The lawyer won a hard-fought legal battle.  律师赢得了一场艰苦的法律战。


近义词:hard-won, closely contested, bitterly fought
反义词:easily won, effortless
词性:adjective
