maestros:/ˈmaɪstroʊz/ (US), /ˈmaɪstrəʊz/ (UK)| n. |大师; 巨匠; |A maestro is a distinguished musician, especially a conductor or composer.  A person who is very skilled at something.|The maestro led the orchestra with passion.  这位大师充满激情地指挥着乐队。|近义词：expert, master, virtuoso, genius, prodigy|反义词：amateur, beginner, novice|Noun
