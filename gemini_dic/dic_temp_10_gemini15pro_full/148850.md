overwatch:/ˈoʊvərˌwɑːtʃ/ (US), /ˈəʊvəˌwɒtʃ/ (UK)| v.|监督，监视；照管，保护|To watch over or protect someone or something.|The lifeguard overwatches the swimmers in the pool. 救生员监视着游泳池里的游泳者。|近义词：supervise, monitor, guard, protect|反义词:neglect, endanger|词性:动词

词源: From "over" + "watch".  "Over" in this context means "above" or "in authority over," and "watch" means "to look at or observe attentively."  Thus, "overwatch" literally means "to watch from above" or "to watch with authority."
