liverless:/ˈlɪvərlɪs/| adj.|无胆量的，怯懦的，没有活力的|Lacking courage, cowardly; lacking spirit or vitality.|He gave a liverless performance, barely moving on stage. 他表演得毫无生气，在舞台上几乎一动不动。|The liverless knight refused to face the dragon.  这位胆小的骑士拒绝面对恶龙。|近义词: cowardly, timid, spineless, gutless, spiritless|反义词: brave, courageous, bold, spirited|词性:形容词
词源: From liver + -less.  Historically, the liver was believed to be the seat of courage and passion.  Therefore, "liverless" literally means lacking a liver, hence lacking those qualities.
