interne:/ɪnˈtɜːrn/ | n. | 实习生，实习医生 | A doctor who has just graduated from medical school and is receiving supervised practical training in a hospital. |  Example 1: The interne worked long hours in the emergency room.  实习医生在急诊室工作了很长时间。

Example 2: The hospital hired several new internes this year.  这家医院今年雇佣了几名新的实习医生。 | Synonym:  trainee, resident, intern (US spelling) | Antonym: experienced doctor, specialist | noun
