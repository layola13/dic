meal:/miːl/ | n. |  (谷物的)粗粉，粗粮 | Coarsely ground grain. | They used meal to make bread.  他们用粗粮做面包。| synonyms: flour, grain | antonyms: refined flour | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *mǣl (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *mālō (“time, appointed time, occasion, meal”), from Proto-Indo-European *mol- (“to assemble, meet”). Cognate with Old Frisian mēl (“time, meal”), Old Saxon māl (“time, occasion”), Old High German māla (“time, occasion”), Old Norse mál (“speaking, talk, case at law; time, fixed time”), Gothic 𐌼𐌴𐌻 (mēl, “hour”).
