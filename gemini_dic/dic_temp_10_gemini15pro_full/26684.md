cadaverousness:/kəˈdævərəsnəs/
noun
形容词性：cadaverous /kəˈdævərəs/
憔悴；面色苍白；尸体似的
Resembling a corpse; gaunt and pale.  The illness had given him a cadaverous appearance. 疾病使他面色苍白，形容枯槁。
His face had a cadaverous hue. 他的脸色苍白如尸体。
gaunt, pale, haggard, emaciated, skeletal, ghastly, wan, ashen, deathlike, corpselike
healthy, robust, flourishing
noun
