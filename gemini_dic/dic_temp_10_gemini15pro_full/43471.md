crassamentum:/kræsəˈmɛntəm/| n. | 血块，凝血块 |A thick, coagulated mass, especially of blood.  It refers to the solid portion of blood that separates from the serum after clotting. |The crassamentum was clearly visible in the test tube after the blood sample coagulated. 血液样本凝固后，试管中清晰可见凝血块。|Synonyms: clot, coagulum |Antonyms: serum, plasma|noun
