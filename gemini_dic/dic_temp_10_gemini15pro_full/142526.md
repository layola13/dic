ossification:/ˌɒsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ /ˌɑːsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.|骨化，骨化作用|The process of bone formation, in which cartilage or other soft tissue is converted into bone.|例句：The ossification of the cartilage begins in the fetus.  胎儿的软骨开始骨化。

例句：The doctor observed the ossification of the fracture.  医生观察了骨折的骨化情况。


近义词：calcification, hardening
反义词：decalcification, softening
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ossificationem,  from Latin ossificatus, past participle of ossificare "to turn into bone," from os "bone" + -ficare,  combining form of facere "to make."
