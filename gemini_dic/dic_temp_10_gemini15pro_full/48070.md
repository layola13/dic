declarative:/dɪˈklærətɪv/| adj. n.|陈述的，声明的; 陈述句|A declarative sentence states a fact or an opinion.  一个陈述句陈述一个事实或一种观点。|例句： "The sky is blue" is a declarative sentence. “天空是蓝色的”是一个陈述句。|近义词：assertive, indicative|反义词：interrogative, imperative|词性:形容词,名词

declaratives:/dɪˈklærətɪvz/| n.|陈述句|Declaratives are sentences that make a statement. 陈述句是作出陈述的句子。|例句：Most of the sentences in this paragraph are declaratives. 本段中的大多数句子都是陈述句。|近义词：statements, assertions|反义词：questions, commands|词性:名词

词源:  From Late Latin declarativus, from Latin declarare (“to make clear, explain”), from de- (“fully”) + clarare (“to make clear”), from clarus (“clear”).  源自晚期拉丁语 declarativus，来自拉丁语 declarare（“弄清楚，解释”），来自 de-（“完全地”）+ clarare（“弄清楚”），来自 clarus（“清楚的”）。
