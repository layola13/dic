frumpish:/ˈfrʌmpɪʃ/ | adj. | 邋遢的；古板的；闷闷不乐的 |  Dressed in an old-fashioned or unstylish way; also, bad-tempered and grumpy.  Having the appearance of a frump; dowdy. |  She looked rather frumpish in that outdated dress. 她穿着那件过时的裙子看起来很邋遢。| synonyms: dowdy, drab, frumpy, old-fashioned, stuffy, unfashionable | antonyms: stylish, fashionable, chic, trendy, smart | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain, possibly from dialectal *frump "to sulk, be in a bad mood," of unknown origin. First recorded in the late 18th century.
