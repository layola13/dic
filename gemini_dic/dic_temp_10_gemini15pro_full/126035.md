mussulmanly:/mʌzəlˈmənli/
adjective
穆斯林式的；像穆斯林的
In a manner characteristic of or suitable for a Muslim.
He greeted them with a mussulmanly salaam. 他以穆斯林式的敬礼迎接他们。
Near Synonyms: Islamic, Muslim
Antonyms: non-Muslim, secular
Etymology: From Mussulman (an archaic term for Muslim) + -ly (adverbial suffix).
