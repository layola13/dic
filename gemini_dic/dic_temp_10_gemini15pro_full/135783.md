nonprogressively:/ˌnɒnprəˈɡrɛsɪvli/ | adv. | 非进行式地，非持续地 | Not in a progressive or continuous manner; not developing or increasing gradually. | The verb "to know" is used nonprogressively.  动词"know"是非进行式动词。|  He stated his opinion nonprogressively. 他直截了当地陈述了他的观点。 | 近义词: statically, constantly, invariably | 反义词: progressively, gradually, continuously | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + progressively (adverbial form of progressive, from Latin progressus, past participle of progredi "to go forward," pro- "forward" + gradi "to walk")
