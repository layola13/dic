enacted:/ɪˈnæktɪd/
verb
已制定的，已颁布的
Made into law; passed, as legislation.  Put into practice; carried out.
The law was enacted in 2003.  该法律于2003年颁布。
The play was enacted on a bare stage. 这部戏剧在一个空荡荡的舞台上演。
近义词:passed, legislated, performed, executed
反义词:repealed, revoked, cancelled
