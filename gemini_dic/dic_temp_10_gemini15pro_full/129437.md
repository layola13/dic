noms:/nɑːmz/ | n. | 提名，任命；（法）名称 | nominations (plural), often shortened to "noms."  It refers to the act of officially suggesting or proposing someone for a position, honor, or award. In a legal context (especially in French), it can refer to a name or designation. |  The Academy Award noms were announced this morning. 奥斯卡奖提名今天早上公布了。  | synonyms: nominations, appointments, designations | antonyms: rejections, dismissals | noun
