covenantee:/ˌkʌvənænˈtiː/
n.
受约人，契约受益人
The person to whom a promise is made in a covenant.
The covenantee has the right to enforce the terms of the agreement. 受约人有权执行协议条款。
Synonym: beneficiary, recipient
Antonym: covenantor, promisor
noun
