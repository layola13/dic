musted:/ˈmʌstɪd/| v. |必定，一定（过去时态，表示推测）|A past tense form indicating a past deduction or inference; used to express a strong belief that something was true in the past.|He musted have known the truth. 他当时一定知道真相。|近义词：had to, must have, probably, likely|反义词: couldn't have, unlikely|词性:动词

词源:  From the verb "must," which has its roots in Old English *mōste, the past tense of *mōtan "to be allowed, have to."

一般过去时 (Simple Past):

She musted have been very upset. 她当时一定很难过。

不适用，musted表示对过去状态的推测，而不是进行的动作。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
不适用，"musted"本身蕴含过去完成的含义，且不强调持续性。
