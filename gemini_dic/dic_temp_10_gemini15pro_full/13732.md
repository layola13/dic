aubergine:/ˈoʊbərʒiːn/ (UK) /ˈoʊbərˌʒiːn/ (US)| n.|茄子|A dark purple vegetable, long and rounded in shape, which is eaten cooked.|例句：I'm making moussaka with aubergines tonight. 今晚我要用茄子做穆萨卡。

例句：The recipe calls for two large aubergines.  这个食谱需要两个大茄子。|近义词：eggplant (US)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
