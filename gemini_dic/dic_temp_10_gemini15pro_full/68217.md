fairgrounds:/ˈferɡraʊndz/
noun
露天遊樂場；展覽會場
An area of land where a fair is held.
The fairgrounds were packed with people enjoying the rides and games. 露天遊樂場擠滿了享受遊樂設施和遊戲的人們。
The county fairgrounds host a variety of events throughout the year. 縣露天遊樂場全年舉辦各種活動。
Synonyms: fair, carnival, exhibition grounds, festival grounds
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider) office, home, school
