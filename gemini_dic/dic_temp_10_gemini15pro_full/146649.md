The overharshnesses of the critique were unnecessary and demoralizing.  这篇评论的过度严厉是不必要的，而且令人沮丧。
Etymology: "over-" (excessive) + "harsh" (rough, unpleasant) + "-ness" (noun suffix indicating a state or quality)
例句：
The overharshnesses in his tone alienated his colleagues.  他语气中的过度苛刻疏远了他的同事。

His overharshnesses toward his children created resentment. 他对孩子的过度严厉造成了怨恨。

反义词：leniency, gentleness, mildness, kindness
