despising:/dɪˈspaɪzɪŋ/
Verb, present participle|鄙视，蔑视，看不起|Feeling contempt or a deep repugnance for.|He was despising her for her weakness.|近义词：loathing, abhorring, detesting, hating, scornful|反义词：admiring, respecting, revering|v.

Etymology:
From Middle English *despisen*, from Old French *despiser*, from Latin *despicere* (“to look down upon, despise”), from *de-* (“down, away”) + *specere* (“to look at”).
