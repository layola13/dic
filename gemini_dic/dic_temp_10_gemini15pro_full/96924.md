inexpressiblenesses:/ˌɪnɪkˈsprɛsəbəlnəsɪz/
n.
无法表达的事物；不可名状的事物
The state or quality of being inexpressible; things that cannot be expressed.
Her face held a mixture of emotions, inexpressiblenesses that words couldn't capture. 她的脸上流露出复杂的情感，言语无法捕捉的不可名状之情。
The vastness of the universe filled him with a sense of awe and inexpressibleness. 宇宙的浩瀚让他充满了敬畏和无法言喻之感。
近义词：unutterabilities, ineffabilities
反义词：expressiveness, articulateness
词性：名词


词源：
From inexpressible + -ness.  "Inexpressible" comes from the Latin "in-" (not) + "exprimibilis" (expressible), which is derived from "exprimere" (to express).
