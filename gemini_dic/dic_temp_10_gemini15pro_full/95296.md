incarnated:/ɪnˈkɑːrneɪtɪd/
adj.
 воплощенный, олицетворенный (Russian)
given human form; embodied in flesh; personified
Example:  The devil incarnate. =  魔鬼的化身。

Example:  She is kindness incarnate. = 她是善良的化身。
synonym:embodied, personified, materialized
antonym:disembodied, spiritual, ethereal
word type:adjective

incarnate:/ˈɪnkɑːrneɪt/
vt.
 воплощать, олицетворять (Russian)
to give bodily form to; embody; personify
Example: The artist incarnated his vision in his paintings. = 艺术家在他的画作中体现了他的愿景。

Example:  He incarnates everything I admire in a leader. = 他体现了我所钦佩的领导者的一切品质。
synonym:embody, personify, represent
antonym:disperse, separate, diffuse
word type:verb
