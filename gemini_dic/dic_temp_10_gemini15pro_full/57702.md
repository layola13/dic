drawstring:/ˈdrɔːstrɪŋ/| n.|束带，拉绳，抽绳|A string or cord used to draw together or tighten something, typically the neck of a bag or the waist of a garment.|The drawstring on my sweatpants broke. 我的运动裤的抽绳断了。|synonyms: cord, string, tie, lace|antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "fastener" in some contexts)|noun

drawstring:/ˈdrɔːstrɪŋ/| adj.|有束带的，用绳子束紧的|Having a drawstring.|This is a drawstring bag. 这是一个束口袋。|synonyms:  laced, tied, corded|antonyms: (No direct antonym)|adjective
