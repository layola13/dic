naphthalin:/ˈnæfθəlɪn/ | n. |萘，卫生球|A white crystalline aromatic hydrocarbon, C10H8, analogous to benzene, and used in mothballs, as an antiseptic, etc. |例句：The smell of naphthalin filled the closet.  衣橱里充满了萘的气味。

例句：She put naphthalin balls in her drawers to protect her clothes from moths. 她把萘球放进抽屉里以保护衣服免受飞蛾的侵害。|近义词：mothballs, camphor |反义词: None |词性:noun
