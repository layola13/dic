counterstepped:/ˈkaʊntərˌstɛpt/ | adj. |反台阶的，阶梯状的 |Having steps or terraces arranged in opposite directions or on opposite sides, resembling a flight of stairs but reversed. |The counterstepped wall provided extra stability against landslides. 反台阶的墙体提供了额外的稳定性，防止山体滑坡。| synonym: terraced, stepped | antonym: smooth, flat | adjective

counterstepped:/ˈkaʊntərˌstɛpt/ | v. |反台阶式建造，交错排列 |To build or arrange in a counterstepped manner. |They counterstepped the retaining wall to strengthen it. 他们用反台阶的方式建造了挡土墙以增强其强度。 | synonym: stagger, offset | antonym: align, straighten | verb
