oversolicitous: /ˌoʊvərˌsəˈlɪsɪtəs/ | adj. | 过分关心的，过分殷勤的 | Characterized by excessive concern and attentiveness, often to the point of being intrusive or suffocating. |  She was so oversolicitous towards her son that he felt smothered. 她对她儿子过分关心，让他感到窒息。|  He fussed over his guests in an oversolicitous manner. 他过分殷勤地招待客人。 | 近义词: fussy, meddling, intrusive, officious | 反义词: neglectful, indifferent, inattentive | 词性: adjective

词源: From "over-" (excessive) + "solicitous" (showing care and concern), ultimately from Latin "sollicitus" (anxious, careful).
