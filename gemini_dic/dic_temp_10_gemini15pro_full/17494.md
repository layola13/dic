bearlike:/ˈbeərlaɪk/| adj.|像熊的，熊一样的|Resembling a bear in appearance or behavior.  Having qualities (such as strength, size, or shagginess) characteristic of a bear.|The wrestler had a bearlike physique.  那个摔跤运动员有着像熊一样的体格。|近义词：ursine, bearish|反义词:small, weak|词性:形容词
Etymology: bear +‎ -like
