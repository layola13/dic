hassocky: /ˈhæsəki/ | adj. | 草垫似的；草垫做的；多草丛的；多小丘的 | Covered with or resembling hassocks; tufted.  |  The ground was hassocky and uneven.  地面上长满了草丛，凹凸不平。 | 近义词: tussocky, bumpy, uneven, rough | 反义词: smooth, flat, even | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From hassock + -y.  Hassock itself comes from Old English *hassuc, of unknown origin.
