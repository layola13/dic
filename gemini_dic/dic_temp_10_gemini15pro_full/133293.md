nonexpendable:/ˌnɒnɪkˈspendəbl/ (US) /ˌnɒnɪkˈspendəbl/ (UK)| adj. n.|非消耗性的，非消耗品|Not meant to be expended or used up; designed for continuous use or preservation.  It can also be used as a noun to refer to something that is nonexpendable.|例句：The tools were classified as nonexpendable and had to be returned after use. 这些工具被归类为非消耗品，使用后必须归还。

例句：Ammunition and fuel are expendable, but the vehicles are nonexpendable. 弹药和燃料是消耗品，但车辆是非消耗品。|近义词：durable, reusable, permanent, essential|反义词：expendable, consumable, disposable|词性:形容词, 名词

词源:  From non- + expendable, from expend + -able.  Ultimately from Latin expendere ("to weigh out, pay out, spend").
