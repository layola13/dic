nakora: /nəˈkɔːrə/ | n. |  纳科拉鼓 (一种双面鼓，源自印度)| A double-sided barrel drum from India, similar to a mridangam but larger. | Example: The nakora player accompanied the sitarist with complex rhythmic patterns. 纳科拉鼓演奏者以复杂的节奏模式为西塔琴演奏者伴奏。

Example:  The resonant sound of the nakora filled the concert hall.  纳科拉鼓的共鸣声充满了音乐厅。

|Synonyms:  mridangam (though not exactly the same), barrel drum| Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a musical instrument) | noun
