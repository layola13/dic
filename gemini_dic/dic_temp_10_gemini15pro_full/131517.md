noncombining:/ˌnɒnkəmˈbaɪnɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːnkəmˈbaɪnɪŋ/ | adj. |非组合的，不结合的 | Not combining or uniting with other elements, especially in linguistics referring to diacritical marks that are written separately from the base character and not merged with it.  | This font uses noncombining diacritical marks.  这个字体使用非组合的变音符号。| Near synonyms: separate, independent, standalone | Antonyms: combining,  ligated, joined | adjective
