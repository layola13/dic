cross-checkers:/ˈkrɒsˌtʃɛkəz/ | n. | 复核员，核查员 | People who verify information or data by checking it against another source.  |  The cross-checkers ensured the accuracy of the survey results by comparing them with previous data.  复核员通过将调查结果与先前的数据进行比较，确保了调查结果的准确性。| synonyms: verifiers, validators, auditors | antonyms:  N/A (lack of direct antonym) | noun
