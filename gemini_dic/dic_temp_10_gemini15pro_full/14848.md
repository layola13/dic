axletree: /ˈæksəlˌtriː/ | n. | 车轴 | A bar connecting the opposite wheels of a vehicle or cart.  A fixed bar or beam, usually of wood or metal, with a central spindle on which a pair of wheels rotates. | The old wagon's axletree broke under the heavy load.  那辆旧马车在重负荷下车轴断了。| synonyms: axle, shaft | antonyms: N/A | noun
