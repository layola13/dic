armistice: /ˈɑːrmɪstɪs/ | n. |停战，休战；休战协定|An agreement made by opposing sides in a war to stop fighting for a certain time.|The armistice was signed at 11:00 a.m. on November 11, 1918.  1918年11月11日上午11点签署了停战协定。|Synonyms: truce, ceasefire|Antonyms: war, hostilities|Noun

Etymology:
From Old French *armistice, from Late Latin armistitium, from Latin arma "arms" + sistere "to cause to stand, stop."
