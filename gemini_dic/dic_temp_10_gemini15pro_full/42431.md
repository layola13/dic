counterculturist:/ˈkaʊntərˈkʌltʃərɪst/ | n. | 反主流文化者，反文化者 | A person who opposes the dominant values and behavior of society.  |  Example 1: The counterculturist rejected consumerism and embraced a simpler lifestyle.  反主流文化者拒绝消费主义，拥抱更简单的生活方式。

Example 2:  Many counterculturists in the 1960s experimented with alternative lifestyles and spiritual practices. 许多20世纪60年代的反主流文化者尝试另类的生活方式和精神实践。 | 近义词: nonconformist, rebel, bohemian | 反义词: conformist, traditionalist | 词性: 名词
