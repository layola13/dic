engorging:/ɪnˈɡɔːrdʒɪŋ/ | v. | 充盈；充血；狼吞虎咽 | cause to swell with blood, milk, or other fluid; overeat or eat immodestly;  | The mosquito was engorging itself with blood.  蚊子正在吸血。| Synonyms:  gorging, stuffing, cramming, overeating | Antonyms:  depleting, emptying, starving | verb

engorged:/ɪnˈɡɔːrdʒd/ | adj. | 充盈的；充血的 | filled to excess; congested | The tick was engorged with blood after feeding.  壁蝨吸饱了血后鼓胀起来。| Synonyms:  swollen, distended, bloated | Antonyms:  empty, deflated, shrunken | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French engorger "to obstruct, choke up," from en- "in" + gorge "throat."
