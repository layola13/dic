mistruster:/mɪsˈtrʌstər/| n.|不信任者；怀疑者|Someone who mistrusts; a skeptical person.  A person who is inclined to doubt or suspect. |例句：He was a known mistruster of authority figures. 他是有名的不信任权威人物的人。

例句：She became a mistruster of all politicians after the scandal.  丑闻之后，她对所有的政客都产生了怀疑。|近义词：skeptic, doubter, cynic|反义词：believer, truster, optimist|词性:noun
