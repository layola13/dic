dinner-table:/ˈdɪnər ˌteɪbl/| n.|餐桌|A table at which dinner is eaten.|We gathered around the dinner-table for a family meal. 我们围坐在餐桌旁享用家庭聚餐。|近义词：dining table|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun


dinner-table:/ˈdɪnər ˌteɪbl/| adj.|(Less common usage) Relating to or suitable for dinner conversation.|Dinner-table conversation should be kept light and pleasant.  餐桌上的谈话应该保持轻松愉快。|近义词：dining, mealtime|反义词: (No direct antonym)|adjective
