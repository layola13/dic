forsworn:/fɔːrˈswɔːrn/
adjective
背弃誓言的；作伪证的
Having violated an oath or solemn promise.
He was a forsworn knight, stripped of his title and banished. 他是一个背弃誓言的骑士，被剥夺了头衔并被放逐。
The forsworn witness was sentenced to prison.  作伪证的证人被判入狱。
近义词：perjured, traitorous, faithless
反义词：faithful, loyal, true
词性：adjective

词源:
From Old English *forswerian (unattested), from for- "away, against" + swerian "to swear."  Meaning "having violated an oath" from late 14c.
