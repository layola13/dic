ergometer:/ɜːrˈɡɒmɪtər/ /ɜːrˈɡɑːmɪtər/
n.|测力计，功率计|An instrument for measuring the work performed by a person exercising.  It often resembles a stationary bicycle or rowing machine.|The athlete trained on the ergometer to measure his power output.|Synonyms: dynamometer, exercise bike, rowing machine|Antonyms: None|Noun
