befuddled:/bɪˈfʌdld/| adj.|困惑的，糊涂的|Confused and unable to think clearly.|例句：He was completely befuddled by the complex instructions. 他完全被复杂的指示弄糊涂了。|例句：The long flight and lack of sleep left her feeling befuddled. 长途飞行和睡眠不足让她感到昏昏沉沉。|近义词：confused, bewildered, perplexed, addled|反义词：clear-headed, lucid, cogent|词性:形容词

词源: From be- (making a verb causative) + fuddle (to make drunk, confuse).  Ultimately of uncertain origin, possibly related to Old English *fugel, bird, suggesting the unsteady gait of a drunk person.
