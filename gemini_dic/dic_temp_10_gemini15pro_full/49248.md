deleterious:/ˌdɛlɪˈtɪəriəs/| adj.|有害的，有毒的|Harmful often in a subtle or unexpected way.|The chemical has deleterious effects on the environment. 这种化学物质对环境有有害影响。|近义词：harmful, damaging, injurious, detrimental, noxious, pernicious|反义词：beneficial, advantageous, salutary|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *dēletērius* "injurious, destructive," from Latin *dēlēre* "to destroy, blot out, efface," from *de-* "down, away" + *lēre* "to smear, blot."
