bleary:/ˈblɪəri/ | adj. | 模糊的，朦胧的；视线模糊的；睡眼惺忪的 | (of eyes or vision) blurred or dimmed, as from sleep or weariness;  |  例句：He woke up with bleary eyes after a late night.  他熬夜后醒来，睡眼惺忪。 | 近义词：blurred, dimmed, hazy, misty, sleepy | 反义词：clear, sharp, focused | 词性: 形容词

bleary:/ˈblɪəri/ | adj. | 模糊不清的, 朦胧的| (of a situation or state of mind) unclear or confused. | 例句：The future looked bleary and uncertain. 未来看起来一片渺茫，充满不确定性。 | 近义词：unclear, uncertain, hazy, vague | 反义词：clear, distinct, definite | 词性: 形容词
