outform:/ˌaʊtˈfɔːrm/
verb
胜过，优于
to perform better than someone or something
例句：The company has consistently outformed its rivals in recent years.  这家公司近年来一直胜过其竞争对手。

例句：Our new product is outforming all the others on the market. 我们的新产品胜过市场上所有其他产品。

近义词：outperform, surpass, exceed, beat, best
反义词：underperform
词性：v.
