engraved:/ɪnˈɡreɪvd/ | adj. | 雕刻的，铭刻的 | Carved, etched, or impressed into a surface. |  The couple exchanged rings engraved with their initials.  这对夫妇交换了刻有他们姓名首字母的戒指。| Synonyms: etched, carved, inscribed, imprinted | Antonyms: plain, smooth, unembellished | adjective

engrave:/ɪnˈɡreɪv/ | vt. | 雕刻，铭刻 | To carve, etch, or impress (a design, inscription, etc.) into a surface. | He engraved his name on the tree. 他把他的名字刻在树上。 | Synonyms: etch, carve, inscribe, imprint | Antonyms: erase, efface | verb
