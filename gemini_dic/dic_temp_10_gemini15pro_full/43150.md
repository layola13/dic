cozily:/ˈkoʊzɪli/ | adv. | 舒适地，惬意地 | In a cozy manner; snugly and comfortably. |  We sat cozily by the fire. 我们舒适地坐在火炉旁。 | snugly, comfortably, warmly | uncomfortably, coldly | 副词

Etymology:
From cozy + -ly. Cozy itself comes from Middle English *cosy, of uncertain origin, perhaps related to Scots cosie (“sheltered, snug”).
