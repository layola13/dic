defragmentation:/ˌdiːfræɡmɛnˈteɪʃən/| n. |碎片整理，磁盘碎片整理|The act or process of reducing fragmentation, especially of files on a computer disk, by rearranging data to occupy contiguous storage locations.|After the defragmentation, my computer runs much faster. 磁盘碎片整理后，我的电脑运行速度快多了。|synonyms: consolidation, optimization, reorganization|antonyms: fragmentation, scattering, disorganization|noun

defragmentations:/ˌdiːfræɡmɛnˈteɪʃənz/| n. |碎片整理（复数）|Plural form of "defragmentation," referring to multiple instances of the process of reducing fragmentation.|Regular defragmentations can improve system performance. 定期进行碎片整理可以提高系统性能。|synonyms: consolidations, optimizations, reorganizations|antonyms: fragmentations, scatterings, disorganizations|noun
