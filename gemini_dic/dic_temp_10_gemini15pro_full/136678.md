nonsecretion:/ˌnɑːnsɪˈkriːʃən/
n.
无分泌；不分泌
Lack or absence of secretion; failure to secrete.
The patient's nonsecretion of insulin led to high blood sugar levels. 病人的胰岛素不分泌导致高血糖。
近义词:asecretion
反义词:secretion

词性: 名词

词源: non- (not) + secretion (the process of producing and releasing a substance)
