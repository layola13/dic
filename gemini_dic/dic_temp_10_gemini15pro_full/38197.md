compulsivenesses:/kəmˈpʌlsɪvnɪsɪz/| n.|强迫性，执念|The state of being compulsive; an irresistible urge to behave in a certain way, especially against one's conscious wishes.|Her compulsivenesses about cleanliness were starting to annoy her family. 她对清洁的执念开始惹恼她的家人。|例句：His various compulsivenesses made it difficult for him to maintain relationships.  他各种各样的强迫行为让他难以维持人际关系。

例句：Her compulsivenesses extended to checking the locks multiple times before leaving the house. 她的强迫行为延伸到出门前要反复检查门锁很多次。|近义词：obsessions, fixations, preoccupations|反义词：indifference, apathy|词性: Noun


词源:  From compulsive + -ness + -es (plural). Ultimately from Latin compellere "to compel."
