/ˈfɪltərd/ | adj. | 过滤的；筛选的 |  Something that has been filtered has had something removed from it, usually something unwanted. | The filtered water was clean and clear. 过滤后的水干净清澈。| purified, refined, strained | unfiltered, raw | adjective


/ˈfɪltərd/ | v. | 过滤；筛选（filter的过去分词） | To remove something unwanted from a substance by passing it through a device. | The coffee was filtered through a paper filter. 咖啡通过纸质过滤器过滤。| strain, sift, purify | contaminate, pollute | verb


Etymology:
From the French word "filtre," which in turn comes from the Medieval Latin "filtrum" meaning "felt."  Felt was a common material used in early filters.
