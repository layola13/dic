illusory:/ɪˈluːsəri/ /ɪˈluːsɔːri/| adj.|虚幻的，幻觉的|Based on illusion; not real.  Appearing real or possible but not actually so.|例句：The oasis in the desert was illusory, a mere mirage caused by the heat. 沙漠中的绿洲是虚幻的，仅仅是高温造成的幻影。

例句：His hopes of winning the lottery were illusory. 他中彩票的希望是虚幻的。|近义词：deceptive, misleading, fallacious, unreal, imagined, fanciful, chimerical|反义词：real, actual, genuine, tangible, substantial|词性:形容词
