batterings:/ˈbætərɪŋz/
noun|殴打，连续打击|repeated acts of hitting or striking someone or something.|The house took a battering during the storm.|Synonyms: beatings, poundings, thrashings|Antonyms: protections, safeguards|n.

battering:/ˈbætərɪŋ/
noun|殴打，连续打击|a single act of hitting or striking someone or something violently.|He gave the punching bag a battering.|Synonyms: beating, pounding, thrashing|Antonyms: protection, safeguard|n.

battering:/ˈbætərɪŋ/
adjective|猛烈的，持续的|characterized by repeated and violent attacks.|The battering winds tore at the sails.|Synonyms: relentless, persistent, constant|Antonyms: gentle, mild, intermittent|adj.
