comfortress: /ˈkʌmfətrɪs/
noun
女安慰者；给予安慰的女性
a woman who provides comfort
She acted as a comfortress to her grieving friend. 她作为安慰者陪伴着她悲伤的朋友。
近义词: comforter, consoler, soother
反义词: tormentor, harasser
词性: noun

Etymology:
From comfort + -ess (a suffix used to form feminine nouns).  Ultimately from Latin *confortare "to strengthen much," from com- "together" + fortis "strong."
