minnesinger:/ˈmɪnəˌsɪŋər/ | n. |恋歌歌手，行吟歌手|A German lyric poet and singer of the 12th to 14th centuries, similar to the French troubadours.|例句：The minnesinger Walther von der Vogelweide is considered one of the greatest German lyric poets. 恋歌歌手瓦尔特·冯·德·福格尔魏德被认为是德国最伟大的抒情诗人之一。

例句：Minnesingers often sang about courtly love and chivalry. 恋歌歌手经常歌颂宫廷爱情和骑士精神。|近义词：troubadour, bard, poet, singer |反义词: None | 词性: noun
