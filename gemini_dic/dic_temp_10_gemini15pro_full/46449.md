daguerreotypers:/dəˈɡɛrəˌtaɪpərz/
n.
达盖尔照相师，银版照相师
A person who takes daguerreotypes, an early type of photograph.
Louis Daguerre and other early daguerreotypers experimented with different chemicals and exposure times. 路易·达盖尔和其他早期的达盖尔照相师尝试了不同的化学物质和曝光时间。
Several daguerreotypers set up studios in the burgeoning city. 几位银版照相师在这座新兴的城市里开设了工作室。
