fallen:/ˈfɔːlən/ (US) /ˈfɔːlən/ (UK)| adj. |堕落的，倒下的， fallen leaves 落叶; fallen angel 堕落天使|Something that has fallen is lying on the ground after falling from a higher position.  /  Used to refer to someone who has died in a war or other conflict. /Morally bad or wicked. |The tree had fallen across the road.  这棵树倒在路上。 /  Our fallen comrades will never be forgotten. 我们牺牲的同志将永远不会被遗忘。/  a fallen woman 失足妇女| 近义词：dropped, down, toppled, collapsed, deceased (for a person), degraded (morally) | 反义词：standing, upright, risen, virtuous|词性:形容词

fallen:/ˈfɔːlən/ (US) /ˈfɔːlən/ (UK)| past participle of fall| 跌倒，落下| Used as the past participle of the verb "fall." |Snow has fallen steadily all day. 雪下了一整天。/  He had fallen asleep on the sofa. 他在沙发上睡着了。|近义词：(As a verb) dropped, tumbled, descended|反义词：(As a verb) risen, ascended|词性:动词(过去分词)
