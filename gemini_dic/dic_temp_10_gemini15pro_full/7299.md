/ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ/ | n. | 语言 | A system of communication consisting of sounds, words, and grammar used by a particular country or community.  | Example:  She speaks several languages fluently. 她能流利地说几种语言。

Etymology:
From Old French langage, from langue (“tongue”), from Latin lingua (“tongue, language”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *dʰ₁engʷʰ- (“tongue”).
