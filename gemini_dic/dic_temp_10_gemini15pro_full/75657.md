fukushima:/ˌfʊkuˈʃiːmə/ | n. | 福岛 | Fukushima is a prefecture in Japan.  It is also the name of the capital city of the prefecture.  It became internationally known after the 2011 nuclear disaster. | Fukushima Prefecture was severely affected by the earthquake and tsunami. 福岛县受地震和海啸的严重影响。

Fukushima Daiichi Nuclear Power Plant suffered a meltdown after the tsunami. 福岛第一核电站海啸后发生熔毁。 |  近义词: (Referring to the disaster) nuclear disaster site, disaster area | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps a thriving area or a safe zone) | 词性: noun
