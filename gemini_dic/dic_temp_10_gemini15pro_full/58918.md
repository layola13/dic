durum:/ˈdjʊərəm/ (US), /ˈdʒʊərəm/ (UK)| n. |硬质小麦，杜兰小麦|A type of hard wheat, high in gluten, used to make pasta and couscous.|Example: Durum wheat is the key ingredient in authentic Italian pasta. 杜兰小麦是正宗意大利面食的关键成分。

Example: The semolina used for making couscous is derived from durum. 用于制作库斯的粗粒小麦粉来源于硬质小麦。|近义词：wheat, hard wheat|反义词:soft wheat|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dūrum, accusative singular neuter of dūrus (“hard”).
