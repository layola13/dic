instantaneity:/ˌɪnstənteɪˈniːɪti/ | n. |瞬间性，即时性|The quality or state of being instantaneous; occurrence at a particular instant. |The instantaneity of the explosion was terrifying. 爆炸的瞬间非常可怕。|synonyms: immediacy, instantaneousness, suddenness |antonyms: gradualness, slowness |noun
