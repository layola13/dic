life-givers:/ˈlaɪfˌɡɪvərz/
n.|赋予生命者；生命之源|Those who or that which gives life.  This can refer to things like water, the sun, or parents.|The ancient Egyptians revered the Nile as the life-giver of their civilization. 古埃及人尊崇尼罗河是他们文明的生命之源。|synonyms: creators, sustainers, nurturers|antonyms: destroyers, takers, killers|noun
