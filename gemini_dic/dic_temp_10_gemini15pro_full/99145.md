intercessorial:/ˌɪntərˈsɛsəriəl/ | adj. | 代祷的，祈求的 | Relating to or involving intercession; acting as an intercessor. | The priest offered intercessorial prayers for the sick.  神父为病人做了代祷。|  近义词: mediating, pleading, intervening | 反义词:  unconcerned, indifferent | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *intercessorius, from Latin intercessor (“mediator”), from intercedere (“to intervene, intercede”), from inter (“between”) + cedere (“to go, yield”).
