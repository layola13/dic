fulsome:/ˈfʊlsəm/ | adj. | 过分恭维的，令人作呕的; 过多的，过度的 | characterized by abundance, often to excess; flattering to an excessive degree |  He gave a fulsome speech praising the dictator.  他发表了一通令人作呕的演讲，赞扬了独裁者。| synonyms: excessive, extravagant, overdone, immoderate, inordinate, copious, profuse | antonyms: restrained, moderate, understated, meager, scanty | adjective

fulsome:/ˈfʊlsəm/ | adj. | 令人厌恶的; 丰满的 | causing nausea or disgust; offensively flattering or insincere  | The fulsome smell of the rotting meat made me gag. 腐肉令人作呕的气味让我感到恶心。 | synonyms: nauseating, sickening, disgusting, repulsive | antonyms: pleasant, agreeable, appealing | adjective
