floodgates:/ˈflʌdɡeɪts/| n.|防洪闸门；闸门；(喻)开端|a gate used to control the flow of water, especially in a flood barrier. Figuratively, it refers to something that, once opened, allows a large quantity of something else to be released.|The dam burst, opening the floodgates and inundating the valley.  大坝决堤，闸门大开，淹没了山谷。|近义词:gates, barriers, sluices|反义词:None|noun

floodgates:/ˈflʌdɡeɪts/| n.|（尤指坏事的）闸门， floodgate |something that, once opened or released, allows a large quantity of something else to emerge, especially something which has been held back. This is typically used in a figurative sense.|His election victory opened the floodgates for similar populist candidates. 他的选举胜利为类似的民粹主义候选人打开了闸门。|近义词:outpouring, deluge, torrent|反义词:restriction, constraint|noun

PHRASE->
open the floodgates: 打开闸门；引发…的涌现
The new law opened the floodgates to a wave of lawsuits.  这项新法律引发了诉讼浪潮。
close the floodgates: 关闭闸门；阻止…的涌现
The government tried to close the floodgates on illegal immigration. 政府试图阻止非法移民的涌入。
floodgates of emotion: 情感的闸门
The tragedy opened the floodgates of emotion. 这场悲剧打开了情感的闸门。
a flood of something: 大量的…
The announcement led to a flood of applications.  这项公告导致了大量的申请。
burst the floodgates:  冲破闸门；大量涌现
The dam burst, and the floodgates burst, sending a torrent of water downstream. 大坝决堤，闸门崩塌，洪水奔涌而下。
