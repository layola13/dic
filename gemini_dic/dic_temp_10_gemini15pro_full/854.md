abstractions: /æbˈstrækʃənz/
n.
抽象概念，抽象物
The process of considering something independently of its associations, attributes, or concrete accompaniments.  The act of withdrawing or removing something.
He was lost in abstract thought. 他陷入了抽象的思考中。
The artist's abstractions were difficult to interpret.  这位艺术家的抽象作品难以理解。

近义词: concepts, ideas, generalizations, idealizations
反义词: specifics, details, concretes, realities
词性: noun (plural)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin abstractionem (nominative abstractio) "a drawing away, withdrawal," noun of action from past-participle stem of abstrahere "to draw away," from ab- "away" + trahere "to draw."

USAGE->
Countable Noun:
The artist uses color and form to express complex abstractions. = 艺术家运用色彩和形式来表达复杂的抽象概念。
His philosophical abstractions were difficult for the average person to grasp. = 他哲学上的抽象概念对于普通人来说难以理解。
