exhibit:/ɪɡˈzɪbɪt/ /ɛɡˈzɪbɪt/| n. v.|展览品，展出；表现，流露|An object or collection of objects put on public display. To show something publicly.|例句：The museum is exhibiting a collection of ancient artifacts.  博物馆正在展出一批古代文物。|近义词：display, show, present, demonstrate, reveal, manifest|反义词：conceal, hide|词性:名词,动词
exhibit:/ɪɡˈzɪbɪt/ /ɛɡˈzɪbɪt/| v.|展出，展览；表现，流露|To show something publicly. To show a quality, feeling, or type of behavior.|例句：He exhibited great courage in the face of danger.  他在危险面前表现出极大的勇气。|近义词：display, show, present, demonstrate, reveal, manifest|反义词：conceal, hide|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *exhibitus*, past participle of *exhibere*  "to hold out, display, show," from *ex-* "out" + *habere* "to hold."
