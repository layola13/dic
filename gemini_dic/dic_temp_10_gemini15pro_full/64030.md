epithelial:/ˌɛpɪˈθiːliəl/| adj.|上皮的，上皮组织的|Relating to or consisting of epithelium.|The epithelial cells lining the lungs are crucial for gas exchange.  肺部排列的上皮细胞对于气体交换至关重要。|近义词: epidermal|反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "connective tissue" in some contexts) |词性: adjective

epithelium:/ˌɛpɪˈθiːliəm/| n.|上皮，上皮组织|The thin tissue forming the outer layer of a body's surface and lining the alimentary canal and other hollow structures.|The intestinal epithelium absorbs nutrients from food. 肠道上皮吸收食物中的营养。|近义词:  lining,  tissue|反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin *epithelium*, from Ancient Greek ἐπί (epí, “upon, on”) + θηλή (thēlḗ, “nipple”). Originally referred to the tissue on the nipple, but later extended to all similar tissues.
