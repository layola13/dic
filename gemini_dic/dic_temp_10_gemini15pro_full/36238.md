cognitivities:/ˌkɒɡnɪˈtɪvɪtiz/
n.
认知能力，认知活动
Mental processes involved in acquiring, processing, and storing knowledge.
Her research focuses on the cognitivities involved in language acquisition. 她的研究集中于语言习得中涉及的认知能力。
近义词：cognitive abilities, mental processes, cognitive functions
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用表示认知障碍的词) cognitive impairments, cognitive deficits
词性: noun (plural)


cognition:/kɒɡˈnɪʃn/
n.
认知，认识
The mental action or process of acquiring knowledge and understanding through thought, experience, and the senses.
Human cognition is a complex and fascinating field of study. 人类认知是一个复杂而迷人的研究领域。
近义词：perception, comprehension, understanding
反义词: ignorance, misunderstanding
词性: noun


cognitive:/ˈkɒɡnɪtɪv/
adj.
认知的，认识的
Relating to or involving the processes of thinking and reasoning.
Cognitive psychology explores mental processes such as memory and problem-solving. 认知心理学探索诸如记忆和解决问题的思维过程。
近义词：mental, intellectual, rational
反义词: emotional, instinctive
词性: adjective
