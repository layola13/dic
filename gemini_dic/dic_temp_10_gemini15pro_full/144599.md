outweave: /ˌaʊtˈwiːv/
verb
超越编织；胜过；技高一筹
to weave something more skillfully or elaborately than someone else; surpass in weaving
She outweaved her competitors with her intricate designs. 她用她复杂的设计胜过了她的竞争对手。
The spider outweaved its rival, creating a stronger, more beautiful web.  这只蜘蛛比它的对手织得更好，创造了一个更坚固、更美丽的网。
近义词: surpass, outdo, excel, beat, triumph over
反义词: lose to, be defeated by
词性: verb

Etymology:
From Old English *ūtwefan, from ūt (out) + wefan (to weave).  The prefix "out-" indicates surpassing or exceeding.
