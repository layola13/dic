lobsters:/ˈlɑːbstərz/ (US), /ˈlɒbstəz/ (UK)| n.|龙虾|A large marine crustacean with five pairs of legs, the first pair of which are modified into large claws.  Known for their hard shells and delicious meat.|例句：We had lobsters for dinner last night.  我们昨晚吃了龙虾。|近义词：crayfish, prawns|反义词: None|noun
