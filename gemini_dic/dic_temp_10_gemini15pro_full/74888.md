freshers:/ˈfreʃəz/ (UK) /ˈfrɛʃərz/ (US)| n.|新生；应届毕业生|People who are new to a particular role or experience, especially new university students.|The university held a welcome week for freshers. 大学为新生举办了迎新周。|Example: The company offers a special training program for freshers. 公司为新人提供专门的培训计划。|Synonyms: newcomers, beginners, initiates, probationers|Antonyms: veterans, experienced, seniors|Noun

freshman:/ˈfreʃmən/ (US) /ˈfrɛʃmən/ (US)| n.|大学一年级学生|A student in the first year of high school or college.|He's a freshman at Harvard. 他是哈佛大学的一年级学生。|Example:  The freshman dorm is always noisy. 大学新生宿舍总是很吵。|Synonyms: first-year student|Antonyms: senior, graduate|Noun
