broadcloth:/ˈbrɔːdklɔːθ/ (US) /ˈbrɔːdklɒθ/ (UK)| n. |细布，阔幅布 |A tightly woven fabric with a smooth, slightly napped surface, traditionally made of cotton or wool and used for shirts, dresses, and linings.| She wore a dress of fine blue broadcloth. 她穿着一件精致的蓝色细布连衣裙。|Synonyms: poplin, shirting, muslin|Antonyms: canvas, burlap|noun

broadcloth:/ˈbrɔːdklɔːθ/ (US) /ˈbrɔːdklɒθ/ (UK)| adj. |（指谎言）弥天大谎|A blatant or outrageous lie (informal). |That's a broadcloth if I ever heard one! 那是我听过的最大的谎言！|Synonyms: whopper, fabrication, lie|Antonyms: truth, fact|adjective
