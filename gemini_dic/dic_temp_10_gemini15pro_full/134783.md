nonmeteoric:/ˌnɒnˌmiːtiˈɔːrɪk/
adjective
非流星的，非陨石的
Not relating to meteors; not of meteoric origin.
The scientist studied the nonmeteoric rocks found in the crater.  科学家研究了在陨石坑中发现的非陨石岩石。
The origin of the artifact was determined to be nonmeteoric.  该文物的起源被确定为非陨石。
近义词: terrestrial, earthly
反义词: meteoric
词性: adjective
