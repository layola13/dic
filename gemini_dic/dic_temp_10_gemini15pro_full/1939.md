actinotrocha: /ˈæktɪnəˌtroʊkə/
n.
担轮幼虫
The free-swimming larva of certain marine worms, having a ring of cilia and resembling a trochophore.
The actinotrocha larva is characteristic of the phylum Phoronida.  担轮幼虫是帚虫动物门的特征。

The marine biologist studied the development of the actinotrocha larva.  这位海洋生物学家研究了担轮幼虫的发育。

近义词: larva, trochophore
反义词: adult
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *aktis* (ray) + *trochos* (wheel)
