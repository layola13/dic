endorsees:/ɪnˌdɔːrˈsiːz/
n.
被背书人，受让人
The person or entity to whom a check or other negotiable instrument is endorsed.
The endorsees of the check will receive the payment.  这张支票的被背书人将收到付款。
近义词：recipients, assignees
反义词: endorsers
词性: 名词(plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From endorse + -ee (suffix forming nouns denoting the recipient or beneficiary of an action). Endorse comes from Old French endosser "to write on the back of," from en- "on" + dos "back" (from Latin dorsum).
