nonductile:/ˌnɒnˈdʌktaɪl/
adjective
非延展性的；不韧的
Not ductile; incapable of being permanently stretched or drawn out into a wire without breaking.
The material proved to be nonductile and fractured under stress.  这种材料被证明是非延展性的，在压力下断裂。
This type of metal is known for its nonductile properties, making it unsuitable for wiring. 这种类型的金属以其非延展性而闻名，使其不适合用于布线。
近义词:brittle, fragile, inelastic
反义词:ductile, malleable, pliable
词性:adjective

词源:
non- (prefix): not
ductile (from Latin ductilis, from ducere "to lead"): capable of being drawn out into a wire
