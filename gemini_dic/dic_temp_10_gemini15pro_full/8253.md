anti-inflammatories: /ˌæntiɪnˈflæmətɔːriz/ | n. | 消炎药，抗炎药 |  Medicines that reduce inflammation, pain, and swelling.  These can include both steroidal and non-steroidal drugs.  |  The doctor prescribed anti-inflammatories for my swollen ankle. 医生给我开了消炎药治疗我肿胀的脚踝。 | Synonyms: anti-inflammatory drugs, anti-inflammatory medications, NSAIDs (when referring to non-steroidal anti-inflammatories) | Antonyms:  pro-inflammatories (less common) | noun
