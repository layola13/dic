palet:/ˈpælɪt/ | n. |调色板，颜料盒，（货物的）托盘，（计算机的）调色板 | A thin board or slab on which an artist lays and mixes colors. A range of colors or qualities. A portable platform, usually made of wood, used for storing or moving goods. The range of colours used by a particular artist or in a particular picture.  | The artist squeezed paint onto his palet. 艺术家把颜料挤到调色板上。| Synonyms: palette (the preferred spelling), color plate, tray | Antonyms: None | Noun
