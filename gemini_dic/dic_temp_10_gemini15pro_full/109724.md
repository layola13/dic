leveller:/ˈlevələ(r)/ | n. | 平均主义者；矫正者；夷平者 | someone who wants to make everyone equal; something that makes things equal or uniform | The French Revolution was seen by many as the work of levellers.  许多人认为法国大革命是平均主义者的杰作。 | The heavy rain was a leveller, making the playing field equally difficult for both teams.  这场大雨成了一个矫正者，使比赛场地对双方都一样困难。 | synonyms: equalizer, grader | antonyms: aristocrat, elitist | noun

leveller:/ˈlevələ(r)/ | n. | 水平仪；水平尺 | a tool used to determine whether a surface is level | The builder used a leveller to ensure the foundation was perfectly flat. 建筑工人使用水平仪来确保地基完全平整。 | He checked the shelf with a leveller before putting up the picture. 他在挂画之前用水平尺检查了架子是否水平。 | synonyms: level, spirit level | antonyms: N/A | noun
