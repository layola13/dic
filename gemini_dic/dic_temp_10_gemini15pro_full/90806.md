humphs:/hʌmfs/ | int. | 哼！; 哼哼！| An interjection expressing displeasure, doubt, or impatience. | Example: "Humphs," she said, "I don't believe that for a second."  哼，她说，我才不信呢。 | Example: He just humphs and walks away when I try to talk to him. 当我试图和他说话时，他只是哼了一声就走开了。 | 近义词: grunt, snort, harrumph | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for an interjection of this type) | 词性: Interjection
