obtainable:/əbˈteɪnəbl/ | adj. | 可获得的；可得到的 | Possible to get or achieve. | Tickets are obtainable from the box office.  门票可以从售票处获得。|  The information is readily obtainable online.  这些信息很容易在网上获得。 | synonyms: accessible, available, achievable, attainable, procurable | antonyms: unobtainable, inaccessible, unavailable | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English *obtainable*, from obtain + -able.  Ultimately from Latin obtinēre (“to hold, possess”).
