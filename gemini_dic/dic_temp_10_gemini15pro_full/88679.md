hoay: /hɔɪ/ |  interj. | 嗨；喂 (表示问候或引起注意) | An informal greeting, similar to "hi" or "hey." Used to attract attention or express a casual greeting. | Hoay there, friend! 嗨，朋友！|  Hoay, can you hear me? 喂，你能听到我吗？ | 近义词: hi, hey, hello | 反义词: goodbye, farewell | 词性:感叹词


词源:  Likely a variant of "hoy," which itself may be from Middle Dutch "hoi" (an exclamation of joy) or related to "ahoy."  The exact origin is uncertain.
