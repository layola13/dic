chemosmosis:/ˌkɛmoʊzˈmoʊsɪs/ /ˌkiːmoʊzˈməʊsɪs/
noun
化学渗透
The movement of ions across a semipermeable membrane, down their electrochemical gradient, especially the movement of hydrogen ions across a membrane during cellular respiration or photosynthesis, generating ATP.
Chemosmosis is the process by which ATP is synthesized in mitochondria and chloroplasts.  化学渗透是线粒体和叶绿体中合成ATP的过程。

The generation of ATP by chemosmosis is a fundamental process in all living organisms. 通过化学渗透产生ATP是所有生物体的基本过程。
