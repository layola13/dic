exposednesses:/ɪkˈspəʊzɪdnəsɪz/ | n. |暴露，显露；易受攻击性|The state of being exposed; vulnerability. | The exposednesses of the coastal towns to hurricanes are a serious concern. 沿海城镇易受飓风侵袭是一个严重的问题。|  The exposednesses of children to online predators is a growing worry for parents. 孩子们容易受到网络掠夺者的攻击，这越来越让父母担心。 | synonyms: vulnerabilities, susceptibilities, openness | antonyms: protection, safety, security | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From exposed + -ness.  "Exposed" comes from the Latin "expositus," past participle of "exponere" (to put forth, expose), from "ex-" (out) + "ponere" (to place).  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
