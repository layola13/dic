hydrotherapies:/ˌhaɪdrəˈθɛrəpiz/
|n.|水疗法|The use of water (hot, cold, steam, or ice) in the treatment of disease or injury.|She underwent a series of hydrotherapies to alleviate her back pain. 她接受了一系列的水疗法来缓解背痛。|近义词:water therapy, balneotherapy, aquatic therapy|反义词:N/A|词性:名词

Etymology:
From hydro- + therapy. Hydro- comes from Greek *hydōr* "water". Therapy comes from Greek *therapeia* "healing, service done to the sick".
