encephalitis-lethargica:/ɪnˌsefəˈlaɪtɪs lɪˈθɑːrdʒɪkə/ | n. | 脑炎后昏睡症 |  An illness, also known as "sleeping sickness," characterized by inflammation of the brain, often following a viral infection, leading to prolonged drowsiness, lethargy, and sometimes coma.  It was prevalent in the early 20th century. | The 1918 flu pandemic was followed by an outbreak of encephalitis lethargica. 1918年的流感大流行之后爆发了脑炎后昏睡症。 |  sleeping sickness, Von Economo's disease | N/A | noun
