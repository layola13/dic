nearsighted:/ˌnɪrˈsaɪtɪd/ /ˌnɪrˈsaɪtəd/| adj.|近视的；目光短浅的|Unable to see distant objects clearly.|例句：She's been nearsighted since she was a child. 她从小就近视。

例句：He wears glasses because he's nearsighted. 他戴眼镜是因为他近视。


近义词：shortsighted, myopic|反义词:farsighted, hyperopic|词性:adjective
