copygraph:/ˈkɒpiɡrɑːf/ /ˈkɑːpiɡræf/| n.|复制图；誊写版；手抄本|A copygraph is a copy of a document, typically a handwritten one.  A copygraph can also refer to a device or process for making copies.|The lawyer requested a copygraph of the original signed agreement.|近义词：copy, transcript, duplicate, facsimile|反义词:original|词性:名词
