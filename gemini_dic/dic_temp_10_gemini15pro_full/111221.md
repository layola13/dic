lithospheric:/ˌlɪθəˈsfɪrɪk/
adjective
岩石圈的
Relating to the lithosphere, the rigid outer part of the earth, consisting of the crust and upper mantle.
The lithospheric plates move slowly over the asthenosphere. 岩石圈板块缓慢地在软流圈上移动。
The earthquake was caused by the movement of lithospheric plates. 地震是由岩石圈板块的运动引起的。
Synonyms: crustal, tectonic
Antonyms: asthenospheric
词性: adjective
