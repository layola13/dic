cuckoldom:/ˈkʌkəldəm/| n. |戴绿帽子，妻子通奸|The state of being a cuckold; the fact of a wife's adultery. |He lived in fear of cuckoldom. 他生活在害怕妻子通奸的恐惧中。| He bore his cuckoldom with surprising patience. 他以惊人的耐心忍受着妻子不忠的耻辱。|近义词：infidelity, adultery|反义词：fidelity, faithfulness|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *kukwoldom, from Old French cucuault (“cuckold”) +‎ -dom.  Ultimately from cuckoo, based on the cuckoo's habit of laying its eggs in other birds' nests.
