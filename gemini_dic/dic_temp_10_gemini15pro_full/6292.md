amorphism:/əˈmɔːrfɪzəm/
n.
无定形；非晶形；无定形状态
The lack of a regular, ordered structure; the property of being amorphous.
Glass is an example of a solid exhibiting amorphism.  玻璃是呈现无定形状态的固体的例子。
The rapid cooling of the lava resulted in amorphism.  熔岩的快速冷却导致了无定形状态。
amorphousness, noncrystallinity
crystallinity, structure
noun
