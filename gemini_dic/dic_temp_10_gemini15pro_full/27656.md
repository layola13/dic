candidates:/ˈkændɪdɪts/| n.|候选人，应试者|People who are competing for a job or elected position.|The candidates for president debated on television. 总统候选人在电视上进行了辩论。|近义词：applicant, nominee, contender, aspirant|反义词：incumbent, elector|词性:noun

Etymology: From Late Middle English *candidat*, from Latin *candidātus* "clothed in white," past participle of *candidāre* "to make white, brighten," from *candidus* "white, shining, pure, sincere."  Roman office-seekers traditionally wore white togas.
