dairier:/ˈdɛəriər/ | n. |乳品商，奶制品商；经营奶制品的人|A person who owns or works in a dairy; a dealer in dairy products.| He's a dairier, delivering milk and cheese to local shops. 他是一位乳品商，向当地商店配送牛奶和奶酪。| 近义词: dairyman, milkman, milkmaid | 反义词:  (No direct opposite, perhaps "grocer" as a more general term) | 词性: 名词


词源:  From Middle English *deyer*, from Old French *deier*, from Late Latin *dēriārius* ("dairyman"), from Latin *dērium* ("dairy"). Ultimately from Latin *diēs* ("day"), reflecting the daily milking of cows.
