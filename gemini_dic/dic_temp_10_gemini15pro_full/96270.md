indicter:/ɪnˈdaɪtər/| n.|控告者；起诉人；检察官|A person who formally accuses another of a crime.|The grand jury served as indicters in the case. 大陪审团在本案中充当了起诉人。|Synonyms: accuser, prosecutor, complainant|Antonyms: defendant, accused|Noun
