The deflexed branches of the weeping willow trailed in the water. 垂柳向下弯曲的枝条拖曳在水中。|近义词：bent, curved, downturned, drooped|反义词：straight, erect, upright|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin deflectere, from de- "down, away" + flectere "to bend."
