backdrop:/ˈbækdrɑːp/ (US) /ˈbækdrɒp/ (UK)| n.|背景；背景幕；(事件的)背景情况|a painted cloth hung at the back of a theatre stage as part of the scenery|The mountains formed a dramatic backdrop to the scene. 群山构成了这幅景象的壮丽背景。|synonyms: background, setting, scenery, context|antonyms: foreground|noun
