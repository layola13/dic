chives: /tʃaɪvz/ | n. |细香葱，四季葱 |A small European plant related to the onion, with thin leaves used in cooking. |例句：I sprinkled some chives over the baked potato. 我在烤土豆上撒了些细香葱。

例句：Chives add a delicate onion flavor to salads. 细香葱为沙拉增添了淡淡的洋葱味。

近义词：scallions, green onions |反义词: (No direct antonym for a plant/herb) |词性:noun


chive: /tʃaɪv/ | n. |细香葱，四季葱(单数) |A single blade of the chive plant.  Less common than the plural form. |例句：A single chive can be used as a garnish. 一根细香葱可以用作装饰。

例句：He picked a chive from the pot and tasted it. 他从盆里摘了一根细香葱尝了尝。

近义词：scallion, green onion (singular) |反义词: (No direct antonym for a plant/herb) |词性:noun
