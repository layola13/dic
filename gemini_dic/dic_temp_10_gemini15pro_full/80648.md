注意事项:
词源:  "Gora" comes from the Hindi and Urdu word गोरा / گورا (gora), meaning "fair-skinned" or "white."  While the word itself simply describes skin color, its usage has taken on a derogatory connotation, particularly when used by South Asians to refer to white people.
