cyberhome:/ˈsaɪbərˌhoʊm/| n.|网络家庭，智能家居|A home equipped with extensive computer and communications technology, allowing control of various appliances and systems remotely and offering interactive entertainment and information services.|例句：The advertisement promised a futuristic cyberhome with voice-activated lighting and automated everything.  广告承诺了一个未来感十足的网络家庭，带有声控照明和自动化一切。

例句：With the rise of the Internet of Things, more and more people are turning their traditional homes into cyberhomes. 随着物联网的兴起，越来越多的人将他们的传统住宅变成了网络家庭。|近义词：smart home, connected home, automated home|反义词:traditional home|词性:名词
