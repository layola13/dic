corpulently:/ˈkɔːrpjʊləntli/ | adv. | 肥胖地，臃肿地 | In a corpulent or fleshy manner; excessively fat.  | He sat corpulently in his armchair, a half-eaten pie on the table beside him. 他臃肿地坐在扶手椅上，旁边桌子上放着一个吃了一半的馅饼。|  He ate corpulently and without restraint. 他毫无节制地大吃大喝。 | 近义词:obese, stout, portly, heavily, fatly | 反义词:thinly, slenderly | 词性:副词

词源:  From Latin *corpulentus*, from corpus "body."
