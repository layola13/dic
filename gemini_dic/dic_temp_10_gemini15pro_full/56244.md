dog-it:/ˈdɒɡɪt/ | n. | 顽固的人；坚持不懈的人 | A person who shows dogged determination; someone who perseveres. | He's a real dog-it; he never gives up. 他真是个顽固的人；他从不放弃。| 近义词:  perseverer, go-getter, diehard | 反义词: quitter, slacker | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From "dogged" (adjective), meaning persistent, tenacious.  "Dogged" itself comes from the image of a dog persistently pursuing something.
