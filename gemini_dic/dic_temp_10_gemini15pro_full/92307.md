hypnoscope: /ˈhɪpnəˌskoʊp/ | n. |催眠镜；催眠仪|An instrument used in hypnotism, sometimes consisting of a bright object held near the subject's eyes.  It is purported to aid in inducing hypnosis.|The hypnotist used a hypnoscope to help the patient relax. 催眠师使用催眠镜帮助病人放松。|Synonyms: hypnotizer|Antonyms: N/A|noun

hypnoscope: /ˈhɪpnəˌskoʊp/ | n. |催眠显示器|A device that supposedly allows one to view one's own past lives or experiences while under hypnosis.|Some believe the hypnoscope can unlock memories from past lives.  有些人相信催眠显示器可以解锁前世记忆。|Synonyms: past life regression device|Antonyms: N/A|noun
