contrapuntist:/ˈkɒntrəpʌntɪst/ (US) /ˈkɑːntrəpɔɪntɪst/| n. |对位法家；复调音乐家|A person skilled in or specializing in counterpoint (the art or technique of setting, writing, or playing a melody or melodies in conjunction with another or others, according to fixed rules).|Bach was a master contrapuntist. 巴赫是一位对位法大师。|Synonym: polyphonist|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "monophonist" in a limited sense)|Noun

Etymology:
From *counterpoint* + *-ist*.
*Counterpoint* comes from Italian *contrappunto*, from *contra* ("against") + *punto* ("point," in music "note").
