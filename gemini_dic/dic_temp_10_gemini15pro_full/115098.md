manganesious:/ˌmæŋɡəˈniːʃəs/
adjective
亚锰的; 含二价锰的
Containing or relating to divalent manganese, especially manganese(II) oxide (MnO).
Manganesious compounds are characterized by the +2 oxidation state of manganese. 亚锰化合物以锰的+2氧化态为特征。
This mineral is a manganesious oxide. 这种矿物是一种亚锰的氧化物。
近义词: manganous
反义词: manganic (referring to Mn(III))
词性: adjective
