autosuggests:/ˌɔːtəʊsəˈdʒɛsts/ | v. | 自动建议，自动提示 | To suggest or propose something automatically, often based on partial input, as in a search bar or text editor. |  The search bar autosuggests popular search terms as you type.  当你输入时，搜索栏会自动提示热门搜索词。 | suggests, prompts, recommends |  - | verb
autosuggest:/ˌɔːtəʊsəˈdʒɛst/ | n. | 自动建议，自动提示（功能）| The function or feature that automatically suggests or proposes something. | The autosuggest feature in my email client saves me a lot of time. 我的电子邮件客户端中的自动建议功能为我节省了很多时间。 | suggestion, prompt, recommendation | - | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From auto- (self, automatic) + suggest.  "Auto-" comes from Greek "autos" (self). "Suggest" comes from Latin "suggerere" (to carry under, furnish, supply, suggest).
