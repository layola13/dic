catabasion:/ˌkætəˈbeɪsiən/| n.|下场，退场，结局，（尤指戏剧的）结尾，（古希腊戏剧中演员的）退场坡道|A dramatic exit, especially one at the end of a play; in ancient Greek theatre, the ramp by which actors left the stage at the end of a play.|The play's catabasion was a poignant scene of farewells.|Synonyms: denouement, conclusion, exodus, departure|Antonyms: prologue, beginning, entrance|noun
