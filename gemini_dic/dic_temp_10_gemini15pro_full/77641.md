gelatiniform:/ˌdʒɛləˈtɪnɪfɔːrm/
adjective
胶状的，似凝胶的
Resembling gelatin; jelly-like.
The substance had a gelatiniform appearance, quivering slightly when touched.  这种物质呈胶状，触摸时会轻微颤动。
The bacteria formed a gelatiniform mass on the petri dish.  细菌在培养皿上形成了胶状团块。
近义词:gelatinous, jelly-like, colloidal
反义词:solid, firm, rigid
词性:adjective

词源:
From Latin *gelatina ("gelatin") + -form.
