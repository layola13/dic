homopolic:/ˌhɒməʊˈpɒlɪk/ /ˌhoʊmoʊˈpɑːlɪk/ | adj. | 同极的，单极的 |  Relating to or characterized by having poles of the same kind, or by the influence of a single pole. | A homopolic generator produces a direct current. 同极发电机产生直流电。| 近义词: unipolar | 反义词: heteropolar, bipolar | 词性: 形容词
词源: From Greek *homópolos* "having the same pole," from *homós* "same" + *pólos* "pole".
