autotheism:/ˌɔːtoʊˈθiːɪzəm/ | n. | 自我神化; 自我崇拜 | The belief that oneself is God or equivalent to God.  The individual identifies themselves as a deity or divine being. | He explored the concept of autotheism in his philosophical writings, arguing that it represented a dangerous form of narcissism. 他在他的哲学著作中探讨了自我神化的概念，认为它代表了一种危险的自恋形式。|  Example 2: Her autotheism led her to believe she was above all moral laws and societal norms. 她的自我神化使她相信自己凌驾于一切道德法律和社会规范之上。 | Synonyms: self-deification, apotheosis of self | Antonyms: humility, self-abasement | Noun
