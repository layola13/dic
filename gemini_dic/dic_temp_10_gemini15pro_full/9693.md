antithetic:/ˌæntɪˈθɛtɪk/ | adj. | 对立的；相反的 |Directly opposed or contrasted; mutually incompatible. | The antithetic forces of good and evil battled for supremacy. 善恶两种对立的力量争夺霸权。 | synonyms: opposite, contrasting, contradictory, conflicting, contrary, reverse, inverse, converse, opposed | antonyms: similar, alike, same, identical | adjective


antithesis:/ænˈtɪθəsɪs/ | n. | 对立；反面 |A person or thing that is the direct opposite of someone or something else. |  Love is the antithesis of hate. 爱是恨的对立面。| synonyms: opposite, contrary, reverse, converse, inverse, obverse | antonyms: similarity, likeness, sameness | noun
