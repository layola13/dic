antiritualism:/ˌæntiˈrɪtʃuəlɪzəm/
n.
反儀式主義；反禮儀主義
Opposition or hostility to rituals, especially in religion.
The rise of antiritualism in the Protestant Reformation led to simplified church services.  新教改革中反儀式主義的興起導致了教堂儀式的簡化。
The movement embraced antiritualism and focused on personal spirituality.  这场运动拥护反仪式主义，并专注于个人灵性。
近义词:iconoclasm, irreverence
反义词:ritualism, ceremonialism
词性:noun

Etymology:
From anti- + ritualism.  "Anti-" prefixes a word to indicate opposition. "Ritualism" refers to the adherence to rituals.
