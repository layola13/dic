cartoonishly:/kɑːrˈtuːnɪʃli/ | adv. |卡通式地；滑稽地|In a way that resembles or suggests a cartoon, typically exaggerated or simplified for humorous or dramatic effect.|He grinned cartoonishly, his eyes widening comically. 他滑稽地咧嘴一笑，眼睛夸张地睁大了。|Synonyms: comically, exaggeratedly, ridiculously, absurdly|Antonyms: realistically, seriously, subtly, naturally|adverb
