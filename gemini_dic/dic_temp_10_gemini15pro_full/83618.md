haemic:/ˈhiːmɪk/ (US) /ˈhiːmɪk/ (UK)| adj.|血的；血液的；含铁的|Relating to blood or haemoglobin; containing iron.|例句：Haemic iron is more readily absorbed than non-haemic iron.  血红素铁比非血红素铁更容易吸收。

例句：The doctor diagnosed a haemic disorder. 医生诊断出一种血液疾病。|近义词：sanguine, bloody|反义词：anaemic|词性:形容词

词源:
From Greek *haîma* (blood)
