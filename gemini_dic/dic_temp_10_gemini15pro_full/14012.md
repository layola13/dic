aurochloride:/ˌɔːrəˈklɔːraɪd/
noun
氯金酸盐
A compound of gold and chlorine, typically HAuCl4 (chloroauric acid) or its salts.
Gold aurochloride is used in photography. 氯金酸金用于摄影。
Synonyms: gold chloride, chloroaurate
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a chemical compound)
