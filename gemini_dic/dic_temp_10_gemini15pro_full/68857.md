fashionless:/ˈfæʃən.ləs/| adj.|缺乏时尚感的，不时髦的|Lacking fashion or style; not fashionable.|Her fashionless outfit made her stand out in the stylish crowd. 她那身毫无时尚感的衣服让她在时髦的人群中显得格格不入。|Near synonyms: unfashionable, dowdy, frumpy, outdated, old-fashioned|Near antonyms: fashionable, stylish, trendy, chic, contemporary|adjective


Etymology:
From fashion + -less.  "fashion" comes from Old French "façon" (manner, shape, form), ultimately from Latin "factio" (a making, a doing), from "facere" (to do, to make).  "-less" is a native English suffix meaning "without".
