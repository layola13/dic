magnanimously:/mæɡˈnænɪməsli/
| adv. | 宽宏大量地；慷慨地 | Showing or suggesting a lofty and courageous spirit as well as a disdain for pettiness or meanness. |  He magnanimously forgave his opponent after the bitter election. 他在激烈的选举后宽宏大量地原谅了他的对手。| generously, nobly, charitably | meanly, spitefully, selfishly | adverb

magnanimous:/mæɡˈnænɪməs/
| adj. | 宽宏大量的；慷慨的 |  Having or showing a lofty and courageous spirit as well as a disdain for pettiness or meanness. |  She was magnanimous in victory, praising her opponent's efforts.  她在胜利时表现得很宽宏大量，称赞了对手的努力。| generous, noble, charitable | mean, spiteful, selfish | adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *magnanimus*, from *magnus* ("great") + *animus* ("soul, spirit").
