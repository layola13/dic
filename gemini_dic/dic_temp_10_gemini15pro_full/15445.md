bad-mouthing:/ˌbæd ˈmaʊðɪŋ/ | n. | 恶意诽谤，说坏话 | The act of criticizing someone or something unfairly and unkindly. |  He lost his job because of bad-mouthing his boss to colleagues.  他因为对同事说老板的坏话而丢了工作。

| 近义词：criticizing, slandering, defaming, backbiting, maligning | 反义词：praising, commending, complimenting | 词性: Noun (Gerund)
bad-mouth:/ˌbæd ˈmaʊθ/ | v. | 说坏话，恶意诽谤 | To criticize someone or something unfairly and unkindly. | She's always bad-mouthing her competitors. 她总是说竞争对手的坏话。

| 近义词：criticize, slander, defame, backbite, malign | 反义词：praise, commend, compliment | 词性: Verb
