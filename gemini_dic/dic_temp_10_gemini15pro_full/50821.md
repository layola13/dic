descries:/dɪˈskraɪz/
verb
察觉，发现；看出
catch sight of, especially something distant or obscure.
From a distance, she descried two figures walking towards her. 她远远地看到两个人影朝她走来。
近义词: spot, perceive, discern, detect, observe, notice
反义词: overlook, miss
词性:v.

词源:
From Old French descrier "to distinguish, discern, describe," from de- "completely" + escrier "to cry out, proclaim."  The original sense was "to proclaim, announce," but it evolved to mean "to detect visually" because proclaiming something implied having seen it first.
