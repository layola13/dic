membranaceous:/ˌmɛmbrəˈneɪʃəs/| adj.|膜状的，膜质的|thin and flexible like a membrane; resembling a membrane|例句：The bat's wings are membranaceous.  蝙蝠的翅膀是膜状的。

例句：The plant has delicate, membranaceous leaves.  这种植物有纤细的膜质叶子。|近义词：membranous, filmy, diaphanous, translucent|反义词：thick, opaque|词性:adjective

Etymology->
From Late Latin *membranaceus, from Latin membrana (“membrane, skin of parchment”) + -aceus (adjective-forming suffix).
