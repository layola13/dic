daphnin:/ˈdæfnɪn/| n. |瑞香苷; 瑞香甙|A glycoside, C15H16O9, extracted from the bark and other parts of certain plants of the genus Daphne, as a crystalline, slightly yellow, bitter powder.|Daphnetoxin, a toxic constituent of many Daphne species, can be hydrolyzed to daphnetin and glucose, whereas daphnin is 7-glucosido-daphnetin.|近义词:daphnetin glucoside|反义词:None|词性:noun
