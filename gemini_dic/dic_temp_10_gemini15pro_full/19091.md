betrothments:/bɪˈtrəʊðmənts/
n.
订婚，婚约
the act of promising to marry someone; a formal engagement to be married
例句：The betrothments were announced at the party.  订婚的消息在派对上宣布了。

例句：Their betrothments had been arranged by their parents. 他们的婚约是由父母安排的。

近义词：engagements, promises to marry
反义词：separations, divorces
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *betrothement, from Anglo-Norman betrouthement, from betrouther (“to promise, pledge, engage”), from be- (“thoroughly, completely”) + trouther (“to pledge, plight”), ultimately from Frankish *trūwōn (“to be true, trust”).
