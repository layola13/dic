anapophysis:/ænəˈpɒfɪsɪs/
n.
横突; 下横突
A bony outgrowth or process, especially a transverse process on a vertebra of a lower vertebrate.  Specifically, a ventral or ventrolateral process of a vertebra, as in certain fishes and reptiles.
The anapophysis of the lumbar vertebrae provides attachment for certain muscles.  腰椎的横突为某些肌肉提供附着点。

近义词: process, outgrowth
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider: main body, shaft)
词性: noun
