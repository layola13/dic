hyponymous:/ˌhaɪˈpɒnɪməs/ /ˌhaɪˈpɑːnɪməs/| adj.|下义的，下位的|Of a word or other linguistic unit, having a more specific meaning than another word (its hypernym).  Relating to a hyponym.|A *spoon* is hyponymous with *cutlery*.|近义词：subordinate, specific|反义词：superordinate, general|词性: adjective
