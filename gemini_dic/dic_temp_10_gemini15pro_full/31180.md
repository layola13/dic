changednesses:/ˈtʃeɪndʒɪdnɪsɪz/ | n. | 变迁，变化（罕用） | The state or quality of having been changed multiple times.  This word is rarely used; "changes" or "transformations" are generally preferred. |  I was struck by the sheer changednesses of the landscape after the flood.  洪水过后，我对地貌的巨大变化感到震惊。|  The changednesses in her personality over the years were remarkable. 多年来她性格的变化是显著的。| Synonyms: changes, transformations, alterations, mutations | Antonyms:  stasis, constancy, stability | noun
