echolocations:/ˌɛkoʊloʊˈkeɪʃənz/
|n.|回声定位，回声定位法|The process of locating objects by emitting sounds and interpreting the reflected sound waves.  蝙蝠使用回声定位来导航和捕猎。|Bats use echolocation to navigate and hunt.
|近义词:biosonar (生物声纳)|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider terms like "visual navigation")|词性:noun
