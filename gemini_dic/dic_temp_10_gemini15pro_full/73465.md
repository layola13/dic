foreordination:/ˌfɔːrɔːrdɪˈneɪʃn/ /ˌfɔːrɔːrˈdeɪʃn/
n.
预先注定；宿命；前定
The act of foreordaining; predestination. The state of being foreordained.
例句：Some people believe in the foreordination of events.  有些人相信事件是预先注定的。

例句：The theologian lectured on the concept of divine foreordination.  神学家讲授了神圣预定的概念。

近义词：predestination, preordination, predetermination
反义词：chance, randomness, accident
词性：noun
