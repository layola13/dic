paprika:/pəˈpriːkə/ /pæˈpriːkə/| n.|辣椒粉，红椒粉|A powdered spice made from dried sweet red peppers.|I added some paprika to the goulash for color and flavor. 我在炖牛肉里加了一些辣椒粉来调色调味。|Synonym: pimentón, sweet pepper powder|Antonym: (No direct antonym, but consider contrasting spices like black pepper or chili powder)|Noun
