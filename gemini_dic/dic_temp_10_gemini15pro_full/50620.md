dereliction:/ˌdɛrəˈlɪkʃən/
n.
玩忽职守，怠忽职守；破败，荒废
The state of having been abandoned and become dilapidated.  A shameful failure to fulfill one's obligations.
The house fell into dereliction after the owner died. 这栋房子在主人去世后变得破败不堪。
His dereliction of duty led to serious consequences. 他玩忽职守导致了严重的后果。
synonyms: negligence, neglect, abandonment, dilapidation, disrepair
antonyms: care, maintenance, repair, diligence, attention
noun
