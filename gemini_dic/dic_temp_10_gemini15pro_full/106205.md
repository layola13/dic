词源: Middle English *knoppe*, from Old English *cnopp* ("bud, button"), from Proto-Germanic *knuppaz*. Cognate with Dutch *knop* ("bud, button, knob"), German *Knospe* ("bud"), Swedish *knopp* ("bud, button, knob").
