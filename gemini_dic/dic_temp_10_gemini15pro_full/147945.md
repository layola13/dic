overrepresented:/ˌoʊvərˌrɛprɪˈzɛntɪd/| adj.| 过度代表的；比例过高的|  having more members of a particular group than is justified or expected based on the overall population or distribution.  |Women are overrepresented in the nursing profession.  女性在护理行业中比例过高。|overabundant, disproportionately represented|underrepresented|adjective
overrepresent:/ˌoʊvərˌrɛprɪˈzɛnt/| vt.|过度代表；过分强调|to give too much representation or emphasis to something or someone, resulting in a disproportionate presence or influence.|The media tends to overrepresent violent crime, giving a skewed perception of reality. 媒体倾向于过分强调暴力犯罪，从而歪曲了现实的认知。| exaggerate, overemphasize|underrepresent, downplay|verb
