epizoan:/ˌɛpɪˈzoʊən/
n.|外寄生动物|An animal that lives on the exterior of another living animal.|Fish lice are common epizoans on many species of fish. 鱼虱是许多鱼类身上常见的外寄生动物。|
近义词:ectoparasite|反义词:endoparasite|词性:noun

epizoan:/ˌɛpɪˈzoʊən/
adj.|外寄生的|Living on the exterior of another living animal.|Epizoan organisms can cause significant harm to their host. 外寄生生物会对其宿主造成重大伤害。|
近义词:ectoparasitic|反义词:endoparasitic|词性:adjective
