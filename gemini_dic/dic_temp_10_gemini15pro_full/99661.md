interlibel: /ˌɪntərˈlaɪbəl/
adjective
相互诽谤的;穿插诽谤的
occurring between libels; consisting of mutual accusations of libel.
The two newspapers engaged in an interlibel suit, each accusing the other of defamation. 两家报纸卷入了一场相互诽谤的诉讼，每家都指责对方诽谤。
近义词: cross-libel, counter-libel
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider: amicable, respectful)
词性 adjective
