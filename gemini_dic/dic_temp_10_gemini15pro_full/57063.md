doubtful:/ˈdaʊtfəl/| adj.|可疑的，令人生疑的；不确定的，未定的| feeling uncertain about something; not sure that something is true or likely to happen|例句：The future of the project is doubtful.  这个项目的未来是不确定的。|例句：I'm doubtful about his ability to do the job. 我怀疑他是否有能力做这份工作。|近义词：uncertain, dubious, questionable, unsure|反义词：certain, sure, definite|词性:形容词

doubtful:/ˈdaʊtfəl/| adj.|怀疑的，不相信的|not believing something|例句：He was doubtful about the story she told him. 他对她讲的故事表示怀疑。|例句：She looked doubtful when I told her the good news. 当我告诉她这个好消息时，她看起来将信将疑。|近义词：skeptical, suspicious, incredulous|反义词：trusting, believing, credulous|词性:形容词
