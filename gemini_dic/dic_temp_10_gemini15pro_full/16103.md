bandwidth:/ˈbændwɪdθ/| n.|带宽；频带宽度|The amount of data that can be transmitted over an internet connection in a given amount of time.  It is often measured in bits per second (bps).|My internet service provider offers high bandwidth, allowing me to stream movies without buffering. 我的互联网服务提供商提供高带宽，让我可以流畅地观看电影。|Synonyms: capacity, throughput|Antonyms: narrowband|Noun

bandwidth:/ˈbændwɪdθ/| n.|（处理大量事情或思考多种想法的）能力；精力|The capacity to handle a large volume of tasks or consider a wide range of ideas.|The project manager didn't have the bandwidth to take on another client.  项目经理没有精力再接一个客户了。|Synonyms: capacity, ability, energy|Antonyms: inability, limitation|Noun
