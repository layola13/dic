frostbite:/ˈfrɔːstbʌɪt/ (US) /ˈfrɒstbʌɪt/ (UK)| n.|冻伤；冻疮|Damage to skin and tissues caused by extreme cold.|She got frostbite on her toes during the blizzard.  她在暴风雪中脚趾冻伤了。|近义词：freezecrash,  cold injury|反义词:burn|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From frost + bite.  "Bite" here refers to the damaging effect of the cold, similar to how an animal bite can injure flesh.
