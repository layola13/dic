fore-sighted:/fɔːrˈsaɪtɪd/
adjective
有远见的；深谋远虑的
able to predict what will happen or be needed in the future.
She made a fore-sighted decision to invest in the company. 她做了一个有远见的决定，投资了这家公司。
He was fore-sighted enough to anticipate the problems. 他有足够的远见来预见这些问题。

近义词:prescient, visionary, provident, judicious
反义词:short-sighted, myopic, improvident
词性:adjective
