almanac: /ˈɔːlmənæk/ (UK) /ˈæl.mə.ˌnæk/ (US)| n.|年鉴；历书|A book published every year that contains information such as weather forecasts, astronomical data, tide tables, and dates of important events.|I checked the almanac to see when the next full moon would be. 我查看了年鉴，想看看下一次满月是什么时候。|Example: The farmer consulted the almanac to determine the best time to plant his crops. 农民查阅了年鉴，以确定种植作物的最佳时间。

|Synonym: calendar, yearbook, annual|Antonym: N/A|noun
