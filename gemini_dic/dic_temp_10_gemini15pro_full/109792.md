levulinic acid:/ˌlɛvjuˈlɪnɪk ˈæsɪd/ | n. |乙酰丙酸；左旋糖酸 | A crystalline keto acid formed by the action of mineral acids on fructose or other carbohydrates. | Levulinic acid can be used to produce biofuels. 乙酰丙酸可以用来生产生物燃料。 | synonyms: 4-oxopentanoic acid, acetopropionic acid | antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *lévulinique*, from *lévulose* (“levulose, fructose”) + *-inique*.  *Lévulose* from Latin *laevus* (“left”) + *-ose* (sugar suffix). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *leyw- (“left, weak”).
