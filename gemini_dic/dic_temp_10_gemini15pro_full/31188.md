changemaker:/ˈtʃeɪndʒˌmeɪkər/| n. |变革者，改革者；创新者；有影响力的人|A person who makes changes, especially in politics or business. Someone who is innovative and creates positive change or impact.|例句：She's a real changemaker in the education system. 她在教育系统中是一位真正的变革者。|近义词：reformer, innovator, leader, influencer, pioneer|反义词:conservative, traditionalist|词性:noun

Etymology:  From "change" (meaning alteration, transformation) + "maker" (meaning one who creates or constructs something). The term gained prominence in the late 20th century, particularly in the context of social and political activism.
