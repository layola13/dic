baptism:/ˈbæptɪzəm/ |n.|洗礼，浸礼|The act of baptizing someone, especially in a Christian ceremony, or the ceremony itself.  It symbolizes purification and admission into the Christian Church.|例句：His baptism took place in a small church. 他的洗礼仪式在一个小教堂举行。

例句：The baptism marked a significant turning point in her life. 洗礼标志着她人生中的一个重要转折点。|近义词：christening, initiation, immersion|反义词：None (No direct opposite for a religious rite)|词性: noun
