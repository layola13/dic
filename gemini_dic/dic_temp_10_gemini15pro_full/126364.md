myricin:/ˈmɪrɪsɪn/
noun
蜂蜡醇; 肉豆蔻蜡;  蜜蜡醇
A waxy substance consisting chiefly of myricyl palmitate, C30H61.C16H31O2, found in beeswax.
Beeswax contains approximately 70-80% myricin.  蜂蜡含有大约70-80%的蜂蜡醇。
synonym: myricyl palmitate
antonym:  (No direct antonym for a specific chemical compound)
词性: n.
