eulogist:/ˈjuːlədʒɪst/
n.
悼词撰写人；颂扬者
A person who delivers a eulogy, a speech praising someone who has died.
He was a gifted eulogist, moving the audience to tears with his heartfelt words. 他是一位才华横溢的悼词撰写人，用他 heartfelt 的话语感动得观众流泪。
speaker, orator, praiser
critic, detractor
