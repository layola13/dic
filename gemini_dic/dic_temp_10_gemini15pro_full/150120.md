pamperers:/ˈpæmpərərz/
n.
娇惯者，纵容者
People who pamper others; those who indulge someone's whims or desires.
The hotel caters to pamperers, offering luxurious spa treatments and personalized service.  这家酒店迎合了娇惯者的需求，提供奢华的温泉护理和个性化服务。
She is a real pamperer when it comes to her grandchildren, showering them with gifts and attention.  说到她的孙子孙女，她真是个溺爱者，总是送他们礼物，给予他们关注。
近义词: indulgers, mimadores, spoilers
反义词: disciplinarians, strict parents
词性: noun
