fishes:/ˈfɪʃɪz/ | n. |鱼（复数）|More than one fish; plural of "fish".  It is less commonly used than "fish" for the plural, except when referring to different kinds of fish.| We caught several fishes of different species on our fishing trip. 我们在钓鱼之旅中捕获了几个不同种类的鱼。| 近义词：fish | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a living creature) | noun


fishes:/ˈfɪʃɪz/ | v. (third-person singular simple present fishes, present participle fishing, simple past and past participle fished) | 钓鱼；捕鱼 | To try to catch fish. This is the third-person singular present form of the verb "fish".|He fishes in the lake every weekend. 他每个周末都在湖里钓鱼。 | 近义词：angle | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for this action) | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *fiscas (plural of fisc "fish"), from Proto-Germanic *fiskaz, from Proto-Indo-European *pisk- "fish."
