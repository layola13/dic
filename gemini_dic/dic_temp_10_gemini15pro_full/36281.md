/ˈkoʊˌhɛdɪŋ/ | n. | 共同标题；联名标题 | A heading shared by two or more articles, chapters, etc. |  Example: The two articles shared a coheading of "Climate Change Solutions". 这两篇文章共用一个标题“气候变化的解决方案”。 | Example: The conference sessions were organized under the coheading of "Sustainable Development". 会议的各个环节都安排在“可持续发展”这个总标题下。| Synonym: joint heading, shared heading | Antonym: separate heading | noun

/ˈkoʊˌhɛdɪŋ/ | v. | 共同署名；联名发表 | To give a shared heading to articles, chapters, etc. | Example: The editors decided to coheading the two related articles. 编辑决定将这两篇相关的文章联名发表。 | Example: They coheaded the chapter with a provocative title. 他们给这一章冠以了一个具有煽动性的共同标题。| Synonym: jointly head, share a heading | Antonym: separately head | verb
