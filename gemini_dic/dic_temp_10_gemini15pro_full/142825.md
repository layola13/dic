out-bellows:/ˌaʊtˈbɛloʊz/
verb
胜过…的吼叫声;发出比…更大的吼叫声
to shout louder than someone or something else
The angry crowd out-bellowed the speaker.  愤怒的群众的喊叫声盖过了演讲者。
近义词: outshout, drown out
反义词: be softer than, be quieter than
词性:v.

out-bellows:/ˌaʊtˈbɛloʊz/
noun
(比喻)更大的吼叫声
a louder shout (figuratively)
His out-bellows of rage startled everyone. 他愤怒的吼叫声吓到了所有人。
近义词: roar, bellow
反义词: whisper, murmur
词性:n.
