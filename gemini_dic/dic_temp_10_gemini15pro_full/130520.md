nonaccord:/ˌnɒnəˈkɔːrd/ | n. |不一致，分歧，缺乏同意|Lack of agreement or harmony; discord. |例句：There was a clear nonaccord between the two parties on the key issues. 双方在关键问题上明显存在分歧。|例句：The nonaccord among the jury members resulted in a hung jury. 陪审团成员之间的不一致导致陪审团无法达成裁决。|近义词：disagreement, discord, disharmony, dissent|反义词：agreement, accord, harmony, concord|词性:名词

词源:  From non- (expressing negation) + accord (agreement).  Ultimately from Latin *accordāre (to agree), from ad- (to) + cor (heart).
