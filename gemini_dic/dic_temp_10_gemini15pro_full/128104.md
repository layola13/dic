nestier:/ˈnɛstiər/ | adj. | 更会筑巢的，更恋家的 | Comparative adjective of "nesty," meaning more inclined to stay home or build a nest. | The birds became nestier as the breeding season approached.  鸟类在繁殖季节来临之际变得更恋家了。| The older cat is nestier than the kitten.  老猫比小猫更恋家。 | 近义词: homier, cozier | 反义词: restless, adventurous | 词性: adjective

词源:  From "nest" + "-y" (forming adjectives meaning "having the quality of") + "-er" (comparative suffix). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *nizdaz.
