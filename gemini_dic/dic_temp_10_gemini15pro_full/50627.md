derestricting: /ˌdiːrɪˈstrɪktɪŋ/
verb
解除限制
removing restrictions or limitations
The government is derestricting access to certain classified documents. 政府正在解除对某些机密文件的访问限制。
The company is derestricting its hiring policies to attract more diverse candidates.  公司正在放宽其招聘政策以吸引更多样化的候选人。
synonyms: liberalizing, freeing, opening up
antonyms: restricting, limiting, controlling
