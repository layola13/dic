cha-cha:/ˈtʃɑːtʃɑː/| n. vi.|恰恰舞，跳恰恰舞|A fast rhythmic ballroom dance of Latin American origin, similar to the mambo but with a triple step on the fourth beat.|例句：They danced the cha-cha all night. 他们整晚都在跳恰恰舞。

例句：She's taking cha-cha lessons. 她在上恰恰舞课。

|近义词：dance, mambo, rumba|反义词:None|词性:noun, verb
