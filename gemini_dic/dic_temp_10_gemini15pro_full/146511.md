overgeneralizing:/ˌoʊvərˈdʒɛnərəˌlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. (present participle) | 过度概括，以偏概全 | Drawing a general conclusion from too few facts or specific cases.  | Example:  He's overgeneralizing by saying all teenagers are lazy. 他说所有青少年都懒惰，这是过度概括。

Example: She's overgeneralizing from her own bad experience with dating. 她从自己糟糕的约会经历中过度概括了。 | 近义词: stereotyping, generalizing, simplifying | 反义词: specifying, particularizing | verb


overgeneralization:/ˌoʊvərˌdʒɛnərələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度概括，以偏概全 | The act of drawing a general conclusion from too few facts or specific cases. | Example:  His overgeneralization about women drivers offended many people. 他对女司机的过度概括冒犯了很多人。

Example:  The study avoided overgeneralization by using a large and diverse sample.  这项研究通过使用大量多样化的样本避免了过度概括。 | 近义词: stereotype, generalization, simplification | 反义词: specification, particularization | noun
