break:/breɪk/| v. n.|打破，折断；休息；中断；突破；破晓|To cause something to separate suddenly or violently into two or more pieces, or to become separated in this way.  Also, an interruption in continuity.|The window broke when the ball hit it.  窗户被球击中了，破了。| synonyms: shatter, crack, snap, fracture, interrupt, pause, rest, respite | antonyms: mend, repair, fix, continue, join |verb
