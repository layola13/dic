offending:/əˈfɛndɪŋ/
adjective
冒犯的；令人不快的；违规的
Causing someone to feel resentful, upset, or annoyed; causing problems or difficulties.
The offending article was removed from the website.  冒犯性的文章已从网站上删除。
Synonyms:  insulting, disrespectful, objectionable, annoying, troublesome
Antonyms:  respectful, polite, pleasant, agreeable, harmless
词性: 形容词


offending:/əˈfɛndɪŋ/
noun
犯罪；违法
The action of committing an offense. (Usually used in the phrase "the offending...")
The police tracked down the offending vehicle. 警察追踪到了肇事车辆。
Synonyms:  transgression, crime, misdemeanor, violation
Antonyms:  compliance, obedience, legality
词性: 名词
