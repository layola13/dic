destroyers:/dɪˈstrɔɪərz/
| n. |驱逐舰；破坏者|Warships designed to escort larger vessels and destroy enemy submarines and aircraft.  Designed for speed and maneuverability, they are typically smaller than cruisers but larger than frigates.  Can also refer to anything that destroys something else. |The destroyers patrolled the coastline. 驱逐舰在海岸线巡逻。|近义词：warships, demolishers, wreckers|反义词：creators, builders|词性:名词
