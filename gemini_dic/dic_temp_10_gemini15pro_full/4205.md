airhead:/ˈɛrˌhɛd/| n.|傻瓜，笨蛋|A silly or unintelligent person.  Someone who is not thinking clearly or is not very bright.|例句：She acted like such an airhead when she locked her keys in the car. 她把钥匙锁在车里时，表现得像个傻瓜。|例句：Don't be such an airhead!  Think before you speak! 别这么傻！说话前要三思！|近义词：scatterbrain, ditz, bimbo|反义词：genius, intellectual|词性:noun
