nonsacrifice:/nɒnˈsækrɪfaɪs/
noun
非牺牲，不牺牲
Absence of sacrifice; something that does not require a sacrifice.
The company's decision to invest in renewable energy was a nonsacrifice, benefiting both the environment and their bottom line.  公司决定投资可再生能源并非牺牲，这对环境和他们的底线都有好处。
Near Synonyms:  compromise, concession, accommodation
Antonyms: sacrifice, offering, immolation
词性: 名词
