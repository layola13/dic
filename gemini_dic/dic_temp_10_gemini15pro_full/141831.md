orbulina:/ɔːrˈbʊlɪnə/| n. |球藻，圆房虫|A genus of planktonic foraminifera, characterized by a spherical shell with numerous radial spines.  A type of single-celled marine organism with a globular shell.|例句：Orbulina universa is a common species found in warm ocean waters.  球藻Universa是暖洋海水域中常见的物种。

例句：The orbulina shells contribute significantly to the ocean floor sediments.  球藻壳对海底沉积物有显著贡献。|近义词：Globigerina, foraminifera|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
