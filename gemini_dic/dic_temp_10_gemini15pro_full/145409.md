overcensoriousness:/ˌoʊvərˌsɛnsɔːriəsnəs/
noun
过度审查，过度挑剔
The state of being excessively critical or censorious.
Her overcensoriousness made it difficult for anyone to express their opinions freely. 她过度挑剔使得任何人都难以自由表达意见。
The editor's overcensoriousness stifled creativity. 编辑的过度审查扼杀了创造力。

近义词:hypercriticism, faultfinding, captiousness, nitpicking
反义词:tolerance, leniency, permissiveness
词性:noun
