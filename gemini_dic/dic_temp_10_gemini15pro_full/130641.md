nonadmittedly:/ˌnɒnədˈmɪtɪdli/ | adv. | 非承认地，不公开承认地 | Not admittedly; without open acknowledgment.  The evidence was nonadmittedly damaging to his reputation. | Example: He nonadmittedly used the information for his own gain. 他没有公开承认地利用了这些信息为自己谋取私利。

词源:

"Non-" 是一个前缀，表示否定，来源于拉丁语 "non"，意为 "not"。

"Admittedly" 是一个副词，表示“承认地”，来源于动词 "admit"，其词根追溯到拉丁语 "admittere"，意为 "to send to" 或 "to allow to enter"。

因此，"nonadmittedly" 的构成是 "non-" + "admittedly"，表示“不承认地”。
