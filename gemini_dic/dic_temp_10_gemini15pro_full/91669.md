hyperchlorination:/ˌhaɪpərˌklɔːrɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. |过度氯化，超氯化|The process of adding a large amount of chlorine to water, usually for disinfection purposes.  It is used to kill bacteria and other microorganisms that can cause illness.| The city ordered hyperchlorination of the water supply after the flood.  城市在洪水过后下令对供水进行过度氯化。|
The pool underwent hyperchlorination to eliminate algae. 游泳池进行了过度氯化以消除藻类。| disinfection, chlorination | dechlorination | noun
