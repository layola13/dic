deciphered:/dɪˈsaɪfərd/ | v. | 解释；破译 |  succeeded in understanding, interpreting, or identifying something |  The archaeologist deciphered the ancient hieroglyphs. 考古学家破译了古代象形文字。| interpret, decode, solve | encode, conceal | verb
decipher:/dɪˈsaɪfər/ | v. | 解释；破译 | to convert (a coded message) into intelligible language | I couldn't decipher his handwriting. 我无法辨认他的笔迹。| interpret, decode, solve | encode, conceal | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *deschifrer, from des- "dis-" + chifrer "to calculate, to cipher," from Arabic ṣifr "zero, empty."  The original meaning was "to decipher a code or cipher."
