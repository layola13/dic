mudar:/muˈðar (BR), /muˈðaɾ (PT)/ | v. | 改变，变化，移动，搬家 | To change, alter, move, or relocate. |

例句：
Ele mudou de casa. = He moved house.

Ela mudou de ideia. = She changed her mind.


近义词：alterar (alter), modificar (modify), transformar (transform), deslocar (move), transferir (transfer) | 反义词：manter (maintain), conservar (conserve), ficar (stay), permanecer (remain) | 词性：verb


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):

Eu mudo de roupa todos os dias. = I change clothes every day.

Ele muda o canal de televisão com frequência. = He changes the TV channel frequently.


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):

Estou mudando de casa este mês. = I am moving house this month.

Ele está mudando de emprego. = He is changing jobs.


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):

Já mudei de cidade três vezes. = I have already moved cities three times.

Ela já mudou de opinião sobre o assunto. = She has already changed her mind about the matter.


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):

Tenho mudado meus hábitos alimentares. = I have been changing my eating habits.

Ele tem mudado seu estilo de vida. = He has been changing his lifestyle.


一般过去时 (Simple Past):

Mudei de escola quando era criança. = I changed schools when I was a child.

Ele mudou de carro no ano passado. = He changed cars last year.


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):

Ele estava mudando o pneu do carro. = He was changing the car tire.


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

Já tinha mudado de casa quando você chegou. = I had already moved house when you arrived.

Ela tinha mudado de ideia antes da reunião. = She had changed her mind before the meeting.


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

Eu tinha mudado meus hábitos de estudo por meses antes da prova. = I had been changing my study habits for months before the test.
Ele tinha mudado seu estilo de pintura por anos. = He had been changing his painting style for years.



一般将来时 (Simple Future):

Mudarei de emprego no próximo ano. = I will change jobs next year.

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):

Ela terá mudado de carreira até lá. = She will have changed careers by then.


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):

疑问句:

否定句:

PHRASE->

mudar de canal = to change the channel

mudar de ideia = to change one's mind

mudar de vida = to change one's life

mudar para melhor/pior = to change for the better/worse
