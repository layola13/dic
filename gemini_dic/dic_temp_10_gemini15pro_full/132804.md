nonebullient:/ˌnɒnɪˈbʌliənt/
adjective
不沸腾的；不兴高采烈的；不热情的
Not ebullient; lacking enthusiasm or exuberance.
His nonebullient demeanor made him seem aloof. 他不热情的举止让他显得冷漠。
Near synonyms: subdued, quiet, restrained, calm, placid
Antonyms: ebullient, enthusiastic, exuberant, lively, excited
词性: 形容词
