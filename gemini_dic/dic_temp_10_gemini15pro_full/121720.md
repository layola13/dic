misproduce:/ˌmɪsprəˈdjuːs/ /ˌmɪsprəˈduːs/
verb
生产错误；生产不合格产品；生产失误
To produce incorrectly or inadequately; to produce a flawed or defective product.

The factory misproduced a batch of cars with faulty brakes.
这家工厂生产了一批刹车有缺陷的汽车。

The playwright felt the director had misproduced his latest work.
剧作家觉得导演把他的最新作品搞砸了。

近义词:
mismanufacture, bungle, spoil, ruin

反义词:
produce, manufacture, create

词性
v.
