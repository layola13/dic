density:/ˈdɛnsɪti/ | n. | 密度 | The degree of compactness of a substance. | The density of lead is greater than that of aluminum. 铅的密度比铝大。 | synonyms: compactness, thickness, concentration | antonyms: rarity, thinness, sparseness | noun


density:/ˈdɛnsɪti/ | n. | 浓度 | The amount of something in a particular space or area. | The population density of Tokyo is very high. 东京的人口密度很高。| synonyms: concentration, crowdedness | antonyms: sparseness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *densitatem, from Latin densus (dense) + -itatem (suffix forming abstract nouns).
