office: /ˈɒfɪs/ (British) /ˈɔːfɪs/ (American) | n. | 办公室；办事处；公职；职责 | A room, set of rooms, or building used as a place of business for non-manual work.  A position of authority or trust, especially in government. |  例句：My office is on the third floor. 我的办公室在三楼。

| 近义词：bureau, department, agency | 反义词: home, residence | 词性: noun


office: /ˈɒfɪs/ (British) /ˈɔːfɪs/ (American) | n. | 宗教仪式；礼拜式 | A religious service or ceremony. | 例句：The priest performed the morning office.  牧师举行了晨祷。| 近义词：service, ceremony, ritual | 反义词:  None | 词性: noun
