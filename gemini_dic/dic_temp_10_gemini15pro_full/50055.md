denouncing:/dɪˈnaʊnsɪŋ/
verb
公开谴责，痛斥
Publicly declare to be wrong or evil.
The government is being denounced for its human rights record. 政府因其人权记录而受到谴责。
Synonyms: condemning, criticizing, censuring
Antonyms: praising, commending, supporting
词性: 动名词 (gerund) or 现在分词 (present participle)

denounce:/dɪˈnaʊns/
verb
公开谴责，痛斥
Publicly declare to be wrong or evil.
He denounced the government's actions as undemocratic. 他谴责政府的行为是不民主的。
Synonyms: condemn, criticize, censure
Antonyms: praise, commend, support
词性: 动词 (verb)
