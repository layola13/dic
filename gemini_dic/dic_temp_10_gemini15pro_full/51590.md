devalued:/ˌdiːˈvæljuːd/| adj.|贬值的；被贬低的|Reduced or lowered in value or price; esteem, or importance.  The currency was devalued by 10%.|例句：The currency was devalued by 10%.  该货币贬值了10%。

例句：His contributions were unfairly devalued by the critics. 他的贡献被评论家不公平地贬低了。|近义词：depreciated, cheapened, undermined|反义词: appreciated, enhanced, increased|词性:adjective
