desertedly:/dɪˈzɜːtɪdli/
adverb
荒凉地，无人居住地，冷清地
In a deserted manner; empty of people.
The streets were desertedly quiet after the curfew.  宵禁之后，街道空无一人，一片寂静。
The old house stood desertedly on the hill. 那栋老房子孤零零地矗立在山上。


近义词:abandonedly, emptily, lonely, desolate
反义词:crowdedly, busily, populated


词性:adverb
词源:From "deserted" + "-ly". "Deserted" comes from the past participle of the verb "desert", which ultimately derives from the Latin "deserere" (to abandon).
