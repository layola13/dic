baunscheidtism: /ˈbaʊnʃaɪtɪzəm/
n.
針刺疗法;放血疗法
A form of alternative medicine that involves pricking the skin with a needle-studded instrument and applying an irritant to cause blistering, believed to draw out toxins and treat various ailments.
Baunscheidtism is a rarely practiced treatment these days.  针刺疗法现在很少有人使用了。
The doctor suggested baunscheidtism as a last resort. 医生建议针刺疗法作为最后的手段。
Synonym: counter-irritation, derivation
Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps conventional medicine)
