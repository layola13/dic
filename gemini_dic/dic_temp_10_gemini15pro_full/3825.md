agonised:/ˈæɡənaɪzd/ | adj. | 极度痛苦的，苦恼的 | Experiencing or expressing great mental or physical suffering. | She let out an agonised cry. 她发出一声痛苦的哭喊。|  He spent an agonised night waiting for news. 他度过了一个焦急的夜晚等待消息。 | synonyms: tormented, anguished, distressed, pained | antonyms: comfortable, relaxed, calm, soothed | adjective
