delve:/dɛlv/| v. | 探究，钻研，深入研究 | To search deeply and thoroughly into something. | 例句：He delved into the archives, searching for clues. 他深入档案馆，寻找线索。

例句：She delved into her handbag for her keys.  她在手提包里翻找钥匙。

近义词：investigate, explore, probe, research, examine | 反义词: ignore, neglect | 词性: 动词

词源: From Old English *delfan (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *delbaną (“to dig”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰelbʰ- (“to dig, carve”). Cognate with Old Saxon delban (“to dig”), Old Frisian delva (“ditch, trench”), Dutch delven (“to dig”), German delben.
