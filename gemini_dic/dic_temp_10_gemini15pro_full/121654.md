misphrased:/ˌmɪsˈfreɪzd/
adjective
表达错误的；措辞不当的
expressed badly or incorrectly
The question was misphrased and caused confusion. 这个问题措辞不当，引起了混乱。
Synonyms: wrongly worded, badly expressed, inapt, inaccurate, clumsy
Antonyms: well-phrased, articulate, eloquent, clear, precise
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
mis- + phrased
mis-: prefix meaning "wrong" or "badly"
phrased: past participle of the verb "phrase," meaning "to express in words"
