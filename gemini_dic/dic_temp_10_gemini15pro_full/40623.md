convening:/kənˈviːnɪŋ/ | n. | 召集，会议 | The convening of the conference was delayed due to unforeseen circumstances. 会议的召开由于不可预见的情况而被推迟。 |  近义词：assembly, meeting, gathering | 反义词：adjournment, dispersal | noun

convene:/kənˈviːn/ | v. | 召集，集合 | The committee will convene next week to discuss the budget. 委员会将于下周召开会议讨论预算。 |  近义词：assemble, gather, summon | 反义词：adjourn, dismiss | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *convenir "to agree, be suitable," from Latin convenire "to come together, assemble, meet, agree," from com- "together" (see com-) + venire "to come" (from PIE root *gwa- "to go, come").
