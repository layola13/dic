hoariest:/ˈhɔːriɪst/ (US), /ˈhɔːriɪst/ (UK)| adj.|最古老的；头发最白的；霜最重的|Covered with white frost, especially thickly; extremely old or aged; having white or gray hair.|例句：The hoariest of the ancient trees stood sentinel on the hilltop.  古老的树中最古老的那棵像哨兵一样矗立在山顶上。

例句：He told the hoariest joke I've ever heard. 他讲了我听过的最老掉牙的笑话。|近义词：oldest, ancient, venerable, grayest, whitest|反义词：youngest, newest, freshest|词性:形容词
