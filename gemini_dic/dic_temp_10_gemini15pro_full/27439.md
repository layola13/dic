cammock:/ˈkæmək/| n.|金雀花，染料木|A spiny shrub (Ononis repens) with pink or purple flowers, common in Europe and naturalized in North America. It was formerly used as a source of yellow dye.|The cammock grew profusely along the roadside.|近义词：rest-harrow, petty whin|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
