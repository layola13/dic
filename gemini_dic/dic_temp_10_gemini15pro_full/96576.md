induced:/ɪnˈdjuːst/ /ɪnˈduːst/| adj.|诱导的，感应的，引起的|Brought about or caused by something else; stimulated.|例句：The drug induced a state of euphoria. 这种药物引起了一种欣快感。|近义词：caused, stimulated, brought about, produced, generated|反义词：natural, spontaneous|adjective

induced:/ɪnˈdjuːst/ /ɪnˈduːst/| v.|诱导，引起，感应|Succeed in persuading or influencing (someone) to do something.|例句：The advertisement induced me to buy the product. 这则广告诱使我购买了该产品。|近义词：persuade, influence, convince, prompt, encourage|反义词：deter, discourage, prevent|verb
