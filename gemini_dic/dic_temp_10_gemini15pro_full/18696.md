berate:/bɪˈreɪt/ | v. | 斥责，责骂 | To scold or criticize someone angrily and at length. | The coach berated the players for their poor performance. 教练因球员表现不佳而斥责他们。|  The teacher berated the student for being late. 老师责骂学生迟到。 | synonyms: scold, rebuke, reprimand, chastise, upbraid | antonyms: praise, commend, laud | verb
