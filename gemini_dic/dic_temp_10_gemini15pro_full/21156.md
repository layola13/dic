blizzarding: /ˈblɪzərdɪŋ/
verb
暴风雪肆虐；下着暴风雪
Engaging in a blizzard; experiencing a blizzard.
It was blizzarding outside, making travel impossible.  外面暴风雪肆虐，出行根本不可能。
The mountain climbers were caught blizzarding at high altitude. 登山者在高海拔地区遭遇了暴风雪。
近义词:snowing heavily, blowing a gale
反义词:calm, sunny
词性:verb
