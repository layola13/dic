nonfrenetically: /ˌnɑːnfrəˈnɛtɪkli/ | adv. | 非狂热地；平静地；不慌不忙地 | In a way that is not frantic or hectic; calmly. |  He worked nonfrenetically, taking his time to ensure accuracy.  他工作不慌不忙，慢慢来确保准确性。 | Synonyms: calmly, peacefully, tranquilly, sedately, leisurely, quietly | Antonyms: frantically, hectically, frenetically, excitedly, wildly | adverb

Etymology:
* **non-**:  Latin prefix meaning "not".
* **frenetic**: From Greek *phrēnētikos* "delirious, frantic," from *phrēn* "mind, heart, diaphragm".
* **-ally**: Adverbial suffix.
