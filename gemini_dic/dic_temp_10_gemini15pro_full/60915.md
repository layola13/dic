elephantiasis: /ˌelɪfənˈtaɪəsɪs/
n.
象皮病
A condition characterized by the gross enlargement of an area of the body, especially a limb, due to obstruction of the lymphatic vessels, causing edema.
Elephantiasis can be a debilitating disease. 象皮病可能是一种使人衰弱的疾病。
Elephantiasis is often caused by parasitic worms. 象皮病通常由寄生虫引起。
近义词: lymphedema, pachydermia
反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific medical condition)
词性: noun
