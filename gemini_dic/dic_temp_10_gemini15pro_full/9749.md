antizymic: /ˌæntiˈzaɪmɪk/ | adj. | 抗酶的；抑制酶活性的 |  Relating to or denoting an antizyme, a protein that inhibits the activity of an enzyme. |  Antizymic activity can regulate metabolic pathways. 抗酶活性可以调节代谢途径。| synonyms: enzyme-inhibiting, enzyme-blocking | antonyms: enzyme-activating, enzyme-promoting | adjective

antizyme: /ˈæntiˌzaɪm/ | n. | 抗酶 | A protein that inhibits the activity of an enzyme. |  Ornithine decarboxylase antizyme is a key regulator of polyamine synthesis. 鸟氨酸脱羧酶抗酶是多胺合成的关键调节因子。| synonyms: enzyme inhibitor | antonyms: enzyme activator | noun
