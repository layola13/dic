nymphiparous: /ˌnɪmfɪˈpærəs/
adjective
蛹生的；若虫产的
Giving birth to nymphs or young that resemble nymphs.  A nymphiparous insect gives birth to nymphs, which are immature forms that resemble the adult insect.
The tsetse fly is a nymphiparous insect.  采采蝇是一种蛹生昆虫。
近义词: larviparous, pupiparous
反义词: oviparous
词性: adjective

Etymology:  From Greek *nýmphē* (nymph) +  Latin *pario* (to give birth to)  + -ous (adjective suffix)
