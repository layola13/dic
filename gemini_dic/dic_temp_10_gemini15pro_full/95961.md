inculcate:/ɪnˈkʌlkeɪt/
verb
灌输，谆谆教诲
instill (an attitude, idea, or habit) by persistent instruction.
例句：The teacher inculcated in her students the importance of honesty. 老师谆谆教诲学生们诚实的重要性。

例句：Parents try to inculcate a sense of responsibility in their children. 父母试图灌输给孩子们责任感。

近义词：instill, implant, infuse, ingrain
反义词：eradicate, extirpate
词性：verb
