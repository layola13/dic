fulcrate: /ˈfʌlkreɪt/ | adj. | 支点的，杠杆的 | Having the qualities of, or acting as, a fulcrum. | The small bone in the wrist acts as a fulcrate for the hand's movement. 这块腕部的小骨头充当了手部运动的支点。 |  近义词: pivotal, supporting | 反义词: unsupported | 词性: adjective

fulcrum: /ˈfʌlkrəm/ | n. | 支点，杠杆 | The point on which a lever rests or is supported and on which it pivots. | The fulcrum of the seesaw allowed the children to go up and down. 跷跷板的支点让孩子们可以上下移动。 | 近义词: pivot, axis | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
