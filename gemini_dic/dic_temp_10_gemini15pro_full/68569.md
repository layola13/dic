fangs:/fæŋz/
noun
尖牙; 犬齿; (蛇的)毒牙
long, pointed teeth, especially the canine teeth of a dog, wolf, or other animal, or the hollow teeth through which a poisonous snake injects venom.
The dog bared its fangs.  这条狗露出了它的尖牙。
teeth, tusks, incisors
gums
