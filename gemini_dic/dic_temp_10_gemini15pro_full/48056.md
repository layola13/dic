declaims:/dɪˈkleɪmz/
verb
慷慨陈词；朗诵；演讲
to speak rhetorically; to make a formal speech
The actor declaimed his lines with great passion. 演员充满激情地朗诵着他的台词。
近义词: orate, recite, deliver, pronounce, preach
反义词: whisper, murmur, mutter
词性: verb

declaim:/dɪˈkleɪm/
verb
慷慨陈词；朗诵；演讲
to speak rhetorically; to make a formal speech
She declaimed against the injustice of the system. 她慷慨激昂地抨击该制度的不公正。
近义词: rant, inveigh, fulminate
反义词: commend, praise, support
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin declamare "to practice speaking, declaim," from de- "down, completely" + clamare "to cry out, shout."
