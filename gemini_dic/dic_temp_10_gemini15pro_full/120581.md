mirky:/ˈmɜːki/ (UK) /ˈmɜːrki/ (US)| adj.|黑暗的，阴暗的；浑浊的，模糊不清的|Dark and gloomy, or thick with fog, smoke, etc.; obscure or morally questionable.|例句：The future of the company looks mirky. 这家公司的前景看起来很暗淡。|例句：The mirky depths of the swamp concealed unseen dangers. 沼泽阴暗的深处隐藏着看不见的危险。|近义词：dark, gloomy, murky, obscure, shady|反义词：bright, clear, transparent, light|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *mirke, from Old English *mierce (“dark, murky”), from Proto-Germanic *mirkaz (“dark”), from Proto-Indo-European *merk- (“to blink, twinkle; to be dark”). Cognate with Dutch merk (“mark, sign”), German merken (“to notice, perceive, mark”), Old Norse myrkr (“dark”), Gothic 𐌼𐌰𐍂𐌺𐍃 (marks, “a mark”).
