millipede:/ˈmɪlɪpiːd/| n. |千足虫，马陆|A myriapod invertebrate with an elongated body composed of many segments, most of which bear two pairs of legs.|例句：I saw a millipede crawling on the forest floor. 我看见一只千足虫在森林地面上爬行。

例句：Millipedes are important decomposers in the ecosystem. 千足虫是生态系统中重要的分解者。|近义词：myriapod|反义词:None|词性:noun
