impregnable:/ɪmˈprɛɡnəbl/| adj.|坚不可摧的；无法攻克的；不受影响的|Unable to be captured or broken into.  Not able to be affected or overcome.|例句：The castle, situated on a high crag, was virtually impregnable.  这座城堡坐落在高高的峭壁上，几乎坚不可摧。

例句：He built up an impregnable defense against criticism. 他建立了无懈可击的防御来抵御批评。|近义词：invincible, unassailable, secure, impenetrable|反义词：vulnerable, weak, defenseless|词性: 形容词


词源: Late Middle English: from Old French imprenable, from in- ‘not’ + prenable ‘able to be taken’, from prendre ‘to take’, from Latin prehendere.
