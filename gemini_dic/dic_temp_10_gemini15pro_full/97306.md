informable:/ɪnˈfɔːrməbl/ | adj. | 可通知的，可告知的 | Able to be informed; capable of receiving information. | The committee is informable about the latest developments. 委员会可以被告知最新的进展。| The public should be kept informable about the ongoing investigation. 公众应该被告知正在进行的调查。 | 近义词:  notifiable, advisable, apprised | 反义词: uninformed, ignorant | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From "inform" + "-able". "Inform" comes from Old French "enformer" and ultimately from Latin "informare" (to shape, train, instruct).  "-able" is a suffix indicating capability or suitability.
