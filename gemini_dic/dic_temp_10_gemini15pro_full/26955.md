calceolate:/ˈkælsiəleɪt/| adj.| 拖鞋状的，囊状的|Shaped like a slipper or pouch; having a pouch-like appendage.|The lady's slipper orchid has a distinctive calceolate flower. 女士拖鞋兰具有一种独特的拖鞋状的花朵。|近义词：slipper-shaped, pouch-like, saccate|反义词: - |词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *calceolatus*, from calceolus (“small shoe, slipper”), diminutive of calceus (“shoe”).
