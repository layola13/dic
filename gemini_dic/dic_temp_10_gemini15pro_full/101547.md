ironing:/ˈaɪərnɪŋ/ /ˈaɪərniŋ/| adj.|熨烫的|Relating to the act of ironing.|She hates ironing clothes. 她讨厌熨衣服。|Synonyms: pressing|Antonyms: wrinkling|adjective
