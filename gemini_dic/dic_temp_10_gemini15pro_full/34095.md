civilizers:/ˈsɪvɪˌlaɪzərz/
n.
文明化者；教化者
People or groups who bring civilization to others, often implying a sense of superiority.
The Spanish conquistadors saw themselves as civilizers bringing Christianity and European culture to the New World.  西班牙征服者将自己视为文明化者，将基督教和欧洲文化带到新大陆。
The missionaries considered themselves civilizers of the indigenous population.  传教士认为自己是土著居民的文明化者。
