disprofit:/dɪsˈprɒfɪt/
n.
亏损，损失
a loss in a business or transaction; opposite of profit
The company reported a disprofit of $1 million this quarter.  这家公司本季度报告亏损100万美元。
近义词:loss, deficit, damage
反义词:profit, gain, advantage
词性:名词

词源:  "dis-" (prefix expressing negation) + "profit" (from Old French, ultimately from Latin "profectus" - advance, progress)
