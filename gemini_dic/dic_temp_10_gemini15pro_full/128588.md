nice-looking:/ˌnaɪs ˈlʊkɪŋ/ | adj. |好看的，漂亮的|Pleasing to the eye; attractive. |例句：She has a nice-looking car. 她有一辆好看的汽车。|例句：He's a nice-looking young man. 他是一个英俊的年轻人。|近义词：attractive, beautiful, good-looking, handsome, pretty|反义词：ugly, unattractive, plain|词性:形容词
词源:  Compound adjective formed from "nice" (meaning pleasant or agreeable) and "looking" (present participle of "look," referring to appearance).
