defocalization:/diːˌfoʊkələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |散焦，去焦，失焦|The act or process of making something less focused or distinct; a blurring or softening of focus. |The artist uses defocalization to create a dreamlike effect. 艺术家利用散焦来营造梦幻般的效果。 | The defocalization of the image made it difficult to identify the subject. 图像的散焦使得难以识别主体。 |近义词：blurring, softening, diffusion |反义词：focusing, sharpening |词性: noun


Etymology:
From de- (prefix meaning "removal, reversal") + focalization (from focus).  The word "focus" itself comes from Latin "focus" meaning "hearth, fireplace" (the center of heat and light).
