corniferous:/kɔːrˈnɪfərəs/
adjective
含玉米的；产玉米的；[地质]含角石的
Containing or producing corn; (Geology) containing hornstone.
The corniferous limestone is rich in fossils. 这种含角石的石灰岩富含化石。
Synonyms: corn-producing, corn-bearing (for agricultural sense); hornstone-bearing, cherty (for geological sense)
Antonyms: barren, infertile (for agricultural sense)
word type: adjective
