bogus:/ˈboʊɡəs/| adj.|假的，伪造的，冒牌的|Not genuine or true; fake.  Something that is bogus is a sham, counterfeit, or fraudulent.|例句：The company was selling bogus products. 这家公司在销售假冒产品。|例句：He presented bogus credentials to gain access to the building. 他出示了伪造的证件以进入大楼。|近义词：fake, counterfeit, spurious, phony, sham|反义词：genuine, authentic, real|词性:形容词
