chillax:/tʃɪˈlæks/| v. |放松，冷静|To calm down and relax.  A blend of "chill" and "relax".|例句：I'm going to chillax by the pool today. 我今天打算在泳池边放松一下。

例句：Just chillax, everything will be alright.  放松点，一切都会好起来的。|近义词：relax, unwind, de-stress, chill out|反义词:stress out,  panic,  fret|词性:动词
