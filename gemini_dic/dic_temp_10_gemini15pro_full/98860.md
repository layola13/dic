intemperateness:/ɪnˈtɛmpərɪtnəs/
n.|放纵；无节制；过度|Lack of moderation or restraint, especially in the indulgence of appetites or passions.|His intemperateness in drinking led to his downfall. 他的酗酒导致了他的垮台。|近义词：excess, immoderation, intemperance|反义词：moderation, temperance, restraint|词性:名词
Etymology: From Late Latin intemperantia, from in- "not" + temperantia "moderation."
