cirrigerous:/sɪˈrɪdʒərəs/
adjective
卷云的；有卷云的
Producing cirrus clouds.  Characterized by cirrus clouds.
Cirrigerous clouds often indicate an approaching weather front. 卷云通常预示着天气锋面的临近。
The cirrigerous sky hinted at a change in weather.  卷云密布的天空暗示着天气即将变化。
Synonym: cirrus-bearing, cirrose
Antonym: (No direct antonym, consider antonyms for "cloudy" like clear, cloudless)
词性: adjective
