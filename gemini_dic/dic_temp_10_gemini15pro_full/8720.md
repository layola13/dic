anticyclonically:/ˌæntɪsaɪˈklɒnɪkli/ /ˌæntɪsaɪˈkloʊnɪkli/
| adv. | 反气旋式地 | In a manner relating to or characteristic of an anticyclone; clockwise in the Northern Hemisphere and counterclockwise in the Southern Hemisphere. | The wind blew anticyclonically around the high-pressure system.  风以反气旋方向绕高压系统吹。| clockwise, | cyclonically | adverb

anticyclone:/ˌæntɪˈsaɪkloʊn/ /ˌæntɪˈsaɪkləʊn/
| n. | 反气旋 | A weather system with high atmospheric pressure at its center, around which air slowly circulates in a clockwise direction in the Northern Hemisphere and counterclockwise in the Southern Hemisphere. | The anticyclone brought clear skies and calm weather. 反气旋带来了晴朗的天空和平静的天气。 | high-pressure system, high | cyclone, low-pressure system | noun
