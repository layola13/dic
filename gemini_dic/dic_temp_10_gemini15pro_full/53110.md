diphygenic:/ˌdaɪfɪˈdʒɛnɪk/| adj.|双源的，二源的|Formed from two distinct sources or lines of descent.  Originating from two different sources.|例句：The diphygenic theory suggests that the human species evolved from two separate ancestral populations. 双源论认为人类是从两个独立的祖先群体进化而来的。

例句：Diphygenic inheritance can complicate the study of genetic disorders.  双源遗传会使遗传疾病的研究复杂化。|近义词：dual-origin, biphyletic|反义词：monophyletic, single-origin|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *di- "two" + *phyle "tribe, race" + *-genic "producing."
