overextended:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈstendɪd/| adj.|过度扩张的；过分伸展的；承担过多义务的|Having stretched or expanded beyond a safe or reasonable limit, especially financially.|The company was overextended and had to borrow heavily to cover its debts. 这家公司过度扩张，不得不大量举债以偿还债务。|近义词：overstretched, strained, overburdened|反义词:underutilized, unexpanded|词性:形容词

overextend:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈstend/| vt.|过度扩张；过分伸展；承担过多义务|To stretch or expand beyond a safe or reasonable limit, especially financially.|Don't overextend yourself by taking on too many projects. 不要承担过多的项目，以免力不从心。|近义词：overstretch, strain, overburden|反义词:underutilize|词性:动词
