leprechaun: /ˈlɛprəkɔːn/ (US), /ˈlɛprɪkɔːn/ (UK)| n. |小妖精，矮妖|A mischievous sprite in Irish folklore, typically representing a little old man, often believed to reveal the whereabouts of treasure to those who catch it.|例句：Legend says that leprechauns guard pots of gold at the end of the rainbow. 传说小妖精在彩虹的尽头守护着一罐罐金子。

|例句：The children were searching for leprechauns in the forest. 孩子们在森林里寻找小妖精。
|近义词：fairy, pixie, sprite, elf|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "giant" as a contrasting concept)|词性:noun
