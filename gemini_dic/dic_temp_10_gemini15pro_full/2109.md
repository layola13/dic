adansonia: /ˌædənˈsoʊniə/
noun
猴面包树属
A genus of deciduous trees of the mallow family (Malvaceae), native to Africa, Madagascar, and Australia and having large, thick trunks, palmately compound leaves, large, pendulous white flowers, and gourdlike woody fruit.
The baobab tree, an adansonia, is known for its enormous trunk.  猴面包树，一种猴面包树属植物，以其巨大的树干而闻名。

Adansonia digitata is the most widespread of the Adansonia species.  指状猴面包树是猴面包树属中最广泛分布的物种。
