dapperer:/ˈdæpərər/| adj.|更衣着讲究的，更整洁的|More dapper; neater in appearance.  A comparative form of "dapper."  Indicates someone is more stylish and well-groomed than another. |He was always the dapperer of the two brothers, meticulously dressed in the latest fashions. 他总是两兄弟中穿着更讲究的那一个，一丝不苟地穿着最新的时装。|近义词：smarter, more stylish, more elegant, sprucer|反义词：scruffier, shabbier, more unkempt|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From dapper + -er (comparative suffix).  "Dapper" itself likely comes from Dutch *dapper, meaning "brave, valiant." Over time, the meaning shifted to "active, nimble," and eventually to "neat and stylish."
