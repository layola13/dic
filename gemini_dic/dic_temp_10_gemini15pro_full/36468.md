coldly:/ˈkoʊldli/ (US) /ˈkəʊldli/ (UK)| adv.|冷淡地，冷漠地|In a manner lacking affection or warmth; in a detached or unemotional way.|She looked at him coldly and turned away. 她冷冷地看了他一眼，然后转过身去。|例句：He coldly refused her request. 他冷淡地拒绝了她的请求。|近义词：indifferently, aloofly, unfeelingly, heartlessly|反义词：warmly, affectionately, kindly|词性:副词
