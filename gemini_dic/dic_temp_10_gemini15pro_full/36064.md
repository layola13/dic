coediting:/koʊˈedɪtɪŋ/| n. |共同编辑|The act of editing something together with one or more other people.|We are coediting a special issue of the journal. 我们正在共同编辑该期刊的一期特刊。|近义词：collaborative editing, joint editing|反义词：solo editing|noun
