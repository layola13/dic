idealogue:/ˈaɪdiəlɔːɡ/, /ˌaɪdiəˈlɔːɡ/ | n. | 理论家；空想家；意识形态拥护者 | A person who strongly believes in a particular set of ideas, especially political ideas, and tries to persuade others to accept them. |  He's a staunch idealogue who refuses to compromise. 他是一个坚定的理论家，拒绝妥协。

|  The party is dominated by ideologues who care more about theory than practical results.  这个党被一些意识形态拥护者所控制，他们更关心理论而不是实际结果。 | 近义词: theorist, doctrinaire, zealot | 反义词: pragmatist, realist | 词性: noun

Etymology: From French *idéologue*, from *idée* (“idea”) + *-logue* (from Ancient Greek *λόγος* (*lógos*, “word, speech, reason”)).
