overmelodiousness:/ˌoʊvərˌmɛˈloʊdiəsnɛs/
noun
过度旋律化；过分悦耳
Excessive melodiousness; an overabundance of melody.
The overmelodiousness of the music made it cloying after a while.  这音乐过于旋律化，过了一会儿就让人觉得腻了。
Near Synonyms: cloying sweetness, saccharinity, excessive melody
Antonyms: dissonance, cacophony
n.
