county: /ˈkaʊnti/ (US) /ˈkaʊnti/ (UK)| n. |县，郡|A political and administrative division of a state or country. |例句：He lives in Fairfax County, Virginia. 他住在弗吉尼亚州的费尔法克斯县。|例句：The county is responsible for maintaining the roads. 县政府负责维护道路。|近义词：district, region, shire (UK)|反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *conté (Modern French comté), from Latin comitātus (“escort, retinue; county, shire”), from comes (“companion”), later (“count; earl”). Doublet of count.
