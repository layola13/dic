non-pressure:/ˌnɑːnˈprɛʃər/| adj.|无压力的，非强迫的|Relating to a situation or environment where there is no coercion or obligation; relaxed and informal.|The coach created a non-pressure learning environment for the young athletes. 教练为年轻运动员创造了一个轻松的学习环境。|The event was a non-pressure social gathering, a chance to just relax and chat.  这次活动是一个轻松的社交聚会，一个放松聊天的好机会。|Synonyms: relaxed, informal, casual, easygoing|Antonyms: stressful, demanding, intense|adjective
