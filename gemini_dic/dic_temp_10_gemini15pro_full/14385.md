autosaves:/ˈɔːtəʊseɪvz/ (UK) /ˈɔːt̬oʊseɪvz/ (US)
| n. | 自动保存 | The act of a program automatically saving data to prevent loss in case of a crash or other interruption. |  The autosaves feature in this software is really helpful, I don't have to worry about losing my work.  这个软件的自动保存功能真的很有帮助，我不必担心丢失我的工作。 | synonyms: automatic saves, backups | antonyms: manual saves | noun
