out-voter:/ˈaʊtˌvoʊtər/
noun
外出投票者;在外地投票的人
A person who votes outside of their usual electoral district.  This often applies to students voting in their university town rather than their home town.
Many out-voters registered to vote in the swing states. 许多外出投票者在摇摆州登记投票。
synonyms: absentee voter, non-resident voter
antonyms: resident voter
n.
