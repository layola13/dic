noncollapsibility:/ˌnɒnkəˌlæpsəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可折叠性，不可坍缩性 | The property of a statistical model or data structure that prevents it from being simplified or reduced in dimensionality without losing information. | The noncollapsibility of the odds ratio is a well-known phenomenon in statistics.  不可折叠性是统计学中众所周知的现象。|  The study highlighted the noncollapsibility of the hierarchical model used. 这项研究强调了所使用的分层模型的不可折叠性。| Near synonyms:  irreducibility, non-reducibility | Antonyms: collapsibility, reducibility | noun
