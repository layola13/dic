pancreatography:/ˌpænkrɪəˈtɒɡrəfi/ /ˌpænkrɪəˈtɑːɡrəfi/ | n. | 胰腺造影术 | Radiographic examination of the pancreatic ducts.  It is a medical imaging technique used to visualize the pancreatic ducts. | The doctor ordered a pancreatography to investigate the patient's abdominal pain. 医生为调查病人的腹痛，安排了胰腺造影术。

Pancreatography revealed a blockage in the main pancreatic duct. 胰腺造影显示主胰管有阻塞。| 近义词: ERCP (Endoscopic retrograde cholangiopancreatography) | 反义词:  None (No direct antonym exists) | 词性: noun
