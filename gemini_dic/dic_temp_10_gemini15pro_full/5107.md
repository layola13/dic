allegeable: /əˈledʒəbl/ | adj. | 可被断言的，可被宣称的 |  That which can be alleged; allegable matter. Something that can be asserted or declared, often without proof. | The defendant's alleged motive was allegeable, but not yet proven.  被告的被指控的动机是可以断言的，但尚未得到证实。 | Synonyms: assertable, claimable, arguable | Antonyms: unprovable, unsubstantiated, undeclared | adjective
