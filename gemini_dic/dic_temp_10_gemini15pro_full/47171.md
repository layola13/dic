dawns:/dɔːnz/
| n. |黎明，开端|The first faint light of dawn appeared on the horizon.  地平线上出现了黎明的微光。|
| v. |开始出现，变得明朗|It dawned on me that I had left my keys at home. 我突然意识到我把钥匙忘在家里了。|
近义词: beginnings, daybreak, sunrise, starts
反义词: dusk, sunset, endings, conclusions
词性: 名词 (n.), 动词 (v.)

Etymology: From Middle English *dawen, from Old English *dagian (“to become day”), from Proto-Germanic *dagōną (“to become day”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰegʰ- (“to burn, shine”).
