exsuscitate: /ˌɛksˈsʌsɪˌteɪt/
verb
使甦醒；使复活；激起
To rouse from inactivity or apparent death; to revive.  To stir up; excite.
He attempted to exsuscitate the dying embers of the fire. 他试图使将熄的余烬复燃。
The politician's speech exsuscitated the crowd's anger.  这位政治家的演讲激起了群众的愤怒。

近义词: revive, resurrect, reanimate, awaken, arouse, stimulate, provoke
反义词: suppress, quell, subdue, extinguish

词性: verb
词源: From Latin exsuscitatus, past participle of exsuscitare ("to rouse up, awaken"), from ex- ("out, up") + suscitare ("to lift up, rouse").
