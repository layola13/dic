homiform:/ˈhoʊməˌfɔːrm/| adj.|同形的，形状相同的|Having the same form or shape.  Relating to or being homomorphs.|The two butterflies are homiform, despite being different species. 这两种蝴蝶形状相同，尽管它们是不同的物种。|近义词：isomorphic, similar, analogous|反义词：heteromorphic, dissimilar|词性:adjective
