fiddlestick:/ˈfɪdəlstɪk/| n. |胡说；废话；小提琴弓|A trifling thing or matter; nonsense.  It's often used as a mild exclamation of annoyance or disbelief.|例句：Fiddlesticks! I forgot my keys. 废话！我忘记带钥匙了。|例句：Oh, fiddlesticks, I spilled my coffee!  哦，真糟糕，我咖啡洒了！|近义词：nonsense, rubbish, balderdash|反义词:truth, fact|词性:名词
