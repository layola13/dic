glazed:/ɡleɪzd/ | adj. |  光滑的，涂釉的；呆滞的，无表情的 | Covered with a smooth, shiny surface, as if with a glaze; having an unfocused or expressionless stare. | The baker took the glazed donuts out of the oven.  面包师把光滑的甜甜圈从烤箱里拿了出来。

The glazed expression on his face suggested he hadn't slept all night. 他脸上呆滞的表情表明他整晚没睡。 | 近义词：shiny, glossy, glassy, vacant, dull | 反义词：matte, dull, alert, expressive | 词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *glasier, from glas "glass," of Germanic origin.
