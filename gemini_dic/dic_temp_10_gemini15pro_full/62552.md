enemys:/ˈɛnəmiz/ | n. | 敌人（复数，非标准用法，标准用法是enemies） |  A non-standard plural form of "enemy," meaning people who are actively opposed or hostile to someone or something. The standard plural is "enemies." | He saw his enemys approaching. (Non-standard) 他看到他的敌人正在靠近。（非标准用法） | 近义词：foes, adversaries, opponents | 反义词：friends, allies, supporters | 词性：名词

词源:
From Old French enemi, from Latin inimicus "unfriendly, hostile," literally "not a friend," from in- "not" + amicus "friend."
