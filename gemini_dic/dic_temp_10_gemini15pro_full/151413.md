parochialism:/pəˈroʊkiəlɪzəm/
n.
狭隘；地方观念；眼界狭小
A limited or narrow outlook, especially focused on local concerns and excluding wider perspectives.
The mayor's parochialism prevented him from seeing the benefits of the regional transportation plan.  市长的狭隘观念使他无法看到区域交通规划的好处。
narrow-mindedness, provincialism, insularity
cosmopolitanism, broad-mindedness, worldliness
noun

parochial:/pəˈroʊkiəl/
adj.
狭隘的；地方性的；教区的
Relating to or supported by a church parish; having a limited or narrow outlook or scope.
She accused the school board of having a parochial view of education. 她指责学校董事会对教育持有狭隘的观点。
limited, narrow, provincial, local
broad, universal, cosmopolitan, global
adjective
