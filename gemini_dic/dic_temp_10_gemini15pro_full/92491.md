hypothecium:/ˌhaɪpəˈθiːsiəm/
n.
子囊盘下层，子囊座下层
The layer of fungal tissue immediately beneath the hymenium in some ascomycetous fungi.
The ascocarps have a well-developed hypothecium. 这些子囊果具有发达的子囊盘下层。
The hypothecium is composed of intertwined hyphae. 子囊盘下层由交织的菌丝组成。
synonyms: subhymenium
