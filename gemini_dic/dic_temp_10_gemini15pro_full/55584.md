disulphide:/daɪˈsʌlfaɪd/ /dɪˈsʌlfaɪd/
n.
二硫化物
A compound containing a disulphide group, formed from two sulphur atoms joined by a single bond.
Iron disulphide is a common mineral.  硫化铁是一种常见的矿物。
