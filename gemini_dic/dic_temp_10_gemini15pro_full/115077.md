maneuverably:/məˈnuːvərəbli/
adverb
易操纵地，灵活地
Easily maneuvered; in a maneuverable manner.
The small car was maneuverably parked in the tight space. 这辆小车灵活地停在了狭窄的空间里。
The new wheelchair is designed to be highly maneuverably, allowing users to navigate tight corners with ease.  这款新的轮椅设计得非常灵活，可以让用户轻松地通过狭窄的角落。
近义词:nimbly, adroitly, deftly, skillfully
反义词:clumsily, awkwardly
词性:adverb

maneuver:/məˈnuːvər/
noun, verb
策略；调遣；演习；用策略使…；调遣；演习；巧妙地操纵
A movement or series of moves requiring skill and care.
(v.) To move skillfully or carefully.
(n.)  The troops carried out complex maneuvers. 部队进行了复杂的演习。
(v.)  She maneuvered the car into a tight parking space. 她巧妙地把车停在一个狭窄的停车位里。
近义词:(n.) tactic, strategy, operation; (v.) manipulate, navigate, steer
反义词: -(n.) inaction
词性:noun, verb
