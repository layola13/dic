parallactic:/ˌpærəˈlæktɪk/| adj.|视差的，由视差引起的|Relating to or caused by parallax; pertaining to the apparent shift in position of an object when viewed from different locations.|Astronomers use parallactic measurements to determine the distance to stars. 天文学家使用视差测量来确定到恒星的距离。|Near synonyms: parallax,  differential|Antonyms: None|adjective

parallactically:/ˌpærəˈlæktɪkli/| adv.|视差地|In a parallactic manner; with parallax.|The star appeared to move parallactically as the Earth orbited the Sun.  当地球绕太阳运行时，这颗恒星似乎发生了视差运动。|Near synonyms: differentially |Antonyms: None|adverb
