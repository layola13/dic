cves:/siːˌviːˈiːz/| n.| 通用漏洞披露 (Common Vulnerabilities and Exposures) 的缩写| CVEs are a list of publicly disclosed security vulnerabilities.  通用漏洞披露是一个公开披露的安全漏洞列表。|  Many security tools use CVEs to identify and categorize vulnerabilities. 许多安全工具使用 CVE 来识别和分类漏洞。|近义词：vulnerabilities, exploits|反义词: patches, fixes| abbreviation (缩写)
