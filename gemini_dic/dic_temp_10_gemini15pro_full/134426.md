nonironic:/ˌnɒn aɪˈrɒnɪk/ | adj. | 非讽刺的，真诚的 | Not ironic; sincere and straightforward. Expressing actual feelings or opinions, not the opposite of what is meant for humorous or emphatic effect. |  He gave a nonironic thumbs-up after finishing the race. 他在跑完比赛后真诚地竖起了大拇指。| synonyms: sincere, genuine, earnest, heartfelt, straightforward | antonyms: ironic, sarcastic, sardonic, mocking, facetious | adjective
