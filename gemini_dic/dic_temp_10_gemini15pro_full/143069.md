outboxing:/ˈaʊtˌbɒksɪŋ/| n.|外击；超越常规思维；|The act of thinking outside the box, especially in a competitive context, to gain an advantage.|The company's success was due to its outboxing of the competition with a truly innovative product. 该公司的成功归功于它凭借一款真正创新的产品超越了竞争对手的常规思维。|近义词：lateral thinking, unconventional thinking, innovative thinking|反义词：conventional thinking, orthodox thinking|noun
