cancellation:/ˌkænsəˈleɪʃn/
n.
取消，撤销
The cancellation of the flight caused a lot of inconvenience.
航班取消造成了很多不便。
The cancellation of the contract will result in penalties.
合同的取消将导致罚款。
近义词：abrogation, revocation, annulment
反义词：confirmation, approval, validation
词性：名词

Etymology: From Late Latin *canellationem, from Latin cancellare “to cross out,” from cancelli “lattice, grating.”  The original sense referred to crossing out writing with lines, like a grid.
