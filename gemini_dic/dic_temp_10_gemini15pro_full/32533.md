chip-in:/ˌtʃɪp ˈɪn/ | v. | 凑钱；捐款；插嘴；帮忙 | To contribute something, usually money, to a shared cost or fund. Also, to interrupt a conversation or to offer help. |  We all chipped in for a birthday present for Sarah. 我们都凑钱给莎拉买生日礼物。 | contribute, donate, pitch in | withhold, refuse | verb

chip-in:/ˌtʃɪp ˈɪn/ | n. | 凑钱；捐款；插嘴；帮忙 | The act of contributing something, usually money, to a shared cost or fund.  | Everyone's chip-in helped us reach our fundraising goal. 大家的捐款帮助我们达到了筹款目标。 | contribution, donation | refusal | noun
