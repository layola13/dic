machicolation:/məˌʃɪkəˈleɪʃən/
n.
射击孔，垛口，杀人孔
An opening between the corbels supporting a projecting parapet, through which stones or other missiles could be dropped on attackers at the base of a wall.
The castle walls were studded with machicolations. 城墙上布满了射击孔。
crenel, embrasure, loophole
-
noun
