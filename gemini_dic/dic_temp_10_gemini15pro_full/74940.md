freudian:/ˈfrɔɪdiən/ | adj. n. | 弗洛伊德的；精神分析的；弗洛伊德学说信奉者 | Relating to Sigmund Freud, his theories, or his methods of psychoanalysis.  A follower of Freud's theories. |例句：His paintings are full of Freudian symbolism. 他的绘画充满了弗洛伊德式的象征主义。
