nondeformity:/ˌnɒndɪˈfɔːmɪti/
noun
无畸形；不变形
The absence of deformity; the state of being normal in shape or form.
After the surgery, the doctor confirmed the nondeformity of the limb.  手术后，医生确认了肢体没有畸形。
The brace ensured the nondeformity of the healing bone.  支架确保了正在愈合的骨骼不变形。
normality, soundness
deformity, malformation
