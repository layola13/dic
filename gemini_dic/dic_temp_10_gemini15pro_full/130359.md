non-unanimous:/ˌnɒn juˈnænɪməs/ | adj. | 非一致的，不全体同意的 | Not unanimous; not having the agreement of all people involved. | The jury returned a non-unanimous verdict. 陪审团做出了非一致的裁决。| The decision was non-unanimous, with two members dissenting.  这个决定并非一致通过，有两名成员表示异议。 | Near synonyms: divided, split, disagreeing, discordant | Antonyms: unanimous, united, agreed, concordant | adjective
