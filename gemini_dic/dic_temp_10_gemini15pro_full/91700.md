hyperconscientiously:/ˌhaɪpərˌkɒnʃiˈɛnʃəsli/ /ˌhaɪpərˌkɑːnʃiˈɛnʃəsli/| adv.|极其认真地，过分谨慎地|In an excessively conscientious manner; with extreme care and attention to detail, often to an unnecessary degree.|She proofread the document hyperconscientiously, checking every comma and full stop. 她极其认真地校对了文件，检查了每一个逗号和句号。|Example: He hyperconscientiously followed every rule, even the most obscure ones.  他一丝不苟地遵守每一条规则，即使是最晦涩难懂的。|Synonyms: meticulously, scrupulously, painstakingly, rigorously|Antonyms: carelessly, negligently, perfunctorily|词性:副词
