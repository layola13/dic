gutturalism:/ˈɡʌtərəlɪzəm/
n.
喉音化；喉音
The act or practice of using guttural sounds in speech.
His speech was marked by a distinct gutturalism. 他的讲话带有明显的喉音。
gutturality, gutturalness
---
Etymology:
Late 19th century: from guttural + -ism.
