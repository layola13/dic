half-radical:/ˌhæf ˈrædɪkəl/ | adj. | 半激进的；不彻底的 |  Partially radical; not fully committed to radical principles.  Referring to someone or something that adopts some, but not all, of the tenets of radicalism.  It implies a degree of moderation or compromise within a radical framework. | He proposed a half-radical solution, hoping to appease both sides. 他提出了一个不彻底的解决方案，希望能安抚双方。|  moderate, reformist, gradualist | extremist, radical, revolutionary | adjective
