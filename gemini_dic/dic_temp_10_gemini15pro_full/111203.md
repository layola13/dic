lithonthryptic:/ˌlɪθɒnˈθrɪptɪk/ | adj. | 碎石的，溶石的 |  Relating to or designating an agent that dissolves calculi (stones), especially in the urinary tract.  Derived from Greek *lithos* (stone) + *thryptein* (to crush). |  The doctor prescribed a lithonthryptic medication to help dissolve the kidney stones. 医生开了碎石药来帮助溶解肾结石。| Synonyms:  antilithic,  calculus-dissolving | Antonyms:  lithogenic (stone-forming) | adjective
