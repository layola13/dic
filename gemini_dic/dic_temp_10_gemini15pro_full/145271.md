overbooking:/ˌoʊvərˈbʊkɪŋ/ | n. | 超额预订 | The practice of selling more tickets or reservations than are actually available, anticipating that some purchasers will not show up. | The airline apologized for the overbooking and offered compensation to the bumped passengers. 航空公司为超额预订道歉，并向被拒载的乘客提供赔偿。| 近义词: overselling | 反义词: underbooking | 词性: 名词

overbook:/ˌoʊvərˈbʊk/ | vt. | 超额预订 | To sell more tickets or reservations than are actually available. | The hotel had overbooked the rooms, and we were left without a place to stay.  酒店超额预订了房间，我们没有地方住。| 近义词: oversell | 反义词: underbook | 词性: 动词
