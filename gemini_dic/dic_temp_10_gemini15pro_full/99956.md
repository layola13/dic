interocular:/ˌɪntərˈɒkjʊlə(r)/ /ˌɪntərˈɑːkjələr/
adjective
眼间的；两眼之间的
Relating to the space between the eyes.
The boxer received a powerful interocular blow.  拳击手受到强烈的两眼之间的一击。

The horse's interocular distance is wider than a human's. 这匹马的两眼间距比人类的宽。

interocular:/ˌɪntərˈɒkjʊlə(r)/ /ˌɪntərˈɑːkjələr/
adjective
明显的，一目了然的
Obvious; plain to see.  (Figurative meaning)
The evidence was interocular; the suspect was clearly guilty. 证据确凿，嫌疑人显然有罪。

The flaws in their argument were interocular. 他们论证中的缺陷显而易见。


近义词:between the eyes, evident, obvious, apparent, manifest
反义词:hidden, obscure, unclear
词性:adjective
