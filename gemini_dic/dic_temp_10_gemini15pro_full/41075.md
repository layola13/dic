例句2：During the Civil War, copperheads advocated for peace with the Confederacy. 在内战期间，主和的北方民主党人主张与南方邦联和平相处。|近义词：snake, pacifist (for the political meaning)|反义词：warmonger, hawk (for the political meaning), patriot (for the political meaning)|名词
