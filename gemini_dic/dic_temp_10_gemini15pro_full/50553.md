deputies:/ˈdepjʊtiz/ (US) /ˈdɛpjʊtiz/ (UK)| n.|代表，代理人，副手|A person appointed to act for or represent another, especially in an official capacity.  A deputy is often a second in command who can take charge when the main leader is absent.|The sheriff's deputies patrolled the county. 警长的副手在县里巡逻。|Synonyms: representatives, assistants, substitutes, agents, lieutenants|Antonyms: leaders, principals, superiors|noun
