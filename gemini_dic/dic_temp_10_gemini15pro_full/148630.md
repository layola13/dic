overthinking:/ˌoʊvərˈθɪŋkɪŋ/
n.
过度思考；想太多
The act of thinking about something too much or for too long.
She was paralyzed by overthinking and couldn't make a decision. 她因过度思考而不知所措，无法做出决定。
He lost sleep due to overthinking his job interview. 他因为过度思考工作面试而失眠。
近义词：rumination, dwelling, brooding
反义词：decisiveness, action, acceptance
词性:名词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "thinking" (present participle of the verb "think")
