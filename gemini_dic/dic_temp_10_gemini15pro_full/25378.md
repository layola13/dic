bulbiferous:/ˌbʌlˈbɪfərəs/
adjective
长鳞茎的；产生鳞茎的
Bearing or producing bulbs.
例句：Some lilies are bulbiferous, propagating themselves through the development of small bulbs.  一些百合是长鳞茎的，通过小鳞茎的生长来繁殖。

例句：The bulbiferous plant quickly spread throughout the garden.  这种长鳞茎的植物很快就在花园里蔓延开来。
近义词：bulbing, proliferous
反义词： (No direct antonym, could consider words like "barren" or "sterile" in specific contexts)
词性：adjective


Etymology:
From Latin *bulbifer*, from *bulbus* ("bulb") + *-fer* ("bearing").  The "-fer" suffix is derived from the Latin *ferre* ("to bear" or "to carry").
