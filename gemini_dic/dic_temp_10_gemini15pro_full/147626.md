overplotted: /ˌoʊvərˈplɒtɪd/ (US) /ˌəʊvəˈplɒtɪd/ (UK)
adjective
过度绘制的，过度标绘的
Covered with too many plotted points or lines, making it difficult to interpret the data.
The graph was overplotted, making it hard to distinguish the individual data points.  图表过度绘制，难以区分各个数据点。
The scatter plot was so overplotted that it was useless for analysis. 散点图过度绘制，以至于无法进行分析。

近义词: cluttered, crowded, busy
反义词: sparse, clear, uncluttered
词性: adjective
