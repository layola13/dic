carrytale:/ˈkæriˌteɪl/| n.|搬弄是非的人，告密者|A person who carries tales or gossip, especially one who maliciously spreads rumors.|He's a known carrytale, so be careful what you say around him. 他是个有名的搬弄是非的人，所以在他周围说话要小心。|近义词：gossip, informer, snitch, tattletale|反义词:diplomat, peacemaker|词性:noun

词源:  From "carry" + "tale," meaning to carry stories or information from one person to another, often with negative connotations.  "Tale" in this context refers to a piece of news or gossip.
