outerly:/ˈaʊtərli/
adjective
外在地；表面上；完全地
Relating to or characteristic of the outside or outer part; completely or utterly.
He was outwardly calm, but inwardly he was terrified. 他表面上很镇定，但内心却很害怕。
The project was outwardly successful, but plagued with internal problems.  这个项目表面上很成功，但却充满了内部问题。
近义词: externally, superficially, completely, totally, utterly
反义词: inwardly, internally, partially
词性: adjective
