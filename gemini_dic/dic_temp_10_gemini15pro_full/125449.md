multiplexes:/ˈmʌltɪˌpleksɪz/
n.
多路复用器；多厅影院；复合物
A multiplex is a movie theater complex with multiple screens.  多路复用器是一种具有多个屏幕的电影院综合体。
This multiplex has 12 screens showing a variety of films.  这个多厅影院有12个屏幕，放映各种各样的电影。
synonyms: cinema complex, movie theater, megaplex
antonyms: single-screen theater



multiplex:/ˈmʌltɪˌpleks/
v.
多路传输，多路复用
To multiplex is to combine multiple signals into one. 多路复用是将多个信号组合成一个。
The system multiplexes several television channels onto a single cable. 该系统将多个电视频道多路复用到一根电缆上。
synonyms: combine, merge, integrate
antonyms: separate, divide, split
词性:  v. & n.
