ouzo:/ˈuːzoʊ/ (US), /ˈuːzəʊ/ (UK)| n. |茴香酒；乌佐酒|A strong, dry anise-flavored aperitif that turns milky white when water is added, originally from Greece.|I ordered a glass of ouzo before dinner. 我在晚餐前点了一杯茴香酒。|Synonym: aperitif, pastis, absinthe|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps non-alcoholic beverages)|noun
