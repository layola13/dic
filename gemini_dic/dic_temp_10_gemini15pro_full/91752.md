hyperelegant:/ˌhaɪpərˈɛlɪɡənt/| adj.|极其优雅的，非常精致的|Excessively or extremely elegant; characterized by an over-the-top refinement or sophistication.  The word combines "hyper-" (meaning excessive or beyond) with "elegant."|She wore a hyperelegant gown to the gala, dripping in diamonds.|Synonyms: ultra-elegant, excessively refined, overly sophisticated|Antonyms: plain, simple, unrefined, crude|adjective
