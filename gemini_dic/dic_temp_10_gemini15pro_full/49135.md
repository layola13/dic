deists:/ˈdiːɪsts/
noun
自然神论者
People who believe in the existence of a supreme being, specifically of a creator who does not intervene in the universe.
Many of the Founding Fathers of the United States were deists. 美国许多开国元勋都是自然神论者。
近义词:freethinkers, rationalists
反义词:theists, atheists
词性:n.

Etymology:
From French déiste, from Latin deus (“god”).
