breakman: /ˈbreɪkmən/ (US), /ˈbreɪkmən/ (UK)| n. |制动员，刹车员|A person who assists the driver or engineer of a train, streetcar, or other vehicle in applying brakes, especially on freight trains.|The breakman signaled the engineer to slow down as the train approached the station.  制动员向司机发出信号，让火车在接近车站时减速。|近义词：brakeman (alternative spelling), switchman|反义词: N/A|noun
