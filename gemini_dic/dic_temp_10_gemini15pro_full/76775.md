gamete:/ˈɡæmiːt/| n.|配子|A mature haploid male or female germ cell which is able to unite with another of the opposite sex in sexual reproduction to form a zygote.|Human gametes contain 23 chromosomes.|近义词: germ cell, sex cell, reproductive cell|反义词: zygote|词性: noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek γαμέτης (gamétēs, “husband, spouse”) + -ete
