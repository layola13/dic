initialing:/ɪˈnɪʃəlɪŋ/ /ɪˈnɪʃlɪŋ/
n.
签署首字母；用首字母签名；草签
the act of marking or signing something with one's initials
例句：The initialing of the contract signifies the agreement of both parties.  合同的首字母签署表明双方同意。

例句：Initialing the document is a common practice for quick approval.  在文件上签首字母是一种快速批准的惯例。

近义词：signing, endorsing
反义词：N/A (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑 refusing to sign 或 rejecting)
词性：名词
