parried:/ˈpærid/ | v. | 挡开，避开，回避 | to ward off (a weapon or attack), especially with a countermove | The boxer parried the blow with his left arm. 拳击手用左臂挡开了那一击。 | Synonyms: deflected, blocked, averted, evaded | Antonyms: accepted, received, allowed, permitted | verb

parried:/ˈpærid/ | adj. | 被挡开的，被避开的 |  (of a weapon or attack) successfully warded off |  His parried thrust left him open to a counterattack. 他被挡开的刺击让他容易受到反击。 | Synonyms: deflected, blocked | Antonyms: unblocked, undefended | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *parer*, from Late Latin *parāre* "to prepare, provide, equip," itself from Latin *par* "equal, like, a pair." The sense evolution is from "prepare against" to "ward off."
