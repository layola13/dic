in-grouper:/ˈɪnˌɡruːpər/ | n. | 内部人员，圈内人 | A member of a favored group; one who belongs to the in-group. |  Example:  The in-groupers controlled access to the best resources. 圈内人控制着最佳资源的获取。

Example:  He felt like an outsider, not one of the in-groupers. 他感觉自己像个局外人，而不是圈内人之一。 | 近义词: insider, clique member | 反义词: outsider, out-grouper | 词性: noun
