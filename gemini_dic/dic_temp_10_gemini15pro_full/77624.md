geislers:/ˈɡaɪslərz/| n.|幽灵;鬼魂 (通常指德国传说中的)|A geisler is a type of ghost or spirit, often associated with German folklore. They are typically depicted as mischievous or malevolent spirits that haunt specific locations or people.|The old castle was said to be haunted by geislers.  这座古老的城堡据说闹鬼。|The villagers believed a geisler was responsible for the strange noises in the night. 村民们认为夜里的怪声是鬼魂造成的。|Synonyms: ghost, spirit, phantom, specter, apparition|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider: reality, corporeality)|noun
