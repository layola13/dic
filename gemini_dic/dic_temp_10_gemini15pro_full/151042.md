paraphasic:/ˌpærəˈfeɪzɪk/ | adj. | 失语的，言语错乱的 | Relating to or characterized by paraphasia, a language disorder in which a person uses words incorrectly or makes up words, often as a result of brain damage. |  The patient exhibited paraphasic errors, substituting "table" for "chair." 病人表现出言语错乱的错误，用“桌子”代替“椅子”。 |  The stroke survivor's speech was often paraphasic, making communication difficult. 中风幸存者的讲话经常出现言语错乱，使得沟通变得困难。 | Near synonyms: dysphasic, aphasic | Antonyms: articulate, coherent | adjective
